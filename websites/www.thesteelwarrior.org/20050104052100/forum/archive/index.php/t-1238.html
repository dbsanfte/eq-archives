Sev = Severilious in Emerald Jungle. For the warrior epic. Drops Runed Bolster Belt

Rage = Ragefire, now triggered in Skyfire Mountains. ULTRA RARELY drops the old nagafen loot table, including a cloak of flames. I've killed him five times haven't gotten anything other than Fennin Ro armor.

KD = Kelorek'Dar is in Cobalt Scar. Drops Cloak of Crystaline Waters. I had forgotten his name earlier.

Junkbeast = Junkbeast, from Plane of Innovation.

Zlandi = Zlandicar, from Dragon Necropolis. Drops the cowl of mortality, as well as a talisman for the shroud of the dar brood quest.

Felspine = the annoying camp in Siren's Grotto. I'm biased. I pretty much hate everything about Siren's Grotto, so I pretend the zone has no loot. It makes me a happier player. Your mileage will probably vary here.

Gore = Gorenaire, from Dreadlands, who drops RBB.

Talendor = Talendor from Skyfire Mountains. He has Nagafen's loot table.

If you all are doing HoT ... you can always try and see how you do on Dozekar. Completing the platinum tear quest thingie nets you the Silver Bracelet of Speed. That will end your haste worries for a long time to come.

But obviously, Dozekar is no slouch. And he's hard to catch up on my server.
