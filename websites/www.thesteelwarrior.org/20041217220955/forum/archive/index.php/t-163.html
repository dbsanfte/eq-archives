This is what propogates myths in EQ, like FASTEST weapon is ALWAYS best in primary (case in point, a 12/22 beats a 9/18 in primary for a long, LONG time).

2handers are NOT always the superior option. Parse the stuff yourself and find out. And then, parse it again on different mobs. And parse again. And by the time you're done parsing, you'll either a) Hate parsing, or b) Finally be high enough level that it won't freaking matter anymore anyways.

At level 52, his ATK is low. His skills are low. The damage bonus is low. The MODALS on GOOD dual wield weapons (which he has) are higher than on 2handers. I'd be surprised if his 2hand setup "own over anything" his dual wield setup.

2handers don't really come into their own until later.

Especially considering that at 52, the mobs he's fighting are all mostly OLD world stuff, which was much more tailored for dual wielding.

Dual Wield in your early 50s is still a viable option, especially when you're MOSTLY fighting Drolvargs still.

The game at 52 is a DIFFERENT game than the one at 65. And all too often we forget that.
