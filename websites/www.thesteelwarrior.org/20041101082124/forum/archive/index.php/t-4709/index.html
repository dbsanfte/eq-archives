Tugurok
While I'm glad that SoE finally increased the availability of a few weapons with aggro procs, like the Greatstaff of Thunder, or with solid dps, like the Greatstaff of the Four Winds, I just think they look way more stupid than they need to.

What warriors, especially REAL warriors (like ogres), need are big axes, brutal looking hammers, spiked clubs, cool looking swords, etc. What we don't need are completely bizarre things that have no visible relationship to a weapon, and that really look like some kind of caster staff. The GSoT might be a sort of balancing tool making badly needed aggro and dps available to warriors not able to summon up 60 person raids to gather them gear, but it shouldn't come with the penalty of making them look like gimps. I mean, I might be a welfare warrior, but do I have to run around with a sign saying "GIMP" on my back by carrying around some freaky caster staff/weapon?

The Exe Axe or Earthen Blade -- now THOSE were cool weapons. Mithril 2Handed sword? Win! Felhammer? Win! Club of the Ykesha? Win! GSoT? Not Win!

Bring back the Exe Axe!
