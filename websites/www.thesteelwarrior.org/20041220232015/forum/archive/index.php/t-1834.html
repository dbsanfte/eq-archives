Elemental DMG and Bane DMG are added to the weapons base damage. Bane DMG is unresistable, Elemental DMG is.

Dmg Bonus is added onto the end.

With a Axe of the Warmaster as Brutul mentioned (at level 60) you would have a 12+3/20 +11 weapon. The +3 comes from the Magic DMG and the +11 is from the Dmg Bonus. On any given swing, the weapon could be 12/20, 13/20, 14/20, or 15/20, depending on MoB resists.
