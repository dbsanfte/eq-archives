Incite is the warrior's best tool for the entire Kael script. Here is how I single handedly (well, other than tag help) pull the Kael script from Vindi to AOW himself.

First you clear to the left cubby (Why? because left is better because i said so). You setup, buff, etc. blah. Then when read you run up and agro everything in the arena and shout "Train to Vantage" (or whatever your guild is) and kill all 10-12 green cons. After the arena is clear, you assign another warrior to be rampage tank and have him wait in the middle of the arena.

You then arrow tag Vindicator and stay ahead of him just enough to land an Incite. Only after you have used Incite do you tell that rampage warrior to plunk one arrow to Vindicator and then head back to raid.

If you did it right, you now have Vindicator + his two guards following you, and Rampage list already pre-set. It's at this point you merely kite your train in a circle in the arena. Using Incite each time it pops. You have someone else run up and tag both guards off you. Once you have extracted and kill his guards (Incite will keep agro on you with regards to vindi) you're all set. Have them tell you when ready, and come barreling into camp with 7+ incites of pre-agro already. Congrats being able to call engage at 100% and rampage tank already set.

From there, you use the same method with Statue of Rallos Zek. Tagging him, and running him around in a circle and inciting to hold agro before someone can tag the Armor of Zeks off of you. If you landed 7+ incites you can also pre-tash pre-malo and pre-slow the statue before you even bring it into campsite (which we do).

From there, I usually decession with a druid or ranger to zone line and zone to EWastes and get Spirit of Eagle, then get COH'd back. It's not necessary but makes things a shit load easier later on. You then use levitate (or spirit of eagle) and come up the south end of the arena's pit itself (so you are looking north into the pit AOW himself pops into). You tag some green con, or anything, and run NORTH as hard as you can to end into that little notched out ledge north of the pit (which is very close X Y wise and just south of Idol.. it's where you drop down from if you were in Idol's halls). Wait for your green con to catch up and warp up top to you. This will cause assist agro to agro the Idol. HoTT comes in handy here to tell when he agro's so you can get a head start. Just run up into the arena and once again, Incite kite. You'll probably have a few trash mobs as well, so its all good. Just pull them off and after your 7+ Incites are done, pre-tash pre-malo pre-slow and bring him into camp.

AoW runs slightly faster than the others, so this is why Spirit of Eagle comes into handy. To kite him you tag him and run up to arena, his huge size makes him kinda warp around before he gets up top to run out. The key is to run him in a figure 8 in the arena vs. a circle. Using the South ends of the arena to make each turn. Using this method you can again 7+ incite (i usually do less just because this is so high risk to do) the AoW and get tons of pre-agro to again, instantly call engage soon as he hits camp.

This is how we've done AoW the past 5-6 kills and its insanely easy and makes the hit insanely fast since you don't wait on monks for jack. It's a warrior pulled event. Don't even have to mess with the yellow con zek trash in idol's hall since you agro idol with help agro.

It's hard to explain some of the things without drawing a diagram but if you understand what I said, grats a solid strat to making the pulls of this encounter trivial.

-Vendak
