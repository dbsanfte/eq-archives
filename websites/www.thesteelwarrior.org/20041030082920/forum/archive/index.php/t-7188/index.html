Eriatha Egan
Weapon agro works by swings, not by actual contact. While, for instance, a Blade of War will do absolutely no damage to both Seru and Emp, the agro will be 100% identical to what it would be on, say, your average HoH exp mob. I tanked Emp Ssra the last time my guild killed him with my auged Blade of War. Agro was so good, in fact, that he simply summoned me back the moment he casted "Rage of Ssraeshza" on me, as we were killing him in the center.

Bane weapons are crap for tanking, hehe.
