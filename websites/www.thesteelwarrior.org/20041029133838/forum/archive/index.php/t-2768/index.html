Eriatha Egan
As you all know, City of Mist received a partial revamp as of Tuesday's patch. The "partial revamp" included:

1) New mobs
2) Revamped loot tables for the named (Nek`Ashir, Rak`Ashir, Rathe of Jaxion or whatever).
3) More mob placement
4) Faster respawn (??)

The new mobs include the worn traveler model. It's the male figure with the beard who looks like he just washed from shore. There's also some different golems and different oozes.

Revamped loot tables from the named. I remember Rak`Ashir, I think, dropped a 35ac 40hp breastplate, but don't quote me on that one since it was late and I was tired. It was a hell of a nice bp for the difficulty though. I don't think the Ornate Rune Shield drops anymore either, but the shields that dropped were 10x better than before.

More mobs. Lots more mobs! When you pass the fountain to the area around the ooze building, there's more mobs out in the courtyard. More mobs surround the castle now. I didn't check inside the Temple, but mobs now spawn AT the Temple camp. Don't go AFK here please.

Overall, mobs hit slightly harder too. I noticed quite a few low triple digits, like 116, off mobs just beyond the entrance.

Great changes! Can't wait to PL my cleric here. :D
