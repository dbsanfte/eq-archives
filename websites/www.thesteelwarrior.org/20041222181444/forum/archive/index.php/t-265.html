Quest starts in Skyshrine by talking to Commander Leuz!


You say, 'Hail Commander Leuz'

Commander Leuz says 'Hello Ratmandu , Im the new commander of the Sky Shrine Militia. This group of Shrine residents and newly aligned outlanders are given various tasks to do for the benefit of the Shrine. Are you here to request a task?

You say, 'I am here to request a task.'

Commander Leuz says 'We have had recent news from one of our scouts in Western Wastes that there have been Storm Giants wandering the coastal area. We have not heard back from this scout in some time. What we need is a volunteer to go deliver some tools so the scout can accurately communicate what is happening back to us.

You say, 'I will volunteer'

Commander Leuz says 'Very good! Take this to the scout and wait for them to return the report.

The Commander will hand you "Scout Tools."

In the Western Wastes you can find Scout Charisa that stands on a hill just south of the Sirens Grotto zone. Handing her the Scout Tools gave this dialog.

Scout Charisa says' Great! I was wondering when they would send someone out to give me these. In my haste and excitement of this new assignment I forgot these. This scout business is great I must say. It sure beats setting in Southern Ro studying the ruins. I've been following these Storm Giants for days. They've been wandering up and down the coast looking for something.

Faction and exp
CoV+
Yelinak+
Krozmek-

This will spawn 8 giants near the shoreline just south of the Scout. Seven Kromzek Warriors and one Kromzek Captain. Harmony the seven warriors so that the Captain can be single pulled. Kill him and loot the Broken Disk.

Give the disk to Scout Charisa. She'll give you a "Scout's Note" that you can return to Commander Leuz in Skyshrine. She'll also give you the Robe of Benevolence.

If you give her the robe back, you will get a Bracer of Benevolence. If you turn the bracer in, you will get a Talsiman of Benevolence.
