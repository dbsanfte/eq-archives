Hyardgune
I did Run 1 at 55, Run 2 at 62, then levelled to 65. My only two regrets in the entire process was getting Run2 at 62, I should have just waited until 65 and getting Regen over Innate Metabolism.

Unless you are holding at 52 for Naggy/Vox, I would strongly recommend waiting on Run1 until about 55. I seem to recall that each AA is about as much xp as is required to go from 51 to 52. If you are doing AAs at 51 or 52, it is gonna be sssssssssllllllloooooooooowwwwwwww. At about 55, you are at least past the KC stage and are probably getting xp in Seb, Velks, tier 1, etc and the xp moves a whole lot faster. Instead of grinding out an AA in a week, you can grind out an AA in a day or two, then go back to levelling.

As for my recommendation for getting Run1 and then heading for 65, the reasoning goes like this. Run1 gives you enough of a jump that you can outrun just about anything in a dungeon situation. If you are outdorrs, Run1 or Run3 doesn't matter much since you'll usually have sow. Sitting around, grinding out the 5 more AAs to get Baron and then grinding out the additional 12 AAs for ND3 is just a waste. The xp for those 17 AAs is better spent levelling, which gets you more hit points, better resistance to spells, better damage, higher skills (up to 60), triple attack (at 60), higher stat caps (61-65). Once you get to 65 you can quickly blow out AAs on Run3, Innate Metabolism (stuff in PoP hits so hard that Regen becomes useless, imo) then ND3, AE Taunt, then whatever you want.

Would the additional hit points from ND3 have helped on my way to 65? Yes, absolutely. On the other hand, I'd probably still be 62 or 63 right now and in the mid-60s hell of begging friends for groups.

Buun Sofsteel
I think whether you pursue AA or level and when should be based solely on your equipment. Many of the people who say get 65 first were 60 when PoP came out and already had 30+ AA and all their defensives (ND, CA, CS etc...) and as a result they were better able to XP in PoP, regardless of equipment. If you have an unbuffed AC of less than 1180 I don't think you can be effective above Tier 1 in PoP without some defensive AA. Shit above Tier 1 hits hard and you are gonna get pummeled.

Because I am not as well equipped, ie at 60 was only about 1140 unbuffed AC, I spent the time and got RS3 and IR3, Baron, at 60, while still grouping in Disease (right and left corners). At 61 I moved to Storms and Valor and found I was a mana sponge and did CS1, CA1, and ND1 to make up a bit for my poor AC. The biggest difference in any of these for me was Combat Agility. I use Yaulp as a parser and the biggest damage drop came from CA1. I got ND first because everyone, and I mean everyone, preaches that HP HP HP are where it is at. This was true for Velious and Luclin when as MT in HoT you are getting hit in the 300's and taking minor AEs, but not so much in PoP. I'm sorry but I would rather have the 500+ hitters hit me one less time per 10 swings than have an extra 400hp to buy another second for a CHeal to hit.

If you have under a 1300 AC buffed in xp groups and are 61-63 you are a "Tweener". You cant get good level xp in Tier 1 and yet can't effectively tank Razorfiends. So here is what I think...

Over 1300 AC in reg grouping...get 65 then speed thru your AA in BoT, PoV, HoH.

Under 1250 AC in reg grouping...
Get to 60
60 - Baron with Run Speed 3 included
61 - 6 AA on CA1, CS1, and ND1
62 - CA2 (and finish up CA3 only if you want the tag Veteran, but I didn't ;P )
Get to 65


As a 63 War with about 1240 ac in xp groups and CA2, CS1, and ND1 I am tanking effectively in HoH and PoV. Next stop for me is 65 and then will finish my defensive AA.

There is no right and wrong way to do it. Look at 1) what is my equipment and 2) where do I want to hunt.
