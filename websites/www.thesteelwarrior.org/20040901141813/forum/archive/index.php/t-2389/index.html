Here's an odd idea, please tell me what you think of it.

Myself I'm not too sure wether I like this idea or not ;),
but imo, it definately deserves some looking into.

Since imo a Warrior should be able to tank an unslowed mob as well as a knight tanks it slowed.
I was trying to figure a way of making this possible without Nerfing knights, but rather upgrading Warriors.
Here is what I came up with :

Auras: (I think the idea of Auras was proposed with Raaj's poll)


Frightfull/Terrorising/Demonic Aura

Warriors have learnt how to cast fear into the hearts of their foes.

-When a mob hits you, the aura will delay his next attack by 15%, 30%, 50%.
----

What this skill does basicaly, is Slow the mob for the Warrior only.
This allows Warriors to be loved and cherished when there are no Slowers around.

The skill could stack with other slows, but not go over 75%.
So if the skill is used with a BST 50% Slow,
the mob will hit the Warrior with 75% delay, and hit everyone else with 50% delay.


How will the reuse time etc.. be dealt?...

Personally, I'd like to see Auras as permanent disciplines.
Using one aura disallows the use of another Aura.

So you can keep Demonic Aura up as long as you like, but it's stopping you from using Damage Shield auras, or Hate Auras etc...

Making you have to choose between the best ones considering the situation.


Good thing about this Aura, is it will still force people to watch their aggro, but with reason.

So Pros:
-Group members will watch aggro, knowing that if they get aggro, it's a UNslowed mob that will kick their ass.
-Warriors will be loved for groups, without shadowing out Paladins.
-Slow oriented class won't become an ABSOLUTE necessity, but they will keep their priviledged spot, and still be loved.
(If a slower is on, you take him, but if no slowers are on it won't stop you from making a group, as long as a Warrior is on)
-This keeps the aggro difference between knights and warriors that many people wish to keep.
-This won't unbalance raids whatsover, because the mobs have their delays capped anyhow (75%)

Cons:
-other Auras will need to be implemented as well.
Otherwise the Demonic Aura thingy, will have to be either permanent(which will never happen),
or be time based and have reuse time (which sucks and removes the skill's use).
But implementing other Auras isn't a bad thing at all, it only makes the whole thing a bit more complex ;)


Please post constructive criticism or ideas :)
