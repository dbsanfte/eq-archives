Venemous Axe of the Velium Brood. It's 11dmg 24dly, procs an 85 DD at level 35. Great weapon for aggro if your dex is decent (100+) and cheap too. Commonly seen selling for 600-700pp on the nameless.

Barnacle Covered Axe. 9dmg 21dly, 5ac 8sta 5 MR. Proc is Pique- 50dd and +100 hate. <Ghetto frosty ;)> I used this for a while grouped at 42 and 43. When it procced, aggro was mine. Period. Those two weapons both are great for aggro, I'd advise you to keep an eye out in the bazaar for a good deal on either.
