For weapon 2 it will be between 1 and 30 then 15,5+dmg bonus average each 3seconds, wich mean 28,5 each 3s.

-30 swings of the first weapon, basically withouth dmg bonus: 30x10,5=315 dmg

-20 swings of the second one, withouth dmg bonus: 310dmg

Numbers are basically the sames, but, now add the damage bonus:

-3 swings of the first weapon: 705dmg, damage bonus add 390 dmg

-2 swings of the second weapon: 570 dmg, damage bonus add 260dmg
