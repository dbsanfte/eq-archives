Tempur
I would do hierophants cloak over the pernicity one. Raw HP is worth more IMO as its getting easier and easier to max sta out.

Axgoreth's belt is very nice AC but kinda sucks in that it has exactly 0 HP/STA on it. I'd look for that new haste belt from Veksar. Soulscream is its name IIRC. Its 31% haste and has some HP, STA and a few other stats. Another option is to take a few friends and go camp a Heavy Rubicite Girdle in CT. You might get lucky with a Mosquito Leg Earring drop while you are there.

If you are ally to CoV, do the draconic pauldrons and bracer of benevolence quests. They are both very worthwhile.

Earring of the Solstice is a damn fine earring for the price. For about 5k you get 5ac, 5sta, 55hp, and some saves.

Crystal Spider Eyes would be a decent upgrade for your mask. 8ac and 35hp iirc.
