Draconic Pauldrons are real nice for the effort required to get them. While you're there you could try for a Bracer of Benevolence too (check the Keep for many threads on how to do this quest.)

For the Helm you can pick up a helmet of the Raldukan pretty cheap these days, buy a vial of dye aswell unless you want to have a pink hat. (26AC 35HPs 4sta)

Kylong Shinguards from Veksar are nice and dropping in price quickly. Or you could try taking your new group of friends there to camp them. (35AC 45HPs 6sta)

Arms and boots I'd look for the new cultural, good AC and good HPs for both slots there.

Having a regular group that you can log in to is in my opinion the best thing to happen to anyone in EQ. Maybe try taking your group to a few new spots where some nice gear could drop for all of you. Veksar has alot of lewt in it that could be upgrades for you and theres some nice weapons dropping in the revamped droga (you wont have to look far to find threads on those).
