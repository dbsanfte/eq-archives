Guide to camping windblades:-

1. Get your group together. These are undead critters, so necromancers, slay undead pallies, and of course clerics are very useful; the necro can slow them so you don't need a traditional slower.

2. Zone into HoH, invis up, and follow the left hand wall until you reach the three Wrulons.

3. Ahead of you are six Valiant spirits. They are white armors and they are undead. These are not the critters you are looking for. They drop ethereal parchments, spectral parchments, and defenders lightblades; you can kill them as a warm-up.

4. Past these Valiant spirits is a set of stairs down. The camp you want is in the room at the bottom of the stairs.

5. You will see that there are various Valorous spirits up in this room. They are usually black armors which are not aggro. However, they do assist one another. They are PH's for the white armors which are aggro.

6. Pull them with Pacification or Harmony ideally, although they can be FD split if your puller doesn't suck. They cannot be mezzed, snared, or rooted, and they mitigate slow; hit max is 544 from memory.

7. Be aware that the white Valorous spirits usually drop nothing at all, or sometimes ethereals/spectrals. The Girdle of Mithaniel and the Windblade are rare.
