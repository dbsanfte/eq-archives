The first decision a warrior makes is where to spend his stat points. Without knowing what they do though, they can be very confusing.

Stat What does it do?
Strength:
Increases your attack making you hit more often for maximum damage. Also determines how much weight you can carry in your inventory.
Stamina:
Increases your hitpoints as well as allows you to jump or swim longer without getting tired.
Agility:
Helps with defensive skills like riposte, parry, and dodge while also increasing your AC.
Dexterity:
Contributes to your speed of learning combat skills as well as how often the effects on your weapons proc.
Wisdom:
Helps to learn noncombat skills and success rates with tradeskills.
Intelligence:
Helps to learn noncombat skills and success rates with tradeskills.
Charisma:
Improves faction and Prices from vendors. Also used at higher levels to improve the chance of DI going off.

Skills that are not obtained at level 1 must be trained at least once at your guildmaster before you will begin to learn them on your own.
