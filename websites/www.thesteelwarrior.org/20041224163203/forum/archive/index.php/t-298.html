Is being the guild's MT an important part of your EQ experience? Or is day-to-day exping the focus?

If you say MT, run, don't walk to level 65.

If, on the other hand, MTing for your guild is a side thing for you (i.e. you MT sometimes when the usual MT-65 war isn't around), then it probably makes sense to stay lower level and get faster AAs.

As you level up, it will become progressively more difficult to kill mobs within 5 levels(bonus) using just a cleric out of group. So there is a point of diminishing returns where you simply can't level up past X level and still kill things that give the bonus. Given your magelo, but without knowing the cleric's setup, I'm going to guess that you can continue through 61 and doubtfully 62. I'm pretty sure that leveling to 63 will kill your AA exp.
