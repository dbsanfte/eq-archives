Arathena
It works like this:
Anger II main hand x 2 procs per minute = 800 hate
Anger II off hand x 1 proc per minute = 400 hate
Total hate per minute =1200 hate

Anger III main hand x 2 procs per minute = 1200 hate
Total hate per minute =1200 hate.

Now, we could argue all day about the early fight proc distribution and relative merits, but on a hate per (time) basis, they're equally valid.

Arathena
It's correct. Given two weapons of identical delay, 2 Anger IIs have 1.5 times the chance of going off on any given combat round compared to a single main handed Anger II, but there's disadvantages to spreading your hate over two procs:

1: The 400 hate has a larger chance of being insufficent to overcome early debuff aggro. I'm not entirely sure how it all stacks up myself, since I throw my raw hate via terror.

2: What happens 22 adventures down the line when you want to upgrade your hate setup? That's right, you've gotta destroy an Anger II and rep it with a Ykesha or a Anger III. Then you've gotta destroy the other Anger II 22 more adventures down the line. That's a ton of wasted points.

Given this, if you really want to be a tightwad about points, I'd probably go with two of the 75 dd Ykeshas over dual anger IIs, but take this with an entire shaker of salt, cuz I'm not a warrior, I just like to play with maths.
