Khiloor
Need a face and neck upgrade.

Neck Upgrades:
- Clattering Bone Neck (Sta and HP)
- PoK Talisman (for resists)
- Fierce Heraldic Neck
- if you're a barb warrior, look into Qeynos badge quests. the quests are kinda long, but the 4th neck is very easy to obtain and has see invis.

Face Upgrades:
- Eyepatch of Plunder (quest item)
- Ammy Mask (maybe a bit expensive)
- Fierce Heraldic Visor

And get rid of those shoulders too. eeechh.. at least get some IGS (imbued granite spaulders) for Resists, and Fierce Heraldic for AC/HP.
