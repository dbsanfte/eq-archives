From what I could tell Fried, he's not bad. Its simply having an enchanter or a rooter to control the add he/PH comes with. His room has 3 mobs in it (including him). In the center is the PH which is a huvul strategist. On his left (farther in) its an elite GoD mob (usually have seen kyvs and ukun, and ikaav occasionally) and on his right (about as far in as the elite GoD mob) is a dragorn (battlemaster from what I saw) More on the mob itself, he was no harder than his PH with defensive on. He was dead before defensive got to 40%, I would say he's easier than the elites are.

We had the enchanter target the elite for the mez due to the stupid powerful chaos claws and the ranger to root the dragorn. When I pulled the PH (and the named as well) at level 68, the elite added but the dragorn did not, even though he was as far back as the elite was. Also, to get to him he's in the hexxagonal room in the complex near projection of body. Its quite a hike, but you can invis as far as the huvul that guards the path to enchainer kurou area.
