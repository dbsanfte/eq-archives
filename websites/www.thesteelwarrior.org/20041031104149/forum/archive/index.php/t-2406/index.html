Cat warrior ... need armor .... been there ... done that ...

Unfortunatly, your only options are Velious Quest, CT Smithed (Cazic thule the zone, Tae Ew stuff), or PoP Smithed (if you can get elemental smithed stuff, go for it).

I suggest, as someone else already did, Velious Quest stuff, either Kael or SS to start with.

Pick up a set of Tae Ew plate legs (90 HP, 35ac, you can wear them), bracers have 45hp on em as well. If you can find a smith who makes the stuff anyway, thats the hardest part.

If Tae Ew stuff is too hard to find, look into Silver Chitin Wristband x2, or Bracers of Benevolence.

As a Vah Shir, unless your raiding, you wont find much for armor slots due to lack of cultural options.

Since the prices are comming down quite nicely, you can also look into Ornate armors. They would be your best option, but the most expensive as well.
