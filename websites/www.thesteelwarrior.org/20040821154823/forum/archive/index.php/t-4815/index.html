Gharghael
Azrael is correct.

Technically (from Lucy):
Fortitude Discipline = "Increase Chance to Avoid Melee by 10000%"
Furious Discipline = "Increase Chance to Riposte by 10000%"

I've hardly ever used either myself either, but I would think Fortitude would be best used when not facing (read: running from) the mob, whereas Furious would be the choice when actually facing the mob since you can't riposte attacks from behind. There may be other circumstances where one is preferential to the other, but I haven't thought of them yet.
