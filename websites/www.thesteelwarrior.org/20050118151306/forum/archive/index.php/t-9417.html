Any dex lowering debuff will lower the proc rate. Cripple is a great proc rate slower. Using a 2HS weapon will lower the riposte, therefore less damage you take and you mights try using a rune on you to ease the initial shock until the cleric gets his mantra and the debuffs have landed. I know runes cost pp but there is a reason they are in the game. The spell absorption buff with take 10% of the proc damage. The vie melee buff can take 10% of melee damage and a good furious disc will save you a few initial hits. A spell shield item would be ideal, one reason the OOW exp. is supposed to be a challenge for the POP kids. Sometimes stopping your melee attacks will land you a few seconds of time when you hit 20% health and get low hitpoint aggro, since you won't get riposte damage. Slippery Attacks is an OOW aa you should get a good look at if you plan to dual wield here. I recommend 2hs for it's survivability on the tougher mobs. :eek:
