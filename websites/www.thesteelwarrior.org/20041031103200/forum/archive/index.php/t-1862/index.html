Lilwee Beasty
First suggestion would be to lose your earrings,and get either orc fang (very cheap, and ok stats) or the enchanter made earrings. Electum black sapphire I think they are...3 ac, 35 hp. I'd also suggest selling you mask and picking up crystal spider eyes, or a death mask of the elysians if you can afford it. I'd also sell your shadel bandit ring and get another 6/65 ring...not often I find that the resists outweight the better hp on the 6/65.

For your weapons, I'd highly suggest selling your claws, and picking up a second Honed Ykesha blade if you can find one. I've hear they are excellent aggro weapons on par with the frostbringer.

As for Fierce Heraldic....get ANY piece you can afford. I'd say bracers are the best buy, and will last you forever since there are *very* few upgrades to them until the elemental planes. After that, legs would be the best, then any other piece other than BP or hands. Although neither of those are to shabby and if you find a good price on them I would pick them up also.

My best suggestion, is simply browse the bazaar. Put in a nice specific search (warrior useable, your race, etc) and then go through each slot....try and find cheap items with HP and sta and ac. With a few thousand PP, you should be able to upgrade your HP by at least 100, and possibly your ac as well.

For weapons, ditch the claws and buy a Frostbringer. That paired with your Yak should be great aggro, play with with to mainhand based on proc rates. Never had a player made Yak but the Frosty ruled when I used it, the proc was great aggro and ratio is nice for DPS.

Neksnappa
First off, those weapons *should* be fine, but when you hunt in the planes, you'll run across other melees with epics and weapons just as good as yours. Combine that with their spells/abilities/higher dps, it's hard to hold aggro. You basically have to either get weapons with good aggro procs, like the crafted ykesha or two and a frosty, or get a good 2hs/2hb and hope you don't miss too often in the beginning of the fight. Some weapons like this would be widebladed greatsword, reaver, stonebladed axe of dismay, etc. The axe is 44/44 75hp and costs less than 3k pp on most servers since it's warrior-only. It's what I've been using in groups since level 51, and while tanking in the lower level planes, I've rarely had aggro problems. You might want to look into getting something like it yourself and see if it works. I'll throw in a few armor upgrades as well.

-Stat food/drink- Buy a stack or two of halas 10lb. meat pies and grobb liquidised meat, together those add a hefty 11 stamina and 30 hp, along with other stats.

-Keep up the hunting in kael, hope for some plate drops.

-Sell the kylong greaves, buy crusty. You'll take a small dex hit, but the +16 stamina is awesome. Only get these if you see them cheap and don't think you'll get thurg greaves any time soon though.

-Try and grab Stout Plate Manacles in the bazaar, to replace your shaded wrist. The manacles are 14ac, 4 str, 3 dex, 5 wis, 30hp, and should run you under 1k pp.

That's about it for cheap upgrades, some pricier ones would be:

Earring of the solstice MQ (3.5-4k)
Clattering Bone Necklace (2kish?)
Valorium Ring(s) of Gallantry (6.5-7k each)
Cloak of the Maelstrom (3k)
Any fierce heraldic or SS MQ you can get.
