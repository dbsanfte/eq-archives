Shredder
I can't say I've noticed any push with DD component only spells. There are a few, but they're very situational.

Where you see it is when the cleric decides to make use of those ornate gauntlets and nuke away with Condemnation. On raids though, they're usually much too busy either in the chain or patch healing/rezzing.

The only other two classes that I've seen that enjoy employing the push spells are wizards and paladins, on a regular basis. More than likely, it's the pallies doing it. Their only "nuke" is also a stun which has a pretty hefty pushback effect. If you set up a minimized spell effect window, you can filter the text to it, and verify later with your favorite log parser.

Also keep in mind that any weapons with a DD + stun also have a minor push effect. You don't have any berserkers using a Chetari Wardstaff do you?

I don't think you can move permarooted mobs in any fashion. I don't recall being able to budge any of the permarooted ToV dragons, but that was long before PoP.
