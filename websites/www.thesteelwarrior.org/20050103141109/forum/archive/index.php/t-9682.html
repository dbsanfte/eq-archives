Good explanation on paladin board too:

http://p202.ezboard.com/fpaladinsofnorrathfrm22.showMessage?topicID=1530.topic

Summary: the new values are duration caps in ms, not hate values.

We have known since 10.0 second stuns were introduced and given to us with absurdly high mana costs that they never did and never will stun for 10.0 seconds. We couldnt offer up a reasonable explination for this, people thought the turning of a full tick stunned would break stun, some thought it was other stuns causing it to break early (which you could disprove), others just figured spells were hardcoded to NOT stun for the full 10 seconds.

It is the latter who are correct, shown in Lucy's current update.



For example, for a stun on a slot X :

- 1s stun => baseX = 1000 / base2_X = 500 (Anger, Force of Ykesha, Pestilence Shock Strike, Call of Fire Strike)

- 2s stun => baseX = 2000 / base2_X = 2000 (Trample)

- 3s stun => baseX = 3000 / base2_X = 3000 (Time Rend, Static Blow)

- 4s stun => baseX = 4000 / base2_X = 4000 (Ancient Force of Jeron)

- Ys stun (with Y > 5) => baseX = Y000 / base2_X = 5250 (Tactical Strike, Serene Command)



Base1 - base_4 are linked to stun duration on players (why tactical strike CAN stun you, the recipient of it, for 30s, but on a mob, if it were possible, the base2_x value is used, which is why mobs will never stay stunned for the full duration of any stun spell lasting over 5.25 seconds.


Stun duration on a Player casted spell caps out at 5.25 seconds.


So as we have known for years, there is

NO SUCH THING

as a 10.0 sec stun.


Dont believe me? Try it for yourself.


Thank you Lucy for finally showing us what the deal is.
