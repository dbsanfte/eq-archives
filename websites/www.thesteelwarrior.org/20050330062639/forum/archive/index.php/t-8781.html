Everyone will kill me but ill give you the run down.

1st you gotta save the True Image of Noqufiel till the end, dont attack it till you have the mirror image below 10% and alive before you kill the true image.

2nd Each mirror image has to be killed by a different class. Look for the emotes that tell you what class, if you dont see the emote your doing something wrong and need to leash and restart the fight.

3rd Noq is susceptible to blunt damage, make sure your tanks use blunt weaps.

4th and most important, only small characters can open the doors to his room.

5th For the DT mobs try and get a few together, have your warriors AET and ripo the DT mob (they are different DT mobs than previous event).

With this strat you will get to Txevu in no time
