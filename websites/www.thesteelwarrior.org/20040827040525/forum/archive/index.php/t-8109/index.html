I totally agree with Battleblade on this. Shields could be a lot more effective if they were redesigned.

The Furious Bash effect should be a 10% worn agro mod (proc and dps) per level (ie FB 2 is 20%, FB 5 is 50%, etc.) to compensate for our loss of our off hand weapon when we use a shield.

The extra bash damage component would help to offset our loss of DPS for not using a 2h weapon. This would be nice if you are not the MA in situations where high damage ripostes and AE rampage is a significant factor and the extra AC would be helpful.

If the stun component fired every bash, it would be a little overpowered since you can bash a lot more than 3 to 4 times a minute (what a double stun/A3 auged off hand weapon would give on average) so making the stun into a regular proc on the shield activated by bashing is a good idea.

I would also like to see some kind of Furious Bash augment to put on existing shields. This would be useful to add to or upgrade the FB level for extra agro (similar to the anger 3 idea) but especially to help warriors that do not have access to the mobs that drop shields with more than FB2 on them. As an alternative, making the shield able to accept Anger 3 or any other weapon augment and fire on bashes would be nice.

It has also been mentioned that an AA to allow wearing a shield and dual wielding might be a good thing. This would be a little overpowered but perhaps if the shield stats were reduced by half when equiped with a 2 hander, it might be doable.

I don't think you should be able to do as much damage with a sheild as dual wielding/2hander. It makes no sense. I think all three options should have their trade-offs or everyone will just use one option all the time and the others become useless.

The options now are:

Dual Wield...good dps, great aggro, lots of hps, usually not as much AC
2-hand...good dps, low riposte damage...generally less ac/hps than dual wield
Shield...Best defense, low ripostes, most AC/hps of any combo, worst aggro and dps

In my mind, sword and board should be for maxing out defense. It should be the worst of the three options for DPS and aggro, but should make a noticable difference in defense. Not just ac/hps, but some innate advantage to having a shield equipped instead of an offhand sword or a 2-hander. Raising the AC cap is good, but I'm not sure anyone is at the cap anyway hehe. Innate block, some kind of shielding effect (imagine that!) something that makes it very important to use a shield on the hardest-hitting encounters, but with a significant drawback (like major loss of aggro and dps) to make it balanced. If knights are going to be able to use this there should be something to balance the fact that they don't lose aggro generation capability using a shield like warriors do.

I do agree that improving shields to make them a more viable option is a good idea though.
