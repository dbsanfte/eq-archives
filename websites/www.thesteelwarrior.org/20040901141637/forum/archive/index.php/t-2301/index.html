Roxxanne
Question from a shammy here seeking your opinion.

Is it better for the shammy to set up his slow announcement hotkey:

1) "Inc. SLOW on %T in 3.0 secs" [used at the start of casting]

or

2) "%T has been SLOWED" [used upon seeing the "yawn"]

Using both seems over the top on the battle spam and uses too many hotkeys.

It seems to me that 1) is more useful to the tank, but 2) is more useful to the cleric.

I have been using 1) on the theory that slow is seldom resisted when I Malo first, and the Warrior can better time his taunt with 3 secs advance warning than waiting until after the slow lands. If there is a resist, folks know when they see another cast message.

What do you think, or does it even matter from the tanks perspective?
