Step One: Go to Permafrost and kill the goblin Archeologist. You can solo your way in there and kill him fairly easily. EQ Atlas has a map with his spawn point. He drops a weight reduction bag. He's on about a 25 minute timer, sometimes has a PH. Whack mammoths on the way in and out.
Step two: get Virtue, Fo7, SoW. Get a SoW potion or two for emergencies.
Step 3: go to Grimling caves in Ten. Mountains. Be careful of adds. It's a bit of a run out there, and you have to pay attention on your way there (you run by some grimling villages in the Grimling forest and some sonic wolves in Ten Mnts.--you can handle one or two sonic wolves, but three would be nasty).
You'll loot acrylia, pp, some other stuff. Don't bother with the picks--they don't sell well and are heavy.
If you see the grimling runner, kill him. He drops the pattern for the rallic pack. I see you have one already, but you can have a pack and a pattern at the same time.
You can get 200-600 pp per session doing this. Don't try it if you're KoS in Shar Val.
One of my characters is a 52 warrior and this works for him.

Provided you have a way to leave a bazaar trading running, farming regular spiders silks in the karana's is totally the best bang for your buck. It's a nasty hike out there, but you could befriend a druid, or take the nexus scion out there. You shouldn't need to worry about dieing at level 52, either. I forget which karana has the crag spiders, but it's the one with the border to hhk. The crag spiders spawn on both sides of the path to hhk, right before it enteres the mountains. Kill them, they drop up to 8 silks. On my server, each silk is worth about 10pp. Combine them into swatches and sell for 25-29pp. Clear it twice and you've easily made 500+ pp.

PoJ basement is good for gems too, but you'll need a solid full group to hold it down at 52. I cleared it 4-5x last night with a group of low 50's friends and ended up with a 950pp split.
