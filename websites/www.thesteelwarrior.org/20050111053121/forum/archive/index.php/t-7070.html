Clear the room and the corner your going to kill in should either hold the cats mezzed or kill them last. Respawn in 15 minutes. Group with a bard and cap out MR to resist his proc. Get bulwark of vie / alendor to help mitigation melee and proc damage and it doesnt hurt to have someone Spellshield you if this is your first time going in.

Other than that, just melee kill him down and have a CH rot. At like 40% he starts chain casting so his melee dps goes way down but dont let the healers stop casting because he will soon spike right back into normal attack mode.

When he dies the PP will spawn in middle of the room, just send out a kiter to kite the cats on opposite side of the room from you, and get flags and loot then AE tl out!

05-11-2004, 02:05 PM
In response to bards AE-kiting huge numbers of mobs in HOH, a special anti-kiting feature was added to HOH. I believe it works like this: whenever you have 9 mobs pursuing the same target, and a 10th mob wants to join the pursuit, the 10th mob will gate to bind point and wipe its hate list instead. Thus you can never have more than 9 mobs attacking a single target in HOH.

As a side effect, this anti-kiting feature was also implemented in HOHB where Mithaniel Marr lives.

As a side effect, the anti-kiting feature will activate whenever you have more than 9 PETS (of any type) attacking Mithaniel Marr.

So your MM fight may be going just fine with a couple of charmed pets, and then someone decides to use their PET SWARM AA for example, which raises the number of pets beyond 9, and suddenly the charmed pets gate to bind point and wipe their hate lists, and everyone says "WTF?"

The charmed pets then die at bind point to the mobs around them, and/or sit at bind point until charm wears off, and then you get a big fat train of mobs which arrive just when you thought Mithaniel Marr was about to take a dirt nap.

(Edit - corrected numbers based on Besrikarle's comment)

Hehe, you don't need charm pets to down MM though. The wrulons are so freaking MR resistant that it's hard enough to mez them (and keep them mezzed), let alone try charming. Are they good dps? Shit yeah. Are they needed? No.

LMM does have a shitstorm of hitpoints. He's got around 1.2-1.3 million I believe, so it can take a while to down his skinny arse. He also has a *GIGANTIC* agro range, which leads me to say the following.

Clear his room while the raid is outside of his chambers. You can hide your raid in the corners, but it all it takes is one person not paying attention to be out too far and agro LMM. If that happens, you have himself + 2x quad-1100 hitting guards storming your raid. It's not fucking pretty. Simply put, clear his room and his guards while the raid is just outside his chambers. That way no fucktard can agro LMM. Once his guards are clear, move in to his chambers, either to the left or right, and pull LMM single. If you're lacking dps, then you may get a wrulon add during the fight, but you can easily kill it and move back to LMM when you're done.
