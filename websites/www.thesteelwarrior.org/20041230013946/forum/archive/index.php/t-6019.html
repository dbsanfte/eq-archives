FulorianC
03-11-2004, 11:58 AM
Wake The Dead. Necro spell, brings a corpse back to life as a weak pet, but that retains the procs and spells of the mob in question. Can be very powerful.

Edit: To explain in more depth. Imagine a necro pet that hits for 50, has 3k hp, and procs lifetap for 5000.

Now imagine he's helping you kill Raex in the Carprin ring.

That pet happens to be Vindor's corpse! Which is why Vindor is usually killed first and not fully looted. His woken damage output makes Raex, the harder of the two, less the harder of the two.
