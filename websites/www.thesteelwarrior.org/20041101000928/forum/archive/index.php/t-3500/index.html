Taminy
Force Shock, Anarchy, and Sanity Warp (and some other stuns on warrior weapons) do indeed have a small pushback effect.

I've also heard that blunt weapons create more push than slashing weapons which in turn create more push than piercing weapons. I'm not sure if it's true or not really. Part of where it may come from though is monks do seem to generate quite a bit of melee push (usually more than other melees). Monks obviously can only use blunt weapons. However, monks have higher dual wield / double attack skill caps and max them out quicker than any other class (monks max out dual wield at like level 35, hehe).

I'll also include as a note that more attacks definetly causes more push (try a weighted axe then switch to dual wielding sub 20 delay weapons if you need to convince yourself).
