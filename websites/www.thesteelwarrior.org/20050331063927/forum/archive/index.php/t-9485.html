I did him the other night with 4 people + 1 bot. It was me, my cleric bot, a real cleric, a ranger, and a paladin. Basically, the real cleric healed me, my bot healed rampage on the pally and spot-healed on me a couple times, and the ranger trueshotted him. I opened with furious and then used stonewall. He died a few seconds after stonewall wore off. I was probably the best-geared character there, with the others (except for my bot) in a mix of elemental and lower items. Wasn't easy, and I came close to dying quite a few times, but he went down on the first try. I had gone down earlier with just me and the cleric bot to see how hard he was and managed to keep myself alive just chaining CH until stonewall wore off, so if you have a decent tank and enough dps to kill him in under 3 minutes a 1-healer group is definitely viable. Range DPS is important on this fight if you don't have a charmed pet. Too many melees and healing rampage becomes difficult. Rangers and nukers are the best damage dealers for him.
