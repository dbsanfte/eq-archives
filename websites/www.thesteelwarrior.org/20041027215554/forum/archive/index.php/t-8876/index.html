No to ornate. You could get more of an upgrade buying as much Fierce Heraldic armor as you can versus buying one or two ornate molds. Valorium rings if you can find a good deal. Same with Mosquito ears if a good bargain is found.

Wompem Ondahed
As above, most Fierce Heraldic armor is from 4-8k apiece and you would be amazed how your HP will jump, as well as AC. It is solid armor and even has some saves. I still wear the pants and a bracer.

You can probably have a complete set for 50k, and maybe if lucky even buy a couple of Valorium Rings of Gallantry. That will put you around 5k hp I am betting. Your neck is a soild item, if you don't want to fork out the cash for the FH neck.

If you have friends then PLEASE go HUNT for the Copper Hammer of Striking and Sword of the BloodSworn. At your level the mobs will almost fall over for you, and you and your friends can walk out of Droga with anywhere from 2-8 weapons in a 4 hour period. If you have success and a steady group of friends then you guys can farm that spot and fund the rest of your armor upgrades.

Once you and friends are satisfied, go hit up some LDoN's for future upgrades and ok XP/AA.
