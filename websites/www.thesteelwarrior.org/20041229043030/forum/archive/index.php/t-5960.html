This was brought up the other day in the channel and was looking to see if it has been posted here. Didn't really find anything so I thought I would make my own thread.

If you are always looking to increase your hp like me sturdiness looks like a reasonable offer (well its the only new offer). Each level of sturdiness gives 100hp at a cost of 4 8 12 16 20. Planar Power lets you get your STA up 5 more points each level at a cost of 2 each time. Its already been stated that 1 STA = 6hp.

For some context when GoD came out I did not have PP or any AA saved up for sturdiness. So I'm trying to decide which to get first to increase my HP. Obviously sturdiness 1 and 2 are the best rewards for the cost. But then when you get to sturdiness 2 you have to think about it. 12AA for 100hp compared to... Well given the formula above 25STA would be 150hp. All that for only 10AA. It seems a better deal than sturdiness 3 right?

Well I now have PP5. Its not 150hp. Before someone mentions it yes my STA is 305 without buffs. PP1 gives me about 21hp. PP2 gave me like 19hp. Then 23 and so on. It rounded out to just a little over 100hp. After 280 do you start getting less hp per sta? This I wasn't aware of.

But the verdict is if you are exping slowly and want the best bang for your buck while you are doing it go for sturdiness 2 then PP5 then back to sturdiness.
