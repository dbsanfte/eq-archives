01-06-2005, 04:35 AM
GoD as an expansion for the "casual" EverQuest gamer was a total crock of shit. GoD as an expansion for the "hardcore" EverQuest gamer was somewhat of a masterpiece.

The problem with GoD is that the money, the real meat of the expansion, is hidden away behind locked doors which only the raider can unlock. Kod`Taz, Yxxta, and Ikkinz are chock full of fantastic upgrades that a single group would really go nuts over, even if some of the pieces a bit on the rare side. Exp is fantastic, named are plenty, and the reward is well worthwhile. The catch? Unless you're a well-elemental equipped tank, your chances of succeeding the trials to gain access to these zones is virtually nill. Tipt and Vxed trash mobs are no fucking joke. Kyv Heartstriker Jhiru is a mack truck on steroids. Sewer trials are just an ill-conceived royal pain in the ass. No wonder the casual EverQuest gamers are flocking to World of Warcraft.

But with OoW, there's hope. Honestly, OoW made GoD much, much, much more enjoyable for me. I'm always game and eager for challenging content, but there comes a point where you have to draw the line. At level 65 with PoP/GoD AAs and over 10k+/2300ac buffed, I was getting fucking plastered by single group Ikkinz trash mobs. A lot of this had to do with the fact that there are shamans aplenty inside Ikkinz, and they haste the fuck out of everything. Dispelling solved this problem, but all it took was one person not paying attention and a 2000dps quadding Kyv would turn your "fun" into a one hour CR party. OoW adding a lot more padding for simple, should-be-recoverable mistakes with the increased skillups and defensive AAs. Those hasted Kyvs are still no joke, but I now at least have a freaking chance to reach over and hit my cleric's divine arbitration key. Seriously, they were fucking overboard. I bet this very same thing has a lot to do with the fact that even Riftseeker mobs don't come incomming hasted. You'll never see a shaman/enchanter mob haste an ally inside RS. That would drive people insane...

With OoW defensive AAs, quested armor, and improved weaponry, you should have the capabilities of winning these Tipt/Vxed trials. While the MPG trials do yield more shwaggin' stuff for your time, it's not always necessarily in the same slots. Glyphed Prayer Rings, Resonating Earrings of Battle, Carved Vrex Totems, Crafted Wristbands of Strife, and various other P3/P4 quality armors out of Ikkinz would never be passed up by a casual player. Add in the (3) 20ac augs you can get between KT/Yxxta and you might realize that gaining access to these zones might very well be worth your time.

GoD is actually a very good expansion. Too bad it was aimed a wee bit too high though. ;)

Btw, Weapon Affinity and Sturdiness are easily the best things out of this expansion, bar none.
