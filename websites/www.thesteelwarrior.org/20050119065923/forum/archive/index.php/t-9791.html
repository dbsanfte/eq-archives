BP is always a shitty slot to upgrade. I'm still wearing Kizrak's and there aren't many upgrades available to me.

If you guys have anyone Seru keyed, he's pretty easy these days and drops a nice BP. Otherise Dragorn Heirloom or Charged Magnetic will be your best bets, as spendy as they may be.

Like I said BP is a real tough slot to upgrade so don't feel bad about having a weaker BP slot and spending your time/money on bigger upgrades in different slots.

You have some other slots that are much easier and cheaper to upgrade then your BP.

examples:
Finger: Steel Ring of earthen resilience
Ear: Fiercely hooked claw, Tear of the rainmaker, etc
Range: Elemental bow ( i use stonewood) gives you +5AC, +45HP !!
Waist: Heavy Rubicite Girdle (tribute haste is cheap and more haste then you currently have +11AC, +20HP)

I would upgrade first what gives the most bang for the buck. If you want to upgrade your Breastplate it will definately be very expensive. Livestone and Farwater Breastplate would be other options here.

I did it with about 11 ppl (no bane weapons), took awhile but worked fine.

I don't mean to cause offense, but if you think you can't do something, and don't try, it'll become further and further out of your reach, whereas if you keep pushing to raid something -- anything -- you'll gradually get into some sort of groove. Even if you just have a few folks on, cobble something together and just go do it. IMO if you just let ppl xp day after day or play alts, even casual-type raiders will lose their spark and move on, or quit. If ppl logon and see ppl making an active attempt to go raid something, it really can boost morale.

That being said, I've done Seru with about 10 ppl. Been awhile but it was something like 3 clerics, chanter, shaman, mage, bard, paladin, druid, warrior. Bard helped a lot because he provided a large part of the DS which was our main DPS hehe.

Other options for ya might be Ornate (clicky!) and there's one that drops in RSS I think? Celestial Lorica or something.

Good luck.
