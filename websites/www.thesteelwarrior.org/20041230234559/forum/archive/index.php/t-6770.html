Ambidexterity is a really good DPS boost, if you dual wield.

A LOT of people dual wield, due to hate procs like the droga weapons at the not-so-high-end game or Anger 3 augments at any stage of the game.

Which makes Ambi even more useful an AA than before.

Ferocity is a small DPS increase. I believe it was Haas who has quite a bit to say about Ferocity. Heh.

Other DPS AAs a warrior should not overlook:

- Sinister Strikes. Expensive. But it does its job. Again, only good if you dual wield. Again, dual wielding is quite the rage these days because FOUR hate procs are better than 2.
- Tactical Mastery. This really helps warriors since, you know, you're the one standing in front of the mob the most.
- Doulbe Riposte. This also really helps warriors since, again, you're the ones standing in front of the mob and able to riposte it.
- Flash of Steel. See Double Riposte.
- Combat Fury/Fury of the Ages/Flurry/Raging Flurry. All small DPS increases, but when you get all of them together finally, it's more noticable.

So yeah, Ferocity is ok. Punishing Blade seems to still be popular, though I can never figure out why. The same way Ambi is frowned upon because it only helps dual wielders ... Punishing Blade should be frowned upon because it only helps you with a 2hander.
