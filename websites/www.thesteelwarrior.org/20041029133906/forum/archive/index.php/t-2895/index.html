there's been a couple threads about this sorta thing before... best advice i can give you is as follows:

1 - try to get regen item
2 - try to get some good procs (slow, snare, etc)
3 - have run3, jboots, or sow

a friend of mine used to "fungi kite" I think he called it. He's equip a really hard hitting but slow weapon, get close to the mob, attack it, turn off attack and keep running, then repeat. Basically attack the mob whenever your weapon cycles but stay out of range regening with fungi otherwise.

I heard that some warriors with proper gear could solo in PoJ some, but I never tried.
