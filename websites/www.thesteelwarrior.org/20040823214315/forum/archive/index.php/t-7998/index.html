Loral
I spoke to both Brenlo and other developers about this. There will be no new LDON gear until after Omens of War. The ease with which most people are finishing normal adventures doesn't make the risk worth the reward even if it does take a long time. While I would like to see either a point-based system for LDON hard adventures, or the sewer / mountain trials in GoD, it doesn't appear we will see any point system in either old-world, GoD, or Omens of War.

It's a mistake if you ask me. Point based systems are a great improvement to loot in Everquest and it is a shame SOE doesn't capitalize on it more.
