This is not a slight to shamans. Shamans rock and naturally they are outstanding to duo with. I just think you are discounting the power and utility of clerics.

Pacification is powerful and clerics get it early. Pacification means manageable pulls everywhere. It means unexpected roamers don't aggro you. It means you can fight next to a dangeorus mob if you want to. I can't count the number of times that I've made invisibility irrelevant thanks to pacification. I used to do some neat tricks by combining Pacification with Atone in the old days, but modern zone design doesn't really need it.

Clerics have some of the best standing regen (admittedly second to shamans with their Cann5). Their DS has no real drawbacks when you are fighting one mob at a time. They have a variety of tools to prevent death when things start going badly, and death means little with a cleric anyway.
