Rumblingdeth
Kick stuns post level 55, slam stuns at any level. I have always played large race warriors and don't use kick, it does nothing that slam doesn't. Slam also caps higher in skill at high levels, and for those first 54 levels you can disrupt casters with a well timed slam while they can not.

Keep up both. Remember that slam caps out higher than kick, so whilst in the early days you will see more return with kick, slam tends to work out better later on. however as someone pointed out, when your in boon or any other form, slam wont work so you have to resort back to kick.
