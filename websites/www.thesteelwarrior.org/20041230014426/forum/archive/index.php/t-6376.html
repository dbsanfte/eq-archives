You get 2 points of stamina for every level of the AA you buy. The hps you get from stamina is determined by level but I assure you it is not 6x level per stamina.

Basically you get 6hps per stamina if you are level 60-65 (less if a lower level) up to 255 stamina. From 255-305 stamina you get around 4hps per stamina.
