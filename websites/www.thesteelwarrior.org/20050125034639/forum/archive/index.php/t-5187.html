First, thanks for posting this; my warrior is basically in the same gear position as yours, and I'm broke too.
Some short-term AC "bump" ideas you *might* be able to afford:
You might be able to find a Chitinous belt (AC 13, STR 5, STA 5, WIS 5, SV disease 10) if you kept the straw belt and put haste in your hands. Could watch for glimmering mithril torque for neck slot (AC 15, STR 2)--not a final solution, but a bump in AC.
Also the large LDoN visors are showing up in the bazaar cheap on my server anyway, and would be a slight bump for that slot if you don't want to wait for one to drop.
Cobalt boots (AC 25, clicky STR) drop from Caller of Sathir in Karnor's in courtyard 1 (well, okay, sometimes they drop, sigh), so if you wanted an LDoN break you could try that for a lootable boot upgrade.
