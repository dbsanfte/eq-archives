Your confusing what these skills do.

Ferocity makes your main hand double attack more often.

For instance without ferocity you double attack lets say 60% of the time. With ferocity you now double attack 70% of the time. (those numbers are made up to illustrate what it does).

Punishing blade adds the chance at an extra attack per round. For instance on every round of attacks with a 2h wep, you get a 10% chance at scoring an addtional attack.
So without punishing blade, lets say you:
Single attack 25%
Double attack 70%
triple attack 5%

after punishing blade
Single attack 15%
Double attack 72.5% (70% + additional 10% of single attacks which get changes to double attacks)
Triple attack 12% (5% + additional 10% of double attacks which get changed to triple attacks)
Quad attack 0.5% (0% + additional 10% of triple attacks which get changed to quad attacks)

The numbers are just made up, not accurate, just to show you how each of these work. They do stack, they do appear to feed off each other and they also seem to feed into flurry which only goes off on triple attack or better rounds.
