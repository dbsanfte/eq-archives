* Enrage - Increased the number of charges to 12. Decreased the duration to 12 seconds. The new duration is the limiting factor for slower weapons (e.g. Arcanite Reaper will typically get one less swing), while dual wielding and faster weapons will make better use of all of the charges over the duration of the ability.


I do not see this as a total nurf to slow 2H weapons but even a buff in certain situations. Yes, if you base it off auto attack then it is a buff. However, if enrage fires, it means you are being attacked. Being attacked = rage. Rage = being able to use your instant attacks. In PvP you take damage so much faster rage can build quickly. That means you could at least get off 1 if not 2 instant strikes in 12 seconds. In certain situations, maybe 3. Having the extra charges means that even with a 2H weapon, you could possibly get off 5 attacks, 6 if your really lucky. You should at the very least get 3 off, 2 auto attacks and 1 Mortal Strike. If overpower lights up, that is another, and if you got the rage you can do WW too. If the stars align right, you can get 3 auto attacks and an overpoower, 2 MS's, or 1 MS and a WW for 6 enraged attacks. Not likely, but possible. When fighting mobs, you could save your rage for enrage as it almost always does to unload your MS, WW, and then MS again. It does hurt some, but it can work in your advantage some times. Anyone see a problem with this reasoning?
