Jaqyl
Get your warrior to 60 and you can upgrade his face and arms very easily.

The Sludge-Eating Fly (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14230) in the GoD instanced zone Sewers of Nihilia - Lair of Trapped Ones, can drop Stonemite Carapace Mask (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68498) and Pitted Vambraces (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68492), and can spawn within the very first room of the zone, very easy to farm and will drop spells for your other toons as well. Need to be 60 to zone in though, and you'll probably need around that level for your warrior to tank this, though with the slows and all the healing your other toons provide he should be fine once he's 60.

GoD really presents alot of good upgrade options for skilled single groups. If you can get a chanter's help Vxed is a great source of gear, the easiest mob in the zone to pull and kill Chavoor the Trickster (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14588) drops very nice legs and earring for minimal effort, though you'd probably want your warrior to be 65 with most of his hp AA's for Vxed.
