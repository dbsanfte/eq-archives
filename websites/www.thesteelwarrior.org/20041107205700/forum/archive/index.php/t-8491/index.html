Arathena
The plane of War, AFAIK, was to be directed by a very large and complex script, not entirely dissimilar to the Hollowshade Moor war. The zone was scrapped in the latter portions of PoP testing as they could not make the scripts function properly, and the Vallon/Tallon wings were added to Tactics, which was originally concieved to be much like HOH: It would provide a backdrop for the RZ fight much more than anything else.
Sassinak

Arathena
Because the entirety of the zone design was built to support the script: Rebuilding PoWar would have been on an even higher level of effort than the Droga/Nurga revamps, as old pathing and AI would need to be scrapped and rebuilt along with mob population, reitemization, so on and so forth. It's likely that the script was abandoned merely days before the shipping of PoP, with Vallon and Tallon replacing other minis in Drunder, and the MB flag simply being reassociated. After shipment, of course, no one ever found any round tuits, what with the snarls that were early PoP flags.
