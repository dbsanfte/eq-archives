ThornyEQ
There is really nothing for you personally in Velious or Kunark, except perhaps some of the no-drop gear that nameds in NToV drop and some of the gear that AoW and Tormax drop, and you're quite a few levels away from taking those on right now. You won't be replacing your Fierce Heraldic until you hit PoP and start looting ornate molds (and even then, just a few slots will be upgrades).

Right now, I'd really only consider replacing your rings with valorium rings of gallantry, and replacing your bow with an elemental bow. Perhaps someone in your guild is enough of a craftsperson to make these for you, otherwise I'd save some plat and buy them in the bazaar.

For a reasonable upgrade in haste (at the cost of some HP's and AC), you might consider getting a Soulscream Belt (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=65570) from Veksar. It's a good camp for a mid-50's group. If you have the faction, replace your Silver Chitin Handwraps with Gauntlets of the Myrmidon (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=31186) from ToV/HoT (these can be found for MQ quite often). If you hunt in Kael Arena, you'll have dragon faction in no time. With both items replaced out, you'll come out ahead in both HP's and AC as well.
