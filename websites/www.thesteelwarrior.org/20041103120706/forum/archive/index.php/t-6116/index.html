Whoppa
Not sure if you are VT enabled, but there are a number of upgrades you could get in VT. Since you are on Firiona Vie you could buy VT drops as well (not sure on the market for them). If you are VT enabled, there are 4 great drops from Aten that you should set your sights on. You'll want Great Shadow Platemail, Crimson Runed Mask, Earring of Eradication and Soul Essence of Aten Ha Ra (turn in for Talisman of Vah Kerrath). Those 4 items will last you until Time. There are a number of other VT items that would be upgrades for you, but you should concentrate on jewelry/non-visible slots. Belt of Mastery from Aten Blobs is a great item you should look at picking up. The rest is up to you, there is a horde of rings/earrings you could grab.

Once you enter elementals, obviously you will be after Raex. Since your Haste is in a Raex's slot, I would recommend trying to get the Earth Bracer (Bracers of Earthen Energy) as it is not much worse than Raex's or going after the Flowing Moss Mantle which also drops in Earth. There is also a Haste ring from PoA (Band of Swirling Air), but it has low AC, and there are definite better options for rings. The Bracer is probably your best choice as the WAR-only Puresteel Mantle for shoulders is a great item to have and easy enough to get.

As for Weapons, Stone Etched Warsword is a nice DPS weapon, and can be a great aggro weapon with Anger 3 Aug'd. An obvious choice would be Blade of War but if your not MTing I wouldn't bother.

All in all though, your current gear is fine for pre-elemental. I was in mostly Cultural with spatterings of other stuff (very similar to yours) when I entered elementals and I survived and even tanked things. AAs will be huge in upping your tankability too, so look into maxing those out ASAP.
