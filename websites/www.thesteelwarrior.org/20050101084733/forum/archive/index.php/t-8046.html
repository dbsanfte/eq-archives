All disc use endurance, same for some skills created after the refund system of disc. And jumping still consume endurence I think but don't remember exactly.

As a warrior you should look at the Warrior Discipline and abandoned opening skills list (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spelllist.html?classes=WAR&source=Live).
So this means that when you incite or throw a rock :) you use endurance. and when you use a disc you consume endurance and the respective timer (we have four timers now, for a better explanation look at the library : Warrior disciplines and the Incite line ).
