WA doesn't affect directly the thrid proc ... actually increasing the ppms decrease the ird proc ppm. look at parses on Pal boards:
WA0 CE0 -> 2ppm +2ppm +1.9ppm
WA5 CE0 -> 3ppm +2.9ppm +1.5ppm
WA doesn't increase third proc ppm, does it shouldn't affect it. However due to proc mecanics (you can proc only once per swing, you can proc only once each proc per round, you can't proc on ripost and flurry, proc check is ordered innate>aug>spell) when the ppm of the first proc increases it gives less occasion for other procs to fire, same for the aug proc decreasing the spell proc.
Indeed, when you proc from the weapon and the aug in a round, you need at lest to get a triple (or a puni with 2Her) to give a chance at the third proc to fire. decreasing the overall ppm of the third proc. that's why WA does affect indirectly the third proc.
But a non procing weapon, without aug but with spell proc will have a 2ppm with or without WA5. I guessed it was alike with CE but prefered to check.
