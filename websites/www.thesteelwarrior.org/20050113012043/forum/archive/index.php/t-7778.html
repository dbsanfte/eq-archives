I agree with others that a ToD and a 15 regen chest slot item are key. I wear the fungi while fighting and find that I can fight more mobs between bandaging breaks. Having a max'd bandaging skill is very handy. I even went ahead and spent three aa's on first aid and never regreted it. With max bandaging skill and a +10 skill mod item I am able to heal 92 hp per attempt. You get 5, almost 6, attempts per minute. Way down the road I will spend more AA's on improving my bandaging skill and should eventually hit 150+ hp healed per attampt. Limited charge clicky snare items are handy for spliting mobs (assumes you are camping a zone line). Rune procing weapons are very good for soloing as well. Crunch the numbers and you will see some surprising mitigation. The mines in the Grimling Forest, the Deep and now Griegs have been favorite solo spots for me as I have leveled. Breaking the spawn at the zone line in Griegs is a little tricky but once done your set.
