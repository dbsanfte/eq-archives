Sakryn,

Echo of Nortlav is 6boxable, no problem. He is not always a pussy cat, but more or less is doable.

I recommend, pull with incite, hit furious then evasive. Can do mighty strike before evasive if ya want, but you will take some hits, though not too bad.

I'e done him with 65 clr/dru/enc/mag/war/nec. The fight can leave my toons with 40-60% mana or if it goes really really bad but I hold it together, I've gone oom on all of them, that includes 1 mana rod useage.

Stats:
Echo of Nortlav
This mob [Lifetaps] [Summons] [Casts Spells]
Max Hit 225
Ave Hit 130
DPS 140
Max Spell 1500
Ave Spell 1323
2 min 15 sec to kill
Total HP 36654
I've spanked this mob 19 times out of 26 encounters

I have seen him proc 4 times in 2 minutes and 22 times in 2 minutes, he really varies that much. Having rune up is the lifesaver, as without it, he procs quite a bit more often.

Also, keep up mem blur on your enc, as if the MT dies, just drop pets and mem blur him and he will stop killing people. You can then rez and redo him easily.
