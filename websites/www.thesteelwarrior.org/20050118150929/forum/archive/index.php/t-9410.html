A. Make it a self only rune. A big one if clicky, a small one if a proc.

B. Leave it as a small % reduced for the whole group, but increase the total absorbed and increase the duration to 10 minutes.

(neither of the above really appeal to me, but are the smallest change from the current clicky)

C. Ditch the vie type effect, leave the self shielding buff, but make it avoidance and all shielding types, stacking with worn effects (if it isn't already stacking).

D. Self proc buff, either massive damage (i.e. 1000 DD proc, unresistable), aggro, stun, debuff, snare, mez, whatever. Something fun here. Defensive proc or normal, maybe both? Would have to be high frequency though, especially if only up for one minute.
