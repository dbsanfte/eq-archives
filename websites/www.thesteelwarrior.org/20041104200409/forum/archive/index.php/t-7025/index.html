Cuthbadth
Just get one of the cheap weapon procs from 50 - 150 points since it looks like you'll upgrade soon and spend the ldon points on either some armor or hp / ac augments.

Yes, EF armor set is probably the best bang for the buck warrior armor set, since NR armor is 1492. It's all a question of how soon would you max out your armor with EF armor and be stuck looking for upgrades and wishing you bought the NR armor instead. Personally, either armor set looks good to me, but I'm working on the NR BP because I don't think that is something I would get an upgrade for anytime soon and I want to get the best HP/AC/Stat combo I can for that slot. If I'm going to get a 40 hp augment for it later, I'd feel much better having gotten the 100 hp BP than the 75 hp BP.

So, as long as the EF armor has 3 augment slots and you've got points there and don't see yourself getting any ornate or GoD or hard ldon drops soon, go for it.
