You need:

1. Tip of a Snapped Blade (from Mastered Destroyer)
2. Broken Blade Hilt (from Taskmistress Krisz)

One you have the item from Mastered Destroyer, speak to Ranguali in Riwwi. He will respond to that item being in your possession (much like the NPCs for the Cragbeast Queen react for having her drops in your possession).

There are 4 different rewards from Taskmistress drops. This particular reward is a piercer:
