Daabu
Really, I've always used the 1ac = 5hp until you reach Elementals. You don't want to completely ignore AC...I know a warrior who went heavy HP and his AC was looking pretty bad. Another warrior in my guild went heavy AC, although his HP was kinda low. When we were in the EPs, guess who had a higher survivability while MA'ing?

The AC guy.

Now, you're not in the EPs, so it isnt as relevant, but the point is still the same: don't ignore AC.

If you're looking at 2ac vs 20hp, go for the 20hp. If you're looking at 2ac vs 10hp, go for whatever's cheapest. If you're looking at 3ac vs 10hp, go for the extra AC hands down.

As for dex, so long as you're maxed with buffs, don't worry about it. 100dex = 0.5ppm, so 8 dex in and of itself really isnt a big difference.
