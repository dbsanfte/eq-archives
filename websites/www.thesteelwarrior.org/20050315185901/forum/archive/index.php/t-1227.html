07-01-2003, 10:04 AM
Personally, I loved Torment. It was quite possibly one of the funnest zones I've fought in. Lots of surprises around every corner. Like the mobs that cast a quick cast snare. That makes pulls interesting. The birds out front are easy to break. Just arrow pull the one on the right farthest from the bridge, then the one on the left, followed by the last one on the right. Pulling is made easier if you have a monk; lots of steep inclines make pulling with a bow impossible (mostly).

The mobs don't hit too overly hard and some can drop spectrals. XP was about the same as Valor or Storms, I thought. CoD did have better XP, though.

That's my info on Torment. If ya need more, just post here. :)



P.S. - If you have an enchanter with you that has DC. Kill the birds out front over and over again. A mob called "a small blood raven" will spawn. It's DC'able, always. :D
