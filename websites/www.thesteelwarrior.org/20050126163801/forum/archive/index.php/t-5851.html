Chaddu,

Take a look at a WaveCrasher, they should be fairly cheap these days. It has a 20% slow and 20hp a tick DoT on it and is decent for aggro.

A CHoS is definately a good aggro weapon and dont' forget its brother Sword of the Bloodsworn or the 2hander GreatSword of the Bloodsworn.

Also, the Greatstaff of Thunder is an excellent aggro weapon with its DD + Stun proc on it and that item's price has dropped quite a bit recently, at least on my server. Though a 2hander really doesn't shine until you get your Punishing Blade AAs or at least break 60 for the increase in attacks.

Its odd you are asking about Anger3 augs when you're 48 and cannot afford a CHoS, hehe, as I doubt you'd want to spend the time getting the aug to put it on a subpar weapon. I'd think getting an anger1 or 2 aug and putting it on a CHoS would be outstanding bang for the buck, or perhaps hitting the Guk themes to get the Ykesha aug, which would be outstanding for a 48 warrior on a CHoS or GSoT or any weapon really.

IMHO, the only true aggro augs are the Anger (duh) and Ykesha. Some say the Guard augs (I have no experience on them) are as well. The anger only drops in Everfrost theme, to my knowledge. Ykesha from Guk (s.ro) theme and Guards from Tak (n.ro) theme.
