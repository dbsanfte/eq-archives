Neksnappa's suggestion seems reasonable to me, but yeah you'll be in droga territory soon enough I'm not sure I'd worry about it, as your 46 weapons are pretty darn good.
In fact, you'll get more milage now simply paying very close attention to when, how and to whom you lose aggro. In my 40s (and I didn't have nearly as good gear as you do) I used to track such things: by melee/caster, by which classes I lost aggro to, by what part of the fight (early? late?), by mob type, by what I'd done to increase my aggro up to that point, and what if anything I might be failing to do. I'd try to assess whether I was losing aggro to someone because he was not playing his class very well/intellegently, or whether the basic problem was that the class was having to take unusual steps to throttle back on dps/spells to let me keep aggro or whether I was failing to do everything I could to acquire and hold aggro.
Also, ponder your Dex--it's reading something like 130 pre-lvl 50, right? With 130 dex you won't exactly proc like a .50 calibre machine gun. As you level, keep an eye out for dex improvements; they'll never be on the same level of importance as AC/hps, but after you crack 200 dex your procing weps will get better.
(Now it occurs to me you know all this, but just in case...)
