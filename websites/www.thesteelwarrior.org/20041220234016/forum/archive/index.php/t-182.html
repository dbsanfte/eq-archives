Its relative....
For non PoP HP = King

As for PoP
A few parses have showed that +100ac is 2% less dps taken.
(Check the threads, numbers arent totally exact but they seem ok)
Thus 100ac will give you 2% more hp (since you'll take 2% less dmg)

This is a very blunt formula :

1200ac 6000hp +100ac = 2% more hp = 120hp virtually gained
So 1300 6000 = 1200 6120

1400ac 8000hp +100ac = 2% more hp = 160hp virtually gained
So 1500 8000 = 1400 8160

For me atm :
1800ac 9600hp +100ac = 2% more hp = 192 hp virtually gained

After some point it is insanely hard to raise your HP.
Raising ac is still relatively easy and will give you more benefit.
