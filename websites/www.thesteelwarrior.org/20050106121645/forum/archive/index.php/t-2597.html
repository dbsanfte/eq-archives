In my opinion, if you are MA/SA, then hp's is far more important than saves. Damage is damage, and you are going to have a healer watching you full time, so worry about hp's first. If you are not MA/SA, then get those saves up(runed bone fork + that crown with the saves whose names escapes me this early in the morning are great for HoT). Always remember when not tanking, if your hp's start getting low, back out till a heal lands. First few times in HoT are a blast, and you will learn a ton about raiding, have fun.

Once you hit 60 , you resist a lot of AoE in HoT. I'd recommend MA get to 60 asap.
The lower people get the more the AE eats them alive, especially if there are level 55s.
Usually 100 Cold, fire, magic resist is good goal .

A few other comments: HoT is much harder without a good bard to sing resist song. Also, the gating mobs will wipe you if you don't have someone (a couple high enchanters) to seive them .

You can get fooled by all the people who talk about doing HoT with 4 people...yes , but not when they are first trying.
