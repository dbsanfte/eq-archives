Superchum
Contrary to what the whiners may have you believe if you read the CoH boards ... Hasten is STILL an amazing power pool power.

If you're a tanker, then Provoke is excessively overpowered.

Stealth is great. A lot of people use it. I finally got it the other day and loved it, and am thinking of getting it on my scrapper, dipping into a third power pool for it.

Stamina is great for a lot of different builds. It helps a lot. But requires high level, lots of slotting and good SO enhancements to be as effective as everyone likes to pretend it is when they get it. In other words, stamina requires a committment.

Teleport Friend, Teleport Fore, ALL of the leadership buffs are all quite popular.

Arial Superiority? I've no clue about. I guess it does add another melee attack to your repetoire. But not much in this game flies at the low levels.

Combat Jumping is popular, due to a defense bonus.

Some pool powers I think that get overlooked are:

1- Tough
2- Weave

Defense boosts available to classes that can't actually get them, are something a lot of people are overlooking.

3- Swift
4- Hurdle

The fitness line is really popular, but many people act like these powers are wasted, and only get them to open up stamina. When actually they're both quite effective.

5- A pet peeve of mine. FLURRY. I love this power. Not many people like it, due to low damage. I, however, look at it as a time filler that does more damage than brawl. So I really like it and how it can help fill out a melee character's offensive arsenal.
