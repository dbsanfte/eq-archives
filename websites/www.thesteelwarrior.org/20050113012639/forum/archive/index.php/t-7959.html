07-09-2004, 05:21 AM
An anger3 BoW is hard to dismiss even at the Fleshbiter/EoE stage frankly. It is much like any big 2-hander augmented (eg VDD etc) in non-raid fights, it gets nice snap aggro from damage (or damage potential if you prefer) and accrues decent aggro over the course of a fight. Naturally the DW setup will gain over the first-proc onwards stage and will actually catch up even without procs fairly quickly I imagine.

Still, I wouldn't ever consider a BoW a bad aggro weapon, especially for single-group fights. In environments that require severe aggro (such as exp content that requires chain debuffs to maintain efficiency) then the DW setup shines more but the 2handers still have a good following for a reason.

Anyhow, if you've got a BoW, I wouldn't trade it in for anything else until PoT. Even then I'd be thinking other slots until you saw a DBotW to go for. As a Rogue I am always happy with a BoW tank I can tell you.
