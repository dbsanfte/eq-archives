Cuthbadth
Assuming youre not getting 1 rounded by the stuff you tank or your healers cannot be efficient due to you having low hps ... ID is a much better buy. Period.

My recent experience is that HP is great, but being able to miss an entire hit that is for 1k of damage or mitigating it down to less than 500 HP is going to make things a lot easier on your healer than an extra 100 HP. Also, Sturdiness grows costly quickly, 4, 8, 12, etc.

I should add that I do not have Sturd 1 as of yet, but recently went through a similar conundrum with regards to AA progression.

http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=8122

Obviously for much of the old world content, you won't need any more AA. However, don't underestimate GoD sewer mobs or Hard LdoN's.
