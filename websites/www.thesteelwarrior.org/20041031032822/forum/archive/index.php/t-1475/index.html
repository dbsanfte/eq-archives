Personally I would use mallet mainhand and scimitar offhand for any situation and adjust accordingly. The Ykesha proc "should" aggro more than frostcall proc but the mallet will far outdamage the scimitar so its may aggro better from a DPS standpoint. If you find aggro difficult, switch them around.

I would work towards a Frostbringer and pair it with the mallet long term. Should be good aggro and decent DPS. If you aren't tanking, I would pick up a 2 hander that is close to 1:1 ratio for DPS.

It is difficult to tell without more info like what level you are, what you are fighting, etc. Keep in mind that I usually hunt with friends for exp and we rarely have the ideal group so my damage does count and I hate gimping my damage output badly just to hold aggro. I realize though that sometimes it is necessary.

Atm, we are farming UP with a 59 cleric, 63 druid, 63 warrior, and filling in the group with our friends who are lvl 45. We all get exp (although no where near PoP) and we are outfitting them with gear. In this scenario, the druid and myself are doing the majority of the damage.
