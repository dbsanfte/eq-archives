Start off with dark blue nocs in Natimbi. You should find them to be trivial, and medium XP. Then zone into Qinimi and try mobs there. I suggest pulling to the Ferubi zone line but you can try any part of the zone. Qinimi mobs are somewhat like normal LDON these days, in both difficulty and XP, and you should find them pretty easy too, perhaps boring. When you want to try something harder, invis and run from there to Riwwi. Try mobs at the zone line inside Riwwi first, then head into the open areas, and then into the arena. You will probably find your comfort level somewhere in Riwwi. If it's still too easy then move on to Barindu. The mobs in Barindu will not be trivial, but you still might have faced harder mobs in POP or hard LDON with your group in the past.
