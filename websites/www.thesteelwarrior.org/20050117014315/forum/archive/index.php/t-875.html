alright, no one made a responce so I will try to give this a shot. First, keep reading this forum and you will pick up lots of ideas about ac/attack/strength/weapons skil etc.

Anyway. max damage is based on level and strength(I think). Your level wil cap your damage the most. Next, the (2*damage + bonus)/delay/100 is a rough approximate I use for estimating which weapon can do the most dps.

Finally, attack rating will determine your average dps... that and weapons skill and level. Now, your weapons skill directly relates to your attack rating. There are other contributing factors such as the mobs ac and your strength(it raises attack rating) and buffs.

So, what benefit does the bonus give? Simply, its damage added on to every hit.

now, 2handers have a much larger bonus and the 2handers available at the high end have very nice dps.
