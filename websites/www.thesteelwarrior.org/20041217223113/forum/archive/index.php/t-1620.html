I see a few things you guys need to do to greatly increase your power:

1. Level - work hard to get everyone 60+. This will do more for you than any drops you could get in a similar amount of time as it would take to get the levels. I would concentrate on your warriors, 2x 56 warriors are not very good back up for you. Same with the bard, need to get the bard leveled up so you can have the better songs.

2. DPS - you are a little light in the DPS area. 4 rangers are nice if they have EQ+AM3. But you only have 1 monk, 1 rogue, 2x mages, and 2x wizards. I would try to get a few more of these classes.

3. Knights - you only have 1 of each. While they are not necessary, they do have thier use and they are nice to have around. I would try to add a couple more knights.

If you could make these changes, then you could basically take any Velious mob and the majority of Luclin mobs. You could also do quite a few PoP bosses as well.
