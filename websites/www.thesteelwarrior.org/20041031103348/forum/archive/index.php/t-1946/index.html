Choppin
bah. i'm in elementals and i still wear warlords bracer / silver chitin.

my recommendations for VT stuff:

-legs: leggings of fiery might (best there is till elemental greaves), fairly common drop

-back: cloak of icy shadows, or cloak of the black wind (if you want cloak haste).

-arms: cerulan sleeves OR umbral vambraces. i'd get whatever drops first, 35hp loss but 25ac gain on umbral. cerulan are very rare, umbral are not.

-head: helmet of shadow. or if your luck is down or you think your guild is about to leave VT soon, get the first circlet of disguise that drops.

-wrists: umbracite bracer(sp) the 38ac 75hp one, or cloudy mark as you said, and bracer of darkness. 100hp on the latter, but has a focus effect. since you're DKP, screw the casters, not like they're lacking focus items from VT.

-shoulder: shroud of vigor. this is extremely rare, and if you don't want to wait for it, get the spiked shoulderpads.

-rings: ring of the crimson bull, not sure about the second ring (RotCB isn't lore though ;))

-belt: belt of mastery. quite rare, but basically the only useful belt from VT iirc.

-face: crimson runed mask from aten (probably high DKP), or cloudy steel mask which is less rare and probably less DKP.

-range: orb of satisfaction, or orb of evasion. i'm pretty sure the first isn't that rare combared to the evasion one. or you could take an eye of darkness if you're desperate. but since you're about to kill agnarr, i would personally (and I did) wait for longbow of the stormcaller.

-neck: gorget of superiority.

-ears: earring of eradication and earring of battle ;) or earring of vigor as it's probably much less DKP.


I can't really say what will give you the most "bang for the buck" looking at your current gear, but you already got good helmet, ear, gauntlets, belt and BP. so basically get rid of everything else first is my advice.

if you're not going to stay in VT long, it's just a matter of getting the stuff that gives you the most HP/AC upgrade each drop.
