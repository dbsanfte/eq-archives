Best advice was already given. Work on your skills and level up. Max out what you can, look for higher ratio and lower delay weapons. Don't ignore the proc'ing weapons though. Some have so-so stats, but the proc makes up for it in helping to keep the mobs attention.

Your job as a warrior is moving into being able to hold the mob's attention. Take the damage so others can deal the damage.

As I grew, I made sure I had at least one melee skill maxed as soon as I could, then I worked on the others so none would fall behind. It's hard to do on all of them, I did let 2HB lay dormant a bit too long, but I am catching that up now.

When you hit 52 I think it is, you will have a good chance to catch your skills up that you left behind.

In my 40's I used a Centi Shortsword and a Shaded Blade and they did me quite well until I got into my 50's.

For you, now is the time to experiment. Learn what works for what situation and what doesn't. Don't stick to one thing. Adapt, change, try new things and see what works best for you.

There is no set answer when you are a warrior. What works well for one, doesn't always work well for others.
