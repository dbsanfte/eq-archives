Whew, I just leveled from 53 to 57 in 4 days. 4 levels in 4 days. I stayed in Poi (at the entrance no less!) until 55. At 55 I hit PoD and took LCY camp until 56, then the RCY camp for 57.

As for the hole, forget the entrance. The entrance is full of mostly warriors, not crusaders. The crusaders are what you want at 54. I do the hole all the time and would only do the entrance up to 54. At 54, if you're doing the hole, you best move in imo.

The half-pip is fine for you and the druid (altho the druid wouldn't get much xp unless the druid is doin altexp).

if you get a slower, and especially if the slower is a mezzer, you could move in a lil. Unless you got a full group, though, I wouldn't suggest going as far as the ratmen.
