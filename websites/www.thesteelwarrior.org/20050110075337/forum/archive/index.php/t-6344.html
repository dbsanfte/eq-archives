The biggest bestest advice I can give you on these two is to not fight them at their spawn points. Use walls to prevent ae damage on those that don't need to get hit (clerics+patch healers). I pull vox to zone in, keep the force small (10 or less) so she can blow all her mana before she gates, or just have chanters mana sieve. I don't even clear to her, just invis in and train her to zone. The giants will come first and by the time you finish them all off she'll come in last. Her body has a harder time traveling thru walls then the giants. You have to train her past the king room slowly at first, once you get past that you can go straight for the zone. By all means have a 65 cleric as main healer if you want, she is only going to banish the cleric outside, by the time you get back you can get off another heal.
