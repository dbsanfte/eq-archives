Hi all, new to the site, but older to the game.

I was at DragonCon in Atlanta this weekend and there was actually an EQ forum. The forum was meant to be for PP tricks / quests, which I figured might just be funny to listen to and laugh at some scrubs, but it turned into a riff session on the game.

One member of the panel was a marketing guy from SOE, who used to be the GM on a server (Xegony I think), but now organizes Fan Faires and such. His name escapes me at the moment. Apparently they actually prioritize their list of back fixes based on how much people complain, rather than the severity of the problem. If I find the guy's name, I'll post it.
