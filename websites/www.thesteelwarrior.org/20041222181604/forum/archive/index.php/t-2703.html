AGI/CHA for when you get big, if you plan on being a 65 raid tank. Even with my gear, I can easily max my sta by many many points over. My agi though....not so much. Since AC is very important now, any bit you add will help. I have friend who insist that agi helps with your avoidance too. Maybe one day I can test that out...

Cha is pretty simple. Divine Intervention. The higher YOUR charisma is, the better the chances of getting fully CH'ed when it fires off. Unfailing divinity helps, but it's not the end all be all. The more cha you have, the better your chances of survival.

That being said, there is a TON of gear out there that will max your stats, some say your sta/str are really important, some say agi/cha. If I had to go back and change, I'd make a RZ wood elf, and split points between agi/cha because I am a raid tank, and DI is very useful for me.

Who you listen to in this thread depends on what your plans for your warrior are. If you plan to power to 65 and join a raiding guild, then CHA is probably a reasonable choice. If you are going to level normally, and expect to spend most of your character's life in exp groups or low-end raids, then CHA will do absolutely nothing for you and STR/STA would be much more useful. CHA is only useful for tanking very high-end PoP mobs, which the majority of warriors won't be doing.

When it comes down to it though, starting stats are not at all important at any level of the game, so don't sweat it.
