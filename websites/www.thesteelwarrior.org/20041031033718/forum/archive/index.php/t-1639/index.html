Looks like ~2 procs per minute from those two posts. Coupled with that DPS thats some mean aggro.

Of course BF in mainhand procs 3X a minute (proven from parses) even without an offhand EB proccer.

As a total guess, BF+ offhand EB procs probably about 4.5X a minute. Looking at the total aggro of the two.

With full AA
DPS of BoW = ~ 140 on most PoP mobs average
DPS of 16/20 + 16/20 = ~ 115 on most PoP mobs average

As for parses well:
15 min sets on a certain banker:

BF primary/non proc offhand = 44 EB procs
BoC primary/non proc offhand = 28 EB procs
BF primary/BoC offhand = 73 EB procs
BoW primary = 31 EB procs (and I have several 40min xegony parses that confirms that a 2 proc/min is what you can expect with BoW).

BoW = 2 X 700 hate per minute = 23 hate per second from procs.
BF+BoC = 5 X 700 hate per minute = 58.33 hate per second from procs.

Total BoW hate = 140 + 23.3 = 163.3 hate / second
Total BF+BoC hate = 115 + 52.5 = 173.3 hate / second

I guess you can see why people argue which is more aggro, they appear to be roughly the same from #s :p
