A fix is inc, omg!


We've also finally rectified and rebalanced the many pure melee issues. Monks have been given the ability "Reduced Vulnerability", which reduces by 10 percent per level trained their requirements for food, air, and water. This AA has 10 levels, each costing 5 points. Fully trained, this AA will be the equivalent of permanent enduring breath for this class. We believe this is a significant and final step towards our vision of the monk class as being something you can forget about and leave alone in the corner without it dying.

With Warriors, there are disturbing indications both that taunt is broken and that warriors with /disc agressive are sometimes drawing agro when they do not wish to. Accordingly, we are fixing this by removing the ability Taunt from warriors and replacing it with a new ability, "Happy Smile". Fully trained, this will reduce your position on the hate list much as a rogue's evade will. This will permit warriors to do more damage, without ever worrying about grabbing agro from a hyrbid tank.

In some cases, rogues appear to be approaching wizard and mage damage. Since this is clearly not SoE's vision, we have removed the "backstab" ability from rogues.

We thank all pure melee classes for their patience, and are pleased to announce that this lengthy melee balancing process is now over! Finally, we will be able to get back to the important issues of enhancing caster and hybrid power. Many exciting changes are coming, including a new AA for bards called "Defensive", and a new AA for Shadowknights called "Death Touch".
