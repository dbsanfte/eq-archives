If I had to gear up a warrior today, this is what I would go for -

Royal Attendant Breastplate of Sand
With GoD augs and 3 slots to augment, this BP can be made in to 60ac 200hp, 69ac 160hp V4, or 69ac 170hp AoB. This should last you until you get Raex's. Having clicky mini-Focus is simply not worth the 150-200k or more that you would pay for the BP. If I looted an ornate I would sell it and buy a lot of other upgrades. You can buy a lot of Fo7 in PoK for the plats you save.

Pitted Vambraces
Not quite as easy to get since the nerf, but the Sludge-eating fly is still pretty easy to spawn. You could easily farm these faster than you could farm the PP to buy ornate.

Burnished Antique Greaves
Again, not quite as easy to get since the nerf, but Chavoor is a 15min spawn with a PH. You'll get several repops in the time it takes to get your 100 kills doing the trial normally. If you are just in it for the loot, the exp is pretty good and there are several named you could kill if you have a tracker. These are a lot easier to get and you are only giving up clicky STA over ornate.

Bloodied Boots of Dark Rites
Better boots than ornate, usually cheaper in the bazaar too. I would just farm them myself before spending the plats. Drop in Mistmoore hard, one of the easier hard dungeons. If you have the plats to spend, these are tradeable and generally cheaper than ornate due to the effect ornate boots have for hybrids and clerics.

Warlord's Steel Gauntlets
Other than the clicky haste, these gloves are better than Ornate. You can farm them yourself from Ruj hard or buy them in the bazaar, most often cheaper than other gloves of similar quality. Unless you absolutely have to have the clicky haste, I would go for these gloves.

Kizrak's Helmet of Battle for blind races or Malevolent Helmet of Suffering if you need the haste. Either helm is pretty good. If you can farm the rest of your armor and save your plats for a single item, I would save it for a Helm of Rallos Zek. By far the best single item you can buy in the bazaar, but the price usually reflects that.


Bracers are really the hardest visible armor slot for warriors to equip well. Not only are good bracers hard to find, but you have to get 2 of them! Naturally, most bracers are also LORE so that just compounds the problem. There are a few options though.

Mana-Threaded Bone Bracelet
The 2nd best tradeable warrior bracer. Farm this yourself in Everfrost hards while you unlock the vendor for the A3 augs you will be buying anyway. Tradeable so can be purchased if you have rotten luck with the drops.

Canescent Bone Bracer
These are a random drop in Ferubi. Not a bad place to exp since the retune, one of these might be worth camping, particularly if your group is strong enough to take the mini named that drop augs.

Miremuck Bracer
Drops in the sewers, no real info on this bracer though.

Malevolent Bracer of Suffering
Everfrost LDoN merchant, with augs makes a very nice bracer. Considerably cheaper than the Tak bracer and is of equal quality as Ornate.

Crystallized Acid Bracer
Not only the best tradeable bracer, but not LORE. Get these if at all possible. The price tends to be prohibitive, but you can easily farm the Acid Mass with a single group. They are a rare drop, but these bracers will serve you well until you get Raex's.

Cultural bracer
For any race other than Vah Shir and Iksar, cultural is still an option for bracers. The price of the Ornate just doesn't justify 3ac 25hp V2. Cultural will work well enough until you can upgrade to any of the other bracers I have listed here. Ornate bracers are much better for clerics and hybrids.

Really I am surprised that Ornate is all that cheap on other servers. I do a lot of buying and selling in the bazaar and most plate ornate is ridiculously priced on Terris Thule and Prexus. Typical prices -

Plate BP mold - 200k+
greaves mold - 80-100k
bracer mold - 50-75k each
helm mold - 50k
boot mold - 75k
gauntlet mold - 30-50k
