Jakle Attakle
Usually anything that requires an MA will include enough people to snare/Tash.

I *suppose* you could use Jstick until you get a Tash off, but it really isnt that good a use of time. In the time it takes hoping that you will get your Tash, you may have dead casters/much less DPS due to you not just tanking as MA.

This goes double for snaring, as there are multiple snare classes.

If you are talking about soloing or duoing with no ench or snarer, that is a different matter.

However most things difficult enough to actually require Tash usually also require bringing an enchanter to help kill it faster.
