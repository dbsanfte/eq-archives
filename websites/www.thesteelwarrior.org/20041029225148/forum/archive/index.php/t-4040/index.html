Daramin
There's a lot of info on the Safehouse you should look at. I'll give you the highlights for each:

0) Keep MR up and have a bard singing PoV in your group for all of these. Some don't need it, and you can optimize what the bard sings once you know what's really going on. Enough of them lifetap for 1500 or 5000 (yes, 5000) that you kinda want it.

1) Carprin -- we usually do it at the zonein, monks have no problem splitting him off his guards. Be ready for his respawn of course, usually someone dies when he pops, but he's not too hard. I forget if he rampages or not, it doesn't matter tho.

2) Avri somethingorother. This dude spawns 4 skeletons every time a player dies or feigns, or every time a pet dies. Agro control has to be 100% locked down. He's rooted, and he rampages. Often you'll find some skeletons around from folks failing, or from monks feigning around him. They depop after 20 or 30 minutes. He has low HP, so you can burn him down as long as you keep agro locked so that no one dies. Our first win on him was during a wipe, so that's possible, but he's far far easier if you just control agro and do it right.

3) Bishop Whoever. Rooted, don't think he rampages, not too hard. Killing him spawns the next three.

A brief note about this. Raex and Vindor (Raex especially) are tough mofos. You should probably clear the entire room there before you spawn them, otherwise get ready for a lot of monk/sk drags (if you have many bards, maybe they can make it all easier with FM). Splitting Raex from Vindor is a learning process. You do NOT want to try them both at once, at least not your first few times through.

4) Vindor BlahBlah. Slowable, flurries, not too bad. Your best tank should not tank this one, since Raex is next.

5) Raex KillsALot. Not slowable, hits for 1700, rampages, 5k lifetap. You want your best tank on him. It's possible to do him before Vindor, just depends on how your pullers split it. You definitely want to know when he's coming. This guy is no pushover, he has enough HP to make agro lock a necessity, and the rampage can wipe you fast.

6) High Priest Ultor. The reason you're here, he's a pushover after Raex. Rooted, procs a 90% slow. You need someone (two someone's?) chain-casting Abolish Disease on you, because it's 36 counters.

Just did this again last night for the 7th time, so I'm pretty fresh on it. Eventually it gets real boring, although Raex is always a nice kick in the ass ;).
