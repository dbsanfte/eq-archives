First off, it has normal proc rate mod. Delay really has nothing to do with it. It is the base 2 proc per minute at 305 dex.

The proc is no extra agro, it is just 119 damage nuke.

For any grouping situation where you have an intense agro class, like a necro, a good shaman, a druid. Any class that is trying to dump out good dps with a combination of snares/debuffs/slows you will lose agro.

The dps from 13/17 is actually quite good in your main hand. So in the average exp group. Incite + this and good off hander. I bet if you used incite had boc offhand and this mainhand you would probably not have agro problems most of the time in groups.

If you are a grouping tank, or low in your tank rot. This weapon and say a stun proc aug would go along way because it high dps for a warrior. But a serious raid tank, or even a god trial/hard ldon tank will likely pass this weapon by because of ripostes and the need for every proc to carry a nice agro punch.
