i would totally go with stamina or regen... that is unless your poor and cant afford the stat food... not like its expensive and its a lot easier to get pp nowadays... the stamina is good, esp. for non ogres, because it will help you get as close to max sta as possible unbuffed, every 60+ warrior should prolly have max stamina buffed, even with planar power AAs, but unbuffed is very important too for comparison's sake. when you are being compared to other tanks in a raid(usually open) situation many times your unbuffed hp will come into consideration

its a bit of a stretch, but many fights must be done buffless and that extra hp can make a huge difference, plus if your in ldons with no shammys or bsts it will be invaluable.

yes eventually your gear will put your stamina at 305 unbuffed and then those 3 AAs will only help when your naked, but think of how much it will help you up until then. also by the time your gear puts you that high you shouldnt be worrying about a couple hundred pp every once in a while for stat food, at least i never have...

the regen can help too, esp once you get all your regen AA's(which have prerequisited of previous regen AA's, so your gonna end up getting it eventually) you can get a hella lot of regen, which means that much less healing which makes ya that much easier to heal : )
