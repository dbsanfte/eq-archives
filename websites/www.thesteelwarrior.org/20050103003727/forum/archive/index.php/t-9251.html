If your goal is to get the most from your time, levels >>>>>>>>> AA's. Gaining a level increases skill caps, hp's, resist rates, and many other things. You will do more damage, and take less simply by leveling. While there are valid reasons to do AA's before 70, and run3 probably still should be a priority to get earlier(run 3 never fades at a bad moment, unlike SoW/shrew), 90 % of the time, leveling is much more important than AA's.
