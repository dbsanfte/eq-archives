"Valor involves raiding Stratholm, Scholomance, and Blackrock Spire to get the piece.

Specific pieces drop off specific bosses in most cases. For example, the Baron in the UD side of Strat drops the Legs, I *think* the gauntlets are random in UD Strat (Undead side Stratholme) or maybe all of Strat, the Bracers come from LBRS (Lower Black Rock Spire). The Valor BP comes off the boss of UBRS (Upper Blackrock Spire) General something or other, level 61ish elite dragon. Not sure on the other pieces, but a quick search on wow.allakhazam.com will tell you.

As said above Might comes from Molten Core, 40 man raids. A guild that is doing MC will have high standards for warriors, so don’t expect to get into a MC raid unless you’ve already tanked all of the above zones, unless you’ve got some friends who are willing to take you along. Valor:

BP Genearl Drakk in UBRS (boss)
Shoulders Rend UBRS
Bracer Random mobs in UBRS
Legs Baron Rivendare Undead Strath
Gaunts Ramstein Undead Strath (right before Rivendare)
Helm Darkmaster (or some crap) Gandling guy at the very end of Scholomance
Boots Kirtonas the Herald in Scholomance. (not too far in, need blood of the Martyr to spawn him)

Might all drops in Molten Core now. Lets are from Magmadar, BP is from Golemagg, bracer and belt are from random zone mobs, gaunts/boots/helm I forget, but from some mobs in there. (Garr, Lucifron, Ghennas, Shazzrah, Gedon, Sulferon, just can't remember which drop which set pieces, I think some can drop a couple different ones)

Wrath is mostly removed to Blackwing's lair until it comes out. Wrath helm still drops from Onyxia for sure, legs MAY drop from Ragnaros, but I'm not sure on that one.

There are also some good alternatives to full Valor set. I personally had almost a full set and never wore it. I used Rend's legs and Adam BP to tank. There are even some good alternatives to Might/Wrath. None of the set bonuses are really that good, although some of the piece bonuses for Might/Wrath (improve dodge, improve crit, improve parry etc.), are pretty good. Hope that helps.
