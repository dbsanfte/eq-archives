From memory, here are some directions to the bank:
Assuming you just zoned into Katta from Tenebrous Mountains, go past the zone in area and as you come into the main part of town there will be a fence-like stone structure on your left, follow that a little ways and once you have the option to turn left, do so. Follow this path until you take the first right. You will now be walking down a narrow path along a wall way way above the ground. Be careful. If you fall from the upper parts of this path, you will shortly be back at your bind point with your corpse right behind the bank in Katta. (I've tried jumping from the upper part of the path to the roof of the bank. It didn't work the first time, and I don't feel like trying a second time.) When you get to the end of this path, you will be at a lower level, with a tavern ahead of you and to the left, and the bank ahead of you and to the right. You can parse on both of these bankers. From the front they occasionally will hit you for a very minimal amount (assuming level 65) and from the rear they just stand there shouting, "guards! guards!" or something.

If these directions aren't sufficient, refer to this map (http://eqatlas.com/luclin/kattacastellummap.html) from eqatlas.
