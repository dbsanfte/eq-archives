richardkent
Well,

The arena is a decent spot, plus if you move into the temple area the drop rate increases. The bank is the normal 'solo' / mini group area. It can be done solo or perhaps just two people, depending on class & levels. The drop rate on the bank is nowhere near as good as the arena, but it beats not getting drops.

The other popular spot is the Plate House aka Icehouse (i believe) depending on your server. It is basically a cycle you start by chatting to a dwarf in the house (he's part of the coldain ring quest, if memory serves me). You basically get mobs spawning and as you kill one another spawns. I believe it is six waves of mobs, each wave getting harder and harder. You can also evac out and restart the cycle if it gets too hard. It has been a long time since I've done it so I'm hazy. But searching on Plate House / Icehouse should get you the details. I did a long post on the old message board, be dammed if I can remember all the details.

richardkent
Corroded breastplate is always plate armor. The chain armor says corroded chain in the description.

I have seen a lot of chain armor drop in Kael the numerous 'raids' I've done there. The plate gauntlets are pretty common, while the breastplates I've only managed 2 so far. I think I've managed to get 2 full sets of chain in the same number of raids. About 1.5 full sets of plate armor as well.

I have seen 3 pieces of leather armor and perhaps twice as many silk pieces drop. I wish more leather would drop, hell, I'd go for a silk chest piece as well.

If you need plate armor badly, I'd give the plate house a shot (aka ice house). I haven't done that many cycles, but I know way back it used to drop a ton of plate. I suppose by now the drop rate is much lower, but considering the level of the mobs in the waves your odds of getting drops should be good.
