TM is an offensive AA. It allows you to dmg through an opponent who would otherwise parry, riposte etc.. you.

You will still get hit like normal when attacking an enraged mob. The only difference is that with TM you will actually do some dmg back to that mob.

You will not take any less dmg when getting TM.
