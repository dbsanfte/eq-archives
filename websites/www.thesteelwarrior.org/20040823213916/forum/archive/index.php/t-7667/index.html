Grendonbb
GoD Tipt/Vxed and Sewers trials offer a very challenging single group environment. Vxed is more boring, requires less movement, but all are fun to do, with sewers being by far the easiest. LDoN normal adventures are pretty *yawn* after a while, but the hard adventures are very challenging. Both GoD events and LDoN hards net very nice results for the difficulty (in my magelo, greaves and right earring are from vxed). Additionally, the ability to augment items is pretty neat, though you have to do a lot of LDoNs to afford many of their augments. Tipt in GoD drops several nice augments, as well as the boss dropping an amazing bracer for each armor type.

I initially thought GoD sucked, and I was burned out on LDoN. Then I started doing hard LDoNs, and working through GoD trials, and I am having a lot of fun in the game. I'm glad I stuck it out, and didn't give up on the game. GoD and LDoN hards, in addition to guild advancement, are keeping me in the game.
