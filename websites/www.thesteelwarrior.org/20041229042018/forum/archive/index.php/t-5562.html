I generally just leave SoPP in my offhand all the time, and swap DBotW (w/ anger3) for aggro and CoW aug'd with lifetap for non-aggro DPS. And then often swap in a shiled w/ DBotW.

I guess the advice that I would give is just keep which ever one you have. It's not at all worth it having both of them.
