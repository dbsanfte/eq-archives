I don't anticipate Knights getting a 30 second auto-parry or auto-reposte disc. I believe the upper limit is likely to be 12 seconds.

Basically, I believe Verant/SoE has intended Warriors to be the only tank capable of exceeding normal mitigation long enough to survive even a 30 second fight vs. a brutal hard-hitting mob. Knights are intended to fight nearly (but not quite) as good as an identically equipped undisc'd Warrior and cast spells some of which enhance their survivability during combat and some of which serve other purposes.

This is exactly what the case has been since Kunark, what it was intended to be, and what it should continue to be. The role of Knights is to be able to tank mobs not requiring "burst mitigation" (and to cast a multitude of spells) and the role of Warriors is to be the tank that can achieve the "unmatched ability to survive".

Fortunately with SK's having base level tanking and also being able to produce damage in a number of ways, "snare", FD, cripple a mob and inspire Fear in it their role in any sized party is assured.

Fotunately with Paladins having base level tanking and also able to heal, buff, group heal, Root, GRC, etc. their role in any sized party is assured.

Should Knights need spell improvements so they can meet their class description, they should be forthcoming. Should a Knight perceive a need for "burst mitigation":

a. they are undergeared for this particular mob OR

b. Have engaged a Warior prefered mob through error and will require additional support OR

c. Have encountered improperly adjusted content and should inform SoE.

Defensive (by any self supplied means) is a Warrior class defining ability that should not be shared. Not for three minutes and not for a long enough duration to provide "burst mitigation" for any entire fight no matter how brief.

A "burst mitigation" ability long enough to be useful in an emergency, at initial contact so the healers can get "synched up", etc. - that seems reasonable. But not long enough to make them Warriors with spellbooks in any fight.

I believe most Warriors understand these issues, SoE has shown general agreement, the PoN Class Balance board has finally generally come around, and only a few players continue to argue to the contrary hoping to wear down opposition through their persistance.
