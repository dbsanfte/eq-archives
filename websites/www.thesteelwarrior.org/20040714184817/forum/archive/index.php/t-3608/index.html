The diminishing returns start pretty low, let's say 1600-1700? It basically start when your highest number of specific hit is for minimum damage. Since you cannot redcuce a hit below minimum dmg (with shielding you can thought, but different thing) you start experiencing a diminishing return on ac versus dmg reduction. This can only start to happen when you already mitigate alot of dmg and not like it was with the velious cap where on mobs you were not really mitigating alot of dmg but you could get no added benefit from higher ac.

Boss mobs hit much harder then your average trash xp mob and you will start experimenting with a diminishing return much later.

There is probably a cap somewhere, but at least now it seem to be high enough that people aren't easily reaching it.
