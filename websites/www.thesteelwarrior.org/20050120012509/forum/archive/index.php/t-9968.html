I heard some tragic news this morning from a friend when he told Darrell Abbott ("Dimebag" Darrel) was shot and killed doing a gig at a night club last night. Even though Pantera had already decided to go their seperate ways before this incident, the band earned and kept my respect over the years and so I just wanted to take a minute to tip my hat to the now deceased.
