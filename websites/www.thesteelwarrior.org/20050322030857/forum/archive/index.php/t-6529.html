If you mean you've accumulated over 1400+ points in each of the 5 different adventure themes, then yes you definitely should see every item that the adventure merchants have to offer. If, however, you've only accumulated a total of 1400+ points in all 5 different adventure themes combined, then it's a different story. The adventure point merchants at each theme camp will only show you the items up to a cost of 1.5 X your AP for that theme. In other words, if you have 1400 points total, and 800 of those are at the North Ro/Tak camp, the merchants at that Tak camp will show you items costing up to 1200 AP (800 AP X 1.5), even though you will only be able to actually buy items at that camp costing 800 points or less. Until you get more Tak points and unlock more items, that is :p

If however, you already know all that, and have over 1400+ points accumulated in each camp, but can only see items costing up to 1150 or so; then yup, you have a bug of some kind that I haven't seen or heard of yet. Maybe try petitioning.
