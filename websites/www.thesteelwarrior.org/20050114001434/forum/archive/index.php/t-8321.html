1ac = 6hp = 1sta until sta is capped. Live by that formula and your gear choices should be very clear. Str doesn't matter. Dex doesn't matter. Agi doesn't matter. Wis and int don't matter either... see the trend? Resists don't matter either! Attack matters a little bit, but not more than a little bit of hp/ac. Same with regen. Better haste would be cool, but not immediately vital.

Your weapons are solid. Stay with them.
