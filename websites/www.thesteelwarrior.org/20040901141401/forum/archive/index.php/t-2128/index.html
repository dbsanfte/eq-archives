Bunnie Burner
Here's an idea :

Add "Limit Effects" to current equipment, as well as make available a couple new items with Limit Effects.

What this Limit Effects would be, are simple effects that would have charges.
But these charges would be dynamically recharged.

What event recharges the item?... well that's where you let imagination flow.

Here is a simple example:

Bracer of Hatred : (quested off a new npc in Plane of Hate)
-----------------------

AC: 15
HP: 45 STA:10 DEX:10 STR:5
Limit Effect: Innoruuk's Rage
Charges: 2

Item description: Item regains strength upon removing life from a creature.
-------------------------

What will this do?

You can right click the effect whenever you want. Casting Innoruuk's Rage onto your target, adding 200hate.

You wont be able to click it more then twice though.
Not without recharging it first.

To recharge it, you have to simply land a Killing Blow.

Everytime you land a killing blow, you could see the text:
"Your Bracer of Hatred bathes in a shimmering light" or something :p
and there you go, one more charge added to your bracer.


The possiblities are endless.

Why not a :

Undead Hierophant's Sandals
---------------------------------------
Limit Effect: Ensnare
Charges: 5

Item Description: Item regains strength upon the death of a challenging animal.
(not a killing blow, but a simple exp message from the death of an animal :p )
----------------------


Warlord's Incadescent Visor
----------------------------------------
Limit Effect: Call of Rallos Zek
Charges: 1

Item description: Item regains strength when the holder becomes Berzerk.
Spell Description: 20% Haste that affects group, and exceeds haste cap.
-----------------------------------------

etc....
