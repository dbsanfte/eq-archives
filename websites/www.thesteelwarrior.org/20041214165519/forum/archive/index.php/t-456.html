05-05-2003, 08:05 AM
I duo'd my warr and cleric before PoP came out for months to farm aa's and what not. The general population suggests shamans as better partners, but if you dont have that option, clerics definately work as a duo partner. Having some extra accessories make this duo make up so much easier though.

A slow weapon (Willsapper, ToD, Wavecrasher) will help immensely, I also learned that you need something to snare a mob with (I ended up with a Silken Whip of Ensnaring), if the cleric can do the quest from Neriak for the snare necklace, all the better.

A self haste item definately wouldnt hurt but I wouldnt say its necessary (Ornate gloves, Eyepatch of Plunder, Belt of Marvelous Visions, etc).

I also made it a point to have as much of a self damage shield on myself as possible (Blazing arms of Fennin Ro from Zordak and the 8th ring worked great for me). Also make sure the cleric drops a Mark of Retribution or Mark of the Righteous on the mob for another 15 or 34 pts of damage every time it lands a hit.

None of the things i mentioned are really must haves (well, maybe the slow weapon is), but will make duo'ing with a cleric soo much easier if you can get any of them. I personally prefer the cleric as a partner, mostly because i can get a rez if i do something stupid.
