Well, aside from the usual pro-ughre propaganda, some of this is decent info.

In the end game, all the races even out. Until then there are some subtle differences.

Ogre - has slam, unstunnable from frontal melee.
Troll - has slam, regen.
Barbarian - slam, not us ugly as ughres, night blind
Dark elves - have ultravision
Iksar - innate AC, regen, lower level armour difficulties

Woodies, Half elves and the runt races are all about the same, with some variation in base stats.

Your god of choice is important if you wish to take advantage of cultural armours. Contrary to the Ughre Propagandists, Tribunal > Rallos Zek but Helanic Tundra (which is limited to barbs, humans and half elves) is harder to find than Fierce Heraldic.

Ok not gonna talk about race as most everyone here has been doing that - but just a few short pointers.

if you go for a large race - dont waste money on shrink potions at 150pp a pop. Spend 500 pp on a anizok minimising device - comes with 20 charges but can be recharged with a class 6 mana battery - which u can pick up in bazaar for about 150 pp. so with it for 150 pp u get 20 charges of shrink as opposed to 10 charges for the ants potion for same price.

when u start out, get a 1hs, 1hb, 1hpiercing and 2 handers - personally i didnt go for 2hb, but theres a lot of nice 2hb weps out there now so if i started again id make sure it was maxed

ok heres the trick to keep all your skills levelled. on your hotbank drag a couple of blank inventory slots to a hotbank button. also drag your primary and secondary melee wep slots to the same hotbank. if you then equip your weapons, you can interchance them via the hotbank buttons without having to open inventory. that way - you can rotate your 1 handers and your 2 handers during the course of a fight with ease and thereby keeping them all maxed. if your swopping out 2 1 handers for a 2 hander in invetory - remember that the other inventory slot has to be free to be able to accomondate the 1 hander - so always keep those both free.

i also do the same thing with my ranged item and bow , as ranged has better ac - i tag with bow then swop out with range item when mob is inc. so i drag the range item and a third inventory slot to the same hotbank.

if you go large race, i woudnt worry about being kos. with PoT ability to move around quickly, luclin to buy all your stuff, theres really hardly any need to go to old world cities like freeport anymore.

when you get to lvl 35 you get shield - dont forget to use it, theres lots of threads here about how to do it best.

have fun.

Race really doesn't matter much. I like the Ogre's natural Slam and immunity to frontal stuns but neither is all that great. When it comes down to it, it doesn't matter what you play, just do what ever seems cool. If you go Ogre, do not be agnostic.

The whole Evil vs. Good thing is pretty much a wash since Luclin and even more so since PoP. When you can travel almost anywhere without seeing a city, can trade in Shadowhaven and Tanaan without a problem, Old World racism isn't such a big factor.

For starting stats, get agility to 75, put the rest in stamina.

Weapon skills, this is more than minor. If you only use 1hs your entire career, you absolutely deserve the pain you'll have in your 50s and 60s when kickass weapons drop and you have single digit skill in those weapons. Train ALL five skills. Even piercing, which caps lower than the others, but there are some nice ones anyway. Yes, it is a huge pain to train all five weapon skills and you will always be behind until your late 40s, but work at it. The best advice I got when I started was to train all 5 skills. My current primary weapon is now a 2hb.
