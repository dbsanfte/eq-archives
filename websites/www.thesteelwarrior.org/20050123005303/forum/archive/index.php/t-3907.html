According to the formula from the arena thread, the weapons _should_ stack up as follows:

Anathema 36/30 (36 db) = 26.6 swing hate per second.
BoW 48/32 (37 db) = 27.1 swing hate per second
SEWS 45/30 (36 db) = 28.6 swing hate per second
twin BoC 15/20 (13/0 db) = 25.3 swing hate per second

80% dual wield rate assumed for BoC.

700 proc hate twice every minute averages 23 hate per second, so you should get the order BoW > Anathema > twin BoC > SEWS, with the BoW only being < 5% higher aggro than anathema.

On the other hand, an augmented SEWS is probably better than either other weapon unaugmented, and is the best aggro in the case you get no procs.

Well, thats a lot of theory, interesting to see if it matches real world experiences.
