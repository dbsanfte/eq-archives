How exactly, then, is this mob doable for nonraiders with less than a semi-zerg force? Honest question, here. I'm open to suggestions. If nonraiders _really can_ take him down with the 1-2 groups that I understood Epic 1.5 was intended for, then I'd like to hear about it.
