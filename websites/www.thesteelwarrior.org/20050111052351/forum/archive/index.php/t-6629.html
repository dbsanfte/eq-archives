I'm guessing that infuriate is a multi target triggerred rampage. It's not quite the same as AE rampage, because many people in rage and on the mob's hate list don't get hit or even swung at during infuriate, just a large group of people.

AE Rampage is the biggest misnomer in EQ anyhow, no mobs actually "AE Rampage", they simply have a rampage list that's large enough to include everyone on the hate list. If you didnt' do something to actively get on an "AE Rampage" mob's hatelist by either performing a detrimental act on the mob, or performing a beneficial act on someone who was performing a detrimental act on the mob, then you will NOT be hit by the "AE Rampage". I used to stand next to RZTW the entire fight and not get hit when I was tank #2 because I simply didn't engage. The same is true with any AE Rampage mob... be it Fennin, Ginto, Hinvats, whatever... if you haven't performed an active act to get on the mob's hatelist, you will NOT be hit by it.

Based on what I've seen, infuriate is similar to AE Rampage, but the list is simply shorter than AE Ramp, and the mob can leave off a number of people.
