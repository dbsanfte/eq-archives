ZajeerSOE
Also, real quick, since I'm already here and some players have been wondering. This is how recast types work:

Basically, on the item, it can have a recast type between 1 and 20. If you have 2 items that have recast timers on them, but share the same recast type, then you can only use one or the other at one time. For example, you have Nuke Staff A that has a 10 minute recast and Nuke Staff B that has a 15 minute recast. Both are on recast type2. Let's say you click the Nuke Staff B and now you want to use Nuke Staff A. You will have to wait 15 minutes before you can use either one, because the type2 timer decriments from 15 minutes, because that was the last clickie used.

Conversely, if you clicked Nuke Staff A, you would only have to wait 10 minutes to use either Nuke Staff A or B. What this does is allow us to design items under specific recast types that provide upgrades from the previous clickie. So maybe Staff A is a 2000pt nuke found in Plane of Time. Maybe staff B is a 1000pt nuke found in elemental planes. A player would upgrade from B -> A because it's both a larger nuke, plus has a faster recast. And since there can be up to 20 recast types, this allows for multiple nuke staffs on different recast types, allowing a player to have multiple clickies to choose from.

In the future, you'll be seeing more items with more powerful spell clickies on them, that will be balanced around the fact that they will take a while to recast.
