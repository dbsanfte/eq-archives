Okay... Sanori >> Enter PoJ, exit GY, go down the stairs, go around (right or left, its a circle) go up the stairs on the backside... then go straight, thru the door and u are facing the cellblock of mobs you want to kill. You want to camp in the 'exit' so go either right or left and its the open doorway either on the right or left dependings... in the back there is a door that when u click on it u zone out... generally you want to camp at the end of one of the sides of exit and pull the 16 mobs, 4 mobs per 4 walls

Gaylon >> Problem is, you REALLY want a slower in jail/caves. Caves moreso than jail. Jail you can PROBABLY do, but you will want to harmony pull the mobs so you can get htem single. Harmony left, pull right works WONDERS. Assuming you know how to get to jail.. you can either camp outside of the 4 outter mobs or in the right corner (hug right as you enter the second door or u aggro the trap) in the main room in front of the grate (for repop reasons). Caves you want to hug the right side of the room and keep the mobs AWAY from the left wall (or you get aggro). Here too you want to harmony pull. You CAN get them all single, there's just a trick to the second set... there are 2 mobs at the entrance to the cave system, clear those, then there is a larvae on your right, harmony the larvae and then hug the wall to the right of it, you should aggro the next mob and get it single... otherwise pulling the right larvae will bring the two. *IF* you get two ya want your druid or ranger to root one away from the group. The mobs drop pieces for a 20 wis ring which likely your druid friend will want... so in the caves pull to the right. The larvae drop the alkaline stones and the scarabs drop the cloudy eyes. If you have a good mezzer/slower you can move in to the right side...right at the first fork, camp at the second. In summary: Jail is better loot and easier but you get the uber n00bs that have to get to Mavuin trapsing thru every once in a while. Caves is better exp and no one will bother you.

Also: If you have T2/3 access you probably aren't looking for where to exp :P You know where the kickass exp is :D

Roma
The Guards are non-KOS. There are 2 factions in PoJ: The Creatures and the Beasts. Creatures are the faction giving racial mobs and the Beasts are the ones that look like zone mobs. Killing Creatures gives positive Beast faction and negative Creature faction. Killing Beats gives positive Creature faction and negative Beast faction. There are a couple quests including the self-PoTorment key that require you to have one of the factions so you can talk to one of the mobs. Other than that, I have found no reason to pick one faction or the other. I just keel em both :D Its toooo easy to fix your faction with one of the groups if you want it.
