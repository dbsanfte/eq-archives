One thing that can help when doing LDoN's with a small group: if you have the healing power to do so, don't slow the mobs. use the best DS you have access to and have the cleric MoR the mobs when possible to add to the damage taken by the mob. You will burn through more healer mana this way, but if you can do so without adding down time, the added DPS can be a very significant factor.
