I used to frequent Rathe Mountains to farm Hill Giants. Depending on how much plat they decide to drop that day, and how many druids and necros are in the zone, I actually made a fair amount of cash there. But I'd do a /who when I zoned in, and just turn around if there were more than 10 people in the zone. When it's crowded it sucks balls.

I like Tenebrous Mountains a lot more. For the most part it's almost always empty except on weekends, mostly because it's kinda out of the way I think. The focus here is the pair of caves along the southern wall of the zone, east of the zone-in from Grimling Forest. These two caves are full of static spawns for grimling orefinders, grimling geomancers, and the occasional grimling foreman. Also, grimling laborers will path in, through, and back out of the caves intermittently. The orefinders are green and completely trivial (I think they're level 30ish). Your HP will barely wiggle. They have about 1khp. The geomancers have around 700hp and will try to cast spells that won't stick. The foreman are slightly higher level and have about twice as much hp as the orefinders. They might bring your hp below 97% if they get some lucky hits in. The laborers are about as weak as the geomancers, but are melee only.
Every single on of these mobs will drop one or more Etched Acrylia Runes with varying names. As far as I know, these are good for nother but selling to a vendor. You can get anywhere from 9gp to 10+pp for each rune. Obviously the cheaper ones are more common. Each of these mobs also have the chance to drop useless weapons that sell for a few plat each. If you spend an hour farming these caves, you'll probably rake in between 200-400pp just from runes and weapons.
The real money here though is from the gems and acrylia. There are small pieces, small bricks, large bricks, and blocks. These sell to players in the bazaar, and depending on the current supply you can get anywhere from 40pp to 100pp for each piece (on Fennin Ro that is). Windstones also drop pretty often, and sell for at least 100pp each to players. A lot of other random luclin gems drop here, most of them selling to vendors for 10-20pp. Some of the more rare gems like Luclinite and Umbracite sell for 80-100pp to vendors.
At the rate that I clear out one of these caves, the first ones start to repop a couple minutes after the last I kill. Your results may vary depending on your DPS. I'm guessing the respawn is about 10minutes. If both caves are uncamped, you can clear one out, clear the other, and come back and the first will be fully popped again.
They also drop vials of blood which are pretty worthless, and mining picks and pieces of pyrrohite (sp?) that are good for faction turn-ins in Katta Castellum. There are some nice quests in Katta.
One of which is the Rallic Pack quest. For the uninitiated, the Rallic Pack is a 10 slot bag, capable of holding giant items, with 85% weight reduction. Basically, you turn in a grimling runner's pouch to a guy named Rallic in Katta to receive a Rallic Pack Pattern. He's on Concillium Universus faction I think, one of Katta's many factions. I believe the pyrrohite sample turn-in quest gives CU faction. Factioning for Katta sucks though. I always just keep an Illusion:Human (any good race works) Potion in the shared bank just for the purpose of doing the Rallic turn-in. I'm a troll. If you're a good race and haven't screwed up your faction, you shouldn't have to worry about it. You combine Rallic's pattern, a perfect owlbear pelt, a shade silk cord, and a shade silk thread in a sewing kit to receive the Rallic Pack. The grimling runner pouch that you turn in to Rallic for the pattern drops off of a grimling runner in Tenebrous Mountains. He spawns just inside the cave to the Grimling Forest zoneline 90 minutes from the time he was last killed. He's about as wimpy as a grimling foreman. From there he'll path along the south edge of the zone going just inside the two orefinder caves and back. The pelts can be obtained from owlbears in Grimling Forest or wherever, but I just buy them in the bazaar for 40pp. The shade silk cords and threads are made from shadeling silks which I just buy in the bazaar for 2pp or farm from xakthra (sp?) silk worms whenever I go through Shadeweaver's Thicket. Trivial on the cords and threads are around 20 or so, and there is no skill-check on the final combine for the pack. These are LORE. I usually sell them for 700-800pp a piece. All of my alts have one, and there's always one in both of my shared banks ready to sell. My sewing kit is always full of a handful of pelts, silks, cords, and threads, and I usually have a spare pattern or pouch on hand.
