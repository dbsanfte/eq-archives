This is a decent link to get a *rough* idea of how Stamina equates to AC at a given set of values (level, current hp, current ac). You still have to weigh the (many) other factors* involved, but it gives you a starting point at least.

http://relicguild.org/calc.cgi

For reference, I got about 1 Stamina = 24 AC at level 56 with 5477 AC and 4501 hitpoints.

* Other factors may include: spell damage, PVP mitigation rules, mana efficiency, healing buffers, heal aggro, diarrhea, vomiting, and death.
