A while back, the Druids from EQDRUIDS forum got together and started a dialogue (I suppose via email) with developers or whoever and presented a case for improving their class. They basically hashed out what they wanted on the boards and came up with a final draft to present to Verant and got some changes made to the benefit of their class. Their basic case was, "we aren't really necessities on raids and we can't heal worth a shit compared to a cleric"...as a result they got two partial Cheals, Nature's Infusion (damn close to a cleric supernal), some better nukes, and some other things.

I suggest warriors take a page from the Druids "get shit changed" Handbook. Elect a leader, brainstorm, hash it out on the boards the things we want that won't be "Unbalancing" and try to implement change in a positive way. If you are already over EQ and waiting on SWG and WoW then you probably don't give a damn. However, bitching and whining and threatening to leave for the next game isn't getting it done. I seriously doubt developers peruse these threads looking for malcontents or ideas. Take the bull by the horns and go after them.

I'm not saying unionize and every warrior cancel their account for thirty days to try and impact the sony pocketbook, what I am saying is make a detailed "list" of what will make the warrior class better and then figure out how to start a positive dialogue. We don't want warriors to be the ultimate God class of EQ (well I do, but I'm a bit more realistic), but I think we all are hoping for some tweaks. Let's not wait for the next expansion and pray something good happens, lets go after it...

One of you Overlords step up and become a leader, or nominate someone and get started.
