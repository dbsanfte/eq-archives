AC = Shoot for 1000 AC. Then go from there.

STR = Shoot for over 100. Then try and get it to 155. Let avatar do the rest later.

STA = Shoot for 205 unbuffed. You're an ogre. You can DO IT!

AGI = Shoot for like 101 at first. Then 120. After that it's mostly meaningless. But at 155, avatar will do the rest.

DEX = shoot for 101 dex. Then 155. Then let avatar do its thang.

Weapons to look for = Black Blade of Tormenting. Anything with the proc Ykesha on it. Anything that STUNS. Frostbringer. Wavecrasher. Snowchipper and/or Spiked Velium Mallet. Anklesmasher or a Silken Whip of Ensnaring. Kunzar Ku'jich.

OR, just think about a Windblade at around level 45 or so.

In the meantime, at your level, since procs don't start happening until your 45+ levels, think about Lamentation, Centi Longsword, Wurmslayer, Centi Shortsword.

If you REALLY a weapon that procs at an early leve, Wakizashi of the Frozen Skies.

Good luck.

Have fun.
