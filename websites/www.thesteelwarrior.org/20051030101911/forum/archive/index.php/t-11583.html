You know, if they really wanted to fix much of the problems with instant strikes, there is an easy fix. Base instant strike damage not off raw damage, but off of DPS at say 3.8 or 4.0 seconds. That would mean any time you used a Mortal Strike, WW, or OVerpower with any weapon, not matter the speed, it would hit like a ton of bricks. You could say then fast weapons would have the advantage, but I do not think so. Each would have its advantages, with fast weapons more spell interrupts and misses hurt a lot less. You also might get 2 attacks in where you may only have gotten 1 before too. Slow weapons would still not be hurt as much by player movement in PvP and would always hit harder up front because first attack ignores weapon speed.

That would make swords viable for MS, and boost Fury because you are still using overpower, WW, and even Slam. Weapon choices would open up and not everyone would be carrying around a reaper. That would be a start, and then they could look into the talent problems for Fury and Protection.
