"How much do STA augs cost in LDoN?"

6 Stamina ones drop a lot, usually every 3rd adventure or so I see one in a level 65 group.

I wouldn't buy a stamina aug unless I had a lot of points and nothing better to buy. There are better things to spend points on like Anger III augs or pure hp augs or some of the nicer armor for example. Pure hp >> stamina because a shaman can usually buff you to at least 255 and after that it is diminishing returns on stamina gained hit points. I would probably suggest you spend 5 to 15 AA to increase stamina before you spent LDoN points on a stamina aug if feel you really must do it.
