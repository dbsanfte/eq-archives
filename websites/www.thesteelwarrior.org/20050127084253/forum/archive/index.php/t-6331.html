With increased proc rate, BF does 2170 hate per minute. GBoS aug'ed with Anger III does 1200+958 or 2158 (assuming stun is 300 hate). Roughly the same hate. However, the GBoS does it in four procs (average) per minute, while the BF does it in three. And the GBoS has a slightly better ratio than the BF. Thus the GBoS will have slightly more reliable early aggro, and probably roughly equal long-term aggro, making an aug'ed GBoS probably slightly better to mainhand than a BF.

If stun is only 200 hate, the aug'ed GBoS does 1958 hate per minute, which is probably somewhat less hate overall (including its fractionally better ratio) than the BF gives. I'd still prefer to mainhand it, though, as the four ppm makes an early proc more likely than the BF's three ppm.
