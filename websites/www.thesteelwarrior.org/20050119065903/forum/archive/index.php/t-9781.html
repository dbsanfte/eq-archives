11-18-2004, 02:13 AM
/agree with oomwarrior. You can definately survive there and could hold down say Blind/Task ( I use this for example because its the camp im most familiar with.) and do very well. A couple things that will make it either doable or not worth anyones time:

1. You will want a back-up healer of some sort, no question. Some of the mobs there can get away from ya with a single healer. The lightning guys and the elites for example. Some flurry. Some ramp. Nice to have a druid, shammy, pally, beast, soemthing to toss a heal in there to bridge the gap between cleric heals.

2. Unless your cleric is really well geared i.e. good FT, focus effects, aa's it can keep down time to a minimum to have a mage for rods or a necro for twitch, etc..Really easy to run a cleric oom there.

*3. No "specialty" puller necessary for a lot of the camps and nameds there. Again, Blind/Task best example...all single pulls.

4. Don't be afraid to make your group move. People are pretty used to sitting between Blind/Task and having every mob brought to them. This is cool, but it can also cause unnecessary puller deaths. Have em move up to one mob before named to make pulls shorter if you feel you need it. Some of these guys are really quick and can catch ya, stun ya, kill ya real easy if you not careful.

*although not needed to get single pulls, having a bard bring in pre-slowed mobs is a win.
