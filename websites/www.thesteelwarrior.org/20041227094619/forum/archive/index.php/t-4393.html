/agree with going defensive for a stretch. Note that PE not only enhances ND, but also CA/CS, so you're 'wasting' 2/3 of PE if you don't max CA/CS.

After that, since he's got PB1, I would recommend maxing to PB3 (if you're 2 handing on a regular basis). The only time I ever saw a dramatic increase in my DPS due to AAs was when I went from PB2 to PB3. I'd held steady at about 80 - 85 DPS for the longest time (while working defensive AAs with only PB2 for offensive), and without any other changes, when I plugged in the points for PB3, I immediately shot up to 95 - 100 DPS average (windblade). I was parsing religiously both before and after, and was amazed (and delighted) at the 10%+ DPS boost.
