Lets face it, as warriors, green weapons are not acceptable. You want to have blue or purple ^^

Sword of serenity is a real nice weapon if you can get it around 40ish, I still use mine for the offhand. At 45, it's an easy money quest, and if its an upgrade you should probably go get it.

Thrash Blade is an excellent sword, but is a fun one to get pre 50. Princess is a real monster, and has some of the most annoying attacks to beat down melee ever. Tip: Bring nature protection potions

The TS champ is an expensive sword yes, but most of it's expense comes from the breath of wind drops. They come off of wind elementals in tanaris. It takes 4, you can probably knock 20g off the price of the weapon if you farm them yourself.

Other than those, there are no real "good" swords in that level range that aren't random drops off of mobs, expect to be camping the AH if you can't get your hands on any of the above.
