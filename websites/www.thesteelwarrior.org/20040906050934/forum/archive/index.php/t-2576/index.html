Kaesorn
Basically they're saying there's two different versions of the red scabbard. The first one you get is only used to combine the two hilts and blades to make the epic. Making the epic the first time gives you the second red scabbard.

AFTER you make the epic, you can make another set of 1handers by putting the sword of runes into it and combining it.

Obviously it's not going to work on the first scabbard you get, before you do the final combine. The epic would be more trivial than the rogue epic. However the only way to get the second scabbard is to do the final combine, so that's why it works on one but not the other.
