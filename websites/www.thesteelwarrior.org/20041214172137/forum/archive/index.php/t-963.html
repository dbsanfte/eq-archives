Yea, I remember people calling Dawnshroud 'the new Dreadlands'.

Run back and forth between DSP And ole' DL, at 45-51 you might wanna try good ole' KC. There aren;'t many people in there, but it's not a zone very popular for soloing either, so if you stick to it after a few days you get to know the regulars and find groups without too much trouble.

Crystal Caverns, even though it is a very nice zone for grouping, it is one of those zones that just is not practical to run through and to get to, so sadly it has never been a popular group zone to my knowledge..

Same for Iceclad I guess, used to be a popular camp for the cougar claw earrings at your level 35-40ish.. but again in those PoK portal days it's not practical to get to. We get lazy :(

How's Netherbian Lair? I thought it was kinda hard for the exp, but its right by DSP, so you may be able to pull a group down there and do one of the caves area.

If you have just one healer friend who's willing to go with you, the tent near ToFs tower in Iceclad is a nice cozy camp.. no cougar ever get close to it except one very rare spawn. You can single pull cougars easily with a bow, andI think the exp is good till 40.. but I could be wrong .. been a while ago.
