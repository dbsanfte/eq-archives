It has been awhile so my memory might not be 100% on this.
The general can not be mezzed but can be memblurred. You can charm him but you can not heal him or cast regen on him. If you have some one tank him for some down time make sure they click off there DS and they might want to bag there weapons to keep from dmg'ing him alot from reposte.

Use caution if you use any illusions because some will drop your faction and can make you kos.

You will want to keep your group close to the general if you do not know where he is heading.

I would suggest putting your best tank on the named giants just because they hit the hardest.

If the fight starts to turn bad just kite the mobs for awhile just becarefull that the general doesnt get killed.

Becareful about membluring the general during any fight he tends to think all the giants are dead and roams off to the next fight.

He moves slow just wait on him agroing him and training him to the next fight confuses him.

Some times the general gets a little lost going to the gnome camp but normaly he will eventually make it. We had him take 2 hours to make it there one time the person got lucky that they decided to wait at the camp for there /petition to be answered.

The biggest thing is do not lose the fight it would really suck if that person had to do the 8th shawl tradeskill parts over again.

You all will be fine, here are some pointers though,

1. If someone is KOS to dorfs they NEED to stay FAR away from the general. Not that he will kill them, its simply a PITA to loose aggro then get him pathing again.

2. Nothing in Iceclad that is "normally" there can/will kill the general. The quest mobs can, but it takes a few minuets so you will have time to peel off of him.

3. The easy way to prevent dorf aggro and make this go fast, find the spawn points ( I am to lazy to look them up, but Allkhazam should have them). Send 2-3 people with the general, have th erest run to the spawn points, kill all the crap there, as the general is walking up, then head to the next spawn.

elfdragon
This may seem like a given, but often happens, if you have any warriors with AE taunt, tell them NOT to use it, since it will aggro the dwarf too. Best bet is to have someone with atone or a spell like it up in case someone aggros the dwarf, cause it seems someone always does... the other option if someone aggros the dwarf is to duel the person who got aggro and kill them (have them click all the buffs off, makes it quicker).

If the dwarf needs to be atoned/memblurred you can root him before atoning if you need time to kill the spawns without him wandering off.
