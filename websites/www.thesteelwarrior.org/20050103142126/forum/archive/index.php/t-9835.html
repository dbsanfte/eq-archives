To answer your Question about the best racial combo: In the end there will be no big difference so play what you like most.
Some advantages/disadvantages of some races/deities:

advantages:
Iksar: higher natural regen, AC bonus
Troll: higher natural regen
Oger: Stun immunity
Halfling: hide, sneak, fastest leveling
woodelf: hide, sneak, forage
small races: need no shrink
Rallos Zek deity: allows you to wear Fierce Heraldic armor

disadvantages:
large races: need shrinks often
iksar: cannot wear some plate armor (for example Fierce Heraldic)
iksar,troll,oger: exp penalty

Stats like STR, STA etc will not mean much in the end. With Raidbuffs you will hit the caps anyway.

Best Race(s): Erudites and High Elfs
Why: They have highest CHA. Warrior's need CHA in the end game because of DI. DI makes a check vs. CHA, which means the higher your CHA, the more you will live, the more succesful the raid will be.

/sarcasm OFF

Seriously though. Iksar and Ogre are the best races to be a Warrior with. Iksar because they will always have 54 more AC than any other race, and Ogre's because they have frontal stun immunity; although that can be fixed (to some extent) through AAs.

The weapons I would reccomend getting: Frostbringer (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4851) and Barnacle Covered Axe (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19649). Although you need to be levels 40 and 35 for the procs to work, these should last you until you get your epics (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=768) or until you get a Greatstaff of Thunder (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17984). Before I was level 35 I used a Centi Shortsword (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7260) and a Sarnak Skullsplitter (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=331).

Equipment is easy. Get HP/AC/Resists/STA Items. Don't worry about any stats, just HP/AC/Resists/STA -- in that order.

There. That should last you until you get the hang of things.

GOOD LUCK and HAVE FUN.
