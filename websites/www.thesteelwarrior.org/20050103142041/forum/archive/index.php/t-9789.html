It won't stack with any regen spells (chloro, regrowth, etc)
Won't stack with coldain ring effects, fire elemental illusion, kilva's DS potions
And it won't stack with a couple other more specific higher-end items that are listed on that page, under the description of the effect.

Basically, if any of those spells has higher regen, DS, or atk then it will overwrite what you have. I dont think you getting dotted or debuffed will overwrite that effect, but you wll have 5 more hp/ds/atk than you normally would after the same dot or debuff.

Damage shields come in a few categories.

First there are most of the cast ones (druid, mage, ranger, wizard) then there are another set that includes the coldain rings, Call of the Rathe, the 2nd set of Wizard gimp DS's and Khiva's. There are also some items that go into another category. The Cloak of Icey Shadows from VT is one like that.

If you look at the Stacking page for this spell on Lucy you'll see what it won't stack with. The DS in this case is the 2nd category. The regen and atk parts also give it a bunch of spells with which it won't stack.
