It would appear that the item field that determines weapon proc rates has finally been found.

In this thread (http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=19192)

Basically, go here (http://eqemulator.net/search.php). Find the weapon you're curious about. In the right hand column is a field for "unknown070". The number after that is the proc rate modifier. 0 is default, 2 procs per minute.

For example.

Bloodfrenzy (http://eqemulator.net/item.php?id=OTI3ODguNDAzOTgzMTU=). We all know it procs around 3 times per minute. It's unknown70 value is 55. It procs roughly 55% more than default.

Furious Hammer of Zek (http://eqemulator.net/item.php?id=NzM2NjEuNTQ4MDg2NzE=). Cleric hammer from VZ(tactics). Is 530.

Anyhoo, there's a full list of all 38 items in game with modified rates in the SK thread. Looks 100% accurate.

Warrior-centric ones are...


Darkblade of the Warlord - 30
Blasphemous Blade of the Exiled - 65
