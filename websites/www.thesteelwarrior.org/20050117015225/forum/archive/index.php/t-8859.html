I'd upgrade your weapon first, as previous posters have said (mainhand). Do a search on this site for "goranga" and look at how to get the spiked club for free (possible to do at your level if you are polite). It won't solve your problem, but it'll be better than what you have now (if magelo is still up to date).
I'd go to East K and slaughter lions and spiders. You'll be looking for low quality, medium quality etc hides from the lions and silk from the spiders. Both will sell fairly well, and if you stay away from the giants not much can kill you there.
Rockhoppers and shadeling silks are another option. Look at the EQtraders message board on smithing and tailoring for some tips on where to farm these things.
When you get some pp, upgrade your newbie armor slots with gothic or frosted armor, which you can buy from PoK vendors near the smithing forges for around 100 pp per piece. (Smiths using them to skill up on sometimes sell them to the nearby vendors, so they aren't always available, but they'd be better than what you have--just be sure that you're buying the right size for your race).
You can also get fairly good rings in the bazaar for 250-500 pp.
I'd suggest that stuff not because it's going to last you long, but because if you have that stuff you can group without being a total mana sponge.
then, look for a weapon with a good aggro proc and research components for cultural armor. Keep your eyes peeled for smiths & make friends with them.
