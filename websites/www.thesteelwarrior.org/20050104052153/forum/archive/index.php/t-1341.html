06-19-2003, 11:02 AM
Two things I would consider before bothering with it.

1. Primary only iirc
2. 6 Dam / 27 Delay iirc

For the 25% slow to be "efficient" you would have to proc it immediately or not have weapons that outdamage this by enough to negate the benefit of the slow. By level 60 I think the last part is a big stretch of the imagination. If you proc slow (not resisted) halfway through the fight on average, how much DPS have you lost and how much longer has the mob been alive and beating on you because you are gimping your damage output?

This is why I only really deem the Willsapper and Truncheon of Doom as viable slowing weapons for warriors. They have decent ratios and higher % slows to make up for the slight loss in DPS they result in. IMO, the Wavecrasher doesn't make up for the crappy ratio it has with the slow. It should be used mainly for aggro IMO.

Now, that being said, I always wanted a Wurmslayer as a younger warrior but by the time I could do the quest I had better. This looks exactly like a Wurmslayer so it has some nostalgia associated with it for me.
