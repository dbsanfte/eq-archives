As a barbarian Tribunal worshipper myself, I am pleased with the Helanic Tundra pieces available to wear. Nearly ever piece has very good +hp stats.

6k might be enough to get a bracer or a belt ... check the bazaar for sellers, if you don't have the smithing skill yourself.

Woops -- forgot to add this bit.
The quests provided by Herald Telcha (small sarnak just inside Chardok) are well worth it. The regal band of bathezid is the 2nd step and has a right nice bit of raw HP as well as +5 sta. Additionally, as long as you maintain faction in Chardok, can recharge the damage shield effect by handing it back to the Herald!

Good luck!
