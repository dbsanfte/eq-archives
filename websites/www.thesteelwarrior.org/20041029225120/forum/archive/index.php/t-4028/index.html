Telen
There just aren't a great variety of places to level in your 40's. LDoN is great, get it if you can. Of course you need 4 to go into a dungeon, and you really want at least 5 if you want to win, so you will have to add some other folks.

For Luclin, check out Hollowshade Moor. I think a lot of stuff goes low blue or green around 40, but there are some great drops there and if you do the war it yields some super gear, 16% haste cloak, 11% haste belt, etc. Grimling Forest also has some great drops, but is better for mid-40's.

In Kunark the mines of Nurga are great, but they are more 45-55. Also mid-40's is really the starting point at the earliest. Some super drops there also and good xp bonus. Sebilis really starts low 50's I think, but you may be able to handle the entrance in mid 40's, but you have to get a key.

SolB and Lower Guk are also good, but again they are better in your mid to late 40's. Splitpaw lair is a good suggestion. I haven't been there in ages but I remember liking it there.
