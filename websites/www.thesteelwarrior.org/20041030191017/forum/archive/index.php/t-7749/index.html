A Tear of the Rainmaker is a decent choice over the Blood Earring of Rage. Ornate also might be better, especially the BP. You can likely hit up the BoT tower bosses with even a casual raid force. There is a wonderful guide to the 4 bosses HERE (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=4626). It'd be really good for him to pick up a GBoS as well.

In general, 6 hp = 1 ac = 1 stamina under the cap. No need to raise ac at a higher cost than that, so keep an eye on bazaar deals and get those major upgrades.
