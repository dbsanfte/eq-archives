My advice is:

Don't aug it.

Instead, buy agro augs for your weapons. Improvements to your hat/shoulder/leg slots would be the next things I got.

If you feel you must aug it, a hitpoint aug for the type 2 slot would be your best bet. Then stick the augs you find in the adventures in the t1 and t3 slots. You can remove them later when it's time to upgrade.
