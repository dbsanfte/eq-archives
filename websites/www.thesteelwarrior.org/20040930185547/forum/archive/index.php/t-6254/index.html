There was a GM run event about a month ago in the Arena zone off Lake Rathe. Buy in was 1k. The event was the Fatties against the Skinnies. Large races (Ogres, Barbs, Trolls and Human ) against small races. It looked well thought out until people started showing up. When the event started there were twice as many on the Skinny team as on the Fatty team. Now, for the most part, there aren't too many casters in the Large department. So you can see where this is going. The casters basically wiped the Arena floor with us! It was somewhere around 23 Fatties mostly warriors with a few shaman and beasties against almost 50 pure casters and hybrids. In the end we only killed 14 of them while they won the event. I was playing my 65 shaman and was killed by some level 49 mage! I still remember him sending me a tell after tell afterward asking me how it felt being killed by him. I told him to step back into the Arena with that attitude and no one to back him up and he promptly stop sending me tells. Needless to say, it was a fun time had by all (even us losers). The GM's rezzed everyone that had died and lined up the winning team against the Arena wall and called the forth one at a time to give them a reward of 3k. There were also 3 people from the winning team and 1 person from the losing team picked as MVP's these people got some somewhat nice gems and equipment. The only one I saw was from the losing team MVP and it was a Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement.

Anyway, I think the GM events have been somewhat lacking, but that is due to the fact that just about everyone in the game is more high end now. There are very few actual n00bs. Most people are so twinked out by level 2 that there's no reason to give out anything other than cookies and milk at a GM event!
