Telen
The GM's on PvP servers have for the most part let PvP become a free for all, very few rules exist. I hear from some friends and family of mine who still play on them that they are cracking down a bit recently because of the extremes people are going to in order to "cock block" content. I spent four years on a PvP server and moved off about a year ago and haven't really looked back. The PvP servers used to be enjoyable because the player community as well as the GM's used to enforce some basic rules, such as loot and scoot, no bind rushing, no training, etc. Now it's pretty much a free for all, do what you want, be as lame as you want.

Having said that, a family member of mine, a 65 warrior with elemental and time gear, kicks ass in PvP. Warriors can crit and crip for insane damage with high damage 2H weapons, and can take out casters very quickly. Also, they have the HP's to take a lot of damage and still survive. In order for a warrior to be good in PvP they need excellent gear and they need resists in the 200+ range unbuffed for all resists, the warrior I'm speaking of is closer to the 300+ range unbuffed in all resists.

Warriors with sub-par resists and sub-par gear suck in PvP and will get owned. Warriors with great gear will kick ass in PvP. That can be said for pretty much any class in PvP nowadays, skill is only a small part of the equation. If you don't have a lot of playtime to get into the top guilds and get the best gear, then expect to get your ass handed to you repeatedly by people with better gear than you. If you really want to stay on a PvP server then suck it up and get used to it, and learn to log when you're being cc'd.

Edit: Oh, and yes you need potions. SoW potions are a must. Cure disease and poision. Pumice. Gate potions. Also, buff order is important as is leaving a top slot open for nasty dots so that you can dispell them. That is where the strategy comes in. Basically plan on spending twice as long to level and get aa's or so due to PvP issues, and just get used to it. If you don't like it, change servers.
