I made a post about a month ago here (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=8277) , where I stated that I was going to attempt to start progressing my way thru GoD, with a single, non uber group, in order to basically prove that it could be done. I've been requested numerous times to keep people informed, and that's pretty much the first thing I get asked in chat now too, so there is apparantly some interest in how it's going. Here's monthly update number 1.

We decided to go with the group of Warrior, Shaman (both me), Bard, Rogue, Cleric, Enchanter. Of course only having 3 of those characters existing already, the others needed to be created. Currently our Bard, Cleric and Enchanter are all 51 and are in the process of finishing up Run 3. Fear not..we're not slacking here, and it took an entire month to get 3 toons to 51/3.

The cleric is outfitted in full Skyshrine quest armor because I had a shitload of that stuff rotting in my bank from our HoT farming days. The enchanter isn't far behind...he's got extremity pieces of Thurgadin and Skyshrine stuff. The bard we just rolled up as RZ, which makes his gear extremely easy with my 250 Human smith.

Besides Claws of Veeshan faction work, we also finished the cleric epic last week, and did the bard epic last nite. We kinda got lucky on Cleric camps, he finished all his piddly camps in one weekend, then it was just a matter of spawning Ixblat and pretending he was a challenge, smacking around the overking (dude is such a pushover now it's unbelievable), and shakerpaging Skyfire for shards to spawn Ragefire.

Bard epic wasn't bad either, took us a little over a week to get the bard piece from Phinny, and in the process we found out that the goddamned wizard staff that dropped 3 times in 5 kills isn't MQ'able in any way that I can figure out. So, after a month, i'm pretty happy with our progress. We got our characters to a useful level to group with our 65's, we're in the process of finishing up Run 3, we've got arguably the best 2 epics in the game on our level 51 characters (Dance of the blade is sooooooo nice it's not even funny), they're geared appropriately (3k mana on a level 51 is damned decent), and we got all the crap out of the way, so we're ready to head on to 65 and get cracking on the GoD stuff. Here's what we've got planned for the next month --

-- Get the twinks PoJ flagged, basically so we can get them BoT and Tactics flagged before we can get them into the zone free at 62. We need to make a quick stop and farm up some Ornate for them so we can stop tributing spell Focii

-- Then from there, we just need to get critical AA's out of the way, like Spell Casting Mastery and such, so that we can start looking at doing GoD content. I suspect that in just over a month's time, we'll be ready to start working our way thru GoD. I haven't had time to research the zones or the progression yet, so I can't get too detailed on what I expect us to accomplish, but I should have a better idea in a month.

And, from where I'm standing right now, I just hope that WoW doesn't come out before I finish this project, because as much as I hate giving SoE my money, I'm actually kinda having fun in EQ again.
