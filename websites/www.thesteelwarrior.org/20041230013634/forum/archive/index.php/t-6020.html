There's actually a little middle ground. Do ferocity first, it affects both. Also, if you're not an ogre get stalwart endurance. Not being stunned = more chances to attack/proc.

Specifically about ambidexerity, wait until you're done with the 100ish defensive AAs and ferocity/SE. WA will probably prove out to be a better aggro investment, and you might have better weapons (DW or 2handed) by then.
