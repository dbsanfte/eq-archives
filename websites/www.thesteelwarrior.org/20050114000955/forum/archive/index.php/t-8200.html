I don't have a whole lot of experience but I can see a obvious suggestions I should make. You need to get rid of the SCHW... those are taking up a good slot where you could put some FH Gauntlets, or better which will give you a big boost to both your AC and HP. Selling those should get you to about 45k, and you can spend 6-8k on the gauntlets then.

Use some of the other money to buy some haste back. I'm not sure what the good haste items are now, but Spiked Seahorse Hide Belt would do you well, and you could sell the girdle. If you're using tribute haste this is a different story.

Another slot to replace would be your neck. An Amulet of Mummy Wrappings would be decent, for maybe 1k. I'm not sure what else there is out there. I would hit up your guild trade-skillers for some Bloodmetal Earrings of Engagement. Those would be a good upgrade to both of your ears.
