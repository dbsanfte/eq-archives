wandor
The best advice anyone can give you is to level up. Not only will you get innate increases in HP and AC due to defense skill, but you will have a much easier time getting plats. You might consider doing some LDoN to get Death Embracer's out of Mistmoore. The items are 150 pts each so they should not be too hard to get. Once you can start saving some plats, I would recommed either purchasing or working a trade for some Fierce Heraldic armor. Those items should last you a long, long time. Your weapons should be fine for now. When you get some levels you should work toward getting a Copper Hammer of Striking and a Sword of the Bloodsworn. Also at 55, I highly recommend getting a Greatstaff of Thunder - the best tradeable weapon you can get for the plats. They often sell for 100pp. If you where on my server I would give you one!

The only immediate changes I would make to your gear are your earrings and 1 ring. Get another 6/65 ring and try to get a couple player made 35hp/25mana ears. Bag the gobby ear for later use.
