As a cleric main I find it funny how some of you prefer to pretend that playing a cleric is far easier than playing say a warrior.

From my point of view it seems clerics have atleast as much to do as warriors and probably more.

Prepping:
- Clerics are buffing (DI, Vie, BoR, KM, Virtue)
- Warriirs can afk

Deaths / Wipes:
- Clerics are rezzing and rebuffing
- Warriors can afk

Coordination:
- Clerics rely constantly on 5-8 other clerics to perform within seconds
- Warriors more of less take turns working meaning the reliance and hence need of coordination is several orders of magnitudes less.

So, sure you can bot a cleric and be part of the chain - but then you likely wont be the one doing the actual work of rezzing, buffing and coordinating the heals - thus giving you the impression that its dirt easy.

Mind, I am not complaning - but as a main cleric that actually does my job I dont see many classes that are busier than I during raids. Pullers and raidleaders - definitely. MT for long stretches of trash, certainly. But thats not full classes - merely selected individuals.
