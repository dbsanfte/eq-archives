Sorry, to address your question more accurately, you basically cannot buy or single-group any dual wield weapons that will outdamage a windblade.

Ignoring the BOC and SOD, you have only a few choices. A splitblade of destruction (14/19, tradeable, from Drunder) paired with an ornate stone battlehammer (13/19, tradeable, from hard Rujark LDON) is probably the highest DPS setup that you can buy or single-group at this time. You should get about 90 DPS with this combo fully hasted. That will come close to windblade DPS, but is unlikely to exceed it.

The only way you are likely to improve on that is to spend about 5300 LDON points to make yourself two of the 1-handed weapons at the Wayfarer camps, 16/19 and 17/20 I think. This combo would give you about 115 DPS fully hasted. This would exceed a windblade, but not a Greatstaff of the 4 Winds.

As for augments, if DPS is what you care about, then yes, definitely get vengeance augments or damage augments. You can expect to gain about +0.1% DPS per +1 ATK. If you don't have it already, then one of the best ways to gain 10-15% DPS is to buy a couple of ST keys and get a prismatic weapon (theoretically 1-groupable) to swap in for the proc. This assumes you are nowhere near the worn ATK cap, so that you get the full benefit of primal avatar.
