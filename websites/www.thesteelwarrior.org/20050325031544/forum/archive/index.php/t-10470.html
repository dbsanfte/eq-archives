I'm not into the late game yet, so I don't feel as if i'm the guy you want commenting on most of this stuff, but I can say what defense does.

Each point in defense increases your chance to block, dodge, parry and supposedly reduces critical hits done against you. You can check it out yourself by not wearing any gear with +defense and observing your dodge/block/parry % in the skill book window, and then equipping anything with +defense. The bonus per point is pretty small, but from what I hear, gear with +defense on it is pretty common, and they give big bonuses too. I imagine it'd all add up to an amount noticable without parsing easily, but whether or not thats worth sacrificing stats or HP for is not something I feel i'm eligible to comment on.

Finding the AC cap should be easy, if there is one. Just unequip all your items and put them on one by one while noticing the increase in damage reduction (you can see damage reduction % value by moving your mouse pointer over the AC value in the character screen). I think it will behave linearly to a certain limit which should be the AC cap. Either way the graph will start to flatten out at some value where the damage reduction gained due to AC starts getting small. That should be the first cap.
