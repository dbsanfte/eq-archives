Because I was really curious, I decided to see how high a casual player could get without raiding, using both LDoN and GoD since LDoN is considered a godsend for non-raiders. I was surprised to see just how high they could go.

First off, this is based on the idea that everything listed in Ciae's list can indeed be gotten by one group without raiding. I'm hoping Haas can prove that right or wrong.

I went ahead and stuck the Adventurer's stone to 100%, since I'm getting the max capable stats.

Maxed out hp AAs, added augs. Here's the Magelo (http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=1034967). ~8k hp unbuffed is pretty darn nice. I think the GBoS can be one-grouped by now.

I couldn't think of a better helm (other than Rallos Helm). Ciae listed the Miremuck one, but since I don't know for sure where it's gotten I left it off. I like Iksars, so I used one) With a clicky bramblecoat, you can get this guy's AC up to over 1700 (maybe mid 1600s on others). I chose the AC augs from LDoN for those slots also, instead of just looking for highest hp.

Maybe someone can suggest some better items for a LDoN/GoD hybrid? I'm not going pure hp, but high hp with a good AC as well.

I ventured into GoD today, for the first serious effort I have given it myself.

I 4 box my Warrior, Cleric, Chanter, and Monk. I have the basics, but it's utility I lack. For the longest time, I wrote off GoD due to the inconvenience of it all. The run there sucks, I lack utility of things such as snare, evac, group invis, etc. After today, I'll learn to deal with the convenience issue.

I marched into Barindu today, and went for the Sewers. From what I read, Plant hit the hardest, but things were single pullable, and didn't run. I'd like to think I'm a solid tank, so I decided this trial would be my best bet. Originally, I figured I'd just go in, see if I could stay alive, and after that see if I could get some reasonable XP. I got in, and I was surprised that once slowed, the mobs were cake at best. The only death I had occured when the chanter got agro and died on tash. I went on to tank it unslowed and live just fine, regardless.

After a half an hour or so (yea, my dps is somewhat lacking) the named spawn. Kri V'lak, to be specific. I figured "What the hell? The mask is nice, and my monk could use the staff." Now, I really had no hopes of actually making it to the named. From what I hear, most people just invis/paci to the named. I can't do this. Lack of group invis, and the fact that I have to move 4 characters at once just doesn't appeal to me. I decided that I had the time, and could use the XP, so I cleared to the named. Surprisingly, the only trouble I had was the unslowable abandoned workers. They hit like mack trucks.

All went well, and I got to and killed the named no problem. The whole deal, including the run to Barindu (see invis mob cause a death otw), took me roughly 3 hours. Now, 3 hours for the 1 or so AA didn't seem amazing, but the fun I had, combined with the chance of loot (Kri V'lak dropped chains arms, which rotted), and the XP, made it worth it in the end.

I'll definately be going back, and once I figure out how to reliably deal with mobs that flee, I will be headed to lair for a few items I need. My only regrets about GoD, is I didn't explore it sooner.
