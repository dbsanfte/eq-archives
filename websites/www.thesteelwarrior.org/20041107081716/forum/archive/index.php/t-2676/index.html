Fair question, my own humble opinion on EQ posting:

1) If you mis-type it/mis-pronounce/don't do your homework the flames will come (aka think before you write, re-read what you write before hitting post, a rule of thumb "When I re-read this did it say "exactly" what I meant it to say?").

2) Generally if you wish to get a succinct and polite response to a question the question must be presented politely and succinctly.

3) If you get flamed probably the best move (if wish to avoid the flame to continue) is not to type one of your own. "Suck my balls" well...thats kinda not helpin yer case.

4) As is true in life and business, you may be a darn smart guy with loads of charm and personality but if your presentation is not good with the communication mode you are using then all people see is that side of you. *hint* this is a WRITTEN forum...its not personal to Slammer but man he did not present well in that thread.
