Aurthur
Depends on which nameds you're referring to. In my experience there are 4 different 'types' of nameds in Ferubi:

1.) Nameds with PH's -- These mobs have actual placeholders and have a chance to spawn if that PH is consistantly killed. Examples of these are Hexxt N`Vak Tkan, Pxet Vzanki Kzan, Hexxt Pzik Shziaak. My favorite place to camp for nameds is just south of where the east and west routes converge. It's where you make the turn either to smith rhondo or Zum Votal. There are 4 PH's and 2 'master' spawns you can pull with 1-2 groups (1 group would need to have seriously crazy DPS to pull all 4)

2.) Nameds that just *shrug* spawn for some reason -- These would be the various Pxet Elite XXXXXX's. There are 4 places these guys spawn, and I've never understood how they spawn. Basically, every so often, if you're in the area, they'll spawn. I've seen them be up when I walk through, and I've seen them refuse to spawn after clearing their area for 3-4 hours. They have no PH that I can determine.

3.) Triggered nameds -- Namely, Smith Rhondo and the Weaponmaster. Raid combat, all the way.

4.) Raid nameds -- These would appear to have the standard 'raid mob' respawn time, require a decent raid force to take down, and the only two I'm aware of in Ferubi are the Packmaster and Zum Votal.

Hope that helps.

The named you list are on my map, but again I'm not sure if each one has 1 PH or multiple PHs. We just kill the entire room they're shown to be in.

Using this map:

http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/attachment.php?attachmentid=376

I've found the best place to camp for nameds is just south of the room labeled 4 (at the inverted T). The PH in 4 is the center spawn on the north wall (Hexxt Pzik Shziaak, drops the 36% haste augment and some other stuff, archer mob).

To the east, where the hallway suddenly does a sharp dogleg left (enroute to room labeled 6), is another static PH (Hexxt N`Vak Tkan, archon mob, hits for 1500's but not all that hard).

In the room labeled 11, the SW corner mob is the PH...not that you'll be able to pull it w/o clearing the room prolly anyway (Pxet Vzanki Kzan, bug-chick).

The room with the Elite Brawler is extremely difficult to pull with a small crew...tons of roamer dogs and mobs along the walls...coupled with a stairway leading up into it. That plus the water on the sides make it a mighty tough pull. Beware. The Elite Hammermaster's room, you're probably going to pull anyway, so it's a bonus.

The Rav Ptvin PH is a wandering dog that wanders the north-south passage above the area marked 3. The Hexx Gkon PH is in the SW corner in front of a pillar of the room marked 2.

Unfortunately, I've never had the chance to observe exactly which spot is the PH for the named in 12...but I've heard tracking reports when they were up (when of course we weren't pulling them).

Hope this helps...I really like this area, though I really wish there were less wanderers and a slightly safer spot to camp.
