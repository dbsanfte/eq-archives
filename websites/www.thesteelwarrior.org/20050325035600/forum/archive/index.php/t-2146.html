IV is easy, and you don't need a wall of ogres. Here's how:

1) fight your way to around behind the IV. There is a short stone wall right there. Get everyone possible behind it.

2) Have an enchanter repeatedly cast Tash on IV.

3) Have the MA create a hotkey with the /rsay message "I am BLIND".

4) Assign someone to keep the MA targeted and cast cure blindness when the MA indicates he is blind.

5) When all the above are done, have the MA walk up to IV, put his (the Tanks) back towards that low stone wall, and hit Defensive, AE Taunt, and auto-attack.

6) Have the raid engage the IV in a furious beatdown.

7) When the MA is blinded, hit your special hotkey. When you can see again, move up a step if you need to. I have never needed to, but you never know.

Using this method no one but the MA gets hit or blinded. It really is a pretty easy fight. If you zerg your way from zoneline to the IV room the whole raid can be over in maybe 30-45 minutes.
