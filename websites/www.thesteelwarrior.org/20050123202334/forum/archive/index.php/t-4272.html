11-14-2003, 11:12 AM
Each skill mod, dodge, parry, riposte, etc when parsed by itself makes a very small difference in the stats of the parse (there is a difference, it's just hard to see and takes a ton of parsing to see it and remove the statistical variance). But combined, they do make a noticable difference. This means combining different skill mods, because mods of the same skill do not stack.

That said, I can't tell you that aug'ing your items with defensive skill mods will gain you more damage mitigation than you would gain by simply aug'ing your items with AC.
