Evangelist wasn't a mob, as I recall, I think you can melee out of rampage range, and I'm pretty sure you have to tank him while adds spawn. The adds didn't stop spawning as far as I know. He also procs a knockback/dispell, so make sure to have a clicky up top and try to corner him somehow. Shrink was still all weird at the time, so I couldn't corner him and we had to do some bizarre version of wall tanking. We mezzed the casting mobs and mindwracked them while just killing off the golems, loaded up Inny with tons of dots and just whittled him down. It was a long fight. Good luck with it :)
