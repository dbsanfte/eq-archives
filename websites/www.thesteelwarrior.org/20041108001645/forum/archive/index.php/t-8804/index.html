Sassinak
Well, in general, you can have the following buffs all at once:

Hit point type one (Bot9 or Faith or Heroism or Resolution)
Symbol (Kazad or Marzin or Naltron)
AC buff (Shield of Words or Bulwark of Faith or Ward of Gallantry)
---NOTE: the above 3 lines are covered by Temperance or Aegolism or Virtue
Focus line (Focus of 7th or Focus of Spirit or Talisman of Kragg or Shield of Arcane or caster self-buffs)
Hybrid buff (Divine Vigor or Divine Strength or Brell's Aegis or Brell's Mountainous Barrier or Brell's Stalwart Strength or or Spiritual Brawn or Spiritual Strength or Spiritual Vigor or Warder's Protection or Strength of Nature or Strength of Tunare)
One attack buff (Primal Essence or Primal Avatar or Ferine Avatar)
One haste buff (these will stack if they have additional effects, but only the best haste icon provides haste)
Plus stat and resist buffs (generally no stacking issues with those)

Phulavit
Hey all~

I started a thread about this at Allakhazam a ways back and really got some great responses on how to maximize your hps....

Here's a summary of how to get the best hps for your buck:

Primary HP/AC buffs:

Group "A"

1) Symbol of Kazad (Cleric) 910HP (costs a dot)
2) Ward of Gallantry (Cleric) 54AC
3) Protection of the Nine (Druid) 618HP/32AC

or

Group "B"

1) Virtue (Cleric) 1405HP/72AC (costs a dot)

To which you add Shaman buffs:

2/4) Focus (or Focus of the Seventh) (Shaman)
3/5) Talisman of the Boar (Shaman)

And then one of the following:

4/6) Brells Stalwart Sheild (Paladin)
5/7) Spiritual Vigor (Beastlord)
6/8) Spirit of Tunare (Ranger)

These buffs are all listed in "stackable order", ie. You can't mix Kazad with Virtue...

Also you need to consider that any spell from the Cleric line of "Symbols" will only last an hour or so, while extended Virtue will more than double that.

I hope that clarifies things a bit..

Cheers,
