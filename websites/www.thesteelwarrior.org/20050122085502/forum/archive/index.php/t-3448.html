You can't go wrong with putting vengance on an item. Heh. More ATK is always good.

BUT ... the funny thing about LDoN ... it's bringing back the usefulness of regen.

The style of the adventures (lots of crawling, etc.), along with the damage output of the mobs, really does bring back the effectiveness of things like Fungi Tunics.

So the regen augment is also a great idea.

But, Enduring Breath is still a favorite effect of mine. Heh.

My advice ... if you're planning on spending a LOT of time in LDoN for the time being, put the regen on the pants.

If you're planning on using these pants in PoP encounters ... put the vengance on the pants.

If you're tired of swapping out for a fishbone earring or whatever your other EB item was, then go with the enduring breath.
