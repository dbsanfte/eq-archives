Here is a decent list for crit gear and where to get some of them. I haven't gotten any of it yet, but am really just starting to work on it.

Head - Lionheart Helm (+2% crit and +2% to hit) - rare recipe, collect mats and pay someone on server to make for you, otherwise if you can find a green Helm of Monkey, you can get some agi.

Shoulder - Valor, Strtaholme Militia Shoulderguard (if you are willing to wear on peice of mail, +22 agi and 9 STM) - UBRS off of Warchief Rend Blackhand, Shoulderguards random drop in Strat, or you can wear green plate of monkey such as Commander's if you can find it.

Chest - Valor (some agility) , PvP superior chest (+1% to crit), Chromatic Chest (10 agi) - Valor I am not sure, PvP chest is rank 8, and Chromatic is epic quest

Waist - Omokk's Girth Restrainer (+1%) - Quest in LBRS

Legs - Eldritch Reinforced (+1% and agi) or PvP superior Leggings (+2% and some agi) - Eldritch is DM drop, PvP is rank 8

Feet - Ribsteel Footguards (10 agi) or Battlechaser Greaves (13 agi) - Ribsteel is drop in BRS i believe, Battlechaser random drop in BRD, both add agility

Wrist - Battleborn Vambraces (+1% to crit and hit), Vambraces of the Sadist (+1%) - Battleborn drop from Rend Blackhand, Sadist drop from Timmy the Cruel from Strat/Scarlet

Hands - Reiver Claws (+1%), Gordock's Handguards (+1%), Stronghold Guantlets (+1%) - Reiver is drop from General Drak in UBRS I believe, Gordock's is quest in DM, Stronghold is recipe drop, get someone on server to make for you after you collect mats

Neck - Mark of the Fordring (+1%) or Emberfury Talisman (+1%), Onxyia's Tooth Pendant (+1%) - Fordring is quest starting EPL, Emberfury drop from General Drak I think, Onxyia's tooth Pendant is self-explanatory

Back - Eskander's Cape (+1%) - Drop from Epic Mob in Blasted Lands I believe

Finger - Painweaver Band (+1%), Magni's Will, (+1%), and Julio's Band (+1% to crit and hit), Quick Strike Ring (+1%) - Painweaver drop from UBRS, Magni's Will is alliance quest from King Magni, Julio Band is Faction reward for being exalted with Alterac in Battlegrounds, Quick Strike Ring is either a MC drop or off of Onxyia

Trinket - Blackhands Breath (+2%) - quest starting in LBRS, ends in UBRS

Hope that helps.

I also wear a Wanderer's Hat of the Monkey because it looks cool

couple typos

Eskhandar
Breadth

and clarifications

-Valor BP drops in UBRS off General Drakkisath.

-Eldritch Reinforced Leggings drop off Prince Toltheldrin in Dire Maul. he's the end boss of the west instance, located in the library. he doesn't go hostile until you kill the demon dog Immol'thar, who you can't kill until you kill all the elementals around the 5 pylons in west.

-Emberfury Talisman drops off the Pyroguard Emberseer in UBRS. he's likely the first boss you'll fight in there, he's pretty easy.

-Gordok's Handguards quest becomes available after you do a North wing tribute run without killing Captain Kromcrush. just go up to Kromcrush and he should have a quest ! over his head. the item you need is located in a chest by Toltheldrin. the chest is unlocked after you cap the prince.

-Eskhandar's Pelt does in fact drop off Lord Kazzak in Blasted Lands. basically the fight is a shootout - you want to kill him in 3 mins or less, otherwise he starts throwing out 1000 DPS dots on the whole raid.

-Quick Strike Ring drops in MC only off several of the bosses.

also, I recommend Blackhand's Breadth to all warriors. I got the Mark of Tyranny before DM came out and I regret that decision. if you need +dodge/defense/armor trinkets, you can get several. two are in DM - one's a quest that involves a warrior-only book turn-in in the library, and the other drops off Immol'thar.
