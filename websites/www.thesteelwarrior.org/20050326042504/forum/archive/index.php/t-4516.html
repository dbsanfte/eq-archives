We usually do him with about 30. We do it as a ranged fight to stay away from his AE Charm. MT, RT, and a Bard stand under him and fight. Our tanks get to 400ish Magic Resist with GoD and Bard song and don't have any trouble with charm.

Healers/Debuffers stay as far back as possible. Then break out the Bows and the Nukes. And NO pets allowed.

Not sure of the exact range on his Charm. My monk gets too close using his 100 range Shurikens but the other monks are fine if they are using Shissar Fangs with 170 range.

Have the tanks lock him down and then burn him down. Takes 15-20 minutes depending on raid size.

We fight him. In melee range.

We just time the firing of his AEs. It's actually a simple fight once you get his AE down pat.

Position him right, and then do what I like to call the "cursed dance" or the "snake waltz."

Take him out easy.

But to the original poster, I'd do what the person above suggested ... look for XTC and go from there. The Sraa bosses really are sort of a progression of tactics. Knowing how to beat XTC, and beating him with ease, will invariably help refine you for High Priest and Cursed, who will get you ready for Emperor.

Good luck.

Sraa raids can be a blast sometimes.

Other times, eh, it's just snake, snake, snake, snake GOOSE
