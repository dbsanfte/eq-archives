Callon
Okay, maybe my pullers did something screwy... but the spawn times of the cats closest to LMM seemed different than that of the corner cats tonight.

We cleared the cats on the sides, then the ones on the steps near LMM and those back right by him. At least I'm almost positive that's how we cleared them because the pullers would have had to work at it to get the ones on the steps before the right side cats.

So after we killed the sides on the ones up by LMM, we went in a were about to pull LMM when i noticed the left side cats were repopping already. I decided, let's kill the left and right side real quick then we'll pull LMM before the ones by him pop and we'll be good to go. But instead after we killed the 2 on the left side, the steps repopped. In fact most or all of the cats by LMM apparently repopped before the right side. So are they on a faster timer than the sides? I'd assumed they would all be on the same timer.

Then our monks seemed to be having a hell of a time splitting the last 2 cats by LMM. Finally since both those cats were blue cons I said just pull the damn things. Blue cons are mezzable. We'll lock them down and kill MM. This turned out to be a mistake. They are mezzable in theory, but in practice our chanters had a hell of a time. The cats were tashed and malo'ed and they still very frequently resisted. Once the right side popped 2 more cats, giving us 4, our chanters were about out of mana already from the many resists they got. Sigh.

We killed LMM before we wiped to the damn cats, getting no flags. Suicide runs were able to get the loot for the winners.

Next time I guess I do this like our first LMM kill. If any cats are accidently pulled with LMM as happened the first time, I'll have the entire raid but me kill the damn cats while I tank LMM.
