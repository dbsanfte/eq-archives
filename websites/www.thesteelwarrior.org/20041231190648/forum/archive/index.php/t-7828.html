Well, you DO have to get 12 archetype AAs before you can get AE taunt. I'd suggest finishing up ND, then getting CA1 and CS1 then PE. AE taunt is very nice, for sure, but it isn't as constantly useful as more hp and taking less damage.

Also, with the number of procs you have I'd suggest spending 2 AA in WA1 very soon. It's a great return for 2 AAs... It should add approximately .6 procs per minute, which would increase your aggro very nicely.

As for gear, you seem to have the idea in general, though your ac seems low to me. I suggest using a 1ac = 1sta = 6hp formula, until you're capped on stamina. For specifics, I'd try to replace that helm, hopefully with ornate.
