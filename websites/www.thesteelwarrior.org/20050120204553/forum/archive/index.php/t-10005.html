My guild and I just fought Krekk for me tonite.. and to tell the truth, it was a huge learning experience. I wasn't really expecting some of the stuff he threw at us. But maybe this will help some of you who haven't gotten a chance to give him a go.

We had about 28 people, all pre-elemental, and we've cleared VT a handful of times. We cleared his area, moved in and I spawned him. We were expecting a 2k hitter with a knockback / stun AE. Which he does have, but also every 30 seconds he procs 1500 dd / stun / knockback on his target, and every 90 seconds a 2k dd / slow on his target, and every 180 seconds a 2k dd / stun AE feign death.

We got him down to 20% on our first try. The FD was a killer for us, thats when he started eating people. The 2nd go round we tried to position him on the wall around the building area he's in... and he went right through it to the other side, summoned me and wouldn't follow back. Had our positioning been better on our first attempt, we would have won.

So there's that, he rampages and flurries, but not very often. He is slowable, probably a partial but not sure. Max hit he got on me was 2235, min hit was 350. His melee really wasn't that bad. was a bunch of 500-800's and then every once in awhile he'd get a lucky 1k-2k shot. I was buffed up to 11.8k hps / 1950ac.

The Whirlwind slice spell was fairly easy to resist with a bard. My MR was 500 and I resisted it probably 3/4 of the time.

I did notice that his agro was really screwy too, we had a hard time trying to keep it on one person. Every time I got knocked back he'd just turn around and whack some poor defenseless rogue or beasty. I think next time I'm gonna take some advice I read and pull him to zone in, will at least provide a good solid wall that he wont get pushed over / around / through.

I dont know if I read anywhere what happens when you fail on Krekk. So if anyone's worried that they might lose their stuff or whatever, dont be. I was kinda worried too, but after we wiped on the first try, I invised up and walked up to Krekk, and he was non-agro. I hailed him and it started the script again, and he proceeded to give me a quick death. So it appeared they instituted a char flag so you can re-trigger as much as you want.

Spell info:
Crushing Blow (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5604&source=Live)- 1500dd / knockback single target
Whirlwind Slice (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=6119&source=Live)- 1250 dd / knockback / stun AE
Death Strike (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=1068&source=Live)- 2k dd / slow / agro reducer single target
Whirling Smash (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5556&source=Live)- 2k dd / stun / AE FD and drains 1k enduro / mana

Hope this helps at least someone.

A few fine folks came out to help me do Krekk recently. We had 21 total with 2 clerics, no druids, and a couple of shamen. The fight was much easier than I had expected.
