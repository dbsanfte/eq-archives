Well you're iksar so you don't have great charisma.

But, plague rat tails drop in newbie yards, along with a lot of other things like skeleton tibias and king snake skins and stuff like that.

I've found Qeynos newbie yard to have the widest variety of amazing newbie drops. But all the others have them too.

The Plague Rat Tails sell for 1 plat each.

The other stuff sells for a few gold each. My level 8 wizard racked up 40 plat just on this stuff while levelling.

Also, you're on target with the pelts market. Hides, spider silk, and other tradeskill items sell well and sell frequently.

That's probably your best bet for good money at 16 ... tradeskill items like spider silk and LQ pelts for leather padding that smiths use and HQ pelts for tailoring.

Paludal Caverns has quite good money drops Iksargo, and you can often go there at level 16, but (apologies that this is off topic):

Warriors are far and away the most gear dependent class in the game. Most warriors that get to mid / high levels these days are equipped by people who already have masses of plat from a high level character.

After a certain level (and well before you can get access to decent gear from raids), you'll almost certainly need much more gear than you can afford, get discouraged and give up.

If I had a friend starting EQ for the first time, sadly a warrior is probably the one class I'd recommend he avoid more than any other.
