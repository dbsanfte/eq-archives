I have a pretty nice ranger alt. What I do in groups usually is root or harmony pull if outdoors. I have am3 and eq so I mostly use archery unless I am in an area where its not working.

I can also do CC if there is not an enchanter around with root. I also snare and if there is an enchanter in the group and we get a lot of adds I will try to root them with earthen roots which is no damage so they can mezz easier. I also can tank when the excrement hits the fan and the tank dies or I can off tank if needed.

It can be a lot of fun to play.

Also I will buff the group with attack buffs.
