A mob is only considered in one small spot no matter how big it looks. For example when you get summoned on a mob you always appear in the same area, which is where the mob is, as far as the game is concerned. Many mobs, especially ubers, have a melee radius that's much bigger than the AE taunt range.

So you can hit AE taunt and not be in range of where the mob actually is and fail. There's also Z-axis issues, i.e. where the game considers the mob to be is too high for your AE Taunt to reach.
