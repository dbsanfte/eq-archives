If you're just considering auto-attack agro, given 2 weapons with the same DPS, the faster weapon will provide more agro. Theres seems to be a bonus of hate applied each swing, and it looks to be very small.

That said, if you're a tank, you have plenty of tools at your disposal for managing agro. Here's a few tips to keep in mind.

Abilities with agro increasers

1. Taunt - single target taunt
2. Shout - encounter targeted taunt (taunts ever mob in the encounter)
3. Taunting blow - taunting attack
4. Hold the Line - self-root with encounter taunt on each attack

Abilities with agro increasing side effects

1. Mangle - the debuff adds extra hate as a side effect
2. Wound - Dot adds extra hate over time from damage
3.Concussion - power drain effect adds extra hate
4. Shield bash - stun, who likes to be stuned, I don't, neither do mobs
5. Assault - Can be used as a really weak pbaoe taunt
6. Call to Arms - Buff your group during combat to make mobs like you more, they love it
7. Rallying Cry - yet more group buffing goodness that mobs love so much
8.Toughness - really weak, but its a buff, mobs uniformly hate even self buffing

Hero Oportunities agro

1. The mangle self HO is great agro.

Agro decreasing abilities

1. Hunker down - use it manage a tank switch or lose agro if you aren't MTing

Really, if you are losing agro easily, you are doing something very wrong. Taunt, auto-attack, Shout, and Call to Arms pretty much makes me a wanted man in each and every encounter. On really tough encounters with many opponents, spamming group buffs is great agro when combined with your taunts.
