The absolute cheapest are mage-summoned arrows. Next cheapest would be made in a fletching kit using silver tips, wood shafts, large nocks, and round fletchings. Trivial at 182 fletching.

You can improve their range by using small nocks and parabolic fletchings for a very small additional fee. Increasing the damage rating, on the other hand, can dramatically increase the cost of the arrows.
