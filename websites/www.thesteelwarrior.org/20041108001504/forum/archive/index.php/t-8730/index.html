Phantron
2H weapons outdamages equivalent DW counterparts in virtually all parts of the game, and you only need 1 weapon instead of 2. That alone is enough incentive to use 2H weapons.

Anyway Greater Sinister Strikes is there to balance the fact that 2H weapons get higher damage bonus than 1H weapons. At level 70, a delay 28 2H has a damage bonus of 41, up from 35 at level 65.
