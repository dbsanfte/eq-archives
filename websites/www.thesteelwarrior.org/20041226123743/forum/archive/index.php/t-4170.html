A number of people have posted on these boards that PoP mudflation is entirely responsible for Paladins being able to tank like Warriors and cast spells with near abandon.

Actually, it can happen whenever there is enough Plate/All items with mana, FT and focus effects in a tier.

While these are probably not well constructed (I don't know Magelo good enough) take a look at what was possible pre-PoP, no AA's, and at level 62.

Warrior - Lucky Dogg - 5512HP 1575AC
http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=853928

Paladin - VeryLucky Dogg - 4904HP 1568AC 4608Mana and nice FT
http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=853934

No matter how much a Warrior raids and farms, nothing will give him aggo control as good as a Knight.

Knights CAN have very nearly Warrior HP and AC while having powerful spells and plenty of mana. The gear is out there.

With LDoN, even a non-raiding Knight can can purchase with Adventure Points and at the bazaar sufficient equipment to be a powerful tank and a powerful caster. 69AC BP's, 48AC Legs, FT augments, Narandi's Helm, Earring of Solstice, Jurglash's Legacy, Spiritmaster War Beads, AoTC, and KIE will do the job nicely.

I agree that SoE shouldn't make radical changes based just on PoTime mobs.

But even at lower raid tiers and using LDoN:

Warrior = survivability + aggro control

Paladin = near equal survivability + suprior aggro control + spells + mana + mana regen

I don't advocate a nerf. But let's not try to pretend there isn't any class imbalance. Now, without a nerf, give all Warriors aggro control up the the standard set by Knights and real (not more inferior pseudo-spells) compensation for not having a spellbook.
