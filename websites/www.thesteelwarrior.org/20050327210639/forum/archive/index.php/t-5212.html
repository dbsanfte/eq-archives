It really depends on which type of zone you like to do, at 37 you prob have more options open to you than any other lvl.

Dawnshroud peaks is doable at 37 and should be good exp if you like outdoor zones. Maybe check some of the other outdoor zones in luclin also.

Tower of frozen shadow is a riot. So is lower guk. If you want to lvl fast prob go to kedge keep.

Lvl 37 is when you should be establishing regular party members durring your play time. Get to know a few clerics, and enchanter/bard/shaman and some other regular group members with similar play times and get to know them. This is going to be far more rewarding than anything else in the game from my perspective.
