Precision Strike – A Stormhammer guild?

Maybe you’ve heard, maybe you haven’t, but either way you’re going to hear it now.

Precision Strike is moving to The Rathe!

A lot of thought has gone into this move, and there are quite a few motives that ultimately led to this decision. Let’s travel back in time a little over two years ago…

SoW was a fledgling guild with very high standards. Very few had faith that the guild would grow and flourish with such strict recruitment policies, but boy were they wrong. Twelve people at maximum were invited each month, never more than that. I personally had to wait as an applicant an extra month when I joined because the previous month (January, 2003) they had already invited twelve people. I didn’t mind, I knew it would be worth it in the long run. A month passes, I’m still camping VT shards and my Emperor ring and I’m finally invited. I finally discover what a real, skilled guild is all about.

Cut to now. A few of our rival guilds have left the server over the past year. Europa /mourn left and now have very little competition on their new server. Leviathan left, and now they are, against all odds, amongst the top guilds in EQLive. Vexare left, changed their name to “Plan B”… then “Prodigy of War” (how original) and finally Vindictae. I think they’ve disbanded or something.

The current content that we’re fighting is uncontested, the zones are not overcrowded, and the churn of the server has slowly come to a standstill.

So why are we leaving?

1. This server is pretty much dead. People are leaving the server everyday, and not enough are coming to it.

2. There is no recruitment base. There are very few people on this server who are up to our standards, and we will not lower them in order to fill out our ranks. On top of that, it is almost impossible to cross-server recruit and get people to shell out $40 a month. Which brings me to the next point.

3. We are not getting our money’s worth. Is this really worth $40 out of your pocket a month? SoE is marketing this server to the level range of roughly 35-50. The majority (80%) of active EQ accounts highest level character is somewhere in that 35-50 range. See all of the GM events lately? Oh, that’s right, you couldn’t go because you’re too high level. Oh look, that level 49 Berserker was awarded a PoTime BP.

4. LFG times. Do you like sitting with LFG above your head for hours on end? I know I sure don’t.

5. A new environment. Change in general is good. There will be a better variety of players to recruit, without having to poach.

So to summarize: We can make recruitment easier without having to poach. We can save lots of money. We can meet new people, hopefully enjoy it again, and get pretty much the same customer service we (now) receive on SH with the new petition system. Hmmmm… that’s a tough decision.

Sorry to post such a lengthy explanation but at least you have info from the people involved. I was a Stormhammer member from the day the server opened, and the ONLY reason I never left SH was the fact that I was a member of a great guild and had great friends on the server. Yeah, $40 was a bit more to spend, but at the same time, I had people that I did not want to abandon - and I know that same feeling is/was shared by a great number of people from my guild.
