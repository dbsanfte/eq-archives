Bert has laughable hitpoints. Its his rampage and other nasty tricks that make him dangerous. Set up in one of the back tunnels where it ends in a fork. Have your monks pull Bert into the tunnel and sacrifise a ranger with weaponshield to pull him back into the cubby. Have your ramp tank pop him as the ranger pulls him back to you. Pop disc, ae taunt, count to five, and have the raid push him into the corner to get pummeled. With luck your clerics have half a brain and your druids/beasties have mgb to pop off ae heals. You'll have your flags about five minutes after you engage him.

All the above are good suggestions. I would recommend WS ranger tank while debuffing Bert (resist, dex and atk debuff).

The WS ranger will more than likely die from Rampage.. but thats ok.

To give you a better idea of how to get aggro, here is Bert aggro list should be:


Order of ENTERING Bert's Aggro list
1. WS ranger
2. RT/MT (doesnt matter)
3. RT/MT
4. all the other tank classes (in case the RT dies)
5. debuffers
6. Healers
7. everyone else.

If you can set this and keep the MT and RT alive, you win.

a piece of advice for the RT, its better to have a defensive warrior to take Ramp. Bert hits max 1957 and 1300ish after dish. Use Disc before you engage as you wont have time to react and press disc when you realize you are on ramp.

Also, CC team be rdy for repop.

We do it a bit different. With our strat, we have everyone except the MA, RT and Suicide Shammy go into the tunnel and set up healers in that cubby back to the left (nearest to Bert room). The Ramp tank (we use a warrior) pulls and the MA AE taunts so ramp is set. The shammy is immediately slowing on the pull.
Because Bert's hitbox is so freeking huge, you can NOT pull him to the corner, MA/RT have to go out to meet him. As soon as the MA calls attack, ALL the melees run out and PUSH Bert into the corner closest to where the healers are behind the wall (to avoid the AE). Once Bert is in the corner, its like doing AoW. As long as melees stay at max melee (which is half way across the room) and healers keep the tank up, Bert is cake at this point.

btw: since Bert is such a pita, to ensure sucess we use a cleric rot and chanter rune rot on MA and well as a druid rot on ramp tank. May or may not be overkill but we're three for three on Bert using these tactics. Good luck!
