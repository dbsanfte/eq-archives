So, I've been spending a lot of time in Droga lately, because my guild is working on chardok faction for rings/cords, and so that we can epic up the brand new clerics a bit easier. In my bank I have loads of Sashes, Ice Fetishes, Earth Totems, Depredator Blades, and Hoodlum symbols, as well as items from each of the level 60 named in there, so I decided to do some digging and find out what they're for. I must say, I was rather impressed with the results, for the most part. Cave Lord Sashes and the Earring off the King nets this --

Darned nice. It seems that some chimp down at SoE headquarters fubared the Intelligence stat and put it in as wisdom, but hey, I still don't know anyone who would turn down 75 mana and FT 2, even if it does have wisdom on. So after questing up a few of those for the chanters and whatnot, I figure there must be items for other classes, and yep, sure enough, 3 ice fetishes and the staff off Cialin grants you this :

Sacred Mantle oF Stone
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM NO DROP

Races: All Races

Well hot freaking damn. A quest item I'd actually use, 2 of them in fact. What a great idea. The next item I came across was the hybrid earring --

Aged Earring of Aptitude Submitted by: Velsor Secarne
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM NO DROP
Weight: 0.5 Size: TINY
Slot: EAR
AC: +5 Str: +10 Sta: +5 Wis: +6 Int: +6 Disease Resist: +7 Magic Resist: +7 Poison Resist: +7 HP: +70 Mana: +35
Effect: Flowing Thought I
Classes: Bard Shadowknight Paladin Ranger Beastlord

That's right, no more must you make the choice between a Bloodmetal earring or a Solstice earring, this is the best of both worlds, one helluva hybrid item I must say. Which brings me, unfortunately, to the last item, the Pure melee item.


Soft Leather Bracer of Gore
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM NO DROP
Weight: 1.4 Size: SMALL
Slot: WRIST
AC: +5 Sta: +8 Agi: +8 Disease Resist: +10 Poison Resist: +10 HP: +70
Effect : Vengeace II
Classes: Warrior Rogue Monk
Races: All Races

Pardon my french, but.

WHAT THE FUCK?!

You have got to be kidding me. Every goddamned set of classes in the game gets a completely USEFUL piece of gear, and we get this mediocre at best piece of completely worthless shit. Every warrior rogue or monk I know is either wearing Cultural Bracers (ac 25, 55hp, 5 all saves) or bracers of Benevolence (AC 17, 8 sta 25hp, 12 mr/cr) and we get this utter garbage. I don't even know where to begin with what's wrong with this picture.

Every other item off these quests gives 3 saves, not to mention that the caster items give an entire 12 points more of saves than the melee items do. 5 ac is completely bogus for the level that this quest is at, not to mention the fact that it gives exactly the same amount of HP as the Hybrid earring, and the earrings has other stats that make it a better piece of gear. Every other item is definitely equippable for a pre-heavy raiding player, because FT is infinitely useful, but we get 10atk....I get that off my goddamned goblin earring. For me to even think about equipping this bracer, it would have to be upgraded to 20ac OR Vengeance 4 or 5 and even then it would be a lateral upgrade at best. No one is gonna give up 20-30 modded AC for 10atk, even the monks and rogues I group with don't wanna drop the BoB's for this piece of shit.

Still don't think pure melees have been totally forgotten in this game? Think again. No one who designs gear down at SoE has any idea what to do when it comes to us, and we're taking it up backside for it.
