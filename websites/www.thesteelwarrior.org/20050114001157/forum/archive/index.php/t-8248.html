2 handed weapons generate more aggro initially, if the DW setup doesn't proc early. Seeing as early on in a fight is where aggro tends to be most sketchy, a solid 2hs can often do well on short fights due to the reliability. On longer fights, procs WILL happen, and WILL generate more aggro over time.

I'd go BoC mainhand and FSSOY offhand. FFSOY should have around the same aggro as a CHoS, and it's hands down cooler. BoC is by far your highest aggro weapon of that list.

Well, some buffs give aggro and some don't. Guard has been very poorly rated by most people, but rune 3 gives 200ish aggro per proc. Not all debuffs are high aggro either. For example, frostbite, an ac debuff, gives decent aggro as well as obsidian shatter, another ac debuff. However, fiery curse gives absolutely no aggro at all as a fire debuff, more like malo than tash.
