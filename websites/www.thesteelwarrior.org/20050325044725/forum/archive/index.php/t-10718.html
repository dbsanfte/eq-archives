Well, all the ikkinz trials and raids use the same map, just with some parts closed off depending which one you're doing. Ikkinz 1 is the easiest ikkinz raid pretty much, and it's really just a case of making your way in, clearing as you go, and killing obvious named mobs who drop parts of keys, or sometimes loot. Iirc, the order is

Kill 3 priests 1 at a time, to get key and 1 loot
Kill 2 defenders 1 at a time to get to custodian
Custodian is a little tougher, always hits for his max and flurries unslowable, also mem blurs or so, he drops 1 loot
Guardian is behind custodian, rooted in a pit, slightly trickier fight with mobs activating and deactivating throughout, plus some 4.5k dd+FD proc or something like that. Once you aggro him he will nuke you even at zonein, caused some chaos our first time.
Spirit is final fight, really straightforward, but kinda cool mob model, keep an eye on it

That's my recollection, been a while since we've been there. It's not too complicated but there's some variety in the fights.

in ikkinz 3 there are 4 mini golems which give you an emote when you kill them hinting what class needs to kill them, something like "a true veteran" = war or whatever.
