I suggest the following:

2x Earring of Engagement
Get rid of that neck item and get something with some AC and hps on it, the Amulet of Mummy Wrappings is an excellent cheap item, its 12 ac and 45 hps IIRC.
You could also farm a Flawless Steel facemask from SG. Its the same AC as what you have, but it also has 55 or so hps.

Then...I suggest doing LDoN to upgrade your armor slots. I suggest doing EF and getting the armor from there. While the NRO armor is better, it seems that EF would give you better bang for your buck. NRO is good if you need to upgrade one or two slots. EF is good if you need to upgrade a lot of slots. Also, by doing EF advs you will get the points for Anger Augs.
