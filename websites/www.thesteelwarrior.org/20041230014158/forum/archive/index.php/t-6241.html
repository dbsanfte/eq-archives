I consider myself very well versed in how aggro works in EQ, but there are many on this forum that know far more. Lately a guildie asked me a question that I found I couldn't answer.

It's quite simple.

Mobs like to debuff people who are lower on the hate list.

The mob always faces the person it is casting on. BUT, if you assist the mob at that point, you will STILL get the player that is at the top of the hate list.

Debuff spells include roots, snares, malo, slows, mezzes, AE versions of these spells, and many dots. Any of these might be cast on people who are lower on the hate list. But from what I have seen, mobs save their pure damage spells for the person who's at the top of the hate list.

So don't fret if a caster mob turns away from you to cast something. That's just the mob AI trying to stir things up. Assist it, and you will still get yourself on target. You're still at the top of the hate list.
