Keep your shortblade of lightning. Enjoy it at level 46. It's seriously good and should be able to hold aggro quite well over what most other level 46 types have.

Mainhand Frosty when you're sure you want a lot of PROCS to get aggro.

Pick up a black blade of tormenting or a barnacle covered axe CHEAP in bazaar when you can save some extra plat, and then if you EVER find yourself in a position to REALLY need a ton of proccage swap in the other proc weapon.

But until you start using Defensive and Evasive a lot, your reliance on procs shouldn't be too pronounced.

If you're having trouble with aggro, it's not YOU. It's your group. Talk with them.

Make sure they're not standing on the mob's toes. Make sure they're evading or feigning death or NOT chain stunning. Make sure they're not casting snare before the mob gets into the camp. Make sure they're not trying to cast slow 5 times after taking 5 tries to debuff the mob. Make sure they're not chain casting nukes and not even bothering with concussion.

AND, above all else, don't get too frustrated with bouncing aggro in the planes. It happens. A lot.

AND, remember that if you're grouped at level 46 with a bunch of level 55+ toons, yeah, aggro is going to be HARD to hold. Levels and level-based DPS contribute a lot to that.

IMO, your weapons are just fine right now. So play some and ENJOY them. I wish SBoL existed when I was your level.
