Ok, I know it's not a big deal anymore to most people on this list since you're way beyond it, but my group just killed Lodi for the first time yesterday! :D (about 10 low to mid 50's)

We got agro too fast initially and it cost us a wipe on the first attempt. The second time we had another mixup, but we recovered and took him down. The main problems were we lost the slower early - before slow landed - and we only had a 55 and 53 druid for healing...the druids couldn't quite keep up with the heals the first time around...he was down to a sliver of life when I died, but then the casters died in a few seconds after I dropped. The second time it was fairly close, but we won. (druids at 20m, and me at 1 bub)

This was the biggest mob my warrior (or any other character I have) has faced and it was a blast. I got the map, so now I'm off to get the eyepatch of plunder.

One thing that really surprised me is Lodi didn't mitigate my damage very much. I didn't run a log, but I know I did much better dps then I normally do in The Grey or PoP zones. Not sure if that's normal or not...

By the way, thanks again for all of the great advice I've gotten from this board!
