I have anger 3 in my splitblade, and with having my epic already proc hate I didnt think putting another hate proc in my epic would be benificial.
It is always beneficial to add another hate proc. In fact, ideally your weapons would have their own hate procs PLUS anger3. The splitblade essentially has no aggro of its own. Ideally you would start with a much higher aggro weapon than a splitblade, and THEN add anger3, putting you much farther ahead. That's why other people in this thread suggested that you move the anger3 augment to your epic, and dump the splitblade.

The weapon delay has no effect on how often you proc, so don't let that concern you. You will average 3 procs/minute, no matter how fast or slow you swing. (with 305 dex and weapon affinity 5)

Your offhand procs half as often as your mainhand. Use this rule of thumb to decide which weapon to put in our offhand.

Yes, get the copper hammer of striking and the sword of the bloodsworn.

And, you weren't *entirely* misinformed about low delay for a mainhand weapon, it's just that delay influences non-proc hate of the weapon, particularly the mainhand. But once you get to the level where you can use good proc weapons and your DEX increases, the delay issue becomes a progressively trivial component of your hate generation. The posters here are simply pointing out that you are well past that point now, and the CHoS and SotB have fairly low delays *and* good procs.
If you have 30K then on most servers you'll have a fair bit of coin left over after getting these two recommended weapons.
