I'd go Intel chip. Both AMDs I've had, and 2 others that I've seen friends with, have all burnt themselves up under normal usage (no overclocking or anything, case fans all over the place). My Intel chips, with nothing more than the CPU fan alone in the case, not a single overheating problem ever.

I'd also go Nvidia. I've never had a problem with the drivers nor performance. I've had ATI drivers error out during installation and take the entire operating install with them multiple times. Also, almost everytime I read about someone complain about their video card (spanning many different games and gaming web sites), most of the time it was an ATI.

I think ATI cards have a sharper picture. Nice image quality and vivid color. But they don't have the consistantly high frame rates, ease of driver installs/upgrades, game compadibility, and stability that I've seen using Nvidia.

These are just my experiences though. A lot of people have had wonderful results with ATI and AMD. Myself though, getting burned once is one thing... but personally seeing 4 AMDs fail in the last few years, reading constant complaints of games not working from ATI users, and having to reinstall entire operating systems multiple times cause of failed driver installs, has swayed me away from them.
