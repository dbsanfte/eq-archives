Well, here's the deal on the SoD. I currently use BoC/SoD for raid shit, but for XP groups, I bag the BoC, swap the SoD over to main and pull out the ol copper hammer for more DPS. The other warrior in my guild uses Copper Hammer/Sword of the Bloodsworn, and I have VERY little aggro trouble. I keep aggro over him probably 95% of the time (maybe 1 in 20 mobs he ends up tanking), altho he is a very close number 2 (ahead of everyone else, if something happens to me, mobs turn right to him).

I think the sword of the bloodsworn proc is slightly less taunt than Force Shock or Anarchy. One Anarchy or Force Shock proc will keep aggro thru a slow from my shaman, one planar strike proc doesn't always keep aggro after a slow. I've seen it happen twice now where his Bloodsworn procced right away and then the mob turned to the shaman after the slow. I've never seen that happen after Anarchy from me or a Force Shock from either of us.

So, is the SoD an upgrade to the 2 droga weapons? Absolutely. Is it a 200k upgrade? For your average joe, probably not. There's not a lot of things left that I could buy that would be real upgrades for me. Spending 200k on a weapon that gives me a little bit more aggro was a good investment based upon my gear and the returns that droppable upgrades would give. For a lot of other people, that's probably not the case, in which case buying an SoD would be foolhardy at best. The Sceptre of Destruction is still top of the line....but if you don't have top of the line gear in every slot, then it's not a worthy purchase anymore.
