Hello, I just had an idea that might help our situation. I’m an lvl 54 Warrior on Kane Bayle. We engage a mob and then a enchanter cast a slow or a debuff of some kind and the MOB without a second thought goes over to hit the enchanter with no consequences. That is what kind of ticks me off is the fact that a mob can disengage me with out having to pay a price for turning his back on me in a fight. There should be some kind of penalty to a mob that is engaged with a warrior and turns his back on him. Yea I know that a mob has less ac on his backside but the mob is not paying any serious consequences for turning his back on us to go beat on another group member. Maybe if warriors got a free Backstab for good damage along the lines of a Crit and the potential for some truly serious damage along the lines of a rouge Back Stab ability to make the mob think twice about turning his back on us when we have it engaged. This would be in addition to our normal Offensive output. Of course we could not do this at will but after we have established agro and then if a mob decides to leave our melee rang the ability should take effect. This ability would work like an extra taunt in the fact that If we lose agro to a pure caster we would have a chance to get it back from damage and if that did not work then we could fall back on our ability to taunt.
