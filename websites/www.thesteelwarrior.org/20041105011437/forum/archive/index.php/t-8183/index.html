Doesn't look too bad at all, honestly. 1ac = 6hp = 1sta until sta capped unbuffed. Go to the bazaar and have at it! When you get to 50, replace the Wavecrasher with a Copper Hammer of Striking. It'll be more DPS and at least similar aggro. I'd suggest working towards an anger 2 or ykesha augment as you go, and sell it back when you can afford anger 3. It'll take a while to get that many points, though. 55 is minimum level for ornate, as I recall, which will last you a long time once you can get it (even if it's not resellable). Otherwise, it looks like you're on the right track. Just keep it up and soon j00 will be ubah 4 realz.
