In Ssra, you can set up rampage very easily, due to the rampaging mobs being rooted (save Emperor).

What you need to do it quite simple. take Mr. MT and Mr. RT into the room where the rooted snake sits. Bring and enchanter too, for mem blur. Then the both of you (MT/RT, not the enchanter, cannot stress this enough) start shooting arrows at the snake. Make sure you don't have any bard songs on. Also make sure noone except MT and RT are in LoS (line of sight) of the mob prior to engage.

After a few stacks of arrows, the both of you run up to melee range of the mob to trigger a rampage. Everything should be working fine now, so at engage, run up with the MT and start swinging to keep the rampage on you, but not get aggro (not that important since you're gonna get healed anyway).

Basically, during a short period at the start of a fight the mob decides who it hates most based on who's done damage so far, wether a bard is singing in range, heals dropped on MT etc. that 'Rampage list' stays 'fairly' constant during a fight, rampage attacks are applied against the next most hated target(s) on that list to the MT (who should, in effect, head it). Typically if your Main tank goes down the Rampage tank gets promoted to MT and the rampages shift down to the next person below him on the list.

What people try to do is stack the rampage list so that the people they WANT getting hit (usually warriors or other plate tanks for their high durability) take the rampage.... Choppin gives one example of that above....
