Getting to 8k unbuffed ghetto style (without elemental gear) takes time. You need to get all the 40hp augs you can from LDON. Plus you need to go back to Tipt, not just for a shot at the bracer, but also for the two 30hp augs that drop of the cragbeast mother. Then work the rest of the pre-KT trials for their plate drops.

Then try to do the new VP, KT, or hope something comes out in OOW.

I am a firm believer that people can skip the elementals to advance, just like they could skip VT and hit the elementals, but it ain't the easy route.
