How about just allowing us to solo anything dark blue. :D

With the incite line to allow a aggro over time effect and the ability to add anger procs to weaposn via LDoN adventure point spendings, increased proc rates for those anger causing procs via the Weapon Affinity line of GOD AAs, etc, the ability to grab and hold aggro is much closer to where it needed to be than ever before. I think that this was a primary concern and frustration for many of us warriors for a very long time, and these things have helped with that quite a bit.

In addition, the previously mentioned splitting of disciplines over a couple of different lines is very nice since it allows you to use a couple of them back to back if you have the endurance to do so. For example, say you are going to fight some big boss mob. You can start out with and Incite for your aggro over time and then use Furious to offset the initial brunt of the attack while the mob is slowed and the heal chain gets started. Once that wears off, you can then go right into defensive/evasive/stonewall. This adds a new dimension that was never available before when all of the disciplines were all on the same timer. You usually will not stack like that, but it is very nice to have it when needed. You can also click off a discipline and start another if you ever should find the need.

We still can't solo for squat, but they have provided some nice changes that make doing our job, which is tanking and holding aggro, a much more reasonable task...epecially the holding aggro part.
