/shield is best for situations when your healer/slower has grabed way to much agro and you cant taunt the mob off them.

A good hotkey macro is:
/assist
/shield
/g shielding %T

assuming you have the mob targeted you should end up shielding the person getting bashed, you do have to retarget the mob afterward, but if it saves your group some downtime then its all good.

The range on shield is really small, you basically have to be in mellee distance of the person you wish to shield for it to fire properly, but the good news is, if you aren't in range then you havent wasted it, you can just hit it again.

i actually prefer to set up 2 /3 separatehotkeys rather than the assist method which i find can be glitchy sometimes.

Whenever i start a group i have one for cleric/sham/ench or their equivalent.

also, dont shield just because a mob is whaling on caster, try taunt first, if that doesnt work, then shield. its useful to tell the group before you start that you are setting hotkeys for them and tell them if they are getting hit to stay put and not run around like headless chickens. for it to work, you really have to be standing immediately adjacent to the caster facing the mob.

as the slowers normally get aggro most commonly, when i pull i will just run straight to them and stand next to them so if they do get aggro they are already next to me.

your group will lvoe you for it. its definitely saved a few lives afaik.
