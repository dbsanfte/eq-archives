And the best part is: These are all smithed with a trivial of about 262 (GM Smiths rarely will fail) and the parts are supposedly very easy to obtain... So if you're like me and agnostic, or poor, or unguilded... check out OoW and try to pcik up some Magnetic Gear.

I checked out EQTraders, bought a couple pieces of the armor in the bazaar yesterday, and had the guild smith craft me a pair of the vambraces. Total cost of 11k for vambraces that are basically ornate comparable, I was fairly pleased.

As far as the upgradeable versions I did some checking into them as well. To upgrade them is also a smithing combine, and the items are attunable, so if you have someone else craft them for you initially you can't wear the armor until after you do the upgrade since you won't be able to hand them back to your smith. If the smith fails on the upgrade you lose the piece of armor as well. Overall a very nice addition, I'm very pleased with this stuff so far.
