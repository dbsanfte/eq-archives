I've heard efficiency is now very difficult now as you cant avoid or even cure the zone wide mana tap AE. Back when you just had to time the bard DA song it was by far the easiest trial.

It really depends on your group makeup. Judging from your magelo you will need to have 2 CHers to even try the bosses of Subversion (2800 hitter) or Ingenuity (2k unslowable hitter). If you have a few great crowd control people fear should be doable (use furious on the initial onslaught or you wont survive it). If you have another tank and good dps destruction would be the best bet.

When you first try the trials bring along a rez-capable toon that you can park outside the trial room to rez you in case of wipe. Most spots outside the trials are safe. Subversion is the exception because that one has a roamer that can path by and aggro.

EDIT: If things go downhill and theres no way to win just quit the expedition and run like hell for ~90 sec. Sometimes you can live long enough for the expedition to boot you out to safety.
