Hola-

Just to summarize then, cha works as follows with DI (please correct if this is wrong/inconsistent):

-caster's cha does not matter
-target's cha WHEN DI IS CAST*** decreases chance of failure as cha increases.
-cleric AA converts fails into partials.
-each level of cleric AA = ~1600pts healed for partial
-target's and caster's cha when DI gets triggered does not matter***

That sound right? Re-read the cleric thread, but there wasn't any good summary of results. Note that the above is consistent with what I've seen while MTing (base 40 cha...).

*** = I've seen it suggested both ways, but not enough data to support either. I typcially drop cha buffs after DI hits, and (empirically) haven't seen any drop in effectiveness. Fairly small sample size tho.
