Okay, this is probably old news that everyone but me already knows about. But I took a little trip to the Abysmal Sea the other day with an alt, going spell shopping for a couple of different alts. And when I jumped on the boat to leave, it headed into Natimbi (sp). So I'm thinking, uh oh, better stay on the boat and not go ashore anywhere just to be safe since the toon was only level 54.

The boat docks near the merchants, and then heads out. While standing in the middle of the boat not one, not two, but four mobs aggroed on my character as we're headed out of Natimbi. Of course they were underneath the boat so I couldn't even target them to fight. And of course because I'm on a boat, I can't cast any spells. And of course the boat was moving just slow enough to make sure they stayed in contact. So there I am, running in circles on the boat in hopes of avoiding contact long enough for the boat to zone, while my HP pool is dropping lower, and lower, and lower, with no options available other than jumping into the ocean and ensuring I die at sea. Finally, just before the boat zones, I get whacked and my corpse drops through the boat to the bottom of the ocean. Lovely. I eventually bribe a Necro, after making my way back to Natimbi via Nadaria's landing, to corpse summon me. A very nice end to an evening, thanks SoE.

So here comes the rant part. WTF is this? How dumbass are they at SoE to design an expansion where just standing on a boat gets you killed and your body dumped out at sea? Don't they do any fucking testing on any of this shit? Did they fucking intend the boat to be a death trap? Is that one of the "trials" of GoD I keep fucking hearing about? The "trials" are really figuring out what dumbass untested piece of shit SoE has introduced into the game and how to figure out where the fucking "land mines" are? No freaking wonder their crap is so broken all the time, they can't even get the simple stuff right! Yeah, I know someone is going to respond with suck it up, it's a new cutting edge expansion, expect the unexpected, blah blah blah, but I'm sorry killing people for standing on a boat is just dumbass. That's not challenge and that sure isn't freaking fun.

Okay, got that off my chest.
