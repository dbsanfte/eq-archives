PoP crafted bows are not cultural, I mean, Nightmarewood and the other plane's bows can be crafted by any fletcher, of course that the skill needed varies.

Im a 202 fletcher and I use a Nightmare bow, along with Silver tipped arrows (wood shaft, small groove, parabolic fletch) and its a nice 3/150 and hits PoP mobs (if the arrow is not MAGIC, it wont).

I started fletching long long ago just to make my arrows and ended up taking it to 202, I often make bows for guildie's or friend's alts now, mostly Darkwood bows and they have a nice ratio (can get to 19/41 if you want to, or 25/68 if you want more dmg). All I can say is that is nice and I enjoy it, never made much of a profit tho.
