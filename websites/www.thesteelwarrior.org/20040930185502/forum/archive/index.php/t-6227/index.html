KoGuiN
Yes sol ro is better xp than tactics. Once you are ready to roll go up the left stairs, clear you way up. once you are up those stairs you fill face a huge room. Pull the 2 mobs up the very fisrt stairs you see on your left upon entering the room. Go up those stairs and you will be at a fork, either go straight or go left. Go left, clear mobs and go all the way up that narrow alley. 2 more mobs are at the end of the narrowed path. Once dead open the door click on the torch and you will be ported in a room preceding some caves. Pull to that room then move up some as you want. Mobs arent lvl 65 anymore in the caves but they die way faster than the flamming ones.
-ANY mob can drop ANY ornate but chest piece. Yes legs do drop.
-There is NO zone out.
-Mobs before porting into the caves have HUGE aggro range.

If you have anymore questions feel free to post :)
Hope that helps
