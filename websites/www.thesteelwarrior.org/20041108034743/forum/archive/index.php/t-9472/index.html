To answer the original question, 8% dodge mod will stack with your shoulders. One increases your skill, the other increases your CHANCE to dodge, which are two different things.

As for the dodge mod, at a time 5% dodge wasn't worthwhile but 8% was because your skill crossed a threshold (I think 8% took you over 200 dodge but 5% didn't but I could be wrong...)
