couple leveling tips for warriors.
1) try to be one of the higher levels in your group especially if you are the only tank. if you are tanking for the group and are one of the lower levels you are going to require a lot of healing.
2) if your groups are melee oriented concentrate on blue cons.
3) pay attention to the mobs you are fighting. you will find some mobs that are hard to kill give the same pathetic experience as some that are easy to kill. select the mobs you like when pulling.
4) if your rate of experience gain is slowing way down look for another zone. some zones are just plain hard to level in.

i admire you for giving away your "freebie" gear that i guess someone gave to you. when i first started i refused to accept charity from any real life friend but would from anyone i met in game. think of it this way. time spent talking is time not spent leveling. if they give you something because they think you are a nice guy then the item will help make up for the time not spent obsessively leveling. and yes when you reach 40 or 65 even you will indeed take satisfaction that you did it without hand-holding. above level 5 by the way you wont receive many gifts anymore.

i gave a 200pp weapon to a level 2 wizard recently because i noticed she had been playing for a couple hours already and had no armor but her trainer tunic. i then put her on my friend list but never saw her again. thats sad. i dont like to see a discouraged beginner. but sounds to me you dont get discouraged quickly :)
