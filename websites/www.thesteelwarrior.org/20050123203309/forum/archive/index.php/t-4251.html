Use the best armor you possibly can.

Don't look at armor "type" ... just look at its stats.

AC, hit points, etc. etc.

Things like Hardened Grey Flesh Sleeves, Spider Fur Belt, Clattering Bone Necklace, Padded Steel Woven Belt, Barbed Dragonscale Boots ... they are all popular items with monks.

But they are all also very usable warrior items, that I have seen a lot of warriors in their 50s use.

The BENEFIT you currently have being a warrior is that you can wear all that stuff your monk was wearing, AND you still have access, or rather a CHOICE to wear plate mail armor when it becomes available to you.

So, in other words, you'll be fine using your monk's old gear. Just upgrade your gear as you normally would have. Everything will work itself out in the end.

Good luck. Have fun.
