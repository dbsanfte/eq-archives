I tried him out this weekend. At lvl 60 with 238 MR I let him proc on me around 100 times.

There were zero full resists.
~ 75% of the procs hit for full.

Since I was tanking him.... I also tried to disarm him (no go)... weapon is magic. We also tried to fear him (no go).... too resistant...
