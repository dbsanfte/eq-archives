The following is what you need to do to ensure that your flags have had the appropriate "oil change".

- When you killed Grummus, did you first go to Myelik outside of PoD and ask "what ward"? If not, go do so.

- This one is gay... when you killed Grummus, did you zone into CoD *from his lair*? If not, you must go back and zone into CoD From grummus' lair.

- After Killing Grummus, did you return to Myelik and Hail him and get the next flag? Try doing so.

- After Hailing Myelik, did you go to the sick bay and hail Elder Fuierstel? Try it a couple of times, since you need one flag before killing Bertox and one Afterwards.

- When you did the Hedge Maze, did you go to Elder Poxbourne in the sick bay? If not, hail him.

- After killing Terris Thule, did you go hail Elder Poxbourne in the sick bay and get flagged? If not do so.

- Did you hail Fahlia Shadyglade in the PoT sick bay? Did she talk to you? If so, then tell her "I will go to Torment" (note: you can NOT say "I will go the plane of Torment" because the game doesn't recognize a "plane of Torment", it is "Torment, the Plane of Pain"). That is the Keeper of Sorrows Pre-flag, after saying that, hail her again for the Tylis Newleaf flag.

- After doing so, go to Maelin Starpyre in the PoK library... he's messed up a little too. First hail him, then say "I have information" then say "I have lore".... then go to Seer, and AGAIN go back to him, hail him and say "I have new information" and "I have new lore".

As others have said, many times it takes a few trips to the seer to unlock memories to get this "oil change".

If you are certain that you did the grummus pre-flag mess and zoned into CoD by Grummus, then go to the sick hut, and Hail Elder Poxbourne, and Elder Fuierstel (you'll get flags every time you hail them), then try to go to Fahlia and Hail her, if she's willing to talk, then say "I will go to torment" then hail her again, if she's NOT willing to talk and you got flags from both Fuierstel and Poxbourne, then go to seer, and repeat.

If you have any additional trouble and I'm on EQ, you can send me a tell at seventh.Frodlin, and I'll try to walk you through it.
