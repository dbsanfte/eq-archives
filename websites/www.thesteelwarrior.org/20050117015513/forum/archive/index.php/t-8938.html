There was a lot of confusion about this yesterday, so here's the truth about the /claim item.

It is called a Frozen Crystal Shard. It is a range item with 2ac 10hp 10mana and 10svF with a 3.0 sec casting effect called Gelidren Aura. Some people got a Burning Crystal Shard, I don't know how or why.

Gelidren Aura adds a frosty particle effect around your character. So far it stacks with everything and it does have a buff slot so grats everyone on a nifty new dummy buffer. You need to have spell effects turned up to at least 50% Opacity and Low Density to see it.
