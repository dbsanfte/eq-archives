Hmm, ok, good point.

OK, with full AA, etc, you get 17% more HP than the raw score on an item, and you get 55% more AC.

Assuming 100 net AC has the same effect as 4% more new HP, then 100 net AC is roughly equal to 440 HP (I personally think HP is more useful, because of spell damage). Call it 100 net AC = 400 net HP.

To achieve 100 net AC you need 65 real AC on items. To achieve 400 net HP, you need 342 real HP. So, 65 AC ~= 342 HP, yeilds a factor of 5.26.
