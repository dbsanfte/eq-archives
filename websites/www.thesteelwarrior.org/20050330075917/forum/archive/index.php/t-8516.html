Sassinak
08-17-2004, 12:07 PM
Dulak's harbor is a good idea, head to the eastern side of the zone for higher level mobs.

Howling Stones would be a really damn good powerlevelling place at 44, the mobs are so weak for their level there, and they start right around level 42-44. But you'll need to make two keys (trivial really, just takes a bit of time).

The "hotspot" listed for 45-50 is Nurga. However, you'll have to invis pretty deep to find the mobs that are good XP at this range. Nurga has 4 tiers of difficulty, and the front 3 tiers are probably too low level for you now.

The "hotspot" listed for 50-55 is Grieg's End. You could try that now or real soon, I'd think. Huge advantage there in that nothing runs, nothing sees invis, I don't think there are any traps anymore, and small aggro/assist ranges.
