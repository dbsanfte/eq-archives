"The reason I asked is I was wondering if the click was like the eyepatch of plunder, and would stack with spell haste."

Spell Haste and Song Haste do not COMBINE when they stack.

What stacks are the OTHER effects from the spells.

A fine example of this is the monk epic.

It stacks with Speed of the Shissar.

I do not get 108 percent haste from the combination of Speed of the Shisar and Celestial Tranquility.

I just get the haste from Speed of the Shissar.

HOWEVER, I DO get the added ATK and Armor Class from my epic click. And I DO get whatever other benefits Shissar gives (I never paid attention much, Vallon replaced it pretty soundly and Vallons doesn't stack with my monk epic).

Anyways ...

A good rule of thumb ...

ITEM HASTE "combines" with either SPELL HASTE or SONG HASTE. Whichever is higher.

Spell and Song do not combine.

Unless it's a specific bard song (what are there like three?), designed to OVER-haste.

Soooooo ...

Ornate gloves ... That warrior belt from Luclin ... the eyepatch ... the monk epic ... the ranger Sky cloak ...

They do NOT "combine" with the haste you receive from Vallon's ... Celerity ... Quickening ... or Shissar ... or whatever.

Even if the buffs are both up at the same time.
