The AE's are designed to make rushing the room and getting set up difficult, so don't rush the room. Set up in Behemoth room before Behemoth wakes up, as soon as the spider killing begins. Put tanks closest, healers furthest, and wait ~10 minutes while the spider groups do their thing. Then Behemoth will have no reason to leave the room, and there will be no confusion related to rushing into the room later.
