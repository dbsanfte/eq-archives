For ALL** FUCKING WARRIOR USABLE WEAPONS that are NOT a Blasphemous Blade of the Exiled, Bloodfrenzy, or a Darkblade of the Warlord the following applies -

For Main hand weapons:
105 DEX = 1 proc/min
205 DEX = 1.5 proc/min
305 DEX = 2 proc/min

For offhand weapons:
105 DEX = .5 proc/min
205 DEX = .75 proc/min
305 DEX = 1 proc/min

This applies for proc augments, spell procs, as well as innate procs. Augmented and spell procs are actually very slightly lower due to a weapon only being able to proc once per swing and innate procs getting the first opportunity to proc. Proc rates DO stack, a weapon with 2 procs will proc at 2x the rate associated with your DEX.

The only weapons that have been show to have different proc rates are BBoE, BF, and DBotW, all of which have a substantially higher proc rate. There are also some caster weapons - cleric hammers, Greatstaff of Power, etc. that also have higher proc rates, but those are useless to a warrior.

** All means every WARRIOR USABLE weapon you can think of. All of them. Even your snare whip, fleshgrinder, primal, frostbringer, wavecrasher, and uber proc weapon_03. All of them. If you find one that is NOT 2 proc/min then please post a parse because those weapons are rare.
