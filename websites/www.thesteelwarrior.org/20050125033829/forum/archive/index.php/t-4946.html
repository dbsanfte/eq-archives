There are actually several options available. If you're capable of Vindi, you're probably capable of Yelinak, who has 2 decent 41% haste items (Gaunts of Dragon Slaying and Yelinak's Talisman) which would both be upgrades to your current gear. That'd free up your mask for an Amy mask or the ole Pirate Eyepatch.

Also, you'd get probably about the same aggro, slightly less DPS, but more HP with a dual-weild configuration instead of the staff. CHoS and the shortsword from Droga each have reasonable stats.

You also may want to unlock Takish armors, the sleeves are exceptionally good, imo...especially with the 40hp aug at the same camp.

Shoulder consider camping for a barbed rubicite pauldron, or if you think you can muster the forces to OT the Guardian of the Seal while you kill his guards, the Strength of the Elements would work.

Another good, and often overlooked, necklace is one I recommend: Torque of Tamed Flame from Sol C. AC: 10 STR: +8 STA: +8 CHA: +5 WIS: +5 INT: +5 HP: +50 MANA: +50 SV FIRE: +20 SV DISEASE: +10 SV COLD: +20 SV MAGIC: +10 SV POISON: +10...I mainly love it because it's a universal resist item.

And, also, again, if you can kill Vindi...you can probably easily handle the 10th Ring event. Yes, it takes 90 mins or so...but the ring and the other rewards usually keep folks coming.

Range item: Nightmare is ok, but if you can scrape up *some* plat you could probably get a featherwood/stonewood which would be as useful an upgrade, if not there are several +50 hp items that can go in the range slot for tanking purposes.
