You didn't look hard enough.
The 40 hp type-2 augment is at Tak camp.
The 40 hp type-7 augment is at Rujark camp.
The 40 hp type-8 augment is at Mistmoore camp.

The augment that you saw in Omnus's magelo was type-7 because he put it in a type-7 slot.

The augment that you need for your boots is type-2 because your boots have a type-2 slot.

You cannot purchase hp augments that go in your type-1 or type-3 slots. You can find them as loot in Gates of Discord, though.
