RZ or Tribunal is definitely a huge advantage (for Fierce Heraldric or Helanic Tundra armor)
Put that in all armor slots.

That leaves:
Finger (2): Valorium Ring of Gallantry
Ear (2): Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement
Neck: Black Plated Collar
Face: Captain Nalot's Eyepatch (quest) or Grimling Contraption (tradeable)
Back: Cloak of Flames
Waist: Heavy Rubicite Girdle
Shoulder: Draconic Pauldrons (quest) or Bonespur Mantle (tradeable)
Range: Idol of the Wavecrasher

note: the neck and range wont be quite full stats but still very good

That's pretty much the BEST you can do.

On a budget, get as much cultural as you can, then fill in with the above when you can afford. Until then, use: fingers-6ac 65hp rings, ears-35hp 25mana earrings, neck-clattering bone necklace, waist-putrid belt of rotten hide, range-runed bone fork, shoulder-elemental blanket(MAG summon).

For your major purchase I suggest either:

CoF + Putrid Belt of Rotten Hide
or
Cultural Cloak + Soulscream Belt (<- cheaper)
