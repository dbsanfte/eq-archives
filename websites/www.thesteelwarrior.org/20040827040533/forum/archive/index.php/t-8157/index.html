Words cannot describe the appreciate and exhiliaration I had tonight. My guild just defeated The Rathe Council! Not only that, but it was our very first attempt at it. All the team work, all the gearing up, and all the work I've put into my guild payed off so much tonight.

"Is it just my screen or did that councilman vanish?"

Vanish? What the hell. I frantically looked around. The script takes time and patience. Having that work you just did to beat him down, get him debuffed, and keep him locked down thrown underneath the carpet for you sucked. Where did we find the Hudini? At his bind point, peacefully regenning his health around the blue campfire. Mind you, the councilman regen very, very slowly. We had to sit there and wait a half an hour for him to regen from 5% up to 100% so he wouldn't annihilate our pullers on agro. The councilmen quad hard and fast for like 3k, and the mezzables retain their strength no matter how much health they have.

It sucked having to sit there and wait, but two hours later, the Avatar of Earth's corpse was at our feet. Rather odd occurance though.
