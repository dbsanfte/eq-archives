charm a kriger once you get up to the top level. the charmed kriger should give enough dps clearing that repops are never a problem. if the fight last more than 15 minutes you might as well call if off anyways. you can corner him very nicely just to the left of his spawn point (facing him). the corner is a bit buggy in me experience at first, and he will try to pushyou through the wall ... if you walk out of the wall when he starts pushing you in, you shouldnt have any more problems with it .... at least thats the way it has worked for me the last 5 or 6 times i've done it.

make sure the chanter lines up the pet so it pushes straight into the corner .. same with shaman if he uses his pet (though it wont live for too long).

if you have your defensive aa's, then at 8k hps i would guess you could tango with him all day with 2 clerics and a shaman to spot heal. after that, its just a matter of the ranger and ench managing the pet if it breaks and getting it back in there. krigers charmed and hasted parse out at around 700dps ... closer to 850 if you give them focus and ferine avatar. at that rate, assuming icestorm has ~400k hps, you should be able to drop him in about 5 or 6 minutes.

repops shouldnt be an issue as the backpop time is pretty long. if you set it up right, it is possible to kill icestorm with full pops and no assited aggro.

in the end its all about placement and mob positioning. he isnt difficult at all if you can get your casters set up outside the ae where they need not worry. he hits for 850 in triples and quads ... slowable ... and spends a lot of his time after 50% trying to heal himself.
