bardeil
I parsed both these combo's at level 56, and found them to be roughly equal. After a lot of fights in PoV, neither combo really pulled ahead from the average of 55-60dps.

If you're going for aggro, you'll get more procs from the 1-handers so I'd use them. As you get closer to 65, the GSoT should start to pull ahead, as it's damage bonus will increase a lot more than your primary 1hander bonus will.

If you want DPS, buy a WMBS. At that level mine parsed out noticeably higher than the others, by 5-10 dps.
