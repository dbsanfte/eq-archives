Eoden
It really depends where you want to take your character. Cobalt bracers fetch a good price because they have always been a pita to get and are very nice to have when you start raiding - shrink yourself for cornering, shrink that fat paladin who always stands infront of you when you are trying to get to a mob.

I finally found a cobalt bracer forsale after 3 months of bazaar searching, /auct wtb-ing and posting on my servers pawn shop forum. Cost me 25k and worth every penny, and thats as a half elf. I was going through a class 6 mana battery every 2-3 days just keeping myself shrunk, now I can keep my whole group shrunk and not worry about recharging. Also great for LDoN groups where there not much space to get around.

Haste on the otherhand will be great now and if you are going to be a non-raiding bazaar equiped warrior a cloak of flames would probably be the only haste you will ever need.

So the choice is between setting yourself up with haste from now until you reach the point of being able to obtain a haste item from raiding, or hanging on to an item that you will regret selling once you reach the point of raiding every night.
