If you solo a lot you might find the regen useful but if you are after that I would suggest one of the lifetap augs instead. This way you damage the mob and heal yourself and it should do more than a Regen 5 aug for you. I would consider the regen aug for solo/farming if you have a regen bp to put it in after a lifetap aug. A 50 pt lifetap aug proccing 2 times per minute will heal the same 50 hps to you if it is resisted once, plus it does 50 hps to the mob.

If you are tanking in a group go with the AC aug. I have noticed (not parsed) that I seem to take less full hits when I broke 1800 AC and then again when I broke 2000 AC buffed. Much more so than the 50hps per minute that a regen 5 aug would heal. If healers are using complete heals on you and you are not with in 10 hps of dieing then the regen 5 is negligable. Cheal is 10 seconds to cast or just under 2 ticks. In 2 ticks the aug has healed you 10 hps.

I have a regen bp I got in Riwii. It regens 17 per tick with a 2 regen aug. It has 25 AC. I have an ornate bp as well at 63 AC. I wear the ornate when tanking hands down. To me the 38 raw AC is greater than the 17 Regen. If I am farming light blues or greens I wear the regen one coupled with a lifetap weapon.
