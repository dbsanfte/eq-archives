I hydra a 65 warrior/shaman combo.

When I need cash, I generally go to enforcers. You may get lucky and get several diamond drops, or you may see nothing at all for a while. There's also a few other sellable items that drop there, a box, a coin, a rock .. all not stackable. They sell for somewhere between 40 and 100 plat each. And then there are the enforcer stones, worth around 9 plat, and the occasional peridot, and ethereal parchments. On a good day I can make somewhere between 500-1000 plat per hour down there, on a bad day somewhat less.

It's not great, but it's easy to get to and it's the most reliable cash I have found.

A guildie, a 65 enchanter, goes to Droga, with a mage/cleric hydra. The cash drops there can be quite good (up to 15 plat per mob), and if you get a decent camp and can hold it there are decent bazaar sellables down there. The hard part for me is that the good camps all have more adds than I like to deal with on my hydra team without friends, but maybe it would work for you.
