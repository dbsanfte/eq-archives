We have been using an unusual loot splitting method lately that no one has had any complaints about. We either do the 'stair camp" in RSS fire side (4 PH) or the "ice camp" in RSS ice side (5 ph). I prefer the ice side but a bard puller/spilter is necessary whereas on fire side my warrior can pull its so easy.

At the start each person choses a catagory from one of 4.

Level 69 Runes
Level 70 Runes
Items
Experience

So if you are in the Exp group you get no loot (unless eveyone passes) BUT you are getting 6percent AA per kill and 12 toons kills mobs in 24ish seconds. So you can get 12 percent AA a minute (on SZ yours will be 20percent less).

Some people are maxed out on AA (Like my cleric has 400plus AA and everything I could get for him is total trash at this point so I no longer keep him in the exp group) but want runes (everyone wants level 70 runes lol).

Although no one has ever pass on a rune, items are passed on often (if its not your class its waste to roll, or like me I have most everything that drops), and when those who choose items pass then it opens up for a roll by anyone.

So like 6 people chose that super exp of 12percent AA per mintue during up time (downtime is low cause with 12 toons killing a mob you need less dps per toon) and then 2 on rune70, 2 on rune69 and 2 on items you have a 50/50 shot of getting a rune70 if it drops and that was your choice. Compare to all 12 rolling for the rune under other systems.

People are very happy with the fact that they can focus in on what they really need.

For example Cill our Mage came 3 days in a row choosing level 70 runes and got absolutly nothing, no exp and not a single item. Then on the 4th day we saw our first level 70 rune, and she was the only person who choose rune70 and its all "grats Cill" with no roll and no one complaining that she got the only level 70 to drop all week.

All and all a very fair and resonable loot system when you have more then 6 in your group.

Also another edge is that doing the ice side (2 romers that can pop in camp) with 12 toons is way easyer then doing it with 6 toons, so doing things with 12 toons does open up your possible targets more, AND you never have to turn LFG people away, people send me a tell and I reply "sure come on more the better, and if you have friends 69 or higher tell them to come also!"

Next time you are looking for group and they say "we already have 6" you say "Hey check out the The DeathWalker 7-18 toon loot distribution system!"
