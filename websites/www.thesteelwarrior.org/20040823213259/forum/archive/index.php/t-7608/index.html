New bug this patch, most likely due to the collision fix. If two characters port in at the same time and remain where the port places them, they'll be on top of each other and one will constantly take falling damage off of the others head. And not normal damage either. I port all over hell while looking for something to do sometimes... I 3-box, one of them being a wizard. Upon almost every port, two of my characters start taking 122 or so damage over and over from falling damage.

I've heard of a case where someone died because of this already, and it doesn't suprise me. If I had hit port, then went afk, I would have came back to two dead characters.

Be careful when porting and zoning. I would assume that this would also happen while zoning into any zone that places all of the characters at the same point. Don't go afk after hitting a zone or being ported, wait for it and move your character off the spot just to be safe.

On a related note, I recently worked on /corpsedrag and /corpsedrop commands which allow a player to drag up to two corpses automatically (one for each hand ). The system can be extended to support more corpses at once if the design team decides they want it; I'm hoping it becomes an AA for classes that tend to do a lot of corpse recovery but please don't take that as a promise this will happen. It was sort of a backup plan in the event the real corpse bug (as described above) didn't have a fix identified. These functions are done and seem to work just fine but we didn't want to give QA even more to do since there was so much going into this update. We'll activate them on Test in a day or two and see how they do. It's pretty nice to just run around with corpses like that, no spamming /corpse keys. Look for them on the next update to live servers (not tomorrow).
