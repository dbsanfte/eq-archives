I always do well for raw cash loot in the grimling caves in Tenebrous Mountains. Out of the way spot so there's rarely anyone there too. Katta Castellum is right next door if you can sell there, or the Vah Shir fort in Grimling Forest isn't too far if you're not so friendly with the Combine.

As a 67 warrior you could put on your DPS set-up and easily clear both caves. Just doing the west cave (I'm lazy) I net about 250-400pp per hour in vendor loot (gems and runes) and 4-10 bricks of acrylia. No cash drops there but the acrylia runes add up pretty fast plus the weapons they drop sell for 4-6pp each. Just bring lots of bags with you, the more bags the greater the profit.

If you are waiting for respawns the wandering alien mobs (Gor Taku, Cral Ligi, Meeda Hune) also drop gems, acrylia and essence of wind. The weapons they drop (spiny) aren't worth carrying out though.

I'm sure you could do a lot better with player tradeable stuff but if you just want some quick cash with little hassle it's an idea.
