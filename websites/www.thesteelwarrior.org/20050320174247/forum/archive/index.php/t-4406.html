I spent 3 years on VZ before I moved to Brell and played both a warrior and an SK. The shammie/sk combo would be a hundred times better. Being able to dark or snare in a pvp encounter is a HUGE plus. Another great combo would be a shammie/ranger combo. The ability to track is a nice perk and they can also snare.

I've played all three of the melee classes mentioned so far and if it were me I'd go with the shammie/SK setup. While the ability to track is nice the SK's ability to FD will come in very handy. There's going to be plenty of times where people will train mobs on you in an attempt to give you an exp death. Being able to FD and have your shammie gate will prove to be invaluable in my opinion. Not to mention the fact that...what goes around comes around. Some numbskull tries to FD train you, lay it right back on him. Another plus would be the ability to summon your corpses. If someone is corpse camping you just make your way into the zone somewhere and summon your corpses.

As far as guilds go..you won't have a problem finding one. Guilds on PvP servers are a matter of day to day survival. Pickup groups are nowhere near as common and you'll find alot of zones you're used to seeing alot of people in are underutilized on pvp servers. Guilds are how you get groups and get things done on a pvp server.

Do what makes you happy but playing the underdog team is highly overrated. Whatever team you choose you'll have plenty of opportunites to get your fill and then some as far as PvP action goes. All playing the underdog team does is limit the zones you can hunt in for exp and make it harder for you to get ahead as far as character progression goes. It's why I left VZ in the first place. Take that for what it's worth.

With a shammie partner doing debuffs getting dark to stick to PCs will be alot easier to you. And if you plan on duoing for exp then having a tank that can snare will easily double the rate at which you level and make plat. Indoor zones usually have better exp modifiers and drops, Guk and Seb being good examples, but if you try hunting there without a snarer/darker you're going to be ass out. SKs also have a certain intimidation factor to them. You're not going to want to PvP every single time you log on, sometimes you'll want to exp or quest. After a couple darks, even if resisted, people will often decide you're not going to be an easy mark and move on. Alot will leave when they get low on life too since they won't know whether you have your Harm Touch ready to go or not. And all that is without factoring your shammie partner in.

TZ/VZ are hard coded by race not deity or class. So a human SK will be "blue" team with your froglok shammie bud. You should have no problems with that racial combination. It's been awhile but I'm fairly certain froggies are blue team. Someone else who still plays there should be able to confirm or deny that for you.
I haven't played TZ for years but VZ is pretty well lighty/xteam dominated...just as an FYI.

In any case, PvP servers can be a blast and I wish you the best of luck.
