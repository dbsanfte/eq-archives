Sassinak
Start a new character to see it. :) Players can go up to level 5 in the tutorial, I believe. It is very well done.

It's basically a Rujark LDON themed map. First it teaches you the absolute basics: targeting, movement, hailing, and attacking. Then you get ported to a cave containing a bunch of NPCs, each of which teaches you about something: spells, disciplines, questing, tradeskills, grouping, swimming, etc.

Among other things, everyone gets a free spell or throw stone disc (even hybrids get a wussy spell), one NPC improves your starting weapon when you hand it to him, and you also get a quest resulting in a charm with something like +10 hp/mana (which is HUGE at that level).

When you head into the tunnels you find kobolds, spiders, rats, etc. They start at level 1 but get higher as you go deeper. Named kobolds can pop who are level 5 or so, and maybe drop loot (didn't kill any). You can bring stuff back to the NPCs and get pieces of basic armor in return, or just sell it for plat. Spellcasters can buy the rest of their level 1 spells within the tutorial, and there are the usual backpacks and stuff for sale too.

You can group up with others who are in the tutorial. I think there's some kind of LDON instancing going on, but you always seem to share the tutorial zone with a few other people at a time.
