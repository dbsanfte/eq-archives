Uira Tawhaki
Some of the information here seems misleading to me..

Good xp is not measured per kill. It is measured over time.

1 AA every two hours is good xp to my enchanter. 25-30% of a level per hour is good xp to my warrior.

If you measure xp per kill you may actually end up getting bad xp. Fighting mobs that take you 2-3 minutes to kill for 2% is a lot LESS xp then killing a mob every 45 seconds for only 0.75% per kill.
