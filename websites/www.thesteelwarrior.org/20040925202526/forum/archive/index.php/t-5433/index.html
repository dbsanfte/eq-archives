Friedrick
In my opinion, 1 Anger III proc is better than 2 Anger II's. Why you ask? Look at the simple math.

Anger III mainhand, 2 procs per minute = 1200 Hate per minute.

Anger II main/offhand, 2 procs main, 1 proc off per minute = 1200 Hate per minute.

Hmm, so now if I upgrade off hand I immediately lose 400 Hate per minute via proc going Anger II route. If you swap primal in and out of offhand, I still get 1200 Hate from main hand procs, with Anger II I will get 800 or if lucky 1200 if both primal and offhand fire in the minute.

IMO, the Anger III is worth the extra 100ish points over 2 Anger II's just from the fact you can upgrade your other dual wield weapon and keep the same aggro from the procs. It would also allow you to not Hate aug a decent DPS weapon to keep for those non tanking situations.
