kiztent
Level until you can get exp with the chanter (42 I believe) then have the enchanter DC clear a zone (argh, same account, never mind). It's the best exp there is. Your skills will suffer somewhat, but since they are capped pre-50 by level 40 you should by ok.

For exp, you could probably hit Sol B at 31 with backup and level to 60 there (window/tiger to 40, KPC to 50, bats and bugs until they get LB, then LDCs to 60). Just make sure you have a snarer.

I'd snag a pair of crystal spider eyes, that's the only upgrade that really jumps out at me. If you want to clear Kael, have the enchanter do the looting, then park both characters in Thurg to MQ that way.
