Limiting yourself to just one combo really isn't what a warrior is about. I carry around a bunch of weapons and use what needs to be used in a given situation.

I would say to use the BoC(main)/driver(off) if you do tanking in groups, and use shell(main)/boc(off) when not. try to pick up a non-aggro weapon because we don't always tank. It's our primary role, but sometimes you group with another warrior so you wanna be able to swap to 'dps' mode.

also - rune will mitigate an attack, not negate it. So if a mob hit you for 800 and you had 200pt rune, it would land for 600.
