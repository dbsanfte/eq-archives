Bonequest
If you can save up about 5k, consider buying an argent protector. Its a very nice sword for low to mid level and it has no minimum level and a pretty decent ratio. It doesn't have haste but FBSS is like 1.5 to 2k and is plenty of haste pre-45.

Also, at North Ro at the LDoN merchants they sell a warm emerald shard (50 dd, rec level 30) augement for 40 points that you could put on it once your guy is level 30 or so for a little better damage. The points come real slow below level 45 so I wouldn't try saving up for a Ykesha proc or anything until later, not worth the effort and you will get better weapons in your 50's anyway.

Mithril 2-hander sucks even with the 31% haste. 21/40 becomes what, 21/35? Hardly a decent weapon.

I'd recommend you look into the velium weapons out of dragon necropolis, the velium claidhmores. They are cheap, have no level restriction, and look really cool to boot (long, sharp, silvery blade, with a sharp point and golden hilt & pommel).

The best of these is 32/36 (Exquisite Velium Claidhmore (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=27046)) and the worst is 30/40 (Velium Claidhmore (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=27037)).

Even the worst one is good at 30/40, holding agro pre-50 is easy with anything, and you can get these cheap as they are now 3 expansions old, from the Velious era.
