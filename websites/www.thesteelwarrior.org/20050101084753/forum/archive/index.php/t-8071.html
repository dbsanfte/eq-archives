While the cultural armor is great for sta and hps, I'd keep an eye out for similar gear but with some resists. You should shoot for 100 in all resists (to start with anyway). Fire +6 augs have been rare for me, but I've seen lots of Poison augs by comparison.

In no particular order, CA3, Planar Durability/Power 2, TM1, WA2, Fero 1/2, LR, ID (after LR).

Velium Gemmed is a nice dps weapon when you're not MT, but dual aggro proc weapons (meaning 2 aggro procs on the weapon, not necessarily dual wield as well) are practically a necessity to MT.
