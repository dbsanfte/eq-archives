yep, I know I've seen recent posts along this lines also so I highly advise doing some searches here ;)

My guild is in a similar situation numbers wise, although are average level is about 63 and most people are 65 with at least 50 AA's. Our 3 tanks range in hp from 7500 to about 8300 fully buffed and I think we all have full mitigation/avoidance AA's.

Here are some recent targets we've taken down:
PoI Dragon - last night with 18 (took him out on the first try with no deaths...very sweet!)

Nadox Inny - done him with as few as 14 I think...just gotta have a solid tank that can keep him cornered. Also, if you have to bring him outside his room, be prepared to deal with him gating - have the MA immediately run back to the raid after he is summoned...he may die, but this prevents adds from his room. (and be ready for a tank switch if this happens)

IV in Ahkeva - I forget what IV stands for, but he's a fun one and he drops nice caster upgrades. You need a couple people with RGC (we only have 1 and it works, but 2 would be nice) and a decent tank, aside from that it's just patience - one time I it lasted long enough for me to go defensive twice :p . I think we did him with 12 once, and definitely with 14. By the way, defensive isn't needed on this guy...he doesn't hit hard at all. What is needed is good discipline and a patience. If you get the wrong person on rampage or get impatient with the pulls when clearing the room, things will go very badly very quickly. Also, before we got our tactics down, we failed twice with 24...

Itraer Vius. I've killed him 5 or so times now and never seen this DoT that he supposedly has. Last time my guild killed him they said he now Ramped (this ability was also added to Cazic Thule in Fear, to our surprise). It is not necessary to cure anything during this fight and if done correctly you should not have to deal with any adds. He hits like a little girl. The key to IV is summon. Have an SK or Pally chain cast aggro spells at him for a while before engaging. Then when they get knocked back they will just get summoned again. Easy fight.

While you are in Akheva, stop by the Va'Dyn and drop him. He's cake. He can probably be Duoed by a solid Warrior and Shaman. Hits in the 800s and flurrys, but fully slowable. He just has a crap ton of HPs. He sometimes procs snare or a small DD on you, which means he is wielding one of the weapons he drops. To pull him, set up in the boxed in corner in the room before his. Have an SK/Monk aggro him and his guards and run through the room towards IV. FD, (CoH if mage is there) and Va'Dyn will come back alone ahead of his guards. His guards are slow. Then just tag him and he's alone. He has some crap drops, but he also has quite a few nice drops.
