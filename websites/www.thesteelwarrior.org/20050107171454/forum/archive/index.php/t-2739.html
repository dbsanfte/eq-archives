Thaak mentioned rushing him, which works just as well as pulling. But if you're interested in dragging him to the camp, it's pretty simple... get a Spirit of Eagle, or any speed buff, the faster the better. As soon as Sev's in visual range, (If your clip plane is at maximum) he'll make beeline straight to you, the trick is to not do hard turns at all on the way back to your camp. Do not do dramatic turns, do not stop. He'll catch up to you pretty quick if you do and since he's a dragon, his melee range is extreme.

Anyways, everyone has given you the info you need, just thought I'd add my 2 cents on pulling him instead of rushing him. Hope it helps.
