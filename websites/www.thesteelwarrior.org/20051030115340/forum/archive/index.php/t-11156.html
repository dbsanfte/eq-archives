Weapons are about style, crits come from your build or other gear.

If you go down the arms tree:

Swords = Extra Swings
Axes = Extra Crits
Polearms = Extra Crits
Maces = Chance to Stun (likely the worst ability, you stop people from using a good 5 second stun by using your 3 second random chance stun, no stun beyond the first even interrupts damage, a mob will swing as soon as it becomes unstunned, so only a stun of 3seconds or more will actually mitigate damage, losing a 5 second stun for 3 second stuns is just all kinds of bleh)

If you don't get the weapon specializations its all just personal choice and whatever the best weapon you can get is.

This is a misconception. If a mob is going to hit you in half a second and you stun it for 1.5 seconds, it's attack will get delayed by one second. Also if a mob is going to hit you in 3 seconds and you stun it for 1.5 seconds, it will not hit you sooner because of the stun.
