For pure aggro, you'd want Frostbringer in mainhand and Ykesha in offhand.

For DPS you'd want Frostbringer mainhand and Veno Axe offhand.

Or red epic/blue epic. Heh.

The Ykesha proc is more aggro than the Veno Axe. But the axe has a better ratio.

Frostbringer is the most superior weapon of the ones you're looking at.

Until then I'd probably go Ykesha main, Veno offhand. Or if you don't want the Yak proc (meaning, you don't want to draw aggro, as someone else is tanking), Bed Leg Club main, Veno offhand.

Part of the fun of being a warrior is playing around with your weapons. Heh. Enjoy it. Have fun. And carry a big bag around for them all!
