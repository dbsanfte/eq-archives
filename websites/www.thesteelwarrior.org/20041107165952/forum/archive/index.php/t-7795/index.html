After doing a lot of LDON, as a mage, I think one of my favourite group make-ups is NOT the usual boring single pull mezz...yawn yawn-type.
On *easy* settings, having 3 or more melee players is MUCH better, faster and fun.

Ok, basic point is to kill as many mobs as fast as possible in LDON. Killing 1 mob at a time is slow....pull one carefully....and you have to have a group set up just for that same old same old safe pull: 1 tank/1 mez/1 healer and who ever. But a huge amount of time is wasted on pulling and you have less DPs. If however you have say:

3 plate melee(or high end ranger)/healer/2 others
You can do MUCH better. You pull multiples and each is tanked, slowed, AoE nuked, AoE slowed...and nearly EVERYONE is doing DPs!

A typically melee heavy group I may be in looks like this:
1 warrior/1 paladin/1 ranger/1 cleric/1 shaman/me (magician 65)
If you pull 3 to 5 mobs, keep them reasonably close, wizards, druids and mages can use RAIN spells, which are normally pretty crappy, but when you have multiple targets, rain spells can do a crap load of damage, or you can use point blank AoEs without needing AOE stuns because the melee's will be aggroing the mobs to heck.. All the while the melee are whittling down the mobs. Warrior for best tankage and AoE taunt, paladins for tankage/group heals some CC with stuns and root, rangers and Sks can help with pulls and somewhat better DPs...

The end result is MUCH MUCH faster kill rates than standard pull/mezz. It's more risky, but more fun, and not suitable for Hard adventures unless you are truly uber. If the melee have AoE weapons, mmm!
Group damage shields are also very nice in this system.
So, when making a group up for LDON, why not try and get several warriors and try multiple mob killing?

Something to think on? :)
