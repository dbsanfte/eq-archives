You can start with player crafted jewelry even though you'll have to save a bit more than you have right now.

Valorium rings for your fingers.
Earring of the solstice(quest) and Lupine Frenzy earring(ldon drop) until you can upgrade to Bloodmetal earrings.

You could take a group to The Deep and camp Inscribed Mask of the Beyond http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62224 . Greebam the Lost drops it and his placeholder isn't far from the zone in. At your level the group would be getting decent exp too.

If you do LDoNs much then getting enough points to buy an Everfrost bracer to replace the Bracer of Scavenging is an upgrade that will last you a good long while and will let you save your money for less easily upgraded items.

This is just my opinion but sell your drogmor drum back and use the money to get a bow with stats. The bow I currently have should be available for less than what you get back for that drum.

As far as farming goes to raise bazaar cash don't overlook Upper Guk. The spiders there are static spawns and drop plenty of silk. They run and you won't get exp but they'll provide you a means of generating some income when you're solo.

That should get you started :)
