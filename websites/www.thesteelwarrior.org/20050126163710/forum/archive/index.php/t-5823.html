Damage Modals:

For two handed weapons ...
(2 * weapon damage + Damage Bonus)/Delay


For mainhand weapon 1hander ...

(2 * weapon damage + Damage Bonus)/Delay

For offhand weapon 1hander ...

(2 * weapon damage) * (dual wield percentage)/delay

Add the two up.

Now, keep in mind that Sinister Strikes adds an offhand damage bonus, so if you have that, it changes it to (2 * weapon damage) * (dual wield percentage) + offhand damage bonus/delay

Also keep in mind that dual wield percentage has increased for level 65 toons. And that Ambidexterity increases offhand dual wield percentage.

But here's the best explanation of modals I've ever seen, even if some of the data is dated (2handers now display their damage bonus for example)
