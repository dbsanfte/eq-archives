As you just joined, you probably won't MT the bosses much. But you may end up doing rampage tank or 2nd, 3rd etc. tank duty. For rampage tanking, fortitude then defensive. For tank switching when the previous tank dies, furious - AETaunt- incite - defensive in that order. Talk to the other tanks in your guild about how they prefer to do things like add splitting, setting rampage and tank mezzing.
