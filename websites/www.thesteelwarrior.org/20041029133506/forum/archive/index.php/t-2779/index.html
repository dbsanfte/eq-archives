Smakz
Adjutants = cleric mobs... They gate. They summon

You most likely had a mob gate on you, then summon you.

Here's a way to keep the mobs from gating. Giants cannot be stunned so you cannot stun them to keep them from running. Giants cannot be mezzed either, so using mez to break casting wont work. And when something gets charmed, you cannot attack them, so it makes it difficult.

When a mob gets to gating health, have all your casters go full burn till its dead, this ensures it gets dead before it has a chance to gate.

Also, have you melee push the mob. Yes, pushing is good here, a moving mob is a mob that has a harder chance of channeling a spell. If its moving, it has a much lower chance of gating.

Again, Adjutants are the ones you must worry about, all the rest are melee mobs. Cept the named, but dont worry bout those just yet, get the normals under control first, before you tackle Kallis and his PoZs, or the named Gladiators.
