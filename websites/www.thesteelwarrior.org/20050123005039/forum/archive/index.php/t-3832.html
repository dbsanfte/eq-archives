Neck: ANYTHING for an improvement. Clattering bone necklace are reasonably priced and would do you well for a while.

I am not sure if the fine heraldic stuff is any good, but you might want to check it out as well. Not nearly as good as the fierce heraldic, but worth taking a look at I would guess.

I am also guessing that the Thurgadin quest armor might be upgrades for you in some slots as well and the faction for that armor is easy to come by if you do not have it already. You could probably find some of those pieces to have MQd to you relatively cheaply if you have the needed faction. I can't remember which pieces are worthwhile, but you can always look it up and see.

If people are like me, I have a few stray pieces of the quest armors from Kael and HoT laying around colelcting dust in my bank, so you might be able to get lucky and find some rather cheaply.

As has already been mentioned, get some haste ASAP first thing!

I would recommend that you just do some searches in the bazaar mainly looking for hit points and then see what else is on the item...hp/AC/sta combos should be your focus. Try upgrading your weakest areas first, because those will make the biggest improvement for you...such as your neck slot. ;)
