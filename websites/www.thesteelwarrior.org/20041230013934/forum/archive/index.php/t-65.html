At your level and with the ammount of money you have your best bet is to just go to the bazaar and look for anything you can afford since there are so many items out there. Alot of people just want to get rid of there old gear fast so they sell it cheap. You may score a really nice item for 1/10th what other people sell it for.

Heres what you do. Set up the search window for your race/class then set it to show item by ac or whatever stat is most important to you. Also enter the max ammount you want to spend for each item. Then just select one slot at a time and hit search and write down the top 5 choices and there price. Then do this again for each slot. Now with a little figuring out you can decide what combonation of items to buy will get you the most AC for your money. You may not be able to buy items for every slot, and dont take all day figuring this out because an item might get bought before you get to it.

That is what I did for my warrior frog. All the stuff I have cost me about 25k, thats including all weapons. Click the link below to see.
