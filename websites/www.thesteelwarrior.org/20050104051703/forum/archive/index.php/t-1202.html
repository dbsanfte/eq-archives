Well my own experiences with Aegis of the Earth were promising last night. I would put the aggro level of my combo, Bloodfrenzy+Aegis of the Earth at the same level as the ol' faithful Bloodfrenzy+BoC. However, I do not feel it had the same aggro power as the Blade of War.

Prior to this change I did not feel the BF+Aegis held enough aggro to make it viable for tanking.

The good news is that Bloodfrenzy+Aegis is good enough aggro these days to use it as an MT on a raid and not make everyone walk on eggshells with their aggro.

I hope the devs continue down the "Block" path, however, as the addtional benefit of the shield stats really do not matter given the tradeoffs of Increased Riposte rate (BF 20 delay vs BoW at 32 delay), Decreased Aggro (vs. the Blade of War), and Decreased dps (80 or so dps vs 150 or so dps).

Additionally, I wouldn't want to use any other one hander I have used to date besides the Bloodfrenzy, which is a SoL drop, and highly contested by a large amount of lower end players at that. For high end warriors that missed Bloodfrenzy, I can't see this shield as working at all. I would suggest adding a 18/20 EB 1hs weapon with the same proc rate as the Bloodfrenzy to some elemental god's loot table to avoid bottom feeding. Put it on Fennin or Xegony so as to make it reasonable to farm.

I would say the shield is going to need to be able to block 7-10% of the time to make the tradeoff truly worth it.

To sum up, left to do to make the use of a shield a viable option for warriors:
1. Add a 7-10% block chance.
2. Create a Bloodfrenzy replacement in the elemental planes.

With these two changes, a warrior can choose to go for a shield that will give him more survivability, but less dps and less aggro, or a warrior can choose to go for a Blade of War for more dps, more aggro, but less survivability. Now all they will need to do is come up with a good reason for us to use dual wield.

As an aside, I really wish warriors could truly use all weapons. I would really love to go into battle swinging a Hammer of Crushing Waves and an Ifir, Dagger of Fire and be able to perform my function on a raid. It would be really nice if the developers would remove Enraging Blow from the game in it's current form. From reading and thinking about what warriors should be, masters of armed combat, some ability to choose and customize our characters via weapons would be very fun, rather than forcing us all into the current standard patterns: BoW, BF+Shield, BF+BoC, BoC+SoD, etc., etc.
