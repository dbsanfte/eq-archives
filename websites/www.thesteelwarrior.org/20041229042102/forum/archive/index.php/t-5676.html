As far as I know, there's no easy way to work out the aggro generated by nukes, heals, or stuns. The *simplest* way would be to have two characters: a Shadowknight (who has a fixed-aggro spell) and one of the classes you mention. You'd get the wizard/cleric/shaman to cast one or more of their spells, and then see how many casts of the shadowknight spell would be required to gain aggro... this would give you a rough estimate of aggro per spell cast.

However, it's my understanding that there's a problem with doing this. Apparently the Shadowknight spell does little-to-nothing to a mob that hasn't been engaged by the Shadowknight already, meaning that he would have to attack the mob and then do his casting which would throw off the results a bit.

Take this with a grain of salt, though... I haven't done the tests myself, nor do I even have access to the right toons to test this. This is all just compiled ideas from reading this board and those of other classes.
