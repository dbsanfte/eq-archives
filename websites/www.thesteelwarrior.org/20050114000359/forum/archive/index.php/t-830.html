05-26-2003, 11:45 PM
A bow is an invaluable tool that many warriors overlook. The reason being, we are not rangers.

Get a bow, skill makes no diff for pulling. Learn fletching to make long range arrows, it's not too spendy to get that high. And use it along with prox aggro and other tools to pull in all situations.

Later on there are bows that are full time range slot items. The player crafter PoP bows being the prime example.
