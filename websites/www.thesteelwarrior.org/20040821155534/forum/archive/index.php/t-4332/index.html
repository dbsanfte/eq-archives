Shadowfrost
Only part of that I'd take issue with is this:-

4) I want to see something that benefits melees as much as clerity helps pure casters.

While I understand your frustration, this spell line does in fact exist already. You're talking about haste.

Haste has been in the game as long as clarity has, and has the same dramatic impact on melee performance that clarity does.

It would be reasonable to ask for enchanters to be able to cast a 2.5 hour base duration haste spell like the 2.5 hour base duration clarity so you can go get it before you start your adventure and not worry about having to have a haster in the group. That would put melee and casters on an even keel.

It might even be reasonable to ask for better haste buffs and an increased haste cap for pure melee. 50% more haste would go quite a way towards solving pure melee problems.

200/tick regen, though, strikes me as pretty unlikely unless it's something massively short term like the clerical Heal Over Time spells.

I'm not sure soloing is a key aspect of melee balance, personally.

I'll join in on the fun.

Things I want to see WITH a new combat system (most of my stuff doesn't address a new combat system, per se, but that's only because I question SOE's ability to actually mess with the combat engine at this stage in the game):

1- If monks are to be "avoidance" based melee combatants, I want to see it, and feel it, and be able to notice it. I want to avoid STUNS when running away from mobs. That shit's been getting on my nerves for over a year now. If I'm a get totally wrecked each time I get hit, fine. No problems there. Just make me AVOID THAT CRAP when pulling as I would avoid it normally. I'm a kung-fu master, I have EYES IN THE BACK OF MY HEAD ABSOR-SAN! (hey, I sense your mocking ... don't make me take some chopsticks to your fly!)

2- Melee Archtype AAs need to fixed. Plain and simple. There's no reason for Hybrids to share ALL melee archtype AAs and casters to share all the GOOD melee archtype AAs. Either have melees get MORE out of their "defensive" AAs or cut back on the benefits casters and hybrids get from them. A wizard's "lightning reflexes" should be nowhere near as good as a rogue's. And an enchanter's "innate defense" should be nowhere near as good as a warrior's.

3- Purify Body AA for monks. Fix it to match the cleric AA. This is my crusade.

4- SOME TOYS. It's been a long time since melees have gotten ANY new skills, any new INNATE toys. Some call this utility, others call it viability, I don't care. Point is ... if you're revamping combat, keep in mind that one thing to keep it fresh will be to give melees something ELSE to do besides auto-attack and make sure they're in melee range. This is one of the things I want most. How many new expansions with how many new spells have come out? And still, the last thing I got was triple attack. Before that, DISARM. Sheesh.

4a- Don't make the new toys like DISCIPLINES. Crap I can use once every hour and a half, and only lasts for 8 seconds. It will be a complete waste of everyone's time.

4b- While we're on the topic of disciplines ... Ashenhand is pointless in light of finishing blow AA. Silent Fist is pointless in light of Dragon Punch AA. Warriors have discs they find pointless. Do something about that. Give us NEW buttons to push (new toys) and make the OLD buttons worth pushing again.

5- Ranged Combat. It's seriously out of balance. Rangers and casters are effective at range. Melees are a joke at range. I'm a KUNG-FU MASTER! My ninja stars should be able to do more damage than 7 points! Warriors are MASTERS of combat. They should be able to sustain better BOW damage than they do. Atilla the Hun was NOT a ranger.

6- Adjust the worn regen cap for MELEES ONLY. It's nowhere close to being balanced against the worn FT cap. Something like 60 to 70 hp per tick would be far more like max FT considering how melees don't have focus effects.

7- Give melees a benefit to having a MOUNT, comprable to casters. Wizards on a steed? What the hell? KNIGHTS and WARRIORS on a horse, with a big ass polearm, charging into the FRAY. That's the image from good movies and books. Not pansy ass Raistlin on a horse. Howbout an ATK modifier (200 raw ATK beyond the atk cap) and sitting REGEN modifier while on a mount? That way a knight with a lance or polearm and a warrior with a battle axe "CHARGING" into battle makes sense.

8- Skill caps. Raise them for melees, and melees ONLY. 305 in offensive skills. It should make a difference.
