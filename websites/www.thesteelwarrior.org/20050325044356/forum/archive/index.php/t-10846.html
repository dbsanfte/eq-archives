Here is a rough map of the general area that one of my scouts drew for me:

http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v397/necrro/Sharpshooter/Sharpshootersenlarged.jpg

Basically the way we do it is to set up and buff on the hill south of Jaerth which is 100% safe. They we clear around Jaerth and rush and kill him. As was mentioned, the other SS's will AE the raid during the fight, but the AEs are very small radius so if the tank stays on one side and everyone else on the other they can be mostly avoided. After Jaerth is dead we move back to the hilltop and camp the raid out to lose aggro on the other 2 Sharpshooters. Its very important that everyone camping out do so in a place that the Sharpshooters do NOT have line of sight to them. Otherwise the AEs will interpupt camping (30 second recast time on the AEs).

Once the raid has camped out, reassembled and everyone is rezzed and rebuffed we clear the wanderers and then move to the hilltop SE of Nihl and the clear around him and rush him. Once again once he is dead we camp out on the hill to lose aggro. From there we just clear around counter clockwise until we get to Nass.

I'm in a small pre-elemental guild and although this event is a challenge for us its doable and we are getting it down to a science. In terms of risk vs. reward it can be a lot of work to get one piece of loot and two runes but the loot is very nice.

I think you are making this a lot more complicated then it needs to be,

The AE has a very small radius, so you can put the healers pretty much anywhere and keep them out of it. Even the melees don't need to get hit as long as they stay away from the tank.

Having a knight tank from outside of los is probably not going to work for a couple reasons. First, I am 95% sure that the Sharpshooters summon. I recall both myself being summoned while tanking this as well as having one of our over-zealous wizards summoned. Second, after a certain point (I think it's around 30% health) the Sharpshooters do come unrooted and will move around.

Just have a warror tank. Have the healers creep down the hill toward Jaeth to be in range for heals. Once assist is called the melees rush down the hill and stay as far away from the tank as possible to avoid the AEs. If you want, put 2 knights and 2 healers on the other Shooters from range, although this is optional. If you tank is storng enough and you have a healer that can MGB Cel Regen during the fight, there really isn't any reason to have tanks on the other two. After Jaerth is dead either camp the raid out or mem blur the other 2 Shooters to lose aggro. Rez, rebuff, move on to the next.

Wash, rinse, repeat.
