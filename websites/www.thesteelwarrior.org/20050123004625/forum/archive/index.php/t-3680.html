This shows that the proc rate is similar to all other non-special weapons for both MMM and EoE. There is no 'enhanced' proc rate like can be found on BF, HG, DBotW, etc.

For DPS comparison, my extended VDD parse came out to 111 DPS. So this combo was 8 DPS less than the Aug'd VDD (with PB1). Throw on 2 DPS Augs and Ambidex and this should close that gap fairly well... but of course PB3 would likely make the VDD increase in DPS by a good deal also. Of course the EoE/MMM combo does more DPS than MMM main hand.

Still a fun weapon. Would be interesting to see it parsed with a ED for insane Proc DPS, hehe. Also would be interesting to see a parse in Ssra ;)
