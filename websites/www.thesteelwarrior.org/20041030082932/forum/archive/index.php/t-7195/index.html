Grongl
Incite/Berate/Bellow etc. is the best pulling tool you have, no question. It is non-damage agro meaning it triggers much less assist agro when pulling, so you're much less likely to get adds. I've pulled singles or doubles out of clumps of mobs in LDoNs and Ikkinz trials that other people didn't even want to try. Pulling with it also gives you a significant agro bump for when the mob gets to camp. If you haven't tried pulling with Incite, you really should. Once you get used to it, you won't go back to arrows or clickies. You also don't end up locked into a bow as a range item.

Blazing bracer is a nice tool to have for really, really long pulls in clear areas, but the cast time is a pain in the ass if you're trying to keep things fast. It's also a bolt spell, so it will frequently get blocked by trees, rocks, shrubs, stalagmites, etc. And in those situations you're probably better off with a long range bow. But I haven't run into anything that needed to be pulled from that far in a long time. I still keep my bracer on me for nostalgia but I rarely use it or my bio orb these days. I've even got Snakefingers in the bank!
