- Red epic, if resisted causes little to no agro

- Droga weapons produce more DPS

IMO, unless you can afford to buy a BoC+SoD then the best agro set up you can get pre-EB is Sword of the Bloodsworn + Copper Hammer of Striking. I regularly participate in late night raids to farm all kinds of armor and gear and I bring my 59 SotB+CHoS weilding warrior with us for the additional DPS all the time. I have found that I often have to bag the droga weapons in favor of a Dragonrib Club + Shortblade of Lightning due to pulling agro when I get procs. The warriors that usually tank are weilding weapons like BoC, Greatstaff of the 4 winds, Greatblade of Storms, and a couple of them use red epics offhand. In some cases, the only way to shake agro has been to turn off attack and back out of melee range. Considering my warrior is only 59 with no AAs, I must say that his ablility to hold agro with these weapons is very good. I don't think you will be disappointed.
