A few things jump out from your profile.

First of all, you have far better gear than I did at 53. Grats on that.

With only 5K to spend, there won't be a lot that you can do.

I suspect that everyone will have their own idea of what is the "first" thing that you should get. A lot of it depends on what your usual role is, what kinds of mobs you are fighting, that sort of thing.

In your place, the first thing I might upgrade is the head piece. I snagged a helm of Raldukan for 4K, that would be a major upgrade for you, but it seems to go for a little more than that, usually.

I think the sectoid scale bracer has to go. I'd get a silver chitin wristband there. That should run you around 4K.

Or you could get imbued granite spaulders for the shoulders. 20AC, 10 to all saves. The downside to these is the weight (and no HPs or other stats), but the resists can't be beat on the shoulders. Whether you need resists depends on the kinds of mobs you are facing.

I'd go for a better ratio weapon in the offhand, too. Something like a longsword of freedom. If you are MT a lot, then something like a snowchipper or a spiked velium mallet. Each of those weaps would be in your price range, depending on your server's economy.

Another nice upgrade would be earrings. The earring of the solstice kit is around 5K on my server, as is the electrified earring of tempests.

There's probably some deity stuff that can be smithed for you that would be a major upgrade, but I'm not an expert on the deity armors.

I notice you don't seem to have any no drop stuff. Are you not in a raiding guild? In not too long, your droppable upgrades will become very expensive, and the best way to upgrade will be through raiding and winning the rights to no drop items.
