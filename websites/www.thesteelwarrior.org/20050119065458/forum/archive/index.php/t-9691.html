Just want to make sure that you fully understand how ldon points work.

In order to buy an item from Miragul's for 1492 points, you must have at some point earned a total of 1492 points at Miragul's.
If you then go and earn 1492 points at Rujark, you can spend those 1492 at Miragul's, even though you already spent points at Miragul's.

Points will always go to the same spending pool, your points are not affected by where you earned them. The only reason they are tracked per camp is to make sure that you have done enough adventures at said camp before you can buy stuff there. After that you can earn your points wherever you want.

-----
Now, to answer your first question, they go to the same pool as all other points, this doesn't affect your spending abilities at any camp.

To answer your second question, get the Anger III aug or Force of Ykesha aug. Both are viable choices. Being able to hold aggro is more important that any amount of AC.
