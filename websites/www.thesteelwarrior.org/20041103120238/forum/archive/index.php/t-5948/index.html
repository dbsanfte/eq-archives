Easy drop in Torgiran's (for 0K :D) ... Grobb Hide Mask (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=40128).

Zone into Torgiran from Nadox, go forward thru the door, and down the stairs. There's a good camp spot on your left after you come thru the door, a wee bit down the hall. Pull from farther down the hall, and watch for a fake floor along the way - it drops into a water filled tunnel with a luggald at the end of it. No biggie. A high 50/60ish group won't have any problems there. There's a troll treasurer type that spawns every four hours or so, drops 6 gems. Nice bonus.
