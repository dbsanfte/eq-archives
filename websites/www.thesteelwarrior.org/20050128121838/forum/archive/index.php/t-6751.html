What are you planning to put these augments on? I don't see any weapons in your magelo that have slot types 5 or 6.

As far as I know, there are no augments available in type 4 that add DMG to a weapon. Perhaps there's an augment dropping in pre-Kod'Taz GOD that I'm not aware of.

As for elemental damage in general, I usually weigh it at half value, on the assumption that most mobs will resist it half of the time. So for example, a 14/19 dirk from Everfrost camp with 2 Fire Dmg augment added, I would expect it to have the same DPS as a 15/19 weapon in general (it would perform worse in a "fire-based" zone like SolRo, and better in a "cold-based" zone like most of Velious).

The high-level LDoN 2-handers aren't impressive and generally, you want a 2h for DPS.

The 1h are nice looking but because they don't have an innate agro proc, high-level warriors avoid them because they need more agro. Your 1h combo is excellent.

I'd suggest either a War Marshal's Bladed Staff or a Windblade for DPS (WMBS is slightly more DPS but Windblade is slower for fewer ripostes if you're helping position from the front)

If you're looking to burn LDoN points, consider Ykesha or Anger augs, Armor, or AC/hp augs for your nicest pieces of armor.
