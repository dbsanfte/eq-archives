You do not need a BoW to succeed in PoP.

While the BoW is probably the best overall package
(insane dps, decent proc rate, low ripostes, nice ac/hp).
It is not needed.

I have a BoW, i would probably trade it for BF/HG/Aegis combo
to be more efficient when disc drops, but its not that much of a gain.

BoW is the ultimate weapon before Time, but its not a requirement for sucess.

Ps : As for ripostes ...
A good CH line is pop is designed around healing the MT for 40% hp.
Aka when i get a CH over 4k, i know there was something wrong.
Either some lag, a fizzle, or a bad round.
1 riposte will not make any difference on a normal round.

Take a normal Xegony/Fenin hell even Raperm fight.
How many CH have you gotten over 7-8k and lived to tell the tale.
Almost none. Maybe one or two once in a while.

The reason is simple, once you go above the 8K CH mark
You are into the danger zone, at that point 1 hit will kill you.
Which is often the case, thus explaining the very few 7-8k CHes

If 5% of the CHes you take are above 7k-8k.
Lets say the dual wield riposte rate is 7% (compared to 5% with a BoW)

You have a meager 5%x7%=0.0035.

Thats a lousy 0.35% chances of dying to riposte dmg when dual wielding.
While that number is 0.25% with a bow.

Its so minimal nowdays the impact should barely be parsable.
