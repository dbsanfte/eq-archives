Left click and hold over your primary hand until it pops ont your icon, after that drop it on your hotkey of choice. Then repeat this with secondary hand as well as wn Open inventory slot (or a bag with weapons).

At this point you can use the 3 slots (or 2 and a button openable bag) and rapidly swap gear without opening your inventory and all being clos together. This method also works well for linking clickable items so you cna refresh selfbuffs in less steps.

Hope this helps
