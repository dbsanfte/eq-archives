Okay i found the person in question and her name is Corfia Nultethen in Firiona Vie. She is a high elf located at -3820, +1400. This location is on the large dock that leads to the Translocator, shes located on a small room on the left. To talk to her, you must speak Elder Elvish. I got my friend Phelon to PL me for 10 minutes to max the language.

Here's how the text goes from here and the tasks she asks of you:

You say, 'Hail, Corfia Nultethen'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Yes, what can I do for you?'

You say, 'Korbuk sent me'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'That's really quite fascinating, but doesn't mean a whole lot to me. I really don't care who sent you. You don't have any [items of value] to me, so be gone.'

You say, 'What items of value?'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Well... if you must know, I'm in need of a platinum bar so I can continue my work.'

At this point you go and buy a platinum bar for roughly 105pp, can be bought a few hundrd yards away in FV.

Hand platinum bar to Corfia.

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Aha, something worth my time. But I've changed my mind. I'd prefer if you made me something that really showed me how interested you are in my craft. We jewelers are hard to please, but if you can present me with a [Nocturnal Mask of Acuity], I'd be more than willing to atleast speak with you.'

You say, 'What Nocturnal Mask of Acuity?'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'The mask is an interesting blend that will allow me to see much better in the darkness, where we elves can sometimes have trouble. To make it, you'll need an enchanted electrum bar and two darkened star rose quartz. Show it to me when you've made it.'

The items needed to make this mask can be bought off of her really cheap, maybe 5pp at the most for each of the three items.

I had my friend with a 231 skill in jewel craft make the item. She enchanted the electrum bar, then combined it with the two darkened star rose quartz successfully on first try. The result was the mask:

Nocturnal Mask of Actuity
Slot: Face
CHA +2 INT +2 HP +2 MANA +2
WT. 0.1 Size: SMALL
Class: ALL
Race: ALL

Hand mask to Corfia.

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Well done, but not great. It doesn't have the sight properties I'd hoped for, but it looks nice nonetheless. I half thought you'd shy away from creating such a lustrous piece of jeweled metal. You didn't though, so now you have my attention. What is it that you have for me to look at?'

Hand in hilt to her.

Corfia Nultethen says, 'What a filthy piece of rubbish! This will need a good deal of work, I can guarantee you that. I have a small repository of supplies that I only rarely use, and one of the things I have is a cleaning solution. I don't often use it for most mundane things, but I'd be willing to part with some if you're willing to do me a [favor].'

You say, 'What favor?'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Yes indeed. I've been spending such a long time in the city lately that I just haven't had time to go on the expeditions I used to. Such a strong and capable person as you would be able to fill in nicely. You see, I've always wanted to explore the depths of [Veksar] to find out what treasures are hidden down there.'

You say, 'Veksar?'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'I've heard plenty of stories and read a number of tales about artifacts of great wealth down there. In fact, I was able to get a sneak peak into the ruins some time ago, but not enough to find what I needed. One of the most interesting things I've read about is a tome of bones made for a necromancer named [Galuk Drek].'

You say, 'Galuk Drek?'

Corfia Nultethen says, 'Oh, he was a beast, to be sure. One of the nastier creatures down there, but also one of the most brilliant, from what I've read. Drek kept the tome full of information on various crafts, and one of them just happens to be jewelcraft. I'd love to set my hands on that tome, and if you can find it, I'd be more than willing to finish up this hilt for you in no time!'

- Find Petcas Coldbeard at Thurgadin at loc -47,94. Walk thru the script and give him 350pp for Special Beans. Also buy 3 of the generic beans he sells.
- Find a brew barrel
1) combine 3 generic beans to get Generic Coffee Brew
2) combine Generic Coffee Brew + Special Beans to make Coldain Coffee
- Find Dardek at bar in Thurgadin and hail. He repops as Dardek Bladewright hand him Coldain Coffee. Follow him as eh walks to the forge on the otherside of Thurgadin.

- Give Dardeks Forged Blade to Korbuk Brimblade in Dranic Scar and get Korbuks Blade of Mastery.
