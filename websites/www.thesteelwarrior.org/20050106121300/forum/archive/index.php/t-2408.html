Raex BP doesnt "suck". Its effect is an upgrade to our biggest weak point(agro generation).

To answer the original posters question IMO the Raex BP is a bigger upgrade to a warrior than the paladin BP is to a paladin.

Experienced paladins probably already have Marrs Gift from Xegony ring(or similar, forgot if there are more they can use).

So the jump from a +0-(20-25)% heal to a +0-30% heal (marrs blessing) isnt that big, and doesnt compare with going from +0% agro to +15% agro.

However, given the current state of the game I think the balance between these two BPs are appropriate. The paladin BP simply serves as a straight up HP/AC/Resist upgrade, whereas the Warrior BP serves as a band-aid fix for our agro issues. Not the right way to fix it imo, but thats a totally different discussion, at this point I think warriors are willing to take whatever agro bonus they throw our way heh.

Although they could have been a little more creative with the paladin effect I suppose, but then again.. who cares.. paladins have nothing to complain about atm imo.
