you're just about the right level to put that wurmslayer away ( tell ya why in a minute ) and find yourself some decent 1H weapons that are a bit faster.

Back when we had the Wurmslayer discussions; most people decided that in the mid to upper 40's the damage bonus on most 1H weapons actually caught up; if not surpassed; the wurmslayers dps and having the faster weapons there with more hitting going on proved the most beneficial.

probabally one of the most decent 1H weapons hat are reasonably priced and can give you the most " bang for yer buck" are :

1. Bed Leg Club ( basically a warr only lammy blunt clone; the +magic bonus is nice ) - price: dirt cheap well, on terris they sell for 100pp~ and i think they're primary hand only; but you can move your primary hand weapon to offhand if you think it'll be more beneficial
2. Guard Captain Mallet - really nice offhander and even a decent mainhander if you don't own a less than 20 delay weapon. I used one right up to my epics for pure DPS.
3. Jagged Blade of Mourning - another very nice offhander in the 1K~ price; nice MR and dex and AC. ratio is good too for an offhander
4. name Velium weapon - these are the weapons from Dragon Necropolis...most are of a 1:2 ratio if not better...consider though as the ratio gets better the price goes up..see paying 500pp - p for one of the graded velium weapons.
5. For Aggro specifically and not much else...Black Blade of Tormenting is a solid weapon for situations where you need cheap aggro and can't afford the frostbringer yet...or if you're a good race or have seru faction do the Praetor Blade of Debility quest....guaran-damn-tee you for a free weapon that takes longer to run between zones than to complete you won't find a better weapon ( stat wise it blows but the proc is devastating )..until my epics I MT'd mobs from Black Golems in CoM up to Dozekar/HoT nameds/ Lend/Telk/Derakor/KT/Dain/Inny/PoG named trees with them and they performed exceptionally for the relative cost/time they took to obtain and were only replaced by my Epics.

IMO though the velium swiftblade you're a bit too old for; sure they're nice for the haste but I feel you're losing too much DPS for it to pay off.
