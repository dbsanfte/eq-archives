Fitemore
Other upgrades: Go to the bazaar, do a search for ac gear and sort by hp, then compare that to the list sorted by sta. 1ac = 6hp = 2sta (or whatever, something like that for your level). Ignore str, dex, agi, wis, int, cha and resists unless those are the only differences. Do this for each slot. Buy what you can afford and shop around. Fill the empty slots with anything, it's (by definition) better than nothing.

Pick up some weapon that procs slow/snare/stun/hate/ac debuff at your level for aggro. I don't know for sure of any good ones at your level, and if not just look for ratio until ~ 40-45. Around then you should be able to get an aggro proccing weapon (and NEED it). If you do LDoN, just get the dropped augs for now and save points. Miragul's armor is great for the price once you can afford it.
