Your HP is falling a little behind, which could become an issue now that you're eligable for the 55+ zones of PoP. I would make an effort to start replacing non-hp gear to gear with HP. This would include your Cobalt pieces.

Several of your items are also excellent resist pieces, but should be put in your resist kit and replaced with more full-time hp/ac gear. Your current neck and shoulders are examples of this.

Your best upgrate lineup at your level would probably be the Velious quest armor sets. Champion's gear (Thurg quest plate) is probably going to be the most accessable to you. Your guild should be able to handle the arena to get the Corroded Plate pieces, or you could watch for people selling MQ's. You could also look for some Fine Heraldic if you are of a race that can use it.
