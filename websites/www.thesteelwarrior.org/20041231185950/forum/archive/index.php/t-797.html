From my time hunting Wyverns I found that they drop flawed / chipped gems which sell for around 40pp to a vendor. The only slots you will get gems for a warrior from the wyverns are the arms and legs, not sure which you will get for a druid.

However as mentioned above because the wyverns are such popular territory for kiters the market is pretty well flooded with these gems so don't expect to ever get more than 75-100pp for them. The real popular quest gems are all crushed (crushed topaz and crushed flame emeralds in particular) which you need to go to Velks / Sirens grotto for.

Wyverns have around 3khps and hit for up to 100 so you may find its a little steep for your druid to keep you healed without too much down time. I certainly found that in my high 40's when I duo'd a few with a druid it was hard work and the druid could kill them much more efficiently without my help - however this was towards the end of velious and my gear back then was spectacularly crap compared to the kit you can buy in the bazaar today so that in itself may make this alot easier for you.

I agree with what the dude above said about Crystal Caverns - nice XP and you can walk out with some pretty decent cash at the end of a session even - gems and also crystalline silk and velium which sell well to tradeskillers.
