You've encountered the curse (or blessing) of the FH geared warrior. Not much outside ornate or Tak LDoN armor will ever beat it if you aren't in a raiding guild.

Personally, if I were you I'd be kind of happy where I am at the stage of the game you are in. I know we all are constantly searching for upgrades because it gives us a feeling of improvement and satisfaction, but honestly your gear is more than sufficient all the way to 65. Stop worrying about upgrades, grind your way to 65 and you'll find your options will open up extraordinarily. You will be able to hunt for your own ornate in BoT without having to spend time in the bazaar and groups in LDoN will be more likely to take you. Besides, the points gained in LDoN for the time it takes and exp you get isn't worth it prior to lvl 65 IMO.
