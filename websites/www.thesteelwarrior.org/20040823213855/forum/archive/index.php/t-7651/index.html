Slein Jinn
1) She said cap, not softcap - going on that and what I've observed of her posts in the past, I doubt she was referring to anything beyond the player's AC. Parses have shown that shields give no benefit beyond their AC.

2) With the way the "softcap" on AC worked (I say worked because it's basically non-existant in current content), a shield raising the softcap is simply impractical and improbable. Mob damage is calculated based on mob atk vs player AC - when AC reaches a certain point versus mob atk (we can't determine that point since we do not know mob atk values) it becomes less valuable, almost certainly due to the nature of the formula used.

3) Alot of warriors use shields at the high-end, and very few knights do at the low end.
