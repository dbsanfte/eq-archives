I dunno if I might just be the first person to get this weapon but it is the result of a quest in Sol-C.

If you zone in from Sol-B and fight through the few kobolds until the brick-built area there is a named Kobold and a "shadowed man" in a small room to the left. The named kobold is not attackable and not aggro depsite what /con tells you. The shadowed man type npc is what you want, he'll tell you that he has infiltrated the kobolds to destroy them and that if you bring him four items that "still retain heat after death" from mobs deeper in the caverns he can make a temper for such a weapon.

Forgive me if I don't recall the precise names of the drops but you'll see them as obvious if you crawl thru the dungeon. Anyway you need a smoldering tentacle from the tentacled mobs, A soul of fire (or something like that) from the fire elemental mobs, a brain type thing from the slimes and I'm buggered if I can remember what the fourth item was, though I think it came from the winged mobs that hang around the fire elementals.

All of them ID as "blah blah blah emanates heat" or some such.

Take those 4 items back to the npc (Ghaib something his name is) and he gives you a temper and a little exp. Then he says that to make a weapon from it you need to find the parts of a weapon that were scattered among the kobolds. I just happened to have all 3 parts - they are 1) shattered blade - id's top half, 2) shattered blade - id's bottom half, 3) melted hilt.

Give those 3 items and the temper to the npc and you get some exp plus:

Ghaib's Flame-Forged Longsword
Str+10 Sta+10 Wis+5 Int+5 HP+50 Mana+50
Effect: Servant's Bolt (Combat)

Effect is a 150 fire DD with -100 resist adjust.

Took me less than 6 hours from meeting the npc to getting the weapon (including accidentally porting out once hehe).
