Rif isn't going to be your problem. All you need is to get a levi and stay to the far right when you go over the ice bridge to get around the Rif mob. I avoided him for weeks while working Max faction.

The problem is going to come once you are inside the TR. At least the 2 TMax guards are on Max faction and you are going to have to run pretty close to them to get to the quest mobs. I was never brave enough to try to run around them because the space is pretty tight and if you agro one, the entire TR will assist - you will die fast.

Turn in dorf heads to Bvellos until you are not KOS to Max and Rif will take care of itself.
