I'd try punishers/enforcers in the PoJ basement. They barely hit my warrior (which is geared worse than yours, with 90-ish AA), and they don't run so you can skip the snare portion of your routine. Some are light blue at 65, but you can mow them down fast enough that the exp isn't terrible, and the loot doesn't suck, either. You will sometimes get 2 on a pull though, so be ready to root with your cleric. To keep that to a minimum, whenever there are 2 across from each other, con them both. If one is light blue, pull the other one, and it'll come solo.
