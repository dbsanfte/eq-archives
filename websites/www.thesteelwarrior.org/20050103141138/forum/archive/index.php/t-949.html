I'm seeing many many posts here about problems with Warriors.

Warriors definately have a problem (which closely resembels that of the rogue), the thing is, people try to solve it in the wrong places.

Warriors having a key role in raids, it is very very dangerous in upgrading them. And that's why we've been getting nothing. If not simple hp upgrades to get along with the damage upgrades of mobs.

Wanting better aggro and better defense abilities, is the sort of wishes that won't be realised, because all of the current expansion's design was made in regard to this.
As much as it is an honor for a warrior of being one of the pillars of the game, it's also a curse.

What warriors should ask for however are other upgrades that do not touch the domain of actual tanking.

What could be given to warriors that would give more utility in groups, other utilities in raids other then just Tank or wait to Tank...???
Great ideas in these domains DO have a chance in getting in... that is if SOE even listen to playerbase anymore.

What about a simple Knockback skill? That refreshes every 10min or so. It could generate a little aggro as well, to close that Knight/Warrior aggro gap a bit more.
Would add usefullness and fun to the warrior, without unbalancing it.

AoE stun with a large reuse time?

It would be nice to come up with a few skills or disciplines that would not heighten the warrior's aggro/tankage, but still upgrade them.
And there's many many potential great ideas out there!
And a few of these skills could have a bit of aggro addition... just a tiny bit. To allow to get a headstart after a taunt.


Another solution would be putting in actual warrior equipments.
Giving warriors a wide range of armors to choose from.
Making their armory the equivalent to a Wizard's spellbook!

Currently, in my opinion, the Warrior's greatest flaw is being... Simple...
You get most hp possible, and you tank.. period..

Give us something new! And we'll see what we can do with it!
Defensive is not enough to make One seperate class.
Warriors need something to call their own, skills, disciplines, stances whatever! but Something!
