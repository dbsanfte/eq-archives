Basically, a list of important aspects of the WARRIOR class, as well as with eq as a whole.




1. Update LDoN so that there are level 70 hard adventures with a new droppable loot table. If no update, at the very least please upgrade the experience gained in LDoN adventures. For warriors, this is still the only spot to recieve Anger augments.

2. Would be nice to have a few more "fluff" item quests like the wood elf illusion clickie.

3. Feels like mudflation has gone through the roof. Reaching 10k hp was a wonderful feeling, 11k was cool, by the time I reached 13k I stopped getting really excited. Don't know how to solve this, just pointing out my feelings.

4. A few under-used zones could use a revamp. Revamp doesn't necessarily mean "make the mobs hit for 1000," but would be nice to see new types of mobs, storylines/quests, loot tables, factions, etc. Something like Kael B springs to mind, a new zone off Kael with new faction quests and some challenging new raids.

5. Actual consequences for Ninja Looters and exploiters. We had something ninjad about a month ago and SoE refused to tell us who it was or even if they took action against the individual.


Now I'm going to stop being idealistic and say that the chances of us getting even 33% or so of our ideas implemented is absolutely nil, based on the last Summit:

They promised to fully instance Time. They have not.
They promised to put in backflagging solutions. They have not.
They promised to look at auto loot. Where is it?
Where are our solo / duo LDoNs?
What about them looking at guild wars?
Where are our new models? Where are the new race/class combos?

I referenced this thread (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=7457) for my claims.

I think it would be dandy if they started fixing what they said they'd look at the last time, but I for one am not holding my breath. I think having another Summit is a good idea but SoE's track record is abysmal.
