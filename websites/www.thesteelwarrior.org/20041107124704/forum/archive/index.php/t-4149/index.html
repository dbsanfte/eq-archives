Kizef
2 patches ago they made the ~ key work just like the F8 key.

Go under keyboard in your options window.
Under the target menu.
Find "target nearest mob" Next to that it should have F8 and ~ as main and alt keys.
Click the ~ then press ESC. it will cancel the ~.
