1. Warriors and knights are all pretty close when it comes to AC/HP/avoidance/mitigation. I think it needs to stay that way since we're all tanks.

2. Warrior vs knight aggro control needs to be roughly proportional to the difference in tanking efficiency between them, preferably through upgrading warrior aggro control.

3. Warriors need more utility. They need to unlink our discipline timers, tweak some of them if that makes some of them too powerful, and add some new higher level disciplines to match up with the added utility that knights get through spells.

Examples of the types of things I'd like to see added as disciplines:

Aura of the General: 60 second song buff that adds 15% to both melee damage and spell effects (nukes and heals) for everyone in the group (warrior barks orders out increasing the tactical position of everyone in the group)

Force of Will: Warrior is 100% immune to detrimental movement affecting spells such as snare, slow, charm, root for 60 seconds

Force of Endurance: Warrior mitigates all non melee damage by 50% for 60 seconds.
