Fitemore
I really enjoyed my Frostreaver, I was surprised how good of aggro it actually was. A GBoS and something else likely beats it out in both aggro and DPS, however. I'd go GBoS and SotB or CHoS.

The ornate BP is probably worth it. Very few BPs out there compare to the warrior ornate at 63ac 115hp and the clicky is just a very nice bonus. The 6ac 65hp rings are surprisingly good still, just get 2 of them for now.

Basically, use the standard 1ac = 1sta = 6hp = 1 point until sta capped for now, and then convert the armor stat upgrades into pp per point. Then, spend your money accordingly. Always get an upgrade that will last compared to an upgrade that you'll likely replace soon.
