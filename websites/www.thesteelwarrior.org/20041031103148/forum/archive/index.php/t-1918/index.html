Wompem Ondahed
It was like that for me too. The thing about PoP is that you gotta have quick aggro ( /rude Paladins ) Slowing a mob quick to keep the healer's mana is a big concern when mobs start hitting from 300+.

The reason the Windblade is fairly good at that is because on your first round of swings ( if you hit both swings ) you probably do 200-500 dmg at your lvl. If you are fighting with similarly gear friends that dual wield, they won't be putting out your dps, atleast not in the beginning of the fight. That is a huge dps aggro compaired to Frosty and BCA. Now when BCA and Frosty start proccing they would normally out aggro you. If you miss a round or two of swings w/ a Windblade it becomes difficult to make up the edge you had, however.

My first aggro weapons were BBoT and a Frosty. I got a Windblade for a super-sweet deal and I had it happen just like you did. Unfortunately any DPS aggro edge you have over your friends disappears at 55 usually. I went for a pair of smithed Ykesha weapons and was very pleased. They procced well and held aggro well enough till lvl 61 when I got my GSoT.
