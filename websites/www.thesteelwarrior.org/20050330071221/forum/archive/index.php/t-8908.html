We kill him in one of the big chambers before you get to him. You need to be able to kill fast to pull this off, otherwise repop will bite you on the ass. But the big chambers give us enough room to setup DPS and spam healers outside the AE, while a CH rot with bardsong is inside the AE, plus another bard with the MT group. Doing this without two bards is much more difficult in my experience.

As far as the blur goes, our very first try at him the recommendation from friends was to hybrid tank him. We tried it, but his DPS was >> our hybrids at the time. The warriors ended up tanking once the hybrids died, and all noted that he seemed kinda gimp to tank. At that point we switched to warrior tanking, and telling people to make sure they stay far back and moderate their DPS. Slow and steady beats fast with this one.

I've personally tanked him about 4 or 5 times now, and we lose maybe 4-5 DPS throughout the fight because they get summoned. I have to admit I never even noticed the memblur, it doesn't affect the fight for us as far as I can tell. It's certainly no worse than Gintolaken, not that you can have done that yet. ;)
