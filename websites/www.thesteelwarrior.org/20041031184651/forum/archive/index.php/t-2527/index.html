I'd highly recommend the greatstaff of 4 winds. It will work as a good aggro weapon for you now, and a good dps weapon for you later. (Yes, i've seen a well AA'd warrior with GSof4winds easily out aggro a BoC/SoD tank.)

If your guild has access to Sol Ro, then I strongly recommend you farming a few Obsidian Greatblade (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=25997) which can be obtained with probably 3 or 4 groups without much trouble. I'm using this weapon until my number comes up for BoW. When tanking various trash mobs on raids, as long as I get 1 or 2 rounds headstart, usually I hold aggro off of BoW tanks. Lucky procs can make it go either way of course, but the obsidian greatblade seems to have a greater proc rate than BoW. On fights that are a bit longer though, BoW will win every time of course *grin*, but it's a great, easy substitute.
