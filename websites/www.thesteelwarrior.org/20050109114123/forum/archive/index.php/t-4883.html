Warrior Hunting guide from level 1 to 70 ( for when we can get to that level ):

You have your pick of two options!

Hunting option A. Go to where people in your level range are exping and get a group.

Hunting option B. Get 5 other people in your level range and go to where you can kill things that give exp.


Now for some usfull advice, being the lowest level person in a group and the tank is an extreamly bad thing. You can get away with that kind of thing if you are DPS but as a tank you will just end up getting chewed up and spit to bits.
