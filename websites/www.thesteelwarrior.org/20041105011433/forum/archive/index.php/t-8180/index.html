Brabble
Looks to me, given that you've been gone for two years, that you're fairly intelligently geared. Yeah, gear has changed a bunch in the last two years, but you are not terribly undergeared at the moment.
Once you get LDoN, I think you'll find several avenues of gear upgrades are available to you. I'd farm plat and keep an eye on bazaar prices for a while (prices as well as gear have changed since you've been gone, as you've probably discovered already).
Looks like you know the weakest slots; your fingers are probably not the first thing I'd replace, but your list looks good to me.
You might check the prices on certain tradeskill items: silks, leather padding, hides, acrylia, velium, and windstones. You could farm all of these things (well, not the leather padding, but the components for it), and, if you can run a trader in the bazaar, make some plat. Or, of course, check with your tradeskill guildmates to see who needs what.
