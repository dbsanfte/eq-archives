I really hope they up our damage mitigation. That way Knights can still tank xp mobs, only we'll tank them alot better. And while the DPS classes may be screaming for the group to "grab that knight", at least the clerics will be voting for the warrior.

The only issue I see with this is trivializing large sections of the game. Anything that can be done with warriors in their present state will become that much easier if our damage mitigation is increased. Perhaps large scale raid encounter mobs could be reworked to account for this change but that would require alot of effort.

I worry about an increase in our ability to hold aggro as "the ultimate solution". DPS classes use abilities and spells to reduce their hate as is. They are restricted from going all out by the limits of the tank. If the tank suddenly becomes better at holding aggro the DPS classes will just up their output to compensate for this fact. So rogues evade less, the ranger jolts less and the wizzie casts concussion fewer times. Again this could lead to the trivializing of certain mobs, or at the very least make them noticeably easier. Either way, they'll be back to stealing aggro off us in no time, only it may take a few more rounds of combat.

I for one like the fact that warriors, because of their present state, aren't being chosen by every wanna be 12 year old power gamer out there. I remember Kunark. I remember how many knights I saw back then, it wasn't alot. Now that casters and hybrids are so powerful they seem to be everywhere you turn. While this melee revamp may be a good thing for us in the short term it may come back to bite us in the ass down the road. However I do agree something should be done to help us have a more definable role in the game.
