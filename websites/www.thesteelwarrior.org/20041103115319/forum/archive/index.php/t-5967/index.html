Thought id pen a quick guide to farming the maternal cragbeast.
You don’t really need time /ele gear to do this. If you can buff to around 8k hp +, id say youd be ok. Maybe just get more dps to kill faster and make sure cleric spams SL. DI also helps.

It’s a quick farm of about 30-45 mins and I was doing this with a cleric druid and a botted bard /ench. The loots are

30hp /mana aug
10atk 2 regen aug
FT2 Regen 2 aug

Get the tipt expedition. Kill the first mob at ZI which normally sees invis. After that, you can invis all the way to the bridge. Kill the wanderer in the middle of the bridge and clear the golems on the far side. You only need to clear one on the left or right and middle one and you can skirt the other. Work up and kill the 3 spawns on the ramp up to just below the trap area. From the time you kill the last mob before the trap area, time 15 mins for repop. Be careful though, theres a little trick on the last mob as it seems to alternate between a 1 spawn and a 2 spawn.

3 people invis up and spawn the hatchlings. Then head back down the ramp a little. Monk/bard pull hatchlings and kill. If your puller is good, you can get em all done before the 15 min timer runs out. Once last hatchling dies (6 total), maternal cragbeast spawns.

You can rush to her and fight in the middle of the trap area, just in case your running close to the repop timer. With defensive/stonewall, max hit is around 1300 or so but that is without BV. She mitigates slow, but can be crippled which helps. She also rampages, so be sure to set up a rampage. Doesn’t have a lot of HPs. With our group, the only dps was mine, bard and ench and we still killed her in about a minute.


Once you kill, loot, rinse repeat till you (and your alts) all got what you want.
