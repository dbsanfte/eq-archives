Overall, I think you're doing pretty well. Just shooting from the hip here, the more experienced gear gurus will be along shortly.

Haste. You're going to want some haste, soon and badly.

Weapons. You're going to want to get an aggro combo, as critters are doing exponentially more damage than they were just a couple of levels ago and that means splattered casters. For your level and whatever plat you state you have saved up, the gear gurus will point you to some fair aggro weapons.

Cultural. Since you worship the Tribunal, you've got access to some of the better +HP armor out there. Save up and snag a few pieces of it, you won't be sorry.
