Daemonwynd
The warrior epic is fairly decent in stats. However, its effects, I believe, suffer from poor design and should be upgraded. The only thing epic about them is how bad they are.

Take the reduced recast time on the Incite line. This reduced time does next to nothing to help with aggro generated by incite, and on top of which excaberates the issue of running out of endurance when using incite often, especially with the newer ones.

With a two second cast time decrease for Incite, a warrior can get in around one extra Incite for each 15 or so uses of the skill.

With unfocused Incite, the aggro generated on 15 incites chained every 30 seconds (450 secs duration) is 11,250. Endurance cost is 3,750.

Focused Incite, the aggro generated on 16 incites chained every 28 seconds (448 secs duration) is 11, 650.

Operating under the assumption that each incite overwrites the previous incite, therefore losing the other 30-32 seconds of taunt DoT, this works out to only a 3.5% increase in aggro generation... with a 6.6% increase in endurance used.

At the end of the day... that focus gives nearly an unnoticeable increase in aggro, with a FUTHER increase in endurance used... and the issue isn't aggro generation any more, especially with TWO aggro procs available on the epic, it's a question of running out of endurance. Issues are starting to arise with running out of it, this makes it worse.

The focus effect on the warrior epic, therefore, is designed poorly and therefore should be fixed.


To find suggestions for a better focus effect, first I ask what the warrior's role is:

The warrior specializes in generating aggro on mobs to make them the focus of all the mob's attacks.
The warrior specializes in being able to take a beating better than all others, and survive.
The warrior specializes in melee weaponry, being able to use all weapons with equal ability.


Taking ideas from previous items in the game that have helped warriors, as well as looking to other classes epics, there are many suggestions for a warrior epic's worn focus effect:
1: Increase aggro modifier of all actions taken by the warrior: 20%
2: Increase aggro gained from usage of incite by 25%
3: Decrease endurance usage from disciplines and/or incite by 20%

All of which are truly useful focii to have. The first, and preferred, one is already in the game as a 15% version. These all scale up to the next epic.


Next, the epic click effect:

Warriors don't buff people. We just aren't that type of PC. Most especially, we do not cast clerical line group buffs... if ANY tank should, it should be a paladin. NOT a warrior.

That clicky effect might be useful for all it's shortcomings, were the warrior epic not able to have two seperate aggro procs, and were the warrior constantly having aggro issues. With the epic at least, this is not the case. Therefore, the group will not have any meaningful benefit from this spell. Without the rest of the group gaining benefit from the spell, the analysis must be of how much the effect improves the warrior alone, and the analysis is dismal.

In one minute, with a minimum DPS of 125 (very reasonable), the warrior's DPS is reduced by 10%, for that one minute. On higher end fights of 1000DPS, the DPS reducion by this clicky goes down significantly, a 1.25% reduction, FOR THAT ONE MINUTE. The clicky has a refresh time of 10 minutes, so in sustained terms, warrior damage taken is reduced by a maximum of 1%, to .125% or lower.

-Instant click Warrior's Tenacity of significant power (minimum 5000pt for 1.5) balanced as the warrior doesn't keep the health, it goes away.
-Clicky AETaunt+AEIncite

etc etc.
