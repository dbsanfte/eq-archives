Daabu
Of the six mentioned here (the 3 you mentioned, plus vxed ornate and ldon), the umbracite is the best of them. 25-40hp doesnt make up for the 15-20ac extra you get on the umbracite.

Personally, I'm using ornate legs, and I'm confident that I will not upgrade them until I get Raex legs. By the way, most guilds consider Elemental legs a time drop nowadays, because the majority of people get their legs in Time.

So, if you get any of those mentioned there, aug em up, cuz they'll last you a good long while. If you get your choice, I'd go with:

1) Umbracite ($$ AC)
2) Royal Attendant (more aug slots and an extra resist to poison)
3) Ivory Crystal (comparable to Royal Attendant, but with less aug slots and less resists)
4) Ornate (good clicky if your sta isnt maxed, OK resists/ac/hp)
5) Vxed (nice hp/resists, but the AC is kinda low -- I'd take it if I didnt have one of the above first, though)
6) Fiery Might (horrible in the AC department...unless you want the clicky, let chain or leather or somebody hit that instead)

I'd take any of the top 5 and ride it until I got Raex or GoD legs. I wouldn't touch the Fiery Might, because the AC just makes me want to cry...that's just me, though.
