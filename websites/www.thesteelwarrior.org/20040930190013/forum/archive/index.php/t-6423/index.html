Qaladar
You can't. Just have to be prepared to do a lot of faction work when you need to... and theres only a couple of reasons.

Dain faction for 8th shawl quest (this is largely pointless for warriors so not really an issue).
Dain faction for 10th ring

Tormax faction to turnin both Yelinak and Dain heads for belt/gauntlets. This is a good one to keep high, but then Tormax also drops some sweet melee gear.

Yelinak faction to turnin Tormax head. White dragonscale helmet is nice but if you are killing Tormax, this helmet is I think better for tanks. This is mainly for casters... dragonscale boots are nice.

Basically... you want to be on Tormax faction. If your guild feels like doing a 10th ring, you need to switch. Also every time you off Tormax for his loot you may want to work it back up.
