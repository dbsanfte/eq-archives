Please Note: These are currently on test ONLY and are subject to Change, alterations, adjustments, and any other disclaimers that one can think of. Also, there are no promises made as to when/if this is going live:


Decaying Lord Galuk Drek

This NPC in Veksar is having a make over! Woo! Changes:

• Triggered NPC. When a warrior with the appropriate flags (read, once you reach this point of the quest) enters Veksar, if Galuk isn't up already, he'll spawn. This happens when you zone-in only.
• If you recently spawned him, you will have to wait 2 hours before spawning him again (if he depops or is killed). This respawn time is on a per-person basis, so if you spawn it and he despawns, you'll have to wait 2 hours, but the warrior next to you can try spawning him right away for his or her chance.
• You have a 15-minute grace period where he will not depop. This is to allow you time for a death or to zone out and back in, go LD, etc.
• If you've previously spawned him and he despawned before you could kill him, you'll get a message indicating *approximately* how much time you have to wait. This is not exact, so you'll have to make a guess on it.
• When you zone in, if you are the one to spawn Galuk, you'll receive a message on screen and in your chat window indicating so.

Hopefully that helps.

Dragorn Champion
