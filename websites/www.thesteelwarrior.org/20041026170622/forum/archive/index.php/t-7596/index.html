My suggestion would be to have a new tier to LDoN. Level 70 adventures, with a level 70 high risk counterpart. Preferabley bringing in a new sixth theme as well for a little more variety. New elemental sized armor, purchasable by points gained from level 70 adventures only (regular and high risk). Double the point cost over the current high LDoN armor. Have a quest NPC that requires your "maxed" stone, giving you a flag for the level 70 adventures. The NPC also returns you a similar adventure stone (same stats as the maxed one you gave the NPC obviously) that has the ability to grow a little bigger through your number of wins in the new sixth theme.
