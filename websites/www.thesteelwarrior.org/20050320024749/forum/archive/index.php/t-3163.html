There isn't a slot 4 augment. Lemme give you an example of an item..

Binn's breastplate of Noobness
ac +1
hp -1
mana +1000
war only
slot 1 : type 1
Slot 2 : type 5
Slot 3 : type 2

There are 3 types of removers... slot 1, slot 2, and slot 3. Lets say you had all those slots augmented, and you wanted to take out the aug on slot 2. You would then buy the slot 2 aug remover.

There are only 3 slots to augment things, and the type of the slot doesn't matter when removing an aug.

Weapons are type 4 , not slot 4... so just look at the slot number (#1 if it's an oldworld item) and use that number slot remover.
