"Question 1. What is an appropriate amount of hps to tank named in BoT? hps/aa's actually"

The appropriate amount of Hit Points to tank XP mobs in an XP group in BoT is 3k or so unbuffed. It's not about the amount of hit points you or the tank has. It's about the attentiveness and mana of the healer, and how good the slower/crowd control is.

To tank nameds ... well, it depends on the named. Some are easier than others.

I know the boss of upper sand is very hard to slow, so he's much easier to kill with a defensive tank and a cleric that knows to cast CH earlier, or has the mana to spam faster heals like a fiend. (Usually the latter is a BAD idea). But if I'm in a group with a chanter AND a shaman, I know I, a crappy mitigating MONK could tank the guy. (I know this from firsthand get-my-ass-beat-down experience, heh).

To answer question 2 ... BoT attracts lazy people, who want lazy XP. So they really don't want to have to work WITH a tank to get their XP. They wanna show up and click their buttons like good little gerbils.

If I were you, I'd go dungeon spellunking.
