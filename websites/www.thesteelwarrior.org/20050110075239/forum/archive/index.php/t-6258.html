1. Clear area around Shei (vendor is dubious).
2. Put raid in north room.
3. Bard aggro Shei and Fade when he see the 4 guards.
4. Monk aggro the 4 guards and train them to IV somewhere, FD, get CotH (or wait 8 minutes for them to depop).
5. Raid move into Sheis room. Put Manausers in south alkove away from ae drain. Designate corpsedragger+resser+buffers for MTs.
6. Engage Vendor shei at spawn, place a DA-capable sacrifice on carpet where real Shei will spawn.
7. JUST before vendor shei dies, sacrifice does DA and /sit on carpet and eat 20k-nuke.
8. MT jumps down and engages, slowers slow.
9. After 2 minutes Shei will 20k-nuke MT. Next MT take aggro. A Pally or something engage the add. ALL dps on add until dead. Repeat 9 until Shei dead.

The 20k-nuke is interruptable with Pally/Cleric lvl 65 stuns. Just VERY hard to time it right.

Pull the DT-adds to middle of south wall so nukers inside south alkove can nuke without needing to step outside and eat manadrain.

thats it.
