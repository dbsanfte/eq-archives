I was kicking around an idea this weekend while waiting for plenty of rezzes on spider island in Air/Wind, and wanted your opinions.

Since a lot of high end warrior gear is multi-classed nowadays, and has Mana+ bonuses on it, I thought it would be nice if warriors either automatically or via an AA were able to "transform" that Mana+ bonus into HP. i.e. - a 100hp/100mana item would translate to a 200hp item on a warrior only. That way, tanks can get the same amount of an upgrade that a paladin/shadowknight could get out of the item, since they can use the mana.

Now, perhaps this would be a bit overpowering for a 1mana = 1hp ratio, but even if they downgraded it to a 50% HP bonus, it would still kick ass. i.e. - 100hp/100mana would translate to 150hp item on a warrior.

Any thoughts/opinions?
