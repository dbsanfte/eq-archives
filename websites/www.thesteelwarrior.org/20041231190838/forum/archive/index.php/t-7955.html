If i'm not mistaking, the biggest advantage for nome and assling war is their "race" xp bonus. they have a 10% xp bonus over the baseline(human) on xp/level. So, if your game is to get to level 65 on the bullet train, then nomes are the way to go since they also get to tink.

but seriously, nomes are very strong tanks because they get there faster then any race in the game. verant has removed the "class" xp bonus/penalty, but I believe the "race" xp bonus/penalty is still there. Ogre has the worst xp penalty for a warrior but they do make up for it in the immune to frontal stun department.

as anyone would tell you, play whatever race you enjoy because at the end of the day, only 2 stats really matters and that is HP and AC. it's almost impossible to get to level 65 w/o getting max sta/str these days. so dont let those stats be a concern. if anything, concentrate on CHA as a primary stat because it's the hardest stat to raise for a war IMO and it becomes rather important when you start to tank real mobs. Looking back, IMO, the primary stats you should focus on are CHA and AGI because everything else can be buffed to the max rather easily.(excluding int/wis of course but those are useless) nomes offer a good balance between CHA/AGI/DEX IMO for future development as you gain gear. And although asslings are better in AGI/DEX, their CHA is lower and cant use nome armor in the early game which is another great xp advantage for nomes over asslings.

so... what do you get for being a football for the real tank races?
race xp bonus
immunity to shrink:) / innate cornering ability:)
tink
nome armor
"balanced" stats for development/growth
GOGGLES!!!!

and remember, ogre dont get as much out of the stun resist AA as all of the other races, so in a way, they are nerfed, AA wise, for losing 1/2 of their stun resist AA's to their innate ability.

well, hope that helped. looking forward to playing more nomeball:D
