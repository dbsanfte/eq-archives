Since the hardcap of ac was removed and never heard of a new one implemented, I'd prefer to get AC as high as possible for my character with a nice balance of hp. Since both help in different ways, I use the old 5hp = 1ac formula. Using that formula, I'd personally keep the kiz bracer and dump the other.

28ac / 45hp = 185 (Kizak)
18ac / 65hp = 155 (Magnetic)

23ac / 90hp = 205 (Flame)

(Yes, I know. These numbers don't mean anything and aren't used anywhere in eq, it's just more of a point system I use to figure out which item is best for my char. I know some used to say the hp:ac ratio was closer to 4:1 or 6:1, I just split the difference and use the 5:1 ratio of hp:ac.)

I enjoy getting as much raw hp as possible to get those nice flashy raid buffed numbers but I have to always factor in AC since it does help with my incoming damage mitigation. Just my 2cp.
