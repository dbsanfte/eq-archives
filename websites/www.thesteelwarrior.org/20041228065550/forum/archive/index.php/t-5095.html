You can make a hotkey for the new Combat Abilities by right-clicking on a slot and selecting the appropriate menu-tree item.

You can also make a hotkey for the Active AA Abilities (e.g. Area Taunt) by going into the AA menu, selecting the appropriate AA Ability, and clicking the "Hotkey" button.

The advantage of doing it this way, versus using /alt active 110, is that once you activate the ability the window will stay greyed out until the ability is available to be used again.

And as far as I remember, "/disc defensive" can still be typed in manually or added to a manually generated hotkey.

All the original and the new discos can be added to hotkeys by simply typing /disc *name of disc* into the hotkey (same as you prolly did with defensive/evasive before).


This is particularly useful for those of us that like to spam their groups with useless messages about discos.


For instance I made a key for incite that says some obcenity in group to let them know I was inciting something :p.


This does work for all abilities we have thus far, cause I just checked it. Because I'm bored I'll list it for everyone.


/disc Incite (or bellow/berate/etc)
/disc Healing Will
/disc Focused Will
/disc Stonewall
/disc Evasive
/disc Charge
/disc Mighty Strike
/disc Defensive
/disc Aggressive
/disc Precision
/disc Fellstrike
/disc Fearless
/disc Resistant
/disc Spirit
