For about 5k, depending on your server's economy, you can get a Bloody Ancille's Pouch which summons basic arrows using, not sure on this one, Purified Crystals? as a component. These stones can be bought at adventure camps and are stackable. Each stone makes 20 arrows, so one stack is 400 arrows for ya.

This is what I use and it's pretty good because you don't use up a lot of inventory space and it's fairly cheap.
