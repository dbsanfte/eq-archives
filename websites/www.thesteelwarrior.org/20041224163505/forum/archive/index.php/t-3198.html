Just because the buffs stack doesn't mean the haste does. The illusion is version 1 haste just like Speed of Vallon, so if you have SoV and get werewolf, the buffs will stack, you will get the other effects of the illusion, but the haste will not stack.

It's no different than stacking Alacrity from a shaman with SoV. You get the other effects of the buff, but no additional haste. But go ahead and get project illusion if your guild fights anything that needs to be corner tanked. I pretty much have to get gnomed for any cornered mob these days.
