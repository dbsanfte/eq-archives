the only requirement for LDoN normals seems to be a priest class to heal, though there have probably been a couple of successful groups to go through without even that.

Some of the less vanilla groups I have been in
- 3 bards, warrior, druid, rogue
- war, pal, enc, shm, nec, wiz
- 3 mages, enc(me), cleric, monk

Lower DPS on the mobs means that you can get by without a plate class to tank, without a cleric or druid to heal, and without a slower.
