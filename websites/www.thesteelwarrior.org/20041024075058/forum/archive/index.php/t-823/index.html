GSW
You won't need more than 1 group. You'll need a good MT(60+ish), a FD puller that knows what he's doing, good 60ish chanty and cleric, an evacer/porter, and 1 more. The most important thing is to have a good FD puller and chanty, there is lots of mobs. Once you get out of the shroom tunnels, set up camp in the big room past the small gate/bridge type thing where 3 or so juggs sit. Try to figure out how often they pop there so you know when to hold pulls. To get into the room with juggs and reets, click the fire pot. When the FD puller is inside doing his work, he should intruct you when to open the door(can't be opened from inside)
The crown only drops off reet frogs, and it's pretty rare, so...be prepared to stay down there for a long time. I'm not saying you'll get it in 1 day of camping though.
