Well, of course the LDoN 10 AC augments from mistmore would be a huge AC boost, you'd get about 80 AC if you got one for every slot they can go into. Gear wise, pretty much all your equipment could get at least some AC boost from 1 groupable or bazaar gear, but the standout item would have to be the djarn's amethyst ring, 80 hp or not you need to get a ring with some actual AC on it. For droppable augments, the two 15 AC augs from ferubi and the 20 AC Girplan Eye from MPG off of Blindhunter are probably your best bets.

Also, AAs don't raise your actual AC number, just added mitigation/avoidance.
