landrain
You are absolutely correct in you assesment as to the lynchpin of the problem being slow.

Slow is needed in PoP.

Slow Is Huge aggro.

Slow kills chanter/shaman when the tank looses aggro.

But that is not the source of the problem. The source is much deeper and older. Changing slow will only minimize the problem for now.. until the next time when something else happens to bring the problem back into the light.

the problem is that warriors are no longer the BEST tank in a MAJORITY of situations.

You can say all you want about our better DPS, better Mitigation, both which have been reduced signifigantly in the last 4 expansions. But neither one of those abilities will make us the best tank in a MAJORITY of situations. The reason? Aggro.

Much has been said about a 20k hp tank who cant hold aggro as being useless. The essence of that argument is essentially true. If the Tank cannot maintain the mobs attention, he is a useless tank.

Sure we have been able to hold aggro before, and sure we 'can' do it now, but nowhere near as effective or as consistent as a Knight.

The problem is with KNIGHT Tanks that its a 'no brainer'

Little to no thought is needed when a Knight is the MT vs a Warrior

There is a new generation of EQ players. No longer can you expect 90% of the people you meet to be 'Roleplayers' and be experienced in games such as D&D. Now a majority of the players are PS2 kids.. They play for the fast action instant gratification. Having a Knight as a MT allows them to achieve that.

Everyone who says we can hold aggro in Kunark... Velious.. Luclin... PoP.. you just suck as a warrior, whats wrong with you. Are either too egotistical or too proud to look around. Yes it 'IS' possible to hold aggro, but the likelyhood of losing aggro is greater now than ever before.

Essentially the whole problem comes to the fact that through 4 expansions, ALL casting/hybrid classes have seen their power level increase increadibly. More mana, more hps, more focus, more mana regen, more dps, more spells, more efficient spells, more gear, more AA's... more more more.

And during that same time melee have gotten? more hps, more gear (of course 95% is usable by caster/hybrids), more AA's (melee AA's are shared with hybrids)

The aggro-generating capacity of every class has increased, they do much more dps and detrimental spells than ever before. During that same time the aggro-holding capacity of Knights has also increased dramatically.

Some say knight always had this advantage, partially true. However they did not ALWAYS have huge mana pools, and high mana regen abilities that essentially allow them to cast at a high rate for extended periods of time with virtually no down time at all.

To compound this, the gap in tankability of Knights vs Warriors has decreased dramatically. in Velious what used to be a 1000 hp gap between warriors and knights is still a 1000hp gap, even though the hp's of both have doubled. No matter what math you use, the gap has decreased, add to that the increase in knight skill caps to equal our own. Has made Knights virtually equivalent tanks as warriors.

many things have contributed to the problem. Mobs that REQUIRE SLOW on INCOMMING only have brought it to light.

Melee need extensive overhaul and upgrade to bring them up to the level of the casters/hybrids.

Until that happens, we will continue to experience the same problems.

IF/When we do get this upgrade, we need to STAY on the devs that no longer will every expansion ignore melee needs.. Or else they will be doomed to repeat their mistakes.
