A few facts to start with:

-The first 30 levels go so fast, equipment will only be usefull for a few hours at best. After that, it is outdated.

-When you are lvl 60, 1 gold is nothing, when you are level 10, it is a fortune.

Of course, if you want to RPG all quests and see whatever stories there are etc, you could try to do all quests. If however you want to play efficient, do the following:

Take all quests when you enter an area. Move out to the place where you can do the nearest quest, killing everything on your way there. Then move on to another closeby quest, again killing everything on your way there. Do some quests that are not too far like this and keep killing everything on your path.
When you return to the city and turn in the quests, you most likely have some of those quests remaining that were too distant, too difficult or even require you to go to another area. You can drop these now, or just drop them when your list is full and you need space for more quests as you start doing the same thing in another are.
Just do not spend too much time on one quest. Do not focus on only the quests, but kill everything you see while moving around for the quests.

Before level 30, gold is not important to treasure because soon it will be an insignificant amount. Items are not important because they will quickly be outdated.
Therefore, focus on the experience, not on material. The gold you do have, just spend it on some items as you like. You don't have to keep searching for the best items at your level. Don't waste time on it. If you find your damage to lack, go to AH or nearest store, buy a weapon and move on.

After level 30, you can start saving all your gold to buy a mount at level 40.

Honestly i'd suggest not buying anything on AH at all until 40, at least. Because you'll want the money for a mount. Of course, this doesn't mean cut short on abilities! If you do instances and all the quests, you'll have plenty of gear though.

You don't need to be doing much research yet(except for instances, which you should look into soon), just do all the quests.
