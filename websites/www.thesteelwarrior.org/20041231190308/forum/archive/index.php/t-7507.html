Theres a rule you can follow for these, as well.

General: constant price (if it cost 1 for level 1, it will cost 1 for level two)
Archetype: linear scale (if it cost 2 for level 1, it will cost 4, then 6 for 2/3)
Class: linear scale (3/6/9)

PoP General: constant
PoP Class: this is the only tough one to figure out. I think its "New Skills are linear, Improvements to old ones are constant". ie: if it improves something you already had, it will cost the same. If its something new (like punishing blade or stalwart endurance), it will scale

Gates: Linear
