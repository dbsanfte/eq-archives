In general, VT is best for non-visible slots. Cloak of Icy Shadows will take care of you until Time, especially if you pick up a gorget of superiority for haste. In EPs, get molds and hopefully a puresteel mantle. If you can't get AHR gloves, get an orb of sky. It's worse for sure, but it's a heck of a lot better than nothing. Personally, I wouldn't worry about the BP from AHR as much as the mask, gloves or earring. An ornate BP is fairly close, and comparatively the other AHR loot is a much bigger upgrade.

I'm a raid leader for my guild and hate Aten for this very reason - Everyone gets really personal and pissy when *THEY* don't get a specific drop or such and such item doesn't go to their class.

The neck is great for every class. There's a reason it's All/All, you know? The fact is, no class will ever find a replacement for the Aten neck until Time. Warriors benefit immensely from the 35ac/125hp/+20 all svs. Hybrids, especially Knights, benefit from every single stat. Necros benefit enormously from the regen/ft. You can pick any class and there will be something on that Talisman that is just downright freaking awesome.

And that's why I hate it, hehe.

Aside from that, if I were to have to pick just a single piece of Aten loot, my vote would go to the face, which I did (Crimson Runed Mask in my magelo). The Great Shadow Platemail is amazing, but Seru/Cursed BPs are very comparable. The Earring of Eradication is awesome as well, but with PoP having the Signet of Might quest and with the Sigil Earring of Veracity being pretty easy to do these days, there are some nice alternatives.

But the mask? Sure, there are some really nice masks in the elementals, but none have *NEARLY* the amount of AC you find on the Crimson Runed Mask. Inny (Hate) drops an 18ac 115hp mask called Rusted Wicked Faceguard. One of the Avatars in Air (Can't remember which) drops an 18ac 140hp mask called Fine Hammered Faceguard. Then there's the Solusek Ro mask (Baroque Half Mask of Solusek Ro - 20ac 135hp) and Great Mask of Flame off Fennin Ro (20ac 150hp). Imho, while these masks *ROCK*, I don't think they hold a candle to the Crimson Runed Mask because of its sheer AC. Since you have the opportunity of getting one, that's what I'd go for.
