Cyphan, are you only looking for VT upgrades? If you can get a couple groups together you can easily get one of either Yeli talisman, Gauntlets of Dragon Slaying, or Xanamech neck, all 41% haste items that go into some of youre weakest slots, the Shaderock Amulet and Myrmidon Gauntlets, items that need to go soon anyway unless you're banking on both Gorget and Aten gloves.

People mentioned earlier in the thread that lots of that VT loot will last you until time or can't be replaced before time, I'll agree with the first claim but not with the second, for items like Belt of Mastery and Cloak of Icy Shadows there are comparable items even before VT (i.e. Belt of mastery is pretty much a Ssraeshzaen Blood Cord with 25 hp and attack with less stats/saves, Cloak of Icy Shadows is a Cloak of Thorns with 25 more hp for a longer clicky cast time and 5 less AC.) And BP of Speed isnt really that hot unless you need haste badly, Kizraks and Royal Attendant both have advantages over it if you can put haste somewhere else, not to mention Aten and Seru BPs. If you're looking to get into EPs and/or Time relatively soon (which isn't necessarily true, but since most of this thread uses Time/EPs for comparison, lets say you are), I'd consider pretty much anything out of VT you're looking at except for some of the Aten loot to be a temporary upgrade.
