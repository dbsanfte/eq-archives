/hijack on

Incidentally, the skygge AE is directionalThere is no such thing as a directional AE. There are exactly 2 type - point blank AE and targeted AE. People who think AE is directional are usually the first to get raped by the Jord Skygges because they stand behind the mob, but still in AE range because the mob is cornered.

This is how AEs work -

Point blank AE - eminates from the caster outward 360 degrees for a specified range.

Targeted AE - is cast on a targeted. There are 2 ranges associated - the range from the caster to the target, usually prettly long range, and the AE range which is measured from the target outward 360 degrees around the target. In the case of the Skygge AE, it has an AE range of 20 feet. So anyone within 20 feet of the MT is going to be hit - regardless of where they are standing in relation to the mob. You can have your head up the mobs ass, if you are within 20 feet of the MT, you are going to get hit.

oh yeah, to answer the original question ...

There really is not a lot of difference in difficulty in getting to the mobs these days. If you have a non-kos mage you can CoH right to Aary's spawn. If Aary is not up, you can get to the pit pretty easily. You do have to clear the flurries, but they are not the 1 mob wrecking crew they used to be. They can easily be tanked without defensive. Long fight, but not difficult.

The tower named are also pretty easy to get to, in most cases you can lull your way to the boss. If you get struck clearing your way it, it kinda sucks because the mobs have quite a few HP and there are a lot of them. The bosses themselves are a higher difficulty than NToV dragons, however as someone already mentioned, they don't have nearly the amount of HP so the fights are a lot shorter.

Tower bosses drop better loot, NToV bosses drop more loot. I guess it's a matter of priority. If you need to equip a lot of people in one shot, you will get a lot more loot in 1 NToV clearing than you will in 1 tower boss clearing. However, the tower bosses will be up 6 hours later. They are completely different enounters, you might as well do both if you can manage to get some mobs up.
