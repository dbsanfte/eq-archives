Based on the original poster's numbers, his data shows that an aug'd weapon in primary will proc twice per minute, if the weapon itself has a proc of its own. This is wrong. Almost all warrior weapons, when used in primary, proc twice per minute on average. Adding an augment to the weapon will NOT keep overall procrate the same. Your weapon proc will still go off twice per minute, and the augment proc will also go off, although I've seen different logs claiming different proc rates for the same augments. I'd say anywhere from 1-2 procs per minute on an augment is the most likely. Therefore, aug'ing a weapon with a proc of its own should increase total procs per minute to 3 or 4 or thereabouts.

Also, go to http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?threadid=5570 for a thread on proc rates. The link in the first post is to a database where you can search for the proc rates for every warrior weapon. Some surprises to be found include GSot4W and Obsidian Scimitar of War, both of which proc at a higher rate than the 2/min.
