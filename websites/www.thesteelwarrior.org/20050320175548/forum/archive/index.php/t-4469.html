OK, since we are talking about weird fantasy zones, here are some really weird ideas I have had over the past couple of years that might or might not make good EQ zones:

I want a dungeon where you can walk on any surface. I have seen movies where people were in rooms and you could walk on the ceiling and walls and on the bottoms of stairs, etc. Gravity would not be always the same way. Objects could exert forces on you also--if you walked to close you could be pushed away from or towards certain objects.

There could be alice in wonderland-like zones where people look shrunk or distorted as they move around in them (example: you are twice as tall on the left side as the right side). If you stand too close to a wall, you might set off a root trap that makes you stick to it for a while (kind of like those flowers in echo cavens) or perhaps just debuffs you or dispells you. There could be electrified walls or objects that do a small amount of damage like a damage sheild if you get too close to them or try to pick them up off the ground.

Another idea would be a zone shaped like the inside of a huge donut. Using toroidal (donut) coordinates, you could make the direction of "down" be the nearest surface and you could look up to see people standing on the ceiling way above you. Also if you walked far enough in any direction in a straight line, you would come back to your starting point. Zone outs could be fixed objects like in pop or they could be moving objects that randomly wander around and you have to find them. The donut could even be twisted around so that eventually it meets itself, kind of like a huge snake eating its own tail but while all coiled up.

Another cool zone would be a water zone inside a cave (kind of like plane of water but not as high level) where there are huge tides and wind everywhere and if you get into one of the streams of water or air, you could move very fast along it. Mobs could sit inside giant whilpools or tornadoes and your raid would literally revolve around them really fast as you fight them. There could be waterfalls to fall off and geyser traps that blow you way up into the air. There could be air pockets and places in the floor that would break if too many people stand on them too close together and flood the compartment or make everyone fall to a lower level.
