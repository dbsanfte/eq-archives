There is a gnome in Butcherblock that sells a augment removal potion. You have to look at your augment to see what type of distiller you need and buy the appropriate one. I believe the Anger 3 type 7 augment costs either 750 or 1k pp. Then you can sell back that augment to any Adventure merchant for 70% of the value you paid for it.
Frostpaw
08-26-2004, 12:31 PM
When you inpect your augments from inventory it will say what type distiller you need to remove them without destroying the augment. The gnome near the birdbath (Mimzit Furnov) in the BB LDoN camp sells the distillers. Then you use the birdbath just like with normal augs.

Also, you can now sell back LDon merchant bought augs and equip if you have some things you'd like points back for. The return is like 70% of the original purchase price
