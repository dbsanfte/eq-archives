Its not just the ratio that is taken into consideration.

If two weapons have the same (or very similar) ratio, take the one with the lowest delay (so long as it doesnt go lower than 28). This is because of the way the damage bonus works (remember the old adage "the fastest weapon gets the most dps?" It works for 2h as well, assuming the dmg/dly ratio is the same)
