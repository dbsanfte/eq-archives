If you have free time and a healer friend, ie cleric or druid with Cheal, I would consider the Coldain ring quests. Through 9 can be done 2 people with the exception of 2 fights for the 9th ring. The first is the icehouse in Kael and you just need the dwarf head from there and most peeps doing the cycle let it rot. I got mine this way so I didn't have to bother friends to do this for me and many small guilds doing the cycle would love a 62 warrior to help that doesn't want armor. The second is the fight in Icewell and if you have a cleric or healer, all you need is a few others for damage which usually isn't hard to find a couple of friends.
