Phantron
There's no guesswork involved. Aggressive is increase damage modifier by 30% for 1 minute. This is, ignoring damage bonus, exactly 30% more DPS for 1 minute.

Fellstrike is increase damage modifier by 100%, increase min damage modifier by 400%. If we assume Aggressive is 30% DPS increased then certainly Fellstrike can't be less than 100% DPS increase (damage mod + min damage mod).

If you do X DPS, then you gain 30X DPS from Fellstrike, and 18X DPS from Aggressive. Fellstrike will always win. Just the damage mod alone from Fellstrike is more than 3 times that of aggresive even though it only lasts half as long, and it even has a min damage mod which aggressive does not have.

As for mightystrike, a crit hit is about 1.7X normal damage, and a crippling blow is aroudn 2.5X normal damage. If you're berserk, mightystrike will do more damage. If not, fellstrike will do more damage. It is fairly unlikely to stay berserk for 30 seconds though on anything where your DPS might matter. Besides, Fellstrike leads to more impressive crits/crips.
