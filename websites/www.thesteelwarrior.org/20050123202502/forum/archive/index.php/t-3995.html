There's an old analogy, "Using a sledgehammer to kill a fly." There are trade offs to be had for sure. Lose a hell of a lot of DPS and some agro for sure, just so you can take a FEW less ripostes? sorry but no thanks. You don't get riposted THAT much (lets face it the percentage is small compared to your total number of attacks). Add in the fact that your dual wield check and double attack for your offhand will fire off a bit less than your primary weapon and you're really saving very little in the way of ripostes.

The trade off from Dual Wield to 2Handers (like BoW and any number of other similar weapons) is different. Less possibility for procs, for added DPS and riposte reduction. In this case the positives outweigh the negative.
