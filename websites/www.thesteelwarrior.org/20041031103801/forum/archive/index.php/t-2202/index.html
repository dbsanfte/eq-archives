Estebar
Part of the reasons for this is that some pieces are nicer for different fighting types.

Plate has pure melee, hybrid, and wis caster, so there's a more even distribution of "good pieces" for certain classes. Like hybrids getting ft3 boots but clerics have ft2 bracers. This keeps the prices more consistent across the different pieces.

Now all silk users are int casters, so the bracers are in high demand, but many could care less about getting truesight on a hat.
