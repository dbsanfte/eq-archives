My advice ... don't do it.

Don't start to parse.

It will make EQ less fun. It will become all about numbers and number crunching.

So you have two weapons that are far too close to call on which is better? Well, since you have to HAVE those weapons to parse them ... don't stress it. Ask around. Ask some friends. Ask here. Whatever. Then go with what you feel.

You'll know upgrades when they come. They'll be that obvious.

I just think the moment you start crunching numbers hardcore, you lose some of the fun of the game.
