Demorgoth pretty much hit it on the head.

One thing to be careful about is to avoid items you will replace. Not only will you be wasting DKP if you are in a DKP guild, but you'll have the pleasure of augmenting the new gear. Ring of Force looks pretty decent at first glance, but not only are there better rings in PoTime but also Glyphed Prayer Ring is not difficult to obtain in GoD.

Don't bid on Raex legs only to replace them with Greaves of Furious Might and then wish you had waited for Jointed Scale Greaves.

Don't replace your Raex helm in PoTime if you believe serious progression in GoD isn't that far off - Vadd's Helm of Elite Combat rocks. If this is practical for you, you might concider Arms of the Brute should Faerune be an effect you want to keep.

Every item Demogoth listed is either best in game or close enough that you will not be replacing it for a very long time. Lower priority than the items he listed I'd add Earring of Corporeal Essence, Platinum Cloak of War, and Necklace of Eternal Visions - not in the same league as the other items listed but worthy.

I think haste may be the biggest conumdum you'll face. There's a tendancy to put up with either 40% haste (Earring of Corporeal Essence) or an inferior item in a slot just because it has more haste. I may be wrong, but the best choice may be Shroud of Survival (haste)/Girdle of Intense Durability or Cord of Temporal Weavings (haste)/Pauldrons of Devastation.
