paladiin
Yes, procs are DEX based. Higher dex will result in more procs.

Also, keep in mind, that some weapons "like" to proc more often. That Barnacle Axe procs for crap - so infrequently that I found it wasn't even worth using. Many fights it never proc'ed at all, with a dex of 150ish. Other times, I got 1. Never, ever got more than 2 in a regular xp fight.

At the same time, my Black Blade of Tormenting (9/22 and procs Torment at lvl 46) procs like a madman with the same dex. Most I've done in an xp fight is 6, always at least 1, and usually 3-4. From 46 to mid 50's, it's a great inexpensive agro weapon, simply because it "likes" to proc.

I've also found that the Venomous Axe is another that doesn't like to proc a lot. 1-2 a fight is usually all I would get as well. Nice for a little extra damage, but not reliable enough to be a big source of agro.
