If your going for agro, take the CHoS. Nothing incites my anger more that people using it for DPS, seems like everyday I'm explaining to idiot_ranger01 that it is an agro weapon and should not be used for DPS. Drives me nuts to have to fight agro with some random DPS class to switch weapons from stun procs and whatnot.

The velium weapon wins, hands down on DPS. It has an excelent ratio and its very fast to maximize that mainhand ratio. But more to the point of your question, no, the ChoS proc will not make up for that extra DMG, especially on high level mobs where resists wil be much more common. Its not completely cut and dried, but if pure DPS is your concern, you would be best suited with the velium for low agro DPS.

I'm sure some will disagree with me, and say that the MT should worry about agro, if he's having problems then thats his fault. Ofcourse I'm also usually inclined to explain my issue with the weapon and after that if they take agro, they keep it. Usually after one or 2 times of them running around screaming for a heal (because I told the cleric to not to heal him if he takes agro willingly after having explained it to them) I find that they can usually procure another suitable combo with which to provide agro free DPS. This is coming for mid-50s experience groups that I usually MT for, and most of the time get compliments on my ability of sustain agro. (I guess noone knows how to use provoke/taunt correctly, or at least not in common knowledge.) My server is a bit slow I guess. Once a week I still come across a tank or melee that doesn't know about the revised disc system and/or the provoke line.
