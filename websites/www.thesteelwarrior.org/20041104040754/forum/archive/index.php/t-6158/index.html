Lilwee Beasty
Do Everfrost adventures. Thats the standard 'proc' slot name, for all weapons other than Time level and above.

Anger 2 is 400 pure hate, 510 points in EF
Anger 3 is 600 pure hate, 1150 points in EF

It's *very* tempting to get anger 2, since it's less than half the price of anger 3. However I personally recommend saving up for anger 3, if that is a combo you see yourself keeping for quite a while.
