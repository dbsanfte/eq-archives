Kizraks Bracer (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17303)

Give 500pp to Bor Wharhammer in Plane of Tranquility to recieve a Warrior emblem and a Mystical Furnace of Ro. Then Take your Mold the Emblem 1 Sheet of Ethereal metal and a Tanaan smithing hammer and combine them in the Furnace. That makes the Bracer.

I believe you need to be lvl 55 to do this
