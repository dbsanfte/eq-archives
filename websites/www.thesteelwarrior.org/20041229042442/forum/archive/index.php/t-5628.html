When I am chain pulling I pretty much loose endurance in a steady manner. Because I am using it as soon as it refreshes.

Incite eats away 8% endurance per use. You normally regen 5-6% in 30 seconds while standing.

Whenever I get to Tank-mezz a Rathe Councilman I end up OOE shortly after defensive runs out.

I start up with defensive and use Incite whenever it refreshes. I need all the aggro I can produce since I tank the Councilmen with a shield and a hategiver.

While Defensive is up, you dont regen endurance. You simply loose it at a steady pace. Every incite is -8% every 30 seconds. You can get 6 incite off during defensive. So thats -48% endurance loss to incite alone while defensive is running.

It takes defensive and around 12 incites to go OOE.

It takes around 40-45 seconds or so to go from 0% to 8% endurance.

I'm Level 65 and 305 on all melee stats. No endurance regen AA nor endurance+ items yet.
