Best 3 to add to those 3:

Bard. ~75DPS melee, +30% more DPS for other classes.
Druid. Spot heals, Circle of Seasons, Nukes, Evac.
Shaman. Malo, Slow (chanter slow sucks) debuffs, dots, heals.

At least get those 3. Hell, you can do without the cleric and chanter for that matter, but at least have those 3. I have trio'd HoT with a shaman, bard, and druid, so add a real tank and healer and you are rocking. I would personally ditch the chanter, as they will be completely useless there with a bard, druid and shaman (bard will cover haste better, shaman has better slow).

With one group, your kills will be slow, so gaters will nuke you till they are oom anyways, and the bard has a plenty good mana drain song at 60 to cover the last bit after they stop nuking.

I would pick the following if I had to choose the best group:

Warrior
Cleric
Shaman
Druid
Bard
DPS class
