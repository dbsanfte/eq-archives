I agree. 65 first, AAs second. Having AAs won't help you get groups in your 50s, and the AAs really will roll in faster at 65. AAs, as I recall, are a set amount of experience per AA, and at 51 you're getting much less experience per mob than you are at 65. Therefore the AAs will come slower for you now than later. If you really want a couple AAs, I'd recommend getting run 3. It saved my life so many times that I really feel it's the only justifiable AAs to get early.
