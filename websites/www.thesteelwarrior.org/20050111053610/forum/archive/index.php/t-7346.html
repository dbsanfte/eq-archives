Some of you might have seen the screenshots from the upcoming Omens of War expantion...Link --> http://everquest.station.sony.com/omensofwar/screenshots.jsp


Well today a friend and I where roaming around Nedaria's Landing and came across a Newer Model Taelosian female(Nideno Eliagy) in a cave up the river from the Wayfarer camp.

Nideno Eliagy gave a small description on her purpose and then refrerred me to a npc named Eldros Damor who is located on one of the docks in Nedaria's Landing. After some responses to Eldros I was given an Expadition(6 people/1 group) and the course was marked on my compass. I was lead back to Nideno, I hailed with the response "forgotten halls". She continued to explain the purpose of this Temple that has been hidden from the armies of the Muramites. When she was done, the rock behind her began to glow faintly, we then clicked in.

This zone was fairly laggy for me and amazing laggy for a guildy of mine.

The architecture is what the taelosians great temples and buildings are to resemble before the destructive Muramites came. One room in particular is very similar to the zone in at NTOV(room with the light gleaming into the center), it is quite a site imo. The zone itself is small, consisting of 6 rooms all connected by halls and non kos npcs. Three of the rooms have the new bat/rat models you seen in the above screenshots, one room consists of 12 or so bats, one with 12 or so rats, and another with a mix of bats/rats and small golems. Outside two of the rooms a npc asks you to get rid of the vermin inside the rooms, SK partner with me tried to single pull and got the whole room. The mobs hit for 140-470ish melee, a necro friend of mine zoned in and we did manage to kill 1 bat, but with it being early morning decided to leave.These seem like small tasks that might or could lead to another task and possibly be a small ring event. There is 2 oversized golems in the room that is connected to the first hall you see when you step out of zone in. They are being controlled by a few npc's who are making them fight against each other.


The purpose of this zone (imo) is just to give us a taste of what the Taelosians lifestyle was like pre invasion. Also it seems that this is all the screenshots that SoE has released on the upcoming Omens of War expantions compiled into a zone, a teaser to a degree.

Anyhow I'm posting this mostly becuase I had heard nothing prior to us stumbling upon this zone, and wanted to spread the word.

Please post here if you find any further info on this zone and anything I may of left out.
