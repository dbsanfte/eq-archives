First off, soloing doesn't have to end at 50. It just slows down. I did it and have never owned a Fungi tunic and I didn't get my clicky BP until lvl 60. Embalmers skinning knife is almost a must. I found grouping though to be faster and safer exp and I generally solo'd only when I was short on time to find a group or before PoP made travel almost trivial. I also used to kill low dark blue / light blues while working on BW.

Secondly, I have never owned a Wavecrasher so take this for what it is worth. Frostbringer is enough to hold taunt in offhand by itself with almost any "decent" weapon in onhand. SBoZ qualifies for that role. IMO your DPS if you choose to solo will be greater with SBoZ and Frostbringer. DPS is very important while soloing. Although, I would certainly grab a Truncheon of Doom when it will proc for you (50?). I am biased against 9/19 weapons as they seem to do craptacular damage so I never pursued a Wavecrasher.

For the pp a Wavecrasher will net, I would look at one of the 2 handers out there with a 1:1 ratio. Most are less than 10k and selling the Wavecrasher should net you one and pp to spare. This way you will have a good DPS/Agro 1 hand setup as well as a good DPS 2 hander.

One final note, if you have trouble with the aggro from SBoZ/Frostbringer, try reversing them for more proc's.
