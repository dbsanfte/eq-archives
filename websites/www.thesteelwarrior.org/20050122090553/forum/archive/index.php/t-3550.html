If you don't care about faction, its pretty easy to farm the high elf guards in Felwith near the zone. They are palidans but they drop 2h swords that sell for 20 plat each and occasionally they drop a fine steel weapon. There are 2 wanderers and 2 on bridge, then 2 behind wall by bridge. Also one on a catwalk past bridge and another up on the cliff you can get to come down by taunting. I usually make around 1000 plat in 4 to 5 respawn cycles with a charisma buff selling just the swords to vendors. They respawn just fast enough that I can sit and regen to full after each round. Be careful of the exterminator, he is green but agros and has a lot of hit points so you can not solo him. He is easy to avoid though. Take an invis potion so you can get by the higher level guards just outside the zone since you will be too encumbered to run. If you have a non-kos hydra, sell the swords to the NPC merchant next to the bridge.
