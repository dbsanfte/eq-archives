Just sitting here, on a Sunday afternoon, not doing anything ... and realized that this is the last weekend before the "patch."

Next week, lots of things are going to change.

Melee classes are going to start to become radically different.

My game. Your game. It's going to change.

So, here, I raise a toast on this final weekend, to all you warriors.

To the warriors who've been playing since Day 1, starting off in your newbie tunics and your rusty swords, running from those fire beetles back to the safety of your guards.

To the warriors who started just last week, with max sense heading, a free WR bag with the expansions you bought, and who knows what other goodies.

To the warriors who used to sport bronze and feel uber.

To the warriors who used to sport Cobalt and feel uber.

To the warrior who sport Raex's and feel uber.

To the warriors parsing out the differences between Vanazir, the Darkblade of the Warlord, and any other plane of time weapons you can get your data streams to analyze.

To the warriors who wouldn't even consider parsing a Forlorn Naginta!

To each and every one of you.

Here's to the past four years of Everquest. And here's to the upcoming changes. The future holds some hope.

:-)
