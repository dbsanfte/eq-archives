About the Coldain Ring Quest --
Some pitfalls to avoid:
Don't let your druid friends turn anybody into a wolf at any point while you're doing the quests. Imagine how bad it sucks to have the guy you're escorting across the zone suddenly decide that he needs to attack you. I riposted one of them to death while trying to camp.
On the 7th ring, don't hand the Dwarf anything (like your ring - Doh!) to try to get him going again after you defeat the giant/orc party that jumps you during the escort. He won't give the ring back before he poofs, and you get to start over (or whine to a GM).
On the 8th ring, make SURE both NPCs are up before you do your hand in. The second NPC (some dwarf wench) is NOT spawned by the hand-in; she's got to be up already on her own spawn timer.

Good luck!
