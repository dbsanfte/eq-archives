To make Brutal's argument even simpler:

Both ID and LR reduce incoming melee damage by 1.5%/level or 0.5%/AA. Putting that into HP terms that is 0.5% of 5400 or 27 HP/AA for unbuffed HP. Buffed at 7500 HP ID/LR get you 37.5 HP/AA.

Sturdiness give a 5400 HP tank 1.8% more HP for 4 AA, or 0.46% more HP per AA unbuffed. The math for Sturdiness is easy... 25 HP/AA (for level 1) and that does not change with buffs.
