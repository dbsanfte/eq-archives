As for "server of price gougers"... any server has people who will sell for the most plat that they can. It's no different on any other server. The only differences are amount of gear/plat in circulation, and the number of people looking for said gear. You don't have it any worse than anyone else.

As for your gear; work on upgrading the gear you currently have in slots where you have no HP. The gothic gear you are currently using would be a good starting point. Just keep an eye open in the bazaar, and when you do see something you could use and afford, buy it. As well, I see you already have some thurg quest armour. Keep hitting Kael, and get more. If your guild is doing the kunark dragons, then you should also be able to do HoT, for the SS quest armour. If not, keep going back to Kael and gearing up until you do have the gear to hit HoT. Also, keep grinding out in LDoN. Not only will you keep accumulating adventure points and charm stats, but you'll also get plat, and loot you can sell, in order to upgrade. Veksar and Droga would also be good options for you, both in useable gear as well as saleable gear. Bottom line is, with all the numerous in game options there are today, there is no shortage of ways to gear up to very nice levels. All it takes is some time and effort on your part.

As for places to exp; PoP planes are still far and away the best exp you will see for some time, imo. While you may think the Tier 1 planes are slow now, they're actually still quite good. At level 55, they're awesome. Places such as BoT, HoH, PoTactics are very nice, but you'll need more gear and levels to go there.
