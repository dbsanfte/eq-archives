I'd try the zone in for a bit, to get a feel for the place. Most mobs can be pacified very easily, I assume that harmony works as well. The courtyard just past the entrance is a good place to get your feet wet. Every mob can be single pulled with the proper use of pacify and an awareness of mob line of sight aggro.

The mobs that are really the most difficult are the ones that cast AE damage spells and slows. Bane of Thule is a nasty slow/dot that many of the casters proc as well.

Once you have a feel for how tough the mobs are and what you're able to deal with, I'd consider going in deeper.
