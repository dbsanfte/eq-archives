There is a mask/necklace set which drops in Hard LDoN which would be upgrades.

The best stuff in EP for warriors is Raex gear. Honestly i would get these 2 items to tide you over till 1 of 2 things happens.

1. you break into time and get upgrades there
2. your guild farms EP to the point where these face/neck items are extremely common and therefore cheap.

Concentrated on raex now as it will upgrade you the best. By being patient in the off-slots, you'll end up making much better use of your DKP.

Lance Armstrong doesnt win the Tour de France by trying his damndest to get first place in all of the flat beginning races ... he's patient and waits and saves his strength and goes for the throat where/when he has the advantage in the mountains.

If you jump high for all the nonsensical loot, then you will be left behind on the Visible plate which really matters.
