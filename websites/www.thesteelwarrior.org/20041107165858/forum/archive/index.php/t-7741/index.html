Sassinak
You'll find the GSO4W is better aggro early in fights, and might hold aggro through early slow where your dual wield does not. Therefore the staff might be preferable in places where mobs die super-fast, such as easy LDON. For any fight that takes a minute or more, you'll want the aggro procs from your dual wield.

Sassinak
There have already been threads about this. After the first few procs, aggro proccing weapons pull away and are of course superior. But over the short term (i.e. when the number of procs is zero or small), DPS weapons with high damage bonuses have superior aggro to low DPS or low damage bonus weapons. In an LDON where mobs die in 30-45 seconds, different weapons work well compared to what you'd choose for 3-15 minute fights.
