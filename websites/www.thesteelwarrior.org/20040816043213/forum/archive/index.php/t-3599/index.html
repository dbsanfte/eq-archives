While sitting here at work, righting a rather long and rambling post about class balance I came upon an idea.

One so simple and elegant to fix itemization, that would only require a couple weeks or so to fix, That I think SoE should really look at.

One problem I see in EQ is the LARGE amount of all/all gear and such.

One older problem was with Class specific gear that would drop, and then rot because it was no drop.

Both of these are problem, so what was the fix.

Well they made velious quest gear and now ornate and elemental gear. These are better than before, and yes there are still problems with them, but that is another discussion.

How about this idea.

You go out and you get an item of gear.
Lets say you get The Strength of the Elements...
nice shoulders
all/all AC 15 Str 15 HP 100 FR 15 CR 15

Now, lets say you take these shoulders and give them to your CLASS GUILDMASTER and get

The Warriors Strength of Elements...
War Only - AC 20 Str 15 HP 125 FR 15 CR 15
or
The Paladins Strength of Elements
PAL Only - AC 15 Str 15 Hp 75 Mana 75 FR 15 CR 15
or
The Enchanters Strength of Elements
ENC only AC 10 Str 15 Hp 50 Mana 100 FR 15 CR 15

Does this sound like a feasible soloution to you? They still need to learn to balance hp/mana and caster/melee stats but thats another argument.
