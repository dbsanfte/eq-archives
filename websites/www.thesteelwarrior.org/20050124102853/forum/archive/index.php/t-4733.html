Hi.

Monk here.

And this is an old, old topic for us monks.

Our epic is a bard song that boosts haste.

It stacks with worn haste.

Both the Celestial Tranquility Icon and the Visions of Grandeur Icon can be UP at the same time in your buff window. The hastes, HOWEVER, do NOT stack.

The other affects are what is stacking (AC and ATK boost from the monk epic click).

Song Haste IS Spell Haste.

And the HIGHEST haste you get either from a song or a spell will stack with your WORN haste.

That's how it works.

What confuses the issue is BARDS have special songs that are called OVER-haste songs. These songs will indeed STACK with spell haste like Vallon's Quickening, as well as worn item haste like Cloak of Flames.

These select songs were designed to do that however.

And as of right now, no item clicky has been introduced into the game yet that has OVER-haste capabilities.

I hope that helps clear it up a bit. If not feel free to ask more questions.

i'm pretty sure if you wanna check stacking on haste you just go to lucys & look up the spell/effect.

If the description says "Increase Attack Speed by x%", then thats "spell haste" & stacks with worn haste.

If the description says "Increase Haste v2 by x%" than that haste will stack with worn haste + "spell haste" up to whatever the haste cap is.

If the description says "Increase Haste v3 by x%" than that haste will stack with worn + "spell haste" + v2 haste & will increase the haste cap over whatever the limit is.
