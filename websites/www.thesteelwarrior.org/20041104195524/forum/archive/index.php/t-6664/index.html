Farming Tipt couldn't be any easier.

How to farm the Enraged Maternal Cragbeast
Group:
1 bard
1 cleric
1 enchanter
3 <insert class here>

SoE was kind enough to put a pooka at the base of the hill just before the mama lizard ring - this is the key to winning. The trick is to get an instance that has as few dogs as possible before the bridge. They are the only mobs that see invis before the bridge. Get a good bard to pull any dogs that are in the way and kill them at the zone in, any good bard can cherry pick single mobs easy. Once the dogs are dead invis up and run to the middle of the bridge and kill the wanderer golem. Then clear up to the lizard ring, don't forget to charm the pooka. When you trigger the mama lizard, have the bard preslow, then send in the pooka. Loot, leave expidition, rinse and repeat.
