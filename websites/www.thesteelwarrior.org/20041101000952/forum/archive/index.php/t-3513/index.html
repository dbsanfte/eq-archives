Eoden
I used to use BBB to offtank and regularly pulled agro off the tank to be splattered early. I recommend a good ratio 2hs with no proc or 2 decent ratio 1 handers.

DD or lifetap procs are ok, Rune 2 on the SEM doesnt seem to pull too much agro, but Rune 3 on the BBB seemed to be big agro.

For raids as a non tanking warrior not pulling agro is as big a part of your role as doing additional DPS and since almost every other melee aside from warriors and paladins have some way to mitigate agro it is something we need to be very careful with. It is worth wielding lower ratio non proc weapons if thats what you've got to do to stay down the hate list as you do more DPS in the long run and serve your guild better if you do 90 DPS for the whole fight rather than 110 DPS for half the fight and then pull agro and die.
