I don't think we've seen enough tests to say for sure but we do know that at least some spells have aggro that scales with level and/or HPs. Just because a casted stun scales doesn't mean that a stun proc scales the same way though. Same with other spell effects.

I'm not doing any reproduceable tests but I do have my offhand auged with the 125 DD ykesha and the aggro on it is quite noticeable. At the beginning of any xp group fight if any of my 3 main procs fire off (Anger, Anger 3, or ykesha) I get aggro and never lose it. Call of Fire seems to be noticeably less aggro but still significant. IMO the aggro difference between Anger 3 and Ykesha is small enough for high level mobs that the difference is insignificant.
