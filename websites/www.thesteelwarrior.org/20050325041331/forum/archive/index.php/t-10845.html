31/5/15 will serve you fine. The only things you need to hold agro is revenge and sunder armor, and all warriors regardless of talent spec have them.

Keys to said spec:

Full arms tree for damage boosting as well as +6 rage per charge
5 Cruelty for double base critical hit % rate
15 protection for 10% ac boost, 5% shield boost (or +10 def boost), 50% reduced bloodrage hp cost, instant 30% hp heal, almost autoblocking twice for 10 rage and if you are so inclinded 20% chance of stun per revenge.

The only thing you lose out on really is ae snare, enrage (becomes less of a factor the higher +def gear you accumulate), defiance (offset by your increased dps) and concussion blow (diminishing returns really neuter this hard). Of those 4 personally I'd only really miss ae snare for pvp or running from adds.
