MAF replacement on most vehicles is simple and straightforward, takes a few minutes. It's just a matter of removing a few bolts and the connector, removing the MAF and putting the new MAF into place, then re-installing the bolts and connector.

I'm not familiar with Chevys but the TPCS is probably accessible by dropping the transmission pan where the shift solenoids are usually located. I'm only familiar with imports so I could be totally wrong.
