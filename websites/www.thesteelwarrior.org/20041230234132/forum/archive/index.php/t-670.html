I agree that age is not necessarily a reliable indicator of maturity. I've met some 15 year olds who were quite mature and I've met some 30 year olds who were extemely immature. But age is an indication of experience. And with experience often comes, but not always, greater maturity.

People use the phrase, "you're behaving like a child" for a reason. Children tend to behave differently than adults, do they not?
