I don't know much about charms, in fact I've never even seen one in game, but I have heard how the effects work. An effect like CHRMRaining would mean that the charm would gain its full effects when it is raining. The charm with CHRMFewPlayers will get better stats on it as the number of players in the same zone as you decrease. So for example that charm would be good when soloing in an empty zone or something. If you just read the effect it's pretty much self-explanatory. Hope that helps.
