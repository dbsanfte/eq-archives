Battleblade
Neither the Obsidian Scimitar of War or a Sparkling Shortsword are aggro weapons. If you must chose between the two, the Obsidian Scimitar of War's minor AC debuff will generate more aggro. To be honest, I'd suggest obtaining a Copper Hammer of Striking or Sword of the Bloodsworn instead if possible.

An anger aug is fine for generation aggro on your offhand weapon. Permafrost is not - DD alone doesn't really generate aggro. Better yet might be Force (or Strike) of YKesha.

In a nutshell, my suggestion would be to obtain a CHoS and augment it with Force of YKesha.

If however you must stick with the weapons and augs you have mentioned, I'd suggest the Obsidian Scimitar of War with an anger aug on it.
