That 18 hour respawn on Stormfeather is wrong, I have camped him twice now and went by what someone had posted on the old board for his spawn time... 32 hours. I also have found that he does have a variance to his spawn time, +/- 2 hours. So sticking to the 32 +/-2 hours I have seen SF many times, though I did not get to kill him due to someone else being there ahead of me.

Honestly though you should look into getting ornate gauntlets, better and longer lasting clicky haste, unless of course you are set on geting the eyepatch.

So to anyone else camping him just remeber these few things, SF is NOT an 18 hour respawn, he is a 32+/-2 hour respawn, SF does NOT skip spawns, most importantly, make friends with who ever is camping him, if you can get a time of death from them you'll have a leg up on ALOT of other people who are camping him. Also if you just want to stand there with your' warrior to camp him, stand underneath the tree just a tad NW of the gambling gnomes, and look to the north, no coguars will aggro you and you'll be looking directly at SFs spawn point. One last thing, he is NOT up on server reset. :(

Hope that helps you and anyone else who's starting to go insane camping him. :)
