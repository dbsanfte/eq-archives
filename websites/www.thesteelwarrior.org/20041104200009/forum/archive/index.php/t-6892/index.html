Eriatha Egan
The effort put into this quest is NOT wasted. You will get AAs over time. Trust me, they'll come. However, you cannot simply pick up a 50ac 75hp backslot item any day of the week without putting any effort into it. Getting your AAs may pay off now, but later on down the road you'll kick yourself for not getting the shield earlier.

There is no "trick" to spawning Heratius Grolden. It is a completley seperate spawn time with his own completely seperate spawn point, which is located just in front of the right coterie if you're looking at the balcony from the hill (or left coterie if you're looking at the balcony from inside the castle). Once he spawns and you kill him, Valdanov Zefveer will spawn right smack in the middle of the balcony. You'll have about 45 minutes to engage him before he depops.

Believe me, I've done it. It's tedious as all hell, but once you acquire the ashes, you'll feel a sense of relief greater than you ever have been. The shield is just *that* awesome and I don't even plan to replace it until Time. Stick with it. The effort involved will pay off.

(And the storyline to the quest is very good too. Enjoy it for what it is.)

Mathas
Is there a place that you can build faction with the Katta questgivers as well as the guards?
Yes, in Sanctus Seru. For every faction that exists in Katta, there are mobs you can kill in Seru to raise it. The quest-givers for the Loyalist Shield of Honor, if I remember right, were Concillium Universus... to raise that, kill NPCs in the "Heart" building. I don't remember which building on the arx circle that is, but it's the one with the heart icon above the entrance doors. Shouldn't be hard to figure out.

Third floor has a lot of level 60 mobs on the appropriate faction, so you can even get some exp while working on this (assuming you're level 60+ yourself). Mobs are easily duoed/trioed, just avoid Praesertum Matpa. He's in the inner room at the center of the third floor.

And yes, it is possible for a cazic-worshipping iksar to be non-kos. I know the shaman who helped me when I was working on my earring quest is. I'm not sure how good is faction is, but I know he's not kos... so kill until it says "Your faction standing with Concillium Universus cannot possibly get any better." then get a good-raced illusion to be safe. You'll be fine.
