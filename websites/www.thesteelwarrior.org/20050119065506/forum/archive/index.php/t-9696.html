Well, Athrias's first point was the utility of the duration stun at that level of the game, and i could see how it would be handy.
However, at level 56 Athrias also appears to be mainhanding a BoC with Yeksha aug on it. I would think that mainhand gives a 56 war some fair latitude in chosing an offhand with utility and still retaining aggo based on the very nice mainhand attributes.
For the original poster, the question is how to maximize aggro and dps. Looks to me like the best dps weapon is the whip, and I don't think you should switch out a CHoS for the sandstone sword. Even on the level of utility, I think a regular group including a shaman and a druid would reduce the need for the sandstone's utility component.
If you're trying to squeeze every bit of dps out, you could do about three LDoNs for the heated blade aug or around five for the icicle aug out of Everfrost (sort of guessing on how many missions it would take. the heated blade is 60 points and the first icicle one is 100 points). They'd add a bit of dps for you without migrating to higher dps weapons that have no aggro procs.
Getting weapons that produce substantially better aggro *and* dps than your current set up outside of raiding and given your level might involve lots and lots of plat.
You are probably already pondering an LDoN Yeksha/Anger III augment combo, so I won't go into that.
