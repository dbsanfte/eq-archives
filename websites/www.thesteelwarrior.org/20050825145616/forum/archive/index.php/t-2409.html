As far as AC setups go you are missing a couple things:

Until I get an Aegis of Earth, I am hardpressed to go over 2K AC. So when I tank Xegony/FRo/Gintolaken/etc, I am in AC mode; a tiny bit of extra ATK/DS/AGI means nothing vs. survivability against a nearly untransitionable mob.

Speed of the Shissar (Xegony's AE overwrites SoV/VQ anyway.)
Slot Description
1: Increase Attack Speed by 66%
2: Increase AGI by 40
4: Increase Stamina Loss by 0
5: Decrease Stamina Loss by 3
6: Increase AC by 12

Niv's Harmonic
Slot Description
1: Increase Absorb Magic Damage by 10
2: Increase AC by 24

Bard mods are capped at 3.6. Ayonae's Tutelage will allow a bard to raise that cap, up to 3.9 (It might be 4.0, can't confirm).

For singing, you can stack:
- 1 instrument mod (epic, mask, etc)
Epic is 0.8 mod
Abalone Mask is 1.0 mod

- Resonance (Voice of the Serpent) or Harmonize (Shadowsong Cloak)
VoS is 0.6 mod
ShadCloak is 0.9 mod

- Amplification
Amp lvl 60+ is 1.0 mod

- Singing Mastery.
SM3/IM3 is 0.6 mod

base mod is 1.0 = 100% normal mod.

With Bard epic + Singing Mastery 3 + Amplification + Harmonize
1.0 + 0.8 + 0.6 + 1.0 + 0.9 = 4.3 mod

With Abalone Mask + Singing Mastery 3 + Amplification + Harmonize
1.0 + 1.0 + 0.6 + 1.0 + 0.9 = 4.5 mod

With the above and AT3, a bard will be over the cap, giving Niv's an effective 24 * 3.9 = +94 AC

To compare, Psalm of Veeshan would only give me ( 7 * 3.9 ) = +27 AC...Plus Niv's has a longer range (75 vs. 50), keeping the bard out of AE/ramp range. Let the other bards play resists if needed.

With Nine + CotR + WoG + SoS, I gain 132 AC from buffs + bardsong (your milage may vary) for a total of +226 AC (we have good bards).

Also, with maxxed STR/STA/DEX/AGI, I find the bard is more useful playing Niv's and CHA song. Solon's Charismatic Concord is also singing-based, so it reaps the same benefits from Harm/Amp as Niv's (45 * 3.9 = +176 CHA). Add Unfailing Reverence (SHM) for us oogly ogres and you are looking at +231 CHA, to help those Divine Interventions fire.
