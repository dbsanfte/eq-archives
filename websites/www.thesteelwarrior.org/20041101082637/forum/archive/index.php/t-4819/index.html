Bonequest
SsRA is harder than NToV. Gear up in Halls of Testing in ToV for a few weeks, then do NToV for a few weeks, then you will be prepared for SSRA. Once you have mastered SSRA (might need to recruit a few more people to do hard mobs like Emperor) you can move on to farm Vex Thal. Once you farm Vex Thal to the point of utter boredom, your guild will be able to easily get through the flag process to go to elemental planes. If you skip SSRA and VT, you can stiill do flags but might need a few more people and a while longer to do it. You can form an alliance with a similar geared guild too, instead of recruiting more people.
