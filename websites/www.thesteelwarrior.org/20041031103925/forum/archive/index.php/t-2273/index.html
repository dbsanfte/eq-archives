Jakle, I don't know about your server but certainly on mine the top guilds bottomfeed the hell out of Ssra and AoW, finding those mobs up is nearly impossible. Nothing quite like groups from Elemental guilds killing Ssra mobs to gear up their alts to make otherwise Ssra capable guilds really hope all the ubers quit when LDoN comes out.

In answer to the original question, since you are NToV capable, definitely work on gearing up your warriors with the TSBS off Eashen. Good for dps and shouldn't steal aggro too often. Outright buying GoTs rather than trying to deal with getting Middle South camps in BoT might be your easiest bet for 2h aggro weapons. The hammer and sword from Droga should be well within your grasp also. Xanamech isn't too hard although my guild has only done it with 65 warriors. So far only one sword off Xanamech but we have horrible luck with those kinds of things.

Once you have more folks in the 62+ range, try Auliffe in BoT for the Greatstaff of Four Winds to replace the TSBS.

Anyway, your best bets for gearing up your warriors in aggro weapons is xp/loot groups in Droga, raids on Xanamech, and buying GoTs. For dps, beat Eashen like a drum until you get a TSBS for everyone.
