07-02-2004, 04:51 PM
rampage list is set by the order in which people aggro the mob. The moment you pop onto his hate list, you're on his rampage list. Mobs that have single target rampage start with highest person on that list (first to aggro) and work down, changing targets because of one of three things-

1. the highest person on the list dies
2. the highest person on the list is out of the mobs rampage "range" (far enough away that rampage can't hit you)
3. the highest person on the list gets aggro

If any of those happen, Rampage moves on to the next person on the list. There is no such thing as "AE rampage", it's just a mob that has enough active rampage targets that it can hit everyone in the raid within range with it's rampage.

If you really wanna do AL and set ramp correctly, let paladin's and shadowknight's pull the room before AL and AL's room using harmshield/divine aura's. The pallies/sk's that do the pulling will be at the top of AL's hate list, and will effectively be your rampage tanks for the fight. Using the HS/DA thing is so they don't get hurt and healed, if they do get healed, the priest healing them will be high up on the rampage list as well.
