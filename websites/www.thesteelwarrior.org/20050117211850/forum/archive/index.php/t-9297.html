Kizrak's BP is much better then Vindi's BP or a BP of eradication. It'll last you until you get enough points for a Royal Attendant BP. In the meantime, a velious BP isn't a bad choice.

As for getting it for the clickie, that's just a perk. It doesn't stack with focus, but I find times where I use it anyway just for the AC and MR boost.

I'd say ornate gauntlets first. Clicky haste is a HUGE bonus. You will NEVER have to worry about trudging out to Iceclad and looking for Stormfeather. :)

Other ornate pieces I'd say is a matter of preference. I found fierce heraldic bracers to be superior to Kizrak's, but I wasn't concerned about the ATK. I wanted AC, HP, saves, and dex. Similar with the boots. There are better, tradeable things, that you can pick up cheaper instead of ornate boots.

Blindeye makes a nice bow slot item too. It's a decent pre-EP bow, and permanent see-invis is handy.


Go for the biggest bang for the buck.
