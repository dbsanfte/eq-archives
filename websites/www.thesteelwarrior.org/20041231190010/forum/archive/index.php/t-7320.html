From the Library i retrieved links to get these Lucy Raw Spell Data Sets:

Evasive

Description
1: Increase Chance to Avoid Melee by 50%
2: Decrease Chance to Hit by 33%


Defensive

Description
1: Increase Melee Mitigation by 45%
2: Decrease Damage Modifier by 55%
3: Chance to Critical Hit(0)


Stonewall

Description
1: Increase Melee Mitigation by 45%
3: Chance to Critical Hit(0)
12: Decrease Movement by 70%


How does the Melee Mitigation increase from Stonewall compare to Defensive?

Obviously the chance to avoid in Evasive would be better suited to Mobs with high damage bonuses or procs / flurries etc. But if Defensive is halving the damage interval with a damage modifier decrease AND increasing melee mitigation, what would be the benefit of Stonewall other than the increased DPS?
