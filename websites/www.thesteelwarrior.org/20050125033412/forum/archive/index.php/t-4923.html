There's a new dwarf NPC over in Freeport East, where your old epic quest NPCs are.

Talk to him. He will offer to "swap" your old scabbard for new versions of your swords.

You hand him your scabbard and you get all three swords, the 2hander, and the two 1handers back.

These new versions have slots for augmentations.

Now the fun part is that if you already HAVE all three swords (you did the mage summoned sword trick), you will now have duplicate copies of all of them!

So you can have two red blades, two blue blades, and for whatever reason, two jagged blades.
