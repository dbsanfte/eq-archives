If you're getting into a guild nowadays that's just now breaking into Time, then you're looking at a guild that is primarily doing the following content:

-Pantheon encounters
-Plane of Fire
-Plane of Earth/B
-Working for Ikkinz

Now, a lot of guilds don't stop doing the elementals the moment they reach Time. They usually know that, in order to be initially successful with the Phase4 gods, that each member must be reasonably equipped. At least on my server, many guilds who hit Time dabbled in the elementals for a good month or two afterwards, or until they finally defeated Quarm. Not to say that they went after elemental mobs every waking moment, but if a full pop was up in Fire, for instance, they would be all over it. Earth-B is very quick and very easy, so that would be included in the list tool, not to mention that a guild breaking into Time doesn't need 60+ to defeat these minis like a guild who just defeated Rallos Zek would.

So, with that in mind, you'll probably want to look at loot, for the most part, found in these zones.

-Raex molds are a given. They will also be your #1 biggest upgrades.
-Blade of War if you can get your hands on one. From everything I have read, the Darkblade is incredibly rare. Snagging a Blade of War would be a solid, wise, long-term investment. :)
-Clawed Earthcrafters Hoop cause you'll probably be doing EarthB a lot.
-Drape of the Agile. It's not a spectacular backslot, but you may it to be incredibly cheap if you're using a DKP system.
