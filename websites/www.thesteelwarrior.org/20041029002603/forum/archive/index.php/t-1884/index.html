Surwraith
I think there are several good Kunark places that are commonly overlooked these days:

Dalnirs from 27 to 34+
(did a weekend LAN party of alts from 30 to 35 and owned every mob in the zone. BL, SK, War, Clr, Ench, Bard) Bring your own group.

CoM from 35 to 45+
Tons of mobs here, and a Cleric and Warrior can duo nicely.

Kurns Tower 1-20
Yes, I Pl'd here. But that was with a cleric healing me once in a while for about 5 hours to get to 20. At about 19 I went to PC and got to 25 in another 3 hours simply duoing with another warrior

Dreadlands 35 to 47+
Yes, this is slower. Yes, this is not the best XP in the game, by far. Yes, you WILL get a group inside 30 mins 99% of the time. Longest it has taken me to group was getting from the portin towers all the way to KC ent with SoW. So, 10 mins? As wars can't really solo, we need a place where we're assured of a group. DL is the place for this reason alone. If you can get a group nearly any other dungeon, forget DL.

Kaesora from 33 to 45
Bring your own group and be prepared for an AWESOME ride. This is one of the best kept secrets in Norrath. You can bind right outside (EVERYONE, there is a bind spot), PoK will take you right to it (as will a wiz port in to EJ, though that takes Levi + wall scale + a zone to get to). And to top it off, there is some good stuff there still. +25% XP bonus, group bonus, and a lot of fun.

Sebilis 45+
Still a great place. Good money-maker, great XP, and tons of fun.

Old world?

SolA and SolB are nice, and if you're in the 30's+, SolB will get you faction to eventually go get a tinker's back (a must need for any player).

Guk 20+ (Upper) 35+ (Lower)
Still people lingering around here, and I still find this a fun zone to play around in.

Hole 45+
tons of mobs + XP bonus = GOOD.

Droga + Nurga
Experiment here. They have upped the zone recently, and where I would mosh hard with a full group of low 30's before and they were blue, now they're red and I was duoing Necro and Warrior through the entrance mobs getting 3% a kill. This was yesterday.... But we're twinked somewhat and both serious alts, so take that into account.

Lots more to explore. Play around and have fun. And as soon as you can get PoP group, DO!!!! 51+ should get a group in most PoP zones. If you have the skills it takes to be a GREAT warrior, then 51 will get you a group in PoJ/PoD/PoN/PoI regularly. PoP XP is insane, plain and simple. I have tested it out every way I can, with a warrior, wizard, and necro, and PoP is greater than any place I can find solo, grouped, duo'd, etc.

Hope that helps!!!!
