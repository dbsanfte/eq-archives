Allow me to explain, whislt the HP>AC Warrior and the WIS>INT warrior will come out fairly equal on standard XP mobs bugs, gnolls, spiders, orcs etc. The wise warrior has the advantage when it comes to the real bad boys out there ie Giants, Spectres, and for the really adventurous Terrorantula (VERY SCARY VERY BIG SPIDER) In these cases the WIS>INT warrior has a huge advantage because they will normally have the brains to run away rather than stand there and go duh hit me.
