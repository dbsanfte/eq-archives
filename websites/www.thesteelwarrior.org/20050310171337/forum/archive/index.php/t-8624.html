1)MT=Main Tank.A heavy tank used for the hardest hitting mob generally.Often also the MA.He is the MT because he tanks(keeps aggro and eats the bulk of the damage ) the main mob.

a)ST=Second Tank.A heavy tank that will normally step in for the MT if he falls,or, tank another heavy hitter.

b)3T=Third Tank.Third tank in the order.Same basic job as ST....this can go on as long as needed with the descending order of heavy tanks.


2)MA=Main Assist.A person that designates targets for the dps.Often the MT.Often a tank when not the MT.However,it could be anyone who is best situated to call targets.Generally it's safest when things are busy for this person to be a good aggroing reasonable tank who can take hits from trash and or handle mez breaks.Doesn't have to be tho.The type of fight plays role in the appropiate choice.

a)SA=Second Assist.If the MA gets incapacitated the SA assumes his role.

b)3A=Third Assist.As above and so on...

Lots of fights are differant and benefit from differantfolks in differant jobs.Often certain people are more practiced and therefore get the jobs by default.

ie: When we were in POW I preferred a fast paladin to ma with their good ranged aggro on clearing . I would get aggro on their target and do the most tanking.I found it easier with a good knight because debuffs would start at range with multiple mobs in a slow to close environment.I still normally tanked the most when stuff hurt so i was MT and the knight was MA.Because folks were used to me calling targets and following me around I was often assingned the MA & MT role as one.There are also lots of fights where you don't want the dps on the MT's target so they are directed to add targets by an MA.Folks here could go on forever with examples...
