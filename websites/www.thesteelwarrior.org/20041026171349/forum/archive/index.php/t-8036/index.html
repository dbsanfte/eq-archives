Eriatha Egan
Bertox is single target rampage. Start with Furious on the MT and Fortitute on the RT the moment he is pulled into camp (and he has a huge ass melee range and hit box, so be careful). Bertox has pretty lowish hp (~600k), but makes up for it with some nasty, nasty damage output. (~1100dps)

Rallos Zek is the AE Rampager, although his melee isn't all that vicious (500-600dps). He does have a crazy AC debuff though called Sedition, which makes most of his hits land for near max. Still, even with the debuff, his DPS isn't what makes the fight difficult. He's got around 1.2 million (or more) hitpints, as well as an army behind him to take care of. :)

Lord Mithanniel Marr is a bit in between, having high damage output (850-1000dps melee + proc damage) and high hitpints (1.3+ million). It's a straight shot MT fight though with no [real] rampage or AE rampage to worry about. Just gotta kill him before his room repops.
