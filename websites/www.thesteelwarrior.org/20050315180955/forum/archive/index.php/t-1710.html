Echo caverns from 25-30 something. You may be able to go up to around 55 in there moving to different spawns.

Between 30 and 35 the fungus side starts to green out while the deep side is still a bit tough. Over 50, most of the deep is going to be turning green, but you can kill the taskmaster.

Velks or PoJ can probably get you to 62, although 61 is going to be endless pain. At 62, PoJ zone out greens out.

If you're evil, OT works in the 30s - mid 40s. Nurga in the mid 40s, at least until the zone gets updated in a week or two.
