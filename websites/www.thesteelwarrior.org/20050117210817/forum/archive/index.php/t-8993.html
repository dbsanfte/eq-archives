Get a good set of weapons and some haste, and dont bother with high end armor till you get to at least 50, or else like others said you'll be buying and selling too much. I'd recommend the newer plate smithed armors, like sandblasted or gothic steel. Or perhaps some Crustacean shell, for the mega stamina boost. And either a Fungi or Ceremonial Iksar Chestplate works wonders, the Regen makes you unkillable through the lower levels. Just get solid AC and some HP rings/earrings to round it out. All this is cheap stuff you can level quickly with and save your cash for when you can get full benefits out of the recommended levels.

Edit: check out this magelo of a warrior i just threw together in about 20 minutes. 22% haste from the SCHW (3k or less) and almost 500hp at lvl 1... weapons as personal preferences dictate, but none of the gear on this character costs over 5k, total prolly about 12-15k worth of gear depending on the weapon expenditures, or you can substitute a regen tunic for the Velium BP. This set up will easily get you to 40, when you can start substituting out for more expensive lvl 50+ recommended gear.
