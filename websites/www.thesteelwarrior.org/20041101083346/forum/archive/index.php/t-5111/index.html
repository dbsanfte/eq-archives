It probably does exist, but, you won't get it, not without putting your guild through torture. And hanging. And fire. And lashing. And stoning. And even execution.

From the Allah link, it drops off the seventh hammer, and from the name/stats, it's about right. To get to the seventh hammer, you need to do all six of the Justice trials, successfully. You'll need about 30-50 people to do this, depending on the strength of your guild, then you get to fight the Seventh Hammer. I've never seen him, but he's probably on the Terris Thule level, maybe harder. Has very nasty mana-drain AEs, and no place to hide.

Good luck.
