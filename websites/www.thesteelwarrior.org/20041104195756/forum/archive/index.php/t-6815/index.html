tactics is your friend ... i can think of a plethora of 2-3groupable bosses (not counting VZ) who drop good stuff.

anival drops a heavy tooled weapon belt and ornate greaves
the tallon zek side guy drops the earing 85hps
the guy at the back of the zone drops the black plated collar (very nice)

if you have very talented strong friends you can also do vz with anywhere from 6 to 18 people. his scimitar would be a healthy upgrade aggro/dps/statwise .... and his necklace is 100hp and 18 to all saves with some stats. his belt is ft3 ... but it also 100hp.

i wouldnt roll against any casters in my guild for the belt ... and maybe the neck (not sure on that one yet), but if you have strong alts/friends, then farming him is pretty easy to do for personal interests.
