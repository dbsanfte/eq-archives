you're confusing Faction with Religion/Diety.

Faction is how well certain mobs in EQ like or dislike you. Doing quests, killing mobs, gaining faction modifier items -- these are way you can alter your factions.

Religion/Diety is fixed from the character creation screen onwards and cannot ever be altered.
