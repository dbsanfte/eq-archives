Ah, hmmm. Okay, as has been pointed out, level to 52 and you'll get an improvement to you incite line (Bellow, which has an initial hate of twice what your current incite-line disc -- provoke -- has).
I'm going to say that you won't see a dramatic improvement in your aggro until you get either the copper hammer of striking or the sword of the bloodsworn. Buy whichever is cheapest first. On my server, that would be the bloodsworn, but check prices for a few days. Again, server prices vary, but there was a bloodsworn up on my server the other day for 7K, so 7-9K is probably what you are looking at needing. If you can't get both of these by the time the Great Staff of Thunder will proc for you, get that and use it until you have both droga weapons. Get it anyway as it costs only 200 plat or so and you'll have a 2-hander (can't equip until lvl 55, if I recall correctly).
While you are in the bazaar, buy some Halas 10-pound meat pies and (if there is any for sale) grobb liquised meat. (Make sureyou know how to use stat food; there a thread here somewhere on it if you don't.)
Second I would say that probably the best aggro set up you have now would be the cutlass in mainhand and the bloodguard in offhand. Eventually (once you get closer to rec level) the defenders will be better offhand than the bloodguard, but right now it isn't. it would be advisable for you to get one of the droga weapons before that happens, though.
Because it's critical for you to hold aggro, I'd pay absolutely no attention at all to keeping your weapon skills up at this point. Use the best weapon set up for aggro that you can. Sell everything else, as Holywrath says.

You didn't ask, but you're also going to need better armor soon. What you do for that depends on your capacity for farming/running a bazaar trader, whether or not you have a group of friends you regularly hunt with, and so forth. If you don't have a regular group, you can probably get a group in PoJ for the bump you need to level 52; you can also get LDoN groups. You're better off in the long run saving Everfrost LDoN points for anger augs before you spend anything on armor, but you're getting close to having such low ac that you will frighten clerics (the cowards!). But fi you do some BB LDoNs, and you are really desperate, I think a lot of the 150-point armor would be an upgrade for you, and I don't think I'd spend any money on armor until I got my weapons squared away.
If you have a good group of friends and you get to lvl 52, you can hope for dropped augments in LDoN; you might be able to convince some of them to go to Kael, where, if the random number generator favors you, you might get a corroded drop. Champions would be the easiest quest armor, I think.
Around in there you could also try Veksar. Research kylong shinguards and the soulscream belt (your haste is fine, but the belt is sellable). With a good group in the mid 50s, you could get to both mobs, I think.

You have a lot of work to do: welcome to life in the low 50s as an untwinked warrior. Don't get discouraged.
Good luck
