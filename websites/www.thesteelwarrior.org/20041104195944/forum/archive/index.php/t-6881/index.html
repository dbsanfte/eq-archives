If you have the cash to blow, try this:

Fierce Heraldic Kicks mojo ass. Full set and you are rockin. Sure, resists might be low, but hey your AC / HP will be smilin. If you have the cash and the levels where all this junk would make sense...

Rings: Steel Rings of Earthen Resilance (or Valorium rings of Gallentry to save some bling)
Ears: Bloodmetal Earrings of Engagement
Waist: Heavy Rubi Girdle
Back: Cloak of Flames (haste)
Feet: Bloody Boots of the Dark Rites
Face: Amalgdyn Chain Mask (or however you spell it)
Shoulders: Horn Spiked Shoulder plates
Neck: Black Plated Collar (or Talisman of Fate or GoD Shell collar thing or Amulet of Mummy Wrappings)
Head: Helmet of Rallos Zek
Wrists: Crystalized Acid (or Mana Threaded Bone Bracelet - although the Fierce Heraldic Bracers are quite good in and of themselves)
Hands: Warlords Steel Gauntlets (or Velium Encrusted or for less $$ go Kylong Darkmail)

Pick and chose with all your $$ and you will be outfitted as best of the bazaar.

If you have the BoC to mainhand, pick up a Copper Hammer for off hand (or a SBoD if you think your BoC is enough agro :P ), WMBS for dps 2hand and a GSoT for 2hb work.
