Battleblade
Nod Mistmoore.

It will help if you know the zone thoroughly and use a tracker to make sure some name mobs are up. While the loot isn't great compared to what you can get in the bazaar, if you are all somewhat undergeared spoting the Cloaked Damphyre, Maid Isis, Mythi Davisi, Advisor, Avenging Caitiff, and Garton Viswin could net you some usable items. There are also a number of quest items, particularly for Paladins. (And Shaman, but the Black Dire bites.)

There are two major great features. Repop is twenty minutes. So, if you clear all the named mobs or their placeholders there's a chance that other named will apppear on repop. Secondly, it's a classic dungeon crawl. There are mobs to avoid, see invis mobs, walk through walls, and places where the whole raid has to stop and fight with a good chance of being wiped if you don't have your act together.

Nice and scary at level 20-26. One of the very best zones in the game.

A shame Mayong went on vacation and has never returned. He would make the zone perfect in every way.
