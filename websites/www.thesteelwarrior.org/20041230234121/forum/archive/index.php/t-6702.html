You have two choices when it comes to picking a race for your warrior;

1) What you think is fun

2) What is most effecient/easiest.


If you want to play a gnome or halfing, go for it.

However, if you pick a race that can choose Rallos Zek as their diety, you'll have a significantly easier time playing. Fierce Heraldic gear is comparible to ornate gear, but it costs MUCH, MUCH less, and you can even make it yourself if your up to getting your smithing to grandmaster and farming the components for it.

If you choose an ogre warrior that worships rallos zek, you'll have the highest starting STR, STA, and you're guarenteed to never be stunned from the front - which is great. Other classes have to spend AA points to have a reduced chance at being stunned, and ogres can get it as well to be practically unstunnable from any direction.
