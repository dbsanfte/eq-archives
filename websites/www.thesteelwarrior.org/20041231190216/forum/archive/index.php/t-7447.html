06-05-2004, 02:18 PM
Don't put any into int or wis. STR STA AGI DEX and CHA are the stats that matter on a warrior. STA is the most useful, but also one of the easiest to max. CHA is the hardest to max, but only matters on high level raids, so it's a good choice if you're planning on going to the high end, but useless otherwise. AGI and DEX do next to nothing. If it was me I would go CHA if you're planning on leveling to 65 asap and raiding, STA if you're not.

I'm guessing you're coming from Diablo II or some similar game? Really starting stats in EQ aren't all that important. With a troll it's impossible to make a bad warrior (any race really, but trolls have really solid stats no matter what you do) so don't worry about it. You could put all your points in INT (which does nothing for a warrior) and still wind up with a great warrior.
