Warrior Disciplines

What is a discipline?
A discipline is an activated skill. They can be used for a variety of situations.

How do I use a discipline?
First, you must purchase the discipline tome and hand it in to a warrior guild-master. These can be purchased in East Commonlands or in the Plane of Knowledge. After you hand the tomes in, there are two ways to activate a discipline: You can type /disc Discname (ie: /disc defensive) or you can right click your combat abilities window (ctrl+C) and navigate to the correct discipline. This will create a hotkey.

The ability to use a discipline also requires you to have sufficient endurance.

Can I stop a discipline once I've started it?
Yes. To do that you click the "buff" icon in your combat abilities window.

What is a reuse timer?
Every discipline has a reuse timer. This is the 'cooldown' time until you can use that same discipline again.

What is an upkeep cost?
An upkeep cost has to do with endurance. In EverQuest, a 'tick' is every 6 seconds. For disciplines, the upkeep cost is deducted from your endurance pool every tick. This means you could find yourself running out of endurance very fast if you don't pay attention to it.

Which disciplines are on seperate timers?
We have color coded the chart below to help you with that. Disciplines under different colors are on independent timers. There are 4 different timers, so you can (assuming you have the endurance) use four disciplines back to back.
