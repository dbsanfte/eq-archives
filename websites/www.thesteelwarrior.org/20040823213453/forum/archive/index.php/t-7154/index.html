Ok, I am a member of the Guardians of Light.

We are a bit different then most of the other elemental guilds. We don't have level restrictions or AA requirements. If someone wants in the guild and gets the vouches, they could be a level 1 for all that it matters. We also don't require raid attendance.

As a result we have a real mixed bag on raids. Sometimes 30 people show up, sometimes over 100. For the smaller raids it isn't unusual to have one of our paladins acting as raid main assist. The warriors are saved for off-tanking unmezzables and bosses.

Adlerr is one the paladins that occasionally acts as main assist and him getting a BoW wasn't a problem.

As for DKP, we don't bid. Highest points gets the item. However, there is a sanity check; our definition, not popular opinion. If a ranger had bid on the item against a warrior or one of our raid main assists I am sure they would have been refused regardless of DKP.
