ThornyEQ
I ended up giving my SBoZ to my little twink ranger; she's a slice-o-matic with that thing. I think it's a great weapon for the lower levels (meaning below 50), especially if you're on a mission to get to 50 and beyond quickly. The delay, ratio, and 100 HP's it gives you are great for getting your skills up and not worrying so much about dying.

However, as you may have noticed, I (meaning my warrior) no longer use it. The CHoS and SotB from Droga are excellent weapons post-50, until you can get some of the TB/EB weapons. Also, the Greatstaff of Thunder (GSoT) is by far the best aggro bang for your buck at 55+. GSoT's are 1-2Kpp on most servers...crazy low prices for such a great weapon. I paid 32Kpp for mine when they were first showing up and I don't regret it one minute; it's served me well.

But back to your original question: no, you didn't make a mistake. The SBoZ is a great weapon for getting you up in levels. You'll eventually replace it, though, and that's just a fact of EQ life.
