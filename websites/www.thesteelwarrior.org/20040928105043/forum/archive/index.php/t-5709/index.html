Just to toss a few figures out there(assuming scrm(50% aa extension):

SLTW mana cost in one hour(5 melee in group): 3750 mana

ToA mana cost in one hour: 1500

So a difference of 2250 mana over an hour.

Assuming base(no item or spell) total melee dps to be 250:

SLTW(41% item haste will be assumed for all comparisons): 502.5dps

ToA: 477.5dps

Given perfect conditions, damage difference in one hour: 90,000

The more melee you add to the group the more the damage difference will be between the two, and the more dps prehaste the larger the gap as well.

For instance if the group's unhasted dps was 500 there would be a 50dps advantage using sltw and a damage difference of 180,000 over the hour.
