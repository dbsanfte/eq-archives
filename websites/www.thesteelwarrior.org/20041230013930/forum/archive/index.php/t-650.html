Well, first thing's first ... filling empty slots for free would be a good idea.

So Dervish Cutthroat Ring > Empty Slot.

;-)

Uh, regarding rings ... 6/65's would be the way to go if you're looking for something to buy.

Black Sapphire Electrum Earrings have good hit points.

Glimmering Mithril Torque is pretty cheap for a neck item that'll serve you well till you get better.

Tattered Mantle is your standard shoulders fare till you get better.

Hooded Black Cloak will be cheap and easy on the HPs and AC for your back. Again, until you can afford better or loot better.

I'd look towards getting a haste item, like an FBSS or something like that.

Weapons wise, there's a TON of good options in your price range.

2handers, maybe a Reaver or a Stonebladed Axe of Dismay, or a Tae Ew 2hander or a Tae Ew War Maul or something along those lines. Greatsword of Shadows even?

1handers, hmmmm ... Frostbringer and a Guard Captain's Mallet? Or a Velium Warsword of some type? Or Curved Claws?

I'd recommend going cheap on the empty slots of ARMOR and saving up for good weapon(s). That'll help you get back on your feet quicker.

Good luck. Have fun.
