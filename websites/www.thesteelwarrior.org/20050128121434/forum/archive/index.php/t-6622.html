" I've seen a 40/40 (non magic) sword for 2k a couple of times and might be able to sell some some stuff to get one."

The Reaver, from Kael Drakkel, is now tagged as magic. This change is over six months old.

"I'm sure it will get better after I hit 20 right?"

You're not even level 28. Which means ratio is all that matters for your weapons. At level 28 you get a damage bonus. Then delay becomes a small issue. HOWEVER, keep in mind that faster = better doesn't REALLY begin to matter until your damage bonus actually becomes substantial. This is usually in the 51 to 65 range of levels.

Another thing to consider about 2handers: Their damage bonus sucks ass until level 55 or so. Which means dual wielding, with the really good weaponry that's available today, remains probably the most viable DPS option for dual wield classes until level 55+

That being said ... a Reaver would work just fine for you AT LEVEL 28. Before that, use an Ornate Greatblade or Nathsar Greatsword (the sword you currently have). Because your damage is capped till 20, and your damage bonus doesn't even begin till 28.

Keep all your skills trained.

Just remember ... the big hits and fancy crits are nice to look at, but good ole dependable dual wielding remains one of the most effective DPS options for monks/warriors through MOST of their levels.

This changes at 60, with triple attack, and high damage bonuses, and RAID QUALITY WEAPONS. But you're nowhere near that level of play yet.
