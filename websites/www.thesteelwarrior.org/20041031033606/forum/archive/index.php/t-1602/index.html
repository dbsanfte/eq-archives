well, first off, if your AL capable, Ebony Armguards ( i think thats what they are called, 80hp arms from AL)

Also from AL, Great Helm of True Vision (45ac 100hp helm)

AL is on par with AoW, so look into killing big orange.

Ditch the Pearly sarnak bauble, pick up a mosq leg, Bloodmetal, or Lizard bone/tooth earring.

Va'Dyn is your friend, he has a Shaderock Amulet that he drops quite a bit, and would look really good around your neck, or hope for Faded Skull Medalion from AL.

if your in Ssra, i assume you can do NToV with ease. Eashen has a nice mask you may want to look at, Mask of the Sky. As well as a ring, Ring of the Sky

If your doing AL, you can kill everything in ssra save emp and High priest. Convince your guild to kill cursed till you have a bloodfrenzy, and kill Creator till you have a Hategiver.

Cursed also drops a nice BP, and a very nice cloak (BP of the Void 65ac 110hp, Cloak of Dark Shadows (see my magelo))

Creator has a nice bracer that seems to elude me every time it drops (adamantium wristguard), and is a much easier kill than people think, hell, we had a chanter tank him once, just for shits and giggles.

this should give you a few ideas. hope it helps
