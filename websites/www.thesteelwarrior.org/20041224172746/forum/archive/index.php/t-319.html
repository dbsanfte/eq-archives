Frostbringer has better DPS to go along with a relatively nice aggro proc. So it will, overall be the better weapon.

Anklesmasher has the same ratio as snare whipe. So in the offhand, they're even. But the stats edge goes to the 1hb. So it's probably the better option.

MAINHAND, Smasher will be better than the whip because of the damage bonus being applied more.

But overall, Frostbringer is the slightly superior weapon both mainhand and offhand.

But try not to stress about it. They're all on roughly the SAME level, in regards to weapon quality and aggro generation. You won't see too huge a difference between them.

Sure someone will post here saying "Oh YEAH? Well my Frosty kicked the crap outta the Anklesmasher I used to wield." And someone else will say "Oh YEAH? Well my anklesmasher procs 16.2 billion times per combat round once I got my dex up to 101" ... etc., etc., etc.

But seriously, Frostbringer ... Anklesmasher ... they do the same job at about the same level of effectiveness.

Then comes your red epic.

Then comes the Cord Hilted Spike Driver.

The comes Enraging Blow weapons.

And somewhere in the middle of ALL that crap is high DPS 2handers.

Enjoy the puzzle, it's part of what makes being a warrior fun ... the TOYS!
