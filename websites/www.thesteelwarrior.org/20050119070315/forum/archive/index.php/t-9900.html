Unless you really want to fill that slot badly, I'd say nothing. Other tak armors may not be as big an upgrade, but you'd still get more use out of another piece of armor than a slot 3 aug, especially if you're looking at paying something like 1150+ for an aug. The only tak upgrade that wouldn't be an upgrade is helm, and bracer looks like the most likely choice for a second piece, since even if you're looking for oow upgrades, it will take a while to get two, plus you may come across an all type HP aug to put in the slot3 eventually. Although looking at your magelo, upgrading those anger2s is probably your best bet after the BP (or even before.)

Any effect you put in that slot is going to be minor and a marginal help, so it's really whatever you feel like you need the most. If you really don't see a need for more tak armor at all, and you want to spend the points on a type3 aug, vengeance is probably your best bet, unless you need an effect like see invis or EB. Yeah, people might tell you that attack doesn't help that much, but unlike regen it's pretty much always helping you (i.e even when youre at full health or when heals are so quick that the regen doesnt come into play.)

One other option for your slot 3 is an Enduring Breath aug out of the Guk camp in South Ro. Costs around 300 points and eliminates the need to switch out a high ac/hp item for some crap EB item when you are raiding or doing exp in an airless zone.

The augs out of Tipt are a great suggestion though and I would recommend having both the 30/30 type augs and making your friends happy with free 2flowing though/2regen augs and 10attack/2regen augs. All of these can be used in any slot and any type. If you are careful you can single pull all the way to the queen area where 6 mini cragbeasts spawn. Clear them and when the sixth dies the Enraged Maternal Cragbeast spawns and charges the group. Can use ripost disc or defensive/stonewall. Hits hard but dies fairly fast. At 65 we'd farm this with a group of 4-6 for augs and we'd make close to an AA getting to her.
