GBoS is the best aggro weapon you have listed there. The obsidian scim procs quite often, but it's not really noticable aggro. SBoD is beyond terrible for aggro purposes, and I've never had any experience with the tunare whip.

I think you're trying to do too much, just put GBoS in mainhand and SoTB in offhand, taunt when debuffs land or when you lose aggro and don't worry about the rest.

I recommend using LDoN to work on your AA, that way you can get anger augs to sove a lot of aggro issues.

If you have fun switching all that stuff around during a fight that's one thing but I don't think it's really doing anything for your aggro, except switching in primal to get avatar which does give you more dps.
