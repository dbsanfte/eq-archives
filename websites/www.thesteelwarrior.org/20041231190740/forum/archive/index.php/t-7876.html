Once you hit 40, as stated above, I would go to zones I hunt in and just ask random groups if they minded me adding some dps to their kills, outside the group. Most people are more than happy to allow this. You help them, they help you, but they are not depending on you as their "tank".


Take a few days and just go work on skills not in group. It may seem like a waste but it achieves your goal in a day or 2 rather than slowly over months of using a weapon for 10 minutes here and there in group.
