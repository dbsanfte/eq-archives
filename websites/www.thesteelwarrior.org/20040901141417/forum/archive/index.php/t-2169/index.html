Friedrick
If one has a non kos mage and rogue and has some key classes with a lot of guts you can summon to the hall way above the king and queen (after the pit trap and false wall). One raid I attended there, we had a mage CoTH a chanter in, followed by cleric, followed by me (MA). There were 3 mobs in hall iirc. Chanter has to mez quickly, followed by cleric to heal/root. Then the best tank (just a stalling action til damage arrives) you have for the raid then damage dealers (wizzies<snare> then rogues). After you clear out the hall you are fairly safe here plus you avoid the pit trap that kills many raids. Just be careful though of respawn in the hallway for when you are clearing king/queen rooms.

Prior to CoTH you want to buff to the hilt and rune the chanter/cleric/ma. This strat requires a gutsy chanter and cleric but can be done. It also requires that you let no runners escape or you have a whole world of hurt incoming. Root em if you need or have wizzies/druids snare. It isn't a bad idea in my opinion for MA to pop defensive/evasive upon being coth'd in to minimize healing mana from cleric until the damage dealers arrive.

Also, and I can't emphasize this enough, SNARE/ROOT so you have no runners. Do this early, do it often. If you have to rez a druid/wizzy from an early snare it is much easier than summoning the raid from having the library and side mobs add at a bad time. The mobs run and fast. My queen blade came when she dropped with just her feet sticking through the library door. The raid wiped but I was able to get back and get coth'd down to loot blade. I died almost immediately upon clicking yes though on the no drop item. But the coffins were worth it as it was my last piece.

One last item, have entire raid shrunk before the CoTH as the hallway is narrow and make sure casters stick their butts to the walls so melee can get to the mobs. This strat was quite a rush for me, I can't imagine what it was like for the chanter and cleric, heheh.

Khiloor
Every time i've done Chardok Royals, we did it this way. 6-8 ppl are the CoH team, 1 non-KOS to call what gets pulled next and how many are inc. Kill all the way to the beginning of the castle, then CoH the whole crew in. Kill mobs that have spawned, then AE until you get into the library. Pulls after that consist of 5-6 mobs at a time. Watch mob pushing and make sure you stay away from the walls because it WILL aggro the king room below. Clear the bottom of library and get everyone DMF or levitation. Pull the rooms after the library then we move into the that area, taking the platform on the left as you enter in. Clean up some wondering mobs, then start pullling the king room to that spot.
Have non-KOS person spam hail the king, as a warrior pulls just about everything else. (i've used defensive on this pull so i can make it back to the party alive still. eheh). Pull the king and whatever is left in his room, and kill him (fire resists against him). Move into the king room. rebuff and kill respawns. Everyone stays in the king pit area. Have a suicide SK or Pally or whatever pull the whole queen room into the hallway, then FD or die. Have non-KOS person spam hail the queen to keep her there alone while the rest of her friends go back to the room. MA pulls queen to top of king room pit steps when it's clear for a single pull. Everyone hops out of king pit, melee range behind the queen, and blows all offensive discs, full nukage right away. enchanters handle any spawns that occur, pallys stun, lifeburn / manaburn, full dps. Cheal cycle shouldn't be needed if you have a half decent tank (over 6k raid buffed). The key is the quickness because she does a nasty ae dot that'll wipe you if you take too long. If fight lasts more than 60 seconds, a good chance you'll wipe. Queen fight should take around 15-20 seconds. At this point you can stay in king room and clear spawns and wait for another King to spawn (2hr timer if i'm not mistaken). Or just TL out of there.

I've been on about 20 Chardok raids that were done this way, and I've lead the last 12 of them. Only wiped occurred when we pulled the Queen for the first time, didn't kill her quick enough so her AE wiped us.
