Oh, just thought I'd add something about the hotkey you were talking about before. Beg should be removed from it. It only adds aggro on critical failures (fairly uncommon). Also, the amount of aggro it generates is low. Add in the fact that as he uses it, his skill goes up, thus there will be less critical failures, so it's really not worth it since you have to turn attack off to do it.

As for disarm, Varaho (a warrior on these boards) recently tested disarm for aggro. From his test, disarm only gave aggro the very first time it was used, and all other times it was ignored. I myself then tested it also, and got the same results. The amount of aggro for the first disarm is fairly low also, not worth bothering about imo.

Taunt. Make sure he isn't hitting it each time it pops up. The way taunt works is if someone else has aggro, and you successfully taunt, you will be put at the top of the hate list plus one. If you have aggro, then taunt does nothing at all. So it's best to save it for when you lose aggro.

Weapons will help him with aggro some, but the biggest thing you can do is work with the rest of your guild to have them keep aggro on him. Jolt, fd, evade, and even just turning attack off and backing up a bit are your best options for him to keep aggro. Hopefully he'll get the levels soon so he can do more himself.

I still say a willsapper would be a great weapon for him if you can afford it. Frosty offhand, or maybe one of the new troll smithed yaks.
