He is located in The ruined City of Dranik, pos 930 neg 1130, north west corner, with several trash adds. Kill them before the battle, buff all resis for the AoE FD, and any form of levitate. Buf with alendar and SS, even, set AoE heals if the AoE FD does too much damage.

Yellow con at 70 both versions, the kos non-agro triger, and the triggered one. You can see him kos, but he is holded until you give him the 3 items. Then, he starts to attack you.

Hits max about 1900, normally about 800, rares max hit (15% shielding, 2500 ac)
Rampages, normal rate, but dificult to fix them cause the first in rampage list will be the warrior who triger the event, and probably the next will be a healer, so keep the tank healers away from him. 2 clerics on tank, and a cleric or a druid can heal the rampage, depending who takes them.

Hps: about 800k estimated based of fights in PoTime. My battle was about 8-9 mins with 22 ppl, time+ geared. Easy kill, no deads if u are advised (no parses of hps, just my estimation)

AEs used:

http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=6119&source=Live - This one is really easy to resist.

http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5556&source=Live - Hard to resist, but interrumptable, so dont try to corner him, just keep him turning around you. Only 2 landed im my combat in 8 mins.

Dead at first try, so no idea about if the encounter ends with a wipe.

Good luck for u all with this mob )

Edited: if u stand up quickly after a FD, dont loose agro. But just have about >1 sec to do it. After a sec, the mob starts to hit anyone but you hehe
