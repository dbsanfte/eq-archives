"How long is the quest and finally is it hard"

The quest is long. As with all Luclin quests, it's a timesink. I wouldn't call it HARD per se, but the length of time it takes to get some of these mobs you need, can make it hard.

The final mob, the named vampyre in the castle, is one annoyingly rare spawn. Also you need vamp bane to hit him (though vamp bane is pretty easy to get. There's a stake for rogues and warriors. A 2hander for paladins. And 1hbs that are stupid easy to get for everyone else). Casters can burn him down pretty toasty. The hardest part is getting him to spawn.
