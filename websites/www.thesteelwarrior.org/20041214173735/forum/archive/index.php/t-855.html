Folks-

Back when they added AE rampage, it was bugged and you could "instantly" refresh it w/ a click buff, for an effective delay of say 1 second or so. Was MASSIVELY broken, warriors could run up to shroom patches in FG and kill them in 20 seconds or so.

Add in PoP levels and AAs, plus the (hopefully) upcoming exp changes, and multiply that time by 10x (for a 10 sec delay) and it'd STILL be massively overpowered. Soloing a 6 mob shroom patch in 2 minutes just isn't reasonable.

This isn't even considering pure melee damage options; since rampage is "instant" (weapon delay independent), high weapon delay becomes GOOD- something like a weighted axe (at 150 dly) has a 100pt or so dmg bonus with a 50 base damage; for *just* rampage it's about the best pure melee weapon there is, tho the UP hammer is quite good too.

For smaller pulls, something that procs a rain spell might be best; if your PR is up to snuff, it'd be hard to beat a poison wind censor, for instance. Appears to have a fairly high proc rate and the rain does nice damage over several ticks.

Anyway, enough musing and back to the point... dropping rampage's reuse time significantly would require fundamental changes to the AA to keep it balanced, such as preventing weapon procs or crits with it, or limiting the number of targets. Both of which I would not like to see. I say keep it as-is.
