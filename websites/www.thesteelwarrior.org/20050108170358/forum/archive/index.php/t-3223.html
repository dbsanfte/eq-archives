Gorget of Superiority is your definite best bet for haste. UNLESS you have a shot at Aten neck, go for Cord of Midnight.

In EP's (and 1 tier lower), good neck, face and waist items are hard to come by. Most have shitty low AC.
Examples:
Belt of Mastery (35ac 125hp) vs. Cord of the Pyre / Girdle of Earthen stability (both 18ac 140hp). I'd probably even keep Belt of Mastery over Coirnav belt if I had the choice.

Gorget of Superiority (25ac 80hp +haste) / Talisman of Vah Kerrath (35ac 125hp) vs. Necklace of Reflected Flames (15ac 135hp) / Fire Marked Rune (20ac 125hp). I'd take the Fire Marked Rune here, but it is very rare. I don't really want a necklace of Reflected Flames, the AC isn't good enough.

Crimson Runed Mask (50ac 125hp) / Cloudy Steel Mask (20ac 125hp) vs. Great Mask of Flame (20ac 150hp) / Baroque Mask of Sol Ro (20ac 135hp) / Fine Hammered Faceguard (18ac 140hp). Now see here, neither the Fennin mask, AoWind mask or the Sol Ro mask are better than the Aten mask, and they're minor upgrades to the Cloudy Steel. Sucks, no?

If I had known, or rather given it more thought when we were farming VT, I'd have gone for the Gorget, Crimson Runed Mask and Belt of Mastery. Didn't have a shot at Aten neck, as it was pre-awarded each time.

Believe it or not three very sweet warrior items drop off the Thall Va Xakras; Cloak of Icy Shadows, Cord of Midnight, and Umbracite Bracer. Out of those three the best of the picks for you seems to be the Cloak of Icy Shadows. Great hitpoints, clicky damage shield and five saves versus all resists.

Otherwise you are better off waiting for other mob drops unless you want to upgrade your haste with a Cord of Midnight. Suggestions I could make would be the Gloves of Warding and the Leggings of Fiery Might of Thall Va Kelun for other big upgrades(nasty mob, a true test for a guild.) The first floor Diabos generally have very little to offer a warrior, Ring of the Crimson Bull being one of the few exceptions.

Oh my fellow evil doer mentions the Dark Wind Shroud off of Blob One. Don't hold your breath on that one as its a rather rare drop. If you can get lucky and have enough dkp to get it though its a must have item. As you've got 41% haste already its not a huge concern for you yet. Concentrate on weak points in your slot and you should do fine.
