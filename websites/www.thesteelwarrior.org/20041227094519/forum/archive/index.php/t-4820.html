With the onset of the new separated disc system and very neat and exciting way of doing these has come up. Some of you at Fennin tonight may have seen this.

Say for example on Fennin the main tanks disc is about to fade. Instead of letting it fade and hoping for the best, we can;

1) ST runs in hits riposte disc (furious)

2) St hit AEtaunt then hits Incite to ensure aggro

3) Clerics have 12-15seconds to switch healing to ST. During this time Druids/Shaman can heal to cover ae dmg.

4) As soon as Furious Fades which will generate alot of aggro blocking and returning every attack, ST will hit Defensive/Stonewall depending on situation.

By this time the chain could be easily transferred without fear of clerics dying on MT death.

If you know there is a good chance that you will have to do a tank swap during a fight you can easily pick four tanks and put them at 1, 2, 3 and 4 on the agro list using the new taunt's.

Tank 1
Uses incite pops it right when they start and pretty much every 30 seconds after that. Since this is the only tank who will be using procing weapons there is no chance they will lose agro with the extra instant agro and the hate modifer.

Incite
1: Increase Hate by 398 (L63) to 400 (L65)
2: Increase Modify Hate by 68 (L63) to 70 (L65)

Tank 2
Uses a non hate procing weapon and pushes the berate button every time it pops. (Be carefull to save enough endurance for fortitude, defensive ect, however. An extra 300 hate every 30 seconds and a hate modifier of 60 should be plenty to keep this tank ahead of anyone else on the raid but still behind the number one tank.

Berate
1: Increase Hate by 291 (L56) to 300 (L65)
2: Increase Modify Hate by 57 (L56) to 60 (L59)

Tank 3
Exactly the same as above but uses bellow. AGain 200 hate should be enough to keep this tank ahead of most of the raid and just behind the 2nd tank.

Bellow
1: Increase Hate by 200
2: Increase Modify Hate by 50

Tank 4.
Same as above with provoke

Provoke
1: Increase Hate by 85 (L20) to 100 (L35)
2: Increase Modify Hate by 25 (L20) to 45 (L40)

When the first tank dies

Tank 2 Uses incite
Tank 3 uses berate
Tank 4 uses bellow
Tank 5 uses provoke if needed.

To be quite honest. As much as I love these changes. I think they are going to make tank swaping extreamly trivial, where it did used to take some skill.
