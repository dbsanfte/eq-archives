Is Brellium as good as Fierce Heraldic? No, but then what cultural is?

The fact is that Brellium, as well as other non FH cultural, is as good or better than any of the Velious quest armors. On most older servers, prices are also very inexpensive so you can get some pretty good gear for a pretty good price. If you want to upgrade Brellum, you are going generally going to have to get NToV+ armor. If you are looking for tradeable, Ornate is the next step up, with a few single pieces here and there that are marginally better. Off the top of my head, I cant think of any upgrades to Brellium that don't cost quite a bit more than what you could get several pieces of Brellium for, at least on my server. If you are short on funds, the bracers and the legs are the best pieces. I wore those until I upgrade to ornate/crystallized acid bracers.
