Well, since almost everyone loves Scars of Velious, I was thinking to myself, "Hey, why don't we just do Velious all over again?".

EverQuest: Rise of the Storm Guard


On the verge of decline after being broken by several defeats, Kael experiences a violent, but reletively bloodless, shift in power. After a successful assassination attempt upon King Tormax, the Generals move in and take their reign. Derakor is hanged and the new Priests of Rallos Zek begin focusing their energies to rebuild the once defeated Avatar of War. City workers and citizens begin rebuilding the layouts of city in preparation for the new order.

Eyeing the recent advances of the human race, the new war council agrees to liscense the outcast Velkator into building them new steeds of war. After a successful and brutal raid into the Western Wastes, Velkator would be supplied with the eggs of the giants very own enemies - the dragons themselves. Using these eggs, Velkator would manifest and twist the newborns for their new purpose. The Kael Storm Guard is born.

Scouts of the Coldain began reporting giants riding on the backs terrifying creatures. These new creatures were fast and tremendously strong. Dain Frostreaver grew worried, but nevertheless agreed to dispatch a small task force to analyze the situation more closely. This decision would not only prove useful in the information that was provided, but it would also prove to be one of the most disasterous defeats the Coldain had ever experienced. The dwarves were slaughtered on open ground by these dragon riders as they tore through the lines and ranks of the Coldain. Some dwarves improvised and attempted to blockade the charging nightmares with giant spears, but their brutish strength and heavy armor often allowed them to tear right through the lines with impunity.

The dragons, meanwhile, go weary of the Giant's military and technilogical advances. While the dragons still clutched their hold on the reigns of the sky, these new beasts of war the giants often rode allowed them to equally challenge almost any dragon on the ground. Even the most fiercesome of wyvern warriors found themselves barrelled right over by a terrifying charge of a Storm Rider.

Realizing that no single dragon or dwarvish army could ever match the new Kael Empire, the Claws of Veeshan and Coldain drop any differences they once had and band together, or else suffer an impending demise.

******************************************

With the story being said, new features we would see is exactly what everyone loved in the old Velious - Rewarding but difficult quests, multiple high end playgrounds to romp through, an interesting storyline, and difficult but non BS high end encounters. Kael will appear completely different with a horde of new and powerful named to kill ranging from Generals, Priests, Captain of the Guards, Storm Guard Heros (Fighting on these new mounts ala Vhaksiz ;)), Overlord, and the newly refocused and rechanneled Avatar of War. Of course, these new named would share the same difficult their last predicessors had when the armies of the mortals challenged them - massive melee dps. **OPINON** That's what made them fun. None of this lets-train-1000-mobs-around-while-we-kill-the-boss.

And, of course, it's Velious! :D
