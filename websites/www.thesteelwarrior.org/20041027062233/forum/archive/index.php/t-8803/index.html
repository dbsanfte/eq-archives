Maxtheslayer,
I used to farm him for red dragon scales and a way to purchase upgrades. My group consists of a fairly non-ideal arrangement for him:
65 war / clr / dru / enc / nec / mag

I get almost all partial nukes with my characters, so really the war and 3 pets are doing all the damage. Listen to Haass as furious is the way to start the encounter. I pop mightystrike (or fellstrike) after then evasive after that.

To negate his lifetap DD, which procs quite a bit and also heals himself, I have the enchanter drop uproar and rune of zeb. onto the warrior and keep them up during the whole duration. This significantly negates his DPS and makes him much easier for me as my DPS is negated by his resists.

If you have a melee heavy group (which Haass has vs mine) then the enc w/rune deal may not be necessary. Even without enc w/rune, a cleric is more than enough to keep up as prior to getting uproar, my enchanter could not keep rune of zeb. on the warrior 100% of the time and healing was necessary, which the cleric was more than capable of doing.

As for hp/gear and the like, my toons were pretty much all Skyshrine armor clad, droga weapons, some epics, 100aa+ and some (very few) BoT Tower items.

Since getting uproar on the enchanter, my success rate is 100%, though the fight does drag on quite a bit. As a note, I have never slowed him with my enchanter as his MR is amazingly high (enc tash and mage mala) and enc nukes never land nor do further debuffs. I also have a hard time getting dots to land from the necro, which doesn't happen often. Straight up nukes are your best bet, but plan on the DPS being very minimal (perhaps lures on a wizzie would be the win). Personally, I'd love to drop my druid for a melee (rogue/monk/etc), replace the enchanter as well and you'll probably do quite well against him.
