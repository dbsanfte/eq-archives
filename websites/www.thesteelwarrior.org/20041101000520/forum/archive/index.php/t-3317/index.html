Flair
Some observations I've made about LDON agro:

1. proximity seems to really be king (yes its always been king but its ridiculous now). The taunt button seems to almost be an after thought against proximity agro now.
2. you almost will never get a mob to flip its facing (or very rarely). I always position myself to be in its face no matter what the rest of the group is doing (sorry mage pets, rogues etc). This of course ruins any positioning skills you might have.
3. Trying to turn a mob is almost impossible and more than likely will cause you too lose agro (sorry group that doesnt want a mob pushed in a particular direction like through a door)
4. A mage/wizzie nuke or good CH will gank it off you like a ping pong ball. Know where those casters are when are on the mob, turn and get a forward facing between them and the mob asap to limit the damage.

As far as weapons, using a 2 hander with less than max haste just doesnt seem to hold up well against "spike" agro in ldon. Unfortunately I've gone back to dual wield with 4x procs to maintain agro (most of the time I group with ellie equipped folks so your mileage may vary).

I'd like to get a stun proc for my GSoT so i can try a double stun setup to see how it flies.
