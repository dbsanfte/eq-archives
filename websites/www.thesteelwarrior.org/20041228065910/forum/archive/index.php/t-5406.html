If you got Vindi to 57% then he is as good as dead.

Bad strats will get you dead way faster than that.

The best things to do....

Use a shaman potion.

Call assist 15 seconds in if people cant hold agro. Wait for some good agro procs etc.

Use Mana regen on clerics.

Make sure people turn off attack completely during a tank switch.

Use AE taunt to switch.

Use Paladins to LoH if the MA gets low/Cheal is missed.

Assign a Rampage tank, and a dedicated healer to heal them.

Make sure the Rampage tank is the first person to touch Vindi other than MA. This includes bard songs.

5 Seconds is fine while defensive with that tank.

Use Divine Intervention in case the MA misses a heal again. He should not die any sooner than 3 minutes into the fight.
