I have 3 wizards, a chanter with color cloud, and a cleric and a warrior with EarthShaker.

I bring in a good bard puller (lowdown is able to pull from the Nightstakers all the way to the hobs and only leave like 5 or 6 mobs up), and another chanter, thats 8 toons total.

Ok the chanter slows the warrior (tash, forlorn deeds and then Night Dark Terror to get Dex up), so he is ready to rampage, by way of duel. Then the chanter zones and returns.

The puller brings in the big train, and the chanters are a bit away from the wizards/cleric/warrior, opposite the train. The mobs hit and get stuned by the color cloud spell (8 seconds up to mobs level 65, thus PoN trash does not resist much), and the other chanter using a color stun, both chanters then spam their stuns, this will ususally lock down most of the mobs.

Meanwhile the wizards are using winds of guile and jyllys spells to pbaoe the mobs, and the cleric is casting a group heal ... all this is spamed.

Finally a few seconds after the mobs are stuned (so the trailing mobs catch up and bunch with the stuned mobs in the front of the train) the warrior hits rampage. If he is slowed and has some good AA (espiciall weapon aff) he will proc a lot and almost kill the mobs outright, but if not no problem they are low HP and stun locked and the wizards finish the job.

Result is as you see from the photos. 40percent AA for everyone in the 6 person party.

And the beauty is that as soon as the mobs repop its time to pull again! Warrior not using disc means no wait at all, cept for rampage which is like 10 minutes....

So basically with luck can do a pull every 15-20 mintues (the bard takes awhile to round up all the mobs)....
