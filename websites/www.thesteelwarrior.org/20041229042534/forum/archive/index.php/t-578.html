Okay I have a Narandi's lance.

I bring it out now and then. The boil blood proc is good aggro and procs 6-8 times in a fight but this is very important. That is with 280 dex which I can produce myself with an avatar weapon just before bringing out the Lance.

Without that dex seen it only proc about 2 times in an entire battle so if you have a windblade and low dex just use it and keep the lance at home. If you have high dex then sure use the lance as an aggro type weapon but MAKE SURE YOU ALSO HAVE THE AA OFFENSIVE SKILLS THAT HELP 2H WEAPONS TO BACK IT UP. Better yet make sure you have those same skills if you have decided to go 2h as your choice of weapon most of the time.

If I had my way would rather use a weapon that was high in dam and low in delay like 35/23 with a proc rather than the lance but until I get one I still keep the lance around.
