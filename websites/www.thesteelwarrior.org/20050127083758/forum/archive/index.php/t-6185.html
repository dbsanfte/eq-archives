I see two options:-

If you are in no rush, give the lower level warrior a try, and if you find after a while you can't take the levelling anymore (wild horses could not drag me through 50-65 again as a warrior without power levelling at his disposal, I would first reroll another class) go back to your main.

If you want to get to 65 asap keep your dwarf. Its harder to get the equivalent of cultural as an agnostic/insignificant god worshiper but a level 65 dwarf in lesser armor > a level 55 warrior in cultural.


Having said all that, personally having made 3000 alts in this game over the 5 years I have played (and even though I often grumble about cultural armor as an agnostic) the best advice I can give you is to not make a character based on loot. You will burn out on it fast. Play the race you want to play. The penalty is not large enough to where you cannot do your job.
