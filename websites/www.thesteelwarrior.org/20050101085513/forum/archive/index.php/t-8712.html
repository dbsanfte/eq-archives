A few months ago I was 2boxing the bank area. Once with my warrior, and once with my ranger.

Took about 3-4 clearings (had the whole zone to myself pretty much) to get from KOS to dubious/apprehensive. From there, all those toes and helms you pick up, you can take to Skyshrine and turn in. Didn't take too long to get to amiable after I turned in toes/helms.


I normally con Rollandal along the way to Skyshrine and turn back if I'm not ready yet.


Your best bet is the Arena, and especially some Vindi hits. But this'll at least help ya get to non-kos pretty quick.


Also, not all Skyshrine quests require ally faction. The armor quests, and the ToV quests do (probably the ST ones at well.) But things like the key to cobalt scar, the scout charisa quest, bracer of hammerfal and a few others don't. You can do some of these before you get ally and do your armor quest(s).
