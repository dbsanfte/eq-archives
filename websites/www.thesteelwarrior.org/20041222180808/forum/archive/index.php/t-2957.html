Shields and blocking



We're planning on reviewing the possibility of giving an innate chance to block when using a shield. We've been hoping to do this for a while, and it should be getting a realistic review in the near future. We'd like to increase the value of shields, and make it a worthwhile choice for players who might normally dual-wield weapons or use a two-handed weapon. We plan on balancing this against classes which use shields by default, and therefore wouldn't be making any trade-offs to equip a shield

I hate having one of the major weapon combinations in the game completely worthless at the higher levels of the game. Look at the cover art for LDoN, what do you see? A SHIELD!! Of course, no high level tank will use one. This seems odd to me. I hear the complaints that the game is too old for new ideas, and that any change to shields could only help Knights. I'm going to try to look outside the box a little to see what it might take to upgrade shields in a way that they become viable for WAR/PAL/SHD.

If shields were increased in an 'even-handed' manner, the changes would help Knights more than Warriors. Added defense, given to both in the same level, will help the group that had poorer defense to start with, Knights.

OK, now that I have acknowled that, what could be done to make 1H+Shield a viable combination for Warriors and Knights in a way that actually helps both about the same?

I see three things to consider:
1) Warrior 1H weapons < Knight 1H weapons
2) Warrior aggro would suffer with loss of DPS/2nd Proc while Knight aggro would not
3) Warriors would need a larger mitigation increase than Knights for it to have equal impact to both

1) and 2) can be addressed more or less with one strike. The answer is to make Warriors using 1H weapons do about 90% of the damage they would with Dual Wield. This would still allow them to DW for that bit 'extra', but would reduce the hit they take by using a shield. This could be done by creating warrior 1H weapons with Knight 1H ratios that cannot be used to DW. Similarly, you could code a Warrior only bonus to 1H weapons when a shield is equipped. In any event, allowing Warriors with 1H+Shield to do 90% of DW damage keeps their DPS up, and reduces aggro lost due to lost DPS.

The aggro issue can also be helped because those Furious Bash shields will be used occasionally now, instead of living in the bank or bag. Also, other shield procs could be introduced, preferably on WAR only shields.

3) can be addressed with level limits. Add a 'block' skill for WAR/PAL/SHD while using a shiled. Let Warriors top out at 225 and Knights top out at 175. Make this increase Knight mitigation by 10% and Warrior mitigation by 20%. Have it scale based on the level of mob you are fighting so that it works out to 3% and 6% respectively against PoP boss mobs (so we don't really break raid content).

The effect of this would be to allow both Knights and Warriors to get a real mitigation increase out of using a shield, with a loss of about 10% to DPS (probably more than 10% for Knights, actually unless you are looking at the 2-3 best 1H in the game). It also recognizes the fact that Warriors would need a larger increase to find a change worthwhile.
