You lose 23 raw AC (36 or so actual AC), gain some stats that are likely dubious by that stage in a warrior's career, gain 25 HP's (call it 38 or so HP's due to the extra STA), and some resists.

Is it worth losing AC to HP at close to a 1:1 ratio? I think not. The resists are okay, but in the greater scheme of things minimal considering what you're losing.

This is just my personal opinion based on my own experiences, but I wouldn't consider the bracer of gore at all. Now...for my rogue, I'd consider having it when fighting mobs that AE, but that's about it.
