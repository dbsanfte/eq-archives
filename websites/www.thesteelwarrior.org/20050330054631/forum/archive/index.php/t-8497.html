A decent yet quick to get boring spot for you two would be just outside the jail in Plane of Justice. There are 4 mobs that spawn in this area, about level 50-52. Your cleric will have no problems keeping you healed and could even bust out the spammer hammer to help some. The mobs don't run when low on hp and they drop decent cash loot at times.

The four mobs I am talking about are the first KOS mobs you will come to when entering the jail area. Just past the high guardians. The first one is a rock creature, then there will be two Punisher's that will need to be split with the clerics Pacify spell. Once spawn is broken you are home free. About the time you get all four dead, you will have a minute or two to rest, then respawns will happen.
