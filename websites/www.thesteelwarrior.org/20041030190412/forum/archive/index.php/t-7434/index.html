Friedrick
Why do you want to bump up your ac? You are already twinked beyond belief for your level. Bumping up your ac I assume is so you will take less damage. Equiping a shield will cause you to take less damage per second but your aggro will go in the toilet. Now that your aggro is in the toilet your group will have to lay off the mob some which will cause the fight to last longer since your group is doing less dps to the mob. Now the mob does more damage to you in total, just less per hit. At level 48 one rounding a warrior isn't possible with experience mobs you should be fighting.

I have a Stormrider Carapace. When one drops all the warriors in my guild /random and the loser has to loot it if that tells you anything. I use it ghetto bashing to interupt casters in LDON. I also RARELY equip it if I am tanking a mob that pops adds during the raid script and I cannot damage it too much before the adds are dealt with or it will spawn more. I also equip it once in a blue moon when a raid boss is about to enrage and I want to cut down my ripostes that I take until I can get attack shut off.

In short, say no to shields for a warrior as it defeats what you are trying to accomplish. I cringe everytime I see a warrior using a shield in an experience group or LDON other than to bash a caster.
