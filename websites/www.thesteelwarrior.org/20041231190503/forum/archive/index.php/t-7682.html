Depends on the average level present. Assuming your level is a good indicator of the average level in your guild, I'd try to have two to three tanks (as some epic mobs death touch), three to four healers (with complete heal, of course), two to three enchanters (crowd control is VITAL in Plane of Fear, Plane of Hate and Chardok), and a good deal of dps (rogues, monks, berserkers, and well-geared rangers/shadowknights).

If you can field three-group raids which include the above, you should be able to take anything needed for the war epic.
