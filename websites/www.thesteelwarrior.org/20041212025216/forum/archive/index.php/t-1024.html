It is level 43 that can group with a level 65. I personally would either choose a Cleric or a Shaman for your needs. Cleric is very useful in the regards of being able to rez and not lose your camp and having minimul downtime. Also handy if you decide to duo an and die, dont have to seek out a rez, you already have one. Shaman is useful due to the fact of slow and buffs. Both classes play a crucuial role in forming groups. Also factor in the fact of how good you are at twoboxing, skill wise. If you arn't the most skilled in the art, play a cleric. They are much easier to two box then a shaman. A shaman isn't too difficult, but will take a bit of practice, especially if you never played a character similiar.

As far as how to do it, never pled with a warrior, but if you are able to get your hands on a caster friend to help you all the better. Get your noob a hp buff of some sort, probably a kei. Get you a dmg shield potion, pull a group of mobs have your noob cast a nuke or an aoe to do atleast 1pt of dmg to the mob. Taunt off mobs as necessary, and make sure you are ducking to avoid reposte. If you accidently deal damage to the mob the noobie must outdamage you for xp. Best to stick with undead mobs and other mobs that dont run away.

1-10 (or 1-17) Unrest
1-10 Kurns tower
10-22 or 24 (paludal caverns)
23-34 (tizmaz and kodiacs are decent xp at that level, in gd)
35-43 kaesora

Also a thing to keep in mind, although may be tricky, keep in mind how much dmg your noob does, long as you are able to outdamage your warrior it is all good. W/o a dmg shield potion could deal 51% dmg with noob and kill off rest of mob w/ warrior. Or you can get it to 1% health and zone your warrior and let noob kill it, either works. Then once you get it to level 43 just group him up with warrior and go farm seb or something till he gets 46 then take em up to the planes, probably poj or whatever you can handle out there. Good luck to ya.
