You armor is pretty good for a 52, I would be more concerned with weapons and haste. Upgrade your haste first. You'll need to decide whether you want to go with back haste or belt haste since it is going to determine your upgrade path for some time. With the funds you have right now, belt haste would be the way to go. IMO though, you are better off saving for a CoF - it will last you a lot longer and net you better gear in the long term until you can get a good NO DROP haste item.

If you go ahead and go with belt haste, look for either a Soulscream belt, Broken drive belt, or Runebranded Girdle. That will allow you to get some decent gloves. SS MQ would be a great choice, but if that is not possible, you can always get a pair of FH gloves.

If you can hold out for a Cloak of Flames, then you go with a Putrid belt of rotten hide until you can afford a Heavy rubicite girdle. For hands you can go SS or FH again.

As for your weapons, you can camp a Copper hammer and a sword of the bloodsworn yourself, that combo will last you a very long time. For DPS I would recommend investing in a Greatstaff of Thunder, it is by far the best weapon you can get for the pp. On my server they are down to 5-8k now which is unreal for a weapon of that quality.
