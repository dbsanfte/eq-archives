That should have been plenty to kill him. Don't worry too much about bane, it's really more of a hassle then it's worth with seru... just bring along some nukers instead and let your melee go afk until loot ;)

Wall aggro can be very bad in Seru's room. Here's what we do, and we get zero adds every fight. Not even respawn adds.

Enter Seru's room and hug right. If you're looking at his pad, you'll see a couple pillars right next to it. You want to hide your clerics behind them, while you tank Seru near the wall straight ahead, but far enough away from the closest door that you won't aggro respawn.

Everyone else with the possible exception of a bard stands on the pad where he spawns. Have debuffers do so from melee range so Seru doesn't run over your casters and clerics and dot them in case debuffers get aggro.

As long as nukers watch their aggro, fight should go smoothly. Mages and bards make this much easier due to rods and chorus...Seru's melee is pretty gimp so clerics should have no mana trouble.

If you do happen to get adds, you'll have a ton of melee standing around doing nothing to take care of them. Just have a knight get aggro, shamans and druids should be able to handle heals np.

We do pretty much the same thing as listed above. Once we port upstairs, we clear the hallway and when ready, send two tanks into Seru's room, hugging the right wall to the first corner where the pillar is. Then the MA pulls seru to the corner while the rest of the raid rushes to the middle of the room by his spawn point.
While the MA esablishes agro, we debuff. At the same time, the 2nd tank is attacking and taking agro when the first tank gets stunned (usually lasts about 20 seconds). Once the stun is off, the agro just about always reverts to the MA.
After about 3-4 minutes (depending on # of EB procs) the casters will SLOWLY begin nukes. This goes on for a while until about 50% when you can start speeding up nukes a bit.
Just keep on doing this until he's dead. When and if you get any adds, just sick the melees on him who are standing around with their thumbs up their ass.
We have no seru baned melees, so your tactics may vary if you want to use them. It takes a while the way we do it, but for what its worth, we've never failed with our system.

Good luck next time :)

Pets cannot damage seru with their melee, but their procs can. Beastlord warders proc a nuke of some kind, necro pets proc lifetaps.

We generally don't use pets to avoid positioning problems (our necros don't anyway, they're too busy dotting to watch pet, we do have a beastlord that uses his warder when he's there).

Also, using a Weaponshield pull can be useful here to get the MA a ton of aggro from the get-go. Less chance of debuffers getting aggro and if he/she gets stunned fairly often early on, less chance of your damage dealers stealing aggro as well. The only time we didn't use WS pull, MT was stunned a few seconds in and a chanter pulled seru off her after tash.
