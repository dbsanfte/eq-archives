Redhenna
STA and HP's, you need both. If possible, get a group to go to UP and farm moktar, free nodrop upgrades, moktar is better than you currently have in most slots, and at 52, should be good exp. Also look at camping Soulscream belt in Veksar, more haste and decent stats in waist slot. SCHW is another possible upgrade you can get farming.

Now for the stuff you need to buy. Ears, as mentioned the 35 hp ears would be a nice upgrade. Fingers should be the 6/65 rings. Clattering Bone Necklace for neck would also be great. Bracer, either Fine Heraldric or Helenic Tundra if you can afford it, otherwise, maybe bracer of scavenging. Crusty helm would be a big, cheap upgrade for you untill you can get something better. Acrylia Studded Boots would be an awesome upgrade for cheap as well. Crystal Spider Eyes maybe for face for a few more hp's.

Remeber that your gear is always a work in progress, so always keep an eye out for ways to upgrade. Try and get in on Kael raids as soon as possible, Thurg quest gear is not the greatest armor, but it is not bad either. There is also some nice 60 HP gloves that drop rarely near arena -).
