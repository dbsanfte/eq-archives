First In Force is a concept that most servers seem to use for determining who has "ownership" of a camp or boss. I'd never use that rule for something as trivial as an xp camp, but guilds do it all the time for boss mobs. If you've got the force, go ahead and announce that you're claiming it. But don't be surprised if someone asks you to prove it by actually engaging if your force looks insufficient. Etiquette would demand a reasonable amount of prep time of course.

If you're there first and have a reasonable force for the encounter, most people and most guilds will give you the benefit of the doubt and let you have it. If you're claiming a camp for hours on end hoping more people from your guild eventually log in then you have no claim to it. Either engage or leave. Most of the time it's not even an issue. If someone is claiming a boss or some camp and there's something equivalent available elsewhere then just let them have it and move on. The disputes normally arise when there's no comparable content available.
