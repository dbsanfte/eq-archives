Kellaen
They're all easy at 66+, all revolve around killing 4 waves of 4 spawns then a boss.

Flame kill mobs before they hit center pillar, you get hit with svf ae's at various stages of the event
Lashing kill mobs that spawn spirits, kill spirits to despawn lasher
Stoning kill mobs that run through doors, archers will fire at prisoners and you
Exe, self explained, kill kill kill
Torture, run to centre room with the prisoners, kill all mobs + banshee at end each wave
Suffocation kill spirits of suffocation as they spawn or prisoners die. Kill SoS, normal mob, SoS, normal mob.

I did them all last night, not knowing much other then exe in 2 hours including a couple fails from not knowing what to do, in a 2/4 boxed group with only 3 real sources of dps. (War rng wiz enc sha clr). It was easy.
