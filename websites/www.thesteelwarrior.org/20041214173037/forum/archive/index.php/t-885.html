The old world mob exp problem and the no tier 2+ access problems are about to be solved. With level 50 mobs giving exp to 65 and most PoP zones being opened up for exp in an upcoming patch, there is absolutely no reason not to level to 65. You already have the basic AAs and your Veteran title, your best bet is going straight to 65 so you are ready when the patch to open the PoP zones goes live.

The best exp zones in the game (unless you are elem flagged) are the tier 2/3 zones and you are never going to get a group if you are 60-64 when level 65 tanks commonplace. Not only that, but at 65 you are a better tank than at any previous level, there is no disputing that. HoT will be a breeze to tank at 65 with your better taunt, better DPS, better resists, and better HP from leveling to 65.
