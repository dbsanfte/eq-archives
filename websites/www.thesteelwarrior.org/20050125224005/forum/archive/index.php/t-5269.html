I'd suggest NOT spending any points to augment rings with resists. The returns are simply very poor for the investment, and at most +16 to resists. If you really want to augment the rings with resists, do it with dropped augments. Another option is the 20hp/mana augment for 1150 points in Guk, which is also a pretty poor return. One utility augment you could use would be enduring breath. Fairly cheap at 510 points, and rings just don't have many choices.

If you're not worried about attack, then you'd be best off augment virtually any other piece of gear. For example, 40hp for the helm and the breastplate is a really good choice. 10ac for the mask is a good deal too. Those three pieces will last you at least into the elemental planes.
