Fitemore
Technically, the innate weapon proc takes precedence over the augment. You *do* lose some insignificant amount of augment procs from augmenting a weapon with a proc. However, it only matters at all when both would proc on the same swing. Seeing as that is a very small amount of times, it is *always* worth it to augment an aggro weapon with an aggro proc over augmenting a weapon with no proc.
