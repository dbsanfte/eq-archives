In the original EQ beta, phase 2 there was separate numbers for both parts of ATK, and AC. Then in phase 3 of beta, they merged the AC numbers into 1 number due to confusion among the player base. The back end is still 2 separate numbers; they are just represented as 1 number in the UI.

Just to reinstate what I said before. Agility does add AC. It goes towards the avoidance AC pool and added to your mitigation AC pool thus coming up with the number you see for you AC. But...actual avoidance is a set percentage depending on the class.

Here is a grid of AC gain for agility. As you can see here, you will gain 39 AC at 75 agi if your lvl 40 or higher. Max gain at lvl 40 or higher is 65. a 26 AC difference.

Here's the full formula for AC:

Avoidance = (T from table) + trunc(Defense * 16/9), but never less than 0
Caster Mitigation = trunc(Buffs/3) + trunc(Defense/2) + (Equipment + 1)
Anyone else's Mitigation = trunc(Buffs/4) + trunc(Defense/3) + trunc(Equipment * 4/3)
Displayed AC = trunc( (Avoidance + Mitigation) * 1000 / 847 )
...
The formula doesn't work for monks because of the weight limit thing, but it should work if they keep their weight under the limit.
