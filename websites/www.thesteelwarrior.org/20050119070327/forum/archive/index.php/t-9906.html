You're doing okay on gear, though the BP's light on Ac, which you already know. AB breastplate will be a good option.
About the time you hit lvl 50 (perhaps even before) you'll be sort of transitioning into the phase of the game where proc hate is a bigger and bigger factor in your ability to hold aggro. Hence the Sword of the Bloodsworn and Copper Hammer of Striking suggestions.
I'd also start saving for better haste, because even after you get proc weapons, your dex and lack of AAs/weapon affinity will make the "potential damage" part of your aggro generation relatively important for several levels. The haste you have now is okay, but you'll be happier with 31% or better in about 10 levels. Until you can afford tribute haste, commonly available haste items include the soulscream belt, runed bolster belt, cloak of flames (listed in order from least to most cost).
I'd also be aware that at least for now, most hate augments come from doing tons of LDoNs, so that might be in your future.
Also, one thing you can do to sort of get an idea of what gear folks are wearing is to go to eqrankings.com and poke around looking at Magelos of folks at and just above your level (in your case, paying special attention to lizards).
You have an interesting array of utility weapons. You might also check utility items listed in more advanced warriors' bags for ideas on resist gear, etc. No big hurry on that stuff, but it doesn't hurt to be aware of it. I see you have components for baking spider legs, heh. Check the bazaar for Halas meat pies and Grobb liquidised meat for cheap stat bumps.
If your shaman has been supplying the SoW potions, see if he'll make you some Kiva's blistering (er whatever--flesh?) potions too. Kinda handy if you solo.
