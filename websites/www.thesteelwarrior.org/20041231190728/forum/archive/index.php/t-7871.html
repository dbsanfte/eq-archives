I would like to add that you can't crit on DD portions of procs IF they leave an icon. Example, (made up proc) 200DD, 30% slow and AC debuff. Even though there is a DD portion of this proc, it will not crit.

You can still have combination procs that will crit. Anger for example, 100DD, Stun, Hate. Since this does not leave an icon on the mob, it will do a critical blast. The hate portion is not included in the critical portion, just the damage.
