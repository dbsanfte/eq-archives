Here is your best course of action, in my opinion:

Orb is too slow for a warrior, sell it or give to a ranger alt. Use CHoS main hand, SotB in off hand for best results in DPS and agro when dual wielding and tanking. Sell the longsword of freedom or give to a ranger alt. Get a WMBS for non tanking DPS and a GSoT for a decent cheap 2h agro wep (for raids mostly) if you are over 55. Dual wielding should be ok for experience groups but some boss mobs do riposte kind of hard and sometimes its nice to have the 2h for that reason.

That weapon set up will be totally adequate for most experience groups and raids. If you are raiding something that those weapons are not good enough for, you will probably have access to better weapons anyway.

The thing is--you are 1/5th the recomended level so your damage will be very low with those weapons right now.

For example the CHoS has a recomended level of 56:

11/56 x 13 damage on CHoS will make it 2 or 3 damage depending on if it is rounded up or down. War Marshall's is going to be a similar problem as it has a mid 50's recomended level. I would look for something like an Argent Protector (not defender btw) which has no recommended level. That is a very decent 2h sword and will be useful for a lot of levels.
