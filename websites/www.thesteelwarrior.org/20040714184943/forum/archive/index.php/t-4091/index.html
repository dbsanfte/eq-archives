The easiest way to look at it. The mobs in plane of time are instanced but the zone is not. The timer for each instance starts when the first person from your raid clicks into time B. While that timer is running you must be part of that raid to gain enterance to time B.

time/phase
1 hour-phase 1
1 hour phase 2
75 min phase 3
4hours phase4
4hours phsae 5
2 hourse quarm
13.15 hours total.

If i fail to kill all mobs within the aloted time your timer will expire and the zone is ready for another guild to start immediatly. If you clear phase 1 through 4 in 4 hours and gate out another guild will not be able to zone in for 11.25 hours. Their is no way to end an instance early that i'm aware of.
