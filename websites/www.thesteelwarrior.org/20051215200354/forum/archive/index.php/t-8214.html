There's been a lot of threads arguing whether Gates is good for "casuals" and the debate has mostly boiled down to single group content. Your server may vary and it might be hard to actually group in some of these places (Maelin is an extremely top-heavy server, for one), but it isn't fair to just ignore and dismiss the content that exists in these zones. I've made up a magelo (http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=1071587) of items that come entirely from GoD single groups. Nothing beyond Tipt, nothing LDoN, just Natimbi, Qinimi, Barindu, Ferubi, Riwwi, and the surrounding instanced zones. Like Frodlin said, there's a GOLDMINE of gear in Gates, though not quite enough to skip six expansions (nor should there be). Gates single-group weapons, for example, need more variety. If a weapon on par with GBoS dropped in Barindu/Riwwi, people would use those zones more.

Also note that I didn't include a Breakdown in Communications augment, since due to the flawed design in that quest, the first parts aren't single groupable and are much harder than a number of the later parts of the quest. It doesn't matter that a single group can do parts #3 and 5-8 because having just those parts done doesn't matter; it isn't possible for a single group to complete parts #1, 2, and 4, and so the Socketed White Crystal is useless for this thread.

Over 7000 unbuffed HP isn't bad for entirely non-raider content out of ONE expansion. Now add some buffs:

PoP: 7268 (base) + 618 (Nine) + 544 (Seven) + 910 (Kazad) + 330 (Brell) = 9670hp

OoW: 7268 (base) + 805 (Steeloak) + 707 (Wanvu) + 1185 (Balikor) + 430 (Brell) = 10395hp

Let's go even further and add some augs, all obtained from Gates; not a single Adventure Point is needed for any of the augs listed in the above magelo, for an increase of 367hp and 47ac.

PoP: 9670 (buffed) + 367 = 10037hp

OoW: 10395 (buffed) + 367 = 10762hp

Ten thousand hitpoints, without ever raiding, without ever setting foot in LDoN, without ever setting foot *anywhere* except Gates of Discord. That doesn't include the extra HP from a stamina buff, since I don't know how much of an increase it would be (I'm a cleric). It's a little light on AC, but this is only one expansion. A loyalist shield, for example, could help fix that.

Every part of the BiC quest can be done separately from the others. The only reason the order matters is that for every portion of the quest you finish, you get a gem shard. Combining the Socketed White Crystal with the gem shards in the correct order makes the BiC augment.

Sashes 9 and 10 are on the list because the 10th battle is much harder than the rest (3 unmezzable ukun + 3 mezzable aneuk) and the stats on both are very similar. I don't think the quest can be repeated to get a 9th and 10th sash.
