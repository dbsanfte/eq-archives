Vaniki Doubles 200's, I would guess 40 - 50k hp, Flurries, Gates, Lifetaps as a proc for 1500dd.

He spawns in the tunnels in my experience, Go east across the zone, past spiders, past Jaled's shade. South east of Jaled is the entrance we use, should spawn down there.

To kill the fool, 2 chanters alternating runes on a warrior in evasive, let him build agro a bit then have your dps go all out to minimize the heals he gets from lifetaps.

Its a burst fight, as he has some staying power with the lifetap proc.

Trick is really just to use rune and evasive.
