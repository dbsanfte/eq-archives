Planar Stun has a knockback so it will have more aggro than the Pebble crusher(slightly) due to that.

In my findings (3+ years playing a Paladin) All stuns generate about the same aggro, with resisted stuns generating slightly less. The only things that differentiated stun aggro is was if there was a DD component or a knockback.

PS and for those warriors who tell you Stun Immune MOBs don't get aggroed from Stun they are flat out wrong.

Not sure how it equates to HATE points but 1.5 stuns @ = to 1 slow/mez worth of hate (on most MOBS) (love to see numbers assigned though)

Where you'll notice the differance is Bronzewood/PC will only decrease your damage/interupt MOBS >55 while GSOT will interupt MOBS up to 65.
