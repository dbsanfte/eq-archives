ZajeerSOE
there may not be any cap required if the gear does not let you get too high. Definitely Gobsmash. There was no hard-cap on Time mods until Gates. It was much easier to control Time mods and progression when it was only coming from 2 sources: Plane of Time and LDoN raids. However, with more zones being Plane of Time in terms of loot quality and beyond, it wasn't easy balancing the fact that a player could go with all spell shielding items, for example, if they wanted to and it would have been harder to balance out. A hard-cap was put in so I could have more flexibility itemizing without the worry of someone getting 100% spell shielding at some point and making themselves immune to DDs.

I don't have all the numbers in front of me right now, but I'm pretty sure it's going to be extremely difficult hitting a time mod cap unless you really work hard for it; by sacrificing some hitpoints, stats, and other mods, just so you can concentrate on that one specific mod and get it to max in Gates.
