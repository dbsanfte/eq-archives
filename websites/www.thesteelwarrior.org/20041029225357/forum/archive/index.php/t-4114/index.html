You're gear setup is nice. Should work really well at your level.

Some stuff you could maybe look for on the cheap in bazaar:

Brellium shoulders and Brellium pants (dwarven cultural). They'd be a bit of a boost to what you're currently wearing in both AC and hit points.

Helm of Raldukan would be an upgrade to the helmet, and they're running pretty cheap these days in bazaar.

Solstice earring packages are pretty cheap these days too. Could probably, with a bit of patience, totally make a killing on one.

Those would be my suggestions for things you could look for at your liesure. Your gear's fine for now, so don't feel like you have to go upgrade like a madman.
