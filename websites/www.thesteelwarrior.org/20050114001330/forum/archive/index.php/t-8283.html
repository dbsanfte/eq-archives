We tried several different things and finally settled on the following and have had minimal trouble for all the flagging/backflagging along the way.


We make a tank group with bard and two healer groups with bards for resist song. Everyone else relies on AE song but so long as tanks and healers don't get feared, it's no biggie if others do occasionally.

We put the tank group, healer group and debuffers in one corner. Everyone else in opposite corner. Healers deep in corner, MT out almost at max range for heals. We pull trigger there and tank group kills it. Rangers and/or wizards shoot it from range and back off with trigger at 5% or so. MT and ramp tank finish off trigger and RD pops on top of them. Both tanks pop def/evasive/furious (whichever) and heal chains are already going when RD spawns (started them when trigger was at 2 or 3%.)

As soon as RD pops, MT calls attack. Melee spread out around target to minimize push, since pushing it up a wall or pillar is an easy way to wipe. Range attacks stay at max range. If you have a lot of melee and/or pet casters, you can park pets with the healers to push opposite melee. Enchanters will sometimes charm guards to send in also.

I think that's everything.
