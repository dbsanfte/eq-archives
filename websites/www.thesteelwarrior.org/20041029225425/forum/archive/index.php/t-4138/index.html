Magelo is down, so I can't see what other weapons you have but...


The 50pt tap isn't going to be much aggro, so that's not a factor. I'd keep whichever has the highest dps/ratio in primary.

I use a Great Blade of Storms in primary and CHSD in secondary. The procs are nearly the same aggro as far as I can see so I keep the dps weapon in my primary.

If you're really concerned about dps, go with a Greatstaff of Thunder 2hb. Dirt cheap with a huge aggro proc and hits very fast with a good ratio. And you avoid quite a few ripostes which is always a bonus.

Once I have around 2 procs with the Greatstaff, depending on who's in my group and their weapons, I bag it for a higher dps 2hander like TSBS or war marshall's bladed staff.

/edit: Magelo is back up, for dps in LDoN, I'd go with the GSOT you have in your bag. But I'd keep the CHSD in primary considering the ratio of the CHoS.
