Something you might wish to try, is to go to Eqtraders.com, and visit their message boards. There is a specific forum for each server's tradeskills, and you might have good luck posting for a barb smith on the FV board there.

In any case, check out the bazaar now already, collect whatever you can of the materials used to smith that armour. There is a lot that you can farm yourself, such as velium *large bricks or blocks*, leather padding or the materials used to make padding, ice goblin blood from Permafrost, etc. Check out the same site, eqtraders.com, under the "recipes" section, then "cultural armours", and so on, to see exactly what is needed. Keep an eye on the bazaar for cheap essences of winter, blue diamonds, etc. I know when someone asked me to smith some gear for them, if they were a person who had already done some research and collected materials ahead of time, I was a LOT more likely to be favourable towards them. *Note- getting /tells such as "Yo, bish/d00d/man, come smith me some of that 000ber ph4t l3wtz gear you can make kkthx~" does NOT work very well at all*

Good luck !
