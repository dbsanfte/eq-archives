I started tanking PoI entrance at 53 with worse gear. Do your best to group with friends who will trust you and understand when aggro bounces, because no matter how good a warrior is, sometimes there is aggro ping-pong.

ALWAYS have a slower ( ench, shaman, suicidal bard/beastlord ) in PoP unless you got some sick number of ac and hp. They make the difference. In PoI, if you time your spawns, you can single pull the left side of entrance all the way to "Weathered Camp". You will need some practice on the order though, because some mobs spawn in a clump of 3, but their assist aggro is short-range as long as you pull with a bow.

So, get friends, have a slower, practice pulls, and expect a death every once-in-a while. PoP xp makes death not hurt so bad. When you can get a 90+ rez and then kill 3 mobs to have the same xp as pre-death, you will know why we all love PoP xp.

EDIT:
Although my gear is pretty fair on my magelo now, at 54 I was in Barbed ringmail w/ mostly crusty armor and wielding a lammy/Centi. Get your levels and gear will come along for the ride, just don't be discouraged.

I was tanking in PoD (goos, rats, and spiders) at 54 with about 2800 hp unbuffed and 1150 ac. It was ok, but a bit tough at times. I've gained a lvl now, bumped the ac to 1200 and hp to 3k, and things seem a little easier - I'm actually somewhat confident tanking there now;) .

I usually play with a druid, mage and enchie (all 54 to 56) and the way we do it is the mage pulls with maloi (or whatever the magic debuf is), when he gets close the enchie slows, the druid snares, and the mage pet roots. Once the slow lands I step in and get agro. The big advantage is I'm not getting hit until the mob is slowed and we spread part of the damage to the mage pet - which has great hp regen. Using this strategy the druid is able to cover heals and add some dps. If the pet misses on root I step in immediately. Sometimes the druid or enchie has to run a few feet, but never for more than a few seconds - and this rarely happens.

Oh yeah, almost forgot....I alternate between windblade (no agro probs here), and cheap 1 handers for skillups (blood point, venomous axe, sarnak warhammer, club of ice ocean.)
