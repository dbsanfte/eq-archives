The problem with making warriors solo 2-3 mobs at once is balancing them in grouping and raid situations.

If you make it so a mob does less than 1/6 of a warrior's HP's in the time it takes to kill said mob, then there has to be a drastic scaling in things that are meant to be raid or group targets, otherwise it wouldn't even be fun. It would just be a straight mow down of everything in sight with a warrior and anyone who could cast a heal spell.

Unless you've got any bright ideas on how you can change this simple fact without completely redesigning every instance and elite encounter in the game in a manner that you really don't want them to change them in, then I suggest you either decide to be a warrior, or decide to be a shaman or a paladin.
