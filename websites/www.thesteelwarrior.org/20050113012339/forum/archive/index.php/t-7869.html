Hi Tanks!!!

Kalanu here. I was selected as the Warrior Class Correspondent.

I'm going to tell you a few things about who I am, why I applied, and what I hope we can do for the Warrior Class.

My main character is Tsaotsao. I've been a member here on The Steel Warrior for a long time. I've been in EQ around 3 years. I still need to look in game and check exactly when I started. But I've been playing MMORPG's since 12/97, where I started in Ultima Online. I don't post here a lot, but I read here a lot.

After a year or so in UO, I joined their counselor program, where I served the player community for 3 years until the program closed. I was a senior counselor there for about 2 years. I was known as SRC Kalanu on the Chesapeake server. Counselors hda a few more powers than I think Guides do, but the job was essentially the same. I was passionate in my role of serving players and being an advocate for players.

A lot of you don't know me. And, I have already received a lot of criticism for being selected, believe me, this is going to be a lot of work. I didn't apply to get recognized, I did it to serve you the Warrior Community.

I've been playing Tsaotsao since about my 3rd month in EQ. I was in a family guild to start, one that I had been with in UO, that moved to EQ, Dalridian Knights. In the past year, I applied to a raiding guild on Terris Thule, Dwarec Mercs. I was a guest/app with them for about 6 months before I became a member. I had to wait for apps to open, but I raided very chance i got and learned the elemental planes and I was involved in DM making it into Time.

Why am I posting under the name "Kalanu"? Because of two things. One is that I served a player community with that name before, and I was well accepted and I feel like I did a good job. The second is that I want to still be able to post my personal opinions unser Tsaotsao. When I post as Kalanu, I post as your correspondent.

Like I said before, a lot of you don't know me. I've been open in my posts here as Tsaotsao. A lot of folks would not admit that there gear isn't the best, or whatever, I will continue to post honestly and do my best to be complete. There are what? around 400,000 players in EQ. If only 10 percent are warriors, then that makes... 40,000 players. No one is going to know everyone.

So, what is my role as Warrior Class correspondent? My task is to record what *YOU* think is important. What are the most pressing concerns of the Warrior Community. It doesn't matter what I think, it matters what you think.

I am not paid by SoE. I get no reward from having this task, except that I get to help you get your desires, goals, frustrations, complaints communicated to SoE. The goal is to give them some focus to what the Warrior Class needs.

With that task set before me, I will do my best to do the following:

I will post the lists of what I am sending to SoE before I send them.
I will post the responses they give me on the concerns I send to them.
I will ask you what your opinions are on the issues.
I will scour the Warrior Community message boards to find what Warriors are concerned about.

I'm not perfect, I'm going to make mistakes, but I promise to do the best job I can for you.

I've already been flamed for not getting out here faster to post. I found out SoE selected me after some of you did. So I haven't had a lot of time to prepare what to say. As far as flames and criticisms go, if it makes you feel good, then FLAME ON!!! But I'm here to listen to your concerns about EQ and the Warrior Class. I care deeply about the Warrior Class. I play it every day. I'm not a paladin, or monk, or anything but a Warrior as far as EQ goes for me.

If you are really upset about me taking this role, I'm sorry. I would ask if you applied? Do you want to spend you time reading everyone's opinions in an objective and non-judgemental way? Are you willing to share them with SoE in a logical and clear manner without over-writing them with your own judgements?

Now, with that said, let's get on with posting what your concerns are for the Warrior Class. I'll be collecting concerns from the Warrior Class boards at EQLive, and from here. And I'll be checking around for other boards.

Most Warriors play on a team. It's fundamental to our class. Let this be a team effort to tell SoE exactly what WARRIORS WANT for our class. I can't do this without you. And understand that I serve this community.
