Riposte damage these days is generally a relatively small percentage of incoming damage, after the great riposte reduction of early PoP. Thus, it is not such a large factor in choosing weapons.

Look at it this way: Your warrior can take 10% extra damage. Or, your cleric / wizard can take 400% extra damage. Which is better for your group?

Brabble
Concerning ripostes: think of it this way. If the warrior uses max aggro weapons the fight will be shorter, because the dps folks in the group/raid can ramp up their damage. On a per mob basis, you might actually take fewer ripostes because the mob dies faster.
Of course, the warrior takes no ripsostes at all if the mob is beating on the shaman, cleric, necro, etc. So, the ultimate in riposte reduction is not optimal. Go with the best aggro ya got.
If you are aiming at a really scary mob--say one that has in fact splatted the warrior with ripostes--you could try something like a GSoT with a hate aug on it.
Beyond that, it's time for the healers to see if they need to work out a bit different healing strat for the bigger mobs you're meeting.
