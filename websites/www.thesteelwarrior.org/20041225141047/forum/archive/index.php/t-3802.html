Every warrior has been in the situation many times of being below 10% health and still several seconds away from any type of heal.
Most warriors tend to start backing up in this situation,trying to put themselves out of the mobs melee range.This rarely works and usually causes either one of two things.
A. You dont get out of the way in time or the mob just takes a step forward and kills you anyway
B.Mob turns on caster,stunning away the heal then proceeds to munch the group.

Lately Ive started a new method that is much safer for me as well as the group.This is completely based on perception.No real way to "prove" it other than trying it yourself.Note:Only works well on large mobs
When I am in a situation where a mob is probably gonna kill me before a heal lands,instead of backing away I actually run under the mob(assuming its a large mob )...Im still right next to it,but being behind it,it cant hit me without turning around, as it turns around, I back up to my original position,thus making it turn around again.Doing this enough will usually give enough time for a 3 sec heal or the CH to finish,but since Im still landing a couple hits every once in a while and I have the closest proximity Im not causing aggro on the rest of the grp.

Works for me,and the cleric doesnt complain about lack of trust (Healers hate it when tanks backup)/grin
