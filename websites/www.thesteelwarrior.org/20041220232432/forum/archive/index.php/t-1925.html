When you beg, one of 3 things happens. 1 - you succeed, get 1cp, and no aggro. 2 - you fail, but get no aggro. 3 - you critically fail, and get a tiny tiny amount of aggro. Now, as someone mentioned, due to the nature of the skill, the more you use it, the less you critically fail. Also, the chance to critically fail is small to begin with. Another thing noted above, you have to turn off attack to beg, which removes any minor gain you would have gotten from a critical failure, assuming you were lucky enough to get one. Basically, the skill is not worth using for aggro, and since I've seen people with 200 beg, who never got more than 1cp at a time, it's not worth using for anything imo.
