I did rings 1-8 in a weekend at lvl 63. I sometimes had the help of a 58 cleric and or a 61 bard. A bard is invaluable in this endeavor because of many of the shortcuts they can use. You start the walk and when the mobs spawn that will attack the Dwarf, you have bard aggro dwarf, pull out around mobs to just outside coldain camp, charm him, camp out, and avoid the fight altogether. I'd say you should be able to duo the first traitor in GD together with your druid.
Have druid harmony room and pull him out.
Next is Captain baradain, after handing him the tainted avalanche ale you can use same tactic (harmony pull).
For Peffin you will definitely need help.
I could pull it off with a cleric partner but barely.
5 rooted elites all in hitting range when you engage peffin are a lot of trouble.
Wake of tranquility can keep them off you for a bit but i had like 7120 or so hp with gaudralek and was using riposte disc on engage so it helped tons.
The low agro supernal elixier of my cleric prevented him being summoned by the giants(i am not 100 percent positive they summon at all).
A group should be all you need to make this safe.
The kael traitor needs a bit more work, i had a 61 chanter with dc pet, another warrior (level 59), a 61 ranger and my two characters trigger the dwarf.
I would not have needed to finish it as i already had the head and only needed ring.
But was a nice challenge for a small group with rather low dps.
