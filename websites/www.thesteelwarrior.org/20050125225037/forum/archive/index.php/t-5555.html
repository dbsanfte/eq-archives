Haas is correct, you should keep it under your hat.

BUT ...

"Personally I can't see how you would be doing more damage with 2 1hand weapons at level 49 than with a good 2hand weapon. "

Two words: DAMAGE ... BONUS.

Look at your damage bonus prior to level 55. It sucks with 2handers. It's not so hot with 1handers, but prior to level 51, dual wield options tend to be a far better DPS choice than most 2handers. But that obviously varies depending on what you have available (A Reaver is probably going to outdo a Wurmslayer and a Centi Shortsword).

Monks are in a slightly different boat than warriors. Their dual wield skill caps at 252 in their mid 30s. Monks will get more out of a dual wield setup than a "comprable" 2hander setup until at the very LEAST level 55, but more than likely level 60, where they get a damage table upgrade AND triple attack.
