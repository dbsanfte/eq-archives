Howdy,
Like Vikken said, everytime your .exe files get patched your firewall sees that as a potential problem. It doesn't know whether it has been changed by a patch or by a virus and thus ask's you if you want to allow it to go out to the internet.

I use Zone Alarm and it does the same thing. The easiest trick to it is when you see your .exe files getting patched switch your security level to LOW (or whatever setting that will allow all programs to go out) when you are FULLY logged in with your toon hit the windows button (or whatever way you like to get back to windows) and change your setting back to med. The next time you log in, assuming that EQ .exe didn't patch again, you won't have a problem as the file will be valid in Zone Alarms eyes. This saves your poor computer from you turning off your firewall and having it naked to the world! :eek:
