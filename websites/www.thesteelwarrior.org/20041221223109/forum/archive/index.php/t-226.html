Uh, anyways ...

BCA = Barnacle Covered Axe, a new drop from Legacy of Ykesha Zones, with a proc called Pique that adds 100 points of hate. A hot topic of late is how useful it really is, and how it stacks up to other weapons of its level and power ...

Praetor's Blade of Debility which has a shammy debuff spell proc.

Black Blade of Tormenting, which has a fast acting Damage over Time spell proc, Torment.

Frostbringer, which has Frostbite, a 60 point DD spell and AC debuff proc.

Any of the Ykeshas, which proc Ykesha.

etc. etc.

What other Acronyms you need? BoC? Blade of Carnage. Thurg = Thurgadin. SS = Skyshrine. Kael = Kael Drakkel. Usually in reference to the quest armors specific to that zone. Or just Champ's, Myrmidon's and Warlord's.

AA acronyms that are commonplace here are ND for Natural Durability. Run 3 for Increased Natural Run Speed, Level 3. Regen 3 for increased natural regeneration, level 3. AE Taunt for Area Taunt. CF 3 = Combat Fury 3. CA and CS are Combat Agility and Combat Stability. LR and ID are Lightning Reflexes and Innate Defense. TM is Tactical Mastery. Flurry and Rampage usually get spelled out. PE is Physical Enhancement, not the 1990s rap group. PD is Planar Durability, not a police department.

That's all I can think of for now.
