There is currently no better place to hunt spider silks than in EK. Crag spiders can drop up to 8 silks per spider, and the carrion spiders can drop up to 6 silks per spider. Just keep running the loop from the bandit camp up over the back of the mountain (the one with all of the named gnolls, etc), then around to the front of that hill and back around. Just about everything is a PHer for spiders, so if spiders are slim pickings, you can other stuff to help with spider respawn. Biggest problem is that there is almost always competition for the spiders.
