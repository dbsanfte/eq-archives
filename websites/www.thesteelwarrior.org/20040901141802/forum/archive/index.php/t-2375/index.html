Khumak
IMO there's basically 2 separate stages of balance that need to be looked at. First is actually tanking ability. This includes both healing efficiency and the ability to get and hold aggro. The 2 need to be proportional. The easiest way is to make aggro and mitigation approximately the same for all tank classes. That balances our ability to tank in xp groups without affecting raid dynamics.

The second stage involves balancing all the other spells and abilities each class gets against each other. You can't compensate for tanking ability with utility. That was proven back in Kunark. By the same token when you eliminate that tanking advantage that warriors used to have without adding any utility or DPS to warriors there is no longer any disadvantage to having a spellbook for knights.

So in short balance knight vs warrior ability to tank first (only healing efficiency and aggro considered). Then compare all the rest and adjust skills, spells, dps, and disciplines to get those balanced.
