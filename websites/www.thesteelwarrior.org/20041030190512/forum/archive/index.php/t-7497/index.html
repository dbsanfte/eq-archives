Beornegar
The epic is crap. If you want a nice badge of honor, go for it. If you want better aggro weapons go to Temple of Droga and kill the goblin king and earthseer gramolk and get a copper hammer of striking and sword of the bloodsworn. Also, go to the bazaar and grab a Greatstaff of Thunder. You probably won't find a better droppable 2h aggro weapon, especially for the 50pp the GSoT probably costs in the bazaar.

Everfrost and North Ro LDoN both have good plate armor sets. For the most part, the North Ro stuff is better, but in some cases it's a real close call or the EF stuff is a bit better. North Ro is just over 1400 points per armor piece, EF is 760 per. Ornate armor molds (roughly equivalent to LDoN armor sets) drop in Bastion of Thunder (need to be 62 or flagged to go), Plane of Tactics (need to be flagged to go), and some higher zones depending on which pieces you're talking about. Get a mix of ornate, EF, and Nro armors (There's also some stuff that drops on hard LDoN adventures that you can buy in the bazaar if you have lots of cash). AC/HP aug the armors, get anger augments for your weapons, and you'll be as good as you're likely to get without joining a raiding guild and hitting up HoT, VT, and elemental+ content.

Edit: Since ldon points dont come as fast at your level, you might also want to hit up veksar for some droppable upgrades. Nice haste belt, and decent BP and greaves drop there. Also some nice DPS weapons off some of the more rare mobs.
