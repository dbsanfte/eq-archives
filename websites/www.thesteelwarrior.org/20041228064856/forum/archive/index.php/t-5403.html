"i'm i going about training these skills wrong or something? i do each one 10 points at a time and right now i'm around 50 with them all. is there a better strategy i should be using?"

You mean you use the weapons, and after about 10 points in skilling up, you switch to a different weapon right?

You don't waste your actual training points on weapon skillups do you? I know they're not totally needed for anything, but it just seems odd to toss em into your skills as you go, when beating on monsters will do the trick just fine.

Uh, if you are just swapping weapons every 10 points, I don't think you're doing anything WRONG, per se. I just think you're finally starting to feel the backlash of what's happened with SOE making levelling so fast at the early levels. For the first 30 to 40 levels of your life, you are going to outlevel your skills really briskly.

My advice would be to pick ONE skill (usually 1hs for warriors is a favorite due to its high taunt factor and superior DPS at the lower levels) and get that maxxed as soon as possible. I bet you could have 1hs maxxed by level 25. Easy. THEN, as you go along, slowly try and keep the other skills close.

Till you hit your 40s, when levelling is reduced from the clip of 4 or 5 a day (well, if you have a long play session) down to one a day or one every 2 days. Then your skills will catch up and even out.

By the time you're 55, I bet you'll find ALL your weapon skills have caught up. And you'll still have 10 levels left to grind. The longest 10 levels in the game.

Good luck. Have fun.

Brabble
03-09-2004, 02:51 PM
Light blue will raise your will raise skill unless they've changed things recently.
To raise archery skill, get the cheapest arrows you can find (summoned arrows would work) go to Everfrost near the Permafrost entrance. Almost certainly there will be someone there soloing giants. Ask if they mind if you stand there raising your archery skill. They probably won't, if you are well socialized enough to ask politely.
This might be kind of tricky if you're an evil race warrior: there are mountain passes you must go through to get to the Permafrost entrance that require you to pass some Barbarian guards. However, you can do the same thing in any camp with a stable (that is, boring) high-hitpoint spawn that people farm.
A Dex buff will, I believe, speed the process up.
When I was playing my first character, I worried quite a bit about the fact that I could never cap my skills before I leveled. I now think that unless you die many times each level and refuse to be rezzed, you can't do it. But eventually your skills catch up as you get faster weapons and each level takes longer; and as a previous poster mentioned, for a while your skills are capped for several levels, giving you a chance to catch up.
