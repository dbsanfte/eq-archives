07-19-2004, 07:50 AM
yes they stack (in a particullar maner).

if you have an A3 auged SEWS, and that you click the SotP, you wil have potentially three procs on your SEWS. but remember that you can fire only one proc at each time, that the order of proc checking is Weapon Proc then Augment Proc (never tried what happen with two aug proc) then the spell proc (you can have only one spell proc at a time, if they don't overwrite each other because they are on different buff slot then only the one closest to BuffSlot1 will fire). Moreover, the spell proc chance is not increased by WA, all of this situation goes to a sad result parsed on PoN board : with WA0 you will have aproximatly 2 ppm for each proc with a little decrease, with WA5 wil have 3ppm for the first one, almost 3ppm for the aug one and much less than 2ppm (around 1.5 if a remember) for the spell one (looks like all the before procs eat the chance for the last to proc).

for more explanation look at the library there is a thread that link to other boards parses.


SO yes it is worth it to have one spell buff that give proc. put it's up to you to chose the one you want depending on cost/power/accessibility.


NOTE : with a DW combo we can thus have 6 procs firing, this his how we can get to 10 ppm with WA5
