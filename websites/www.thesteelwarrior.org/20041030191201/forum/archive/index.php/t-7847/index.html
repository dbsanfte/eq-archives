If I choose the Velium Gemmed Long Sword I can add both augments.

No you can't. That weapon has one type 4 and one type 7 augment slot. Proc augments require type 4 slots. That means that you will only be able to put one proc augment on that weapon.

Buy Anger 3 and put it on your BoC and on either the hammer or sword or the bloodsworn. Both weapons are nearly identical and as the previous poster said, augments will be reusable meaning you will never waste getting Anger 3.
