Fafnir
I think these abilities are pretty unfun as implemented.

Needs, with this system:
* skills without openings - you want to drive the pace of combat, not "get lucky";
* (various) auto reactions.

To explain the last one, preferred responses to openings are largely the same every time dependent on your role in the group. Examples:
* if you are tanking, generally you want to either add more agro or take less damage
* if you are raiding but not tanking, you either want to do more DPS or to help the MT take less damage
* if you are just in a DPS role, you just want to do more DPS.

So, since the result is very often dedicated to one reaction linked to your role in the group/raid, then just

/setautostyle #

where # gives the type of reaction you do to a particular opening automatically. To turn it off and go to the clicky approach (or to conserve endurance for use of something proactive, like evasive/defensive), just put in a 0.

This seems like a useful addition to me - take the reaction time out of it, but be less flexible in how you react to openings.

Kinda defeats the purpose of putting them in in the first place, eh? I mean, if you are tanking and want to take less damage, by making it auto just works like increasing your chance to dodge or riposte.
