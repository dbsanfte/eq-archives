The main problems I see with warriors using shields are as follows:

1: Major loss of DPS and associated agro as well as proc agro from offhander
2: Increased riposte damage from faster delay, causing more damage to be taken than the benefit gained from the ac/hp on shields

This leads to shields being completely ignored by warriors in 99% of situations.

Potential solution: Put a focus on a lot of shields. Maybe even a 'bolt on' in LDoN. Maybe even a high cost/scaling AA:

Focus: 1handed mastery

This focus greatly enhances your ability with 1 handed weapons when used with a shield.

RP excuse:
By only using a single one handed sword, you can devote more attention to it's use, putting all your being into striking devestating blows.

Aim:
To bring up the DPS (and associated agro) on a 1h/shield combo without overpowering that same 1hander when used with another weapon offhand. Also to bridge the huge gap to using a 2hander with a shield (which would kill dual wielding completely)

Mechanics:
+50% to primary hand weapon delay
+100% to primary hand weapon damage
+50% to primary hand damage bonus
maybe +some strikethrough/parry/accuracy mods

The way shields are implemented in EQ, it makes no sense to use them if you can dual wield.

In medieval combat, a shield was quite effective as a defensive weapon compared to armor. The shock of the blow was absorbed by the shield and the skill of the fighter to deflect the blow. THe strength and flexibility of the arm and shoulder helped to minimize the shock to the body. The shield was used as a weapon, to block or deflect strikes, and as a canopy to survive arrow volleys. The more fighters who had shields, the better the groups protection from ranged weapons.

Sometimes they were painted with a coat of arms. A symbol of the fighter's family, or clan. This would clearly identify them to their enemies, in a way "taunting" them. As long as their enemy saw the symbol up, the enemy would want to target it... wouldn't they?

To make shields realistic to a warrior, they would need to be at least 200AC, have a skill(s) associated with them for blocking, striking, and shielding. And they would have an agro factor to them. At least to fit into gaming.

As far as damage goes, it looks like SK/Pally's were given better ratio 1HS to compensate for having shields. If warriors were given similar 1HS, they would simply dual wield them unless the shield was SUBSTANTIALLY better than it is now.

So, for shields to be viable for warriors in EQ:

minimum +200AC
Enhanced taunt/agro factor
Use as a weapon
Blocking
Effectiveness against ranged attacks
Magic Shields have effectiveness against various magic attacks

Will it happen? ROFL.. no, I doubt we would ever see it. Not where it dropped at a point in the game where it would matter.
