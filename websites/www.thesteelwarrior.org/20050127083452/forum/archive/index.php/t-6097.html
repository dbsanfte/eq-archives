It makes you really think about what you're doing to your equipement. Like the +6 stat augs? Who cares? But for like Anger3 or the 40hp augs, you really have to pay attention to where you're putting them. Like I put anger2 on my BoC when I got the points to. I don't regret it, even though I know that I'll have to strip it off and give back my BoC when I get an upgrade for it.

Last week I was in a raid killing the key guys for the Smith raid (or something in that zone!), and I recieved a 15 ac aug. The question for me was "where do I put it?" I thought about it for a while, and figured I'd put it somewhere that was good on my hps (ornate legs), and that I wouldn't be upgrading in the near future to get the most use out of it. Its just like with the 40 hp, V3, Regen Augs. I wouldn't put a good one on my valorium ring of galantry, or my SS gaunts (yes ghetto!), but I'd put the 40hp one on my BP in a heartbeat.

You really have to figure out what you will get the most value for your points invested. Putting anger 3 on a Windblade? Not worth it to me, but Anger3 on a GSoT, much more worth it. You can use a GSoT for a LONG time, and adding Anger3 to it will only make it that much better. The windblade, because of its price, lack of hps, and lack of an origional taunt proc, is not a good of value for the augment.

I personally like the system. You get rewarded for your work, but its up to you to get the most value out of the reward
.
