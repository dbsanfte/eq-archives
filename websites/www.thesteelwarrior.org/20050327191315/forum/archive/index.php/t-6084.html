The Remains has a max hit of 778, and quads as the previous poster said. It has approximately 52K hit points. It has no procs or AoEs. Warrior class mob. In my opinion, the Remains is much tougher then a PoS giant, as it has more hitpoints, and quads, which I don't think giants do. Hits a bit harder too.

You shouldn't have to worry about adds. Your concern should be the druid's mana pool given the mob's hit points.

CRs are pretty easy. The Remains doesn't see rogue sneak/hide, and doesn't see either invis or ivu (forget which). Some nobles do see invis and ivu. Since it's easy enough to CR (assuming you can find rezzes), I'd say give it a try.
