We took him down first time recently with 24. The key really is the clerics. For the first time i would say 4 as a minimum, but every one extra means a much large margin of safety. Also, make sure they are all the same level if possible or thereabouts - we had a some mid 50 clerics mixed in with 65 clerics and they went oom much earlier in the fight.

We fought him in the corner by the ramp, either side is okay. For the first time, try and keep it to a single tank, even if you dont kill him within 3 minutes, its just easier.

Make sure to assign a rampage tank to tag vindi on incoming so hes up on the rampage list. Give the rampage tank a full time cleric and if hes a warrior get him to go defensive.

If your the MA, shrink urself as much as possible so you can get boxed into the corner by vindi - that way his back is nicely exposed to the melees and they can push him into the corner.

Stack DS potions on yourself, a bard in your group helps as well. On our fight with him our MA parsed that vindi hit him 400 times.
If you can stack 100 plus worth of DS points on you, do the math - thats almost 1/3 of his hps attributable to DS alone.

As long as hes singled pulled, get the rampage tank to tag first before any heals go in to you. Druids shams etc spam short heals on you and get the CH chain up. 10-15 secs of solo time then calll in your melees. As long as hes locked on to you, clerics have mana, then it should go easy. With defensive, vindi really doesnt put out as much melee as you think.
