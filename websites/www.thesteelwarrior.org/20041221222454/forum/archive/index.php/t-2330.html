Here are a list of things that I see that could benefit the warrior class overall and wont "destroy" the balance of the game.

1) Beserker Fury - I believe that one of the most original and oldschool skills in this game that could use some tinkering with. I believe that at level 50 the life percent in which this skill kicks in should scale upward.

Example:
Level 50 - 28% Life remaining..
Level 51 - 30%
Level 52 - 33%
Level 53 - 35%
Level 54 - 37%
Level 55 - 40%
Level 56 - 42%
Level 57 - 45%
Level 58 - 47%
Level 59 - 50%
Level 60 - 52%
Level 61 - 55%
Level 62 - 57%
Level 63 - 60%
Level 64 - 62%
Level 65 - 65%

That way we could get some damage output from our higher ATK and crippling blows. After playing a while i noticed when i do exp groups, my hp rarely ever reaches to the point where i go into my Beserker Fury, even if i do i usually get healed the next second.

2) To help with the Itemization and the HP difference, I say SOE should relook the balance of the PoP armors. The Warrior armors should all recieve at least 10HP more on each piece and around 2 more AC a piece. That way we dont get a drastic increase in armor quality compared to other classes but still take the lead with our main stats.

3) Weapon itemization, i believe needs also a bit of a boost. A viable option i see that SOE can do is add some Warrior only weapons. Since we are the masters of armed combat, we should be given some weapons the other classes just dont have skill for just like how Monks do.


4) Aggro Issues... this one is a hard one. I know most of us want some kind of aggro lock in the begining of a battle, BUT not so powerful enough to rape paladin aggro. I believe on of the options that SOE should consider in doing is making the the "Taunt" skill longer range, say about 100 Range worth of a bow. To help with the complaints of other classes saying we dont taunt, a viable way to end this would be give the warrior a YES or NO msg if the taunt landed or not. Like...

Failure:
"You have taunted at a (Target), and he ignores you"
Success:
"You have taunted at a (Target), and looks angry"

The catch is, the msg for both will show on the warrior's screen, but the success will broadcast to the group just like the tradeskill spam.
