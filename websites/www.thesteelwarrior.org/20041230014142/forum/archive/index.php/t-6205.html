Well, Groovin FV, you never mentioned if you were shrunk or not, nor do I know what race you are since you don't have a Magelo posted. Yes, cornering IS that easy, provided you can hold agro and get to the corner before the mob does, and you keep the raid off till you call assist.

Once in the corner, I hold the down arrow key to stay back against it. I look up at the sky and make the intersection of the walls center on my screen forming a nice inverted v up there. If you keep looking straight ahead in your normal perspective you'll usually get "you can't see your target" hence the reason for looking up. (Unless Im tanking Blob Ha Ra's in VT in which case I look down)

Oh, one other thing... sometimes you'll encounter a mob that can hit you while you're in the corner, but is out of range for your mellee. In those cases have a monk with dragon punch or a class with swam pets position themselves behind the mob and push it into you more.

Heyas-

The smallest non minor illusion player character model is a double shrunk skele (chanters can get smaller using minor illusion and a dropped dagger, etc.).

One other thing to watch when cornering- pets are your nemesis, especially non-controllable ones when the mob has a popcorn AE (i.e. VT). If a non-controllable pet (wizzie swds, clr hammers, PoP class pet procs, swarm pets, etc.) gets into the corner, you simply won't be able to. Tends to happen a lot with knockup/back procs, tho if you make it to the corner first and run back into the wall, you can usually prevent the knock up from affecting you...
