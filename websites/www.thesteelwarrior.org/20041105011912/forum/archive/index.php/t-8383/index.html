As far as I can tell, there are three reasons to keep "up" with piercing:
1. You just never know when SOE will dink with itemization and then raise our cap.
2. You're lvl 40 and not rich and some items might make sense because piercing items are often priced lower than similar slashing weapons -- like the jar. trident is fast/cheap and the shimmering partisan has nice stats.
3. You're a warrior and you're really really stubborn.
