Here is the reason you 20k on the AE (i'm assuming the AE is a knockback/fling type):

You are on a horse or drog: Take your horizontal speed (say you have jboots speed horse) and are moving at that speed when AE goes off. If you and your mount leave the ground by even an inch the game thinks that your horizontal speed is your vertical speed as well and the distance/speed you travel ends up in 20k death.

Had this happen to a mage friend of mine outside kael as she ws galloping over one of the ridges in wakening lands. Tearing thru the trees she caught a little air and 20k'd.
