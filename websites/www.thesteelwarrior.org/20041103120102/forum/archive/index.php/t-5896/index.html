Ugh. You wouldn't be able to tank anything in the EPs. Sorry to be so harsh, but I voted against applicants with as bad of hp/ac as you have for an EP guild. But you do want help!

General guidelines: 1sta = 6hp = 1ac until you're capped with sta. 1ac = 6hp always. Resists are a bonus, and a LOT of resists will sometimes win out over a hp or two. All other stats are worthless, ignore them.

Searching for new stuff: Go to the bazaar, do a search for every slot, one at a time, first by ac then again by hp. Apply the above formula and look for bargains.

Good things: Your earrings are just fine. They'll last you a long time. Your BP and leggings are decent, don't worry about them until later. Your cloak is ok. Your weapons are passable.

Bad things: First off, your hero bracer makes me cry. I'm not joking, just seeing it made me cringe. Look for Fine Heraldic stuff (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=75&rsa=Smithing&rc=HUM&sb=item&sub=qeynos&menustr=080040070040). It's not diety restricted, so load up on it. The ac and hp should improve alot of your slots. Next, replace the rings. Hp w/o ac isn't doing so hot. Look for valorium rings of gallentry or the like.

If you have any cash left over after that, spend it on 10lb Halas Meat Pies and Grobb Liquidised meat. Get a stack of each, then just force eat other food and drink while leaving them above your normal stuff.

Since I'm not EP (nor is my guild even close to it), can't mention much about that, but I do see some other upgrade options.

For reference, me and you are almost identical in terms of level and AA's. If you check out my magelo, you'll notice that you're about 130AC and 350hp away from me, and tanking BoT krigers and mini's make me squirm. I point out the AC, because it seems you have sacrificed a lot of AC for HP. So my suggestions come from where I am in the game.

Ears: Look ok.
Neck: Amulet of Mummy Wrappings as suggested, for AC and HP.
Face: Fine Heraldic for AC/HP, or for a real nice item try to get a Flawless Steel Mask from SG (20ac 5sta 55hp and other stats).
Fingers: As mentioned, Vallorium Rings
Belt: Soulscream Belt

Ok, that's the easy stuff to get. Now for the hard but important stuff:

Hands: Gauntlets of the Myrmidon. 27ac and 90hp. Huge upgrade potential there.
Arms: Ornate or Malevolent Vambraces of Suffering (LDoN - EF 760)
Wrists: Ornate or Crystallized Acid Bracer
Feet: Kingly Boots would be the initial upgrade, but even then the AC is low. Ornate or Malevolent , or possibly Vindi boots.
Head: Ornate, Malevolent, or Helm of Viscous Ooze (Guk 442 - what a bargin I think).

The short of it - be ready to do lots of Ornate camping or LDoN hunting. By the time you gather enough Ornate or LDoN points you'll have a big chunk of the initial AA's done too. Even if the EP guild accepts you, I'd still stick my rear in LDoN and PoP Ornate zones until geared up appropriately.
