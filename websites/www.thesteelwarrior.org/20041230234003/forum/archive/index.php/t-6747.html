After the discs were split up, I started to actually use the offensive skills. My observation is that Mighty Strike is the most beneficial to me, as a solo warrior.

I typically wait until berserk to launch this skill and am dual wielding my fastest weapon combo (Fleshgrinder and Sword of the Bloodsworn). In contrast, when using Fellstrike, I am wielding my Windblade, becuase of the insanely higher base damage. While I get a few really massive hits (945 cripple with Fellstrike is my best), the overall numbers don't appear to be that much higher than a normal attack sequence.

I am self buffed and Magelo should reflect my gear just prior to the patch of April 14th. I haven't had a chance to update that profile yet. Because of the rather lengthy recycle time and that I don't consistently use it against identical mobs, I haven't gotten a large enough parse sample to crunch data to compare Mighty vs. Fell. Any input you folks could share to help me better use the available discs would be appreciated.

Blade of War -

With Mighty Strike:
Max Crit - 528
Max Crip - 827 (I think)

With Fellstrike
Max hit - 605, Max Crit 1011

So with Fellstrike your max hit is more than your normal max crit and if you crit on a fellstrike hit you hit for more than a max Crip under mighty strike.

I havent intentionally gone out of my way to use fellstrike while berserk but I know I've seen crippling blows in the 1600's, no idea what the max Crip under fellstrike is with a 48 damage weapon, someone will have the formula to work it out.

As far as eye candy goes, doing 1x 1011 crit is far more impressive than doing 3x 528 crits (and I've had runs of 3x 1k crits in a row). Fellstrike is more DPS and IMO much more impressive eye candy too.
