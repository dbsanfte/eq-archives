No prob Wompem. I actually only Modded with an Anger 2 (200 pts from opening 1150 on Mir tree). I have Ferocity 3 and PB2 if that helps with your context.

The folks I usually group with are some former comrades from an elemental guild (I am by far the weakest equipped as I quit the raiding scene pre VT). The main agro contenders I have are a well elemental equipped Wizard and Paladin (my paladin friend is about 10k buffed and has a nice array of mind boggling weapons). Usually if the pallie is around we have him pull/tank and I do off tanks in LDoN....if he isnt I tank and chain pull with the group going flat out most nights regardless of pong.

Before I modd'd my GSoT:

1. I couldnt use it with less than enchanter haste and even come close to offsetting agro against the ellie wizzie and/or slower. Mobs ponged like crazy. I wound up using my CHSD/Bloodsworn just to keep agro..even though it cost me DPS.

2. In a toe to toe with my pallie friend (periodically as old friends we test against each other) it was just plain sad. Rarely would I grab agro off him.

Now:

1. the double stun/anger 2 proc lets me use GSoT with self haste only and keep agro. If a mob pongs odds are I'll have it back fairly rapidly.

2. In a toe to toe...with him stunning (we were screwin around that night I got it) I can take agro about 50 pct of the time. So much so that I use WMBS if I'm off tanking so that I dont grab agro from him by accident

The anger mods are a wonderful thing. For high end warrs they are a nice boost up. I'm afraid though for the point cost that mid level warrs don't get to see the same benefit.

My next goal is to add an anger 3 to my CHSD and see what havoc unfolds ;-). I cant tell you which way to go (GSoT 2x mod vs dual wield EB 2x mods)...personally I'm sold on big 2 handers...unfortunately LDoN had a dearth of them (but a nice selection of 1 handers).
