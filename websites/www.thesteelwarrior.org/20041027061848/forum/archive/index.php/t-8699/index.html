n many slots, crusty or crafted would be an upgrade for you, and should be fairly cheap.
At about this level of gear are also some player-made armor pieces that require components from LDoN--gothic, sandblasted, frosted, etc. They come in various sizes, so be sure you can wear what you are buying. Check the merchants near forges in PoK or bazaar.

Don't know what your budget is, but the above suggestions are short-term only: as soon as you got them, you'd need to look for better. If you can afford to spend 2K per piece of armor rather than 100pp, then look around for barbed ringmail stuff or steel-wrapped (bah, whatever it's called).

But, basically, search the bazaar by your budget, race/religion, class and order by AC. Look at the best AC stuff, then STA/hitpoints among those choices.
