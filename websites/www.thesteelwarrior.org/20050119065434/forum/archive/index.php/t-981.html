This one is easy. With 20k you might be able to get enough components to have a GM smith make you a full suit of cultural armor. Lucky for you, frogs get quite a few sources for cultural armor.

You can wear:
Full Mithril Plate (high elven smith made)
Rigid Steelweave (Erudite tailoring made)
Luminant Heraldic (human smith made)
Fine Heraldic (human smith made)
Brellium Platemail (dwarven smith made)

Any set would be a huge upgrade to what you are wearing now in both AC and HP. Each type has similar stats so you just need to pick the one that will be the easiest to get.

Or if you want to be lazy, you can buy a few pieces of armor for 20k then sell the parts you replace for more plats to buy more upgrades.
