The buff does pose some problems though. It is basically pointless for them to not let the new AotC upgrade stack though. The old one does and if they'd allowed the old one to keep stacking, it essentially would mean that paladins would have gotten a fairly useless spell in the expansion as we'd be using AoTC on raids and only really be missing out on 100 hp in theory.
