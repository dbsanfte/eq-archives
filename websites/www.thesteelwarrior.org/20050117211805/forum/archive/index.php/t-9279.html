Your guild should easily be able to do the quest for the Loyalist Shield of Honor, which would be an astronomical improvement at this point. Also take a group to Veksar and maybe camp some Kylong Darkmail Gauntlets, as they will yield 3 more AC, 10 more stamina and 45 raw HPs.

Buyable options that I can think of offhand:

-Bloodmetal Earring x2 or Bloodmetal Earring/Earring of the Solstice.
-Fierce Heraldic Vambraces, Magnetic Vambraces or Armguards of Repentance
-Despite the loss in Stamina, another Fierce Heraldic Bracer would boost your AC significantly, without much of a loss in resists.
-Blindeye's should be cheap. Good bow with good stats and a pretty nice effect as well.

Also don't forget tribute. You can get 41% haste, 150HP and 47 AC for around 600 points an hour. That would free up your belt slot for a Fierce Heraldic or Heavy Rubicite Girldle (buyable or campable in Cazic Thule), as well as give you a nice DPS increase which would result in better aggro as well.
