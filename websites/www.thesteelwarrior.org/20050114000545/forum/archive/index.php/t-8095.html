0.0 second stuns pretty much have no real application in grouping to serve as actual stuns, but it is always cool when a stun actually lands on the right time (though it could easily land at a bad time, since CH and gate are the only two dangerous spells mob cast, so interrupting spell in any other circumstance is actually not beneficial since mob melee DPS >> mob spell DPS). In a raid, though, having enough 0.0 stun weapons ensures a significant confidence that someone's weapon will stun on the right time, sort of like having enough warriors taunting ensures the mob will be hitting at least a warrior.

Back to the topic on hand, EoE > ED for everything, except the fact that you can't pull off 1500 crit procs to impress people with.

Bards do less melee damage than warriors, and therefore a higher percentage of your damage with proc weapons comes from just the proc. This means that while an ED might come up as more damage than an EoE for a bard, it might not for a warrior. I'm not making claims of which one WILL do more DPS for warriors, but just keep that in mind when using bard to warrior comparisons.
