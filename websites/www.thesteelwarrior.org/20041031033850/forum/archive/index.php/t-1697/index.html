Few things here ...

Finish up your tanking AAs, those help a ton. ND3 = ND2 x 1.5 so get that asap. Then get PE and PD, that should give you a couple hundred HP itself. DO NOT WASTE AA ON INNATE STA. I can't stress this enough. You should not be putting AA into innate stats until you have everything else maxed. You are not that far off from being able to max your current stats max, and once you get to that point, those AA are totally useless and you don't get them back. So what if they are free HP for 2 weeks, they are still wasted as soon as you get 200 STA.

Also, don't waste AA on Planar Power until you can actually use it. Really the only stat I would care about is STA, once you can get STA over 280 buffed, then consider getting enoug planar power to make use of any STA over the cap you can get buffed.

Some easy upgrades:
Darkwood bow of suffering for ranged. Not only do you not have to swap in a bow, but it has 30HP 5STA 5AC. BoB are nice for the 8STA, but they are a little lacking in AC. You might consider a second cultural bracer. Myrmidon gaunts are the way to go as well, plate gloves are common drops in ToV so should be easy to get.

Your biggest upgrade will be your BP by far. Champ's or Mrymidon's are both huge upgrades in AC and HP or if you can swing a Vindi BP, all the better.
