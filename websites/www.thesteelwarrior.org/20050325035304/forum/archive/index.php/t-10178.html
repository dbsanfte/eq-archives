The word 'comfort zone' is actually really good.

When you reach that state, then you start increasing the effectiveness of the group. A bit all the time and then you end up with a killer group doing it's best.... or you end up wiping if you over do :p

Anyway, even though the group does it's best and really do some prof work, then there are still limitations.

Some factors already listed:
The Zone.. exp each mob?
The Camp.. many mobs and easy to pull?
Do you have the aa?
Do you have the leader aa to max dps?
Tribute ?

What gear do you have..
Focus effects?
attk effects?
Accuracy and strikethrough?
Cleave effects?
Improved duel wield effect?
Mana regen

buffs..
all attk buffs?
your click attk buffs for high dps classes?
the best mana regen
..
..

Spells..
The best GoD or oow dmg spells?

And at last the combination of classes in your group.

This should give some indication of most of the things that play a role. There are a lot of factors. Some people will have an easier time maxing our these factors than others and some will never be able to reach that level.

However personally, I would find my own goal for aa each hour and aim for that. :)
