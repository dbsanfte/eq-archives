Renix,

Looking at your gear and remembering myself at 54 (seems like yesterday) I would have to say you're very well equipped. My war is an Ogre, so I had a jump on you for stats, but you're gear is quite nice compared to what I had.

Suggestions? I would think about the following items in order:

1) Hitting level 60 is a huge difference. I didn't believe it, but you take so much less damage that you'll feel like superman. I was in the exact same gear from 52 - 65 (how sad) and I noticed a huge difference fighting the Netlings in PoN (did that or several levels and was there for 58- 61). 60 is the biggest jump and benefit you'll get by far. I noticed I kept doing better up until 65, but 59 vs 60 is very noticeable.

2) Grab your Kael armor, I did it all through my low 50's. A single group of mid 50's can fight there, so long as you have a FD puller. The fights will be hard and the drop rate is low. You will get a nice set of armor in the end that will fill your main slots for a while.

3) Or, be smart (unlike me) and just level your butt to low 60's. Then hit Halls of Testing. Talk about amazing drop levels. Every hunt there has made me regret the months of wasted time in Kael. All because I thought I'd never be able to 1 group HoT. HoT can be done (very slowly) with one group in your 60's. At 65, I find the fights take 9minutes tops, but I don't have the perfect group for there. The grind, in your existing gear, to low 60's then doing HoT is very nice. Of course, resist gear helps a lot in HoT, but just having a mage and a druid in their 60's does help quite a bit. You got druid seasons spells plus 40 or so MR on the mage DS, that you'll be pretty high even without specific gear.

Hope that helps a bit, sorry I can't think of much else right now. I've done fine in my group in full thurgadin gear through all the planes, as it wasn't until recently at 65 that I hit full SS armor. The difference helps, but the cleric/druid buffs were really the meat of my HPs to offset my crappy gear. The last thing I will recommend is saving your PP and focus on getting better weapons. What you have is good, but there are some decent weapons at reasonable prices that will boost your sta/hp a bit more. Shortblade of Lightning, new droga weapons (copper hammer / bloodsword) as well as old reliable Swiftblade of Zek. They'll up your damage a bit as well as up your HPs quite a bit and they'll last you a while until you get better. On my server those weapons are rather cheap in the 10-30K top range, but my server is a bit wacky on prices as I've seen a lot of other servers cheap.

GL and happy hunting,
