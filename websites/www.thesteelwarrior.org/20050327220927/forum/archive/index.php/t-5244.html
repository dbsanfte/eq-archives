01-16-2004, 03:20 PM
Make a cleric and DA jump. The Guardian isn't KoS. Hell, even if he was, it wouldn't matter anyway. If he killed ya, at least you'd know he's up.

Strategy!

First you must pull his guards. A lot of people say they're unsplittable, but that's farce. I used to think that myself honestly. Guardian will assist his guards if you try and FD pull him, so that's a bad, bad idea. So, what do you do? It's easy. His guards split like cakewalk if you pet pull them using an SK/Necro. The guard on Guardian's right is a cleric who drops the pieces for the Reactivate the Portals quest. The guard on Guardian's left is a wizard who drops the ore for the Strength of the Elements quest. Easy peezy, but the guards have a lot of HP.

Guardian of the Seal has almost no HP. I'm serious, he only has about 80-100k hp. I kill him with 6-7 people a lot and he usually dies about the time my Defensive drops. However, he makes up for this with his FEROCIOUS damage output. He quads for upper 900's and has very high ATK. In Defensive, his max is upper 600's. He *is* 40% Disease slowable, but don't think that by slowing him he's going to instantly become a walk in the park. To put it into perspective, I pull Guardian with Incite and immedietly hit /Furious. My friend, who plays a shaman, slows him as quickly as possible and I resume with /Defensive the moment /Furious drops. Even slowed, druid debuffed, and in /Defensive, my CH's often land for 7k+, and yes I chain them.

Once Guardian reaches 30% health, he's going to change from being black steel to a whitish rock. His DPS remains the same, but now he has a grav flux proc that sends you in the direction that he's facing and does some damage as well. This is resistable if you have high enough MR, but normally that's not always the case. Your healers can avoid this! Since the zone is indoor, the AE's don't go through the walls. When you're staring directly at Guardian, look to your right. You're going to see a little cove where the old Nortlav used to be. You can corner tank Guardian in that corner and have your healers around the corner on the 90 degree angle wall. It's long, but CH will reach and the grav flux won't hit the healers if they're standing there.

Guardian always drops two loots and occassionally drops the book for the Reactivate the Portals quest along with it as a third drop. His most common drops are the uber earring and the shoulders. The FT2 gloves and bitchin ring are far, far more rare unfortunetly. :(

DPS on this guy:
Unslowed - 1000+ dps
Slowed/Debuffed - 400-600dps (More than Vindi)
