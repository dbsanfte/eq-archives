This isn't related to quests persay, but I've found that untwinked the best way to make money is to farm silks or hides - spiderling silks sell for 20pp per stack, spider silks for 40pp per stack, and hides can go for 50pp or more for a HQ. Innothule near Gutka is a great place for spiderling, and also has a npc who trades 3 lower level hides for 1 higher level, so 3 ruined for a LQ, etc. The lanuage barrier is tough also, so try running to PoK and asking a halfling who isn't busy to teach you elvish or human. Sorry if you knew all this, helped me alot though so I thought I'd share.
