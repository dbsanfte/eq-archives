Well, I'm more interested in seeing some feedback than provoking an argument. But :)

My sense thus far is that max hits are down quite a bit, but I felt damage was pretty choppy. You could get hit for 200 and the next hit for 1400. From a Warrior standpoint, I found mobs in the border region between wanting to cut down damage with Defensive (just to give my single group healer a lot of slack) snd the knowledge that if I use it, I won't have it for the next mob which will be coming before Defensive would have a chance to refresh. So, with a single Cleric not paying full attention to you, I'd call it challanging.

My feeling is that IF I was a Paladin I might very well want to toss myself a heal should my HP's drop near 1/3rd and there wasn't a spot healer (Shaman or Druid) in my group in addition to a Cleric's more focused attentions. Light of Order (assuming the PoTime BP focus effect) actually looks tailor made for this purpose (to a non-Paladin).

I think the result may be (from the mobs I saw) riskier with a well equipped Paladin but very doable.

Stuns that interrupted for a second or two would definately make the mobs more Paladin doable and maybe even give Paladins a substantial advantage, not that the choppyness wouldn't still be disturbing.

They may have missed some mobs and fine tune a bit more, but I "felt" the mobs were much more Paladin tankable and interestingly enough a bit too tough to be tanked by a Ranger.

So, thus far, not badly out of balance. I did see on PoN a Paladin who IMO is about 700HP's short of a full deck say he was tanking pretty well except in Ikkinz. Which given that all the one group trials are no longer manditory (if I understand correctly) might have been left as a "preserve" for top tanks - those trials do drop near PoTime loot.

Obviously the jury's still out but I think it may be looking better than some of us expected.

BTW, since I generaly don't use Defensive unless its a "serious" mob, I too have a feeling that the dice can come up wrong and the Cleric be a tad late and I'd be dead too. IF you are sufficiently equipped it's always RNG that kills ya (or a late heal, generally because the mob got a luckier round than expected).
