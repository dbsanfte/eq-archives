The epic proc ORIGINALLY was a debuff spell. The SPELL still acts like it in the mechanic (Have you ever noticed that the spell will eventually WEAR off? How's that happen? Because the icon that was up and flashing on the mob has finally wore off).

This is the mechanic that makes the the RESISTED Rage of Vallon and Rage of Zek still very useful aggro for a warrior. It's acting like a resisted debuff. Which can and does piss a mob off. IMO, it pisses a mob off more than a stun proc on the Greatstaff, but I don't have any hard data to support it (hence why I said it was just my opinion).
