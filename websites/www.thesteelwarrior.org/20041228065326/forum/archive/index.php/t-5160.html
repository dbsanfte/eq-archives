Here are some of the AA skills I really would rather see than some of the ones tey already have in beta:

Increased Melee Stat Cap: Raise str, sta, agi, dex up to 355.

Improved Dual Wield (aka Ferocity part 2): More ranks to improve dual weild skill. Ferocity was nice, but our DPS from offhand is still only 25-30% of our total damage at best.

Offhand Damage Bonus: allow us to have a damage bonus with our offhand weapon.

Improved Bash/Slam Damage: They've got it for kick, why not also for bash/slam?

Double Bash w/Shield: ability to have a double bash when you have a shield equipped. Skill should end up being as frequent as double attack is for regular weapons with all ranks maxed. Give us more offensive damage when using a shield. Also makes Furious Bash shields more useful.

Anti-Riposte: Give us a skill that can actually help us avoid ripostes. Not just strikethrough (tactical mastery), but also avoiding the return attack of a riposte.


Give me a reason to Dual Weild again.
Give me a reason to use the FB4 shield I carry around with me.

Sturdiness and Weapon Affinity are great, we just need some more help like the stuff I listed above.
