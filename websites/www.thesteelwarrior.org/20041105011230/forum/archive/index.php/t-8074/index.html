try to find an aggro weapon at least for your primary and then put your whip in secondary until you find a second aggro weapon.
all the aggro weapons have an innate proc so check that it will fire at your lvl otherwise it's either a waste or your future one (ie you have to bank it 'till you get the proc).
at your lvl you won't proc often so your primary weapon is not that bad untill you get a true aggro weapon. remember that DoT is shit for aggro, heal/DD and liftap are weak aggro. so look for slow/debuff/stun.
some are smithed (honed blade of ykesha (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=9784) : no requiered lvl) and so are good for low lvl players.

the best aggro combo between lvl 56 and 65 is the droga one : CHoS (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62186) / SotB (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62188)
