Allright for those of you that do not know -- You can have all three epic swords on you at all times. Here's what you do:

Create the 2hs epic. Put it in your inventory. Have a mage summon you a sword of runes. Put it in the red scabbard, just 1, and hit combine. Out will come the 2 1hs epics. Voila -- you have ALL THREE EPICS on your person and you can bank the scabbard.

Now with the introduction of LDoN something amazing has happened as a side effect of this. Make sure you have all three swords on you. Get your Scabbard. Go see the Dorf next to Wendan and Kragek in EFP. Hand him the 2hs and the scabbard.

BINGO - you now have all three epics given to you in their NEW / unsummoned form. THIS MEANS THEY HAVE DIFFERENT ITEM TAG IDs. Take note that you now have TWO red blades, TWO blue blades, and the Jagged Blade of War.

THATS RIGHT YOU HAVE TWO -- 500HATE PROCCING 1hs WEAPONS! Enjoy.
