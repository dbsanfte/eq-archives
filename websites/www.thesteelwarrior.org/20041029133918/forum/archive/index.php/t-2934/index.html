Look at this in relation to Brutul's "Rethinking Taunt" thread in the arena.

There seems to be some aggro "bonus" for being the first to engage a mob. Well, if you're 2nd and the #1 dies... then it's theoretically possible that you have assumed his "bonus".

"Rooted" mobs don't have the same proximity type of aggro as mobs who are actually bound to the ground by the spell "root". LTK, Telk, yadda yadda have a run speed of 0, they're not "rooted", and they follow the same aggro principles as any unrooted mob.
