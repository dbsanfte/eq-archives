Raivene
The reason they are asking the questions has nothing to do with SoE getting into the plat or character selling business, though I have no doubt if there was enough support from the player base they would do that as well.

It's for the exact same reason you can now change your last name by yourself now. It would reduce the core customer service requirements by removing a great deal of fraud from the plat and character aftermarket.

In one of the summit reports (and no, I don't remember which) they were talking about the number of petitions received and what *70%* of them are for is fraud.

Now one of OUR complaints is lack of customer service to in game (and to most of us that means non-fraud) issues. If they could develop a system that elminated even 50% of user to user seller fraud, then available customer service to the rest of the petition queue would improve (at least on paper) by 35%.

The reality is SoE can't shut down the Yantis' and others selling stuff for cash. It's like the government trying to shut down alcohol during prohibition, it just doesn't work. If SoE put all their customer service resources on shutting it down no other petition would get answered ever... even at double their current resources. But you can control it somewhat by putting mechanisms in place and in doing so free up the "cops" to do other things.
