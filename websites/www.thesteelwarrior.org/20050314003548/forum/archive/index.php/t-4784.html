There's really no reason not to put an Anger 3 on your BoW. You're shortchanging yourself on a lot of aggro by not putting it in. If you want to do damage, BoW really isn't the weapon you want to be using anyway. Even unaugmented it'll probably get you killed in the wrong times more than once if you use it solely for DPS, unless everyone else's aggro is way better than you (i.e. time level weapons). For a DPS weapon, I'd recommend SEWS with a damage proc, and it's cheaper than putting 2 damage weapons with 2 damage procs too.
