Endurance isn't a stat, as STA, DEX, STR (etc.) are. It's a quantity, as HP is. There is no cap per se, although there is a number you can't get past with gear currently available in the game. And that number, just like HP, will keep changing with expansions and so on. (And I dunno what either number is ... bigger than mine, tho. :p)

+END is not about *sta regen* (it's endurance regen, now), it's about how large a wad you can blow all at once. The more Endurance you have, the more /disc's you can chain together, or the more you have in reserve when you need to throw a couple up in quick succession. For improved regeneration, there is the AA *Heighened Endurance*.
