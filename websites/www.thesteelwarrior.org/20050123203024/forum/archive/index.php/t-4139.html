I play my warrior as my main, and I don't use alts to try and grind pp for him. My advice to you.

Worry more about your haste item than your weapons. You won't see significant changes with upgrading the weapons you have as much as you will with upgrading (or getting your first) haste item. If you dont' have one yet, save up your pp for an FBSS or SCHW. Your weapons are decent enough and spending more on new weapons (barring most likely a very expensive upgrade) you will not see much improvement. Going from no haste to an FBSS (1.5k on my server now) you will see a drastic improvement.

For "bargain weapons" check the bazaar frequently. This is going to vary from server to server, but the best "deals" I have found are for Warrior only, min level weapons. This kind of stuff you aren't going to use until you get to a higher level, but I post it in order to give you an idea of what to look for. Some examples that have made me happy (with min level 50+):

Bed Leg Club
Great Staff of Thunder
Club of the Ice Ocean
Defenders Lightblade
Stonebladed Axe of Dismay

At your level check out maybe a Jarsath Trident, Centi Shortsword, Centi Warspear, or a Centi Warhammer. These can sometimes be found cheap in the bazaar (my server anyways). Oh, and last but not least KEEP ALL YOUR WEAPON SKILLS MAXXED!!! Nothing sucks worse than trying to gain 200 points in a weapon skill with a higher level character (2hb comes to mind as a good example).
