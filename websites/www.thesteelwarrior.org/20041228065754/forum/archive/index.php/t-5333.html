Payn, originally /shield did not work with other discs because you could potentially /shield someone while you had /def activated, thereby, not only reducing the damage to the MT, but also mitigating the damage absorbed from the MT. Also, if the MT had /def on as well, the entire damage output could be reducued greatly.

In addition, if /shield stacked with discs, then they could stack with each other, in other words, have 4 warriors, all /def'd, and all /shield the MT who is /def'd...

The scenarios for trivializing encounters were enormous.

To that end, /shield was not intended to stack with any other discs, the fact that they do now is an oversight, and not working as intended :)

So the mob is still doing more damage than if it was just one warrior tanking with defensive anyway, its just the MT is taking drastically less damage but you would still need additional healing to keep the shielder up.
