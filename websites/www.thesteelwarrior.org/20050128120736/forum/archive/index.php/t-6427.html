Why would someone think that ONE hate aug will out aggro 2 (2h) or 4 (2 1h)?
Because different weapons have different ratios and damage bonuses, and this results in different amounts of innate aggro. Procs do not automatically overcome this.

An unaugmented Vanazir totally outaggros my dual-wield weapons with Anger 2 and 3 and Force Shock and DOT (see magelo). Clearly the Vanazir is not accomplishing this through its procs. It is accomplishing this through its ratio and damage bonus. The same is true, to a lesser degree, with the Greatstaff of 4 Winds. Fast 2-handers are big aggro.
