05-26-2003, 10:49 AM
Because humans are what you are comparing against - they are the baseline. Consider, for example:

Halfling: 5% bonus
Barbarian: 5% penalty

less than what? more than what? Humans, that's what.

To put it another way, barby xp for killing mob_01 = 0.95*X, halfling xp = 1.05*X, where X is the xp that a human would get from killing mob_01.

Change X and you change the value for every other calculation that is a function of it. So the other classes/races with a bonus/penalty all simply shift, and the delta between them all remains the same.

As to why other races should get more or less xp per mob than humans in the first place, you'd have to ask SOE. ;-)
