I have my 8th Ring now.. sorry for not replying sooner.

The 8th Ring war was pretty simple for me.

First I parked my wiz (invis) in the hut in thr Ry'Gorr fort.

I turned in the note I got from Garadain to Gloradin and casted invis from Mantle of Stealth and ran back into the fort.

By the time I had got there, all the normal-spawn orcs had despawned. Soon after that, the Chief, Firbrand the Black and 2 orc shammies popped midway between the door of the hut (that wiz was parked in and that I ran back to invis) and the gate of the fort itself. All of the other orcs line up outside the fort, and will not aggro you.

I shot the Chief with an arrow and Firbrand came to. I did this part before and got Chief single, so it IS possible to get him single (I was 58-59 at the time) I rooted Firbrand on the wiz, then went to work on the Chief. I slowed him with ToD, snared him with snare whip, then beat him down with my GSoT and a few wiz nukes.

I looted the head and then went to kill Firbrand, I did a lot more nukes since I was worried about time and had about 40% HP. He dropped some Gaunts and a Dark Ember.

I had the wiz invis me (2nd time I was invis.. Mantle of Stealth is the perfect item for this) and ran out of the gate of the fort and a little to the left to meet Garadain.

Despite my hurry (and feeling quite exhilirated.. this was one of the best experiences in EQ in recent memory, much like the first time I saw Vox, really gets your blood going with so much riding on you not screwing up) I still had to wait on Garadain to talk. He talks alot :)

Eventually he starts moving and tells you to follow and dont get hurt. He gets to the fort and shouts CHARGE! Hand over the head and get your 8th Ring.

If ya want (I did) go help the dwarfies. The really don't seem to need the help though, there are a LOT of them and they came out on top the 3 times I saw this. Be careful and don't get killed by them (I did :() it's kind of embarrassing and you don't get to click your new clicky for a couple agonizing minutes waiting on the Cleric.

Hope that helps someone.
