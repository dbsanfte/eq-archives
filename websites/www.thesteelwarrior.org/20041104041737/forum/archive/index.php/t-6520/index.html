Bonequest
Fabled exe axe has decent low agro dps if you are not tanking but it will never be a good agro weapon. Looking at what you have, the copper hammer in your bag should be in main hand and probably the fleshgrinder in off hand for best agro combo. If you can, pick up a sword of the bloodsworn to replace the fleshgrinder and that will get you to 65.

Looking at your gear, its really not bad for your level. You can wear ornate in 3 levels and I would wait until then to get some of that. Also concentrate on leveling until you hit 65. I know LFG for hours sucks bad because I did it for a long time but as an example I have 3100 hp naked at 65 so just leveling and then doing a few hp AA skills will make your hit points go up a lot. You will mitigate better and resist more nukes etc. Once you get decent agro weapons then the word will get around that you are a tank who can hold agro and it will make the LFG time less and less the closer to 65 you get.
