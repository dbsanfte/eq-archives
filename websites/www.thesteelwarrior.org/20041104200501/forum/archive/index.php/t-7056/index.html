I think your best bet is to get to 65 ASAP.

If your goal is to tank PoP progression mobs, you will defentely need to be 65.

After that, try a few hard LDoN, if you can squeek by without the defensive AA's, you will get great exp .. great droped loot .. and great pointed loot.

Alot of my gear is from Hard LDoN, few raid loot thrown in.

As for 41 haste .. few warriors in my guild have opted to stay with 36 haste, there thought is that at 36, they hit the haste cap with chanter haste. I however have the 41 haste from PoI dragon.

Once you hit 65, off the top of my head could do as a raid:
- BoT tower mobs ** Ornate BP and some nice weapons **not hard after a few tries to get down strat)
- PoI dragon (hate hammer and 41 haste)
- Ring of Slime event in CT (100hp bracer)
- Shade in PoN (drops Horn Spiked Shoulder Plates, 65hp shoulders)
- Mujaki event in PoN (drops Cloak of Daggers, 80hp back) ** 24 max able to do, so nice target**
- Paffa event in CoD (drops 85hp cloak)
- Can do non raid targets (so targets "ubers" can kill1 groups) named in Ferbi with a few groups.
In Groups
- GoD 3rd and 4th sewers (first hit very hard ** unless tweeked with last patch **, second has no loot)
- Kill Bari tarsh, FT2 ring drops(to get casters to help) and nice mellee earring "Rhyolite Tear of Thralldom" drops (on my magelo).
- Hard LDoN as previous mentioned
- Normal LDoN if hards are to hard, or if you want to pharm points fast.

That is off the top of my head .. But as stated before, you are GTG to lvl 65, so lvl up ... AND HAVE FUN ..
