Looks like the reincarnation of a velious era warrior.

Im not sure how good the duel red epic combo is, so maybe look at finding an alternative primary to startwith with a better ratio but still with an agro proc. If you can squeeze into a BoT tower raid once you are 62 then a GreatBlade of Storms would be a perfect weapon for you, Copper hammer of Striking is a good option too which you could camp with a couple of friends in droga if you can't get one to drop in the bazaar. CHSD from Xanamech in PoI is another good option and possibly obtainable via pickup raids.

Other than raid drops your progression is really ornate -> Hard LDoN drops for most gear. Once you can group in BoT camping or saving up for Ornate legs would be a good start. Ornate Boots and Arms are more expensive than they are worth so Elbow Spiked Armplates and Bloodied Boot of something (from LDoN Hard) would be better options.

Start doing LDoN adventures aswell. The plate from Everfrost at ~700points/piece is great value and would be a good upgrade for you, the ~1400point gear from Tak is nicer but alot more expensive, really depends how much time you have to put into it.
