Boleslav
Almost certainly easier to farm cash and buy unless you are good friends with a skilled smith. Success rate on this armor is about 50% at max skill. Someone looking to do the combine for free just to get the chance at a skillup may not be close to max skill. Figure 1% less chance to succeed for every point below max skill. Suddenly you are looking at a 20-25% chance to succeed on some items… You could easily exceed the purchase price of many pieces in the value of wasted components.
