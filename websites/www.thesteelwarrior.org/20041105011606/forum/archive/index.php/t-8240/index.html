Nazgrub
Since aggro is basically the same, all that matters is the DPS and maybe tanking stats. I use the equation that is in the library:

(Damage x 2 + Damage Bonus)/Delay for main hand

SoBS = 1.89

FSSoY = 1.83

CHoS = 1.9

I chose to main hand the CHoS, equation comes out about the same, plus the proc is bigger and the delay is a tiny tiny bit higher (ripostes).

For off-hand I just go by pure ratio since damage bonus doesn't come into play, and FSSoY has the best ratio.

Also, yes the stun procs aggro even if the mob can't be stunned.
