Fitemore
Well, one way to look at it is this: If you know how to play EQ, you'll be at least decent at any class. I could pick up any class right now and be at least passable within 30 minutes. I'd be good within a few days. If I was going to play a character from scratch, the main goal would be to get him gear and levels as fast as possible so I could get to high end again.

That being said, definitely make sure your skills are kept up. Piercing is the least important, but the rest are very good to keep up. Defense should stay up as long as you are tanking the mobs. Remember, if you have a DS up your offense goes up but your defense doesn't.

It should be very easy to get levels with that crew. Choose mobs that are far too high of level (10ish above), almost kill them, then memblur. Have your warrior finish the mob off for full experience. That will get you past the first 50 or so levels no problem. Afterwards, just have the warrior tank the mobs and make sure he does the majority of the damage. Any time he might not get exp, memblur the mob.
