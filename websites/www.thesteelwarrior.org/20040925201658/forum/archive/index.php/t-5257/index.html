Shadowfrost
The clerics have been learning about heal aggro. ;)

http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=17872

Conclusions so far are rather surprising. They certainly aren't what I would have predicted! More testing's needed but here's what we think so far...

1. There is a significant chance - sample sizes are small as yet, but it's quite possibly a chance in excess of 50% - that a direct heal will generate zero hate. That's right, none at all.

2. Where a heal generates hate, the aggro caused is based off the total non-critical amount actually healed - in other words, a heal for 1,000 generates a fixed amount of hate whether the spell used was Supernal Light, Complete Healing or whatever - and a critical heal for 2,000 generates the same hate as a non-critical heal for 1,000.

3. "Unused" healing doesn't generate hate. In other words, if I cast a CH for 8925 but it only lands for 4k, the amount of hate I create is based on the 4k rather than on the 8925.

We haven't proved whether heal aggro is capped or not.
