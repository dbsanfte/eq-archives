Hi there.

Go to your Actions panel, and click the third tab along (the one with the tiny crossed swords on it).

Right click one of the buttons and choose skill "Taunt". This gets you a combat button to taunt, so far so good.

Now, move the pointer over your new taunt button, and hold the left mouse button down till it attaches the button to the pointer.
Then click on your hotbutton panel to transfer it and you're good to go.

What most classes describe as taunting and what warriors call taunting are two different things:

If the mob turns away from you, get close to it and hit taunt. That's what a warrior thinks of as taunting (or a good SK or Paladin).

Otherwise if it's already hitting you, just slam (or kick) and hit it to make it hate you more so it won't attack anyone else. That's what everyone else thinks taunting consists of.

Have fun :)
