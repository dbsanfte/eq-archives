The only real (not alternate entry) flag to upgrade this charm that you can get with one group is the trial of execution in PoJ. You might be able to do other trials but they are usually considered harder.

You might be able to do BoT tower key with 2 good groups if they have really good gear like all VT stuff but usually 3 to 4 groups is safer on the mini bosses in the plane of storms if the people don't have good gear.

You can also get alternate entry flags to (I believe) tactics, torment, crypt of decay, and halls of honor with just one group. These are basically to let you group in these zones but they don't count towards elemental progression. They do increase stats on the charm and give AA points.

So basically, you can probably get all 6 pre-elemental zone flags with a small guild which gives the stats below but to try to go farther you need a more powerful guild that can kill Maneatic Behemoth, Terris Thule, Aerin Dar, and Grummus to get the real flags.

Intricate Wooden Figurine
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
Slot: CHARM
AC: 15
STR: +9 DEX: +6 STA: +9 AGI: +6
HP: +60 SV FIRE: +6 SV DISEASE: +6 SV COLD: +6
SV MAGIC: +6 SV POISON: +6
Required level of 51.
WT: 0.1 Size: TINY
Class: WAR
Race: ALL
