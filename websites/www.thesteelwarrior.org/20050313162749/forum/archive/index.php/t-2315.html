Melees really get hosed when it comes to elem exp because you are bound to having a healer and most often a slower in group. Those 2 DPS spots cost you when it comes to getting max exp. The thing to do is to get in to an all caster group in the 2nd castle. If you are lucky, you can find a group that isn't full. In most cases, if the group has any sense they will invite you because the amount of exp per mob goes up once you get to 4+ people in group. You can play puller and free DPS guy once the kiter has good agro.

If the group knows what they are doing, you can kill a massive amount of mobs before repops. In a full group you get a between 5-6% AA per kill and you can kill at a rate better than 1 per minute. Using horses for mana regen and swapping out kiters as they go LOM you can keep up that pace until you get tired of racking in the AA.
