The best way to get your weapon skills up is to use them.

You have 1HS maxed? Try putting a fast Piercer or 1HB in your off hand. You keep playing as usual, but you are working on your other skills. Go back to dual 1HS only if you really need to, and then just as long as you need to, then switch back.

For 2HS, get a Nathsar Greatsword. 17/30, I think. They are fairly inexpensive, and once your 2HS is up, you can either sell it or share it with a friend.

For 2HB, get a shovel. Not sure on the stats, but it is fast for a 2HB.

Now, for those 2-handed weapons, go into a zone, and find a warrior and ask him if you can assist him and that you only want to work on raising your skill. Once when I was in Dreadlands, a Troll Warrior asked if he could assist me so he could work on skill. Since I was holding agro, he got more hits in and he raised 2HB 50 points fairly quickly.

You can raise skill on Light or Dark Blues.

A friend of mine just got a GSoT, and his 2HB was 20. He had it at 250 in a weekend. The GSoT is a fast weapon, so it is good to train with *IF* you have one. Not many of us can afford such a great weapon.

If you can get most of your skills over 100, then don't worry. When that good weapon comes, just use it.

My piercing was like 120 when I came across an awesome 2HP. I started using it as my main weapon and my piercing was over 200 fairly quickly.

Raising weapon skills is a Zen thing. Don't think about it.

Just do it.
