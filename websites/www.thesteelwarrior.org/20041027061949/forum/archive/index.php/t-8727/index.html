Armugol,

I looked at it this way (I thought about the exact same thing)..

BoC - yeah, it is an ok DPS weapon, but you can get a 17/20 from LDON easy enough. BoC's proc is a pure hate proc which means it is solely for keeping aggro.

Is the ykesha aug an aggro dealer? Sure it is, it also adds some damage (figure 2 procs per minute so 250 extra dmg tops (without crits) so maybe 4.2 extra dps).

Most folks agreed (way back before anger augs changed) that ykesha aug is similar to anger2. Anger3 is definately more aggro.

Assuming we agree with this then do you want 4.2dps (ykesha) more or 6.7 hate/sec(anger3) more? (200 more hate a proc, 2ppm so 400hate / 60 seconds).

Keep in mind that 4.2dps most likely does not equate to 4.2hate/sec. Also, why take a weapon which really is only meant for aggro generation and try to turn it into a DPS weapon? If you intend to mainhand it and want to keep aggro, then I'd go for the Anger3 aug as it is unresistable hate added ontop of what it already generates. If you're there to keep a mob glued on you, then DPS is irrelevant, the more hate you generate the harder the rest of the folks can hack/slash/nuke the mob and not worry about taking aggro.

Perhaps you're only in one group situations and not raiding, in which case I'd say sell the BoC and mainhand your GBoS w/ykesha aug and offhand a copper hammer of striking w/some DD aug and you'll keep decent aggro while dishing some ok DPS. You could also get your 2hander AAs and go that route if you can get a decent 2hander.
