Since there is supposed to be a huge patch coming up next Wed, I wanted to discuss what you would like to see in the next big patch.

Here are my thoughts:

Class balance changes:

- Warriors:
Improved clickie on Epic 1.5 and 2.0 to mitigate twice as much damage.
Revamped Aura of Runes to take less endurance and increased size of rune proc.
Revamped the level 70 proc to give a sizable + hate whenever a mob hits the warrior as well as stunning it.
Savage onslaught is now a straight upgrade to fellstrike, 10% linear upgrade.

- Wizards:
Introducing an entire new spell line designed to sustain DPS over time. Those spells have a range of 50, low damage, very low mana cost, and start at level 50 and ramp up to level 70.
Mana regen on familiars has been increased across the board.

- Magicians:
A mod rod upgrade has been made. This has the same restriction as old mod rods as well as shared recast timer. The click is twice as much mana for twice as much HP as an old mod rod, with a recourse of a 10 second silence spell after clicking the mod rod.

All summon spells are now targetted, including pet weapons that automatically go on the targetted pet.

- Druids:
A new hand of ro upgrade spell has been introduced. Epic clickie has been changed to be +10% damage for 1.5 and 25% for 2.0 making it less than useless.

- Shamans:
-The hastened rabid bear AA has been changed into an AA to decrease the amount slow is mitigated by. Each rank of the AA increases the effectiveness of slow, up to 10% extra slow at maxed rating - thus mobs that moderated slow down to 50%, now slow up to 60% for shamans only.
-An AE malos is now available as an ancient spell.
- Decreased aggro on dots.
- Increased effectiveness of cripple line of spells, those spells should now have a noticable effect on reducing mob DPS.

- Clerics:
- Increased value of effective AC on clerics by a fair amount. Clerics should now take damage just below ranger and other chain wearing melees, however should not have the damage avoidance abilities those classes have.

- Necromancers:
- Added option for other people to filter DoT damage to make parsing DoT damage on raids easier.

- Rogues:
- Put Duellist and deadeye on the same timer. Having separate dps increasing discs was leading rogues to outbursting wizards which was not desired. Rogues should still sustain a much higher damage, just in very short fights wizards should be ahead.
- Damage increasing augments like those found in Ikkinz4/Uqua are now usable only on offhanded weapons.

- Monks:
- Mitigation nerf reduced slightly.
- Monks now have the ability to purchase tactical mastery.
- Restored the ratio of melee damage to 100/110/140 - if a monk is doing 330 DPS, a rogue should do 420 DPS.

- Beastlords:
Increased beastlord heals slightly.

- Rangers:
Every time a ranger dies, each person in their group gets some cake in the inventory, this way they will finally have a use~
Harmony upgrade available for rangers. Auspice of hunter has been upgraded quite heavily now.

- Bards:
The 65 + Rizlona have been upgrade.
One now has a + avoidance mod and a + heal component that stacks with focus, and the other has a + CE and + accuracy. The ancient spell has everything.
The new snare song is fixed.
Missed notes are eliminated with mastery of the past.

- Berserkers:
Hell if I know

- Enchanters:
Their mez now has a -50 resist adjustment base. Epic 2.0 effect is fixed. Lowered aggro on Tashan to match aggro of malos.

- SK:
Changed epic clickie to increase amount lifetaps heal the SK by 3x and 6x for 2.0 for a minute.

- Paladins:
Mostly balanced.

Content changes:
Increased damage on some mobs in some trials, but keep mobs fully slowable. HP's increased some but resists decreased to make casters more friendly in some trials. increased number of events where good play is rewarded (ala fear trial) and decreased events where gear and amount of healing required are key (ala ingenuity trial)
