Crystara
Scalecracker if you can get ahold of one is a pretty difficult to replace 41% haste/range slot, 33ac and 100hp as well along with a few minor stats. With our incite line a bow is no longer necessary so it doesn't bother most to use range for something other then a bow.

As far as the rest of those listed, I personally went for a bracer, I had a Silver Bracelet of Speed from Doz for the longest time, and Earth bracer was about to rot basically so I went ahead and upgraded to it, it is the only EP haste item I would take even though it wasn't really a massive upgrade.

Moss mantle is the other decent choice, I would not suggest relying on recieving a puresteel mantle, it's both rare and Air is supremely retarded so most hate raiding it.
Zhisz
I would say that if your guild kills TXD, get a Breastplate of Speed - but you've already aug'ed your Kizraks BP, so the bracer / shoulder slots are in bigger need of an upgrade.
Bracers of Earthen Energy is a good choice, with flowing moss mantle also being a good choice - unless you are counting on alot of farming in Air to get the Puresteel Mantle.
If at all possible for you, pick up a Talisman of Vah Kerrath. Asides from good ac and good hp, it also has a very nice effect.
