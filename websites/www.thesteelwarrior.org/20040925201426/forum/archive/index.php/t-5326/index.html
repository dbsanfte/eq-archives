The three named mobs in the Corinav ring all have a chance to drop elemental bracers. If your guild is planning to do Arbitor as a back-flag, they'll need to hit up the PoE rings that drop loot (the vine ring drops plate bracers relatively often). The mobs in the FEnnin Ro cycle have a very small chance of dropping loot as well. The only choice you really have for 2-handers if you don't manage to get SEWS or BoW is to get a Plankton Greatsword from PoWater named. They're pretty much one groupable. The greatsword is 75/65 with stats and 125hp, I dont' believe it has any procs. It's not really much for agro either unless you aug it with Anger III.

Since you are just apping, I would assume that chances are pretty good you won't have first choice in drops for the most part. The good news is that chances are also pretty good that there are some nice items that are likely to be rotting if the guild you are apping to has been in Time for long. I won't recommend any god loots from the EPs because that stuff is still pretty good and likely to be snatched up by regular guild members. Obviously, try for any Raex's that you can get your hands on. The only real clearing that might result in armor drops are on the way to the Rathe and the 3 minis in Coirnav.

As for the Time drops, there are actually quite a few options. Tier 1-3 loot is still very good and usually rotting or going to apps unless the guild has recently broken in. Here's the short list -

Tier 1 -
Pulsing Emerald Hoop
Ring of Force
Smooth Onyx Torque

Tier 2 -
Cloak of Ferocity
Cudgel of Wrecking
Gauntlets of Disruption
Girdle of Restoration
Glowing Chains
Hoop of the Enlightened

Tier 3 -
Cloak of Wishes
Necklace of Celestial Energy
Earring of Xaoth Kor
Faceguard of Frenzy
Fiery Crystal Guard
Mask of Strategic Insight
Timeless Coral Greatsword
Ethereal Destroyer
Elem legs

You should be able to get quite a few of the above items. If you plan ahead, you could end up with at least 8 or 9 quality upgrades that will likely put you over 10khp buffed.
