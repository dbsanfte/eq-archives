Mahulk Keras
Don't need AE taunt to switch tank on AoW. Agro on AoW is all about range. Your 4th and 5th tanks most likely were closer to AoW (or WAY ahead in damage/agro) so they got eat before 2nd tank. Make sure everyone knows AoW range agro and you will have less trouble to get control on the fight.

AE Taunt worked when unshrinked and levitated couple months ago. They might have change it tho and I think it depends on the race of the warrior.

Don't use /pause of you will not be able to kick/slam/taunt/disarm and all during the pause (not that it matters much for AoW). Simply use /disc to see timer. At level 60+ timer for defensive reuse starts around 10min 30sec, so at 8mins you can call disc down in 30sec.

Bumamgar
Yeah, single tank is the way to go on AoW these days. From your magelo, it looks like you should break 8k raid buffed.

We've been single tank killing AoW since back in the day when I was around 7500 raid buffed.

Just set up a solid 3 second CH rotation (can be done with as few as 5 CH'ers [Clerics/Druids] assuming decent mana regen)

Have them run at 3 seconds the whole fight, from start to finish. Get gnome illusion, corner him, kill him.

30 people is more than enough to lay him out.

If you want to "practice" the encounter without blowing it, do the Idol unslowed. The Idol doesn't despawn, so you can try on him over and over, and the AoW fight is essentially the same as the Idol is when unslowed. Idol has less HP, but his DPS unslowed is very similar to AoW.

Have your highest HP/defensive AA warrior tank him (CA3/LR5 are gods against AoW)

If your MA goes down, have everyone except the SA turn off attack and back away. Let the SA build solid agro, then have him call in the troops.
