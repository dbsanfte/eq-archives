08-04-2004, 01:28 PM
Go to your starting home town.

Press Ctrl-F.

Select "Tribute Master". Click "Find". Follow the glowing path.

If "Tribute Master" is not listed, then zone to the next sub-zone of your city and try again.

If two "Tribute Masters" are listed, then try each of them. Only one will work for you.

Right-click on the tribute master to open tribute window.

Browse the tribute effects. Choose the ones you want. Note the point cost that is listed.

Donate some tradeable items to the tribute master to get tribute points (just like selling to a merchant).

When you are happy with your tribute points and chosen effects, you can leave.

Whenever you join an XP group or raid, hit Alt-U, and click "Activate".

Whenever you leave the XP group or raid, hit Alt-U, and click "Deactivate".
