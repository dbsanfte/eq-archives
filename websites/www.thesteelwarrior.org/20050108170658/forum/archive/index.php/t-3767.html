"So actually you could have the ranger enter the zone and the cleric immediately rez. Then its just a matter of the ranger accepting rez at the first signs of trouble."

The entire zone of Fear is trackable from the zone in, and because of con, those 3 mobs will be within the top 6 mobs on the list (assuming the ranger has tracking set to /con). A ranger will know if they are up the second he zones in, without the need to take any DT's.

And you don't even really need a cleric. It means buring a disc, but a ranger with a gate potion less than 20 seconds in casting (Thurg, etc) can zone in, check track, move to SW corner and gate out. If mobs come, trigger WS.
