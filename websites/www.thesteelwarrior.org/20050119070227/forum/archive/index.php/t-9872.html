I marked the places where i used to camp in dulak on a map. Camp where the red square is. Get to camps invis.

http://www.kuhlendahl.de/Everquest/dulak.JPG

Spot 1:
Camp at the wall. You can single pull the whole open area, the wandering guards and armory guardians. Scouts could be already LB as you said, but give nice cash loot (some PP and gems). This is the easiest camp in this zone i found.

Spot 2:
This is a nice safe spot at the wall. Sometimes groups camp here. You can single pull the whole open area.

Area 3:
I used to hunt here too (no special camp place). Most mobs are single pulls. If you get more then you can handle run up the ladder and zone to torgiran. Just make sure you are sowed all the time and don't play the hero. Then you shouldn't get in trouble.



Something else to mention:
On the way to dulak i always had a short look into Nadox. When you zone from the caves of Gunthak into Nadox there are 3 Fire Elemantals near ZoneIn. They are all single pulls and great exp at your level. I always killed them on my way when they were up. If you cant take them you have the zoneline right behind you.


Good luck :)
