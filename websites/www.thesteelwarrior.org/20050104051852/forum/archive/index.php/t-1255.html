Today we were exploring Veksar with a group of guildies, if you know the place, I'll describe the location... its near the golem area, before you get there, theres a pool, kinda big, with a few mobs inside, EVEN before getting there, you have to swim thru an underwater tunnel, right? Ok, you come out of water, turn right and theres a room with a few rotting peasants and 'an animist' which is the PH of a Lord Somethingie. (This is before getting to golem area). So we killed animist and kept going, druid tracked a named spawn and we came back and found this Lord in the place of the animist, and dropped:

Kylong Shinguards (Leg slot)
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM
AC: +35
Sta: +4
Wis: +6
Int: +6
HP: +45
All plate classes

He was DB to 61, we killed him with a CHing druid, necro, another war and a mage, he dropped kinda fast, so I assume he was around lvl55. Awesome legs for a single groupable spawn while you are lurking around and exploring.
Like everyone said, stay in Kael. The Draconic shoulders quest is easy if your CoV faction is good (15ac 12str 12sta 6SvC/F).
I wouldnt get Erolisi armor, like Mithaniel (my god), the imbued version adds stats that are not useful for a warrior, so you might as well just buy the Agnostic version (Fine Heraldic). The neck is a good choice, also you can buy shoulders (20ac 5dex 30hp) if you dont feel like questing (The neck is not really expensive but the shoulders might be).
Theres a cloak that drops in Torgidan's mines, Steel Mesh Cloak, 12ac 10sta 12str and other stats, its off a named mob in a circular cave, cant remember the name but try and look up nameds in Alla and their loots :)
If you and your friends hit UP sometime, maybe as a guild raid, you might get lucky with a Raldukan Helm (26ac 4sta 4str 35hp and resists) or a Bracelet of Fortitude (12ac 15sta 6SvAll).
Hope this helps :)
