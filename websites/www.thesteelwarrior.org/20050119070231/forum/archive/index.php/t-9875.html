Ok, since you are in a close-knit guild, I'm going to make some suggestions that might be just a bit difficult for you, but not altogether undoable.

Boots: Take some of your guildies and pharm hard mistmoore ldons. The experience isn't great, but leadership AAs in ldons move fast, and you'll save up points as you pharm. These points will come in handy for my next suggestion of:

Arms: Using the points you've already gained from pharming mistmoores, start to unlock the royal attendant armor in north ro. Though you'll need to fully unlock the merchant, you can grab some nifty stuff using the points you'll already have. I believe there is a nice two-hand slash that you can get off the merchant in nro, when properly aug'd, you can use as an alternate to the weapon setup you're currently using.

Pants: Your kizraks can last you a while, but you might consider pharming burnished antique greaves from vxed over in GoD. You might also consider pharming Kuua Infantry Leggings from Muramite Proving Grounds.

Hands: Velium Encrusted Gauntlets off Tormax, or Warlord's Steel Gauntlets (pharm these in LDoNs)

Wrists: Consider pharming the ring of slime event in Cazic Thule for the Acid Bracers. While you spend some time in CT, you can collect items to spawn the Construct for:

Face: Thulian Visage of Horror. Probably the best tradable mask available for a Warrior in the game.

Offhand: Take your guild to Bastion of Thunder, and grab a Great Blade of Storms off Eindridge Icestorm. As an alternative, take your guild to Plane of Tactics and kill Vallon Zek (one groupable with great gear, so most definitely doable with 2-3 groups and the right strategy) for an Obsidian Scimitar of War.

After you grab some of this stuff, consider leveling up to 69 and making your way into Riftseekers and the Ikkiniz Trials in GoD. There you can upgrade your:

Ring: Glyphed Prayer Ring. Drops off the loot names in trials 7, 8 and 9. While you're pharming these trials, remember they are pretty ok for experience, so you may want to try to get a:

Face 2.0: Death Incantor's Mask (this may not be the actual name, but I'm lazy and don't wanna look it up right now) it has a focus for dots however, so if you bring a shaman or necro, be prepared for some competition.

Range: A nice range with 25ac and 145hp drops in these trials as well. I again can't recall the proper name, but it drops off the Rav Hunter in Ikkiniz 8, Carved Totem of somethingorother.

Breastplate: Celestial Lorica. Very nice upgrade from items available in the bazaar. As an alternate breastplate, consider pharming Lord Inquisitor Seru over in Sanctus Seru.

This stuff should put you pretty far over what you're at right now, if you have the patience to go out and get it all.

For the content your referring to you should have no problem smashing it. However if you want to see the biggest bang for buck when it comes for spending time in front of computer and boosting abilities. Right now I would probably sit an some LDoNs and GRIND GRIND GRIND those AAs and Augs. Once your done with the augs you need move to levels and start hitting places like WoS, RCoD, etc... Lv 70 is a major boost (imo) in mitigation. I don't have a very expansive log for it but my numbers show a sizable boost in on the mitigation tables from 69 to 70. As well as a good chunk of HP.

I wouldn't even try MPG until you have at least around 1650 to 1700 AC. The biggest problems you will have in WoS & MPG is the elite xxxx, they are the "proc'0'matic" 680 to 720DD, and a pain to resist short of some killer 300+ SvAll. Only major difference is that in WoS South fields your looking at max hits of about 680, North field max hits of around 780, and MPG max hits from about 980 to 1130 for some stuff. Also be careful of the lightening guys in MPG, they like to flurry and any tank under 8K is in danger of being "one rounded".
