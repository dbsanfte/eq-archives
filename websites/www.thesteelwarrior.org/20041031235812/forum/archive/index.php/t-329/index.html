Beornegar
Sarnak fort is fun. Solusek goblins should be fun and profitable and that dungeon should keep you going for a while. (I remember still getting xps for some stuff at around 35th-40th level). Inside splitpaw and outside at the spires are both good from about 25-35. While cash doesnt sound like a problem for you, in both sol and paw the mobs drop fine steel weapons too. In the 30-40 range crypt of dalnir and kaesora are both really fun dungeons. Guk is pretty good from about 25 up...upper guk for lower levels and lower guk for upper levels. There's lots of places in velious and on the moon for the mid levels too. That taps out my little grey cells.
