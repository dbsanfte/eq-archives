I have been fiddling with this now for a couple weeks.

My conclusion is that FB shields are only truly useful as a "hate pump"; i.e. if you swap it in to proc it on bash when you need hate/agro. I have found that leaving it permanently equipped is really not an option; at least with a CHSD in main hand. There just is not enough hate generated to "pump up". However, when using shield swap or "ruatha" there is a BIG BIG difference to me in the amount of hate I have...to the tune I had two wizards try to knock agro off me (as a test) and I held it fairly consistently (red epic offhand).

Of course these are subjective observations. Unfortunately there are no empirical experimentys I can think of to back it up (ideas?).

My conclusion:
- With "ruatha" bash: a significant upgrade
- Alone (i.e. keep equipped): not sufficient
