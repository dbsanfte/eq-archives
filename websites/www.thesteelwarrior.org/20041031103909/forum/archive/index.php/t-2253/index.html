Duke73
Just played around with it for an hour 2boxing with my shammy and I can say confidently Great Sword of the Bloodsworn >> Sword of the Bloodsworn and Anklesmasher, my current aggro combo.
The snap aggro like all 2handers is much better than duel wielding so I can slow immediately with shammy and grab aggro with 1 taunt, and with an early proc can cast Bane of Nife (1600dmg dot) with shammy and still maintain aggro if he's out of melee range. If he's in melee range he would grab aggro 1/2 the time but 1-2 taunts took aggro back np.
Still have to try it out in a full group but I'm very impressed so far :)
Only downside is the proc being a dot, although short duration (5 ticks) dot's can wreak havoc when you have a chanter mezzing.
The upside to this over Sword of the Bloodsworn, or any stun proccer, is the dot proc on this doesn't break root.
As for the sword graphic, it looks like a Silver Swiftblade with runes down the middle of the blade.. or a very wide Velium Claidmore, not too bad imo. The proc however has no graphic.
