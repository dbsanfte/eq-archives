Bought a couple of the AC10 Augs from BB camp the other night. When I put one on my AMY Chain mask (face), the net result was 17 AC.

If this idea holds out, and there is no forced need to place Augs, it could be that placing these AC Augs adds more AC when in plate-type items, versus chain, leather, or cloth.
