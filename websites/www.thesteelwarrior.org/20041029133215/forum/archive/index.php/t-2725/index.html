Haarz,

Having pleveled quite a few of my own characters, I'll give you my progression...

I have a 65 cleric/enc/druid for the plevel group, the enchanter isn't necessary but helps on the mana regen side is all, plus haste.

levels 1 - 3 (or 5 if feeling generous) west commons - because there is a druid port there and lots of red mobs nearby

levels 3/5 - 10/15 - east karana - another druid port spot, with the snakes/spiders being awesome exp on the low end and as you get higher the wolves/crags are excellent.

levels 10/15 - 20/25 - unrest - keep everyone in wolfform so you get no aggro. Initially at level 10/15 I don't wolfform the twink and run him around the outside area grabbing all the skels/beetles/etc and hitting them all once with DS plowing them down. After 1 or 2 levels (depending on how fast it goes) I go inside to the circular room with 2 spawns in it and 1 roamer. I then pull mobs down, single, with the druid either snare pulling or low level DD. Once I'm closer to 20, I have the twink run around and pull 3 or so mobs per pull. If you're good enough you can pull trains. Respect others and don't do that if they're in the areas, you'll kill them and take spawns. I always share spawns even if a 40's something is in the tower, there are plenty of spawns for ya to pull, hit the basement with druid pulling should there be a few people here. Normally, on my server, late at night, there are 2 other ppl tops and they're outside of the building.

levels 20/25 - 30/32 - mistmoore castle - not my most favorite choice here, it is fairly painful, but doable. I find myself leaving a lot once I hit level 28/29. I used to do cazic thule 22-30 by pulling massive trains at the inner temple area, but that won't work anymore ;( You may want to think of an alternative spot, though mistmoore rarely has anyone in there and you can pull tons of mobs from inside out to the ledge. I normally fight in the tunnel, just pass the graveyard. There is only 1 roamer and depending on your puller you can harmony/single cuz of level from the whole valley area, plus backtrack to graveyard or even pull from inside down there. My favorite alternate place for these levels is Tower of Frozen Shadow (I have all the keys, a must). The best level is 2nd (library) and have my 65 war single pull, beat the mob down, snare/mem wipe (they all run) then twink kills. This is the BEST level for exp... period.. end of discussion. Levels 3/4 mobs have much more hp or are casters for the SAME amount of exp. Level 5 is a close second. Pulling the wedding party zombies (all green at 65, but actually semi-high with very low hp) is great exp at these levels!! I fight right in front of the wedding room and you'll not aggro anything there. Conversely, you could do runnyeye. Pulling the fungus mobs from below or some of the goblins works fairly well. I found the fungus are the easier to kill. I fight at the top of the first down ramp to the lower areas. There are 1 or 2 statics mobs there, but they're super low level.

levels 30/32 - 40 - I used to do old temple of droga due to the exp bonus, that's kind of key to pleveling efficiently is seeking out dungeons with an exp bonus. I haven't done a character since droga changed (though you could hit the new 'old droga' outside in frontier mountains, I'd expect it to be similar, but I dunno if there are enough mobs there....), though I did do 30-39 in the new droga using a 65 war to mow the mobs down then mem wipe and have twink finish it. The best spot, I have always liked, is the area after the large room with all the tunnels off of it, hit the far right most tunnel and go down until you come down a small ramp with a torch and 1 roaming spawn. This area is before the bridge cavern with plenty of mobs to pull. The key is snaring mobs (as they'll run) then mem wiping. I wouldn't recommend a 30+ war tanking in droga.. unless you're crazed.

levels 30/32 - 40 alternate - tower of frozen shadow - at this point, the library isn't so hot, so I move to level 6A. This has the drolvargs/spectres. Having a high tank is key here, he pulls then beat down/snare/mem wipe. Drolvargs are the best to pull. Pull them all to the point you TP in at on 6A, which is totally safe. Level 7 where Tserinna is, is a total waste, mobs have same hps and are equal/lower level with not as many to pull from. Hitting 40 here is a tad slow, but it keeps moving.

Now that you're 40, you really should have a level 60 char to group with, then simply hit whatever zone you can plow mobs down in, preferrably mobs that are green to the level 60 and you'll do great.

If all you have is the 61 druid and the 23 war to work with... things are a bit harder, definately slower. I'd have to guess the following may work for you:

23 - high 20's - unrest - perfect for druid only, might hit low 30's, but it will be slow

30ish - 40ish - old droga in frontier mountains - might be good, I can't say. Another alternate would be the overthere. Mobs hit semi hard, but it is doable. Exp isn't too too hot, but you could fear the chickens (I think they're animals) and minimize your damage. I'd fight in the valley before the frontier mountains zone, or by the skyfire zone entrance. Plants are the easiest kills, followed by chickens, rhinos, tigers (though some tigers are very low and on par with plants)

40's - 50's - timorous deep - you could quad kite with your druid at the spiroc treehouse. I did 49-54 there with my druid. Nearing 54 I found that I could kite 8/9 mobs and suck all my mana (not the best equipped wizzie, plus pre-AAs and all that jazz, velious had just come out). This may yield decent exp, I cannot say, been a long time and I know things slowed down a lot around 54. Exp at 49 was decent, for the time. You could also hit lower guk and do the lord/eye/arch magus. If that's too much for you, you could hit the sage/executioner/cavalier camp, though they're a few levels lower.

Once you hit 46 you could also charm ravens in plane of nightmare and kill stuff that way, exp might be very good. The warrior would be sitting on his butting sucking exp though, I doubt you'd want him and pet to fight, but that's up to you.

Once you break your 50's you could do Karnor's Castle, if you like pain. You could also hit old sebilis as mobs don't have that many HPs and I think you can single pull with your druid, but I can't really remember.

You're on your own at that point, as I used to have to fight in lower guk at those levels and it was fairly painful. I then hit old sebilis for my lower 50's. It wasn't until 56 that PoP came out and I've exp'd there ever since. I enjoyed the docks in plane of nightmare as you can pull tons of netling wraiths with a warrior and get singles if you pay attention. I also enjoy plane of storms in the desert (take a left once you enter the desert and setup in that corner after the rock).

wandor
I PL'd my monk to 38 before I got bored with him. I used a level 60 druid for heals and buffage. He has all of his skills except ID maxed with a grand total of around 12 hours /played. I did it when you could still get high level group buffs on twinks, but you should still be able to get pretty good results.

First of all, you want to get a temperance for the warrior - there is no level limit on temperance and will give you plenty of HP to work with. If you can pick up some lower level shammy/ranger/paladin buffs, all the better. You will also want to get Shield of Thorns (24pt damage shield) and Chloroplast (10 hp/tick regen) from the druid. It would not be a bad idea to get some shammy DS potions as well since the DS is going most of the work.

Head over to Paludal Caverns. Get a SoW on the warrior before you go in and pull an assload of mobs. I would try for 8-12 mobs at a time. Make sure that you hit every mob at least once. If the DS does 100% of the damage you wont get the exp. Once you have whacked as many as you can kill them one at a time. They are social so they won't run at low health. If the last one runs, its no problem, auto-pull is a good thing. If you don't want him to run, just have the druid snare the last mob. You can get to the late 20s in Paludal, your pulls can get bigger as you level, I would stay there until the mobs start turning light blue.

Next move to Dawnshroud. I always fought at the entrance to Seru since there are less people over there. Get a wolf form so the zelnithaks will become KOS to you. I avoided pulling crawlers because they blind and that just slows you down. In wolf form the dogs won't agro, this is good because they have a lot more HP than the cows. Pull these in mass as well. I was doing 6-8 at a time pretty confortably with my monk in moderate twink gear (Fungi + mostly acrylia studded/reinforced armor). This should get you to 40 pretty easily.

At around 40ish, head for SolB. Start at the zone in and make your way toward the CPK camp as you level up. Once those mobs start getting too low, head toward the bugs camp on the way to Naggy's lair. You should be able to get into the 50s here. You should also be able to get better buffs and bigger DSes once you get to 40 so don't forget to upgrade buffs as you progress.

Once you can tank PoP mobs - head to PoN. If you can get in a full group, you will make better exp, but you should still do well duoing. I haven't been back since the revamp, but the Hole entrance might also be a good choice if they didn't totally fuck that zone up.

In all cases up until the warrior gets to around level 45ish, the druid should have no problem tanking any heal agro he gets keeping the warrior up. Find a nice wall to back up to, this will help with interupts. I put a DS on the druid as well and just made it a point to switch to the mobs attacking the druid so the monk would get all the exp.
