If your gear and AA don't change you'll have ~3300 HP at 65. 4k would be ok for BoT, more is better of course.

HP and stamina should be your focus, along with AC. Try not to sacrifice DPS or haste either. Your other stats are basically unimportant. Besides stamina which you'll prolly be working on, your agility should stay above 75 otherwise you'll drop ~40 AC.

What Isk said ... For some targets you want to be atleast 4khps and 1150 AC when you are tanking in Tier 2 planes and above, the only thing you don't want to sacrifice to achieve this is haste. Personally I hit 60 before PoP came out and stopped XPing, then when the level limit got increased I stayed at 60 until I had finished ND3 which wont take too long at all these days.

At 60 and above 1 point of stamina gives 6hps up to 255 stamina after which the returns are reduced. Talisman of the boar (shammy buff) raises stamina by 60 so you should aim to have stamina over 195. If it gets over 220 you can afford to drop stamina for raw HPs until you get round to working on Planar Power AAs, though those will probably be pretty far down the AA track even once you hit 65.

As said above with your current gear at 65 you'll be around 3300 HPs. With ND3 and PE you can add another 12% to that which will put you at close to 3700hps and from there its just a short jump to get that goal of 4k.

You have an advantage with your partner playing a cleric in that you probably wont have to be one of the LFG crowd warriors and you hopefully wont have to spend too much time with a druid healer (and therefore without cleric buffs) so your HP total wont be as important as for other warriors in similar gear / AA situations, but otherwise a very good reason to stay 60 and AA would be to not get a rep as a paper tank by the PoV/S/HoH/BoT crowd cause thats only going to make it harder to get XP groups.

My advice is if you can get to 4k hp's with gear, that is better than doing it with AA's. Part of that is just that, with 4K hp's before you do ND, you will get more for it, and part is that you will get AA's faster at 65 than at 60, most likely. What stats to sacrafice for HP's? Everything but STA(for hp's), and to a certain extent AC. No other stats matter significantly.

A couple gear ideas for you.

1: Soulscream belt...more haste, more AC, more STA, more HP's, you can farm it easily.

2: You are not going to do significant bow damage, so get the best use you can out of that range slot. Replace nightmarewood bow with either a bow with sta and hp's, or better yet an Idol of the Thorned.

3: PoTC earing. More hp's, plus STA.

4: As Eodin mentioned, if you can get SS quest guants, grab them. One of my favorite pieces of gear.
