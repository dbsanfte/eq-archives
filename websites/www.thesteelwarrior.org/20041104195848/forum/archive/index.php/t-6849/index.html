Sassinak
I replaced CHSD with the Fist and it was definitely an aggro upgrade, although not a huge one; so it would be an upgrade from CHOS too.

If you don't see weapons with aggro procs in your near future, then you could put anger 3 on this and it would be okay. When I took it, my guild didn't touch Ssra or AOW and wasn't close to RZTW. I would have taken any 16+1/19 or 17+1/20 weapon from Tier 3 and put Anger 3 on it at the time. If you are in the same situation then maybe it's worth considering. I consider it equivalent to Agnarr sword, Jiva sword, GRMK, or a fully aug'd LDON weapon.

I know that this weapon violates all of the collective wisdom of this site, and yet I have managed to tank for my guild through elementals with it: LMM, RZTW, SolRo, Quavonis, Offossa just to name a few. My feeling is that any weapon with a ratio near 1:1 is okay aggro when it has Anger 3 on it. Naturally, better aggro weapons would be better, but so far my guild's bad luck with aggro weapons persists (zero BoW, zero SEWS, 2 months after reaching elementals), so we make do.

The Fist outaggros the GBOS and the OSOW. Bloodfrenzy outaggros it. BOC beats it by a small margin. Scourge is a DD + DOT + 4 disease counters, and it appears to produce aggro from all 3 of these components. I would estimate its hate value at 250 points initially plus 24/tick for 21 ticks. In 5 months I've had mez trouble maybe 5 times. Generally, if I'm attacking a mob, then it's not a mez target.
