Konjunction,
If you have 2 solid groups that are used to working together, you may want to look at heading to the BOT towers. If your group is used to working together, it makes things alot easier as the need for specific communication is down and everyone knows how to anticipate what each group member will do.

Eriatha Egan
I've singled grouped Vindi before, even before I had any Raex gear and my BoW. He has about 200k hp on the dot, but you're chaining CHs, so it's a matter of burning him down before your cleric runs OOM.

I've also single grouped Korucust. It was actually seven though because we had one bard out of the group. Tough, tough fight though. Korucust's dps is less than Vindi's, but it's more spikey because of his 1600dd proc (unresistable). As long as you keep the AEs (manadrain and spell slow) off your cleric you (or simply cure it IMMEDIATELY), you're golden. Korucust has about 225-275k hp.

Guardian of the Seal in The Hole. Get an SK or Necro to pet pull his guards so you have GotS single. He's a rather tricky mob though because his dps is extremely high. He doesn't hit quite as fast as Vindi does once slowed (disease, partial), but he makes up for it with his much higher ATK. Essentially, you have 3 minutes to kill him because once your Defensive drops, you're dead. The offset? GotS only has about 125k hp. I've single grouped him on many occasions. Oh yeah, druid ATK debuffs work wonders on this mob.

I could see Cursed being single groupable. I think your hardest challenge would be defeating Exiled really, as he Death Touches. Cursed's dps is extremely high unslowed (Quad 600s + Flurry), but he's FULLY slowable. His dps drops down to about 220-260 or so once slowed, crippled, and debuffed. Just time his AE to minimize group healing and you could probably CH all day long. After that, it's just a matter of burning through his ridiculous amounts of HP/AC. I'd imagine Cursed has around 600-700k hp or so.

You could probably single group all but Protector of Dresolik in Sol Ro Tower. It's just a matter of GETTING to them first. Jiva might be pushing it though, but none of the minis are even remotely difficult. They just have gobs and gobs of hitpoints.
