If you want to win a dual against your monk friend then be tricky :)

- Always use DS potions
- Get a Truncheon of Doom
Tepid Deeds proc will slow his attack for 50%. (but needs level 50 to proc i think)
Other slow proccing weapons are "wavecrasher" 20% and "willsapper" 35% (if i remember correctly)
- Get a Herbalists Spade
Paralizing earth proc will root him. Move away and shoot him with a good bow. (max your archery)
- Silken whip of ensnaring/ Anklesmasher will snare him and allow you to kite him too
- You can get instant clickie snare items too.
Fungusbeast gland (expensive), Scepter of the Forlorn (cheap)
- During the fight dont stand still. Try to get in his back so he cant hit you.
- If you kite him regen gear will help you of course
- General suggestions:
more HP: you will last longer
more AC: you will take less damage
- Get some tribute points. You dont have to use it at all times, but for a duel it can really help. I would suggest max levels of haste/regen/Attack/AC/Hitpoints

Good luck !

So unless you get some DS clickies or just get really really lucky, don't expect to beat him until you get some /discs to pull out.
