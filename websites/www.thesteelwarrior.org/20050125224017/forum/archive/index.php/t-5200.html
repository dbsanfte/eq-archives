well courtesy of mudflation which causes weapons that used to be rare drops coveted by high level warriors to now be bazaar / twink trash there isnt really anything weapons farmable at your level that you are likely to want to use. Once you've saved up a couple of hundred plat you should be able to afford a few decent 1 handers for your level - centi shortsword, jagged blade of mourning, lammentation etc all go pretty cheap these days.
