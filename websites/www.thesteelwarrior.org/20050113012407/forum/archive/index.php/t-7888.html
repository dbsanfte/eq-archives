Somewhat redundantly, since others have answered the same way, I'd be slightly inclined to go with the 2h option. Also, if I had a decent 2h dps weapon already, then before I spent fantastic amounts of plat on upgrading it to a slightly higher dps weapon, I'd take a long hard look at whether or not I could use that plat to improve my armor. This is especially true if the whole reason I'm looking at dps is that I'm not one of the top five tanks on the raid. Better dps is not going to get you into the top five tanks in your guild. Better armor and aggro weapons will do much more in that direction, so I'd really put more resources into that.

Of course it's a very good idea to have a legitimate non-aggro dps set up. Bad things can happen if you have an aggro set up for dps and assume that because the MT has a "better" aggro set up you're okay: the rng will give you some "lucky" procs at some point and you'll gank the MT's aggro at the wrong time.

EQ is 5ish years old and there are lots of servers and people raiding 24/7. So somewhere, at some point, it's possible that the dps output of the 7th tank on a raid saved the raid. It's also possible that you will be stung by a bee while you are playing EQ, and have a fatal allergic reaction but that before the bee venom can kill you, lightening will strike you dead.

Sometimes when you are not the main tank you have to get in front of the mob anyway. If you are using DW, and the mob ripostes, then at that point you are higher maintence than if you are using a 2-hander. If you already own three weapons (a dps DW combo and a dps 2Hander) then as frostpaw pointed out, you can sell the two DW weapons.
