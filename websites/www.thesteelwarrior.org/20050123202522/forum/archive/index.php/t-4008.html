You want to try and add +HP/AC/sta items to increase your tanking ability. The 6AC 65HP rings I have seen as cheap as 350 on Povar, your server prices may vary. Crystal Spider Eyes for face are 8AC and 35HP, have seen for 350 as well. Check out even the lowest level plate class armors in Butcherblock camp. If you're doing a lot of LDoN you might save up for a piece or two if they are Iksar useable as well. Skull Shaped Barbute for head is nice for quite awhile, about 100PP, but again, no idea if Iksar wearable or not. The Barbed Ringmail would be a nice upgrade for you as well. I wore mine until level 55 when I got lucky on a Moktor BP.

best thing though. hit the baz. at this lvl you are looking for stats as shammys are not maxing you out.

most important things to you at this point should be sta, followed by raw hp, then dex.

what I did when I had 1k or 2k was hit the baz. put in class race and max price would be 1/4 of the money i had. sta would be the limit to stat.

arrange by stat and start working your way down the list.
