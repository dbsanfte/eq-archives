Ratio is the primary factor for offhand DPS, since you get no damage bonus for your off hand. For DPS purposes, mainhand lammy/offhand axe. For aggro purposes, the more procs you get, the better off you'll be, so you might try swapping them and seeing how you do. Incedentally, I still use a veno axe offhand alot of the time, it's decent dps and the proc is pretty good aggro. Nowhere near the best out there, but I'm short of funds most of the time, so I'm still using it at 57 and don't have too many problems unless my groupmates lose their minds and go full burn. (or if they're knights with little man complex) :) Oh, and the lammy isnt a terrible offhand weapon, it used to be fairly common to see high level warriors dual wielding lammy's. It's just not nearly as good as gear that drops 4 expansions later. but I remember dual wielding langseaxes at level 30, so my opinion is somewhat biased. :)
