Khabok
There are some great guides around to Plane of Sky. Aadawens (http://members.cohesive.net/~rtaylor/EQ/skyguide/) is one Catranizi's (http://www.geocities.com/lordhong/sky.htm) is another.

Using a Bridge requires a class that can summon corpses, Necro or SK with them being able to use the small coffins as a bonus, and someone who can Rez, camped out in a non aggro spot on one of the islands. The general strategy used is to log them in, port the raid to Isle 1, use an Eye to get a target on the Isle they are on somehow and group them with someone on Isle 1. Or alternate is to have them duel, cleric kill Necro, wizard meet necro at his bind, group together, then cleric rezzes necro back.

Now ya have the bridge. Next you invite folks to the bridge group one at a time, have them attack the Key master on Isle one and die. Dueling on first Isle can cause wierd aggro sometimes, and duel corpses have a real short re rez time. Then they get rezzed back to Isle 1, corpse gets summoned, get a second rez and they are there. Do the whole raid that way, being sure to leave 2 clerics on Isle one till last so they can rez each other back, and ya just saved a day or two of raiding the way there.
