My favorites:

1) The Sword of Ssraeshza

This is, hands down, the best looking sword in the game. Everyone knows the look of this weapon, and if I could really describe it to you in case you haven't, you would probably recognize it in the back of your head 'cause chances are, you've seen it once before while roaming around PoK.

2) Gaudralek, Sword of the Sky

This is also the same graphic for the Tunare knight only sword. Its looks are very similiar to the Sword of the Bloodsworn, but it's longer and in a two-handed treat. It has a gem embedded in the pommel of the blade. Easily recognizable and doesn't share graphics with too many other weapons. It's rather rare too since it's Warrior only and drops uncommonly off one of the hardest dragons in NTOV (Lord Vyemm). I always wanted one. =(

3) Ornate Greatblade

Just as the name describes, it's ornate! It's a beautifully rendered sword, and I'm sure everyone here has seen it at least once since the damn thing drops so commonly. It's also a damn fine weapon with good stats and a solid ratio. You'll probably find this sword on a Paladin or Shadowknight more commonly, but don't be suprised to see Rangers and Warriors wielding it too.

Other runners up:
-Bloodfrenzy / Blasphemous Blade of the Exiled / Tae Ew Longsword
-Obsidian Scimitar of War
-Edge of Eternity / Gold Rune Mithril Knife
-Vanizier's

Worst Graphics:
-Greater Stormwatcher's Horn
-Split Blade of Destruction
-Anything from fucking GoD

I never liked the Blade of War when I first saw it. I thought it looked freaking awful. I had to get the graphic to grow on me and now I think it looks pretty good. Still, there is room for improvement.
