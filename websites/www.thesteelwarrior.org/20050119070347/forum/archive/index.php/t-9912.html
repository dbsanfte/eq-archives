Well, I think you have a couple of choices.
First, if you're new to the game, or even new to melee classes, i'd do at least a few newbie armor quests. the armor's okay until lvl 20ish, but better than that you'll start to get a feel for the advantages and disadvantages of the class.
The other thing to do is get some crustacean armor, which is good/standard gear for warriors and will last a good while. Look for those pieces (couple hundred plat per item, I would think) in the bazaar, and then do various searches by class/race/diety to see what else is out there for a similar price. Use crafted/crusty as a baseline, but get the best bang for the buck. Sometimes there's frosted or sandblasted armor around too that is roughly equivalent, but make sure you're buying a size that will fit you.
Look for AC, STA & hitpoints in your armor. Other stats will come later, but these are always good things to have.
The Centi short sword is a pretty good warrior weapon early on & none of the centi stuff has level restrictions, as far as I know.
If you can find a bow with 150 or better range, that will do you for a good long time (basically until you start looking for stats in the range slot).
Don't know what your capacity to raise plat is (on other characters) but if it's limited, this stuff will give you a decent start and should be in your stated budget. And, even if you have tons of potential plat, this stuff will allow you to do your job as a warrior for 30 levels or so, and by that time you'll hav fallen into some upgrades.
Also note it's been a while since I leveled a new warrior, and my advice is free, and as with so many things, you're kinda gettin what ya pay for here, heh.
