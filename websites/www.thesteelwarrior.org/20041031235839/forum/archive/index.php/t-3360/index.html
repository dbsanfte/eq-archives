Gellio,

Ahh, the days of doing Naggy and Vox. Vox was a total nightmare for me, think I lost over a level back in the day for a single raid and we didn't even get her. Damn those pickup raids!!

Good resist items:

slot - item name - resists
=========================================
range - stone of mordid - large races only?, 20fr/cr!!
face - golden diamond mask - 10 fr/cr
neck - golden blue diamond pendant - 11fr/cr
shoulder - imbued granite spaulders (can be expensive) - 10fr/cr
ear - blue diamond electrum earring - 6fr/cr
head - platinum blue diamond tiara - 8fr/cr
finger - velium diamond wedding ring - 8fr/cr
wrist - velium blue diamond bracelet - 9fr/cr

Those are some items I used back 'in the day'. They're pretty ghetto these days, with better items to be had. They're kind of old school, in the sense that you had guild jewelers cranking this stuff out as doing Naggy / Vox was a big deal. You should be able to pick those up pretty cheap.

You didn't mention money, so I assumed you're semi-poor as I was back then. If you have a bigger budget, think about getting items that will last you a lot longer (pay attention to rec/req levels though)

cazicite jewelry items - much better than above, I'm uncertain on cost though, assume semi-expensive. All recommended level 51. Roughly 8 or 10 to fr/cr per item!
ring - valorium rings of galantry - 5fr/cr (req:46 rec:60)
ear - electrified earring of tempests - 12fr/cr (req:46 rec:60)

You may find at 46 that they're not worth the cost. I'd definately think about hitting 51/52 before doing a Naggy/Vox raid, you may find it hard to even get into those raids at 46. The reason being that you're going to get chomped up by the AoEs on those mobs. I did manage to go at 46, but only because I was a wizard and they couldn't get many wizzies at the time to go along for ports. I was not really able to cast on Naggy at that level, so it was pretty pointless for me to be there other than transportation.

Good luck, may the loot be plentiful,
