Redhenna
Neck, glimmering mithril torque: ac 15, str 2...100pp

Finger, Platinum or velium fire wedding rings: AC 5 or 6, hp's 55 or 65, 200 to 400 pp

Wrist, bracer of scavenging: Ac 9 Dex STA 5 200pp

Weapons: Centi SS, Shaded blade, smouldering brand and cane of the tranqual can all be had at resonable prices in the bazaar.

To be honest, for your level you are doing very well(much <3 for filth covered boots, I camped them at level 15 and wore them till I was well into my 40's...first peice of gear I ever camped). As far as buying gear upgrades, bazaar search is your friend. Look for gear with more AC, more STA and more hp's than you currently have, and if it is affordable, there you go. Do not worry too much about getting constant upgrades. If you are not getting beaten down badly, put upgrades off till you have more money, and can afford an upgrade that will last longer.
