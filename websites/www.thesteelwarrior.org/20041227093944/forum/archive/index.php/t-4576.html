Not sure if the Lucy ones are base re-use and it still gets lower, or if they stay the same. Sounding like they stay the same but I'm comparing to base.

Rogue:
Duelist listed as 2 ticks now. That would be 12 seconds, up from the current 9.
Blinding Speed 3 ticks, so gains 3 seconds. Reuse down to 25 from 30.
Kinesthetics reuse down to 25 from 30.
Deadeye 3 ticks, gain 4 seconds. Reuse 25 from 30
Counterattack 2 ticks, gain 3 seconds. 45 min, down from 60

Monk:
100 Fist 3tick, gain 3 second.
Inner Flame 2 ticks, gain 3 seconds. I think.

Whirlwind listed same as Inner Flame right now, bug I assume.
Stone Stance reuse listed as down to 4 minutes. 8 faster than normal base, but 1 *slower* than level 60+ reuse.

Warrior:
Evasive Reuse 10.5 min, 4.5 faster than base, 4.5 slower than 60+ reuse.
Charge 3 ticks, gain 4 seconds.
Mighty Strike 2 ticks, gain 2-3 seconds. Reuse down to 30 from 60.
Defensive Reuse to 10.5. 4.5 faster than base. .5 slower than 60+ reuse.
Furious 2 ticks, gain 3 seconds. Reuse down to 45 from 60.
Fell Strike 2 ticks. Gain 3 sec? Not sure.
