It's a fun zone but certainly a bit different. Here's an old map & guide to HS (http://cloud.prohosting.com/~talone/Charasis.htm), from before the revamp, but still mostly accurate.

Briefly: There are four wings (north/west/south/east, in that order) and a basement. The four wings are locked, and the east stairs are illusionary. Each wing contains the key for the next wing; the first key is dropped in the basement. If you travel all four wings then you'll find items to make a permanent Master Key for all of the doors. Portal pads in basement and first two wings take you back to zone in. Last two wings' portal pads take you out of the zone. Here's the progression:

1. Drop to basement and kill bottomless feasters until you loot a "tallow-marked candle" (key to north wing). Use portal in center of basement to go back to zone in. Use candle to unlock north door.
3. While killing in north wing, pick up ground spawn "Sacrificial Robe Clasp" (key to boss in north wing) and "Dismembered Finger" (part of the master key, loc = +331, +207)
4. At end of north wing, kill "Crypt Keeper" and loot "Sigil of the Calendar" (key to west wing). Take portal back to zone in. Use sigil to unlock west wing.
5. While killing in west wing, pick up ground spawn "Skull engraved coin" (key to boss in west wing) and "Dismembered Thumb" (second piece of the master key, loc = -143, +623)
6. At end of west wing, kill "Spectre Sepulcher" and loot "Broken Sacraficial Blade" (key to south wing). Take portal back to zone in. Use blade to unlock south wing.
7. While killing in south wing, pick up ground spawn "Coin Depicting Innoruuk" (key to boss in south wing) and "Mutilated Forearm" (3rd piece of master key, loc = -567, +152)
8. At end of south wing, kill boss mob. Portal pad is zone out. BUT, there's a fake wall in the same room, which leads to a secret passage to the east wing. Head through secret passage to east wing.
9. While killing in east wing, pick up ground spawn "Blood Encrusted Glove" (4th piece of master key, loc = +313, -567, combine other parts in glove to make "Hand of Glory" so you never have to pick up keys again).
10. Kill Drusilla Sathir if up; zone out is behind her room.
