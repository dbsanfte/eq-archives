The issue is not so much never having to depend on the RNG, but more of what are acceptable odds.

If the odds of a knight fizzling or failing to channel are small enough that the knight can have acceptable agro from this, at what point do the odds of getting a weapon proc within the first 10 seconds, combined with the odds of you having a successful taunt after slow lands become acceptable.

If for example a BoC procs on average 2x per minute and adding a 2nd proc gives you 4 procs per minute would 1 proc every 15 seconds be acceptable odds against the RNG? How about your droga combo with a 2nd proc on each, assuming 2 procs per minute from each proc with 4 procs you now have 8 procs per minute. Pretty decent odds to get some sort of hate proc every 10 seconds, is that acceptable odds ?
