At 31, I would suggest, if you can get the people together to do it, exploring dungeons. Dalnir was my big dungeon at that level. Exciting, challenging and great exp. You could look at SolA(think there is still good exp there at 31), Kaesora(need an evacer for it) or probably even Splitpaw. The nice thing about all those places is that exp is good, cash drops are good, and the named drop things that on Stromm might still be worth something. Another good zone at that level is Great Divide. Eventually you will be most likely wanting to get Thurg quest armor, and to get yout Thurg(Coldain) faction up, you can hunt drakkel wolves and, carefully at first, giants. There are a number of other good zones at your level, and most importantly, I recommend you take some time and explore a bit. I think I had more fun in my 30's than any other time ingame.
