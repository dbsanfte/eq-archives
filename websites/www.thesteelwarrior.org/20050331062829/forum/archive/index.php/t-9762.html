shielding % = 40 HP is a low but reasonable estimation, reasoning being stuff that can really kill you tends to have DB in the 1000 range, and you should take 4 hits before you die (if not you shouldn't be tanking it in the first place), so you get 40 HPs for each shielding. The actual savings of shielding is DB X % X # of hits you expected to take on the given mob before you die and it varies a lot depending on the mob.

Well basically to figure out what shielding is worth, you get:

DB X shielding % X average number of hits you expect to take before you die.

1000 and 4 is a reasonable estimate on most bosses. Shielding is still useful on trash because although their DB is lower they also need a lot more hits to kill you. However it's a lot harder to estimate how many hits you're expected to take when it's greater than 4.
