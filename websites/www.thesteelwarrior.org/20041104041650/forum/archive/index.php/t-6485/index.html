Sakryn
Which one do you get?
Here's how to determine what sparklie you will get:

Hit /played and look at your character's birthdate. Now check where it falls...

1999 - Nagafen's Fire
2000 - Trakanon's Tail
2001- Tormax Candle
2002 - Solusek Sparkler
2003 - Miragul Rocket

Also note that the years apparently go from March to March of the next year, not Jan - December. For example, a character born January of 2000 would get Nagafen's Fire, and not Trakanon's Tail. Hope that makes sense!
