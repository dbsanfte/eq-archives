Rolandal is the white dragon in Wakenling Lands that sits approximately the mid point between Kael Drakkel and Skyshrine zoneline along the zone wall. If you are coming out from kael you would keep your right shoulder to the wall and follow it till you saw the dragon, and reverse (left shoulder) from skyshrine.

As for not being up, there is only one time since the dragon was added that I have ran by without seeing him up. That was when the resident uber guild wanted to kill him to check for drops, once it was established it dropped nothing I have seen it there everytime.

One more possible reason it wouldn't be up when you ran by, I remember awhile back an enchanter using Rol to "solo" Wuoshi using charm fighting. Didn't follow the thread through to see if it was nerfed or not but it is possible if it was not nerfed that a charmer is playing around and killing off Rolandal.
