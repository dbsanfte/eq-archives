I would disagree that 1h weapons are still better for agro. They are better in some cases, but only because the good 2h weapons are out of reach. I see the evolution of a warriors weapons currently as 4 stages:

Stage 1
Dual Wield Frosty + another inexpensive weapon - for most warriors, Frostbringer is the first real agro weapon they get. This is a great weapon and serves its purpose very well unril you can move up to the next level.

Stage 2
2hb Greatstaff of Thunder - This is one of 3 weapons that has killed DW. Unless your guild is capable of getting you dual EB weapons then this is THE weapon to have for agro. Not only is it better agro than all but the very high end 1h weapons.

Stage 3
2x Enraging Blow - Once your guild is capable of getting you dual EB weapons, then you can reliably hold agro better than a GSoT. In most cases, you are sacrificing DPS for better agro because you can do better DPS with a decent 2h over very good 1h combos.

Stage 4
Blade of War - This is the other weapon that has killed DW. Once a warrior gets a BoW, the have no reason to dual weild again. There may be some arguement for using 1h + shield on some mobs that hit particularly hard, but that becomes a moot point as you gear up in the elemental planes and eventually get to the point where the extra AC is not needed. In the majority of cases though, the BoW is superior to DW for agro and DPS.

We may see the emergence of a 5th stage with weapons that come out of the Plane of Time, but at this stage in the game, it's pretty hard to beat the BoW for agro or DPS with a DW set up.
