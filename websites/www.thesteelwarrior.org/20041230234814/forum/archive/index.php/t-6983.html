*Aggrowhore begins to use Feysboil as a living shield!* and *Feysboil ceases protecting Aggrowhore* are under Filters > Melee > Your Hits (/boggle).

*You must first target a living PC Character*, *Your target is too far away, get closer!* (and any others) are under Filters > Skills.

*You may use the abilite /shield again in ... * is under Filters > Melee > Disciplines.
