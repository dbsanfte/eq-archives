Those weapons are quite sufficient for your level. They are far superior to what many warriors including myself had at lvl 40. As far as the PK'ing I don't have much advice as every class is different in fighting and require different tactics. Some you want to joust, hit with a big 2 hander and move away until the delay refreshes. For casters, the 1 handers would probably be better to help interupt their spells unless they stack a big damage shield on them. You will just have to learn which classes to use which weapons against.
