09-30-2003, 11:01 AM
To put it another way:

Type:All
This is on augments. This means you can put the augment on any item with a free augment slot, irrespective of type

Type:1
This is on items. This means you can only fit Type:1 or Type:all augments into this slot. Since there are no Type:1 augments, this effectively means you can only fit Type:All augments into items with Type:1 only slots.
