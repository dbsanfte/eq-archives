I wish there were a variety of weapons that were similar to those two, so that warriors were more unique in the weapons they use, but the reality is those two weapons are the best single groupable weapons you can get. There are some decent drops in BoT, the disemboweler is essentially a 15/20 (+3 magic dmg), which makes it a better ratio than the SotB or CHoS, but it has no proc, which makes it inferior aggro. The wind-worn handblade is also a better ratio at 12/18 and has a proc, but the proc is a dd and therefore is not good aggro. However, if you really want to be different you could get one of those two weapons and anger aug them, but.... they are also 10-20k bazaar weapons, just not quite as common. I saw a 16/20 recently, gemmed velium sword or some such, but no idea where it comes from... that is a very nice ratio and with an anger proc would rock, but no idea where it comes from.
