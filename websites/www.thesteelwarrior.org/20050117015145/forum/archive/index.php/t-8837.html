I get a kick out if it when I hear people having thier healers log out before they engage each boss, that probably makes things harder. Perma rooted mobs act differenty than PC rooted mobs in that they wont attack anyone other than the person at the top of hate list.

So say your cleric has agro on the next flesh hunter from the previous one you killed. No problem, just move in the room of the one you just killed and have anyone you dont want to get hit hide in the corner, might take a DD spell here and there but in no danger whatsoever. Med up, wait for disc or whatever you gotta do to prepare for the next hunter in the corner. When you are set, have the tank go in the room of the next hunter and literally stand in front of it, its still agro on the cleric and wont attack anyone else. You can either sit there and taunt till a successful one or go ahead and ae taunt it. When it starts attacking you from a successful taunt it is certain no one will get summoned unless the tank sucks at holding agro. Repeat this process for the next and you should find it to be the easier of the 3 single groupable trials.

It sounds like you went ahead and started attacking it when you cleric had a ton of agro and the moment you got it to 96% it summoned him/her.
