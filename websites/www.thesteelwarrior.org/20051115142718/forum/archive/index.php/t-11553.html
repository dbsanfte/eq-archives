For tanking Sta, AC and +Defense are your 3 primary stats. Draconian Deflector is barely better in AC but clearing better in Sta and +Defense, so it is the better sheild. +Defense is a fairly important stat for end game tanking because it reduces the crit against you(while also increasing your Dodge%, Parry%, and Block%). Damage spikes are what usally kill tanks, so keeping the crit rate against you low is important.
