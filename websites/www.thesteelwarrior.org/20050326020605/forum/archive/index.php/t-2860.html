Legionnare's in Katta down by the bank area. You could probably single pull them, and the shaman can definately land a slow on them.

If those are too easy, move upstairs to the east tower (the one with warrior, cleric, and a few other trainers) and do the Centurions. Shammy should be able to slow them because they don't have a high MR but they do summon. They're level 52-54 with 12 minute respawn timers. They drop VC armor and weapons that sells for like 4-7 pp each as well as dropping 2-9 plat / kill. They don't run. They're melee so there's no spells or healing or anything you have to worry about.

Myself (65 warrior) and 53 shammy duo'd there and we could do 6 Centurions before the first would respawn. (the 2 guarding the door, 2 inside, and 2 by the steps). I'm pretty sure you 2 could do just the 2 inside and leave the other 4 alone. You can sell the stuff in the building across the street, and the bank is just down below. There are faction hits but very minimal. I went in Katta as amiable, killed for about 6 hours and left as indifferent. Shammy was dubious and still is dubious.
