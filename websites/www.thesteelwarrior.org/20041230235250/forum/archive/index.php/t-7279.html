On the 1H blunt question.
If your guild has a few folks in their 50s who are willing to help you, you could get them to fight at the entrance of Maiden's Eye from the Overthere zone-in. Mobs there drop the Gorgana spiked club, 12 dam, 23 delay, AC 5, STR 2. It's no drop, so you have to go with them and loot the thing, which means being careful going through OT and being very careful around the zone-in once your are in ME.
Even without guild help, you can travel there and /ooc asking politely if anyone who is letting the weapon rot would mind if you looted it.
It's not a quest, obviously, but weapon should be free and there is some risk in getting there.
