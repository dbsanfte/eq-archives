On this day, I'd like everyone to raise a mug to the EverQuest teams of yesteryear, and today, who work so hard every day to bring us the most phenomenal computer gaming experience ever created.

I know its always been 'popular' and 'cool' to flame 989, and then Verant, and finally SOE, for everything you dislike about this game. But think about it. They've done a phenomenal job over the past 5 years keeping this game fresh, new, balanced, phenomenally more cheat-free than almost all games out there, and usually just never lets our efforts get marginalized.

This is a wonderful game and has given me and many others 5 years of enjoyment, and I have never regretted it for a moment. The vocal minority has for too long been allowed to complain about every trivial problem with this game, and use it as an excuse for saying this game is worthless, and personally hate the developers of this game.

No more. On the fifth anniversary of EverQuest, let us put down the complaints and simply revel in the good times we've had, the good times we will have, and the cheap as hell entertainment we've had. The 200+ amazing zones that vary so much its staggering, to the dozens of truly rewarding and enjoyable quests, and to the fantastic job of keeping this one of the most stable games ever made, and was and always has been a true marvel in coding. Thank you.
