I don't think splitting the ATK number up would do anything for us at this point. Unless I'm missing something the only factors involved in your chance to hit something are Level, offensive skill, and weapon skill. So once you cap your skills at level 65 you have the same chance to hit something with a 3000 atk as you do with a 500 atk. You'll just hit it a lot harder with the 3000 atk. I seem to remember someone showing that through parses (identical hit percentage at all levels of atk) quite a while ago but I could be wrong.

To my understanding AC pretty much behaves the same way. Your chance to be hit is related to level and defensive skills. AC doesn't matter. The AC just reduces the amount you'll be hit for if you ARE hit.

So while ATK and AC may both have separate mods to hit and damage, once our level and skills are capped we can only affect the damage mods so the accuracy component is basically the same for everyone of the same level with their skills maxed.
