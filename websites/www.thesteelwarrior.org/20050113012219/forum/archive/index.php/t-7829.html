It looks like the helm would be the best straight upgrade. More ac, more sta, more hp than any of the other choices. Also, the helm can take a 36% haste augment, allowing you to upgrade your gloves in the future.

As for 2 pieces of armor vs 1 piece of fully augmented armor... It's kind of a toss up. I prefer super armors that are basically not replacable. If you don't think it's very likely at all for you to upgrade, however, just consider your gain vs points spent. Take it to a formula; that is, I'd say 1ac = 1 sta = 6hp = 1 upgrade point until sta capped, then I would consider the LDoN points spent for each upgrade point with the various armors vs that spent per augment. You'll probably find that more armor is better than the augments.
