Raunstonecutter
If your guild can hit the BoT giants and their ilk, I'd suggest you go pimp of Xanomech. I am a huge proponet of killing him a lot early. Once you get him down, you can farm him until the cows come home. 41% haste and a taunting blow weapon would be helpful.

A really good set up.
