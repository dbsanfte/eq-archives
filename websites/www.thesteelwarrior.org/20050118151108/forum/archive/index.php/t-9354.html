You can obtain 10AC, 40HP, or 20HP/20Mana/20End augments for a number of slots in LDoN. In total you would add roughly 40AC and 200HP's.

Working on your AA abilities would also add significantly to your HP pool and resiliance. I suggest trying to obtain CS3, CA3, ND3, PE, AT, PP5, PD3, LR5, ID5 and Sturdyness 5.

There are two or three better back slot items in the bazaar that are modestly priced.

In many slots, Magnetic armor while currently IMO overpriced is a good upgrade.

Since you ask, "where I can group in order to aquire some decent drops", I'll try to answer - Bastion of Thunder for Kizrak's armor, LDoN for AP earned augments and possibly a BP, a few of the earlier GoD zones - Frodlin provides an overview in a few posts here, and eventually Wall of Slaughter in OoW.
