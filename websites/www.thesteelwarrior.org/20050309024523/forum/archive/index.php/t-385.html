If you want a soloable fighter, you can't beat a Paladin.

Not only is it an excellent melee class, able to wear most of what a warrior can wear, but the combination of healing spells, root, lull/soothe line of spells, AC and HP buffs and of course the stunning Lay-on-Hands, it makes the Pally a great soloable class indeed.

Get a Paladin KEI'd for the heals and you can spend an easy 3 hours soloing to your hearts content with no downtime.

I was in Splitpaw soloing with my 33 Pally (KEI'd) last night and in 3 hours I went up 2 levels and amassed over 200pp in loot and platinum. No downtime, no deaths.

And if you want a great Pally weapon, check out the 1-hander Massive Dragonclaw Shard - 28 dmg, 30 dly. Combine that with a decent shield like the Sarnak Battle Shield, and a haste item like Silver Chitin Handwraps, and I can even solo red cons.
