Uh ... hmmmm ... I think what I was trying to say is that TRADABLE gear isn't much of an UPGRADE for you anymore.

Look at levels so you can start getting the no drop upgrades.

Some tradable gear that's an upgrade for that magelo:

1) Helm of Rallos Zek (way too much money, but hey, it's tradable)

2) Sceptre of Destruction or Blade of Carnage (again, way too much money)

3) Idol of the Wavecrasher

4) Heavy Rubicite Girdle

5) Amygdalan Chain Mask

6) 9th Ring MQ

7) Cobalt Greaves (even if they stay in your bag, the utility is there ... but actually they stack up well against the FINE heraldic armor)

8) An invigorate BP

9) Anizok's Gauze Press, with collapsible toolkit and some mana batteries

10) Truncheon of Doom

11) CAMP stormfeather, get a new mask if Amygdalan Chain Mask doesn't excite, and you don't want to level

That's pretty much all I can think of.

Maybe look for resist gear too.

In any event you can take the gear you're currently in and ding 65 in it and still be rather well off.
