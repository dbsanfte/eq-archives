I know I may well get flamed for this, but IMO pure hate isn't as good as its cracked up to be. I use Blade of Carnage, Sceptre of Destruction, and Great Blade of Storms as my aggro weapons. I also have epics, and used them for many months, though I have them banked now.

While I can't speak for CHoS/SotBS directly, SoD's anarchy proc was definetly more aggro than Rage of Vallon. Stuns + DDs are definetly big aggro, and so is +hate, but it's not like you can get one hate proc, and turn off attack and go afk.

As for SoD and GBoS vs BoC, I really don't notice much difference in the aggro per proc or aggro per fight between the weapons (this is where I'm going to get flamed I'm sure). In fact I usually use SoD/GBoS when exp camping. I hold aggro quite well, though of course not perfectly and there are still occassional aggro problems. However, if I put my BoC in it's not a magic cure all. I can still have problems at times with it too.

There is one situation where BoC really shines over SoD or GBoS though, and that would be on very resistant mobs (Seru and Emperor are the best examples of mobs I fight now). Resists still generate aggro, though they don't seem to generate as much. Enraging blow can't be resisted though, of course.

Anyways overall my point is that pure hate is not always clearly better than a stun (or debuff I suppose for that matter). And even though I haven't used CHoS/SotBS, I would be willing to bet that they're both better than the red epic, in the main hand at very least (24 delay is just too slow for a main hand weapon)
