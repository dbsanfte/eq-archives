Beyond the three things you listed you're pretty much limited to a couple of things. Vengeance items will raise attack, but if you mean affordable most are not. You can however purchase a bunch of them now with the release of LDoN. Certain spells will increase attack, most notably ranger's Call of the Predator. Spirit of Bih' Li will also raise attack by 10 I believe if you are looking for a low lvl spell. Correct me if I'm wrong (I'm sure someone will :) )but i *think* some of the high level enchanter hastes contain an increase to attack. From there you begin to look at other items with clicky effects. Shrunken Goblin Skull Earring has the Grim Aura clicky effect. I believe the Eyepatch of Plunder clicky also contains an attack buffer in it. Check in the library, I'm sure there is a list or two of items with useful clickies, you might find some more there. And I can't think of any AA's off the top of my head that actually increase the "attack rating, " though of course there are offensive ones that will increase overall DPS.

Like Khaz said the Ranger spell line that starts with Mark of the Predator has a +20 attack component. Call of the Predator Ranger level 60 is +40 Attack. Strength of Tunare Ranger 62 is +60 Attack. Spirit of Predator Ranger 64 is +70 Attack. Spirit of Bih'li, Shaman level 39 is +15 attack and upgraded SoW. Avatar and Primal Avatar, both Shaman 60 are +100 Attackand some melee stats Ferine Avatar Shaman 65 is +140 Attack Spiritual Vigor, Beastlord 62 has +40 attackand +225 HP and does not stack with Brells line . Ferocity, Beastlord 64 is +150 attack +40 Stamina and +65 to all saves. Boon of the Garou from Enchanters level 44 is +30 attack, Night's Dark Terror, Enchanter 63 is +45 attack and scarecrow illusion. Not all of these stack with each other though I don't believe, and like the Paladin and Beastlord lines, some have stacking issues with other spells

edit: Beastlords also get Spiritual Brawn at level 49 +10 attack and +75 HP, and Spiritual Strength at level 60 +20 attack and +150 HP
