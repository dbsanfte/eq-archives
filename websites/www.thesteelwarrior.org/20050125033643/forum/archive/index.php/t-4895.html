Looks like a good start, and let me start by saying I hope you enjoy the warrior class, which should seem refreshing after being a finger-wriggler.

Everything Ghar pointed out is sound advice, and I actually typed out about 90% of what he said before I read what he posted and realized it was already said, so there's some stuff to look for.

One other comment would be to look for something like Grey Flesh sleeves, or any others with decent ac, as well as stam/hp. I never was much of a fan for cobalt; having nothing but ac on an item just seems like a waste of a slot to me.

Other than the suggestions Ghar gave you, I'd just say to level up, and look for thurg/SS armor once you get another 10 levels or so, and then maybe grab a few cheap pieces of ornate armor after level 55. Or you could just skip both steps and save up for the LDoN plate armor, which will last you until the e-planes easily.

Happy twinking,
