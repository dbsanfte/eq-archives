04-14-2004, 06:07 PM
People go for LR5 first over ID5 because parses show that you take less damage with LR5 overall than if you had ID5. The benefit ID5 has going for it however is less/smaller damage spikes. (Think /disc def versus /disc evasive- with higher mitigation ie id5 and /disc def, you get smoother damage. With avoidance ie lr5 and /disc evasive, you still get the spiky rounds of max hits on each swing) Both of them are important AAs to get, so as long as you get them both eventually, you're golden.
