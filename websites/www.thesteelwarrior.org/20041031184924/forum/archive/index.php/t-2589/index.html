Neksnappa
Once you get dragon-allied, do the bracer of benevolence bracer quest twice, and the draconic pauldrons. These will be pretty significant hp/stam upgrades, and wont cost you a penny. Only takes a few people to do each of them as well.

Fierce Heraldic Greaves, Boots, arms, gloves are the best pieces for you, they'll set you back 4-8kpp or so each though. Valorium Rings of Gallantry are nice, with lots of stats, but again, they're 4-5k each.

You can MQ an Earring of the Solstice for around 4k, and check the bazaar for another cheap earring with decent hp on it. Same deal with a mask, just look for anything with decent ac along with stam/hp that's not too expensive. It is very very easy to find cheap upgrades in the bazaar, just spend a few days there getting to know the market well, and grab any upgrades if you see them cheap.

Those upgrades should hold you until you get some better ntov or PoP drops.

I made it to 65 and still had some Cobalt on me so you'll be ok eventually. The weapon drops in Droga are nice, but you are in good shape with a GoT. Also the Go4W off Auliffe in BoT is a nice, nice dps weapon (holds aggro pretty well too).

For armor, hope to get lucky on Ornate or get cultural. Since you are an RZ worshipper you have access to human and ogre cultural which is good. A Vindi BP would be a nice upgrade and is not hard to get. The Va'Dyn in Akheva is real easy and should be well within 30 60+ people's range, he drops a 100hp neck. Iron Scream earring off the Terror Matriarch in PoN and the earring off Hreidar (lower mini in west wing BoT) are good options, as is the Solstice. I'd leave the 6/65 rings for now, Valorium is nice but you have bigger needs than that. Upgrade your face if you can, there should be a few cheap masks in the bazaar.
