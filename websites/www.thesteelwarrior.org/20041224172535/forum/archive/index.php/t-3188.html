Another thing to keep in mind about SU is that it's minimum is much higher than a normal min hit. The mobs that you see what seem to be outrageous damage from paladins are generally against very high AC luclin mobs with holy forge being used. SU is going to be a much bigger increase on mobs with very high AC if the guild involved doesn't have much in the way of atk gear. As +atk gear becomes more prevalent in the guild, the jump that paladins get from SU becomes smaller and smaller relative to the other classes.

In the situation where a lower tier guild is fighting an undead boss mob in luclin (all 2 of them?) with very high AC, Slay Undead gives a much larger percentage increase than it should.

For those who see the comments that "SU isn't a very big increase in damage" and "it is a very significant increase in damage" and wonder which one is true...they both are. For a paladin, that 20% increase is massive. Compared to the DPS of just about any other class, the amount of increased damage is relatively insignificant. A paladin doing 50 DPS with windblade now does 60 DPS against undead. That 10 DPS improvement has to be taken in context.
