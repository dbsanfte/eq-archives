Can't say I agree. Extra STR means higher ATK (I think), and more damage per hit, on average, over time (I'm sure). After HP and AC, STR is the most important stat for a warrior.

Haste is more important than STR, and that's what the debate is about. The amount of damage gained by improving your strength will not offset the amount of damage you'd lose by choosing Strength over Haste. Choosing a 15str belt over something even as simple as a Flowing Black Silk Sash is the wrong decision if you want DPS.

Will Strength increase your DPS? Sure. Marginally. It's not a big enough difference to worry about. Your max hit will be a couple points higher, but unless you have massive ATK gear, you won't be hitting for max very often anyway. Get a strength buff from your shaman (+67) and you'll be high enough to not have to be concerned with it. Maxing your strength at the expense of HP, AC, or Haste is not the way to be productive with your gear.
