(Note: this is Enderwere posting, it won't let me load that character because Tichondrius is down apparently.)

1) Thunderclap is pretty nice now at level 60. Its 30 second duration so lasts the entire fight. Its base damage is I believe 108 but its modified by AC so it does less than that, it was hitting the mobs I fought for low 60's but since I was fighting groups of 3 the 30 second duration made it worth it. The real test is going to be whether or not it works on raid mobs.

2) Heroic Strike rank 8 was listed at 138 damage, still sounds anemic.

3) Battle Shout reduced to 10 rage, thank you blizzard.

4) I didn't get to test long before servers crashed but I was not missing much at all against the lvl 56/57 mobs I was fighting. The fights ended with about the same health as they used to though, the changes didn't seem to make a large difference. This is from a small sample size of testing though.

5) The first thorium mine i hit had an arcane crystal, not sure what that says about mining but it pissed me off~.

6) Blood rage did around 330 dmg to me when I had just under 4700 hps unbuffed.

Heroic Strike improvement wasn't really impressive :( (will take a while before I use that skill ever again)
No words on the rage issue yet, but overall nothing that will balance warriors imo.
