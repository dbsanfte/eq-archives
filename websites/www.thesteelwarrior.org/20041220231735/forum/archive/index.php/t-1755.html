People may think its overpowering if warriors can suddenly replace slowers.

With such a skill,
Warriors can become powerfull if time is devoted into them.


Controlling a warriors proc would fix many many things:

- Fix the aggro nicely, without stepping on Knight's toes
The proc can be used for snap aggro, or taking advance in aggro after a taunt.
But only every 12 seconds, thus still giving wizards a limit they wouldnt have with knights.

- Make Warriors a lot more appealing.
Warriors with the correct equipment could become very appealing for groups. Replace unavailable slowers, snarers.
As well as giving warriors a possibility of soloing decently.. though still being the worse class at such a thing :p

- Give warriors a bit of needed Versatility
Dont tell me you all love spending 4 hours in an exp group pressing just Kick and Taunt.
It can get very mind numbing very fast.
Having warriors choose between snaring, reslowing, getting more aggro etc... Can make things a bit more exciting for warriors.

- Giving a Lifting to a few old world items
Quite a few weapons already existing out there will have a sudden new meaning to their procs.
Getting a few stacking dot weapons etc...

- Allowing for more easy fixes in the futur
Its a lot more easier to fix spells for SOE, then fixing skills etc...
Reason we're seeing so many new spells while melees are still in the dark.
With such an ability, adding in certain weapons will be nice fixes and upgrades to warriors.
