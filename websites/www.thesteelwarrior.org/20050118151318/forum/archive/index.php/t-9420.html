I have no idea what your budget is, but it appears that you fell into the same trap that I did when I first started out.

Strength is not the best thing to work on. Yes, it helps, but as a warrior, your primary responsibilities are survivability and aggro generation.

The formula for deciding what gear is best for a warrior to use (as has been posted on this board repeatedly, I'm just regurgitating it =) is the 1 AC = 6 HP = 1 STA formula. Since you're level 37, a point of STA isn't going to increase your hp by 6, it should be somewhere closer to 4, so substitute 4 for that 6 in HP until you get higher up.

Some things I recommend:
Velium Fire Wedding Ring (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4243): 300-400pp
Black Sapphire Electrum Earring (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1919): 750-1000pp (you can forgo these if you don't have the platinum)
Black Sapphire Platinum Necklace (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1149): 1000-1500pp

Basically do what the other folks have suggested, check the bazaar and check for your race/class/deity, sort by AC or HP and find the best bang for your buck.

If you have any questions or need anything, I'm on Torvo as well, so feel free to ask me =) I'm not uber or anything, but I definitely came from the same path you're on now, and hopefully my experience can help you through it.
