Yer welcome to my opinion, it'll be interesting to see other ones as well.

I'm a 65 Warrior and I still keep ToD in my weapons bag. I use it for soloing big green mobs when I'm bored and when I need to be an emergancy slower. It is amazing just how high the mobs are that it will slow. Sure, you will get resists but so do Shamen.

Look at the ratio, 33/44 - not good but not bad. When it hasn't procced you are still doing damage. If it was 44/44 people would be extolling it's virtues from the mountaintops.

Soloing (no Cleric ;) ) regularly for exp until about level 60, I used ToD, Obsidean Great Axe, and Silken Whip of Ensnaring to fear kite.

IMO ToD is a must own weapon for mid-level warriors.
