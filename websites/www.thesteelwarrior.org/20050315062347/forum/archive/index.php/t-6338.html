There are some thing you have to keep in mind about riposte that people usually don't understand. First of all, you have to understand that riposte rates are very low, just a small percentage of total swings. The deceptive thing about ripostes came from a parse a while back that attributed a large percentage of a mob's DPS to riposte. The misconception stemmed from this due to the fact that a) the mob in the pasrse was 70% slowed and b) while the % increase was large, the amount of damage taken was negligible in the grand scheme of things. The difference in healing required to keep up with riposte damage was not a problem.

The reason many people opt for 2h on big hitters is not so much for the increase in DPS of the mob, but rather the increased chance of a tank being 1 rounded. As the chances of being 1 rounded go down, the usefulness of using a 2her is smaller. Most high end warriors have such a small chance of being 1 rounded on most mobs, that the extra agro is much more important than a .05% chance of being 1 rounded.

From the perspective of a DPS class, 1h is far and away the better choice. The increase in hate procs allows for a substancial increase in DPS, which makes fights shorter. The shorted a fight is, the less likely you are to lose the MT.

1h vs 2h is a very very simple arguement assuming you can hold aggro with both types of weapons. If your gear is marginal and you find yourself zerking a lot or dying as soon as your disc wears by all means use a 2h and cut down on riposte damage, and also dont kick or bash. If aggro is an issue A you probably shouldnt be tanking anyway and B you may wanna use 2hs just to ensure you have aggro. In summary 2h is used when the margin of error on a mob is very very small warrior/rotation wise. Most end game warriors use 2 weapons for 2 reasons. 1 is they are marginally higher dps, 2 they have better combined stats, and 3 the margin of error on pop mobs is significantly lower than someone who has just gotten into elementals or time. You also have to consider the riposte rate on god mobs far far lower than it is on pop mobs.
