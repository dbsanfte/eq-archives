While not the same quality as the point-purchased armor, some of the drops in level 65 dungeons are decent. Steelslave Bladed Boots drop in EC, cost about 4K on Sol Ro if you don't want to camp them.

I assume you want the mask for the effect. If you don't mind switching it out, there's a mask (Mask of Flourishing Fungus?) that drops in level 65 South Ro (Guk) missions. About 2 or 3K on Sol Ro. While rare, a FrightForged Mask from Cazic Thule offers more hitpoints, and more AC, then what you have, without the resists. I've seen them go for 1K, but they definitely are not common. A Kylong Darkmail Visor (5K) would also be an upgrade. It's from Veksar, and be easily obtained by 1 group.

Kylong Shinguards might be slightly better for the leg slot. Kind of depends if you get Stamina buffage from shamans regularly or not.

If you don't use a bow to pull, a Bloody Ancille's Pouch from level 65 BB missions offers a lot of hitpoints for the Range slot. About 3K. Other affordable upgrades include the oldie but goodie Idol of the Thorn (1K) and a Broken Hynid Claw (also about 1K). Both give fewer hitpoints then the Pouch, but more AC. In most indoor dungeons, and all LDoN dungeons, you don't need a bow. Incite or proximity pulling work just dandy.

I would recommend mainhand BoC, offhand CHoS and sell the SotB. Better proc and almost identical ratios give the CHoS a slight edge IMO.

You have a pretty good base for your group. I would recommend adding a cleric for the 6th slot if you can. Once you have all gotten 65 and started working on AAs here is what I would do:

1. Experience in Veksar while your buddies get to 65. There are oogd drops for all classes there. Move to BoT when you are all 65 and have gotten the Veksar items you want. There are many camps that drop decent items and ornate. AA should come pretty fast here.

2. Learn to do LDoN hards. Once your rogue gets SoS, he can scout a dungeon and see if there are any named mobs up. Get over your win/loss record and just do adventures with easy to get to named. Once you get an instanced with easy to get to named, complete that for the points. There are several VERY good drops in LDoN hard dungeons.

3. Start working on GoD trials 1-4. Again, there are many named that drop some very nice loot in those trials. When you have beaten trial 1-4, move in to trial 5-6. Those trials are considerably harder and you will likely have a hard time winning, but with a charmed chanter pet, you can go in specifically to farm the named.

Good luck, there is a ton of single group content that will get you and your friends gear up.

I suggest a combination approach once you get to 65. I'd do BoT/HoH (or even better if you have a wiz to port you, Plane of Hate since it's less crowded) until you get the first 30 to 40 AA's. By that point you will be able to breeze through LDoN. If you go straight to LDoN you may find it difficult at first - which translates into a lot more time to get the AA's and drops. For me, normal LDoN mobs became trivial around 60 AA's - but it all depends on your gear, supporting cast, etc.

Also, in normal mode, AA's are very slow in LDoN. It usually takes me 3 normal adventures to get an AA. Considering the startup time involved and the fact that we can only do 1 a night on weekdays, planes xp is dramatically better than LDoN.
