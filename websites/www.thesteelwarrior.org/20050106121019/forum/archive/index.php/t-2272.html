Here are a few thoughts that might help you compare different pieces of gear.

Extra procs/crits from high dex is a good thing. But compared to HP/AC it is nothing.

If your choice is between two items:

a) 10ac, 15 HP

b) 10ac, 15 HP, 15 dex

Then sure... grab item B and be happy that you got a dex bump that might net you an extra crit from time to time.

But if another item gets tossed into the mix...

c) 12ac, 15 HP

Then item C is the winner. HP and AC are always more important than dex.

The only time that it should be hard to judge the gear is when one has a high ac, and the other has high HPs. In that case, a generally accepted rule of thumb is 6hp = 1 ac for comparing.

Stamina and agility are really the only stats that come into play, until they are capped. All other stats are "nice to have" but don't really matter.

Stamina = HPs.

Agility: If your agility is less than 75 you take a huge ac hit. So if your agility is under 75, it is very important to raise it.

But once it is over 75, you can pretty much count on 5 agility = 1 ac.

Resists are a separate subject. Usually people carry a separate set of gear for special encounters when resists are important. At your stage in the game I wouldn't worry about resists yet. As you progress they will eventually become important, but by then you'll have a better idea of what you can and can't trade off to get them.
