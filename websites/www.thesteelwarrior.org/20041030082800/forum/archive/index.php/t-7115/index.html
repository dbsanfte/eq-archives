Morimoto
Don't forget about Ornate gear, could do ya good in arms and bracers and for a lot less than some of the other stuff if money is an issue. Definitely go for an elemental bow, prices are coming down alot on some of them.

Priorities would probably be:
Replace bloodpoint
Elemental Bow (any)
Ornate (or better) arms
Ornate (or better) bracer, especially for the slot with 0hp
Helmet of RZ (if you're gonna spend that much, dont skimp for the much lower ac white dragon helm)
Tae Ew pants are a good idea

After that stuff, you could look into Heavy Rubi Girdle or rings, but bigger HP upgrades in those other slots.
