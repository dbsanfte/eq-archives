For those who are in guilds that do not have the personnel or the guild culture to raid 4 to 6 nights a week, they are less further along in PoP content because that type of content takes committment to get it done. Things will continue to get worse on this until some more content comes out. This is going to be difficult with instanced high level content. Right now, you have quite a few servers with multiple guilds on exactly the same level...nearly totally equipped with time gear...and multiple guilds not far behind. This is somewhat unique in that alot more guilds will be starting off on equal footing now, and with instances, they won't be getting in each other's way. Flagging, for instance, was a serious bitch. It caused a helluva lot of strife in mid level guilds as the more focused characters quickly got into areas out of reach of the more casual player, but by the same token, it let there be some seperation between guilds getting into Time, and slowed things down.

I do sympathise with the lower end guilds. It wasn't that long ago that I was a member of one, looking up at the 2 or 3 high end guilds on our server, and getting pretty pissed that I had nothing to raid because they were busy filling thier coffers with plat because they were bored (or in the instance of one of them, paying their guild leader's rent).
