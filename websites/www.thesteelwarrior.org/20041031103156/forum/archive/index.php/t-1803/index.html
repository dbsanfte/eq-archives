1, Several bits of Fierce Heraldric are better than Warlords / Skyshrine armour, so yes FH is great. Armour of a quality which used to take raids to achieve for a good price in the bazaar.

2, Neck and Belt- could always go FH here aswell. Clattering bone necklace from the grey and Heavy Rubicite girdle from CT zone are frequently recommended for these slots as well.

3, 9th / 10th coldain rings are both nice, but for the amount of effort Valorium Rings of Gallantry are easier to obtain these days. The insta click on 8th ring+ is nice to have tho and fills an entry requirement of many guilds. Chardok ring and cord are nice items and will last you a long time.

4, Most of your gear is pretty nice- really nice for your level. If you are weighing up between FH and SS you might want to swap your 2nd bracer, legs and boots to FH but that really just comes down to budget. The gear you have is fine for now.
