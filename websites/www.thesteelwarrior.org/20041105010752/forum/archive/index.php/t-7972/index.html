Eriatha Egan
13 clears of Vex Thal and worth every penny.

1) The best warrior loot drops off Aten Ha Ra. Pick one item from her melee loot list and stick to it, as you're probably not going to enjoyed 2 of her loots or more unless you either are planning on clearing VT a lot or you are one hell of a valuble, high attendance, solid member. This includes:

-Great Shadow Platemail (80ac/125hp)
-Earring of Eradication (25ac/125hp)
-Crimson Runed Mask (50ac/125hp)
-Talisman of Vah Kerrath (35ac/125hp)
-Gloves of the Crimson Sigil (??/??... We never had a single one drop in 13 clears)

I can tell you now that the Earring of Eradication, Talisman of Vah Kerrath, and Crimson Runed Mask are not challenged in any way, shape or form, in terms of similar quality items out of the elemental planes. The only one that is, is quested - Signet of Might.

Now, the Gloves of the Crimson Sigil and Great Shadow Platemail are still both awesome, but they do have some similar counterparts in the elementals if you look at it from a giant perspective. The BP off Rathe is technically better than the GSP, but I really wouldn't consider it so since Rathe is generally the last mountain before guilds reach Time. Seru also drops one fucking awesome breastplate as well. The Raex Gloves have better melee stats than the Gloves of the Crimson Sigil, though the Aten gloves do have better SVS. Still, you can get an Orb of the Sky from one of the lower level Diabos and still get the same effect, granted the clicky on the Aten gloves lasts a shitload longer.

2) As the poster said above, there is a LOT of loot in Vex Thal. Do NOT limit yourself to the "Only The Best" attitude. You will be averaging 25-35 pieces of loot per clearing depending on if you hit the TVX's or not. Other notable, awesome loots:

-Ring of the Crimson Bull (20ac/100hp)
-Belt of Mastery (35ac/125hp)
-Ring of Resistance (10ac 80hp, but 170 overall SVS)
-Spiked Shoulderpads (30ac 90hp)
-Umbracite Bracer (38ac 75hp)
-Earring of Vigor - 15ac 80hp
-Earring of battle - 15ac 100hp (non lore)
-Umbral Vambraces - 50ac 90hp
-Umbracite Plate Leggings - 55ac 85hp (nice if you're unlucky with Kizrak Greaves)
-Bone Necklace of Battle - 25ac 85hp
-Helmet of Shadow - 50ac 125hp
-Cloudy Steel Mask - 20ac 125hp (clicky invis)
-Breastplate of Speed - 60ac 125hp 40% haste!

And there are more. Like I said, do not limit yourself to only Aten loot. There is so much loot in Vex Thal that you will be doing yourself a disfavor if you "only hold out for the best". That's just wrong, not to mention, just selfish. If your guild has their heads on straight, they will continue to do Vex Thal alongside the Elementals until they feel they have run out of upgrades in VT.

VT loot + Elemental loot go hand-in-hand.

Just for clarification... Orb of the Sky and Aten Gloves

Orb of the Sky drops from an easier boss, and you can probably buy it much cheaper than aten gloves. Its a clicky Call of Sky, a ranger spell that does 35 magic dmg and a 0.0/55 stun... the warriors that I know that have one love it, free agro!

Aten gloves drop from aten herself :) I'd expect them to sell for more than orb, if they won't work as an upgrade for you. Its a clicky Call of Fire, a ranger spell that does 65 fire dmg and a 0.0/55 stun... I'm not 100% on this but the 30 dd difference won't account for very much extra agro, so unless the gloves are also a solid upgrade then you might want to consider jsut getting the orb.

Ring of Resistance is the tits... decent stats, comparable to tier 1-2 loot, but phenomenal resists, and to boot you get a Slot 1: Increase all resist by 10 clicky. Other than aten loot thats the one item that seemed everyone wanted in my old guild.

Clickies are the bomb diggity from VT, I'd love to go back to VT for a month or two just to stock up on the clickies :) If you have an Allakhazam's account, I'd reccomend searching for items in each slot, only allowing Luclin and PoP gear to be displayed, then you can get a good comparison of what items you'd be likely to keep for a while, and which you might want to just hold off and save points for. If you plan on going through PoP, my advice in VT is to go for non-visible slots, legs, and BP. As for AHR specific loot, go with either the neck or the mask. The earing, BP, and gloves are nice, but can find similar. BP off of Seru is very comparable, so is Seru earring and tradeskill earring. The gloves have become less desirable since the WA line of AA has come out. Here's what I would personally recommend for each slot (my guild has a rule of only one AHR loot per person):

Ears: tradeskill earing, Seru earing...other nice options are earing of battle from mini aten, crystallized rain from Agnarr, and clawed earthcrafter's hoop in PoEb.

Neck: Gorget of Superiority (41% haste) or bone necklace of battle, both are from VT

Head: Raex

Face: Crimson Runed Mask from AHR.....don't settle for anything worse, the best PoP mask out there i believe is 20ac 140hp off of fennin, where as aten mask is 50ac 125hp

ring: emperor ring, ring of the crimson bull (VT), moss encrusted band (plane of water), ring of destruction (AoW)

wrist: raex, earthen bracer of fortitude (41% haste) from PoEa

arms: raex

hands: raex

shoulders: puresteel mantle from plane of air, flowing moss mantle (41% haste) PoEa

feet: raex

legs: umbracite plate legs from VT, kizrak's (raex only drops from elemental gods)

belt: belt of mastery, cord of midnight (41% haste), both from VT

chest: seru bp, cursed bp, breastplate of speed from VT (41% haste and some ATK), kizraks (raex only drops in time), ldon BP

back: cloak of icy shadows from VT, cloak of thorns from NToV, quested loyalist shield of honor
