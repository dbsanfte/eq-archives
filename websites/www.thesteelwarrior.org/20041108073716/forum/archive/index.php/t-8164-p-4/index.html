Here's my opinion of the situation.

Summary: EP gear on LDoN's is a BAD idea. Let LDoN's do what they were meant to do. Allow folks to skip over much of Velious and Luclin content.

There are three current advancement paths to progress along.

1. Level
2. AA
3. Gear

Prior to PoP releasing, the main paths to advance in power were level and AA. This meant that opportunity for progression was there for everyone since all you had to do was keep grinding out amazingly dull xp camps and you'd get it. Your hard core players would level up quickly and consume the content. Your more casual players would take their time, pace it, do mostly group xping but still eventually attain that level of power.

Better gear was available to raiders but it wasn't until PoP came out that it became a major progression path in and of itself. Once that happened the opportunity available to raiders and non-raiders became drastically different. No matter how much time someone put in, they basically needed to raid to progress.

I don't believe that the solution resides in adding EP gear to elementals though. I believe that any such solution would be hacking old broken content with a chainsaw.

Here's my proposal:

1. We need to get back to level based advancement. A level 70 Time geared player SHOULD be much more powerful than a level 70 LDoN geared character. However, a level 70 LDoN geared peep should be powerful enough that with 3 or so groups they could take down the flagging mobs in PoP and gain access to the EP eventually. This is consistent with all past expansions. You can't be a dork and do this.. but a quality player who simply chooses a different style of play should be able to manage.

2. A level 70 in Time gear, SHOULD be able to race through new content ( GoD/OOW ) much faster than a level 70 in LDoN gear. However, a 70th level character should be of sufficient power to participate in new content, at least to the point that they can upgrade themselves enough, with the appropriate amount of work and effort, to continue progressing through the new content.. in effect "skipping" pop if they so desire. Risk reward should still be in place, but at the same time, people should experience progression while consuming new content, not endless hours of ldons over and over again. I dont think anyone really wants to do that.

I believe much of the strife we're seeing today in GoD stems from the lack of level 70. The encounters were geared towards level 70 characters. So if the whiners can suspend their moaning for one moment and just imagine. If level 70 were in game today... would it be fair to say that a LDoN/Pre-EP tank would need to be 70th level to tank the same things that a 65th level PotTime geared warrior could?

My opinion is that this is perfectly reasonable. If you think so too, then the answer is to just hang tight until OOW releases with the level 70 expansion. Putting it another way... I'll agree that the content now is BROKEN.. however, I think everyone needs to look beyond our current content to find the solutions.
