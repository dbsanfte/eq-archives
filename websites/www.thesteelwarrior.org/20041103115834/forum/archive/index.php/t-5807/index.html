Brokkah Narrow
As Hussar suggests, Loyalist Shield of Honor will give you a massive AC boost over a heiro cloak.

Other slots, all of these are tradable so farming pp for them is an option.

Face - Thulian Visage of Horror is 30AC 100hp droppable mask, from an event in CT.
Rings - Steel Ring of Earthen Resilience if you can farm up the PP, although the cost is high for the level of benefit you will get.
Bracer - Mana Threaded Bone Bracelet will give you a good AC boost. Drops in hard LDoN or you can buy it.
Range - Anything with AC, an elemental bow is the ideal since you dont have to swap a lot but even a Goranga Idol would give you +13AC or so.
Shoulder - Horn Spiked Shoulder Plates will net you about +20AC
Head - Ideally a Helmet of Rallos Zek, but ornate would be a noticable improvement.
Eoden
You are wearing primarily velious gear and raiding velious content so your AC shouldnt bother you too much.

Back when Velious and SoL were the end game it was pretty well accepted that AC was capped and AC beyond 1300 was just for making your magelo look pretty. You have 1188 at the moment, so you should be hitting close to 1300 with Bot9 / WoG / CotR. Unless you are wanting to get your guild into raiding some PoP content - BoT towers and such, its not something that needs to be worried about too much at this stage.

For some upgrade actual advice:

Bracers - Full Mithril bracers would give you an AC upgrade at the trade of some stamina. 2 bracers would be +16AC -16sta. Raw HPs would be the same with an improvement to saves.

Range - you could take a group to Maidens Eye and try camping for a Transient Idol (15AC, 50hps, some stats)

Shoulders - Crystallized Acid Pauldrons or Horn Spiked Shoulderpads are both a nice upgrade and both Bazaar buyable, but also both in the same price bracket as some ornate upgrades
