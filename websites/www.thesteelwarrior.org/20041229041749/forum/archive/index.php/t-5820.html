It can be done.

Make, say, hotbutton #10 your gloves. Then, in options, make the key to activate #10 the same key that activates whatever hotbutton slam is. It's how monks tie their epic gloves to flying kick.

The downsides, besides the very viable ones listed by Ain, are that you lose a hotbutton, and on any other hotpage, and on any other character, you will be pressing 2 hotbuttons at once every time to hit the key that has slam. Which in certain situations can be very bad. ;)
