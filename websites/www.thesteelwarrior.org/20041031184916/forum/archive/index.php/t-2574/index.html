bigguard
Buy a jagged bone earring or something to that effect, 6ac 40hp 40mana, i got one for 500pp, decent upgrade to orc fang.

Helanic Tundra greaves would be a huge upgrade to your acrylia plate greaves, you could only afford that if you sold both your weapons and bought a gsot though.

You could sell your belt of the great turtle, then pick up a putrid belt of rotten hide (16ac 10dex 10agi 65hp) for real cheap, and that would give you some more cash to work with. The effect on the belt of the great turtle really isnt worth not having some good upgrades in other slots imo.

Any helanic tundra armor would be great for you, and your helm definatly needs an upgrade. Dented kunzar war helm from the ballroom in veksar (across from shop)would be a really good helm for you. 23ac 8sta 35hp, ive seen it for as low as 2k on mith marr.

Anyhow, thats all i can think of for now, if i were you i would definatly sell both my weapons and my belt, buy a GSOT and putrid belt of rotten hide and see how much cash you have to work with from there.

You could always camp the copper hammer and sword of the bloodsworn again easily if you really love them. IMO the GSOT is just as good as that combo though

Definatly get haste though. Soulscream belt is only about 20k, 31% haste and some decent stats, and enchanted sash of wonder from droga (5ac 35hp 31% haste) sells for about 17k on mith marr.
