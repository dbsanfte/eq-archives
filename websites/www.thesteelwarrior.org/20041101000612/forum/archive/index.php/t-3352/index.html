Wow, nice twink job.

I wouldn't bother with th black sapphire neck, 65hp is nice, but no AC/STA or anything else useful really makes it a caster item. There are a few decent neck items you can go for instead that have near as many HP but also have other useful stats.

Ritual Talisman of Fate (Veksar)
+10 AC +10 Str +10 Sta +8 vsMg +8 vsFire +50 HP +35 Mana +0.70 Wt

Fierce Heraldic Collar
+14 AC +5 Agi +50 HP +3.00 Wt

Mithril Spiked Collar (Veksar)
+9 AC +5 Str +6 Sta +4 Agi +10 vsMg +45 HP +0.40 Wt

Amulet of Mummy Wrappings (LDoN - Ruj)
+12 AC +7 Str +3 Sta +5 Agi +10 vsCold +45 HP +25 Mana +0.80 Wt

Necklace of Flame Marked Links (Tower of Sol Ro)
+9 AC +5 Wis +5 Int +10 vsMg +20 vsCold +65 HP +65 Mana +0.40 Wt

As for a good 2h weapon, I would go for a Greatstaff of Thunder for agro and a War marshalls bladed staff for DPS.
