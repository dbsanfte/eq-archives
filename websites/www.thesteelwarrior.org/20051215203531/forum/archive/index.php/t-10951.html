Anyone have any info on how to Mauradon to share?
Whats this staff/key quest to do?

There are 3 "entrances" into Mauradon. Two are actually "zone lines", the third is used by obtaining the staff.

The two "zone line" ones are in the Purple and Orange(?) caves. Each side leads into a different section of Mauradon, but they eventually connect in the middle. There are a couple of bosses in each section which are needed to make the staff/key. Once you have that, you can head to the area where they connect and head to another boss (Celebras) who you need to kill, but who will then give you the Staff. Past the waterfalls behind him is the "harder" part of Maraudon, with a bunch of other areas and bosses to slay.

Once you have the Staff, you can go to the little alter behind the first Kahn (Kolk?) and use it, which will open a portal to the area just past the waterfalls.

Hope that helps.
