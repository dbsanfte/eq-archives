11-05-2004, 07:49 PM
Savage Onslaught is good when you need things dead fast in an xp group, which happens a good deal in the instanced zones. OoW mobs generally don't hit so hard so you end up losing due to running out of mana, and it's really hard to heal slower in a mess just because you're disced. In those cases any offense you can do helps.

It's also decent DPS that stacks with Fellstrike, which is still our best offensive disc unless Veteran's Wrath raises crits to more than 100% (at 100% Fellstrike still wins because it also raises minimum damage modifier). The endurance cost on Savage Onslaught is pretty prohibitive though. You should only use it in situation where you'd be likely to die (then the cost doesn't really matter) and Fellstrike is down, or in situation where you don't need to worry about what happens after the fight (like most ubers).
