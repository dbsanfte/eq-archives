My suggestions, however, this will likely require time farming plat or farming the items themselves.

Ears: easiest/cheapest to more costly
Black sapphire electrum earring (2ac/35hp)
Jagged Bone Hoop (6ac, 40hp)
Solstice earring (5ac, 5sta, 55hp)
Mosquito leg earring (7ac, 8sta, 65hp)
Bloodmetal earring of engagement (7ac, 8sta, 70hp)

Face:
Crystal Spider Eyes (8ac, 35hp)
Death Mask of the Elysians (12ac 35hp)
Gantru War Mask (11ac, 5sta, 25hp)

Fingers:
6ac/65hp ring
another valorium ring

From the looks of your armor, you CoV faction is likely trashed. If you feel up to it, spend time in kael fixing it and possibly farm thurg armor there. Picking up a thurgadin helm would not be a bad idea. Once it is fixed, bracers of benevolence and draconic pauldrons quest are options.

Factionless suggestions
If you can strike up a deal with a high elven smith, try farming materials for Full mithril armor and go for arms/legs/boots/bracers.
Head: Helm of Raldukan, this you can hunt with a group in UP
