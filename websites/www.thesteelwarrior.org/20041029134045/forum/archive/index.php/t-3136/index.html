Honestly...

To make a really long post really short, you can:

1. Start working on Ssra and VT.

2. Start doing LDoN exclusively to upgrade your BPs and Legs.

3. Team up with another guild and get access to everything but Rallos Zek.

4. Mix in upgrade raids with a lot of LDoN if your people need AA's or Levels anyway.

5. Recruit about 10-20 more people.

Details to follow:

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Option 1. If you have that many people with lots of DPS, and a bad ass tank or two, your next move is probably to get into VT.

Some guilds can skip VT on the basis of pure numbers. You will most likely not be able to do this.

If you want to keep it small and tight, have people learning the Ssra mobs and getting VT keys. You could definitely use 2 more clerics.

If you can get it to yourself, it will yield similar or better upgrades for the time spent, but with LDoN you can do the whole guild at once with no competition, and the items will serve you until you are actually killing the elemental gods. If you get one ssra mob a week, then you will not be able to get the same bang for the buck.



Option 2. You could have everyone doing LDoN full time, 4 or 5 adventures a day for the next two-three-four months.

At that point you would have a BP and Legs with FT 4 for the casters or +120 hp upgrades for the melee on each slot.

2984 points for armor, 2300 for the caster upgrades and 6900 for the melee upgrades...The other benefit of course being the extra AA exp you can generate.

P.S. Getting BP and Legs to Elemental Level with Augmentations is the best bang for the buck, due to the rarity of those pieces even after you gain access.

Look at some of the threads around here about rarity of various slots/class type armor and try and make sure people are filling out with those things first from LDoN.

At that pace, in 3-4 months all your plate classes would have 220 hp BP's, 200 hp Greaves...and they will have generated on average another 70 or so AA's.



Option 3. You are going to hit a wall in flagging purposes twice no matter what you do with that many people.

1. Rallos Zek (the adds will wreck you due to lack of DPS)
2. Rydda Dar (the Max hit streakiness means loss of DPS at some point, and you won't be able to catch up when the MA and TA go down)

Xarim, my honest advice to you for flags is to team up with some other people to your mutual benefit.

If you just want flags and are not concerned about keeping the loot in the family, then either grab a comparable guild, or host a 72man open raid like we do on Drinal.

You can easily take down Saryrn with this method.

Same with Terris Thule.

Behemoth is a little harder but with 72 Level 65's you should have no problem.

Aerin Dar is a Snap if people use their heads.

The first two Trials in HoH are easy as well.

Rydda Dar is just a matter of keeping that MA up for as long as possible to keep the MAX amount of dps on him. I beat him in early PoP with a pickup raid (but had 96 people at the time - avg level 63 though :p )

Caprin cycle will always be tough to accomplish with Open raids due to the fact that every person that dies forms another add, but it is doable.

Drinal has done Mith Marr with pick-up raids before as well. If you have Bards and an MA with high hps, plus plenty of clerics, you can do MM.

So look into teaming up with another guild or group. With 4 clerics, a strong MA, and some bards/chanters/kiters, you will definitely hold up your side of the bargain.

The best part is that you can plan this twice a week and have people working on LDoN the other 5.



Option 4. Faster than all of this of course, and the bane of all fun family guilds anywhere, is just to recruit about 6 more clerics and 18 more rangers and Wizards and Mages.
