First off, your sta is over 100 so any additional sta just adds hps. Now, how do you value stats? Personally I am only worried about AC and HP's (sta = hps).

I would figure out how much you value the stats that are affected and see how much you get for your money. To do this I would assign a value to each stat affected in terms of hps.

Say you come to the following numbers (yours may differ)

1AC = 10hps
1HP = 1hp
1STA = 4.5hps

After deciding on these relative values, determine the value of the options in terms of hps. Then divide by the cost of the item (after selling your current)

For example, the helm in this case would be

9*10+35*1+4*4.5 = 143hp

and say it cost 5000pp more than your current helm sells for.

143/5000 = 28.6hps/1000pp (easier to do per 1000pp imo)

Now you can see which 1000pp gets you the most of what you value.

If you value str or other stats you can come up with how many hps each point of a stat is worth to you. There are no right or wrong values, just how you value each.
