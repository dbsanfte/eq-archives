I consider myself to be a casual warrior. I usually buff up to around 8800-9200 with around 1600 AC, and that has worked fine for WoS and RCoD for the most part with one exception.... the elites.

For those of you who have grouped in the northern part of WoS, and pretty much and part of RCoD and MPG you know what I'm talking about. Any mob named "An Elite Noc........ An Elite Kyv........ and An Elite Ukun........." hits for around 150-750 max, which isn't bad by itself, but when you add the fact that they mitigate slow and proc a 650-750 DD almost every round, it makes fighting them a real bitch.

I guess besides a rant I'm wondering, am I the only warrior to get my ass kicked by these gems? One full quadded round with some full-hits and semifull-hits, add in a couple procs, can take me down 30-40% of my life. I find myself burning evasive every time it pops when these guys are pulled just to try and let the cleric breathe.

Anyway, for those who are in the same boat, are you doing something that I'm not? Maybe I'm missing some key concept to tanking these that others aren't. Let me know if they are a bitch for you all too.
