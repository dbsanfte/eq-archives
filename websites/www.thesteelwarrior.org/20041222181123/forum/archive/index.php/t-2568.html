What takes a long time in sky is coffining and people not knowing how things work, people falling off the edge, etc. If you had a solid group of decently equipped 60+ type and less than 4 groups so you don't have to mess with coffins you can move reasonably fast. Make sure that everyone coming with you is there for the long hall because people having to leave and be replaced can cost you hours.

You also need a raid leader who understands how sky works and knows all the tricks. Those guys are getting more and more rare these days, and a lot of us that knew how it worked 2 or 3 years ago have forgotten a lot of it by now (I know I couldn't remember how things worked last time I went up there to do KoS. Point is, do your homework, and bring as many people as you can find that have experience in sky.
