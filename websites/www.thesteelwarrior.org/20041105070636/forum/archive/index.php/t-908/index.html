Kbam
West wall breaks seem to depend on how well you handle the scarelings and just how far they shadowstep you. All it takes is for one person to get shadowstepped a large distance away and the number of mobs you're fighting can go from a couple to 10+ in a few seconds. I'd only recommend west wall breaks for those who have been there before.

For first timers I'd suggest looking at doing a north wall break. A north wall break allows easier CRing and recovery from a wipe although it does take some coordination to get a cleric in the right spot and camped out safely at first. Once at north camp there is only one patroller to worry about so it does afford a larger margin of safety. Downside to this is the longer set-up time.
