Ikkinz Group Trial 1: Singular Might
Speak to the NPC near the ZI of KT first. He will give you a good history lesson and tell you to go talk to Ganek (I may have mispelled, but he a FD NPC near the 3rd temple to the south) When you run through the dialogue with him (you can be invis, but be careful, there are multiple npcs in the area, including some see invis pathers) he will give you SM Trial. The zone in is in 1 of the 3 tunnels that are at the back of the temple and go north, middle, and south (this should make sense when you see them)

A lot of see invis mobs in the way here, but you can normally figure out which tunnel you need to run into before you run to save yourself. I suggest using lev and invis to go as high up the south wall as you can. Using fortitude will help here, and you should be ok since it is unlikely you will need it for the first hour on your first couple of trips.

PA , if it spawns for your trial, will be the 3rd mob you pull if you skip the roamers. Roamers respawn, nothing else does. I will leave the rest for you to learn.


Ikkinz Group Trial 2: Twin Struggles
Far south temple. I forget npc's name, but you have to defeat Singular Might before you can get this one. Basicly, once you understand how to get to SM you cna get to TS easy.

Ikkinz Group Trial 3: Don't remember offhand, have not beaten TS yet

Good luck, Ikkinz is a lot of fun imo. And very few spoilers, so you actually get the enjoyment of learning most of the encounters yourself.
