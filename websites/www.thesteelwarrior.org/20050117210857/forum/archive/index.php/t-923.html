I picked up a GoT yesterday on Saryrn for 10K. I don't know what the seller was thinking.

You can not judge proc rate by one or two fights it takes hrs to get a good number plus what you think you see and what happens are two different things. I have logged many fights with both the GoT and ecpis (both 1hs and 2hs) and they all proc at the same rate. With 270-280 dex I get 1 proc every 30 sec no matter the zone. There are times I would swear it was higher or lower but over hrs it comes 2 proc a min. IF the epic is not resisted the two procs seem about the same for argo but if the epic is resisted then it seems to have far less argo. The GoT does about 10 dps more then the epics, this seems to make a huge difference on how well taunt works on the times you do lose argo. I seem to get argo much faster at the start of a fight with the GoT if I dont get a proc.

I see two problems with epics, first LOW DPS. If you dont get a proc early then its hard to get argo based on damage and taunt. Next is the proc is fire based and on fire resist mobs it just dont pack the argo you need.

The biggest problem with GoT is the knock back, about 1 ft, you best be ready to follow the mob, but this is GOOD and BAD.
