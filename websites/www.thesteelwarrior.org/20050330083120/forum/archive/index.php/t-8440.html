I've always been a large fan of clearing up to the first static cragbeast and then pulling pookas into that corner. Pulling the front pooka on the right will get you 2 adds, so you can either mez that one right away or drag all 3 back. I *think* that's the pull where you'd get his PH. If not, you'll just have 2 pookas left. Chavoor will NOT add if he's up with that initial pull. From there, you can move everyone down to the zone in and let the puller (enchanter!) do their thing. Even if you wipe, it's an easy CR without a drag/summoner. Oh and if nobody needs the loot that drops, you can gimp in someone that could use.
