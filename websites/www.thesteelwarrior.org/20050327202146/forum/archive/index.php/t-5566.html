"Dok-b" - Chardok B, aka the Halls of Betrayal, situated at the bottom of Chardok.

The zone is perceived to be hard to get to without decent Chardok faction, despite the fact that someone who knows the way and has a sow and a brain can run down there taking minimal damage. Therefore, it's usually uncamped except for the occasional farmer.

Xp is moderate to poor there - Droga is better.

"Ring quest" - quest for a WIS caster ring which might go well on your druid. Involves farming four pages (which most classes can solo at 65) then a final boss encounter which is easily completed with a single group.

"Ft2 necklace" - Spiritmaster War Beads, a tradeable necklace with 25hp, 25mana and Flowing Thought II, recommended level 55. Obtained by camping in any area where Ferocious Chokidai spawn. In these areas A Rabid Chokidai is a rare spawn, and its rare drop is the necklace. Your group could easily take the Rabid Chokidai; I've soloed that critter with a 65 cleric.

Areas where a Vicious Chokidai spawn (e.g. the entrance) also have a chance of spawning a Rabid Chokidai. However, Rabid Chokidai in these areas only drop earrings.

The only reason most people go to Chardok B is to farm Spiritmaster War Beads; on my server people ask 150k in the Bazaar for them.
