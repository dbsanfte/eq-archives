It might also be useful to add a paragraph discussing the Haste Cap and calculating Total Haste or a link to such a discussion in the Library. I'd do this, but frankly I'm a bit confused by the issue. Afaik it's:

Total Haste = v1 Haste + v2 Haste +v3 Haste

v1 Haste: Maximum haste of any Haste(worn) or Augment. I'm not sure if any bard songs, buffs or Haste(Combat) belong in this catagory.

v2 Haste: Maximum Buff or v2 Bard song haste. Right click effects like Marvellous Visions are in this catagory. Stacks with v1 and v3 haste, but v1 + v2 haste can not exceed 100%.

v3 Haste: v3 Bard song "overhaste" (includes Battlecry of the Vah Shir, Warcry of the Vah Shir, etc.) and a very few items - Silver Hoop of Speed (+5% to haste/no cap). v3 effects stack with v1+v2 (but not with other v3 effects like other overhaste songs or Silver Hoop of Speed ??) and allow the 100% haste cap to be exceeded.
---------
Finally a paragragh showing the effect of Total Haste on weapon delay or a link to such a discussion in the Library would pretty much round out this thread.

Weapon Delay (Hasted) = Weapon Delay/(1 + Total Haste)

eg. DBoW DLY=35
Earring of Corporeal Essence = 40% haste [v1 item(worn)]
Visions of Grandeur = 58% haste [v2 buff]
Total haste = .98

DBoW Weapon Delay (Hasted) = 17.67 (18 DLY)
