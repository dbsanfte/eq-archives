Without any PL whatsoever, and not buying the armor, the Crafted can be a pain at the real levels you are talking about atm.
I remember from 1999. level 24 was just too low to get some of the items.

P.S. the werewolf that spawns at midnight at the border of Qeynos Hills and WK does NOT drop a talon, unfortunately.

Start with Bronze. A good set of Bronze will help the AC get up.

btw, your 150pp will only net you one piece of crafted total if you try and quest it yourself due to the cost of gems.

The way in which I got myself from level 24 with all leather and some bronze to full Crafted and an exe axe in a couple months was to trade in the market.

Buy low in the bazaar and try and sell high. In the n00b economies of 1999, 150pp was absolutely enough money to make 10,000pp in about 3 months...

Just remember that you wont be levelling during that time.

With no PL, trading was the single fastest way to improve your equipment, Bar NONE.
