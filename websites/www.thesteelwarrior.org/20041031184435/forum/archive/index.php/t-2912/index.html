Doesn't look like you're doing bad at all for a level 60, non-ND3 warrior. Some more HP's would be a plus for a safety in PoP though.

Your equipment is pretty good for your level, just work on the ND's when you can. Some upgrades to what you have:

Tracking Wolf Collar/Clattering Bone Necklace. Both of these are dirt cheap an' much better overall to that Mithril Torque. You might want to invest in a Darkmetal Visor, or better depending on how much cash you want to fork over.

Grab some plate gaunts from ToV if you can. An' replace the cloak with Iksar Hide Cape/Hierophant's Cloak/Or any other high HP's cloak.

You can upgrade your shoulders with Draconic Pauldrons too. You have some Myrmidon armor so I'm assuming you're on CoV faction.

Replace the Nightmare Bow eventually too... it's a nice bow and all but light on the HP's.

Other than that, I don't see much of a problem. The upgrades I mentioned above would only run you about 10k or so, and they'd probably take you past 4k HP's. Well, that's all I know. >_>
