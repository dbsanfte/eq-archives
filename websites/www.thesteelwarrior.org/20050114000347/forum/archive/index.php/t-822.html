FIrst item I would recommend is a Greatstaff of Thunder. It will get you 85hp plus it is a far superior agro weapon over your current set up. Once you have that, in no particular order:

Mosquito leg earring
Fine Heraldic boots
Fine Heraldic greaves
Fine Heraldic bracer x2
Fine Heraldic BP (until you can get Vindi)
Darkwood Bow of Suffering

Those items should take you over 3800hp without any AA work. It sounds like your guild is either capable or near ready for Vindi. Hold out on a Vindi BP. It is better than Myrmidon's and you will probably for near the rest of your EQ career. Get a cultural until then, at least you can sell that when you do get a Vindi.

I know you only have 40k to work with, but you should be able to get all of the armor for basically the time it will take you to gather components. If you can get enough components for say 2 or 3 attempts, many smiths will take that in trade for 1 completed piece. You get your armor cheap, and the smith gets some extra pieces to sell for profit. Lucky for you, human GM smiths are pretty common compared to other races.
