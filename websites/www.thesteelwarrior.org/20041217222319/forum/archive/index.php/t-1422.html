My guild spawned Garudon the veksar dragon last night -- which you do by camping the theater in veksar and looting 4 dragon bone shards and handing them in to some npc.

He has a dracoliche model, a relatively small AE (100/tick dot I guess), is undead, is slowable, hits for 990 or so..

..but has the hitbox of a willowwisp!

I'm not kidding, this thing is almost unhittable. And you have to be in melee range to cast, so all the casters have trouble too.

I was MT and completely lost aggro because I simply couldn't hit him. The moment he turned from me I couldn't locate his hitbox again and get aggro back consistently.

I'm thinking this is a mob best tanked with a knight, since for the times the MT can find the hit box it's probably best to maximize aggro. Aside from the hitbox thing, I really don't think he's likely to be very hard.

I think we also messed up our positioning -- wasn't really able to hit him until we had him bunched in a corner, but by then it was too late and we wiped. He proceeded to camp our corpses, and hadn't despawned for at least an hour after the wipeout.

My guild is a fairly wimpy one with a fear of raiding, so after the one wipe over half the raid simply left. Didn't get a second shot.
