Warriors like most melees get "disciplines".

http://ads2.crgaming.com/cgi-bin/AdServ32.dll?GetAdd&region=7&burst=12345

A discipline (called disc by 99.99% of EQ population) will enable
the warrior to adopt a specific fight style.

That fighting style will vary depenting on the situation.

- You can hit more often
- You can hit harder
- You can get missed more often
- You can get hit for less
- You can block all melee hits in front of you


**Small example**

When fighting "The Hulk" you want to avoid his hits
(1 hit and your dead, so you better not get hit)

When fighting "Bruce Lee" you want to be able to endure his hits
(caus he's gonna hit you a billion times anyway, useless trying to avoid)

The EQ world ressembles closely to that example.
Depending on what you fight, how you fight it, you may need to use a "special" style.

That "special style" is a fighting discipline, also known as disc.

At certain levels warriors (and all melee) recieve innate abilities with a duration and reuse time. Each ability allows us as warriors to perform better at one aspect of our class. The most common discipline, defensive, allows us to mitigate damage against us for about 3 minutes, however we cannot activate it for another 10 minutes and 30 seconds after the discipline has been activated, a real time downtime of about 7:30 minutes.

The disciplines we gain follow this pattern. (copy and pasted from casterrealm)

LEVEL 30: THE DISCIPLINE OF RESISTANT
LEVEL 40: THE DISCIPLINE OF FEARLESS
LEVEL 52: THE DISCIPLINE OF EVASIVE
LEVEL 53: THE DISCIPLINE OF CHARGE
LEVEL 54: THE DISCIPLINE OF MIGHTY STRIKE
LEVEL 55: THE DISCIPLINE OF DEFENSIVE
LEVEL 56: THE DISCIPLINE OF FURIOUS
LEVEL 57: THE DISCIPLINE OF PRECISION
LEVEL 58: THE DISCIPLINE OF FELL STRIKE
LEVEL 59: THE DISCIPLINE OF FORTITUDE
LEVEL 60: THE DISCIPLINE OF AGGRESSIVE
