I'd consider upgrading your grimling contraption mask to amygdalan chain mask, and I'd see if you can get a Cloak of Flames in your price range. The CoF will allow you to upgrade waist slot to FH belt, gaining some AC and HP over-all. Your arms would see a decent upgrade going to FH. Nightmarewood compound bow would give you 5 ac and 5 HP at the loss of some sta *which you can max out with shaman potions for more hp as well*. If you don't need bow for pulling, then goranga idol would be a cheap range slot upgrade, or golden idol of rallos zek if you need more str/sta. I'd also upgrade your shoulder slot to FH pauldrons, or possibly horn-spiked shoulder plates *might be a bit pricey on those*
