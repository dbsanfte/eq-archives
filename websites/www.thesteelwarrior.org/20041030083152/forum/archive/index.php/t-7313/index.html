Making the switch to Kingly boots of splendor with a shaman healing you is a bad idea.

You're pretty much at the top for what you can do with an Agnostic duo. Anything else you could do yourself would be minor at best. Best to find something expensive you can farm (IE : droga stuff that sells for good cash), and start buying bigger stuff...ornate, boots of the storm, etc.
