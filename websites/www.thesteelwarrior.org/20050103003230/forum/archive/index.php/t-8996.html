A recap from various sources including quest information from Allakhazam's (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2927), and this (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=8762&page=1&pp=15) thread, who's major contributor was Kaesorn.
---------
New OoW Warrior disciplines are obtained by turning in dropped Muramite runes to Karsor the Mad in Dranik's Scar - SW corner n1670 n40 (as are the spells and disciplines of lesser classes).

Level 66-68 runes are droppable and are found on single-groupable mobs. Level 69-70 runes are No Drop and are found on raid mobs.

-- Single groupable --
Level 66: Minor Muramite Rune - Aura of Runes Discipline
Duration 1 minute/Reuse 15 Minutes

Weapon buff (conflicts with Call of Fire, Symbol of the Planesmasters, and other right clickable weapon buffs). When it goes off it puts a 400 HP damage absorb buff in the Bard song window for up to 5 ticks.

Level 67: Lesser Muramite Rune - none

Level 68: Muramite Rune - Savage Onslaught Discipline
Duration 30 seconds/Reuse 15 minutes

Increased chance to Critical hit. Increased Crit damage. The Crits become Cripples like Mightystrike without having to be Berserk.

-- Raid --
Level 69: Greater Muramite Rune - Bazu Bellow Discipline
Duration 1 minute/Reuse 30 seconds

Next discipline in the Provoke line. Initial 600 Hate, 100 Hate/tick

Level 70: Glowing Muramite Rune - Shocking Defense Discipline
Duration 1 minute/Reuse 15 minutes

Defensive Proc. Conjecture - a chance that if you are hit, the mob will be stunned for up to 2 seconds (up to level 70).

BB
