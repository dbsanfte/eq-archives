This chart has stood the test of time. I relied on data points gleaned from others when I created it, and have used it myself. May it serve future Beastlords well.

Prior to level 51, the skill cap is simply (level*5) for Offense and Defense, and (level*5)+5 for the other skills, up to the level 50 skill cap listed in the first column of the chart below. Note that it is possible to reach the level 50 caps well before level 50. i.e. Dodge can be maxed as early as level 24, while Alteration cannot be maxed until level 46.
