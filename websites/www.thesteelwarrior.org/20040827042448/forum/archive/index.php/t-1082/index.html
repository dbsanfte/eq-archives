The quest is real easy, I've done it two times so far and both times tanked the mercenaries at level 55. You only need one group of level 50-55 to do it.

They have a fair amount of HP but are really sissies damage wise. Defensive is not needed at all, and you could even do without any resists.. though I didnt take the risks I hate shammy slows so I switched in some MR gear.

Basically you have two and two, you need to split the first two (dwarf cleric and pally) and then the next two (erudite wiz and barb shammy).

If you have no means of stunning the first two (healers), you MAY be in a little bit of trouble due to their amount of HP but otherwise it sohuld be a walk in the park.
