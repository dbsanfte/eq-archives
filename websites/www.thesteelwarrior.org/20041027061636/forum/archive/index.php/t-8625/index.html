Timmok
Unfortunately I dont have the data anymore, but I did do 2 overnight test sessions with and without my Improved Parry 2 shoulder. The data suggested that improved parry 2 does exactly what Lucy says it does: "Increase Chance to Parry by 16%". Which is also what Brael finds on SK.org I believe(http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?t=22474).

I am not sure I agree with his conclusions that it is useless though. The mob type(lvl 50 mob) I tested on went from a 43% to a 44% hitrate when I remove the shoulder, which(correct me if I am wrong), is equivalent to a 2.3% increase in damage.

Of course end-game mobs like Xxeric in Tacvi have hitrates closer to 65%, so going from 65->64% hitrate would "only" be a 1.5% reduction in average damage, but its still great bang for the buck for a single item.
