Lillyann
Dawnshroud Peaks till 47, easy. I was soloing there through 46-47, I was getting near half a blue per kill solo, so for a duo it should be decent exp, since you wont have Bind Wound downtime.

You can sit by the camp fire mid way on your way from Netherbian Lair, zone, to the Sanctus Seru zone (that's going to your right when coming from Netherbian Lair).

Pull wolves, named wolves, large zelniaks and named zelniaks. With a healer you should have zero downtime.

Once in a while you might get a lightcrawler or wolf aggro at the camp fire, but it's relatively safe ( I was Hiding while I used Bind Wound ^_^ ).

Also if your cleric partner sits behind the fire, between the NPC's the wolves that pop nearby will attack the NPC's first.

Echo Caverns, sure.. but good luck, it's over camped.

As for Dulak Harbor, wonderful idea. I wish I was a low level again to do some of those zones. Legacy of Ykesha is great if it wasn't for the hassle of getting there. Definitely go there, good exp, great zone with pirate boats and all, lots of funny loot, and rare semi-rare named that pop on any of the boats and in most of the LoY zones after you kill a bunch of the common mobs.

Also , dunno if you have worked on it already, but this is the place you want to go to help your cleric partner do her Temperance quest.

gulug
Try the spectres in Oasis. I quad them with my druid whos 45 and the exp is not bad. They drop cash loot which you can sell straight off to the gypsy vendors.

Breaking them may be a little tricky, but once they are broken it should be quite easy and the respawns are perfect for a duo. You can also take down whatever sand giants spawn in the area.

Another option is crystal caverns,not bad exp and you can sell the acrylia bricks for decent pp in bazaar to tradeskillers.

At your level you may also want to try the kael WL entrance giants. They may hit a little harder, but you will be getting solid faction so that when you start to do arena you can be ready to get your thurg armour straight away. Plus the giants always give pretty decent cash drops and you can sell the heads in the bazaar for about 100pp for people wanting faction hits.

As you are not duoing with a slower, I would recommend that you stack up on some DS potions before each session as that will help with your dps.
