There's at least two seperate Death Touch spells in the game:
Cazic Touch, AKA: Old world DT - This is the Extraplanar flag Death Touch. Instant cast, unresistable, you lose, 20k damage.
Touch of Vinitrias, AKA: Shei Vinitiras, Exiled's happy zappys - This is a 'false' death touch. It kills you dead, but has a different recycle time, can be blocked by Invulnerability, has a cast time, and can be interrupted.

Rule of thumb: If it goes SASSINAK!, the bane worked.
