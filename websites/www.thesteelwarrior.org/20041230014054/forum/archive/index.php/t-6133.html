Well, he asked for our advice, so here's my suggested order:
PE (very good for only 5 aa)
PD3 (lots of HP for the AA cost)
LR5 (for only 10 aa it really seems to let you avoid a lot of hits)
WA3 (spend 12 aa to seriously buff your proc aggro)
Stability2 (another 12 aa for another 200 HP)
PP5 (spend 10 aa to net just over 100 HP + other little stat bonuses)
Rampage (break time: spend 5 aa on some fun; after this it's grueling)
WA5 (18 aa for more uber proc aggro)
max Endurance Regen
Stability5 (300 net HP for your 48 hard earned aa)
max Run Speed
Aggresive3
max HI
Everything else

Of course I'm assuming you have average HPs, if you are really embarrassed by yours move the HP AAs up some. The biggest advice that I can offer: ignore all offensive AAs: fury6 + flurry6 + double ripost 6 + other junk like that < WA by a long shot. So get at least a few WA early for some huge aggro, and don't worry about any other offensive stuff until you finish WA and every defensive thing out there.
