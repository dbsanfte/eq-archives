In Plane of Earth, there are multiple spawnable named mobs that can easily be taken down by a single group of non-elemental equipped characters, even though they con red. They include A Korascian Warlord, The Living Earth, A Prismatic Basilisk and more. These mobs are spawned by triggering traps all though the zone (a good map of these traps can be found here (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=7110)
When the traps are triggered, either the named or their place holder will spawn.

The itmes that drop are very nice, including Whip of the Earthcaller (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=22980), Earthtoned Band (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=10249) and Cloak of Earthen Twine (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=15827\).

On my server at least, these actually frequently rot :( due to them being so common to pop and so easily killed by one group.

Hope this helps!
