It's really up to you and your playing style. I have about 150k in the bank and usually make about 10 to 15k a month. I'm not raiding that much yet and my guild is nowhere near elemental level (great people and very skilled...just small atm.)

For me the criteria I used was what would give me the best combination of ac and hp over my current gear and what I could buy with 60k or less (I don't want to blow all my plat on 1 item;)? ) I also considered things I could reasonably expect to get as drops in ldon or tier 3.
