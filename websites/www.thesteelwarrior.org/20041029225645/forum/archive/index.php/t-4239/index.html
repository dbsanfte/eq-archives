Fynepisa
The thing you gotta remember is that augments cost points also. IMO, it is a waste to buy either armor and not put the hp augment in the type 2 slot. This being said, the Tak armor starts to look a lot better if you make your basis constant HP.

Tak armor is 732 points more per piece for 15hp + ac + resists.

However, you can put 25hp augment in the type 2 slot for 510 points as opposed to 1150 for the 40hp. By doing this both the Tak armor and Mir armor have equal HP with Tak armor having additional AC and resists.

So now you have Tak armor with equal HP, and about 4ac and 6 resists more then Mir armor for only 92 extra points. Most people are willing to pay 510 points for 10ac at MMC, I don't see why you wouldn't pay 92 points for 4ac and 6 resists.

Going this route also gives you the option to upgrade to the 40hp augment if you so desire down the road without buying a completely new piece of armor and augment. You simply lose the 510 point 25hp augment.

Fynepisa

P.S. I'm not saying that putting 25hp augment in the Tak armor is better than just putting the 40hp into the Tak armor, but I think if your plan is to buy Mir armor and then put 40hp into it, you are better off buying Tak armor and putting 25hp into it.
