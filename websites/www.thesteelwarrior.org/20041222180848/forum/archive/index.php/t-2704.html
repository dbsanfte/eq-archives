Here is a nice way of fixing Warriors all around, all while making them funner to play, balanced, and without using spellish skills!!



Warriors get a new bar which replaces the mana bar... (it could also replace Sta bar, but would have to change Sta a lot, and remove zing spells).

This bar represents your energy, or willpower.

Warriors also get a new Skill, "Energy Surge" of however you wish to call it.
The skill enduces pure hate into the adversaries (thus best used after Taunts).
But each time you use this skill, it takes away 10% of your Willpower bar.
Hate induced would be around 300hate.

The Willpower bar will regenerate while you're seated, or simply not combatting.


Now disciplines are entirely unlinked. Each different disc will only keep reuse timers for themselves.
Meaning once defensive is used, you can immediately use another disc... but not defensive, until it's 10min timer has run out.

Now here is the important part.....

Each use of a Discipline will demand Willpower.
Using up 50%-30% of your Willpower bar.

-----------------------------------------------------


-This way, we can have knightish aggro at the cost of not being able to use a disc.
-Or, we can moderate our aggro, and be able to use Defensive or whatever disc is needed... using little "juice" to maintain aggro along with our EB weapons (this keeps the EB bonus from weapons, still very usefull).
-Or, if we're not tanking, we can go all out on our aggressive discs. Dealing decent dps.

In groups:

Since we hardly use defensive, we can choose to go full aggressive for more dps, using very little "juice" to maintain aggro.
Or a lot of our juice to hold some Knightish aggro.
But if you use too much, there's a point where the rest between pulls wont be enough regen, and youll either have to have a bit of downtime, or go back to using Taunt only (the way we're doing it now).

So most will use the Juice for initial aggro only. Giving Knights an advantage in the aggro departement. But a balanced advantage.


In Raids:

Defensive and Evasive are very important, thus Aggro Management still plays a very large role. And wizards cant go all out.
The warrior only has limited Juice, and most of it is used for those 2 discs.

Thus raids stay pretty much as is for tanking....
but Warriors that don't tank can go full dps!

We can also be pretty decent offtankers, able to keep hate without breaking mezzes, and a longer distance in aggro getting.


So this solution pretty much fixes all our problems, without stepping on Knights toes, and without overpowering us.
It puts us JUST where we want to be, and should be :).


And most of all, half of this solution (the juice part), is already rumored to be in SOE's line of idea.

At first I thought regen while not fighting was good.... but there would be problems with very long fights like in Vex Thal or Xegony.... where you can pull off 3 or 4 evasives.

So a small regen while fighting would be nice, one that regens you around 20% of your bar within 10mins.
Doubles if you're not fighting.

And even then, problems can arise, but need to test to see them.


As for Willpower mods and the such.
Why not? :)
Definately NOT use wis or int for it though. Stamina if anything, and Dex/Agi for monks and rogues if they get something similar.

As well as Flowing Juice items ;p.... can't hurt to add a few effects to items that amelliorate juice regen.
The balancing part here would be the hard part.

And no buffs for juice.
Buffs are meant to bring your character to higher levels depending on the classes that are with you....
you don't want your ability to use Disciplines or to get Aggro to have to depend on other classes do you? :)

Starting Willpower = 100

once Stamina goes over 100:
2 Stamina = 1 Willpower point.

You can put levels into this as well....
for a end result where a level 65 with maxxed Stamina has around 3 times the willpower of a level 1 with less then 100 stamina.


Amount of Willpower to use for a discipline : 90

Amount of willpower to use for a Energy Surge : 10

Willpower regeneration while fighting: 1/tick

Willpower regeneration while sitting down: 3/tick
