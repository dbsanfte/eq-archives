More accurately for warriors at lvl 65, it is 6.5 per stamina point up to 255, from 255 to 305 it is 3.25 per stamina point. If you have max hp aa's you multiply by 1.165.

So 65 with all hp aa's under 255 stamina = 6.5 x 1.165 = 7.5725 per pt
after 255 = 3.25 x 1.165 = 3.78625 per pt.

the 1.165 also applies to equipment with raw hp on it, so if you get a piece of equipment that is a 20 raw hp upgrade it's really a 20x1.165 = 23 hp upgrade.
