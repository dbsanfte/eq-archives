They removed the "mark read" function when they revamped the boards. Now it will mark a thread "read" if you view it for a certain amount of time, unfortunately that time is waaaaay too long, and you can't set it individually, its a boardwide setting. I'd ask that they reduce the "read" time to something like 5 seconds, thats often all it takes for me to determine if I need to read a thread or not, or even to read minor updates to a thread I've been watching.

Personally I wouldn't mind having the option to switch back to the "mark read" system. I often leave TSW up in the background while I play and read as I get time. With the current system I might miss updates to posts if I leave it up too long and it sets those threads as read due to having visited the boards. Or if I come, read half of the threads that are updated, and have to stop to raid, or do a hardcore group, or something IRL. By the time I come back those threads could be marked read.

Having tried both, I prefer marking threads read, rather than having some arbitrary timer decide it for me.
