As far as a physical number, it is a hard one. In the end you need ally. A Ghoul Lord Scalp will take you from Warmly to Ally, it did for my ogre. Easy camp and spawn of Ghoul Lord or Placeholder is every 28 minutes.

If your race is evil but cannot wear the scalp, I am pretty certain that a potion of illusion: Wood Elf can take you to ALLY from Warmly also. May be the quicker way, jusy buy one and find out.
For faction, just goto Sebilis with friends and fight until you recieve "Your faction standing with Froglocks of Guk could not possibly get any better".

The froglock reet crown is a pain in the a$$. My friend and I got one between 4 and 8 hours. We spent the next week (35-40 hours total) waiting for the 3rd to drop for my wife's halfling cleric. So, its pretty frickin random to say the least. High point is you can get other loot drops while down there, I got a Cobalt Bracer on the clearing of Mycos on the way to Reets/Juggs on the 4th day. Got a Cobalt helm that went to the Baz Trader on the 5th day. Undead dragon sinew for Spirit Wracked Cords from Juggs ( had about 8 of these drop in that week) . Made atleast 300pp per night per person off of jewel loots, and I outfitted my guild (casual guild) with over 70 spells lvl 57-60. There is something down there for everyone, and I pinged 3 aa's down there.

Goodluck and be patient. The WE night vision is superior to my ogres, and it screws with the noobs when I transform at will.
