Fitemore
WMBS is very good for so cheap. It has a delay of 28, which is the point where the 2 handed damage bonuses start increasing. If it had 27 delay, it would have a much lower damage bonus. Its DPS is very close to a Windblade, as I recall, and is generally much cheaper. Honestly, the droga combo won't be that bad of DPS at 65. The WMBS will beat it out, but not by much. Regardless, the WMBS will be a very decent DPS weapon for quite cheap.
