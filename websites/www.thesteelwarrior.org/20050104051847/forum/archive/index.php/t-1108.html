As for the reason you are not having a problem holding aggro is because its been found thru testing over at The Shaman's Crucible that proc's are figured in a proc/min fashion.. so haste/slow have no effect on the overall proc rate. The only thing that was found to affect proc rate is DEX.

Just say that the SoD is coded to proc 6 times /min.. assuming a constant DEX level. you will get 6 procs per min with no haste, some, haste, fully hasted. even slowed.
