Eriatha Egan
Damn boy, you almost have better shyte than I do!

-Weaponry:

Yeah, I'd try and win a GBoS from an open tower raid if I were you. That's one hell of a solid agro weapon, not to mention it does very good dps to boot. My fellow warrior guildies use the GBoS and while I snatch agro faster than they do, they *always* take it from me. GBoS + SoD all the way.

-Armor

The Strength of the Elements is a very solid and easy gear piece to work for. Phinney spawns every 12 hours and always drop the ore. You can't miss the ground spawn pickup in Skyfire. Guardian Kozzalym is hard to track, but he's duoable as long as you don't get dragon adds. Mind you though, you will. Hehe. With Yael gone, the ore from him now drops off the boss's guards. The only problem you'll have with that quest is dragging your friends out of LDoN and convincing them to help you for 5 minutes.

Helmet of Rallos Zek. You said you had the cash. Pick one up. This will easily last you well into Elemental Planes, so consider it one hell of a long term investment.

Kizrak Armguards. Check my magelo, these things are pretty spoogin'. At least on my server, they cost the least out of the ornate inventory.

You might as well pick up another Steel Ring of Earthen Resilience, but I'm sure those are undoubtedly rare in the bazaar. Do no be afraid to possibly quest the 10th Dain Ring as well. It's long, it's hard, but everyone I've talked to said it was well worth the investment time.

If you do HoT, try doing some of the quest out of there also. The thing is, SoE has itemized back slots to carry the same ac values as breastplates. Don't believe me? Check my magelo. What I'm trying to get at though is that you possibly lose a lot of ac by wearing your haste item in your back slot. Quite conveniently, there's a quest out of HoT that results in a 38ac 100hp 41% haste bracer. It's hard, but warriors aren't supposed to be easy. Do the time for that one and you'll be wearing it well into the elemental planes.

Judging by your neck item, it looks as if you've done NToV with other guilds. Lady Nevedaria, probably one of the hardest dragons in NToV, drops a spoogin' 35ac 12sta 100hp cloak + clicky called Cloak of Thorns. I wouldn't count on that one any time soon though. If you're raiding with another guild, it'll probably go to one of them. If you don't have access to that, then the Loyalist Shield of Honor is a viable alternative. That one is 50ac 15str 15wis 75hp and can be done with a single group.

***EDITED***

Keep in mind though, with LDoN, there are a TON of upgrades for warriors that have yet to be found. Play the game, wait it out, save your points, build some AA, then choose upgrades that are going to upgrade you the most.

wandor
Looks like your biggest upgrades are going to come from helm, earring, bracers, and ring. Get yourself a helm of RZ, another Bloodmetal ear, and another Earth ring. If there is a chance you can get a ToV quest bracer, go for it, it opens up your back slot for all kinds of upgrades. If that is not an option, I would spend the plats for 2x Crystallized Acid bracers. You have enough to be able to afford a couple now depending on the market on your server.

As for your weapons I would do 2 things -

Keep trying for a GBoS, solid agro and DPS weapon.

I would also do what a lot of people on this board consider heresy - I would sell/trade the SoD and replace it with a BoC.

By switching out to GBoS main/BoC off, you will get an increase in agro, DPS, and stats. You really can't go wrong with that set up.

Some marginal upgrade that I would also look for (to loot, not to buy) - Kizrak BP and legs, Boots of the Strom, and either Velium encrusted or new LDoN Warlord gaunts (bag the Kizrak's for the effect).
