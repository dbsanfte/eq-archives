I found this info and got this drop the other day so I thought I would share it as I think this is a good aggro proc and a good alternative to doing lots of LDoN's for an Ykesa proc.

In Bloodfields there is a mob called an ukun slaughterhound that is a PH for the Domineering Ukun. The uncommon drop off the Domineering Ukun is a stone called a Bloodstone of Agony. It is a type 4 aug with a Bite of Ykesha proc, which is a 50 dmg 0 sec stun proc. Not quite as good as the LDoN versions, but close and should have the same aggro. The common drop is an ok cloak. We got 3 cloaks before getting the aug, I would say we probably spent four or five hours before getting the aug.

To get to the mob from the DS zone in just run to the first open courtyard and just past the archway there is the first open area. In the first open area will be the wandering ukun slaugtherhound, he will wander across both sides of the path. Just keep pulling him until the named Domineering pops, probably about 1 in 4-5 or so.

There is one other named in the general area, the Ikaav Slumberer, and the miragenist is the PH for it. So you can pull both of those, the Ikaav Slumberer drops a decent clr/dru/shm belt as the common and a nice +2 dmg shield aug as the uncommon. During the four to five hours there we also got the +2 dmg shield aug as well as the bite of ykesha proc.
