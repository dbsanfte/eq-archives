Copied from EQ Forums:

Based on this thread and the one on TSW and talking to people in-game, the following "list" is the primary concerns/suggestions of the casual warrior (note: I'm trying to retain a warrior-centric list, since this isn't a "casual" thing, but a warrior thing)

1. LDoN armors: While augmentable, Royal Attendant gear is not geared toward warriors, but a generic cookie cutter "best plate" armor available. Would like to see more class-oriented class-specific gear available through less raid intensive means.

2. Mid end raid content and availability: Weapons like Bloodfrenzy, Hategiver, and Blade of Carnage are wonderful additions, but are rather limited in availability due to the "public" nature of the mobs who drop them. While content like Time isn't "contested", and the availability of Rallos Zek isnt' a factor in attaining a Darkblade, simply being able to beat him and getting the item on the spawn table is, the casual players don't have that luxury. All of the spawns capable to be defeated by more casual guilds ARE contested, and are only available to a single raid force per spawn cycle. With an ever increasing pool of people capable of defeating these encounters, the likelihood of finding it up and available is less and less. It was bad for instance in Luclin when 3-4 guilds were fighting over Cursed, nowdays 20+ guilds are able to kill him per server. The potential solutions are as follows:

A. Instanced "Raid Content" which ensures that the defeat of encounters is the roadblock and not the availability of contested spawns.

B. "Raid Content" which matches the difficulty and the Rewards of Velious/Luclin era content, but removes the availability factor.

C. Sticking with the plan to itemize tiers 1-3 in Planes of Power, to review and add items, and increase the value of items found there. (Of particular interest is the potential itemization of Saryrn's Tower and all of the Plane of Torment, a ghost town currently)

D. Reintroduction of Fabled mobs to the game. They breathed new life into many old zones, life that has been deflated again.

E. EXPANSION of Fabled type mobs. Those were great ideas, and "Fabled" Kunark and Velious type mobs would also be a good idea.

F. Mid level gear in new content shouldn't be lesser than middle upper level gear from previous content (i.e. Bertoxxolouss shoudln't have lesser quality gear than Vyzh Drah the Cursed or Exiled).

3. There's a massive weapons shortage in Gates of Discord prior to Uqua+ content.

4. Place Zoneouts in ALL zones. If there's a "safe spot" there should also be a zone out. Warriors shouldn't have to be at risk by being trapped into zones like Sol Ro Tower. While gate potions and necks are convenient, they shouldn't be necessary during the normal course of play.

5. Alternate Key Quest into Sol Ro Tower.

6. Alternate Key Quest into Elementals.
