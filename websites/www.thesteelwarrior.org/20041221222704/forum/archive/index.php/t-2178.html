SoE wants more people to use shields. Warriors want to be able to hold taunt better.

So here is the idea I had (if its a repeat, I apologize)...

Add a new weapon skill for warriors (when introduced put the skill at the level cap for the warrior so they don't have to spend 3 weeks skilling it up) called "Shield". This skill would be similar to other weapon skills: slashing, piercing, blunt. Give all warrior equipable shields a delay, base damage off of AC the way the Bash skill currently does. Unlike the Bash skill however, the innate ability of "Shield" or "Offhand Bashing" would not be a chance to stun but an aggro increaser, guaranteed every swing and based off the delay (similar to the 2handed damage bonus).

In this format, you would allow the non-tanking warrior on a raid to use a 2hander or 2 1handers for damage, but the main tank would equip a shield for the added taunt value.

This could also be designed to give the warrior a bonus to block based on his skill in shield while having a shield equipped.

Anyway, that's it... thoughts?
