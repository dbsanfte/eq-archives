The 55 character will gimp the difficulty.

I 3-boxed a 65 war, 65 cleric, 58 shammy. The mobs hit for significantly less than a normal difficulty all-65 adventure (200ish max). XP was worse, and a win only got me 34 points. So it really wasn't worth it.

On the bright side, the cash was nice. I did get some augs (but they were also gimped by the difficulty of the encounter - +5's). Having a zone all to yourself is nice.

The big challenge for you will be pulling. If you can figure out how to pull singles, or can handle more than 1 mob at a time, you'll be fine.
