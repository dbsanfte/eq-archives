Looking pretty good there, especially for a scalely warrior. My suggestions.

Ears: 2ac/35hp (black sapphire electrum), or save up and do an earring of the solstice. (Prices are constantly dropping, pick up what parts you can when you find a deal.)
Neck: This can be campable with a partner or two in the grey, just make sure you have enduring breath. Clattering Bone necklace (6ac, 6sta/str, 30hp)
Wrist: I'd swap out the bracer of scavenging with silver chitin wristband (14ac, 7str/dex, 40hp) or quest a bracer of benevolence (15ac, 8sta/str, 30hp, 12sv mag/cold, 5sv fire). By the looks of your armor, you CoV faction should be good to do it.
Shoulders: Do the draconic pauldrons quest. (15ac, 12sta/sta, 6 sv fire/cold)
Legs: Keep camping (or buying MQs) KD and aim for champ's greaves or take a group to veksar and camp the Kylong shin guards. (35ac, some str/sta and 45hp)
