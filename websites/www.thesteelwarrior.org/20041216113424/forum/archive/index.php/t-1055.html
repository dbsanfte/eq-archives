If you are on COV faction, I am assuming you are in good standings with Coldain Dwarves as well and I would suggest working on the Coldain Ring quests. You can see the result of the 9th quest in my profile. Most of the quests can be done with 2 people, you and some sort of healer. I did rings 1-8 in a weekend and 9th took me a week or so off and on. If you don't want to involve a lot of people, go to Kael and politely ask a guild doing platehouse cycle if you can help kill mobs in return for the dwarf head needed from there. Most people could use another tank that doesn't want armor from there and most times the head rots.
