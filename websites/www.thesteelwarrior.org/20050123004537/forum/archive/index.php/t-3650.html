AC above stats until you are "uba" right?
Not beyond a certain point. You probably don't need 600 AC at your level. Sure, it doesn't hurt to have it, but you're getting diminishing returns beyond a certain point, and if you're sacrificing something else just to get AC, you may be hurting yourself.

HP's (STA) are always helpful - the more the better once you've got your AC to a good number for your level.

I would not recommend going out to fight with your Dex below 75. I would be reallly surprised if you got many skill increases with a Dex of 70 for one thing, and once you get a procing weapon you definitely want it above 100.

If you can sacrifice a little AC and get your AGI above 100 and your DEX to 100 or close to it, I think you'll be better served. There appear to be 'break points' with stats - 75 is definitely one - anything below that and you're getting what boils down to a negative effect. For example - drop your AGI below 75 and you will see your AC drop precipitously. 100 has always appeared to be another one. Getting a stat to 101 seems to make a bigger difference than getting it to 98.

The Glowing Black Sword I mentioned earlier would still serve you adequately as an off-hander and has +20 dex.
