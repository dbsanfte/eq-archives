Originally posted by aeternum
Just curious, but I'm wondering why I need to train all my weapon skills? I get the feeling that this is a flammable topic, but why can't I get by with just dual wielding 1hb and 1hs?

Hm. Ok well I'm not sure how the mechanics of 1handers vs 2handers works in the level 20-50 or so range. They've changed quite a few things since I was playing a melee of that level.

In any case, there are quite a few reasons to have your 2h weapon skills maxed too. At higher up levels, 2handers will usually do significantly more damage than fairly equivalent 1handers (by equivalent I mean difficulty to obtain). 2handers are also nice to cut down ripostes, keeping aggro off you if you don't want it, and prevent you from killing yourself on a nasty damage shield.

You also never know what the next nice warrior weapon will be (ie like CHoS/Sotb). It could be 2hb or 2hp. IMO you should keep your 1hs, 1hb, 2hs, and 2hb up. You can let piercing slack I suppose if you want, because there aren't many good piercers and the skill is easy to raise quick if you do happen to get a good piercer.

Seriously though..... why limit youself to just 1hb/1hs?

You have to actually move the weapons to equip them. Simply clicking on them won't do it.
