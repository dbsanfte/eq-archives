Boots ... 10 AC is kinda low. Cobalt would give you a lot more. You just lose the stats.

Shoulders ... 7 AC is kind of low. Popular items are Imbued Granite Spaulders or Cultural armor, and either option would give you a lot more AC.

Gloves ... your haste item is there. A lot of people have Cobalt there. Again, tons more AC.

Sleeves ... 16 AC versus the 25 AC of yup, you guessed it, COBALT.

Cobalt lacks the stat boosts of some of the stuff you're wearing, but since you asked about AC, and since boots, vambraces and gloves are really cheap cobalt items, I just thought I'd use them as an example of where to gain AC.

Helmet ... well, Cobalt or the much better Raldukan Helm.

In ANY EVENT, you are a barbarian who worships the Tribunal. You are now over level 45. Disregard Cobalt.

Go for your cultural Helanic armor. The AC is far superior to many of your items. And will have oodles of STA and Hit Points, and just totally put you where you want your stats to be as a warrior in his or her 50s.
