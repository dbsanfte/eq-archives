over aggro is not an issue on time tz..assuming you tank him properly. First of all 3 of his aes are 30 range the 4th if 50 range, so the only one that will land at max melee ect. When i tank im i make sure i have full buffs ect all 15 slots and trash up front. so jboots acumen cotr aura of green petals legacy of bracken vot divine intervention protection of seasons, tribunal, pot9, symbol, ward, brells ,focus, haste. The one ae you need to worry about as mt is tallons balance. Its neg 300 all resists and will most likely allow his other shit to land, slow aggro debuff ect. So what i do is group with a bard and have him mem high level dispell song. With full buffs his aes eat your top buffs, and bard dispell is top 2 slots only. It will occasionally skip a buff but very rarely. So I work with a bard to dispell that shit the whole fight and fill in the blankks with clickies. You can also work with a rune rotation or vie ect depending on how sloppy your dispelling gets. If they are top two slots youre fine, as long as they arnt eating something importatnt. I dont place them lower because they longest they last is 3 seconds and then you have a threat of his slow or debuff dropping lower in your buff order and you are fucked until it fades. Anyway doing this i have no problem aggroing and we normally have him to 25%ish before disc drops, then just fill in the blanks with druids and shaman.

edit: dispelling his resist debuff will also save spike dps as he has a 3k ae dd and a 3k single target dd.
