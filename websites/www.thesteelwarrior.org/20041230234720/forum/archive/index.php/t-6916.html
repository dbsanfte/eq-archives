Minimum damage on a mob hit is Damage bonus + 1*Damage Interval, it is never anything else. You can test this by putting on all your armor, and letting a greenie beat on you. Take your armor off and you will see that he hits you the same percentage of the time. If you could mitigate a hit to zero, this would not be the case with increased AC.
