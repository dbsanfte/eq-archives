Tarn Rider
I've done Vxed with no chanter or mezzer what so ever, this was done on my Main, a Ranger.

The gear was Elemental, Time Gear, and some minor GoD gear for most people.

The group make up was a Warrior, Cleric, Shaman, Ranger(Me), Monk, Necro.

The monk pulled Single Pulls each time, since with 6 hours it certainly isn't a rush rush type of thing.

The killing of the 50 was a bit annoying, without the added Charmed DPS, but we did alright, and mana seemed to be fine. Generally kill ten, have a brief rest for Cleric's mana, and keep going.

Once we killed the 50 to spawn the hail NPC, then the Monk & Necro did their things.

With a combination of Invis & Feign Death, the Monk & Necro made their way up to the room. The Necro would FD, the monk would tag several mobs ahead, draw them down the path, the necro would jump up running, and move further up. Clearing the way for one another.

The Necro got to the room, and the monk was stuck part way down from the last pull of mobs to give the Necro a clear shot.

So then we basically all went and found a mob and killed ourselves. We zoned back in. On the walli n the last room, high on the wall, to the side of the tombstone exit, there is a safe spot. you can stand there, not moving much and be agro free. the necro summoned the body of the Cleric, used an EE to rez the cleric. Then summoned the rest of our bodies, and the cleric clicked us.

We did our hails, then quickly clicked the tombstone.

It may not of been the most efficient, but it worked for getting people their flags.

Again, this was in Vxed.
