Bashiae
If you're looking to solo for cash, your bet is probably going to be some highly unpopulated greenie zone with plat dropping mobs. Perhaps City of Mist, Kaesora, or even Kurn's Tower for Kunark level mobs (don't hit hard, have relatively low HP, can pull massive numbers of them without worry)? I don't know off hand which actual zones/mobs have good plat dropping mobs unfortunately, so I'm making educated guesses rather than certain answers.

Crystal Caverns is another good place I believe; lots of lower level mobs packed together than can get you plat and random vendor loot. You might also look into some Luclin lowbie zones, like Paludal Caverns perhaps. A few LoY zones are pretty good for farming cash too, but the mobs there tend to hit a bit harder on average than earlier expansion mobs, so it depends on your setup and weapons and such.

If you use this method, just remember to take full advantage of your discs (furious and a high damage two hander is really good for large trains when farming greens) and of course the new healing disc is quite handy for soloers now, with a reuse of 5 minutes.

Good luck,

-Bashiae-
