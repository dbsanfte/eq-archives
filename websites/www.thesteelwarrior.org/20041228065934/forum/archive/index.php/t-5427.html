This is the right place to ask about the Twilight armor, but IMO it's targeted at the 30-40 crowd. Unless there is some specific piece you'd like to get, it's probably not worth your effort as there are a lot of pieces to collect and, in my experience, the time/risk/reward just isn't there.

(/set WaxingOpinionatedMode ON)

I think the original idea for the Twilight Armor was to accomplish a couple of goals:

1) get mid levels up to Luclin for Phat Lewt - the stuff is arguably better than Sol Ro for the classes that get them, leading to

2) give >>all<< the classes at the Sol Ro Armor levels (30-40) some armor quests (monks and druids didn't get Sol Ro armor at all, and even the Rogue armor of the line was dropped not quested.) Oh, and have armor for all slots - Sol Ro is mostly visible pieces and a weapon or two.

3) get mid levels working together - the drops are all over the place and most servers started a "Looking for Twilight Armor..." forum on their server boards.

Bottom line tho, it's a couple of expansions later, better is LDoN era available from player smiths or AP purchase (4 sets from Adventure merchants - top end rivalling Elemental I believe) and no one really does them so you'll literally spend weeks getting the drops.

Unless you're a total quest junkie, and have friends who you like to group with in odd zones of Luclin no one goes to (well, mostly) and want to fight never ending greens - have at the Twilight Armor. Great idea, good (but not great) at the time but showing it's age lately.

I'd go for Thurg/Kunark armor if your guild is going that route, and work towards Skyshrine. If your more of a loner like me, might be best to keep on those LDoN's and get some points.

And definitely ask in the Gear forums, wiser heads than mine frequent there - Good Luck!
