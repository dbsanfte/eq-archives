Shield Disc is not an inefficient way to generate threat. You can get off 2 Shield Bashes within the duration of Disc. Since Disc increases threat generated by Shield Bash by 100%, that is effectively like getting 4 bashes for the price of 3.5 (10 + 10 + 15) AND you get the added bonus of all that hate being done in half the time. Since Shield Bash is considered by many to be one of the best threat generating skills we have, I would say that is a significant benefit.

The rest of the benefits provided by Shield Disc are questionable (a bit more damage dished out, a bit less taken), but it is misleading to say that it is inefficient at gaining threat.
