Heres a tactic thats served me well.
#1 Kill all the killable guys in the level. All minions etc
#2 Play cat and mouse.
Snipe him, hit and run. Shoot then fade to the next room.
Eventually you will be back at the zone in if you do things right.
#3 Hit as hard as possible then zone out.
Did you know you get a full heal when leaving a mission? :)
As soon as your END regens a bit go back in, and waylay the boss on the
way back to his spawn point, he'll be calmly walking back.
This works even better in missions with elevators. Nothing follows you thru an
elevator/door to the outside.

I play a defender so the basic tactics should work the same for a blaster.
