If you have Gates, take a group and go to the arena in Riwwi. One of the Pixxt that spawn will path through the arena, a female with the lower torso of a snake. She can drop some dang fine greaves.
The stats as best I recall:
38ac, 70hp/mana/end, some str/dex/int, attk 20, regen 2 and assorted saves.
Plate classes only.
VERY nice greaves and single groupable. I think they are called Hardened Serpentscale Greaves. I had these myself, but gave them away as I had slightly better.

If you feel like goofing off in natimbi, camp a Shell Inlaid Collar or buy it for too much in the bazaar.
It is also past time for the indo arms to go! Something as simple as unimbued brellium vambraces will hold you over til you can camp better or buy something from ldon.
Also, if you do hards, try Everfrost for the mana threaded bone bracelet, or keep an eye out in the bazaar.
Your offhand won't net you much aggro. Use your SotBS or CHoS. Oh, and put your CoF back on. ;)
