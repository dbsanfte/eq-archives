This is more of a warning and tips for how to cr if the warning isn't heeded. There is a new mob in Fear. It is a large imp that has 9 imps surrounding it. The main imp has a dr based stun memblur that is very hard to resist and can wipe out a guild that doesn't have high dr or bards. Here is the kicker. The imp is located on the west wall everyone used for breaking in.

Now for how to cr

I was asked by a friend in a family type guild to help recover his corpse. I ran in with a small raid of casual gamers and my cleric friend (we are both somewhat elemental geered) While he and i managed to survive by sheer dumbluck 9this was the only time sheer dumbluck worked as we took a few deaths trying out strategies) and my wizard bot's fast evac trigger finger, the rest of the raid wiped with no way of retrieving the corpses as any attempt to move from the hill triggered the imp and its 9 mobs. After about 2 hours of trying different things i decided to use the NE corner of the hill near where the long thin tree is and kill wanderers there. It is best to use a rogue with sos as he can melee stuff and if your recovery party dies you can go trigger the imp's ae and hid while you're stunned and memblurred.

In all it took about 1 hour to recover 40 or so corpses in this manner but three to come up with a decent strategy that worked. It would have been better if i had a full group of equipped people but it was late at night (3ish mtn time) and the 6 deaths i took came from the fact my cleric had no gear we had 0 dps except for myself and scarlings suck.


Hope this helps.
