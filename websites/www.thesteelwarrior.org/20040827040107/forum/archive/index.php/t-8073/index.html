Ciae Redemption
You can replace people in sewer groups using the /dzadd and /dzremove commands, moving people in and out of your expedition, at any time. There *are* objectives that are supposed to be done, but there's no time limits unless you get to that part of the expedition and deliberately trigger it, there's no win/loss record or a leaderboard, so you can go in and grind all you like. Mobs respawn, and can respawn as named if you don't get any to spawn once you kill 20 (Sewers instance named only spawn - as either a named or a PH - when you either kill 20 mobs or 30 minutes passes. Vxed used to be like this, but when they reduced the # of kills for a win from 100 to 50, the # of kills before named spawn was also cut in half to 9-10.). Groups don't go to sewers all that often, so if you're forming a group and looking for people, look around for clerics/druids in upper tier guilds still missing their yiktu geomancy stones (Holy Light is **huge** for a cleric) and just about anyone that are working on the BiC quest and still need parts from sewers. BiC is the long, long gates quest with a few pieces from each zone - since sewer groups are rare, I'd imagine many people on your server still need pieces from there; I know I do. Even if you can't find people to join that are looking for stuff, the xp isn't half bad.
