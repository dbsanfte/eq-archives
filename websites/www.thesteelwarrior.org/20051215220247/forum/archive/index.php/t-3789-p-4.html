Trying to work out the relative hate effects of damage bonus and weapon damage.

I am avoiding ascribing any fixed amount of hate to ToT casts, as people have mentioned the hate generated isn't always linear in the number of casts.

We have a test where the weapon delay, hence the damage bonus, is constant, while the weapon damage varies. This is the 12/50 and 22/50 test.

So, I can do some algebra on this.

SmallHate = hate from 12/50 weapon per swing.
LargeHate = hate from 22/50 weapon per swing.
SKHate = amount of hate the SK built up that the weapon had to beat.

From the 12/50 weapon tests we have:
34 SmallHate >= SKHate >= 33 SmallHate
From the 22/50 weapon tests we have:
30 LargeHate >= SKHate >= 29 LargeHate

Now, we know LargeHate is more hate than SmallHate. Call the difference in hate Y, and renamed the small hate to X.

Then,
SmallHate = X
LargeHate = X + Y

Rewording
34 X >= SKHate >= 33 X
30 X + 30 Y >= SKHate >= 29 X + 29 Y

Rearranging, and making the inequalities strict:
34 X > 29 X + 29 Y
30 X + 30 Y > 33 X
Or
5 X > 29 Y
30 Y > 3 X

Y is the hate caused by the 10 extra damage, and X is the hate caused by the 12/50 weapon per swing.

Ok, now lets try out the two theories.

The first one is that every point of damage adds a certain amount of hate, and every point of damage bonus.

Call the amount of hate per damage D, and the amount of hate per damage bonus B.

Then X = 51 B + 12 D and Y = 10 D.

Using:
5 X > 29 Y
30 Y > 3 X

255 B + 60 D > 290 D
300 D > 153 B + 36 D

255 B > 230 D
153 B < 264 D

0.9 D < B < 1.73 D

So, the hate from a point of damage bonus would be anywhere from 90% to 173% of the hate from the "weapon damage" field.

The other theory is that aggro is steppy. There is the hate from the damage bonus, and a constant amount of hate that goes up as you break certain thresholds.

Let T be the hate caused by a 5 to 14 damage weapon, and 2 T be the hate caused by a 15 to 24 damage weapon.

X = 51 B + T
Y = T

Using:
5 X > 29 Y
30 Y > 3 X

We get:
255 B + 5 T > 29 T
30 T > 153 B + 3 T

255 B > 24 T
153 B < 27 T

Rewording as T = K B.

10.625 > K > 5.67

Someone thought that K might be 11 (ie, 11 "hate" for a 12 damage weapon and 22 "hate" for a 22 damage weapon, and 1 hate per point of damage bonus). This is inconsistent with experiment.

I'm definatly going to set up some controlled experiments.

A> 14 damage offhander vs 15 damage offhander, determine if there is a hate step based off min damage (or discontinuity).
B> "one more hate spell" comparisons with the same weapon, to see if we find a conversion value from +hate spell effects and our unconstrained weapon hate.
C> Compare hate from same-damage, different bonus, weapons.
D> More comparisons of hate from same-bonus, different-damage weapons. (vs same amount of hate)
E> Some 1 H primary weapon tests.
