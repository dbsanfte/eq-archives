Maelstrom of Ro (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3486&source=Live) = 45
Kilva's Skin of Flame (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1331&source=Live) = 33
Shield of Pain (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3608&source=Live) = 10
Blessing of Vah Kerrath (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2247&source=Live) = 8
Spikecoat (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=518&source=Live) = 4
Psalm of Veeshan (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3368&source=Live) = 15 * 3.9 = 58
Warsong of Zek (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3374&source=Live) = 15 * 3.9 = 58
Mark of the Righteous (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3477&source=Live) = 34
Spirit of the Wood (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3279&source=Live) = 55
Wrath of the Wild (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3257&source=Live) = 650

So you can have a 955 point damage shield for one hit, a 305 point damage shield for 5 ticks after that, and a 250 point damage shield from then on. You need Puresteel Mantle (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=13690), Talisman of Vah Kerrath (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=8364), Girdle of Living Thorns (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=2510) or Gleaming Bauble (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=32024), a bard with Blaring Horn of Fire (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=27996) and the right AAs, and a druid with the right AAs though.
