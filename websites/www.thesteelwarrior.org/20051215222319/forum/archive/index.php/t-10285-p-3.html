I'm not sure about the lag thing. I can play all hours of the day and not experience any noteworthy lag. I do know that what many people label lag is actually bad framerate. With all the character moving around in the AH during peak hours, you are going to need a bad ass system to display it smoothly. Thats on every online game when lots of unique avatars are taking up memory space, slowing down your framerate.

You will not be able to move under those circumstances. However, if you are experiencing the real lag, data tranfer delays, you can move all you want to around in the gameworld. Just like in EQ, if you unplug your internet connection while running across a zone, you will continue to run. The client is still going to try and send posistion updates to the server, because it doesn't wait for a server response.

If you can't move, you're having performance issues with your client, not lag. Lag is fighting a mob and having it warp or run across the screen to catch up with its position updates from the server, looting a corpse and having to wait for the item to be moved to your inventory, or having a fight stop responding mid battle with no damage updates.

If you want baby smooth and consistant framerates in an online game. You are dreaming. If designers keep making games with a set amount of memory that they are going to use for textures, optimize performance and system requirments around an average value of avatars to be rendered, and then give each avatar unique and varied textures, and then let all those player congragate in the same area, you are going to have performance issues. Its why upgrading your video card improved eq more than upgrading your connection. Just like it is in AO, DAoC, SWG, Horizons, Second Life,...etc. If your having these types of performance issues, you do what you can, but otherwise you are outta luck.

Personally, I have very little issues with only 1GB ram, ati 9800, and 1.2Ghz. I consider that a bare minimum now days for a new premium game. If you want to whine about performance go ahead, but saying that they should do something about it is another thing. My systems specs are barely above recommended specs, and I have little to no issues with the game.

I played on hellscream for a while, to see how bad it is, and it WAS bad. However since the upgrades, I haven't had any problems outta all the times I've been playing an alt on that server while my main one was down. Which was a huge improvement for that server. I hate that things didn't go so well for my server, but I'm confident that blizz will deliver, and refund when they don't. Fanboi or not, this game has the best customer service I've even seen on an online game. I've had a few tickets for various issues, mostly player disruption, and they were handle by the GM staff within an hour. I've played a hell of a lot of online games, nearly every major one on the market, but never have I had such responsive support.
