Here's another, more general test for how to tell if you are on target with your AC/hps/gear. Pay attention when you join a pick-up group that has a couple of players you've grouped with before. If in greeting you they say things like, "Woot! Now we have a tank!" You're good to go. If, on the other hand, part way through a session you start getting tells from your cleric like, "You like leading with your chin, don't you?" Then it's time to upgrade.
More directly, if you're grouping with guildmate healers who are experienced players, simply ask them whether, relatively speaking, it's hard to keep you alive compared to other tanks.
Here are three things that are always true about experienced/decent healers:
1. You are always naked before your healer. You cannot hide much from them.
2. An experienced healer will always know more about your relative mitigation than you do.
3. Your healer is always right when he says he needs a med break.
