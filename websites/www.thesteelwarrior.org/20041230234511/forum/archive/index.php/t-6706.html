04-16-2004, 02:35 PM
I use EQC to parse guild total damage done on boss mobs and it works fairly well. Just create a new chat box and make it really small. Divert all non-melee damage and others hits to this box so it can be captured in your logs but wont spam you out in your main chat boxes.

Doing this a melee can get a very good idea of total damages of other melees and of nuke casters. You won't be able to parse the dps from dots or from pet procs though but it is better than nothing.

You only pick up other hits and non melee hits when you are closer to the mob's hitbox, not to the person doing the damage. The best parser would be the MT. On mobs that AE rampage or where you stay far back for prox aggro, you cannot accurately parse others even if they are standing next to you. You can parse rangers and casters even if they are 200-300 range away from you as long as you are right at the mob.
