In addition to the crystals, you get a token to turn in for faction when you complete a mission. If you have your characters on opposite faction, then only one or the other of them will be able to get faction on any given mission. Since faction is a big part of the DoN progression quest, I would definitely suggest having both of your characters on the same faction. Once you pick a side, you can start missions and your kos character can do the missions and get faction tokens for the other side. You can turn them in at the Mercenary camp outside to get faction until you are no longer kos. There are also solo factions at the mercenery camps that you can do to raise faction with either side. Took me 3 missions and 2 tasks to get out of kos with the good camp.

The token you get at the end of a mission appears on your curser. If you deceide to go good faction, take it to Mercenary Guard Youra near SOlB entrance and turn it in to her. 2: You can start doing missions right away. In fact this is a good source of faction.

3: You get 2 rewards for completing most missions. Crystals, with which to purchase things from the DoN merchants, and a faction token, which you turn in for faction(and I forget the name of offhand).

4: If by crystals you mean the faction token, then yes. Mercenary Guard Youra is indif to all, and takes the tokens to boost good faction. I am unsure of the person to turn in for evil faction.
