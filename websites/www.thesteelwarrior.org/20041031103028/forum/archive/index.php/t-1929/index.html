Looking at your magelo I reckon you've got pretty decent gear as far as whats available tradeable / MQable. Once you have ND3 you'll be sitting over 4k which I would say is a respectable amount for a non raiding warrior or warrior just starting the NToV / Velious "ubers" level. I was in a similar boat when PoP came out being light on HPs so I stuck it out at level 60 till I had ND3 under my belt and figure it was worth doing getting groups easier in PoD rather than getting known as a paper tank in Tier 2.

The only spot that really jumps out at me for being cheap and easy to upgrade is the boots - Fine heraldic would score you another 12hps and 5AC.

With a bit more cash some other upgrades you could plan for:
Weapons - neither of your weapons has HPs on it, so maybe planning for a fleshgrinder or Horn of Hsagra. These might be too big a sacrifice on agro for you though.

2 New tradeable weapons from the revamped Droga could be good options and have procs that should generate some taunt, so keep an eye out for:

Here is what I would suggest. Grind like there is no tomorrow for the next 3-4 weeks. This should be enough time to get at least ND3 if you play fairly regularly (if you play a lot, 4 weeks is enough time to get upwards of 40AA so ND3 should be cake.)

Try to grind in Droga, ChardokB, or Veksar. There are some pretty nice drops coming out of those zones that would be upgrades for you.

While you are grinding away, play the market as much as you can. You are going to need some PP at the end of your grindage. Save every copper you can. You might consider selling some easy profit items - like celestial essense (player made like 90% profit) to help build up some pp.

When you finish your grind-a-thon, you should be sitting pretty good at around 4400hp unbuffed. Ornate molds are dropping like rain in the Karanas now. The best zones require flags (Tactics and Sol Ro) but Bastion of Thunder seems to be decent as well. At 4400hp you should be able to tank BoT well enough to move your grinding there. If you are lucky you can win a drop or 2. Any drop will do since you can trade/sell for plate drops.

Start watching the baz in the next few weeks. Right now, ornate is still pretty expensive since everyone wants to be the first to get the armor. However, I can pretty much guarantee that the market is going to be flooded very soon. On my server it's not unusual to see 40+ people in Sol Ro at 1am in the morning. People are farming the living shit out of that zone. Some for twinks, some for plats, but it accomplishes the same thing. The price for ornate molds is going to sink like the Titanic. You can also bet that this trend will drag down the prices of BD cultural and other nice tradeable items including player made jewelry.

Some items you should start watching for:
Mosquito leg earrings
any ornate plate molds
bloodmetal earrings
veksar shinguards

As far as your weapons are concerned, sell that wavecrapper while it is still worth something. I would sell the frosty as well, but if you are worried about agro, keep that for offhand use. There are a ton of quality weapons you can get with some HP or STA that will be much better for you.

Some nice weapons:
Horn of Hsagra
Swiftblade of Zek
Shortblade of Lightning
Fleshgrinder
Razorfang club
Disemboweler

Hang in there a few weeks, upgrades should be a lot more affordable with all of the new items coming out of the new and upgraded zones.
