If you are quick with buttons while a mob is incomming right in front of you -

-Click Spirit of Rage
-Incite
-Click off Spirit of Rage

-Wait for mob to get in melee range

-Click Furious
-Furious ends, click Defensive or Evasive, depending on the mob

I've done this and it seems to work pretty well. Then again, agreeing with you, just standard Incite+Furious tends to be a good enough edge for starting a boss encounter.
