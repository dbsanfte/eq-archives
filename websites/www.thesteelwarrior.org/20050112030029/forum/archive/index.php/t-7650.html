I suggest you save your money for awhile and wait how prices will develop until you are in a level range where items with recommended level become interesting. Until then you are good enough in any way but weapons. You might want to get one of each kind to keep skills up.

As for expansions, that depends on what you plan to do. LDoN is good for groups and getting some good gear without raids. PoP is now, with the opening of PoK to all, something for xp in higher levels. LoY is more mid level but not that popular for anything but increased bank space.

If you look out there might be some bundles to get multiple expansions (all but GoD) for a decent price.
