I went up to PoJ the other night looking for an exit group. There was only 1 and they were full. So I plopped down in the other corner while I checked to see if any guildies had a spot for me. A monk came up and asked if I wanted to start a group with him.

We made the group and he said he needed a quick afk. So I start looking for a healer and a slower. Do a /who cleric and find a 51 out in tranquility. Send a tell asking if she'd like to join us and she said sure. Cool. Do a /who chanter. Nada. /who shaman. Nada again. Just then I see an /ooc for a 50 chanter. Get her to come join us. Cleric, chanter, monk, and me...good start.

Now for some dps. Checked for every class under the sun. Everyone is 60+ or in the 30s. I see a 53 war shout he's lfg and pick him up. And I notice the cleric still isn't here. Do a /who all for the cleric. She is still in tranquility and its been about 15 minutes. Send another tell asking if she is still coming. She says she is. Ok, try to hurry, everyone is here.

Everyone asks their friends if they need a spot. Nobody is on yet. Cleric is finally in PoJ. Wait about 5 more minutes. Cleric is finally at the exit. Invite her and wait for her to start buffs. She cast Temp on herself and everyone else said they were good on buffs.

Quick mana check and the cleric doesn't answer. We figure she has to be FM, she only cast 1 buff. Send the other warrior out to pull 1. He says he has 1 rat inc. Cleric starts screaming she isn't ready. Kinda late now isn't it?

Chanter jumped the gun bad. Kick hadn't even refreshed and she tashed. I tried to /shield her but I musta been half a step out of range. We get agro back and start beating the rat down. The other warrior is at about 25% health and the rat isnt slowed. Cleric still isn't doing anything. I get lucky and finally pull agro.

We get the rat down to around 25% and i tell the cleric to root the rat before it can run. She says that rooting isn't her job. WTF?!?! I tell her that if she doesn't, we are going to get 10 adds and wipe if she doesn't root it right the hell now. She replies that she didn't have root memmed and we pulled when she wasn't ready.

Rat runs at 20% health. We chase it hoping we can catch a lucky streak of crits. We didn't. 2 rats and a sentry jump us before it died. We make our way back to the exit hoping the cleric has her head out of her arse by now and will do something. She hit us each with a HoT and zoned. I held them off and the other warrior ran for it.

I disbanded her as soon as I popped in PoK and found another cleric. Got everyone rezzed that needed it and watched the exp roll in. Made just over a lvl in 3 hours or so.

But I still gotta wonder how the hell that 1st cleric made it to lvl 51. How can you take half an hour to get from any part of tranquility to the exits in PoJ and then NOT be ready to fight? I dunno, I've got her name written down and won't group with her again.
