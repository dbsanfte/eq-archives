Hey all...

I was just fartin around today and an idea came to me... a way to maybe help the current problem with the taunt skill. It wouldn't be a fix, just a way to make it a little more palatable. And if this has been suggested before, I apologize... I like to think I have read all of these threads but I'm sure some have slipped by.

Anyway, right now when we taunt, we get a text echo saying "You attempt to taunt blah blah blah", same text no matter what. What if they made the simple change to have that message report whether the taunt was successful or not? They have a message for trade skills... heck, almost every action in the game has a text message reporting it's success or failure. Like I said, it doesn't really fix the underlying fault with the skill, but it would take a bit of the uncertainty out of it. It would be nice to be able to say over /rs, with every degree of certainty, "%T taunted!"

Again, if this is old hat, feel free to let the thread float to the bottom and die. ;)

A large part of pre-EB aggro is relying upon your group to work with you to make sure you are taking the beats. (Heck, I'm sure there is some of that still needed post-EB, but I can't speak to that as I do not own such weaponry.) And grouping, ie. teamwork, is all about communication. And right now, aside from constantly assisting the mob to make sure we are still the target (which sucks), we have no way to know FOR SURE if we are the current target. Sure, we can tell if we are taking hits, but with a sufficiently slowed mob, sometimes (read: many times) it is hard to tell whether the mob is just between rounds or hitting someone else. Or casting, for that matter.

Again, I know it isn't that huge of a change... and I understand some of the more negative posts by the people who have been jaded by the ongoing debate. But I'm of the opinion that anything will help... and by asking for something small and easy, we are much more likely to get it than by requesting a complete redesign of the taunt/aggro situation.
