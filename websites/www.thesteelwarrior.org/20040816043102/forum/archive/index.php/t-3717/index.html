landrain
My Ideas of class balance and melee balance.

DPS upgrades
- Offhand weapon Damage Bonus.
- Raise the attack levels of pure melee, move all pure melee up a table.
- increase ratio of pure melee weapons
- increase skillcaps of pure melee to 305 for their weapon types (fix piercing/archery for warrior)
- increase dodge/parry/ripost/kick etc skill caps to give pure melee an advantage over other classes
- A critical hit should do a MINIMUM of weapon Damage x 4, 17pt crits are dumb.

HP upgrades
- Mana = HP's : ratio is up for discussion but 1-1 or 1-2 for pure melee
- Allow AA's to affect Buff Spells for pure melee

Mitigation
- Upgrade Warrior Mitigation by a real amount - reduce Total damage from all melee attacks,
by 10-15%.

Stats
- Advanced Body AA - allow melee (str/dex/agil/sta) stats to increase to 355 (pure melee only)
- FIX Stamina- Stamina should give MORE HP/StA as it increases instead of less.
- Make Agility a more usefull stat in regards to AC/mitigation

Disciplines -
- If nothing else UNHOOK all disciplines- each has its own refresh timer,
and have a BASE refresh timer between discipline uses of 3 minutes.
- Ideally, make disciplines like quasi-spells/bard songs. Give us a book of tactics.
Use the SPELL GEMS for them. This has built in refresh timers etc. Easier to use, upgrade,add etc.
- Take a look at the length vs refresh of several disciplines, and the effectiveness of them.

Taunt -
- GIVE A SUCCESS or FAIL MESSAGE!
- increase range to 50'
- increase effectiveness - ie 60% success is unacceptable for mobs levels less than player.
- Add XXX pure hate for every taunt.
The XXX would be the skill level of the taunter OR Top Hate + 1 whichever is more.

Ranged Taunt -
- New AA ability -
Call Out (3 levels 3/3/3/3/3)- this ability allows a warrior to target a mob and Call Out them to melee combat.
Some Mobs can be flagged immune. This ability gives the warrior + 700 Hate.
Level 1 - Trainable at Level 51 Affacts mobs Up to level 50 - Range 100 - reset 1 minute
Level 2 - Trainable at Level 55 Affects Mobs up to level 55 - Range 125 - Reset 1 minute
Level 3 - Trainable at Level 59 Affacts mobs Up to level 59 - Range 150 - reset 1 minute
Level 4 - Trainable at Level 63 Affects Mobs up to level 63 - Range 175 - Reset 1 minute
Level 5 - Trainable at Level 65 Affacts mobs Up to level 65 - Range 200 - reset 1 minute

Melee Focus effects-
- Deflect Focus 1-4 - increases parry by 2,5,10,15%
- Dodge focus 1-4 - increase dodge by 2,5,10,15%
- Strikeback Focus 1-4 - increase riposte by 2,5,10,15%
- Weapon haste 1-4 - Decrease weapon delay by 1,2,4,6
- Weapon Power 1-4 - Increase weapon damage by 1,2,4,6

AA abilities-
- Advanced Body
- Call Out
- Deathblow - 5 AA trainable at level 65-
this ability allows the character to kill instantly with a single blow a mob that is signifigantly below its level.
this AA works on mobs that are green.

Itemization-
- Remove all stun spells from weapons. Replace them with a new line of effects that do a DD + hate equivalent to what they currently have.


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

a few changes I would make to casting is aggro.

IMMUNE SPELLS - cast a spell on a mob that is IMMUNE to the spell, and this will cause +1 pure hate and that is all.
RESISTED SPELLS - Spells resisted fully will cause HALF NORMAL Hate
MITIGATED SPELLS - SPELLS MITIGATED will cause Hate = normal HATE x Mitigation factor.

I think this may be a bit excessive. If you add XXX pure hate even on a failed taunt then 60% success isn't unacceptable anymore. My personal taunt suggestion is similar.

Keep taunt the way it is now however add the XXX pure hate on a success or failure. Make the XXX pure hate part work at range, whatever range seems appropriate. This lets warriors generate aggro on the inc with taunt as well as a bow. It still requires the warrior/knight to be in melee range to use the top of hate list part of taunt which seems balanced as thats a pretty powerful feature when taunt is adding pure hate as well.

I also don't really like AA to improve taunting. From my experience low end warriors have the most problems with aggro, high end warriors the least, low end warriors can't afford more places to spend AA, high end warriors can. It seems to target the wrong group for improvement. Just make taunt good enough that it works without any AA.

I agree with your other suggestions though I think your numbers are mostly too high for improvements, adding 50 to skill caps is a large change, focus adding 15% may be too high etc.

One other suggestion I'd make is to make ripostes only effect main hand weapons and fix DMG bonus so that a 10/20 weapon performs as well as a 20/40 weapon in main hand. This lets warriors use a high delay 1h in mainhand to maintain DPS and decrease riposte dmg against them by a large margin. The balance then becomes you want a high delay weapon to avoid damage shields and ripostes but you want a low delay weapon for lower spikes in dmg and steadier aggro generation. So you face real decisions when picking a weapon, instead of faster=better.
