Brutul
I think those two combos will be close on DPS, but I bet the dual wield combo edges out the 2-hander by a hair.

I say this based on the parsing I did in Katta. In my test, attacking unbuffed from behind on a green-con mob, GBoS (14/18 + proc) and Blade of Carnage (15/20) beat out a WMBS by about 5 dps. Your 2-hander is slightly better than the WMBS and your offhander is slightly better than a BoC dps wise, so I think you will find either option is about equal for just damage. Only way to be sure is to parse it yourself though, I don't trust those weapon efficiency calculators when comparing 2-handers and 1 handers.
