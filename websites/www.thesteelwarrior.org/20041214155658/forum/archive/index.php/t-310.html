I also play a Vah'Shir warrior, and up till I got into the higher end part of the game, I was pissed about not having cultural armor. Also was pissed about being a largte race without Slam, or the innate stun resistance from the front, or the fact that we eat twice as much as any other race in the game. (Get your HP AA's outta the way, and then get metablosim 3... saves a TON on food) But as I have gotten into the higher end part of the game, I have found that cultural armor, and all that stuff is moot anyways.

I am level 65, I have 10 AA's, and I think my little warrior is doing fine. I can MT if needed, even on some of the higher end mobs. I really mis-spent my points when I made the toon to begin with... mostly into str and dex. (Don't ask me what I was thinking, it was the first toon I had ever made, and I knew NOTHING about EQ) but I have gotten enough eqipment and AA's to help me get him to over 200 in str, sta, and agi unbuffed, which is very nice for those xp groups where I don't have a shammy to boost those.

So is Vah'Shir as a warrior a bad choice? In retrospect, if you are not in the high end part of the game, maybe. If you are getting into places like Vex Thal, and second teir to elemental planes, then the mobs don't care what race you are, they will beat you silly with no discrimination. Vah'Shir are fun for a lot of reasons, which is why I chose it for my toon.

1. Unique look. You'll be easily targeted on raids.
2. Safe fall... even though it's not a very high skill, it still does come in handy.
3. The fact that we eat so much food is the reason why DoT's don't stick on us as long.
4. Sneak comes in handy once in a while too.
5. Our starting stats, pretty much suck, but with high end gear it doesn't matter.

So overall... I love my Vah'Shir Warrior.
