1) DT is every 30 seconds. You will need more than 1 group. 2 groups should do it, 3 groups is golden. Bring some cannon fodder to soak up at least the first DT on pull. He will probably be half health when the 2nd DT goes off if you bring 2 groups.

2) 32k hps like all old world mobs, not many at all

3) Clearing is the only way. Even if there is some sort of trick to jump down the hole, Yael will DT you when you log back on. with 2 or 3 groups of upper 60's, clearing is a joke and much easier than trying to get around it.

4) Depending on scalekeeper's position, you should be able to pull yael away from the scalekeeper. We killed Yael the other day and the scalekeeper was between us and Yael, so we had to kill him. Just buff your MR up over 250 and you should resist most the lifetaps. i had just under 250 and resisted some and the ones i didn't partially landed. He has low hp's too, so not that tough.
