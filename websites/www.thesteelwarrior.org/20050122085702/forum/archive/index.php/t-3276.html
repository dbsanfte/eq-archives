You can't lock aggro with DPS, period. You can give the illusion you hold aggro, but if that mob isn't dead shortly after a taunt succeeds, it's far from a "Lock"

Lock would be like one anarchy proc, one force shock proc, 1 EB proc. That's when you've got the shit on you for the long haul. The shaman can slow, the druid can snare, and you can DoT the living fuck outta that mob and it will stay firmly planted on the warrior. You can not duplicate that reliably with DPS. If you find non proccing weapons work for your particular setup (it being fairly unique) then fine, use em. The problem is that most people aren't grouped with 4 wizards and keeping aggro for longer than it takes 4 wizards to burn a mob down is just a tad different than what normal people have to do.
