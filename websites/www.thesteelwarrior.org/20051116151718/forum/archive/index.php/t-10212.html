Its very small, a tiny tiny boost to parry and dodge. Some folks thought that it ment you can tank as a lvl+ warrior. So for instance, every +5 skill means that you are +1 lvl better as a tank. After further testing, they found that only the crit % was effected enough to show up.

IE, the higher your + defense skill, the less likely that mob or mobs above it will land a crit on you. If you fight mobs +3 to +5 higher than you, they crit you a TON. with the + skills, they crit you less.
