Korak Sarathai
Back in the day, my guild never bothered with the weaponshield tactic. Not that it isn't valid, just not really needed. Definitely run dual ykesha. Just make sure that no one but the puller and tagger (if you aren't the tagger... some like to use SKs to tag so they can FD out of bag tag)... make sure no one but you and those two people shoot at or otherwise get aggro on Vindi as he is coming in. Make sure everyone is standing when he gets near camp. Have the raid give you 10 sec of solo time, and have all the druids and paladins spam you with small heals for the first 10 seconds. We've found that method to give good results and ensure the CH rotation gets started smoothly.

Friedrick
First off, let me say it is possible to tank Vindi at 59, I did it. To this day I have no idea how though as it was prior to the patch which opened up taunt for warriors under 60.

Personally I would shoot for lvl 60 though first. It should make your tanking him easier as your taunt should work better and you will gain another 150 or so hps. In addition, you will gain triple attack (small advantage) and be one lvl closer to Vindi.

One final piece of advice. On your first attempt at Vindi, I would use defensive. Already you don't know what you are up against and throwing in random damage on the main tank would add confusion in my opinion. The RNG is a harsh mistress. At least with Defensive you should take a more predictable amount of damage for your clerics to deal with.

Just a couple thoughts to consider.
