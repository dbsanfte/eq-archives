I'm a 56 warrior not in a guild but do have a 65 druid that I can 2 box with. Any advice on his equipment? I don't have a lot of money to spend so I'm mostly looking for droppable items I can 2 box for or duo with a 64 chanter with my druid for. Thanks for any advice that anyone can give me.
Feet:
Boots of Kingly Splendor (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62178): hp/resist upgrade, ac downgrade
Fine Heraldic Boots (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=23843): ac upgrade, 9sta loss

Wrist:
Fine Heraldic Bracer (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=23844): ac /hp upgrade over the +dex bracer (if you care about the +dex, it would be replaced with various pieces of fine heraldic)
Rock-Molded Wristguard (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=61200): ac/hp upgrade over the +dex bracer, not sure how viable it is for a duo, though

Shoulder:
Fine Heraldic Pauldrons (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=23846): ac upgrade, 10 sta loss
Grand Pauldron of Study (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62353): ac/resist upgrade, 10 sta loss. Not tradable, but easily one-grouped

Back:
Loyalist Shield of Honor (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=7398): massive ac upgrade, 10 sta loss

With your sta as high as it is, you can afford to lose a bit and still reach your max(255) with buffs. Personally, I'd pick a couple of the above items trade some of that sta for more ac and raw hp. Hell, you could afford to lose 19 raw stamina and still buff to your level 65 pre-aa max.
