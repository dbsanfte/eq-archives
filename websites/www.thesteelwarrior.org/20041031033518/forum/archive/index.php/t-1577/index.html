wandor
When you're dealing with 305 sta, how many ppl don't have issues maxxing it? In most cases the difference in total STA from 3 slots is not going to justify taking a hit in haste % regardless of whether you can max STA or not. Plus, don't forget that you can get 80 STA from a shaman so you really only need to get 225 STA to be able to max it.

For the sake of having a good comparison, here are the stats from the combos we are talking about:

Back haste
HoRZ/CoF/HRG HoRZ/CoF/HTWB
AC 90 77
HP 235 220
STA 19 25
Haste 36% 36%

Head haste
CoM/Hiero/HRG CoM/CoGP/HRG CoM/Hiero/HTWB CoM/CoGP/HTWB
AC 51 53 38 40
HP 210 185 195 170
STA 17 23 23 29
Haste 36% 36% 36% 36%

Waist haste
HoRZ/Hiero/SSHB HoRZ/CoGP/SSHB HoRZ/Hiero/BD HoRZ/CoGP/BD
AC 75 77 75 77
HP 200 175 205 180
STA 23 29 24 30
Haste 34% 34% 31% 31%
While the Heavy Tooled Weapon Belt and Heavy Rubi Girdle are pretty close in stats, when you look at the combo it becomes pretty obvious that the HRG is the superior belt. While you gain 6 STA with the HTWB, you lose 15HP and 13AC which is not really a good trade off.

Also, as you can see from the comparison, going with a CoM for haste is a bad idea for a warrior. You are giving up a ton of AC for little to no gain in STA/HP. If you want 36% haste, CoM is not the way to go.

So that leaves us with back or waist haste, like the original poster was asking. The stats are a little closer here, but Helm of RZ+CoF+Heavy Rubi Girdle still comes out on top by a pretty large margin. You lose 4-11 STA, but in exchange you get at least 2% haste, 15ac, 30hp.

BTW, I didn't include the Loyalist Shield of Honor because it is a pain in the ass quest and the shield is NO DROP.

Also, keep in mind that if you can't afford a Helm of RZ the comparison changes pretty dramatically and the CoM becomes a much better option. However, the difference in price from a CoM to a HoRZ is small enough on most servers to justify saving the extra plats to get the superior combo.
