Like the thought had never occurred to me before! OMG! Me dumb warrior. Me no think good. Me not know how fight with cleric. Me not know how group.

If the bastard hadn't been 20 levels higher than me I'da dueled his ass right then and there. Probably shoulda anyway, even though I woulda died. It was a just cause dammit!

I get so sick of this condescending "you really oughta find a nice group, lil girl, don't you know soloing is daaaaaaangerous?"

Hey - DANGER IS MY LIFE, I thrive on it, live for it, sharpen my sword every night DREAMIN' of it... if I choose to fight a dumbass battle, then thats my choice, heal me thats fine - but don't fuckin' lecture me about what a dumbass I am....

These druids have no honor. They've gotten too soft and flabby using spells all the time, not developing their muscles, relying on us meat shields to tank it for them....

And what's up with this M'Lady crap? I'm a goddamn warrior. I ain't wearing no dress. Save that language for the High Elves with their pretty hair and embroidered-shmoidered finery. I live by the sword, I ain't afraid of dying by it.

If your heals come with a lecture and a "lil girl" then save 'em for someone who isn't afraid to die, cuz I'd rather trudge through all of Norrath naked looking for my corpse than listen to that schmaltzy crap.
