A corpse lewting tip for j00 all...

If you loot everything on your corpse accidently (who hasn't!) but you have not yet stood up, (ie, you're still in loot mode even though you have looted all the gear) simply stay down. Within a few minutes, a timer runs out and you stand automatically. But your rezzable corpse will remain.

I found this out accidently with my necro. You can also /q out and leave a naked rezzable corpse also.
