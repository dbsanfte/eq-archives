Roughbeard
1) Helm, get rid of it, Raldukan will serve ya well
2) Gobby Earring - Bag it, buy a Solstice Earring
3) Grab some friends and quest some BoBs, or buy 2x SS/Thurg Bracer, or 2x Silver Chitin Wristbands.. somethin with hps
4) Frostbringer/BBoT combo would be good for ya.
5) Decent Haste Item, Rgb, SBoC, RBB etc etc. dont skimp on haste
6) Quest some Draconic Pauldrons
7) Eyepatch of Plunder, Death Mask of the Elysians, Gantru Warmask, Rotten Eye Mask.. just get a better mask
8) Darkwood Bow of Suffering, more hps/sta than Goranga, has 5 ac, and ya dont have to switch out every time you need to pull!
9) Darkwater Pearl Ring is not worth the -45 hps.. get 2 Valorium Rings of Gallantry
10) Booties with Hps.. SS MQ or something.
11) Clattering Bone Necklace
12) 1 35hp earring to replace your orc fang.
13) SS Gaunts MQ
14) Heiro/Maelstorm/Pernic Cloak
15) Work on your AC, dont give up AC all the time for more hps. Thurg Greaves MQ would be good for ya, and with the 7sta it should be close to the same amount of hps, with 12 more ac.

so pretty much by sellin off your fungi you can upgrade in every slot and still have some cash left over :p good luck
