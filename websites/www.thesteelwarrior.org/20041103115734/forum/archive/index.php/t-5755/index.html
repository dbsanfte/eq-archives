OSoW procs 25% more than just about everything else. If you put it in your off-hand you'll get ~1.25 procs per minute instead of ~1. Most people should put OSoW in main hand to get an additional 0.5 procs per minute instead of 0.25 in off-hand. Sure, the amount of agro from the proc may be less than some other procs, but it is still significant agro and most of the time we're looking for an earlier proc which the OSoW provides.

The only weapons I'd main hand over OSoW pre-Sol Ro+ are BBotE or Bloodfrenzy.
