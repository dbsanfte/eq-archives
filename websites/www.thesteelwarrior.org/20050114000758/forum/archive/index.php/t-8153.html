07-19-2004, 06:05 PM
For a MT, the avoidance and mitigation AA's are just as important as hit points.... in many ways they are even more important than hit points. If the mob cannot kill you in a single round (as with Grummus for example), then it doesn't really matter how many hit points you have. What matters is how much damage you can mitigate and avoid. Bottom line: it is more efficient to take 10% less damage than it is to have 10% more hit points.
