Do I think the parses are balanced? That's a tough question. It's a handful of parses, on a few select mobs, with very little information. There are too many factors, too many intangibles, and too many variables to make an educated determination if the parses are "balanced".

One thing I will say is that the M'sha's are not good mobs to use when making dps comparisons. Rogues always tend to put up some inflated numbers due to their lower avoidance/mitigation.

An obvious trend that you'll notice is that as fight duration went down, dps went up. The last parse is just over 2 minutes..a rogue can stay under discipline for half of that (using deadeye, and duelist back to back). So, obviously fight duration is going to be a significant factor in the totals. While not nearly as significant to rogues as it is to wizards, it's still very relevant, and at least partially explains the astronomical figures in a couple of the parses.

Backstab damage has gotten to the point where a good disc can inflate your numbers to jaw dropping proportions, BUT it's not nearly as common place as some would like you to believe. I've had discs where I've put up an additional 15k in backstab damage and then some where I struggled to break 2k. It's very very streaky, and has a tremendous affect on your numbers.

A very skilled rogue, with bleeding edge weaponry, and equipment selected for the sole purpose of increasing his/her damage is going to put up some sick numbers. However, as so clearly shown by the parses the damage output from one rogue to another varies pretty widely.
