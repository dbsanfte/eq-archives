Redhenna
Love the title to this thread, as it is kinda walking a line, trading off sometimes one for te other. Kailyn is right that your SS boots are definatly better than Kingly Boots of Splendor. 14 ac + 4 sta > 40 hp's. I have used a formula that I found on this board that roughly 1 ac = 1 sta = 6 hp's. It is not exact, but should give you a rough idea on how to balance gear. Simply put, you cannot have too much AC, nor too many hp's. I found myself alot deciding to not upgrade a slot for marginal returns, and holding out for clear gains(ie, getting both more ac and hp's). In the example you gave on boots, changing is not a clear gain, so keep what you have(note that I still wear SS boots, and will not change them till I get ornate or better).

I disagree with Kailyn tho on her comments about Tak vs Mir armor somewhat. 750 points vs 1450 pts, with a difference of a few ac and hp's. if you have the time and patients, go for Tak armor, but if, like me, your schedule would make it unreasonable to get Tak in any kind of timely fasion(don't forget, a new expansion is 3 months away, with probably tons of new gear making a lot of what is out there obsolete), then get Mir armor and enjoy a nice upgrade.
