So, How has 2003 affected everyone in Everquest?

Seems out of all the new expansions that have crossed paths with our logins; and new zones we've all become accompanies with.... I think 2003 has brought a small ray of sunshine to the Warrior Class.

It seemed Late winter/early spring here finally took us away from the EZBoard beast; and witht he help of sHORTY and Raaj found us a new little niche here on our own terms; with most of the old gang coming along plus gaining some new faces as well.

I for one am glad to see how this community has thrived over the years; nevermind my horrendous postcounts number; and I still fondly think back when I myself was a n00b over at the old boards and getting beat_down by Ganzo; hearing about how the Half Elf Conspiracy(tm) was still in it's infancy being led by Jaikar and Adex.......and Xexys spilling juice all over his Keyboard and typing god-knows-what......and to really show my age here at TSW, How Vanzandt decided one fine patch day back in Kunark to take his wurmslayer; wrap it up real tight with bandages, and stick it in the nether-regions of EQ's collective gluteus maximus....

What Memories.

Back on topic though. Come September or so; we were informed for the (x) number of time that SoE was goign to try for 'Melee Balancing'...oh how our ears bled at those very words....

The funny part was...come December...They came True.
It was incredible. Thansk to our rather large and often explicitally vocal community and with help from our Plate Hybrid buddies :D ; things actually might have been considered and with help from our Test Server Players here plus everyone else who got a try to go over and try out what SoE planned for us, the Ball started Rolling.

And Roll, it did.

Now; we're fresh into 2004; and the Warrior class has recieved quite the breath of fresh-air; from the ones here who played there Warrior since the Early Days of EQ when there were only 6 hotkeys; right down to the six-boxxing ( hehe ) abominations who god only knows how continue to keep the warrior class on the map.

So, to all my fellow Warriors and Warrioresses whom deal out death and destruction firsthand and; as my friends fondly say, "Tanking it in teh Face!!"...lemme just say thanks for all the hard work you've all put in; and continuing to go forward and helping shape the future of EQ and all the young Warriors who decided one day that they themselves want to share in the pride and glory of being the one who stand tall in the heat of battle with nothing but the armor on your back and whatever weapon you got in your hand.

We sure moved forward this past year. Let's keep the ball moving and showing pride in who we are; and what we can do no matter what adversity stands before us. We're Warriros. We can take it.
