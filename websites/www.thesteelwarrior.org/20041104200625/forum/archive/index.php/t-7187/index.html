Grendonbb
Uhm, if I recall correctly all the tower nameds except north wing have a rather large dd/stun... believe east wing is 500dd/stun, west wing is 1000dd/stun, and south wing is 1500dd/stun.

In addition to being very hard (in case of South Wing completely impossible) to resist, these can cause huge damage spikes that take you from half health to dead in the matter of a blink of an eye. Since mob innate procs cannot go off on a miss, the theory is that with Evasive up you'll get hit less, thus getting procced on less.

I must say, I was quite the defensive goon back in the day. and after making the transition to evasive I am really pleased with it. You get hit for full power, but the number of misses on you truly makes it worthwhile in many circumstances. In EXP groups and on mobs that have any innate proc, I always use Evasive unless specifically ordered to do otherwise. It can truly be the difference between life and death.
