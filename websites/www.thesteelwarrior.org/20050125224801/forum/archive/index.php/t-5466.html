When they released LDoN, they added an NPC in Freeport who will give you augmentable versions of your epic if you turn in your 2hander and the scabbard.

So if you already have the summoned versions (i.e. you're walking around with the 2hander AND the two 1handers), you'll have an extra set of 1handers.

Is it better aggro? Not in my experience, no.

EDIT: THIS may require some qualification ... so here goes ... in my experience, the red and blue blade or the 2handed epic hold aggro just fine. And the other red blade in the offhand doesn't proc much (see various threads on proc rates). So that I have yet to notice much of a difference. They've been about the same for me. BELLOW has been a noticable difference for me, and has improved my tanking experience quite a bit. The second red blade gave me no noticable improvement. I hold aggro great right now, but not because I can dual wield the red blades ... but because I can point at mob and BELLOW it.

Is it an exploit? No.

Is it worth the hassle? Well, if a 14/24 is good enough DPS for you, then yes.
