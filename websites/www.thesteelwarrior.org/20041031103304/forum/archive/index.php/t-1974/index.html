If you don't mind a bit of questing, LOL. Wow is that even an understatement, that is unless 'a bit' = countless hours. 50ac 75hp is pretty sweet for a back slot, but you could upgrade 5 or 6 other slots camping gear in Droga/Veksar/Chardok b.

Anyway, like Khana said, a Hiero cloak is a long term upgrade. You might be hurting for STA now, but a couple of months from now when you can max STA with shammy buffage, the Hiero will be the better cloak.

Also, there quite a few other slots you could upgrade and get better results. Here are some ideas:

1. Ear - Earring of the Solstice (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=28771)
2. Gloves - Gauntlets of the Myrmidon (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=31186)
3. Shoulders - Draconic Pauldrons (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=12557) or Forged shoulderpads (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=13689)
4. Wrist - Cultural bracer (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=23807)
5. Neck - clattering bone neck (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=6418)
6. Belt (haste) - Soulscream belt (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=65570)

You should be able to camp or buy all of these items pretty easily. All of them but the belt are cheaper than the cloak you are looking to buy. For the price of the cloak, you should be able to buy all 9 earring of the solstice gifts and a cultural bracer and maybe even a clattering bone necklace if prices are low on your server. You should be able to camp the belt in Veksar, the drop seems to be fairly common.
