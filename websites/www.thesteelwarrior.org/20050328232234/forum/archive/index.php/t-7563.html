Ragg, keep in mind if you go to Paludal Caverns that what you see in there more resembles a barfight than organized hunting (at least on my server). The experience rate of gain for your character is without parallel at your level, which is why people go there. And because a lot of people go there, you can get a group fast.
Back in the day, people used to get derv camps in the commonlands, then Northern desert of Ro and the Oasis. On my server, there are still enough folks doing this that you can get groups. Experience is far slower, but it's a better route for developing your playing skills, in my opinion.
