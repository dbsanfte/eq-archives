Three comments:

- Who knows, it might be that finishing blow works like an extra proc on your attacks, kind of like the lifetap proc on Boon. If that were the case then the proc rate for finishing blows would basically be constant for a given dex (ie. weapon delay, haste and slow wouldn't change the number of procs you get per minute on average.)

- Another possible use for finishing blow I didn't see mentioned is if you're hunting solo. After all, if you're soloing as a warrior, odds are the stuff you're fighting is low enough level to use finishing blow. Might save you having to chase a mob around at the end of your fight if it's not snared.

- Two things I don't like about FB. One is that you basically need to completely max it out for it to be useful up to level 59 mobs. So without that huge AA investment the skill isn't helping you much if at all. Compare that to any other AA where even the first rank of it helps you somewhat.

The other thing I don't like about it is that it is totally worthless in the tier 2+ planes. And while LDoN is fun and has better cash and mission rewards, the exp in the planes is much better.
