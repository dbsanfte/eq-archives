Brutul
Try Barindu. There are quite a few random named in the courtyard area that are pretty mean but doable (hit for 1100ish, at least partially slowable) and drop some pretty decent stuff. The Ferubi zone is probably the safest place to pull but I haven't spent a lot of time there. Be sure you have the latest maps from eqtoolbox, there are some really good GoD maps there.

If Barindu is too easy, give ferubi a shot but don't expect to get any named unless you have at least a couple hours to clear to them. Of course there are some very good random drops there, on par with Ssra loot. If the Barindu courtyard is too hard, the hallways near the quinimi zone are easier or you can try Quinimi. Riwii should be similar to Barindu for difficulty, but I haven't spent any time there.

Anyway, I'm pretty much a GoD newbie myself, but that should give you a couple things to try. Let us know how it goes.
