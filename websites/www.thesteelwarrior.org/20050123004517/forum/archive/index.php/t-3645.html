Lifetaps have a low resist, most have around a -200 resist modifier. Unless your MR is over 200, don't bother trying to resist those. The level 65 one actually has a -500 modifier (470hp, focusable and crittable). This includes their Lifetap over times, such as Bond of Death.

Spears are on Disease resist, and a good disease resist will actually give you a good resist rate on those. Improved HT (the one that goes to 8k or so) is unresistable. Those guys aren't common, but more and more are getting them. The good thing is that he can't take off more than 75% of your hp in one shot, the bad thing is you will have a very hard time getting that hp back. If you know they don't have improved HT, they'll most likely be using their disc'd HT...that's on a disease resist, and can be resisted but it has a -200 modifier also.

Almost all their damage spells except the spears have a -100 modifier to resist, with lifetaps and HTs getting a -200. A method of stunning them would be infinitely useful, as would a way to remove their buffs (especially their proc which gives them mana), and having Fire/Poison/Magic/Disease all over 200 or even higher is the only way you're going to be able to beat a decently skilled SK. If they have the 8k HT...my advice is just don't bother. Even with the modifiers for PVP, he's gonna have so much of an advantage over you it's pointless.

My advice, don't even bother unless you've got insane resists.

As a 65 SK, I can tell you that unless your gear is several tiers above his, you have zero chance. You would need extremely high unbuffed resists, excellent ac/hp and dmg/dly to stand any chance, and against AA HT even that won't help.

If you are going to try it, my advice is:

- never dual wield. Assuming he knows what he is doing, he will stack every damage shield he can, which even without right click DS items can be a lot, especially with a DS potion. Dual wield = death even if you have way better gear.

- at least have your MR high. 150 or above will give you some chance to resist snares, if you get snared he can run circles around you. Then all he needs is to stack dots on you and lifetap till you die.

- use damage shield. Won't hurt him much, he will be using 2hander, but it's something, and it will hurt his pet.

- farm some ice giant toes (not velious, you need everfrost ones), have a shaman make you instant heal potions. A lot of them. If you have lots of them you might have some chance, if he goes oom.
