I am not sure whether it's in the library at this particular web site, but yes, parses have been made. Going from memory:

Hit% is based on the difference between your level and the mob's level. It is not based on skills.

Hit distribution is affected by your ATK (and your skills that contribute to ATK).

There was a well-known experiment made years ago by a monk who was quitting the game. He delevelled from 50 to 8 (if I recall correctly). He discovered that his combat skills remained high, and naked at level 8 he was able to slaughter level 30 mobs such as aviak avocets. That was the first time we learned of the real function of combat skills.

Incorrect. Your weapon and offensive skill is what determines your hit rate. Attack only shifts the average hit damage higher. With ~ 250 skill in weapons and offense, you will hit a mob approx 59.5% of the time regardless of it's level.
