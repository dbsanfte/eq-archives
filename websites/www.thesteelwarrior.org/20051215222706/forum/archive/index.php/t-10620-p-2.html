Mauradon looks like two zones at first, but it is actually one. The "purple side" and "orange side" eventually meet up then go on to the end bosses. There is a quest that creates a staff, this staff allows you to port beyond the orange and purple straight to the end bosses. This makes it possible to hit the four end bosses (with the best loot) in less than an hour. Once you have the staff, you will probably never use the normal entrances again.

The staff quest starts from some chick standing in a pool on the orange side (before entering instance). Got to kill a few bosses to finish staff. Can be difficult until the group gets closer to level 50.
