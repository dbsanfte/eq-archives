01-26-2004, 01:20 PM
a good 8k buffed and around 1400ac is the norm for the typical pre elemental raid tank. a guild MT at that lvl would probably be just under 9k and around 1500-1600ac. the AA are important also, they should shave off about 30% of the incoming damage and greatly reduce the risk of spike damage.

breaking it down by mob, the typical guild at that lvl of progression would need about 4 warrior for the agnarr script, most of them can be around 7-8k fully buffed, but you'll need at least 1 tank with well over 8k for emmerick due to his 3k AE nuke and agnarr that hits for 1400s. more importantly is having 4 wizards with the disintagrate spell.
2 warriors for TT with a min of 7k, or 1 warrior with close to 9k.
tallon is just a DPS zerg fight, so tanks arent really that important since you cant really hold his attention for very long.
vallon anyone can tank, its more of having 5 healers and 5 slowers, or using some very cleaver kiting tricks.
saryrn a ranger could tank, getting to her is harder/longer than actually killing her.
LMM you'll need a really good tank with close to all the defensives. i'd say 2 or 3 warriors with well over 8k to be safe, but he'll rarely get past a 9k warrior with all the AAs. LMM by him self is rather easy if you've done AoW enough, the timing on the adds and controling them makes it tough.
the bert event will need about 3-5 warriors with over 8k to keep up with the 12 kings, mostly for a defensive/evasive chain. 12 kings in 2 hours (120 mins) mean you need a defensive warrior every 10 mins, and since reality doesnt work out that evenly, you could end up with 2 kings back to back. at least one of those warrior should have close to 9k, bert can almost hit for 2k.
most of the actual gods are endurance fights, as opposed to AD RD and behemoth where the those mobs do so much damage you need to burn them down ASAP.
