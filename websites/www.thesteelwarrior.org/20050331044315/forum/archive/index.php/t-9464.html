Watering Hole has a Girplan mover as PH in the south dead end at first intersection. Its the middle Girplan in that room. The other PH is a Discordling Warrior, and is located on the north side tunnel, between the L shaped room and the round room half way down. It is the only Discord Warrior so should be easy to find.

And with a good group you can almost clear all the mobs between PH's by the time they respawn.
