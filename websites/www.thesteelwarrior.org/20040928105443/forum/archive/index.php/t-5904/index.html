Decaillin
Two things to ask yourself:

1. How crowded is VT on your server? If there are lots of guilds hitting it, then you may not see it much...unless you can beat them there.

2. How committed is your guild? Are people willing to put a month aside to farm keys/banes/shards? You will need the whole guild to do it obviously, so if you only have a handful of people willing to put forth the effort, you might not get good results.

VT is definately a great place to get lots of high quality upgrades in a short amount of time though, so its well worth it if your guild is willing
