Angry_Tank01
05-19-2004, 10:40 AM
Phase1. You have to complete 5 trials within 1hour, your allowed 18 players per trial, we have the first18 take Fire then Water, second18 take Air then Earth, then the other 18 take Undead.
Have people in raid as reserves able to help out if a trial is going bad.

You'll probably complete Air / Earth / Fire first time no problem, Water + Undead are a little tricky.

Phase2 is just killing trash and 5 named, (you can zone more people in now to help) this shouldn't take you the whole hour.

Phase3 is a boring time sink, kill 8(?) waves of 14(?) mobs + 2named per wave. Nothing hard about it really, yellow cons are mezzed, red cons offtanked.

Phase4 = TT, Saryn, TZ & VZ. TT and Saryn are elemental level hitters, with annoying AEs and adds which spawn at certain %hp. TZ is similar to TZ from the RZ script, except he hits alot harder. VZ is your first big problem in Time.

If you're anything like my server you'll only get 1 day per week rotation, make the most of it, and GL.
