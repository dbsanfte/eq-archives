Upon taking into consideration the performance of my system running other games (Soldier of Fortune 2, UT2K3, C&C Generals most recently) where frame rates are amazing even after turning on every little bit of eye candy I can find, the conclusion I have come to is the same as Lillyann.

I ran with full luclin models on, particle effects on low (not for performance but simple because they can be overwhelming when you run them on high), 3D sky on, mipp mapping / dynamic lighting and social animations all on since the luclin release until about 2 weeks ago and performance for XP grouping was fine, a little choppy in PoK and going to the bazaar was a case of either clip plane to zero or stare at the floor.

Raiding with 40+ people could cause the frame rate to be a little choppy and effected the speed at which I could react in some situations however so when I started raiding regularly in PoP / Luclin recently I turned models and particle effects off and now enjoy a decent frame rate in all situations and have been pleased to find that the old models grow on you and soon enough you dont miss the new ones at all.
