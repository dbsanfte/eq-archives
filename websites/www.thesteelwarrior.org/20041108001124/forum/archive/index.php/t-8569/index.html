This may be a stupid question - but how do you use area taunt? I am going to get this AA pretty soon, but I can't seem to find any information on how to use it...do I need to create a hotkey? Please help.
Quarg
After you buy the AA, in the AA window there's a button you can hit that says "Make Hotkey." It'll put a hotkey on your cursor that says Area Taunt that you can put in your hotkey bank like any other and use it.
