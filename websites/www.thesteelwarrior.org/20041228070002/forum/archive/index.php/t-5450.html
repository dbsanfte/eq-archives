Are you kidding?

The reason I ask is because that's one of the big benefits of the new Disc system is the ability to cancel a disc in progress and save any further END loss. The timer on that particular cycle of disciplines is lost, however. So if you 'accidentally' trigger Defensive, then you can't use Defensive, Stonewall, Evasive for the next 8 minutes or so...no matter whether you cancel it or not. However, you can quickly cancel one disc type then cycle to another...so, you could if you wanted, use defensive for 1 second, then furious for one second, then fellstrike for one second...then healing will.

I personally use that one UI mod that someone created and posted here the day after the discs were changed that has a small footprint, and an easy method of cancelling them.
