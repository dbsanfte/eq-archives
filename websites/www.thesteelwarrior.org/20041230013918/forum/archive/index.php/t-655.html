When grouping: "loot like a warrior".

Unfortunately as a class I dont think we have any means of making great plat in the way that other classes do. But if you are in dire need of cash heres a couple of things you could try:

Fine Steel weapons: Find a dungeon you can solo in reasonably successfully and collect back packs of fine steel weapons to sell at a vendor. Best to go somewhere like runny eye where vendors are only 1 zone away.

Hill Giants: Might need a healer for these guys at your level, and could be hard competing with tracking classes, but they drop the best plat per mob you can find.

Tradeskill supplies: Plenty of rich tradeskillers out there either working their skill up for trade skill quests or to make funky items or just cos they are bored. Lots of these guys can't be bothered farming the things they need for tradeskills so try farming them for them to sell in the bazaar. Some good ones are:
- Leather padding (Spiderling silks and low quality pelts) Blacksmiths love to buy these by the stack.
- Velium blocks (from Cystal Caverns) Need lots of these for the 8th Shawl
- Acrylia blocks - time consuming to farm so can charge a good price for these

Edit - another thought...
Mammoth tusks from everfrost sell for a decent amount of plat and are probably solo-able easily at your level. Will you're there you can also offer to be a 'banker' for people hunting the ice giants - you give them 50pp and they give you 590gp, 700sp, 3000cp and half a dozen FS weapons kinda deal.
