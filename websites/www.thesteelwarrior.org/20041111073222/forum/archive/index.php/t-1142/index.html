QuirtEvans
Remember that AA exp doesn't scale like regular exp. So it goes faster at higher levels.

That said, RS3 is just great. PoJ isn't where it's really needed, though, because you can cast SoW in PoJ. RS3 makes a huge difference in dungeons, where SoW won't work.

All else equal, if you got levels first, the total time to get levels and AA would be less. But that dungeon pulling thing .. if you are often a puller, I'd go for RS3 somewhere around 54.
