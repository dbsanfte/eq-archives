Arathena
There are very odd caps involving pure hate and the very early stages of the battle. What you're seeing here is the results of those caps, and it's the same kind of thing that happens with terrors. The provoke and terror lines are unique in the game in that they are the only methods in the game to generate true, raw, hate. Even a miss-and-proc from an Eblow weapon carries the hate generated by the missed swing. (Search for the various melee-aggro generation threads for corroboration.) There's no real consesus among SKs on how to use terrors, since because of this factor, the value of terrors is very much crippled.

Those that do use the terrors, however, will generally use it to solidify aggro, rather than try to regain it. In general, aggro gaining for me tends to go Snare-Melee-Terror. Like us, I suspect that as an 'initial aggro' tool, you're vastly underusing the initial 400 aggro.

In example:
Monk pulls mob. Pull process generated 10 hate. Mob on monk.
On inc, warrior incites mob. Cap generates 200 hate. Produces 150 more melee hate +70 more Incite hate, before Slow lands, generating (say) 500 hate: Result: mob on shaman until warrior generates 80 more hate.

Alternate Scenario 1:
Monk Pulls mob. Generates 10 hate.
Warrior engages w/bow or melee, generating 20 hate. Mob on warrior. Warrior incites, generating 400 hate. Warrior generates 80 more melee hate + 70 hate incite tick, placing him at 570 when shaman's 500 hate slow lands. Mob never blinks at shaman.

Alternate Scenario 2:
Monk pulls, generating 10 hate. Warrior engages, generating 100 melee hate before 500 hate of slow lands. Warrior incites, generating 400 hate. As shaman and warrior have same aggro, lazy mob swipes at nearest target.

The capped nature of isolated hate is very difficult to pin down exactly, and no one fully understands it to our knowledge.

There is, of course, the possibility that I'm talking out my ass, but something's gotta explain why I can generate 200+ aggro a second with terrors and still lose aggro to a single slow if I don't back it up with something 'real'.
