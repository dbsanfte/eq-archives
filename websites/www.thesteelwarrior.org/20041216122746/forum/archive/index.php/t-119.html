Yes offhand ratio matters. From what I understand when the delay on an offhand weapon recycles a check for dual wield is made, if that check is succesful your offhand gets a swing, then a check for double attack is made if that check is succesful your offhand swings again. If the dual wield check fails you get zero swings and you have to wait for the delay to recycle again. You should basically look for the same things for your offhand as you do for your mainhand; Agro procs, good ratios, HP, sta, whatever.

Offhand makes dual wield and double attack checks independant from mainhand.
