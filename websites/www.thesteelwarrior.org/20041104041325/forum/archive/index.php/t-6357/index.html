renasu
Enchanter vs warrior fights can be one of the most interesting. The chanter definately has the advantages, but you can even it a bit if you plan ahead and have the right environment.

The fight can go pretty bad though. If you get mezzed, then you may find yourself tashed, slowed, rooted, and being pounded by a fast hitting pet. Since we have zero channelling skill you wont be able to pumice anything off. So to have a chance, you need to be on the offensive as much as you can.

You must break the initial rapture by having a dot on you BEFORE you are mezzed. The crimson potion can work as can our Warlord's Tenacity dot. Once free, pumice, pumice, pumice (him) in hopes of bringing down his rune. When he tries to recast it, hit him hard and bash/kick to interrupt. If he gets rune back up, pumice immeditately. Try to repeat this process as every real hit is important. You may want to stop and redot yourself, but its a hard choice to make during the chase.

A few other tricks:
- If you cant pumice self due to a pet, use the fortitude disk.
- There are insta click items, like forlorn sceptre, that can snare or root. These dont work on a player with decent resists, but do work well on pets.
- Don't underestimate how many pumice stones you may need. Don't get two, get a dozen.
- Most of our killing power comes from when we are beserk plus the Might strike disc. If you get rooted/pet, you may want to let him get your down to beserk until you fortitute/pumice free then surpise him by stunning then killing him in 3 attacks.

I'm not mentioning the tactic of uncharming his pet, since I dont ever do that and he may not even use a charmed pet. But that may be the way to go though.
