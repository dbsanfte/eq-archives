I use DBOTW+Painsipper in offhand. I previously used DBOTW+Anger3 augged EoE. The loss of stun proc+anger3 in the offhand have been surprisingly unnoticeable(although i gained 15 combat effects around the same time, so might have offset the proc loss slightly).

Once they make augs removable so I can move my EoE's anger3 proc to the demerix I will be a very happy camper.

As for painsipper versus CBS I prefer the slightly better stats on the painsipper over the cbs(HP/AC), and I think on some mobs that the buffslot you can risk losing from the scimitar might be an annoyance. I am also "hoping" that 26 delay will mean slightly less ripostes compared to 21 delay.

Agro wise the scimitar probably have a very slight edge because of the rune like proc, but it depends if you can/want to live with fact that it might occasionally block an important in-combat buff like BoV or MGB wood/paragon/CR etc.

Anyway, besides all that, the painsipper simply look badass on an ogre hehe.

With 225hp primary+250hp offhander, using 2-handers is beginning to look alot less attractive. I used to switch to my vanazir after disc run out, but now the stat loss is so big I usually just stick with the dbotw+painsipper setup through all fights.

When I'm not MTing and want to be sure not to get agro, I bag the DBOTW, mainhand the painsipper and offhand the EoE. Then I can lean back and just pop fellstrike and not worry too much about agro. Nice for afternoon clearings of the Qvic Minis and stuff :)
