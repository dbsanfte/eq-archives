Tarkas is correct....Primary and 2ndary weapons swing indpendently from each other. For a brief example, lets say you have a 20 delay weapon in mainhand and a 24 delay in offhand.

When you press auto-attack, every 2.0 seconds (assuming no haste for simplicity) your primary weapon will swing. Every 2.4 seconds the combat engine will make a check vs your duel wield skill. If the check is successful, your offhand weapon will swing.

What happens with your mainhand does not affect what goes on with your offhand in any way. If you want to prove it to yourself, get two onehanders of vastly different speeds and of two different attack types i.e. slash and bash. Watch the battle spam and you'll see how it works.
