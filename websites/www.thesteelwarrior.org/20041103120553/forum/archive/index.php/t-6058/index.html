Sangiovese
I use the same formula that crashbomba posted. Your level plus 1/2 your level = the highest level you can group with and get exp.

As for rounding... I know that in the case of a level 43 player, it rounds up. 43+21.5 = 64.5 And a level 43 can group with a 65 and get exp.
Sangiovese

Sassinak
Here's an easier way to judge it:

If the lower character cons green to the higher character, then the lower character does not get XP.

If the lower character cons light blue or better, then they get XP.
