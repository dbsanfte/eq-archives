Jaqyl
As a gnostic dorf I've had to deal with the lack of good cheap buyable gear as well. Here are some suggestions outside the above listed.

The new droga ring event (http://pub146.ezboard.com/fthesafehousefrm13.showMessage?topicID=6706.topic) results in a decent bracer (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=62203). Since you're SolRo flagged, it should be pretty easy to do up to the second gate neck (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=28290) quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2287)(you should do this anyway you'll need it later). The named in the maze event in PoN often drops these arms (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=11423), you can buy them too, but if you're buying you're better off with ornate. An alternate to ornate for your legs are Tae Ew Plate Greaves (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=27777), if you can find them for sale. Grab another bloodmetal earring, those things rock. Also, while very time consuming, the chardok quests result in two nice pieces of gear I'm still wearing at 6.4k unbuffed.

Work on PD3! HP AA's make a huge differance, you're almost there keep going. The 4.5% extra from PD3 even without any gear upgrades will give you 211hp. Also I'd play around with switching your onhand/offhand, you'll probably get better dps and better agro with the GBoS in the onhand.
