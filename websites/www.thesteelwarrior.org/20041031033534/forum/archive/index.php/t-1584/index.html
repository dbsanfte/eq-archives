Your force sounds very sufficent. My advice ( it worked 3 times for us so far ) is to kill Jumpy and Guards if he is up just before the turnin so you have plenty of time before gaurd repops. Everyone setup shop near Undead Bard, have CotHer in MT group and ST in same group. Have ST stand with bard, let bard turn in and run away. Have ST aggro Trak and get banished, while CotHer summons ST, have MT engage and tank for 30 seconds. By then MT gets Banished and ST steps in, Trak will prolly drop in 50 seconds with that that force.

Make sure all classes that can cure the DoT mem their cures, but cure casters first since melee can take it. Most melee can survive till the end of the fight easy, and be cured then. Make sure bard plays AE resists.

Goodluck and let us know.
