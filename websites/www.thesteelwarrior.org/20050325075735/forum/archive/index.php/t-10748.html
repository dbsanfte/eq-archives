No no no, don't spoil one of the best zones in the games!

Here's some basic stuff:
Most if not all trash mobs cast AE's like Uqua mobs did. 1 type spawns 3 golem adds. 1 type is mezzable.
Njacka the Scourge hits about quad 800 consistanly, not bad but can mess you up if pulled with 3-4 others.
Wanders are on a 5 min repop timer (give or take) statics are once per instance.

Kelekdrix:
Banishes, think of a way to deal with it. The hardest part is before kelekdrix not the mob itself. Buff all resists for the AE's.

Stonemites: Not giving hints here as this stops alot of guilds, use your head, if you can't do Inktu'ta then your fucked for MPG trials and Txevu. It's not as hard as it seems.

After stonemites are golems, good offtanking and good heals are the way to go.

Cursecaller: Run, you figure the rest out.

Noqufiel: Again, work it out for yourself.

Asking for a full spoiler makes your guild look unable to do anything without a plan laid out for them, past Inktu'ta unless you have a friend feeding you strats from a top end guild, you'll have to work it all out, and it really is the most rewarding thing to do it yourself. Tonight we did Zun in txevu for our first time after 6-8 attempts and trying plans, and it feels great to of done it with only the general info on the encounter. Though saying that, I am not against people giving small hints and background information so the guild can use that to help themselves as I'm sure others here will, just don't spend you're whole time using other peoples strats.
