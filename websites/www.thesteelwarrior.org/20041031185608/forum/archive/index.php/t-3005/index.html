ThornyEQ
Do you have a built-in group (meaning a group of friends you always play with or regular access to guild groups)? If not, start something other than a warrior. Pally was suggested, and SK isn't bad either; in many pick-up situations, they'll go for those two before they go for warriors. (If you really want to be desired in just about any group, make a cleric, enchanter or shaman instead.)

But, if you have a built-in group, or if you've made up your mind and warrior is what you want, I'd next recommend you rethink your race. Yes, in the end game your gnome will be just about as effective as an ogre, but before then, an ogre will rule with way more HP's and stun immunity from the front, as well as being a large race that can slam/bash without needing to equip a shield. There's also the matter of perception. A pickup group rarely knows who you are (at least for the first few times you group with some of them), and a big burly ogre just sells himself much better than a scrawny little gnome. People may not admit to it, but on first impressions, size matters!

Also, make the ogre worship Rallos Zek and for the plat you have you can outfit him not only with a pair of good fast weapons (plus nice 2HS's and 2HB's), but with a full suit of Fierce Heraldic armor! It's got a recommended level of 45, but in the meanwhile, you can still wear it from level 1, and for the first 30 or so levels your fungi will do just fine for you.

Is this the cheap, unimaginative, dirty way to do it? Yes...but it's also the way that you'll probably get more groups as a warrior if no one really knows you. They probably don't need a warrior, but if they have no choice but to get a warrior (like, say...there's no pally, SK, rogue, ranger, cheerleader, or quadriplegic nobel prize laureate available), level for level, and based on similar equipment, they'll take the "fatty" over the trash-can.

Having said this, I still regret, to this day, having deleted and restarted my halfling warriorette some 2 years back. Given that I've always had a built-in group, I should have kept her and not given in to my base desire to have a burly barbarian with slam instead.

YMMV, but good luck with whatever choice you make. :)
