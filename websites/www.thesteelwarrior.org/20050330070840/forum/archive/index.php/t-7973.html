We use a ranged fight on Cursed to deal with charm. Put your tanks in a group with a bard singing Psalm of Purity to pump disease resist and have a necro DMF the raid for additional disease resist. His charm is disease based with no modifier, so pumping up your disease resist should allow you to resist this often. Normally I tank him by entrance to where the taskmasters/mines are in a little corner by the doorway and rest of raid is by the little walled area in the middle of the room where you enter the basement. Healers check their range on me before starting the encounter, and then when I have Cursed where I want them raid nudges up to where they are in range to heal me/nuke Cursed.

His other AE is poison based and short range. With a simple CH rotation it's not even worth bothering to try to resist. Other tanks just call out when they need heals. If MT goes down next tank in line just AE taunts immediately to prevent Cursed from running to the raid and healers switch to new tank.

If he gets into the main body of the raid, chaos supreme will reign, although it's funny as hell when it does happen. :D
