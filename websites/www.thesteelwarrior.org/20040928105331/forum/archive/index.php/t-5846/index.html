Phantron
You do not react to damage taken in Ikkinz trials. Doing so will guaranteed a quick death. You just have your healers blast heal (or CH if you're brave) as fast as possible and hope the mob dies before you run out of mana.

Reducing DPS is therefore not as useful as reducing spikes because you do not save the healer's mana either way. Evasive almost certainly will beat Stonewall in damage reduced, but it does not beat Stonewall in spikes reduced (a mob can still hit 4 times a row under evasive for higher spikes). Also Stonewall lets you do more damage than Evasive, and that difference is significant when you're looking at a fight where every bit of DPS counts.

The only reason to use Evasive would be if you've 2 clerics doing a CH chain on you.
