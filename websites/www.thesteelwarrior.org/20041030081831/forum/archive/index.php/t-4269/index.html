Well, you're never going to have "instant" aggro, no matter what you do. The best you can hope for is to put as many hate/DD-stun procs on your weapons as you can fit. There are still going to be times when you're going 30-40 seconds without a proc, but they should be far less rare.

If you truly don't think that an SoD/Copper Hammer combo is suffiient aggro for you, then yeah, you might wanna augment that if you don't see yourself getting a Bloodfrenzy/Blade of War any time soon.
