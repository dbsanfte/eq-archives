VZ has larger aggro radius and a bunch of guards who spawn on top of him. This has been the case since the script was changed when GoD was released. Use a DA'ed pal/shad/bard with sow/shrew to pull VZ and every mob in the map room out the opposite side and away from your raid. If the DA'ed person gets VZ and all adds far enough away, VZ reaches his tether and warps back to map room, alone, where he can then be pulled singly.

Yes, the first few times my guild encountered this change, we got premature aggro with 6-8 adds, and only won once in 5 attempts. Now we have this portion of the event covered pretty easily, but respawns in the hall while we kill vz/rz and all of RZtWs adds still give us trouble.
