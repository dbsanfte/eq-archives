Emilia is exactly right. The best way to get better at tanking is to get experience up to the point where you dont really think about what you're doing at all.

As for cornering Vindi...
You have to make sure your guild stays well away from the corner where you plan to tank him and when the pull comes in, the puller will FD and you have to be the closest person to him so that he agros you. Give him a few whacks and as you do this back into the corner. This is pretty much it for Vindi, although you will prolly find it easier to corner him with the illusion of a smaller race on.

In terms of when to call your guild in...
At your lvl there is no way you have an EB weapon yet, so you will have to figure out what point your agro goes from static, to 100% onto you. After a bit, you just know when you can call assist

Best of luck,

The whole "You'll have a feeling" answer probably is not what you are looking for, but it is pretty much what happens. At your level you can afford to be conservative, take your time and make sure you have aggro locked. As you get more experience you can start reducing how much time you take.
