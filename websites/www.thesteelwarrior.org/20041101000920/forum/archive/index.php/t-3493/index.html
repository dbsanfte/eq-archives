Amazingly, 15k doesn't twink a warrior very far, especially if you're looking for gear that will last a very long time (ie post 50). Therefore, you should separate your gearing into pre-50 and post-50 twinkage.

Pre-50, stuff like Crustacean Shell, Acrylia Plate, and Cobalt are all very viable. Add inexpensive weapons like a Lamentation, Centi SS, and others with a low delay and a ratio around 2, and you're set. SCHW is a popular haste item, and would easily last you to 50. And of course, more stuff you can get with both HP and no level restrictions, the better.

Post 50, your 15k won't even begin to make a dent in what you could buy. Fierce Heraldric is of course a good option, but you will only be able to afford a few pieces before your twink fund is exhausted. But worry about post 50 gear once you're there.
