The only one I can think of is Ssolet Dnaas (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=4382), and he's only good for range attacks. He's on a little island in warsliks woods. You can just hug the coast till you see an iksar kickin it all by himself on a tiny island. You can stand on the coast or even on his island and bow/throw till the cows come home. He's got mega regen and won't attack unless you get in melee range. For archery, just bring your bloody ancille's pouch and a few stacks of reagents. For throwing... get a mage to summon you A LOT OF whatever orrr... there's the dusty soriz worker pouch (http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=10563) that summons a 170 range throwing item - it comes from ssra heh. And I just now found a quest that involves some turnins in skyshrine and thurg and MAY net you a bracer of hammerfall (http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4260) that summons throwing hammers. (Personally I'm going to look into doing this one)

As for working on melee skills... I don't know what to tell you other than, "Hey, do you guys mind if I practice 2hb on your mobs?" It worked when I was lvl 40 in DL, and pretty much guaranteed a spot in their group when someone left lol.

The easiest way to get skill ups with no risk is to contact your local pet user with /pet hold skill. You can skill up on the pet and it won't attack you back. Even better if you whack it from behind, you won't get riposte (although it's minimal anyway).

The 61+ pets all work well for it (cept the mage fire pet)
