One of the big problems with shields is that Knights already have a fair number of decent 1handers in the game designed to be used with a shield. While Warriors really don't have many, if any. All of our 1handers have been designed with the idea of our duel wielding. Our aggro generation is designed with the idea of duel wielding, although this is less true these days with incite, it's still true.. Using a 1hander and shield a Warrior would lose significantly more than a Knight and it would be difficult to make up for that for the Warrior without boosting the Knight more at the same time. Shields are very problematic.
