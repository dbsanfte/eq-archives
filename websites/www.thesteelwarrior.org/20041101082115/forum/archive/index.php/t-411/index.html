Well, I'm back, been off the boards a couple days, but I had to relate how proud I was of my guild last night. My wife and I logged in and I started my usual nightly, "who wants to group with Deeliah and I and go somewhere strange?" routine. I got a couple of responses (one from the guild leader) saying, "where are we going" So I think, all right, lets try this idea out. So I window out and check EQ atlas for ideas, and come up with ToFS. At first everyone was a little hesitant, but we managed to gather up a Chanter and Shaman, Two Druids, the GL's Monk, myself, and a Ranger. The GL's wifes Druid dropped us off in Iceclad, and we headed toward the tower. We got inside and set up groups (Since there were more then 6 of us we got the raid set up). I then started pulling stuff from the first floor. I must say I was impressed by the way our gguild worked together! We quickly got a few extra keys (I got one and the two Druids each got one, the Monk already had one), alot of Gnome Skin stuff, and alot of cash it seemed. We headed up to the second floor, and the first kill there, we got Ro's Fiery Sundering for one of our Druids! We started making our way around the bookcases, moving the group as we went, and got to the back right corner when guess what dropped? Teacher's Syllabus! A rarer drop I am told. Things are looking good for us. We continue around and complete the circuit, and start again. We get halfway down the left row when guess what drops again? Teacher's Syllabus!

I won't bore you with any of the other little details, but all in all we had a great time. This was the first "raid" I had ever been on, and I think our guild really proved to itself that it (at least these particular members) could work together to get something done.

Very promising when you consider our average level is 40. Good practice anyway!

Well, that's my story!
