Hes right but for the wrong reasons.

riposte mod does diddly

25 AC is nice, but its maybe a 1-2% overall increase in your tanking ability

125 hp is nice, but its maybe a 1-2% overall increase in your tanking ability depending what its replacing

The real reason you should hold out for a bloodfrenzy, is because no other 1h proccing weapon in game comes anywhere near the aggro a BF does due to its high innate proc rate.

Bloodfrenzy procs 50% more per minute than a BoC or Hategiver.

Bloodfrenzy + boc or hategiver offhand = top aggro in game (yes more aggro in the long run than Blade of War, although your relying on procs rather than DPS)

Getting a bloodfrenzy if you currently have say BoC+ primal or some other combo will up your aggro by 50% or more.

Hold out for the bloodfrenzy, there is no replacement for it on fights where guild DPS (and therefore MT aggro generation) is key.
