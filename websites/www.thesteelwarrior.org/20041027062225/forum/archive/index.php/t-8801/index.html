Lathen
I used the BoI for 5-8 levels or so and loved the weapon for its ratio, its looks, and its utility for day-to-day exp groups. It is not an aggro weapon, though.

The ratio makes for nice DPS, and that jagged scimitar look is really cool. The proc is the hard part. I never once saw a mob whip around towards me when the itching madness proc went off (imagine for a second the way a mob turns to you when the Sword of the Bloodsworn procs). On the plus side, my group mates had an easier time landing their spells on the mob once my proc had gone off.

So -- it's a very mixed report from my end. I loved the weapon, but I had to give it up because I just couldn't rely on it to keep my friends from harm.
