So the servers go down and I'm running through Droga with my wizard. I see this Earth Mystic Gedak dude up, so I pull him. I screw up the pull and get myself pumelled.

I come back and some druid has taken him. Oh well I say to myself. I'm going to get some revenge.

So I camp him for a bit, and he pops after a couple placedholders. He drops his common, ornate long sword of slaughter. Crappy warrior weapon, but should net me 7-10k on Stromm, not bad.

Next time he pops he's not wielding the ornate long sword, so I'm thinking he must have his rare. I get my revenge and he drops his rare, the Monstrous Flamberge of Alacrity! I'm looking at it. It is 25/23. So the damage bonus is pretty weak and it's 10AC, 65 HPS. I have a velium gemmed great sword (got a GSot4W later that night), so it's definitely not an upgrade. So I'm thinking, ah well another decent sword to sell.

I decide to equip it to check it out.

Holy crap that is a freaking cool looking sword! I think it's the best looking sword I've seen in EQ. Two eagle heads on each side of the hilt, big blue gem in the middle. Cool shaped gold blade with sweet looking runes carved on it. Yeah, I know, a weapon should be for utility, but I'm keeping this bad boy for style for a long time to come. Screw doing my epic, this sword looks way better....
