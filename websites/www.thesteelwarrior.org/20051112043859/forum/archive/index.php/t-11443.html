Pickup groups suck. Harcore.

I have met more morons/idiots/whatever in 4 months of WoW than I did in 4 years of Everquest. Granted, I've done a few pickup lbrs/DM/strath 5-mans that were damn good, as well as the occasional pickup strath/scholo raid, but on the whole, pickup groups usually have at least 1 incompetent member. If it's the priest, you're screwed. If it's the tank, you're usually screwed (I play a warlock in WoW, no longer a warrior =/ )

But yeah, as a warlock, I get to seduce/banish mobs for crowd control. I also Curse of Doom said mobs (when we pull 3+) to do basically a free 3200 nuke. Most annoying thing in WoW so far for me.

Loose mobs in WoW kill people fast, and a group that realizes and controls this has a good shot of accomplishing their goals.
