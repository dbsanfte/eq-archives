Probably talking about the 'Symbol of the Planemasters' http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=20898 which has a rightclick buff which adds a 75dd + 0.0sec stun proc to all weapons.

Easy to obtain? It drops from Saryn in PoTime, so I guess that depends on what level your guild is at. The only other options on a simlar idea are:

Orb of the Sky ::: VT - Diablo Xi Xin ::: 35dd + 0.0sec stun
Gloves of the Crimson Sigil ::: VT - Aten Ha Ra ::: 65dd + 0.0sec stun

Gloves probably come out as 'best' because they have only a 0.5sec cast time meaning you can recast midfight, at the expense of only 10damage under symbol. Symbol has a 3.0 cast time and Orb is 10.0, making it near impossible to cast while tanking.
