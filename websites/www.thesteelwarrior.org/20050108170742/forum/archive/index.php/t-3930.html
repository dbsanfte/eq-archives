There is no need of clearing or break in at all.

Just have someone with some HP (warrior does fine), get invised and zone in.

Get as much aggro as he can and Kite every things away and keep kiting until all corpses are dragged to the safe points (west wall) and rez.

For the kiter, there are several ways to get him out. CoTH works the best or you can just do the timing and evacing.

This same method can be used for initial zone in so no one will get unnecessary aggro (except the kiter) before getting to the zone in.

Just dun aggro those DT golems while kiting. Sow is enough to outrun, only thing to worry is being rooted.
