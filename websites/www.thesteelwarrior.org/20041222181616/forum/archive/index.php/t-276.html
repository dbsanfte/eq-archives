haste > AC

haste is probably the most important stat for any melee. Nothing else you do will increase your DPS by a single item more than a haste item, and if you duo with your wife a lot, your DPS is important. Making things dead 22% faster is gonna be of more benefit to you than the slight reduction you'd get from increased AC.

But don't take my word for it. Run some tests yourself. Go to Velks, or Seb or Chardok and kill some beasties, swapping the SCHW for the Mym's gaunts after a while and see how it feels to you. Maybe get a parser and log your fights. Do a hunt for an hour with the Myrm gaunts and see how much damage you take per fight with the increased AC.. Then do the same with the SCHW and see if the decreased kill time has lowered the damage you take, even with lower AC.

I'd recommend Velks, cos the spiders don't run so you won't need snare, and they're all pure-warrior mobs (some are rogues, don't turn your back)

Personally I would sell the SCHW and buy a FBSS. You will go from 22% to 21% item haste. You also will lose 3 raw AC (5 equipped). You will gain however 90hps and 5 dex.

Personally I am not sure it is an upgrade now but it is for the future. I don't think it is a downgrade either though now. This has to be about the closest swap I have seen. More than likely though you will come out with 1k pp ahead though since the SCHW's are more valued than a FBSS.

As to duoing with your wife's shaman, you will probably see things die about 0.6% slower (assuming 50% spell haste, 171/172 = 0.994) so it will have a small negative impact on your duoing as the hps won't help you with a shaman healer. You already have enough hps for the shaman mini cheal. For a druid or cleric though your extra hps helps with their versions of Cheal.

You may be able to take the extra pp from the SCHW's and upgrade another piece of equipment with it to make up for the AC loss. I really doubt you will ever "notice" the 1% of haste change.

Not sure any of my rambling helps, bottom line reasons I would change are this:

1) SCHW's probably are only going to continue to come down in price

2) traditional haste upgrades are more easily obtained for belt/back slots

3) you will be hard pressed to upgrade over myrmidom gauntlets

4) you are farming HOT I assume where the 90hps helps with aoe's.

5) you should come out pp ahead for other upgrades

6) you gain 90 hps for 5 ac
