Earth you can spawn the events whenever you want, however they only drop loot once every 3 days. Your options (for the loot versions) are to keep track of the time of death, use something like ShowEQ, or go blind. If you are after the Mystical Arbitor of Earth, doing "dummy" rings is a good way to pop him.

Boss names are Monstrous Mudawalker (mud), Peregrin Rockskull (stone), Derugoak Bloodwalker (vine) and Perfected Warder of Earth (dust).

Air, the avatars do have another mob that pops when they are up, I can't remember the names, either.
