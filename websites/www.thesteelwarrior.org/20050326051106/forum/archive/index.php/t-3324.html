This has come up from time to time since PoP released and I have always been a huge supporter of VT first. Not only will your guild pick up valuable raid experience and strat lessons, there is no better source for high end loot in the game pre-elementals.

On the old boards one of the first times this discussion came up someone posted that their guild had gotten to RZtW and had never been to VT. They where about 3-4 months behind the guilds that did VT first.

You can certainly get all the way to time without doing VT, but you will spend a lot more time and effort gearing people up and you will have a much harder time breaking in to farm mode in the elems without VT gear. The FT, haste, effects, and Vengeance you get out of VT will make your PoP progression 10x easier.

Ask Shik and his guild about this. Their guild leader decided to go the VT route after reading the discussion on the old boards. While a few people where not happy about it at the time, I think it is safe to say that it was a good decision now. They went from being well over an expansion back of the top guilds (AoW/NToV when PoP released) to just a couple of months behind (in elems just after Time was beat).

Well I just finished up keying for emp and VT so let me post some of my experiences.

Emp Key: At 65 ssra is a cakewalk, you can put together just about any group and destroy the place and really ssra isn't terrible xp. It took me maybe 10-20 hours doing various stuff in ssra to get my key but this was grouping with other guildmates who also needed their keys and rolling on drops. C1 / C2 are the biggest headaches, pouch is very easy to get and c3 will just take one mini raid to get a ton of insignia. Honestly the emp key is a nice ring. I almost would say its worth getting just for the ring itself so getting a useful piece of gear from the quest is a nice side benefit

Emp Bane: Getting the ore for the bane sucked, it took me maybe another 10-20 hours for the ore. The good thing is that the mines in ssra are pretty fun and better xp than the snakes. If you can put together an AoE group down here you can really clean up the place and should get your bane ore pretty fast. A side benefit is that the ssra shard drops in the mines and isn't too rare. You should have everyone outfitted with shards by the time you get your ore

Shards: I honestly found the shards to be pretty fun. Half of them are very easy to get and you can two box them very quickly. The other half require any sort of group with a healer but are really not too bad. I found it sorta fun to go back ot the old luclin zones like the deep and AC and AR and destroy stuff. The only bad shards for me were ME and FG but even those weren't terrible. I think it took me basically 1 week + a long weekend to get all my shards and the shard box.

Overall keying for VT wasn't as bad as I feared it would be. Some parts were actually pretty fun.

10-06-2003, 08:03 PM
Emp Key:

Com 1 - Can be duo'd (my GF and I duo'd with SK and enc for ours) easier with 1 group as you can clear more spawn points. Spawn rate was about 1 per hour.

Com 2 - Probably could duo this too. Did this with 1 group, Spawn rate was approx 1 commander every 30mins. I think we we're lucky on this one, but everyone had their comm2 bit after about 3hrs.

Insignia - Usually do these on our way to emp with a raid force, but one groupable, much easier with 2, can get 2-3 named per spawn so 2 every 30 mins.

Pouch - Usually about 3 pouches when doing cursed cycle. Most Taskmasters are one groupable, some can be duo'd. 3 every 2hrs and you can farm ore and shards while waiting for respawn.

Shards:

Acrylia Caverns: Seemed a pretty common drop. Had guildies farming loot in AC who would advertise shards over /gu for people to run in and loot, drop rate was 1-2 per 30mins. Wizzy's, Mages and Necros can all solo this easily. Probably want atleast a healer partner as a warrior tho.

Akheva: This one sucked for me, could duo it, but was much easier 5ppl to a group. Drop rate was about 1 per hour but opinion from most ppl I spoke to its usually much more common.

DSP: Warriors can solo this easy. Both my GF and I got our shards within 20mins here and I haven't heard any horror stories about bad drop rates so I assume its pretty common.

Fungus Grove: This one seemed rare for me. Farmed with enc + SK duo, drop rate was about 1 per 2hrs.

Maiden's Eye: This one seems to be accepted as the worst shard to get. The 2 I farmed took about 1hr each solo'd as an Enchanter, though I have heard of 10 camps not being too uncommon.

Scarlet Desert: Can solo as a warrior, got my shard from here at lvl 50 something helping a friend quest the shield of bane warding, saw heaps rot, drop rate was about 1 every 30 mins.

Seru: Easy to duo. Spent 4 hours clearing the ring around the top and didnt see any shards. Got a tip to kill the zone in spawns and have been getting them 1 per 30 mins since then.

Ssra: About 1 per hour, can be duo'd but would be alot slower. I found this a hard one to get people down there to farm, but once we got going the drop rate was reasonable.

The Deep: I've heard this is easier since the revamp, but before it took a group to fight down and over the bridge. About an hour to clear in and then one per 30 mins after that.

The Grey: I've heard of warriors soloing this, but reckon it wouldnt be easy. I solo'd these as a chanter, warrior can duo with a healer easy. About 1 per 45 mins.


Considering that Drop rate I would say I put about 20hrs into my own VT key, ofcourse what makes it such a time sink is that you have to do it for every member of your guild. The key to this is your guild's attitude and if you have every member out for themselves its going to be a long road to VT.

When I did my key we had a regular group of about 8 of us who worked together to complete all of our keys. This meant that when we started a shard camp, eg Ssra we would stay there until everyone had their shard and if we couldnt finish it that night we'd go back the next day for those who missed out before moving on to the next bit. There was no selfishness and no only staying until you got your shard then leaving before the rest of the group had thiers, that sort of attitude is what makes VT keys hell.
