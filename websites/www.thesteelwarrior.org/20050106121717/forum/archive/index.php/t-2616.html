The least expensive upgrade I can see atm for you is the Velium Fire Wedding Rings, 6AC/65HP's. I am guessing they will run 350-400pp each so it will eat up a considerable share of your hard earned pp. After that I would try to get your guild into Kael to farm armor. At times when you are hunting for experience, take a healer and maybe another melee or damage dealer and hunt seafury cyclops in Ocean of Tears. The exp won't be that good but the cash and gem/jewelry drops helps the pp situation and you get a little exp along the way. Seb could be another alternative to that as well although more difficult.

As far as campable upgrades, I will suggest a few more. Take a full group to Burning Woods and kill the named monkey (Gullerback?) there for a tattered mantle, shoulders ac12/20hps. Go to Maiden's Eye and kill the robed figures for Axeroths (sp) belt of defense, AC20/15STR/15WIS. Crystal Eyes in Velks lab drops a nice face piece, 8AC/35HP's (good exp camp). Giants in Frontier Mountains drop a 10AC belt if the one from ME is out (those ME mobs are fairly high level). Can't remember the name of the mob, but one in Scarlet Desert drops a bracer of defense, AC15/3STA/20HP's (not sure on exact stats).

These all could be possible things you could get with a full group or two (not sure of your guilds gear/levels/etc.) These won't be extremely easy mobs so don't underestimate them, bring plenty. Like in ME there are some nice drops for many classes I think so maybe a guild could make a day of it. If ME proves to be easy I would take them to Umbral Planes right next door. There is Moktor armor that drops there and some pieces would be upgrades for you. There is armor there that drops for all classes although the shammy armor from there sucks for some reason.

Hope these ideas help. Before I would try to kill these I would research them a lil bit on Allah's for possible drops, levels of mobs, etc so you know how many to bring. But to me it looks as if your guild should start heading to Kael to get armor if the rest of your guild is similar in level for equipment.

As far as aggro, a Frostbringer is a very good weapon, unfortunately will be out of your price range for a while I think, 5-7K probably. I have heard that a Black Blade of Tormenting has a good aggro proc on it and is fairly cheap I think <1k. A quested no drop weapon Praetors Blade of Debilaty (sp) is suppose to be similar in aggro as well and it is free. Some other cheaper alternatives for aggro might be a Journeyman's walking stick (tash), Silken whip of Ensnaring (snare), Kunzar Kujuch (engulfing darkness) or even a 3/20 whip from Blackburrow that procs Stun.
