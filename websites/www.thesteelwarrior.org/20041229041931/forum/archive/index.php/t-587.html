05-12-2003, 06:18 PM
Cowl = -10 AC +5 Sta (25ish hps) +15 raw hps over current helm

CoF = +50 raw hitpoints over current cloak.

So just getting one or the other, the CoF is a better upgrade. Now, if he were to get a cloak of the maelstrom AND the cowl, it would be a net gain of 15 Sta (roughly 75 hps) and 70 raw hitpoints for -9 AC.

So, Cowl + Maelstrom = -9 AC +145ish hps
CoF = +50 hps

Basically it's 95 hps vs 9 ac. Pretty much an even tradeoff if you believe the 10 hps = 1 ac theory. Cowl and Maelstrom will be a lot more expensive. Also, I'd bet it's easier to find a high ac/hp helm than cloak.

Cowl of Mortality was uber back in the days of the AC soft cap. If you value AC though, it's still a good item, but it is very low ac for the helm slot on a warrior.

Nice gear, looks like you are set up pretty well. I really wish my warrior could wear FH!! Here are some suggestions with current TT pricing -

Heavy rubicite belt - 25-35k. I wouldn't bother with this for a while, only a small upgrade in your case.

Bone carved shortbow - 40kish if you can find one. These seem to e pretty rare. Also not a very big upgrade for the price. I think you would be better off saving for an elemental bow. Currently on my server you can get bows from 150k (Air bow) to 220k (earth bow). ITs a huge upgrade and most likely the last range item you will ever need.

Crystallized acid pauldrons, wrist - acid bracers are going for about 100-125k on TT. Never seen the shoulders for sale. The wrists are the last upgrade you will need before getting Raex's. You might want to consider Horn Spiked Shoulder Plates, only 5hp up grade, but the 31ac rocks. You can get those for 30-40k.

Amygdalan mask - Never seen one of these for sale. You will probably have to go and get this yourself. Not too bad for a group of 60+. If you happen to find one for sale I would expect to see a pretty hefty price tag on it, upewards of 75-100k.

Boots of the storm - These are starting to get a little more common. They seem to be popping up for around 125k on Terris.

I would try to score Boots first, then 2x acid bracers. From there just save your plats until you can afford an elemental bow.
