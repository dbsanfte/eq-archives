Originally posted by Welini
And could someone please explain "proccing"? /em sighs

Proc'ing is short hand for processing - this is when a weapon with an effect on it processes an occurrence of that effect firing.

For instance - velium weapons have an effect of Velium Strike listed on them. Every time you swing (not every time youhit, but every time you swing), you have a random chance of that weapon processing that effect - or essentially of the weapon casting that spell.

That casting of the spell is what is referred to as a proc.

Some of the spells affect the mob you're attacking, some affect you, and some affect both the mob and you. There's even a few that proc an aoe type spell :)

Hope that helps :D
