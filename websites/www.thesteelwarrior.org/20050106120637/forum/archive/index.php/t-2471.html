Cloak of Thorns - Lady Nevedria. This is an extremely annoying fight, and the cloak rarely drops, but who's to argue with 100HP on the back?

Gaudralek, Sword of the Sky - Lord Vyemm. Excelent ratio (43/38), 100HP, and 179DD unresistable proc. I wield one and love it.

Onyx Gaunlets - Lord Vyemm. 100HP Gauntlets. If you've got SS Gaunts, pass on these.

Ring of the Sky - Eashen of the Sky. 90HP and 10STA on a ring.

Mask of the Sky - Eashen of the Sky. 90HP mask along with excelent resists.

Belt of the Destroyer - Dagarn the Destroyer. 41% Haste and 75HP belt.

Greaves of the Deep Sea - Lord Koi'Doken. 55HP and Enduring Breath. Excelent greaves if you're agnostic/non- RZ.

Thats about all I can think of for top-notch NToV items. I'd concentrate on the Sword from Vyemm, Cloak from Lady Nev, and Belt from Dagarn.
