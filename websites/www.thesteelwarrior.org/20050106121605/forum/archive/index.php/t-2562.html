For essences of sunlight, you can kill sand giants in oasis/south ro or chromodracs skyfire. Bring a healer partner if needed. (You will for the chromos, not sure about the giants.)

As for upgrading, junk the black pearl ring and neck and forest loops ASAP.
Ears: electrum black sapphire earring (2ac, 35hp) or Jagged bone hoop (6ac, 40hp, 5sv fire/disease)
Fingers: plat fire opal (5ac, 55hp) or velium fire opal (6ac, 65hp) are pretty hard to beat til you can afford valorium rings.
Neck: Clattering bone necklace (6ac, 6str/sta, 30hp) or an advanced cultural neck (usually 14ac, 5agi and 25 or more hp) are good choices.

Those items should not be too hard to find in your bazaar. :)
