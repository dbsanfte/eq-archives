Fenring
Nothing to add on Tunare herself but there are a couple of encounters while clearing you need to watch out for.

Ail the Elder: Will probably give you more trouble than Tunare, he roams so keep an eye on him until your'e ready, he has an AE silence and if it lands on your healers you could be in trouble. If you keep him moving with push it will keep him from silencing everyone, just kill him fast as you can. Silence is avoidable with high MR.

Guardians of Tunare: 2 panthers, one has AE silence and one dispells. Kill them seperately and they aren't too bad but if you get them at the same time it can be rough.

Thifling Focusers: Not bad but a pain in the ass, don't pull them at all, they have a million HP and a small hit box so dps sux against them. They don't assist Tunare so just avoid them altogether.
