gulug
For the first time in a while last nigh in BoT i was not the MT so i figure id have some fun doing some DPS parses and swopping round some weapon combinations.

For background info, all my melee skills are maxed, we had a bard in the group, parses were done via YALP and all melee was from the rear. My stats stayed about the same throughout (although there may have been a couple of dropped songs). I do not have any offensive AAs.

The weapons i tried were

Flaming Dirk 11(+3 Fire dmg)/ 20
Copper Hammer 13/20
Sword of Bloodsworn 12/19
Greatstaff of Thunder 31/30

For all test runs Id have a minimum of 5 giants and Id clear the logs before each run.

Anyway the results took me a little by surprise.

With the Hammer in primary and the dirk in secondary, i was getting in the region of 76-79 DPS.

Then i swopped out to the GSoT and was shocked to see it parsing at only about 70-73 DPS. I was surprised to see this as i was always under the impression that a 2hander with a ratio of 1 or less would outdamage most 1 hander combos. Anyway just to be sure there wasnt a mistake i reparsed these fights a few times and the 1hander combo came out consistently higher.

Even swopping to the hammer/bloodsworn combo, i was still getting a dps of 69-72 which was not off the GSoT figures.

Finally i also parsed the flaming dirk in primary and hammer in secondary and that returned a figure of around 78-79 as well.

I always proceeded on the basis of 2 hander proc for exp group and for added dps and the 1 handers for aggro situations but on these resuslts, i may reconsider that.
