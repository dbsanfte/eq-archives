Oh yah, as for real advice...

It really depends on how fast you think your crew can do 32k of damage to something. (32k is safest to think about, Spiroc Lord etc, have less hps)

If you can do 32k in less than 2 minutes, then it is usually safe to just let your warrior take a DT if they have agro and have someone else start tanking.

If you think 32k damage is going to take 4 minutes or more, you better be ready to have another class tanking during the DT parts of the fight.

The final consideration is how much tanking power and healing power you have. If you think there is another tank in the raid that can handle getting hit for 300 or so (think that is Golem hits) I believe he doubles...then it is okay for you to die. Obviously they need a healer in group that can handle that much melee.

The best 2nd tanks are paladins or SKs or a warrior with AE taunt.

Once the first tank goes down, make sure your secondary tanks know that they must get agro immediately.

Usually though, its not like 5 friends walk into fear and decide to start killing Golems. I have been on one or two raids out of 40 or so where you couldnt just Zerg the hell out of the Golems or Spiroc Lord or whatever.

Their epic pieces at the appropriate levels (55-60ish) are in huge demand, so people tend to bring more than 12 people to those fights hehe.
