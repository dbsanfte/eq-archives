2 old world suggestions...

1) umbral plains. the moktors like most things in the zone summon but thats no problem for a warrior. the no drop moktor armor may be an upgrade for you and there are a number of named or quasi-named mobs that drop decent sellable gear. you can do them safely at the zone-in and its not usually heavily camped.
2) maidens eye toilers (toiler hill near the lake. again not heavily camped. most are dark blue to 61 a few are light blue. they are among the easiest things to kill in the zone and have extremely good tradeskill loot if you can tolerate the bazaar. this is best done if the druid is skilled with pets and then you would assist the pet and tank for him. be warned. the pet will outdamage you about 4 to 1 and this isnt good for your ego but its very good for your experience. and again its safer than the plains. its comparable for experience at your level. it gives you an excuse to do aa since if you level past about 62 you will have to leave the area due to proliferation of light blues.

yes after the wolf breaks charm he hits extremely hard and fast. only sensible way to tank it is if you have furious up and nothing better to do with it than have fun slaughtering a hasted wolf.
