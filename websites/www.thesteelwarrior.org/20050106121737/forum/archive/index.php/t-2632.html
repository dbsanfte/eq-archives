Looking pretty good to me.

The first thing I would do is ditch your two existing wrist slots in favor of two Fierce Heraldic Bracers (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12356). That would boost your AC quite considerably as well as add some HP. Not to mention, the added saves from both items combined are priceless.

Second thing I would do is start saving for Kizrak's Armband of Battle (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17890). That would more than double both the AC and HP gained in that slot over the Hardened Grey Flesh Sleeves.

Get rid of the Shortblade of Lightning in favor of a Copper Hammer of Striking (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=22010). The agro gained from this weapon should be doublefold, if not triplefold, over what's in that slot now. You might lose STA at the outset, but just remember a shaman can buff stamina quite considerably.

I'm quite biased when I say this, but you would thank me in the lost run. Go and quest the Loyalist Shield of Honor (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=15971). A 50ac/75hp backslot item is damn near priceless considering PoP mob damage output scales according to AC. If you're going to be entering NToV any time soon, then a Cloak of Thorns (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6423) off Lady Nevedaria may be something you will want to cross your fingers for instead. However, if you're not, the shield is attainable with less than two groups with rougly 1-3 days worth of work. It's really bloody easy actually.

Lastly, work your faction and buy a Gauntlets of the Myrmidon (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=5550) MQ. Those gaunts are about as good as you'll ever get until you can get your hands on Gauntlets of Dragon Slaying, which involves killing Lord Yelinak.
