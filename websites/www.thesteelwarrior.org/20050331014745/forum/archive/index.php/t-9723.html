With a good cleric and shaman, and some solid DPS, you shouldn't have any problems tanking the krigers or named mobs. I prefer to tank them in defensive myself (65/21, CS2/CA2/ND1), but it's certainly not necessary, especially once they've been slowed. The only thing you have to be careful of is the DD procs. Some of them hit for 1.5k DD, and if you're extremely unlucky (like I was with Gaukr twice in one night), they can take you down hard and fast.

Even with my moderate at best gear, I could tank most everything in BoT at 63. Your gear is a little better than mine, so you shouldn't have many issues. Some of the harder nameds will keep you on the edge though. Good luck =)
