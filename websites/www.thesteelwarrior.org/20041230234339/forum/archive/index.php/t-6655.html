How long does the HP increase from Tribute last? I don't understand the Tribute system and have been unable to figure out how it works, as far as duraction and so on.

As long as your tribute points last. For instance, if you have 5000 points banked, and have HP5 & AC5, for a total of 60pts (rough estimate). Every 10 mins you are going to be charged 60 points.

So in one hour, you will be charged:

60 x 6 = 360 pts.

For 5000 pts you have total in tribute system remaining, these 2 effects would last you approximately:

5000/360 = 13.88 hours.

Hope this explains tribute system. Its a nice way of getting effects/bonuses/zists, but you have to keep it going by giving loot away. LDON hard loot can really help in this aspect.
