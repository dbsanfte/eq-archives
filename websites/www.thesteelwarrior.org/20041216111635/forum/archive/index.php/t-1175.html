Most of my duo exp comes from Crypt of Decay.
I did a lot of tier one when i was bored and the cleric was not yet 60 (i didnt want to bother anyone to push him through exe trial although i had the offer before that).
PoI entrance, not bad, often camped though, exp not that good (at least not for a level 65).
PoN, tried left side from GY toward the canyon, could single pull easy, but not that hot, snare whip highly recommended.
PoD, never really tried.
PoV, duod outer caves for a bit, not too hard but highly contested.
Halls of Honor, probably very nice, but hard to get to a camp due to being dependant on potions for invis.
Tactics, nice exp, but dont try without kei on cleric and a snare whip is a must.
PoS, did frog cave, not really worth it though, desert worked better, pulling the cactus type mobs for reasonable exp and some cash loot, risky with adds as snakes are sometimes hard to see and assist the cactus for some reason(they are non agro usually).
CoD is always my first choice for my duo. When i want to get an AA especially bad, i dont group them, just bot the cleric.
Have to use my clerics earrings then to cure disease or poison depending on mob after kill.
With pacification you will not have any problems to break entrance and i can for the most part single pull without knowing pathing, proximity agro.
Wake of Tranquility works ok as well unless there are some greater foul/corrupted puslings up that are above the level limit (drop some crappy pally only 1hander or cirled toe bones).
I'd suggest to get a KEI for your cleric.
I had a hard time keeping up with the healing when i had ~40 or 50 AA.
Now i can do with 2 to 4 Complete heals, depending how fast slow takes on the mob and with my cleric having ft1, mc1 and scm3 he doesnt need kei anymore to keep up on healing.
The greaters are bad news to you usually, as they cannot be snared or slowed and hit for 600+.
I can down them now but it uses around 80 percent of my clerics mana pool for 8 to 9 percent aaxp (solo).
regular puslings give you around 4.2 percent aaxp (21 percent for 5 kills).
Drops are tainted planar essence (sells well to peeps dabbling in pottery), bricks of ethereal energy, strands of ether,all 3 diamond types (raw, blue or regular diamond) as rare, some foul liquid, chunk of flesh as cash loot.

Just check the places, might no longer as easy as it used to be as you are dependent on a few select spots where you wont get adds and a snare whip.
