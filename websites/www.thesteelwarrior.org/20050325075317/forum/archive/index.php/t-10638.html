I was really wondering this question a while ago after Tacvi got partially revamped a bit, but I wanted to wait until a few guilds got their feet wet with the 3 loots, 5-day respawn kinda thing. I've taken a look at a lot of the loot in there and some of it looks to be really nice. The real question is though, is Tacvi worth all the effort getting there and farming it out? My guild is currently at the MPG trial stage having Corruption and Foresight finished out already. I guess you could call that a "tier", but some trials are easier than others, so I dunno. We defeated King Gelaqua (Watergel, wtf?) and Queen Pyrilonis quite easily and have basically gone on an OoW rampage farming out Feratha, Chailak, Volklana, Rhorious, Hulcror, King/Queen, etc whenever we can find them up. As far as GoD progression goes though, we haven't even gotten to Noqufiel yet. It's not that we couldn't probably do it, but we've only stepped foot into Inktu`ta a total of two times since defeating Barxt.

I guess the real question is this: The goal, obviously, is Anguish, but would it be a strategically sound idea to open up Tacvi (and Txevu) as another source of loot? Or should we just stick to the guns with what we're killing already and set a straight sail to Anguish?

Any and all opinions are appreciated.
