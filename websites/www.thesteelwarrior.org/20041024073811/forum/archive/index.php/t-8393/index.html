Ansoul
The following is a walk-thru compiled from many different sites and my personial notes. Forums information drawn from are here, Champions of Cabilis. and The Unfinished Quest Board. One thing to note I did not include Trivals for any of the Weapon Combines, as all were trivial at 180.

There are three sections. First section is the In order of quests. Second Section is a list of zones and what you need from those zones. Third Section is the Recipies for the Combines. Hope that it helps up and comming Legionnaires.



Geozite Tool
After heading to the top of the Legion Guild Hall in New Sebilis and giving Drill Master Vygan your note. Ask him "what Geozite tool" to recieve a 2 slot pack that you put 2 Large Scorpian Pincers (FoB) in and return to him to recive a Note. Now run into Field of Bone and give that note to Warlord Zyzz. If he does not give you a box for Charred Bone Shards. Repeate until he does.

Kill and loot 4 Charred Miltia bones. They can be found upon Miltia Skeletons that spawn inside of FoB to the right of the path leading to the pit (near FoB Book) from Town. Combine and return to Warlord Zyzz. Bring his note back to Drill Master Vygan in the Legion Hall. You will gain Geozite Tool. Something you will become very farmilar with over the course of all the rest of the quests.

Miltia Pike
Ask Drill Master Vygan "what Miltia Pike" He will send you on a quest into the Swamp (SoNH) to kill a Froglok Escort. Head into the Swamp and go left roam around there (or bring Tracker with you) at night to fine the Escort Kill it and loot the Fife and Bixle Berry Pie .Make sure to that the pie is not the first food item in your inventory Put it into a bag your first bag should be food.. put it into second bag.

Head back into Town go to the third building from the bank on your right if leaving bank. Give Pie to Baker.. The one when you Hail actually responds to you. This will spawn a Traitor just outside of the building. Simply kill the Traitor and loot the head.

Return to Legion Hall and give Drill Master Vygan the Head, Fife and your Partisan Pike to Recieve Miltia Pike.

Footman Pike
Head down to the landing halfway down stairs and speak with Drill Master Kyg. Ask her "what weapons of our Foes" to recive a 4 slot bag. Kill 2 Goblin Hunters in the Lake generally between town and the first Statue and place their spears within the bag. Head back to the Swamp and Kill 2 Froglok Bounders generally found on the second or third island along the trail straight out in Swamp place 2 spears into the Bag. Combine and return to Drill Master Kyg along with Miltia Pike to recieve Plans for the Footman Pike.

Soldiers Pike
Head to War Barron Eator ask him "what Memory of Sebilis"
Head to Kurns Tower. Once inside of the Tower go downstairs Take a left once you go thru that door, a right and then a quick left. Follow that hall around to the right and go down the stairs on your left. Go thru that room and you will come to a fork left or right. Go right thru the stables to the next corner. Wait there the Thick Boned Skeleton spawns there. You need a Skull and Gem from him. While you are there kill the Undead Jester which spawns down either hall or in the first room on the left after you turn that corner for an iksar berserker club.

Now go back to the main floor. Go up to the top of the Tower. You will find a room that has a broken dining table as well as two archways that are blocked off with rubble. In the far right corner opposite where you entered room you will find either a Cook or a Thick Boned Skeleton. Kill it and loot the second gem.

Return to Town and place the skull into Forge with a Forging hammer to get a Ruby.

Return to War Barron Eator and give him the Sapphire, Emerald and Ruby as well as your Footmans Pike to recive plans for Soldiers Pike.

Troopers Pike

Go speak again with Drill Master Kyg ask her "who is Trooper Moglok"
She will give you a note to give to Trooper Moglok.

You can find him inside of the Swamp sitting in a corner one of the buildings on the left of the path. He will give you a Froglok Head and tell you that you need 2 more. Go to the far side of the zone. the cave going into Traks Teeth. Turn around and Face back into the Swamp at the top of that falls. Turn left and go down to the bottom of those rocks there. You will come across two mobs in a static spawn. Kill them both until two Froglok Outlanders Spawn. One will drop a head and mushroom (no known use) and one will drop a head and box ( no known use) it may take a while as something the second one takes a bit to spawn.

Return to Town and give all three heads and your Soldiers Pike to her to recieve Plans for Soldiers Pike.

Legionnaire Mancatcher

Go to War Barron Eater and tell him "I am a brave trooper of the Empire" he will send you to report for duty to the Warlords of the Swamp, Lake and Woods.

Report to the Warlord of the Swamp tell him " I am a Trooper Reporting for Duty" he will send you to get 4 Krup Guard Shields and give you a 4 slot pack. They can be found in Frogtown. Drink a Cloud Potion and go up the ramp once you get up walk right and go across to the next section of the Town. in the third building to the right you should find a Krup should be the only mob in the building. (it helps to bring a Tracker with you there) Kill and loot 4 shields combine and return it to the Warlord of the Swamp. to Recieve Note to War Barron Eator.

Report to the Warlord of the Lake tell him "I am a Trooper Reporting for Duty" He will send you to kill 4 Sarnak Legionnaire and bring back the heads as proof as well as give you a 4 slot bag. Run over to the Sarnak Fort, back side. Kill 4 Sarnak Legionnaires place their heads into the pack and combine it. Now head out towards the lake.

You need to Kill 4 Goblin Warlords and grab their beads.

Return to Warlord Geoz and give him the bag to recive a second note for War Barron Eator.

Report to the Warlord of the Woods tell him "I am a Trooper Reporting for Duty" He will give you a 4 slot bag place the Goblin Warlord Beads into it. Combine and give back to him to recive your 3rd Note.

Return to the Legion Hall and give War Barron Eator all three notes as well as your Troopers Pike to recive plans for the Legionnare Mancatcher.

Champions Mancatcher

Report to Drill Master Vygan tell him "I am a Legionnaire" He will send you to your doom. err on a quest to gather the notes from the 4 sarnak messengers.

Go to Lake near back door of Fort and Kill the Messenger to revive his message.

Go to Woods near the Southern trail leading to the east side of the Goblin Fort Kill the messenger and get his note.

Go to Overthere Kill the Messenger that runs between Woods and Frontier.

Go to Frontier and Kill the Messenger that is near the Nurga Mines Enterance.
(note - bring tracker with you)

Go to Field of Bones and Hail Tropper Drix, folow promts. Combine all 4 notes in bag Trooper Drix gives you. who can be found between the 4 Pillars just to the northwest of Kurns Tower. He needs three things to translate the messages.

Go to Lake and Kill Bloodgill Scavenger under water from the pillars first tower on left Loot Silver Oxide

Go to Overthere Kill a Berserker and Loot the War Braid.

Go to Dreadlands and Forage up a Frost Crystal.

Give the pack (with combined notes) and those three items back to him to Recive an Sealed Message.

Go back to the Legion Hall and give Drill Master Vygan the Sealed Message to recieve Plans for your Champions Mancatcher.


Warlords Mancatcher

Go to Nurga with a Tracker. Find and kill A Victorius, Fearless, Challenging and Rebellious Goblin. They will Drop 4 items Tattered Bracer, Symbol of the Legion, Cracked Shield and Broken Mancatcher Crown.

Go to Lake and talk to Warlord Geot ask him "who was Xyzith" He will tell you about how his friend dissipeared. Give him the Tattered Bracer to recive a 4 slot pack.

Combine those 4 items from Nurga and give the pack to Warlord Geot to revieve a note.

Go to the Legion Hall and give Geot'z note to Drill Master Kyg to recive her approvial.

Give Drill Master Kyg's Approvial to War Barron Eator.

Return to Lake ask Grot "where did Xyzith Search for the Legendary Metal"

Go to the Tower of Death ask Master Rixiz "can you speak to the dead?"

Go to Emerald Jungle and Track down Iksar Traitor. Kill and loot his head.
Return to Tower of Death and give Master Rixiz the head he will summon Warlord Xyzith

Attack the Ghost (not undead) and bring him to near the woods zoneline. you and cleric should be able to kill him. He is blue at 47. Drops Destroyed belongings

Give Belongings to Master Rixiz to get his seal for War Barron Eator

Return to Legion Hall and give the seal to the War Barron Eator to recive plans for your Warlords Mancatcher.


Pride of the Legion
Go to Droga Kill Stone Seer Kaigek (loc n884 n517) shes in that room gonna be the left most spawn point. Grab a Broken Medalian from her. (lv 52 dont recall con. was with xp guild group mostly 65s working on aa points.)

Go to Lake and give the broken medal to Geot. As hes looking at it he noticed a black stone inside of it that he will give to you and tell you to find someone that studies stones.

Go to Timerous Deep and give the Black Stone to Izatri who then tells you to bring it to her friend Herophant Lyix who can be found in Field of Bone. Izatri is located near the chessboard, in spitting distance from it. Once you swim down there turn around on the chessboard so your facin north Follow the mountain on your left on ground level you will come to her. Just watch out for that Roaming Gnome hes hostile and blue at 52.

Go to Emerald Jungle with a Tracking Friend and kill a Reflective Soul (blue at 50) and loot the Mirrored Stone.

Go up to Plane of Knowledge and make two Celestrial Essense's

Go to Field of Bone with a raid group (2-3 parties) give Herophant Lynx 2 Celestrial Essense's (unstacked), mirrored Stone, Black Stone To spawn Warlord Xyzith. Have your parties located at the Book so they do not argo Lynx (unless they are not kos to the Legion). Attack the Warlord (spawns at Lynx defult location) and drag it over to the book so that your raid force can kill him in 30 seconds.. hes blue at 52 so a good party could probally take him out in a few minutes or so, but overkill is better then a wipe. Loot Spirit of Xyzith.

Go to Town give Spirit to War Barron Eator to recive plans for Pride of the Legion

Congratulations!
