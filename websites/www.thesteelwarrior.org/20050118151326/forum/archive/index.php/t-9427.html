Aandien
10-26-2004, 03:29 PM
You must kill 4 rings (not necessarily 4 different rings) between patches. 24 hours, 48 hours, 72 hours, whatever, it doesn't matter.

Every time a ring is killed a counter is incremented. When that counter = 4, the counter resets and the arbitor (or his PH -- which also gives flags) spawns.

If the zone crashes, or the servers are brought down for a patch, the counter is reset.
