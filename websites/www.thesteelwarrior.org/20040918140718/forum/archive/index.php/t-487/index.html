You'll need some image editting software like Photo Shop. Alot of digital cameras come with editting software, so you may have something already in your house. My cheap Polaroid camera came with Photo Max Pro that does a pretty decent job of editting and touching up photos.

Open up the photo or screenshot you want within the software and play around with it. That's how I learned the little bit I know.

Also, you'll want to save your creation as a .jpg since its generally a smaller file size which is good for message boards. EQ screenshots default as .bmp so don't forget to convert them before uploading to your website for hosting.
