A glance at your magelo, Partha, and I'm thinking you should probably be okay through Barindu, but you'll definitely need /discs for the nameds. It'll be a definite challenge, though. I do reasonably well in Ferubi at 5.7/1500 ish unbuffed + Full Defensive AA, but it's rough going. Of the sewers, the Crematory is the 'medium' LDoN that everyone wishes existed, and the pools and lair are somewhere above BoT but beneath the Eles, while the Purifying Plant is just an asskicker. As a warrior, I'd want 6k/1400+ AC to work with even bare minimum comfort in the Plant. I can't comment on Vxed/Tipt, other than to say that the two times I went to Vxed, I hid behind a guild warrior, and she took a royal beating at a buffed 9k/1600ish AC.

Okay, just did a group in GoD yesterday. Group was WAR/SK/DRU/SHM/BRD/ENC, the other five being solid EPish geared toons. The only Magelo I can find of them is the Bard's (http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=893852) . The rest of the group is similarly geared/AA'd, I believe.

(Of course, they went to Purifying Plant.) :eek:

With this group, I was able to tank trash mobs just fine, which surprised me greatly, given what I've heard about GoD. Mobs tended to hit in the 700-950 range max, which compares with Hard LDoN to me. Sadly, we were not able to attempt the named, due to the Druid's untimely death in a bad spot, followed by time constraints with other folks in the group. All in all, though, it was highly educational. The SK was originally tanking, then they had me take over while he was AFK for a few mobs. I'm sure I would not have done nearly as well with a lesser geared group, however.
