Chavoor the Trickster - Always pops in that first southern culvert to the east.

Ikaav Firecaller(gater) - Seems to have two static spawnpoints. Either in the far north east room(labeled "Camps") or from the first split go left, left, then on the right side of the room with the north, west, and south(where you are coming from) exits.

Ukun Bloodbeast - I've seen this mob to the west of the western spawnpoint of the Ikaav, or in the first room on the middle(north) path.

Umbiki(rampages) - Spawns in several places through the zone including close to Chavoor, in the east/west path connecting the middle and right paths, many spots in the loop on the west side.

misguided bel? Creque(sp?) - Supposedly a named this mob only pops in the middle northern room labeled "Crypt"

Kyv Hunter - I believe this one tends to spawn on the western path, but not sure.
