Korak Sarathai
Korak strides into the tavern, obviously tired, but still firmly upright. He stretches his arms back in a mighty span, the pops and cracks of his weary skeleton clearly audible through his armor.

"Ahh, ye 'aven't forgotten ta leave me seat open."

Sitting down with a great thud, he speaks again, "Tonight, drinks are on me. I've been long gone, and have many tales to tell, and to hear. Gather round old friends, and share."
