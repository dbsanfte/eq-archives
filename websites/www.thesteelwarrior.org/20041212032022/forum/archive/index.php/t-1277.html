Not sure what to advise now that the level range for blue con's has been expanded and more PoP zones have been opened up, but here is what I would have said before the last patch:

When you get to level 60 stop and assess your postion.

If you have a regular grouping crowd, lots of friends levelling up with you or are in a guild who you group with regularly then level up to 65. If your gear is good and your HPs are hitting the 4k mark then sure, level up to 65.

However if you rely on pickup groups and are sitting in the 3k-3.5k hp range then stop and AA because in tier 2 PoP and above (cos back then you wouldnt want to XP anywhere else past 60) you'll have alot of trouble getting groups when you are competing against level 65 warriors and knights for the tank spot and get to be known as a lightweight tank who cant keep mobs off the casters and cant tank the mob unslowed long enough to build up agro for the slower and healer to do their thing without pulling the mob off them. So if you're in this boat get level 60 then work on getting ND3 to get your HPs up a little. Once you have your HPs up asses it again and maybe AET and some other defensive AAs or choose to level up to 65 before switching back to AA.

Ofcourse now XP in sub Tier 2 PoP prolly doesnt blow as much for the 60+ crowd so maybe it is worth levelling up before you get the AA's.
