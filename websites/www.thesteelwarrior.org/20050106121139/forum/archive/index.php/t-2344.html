Myself and 5 hardy adventurers decided we'd try to camp the king area in new Droga for the weapon drops, so armed with an Eqatlas map and the limited knowledge I'd gained about the revamped zone I found on the web, off we went.

Firstly we knew the mobs don't see invis, so we zoned in, invised up and made our way to the king camp, no problems so far.

Then we decided our stout paladin would aggro one of the mobs in the king room, drawing them all to the chanter who would AE stun them all and we'd then pick them off one by one at our leisure.

Mistake number 1.

These mobs cannot be AE stunned.

Ensuing mayhem and two dead adventurers later, our wizard managed to evac us back to zone in.

We then went back down to the small hidden room near the king room and pulled the two corpses back to there.

Mistake number 2.

Don't sit down to mez in that hidden room - the nearby gobs will aggro you.

Ensuing mayhem and one dead druid later, our wizard evaced us back to zone in.

Undeterred we invised back down to the hidden room, rezzed the druid and made sure we all stayed stood up.

We then thought we'd try to pacify the outer room and take it before venturing to the king room. But our intrepid paladin found out these mobs cannot be pacified.

Whilst deliberating what to do next, invis dropped on one of us, who happened to have his arm sticking out of the hidden wall.

Aggro.

Ensuing mayhem and no dead people later, our druid succored us back to zone in.

By which time I had to go do some unavoidable r/l stuff.

So all in all a most frustrating afternoon, and we still don't have a good strategy for breaking into the king camp.

Anyone help us out please?

Here is some info brother.

The agro range in the King room is beyond tiny. You can just set up in the far east side of the king room with the oven by the table and uninvis right there on that mob and no others will come.

You just work your way around the room counterclockwise and you will get singles every time.

I think you may have psyched yourself out on this one. It really is that easy. They dont look like they pull single, but they absolutely 100% do.

P.S. I am not sure what hidden room you are talking about. Just go straight into the king room, set up in the far east corner right by the table. Let invis drop on the warrior first, and fight the mob you are sitting on. If that crap is happening in whatever "hidden" room you are in, don't go there. There are at least 5 places you could wait with only one mob to fight.

If you cant take one mob attacking you from invis dropping, then I wonder if maybe the dungeon overall is too tough for you...
