Ok, I see that your guild is in Qvic. Getting in to PoTime does not seem very likely for you unless you can get lucky on the Rathe, but I wouldn't count on it at this point. You need to spend every waking moment grinding AA. Make sure you finish off any tanking and HP AAs as soon possible.

I would suggest grinding in LDoN hards if at all possible. You need to rack up LDoN points to get some augments I noticed you are missing. You need to at least get the 5 10ac augs from LDoN. They are cheap and you can't pass up 50ac. Work on getting A3 and the 40hp augs as well. Any dropped augs you can get your hands on should go in slots that you cant put 10ac or 40hp LDoN augs in.

When you are not grinding AA, you need to try and get in on as many Qvic mini kills as possible. All of the drops you don't have are pretty substancial upgrades to what you have now. You should also have access to some pretty sweet weapons as well, a Fleshbiter or Jagged Onyx Staff should be easy enough for you to get. You are going to get kind of hosed on non-visible armor slots as you probably won't be getting Time gear and the GoD stuff is likely to carry a pretty high price tag. If you have your PoEb key, then you need to find out who is killing the Rathe still. Do what ever you can to get on a raid for the flag. Getting in to Time before your guild stops farming there would be tremendously beneficial just by picking up the rot in the early tiers.

Also, I forgot to mention one other thing. I noticed that you mentioned that you are not likely to be MA any time in the near future. Forget that, that's rubbish.
