It's pretty simple really. Easiest way to set them up is by using your personalized hotkey menu in game. You can find the names of your discs by typing "/disc" that will bring up every disc you have. Then in your personalized hotkey area give it a name you'll remember (example) Name: Mighty (As in mightystrike) Then in the command line below type in /discipline mightystrike. Then whenever you hit that hotkey button your discipline will be activated. :)
