Maybe someone can explain to me why the discipline timers are so long. I can under stand the power built into furious, but over all most of the disicpline are not totally devastating. Basically I see them as having to ends to the spectrum of their use that SoE would be concerned about:

1) How many time could disc001 be used during a UberMob001 fight (you know the mob with HP just shy of a bizzilion)

2) How badly would they effect Minibossmob001 (the less than uber types.)

In the case of number 1, a warrior getting 30 seconds of crits is mouse nuts compared to the overall amount of damage needed to take these guys down. You could riposte Seru for a minute and and a half and not see his HP move much. So does it really break the game if disciplines are down to every few minutes with a cost to endurance? rather than these long ass delays?

IN the case of number 2, if you are talking a 32k hp mob, the content may be come a bit more trivial for the melee types if the disc refresh was lowered, but for a large portion of the population 32k hp encounters are pretty trivial at this point anyway. What difference does it make if my disc is ready again in a few minutes if the miniboss001 is dead? should I use my disc again on henchman001, who will die before the disc is even done?

It just seems to me that the disciplines are overly guarded due to a few of them having rather powerful implications, when in practice most of them are either useless due to long timers, or just flat out useless.
