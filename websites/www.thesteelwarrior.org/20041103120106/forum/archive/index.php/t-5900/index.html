Eoden
1st, Replace the snow chipper with a weapon that will give you a bit more agro, either sword of the bloodsworn or copperhammer of striking would be the best way to go. If your budget stretchs far enough you can replace the frost bringer too but you shouldnt need to. An alternative would be to get a Great Staff of Thunder.

2nd, Haste - I'd get a soulscream belt, or try to camp one if you can in Veksar. Spiked Seahorse belt is a decent alternative to this, or maybe runed bolster belt.

The rest of your gear isnt in too bad shape. The only slots that need attention really are ears, legs, bp and 1 wrist.

- replace the bracer of defense and legs with Full Mithrill (bracer 25ac 30hps, legs 40ac 40hps)

- look for any 2 earrings with more than 50hps each, theres plenty to choose from these days, Mozzie earrings and Bloodmetal earrings of engagement are getting cheaper.

- Barbed ringmail isnt great, but it isnt too bad either and I think you'd get more bang for buck upgrading other slots than buying an expensive BP upgrade. With whatever pennies you have left have a look for something like an architech's bejeweled chest plate.
Lidan
Weapons: CHoS, Sotbs, greatstaff of thunder
Bp: anything with over 30 ac and 50hp in sta or hp, thurg bp MQ, myrmidon MQ
Earrings: bloodmetal would be nice for you, if you cant get get somethings with ac and try for 40hp+
Legs: Cobalt, Kylong shinguards
Waist: Soulscream belt, enchanter sash of wonder, anything with 31% haste or more
Back: Cloak of greater pernicity would be a good upgrade or a crinose cape of the werewolf
Shoulders: Mantle of distraction, bonespur mantle, some other ones with good sta and hp together
Wrist: bracelet of fortitude or quickness
Neck: amulet of mummy wrapping is a good one

The myrmidon gear will last you for awhile.
