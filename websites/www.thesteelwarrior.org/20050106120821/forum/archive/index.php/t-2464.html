Gimmel
08-13-2003, 07:00 PM
With all "slow" type spells its cure disease not cure curse.

Not the case with Gore's slow. Cure disease will cure all standard shaman/ench slows, because they all add disease counters. Gore's AE is Freezing Breath: http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=845&source=Live which does not add any disease counters. The only way to cure it is to dispell it, or to use Radiant Cure, which has a chance to remeove detrimental effects as well as cure poison/disease/curse counters.
