CCoM is actually a pretty good suggestion for haste, you want to get rid of the Helmet of Raldukan anyway and your back and belt slows are decent. Besides that, your rings look like a place to get some healthy upgrades in my opinion, Steel Ring of Earthen Resistance x2 would be a good AC/hp boost. Your weapons are pretty good, Sword of the bloodworn would be a good offhand upgrade but there's no reason to get a wind-worn handblade, not much agro and only a slight bit more dps than sotbs. Unless you're going to have tons of money flowing in regularly, or ornate/magnetic prices are different on your server than mine, magnetic gives you a lot more bang for the buck, only 5 less AC and 15 hp less than ornate, so all you really lose is the clicky (which isnt a big deal if the bst you farm with is a 2box.) Other than that it looks like you're looking at fairly expensive upgrades for less return in the other slots, head and rings seem the worst off, although you may want to consider an elemental bow too depending on price, and if you need the see invis from Blindeye.
