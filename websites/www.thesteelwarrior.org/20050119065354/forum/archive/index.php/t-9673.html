Actually, there are a few opints in favor of a raid force regarding aggro :

1. More than one debuffer, thus they can spread the hate so it's not all on the same person.

2. In a raid, people are more patient and don't mind waiting for 10 seconds before starting nuking like mad. Which means you have longer to build up aggro.

3. People are often more "awake" during a big fight, and thus makes less mistakes.

4. Squishies are more alert to the fact that they are just that. Especially those who can reduce their own hate (Evade, Feign Death, Concussion, Jolt .. etc.) and do so more than in an exp setting.
