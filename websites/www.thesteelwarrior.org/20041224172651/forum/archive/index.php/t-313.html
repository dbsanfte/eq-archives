You really don't need any new equipment, but I would start clearing out the bank.

I would keep the idol of the thorned, seahorse cloak, cc gaunts, froglok crown, and the resist jewelry, then try to sell off everything else. You could wear the idol and cloak, but keep what you have on now in the bank.
