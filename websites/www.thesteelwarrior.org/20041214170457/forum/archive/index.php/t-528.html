Actually, agility (http://www.users.qwest.net/~taleisin/pages/AC%20from%20AGI.htm) is a fairly smooth progression past 75.

Dex > 75 and > 100 seem to be the 2 main levels where things just work a bit better. There's a clear bonus to learning offensive skills when Dex > 150 and Agi > 150.

Unda was PL'd by a shaman through levels 32, 33, 34, and 35 at the Kodiak caves in Great Divide.

For levels 32 and 33 (most bears yellow and red) I was unbuffed Dex/Agi because I wanted to see if skills would lag -- with dex/agi a hair over 100, skills were not maxed before the next level.

At 34 and 35 (with most bears turning even and blue) with Dex/Agi over 150, skills were maxed before leveling.
