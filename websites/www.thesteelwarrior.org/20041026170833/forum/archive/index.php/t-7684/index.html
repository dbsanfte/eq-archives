Sassinak
Sol A pulling to rug room will get you to 28 or so fairly easily. Upper Guk shin lord area is even better/easier for the same levels, but only if you have a snarer.

Kaesora library level is outstanding for groups of 3+ starting around level 28. It will slow down around level 36.

Gunthak beach is an excellent spot for 2+ people starting around level 34. Even better if you have harmony/pacify available to split up mobs. This spot is good up to level 44, at which point you are ready for Dulak.
Xebokan
Solo:

Warslike woods, forest giant fort
Paineel, skeleton guards (need to build faction first)

Grouped:

High Hold Keep basement, goblins
Kailyn
LDoN if you can find a group. Lots of fun and good XP at low levels.
