I'm looking for information on the point cost progression and effect progression of the group leadership abilities.

I'd like any info anyone has available on the diff abilities and what they do/cost. Heres a breakdown of what I know right now.


Title - Cost - Effect

Mark NPC - 3/4/? - Using a hotkey accesable only by the person with the skill, it places a "1" at the beginning of the targets name and also places a target ring around the mob that is seperate from the normal target ring(note: currently this extra targeting ring may be adversly affected by custom UI's and my not display properly). The second and third levels allow you to mark one additional mob each with a "2" and a "3".

NPC Health - 2 - Highlights mob's name and adds its health at the end in the format "NPC Name - %"

Delegate Main Assist - 4 - Adds a White and HIGHLY noticable second target ring around MA's target

Delegate Mark NPC - 4 - See "Mark NPC" above. The only difference is the group leader with this skill can chose to give anyone in the group the marking ability. Limited to a single marked NPC.

Inspect Buffs - 4/6 - Allows group members to get a list of a targeted group member's buffs via a hotkey made from the leadership window. Second level shows the time remaining before buffs fade.

Spell Awareness - 6 - Allows you to know all spells being cast in your vicinity. Format - "NPC/PC begins to cast a spell. <Spellname>"

Offense Enhancement - 4/5/6/7/8 - 10atk/20atk/30atk/40atk/50atk

Mana Enhancement - 4/6/8 - 30mana/60mana/100mana

Health Enhancement - 4/6/8 - 30hp/60hp/100hp

Health Regeneration - 4/6/? - 1hp pertick/?/?

Find Path to PC - 4 - Creates a Find style path to targeted group member

Health of Target's Target - 7 - Adds an extra target window showing the name and health of your target's target. Clicking this allows you to target your target's target.


Unless noted otherwise assume all abilities are available to the full group and all effects effect the whole group. Leadership abilities are only active when the person that has them is the leader of the group. Also it is assumed that all effects hp, mana, atk, regen stack with current worn caps.

UPDATE: According to a post on the cleric boards the hp enhancement GROUP ability stacks with the RAID ability, I'm assuming this applies to mana, atk, and regen as well.
