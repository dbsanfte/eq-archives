The toughest part of getting the haste aug is clearing down to the room it drops in. The placeholder of Hexxt Pzik Shziaak spawns on the north wall of the center room of the zone alone. Once your group is down there he is a solo pull and hits no harder than the regular mobs in the zone. I would guess that you could park your druid and warrior in the north-west corner of the room once there and farm him until he drops your aug. We used to park a spare cleric in that corner so I know it is safe.

The worst part of Ferubi is getting around in it. Every time you want to farm bits for Smith Rondo you have to kill every damned mob along the way. If you wipe you need a SoS rogue or a corpse summon to CR. The mobs get tougher past the haste aug room but the experience gets better. You can pull Hexxt N`Vak Tkan to the haste aug room if you pull out the south door and grab the mobs along the south-east branch. He drops the melle ring with 11ac and 110hps. The 15ac augs drop in the deepest parts of Ferubi and can be challenging to get the Hammermaster and the Brawler to spawn. Unless you are lucky and trigger them clearing down you need to pull mobs all around them until they spawn. Can take awhile and often is frustrating.

At level 65 until we learned the tricks we found Ferubi to be a bit of a challenge, especially the southern parts of the zone. Was there yesterday farming Rondo bits and the mobs were an absolute joke. The cleric, enchanter, ranger and monk in my group were being 4 boxed by one guy, we had a 62 wizard twink along for augs and I was pulling and bringing whatever I aggroed with my arrows. OoW has trivialized this zone alot. So bring 5 friends and I'm sure you can mine what you want out of Ferubi with little trouble.
