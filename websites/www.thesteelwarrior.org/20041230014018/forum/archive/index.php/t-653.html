The price is directly related to the cost of making the item. A full suit of fine plate will yeild quite a bit more AC than a suit of banded, and it will also cost quite a bit more in materials to make. Not only that, you are paying for the skill level of a smith capable of making fine plate.

As for AC, 1AC = 1AC regardless of the material granting the AC.
