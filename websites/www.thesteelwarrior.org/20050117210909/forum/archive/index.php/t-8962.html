I'd suggest PoP for faster exp than the LDoN route. Since you dont need points (or loot at all for that matter, looking at your magelo), I'd suggest tier 1/2 zones.

In PoD near the castle there are these beetle lookin things. The group is called LC or RC on Tunare, but its in the corners beyond the bone wall on the sides of the castle. Great experience for a solid group, you can tank them no problem with your gear. I went from 53 to 60 there in maybe 2 weeks with guild groups. (pre-LDON of course)

Also CoD is good if you can convince a solid group to head there, and PoV inner/outer (cave groups) are incredible experience camps up to 62 or so. Usually full groups evenings and weekends. I think PoTorment is a hot spot right now too, but its always empty on my server.

Guys, LDoN's are really bad experience at level 58 (MAYBE 8-10% for a collect 25 which will take 50 minutes plus travel and get adventure/buff time). Unless you can get a reliable group to do hards, it's just not a very efficient way to go. I say reliable group because while they are not THAT hard, one dumbass can ruin a whole lot in a hard LDoN at any level.

Definately hit PoP, PoI factory if you can get there is $$. Mobs aren't that much more difficult than regular trash and XP is almost as good as PoV Inner Caves or even BoT.

However, this all said, a month to gain a level no matter where you are is pretty bad. Are you soloing? Where and what are you fighting?
