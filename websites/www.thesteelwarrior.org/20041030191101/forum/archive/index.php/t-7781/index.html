Fitemore
Don't even question it. In the EPs, 2k+ ac is a good thing. Time, it's even better. Resists are a bard's job, and yours are plenty high enough already. Let's apply my formula: 1ac = 6hp! BTW, not counting augments because they're universal.

Loyalist Shield of Honor: 62 points.
Cloak of Combusion - 46 points + 5% dodge
Ensorcelled Metalcloth Cape - 43 points
Leaf-Jointed Cloak - 48 points
Exquisite Phoenix Feather Mantle - 46 + 8% dodge
Cloak of Icy Shadows - 51 points + clicky

Let's see... According to that formula, Loyalist shield wins unless you consider 8% dodge to be worth 16 raw ac. I, personally, don't. Nor do I consider the DS clicky on the Cloak of Icy Shadows to be worth 11 points. Your resists are at around 200+ unbuffed... with a bard and resist buffs, you should be easily maxed for every resist, which is why I didn't even care about the resists.
