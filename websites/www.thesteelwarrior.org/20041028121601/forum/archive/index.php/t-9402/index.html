Lathen
I was going to say that you should be able to tank GoD sewer 1 with no problems based on the fact that your AC and hps are similar to or better than mine (I've successfully tanked that trial). But then I looked at your AA's, and now I'm not so sure.

I think that if I were you, I'd turn off the level exp for bit and finish up the archetype defensive AA's (CA, CS, ND) and physical enhancement from our class AA's. Your healer will gush with joy over your ability to mitigate and avoid damage, and all the raw hit points you've already got built up will get a really nice boost from ND3.

That's my 2 cp. :-)
bardeil
/agree Lathen. You're not going to see many solid upgrades from GoD pre vxed/tipt, and I wager you'll get beat up pretty badly in there. You're going to find it harder to make AA at level 70 too, as a lot of the popular hunting places for people around your aa/gear level will be outside the 5 level "bonus range". finish defensive aa's, and focus on getting gear upgrades from your vt raids.

I think you could get through sewers if you wanted to and had a good group, but I don't know what upgrades you're expecting to see from it. They'll be minor at best, and you'll probably take an AC hit. You could try for the trash rares in barindu and ferubi, but you're likely to be very disappointed.

If you're guild (or open raids on your server) kills the skyshrine dragon you might look into those quest items though, they're one groupable and very nice. You might check my magelo and "1 group" alt profile for some interesting 1 group upgrades, although most of them are gonna be tough for you to swing, at least while tanking.
