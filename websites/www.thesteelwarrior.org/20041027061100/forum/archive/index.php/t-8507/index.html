Arzul
Get the BL to 62 to get a good pet, then hit up Maidens Eye, Griegs End, PoNightmare, PoInnovation, PoJustice. My wife is a BL and Im a warrior we can duo in PoStorms/Valor easy. Just remember to be hasted and regen and Spiritual Dominion. Have Infusion of Spirit, dex, str and Talisman and your good to go. Pull with slow and taunt the mob off the BL. Its very easy really. And use healing will at the end of each fight while BL meds. Use Stonewall if your getting hit on too hard for BL to be able to keep up with heals. In the Planes, I switch to 2HS because riposte from the <20 delay weapons makes me take a lot more damage.

One other thing, cast the pet proc that doesnt cause a knockback. it helps when 2 boxing.
