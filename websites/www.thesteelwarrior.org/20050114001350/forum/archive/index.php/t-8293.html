I estimate JBoW proc aggro at 616 (500+175*2/3) and GSoT proc aggro at 350 (simple stun~350 because droga combo is around 400 and 450 each).

with WA5 CE0 and 305DEX, and no augment, we end up with around 1900 hate for the JBoW and 1100 for the GSoT.

and with 36% we may expect at least 3000 Hate from dps for the JBoW and 3700 for the GSoT.
at 100% haste we end up with at least 4600 for the JBoW and 5500 for the GSoT.
at 130% haste we achieve at least 5200 hate from dps for the JBoW and 6300 for the GSoT.



so they are globally the same, it depend on your toon to prefer one or the other. But remember that Hate from dps is best for short fight (trash, LDoN easy, ...) where we don't have many minutes to let the weapon procing alot.
Moreover as Wandor said, any procing weapon become a strong aggro generator as soon as you put A3 on it.
so if you are afraid that JBoW proc will stop generating aggro when resisted (even if I don't think it's possible), or if you always fight at max overhaste, or if you prefer the sure basis of dps' haste then go for the GSoT.
on the other hand if you think the a resisted proc draw as much aggro than a normal one (no metter which spell is it), and that you don't have confidence on dps aggro because it has been underparsed, and that you want to wield a weapon that you can be proud of, and that you don't have much haste most of the time then go for the JBoW.
