Shields aren't worthless.

For those classes that aren't expected to do significant melee damage, shields often have big time AC, stats, and even effects. It gives them the option of putting something useful in their offhand should they choose to use a one hander in their primary slot.

Like any piece of armor a shield's AC protects the user. As a bonus, melees (Why just melees? /em shrug) get modest damage by bashing with it.

Shields are perfect right now for those that choose to hide behind a "portable wall". Boost the value of shields and to be fair ya must find a boost for those that fight with weapon/s in their hands. Block reposts? Then add an offhand parry reposte. Additional mitigation - for a piece of armor? What's next? Dual Weilded Shields with double-bash?

What would it take for Warriors to use shields? Lessee:

1. Near double damage from the primary hand - a lot of us have Ambidexterous and do significant damage with our off hand.

2. Near double proc frequency from the primary hand.

3. High quality Warrior only shields to drop like snow flakes in a blizzard to make up for the fact most warriors have spent 4 years not even caring if they ever looted one.

4. Warrior shield effects on par with the best melee and caster effects on Paladin and SK shields.

5. Warriors fight and not hide. We should have a higher bash rate and stun frequency - a Warrior would only use a shield if it were an offensive tool. We don't carry them to protect ourselves from enemy lances when jousting.

6. Unique graphics - no Warrior would want to be asked for a Res or Brells. I'm thinking maybe a slogan like that guy's wallet in Pulp Fiction.
