The high level bonus is there is some bonus for killing stuff that is close to your level. It used to be that due to mob power increasing faster than your power per level, the best way to level was to kill something as low level as possible, but still blue, and kill faster (hellooooo Velks). The bonus for closer cons was added to offset this.

ZEM is the zone experience modifier. There is a bonus for each kill in a zone for certain zones. These zones tend to be indoors. PoP used to have out of this world ZEMs, now they're just insane.

Like someone else said, be happy that they fixed hell levels and PoP has such great exp. About a year ago, there was a thread on the wizard board a guy started when he decided to do all of level 59 in 1 sitting. People said he was crazy and it couldn't be done. A lot of people on his server showed up to help him out and he did most of 59 in AE groups or quad kiting (at the time the best exp you could get) and it took him 39 hours to complete level 59. Be glad it's not like this any more.

The 50s are considerably slower than anything pre-50. Levels do take 3-4x as much to get through. Get used to that. The trick is to find the place where you can cash in on all of the various experience bonuses. Since you are a warrior, you need to make sure that your groups are always full. The more people in your group, the higher the bonus is and it scales up relative to the amount of people. Next you need to get in a zone with the highest ZEM (here is a list of most zones - http://www.graffe.com/library.php?id=20). Also, you should try to hunt mobs that are as close to your level as possible. There is a bonus you get for fighting mobs that are within 5 levels of you or higher. Finally, don't try to group with people who are much higher level than yourself. The amount of exp per mob is divided among the people in the group with the highest level people getting the largest share. You might be fighting reds in a high ZEM zone, but if you are 53 in a group of 65s, your portion of the experience is going to be a lot smaller that theirs.

Your best bets are to exp in PoP. Dobbo pretty much nailed it, those zones should get you be best exp/hour if you can get a decent group.
