Ever thought of the Loyalist Shield of Honor?

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/equipment/graphics19/Loyalist_Shield.jpeg

It's a rather simple quest out of Katta Kastellum that's called Vampyre Troubles if you search it under Allakhazam. I've managed to easily trio everything up until the last spawn (60 warrior, 61 rogue, 65 shaman), so it's by no means difficult at all. Finishing up everything but the last kill will take you anywhere from 6 to 8 hours depending on if you have port ability between Katta Kastellum and Shadowhaven. However, the last kill will probably require a solid group since Valdanov Zefveer is not only a rare spawn, but has lots of hp and a 1k lifetap proc.

Very worthwhile investment. Too bad it doesn't have resists on it though.

***And yes, I know this isn't droppable, but the quest is easy and the reward is, in my opinion, better than anything you can buy.
