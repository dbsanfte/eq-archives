Jakle Attakle
playing the shaman, you should just keep going at least to level 60. The level difference there makes a huge difference in ability to Slow plus the buffs you can provide.

Then, if you are serious about playing the warrior, why not get the warrior into a group with "perks"

A level 60 Warrior with an out of group slower, buffer and dot'r is better than a level 61-62ish warrior without it :) Especially if the Shaman can land the slows.

Head to PoV and try that out for a little while if you want to get the Warrior to level 65 or grind out some mega AA's.

I always seemed to get a group with that setup (I had an ench or Druid for more DPS) Over time, if you are doing a great job, they may just let you bring the bot into the group. (remember, as the 6th person, the bot takes no exp away from the others - you just have to play them as well as a 6th person)

Either way, you are now killing in PoV with the DPS of 7 people instead of 6. You will be blown away by the amount of exp you receive here (Trust me on this one :) )

That's about it. That would be my suggestion. You will have some tough times LFG'ing at first, but I would love to have a Tanker and a Free shaman in my group in PoV any time :)

Good luck,
