Getting a BoB or Drac pauldruns is child's play compared to making all the pieces for the Earring of the Solstice solo. You can do the Velious quests you mentioned in mere hours if the NPC's/mobs are not up, minutes if they are.

On the other hand, you'd have to spend hundreds of thousands of plat (and a bunch of AA's) to grandmaster in the various trades (and let's face it, learning trades is all about removing plat from the economy, so they cost...a lot) and spend countless hours farming and foraging for items in order to make the required ingredients for the quest.
