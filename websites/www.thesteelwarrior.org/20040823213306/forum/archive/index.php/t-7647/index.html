Baadan
Copy this into your Charname_servername.ini file

Page2Button7Name=INC
Page2Button7Color=18
Page2Button7Line1=/g INCOMING [ 0028071-00001-00001-00001-00001-000015ED87FFC %t ]

Your groupmates will see:

Soandso tells the group, 'INCOMING [ Target ]

The color=18 line means that the color of the hotkey title is light blue (my code for knowing it is a /gsay hotkey) and the Page2Button7 thing means it is on the second page, 7th button of the socials bank (the window that has the Main, Combat, Ability, and Socials tabs, it used to only have the 10 slots that came preprogrammed with stuff like /who all Friend and /who all GM but now has 10 pages with 10 slots) The Page#Button# has no bearing on the hotkey's location in your hotbox (the hotbox is the window with 10 slots where you drag the hotkeys so pressing '1' will make you Kick or Taunt or whatever.
