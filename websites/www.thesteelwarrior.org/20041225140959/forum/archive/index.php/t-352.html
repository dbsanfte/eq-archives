"You guys think a warrior with Frostbringer + CoIO would be able to deal enough damage without draining the cleric near death?"

This depends on what/where you are hunting. I would say in nonPOP zones, SEB for example, you would be fine. For POP, I am guessing you would soak up a lot of healing before anything ever died. If you want to test this, go to POD and kill the Tsetsi flies straight out from the zone. These are the weakest mobs I have encountered in PoP. Velks would be another place I would think that you could duo without a large drain on the cleric.

IMO the Wavecrasher is an aggro weapon purely from the proc. A 9/19 weapon doesn't deal very good damage and that is what you need when duoing. I personally would grab the best 2 hander I could get along with a Truncheon of Doom (50% slow and 33/44 2hb) if you need to slow mobs. I use a ToD a lot swapping it with Prismatic 2 hander (45/44 2hs). Sometimes I slow the mob almost immediately, other times I go the whole fight (doing subpar damage) without getting a proc. Proc's are unrealiable at best and keep in mind I usually have a 200+ dex with Avatar on. My point is that sometimes I would be better off trying to damage the mob as fast as possible instead of relying on a proc.
