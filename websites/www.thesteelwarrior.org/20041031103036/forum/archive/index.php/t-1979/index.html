the main factor is coin. those 2 bloodmetal earring you'll basically retire with but unless you got 70K to blow on them your tempest ears are fine ( bloodmetal ears sell for 30-35K on terris..'skeeter leg ears are cheaper )

but overall the only 2 gaping holes in your gear are the gloves and waist. definately get them thurg/ss gloves..or hell even looting a pair of silversilk gloves from kael will net a pile of hit points.
for haste you can't really argue with the soulscream belt. same haste as the RBB, plus a bit better rounding of stats. nearly the same price if not cheaper since they seem to be dropping like rain.

weapons...the reliable forstbringer will do nicely. move the Tae Ew sword to offhand, and sell the lightblade...

from there you should be set.
