Arathena
Spell awareness is a lot more useful for other members of the party than it is for the tank. It's fairly valuable for a puller - It establishes quick identification of new mob types, and allows more effective decision making on when an FD puller should be FDing - I react to condemnation in a different manner than lure of frost, for example. More, it allows for more enlightened decisions for whomever is responsible for stopping gate/cheal, etc: The added mana effiency for say, an enchanter, to just let condemnation and greater healing and the like go through and concentrate on watching for cheal, gate, slow, AE spells, etc can be enormous.

Edit to add: Mark NPC and Delegate Mark are excellent ways to give a character the ability to communicate the way the battle should flow. While a 'kill %t' next hot key functions, the delegate mark gives a way to sort out a number of identically named mobes from a pile. Again, this is not useful so much for the MA as it is for the group's CC options.
