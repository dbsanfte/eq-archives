Go find the mines in GD. Kill dorfs until your eyes bleed. Collect the heads they drop and turn them in to the Kromrif recruiter at the EW zone line. He will not agro even when KOS so you can have someone invis you after you open a trade window to do the turn in until you are not KOS. This gets you all 3 giant factions. Once you are not KOS to Kromzek, turn the heads in to Capt. Bvellos in the bank. He gives better Zek hits and Zek is the faction you need for armor turn ins. It will probably take anywhere from 100-150 heads to get ally depending on how good/bad your faction is now.
