BoC + anger3 and shield is much less aggro than a SEWS + A3 imo.

It really depends on your offensive aa's though. If you have max pre ooW flurry and max triple attack, they say SEWS is very close to a BoW for raw aggro, and a BoW is much nicer than a BoC + anything other than a BF.

Opting for a SEWS means trading off some proc aggro for superior dps aggro. DPS aggro is with you all the time .. rain or shine ... luck or not. I'd honestly go with the SEWS, but a BoC + any weapon offhand with an aggro proc + another anger3 will more than suffice for aggro.

In my opinion BoC is a far lesser weapon, so reverting back to it unless you have something on par with the epic 1.5 to go with it is just plain silly.
