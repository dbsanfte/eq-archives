You can tank PoI entrance np. I started doing it at 54 with similar gear and was totally fine. The best XP I was seeing at 53-54 was PoI entrance. PoJ caves were also good. PoD flies are also good, but I think PoI is better. Both of those spots will be slightly faster than LDoN or PoJ exit types mobs. It just seems to me like those levels are slow, although they are cruising compared to my first char I went through them with through Kunark/Velious era. I find PoI and PoD pretty boring though, the PoJ caves are a bit more interesting to me, and LDoN is even better. Yes, LDoN is a bit slower, but not significantly so, and the chance for augs and points is good. It just depends on what you like... if you want your xp bar to go fast then try PoI, especially if you've got your own group. If you like dungeon crawling do LDoN. If you want to try some different areas, hit Veksar, xp there is comparable to the other spots and there are some nice gear drops although most won't be upgrades for you, you'll make some nice pp. You can also hit Nurga which has some nice drops as well with decent xp also. Veksar and Nurga won't compare to PoP, but in my opinion they are much more enjoyable zones.
