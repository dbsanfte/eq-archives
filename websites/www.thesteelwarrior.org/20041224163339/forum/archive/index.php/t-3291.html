1a) Using stuns or darkness/terror gives a smart knight the ability to lock aggro from long range. All that a warrior has for long range aggro is a bow, which can be extremely useful but has far more limitations and effect than a spell. Give it a 200 max range, 50 min range, 30 second refresh, adds +hate equal to your skill. The +hate hit is small enough to not be unbalancing and the 50 minimum range keeps it from screwing up raid tanking.

1b) Aggro lock. This may not have been originally intended but right now it has become the signature feature of the knights. Considering that any nerf to this would also devastate the pre-BoC warriors, while only having a minor effect on high level knights, I don't see this changing.

2) Itemization. This has been an issue since Velious and only gets worse. Solution #1 - more pure melee only accessories (earrings and stuff) and more class specific pieces. Solution #2 - let pure melees get some use out of brain stats and mana. If there was some kind of conversion of mana into ac and/or hp that would at least mean that hybrids did not gain substantially more benefit from the same gear.

3) Utility. Right now, our utility consists of /shield. The range on that needs to be increased. Also, if we don't get some kind of mana converter, allowing pure casters to siphon our currently useless mana pool might be an idea. Actually, making shields into weapons might not be a bad idea. Anyway, just some ideas and yeah, it should probably have been in the War Room.
