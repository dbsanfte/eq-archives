The problem with POI is that any spawn loc can spawn anything, and some of the mobs are too high to harmony, unless you have one of the 61+ spells. Once you get a pair spawned where you can't harmony either one, you can't get singles any more. Also, these higher level mobs are immune to run speed changes, so you can't root park them either. Without a slower and an off tank, you're done dancing when this happens.

I haven't run into this problem killing goos in pod yet. Just head left along the zone wall from zone in.

Personally, I like to xp in velks. Sure, single mobs are worth a lot less xp, but they are far easier to kill, I kill piles of spiders every hour. If I solo with a slave cleric to heal I can do an AA in a bit over 2 hours. The large blocks ov velium sell well in bazaar at 75p each, the silks for 5p each, gems for 100-300 depending on demand, and stuff from the named in the ledge camp make for a nice payday too. It's not unusual for me to clear 1k per hour of camping.
