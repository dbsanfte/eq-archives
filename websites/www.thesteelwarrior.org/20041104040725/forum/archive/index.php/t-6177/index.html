Ah, yes. The sarnak ceremonial sword. You'll notice that it's magic. In a couple of levels, use it to kill wisps. Save all the greater lightstones you get and take them to the gypsy camp in North K. Give them to a female gypsy there and she'll give you a book that requires an open inventory slot. turn around and sell the book to one of the other gypsy merchants for about 10 pp.
If you can spend time in the bazaar, then kill spiderlings and spiders. Spiderlings drop spiderling silk, which can be used by smiths; tailors use the spider silk, which drops off larger spiders.
Save all Low Quality, Medium Quality and High Quality bear & wolf hides you get, sell them to players.
If you can do the bazaar, the silks and hides are your fastest and easiest ways to get pp.
If you go to the tundra area of Everfrost and can handle the mammoth calves, vendor the small tusks and save up the hides until you get 4 of them. Give them to the male rogue upstairs in the bank in Halas (four at a time). He'll give you some rogue-only armor, which you can sell to any vendor in halas for between 3 and 10 pp.
Also, save up every piece of mammoth meat you get. Some players who bake still make Halas 10-pound Meat Pies, and mammoth meat is a component in it that is not regularly stocked by any merchant. Whenever you are in Everfrost, watch for someone shouting for mammoth meat (which can drop off calves). Give them what you have, but refuse plat for payment. About half of these bakers will say thank you and leave. The other half will give you nice things. You'll be ahead on the meat if you don't sell it.
Even if you can't run a bazaar trader, spend some time in the bazaar searching for weapon and armor slots you want to upgrade. Get a feel for the regular price of things on your server so you'll know a good deal when you see it.
