A Knight Defensive-like mitigation capability would defeat the purpose of balance changes introduced by SoE because other classes tanked too much like Warriors.

"....warriors are unmatched in their ability to survive the most brutal battles." "Warriors also learn to taunt their opponents, making themselves the focus of all their enemies' attacks, allowing the less resilient members of their group to remain safe from harm."
The Arch Overseers proved that with a party of 72, all PoTime bosses could be killed without using a Warrior to MT.

The Warrior survivability advantage had become narrow, and with 72 members in a party was not required even against (then) current content most difficult mobs.

SoE made needed balance adjustments to Warrior including the "-1 DI" mitigation change. Later they added Sturdyness - 500HP's for 60AA.

Obviously, these changes would not have much effect where a large party could make adjustments which allowed other classes to MT hard hitting named mobs.

However, in a smaller party, the flexability to compensate would not necessarily be present.

In some of the GoD trials, the designers created named mobs with (approximately):
DB=400
DI=80
Stun Immune
No AE
No Proc Effects
Slowable
With few enough HP's that they can be killed in under 3 minutes.

No one should be shocked that Warriors have an easier time MTing it in a 6 person group.

If such a mob had been created say for the Beserker Epic and plunked down in EJ, then players may well have concluded after awhile that is was one group doable with a Warrior MTing. If a Paladin was the MT they better get a few more people.

This is the way mobs have always been in EQ.

Not all mobs are equally tankable by the three Plate tanks with the same number of people.

Warriors have a mitigation advantage including Defensive that make them the prefered MT vs. hard hitting (high DI) named mobs.

This is the way EQ has been for quite awhile.

Paladin calls for a Defensive-like mitigation change so they can successfully MT hard-hitting named mobs in the same-sized group as a Warrior is an attempt to co-opt the reason for Warriors to exist.
