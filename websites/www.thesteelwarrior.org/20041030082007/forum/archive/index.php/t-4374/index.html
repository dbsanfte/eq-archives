Keep in mind Nimbex, You only HAVE to get points in a certain camp to unlock the vendor items. You can spend points from any camp at any merchant after that.

For example:

Earn 510 Adventure points in Butcherblock, unlock 10AC Aug.

You can then buy the AC aug AND use the 1100 Everfrost points you have to buy 2 more if you want.

So in the long run with the camps its really more a matter of which vendor do you want to open up first than where to aquire points.

Keane Abrams
No, that is incorrect Jak,

You need to have the correct amount of points in each specific camp to be able to buy an item. But after the item is "unlocked" you can get points from anywhere and come back and buy any item you unlocked.

For example, the Anger 3 augment in Miraguls is 1150 points. That is 23 wins at level 65. You need to get 1150 points ALL from Miraguls. But once you get 1150 points there you never need to camp in Miraguls again. You can get another 1150 points from any camp and come back and buy a second Anger 3.
