If you can't find one, grab a shaman, works just as well. Regular advs don't require much healing, unless you are pulling 5 at a time. Things die way too quickly to do much dmg.
It goes like this. Knock off an adv in 40 mins or so. Usually when you go through one that fast, people don't mind staying for another real quick one. Have 1 person gate back to camp, make em leader, get another adv, run back to camp. Another adv started in 5-10 mins from end of last one. Another 40 mins, another adventure done.
Now I'm not saying this is the norm. But on an average, it doesn't take 4 hours to do 2 adventures. If every single day you log in it takes you 2 hours to do an adv, something is wrong. 2 1/2-3 hours on average seems about right if you're taking it easy. I consider 3 hours of play a day casual. Probably alot less time than most spend watching TV.
