Basically, it's like this: mobs who have crowd control spells will cast them on people who are further down on the hate list. This makes sense if you think about it, because the purpose of those spells is to handle multiple enemies. :)

Lots of spells fall in this category: malo, tash, root, snare, charm, slow, mez, fear, feeblemind, dispel, dots - just about any crowd control spell you can think of.

You'll also see mobs turn away in order to cast beneficial spells upon their friends (heals, dispels, and occasionally buffs). Again, the HoTT window will still show you as being targeted.

HoTT is a window into the mob's hate list. All of the mob's direct damage spells and melee will fall upon the person targeted. The other people on its hate list receive crowd control spells.
