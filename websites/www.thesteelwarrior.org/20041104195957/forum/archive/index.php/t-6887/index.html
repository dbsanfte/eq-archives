Neksnappa
I've had my OSoW for a while now, and would say it is comparable to a GBoS. As I've said before, if I get any combination of incite, obsidian shatter, anger2, or taunting blow, I can pull aggro off a slower. Since slow is right around 700 hate, and 2 obsidian shatter procs out-aggro it, I believe it to be right around 350-400 aggro. With WA4, it procs considerably more than my anger aug in it, due to the enhanced proc rate of obsidian shatter. If I got a GBoS tomorrow, and put in an anger2 aug, I would not mainhand it over the OSoW. Their procs are both enough hate, along with incite, to keep aggro when a mob is slowed, which is pretty much the only time I ever lose aggro. Since the OSoW has the increased proc rate, my odds of getting a proc on the first several rounds of melee is higher, which means the slowers are happier.

Looking strictly at aggro-per-proc of OSoW, GBoS, and any Enraging blow weapon, the order is this: EB(700 hate), GBoS(400-450ish), OSoW(350-400). But without any WA, a GBoS procs twice per minute (mainhand) on average, for a total of 800-900 aggro. The OSoW procs 2.5 times per minute, for a total of 875-1000 aggro. OSoW > GBoS in my opinion, go with what works for you though.
