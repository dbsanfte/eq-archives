You can use a bunch of CoD pets (and many do) but it certainly is not needed. The real key here, as in many places, is to keep a taunty tank up with high MR.

We just use a bard in the MT/SA group and spam quicker heals with the occasional MGB SoTW/Paragon/Elixer. I'd recommend just taking her on in the middle of the room though, not much point in playing with the pillars. Our early fails were all related to pillar-poofing. Use melee in the semi-circle with the MT's back to the casters like any open-field encounter and she should stay reasonably stable for you. Sometimes people use things like WS rangers but that's more a question of preferance I think.

Don't get me wrong, RD is certainly tougher than the fights before her. She is not impossible though by any stretch, although your class composition will dictate tactics. Definitely the easiest method is likely the charm-pet version, although we don't use it ourselves.
