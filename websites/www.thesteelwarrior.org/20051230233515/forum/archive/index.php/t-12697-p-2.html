Bloodthirst vs. Shield Slam from a post I made in WoW Warrior forums


Assumptions: 5x defiance, 15% crit, 1200AP, infinite rage and 25% Damage reduction (fairly typical with 5x sunders), 1.9 values for bloodthirst, use bloodthirst instead of shield slam, +1% hit (might gloves),

Shield Slam
500 * .75 = 375 damage
4%miss 5%dodge, 5%parry, 15% crit: 13.36% 0 damage, 15% 750 damage, 71.64% 375 damage = 381 damage
= 381 * 4 + 950 = 2471 hate

Blood Thirst (he doesnt even do this)
1200 * .45 = 540 damage * .75 = 405 damage.
4% miss, 5% dodge, 5%parry, 15% crit: 13.36% 0 damage, 15% 810 damage, 71.64% 405 damage = 412 average damage
= 412 * 4 = 1648 hate
Question here is what does the +100 healing do for hate? It should be somewhere between 100 and 400 hate value. There is also the possibility of some innate hate on bloodthirst.. but lets just put that the minimum hate should be
1648+100 = 1748

2471 - 1748 = 723 hate /4 = 180.75

You must do 180.75 extra damage (or other agro) in the 6 seconds between 31 point abilities with your flurry build to make up for the lost hate from shield.

with 15% flurry, your flurry should be up approximately 40% of the time. This also increases your dps by
With Quel
2.0 /1.3 = 1.54 speed with flurry
1.54 * .4 + 2 * .6 = 1.816 adjusted speed
This means in ~18 seconds, you will swing 10 times with flurry, and only 9 times without giving you 1/3 of an additional heroic strike every 6s
Heroic Strike: (this does not include the agro you would have had from your regular attack)
1020 hate / 3 = 340 hate

723 - 340 = 383 hate /4 = 95.75 damage

Now we are getting really close.
Did that 1/3 of an attack give you the 95.75 damage you would need to gain agro? What about crits from shield bash/revenge proccing flurry and increasing the % of time you are in a flurried state? What about higher crit %s and AP? What about extra procs from your weapon due to the increased attack speed? In a non-infinite rage state, you also increase your rage generation by doing damage.

All of these things get better for flurry/bloodthirst as your gear gets better but do not affect shield slam nearly as much!
