08-15-2003, 03:30 AM
but now there seems to be a cap on how much hate that it can bring you up.

Think you did not make it thru all 22 pages of brutul's taunt test thread. Current thinking as I understand it is that aggro = Hate + HateBonus and the skill Taunt gives hate. It does not give any hate bonus. The amount of hate it gives is enough to match whoever has the highest hate value of the Taunted NPC, plus a marginal amount. So even tho you succesfully taunt, you may not actually get agro. You do gain enough hate to be highest hate.

Note I stole a great deal of that explanation from Arantius excellent post in Brutul's taunt test thread. You can find his post here (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&postid=33240#post33240) .
