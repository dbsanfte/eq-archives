Originally posted by Omnidream
How does raw hp > sta hp if in the end I have less hp total because I go with raw hp please explain to me the mathmatics behind this equation. Simple, STA is capped, raw HP are not. Once you get to the point that you can max STA with buffs, all other STA is wasted (other than to free up a buff slot). Look at it this way -

Warrior_01:
6500hp unbuffed, 305sta unbuffed, +2000hp raw from items
8902hp raid buffed, 305sta raid buffed

Warrior_02:
6500hp unbuffed, 255sta unbuffed, +2200hp raw from items
9102hp raid buffed, 305sta raid buffed

While these numbers are made up, they are pretty close to accurate. If you are in a position where you are maxed STA now, but you can trade 50 STA for 200 HP raw, you'll end up with about the same HP unbuffed, but 200hp higher once you get buffed. You will always be better off in the long run by going raw HP over STA HP since STA is capped.
