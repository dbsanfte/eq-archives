You have 3 types of regen and they stack with eachother...

Innate (IE. Troll/Iksar, AAs, ect...) - Uncapped

Worn (IE. Fungi Tunic, Chardok Bracer Quest, ect...) - Capped at 30 or 35

Spell/Song (IE. Chloro, Regrowth, or any clicky regenerative effect like Fungi Staff, Bard Songs, ect...) - Uncapped.
