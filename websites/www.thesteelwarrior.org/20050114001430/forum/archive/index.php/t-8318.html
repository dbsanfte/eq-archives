Whats that mean? Enlighten us!

It's a hack. The x,y,z coordinates of where you stand in a zone are completely client side. The client tells the server where you are, not the reverse like in first person shooter games. This is why the old unplug the modem, run through mobs, plug the modem back in exploits work. The client is sending your coordinates to the server, and the server is just accepting them thinking it can trust the client fully. Having a game do it this way cuts down the appearance of lag down massively, but also leaves it open for easy cheating. People can use Macro Quest to change where they are standing, warping their character instantly whereever in the zone they want.
