wandor
There are a couple things you need to consider when purchasing LDoN vendor items, and your current situation will impact these differently.

1. When considering armor purchases, you need to compare each item individually, because as Flair pointed out, there is a substancial difference in cost compared to the amount of upgrade when comparing Tak and EF armors. Like all other armor sets, you need to determine which pieces suit you the best for the poists cost.

2. When considering any augment, you have to take into account that you will lose the augment when you upgrade the item you upgrade. If you have some cultural armor but you are in a VT or elem+ raiding guild, it's not really a good idea to put +40hp on those items beacuse it is very likely you will get a better item in the near future anyway. Same goes with weapons augments. If you don't stand a chance at getting better than CHoS and SotB then put Anger/Yak on them. If your guild is killing AoW or BoT towers or at least near there, then you probably want to wait until you can get a BoC or a GBoS before buying an augment.

Basically you want to spend your points where they will last the longest because once they are spent, you can never get them back.

Originally posted by Steenky
Also, do I need to spend the 1492 points on one item to unlock all the dungeon gear, or can I save up 1492 and buy 2 cheaper items? I'm sure this has been explained elsewhere, but I thought since we're on the subject and all, I might as well ask here. There are actually 2 pools of points you are working with.

1. "Faction" points - the number of points you have earned in a particular theme determines how many of the items you can see in that theme. You have to have 1492 Tak points to see 1492 Tak items. Once you have unlocked a level of items in one theme, you will always be able to see them no matter how many Total points you have.

2. Total points - once you unlock an item, when you buy it, it comes out of the TOTAL amount of points you have earned across all adventures.
