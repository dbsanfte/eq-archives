So, lets run the math.

BoC mainhand: 13 + 22 Hate Per Attack (HPA) = 35 HPA.
BoC offhand: 22 HPA.
BoC proc hate per second: 700 / 30 = 23.33 HPS

BoC mainhand hasted HPS: 1.8 (DA) * 2 * 35 / 2 + 23.33 = 86.3333 HPS

BoC offhand hasted HPS: 1.8 * 2 * 22 / 2 * 0.8 + 23.33 = 55 HPS. (probably have proc rate wrong for an offhand)

Primal (or other 20 delay, < 15 damage weapon) offhand hasted HPS: 1.8 * 2 * 11 / 2 * .8 = 15.84 HPS

BoC primary/Primal offhand HPS: 102.17 HPS.

GSoT HPS (no AA): 1.8 * (33 + 36) * 2 / 3 = 82.8 HPS + stun proc.

Placing a stun proc at 700 hate, that's 106.133 HPS. GSoT aggro should be roughly the same as BoC+Primal. It needs 575 hate per second to match the hate of a BoC + Primal.

If the 2 H AA abilities boost 2H swing rate by any significant degree, the GSoT will own a BoC+Primal (or the like) combo.

There are a few edges to 2 1 H weapons. First, if you can get both of their damage at 15 or above (according to the round rules outlined), you are in a good place. Second, if they both have aggro procs, and both are augmented with LDoN +aggro procs (either stun or anger), you can get (probably) better aggro than with a single proc and single LDoN +aggro proc.

Very interesting model, however.
