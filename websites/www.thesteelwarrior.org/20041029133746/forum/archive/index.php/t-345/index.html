Ainian
DI has no such thing as a partial heal, or a full heal, or whatever you're talking about.

It's a full restoration (Complete heal Cap does NOT apply to DI) of your HP, when you fall under 15% life, not including when a mob "one rounds" you from above 15% to 0% (death)

Hard to explain, but it restores ALL your HP whenever you land between 1hp-15% of your HP.

When it fails, you get the text "You are no longer watched." Keep in mind, it can fail and you still survive if another heal lands before you drop to 0% (death)


A bugged DI, on the other hand, triggers the "Ainian has been rexscued by Divine Intervention!" text, and if you recieve damage immediately after the save, you die. It's like, the heal lags, or something. Remember when you would recieve a heal, and still die back in Velious/Early Luclin? It works exactly like that. Play with ALL spells on, and it doesn't bug out. For me, anyhow.

Bugged DI looks something like this:

A fire beetle hits YOU for 1188 points of damage.
Ainian goes into a berserker frenzy!
Ainian has been rescued by Divine Intervention!
A fire beetle hits YOU for 1744 points of damage.
You die.
You die.
You die.
