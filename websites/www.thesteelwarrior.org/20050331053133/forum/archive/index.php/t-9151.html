There's a huge thread about it elsewhere, but here's the basics:

Earthshaker is a 37/70 2hs that has a proc that is an AE DD. So if it procs, every mob in range will be hit by the AE. Rampage, an AA skill, allows you to simultaneously have a swing at every mob in melee range. If your earthshaker procs an average of once per 3 swings, that means you should average 1 proc for every 3 mobs in range when you rampage. So if you train 100 mobs to one spot and rampage, you'll get ~33 procs with these numbers. Using this concept, you can train a huge amount of mobs to one spot and rampage, and hopefully kill them all instantly, since the more mobs you have = the more chances to proc.

explanation :
* the earthshaker is a 2HS with a big delay (70) that proc a 250HP PBAE spell. thus when this weapon procs it domages every mob around the warior (within a given radius) for that much.
* since it has a big delay, a fully slowed warrior with WA5 has more than 70% chance to proc on each swing.
* rampage is a triggered warrior AA that hits once every mob around the warrior with the primary wielded weapon. It has the particularity to check for possible proc to fire on every swing (one per mob).
* the combinaison of 1. a fully slowed warrior with WA5 and wielding shakerpage, 2. a very very big train pulled (100+ mob) headed on top of this warrior ; leads to more than 70 proc of PBAE at the same time (rampage hits every mobs at the same time) on every mob around the warrior resulting in domaging all the mob around him by around 20kHP (no resist, enougth mob, lucky proc rate).

So when you click the rampage button, you havem ore chance to proc.

Let's make a weapon up, XXII. It has a 500DDAE proc.
XXII has 100 Delay (10 seconds per swing) without haste. The person has 3PPM. Since you swing 6 times in a minute, there is a 50% chance to proc each swing (Because you have 3 procs per minute, and only swing 6 times, 3/6) Now let's try rampaging! You pull 100 mobs and click rampage, without haste or slow. Since you have a 50% procrate, you'll on average proc on 50 of those. 50x50 = 25kae, grats!

Now, let's rez you up. Rez effects is a 95% slow.
Without haste, that'd be 100 delay times 1.95 (The amount you're slowed, 95%), which brings us to realistic speed of 195 delay, or 19.5 seconds per swing. 19.5 x 3ppm = 58.5/60 = 97.5% chance to proc. Now, you pull 100 mobs again. You gather them up and click rampage - You have a 97.5% procrate, 500x97(roundeddown)= 48.5kAE

That, my friend, is why slow owns.
