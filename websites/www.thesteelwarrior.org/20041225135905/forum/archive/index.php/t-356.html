For haste I had Vallon's Quickening and a Custom Cowl of Mortality.

I'm not saying I hit like that all the time. If you notice, the samples I provided are several seconds apart. Think of it like the rounding on your experience bar. Sometimes when you kill a mob, you'll get 1 percent, then next mob 2 percent, then 1 percent, then 2 percent.

Say you get 1.3 percent actual experience per mob kill. Every time you kill a mob, it's going to add 1 percent until you kill mob #4.

Example:

0 % - 0

1 % - 1.3

2 % - 2.6

3 % - 3.9

5 % - 5.2

I know this has nothing to do with delay but it's probably an anology you'll easier understand.

On the same note, you'll swing, say once a second according to the log, but every 2 or 3 rounds of attack, it'll appear like you got in a whole extra set of attacks. It's not because you recieved extra attacks, it's the delay being faster than the log will show with its 1 second intervals, MUCH like how you didn't get an extra percentage of experience above, even though you jumped from 3 to 5 percent. The experience bar can only show in 1 percent intervals much like the log. It's just math. Do the math :p.
