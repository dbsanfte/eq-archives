A new Sirran spawns if there is no Sirran currently up anywhere in the zone.

The best way to deal with Sirran is to move kill locations a bit each time you kill the Spiroc Lord. There is plenty of space near the island's edge to do this.

If your general kill location is at one of the island corners nearest to the Spiroc Lord, then you will generally not get Spiroc Guardian aggro. But be prepared for it, and have everyone camp out in that case.

Since you are dealing with deathtouching mobs and Sirran's uber melee, I recommend always having a cleric bot camped out for the duration of your wingblade farm.

Unless things changed, you can expect a drop rate of about 40% on the wingblade.

Ok, here's how we farmed TSL. When you port in you should be safe as long as no one moves towards the bird houses. The little spirocs were all content to just run around in circles.

If you move to your left from the arrival point it will make things easier.

Once in the SW corner, pull the nearest birdhouse. You will get three maybe more. If you also get the guardian, don't worry. If you get a guardian, move away from his spawn point while fighting. Kill the guardian first (if accidentially pulled), then the vanquishers, then the other mobs. Just make sure you have people calling out what they are off tanking and have the MT grab those mobs quickly in order.

By killing the guardian first, he immediately respawns. Back at his is perch near the SE corner

Kill what ya got and then move north along the west edge of the island.
Here you will be near one of the bird houses. Pull it dispatching the vanquishers.

You may or may not of gotten the third bird house by now. If not stay at your camp and pull the mobs over there.

Once everything is dead except for the guardian and the lord farming time begins.

The Spiroc Lord spawns near the portal pad in the NE corner of the island. To do this, pull the spiroc lord to your camp along the west edge. Once there kill him and do the hand end of your keys to Sirran. Now you will need to move your camp closer to the spiroc lord. There is a swail between your current camp and the spiroc lord. I guess you would say this is about North Center of the island. From this camp, you will be able to see TSL at his spawn point Pull him here and dispatch. Pull him again and kill it.

Now here is the trick. One Sirran spawns, you are on a timer. Watch the clock carefully and time your pulls. If you can not kill TSL before Sirran despawns, then wait. Once you are in this waiting pattern for Sirran to despawn, move back to the West edge camp where Sirran spawned. Immediately up Sirran's death pull TSL to here and kill him. Sirran will spawn here which means you move back to the North Center camp.

Basically, the idea is to watch the clock and kill TSL in a place where Sirran wont aggro on you. Then move to another camp and repeatedly kill him until right before Sirran despawns (20 minute timer).

After a while you will get this down. We went thru 20 spawns of TSL and recovered 6 wingblades for epics.
