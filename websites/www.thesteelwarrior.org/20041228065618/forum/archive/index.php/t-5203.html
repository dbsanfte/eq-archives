Each SKILL mod of 8-15% etc will increase the number of times you do X by about 1-3%. An 8% dodge mod will help you dodge about 1-2% more often.

There's already a number of posts that explain this.

You decide if dodging 1-2% more often is worth it. Also note that it's possible to dodge 1-2% better, riposte 3% better, AND parry 1-2% better...

Given the choice between these options or having more HP/AC, most warriors simply add an AC or HP aug.

Dodge +8% probably amounts to adding about another 10-20 HP. Your mileage may differ since it depends on your total HP but should at least get about 10 HP of usefulness out of that mod. Note that dodging 1% more of incoming attacks (roughly going from getting hit 50% of the time to 49% of the time) amounts to roughly 200 more HP on a 10K warrior so yeah of course you're not going to dodge that much more. The additional avoidance has to be minuscule due to the nature of avoidance. Avoiding 1% more of total incoming attacks is at least 2% more HP (since you only get hit 50% of the time to begin with).

I'd probably get a Vengeance in that slot since its effect is most tangible though.
