Battleblade
Yep, Warriors often arrange their Magelo's for bragging rights. If every Warrior did so for maximum HP's you would have a way to compare them - a standard if you will.

In some guilds a Warrior's opprotunity to MT is often based on max HP (and AAExp and aggro weapons) with people arguing that an extra 100 HP's might just mean the MT survives a melee round.

But the truth is a lot more complicated than that. For any given mob you might have to get a particular resist to 500. So, comparing HP's without taking into account resists is pretty silly.

A mob might stun heavily so Stalwart Endurance is suddenly a must have.

And trumping everything in the end is ability and experiance with the mob you are facing.

Still, maximum HP's is one way to do a quick and dirty ranking of Warriors. While people believe a Warrior with 10000HP > than one with a mere 9900, we'll keep answering questions about our max HP's as if it were the Holy Grail.
