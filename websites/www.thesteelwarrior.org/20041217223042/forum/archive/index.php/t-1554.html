Defined 'bugged'?

The fact that a PH for the main boss spawns at the end of the ring is not a bug. This is this way so that you can finish the zone and get into poearthb even if the loot-dropping bosses have been killed. The PHs are identical in every way to the real bosses, other than they don't drop loot (sort of like the Cursed cycle in Ssra).

The ring events are harder for xp groups to disrupt. I'm sure they are still blowable, tho.

The only way a ring will be "bugged" is if you totally finish the ring and kill the named. The other night we got to the vine ring and it was 2 am and we lost due to lack of numbers at the time. We came back the next day, redid all of the vine ring and kill the named.
