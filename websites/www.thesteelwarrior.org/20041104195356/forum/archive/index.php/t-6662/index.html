Lilwee Beasty
Weapons-Use the SEWS for any and all grouping situations I can think of. It is THAT much better than your Dual Wield combo. It has the most DPS, and the most aggro, to not use it would be like tieing an arm behind your back.

AA's-In my opinion, go with CA-LR, then CS-ID, after than, you can mix and match proc/hp/offensive/fun AA's to your hearts content. He already has enough offensive AA's, combined with an *excellent* weapon, to be an aggro magnet.

Gear-I'd suggest trying to farm Sol Ro for ornate armor, if your having terrible luck there, hit up the Bazaar and search warrior-hp-helm or whatever slot your looking to upgrade.
