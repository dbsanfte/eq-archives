wandor
If you are wanting good agro on a budget you have a couple choices. First of all, I would sell both tradeable weapons. While the Frosty may have the best agro, it's really not that spectacular. Once you sell your tradeable weapons - all of them - then start shopping for a GSoT. The way prices are dropping you should be able to pick one up rather inexpensively.

Once you get a GSoT, you will have a pretty good all purpose agro/DPS weapon. Now you can spend some time farming. Get a group and head to Droga for the king camp. The mobs are easy, but they do have a bit of HP. Take a DPS heavy group so you can kill fast and keep up with a lot of spawns. You want to be able to keep the king room AND the pool room right outside clear. This will give you a shot at a Copper Hammer of Striking and a Sword of the Bloodsworn if either named pops. Then you can ditch the SH and use droga weapon main/ribcracker offhand. If you get lucky you might get both. Either weapon is great to pair with a BoC when you get one.
Aratus
Faster isn't always better. Don't forget that you have no damage bonus going to the off-hand weapon. So generally it's best to stick the best ratio weapon you can in there, unless your dual'ing proc weapons or something.

As for your aggro on proc questions: Regular DD's give a very low amount of aggro, I can chain proc a Snowchipper three or four times and not pull off a Ranger Call of Fire proc. Dots'll do a decent amount of aggro depending on the dot. Things hate being AC debuffed, and Stuns are golden.

Greatstaff of Thunder all the way, it has the versatility and multi-purpose role your seeking.
