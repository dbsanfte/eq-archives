The reason you put a fast weapon mainhand is to take advantage of the free damage you get from your main hand damage bonus. The problem is that until you get into your mid to late 40s, your damage bonus is negligible. It doesn't add enough damage to justify sacrificing speed for ratio. Since you are relying on DPS for agro, you will be better off weidling a Wurmy for another 10-15 levels. Keep the Lammy bagged and swap it out when you get to level 45-48, until then the Wurmy is a superior weapon.

All the damage bonus does is after you hit the mob it adds the base damage you have done + the damage bonus per hit.

Example:
weapon dmg: 6
weapon dmg bonus: 3

You swing and hit for 18. That means you hit for 15 and the +3 damage bonus was added after the strike.

As was stated previously, go with the weapon with the best ratio (wurmslayer) until you get to your late 40's, I think it's level 48 or 49 (did the math a long time ago) where the lammy will begin to outdamage the wurmy due to your damage bonus. The other alternative is to use a faster weapon with an aggro proc. And the wakizashi, while adding to overall DPS if it procs alot, wont be all that good aggro wise, 'cause it's pure DD. Anyway, the point is, until your damage bonus gets to be about 8 or so wurmy is better than lammy from a pure DPS perspective. Use a proccing weapon in offhand (snare whip, ykesha, or better) and you should have no aggro problems at all unless someone is really trying to pull aggro (or is a total retard, which usually amounts to the same thing) :D
