If I had a long session, I would go to the Dreadlands & bring a good bow and some long-range arrows & lots of food & drink.
If you're in a very good group/get a couple of levels, you can try Karnor's.
If I just had a couple hours to play at a time, I'd do an LDoN. The LDoN will be the better loot.
If you have a group of friends you regularly group with, you have more options. If you're lfg, you have to go where the people are. Certainly they are doing LDoNs; not sure there are as many folks still doing DL.
PoJ entrance is good exp, marginal loot. Always groups fighting there, too. Sometimes can be hard to get a group there if you just show up and shout 46 War lfg, though, because a lot of 46 warriors aren't geared well enough to tank there, and just sort of sponge.
Mostly I'd start finding some really good players who need a tank for their group. Lots of people will talk about which zones give you best exp, but the fact is that playing with good players is exponentially the biggest ZEM in the game. Conversely, playing with poor players will make your troll exp penalty look completely trivial.
