04-25-2003, 12:21 AM
You do not gain a Natural Durability HP increase on any buff, aside from buffs that increase your stamina.


The amount of HP you gain from Natural Durability, comes from your base unbuffed hitpoints, including Stamina buffs only. IE - Talisman of the Boar, Righteous Health, etc.

Buffs like Foucs of the Soul, Virtue, etc, do not get a Natural Durability bonus added onto them.
