The Sword of Ssraeshza
Guadralek, Sword of the Sky
Great Blade of Storms
Keen Edged Cleaver
Sword of the Bloodsworn

I will have Avatar from primal most of the time, and my attack is usually about 1500 in a typical group (group with rangers a lot). I read some reports on the board that the OG is only brought to a good DPS point when you have an ATK to overcome the AC of whatever you're fighting, but it seems like LDoN stuff has relatively low mitigation (have not parsed, but similar posts + anecdotal experience seems to support this). Should I aim for the OG and ditch the rest of the weapons?

Thanks.

Update: Just wanted to add that I realize that a call like this is highly subjective. My intent of this post is just to get people's opinions and the backing reasons, so I can get a bit more data on which to make my decision.
