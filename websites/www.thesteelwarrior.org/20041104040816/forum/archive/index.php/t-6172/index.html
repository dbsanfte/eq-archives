wandor
2 things come to mind here:

1. The zerker you where grouped with was an idiot.
2. An SoD is not that much of an upgrade over a SotB to warrant the insane price. Unless you have the very best tradeable gear available, I would recommend putting the SoD up for sale and upgrading the crap out of your gear. You would be MUCH better served picking up a couple 100 HP and AC from upgrades than you are from the 5% increase in DPS you get from an SoD over a SotB. Don't just blindly buy ornate either, there are quite a few items that are as good or better than ornate for a whole lot less if you shop around.
