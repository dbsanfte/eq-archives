Noramally I would recommend the CoF because you lose less AC/HP over upgrading other slots, but you can really put the 20k difference to good use by upgrading other slots instead. Depending on your server economy, you could end up with a Soulscream Belt and a couple of other upgrades to really give yourself a huge boost in STA.

waist: Soulscream Belt (+10 sta)
rings: 2x Valorium Ring of Gallantry (2x +8 sta)
ear: Earring of the Solstice (+5 sta)
range: Darkwood Bow of Suffering (+5 sta)
mask: Grimling Contraption (+5 sta)

For the 45k you would spend on a CoF, you should be able to get all of the above items and end up with an additional 41 sta.

Once you get those, I would look for:

ear: Mosquito Leg Earring (+7 sta)
arms: Kizrak's Armband of Battle (+6 sta)
boots: Kizrak's Boots of Battle (+3 sta)
hands: Kizrak's Gauntlets of Battle (+8 sta)

In any case, you need to get some haste ASAP, no haste is hurting you more than you can imagine.
