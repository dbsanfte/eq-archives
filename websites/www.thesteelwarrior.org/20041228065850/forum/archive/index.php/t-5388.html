** 2)does slow make a higher procchance ? **

your proc rate is "procs per minute" if you hit someone 10 times per minute and proc 1 time per minute your chance to proc is 10%

if you hit someone with same weapon/dex 5 times per minute (becuase your slowed) you will still proc 1 time per minute, but your chance to proc is 20% ....

you wont proc more, but your chance to proc on a SINGLE hit is higher

I've had Rampage and an Earthshaker for quite a while now. I can tell you there is no way to get exp with it for yourself unless you go to the extreams those three warriors took in PoFire. At best I average about 2-4k damage per mob with a good 50+ mob train, sometimes less. Thats enough to kill a bunch of gimpy 900 hp hollowshade mobs, but not enough to exp off of. *perhaps* if you threw in a furious with a really heavy 2her, you could take out a bunch of light blues.

I have found that you can power level twinks (sorta) with this. Get your random twink to 43, then group with them. Rampershaker a zone like dulak's harbor or the great divide. Can get them good exp up till 50. Even here its a bit hard because of the mix of mobs you get, some you'll kill out right while others will flee. So it takes more work than you may think.

At least furious/rampage+earthshaker gives us some sort of AoE presence.

When GoD comes out, the weapon affinity aa's are the first im going to get. I plan on parsing my rampershaking after I max it (damage per mob, not procs per min - in the end thats what matters). Should be interesting.
