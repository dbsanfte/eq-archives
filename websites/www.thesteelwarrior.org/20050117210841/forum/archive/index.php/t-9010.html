These changes are actually really silly and un-needed.

Even without linked spell timers, paladins only really had one viable omens stun in exp groups (force of piety) that would land on level 66-70 mobs.

The other stun (serene command) also works on 66-70 mobs, but has a huge knockback, and anyone who's been in a group with a pally tanking will tell you how much they hate knock back stuns - so much so that it isn't memmed all that often.

The linked timers don't prevent paladins from casting Force of Piety or Serene Command back to back - both of which land on level 70 mobs.

What they prevent is paladins casting Force of Piety (max level 70) back to back with Force of Akilae (max level 65).

In theory this limits you from overstunning mobs that are level 65 and below. (potentially there would have been 4 stuns that would land on level 65 mobs)....

In practice though most pallies only mem two stuns because there are not enough spell slots left to mem other things (2 heals, group heal, DA, root, cure etc....), and even with 2 stuns it's possible to stun lock a mob by co-ordinate with the togeher classes in the group that also have stuns.

Pre-omens pallies would mem only the two force stuns previously (62/65 limits), and at level 65 had a total of 3 spells that would land on a level 62 mobs.

Post omens pallies have to chose between memming the 62/70 stun combo or the 70/70 stun combo with the knockback.

This means with the old combo that the level range of mobs that were only stunnable by one stun (from the Force of Akilae/Akera combo) was 3 levels, and the maximum level you could stun lock was level 62.

In omens - pallies did not receive a force of Akera upgrade, so this gap would have widened to 5 levels even without linked spell timers. By introducing linked spell timers it has remained at 62.

The worst part of this is the biggest impact to paladins here has not been on stunning ability, as stun locking is still possible with carefully co-ordinated stunning between cleric/enchanter/pally - it's been on the rest of the pally spell books:

It's no longer possible to cast any of the following back to back

Instant cast heals
Undead nukes
Group heals
