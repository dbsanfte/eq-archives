Redhenna
Been a bunch lately of gear upgrade questions and it got me to thinking. What are some good places people can go, 50+, to exp and have a reasonable chance of upgrading their gear at? Part of this is assuming that, like so many warriors, you simply do not have alot of cash. I remember my 50's and early 60's(and continuing on even to now, tho it is not nearly as bad) as being very poor, and not being able to afford all those wonderfull things in the bazaar. I also remember not wanting to spend countless hours farming greans for cash or some ultra rare drop. I wanted to spend as much of my time as possible exping. So, what are some good exp spots where you can also attain servicable armor? Let me start with a few.

1: Soulscream belt, Veksar: Can probably start doing this camp about 50 with a regular exp group. Veksar exp is not bad at all, mobs all drop plat and some weapons, and a chance at a 31 % haste belt make for a win win situation.

2: Mosquito leg earing, Cazic Thule: full group 55 + could probably handle this camp(maybe higher for your CC person). A good droppable earing, what should be decent exp(tho not great), and not usually camped make this a nice possibility. CT yields several other nice pieces of gear, and sewers is a good place to start in CT.

3: Moktar/Ralduken armor, Umbral Plains: Early SoL this was a scary zone to a lot of more casual players. Guild I was in at the time would take 3 groups to slowly farm ZI. This is no longer the case, and 1 group 50 + should be able to farm ZI with little trouble. Moktar/Ralduken armor is not the greatest stuff in game, but some pieces are servicable into the 60's.

Those are 3 that pop to my mind, what other good ones are out there?
