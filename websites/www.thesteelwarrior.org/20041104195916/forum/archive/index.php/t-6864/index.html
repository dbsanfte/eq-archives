Beornegar
I can only reiterate what others have said:
I solo'd alot up until about level 45. My gear was not very good (almost full crafted, dwarven work boots, skull shaped barbute, various relatively crappy weapons). It was absolutely miserable between level 25 or so and level 45 when the level of gear I had simply did not allow me to solo anymore. With the right gear it can be done, but at best it will be slow, painful, and many times you'll die and end up losing more xps than you gain. (this can happen alot when soloing with a warrior) Your best bet is to duo with a healer, and at your level that will be best done with a shaman or a druid (slows and damage shields respectively), although a cleric is a good option too. As you get to higher level, the cleric will start to outshine the druid because of better hp buffs and better heals, but shaman will still be the best for stat buffs and more importanly slows (you can easily buy virtue at this point). I still get a lot of my AA xps duoing with a shaman in lower end zones like PoI or deeper areas of veksar.
