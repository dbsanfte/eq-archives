The best way to tell would be to parse your weapon logs.

I also like the Luthair damage calculator. It estimates your DPS given weapon stats and skills. It seems pretty accurate, but it doesn't take into account high level AAs and such. I also don't know if it has accurate damage tables for levels 61-65. But for people level 1-60, it looks pretty good. For proc weapons, just assume you'll proc 2 or 3 times per minute with your main hand, and about 1 or 2 times with your off-hand, and you should be good.

The URL for Luthair's site is http://www.geocities.com/luthairsite/weaponCalc.html .
