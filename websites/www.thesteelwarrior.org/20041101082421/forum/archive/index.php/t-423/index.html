Once the shaman is 58 and gets Kragg's mending your options will open up to most regular tier 1 mobs. Your best option is to root the mobs, back off warrior, slow, then move in. Another option is to have the shaman slow pull, but unless you have kite space this is scary. The fewer hits you take before slow the better. With KEI on shaman you will be going nearly non-stop.

Note: exp is better in a good regular group but like someone mentioned, you can pickup LFGs as they come. I'd seriously reccomend another healer though. While a shaman can heal one tank and self pretty well at 58, when you have 6 potential targets to heal it can get scary (especially when 2 boxing like I do! :) )
