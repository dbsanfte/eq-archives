Neksnappa
I did Eastern wastes solo from 43 to 45. In the northeast corner of zone, up by the shore, theres lots of ulthorks/walruses to kill. The walruses are lowest level, followed by ulthork hunters, then the warriors.

Fairly simple to solo- just go pull one, run it back towards the tree on the edge of the shore ( Only one there is, cant miss it, it's near the dorf huts) Just hack at the thing til it starts to run, finish it off, then bindwound back to 50% health.

My favorite way to do this was to break out my weighted axe. Jboots/sow potions are helpful here. Just take a swing at the mob, hitting upwards of 200+ in a round commonly, run away and wait for your delay to refresh. Do another run past the mob, take 1 swing, run away again. Worked really well, I only lost maybe 3% of my health per mob.

I soloed them with around 2200-2300 hp, 880ac, and my troll regen. Enjoy.
