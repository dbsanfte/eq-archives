Though I did fail the first attempt solo, I grabbed an invis potion, ran back in, recovered my body, killed yard trash to proc avatar, and then went back to Skargus' room with ToD in hand and found he was still there! Having already taken the HT, it wasn't so bad the second time. When I was at about 60% health the ToD finally proc'd and I switched to a Windblade and whacked him until he fell, leaving me with 8% health and 2 adds on me. Whacked the adds and healed up a bit in his room and then made my way back to Chardok and got my ring =).

A note to all people who still need to do the Skargus fight... There are rumors that Droga/Nurga are being revamped as lvl60+ zones on test server, so I'd recommend you get down there and whack him while he's still fairly easy to get to.
