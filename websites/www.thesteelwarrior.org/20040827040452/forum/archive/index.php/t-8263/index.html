1. Attacking people based on their level of progression in the game will get you booted, period.

5. Modding the channel for innane reasons to make immature announcements or to flex your authority will be cause for the removal of mod powers.

6. Multi-line ASCII hotkeys, while cute, are nonetheless discouraged.

7. Heated discussions about hot topics related to EQ will occur, and are to be expected. From time to time, I'll inquire about hot topics, such as "the list" to present on Sony Forums.

8. Racism, and Anti-Seimitism will not be tolerated, period.

9. Members of other classes are welcome into the channel, members of jihads to the channel will be instabooted, and kept that way.

I'll be looking for people to make mods, if you're interested, send me a tell in game.
