apolyon
For this raid you need both a designated MT and a designated MA (not the same person).

Upon clicking up into each room, the MT, named debuffers, and his respective clerics need to get on the named mob. Everyone else follows the MA around in a preset direction destroying the portals. Wizzies can disentigrate them, so be sure to bring the proper reagents. After all portals are gone cycle through the now mezzed adds and kill them. Once the floor is clear assist the MT on the named.

The named on the second floor is a ranged dps fight only as he has some nasty little effects including a huge slow and some serious dmg nukes.

When you get to agnarrs floor have a chanter quickly tash Agnarr .. shaman malo ... mage mala .. and have every ranger chain snare him till you land a snare. Once he is snared a designate ranger will aggro kite him whereever you want him kited. Same old same old on the room strat .. mez adds and cycle through the portals (all but one which you cant kill) and then kill the named. Once you've cleared the room you have all day to set up for the agnarr kill.

Most gulids kill agnarr upstairs in a corner because it is far from the named giant pops and cornering helps with the area effect mana drains etc. Posistion healers and nukers at max range .. pull agnarr and heal your tank. Heal the melee's in the ae now and then with an ae heal and you've toasted Agnarr.

Now, there are 3 named spawns through the agnarr fight and 2 ways to deal with them. You can either all assist and kill them when they pop, or you can kite them and burn down agnarr. We usually use the latter strat, as it makes for a quicker, less confusing script. We have a bard and 4 rangers downstairs all in the same group with a druid to patch heal as needed. The bard keeps runspeed up on the rangers while they each kite 1 named each via snare aggro. The extra ranger is backup in case one of them dies. After agnarr is dead the rangers bring their giants up one by one and the raid consumes them.

It's an easy event if you follow the order correctly.
