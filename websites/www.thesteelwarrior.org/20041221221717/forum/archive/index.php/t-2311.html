Ok, I usually don't post or even read in this mini-forum, but I had a thought that I don't recall seeing here before...


Name: Hastened Instigation
Pre-Requisite(s) Area Taunt
Level Requirement: 63
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Description: This ability decreases the amount of time required between uses of the Area Taunt abililty.
The ability decreases the time between re-uses by 10% each rank. The costs are as follows:
Rank 1 (Level 63) - 3 AA Points
Rank 2 (Level 64) - 3 AA Points
Rank 3 (Level 65) - 3 AA Points


Should this not also work on regular taunt? I mean, monks get an AA skill that lets them feign death twice as often at level 3. Why the hell does our "HASTENED INSTIGATION" do nothing for normal taunt? If we could taunt twice as often, even if it's at the cost of an AA skill, then that lessens our chance of being screwed over by the RNG. Anyone else have any thoughts or comments on this?
