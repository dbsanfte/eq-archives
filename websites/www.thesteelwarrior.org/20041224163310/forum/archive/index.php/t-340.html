The dly of the weapon does not affect # of procs over a given period of time by the way.

Two weapons with different proc modifiers will differ but I do not believe SoD or BoC have any positive (or negative) modifiers to proc rate.

Certainly, Blade of War and Primal Velium Claidhmore will proc the exact same number of times over a 8 hour period for instance. This is absolutely not the case comparing Bloodfrenzy versus Blade of Carnage (where the 'frenzy procs 50% more).
