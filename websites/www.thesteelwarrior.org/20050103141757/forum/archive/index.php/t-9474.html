Spell Shield - Reduces the damage you take from direct damage spells
Strikethrough - Allows you to attack even if your opponent dodges, parries, ripostes, or blocks. Same as Tactical Mastery AA.
DoT shielding - Reduces the damage you take from DoTs.
Accuracy - Increases accuracy
Avoidance - Increases avoidance
Combat Effect - Increase proc rate
