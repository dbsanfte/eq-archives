Now, I have a complete parse of him, but I found an oddity. Yalp will not pick Emp's damage up because there is an extra space at the end of his name. The following is what 1) the parse text came out as and 2) what it should look like in order for the parser to pick up.

1) Emperor Ssraeshza hits YOU for 951 points of damage.
2) Emperor Ssraeszha hits YOU for 951 points of damage.

Yalp won't recognize Emp with the extra space at the end of his name (1). I went in and manually took some of those spaces out and it picked it right up.

Slightly bugged, maybe? Cursed/Exiled/Creator/etc did *not* have this anomaly when I parsed them.
