Newbie armor should make it into the mid-20's

Look at the quests for crafted armor but in the meantime look up some minor gear upgrades at Alla's and go camp them.

The secret that nobody knows however is that green mobs are easy to kill and drop loot you can sell to vendors.

In short you will not be able to get gear that lasts untill level 50 right off the bat, thats a caster thing.

You may need to split your time between grouping for experience and killing greenies like spiderlings and animals to loot tradeskill components. Bone chips off skellies work also. Newbie armor works to 20s as said. Make friends with healer types, because as a Warrior you cannot solo well for experience, especially on Stromn where you cannot twink yourself. Don't loot every kill or you'll get a bad rap, but definately loot your share, since odd ammounts of coin do not split and default to the looter. You're an Ogre, collect every piece of crap that you can off mobs, it will all add up eventually

Have fun
