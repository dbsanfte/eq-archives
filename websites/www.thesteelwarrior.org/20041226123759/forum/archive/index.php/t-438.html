Some good advice. I would also try to get some Fine Heraldic in every visible slot other than gloves. Buying it outright would be expensive, but you can usually make a deal with the smith to do combines in exchange for extra ingredients. Usually if you bring enough to do a 2nd one they will do the first for free. With some farming you can get most of a set of armor for nothing more that your time spent. Champ BP + myrmidon gaunts + fine heraldic will get you 4k pretty easy. With ND3 and PE you should be comfortably in around 4400hp.

I wouldn't waste any AA on STA. You can buff STA and your gear will have a lot more STA on it as you upgrade. Those AA would be better spent on something that doesn't become useless when you can hit the STA cap with gear+buffs.
