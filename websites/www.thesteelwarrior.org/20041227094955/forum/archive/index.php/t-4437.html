There is no "trick" to getting Heratius to spawn except that he is the named on the top floor. You only have to kill the PH there and keep rechecking when it repops (I dont remember the timer there).

Valdanov pops out on the balancony when Heratius dies, but invis's himself shortly after he pops so make sure you can see invis.

I spawned him 3 times in a day when I did the quest (wiped twice and called in reinforcements the third), which is just to say it's not that difficult to spawn him if you know what to kill but you can't just walk up and do it necessarily. Of course, you may get lucky and just catch Heratius up sometime by planting a tracker to look for him while you do other stuff.

Good luck.

Heratius Grolden has no placeholder. He just pops when he wants to pop on the right side of the balcony just in front of the centurion if you're looking at it from the mountain behind the castle. Sometimes you may get lucky and see him twice in one day, while other times you may actually go several days without seeing him. When I did the quest, I recorded his spawntimes for just over a week and noticed his chance to pop seems to come every three hours and it's a 1/20 shot in doing so. However, I can probably link you to countless posts where people mentioned he popped almost immedietly upon death. Goes to show why small sample sizes truly do suck, eh? ;)

However, after using the small sample size that I had, I was actually one interval off when I predicted his spawn time, which really me raise an eyebrow. I predicted he would spawn on a Saturday at 7:10pm and he showed up promptly at 10:10pm.

Needless to say, keep at it! The shield is an amazing backslot item and chances are you won't be replacing it until Plane of Time. 50ac 15str 15wis 75hp in a backslot? Oh hell yeah. Too bad it's not 50ac 15sta 15dex 75hp though. :P
