Sassinak
First, do the tutorial. There are some nice rewards if you talk to all of the NPCs in the tutorial and do what they ask. If you are totally new to the game then this will give you enough info to get started. You can also go up to level 5 by hunting monsters within the tutorial's caverns if you like. I thought it was fun and worthwhile.

When you decide to leave the tutorial, you'll appear in Rivervale at the warrior trainer. The trainer has some quests for you which result in pieces of warrior armor. These quests are a lot of fun and definitely worth doing. As you gain each level, you'll become strong enough to do one more piece of the armor set. By level 15 you will have a complete set of armor and probably a new weapon too. This is the most fun and rewarding way to get up to level 15.
