I have to agree, the effect of DEX on procs is not very substantial. And if you are soloing and can not rely on shaman's buffs, even then, HP / AC will server you much better.

The frquency of procs seems really down to the type of proc or the unique weapon that holds the proc.

For example.. Frostbringer procs a lot, even with my crappy base 107 dex. Ykeshas proc a little less, and some weapons just dont proc much.

After you get a shamman buff and your DEX is over 160 or so, the difference in terms of 'proccage' is hardly noticable.

That said, if you want to boost your Dex a little and do a good upgrade you could buy a Cloak of Maestrom which has 10 DEX on it and some very nice HP as well, since you already have a low AC back item, it won't hurt your current AC It sells around 3k.

I'd agree with dex being underestimated. I seem to notice a huge difference in procs with dex maxxed (~275 atm) then when its mid 100's.

Being a warrior though, I'd never buy gear specifically for dex. Go HP/Sta/Ac as the stats sought for each item. You really want to ensure that once you hit 60+ your sta is maxxed, your HP items are good, and your ac is 1300+. When you have focus etc. you'll find you dex will be suffice. Don't forget, illusions also add dex (scare crow being my fave)
