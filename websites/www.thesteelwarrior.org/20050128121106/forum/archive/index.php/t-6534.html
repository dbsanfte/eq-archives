There are many "damage caps" as you level up. Mostly it pertains to your weapon skill, ie, 1hs, 1hb, 2hs, 2hb, piercing. The higher your level (and your weapon skill) the higher your damage cap. There's really no such thing as "Once you hit level 30, no more damage cap!".
