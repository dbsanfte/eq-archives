I don't like to take my eyes off my current target I'm tanking because I need to know if it is still aggro on me at all times, especially during the start of the fight. I propose something like this as an optional indicator for openings:

http://home.covad.net/~sunwoo/EQ/Pictures/Warrior/Opening_Graphical_Indication.jpg

In game all the indicators would animate from near where the mob's name is to near the center of the mob. The text will fade in and out as the animation plays and the text animation would play from the start of when you can execute the appropriate action for the opening until the end. This text would appear "on top" of all objects including walls that may be partially blocking your view of the mob.

I really don't want to have to pay attention to a separate window for opening information and would much rather keep my focus on the action.

This would be a separate option that can be toggled on or off using a command similar to /showspells (maybe make it /showopenings on/off)
