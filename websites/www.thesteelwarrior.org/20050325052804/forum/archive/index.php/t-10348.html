One of my favorite zones is Scarlet Desert. You have a druid who can port you to Twilight Sea and it's just a short run to SD. The warrior and bard can run over to Katta and bind just inside, making for a short run back if a wipe occurs. Zoning into SD from TS, the mobs should be about right for your levels, and you will find that they increase in level as you move on around the walls of the zone towards the Grey zone-out. I've leveled several of my toons through SD, and pulling at the Grey zone I've leveled a couple of them all the way to 60 there...plus a fair number of AA's. While I'm not suggesting you grind in this zone for that long (I have an amazing tolerance for boredom) it's still a lot of fun. One thing I have never found to be fun is a long and dangerous CR, and that is not a major issue in SD. Try Acrylia Caverns. Fair amount of casters, but they have very low HP and don't hit hard at all. Also, the weapons they drop semi-rarely sell to vendors for over 150 pp each. Acrylia still sells well in the bazaar for fletchers, so take a few weight reduction bags if you can.

To get there, port to Grimling Forest, (make sure you have a map here) then invis, and run out of the fort, upstream for a bit, and then look for the big grimling camp on your left. The tunnel entrance is along there. Run down that tunnel, which has quite a few offturns, and you'll eventually come to AC zone in. Be warned that a few of the grimling rogue-mobs and the dogs right at the entrance can see through invis. Just keep runnin at SoW speed and you'll blow past them. The ZI is in the far left corner behind some rocks, in that final big room.
