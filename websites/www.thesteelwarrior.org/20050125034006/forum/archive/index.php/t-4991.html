Champions Armor - Farmed in Arena - Requires coldain faction

Myrm Armor - Farmed in HoT - Requires Skyshrine (NOT Lord yel faction)

Warlords Armor - Farmed in ToV anywhere BUT HoT - Requires Kael Faction

Coldain Faction is Raised by Killing Kromriff giants (mostly). Arena doesn't help a lot for that.

Skyshrine faction is raised by killing Kromzek giants (Arena is the best spot)

Kael Faction is raised by killing dorfs or dragons.

There are quests to help with faction in all 3 cities as well.

You can MQ any of the armors the way you said, though I think both may have to have the required faction. (not sure on that last part)

Timrum,

It has been said a few times in this thread, but I wanted to re-itterate and add to it.

1) Yes, kill in the Arena area of Kael. You will get the biggest faction hits here, it seems like clearing the higher end mobs nets faction faster, at least to me anyways.

2) You can do the MQ so long as everyone handing in the items is Ally (max is not necessary). You can also hand the items in any order, the person to hand in the last necessary item gets the armor. I've done this many many times between all my toons and I normally have one toon loot all the armor drops in HoT. The one and only time I tried with a non max-ally MQ'er we failed and I haven't tried it since then. It is possible it would work, but I'm highly doubtful and would say do not risk it.

3) If you want myrmidon's armor, then you're going to Halls of Testing (HoT) in Temple of Veeshan. It is much easier to get there and hunt there if you're not KOS with Claws of Veeshan faction.

4) One of the safest spots to test claws of veeshan faction is to zone into Skyshrine and walk in until you see the golem guards there and con them. They shouldn't aggro and if they do it is very easy to run back out of the zone. Entering from Wakening Lands is the area I'm speaking of.

Gl and enjoy!
