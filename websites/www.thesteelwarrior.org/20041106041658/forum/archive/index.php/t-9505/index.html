Warhead42
If you have access to all planes, your charm should be at 90%. If you go under manage this character (or something like that, I'm at work and can't look it up), there's a section that asks for the bonus on your charm, as a percentage (0-200). Since you have 9/10 upgrades (you'll get your 10th as soon as you are flagged to enter time), the charm should be 90%.

As far as your adventurer's stone, divide the number of wins in each camp by 10 (drop any remainders), add the results up and multiply by 2 for the percentage on your adventurer stone. It's not based on the number of points you've earned, but the number of wins you have.

As far as gear goes, I can't check magelo from work, but I doubt I'd be able to help, as I'm nowhere near being EP flagged yet :( Best of luck, I hope that helps with getting your charm updated in your magelo.

As a general rule, I always figure that for MT'ing, hate generation of the weapons you're using >>>>>everything else. So, hate aug your best aggro generating weapon and tank with it.
It can make me wince to give up the "free" stats on a weapon to use something with more hate. But if your group/raid can ramp its damage up to take advantage of your additional aggro, the fight should be shorter and the damage you take ultimately less than the damage the stats would save you in a longer fight.
That's the theory, anyway.
