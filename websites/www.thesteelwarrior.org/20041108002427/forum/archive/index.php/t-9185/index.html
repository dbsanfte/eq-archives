Well first off, the only Alternate Abilities you lose from deleveling are the ones you have to activate, IE the ones that require buttons. You are not noticing any more flurries or crits than before, because you always had the same level of those aa's working, regardless of your level. Any GM will tell you this if you petition and ask them.
lokkak
Are you saying you leveled to 65, gained 90 AA and then deleveled to 51 to then start leveling back up to 65?

If so, yes you may find passive AA not working since the patch almost 3 months ago to install checks into the game. There were so many "level 65 with 100 + AA in passive abilities" killing nag/vox solo that I guess they needed to prevent this by adding checks to disable passive AA abilities that require levels that the character no longer has. What this means is that if you have LR5/ID5, but are currently level 52, then you don't have access to LR5/ID5/CA3/CS3/PE or any other purchased level 55, 60, or 65 abilities passive or activated.
