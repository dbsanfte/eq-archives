Ahz
Your gear is on par or maybe better than mine and I have no problems tanking in BoT. If your skills are typical, and if you have a problem tanking, then
1) you have unrealistic expectations of yourself. We are not Conan, able to slay mobs left and right. We are defensive tools that pin down the enemy until others kill them. I had unrealistic expectations of myself and it got me down. I finally realized it, and am doing better now. Or...
2) the problem lies in your groupmates. there is no amount of warrior skill or gear that can overcome an overnuking wizzie, chain stunning pally, or a bad puller.

AAs will help.

First, get Area Taunt. It is the best AA we get, though it's importance is somewhat reduced as of late. But when you get the two-pull, and you can get both mobs' attention long enough for a mez to land, you'll be a happy person. Chances are, no one will realize what you did :-D

After AT, keep cranking the defensive AAs even when it sucks to do so.

Also, if you have DPS in your group, don't be afraid to go defensive and conserve the healer's mana if things get a little tight.
