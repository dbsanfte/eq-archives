If you take your total haste, divide it by 2, you get the approx. amount of reduction on delay.

So if you have a 20% haste belt, and get a 40% haste spell, your actual weapon delay will be 30% less then normal.

The way most people calculate delay with haste, is to divide your weapon delay by 1.XX where XX is total % haste. And if you have spell/song/item and are at 100% haste, you would divide by 2.
