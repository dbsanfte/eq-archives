I've been tanking with A2 in offhand for a while now, and don't really have the desire to grind out more point to upgrade to a3. I very very rarely have aggro issues, and those only happen when someone else goes totally overboard dps/aggro, or if the RNG shats on me and I get no procs for 20-30 seconds.

On the other hand, I would rather have a yak aug in offhand than the anger2. Same aggro, but more dps, and there's also that whole discussion that stun aggro might be much higher depending on the level range of your target.

Still, A2 is decent for aggro, and as mentioned, upgrade to A3 will net you like 300 aggro per minute more. If you've got the points, or can get them easily, go for it. If not, no worries.
