The reason we train them out is because with the exception of a couple people training, everyone else is going full burn on Coirnav. Enchanters can nuke instead of worrying about CC. Warriors make good kiters, because they have discs for mitigating damage while kiting.

It's not so much hinging your entire raid on trying to kite out every last mob. It's just expending a little coordination just prior to to the last mini dying to get *as many* of the little adds around Coirnav out of the way as you can. And with a little preparation, it doesn't slow the raid down at all, since warriors are prepositioned ahead of the raid so there is no waiting on them to go in and grab mobs. Last mini dies, raid moves to Coirnav. By time raid gets there, warriors have already snatched up what they can and kiting them away.

But there's always a dozen ways to skin a cat. As long as he dies, your strategy is successful. :)
