Sassinak
I can answer a couple of your questions but not all...

The earth rings are basically always up. The only question is whether they end with loot dropping bosses or with lootless placeholder bosses. They produce loot once per 3 days. The only way to know whether they are in "loot mode" is to track other guilds' kills of the rings, or to have a guildmate running ShowEQ. Hypothetically, if a person was to use the latter method, then they would probably already be aware of how it shows the Earth rings, from having read the ShowEQ message boards.

As for easier targets, there are several mini-mini's in Air (mostly in the 4 buildings) that drop a single half decent non-mold item every time, and they are 3-groupable, but you will probably want 40 people in order to clear the long route to reach them. Hydrotha and Kriziik in water are two of the easiest mold droppers in the EP's in my opinion, watch for them.
