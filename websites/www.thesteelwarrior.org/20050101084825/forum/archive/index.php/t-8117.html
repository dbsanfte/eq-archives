07-17-2004, 05:13 AM
Stun without a level specified on them maxes at 55. For example Ykesha is stun 0.0 and it won't stun anything higher than 55 at all (easily verifiable).

Most bosses don't actually have stun immunity. They're just immune by the virtue of their levels. For example, at least half (if not all) of the Rathe Councilman can be stunned.

PCs do not gain immunity to stun/mez by the virtue of level. Also anything an NPC cast automatically ignores any level restrictions, which is why you can charm or mez any uber with a WTD enchanter (or for that matter, a necro) or a Doppleganger.
