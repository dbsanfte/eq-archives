You could make the zone out a moving object that followed some repeating path that slowly moves across the zone.

You could also have areas of zero gravity, low gravity, or high gravity, etc. where you would float or take huge falling damage or have your ability to jump drastically modified.

I also don't see why you couldn't have rivers or airstreams where if you enter them there is a force that pulls you along in a certain direction or spins you in a circle. Perhaps only people with SOW or run3 could move slowly against the flow and the rest would see these areas as effectively one way.

There could be portals or doorways where you are shrunk or grown as you pass through them or give you some kind of movement boost. There could be zones or areas where all colors are negative or black and white or everything has a red tint to it.

There are lots of things that could be done, a little imagination could really improve a lot of zones. The current game engine might not be able to handle these things as it is but perhaps some future expansions could extend the physics of EQ to be a little more interesting.
