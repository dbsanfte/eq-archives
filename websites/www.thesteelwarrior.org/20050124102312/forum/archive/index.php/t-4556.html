There are numerous posts about skill mods. You can expect it to have 'some' effect, but this effect will not be noticable to you in normal day to day fighting. Also, it will take hours worth of parsing to actually see the 0.5-1% increase in dodges that you gain.

That said, every bit helps, and if you get a 5% mod to parry, dodge, and riposte... you will be able to parse an overall damage avoidance increase of around 3%, which is easier to notice than 1% for sure...

Also note that skill mods of the same skill, do not stack.
