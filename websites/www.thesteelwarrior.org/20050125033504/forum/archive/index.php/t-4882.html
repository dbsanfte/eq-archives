If your LDON playtime is limited, I'd go angerII if you dual and angerIII if you use a 2hander. 2 x AngerII is 20 wins, at least 10 of them in Miraguls'; angerIII is 22 wins, all in Miraguls'. I have angerII on CHoS and on SotB and was doing pretty good on aggro even before incite.
