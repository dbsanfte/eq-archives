My tests show that since about 2 patches ago, being bubbled no longer generates rage while taking damage. This really isn't a big deal, because it amounts to about 10 rage (900 damage). Chain shielding might be a problem.

Priests should defintely use it to "heal around" mortal strike, and other healing impairing effects (veil of shadow comes to mind) if possible. Pre-shielding is good, as with mana regen, it is typically a "free" 900 hp. The only real time I run into a problem with it is if someone shields me immediately upon me getting aggro, it'll typically pull them if I haven't got a chance to hit anything yet.
