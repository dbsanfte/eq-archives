Aary- hits for 950ish, triples... aoe is a mana drain/dot, but its buggy sometimes (can't exactly explain, maybe someone can explain her aoe better)... best thing to do is position her around corner from ramp down to itak/eashan, casters on ramp, melees around corner. Pin her against wall near corner, and call ae... 30 seconds on the ae so once you call it, casters have 30 sec to come around corner and nuke a few times, then duck back. would be smart to position a group near casters to take care of the hatchlings that add during the fight. aary is definately the most annoying fight there, and shes tough.

Eashan is unslowable, hits like 650ish tops... gimpy aoe. IMO hes the best RvR mob in Ntov, because he drops a lot of decent stuff (Claws of Lightning, Fist of Lightning, Mask of the Sky, Cloak of the Sky, Twisted Steel Bastard Sword, Onyx chain sleeves), and hes quite easy, and can be killed without dealing with aary since hes before her. Itak is even easier than Eashan, but doesn't drop nearly as nice of stuff, but still has a few good items.

Overall, Eashan and Itak are good mobs, but if you cannot kill Aary yet then you shouldn't bother with NtoV, because there's quite a small limit to how much you can do just by killing the two of them, and the time to get to them alone isn't worth it.

Best way to get there is to have a mage in your guild stay non kos and run in, and coh people. On the down side, this mitigates the usefulness of this mage on the raid down to mod rods, since if they nuke even once they'll suck the faction hit that will quickly make em kos.

To clarify the Aary AE thing ...

It was already mentioned that you need to possition your caster out of the AE or they will end up OOM in no time. Even if your casters are around a corner, your UI is going to show that they got hit and their mana is going to drop as if they took the AE. This is just a ghost AE but it will keep them from casting because the mana check before casting a spell is done locally and your UI thinks you have no mana and a mana DoT AE on. All the casters need to do is click any insta-cast item to clear the ghost AE and restore their mana. Even j-boots work to clear the ghost. All casters should have at least 1 clicker to fix the AE or you are going to have a rough fight.
