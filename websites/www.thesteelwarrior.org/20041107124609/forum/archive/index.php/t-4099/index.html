Heals that are decent *when needed* but mana hogs, as they are now. When you look at this type of paladin and compare it to a warrior, its not as out of whack. Lack of undeserved buffs will help maintain a warriors damage absorption edge, while paladins can maintain some agro edge...creating a trade off in what a group wants for a tank...some kind of meaningful choice. This of course has to be coupled with itemization.

Now, you can scrap that. And decide you want to keep all the spells that promote an unequal competition for abilities...and suffer in other ways related to that. Thats just not my preference, so I didn't bother going into it.

This does not destroy a class. And it is also not the call for vengeance some warriors would love to see. You need balance though, in some form. It doesn't matter that the cleric can cast Word of Redemption and theoretically get over 50hp per point of mana spent, because the group isn't taking enough damage - when your group is fully healed and you can still chain-pull, you don't need any more healing.

I'm telling you that not just once, but on many occasions, my cleric hasn't cast a heal in a LDoN group because the group's paladin's healing was sufficient to do the job. If you don't believe that, then I can only suggest that you go and try it.

Yes, I'm calling for nerfs to paladin aggro, buffs, heals, and mitigation. All of these things.

The aggro should be reduced to a level where a paladin can generate significantly more taunt than a warrior - but not all the time. Hence, increasing the mana cost of stuns.

The buffs should be reduced to a level where clerics buff significantly more hp than paladins, and where paladin self-buffs don't place them near warrior hp.

The mitigation should be reduced to a level where a paladin who is stunning with sustainable frequency doesn't mitigate any better than a warrior who isn't stunning.

The heals should be reduced to a level where paladin healing is a useful backup to a group but does not take over the healer position in a group. Further, heals should cost paladins sufficient mana that they can't both heal and use stuns to hold aggro.

The group roles for a pally should be:-

* Group main tank. Tradeoff: less hp -v- able to hold aggro effectively in short bursts using stuns.
* Group minor damage dealer/backup tank/backup healer (swiss army knife). Tradeoff: mana cost of healing precludes using stuns to hold aggro at the same time.

The tradeoffs are what's missing, and therefore nerfs to all the aspects I've mentioned are necessary.
