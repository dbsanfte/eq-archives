I screwed up the final turn in on that quest also, not quite as bad as you did, but I thought for sure my ashes were lost at first.

I had done the fed-ex portion of the quest (everything up to and including spawning the Vah Shir in Tenebrous) on a lvl 25 druid. After I had finished the rest of the quest and had the ashes and the third item for the final turn in on my warrior, I handed in the medal of service (the third one that I got after handing the second one to the Vah Shir in TM) with my druid and got a response something to the effect of "Thanks, but I don't have any more work for you right now." I went ahead and handed in the 2 other pieces with my warrior, and got the standard "I need all 3 items before I can give you your reward." response twice, and no reward.

I re-did the whole fed-ex portion of the quest with my warrior, which includes handing several different items to the same npc as the final turn in. Somewhere around 3 hours after I had handed in the ashes and the other piece, I finally handed him the medal of service again and got my shield. Needless to say I was extremely relieved, and pretty surprised that it had worked.

So anyway, if an npc has multiple quests, it looks like they keep track of them seperately, and completing one quest won't affect a different quest that's already been half-MQ'd. I'm not sure if there's any kind of a timer, but I'd guess that there's not.

If your server hasn't had any down-time since you handed in the ashes, the npc just might still remember that someone had turned them in. In my opinion, it would be worth your effort to do the other parts of the quest again as quickly as you possibly can.
