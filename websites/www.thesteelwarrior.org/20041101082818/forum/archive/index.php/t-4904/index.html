Hyardgune
Work on all of your weapon skills. Yeah, use the 2h before 20. Don't worry about the damage cap, everything is pretty easy at low levels anyway so your dps doesn't matter all that much. Concentrate on getting fast weapons rather than high damage weapons like the Wurmy. Just rotate your weapons around and keep your skills at about the same level.

When I was levelling up, I used 2h as my main weapons but at the beginning of each level I dual-wielded to get my 1h skills up as well as stuff like double attack. That worked fine. I was kinda behind in 2hb but I brought that up in the 40s.

For weapons -
2hb - Sarnak Battle Hammer (30/40 war only, less than 100pp)
2hs - there's a bunch, go for one with a 40 or below delay
1hb - Jade Mace would likely be ok (9/18) drops in KC, probably fairly cheap these days.
1hp - I dunno, you can always get a walrus tooth or something and off hand it for the skillups
Bonequest
Since you have a 64 SK, you might ask some friends if they have a Horn of Hsagra(13/20) sitting in the bank that you could borrow for a piercing weapon. They drop like candy off of Tormax in Kael. Its one of the best weapons for a new warrior that I have seen (75 hp/ 35 ac/ 10 sta /25 resist cold), if you can find one.
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6093

Ketchata Koro Mis (7/30) is a good practice weapon for 2h blunt but don't use it unless you are just assisting since it has low damage.
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=479

Don't forget to practice archery, it helps to be able to hit the right target when you pull, especially at higher levels. Best way to do this is get a lot of arrows and go ask another group if you can shoot their mobs for practice while they fight. Just don't stand opposite any guards like I did at first...
