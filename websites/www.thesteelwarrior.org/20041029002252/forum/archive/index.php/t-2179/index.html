Pre-Ring information:
1) Set up camp on the bridge. Stay in the middle area. Buff up properly.
2) When everyone is ready, start the ring.
3) ONLY one monk goes into the ring. This is the Ring Trigger (RT)
4) RT stays standing until mobs spawn. RT feigns once mobs spawn.
4a) If the RT dies, a Backup Ring Trigger takes his place until the RT is back.
4b) RT stays grouped with a shaman to heal and a small enchanter team.
4c) The RT and BRT should use hotkeys to alert the guild to every SPAWN and SPLIT.
5) Once the ring is clear of mobs, the RT stands again and repeats.
6) Before every boss, and sometimes after as well, a CH/Gain Mana will cast on raid.
