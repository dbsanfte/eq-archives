The damage seems pretty obvious. Make a L 25 character on test, and do a few backswings and flurries, you'll notice they look a hell of alot like 5 and 10 damage weapon swings. You don't need no dev help to figure it out, trust me.

I haven't done enough head bashes yet to figure out what the 2nd number means. Possibly "damage bonus*10" (ie, 75 means a damage bonus of 7.5) as a wacky groundless theory.

The type of damage you do (ie, crush/slash/bash) is determined by the "skill type" of the "spell". So, flurry is crush, backswing is slash (for now). Ideally they would be tied to your weapon.

Personally, I wished they had tied it more to the weapon in your hand, but I can't figure out a way to do that and stay balanced.
