Well, the bad news is that the tank plays about the least important role in the trials. All you have to do is get in the way of the exe while the rest of the party beats the crap out of him.

All of the trials are basically about DPS. The more DPS your group can crank out, the better. Being that my main is a wizard, I am partial to the AE approach, but straight nuke the shit out of the target the MT has targeted works well too.

Here is what you need to do basically -
1. Get the casters all in the corner opposite the platform, opposite the side with the exit mob. Plant yourself between the casters and where the mobs spawn in the benches.

2. Sit down until the mobs spawn. This is a sure way to get all 4 mobs to jump you.

3. Target mobs fast, F8 is your friend. If you can, try to get the rock mobs first since they are not mezzable.

4. When the executioner spawns, you may be called on to kite him while the casters regen mana. If this is the case, make sure that no one has any pets up, the Exe has an AE that will agro pets and you don't want him summoning. Run him around the platform. I haven't done it since the pathing changes, but it should still be easy to kite him with run3.

IMO you are best off getting wizards or mages for DPS because they have burst damage that melees are going to have a hard time matching. You will need:

1. Tank - you
2. Healer - cleric is best, druid or shammy ok
3. Slower/crowd control - enchanter is best, bard will do
4. Wiz/Mage
5. Wiz/Mage
6. anyone

I have done the exe trial successfully at least a couple dozen times with that type group make up and it works very well.

***** DISCLAIMER ******
Except in the #6 wildcard spot, everyone needs to be 60+ and preferably 65. The higher your average level of your group, the easier the trials are in general.

I know alot of this has been said above already but in my opinion the best group non AE style is :

Warrior
Cleric
Chanter
Mage
Mage or wizard ( also have used rog/ranger)
any 55-56 or above character

Now in the above model you have a basic core group of 5 and room to sub in any one random class. My guild started doing this when the core group was about 62-63 and we did ok...now we are all 65 and the 5 of us alone can do the trials np... we have used the above combination to flag all of our members and alts in our guild (about 40 flagged).

Now as far as actual tanking strategy goes...

When we go in I sit in the middle of the benches i use a wide view so that i can see myself and all 4 spawn points then as soon as they pop i take a split second glance hit F9 (which changes me back to first person) then target the nearest rock and attack, if there are no rocks i target the nearest mob and go. The rest assist me and attack....as i am fighting i usually spin the mobs around so that i can see my next target clearly..watch your text box not the screen for the kill...you will see the yellow experience text before you see the mob fall over....as soon as you that text your next target should be in front of you if you spun correctly and you hit F8 and attack. repeat this process till all are dead. Then repeat for next waves. Also be SURE that all casters stay real close to you....you really dont want one of em to heal or nuke or break a mez and the mob takes off running after them...if they stay real close to you and they get aggro all that should happen is the mob spins around to them....tell them to stay put and you taunt like mad to get aggro back....whatever they do dont let your casters get far away from you.

Now if your group dps is good enough to make it to round 4....

as the last mob dies have the group move back towards the guard in the corner...then if you need to kite go up to the stairs as the last one dies and exec will aggro ya...run him around the stage if ya can...have someone call out if he stops or changes directions on ya.....don't hit him either or he will summon you and your kiting will be pointless as the cleric has to heal ya....the best class to kite is a chanter or a wiz with some aggro spell...just dont do dmg....anyway either you've kited him or you havent'....your about to engage him....just go up attack go defensive and taunt like mad...you shouldn't ever lose aggro on him...watch out at the end he will enrage so don't plow. Loot your mark and have fun...

OK i think thats it....if i missed something sorry....there are lots of group combos that can pass and strats that work...this just happens to be the one that my friends and I use.

Good Luck to you and if you fail keep trying you'll get there!
