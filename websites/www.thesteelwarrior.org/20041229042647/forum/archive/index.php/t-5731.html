One other thing to consider, which helped me choose which order to get the rest of my old AAs, as well as the new ones, is the fact that as your guild progresses, raid mobs tend to have bigger and harder to resist AEs. I am one of several similarly-equipped warriors in my guild, so we end up switching the tank responsibility around on raids. Because of these two facts, what I concluded was that extra HP will always be useful, while getting more procs is situational. Be it in exp groups, while tanking a raid, or just adding more dps at a raid, more hp is better. While I'm not tanking on a raid, and using my GSo4W for dps, and extra 50% procs (which is what WA5 seems to add, as posted in other threads) would be a minimal dps increase in the grand scheme of things.
