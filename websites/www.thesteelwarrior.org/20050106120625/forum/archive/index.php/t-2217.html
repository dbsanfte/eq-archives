Tough one. Magelo is down so I can't tell what your stamina is at.

For the most *direct* HP's, Siblisian and Putrid Belt of Rotten Hide would yield the most (115), and that would put you at 27% Haste.

SBC + Putrid = 22 AC, Sta 9, HP 115, 26% Haste.
SBC + RBG = 16 AC, Sta 17 , HP 75, 27% Haste.

RBG + CoGP = 19 AC, Sta 18, HP 75, 27% Haste.
RBG + Hiero = 17 AC, Sta 12, HP 100, 27% Haste.

If you can still benefit from stamina, then go with the RBG and Cloak of Greater Pernicity. (Suggestions are only from factoring in HP's and HP's from stamina, dunno if you're worried about the stats too.)
