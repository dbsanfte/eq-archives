I did this quest not too long ago. I suggest going down to the west end of the lake, and then just going north up into the hills from there; easier that way. Keep bearing to the left/west, and you'll run right into it. Clearly marked on this map: http://www.worldofwar.net/cartography/worldmap/redridge.php

As for the difficulty, it's ridiculously easy. The drunkeness is negligible, the mobs are easy, and you finish the cave objective as soon as you enter. Shouldn't be difficult for anyone to solo as soon as they're high enough to get the quest, and probably within ten minutes.
