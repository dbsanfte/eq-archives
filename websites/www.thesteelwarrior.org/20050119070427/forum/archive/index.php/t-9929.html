rule #1 : don't lvl to 70 now ! stop at lvl 65
reason : a lvl 70 war without 100AA sucks, and at lvl 70 there is fewer hunting zone, and you won't find any LDoN group at lvl 70. Much of lvl 65 zones are getting fewer people now so it's harder for LFG people, but it's a great time for you to hunt if you have friends, and if you don't it will give you some (hunting allways with the same people because there is noone else...).
rule #2 : you need aggro aug for tanking, since you have few time to spent, coose the Ykesha aug since they are cheaper than A3 and they are comparable aggro (a bit less of course but still very ok). you will need enougth points for 2 (ideally 3) ykesha slot 4 then reimbourse them and buy back 2 (ideally 3) type 8 when you will use OoW and GoD weapons. Thus bank LDoN points now that you can hunt there because after it will more difficult. So in summary, LDoN are a forced zone for us because it's the only way to get aggro aug. But all the other aug (HP, AC, stats, ...) can drop in GoD and OoW (they are even better on hard mob), so if you don't like LDoN just get your ykesha and run away.

rule #3 : post your magelo, to do so easily past the link line of the web page of your magelo directly in the post, like this http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?p=152633#post152633

rule #4 : have fun ! it's more important than lvling when you don't have 35H+ to spent on EQ !

rule #5 : OoW is great for exp, loot and fun but remember that you need 50AA before getting lvl 66 (you can do without but it won't be fun).
