Your current agro weapons are good, and will last you until you can upgrade via raiding. If you aren't always tanking, then yes a non-agro 2H weapon would be a good idea. I'd recommend War Marshal's Bladed Staff. They're usually around the 10k mark or so on most servers, it seems.

For possible armour upgrades; another Valorium ring would be nice. Also on my list would be to replace your bracelet of fortitude. Ornate would be nice there, and the molds seem to be coming down in price lately. As well, you can camp ornate in BoT with your friends; a pair of bracers, and the arms would be nice, and the helm would be very nice. Overall, your gear is pretty solid. Concentrate on HP/AC first. Some ppl will tell you that HP>>>> AC all the time, but that opinion varies widely. For myself, I use the rule of thumb that 1 AC equals about 5-8 HP or so. Also, gear with more sta would be nice, for the extra HP. Saves are also really useful, imo. More dex on armour sure can't hurt, but the effect of it is fairly minor. However, it does all add up over time.

Also, do lots of LDoN adventures if you can. The anger proc augs you can buy will make a HUGE difference in how well you hold agro, and as well, there is some very nice armour available from the adventure point merchants; Malevolent gear from Miragul's, and the Royal Attendant armour from the N Ro camp.
