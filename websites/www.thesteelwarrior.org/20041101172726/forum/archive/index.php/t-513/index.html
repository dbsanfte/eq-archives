With that group,

Goto Allak and read up on which mobs drop which keys.
Fight your way up to the top floor of ToFS. The first 2-3 floors will be green to light blue. (sorry but its a key zone)

set em up and knock em down.

If your Lucky someone will come up help you take her out
if your REAL lucky a high lvl group who is praying for her robe to drop will let you loot her master Key.
thats your goal... get the master key so you can just zoom right on up. you only need one key per group.
The top floor will keep you in decent exp (not as great PoP) they should start turning light blue around 55. Then you can go back to lvl 6b and kill bats, and the named maybe try to take out Tserrina then.
Respawn rates are about 10-15 minutes.
I went from 49-53 there on top floor then moved to the older zones that are now vacant. Seb, chardok, etc are baren most of the time on CT.

You may run into some crowds on the lower floors...but for the most there should not be many (if any) in zone especially on the 4-6th floors. Peops may camp the wedding area. but theres plenty of Mobs around.

ToFS is a one of the best designed and thought out zone IMHO.
