TM / Strikethrough:
They give you the ability to attack and hit the MOB even though they dodge, parry, block, riposte. However, in the case of riposte the MOB still performs this action and still hits or misses based upon their own roll.

In other words:
YES, YOU STILL GET HIT BY RIPOSTE WITH TM / STRIKETHROUGH. IT DOES NOT NEGATE RIPOSTE.
