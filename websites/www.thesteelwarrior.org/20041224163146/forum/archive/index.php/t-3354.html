CH rotations, main tanking (esp using defensive for tanking as opposed to using it to flea), and knight aggro abilities may not have been 'envisioned' originally but it is hard to argue from the encounters of the past 2 years that such is not the roughly desinged case. Encounters such as Creator and Emp in Ssra were explicitly designed to relay on no to low damage ATing that was done by knights, this is nothign new.

The fact that our warriors die far more frequenly when their disc is down that: A. the disc is important, and that B. we have yet to trivialize the encounter (by numbers, gear, or skill) to the point where it is only a small factor.

Just a voice to counter some of the piling on of Paladin's healing abilities.

Paladins are not 'nearly as good as clerics' in the healing department. Our heals are neither as large, nor as efficient as Cleric spells. Significantly less efficient. Verant envisions us as 'emergency healers'. When things get really tough, we can pour out mana to do some decent healing. This is a powerful ability, but we are nowhere near the priest classes in the ability to provide sustained healing like an experience group needs to function.

Paladins are good substitute HP buffers. I know that Paladins can buff more total HP than Clerics, but remember only one of those buffs will be used if a Cleric is around. The biggest upshot of this is that when in a group with no Cleric, a Paladin can buff himself up to nearly within 300 HP of what could be done with a cleric there. The Paladin could also buff up a Warrior or SK if they were in the group. Other classes would prefer the Druid's PO9 to the full suite of Paladin buffs, although they would take the Paladin's inferior symbol to go along with it.

While a Paladin can replace a Warrior in many situations now, they cannot replace a Cleric or any other 'real' healer. They do make it much easier for a group to work with a non-Cleric healer though.

None of this is to say that Warriors don't need help, or that Paladins aren't getting more than their share of experience groups these days. It just seems that every single Paladin ability is under attack lately. It isn't reasonable to expect a nerf to every Paladin ability and an increase to Warrior aggro generation. I know that the majority of folks aren't actively pushing for Paladin nerfs, but the crescendo seems to be building.
