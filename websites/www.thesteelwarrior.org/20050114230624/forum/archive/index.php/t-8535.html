Well, there is some gear the VT group could get for you. Droppable items in LDoN hard missions come right to mind, the gloves and boots. It is also possible you could get the Boots of Storm. Once you leveled to 60 you could actually do the missions and get the earring, cloak, rings, neck, etc...tho maybe you can do them earlier.

If the group was really solid, maybe they could drag you along if they did Vxed or Tipt and get you the earring and bracer, tho Im not sure if there is a level restriction.

Other items that come to mind is the neck from Veksar, the neck from any dragon in WW, the Flawless Steel mask in Sirens Grotto, the chain mask from CT (I forget the name) , Heavy Rubicite Girdle from CT...etc...

Lots of choices if you dont mind gear from the Velious Era.

One other thing, I hope the Shield is in your magelo just for eye candy. You wont hold agro very well with it if you are in an exp group. To solve that problem you could get the Copper Hammer of Striking or Sword of the Bloodsworn.
