Phantron
PoP has to have better XP because it's actually balanced around the AAs. In Luclin if you're a warrior and you have ND3 and AE Taunt you're pretty much fine. Offense do not matter on fights that take forever and not even defense matters very much when you consider you can get through 2 rounds of max hits without any heals. Take Emperor's 900ish melee, 2 rounds at max is 7200 which is a number reachable at Luclin. Compare this to PoP where it'd be unthinkable anyone can survive 2 rounds of max hits on anything that does a nontrivial amount of damage. This also makes every bit of DPS count because it is nontrivial to prolong a battle. Whereas in Luclin you don't really feel the difference between a high AA person and low/none AA person because it's pretty much impossible to lose a battle once you know what to expect unless there are serious lapses in healing, in PoP everyone does have to pull their weight somewhat.
