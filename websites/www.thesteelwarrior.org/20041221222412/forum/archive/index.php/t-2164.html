there is a lot of threads on this forum explaining how damage is calculated. I won't comment here
why a ranger damage is more constant : rangers usually uses 2 fast hitting, low damage weapons, so the max hit is low too, and the result is the damage seems more consistent, when it is not.
I as a ranger can hit everywhere between 1 and 80. Not really consistant.
U will always hit for anything between 1 ( or DB+weapon damage/10 if u are using 2h weapon) and your max hit.
What will change is the chance to hit for those values ... a non skilled war will often hit for min, and nearby never for max. a max-skilled war will hit a bit for every value between min and max (i won't explain damage, repartition, there is nice graphics for that on other threads). what is important is the average damage, and more specifically teh damage per second (DPS). What will influence your chances to hit for max instead of min is the mob AC, and your ATK. this number is calculated based on skills ( offence + weapon related skill : slashing for example), on stat ( str for melee ) and on +atk items, more some miscelanious things ( rangers get +5 atk per level in teh end game).
