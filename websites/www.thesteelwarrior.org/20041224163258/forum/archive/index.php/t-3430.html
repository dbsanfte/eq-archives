Open your combat skills window and rt click on a button to bring up a list of skills. Bash and slam are listed separately. To bash w/o a shield you need a key saying slam. Note that you cannot slam under the effects of most illusions including share wolf form, boon of the garou, trickster's augmentation and night's dark terror. Even that pesky necro dd that turns you into a skellie blocks slam. Been that way as long as I've been playing and I started just before SoV was released.
