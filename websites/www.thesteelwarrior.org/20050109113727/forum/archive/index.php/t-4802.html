arf arf

Upkeep is the Endurance over time that you spend on a disc. So, while defensive, your endurance bar keeps going down as, each second, defensive "costs" what its upkeep value is (which I don't remember).

60 seconds of defensive costs 1/3 of what 180 seconds of defensive costs. Some of our discs have a burst cost, some have upkeep costs, and (I think) some have a mix of both endurance pool drains. To put it another way, something with an upkeep cost of 100 will cost 100 Endurance after 1 second, 200 End. after 2 seconds, 300 after 3 seconds, and so on.
