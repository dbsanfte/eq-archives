Haass
I'm not XP'ing in BOT, nor do I plan to be in the forseeable future, but I can tell ya what I've seen.

In my favorite dungeon, a 4-some with constant pulls gave me an AA in just under 2 hours. I took my 2 toons to my duo spot today and altho it's nowhere near the XP rate I get grouped, it's not bad. It's definitely better than it used to be.

It's my contention that 2 boxing will yield better results than pre-patch, however if you have the option between getting a group and 2 boxing, get a group man, the XP freakin flies with 4 or more people and no downtime. If you find yourself having difficulty getting groups for whatever reason, then I think 2 boxing is a viable solution to that problem, but it's nowhere near the XP rate a full group could rake in, nor should it be. 2 boxing should be what you do when there's no one else online and you just wanna finish up a few blues here or there.
