Piercing is maxed at 240 at lvl 60.. yes we all knew that.

it stops at 200 at 42 then starts going up at 5 points a lvl again when you hit 51.

but for some reason the last few lvls till 60 (i think around 54-55) it slows to 4 points a lvl till it maxes at 240.

There is a general formula where you take the difference in the 2 cap levels (240-200= 40 points in piercing) and divide it by 9 (number of levels from 51 to 60)... so..

40 / 9 = 4.4 skillups / level

The number gets rounded, but remains more or less even (+/-1) till the end cap limit.

The formula may be a lil off but generally correct.
