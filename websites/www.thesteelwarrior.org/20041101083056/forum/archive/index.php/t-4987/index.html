In order:

Replace the SBC with a CoF until you get a 41% haste item.

Get an Anger III aug for the BoC

Finish your mitigation and advoidance AA's

When you get a 41% haste item, sell the CoF and get a Steel Ring of Earthen Resistance.

Replace the boots.

Replace your offhand weapon.

Get a second Steel Ring of Earthen Resistance.

Replace your gauntlets.

Get a better mask. (One of the Zeks drops one I think)

Get a BoW or Bloodfrenzy. Aug them.

Get a Stonewood Compound Bow when the price drops a lot.

Get the Aten Ha Ra gloves.

That ought to hold ya for a bit. Good Hunting.
