Hulanta
I am a Tier 4 warrior that raid buffed hits about 1890 AC and 9800 HPs with Focus of the Seventh.

Focus of the Seventh:
Increase HP when cast by 544
Increase STR by 75
Increase DEX by 70

I just got a Kizrak's BP (weird to have Raex in all Raex slots but BP and Legs and then UPGRADE your BP with a Tier 3-style BP) and can cast Shield of the Arcane on myself but would lose Focus of the Seventh. I don't need the Strength. I could get a Dex buff and fill up one extra slot for the small amount of extra dex I would need to get me to 305.

Shield of the Arcane:
Increase Max Hitpoints by 300
Increase AC by 36
Increase Magic Resist by 30

With Shield of the Arcane instead of Focus of the Seventh I get:

-244 HPs
+36 AC
+30 MR

Which would put me at about 9550 HPs, 1920 AC and put my MR over 300.

Is this a decent trade off for MTing? For MAing? For SAing? For exp group?

Just wanting some feedback on this. Obviously this is a big win for an enchanter but no shaman group.
