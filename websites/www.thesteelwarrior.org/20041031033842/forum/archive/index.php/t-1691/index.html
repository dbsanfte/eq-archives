wandor
I know what you are trying to say Ainian, but you are thinking about stacking issues and how spell effects overwrite other effects. In this case there is no stacking issue since the stun does not leave an icon or overwrite any effects. When they changed stuns, they made it so that spells with a stun + an effect where treated as 2 seperate items. Before the change, if the stun was resisted, then the whole spell was automatically resisted. This is no longer the case. Now if the stun portion of the spell is resisted, the other components get their own resist check so they are unaffected. It works the same with stun immunity. The effects each have their own resist check so you will still be landing the DD and the Hate portions of the proc.
