The two biggest HP bumps I believe you could possibly get for 5k would be...

1) Clattering Bone Necklace for your neck slot.

2) Skyshrine (Myrmidon) Gauntlets MQ for your hands.

The Gaunts alone will add 90hps. The Clattering Bone Neck I believe will add 50+

If you have money left over, get a 65 hp ring. That will add 33 more hps in that slot.

I believe all of the earring upgrades and buyable Arm stuff will mostly cost more than that.

See if you can get the SS gaunts and Arms at the same time.
