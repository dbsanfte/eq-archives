IMO really depends on what you are looking to acomplish with your two box combo. If you are looking for the best duo combo, best combo for groups, or the best for a raid. Then you also have ease of use. Few factors you must concider:

Your own skill level of being able to two-box is a critical factor. If your out for soloing, do you want to be able to slaughter the little mobs faster or be able to take down the bigger tougher mobs.

Personally I believe for a duo combination a necro/shaman would make a very powerful combination because: 1. Both have the highest mana regen rates of any class in the game. 2. Abilities compliment each other really well, necros get a strong pet, shaman's get slow and some very good buffs to compliment it. 3. Both are concidered towards the top of the list on soloing, depending on the situation sometimes the best.

If you are talking about a group combination, I would say shaman or chanter/cleric, shaman or chanter/tank, or cleric/tank. Every group needs 3 key classes to get started, a slower, a tank, and a healer. After that you just fill in the gaps. Starting off w/ 2 of 3 makes that much easier to get a group started somewhere.

For raids really depends on your guilds current situation and what they need. A cleric/wizard might be extremely powerful in a raid, or a cleric/shaman depending on what your guild currently has.
