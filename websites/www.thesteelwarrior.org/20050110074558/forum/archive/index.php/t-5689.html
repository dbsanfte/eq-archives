Ok first off to acquire a trial you must speak to the High Priest in Barundi. If you have never talked to him or hailed, hailing him 2x will give you the first trial. These trials are not timed based for the most part and you can swap out people in your group during the trial if people have to leave.

Hailing the High Priest he will give the person who has hailed the lowest trial he has not completed yet. Say you have never done a trial, you will get trial 1. If you have done trial one and 4, you will get trial 2. If you have done anyone one other than 1, you will get 1.

You will not recieve a Real Flag unless you do them in order. If you happen to do them out of order you will recieve temp flags. Make sure your entire party is in the zone at completion or they will not recieve a flag.

And one final hint having a rogue with you on all trials makes them 100x easier. There is respawn during these trials.

Trial #1 - Purifying Plant

This trial involves clear or invis/ivu running to Anashu. Once you get to Anashu the leader of the expedition hails. This should spawn 8 Aged Stonemites. Head ivu/invis/clear to these mobs(in stonemite room if you have map).

Trash mobs on this trial tend to hit for 1200max and the golems are unslowable.

The Aged Stonemites must be killed to spawn the final boss. The Aged Stonemites hit triple 1200 or so and ae rampage. Fight them in one of the corner in the room where they spawn left of stairs is where we did. You can only cast on them from above water so, but good thing is you can melee them from above water so best way is to lev above the water and fight them. The AE rampage range is small so keep casters away and cleric especially.

After killing these 8 Ancient Kyosops (Spelling?) spawns and will autopull to your group so hold off killing last Aged till ready. The Ancient Quads 1400 and flurries only hits 981 on defensive war. From what I remember they are all slowable. Once he dies your done and recieve flag.

If you die, as long as one person stays in zone the expedition won't reset(not sure on time but happened last night). The Aged Stonemites will not respawn so invis/ivu/run etc to them for cr etc till they are dead. Dunno what happens if you wipe on Ancient.

Trial #2 - The Crematorium

Ok this one you kill random junk trash mobs till you recieve 4 different remains(Yogundi, Mabiki, Nyogi, and Taloki). Make sure if you have Rogue with you he collects these or else you have to race at the end.

Trash mobs on this trial start off hitting 500-600max and towards end are a little more resistant and hit upwards of 1k.

Once you have these 4 pieces you head to furnace area if you have map where the ovens are. Have the rogue run out into middle of oven area to spawn the 4 Ghosts. These ghosts are cake they can be single pulles by me of all people and hit for around 900 and cast random junk.

Each time a Ghosts dies a Will-o-wisp spawns DO NOT KILL THESE. These will start travelling upon each ghosts death to the Gem room on the map. Once last ghost dies you have to race these back to the gem room or if you are smart and brought rogue its cake. The Named ghosts depsawns fairly quickly so you have to move fast good thing rogue can out move the will-o-wisps.

Once these arrive in gem room they will coalesce into a named ghost. Turn in the 4 remains to him bam your done.

Trial #3 - ??? I forget ???

Ok if you do not have rogue on this trial you are in for a world of hate, and a long ass trial. Before entering this trial the HP will give you a seal to use. Rogue is only person required to get it and you can recieve it again if you quit during. Does not have to be a rogue but like I said if you dont your in a world of hurt.

If you do not have a rogue clear to last room on the map and talk to NPC there. He will tell you you need 3 Shaping tools which can be gotten from the Coccoon room(SE Bottom room), Wheel Room(SW Bottom Room) and Entrance of this Dungeon(Middle Bottom Room). These are not labeled on Map but easily distinguishable. This NPC is hard to see he is under debris trapped.

Once you do the turn in the 218 or so mobs in the zone despawn to about 1/4 of what they were before. Kill mobs in each room till you see emote that you got a tool then move to next room.

Once you have all 3 tools send rogue or if you like pain clear run to final NPC and hail. Then say something along the line of I have your Shaping tools. Blam your done got flag evac clear or whatever out.

Trial #4 - it was 5 am I totally forget

Ok Named npc spawns about 3 rooms in go and hail him he will tell you he needs 4 map pieces. Upon talking to him a named Slime Cube spawns in the very last room on the map(go figure). Head clear/invis/ivu or whatever to this room. Kill the cube pretty cake hits 1k or so.

Upon killing this slime cube 6 more cubes spawn through out the dungeon, funny thing noon in the room you are currently in. Head back killing parts of slime cube on the way till you loot 4 map pieces. Once you have 4 map pieces (remember rogue loot) turn them in to npc blam your done.
