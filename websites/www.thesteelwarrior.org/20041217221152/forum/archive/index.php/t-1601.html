I would personally go with the fungi. unless you really are going to have that cleric with you a lot, then you wont really need it too badly. You will indeed need a haste item, SCHW, and FBSS are cheap, if you want a little more haste and dont mind blowing some of your cash go for the RBG, or a Silvery belt of contention.

To save some cash on weapons look at mainly Damage Delay. Ignore the proccing weapons because you wont be proccing most of them until you hit the high 40's low 50's (dont most kunark weapons not proc till 51? trunchean, bloodpoint etc). Good Damage delay for cheap are weapons like sebillite croaking dirk (10/18 for around 4-5k) Lamentation/defiance or the velium blades out of DN that have just dam/delay and no stats and sell for very low prices (you can get an 11/21 for like a K).

2hs Hands fricken down, Buy a REAVER. These damn things are 40/40 with saves and NO LEVEL RESTRICTION. They go for about 5k on my server because they used to be non magical. well they were made magical and apparently noone has caught on and they still sell for half the price of inferior damage delay 2handers.
For 2hb, get a velium brawl stick they come in various damage/delays but all of em are more than adequate to twink out with and once again you can equip it at level 1.

Armor wise, dead on with the Crusty armor. Very inexpensive and good stats. get some 6/65's for rings (later on you will want valorium rings of gallantry), grab a couple silver chitin bracers if you have 10k to spend otherwise just grab some bracers with Good AC. For your backslot (unless you blow some cash on a seb beserker cloak as your haste) pickup something with some good ac like a seahorse cloak or a cloak of pernicity (little expensive but has some really nice stats). There are not that many good droppable neck items out there to buy, I usually just go with spider fur collars on most twinks, you might be able to find something slightly better for a lot more platt.


More or less a lot of the stuff you will be able to get is dependent on that big fungi purchase. If you get one, you will need to keep it low on the rest of your stuff. If you dont get one you will definitely need that cleric partner otherwise your going to go nuts waiting to heal. An iskar ceremonial BP is a good compromise for only 6k or so if you want something for the times you dont have the cleric on and still be able to recover quicker (just buy lots of bandages or one of those gnome bandage makers, dont get the dumb skinning knife, youll find yourself taking more damage than its worth trying to get the damn thing to proc).
If you dont buy the fungi you will be able to buy a nice fat haste item with stats and maybe that swiftblade.

Really it is very inexpensive to twink out a melee if you know what to look for. AC is king, and you dont want anything that is too slow weaponwise so you can keep those stats maxed. Especially till you hit your 30's and the damage caps on your weapons start going away.

I just made a ranger twink. He uses a wavecrasher and an ornate sword of the general. These would also make good pre-55 warrior weapons. I soloed very easily from level 1 to 31 using these (about 3 days played).

Wavecrasher is 9/19, has a 20 percent slow, has a 200 DoT, and procs at level 1. Costs under 15k on my server. Main hand only. The DoT is a death sentence for most mobs under level 10 and the slow helps a lot. This sword is good to level 55 or so and helps hold agro.

Ornate sword of the general is a no drop 10/18 sword that drops off a level 55 unslowable mob in Echo. A solid group of level 60+ can kill him in a few minutes. Use a warrior as tank and have him/her use defensive and its a relatively easy fight as long as you split him off from adds. Very good off hand weapon to go with a wavecrasher until you can get better.

A velium black saphire neclace has 65 hp and is around 750 plat. It is a lot of hit points for the amount it costs and it is pretty easy to find them in the bazaar since they are player made.

You might also want to consider multiquesting some Champion or Myrmidon armor, especially the Myrmidon gauntlets which have 90 hit points on them. You need ally faction but you can get ally to Coldain and Claws of Veshan in a few days while high levels kill giants for faction at the fort in GD--just take lots of arrows and practice archery from a safe distance.
