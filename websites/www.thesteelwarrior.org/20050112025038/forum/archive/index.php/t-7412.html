Before you get all defensive, keep in mind that I am not trying to be an ass. I am going on what we know to be true about how game works after hundreds of hours of parsing and discussion on these boards.

You really should go parse on the Katta banker to get a real parse. Anyone can get a parse with a high proc rate with a little luck. But a few nights later you will get a parse with an extremely low proc rate that will bring down the overall average down to what we expect to see. With your set up over the long run you are going to see around 9 ppm. We can do some simple math to see how the procs are going to stack up -

Scenario 1 - 2 X SoD+Force of Ykesha
Main hand
Anarchy 3ppm = 288x3 = 864
Ykesha 3ppm = 125x3 = 375
Offhand
Anarch 1.5ppm = 288x1.5 = 432
Ykesha 1.5ppm = 125x1.5 = 187

Total proc damage = 1858 per minute or 31 DPS from procs
9 stuns of agro = approx 3600 hate per minute

Scenario 2 - BoC+Force of Ykesha/GBoS+Force of Ykesha
Mainhand
Enraging blow 3ppm = 0 damage, 2100 hate
Ykesha 3ppm = 125x3 = 375
Offhand
Force Shock 1.5ppm = 179x1.5 = 269
Ykesha 1.5ppm = 125x1.5 = 187

Total proc damage = 831 per minute or 14 DPS from procs
6 stuns of agro = approx 2400 hate per minute
3 EB of agro = 2100 hpm
Total hate = 4500 hpm

Scenario 3 - GBoS+Force of Ykesha/BoC+Force of Ykesha
Mainhand
Force Shock 3ppm = 179x3 = 537
Ykesha 3ppm = 125x3 = 375
Offhand
Enraging blow 1.5ppm = 0 damage, 1250 hate
Ykesha 1.5ppm = 125x1.5 = 187

Total proc damage = 1099 per minute or 18 DPS from procs
7.5 stuns of agro = approx 3000 hate per minute
1.5 EB of agro = 1250 hpm
Total hate = 4250 hpm

So basically depending on whether you put BoC or GBoS mainhand, you would have to overcome between 13 and 17 DPS in procs. The question is now is 14/18+15/20 going to generate more than 13-17 DPS. Without parsing I would make an educated guess that it will be pretty close, with the slight advantage to the BoC+GBoS.

Since DPS will likely be very close, you would have to decide between 100hp 100end 3600 hpm vs. 185hp 85end 4250-4500 hpm. You can do what ever you want, but you came here asking for help. Sounds like you had your mind made up before you even posted.
