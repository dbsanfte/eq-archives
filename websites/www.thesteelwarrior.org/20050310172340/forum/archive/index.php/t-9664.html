Given the conditions of soloing and "something different" from a warrior I think the necro option is probably the most powerful at the moment. The beast option is good if by "different" you mean "melee is still okay" and by "when I can't get a group" you mean "I'd still like to keep grouping options open with this alt" -- beasts are a very handy and powerful class at the moment.
I don't see so many druids coming up through the ranks. Perhaps I hang out with the wrong crowd, heh, but I have a feeling that over the last few years druids have taken a few power hits relative to other classes. Yet I know a druid who has been playing since early 2000 and she's very good indeed at playing the class. It's truly an eye-opening experience to go from grouping with her to grouping with an "average" druid.
One of the signal aspects of warriors is that they might well have the largest gap between "ability to generate plat" and "need for expensive gear to play well." If I were going to start (yet another) alt, I might consider what class has the narrowest gap between "need for plat" and "ability to generate plat," at least pre-65. I'm not sure what that class is, though.
