5k unbuffed at your current lvl would be a stretch. I was doing BoT with 4500 and never had a problem. One suggestion would be to get 65 before you go all out defensive AA's. The benefits of being 65 out weigh lvl 62 with full defensive. You also won't get many groups in BoT at 62 even though you could do it easily. People just don't like lower lvls.

What you could do is hang out in PoV for a while. Once I hit 60, I stopped and got myself ND3 because at the time, my HP were lacking. And PoV is not bad exp either. I'm not saying you should get ND3 right now, but it is a path that may help you out a bit. The better choice IMO would be grind out to 65 and then check your HP. You get a nice HP boost in that range I believe. Your gear looks on par for a nonraid guild.

Change up you magelo a bit. Make yourself 65 and give yourself ND3 and PE. I would be willing to bet you would be happy with the results. Just a matter of getting the lvls and a few important AA, but you are in the same boat I was. Your AC is right where it should be. If you can break 1200 then you are doing good. 1300 is a bit harder untill you start getting more ornate.
