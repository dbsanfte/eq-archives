Leadership xp is not some fixed pool that diverts 20% of your XP in. Otherwise people at level 35 should never even see that bar move. What it does is that it takes away 20% XP, and then computes the leadership xp you gained by some other criteria that has nothing to do with how much total XP you're getting.

Raid is probably the best time to get leadership XP. It is surprisingly high and you lose the least amount of XP that way.
