no easy way to do this step in the ladder, you either trash your faction and move to boss mob encounters, or you stay in HoT forever, and watch all your guildmates move on to guilds that dont care about faction, and actually progress.

after we finish gearing up at HoT

Pretty much like is said here you want to equip and gear up for NToV then VT.

Here is some of the things the guild my old main did between HoT and VT (well we fell apart before VT cause too much stress was put into getting Emp keys in short time)

Farm UP raid names for needed items if any.
Started with easy mobs who drop level 60 spells(BoA, Torpor, KEI, Focus) (TVD, maybe Trak, The Burrower Beast) You can prob buy these spells now a days without breaking the bank but the practice of mobilizing for names is nice.
Our guild did this before PoP so maybe, depending on your level, you can farm BoT names for runes.
Get healers the Spell Haste and IH IV items from PoI, Prototype somethings I think. If you can kill Vindi you can do these imo. The drop allot and are really nice stat wise too. We did this later but there mobs were a joke by then. If you can do Vindi I think you can do these.
Start hitting easier ST key mobs (Zland, Kland) Gettin all the keys you need will be take a while.
Also maybe wanna farm WW. Dragonbane is nice for ToV and ST keys mobs.
Lord Vyrmm(sp?) should be doable and gives your monks/BLs nice upgrades.
Also look for easy FT items, Cazic Thule, Burrower in AC. Maybe there is some other easy ones that have come out in last 5 ot 6 months too.
Dain is nice too. Just about everyone can use that belt.

Important thing here is allot of mobs drop specialized loot, need to keep everyone happy so hit allot of targets.

While you are doing all of this push your people to go to Ssra on their own and farm for emp keys and other places to farm for VT shards. This process could take a while and more time spent on it here is less time spent when you need to hit Emp cause you are ready.

Once you have upgraded/levled/aapts some start going for Yeli (has ST key and quest drop for ubah haste item) , Sont (ST key + spells), King Tormax(St key + quest item with $$ stats), and statue (St key + nice melee stuff) Lendiniara the Keeper.

This is about the time you start doing Aary and the rest of NToV names as well. Also other ToV names drop nice dps stuff for hybrids.

Once you get 2-3 groups (assuming close to 65) ST keyed start farming the 1st two ST names for Primals.

Also kill mobs who drop RGC you will need it allot soon and prob should need it now and then already.

After a few month of all of this, start focusing on Ssra names and geting people keyed for Emp for VT keys.

Depending on how hard core your guild is you will be doing VT in less than a year.

Also you will need to read up allot about all of these phases. From here on out it is not about just healing and doing damage but allot of strat(MT details, phantom effects, AE avoidance, use of AA skills ect) as well, well 95% of it atleast.


Saying what mobs your guild is for is really only a question your raid leaders know.

Some mobs need strong MAs more(King Tormax, Statue) some need ubah DPS (Sont) some are all about positioning (Aary)

One of the things you noticed is you are going from CoV faction to KoS to all. It happens. You want to keep a mage on CoV faction tho. For CoHs to ToV mobs and eventually CoH to Yeli. If you can get CoH bish then planting one in deep NToV then later deep ST would be nice.

I used allot of abrevialtions cause I am lazy so feel free to ask what they meant.

edit - all this took my guild about 6 or 7 months raiding 4-6 nights a week. So do not expect to do it all in a month or to be able to do some of the mobs on the list in a month. It is a progression.
