Zanakus
IMO Torment is the most underrated exping plane in the game. Mobs there are tough (18khp to 25khp on non-named, and they hit occasionally in 400s) but the experience is simply phenomenal. At 56 I was getting a blue tick per kill, compared to the 1/3 of a blue tick I'd get in Innovation and the 2/3 of a blue tick I'd get in Lxanvom. Also, while the mobs hit hard they don't damage spike like the puslings in Lxanvom - I was the tank in group at level 56 and didn't have much trouble staying alive when mobs were slowed. Bring a good group to Torment and you'll be pleasantly surprised, I assure you.
