Dobbo, you're not telling me anything I haven't heard before, a couple of dozen times at least. First off, the problem is not knights stealing aggro. The problem is, for the most part, the slower getting the crap beat out of them on a regular basis. Doesn't your group slower take aggro regularly? I bet they do. Does taunt always work on the first or second try? I seriously doubt that it does. Does your slower die once in a while because the combination of resisted slows, failed taunts, and mis-timed heals? I'd bet serious money that they do. Do you realize that every time your slower dies in this kind of situation the chances are that a knight wouldn't have had the same thing happen? Do you realize how much more stress and effort is involved with the healer having to not only worry about timing your complete heal correctly given the massive damage of PoP mobs but also worrying about getting fast heals on the slower in time to save them? Do you think either the slower or the healer are really kean about this arrangement? Do you really think it's one they will jump into if they can easily avoid it? If you do, why? Why on god's green earth would a slower and healer, looking for a tank, say let's get the guy who is going cause us more stress and grief and possibly less experience in the long run over the guy who can make our lives easier?

No one claims that Warriors can't tank. The point is that while we can tank, using a Knight at this time is just a hell of a lot more fun for the people who ususally make that decision. Isn't the game about entertainment? Having fun? Is sitting at the zone line being passed over for a Knight with worse gear and fewer AAXP than you because they have an inherent ability to keep the mobs off the slower and/or healer really your idea of a good time?

No one guarantees us that we'll be able to find a pick up group when we log on. If most guilds are like the guilds I've been in, your guild doesn't guarantee you a slot either. If we're less fun to group with than another tank class, people will ditch us and take the other tank class. Right now, using a Warrior tank, with the possible exception of Warriors who have the best aggro weapons in the game, really isn't very much fun for either the slower or the healer in PoP. And the other classes are finding their lives are easier with a Knight tank as well, which is influencing who they prefer. What does this add up to you? Can you do the math on it?

So before you tell people to shutup about it, consider this. We all spend our $13. We've all put in massive amounts of time and energy into leveling and aquiring gear. And at this time one group of players is receiving more entertainment value for their $13 a month over us because of a flaw in the game. We're no less worthy. We've jumped through the hoops just the same as everyone else. You might even argue that we've jumped through more hoops because our character type is gear dependent to the extreme, even beyond the hybrid tanks. Yet here we are, a less valued commodity through no fault of our own.
