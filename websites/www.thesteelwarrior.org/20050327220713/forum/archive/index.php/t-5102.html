For conventional tank/healer groups, you are probably better off exping in C1 like others have mentioned. The Field of Gib is probably not to bad either, a bit harder than C1, but not all that bad.

C2 is not as good for a traditional tank/healer group because most of the mobs are red cons and they hit pretty hard. It become a lot more viable when you get more HP/AC but you are better served in other camps when you are first breaking in to the elems.

Once you can handle C2 without too much trouble, I think you will find that this is the best exp anywhere. In all caster kiting groups we used to crank out AA in 20-25 minutes, pretty hard to beat that. You can get pretty close to that in a high DPS group once you have the HP/AC to tank without being a mana sponge.
