Get run3, get regen3 or metabolism 3 or whatever floats your boat for the other 3 you have to have in general AA, then get 65. You'll get groups alot easier, the AA's will go faster, and a 65 warrior with no AA is probably still a better tank than a 60 with defensive AA's. At 65 I would get:
ND2
CA2
AE taunt
Then go back and finish up all your archetype defensive AA's, get PE, PD, LR, ID, then start working on offensive AA's. Opinions vary on the order you should do these in, but this is the route I'm taking. YMMV.
