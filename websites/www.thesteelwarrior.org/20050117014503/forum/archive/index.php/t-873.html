i dont know about others but i have found a difference in my weapon usage recently. In tier 1, i was using my 2hander almost exclusively (a narandi's) and was having no problems with aggro control.

since getting to tier 2, its been a different kettle of fish for a couple of reasons. first off,and this may seem logical - but in the higher tiers you will find yourself grouping with levels 62-65 and they tend to have better quality weapons etc. Since moving to tier 2 i have also started finding in my pick up groups those people from the really high end guilds who have pretty decent weapons whom you just dont see in tier 1.

also, as everyone knows, mobs do hit a lot harder in tier 2. so anyway, after a bit of experimenting, i found myself having to go back to my old combo of wavecrasher/frosty (still working on epic atm and should get em soon) to help me with aggro control in the first portion of the fight. once slow landed, i was then doing what i normally did and swopping out to the 2 hander for dps.

However, i started finding that i was still losing aggro just on pure dps output from other melees. luckily i got that 14/20 piercer that drops in UP from tawro, so now i swop that out with the wavecrasher to give me a bit of the edge in the dps war and it seems to work ok. i still keep the frosty in offhand cos its dps is not so bad as the wavecrasher and the occasional proc during the fight helps too imo.
