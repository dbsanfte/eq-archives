Kaesorn
I played Ragnarok Online for a short while, and one of the best ideas I'd seen on there is the ability for the "warrior-type" class to significantly raise his regen. Although this gave those characters with a small ability to solo a few experience monsters, I thought it was one of the best ideas for the stout tank to have.

The Swordman class/job in that game has a passive ability to increase their regen. It has 10 levels. They gain 5hp/10 seconds for each level they get. In addition to that, they receive 0.2% of their max hp for each level they get (max of 2%). It's mainly used for downtime in that game, it seems. Not only that, but healing potions receive a 10% bonus to their healing amount for each level. this would make them full of health from 1hp in about 5 minutes.

In this case, a swordsman with 5k hp and maxed regen would receive 150hp every 10 seconds just from regen. Also, any potions they quaffed in this time would heal him for double. It allowed them to kill very low monsters that really shouldn't be killing someone as buff as them.

I thought it was the best idea ever, and still do. I often wonder what the hell EQ is doing with it's 3hp/tick on a tank with 7khp....

Although it'd have to be altered, I wish they'd institute something like that starting at low levels for all classes, and gave the tank classes twice as many levels as anyone else (5 levels for everyone, 10 for tanks).
