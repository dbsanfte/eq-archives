Lurid dagger procs fairly well. I only remember one or two fights where it didn't proc within the first 5-6 rounds of swinging for me. I normally switched it out after the first proc though, because I wouldn't see aggro jump again the subsequent times it procced.

I also liked that not only did it debuff the mob, but it gave the AC to you. Made a great junk buff when you needed it. I used it when one of our druids fought Fay and it went of 6 times during the fight. Saved quite a few of my nice buffs.
