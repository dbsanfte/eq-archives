A thought creeped into my brain. One thing we're learning about GoD is the value of AC is significantly higher than it had previously been. We also know that affecting a hit value of 100ish can have a dramatic result on durability.

So here's the issue:

We kind of tend to have a lot of kneejerk reactions to a lot of things, chief among them is the importance of adding hitpoints. Well, in this game there's some tradeoffs, and one of the possible tradeoffs is Shield of the Arcane vs. Focus of the 7th.

Focus of the 7th is 544 hitpoints.

Shield of the arcane is 300 hitpoints, 36AC and 30 stackable MR.

The "hp to ac" calculation cannot remain stagnant from Velious to now with the DI of mobs getting into the realm of insanity, and therefore I would think that the old values of 5-6HP/AC is outdated and it's more along the lines of 10-12hp/AC if not more. Knowing this, it seems to me that Shield of the Arcane may actually produce more benefit to us than Focus of the 7th (which adds useless stats and pure hitpoints).

I think it's testing time!
