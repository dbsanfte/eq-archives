Lessiter1
Any haste item 32% or higher is all you really need. Speed of Vallon is 68% haste, so it should put you at the 100% haste cap with one of those items. Speed of the Shisshar (enchanter epic) is 66% haste, so a 34% item will put you at the cap.

31% items - Runed bolster belt
Soulscream belt
That new mask from ChardokB..
34% items - spiked seahorse hide belt
36% items - Cloak of flames
Cloak of crystalline waters

Any of those will do just fine for haste.. being at 99% vs. 100% haste with enchanter buffs doesn't make any difference at all.

The soulscream belt goes for 30k or so on Prexus, and it has reasonably good stats. Pick up one of those and you'll have 30k left.

After that, you can either buy Skyshrine HoT armor MQ's with the left over cash, or depending on your toon's race/religion, pick up some of the cultural stuff available to you.
