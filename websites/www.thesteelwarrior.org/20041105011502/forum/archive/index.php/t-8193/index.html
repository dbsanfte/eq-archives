Vikken
Tribute haste is item haste. Item haste does not stack, only the highest value will take. It will stack with casted haste (clicky/proc/shaman/enchanter/beasty/bard) up to 100%. Most bard songs are casted haste and follow the same rules as normal. However, Bards do have special songs that they can use that will stack, giving you over-haste (>100%). You are wasting money with your tribute haste (26%) being lower that your already worn haste (36%).

Normal bard songs are the same as spells. They don't stack with spells, and are capped at 100%. Example: Warsong of Zek (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3374&source=Live)

"Haste v3" bard songs stack with everything, and can take you beyond 100%. Example: Rizlona's Call Of Flame (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3362&source=Live).
Does Bard haste stack with Tribute haste?
Yes, since tribute = item.
