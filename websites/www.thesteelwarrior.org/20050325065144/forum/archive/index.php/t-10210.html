Hey guys, I really liked the last DPS thread with parses started by Timmokk, and I wanted to see how the situation was now with new AA/gear.

A few general notes about parsing. It is usually a terrible idea to 'parse' mobs with adds, or mobs where the mob is pushed all over the place and it is easy to 'lose' damage.

I did most of those parses on Msha's, for the simple reason that:

- They are easily accessable to anyone who posts here.
- They are always equal, and it is very easy to pick up all the damage from tem.
- They do not have any adds, and they have overall very low magic resist, if properly debuffed. With the new OoW nukes, wizards get perfectly respectable OoW nukes that save vs MR, so the efficiency drop off switching from FR to MR is much lower.

I have often heard that Msha's are 'bad parse mobs' but frankly I disagree. True, the 'ideal' mob for a caster has:

- Low HP
- Low FR
- No manadrain AE
- Allows horses to be used

And frankly I cannot think of anything worthwhile that fits the bill. Msha's are very very caster friendly with the exception of fire resistant, which frankly hurts druids a lot more than wizards.

That said, here are the parses, I will put up more when I get a chance:

A note on all parses:
Mobs were almost always debuffed with malosina/hos/chaos chant/tash. Resists at least on magic nukes were a non issue.

Bards are very rare for us and they are usually busy debuffing, therefore it is very rare melees or casters get overhaste. Bards usually twist marr/hos/chant/resists.

Our top two rogues use epic 1.5 / 1h slasher from primals and piercer from primals / scimitar from qvic.


Note on the 'rogue kick ass parses':

A rogue with epic 1.5, and Legion Kukri puts out 680 DPS. I am really curious to see the parses of the rogues with epic 2.0, if a rogue with epic 1.5 reaches 700 DPS.
