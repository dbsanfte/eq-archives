the bane weapon I would recommend are phosphorous torches (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7441) which can be gotten in exhange for 4 acrylian phosphates (farmed in netherbian layer off of netherbians or baz purchased). These aren't as good as the stake but are easy to get, and neither lore nor no drop.
