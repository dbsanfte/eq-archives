Ejryu
If you're using an FD/fade class to pull Tagrin, FD him just outside his room. Swords spawn (at least some of them do) even when Tagrin paths back and they stay up. There will be 5 swords the entire time Tagrin is alive, ie; killing a sword makes another one spawn and obviously that's bad. At any rate, the only tough part is the swords. You can lock them down via mez and then win. They're summoned, somewhere around level 55/56. Enchanters can charm. Magicians can charm. Bards can charm. The best is having a raid of like 10 and watching all 5 swords beat the piss out of Tagrin. We've done this with as few as 8 people, not even close to vt/ele stuff. Clear the room, leaving the wraith at the front left corner (front being closest to you, as you enter the forge room). Kill it when everyone is ready to go. Pull Tagrin to just outside his room to get rid of the elite add, get your CC to lock stuff down BEFORE trying to charm anything. Tank Tagrin in the corner you just killed the wraith in. If your melee are using max melee range, they'll be out of AE. If they're getting hit by it, step out and call for a heal. Errrm, and enchanters can use Glamour of Kintaz to save on mana if they really want to, as opposed to sleep/bliss that they've got up on the way. Grats on the spoils of victory, wave to the lonely elite guard in Tagrin's room as you click the zone out = )
