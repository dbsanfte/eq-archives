13/20 + stun proc is MUCH BETTER for aggro than 13/19 and no proc.

The DPS difference between the two is insignificant.... and a stun proc is as good as it gets for aggro with the exception of EB.

While DPS is a very important part of aggro, and is much more reliable than procs, you need to weigh the difference in DPS against the value of the proc.

In your case, you are choosing an insignificant DPS advantage over a very good proc... and that is a mistake. If the DPS difference was significant, then it might be a good idea.

Pure DPS does create and hold aggro. (I had great success for a long time tanking with my TSBS strictly because of DPS aggro.) However, that only carries you so far. Over time, casters that I grouped with (and other melee) began to do enough damage that the pure DPS on the TSBS no longer was enough. With the damage that casters are doing in today's game, you need an extremely high end DPS setup (and a lot of offensive AAs) in order to get by on straight DPS.

I notice that you list weapon delay first, and then the damage (19/13 for 13 damage/19 delay). It is common to list them in the order of damage/delay (or 13/19 in this case). I point this out because I am afraid you are misunderstanding what emilia was posting. When she says that she can keep aggro based on straight DPS with ber blade of war (48/32) she is talking about a 48 damage, 32 delay weapon... not 32 damage and 48 delay. A look at her magelo also shows that she has all offensive AAs. Unless you have access to weapons of that quality, you'll be needing to rely on procs... because you will not be doing enough DPS to get by on DPS aggro alone. 13/19 in your main hand and anything in your off hand won't keep aggro off your caster group.

All this random stuff with the procs is not for me
In that case, you made a MAJOR mistake by choosing to create a warrior to tank for your group. Warriors are entirely dependent on random procs and taunt success rates for aggro. If you want "on demand" aggro control, then you should have made a paladin or SK. Warriors have some things going for us... but pushbutton aggro control isn't one of them.
