I have hesitated even bringing this up, because I know it is not high on the list of priorities for most warriors, especially at the higher end, and I don't want to deflect anything from the really essential changes, but...

.. what about soloing for pure melees? I happen to be someone who enjoys soloing (or did when it was still possible), but even for the bulk of warriors, it is a clear disadvantage that we are one of 3 classes that just can not solo effectively (without a boatload of special equipment) for aa's or exp during those frequent times when we can't find a group.

The only solution I see would have something to do with regen - specifically a significant increase in hp regen for pure melee (or at least warriors). This would actually also contribute a little bit to the problem of the hp 'gap' (or lack of it) between warriors and hybrids. Not a lot - but at least it wouldn't hurt.

I'm thinking either a major increase in innate regen as you level, and/or addtional regen aa's. Another possibility would be a 'disc solo'. No time limit, but would preclude the use of other discs and could not be used in a group. This would reduce your hp's and ac somewhat but would give you a big increase in regen while it was in effect (and possibly a haste boost as well).

So I'm posting this for opinions. I may observe the chat on the 24th but I have no intention of posting anything. If there is concern that this might end up sending the devs in the wrong direction then I'd rather drop it, but I'd like to know what the general consensus of the community is on this subject.

landrain
There are 4 things in EQ that in my opinion are extremely broken and have caused the rise of the haves and have nots, so to speak...

1.) SLOW - a 75 % slow reduces a mobs attacks to virtually none.

2.) SNARE / SOW - These two spells combined allow virtual immunity from mobs.

3.) ROOT - similar to above immunity, except when mobs summon.. but these mobs easy to avoid.

4.) Spell Damage vs Melee damage - Explain this to me, melee use weapons, we can hit or miss, on exp mobs we are talking hitting say 50% of the time if we are doing well. Then we do damage, which varies greatly, but is generally on the average of 2x damage + Damage bonus. the odds of us actually doing MAX damage or even 50% of our Max damage are rare, and less than 20% of the time. Then look at casters, who fizzle/resist on exp mobs WAY LESS than 50% of the time, and do MAX damage WAY more than 50% of the time... Why this huge disparity?

The game is completely melee adverse and caster friendly. Add in all the increased caster power with mob decreased hp's and you have generated a hugely imbalanced situation.
