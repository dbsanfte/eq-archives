I went out to do a bit of AE factioning using Rampage and Earthshaker yesterday but I was very disappointed with the frequency of the procs. Previously when I have done this, masses of corpses were the result of the swarm of procs, but this did not happen.

Granted, I tried this only four times yesterday, but the results were very consistent. With groups of c. 60 mobs and 30 mobs I rampaged and go absolutely no procs either time. With a group of c. 40 mobs I got 2 procs and with a group of c. 50 mobs I got one proc. Although I was not slowed, I had no haste buff and no haste item.

Has anyone else experienced proc rate reductions in general or with Rampage? Perhaps this is just an aberration of the RNG, but it is a far departure from my previous experiences.
