Well, like Ven said, get rid of that sword. The last thing you want is an agro reducing weapon. CHoS and SoTBS will work just fine for you. If you want the biggest bang for your buck, get the HoRZ. It will give you 21 raw ac and 40 HPS. No other piece of armor will net that much AC and Hps. Slap the 10 ac augs from LDoN in your waist, back, neck, shoulders and face. Stick the 40 hps augs in the Helm, legs, bp, arms. While you are getting those ldon points, you can also get better gear from hard missions. 34 ac gloves with 90 hps, 34 ac boots with 90 hps (or close). Slap some anger augs on the weapons and yer good to go.
