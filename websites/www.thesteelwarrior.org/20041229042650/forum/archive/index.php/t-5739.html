Chance to fizzle is based on your level (effective casting level), the level of the spell, casting skill, and a "Fizzle Adjust" that some spells have (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=13&source=Live) (CH does fizzle more often than usual). At level 65 and max casting skills though, a caster is well past the minimum chance to fizzle on any spell (5%), so effects that increase (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2200&source=Live) effective casting level are useless, but effects that decrease (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3003&source=Live) it do have an effect.
