10-15-2003, 04:02 PM
Your own flurry messages fall under the "other messages" category in the filters and color scheme. This is the same category used when you gain experience, for instance.

So to move flurry to a new window, you need to set the window for the "other" messages. This will put flurry messages, experience gained messages, and all the other misc yellow messages in that window.

Hope that helps.
