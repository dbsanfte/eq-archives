Gerald
This is the way I do LDoN, and it does not always involve enchanters, clerics, and or shamans!

Get a decent group with some good DPS. Hell I don't always do it with good DPS groups. You just have to have momentum and you have to know hwat you're doing. It doesn't have to be "balanced." If no clerics are available, get a druid. If you have a druid, try and get an enchanter or bard. If no enchanter, get a shaman! so now you have either 2-3 people. Get antoher tankish class like a warrior, paladin, SK, or ranger (I prefer the ranger for attack buffs and they can dish out good DPS and still tank decent), and some DPS (monk, rogue, wizard, mage).

We never ever "pull." I hate pulling in LDoN, and I hate it when I have monks in my groups that think they have to pull (or anybody for that matter, but it's usually monks that have a pulling mechanism bolted into their brains). My groups move TO the mobs and rarely the reverse. You kill 2-3 mobs. You move to the next room/area of mobs. I don't ever bother pacifying or what not. I just charge a room, which gets all the mobs on me. Enchanter or bard can mez, or shaman can slow (the only time I ever care if mobs are slowed is if we don't have an enchanter or bard to mez). Mobs can be root parked. Whatever floats your boat. Your 2nd tank class can tank one while the rest of the group assists you. If a mob gets on the healer, I just beat/taunt it till it's back on me and continue killing my first target. It may get sloppy sometimes but it works fine for me. Don't forget to shield and area taunt if you have it!
