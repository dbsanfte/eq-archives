The devs have a problem designing new grind zones because they've created such a massive gear gap between high end raiders and everyone else. It's especially felt on your tank. Or... you need a lot more zones because you can't just design grind zones for L65-70. You have to have separate stuff for high end raid tanks who get toward L70 and all the other L70 tanks. That's EQ today. Blame the devs who've created this huge gap between people of the same level and have to deal with it now.

There are actually quite a few guilds that have made it to Uqua without having set foot in Time. I imagine that most up and coming guilds willtailor thier progression around what is most available on their servers. If VT is packed, then LDoN+GoD will prepare them for the elems and Time. If the elems and Time are packed, then VT+early GoD+OoW will prepare them late GoD. The people will go were others are not, which I think is a good thing for the game.
