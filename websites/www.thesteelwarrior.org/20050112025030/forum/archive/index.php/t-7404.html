velium swiftblade is an excellent offhand between levels 15 and 19 (!) and then not much good after that. :) ... the haste is great and you cant hit anything with your offhand in those levels anyway :p and the damage cap means you dont lose a lot of hitting power in those levels anyway. also if you use the hsagra in your primary at least you will get a little slashing practice in .. again not much.
