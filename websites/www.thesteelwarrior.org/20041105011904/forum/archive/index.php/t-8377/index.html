Sucana
You have a lot of slots to cover, so you need to be conservative in spending. Look up cultural armor. Have you considered following the smithing guide at www.eqtraders.com, to learn how to make a set of your cultural armor? Learning to smith can be expensive, but you can keep the costs down with some farming. You have all the gear you need to kill spiderlings and wolves. :)

And, of course, you will also want a haste item (soulscream belt, as a pricy example) and weapons. Greatstaff of thunder is probably the most cost-effective weapon, although it might require level 55. Otherwise, sword of the bloodsworn and copper hammer of striking.

The Droga weapons are both dropped items and the NPC's (king & seer) are both in the same room in Droga.

A Soulscream Belt (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=65570) Drops in Veskar on the Rotting Shopkeeper and would do you well hastewise.

As an added bonus you can also go after Kylong Shinguards (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=65543) that Also drop in veskar off Feral Lord Gulok who's camp you pass both on the way In and Out.

No need to spend upwards of 40k of the above when they can definitely be attained by a strong group at your level. Good Luck.
