i duo my 65 warrior and 65 cleric (ft12, 4500mana) in CT occasionally. First off i'd say it's not worth it, the trash mobs don't drop plat, although they do drop the equivalent to fine steel weapons once in awhile. Secondly, while it's possible to kill 3 mobs at a time, its an incredable drain on mana, and the fourth mob is pretty much a guarunteed wipe.

Single pulls leading to named mobs are pretty much non existant, i think dreadfang is the only one. Everything can be pacified though, so it helps a ton. The exp sucks too compared to first tier planes. Not really a great option for making plat, although with the right gear and aa's, it's generally trivial and safe.
