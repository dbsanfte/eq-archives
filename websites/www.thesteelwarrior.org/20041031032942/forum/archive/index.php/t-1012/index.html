moglar
The thurgadin gate potion is what I use. I usually gate one to 3 times per weekday playing session. It takes me approximately five minutes, once I've gated, to run to the NPC in thurgadin and say 'what velium vapors' (she doesn't repond to a hail) for the contraption you make the potion with, run to the bank and combine grab my ingredients, turn around and use the pottery wheel and the other pottery thingie that looks like an oven, then run to PoK. 5 minutes of downtime for the ability to gate isn't the extent of it, however. You also need to either farm or buy the components necessary.

Before I describe all of this in detail, I'd like to point out that it took me 4 hours of intense clicking and about 100 pp to get 122 pottery. Even so, 4 hours and 100 pp is nothing. As soon as I heard about this potion i was all over it. The ability to gate for a warrior! Ofcourse, this is the poor man's version of warrior gate. The expensive version would be a shaman made potion to return you to you're bind point ;)

You need farm or buy in the bazaar:
small pieces of velium (bazaar price on BB ~ 30pp)
small velium bricks (bazaar price on BB ~ 30PP)
crystalline spider silks (bazaar price on BB ~5pp)

You need to buy from a pottery vendor:
Glass Shards
High quality firing sheets

In a pottery wheel combine:
1 small velium brick
3 crystalline spider silks
2 glass shards

This yields an unfired vial. Then turn to the other pottery thingie, the one that looks like an oven and combine:
1 unfired vial
1 high quality firing sheet

This yields a velium vial. Now take the velium vial and put it in the contraption you received from the NPC along with 2 small pieces of velium (the stackable ones).

This yields a vial of velium vapors, which is you're gate potion! Woot!

I forget the name of the NPC you speak to in THurgadin, but I can give directions. Head to the bank from GD. Pass the bank. The path goes down a slope. After the bottom of the slope, the second shop on the left has a vendor. That's you're girl. She doesn't respond to hails, just to the phrase "what velium vapors"

I farm in CC and Velks.

This is actually part of the shawl quest.

On veeshan gate potions sell in the bazaar for around 300-350pp per charge.. can get single, 5 charge or 10charge potions. Casting time is 10 seconds. Personally I carry 2 of the 5 charge gate potions in case I get stuck somewhere with a friend.

Given our lack of channelling skill I prefer the 10 second casting ones.
