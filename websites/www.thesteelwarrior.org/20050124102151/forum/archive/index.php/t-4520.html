Well I had some ideas on other new classes that could/should be coming down the pipeline in the next few years:

1- The Green Knight. The first hybrid of a hybrid. This class will have all the stun capabilities of a paladin, as well as all the DoT, snare and pure hate casting capabilities of a shadowknight. They will also have Gawain Bane damage innate, and will be immune to decapitation. Unfortunately, they will also have to spend some time, once they achieve their class title, being a QUEST GIVER to a lower level Knight class.

2- The Battle Mage. They will have all the nuking power of their wizard parent class, combined with all the sword-wielding ability of their ranger parent. They will have more effective heals than druids. They will cast effective self-only hp/mana regen spell. They will not have to eat. They will be able to snare, and will have "rampage" as an innate skill, from level 5 on. They can wear plate mail. They will receive bigger 2h weapon bonuses than all other classes. Their epic will be the first dual click weapon. It will have a 1 second cast time "aggro" generation spell/unresistable DD/Lifetap ... OR it will have an instant click gate spell. The Battle Mage chooses, by holding their shift key (or not) when clicking their mouse.

3- Ninja. At the drop of a spoon, the ninja class will be able to flip out and cut peoples' heads off. All mammals will be able to become ninjas. Their epic will be the sepuku frisbee. And their newbie tunic will be able to be worn on the head as a ninja mask, with a slight adjustment.
