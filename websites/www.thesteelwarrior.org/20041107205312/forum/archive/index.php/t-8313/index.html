Bruenorg
MA = Main Assist - the person everybody else in the group/raid assists for a target.
MT = Main Tank - the person who is first in line to be expected to keep the attention of the group's/raid's primary target.

In most non-raid situations, the MA/MT is one in the same. Silly to have it any other way really. With the advent of the Luclin expansion, many encounters were added that sometimes require the MT to keep the primary target busy while the rest of the raid focuses on dispensing with adds/secondary targets that are initially part of OR spawn during the encounter. During these types of encounters, you will typically have other tank types keeping these secondary targets occupied while someone else in the raid, the MA, is dictating which target the raid should currently be focussed on.

This type of approach is also very useful when doing multi-tank clearing with mobs that can potentially gate (Vex Thal is a prime example of this). E.g. Puller brings in a pile of mobs; warriors, sks and paladins each grab one keeping them busy while the MA (usually someone who can get a good view of things going on around him/her - int casters work good) monitors health of any gaters and calls the targets accordingly.

The basic point I'm trying to make is that while the MT/MA are a lot of the time the same person, they are essentially VERY different duties. Depending on what content you experience in the game, it may become necessary to split these duties among multiple people in order to preserve order and ensure the success of many raid encounters in the higher end game.
