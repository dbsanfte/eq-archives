Aratus
Your weapons look good, so I'll just focus on the armor.

If you're on Claws of Veeshan faction, working on the quest in Western Wastes is simple and easy. Kill the dragon hunters, hand in their heads in Skyshrine. Results are these (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=12557).

A decent, low cost, upgrade to your neck item can be Tracking Wolf Collar (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=8905) or Clattering Bone Necklace (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=6418). I've seen the Necklace drop as low as 500 platinum.

Valorium rings are a nice upgrade, some new earrings would do you good as well. There's many earrings out there you can upgrade with. Twisted Ring Loop (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=40104) being one of them. These drop randomly off the miners in Torgiran Mines. It's hard to find the right earrings to suggest. There's just a ton of decent HP/Sta earrings out there right now. If you have money to burn though, get some Bloodmetal Earrings.

For your greaves, you can keep an eye out for Barbed Rubicite Greaves (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=27860) in the Bazaar, prices have pretty much tanked on them.

If you're able to, spend some time in Kael/Skyshrine and grab yourselves some Plate armor. Time in Veksar would make for some nice upgrades also, specifically a Soulscream Belt (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=65570).

An' lastly... that range item has gotta go. Lots of stuffs you can put in there. I'm not sure how much a Nightmarewood Compound Bow goes for, if it's cheap enough it'd make for a low cost decent upgrade. If you can manage Nadox though, go for a Darkwood Bow of Suffering (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=40074).

An' like Wychway asked... what's your limit that you can afford? That'd help out suggestions a ton.
