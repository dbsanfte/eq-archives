Lathen
Two things you can do:

One, get a higher level friend to come along and help with heals and/or damage shields (etc.) This is called "power levelling". You will move up quickly. The down side is that you won't learn how to play the class and your skills will all suck.

Two, fight in the zones with the exp modifiers. Paludal Caverns is the most famous, but there are lots of others. As you move up in level, watch for the "hot spot" zones. The down side is that you'll be dealing with idiots and trains (and sometimes trains of idiots). Your skills are also likely to be non-maxed if you do this.

On a side note, why do you want to level him as quickly as possible?
