Bonequest
Wait until level 55 to work on 2h weapons then get some fast non-procing 2h practice weapons, get haste and focus, and head to the outer camp in PoV and ask if you can help the groups there kill mobs while you work on your 2h skills. In 2 or 3 days you can max those skills out and its not really worth doing much before that level anyway. Once you get 2h blunt and slash up over 200 then think about getting a GSoT for tanking and perhaps a good low agro 2h slasher like a windblade or something similar for when you are not tanking.

As for raising 1h weapon skills, just get some fast weapons like a lammy, a gorganga club (common drop that rots in ME zone near DSP), and some fast peircing weapon and just help some group as soon as you can. Those skills are much more useful for now.
Azaza
Unfortunately, the way the game plays out for us warriors, is that we can get away with dual wielding earlier on in the game... and generally better off with 2h later on. Roughly about 55 when the GSoT (aka Lollipop of Doom) is available.

Then as your gear, hps, and groups allow... dual wielding may become an option again.

Any weapon with a slow, stun, or stun w/DD ... those will help greatly at your level. If you got points to aug them (with Anger 1, or an aug with the above effects) then that should help even more.
