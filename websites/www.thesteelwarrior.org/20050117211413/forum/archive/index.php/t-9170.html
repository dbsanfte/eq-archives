I think your stuff is level appropriate for the most part--your weapons are good & haste is fine for now. Your ears/fingers are okay for now. You'll be looking at replacing the rings eventually, but the blood earring will keep you for a good long time.
I don't know about dwarf-specific options, or what the agnostic armor is like for 'em.
I'd get halas 10-pound meat pies and grobb liquidized meat. Cheap stat bumps.
You BP is a little light; you might be able to find a different neck since you have other haste; your shoulders also seem a bit light to me.
obviously, champions would be an upgrade for you if you were in Kael anyway, and apparently you know how to do the quest. Very cheap upgrades in some slots might be barbed ringmail (not sure dwarves can wear it but the shoulders, for example, have 19AC). Kylong shinguards are good. You'd need a bit of help, but the draconic pauldrons quest would result in a shoulder upgrade.
Off the top of my head, my standard advice is to do the bazaar search thing.
Lots of warriors like about 20 AC/level, which seems like a fairly good guide to me.
