As far as getting up your stamina goes, id aim for some augments for one. Its hard to tell you which slots to change out for more +sta, because most of your slots have alot of good stats on them. I am not sure what they go for on your server, but look into the costs of Helmet of Rallos Zek - very good all around helm. Usually at 65 by the time you get a few raid drops sta/str will cap themselves, ie its not something your going to have to worry about. Agi/Dex/Cha are usually the harder stats to get up while retaining the best possible hp/ac, and of those only cha really goes up "slowly". Until you have enough aa to throw some into planar power, your stat cap is 280 anyhow - the shaman sta buff adds 60 - so 220 is your "soft cap" with raid buffs. With an upgrade or two and an aug or two you will easily hit that - and by the time you have enough aa to train planar power and up your cap, you will most likely have gotten raid drop upgrades that will have added sta anyhow. All in all, i wouldnt lose sleep over it - think of it as room to grow, not as coming up short.

As far as BoT goes - fully buffed you can already tank BoT. I would bring alon a good cleric with kei, and a 65 shm so slows land fast - but you can do it without much trouble. I however would problably not use a druid as main healer in BoT until you are at least 65, but thats really just choice. Krigers will not be your friend in bot for awhile, its not so much the 900dmg hits, but rather the 1500 dmg procs that will spike your hp down fast. Try hunting in areas like DS or the camps at top of stairs - you wont encounter krigers in these camps and it will make for nice exp. If you end up in a situation where you will be tanking krigers, try to recruit a wizard into your group and have him spellshield you - althought its something normally saved for raids, it could give you that extra advantage on the procs to make up for your lower hp for now.
