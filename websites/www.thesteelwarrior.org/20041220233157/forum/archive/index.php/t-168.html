I did some analysis. With 36% haste, dex of 250 or so, against a L61 blue mob, I logged 32,600 seconds (a bit over 9 hours) of OBS usage.

OBS proc'd (I think) 765 times. Since there's no emote, it's difficult to tell for sure - I had some periods where I had pet activity for close to 2 minutes straight! Based on looking at a 'normal' pet's total attack events (hits & misses), I figured each pet got in an average of 15 swings. So, total lines of attack(11480)/15=~765.

Each pet averaged 404 total damage. (309,252/765)

Taken over the entire battle, the OBS pet dps was: ~9.5. (309,252/32,600)

OBS main-hand seconds/proc: 42.6. (procs/min=1.4)

I didn't post the dps for the sword itself, since that would be (imho) irrelevant, and would be pretty variable based on the user's attack rating, haste, AA's, etc. However, based on some quick parses I did with Ornate Great Blade against the same mob, OBS+a decent OH nets about 14dps higher than the 2hs.

I'm currently using OBS+Vyzh`dra's Render of Souls for my dps setup, and am pretty happy with the results (and the lack of aggro).

Enjoy!
