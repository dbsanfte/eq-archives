Play neither a knight, nor a warrior. You will NOT be able to do what you seem to want with either of them.

You want a "Tanking" type character, since you are asking about a paladin or warrior, which are both tanks, so that throws out all pure casters.

You want to do "want to do the most damage possible in the quickest amount of time" , so that throws out bards, and knights.

You want to solo, so that throws out warrior, rogue, monk and berserker.

You want to role-play, so that thows out....well, nothing really.

So, you are left with:

Beastlord
Ranger

And if you want to solo effectively from 1-70 +AAs, you really cant be a ranger, well, you can, but it will be VERY rough from 55ish til 59 and AM3/EQ, and even then, root/shoot is NOT great exp.

I play a Beastlord, and I can solo, even at level 70, I can solo mobs that hit for 600 easily. I can do "decent" damage, and with my weapons, and a few AA skills, a /disc, a few dots, and a few nukes, I can do a good deal of damage in a short time. I have a pet, she is named Snarl, and is a level 61 Scaled Wolf, and she has 18AA all of her own, which makes her higher level than many players in everquest, heh. So, yea, I can role-play too, if I want.

I can be Tank, Slower, or DPS in a group, or all 3, which makes getting a group easier, grab a healer, 4 others, and go. One of the 4 a tank, great, now I dont get hit, one of them a shammy, great, now I can use more mana for DPS, etc.

But, if you are dead set on paladin or warrior, the pally is by far the better soloer.

I'd definately vote for Beastlord if you like to melee and solo. My main is a 70 Beast with 237 AA's that buffs to 7.8K with just Conviciton and self Beast-Buffed. Beastlords will never come close to the shear tankability and ability of a warrior to withstand the harsh beatings of content made for groups of that level. Beastlords also don't come close to comparing to necro's or even druids really in the solo department, but out of the melee classes, they are easily the best at soloing. I also have a Warrior alt that I enjoy playing a great deal. While he doesn't solo like the beastlord can, he is great in groups and will one day reach the level of being able to tank the big stuff. I love the thrill of close combat with both the beastlord and the warrior. My warrior stands toe to toe much better, but doesn't even come close to the soloability of the beastlord. Paladins can heal rather well, and tank rather well, but they loose out alot on the damage department. If I were going to have a tank.. I'd want a warrior to play if for no other reason than the thought that you can gleefully bathe in the blood of your evicerated opponent as he gurgles his last muted cries through his blood choked esophagus. Warriors were built to kill and fight, get bloody in battle and stand up to the meanest of mean with the steely eyed resolution of an enraged war boar. My young warrior is only 41 now, but growing swiftly in his thirst for blood and power.
