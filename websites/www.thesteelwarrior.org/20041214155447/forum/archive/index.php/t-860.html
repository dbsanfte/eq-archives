To directly answer your question, the Narandi Lance may be the best aggro, second would be the snowchipper and mallet. The bloodpoint sucks for aggro, so do DD weapons.

I recommend selling either the mallet or snowchipper and getting the token Frostbringer. It's a warrior cliche now on the boards to get one but that is likely the best aggro weapon you will get until (if) you get epics.

Frostbringer main, snowchipper off is decent aggro and dps.
