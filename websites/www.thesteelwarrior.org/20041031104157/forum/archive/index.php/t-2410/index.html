Iskandar
Primal = any number of weapons that dropped in Sleeper's Tomb. If the Sleeper has been awakened on your server, Prismatic weapons drop in lieu of Primal. Prismatic weapon = Primal weapon with minor statistical differences and new graphics. Primal weapons proc Avatar which = +100 ATK/STR/DEX/AGI.

You need a key to get into Sleeper's Tomb. Key components drop off of Yelinak (Skyshrine), Sontalak (Western Wastes), Klandicar (also WW), Zlandicar (Dragon Necropolis), and Lendinaria (west Temple of Veeshan). Also I think Vindicator (Kael) drops a key component. Component goes to Jaled Dar's shade in Dragon Necro for a ST key.

Ya need a raid to get Prismatic weapons unless you're very well equipped.
