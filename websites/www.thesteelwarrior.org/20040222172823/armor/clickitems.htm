Clickable Effect Items.

As warriors have no mana, the only spells we are able to cast come from our items. Here is a list of some of the different effects we can obtain and the items that give them.

Coldain Insignia Ring (8+) - Instant cast, self-only damage shield (Ring 10 has regen as well). Quested from Thurgadin.

Journeyman's Boots - Instant cast, self-only speed spell. Comparable to a cheap horse or run3. Quested in Rathe Mountains.

Bracer of the Hidden - Instant cast, targettable see-invis. Drops from Sarnak Legionnaires in the Sarnak Fort in the Lake of Ill Omen.

Eyepatch of Plunder - 3.5second cast, self-only 20% haste spell. Quested from Iceclad Ocean gnomes.

Cobalt bracer - 7.0 second cast, targettable shrink. Drops from Myconid's in Old Sebilis.

Incardine Brestplate - 8.0 second cast, self-only invisibility. Drops from the Overking in Chardok.

Dusty Soriz Worker's Pouch- Summons throwing weapons. Drops from named in the mines of Ssra.

Kizrak's Gauntlets of Battle - 3.0 second cast, self-only 30% haste. Quested via mold drops in tier 3+ Planes of Power.

Amulet of Necropotence - Instant Cast, self-only skeleton illusion. Drops from PoFear golems.

Cobalt Greaves - 8.0 second cast, Targettable invisibility. Drops from Venril Sathir in Karnor's castle.

Shrunken Goblin Skull Earring - Instant Cast, self-only attack buff. Drops from Grachnist in Warslicks Woods.

Reaper's Ring - Instant Cast, self only attack buff. Drops from Aaronyar in North ToV.

Blood Orchid Katana - 8.0 second cast, targettable Spirit of the Wolf. Drops from Druushk in Veeshan's Peak.

Bangle of Disease Warding - Instant Cast, targettable resist disease. Result of the CoD-Key quest.

Pegasus Feather Cloak - Self Only Levitation. Drops from Quillmane in South Karana.

Orb of the Sky- 10.0 second cast, self only weapon imbue. Knockback+35dd. Dropped in Vex Thall.

Spined Chain Mail - 9.0 second cast, self only Bramblecoat (3ds + 19ac). Drops from the Servitor of Luclin in Grieg's End.

Cloak of Thorns - Instant Cast, self only 4pt damage shield. Stacks with most other damage shields. Drops from Lady Nevederia in North ToV.

Holgresh Elder Beads - 4.0 second cast Eye of Zomm. No longer drops.

Pauldrons of Ferocity - Instant Cast, Self only Aura of Battle. Drops from Cazic-Thule in the Plane of Fear.
