Due to the slower killing rate in Sebilis, the faction earned per hour is about equal between Guk and Sebilis.

The best thing about Sebilis is the unlimited supply of mobs. In Guk it is surprisingly easy to run out of mobs. There are about 100 faction-raising mobs in Lower Guk last time I counted, respawning every 36 minutes. This is a hard cap on your rate of earning faction in Guk, and the presence of other people will reduce it further (especially now during the Fabled mob month).

Yes, you do earn faction while killing reet frogloks. Unfortunately, you do not earn faction while killing dozens of shrooms and juggernauts on the way to the reets. Only the frogloks in Sebilis raise faction, not the other mobs.
