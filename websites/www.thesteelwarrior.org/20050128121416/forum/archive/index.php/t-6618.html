With 305 dex and ingenuity 3, you should deliver a crit blast approximately 9-10% of the time. I put a few log files together and had 16,690 total procs land, 1576 crit blasts, or 9.44%. Don't expect much dps out of it, around 1-1.5 dps gained from the extra damage on the crits unless you're using an ethereal destroyer.
