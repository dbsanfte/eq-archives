For a warrior:

Maelstrom of Ro = 45
Kilva's Skin of Flame = 33
Shield of Torment = 15
Blessing of Vah Kerrath = 8
Spikecoat = 4
Psalm of Veeshan = 15 * 3.9 = 58
Warsong of Zek = 15 * 3.9 = 58
Mark of the Righteous = 34
Spirit of the Wood = 55
Wrath of the Wild = 650

So you can have a 960 point damage shield for one hit, a 310 point damage shield for 5 ticks after that, and a 255 point damage shield from then on. You need Girdle of Intense Durability, Talisman of Vah Kerrath, Girdle of Living Thorns or Gleaming Bauble, a bard with Blaring Horn of Fire and the right AAs, and a druid with the right AAs though.
