The problems that SKs are having and the problems (if any) that the Pallies are having need to be carefully defined: by level, by stage of game, by raid situation, by group situation, by mob characteristics, by MT and offtank roles.

Until the problems are defined, it's unlikely an optimal solution will be found.

There's not much point in having three tank classes if they all tank alike.

There is also not much point in having three classes if one or two of them have severe problems getting groups or finding roles in raids or being useful to their firends and guildmates.

A good many pretty rational players who happen to have SKs as mains feel that they have problems. They want some sort of fix, but in the large majority of cases they are not calling for nerfing other classes nor do they want to break the game by getting some uber ability. When Slein calls for a modified form of defensive, I don't need to agree with him, but I do think it's in my best interest to listen to him carefully. He doesn't strike me as a person who makes up problems, so he's trying to address issues that are real to him. If I disagree that a modified form of defensive is a good idea, that does not mean his problems are trivial or that they don't need to be solved by some other means.

SOE has said knights will not get defensive. They've said it a number of times. Of various possible future outcomes, this one seems to be among the least likely. I don't see how imagining how bad it would be for warriors if knights had defensive will lead to much constructive thought on these issues.
