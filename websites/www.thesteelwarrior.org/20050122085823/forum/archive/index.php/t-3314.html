You got a pretty damn good stuff there, Miss Dwarf. I think it is fine, but if you look for perfection, here are some things I would upgrade (besides arms and BP but it has been already said) :

- Get an another mask, something with more AC and HP. Gantru war mask is cool, maybe a bit expensive. Face Mask of Frozen Mana from LDoN is nice too.

- Drop your Jagged Bone Hoop for something like Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement, if you have the cash. Mosquito Leg Earring is very cool too, plus they dropped much in price.

- Boots. The 15 sta on your Chokidai boots are nice but 8 AC sucks. And neg10 sv disease sucks even more. Even the cheap and simple Crusty boots would be a slight upgrade to that, at least not worse (I NEVER ever equip something that has neg stats, but it is a personal choice, and neg5 cha is better than neg10 disease, plus Crusty boots have Str). Barbed dragonscale boots are not bad too, you would loose a bit of HP but gain AC.

- Gauntlets : Kylong ones are great. If you cant afford them, you can still camp or MQ the ones from Thurg, it would give you a little HP upgrade. The best even would be the ones from Skyshrine, if you have the faction and your guild raids HoT.

Anyway nothing from what I said is a priority, it would just be cool and add some HP / AC, but if you have to farm cash or put much time into that, it would probably not be worth the trouble for the gain. The only thing that really needs to be changed are the arms, everybody seems to agree on this point ;o).
