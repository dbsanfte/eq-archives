Wulfran
Doomshade is all about the MT maintaining aggro and everyone else keeping the mob in place.

I tanked him the first time at 60 with around 6K hp, pre-POP.
- He's not slowable.
- He has hella AC.
- Hits for 400ish.
- Flurries a lot.
- Fairly magic vulnerable.
Get as much damage shield as possible on your MT, both for aggro and DPS. Make sure people understand that if they get hit they MUST turn off attack. We used a fair number of druid/shaman "spam" heals to augment the CH chain, esp when we were lower level/lower hp.

Could get hairy on your aggro if he has a pebble crusher (I hate stun procs) but thats part of the fun :p
wandor
At 3300hp unbuffed the best your tanks will be able to do even with level 65 buffs is going to be around 6khp. DS is going to be really heard to beat with a 6k tank unless you can get a bunch of clerics. I would imagine you would need a 2 sec rotation to keep a 6k tank up against DS. He flurries a lot - seriously a ton, like almost every round even when crippled. To do a 2 sec rotation you will need a minimum of 6 clerics, and even with that many there they will be hurting for mana. If you can get good mana regen a cleric with 3200mana should last about 14 cheals before going oom. That gives you about 2 min 48 sec with a 6 man 2 second rotation, which is not long enough unless yo have some serious DPS.

If you can get your tanks leveled up a bit with some AAs so you can last 3-4 seconds on the rotation, yor chances for success are much better. With 6 clerics on a 3 second rotation you can expect the rotation to last almost 6 minutes with the same 3200mana clerics.

Of course this is dependent on your cleric mana regen, which would have to be around 50mana/tick. You would definitely need a bard, KEI+, and they would all have to have horses. PotG+ and one of the Spiritual Dominion line of spells would help a bunch.
