In many ways I'm not qualified to answer your question because none of the warriors I've played have had a tail.
But the cheapest hp boost you can get is stat food: halas meat pies and grobb liquidised meat.
I'd go to the bazaar and search each slot, filtering carefully by race, reliegion and class of your character. Take notes on the best AC/pp ratio among your various options, then buy the best of the best.
You can often get sandblasted and gothic stuff in PoK from the dwarf vendors in the building with the forge in it. About 100 pp per piece; most would be upgrades for you (heh, check to make sure lizards can wear it). Match that stuff against the bazaar options you have.
