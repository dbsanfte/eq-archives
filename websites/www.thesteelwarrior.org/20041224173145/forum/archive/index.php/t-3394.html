Im a warrior, as most of you know

I started this game around 3 years ago, just before velius if i recall right, i loved my class, my cleric was fun but it just wasnt "home" to me, it didnt suit me, my raw need for power and to be face to face with my enemy came thru in my warrior, with my dorky eye patch and my sarnak greatblade, a gift from sunnyeyez, a kind haffer, i set out as a newbie and starting killing, i loved it, it felt right, it felt good

then when i got to level 28 i found out i completely fuggled my stats, i was a little upset, so after a transfer of my gear to my new warrior i started anew and got to 38,(keep in mind i had alot of alts so this took some time) again i relised this time, i fuggled again this time going with all STR (last time i put it all in agility and wisdom) and luclin came out so i started on my vah shir warrior, i got my stats perfectly, and required levels hadnt started so i got my levels back fast, in my good armor hehe, it truly represented how i felt about the warrior class, in the raw power and ferocity, the death and maiming were part of my duty as a man of travel, a roamer, killing things as i saw fit, justice i delt with the zen of virtue, i was the hero of my group, pulling SYASR all the time (saving your ass so run!) for my groups, people valued me greatly but i was getting less and less pick up groups, my friends didnt play often enough at the time and most had outleveled me with all my restarts, this is when i truly noticed paladins and SKs coming into their own, i felt a little worryed but i figured, im at the top of my game, it would be right to step on someone that has had their fair share of problems when i have had none.

when pop came out,
things changed, i was 46 i starting playing a hell of alot more, struggling to pull myself up to my friends levels, as i really had problems getting groups because of my mediocer armor and weapons, canabalizing my alts via the bazaar i became all about powering up my warrior, i wasnt going down with out a fight, dev team and absor be damned, i wasnt gona start over again as a paladin. i made it to 51 and found the TSW forums and started venting my anger, the indignation i was being beaten out of the job by alter boys and the guys the necro's sent to bring them fresh corpse's to preform their art on.

at 56 i pretty much burned out, loosing most of my faith in my class and the game makers, feeling really put off i took a long break from EQ, when i came back they had removed flags, so i went to PoS with those old friend i mentioned and i worked my way up to 63, now LDoN has come and i feel the game has gotten a little easyer for me, groups come easy with 2 tanks per group being the norm. ive heard promises from the main live site and the FF forum goings about whats being said and whats in the works and its enough to sustain me on EQ.

tho, thoughts of trying linage, DAoC, and some of the others are coming more and more often

forgive me for going on and rambling i just really felt the need to explain where im coming from, i really used to think about all the great things id get to see and do as i leveled, but now that im at the end game i am finding no one wants to do all the things my toon grew up dreaming of seeing, and it saddens me, but i look foward to seeing PoP gods and mini's, as well as LDoN raids. but again, i cant get to 65 with 100 AAs as easy as some classes, so thats kinda far off to :/

sorry for the complete lack of grammer and spelling but this is my feelings on my warrior's development and how ive come to enjoy the game, the enjoyments gone and i felt the need to say why and how, its almost like the warrior stock market crashed, work is hard to find and idle hands turn to protesting and flaming the people that burned down the game class i liked most
