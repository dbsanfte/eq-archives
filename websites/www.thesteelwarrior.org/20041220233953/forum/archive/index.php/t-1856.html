I have thought about this for awhile, and now I'm certain; Planes of Power is Satan Incarnate. So far, all the Planes have caused for me is the retirement of two characters that I truly loved playing pre-PoP, and having to put up with "Uber Guilds" in order to get my money's worth of the game.
I know what you're thinking, "But NinjaBurger, you can travel everywhere in the world with ease, AND get all your shopping done! Plus, get all the spells!" Blah, blah, blah. I personally think that the knowledge books took the fun out of the game of traveling everywhere. It just seems to me that shopping for things I need is trivial, and it really shouldn't be for a GAME. To me, these are actually the only redeeming things in Planes of Power.

-Zones

C'mon, everyone knows that PoP zones have insane experience, and EVERYONE wants in on it. That's the problem. PoP experience IS too good; no one wants to go anywhere else. Why go to Chardok and get an AA every 3 hours when you can get one every 30 minutes? Plus, there's the problem that they've unlocked most Planes zones, and now it's just a freaking mess around BoT and HoT.

-Changes

This is what made me angry. Now that mobs hit HARD and ripostre hard, you have no choice but fast aggro, thus making knights the best tanks. Now most warriors will sit for hours on end at the zone line, waiting for a chance to level. This frustrated me to no end, and what made me, Baron Roreksis Koopatroopa, 54 Iksar Champion, retire my warrior. I'm just sick of sitting on my ass for hours on end waiting for someone to feel sorry for me and get me a group. It just seems to me now that if you don't have a spellbook, you're a gimp. Same goes for monks, who used to be great pullers. Guess what? You're a gimp if you're a monk too. For the love of god, BARDS pull better now. So what we have left is just "Ok DPS" [Sheenadonas Saotome-50 monk(Retired)]

-Raiding material

This, this my friends sucks. I have not had large amounts of experience raiding in upper tiers, but with all the poor itemizing, I'd imagine that most classes are gimped still.

So...there's my writeup. The elegy of the warrior/monk who didn't feel useful anymore after Planes of Power. Quite the shame, don't you think?
