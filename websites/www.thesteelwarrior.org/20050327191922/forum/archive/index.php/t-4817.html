Read, this is both a good and bad thing, so please read.

Leashing Emp was a good thing because you could get him to the zoneline if you really wanted to. Beats everyone getting keys, right? Well, he's not supposed to be at the zoneline and not having half of your raid force dedicated to lockdown makes this bad boy pretty darn easy. So in a sense, this is what should have been done from the beginning.

Now, the bad news? The bad news is, well, very bad. He cannot venture more than about 5-10ft from his spawn location without warping back, but the range changes depending on direction. He will warp if he's directly behind his throne-portal or just past the little ramp in front of him that he has, but his range is about double that at 45 degree angles. It's kinda hard to explain. For those who have fought him, that's an INCREDIBLY small leash range. Oh, but that's not all. Feels like an infommercial, doesn't it? Upon warping, he mem blurs. As if the Diminutive Statue and Rage of Ssraeshza procs that he has didn't give enough agro problems to begin with. :P Oh, but that's not all! Upon warping, he is also completely dispelled. Yup, that's right, the Emp is no longer slowed. Now, while he's no Raex Pwodill or General Rapeass, but an unslowed Emp is still a pretty mean motherfucker.

Needless to say, we ended up wiping. We got him to the 40% range when he warped and suddenly jumped up to 72% health. Talk about a blow to the raid's moral.

Sony, if you're reading this, he's still beatable the way he is, but for Christ sake you could have made his leash a little longer than a baby step.
