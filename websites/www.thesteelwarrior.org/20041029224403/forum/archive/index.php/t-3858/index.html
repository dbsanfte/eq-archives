Phantron
Even mobs that do not run will gate when they're low on health if they're sufficiently far away from their spawn point. For example take any random priest or shaman in Deepest Guk. If you fight at their spawn point they'll fight to the death like most good old Undead, but pulling them 3 screens away and they will gate. Gating is actually a different action from running away and the only thing that can prevent gating completely is by fighting close enough to mob's spawn point, or completely draining its mana (but they seem to always have enough for a gate, no matter how much you drain).

I don't know that a single chanter could mana sieve fast enough to prevent gaters from gating and this would definitely be the major obstacle to single grouping HoT - anyone have empirical evidence on that?

Your alternative (or more probably a supplement) is to interrupt the gaters when they cast - and given your low DPS they *will* get off several gate casts, so it won't be easy with that few peeps (you only need to fail to interrupt one time after all). BST/SHM pets will help (shammy have the tri-pet AA?), also be sure you watch for the gate spell message so stunners know when to stun/kick/bash (and make sure all are stunning from the same direction so you don't negate each others' push). Maybe that shammy stun/root AA might be a worthwhile investment for this purpose? Anyone know if that works in this scenario?

The best bet for sure is to fight as close to the gater's spawn point as possible, but sometimes you won't be able to manage that.

I'm with the poster who recommended you evac/exodus often and early as needed. I'd have the dr00d in the main group and make sure any peeps in an off group are gate-capable or have another evacer in it. You have no corpse summoner so CR is going to be dicey. Probably better just to fight your way to corpses if you only suffer one or two deaders.

BTW, I also noticed that bst pets work very well in the event that mob gets a gate off - they will charge after it and generally succeed in occupying its attention long enough to postpone the first summon and allow evac to hit. Just get that evac off the instant the mob gates. You can always fight your way back.

You should not have folks that are kos near the door at all. You should put casters on one side of that middle wall (opposite where you fight) and have them poke out to nuke/debuff/dot. Then switch sides when you pull the other guardian.

Also, I find the left hand side much easier to work out as fewer roamers and I always get singles, right hand side seems slightly harder as you can end up with 2/3 pulls very easily.

Also, keep in mind that you can pin a mob in any corner and place casters right around that corner and beat the snot out of them and avoid the aoe on the casters. Just be a little creative and you'll find tons of spots, thats what this dungeon is all about. You can also just sit and take the AOEs, but with just a single group, it is much wiser to conserve.
