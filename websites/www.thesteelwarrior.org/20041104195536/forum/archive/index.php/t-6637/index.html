Battleblade
Jeanete,

GSoT is easier to acquire probably than CHoS/SoTB.

Don't overlook the fact that to get the most out of these weapons you need aggro aurguments. Two Anger 3 augs will cost you 2300 adventure points or ~46 hours. In contrast, one Force of YKesha aug for a GSoT will cost you ~15 hours. Even more time if as I suspect you don't get 51 AP's per completed adventure.

Getting augments is a major time investment. One you don't want to repeat if you don't have to.

IMO if you are going to be playing for quite awhile, go with the CHoS/SoTB. Put Force of YKesha on the CHoS and Strike of YKesha on the SoTB. If you later acquire a BF or BoC you can retire the SoTB and put Anger III on your new weapon. This along with Aten gloves when you get to VT should hold you just fine until you can get a BoW.

On the other hand if you are not a so-called "power player" get a GSoT and put a Force of YKesha aug on it. This is much cheaper in time and effort and will with a little effort be quite servicable.
