Equipment: more HPs on your shoulder and face.

Weapons: swap out your offhand for a CHoS or SotB, for better aggro (attack rate and proc rate).

AA? Levels before AA: the XP comes in so much faster once you can do BoT groups, it's not even funny. Once you're getting AA, my sincere advice is to take defensive AAs before offensive AAs, and mitigation/avoidance AAs before HP-increasing AAs.

If you MT raid mobs a lot, however, you're better off getting the HP-increasing AAs first, and the mitigation/avoidance AAs second. But if you're XP grinding, or doing lots of LDoN, you'll benefit more from mitigation/avoidance than from pure raw hit points.

Good luck,
