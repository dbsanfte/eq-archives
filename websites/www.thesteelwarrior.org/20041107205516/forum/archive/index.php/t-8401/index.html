Incite is a disc that adds hate + hate DoT on your target. You can buy the disc in EC tunnel. Hand it in to any warrior trainer to learn it. Same goes for all of the old discs. Since you are level 57 you will probably use Bellow instead of Incite.
Sassinak
There's a new "Combat Effects" window accessed using Alt-C. It's basically a "spellbook" and 8 "spellgems" for disciplines. Your "spellbook" will be empty until you buy the "tome" for each discipline, and then give the tomes to a warrior trainer. The tomes are all sold in the EC tunnel, and also in Plane of Knowledge. You'll see other classes' tomes on the merchants too, so be sure to get the warrior ones.

Brabble
In PoK in the library on the same balcony as all the clerics is a Barbarian NPC named Heldin Swordbreaker. His /loc is n100, pos 1110, n28. He sells the warrior /disc tomes. All the early ones cost essentially nothing: 1 copper-ish. Buy the tomes from him. Take them to any warrior trainer & you have the discs.
The discs have been "unlinked" as poster above describes; they are now on various timers. You can't use more than one disc at the same time, but you can use them one after another as long as the discs are on different "timers" and you have the "endurance" for it. That's what your "endurance" bar is for, by the way. Also you will notice that the refresh times for discs have been changed. As posted above, ALT C gets your disc window to pop up. You can see what discs you have, and you can make hotkeys for them by clicking the "make hotkey" button (sorry, guess that's well into the "duh" category).
(Off topic, but if you hold your pointer over your regular buffs now, you can also tell how much time is left on each buff).

Timer 1
Evasive (lvl 52) 3 minute duration, 7.8 minute refresh.
Defensive (lvl 55) Three minute duration, 10.5 minute refresh
Stonewall (lvl 65) Three minute duration, 10.5 minute refresh

Timer 2
Furious (lvl 56) Two tick duration, 40 minute refrsh.
Fellstrike (lvl 58) Five tick duration, 25 minute refresh
Fortitude (lvl 59) Two tick duration, 40 minute refresh

Timer 3
Charge (lvl 53) 5 tick duration, 25 refresh.
Mighty trike (lvl 54) 5 tick; 25 minute refresh
Precision (lvl 57) 5 tick; 30 minute refresh
Aggressive (lvl 60) 1 minute duration, 30 minute refresh

Timer 4
Focused will (lvl 20) duration 1 minute, 5 minute refresh
Resistant (lvl 30) 5 minute duration, 30 minute refresh
Fearless (lvl 40) 1 minute duration, 30 min, refresh
Spirit of Rage (lvl 61) 2 ticks, 30 minute refresh
Healing Will (lvl 63) 1 min, duration, 5 minute refresh

Incite line (adds a clump of hate instantly, then adds smaller amounts over time). I forget the refresh time on these, but they come back fast--you'll be able to use them every fight, sometimes more than once, depending of course on the length of the fight.
Provoke (lvl 20) 100 hate + 40 hate/tick, 500 hate total
Bellow (lvl 52) 200 hate + 50 hate/tick, 700 total
Berate (lvl 56) 300 hate + 60 hate/tick 900 total
Incite (lvl 63) 400 hate + 70 hate/tick
