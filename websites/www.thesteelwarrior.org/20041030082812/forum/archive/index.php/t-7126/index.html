Its ratio isnt bad, the stats are horrid, but the proc i think outways that. I xp in earth so using two handers can significantly cut down on rip damage taken, which is better for your healer than more raw hp from the weapon because its more consistent.

I only lose aggro when someone else is careless or really really wants it, i dont punch taunt either, i only use incite for initial burst aggro, i believe that anger procs are the safest aggro, so using a weapon with one and aug'ing with ang3 is the best all-around way to go.

Again, yes the stats suck, but until you get a BoW this is a great all-around tanking tool.
