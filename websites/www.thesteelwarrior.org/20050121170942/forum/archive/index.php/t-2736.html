I apologize if this reads very simplistically.

Go to the magelo homepage
click on "Characters" from the left hand menu
(at this point you should see the screen with your characters and more options like "Manage this character's equipment" and so on)

In the middle of the page, you'll see a URL (http://www.magelo.com... That sort of thing.)

Use your mouse to highlight the entire link, the http all the way to the numbers at the end.

Once the whole thing is highlighted, right click the highlighted link.

Choose COPY and go to whatever page you're trying to insert the link to (example: your member profile page here)

Right click where you're inserting the link and choose PASTE. This will paste your link.
