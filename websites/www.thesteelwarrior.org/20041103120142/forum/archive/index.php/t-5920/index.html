paladiin
The math on the CHoS and SoBS show that they are virtually identical in terms of agro. General consensus is that it really doesn't matter what hand the droga wepons are placed in - you won't notice a difference.

On the 2H, yes a 2H weapon can hold agro as well as dual wield, depending on the weapon. However, the GSoT is a) an excellent agro weapon for where you are in the game, and b) nearly identical to the droga weapons in terms of agro. In addition, you'll be hard pressed to find better than the GSoT for a long time. If you want to go 2H, go ahead and equip the GSoT - you'll likely see no real change in agro (which is good). What that means is you can go 1H or 2H depending on the situation and you'll be that much more better of a warrior for having flexibility (I find that 2H weapons are great vs. hard hitting mobs, the 1H combo is great when I need to grab faster agro in LDoN's).

Best upgrade option for the GSoT? Hard to say - if you want more DPS try getting a Greatstaff of Four Winds and augmenting it with Anger III. Otherwise, you can put a Ykesha proc on your GSoT for more agro.
