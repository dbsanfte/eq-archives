As someone else posted, bring a mage with Command of Druzil along.

In fact, have the mage enter Flame and grab a pet, then intentionally fail the trial. Keep in mind that mobs in "flame" will lose agro and head for the stake if you get too far away from them. This makes it extremely easy to charm one mob, then outrun agro and let the remaining three mobs sit on the stake long enough to fail the trial. When the mage says "return" to the Guardian of Justice, he'll keep his pet. Let charm break on the trial platform, malo/tash and recharm. Now the mage can buff his pet (haste, summoned weapons) and take it into any trial, or anywhere in the zone.

Let me tell you, a fully buffed flame elemental from the flame trial can kill other trial mobs solo faster than a high DPS group of 6 can. Try it, you'll like it!

Our CoD mage can pass pretty much any group through any trial just because his pet kills the trial mobs in seconds. It's really insane.

I've used 2 different methods of beating trial of flame.

1. With a VERY good cleric, who can keep you alive AND with low HP aggro, you can just tank all 4 and never lose aggro.

That's how I beat it the first time.

2. The easier way was...

2 Warriors
1 Paladin
1 Bard
1 Cleric
1 Mage

Drink Shaman potion and get DS'd up before going in.

Warriors, Paladin, and Bard all stand at mob spawn points, aggro the mobs, and bring them back to the bard, who is tanking his own mob. Do NOT slow the mobs, each warrior takes turns /shielding the bard, who is singing DS song and AE DoT song. THe mobs will melt from DS damage.
