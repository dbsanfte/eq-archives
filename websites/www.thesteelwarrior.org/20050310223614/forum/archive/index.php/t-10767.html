It's update time again soon, need feedback on replacing item #2, and possibly item #5 (bandolier solves this nicely) and possibly #9 due to Chaotic Strike augments.






The current "Top 10 list" is:


1. Taunt. Success rate, particularly the steep decline vs. Yellow/Red mobs, is too low and too unreliable (currently being evaluated) - Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning

2. LDoN, Hate Augments, and Warriors: There really needs to be some dropped hate generating weapon proc augments. It's not only mobs who hate us after having spent days/weeks grinding out obsolete, unsupported expansions to "complete" our weapons. Our guilds do too. All other augment types found in LDoN are found elsewhere in OOW or even tradeskilled, even some weapon augments are dropped (Such as Taskfiend Caikip's lifetap augment). (currently being evaluated)

3. Endurance Regen: We need endurance regen from Spells and/or items. Modrods should provide an endurance benefit equal to the mana benefit. Also, change + Endurance only augments to +Endurance regen. (currently being evaluated) - Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning

4. Re-Evaluate warrior disciplines. (currently being evaluated) -- Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning
There's a few issues:

A. Endurance costs on all of the new discs is WAY too high

Update: At the Fan Faire, during the Q and A, Rytan said that he'd be willing to look into this when discussing how the endurance system was created when he pointed out that the costs on the older discs to justify not adding endurance regen into the mix of things, but the new disciplines are significantly more costly with a lower "return" than the older discs, and their costs needs to be looked into.

B. Aura of Runes negates the use of inherent proc right clicks. Please consider "suspending" them rather than dispelling them completely.

Update: Warriors report that it's not firing as often as they would expect, particularly for the Endurance cost

C. The level 70 discipline needs to be effective against even con and higher mobs. If it's not reasonable to make level 70+ mobs stunnable (and it's probably not reasonable), then change the effect to a dramatic defensive based damage shield (DD) effect, or a "defensive" hate inducing proc, or something along those lines. As it currently stands, there's not a whole lot of use for the discipline which SHOULD be our best.

D. Savage onslaught needs a longer timer or shorter refresh and a MUCH lower endurance upkeep.

Update: Please let us know if the "Cleave" effect is working with Savage Onslaught.


5. Range attack while pulling (currently being evaluated) - Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning
Note: The proliferation of ranged items which eliminate the use of a bow or thrown item without awkward item swapping, it is requested that the 'Throw Stone" skill be unlinked from the incite line, and a longer range placed on it (100ish feet)


6. The following adjustimets to /shield to make it more useful. (New)
Since it already has significant limitations to it's use (duration/reuse/no disc usage) please reconsider the "damage penalty" aspect, to make it 50/50, or based on the warrior's mitigation, not the target's.
Assist shield: Please enable the ability to simply assist the NPC we're fighting to perform the /shield operation. The current process is too clumsy.
Give it a real hotbutton, not a text command.

7. Mob Summonning Issues: Please make it so that mobs summon rather than in the exact center of their hitbox, in front of them. Being summonned "out of melee range" and turning the mobs all over the place is incredibly frustrating, or better yet make mobs who have been attempted to have been taunted in the last 10 seconds stop summonning. The concept that we're standing in the face of a mob attacking and taunting should be clear indication that we're not trying "to evade" the mobs. (currently being evaluated)

8. Melee Focus Effects: The current level of melee foci are too limited. There are no mage summonned melee foci, and melee foci were not retroactively placed on older gear. The current list of foci is also too limited in what it acheives when compared with caster foci. (currently being evaluated) - Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning

9. "3rd attack" which performs a melee interrupt which is not subject to the weak kick miss rate, such as "Crippling Strike" (At-Will Crippling Blow once per 6-12 seconds or so) when using a 2 handed weapon, or 2 hand bash. (currently being evaluated)


10. Increase the pushback on "Press the Attack" and the chance to interrupt on it. (currently being evaluated) - Most likely this will be considered as part of the class re-envisioning
