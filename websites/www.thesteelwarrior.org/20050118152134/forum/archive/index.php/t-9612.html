Exp in WoS, RCoD, Instanced trials is really variable depending on the make-up of the group. But, you have the potential to upgrade a lot of slots between 66 and 70. With that in mind, I would experience in Omens exclusively. Lots of plusses to it.

1. Get your loyalist faction up as you level. Know what drops you need for the quest armor and collect em as you can.

2. Some very solid upgrades from trash mobs. Blue Flame Neck and Nimble Shoulders from WoS are good examples.

3. Decent cash loots from mobs while exp'ing.

4. Runes for cash (or to get groupmates their spells if you have a regular group).

5. Some good drops that will upgrade you come from the same mobs you gotta kill to get your Quest pieces. Terror Chain Ring and Bygone Butchers Cleaver for example.

6. Everyone seems to be in Omens now so easier to find groups.

Anyway, thats my plug for staying in Omens to level. It sure seems slow I know, but it will happen, hehe.
