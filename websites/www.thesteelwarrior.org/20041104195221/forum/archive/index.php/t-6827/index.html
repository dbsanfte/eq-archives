Hi sorry for this newb question but if a 12 20 wep has a +3magic dam on it, does the wep become 15 20 ? if so what mobs do you have to kill for the dam to change from 12/20 to 15/20
Paen
The mob will have a resist check every single hit determining how much of the mod is applied, if its a total hit then yes its effectively a 15/20 weapon, or 14/20 or 13/20 or 12/20 depending on the resist check each hit. Doesnt matter which mobs you fight, high MR mobs will obviously resist more though and any MR debuffs will benefit you such as tash or malos.
