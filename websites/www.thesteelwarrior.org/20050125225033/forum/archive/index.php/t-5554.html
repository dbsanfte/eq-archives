It is hard to quantify the value of items on FV because most everything is tradeable. On a normal server, 15k for SS gaunts it too high because they are a common drop. Also, we don't get the option of selling the finished product, we are stuck with a no drop, statless item that just takes up bank space. I would try to score some Warlord's Steel Gauntlets (LDoN), Onyx Gauntlets (ToV), or Gauntlets of View (VT). Ideally, you could get Gloves of the Crimson Sigil (VT) but it is unlikely anyone would sell them because of the click effect.

As far as a range slot, a War Bow of RZ or any elemental player made bow is a nice item to have, just a matter of getting some levels so you can use the elem bows.

Lastly, the best charm is the PoP charm (Intricate Wooden Figurine) once you get some flags. It's also free, so you can use your plats for other upgrades. Most charms suck ass to begin with so it only takes a few flags to make the PoP charm better than anything else available. I wouldn't worry about it too much for now, once you get 60+ you should be able to get several flags to upgrade your charm.
