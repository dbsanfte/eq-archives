hehe.. it's Avatar of War. ;) lol got a mental image of a huge bird. ;)

1) It's relative. I took him down with 2 groups and just 2 clerics healing me. But for someone on that level of content I would guess around 40-50, with 7+ clerics in in CH rot on the tank.

2) Pretty simple. Clear arena, pull Vindi(don't HAVE to kill Vindi but may as well), pull Statue, pull Idol(killing Idol un-slowed is a good test to see if you can kill AoW), pull AoW. Corner pin in either corner of the arena for all of them.

3) Pretty random. He doesn't have a huge loot table so your chances of getting one to drop are pretty good. I've seen 3 drop at once before.
