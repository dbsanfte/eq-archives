Everlord and Apolyon have made some very good suggestions.
While the SBoD is pretty good dps, I'd sell the damn thing.
And, meh, you have all the gear you need for the next several levels; the cultural stuff Everlord pointed out could well be your next upgrades.
I know it's tedious, but LDoN hate augs are your next stop. If you know anyone who plays a class that can single pull, and anyone who can do crowd control, and any cleric, you can easily tank hard LDoNs at your level with your gear. My 54 warrior was doing them with 100 less AC, 250-ish fewer hitpoints, significantly less haste, and a GSoT.
Politely ask your shaman to stand next to you when he slows & debuffs the mobs. If he wants to sit down after that, have him wait a round or two and then move back away from you and the mob.
Some Shaman are well enough geared at lvl 55 that they can take a few punches from experience mobs at that level. So, getting hit doesn't yet represent significant risk to them. Nevertheless, there's little point in making you run across the room to get aggro off of them and then re-position the mob with respect to other melee and pets, etc. The shaman might not be going splat, but it's cutting group dps, decreasing the shaman's ability to can efficiently, decreasing the efficiency of group healing mana, and making it take longer to kill the mob. At level 55 and with a tank as well geared as you are to take a punch, very very likely everything that is gained by getting the slow in that fast is lost by ganking your aggro. And if you are using the droga weapon set, then you are generating aggro about as fast as a warrior can at that level.
