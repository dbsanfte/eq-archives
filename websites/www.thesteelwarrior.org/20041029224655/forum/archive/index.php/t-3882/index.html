For really cheap, I'd suggest Greatstaff of Thunder. On most servers, if not all, I think they are at most a few hundred pp. With it's proc, it's also a pretty good agro wep. If you have more pp, a windblade is very nice *around 20k on Druzzil Ro, not sure about the other servers*, or a war marshall's bladed staff. Some inbetween choices include tae ew 2-hander, or widebladed greatsword.

These are all 2hand weps btw, as 2H does more damage than dual wielding.
