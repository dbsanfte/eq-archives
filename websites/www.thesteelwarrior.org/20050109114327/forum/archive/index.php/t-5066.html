01-05-2004, 01:10 PM
You can still make your own hotkeys for activating disciplines. You need to use /discipline evasive (all lowercase) and it will work as before.

If you use a custom UI or the default combat skills window you will see a discipline bar with five bubbles like the existing health, stamina or mana bars. This is a timer for the disc.

In addition, you can click on grayed out keys in your combat skills window, or your hotkey page if you added them, and it will give you the time remaining until the disc refreshes.

Hope that helps. Peace-
