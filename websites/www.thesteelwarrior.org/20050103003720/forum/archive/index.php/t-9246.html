I just have a couple hints for equipment, but nothing along the lines of what you may get in VT. If you're looking for some quick and easy upgrades until you get into VT more for items, I'd suggest getting a couple 1group items outside of raiding for shoulder/face. Since you're getting in VT, and looking at your Magelo, I think you can easily one-group them outside of raiding.

Face - Get a decent group and go to Siren's Grotto. Take a right after entering, under the water to the area beyond the wall. Go to the Sirens tower (up the ramp and across the bridge to the right) and find Mistress Latazura. Kill her as often as she spawns (2-3 days, I think). She drops a 20ac 55hp mask (Flawless Steel Mask) with nice stats all around, don't know on rarity. Very nice, IMO, until you get a raid drop better. You're certainly not hurting for hp (could be wrong), and I think all-around it's better than the Amygdalan Chain Mask even with the 30 less hp...has 12 more AC. (Not better than the Thulian Visage of Horror though)

Shoulder - I'd consider going to kill Vahksis the Shade in PoNightmare for the Horn Spiked Shoulder Plates. Much better than that Mantle of Ill Omen, should be groupable for you with a decent group. Looks good until a raid drop will replace it, and it's droppable.

I didn't see any augments in your so you may have these. In your off-time, get the 10ac augs (Brilliant Blood Ruby of Prowess, BB adv camp for 510 each) and stick them into your Shoulder/Face/Neck/Waist/Back items (one each). They can go in all types of slots, and a good easy AC upgrade (50 raw AC, ~80 modified I think, and can remove and move them to any new items you get). These are good even with VT gear...they just make the items better.
