Weapons obviously plays the biggest part, but Haste & Haste buffs also plays a large part. Once those bases are covered, raising ATK with Vengeance items and regular stats is about the only thing to do. Getting levels also helps raise offense skill cap, which also means a lot to damage, since it raises your ATK. You will see increases again at level 51.


That's about it.
Well, and there's AA's too, but getting levels > AA's


In your case I would ignore Vengeance items and such, since they are much too expensive for you. Getting a nice Haste item would be a good thing, better weapons is also good ... and levels offcourse.

On a side not, definitely start working on 2hb as soon as possible. Otherwise I can almost guarantee that at some point you will go, "Damn. I should have kept 2hb up." That's just how it goes.

Now if you're comparing your damage to other warriors of the same level, the main difference is going to be weapon damage. If they're doing more damage, they more probably just have better weapons. Melee skills and Strength play a very small part. Another factor is damage bonus. Take a look at this chart at lucy. (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/dmgbonus.html) For one handed weapons, the damage bonus is goes up with your level. On 2 handers, the player's level and the weapon's delay both affect the damage bonus on a weapon.

As Anker said, "Level level level"
