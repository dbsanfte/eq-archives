Just a lil head's up. It's pretty easy, if you kill fast enough, you even have time to Bandage yourself between fight's, and the last guy is cake. But BE SURE to bring a Healing potion with ya. Cause ya never know when the WoW Equivalent of EQ's "You can't see your target" is gonna bite ya on the ass for a few seconds, and you will be fighitng 2 or 3 at a time so the heal potion could be needed.

Typically, I use a sword and board. With this fight, I'd go offenseive with eitehr Dual Weild (if you have 2 high dps weapons for lvl 30) or pull out a big 2h'er.

THis fight is about fighting multiple Mob's at once and then a lvl 35 mob within your time limit(Which I found to be VERY generous), so your highest DPS/quickest rage generation option is the way to go IMO. Rage generation, and building alot of it early, was my main concern, so I went dual wield. I refrained from the temptation to execute the opponennt's once they tried to flee .. much better to save the rage for the next fight.

All in all, it's pretty easy.
