-In an exp group where a caster or some damn monk is snagging aggro. (I find it more convenient to taunt a mob off more successfully than having 100-200 more hps in an exp group.)

I'd love to hear of someone testing this somehow, but for now I believe in it. Have to believe in something!

These mods work like the mods for Tradeskills. You have the RNG (random number generator) spin your initial skill and then your mod is added to that number to give you your total modded number. So in the case of an Elemental bow you will hit more often and in the case of the helm you will taunt more successfully. Mods do not add hard numbers to your actual skill level, they only apply to the RNG generated number when you use a skill of any type (casters, melee, trade skills alike).
