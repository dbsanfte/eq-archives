I typically hold aggro on 2-3 mobs in this trial with no real problems.

Sometimes aggro has appeared to be slow to take hold to me, but I'm generally adrenelin pumped on group trials and when I say "slow" we're talking a second or two *maybe*.

I try to stand slightly in front of folks in hopes of getting as much incoming aggro as possible, then pick up the first one, hit attack, hit bazu, then switch immediately to a 2nd, get a successful taunt on it, then cycle through whatever is in camp, keeping future chain bazu to keep aggro off the shaman and cleric.

When I can get 4 in camp early I'll hit AEt because if your dps is up, that's the only time you'll have that many around.

Tell your people to stand at your shoulders so that mobs are constantly in range to you, having someone back off so that they are out of taunt range is the single greatest problem I find in keeping aggro on multiple mobs. My necro runs out of range all the time but I trust he can handle it or he'll run back.

Just my .02 worth, and good luck.
