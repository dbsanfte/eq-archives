Be proud of being Guktan!

Seriously, there is nothing wrong with Froglok tanks. We are a bit Charisma challenged, but that can be over come somewhat with Augs. There are some very good targeted quest, my personal favorite being the series of quests from Sugal the Fist which force you to raise all your combat stats.We have a slight Stamina advantage over the average, and we are very dextrous. My Froggy is a 62 warrior with 5.5k unbuffed and all combat stats and skills maxxed (except Agility, but i'm working on it) I've got almost 50 Defensive AA's and am called on to tank even over higher level plate classes. One of the biggest keys is keeping it simple. Don't switch targets and stay focused. If you're MT for a group set up the necessary Hotkeys (assist call, shield healer/slower, taunt, incite line) and do the job. If you're being groomedfor raiding then learn from thte Older tanks what to do in certain raids. There are different tactics for different raids, no matter what other classes think, we do more than just hit auto attack and go out for a latte.

The best way to improve your equipment at your level is probably LDoN's, it will also help with your money situation. The Death Embracer armor is pretty good and holds multiple augs, it also has good resale value when you are ready to upgrade.

You need weapons to level up all your skills. The most compelling reason for this is you never know what you are going to come across. Yes the vast majority of Warriors equip 2 1 handed weapons, but what if you get a great deal on a 2HS? You want to be able to make use of it. Try to have situational weapon combos, learn which mobs riposte for a lot of damage, when you tank those, use a 2HS or 2HB.

I've had a blast playing a Froglok (I RP Gukta as a Holy Communist State, it's all sorts of fun). As a Guktan warrior you have a size adavantage in cornering similar to a Gnome's and you have both high swimming and innate lung capacity.... which honestly don't help much... but we are by far the cutest animations in the game...
