I got my information from Allakhazam. Just look up Shadow tipped acrylia arrow, or flame tipped or the other one thats + cold damage and youll see the recipes.

Im at 210 skill now and have been making shadow tipped acrylia arrows until I get to 250. To make the arrowhead you need a chunk of condensed shadow, water flask and a file. This is trivial for a smith at like 130 (forget now), but if youre making the shafts already this should be no problem. Then combine the shadow arrowhead, with the acrylia shaft and a large nock, and round cut fletch to make the arrow.
