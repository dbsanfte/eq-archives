The reason there is no aggro lock from it, is because aggro is probably a composite of things such as :

- pure hate (think incite, SK +hate spells, EB and anger procs in this area, pure hate, no damage or other effects)
- range bonus/penalty (you get more aggro when you are near it)
- damage potential (those who hurt most, gets more aggro .. DUH)
- probably a few things more.

If you want good aggro, you will want to hit as many areas as possible, as early as possible.
Incite only covers one area.
I think Slows covers 2 areas, pure hate & effect.
Same with snare and stuns probably also pure hate + effect.

That would explain why some still grab your aggro now and then.
