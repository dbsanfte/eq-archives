Actually, we've never had a class balance forum at a Fan Faire. The reasons for that should be pretty obvious. No matter how well balanced a game is, there's still room for improvement. There's no pretty way to host a forum like that. Tom (the Fan Faire guy) wrote out a sample schedule that had that in it. I immediately told him that there was not going to be any such thing. Somehow that sample schedule was used, not the real one, on the folders that were handed out.

No, a "Class Balance" forum at a Fan Faire would be like the proverbial long-tailed cat in the rocking chair factory.
