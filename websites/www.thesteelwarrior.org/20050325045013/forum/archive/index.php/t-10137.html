It doesnt Glat. Duel wield is pretty decent now for MS build because it does build rage up pretty fast. I am axe speced also so my crit % is up to around 13% atm. A good side effect for duel wield against red mobs is that overpower lights up a LOT when you face them. I would duel wield till overpower lit up, equip a slow 2 hander, and the overpower them.
