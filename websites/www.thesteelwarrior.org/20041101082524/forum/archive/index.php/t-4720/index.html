Superchum
The lifetap proc augment?

Yeah it's worth it. It taps life and you get hit points back.

It's NOT an aggro proc.

But it's useful. I know a couple of people who have it, and a lot more who have the cheaper version. It's not too popular among warriors since they usually save their points for hate procs, like Anger or Ykesha.

I guess to better answer your question, I'd have to ask two questions of my own:

What were you looking to put it on?

What type of use are you hoping to get out of it?

If you're looking for some added DPS and some extra HPs recovered while fighting/tanking mobs then yeah, I think you'll find it useful and worth the points.

If you're looking for an aggro boost, lifetaps probably aren't the best place to start.
