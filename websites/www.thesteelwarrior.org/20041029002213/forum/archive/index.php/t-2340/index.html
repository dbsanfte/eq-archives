Aeryl
What am I doing wrong with my calcs?

Quite simply, you're relying on them too much. You can crunch all the numbers you want, but that alone isn't going to win you any fights. Instead of trying to find the lowest threshold you can win at, go out and improve your guild's equipment and skills. Tell your clerics to work on shawls, make sure they have CT boots, have them get the SCM and MC AA skills, etc. Failing that, get more clerics and stop using 51s in a chain. I don't know where you're at in terms of raid progression, but if you're just starting out and you can only muster 2 clerics and a 51 for your raids, you're going to be in trouble.

1ludor
if you do a 2 sec CH chain(cleric counts 2 then hits ch), its really 4seconds in real time due to latency. otherwise you couldnt get away with using 5 clerics for 2sec CH chain. you can see this for yourself by looking at logs.

in fact the fastest you can do real time CHing is about 2seconds, even if cleric2 casts as soon as cleric1 says go. if there is any lag, it may even wind up being as much as 3-4 second delay realtime.
