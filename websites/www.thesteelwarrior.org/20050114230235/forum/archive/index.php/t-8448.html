Although I too think Berserkers have no real "best thing" role (and that they were probably severely crippled by the scrapping of the "opening system"), in any given case I think a guild should ask itself a few questions:
1. Is it ever the case that you could fit an extra dps hand in a raid?
2. Is the player playing the Zerker good?
3. Does the zerker require little if any backflagging?
4. Does the player's personality fit with those of people already in the guild?

If the answers to all four questions are yes, then you might want to hesitate to reject his application merely because at the moment berserkers don't seem to do anything "the best." Very soon players will have 5 new levels, there will be a new expansion, and SOE has been asking warriors what new AAs they might want. It's possible, of course, that berserkers will get no love at all for years, but it's also possible that they'll get something at some point that will make them fairly handy.
