Puresteel mantle off some named in air you run into while doing a ring.

40ac 20str 15dex 15stamina 135hp 20cold 20magic 10 poison
clickey shield of pain 5sec cast time
warrior only

probably will be cheap being warrior only, but i'd wager it's the best shoulder item you will see till time.

Some other items you could get
1. a full set of raex
2. some real aggro weapons to put aggro augs on (iffir wtf?)
3. Leaf jointed cloak from earth
4. Fang of flame from fire

... there are lots of stuff you can get, but honestly i would look to Hard Ldon for some easy nodrop upgrades such as the follow slots in my magelo:

-ear
-neck
-eroded royal facemask
-my ring (lost elven)

There are several upgrades you can get from single groupable content which are significant upgrades. Upgrading these lesser/minor slots now means you save dkp later and get to spend it on what really counts: Raex, raex, and more raex.

If you want a quick purchasable upgrade look at Fierce Heraldic Pauldrons. They are better HP wise then what you have now and the same AC. They will last you until you get the ones from Air ring.

They are cultural so you can buy them in the bazaar.

Farm Dozekar and pick up Pauldrons of the Deep Flame (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=31472) You can do it in your spare time with a single group, as well as getting some of the other HoT quest pieces like the Silver Bracelet of Speed. The shoulders are very decent, probably the second or third best pre-Time, and the SBoS is also very decent as both a wrist and a haste item. It's always good to keep your haste options decent and replacable.
