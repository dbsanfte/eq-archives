There are of course other options. You could do anything from the half pipe of the Hole to HS to Veksar to CT until 65 if you really wanted to.

The fastest XP (meaning most consistent as well) to 65 will be in POV/POS/BOT.

POV is usually the fastest since it requires the least amount of brainpower and therefore usually has a group to join if they will take you.

Getting from 60 - 65 in POV takes a week of exping 5 hours a day.

BOT would be faster when you reach 62, but your likelihood of getting one of those groups to accept you goes down.

Personally, I think PoD Courtyards to 61, then POV to 65 will be your fastest route.

If you can swing a PON guardian group or a POV group at your level, you should take it.

There was a guy on Drinal who hated the idea of POP so much he exp'd to 65 in Cazic Thule while farming stuff.

It took him about 5 times as many hours as anyone else.

He still got there.

This all depends on group makeup, btw. If you get a rocking group in HOH or CoD, they can exp you faster than POV. But all in all straight easy LFG to Group to XP and stay there all night,

The caves in Plane of Valor are hard to beat.

Just my 2cp on how things work on Drinal.
