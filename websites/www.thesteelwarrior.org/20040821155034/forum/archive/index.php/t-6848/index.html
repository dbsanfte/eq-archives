Battleblade
I have regretted race/diety choice.

With sufficient resources and binding in PoK AFAIK (please correct me if I'm wrong) most of the downside of choosing DE, Ogre, and Troll are gone.

I believe it's possible to get the necessary faction to do the Coldain Ring quests for example, something that would have been unthinkable if the quests were in Kaladim.

Difficulty in cornering mobs can be overcome with illusions and shrink.

Frontal stun immunity and superior class/diety armor are pretty big advantages especially for casual players and twinks that may not advance to the point where they become less significant.

Special items that you may tradeskill also make some race/diety combinations more fun to play.

At level 20, I really would look into this and get some feedback. With sufficient equipment you can easily make level 20 again. At level 65, as a Dwarf agnostic, I am very aware thaat there is nothing I can tradeskill in game that is unique and worthwhile. Most of my tradeskill effort is made by my level 56 Halfling Druid (Karana) - perhaps not the best choice for a tradeskiller, but infinately better than a Dwarf agnostic.

With the Luclin and PoP expansions, faction from deity is pretty much a moot point. The PoK books are ALL in out the way, safe locations. Faction really doesn't matter unless you are questing. (things like Velious Armor, 8th coldain ring, Loyalist's Shield, etc.)

If you aren't foreseeing yourself getting to the point where you could get your upgrades from raiding, go ahead and change to an RZ or Tribunal worshipper. Those 2 suits of armor (Ogre RZ and Ogre CT armors are darned nice, too) are an excellent investment and will last you to 65 and beyond.
