The problem with the mage spell (Talisman of Return) is that the summoned talisman is no rent, so you have to get a new one every time you either use it or log out.

Also, it requires Quintessence of Knowledge which (unless they have changed it recently to be a vendor buy) requires a shaman to make it anyway, and it takes about 100pp in components.

So... to use the mage gate, you have to find a shaman to make Quintessence of Knowledge for you. You need to carry these with you. And then you have to find a mage to summon the talisman for you every time you play.

So, you can either find a shaman and buy gate potions, or you cvan find a shaman and buy Quintessence of Knowledge , and then find a mage to summon a talisman of return.

Why add the extra step? It's not any cheaper, as Potions to Bone Field cost the same as Quintessence of Knowledge .

As others have said, thurg potions are the way to go. Or just buy shaman potions.
