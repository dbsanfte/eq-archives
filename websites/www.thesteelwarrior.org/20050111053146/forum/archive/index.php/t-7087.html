My favorite Raid Leader AAs -

1) Raid Health (+100hp)
2) MA Assignment
3) Target's Target

For those who haven't experienced it yet, Target's Target is fucking COOL. It adds a second target window that allows you to see the health status of the Target's Target. So, for instance, if you're fighting a boss mob, the Target's Target will show the MT's health. This is fucking NICE for tanks to watch the tank line up. After all, sometimes there is hesitation because you're not always sure if the tank died or not. If you're a cleric, you can target a mob and it'll show you who they're beating on. Basically, it's clicky ASSIST, since after all, you can target the target's target through the window (say that ten times fast).

It's also badass because you can click on a mob and see if it's being tanked or not. This is ESPECIALLY useful when you're in a zone like Vex Thal where 6-8 mobs in camp is the norm. Instead of playing agro games with other tanks unknowingly, you can simply click a mob, see who's tanking it, and if it's beating on a caster, you can have at it. If it already has a knight/warrior targetted, you can move to the next mob and see if it's being tanked or not.

It's badass and the moment my guild leader trained it, it made big pulls 100x smoother. Tanks fighting over mobs is now nonexistant.
