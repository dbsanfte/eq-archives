You're question is a little confusing. While you're using the Furious disc, your riposte rate becomes 100%. When a riposte fires off, you take no melee damage from the mob and execute a normal, 1 swing attack. You can still hit or miss, or get your swing blocked/parried, etc. I also believe you can get riposted off a riposte, but not too sure about that. I've noticed sometimes when I'm attacking from the front but not on aggro, I'll get hit once for no reason. Might have something to do with him riposting me and I tried to riposte back, or something to do with Tactical Mastery not working quite right, not really sure.

Last time I checked, if you attack a mob that is Enraged while using the Furious disc, the two effects cancel each other out and neither you nor the NPC will riposte. This seems to change every once in a while, though. I remember way back when during a Sontalak raid where I used Furious through his enrage, and got spammed while we just riposted each others' attacks non-stop without either of us actually landing any hits until I died to an AE.
