Couple of other comments to add:

To start have everyone bind outside the Tactics zonein in PoT.

Run in get the left hand trigger mob for TZ/VZ and mezpark him back around the other side. Kill the two of them (Lumpy and Bumpy as the guild sometimes calls them) at the same time. Have your TZ tank all set to go as he'll pop just after the 2nd -umpy goes down.

The Tallon/Vallon fights are actually all about aggro control. Nice and easy from range on Tallon with your uber-est knight tanking him, (if a wizard over nukes and gets summoned or even worse, brings Tallon into the raid... guildremove him) while your mellee types start clearing across the faux RZ room to clear a path to Vallon. Pull Vallon as soon as feasible after Tallon. Again, he casts an aggro debuffer, so folks need to take it easy. Position your two Vallon split tanks between the raid force and Vallon one, as they always boot it for a cleric when they spawn. Being there waiting is easier than chasing, IMO.

Check buffs/do a quick med whatnot as you move into the faux RZ room. Finish any clearing of the far side hallway near the door.

Get a wizard to cast TL on everyone. No one accepts, they just move the box off to one side on their screen.

Get RZ going in a corner, melles at max melle range to avoid AE rampage. You will get adds thru the VZ side door (this is why you cleared any leftovers b4 pulling RZ) kite, mez, whatever. Just have to get RZ down to... 60 or 50 and he poofs. The second he does, everyone takes the teleport.

You now have 20 mins to get RZtW down. Need lev and invis to get into the far left corner of the pit. (guess thats... NW? don't know if the corridor up to the pit is actually headed north or no...) The key here is to delegate. Get your head chanter and a puller set up before hand to deal with getting some pets. Have a summoner with raid leadership ready to swap people in and out of groups to get anyone who died to the fake RZ room back and mostly looted (rez later). Everyone should be responsible for their own lev and invis. Tell them to get potions as you don't want to blow 5 minutes fucking around with getting that some slowzoning eejit mellee type leved and invised. The first few times you try this, the period of time right after the faux RZ kill will be a clusterfuck and there's no way one person is able to get all the instructions out.

Others have mentioned actual fight tactics, but I agree: the key is DPS, DPS and more DPS. So don't use anyone who has DPS as a kiter. (i.e., extra warriors, knights are your kiters. I think at one point on our successful try, one of our bards actually had all of them chasing him) Kick his ass fast, seabass and your headaches diminish by at least an order of magnitude. In the original PoP, this is one where you brought 72. These days with levels and better gear, we did it with just under 50. If it takes you longer than one defensive to do the 1.2 million points, you're probably fucked. And quicker is better.

Oh yeah... can do the TL thing again on real rallos. You can also click out of the zone once he's dead. The projection sees invis too. The adds don't despawn immediately so someone will have to drag them off to a corner somewhere. Can't remember if the adds see invis

Be ready to try it several times. If your guild kills RZtW first try with a force of people who havne't done it before... I'd say it'd be an EQ first.

I like the idea of the selo's group hanging outside btw. (The friggen adds move at SoE speed.) We're gonna use that one next time.

Still amazes me people say to use a knight for TZ.

The day they fixed the TZ & VZ coming together bug we went in and proceeded to own the event 3 times in one week. I tanked VZ/TZ/RZ/RZtW on all three wins and never died other then when training mobs away at end for others to get flag =P In other words you don't NEED a knight. Wars with solid aggro will do fine, in my case it was BF/Spike Driver at the time. Both anger 3 aug'd, WA5, and aten gloves clicky.

As for a strat...i like to keep it simple.


1) Everyone binds outside
2) Zone in and move towards triggers (otw there stop by the top of the pit and have Raid leader show everyone the corner they will be dropping down into, this will reduc e any possible confusion later)
3) Clear to first trigger on TZ side, snare it and have an enc bring it behind the raid as you get to the VZ side. Kill both triggers.
4) VZ pops a bit behind the raid if your setup in the corner. Slaughter him and pull TZ slaughter him and rez /buff FAST.
5) After 5 to 6 min, AE TL & pull RZ to the corner there and let your tank have about 10 to 15 seconds of aggro then all out DPS burn burn burn, at 65% have 2 wizards ( one close to RZ, one down the hall a bit) AE TL. Anyone who gets below 40% other then clerics and tanks must take the TL.
6) Once all out AE FoEagles. Zone them ALL(including dead folks) in and get those who died rez'd(need to be done with this by about 3 to 4 min) & buffed.
7) Drop down into the corner of the pit as mentioned earlier and have enc's rdy with pets etc, let everyone get to FM, you should be pulling him to the corner and engaging with about 10 to 8 minutes left.

NOTE:

- Use SK's & Pall'y as primary kiters since they aren't as much DPS and can take hits better.
- Rangers *BURN TS EARLY*, they are the secondary kiters and if the fight last long enough for TS to drop you prolly lose.
- Make sure bards are positioned well with wizards/mages/rangers/rogues etc. Use druid healers for those groups if at all.
- Once RZtW hits the dirt, RL makes a call "Kill Adds" / "Zone Adds". If you zone them get someone who has the flag to zone back in and round them all up and move them well away from the body and the flag mob. Good class for this is a ranja since mobs are fast you will wana get their attn with arrows not melee =P
-Enjoy your loots, and if your lucky the phat loot gods will shower you with BoWars
