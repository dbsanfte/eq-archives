Learn every weapon type. 2hs, 2hb, 1hs, 1hb, piercing. Use slam to get your bash skill up (troll, right?). When your skill maxes for that level, which should be your level times 5, plus 5, switch to a different weapon type. Don't worry if some get a bit behind, just do your best. You'll have more of a chance later when leveling slows down more. Stop using a shield when you get dual wield.

I've created new chars on a few servers since PoP. Generally the first thing i try to do is get a magical weapon for them. Take the Great Divide stone in PoK. Stand at the book and ask if anyone could spare one of the velium weps the giants drop. Eventually, you get a couple different weps which is good for learning skills. After you get a decent wep, head back to your home town and do the beginning armor quests for your class. It make take some time but you'll end up with a decent set of armor and lots of tradeskill loots to sell in the Baz. Keep all the Spider/ling silks, pelts, skins, swatches you find. If you are in need of bags, go to Shadowhaven, which is on the moon near nexus. In a small building near a couple tent looking things there is a gnome that sells toolboxes for 1gp. He's near the south side of the zone in the human area. You didn't say what race/diety you were. If you are RZ there are some better weps you can loot from Kael that generally rot b/c they are LORE. GL and enjoy the game. Ignorence is bliss really.
