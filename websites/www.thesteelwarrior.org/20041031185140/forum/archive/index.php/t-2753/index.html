Originally posted by zito
I'm sorry, I should have clarified. I didn't mean proc rates per attack round, but per minute, where factors such as haste don't come into play.

A couple of things affect procs per minute:

- Dex

- Anything that increases your chance to do double attacks or off-hand attacks. Increasing your chance to get extra attacks likewise increases the number of procs per minute. So Ambidexterity, for instance, should increase the number of off-hand procs you get per minute.

- Most weapons have the same base proc rate. Some proc faster than others, though.


That's basically it. Haste and weapon delay have no effect on procs per minute.
