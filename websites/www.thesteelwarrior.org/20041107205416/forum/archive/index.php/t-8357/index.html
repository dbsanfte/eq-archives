Curdlex,

Prior to being EP flagged (well, still ongoing really), I strived to find the best spot possible as well. One difference that can offset things one way or the other, depending on your view, is that I 6 box, so my group integration is tight.

Classes: 65 warrior, cleric, druid, enchanter, magician and necromancer.

In plane of tactics pits ~1 hour AAs. I found this to be, by far, the best place pre-EP for me. I would DC the lightblue pigs with the enchanter and druid which helps a bit. Sometimes I would DC the gnome at the zone in and fight my way down. Do not underestimate the DPS on that little gnome, I'd rather have him over a DC'able pig anyday. I have pretty consistantly pumped out 1hour AAs there and this was with skyshrine armor and some minor pop, non-visible slot items.

I tried plane of storms giants, halls of honor (quite a bit), some camps in bastion of thunder (never done back earth area), chardok B and find they all pale in comparison. Halls of Honor I would clear the 'named' legion camps as well as some of the surrounding armor suits and the XP was nowhere near as good. I will grant, my lack of a monk/bard means I need to paci which can eat into time, plus not keeping up the pace can prevent you from keeping the spawns 'broken' up properly. Supposedly XP in back earth is great, but I always find it camped and simply do not bother.

Keep in mind, all of the above are group centric. If you are going to solo with just a couple folks then things change as you care much more on single pulls, kite area, etc.

Personally, if you have folks who all have the tactics key (which can be obtained with a single group as well) then you can get some great XP.

As for the GoD zones, to enter Vxed you need a single party member that has killed Smith Rondo or completed the four sewer trials in order to enter that zone. This also gets you access to Tipt. I can say as a 8900hp buffed tank (clr/dru/self only) that vxed/tipt mobs put out some serious hits and I die a great number of times. However, through in an enchanter willing to charm a mob as a pet and things get easier and XP flows a great deal faster. However, even at my hp/ac, I cast CH a little before the mob engages me and sometimes I can drop before CH lands, so patching is a good idea.

The GoD sewer zones are decent XP but still challenging, definately more so than say PoTactics pits. You can expect to die more and will have travel time / deaths at a higher percentage than a 'lesser' zone.

If you have a good crew and an enchanter/snarer to do a charmed pet then vxed would be outstanding for you.
