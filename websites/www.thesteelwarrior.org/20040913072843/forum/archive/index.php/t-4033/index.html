Your sk will have similar hp to a warrior (within 600hp or so difference at the end with same gear on), but will be more versatile, hold agro better and get groups much easier. This will only get more pronounced as you hit the higher levels.

Warriors are broken atm, so much so that even in raids knights are used to tank when defensive isn't required more and more. Now's not a good time to make one if you're all alone in EQ, much as that sucks.

Of course, if you have a regular group who will hang out with you all the way to 65 and beyond, being a warrior can be very rewarding and fun experience; I would by all means recommend it provided you don't depend on pickup groups for exp. If you do, you're better off with the sk.
