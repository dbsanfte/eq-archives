Much to my dismay I discovered for myself the truth of how TM really works with respect to ripostes. There are two parts to a normal riposte: You miss the mob, and then the mob gets a free return attack on you. For example:

you try to crush soandso but soandso Ripostes!
soandso hits YOU for 500 points of damage!
--or--
you try to crush soandso but soandso Ripostes!
soandso tries to hit YOU but MISSES!

With TM here's what you start to see:
you crush soandso for 80 points of damage!
soandso hits YOU for 500 points of damage!
--or--
you crush soandso for 80 points of damage!
soandso tries to hit YOU but MISSES!

As you can see, it is difficult to tell if this is really a riposte because it looks like a normal hit/miss -- there is no riposte message. I verified this by standing in front of a fleeing mob that was snared and just standing there, I beat on it and took a few hits in return, clearly ripostes because fleeing mobs do not attack, but I saw no riposte messages.
