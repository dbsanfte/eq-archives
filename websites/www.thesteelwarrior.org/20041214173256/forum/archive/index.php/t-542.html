If you're looking for a spending path to become a marshall, here's one that'll do it with 24 exactly:

Area Taunt: 5
Physical Enhancement: 5
Ambidexterity: 9
Rampage: 5

That gives you the two Class based AAs a TON of warriors go for (AE taunt and PE for more HPs) no matter what.

It gives a useful damage boosting AA for your dual wielding setup (which alas, is less useful in PoP).

And it gives a toy AA to play around with.

It does all this in the minimum 24 points to get your title.
