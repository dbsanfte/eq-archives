The emperor weapons are The Sword of Ssraeshza and the Acrylia Handled Broadsword. Both proc lifetap.

Scimitar of Lifestealing drops in Veeshan's peak. Not too terribly hard to get since it drops off of the first dragon, but finding enough people that actually have the key is pretty rough.

Vyz'drah Render of Souls comes from the Cursed in Ssra.

Darkblade of the Shai'Din comes from Korukust in ChardokB, also a small raid).

Blood point is the only one that is really obtainable easily, or you can buy a LDoN lifetap proc augment for a weapon you already have, but that would take a very long time at your level.

Lifetaps are very uber, and that is probably why they are so rare.
