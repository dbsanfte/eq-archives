The Attuneable tag is basically SoE's answer for a few problems (and it is a good one, at least in my opinion. A beer to the man who came up with this (though I imagine he got it from another game anyway)).

The Attuneable tag means that the item is tradeable until equipped (for gear) or placed into a piece of gear (for augments). After that, the item turns No Drop forever.

This allows for a higher quality items to be allowed into the market without them getting farmed to the point that everyone can have them for little to no work. It's also a way for raid guilds that aren't entirely end-game to take some loot that would otherwise rot and either give it to someone else or sell it to buy something worthwhile.

Hope that clears up your question.
