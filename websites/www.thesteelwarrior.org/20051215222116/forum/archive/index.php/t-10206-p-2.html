This is the talent plan I've come up with to use on my warrior. I'm consentrating on high DPS for solo/PvP, but with some nice Protection abilities to tank Instance Bosses. I don't claim its perfect, or the ultimate raid tank plan. Its what I think will fit into my playstyle of Battle Grounds raids + Offtanking. Equipment should lean towards Sta/Agi/Str in that order. Primary weapon should be a big hurty two-hander with a Mace/Shield combo on switch. Here goes...

Arms Talents (33 points)

# Deflection - 5/5 points - more mitigation

# Improved Rend - 3/3 points - dps + unlocking "deep wounds"

# Tactical Mastery - 5/5 points - necessity

# Deep Wounds - 3/3 points - big hurty 2h will make for some free dps

# Improved Overpower - 2/2 points - This talent is godly, combine with "deep wounds" for teh win

# Anger Management - 1/1 point - necessity

# Impale - 2/2 points - more Overpower dps

# Two-Handed Weapon Specialization - 5/5 points - dps + unlocking "mortal strike"

# Sweeping Strikes - 1/1 point - AE tuant + dps across multiple targets

# Mace Specialization - 5/5 points - more stuns for you switch to mace/shield

# Mortal Strike - 1/1 point - best tier7 talent period


Fury Talents (5 points)

# Cruelty - 5/5 points - dps esp. when combined with "deep wounds"

Protection Talents (13 points)

# Anticipation - 5/5 points - mitigation even w/o a shield, unlocking "iron will"

# Iron Will - 5/5 points - less stun/charm for PvP and Elite PvE effects

# Improved Revenge - 3/3 points - stuns and more aggro when offtanking



Just food for thought if you are thinking of re-specing your War or starting a new one. At the moment my warrior has the first 7 "Arms" talents and is tanking well in instances and holding her own solo and in PvP.
