Varaho
This game has finally stopped being fun.

Some of you will rejoice, some of you may be sad, most of you won't care.

I suppose the only thing that kept me playing as long as I had were the other people. Friends, guildies, random people that I met day to day doing the grind. The low lives driving you to madness, the good guys doing great things to help you when you were down.

Strangely, before it became a seemingly endless grind, exp grouping was once my favorite part of the game. Going around doing new things while talking to new people is perhaps the most fun I have ever had from a game. Mobs could be interesting the first few times you saw them, but after a while they grow stale. People; on the other hand change even when they do not want to.

Goodbye, to all the people I met along the way. Those that would call me friend, those that would call me foe, and those who would call me "that crazy ass tank with the axe and huge lance!" This last drink of mine goes to one such person whom I will never have the chance to see, the very first of many people I met while in Norrath.

Here is hoping that you all have fun with whatever you do.
