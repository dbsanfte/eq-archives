Khabok
He is found in the Library area, past the locked door so you need a rogue to get to him. I am pretty sure mobs in there see through invis so you will need to clear to him, hard to say what group can do it. Also he has a PH so isnt always up, and he doesnt always drop the Anklesmasher when he is up.

Anklesmasher has the same proc rate as all weapons. But in a low DPS group, and with weapon affinity AA's it will usually proc during a fight. I use mine a lot when Duoing in places like Tactics or Droga. Start fight with the aggro weapons till the shammy can malos and slow, then swap anklesmasher in to main hand till I see it proc.
