First of all, get a good 2HS. DPS is much higher with 2HS, which translates into faster kills, and faster exp. Di'Zok Imperial Katana is reasonable, or Blackened Acrylia Greatsword, or Iceflame for piercing.

Second, no reavers, please. The ratio is tantalizing at low levels, but the damn thing isn't magic, and there are too many mobs it won't hit.

Spend most of your money on haste and weaps, and you won't be disappointed. Better haste is worth more than almost anything else you can buy. A RBG goes for only 20K on my server these days, or step up to the RBB (or even a CoF, if you are truly ambitious).
