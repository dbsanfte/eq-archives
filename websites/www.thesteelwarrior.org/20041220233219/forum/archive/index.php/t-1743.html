DI(not DA Ninja), is Divine Intervention. It is a cleric spell used in high level raiding that provides a chance to be healed if your hp's drop to zero. The charisma of the target affects the chance that it will work, hense people saying if you plan to do high end raiding, CHA is a good choice. If you do go high end raiding, CHA will be the hardest stat for you to raise, while STA will be easy.

5 in Dex is not a bad idea, keeping all 4 melee stats above 75 is a good thing. The way you are looking at going should work well for you.
