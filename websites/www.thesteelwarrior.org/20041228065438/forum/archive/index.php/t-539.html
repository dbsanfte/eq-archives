Cobalt boots and arms are both 25AC with clicky effects - str buff on the boots, minor shielding on the vambs so those are both respectable and not too expensive in the bazaar.

Champions (thurgadin quest) both pieces have 24AC and some minor stats. MQs for boots and arms wouldnt cost more than 1-2k each.

Crystal Chitin Arms and boots have respectable AC (arms 18, boots 20) and reasonable stats, but are usable by more classes so the price can be a little higher on these.

And ofcourse plenty of other options out there too, Enchanted Dwarven plate, the new Dorf Cultural stuff(will cost more but will last you alot longer than the other options), heavy / worked / reinforced dragon scale stuff from WW.
