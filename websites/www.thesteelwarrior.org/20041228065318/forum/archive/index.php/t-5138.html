Any tips on getting the saps to drop?

Yeah Kill everything. And find bottled patience and get drunk on it. Unless you farm in the middle of the night you will be dealing with around 3-5 kiting farmers. 1 of them may actually be doing the quest the others are just farming saps to try and sell at overbloated prices (don't buy from them please, let the saps rot on their trader mules~).

The biggest problem you will have is most of the farmer ted crew is rather unintelligent and will only kill spiders. When you kill a spider it only has at best a 1 in 4 chance of repopping as a spider. It's actually lower when you look at all the randoms and the fact that Flame Lord/lordlings have the highest spawn chance. You also have the DC pets and named which can spawn - all on the same table. But the farmers only being there for saps will only kill spiders and thus the spider population will drop. You need to kill spiders and other mobs to keep spider spawns flowing.

There are exceptions to the intellect of the farming crew, some actually will kill other spawns, just as some of the people kiting are just there to get exp and don't care what they kill but they unfortunately are the exception, not the rule.

I normally would dou there with my cleric for a few hours late at night while I was working on the spawn of satan (tailoring). It was doable with wavecrasher.

Btw I don't mean to flame the people farming the saps but when I watch these people sit on their butt for an hour or more waiting on a spider to pop and then they get up and go rush to it, it gets frustrating. Kill spiders first, but don't forget to kill other things so you keep the spiders flowing.
