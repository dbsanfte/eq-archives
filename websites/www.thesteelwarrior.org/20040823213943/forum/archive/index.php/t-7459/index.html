Battleblade
E'ci has/had a very successful public raiding organization - E'ci Public Raiding. I'm not quite certain if they have killed Quarm yet, but they are in PoTime. Afaik they have several tiers and maintain advancement in each.

E'ce has a long history of public raid begining with PoHate and some PoFear I believe. These raids took down targets like VS and Trak fairly early upon expansion release.

Many servers have alliances. Generally these are to allow friends and family or more casual guilds to progress. Some of these do cooperative flagging so guilds don't have to continually reflag as they recruit new members.

Also many servers have particular targets, like Vox and Naggy, that have become public raids. The number of sub-52 guilds has fallen off and without public raids these targets would probalbly not be taken down much.

Some of the raids are resounding successes, some of them wipe hard... It's kinda a mixed bag. About a quarter of the time though some idiot ninjahails the PP or ploots something.
