Good, now that we have a few more people saying it, it's easy to see what the deal is. It's all in the order that text is displayed. When you riposte a mob, you see your hit, and then you see that the mob tries to hit you, but you riposte. When you flurry, you see your extra hits, and then you see "You Unlease a flurry of attacks." When you proc weapons, the spell damage comes before the melee hit. If the spell kills the mob, then obviously the swing will never come since you don't do damage to a corpse.

It's not that you don't swing, it's that the mob is dead by the time your swing message would have come up, it's all in the order that messages are displayed.
