Well the ND advice was for dealing with AEs that were fairly common in places like ToV and mid and high level raids. Since only one tank can tank a mob a time, unless you knew that you were going to be MT, HP were the order of the day for helping reduce the pressure on the healers when dealing with the AEs.

Eoden
End of Luclin HPs (and as such ND) were king, now in PoP / GoD AC is king again so people are saying maybe CA/CS should be picked up 1st.

Whats really important is:

- You want all of these abilities eventually. Each individually doesn't do a hell of alot, but when you have all your tanking AAs you are a better tank than when you didnt. Whether you buy CS1 or ND1 now isn't going to make enough of a difference to really matter.

- Luclin AAs give better return on investment than PoP AAs. regardless of whether you think avoidance AAs, mitigation AAs or HP AAs are going to serve you better finish off ND/CS/CA+PE before you start working on LR/ID/PD

- In your mad rush to get all your tanking AAs don't forget to pickup Area Taunt when you can.
