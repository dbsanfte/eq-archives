You do have to bear in mind that there is a CAP on Stamina, whilst there isn't one on AC.

Consider the following:

1. You are unbuffed, and your Stamina is not maxxed. Yes, the 44ac 8sta legs do seem more attractive.

2. You are unbuffed, and your Stamina is maxxed. No, the 44ac 8sta legs won't be better than the latter.

3. You are buffed, and your Stamina is not maxxed. Yes, the 44ac 8sta legs do seem more attractive

4. You are buffed, and your Stamina is maxxed. No, the 44ac 8sta legs won't be better than the latter.

There is a point in game where you look at your equippment in another way than before.

1. You are mostly exp grinding, youre Sta is not maxed, youre mostly out of PoP = AC is not so important than Sta and HP.
2. You are raiding more and more and youre the MT or one of the MTs fighting more and more PoP Mobs .. AC is a point to look after. Youre Sta should be maxed ( buffed ). At that point sta is ok but not the nessessary part . So AC HP are better then.
3. You are raiding Mobs with damnd AEs or damnd single Target DD/Dots ... and youre hit quite often with that crap cause youre resist are so lala .. the resiststats will become more interesting so you look for HP AC and Resists ( Sta maxed when buffed ) maybe at that point also atk ( some clickies maybe too ).
4. Youre VT/Elemental .. mostly all you need is on the gear you can lay your hands on ( except of charisma ).
5. The DI is often not going cause of the lack of charisma on your gear and your extrem "high" natural charisma and you start to kill everybody in an Ldon when he tries to roll on an charisma augment. :D
