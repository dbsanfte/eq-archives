Futumsh
Spawnable every 3 hours. Intact power cell + Copper node + Bundle of superconductive wires to the gnome in the building in the northwest. Escort the gnome up to the northeast corner.

He hits for 1400, slowable, 3 different aes (dd+stun, dd, snare). I'd guess roughly 150k hps.

He doesn't become targetable until the gnome gets to him, so you can pre-arrange your people around him. Make sure you face him away from the door if you need factory flags (or the gnome will probably die to enrage).
Raunstonecutter
He doesn't enrage anymore, but the gnome will summon him, which is really annoying. Its a fun encounter, be sure to have some chanters running gnome control! Don't let anyone get in his way when he moves though, or else he will despawn. If you have a decent SA, then you and SA should be pulling mobs to raid. You can get to the dragon's room in about 30-45 minutes if you keep your pulling pace fairly quick.
