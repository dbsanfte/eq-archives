I've done this way to many times! Starting past Ansharu - When at bottom of ramp before pool, clear all see invis mobs, you may have to paci a few. Once ghost are cleared in front of the pool, paci any non-see invis mobs that might agro, levi and invis everyone (some may need see invis). Dive into the pool, swim down and west to the hole in the wall. Swim up the ramp, there will be a noc there but can't see you. I usually wait for everyone to get there. Once there you can make it to the AK room with invis. There will be one room with a cube and/or a ghost in the pit. If you hug the walls you will not agro it.

Once in the AK room get everyone on the ledge. Iv'e done it another way where I tanked them on the stairs in the corner but the ledge is more effective because if a caster accidently gets aggro it's easier to get to without worrying about an add from the passageway. Have casters stand out of ae rampage range. F8 target the stonemites and pull 1 at a time. I have the cleric HoT me first then I pull with an arrow. You WILL ALWAYS get a single pull if you target smartly and F8 target the ones closest to you. Don't pull til HoT hits, Cleric will get agro if you pull and hot lands as the stonemite is comming (that's been 100% all the time it's happened on my expeditions). Kill all the stonemites xcept the last one. Med to full. Pull last stonemite and when stonemite is at 5% tell EVERYONE to stand. They should not cast anything on you until you call an assist because when the stonemite dies the AK will spawn. I immediately hit F8 and pop an arrow into it. I don't bother with anything but defensive disc. It's hit me before for 1.4k w/o disc and around 6 to 700 with it. Have a chanter cast runes if you have one and cleric/ druids manage their heals. As long as you maintain control of agro and have decent dps you should win. ALWAYS fight on the ledge (keep levi on everyone).

I do this alot, normally to backflag guildmates. I max out at 7.4 - 8.1k hp depending on buffs available and have 63 AA. Normally balance out group with cleric,shaman,chanter, druid/dps and last spot dps type (two box with a druid). If it were not for backflagging, I wouldn't even bother since AK has never dropped jack when I've killed it. You will get a pre-flag though if you don't have it (zone out and go hail High Priest for perma flag) . I am not EP and have decent gear. It can be done my friends.
