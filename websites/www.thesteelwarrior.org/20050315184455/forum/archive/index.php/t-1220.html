At 51, switching to a 1:1 ratio 2hs will make a big difference in dps. I mean, at 49, my frosty/barnacle axe have a slightly lower than a 75pp weighty polearm (28/34 at my level). When I was using a reaver, my DPS jumped by 10-15% while grinding.

And, as stated above, you'll never come near a mage dps without the druid chain-nuking, but there are a few things she can do to narrow the gap.

Elder spiritist's vambraces- clicky level 34 DOT spell, it's mana free, but has an 8 second cast time on it. If she casts this at the beginning of the fight, then throws on the level 44 DOT and immolate, she's doing a decent amount of damage.

Starfire, the best 49 druid nuke, is pretty mana-efficient, and a few of those makes a nice difference.

Another way to save mana so she can cast more dots/nukes would be to get a mana preservation 2 item, and use the 16pt dmg shield, rather than the 24 pt one. At level 51, her newest damage shield doesn't last very long, and those things are mana hogs.

Last thing- be sure she keeps a strength buff on you. Strength = more atk = slightly more dps. every little bit helps.
