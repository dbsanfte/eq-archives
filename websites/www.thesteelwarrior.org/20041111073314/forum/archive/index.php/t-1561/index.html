Hi everyone, this is my first time posting here but I've been a long time lurker and have learned TONS from the people here - so first, I want to say "thank you" to everyone for all the help in making Blackbelly the warrior that he is today.

Just as an idea to fix taunt, what if SoE replaced the "Taunt" button for warriors with an "Engage" button. Same refresh timer and everything, just a different, warrior only, skill. Engage would be successful in putting the warrior on the top of the Hate list say 80-90% of the time. The downside is that warriors could only "Engage" one target at a time - meaning that the warrior couldn't just hit engage on all the mobs that come into the camp and let everyone else just AE the mobs (AE slows, AE nukes, etc.) - so when fighting mutliple targets everyone would still have to stay on their toes and watch things like healing aggro, proximity aggro, etc.

I understand that SoE doesn't want to trivialize end-game encounters, but if they can make mobs immune to charm and mezz surely they could make mobs immune to "Engaging".

This would fix a number of things, firstly it helps with the DPS issue - instead of having to get weapons that proc "aggro" generation spells we could get weapons that are great for DPS (Fleshgrinder, SoD, etc.).

Secondly, it makes warriors a viable group member again - we can lock down mobs and kep them on us the whole fight, that combined with the impressive DPS that we can add to a group now that we don't have to lug around Aggro Generation weapons anymore should really help us out.

Alright, well, that's my idea - please rip it to ribbons. ;)
