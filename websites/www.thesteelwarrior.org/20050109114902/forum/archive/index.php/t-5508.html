Even today OT and DL are good zones to get groups in, I think. That's where I leveled my warrior a few months ago, and even post-LDoN there are lots of folks around. Furthermore, warriors are in demand in my experience (at this level casters are beginning to see the advantages of someone else getting hit, lol). It's nice to go through 15-20 levels and never wait more than a minute and a half for a group (enjoy that while you can). Just bring your bow, hotkey shield, hold agro and position mobs and people in these zones will beg you to come tank for them.
The main disadvantages of these zones: they have no experience modifier and they are both wide open outdoor zones, and hence provide you with no experience fighting in dungeons or dealing with tough-to-break spawns.
You also, in my experience, won't get much practice grouping with chanters in these zones. So, if you level here and don't have a lot of experience with dungeons or with chanters, be aware that those are things you will have to learn later.
Especially in OT there are a good many players around who are not only just beginning to learn their class, but apparently just beginning to learn to be human beings. Expect classic pick-up group problems, heh. Oh, and do make sure that everyone knows what goons are, particularly if you're a warrior who doesn't like to zone before his casters are safely zoned. Remember once calling zone when a goon came by. One group member said, "Why? There's just one of them." Needless to say, there wasn't just one of them, and that caster didn't make it to zone, and neither did I, lol.
The reason these two zones are so popular is that fighting there is not especially difficult. More interesting and riskier zones like Splitpaw do have a modifier, and are more challenging. They are also completely empty, so you'd have to bring your own group. But you probably won't be able to convince a group to go to places like that.

If you're having trouble finding groups then you will need to find the popular zones for your level range.

/who all 30 35 lfg

will list where other people in your level range are located who are looking for a group. A lot of people don't use /lfg, so you might try:

/who all 30

A lot of formerly popular zones become deserted when new zones become available with better experience modifiers.
