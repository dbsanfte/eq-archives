Draganetti
You guys are going to be mighty surprised when you try to put 40hp aug on a ring, or better yet 40hp AND 10ac aug on a mask. Please be careful and fully understand the augmentation system before giving others advice.

10 AC augs will go on mask/neck/shoulder/belt/back

40 HP type 2 will go on all visible armor slots only.

40 HP type 7 will go on all visible armor slots only, except for wrist.

Personally, I made a takish 110hp bracer with 8% dodge and 7charisma. The breastplate slot may be the best for you to go with though, it sounds like.

Oh, if you want to add hps to a ring or mask or whatever, you can buy 20hp/20mana augments for the same cost of the 40hp one that i *think* will go into those slots. I personally am not gonna spend 500+ points for mana.
