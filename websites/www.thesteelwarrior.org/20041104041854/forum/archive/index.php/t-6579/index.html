Sassinak
If you're that intent on not using a Droga weapon, then just use the SBoD as-is in the offhand. Don't waste valuable LDON points on it.

I would rather see you spend your LDON points on building a spiffy-looking Malevolent Dirk of Deception from Miragul camp, and augment THAT, rather than augment a truly shitty weapon.

Probably 3/4 of your aggro comes from the mainhand, so your BOC will still result in decent aggro.

Just be aware that you are limiting your entire group's overall DPS by choosing the lower aggro of the SBoD. This means longer missions, slower XP, more attrition on raids, etc. all for the sake of your vanity. The difference WILL be noticeable to any DPS class.
