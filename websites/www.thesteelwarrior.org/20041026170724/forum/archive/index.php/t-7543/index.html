Grieg's End...

- Nothing see's invis.
- Nothing summons.
- Nothing runs.
- Good loot for tribute from the random named.

Invis up, hang a left u turn at first room pass the broken door, past that room, open the door, down the ramp, hang a right into the open room, take the ramp on the left into the big open room with like 9 static spawns. Pick a corner, break in the camp, then single pull all day.

I 2 boxed my 65 dru and 58 war there up to 62 and still doing it when I can't get a better xp group.

Mobs have a fair amount of hps, but hit like girls. Plus it's REALLY easy to box.
