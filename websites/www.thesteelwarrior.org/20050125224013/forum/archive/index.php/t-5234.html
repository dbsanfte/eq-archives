I enjoyed Champions of Norrath (CoN) and Baldur's Gate : Dark Alliance 2 (DA2) both.

However, I wish CoN used DA2's interface and ranged attacks. One of the major failings of CoN is that aiming is pretty damn difficult (unless there's a special method listed in the instruction book, which I didn't get with the rental). With DA2 you had unlimited ammo, and an "aiming" line for firing. With CoN, it's all guesswork.

However, CoN supports up to 4 players, and has a much higher replayability (since the levels just keep going up). DA2 probably ends when you beat the game, since there's not too much to get, ability wise, once you max out a few key skills on each char.

Lastly, CoN allows you to play duplicates of the characters - you can have 4 male barbarian warriors if you like. DA2 only allows one copy of each of the 5 pre-named, pre-determined chars to exist at once (thus your pal can't import his awesome favorite necro to your game if you're already playing the necro).

Man, if only these games would meld into one :(
