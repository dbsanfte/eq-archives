apolyon
race has little or nothing to do with it. just concentrate on baby steps as per hps/ac gear upgrades and invest your aa's in hps. the baby steps add up rather quickly, and you'll be amazed how much you can beef your hps in a relatively short time.

buy physical enhancement ... it is the best 5 aa you can spend. adds 2% to hps/avoidance/mitigation at the same time. once you do some more defensive aa's and make some gear upgrades, you can start farming hard ldon for nodrop gears. before you do that i highly reccomend 1 or 2 aggro procs though.

depending on the str of your friends, you may be able to farm GoD and Hard Ldon at present, but i wouldnt put you up to tanking them efficiently just yet.

a few nodrop goals to shoot for (relatively easy to get with help):
Stonemite carapace mask
Cape of Broken enchantements (17ac 90hps 36% haste)
Mana-Threaded bone bracelet (buyable)
Bloody Boots of Dark rites (buyable)
Warlords Steel Gauntlets (buyable)
Earing of Sand Tides
Lost Ancient Elven Ring
Trueborn Elite ring
Necklace of the Troglodyte

all of these come from hard ldon and GoD. with a bit of help from friends all of these should be relatively easy to get, but may take some time.

You gear is not that bad. Apolyon has some good suggestions, the only one I would skip is the mask, your mask with 20ac 55hp is pretty stout already. If I were you, I would work on AAs more than gear. Once you have LR5/ID5, PE, and a couple levels of Sturd, you should be able to tank LDoN hards with a good group. That will be the first step to getting the good no drop LDoN items. For now, crank the AAs in LDoN so that you can collect LDoN loot to use for tribute. This will allow you to constantly run max AC/HP from tribute which is a nice boost.

Once you are over 6500hp/1500ac unbuffed, you should be able to tank Tipt/Vxed named for even more loot/augments.

Ernie
You might consider replacing the valorium ring with a Steel Ring of Earthen Resilance. On Ayonae Ro they are down to about 60k in the Bazaar. Great ring :)

Also, if you are looking for a 2handed DPS weapon, you might pick up a WMBS. Works nice for raids where you don't want to draw agro and still turn out some decent DPS. Hit tactics for AA grinding or pick one up in the bazaar.

I also had the same back/waist combo as you, but decided to switch over to a CoF and Heavy Rubi Girdle. Sure you lose a bit of hp/ac from the back, but you make up for it more with the Heavy Rubi.

You might also consider getting a group to kill Veskhiz the Shade in Plane of Nightmare for a pair of Horn Spiked Shoulders... or look to pick up a pair from the bazaar (run around 60k on ARo where I'm at).

Oh and one comment on your AA... get AE Taunt! A definate must have. It may not seem like a big deal to you for regular, everyday EQing, but when the shit hits the fan, nothing like having a guaranteed taunt to save the day with in your bag of tricks!

Hope that helps!
