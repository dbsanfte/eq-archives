Phantron
06-24-2004, 01:17 AM
Ratio is more important for initial aggro than procs. You can always fail to proc, even with WA5 weapons and 2 EB weapons, but you'll never fail to swing your weapon.

Even Great Maul of the Mighty (59/31 125dd proc) holds aggro pretty initially due to its hefty ratio. I had no problem holding aggro over a DBoW for 30 second type yard trash clearing with Great Maul of Mighty which has a significant ratio aggro bonus over just about anything you can dual wield right now. Sure DBoW will catch up when it gets in a proc or two in, but then the mob's usually dead by then. You should see a high end monk holding aggro. They are very good at initial aggro and they certainly have no +aggro proc to speak of.

Most of your aggro comes from ratio, not procs, and if you want snap aggro, Chaos Cry and ratio is where it's at, not procs. If I'm going to guess, I'd say 75% of your long term aggro comes from ratio alone.
