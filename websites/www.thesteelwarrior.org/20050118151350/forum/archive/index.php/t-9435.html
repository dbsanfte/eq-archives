More agro is better is the mantra I believe more and more in every day in EQ (first thing I learned off of reading these boards). Your aim should be to have the most agro you can get. This sword is a good one, nice stats, ratio etc. I put a dps aug on mine and and use it when i want dps. I have another set up for agro.

While agro management is a grooup effort, and a good group will adjust to the amount agro a tank supports; it just makes things go smoother if you get a lot of it. (less mob chasing, dps classes can really open it up and whack mobs good....)

That said, if you want to anger aug it, it will probably work fine if you know your group. I have used this in some group/raid situaions, with a 150 DD aug, and it does pull agro once in a rare while. I wold assume that if you put a anger III aug, make first contact, incite, and have taunt ready, you will do fine.
