07-28-2004, 03:44 PM
People have way too much faith in Damage Bonus. The difference in DPS between the best 2H damage bonus (damage 35 delay 28) and Jade Axe (damage 14 delay 22) is (35/2.8 - 14/2.2) = 6.14 DPS. We'll be generous to say you never missed and you always hit at least twice per round, then that's 12.28 DPS there. When people can pull 200 DPS with ease, the damage lost due to damage bonus accounts for barely 5% of the total damage.

As for tanking with 2Hander, I have tanked with Vanazir for the longest time. Yes it's no DBoW but a good 2H with a good proc is quite serviceable, and usually you get okay DPS out of it with an added bonus of not having to worry about ripostes. Now, I don't think Jade Axe would be good for tanking due to not having a good proc but it's certainly a good DPS weapon. Like Aspit says most of aggro is DPS, and the game certainly favors DPS on 2H more. A Great Maul of the Mighty can occasionally pull aggro off DBoW users in yard trash clearing and ratio is the only possible explanation for this to happen.

You don't need crazy attack buffs for a weapon with a ratio of 2 to beat a weapon with a ratio of 1.5. The only difference at most is 12.28 DPS between any 2Hander to a typical delay ~20 2hander in terms of damage bonus. Having good attack just makes the better ratio weapon that much better.
