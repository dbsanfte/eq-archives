Forget LDoN augs. Go to RSS and you can get AC and HP augs which are all better than their LDoN counterparts in a fraction of the time.

That being said, your gear is pretty good for a non-raider, but you really need some augs.
As others have said, I'd start in MPG and pick up maybe a neck and legs off DEK, as well as a 20AC aug and 55hp aug from Blind Hunter and Odiumate.

From there, you have two options which will net you some nice stuff.

First, since you need aa, I would go to riftseekers and start exping in camps like Researcher (70hp non-lore aug for ring/finger) and Riftseeker (25AC non-lore aug for 4 more slots). Other drops here that are upgrades would be Celestial Lorica (chest), as well a belt, ring, and shoulder.

Then I would do the GoD trials and KT. Here you can pick up the following:
2x 20AC augs
Glyphed Prayer Ring
Resonating Earring of Battle
Carved Vrex Totem
Crafted Wristband of Strife (slightly worse stats than your bracers, but 46 haste so is a personal thing)
Bracer of the Heartstriker

Finally, once you got some nice stuff (or maybe before, I haven't tried yet personally so don't know the difficulty), go to the MPG trials. There is a drop there for every non-visible slot which as well as 2 weapons which are upgrades to everything you can get with a single group outside the trials.

That should keep you busy for quite some time and net you some considerable upgrades.
