Looks like from what I see, the biggest upgrade potentials are your neck, hands, arms, haste, and offhand, probably in that order.

Clattering Bone Necklace is a good, and fairly cheap neck solution.

For Hands and arms, looking for something with AC and HP is a good idea. I'm sure you can pick up a Champion's MQ for cheap, or even drag your guild to Kael for a few plate cycles. On FV, the corroded plate gauntlets (droppable there) sell for around 200-300pp. For a nicer option, your guild is probably approaching HoT potential (if not there already). Picking up some Gauntlets of the Myrmidon (27ac 90hp 8dex) would be nice.

For your haste, a Soulscream Belt (9ac 9str 10sta 30hp 31% haste) should be trivially easy for you to get in Veksar. Not a huge upgrade, but it's free and not difficult to get. And who can complain about more haste?

You might want to check out adding a Sword of the Bloodsworn to your weapon setup - Stats aren't all that different from your current offhand, but it adds a proc for 75dd + 0.0sec stun, so it'll improve your agro.

Those should keep you busy inbetween LDoN trips.
