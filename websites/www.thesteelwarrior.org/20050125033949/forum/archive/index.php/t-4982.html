My best advice to you is to not wear a single piece of gear that does not have raw hps on it (Other than your haste cloak).

Two things matter for warriors. AC and Hps. Everything else takes a back seat.

Look in the bazaar at every slot that you do not have raw hps on. See what items cost on your server vs how much you have to spend. Look at the magelo's of other warriors at and above your level, see what they are using.

Specific advice: lose that neckpiece now. Same goes for the bracelet of quickness.
