Until the CH lands and they're back.

"My regen is a gift that just keeps on giving, and I can guarantee I regen more than your 20 back by the end of the fight. (when your 20 is still gone)"

It's a moot point, really. They're both useful in a fight, for different reasons. The added hard hit points from increased Stamina makes the CH a wee bit more efficient. The added regen from general AAs makes you a tad more efficient in a solo fight or a fight with a bad healer.

There is no RIGHT answer here. Get whichever one YOU LIKE. You'll be fine either way.

In regards to stamina being capped with gear ... consider three things ...

1) The STA cap is much higher post PoP.

2) The more innate STA you have, the more flexibility you have to choose an item with less actual stamina and more actual raw hit points on it. (This is more of a monk issue than a warrior issue as warrior gear tends to have oodles of stamina and hit points regardless).

3) Not all races started off with MACK TRUCK like stamina of Ogres and Trolls. Heh. So the AAs to raise a single stat like that can help offset character creation deficiencies. So many times I see monks say "I wish I had gone all STA at character creation instead of AGI and DEX." Well ... with AAs now you can.

In any event, really, in GENERAL AAs there's no WRONG answer on what to take.

Run 3 is about the most useful AA you can get. Then you've got 3 AAs to play with. Get something you want to get. HAVE FUN. It's 3 levels of your life and time there, do with it what you WANT to do with it, be that Metabolism, Stamina, Regen or INCREASED NATURAL CHARISMA!
