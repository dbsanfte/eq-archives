Snarlwart,
As you can see, we don't actually know what you mean by "cheap."
If you you need weapons under a couple hundred plat, search in the bazaar by race/class/religion and your budget (and, of course, the "slot").
If you are purely looking for skill ups, get the combo with the cheapest/lowest delay you can afford and don't worry about damage at all.
If you are going to use the weapon in pick-up groups and would actually like to do your job with it, and you are looking for really cheap ( under a couple hundred plat) stuff, then use stuff like the centi spear and the sarnak battlehammer as a floor. Stuff like that will be usable if you work at it, but obviously if you see something better and you can afford it, get it.
The sarnak thing is 30dam/40delay, which is pretty slow, but you'll train up okay on it and I used to be able to hold aggro with it in the 20s, especially if I pulled. And it hits good.
Not sure how much you've looked around here, but keep in mind that warriors' current thinking is that the aggro you generate outside of weapon procs is a function of the potential damage of the weapon, not how much damage the mob actually takes.
In other words, if you swing a two hander at a mob twice, and once you miss and once you hit for full damage, the swing that missed will cause the mob to hate you exactly as much as the swing that hit for full damage.
Basically, that means if you get a great weapon for tanking, then you can feel good about using that weapon in group situations even if your weapon skill is low; your low skill in that weapon will not influence how well you keep aggro. It will lower your actual damage output, but especially later in your career, if the group is depending on your damage output to turn the tide, the group will sooner or later get wiped out, heh.
