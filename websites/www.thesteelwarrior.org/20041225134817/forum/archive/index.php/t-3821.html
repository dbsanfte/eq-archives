One of the problems with melee right now is that turning on attack punishes the player. Making a player attack at an even faster rate invites two problems as a class that desires to tank:

- DS are a drain
The best example of this is in Sol Ro. Monsters there have damage shields which chew through Monks and Rogues which I simply shrug off because of my slower lower rate of attack.

- Reposte/Enrage is killer:
If you want to rudely wake people up, turn off attack and Taunt right as the monster goes Enrage. Your DW who aren't paying attention drop dead. This is kind of harsh but shows the problem. Fast attacking classes kill themselves in the face of something that has a high reposte rate. The big issue behind 2H vs DW has this problem at its core.

I apperciate what you are trying to accomplish but at the moment player melee is dangerous. While it is still very true that speed kills, it is a very sharp double edge sword and now more than ever it cuts both ways. Uncapping haste is just as dangerous as it is helpful. I really do think that the low end of the melee DPS spectrum is too low. I don't see haste as a way to solve it.
