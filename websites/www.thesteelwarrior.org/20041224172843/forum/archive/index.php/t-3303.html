When talking about the mana, WIS, INT of the Tak armor being a benefit to other plate classes more than Warriors you have to compare the levels of those stats to the levels that are on the Ornate of each class as opposed to Warrior Ornate.

Paladin Ornate has 42 WIS and 550 Mana. So you lose 16 WIS and gain 200 Mana by going to Tak over Ornate. Not a huge jump relatively speaking.

Also, I think a group with both a Paladin and a Warrior (same level and gear assumed) that lets the Paladin tank is missing out. The Paladin loses a lot of the utility we have when we tank. While that utility usually does not get us groups, it is silly to waste it when it can help the group. When I am in an LDoN group with a Warrior I usually pull. I stun the mob on incoming, and once more. The Warrior taunts off of me and tanks that mob till it is dead. If we have a chanter, he mezzes the add mobs, if not I off-tank. When the first mob is near death, I switch to the mezzed mob and break mezz so that the group loses no time between mobs and the Warrior taunts off me again. When the last mob in camp is near death I go to look for more.

Once the Warrior taunts off a Paladin who has dropped 2 stuns on the mob, he can usually hold aggro easily for the rest of the fight. Meanwhile, the Paladin is free to do other things to help the group. Perception can be a problem though. I have had to convince groups to try this a few times, myself.

Lastly, at the level I play, LDoN groups all have at least one tank. (65 with 20 – 100 AA average) I guess some of the truly uber folks can rip through the adventures having a mage tank, but I thought the most pronounced problem area for Warriors wasn't at the very high end.
