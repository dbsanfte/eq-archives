There's a couple of things that I'd like to see implemented in-game which shouldn't be too hard, and there's no reason for them not to be implemented:

1. A buff ignore list:

I want nothing to do with certain buffs, and don't want them MGB'd on me screwing my life up, and generally irritating me. I'd like an ignore list for certain buffs that I never want on.

1A. This should also hold true for MGB cursor items such as modrods, food and water.


2. Saved Equipment Setup:

If casters have saved favorite spell lineup, I see nothing wrong with a saved favorite gear lineup for reasonably timed switching of weapons/armor setups.

3. Consent list

I have a hotkey to consent people to drag my corpse every time I die. It's always the same people, I don't see why I can't just have a consent list of people who are perma-consented. There's no roleplay justification for making me consent someone post-death as opposed to in advance.

Thank you for listening, have a nice day
