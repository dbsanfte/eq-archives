/disagree yoda...I don't agree that FH is "outdated" for level 65 toons. FH is very very similar to Ornate/EF LDoN armor AC/HP wise at a fraction of the cost. Short term i would look at switching Helm, Arms, and bracers to Fierce Heraldic. Spend early LDoN points on an agro aug for mainhand.

/agree Yoda with selling RBG and using tribute haste, and replacing belt with FH or Heavy Rubi Girdle if you have the cash.

With these changes I think you could pull-off hard LDoN's in the right group. "Right group" in my mind being a very good puller, and a patch healer to go with your cleric. Anyways, you are not that far from being able to do hard LDoN. Good hunting to you.

1st edit: A lot of this depends on your cash situation. You can buy much nicer upgrades than the FH, such as the droppable loot from Hard LDon's and certain pieces of Ornate, but generally those items are pricey. Would only do Ornate where you get em as drops for free.
