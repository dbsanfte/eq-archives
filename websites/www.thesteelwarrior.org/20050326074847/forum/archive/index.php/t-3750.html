Marus Seru is nice for that level...if you can find others to group with. Kill greyhoppers for xp and pelts, and kill stonegrabbers for xp. Both are non-kos, but they do assist others of their kind. Most groups set up camp on one of the hills just inside the zone from Netherbial Lair. The Neriaks are KOS, and have a huge aggro range. If you don't want to fight them, you can train them to the Recuso's (at their tents to the right of NL when you zone in) as they will kill the neriaks for ya.
