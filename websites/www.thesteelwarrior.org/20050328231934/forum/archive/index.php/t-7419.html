There are generally two tiers of trash mobs in each elemental plane.

The weaker tier tends to be level 64-65 and is easier than hard LDONs, similar to SolRo trash and krigers in BOT. Generally hits for 700-900.

The harder tier is closer to the mountain trials in GoD, the mobs are 66-68 and generally hit for 1200-1400, and are overall harder than hard LDONs.

PoFire has a third, extra-easy tier of mobs in the first field, which is a total joke in terms of difficulty, and is probably equivalent to the PoValor wasteland.
