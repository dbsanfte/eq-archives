First of all, you can't compare how one weapon + an EB weapon would hold agro against a non-EB weapon. Obviously if you can get a hate proc weapon the caliber of CHSD or better, you are going to be producing more agro solely by virtue of the EB weapon.

While this looks to be a very nice weapon and I would love to have one, I would say that this weapon would need to be paired with a very good DPS 1her to equal the amount of agro a GSoT produces. The proc on this weapon is just the proc of the GSoT+75 damage. We all know that the damage portion of the proc helps produce DPS agro so the real agro portion of the proc is the 0 sec stun, which is the same as the GSoT.

I would venture a guess that this weapon paired with another weapon of similar ratio would produce equal agro to the GSoT so long as both set ups are producing similar DPS.
