By the time you got your crafted and your executioners axe, you had seen more than a couple warriors run by in Indicolite and you knew what your next goal was. It was to look as cool as those guys did...because all the people your level wore crafted and you didn't stand out. By the time you had a few Hate raids under your belt, Kunark came out and now the best of the best were wearing Cobalt blue, and it stayed that way for a LONG time. The best warriors on the server were decked in full blue. If you were middle of the road, you had the gauntlets and the boots and the vambraces. If you were PL'd by your buddy's druid, you were probably wearing crafted still, and you wouldn't be a good choice to fill that tank slot in the group. There was no questioning of it, you could look at someone and tell if they were at your gear level and going to be able to suffice in the group you were doing.

When Velious came out, Haass was in his 30's or 40's and I took absolute pride in the full set of Guardian I managed to farm up for myself in the first month or so of the expansion. It wasn't much better than crafted in most slots...but until the top guilds started doing Kael every nite, there wasn't anyone else on the server that looked like I did.

That's something that has been missing from the game since Luclin, and then was killed entirely with the dye system in LoY.
