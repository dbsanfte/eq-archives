Even with 1 group, there are plenty of things you can do to obtain upgrades not only for you but for other people in your guild.

For starters, try camping to obtain a Copper Hammer of Striking, main hand that one and off hand the Sword of the Bloodsworn. It'll be one of the best dual-wield combos other than trying to obtain a Sceptre of Destruction or Blade of Carnage.

See if you can try going PoStorms and hunt the Diakus in the Desert area to complete the Diaku Emblem Quest for alternate access to PoTactics, farm the mobs in there for Highly Polished Gem, and find a Jewelcrafter to craft Bloodmetal Earrings of Engagement.

Try getting into BoT groups, farm named and if you get Ornate drops, you can either swap them in to obtain Ornate upgrades, or sell the Ornate molds for cash to upgrade your other slots.
