Honestly, you're going nowhere with that crew. You don't have a snarer. Snaring is an absolute must for a hard LDoN. Lots, and I mean lots, of stuff runs. In the rujarkian and tak themes, they tend to run SoWed. You might be able to root a few runners, but the one that gets away will wipe your group. Period.

Also, without CA 3 and CS 3, I think your cleric is going to have some mana issues. Everything hits for 990 (before mitigation AAs, which you don't have). That's a quantum leap from an LDoN normal.

IMO, hard LDoN mobs hit harder then even WoS north field kyv elites. No chaos claws proc, but the steady pounding at 990 really adds up. That's a third harder per hit then WoS mobs, though it's possible they don't swing as often. Also, it's much, much easier to single pull WoS, and your cleric can use a horse for mana regen in WoS.

It's worth a try. But I think you'll have real issues. I would strongly suggest finding either a ranger or a bard. The loot from hard LDoN nameds is generally very, very good (trash like the Granite Halberd of Chance being more the exception then the rule), and track is extremely helpful for finding them. Snaring's a must.

Bards can snare too, and track, and pull, and sing, and cook, and....

Bards are the single best class for a hard LDoN. Try to find one with Fading Memories.
