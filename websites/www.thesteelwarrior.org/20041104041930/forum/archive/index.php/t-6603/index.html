The Kizrack's Helm is ac 34, stam 8, hp 60, ultra vision, see invis
The Cowl of Mortality is ac 16, sta 9, agi 12, hp 50

Do the math

Shadowfrost
The graphic on the Custom Cowl of Mortality is the plate helm for your gender and race. The Cowl (not custom) has the chain helm graphic. If you don't dye it, it's black.

My warrior wears a CCoM because she has high hp items in the back and waist slots (if you can afford a CCoM, you're definitely wearing at least 80hp in your back and waist slots - Crinose Cape and Rubi belt or better) and it's harder to find a high hp item in the head slot. But then, she's a dark elf with natural utravision, so I didn't see Kizraks as quite so important.

If you need the nightvision, Kizraks and COF is probably the better choice.
