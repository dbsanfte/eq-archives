Abividad
I'd say if you're not having trouble keeping your weapon skills maxed for level to go for the Wrumslayer also. You'd want to use that till about level 40 or so. By then your damage bonus will be starting to add up a bit, and a fast delay weapon like a Lammy or Defiance will outdamage the Wurmslayer. On a level 35 alt that I play just for fun and not min maxing, I have a Venemous Ax of the Velium Brood mainhand (85 DD Poison proc starting at level 35) and a Wakizashi of the Frozen Skies (81 DD Cold proc starting at level 20) in offhand. I mainhanded the Wakizashi until I hit 35. I'll retire it at 37 when the proc on Short Sword of Yekesha starts to fire (75 DD + stun).

If you have not done so already. Go to PoK library or EC tunnel and pick up the Bellow Tome for level 20. Helps some with generating agro.

Also just looked at your magelo skills. Get a 1hb and practice as much as you can. If you have signed up for Premium Service, this option should be available to you in the Magelo Update program. Launch Magelo Update then play Everquest (just logging in will suffice) then exit the game. Once you see the character information displayed by Magelo Update, go to the Magelo menu and select Synchronize. Checkmark the characters you wish to have updated. Fill in your Magelo username and your password and hit the Synchronize button. This may take a few seconds depending on your connection speed. You're done.

Brabble
Caldron's right.
On my tank alts when I'm in LDoNs I usually mention that I will try to position mob with its back to any pets in the group. That gives rogue/other melee a place to stand, and it makes a bit of sense because it's a bit hard to quickly reposition pets. There's usually time to talk to your group about this just before you go in the dungeon, while buffing is happening.
If there are no pets, it's actually often faster for everyone else to move rather than you to move the mob. But yes, it's good when everyone else is behind mob. Even better if everyone else will watch push and back up if mob starts going into a wall.
