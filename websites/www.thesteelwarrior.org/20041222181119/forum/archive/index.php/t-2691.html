I'll answer the second one, yes, the Willsapper proc will still give aggro even if the mob has been slowed.

I have(had) all 3 weapons on my retired Warrior. What I found is that the WS/CHSD held aggro better (most of the time), but I also found that 'most' of the time I could hold aggro with the CHSD/WWHB, so I mostly used that combo cuz I got more DPS from it.

So if you can get away with it, I'd use the CHSD/WWHB, but if you are struggleing with aggro, I'd switch to WS/CHSD.

On another note, I'd try to buy a Copper Hammer of Striking (nothing more badass than Warrior weilding a pair of hammers). DPS with the proc is better than CHSD/WWHB, and it holds aggro better.

that's just my opinion though
