I am not quite sure of your question, we got some like this sometime, but can vary dependently on what you wanted to say. so my answer will be :
No proc never 'stack', in the way thay don't fuse in one bigger proc. but they 'stack' in the way that they both are able to fire alternatively (they can't fire at the same swing, but can within the same round via double attack, etc ...).
so the red epic, in main hand, with WA0 and 305 DEX will proc around 2 times per minute, if you put A3 on it, you will add 2 more proc (of A3) per minute.
currently you have SBoD+A3 (600hate 2ppm) / BoS (566hate 1ppm) : 1766hate per minute from proc only.
if you sawp the A3 on the red epic and put it MH you end ep : BoS+A3 (566hate 2ppm & 600hate 2ppm) / SBoD (0ppm) : 2322 hate per minute from proc only.
so with the red epic / copper hammer of striking combo (both A3 auged), you end up with 3390 hate per minute from proc only (alomst twice as much as your current state).
and if Wandor suggest you to replace the red epic by the SotB it is because both CHoS and SotB have much better dps aggro than the red epic, with a comparable proc hate, so even if you decrease a little bit your proc aggro, you will still end up with a far better hate generation. So yes the CHoS+A3 MH with the SotB+A3 OH will make you uber compared to your current state.
