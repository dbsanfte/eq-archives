Well I will tell you how we do it.

At the north arch have healers inside on the wall next to the arch. Have 2 tanks, each one with 250DR or more on other side of arch, casters and rangers back near the port down tube at max range.

We pull him to North Arch and 1 warrior goes Defensive and taunts initially till debuffed then the 2 warriors trade off agro back and forth keeping the Cursed balanced in place, not on the wall and not far out from the arch so you are in range for heals. Keeping Cursed in place is key for the fight. We have 2 cho's going on the tanks with usually 3 cleric/druid combos in each one. ONLY MELEE WHO HAVE 250DR are allowed to enter the melee at 50 life and if I see one getting charmed alot I will order their ass right back out of the melee. Sure the fight is long this way but once heals are flowing it takes something really unexpected to mess up on Cursed.

The only problem we had with him once getting it down was other guilds waiting around for you to fail and getting in the AE charm without resists up to par. Nothing sucks more than some random wizard nuking your tanks while we try to maintain a long battle.

Good Luck
