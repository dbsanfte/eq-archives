For Thurg armor, there is no level restriction (I think), all you need is good enough faction. Can't remember what level you can start doing kael, but if you're going in as a group rather than with a raid, I'd say low 50's minimum, at least if you want a decent chance at armor drops. See what you can buy in the bazaar. Odds are there will be stuff there that's dirt cheap and will tide you over until you can get Thurg gear. Or if you have plenty of cash and were wise enough to start a tribunal worshipping barbarian or an RZ worshipper, you can get Fierce Heraldic or Helanic Tundra armor that'll be better than Thurg quest gear in most slots.
