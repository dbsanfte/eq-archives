A warning for anybody working on this quest:
Turning in your figurine to Lathin Firetree spawns the quest version of Ajeea immediately. If you aren't ready to fight the 4 shades in the caster building right then, do NOT hand in the figurine. You will not be able to spawn the quest version of Ajeea with that figurine ever again.

My experience:
I knew it would take me forever to succeed at the pottery combine, since I have no int, wis, or pottery to speak of. So I went by myself to Katta and laboriously slaved away at the pottery until BINGO, the RNG had pity on me. I figured I would go ahead and do the turn-in to Lathin Firetree so that when I came back with a group to whack the shades, we could get straight to it. So, I did that. He gave me back the figurine and spewed a bunch of quest spam that I didn't read. It was late, so I logged out.

Three weeks or so have elapsed. Last night I took a group to Katta to whack the shades. Ajeea was not up in the summoning circle where I half expected her to be. Uhoh. I went to see Lathin and gave him the figurine again. He told me he didn't need that and gave it back. Ajeea, of course, did not spawn in the summoning circle. I searched the building and found her in one of the corner rooms. Great! I have her the figurine, hoping she would move to the summoning circle and do her thing (fond fruitless wishes!). No dice. Thus, the figurine at this point is worthless to me, and there seems to be no way to go backward in the quest.

I have resorted to /petition to beg some rarely-appearing GM to trade me the first version of the figurine for the one I have now. I'm pretty sure that won't happen. Which means I get to start over from the beginning.

Screwin' up quest turn ins for 58 glorious levels,
