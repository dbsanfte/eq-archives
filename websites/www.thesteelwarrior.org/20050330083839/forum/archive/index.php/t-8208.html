actually it's more :
basic mode (ie lvl 1+) : Battle Stance, give a bit of DPS and of Defence and of Aggro. skills : Charge, DoT, DD, DD + slow, AE DD + slow, DD (~strikethrough), DD + stop casting, DD + Taunt.
tanking mode (ie lvl 12+) : Defencive stance, give a little bit of DPS, and good tool for Defence and Aggro. skills (* mean need a shield) : AC Debuff, Block*, Bash*, Desarm (one that works against mob, as a duration), Taunt, Counterattack.
DPS stance (ie lvl 24+ ? ) : Beserker Stance, give a boost of DPS and a bit of aggro but no defencive tool. skills (not complete) : DD, Stun, AE Swing.
Utilities : Shouts : AE Melee Buff, AE Fear, AE Melee Debuff, AE Taunt.

note : a new talent system is implemented, that looks like the D2-barbarian skill trees, that allow to improve skills (exmple, increase domage of swords, increase chance to stun, increase power of ...).

Remember its still a beta game, and some may change and some will certainly be added (we haven't reach the max lvl possible).
