OK, this seems to be the whole focus of this thread in one line, but lets honestly look at it.

Warriors CAN be effective in exp groups if we have 4 things:

1) Sufficient equipment... this has always been the case to a certain degree with us and while it is frustrating at times its what we all accept.

2) A certain level of skill... which is acceptable to me: we all want to play with smart/skilled players, not stupid idiots.

3) A certain level of AAs... this is where I start to disagree: how does average warrior get a group to get his AAs?

4) Cooperation from his groupmates... I have an issue with this, as well as it impacts on #2. The DPS elements in a group don't need to be skilled or cooperate much with a knight, because they won't gank aggro. With a warrior they are required to ride the edge.

I don't even try for pick up groups anymore because I know that any short comings are blamed on my "lack of skill" rather than the ability of a group to realize where my limits are and scale back accordingly.
