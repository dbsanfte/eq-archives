I'm having some problems, all relatively new, somewhat corresponding to the DX9 changes, but I can't say for sure that they're related.

The first is that on two of my computers, I have a problem where the spacebar will stick or won't register at all when pressed, it happens only in EQ not with other programs. Makes carrying on a conversation a huge pain, and the frequent unplanned jumping is a major annoyance as well. I've tried removing and installing eq repeatedly, used to make a difference for a while, then the bug would return. I've resorted to moving my fastest computer to a backup role because it's basically unplayable for EQ.

The second is with discipline timers, and is why I've put it in this forum. If I use a discipline and die before the timer has refreshed, it adds approximately an hour to the refresh time. For example, I use defensive, as long as I don't die, the countdown continues as normal (e.g. you have x minutes before you can use the defensive discipline message), and it will refresh as normal. However, if I die before the timer has expired and the discipline is refreshed, I get a message saying I have an hour or more until I can use the discipline.

No custom UI, all updated drivers, no virus problems detected or anything like that.

Anyone else experiencing these kinds of things?

To start with, rekey your space bar to Con stuff, and put jump on the letter j. Have fun rekeying stuff in general, there are much better configurations than the default.

Your other problem may be related to your clock. Reset your clock and make sure it's not lagging or off of daylight savings time, etc. There is a bug with the clock (and a more subtle one which I'm not qualified to talk about with your BIOS clock, contact customer service) which results from your computer's time and EQ time not corresponding correctly, which often results in timer problems for discs.

Good luck.
