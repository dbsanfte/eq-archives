ok, this is the thing about cursed cycle. You have to move fast, waiting around taking 2 hours to buff to try the second mob in the cycle wont work. Buff up, engage cycle, rez/buff people as they die, but for god's sake man, take only what time you need to take to finish loot, then pull the next one.

Exiled is the hardest of the 3 in the cycle, though, cursed takes the longest to kill. Make sure you have a 3 or 4 tank line up, as he DTs every 2 minutes, and yes its like 22 minutes or so for depop.

If you get caught in depop, or fail an encounter, you have to wait 2 hours to spawn the cycle again (the time it takes for TMs and Rzoths to respawn), you then have to fight Glyphedclones, and Exiledclones, that drop no loot, but otherwise have the same stats as their brothermob. This is just to fill the cycle, nothing more.

So, if you kill Glyphed and Exiled, and fail to cursed, if you want to wait 2 hours to try him again, you still have to rekill Glyphed and Exiled, though, without loot.

Sounds like you just wasted too much time sitting around, you cant do that on these type of events, you have to keep at them.

Dont wait till 5 minutes before a kill to talk strat when your on a timer. Before you engage TMs and Rzoths, talk strat, so you dont have to spend an hour between kills. Make sure everyone knows what to Expect from all 3 fights, BEFORE you engage glyphed.

If you have to stop after every mob, and hold everyone's hand, you will fail every time.
