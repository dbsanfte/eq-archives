Kogur
For your offhand, I'd go with the Fabled SSOY.

1.) Your offhand weapon swings independantly of your main hand based on your dual wield skill...trust me I have had many rounds where I only strike with offhand weapon(with damage bonus it's easier to tell than it used to be). Ratio is all that matters for offhand dps, and the ratio on the SSOY is slightly superior.

2.) It's a fairly unique weapon, there won't be 500,000 twinks running around with them.

3.) The Fabled has 40HP less but 8AC more...going by the general 1AC=5HP this puts them on a pretty even level there.

My conclusion: Fabled SSOY has a slightly better ratio, a near identical proc, similar stats, and a less common look to boot.

F-SSOY.
