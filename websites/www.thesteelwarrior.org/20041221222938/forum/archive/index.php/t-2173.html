Just to help explain the whole" AAs come faster at 60 than 51" issue. Note that 1 AA takes about the same xp as getting thru level 51. If you are level 51 and trying to earn an AA point, it will be the same speed as getting level 51 because of the level of mobs you are killing. But when you are 60, you will (should!) be killing higher level mobs that give more xp, so you get more AA xp per kill.

I've been wondering now recently, at lvl 57 what is to be concidered "good" AC/HP ?

I would say that about 1200ish AC should be fine, more is always better tho. HP's...3K at least, shooting for 3500+ at 60 if you can(I did not make it myself, think I was at 3200 at 60). Note that the hp's are far more important than the AC. If you are having trouble making the 1200 AC, I would not worry about it too much.

Also, should i be thinking about going on AA's yet, or wait till i hit lvl 60 as some people have told me (apparently it's faster to get AA's at lvl 60?)

Run3 is a good bet, I would get that one out of the way, after that, I would do no more till at least level 60, and if you are not having real trouble and can put it off, level 65. You will get a greater return on your time spent going 100 % regular exp than by doing AA's, with the assumption that you have enough hp's and are able to tank the exp mobs at those levels. The difference between level 60 and 65 is night and day, while the difference between aa's is definatly not.

Another consideration here is to realize that after you level past 60, you have cut out much of the old world as a good source of exp. If you enjoy exping in PoP zones, this should not matter much, but if you prefer old world, it might.

Also, if i am going to AA's, where would i be seeing best to get decent grind groups? Planes? or out of plane zones for the AA's

PoP zones are about the best source of AA(and regular) exp. They can also be horribly dull. If you want fastest, then planes. If you want a little more fun, check out some old world, especially the newly revamped zones.

Which AA's should i be going for good measure

After run3, I would suggest regen 3, followed by ND 3, followed by AE taunt. That is 23 aa's, so should keep you busy for at least a little while:) .

At 57, a good ac to shoot for would be 1150 or so .. don't worry about cracking 1200-1250 until you hit 60. And I agree, 3500hp is a good target to shoot for, and shouldn't be too hard, depending on what you guys are raiding.

As for aa .. get 60 first. Then do your run3 and regen3 .. the do PPand PD as they become available (i.e. get 61, then PD1 and PP1, get lvl 62, then get PP2, get lvl 63, then get PP3 and PD2, etc, etc) .. this is so you get the most HP for your buck so to speak, as you level.

PoI is probably your best bet to get you to 60/61 quickly (fast kills). PoV (wasteland mobs) / PoS (frog caves, dillos, giants) will be slower, but more xp per kill.

After that work on your defensive aa .. you can have all the offensive aa in the world, won't mean a darned thing if you're dead though :)

the do PPand PD as they become available (i.e. get 61, then PD1 and PP1, get lvl 62, then get PP2, get lvl 63, then get PP3 and PD2, etc, etc) .. this is so you get the most HP for your buck so to speak, as you level.

PP and PD are terrible aa's for raising hp's. I, with about 5 K hp's at 65, spent the 3 points for PD 1 and got a whopping 60ish hp's. PD is 3 aa points for 1.5 % hp's, compared to ND which is 2 points for 2 %, then 4 points for 3 %, then 6 points for 5 %. Much better bang for your buck. PP will only raise your hp's if your STA is already maxed, and then by a small amount. ND3 is far and away the best value for gaining hp's, followed by PE(because you get mitigation and evasion with it). PD will be the AA I take the rest of when I finish all the other defensive aa's, certainly not first, and without question not till finishing the track to AE taunt.
