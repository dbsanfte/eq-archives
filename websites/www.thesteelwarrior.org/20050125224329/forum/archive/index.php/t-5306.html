I'd probably go with a Copper Hammer of Striking and Sword of the Bloodsworn, since they can essentially last you to 65 if needed. Don't worry too much about the recommended on these - currently these still proc at 1 and the recommended doesn't affect damage, as I have been told.

For armor, look for decent non-recommended level gear. Acrylia Plate or Crustacean gear are ok and should be fairly cheap. Skull-Shaped Barbute is a great twink helm and always cheap.

Don't forget haste too. SCHW is probably the best bang-for-your-plat, so I'd give them a look see.

6/65 rings of course are a great option as well.

I'd spend the 55k on weapons and haste - even then 55k probably wont go as far as you'd like.

SotB and CHoS is one of the best buyable weapon combos for a warrior at the moment, and have heard they arent effected by recommended level with regard to damage/delay/proc.

Try for a haste item with >30% haste, Cloak of Flames is your best option, but failing that Soulscream belt or Runed Bolster Belt will do.

Get 2x 6ac 65hp rings and fill the rest of your slots with crustacean / crafted / gothic steel type stuff which is dirt cheap in the bazaar. You can upgrade with Fierce Heraldic (assuming you do the right thing and worship RZ) as you level.
