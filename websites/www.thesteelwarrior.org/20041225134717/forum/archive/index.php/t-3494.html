It depends what your goals for the character are.

At level 65 kitted out in raid obtained gear race makes very little difference.

At 55 - 65 XPing in bazaar gear the extra HPs from playing a fatty can make a significant difference.

From 1-10 the extra melee stats from playing a large race make a huge difference.

Troll and Iksar regen bonus are both nice if you plan on soloing a bit of the way with your warrior, Halfling sneak and hide can make doing some quests much easier, Gnome tinkering gives you access to tinkered armour with fun clickies on it, Large races get slam so you can interupt casters pre 55 (kick can stun after 55).

Trolls, Iksar and Ogres have an XP penalty, asslings have a bonus.

Faction issues are pretty irrelevant with PoP.

Having played a half elf I used to really lament the extra hps I would have gained from stamina as an ogre as I XP'd through my high 50's and through the 60's, but now that my gear makes this irrelevant I wouldnt change to fat and ugly for anything.

Don't play a dorf tho, they can't worship Rallos and they are ugly so chicks will never cyb0r you.

What does Race mean?

Well it really depends when you started the game.

Some Races have better stats, these can be mitigated by equipment.

Some races have different dieties, these help to create some of the gear that has good stats. RZ and Tribunal are great for warriors, Vesheen not so good.. etc.

Some races have added benefits, Ogres Stun/slam, Barbs Slam(blind as bats too), Trolls Slam/Regen, etc.

Some races have faction, um concerns... In the days of PoK/Nexus, this is pretty much a non-issue.

All of these concerns are valid when forming your warrior, Ultimately what you make is up to you. You must have fun with it.
