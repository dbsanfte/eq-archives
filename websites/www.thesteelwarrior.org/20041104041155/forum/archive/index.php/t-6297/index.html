Lilwee Beasty
Great Blade of Storms would be a nice, relatively easy to farm piece for you. Drops off the BoT tower named Einride Icestorm...14/18 ratio, 85 hp, 175 dd/stun proc.

Einride also drops the Ornate plate BP mold, so he's an extra good target for farming imo. Easily killable with 2ish groups, maybe 1 group if they are mostly elemental gear.
Sassinak
I've had good results with a Rotting Fist from Bertox with Anger 3 on it. It's a noticeable step up from an augmented Great Blade of Storms for aggro as well as DPS, and also beats an unaugmented BOC (to my surprise). I got this weapon because I was in a similar situation as you. I got tired of waiting for an aggro weapon from targets that my guild never hit, and took the next weapon I could that had more than 100 hp and a high ratio and a proc. If you are willing to burn an Anger 3 augment on it, then you might want to do similar. I don't pretend that it's the absolute perfect aggro setup, but it won't let your guild down. A Gold Runed Mithril Knife with Anger 3 would be pretty sweet.
