I think all Pre-PoP Stuns (spell and proc) have a level-limit that is lower than lvl60, the exact level-limit depends on which spells/Proc you use.

Only PoP stuns (both spell and proc) can stun the lvl60+ mobs.

Having said that, the general consenus is that even your target is immune to the stun effect, it still creates aggro. But whether or not the amount of aggro will be the same if the stun would have stuck, it depends who you talk to.

SotB procs Planar Strike, which has 2 effects: A DD and "Stun(0.00 sec/65)". That stun is level capped at level 65 (I assume).
