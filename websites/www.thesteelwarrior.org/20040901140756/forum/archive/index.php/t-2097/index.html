lunaticfringesk
I have been linked the old VI letter about XP changes and this is what I think it boils down to:


Warriors get the highest XP bonus in the game at 10%

Rogues are the only other class to have a bonus, and that checks in at 9.5%.

Classes no longer have a true class-based penalty, making it easier for people to play the class they want to play, rather then the class that they feel compelled to play due to faster development.

Race based penalties are no longer shared with the group, but still exist individually. The following are the races with penalties:

Troll/Iksar -- 20%
Ogre -- 15%
Barbarians -- 5%

Only the Halfing has a bonus, which checks in at 5%.


Class based penalties came about as it was immediately apparant that some races and classes would be more powerful than others given versatiliy and other factors. Subsequent to their initial evaluation, it quickly became apparant that he concept of "more powerful" quickly became dubious, as everyone capped at the same level.

VI, after some heated discussions, then concluded that classes are roughly equivalent in power throughout the level ranges, and that versatility does not make up for that penalty. As a result, they removed the class-based penalties. Ironically, however, they opted *not* to remove the class *bonuses*, as the bonuses were deemed to not be so severe that they unbalanced the classes.

I guess, at the end of the day, those of you WAR who are screaming *Nerf the Knights!* to the "Nth" degree and such should prepare yourselves big time for the XP bonus argument. Im not necessarily making any conclusions whatsoever, but the fact remains that WAR *do* get a 10% XP bonus, whereas the SHD/PAL do not. I have yet to see this brought up in *any* of these threads here, but it exists and it wouldnt surprise me that that argument will be made soon enough.
