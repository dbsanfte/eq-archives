If another warrior is stealing aggro from you when you're the maintank of the raid, he should know better. If he's not the main tank, make him put away what ever aggro weapons he's wielding and bring out the dps setup.

When ever I'm tanking stuff in a group I'll usually tag it on incoming with an arrow and have the puller run past me. That usually gets me initial aggro, then if I haven't proc'ed by the time malo lands, I'll use Bazu Bellow before slow lands and that usually keeps the mob on me. In the case of an enchanter landing tash a bit early or on incoming, well, there's not much I can do about that the first time it happens, but if it's consistent, I'll tag and then use Bazu on incoming instead of hoping for an early proc. This usually keeps me the target of the mobs attention, occasionally the procs will be streaky or downright non-excistant, but I guess that's the power randomness. Most of the time a couple of swings will get the mob back on me and if not, there's always the 1 in 5 chance that the first taunt will be a successfull one :p
