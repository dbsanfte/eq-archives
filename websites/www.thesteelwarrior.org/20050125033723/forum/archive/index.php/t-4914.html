Don't forget on FV, all Velious armor pieces are droppable. You can easily get Champions, Berserkers, Myrmidon, and Warlord gear from just hitting up the bazaar. Best part is that it's Iksar wearable.

A 65 bard can farm enough plat to buy these pieces, or the quest pieces and the gems (usually cheaper I've noticed). Best part is that none of these have required levels and will last you through your 50's. BP is the rarest and most expensive, but yours is decent enough for now anyway.

You'll want to upgrade that Barnacle Covered Axe soon - I personally hated it for agro. For the weapons you have, the Kunzar Ku'juch will be better agro if it's procing for you now (which I think it should be) or for DPS, the Goranga would be better too.
