After Emperor comes VT and/or PoP. Some of the early PoP fights would be more Tier 6ish but once you get past the first round of PoP mobs I'd say you are Tier 8+.

That isn't what I'd suggest, btw. It is just a breakdown of encounter dificulty levels and maybe some ideas.

I'd go Kael arena and if that is too easy move to Temple of Veeshan: Halls of Testing (the +CoV faction armor - you should have the faction after Kael) when you have people in HoT or better armor consider moving into North wing of Temple of Veeshan. NToV is great training for most of what comes later although the upgrades aren't what they used to be. You can skip it but... what could be more fun then killing dragons???

The 3-4 group PoP encounters might be really good too once you have at least HoT level armor. Mujaki is lots of fun and has some nice drops. Might want to take 2-3 groups to Barindu and do named. They can drop spells which will make your casters happy and have some nice loot as well. If you can handle Barindu, move into Ferubi but definately don't single group here - take a 3+ group raid the first few times. It is single groupable for raid guild people but not for people who aren't in at least VT level gear. LoY also has some raids that might be fun to try and shouldn't be too hard.

I looked briefly at your magelo and it looks like you have a mix of ornate and tradeable items. I'd suggest a few things you guys could try would be:
1) Kael giants for faction everyone and armour pieces for the lower level char's
2) Halls of Testing, park an alt inside to see when its not busy and farm there(guides available on a lot of sites)
3) Tawro and Radir as suggested by others
4) Plane of Storm's Mini's. Quad 900+ type mobs plus storm pet procs but doable with 3-4 groups of level 60-65's. Gives BoT access for ornate farming. Likely you'll need 3-4 clerics+spot healers though with the levels you gave.
5) Prototype's in Innovation factory(need a keyed person). They are not tough exactly, but dismiss to each other's rooms those they proc the dismissal on. It requires lots of pets and spot heals, and nukers to manage their aggro.


One thing you might also want to work on is PoJ flags to prepare your people for BoT keys, and farm Daiku raider emblems to get the guild flagged for Tactics. That lets you equip everyone with Ornate from mini's in those zones.

Plane of Storm giants, BoT giants and Tactics mobs would help your guild also build up raid and group leadership aa's if you can get 18+ there regularly. One thing you'll need also is shoulder/earring/ring items to do along with the ornate. Bloodmetal earrings for example for your melee's.
