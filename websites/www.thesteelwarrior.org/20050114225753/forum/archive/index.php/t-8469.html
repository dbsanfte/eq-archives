Before the servers came down I took a peek at some of the new stuff which seems to be themeless (aka you can get it at any camp).

Some of the stuff could be described as VT quality whereas some is more of a bastard VT/ornate hybrid. Most of it gave me a mixed feeling . Some of it actually seems too good :

-25 ac 125 hp AHR like earring, Gem of Deception for 1900 pts (too close to a Signet of might or other EP earrings considering the effort imho)

-22 ac 115 hp mask for 1900 pts, Mask of Deception. (The mask that drops off Rallos Zek is worse than this)

On the other side, some of the expensive stuff really is rather gimp, expecially for warriors:

-52 ac, 125 hp bp with V4 and FT2 for 1900 pts, Breastplate of Guile
-69 ac, 105 hp bp for 1500 pts, nothing new here, Breastplate of Rage

On the other side however, there is now so much FT in gear that any caster can easily be capped with LDONs.

None of this equipment is directly relevant to me anymore, but it all does kind of leave me with mixed feelings. I would have applauded the possibility of gaining a BoC-like weapon for example, but a lot of the stuff here is rather poorly matched (good or bad) to the amount of effort needed.

What really did irk me tho, is that there will now be far more people wanting to do LDON's, which holds absolutely nothing new for raiders. It was never great exp, but als no new hp augs, not even the ability to use tupe 7 in type 8 slots so you still need to get type 8 s when you upgrade. I spend most of my time on EQ raiding, it would have been nice to throw us a bone and cut down on our time needed for LDoN's but I digress.

For sure, this is a big middle finger to any guild trying for VT right now; while shard camping and VT clearings are hardly a high point in EQ, it really does make most of Luclin obselete.
