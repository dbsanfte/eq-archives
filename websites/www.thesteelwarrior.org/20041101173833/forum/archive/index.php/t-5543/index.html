Eoden
Your gear isnt bad, and at 44 you're getting close to the point where you can start using items with recommended levels or restricted level so it might be worth pushing on to 46 before you spend that plat.

Easiest and bested upgrade will be rings. At 44 grabbing 2 Velium fire wedding rings would be the way to go, but if you hold out till 46 you could get a couple of valorium rings of gallantry instead. Not sure what these go for these days but probably in the area of 4-5k each.

Next I would start buying up pieces of dwarven cultural plate. These you can equip now and will get the full benefit of stats at 46. Bracers at 25AC +6svall +30hps are particularly good value, Legs at 40AC 40hps are almost as good as SS. Boots are pretty good value too.

Earring - get rid of that forest loop and get something with HPs. An orc fang would be a cheap improvement at about 15pp, an electrum black sapphire one with 2 AC and 35HPs would be better, or any of the selection of > +50hp earrings that you can get in the bazaar now. Your call on how much you want to spend really.

Some other slots to upgrade on the cheap -
Neck - try for an amulet of ancient mummy wrappings (45hps) these should be going cheap considering the rate at which they drop

Face - Crystal Spider Eyes are a cheap starting point at 8AC 35HPs. Depending on whats going cheap there are a few other options to look out for too tho, Death Mask of the Elysians, Face mask of Frozen Mana, Grobb hide mask. etc etc

Head - Keep an eye out for a helmet of the raldukan. You used to be able to pick these up ridiculously cheap for their stats (cos they are pink - you have Dyes right?). 24AC 35HPs is a decent helm that will last you a while.
