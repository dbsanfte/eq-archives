Keane Abrams
Your BP has type 1, type 2, and type 3 slots on them. That means you can use the type 2 40hps aug from Tak but not the type 7 40hps aug from Ruj.

You can only put specific augments in their appropriate slot. Just look at what type of slot an augment has before you buy it.
Grendonbb
If you are actually interested in getting the greatest performance out of a slot, check the arms in my magelo for the names of the augs. I have a 30 hp/end (any slot auggable) in my slot1, with +40 hp in my slot 2, and AoB in my slot 3. The slot 3 is questionable, and generally accepted as the "utility slot," since you can fit Enduring Breath, Haste, Regen5, Veng4, etc into it. I chose the AoB because it was free, dropping out of Tipt.

The 30 hp/end augs are pretty much the easiest augs to get that fit in slot1, besides the base +5-7 single stat augs out of LDoN. I have found no augs that are type1 in nature, so the only ones you are going to find fit it are augs that fit in any slot type. 30 hp/end drops out of Tipt off enraged cragbeast mum, first "trial" in the event, and is farmable by killing her, leaving adventure, gettin a new one, and killing her again. Also drops +30 hp/mana, Aura of Battle, and +2 hp/mana regen, so it's good for all.

3: Vengeance/Haste/Useful Effect (EB, Vision)
