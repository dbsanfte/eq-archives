40s are the worst stretch for a warrior, but it will get easier by the mid to late 50s. you really just have to stick it out. 40 is about where you really need a group to advance further unless you're a solo class. sounds like your better equipped than i was at 40. i couldnt/didnt leave kunark until about 45 so my gear mostly consisted of trooper scale with random filler slots from droga, CoM and the like.

the armor out now should be alot better, considering i couldnt even wear half of it and the bazzar makes it easily accessable as well as cheap for the casual person. what you want to look for are fast weapons (around 20 delay), or heavy weapons that'll do a lot of damage on the first swing (wurmslayer). that combined with your provoke ability should be decent at holding agro. luckly, tash and slows wont be big agro until around lvl 50, by then you should look into getting a weapon with an agro proc like copper hammer, sword of the bloodsword or reinforced sandstone-hilt sword. the yak you have is a good start, but most people now will have weapons with a much better damage/delay and will simply take agro from you by putting out more damage.

armor, as mentioned before, you should just surf the bazaar and look for what you can afford or work up the cash any way you can.
