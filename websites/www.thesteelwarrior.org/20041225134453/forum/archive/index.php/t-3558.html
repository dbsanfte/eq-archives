Not true.

CA/CS are about equivalent, as is LR and ID.

On a related note, you would almost always want to finish CA/CS before starting LR/ID. Luclin defensive AAs generally give you more bang for your buck than PoP defensive AAs

A WAR can probably flip a coin in terms of which to get first. Granted, many believe that the reduction in proc damage / stun gives the nod to CA, but that is generally not a significant amount.

If a hybrid, on the other hand, was going through that decision process, CA might be a better decision as they also cast spells in combat (e.g. less interrupts). Again, it generally isn't a huge margin though

Luna has it exactly right. CA and CS reduce the same amount of damage taken over time, and ID and LR have the same effect. For further reference, go the The Library and ready Sccaley's LR/ID parse. The difference is that to max CA or CS takes 12 points each, while LR and ID take 15 points each, making CA and CS a better bargain. You will want to get all of them maxed eventually tho.
