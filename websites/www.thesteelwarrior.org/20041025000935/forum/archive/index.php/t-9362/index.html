Then add based on the following considerations:

-If you have an Enchanter you will want somone who can snare so a charmed pet can be used safely.

-A backup healer is of course good.

-A backup rez is very good, especially a necro.

-Evac is good, not only to save from a wipe but to return to zone in after first borerling.

If I was hand picking a group I would choose:
warrior
cleric
bard
necro
druid
enchanter with charm pet

You don't usually have such a choice, so just make sure you have a cleric and CC, then add as many of the bonus classes as possible.

Nothing tricky about the run, just need to keep moving and stay on your toes. Adds can pop/wander into camp at the worst time, keep camp tight and pay attention.
