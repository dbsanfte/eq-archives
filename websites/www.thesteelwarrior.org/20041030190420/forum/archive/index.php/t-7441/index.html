I think people generally overrate the effect (Force Shock) that GBoS has. For one, it can be resisted like most procs other than anger. Secondly, Force Shock is not a Stun. It is an interrupt. That means that the universal ~400 aggro or whatever peeps think a stun is equivalent to is not really on this weapon. I would assume that if a TRUE stun like that on DBotW or GSoT is ~400 aggro, then the partial stun/interrupt on the GBoS is less (maybe 200 or so). So really, comparing GBoS (14/18 weapon w/ it's proc) and a DESB (18/20 wpn w/ it's proc) are much closer than you might think. I get slightly bothered when people ignore the fact that DAMAGE = HATE. Hence...if either weapon had no proc the Stormblade would be much more aggro.

Just a couple things to think about before waving off the Stormblade as a complete non-aggro weapon.
