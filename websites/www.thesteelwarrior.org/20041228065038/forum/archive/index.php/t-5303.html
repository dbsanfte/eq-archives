its a discipline you get at level 35. its activated by /shield and you need to target someone. it lasts for 10 seconds and the refresh time is 3 mins i believe.

what it does is, provided your right next to the person your shielding, to transfer the damage done to that person to yourself. if im not mistaken i think the formula is that they take 50% of the damage but in transferring it to you you take a bit more than what they take.

what some warriors do is to hotkey it. and when you start a group you put the persons name in, so you dont have to target them. e.g
call the hotkey Slower
/shield (insert name of shaman ench etc...)

Personally I like to use it when taunt fails and the mob is bashing on someone more fragile than I. My hotkey is as such:
/assist
/pause 4
/shield
It assists the mob I was just trying to taunt, waits a short moment for the target to change, and /shields. I don't have to change it everytime I join a different group. You can also just type '/sh Bob' real quick. The first few letters of someone's name is sufficient as long as it's unique to the targets around you.

I like to think /shield is handy sometimes, maybe it's saved lives, maybe not, but I use it pretty often.
