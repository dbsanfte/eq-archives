Gryzx
On average you get 2 procs per minute in your main hand and 1 proc per minute in your offhand. This is with 305 dex and no WA.

It has been shown that your dps aggro is also roughly half in your offhand, although your actual dps is about 66pct if you put something in your offhand (ie if you do 100dps with a weapon in your main hand, expect to do roughly 66dps with it in your offhand)

Generally if you have a good hate proc'ing weapon you want that in your mainhand, although if the hate on the other weapon isn't too far below the proc weapon and it's dps is significantly higher then it might actually be better aggro to have it in your main hand instead of the proc weapon. It would be a strange situation most likely though so you're probably safe sticking the proc aggro weapon in your mainhand.
