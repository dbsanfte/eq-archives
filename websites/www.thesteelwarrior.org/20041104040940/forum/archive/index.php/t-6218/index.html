Tried to look up the details I kept on doing this, and couldn't find them, so will have to remember this off the top of my head.

Troll here, had to have been maxed kos, because lived on the Live side of Lower guk, since most good races stuck to the dead side.

Scalp will help either 300, or 500 faction kills. Don't remember which, but was one of them.

Total kills to get from Kos up to good enough, with scalp, was around 2700 I think. I also bribed an enchanter to come down and cast IMP illusion, which was a 1000 point modifier, but have heard that trick no longer works.

Did make some decent money after all the fine steel.. some of the drops sell to the vendors for much more than the market... 80-85 pp or so.

I bent over backwards trying to be considerate of people that was fighting down there for exp. Often I'd offer if they wanted to join up, and get all the loot, would often difuse any hard feelings, and help the kill count go faster.

I have to pass on the funnest time with this... 3 of us. Me 65 warrior, 65 cleric, and a 51 shammie friend. We are in the Lord room, only ones on the dead side. I announce to my group "Inc 75 !" I think my shaman friend about fell out of his chair. Got almost every undead frog in the zone. Run to the back of the Lord room.. wait for the train.... repose on almost all of them (remember need to get a hit on them for faction). As my repose finishes, cleric does an AOE undead AA ability, finishing them off..... Shaman friend gets 75 exp messages *laughs*... Given our refresh times, we could do this about once an hour... was an absolute hoot.

Good luck on the quest... quite handy for a troll.
