Vxed is a great place to farm gear. Chavoor the Trickster (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14588) is any easy pull and kill, drops both Burnished Antique Greaves (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68582) and Polished Garnet Loop (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68591). Also in Vxed, but much deeper(middle northern most room) is a misguided beludu creque (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14747) which drops Cloak of the Caverns (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68586), not the best of tank cloaks, but an upgrade for you nonetheless.

Also a 1group mob in Barindu(spawns in the building in the northern portion of the zone, just to the east of the northernmost creek) drops Blood-Speckled Scaled Boots (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68428).
