For earrings for 10k each or so you should be able to get an Iron Scream Earring and a Blood Earring of Rage. Bloodmetal earrings are a bit better but are higher in price. I'd also replace the platinum fire wedding ring with another Valorium ring. I'd also upgrade your BP to a Fine Heraldic or something with higher AC or wait for Champions or other Velious quest BP. Copper Hammer of Striking would be nice for a mainhand weapon and then offhand your SotB but you may want to wait until you can camp it as hammer is pretty expensive. When you get in that level range you can also camp the 31% haste belt in Droga or hit Veksar for the Soulscream Belt for a haste upgrade, although you have a real nice stat belt right now so you may want to keep the handwraps until you can get a CoF or CoCW or something better.

Edit: Also Blood Earring of Rage and Iron Scream will up your dex a ton which will help your procs and you will also makes gains in your other stats as well without losing anything.
