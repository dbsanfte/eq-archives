Dont mix up sewers / vxed / tipt trials and ikkinz trials. Everybody needs sewers 1 - 4 (or smith) + vxed + tipt in order to enter Kod'Taz.

In Kod'Taz itself are 3 single group trials + 4 raid trials. These take place inside Ikkinz and are triggered from kodtaz. In order to trigger such a raid you need to pass single group trials 1 - 3 and some minor farming in Kod'Taz. Only one person needs those single group trials, that person can trigger raids for the whole guild then.
After successing the first 3 raids, any person with those 3 trials can trigger the 4th raid (SoE calls it ikkinz 7) for the whole guild. Prior to that patch every person needed raid1 - 3 to enter radi4.
I hope that clears things up :)
