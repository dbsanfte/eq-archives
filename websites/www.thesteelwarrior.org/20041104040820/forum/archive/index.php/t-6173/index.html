Sassinak
The first thing to notice is that Mirror III only lasts 3 ticks.

The second thing to notice is that Mirror III only reflects player castable spells.

The third thing to notice is that Mirror III has close to no aggro.

Remember that with 305 dex, on average, it will proc every 5 ticks in your mainhand, or every 10 ticks in your offhand.

Since it takes an average of 5 ticks to make it proc the first time, this doesn't leave much of any fight, in which you are being cast upon by player-castable spells, for the 15% chance to actually fire.

The best thing about this weapon is that it sells to ignorant players for great coin. This is a Splitblade of Destruction for non-warriors.
