I'm an older Iksar myself, and I think one of about 4 Iksar Overlords who regulary play on my server.

The good news, is Iksar are much better off at low levels now. You can, for example, pick up a set of Trooper Scale Armor easy. If this armor doesn't have a big selling base in your server (Likely not, Iksar warriors aren't big) then you can do the quests very easy with your beastlord. Trooper Scale is what we Iksar have instead of Crafted.

You might also look into some of the newer armor sets- Crusty, I beleive, took over for Trooper/Crafted in many cases, and that should be availible in the Bazaar easily.

Iksar originally had a "No Plate Armor" rule. This rule was forgotten early in Velious, so any modern drop/quested armor you will have no problems with. The only modern downside, IMO, is Cultural- Iksar Cultural Armor seems to be Chain Armor designed for Shamans, with lots of wisdom and mana, and not much AC, HP, and sta. This is called Fearsome Skyiron. The only other minus to this rule is that we can't use Cobalt or Indicarne, which means no unlimited charge right click invise.

OTOH, when you do wear the same armor as another race, for example Champions gear quested from Thurgadin, you will have more AC then any other race wearing the same equipment. This can be a slight advantage. Our Planer Armor (Legionerre Scale Armor) from Plane of Hate, for example, has less AC, but better stats and resists then the Softskins have. When you combine that with our AC bonus, you actually end up with a better armor set- same total AC, and more resists/stats. And again, when you move up to Velious Quested Armors or further, Iksar will have a higher AC then any other race with the same armor. Right now, and for the foreseeable furture, Iksar Warriors would have the highest possible AC in game.

Another factor to not forget is our regen. Much like AC, this isn't a game-breaking factor, but it IS a factor. Pre PoP, I could solo better in Velks and Sebilis the most warriors, for example.

A third factor, which is FAR more significant then the other two, is that Iksar have tails. In fact, Prior to SoL, we were the ONLY race to have tails. You might not think this is big, but trust me, when trying to score red hot cyber, the tail is VERY important. Pre Vah Shir, I've parsed this out as about a +75% to cyber, especially if you can roleplay using your tail. It's probably been nerfed down to a +50% since we have to share it with Vah Shir now.

No other race, IMO, has the same amount of advantages as we do. Yes, Trolls have the same regen. Yes, Ogres have the Frontal Stun Immunity, which is arguebly the same or better then our racial bonuses, and yes, Vah Shir have about the same Cyber Bonuses... but no other race has AC, Regen, AND a Tail.
