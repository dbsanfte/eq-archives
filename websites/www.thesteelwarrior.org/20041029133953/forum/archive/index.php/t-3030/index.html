Uira Tawhaki
Let me see if I can clear this up a bit...

1. The Adventure Stone
This is an Augmentation for a charm, not a charm itself. Combine it with your Intricate wooden charm or some other charm.

2. Wayfarer Flags
After you successfully complete 2 missions (102 adventure points on normal level) in a given camp you can talk to an NPC there and get a flag similar to the one you got when you got the Adventure Stone.

It is still somewhat unclear how this works
A. Is it the 2 succesful adventures that earned you the flag or is it the <= 102 adventure points that earned you the flag? [I would lay money on points]
B. Do the number of successful adventures / points required to get the next flag scale or do they remain at a constant 2 / <102?

3. Improving Stats on Adventurer's Stone
The stone improves as you get flags with *all* camps.

The statement that you need 2 succesful missions per flag and 5 flags to improve the stone is accurate in that the first flag per camp comes at 2 succesful mission (or <102 adventure points, see above) and you need flags from all five camps to improve the stone.

Not quite true (or ambiguous at least)

You need to get a certain amount of points in a theme to unlock the items in that theme's teirs. Once unlocked, you can use points from anywhere to buy them.

eg:

You have:
300 points from Guk
400 points from Everfrost
600 points from Butcherblock
Total = 1300 points

You cannot buy items from the 500 tier in Guk or Everfrost, but you can buy from the 500 teir in Butcherblock. You can buy 2 items from the 500 point tier from Butcherblock if you wish, leaving yourself with 300 points left.

If you then went out and gained 700 points in Mistmoore (total points now =1000), you could buy a 500 tier item or two from Mistmoore, or another one or two 500 point items from Butcherblock

If that made sense.
