Usually you want Stamina and Hp Gear if possible. Natural AC bonus will help you decide about taking Skyshrine vs. Kael armor.

The problem for iksars is that it is going to be 100% a patchwork of items. (as was anyone who did everything up to VT in Luclin)

The best is not one set. Things like Seru BP and Gaunts of Dragon Slaying and Cazic choker and Belt of Dorf Slaying and Strength of Elements and Spirit Wracked Cords. (Just to show the range of debatable items and all the weird places they can come from.

(Not sure on Vex Thall upgrades, I am sure there is plenty of +125 stuff there)

You honestly would have to go slot by slot and take a look at the different spots available starting with raw hps, making sure you have 41% haste, then moving to Stamina if you are challenged in that regard...

AC will mostly take care of itself here as you are talking the highest-end Luclin and Velious stuff and especially if not being used on the mega-hitting PoP mobs.
