Exiled is stunable, so have a paladin or 2 chain stun him and he will not get off his 20K DD. It is not a DT and can be interupted.

If you are going to 1 group the cycle I would reccomend 2 paladins to tank and wave heal. Bard for Cursed to resist disease based AE charm. Rogue for dps, cleric and shaman.

Cleric heals MT, paladins keep the group healed, Bard keeps group from being charmed. shaman keeps the mob slowed.

I 2 grouped this cycle back in PoP at lvl 65 as welll and it wasn't hard. Therefore I firmly believe a good group of lvl 70s could do this encounter.

Glyphed is easy CR/MR resists for smal hp dot and AE FD. Dain fight on steroids and fully debuffable. Small Rampage.

Exiled you DA pull and he is unslowable, but cripple is good. His 20K DD is interupptable and goes off every 60 seconds. if you time it right a bard DA song can bounce it, but just easier to chain stun him. Basicly a Vindi fight otherwise. FR/MR resists for the small HP Dot.


Cursed is the easiest of the 3, just alot of HP and flurries, but slowed a lvl 60 cleric with FT 15 could keep one MT alive. Keep anyone without bard song away from him and you could prolly trio Cursed, just will take awhile. DR/MR resists for AE charm and small HP dot. Wave heals are awesome if one grouping this.
