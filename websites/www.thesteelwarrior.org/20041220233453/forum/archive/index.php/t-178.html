I would say one of the things you could do to really help yourself is get some friends and do the "plate house" in kael. This is the royal house that is part of the 9th coldain ring. If you start the cycle and finish it yusually a couple plate items drop, pretty much how i got all my thurg armor. We got a bunch more drops that you would ever get in the arena, and it was fun to do.
Orc fang earing wouldn;t be a great improvment . . . save up for the earing of solstice, and Val Shir earing those are some nice earing slot items. I'd ditch the mushroom veil for crystal spider eye's. Shoulder slot items are where dwarves are screwed bad (just not much available for us). I wear crested spaulders (had them since i was 40ish). There is a easy quest (dragonic pauldrons?) if you are on CoV faction. I would also suggest you get another 6ac 65hp ring and ditch the other one. I would also suggest you try and sell the snowchipper, it's kinda slow and proc is not much aggro. Get a frosty, and get something like defiance for your main hand (or just sell the snowchip and upgrade a few armor slots). I had a snowchipper and hated it . . . even with 255dex it never wanted to proc for me, when it did really didn;t help aggro at all. The dwarf cultural is pretty nice, but you may find thurg is much easier to get (because cultural can be so expensive).
As far as goals just keep raising the bar for yourself, and don;t get too discouraged. At 50 i was in full crafted barely 2k hp's and an ac less than 950. I just kept working on it, grinding away in sebelis upgrading each slot as i could. It would really help you if you have a good solid guild to help. We farmed the crap outta umbral planes . . . heck even my alts have killer weapons outta that zone. Just set yourself realistic goals, look up stuff you can camp on alakazaam (or do with guild) and eventually you'll reach your goal and then set a new one ;-).

You are about to enter a few levels that I found really hard - getting to 55 is hard work.

Dont sweat you equipment or stats - I am an Ogre with maxed sta and while it helps at 65 with no shammy around its of limited use really. The 44 44 axe would be a great bit of kit for you.

What I did when i didnt have much cash at lev 50 was to be quite mathmatical with the equipment I could buy and the upgrade it gave me (6 ac 65 hps are unsurpassable on this formula - as also are Crusty legs for the 14 sta)

If you dont mind doing it - selling the 'Chipper and get a Frosty. I would get a Snare Whip also as I used it to 58 with great agro. In the other slots try and Cobalt (Legs, Arms, Hands) as its real cheap now because Thurg MQs are dirt cheap.
