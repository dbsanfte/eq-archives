Cuthbadth
i didnt notice your weapons the first time looking at your magelo, but now that i do, you should probably get new ones before getting any more armor. it wont be doing you any good if you're not getting beat on.

The very first thing I would do is replace your neckpiece, 0 AC is bad.

Amulet of Mummy Wrappings are pretty cheap, although I'm sure others will suggest something better.

Also, upgrade your other wrist, lots of choices there. 15 AC is low for a warrior, heck the bracer of benevolence quests are free if you have good CoV faction and a couple of friends (only a few ac better, but ok sta/hp).

Obsidianwood Compound or similar bow would give more ac and hp...can be expensive though, not sure how your money situation is.

Of course, LDoN can provide upgrades for about every slot if your plat poor but online a lot. I'd also consider replacing your weapons with some agro generating ones if you're main tanking a lot (if you're not not tanking for groups, then your ac is wasted of course). My weapons and my belt all came from Droga, if you can get a group (or at least 1 healer + 1 slower) to go with you, you can get all the drops yourself.

Your legs should be (relatively) cheap to upgrade: Kylong Shinguards or Greaves of the Orcish Master.

To be honest, I go into bazaar from time to time and just set all the settings for my warrior and just see whats affordable but yet still an upgrade for me. If you get too much junk, up your minimum price to see useful gear.

All that being said, I know personally I dropped close to a hundred hp in gear just a month or two ago to gain 50 ac. Since you don't have many AA yet, remember you'll get more HP that way too. I typically use the 1 ac ~ 6 hp rule. I also try to get pure HP now over a similar item with STA, that lets me avoid having to spend aa to up my sta cap for awhile longer.
