Your gear has alot of decent upgrade options. I'd recommend first putting either the anger 2 or 510 pt yak on your CHSD. Then work on getting a couple ranks of weapon affinity (WA). Should be plenty of agro.

If you have some decently geared friends you should see notable upgrades in hard ldon at the finger/ear/hand/wrist/feet spots. You may also wish to consider the 760 legs from MiR and the 1492 (select pieces) from the the Nro camp.
