i was under the impression the cleric aa did not increase chance of DI to fire, but increased chance of full heal as opposed to partial heals.

There are 3 levels of the cleric AA.

(going from memory)

First level - gives an additional chance to fire (if you fail your check, it will check again)
Second level - chance of a partial heal even on a fail
Third level - increases heal to full

The AA doesn't seem to work exactly as it is worded in the description. According to the text it seems like the third level will ALWAYS heal but we know that isn't true. But given good CHA on the tank and level 3 of the AA, it has been parsed at (again by memory) 95% fire rate (including partial heals).

The main problem with DI nowdays isn't the fail rate. I don't think you can get much better then 95%. The problem is that some mobs hit so hard that it never gets a chance to check.

On a 10k tank, 15% is 1.5k HPs. Unlike in Luclin when the cleric AA first came out, a LOT of mobs hit harder than that. Even 20% is only 2k HPs - by elementals you are running into 3k hitters so this still wouldn't save you all the time but would give more of a chance for there to be a chance for it to fire.

I'm not sure what high end tanks are up to these days but 20% of 12k is still only 2.4k so again its not a free ride. You'd have to get up to 15k HPs before you could have a 3k safe zone. I'm sure there are tanks that will get there but when they do the mobs will probably be hitting harder then 3k anyway? Someone further along in the game would know more about the details here but I think in theory I'm right?

I wouldn't mind it firing more often. I hate it when it never has a chance to fire and we lose a tank.
