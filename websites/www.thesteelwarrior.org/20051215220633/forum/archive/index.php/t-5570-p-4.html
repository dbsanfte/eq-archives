Why is rampage a special case? Weapon delay does not change the ability to proc per swing. The effective weapon delay certainly will affect the chances to proc on a particular swing, but it does not change the fact that any single swing can produce a proc.

The fact that individual swings can proc is the reason that someone with max offensive AAs gets more procs/minute than someone with the same DEX and no offensive AAs. This has been hashed out many times, in particular when people claimed to have weapons with multiple procs going off on the same swing.
