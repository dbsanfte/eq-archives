As far as AA's I would do last one in run3, then ND3, AT, CA3, CS3, PE, and that will keep you busy for awhile. I would suggest that you get Anger II( 510 points in EF) or III ( 1150 Points) for your CHoS. But keep in mind if/when you upgrade your weapons later your augs will go poof, so choose them wisely.

Ldon exp vs. PoP really depends on what group you get into. I'ts really hit and miss... Figure a level 65 normal Ldon adv is approximately 1 percent AA exp per mob, and BoT, PoV, HoH is about 2 percent per mob. So a good Dps PoP group with consistant pulls will be best exp. If you plan on grouping in PoP I would say the ones I just mentioned are your best choices for exp, unless you are flagged for Tacticts.

ooops...

ND3= Natural Durabilty 3
AT= Area Taunt
CA3= Combat Agility 3
CS3= Combat Stability 3
PE= Physical Enhancement
BoT= Bastion of Thunder
PoV= Plane of Valor
HoH= Halls of Honor

Hope that helps :)
