"I just want to know if the various no drop things (Like Sky Shrine Armour) is possible to get without a clan."

At level 65, the Halls of Testing, where skyshrine armors drop, are quite 1 groupable. Definitely 2 groupable.

There are ALSO quite a few upgrade options available in some of the lower tiers of planes of power to smaller forces.

BUT, as the person above stated, you will find the BEST available no drop items for the small, 1 to 2 groups of friends or forces come from Lost Dungeons of Norrath.

Either the stuff that drops in the hard adventures. Or the stuff you spend adventure points on.

It takes time, mind you, but you can get some Vex Thal quality items from LDoN, without the forces needed to raid Vex Thal.

Good luck. Have fun. And try not to let the fact that you have kill five BAZILLION sand elves to get anywhere discourage you.
