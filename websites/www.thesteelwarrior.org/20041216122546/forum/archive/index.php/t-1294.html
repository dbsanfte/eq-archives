Assigning loot such that the Main Tank, or the top few tanks get all the upgrades first is without doubt the best way to progress faster, have less wipes on raids and less frustration..

However guilds that do this seem to implode a lot, or go through several incantations. Several guilds on povar that have huge member turnovers come to mind hehe. I dont think its much coincidence that they to have a officers assigned loot system.

My guild uses a DKP type system which tends to spread out the items amound everyone. Everyone earns points for attendance and items are auctioned to the highest bidder.

The plus side is their is much much less drama. Very rarely do people argue about loot assignments. Makes for a much more stable guild.

The down side is rarely a casual player who has hoarded their points for the past 6 months will walk away with a very top of the line item which is effectively wasted. Also some heavy raiders tend to spend up big and actually do worse in terms of loot compared to more moderate raiders who spend more wisely.
