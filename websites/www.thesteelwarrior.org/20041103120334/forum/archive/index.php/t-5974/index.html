To advance in PoP, you have to get flags. These flags come from killing raid mobs (often the gods/goddesses of eq) in their own environment. Once you get the flags from these mobs (or combination of mobs), you can move on to the next plane in that line of flag progression. Generally accepted tiers are as follows:

Tier 1
Plane of Nightmare
Plane of Innovation
Plane of Disease
Plane of Justice

Tier 2
Plane of Valor
Plane of Storms
Plane of Torment
Crypt of Decay

Tier 2.5
Plane of Tactics (it doesn't really fit into tier 2 or 3...it's as hard as a tier 3 zone, but only takes a mob from a tier 1 zone to get in)

Tier 3
Bastion of Thunder
Halls of Honor
Tower of Solusek Ro

Tier 4
Plane of Fire
Plane of Earth
Plane of Air
Plane of Water

Tier 5
Plane of Time


I think that about covers it.
