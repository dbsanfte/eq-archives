By all indications PB works WITH ferocity. It adds attacks over and above what ferocity will give you. So if you score a natural single swing round, a succesfull ferocity roll gives you 2 attacks then on top of that you can score a successful PB and make it a triple attack round from a single attack round.

The big question now is, will flurry proc off an unnatural triple thus giving you 5 swings in what was supposed to be a single swing round? It's hard to say for certain, but all the extra attacks add up for all the AA's spent. I'd still go with Ferocity first personally if I had it to do over again.
