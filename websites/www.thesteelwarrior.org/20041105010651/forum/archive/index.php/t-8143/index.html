Depending on how much you want to spend, there are a few slots you could buy some nice upgrades. LDoN looks like the best route at this point since it will get you the AA you need on top of some upgrades.

Helm - Helm of Ralloz Zek, RA Helm (put haste here adn you can upgrade belt to Rubicite)
Legs - Ornate, Fierce Heraldic, RA
Finger - Steel Ring
Wrist - RA
Ear - Tear of Rainmaker, Ear Symbol of Veteran

If you get some defensive AA and can do some hard LDoN, there is a slew of upgrades you can get from drops in there (Cape of Broken Enchantments, Necklace of the Troglodyte, Earring of Sand Tides, Lost Elven Ring, Bloodied Boots of Dark RItes)
