Getting AA before 65 is a waste of time unless your gear is seriously subpar. The only AAs I would recoommend before 65 are run3+3 others just to get your title. If you get any amount of flags at all, you can do most of your first 6 without ever turning on AA exp. Once you have your title, head for 65 before you ever do any AA. As long as you have your title, there is no way of knowing just how many AA you actually have, and most people won't bother to do the math to figure out you must have less than 24 total. When people put groups together, they will take the higher level warrior, it's human nature to take the bigger better number. No one ever asks how many AAs you have, they just do a /who all lfg 65 and pick from that list.

There are tons of other tangible reasons to get 65 as well - more HP, better mitigation, better taunt, more DPS, better chance to resist spells, and more AA options are just a few.
