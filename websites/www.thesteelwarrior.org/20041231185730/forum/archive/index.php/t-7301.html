Here are a number of things I like about CoH and that I disliked about EQ.

No corpse runs. As a Warrior I always hated corpse runs in EQ. After having fought tooth and nail to some deep dark corner of a dungeon, to finally lose for one reason or another, and then to have to try and get my corpse back was always very frustrating. I can remember more than once staying up way past the point I should have been in bed because my corpse was down at the bottom of some dungeon along with the rest of my group and if we didn't get it then, we might not get it at all. Hated it. It sucked. I know some people wax poetic about CRs, but I'm not one of them.

Everyone can solo. I love this about CoH, the fact that if you don't care to have a group or can't find one then you can just go solo on your own. As a Warrior I always hated being so utterly and completely dependent. Gone are the days of my sitting at a zone in someplace, like sebilis where you can't even get back out easily, hoping like hell someone will pick me up for a group.

Death of the uber elite. Hate to say it, but I despise the path EQ has taken there. I have friends in the game that I would have enjoyed playing with, but couldn't because they were always at a raid. And even when I could play with them, often times we couldn't play where they'd prefer to play because I didn't have the flags required or the gear level necessary. So we'd end up playing alts. But it still kind of sucked. And I also hate how in EQ if you don't raid, you basically reach a point where you can't progress any further except through AAs or by doing a couple of hundred LDoN missions. Sorry, but LDoN just isn't that exciting a place to play in. CoH doesn't have that problem. Even if you out level your friends you can at least sidekick one of them. And everything seems to be relatively equally available to anyone willing to play the game and not just to those willing to "raid".

Death of the "virtual penis". Everyone gets enhancements, but no one in CoH talks smack about how their enhancements are better than your enhancements or how they have all these uber inspirations while you don't. While I didn't suffer from it too often, there always seems to be someone around more interested in running their mouths about their "gear" than in playing the game in EQ. But in CoH that absolutely is not true, as far as I've seen.
