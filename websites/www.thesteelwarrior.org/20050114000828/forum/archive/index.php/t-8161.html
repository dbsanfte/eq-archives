Waist:

i) Visit Droga in a couple levels with your shammy (friend /wink) and get yourself a sash of wonder from the Bone Knight Runri camp. Heck, your shaman might be able to solo that now.

ii)If you don't want haste, there is a rare belt that drops in Maiden's Eye from the four arms. 20 ac 15 str 15 wis no level req, if I recall.

iii)If you don't want haste, don't care about level req, there is a nice belt that drops from CoD rats (it should be in my inventory if you click magelo, I use it when I have tribute haste on for an extra hp/ac bump).

Earrings are expensive, your best bet is either save money and do as suggested above or pick one of a few select quests that you will not finish until your in your 50's or 60's (Luclin Era)...presuming LDoN is not an option, otherwise Tak has a nice cheap (100 points) 2 slot earring that isn't too bad since you can sell it back later.

Shoulders: You could get CoV faction and get the same ones I have fairly easily with your shaman or you could wait until you're higher and try for Strength of the Elementals one.

Neck: as mentioned above (it's a normal LDoN Tak drop, at least at 65 it is). Maybe you could talk your shammy into doing a few and see if he/she could roll on it for you or just buy one. But honestly, I'd buy earrings first, get the biggest bang for your plat first.
