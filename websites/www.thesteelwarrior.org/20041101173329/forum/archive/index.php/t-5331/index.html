I fell into 230k myself the other night (sold old livestone greaves). I'd advise that whatever you buy make sure its droppable. Why? Becuase as you upgrade via LDoN/the odd raid you can then turn around and re-sell the item. Your gear as such becomes a store of value.

Ornate in my opinion is the LAST thing you should buy. Basically you are destroying your pp.

In the line of thought I would suggest:

1. Black plated collar
2. Stonewood bow
3. Amy Mask
4. Barbed Rubi Pauld
5. Boots of Storm

Obviously you can't get all of them (or maybe you can I don't know yer server). Also remember with GoD coming out that generally with the release of a new expansion that generally you see price deflation as new substitute items are introduced into the market. If you can hold onto yer pp til then it may go ALOT farther.
