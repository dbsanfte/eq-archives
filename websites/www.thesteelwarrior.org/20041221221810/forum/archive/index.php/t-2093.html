Because there are quite a few people who can't hit 255 stamina..either thru gear, or maybe they don't have a shaman around all the time. The deal is that the only way you're going to max stamina is if you meet one of the 3 conditions --

1) You rolled an Ogre.
2) You have enough platinum to get the correct stamina maxing gear in the bazaar
3) You're in a high end guild.

Guess what...the majority of the players in this game DO NOT meet any of those requirements. Stamina is the best choice for them simply because the 150 extra HP they could get from 25 stamina points at level 60 would be a HUGE deal....far more benefit than 25 points of AGI or STR or anything else you want to put your points in.

If you don't meet those requirements above, stamina is the way to go. If you do, I guess it really doesn't matter where you put your starting stats, because sooner or later you'll end up with 255 anyway. I'd even go so far as to say that it's useful for ogres and rich people too..because the sooner you cap stamina, the sooner you can start dropping stamina for HP gear, giving you yet a bigger lead over someone who didn't pump STA at creation.

Depends what your goal with the character is...

At low levels strength will make the most difference because it effects your attack so high strength will make it easier to clear through the moss snakes. Early on stamina makes very little difference to your HP total at all.

When you hit 50-65 ish range and your building up your gear and assuming you aren't a filthy twink (filthy twink being one with a BoC at level 10, not some dude with a couple of bits of cobalt and a lammy at 30) you'll be watching your HPs and at this stage as people have mentioned the extra HPs you get from 25 stamina will be significant.

If you play your character all the way to uber raiding guild level, (or your the type of filthy twink that I mentioned above) then your stamina will be on of the first stats you cap. I have a naked stamina of 80 and at the moment it buffs to 281 (1 point over my cap) so I'm not upset about not putting points into stamina now, though when my gear was lacking I used to really lament the number of HPs I lost to fattys just on the account of starting stats.
