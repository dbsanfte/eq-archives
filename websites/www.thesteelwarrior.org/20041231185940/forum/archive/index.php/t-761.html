1) Weapons. Ditch the Bed Leg Club. Work on the epic and get a Frosty in the meantime. Sell that Great Maul of Hatred. On Zeb you would be able to get a Frosty with that sale.

2) Gear. Hunt in UP and get the Moktor Legs. They will be a significant upgrade to your current ones. Get those or buy cultural or Thurg legs.

Replace the wolf fang earring with the ME 4arms drop, Vah Shir Claw Earring. It is a fairly common drop and should only take you an hour of hunting (Do it at the Akheva zone) to get one.

Do the Draconic Pauldrons quest if Ally to CoV.

Get more Thurg gear (Helm and boots are better than what you currently have)

3) Ac vs Hps. Conventional thought is for Pre-PoP get to 1250 AC then look for raw hps. I would recommend getting over 1200 unbuffed and then going hps.

Good luck.
