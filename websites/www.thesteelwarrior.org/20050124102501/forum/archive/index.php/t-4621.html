Go beat up a skeleton in a newbie area to get a rusty 2H weapon.

Then go and smash up some Bandits in Paludal Cavern (with a group would be cool), you can tank them fairly well, even naked and they drop ringmail type armor. Heck, you might even get a tiny sliver of exp, though most should be green to you.

Also, sometimes they drop 2-3 pp and some other sellables, so loot up a storm.

Buy a better 2H in the Bazaar ASAP offcourse.
I must admit to not knowing what is good at that low levels, waaaay back in the days when I was at that level I used a Langseax of the Wolves ... which is a pitiful sword by todays standards - But hey, that was pre-Kunark.

And if you feel like buggering people, try and ask someone in POK (if you have planes of power expansion) or Cabilis to buy you a full suit of Leather armor on behalf of you having lost your corpse. Since leather pieces are only 2-4 pp a piece, someone are likely to help you get restarted.



Edit :
Oh, and if you ask people for help on getting some leather armor, make sure to find the leather armor merchant before asking .. one thing is asking for their plat, another is asking for them to spend time on finding the merchant.

Also, when in Bazaar, use the 2H slash filter in "Type" and limit the Platinium range to be searched on. When the search pops up, sort by prize and start looking at the lower end of the range, check all the list and see what is the best bang for the buck.
