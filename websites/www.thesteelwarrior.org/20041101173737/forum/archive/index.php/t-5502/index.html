LDoN can be your friend. I did LDoN's at 64 for xp, now do them for AAxp.

Many times with even a mediocre group you can get a regular LDoN done in 35 to 40 minutes (good group we are getting those done in 25). For reg xp I was averaging about 6 blues per adventure. On AAxp I am averaging about 13 to 15 blues per. So if you can get 3 or 4 of those in per night you can get the aaxp plus make some cash and what not.

Just my 2cp's worth
Khabok
I 2 boxed warrior and shaman through the 60's a lot. At that level you should be able to make decent exp from the Gamite frogs in Valor. Just head east from the zone in to the river and at corner of zone there is a rock with a frog on top and one to each side. I used to single pull them easily with the shaman using Malo. Also there are a couple of the single worm things nearby. You wont make the exp you do in a full group but its better then sitting around making nothing looking for a group. Also the middle frog on the rock is a PH for a named type that once in a while drops a 2 hand sword ya can prolly make a few K from in Bazaar.

If those are too tough for the duo, before that I used to do the Hobgoblins in PoN. Set up across river and you can easily single pull the croc and the first three hobs along the river, just have to watch for trains from folks heading to hedge or PoNB.
