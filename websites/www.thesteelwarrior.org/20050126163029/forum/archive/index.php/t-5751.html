If STA isn't maxed yet, SotB can give you an extra 50 or so HP's, plus a 75 DD proc as opposed to 30 DD, and the proc can stun up to level 65, which the RSHS does not. The 2-second stun is nice, though, but it wouldn't make any difference against unstunnable mobs, or against mobs over level 55; the stun aggro is probably the same as for the SotB. The END is nice, but as I've yet to run out of END where another 40 would make a difference, it's not that important to me.

It has a lower level rec, so I tend to think (and agree that) it's designed for lower level characters, kind of the pre-Droga taunt weapon, if you will. I might buy it if I didn't own the SotB and it was markedly cheaper than the Droga weapon, but I wouldn't "upgrade" to it from an SotB, not as a warrior.
