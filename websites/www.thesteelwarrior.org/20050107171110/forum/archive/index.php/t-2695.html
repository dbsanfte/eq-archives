If you have a network and another computer, you can use eqcompanion to parse yourself as you play. You can use one sword one fight, and the other another, and instantly see the results. 2h is quite random though. I use a greater poleaxe of sorrow (35/24) when going for low aggro, and from behind the mob with standard buffs (1450 attack for me?) I get in the ballpark of 100 to 110 dps on average (with most offensive AA's).

I have heard your theory about high attack discounting the damagebonus problem on weapons with less than 28 delay. I think probably the issue is your attack vs. the mobs AC. If a mob has high enough ac to mitigate alot of your hits, then a high damage bonus is really going to help you. If your attack is enough to penetrate a mob's AC, then the lack of damage bonus isn't as significant. At least, that is my guess of how it works. You really are probably just gonna have to parse for yourself. My guess is that the 45/35 will perform better.
