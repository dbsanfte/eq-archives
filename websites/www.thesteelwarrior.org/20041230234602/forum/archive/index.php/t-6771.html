What are tomes? I thought i read somewhere about new disciplines for taunting?

Yes we have new combat abilities for agro starting at lvl 20 with provoke. The upgrade to provoke isn't until 52 w/ bellow. You can buy tomes in the PoK library or the EC tunnel (voris flatblade is the merch he is along the wall near the adventure camp).

Just turn the purchased tome into a warrior guild trainer to be able to use the skill. Alt + C opens up your combat abilities window listing which ones you can use.

The fungi should be able to get you enough cash to buy some decent gear they sell for around 100k on my server usually. It can vary....
