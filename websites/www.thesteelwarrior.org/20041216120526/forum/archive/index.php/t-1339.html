I *used* to have a baby warrior but no longer, instead my play character is an enchanter. Being one of those classes that generally lives and dies by aggro control, the differences between warrior aggro establishment and pally/sk aggro establishment are of a more than passing interest to me.

As it stands, if I have a warrior MTing, even one who is very well equipped and skilled at his class, I have to massively throttle down my casting, delaying my slows/tashs and spacing out my (pathetic) nukes. With a hybrid tanking, I can go full bore on incoming with slow and all is good.

That's an imbalance. If by my throttling back with a warrior MT had as discernable a net benefit (IE with a warrior MT despite having to manage aggro more carefully the reward is greater), as having a pally/sk MT does now, all three would be equally desirable in groups, but as it stands, warriors are the best at the 'passive' side of tanking by a ridiculously small margin, while Pallies/SKs are the best at the aggro side of tanking by an outstandingly large margin.

The solution isn't to nerf hybrid aggro OR to give warriors some sort of innate +aggro ability, I think the solutions lies elsewhere and did a little brainstorming.

Right now, in terms of 'special' innate abilities, paladins get LoH, SKs get HT, and warriors get berserker frenzy with crippling blows. LoH and HT scale and are affected by specific AAs, berserker doesn't.

Right now, certain mobs and pets have the ability to enrage at low life for fixed periods, auto-riposting all attacks from the front...

What about a combination of the two? Say at lvl 60, instead of just going into a berserker frenzy and getting crippling blows, instead the warrior would go into a berserker frenzy, get crippling blows, and be enraged for a fixed period, say 6 seconds (less than the cast time of CH to avoid trivializing end game encounters)

Now when the warrior is facing that unslowed mob in PoP that hits like a mack truck on steroids that's been hopped up on amphetamines, there's a clear benefit for groups and slowers and healers in timing things and having the warrior MT, once frenzy is triggered, suddenly there's 6 seconds of respite from damage, with the warrior dishing out ripostes and possible double ripostes to build even more damage based aggro, bumping up on the hate list that much faster so that when slow lands I might not get the mobs immediate attention, while also allowing the cleric to wait just that bit longer to prevent getting aggro for the CH to land... it would also somewhat reduce the absolute dependance on slowers in groups, since with a warrior and cleric an unslowed mob is no longer the OMG THIS IS GONNA SUCK situation that it is now.

Pallies/SKs still shine as tanks with their aggro management, but now warriors have a true BENEFIT to a group while being less of a liability in aggro terms, win/win yes? All of the code for this is already present in game, it would just need to be linked together properly and tied with a bow. Heck, combine this with some of the warrior disciplines and warriors have a way of contributing some serious DPS while tanking... all without having any spells, +aggro self-procs, or any swings of the nerf bat
