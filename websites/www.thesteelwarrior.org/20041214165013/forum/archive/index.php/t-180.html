Bash is by no means as important as your other weapon skills, but it is a pretty handy skill to have. If you're in somewhere like Sebilis before level 55 and need to stop mobs from gating its a good trick to have up your sleeve.

If you're planning on taking the Warrior to the high end and get involved in some major raids and such, Bash is very nice to have for those occasions when you need to tank-mez stuff.

Is it important? Not really. Is it nice to have for those situations when you need it? Yes.

If you are a small race, the only reason that I can think of to work up your bash skill is if you are planning on using the "ghetto bashing" technique. That is basically swapping in a shield to bash when fighting a caster when you see him casting, and then swapping back after he is interrupted or successfully casts. Some warriors are very good at this, and it is much easier to set this up effectively under the new UI, but it can be a pain and most do not bother.

If you are a large race, just use Slam instead which uses the bash skill numbers and can also interrupt casters. At level 55 as previously mentioned, a warriors kick can also interrupt spell casters, so at that point small race warriors just stick with kick.

Also worthy of note is the fact that the skill cap for bash is much higher than kick. At higher level, you'll hit more often with bash than with kick. If you're a large race, bash(slam) is better. You might want to max kick out for those times when you're under some form of illusion that doesnt let you use slam. If you're a small race, you're pretty much stuck with kick unless you want to keep swapping your offhand weapon for a shield, or unless you want to get a furious bash shield for tanking really heavy hitting mobs.
