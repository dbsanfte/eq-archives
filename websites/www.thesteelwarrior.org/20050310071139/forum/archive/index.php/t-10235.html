I did a quick check on Drinal. Logged on a character who never done LDoN, he's 6700th place. Back in golden age of EQ, that number is around 10-12K. So, it's at worst 50% decline.

A character shows up on the LDoN ranking list if they have at least one LDoN done (win or loss) and they've logged on in the last 30 days. It is easily the best snapshot of a server short of having a script to do a zillion /who (and even then you would miss the anon guys).

And that really doesn't take into the account of the fact that no one does LDoN anymore when OoW come out so any newly created character after OoW will pretty much never be on the LDoN ranking list. Therefore even if the population of EQ never changed, you'd expect less people on the LDoN ranking list now as opposed to back when LDoN first came out. Drinal has always been on the lower end population, so I don't see any reason why we would get hit harder or not as hard compare to any other server, so I feel it is reasonable to use this to extrapolate to rest of the servers. Of course, your guild/server might get hit harder, but that's just how average works out.
