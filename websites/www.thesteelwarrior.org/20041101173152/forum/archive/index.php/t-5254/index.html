Fitemore
Anger 1/2/3 is 200/400/600 hate per proc.

Two anger 2 procs would generate ~ three procs per minute (two mainhand and one offhand) at 400 hate per, or 1200 hate per minute from the augments. One anger 3 proc generates ~ two procs per minute at 600 hate per, or 1200 hate per minute from augment. It's the same hate per minute, but the more even distribution of procs from the two augments would generally be better. Personally, I'm all about saving up for anger 3 augments because it makes for 1 much better weapon rather than 2 somewhat better weapons.

Anger 3 on an agro weapon (a weapon that already has a stun or enraging blow proc) better than ANY agro weapon without an agro augment on it. PERIOD.

Anger 3 is like run speed 3. No one who has posted here has said, man, i regret putting anger 3 on my agro weapon. Just like no one has said, man, those points in run speed 3 were a waste.
