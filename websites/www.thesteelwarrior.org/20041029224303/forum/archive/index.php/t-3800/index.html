Taminy
With Dozekar, less is more.

You need some basic things.. you need one person to tank him of course. You need one person to slow him. You need enough healers to keep the tank alive. Really, that's it - it will take a long time for one person to melee him to death though.

What kind of slower is good enough? Hard to say - a level 60 shaman would probably have trouble slowing him without an enchanter to tash. I was able to slow him without much trouble with my 65 shaman though (no enchanter, just tash stick + malos). Enchanters would probably have some trouble slowing him without malo/malos.

How many main tank healers you need will depend on their FT/mana preservation stuff (go SCM) if they have clarity (and other mana regen buffs) up or not, and how many hps/ac/defensive AAs your main tank has.

You can throw in some DPS.. but realize that any DPS person you add will require you to have more healing. This is more so true for melee DPS than caster DPS (Dozekar does a 400 damange + dispel AE every 15 seconds or so on the melees). One priest should be able to keep a few melee DPS people alive if they're doing nothing else. Oh yeah, any melee person without an insta clicky shouldn't even bother fighting.

I know people would one group him even before PoP. The typical group I saw would be: warrior, 2 clerics, shaman, enchanter, rogue (or monk or ranger). Both clerics heal the warrior. Shaman debuffs, slows, and heals the rogue/monk/ranger (go Torpor). Dot if mana available. Enchanter tashes, helps get early slow to stick, then I guess just nukes/dots. What the warrior and rogue/monk/ranger do is pretty self explanatory.

Now warrior, cleric, and shaman can trio him (some people on this board have 3 boxed him - though pretty sure all of them were at least somewhat elemental equipped). Warrior, druid, shaman might work too if the druid has good healing AAs and FT. As for duoing him... hm I don't know, that would be tricky, but I'd sure believe it if I saw it. Very well equipped tank/shaman maybe.

He's more about strategy than being trivial (he has so many HPs he's nearly impossible to zerg). He can wipe out 50 people if they all stand in AE range.

Oh yeah, ranger should be able to track him; I think a druid can as well. Plus there's always good ol' eye of zomm (go stalking probes).
