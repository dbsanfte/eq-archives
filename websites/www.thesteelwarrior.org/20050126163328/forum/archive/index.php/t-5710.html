Save a piece here and there you have already surpasssed NToV loot. I see that you have a sword off of Arch Lich in Ssra. NToV doesn't offer much in the way of Flowing Thought or Vengeance.

My advice,

Make a few runs through NToV just to get practice raiding, then abandon ToV(save Dozekar if you have the patience for some really nice shit). Move it on up to Ssra Temple, AoW(yer a big enough tank with 6 clerics behind ya on a 2 second rotation), velious kings(Dain, Yelinak, and KT will all be pushovers if your guild is equipped similarly to you) until you get a little flowing thought on yer clerics and some real Hitpoints and aggro on yer tanks.

At level 65 there's really no rason to settle for NToV loot...if yer good enough to raid NToV, yer good enough to kill everything in Luclin short of Emperor Ssra(yes that includes AHR, because by the time you get to her you will have a bit better gear and she's a PUSS once you manage to get by Emp).
