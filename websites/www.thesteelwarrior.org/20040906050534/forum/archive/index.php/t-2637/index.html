How about this sort of skill system? :

----------------------------------------------

The Art of War has improved greatly since the apparition of the Plane of Knowledge.
Warrior Guild Masters accross the world have been able to exchange their experiences, creating a whole new form of Art.
Soon enough, these masters have started teaching the basics of the new art to their disciples. Teaching them known weak spots, and ways of exploiting them.


New Weak spots are learnt through out the levels:

Level 20 - Body Shot
Level 35 - Arm Shot
Level 45 - Leg Shot
Level 55 - Head Shot

Among these Weak spots, different attacks can be used to cripple the enemy. These attacks are learnt throughout levels as well.

WeakSpot
level - name - (action required,chance of success) [Angle from mob] --> effect

Body Shot
Level 20 - Rib Jab - (Pierce,30%) --> Double Damage + Aggro
Level 30 - Chest Bruise - (Crush,30%) --> Double Damage + Aggro
Level 50 - Chest Impact - (Bash,50%) --> Double Damage + KnockBack/Stun
Level 60 - Gut Slash - (Slash,40%) --> 300 Damage + DoT
Level 65 - Dirty Strike - (Taunt,100%) [Back] --> Taunt + Aggro

Arm Shot
Level 35 - Vein Slit - (Slash,70%) --> DoT
Level 40 - Arm Lock - (Disarm,70%) [Back] --> Disarm + 100 damage + Stun
Level 59 - Hand Crush (Crush,30%) --> Attk Reduction
Level 62 - Arm Sprain (Taunt,40%) --> Slow

Leg Shot
level 45 - Trip - (Kick,90%) --> Feign Death + Stun, on fleeing mob
level 52 - Tendon Slice - (Slash,30%) [Back] --> Snare
level 59 - Hip Shot - (Archery,30%) --> Snare + DoT
Level 63 - Knee Crush - (Crush,30%) --> Root

Head Shot
level 55 - Eye Poke - (Taunt,40%) [Front] --> Blind + Stun
level 60 - Neck Snap - (Taunt,20%) [Back] --> 30,000 damage to mobs level 45 and under.
level 61 - Memory Lapse - (Bash,60%) --> Blur
level 64 - Skull Shatter - (Crush,30%) --> Long Stun


This is how it works:

You can concentrate yourself upon a certain weak spot. (Press one of the 4 WeakSpot skills).
During this time, you can deal certain attacks on that weak spot.
Different attacks demand different actions, to be executed. And there is a chance of success for each of these actions.

Each WeakSpot skill will last 3 seconds, and have a 30 second reuse.
All the weakspot timers are linked.



Example:

you want to snare a mob. So you have to slice it's tendon.

Equip a slashing weapon, get behind the mob, click Leg Shot.
You then have a 30% chance of snaring the mob for each Slash, during 3 seconds.

None of the effects would be resistable. This isnt magic ;).
You literally cut the mob's tendon.
