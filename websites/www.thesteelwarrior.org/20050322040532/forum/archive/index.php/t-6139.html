On the way to 65 and high end content a war/sk/pal would all be about the same given the same amount of time invested in their gear/spells.
At 65 it really depends what you're aiming for. If you want to duo/trio most of the time then the sk/pal would probably be 'better' due to more utility functions in the regular xp group. If you're planning on doing hard LDONs and GoD trials, then a warrior will make a much better tank assuming the same aa/gear effort. In the long run with 500+ AAs any of the plate tank classes will work. At the same time, a shaman would have a hard time being the only healer on a hard ldon for the first 150 AAs really. It can and is being done, it's just not efficient at all.
