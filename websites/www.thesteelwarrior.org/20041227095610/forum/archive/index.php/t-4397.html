Semi hijack - you should be using /shield in normal situations. It really is a nice ghetto AE taunt, I use to protect people who debuff or heal early and get aggro (especially if the mob resisted a slow).

In addition to stopping damage, it seems to make taunt work better and the mob more likely to turn and face meIt has a quick refresh of three minutes so you can use it in situations where you may not feel there is a dire immediate need for taunt (this is where you AE taunt).

The only downside to it really is that your shielded target has to be very close to you, and a lot of non-warriors don't know much if anything about /shield.
