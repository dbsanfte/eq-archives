CHoS/SotB combo is better for aggro, the reason the GSoT is so widely regarded as the best thing since sliced bread is the somewhat limited amount of 2h aggro weapons available, especially for casual non raiding folks, in tandem with the fact that especially early in PoP, before folks were geared up ripostes were pretty harsh. That having been said, if ripostes are a problem for you, GSoT is the way to go. If aggro is key, chos/sotb is stronger aggro. Tradeable upgrades to these are going to be few and far between (there are a few, but they're ridiculously expensive), BoC and SoD are the only two I can think of off the top of my head; some of the new GoD smithed items look decent though, will have to wait and see on those.
