I was curious about comparing ornate to fully augmented LDoN armor. Here's a sample:


Ornate gloves:
AC 33, STR 4, DEX 5, STA 8, AGI 3, HP 60
Fire 6, Cold 3, Poison 6
Miraculous Visions (30% haste buff spell)
one type 7 slot
(example: +12 hit points aug)


Royal attendant gauntlets of sand
AC 34, STR 8, STA 6, AGI 5, HP/MANA 75
Fire 7, Disease 7, Cold 7
Two type 2 slots, one type 3 slot
(example: Vengence IV aug and two +40 hit point augs)



Note that without any augments the two armors are pretty close. But because type 2 and type 3 augments are so nice compared to type 7, if you fully augment the LDoN armor it probably beats the ornate. In the example of gloves, I'd say Vengence IV and +80 hit points is better in a group than a haste buff spell (not a worn effect) and 12 hit points. After all, how often are you in a group and not already hasted with a better spell? The ornate is better if you're solo or have no haster.


I guess bottom line, though, they're both really, really nice and I wouldn't turn either one down short of something like elemental armor.
