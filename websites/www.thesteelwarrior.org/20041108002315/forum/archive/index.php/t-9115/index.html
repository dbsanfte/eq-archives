Kaesorn
Okay, here's what I've learned about these augs.

There are 21 dropped augs in OoW that are Attunable. They start out with certain stats, and can be upgraded to one of three levels by a character with high jewelcraft.

Levels go from Base -> Polished -> Brilliant Cut -> Radiant Cut. A stat aug will go 4/6/8/12, respectively. Ability augs tend to go 1/1/2/3 (Polished doesn't do anything, it seems), with DS aug going 2/2/4/6.

Then you can have a Shaman or a Rogue use their class ability (Alchemy or Poisonmaking) to add a Minor effect to it using Marks (bought from vendors). They can then upgrade those minor effects to either Medium effects or Pure effects. These special effects comprise of both Caster Foci and Melee Foci.

The higher the level, the higher the trivial for the combine (more difficult).

Also, stat augs can only be placed on FACE, WAIST, BACK, NECK, SHOULDERS. HP can only be put on HANDS, ARMS, FEET. Mana/Endurance can only be put on HEAD, WRIST, RANGE. Ability augs only fit in one spot, and it depends on the aug.

All augs are type 7 and 8.

The base gems are as follows:

Bloodflow- Strength
Mudflow - Stamina
Waterflow - Dexterity
Fireflow - Agility
Starflow - Intelligence
Boneflow - Wisdom
Skyflow - Charisma

Emberflow - Fire Resist
Stormflow - Cold Resist
Voidflow - Magic Resist
Mossflow - Disease
Shadowflow - Poison

Kyanite - HP
Sodalite - Mana/Endurance

Titanite - Regeneration - CHEST
Tourmaline - Mana Regeneration - HEAD
Sphalerite - DoT Shielding - BACK
Apatite - Stun Resist - NECK
Citrine - Damage Shield - SHOULDERS
Howlite - Strikethrough - FACE
Celestine - Spellshield - WAIST

Effects:

Melee:

Mark of Ferocity - Ferocity Focus
Mark of Dexterity - Dodge Focus
Mark of Haste - Haste
Mark of Battle - Attack
Mark of Sundering - Cleave Focus

Caster:
Mark of Aggression - Improved Damage Focus
Mark of Alacrity - Enhancement Haste Focus
Mark of Celerity - Affliction Haste Focus
Mark of Efficiency - Mana Preservation Focus
Mark of Extension - Extended Enhancement Focus
Mark of Healing - Improved Healing Focus
Mark of Potency - Affliction Efficiency Focus
Mark of Quickening - Spell Haste Focus
Mark of Sight - Vision Effect
Mark of Virility - Burning Affliction Focus

You can end up with augments that give 45hp and Ferocity III (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=75334) or Strikethrough 3% and Cleave III (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=74774).

Pretty useful, IMO. There's a downside on that except for Regeneration, a warrior cannot get any of the special abilites without losing a slot for a 10ac aug. Lack of AC augs or Shielding kinda make it lack effect...but the ability to gain Dodge/Ferocity/Cleave is pretty nice, as well as the 45hp augs.

Note: I left out the information on the actual combines other than the main items. On both cutting the gems or adding marks, you have to use 1-3 other items as well.

Edit: To remove the augs later, you must use a Class ** Augmentation Distiller that's appropriate to the level of the Gem:

Base - Class I
Polished - Class II
Brilliant - Class III
Radiant - Class IV
