You can do SM in the mid 30s; just start with the rightmost instance and work your way left as you level. There's good drops for most classes. Doan drops a nice dagger/staff for casters, Herod drops a nice helm/shoulder for tanks as well as a 37 DPS 2h axe, Mograine drops a 1500 armor shield (in fact it's his most common drop by far) and a 39 DPS 2h mace, and Whitemane drops a very nice hat for casters/priests, a 30 DPS +healing mace, and an excellent amulet for casters, priests, and paladins.

Pretty much everyone who's eligible should do In the Name of the Light. The prereqs are nothing, just a little running around to areas you should get gryph points to anyway. Last night in the final SM instance at 42 I was getting 110-160 exp per mob in a full group. When I solo in my chosen spot I get 200 exp per mob and obviously kill more slowly. Just make sure you kill Loksey in the library - a lot of people miss him. He's in the right wing as you enter the first courtyard, accompanied by three dogs.

Also an NPC in the Ironforge Explorer's League offers Mythology of the Titans - find a book in the SM library instance and you get a 6 sta/spr necklace and some exp, not bad at all. The book isn't out of the way, it's in the bottom left corner of a room you enter.

Additionally, when you do the final instance, don't forget the "secret" boss, an undead priest who drops several good green items. Once you get to the final cathedral with Mograine, check the room to the right of him, specifically a torch in the wall. If you can kill Mograine, you can kill Fairbanks easily.
