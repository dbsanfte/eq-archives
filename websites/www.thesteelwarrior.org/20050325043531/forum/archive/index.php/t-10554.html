Unless you're getting enough dodges so that you have an extra Overpower that you can't use due to recast (assuming the cost of Overpower is negligible) 1H or 2H obviously comes out in favor of 1H for overpower damage.

Let's say a 2H has twice the delay and twice the damage as a 1H (even though that's being very generous). each Overpower will hit twice as hard, but you only get Overpower half as often with a 2H. It comes out to be the same damage (actually 1H is more, because it's not just weapon damage).

This of course assumes you're not swapping weapons for overpower. This also ignores that if you dual wield you get way more than double the swings, even though your each overpower is still only 1/2 the strength as a 2H.

Another advantage to daggers that I have seen listed here is that adding damage to them is generally more beneficial than adding to other weapons. For instance a dagger with 1.5 delay that is the same base DPS as a sword with 2.5 delay will benefit much more by enchanting and/or sharpening stones applied to that dagger, making it a higher DPS weapon after the fact. As a protection/fury spec with no MS I find myself using the fastest weapons I can find, which often times are daggers. With a hotkey to weaponswap for overpower I can get the benefits of both worlds.

Another advantage of fast weapons is when used in conjunction with heroic strikes. If you ever find yourself with a lot of rage and not much to do with it (max sundered mob w/shouts used) spamming heroic strikes with a 1.5ish delay weapon can put out some pretty good damage.
