Neksnappa
First off, the frosty procs at level 40.

At level 38, a wurmslayer is still the most damaging weapon you'll be able to buy, and it will stay that way until you are almost level 50. Dual wield isnt the way to go if you're looking for dps. There's tons of 2hs/2hb weapons out there with a dmg/dly ratio of 1 or better nowadays, but most of them are required: 51 or higher. One great weapon for you would be a Reaver. It's 40/40 and has 12 or so to all saves. This will be a great dps improvement from dual wield. My advice to you would be keep the GCM/Frosty for when you need higher aggro, and a good ratio 2hs for dps.

Once you hit 51, start looking for weapons again. Just keep checking the bazaar for good deals on weapons that look better than yours.

Also, with 12k you might be able to upgrade your haste item. What are you using for haste now?
