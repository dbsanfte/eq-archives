With the dumbing down of Everquest continuing, and making it always easier to level without the time put into their characters, it constantly amazes, and eternally annoys me that I'm often grouped with lvl 60+ people that wouldn't know how to get to a zone or dungeon in the game without a porter. These are the same people that are clueless about most aspects of other people's classes, and to a large degree their own. I could talk about the mage that I saw pull 4 wurms in skyfire and died, then asked me (after I ressed with my bot cleric) how he'd been trying to "quad" for 3 days now but couldn't get the hang of it.

/sigh

To this end I've been swinging my guild around to the less used zones where you actually have to expend some effort to get into them.

Currently we did the quest to get everyone into Tactics where we pretty much have the run of the zone unless one of the god mobs is up. It's fun, we get some nice drops, and the best part is that THERE ARE NO FREAKING NOOBS!!!

I'm not worried about offending them, they don't know how to open a browser. :D

I'm willing to bet that the same situation exists in Torment and can key my guild for Torment with some effort on our part. But after kind of browsing 'khazam's mob and loot list for the zone, I don't think it will offer the same reward for time put in.

However, I've held the following belief for ALL mobs and zones in this game:

1. Good Loot
2. Good XPS
3. Easy Kill


now pick 2 of those. They apply to most mobs in game. I'm willing to bet that the xps to be gained in Torment outstrip what is found in Tactics or BoT. (I stay out of BoT because of the huge amounts of ppl camping anything that moves and the xps crawl unless you're in an upper camp.

So....is it worth my time and effort to get my guild keyed for Torment?

We're about 2 groups of 65's that REALLY play, with another 2 groups of support that are more or less on.
