This is how to get the MAXIMUM DS:

45 = Maelstrom of Ro (MAG L65 (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3486&source=Live))
33 = Kilva's Skin of Flame (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=1331&source=Live) (Potion (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=14453))
15 = Shield of Torment (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3609&source=Live) (Item (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=28664))
8 = Blessing of Vah Kerrath (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2247&source=Live) (Item (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=8364))
4 = Spikecoat (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=518&source=Live) (Item (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=2510) or Item (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=32024))
58 = Psalm of Veeshan (BRD L63^ (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3368&source=Live))
58 = Warsong of Zek (BRD L62''' (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3374&source=Live))
34 = Mark of the Righteous (CLR L65 (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3477&source=Live))
55 = Spirit of the Wood (DRU AA (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3279&source=Live))
650 = Wrath of the Wild (DRU AA (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3257&source=Live))
9 = Shield of Flame (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=411&source=Live) (Item* (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=4560))

So you can have a 969 point damage shield for one hit, a 319 point damage shield for 5 ticks after that, and a 264 point damage shield sustained DS.

You need Girdle of Intense Durability, Talisman of Vah Kerrath, Girdle of Living Thorns or Gleaming Bauble, a bard with Blaring Horn of Fire and the right AAs, and a druid with the right AAs.NOTES:
Kilva's Skin of Flame is from a Potion made by Alchemy.
Spirit of the Wood is a Druid AA ability.
Wrath of the Wild is a Druid AA ability.
Girdle of Living Thorns is dropped from Tunare in Plane of Growth.
Girdle of Intense Durability is dropped by Saryrn in Plane of Time.
Gleaming Bauble is from this quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2204).
Talisman of Vah Kerrath is dropped by Aten Ha Ra in Vex Thal.
^ 15 * 3.9 = 58 (modded with AAs and Blaring Horn of Fire)
''' 15 * 3.9 = 58 (modded with AAs and Blaring Horn of Fire)
*Shield of Flame only works when casted on the tank by a Bard using Singing Steel Bracer.
