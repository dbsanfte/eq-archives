I like the IDEA of weapon bane.

I just think they implemented it totally wrong.

What they SHOULD have done is something far more akin to Gnollslayer, but on a bigger scale.

There should have been an Orcbane weapon quest series. So that players could acquire anti-orc weaponry from their newbie levels all the way up to their 50s.

Along with Giant-bane.

And of course DRAGON-bane.

That's much more in line with the fantasy aspect, quest aspect of everquest. You quest to get the weapon that will put the smack down on the big bad dragon.

They sort of offered this with the vampyre bane weapons.

But Shissar bane being required, Seru bane being required ... it was just a "gimmick" to make completing that part of the game take longer.

Effective to a degree, since it is a pretty well implemented "time sink" ... I mean hell Seru bane is still completely annoying, and completely a waste of time.

But it saddened me that they didn't go in the other direction. The stories involving orcs, dragons and giants can be soooooooooooo interesting. Having bane weapons for their ilk would have been much more popular a series of quests than "Greater Akheva Bane."

I mean, greater akheva bane? Who freakin cares? Zek bane? Not worth the time.

But how cool would it have been to have a quest available to you, that was involved, but FUN, that allowed you to get a Dragonslayer weapon, with Dragon Bane, so that you could go into the Temple of Veeshan (whether for your FIRST time or just to re-live some old memories) and WHAM, hit them up with a weapon designed to kick their ass?

Dev team dropped the ball on that one, imo.

That gnoll bane exists and orc bane doesn't underscores exactly how half-assed the bane weapon idea was implemented.
