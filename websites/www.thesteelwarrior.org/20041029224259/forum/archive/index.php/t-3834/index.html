Solinar
The only time resists over 500 "matter" are when you have a debuff spell on you.

Resists of 800 capped at 500, with a boss mob who casts some AE with a neg 400 adjustment, will hit you every time as you have an effect resist level of 100 (500-400).

Resists of 800 capped at 500 where a mob first casts an AE that is -400 to all resists will leave you at 400 resists (800-400).

Real world examples:

You have 600 resist capped at 500. Some Wizzie mob tries to lure of ro you (-300 resist adjust). You will resist the spell as if you had 200 resist (500-300).

You have 600 resist capped at 500. Some NPC casts malosinia on you and it lands somehow. Your resists are now 600 - 70 = 530, or capped at 500 still.

Spells that debuff (duration) a resist are helped by resists over 500. Spells that have innate resist adjustments are not helped by resists over 500.
