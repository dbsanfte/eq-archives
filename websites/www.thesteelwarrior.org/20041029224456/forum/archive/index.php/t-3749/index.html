At 850 plat ... I'd probably save up a few more hundred and go for the Centi. The Blackened Acrylia Blade is a 14 damage 22 delay sword from the new Inner Acrylia in Acrylia Caverns. It's a great "twink" weapon or lower level weapon because it has no required or recommended level so you get the full stats and damage from it. But it usually sells for quite a bit more than you have right now (last one I saw on my server's bazaar was something like 8500 plat).

Of course getting a good ole wurmslayer is probably a good idea too and you can probably snag one for a lot cheaper than a centi.

Good luck.
