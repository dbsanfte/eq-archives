Warriors can already taunt a mob, but what if we got a skill that made us take the blame for others actions?

I was thinking about something like this,

Skillname: Take the blame (someone might find a better name)
Target: Group, except caster
Duration: 5 ticks
Refresh: 30 ticks @ level 50 -> 25 ticks @ 60
Effect: All agro generated by the groupmembers add 10% to the warrior as well.
Timer: Same as defensive
Buffwindow: Short duration buff window

This skill will allow the warrior to benefit from the other groupmembers agro, but they still have to watch it.

Example 1.

Ranger fires an arrow in a mob and cause 30 agropoints, which gives the warrior 3 agropoints.
The chanter tash on inc and cause 300 agropoints, which raise the warrior to 33.
Warrior still needs 267 agropoints to get agro over the chanter.
Chanter continues with a slow right after tash and generate 300 more agropoints, rising to 600 agropoints on the mobs hatelist, but the warrior also gets 30 more, which brings him to 63 or 557 less than the chanter.

This example shows how a warrior is only helped a little with this skill.

Example 2.

Ranger fires an arrow in a mob and cause 30 agropoints, which gives the warrior 3 agropoints.
The chanter tash on inc and cause 300 agropoints, which raise the warrior to 33.
A shaman slows the mob and cause 300 agropoints, and at the same time increase the warriors agro to 63.
At the same time the cleric cast a heal and cause 50 agropoints to himself and 5 to the warrior.
After the slow the wizards decides to cast a nuke that cause 300 agropoints to himself and 30 more to the warrior.
All in all the warrior now has 98 agropoints and the chanter and shaman both have 300 agropoints.

Here the skill helps the warrior alot.
The skill gives the group the most benefit from keeping everyones agro high, but not higher than the warrior's.
Its a really a fine walk on the line. :)

The duration can probably not be adjusted much in order to balance it, simply because it would be hard to use it then, but the amount of agropoints that are transfered can easily be adjusted to make it balanced.
The use of the same timer as defensive means it will have no use on the big boss mobs where defensive is needed, so the usage will be yard trash on raids and XPing in groups.
