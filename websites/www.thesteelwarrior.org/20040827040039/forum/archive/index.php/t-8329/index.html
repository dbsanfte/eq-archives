Utterly wonkey idea, but bear with me.

A defensive-style ability (ie, one that reduces the damage you take from melee) that eats your endurance based off the amount of damage it soaks.

An example with made up numbers:
33% of incoming damage is negated, for every point of damage you negate you lose 2 endurance.

In the long term, this allows a warrior to use endurance as a sort of pseudo HP-regen, amount other things.

It also allows a warrior to deal with short predictable reasonably sized damage spikes (like, a large pull in a group, or a named mob before it is slowed) better.

It doesn't increase the raid tanking ability of a warrior (your END pool would evaporate immediatly under any raid opponent).

It does allow warriors better soloing abilities, especially at lower and mid levels (where it matters).

Variants on this ability:
1> Higher HP->End conversion ratios.
2> Larger % of damage soaked by endurance.

You could even have different flavours. More efficient, lower %, and less efficient higher % variants, which you'd have to choose between.
