I can't view magelo at school, but you should be able to handle DS instances solo. Even with the number of nerfs to them, they are still great cash. Find out where the named/ph spawn in whichever instance you want to do, then invis yourself and sham to a safe spot near there. You can farm the nameds for runes and walk away with one or two a grind session if you're lucky, which can be 25-60k or so.

As for exp, duo'ing WoS is nice as long as you can snare mobs reliably. NC also works, and if you can get a camp with a few nameds you can make money too.
