The chart is correct, but misleading. When people say "you need Saryrn" it is always implied that you also need the TT and Bertox chains of flags. Fahlia will not talk to you without those, and you cannot sort out the Cipher of the Divine Language without her dialogue.

To zone into Sol Ro you need:

POJ trial + 3 HOH trials + MM + Behemoth + TZ + VZ + Hedge + TT + Grummus + Carprin + Bertox + KoS + Saryrn, and a lot of hails along the way.

To zone into Sol Ro you do not need:

AD, BOT entry quest, Agnarr, or RZ.
