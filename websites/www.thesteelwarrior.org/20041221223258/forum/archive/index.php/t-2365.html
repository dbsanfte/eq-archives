I hear a lot of wars saying "learn to play your class better" and, well, I am a 65 warrior but certainly don't know everything (hell, Kildace posted about using AE Taunt and disc /furious in emergencies to buy camp time and I hadn't thought of that).

So... if ya have some gems of wisdom let's hear them.

The biggest thing I have found is EQ Watcher ( eqwatcher.com ). It runs in the background and with the MS text to voice it TELLS me when to taunt. I don't have to watch a sep text window now while fighting to look for slows, this sexy fem voice says "A Jord Militis has been slowed" (yes, it recognizes slows, yawns, and "loses its fighting edge" - Beastlord slow). In playing around with it I have set voice commands now for "A Jord Militis has been crippled" and have messages for Tash and the druid Ro debuff line. You can set it up with messages like "A jord Profet is attempting to gate", etc.

It is a handy tool.

EQW doesn't effect anything in game fyi. You just toggle /log on and as you fight it reads your log and certain text "cues" set off different sounds. It also tallys various damage totals and reads them to you...which is sub-par imo.

Ex.) "%T slows down" triggers a "slow noise"

It definitely helps me to be more aware of what is going on without missing anything. If you want more info on what's happening and when during a fight, it's worth a try.
