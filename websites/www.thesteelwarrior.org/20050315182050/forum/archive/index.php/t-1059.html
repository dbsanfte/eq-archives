You go right from zone in and clear untill you get to a hut. Gnome is inside that hut, no PH.

Few things,

Gnome location has already been given. Take the raid force ahead clearing the way to dragon, then have someone do the turn in to the gnome and have your favorite chanter babysit the little fella keeping behind the clearing. Note, do NOT let your people get in his path as he has been known to despawn if his path is too obstructed...just keep him behind the fracas.

Once you get to the gate, pull the 3 mobs inside, buff up, get raid force positioned and let the little fella do his thing.

We use an 8.5k tank to MT fight normally, but you really don't need that much. I'd compare dragon to most of NToV mobs. It can be debuffed and slowed so once thats done, simple CH rotation should do just fine. I believe he melees around 1400 and has an AE Oil Spray (snare and 300dd I think?)

We did last night and although not a big force (think we had 30) it took just over 2 minutes to give him his dirt nap (grats me BTW on the CHSD).
