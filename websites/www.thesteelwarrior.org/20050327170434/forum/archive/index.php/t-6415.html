05-12-2004, 03:15 PM
I got into phase 2 beta, and am currently a level 34 dwarven warrior(yeah, I know, real original). I'm absolutely loving it. Aside from all the things like a great quest system, a tradeskill system that produces items you actually use during combat and downtime, and the other various things available to everyone, I'm really enjoying the utility of being a warrior. Thats right you heard me, I used "utility" and "warrior" in the same sentance.

Without getting heavy into the mechanics, we have a Battle Stance, a Defensive Stance, and an undeveloped Berzerker Stance, and probably more to come.

In the Battle Stance you become damage/utility, you have single hit damage increasing abilities, a snare(warriors being one of the few classes that has snare), a dot, a battle starting charge that stuns and gains rage, a casting interupt skill, a riposte skill, an AE(up to 4 targets) dmg/slow, and a longer reuse mightystrike style crit skill.

In Defensive stance you gain an innate addition to your Defense skill, a taunt that works and has visual cues(to my knowledge we are the ONLY class currently that can taunt, or gain the mob's attention through any method other than dps or healing), shield block, shield bash which interupts casting(sword and board is actually useful in WoW, and the prefered choice for defensive tanking), disarm(which actually means something since just like players mobs cannot use certain abilities without a melee weapon equiped), a reverse riposte, a stackable AC debuff, and a longer reuse 75% incoming damage reduction skill.

Berzerker Stance is currently unfinished and only has one skill atm, but it comes with an innate 10% haste boost at the cost of increasing incoming damage by 20%.

Add to this a Shouts system that is independant of your stance and currently includes a group atk buff, an AE atk debuff, an AE taunt, and an AE fear.

So far the utility and tanking ability I bring to a group, often using more than once stance in a single battle, as well as the ability to ply all the above in solo situations effectively have me very impressed with the design of the warrior class in WoW.
