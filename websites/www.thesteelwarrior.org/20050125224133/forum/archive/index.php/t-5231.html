Snarewhip, Anklesmasher (rare) for snares (duh)
Truncheon of Doom (best slow), Willsapper, Trident of the Deep Sea, Wavecrasher, Canyoneer's Pike for slows
Journeyman's Walking Stick for tash

Other debuffers typically just reduce atk or some resist a little...so I personally don't bother carrying them.

As for stun procs, depends on whether or not you're looking for real stun or a stun/dd. There are several selections of each... but you'll probably settle for the standard Copper Hammer of Striking, and Sword of the Bloodsworn with a Greatstaff of Thunder for 2h. All 3 of which are cheaply attained, very decent aggro until you get better, and not too bad dps.
