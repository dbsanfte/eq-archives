Pardon me if a comparison has been done before on this site, but I wasn't able to find one searching for Crimson Darkscale. I recently created a Troll Warrior and Ogre Warrior and have been levelling them both with left over equipment from previous alts since I didn't want to spend the money on an alt that I didn't know I would like or not.

At any rate, they are both nearing 45 now (yes, I'm still undecided between the both, I love my Ogre's Unstannability but hate the fact that when I do a /who all ogre warrior it has so many returns; and I love my Troll's regen and relative [to the Ogre's] scarcity/rarity, but hate being stunned :p) and so I was looking to having a friend do some crafted armor for me.

This is where I found out that for Ogres and Troll warriors of Rallos Zek, there are two choices: Crimson Darkscale Armor (also able to be worn by SK's) and Fierce Heraldic (also able to be worn by Bards) which I'm sure all of you are aquanited with by now. Since I was bored at work, I drew up a simple table in Word with the stats of both sets and what I would gain and lose by going with a full set of each. Please note that I included Wisdom, Intelligence, and Mana for the sake of completeness. The results are as follows:

With a full suit of Crimson Darkscale (two bracers):
+353 AC
+40 STR
+15 STA
+15 DEX
+10 AGI
+645 HP
+10 vsMg
+10 vsFire
+10 vsCold
+10 vsPos
+10 vsDis
+5 INT
+10 WIS
+215 MANA

With a full suit of Fierce Heraldic (two bracers):
+327 AC
+10 STR
+10 STA
+40 DEX
+35 AGI
+830 HP
+10 vsMg
+10 vsFire
+10 vsCold
+10 vsPos
+10 vsDis
+5 INT

So if I go with Crimson Darkscale I gain 26 AC, 30 Str, and 5 Sta over Fierce Heraldic. However, I lose 25 Agi, 25 Dex, and 185 HPs (not including intelligence, wisdom, or mana into my final tally since they are as a whole irrelevant to a warrior). When I looked at the two armors from this angle, it would seem to be that Fierce Heraldic is the clear winner, is it not? Only one piece of Crimson Darkscale had the same HPs as Fierce Heraldic (the vambraces both had 60 HPs) and all Crimson Darkscale pieces have 2 more AC than their Fierce Heraldic counterparts.

So I guess, this long winded post was all the result of my boredom at work and curiosity between the two armors for my newbie warrior; all to come to the conclusion that to me Fierce Heraldic is vastly superior because of the big amounts of raw HPs I gain from going with it instead of Crimson Darkscale. However, I might have missed something somewhere in all of this and I'd really appreciate someone giving me any input on the situation.

Thanks to all and I'm happy you made it to the end of this post without falling asleep.

Well each Stam is worth 6 hp's pre 255 cap. A lot of warrior's get to about 210-220 stam and concentrate on +HP items (I do). Reason is that I group with a shammy or Bst 98% of the time and they have stam buffs. If I was maxed stam, I would gain no bonus from their buffs. So I leave my stam a bit lower and use a lot of +HP gear.

That ensures that my HP is as high as it can go. As for Crimson vs Fierce, go piece by piece. Fierce is gonna win most of the time because you dont need +mana on any of ure armor. Plus Int and Wis are useless to us.
