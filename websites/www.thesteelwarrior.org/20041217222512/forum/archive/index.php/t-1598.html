We have no idea how the dmg tables are made. Its a well kept secret.
However we have found out what is the formula used.

DMG = (Weapon dmg * X) + Damage Bonus

X = DMG Table Factor, and this number varies from 0 to 6.

0-6 beeing determined by the famous dmg tables using :
Melee ATK, Melee Skills, Melee Bonus (monk only),Mob Mitigation

So the avg warrior at 65 will probably use 3 in PoV
The well geared 4, and the raid buffed 5.
(Warrior ATK beeing the deciding factor, like 1400 vs 2100)
