First of all, "12.8% better mitigation" is misleading. You're comparing how much damage a paladin mitigates vs how much damage a warrior mitagates, which gives an inflated number which doesn't reflect reality. A more real comparison, and what's generally accepted, is putting it in terms of damage taken. Why is this? Because say you have a guy who mitigates 20% damage, and one that mitigates for 40% damage. The 40% has 200% or 100% better mitigation than the 20%, but in terms of damage taken, it's not half as much as the guy with half the mitigation, he takes SEVENTY FIVE percent of the damage taken by the one with low mitigation.

That mitigation difference in this case means that a warrior will take 91.8% of the damage of a hybrid , so 100% - 91.8% = 8.2% less damage taken.

Noone here is claiming that it isn't enough or that it should be more, thanks.

And it's 45% for defensive, not 40%.
