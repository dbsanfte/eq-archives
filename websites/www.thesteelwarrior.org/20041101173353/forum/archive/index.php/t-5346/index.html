What level are you? What weapons do you have access to? What weapons are you using now?

Sure a 2 handed weapon is less ripostes and generally more DPS, so in general the answer to your question is yes, it is worth it if you can hold aggro. If you're putting anger 3 on a finesteel greatstaff, then no, it's not worth it.

If you're over 56 and using bazaar bought weapons, do a search on any of the 100 threads on Greatstaff of Thunder. If you're swinging around a Darkblade of the Warlord and Edge of Eternity, then you're going to take an aggro and DPS hit with basically any weapon you get and it's probably not worth the lessened riposte damage.
