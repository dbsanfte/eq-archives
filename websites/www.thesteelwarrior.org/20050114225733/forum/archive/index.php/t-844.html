Most guilds don't require you to have 100 in all resists at the same time, usually they just want you to get to 100 in magic while you are at 100 in each of the other resists, one at a time. The point is, you can go for stuff that just has high DR, and you don't need to have high FR and CR at the same time. It means you have to have a greater number of resist items, but I think it's the more cost effective way to go.

On to the recommendations.

Personally, I won't wear any BD crap. You give up too many other stats when you wear BD stuff. You're a warrior, chances are you're going to get hit, so taking less damage from AoE but getting slapped around by melee damage doesn't seem like a particularly wonderful trade-off to me. I try to find resist gear that won't cost me too much in terms of AC or HP.

I agree that the UP bracers are a good choice.

The runed bone fork is good, and it's very cheap. 10 to SVF, SVM, SVC, from memory. A good alternative in the range slot if you're OK on SVF and SVC is the Nightmarewood compound bow, which has 15 SVM on it.

As someone mentioned, the crown of the froglok kings has 20 SVF, 20 SVC on it. Circlet of Vallon is an alternative, it has better AC (I have a tough time losing all the AC the froggy crown would cost me) and has 7 SVF, SVD, SVC on it.

Helm of Raldukan, which can also be regular gear, has 7 SVC, SVD on it.

One of the better resist items is imbued granite spaulders. 20 AC and 10 to all saves. Regular gear for me.

Tracking wolf collar is a decent neck piece and has 10 SVC on it.

The stone visor, if you can get it to drop in ME, is a really really nice face piece, and has 7 SVD, SVC, SVP on it. This has the benefit of being regular gear for me, not just resist gear (20 AC, 10 STR, 10 AGI). You can switch out to a peerless dragonhide mask (13 AC, 7 SVF) if you really need that SVF sometime. Or you can switch out to the coral adorned mask, 15 SVM, 15 SVF, from Nadox.

There is stuff from Fungus Grove that boosts disease resistance a lot. One example is the mushroom veil, it has 15 SVD on it. I would never wear that one because of all the other stats I'd lose, but if you have to demonstrate 100 in SVD in the backpack it's an option.

Electrified earring of tempests is also regular gear for me, 12 SVM, SVF, SVC.

Necklace of curled cacti is not expensive (a little more than 1K), has 12 SVF, SVM, SVC on it.

The resist I have the most trouble with is poison. I seem to have an easy time finding FR gear, the PR stuff doesn't jump out at me.

Since you're recruiting, I don't think you will need to worry about being main tank and having resists together for a while.

My recommendation is to get the requirements first, then start from there to work toward a set of gear you really like.

I'm gonna assume that it's single resist+magic. Fire/cold and poison/disease are paired for resists a lot.

Looks like IGS, velium bd bracelets, froglok crown/tarmok headband, holy cake, kaladim constitutional should get you there using the reaver.

After that, you can do stuff like swap out for full mithril or luminant heraldic bracers, tweak out different setups.
