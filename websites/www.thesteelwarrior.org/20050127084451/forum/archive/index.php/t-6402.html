It is generally agreed that Anger II < Yak < Anger III. The aggro difference between the two Yaks is so trivial that it can basically be ignored. So the 100dd/stun Yak augment (510 points) is a better deal than an Anger II aug, but it's lore, so you can only buy one.

Anger II and Anger III are not lore, so you can have as many of them as you want. But for your first hate aug, if you don't want to spend 1150 points on Anger III, the cheaper Yak augment is a great buy. I'm probably going to put one in my CHoS, expecting to offhand it when I someday get a GBoS or CHSD.
