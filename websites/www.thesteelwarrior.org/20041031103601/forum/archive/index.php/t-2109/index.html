1. Using two 2Hdrs can get you reposted to death.

2. BoC doesn't proc often enough (IMO) that you can use a Furious Bash shield.

4. That tiny knockback can actually be a big plus for positioning mobs.

If resisted stuns produce good aggro I'll probably at least try a weapon with a stun in the offhand.
