I've duo'd in HoH with a shammy. As long as you don't start the trials, you should be able to kill pretty much everything in HoHa. Your problem will be pulling. Without lull or fd, you will most likely have 2 every pull. Most mobs are rootable, so if you get 2 it shouldn't be too bad. Named humans (white con) are not rootable or snarable. Named humans that con red are to start trials and shouldn't be messed with. All mobs seem to share the same drop table (rarely a good item, "soldiers" items are just cash loot).

I would say best place to go is down in the trial starting areas. There are 3 of them, all are basically basements. There you should only get 2 mobs per pull. Be careful though, when you first enter the buildings, the white armor guys randomly can see invis.
