I've been trying to find a solution to help agro generation.
Some solution that will not be overpowered, and will not
give us paladin/sk lock down, just a lot of initial agro.
Because thats what most warriors need. 5-15 seconds of soloing the mob.

I think i have found it : The Initial Agro Multiplicator

Its based on 2 simple ideas :
- When a mob is alone, its agro/hate list is EMPTY
- When you take a hostile action towards that mob you ENTER that list

Once that is said, the solution becomes obvious.

Multiply by a specific (or semi-random) number the Agro value
of the hostile action that puts the warrior in the hate list.

That number should be between 10 and 20.

What will that do ?
Very simply it will give us great snap agro, or i should really say initial fight agro.
All raiding warriors will agree that a 5-10 second head start
assures very good agro lockdown.
The Initial Agro Multiplicator gives an Virtual / Innate 5-10 second
head start on every single mob that agroes a warrior.

Simulation :
- Monk pulls a mob (so only the monk is on the agro list)
- Warrior either bow tags or melees the mob
(thus entering for the the agro/hate list)
- the bow tag or 1st melee hit, does 30 dmg for X hate points
- Initial Agro Multiplicator kicks in
- the warrior is awarded 10-20 * X agro points as bonus
- the warrior now has insane initial agro rivaling a PAL/SK
- the warrior 2st hit does 60dmg to the mob for 2X hate points
(thus his 2nd entry in the agro/hate list)
- Initial Agro Multiplicator will NOT kick in ANYMORE,
because the warrior is already on the agro/hate list.

What will this do ? In essence the mob will hate the warrior as
if he had hit it for 300 to 600 dmg (depending on the random generator)

Over the course of the fight, keeping the agro will remain just as hard.
Exept we now get a 5-15 second "virtual head start"
On every single mob we agro.


PROS :
- Will give warriors what they really need : great initial agro
- Will not give warriors the snap agro of a pal/sk (still hard to regain)
- Will not break the game trivialising agro control
- Will not affect raiding agro, because in raids we are already
given those 5-10 seconds head start.
- Reducing a boss fight by 5-10 seconds at most
(since head start wont be needed) will not affect the triviality of the fight.
- Very very very very Easy to code
IF the person entering the hate list is a warrior THEN multiply the
agro factor of that action by 10-20 ELSE multiply the agro factor by 1


CONS :
- If Proximity agro (beeing seen by the mob) is coded the same
way as Action agro (taking a hostile action) this will make
warriors mob magnet (like wearing a "HIT ME" sign on the back)

So it has to be coded to work for "hostile actions" and not "proximity visual agro"


The Initial Agro Multiplicator seems to me the best and simplest solution that has been proposed so far.

It will give Warriors Virtual / Innate INITIAL AGRO
bonus that will rival or even surpass pal/sk without competing
on the long run Or trivialise agro control in raids.

Any Comments ?

PS : If this has already been proposed then i'd like to give credits
to the original author and apologise, but i dont remember ever
seeing a proposition like this one.
