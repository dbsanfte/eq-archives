DISCLAIMER: I fully acknowledge that some of my numbers may be off a little bit (how often one flurries, the hate generated by different weapons, etc). I'm just posting this as a "food for thought" topic, and encourage people to correct/disagree/flame/sendCookies.

So, with all that said, some quick number crunching:

Hate per swing for MSC = 73+(44*1.4) = 134.6 per swing, (delay 33)

Hate per swing for BBB = 42+(15*1.4) = 63 per swing, (delay 31)

(using Dmg+(DmgBonus*Mod1/Mod2) formula)

Imagine that you have augged both with the same exact aug (and remember both have the same proc/procrate).

This means that the BBB alone is producing less than half the hate as the MSC. Imagine you were even able to dual wield the BBB's -- melee'wise, you're still doing less hate per second.

Now, of course, your offhand will produce more procs. If we had an offhand Anger proc, then of course dual wielding anger weapons would own, but we don't...so let's just assume that the pure hate per stunproc is the same as an anger3 proc (you don't have to agree with me here, the endresult is still sound)....

So, you have an extra 2.5 procs per minute at 600 hate per proc (1.5 from WA5 and builtin proc, 1.0 from aug)...that's 1500hate per minute extra that you're making.

Now consider how often you flurry a minute (I haven't done parsing, so I'm just going to guess here and say 3x per minute when you have the new OOW AA). 3 Flurries per minute = 6 melee rounds a minute = 71.6 hate per swing * 6 swings = 429 hate...so we're looking at roughly 1k more hate in procs.

Consider that your offhand is only swinging about 50-60% of the time. That's less hate per swing.

Overview:
- MSC does 135 hate per swing
- BBB does 63 hate per swing, but if you were dual wielding weapons of similar dmg/dly you're looking at 100 hate per swing.
- Including flurries, MSC generates about 500 hate per minute more.
- Off hand proccing generates about 1500 hate per minute.

Conclusion, in order for MSC to produce the same amount of hate as dual wielding hate weapons of BBB calibre, one would need to make up that 1000 hate with a 35hate bonus per swing. That amounts to 28 swings in a minute (not including flurry). With a 30dly weapon you have 18 rounds (unhasted). You double/triple attack enough to make up that hate. Take into consideration haste/EoA and you're looking at VERY similar numbers, if not numbers that will indeed perform better than dual wielding.

In other words -- unless my numbers are severely off (which is possible), MSC *will* produce JUST AS MUCH HATE over a course of a minute as Tacvi-grade hate weapons.

Discuss.

The arguements are similar Eriatha, but like Zotha pointed out, there are some differences that are no longer factors. The BoW had 2 main advantages 1) minimized riposte damage and 2) quicker agro from the insane agro DPS difference. Those advantages were basically the arguements for using 2h vs. dual weild for a long time. Since then, with the retuning of riposte on most mobs, the advent of better than 1:1 one-handers, proc augments, CE gear, and WA5 has largely made those 2h advantages irrelevant. With 4/5 procs and the vastly increased ratio of end game primary weapons, initial agro is basically trivial. Nothing is better for grabbing quick agro than early procs, and with current proc rates for end game tanks, the chances for an early or several early procs is much more likely than not.

I have no doubt that the MSC is a great weapon, but I can't see many tanks giving up end game one-handers for this on a full time tanking basis. No question, this is by far the best non-tanking weapon yet in that the DPS is insane and the agro is workable, but when tanking, the AC/HP and proc agro advantage that top end dual weilding affords just can't be beat. Of course, if you don't have a DB/BBoB/1.5 then this is certainly a good replacement particularly if getting one of those weapons and a good mate for it is not on the horizon.
