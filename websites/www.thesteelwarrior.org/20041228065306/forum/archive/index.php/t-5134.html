Combat Expertice:
3 ranks, war/monk/rog/ran only.
rank 1: 5 AA
rank 2: 10 AA
rank 3: 15 AA
Increases the innate damage bonus on all weapons wielded (including bows) by 1, 2, and 5 points respectively.

What does this accomplish you ask? Well, it's simple, the new aa:
- Adds a very powerful but costly melee offensive AA (I mean, PoP was great, but considering how powerful the skills were, PB and Ferocity were WAYYY too cheap cost-wize)
- Addresses and balances the duel-wield issue. Because the AA would affect all weapons wielded, off-hand weapons would receive an additional damage bonus. The aa would boost the DB on primary 1hs to 18 (at rank 3), and in essence, would increase the the damage bonus by 10 while duel-wielding vs. only 5 from using 2 handers.
- Gives melee classes the only offensive aa's they receive in this expansion (with exception to monks).

Combat Expertice; add it.
