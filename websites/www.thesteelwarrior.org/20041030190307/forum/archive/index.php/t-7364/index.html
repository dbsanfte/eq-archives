Zanakus
Ouch, you definitely have some work to do on your ac. Remember that for each level you should aim to get 20 more ac - at 59, you should have roughly 20 x 59, equaling 1180 ac. And those guidelines are for a non-raiding warrior.

Sell the crafted pauldrons, crusty greaves and boots, mithril arms, dragon bone bracelet and black sapphire electrum earring.

Here's what I'd replace those with if I were in your situation and needed to get decent gear fast:

Earring: Vah Shir Claw or Lupine Frenzy Earring are good but can be expensive; Geomancer's Ritual Hoop (80-ish AP at Takish camp) is actually pretty decent as well and should hold you over for a little while.

Neckpiece: Amulet of Mummy Wrappings goes for several hundred plat on my server and has 12 ac, 45 hp, 3 stamina and some decent resists. I'd buy it.

Shoulders: Wretched Pauldrons aren't too bad, but they fluctuate in price and aren't sold too often, at least on Rallos. Get them if you can find them cheap.

Greaves: Blood Runed Greaves are excellent but expensive, same with Greaves of the Orcish Master. Buy them if you can afford them, otherwise save up AP for Malevolent legs.

Boots: Kingly Boots of Splendor are excellent as are Greatbear Hide Boots, you may want to consider them if you have the money. If you don't, save up AP for Malevolent boots.

Bracer: Rock-Molded Wristguard is incredible - if you have enough money to get it, do so.

Arms: Blood-Spattered Vambraces are amazing for the price, try to get them.
