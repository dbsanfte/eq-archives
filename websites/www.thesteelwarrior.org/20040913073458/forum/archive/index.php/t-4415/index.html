I don't know what Absor or Sony would say but this is my thinking on it:

It looks like all it does is look at the zone description files stored on your computer and renders a zone based on those files. It isn't actually logging into EQ and it doesn't look like it is modifying those files in any way so I wouldn't think Sony would really care about this any more than someone looking at a series of screenshots or movie of your character walking through these zones. The mobs are not in the zones of course and all this is really good for is seeing the layout of zones you are curious about but have never seen before. Since it doesn't tell you anything about mob locations, it seems very unlikely this could be considered a cheat program since it doesn't seem to give any unfair advantage to a player that uses it. If they consider this a cheat program, than any spoiler site out there like Lucy that lists spell and item stats should be considered one also.
