You can't equip a GSoT until level 55, so it would be a total waste of money at this point given how many other slots you have which are seriously lacking. Most of your armor is absolutely terrible for your level. You should at least be in full crafted minimum. I don't have any idea what your server economy is like, but when I was level 30 I had mostly crafted except for a couple of slots(crafted helm sucks, so I had skull shaped barbute for example), and I was totally untwinked too. There is a long list of stuff that should be available to you. Get some friends together and kill Martar Icebear in EF to get a cloak of the icebear. He's level 30ish, bring friends, he's tougher than he looks. Crafted or Crusty boots, Crafted or Crusty greaves, Crafted, Crusty, Hero, hell even Silver Plated bracer would be an upgrade. Anything from blackened alloy to think leather apron would be an improvement for waist slot. Orc fang or cougar claw earrings, or something with more hps. Make these changes and your ac should increase by about a couple hundred points roughly. As a rule of thumb your important stats are AC/sta/hps. Which order you put those in depends on what level you are and what level of the game you're playing at. Right now, your ac is way too low, but all of the stuff I've listed should be attainable by a group your level even if your server economy doesnt allow you to buy it.
