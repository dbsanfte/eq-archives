Its a great DPS weapon, not a great aggro weapon, hence favoured by pallies and SK's more than us warriors.

But with ldon you can add a proc to it i.e. hate/stun, which now makes it far more attractive again, or so some might think, though this is a very short term view.

Regarding prices, every server has their own economy, on Mith Marr, decent weapons and gear are abundant and therefore dirt cheap, GSoTS have gone for under 1k for about a month now, as they are a common drop in BoT, the cheapness and availability of them has driven the prices on windblades and other nice 2handers down very hard, also the uncertainty regarding the impact on the economy because of LdoN has ensured people who would traditionally have to upgrade gear by buying it have a new option, which means not parting with hard earned plat for bottom feeding guilds who sell it on Ebay.

Don’t waste plat unless you have it too waste, get racking those points up and buy your own decent 2hander – with MORE augment slots, then upgrade it.
