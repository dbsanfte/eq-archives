Keane Abrams
I spent one point on it because I had just done 4 levels of straight exp and wanted to get something out of my aa's right away.

With ingenuity1 the boon proc (35dd lifetap) becomes a 46 dd lifetap.

So that is a 30 percent increase in damage on the crit. That would put the Fleshgrinder at a 208 crit with Ingenuity 1 assuming the dmg increase of 30 percent is what it is really at.

And FYI, it does not work with the epics.
Apocaly
I have Ingenuity 3 and it doubles the damage of all DD procs that crit blast. To put this in perspective, you have a 7% chance to crit blast from procs which occur maybe 2-5% of the time. This sounds small but it actually pumps out some nice added damage. I also use rampage with my earthshaker for a 246 AE DD and get some multiple crit blasts of 492 to thrill and amaze my guildmates.
This ability progresses thru the 3 levels like so:

level 1 adds 33% to DD damage with crit blasts (about 2% of procs)

Level 2 adds 66% DD damage with crit blasts (about 4% of procs)

Level 3 adds 100% DD damage with crit blasts (about 7% of procs)

All this for a investment of 6 points.
