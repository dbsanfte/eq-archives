I used to duo in the pits in Velks with my 60 warrior and 54 cleric. Made most of the 200+ AA that I had before PoP came out that way. Pits are nice because they are out of the way, you don't have to worry about someone stealing your pulls or training you. They were also rarely ever camped when I used to go there.


Some things to consider though...
- You can solo pull around any of the lesser kobolds. Champions, Prophets, and named, however, will agro.
- Prophets can complete heal, and gate, kick stun is nice, but not terribly reliable, so the cleric stun was nice.
- Champions and named can crank out some damage. I am not sure if your shaman will be able to keep up with the healing, even after they are slowed.
- There is no legit way to get out of the pits without fighting all the way through them to come out by the castle or use a gate potion.

You won't be able to clear more than a dozen to fifteen mobs or so before respawn, and once you get it broken up, it is pretty easy. There are some areas where 3-5 mobs spawn in close proximity, and if you get 3 Prophets in those areas, you need to avoid them.
