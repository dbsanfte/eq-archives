fleshgrinder about sucks a nut for aggro, its pure DPS, thats it ...

even though i hate the weapon with the bowels of my soul, i cant ignore it .... Buy a Greatstaff of Thunder, its the best aggro weapon you can get for your price range.

or, if you like Dual weilding, Pick up a Frostbringer, and look into one of those new weapons comming out of Droga or Chardok B, they will be selling cheap, but get em while you can, when the zone drops, they wont be cheap anymore.

RBB is more than adaquate for a non-Ssra+ warrior, i would keep that right where it is, on your waist.

Looking at a Helm of Rallos, with only 50k to toss around is kind of pointless, as you might be able to buy a few rivets off of it. Your helm of Radulkan is fine for now.

Your Breastplate however needs some work. Look into MQing a Thurgadin chestplate, or take a few friends and sit in Kael Arena for a while, till you get one to drop for you. Or look into getting into a Plane of Growth raid, and pick up a Berzerkers. There are a few nice BPs dropping in Umbral Plains that are single groupable also (Moktor BP, 35ac 15agi/stam/str 15hp ... and Radulkan Breastplate, which is 46ac 100hp, and a few other stats i cant remember).

and most importantly is levels, that should be your primary concern right now ... when you hit 60/61 then you should start worrying about gear a little more.
