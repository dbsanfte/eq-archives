The FB shields have very significant AC on them. If aggro is tolerable with a FB shield then you should expect to give up a lot of DPS because you're getting the AC on the shield.

There are basically three generic types of weapons you can expect to use based on what we've seen so far:

Dual wield - good HPs, best aggro (in theory), high riposte, low AC (typically)
Weapon + shield - best HPs, lowest aggro, medium ripostes, highest AC.
2H - lowest HPs, medium aggro, lowest ripostes, medium AC.

Since weapon + shield has highest AC & HPs just by the way shields are designed, and riposte rate with 1 weapon is tolerable, this means wepaon + shield always has the best tanking stats. If weapon + shield aggro is even the same as 2H or dual wield there would never be any reason to use that type of weapons for tanking purposes.

So therefore weapon + shield by definition can't do a lot of aggro, otherwise that's the only kind of weapon you'd use.

A worn 1-8% aggro increase on a FB shield would do even less than whatever it's supposed to do now. You're basically saying that you can hold aggro with a 1 hand weapon if you've a Voice of Thule (12% aggro increase).
