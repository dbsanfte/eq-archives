WA5 gives ~50% more procs with innate weapon procs, and proc augs as well. I think people often confuse this with a weapon's own proc modifier, which will only cause the weapon proc to be increased/decreased, and has no effect on the aug.

With capped dex (level 65, no new +stat aa) your primary weapon's own proc will go off 3 times per minute on average. An aug in that weapon will go off like 2.9-2.95 times per minute, since it can't proc on the same swing as the innate proc.
