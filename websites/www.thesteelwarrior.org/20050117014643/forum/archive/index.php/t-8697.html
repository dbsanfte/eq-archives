Heres the test I did way back when

Two test mobs, each consisted of me pulling haven guards in netherbia with a succesful taunt, SK then casted terror of thule (+600 direct hate) 2x to gain agro from me (so initial agro bonus is between 601-1199?) then I would cast runes on my enchanter until I got agro and he would taunt with the taunt key at that point making us within 1 point of each other on the hate list and negating any initial agro bonus. At this point we had the following results both times

2x thule (1201 hate) took 1 rune of zeb (850pt rune) to snap the guard to enchanter, SK then succesfully taunted

2x thule (1201 hate) took 1 rune V (700pt rune) to snap the guard to enchanter, Sk then succesfully taunted

1x thule (601 hate) took 2 rune 3 (230pt rune) to snap the guard to enchanter

While this doesnt prove what the multiplier is, it does suggest there is definitely a multiplier and its higher than 1.7 and less than 2.6. Wish we had more time for the tests, hopefully we can do it again and find out the exact multiplier.
