As some of you know, lately I've been contacted by two different companies interested in purchasing or forming a partnership with The Steel Warrior. Both routes involve an advertisement for the company (Mysupersales or IGE).

I don't think the advertisements are that intrusive. No popups, no flashing "shoot the monkey", etc.

The other aspects of the site wouldn't be changed at all. The moderators remain the same, and the boards would more or less remain untouched. However, there would be one banner advertisement at the top of each "static" page on the site. The advertisement can be found on eqdruids or eqbeastlord.

The amount of money currently being offered by each company isn't insignificant. I'll be honest - it's really not bad at all. However, I don't want to kill this community for cash. I want to see your thoughts on the issue. The Druid and Beastlord communities exploded when their site was sold - mostly because their administrators handled it a bit differently. In all honesty, you could avoid the advertisements by bookmarking just the forums and reading them instead of the content pages (which aren't finished yet as it is). You'd notice no changes if all you read is the forums.

The benefits for us would be the stability of their server, and never having the need to get donations from the community again. Along with that would be increased traffic, and we would maintain complete control of everything, minus the small advertisement on the static pages. Your emails will not be spammed, nor will this forum be censored to either companies interests.

I'm not making a decision right now, I want to know what you think about all of this.
