We had two rotations on Erollisi Marr - Venril Sathir and Trakanon - up until just a few months ago. The EMarr community was split about it, both with very good points:

1) Not all guilds can mobilize easily, so this gives access to those who play in a low-pressure guild, to do epic / decent drop mobs, without too much hassle.

2) No other epic mob or dragon was on a rotation, so why bother in the first place, for these two?

It is my understanding that the rotation dated back to the heyday of Kunark, when it was set up to prevent direct conflict b/w the power guilds on our server. Why it stopped there, and why those rotations lasted so long, is a tribute to both of the points made, above.

Personally, I like the arrangement of the mobs being open, now. I don't need a rotation for "a large rat," I don't need a rotation for any raid-level mob, either. If we can take it, we will have the force ready, or we don't get to take it ><
