The first 3 minutes of the fight, I'm fairly certain that a BoW or SEWS will hold aggro over a BF/BoC.

Riposte discipline generates a LOT of aggro on an unslowed mob.

DPS on a 2h weapon generates a LOT of aggro. I'm using a SEWS, and I don't care if a taunt failed before I break mes, because an incite + three melee swings is enough to get aggro from messed mob (if its been slowed by the same person who messed it, I usually wait a tick for incite to build up 70 more aggro before breaking and almost always get aggro back after the second round).

Yes, over the course of a 40minute fight, the tank using a BF/BoC will have generated more aggro than the tank using the BoW or the SEWS --- but since most mobs die before stonewall drops (pre-time), you'll still probably generate more aggro in that time using furious with a good 2h than you would dual wielding.
