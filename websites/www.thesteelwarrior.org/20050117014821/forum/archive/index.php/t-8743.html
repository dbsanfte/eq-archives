I would suggest starting in Plane of Tactics and farming the armorer/supplier for ornate. Once you get a couple of pieces (sell the stuff you can't use to buy what you can) head to BOT and whack Einride the tower boss. GBoS and Plate BP come from him, both very good upgrades for you. After that, kill Vahkiz the Shade in PON for shoulders. Getting others to help is easy since he drops a sought after quest piece.

After that I would try the named that drops legs in Tactics. When you have Ornate BP, legs, helm, gloves and boots I would head out to GoD and have a go at the trials. There are a couple of nice upgrades there that aren't too bad to get now. I wear a ring and arms from one and there is a great haste belt and face piece from there as well.

Just my suggestions.
