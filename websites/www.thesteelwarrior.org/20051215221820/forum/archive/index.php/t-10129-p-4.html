On a side note... does anyone know of a way, using macros, to switch from a 2H weapon to 1H + shield? I had read on a WoW board somewhere, where some guy had mentioned he could do this without the screwing around of equipping and un-equipping while fighting.

exemple : we keep the first two slot of the 4th bag free for switching weapons

going from 2Her to 1Her + Shield
/script PickupInventoryItem(16);
/script PickupContainerItem(4,1);
/script UseContainerItem(4,2);

going from 1Her + Shield to 2Her
/script PickupInventoryItem(17);
/script PickupContainerItem(4,2);
/script UseContainerItem(4,1);

note : cosmos adds newmacro such as /stance [#] (#=1/2/3 for Battle/Deff/Berzerk) that can be added at the end of the weapon switch.
