Some ideas:

1- Replace the Willsapper with a Copper Hammer of Striking. Not much of an upgrade, but eh, it's an option. If you want to keep the sapper, it works just fine.

2- Replace Belt of Contention with Soulscream Belt. More haste.

3- Bag the Fennin Ro Bracer, keep it around cause it's always useful to have around. But get a HP bracer in its stead. There are quite a few options out there, just shop around.

4- Same deal with the gobby skull earring. Bag it. Keep it around, cause it's useful. But replace it with a Mosquito Leg Earring or something like that. Maybe consider Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement if you've got the plat. Good hit point upgrade.

5- Consider replacing the velious quested armors with ornate.

None of that is really pressing though. I mean you can be pretty effective with the gear you currently have. Make sure you get your SKILL TOMES, and redo your disciplines, AND get your INCITE skill. That'll be the biggest boost you can get. Moreso than any gear. After that I'd focus on replacing the bracer and the earring before anything else, since they currently have zero hit points in their slot.

"where best should I go for getting exp these days? "

The short answer: Plane of Valor till you can zone into Bastion of Thunder. After that BoT.

OR ... LDoN. LDoN can be a lot more fun than PoP. But PoP XP is still pretty brisk.

Regarding levels vs. AA ... that's all on you there. It changes with your situation. If you need levels to catch up to your old guild for instance, then get the levels. If not, then get some AAs, as they'll help you tank better. It's really dependent on where you are at in the game, so there is no right or wrong route to direct you too.

I wouldn't worry about your weapons for now, they will get you by just fine. Top priority whould be getting a better haste item and a bloodmetal earring or two and then start working on your armor. Especially boots and bracers.

You could go for ornate, and leveling in PoP is probably the fastest experience, but considering your gear, I'd live in LDoN if I were you. The normal mobs are a lot more forgiving than stuff in PoP if your gear is subpar, and you can save up for royal attendant armor and get exp at the same time. By the time you save points for a set of Tak armor, you will be 65 with a decent amount of AA and up to speed to raid with your guild. Be sure to go on any PoP flagging raids your guild does even if you're not ready to raid full time, it is much harder to get caught up if you miss the first round of flagging (assuming your guild isn't in the elementals/time already).

I'd go to 65 and then do AA if I were you, but keep in mind that your gear will hinder you if you want to do places like BoT or Tactics.
