the warrior is more reliant on gear imo than many of the other classes, specificaly priests and magi...

though you will have it easy early on, it will start to get tedious when you have to wait for quests when you see a mage or shadowpreist doing the same quest 3 levels before you can even attempt it...

In the end though, if you are going to be dedicated to your char, you will be able to get him leveled relatively quickly and will be able to solo effiecently with the use of first aid / potions... If your server is goign to be a new one, i would strongly suggest going skinning / mining and putting everything you can in the AH so you can buy upgrades for yourself...

Upgrading... this is somethign you will need to do every 5 levels or so.... there are some exceptions, but when it comes to the warrior class, you will be very gear dependant... go for the best dps weapon for your level.... also keep all weapon skills near tip top... its a pain to raise 1hs from 35 when you are level 55 >_<

Stamina is your friend.. but str and agi are important too... when buying upgrades... please for the love of god, mix in some str / agi gear too.. str and agi will help boost your dps... (agi will boost your dps through an increase in crits, it also helps give a b it of AC too...)
