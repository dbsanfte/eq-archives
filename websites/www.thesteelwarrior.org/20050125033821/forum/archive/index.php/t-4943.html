Well geared and experienced warriors become very important at the high end; they are also very rare. A guild should honestly work at keeping any skilled warriors happy and making them tank.

I got thrown off the deep end when I joined my current guild; Initially I was one of 9 warriors. The experienced warriors made absolutely sure I knew how to MT or RT on most encounters, knowing 1 day it would be needed. Quite a few of them had to quit EQ due to RL issues so now I am one of 3-4 active raid warriors, which is not really enough. (there are plenty of fights were you need a RT and 2 or more MTs)

Also we have found the leaving warriors very very hard to replace. A cleric can Cheal in cloth armor if need be, but a warrior needs gear AND experience. An experienced Time geared warrior is worth his weight in gold, more so than any other class I feel.
