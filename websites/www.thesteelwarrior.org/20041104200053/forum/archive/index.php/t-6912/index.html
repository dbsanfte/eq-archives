refering JUST to riptose damage, yes, teh SLOWER weapon will pull less riptoses, they can only riptose a hit, and if you are hitting them slower, odds are there are less chances for a riptose in a given fight (90 hits in a fight compared to only 60 with a slower weapon).

another thing to consider is that slower 2hander have a higher delay, with makes or brakes a 2hander... i have a greater poleaxe of sorrow, freak'n awesome looking weapon, but it doesn't preform NEAR at the level you would think it would due to its fast delay killing off its damage bonus.
