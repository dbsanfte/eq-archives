Interview with Kendrick - by Safehouse Enquirer  

 

 

Interview with Kendrick "Grandfather Rogue"
by The Safehouse Enquirer
We recently had the opportunity to ask some questions from one of Verant's recent acquisitions, John Capozzi (aka Kendrick).

[Safehouse Enquirer] You've recently taken a position at Verant. What exactly do you do there?

[Kendrick] My title is Associate Cabana Boy. I am tasked with mixing frozen tropical confections, changing towels, keeping the strange floating objects out of the pool, and being extra nice to the female octogenarian guests. Oh, wait. That was my old job. My NEW job is Associate Game Designer, EQ Live. What that means is that instead of creating drinks and playing a lot of shuffleboard, I create content and play a lot of shuffleboard.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Now that the world of EQ has been stabilized for awhile, did the game turn out the way you thought it would be?

[Kendrick] I had a pretty clear picture how the game would be early in beta. I didn't expect that it would be quite this popular, we're getting close to half a million units sold now, which is pretty damned impressive. Just like anyone else who plays the game, I can find things to fault about EQ, but if you step back and look at the game as a whole, it's a masterpiece. And we are working hard to correct a lot of those perceived faults. EQ isn't done yet and that is one of the great things about it. As long as we can keep power to the servers, we can improve the game, and fully intend to.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Can you tell us anything about the upcoming Rogue uber-weapon (ie: the Rogue Fiery Avenger)?

[Kendrick] Well, yes, I could tell you, but then I'd have to kill you. I can say that all the class quests are in the final stages of testing at this time. That means they will be in..soon.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Do you agree that many of the planes items are quite underpowered with the addition of so many easier to obtain Kunark items? Any chance that any of these items might get an upgrade?

[Kendrick] That's a given. We've got Velious to get out the door first, then we fully intend to go back in and retune the Planes. That includes the difficulty of the ultimate encounters in there, as well as revamping the equipment.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Are there any plans to make 'Detect Traps' more applicable? For example, identifying floor traps?

[Kendrick] We've got an idea we're working on right now that will make Sense and Disarm Traps work just like a player would expect. And traps to use them on. Game wide. How's that for a teaser? ;)

[Safehouse Enquirer] Are you saying they are going to actually be ADDING traps to the current world? Will they be changing the few current ones to actually do damage?

[Kendrick] We've rethought the concept of traps, or rather, someone here had an epiphany that should make traps do what they traditionally do in RPG's. It's still in the concept stage at this point, but to partially answer you question, Sense Traps will sense traps and disarm traps will disarm traps, and traps will be a lot more..dynamic.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Would there ever be a chance to change the 'consideration' command to accurately report if a mob can see you or not? For example, why would a mob be 'scowling' at me if he can't even see me?

[Kendrick] It is a quirk with the way factions are set up. Factions work on yours and the target's race, in addition to any faction assigned to the mob. Any mob tagged "no faction" will tend to scowl even if they can't see you. We can't go through and change all "no faction" to some faction, for a number of reasons. Besides, to me, it's one of the cooler parts of mastering the class, learning what can and can't see you. There should be no absolutes.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Will 'apply poison' ever be changed to a 'time based' skill instead of a 'failure' skill?

[Kendrick] Apply will most likely remain the way it is from now on.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Any chance for having more than just the standard stat modifiers for weapons or armor? I would enjoy seeing a weapon with a 'Attack + 15' or a shield with 'Defense + 10'. Other suggestions could include 'Movement + 15' or 'Piercing + 5'.

[Kendrick] That is an idea I like too, and brought up a few times. The system doesn't really support direct skill boosts via equipment, and would be very difficult to implement.

[Safehouse Enquirer] Is everquest ever thinking about a 'Everquest Newsletter' or something that will describe ongoing GM quests? I still don't understand the entire storylines on the 'Lifting of the Veil' for Kunark, nor the connection between the Plane of Fear, Lanys T'Vil and Kithicor Forest. And with Velious coming up, I would like to understand what is happening.

[Kendrick] I'm with you on that 100 percent. One of those faults I mentioned above, to my mind, was the limited depth to the world, and no real place to go for a good solid dose of backstory. Keep an eye on the Everquest.com webpage, there should be more content in the near future that will address a lot of the above. And we're working hard on making the world more vivid while ingame, with expanded dialogues, more "local color", and an ever increasing staff of Quest Troupe members to further animate the game. I feel strongly that if you "live" in Norrath, there should be a wealth of information available for you to absorb, just to ground you in the world.

[Safehouse Enquirer] What are your thoughts on "twinking"? For example, a level 5 Rogue decked out in full ravenscale wielding a Trak tooth. Can anything be done about it? Or should anything be done at all?

[Kendrick] I don't like twinking. But twinking is not going to go away. That's about all I can say.

Thanks Kendrick!
