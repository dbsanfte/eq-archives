EverQuest Study

Thank you for participating in my study of EverQuest. This study began on 10/06/00, and was part of my senior thesis research in psychology. I have since graduated from Haverford College, but am still very much interested in continuing my research in MMORPG's.

For more detailed information on this study, please refer to the FAQ below.

I would also really appreciate it if you could spread the word of this ongoing study by telling EverQuest gamers of this webpage address. I would like to include as many EQ players in the database as I can.

Please direct all comments and questions to Nicholas Yee.

Old surveys and questionnaires that are still open for responses are listed separately for the convenience of returning participants. New participants should feel free to complete them.

Current Phase (September 2001)

Questionnaires

Demographics: 14 multiple-choice questions on some real-life and in-game demographics.

: Exploring the potential appeal of fashion and style in EQ.

Gadgets, Potions and Real Estate: What if they sold hair-dye or private homes in EQ? How much would you pay?

EQ Friends: 14 multiple-choice questions exploring the friendships that occur in Norrath.

Playing PvP: 9 mainly open-ended questions that try to understand the dynamics on PvP servers and how they are different from non-PvP servers. (For players who have tried PvP only)

Past Phases

Personality Inventory Inventory Set 1: This personality inventory set will allow me to analyze your responses in terms of personality dimensions, and to connect this data to past studies I have done.

 

FAQ:

What is the purpose of this study?

The purpose of this ongoing study is two-fold. First, I want to understand in greater depth some of the dynamics and interactions that my previous studies shed light upon. More importantly, I want to build a database of EQ players that will allow me to analyze responses across all the surveys you have completed. This database will also allow me to research finer questions because I can target a specific subset of the EQ population.

As I understand more of a certain aspect of EQ, this knowledge will prompt new surveys targeting that area. That is why this study is ongoing. You will be invited to respond to surveys as they become available. The strength of this study will rely on being able to refer to your responses across different surveys. Thus, your continued participation will be deeply appreciated.

Wait a minute, if you've graduated from college and you're not doing this for a grade, what are you doing this for?

In fact, only about 2-3% of all the data I have gathered in the past year was used for my senior thesis. I did most of that work outside of any academic requirement. I know it sounds crazy, but gathering data from and understanding EQ players was in and of itself rewarding enough to keep me wanting to know more about the world of Norrath. And that is still the case.

Are you going to spam me if I give you my email address in these questionnaires?

No. You will be notified by email when the next phase is available (usually in about a month or two). And you will also be notified when a newer version of the report is available (every 3 months or so). Apart from that, you won't receive other emails from me. And your email addresses will be kept confidential.

What kind of confidentiality can you guarantee me?

I promise to never publicize/sell/give your email address to anyone, nor will I publicize/sell/give the data I collect with your email address attached. Nor will I ever publish any of the data with your email identified.

However, I may quote your responses anonymously, but with the age and gender shown.

I am not interested in politicized debates or slogan-based discussions. I am interested in exploring the rich social interactions that emerge in massively multi-user worlds.
