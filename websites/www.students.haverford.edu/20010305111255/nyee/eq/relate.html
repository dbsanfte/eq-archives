Relationship Questions

Thank you for taking the time to participate in this online study. Do not feel you have to answer every question. Relevant and honest answers will be much more helpful to me than superficial ones. Write as much or as little as you find appropriate.

I try to ask questions that generate genuine responses. But I understand that you may feel that some questions are phrased in a constrained way. If you find that a question does not hit the center of an issue that interests you, feel free to elaborate on that issue in the question that triggered the feeling.

Don't forget to press the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page when you are done. Any questions or comments should be directed to nyee@haverford.edu.
