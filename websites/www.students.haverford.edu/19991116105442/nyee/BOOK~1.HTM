Warning (11/15/99): This project turned out more to be a good exercise of integrating and re-indexing my own worldview than of writing an integrated approach to philosophy. While I still agree with the fundamentals of this worldview, I would now argue some of the points I make differently or with better examples. The warning is that you should read these chapters as individual essays of my attempt to explain and argue my worldview. So if you disagree with anything, I'd be glad to hear your reasons why.

 
This book was meant to be an integrated approach to objectivist philosophy, evolution and evolutionary psychology. So far, only the first section on philosophy has been written. To know more about why I am writing this and what to expect, click on the links below. Otherwise, just plunge right in.

In any case, if you find any typos, editing errors, missing pages or you just want to comment on something I wrote, please use the submit form below.

Thanks for taking the time to read it.
