EverQuest Study

Thank you for taking the time to participate in this study of EverQuest.

I am interested in several different aspects of EverQuest and have created separate questionnaires for these areas. I invite you to fill out as many or as few of these questionnaires as interests you. Feel free to browse through them before selecting one to fill out.

Also, I would really appreciate it if you could spread the word of this study by telling EverQuest gamers of this webpage address. I am looking to gather as many responses as I can. These forms will be up until April 15th.

Any questions or comments should be directed to nyee@haverford.edu.

"Tell Me About ..": questions about your favorite mobs, your favorite zones, your most memorable moments in EQ.

Relationships: questions about the people you meet through EQ, how well you think you know them, and how you relate to them.

Gender in EQ: questions about what it means to be male or female in EQ, and what the advantages and disadvantages there are in gender-bending.

Comparison with Traditional RPG's: questions about your experience in traditional RPG's, how the two differ, and whether EQ captures the essence of them.

Character Creation: questions about the characters you have, the classes/races you prefer, and how you relate to your characters.

Meta-Game: questions about why EQ appeals to you, what other computers games you play, what is good and bad about EQ, and what you might change about EQ.

Game Dynamics: questions about your playing style, how often you group, how often you lead, and your experience in a group.
