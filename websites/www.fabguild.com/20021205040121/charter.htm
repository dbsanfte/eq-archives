Fire and Brimstone Charter

Fire and Brimstone is a SOCIAL guild and is about having FUN in exploring the world of Norrath and the Planes above.  We exist to gather as friends and adventure the lands.  We raid when we can and we are hear to help each other out and have fun while doing so.  Attendance to events is not mandatory.  We have a chain of command and a format for doing things to keep things running smooth and anyone who has suggestions is welcome to voice them.  We realize that some people put more time into the game and each play for there own reasons.  Those that put more time into the game and helping the guild and guild members will general accomplish more, that’s just the way it is.  ENJOY the game and have fun.  Membership in our guild is a privilege please treat it as such.  The more you put into the guild and helping it and its members the more you in return will get back, its all relative, keep this in mind.  

 

Membership requirements and how to join our guild can be found HERE.  Review this page before requesting membership.

 

General Conduct is a very important thing.  When you are a member of Fire and Brimstone you wear the guild tag and represent the guild as whole.  If you chose to be anonymous that is your preference but we ask that you roleplay to show your guild tag so we know who is online.  Shouting and OOC spaming a zone for a dispute or whatever is discouraged, take it to tells and or contact and officer.  Ninja looting and rolling a higher rank than you hold will not be permitted and you will be removed from the guild.  General disputes with non-guild players or another guild needs to be done with an officer.  Please use common sense and act accordingly as you represent the guild as a whole when you wear the Fire and Brimstone Guild Tag. 

 

Epics are in some way or form on everyone’s goal list usually.  If you are working towards your epic and wish to request the guilds help in a part of the quest then do the research, prepare for the battle do everything you can up to that point.  Over 50% of the epics can be achieved without a major raid.  Once you are of the level to begin the epic quest you can usually do a good portion of it without a lot of help.  Take it on yourself to do these things and prepare the best you can.  Once you are at a point you need to organize a decent size force to continue on your epic quest then ask the guild for help.  Get with an officer and inform them of your target and we will get a raid setup for you.  Also keep in mind that many others are also working on their epic quests as well and whoever is farthest along on there quest will take precedence.  If its your epic raid you will be the raid leader – its your responsibility to learn the zone and plan the take down.

 

General Raids will be scheduled from time to time and posted within the forums.  If you have a target you want to raid POST IT.  When a target seems to be a general guild agreement then that target gets scheduled to be raided.  A poll is done for what day and time and the raid setup.  The person who picked this target will be the raid leader unless they pass the duty to another.  Planes of Power Raid option will be used at all possible events.  If it cannot be used for any reason and standard grouping format is used keep in mind that you don’t raid for exp generally speaking.  You raid to gather the guild and take out a target and help a guildies and have fun.  DO NOT complain about not getting exp or in the event you die on a raid, its part of the game.  Raids will be done in our standard raiding format:

Raid Leader – Overall in charge of the raid (makes the groups)

Pullers – Healers – Melee – Casters

Each of these 4 areas will have a specific leader that gives out there responsibilities

Designated Looters for the raid will be assigned for the raid

All NO DROP loot will be called by the looters and Usable Classes will roll

All DROPABLE loot will be gathered and rolled on at the completion of the Raid

Any loot disputes that arise will be handled AFTER the raid.  Send a TELL to the Raid Leader or an officer and it will be discussed at the completion of the raid.

 

Rank structure will be used for rolling on class specific loot and epic drops on raids.  Your rank will determine what numbers your roll and higher rank will increase your chances.  Thus – the more you contribute to the guild the better your chances of reward.  Rankings will be kept in the forum and after completion of a raid – attendance will be posted by an Officer.  The Rank structure will be as follows:

81-90 raids rolls 900 - 1000

71-80 raids rolls 800 – 1000

61-70 raids rolls 700 - 1000

51-60 raids rolls 600 - 1000

41-50 raids rolls 500 – 1000

31-40 raids rolls 400 - 1000

21-30 raids rolls 300 - 1000

11-20 raids rolls 200 - 1000

1-10 raids rolls 100 – 1000

Probation rolls 1 – 1000

 

Mains and Alts and the rights that each character has always seems to be a concern in some way shape or form.  Fire and Brimstone as it is a SOCIAL guild and laid back, this is kept in mind.  Some people play one character all the time and that’s it.  Others have 8 alts and max out an account and goof around with a bunch of different toons.  So be it – play so you enjoy the game.  We simply ask that you designate one character as your main.  This is the toon generally highest in level, the one you raid with. Go after your epic etc.  Can you bring an alt to a raid?  Sure can – do whatever you like.  Raids do not however stack, meaning:  Each of your characters will have a rank and attendance for your main and your alts does not add up together, each on is separate.

 

Any conflicts between members should be resolved at a one on one level in TELLs if possible.  If this does not work contact an officer asap and let them sort it out.  If this does not still solve the problem then the guild leader and all available officers will discuss it at the earliest time available to solve the problem.

 

Power leveling is done out of the kindness of fellow higher level guild member.  Asking to be Power Leveled in guild chat WILL NOT be permitted.  Don’t asked or beg for it, you are only turning guild members away from you.  If the guild setups up a PL session for lower levels then attend or ask your guild friends in private tells.  DO NOT spam guild chat asking to be Power Leveled.  Don’t be in a hurry to grow up in levels, ENJOY the game.

 

Above all have fun, Kill mobs with reckless abandon, hang with yah guildies and watch each others back.

 

Hail FIRE and BRIMSTONE!
