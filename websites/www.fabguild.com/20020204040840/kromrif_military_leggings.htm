Kromrif Military Leggings

AC: 14
STR: +6   WIS: +5   SV DISEASE: +5   SV COLD: +5
WT: 4.0
Class: ALL
Race: ALL


Dropped Items Needed:
>Bottle of Karsinic Acid  (Orcs and Geonids from Crystal Caverns)
>Blood Wolf Harness  (Blood Wolves in Great Divide)

NPC's Involved
>Erdarf Restil  (Thurgadin)
>Vores the Hunter  (Great Divide)
>Shardwurm Broodmother
>Grakl and Fergul  (Great Divide - triggered spawns)

First things first...Best place to start off would be in Crystal Caverns, simply because it's the only item that drops or is obtained in CC...everything else can be obtained in Great Divide.  It seems that the "Bottle of Karsinic Acid" drops more frequently off of Geonids than Ry'Gorr (IMHO).  And besides, you'll welcome the nice amounts of XP and LOOT!!!  Once you obtain the bottle, move on to GD...

Vores the Hunter is the Dorf that hands out the quest.  I never really got him to "hand" me the quest, but you don't have.  You can pretty much skip all that.  Before you even hand anything to him though, you need to obtain a "Blood Wolf Harness".  In order to do so, you need to kill either a "Drakkel Blood Wolf" or a "Coldain War Wolf".  Both drop the same item.  Choose which faction you want to hurt.  After you obtain the harness, make your way to Thurgadin, and find Erdarf Restil.  He is usually inside the bar (The Happy Harbringer, or something to that effect) that resides near the Temple.  Hand him the Bottle of Karsinic Acid, and will receive a Gem.  Take this Gem and the Blood Wolf Harness to Vores the Hunter, and he will in return give you a "Control Harness".

Take the Control Harness and find the "Shardwurm Broodmother".  She spawns when you hand in the items to Vores the Hunter, and more than likely, she will spawn inside the Wurm Caves.  Remember, some of these Shardwurms do see through Invis, so be careful if you go inside and try and find her.  When you find her, hand her the Control Harness.  At this point, 2 named giants spawn at the entrance to the Giant Fort near the EW zone (Grakl and Fergul).  The Shardwurm Broodmother will then depart to the giant fort...when she gets there, she will start attacking Grakl, causing Fergul to run to the zone line (in all reality, Fergul will despawn quite a bit aways from the actual EW zone, so don't get caught with your leggings down).  Stop Fergul from reaching the zone line, kill him, and loot the "Kromrif Military Leggings".
