This was on the Ranger's Glade
From Vladimirr
For those of you on Bristlebane, I'm sure you've heard of Xoleres Whitewolf... for the rest of you, Xoleres was the first and best legend of Bristlebane. He was a good man, a great leader, and a friend to those who crossed his path. He led the first Vox slaying on Bristlebane, and was a force in the first killing of Nagafen. In fact, the GM's later replaced his surname with that of "Dragonslayer". He founded the guild known as Ring of Valor - for Bristlebane old-timers I need say no more than the guild's name to elicit the honor associated with this name.

In February 2001, as Xoleres was driving home from a friend's house, he was killed by a drunk driver. Recent EQ scams made many skeptical about his death, but after some confirmations we determined that this was true... either way this is not the point of my post. Last night was his memorial service. Over 60 people attended, including our Server GM.
