Guild Website Submission
Sat, Mar 20 2004

You may now add your guilds website to bristlebane.com's guilds webpage. There are future plans to provide features to modify existing guild entries - this is based upon your registration to the forums. Only registered users will be able to submit guild website links, and that specific username will be allowed 1 guild listing in the database. When modifications are allowed, any further guild submissions will update the existing entry. Currently, submitting another entry will just fail.

Update: Easier than I thought, modifications are now a possibility, report problems to admin@bristlebane.com
