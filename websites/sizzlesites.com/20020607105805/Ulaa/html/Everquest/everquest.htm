Welcome to my Everquest Characters page. The picture shown above is my primary Character Ulaa. There is a reasonably long story to the creation of Ulaa and I am going to detail it here.

Ulaa was created on Jan 26, 2000, 01.14.42 AM. My /Played is 60days 7hours 35 mins as of March 6th 2002. I created her specifically to twink my Troll warrior, Garakk. You see Garakk had a wee accident. I asked around and found the best class for twinking was the Necromancer (no-one told me I would need to be at least 50 to do this effectively) and so I started Ulaa.

Ulaa originally started as an Erudite, I got her to about 8th and got sick of running around blind in the forest ( This was well before Paineel ). So I logged in a couple of newbies, tried a Gnome and a hoommie and hated them. So I started a Dark Elf Necromancer and have been flying ever since.

When I was around level 16 I was hunting in the west Karanas, killing off a nasty little Bard called Tricia Windsong. I had just logged in and I ran into another DE (Male) Necromancer called Tindal. We got chatting about sharing the spawn and most of our chat was in character (I.E. we were role-playing). He was there showing a young DE SK how to kill the Bard, I ended up sharing the spawn for quite some time and had a good chat with him. When I was about to log off he asked me to give a /tell to Ameron the next time I logged on.

Well I logged back in and had a chat with Ameron (Tindal), Ameron was at the time about a 40th level Ranger. It turned out he was the Guild leader of a guild called The Silent Watch. They were a small group of friends, largely from the UK, who hung out and played together, he invited me to join and I accepted. Ulaa has been a proud member of The Silent Watch for over two years now.

TSW has gone through many incarnations and has come damn close to completely disbanding at least once. But we have survived. We are currently rated about 4th in terms of sheer power on the Karana Server, not quite Ubar but getting there. Unfortunately I am unable to join a lot of my guilds raids, well any of them really. I really wish I could, I wish I could do my Epic and I wish I could get my SS armor, unfortunately because TSW is largely UK based and I live in New Zealand its just not possible.

Most of TSW's raids are based in GMT, so they will organise to raid at say 7pmGMT on a Thursday. Now to me the equivalent time is 7am on a Friday. This really sucks as a lot of the time I feel like I contribute nothing to the guild, but unless I suddenly move to the USA or the UK its simply the way it is.

Currently Ulaa is based in SkyFire. This is a dangerous high level zone in Kunark just across from the Overthere zone. It is full of Wurms, Wyverns, Drakes and floating sperm. The zone is basically a minimum 51+ zone, Ulaa is currently 55 and is happily soloing the named cycle near the graveyard to get her 56 pet. I am 4 blues off 56, I hope to reach that illustrious level this weekend if possible. I have some screenshots of some of the stuff I am hunting to follow.

Chromodracs are pretty wussy as long as you keep them under control. They have a nasty AE debuff (so dont pull and park them next to a group, not nice) and a nasty DOT. Actually the DOT is one of mine. Its an early Necro spell called Boil Blood. It does about 600 damage in 2 mins. Keep the wings as a stack of 20 of them goes for about 20pp or so. Wyverns are also fairly wussy, tho they can be nasty with resists and early breaking roots. They hit damn hard and if Dark doesnt stick on first cast, run to the zone. You do have Jboots dont you!?. The nastiest of them all is the Wurm. No spells but they have a massive attack range. They can hit you when they appear to be about 50 feet away. They hit hard but not particularly fast, trick is to keep em rooted/snared and dot the hell out of em. Do not let snarte wear off!.

One of my biggest wishes (Next to Epic) is to get the SkyShrine armor. To do that I need to go and do a couple of big raids (actually one) into the arena at Kael Drakkal, this will give me enough faction to get to Ally with Claws of Veeshan and I can then attempt to get the silks (the guild has a couple of items I can MQ I have been told). I spent 51-54 in Cobalt Scar so I have all the gems I need overflowing from my bank, just gotta get the silks.

After 55 there is a spot in the city of Katta that I am going to go and try, I should be able to solo through 56 on the guards there, and Ignite Blood works! yaaaay!. Once I hit 57 the cute and cuddly little Otters in Cobalt Scar are going to take me through to 60 easy !.

 I received the game of Everquest from the lovely folks at Amazon.com around March 1999 and I created my first character, Garakk the Troll Warrior. Now I had great fun with Garakk and at the time was a member of The Undergroundwarlords guild. Unfortunately UW collapsed about 3 months after EQ's release and Garakk was guildless.

I managed to get Garakk to 18th and took him into the Undead dungeon of Najena in EC. While there I was asked to help a druid recover his body which had fallen down the well and was being camped by the nasties at the bottom. I basically jumped down the well, aggro'd all the nasties at the bottom and ran like hell into the depths of Najena. Of course I had a massive train following me and died fairly fast.

The druid however was able to drop down the well and get all his gear and get out again. I ran back from my spawn point and found the Druid camping, I asked if he would help me get my body and he said no and camped. So now I am a Naked Troll in Najena, I shouted and OOC'd etc etc to get someone to help me find my body, no one responded. Eventually Garakks body rotted so I had a naked troll with about 50pp in the bank and no way to re-equip him.

About the time Ulaa reached 49th or so I was able to start twinking Garakk with good gear. I finally got him re-equipped and headed him out to the OT. I was able to solo him extremely well on the newbie side of the OT until he hit about 22, then he headed out to LOIO and he has done the classic path ever since.
