NoD Vision

The Nexus Of Dreams was founded in February, 2002 for the sole purpose of bringing Everquest gamers together in fraternity and for sharing pleasure in the game.

This guild was founded by a close-knit group of friends who spent a lot of time enjoying each other's company in EQ. We are looking to keep that close relationship and to make new friends that have common likes and interests in Norrath.

The founding members believed that a Guild Policy was not necessary at the time because they had common goals and beliefs which constituted a mutually beneficial gaming experience. Due to our increase in numbers, the time has come to express those beliefs in a Guild Policy, which will be enforced by the officers of the Guild.

NoD is looking for friendly faces that believe in taking care of their own. We are looking for people with a high level of maturity and responsibility that can play their class skillfully while having fun. FUN is the big word here!
