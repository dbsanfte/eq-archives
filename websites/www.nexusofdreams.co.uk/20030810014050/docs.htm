Guild Rules

General EQ rules

  • Always be kind, courteous and friendly
  • Never ninja loot in groups
  • Never be abusive and/or rude to another EQ gamer

Guild General Rules

  • Remember you are representing Nexus of Dreams at all times
  • Roleplay and Anonymous can be used (must be used by players under lvl46)
  • Attempt to assist fellow guildies with any problem they may have
  • Always be kind, courteous and friendly

Recruitment

  • Level46+ (exceptions can be made!)
  • NO alts allowed that are from other guilds (except Dreamers of Nexus)
  • Must post: a) Greeting Message, b) Level + Class, c) Factions on, d) Keys held, e) Why want to join NoD?
  • Pass applicants to the relevant people
  • 2 week trial period to become full members (no complaints against person, and intermingles) - if you don't hear from an officer after that period - WELCOME!

Raiding

  • Always assist the MT using a /assist hotbutton
  • Always follow/listen to the Raid Leader and Group Leaders
  • Learn your class specific raid role
  • Make sure you have done researched the zone and printed a map
  • Raid Levels is 46+
  • Check your Raid Points status before each raid
  • Never panic
  • Never expect to gain EXP on raids
  • Always expect to DIE.
  • Turn up on time or you may not get a raid invite!

Problems and Issues

- Within the guild

  • Please involve an officer to oversee the issue. Set up a channel
  • Invite the officer and the person(s) you are not happy with into the channel and discuss the issue like adults

- Complaint about another guild/external player

  • Please inform/involve an officer of the issues. In turn the officer will pursue the issue to the best of his/her ability. Please accept his/her decision gracefully

- External complaint about a guildie

  • Inform 2 officers of this. Please assist the officers when they deal with complaints about you, even if they are false
  • The officers wil view both sides and make a judgement call. Please back their decision and they will enevitably see you in a good light

NOTE: Please try and take or have: /log on to record such events.
