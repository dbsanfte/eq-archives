    Abashi Speaks Out Regarding eBay
    2/13/01, 0:02 PST | Aracnakat

    Ebay'ers
    We're reviewing the auctions up on a daily basis and they are being removed by eBay following our review. The reason we are reviewing them rather than having them taken down outright is that there were a few legitimate auctions up (such as those for the ToyVault EQ action figures).

    Despite the popularity of those disclaimers, there is no disclaimer that someone could put that would result in an illegitimate auction becoming legitimate. They'll be taken down according to the duck test: You know, if it looks like a duck, walks like a duck, and quacks like a duck... It's a duck.

    Absor Class Talks Continue
    2/10/01, 13:10 PST | Aracnakat

    Bard Talk Continues
    Tell me what needs balancing for bards. You may as well give me bugs and broken stuff too. But my focus is going to be on things that are needed to move bards closer to 'balanced' in the game.

    Cleric Talk Continues
    I’m not sure that I really see anything major that needs to be addressed about clerics. But I’d like to hear your opinions about what you would like to see changed, what’s broken and all of that. Primarily I’d like to focus on ‘balance’ issues, but I’m always open to other input as well.

    Druid Talk Continues
    I want you to understand that I think druids are pretty well balanced all around, and I really see little that needs to be changed (and that’s partially why I picked them to be the topic today…). So you’ll have to be convincing, logical and articulate to convince me.

    Magician Talk Continued
    There is some concern about the use of pets in higher level groups. Pathing in places can make them more of a danger than a help. And any creature with an AoE fear spell will exclude the use of magician pets. I'm not sure that these issues constitute any sort of imbalance, though.

    Enchanter Talk Continued
    I am primarily looking for balance concerns here. But I am certainly willing to put bugs on the list. Also, though I really do understand the fact that you want to emphasize and back up statements by others, please, please try to keep the repeat information to a minimum.

    Test of Tactics Today
    2/10/01, 8:42 PST | Kwill

    The Test of Tactics contest will take place at 4pm PST today, February 10 at the Arena off Lake Rathetear. ToT is single Elimination 6 vs. 6 competition for all rounds. Seven-Man teams can be made up of any race/class combination of levels 52 or greater. Only six may compete per match and teams may switch out any team member for the seventh player between the matches.

    The rules have been changed since the last ToT competition, so check out all the rules and details at the Harpy's Head Tavern.

    Good luck to all competitors!
