Intro letter
by Danoctilous Davanhart

Hello Everyone,

Well let me give you a little background before I begin. On 10/14/00 GM Raynara and I were terminated for voicing our concerns with the treatment we and other GM's had received from the Head of CS for Sony Online Entertainment. From what I can tell this is what happened; on 10/11/00 a group of GM's had an incident with the Head of CS.
We have had incidents before with him but this one went beyond too far. All the GM's were asked to take a test and we obliged, but we had a few questions. One of them being if we were to put what happens or what policy states, another was if we should write on the back of the paper. While we were being graciously helped by the Head GM, apparently Mr. Scotto heard us asking questions and didn’t like the fact that the drones were attempting to form a mind of their own to better serve the company and its clientele. He immediately came out of his office, with a raised voice was telling us to stop screwing around, and that we were being stupid and that we have all taken tests before. We reiterated that we were serious, we were stonewalled and the Head GM was pulled away from us. Later that day, we wrote emails voicing our concerns to our supervisor, he sent an email to his supervisor stating that yes were being treated unfairly and that there was a problem. Somewhere along the line something got screwed up and our emails landed on Mr. Scotto's desk, when we came back from our days off on 10/14/00 our time cards were pulled and we were escorted out of the building.
We were told that we were being fired for "insubordination; Refusal to follow orders" but we never were insubordinate. We followed the chain of command and kept our concerns to the point. If you are wondering why it has taken till now to get out into the public I will explain. GM Raynara and I have sent countless emails to people in Verant. Including but not limited to: Human Resources, Smed, and Human Resources Director. We finally went to Mr. Flock, CEO of Sony Online. Mr. Flock was the best contact we had. He was polite and did look into our situation, but after all he has to back up his management and remember the situation if it were to occur again (I would not be surprised if the information he got when he went to Verant was sweetened up for him.)

During my inquiry to what happened to GM Raynara and I, I have uncovered other cases as well, and I have also heard that the other GM's which were brought into Mr. Scotto's office during our situation were spared with a harsh warning if they were to ever complain about Mr. Scotto again they will be fired (Funny that the 2 youngest GM's were the ones terminated). Comments were also made to those GM's when they said that they were getting the impression that they were being treated like children, they were given the response of "You are children!” Keep in mind that these are GM's in there 30's.
As I pushed my investigation even further I learned that there were meetings stating that GM's could no longer talk to former employees, possibly resulting in consequences. That was the last straw, I wrote my final letters to Mr. Flock. He respectfully declined to put any comment on them and now I am here.
I didn’t appreciate the rudeness, and ill-treatment I received from the company, and I have never been treated so badly before in my life by anyone especially an employer, and trust me I am a work-aholic and have worked for many companies, as Network Admins, Technical Analysis, Web Development, and much more.
I even took a pay cut from $35+ an hour to slightly under $10 an hour at Verant because I wanted to break into the gamming industry. I went the extra mile for clientele, to finally be treated like a second class individual at the end.

To close off this letter, I would like to thank the EQ community for the time that I spent with them. I will still be around and you can visit my site www.savethegms.com to contact me and learn more.
Since I am a very active gamer as well you could possibly even see me in other games; AC, EQ, Major Mud, Swords of Chaos, Unreal Tournament, Half-life, Mech Warrior 4, many more and the further games to come (Including some beta's, not going to say which ones though).
I would also like to thank the people on my server Rodcet Nife especially, its awesome guide staff, people that I had to discipline (hey no hard feelings, just had to do my job)(Stop getting suspended Mootalpar! lol :P even I had to laugh at a few of the things you did that required my presence), the people who helped me out I know you were not obligated to and I thank you (you know who you are :P), and a big thanks to the people that complemented me and my guide staff on quests (that means a lot to a GM, especially me since I would stay on 4-6 hours past my shift without pay running them, and drive home for an hour at 4:00 am or so!)

Anyone who wants may contact me at webmaster@savethegms.com. Now I bid you all a farewell but I am not gone for good, I shall remain and continue my quest. May Mithaniel Marr light your path!
