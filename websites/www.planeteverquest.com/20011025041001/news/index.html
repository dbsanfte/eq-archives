UK Server Split Uncertain
10/24/01, 7:36 PST | Kwill

The EQ Live Team has not yet decided on whether to make the Antonious Bayle server a /movelog server or have players start out as new characters. Absor admits it is a conflict and that their decision "won't please everyone," but they hope to come up with "the most pleasing solution."

The advantage of having the /movelog feature, of course, is that players can continue to play as they normally have on their US servers. The disadvantage for some players is that with the /movelog, the established powerful guilds on various servers will move enmasse and will possibly come into conflict with one another, as well as setting the tone for the server immediately.

We will have to wait and see what the EQ Team decides to do.

Spell Changes in Today's Patch
10/23/01, 7:47 PST | Infernal

There weren't many Spell changes in today's patch, but here they are...
  • Divine Barrier (Clr 29) was slightly modified to include a range of 100-feet. This spell is SELF-ONLY though. How far can one be from oneself? Let's skip the metaphysics, it's my humble opinion that this 100-foot range was intended for Sacred Barrier, part of the skill-based post-60 Luclin expansion. If so, the new cleric ability is going to be a ranged, targettable Divine Aura! Nice!
  • Illusion Halfling (Enc 12) had a typo corrected. I kinda preferred "Illusion: Halfing" myself :) Brings to mind a big sharp knife.
  • Aegis of Bathezid (Item Click/Proc) is now Self-Only. If this is a proc, that's a good thing -- kinda hard to target yourself while proc'ing on a mob :). If this is a clickable effect, then it's a downgrade.
  • The Nexus (Wiz 1) Now granted to Wizards at level 1 (or, possibly, Luclin skill-based).
  • (DUPLICATE) Summon Corpse (SK 51, Nec 39) Added. This spell is identical to the normal Summon Corpse with one important difference -- IT CONSUMES NO REAGENT (COFFIN). This may be a skill-activated spell, as it is at the end of spdat.
  • (duplicate) Camouflage (Dru 1) Added. Instant Cast, but sadly it's Outdoor Only. Likely Luclin Skill-based. /yawn
  • Life Curse (SK 1) Added. 900-HP Lifetap, saved versus Disease. Likely Luclin Skill-based
  • Dragon Force (MONK 1 !!!!) This is the FIRST-EVER Pure-Melee spell. It is a 10-HP DD with a 3-foot KnockBack effect, saved versus Magic. Before you say /yawn, Think: Spell Interrupt!
  • Grimling LT 30 (NPC/Proc/Other) This is likely an NPC spell. If you care (You shouldn't), it's a 40-HP Self-Radiating AE

Test of Tactics III
10/19/01, 9:13 PST | Kwill

Just a reminder that the ToT III starts up next week on Wednesday, October 24, and will run through November 28. Check the schedule at the Harpy's Head Tavern for individual server dates and times.

The basic rules are:

The entry fee for this event will be 25,000 pp to be paid on final roll call on event day to events staff. A team may elect to not pay entry fee, but will then be ineligible for any prizes awarded for winning.

First place team on a server will be awarded the Golden Championship Ring to all team members. First place in the Finals competition will be awarded Platinum Championship Ring as a replacement for Server level ring.

This event will consist of teams of 18 members per team. Each team will have no more then 3 members of one class. The teams will be formed by adding up the levels of each of its members to form a team total. This total number must be 1060 or more levels.

A team will be allowed to have 2 alternate members in addition to the 18 member teams. The alternates may be switched in to a team roster any time before or after a match. When using an alternate for combat the team must still meet the Team Total and have no more then 3 members of one class during combat. Both alternates must be registered at the same time as other team members.

If a team on the day of the contest has less then 18 members to team available and has no alternates to fill in for remaining spots, the team may elect to still compete. They will not be allowed to recruit any addition members to their team and will fight understaffed if they choice to stay in the contest.

The object of each team will be to eliminate as many of the opposing team members with the fewest members lost of their own team in a 20-minute battle.

Check out the existing Team Rosters for each server here.

More Skill-Activated spells?
10/18/01, 9:53 PST | Infernal

In case you missed my previous conjecture, I believe Verant is working on an entirely new concept for Caster-classes in Velious. Specifically, I think they are implemting SKILL-Based Spells, much the same as Harm Touch or Lay on Hands currently works. They are technically Skills, but they actually activate a SPELL. They are all new to the end of the SPDAT file and in sequence to each other, also they are level 1 spells and often 0-mana cost.

New (Possibly) Skill-Activated Spells
These spells are listed in sequence with the spells I've theorized are skill-activated, but they are not attributed to a class for level 1, but I don't believe that would mean they *cannot* be skill-activated. You'll have to guess whether they are going to be Skill-activated or Item-based.

  • Mana Regeneration 2-Mana/tick Clarity. Will not stack with These Spells
  • Resistance Tonic  +10 vs. All buff, self-only. Will not stack with These spells
  • Focused Mind +2 for Effect 112 (unknown). This new effect will have some effect on Casters. See other Effect 112 Spells
  • Dead Aim +10 for Effect 110 (unknown). See other Effect 110 Spells
  • Growth Scale player to 133 % Size
  • Strong Root (Wiz 1, Dru 1) A new LURE of Root, but it only lasts 1-minute.
  • Masochism (Shm 1) Strong Cannibalize, 1924 HP converted into 1066 Mana
  • Rabid Bear (Shm 1) Bear Form, +200 ATK
  • Manaburn (Wiz 1) No parameters are defined, but I'd guess this is an unresistable uber-nuke, burning ALL mana at a fixed ratio for a do-or-die nuke.
  • Restore Companion (Nec 1, Mag 1, BL 1) 1981 HP Pet Heal spell
  • Gather Mana (Enc 1) +5000 Mana, Self Only
  • Frenzied Burnout (Mag 1) Pet Buff, +100 ATK, +17 AC, +100 STR, 195% Haste. Only lasts 1.5 mins though :(
  • Lifeburn (Nec 1) Probably the same as Manaburn, but it's Vampiric. I would guess the Mana:Damage ratio is not as good as the Wiz one, due to it being a Lifetap. This is also unresistable.
  • Words of the Undead King (Nec 1) Mind Wipe + Mesmerise, lasting 42 seconds. Undead Only.
  • Wave of Revulsion (Nec 1) LURE-based Fear AE, lasting 42-seconds
  • Improved Familiar (Wiz 1) I'd have to guess this spell is going to be improved. As it stands, it's inferior to Greater Familiar
  • Undead Pact (Nec 1) Charm Undead. Duration is 0+50, and 0+n are skill-based. I'd guess this charm will last many hours.
  • Servant of Nature (Dru 1) Charm Animals. Duration also 0+50, likely many hours.
  • Dominating Gaze (Enc 1) Charm. Also 0+50 duration, likely many hours.
  • Disease Touch -80 DD, Saved vs. Disease
  • Poison Touch (Wiz 1) -80 DD, Saved vs. Poison. NOTE: This spell may have simply been cloned from a wiz spell.. don't take the class limitation seriously. I'd guess these are Necro/SK or Shaman

Other, Proc, or NPC Spells

  • Storm Strength ADDED. This is an exact duplicate of the Druid/Ranger Storm Strength spell. Very likely an activated/clickable item -- perhaps it will be slightly different, since a clickable item can ignore class limitations
  • Aegis of Bathezid ADDED. This is a targettable 25-hp damage shield. Its name implies it may be a clickable item from Chardok
Ranger
  • Falcon Eye (Rgr 52) Upgraded from 10 to 20 as the parameter to effect 110. This makes the progression from Hawk Eye, Falcon Eye, and Eagle Eye more obvious, at 10, 20, and 40 respectively.

My Question Answered!
10/18/01, 9:16 PST | Kwill

Straight from the Developer's Corner!
Hi,

Just wondering why you have left off your European customer base from the "win a year of EQ sweepstakes"?

Thanks!

Kwill Heartfyre

Basically it comes down to legal reasons. It is very complicated and expensive to try to conform to the legal requirements for sweepstakes in all the different national and state jurisdictions. We certainly have as much respect and appreciation for our customers from outside the United States, it's just not practical to try to work out all the complications that arise trying to support an international sweepstakes.

Investigative reporting at it's finest.


Trigger and Friends
10/18/01, 9:02 PST | Kwill

It's getting harder and harder to come up yet another horse-related cliche--but I keep trying! Absor answered some player questions about horses in this thread on the Developer's Corner:
Most likely horses will not be allowed to function indoors. There are just too many situations that could make problematic for horses indoors, not the least of which might be stuffing the rider into the roof of a low ceiling. They will probably disappear when you zone into an indoor location.

When you die the horse will probably disappear. He will also probably disappear when you dismount, but you will indeed be able to dismount. Ed. Note: and I was so looking forward to seeing "YOU have taken 220 points of damage falling on your dignity, your horse ran off!" You'll be able to 'call' your horse back when you need it (which will also cause you to mount the horse).

If you purchase Shadows of Luclin then you will be able to ride your horse in any outdoor zone, old world or new. However, those that do not have Luclin and all the new graphics it includes (as well as those that own Shadows of Luclin but choose to disable the new graphics) will not see your horse. Only those using the new expansion will be able to see horses.

Absor also thanked the person who sent in the detailed diagram of horse anatomy (hehe, it was sorely needed!) and explains that the horses will look better the next time we see them--the first screen shots were not the best they can do.
