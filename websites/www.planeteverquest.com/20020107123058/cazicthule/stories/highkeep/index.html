The Highkeep Alliance - For the Light!
Submitted by Steihl Guildmaster of The Black Hand
Written by Tyrisis of The Black Hand

Last night an event was held in West Commons in which the forces of good were rallied to defend Highkeep against a Dark Elf (Ogres and Trolls also) invasion. Sinerin, Tyrsis, and Sonati were all in the area and responded to the call for help.

Sinerin was the first to arrive and kept us apprised of what was happening. Sonati and Tyrsis were among the last few people added to the army and were quickly recruited into the forming groups. Mass chaos ensued as the troops were getting restless. Finally, the call of "Attack!!!" was issued and Dark Elf NPCs started spawning everywhere. Our group almost immediately scattered. I worked my way along the wall and toward the derv camp when I saw in the dirt the tracks of a Teir'Dal Chaplain. I quickly honed in on his location and began casting feverishly on him. After a short battle, the fires of Tunare quickly brought him to his knees. On his corpse was a Teir'Dal Morning Star.

I heard the call for reinforcements at the wizards portal and I rushed to help. I saw 3 Teir'Dal Corprals beating down some of the younger members of the army and began firing spell after spell at them. Two of them fell to my hands and the other to a pack of angry warriors. After the first invasion was done I began scouting and found more Teir'dal assaulting the toll booth. A rogue in my group named Shredder and I quickly dispatched another corpral and chased off a few others. We rallied back at the temple where a Commander Xesor was being attacked by an angry mob of the Highkeep Alliance. I lit into him with many spells and brought him quickly from half life to death with all of my mana, yet was unable to secure the kill for my own. Then we rushed back to the druid ring where Sinerin was under attack. Another commander was hitting him for 144s but he quickly ducked out and made it to safety as the mob took him down.

As the battle wrapped up, the dark elf PCs under the guild of "Lany's Army" had the zones surrounded and went to war with the Highkeep Alliance". Almost all at once the war exploded and the area was a battle ground. I implored the druids to use hit and run tactics but many were falling fast. I quickly darted in to the thickest of their ranks and cast sunbeam, blinding them all and then would assualt the weaker ones I could find.

I was hot on the trail of a fleeing Dark Elf as a pair of warriors were on him as well. I closed distance and she was stunned. It was short work from there as she fell.

I then rushed back into their ranks to find a magician engaged with a melee dark elf. Once the dark elf realized he was outnumbered he began to flee. But our casting power brought him down quickly as well.

I then began hit and run tactics on the dark elves that were meditating. An ogre attacked me and I laid into him with spells. He tried to flee but a well placed gust of wind beat out a heal from a dark elf cleric and sent him to his grave.

Things then took a turn for the worse as my hit and run annoyed too many casters and they locked on to me. Lejes, a level 50 necromancer, stayed on my tail and brought me down with some powerful spells.

After the battle was complete. I returned the looted Teir'Dal weapons to Lord Tephys whom came down to declare victory. Tephys spoke of more invasions to come. When they do, we will be ready.
