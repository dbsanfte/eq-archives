More Luclin SPDAT Changes...
8/17/01, 12:07 PST | Infernal

Necromancers
  • Insidious Retrogression (Nec 49) Now has -25 STR and -25 ATK in addition to the existing One-Time DD, and existing Snare effects.
Wizards
    Garrison's Roaming Vision (Wiz 58) now has a new "parameter" of RoamingVis, which may have a different model than the regular Eye of Zomm. Also, duration was changed from 20 Minutes to 0+4 ticks, which appears to indicate a Skill-based duration.
Rangers (also affected: Druids, Shamen)
  • Force of Nature (Rng 49) was previously configured for Dru 34, Shm 24. I had mentioned previously that this was likely a bug -- and this has turned out to be the case. Sorry Druids and Shamen.
BeastLords
  • Spirit of Lightning (BL 15) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOLEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Magic with a 50-point Taunt effect.
  • Spirit of the Blizzard (BL 22) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOBEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Cold with a 50-point Taunt effect.
  • Spirit of Inferno (BL 30) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOIEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Fire with a 50-point Taunt effect.
  • Spirit of the Scorpion (BL 39) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOScEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Poison with a 50-point Taunt effect.
  • Spirit of Vermin (BL 49) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOVEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Disease with a 50-point Taunt effect.
  • Spirit of Wind (BL 52) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOWEffect, a 30-hp Proc save versus Magic with a 1ms Stun (Spell Interrupt) Effect, a 2-foot KnockBack effect, and a 2-foot Upward Push effect.
  • Spirit of the Storm (BL 54) ADDED. Grants your pet a proc of  SOStEffect, a 50-hp Proc save versus Fire with a 200-point Taunt effect.

Cal State Economics & EQ
8/16/01, 6:48 PST | Aracnakat

Edward Castronova PhD the Associate Professor of Economics at Cal State Fullerton sent this in:
I'm a professor at Cal State Fullerton (USA) doing a research paper on the virtual economy of Norrath. Most economists don't know that Norrath exists, let alone that hundreds of thousands of people are active in its markets. So I've started writing a report about it for them.

I can get a great deal of info on such things as prices just from going around Norrath myself. But I need help from other people to find out a few things, such as how a typical person spends time on a character, and what kinds of accomplishments they build up – tradeskills, levels, plat, and so on.

I've designed a brief survey of economic activity, modeled on standard RL government surveys, and I would like to invite Planet Everquest's readers to take it. If you're reading this and you have a minute, could you take the survey?

The survey takes about 5 minutes and has 16 questions. It can be found at business.fullerton.edu/ecastronova/EQON.html

There is also a FAQ if you have other questions.

The results will be used to write a paper for an audience of academic economists. You will not be asked to give an email address, so there will be no follow-up.

I'm more than happy to answer questions and take suggestions :), please email me at ecastronova@fullerton.edu

Eventually the report will be posted at my website at business.fullerton.edu/ecastronova

Thanks for your time.

Edward Castronova PhD
Associate Professor of Economics
Cal State Fullerton (USA)
Stop by and give Edward a hand!

Patch Message
8/15/01, 7:03 PST | Kwill

August 15, 3AM

** Spell Changes **

- Celestial Tranquility and Call of Earth will now stack
- Some stacking issues with Torpor have been corrected.
- Celestial Cleansing will fizzle less now
- Cannibalize should fizzle slightly less often now

** Zone Changes **

- Updated some pathing in Runnyeye, Paineel, Siren's Grotto and Kedge.
- The Drowned Citizens are back, for sure this time.
- Fixed a bug that was not allowing some NPCs in Kael to see invisible creatures as they should.
- Restored "charmability" of most NPCs (merchants will remain uncharmable) in Kael due to fix of combat AI bug listed bellow under ** Miscellaneous Changes **.

** Item Changes **

- The Giant Tree Flayer is now Large instead of Tiny.
- Staff of Temperate Flux has been changed to main hand only.
- Ring of Frost Spiders can now be worn.
- Robe of the Seacaller should now be equipable.

** Trade Skill Changes **

- Imbued Field Plate Boots should be working properly now.

** Sullon Zek Changes **

- Changed the way resists are calculated when a Player Character casts a spell on another Player Character on Sullon Zek. Resist modifiers based on level will now be more smoothly distributed and will allow lower level characters a slightly better chance to land spells on higher level characters. At the same time giving lower level characters a slightly better chance to resist spells cast on them by higher level characters.
- Characters below 6th level cannot be pick pocketed in any zone.
- Guards have a better chance of aggro'ing a rogue that fails at picking pockets near guards.

** Miscellaneous Changes **

- Fixed a recently introduced combat AI bug that caused NPCs to focus unduly on attacking pets rather than PCs
- Added a few crowns to the list of items that can be traded in for new crowns with new textures. The same NPCs that were interested in trading out your helms will now trade your crowns. Please keep in mind that they will not be trading them back to you. Once you get the new look it's yours to keep, even if it clashes with your favorite eye shadow.

Changes to Luclin SPDAT
8/13/01, 6:35 PST | Infernal

Several interesting changes went in recently for the Luclin SPDAT file, more than reported on other sites. Specifically, stackability of several spell classes has changed, some spells have moved, some spells have had their levels and/or values changed, etc.
  • Spirit of Wolf: Now given to BeastLords at level 30 (same as Rangers), was level 39
  • Resurrection Effects: Stacking Changed (Attack speed debuff moved from slot 2 to slot 12)
  • Death Peace: (SK 60) Fizzle Rate reduced from 25% to 5%
  • Celestial Tranquility: (Monk Epic) Stacking changed: Slot 3 AC Buff moved to slot 8. Should remove stacking problems with these spells. (assuming other slots do not conflict)
  • Celestial Remedy:  Was (Clr 44) now (Clr 19).
  • Blessed Armor of the Risen: (Clr 58) Clarity Effect improved from 2 Mana/tick to 4 Mana/tick. Also, Clarity effect moved from slot 5 to slot 4. Introduces stacking problems with these Spells.
  • Life Shield: (Dru 54) Further defined. This spell is still very incomplete, my guess is that the "Water Breathing" effect is a Red Herring. Take this with a grain of salt.
  • Hierophants Preservation replaced with Circle of Seasons (Dru 58). Basically, Circle of Winter + Circle of Summer in one buff.
  • Moonfire: Dramatically Nerfed. Cast Time increased from 7.0s to 7.5s, Mana Cost increased from 410 to 450, and DD reduced from 1115 to 1100.
  • Ichor Spear (Shm 54) (was: 650 Poison DD) now Plague of Insects, a Disease-based ATK Debuff
  • Nife's Lament (Shm 58) (was: 850 Poison DD) now Talisman of Epuration, a Disease/Poison group buff
  • Summon Shield of the Elements (Mag 12) replaced with Summon Elemental Defender
  • Summon Phantom Leather: Now has some item ID parameters, also has added more effects (to summon multiple items?) May summon a stack of items for various armor slots (as spell text would imply, "... summons a suit of Phantom Leather armor")
  • Summon Phantom Chain: As above
  • Transon's Elemental Infusion: (Mag 52) Now a Pet Heal spell, should heal 556 HP for 250 mana
  • Veil of Elements: (Mag 54) Duration increased from 10 mins, to 1 hr 40 mins !!! WOW for an invisibility spell, that rocks
  • Rod of Mystical Transvergance (sic): Now has an Item ID to be summoned
  • Transon's Terrible Torment: (Mag 58) Now resists versus COLD. Congratulations Mages! You now have a Cold DD! Although the text, and graphic Icons, still show fire graphics :(
  • Minor Familiar: (Wiz 29) Now has Resist ALL +10 in the extended slots (should stack with all other resist buffs), and the Summon parameter changed to Familiar1
  • Lesser Familiar: (Wiz 49) Has resist all +15. Also, See Invisibility.
  • Firetree's Familiar Augment: (was: Mag 29, now Wiz 52). Also, now can only be cast in Outdoor zones.
  • Familiar: (Wiz 54) As above Familiar series, but +20 vs. All, See Invis, and +75 Mana buff
  • Greater Familiar: (Wiz 60) As above, but +25 vs. All, See Invis, +150 Mana, and +3 Mana/tick Clarity. Since all the extra effects are in high slots, this spell buff should stack with almost everything.
  • Illusion Imp: (Enc 52) Now has an Illusion ID, Levitate, and +30 vs. Fire (Should stack with other vs. Fire buffs)
  • Trickster's Augmentation: (Enc 56) Now adds Melee Proc Tricksters Torment: a 70 HP Fire DD. Also, Trickster's Augmentation grants Imp Form, Levitate, and +30 vs. Fire (should be tackable with everything)
  • Enchantment of Tormenting: Granted to Enchanters at lvl 58. Still incomplete
  • Koadic's Endless Intellect: (Enc 60) Scaling formulae changed for +WIS, +INT components. Still capped at +25 each, so no significant change. However, 100-foot radius was set for the spell to make this spell actually work on your group :) Also, they introduced a grammatical error to the spell text (duh). Verant should hire someone who is Literate to implement Spell Text :)
  • Scream of Death: (was SK 49, now SK 39) Defined. Now is a 20-minute Add-Proc spell, adding the spell  MentalCorruptionEffect: a 35 Mana Drain Vampiric Effect saved vs. Disease. EDIT: This spell is likely BUGGED, and intended to add  ScreamOfDeathEffect: a -25 HP, Vampiric Magic-based DD
  • Mental Corruption: (SK 52) Adds Proc:  Mystical Transvergance (sic): a -300 DD, +220 Mana effect on oneSELF, similar to Cannibalize. (WTF is Verant thinking? LAME!) EDIT: This spell is probably BUGGED, and intended to activate  MentalCorruptionEffect: a -35 Mana, Vampiric Disease-based spell
  • Torrent of Hate: (was SK 56, now SK 54) ATK Debuff moved to slot 7
  • Torrent of Pain: (was SK 58, now SK 56) AC Debuff reduced (was -80, now -40) and moved from slot 3 to slot 7.
  • Torrent of Death replaced with Torrent of Fatigue: Also, !!!BUG!!! was (SK 60) now (Nec 58). Spell now defined as !!!BUG!!! +15 STR, +15 STAM. This spell likely should be SK 58, and -15 STR/STA.
  • Cloak of the Akheva: (SK 60) Duration improved from 15 mins to 2 hr, 30 min.
  • Divine Vigor: (Pal 39) Improved from +50 HP to +100 HP.
  • Words of Conviction: (Pal 58) Spell Number Changed. 10-Second Targetted Stun
  • Unyielding Resilience: (Pal 56) Drains a little less mana per tick as it Heals (Regenerates HPs). Was -5 Mana / tick, now -4 Mana per tick
  • Wave of Divinity: (Pal 58) ADDED. Group Heal 660 HP for 650 Mana.
  • Hawk Eye: (Rgr 15) Defined. 15 mana, 3.0s cast, 0+9 tick duration (Skill based, should be an hour or more), +10 to Something. (Effect ID Not known at this time).
  • Falcon Eye: (Rgr 52) 75 Mana, +10 (something), See Invis buff
  • Eagle Eye: (Rgr 58) 100 Mana, +40 (Something), See Invis, Ultravision buff
  • Spiritual Purity: (BL 59) Stacking and effects changed. Clarity moved to slot 4, introducing stacking problems with  These spells. Fizzle rate reduced from 25% to 10%.
Stacking Changes:
Spells that had Resist Cold in SLOT 1, or Resist Poison in SLOT 1, have had these effects moved to Slot 5. This is to ensure that Circle of Seasons (Dru 58) and Talisman of Epuration (Shm 58) will stack with NONE OF the Resist Cold series, the Resist Fire series, the Resist Poison series, or the Resist Disease series.
  • Resist Cold
  • Endure Cold
  • Resist Poison
  • Endure Poison
  • Resistant Skin
  • Psalm of Purity
  • Aura of White Petals
  • Aura of Green Petals
  • Splintering Shards
  • Circle of Summer
  • Talisman of Shadoo
