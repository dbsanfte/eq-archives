Frontier Mountains


Frontier Mountains
By Hop

Location: The Frontier Mountains is located between four zones: The Overthere, Lake of Ill Omen, The Dreadlands and Burning Wood.

Continent: Kunark

Creatures & NPCs: Berserk Brutes, Blightcallers, Bloodscale the Vicious, Blugtigin, Bonecharmers, Boneseekers, Boogoog, Burynai Grand Cenobite, Burynai Miners, Burynai Treasure Seeker, Cenobites, Clazxiss, Craigs, Digmasters, Dirttracers, Diseased Brute, Dragoons, Draketamer, Dustback, Eboneyes, Eldrak Howlingbear, Gemeye, Goblin Chieftain, Goblin Chieftain, Goblin Dustscryer, Goblin Headhunter, Goblin Herb Collector, Goblin Iksar Slayer, Goblin Raiders, Goblin Trailblazer, Goblin Whipcracker, Goblinstomper, Gorging Brutes, Gromlok, JooJooga, Krenlek, Mangy Brute, Miner Bordakn, Mountain Giant Hillocks, Mountain Giant Prospector, Mountain Giant Tump, Overseer Miklek, Partisans, Plaguebringers, Rockchanters, Rockshapers, Rockwolves, Sarnak Assassins, Sarnak Bloodbinders, Sarnak Knights, Sarnak Mystics, Sarnak Rebels, Sarnak Scavengers, Sarnak Seers, Sarnak Warlords, Slavedrivers, Slithinis, Snaorf, Stonegrinder, Varithyx, Wandering Stonehealer and Whipcrackers.

Zone Type: Outdoor (Mountains)

Factions: Unknown at this time.

Level Range: 20- 40

Dungeons: Mines of Nurga (location -2800, -600) and Temple of Droga (location -3400, 2900)

Miscellaneous:

  • The zone into The Overthere is found at location 4000, 1550.
  • The zone into Lake of Ill Omen is found at location -500, -4000.
  • The zone into The Dreadlands is a tunnel found at location -3700, -3300.
  • The zone into The Burning Woods is a tunnel found at location -2500, 3800.
