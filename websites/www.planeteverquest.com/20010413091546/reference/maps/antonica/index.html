Antonica
From The Official EverQuest Page


Antonica Zones
By Questers of Norrath

Antonica is the largest of all continents and is home to a vast majority of the races including barbarians, dark elves, halflings, humans, ogres, trolls and some half-elves. The zones within Antonica can all be accessed with the original version of the game.
