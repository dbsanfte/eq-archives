Field of Bones


Field of Bones
By Hop

Location: The Field of Bones is located in the heard of Kunark and is surrounded by several zones: The City of Cabilis, The Swamp of No Hope, The Emerald Jungle and Warslilks Woods.

Continent: Kunark

Creatures & NPCs: Bandits, Bonebinder Hatchlings, Bonebinders, Bonecrawler Hatchlings, Bonecrawlers, Burynai Bane Spiders, Burynai Burrowers, Burynai Cutters, Burynai Excavators, Carrion Beetle Hatchlings, Carrion Queens, Carrion Shredders, Dakoit Iksars, Decaying Skeletons, Emerald Fencers, Emerald Scarabs, Giant Scorpions, Greater Scalebone Skeletons, Greater Skeletons, Heartsting Scorpions, Iksar Bandits, Iksar Brigands, Iksar Dakoits, Iksar Manslayers, Iksar Marauders, Large Scorpions, Militia Skeletons, Scalebone Skeletons, Scaled Prowler Wolves, Scaled Wolf Cubs, Scaled Wolf Hunters, Scaled Wolf Pups, Scaled Wolf Stalkers, Scorpions, Servant of Sythrax, Skeletal Jesters, Skeletons, Sythrax Guardians and Tangrin.

Zone Type: Outdoor

Factions: Legion of Cabilis.

Level Range: 1 - 25

Dungeons: Kaesora (location -1900, -300) and Kurn's Tower (location 850, 1050)

Miscellaneous:

  • This is the low to mid level hunting area for new Iksars.
  • The zone to Cabilis is at this location: 2000, -2700.
  • The zone to The Swamp of No Hope is at this location: -3300, 1000.
  • The zone to The Emerald Jungle is at this location: -1750, -1200.
  • The zone to Warsliks Woods is at this location: 1400, 4300.
