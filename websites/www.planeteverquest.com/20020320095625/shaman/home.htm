'ANCIENT' Spells uncovered

As many of you will know, due to class balancing issues, a type of spell known as an ancient spell has been added. These are powerful spells for a certain class that improve a high level character able to get them tenfold. Shaman have got an improved version of the already powerful spell: 'Avatar' and a powerful dot. The original thread on the matter can be found here.
