Burning Woods


Burning Woods
By Hop

Location: The Burning Woods has three other zones which border it. The Skyfire Mountains are in the upper south west corner while The Frontier Mountains are in the north east corner. The Dreadlands are in the central north end of the zone.

Continent: Kunark

EQ Version Needed: Ruins of Kunark

Bind: Bind self only.

Creatures & NPCs: Ash Hornets, Avengers, Azdalin, Cinder Hornets, Ember Hornets, Entallon, Extremists, Forest Giant Ancients, Forest Giant Arbors, Forest Giant Verdants, Gorgul Paclock, Great Plague Skeletons, Greater Barbed Skeletons, Greater Plaguebone Skeletons, Greater War Boned Skeletons, Gullerback, Gylton, Ixiblat Fer, Klyokz Kol, Korasal Klyseer, Moldering Apes, Moldering Gorillas, Mountain Giant Ancients, Mountain Verdant Giants, Naxot Deepwater, Nezekezena, Phurzicon, Plague Bone Skeletons, Sarnak Avengers, Sarnak Champions, Sarnak Enthusiasts, Sarnak Extremists, Sarnak Imitators, Sarnak Knights, Sarnak Zealots, Scoriae Hornet, Slixin Klex, Slixin Klex, Tatterback Apes, Tatterback Gorillas, Telin Darkforest, Tottering Apes, Tottering Gorillas, Verdants, Wurms and Xyfl.

Zone Type: Outdoor (Lots of Hills & Forests)

Factions: Sarnak Collective

Level Range: 40 - 60

Dungeons: Chardok (6200, -4000)

Miscellaneous:

  • The zone to Skyfire Mountains can be found at 5000, 1500.
  • The zone to Frontier Mountains can be found at -2600, -4150
  • The zone to The Dreadlands can be found at -5000, -1000.
Feel free to submit more information or another map for this zone!
