Factions

There are many factions found in Norrath. When you kill something you may receive a good, bad or both good and bad faction hits. You can also gain and lose factions for completing many of the quests. Below is a list of factions and what they stand for.

  • Agents of Mistmoore: Spies for the Mistmoore.
  • Allize Tae Ew: Lizardmen in Cazic-Thule.
  • Allize Vol Ew: Lizardmen in The Feerrott.
  • Antonious Bayle: The mayor of Qeynos.
  • Arcane Scientists: The caster's guild in Freeport.
  • Ashen Order: The monk's guild in Freeport.
  • Befallen Inhabitants: The creatures that dwell in Befallen.
  • Bloodgills: Watergoblins of Kunark.
  • Bloodsabers: Human followers of Bertoxxulous in Qeynos.
  • Broken Skull Clan: Clan of Trolls.
  • Brood of DiZok: Clan of Sarnaks living in Chardok.
  • Brood of Kotiz: The necromancer's guild in Cabalis.
  • Cabilis Residents: The residents of Cabilis.
  • Carson McCabe: The leader of Highpass Hold.
  • Chetari: Rat clan in Dragon Necropolis (evil clan).
  • Circle of Unseen Hands: The rogue's guild in Qeynos.
  • Clan Runny Eye: Goblins found in Butcherblock Mountains, Misty Thicket and Runny Eye.
  • Claws of Veeshan: Inhabitants of Skyshrine.
  • Clerics of Tunare: The cleric's and paladin's guilds in Felwithe.
  • Clerics of Underfoot: The cleric's and paladin's guilds in Kaladim.
  • Clurg: A bartender in Oggok.
  • Coalition of Tradefolk: The merchants of Freeport.
  • Coalition of Tradefolk Illegal: The rogue's guild in Freeport.
  • Coldain: The residents of Thurgadin.
  • Commons Residents: The residents of East and West Commonlands.
  • Corrupt Qeynos Guards: The seedy gaurds in Qeynos.
  • Craftkeepers: The enchanter's guild in Erudine.
  • Craknek Warriors: The warrior's guild in Oggok.
  • Crimson Hands: The wizards guild in Erudine.
  • Crusaders of Greenmist: The shadow knight's guild in Cabilis.
  • Crushbone Orcs: The orc clan operating in Faydark and Crushbone.
  • Da Bashers: The warrior's guild in Grobb, also the troll guards.
  • Dain Frostreaver IV: The leader of the Coldain.
  • Dark Bargainers: The dark elf merchants of Neriak.
  • Dark Ones: The shaman guild in Grobb.
  • Dark Reflection: The necromancer's guild in Ak'Anon.
  • The Dead: The necromancer's and shadow knight's guild in Neriak.
  • Deadfist Orcs: Orcs operating out of Freeport.
  • Deep Muses: The rogue guild in Ak'Anon.
  • Deeppockets: The rogue guild in Rivervale.
  • Deepwater Knights: The cleric's and paladin's guild in Erudine.
  • Dervish Cutthroats: The dervish cutthroats operating in North Ro, South Ro and Oasis of Marr.
  • Dismal Rage: The human followers of Innoruuk in Freeport.
  • Donovon: A citizen in High Keep.
  • Dread Guard Inner: The dark elf guards of Neriak.
  • Dread Guard Outer: The dark elf guards of Neriak's Foregn Quarter.
  • Ebon Mask: The rogue's guild of Neriak.
  • Eldritch Collective: The caster's guild in Ak'Anon.
  • Emerald Warriors: The warrior's guild in Kelethin.
  • Faydark's Champions: The ranger's guild in Kelethin.
  • Firiona Vie: The City of Firiona Vie.
  • Freeport Militia: East and West Freeport gaurds.
  • Frogloks of Guk: The live frogloks found in Innothule Swamp, Lower Guk and Upper Guk.
  • Frogloks of Kunark: The frogloks found in the Swamp of No Hope.
  • Gatecallers: The mage's guild of Erudine.
  • Gate Keepers: The leaders of the three caster's guilds found in Erudin.
  • Gem Choppers: The warrior's guild and guards of Ak'Anon and the guards of Steamfont.
  • Gnarled Fist Clan: A clan of Trolls.
  • Goblins of Fire Peak: Goblin clan located in Solusek A.
  • Greenblood Knights: The shadow knight's guild in Oggok.
  • Guardians of the Vale: The guards of Rivervale.
  • Guards of Qeynos: The guards in Qeynos.
  • Heretics: The necromancer's and shadow knight's guilds in Paineel.
  • High Guard of Erudin: The guards in Erudin.
  • High Hold Citizen: The citizens of High Hold.
  • High Hold Merchants: The merchants of High Hold.
  • Highpass Guards: The guards of Highpass Hold.
  • Horde of Xalgoz: Werewolf clan found in Kunark.
  • Indigo Brotherhood: The warrior's guild in Neriak.
  • Inhabitants of Hate: The residents of The Plane of Hate.
  • Jagged Pine Treefolks: The druid's guild in Surefall Glade.
  • Kane Bayle: Brother of Antonius Bayle a prominent member of the Blood Sabres in Qeynos.
  • Karana Bandits: Groups of bandits found in the Karanas.
  • Karana Residents: The citizens of the Karanas.
  • Kazon Stormhammer: The King of Kaladim.
  • Keepers of the Art: The caster's guild in Felwithe.
  • Kelethin Merchants: The merchants of Kelethin.
  • Kerra Isle: The inhabitants of Kerra Isle.
  • King Ak'Anon: The ruler of Ak'Anon.
  • King Aythonx Thex: The king of Neriak.
  • King Tearis Thex: The king of Felwithe.
  • King Tormax: The king of the giants in Kael Drakkal.
  • Kithicor Residents: The citizens of Kithicor Forest.
  • Knights of Thunder: The Paladins in the Karanas.
  • Knights of Truth: The Paladins in Freeport.
  • Kromrif: The frost giants of Kael Drakkal.
  • The Kromdek: Giants in Kunark.
  • The Kromdul: Giants in Kunark.
  • Kromzek: The storm giants of Kael Drakkal.
  • League of Antonican Bards: The bard's guild in both Freeport and Qeynos.
  • Legion of Cabilis: Iksar army in Cabilis.
  • Mayong Mistmoore: The vampiric ruler of Castle Mistmoore.
  • Mayor Gubbin: The mayor of Rivervale.
  • Meldrath: Gnomish necromancer in Steamfont Mountains.
  • Merchants of Ak'Anon: The merchants in Ak'Anon.
  • Merchants of Erudin: The merchants in Erudin.
  • Merchants of Felwithe: The merchants in Felwithe.
  • Merchants of Halas: The merchants in Halas.
  • Merchants of Highpass: The merchants in Highpass Hold.
  • Merchants of Kaladim: The merchants in Kaladim.
  • Merchants of Qeynos: The merchants in Qeynos.
  • Merchants of Oggok: The merchants in Oggok.
  • Merchants of Rivervale: The merchants of Rivervale.
  • Miners Guild 249 & 628: The miner's guild in Kaladim.
  • Minnions of Underfoot: The inhabitants of The Hole.
  • Miragul: The Erudite who found the lost art of Necromancy.
  • Mucktail Gnolls: Clan of Gnolls operating in Highpass Hold.
  • Nagafen: The red dragon, Lord Nagafen, that resides in Solusek B.
  • Najena: The dark elf that resides in Najena.
  • Neriak Ogres: The Ogres living the Neriak's Foreign Quarter.
  • Oggok Guards: The guards of Oggok and Innothule Swamp.
  • Oggok Residence: The ogres that reside in Oggok.
  • Opal Darkbriar: A resident of Freeport.
  • Othmir: A race of "Otters" living in Cobalt Scar.
  • Order of Three: The mage's guild in Qeynos.
  • Paebala: Rat clan in Dragon Necropolis (good clan).
  • Peacekeepers: The cleric's and paladin's guilds in Erudine.
  • Phingel Atropos: The ruler of Kedge Keep.
  • Pickclaw Goblins: The goblin clan in Highpass Hold.
  • Pirates of Gunthak: The pirates of the seas all around Norrath.
  • Priests of Nagafen: Followers of the dragon, Lord Nagafen.
  • Priests of Innoruuk: The cleric's guild in Neriak.
  • Priests of Life: The cleric's guild in Qeynos.
  • Priests of Marr: The cleric's guild in The Temple of Marr in Freeport.
  • Priests of Mischief: The cleric's guild in Rivervale.
  • Protectors of the Pine: The ranger's guild in Surefall Glade.
  • Protectors of Growth: The inhabitants of the Plane of Growth.
  • Queen Cristanos Thex: The queen of Neriak.
  • Ring of Scale: The dragons of Kunark.
  • Rogues of the White Rose: The rogue's guild in Halas.
  • Ry'Gorr Clan Snow Orcs: A clan of orcs living in the Eastern Wastes.
  • Sabertooths of Blackburrow: Clan of Gnolls operating in Blackburrow.
  • Sarnak Collective: The sarnaks of Kunark.
  • Scaled Mystics: The shaman's guild in Cabilis.
  • Scouts of Tunare: The rogue's guild in Kelethin.
  • Shadow Men: The Shadow Men found all over Norrath.
  • ShadowKnights of Nightkeep: The shadow knight's guild in Grobb.
  • Shamen of Justice: The shaman's guild in Halas.
  • Shamen of War: The shaman's guild of Oggok.
  • Shralok Orcs: Clan of orcs operating in Kithicor Forest and Highpass Hold.
  • Silent Fist Clan: The monk's guild in Qeynos.
  • Soldiers of Tunare: The druid's guild in Kelethin.
  • Solusek Mining Company: A group of gnomes living in Solusek A.
  • Song Weavers: The bard's guild of Kelethin.
  • The Spurned: The enchanter's, mage's and wizard's guilds of Neriak.
  • Steel Warriors: The warrior's guild in Qeynos.
  • Stone Hive Bixies: Clan of Bixies.
  • Storm Guard: The warrior's gulid in Kaladim.
  • Storm Reapers: The druids of Rivervale.
  • Swifttails: The monks' guild in Cabalis.
  • Temple of Solusek Ro: The inhabitants of the Temple of Solusek Ro.
  • Thunder Hooves: Centaurs found in the Karanas.
  • Trakanon: The dragon, Trakanon, in Sebilis.
  • Tunare's Scouts: The rogue's guild in Felwithe.
  • Tunarean Court: Tunares most blessed creatures in The Wakening Land.
  • Ulthork: Walrus type creatures living in various Velious zones.
  • Undead Frogloks of Guk: The undead frogloks found in Lower Guk.
  • Undead of Kithicor: The undead inhabitants of Kithicor Forest.
  • Unkempt Preservers: The "evil" druids in the Rathe Mountains.
  • Unrest Inhabitants: The inhabitants of the Estate of Unrest.
  • Venril Sathir: The lich lord, Venril Sathir, in Karnor's Castle.
  • Vox: The dragon, Lady Vox, found in Permafrost.
  • Wolves of the North: The warrior's guild in Halas.
  • Yelinak: Royal faction for the dragons of Skyshrine.
  • Zlandicar: Dragon in Dragon Necropolis.

If you have faction information, feel free to submit it for publishing.
