CT Event: Mordavin Telase
10/25/00

The Prize: The Qeynos Shield [Artifact Item]


Last evening, the fugitive Mordavin Telase was recaptured after being on the loose for nearly half a year.

Last year, a trial was held for Mordavin in Qeynos. He was tried and charged with many crimes against Qeynos and humanity. While the trial was still in session, with Rykonis Sw'Vaye just finishing taking the stand, hordes of undead started pouring out of the sewers and began assaulting the mass gathered at the hearing. In the confusion that went on, some undead were able to free the bonds on Mordavin's hands and then helped him to escape.

With the help of Surefall Rangers and a tip from Rykonis, we were able to determine that Mordavin spent his months traveling the plains of the Karanas. Good citizens of Qeynos, Surefall, High Hold, and even as far out as Freeport and Kelethin came to aid in the massive manhunt. Although the plains were scoured over again and again, no lead was produced until a half a week ago, when someone claimed to have sighted him in South Karana. Messengers went the distance and informed all available hunters to quickly head back to Qeynos and meet up and regroup. The posse was formed, headed out, and arrived in the plains of Southern Karana in only a couple days. Rangers and Druids were called upon to use their extrasensory tracking skills, and they lead the group, following tracks and sniffing scents. Initially having no luck in finding Mordavin, good reasoning told us to head to the bandit camps up north to see if he was still working with them. Found he was, and after a skirmish leaving all bandits dead, the party surrounded and arrested Mordavin. Mordavin gave struggle as he had previously, and the anger and rage of so many nearly caused the death of him, but the party backed off after being reminded by Magistrate Vegalys Keldrane that Mordavin was wanted alive, not dead.

Mordavin was escorted back to the Qeynos outpost overlooking the North-South Karana bridge, where he was then tossed into behind bars under the watchful eye of Captain Linarius. The people celebrated the end of five long months of searching, awards and praises were given out, and then many went on their own ways again. Along with the regular guards of the outpost, about a dozen hunters also remained there until a detachment of Qeynos guards would be able to make their journey here and bring Mordavin back to Qeynos.

- Sir Replay Promisekeeper
