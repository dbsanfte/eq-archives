Enchanter

The primary focus of the Enchanter’s art can be summed up by the name of the arcane order to which they belong: Enchanter. Their spells are crafted to enchant people, places, and things. This art allows them to charm beings and make them fight for their new master, or magically enhance the capabilities of a sword. They also have some skill in offensive spells, but are never as skilled as a Wizard.

Enchanters have some very special abilities which make them sought after by any seasoned adventurer. With their abilities to charm savage beasts and clear the minds of spellcasters they are always a necessary part of any group. Even with these great powers, Enchanters will rarely be seen alone, as their offensive spells are much weaker then their counterparts. One special ability which Enchanters share with none is their adept ability to change their appearance, this allows them access to many regions that others dare not go.
