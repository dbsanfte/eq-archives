::4-21-2001::
 

9.30 am PST by Tofe
Back Home!
I arrived back in Denmark on the 28th of March. I left home right away to go to two birthdays. My parents both turned 50. I am home now, waiting for my phone to be re-opened and for ADSL.

The Urge
Now that I am home, I must admit that I have had an urge to start playing again, but I must just remind myself that there are other things out there, that are more important right now. I will have to prioritise (sp?). I would like to play a little, but that is just not possible, in the sense that it will leave me behind in everything that I would do. Having played for about 8 hours a day has left a mark on my play-style. The only way that I would be able to play, will just cut my day, so that I will not have time for the things that are really important.

I still haven't been able to cut lose of EQ totally, so....well, who knows?

The Site?
Since I have stopped playing, my interest in the game as a whole has diminished of cause. This has left me with a choice wether or not to stop doing the site. As I dont have a hands-on knowledge of the cleric class, I am not sure that the "service" I will be able to give is as attractive to the player base, as it was before. Please post any thoughts on the message boards

Of cause if I do decide to play, I will continue doing the site.

The class!
Alot of changes has been made to the class ever since I stopped playing and went to SE Asia. Most of them have been big blows, leaving players despaired. Many have quit, and many are on the verge of quitting. Not just clerics, but all classes, as they are almost as affected by the changes, as the clerics are. I have seen players from otherwise extremely strong guilds quit. Coming from Mithaniel Marr, Afterlife is the best example I can bring up. To my knowledge no clerics from the guild have quit yet, but alot of other players have. This, for me, is a clear indication of my idea about the kind of players play clerics. Dedicated and helpful. no-give-up and optimistic. I must admit that I am not sure how bad it has gotten, but I think that I might have quit because of the latest if I had still been playing. Verant seems to have stooped to an all-time low. With no thought to the impact, they cut'n'slice. Nerf and renerf.

PR to counter immature decision-making?
Not long ago I got an email from the company who was resently appointed to do PR for EverQuest. They were assembling contact info from sites who are dedicated to EverQuest. I think it is pathetic, that Sony/VI have decided to "start" now. I wrote back that I was not sure in what way CoE could be involved in PR. I also described my stand on Sony/VI, and that I would need some sort of insentive, before I would do ANYTHING that could help out. Why should CoE do PR/endorse a company who acts with no regard to their player-base. I think that it shows an arrogant attitude. "Oh yeah let me see...you (Sony/VI) kill MY game and then ask me to help you out?" WHY.....WHY should I. I have not received a response, which to me, is another clear indication, that they are not ready for the open dialog with the players, that is needed to change thing around.

All they are doing is put out fires. It seems they do not really want to do anything about the problems with EQ. All the do is f%&! up and then try to do damage-control. In my opinion they act like infantile bullies. They have no direction and they have given up. The idea of what EQ was supposed to be, is so far from what it is today. Instead of taking time to sit down and really find out what should be do to make things better, they change things time and time again, leaving players, guides and GMs in despair.

The counter.
"My computer" has ended their free service, which means that the counter has been removed. It's not important, but I wanted you to know why it disappeared...
