EQ Quiz of The Week

How well do you know EverQuest? Here's your chance to prove to yourself and perhaps others your intense knowledge of the game. A new quiz will be available each and every Tuesday so be sure to check back weekly.

This Week's Quiz

Quiz 8: Shamen: Healing their tribes with secret herbs, chanting to their gods and looking to mystical spirits for their powers. Good and evil alike, these witch doctors protect themselves and their tribes with potions, spells and spirits of the bear, wolf and other animals. Spell casting isn't all they do however as they are fierce fighters as well ... how much do you know about these ancient and mysterious healers of the tundra?

Past Quizzes

Quiz 7: Paladins: Defending all that is good and true in the realms, these warriors gain special powers from the gods themselves! Much like a cleric, a paladin can heal and resurrect the dead at high levels but they can do much more then that. Their gods grant them many special abilities ... how much do you know about these valiant knights?

Quiz 6: Anniversary Edition: Time sure flies when you are having fun! It's hard to believe that we've all been in Norrath for so long. Many things have changed over the course of the last two years. Some of the changes brought leers from thousands of players but overall Verant has made many wonderful changes which have enhanced everyone's enjoyment in their world. Were Frogloks always called Frogloks? Well...yes. Okay that won't be on there. But there's a lot of other questions that will! So have a go at it and see how you fare.

Quiz 5: Druids: At one with their outdoor environment that some might think that they are indeed born from the earth or perhaps raised in the wilds. So attune with their surroundings that nature itself gives them the ability to change their forms and become one with the wild animals. You see them all the time .. but how much do you really know about nature's prophets?

Quiz 4: Monks: The masters of hand to hand combat.  The ninjas of EverQuest.  They need no armor or weapons to defeat their enemies and when the going gets tough they can lay down and pretend they are dead.  You might already know all of this .. but how much more is there to know?

Quiz 3: Rogues: The true sneaks of EverQuest. Rogues hide in the shadows, stalking their victims from afar, waiting for that perfect moment to strike. These masters of the deadly poisons perhaps strike fear in your mind and terror into your heart .. but how much do you really know about them?

Quiz 2: Mages: You see them running all around the place with their elemental protectors. If they are high enough level they might even teleport you somewhere quickly by summoning you to where they are. But how much do you really know about these summoners?

Quiz 1: Clerics: You've grouped with them. They heal you, they buff you and most importantly, they bring you back to life if you should perish to some foul beast. But how much do you really know about them? Take the test and find out.
