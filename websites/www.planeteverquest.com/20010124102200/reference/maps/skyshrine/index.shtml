Skyshrine


Skyshrine Zone
By Hop


Skyshrine Maze - Upper Level
By Yuegou Windwalker


Skyshrine Maze - Lower Level
By Yuegou Windwalker

Location: Skyshrine is a dungeon with two entrances: The Wakening Land and Cobalt Scar.

Continent: Velious

Creatures & NPCs: Drakes, Gargoyles, Grudash the Baker, Guardian Selbbep, Guardians, Ice Golems, Laoch Malijx, Lord Yelinik, Phoboplasm Cubes, Ruru the Cook, Sentries, Shambling Cubes, Whisperwind and Yelinak the Dragon.

Zone Type: Outdoor Zone

Factions: Unknown at this time

Level Range: 45 - 55

Miscellaneous:

  • You can not teleport in this zone.
  • You can only summon corpses from the entrance to the zone.
  • If your faction is high enough, there are many quests within the zone.
  • The Wakening Land zone's location is -200, -750.
  • The Cobalt Scar's zone location is unknown at this time, but you will need a key to enter. You can obtain this key by killing Whisperwind (or I'm told by completing a quest).
  • Past the Cobalt Scar zone entrance is the entrance to the maze inside the castle. This maze can be very confusing, check the maps which Yuegou Windwalker submitted for help.
