Official EverQuest chat with Sony Online Entertainment

February 7, 2001 4pm PST
RPGPlanet lobby in GameSpy Arcade

Over 85 people were in attendance

Sony Online Entertainment (Verant Interactive)

[SOE]Abashi - Gordon Wrinn, Associate Producer of the EQLive team and the Scars of Velious

[Admin]Cobby - Karen Cobb, GameSpy Online Events Coordinator
