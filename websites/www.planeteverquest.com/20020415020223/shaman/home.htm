Shaman spells effected with latest patch

- Shaman group buff spells (Tumultuous Strength and the Talisman spells - Cat, Brute, Rhino, and Raptor) have been improved to be more in line with the single target versions of those spells.

And also some more not-only-shaman changes:

- Improved the way that Abolish Disease/Poison and Counteract Disease/Poison work
- "Sense" spells should now work while on a horse
- Levitation spells now check to see if you have a second Levitation type spell when it wears off before hurling you to the ground
- Spell memorization speed should now take +Meditate% skill items into account
- Spell casters no longer need to see their spell book when meditating. Once a character has the meditation skill, it will function when the character is sitting down

-Fuudce Omenbringer

Magician Focus Items - What does it mean to Shaman?

Silver Choker: Saves mana on spells below level 50
Shiny Bauble Earring: Reduces agro on spells below level 45
Mantle: Increases Buff Duration on spells below level 50
Ring: Reduces casting time on spells below level 45
Bracelet: Icreases damage by 10% on spells below level 55

The Choker and the Mantle should be useful to shaman, and the Earing will be useful when you are doing alot of debuffs, just make sure you have a magician when you need one... :)

As many of you will know, due to class balancing issues, a type of spell known as an ancient spell has been added.
