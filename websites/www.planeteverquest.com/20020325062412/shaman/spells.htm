Welcome to the spell section, below is a list of shaman spells, with mana, what it does, and where to get it. Note that the locations are all for the Barbarians purchacing the spell. If I have put the location as 'Unknown' it means I am unsure of its location. If you have information on a spell I do not have here, or of a location which I do not have, please email me. Vision spells can be found in Oasis of Marr or normally Enchanter guilds.

A printable version of this guide is now avaliable here.

Spells in BOLD YELLOW are known as Ancient spells. They are only found in the highest level zones off the highest level mobs.

Spells in BLUE are from Shadows of Luclin. The location of these spells are not confirmed.
