<span class="headline">EQLive:  Backflagging Clarification</span><br>
<span class="byline">12/27/04, 11:34 PST | <a href="mailto:kwill@planeteq.com">Kwill</a></span><br><br>
<span class="newsitem">From Lyndro, in answer to a question from a player on the official boards about the new backflagging policy: Question: "I have killed every phase II god except Agnarr and would expect based on what Lyndro is saying that I should have access to earth, air and water. I hailed the bird outside air and was told to return when my will was strong enough. Do I have to kill Agnarr, which would mean I'm flagged for all except fire anyway, or am I missing something. I must say I am very disappointed that this isn't as useful for Elemental guilds trying to re-build numbers as it is for time and god guilds trying to re-build numbers." <blockquote>Answer:  I'm not sure what made you the impression that you should have access to Earth, Air, and Water.  This is a guild (where I say guild I'm talking about any sort of raiding organization) solution and not a personal solution (hence the raid component), everyone will still need to have killed Agnarr, Rallos, Sol Ro, Mith Marr, and Saryrn if their guild isn't capable of killing elemental gods, along with completing a quest.  If your guild isn't killing Agnarr and the Tier 3 gods (PoP was designed in tiers originially, I know its not the nomenclature that most of you are used to, but I have a hard time thinking of it any other way) anymore, in theory they are probably killing elemental gods.  This is a guild solution, so I can't really tell you how it works for you without knowing where your guild is.  In terms of this backflagging solution your guild is either: In Time, killing elemental gods, killing tier 3 gods, or working their way through the storyline for the first time (no backflagging solution for these folks).<p>
 
For the guilds with 20 - 30 EP flagged folks, they are going to have to take their new recruits to kill Rallos, Saryrn, Sol Ro, Agnarr, and Mith Marr.  Then the new recruits will have to quest to finish their elemental flags.  At which point they will have access to all the elemental planes.  I'm not sure how you can say this doesn't help... As a guild you are killing 5 gods instead of something like 9 gods, and 8 sub gods/events to get a single person flagged.</blockquote>

<span class="newsitem">EQ2 fell down and went boom, and now it's getting fixed.  The good news? Accelerated experience gain "early this coming week" and three free days of play.<blockquote>

An Important Message from John Smedley<p>
Hello Everyone, We sincerely apologize for the downtime this weekend. We had some hardware issues that caused us to have to tread very carefully in bringing the game back up. We wanted to triple check everything to make sure there wouldn't be any major problems, and it's taken a while to both fix the problem (we had to fly out engineers from one of our hardware vendors) and to bring each of the servers back online because of the careful validation process we need to go through. We have to do this very carefully and we hope you can understand our desire to do this right. We're trying to balance that with getting the game back up as quickly as possible. So far most servers are back up and the rest should be up in the next 4-6 hours.. hopefully less. I realize that ETA's that come and go can frustrate people.. so we do our best to give you the most realistic estimates possible. Out of respect for our players, we're doing the following: 1) Every player will receive 3 free days. 2) Early this week (Monday or Tuesday) we will have a 24 hour period where we will have accelerated XP gain. Details are still being worked out and we'll communicate this to you as we firm up the plans. We are committed to providing you with excellent service. Our team has been here for over 40 hours straight, and I can tell you our dedication to doing right by our players is the thing that's foremost on everyone's mind here at SOE.<p> Thank You, John Smedley President, Sony Online Entertainment</blockquote>
