Quiz 3: Rogues
 by Aracnakat | February 27, 2001



This test asks you all there is to know about the sneaky, hiding, backstabbing rogues.  How much do you know about them? 



1. How many races can elect to become rogues? a. 6
b. 7
c. 8
d. 9 2. According to Verant, what are the two primary statistics for rogues? a. Agility & Dexterity
b. Strength & Charisma
c. Dexterity & Strength
d. Agility & Stamina 3. At what level can a rogue first obtain the pick pocket skill? a. 1
b. 7
c. 14
d. 19 4. How many skill points are needed to successfully lock pick the door on the first level of Befallen? a. 1
b. 5
c. 9
d. 11 5. What race would a rogue need to be, in order to utilize a throwing boulder? a. Barbarian
b. Dark Elf
c. Dwarf
d. Halfling 6. What is the name of the rogue guild master in Rivervale? a. Shutter Silentstep
b. Heber Druneny
c. Gaffin Deeppockets
d. Lendel Deeppockets 7. Which of the following poisons ONLY works on undead? a. Feeblemind
b. Flesh Rot
c. Injected
d. Liquid Silver 8. What is the rogue guild in Neriak known as? a. Deep Muses
b. Dark Thieves
c. Ebon Mask
d. Indigo Brotherhood 9. What gem, needed for the Burning Rapier Quest, costs approximately 525 platinum pieces? a. Gem of Righteousness
b. Sparkling Sapphire
c. Magnificent Diamond
d. Gem of Deliverance 10. What is the name of the poison vender found in Dagnor's Cauldron? a. Conium Darkblade
b. Elona McMahon
c. Jerris W'Selo
d. Toxdil 11. What is the name of the rogue only armor found in The Plane of Hate? a. Indicolite
b. Ravenscale
c. Umbral
d. Woven Shadow 12. What is the maximum amount of skill points that a rogue can accumulate in the safe fall skill? a. 84
b. 94
c. 104
d. 114 13. What title does Verant give a rogue at level 55? a. Assassin
b. Blackguard
c. Rake
d. Strongarm 14. What is the name of the rogue epic weapon? a. Assassin's Edge
b. Deathbringer
c. Ragebringer
d. Ragefire 15. At what level do rogues initially get double backstabs? a. 55
b. 56
c. 57
d. 58 16.What bard and rogue only mask allows them to become an Iksar (illusion)? a. Guise of the Deceiver
b. Grotesque Mask
c. Iksar Hide Mask
d. Mask of the Lizard 17. Where could a rogue purchase ethereal suspensions? a. Firiona Vie & Thurgadim
b. The Overthere & Firiona Vie
c. Thurgadim & The Overthere
d. Any Rogue Vender 18. At what level do rogues gain the "Dead Eye" discipline? a. 53
b. 54
c. 55
d. 56 19. What is the effect called on the rogue only breastplate found on Trakanon? a. Rejuvenate
b. Enlighten
c. Summon Food
d. Invigorate 20. Which rogue discipline will hasten their attack speed? a. Blind Speed
b. Duelist
c. Kinesthetics
d. Nimble

That's it! You're Done!
