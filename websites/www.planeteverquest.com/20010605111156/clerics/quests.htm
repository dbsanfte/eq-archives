Aegis of Life

First of all I would just like to say that you should be willing to sit out a very boring camp when you embark on this quest. The Grimrot camp can be a real tough one. I didnt have the information when I camped it, that I have now unfortunatly. I will share with you what I know, and anything I somehow find relavent.
Go to South Karana. from the bridge head due south, and you will walk right into the camp. Be aware that Grimrot is a Shadow Knight and he will Harm Touch as his special kind of greeting. The Skellie form is a somewhat easy kill. Not very different from any other SK about that lvl. The human form on the other hand can be a real handful. He has very high magic resists, so the best thing is to melee him. I tried with a lvl 51 druid, and only about 75% of the spells cast on him did any real effect. However he does not have that many hit points, about 1700. There is a 3 hours and 15 min spawn time on the spot, and the PH is a standing putrid skeleton. If you get PH, just kill it right away. You do not have to kill anything in the camp, but since you are there, and you need faction to do the quest, you might as well kill some skellies. I have heard....and this is not confirmed by me, that you can check to see if your faction is good enough, by asking : "Who is Grimrot?" to Camlend Sebold. If he tells you about the quest then you should be ok to turn in the items. If your faction is not good enough he should not tell you about the quest.
When you have gotten both items and go to Qeynos to do the quest, be sure to that you give him the right Scythe (NoDrop).
Zones Involved NPCs Involved Items Involved
North Qeynos Camlend Serbold  
South Karana Dead Lord Grimrot (Level 30ish in skellie form) Decayed Heart (Lore/NoDrop)
  Live Lord Grimrot (Level 35ish in human form) Decayed Scythe (Lore/NoDrop)

 

Bloodforge Hammer

This is fast and easy if you have good faction with Stormguard. All you have to do is go to South Kaladim and kill Trumpy. He is easy, but if you try to kill Im in the bar you will be jumped. To get him out in the open you can either buy him some drinks and talk to him a bit until he mentions S'hrag. When you ask him about that, he will deny everything, and start walking. Follow him and kill him when there is noone around. Guards will help him, although he is not very liked in Qeynos. Take the head to Byzar Bloodforge in the warrior guild in South Kaladim. He will give you the Hammer if your faction is good enough. If you are a dwarf this should be no problem at all.
Zones Involved NPCs Involved Items Involved
South Kaladim Byzar Bloodforge
South Qeynos Trumpy Irontoe (Level 5, ROG, Dwarf) Dwarf Head

Bonethunder Staff

Runethar Hamest is concerned about the corrupt guards of Qeynos. He wants you to seek out Frenway Marrthank and ask him for "The bayle list" Along with the list he wants the Thunder Staff, which is the reward for the Order of Thunder quest. When you return these two items you will be rewarded with Bonethunder staff. There is a problem however. When you talk to Frenway in Surefall glade he will tell you that he doesnt have the list. His son had it, and he has been eaten by Mammoth (Grizzly Bear) Mammoth can be found in the cave behind the waterfall in Surefall glade, but Frenway can not kill the bear, as he is a druid. You will have to be careful when killing the bear, as all the druids in Surefall will kill you if they see you as you kill Mammoth. When you have killed Mammoth it drops a hide and Bayle's List I. Bring these two tiems to Frenway and he will in return give you Bayle's List II. To get the Thunder Staff, take a look at the quest Order of Thunder.
Zones Involved NPCs Involved Items Involved
North Qeynos Runethar Hamest
Surefall Glade Frenway Marthank Bayle's List II
  Mammoth (Grizzly Bear in Surefall) Bayle's List I
Mammoth's Hide

Paw of Opolla

To get the Paw of Opolla there are verious ways to go. I will give you the strieght and "easy" way. You need to get Four rings and a contrainer for them. Copper, Silver and Gold rings you get for turning in the tree items dropped by the Fungus Mutant in Upper Guk. I did the quest before the changes were made, so I can not tell you where the Fungus Mutant spawns. Turn in the items to Melixis for the rings. The Platinum Ring drop off of Gynok Moltor in Befallen. Gynok Moltar spawns right near the entrence of Befallen. He has verious placeholders, and it is a 15-17 min spawntime last time I was there. Wether or not it is his is "random" There are verious theories, and I will add some if I come across any that are worth trying out. My suggestion though is to go there in off peak hours. First time I did the quest I spent 7 hours there. That is the longest wait I have had, but I heard of people staying there for much longer. When you got all four rings (Make sure they have a +1 stat and a -1 stat) put them in the contrainer and press combine...Voila...

Regent Symbol of Innoruuk (About level 30)

You must have completed the two other Innoruuk Symbol quests to do this one
Start out by finding a blacksmith if you are not one yourself and get 2 steel bornings. Give them to Dran in Neriak Foreign Quarter, and he will give you a crate. Take the crate to Dragoon Szorn in Lesser Faydark. He should be around loc neg.900, pos.1150. When you give the crate to Szorn he will give you a receipt. Then go find Ennixy Frennor in Steamfront Mountains. She is a gnome located at about pos.1350, neg.850. Say 'Hate be my guide'. Ennixy will tell you to kill Crusader Swiftmoon who spawns near her. He drops a crate and his head. Take that crate back to Dragoon Szorn, and he will give you a Voucher. Travel back to Ithvol and give him The Voucher, The head, the receipt and your Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk and VOILA!! Bing bada boom...Your own personal Regent Symbol of Innoruuk.

He should then give you the Quest for th Militia Tunic. Talk to him about it and you should recieve a bag for 8 Cutthroat rings. You get those of Dervs in N. and S. Ro.
