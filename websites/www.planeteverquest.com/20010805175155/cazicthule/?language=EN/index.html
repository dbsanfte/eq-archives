    Jack Holt has just submitted a five part story to us titled, The Neriak Alliance.
    The forests of Faydark are relatively safe, but danger can spring suddenly from the quiet, damp, darkness. And so it did. Niffun Tuttle woke to a cry for help. He had been napping near what is commonly called the Orc Lift of Kelethin, and fortunately so. For a pair of the Orcs of Crushbone pursued there a wounded Ranger.

       Tuesday, July 31, 2001

    Sleeper Spawned
    7/31/01, 7:54 PST | Kwill

    Conquering one of the last frontiers of EQ, Blood of the Spider guild on the Rathe server killed Ventani, spawing the much feared Sleeper, the dragon Kerafyrm. Kerafyrm has been locked away for many ages in punishment, and is guarded by four Warder dragons. Killing the fourth warder, Ventani, spawns the Sleeper.

    This feat has been accomplished one other time, by the guild Conquest, which was subsequently disbanded for using exploits to kill the warder dragons. This time, Blood of the Spider was congratulated for their accomplishments.

    Once the sleeper spawns, havoc reigns in Velious. Kerafyrm is a little grumpy when he wakes up, and travels from zone to zone destroying everything in his path. His wake of destruction on the Rathe was only halted by crashing zones, which despawned him on the server. They report he has not reappeared back in Sleeper's Tomb.

    The awakening of the Sleeper has always been rumored to be an apocolyptic event for the world of Norrath, causing destruction not only in the immediate zones but involving all the dragons. This time, it sounds like it was contained to the Velious countryside--but who knows what might happen the next time he is awakened?
