Ohhh God...it feels like centuries ago...where to start. Well, I guess we need to decide what is and isnt a nerf. I'll just list all the goodies I can think of and let you all decide.

-Necro spell lineup changed shortly after final.
:charm removed
:umbral messenger removed

-Pets experience their first nerf. Possibly the first true nerf of the game.
:HPs of pets lowered
:max damage of pets lowered

-Dead Man Walking is changed to Dead Man Floating and given zone restrictions. Most of these zones where DMF is not usable are zones where it would be most useful.

-Screaming Terror is buggered with constantly over a 3 month period. Some would say its not a nerf, but actually it is, compared to what the original spell was supposed to do.

-Kiting nerf goes into effect. Mainly effecting druids due to their method of drag kiting, also effected Necros.

-Lifetaps are deeped too powerful and are placed on a level limit restriction. At the time nobody really knew just how this would effect high end play, but it seems to be quite a negative impact.

-Pets recieve second nerfing of their lives. Dagger delay buff is removed not even a week after Abashi and GZ proclaimed it a legal buff that Necros got as a perk.
(as a side note, this is when mana dump spells were introduced, forever cursing us to the life of a 5th rank support class)

-Feign Death is nerfed to prevent one, maybe two Necros in the whole game from FD camping. Where the Necro would park his pet in an aggro area and FD and, according to wild rumomr, go to bed and harvest exp all night long.(personally Ive never seen my pet tank anything that gave me good exp on its own, with no help from me at all on a consistant basis) Also charmed undead mobs are made to break charm soon as the Necro goes FD. Pets were made to suicide after 2 minutes of their master being FD.

-Pets are again nerfed. Proccing weapons such as Sword of Runes were proccing their special effects on all mobs. This being changed is not the main nerf, but could be considered one by some people. The real nerf was how they went about it. Dex of the pet was reduced to that of a player character of the same level, therefore reducing their ability to proc themselves or a weapon while Verant worked on a solution to the SoR problem. After being worked out and corrected, as far as I know, pets original stats regarding dex were never reinstated.


Here are a couple minor things that some people might not call nerfs, but they did effect us nonetheless.

-pets can no longer wield two 2h slashing weapons, such as scythes and axes. This never made the pet better so to speak, but added to the coolness factor.

-spears made primary hand only. Jobober can no longer dual wield spears for some unknown reason...

Thats all I can think of at the moment. Some of you may agree, some may not. Add more if you can think of anything I missed.
