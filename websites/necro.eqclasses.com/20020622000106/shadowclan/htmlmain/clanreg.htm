So you want to know more About Shadowclan?

Shadowclan was formed back in the Land of Brittanica (Ultima Online) many years ago. The Shadowclan Orcs still exist in those lands, but back when news of the Lands of Norrath began to leak over into the World of Brittanica (UO) some brave orcs left to explore this new land. When they arrived in Norrath they were reborn as creatures of evil... Dark Elves, Ogres, Trolls... then much later, even Iksar and now the Vah Shir. Shadowclan as a guild has existed in Norrath since the Legendary days of Beta 4. Though originally born into the land of Povar, Shadowclan made the trek to distant Xev where they reside to this date. Shadowclan has also invaded the lands of Diablo and Dark age of Camelot during the recent years. The hordes already prepare to invade the realms of Shadowbane once the portal to that land is found.

As for Shadowclan accepting new members right now... Shadowclan is always looking for like-minded players to join our ranks.  Below are some items that you should read that all members are expected to know and understand.

  • 1)  First and foremost, Shadowclan is a Roleplaying Guild... we act like what we choose to be in Norrath  For example:, if you choose to play an ogre, then you are expected to act and speak like an ogre.   This is the foundation of our guild and what we try to do best.

  • 2)  You should make every effort to frequent the Shadowclan Website and the Shadowclan Messageboard.  Here you will find news on changes to the clan, events, gatherings, auctions, etc.  You should feel free to speak up and post as well as read messages.
  • A few things you may wish to review on our Website first if you have not already:
  1. General Information = Which covers topics such as: Guild Purpose, Role-Playing, How You Can Help, Joining,Our Current Location, Rankings, OOC Leadership, and The Monthly Gathering.

  2. Roleplaying = Which covers topics such as: General Roleplay Info, Honor Code, Things Shadowclan does not do, Grouping, Shadowclan Loot Rules, Use of Anon & RP Filters, and the Parting of the ways.

  3. Character Creation = This page was setup to help with creating a character for Shadowclan if not using an existing character.

  4. Languages = Shadowclan speaks a variety of different roleplay languages in order to add spice and realism to the roleplay environment. You can check them here if interested.
