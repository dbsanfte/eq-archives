Purpose

To have fun and provide some fun for those that oppose us.  We have developed into a large group of multi-game online players that are dedicated to playing the evil monsters in the worlds we frequent.  We seek to expand our lands and to destroy the "evil" human and good presence.  By this, we hope to provide a little role-played rivalry between us and the good races.  Also read this page

Role-playing

We act and talk in character, as a monster would.   If any Shadowclan member does not follow at least basic role-playing she/he will be stripped of his/her clan respect (Rank and Title).  Also, if this behavior persists they will be driven from the clan and forced to roam the lands as a lone monster and to be treated as a Shadowclan enemy.  It is very important that these ideas be followed in order to gain support from the other non-monster players in our worlds. 

We don't tolerate Humie company for long so never think yourself a Shadowclan friend, even if we say you are :).  If you helped us, gave Booze or Food or talked to us in the past don't expect amnesty from Shadowclan hostilities.  As the old Monster saying goes,"Shadowclan have many bad hair days and short memory".

On Languages: The Website presents guidelines for how the typical member of each race speaks. The RP guidelines are just that guidelines, designed to assist you in Roleplaying your character. However, no cyber speak d00d, phat loots, pc, afk, woot, etc. is tolerated since it is not part of the roleplay world in Norrath. Aside from that make your character whatever you want, whether mute iksar or troll speaking ogre (haha a little humor). Note: Officers can address Any trends or behavior being deemed as abusive or counter-productive to overall RP within SC individually.

    Rankings/Positions

    Note:  New ranks and admittance for new members is done only at the time of the Gathering each month.  Those wishing to join Clan will have until the next Gathering to convince a Minion to sponsor them into the clan as a Brother/Sister.  Until then they are considered Recruits.   This means that you will hold a given rank or Recruit, or Brother/Sister, for at east 1 months time between Gathering.

    Recruit > Brother/Sister > Apprentice> Minion > Captains > Oracle > Shadowlord >Dark Cabal >Elders >Gods

    Member Ranks

    Recruit
    This is an individual that is petitioning to join Shadow Clan. They have few rights but also more freedoms than one would expect. Since they are probationary members, and not full members of the clan they are usually picked on and ordered around a little, but never so much that they would lose interest in joining the Clan. Recruits may not always be invited on trips or entitled to any of the loot, depending on the mood of those that they are with.  As a Recruit you must try to gain the trust and friendship of minions and such, they will be the ones that vouch for your worthiness to reach next rank.
    Eligibilty: All levels, evil races.
    Powers: None 
    Rights: May come and go as they please, but this may reflect badly on them though.
    Duties:  
    1. New Applicant applies via web site.
    2. They are added to the Recruit Roster List on the web site.
    3. They are guild invited by their race officer but do not have access to the private boards, only the public ones
    4. They have one month to find a mentor of Brother/Sister or Higher rank. If after a month has passed they cannot find a mentor they are booted. This makes them meet members to find a mentor.
    5. It is common practice to seek out mentors of the recruit's own profession and race, but is not necessary.
    6. When they have a mentor they are made an Apprentice. They need to contact the officers to get their title changed to Apprentice, to get them full board access, to move to their race's roster, and to make it known who their mentor is so it can be noted on the rosters as well.
    7. The change from Recruit top Apprentice can happen at anytime, one does not have to wait until the Gathering though it is acceptable to announce a choosing of an apprentice there.


    Apprentice
    These members have managed to acquire a Mentor and are no longer Recruits. They now must work with their mentor in order to obtain their Mentor's approval for promotion later. They must obey the orders of thos above them (within reason).

    Eligibilty: All levels, evil races. Must have been a Recruit before and have found a Mentor.
    Powers: None 
    Rights: Can ask their mentor for guidance.
    Duties:  
    1. They need to contact the officers to get their title changed to Apprentice, to get them full board access, to move to their race's roster, and to make it known who their mentor is so it can be noted on the rosters as well. (yes this is duplicate from Recruit duties in order to reinforce it's importance).
    2. Apprentice post's name, age and class on Scrubdom/Apprentice Forum.
    3. Apprentice are required to complete a task before becoming a true member. Your Mentor is responsible for your quest, not the captains. This provides better individual support and introductions to other clanmembers. The Mentor is required to post the quest given on the scrub/apprentice forum.
    4. The race's cabal or Elder's can alter the quest to maintain fairness overall. Once quest is given, the apprentice has two months to complete the quest.
    5. When quest is completed they are made Brother/Sister at the next gathering. Mentors are than free to take on another recruit.
    6. If quest is not completed in two months they are deguilded. Mentors are than free to take on another apprentice. .
    7. The change from Apprentice to Brother/Sister takes place at the next gathering after they have completed their tasks and the Mentor deems them ready.

    .

    Brother/Sister
    The Brother/Sister is the common rank in the clan once tril periods are completed. They are expected to follow the orders of there superiors and to do as they're told. Brother/Sisters that show that they are loyal to the clan will eventually be promoted to the rank of Minion. Advancement from here depends on your Roleplaying ability, dedication to the clan, and the Interaction with the clan.... Officers must vouch for your ability and worthiness to obtain next rank.
    Eligibilty: All levels, evil races, Must have been an Apprentice and have completed the apprentice quests first.
    Powers: None 
    Rights: Allowed to take on one apprentice at a time.

    Duties:  
    1. Actively seek out other Shadowclan members to group with and hunt together. This again reinforces bonds within the guild.
    2. Take on one apprentice and teach them the ways of Shadowclan. Can only have one apprentice at a time.
    3. Give your Apprentice their quests and post the quest given on the scrub/apprentice forum.
    4. Participate in clan events whenever possible, though not mandatory, your guiildmates will appreciate your participation and compan.
    5. Once a Brother / Sister had apprenticed at least three Recruits up to Brother/Sister rank they are then eligible to become Minions, though they will still need to gain the support of a current clan officer that will recommend their promotion at the Gathering..

    Minion
    Brother/Sisters that prove their loyalty to the Clan will be promoted to the rank of Minion. Minions are expected to fight and die for their leaders. Minions are expected to keep an eye on the Recruits and Brother/Sisters, and report any activity that catches their eye to the Officers, whether it involve those who should be favored, or those who should be tortured.  Only Minions can advance to become officers, and that is only as positions become available and a new officer needs to be chosen.
    Eligibilty: All levels, evil races, Must have Apprenticed at least three recruits to brother/sister status and have been recommended by an officer
    Powers    : May order Brother/Sisters to do what they wish (within reason), and may order Recruits around (be careful not to scare them off).  May organize groups within the clan. 
    Rights: Allowed to take on up to three Apprentices at a time. May join sub-groups within the clan without fear of being called a traitor to the clan (unless that is the group's reason for existence).

    Duties:  
    1. Actively seek out other Shadowclan members to group with and hunt together. This again reinforces bonds within the guild.
    2. Take on up to three Apprentices and teach them the ways of Shadowclan. Can only have up to a maximum of three Apprentice at a time.
    3. Attempt to do things for the benefit of the lower ranked clansmen when able (trips, hunts, events, pet classes, etc).
    4. Participate in clan events whenever possible, though not mandatory, your guiildmates will appreciate your participation and compan.


    Officer Ranks

    Captains
    These positions have many other nicknames, Recruit smackas, captuns, dark inquisitors, etc... seems a version is created which bears better meaning to the race of which the Captain is in charge of.  Each race has one captain (Dark Elves having 2 when member list becomes too unwieldy).  The Captains are in charge of traiing new Recruits for their race.  They guide, beat, teach, and evaluate the young ones to be certain they will meet the Clans standards.  If they fail in the eyes of the captains, then a Recruit is as good as troll food.  The Captains are not class specific so any class may become one.
    Eligibilty: All levels, evil races, Must have been a Minion and been appointed by current clan officers.
    Powers: All the powers of a Minion plus authority over minions.  Casts votes in clan policy.  These Positions are appointed by the Officers. 
    Rights: All the same rights as a minion.
    Duties:
    1. Meet and invite new members into the clan (guildtag them)
    2. Keep eyes open in their travels for potential new members and bring them into the fold as able or needed..
    3. Assist the Cabals/Elders as needed.
    4. Guide clansmen as they may need it. Assiting them to adapt to clan as able in addition to any possible Mentor support they may have.
    5. Watch over the Recruits and Apprentices as able to ensure they adhere to clan beliefs.
    6. Actively participate in discussions within the Dark Council for Clan Policy..

This artifact possesses strange and mysterious powers but is disguised as something that would not seem out of place in one's pack. He who possesses this artifact has great power, but is also in great danger. He who posses the artifact rarely has it for long, as it is the single most coveted item within the clan. Many will do anything to posses it...

    3. Must be Minion or Brother/Sister rank to hold the position.
    Powers: All the powers of a Minion plus authority over minions. Able to change his title to whatever he pleases.  Unique Position. Casts one of the votes in any vote on clan policy.
    Rights: All the same rights as a minion. Can not be Challenged for the Position until after two months of initially obtaining it.

Provide spiritual guidance for the clan by means of dreams, visions, scrying, tossing the bones, reading the cards, etc. This Guidance is what will motivate the clan "rpwise" to go out and do things in Norrath. Having a Dream of a horde of hopping frogluks charging the clans homelands is a sure sign that the Dark gods wish clan to go kill the Frogluks before they kill us *grin*.

    3. Be involved with all the members within their age group and all others as much as possible.

    Out of Character Leadership and Management of the Clan

    ShadowClan is not democratic. However, all its members are encouraged to participate in discussions of clan policy. Votes on clan policy will ultimately be made by the Elders, Dark Cabals, the Race Captains,  and the Oracle. Votes can be over-ridden by the 2 Clan Elders if they choose to do so and only if the Elders agree to do so. The Elders are their own balancing system since they must be in agreement in order to have any power. The Elders' positions are intended to be filled by individuals who "bump heads" often, so to speak. Their actions should always be motivated by the most basic beliefs of the founders of the clan.

    Policy discussion threads can be started by a Dark Cabal member or their designee in order to query the membership for different opinions and ideas. Votes will then be held by a Dark Cabal member when they feel discussion is over. If you have an issue you believe needs to be discussed, submit a request to your Dark Cabal member.

    Election of Dark Cabal - is accomplished by a vote of the clan members who are of that race. There is one Dark Cabal member per each of the four evil races... Tier'dal, Iksar, Ogre, and Troll. So, for example, if we need a new Ogre Dark Cabal member, then all Ogres of Minion status would get to vote on nominees approved by the current Cabal.  The vote will be posted by a current member of  Dark Cabal rank.

    Nominees for Dark Cabal positions can be sent to the current Cabal members. The Dark Cabal may interview the candidates if they wish, and will then pick those that they feel can best handle the position. The nominees approved by the Cabal will then be voted on.

    When an individual is elected to the Cabal and assumes an OOC leadership position, ANY and ALL other official IG leadership roles and titles will be removed. (With the exception of Clan Craftsmen titles since these are skill based).

    OOC leaders have the authority to make decisions on guild warfare, promotions and demotions, banning, humie guild relationships, looting, epic, raiding rules, general clan policy, inner guild disputes, external guild disputes, clan auction, the clan bank, and any other subjects which require OOC leadership and direction. They are also the OOC contacts for other players and groups... if you need to contact Shadowclan, contact members of the Dark Cabal. 

    Replacement of Dark Cabal - if the need should ever arise for whatever reason, there are 2 methods to remove a dark cabal.  The first, the members of a specific race may deem it necessary to oust their own Dark Cabal representative. This has never been attempted, therefore it can also be said that there have never been any survivors!   The second, The Clan leaders may take the issue to discussion amongst themselves an vote a Cabal out if necessary.  If this need should arise then we have faith that the Dark Gods will show us the way to accomplish it.

    The Gathering

    The gods have willed that once a Norrathian Year* a new Shadow Lord will rise to power from the ashes of the old. This prevents the clan from drifting away from the gods under the banner of a single powerful and charismatic leader. As such it is the will of the Gods that on or around the dawning of a Norrathian Year* a competition will be held to decide who is worthy to lead the clan. This takes place each month on the 15th day.

    The clansmen competing announce their desire to compete and from those hopefuls, the ones who will compete are selected by the Dark Cabal. They then compete in a series of one on one elimination battles, until only one remains standing... this is the New Shadowlord.

    The Shadow Lord competition is open to Warriors types only, no mage or priest may enter. It is feared that if a Dark Elf was allowed to compete he might rise to power through the use of trickery and underhanded tactics. If this happened he might not have the backing to lead the brutes and could instead lead the clan to civil war. Hence it is not common to see a Dark Elf in this battle. The competition is a brute force match although it's not uncommon for everything from throwing axes and poisons to make it into the battle. Shadowknights are known to use the powers of their god in the battle as well. The Shadow Lord is regarded by the Ogres and Trolls as the most powerful figure in the clan. He is to be feared and his every command to be followed to the letter. Rarely is he directly challenged. Dark Elves have been known to feign more respect then they actually have for him though, privately regarding him as a puppet. 

    From time to time figureheads of various titles among the mystics and priests have risen to power and become powerful advisers to the Shadow Lord. There is no set means for how this happens, nor does this always occur. Sometimes the figure comes to power as an adviser to the current Shadow Lord. At other times it has been known to occur when a weaker Shadow Lord comes to power shifting the balance more in favor of the Dark Elves. This Grand Mystic or High Prophet is most commonly a Dark Elf but has been known to be a shaman from time to time as well, depending on who the Shadow Lord favors. 

    (*Norrathian year equals a Real World Month, The Gathering is on the 15th of Each Month.)
