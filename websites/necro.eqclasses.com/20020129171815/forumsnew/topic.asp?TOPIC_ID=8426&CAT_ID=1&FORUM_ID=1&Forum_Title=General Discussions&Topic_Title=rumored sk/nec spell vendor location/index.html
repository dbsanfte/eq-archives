first things first.. the map in book is fubar.... dont use it much if at all.

In the northern portion of the zone ... you will find a long rectangular lake thing.... has nexus portals on platforms in middle (2). Shops surround the lake (doors on wall).

One of those doors opens up to a place that you would assume is a door shop.. no vendor... secret illusionary wall is in there though.

IF you go through it and past.. you will zone into echo caverns.

ONCE in echo caverns, move past guards and turn RIGHT... will lead to dead end stone wall.

CLICK IT... tada opens! Head in there.

Bunch of tent things with humans giving you the finger.. echo vendors I think they were called.

ONE tent way in back has a guy in funny robe.. HE is the necro spell vendor. last name deathdealer or vendor or some such.

Also, there are two buildings there.... necro, mage, wiz, chanter, rogue, warr, sk, etc Guildmasters can be found in them as well as a little gnome on a porch overlooking the cave is a banker... assuming it is an evil haven.. the majority were "kindly" towards me.

think the vendor had all lvl 49 and below spells for necros.

One of the most commonly held myths in EQ is that disease spells stop regen. I think this is because back when we were all level 4 (and no iksars existed) we cast disease cloud on ourselves and noticed that we stop getting HP back. So somebody decided that meant disease spells stop your regen and it became part of the games folklore. Nobody bothered to think that the spell just hapened to do the same amount of damage as you would have regenerated. If you are regening more than 2 HP a tick (because you are troll or iksar or a monster wiht a high regen rate) you regen just fine with a disease spell on.
