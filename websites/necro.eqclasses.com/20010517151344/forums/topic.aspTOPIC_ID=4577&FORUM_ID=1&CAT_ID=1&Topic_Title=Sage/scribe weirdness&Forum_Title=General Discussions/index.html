I feel your pain.

It took me almost 2 weeks of on and off camping to finally see my Cowl. I actually saw 12 Cinch Cords, which could have almost paid for a Cowl, but the Sage made it a personal vendetta of mine.

Nothing in Lower Guk has caused me more grief then that damn scribe. And even if you manage to live through a charm, that pet bug is a pain, as having to camp and recast a pet losses your rythmn when you are holding more then just that room.

In the end, I started to do exactly what I tend to do against healing MOBs. Turn up your particle density to full, and make sure your spell effects and detail settings are on and at the highest setting. Then, send pet first and watch the Scribe's hands. The instant you see any spell sparks, hit Screaming Terror, which should cast faster then his charm spell. The problem I found with just chain casting Screaming Terror is that he would sneak in a charm while it was refreshing. By watching his hands it is like you are in a wild-west showdown to see who can draw thier pistol first.

Of course, by doing this, the Scribe is not darked, so you will also need to have root memed. When he does start to run, cast root and back your pet off (so your pet does not break the root), then hit the Scribe with a darkness and stick your pet back on him.
