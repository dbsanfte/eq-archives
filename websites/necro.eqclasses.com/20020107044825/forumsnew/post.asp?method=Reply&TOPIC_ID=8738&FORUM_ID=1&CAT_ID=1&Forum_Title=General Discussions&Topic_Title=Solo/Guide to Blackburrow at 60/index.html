Dulanas Alot of tells i get while in blackburrow, soloing the gnoll commander room. They ask how i'm able to do it, etc. So, i figured i'd make a guide for you guys.

Spells memmed:
Harmshield, ST, Touch of Night, Dooming, Invoke Fear, Pyrocruor, Fiegn Death

Now first of note. This is a 5 spawn room. FIVE....hehe, so you can't just send in your spectre with 55 haste and hope he takes down the place. A 60 can single pull. So what you do first is once you first get in blackburrow. You have to make spectre, haste him, put manaskin on you, dmf, and Magi. Can take out Fiegn Death if you are feeling VERY brave.....and replace it with Shadowbond.

Root/dotting might work well with this area....but i used pet to tank. So slowly but surely, after hasting pet, you work your way down, all the way through blackburrow some into that room with the spikes on the floor.

(Note, do get here, you have to slowly work your way, shadowbonding pet and ToN'ing your HP back. Letting him tank will be getting him pounded on....but with your shadowbonds, he'll make it good enough. If you get any adds....which you most likely will. Use ST, and chain cast that to keep that one busy while hopefully your pet can solo the one he's on.

Now once you're in pit area.....and have cleared it hopefully. You pull a few more gnolls back to you that are in the way of the commander room. But once you have it clear...go up to room, and once you can barely see inside, dooming the nearest guy.....only he should come since you're 60 (59 and below still can't single pull here, so it's not worth it for 59 and below unless you're in full group)

So anyway, single pull should be coming towards you snared....now once at your pet, with mob snared....(oh, sorry, turn pet taunt off now). For awhile you have to run around the upper portions of the spikepit snare kiting this mob around as he chases you with spectre beating on him from behind. None of these summon, so you're good....once you work the gnoll down to about 50% hp, your pet gains enough taunt, that it turns on him. Immediately (cuz you'll be in demilich this whole time) turn around and ToN him twice (filling your HP bar), refresh dooming, and run far enough away from him.....and put pyrocruor on him. You then run around a little while longer, and land a couple more ToN's on him. He should be around 10%hp at this point, to where, if he's snared, he'll just sit there now....not moving, as your pet finishes him off.

You do this with all 5 spawns in that room, just that with commander, you have to kite him around more, refresh pyro twice, and ToN a little more....but keeping the taunt, and keeping snare on him is the key.

Experience was a constant 2 BLUES an HOUR!@$, it's awesome exp.

Loot is standard 2-12pp, but the common is peridots, rare gems, and a 12/20 rogue dagger (sell that bitch), and the rare is a "Soulweaver Mantle" that's better stats than the shawl quests. I don't have a SS of it, but i have one, and it's +15int, +75mana, +75HP AC15, +10 to Cold/Fire/Magic, and +8 to Poison/Disease.

It has some other stats, but those are the most important. Be safe
