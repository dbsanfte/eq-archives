Below is a comment I'm about to submit to the dev board. Please read and if you feel the same way please go to the dev board and write a similar letter expressing why you would like to keep the /facepick command.

*****************************************************************
Dear sir,

I've been a long playing EQ player since late beta and a big fan of the game. Though admittedly I do so less now then I used to Roleplaying has been an important part of how I play some of my characters.

After purchasing and running Luclin and getting to play with the new /facepick command I was VERY excited by this ability to be present in game. I knew that this is slated to be a tempory option and will later be available one time for old characters to select their new permanent faces but I couldn't help thinking of the RP oppurtunity that will be lost here.

By leaving the /facepick command in permanently you open the door for a whole new avenue of RPing. When I loaded my 55th erudite paladin in Luclin and started to arrange his face I began to think to myself that a new older looking face would be needed since that character had seen and done so much.

I looked through the various faces for ones that had grey eyebrows and more wizened and older looking faces. I kept the bald look since it is a sign of his devotion to Quellious. I added a greyish goatee that gave to me the look of an old wizened and hardened veteran of Quellious. I then gave him the calm ice blue eyes as a sign that he still had much spirit within him and wasn't some old fart to be pushed around.

The reason I share this change with you is cause if I had had a choice of face back when I was a newbie I would've picked the shaven young face I had before because it fit the character for that time in his life. But I LOVE the ability for my character to show his age as he advances.

Anyhow I really hope that you keep the /facepick command even if you restrict it to perhaps a 1 time a month thing so as to allow RPers to get things like haircuts and wrinkles and hair dye and eye changes (to reflect wisdom or malice that might have grown in a character).

This /facepick command is a GREAT tool for Roleplaying and for those that don't RP its another facet that keeps this game fun. Please keep it active.

Thank you for your time,
Telumehtar Ben'Kiriath, Knight of Quellious of the 55th sword of light, Wanderer of the Bristlebane server.
