Player Editorial from April 26th - Utnayan Bonewalker

 

I was in the DE city on the evil side - taking the shuttle from Oasis to the big boat... landing at the city of the dead <Dunno what its official name is :)> I was touring around when I ran into the shadow knight necro guild...

FYI: Probably the biggest couple of thngs for necros of 39th and 44th level - you can now buy your pet spells from an SK ogre <Forget his name> in the necro guild. So they are no longer research only :) the 39th pet sells for approx 44pp. 44th pet around 60 or so.

There is a quest on the DIRECT opposite of the ogre SK that if you give her 4 scrolls, she will give you a spell that you cannot buy or research. I do not know if this is for necros, but I am assuming so :)

It was a pretty awesome place! There are undead skeletons that pop off their heads, and are lned up against a wall following orders from a captain :) I also noticed that anyone good is KOS if they take the boat from Oasis. And vice versa - so necros will not want to take the shuttles from felwithe and hop on the titanic of ships <Although I will probably log on with my pally to see that boat - I hear it is big>

The ship we take after the shuttle is HUGE. When you get to the main ship <After taking the shuttle> you will come to the dock area... About Midway, a lvl 51 SK and I found a small boat that tilts up - it looks like it will open something... While he was doing it I was searching for something it might open - but did not see anything.

Along the way you come across the ship graveyard. Occupied by bandits... Which could be good for lower level necros.

I than gated back to oasis and caught a GG from Sro to the dreadlands. I began fighting with a lvl 40 druid - at 38 - most of the creatures were blue. tundra Yetis were pretty easy to blow away, as well as the mountain giants. However, if you go through a snow tunnel, you come out on the other side... Stick on the left wall and you come to a forest <it starts to really slow down here if you have full clip - so unless you have a fast computer I would recommend turning clipping down> on the side closest to the burning forest zone, There are lvl 39 mobs - that hit VERY hard and fast. I will write the names of all the creatures and get them to you tomorrow - but at full mana I was heavily resisted with VOS. I dunno if this is common for all creatures, but a lot of mobs in dreadland resisted poison... I was forced to use Boil blood, and fear, while my druid partner would snare and DD, we could not take down a lvl 39 mob - My VOS at this point was resisted 4 times in a row even when it was down to half health. Again I will get the mobs name :)

I ventured into Fironia last night for the first time, the guks on the left side of the zone see right through your invis, and hit hard and run fast. I had my Jboots on and they were catching up unless I jumped. In the meantime of frantically trying to zone, <Never did find it> I ran across a Scout with a dog pet called Dune. At 39 he conned red, his pet was blue though. <Was a real dog>. And was just a normal guard - this could be very excellent XP for a higher level necro to camp - unfortunately I cannot time his spawn - but he is placed JUST by the waterline...

On the otherside of the dreadlands, I ran into some Spidercamps. Along the zone - basically if you come out the tunnel from the druid gate, and follow the right wall, you will hit these camps. About 4-5 spiders per piece all conning blue. So you might want to group up with 1-2 people. I am going to do that tonight and see how the xp is :)

There is an Axe that drops off of one - 45 Dmg and 150 delay! Heh so a pretty crappy axe by delay standards - but it has nice damage.
