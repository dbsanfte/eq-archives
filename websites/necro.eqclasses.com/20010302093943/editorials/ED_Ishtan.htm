Player Submitted Editorial

 

 

Mobs from 1 to 30ish

Player Editorial from December 29th, 1999 - Ishtan

 

1- For necromancers I have found it to be easier to do level one creatures  first. Allowing the pet to tank some while casting.

 

2-Good idea to do fire beetles and spiders. I have found it easier to travel outside of Nektulos into East Commons because of the massive aggro train that forms up on you.

 

3-Best to do Shadow Wolves, Black Bears, while still doing Fire Beetles and level 2-3 Spiderlings.  Also Large Spiders are good for this level.

 

4-Things start to change here.  With the introduction of Fear, you can DoT, Fear and pet+tap the mob while taking little combat damage.  You might be able to do Lionesses if they are of a similar level.  It will take some practice and a few close calls or even death, but its worth the XP.

 

5-7-SKELETONS!  Absolutely do Skeletons in Nektulos!  They are great XP while stocking up on bone chips at the same time.  Also it might be wise to do them at 4, while taking precaution, and grouping would be very wise.

 

8-10-Orc Centurions and Legionaries in many different places.  Orcs are everywhere.  East Commons is a great place to hunt them.

 

11-Definite hell level.  I did a Crocodile in Guk that is from the entrance, down the hall and to the right, there is some croc that spawns there that gave good XP.  Also, some Dervish groups.

 

12-14-Dervishes for sure, West Commons is where I liked to hunt them.  You can either  solo or group hunt them.

 

15-17-Orc Warriors in Oasis worked great.  The Orc highway is a great place to hunt them, like room service.

 

18-Deep Water Caimans are easy with all the dots and fear at your disposal.

 

19-20-Deep water goblin pets in group is a good idea, good XP, no loot though

 

21-31-TREANTS!  Treants are one of the best mobs I have seen, but its not always easy to get in the group.  They drop a considerable amount of money but usually in small change :(.  They give great exp though, I have seen a level 30 Necromancer get out of the 30 hell level with them.

 

20-24-Giant Skeletons.  Rathe mountains, they spawn near the Lake Rathe zone and I am sure in other places.  They are usually around 21-26.  You may want to group with Clerics, Paladins or fellow Necromancers or, just even a good old Wizard or Mage for some good DD to finish of these great XP mobs.  They also drop the Skeletal Finger and Toe for the Boot and Gloves quest for Necromancers.
