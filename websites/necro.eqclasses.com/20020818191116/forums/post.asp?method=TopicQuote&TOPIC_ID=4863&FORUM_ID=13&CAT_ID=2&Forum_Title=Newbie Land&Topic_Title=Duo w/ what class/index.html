Dunno how these would work out for playing on a second account, but...

  • Rogue:
    Rogues are great partners for Fearing necromancers. You keep your poor unfortunate victim running away crying and they stick pieces of sharp metal in 'em as they run. Since you can heal, you don't really need another healer for this. (I partner with a Warrior like this from time to time and can keep him healed up nicely. A Rogue should be pretty much the same, but with a higher damage output.)

  • Beastlord:
    I hunted Sun Revenants with a Beastlord partner recently and was quite impressed. The Beastlord pet is a monster and with the new EXP rules, you don't have to leash it, to do over half damage. Beastlords have some buffs, but I don't know exactly what they are. (The only one my partner cast on me was Spirit of Wolf.) The damage output from a Rogue pet, a Warder and a Beastlord is horrendous! (In a good way.) Plus, the high level Beastlord clarity stacks with Lich. Another little bonus, but it might take a while to get to that point.

  • Mage:
    Like Beastlords, Mages have monster pets. Plus, they can summon focus items! (Not really sure how useful this is with the permanent foci now available.) And, they can debuff, which should be handy for those highly resistant mobs. Not to mention that they can nuke. But that might not be too vital, with two pets ripping a Feared mob open. Only thing is, (And I don't really know about this, since I haven't played a Mage,) it might be kinda a full time job to keep everything under control.

I do wonder sometimes though, am I the only person left playing one account on one PC? *Grin*
