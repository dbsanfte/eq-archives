Hell Level Hunting

Player Editorial from April 28th - Utnayan

 

Everyone knows about hell levels. 30, 35, 40 and the infamous 45. But where are the best places to hunt to make sure hell isn't as bad as it can be?

Lets start with 30. As Necros, we one thing going. We can always attempt to solo higher con mobs and get better XP to make sure we can get through the hells in a reasonable amount of time.

I have some ideas on how to make leveling through hells a little easier, for players that like to group as well as stick it out on their own.

 

Level 30 - your first hell level. most usable spells: Dooming Darkness, Boil Blood, Lifetap, Poison Shock, Fear, and of course, your pet.

At level 30 you have a very weak and often resisted fear spell <Lvl 4> and your lvl 29 pet cannot take the amount of damage that a lvl 34 can, or give for that matter. You will want to be careful at Hell level 30. These are some of the best possible places to hunt to get out of hell lvl 30.

* SOLO * South Karana: Bard Camp <Well>

Vhalen Nostros hits pretty hard - he is an even con at lvl 30, and sometimes you will find yourself in trouble, but the experience is astounding. Just to be safe, you might want to mem engulfing darkness because of the possible resists and slow casting times of Dooming. Once Engulfing Darkness is stuck, send your pet, and fear. After you have feared, Boil Blood and lifetap and DD with poison shock as needed. You might use all of your mana, but you have 7 minutes for the re-pop - So allure death, and med and heal your pet if needed. At times, you might die here at lvl 30, so the risk is moderate - but well worth the xp to get you out of hell.

* SOLO * South Karana - Treants

Trees :) Hmm... Two spawns every 5 minutes. You will need a partner but do not group. Get another person of the same level who can solo treants effectively and you each take a spawn. A very easy kill is to engulf, send pet, and DD or lifetap. Watch out for the dots, you might want to keep cancel magic memmed - and keep your major shielding off. I actually grouped here with a lvl 32 shaman at 32 - and we hit 34 in 12 hours. So the xp is very good here until then. For a hell level though - stay out of being in a group and solo the one pop. Especially if you are both in hell lvl. I don't know why - but the xp is better if you are not grouped.

* GROUP * Unrest - Altar room/Ghost Room

Although there are usually lists <Hopefully with Rok there will not be anymore> getting into one of these two groups is GREAT experience in a hell level with minimal risk of dying. A good altar group can pull from Blood pool as well as the stairs to the bookcase and it will make for zero downtime. Use your normal group strategy here, DD with lifetap when needed or use poison shock on live creatures, with your Undead nuke on the undead. You should not die here if you are in a good group.

* GROUP * Tool Booth Guards

Guard Colin is usually the weaker spawn. You will want to split the spawn so you can conjuntively work together. To split the spawn fear one of the guards and send both your pets on the other. Fear Colin if you can since he is 1 lvl weaker. have one BB, and one heart flutter to bring down the AC so the pets can do more damage and fear and nuke. By the time he is dead you should have enough mana left between the two of you to kill the 2nd with the same strategy.

These are four of the most popular and should get you out of hell 30 in pretty sweet time. It is your first hell lvl - taking twice the normal XP to get out of. It will take getting used to not seeing your XP bar move.

When you level to 31 do not die. You will lose approx one to 1 and a half bubs ot xp her death. Due to the nature of trains, try not to hunt in dungeons notorius for trains. As of now - the UZ bug was fixed in Cazic - Cazic would be a bad place for lvl 31 players. Unrest is pretty safe if you must hunt in a dungeon. If you can, keep to the same spawns till you get out of 31. If you do die, beg for a rez - because you will lose about 2-3 hours of XP in one death.

 

Level 35 - takes 3 times the normal amount of XP needed to level. At this level though you have some spells that will help you out a great deal more than 30. Invoke fear <Finally an upgrade!>, Venom of Snake 540 DOT in 30-45 seconds, a pet that can tank and DW and double attack for a max of 33 damage, greater shielding, root, we get it all at 34 :)

* GROUP * Troll guards in Rathe <TOWER>

Yeah I know... Before I get ripped for being a necro and killing trolls, I can tell you that after 34 in my opinion, the city of grobb is useless for a necro. With the addition of Kunark and The Overthere, you should not need to enter this city again <I HOPE!> But if you can team up with 1-2 other people, and kill both trolls <One SK and another Shaman> you can level out of 35 very quick - took me 4 days about 4-5 hours a piece here in a group with a 36th druid and a 35th enchanter>. Watch out for the SK's Harm touch, especially if you are using call of bones <Your upgrade to Allure>.. He can harm touch you for a big chuck of HP and if you pull for the group with darkness, he will hit you with it. Send your pet first then pull with Dooming. It wll slow him to a walk - then use VOS and nail a fear on him and have the other members in your group use their abilities. Do not bother using VOS on the shaman, because she has a LOT fewer hit points and if you have a wiz or a mage in the group, she will be dead before 20 secs. Try and get the shaman on the pop - otherwise she will summon an annoying little wolf pet and cons green most of the time at 35.

* SOLO * Guards Toll Booth

You will want to spread the spawn here, but you can easily control the spawn. Fear Valon, and send pet after Colin, than cast dooming darkness, fear, VOS and boil blood and med. Right when the fear will wear off, colin should die, and your pet should be almost ful health still. At this point you should nbe about 45-50%. and still have COB on. Valon should be on his way back by now - hopefully someone didn't KS him - but if he returns, do the same as to him as to Valon. After Valon died, med up and heal pet - the spawn between the two guards should be about 1 to 2 mins apart... more than enough time to pull one without the other being spawned yet. Great XP at 35 - and you could get out of hell in about 15-20 hours of gameplay - possible sooner. Only prob is - the newbies get upset - so if you have a kind heart you will want to help out the occasional "KODIAK to the TOLL!!" shout.

* SOLO * Cyclops Rathe

At this point soloing cycs give you tremendous XP - the only prob is finding enough and having to go through the killing of the placeholders. Lizzies, bears, etc. Easy to do. Darkness, send pet, fear<if you do not see any around> and VOS and DD it. Lifetap to regain health. You will not get out of 35 super fast here if you do this - but it is pretty safe with the exception of Tarskuk running wild on you. A named Cyclops with SOW. I would say you would get out of hell 35 here in 3-4 days playing 5-8 hours a day. without death.

* GROUP * West Common Druids

2 Druids spawn here right by the gate. the great thing is, Slea Pinewhisper is also here but never gets mad at you for killing her druid companions. She is a beta neutral NPC and you can sell to her. team up with another necro, and fear one <They can heal so watch out> and nuke the other one quick to space out the spawn. Darkness, to slow it down, send pet, fear, and DD it with PS or lifetap... By the time the first druid is dead, the 2nd will come back soon and use the same Method. ** If you notice one healing - Cast VOS on it as well to make sure she doesn't out heal the damage by DD's and is only recovering a fraction of the VOS Dot by healing ** - Great loot here too - and once you get bogged down just go sell right at the gate :)) Awesome XP and bot bad loot as well - FS almost every drop and also 1-4 pp per loot :) If mana allows - go nuts and kill an occasional griff or HG in the area.

^^^ Same as above except at the druid ring in East commons ^^^ Same tactic. except this time, when you need to sell - go to the hut directly behind you - and make sure turn your self into a skellie :) With COB you con so you can sell to her :)

Level 36 - make sure you try and stay at one of these spawn points - very good XP throughout - again - the same as mentioned with level 31, try and stay out of the bad dungeons. Unrest starts to get too easy at 36 and doesn't give the best XP as stuff starts to con green in altar and ghost. Cazic with the UZ bug being fixed is not a good place. Unless you know you are in a decent group - I wouldn't bother.. A good place with seasoned group members from what I have seen if you need to get into a dungeon, is the Bed room in lower guk. Good xp and you should be outta level 36 in no time and pretty safe as long as you stay out of the way of the higher level trains.
