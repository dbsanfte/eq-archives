Kedge Keep, Underwater Dungeon

As you all know by now, Kedge Keep was itemized recently and is starting to get some traffic. I personally explored it before it was itemized. Was a great place to earn experience at high levels. The fact that it was not itemized did not mean that there were no creatures. The Link below is to a screenshot page on my guild's site. The page contains various pics of Kedge Keep and the friendly fish down there. This is a well made and "pretty" dungeon to explore. Did have a few pathing problems to deal with but for the most part my pet and I swam all over with little trouble. 

Kedge Keep screenshots

Now here is where old Lifebane gets very annoyed. The spell that necromancers have been given that is supposed to make us "welcome" in groups was Dead Man Floating (a nice buff we can actually cast on self AND others). Well, amongst the spells effects is water breathing. After last patch, when Kedge was itemized, Dead Man Floating can not be cast from within Kedge Keep anymore. So they have basically made Kedge Keep anti-Necromancer since normally this spell can be cast anywhere else you care to, above or underwater... except in Kedge Keep. 

I have been petitioning and sending feedback every day about this and would recommend that you all do the same.... this was of course an unannounced "nerf" on Verant's part since no mention of it was ever made in patch messages. Be heard and boycott Kedge until such time as Verant "fixes" it so that Necromancers can use their "group" spell there again.
