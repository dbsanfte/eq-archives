Bind yourself in the Bazaar. I am an Iksar Necro and basically everyone in SoL likes me. The Bazaar is 1 zone away from free teleports to every continent, it has a banker, it has vendors for every trade skill, it has a vendor for component casting spells (Yes, including small and large coffins). It's PvP free (big bonus on PvP servers), and you'll be able to sell items with the /vendor command when its done. Basically you can go afk for WEEKS if you want here and not be molested, even on PvP servers.

Basically, no one should have to bind ANYWHERE other than Bazaar ever again no matter what race/class you are.

BIG thumbs up on this zone, it is THE hub of all of EQ now. Truely a great way to get everyone together and trade. I dare say its one of the BEST things they've ever added to this game.
