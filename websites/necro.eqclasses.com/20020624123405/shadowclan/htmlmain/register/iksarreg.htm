Shadowclan Registration
Registering with EQ Shadowclan is a 2 step process. 

  • 1)  You must first create or have your character on the Xev Server.  get your exact character name, then proceed to next step. 

  • 2)  Fill out the form below, and sumbit it.

 If Your browser has any difficulty submitting this form then go ahead and email lifebane38@hotmail.com with your character name, class, level, aol im name, icq#, and trade skill if any.  

 

Once you have finished registering............

     There will be an Email reply to your registration, and then you will need to contact your races Leaders to be added to the roster.  You will be added to the guild on a trial basis for 2 weeks.  If during these 2 weeks you group with Shadowclan members and prove yourself as being a Roleplayer and a good addition then your membership will be Final.

     Go register on the messageboards and read through the various different forums.  Under the Scrubdom Forum, new scrubs are requried to post a thread with their character name & race as the subject heading. Then they will write a short blurb about their character either in role-play fashion (preferably) or not - doesn't matter at this point - this will promote use of the  boards for clan communication purposes.  NOTE:  This Posting on the Scrubdom Boards is REQUIRED to get permanent status in Shadowclan.

     During this Trial period you should be making new friends within the guild and proving yourself and your roleplay ability.   And again, visit the webpage and messageboards frequently to stay current on events and happenings.
