How to get a Gnome Necromancer to level 16 in three days

Player Editorial from December 16th 1999 - Nark

 

Okay, Well first of all I started out with a gnome Necromancer. I wanted the ability to tinker. Second I also Wanted the ability to start out in Greater Faydark. It is the BEST hunting grounds for New players. A bit dangerous but everyone dies a lot when they are young so get over it, then get over to your corpse.

 

At level one you can get about one half of a bubble a kill with bats. decaying skeletons, and giant wasps. That will get you to level 2 in just under 30 minutes. (Some people say kill decaying skeletons but I would rather buy my bone chips. There are better ways to get cash like running the bard note from Ak'Anon to Kelethin 5 gold a pop and since it is your hometown you will be running there Quite a bit until you can gate.) Second level you can either Stay on decaying skeletons (for the bone chips) or you may move on to orc pawns. Remember! they hit hard so don't let your pet tank for too long. Under level 16 think of your pet as another DoT just whacking away at the monster's life. also Try to have a mage summon you a Dagger They are quite nice for the pets and I seem to think That pets hit more often with a weapon.

 

Okay Level 2! you are finally ridding the orcs of the world and Working on that god awful faction of yours. Go to the pile of stones and kill the wandering Orc pawns. They are a bit scarce but Worth the wait. in the mean time Cast a DoT on a wasp and let your pet kill it off while you stay Full mana.  The orc pawns will get you to level 3 in no time. Here is where it stops getting easy. now you have 2 choices. you can go somewhere else that is less dangerous or you can follow what I say, but remember you die A LOT but you don't loose exp till 4th so don't sweat it.

 

Level 3! Now you can go find wandering orc Centurions Their level Varies from 3 all the way to 16 or 17 (those are inside CrushBone) outside CB they will get all the way up to level 7. So you can go kill the wandering orc Centurions or you can prove how absolutely insane you are and go on over to the orc hill. (Its great Roleplaying too! people Will be level 11 and 12 hunting at the hill and you will be a mere 3 necro bent on power.)  okay, Don't go charging on in just yet friend. the orc hill usually has people on it. without it being camped this wont work. On the outskirts of the camp there are little orc Centurions that will con Yellow and even to you. Pick them off one at a time they will give you VERY nice Exp. do this until you have reached level 5 or 6.

 

Level 6! Well by now you have gate and a new round of DoT's some Much more powerful like one of my FAVORITE the darkness series. It doesn't do INSANE damage as some of your DoT's will but it slows target movement to a walk which is CRUCIAL to get out of those Sticky spots until you get to the zone. Now go to Butcher Block mountains. this is IDEAL hunting grounds. You will want to be killing Giant bats, Giant snakes, Etc... If you Really think you have the Feel of a necro you can Take on those Red Giant Scarabs. I took them on at 6 and 7 and only died a few times when my spells were resisted more than 3 times. this will get you to level 8 in NO time!

 

Well, level 8 you are flying now aren't you! Go buy your new spells they will be very very nice. your pet starts to dish out some Nice Damage, you get a pet heal which will save your Hide, and DARK PACT! my favorite series of spells. Changes Hp: Mana at an unknown rate to me. also I never used Vampiric Embrace I HATED it, I used my level 4 lifetap till I got to 12. Okay Now it is time to Say goodbye to This New land and head over to North Ro! you should try to Solo dervs while people are grouping to kill them. This will get you to level 12 (If you find Dervs too hard you can go to easier things in Ro until you find that dervs aren't so tough anymore)

 

Level 12! Congrats Bind affinity no more running forever to your corpse (that is if you even die anymore). Get RIGHT over to Oasis! NOW NOW NOW NOW... here is where it gets tricky..... you need to hunt Crocs and Caimans. These are EXCELLENT experience. Remember once again your pet cant tank too long. this is how you should fight these guys (as you should fight most anything in an open arena) darkness, (To slow to a walk) then Heat blood, Then you want to Fear right away your pet is almost dead by now.... if It resists the first fear get into the Melee and try to cast during combat. If you Cant fear Try to throw in a Poison bolt to Get your conjure level up. At level 14 or 13 you can Start killing Deep water Caimans, if you can find them.  That will take you to 16 in no time.

 

Whelp there it is in detail! I am off to lake Rathe now I plan to make either 18 or 19 Today..... And remember not 3 Real time days I have 3 played time days. And Thanks for having such a GREAT website. I also Wanted to throw in That This is ONLY for people who have other characters. If you do not know where these places are and Have not Built up another character PLEASE I mean PLEASE enjoy the World. there is SO much to do I have done just about every Quest and participate in a lot of RP in addition to being a GM of a guild. So don't Just try to get high fast. Learn about Norrath First... nobody can Skip being a newbie.
