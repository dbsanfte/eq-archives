Teamwork is part of Everquest life whether your a die hard soloist or a social animal, at some point in your career you are going to have to team up with other players. The question is, are you grouping because you want to or are you grouping purely out of self interest? Perhaps that isn't even the right question, although it is one that needs addressing- perhaps the question is, why group at all?

Why not just wait till your tuff enough to fight whatever it is you want to kill on your own? Why share the loot and experience with others when you don't have to? It might seem to the melee types that they don't need to team.Well the reason I decided to tackle this subject and hopefully provide some of the answers is because I enjoy playing in teams.

When I first started these pages I had an excellent team of friends who I fought with regularly, but on the odd occasions I had to group with strangers, it seemd that regardless of level - there were those who were naturals and those who weren't, and those who weren't often end up getting themselves or worse still the rest of us killed. These days I'm fighting more and more with people I dont know- and making a lot of new freinds, but the problem still exisits- even at my now guidy high of Lvl 38, some people STILL dont have a clue-- heh- but not many thank god!

Soo, working on the premise that you are never too old to learn, I hope that anyone- regardless of level will get something from these tips, which I hasten to add are not all mine, without imput from your letters and my friends this page concept would not work
