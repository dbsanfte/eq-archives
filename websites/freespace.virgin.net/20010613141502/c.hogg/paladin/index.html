Paladin Guide For the Paladins of the Poison Elves
     
 

This is a total Doozey of a quest, - for a précis of the whole thing got to this link, it is where I got most of this information from. http://everquest.allakhazam.com/ and follow the quest links to The Rathe Mountains Armor of Ro. However why bother as ive laid it all out neatly for you:)?

Basicly there are 3 parts to the quest, Verant have mad it a bit easier by putting all of the paladins you need to talk to in one place as opposed to thier seperate temples (gee thanks guys) however its still a long long quest.

So the basics are:-

1)You go to the paladins in Rathe mountains talk to them- they tell you what you need to bring them for them to give you the mould.

2)You go to the temple of Soulsec Ro and see the Dwarf who will give you a Cauldren to make Ronium.

3) you collect all the items you need, get the moulds, make the ronium and find a forge to make the Armour.

Note- According to the guides ive looked at the consensus seems to be that you no longer have to be a master smith to make the armour or Ronium, the consensus is that a skill of 50 will usually work and 100 almost always, but after all this work im aiming to get it to 100 at least before I start making things.

There! Simple! NOTTTTT!

Before you can do any of this you need to raise All the main paladin factions, most to aimiable two to warm before any of them will even speak to you.

So Ive decided to split this guide into 3 parts, I have put factions first as I personally feel this can be worked on early as opposed to having to spend a whole weekend bored silly catching up:)

 

So this is what we will all need, I think Glasgoss is way ahead of all of us but at level 40 youd expect that, so basicly the plan is to start collecting bits as wecome across them, work on faction and then maybe set up organised hunts with the guilds help for specific harder items.I hope this referance is usefull, as Abner sais "It's good to have a goal!"
