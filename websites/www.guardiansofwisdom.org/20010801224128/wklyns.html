Events

Last night’s trip to runnyeye was a lot of fun. I wanna thank everyone for coming. Large group outings are almost never powerlevel nights due to the amount of time spent coordinating etc, but I think they are much more fun than sittin in a spot solo for 10 days or camping some uber camped place. We had some difficulties early in the night (which I and I think others suspected we would) but as the night wore on, we got more organized and things smoothed out considerably.

 

I think the most important thing gained last night was experience grouping not just with each other but in a multiple group situation, and I think this will pay off big in the long run. In the future, as the groups move up in levels I can see doing this type of event in places such as najena, permafrost, sola, and eventually graduating to such places as mistmoore castle and kedge keep. Knowing how to work together and follow group leaders and expidition leaders will be invaluable when we are ready to start forming guild giant raids and such.

 

I would like to do this next week, and if possible move a bit farther into the dungeon. We will need to do this slowely but I think if everyone sticks close to their group leader that we can move down and all see the castle and such. (no pet bluud :P) - Tika
