Knights of Vengeance - TikaTika (1/11/2000)
If you haven't heard, KoV have expressed an interest in joining GoW. We would retain our structure while KoV would disband then the members invited into our guild. We duscussed this at the meeting and with an almost unanimous vote, decided to move ahead with it. Wind will be getting together with the leader of KoV to discuss the process in more detail this week. If you have anything you'd like to say about this, please email me.
This merger should allow for many things including better availability of members to group with, and eventually the ability do Kedge/plane/dragon runs in the future.
