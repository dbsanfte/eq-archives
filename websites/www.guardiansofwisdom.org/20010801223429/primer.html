Guild Primer

  Knowledge is Power: a tenant adhered to and respected by all throughout our land. However, with knowledge comes a responsibility. How such responsibility is handled will change as the bearer learns more of how to apply the knowledge. This change is the beginning of Wisdom.


  We call ourselves The Guardians of Wisdom, for that is our purpose. To be one of The Guardians, it is not enough for you to desire to know everything that you can about the world of Norrath, but to apply that knowledge in a way benefiting the role-playing community in our land. This can be done in many ways and through many means, both good and evil. We are open to all races and all professions that exist in our world, provided their main desire is the pursuit of Wisdom.

  Whether you are a Dark Elf Necromancer looking to find the treasures that Miragul left behind, and discover for yourself what went wrong with his last spell. Or you are a simple Rogue who knows there is a great treasure just waiting for you, if you display wisdom, there is a place for you with The Guardians. It is our desire to have an expert in every field of study in Norrath, and for those who acquire such knowledge to share it with the rest of The Guardians. With this great knowledge compiled into one place, surely we shall become the great keepers and Guardians of Wisdom that Norrath can offer.

- Windrunner Thomaii - Keeper of Wisdom
- Indreju Thomaii - Guardian of the Glades
