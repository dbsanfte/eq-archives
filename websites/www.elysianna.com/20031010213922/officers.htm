Bastion of Might Officers
 
 
Being an Officer in the Bastion of Might should be considered both an honor and responsibility.  We are fostering growth for out BOM family of members.  This involves giving time for the good of the guild.  Attending Officers meetings, dealing with issues and crisis's while on-line and overall helping guildmates. 
 
We ask that you lead by example, as our actions speak far louder than our words.  An Officers Counsel room will be established in the forums section of the web pages.  Please refer to it whenever possible.  As an Officer you are the counsel to the Guild Leader.  Please know that we want you to openly discuss and debate issues and concerns.  This is one of the main ingredients that keep a Guild healthy and alive.
 
There will be a maximum of 10 Officers.  When someone steps down only then can someone be promoted.  Promotions will depend on a unanimous decision of all current Officers.  If anyone feels that an Officer or the Guild Leader is not doing a good job, tell another Officer and a vote will be held.  The vote will determine if 1.) They are in fact doing a poor job and 2.) Who will replace them.
 
In the event that the Guild Leader steps down for any reason, the Executive Officer will step into that position until a vote can be held for a new Guild Leader.
