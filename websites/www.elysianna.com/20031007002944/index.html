Bastion of Might 

Bastion of Might is based upon two fundamental doctrines.

     We believe that the most important aspects of Everquest are

   to have fun, be challenged and lively engaged.

Bastion of Might is where good humor is considered

paramount and encouraged.

        Secondly, every player in or outside the guild will be treated

     with respect, dignity and in the spirit of friendship.

      Through our actions and conduct we strive to be one of the

        finest and respected guilds on the server.
