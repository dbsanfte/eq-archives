Bastion of Might Bank
 
Bank Guidelines:
 
The Bastion of Might has a bank that is run by the Guild Leader.  The purpose of the bank is to help members who may need it, especially lower level members.  All players are encouraged to donate to the bank.  The following items are welcomed examples:
 
 Any money that can be spared.
 Any items that are not needed and can be afforded to give away with having to sell.
 Reagents such as Batwings, Jasper, Cat's eye agate, Bone chips, etc..
 Any item that can be used for tradeskills.
Items such as Non-stick frying pans, jewelers kits and other such components.
 When possible, if you take something give something in return.

It's not much of a bank if it is barren and tumble weeds blowing through.

 
 
Bank Restrictions:
 
The Bastion of Might bank is to help members when they need it.  There will be a 30 day restriction period for new members before they are able to access the bank.  This is to eliminate those who would join and leave after becoming twinked out.  Also, members should not expect the guild to provide them with 'uber' gear.
