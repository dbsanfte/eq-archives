Bastion of Might Guidelines
What We Ask Of Our Members:
 
  As stated in the Guild Constitution, a minimum level of 20 is required.  For an individual below level 20, the approval of 2 Officers is required.
  All members are welcome to have their alts guilded.
  We ask that you used /roleplay feature instead of /anon.  This allows others in the Guild to see fellow guild members when they do a /who all guild.
  Foul language against another player and/or guild under any circumstances will not be tolerated.
  We require adherence to Verant's "Play Nice Policy".  As indicated in the Policy there will be no Player killing, stealing of any type, camp stealing and abuse/spamming of other players in the game.  Failure to adhere to this will result in removal from the guild.
  Should another player in the game go against the "Play Nice Policy" in relation to one of our members, please consult a BOM Officer for full support in handling the situation.  We are fiercely protective of our members rights to enjoy the game to it's fullest.
  Group with other BOM members when possible and get to know all of us.
  Attend Guild events when possible.
  If you have items to sell, please offer them in Guild before selling them on the open market.  Consider donating items to the Guild bank as opposed to destroying them.
  Be on the lookout for good players to bring into the Guild.  We prefer that you group with them and get to know them a bit before offering admittance into the Guild.  We respect the opinion of all our members so flexibility to this is allowed with the approval of an Officer.
 
What We Will Do For Members:
  We are happy to help you complete quests and obtain items within your level range whenever it is possible for us to do so.
  Whenever possible we will provide ports free of charge.  This of course depends on our Druids and Wizards being in a position to help.  When able please be waiting for them at rings or spires.
  We guarantee transportation to all Guild events.  Ports with start half and hour prior to event.  Once the event has started there is no guarantee that a port will happen.  We also cannot guarantee that someone will be able to port you out before teh event ends.
  On raids corpse recovery and resurrections are guaranteed.  The raid will not end until all members and guests have been fully recovered.
  Please feel comfortable asking advice or help from anyone within the Guild regarding trade skills or anything else for that matter.
  Advice is always free.  If we don't know the answer, we will help you find it.
