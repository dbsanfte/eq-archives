Bastion of Might Constitution
 
 We recruit people from all Race/Class combinations who are level 20+.  We provide the foundation for a strong, cohesive family of players who foster growth, challenge and humor.  We know that change is a healthy part of evolving and welcome input from every member.  Each member has been specifically selected to be in the Bastion of Might Guild.  All are unique individuals who bring with them a variety of playing styles and skills. 
 
 A core value and expectation is that, no matter what level, race/class, time in Guild etc..ALL are treated with the utmost respect and as an equal.  To this end recruitment is based on recommendations or sponsorship by current standing members.  Any exceptions to this well be considered on a individual basis and requires full support of all Officers.
 
 In the event that there is a dispute within the guild (and there will be, impassioned people stand up for what they believe in) we ask individuals to first attempt to work it out together.  There is an understanding that noting is gained by having a divided guild, everything is gained when successfully resolving conflicts.  If individuals are unable to find a solution with each other, the situation is then presented to the Officers for mediation and resolution.
 
Our goal is to have fun playing Everquest while making friends and strengthening bonds.  We ask that you be involved and proactive as much as possible while maintaining real life commitments.  Whether this be daily or a few times a month we welcome your contribution and enthusiasm.
 
 
In the course of fostering growth and lively involvement decisions affecting the Guild and new policies will be made along the way.  Again, we strongly encourage input from members.  The Officer's counsel will work together to weigh the pros and cons of any given decision and it's overall affect on the Guild.  Officers will never do anything to intentionally hurt the Guild or individuals within.
