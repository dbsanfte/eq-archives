Kazral Shadowthirst
The exp kinda sucks in SF but I have never been to JP or SC and I hate PoJ soloing so I don't know how bad those are either.

The kills can take awhile but it really isn't that bad if you just need to fill in that last yellow or something.

Just fearkite near the BW zone, usually it is open or there is enough game to hunt.

Avoid the chromidracs or felia the whatever because they DoT and dispell. If, however, you can manage to find one close to the BW zone and you dont care about kei (if its on) or other buffs go ahead, they have wussy hp (from what i remember).

Most mobs don't frenzy when you pull but then you can always kite them to fear spot and FD split em. They tend to have a small frenzy radius.

And go for the named and rare (lava walker, hella hp and hardest mob I soloed there; soul devourer, gimp; wandering wurm, avg; black scar, kinda hard; etc.) because they tend to drop shards for a cleric friend and spells more often (some spells are really gimp but some are upper 50s spells).

Go after any regular guy but I advise from best to least, mature wurms, wurms, bottomless feaster, bottomless whatevers etc., wyverns and mature ones I don't mess with because of poison when they turn from fear and drakes just dont seem fun to kill but that's just me.

It isn't better exp than a good group. But a good group is most likely the group that you are not in (murphy's). Either join a guild group or partner somewhere good. Otherwise this place isn't bad for steady but slow exp.

P.S. I think bottomless gnashers and some drake was light blue to me but the mature stuff and the named should remain good stuff. The named you should look out for the most is the soul devourer and the wandering wurm. The latter drops alot of shards and the first drops spells and immortals for the monk epic.
