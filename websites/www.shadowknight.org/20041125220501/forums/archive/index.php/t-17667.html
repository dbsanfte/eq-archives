neferaza
LDoN has nice features with the augment idea. There is one thing in EQ playing a melee class that annoys me more then most other things and that is swapping in and out your primal for the proc.

So why won't there be a quest where you can 'melt' your primal weapon and form an augment stone from it with the 'avatar' proc on that. This way you still have to get a primal in ST and it solves the annoying weapon swapping. Of course there is a drawback too, you have only 1 spot for an augment stone on a weapon so for example you can't put one of those lifetap things on it too and thus you lose some DPS/healing from not having those damage/tap procs.

I don't see anything unbalancing in this, it solves an annoyance in EQ in a way that is very easy to add.

Of course this quest could require some additional things and I don't have a problem with it being pretty expensive. Maybe this quest should be given by the guys who give you the adventurers stones - they seem to have some experience with magical stones. And maybe he gives it only if you completed a certain number of adventures in that setting.

Prismatic stone (or Primal stone)
Type 4 augment
Adds proc: Avatar
