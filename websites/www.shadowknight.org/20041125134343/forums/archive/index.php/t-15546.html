I have seen a handful of sites that list Shadow Knight skill caps. Unfortunately none of them have correct information.

In determining what the maximum skill is at any level one needs to know 2 numbers: the 50th level cap and the 60th level cap.

Before 50th level the skill cap for all skills except Offense and Defense is (Level + 1) * 5 up until the 50th level cap. So, at 1st level Two Hand Slashing is capped at 10. Offense and Defense are capped at Level * 5 up until the 50th level cap. So at 1st level Offense is capped at 5.

Beyond 50th level skills increase at (60th cap - 50th cap) / 10 rounded up per level. For example the 50th level cap for meditation is 185 and the 60th level cap is 226, so (226 - 185) / 10 rounded up = 41 / 10 rounded up = 4.1 rounded up = 5 per level.

Due to changes in SK's skill caps or to bad estimates, castersrealm has a few skills which are listed incorrectly. The following are the correct caps:

Dodge : 125 : 170
Taunt : 180 : 220
Offense : 200 : 225

Meditate : 185 : 226
