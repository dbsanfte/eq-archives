I know this might be a newb question also but I tried to get in to Paineel the other day and got a message that it was locked and I didn't have they key. Where and what do I need to do to get the key?

To get into Paineel you need a Bone Crafted Key. And all you have to do to get that is get your Heretic faction up to amiable and ask one of the skelly guards in the newbie area for one.

As far as the Ratman Guard, I've been down there a couple times since the revamp and haven't actually seen him, but I have seen the other kind of Ratman (Inhabitants) down past the bank. The ones I saw were closer to the broken gate, but the Guard may wander farther, I don't know. That's the only area I've seen any Ratmen in yet, so it's definitely where I'd start looking.

If you still have business down there, I wouldn't sweat it. They changed all (most) of the mobs, but I don't think they really made them much harder. At 61 everything is green (even the huge Golems that hang out in the first pit now) at least until you get past the jail. I don't think getting to the bank is really any harder than it ever was. I went down there with a single group (avg level was maybe 60) and we had no trouble at all. In fact, it was so easy we got bored and turned around by the time we hit the broken gate and just bum-rushed our way back to the top. We fought our way up to the tower and across the bridge and out without ever even slowing down. I think a single group in their low 50's could still probably do really well down there.

The zone is different, and maybe more interesting with all the new mobs, but I don't think it's anything to worry about.
