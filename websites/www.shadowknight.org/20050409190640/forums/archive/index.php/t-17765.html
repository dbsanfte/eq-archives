Groff Gorespiller
Keep all weaponskills up.

No particular order for now, just when you max one for your level, move on to another. At around 40, your skills have a pause built in. The cap won't go up with level again until 50, so you'll have plenty of time to work them all up to max then, so long as you've not neglected them. Don't worry about a "primary skill" until you get to the point where it will do you some good. In higher levels, you will want to make more extensive use of 2 handed weapons than 1 handed, but everything is situational, so keep plugging away at whatever is lowest and you'll do fine.

Groff Gorespiller
I would suggest against spending your skill points each level on keeping weapons skills up. From what I've seen, especially on 1 hander weapons, skills will go up very fast on their own with some dex buffs and a bard twisting dex and haste songs. Keep your points for bumping up the slower skills (2 hander skills go up slower because of the delay, as skillups are determined by swing), and skills that you don't have the opportunity to practice easily on your own.

At 57, I just let my level skill increases ride until I see a glaring need to bump one up by a few points. Pretty much every thing else will go up with practice.
