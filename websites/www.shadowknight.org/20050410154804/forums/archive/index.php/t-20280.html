They have changed fear kiting and it won't work anymore that way for your ranger. Load him up take him to the Karanas (or if your are that uber maybe EW) and try it. Now feared mobs continue on there path even when you hit them. That is not a class issue, its a change.

If i was going to solo, I am certainly soloing in the old world not Luclin (where mobs have gobs of HPs). There are plenty of outdoor zones in Velious and Kunark that will not make you fear kite as hard.

However, please be aware that SKs are not the best soloing class. Necros and Beast Lords are both much better soloers. In particular, xp changes in recent months favor grouping dramatically over soloing. This will become more evident at 60+ where kiting turns to aggro kiting. Learn to time fear and snare, at least roughly in your head, and run back when fear drops so the mob chases you to a safer starting point again before you re-fear.

In many respects the game becomes less trivial as you level up, and your role often changes too. Different classes have different frustrations, but the basic issue remains the same.

As an SK you have the advantage that (till you hit GoD and upper tier PoP anyway as a new starter) you'll at least be in demand.
