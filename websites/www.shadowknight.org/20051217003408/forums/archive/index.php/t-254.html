Well if you are still 45 as your sig says, then head to Nurga to the Briarweb spiders. That way you can get xp as well as faction. They're good xp up to 48.

drus = Drusella Sathir, the boss mob of Howling Stones (Charasis) also Venril's ex wife
juggs = Juggernauts, the big bad golems in Sebilis
trak = Yes, Trakanon. The last raid I was on he dropped 3 sinews

Also...you do need to be level 50 to get the first ring

Here's a recap...


Ring 1

What you need:
Level 50, high warmly faction (100 points below ally, 1000 points in absolute terms.)

Give Herald Telcha in Chardok a green goblin skin or two Mt. Death mineral salts and then say, "I serve the sarnak". You will be rewarded with:

Di`Zok Signet of Service (ac2 1cha 1int 1wis 65hp 45mana LORE NO DROP)


Ring 2

What you need:
Ally faction, Ring 1, The head of Warlord Skargus, Drogan Warlord's Insignia

1. Talk to "a goblin traitor" (2-3 hour spawn) in Droga at 0, -175 and say "what evidence". He will give you a note.

2. Take this note to "a Sarnak spy" in Frontier Mountains at 1880, 3600. Follow the dialogue and you will eventually say, "I will get the report." He will give you a forged note.

3. Take this forged note to "a shady goblin" in Warsilik Woods near Cabilis at –1645, -1135 on the 2nd floor, along with a Drogan Warlord's Insignia (random drop in Nurga.) He will give you the "report."

4. Take this report to "an angry goblin" (5 day spawn) in Droga at 150, 1950. This will spawn Warlord Skargus (level 56 SK with a 1k HT). Kill Skargus and loot head.

5. Take ring #1 and the head back to Herald Telcha in Chardok and he will reward you with:

Regal Band of Bathezid
(ac5 2cha 2int 2wis 5sta 85hp 50mana, 5 charges of 25point ds LORE NODROP)


Cords Quest

What you need:
Ally faction
Undead Dragon Sinew (Trakanon, Juggs)
Urn of Spirits (Drusilla Sathir)
Ring 2

Hand Undead Dragon Sinew, Urn of Spirits and Ring 2 to Queen in Chardok. You get ring 2 back and the cord.

Spirit Wracked Cord (Lore, No Drop)
Shoulder, Arms, Wrist
AC12, Sta5, Hp100, SvCold+10, SvMagic+5
Effect: Aura of Battle (regen for 2 hps per tick and +10 atk)
All/All Weight 0.0
