03-29-2002, 06:08 PM
Also remember that if you have any number of casters on you do NOT cast FD if you see "Mob_kicking_your_butt_01 begins to cast a spell" or it won't help you. The spell may succeed, but after you FD the spell will land and they will re-agro on you.
If you see the above after you have started to cast it, hit "D" to duck an interupt.

You heal and regain mana as if standing also. This was a big pain for me 2 weeks ago. I had 3 caster mobs (necro, cleric and an invoker) on me in Dalnir. I barely got off my FD (i was praying) and had 6 HPs when I hit the floor. Due to a pathing bug the Invoker got stuck in the doorway and stopped right next to me instead of going around the corner. Took me 30 minutes before I had enough mana or HPs to back off and FD again.



Another thing to note:
Once you get Hide at 35 you can try to "ctrl+s" and immediately hit your Hide hotkey. keep your mob targeted and then hit "C". If indifferent, sit and med. It doesn't matter how close the mob is either. I had a mob standing on my head and I did this successfully. There is a "lag" between it registering you alive, but it is very short.

What I don't know is if you can hit the hide hotkey while still FDed. I haven't been willing to try this yet as I am normally pretty desperate and low on health when I try the method I do use. I have been 80% successful on the "ctrl+s"/hide method so far and no deaths. *crosses fingers*

Good luck to you.
