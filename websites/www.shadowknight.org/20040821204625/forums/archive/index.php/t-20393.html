A little clarity. The air "mounts" are a taxi service. You don't control them at all, they're just a means of getting from preset point A to preset point B. It's a flight made in real time, and you can see the action going on down below you as you pass over.

The regular, land based mounts are just for transportation. No fighting on them. That nightmare in the screenshot is actually the summoned Warlock steed, it's not race-specific at all. The undead will be getting an undead steed for their mount (skeletal or more zombie like, I don't know!)

Last I heard, trolls and gnomes were tentatively getting the wolves and battle rams respectively, but that may (and hopefully will) change to give the races more of their own identity. They already have to use another races hometown as is.
