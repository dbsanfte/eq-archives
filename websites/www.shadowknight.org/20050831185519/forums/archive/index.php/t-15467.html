He spawns where one of the two iksar slaves spawn. The other night I sat and camped them for a while, and discovered 2 things.

The slave on the left (If you are sitting against the wall inbetween them, facing the tunnel out of the room), when hailed, gives you a message that talks about shackles (usually). Killing him gives no faction hit. In that place spawns another iksar garbed like a bandit from FoB, which gives you +Legion of Cabilis etc. Or another shackle mob spawns.

For the right slave...

Each time I've been there, and hailed the iksar on the right, I've gotten a message about frothing at the mouth, etc etc. He also occasionally buffs himself. Killing him gives you negative legion of cabilis and negative scaled mystics (shaman guild). Ooops. And I've heard it's a big hit, but I haven't checked my faction since. Unfortunately, it seems that after two nights of camping there, only tihs spawn is an actual place holder for Argest. You must kill the iksar slave to get him to spawn.

The second night I was there, I cleared the left spawn repeatedly, never got Argest (About 2 hours worth, 6 spawns I believe). Not wanting another faction hit, I had a friend clear the right spawn, and got Argest on the next pop. So yes, you do have to kill the iksar slaves. Watch the faction hit. I wasn't quite sure how high my scaled mystic faction was as a dark elf in the first place, now I might have to go do some faction work, again.
