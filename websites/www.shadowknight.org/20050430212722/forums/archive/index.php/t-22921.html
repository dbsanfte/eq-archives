Oberyn
I think you will have fun there:)

Two things: Make sure you get singles, or have good crowd control, since with an add on you tanking, you will go down so fast you will go "WTF!?!"

Getting singles is easy, pulls are not particularly hard there.

Second thing, that makes it doable: Slow early. I usually pull, get good pre-agro, and have chanter slow before I engage. Cheesy, but it avoids that huge burst of mob DPS to start with. You can do without, but its a crapshoot. A couple good rounds, a slow heal, and you are toast.

There should be some upgrades there for you, its a challenge, therefore a blast, and XP is excellent.

For the named mobs, get creative, and they usually go down:)

Entrance is off Barindu, on the right side of the zone (coming from Qinimi). Talk to the drowned-looking Manga Elf female there. Zone is behind her in the gully.

Mites summon, Kyvs summon, Ikaavs summon, named's summon, rest dont - which can come in very handy, specially with no chanter.

I've changed my toon a bit lately. My plan is to just hit Oow till I get to 70 and then worry about Vxed/Tipt again.

With that said, your crew could handle lots of spots in Oow that I have been to. Only one of them would have possible upgrades for you.

Wall of Slaughter (aka Dreadlands 2.0) This zone is great xp, and the named there drops runes for spells and some descent items. A couple items are:

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30487
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30482

This is also rumored to drop there. But I've not seen which mob yet:
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=70225

You can look on allah's there might be a couple other upgrades for you. But none of them will be big upgrades at all. Also, the joys of WoS are that it is super crowded. We went the first night and killed 5 named, there were 30 in zone. Every time I've run through there since then there have been at least 110 in zone.

In my opinion, your better option now is just to focus on Oow spells for the group. Getting the Shammy (I think you said you have one) the upgraded spells will do more for you than several upgrades will do. Those, along with your spells should let you start to hit Vxed/Tipt fairly easily I would think. At least I'm hoping they do me.

Another spot for spells are the instances off of Dranik's Hollow. Just look for the named in there. Their Ph's are uniquely named so they are easy to find. These instances are very easy and are very solid xp for the diffuculty.
