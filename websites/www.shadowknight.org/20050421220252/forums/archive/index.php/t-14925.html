Jabbuk
You really don't even need a friend to start the quest.

Go out and get your faction. If you look in this forum, you'll find a number of different suggestions on how to do it. Choose your favorite. Once you've got the faction, bring your DE to the top of the SK guild and hail the iksar standing behind the Arch Duke. He'll give you some mumbo-jumbo about collecting sarnak heads - just follow the text and you'll get a bag.

Go out into LOIO newbie area right near the city and kill every goblin you see. Eventually, sarnak hatchlings will start to pop. Kill them and loot four of the No Drop heads. Put 'em in the bag, hit combine, and return to the iksar who gave you your bag.

Sit him down there, camp out, and make a newbie Iksar SK. You'll land right there. Hand the note to the Arch Duke and you'll get back the first Khukri. Turn around and hand it to the Iksar for the bag quest. DO NOT HAIL ANYTHING. Camp out back to your DE, and Hail the bag quest iksar. Hand him the combine bag, and bingo, you'll have the first of the No Drop Khukri's to start that quest.

If you go into the Dirty Deeds forum, you'll find there's a stickied post there which is all about this quest. Look it over - you'll get more info than you'll ever need.
