Apparently it IS the intent of SoE to reward the groups of players doing the helping with the epic with some form of loot spawned in chests. However you can blame the farmers who killed mobs over and over for the system requiring overhaul.

To that extent:

Cipheron, as well as other epic NPCs that spawn a chest were perma-killed in the last few days and changed as of today. As of today, you will need to be doing the epic in question, as well as being at that step (or near it) in the epic to be able to spawn the treasure chest involved. If your character indeed looted an epic item already that spawned a chest, you will not be able to spawn that same chest again, because an invisible flag is placed on your character indicating that you've spawned this chest already.
