I have and use both...

epic's better imho...

you lose a few ac and a few hps, but the epic has better resists and stats...

I can notice the 4 extra damage on my lance, but I think the bash makes up for it against non casters, and on casters, well, bash is nice against casters :)

the DPS from the procs depends on your dex really.. once you proc your lance, it'll be up 100% of the time for the rest of the fight, as long as you keep your lance equipped... it does 37 damage per tick. Epic proc does 50 damage per tick (and heals 50 damage per tick of course :)) but epic proc is harder to keep up full time... I'd say around 180 dex or so, you can keep epic up 99% of the time without much trouble. With 255 dex I can proc my lance, then switch to epic and do some manaless dot stacking, swapping back and forth as needed ;) I get such a kick out of that

soloing, epic beats the hell out of the lance, there's no comparison

but, buff slots are a problem sometimes (a lot of times.. give me 10 more buff slots and I'll use em all easily, give me 5 more and I'll at least be happy) - in cases where I just don't have the buff slots to have soul consumption overwriting my grim aura or other things if my buff order gets mixed up, I switch to the lance
