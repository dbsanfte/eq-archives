Talonmare
I came to hate the day my shaman turned 46. we camped there until she turned 64. (My hide never dropped in all that time.) Of course I was 65 then.

The problem, as was said before, will be social aggro on your pulling. I'd suggest setting the shammy up at the zone in, and pulling from a good distance away. Once you know you have it single, bring it back to the shammy. that's what I did 90% of the time and had very few problems.

I'd also stay away from the Clerics of Hate and the steps to the right of zone in. The first will probably run you dry with CH's and the second will probably provide to little room to split out.
