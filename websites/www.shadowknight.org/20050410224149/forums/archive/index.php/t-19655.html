Camby_VZ
Step One:

Get to the Room VZ sits in and get to the nearest corner (left corner from the stage) that he sits on and prep for pull .. Have MT ( warrior is good ) arrow pull him and beat him down til he poofs ( Stay in corner ).

Step Two:

After First VZ poofs .. 5 of him will spawn in the middle of the room. Get 5 decently geared tanks in a group with their own slower and own healer and have a SoS rogue go out and individually select a target for each said tank. One VZ will be higher than the rest ( this is the REAL VZ ) kill all the other VZ's BEFORE you initiate the attack on the REAL VZ. Have the tank with the real VZ call out that he has the REAL VZ ( to avoid killing him too soon and getting even more VZ's ) Have 1 MA ( main assist ) target and call out which Vallon Zek to attack and everyone but the 5 initial MT's will attack and kill the fake VZ's in order before engaging the REAL VZ. Once fakes are down .. attack REAL VZ until he poofs and spawns back in the middle with his 4 fakes

Step Three:

Rinse and Repeat 3 more waves

Step Four:

Last wave kill them all in order like mentioned above with the only exception being that the last VZ you kill ( the REAL VZ ) will die and not split .... Hail the projection and split up the PHAT LEWTS. You are done
