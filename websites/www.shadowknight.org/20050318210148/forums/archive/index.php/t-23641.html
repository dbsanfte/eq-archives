You need to mem harmshield, FD (aa if you have it), gather shadow, any darkness if you are on snare... whatever you need to increase your group dps (aura,bite...)). Verify that your SOE is up for a good 30mn.... no horse available in ruin (

From BF zone in go invi, exit castle, take first bridge on left, then the other bridge. You are at the entrance of the murkglider part. It is normaly safe , I had my 55 bard camped here to be able to check on breeder wo problem. I will call this point point A.
refresh invi follow corridor to a room full of glider, take the other corridor you arrive another room (also full of murkglider) that is where the named murkglider and our breeder spawn. Take the other exit (call this entrance point B), climb stairs, on your left is a Dead end with a single murglider, set your camp here (kill this mob and start the job).)

a) go to point B (without INVI), cast harmshield, enter room and run to poiint A to FD ... the long way to do it (aggro everything, specially the breeder, I usually make a run around her to be sure, DO NOT STOP RUNNING you are on a tight schedule), cross the room, enter other corridor (aggro corridor you cannot prevent it), enter other room (aggro it also... looks like a nice train now) your HS shoould stop when you exit the 2nd room as you are faster you should reach thebridge before the murkglider with enough time to stop, make a half turn (as a 65+ sk you should know why)and cast FD (careful you cannot take too many fizzle). You should have make pop 2 murkglider spawn in the begining of the corridor ... wait 2mn (message your ennemies has forgotten you), cast gather shadow , return to camp by the way you came (if your invi drop ... good luvk to you mine did not, you can also COTH if you have a mage).
b) rinse and repeat step a twice (took me 10mn as a cle gave me bestow DA for the 2nd pull, and I refreshed DA for the third).
c) now the easy part you just pull the 2 murkglider nearest to point A (FD pull, pet pull as you prefer), then the breeder (FD pull just to be sure that you bring it single a roamer go sometimes near it and can give you multiple adds else it comes single). Take your time I single pull all of them and the spawns where still up when I ran back to ruin more than 20mn after my first pull.
The important points to remember:
1) all spawn should be in the corridor where you FD (they are also the only murkglider there), so verify that you have exactly 6, you have 2mn to count them (even my ogre managed to learn to count up to 6). This if much easier with a tracker.... if you have one.
2) your camp is in a DE with 1 murkglkider who can pop there, kill it just before breeder pull ... In my experience you can be sure that spawn and add will target your cleric first.... perhaps bad luck but really you prefer to do it without add.
3) if your group want to do something they can pull from the breeder room while you clear aggro 3 times, just be careful on adds (you need a FD puller IMO).
4) always keep a snare ready murkglider run away (except for the breeder who is unsnarable but do not run).
5) No mobs see invi from the zone in to the camp point... and that include the breeder (not sure with the other named which pop in the breeder room, I have seen it only once... and had not time to check before pull). I have seen murkglider which are undead in other zones but these one are NOT.
