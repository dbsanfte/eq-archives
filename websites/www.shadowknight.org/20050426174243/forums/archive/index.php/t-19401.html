you say, i do not have the tool

the toilmaster says, bla bla bla
-- you give the toilmaster a ragged book
-- the toilmaster gives you rites of exoneration
you say, i need the hammer of exoneration
-- the toilmaster gives you the hammer of exoneration
you say, i need a bag
-- the toilmaster gives you penance bag

this part sucks... equip the hammer. go to a newbie zone (i liked LOIO) and go nuts with it, you need it to proc to get the bone fragments. once you get 8 of them use the mortar to combine them into powdered bones (you'll need to do this 7 times) place the 7 powdered bones in the bag with bloodwater(found all through out cabilis), then combine the bag.

head back to squire gristle and give him the combined bag and book
