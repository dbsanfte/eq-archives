If you are going to do Nadox, I would suggest perhaps the Hatesfury zoneline. I spent a lot of time 2 boxing in Nadox with a high 50s SK and cleric. The cleric had KEI but could do singles without it. The Hatesfury side has higher level mobs, thus hopefully more xp.

I would think you could 2 box in Veksar on some of the undead as well.

Without a slower load up on the DSes. Banshee Aura, Cloak of the Akehva and Mark of Retribution is 32/hit. Add in a Coldain Ring and you are at 40. Get Cloak of Luclin and its 45.

If you can take them the mobs deep in the Hole would be good xp. You can now HS/DA jump and land in a non-aggro spot. If you had a gate potion, both could get out no problem. Haven't really thought about this as an xp spot but maybe it would work. It surely would be uncamped.
