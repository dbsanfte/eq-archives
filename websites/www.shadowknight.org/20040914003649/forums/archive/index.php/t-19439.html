Do I mitigate differently by choosing AAs such as SA, ToL and SCF?

My immediate goal is to be the best tank possible, while worrying about dps later on, so I want to choose my AAs wisely.

Lynxal
HPs are still very important. Planning to lifetap to make up a 500HP difference another tank has over you is not really practical. Especially since that tank is either another SK so it's a moot point, a Pally who can heal himself much more efficiently than you could hope to, or a warrior who mitigates damage better than you to start with.

If you are fighting trash exp mobs below your real ability (like LDoN Normal adventures for most 65s), then lifetaps and lifetap procs are probably helping you mitigate the amount of healing you need over a warrior since none of the mobs are really hitting either of you that hard. However, when things go bad a group is going to want the higest Level/AC/HP/AA tank they can get...and that is what pick-up groups are going to look for up front (especially now that any poorly geared warrior using 4 expansion old weapons can hold agro).

On a Hard advenure....unless you are an uber EP+ SK....your life expectancy is dependant solely on your healer getting heals off on you. HPs give you that valuable second for a heal to land before the mob is slowed when it's really kicking you butt. Even when it's slowed they get the occasional good round of 900/900/900 that can be a shock to your health so any HP cushion is a good cushion.

I would go LR5, ID5, PD3 if you are tanking a lot. SA is not going to help your tanking/self healing when 9 times out of 10 you are probably using mental corruption in an exp group to keep up with the fast rate of kills. The more you mitigate damage and smoothe out damage spikes the better chance you have of your healer judging their timing properly....and you living.
