Timourous Deep Gorla's then Spircocs. Can solo, faster grouped, mobs hit lightly, low HP a so you make up for a lack of ZEM (Zone experience modifier) by quick kills. Also I have a few pull and assist calls from there that people who've hunted there get. ("Polly wanna die <Squawk>" and a call using the weird yell the Gorla's make). Probably can start fear kiting Spirocs at your level.

Other notes:
Many new players see "hot zones" and shit and just run towards those thinking it's great. Yes mobs in TD will give you 50% less exp per kill, but with the HP calculations of mobs in LoY you can kill ~3+ Spirocs in the time it takes to kill one Ogre in LoY. Yes, LoY is great for groups and quests, but Spirocs and other Old World camps are greatly overlooked. Pre-Revamp Howling Stones was a great exp spot not for the Exp per kill, but because all the mobs were low HP undead mobs that you could burn through quickly which gave awesome exp over time. Places like LoY (and I'd say a # of Luclin zones like Aheva) need high ZEM's just to bring up the experience over time levels up to something worth while.
