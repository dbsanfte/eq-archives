This is some possible places to try:

Once I left oggok I followed the road down and made the left in the fork. Kept going along the way killing the lowbie types, spiders bats lizardmen scout along the way. I kept on then made another left in the fork in the road wich leads you pass a hut or two, a spire like construction. The roads ends at a pok book. I stayed there and hunted in that spot till I got up to lvl 4-5.

Then head towards the lizardmen camps and farmed them till lvl 7-8. After that I went to innothe swamp and killed anything for xps. Most conned white yellow and red from kobolds to burleys. They also have orc raiders and such so to me its a good place to lvl up to 13-14.

After that its pretty much up to you where you want to go from that point. Me, I went straight towards Nro, and Oasis, for derv camps and orc pass etc.
