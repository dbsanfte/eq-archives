i know you wanted solo tactics but there is like little to know solo upgrades you could get...

concievably you could solo the pieces needed for a Sprit Wracked Cord, another Bracer of Benevolence (prolly duo at least), the 70hp ring from Chardok B (shammy duo prolly) and things like that. However, a better suggestion would be just doing the exp grind in WOS. Just about every piece you need upgraded drops in WOS either from random in zone or from named camps. A new 1 hander, shield, bracer, boots, ring, shoulders, cloak are all fairly abtainable.

To break up the boredom i suggest going to Muramite Proving grounds. This might be a bit too much for you to tank with your current gear however there is GREAT no drop loots that drop there most of the time and you can usually just OOC for upgrades. ITs a great spot to go LFG and try and get stuff as it rots. Warclasp earring 125hp and stats, maze runner mask 145hps and stats, maze runner ring 125hps Cleave 1 and stats etc etc.

Either way, not only will the loot you get will have a substaintial effect on your char but the amount of exp/aa you get will extremely help you from where you are restarting at.

The shear fact of the matter is that there isnt much you can solo with your gear and AA that is gonna do anything for you. If you are low on time, try LDoN as a last resort. Most groups blow threw them in like 30 mins or so now and you can always use the points for armor upgrades and HP aug upgrades.

sorry if i wasnt a help but if you have any questions, keep asking
