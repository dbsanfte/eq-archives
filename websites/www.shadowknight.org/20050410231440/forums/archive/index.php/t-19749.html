I don't think anyone has ever parsed the real loss in dps while casting a lot. Depends on a lot of factors.

If you use a 2H with delay > 32, all your spears and scythes should not make you lose melee dps.

Using BoH and ToI, you will lose some damage, but still will come out ahead.

Summary, although melee dps is lost, you can cast as much as you can and still come out ahead.
