I think Plane of Justice is a good starter if you want a good intro. The run to where you fight with a group is right around the corner, not very confusing or dangerous. Plane of Innovation is also good if you can find a group there. In PoI, you never leave the zone in unless of course you are with crazy bastards that like to run down the path and take a junk room. Really it just gets you used to how hard and fast they hit and what to expect in planes like PoD and PoN, where the mobs get a little bit crazier.n (pretty soon you will be making a post titled "CoD, ouch ouch ouch, Loading please wait...).

But basically I would just stick to LDoNs and get to 50+. I found the xp in LDoN at 46-50 better than tier 1 pop xp. That of course stopped as soon as I hit 51 or 52.
