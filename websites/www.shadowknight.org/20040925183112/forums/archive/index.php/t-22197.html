Nastard
You don't even need harmshield for the pull, I find it easier to do it without harmshield, because then you have to waste seconds slowing down then waiting until you're completely stopped so you can channel the spell. If firetail gets close to you while youre doing that, can get shadowstepped and get really disoriented. Hardest part of the pull is the visibility of course, but once you've done it a few times you can do it in your sleep. Here's what I do:

Bring a shammy with you just to the top of the lava above the cave. Helps to go partially into the lava (not so youre taking damage) and sit so your line of sight is right at the surface. Everything under the lava becomes clear as water and you can gauge where you need to go. On your call, have the Shammy cast Q on you and you can usually make it to Firetail close to full health. Tell the Shammy to stay there and keep you targetted. From here, park your pet near Firetail, and begin to swim back. Once you're sure you're swimming through the tunnel and not running face first into a wall, suicide your pet into Firetail and just continue to swim until you've reached the other side. I've never outran him in quite a few pulls doing it this way. Have the Shammy spam a small heal or whatever on you until you're no longer out of range so just in case you die they're added to the hate list and can simply run to camp to finish the pull. You can basically be in and out in under 5 minutes.
