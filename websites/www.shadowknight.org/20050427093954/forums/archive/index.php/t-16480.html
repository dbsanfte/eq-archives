Ceptic
I know that this thread will get major flamed, but I figured that I would come out of the proverbial closet and own up to making a newbie Gnome SK and why.

I’ve been at this game on and off since July of 99, though I quit for over 2 years, and just recently came back to play, though casually. Of all the races, I find the Gnome, specifically the female to be unique and somewhat rare. Many of you hate Gnomes, I know from reading threads over the past few weeks. However, finding a class/race combo that is both unique and somewhat rare intrigued me. Much has changed over the last few years, and I decided to start from scratch with a newbie to learn the lay of the lands anew. So far, I have quickly developed a love of the little Gnome for the diversity in skills, being smaller then my pet, and the completely unique ability in skill i.e. Tinkering. I will more then likely never see level 65. Will never experience the “uberness” that comes from belonging to a high-end guild. Never know what it is like to have excessive AA points. I will however enjoy my limited time spent wandering the various zones that have become second nature to most of you. Please feel free to blast me for my decision to play a Gnome, as my skin is hardened to the likeness that of an Iksar.

Happy Journeys …
