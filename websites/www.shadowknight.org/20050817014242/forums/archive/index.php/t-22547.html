1-5 and make good pp?? Let me think... Ah, I know, Karnors, would be a good place for you. Especially if you go there wearing cloth armor. There's an AC bonus in that zone, people wearing all cloth armor only get hit for 1-2 points per melee round, for only 2 melee rounds before the mob dies.

And, as if that weren't enough, if you go in there with all cloth armor (yes, even cloth sandals, and a cloth mask and neckpiece) and a rusty short sword, and a buckler, (note: rusty sword and buckler have to be equiped), you get the automatic bonus of also getting a 20 point damage shield (part of the zone in bonus, I swear) so that when the mob hits you, and you hit it, it dies, you get all the xp.

Not to mention the 1kpp per hour drop rate. by time you hit level 5 in there, you will easily make 30kpp.

You can stay there until level 10-12, since it's a newbie area, and by level 10, you will have made close to 100kpp, and have gotten all the rare drop level 8-12 spells, which each sell for 30kpp, minimum.
