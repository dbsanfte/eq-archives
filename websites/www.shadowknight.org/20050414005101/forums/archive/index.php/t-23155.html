A Charged Magnetic Breastplate is probably the best you can attain right now, unless there are some droppable/attuneable BPs that have yet to be uncovered. Maybe keep an eye out for a cheap ornate BP that someone is trying to get rid of. Farwater plate may be close, as well. They usually run 100-130k on Torv. These have the added bonus of being able to be sold back when you do upgrade it later.

I can't really comment on if you could tank the Statue, as I have never been at a raid where anything but a warrior tanked it. If I had to guess I would say that you could though, with a decent healing team. I didn't check your AAs, though.

If you can get a higher group together, Seru should be 1 or 2 groupable without too much difficulty.

LDoN is an option as well. The BP out of Tak can be modified to be pretty damned good, though it does take a substantial amount of points.
