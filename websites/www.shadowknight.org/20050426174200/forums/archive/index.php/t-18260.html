I just finished my Greenmist quest two days ago, DE here :D

Being simply non-KoS is not going to be enough to do the Khukri quests--you need to have "amiable" faction with Crusaders of Greenmist faction (SKs) in order for them to do this quest with you. If you can use an illusion spell to boost your faction with them, you might save yourself some time, but I don't think it's going to be enough to get you from scowling or dubious to amiable. Let me know.

You may be able to start the Greenmist quest before you are amiable to the turn-in people, but by the time I had my items, I was at least amiable to them.
