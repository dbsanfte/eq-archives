Slein Jinn
While necro rez would certainly be a very nice ability to have, it's questionable in terms of balance. The justification for giving us necro rez would be to parallel paladins' rez spell, which is potentially an important asset to a group. The problem is that giving us a rez spell would then swing the pendulum too far the other direction - we would be superior to paladins, as we have the tools to survive a wipe and they do not. For it to be balanced, they'd then need something like Escape or the like, and then you start a chain-reaction balance nightmare.

It's really a sticky situation, and something should be done, as paladins' rez ability gives them an advantage over us (aside from that granted by stuns and such). The problem is that balancing it is far from cut and dry. Giving us necro rez would be equivilant to the warrior upgrades from the melee balancing - taking something from too weak to too powerful. I havn't really decided what my opinion on the necro rez situation is yet, but it's definately not as cut-and-dry as alot of you are trying to make it out to be.
