Drakang
As stated very little needs to be cleared. Clear the front Courtyard and the area to which ever side the ghost is on. Usually the left side now which is also where the mummy comes from. IF you go down to the left you will see a door into the basement of the tower. The mummy comes from down there. I usually clear the skeletons down there so I can aggro Glohnor and pull him rather than waiting on him. However you do not have to. Do the hand in Glohnor charges the one that handed in. Kill him. Note he cannot be snared so make sure folks are ready to blast him hard when he starts to run if you are not trying to keep the front courtyard clear. Did this with 2 groups ranging from 54-65 so you should have plenty of firepower. Don't even bother clearing the actual tower itself.
