courtesy of a post from the pally boards....they have a swarm pet weapon that drops here...so must camp it more...hehe:

the 4 named spawn in specific spots- for a point of reference, if you stand in the middle of the CY and face out towards the 2 named birds:

Captain spawns in the hut to your right and in front of you ( solder or defender is PH)

Vicar spawns in the lil stage/chapel area to your left (always a chaplain PH)

Sorceror spawns behind and to the left along the lower catwalk in the bottom of the stairwell (always a magus PH)

Charmer ahead and to your right, along catwalk, up stairs, and 2nd mob on the upper level (alway a diviner PH)

I've been leveling there 66-70. I can confirm all of the spawn points, except the captain, other than they have the building right. (the monk was pulling when the captain spawned). Sks make great pullers here, because they can invis to avoid accidentally aggroing a mob they can't see through the side of a hut.

A note about the vicar of fire...he paths even weirder that the rest of the mobs...We set up in the ne corner, and when pulling the vicar from the south to the north, he pathed diagonal to the se hut, then made a beeline for the captain's hut, all while i just went straight north. Go figure...just means you might want to clear the se hut before pulling the vicar. The ph paths normal. Both vicar and vicar ph can be pulled out of their platform without aggroing the folk around him. I tend to pull the stair guards just to be safe, however.
