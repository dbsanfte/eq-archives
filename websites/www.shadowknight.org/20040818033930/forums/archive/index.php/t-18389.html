Ganodoar
Personally I think Life Leech is worth it but only if you get it at 50 (or 49 if you get a 50+ friend to get the spell for you). The quest is really fun and it's neat to go back to old school zones like Lower Guk, Sol B, and Permafrost. If you are a troll or iksar too it feels good to bash frogloks while camping the froglok noble in guk. 3k is a lot but beleive me you will come to a point in the game where 3k isn't too hard to make if you really try. Farming Hill Giants is a great way to make 3k, but only in the mornings if you can due to less competition.

Life Leech will last you about 10-11 levels before Drain Soul takes over. Even then life leech can still cut it since Drain Soul has a long cast time. However, I find myself using leech less now but it is still in my spell book. It could have its uses perhaps until touch of volatis and inny come.

As it has been pointed out life leech can be a life saver. There have been a few instances where Life Leech has saved my life. I do agree the recast time is annoying but 2 minutes goes by fast when you think about it. With KEI too you get the mana you lose back relatively quickly.

Do the quest and you will realize how much fun it is. More fun to actually camp the stuff yourself though I can see why people wouldn't break down and buy the droppable pieces in the bazaar. Besides the quest NPC reminds me of Kurron Ni. Both send you out on a quest and end up backstabbing you in the end. So you could say Kazzel prepares you for the beginning of the epic (if you don't run seals that is).
