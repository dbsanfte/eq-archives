Have killed Guardian of the Seal twice now with guild (gratz me on the earring and an Ancient Tome of Research) and after hearing a guildmate talking about a safehouse thread on an associated quest, I've been very interested. Looted a page from a guard on each kill of GotS and picked up a ground spawn bringing my page count to three out of 12 (as it says in the NPC's quest dialogue). From the extensiveness of this quest (kill the 70 NPC, pick up a lot of ground spawns, kill a lot of named spawns, possibly go to other zones and do 'side quest' sections - the gnomes), the end result will definitely be interesting. Presumably it opens the fire pots up for use, which would suggest a one time completion quest only. Could be that the quest gives you some sort of Firepot key.

As for the Jagged Sword of the Dragon Slayer, my guildmate looted it and dunno if he's done any research yet. I presumed some sort of associated quest and at first figured the sword and the Ancient Tome were related but after reading the NPC dialogue I scrapped that guess. Looked through Paineel and didn't spot any new special looking NPCs, but didn't go into the guild areas very extensively. In other words, I'll be looking a little more later. I also was going to talk to Echo of Nortlav, I may have seen a trigger phrase in an NPC's dialogue that will open something more up. More on that later.

(note: for easy access to Echo of Nortlav and to Guardian of the Seal, if you want to see / kill either of them, you can VERY easily and safely DA parachute. Pop Harmshield and immediately jump down the Hole in Paineel - make sure lev is off. You go smack down into the ground and take no damage. There are currently no wanderer mobs going through that room and nothing is KOS to start. Just be careful leaving - there are a pair of stone ward gargoyle NPCs on the other side of the rock fall that now 'blocks' the entrance to this area who hit for up to 260 and respawn very quickly. For raid setup purposes just jump your whole raid down and splat em then have a cleric DA down and rez. Easy :cool: )
