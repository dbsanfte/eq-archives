Naelyan
Well, you missed the fableds, so that's out of the question.... if you're beginning your epic, then you're probably not high enough to get any PoP raid drops..... If you have a guild that raids, try to get something there, if not, try to get on open raids, maybe you'll get lucky, or lead a raid yourself.... Greenmist is also an option, its a decent little quest to do (though time consuming, but about 1/10 of what the epic is), though its 1HS, not 2HS... try to find someone with a deal on a Widebladed Greatsword, War Marshall's Bladed Staff, Argent Protector..... any of those would do you well (except for possibly the last one) until 60-65. Unless you're right set against the idea, maybe look into some Piercing weapons like Narandi's Lance or Two-Tined Pitchfork, usually piercers are a bit cheaper than 2HS are. Other than that, I don't know what to suggest, I don't have the old world/kunark/velious knowledge that a lot of others do, as I didn't get the chance to play EQ back in the day. Luclin has some damn good weapons (SSRA, VT, even just some named), but you'll either need a guild or else a very strong group of friends to take most of them down.
