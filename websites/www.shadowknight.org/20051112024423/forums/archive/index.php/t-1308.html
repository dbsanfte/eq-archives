There are actually 2 points for zone out, but they are in the south and east wings. You will pass the one in the South, then pass Drusella spawn point to get to the one in the East. But to even get that far, you either need the key from the West, which in turn needs a key from the North (both no rent), OR take a rogue with you that has Pick Locks 201+. Best bet, take and person with evac with you.

As you can see, getting to Drusella is not easy, so you will not be waiting for someone to call you to come get it. On the other hand, if you know someone who is going there, tell them to grab it because this can be MQ'd.

Charasis or Howling stones as it's more commonly called is one of my fave zones. LOL in fact I spent so much time in here, i was non kos in mistmoore by the time I got to that bit in the greenmist quest. There's another way to east wing if you can't get a hold of a rogue, and don't have hours available to get all the keys. It basically revolves round call of hero and grav flux.

Pull the 2 golems and wanderer from the east wing door. lev your mage and get your grav fluxer to duel him.... mage then runs to east door easily since he now has altitude and call of hero's the rest of the group up. Camp to reset duel, and voila... easy access to east.

The other downside to HS (apart from needing a porter or gate potion to get out) is that just about every undead mob harm touches. This can really sting if you're pulling, so be careful. It's also easy to get lots of mobs at once if you're not careful, so having a very good chanter or a couple of ae happy wizzies will keep you on top of the zone.

This zone in my opinion is the best xp in the game if you can get in to south or east wings. Whilst the new cazic thule gives more xp per mob, you can't kill huge quantities in there like you can in HS. The mobs here have realistic hp, as opposed to the vastly inflated ooo lets give that moss snake 50000 hp cuz this is an expansion type of mentality that the game is rapidly becoming... he heh but thats another rant.

Good luck with the mirror piece, and avoid the carpets :D
