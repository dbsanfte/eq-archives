Jupke
It has something to do with your aggro.

All freaking mobs have INSAIN regen in pop planes when they dont have aggro. So when kiting them, this happens a lot at low health, they sometimes loose aggro for an instant, they also warp a bit sometimes, cause you ran out of range and their non aggro regen kicks in.
This should account for up to 10-20% in less then a tick.

Best solution is when they are at 20% or less hp, stay very close to them, so they dont warp/loose aggro.

I cant tell for sure if this is your problem, i am just talking from personal experiances in PoS.

To see their insain regen, kite one to half and FD and be ready to be dazzled.
