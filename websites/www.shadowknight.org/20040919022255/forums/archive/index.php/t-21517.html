During EXP sewers instance, nameds are supposed to pop right when we enter the instance or after killing a large bunch of mobs and progressing through the sewer ?

My understanding and experience is that after someone enters the expedition, X number of mobs will not be spawned. After roughly 20 minutes those X number of mobs that were not spawned initially will spawn. These X number of spawn locations have the chance of spawning a named.

As far as getting 3 in a row with no named. That is definitely possible. I think I've seen up to 5 in a row with no named in them. But on average, for me i think it still averages about 1 named for every 2 dungeons, and sometimes you will get 2 or 3 named in a dungeon to bring it to a 1 to 2 ratio.

As far as dificulty, I don't think there is any difference between the normal trash mobs in the xp and trial version. What is more difficult is anything that happens after certain events in the trial version. For example, in the lair expedition, the mobs that spawn after the initial hand in seem to be higher in level and hit harder. At least thats the way it seems to me.
