One issue when using a DC pet is that you need to be very aware of the amount of damage you and the Chanter are doing to the mob versus the amoujnt the pet does. Due to the power of DC pets, they have special Exp rules. If the pet does more than 50% of the damage it take 75% of the Exp. It can be frustrating to have just finished kiting a Diaku in Tactics, and only get a 1% bump. Make sure you outdamage the pet. Under 50%, the pet takes much less than half the Exp, so it is worth it if there are plenty of mobs to kill. Just watch the damage ratio.

Chanter Slows Target mobs and heals SK. I usually two-box the shammy, so I keep her role pretty basic.

Using this process, you can mow through mobs faster than whole groups can, and the Exp is insanely nice. If you know a good enchanter to group up with, this should work nicely for a nonstandard group

Two places I kited extensively before hitting elemental planes - The Deep and Tactics. In both places, I have a DoT which I use for a timer on my snare - depending on my luck with resists, I choose the two minute Ignite Blood or the one minute Asystol. Using the two minute Festering Darkness, I can predict when to resnare a mob and when I have time for other activities.
In The Deep, I tend to lose aggro to Goner if I am in a lazy mood and don't use more than 3 DoT's. So, I use Clinging Darkness and spam it half a dozen times before sending in the pet. In Tactics, holding aggro was simple. Pull with Festering Darkness or Aura of Darkness. Use Asystol or Aura of Darkness for my timer since the mobs resist snares a tiny bit more than in The Deep.
