1) pet is nice, but not awesome.
2) SK's can solo, stick to undead or things in wide open places where you can fear the mob then chase after it beating on it's back (it's called fear kiting)
3) Ogre's have good sta and good HP to start with, so makes the beginning easier.
4) Orge's can't be stunned from the front

Orge SK's can solo, would just stick to the points in 2.
