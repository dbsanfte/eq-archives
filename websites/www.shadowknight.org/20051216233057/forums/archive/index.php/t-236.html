04-01-2002, 10:43 AM
Yes, the pet begging tip was from me. I've tested it and it does work, though it is tedious.

If your Cha is below 100, you have less than a 1% chance per beg to get items back (or so my findings went). If you have cha at 100, it jumps up to (roughly) 5% chance per beg. Of course the higher your Cha, the higher the percentage will be to get your item back from pet.

This was tested at a begging skill of 200. All the times prior to having begging at 200 resulted in nothing being begged back. But then again, I never had my Cha over 100 during those times either, so you might be able to get items back prior to a 200 skill level if you have very high Cha to compensate the lower skill level.

I have tested it by handing pet a tradeable weapon and someone else with high Cha and high begging skill, and they were able to beg the item from pet.
