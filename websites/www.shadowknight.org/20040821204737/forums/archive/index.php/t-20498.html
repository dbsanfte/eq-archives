Manamven Tor'Kurr
Generally the only drug that's going to show past 4-7 days on any drug screening is weed, which can be detected up to a month later. The only exception is a hair test which can detect quite a bit back depending on where they got the hair from (since not everyone shaves all thier body hair and it could have been with you since before taking the drugs). But in most states, companies cannot hold anything past 3 months old against you on that show on the hair tests. Hell, in some states hair tests won't even hold up in court.

As for the saliva tests, mouthwash could beat it as long as it's not testing for alcohol. If they are, you're screwed if you use the mouthwash. Just lay off the weed and other shit for a week or two and you guys should be fine with just a saliva test. They're not 100% accurate anyways (supposively 99%), just kinda an insta-check sort of deal. They're most likely just doing a quick little check on you guys and if anything shows up, they'll then give you either the hair test or the piss test.
