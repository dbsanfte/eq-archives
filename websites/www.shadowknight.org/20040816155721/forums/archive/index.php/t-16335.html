Rhowne
Darkforge (Temple of Sol Ro - most base parts from Lizards in Feerott, 3 pieces used in the epic)
Dark Runed (Thurg - base parts from Kael giants)
Blood Lord's (Skyshrine - base parts from HoT in ToV)
Malevolent (Kael - base parts from W/NToV non-named)
Emblazoned Souls (Ornate - BoT/Tactics/HoH/Tower of Sol Ro/etc)
Grimror's (Elemental - Air/Earth/Water/Fire, Time for Bp's)

The Katta quest armor is crap, so I wouldn't even bother with that. Searching on Alla's quest lists in the appropriate zone should yield you the info.
