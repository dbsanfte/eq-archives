The HoH soloing usually occurs in the basement as far as I know versus the entrance mobs you speak of. I think those basement mobs are higher level and give better exp then the ones at the zone in near the GY.

The soloing in Fire usually occurs at the Tables. Your right the exp is very nice and I would say at 8 minutes per kill using only festering and asystole your doing pretty well. It takes me between 10 and 15 minutes to kite a single mob down and I throw in Blood of Inruku and the occasional Bond of Inruku.

I hate using asystole there because of the resists I get. Those mobs can sometimes resist darkness 4 or 5 times at a stretch and the same can go for Asystole / Dark Constriction. I prefer Blood of Inruku + Blood of Hate as that never gets resisted and I dont waste any mana that way.
