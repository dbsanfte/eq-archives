Mathas
The reports only drop off of specific guards.

Eye - A vigilum cohortis
Heart - A lictor of the heart
Hand - A signeferi of the hand
Shoulder - A custus armorum

The drops are rare enough that it would be hard to tell a difference between the ground level and the upper level, as most people I've seen just move to upper and farm there.

And yes, you and a healer could do it, provided you're careful with your pulls. Slow is nice, and cc is nice(pathing isn't the best), but it's certainly doable without. We've taken 4 level 60 guards at once with sk/shamam/echanter, and barely broke a sweat.

Be prepared to spend a LOT of hours there.
