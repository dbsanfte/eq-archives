Camby_VZ
Silithis Sisnta - Not very hard ... max hit w/ me tanking about 780 ...
Pretty straight up fight. Hail, get agro .. eat the HT .. bring him back to your compadres .. get him slowed .. cake walk.

Completed with 2 shaman ( 1 boxed ) ranger & druid.


Gubblegrot Smashfist - Hardest of the 4 IMO ... max hit w/ me tanking 830 ... had to burn Leechcurse cuz he didn't take slow very well ( turgur's after malos ) He hits pretty quick ( seems to be a bit more resistant that the other 3 ) so I'd suggest a bit more healing unless your shaman has the GoD slow, just to be on the safe side.

Completed with 2 shaman ( 1 boxed ) ranger & druid


Skurza Slickutt - Seemingly not very hard ... max hit w/ warrior tanking ( no disc ) 780 ... Not hard to find, paths to an island just south ( on the map ) of the iksar tents in swamp. The Trak tap is easily resisted with my buffed 410ish MR. Don't think it landed on anyone in the party cept the gimply equipped boxed chanter. Warrior that tanked him has roughly 9k unbuffed HP. Not very resistant to any spells ... wizard had a hay day unloading bombs.

Completed with Warrior / SK / Druid / Cleric / Wizard / 2 Enchanters ( 1 boxed )


Giligattabus Igglebix - Very Easy ... max hit " unknown " he only hit me 2 times for 188 ... Easily took Dark on first cast from me ( lvl 70 / festering ) and never resisted any other castings. I then loaded up Blood of Inruku / Blood of Hate / FR DoT / sent pet and just kept him darked and DoT'ed occasionally casting Terror of Discord to maintain high agro while lvl 68 pet and lvl 70 paladin beat away on his back side. This mob can be easily solo'd if you have the patients and mana pool, I'd estimate 15 minutes w/ pet and DoTs.

Completed with SK / pet / Paladin ( Dark / DoT up / Agro-kite ... 5 minutes to kill )

All four have a good deal of HP so I'd suggest bringing dps to the fights to bring them all down quick. Seem to always be up, quick respawn time and always drop their head on death.
