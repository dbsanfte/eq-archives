Tillesia
This is my take on the issues raised in brael's recent thread - i'm not sure of how to put up a poll so if someone could please do so, i think we could get some more statistical input as to the relative support each issue gets.

1. [Brael] Damage mitigation through spells, abilities, discs, etc... to compete with Paladin stuns.

I think this is almost universally approved, and gives the devs the point that we need to close the pally/SK gap while giving them adequate discretion as to the many ways this is possible. :D

2. [Brael] Removal of or toggle added to spell casting subtlety, toggle being preferred

Arguments for: Obviously broken AA. Might be an easy fix that can be quickly addressed and replaced by another option.

Arguments against: Affects only a small number of high-end SKs. Issue list should address problems of the class as a whole, and the small proportion of SKs that would benefit from this list item does not justify using one of the 10 slots in lieu of a more general request while we have so many competing issues.

3. [Brael] Revision of Leech Touch, it costs more aa but does less of a hp swing than the cheaper skill using it's same timer and cannot be focused.

This is an item where the argument is about relative importance and relevance to the SK community as a whole v. other outstanding issues/requests.

4. [others] Addition of rez ability - either as necro rez or an SK line.

Arguments for: SK utility is sorely lacking, especially as FD pulling becomes ever more irrelevant. Rez ability would increase SK desireability and utility in single groups and other non-"traditional group" situations as it would help reduce downtime and corpse recovery times and general group frustration level (not even taking into account xp benefits). To argue that a rez function is opposed to the SK philosophy is to say that zevfeer's bite and auras should be removed and that summon corpse was a mistake - while our buffs are all self-only, much of our utility (and indeed almost the entirety of our role in EQ) is group-oriented.

Arguments against: SK rez is counter to the entire philosophy of playing an SK. Not only is it counter to the SK philosophy, it is unprecedented and unbalancing to grant a completely new ability to a class that already has enough balancing problems. Addition of SK rez would not help our desirability in raids and hardly so in groups. Added utility is just more ammunition for knight-bashers who say "see you have utility, you don't need to tank any better!" without regard to the fact we're beginning to fail in our primary role. Thus, rez ability is counter productive to our main goal of tanking viability.

5. [others] Re-order the priority of firing of procs such that innate procs go off before weapon procs.

Few argue against this, but instead say that there are other issues to be addressed first.

My hope is to create a poll that is meant to discern how much support each issue between 2-5 have in the general SK community.

We need something to compete with stuns for tanking; we arguably need pulling improvements, which would indirectly be a counter to their healing ability; and we need a utility option to compete with rez, which may not ultimately have anything at all to do with rezzing.
