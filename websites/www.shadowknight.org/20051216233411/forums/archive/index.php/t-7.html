Skurrge,

All factions have a max cap at the high end and the low end.

There used to be a faction cap that would keep you from increasing all secondary factions once you had maxed the primary faction from whatever action you were taking.

There was a change over a year ago that would allow you to continue to get secondary faction increases even if the primary was capped. If you ate a death to the primary faction, I believe it freed up some "room" under the faction cap so you could continue working up the secondary factions, but I never tested it.

For instance, turning in bone chips gives

+Troopers of Cabilis
+Crusaders of Greenmist
+Scaled Mystics
+Cabilis Residents
+Swifttails

Prior to the change if you hit the cap on Troopers of Cabilis, turning in more chips would not increase any of the others even if they were still far below their cap.

So, when I started turning in chips, I had already maxed my Troopers faction at Indifferent. However, I could still turn in chips and get + hits to the other 4 factions. Unfortunately for a Rallos Ogre, they all capped at indifferent. Scaled Mystics and Crusaders of Greenmist started off I am estimating 50 faction points lower than Cabilis Resisidents and Swifttails as they did not hit indifferent until I turned in another 200 chips.
