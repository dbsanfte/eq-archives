Into HS i went, some guildies kind enough to camp there the night before heard my call as they were on twinks ( so as not to be able to buff me ) logged in. Upon arriving to HS the buffs rained down....Virtue, F7, Boar, VoQ, VQ, Ferine.

The 2 East golems were pulled....dead within seconds. Already sowed i DMF'd myself and made the unusually difficult sprint across ....i say unusal cause for whatever reason it took me 6 tries to get across. Finally at the top of the east there was one more golem to kill, snared and deaded.

To my unknown knowledge in the room across from Drusella a 61 necro had camped out previously and through the eyes of the frozen rogue i watched as teh eye came in. The eye stared at the rogue for a few seconds and asked me if i was in there for a reason?....( no jackass, i come to this zone all the time ) ....I replied yes i was there for Drusella and he responded with well you have 2 minutes to take her before i do ....

I easily made it around to teh necro without him seeing me as all the mobs on the way were undead.
