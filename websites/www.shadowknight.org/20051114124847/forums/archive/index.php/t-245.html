Spend 2-3 days in the kael arena with a good, balanced, experienced group of 54+. If you're not 54+ ask to tag along for faction. One good group can take out all the Senior Guards, Legionnaires, Troopers, and Adjuntants faily easily; the faction hits are great, and you have a chance for some really good drops. They also drop the helmets that u can use to speed up your SS faction once you are non-KOS.

On our server, the kael zone is usually pretty sparse during the day, but at around 5pm CST it fills up fast. So if you have the time to go early in the mornings and afternoons with a group, you can score some big faction hits and good camp spots.
