Technically you could probably farm the zone in with the shaman bot. However, if your magelo is current, I would be extremely cautious. They hit for about the same as HoH/PoT mobs, but seem to hit faster and for max more often, at least that I've noticed. And they summon. And are immune to snare. And have aggro range as far as you can cast unless you have an extended range item. And except for the wanderers almost always come in pairs so you'll have to split them. Don't forget you can't zone out either to get away, you have to gate out.

After slow landed, your shaman wouldn't be doing much besides healing, which means the fight will be drug out even longer, meaning the shaman will be doing nothing but healing for even longer. Once an HoH mob is slowed I can sometimes tank it (with a full group of course) without losing much in the way of hps. That doesn't happen with sol ro mobs even after slow. They hit, and you get hurt, often.
