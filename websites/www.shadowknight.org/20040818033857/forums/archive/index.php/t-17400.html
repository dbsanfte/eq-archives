Krazaak
Maybe possible, but definitely very difficult.

The run to the prep spot is very dangerous. If you do make it there and FD, you'll have to regen HP/mana while FD, then /q to clear all the aggro.

Two roamers pass through that area, you should be able to take either individually so long as you regen up before you /q.

Drusella herself can be snared and you'll need to use that to split her. Five other mobs spawn in her room and you'll have to be especially careful when FD splitting her to not have FD broken by her casting. Fortunately, most if not all of the adds are non-casters. There's a chance of one of the adds being a named, which does hit significantly harder, adding to the difficulty.


Fully buffed, you might be able to pull it off.
