1. Running seals? How? Marl is on Kerra Isle--I know this much.

You go to Kerra Island, mem gather shadows, cast it... (Kerrans will attack you and it gets annoying) run to Marl Kastane who is located in one of the huts.
Hail Marl Kastane. ( With invis off.. )
Say, "I have the will of Innoruuk."
Say, "What prophecy?"
He gives you the the "Seal of kastane"
You will need to do faction work to get into paineel, just go to the warrens and kill there for two hours, that's all you need. Hail one of the guards by the noobie area and he'll give you a key, go to Paineel and find Gerot Kastane.

Go to where he is standing, then hide and con him, if you are indifferent then give him the "Seal of Kastane". Once you have your faction good with him he will talk to you. Delete the letter he gives you. Go back to Kerra Isle and do the same thing that I just told you. Once you get your faction fixed then turn the letter into Marl which you can get the rest of the epic quest from Marl.
Just keep repeating till Gerot talks to you and call you his hero.

2. So I've killed a lot of epic mobs in the Hole and in CoM on raids... the Shammy epic and the Ranger epic to be exact-- does this raise or lower truespirit?

Just run seals if you're worried about it.

Just run seals if you're worried about it.

4. Is it still possible to do it the same way Shammy's do? Test of Paitence or something like that.. the truespirits who spawn in the Field of Bone after a certain outcast iksar is killed I think are the thing to turn into.

Just run seals if you're worried about it.
