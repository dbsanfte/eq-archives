This works well vs casting mobs.....

Pull WITH a pet...put pet on guard duty back at camp....do your pull....run back to your pet and hit the "pet back off" untill the mobs are in sight of your pull location...let your pet attack the caster and then feign.

Wait for the rest of the mobs to go home, then pick off the mob fighting your pet. The rest of the mobs don't aggro the pet, as it's an npc. Works best vs casters (assuming your resists are good), because melee mobs will mow through your pet.

Enjoy!

Honestly, sometimes the oldest techniques work the best. If you want to split a difficult room, (like BM in Velks) snare a mob, in BM camps case, one of the broodlings, or any of the 4, except the BM (resistant). After you snare the mob, pull all of the mobs out of the room, then Feign Death. After you FD, let all mobs return to the original spawn point, and have someone tag the snared mob, or if it's pulled back from the camp enough, just simply stand up, hit the mob, and pull it.
