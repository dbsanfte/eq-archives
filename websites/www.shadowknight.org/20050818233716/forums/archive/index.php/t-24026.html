Theory:
The "real" version, which in essence is a fake, is transfering your damage to an invisible Pyrique Redwing. Once you kill the invisible form the real Pyrique Redwing will reappear.

How it's done:
Setup your group in the Tower just before hers. Kill the 4-5 mobs inhabiting the tower and setup camp. Finish any last minute buffs.

The first part is very easy. Have your MA pull her out of her tower. She quads for 900s, nothing else. Healers use quick heals to keep the MA up, as she will despawn after getting her down to 75%.

She will then disappear and 6 shadows of her will spawn back in here tower. Just a side note: dont aggro the shadows until you are ready to begin..5 of them are "fakes" and cast a 1500dmg single target nuke, and they will proceed to nuke anyone who gains aggro during your prep time.

Now you will need 6 tanks for this encounter. Give each tank a shadow to tank, and give each tank a cleric/healer. I recommend putting back up healers on each tank as well (see why later).

As you engage the 6 different shadows 5 of them will hit for 100-200s or less and nuke for 1500. The 6th shadow is the "real" version that hits for 900 damage.

Therefore, as tanks gain aggro, the one who has the "real" version needs to call engage for the rest of the raid and the rest of the raid will /assist said tank..and engage on the "real" version.

All backup healers (Druids) will then switch from healing the other 5 tanks (clerics will remain on their appointed tanks regardless), and will proceed to dps the "real" version. NOTE: IF you are the backup healer and your tank has the "real" version, continue to heal with the appointed cleric, as you dont want the tank to die.

Here comes the confusing part. After hitting on the "real" version for a few seconds all 6 shadows will shimmmer and reappear. This is normal, but it will appear as you arent gaining any ground...read further.

In all actuality you are gaining ground, as the invisible version is being hurt...you just dont know that.

Rinse and repeat: Have the tank that is tanking the "real" version call engage and go from there.

You will do this about 10-20 times, depending on your dps, and eventually the real Pyrique Redwing will reappear at 100%. Healers fast heal the MA and proceed to give her a dirt nap.

Overall a fun encounter.
