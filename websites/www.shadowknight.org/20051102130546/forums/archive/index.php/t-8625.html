It was a PITA to split these to solo them at 61 too. They have the new PoP "feature" of not forgetting you when they get back to their spawn spots.

There is one nightstalker away from the others that comes single, but you gotta be damn lucky to not get two when you try to pull another one, and they almost never forget you. They also share social agro and tell their friends about you too, so pretty soon you pull and get 4 or 5 mobs...most of which don't forget you when they reset.

Once you get one single, they are pretty easy to fear kite tho. Resists are pretty low. XP isn't great, but beats the hell outta sitting around LFG. The only drops I got off them were occasional BDs.

After a couple hours it was obvious that these weren't worth the trouble with the mana loss from FDing constantly, not regening mana like normal while FDed, and the trouble keeping up a pet. Was just too much of a hassle for limited rewards. /shrug

First, you need to be able to outrun the mobs:
Run3, Jboots, horse, sower in grp or sow potions, this is mandatory
You get some distance, stop for like half a second, and cast FD.
Playing in 3rd person view helps alot for this.

Try bringing a second tank in group and ask him to tag your pulls while you stay FD.
To break some camps you will have to stay FD during a few fights, or if you are crazy like me, you can build aggro on adds and kitte em :D
