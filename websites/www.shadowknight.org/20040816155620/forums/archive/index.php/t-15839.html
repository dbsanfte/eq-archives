Drakonn
Its more of an event than a quest.

You have to enter the Maze in Plane of Nightmare (some pre-flagging required, although I forget exactly what).

The event consists mostly of following an NPC around as he collects parts of a dagger... in each room he reaches an increasing amount of mobs pop. Finally he reaches the end and A Construct of Nightmares pops, boss hitting for around 1000+ ... he can drop the Black Bastardsword along with a few other items.

I believe the max amount allowed into the quest at any 1 time is 3 groups, and you'll need all 3 to beat it.

Hope that helps, good luck.
