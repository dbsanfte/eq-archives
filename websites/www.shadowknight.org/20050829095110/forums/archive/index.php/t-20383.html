I don't know why you'd dump the shaman. You can 2-box the pair all the way to 65 and beyond. SK/Shaman is probably the best 2-box combo there is, and once your shaman has all his spells to 65 and canni 5, the two of you can go many places where once a full group of 55+'s were necessary.

As far as soloing, the shaman has far greater soloing potential, and is a decent soloing class (good, but not great).

I would imagine that with the combo and reasonably decent gear on both, you should be able to level both to 65 in about 20 days played or less.

Places to go...

DSP to 40 (work Velious faction in during this time, if necessary).
40-50 - Gunthak, Dulak's, DL, Nurga, Jagged Pine
50-60 - PoJ jail cells, ME, Acrylia Caverns, Nadox, Sebilis (after shaman is 58) PoD, PoN, PoI
60-65 - PoI, PoN, PoD, PoV, PoS, Droga, CoD, BoT, HoH

Check out the ZEM thread on this board for other places with good xp modifiers.
