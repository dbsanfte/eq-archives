window
DS DPS depends on how often you're being hit.
bard DS go in short buff box (they're songs) so they stack with everything that doesn't go in short buff box i believe. i'm not sure about that but i think that's how it is.

anyway, DS DPS = DS damage / hits/second.

so, if you have a 50 point DS and are getting hit once every .5 seconds, the DS will do 100 dps.
if the mobs are being shaman slowed, you'd be getting hit once every 2 seconds instead, and the DS would do 25 DPS.
if the mobs hit once every second and you tank 5 at a time, a 50pt DS will do 250 DPS.
