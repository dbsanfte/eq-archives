Well, the numbers seem to show him being able to lifetap + HT her to death no problem assuming the fight doesn't last a very long time.

He has 3355mana. Spirit Tap costs 144 mana, which means he can cast it 23 times, and with an ID4 item should be able to do over 2500dmg with taps alone if he averages 115 a tap (probably about right with an ID4 item in pvp). Unless the bard starts mana draining as soon as the fight starts or you fight in an area that lets them run out of spell range for long periods of time you should be able to tap + disc HT the bard dead.

Also, spirit tap has a very fast recast of 2.5 seconds. It would take you just over a minute to do that 2500dmg with taps. If the bard does not either: A). mana drain right away. B). run out of spell range (this might not be possible for them if you fight somewhere like the bazaar arena, try to fight there if possible) they are probably going to get low enough on hps to where you can disc HT them. A lot of bards i have faced in pvp don't mana drain right away. Some dot you and run around, some even melee you and try to use their ds to hurt you.

Anyway, Kaleyen is overmatched on level but he has weapons to win if the bard makes a mistake.
