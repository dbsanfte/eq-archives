Today I had a user report a post because of the load time caused by a users sig.

Looking over the post, there were 3 large sig files, 120k, 70k, and 90k. One was a flash animation and the other two were just images.

While I know it's very nice to have a large, detailed picture of your character on your posts, I think we need to come up with a compromise between the people with slower hardware/connections, and the people with gratuitous sigs.

We don't have the traffic of the safehouse, so I don't want to be as limited as them. For the record, they allow a max 15kb sig file.

For starters, I would suggest no Java applets (the ripple water thing that is soooo old) or flash animations, and keep the sigs under 50kb, and of reasonable dimensions. This means no 400 pixel tall sigs. Keep them horizontally minded to save screen space.

Please discuss and let's come with a reasonable compromise.
