Even for Iksar, faction tends to be of little import, unless you want to group with friends in certain zones and need to travel (and you aren't level 30 yet).

Iksar can level to ~34 in zones that are adjacent to Cabilis. They won't be attacked in Shadowhaven or Shar Vahl, and can get Thurgadin and Neriak faction quickly and without much fuss.

If you want to get around and see the world, a gnome or human is probably the better bet, though. Ogres and trolls have good faction in Neriak if I am not mistaken, so they have it a bit better as well.

But if you're not planning on running all over the place until you ding 30, Iksar faction is a non-issue, IMO.
