Keevel
SK's generally recommend to use a bit of everything. Not because it's terribly hard to skill up at later levels, but because we like to keep it tight. And we want to be prepared, no matter what kind of upgrade becomes available, to use our upgrade to it's capacity.

You'll See 2HB and 2HP because in general they are a little cheaper than 2HS because of supply demand.

At high levels SK's generally go 2 handers to get good dps plus have time to cast. Though there is a school of SK thought that uses 1handers, and you'll spend many of your SK's lower levels with a 1 Hander and shiled, in part due to the extra AC/Stats you can get with a shield.

SK's take hits better than monks (comprabably equipped)
SK's have better agro control than monks
Monks have more dps (and a ghetto lull at higher end)
