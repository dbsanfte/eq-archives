I'd suggest to try get a healer and hit pon or poi.
Trio is easy enough.

Else you want somewhere you can kite.

Maidens Eye is good for that round level 50. Tash will enable the SK's snare to stick and you can agro kite the mobs to death.

You can even do DL at that level. Fear kiting in DL is great fun.

If you get set up, you can also agro kite in PON or POD.

Problem is you will cop a couple of deaths without a healer, but the xp rate will be sooooo much higher it would be well worth it.

Frankly though, you will do much better grouping at that level range and that class combo.

Experience is very good in Velks. It's kind of empty a lot on our server, waxing and waning from 12-14 people to just us and the always present rotating pair of 65's farming frenzy for chitin drops.

Got 53 and 54 by holding the left wall room and pulling the back wall, and the ramps. We have been taking any LFG types who zone in and ask, so as a pair, a trio, or up to four of us, usually without a healer, this is still a pretty viable exp camp.

We've also each gotten a no drop Forgotten Ebony Earring, and I have gotten a no drop Velium Etched Helm, both from the Fierce Blizzard Hunter.

The helm is pretty good if you're not a twink(and no helmet graphic for the vain melee type), and I've seen more than one level 10 or so melee just sitting in the entrance waiting for these to drop. Two on a kill, about one spawn an hour is the fierce, and LOTZ of these rot.
