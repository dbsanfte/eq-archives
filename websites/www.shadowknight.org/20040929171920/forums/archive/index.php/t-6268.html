Zeralenn
Not being able to be stunned from the front is nice, but really, being stunned is a minor annoyance compared the amount of time you spend sitting around trying to get hit points back if you are soloing or if you are not running with a healer. So if I was choosing between no stun and regen, I'd pick regen every time.

The only other thing that I will say about running a troll, though, is that the Innothule Swamp zone simply sucks. I have a troll shaman who only goes home to train. She moved out of Grobb and into Oggok around level 2 and never looked back. (If you go a little north then almost due west to the zone line, you almost never run into any mobs at all). If you can build your Oggok faction up enough you might even get to the point that the Oggok SK guild will train you, but that won't happen right away.

...Zeralenn
