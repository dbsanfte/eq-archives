Ttuk
As far as PoF goes, all I did was:

1) Set up in the NW corner and get a pet and park it right in the corner. Pet haste helps if you're not planning on killing all the dispellers.

2) Then run along the N wall til you're right next to the fire wall and run through it.

3) Try to F8 anything in the temple.

4) Use any means neccesary to agro it. Arrows don't work well.

5) Train it out through the fire just NW of the fire wall.

6) Pick out an amy and snare it.

7) Immediately FD.

8) Wait for agro clear message, if you FD fast enough the snare fades right after agro is cleared. He then walks away slowly and you tag it off.

They're very easy to straight up melee even w/o any buffs so just pull it back and have your way with it.

Took me about 30 amy kills to get mine. Mine dropped off of a amy warrior if that helps, I had speculated they'd be knight only, just like our armor...
