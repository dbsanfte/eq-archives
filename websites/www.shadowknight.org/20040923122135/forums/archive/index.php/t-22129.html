Vermadas
It makes more sense that SCF is ignoring SA altogether instead of just considering it at level 2, as the original poster suggested.

The base part of the innate proc is the only thing getting critical'd. For example, Black Shroud Strike is 90 dmg base. SCF adds another 90 to it on a successful crit. SA3 makes the proc 3x its base, for 270. 270+90 = 360.

So basically the the SCF calculation is happening before SA is considered instead of after as it should be.

Base + (Base * 3) should be (Base + Base) * 3 or (Base * 3) * 2.
