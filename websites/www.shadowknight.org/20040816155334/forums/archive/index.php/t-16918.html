Soulfiend lance is a fun quest to complete. To do it, you need Paineel faction. If you don't have good faction already, after you get your Darkforge Armor, head to the Warrens and proceed to decimate every kobold you see, over and over and over and over. If you can kill the Cave Bat Lord, he sometimes drops Azzar's Dreadful Hat.

Every now and then, peek out and /con the guards. It will take you quite a few hours to get past KoS. After that it goes fast. Once you are indifferent, you can give Azzar his hat (In the room above the entrance area with the glass mirrors) for a generous faction boost with the Heretics and a serious hit to caster guilds of Erudin (Bah, who cares.)

Once you are amiable you will have enough faction to get the key to Paineel. (You'll need it for epic later) Head through the Warrens to Stonebrunt then journey northward to a spot southwestish of the Kitty Village in a patch of trees. You will find a red wisp "a spirit."

I reccommend shouting in zone that you are about to do a quest and ask nicely that people clear out of the kitty City to avoid aggro from your quest mob. he WILL aggro anyone not on good terms with the Heretics.

Hand the spirit the Grimy Lance. (You did bring it, right?) you will recieve an idol, and Riddosan the Unliving will spawn and begin to buff himself. Give him a moment and he will begin running east to the beginning of the path that leads to the Kitty City. He is KoS to the wildlife, so you might need to kill a few things for him. As he approaches the Kitty City, he will shout across the zone. it's a good idea to repeat your /shout warning so that some curious Ranger or DrOOd won't track him and kill him.

This is where it can get tricky. If you are high level and not kos to the kitties, you're fine. if you are in your 30's and/or are KoS to the kitties, you might want to find a friend for this part. Riddosan will path to Disciple Okarote's tent. You must now kill Disciple Okarote. Do not attempt to fight him in the tent as he is surrounded by healer. Pull him (or have your high level friend do it) down the path from the city a bit and dispatch him there. He will give negative kitty faction. Ghost of Riddosan will spawn in the tent and will stay up for a short time. Run to the tent (invis if you are KOS to kitties) and hand the ghost the idol to recieve:

Soulfiend Lance
