At first he dived in too but since I was hacking on him pretty hard under water and he could not run away there he jumped out again and selo'ed up (I was OOM from his drain at that time or he would have been dead). Since I did not have ToI yet I needed to make sure his resist song (psalm of veeshan) was down before I hit him with a spell. This went on for a while (the moment I jumped out he ran away every time of course) till I was FM. At some point I jumped out and bashed right in time (I think his resist song was just fading), gave him see invis with my bracer of the hidden (so his first slot was full) and cast darkness on him (it landed in one of his last slots so he could not dispel it easily), then a volatis + spear (even that landed now) and some more melee and he was at 30% then HT and dead bard, I don't think I even would have needed DT in this case but I just did not want him to get away. Anyways

Key to killing a bard is speed. If you don't take us down fast yer toast. Also, if they have ancient mezz it'll go something like this:

Mezz (unresistable, but really rare)
Mana drain (Commonish song, unresistable)
Slow (resistable, but since we're mana draining we can spare resists).
Reason not chain mezz-manadraining is that they are some of the few songs with recast times.
Once that's done, yer finished via chants and HoS, and maybe even snare.


Now if they don't have ancient mezz, it'll be more akin to PoV / Regen / Mana drain, Regen being swapped for maybe OoS or something. Your only chances would be:
A ) Try to spear him before he gets up PoV, if he's not bright and starts a duel with it down.
B ) Use any unresistable or hard to resist damage spell you can.

Then hope you get him further then he gets you and HT him... And pray HT lands :p
