Lunatic,

Stop being an idiot.

Of course you get stunned less at higher levels. Your gear is better, adding to stats, which in turn affects your AC and usually your Agi/other stats. I'm pretty convinced that AGI has something to do with how often a mob misses you completely, as we all know that AC is only useful for damage MITIGATION (IE: taking less damage on a successful hit).

What is more, your dodge, parry and repost skills are higher.

Most RPG battle calculations go something like the following:

(Enemy ATK rating + Random chance to hit at base avoidance) - (Any and All increases from base avoidance of player character) = Did they hit you or not?

After the game knows whether or not you've been hit, it calculates the damage based on the power of the weapon, the type of attack, the proficiency of the enemy at that type of attack and your armor and other mitigation.

In addition to this calculation, in EQ there is a seperate roll on whether or not a bash/slam/kick type attack will stun you. But essentially, the mob has to HIT you with a bash/slam/kick in order to stun you.

If it isn't obvious to you by now that the higher you level the less often you will get stunned, not including those mobs who flurry/frenzy/have an inordinately fast attack, then you can just take MY word for it.
