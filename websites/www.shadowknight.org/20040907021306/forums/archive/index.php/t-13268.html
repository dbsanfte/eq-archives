"At 60+ the only thing regen will do is reduce downtime a bit when you are soloing or when you are lying FD between mobs. Other then that, it hardly has any use."


Sorry, but I hardly agree with this comment. Regen has far more use then that, especially at higher levels for a SHD.

Firstly, regen is a wonderful offset to our *lich* series, Pact of Hate ("POH") in particular. It is extremely nice to offset 30HP/tick rather then substantially all of the entire amount go unchecked.

Its also helpful for just after getting a rez, when your HP is low and you are OOM. PoH is a great spell to cast at this time until the chanter, if applicable, can hit the group with c3 or better. Regen helps in these situations also.

Additionally, regen also helps during raids. Blood/EMP takes what--10-15 min or so to kill?? SHD make terrific TM in this situation and the amount of HP that is added during this time adds up to a significant amount of damage that doesnt need to be healed.

It doesnt have to be, however, a raid to experience the benefits of regen. You will get its benefit anytime you are receiving damage and dont die. While many will claim that the amount is insignificant, I can assure you that if you are in an XP group acting as the MT for such group and you XP for any decent length of time, the HP added to you from regen is considerable and nice to have.

Certainly my *list* above is not comprehensive, nor is it intended to be. My only point is that you dont have to like it for it to yield benefits. Regen = good and its good at all levels and for all races.
