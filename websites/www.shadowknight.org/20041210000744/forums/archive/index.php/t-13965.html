Allakhazam is great for quest info. I dont know of too many good ones, other than darkforge, at your level. You could start collecting braids from Sarnak beserkers to get your faction up with the overthere inhabitants. You would get exp and some loot. You could then do the workers sledgemallet quest. You can't use it to gate until level 50 but once you have the hammer you would not have to worry to much about your faction with them. Many of the Kunark dungeons will give negative faction hits with the overthere inhabitants.

Fear kiting is simple. Snare with a darkness, then fear and attack. Works better with engulfing darkness than clinging.
