Suggestions:

I am assuming you can get this, seeing as you already have som Dark Runed :)

Ear - Ivandyr's Hoop +6 AC +6 Wis +6 Int +6 HP - you can quest it if you raise enough pp for the rubies(has taken me a maximum of 8 rubies everytime! :p ), or buy it from a player.

Neck - Shrouded Medallion +12 AC +5 Str +15 vsMg +5 vsFire +5 vsCold +25 Mana - With a solid group in the low 50's you can go to Maiden's and kill the "Xi" if you kill "Four arms" as well you could get lucky and get this:

Waist - Axgoreth's Belt of Defense +20 AC +15 Str +15 Wis

Face - Validus Custodus Plate Visor +8 AC +4 Str -2 Agi +4 HP - These are easy to get for little pp, ask someone you know that hunts guards in Katta if they want to sell, otherwise just auction for it. :)

Shoulder - Tattered Mantle +12 AC +10 vsDis +20 HP - From Gullerback in BW - Gullerback is a strong gorilla so bring a group :)

The rest you can get in Kael raids, Dark Runed, Black bear pelt cloak etc etc. If not, a Spider fur cloak is very easy to come by.

I hope it helps!
