Okay, basically I'll go out on a limb and state that it'd be extremely difficult to be able to have faction good enough, as an evil class/race, with Phenic (the quest giver) to do the quest without MQ or without pacify/harmony/invis/charm tricks, even with a good race illusion. I'll try to explain.

I won't pretend to know all the factions in Katta, but there are several that I do know of.

Validus Custodius - Katta Guards. Not specifically needed for the quest, but makes running around Katta easier. I capped at Kindly I think. Raised by killing vamps in TM. Not that bad to work on because you can do it at the vamp castle trying to get Grolden to spawn anyways, get some merchant loot and plat.

Magus Conglegium - Katta Casters. Not specifically needed for the quest either but some hand ins and the "shades fight" takes place inside the Magus Conglegium building, and it's a lot easier to do this fight without having to clear the greenie guards or trying to stay in the exact center of the platform to avoid getting adds. For hand-ins on the upper floors, there are some dark blues nearby and some can see invis. I capped at Dubious I think, perhaps Indifferent. This faction can be raised by fighting guards in Sanctus Seru or the greenie guards in DSP or Marus Seru.

Nathyn Illuminus - not sure what it's for other than for Nathyn himself whom is part of the quest. Don't remember what I capped at but it's above indifferent. I raised this while vamp killing in TM. I believe all you need is to not be KOS because he instaspawns after a hand-in as an indifferent version anyways.

Concilium Universus - I have NFC what this faction is for but I maxed it in Sanctus Seru. Someone told me it was for raising Phenic faction but I was still scowling after I maxed it so that wasn't it.

You can get some +faction hits by doing some quests to hand in vamp fangs and bloods or something in Katta (invis to the quest area, the quest npc's are dubious) but that was going to take forever plus I had no idea if it would be the right faction or not -- since I already had gone out of my way to max the incorrect faction.

I still don't know what faction Phenic is on or how to raise it. Maybe you can get big +hits from killing Seru or doing the earring quest or killing for VT shards on the upper floors or deeper inside Sanctus Seru than I went), I can't say because I only killed zone in guards.

Unless pride stands in your way, I'd highly recommend MQ. A druid is ideal in that they will not be KoS to anything in Katta, can port around to do the preliminary hand-ins and runarounds, make for a good partner to clear the vamp castle while factioning or waiting for Grolden, plus can do the harmony and invis on you for the final hand-in. Of course, any other good race will do too, but you might need more external help on some parts. My vote is to skip all the factioning (other than what you get while killing vamps waiting for Grolden) and aim for MQ. Everything, i.e. the entire quest, can be done in a few hours except for Valdanov's ashes. Why be dumb like me and spend weeks doing faction? (Okay I had to do the vamp castle but didn't need to spend any time in Sanctus Seru/DSP/Marus Seru).
