As I have read most of the posts people think that giving a Shadow Knight and Palidin a defensive type spell or disc seems to be the general consensous. A few other idea's are giving SK's life tap type shields and Pal's heal type sheilds as 65 spells. I have a new Idea.

Shadow Knights as traditionally associated with hate, pain, and death a spell similar to this might be what we could use. A spell with that would deal a great deal of damage to the mob with the Shadow Knights death might me a new fix. The amount of health the Shadow Knight has, or the amount of damage delt to the Shadow Knight over time are some options.

Basicly it would do this.

Shadow Knight cast's "Final Revenge" (or whatever spell is called)

Option 1:
Shadow Knight Goes and tanks Mob
SK's Hp: 9k buffed
With the SK's death deal 9k damage to the mob.

Option 2:
Shadow Knight goes and tanks Mob
Mob deals: X amount of Damage to Shadow Knight before death
SK dies and the mob takes X amount of damage

This would be very similar for Paladins but they would perhaps take no damage for 1 minute or possibly come back to life w/ full buffs and hp after they die for the first time.

I would be happy with this as it would work for both group and raid encounters. Slightly different but it "Might" save a group/raid or become massive extra dps/dam absorbsion. I think that it would balance some of the problems that Shadow Knight's are now facing. It might not let us Tank GoD encounters but then it might fataly wound/kill the mobs and allow another tank to get on it with less health for Shadow Knights. For paladins using a bit of Tank switching and you could reduce mana use by quite a bit. I'm not posting reuse time/mana cost ect. I don't know if many would think of this as a good fix however I think that it could make use usefull and possibly required on some raids as well as group encounters. This obviously isn't a end all be all fix however I think it would help balance us out.
