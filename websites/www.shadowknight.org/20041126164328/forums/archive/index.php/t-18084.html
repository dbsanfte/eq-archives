Ramgar SK
Not on him in particular, but assassination targets are two to three times the mob as anything else in the dungeon.

They hit twice as hard, twice as fast, and have a good 3-4 times the amount of health.

A slower is a must IMO, and a chealer is too. Warn your healer to heal early on the first one.

Watch out for enchanter assassinations, they can be a lot of trouble with mez and charm.
Nambkab
Also it helps when the tank is the highest level in the group, or at least higher than the average group level. We fortunately had two healers and when the mob finally dropped I had 2% life. Second assassination I was the highest in the group by a couple levels and the mob was a dark blue con and was quite honestly a wimp. So be very prepared or make sure you are the highest level in the group :) .
