Oagan
There are about 8 spawn points/traps around the Kael entrance, and Tanik can be at any of them. Pick a spot about 1/3 of the way and up from the entrance to Kael (where the guards are). The pet should now face away from Kael.

Walk directly in the path that the pet is facing. Walk slowly. Don't run. If you stay on line with the pet, you'll spawn the KDS or Tanik.

I've done this about 20 times for people, because my SK is ally giants, so the KDS is no problem when they pop. Word of mouth has resulted in my getting several tells from strangers asking for help, and if I'm not busy, I'll usually do it. If you know someone that is a pet class and is on Giant faction, they'll find it quicker, because they don't have to run to Kael every time the KDS pops (I presume you're doing this alone and can't solo the KDS).

Tanik's there. Good luck to you. It will be a little slower, since you're doing the Coldain quests and giants are KoS. Just move him (the pet) out a bit.

I usually have my best luck on the GD side, up by the top of the ramp, not the beach side with the trees. Don't waste a lot of time on the spot given by Alla's. It's only one of several, and he can be at any of them.
