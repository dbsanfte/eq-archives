Satist
Okies, zone into Toxxula Forest from Paineel. While you are standing on the porch, hit the backspace key. Providing you have the Legacy of Ykesha expansion, using the backspace key will bring up a map of the zone. Look carefully at the map. there should be a black arrow on it. this arrow is you. it points whichever way you face. Notice, down in the lower corner, there are numbers. They indicate the size of the map.
Now, zoom out on the map until you see a set of circles on it. This is a kobold camp, that should range from conning yellow to blue to you. Odds are, that when you pull one, the others will come too. It's no big deal, for now. As long as you kill one, using HT if you need to, and die each time, you will manage to get the camp spawn broken. These kobolds should last you until level 9, I think. Also look around, and watch the other monsters that wonder by, killing the ones you need to to finish your beginning armor quests. Once you have finished them, you should be level 8-12, depending upon how lucky you were with the drops of crude bronze, etc.
At that point, you can either go to the Warrens, killing kobolds, gaining molars, and getting faction and perhaps armor from the quest, or you can go to field of bone, and kurn's tower. i highly recommend going to kurn's, not for the xp bonus, but because your pet spell requires bone chips as a reagent in order to finish casting it. you will literally gain backpacks full of bonechips in kurn's tower. this is the reason to go there.
even if soloing there is slow, you will see other classes in there, leveling. by all means, shoot them a tell, meet them, group or partner with them if possible, etc. it doesn't matter if they have better armor than you or not, what matters is that by partnering and grouping, you drastically cut downtime, and you meet people that you might eventually need, or get to know better, etc.
