Someone should probably sticky a description of the faction involved. You never change from apprehensive. But each step gives a faction boost, within the apprehensive range, that is *required* to do the next step. If you choose to do it out of order or MQ with someone who has not also done the same steps, expect to lose your MQ pieces. Theres two ways around it. First, invis during the hand in. Put the pieces in the trade window, get invis, hit trade. It will put you up to indif. Second is running seals, but that doesnt do anything about the MQ person unless they want to do it.
