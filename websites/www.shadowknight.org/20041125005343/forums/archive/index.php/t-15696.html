Shields should be about protection...

1) All shields have a default BLOCK percentage similar to Damage bonus... all classes that can use shields benefit from this... warriors should see a flat bonus whenever ANY shield is wielded.

2) WAR/PAL/SK when using a shield, can use /steelwall. This blocks a higher percentage of incoming blows + the shields block mod for 30 seconds... reuseable every 10 minutes. This ability STACKS with /defensive... it is NOT a discipline, but a function of skill with a shield.

3) Warriors should be able to get shields that add to Damage bonus in primary hand. Heck... some of it might even be innate!

4) WAR/PAL/SK can use their shields to defend others as well as themselves. Knights can block for party members at one-third (minimum 1%) of their block ability. Warriors can block for party members using half of their block score, (minimum 2%). This ability STACKS with more warriors/knights in the party. This ability STACKS with /shield for warriors, giving the target the FULL chance to block using the warrior's score while /shield is active.

(previous abilities increase with LEVEL, and are innate as long as you use a shield with a BLOCK mod on it)

Shadowknight:
Shield of Spite

Shadowknights using /steelwall gain an enhanced damage shield while /steelwall is functioning. Each level of Spite also increases the duration of /steelwall. Spite is considered melee damage. (thus stacks with /disc leechcurse)

level 1 50 damage shield, +2 seconds
level 2 150 damage shield, +6 seconds
Level 3 300 damage shield, +14 seconds


Paladin:
Shield of Reversed Polarity

Paladins learn to channel their holy power into their shield during /steelwall and turn their suffering into healing for their party. (including the paladin) Both amount healed and duration increase with AAXP level.

Level 1 3% of damage taken to party +2 seconds
Level 2 7% of damage taken to party, +6 seconds
Level 3 15% of damage taken to party, +14 seconds


Warrior:
Shield of Frustration

Warriors learn to optimize their shield use, blocking more often, and making their opponent incredibly frustrated and desperately wanting to kill the warrior. /steelwall Duration also increases with level.

Level 1 1.5x current block rate, +2 seconds, +150hate per block
Level 2 2x current block rate, +6 seconds, +300hate per block
level 3 3x current block rate, +14 second, +600hate per block

Ideas, modifications, comments? Better ideas?
