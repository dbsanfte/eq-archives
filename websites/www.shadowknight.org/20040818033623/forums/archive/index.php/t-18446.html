Hya, from the top of my head the only quest i c u can do or start at mid level (around 30), are :

* Darkforge
* Soulfiend lance
* Greenmist (1st steps anyway)
* electrum ring (LoY quest in gunthak)
* Luclin twilligth armor
* sarnak braids in OT (faction and XP)

i might be missing a few, but thats about the major ones.

- Luclin armor is not thats good u can buy better in bazaar IF u have the money OR the time to farm for cash.

- Sarnak braids is very nice for raising VS faction although now with luclin and pop its not very important anymore (exept to get the TPing hammer "worker sledge hammer")

- Darkforge is nice and cheap armor and some parts r requierd for the Epic (helm, BP and legs)

- the electrum ring is well worth the effort (in my opinion) AC, STR DEW HP and SvMagic for free ...

- Greenmist is pretty good but u need to work on your faction

- The soulfiend lance is a nice weapon (procs DOT lifetap) i did solo at 35 but with the old CT. i m sure some will say u can buy better in bazaar, and they would be right, but questing the weapon yourself brings a nice feeling when u wield it after. (and u can always sell it later) but u will need to raise paineel faction for it.

I did not get into the details, because u can find them pretty easily in allakhazam or by using the search feature .

Good questin to u...
