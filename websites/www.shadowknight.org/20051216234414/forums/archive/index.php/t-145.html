You've heard two different stories because both are correct depending on what level you are.

If you're 59 or below, the item haste + spell haste combo cap is 80%ish. The only way to reach 100% haste is with a stacking bard haste song. So yes, at these levels you could reach the cap with a FBSS and SoS.

At lvl 60, the item haste + spell haste combo cap is 100% (some say up to 106% haste depending on your class). So at 60, you need at least a 34% haste item to reach 100% haste with SoS.
