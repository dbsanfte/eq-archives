Well, after looking around, I think I've found the PERFECT spot. The only thing it lacks is sellable drops. But the mobs drop an average of about 1pp each, with some as high as 3pp. The place I'm talking about is the Forgotten Halls. It's the instanced zone off Nedaria's Landing. You can go in SOLO as your own expedition. Nothing in the zone aggros, and the things you kill for xp (rats and bats) are NOT social (at least not at the level range I am in). At level 28 I get about a percent XP per kill, and fast kills. No shortage of mobs either, as they respawn pretty quickly.

I love this zone. It is instanced, so it is ALWAYS available when you want it. No trains, no aggro, nothing. Just nice, quiet xp and platinum. Well...back to hunting.
