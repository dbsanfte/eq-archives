Lots of ways to get people in to the trials for flags if you use your imagination. For example trial one, which for poorly geared players is probably the greatest challenge of the sewer trials - with a bard (other classes can sub in but bards own for this) I can usually pacify/invis to Ansharu who you hail, then pacify/invis/itu all the way to the end room, this takes at most about 20 mins. Then using strats you can find elsewhere on these or other boards kill 8 of the 9 stonemites. For my group at this point I can kill someone rez and decess to zone in to swap people in to expedition, they can run in, die and zone back in for summon, lots of time for the expedition - hell you can even kill all stonemites and evac to zone leaving boss up, invite peeps and then lull/invis/itu back to end room (boss mob does not see invis). The specific choice of how to get others in for flags depends on what classes you have available at the time obviously. Set up and have tank attack when everyone is ready - Ancient Kyserops does path kinda fast around room though so be ready if/when invis drops. Pretty much the boss mob is easier than the stonmmites you pull that have the ae rampage imo.

Here's a tip too, do a /dzhelp to see all the expidition commands.

/dzaddplayer <name> will invite a player anywhere to your expedition. CoH and Drag works just fine.

Make sure anyone wanting flags pre-hails the High Priest, it avoids problems later on.

Yes, you are kicked out within about 30 seconds if you leave the expedition inside, so long as not agro'd. Camping a cleric outside can save some time, especially on total wipes, where you don't want to run a naked cleric all the way from PoK or wherever he's bound.
