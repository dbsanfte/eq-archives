Bankers are on Residents faction, as is a mob standing in a tent just outside the bank. Most residents faction mobs are also merchants, and so can not be lower than dubious unless attacked. However, the bankers and a very few other mobs are on residents faction and yet don't have the merchant faction modifier. Always be careful around the bank if you haven't raised your faction with residents enough to buy/sell to any of the mobs in the center vendor section of east cab. Until you get your faction up to apprehensive, you can't really tell by conning one of the merchants what your true cab residents faction is.
