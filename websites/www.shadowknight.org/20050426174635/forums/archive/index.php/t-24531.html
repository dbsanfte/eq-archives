Aelali
You can significantly reduce your faction farming with an iksar Illusion potion. As a Dark Elf SK worshiping Innoruuk, iksar illusion should be able to take you to above the required faction if you have dubious faction un-illusioned. Also, starting faction for you should be enough to get dubious to the necro guild for that one turn in. The downside to the illusion method is that you will need to be illusioned for all of the turn ins, which include a few not in cabalis, so you end up needing 3-4 illusion potions (give or take, been a really long time since I did the Greenmist quest), but if you bought the hero's khukri, then that probably won't be an issue for you.

And is it worth it? Honestly it falls in the same catagory I would put epic 1's, if you can get it early, (i.e. about your lvl) and with only a decent amount of effort, then it's awesome. I got my enchanter's epic when he was 51, soloing almost all of it and having him loot the pieces (I think I got his first piece at 20 or so and just killed the mob(s) for each piece whenever I happened to see them up, so it wasn't a huge drain on my time, just "oh look, ____ is up, guess I'll get that piece"), and his epic was amazingly awesome until 63, when his casted spell haste became better than the clicky haste, but had I waited until he was 65+ to get it, it would have beeen more or less worthless. Same with the SK epic, nowadays if you can get the help to get it close to 50ish, it will be an awesome weapon for you, but if you can't get it done until lvl 65+ then you gain very little with it. The Greenmist won't proc for you until 45, but if you get it by then, it will be an awesome weapon for you until you get much higher lvl (closer to 65+). It will even compete with some of the PoP weapons for SKs just because the proc is so awesome, you can't beat a STR, AC defuffing(and tapping) lifetap DoT (besides the fact the katana animation is cool looking). I used the Greenmist, and Innoruuk's curse until I finally joined a raiding guild and replaced them with GoD weapons (I didn't join a raiding guild until just before GoD was released), mostly because imo at the time the procs more than made up for the ratio and stat differances in PoP weapons pre-Time (but that of course was my opinion).
