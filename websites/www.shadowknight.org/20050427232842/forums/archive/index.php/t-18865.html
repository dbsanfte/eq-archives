Someone mentioned earlier in this topic that the Troll lore in comparison to the Ogre lore made the choice seem almost silly, and that Ogre was the way to go.

Well, when the Trolls arived on Antonica from Kunark, they settled in Grobb, I have not found any lore stating that they were ever in Guk, but of course I could be wrong. The trolls defended their lands against the ogres until the ogres recognized them as a formidable ally and agreed to make peace with them.

Almost everyone I run into always argues the point that the Frogloks threw the Trolls out of Grobb, thus they are weak and stupid. But the fail to recognize one fact, that the mass of the Troll's forces were out combing Norrath for the Grozmok Stone, and the Frogloks attacked after they had been empowered by Mithaniel Marr. Lets see... Blessing from Mith Marr + depleted Troll forces, how could they have lost?

Not to mention, alot of Dark Elves complain about how the Trolls are stinking up there city, but the fact that they didn't turn them away when they came to Neriak can mean one of two things... 1: that the leaders of the Dark Elves realised the Trolls are an ally that they would rather not lose, or 2: that they couldn't turn the trolls away for fear of a bloody battle between the two.

So please, if you're going to consider lore in your choice, don't overlook the Troll SK.
