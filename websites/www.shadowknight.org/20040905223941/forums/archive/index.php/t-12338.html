I'm a Erudite SK. I don't know how far along you are with your alt but keep a couple of things in mind. Erudites are rare cause our starting stats are just bad. Make sure Agi is at least 75. I've found this attribute to be the hardest to get any decent gear for. Also, put the rest of your points into Sta. I know that starting strength is weak but there is plenty of relatively lower priced gear to get strength up to a decent level. Enjoy the character...you will be unique. Take advantage of the warrens nearby. Good loot and xps for the early part of your career.
