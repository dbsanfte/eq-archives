#1A Have high resists, make the chanter waste all kinds of mana on you

#3 User your DDs and spear the poo out of the chanter. Load all your spears, and cast them one after another

#4 Lifetap him (Life Leech and Touch of Volatis work best because of their relativley quicker cast times)

Other stuffs

Watch Others spell messages..if you see him winding up a nice, big, high mana nuke, toss up Harmsheild and let him blow the mana (go ahead and have a seat for the 18 secs and get a lil mana back, your spells cost a whole lot less than his manawise)

Have Mental Corruption up (especially the high Dex SKs), leech off his mana while you beat on him.

USe augs on your weap that have DD procs and lifetaps or use weapons that have those abilities.

USe your own self-imbued DD/Lifetap procs.

Use only the quickest casting and low mana snares (dont use Dooming Darkness and above, they take too long to cast and too much mana, you will get resisted a lot trying to snare the chanter, and thats time you could be beating on him). Clinging Darkness is your best bet.

Dont waste time with your pet, hell just fear it and make it run away or mez/charm it and make it hit you.

USe your discs. Leechcurse is a great one to hold on to till last. You can turn the tide of the battle with this one. I wish I could see their faces when my health suddenly starts going up the more I hit them. use resistant disc as well. Dont waste time on fearless, PCs cant be feared anymore anyway in PvP/

Then of course there is HT :-) I like to save it for last. I dont like my opponent to have a clue as to wether I have HT up or not. Sometimes its good PsyOps to hit UA even when HT isnt up just to mess with them and perhaps get them operating defensively. HT is best as a finisher.

First of all, DConquest, you're making up special rules to gimp yourself by not using HT. The question was, how does an SK fight an ENC and vice versa, not how do they fight each other if the SK is handicapped. An SK will generally rape anyone except a Bard or in some cases a Paladin in a duel.

Now then, on to the main post.

You say it's a game of Assassins on a blue server. How exactly is that going to work? I will make some assumptions and then give you my advice. Keep in mind my specific experience here is limited...I've never played a high level anything, and I've never played as an Enchanter or SK...and I haven't played since before LDoN came out.

I do have a lot of general PvP knowlege though from playing on Tallon Zek and Sullon Zek for a long time, and I am pretty imaginative in squeezing the advantage out of every small detail.

The first thing I am interested in is how this Assasins game is going to be organized. Here is my assumption: I am assuming that you can challenge him anywhere, any time, in a duel, and he must accept. This gives you a big advantage...even bigger in most ways than you would get being the attacker on a PvP server. Of course the guy who's coming after you has that same advantage over you, so that's something you need to keep in mind defensively. But you're asking about the offense here.

Before I forget, the second big advantage you have is that you know what class you're going up against, and he doesn't - not only that, you know exactly who this guy is and can figure out what gear, personality and skills he has before hand. Again, your attacker has this same edge on you.

Now then...remember some of the things these guys are telling you -

"it doesn take much mana to launch a Spear"

Spears are disease resisted. I've resisted many spears easily in gear that was modest (though resist oriented) for a level 52. I don't know how resist rules have changed since then, or how things are different at 65, but one idea is to pump your DR through the roof. You won't be able to stop his taps unfortunately unless your MR is insane (or DR or whatever the appropriate antitap resist is).

The charmed pet is the best thing you've got on him. Pick a zone with an absolutely brutal pet you can charm with DC and attack him there. Wait till he blows his DT (Death Touch, that is, AA'd Harm Touch)- otherwise you WILL lose. Try to get him injured and LOM and unsuspicious. Also try to get him when he has poor spells up, and doesn't have good MR gear up, or many buffs on...you must keep him from dispelling your pet. Ask the Enchanter boards how to do this...it involves charming the pet, buffing the pet, breaking the charm, rooting the pet and then recharming the pet I think.

One possible trick too is to attack him in a zone he has bad faction in...or you can charm the mobs he's exping on, buff them, break charm and then memblur them so they are stronger than he expects. This isn't likely to be a great plan though since it won't be that effective and once he catches on you'll have lost surprise. Plus the blue server GMs might give you a hard time for doing it.

Unfortunately he'll probably know to raise his MR since most classes use it for their best spells.

In general, get to know the zones he likes to travel through and fight in, or tradeskill in, or whatever, and use them to your advantage. Faction farming might be helpful.

You will absolutely need stellar MR. This is clearly the number one priority. Even though I'm not discussing PvP generalities so much or the classes specifically so much as ways to take advantage of the format of the game, this is one you MUST not neglect.

Good luck!
