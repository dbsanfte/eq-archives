There is really no reason to re-roll your character. I am Erudite, and I have the highest hps of any SK on the server...which just goes to show that race means nothing really in this game. Equipment is so much more important than race, that it really isn't even worth worrying about what race you choose.

If you are ONLY worried about soloing, go with int/str, and sta when you can. You will be fear kiting most of your soloing career (or should be), and so you aren't getting hit all that much..(which means agi really isn't all that important for you). Mana pool is your primary concern, with regen effects secondary. Get as much int as you can, and try to find some hp/mana regen items along the way. Always look for chanters in the zone where are you soloing, and throw a few plat their way for C1/C2/C3. Clarity3 will double (or more) your xp output easily while fear kiting.

Strength is also semi-important, because it is directly linked to attack. Str. equipment is pretty easy to come by, so I wouldn't really worry too much about it. Also, the effect of high attk at your level isn't all that noticable.

Again, there is no need to re-roll. The people who talk shit to others about what race they chose are UNIVERSALLY ignorant about this game. VI made this game to be idiot proof..you CANNOT ruin your character at the start screen, no matter how hard you try. Missing a few points in a stat is so completely irrelevant that it really shouldn't even cross your mind.
What IS important?
-Level- : By far the most important stat in this game. It overshadows everything else by a large margin.
-equipment- : The second most influential aspect of the game is your characters equipment setup. It only takes a few decent pieces of equipment to erase any racial benefits or penalties among members of a class.

Get higher level, get better equipment, and you will perform as well as any other SK. Probably better since mana pool is becoming so important for a SK. If I was forced to re-roll today, I would not hesitate to choose Erud again. I like having a large mana pool at early levels, and equipment at later levels easily makes up for the lower starting stats.

Play to have fun. If you enjoy playing an ESK, then that is the best race to choose.
