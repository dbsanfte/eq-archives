Well, i posted something to this affect on the interealms site having no idea it would suddenly disappear (anyone know why?). Anyway, i would like my thoughts to be heard, so i am going to post it here:


We look terrible

I am a horrifically evil beast who lives to kill and serve his dark lord, i believe death is the solution to every problem, i enjoy battle and killing...and i look like im wearing a sheet of tinfoil. Why, oh why, does the Verant art department make everyone look the same?

With the exception of a few bits of Shadowknight only armor i look exactly the same as a warrior who is not dual weilding. Granted, in real life a breastplate would look the same on anyone, but EQ isnt real. Why not be a bit more inventive. I want to look different from a warrior. Why cant i have a softly glowing hand while harmtouch is ready, glowing green eyes, spikes on my armor, a mace that doesnt look covered in tinfoil, blood spatters, arcane symbols on my armor, i wanna see certain SK only weapons leave blact streaks in the air as i swing them. i dont want a graphic overhaul of the game, what we just got with luclin is very pretty, i want detail. And not just for SKs. A robe on a wizzy looks the same as a robe on a Chanter. IMO the wizzie's should be sparking with energy while the chanters would be slowly swirling colors. Detail would make me, you, and everyone else look a thousand times better in this game.

The WORST offence, IMO, is our "Unholy Steed". Its a black horse, whoop-dee-doo-da-day. I get all the way to lvl 59, spend weeks getting all the AAXP points, and i have a black horse?! Its an UNHOLY STEED! Why doesnt it have armor? Why cant it atleast have red eyes? I would love to see sheets of spiked black armor on my horse, riddled with holes/scrapes/tears from battle, as it runs around leaving fire in its hoofprints. C'mon, you fellas are paid to be creative in what you do. I would love to see more details in every class/race/weapon/item, would it really be that hard?
