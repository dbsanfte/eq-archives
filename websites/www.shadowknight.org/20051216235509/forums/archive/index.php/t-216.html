Hard call as to which is better solo. I'd probably go with the Troll, since I prefer 2H weapons for soloing for added damage output and the Troll can still bash with a 2H weapon.

If you plan to go with 1H primarily, then I'd go with Iksar, since the extra AC will help lower the damage you take in the later levels. In the earlier levels, the AC bonus will help offset the AC lost due to your inability to wear plate and a lack of commonly available iksar armor.

Some people will say that regen doesn't make much difference at higher levels, but I strongly disagree. The difference between a Troll and Darkelf regen at higher levels is about equal to the regen added by a fungi tunic.

I have a level 53 human shadowknight and absolutely love how the world looks, as opposed to when viewed with the infravision or ultravision races. But I've also started an ogre shadowknight.

My human SK has decent stats, but always had trouble carrying his gear around; he's always encumbered. His stamina is also low. Intelligence is ok however, but I tend to melee more than cast (taunt spells and FD).

My new ogre SK should have significantly more HPs. His higher strength coupled with the fact that he cannot be stunned from the front should increase his damage output (how much I do not know). But the major downside of the ogre is his girth. Dungeons are not ogre friendly since they often have narrow corridors. And getting many tanks around a mob (raid situation) is sometimes problematic (shrink or wolf form helps). My halfling rogue on the other hand moves around easily even when there are tons of people around (10-20 tanks attacking a target).

My advice is to choose a race that appeals to you, and to have fun.
