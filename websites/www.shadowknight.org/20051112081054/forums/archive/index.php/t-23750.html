The quest does begin with Tundis. You have to first complete a quest for him in FG, then he'll give you the quest to translate the journal. That's what leads you to Nektulos. The nomad he speaks of is on the path heading north, just beyond the destroyed Nexus Spire. After talking to him he'll send you back to Fallen Gate to get the prayer book. It only drops from the named ghosts in the room with the Fake Gon in Fallen Gate. After getting it head back to the nomad he'll send you back to Tundis who will give you the reward. There are a couple of one handers as well, one we can't use because its a staff, the other a mace with nice stats. Ratio I believe on the Twisted Mace is 9-27 base with a delay of 1.7
I got the Twisted Black Bastard Sword, don't quote me but I recall it having 20-54 base damage with a 1.8 delay Two hander. Best two hander I've seen in the game for Knights in regards to DPS.
