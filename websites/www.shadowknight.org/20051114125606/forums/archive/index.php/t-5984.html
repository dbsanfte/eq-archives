Tolt.....

Magelo does not recognize the regen modifier for troll and iksar races. When you read "health regen of ##" you have to tack on more hp if its an iksar or troll. For instance: say an iksar/troll and a human have equivalent gear on that gives 5 more hp per tick regen; Magelo will state that each race (iksar/troll and human) get "health regeneration of 5hp per tick". This is not entirely true. Its the amount of health regeneration they get for their gear (and now for aa abilities as well, since its included in magelo). A level 60 troll/iksar regens for between 16 and 18 pts of hp per tick MORE than any other race. So in actuality when magelo says "5 pts of regen" for a troll/iksar what it really means is "21-23pts of regen". If you don't want to believe this recognize that I am a 60 troll SK and i have tested my hp regen per tick many times. Based on level it is:

1-19: 4hp per tick (for sure and confirmed)
20-39: 6hp per tick (for sure and confirmed)
40-49: 8 or 10 hp per tick (i cant remember any more)
50-59: 12hp per tick (i cant remember any more)
60: 16hp per tick (for sure and confirmed)
