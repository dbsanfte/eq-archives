I presume you're talking about offtanking since for raid MTing, /defensive means non-trivial mobs mostly get tanked by warriors.

For offtanking, palys are kind of easier to play than SKs, so it's understandable they're often seen as better offtanks and chosen first.

Once you're really comfortable with your spell selection (i.e. you're a bit further up the pole than just spamming CD), SKs are just as good. But they do need noticeably more skill once you get past the simpler stuff.

But to offtank VERY well, at a level where everyone's solid in the basics of aggro and mez breaking, CC and mob positioning skill is key.

And then both classes become kind of even so the skill of the individual is more important.
