Old world:

I'll start with Odus and move east.

First of all, you are *not* KoS in most of Erudin and Erudin Castle. There are exceptions, such as the pally guilds (threatening) and possibly the caster guilds in the castle.

You are KoS in Kerra Ridge.

You will be KoS in much of Qeynos to start (threatening). However that's easy enough to raise, or simply use the catacombs for the bank/merchants/trainer.

You are KoS in Halas.

You are KoS in Rivervale.

I don't know offhand about Highpass.

You aren't KoS in Neriak with the lone exception of the ghouls outside of the necro/sk guild.

You aren't KoS in Freeport for the most part, but there may be some dangerous spots. The tunnels under Freeport are safe.

I'd expect that you're KoS in much of Grobb and Oggok, but the Bashers at least are dubious.

You aren't welcome anywhere in Faydwar as far as I know.

Kunark:

KoS in FV

Welcome in OT outpost

KoS in Cabilis, but since you'll be following Cazic Thule you won't be quite as deep as most other factions. A lot of other threads discuss Cabilis Faction

Velious:
Non-KoS to the dorfs in Thurgadin and CC

KoS to giants and dragons

A lot of other threads exist on Velious factions

Luclin:

Non-KoS to Shadowhaven, Echo, Shar Vahl, the Seru guards in Marus Seru, and the outlying guards of these towns.

KoS in Sanctus Seru and Katta Castellum

That should cover pretty much most areas. You'll find some other mobs aren't KoS to Eru Sks to start with for some reason, such as desert madmen.
