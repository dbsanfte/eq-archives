Brael
It's 3/6/9/12/15 AA just like Intense Hatred is. It gives +5 Bash damage per level. Bash is 6 second recycle, if you were to mash your bash key and never miss it would give you an additional 0.833 dps per rank, however since Bash is only at best 50% accuracy it's really only 0.416 dps increase at best, and probally closer to 33% accuracy meaning only 0.275 dps increase. Also, since people don't mash bash and are sometimes stunned when its up or are doing something as timing it for a cast you really only bash every 12-15 seconds or so (I'll go with 12 for this) which would further drop that 0.275 dps increase to .138 dps. So the net result is 45 AA's for an increase of 0.688 dps.

In short, it blows.
