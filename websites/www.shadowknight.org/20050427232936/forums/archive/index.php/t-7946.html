5 into agi is important because of the penalty of having lower than 75, like has been mentioned.

However, I have to question anyone that would recommend putting points into str, dex or agi(beyond 75), as they are all easily maxed with a primal/prismatic weapon. Most servers have 10+ guilds capable of getting into and doing sleepers tomb, so it isn't as if its rare anymore.

The sta vs int debate is interesting for medium/small races. There are points for putting points into each and what level of the game you plan to play becomes a large contributing factor. However for a large race it shouldn't be a debate at all. An ogre/troll can easily max their sta buffed. Any race can, however large races do it much faster with their much larger starting stamina. An ogre has 57 more stamina than a dark elf at creation.
So why bother putting points into a stat that you are eventually easily going to max? So the logical choice will be to put all points but 5agi into int.
