Quest Walkthrough for Life Leech


Classes:
Shadowknight's
Description:

The Life Leech Quest begins in Rathe mountains off the level 50 necromancer Kazzel D`leyrt, he is a roaming NPC found in the valley south of the Feerrott zone.

To start the quest you must be level 50 or he will not talk to you. If you are level 50 however he will ask you to carry out some tasks for him in return for power undreamed of in Shadow Knights.

He will ask you first if you know a master jeweller who can be of service, ask him about this and he will tell you the quest.

He requires firstly that you give him 2000pp as a deposit, then that you have the hyacinth that he then gives you faceted by the gnome gem cutter Darfumpel, provide an orb of the purest crystal, a gold necklace like the purest snow and the blood of Xenyari.

These tasks are done like so :

The gem which he requires faceting is a Rough Edged Hyacinth which he will give you when once you pass over the 2000pp deposit. The gem cutter is called Darfumpel Zirrubbel and is found at the hut at LOC , +4130, +900 in Rathe Mountains, nearer to the Lake Rathetear zone. If you say to Darfumpel 'rough edged hyacinth', he will express interest and tell you that he can cut it for 1000pp. Give him the 1000pp and hyacinth and he will give you back the Faceted Hyacinth.

The orb of purest crystal part you must get a Crystalline Orb a reasonably common drop item off the Stone Spider in Solusek Dungeon B.

A gold necklace like the purest snow is the White Gold Necklace, a common drop item off the Froglock Noble in Lower Guk (live side). He is quite a rare spawn and pops on the bridge crossing the water just out from the huge water elemental room.

The Blood of Xenyari must be obtained from a druid in that area. She is found at the druid ruins at loc +5150, +1700 in Rathe Mountains, near to where the gem cutter is at. Xenyari is craving a Prickly Pear so you must obtain it for her. Prickly pears are random drops off Icy Terrors. These spawn with the ice giants on the way to Lady Vox in Permafrost. Once you have the prickly pear (make sure to keep it under food in your inventory so you don't eat it!) go to Xenyari and give her it. When she takes it she will cut her finger and get blood on her parchment, which she asks you to hold so shecan clean up. The Bloodied Piece of Parchment she gives you is the item which you must give to Kazzel for the Blood of Xenyari task.

At this point you should have a Faceted Hyacinth, Crystalline Orb, White Gold Necklace and Bloodied Piece of Parchment. When you have these four items go to Kazzel once more and give them all at once, he will then give you back a Faceted Hyacinth Telesm you can tell this is the right item because it has horrific stats, and he asks that you take this necklace and give it to Xenyari as a 'present'.

Go to Xenyari once more and give her the necklace, when she takes it she is struck dead from its evil powers and the necklace is altered, to a Pulsating Hyacinth Telesm.

When you have the item go back to Kazzel and prepare for battle. Once you give over the necklace back to him he will summon a Monstrous Zombie, a Level 53 undead mob, which will then proceed to attack you. The mob is a shadow knight, hits up to 178 and has roughly 8,000 hp. To kill him you will need roughly 9-10 players around the Level 50 mark. Note that the snare/fearing tactic is especially effective on him. Once you kill the zombie make sure you loot (not a group mate) and take the Zombies Heart.

Give the Zombie Heart to Kazzel and he will give you back the Life Leech Scroll.
