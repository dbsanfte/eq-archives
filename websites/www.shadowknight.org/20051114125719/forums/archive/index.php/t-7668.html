I'm a level 50 ogre SK. My gear is ok, but not great. It used to be twink gear, but my level has pretty much caught up with everything I gave myself.

I generally solo, and do pretty well, but I'm wondering if there are things I could do better.

Here's my basic procedure. Any tips would be appreciated.

I am generally buffed with Grim Aura, Scream of Death, and Shroud of Hate. I have my 61 cleric to buff also, but honestly, Aego isn't really necessary, and I usually forego it. The new spell haste buff is nice though, and I have been using that some.

First, I speed my pet, then pull with Dooming Darkness (I'm mostly camping gnome gaurds in Echo Caverns at the moment). While they are inbound, I sick my pet, and cast shroud of hate for the attack buff, and spear of pain. By that time the mob has reached me, and I fear. I re-apply fear as necessary, and recast spear when it refreshes.

It seems to work ok- I haven't been in any danger at all, and I seem to almost keep up with the 4 spawns, with the first repopping as I med from the fourth one. I generally end up at about 85% health and anywhere between 30 and 60% mana. Health generally regens at least close to full by the time mana is up.

The biggest problem I have is that darkness almost always wears off before I'm done killing the mob. I've tried pullng with other spells, but I usually end up taking a lot of damage before finally channeling a darkness, and that increases my downtime a lot. It would be less downtime to cast another Dooming. I have BE guantlets, and have considered pulling with engulfing darkness, then overwriting it with dooming after it's taken some damage.

My spell line up is as follows:

Lifedraw
Dooming Darkness
Shroud of Hate
Spear of Pain
Fear
Strengthen Death
buff
Feign Death

Any tips on my methods or spell selection would be welcome. I've palyed EQ for about 3 years, but this is the first melee I've played to relatively high level, so I'm really kind of a n00b at it.
