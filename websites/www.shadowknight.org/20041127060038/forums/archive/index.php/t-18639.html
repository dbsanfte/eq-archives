i left PC at 26, having solo'd there from 16 on... at 26, i was taking the 2 spawns on the outside of camp 2, as well as (if you are facing the camp, with the water at your back) the 2 huts on the right side (you could prolly do the 2 huts on the left side as easily). I left PC because the mobs were beginning to go LB, but, it was still pretty good xp. I stayed there until was 20% into 26, then left for greener pastures. I wound up setting up shop in SK. I tried Neth Lair, but very quickly grew tired of running any adds to the guards ( if you get one add, odds are you'ree getting more). I also tried MS, and the xp is about the same as SK, but the mobs in MS hit harder, and have more hp. In SK (at the spires), I'm getting about 2% per kill on blues, with whites and yellows being a bit more ( 2.5 and 3% respectively) and, since I'm a troll, I figure that I'm doing quite well, because of the racial penalty. I figure that the non penalized races would be getting 3% to 4% per kill at 26. I fearkite yellows, and I also fearkite the gnolls that have the gnollhide lariat (rather obvious that they have it equiped) because of the stun proc, which seems to last longer than the normal Bash, you're stunned... (speaking of which, don't bother giving your pet the lariat, he's not high enough til the level 49 pet to proc the weapon). The gnolls also drop FS weapons, which is a nice bonus, considering they don't drop much cash . I just give bonehead the first FS 2hander that drops, and save a 2hander for the next time i log on, Another bonus is... even as a troll of Cazic, or an Iksar (partnered with an Ikky shaman) you can sell at the centaur outpost there, which means not having to lug your loot to PoK or Lake Rathe to sell....Anywho, spawn time is somewhere around 18 minutes, and I usually have 2-3 that I do in a circuit before the next respawn. You can also suck down any green birds, lions, or elephant calves you see, to shorten the downtime for regening.
