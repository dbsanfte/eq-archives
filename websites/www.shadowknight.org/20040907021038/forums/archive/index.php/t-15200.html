Talonmare
Breaking fear is one of those things that is best done with less, not more people.

First, send in a monk / sk / Necro to FD at the portal and let you know when the portal area is relatively clear.

Once clear, 1 group zones in and runs for the west wall. This group should all be invis (most mobs in the zone in area will see through it but you never know). This group should have a wizard, chanter, cleric and 3 serious tank like MoFo's.

They kill as many mobs as possible then port out when the inevitable train gets too big for the chanter to handle. Hopefully they kill 5-10 mobs. Rinse and repeat until the mobs in that area of the zone come in ones and twos rather than 5's and 10's.

After one or two cycles the second group should be able to come in. Once again they come in invis and run to west wall. HOWEVER if they get aggro on zone in they stay and die.

Before you know it you have a flawless Fear break.

Theres a 'bug' (yes, I know its not a bug, but its too absurd to be considered intentional) with FD'ing when the golems are up. If you have agro on something and FD it off, *do not* get up without either killing that mob or /q'ing out. The chain agro is insane in that zone. You can almost guarantee that any mob that paths back near the middle of the zone will pass agro to the nearest golem and will DT you immediately. When you do break in, remember that you need to bring your pulls or abort it completely with /q.

But yah, that break in should be fine if your raid force is not ready to take about 12 mobs. Having someone at the portal calling when its clear is the key no matter how you choose to do it.

Sending in fd-ers would be safer I guess, but not everyone has that many on a fear raid, and wanderers roam around the zoneine anyway.

We usually send in 1 group, MT/cler/fd-ers (who pretty much all have good DPS anyway) - then when the get to wall we just start sending in one or two groups at a time; if you get aggro, usually you have to just stand there and die, depending on the size of the train. We usually have 4+ ench and enough clerics to split tank quite a few mobs anyway..

I have cleaned up a few wiped fear raids solo on my necro; if people don't zone in and just wait, the ent clears up eventually. A lot of times 1 person can do what a group would wipe on.

Here's a trick; start hammering alt+# where # is your fd slot - in my case alt-8 before you are fully logged in. EQ actually has you in the world before you can see/move etc - but using that kb shortcut will have you fd'd before you can do anything else. It's the same way that you die sometimes before you're able to move/see.
