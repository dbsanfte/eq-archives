To your basic question, yes xp is better from a yellow/red than from a blue. Where you put that xp is up to you, and has been debated/explained many times here.

Whether it is "a lot" better, is subjective. At 70 in RSS on yellow cons, I'll see 5% aaxp per kill in a full group. In MPG on a blue or white con, it will be 3-4% aaxp. I'm pretty much assuming the ZEM is the same for both zones.

XP per hour would be based on your group make-up and gear/skill. Killing more blues per hour might be better than yellow/reds. If you can kill yellow/red quickly and safely with no downtime, that would always be superior.
