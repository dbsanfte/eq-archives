Replace those boots... Boots of Slaughter (28AC)
Shoulders... Kylong Darkmail Pauldrons (18AC)
Face... Kylong Darkmail Visor (15AC)
Bracer... sell that bracer of quickness and replace with cultural or Rock-Molded Wristguard (30AC)

Magentic is somewhat pricy but really nice upgrades for Arms, legs, helm slots.

Here's some suggestions. Most are easily accessible and farmable (with exp at 62) but probably off the beaten path. I'm going to asume that your hate for "farming" is "farming plat" not camping a named for a specific drop.

Wrists: cultural or rock molded (think there is an NToV quest for a nice wrist with 41% haste if you like quests...probably one groupable now too)

Waist: Kylong Darkmail (if you get other haste), farm Seahorse

Shoulders: Grand Pauldrons of Study - 1st castle room in PoM (they're in my magelo). 3 person easy. (also you could go to the theatre in PoM for ring and back upgrades, but it's more difficult)

Back: everyone likes the lodi, but with no HP, no STA, no int with a ton of wis, you could do much better (switch in Lodi for when enduring breath is needed). Should be able to find something with 15 AC+ and better SK oriented stats - upgrading any one of your other need areas will more than make up for the loss of AC.

Boots: In addition to the 2 mentioned the "Boots of slaughter" were in the bazaar(on Brell) in the same price range as the other 2 (3-7K) or go to Veksar with your group and farm "Boots of the Kunzar Wanderer". You'd still get exp while working towards an upgrade.
