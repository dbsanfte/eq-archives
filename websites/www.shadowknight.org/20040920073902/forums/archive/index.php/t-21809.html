Dyrkk
You have the basic idea. The AC from your gear and stats does have those components, which at one point were broken out to view. There used to be a way to get Magelo to estimate these amounts and display them by adding a command to your magelo address...but I can't recall what it was.

The AAs for mitigation and avoidance add another level of calculation, but are not a continuous addition. They add a % chance of mitigation or avoidance, rather than a permanent raw amount that can be reflected in raw AC. The calculation is seperate for the AA defensive skills, recause their activation is a random event with a certain probability of occurrence, not a fixed and constant figure.

The important thing to realize is that getting 10% from AA mitigation and 10% from AA avoidance is a lot better than adding 10% to your AC. So although your AC # doesn't change with LR/ID 5, your tanking ability changes significantly.
