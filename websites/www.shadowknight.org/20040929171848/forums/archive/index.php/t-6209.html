Bulvyne
Most of it has to do with the "coolness" factor.

Gnome SK's just aren't "cool" in most people's books.

Personally, I don't think they should have been made Pallies or SK's. "Such and such race has a Necro and Warrior, so they should have SK's" is an extremely weak argument. If that's the case, where are Ogre Necros? Troll Necros? Both races have warriors and SK's, so they should have necros, right? I just don't agree with that argument at all.

VI put them in simply because gnomes are one of the least-played races in the game. They wanted to provide another set of class alternatives to entice a few more folks to play gnomes.

As for the race/class itself, I know very few gnome SK's on my server. The one that was in my guild for a short time took a lot of crap for being a gnome sk in the first place. But, if you do your job, kick ass and don't screw up, you can usually shut people up pretty quick.

All I would recommend is, play what you WANT. As you get higher in levels, stat differences mean less and less.

But be prepared: if you play a gnome SK, you're GOING to look GOOFY! ;)
