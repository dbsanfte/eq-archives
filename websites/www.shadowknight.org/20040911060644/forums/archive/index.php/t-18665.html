If you are paranoid you can always do the Hide trick for the turn-in. Open the trade window with the items you are turning in, hide, /con to make sure the NPC is indifferent, then hit the trade button. The trade will be completed as if at indifferent faction, which in the case of our epic turn-in mobs is more than enough. You just need to remember to /con to make sure the npc is indifferent. If not then unhide, rehide and /con again until it is.

But really. If you are getting the "My hero" response you should be good to go regardless. How safe you play it from here is up to you.
