Bert and Hedge are really two different leagues. If you are struggling with Hedge, don't worry about Bert for the time being. Realistically you need to master alot of raid concepts in Teir 1 before tackling bert.

Hedge of course isn't a typical raid set-up, but I'm imagining that trouble with hedge means no where near ready for Bert.

Have your melee in front of Thelin as he enters room, all casters outside of room. Enchanters can mezz everthing that agros. One enchanter is assigned to Thelin to agro him, pull him out, mez him until all adds are dead, and then memblur him.
