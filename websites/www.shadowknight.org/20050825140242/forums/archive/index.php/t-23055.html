Dalnir is an awesome zone...at your level, it really shouldn't be scary, since you can pull to and fight at the zone-in (invest in SoW potions and you're golden; unless you have good resists, be careful with the necro mobs, and always darkness/fear the clerics before they can heal...and, of course, let your pet take one for the team against the shadowknights, hehe)...it's better with a group (especially if you can do the 2nd floor), but solo isn't too bad. The most important thing, if you do decide to fight more than the top level, is to learn the layout of the levels (and, if solo, learning to run before the point of no return).

P.S. - Might also want to learn how to drop down between levels without taking damage (takes practice).
