60: Drain Soul = 338 lifetap for 225 mana at 6.1 cast time and 0 second recast delay.
62: Spirit Leach = 350 lifetap for 350 mana at 2.5 second cast time and 30.0 second recast delay.
65: Soul Leach = 400 lifetap for 280 mana at 2.5 second cast time and 120.0 second recast delay.

After the hybrid casting bonus (30% at level 60), this equates to:
60: Drain Soul = 338 lifetap for 225 mana at 4.3 cast time and 0 second recast delay.
62: Spirit Leach = 350 lifetap for 350 mana at 2.5 second cast time and 30.0 second recast delay.
65: Soul Leach = 400 lifetap for 280 mana at 2.5 second cast time and 120.0 second recast delay.
...since the bonus only applies to spells over 3 seconds in cast time.

Now, if the casting bonus continues at 3% per level above 60, then that gives 15% more deduction of cast time on Drain Soul, making it this:
60: Drain Soul = 338 lifetap for 225 mana at 3.4 cast time and 0 second recast delay.
...and this doesn't include any focus effects which might speed casting.

The ratios of the spells are:
60: Drain Soul = 338 / 225 or 1.5 / 1
62: Spirit Leach = 350 / 350 or 1 / 1
65: Soul Leach = 400 / 280 or 1.4 / 1

So the ratio of Drain Soul is superior. The cast time difference isn't too significant due to the casting time bonus we hybrid's have, but the recast times are extreme by comparison. If they mean Spirit and Soul Leach to be "safety net" spells to get us out of a jam quickly, they need to grant a truck-load more HP. Here's a little wake up call:

1 - We're not going to keep 3 or 4 lifetaps loaded on top of Death Peace, Harmshield, Voice spell for aggro, Terror spell for aggro, other stat taps, nukes, self buffs...jebus...
2 - 400hp is jack and sh** for safety healing. And 2.5 seconds is too slow to be a safety net. In that time I can Harmshield or Death Peace. Hell, in the time it takes for my spell gems to refresh from casting Soul Leach I could Death Peace, stand up and then Harmshield. Think about that.
3 - The recast time on those spells makes them useless for what we use Drain Soul for. Even if the ratios were extremely upgraded from what they are now, a recast time of 30 or 120 would negate the standard use of these spells. I have my 15 minute refresh safety net. Thanks though.

...seems like a very small upgrade, like almost not worth it. Hope I'm wrong.
