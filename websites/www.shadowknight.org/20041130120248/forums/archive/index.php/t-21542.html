I would like an AA line that scales with the new levels called Specialized Necromancy.

To start putting points in this AA you would need lvl 65, your class title and all casting AA's.

SN 1 would cost 10 AA and enable you to scribe and use all necromancer only spells up to lvl 9.

SN 2 (cost 10 AA) would require lvl 66 and enable you for all necromancer spells up to lvl 19.

SN 3 (10 AA) requires lvl 67 and gives access to all necro spells up to lvl 29.

SN 4 (10 AA) requires lvl 68 and lets you scribe and use all necro spells up to lvl 39.

SN 5 (10 AA) requires lvl 69 and lets you use the necro spells up to lvl 49. (after scribing)

SN 6 (10 AA) requires lvl 70 and maybe the next higher title for sk and would finally give you access to the necromancers spells up to lvl 59. (after scribing them)

Essentielly i dont think that would be unbalancing after checking all the necro spells up to 65.
Most of them wont get used by highlvl necromancers and the few that could really be usefull like the necro root/mez/charm would mostly get used against mobs that are trivial anyways.
It would be some fun toys to have and would not be unbalancing since all these tools did work nicely in composition with other classes when they were actual for necromancers.

The most benefit i could see in this would be the enabling of sks as a rez class by using essence emeralds like necromancers do, having an 'ok' pet at lvl 70 and being able to preslow undeads on pull maybe.
A 720 point lifetap at lvl70 would be totally inline since i would expect to have a better lowresist lifetap (-500) at that lvl anyways.

AA's costs could be adjustet as powergain is visioned, maybe make it 10/15/20/25/30/35 it still would be nice to have.

I would like to see the following types of AA come available to us.

Death's Shield - A 30% increase in Shielding and 30% increase in Spell Shielding with a 45 second duration - endurance cost to be determined

Death's Bite - A 30% increase in Melee Hit accuracy and a 30% increase in Melee Damage per Hit with a 45 second duration - endurance cost to be determined

Life Blast - All life tap based spells will automatically Critical Blast for the the duration of effect. Level 1 Life Blast = 15 second duration, Level 2 Life Blast = 30 second duration, and Level 3 Life Blast = 45 second duration

Blood Infusion - The ability to insta-transer a percentage of your current hit points to a targeted player. Level 1 Blood Infusion = 20% current hit points, Level 2 Blood Infusion = 40% current hit points, and Level 3 Blood Infusion = 60 % current hit points

Mind Explosion - The ability to insta-transer a percentage of your current mana to a targeted player. Level 1 Mind Explosion = 20% current mana, Level 2 Mind Explosion = 40% current mana, and Level 3 Mind Explosion = 60 % current mana

Frenzied Knight's War Cry - A 30 second duration Self/Group/Raid area of effect that increases all Self/Group/Raid with in a 200 foot radius. Increase Attack by 100, Increase Armor Class by 100, Increase Resists by 100, and Increase Hit Points by 100. Level 1 = Effect Self. Level 2 = Effect Group, and Level 3 = Effect Raid

Death Touch - Increased Improved Harm Touch. Level 1 = 15% increase, Level 2 = 30% increase, Level 3 = 45% increase

Improved Feign Death - Ability to make current line of FD spells more reliable. Level 1 = Eliminate Fizzles on all FD's, Level 2 = Reduce Recast time by 50%, Level 3 = Reduce Interupt by 50%

Death Walking - The ability to take over your pet and scout ahead in zones with out agro. Level 1 = 50 foot movement raidus, Level 2 = 100 foot movement raidus, Level 3 = 150 foot movement raidus, and Level 4 = 200 foot movement raidus.
