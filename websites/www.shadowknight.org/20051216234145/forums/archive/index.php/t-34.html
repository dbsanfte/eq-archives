At 45, I've soloed him. If you keep the other trash cleared, you can avoid the LoH. At about 2/3, drop every DoT you can on him and land a Darkness. Then fear him once just under 1/2. Burn your Harm Touch and get in his back pocket and waste him. With Fear, he won't cast LoH. The 500+ HT will slow him to a crawl and leave you with about 200 hps left to go to drop him.

Sometimes you can get unlucky and get a short fear. But it's worked for me very well (once in 6 tries -- not a good sampling I know). Incidentally, I've only had him make it out of his room once. Once the fear lands, drop the HT on him and he'll barely move. Often, the HT itself will drop his move to zero. Typically, if I have HT available, the fight is over in about 10 seconds after the fear.
