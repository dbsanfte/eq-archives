asguth
I have played almost every sk race to at least 40 and personally I have come to 2 favorites which are ogre and troll.

Troll Pros

Regen which will equal if not exceed over time the discrepency in hps between ogre and troll. However when fighting in pon or other planes that are hitting you for 250 plus natural regen does not play that big a role as having a good healer. There are times however where sometimes one is forced to fd in the middle of a horde of mobs like exiting crystal caverns and if you dont have friends on to give you a friendly evac as a troll regen really helps alot. At lower levels as well regen helps so much with getting through those beginning levels.

Another pro is the good agi starting stats decent dex and sta. I personally love dex.
I have a troll that i put all the points into int which while is nice for soloing if you use mana efficient spells shouldnt matter too much at least at the level that im at 45.
I can solo even con ravishing dolvargs with kei of coarse and i never have to sit and med. Kinda funny sometimes you see people follow ya while your fear kiting waiting for ya to get a resist ! Even if you get a resist however if your good you can easily recover. High int will help you for pulling when you just dont have time to med and your mana pool begins to dwindle. Also when you solo if you get an add or two if you fear fd then get up to agro wipe the feared one this can be very helpful. Int will help all of this greatly.

Troll cons

Trolls are kinda big and can be very cumbersome if you are in zones like CT and you dont have a shrinker you might not be the most welcome person around hehe.

Exp is so damn slow........... after 50 with my troll beast at 55 he solos very quickly but his exp does not seem to want to move. Ex after hunting for a night in JPF doing blues at 53 in 1 kei i was gaining 1 and a half yellows which is nasty no medding no waiting for pops just constant killing only pausing to buff pet every hour or so. TSK should solo a bit slower unless you find a fear friendly zone.

Fire Resist mobs that use fire spells are gonna get ya good if you dont have good resist stuff and if your new with little plat you proly wont be able to find good stuff besides a rune boned fork.

Im sure theres more but thats all i can think of ATM for troll.

Ogre Pros I had put most of my points into dex except 5 into agi. Kinda of a test that lasted 42 levels and i very much enjoy. at 90 base dex and my very non uber equip i have close to 127 dex and i proc a very good ammount fat boy just luvs to proc.

Of coarse the str and sta is very important. I believe that if you have over 210 str you will get lots of very high hits. if you barely exceed the 200 mark inbetween 200 and 210 I tend to see a major differernce in avg damage which is very important to me.

Since I am not high enough I do not know how sta will affect my high end hps although hp calcs should help ya with that. \

Very good starting resists not much to add to that they are just good.

No frontal Stun = awsome for casting during fights although my troll doesnt get stunned much either. If you can pull backwards tho it can be very helpful once you learn how to.

Ogre cons

Ya your really fat and for pvp this sucks fattys are easy targets.

Your also really fat for certian areas and will not be wanted sometimes if there are no shrinkers.

Your fatness will also hurt your chances with the ladies.

Other than that i love my ogre.

The thing i stress tho is that when someone creates a character they create them with level 65 in mind when the majority of the time your not gonna be 65 so think how your going to play this char. Are you soloing if so go iksar troll IMHO. If your grouping and want to be MT then do ogre or troll. And if you are into just looking good be a female de sk hehe.
