If you're really cheap, do 1-54 through the abysmal sea free skillup quest. Otherwise (or after that) just combine 2 x Mandrake Root (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=1166) until 66 (costs around 3pp per root, vendorsold).
After that I would do crystalline silk armor if I were you, which really only requires you to spend some time in crystal caverns slaughtering spiders. Much much much easier than running around at random in the karanas to kill kitties and hope for a hq pelt, and it'll take you to the skill required for the epic.

Click me (http://www.eqtraders.com/search/search.php) and search for "crystalline silk" to get exact recipes.

Myself, I'm at 187 and heading up... not something I'd recommend if you want to keep your sanity (or your bank account) though.

Khat
I have been working on Tailoring for a bit now but moreso because of the Prayer Shawls.
What I did was basically follow How to Become a Mistress Tailor (http://www.eqtraders.com/articles/article_page.php?article=g110&menustr=040110000000) guide from EQTraders.
I avoided buying anything in Bazaar that I could farm myself to save cash. The only overhead in that was my time. There are some things that you might find are better bought when you weigh the cost against time spent, though. If it's super-cheap then there are some time savings.

As per the guide, I did Studded Masks which got me to ~56. I had hunted the pelts in both East Karana and Nedaria's Landing, both zones have very good drop-rates for differing qualities.

I did Cured Silk stuff up to ~84, which required Spider Silk which I got from Crag Spiders in East Karana. (Save extra for later skillups)

When I was making the crystalline Armour, I farmed the silks in Crystal Caverns. Doesn't take too long to get a lot of them. I did this to get skill from ~115 to ~126.

From ~127-143(current) I have been farming in East Karana for Spider Silk off the Crag/Carrion Spiders and pelts off the numerous cats. Last night I got about 144 silks in a little over an hour. I was alone most of he time until a Druid showed up and obviously drops for me started to dwindle but were still rather frequent if I could find the mobs before the Druid did.
The drop rate for HQ Cat Pelts here are very frequent which leads me to believe they have been adjusted. The Spiders frequently drop 6 silks at a time, too.
Ater making the Wu's stuff from the latter until ~158 I'll move on to farming for Superb Rockhopper hides and Acrylia until ~188.
Unsure of what I'll do after that.

If cash is not an issue, then buying rather than farming will save you a LOT of time but in a pretty hefty outlay of plat over time.
