Check the archives section. The top stickied post has all the Shadowknight skill caps, both at level 50 above.

(Yes, weapon skills are capped at 200 until level 50 and then later can be raised to 225. Other skills have different caps. Up until level 50, dodge is capped at 125, for example, while parry and riposte are 175, I believe. Many of these also go higher after level 50.)
