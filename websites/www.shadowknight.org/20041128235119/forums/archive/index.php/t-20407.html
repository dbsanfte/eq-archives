Since SoE seems intent on making every useful ability SK's get based on AA's, why not give us a pet aa?

For 5 AA's, you get Deathless Squire, a no-cost spell-like AA usable once per hour, with an hour long duration. When cast, it has the following effects

75% haste (Same as rune of death)
+100 STR
+100 DEX
+50 AC (Less than some cleric buffs)
+2 base damage
Enrage ability
SK "epic" swarmpet appearance

Frankly, this ability would not add much to SK's ability to solo and pvp (pet hp is still rather low), but it would add some decent DPS during raid and group encounters in exchange for the work involved in getting 5 AA's in addition to the myriad other abilities available to SK's through alternate advancement.
