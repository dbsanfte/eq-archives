“CD” refers to our low level snare/DoT Clinging Darkness.
It’s a mana effective agro spell.
I for one, use it all the time, combined with heat blood and a spear to hold agro the entire fight. If I do loose agro, sitting down usually focuses the attacks on me again, or use fear (on non-fearable MoBs)
To answer the rest of your question, I guess knowing your role in the group is what makes a good “Tank”.
Are you the main tank? If so, hold agro so clerics aren’t bouncing heals from one to the other. If you’re not the main tank, then it’s best that you don’t try to gain agro. Stand max melee distance and do your thing.

That’s as basic as you’ll get. If you need more info, I’m sure that more experienced Shadow Knights on this board will provide information…maybe not eloquently, but informative non the less.
