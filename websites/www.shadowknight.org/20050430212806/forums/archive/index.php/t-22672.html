Grarg
Getting there, finding it open, etc etc is a pain.

Bots good cause the beeeez spend time casting and hit barshin yer, so if you have good resists you dont take much dmg.

The main drawback with a sham is the long long cast time on heals, if you have somethign that can crank out decent burst dmg you are at risk.

Defensive aa's will mitigate that a lot, once my sk got some avoidance aa's it really opened out the pair, but before that he could get one rounded in places like solro before the sham could land a heal.

Most places if you aura of darkness first will allow you to slow pretty reliably. Matter of a fact I rarely bother with malo now, although you would be amazed the number of people who say to me "what does aura of darkness do?"

Specially in ldons, aura on inc, snare, fear check to see if FA is still on (i dont have gear maxed stats) and only ft4.

Boney adds great dmg in bot, in fact a trip to your local mage vending machine will add about 20 odd dps to the pair (actually more like 40 when you include the fact sham pets wont dual wield without weapons).

SK pet does about 30 dps and sham pet does about 45dps against beez.

My SK only does around 100dps, but my sham can crank out another 50dps without pulling agro (avoid poison dots).

I tend to be able to keep about 200dps up as a pair sustained (im lazy) I could push it to 230-250 when i really concentrate, but cant really be bothered.

So the point is im not really very well equiped on either char, so if i can do it a 62 bot should be able to at a good sustained rate.

Mind you its ferkin booring.

However, you really should look at pumping him through some ldons. The dots, extended blessing augs, and mirguls armour will make a lot of difference to you sham, and you should get groups easy with you tanking and him slowing.

At 65 (on easy) 99% of mobs are all slowable once aura of darkness lands. Not sure on 62, but should be ok.

Will take longer than pl'n in bot, but its more fun, and you can get some gear ups and augs fer the sk.
