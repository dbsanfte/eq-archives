You need to hand in:

Stone Key: penkeeper (rare because he can drop many different keys, copper, steel, bronze, iron, and stone)
Bone Key: penmaster
Bone Necklace: penmaster

to the "enslaved iksar" that responds to "what of Greenmist"

the hardest part I had was killing the 2 iksars that DIDN'T respond to the "greenmist" hails... and waiting 20-25 minutes to hopefully respawn "Argest"...


Once you go into Droga, you need to use shrink because you just handed the keys to get into the jail to Argest... besides, it's much easier to shrink yourself and go in between the jail bars than to keep trying keys in order to open the locked cell doors... and you don't want to get stuck down there since the zone revamp made the mobs much tougher... Levitate will keep you from taking damage on the drops from the trap floors in the zone (which you should use to get to the jail area) and may also help with aggro radius if invis does drop...

NOTHING sees invis, however be warned, if invis is dropping, find an empty place FAST... these mobs hit for 100-200+ fast and will gang up... and casters make Feigning death VERY difficult...

Kowl, I had thought those would be the holdup for me as well but in fact I got both within a day of each other. Couple angles to work:

Make friends with someone wanting the Urn off Drusella. If your server forums have class forums, keep an eye on the Bard ones, they sometimes discuss the quest there and you can get an idea of who is working on it.
