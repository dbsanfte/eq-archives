Macros is how most people do it, that or press the attack key on and off.

Make a hotkey like this one:
/attack on
/doability # (for bash/slam)
/doability # (for disarm)
/attack off


If you are having trouble timing how long you have before swinging is available again try this:

/attack on
/doability # (bash or disarm)
/attack off
/pause (real delayx10)
/doability # (sense heading)

Your real delay is the delay once you have applied the haste, the sense heading message will inform you your attack has recycled.
