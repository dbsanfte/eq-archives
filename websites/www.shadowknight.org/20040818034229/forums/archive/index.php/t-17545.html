Slein Jinn
This game is not set up well to support questing for gear progression. There are nice quests, but you can't support yourself full time on quested gear. Newbie quest armor is going to be outliving its usefulness by the time you can quest darkforge, and darkforge will have outlived its usefulness by the time you can do velious armor quests (and velious armor quests aren't the greatest "quests" anyway...) etc.

You can try it, but don't expect to be at all competable in terms of gear. The game just doesn't support it well enough.
