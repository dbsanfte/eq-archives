I very rarely raid, and when I do, its usually just to help a guilded friend out. Gear is all Quest/bought/One groupable. 65 DE SK.

Only HP AA I currently have is ND3. 3 levels mitigation AAs, Only one in avoidance atm. Highly recommend the mitigation for PoP tanking.

Totally unbuffed Sitting over 4K now (4059 rings a bell?) Don't neglect the AC side of the equation though either. There's plenty of fine gear with a nice mix of AC and HPs available now, commonly.

I haven't been anywhere in PooP where I can't be a viable tank yet, for an XP hunt. Only have up to Tactics atm though.

Pick the highest AC stuff you can find, but get the highest hps in EVERY SLOT you can find. So many people have a few very high HP/AC pieces they have won on raids, but neglect the small slots. It adds up. Also, pack as much stamina onto your gear as you can, as third priority. Anything else IMHO is secondary to an xp group tank.

Farm cash like a maniac to juggle gear, and sooner or later your SK will be one of the (if not THE) most desired tanks in the game for xp groups in PoP. Always with the caveat that the person behind him isn't an idiot, of course:)
