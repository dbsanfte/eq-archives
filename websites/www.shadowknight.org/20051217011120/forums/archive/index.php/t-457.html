Having played a necro to 55, I would definitely not recommend a necromancer post 50.

The class does have its strengths - for soloing but when the shift from 55+ tends towards grouping, Necros fall short. Mobs are killed quicker than dots can have effect, pets are very, very underpowered and need serious tuning and it is many times more efficient to take another meleer over a necro in grouping. Raids are even worse, pets do not hit named or raid mobs and if they do its for very small damage and they die quite quickly due to the aoes and many times, dots are resisted along with the few dds and lifetaps so unless you like giving your mana to a cleric, shaman or wizard, I dont recommend it.

Thurgadin quest robes are even worse. Since camping arena in Kael from Oct up until now, I have only seen 1 silk robe drop and it went to the guild chanter.

If you are want to save yourself some pain and anguish in the later game and want to have a much easier time getting a group and be productive on raids go with an SK.
