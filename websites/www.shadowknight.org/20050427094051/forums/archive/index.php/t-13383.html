Erasmus
I play a Bertox worshipping human SK. In general, its great to go against the grain. Some pros and cons I've discovered (now I play on middle of the road server, though, so your experiences may be different)...

Pros:
Fetid Heraldic is superior armor
You get to be different
Quenos is much much much much better than Freeport
Great role playing options

Cons:
/agree with Jobrill...the gnome thing...

Bertox human SK have no newbie armor quests (Innoruk do). This is important to newer players, at least. Though it didn't directly impact me.

Fetid heraldic is better...but its hard to find anyone to imbue the gems (this has been a problem for me of late as I've been trying to commission some). Innoruk armor is much more readily available, at least in the circles I'm familar with.
