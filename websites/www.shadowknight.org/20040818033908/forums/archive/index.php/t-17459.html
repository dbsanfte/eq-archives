Ok bear with me, we all love the greenmist, those of us that have it and there are still many striving to get one. I was lucky enough to get mine a about 18 months ago. To this day i would love to see it get an upgrade.

Ok my idea has always been that SoE could make a very reasonable upgrade to our old and wonderful Greenmist to make it better suited for the sk class.

1) I would love to see it turn into a 2hs, more powerful weapons generally require both hands to control in rl. I mean we are talking about a weapon that banished the Shissar and freed the Iksar race. I would like to think the Greenmist in existance now is the weakened state of what the fabled blade really was.

I would say for a final up grade.. Make her a 2 hander, up the stats to be around the level of the Sword of Ssra.. after all this blade put the Emperor outta business ;). Keep the bane damage as is as well as the proc. Our class benefits from the use of two handers over that of 1 handers in much of the higher end game. That should be taken advantage of.

2) We all know that at lvl 65 the Greenmist can be soloed in a matter of a week . I personally soloed all the blades at 49 with occasional help from one or two friends. By lvl 51 i had my Greenmist. The only help really needed were the final boss mobs. Of course the IC requires multiple killings of mobs that aren't exactly pushovers. So I say make it fair, Include some good old hi lvl raid mobs for the final form of this quest.

I would suggest look to some part in Veeshans Peak. Namely Phara Dar. Use his drop of Sword of Rile as one of the necessary components for a final combine/ turn-in. There is an easy way to tie this into the story line.

Everquest has always had a love of consperacies in its quests. The Dain rings.. the Giants conspire with the Rygorr and Coldains to take over thurg, you have to expose this conspiracy. The IC, there is the whole prophecy of a blade that will balance out good by providing the equal answer of evil. I say tie in another conspiracy.

You have the shissar and the ring of scale. Looking at the loot tables for many of the Ancient dragons in Veeshans Peak we see a bunch of Shissar weapons and equipment. Is it not reasonable to think that the Shissar and the Ring of Sale once coexisted with trade and knowledge between both empires? The Iksar are natural enemies to the Ring of Scale as it is, why not put an in depth hate between these two races? Rile (wielder of Greenmist) over threw the Shissar empire and brought forth a new age. Lets say the Ring of Scale , seeing that its allies or even just trading partners the shissar have been ousted by their own slaves.. Dragons are naturally aloof believing their kind is superior, from lore the same can be said of the shissar. The dragons as we know made war with the Iksar for ages and eventually Rile did die. A great way to get the last blade quested is to base it on vengeance. The Iksar race after all was created by the God of Fear, and Shadow Knights are well known to hold grudges.

Take a quest through some of the ancient cities, maybe some of the new zones, ie Veksar. Travel to Veeshans Peak , kill Phara Dar, grab Riles "other" sword. Toss in a head as loot for Phara Dar... Now that you have killed the leader of the Ring of Scale and secured Riles Sword finish the job that Rile would have. Make the player go to Luclin. Put a head on Ssra's loot table, kill him loot the head. Now lets say end it by resurrecting Rile by some means. Turn in both heads, Greenmist, and the 2hander Sword of Rile for the new Greenmist , unlocked in its full glory and evil.

**end of idea for now**

I really think this is feasible and can easily be incorporated into the game. It has everything it needs. Hi level raid required mobs to kill.. mini quests to get to the two main mobs (keys), and a sub quest to somehow call forth Riles Spirit.

I know the Dev time looks through these posts for ideas, and I would really like to see some constructive ideas on anything else that could be included to make this a very worth while and rewarding quest.
