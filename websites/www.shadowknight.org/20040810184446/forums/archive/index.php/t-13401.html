I just finished my Life Leech quest last weekend, and I can confirm, he does indeed almost always wander in that area. I have no idea what his spawn time is if someone kills him, but he is unlikely to be killed very often. I did have to wait around 15 minutes one day to catch him wandering through the valley again.

If you find the two undead camps (the first one is a tower, the second is a ruin) and set up on the hill with a tree between them facing south, he'll path right over top of you as he wanders along.
