Currently, I've played around with alot of the races and religions and have narrowed it down to a couple of choices.

1. Troll Shadowknight of Cazic Thule. Reason: It is the original cazic sk, before Kunark and before they patched Paineel in. Trolls tend to be an easy roleplay and the historical rp aspect of this character is that there are only Thule worshipping Troll SKs in EQOA, thus making it easy to create a plausable history.

2. Erudite Shadowknight. Reason, same as troll in everything except that they are a more complex roleplay.

3. Iksar Shadowknight. Reason: Kunark raised the bar of NPC interaction and created a more RP friendly enviroment for the Iksars. While not appearing in EQOA, they have an incredible base of quests to build off of and possibly are the most sound of the Thulian Shadowknight Choices.

These are the 3 choices I am considering. Just thought I'd get an opinion before I settle in on making one my main.

scaledge
AHA! I have the perfect comparison for you friend. It's essentially of these, which you prefer more:

Iksar: lots of sssssssss optional(I dont use them). Are similar to the erudites in their monstrously huge egos, but unlike the erudites, they are more centered around a sense of honor than the other races. Rather than inspiring fear to prove their superiority, like erudites, they are doing so for the purpose of pleasing thule, and because they enjoy it.

Trolls: Me barsh gud. nuff said. For those who like ogrespeak, this is a great choice. Trolls are much more primal, warlike and fanatical than your other two choices, and disorganized as well(probably why the froggies booted em). Primary concerns are simply smashing as much as possible for no reason at all.

Erudites:egos as big as their foreheads. They look down on the other races as "lesser", but unlike the dark elves, this is a superiority issue, rather than a racial hatred. They do not "hate" any races due to this, they simply scoff at them and view them as pathetic scum who arent worthy of being stepped on by them. Erudites are also very bookish, and spend more time concentrating on their sorcery than their swordsmanship. When all else fails, read a book. Erudites also talk in a very serious and sophisticated manner. They do not revel in the pain of their enemies like dark elves, or iksar, but rather fight them in order to prove their self imposed "superiority".

IMHO, choose iksar, they are the simplest to roleplay without seeming goofy(like trolls), but still are fun to roleplay and have few if any restrictions on what would make sense for an iksar that are different than what would for a shadowknight. Plus, they look great with full plate dyed black, royal frosted velium, or dark green and/or red.
