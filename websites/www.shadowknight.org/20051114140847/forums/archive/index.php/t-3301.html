Mage should be a pice of cake, I wouldnt even worry too much about dispelling him unless you use a fast damage weapon and he has a damage shield, just fear his pet or snare it if fear won't stick and dot that mage. Its obvious he has high mr so just use your heat blood, siphons and sword to take him out, always finish with ht, ie wait till mage is at or under 2 bubs of health, if you do it right though you should not even need ht for the mage.

If fear sticks to his pet then send yours in on mage it will distract him alot and add some nice disrupts. Sounds like you mr is low if you are getting rooted by the pet, get it 70 if possable, both roots and snares are usually resisted.

The ranger you should also be able to beat if you get your mr to 70 or more and don't get snared, if he dots you ignore it, a ranger dot at that level is no worse than your heat blood, just dot him back.

Use your 2hd weapon and learn how to joust by hitting him turning off attack and avoiding his hits then running back in and hitting attack again. You will dice him up especially if he sticks with his dual wield.

Sounds like he also has high mr so I wouldnt worry too much about snaring him, try with clinging darkness a few times it only costs 20 mana, otherwise use heat blood and some atk debuffs, siphon str to reduce his attack rating and then siphon life back as needed, wouldnt worry about shadow step against the ranger he will just bow kite you, unless you can't get ya mr up and need space to dispell his snare, then by all means use it and click off all your buffs before ya start as cancel magic just removes a random buff. Pumice stones remove the top buffs.

A few simple things to remember for a duel that i usually follow. Against pc's, your dots are junk due to resist rates. of course if u could get snare/fear to land, it'd be awesome but i've found that i just blow too much mana getting snare to land inthe first place, and when i try to fear it lasts anywhere from 1-10 seconds. Don't try. I've had little more luck with our disease dots, but still not worth it. what i use in a duel?
1) Primary tap
2) Shadowstep
3) Nullify magic/Cancel magic
4) Boil blood/heat blood
5-8) Whatever stat taps you prefer and spells you prefer against certain classes. sometimes i load cancel magic here too in adition to nullify magic. TAKE FD OFF (i've hit it too many times in duels accidently... sure they laugh their ass off,but u take a beating too)

In most fights i don't bother dispelling the opponent because of the number of clickies out there (i keep 2 on me so i'm not dispelled). We have a few buffs that help us in a duel such as our tap proc buff, cloak of akheva and DS at higher levels, so don't worry too much bout em. Root/snare lands? go ahead and click ALL your buffs off yourself, then ur nullify/cancel magic can have it's way with the unwanted ones first shot. I start the battle w/ boiling/heat blood just because it lands easily, then i'll stat tap, and go melee. if your life gets to around 60 try to tap it up to 80-90 or so(low resist, damages him, heals you... what could be better?). if interrupts are a prob shadowstep and tap him. with pet classes as mentioned before, fear that pet before you start on the owner.

Hope that helps with your mage and ranger friend.
