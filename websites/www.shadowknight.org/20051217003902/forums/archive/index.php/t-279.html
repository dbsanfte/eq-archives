IMO the best place to blast 59 in to oblivion is in Howling stones. Take a strong Melee based group into the south wing, and you'll do about a blue every 40 mins to an hour.

The key to getting through is volume. Avoid Luclin and Velious, as the mobs there have alot more hp, and are therefore slower to kill. Velks is only good xp in a well equipped group who can do huge amounts of damage and slaughter mobs fast. Bear in mind that even light blues are good xp if you can kill large quantities of them.

Stick to old world/kunark dungeons as they tend to follow this rule. Use dungeons instead of outdoor zones too, as you can get very large bonuses to fighting in them.

Go for it, and good luck on 60.

After getting sick of losing XP constantly pulling for my guild, I told them all to get bent and spent a week XPing. I went back to my roots as a soloer and made my level in two spots:

Sebilis- Just hanging around near hall in jail, toe to toe against blue con frogs I found here and there. You need a good mana pool to do this, but you can usually hit up whatever enchanter is in Necrosis for C2 while you are there. Critical factor in soloing here is to remember to dispell mobs to pull them, as you want to strip off any haste and Sow they have.

Maidens Eye Gorangas- The Grey is easier, but no where near as good XP wise. Just pull a red guy and fear kite him around the entrance. Only drawback is making sure you dont get trained.

Other spots Im convinced would work-

Chardok- With good gear, you can toe to toe in chardok for mondo XP, but getting C2 is much less likely and its more risky overall.

Howling Stones- Again, its pretty remote and risky, but the XP you make here is outrageous and the mobs are fairly easy if you avoid the HTing ones. Might be too remote to be worth it.

Skyfires- They take forever to kill, but the Wurms are pretty easy to fear kite and reliable XP. Expect druids in the zone to get pissy with you though.
