yerr
STA -> HP
STR -> ATK
DEX -> Procs
AGI -> AC
INT -> Mana

The reason almost everyone suggests putting all points into STA is that our class is a tank class first and foremost. More HPs gives you more time for a heal to land while whomping on a mob.

STR, DEX, and INT I also consider viable choices for starting points in case you're deficient in that stat and don't want to drop all points into STA. I wouldn't drop any points into AGI, unless you're below 75 - large AC boost then, diminishing returns for anything higher.

If you've got good enough gear all stats can be capped, but I'm certainly not at that point. Were I given the choice again I'd still put all of my points into STA.

Cabot
STA this gives you a boost in permanant HPs every level. Good stamina items are hard to get enough of. There is no substitute.

STR strength items and buffs are easy to get, not to play down the SK's need for higher ATK, we suffer in this area. Rainjars and Beast Lords will usually cough up enough buffs to get 1300atk.

DEX Even players of limited means can afford a 25/25 Staff of Lightning. Procs are much less important now. I would not put points here.

AGI In higher levels you gear will supply a meaningful amount of agility, to be really noticeable it would have to be over 200 anyways. Let the AC and Hitpoints your class gets do their job.

INT Playing anything but a large race will give you acceptable intelligence. This is important, but by lvl 65 the difference in mana pools won't be meaningful.

I believe the STA camp is right, I'm not sure exactly how many more permenant hitpoints the 20 STA gives, maybe 200-300hp. I put my points there and have been thankful of it.
