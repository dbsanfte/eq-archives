Just thinking about this the other day. Paladins get a Divine Stun AA, and stun is their form of " spell dmg mitigation ". Ours is lifetaps. Imagine a 1 sec cast time AA mana free lifetap that refreshes every 2 - 3 minutes and does about 800 - 1100 dmg / heal. It would be disabled for PvP and stack with ToL / focus items, but not SCF.

Mana issues on our already " hetic " casting routines already prevent us from using our lifetaps as much as we'd like. This would provide a needed " mitigation boost ".

Personally I feel to truly put SKs in their proper place... combining this with the unlinking of the Leech Touch AA from Super HT, and putting a resist check on spears / lowering the recast time, would be a huge step in the right direction.
