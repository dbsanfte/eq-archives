Step one : Go to Fear plane..kill assorted demi's until you get puppet strings (charged item with a version of the spell charm)

Step two : Hire a debuff team - Malo, tash, OOC, etc and bring them to OT to debuff the quest giver(reports are he is pretty MR)

Step three : Use a charge of item after he is debuffed and hope charm holds long enough to give him the gems so you get your hammer !

Reason this works is that you need to be amiable to do the quest, all charmed critters are amiable, but to their master only ! Thus you can't have an enchanter charm him, only brings him to indiff.
