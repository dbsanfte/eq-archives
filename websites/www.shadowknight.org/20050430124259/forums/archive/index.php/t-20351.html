gromrot
I just dinged level 30 myself and I've been soloing in Echo Caverns on the Fungus Grove side. I've stuck to the Needlite room mostly. I have been soloing there since about level 24. You can single pull basically the entire room except for the 2 in the back right corner. The respawn is pretty slow, but it's very easy to keep it to single pulls there. The only tough fight I ever had was when the Needlite Queen would spawn, but was still doable. I would just try and save my HT for when she spawned. Only once did she pop twice in a row. I was getting around 2% exp per kill there. Not sure how it will be now that I hit 30, but now with FD I can try doing the area's with the mutants and boglings as well. The drops there have been decent for some cash too. The wings and legs have sold for around 2pp a piece and the dagger the queen drops sold for about 18pp.

Probably late info, but hopefully it's some help.
