What Jobrill says is true, if an item is magic, lore, or no drop, it will not drop if the person killing the NPC doesn't get experience from the kill (or if anybody in the group that gets credit for the kill doesn't get experience.) This was originally implemented to cut down on farming, and first introduced on all servers with the introduction of the Warrens and Stonebrunt, and Luclin was supposed to be all TLC as well, but they went back on that decision and decided to take TLC out of the Warrens and Stonebrunt and release Luclin with normal loot rules.

Unfortunately some quests (basically EVERY Iksar quest) was designed in such a way as to make them ridiculously difficult to complete with TLC in effect. I wish they'd code in some exceptions to TLC, because in some cases it really is ridiculous. It's a sledgehammer solution to a problem that requires a scalpel.

On the flip side however, there are very few NO DROP items on Firiona Vie. Items that are flagged as NO DROP on other servers aren't NO DROP on Firiona Vie. Journeyman's Boots, Goranga Spiked Clubs, Coldain Rings, ect. are all tradable/droppable (the practice of passing around your pair of boots for a "hit" of a speed is common on FV, though as you can imagine I've heard some horror stories where unscrupulous individuals run off with the boots, or a player accidentally gives their boots to a pet). But I digress. For the most part the only items that aren't NO DROP are the epic weapons and items related to the epics, so I'm pretty sure none of the khurkris or related Greenmist quest items are NO DROP on FV.

Firiona Vie originally had more to distinguish itself from the other servers (alignment grouping restrictions, which I generally liked, and the lack of a zonewide /shout channel, which I also liked), but now all it really is, is a normal server with a 1 character per account restriction, an easily surmountable language barrier, and TLC. Of those three special rules, the only one I really like is the 1 character restriction. Overall I am disappointed with what the server has become, but I'm not going to start over elsewhere after having invested so much on FV.
