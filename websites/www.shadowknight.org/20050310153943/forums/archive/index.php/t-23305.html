Cryptia
I did it with CT and the golems up, as soon as you zone in run back to the NW corner of the fire wall, then FD off any aggro. As long as you head straight there, you shouldn't have a problem. Just be sure CT isn't wandering around back there at the time, which he usually isn't. :p

Clear a few odd wanderers back there first, then run through the fire and aggro the temple, target an AMY, run out of the fire, then snare your target once he comes through the fire and FD again.

He'll stay right there while all the others reset. After 2 minutes your mem wipe should come and snare should wear off, stand up and nab him. Repeat as many times as you need, took me about 7-9, can't remember exactly now.

Don't expect to do this with buffs, as the dispelling eyes are everywhere. I was repeatedly dispelled everytime I ran towards the castle and aggro'd it.

Maybe that will help you or maybe I typed too much, I get carried away at times.

uaell
i got this drop today. it was easy.

i camped it for several hours last night and killed every single amy in the zone (except those right near fright and dread) without a single death. when you zone in, just hightail it north to the temple with the fire ring around it. FD on the NORTH side of this temple and clear your agro. then buff up and get your pet up. walk through the fire toward the temple, touch the side of it then back immediately back up. it will agro just about everything inside the temple. when they run out, just target the closest amy and snare, as soon as snare lands, FD. after 2 mins you will get an agro clear message and a FD has worn off message about the same time. stand up, kill the mob, and repeat. they are very easily soloable, at one point i was soloing 3 at a time with no problem with only self buffs, and i only have EP level gear.
