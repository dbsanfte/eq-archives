Brael
The description of the AA is:
This is a passive ability; it does not need to be activated.
Requirements: Level: 55, Must spend 6 ability points in the General Abilities.
After training in this ability, NPCs will notice your magical activities 5, 10, and 10 percent less.

Unfortunatley it is doing more than this as I have recently proven while parsing melee aggro. Originally I confirmed that it was not affecting my pure hate spells and altering the results. If that were the case then Terror of Thule would be providing 480 hate per cast meaning Terror of Terris would be capable of outaggroing it with an equal number of castings. This is not the case though so Terrors are not being affected (as it should be, Subtlety would mess all direct hate spells up if that was how it was meant to work). This means that the only thing left to explain this is that melee aggro is being affected by it. The following is my proof:

I won't be going into deep explaniations of how this is proof, if you have been reading my thread about aggro generation you already know, if you haven't you can read up on it here (http://forums.shadowknight.org/showthread.php?s=&threadid=18154). This data is just copy/pasted from it, and condensed a bit, the method and such to arrive at these numbers is gone over in my other post.

SK without SCS hitting it.
Fearsome Shield=25-27 hate/bash
Efreeti War Shield=17-19 hate/bash
Sentient Two-Handed Axe=84-89 hate/swing

SK with SCS hitting it
Fearsome Shield=15-17 hate/bash
Efreeti War Shield=11-13 hate/bash
Sentient Two-Handed Axe=54-59 hate/swing

This needs fixed.
