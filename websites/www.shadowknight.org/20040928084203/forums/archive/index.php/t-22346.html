Just a quick tip from the late, very lamented TechTV show, The Screen Savers, any video card that you pick up by Nvidia/ATI, make sure that you get it in Ultra/Pro form respectively. The data transfer rate is higher, the RAM is the top of the line and the card generally behaves better when your stressing it out (like running 2 EQs). I have followed every piece of advice that they tossed out there and they have always steered me in the right direction.
