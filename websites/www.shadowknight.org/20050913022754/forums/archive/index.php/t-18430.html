Once you have your log file loaded into YALP:
(File/Open, then browse to your EQ directory and choose your log file. It will be in the format eqlog_charname_servername.txt)
(For example, my log file would be eqlog_Khat_vazaelle.txt)

Click the Damage tab.
Choose the mob name from the drop-down list.
Click the DD by Party radio button.


At this point it will list all of the group members and their respective numbers.
You're choosing DD by Party (defensive damage) because you chose a mob name from the drop-down.
If you click on the OD by Party you'll see the damage the mob was doing to you.
