A year and a half ago, it would have paid off to do some of the Darkforge quests (prob. targetted at lev 30-40 or so), but today there's the Bazaar, and though people traded before, the market is now overflowing with newer and better, dirt cheap items.

This makes a lot of old items pretty worthless now. What was attractive for a lev 40 char a year and a half ago would be discarded by newbies now.

So save yourself the trouble of camping rare mobs for rare drops, unless the quest reward is worth it. It feels like EQ advancement is largely reduced to gaining levels and platinum. Of course, there are probably a few quests, here and there, that are worth doing, but I can't think of any for a lev 20 character, off-hand.
