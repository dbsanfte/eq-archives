Cancel magic will remove 1 low to mid level buff or dot
Nullify will remove 2 low to mid levels buffs/dots or 1 high level buff.

Also when cast on self cancel removes your oldest buff or dot, where as Nullify tends to remove the top 2 in your stack.

I rarely use either in pvp, prefering to use Pumices 5 charges of nullify magic, fairly cheap, cast time of only 3.0 and means you have a spell slot free for some other useful pvp spells.
