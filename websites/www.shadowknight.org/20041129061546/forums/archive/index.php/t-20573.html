...............

AA Ability in 3 ranks 5/10/15 aa's progress (30aa's total).

Mana Shield 3:
Instant cast time, instant reuse, puts up a buff: "Mana Shield".
20% of all Melee damage is absorbed by the Mana Shield.
Every 5 Damage absorbed will devour 1 Mana.

While Mana Shield is in effect, all your spells are as "waiting-to-recycle". You can still "cast" clicks, procs and AA's, but your spells in spell bar, you cant cast.

You can at any time click off *Mana Shield* to have your normal self back.

30k x 5 (20% of total damage) = 150k hp's total damage (of witch 30k is absorbed), to make a 6k mana SK / Paladin go oom (and lose Mana Shield).

A 2000 hitting mob would do 1600 damage, 2400 would do 1920.
