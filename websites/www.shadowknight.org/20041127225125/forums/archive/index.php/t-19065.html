With all these new pure melee discs, I'd like to see some new hybrid discs too! Came up with a few ideas:

Shadowknight:

Lvl 57: Dark Knight's Blade
- Causes all attacks to have a 35% higher chance to hit, and do 20% more damage, for 40 seconds. Reuse: 30 minutes.

Lvl 63: Demonic Aura
- Causes all DD element spells to gain a 30% damage focus, lasts 2 minutes, dosen't stack with unholy aura. Reuse: 60 minutes.

Lvl 65: Power of the Shadow
- Causes your weapon to proc on every attack. (If you have more than 1 proc, a random one will go off). Lasts 8 seconds. Reuse: 45 Minutes

---------

Paladin:

Lvl 57: Holy Knight's Blade
- Gives the paladin 25% more chance to avoid an attack, and take 10% less damage (melee only), for 40 seconds. Reuse: 30 minutes.

Lvl 63: Aura of Light
- Causes all healing spells to gain a 30% focus. Lasts 2 minutes, reuse: 50 minutes.

Lvl 65: Holy Precision
- Gives the paladin 75% increased chance to land melee hits. Lasts 20 seconds, reuse: 35 minutes.

---------

As for Ranger and Beastlord, I'm not entirely sure what to suggest, but this forum isn't so much about them =p

Anyways if those ideas suck, suggest some more, maybe dev's will happen to stop by... *looks for blue moon*.
