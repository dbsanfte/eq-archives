Katta is easy (ish) 3 factions and you raise them by killing Coterie, Seru people or through quests. You start out somewhere near max KOS so it will take a LOT of hits to get you to dubious.
You will loose faction with coterie and Seru factions.

Seru has 8 or 9 different factions! and worse they are not aligned, some oppose others. Some are restricted to certain areas, while others wander. Expect to find the 'eye' faction mobs anywhere - these are the spies of the inquisition.
Seru faction is gained by killing in Katta, from killing thought horrors/Spire spirits in Mons Letis and from killing Recuso in Marus Seru. And of course there are quests.
Killing Grieg in Grieg's End also gives a pretty big hit to a few Seru factions.
