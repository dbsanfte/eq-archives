anyone that said not to use terror should clarify themselves. Our whole line of Terror blows ass....until Terror of Discord. I keep Invoke fear and Terror up when mez tanking and it does the trick. In fact it seems that TOD has more snap ago and it usually takes 2 casts if im just using fear.

Another suggestion....try using your AC tap on INC if you can get your target fast enough. The damage and Tap is usually plenty of agro to keep from tash/malos etc stealing agro and the added AC bonus will help you when getting beat on.

more helpful hints if you are trying to mez tank.

1. make a Mez tank hotkey so enc and healers know what you are doing.

2. make sure a healer is in your group to help with quick heals ( i usually prefer shammy so i know my OT mobs are slowed while im mez tanking them)

3. if your healers and enc arent awake, and you are getting beat on try healing yourself with taps. Sure you might break a mez if they finally decide to mez it but you will be alive and still on agro for them to mez again.

4. dont by any means dot mobs....a loose broke mez to a tap is usually fine but a 2 min dot on a mob that is supposed to be mezzed will have your encs pulling their hair out every raid.
