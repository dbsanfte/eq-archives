I don't have a map, but I can direct you do the mobs, which are you looking for?

It's been a couple days, and I'm way over-tired, but here's where I think the mobs are...

for AQ1, the Fallen Crusaders are in the Ruins of Varmarr in the Commonlands. The named you need to "talk" to is at the crossroads, he'll be white ++ to you at 20.

for AQ2, go into the pool room, follow the waterfall up into the cave, keep veering left, you'll get to what ppl have been calling Market2. You may need rats for this? If so, read the AQ3 directions...

for AQ3, from the pool room, go through the small cavern and down... you'll find constructs, rats, corpses, etc. here.

There are safe pull spots for all of these, go charging in and you'll surely get wiped.
