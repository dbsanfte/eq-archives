Sithanator
Best done with 3-4 groups, once your the room, single pull are easy, as long as you keep the room under control. With 3-4 group should be able to clear the room or come close to before repops.

Best advise kill all 4 guards outside the room, pull the 2 door guards and kill them in that room. Enter the room, pick a side left or right. clear that corner, move raid to corner, chain pull to that corner until next corner is clear, move to next corner, rise repeat until everyone who need insignia has it. Named spawn commonly.

As for the different named, General is melee fight. My parses showed as having around 240k-250khp, so be ready for a long fight. Advisor is biggest bitch and any add's or wipes you have are probably due to him, less HP then gerenal(noticeable less), but likes to aoe grav flux so have DMF/bobbing corpse up, makes for a fun little fight, great when you weren't ready for grav flux and start getting dmf up mid fight. Arbitior, is a caster, probably the weakest of the 3. All 3 are easily snareable if you need to split them, however The arbitor likes dispel snare off himself and if the advisor get to close you get the joy of FDing while being grav fluxed. The room can go from being a long boring camp to hair raising raising time in a matter of seconds.
