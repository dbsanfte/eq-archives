ssakaschaigidel
Go to torment.

Set your group up on the bridge past the entry birds. Get a rune or just use your skin buff and split the two at top of ramp. It really isn't hard, just make sure the group knows to stay the hell away til you got the split. If they don't, stay down, f9 and watch them wipe.

Hardest part of the split is the wanderers. Just DP immediately if you get one of them as well.

Once the top two are split, it is completely mindless xp, and it's a so-called hot zone as well. The two ramp mobs, the two wanderers, and the one before the house should be more than enough pulls for you. If you find you are waiting on respawns, then either kill the birds or go set off a spider trap or two. The Inscribable Words also drop off the thing mobs you'll be hunting, so start your VP earring quest.

Torment is one of those places we really shine as pullers, cause the mobs you want snare. Make sure they are snared as well, and you can walk them to camp. If they resist, you can take a hit or two while resnaring.

Also, as an added plus, this zone has a built in idiot checker for when you use the lfg tool. Just warn the new addition that the birds silence, and to watch aggro if you are silenced. Then pull a bird, and claim you got silenced on the pull. When you get back to camp, you learn if they can follow directions. If they can't, well....they are dead, and it's not your fault, cause you were "silenced."

I've trio'd this spot with a 65 cleric and 51 chanter. I've grouped it with a 59 druid healing. Your group should have no problem if even mildly skilled. As you gain in levels, there are lots of places to move to, depending on group make up and how chaotic you like things to be.

As an aside....

No aa's in the whole group? I firmly believe that levels (for a sk at least) > aa's, but damn. Grab two dps classes and push yourselves through the PoJ trials for the free aa, then take a few hours and get run3.
