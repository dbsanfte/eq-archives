I want to get an idea if the SK community at large functions as a part of their guild's pull team either as their primary, secondary or tertiary raid functions. The reason I ask in a raid environment is that we all pull in single group content, and any benefits we see to pulling on raids will trickle down to six man pulling as well.

Since gates, and to some extent in PoP and LDoN (raid content), our guild's use of SK's as pullers has increased dramatically (and this is the case for Brael and some others as well). Issues: Splitting mobs is not the main problem, we have a lot of tools in that regard, although we can always use something to make us be more efficient. Surviving the raw dps of a long or complicated pull, and staying FD through being cast on are the biggest concerns for me atm.
