Cloak of the Ice Bear is dirt common, +20 cold resist, that would help for raid resist purposes. Also has 10 hp on it, not a bad resist item.

You may also want to pick up a Sarnak Battle Shield for raid resist gear. +10 f/c/m.

Imbued Granite Spaulders if you can get lucky, or can afford them, or the "mini IGS" (I forget the actual item name) from Maiden's Eye would both be good for resists and general wear. The ME one would be a nice compromise between the massive weight of IGS and decent AC/resists, I believe it's 15 AC and 5 to all resists, weight 1, plus some str and sta, something like that anyhow.

If you don't mind sacrificing a lot of AC you could also get a Sarnak Emblazoned Tabard for another 10 mr.

Umbral armor makes wonderful resist gear too, with decent AC. The arms are 10 fr, and the boots are 5 cr IIRC, and most pieces have resists on them.
