Well, I did a search, because I thought I remembered someone posting a walk through when I first started on this quest, but apparently I'm suffering from early onset senility or something, because it's not there.

Here is a basic walk through from what I remember.

1) Get your Paineel faction up to at least Amiable.

2) Get a "Grimy Lance" from the spiritualist at the spawn point at the tip of the island in the Feerrott.

3) Head to Stonebrunt. Find the Kejekan city near the middle of the zone. Facing out from the front gate of the city, turn right about 45-60 degrees and run straight out. Not far from the city you will find a red wisp looking mob that is named "a weak spirit" or something like that.

4) Hand your Grimy Lance to the "a weak spirit". He will despawn and respawn as an erudite model ghost, and will give you a "totem of Cazic Thule" or some such. He will then wander off in the direction of the Kejekan village.

5) Run back to the village, head through the front gate, hang a left and run to the corner. You will see a hut in front of you. Drop invis and creep up on the wall of the hut until you aggro the named mob in there. He will be Disciple something or other. He is a shaman, so he'll be casting on you, and you'll probably aggro others as well. Back yourself into the corner and wait for them to come. Kill them as quickly as possible, and make sure that none of them run off to chain aggro others. Loot the bodies and re-invis.

6) Go around to the front of the hut and go inside. The Riddosan ghost will be standing in the center of the room. Try and position yourself as far from the left over mobs in the hut as possible, drop invis and hand the "totem of Cazic Thule" back to Riddosan. He will give you the Soulfiend Lance and despawn. If you have managed not to aggro anything else, reinvis and head out of the village to some more civilized area. If you did aggro something, you're probably better off to try and run out of the village and FD or take the death. Try not to die in the village, as your Lesser Summon Corpse spell only works up to lvl 35, and it was a shocker to me when I did this quest (and died).

Hope this helps. Sorry I couldn't provide you with exact names or locations, but it's been a long time since I did this quest.
