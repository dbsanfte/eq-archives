Some advice... Please don't take this the wrong way. Some of this may seem redundant, you may already do it. Just don't get defensive, I'm not critisising you, just proffering some advice you may not have thought of.

1) Don't sit in PoK. Your level 62, do your LFG in BoT or Valor. A group that needs "one more" might take the sk in the zone ahead of the rogue 4 zones over. This is actually the case most of the time with pick up groups. This one is huge... POK IS A HORRIBLE PLACE TO FIND GROUPS FROM!!! Be pro-active, go to where you want to be, go to the group you want into, and sit next to them, when they have a space open up... they will probly take you cus your right there.

2) If you want to get an adventure, go to the zone you want to adventure in, but be willing to go elsewhere when called. Many people using the LFG tool look at the zone your in, if your not in a LDON zone they will usually take someone else first.

3) Build groups and keep talking to them while you wait on healers. Keep everyone engaged, chat about stuff, ask if they know any healers ect. It keeps people form bailing on you, so when someone does show up, you have a group faster.

4) Make a series of humourous hot keys. This does WONDERS for you, trust me. If you pull a lot, make an entire bank of different funny hotkeys like "Booze + Shadowknight =%t incoming" and stuff like that. If you tank or just melee alot make a series of "banter" keys with the mob. Switch them up to keep people entertained. Even if your the worlds biggest fucktard, you will get groups cus your "nice" or "really fun to have around"... works for me, and I'm a huge fucktard. You can do this one even if you don't like to type, cus you have everything on hotkeys.

5) Use OOC sparingly. About once every 5 to 10 minutes ooc that your looking for a group, do this in the level'ing zone.

6) Use the "note" section in your lfg tool to let people know where you will go besides the zone your in.

7) Keep notes on people who are good players. Put them on your friends list and try to get in their groups later. If they are already on an adventure or in a group don't be afraid to shoot them a tell. If you complimented them the first time you played with them, they will probly like you, and find a spot for you in their group as soon as they can, or fit you into their next adventure.

8) Be talkative.. but not TOO talkative. Find a happy medium, try not to insult anyone. Keep yourself focused on winning the adventure. If the group is good say so. This always scores you some points with people. But don't chat just for the sake of chatting.

9) Never whine/brag about your gear. If something good drops say "ooh nice, I'd roll on that" but don't expect them to give it to you, and don't act like it's crap... you never know who might see it as an upgrade... and only the elite like elitists.... and your not elite.

10) Get level 65 before you work on more aa's. I know what your thinking "but I have trouble getting groups, that stops me from getting 65". That is true... but trust me, AA's are way easier to get once you get 65, and people see a 65 as WAY better than a 64... its pure perception... but it's the way it is.

I hope this helps you out!
