Shadowknights,

We are currently gathering suggestions and feedback from the community on what you would like for your epic 2.0 effects. This would include any clickies, focus effects, or worn effects that we can place on the weapons to make them desirable for the intended class.

While any suggestion is appreciated, please do keep in mind that we do have some constraints on what we can place on an item. We can only have 1 clickie, proc, or worn effect on an item, and we can only have 1 focus effect on an item. In terms of worn mods, we can add any of the currently existing mods to an item (for example haste, attack, shielding, avoidance, etc). For clickie effects, we can and probably will use recast timers; so we can make powerful clickie effects and take on a relatively long recast time (instead of instant recast) to balance out an effect. Please keep in mind we’re talking about clickie/proc effects and are not currently looking for suggestions or feedback on stats, weapon type or design. Please try to stay reasonable with your suggestions (IE: no death touch, AE rez, etc.) but definitely be creative . Thanks for the feedback.

As it stands now, it's a physical impossibility to have more than 1 each of an effect or focus because of the lack of fields on items.

Currently there is one field for each:
focusid = Focus on the item.
spellid = Effect on the item.
The only way to allow more than one of each is to add more fields such as:
focusid1
focusid2
focusid3
...

spellid1
spellid2
spellid3
...
Many items have multiple augment slots because there are fields to account for this:

aug1type
aug2type
aug3type
aug4type
aug5type

Theoretically, any 1 item can hold a maximum of 5 augments.
