Catharsis
Pouch is off named taskmasters + warden. Spawn every 2 hours in the basement. Four to the south, two to the north, two wandering. The only one that I'd consider 'hard' is the south static to the far east. He flurries and is unslowable. Take one group for this.

Comm 1 and 2 spawn on the first and second floors respectively. When I say that, I mean that. They can spawn almost anywhere on that level. Only one of each can be up at a time, so check with a tracker before starting to kill PH's. While this is doable with just a couple people, the more you kill, the more chances you have at getting a Comm. Bring several groups and keep the entire first and second floors clear if you want to get this done.

Imperial guards are on the thrid floor, rogue required. I cant remember the nameds names, but you'll know them when you see them. Take a couple groups, the mobs will be pretty hard for a NToV guild. The insignias drops often. The Advisor AE gfluxes *the direction he is facing*. So, unless you want to agro the rest of the room, keep him facing a wall or corner.

Your guild will probably whine and bitch and cry and stop logging in over this. Best way to get it done fast is to take the entire guild for a week or so and keep the first and second floors clear (comms are the bottleneck).

Elites on both floors flurry, elites on 2nd floor cant be slowed. Commander in my experience would spawn in runs, IE no spawns in 2 hours, then 5 spawns in 25 mins.

Basically unless you have a strong guild wide effort, dont do this. Between the emp key and the VT key id rather cut my big toe off with a butter knife then do this again =P.

Edit: all but the 3rd floor need but a single group. For commander you actually want more cause you want to clear as many PH mobs as possible.

Ashrai
Yea com3 is the easy one relatively speaking- since it takes a decent sized force, people tend to stick around until you have a good number of drops. Some nights you get real lucky and have a lot of named pop and you get quite a few keys finished off that way.


Pouch is cake, com1 and com2 are mindnumbingly boring. Best way to get them is to drag a group of friends down when only a couple of people need a drop. If you go on "farming" nights you end up with 6 people needing the drop, people get tired and leave without a drop still, new people rotate in, or the group grows to two and you get one pop that night and 11 people end up wanting to kill themselves. That's not to say don't take advantage of farming nights- but take initiative on your own to get your piece as well. Because the more people get that piece, the less they will want to go back to that stinking snakefilled zone and camp coms. The only hard part about either camp is the wait itself.

Ore is another timesink- sometimes upwards of 8 hours without an ore drop. If you don't absolutely need the bane weapon then don't subject yourself to it.
