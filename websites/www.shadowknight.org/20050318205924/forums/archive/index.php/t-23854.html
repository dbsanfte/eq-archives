If you're really bored and don't have any friends around to help, it is possible to solo the breeder ph. Note: this tactic is quite risky and doesn't always work. I take no responsibility for any deaths you take :)

First thing you need to do is make sure you have harmshield up with your kiting line up, and probably your pet on hold. If Deathsting is up, attempting this is probably a bad idea. Run into the breeder room invis and get the ph on target. Don't lose this target. Get in a spot in the room between spawn, throw up harmshield, and run your ass off toward the bridge. Your harmshield should run out as soon as you get to the long empty hall. Run up the small set of stairs before bridge and cast Festering Darkness as soon as you can. If luck is on your side, the breeder ph will get in your line of sight before the train you've pulled gets too close. Once you've casted snare, FD ASAP. If everything works out right, after your two minutes pass for a mem wipe you should have a solo breeder ph. You can kite him back and forth in that hall there. It's possible to move around him without getting into melee range, but it can be hard to do, so be prepared to take some hits.

This tactic has a couple holes in it that prevent it from working all the time. The first is that sometimes the breeder ph will cast that DoT the murks have on initial aggro. When this happens, he gets delayed and is too far behind in the pack of mobs for you to get line of sight before it's too late. The second is that darkness can be resisted. Unfortunately, upon failure you have to wait the ten minutes for harmshield to pop up.
