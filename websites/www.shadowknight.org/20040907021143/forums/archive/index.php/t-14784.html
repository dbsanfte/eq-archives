Eatbugs
The 20% penalty is not subtracted from exp you gain, Trolls just require 20% more exp per level than the base races do.

So, no matter the split, the same exp would be going to normal/AA regardless of race, you just need 20% more normal exp to level.

Doesn't work that way. Everyone requires the same amount of experience to level - as a race with an experience penalty, each mob you kill gives you X% less experience. See the numerous threads on racial experience penalties, like this one (http://forums.shadowknight.org/showthread.php?s=&threadid=7484&highlight=troll+experience+penalty).

In answer to the original question, if you're playing a race with a substantial experience penalty you should always have either normal experience or AA experience set to 100%. Due to the way racial penalties work, if you have experience divided between normal and AA you're paying the racial penalty for AA experience as well.
