Brael
Something I had for a quick idea the other day, why not add heal recourses to spears? Lifetaps can't stay at even 50% of the healing power of paladin heals in GoD because the dps increase we would recieve is nothing less than game breaking from such a change. So to ease the problem of damage spikes and attempt to get our healing back up to what should be par for us (or atleast to something workable) why not throw some decently sized heals on spears? They're fast casting already so would be ideal for damage spikes, and with the recast times on them larger amounts of healing could be given without actually increasing our heals by too much. For comparison heres how healing power stacks up between us and Paladins using single target direct heals only aa/focus included (using Paladins simply because we've always had our lifetaps compared to their heals for balance):

Light of Order: 1000 heal 430 mana 1 cast 5 recast
Light of Nife: 850 heal 400 mana 1 cast 5 recast
Touch of Nife: 1125 heal 425 mana 5 cast 2.25 recast

Touch of Innoruuk: 470 damage 470 heal 330 mana 1.8 cast 12 recast
Touch of Volatis: 350 damage 350 heal 280 mana 1.8 cast 12 recast

With max focus/aa's included that changes to:
Light of Order: 1612 heal 387 mana 1 cast 5 recast
Light of Nife: 1371 heal 360 mana 1 cast 5 recast
Touch of Nife: 1814 heal 383 mana 3 cast 2.25 recast

Touch of Innoruuk: 597 damage 644 heal 297 mana 1.6 cast 12 recast
Touch of Volatis: 445 damage 480 heal 252 mana 1.6 cast 12 recast

Or, to simplify that:
Light of Order: 268.67 heal/sec, 64.5 mana/sec
Light of Nife: 228.5 heal/sec, 60 mana/sec
Touch of Nife: 345.52 heal/sec, 72.95 mana/sec

Touch of Innoruuk: 91.25 swing/sec, 21.84 mana/sec
Touch of Volatis: 68.01 swing/sec, 18.53 mana/sec

With standard mana regen buffs, FT15, aa mana (AotC, VoQ, SD Paladin MC/MH, PotN, VoQ, SD SK) we're at 51 for Pallys, 85 for SK's, 100 for GoD SK's.

High end PoP Paladin: (LoN+ToN)
Spike healing 228.5/sec 60 mana/sec
Sustained healing 40.26/sec 8.5 mana/sec
Maximum healing 374.71/sec 87.41 mana/sec

High end PoP SK: (ToI+ToV)
Spike healing 91.25/sec 21.84 mana/sec
Sustained healing 59.23/sec 14.17 mana/sec
Maximum healing 159.26/sec 40.37 mana/sec

High end GoD Paladin: (LoO+ToN)
Spike healing 268.67/sec 64.5 mana/sec
Sustained healing 40.26/sec 8.5 mana/sec
Maximum healing 403.06/sec 90.59/sec

High end GoD SK: (ToI+ToV)
Spike healing 91.25/sec 21.84 mana/sec
Sustained healing 69.67/sec 16.67/sec
Maximum healing 159.26/sec 40.37/sec

Since damage spikes and to a lesser extent maximum healing power is what matters, we essentially recieved 0 meaningful upgrade to our ability, for this reason I'm thinking that heal recourses on spears would correct this problem somewhat even though it would most liekly require an increase in mana on them.

Thoughts?
