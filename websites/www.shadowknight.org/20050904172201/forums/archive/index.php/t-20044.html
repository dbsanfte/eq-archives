The only way I found to raise the necro faction (Brood of Kotiz) is to kill the Iksar outcasts in Warslik's Wood and LoIO. Neither the bone chip quest nor the explorer knives raise it.

Here's the easiest way to do it:-
1. Buy a couple of mage summoned pet toys. I favour the muzzle and belt. For me weapons were optional since my lvl 55 pet is still not high enough to proc any of the good mage summons.

2. Head to WW from OT and follow the path to the ruined hut on your left hand side. There are 3 Iksar Outcast spawns in there, and unlike most of the spawns in the caves to the south, these ones all attack even when green.

3. Cast a pet and position yourself in the middle of the three. This works best if you have a mount, since that stops you getting knocked backward. Bring lots of food so you continue to regen health, cause your mount will steal it all!

4. Go AFK. Go to work, sleep or whatever, this can take some time.

5. While you're gone, the outcasts will attack you and your pet will kill them. You will also get agro from any passing giants of goblins, and this is where the summoned gear comes in.
If you have a pet getting a kicking from several mobs, he is likely to die. The extra hitpoints and haste from the friendly mages will ensure he kills them before they do him (and then you!).

6. When you come back, you'll find a pile of corpses and a better standing with the Brood of Kotiz. Easy, hassle-free and less time consuming than running around killing them yourself when you could be doing something else.

Incidentally, dubious is good enough standing with Residents to do the Frontier Mountains hand-in and apprehensive is good enough to do the History of Greenmist tome turn-in for the final part.

Hope this helps :)
