Blindfoe .....

well, the raw melee damage makes this an excellent weapon. The proc itself is a 300 dam over 15 ticks darkness, like Dooming in how much it slows. It overwrites engulfing, and checks resist every tick, making it unreliable on high MR mobs.

The aggro actually is not THAT bad. I use it a lot on raids, and rarely get I hit more than with Cudgel of Cowardly death.

If you tend to have trouble in aggro control, however, this one is not your toy.

I use it as standard issue atm, cause I cant stand the look on Cudgel, and it has sweet stats too. If you have access to Tunare, Vulak stuff, skip it.
