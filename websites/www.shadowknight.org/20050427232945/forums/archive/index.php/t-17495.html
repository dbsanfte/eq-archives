The only armor that I 'missed' as an Iksar SK was Darkforge and Blood Ember. Outside of that, most if not all good armor is Iksar-usable. I guess you could add Umbral to that list, but they threw in Greenmist to make up for it.

Once you get to Velious, Iksar are pretty much able to use any armor that other races can use. And with LDoN, you can get Kunark-equivalent armor without race restrictions as well. The armor restriction for Iksar is pretty much moot nowadays, so the AC bonus that was given to us to make up for that lack is now truly a bonus, since we're pretty much using the same armor but getting more AC in the bargain.
