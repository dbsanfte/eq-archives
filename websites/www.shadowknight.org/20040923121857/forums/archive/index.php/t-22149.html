KT will not rip apart anyone who is not in time gear. Named, maybe, but for regular xp, KT is easy as fuck to tank. I can tank there; unbuffed, i'm just over 4.5k hp, and 1200AC. The first time I tanked there, I had nothing but self buffs (CoL) and Virtue for hp buffs, topping me out at a whopping 6.2 or 6.3k-ish hp. Just snare pull (festering doesn't get resisted much on xp mobs), kite it for the time it takes to slow, then engage; I was kept alive with a simple chain CH+yaulp. I was a drain on my cleric's mana I'm sure, but not to the point where the cleric actually had to sit and med. The next day we went back with a shammy in the group, with the 7.3k hp or whatever it was I had, plus the occasional shammy heal, it was easy. Anyone who is decently decked out in VT gear will have WAY more hp than I have (my magelo is far from updated).

Named I doubt I could tank; I've only been in on one kill, and we killed it through a combination of ping-pong agro kiting and all-out DPS, we didn't have one death. Granted, I'm sure we got lucky, but for XP, I'm sure a VT geared group could easily handle it.
