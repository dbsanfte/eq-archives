I usually don't hunt in PoV anymore, but a guildie wasn't flagged for much else and I spent some time here recently with them. Some might find it helpful.

Start at "outter" and move North along the riverbank until you're equidistant from all three undead ruins. This is near the 2nd pair of frogs at the water. Summon a pet and get sow and some hp buffs on him. Cast Sense Undead for the nearest skellie. Send the pet in to tag one, then /pet back off to reel it in.

These are never camped.
Singles every time.
They don't hit hard enough to one-round a buffed sk pet.
Bring an undead-friendly class (necro, pallie w/SU3, nuking cleric).

We were only 3 people and were unable to clear the camps due to repops; I estimate this spot would support constant pulls with 5ish people, depending on gear and aa's. Maybe 6.
