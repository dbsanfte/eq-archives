Well, this is semi-offtopic.. BUT!

6 level 65 SKs with all spells could very well be the best 1 group combo in game.

Specifically, with Zevfeers bite, you get:
-6 people casting bite, resulting in 600 mana per minute, or FT60.
-6 people casting bite, resulting in 1200 hps per minute, or 120 hp/tic regen.

If each of the 6 SKs is using Pact of hate, that is another 15 mana per tic regen.
If each of the 6 SKs is using fungi tunic, with regen3, that offsets most of PoH damage.
If each of the 6 SKs is also using Bond of Death, this offsets PoH damage clearly, raising our hp regen to well over 100 per tic. (yes, this is a dot, but I consider it a regen effect in this situation).

Keep in mind, the above effects are ONLY regen. effects. This does not include casted taps, casted DoTs, weapon procs, SoD procs..etc. Also, FT items aren't taken into account..which will add to mana regen.

-6 level 49 necro pets, each providing around 20 dps?..maybe more not sure..this is assuming they are hasted using our new pet buff.

The group now has 12 melees, ~75 FT standing, over 100 hp/tic regen.

Then, you have the advantage of having the DPS of 6 level 65 melees in your group, as well as 6 pets. Also you have the advantage of very precise agro control. Each SK could tank for a bit, FD agro off to another SK, regen hps etc. You could cycle tank this way with very little danger of any one SK dying.

Also, if each SK has SA3, SCF3, Theft of life3, Maxxed HT AA, each wielding a decent tap weapon (goldenrod/SoS), each using both spears...well..you get the picture.

Yes, this group is not feasible on most servers (if any), but..if this group ever did materialize, it would be very efficient, xp would fly, and it would quite obviously be the ultimate in cool seeing 12 skeletons beating the shit out of stuff.

OH, to dream....
