Skulkracker
Lvl 65 spells have been prohibitively expensive lately. And some of the rare ones have not even been up for sale. At least for SK spells that is. I needed 4 spells, ranging in price from 30k -50k at current bazaar prices.

This was largely unacceptable for me, due to the fact that I don't have 30k much less 50k for even 1 spell. Sure, CoM can help me with costs, but SK spells are not exactly high priority and justly so. Instead, I have been working on slowly building cash up via farming low to mid range stuff in easy dungeons when nobody is around to get exp with, and selling it in the bazaar.

Not being particularly skilled at this bazaar thing (not much call for eviscerating dorfs or pulling the legs off gnomes during trading) I have only slowly been gaining plat off the things I farm and sell instead of buying low and selling high. However, I have been persistantly checking the prices and availability of my SK spells.

Today I noticed a huge difference when I logged in this morning before work. There were practically a plethora (thats many many many for you dorfs) of lvl 65 SK spells being offered for sale by more than 3 seperate vendors. And they were having a bit of a price war. Spells I had seen yesterday for 40k were now down to 20k. I got an evil grin and said 'Yes. Come to me my prettys.' Still, I could not afford them.

I was not able to raid that night because I was bone tired and couldn't maintain focus enough to feed myself. Yet, before I dragged my weary soul off to bed I went into the bazaar and checked my spells. My eyes popped. Sitting all by its lonesome amongst my other 65 lvl spells was one I had never seen in the bazaar before, and only heard scoffing legends of in the Shadowknight taverns. Touch of Innoruuk. My ultimate lifetap spell. And it was cheap.

I ran my large gnome-squashing posterior to the bank and grabbed all 12546 of my platinum pieces and cuffed wee halfing dams nursing suckling babes out of my path in my rush to find that vendor. I went down this aisle. Then up that aisle. Then over around that annoying wall and down another aisle. And then my eyes fell on the one I sought: Ryzenn.

I rushed up and pawed through bags and bags of useless crap, looking for scrolls. I couldn't find it. It wasn't there any longer. Some momo had bought it in the 2.5 seconds it took me to get my coin and dash over here. AAARGH!!! Damn you Gods of hate and fear! You are mocking me again.

Wait a minute. In my haste to get the spell I had picked the wrong vendor. Ryzenn wasn't who I wanted at all. It was Lavetz I wanted, and he still had the spell! Up this aisle, down that aisle around the corner and there he was. The most beautiful scantily clad barbarian I had ever seen. I rifled through his pack and then with unholy reverence, my eyes found it. Touch of Innoruuk. For 10000 platinum.

I tossed the coins at his feet with a mumbled 'Thanks, buy yourself a new dress.' , and ripped the spell from his pack and ran off clutching it to my breast and giggling in a gleeful (yet entirely evil) manner. It was mine at last. Now they would pay. Oh yes. They would pay indeed.
