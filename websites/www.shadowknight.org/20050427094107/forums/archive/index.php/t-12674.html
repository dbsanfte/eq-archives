Troll, once you go with green, you'll know what I mean, LOL

Ok so I am a bit biased, but I love the Troll's laying the smack down ability,... but you need to ask yourself what kind if sk do you want to be

Caster: Then go with gnome, eurdite, or dark elf
Mix: Go with Human, Iskar
Tank: Go with Troll, Ogre

End game things do even out except a few area's,

Hps: Generally Ogre, Troll will have about a 500 hps advantage with the end game in mind, but that will become negligiable with proper buffs if all races are geared evenly. No buffs then a definite hp advantage to the phaties

Int: One stat hard to raise, arguementively at end game eurdites, dark elfs and gnomes will shine, but with all the C3, FF items about, that isn't such a terrible thing, but one who does pick a phattie will have to deal with having a slightly smaller pool.

Tankability: If you are not a ogre don't look here, the advantage a ogre has is the ability to get a beat on something terrible and get off a FD without flinching, Makes them great for pulling and breaking hard to break spawns, all other races don't compair in this area.

Pvp:
1) Iskars and trolls generally shine here due to regen, If you think regen isn't a factor you are kidding yourself, with proper gear you will always have a good advantage with regen but.... you need a total package and should really focus on regen items to really make you stand out.

2) Gnomes damm can they be annoying, I have on a pvp server fought many many twink gnome sk's, I am fortunate not to die by any of them yet (knock on wood), but out of all the sk's I fight (human, gnome, eurdite) they are the most pesky for me because they have a great chance of escape, nothing is smaller than a gnome female, and man they can find the weirdest holes to hide in, One can easily fight fire with fire by carrying shrink pots on them if in a town area, out in the open not as bad, but because of pecks I carry shrink pots

3) Ogres and Trolls pre end game gear, due to high hps, us phatties carry a bit of weight, therefore we can afford to not carry as much hp items and go for the bluediamond gear. That is a huge advantage, if you don't think it is ask any gnome sk wearing full set of resist gear trying to overcome the hp handicap they would see facing a phattie, this is all pre end game gear btw.

Looks/Enjoyment: This should be your most critical decision when picking a sk race. If you don't enjoy what you are playing because of looks then leave right away, post haste, or buy some illusion pots =). If you like the pvp aspect of game consider the races I provided, and you will find probably more enjoyment out of them due to your particular style.

I hope this helps you in your decision, and remember this isn't a end all be all list. Good luck out on the battle field
