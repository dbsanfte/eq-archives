Most Royals Raids take the path through the "Gate" across the bridge and past the "Observer's" camp. Continuing down towards the Bridge Keeper spawn at this point you can go towards "Korocust's" or through the "Graveyard" towards the "Royal's Residence". At the Bridge Keeper's area you can continue on towards the mines and before you get to the bank you will find the Herbalist's Shack this is the area that will spawn your target. It is not anywhere near the Royals as previously stated and getting your raid to make this detour for a rare spawn and a rare drop is not likely and entirely up to your powers of persuasion. Good luck !

P.S. If your target is the Shard then the path to take is through the "Levitate" tunnel this will shorten your trip and preserve your force by encountering less mobs on the way.

Ok... a lot of guild preference goes into the path you take to the royals. We tend to go into the gates, jump into the pits go to the fork. You can go through the gate to the bridge. You can also just go into the side tunnel and go down to the fork. I've seen all three ways done. Personally I don't like the take the gate path all the way to the bridge, it's a lot longer and more chances of screwups.

My personal preference is to take the tunnel to the fork and take a right... but the problems of getting people to lev, and run into a tunnel are usually not worth it.

Herbalist area is on like a 20 minute spawn timer (don't quote me on that, it's somewhere around there, I was more worried about getting the different aggros in the area figured out then spawn timers).
