Poxs
Put all your extra points in Stamina - hardest and most expensive to raise via equipment. INT items are pretty common, as are STR and DEX. You won't need to care about Agility as long as its 75+, and WIS, CHA are worthless to you as a Shadowknight (CHA only good for selling to merchants, but not a consideration for starting points at all).

Sounds like Iksar is the one you want, if you want foraging. Swimming starting at 100 isnt' a bit deal on PvE servers, you can build that up easily enough I wouldn't factor it in for my decision.

Nice thing about Iksar, Troll and Ogre, they have an xp penalty. Yes, that's right, I like the penalty. The slower I level the more skillups I can get in combat skills before I ding up a level. IT's easier to max out.

Considering as an SK you want to keep your 1hS, 1hB, 2hS, 2hB and Piercing all up as high as you can (not to mention Bash/Slam), if you level too quickly, your skills will suffer.

The xp penalty takes care of it nicely.

I'm leveling up an Ogre SK now, and I love it, and I do not notice the penalty -- what I do notice is that my weapon skills are much closer to cap than they were with my Human or Gnome SK from times past. I also notice the lack of stuns makes me deal more damage/spells out over time, and I look ubah sexah too.

Whatever you choose, enjoy, SKs are a fun class!
