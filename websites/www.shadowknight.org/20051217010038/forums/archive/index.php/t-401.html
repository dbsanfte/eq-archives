Was doing DL myself at Burning Woods line, similar to above

The best thing about this spot is the pathing the MoB takes. This saves me alot of MANA, thus more kills per Med.

Pull the MOB to near the zone line where the ground is flat between tree lines. This is the wide passage area between zones. I pull with Darkness so I can run to this spot and wait for the MOB, as this spot is a little ways from where the MOBs chill. Fear, and the MOB runs straight for the invisible Wall withing the passageway between BW and DL. When they hit the wall they slowly start moving towards the main area of DL because of the angle they are walking into the invisible wall. By now my Darkness spell has worn off (only level 48), but because of the pathing, the MOB still moves as if it is snared/darknessed.
