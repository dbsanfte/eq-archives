Keevel
Taken from research into doing the epic on my Shaman, and conversations with others, none of the tips to MQ have been verified by me personally:

The easiest part of the Shaman Epic to do (after the initial kill in OOT or Butcherblock) is "The Test of Might". Basically go through part of the shaman epic including talking to a drunk and sitting underwater for hours, then go to RM and kill a pally and an Sk over and over take thier weapons to a lesser spirit in West Karana (you'll need a tracker and the guy doesn't stop so you'll need to give him the items on the run) and you get up TS faction. Keep killing the guys in RM and doing turn in after that. That's the only way I've heard of it. Also shaman are very wary about the MQ'ed tear in regards to the faction issue. so you should go to max faction with the RM kills then make sure you tell the Shaman that. Should get a pretty penny out of it but not sure it would be worth it for you unless you plan on doing the MQ more than once. Though I've heard that after maxing faction you can also kill Black Dire in MM then do the turn in to the lesser spirit in TT and get the shaman mini-epic SoW boots. No Drop but insta clicky sow from inventory.
