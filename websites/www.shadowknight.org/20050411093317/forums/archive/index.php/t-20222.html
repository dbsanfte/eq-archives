Reflan
It really goes back and forth depending on the class versus the mob, a lot of times. Shaman DPS in general is horrible compared to BST, and BST heals for their pets are great compared to shaman heals, which stink big time once you start fighting mobs toward the end of the game. Yet when mobs don't summon and can take roots without just constantly breaking their roots, and the shaman has his LDON dots and good AA, a shaman can solo pretty decently stuff that a BST wouldn't want to try.

And a necro or druid can solo obscenely well. Druids charming in places like POW for instance can do incredible things.

For almost all classes, though, solo experience is so inferior to group experience after a while that when you combine it with how boring soloing can be, it's not compelling at all. If you make a class dependent too much on how good soloing is, you'll be out of the phase where soloing even matters with almost every class quicker than you thought possible.
