Croaakk
1) Give us the ability to sell-back our LDoN Equipment to the LDoN merchants at a reduced amount of adventure points...maybe a 10% reduction.

2) Give us the ability to remove augments from our armor without destroying them. But maybe reduce their value. IE: if you had a +6 Str augment, and you removed it from your armor, it would become +5 str instead.

3) Give us the ability to combine augments together to make a better one...IE: 2 +5 Poison Resistance Augements = 1 +6 Poison Resistance Augment.

4) Give us the ability to sell dropped augments from adventures to LDoN Merchants at a reduced rate...IE: if a +5 augment cost 100 points from merchant, and we got one that dropped in an adventure, let us sell it to them for 90 adventure points.

5) Make treasure chests worth more. Give them better items or more $$. (Rogues would be happy!)
