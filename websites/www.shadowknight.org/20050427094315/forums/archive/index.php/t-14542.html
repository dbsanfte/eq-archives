kassdax
On a couple other message boards I visit several people over the past have posted their views on what strength does for damage output.

Pretty much the general consesus is that strength doesn't really do that much. Yes it does make a difference but a few parses have shown that a difference in strength of 100 points made about a 2% difference in damage output on mobs such as Vindi when two similarly equipped melee's were beating the stuffings out of him.

Personally I haven't tested stuff like this myself but do tend to listen to certain people that have fed me verifiable information in the past. Hope this helps but as always, take what you read on the net with a grain of salt.
