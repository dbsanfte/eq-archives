Originally posted by Sepcieulus
Some might think this is silly but you might also want to consider Looks.
That is not silly at all. I chose Iksar for my SK based entirely on his looks. Since then, my feelings based on playing him to 52 and playing several other SKs/races to 20-30 are as follows. Keep in mind that I played a druid to 25 before I started on my SK, and my SK is my main (the druid is level 30 now!). My perceptions are affected by his relatively low level, no doubt, but also by his being my only 30+ character.

- Regen is noticeable at a far greater level range than I had anticipated. Playing non-troll/Iksar melee characters (2 SKs and 2 paladins), I was amazed at how much downtime I have, even at low levels. At level 20 my human paladin regens 1/3 per tick standing/sitting. At level 1 my Iksar SK was regenning 2/4. Even now, my 52 Iksar SK seems to spend much less time regenning his HP than my 23 paladin does. Of course, at 52 my SK is regenning 6/12 HP. :)

- Being 'unstunnable' from the front still seems vastly over-rated to me. It is a convenient thing to have if you have it, but I don't miss not having it, and I wouldn't trade it for my edge in regen. I rarely get stunned at all. I am either fear-kiting, in which case I am not being hit. Or I am not tanking in a group, in which case I am not being hit. Or I am tanking, in which case my main goal is to absorb damage and keep aggro. I might get stunned 2-3 times in a fight, but often I won't get stunned more than once, which means I hardly even notice it. It doesn't affect my DPS much, nor does it affect my ability to keep aggro.

- Having balanced stats appealed to me at first, but in the long run isn't an advantage. The better melee stats of an OGR or TRL are preferable, IMO. Once AGI is at 75 there's no real use to raising it further, and the high STA for them means you can look for more +STR items, which are plentiful. Having low INT isn't so bad if you get +mana items, like the player-crafted jewelry that adds HP and mana.

In the end, as is often said, find a race that you would prefer to play, either due to looks, or due to playstyle.
