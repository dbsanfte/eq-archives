Originally posted by Kebsis
Could someone explain 'x-teaming'? I only have a vague idea of what it means.

On Tallon and Vallon there are 4 teams. These teams are regular blue to each other (can't stun, AoEs won't hit, can't melee), and other teams are like mobs (can stun, AoE, melee, etc.) - the teams are blind, elf, dark and short.

Because of teaming rules, if you are a high elf enchanter grouped with a ogre SK (on a different team) and a wood elf ranger (same team), and you stun, the ogre will make a resist check and could be stunned, but the ranger will get "enchanter tries to cast on you but you are immune" message.

In the same way, if a dark elf SK decides to go to Kelethin and kill everyone, and he has a high elf cleric following him around, that cleric can heal him, but no one who is an elf can do anything about the cleric the cleric since the cleric is on the elf team (a dwarf or gnome could though). That's cross teaming.
