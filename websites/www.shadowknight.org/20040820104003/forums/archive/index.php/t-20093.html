Greetings Naubi. In terms of progression quests I can only think of 3 off the top of my head. The first would be the coldain ring quest. This is a fun quest and most people do up to the 8th ring cause it gives you a nice clicky. A 8 point DS that stacks with our banshee aura and cloak spell line. If your guild is strong you can even get to the 10th ring. The stats are decent and you get a 10 ds, 10 regen and 10 attack buff. Plus there are a few decent rewards still from the war like the faceguard of bentos the hero and the true prize, crown of narandi.

Another progression quest is the shawls. If you are a tradeskill freak it's perfect. The 8th shawl has FT3 and a focus effect that makes your buffs last 20 percent longer. Not the best effect for sks but still not bad also. The stats are ok too but I feel that FT3 makes up for the low ac on the shawl.

A third is the Greenmist quest. It's progression cause you have to do 7 khukri quests before you can even forge Greenmist. Greenmist is still not a bad weapon either by today's standards. Ranks up there as probably in the top 10 of the one handers.
The epic is a fun quest overall except for the decrepit hide and possibly soul leech. If you can stand testing your luck and possibly spending lots of time in hate or fear then IC is for you.

Eyepatch of Plunder is a good quest though I will say this, if you can stand the hate part of our epic, then by all means Stormfeather should be cake to camp. Just watch out for Ksers. The patch still has decent stats despite the effect (useless when you got a shaman/chanter/beastlord hasting).

Finally the Wood Elf Crown is a neat quest to do when you are bored. It's funny to pretend to be a wanna-be ranger or check out your guy in Wood Elf form. The factioning will take what seems like forever but it pays off. Reet crown is not a problem and if you are daring and brave you could try soloing the reets for the crown. I recommend buying the velium mastodon fur cloak cause ice burrowers (61 mobs) in WW are tough mobs and are one of few phs for mastodons. The fur itself is an uncommon drop.

Hopefully you will enjoy these quests naubi. I have done all of them except for 9th and 10th ring and the shawl quest (not a tradeskill freak). Currently working on the epic and I just need the hide. Then the rest will be cake.

However, I am semi-retired from EQ so I may not finish the epic. It all depends on when I feel like playing again and also if I come back and my friend has the hide available for MQ.

The gate hammer is easy to get, unlimited use, no reagent, no flags. Basically anywhere a proc is possible, either on mobs or duelling, the hammer is better. The neck requires elemental access and snagging a bunch of drops to get the unlimited version, which would be the only one practical for continual use, but still requires a ~100pp reagent. Really, you are best off just getting the OT hammer and carrying a gate potion or Thurg potion as a rarely needed backup. I have done so and have needed to use the Thurg potion maybe 4 times in 3 years.
