Menon Lifeblight
Klotar, we do this with 30ish all the time. No VT, no EP gear.

Have a good snare group of druids and rangers. Festering works well also. It is nice to have a couple chanters to tash the 3rd wave. They are very MR and tash will help snares land. I also use AoD on the third wave, every bit helps. Get everything snared ASAP. Mobs are easy to tank, the only problem is letting them run free.

If you have two bards with the gate song, you are guaranteed success. Just have them hang out by the towers and handle runners.

Kill the captains/generals/warlords last on each wave.

We like to kill the spearmen to the west (toward velks) during the first wave (one group will mow them down, and first wave is numbingly easy) so we can engage the pops there earlier, but it's not necessary.

No reason to bother with the MQ, feel free to run around and fight as the risk of death is pretty minimal. If you lose, it will be due to runners, not because your force was wiped by giants.

From my experience, its nice to have a force of about 40. We have never failed with 40+. Success between 30 and 40 is spotty. Less than 30 would take a truly well coordinated force that really knows where to stand. Even with a capable force, expect to fail once learning where the waves spawn.

No idea on whether someone else can start the war or not.

As someone else said, when you fail its generally because of a runner getting though. This can happen on waves one and two, but is most likely to happen on wave 3 because they are very resistant and root/snare do not seem to work unless you are on top of the mob. Also, remember to kill the boss of each wave LAST or you will start the timer on the next spawn. Two spawns at once on the second or third waves is almost certain failure.
