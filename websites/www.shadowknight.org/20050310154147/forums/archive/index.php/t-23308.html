Breeder doesn't return to his spawn as quickly as deathsting, or any other mob in the room. Thus, he's not hard to split from deathsting. If he kept warping in on your kills, your monk fucked up. Warping is really bad in these tunnels, so the puller has to be extra careful there really isn't anything extra aggroed on him.

You should have been doing the kiting/training with harmshield. At the speed the mobs run, and the number of mobs in the way, it's pretty much impossible to keep the monk from dieing.

This camp is a bitch. The fight basically comes down to pulling/splitting off the spawns and getting breeder single.
