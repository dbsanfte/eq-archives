05-24-2002, 12:22 PM
Illia's says they are 29, but that's WAY off. My lvl 39 (lvl 20 necro) pet can nearly solo one unbuffed. They have around 5-600 hps or so. They hit for mid 30s maximum. Given that, I'd expect them to be around lvl 19-22. The Lead Explorers are a bit higher -- around 700 hps, so probably closer to 24 and are paladins, so they will LoH.

I'd say by 25, you should be able to solo them, if you can break them up. They've been reported as pretty easy for their /con. Were I you, I'd set up shop in Lake of Ill Omen and do pick up groups there till then, and if you happen to see a higher level evil in zone, maybe they'll break the spawn for you. I think at 25, you'd have a hard time keeping up with the spawn solo but could probably keep half the area cleared. If you can get a partner to help out you should have little problems with clearing it.

Also, the explorers are KoS to cats/skeletons/goblins. Often one will roam into aggro range and when they run, pull explorers away from the tower. If you have a hard time pulling singles, you can often find some of them standing outside for a while after killing a hostile mob.

There are quite a few wandering high teens around the explorer tower (undead/Iksar/goblins/cats). So plenty of mobs to keep you and a mate occupied at that level. They are easily avoided if you are remotely aware of your surroundings.

Can't speak to the xp rendered when turning in the sets of 4 knives. At 48, I didn't even notice it.

In addition, killing the explorers/gobs and Iksar will give you Legions faction. In the course of obtaining 5-6 stacks of knives, you should become Dubious to Trooper Vaurk without needing to work over no xp green mobs. Once dubious, you can turn the knives in.

If getting Cab faction is a high priority for you, you can fix it in the course of xping in your mid 20s. And if you do the LoIO thing (which I don't typically advise, but in this case I would), you can power thru your 20s and if opportunity presents, have your cab faction completed save BroodofKotiz.
