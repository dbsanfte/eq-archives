Katta!

Made half an aa there soloing on and off while doing laundry yesterday. Weapons sell for 4-6 plat while the uncommon armor drops sell for 10-20, which makes loot not THAT bad at the end of the day (would up making about 400pp yesterday). Mobs also drop up to 6 plat (rare) but around 2-3 average.

Low risk, fast exp. If you can get a port it's easy to get too. Guards by Graveyard or bank would work well for you. Oh and it's a hot zone so it has a PC like exp modifier for a time.

EDIT ADD: Teraterpa's (the turtles in natimbi) I tried also last night. Not hard to fear kite, less exp per kill than in Katta and kills took longer. Much better loot though (Got a blue diamond and a diamond in less than 10 kills - though I got lucky)
