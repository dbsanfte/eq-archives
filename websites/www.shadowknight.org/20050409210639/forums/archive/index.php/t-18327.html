Blargon
There's a small window of space you can stand on which you won't get aggro from the 4-arms above (this is on the remains platform). Flop there, then stand up and flop again if you get aggro, move a tiny bit one way or another, repeat til you don't get aggro. You can't be large-sized, you have to be at biggest, human sized. Kill all the remains except the one closest to the cloaks out in the room (they will aggro). Bring a gate potion so you don't have to flop back out. =) It's very cool to get frame this way.
