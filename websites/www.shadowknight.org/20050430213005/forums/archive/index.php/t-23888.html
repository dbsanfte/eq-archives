Menon Lifeblight
It's going to be rocky with you tanking in RS. Even with a great slower and healer, and perfect single pulls, you will die occasionally anyway. These mobs will quad + bash/kick for close to 1300 a pop. A couple unlucky rounds before the mobs is slowed and you're toast.

Three pieces of advice. First, get your OoW defensive AAs. You put them off too long already. Second, learn to pull RS like a pro. Although snare splitting isn't real important in most camps, it's easy to mistime a wanderer and bring back two mobs. Learn the number of wanderers, pathing, and spawn points of the mobs, always bring singles, and you'll start getting a lot of group invites. Intelligent pullers make or break groups in RS. You don't have to tank to be useful here. Third, if you're going to tank, bring a necro. The necro can keep the mana up on your cleric who will be fast healing, and can FD/rez when the shit hits the fan. As Elkar said, the loot in this zone is worth a few wipes.

You can check my magelo. Even when I buff to 2.3k AC/12k hp, with full defensive AAs, this zone is no picnic.

I tank RS with my SK bot (http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=240672) grouped with a Shaman and Cleric. The extra HPs from the Shaman buffs along with the support healing makes it alot easier, and of course having Slow is vital. If your cleric can handle the extra mana tax, getting buffed with Panoply of Vie also helps smooth out damage spikes and makes the initial pull (before Slow has landed) safer.

The only difficulty I have had is with mobs that Charm or mesmerize. I haven't tried the self-dot potion to handle mezz but I assume this would be just as effective as always.

I never tanked it before level 70 or with less than max defensive AAs through GoD (excluding OoW AAs), but I suspect it would be a serious challenge to a toon lacking VT+ gear.

If you only have a single healer (Cleric), I think you would need at least 10k HP and 2k AC, and at least the "basic" defensive AAs for dodge and mitigation to pull it off without frequent downtime. Shield Block makes a difference too since dodging even 7% of the time is remarkable when the mob can quad for up to 1.3k damage.
