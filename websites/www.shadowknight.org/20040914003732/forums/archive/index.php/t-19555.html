Klotar AngedeMorts
Aura of Pain: Yes, I agree it is nice but also don't use it much myself, except for a big fight where I am MT. There was a brief period (like just after I got the spell) where I had it up and used it all the time but hardly do any more. It's resisted a bit more than I'd like. I generally don't use Torrents either, buff slots are too precious.

Blood of Hate: I don't use it much any more unless I am in a big fight where I am not MT. I agree it's a very nice spell but at the time I got it, it seemed too mana costly; however, I'm going to look at it again now that my int/mana pool is up.

Shroud of Chaos: I started using this the moment I got it, but went back to Mental Corruption for a long time. Duration is nice compared to MC, meaning I could free up a spell slot, and I just started using this again more recently as my FT increased.

Terror of Thule: I only use Terrors as a mez breaker when there is an ENC in group [with bard mez, I find I don't have to break it], I never use it any other time. That said, most times there *is* an enchanter in group, and a few points of guaranteed hate doesn't hurt when you fail pre-break taunt 3 times in a row.

I realize how dumb I sound saying "these spells are all great but I never use 'em". I generally don't use dot's in xp groups (lately mostly LDoN adv's) but use both Ignite Blood and Blood of Hate when duo'ing for xp, since I have to do a bit more than hold aggro and swing-miss, otherwise the fight will drag out. As for SoC, I am finally at the point where I think I have enough FT to forego MC in favor of a bit more dps/healing from the proc... but at the time I got the spell I had FT1 and MC1 so went with using MC. And Terrors, as I stated above, I generally only use for mez breaking... IMO it's not good for much else, at least for me.

1. aura of pain - most of my grouping these days is in ldon and i'm tanking so i use this all the time. 120 points or what not of ac definately helps and the mana cost is next to nothing.

2. shroud of chaos - didn't start using this much until i got my FT up to 4 and now i don't suffer quite as much without mental corruption up, though depending on the mana regen buffs i have i still sometimes go with MC. if you have a high FT total though , i'd imagine you'd be using this all the time. i have no data on this but it seems like it procs more than MC too.

3. blood of pain - the best of them in my opinion. run out and get this first, it's a nice dps boost. and the mobs only need to stand for 30 seconds for it to be effective. lots of things that resist disease don't resist poison so that's real nice.

4. terror of thule - i think it's useless. if i need a non mez break aggro spell i'll use fear. i think at lvl 62 the majority of what you're fighting is 55+ so i'd say only buy this is you are a completist.
