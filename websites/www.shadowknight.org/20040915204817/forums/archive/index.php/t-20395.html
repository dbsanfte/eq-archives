NPC's won't talk to you until you are at least 50. The reason that there were lower levels walking around with the epic is because the quest was changed from its original form. Used to be that in the final battle, you looted Innoruuk's Curse from Lhranc. So, a high level player could do the quest, and a low level character could loot the epic. Now, when you do the final handin to Lhranc, you recieve a coin. You then give the coin to Marl Kastane, who spawns after Lhranc is dead, and recieve the epic. So you can't MQ with low levels anymore that I know of.
