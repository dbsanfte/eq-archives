Nambkab
I made this camp an art form on Nameless, so here goes:

What Ainilye said about the location is right. Basically kill the guards at the top of the stairs, kill the wandering guard (Xantos I believe) and the guard in the bard room (named Bach, he will add as you kite). Then go to the noble room and stand in front of the door that looks straight in on the nobles. Tag one with Darkness and it should single pull at 52 with no difficulties. Fear as soon as it pops up then beat it as it walks away from you. It will path down the stairs, and normally I would refear when it got to the bottom if it wasn't dead yet.

Now, if it doesn't come single you have a couple of options. Personally, I never had luck with FD splitting, no matter how many times I let one return to spawn point, it would always re-aggro. You can try to split this way regardless, but the best way I found was to dark one, target the non-snared one as it runs at you, then fear it. As it runs helter-skelter through the zone (strangely won't aggro anything else when it returns) fear the snared one and beat it down. Fear snared one again at bottom of stairs, then wait for the other to come running back (fear should be gone on runner at this point). When it rounds the corner for you, re-fear, then finish off the snared one. go sit at stairs where you can see corner of hallway and wait for fear to break again. When he comes back this time, snare then fear and kill away.

Good luck to ya! It is a monotonous camp to be sure, but it is great xp.
