DConquest
I don't really see the benefit of using Terror and HS in an Exp group. I only pull HS out for specialized pulls, and CD is pretty much all you need for aggro. Which brings me to my next answer... No, CD shouldn't be retired. It's a workable snare, cheap, fast, great aggro. Festering is great for FD splits, snaring Raid mobs, solo'ing, snareable named pulls... for everything else CD should do the trick.

Both Aura of Hate and Pain should be up if you can stand the button mashing. The 100+ AC it provides is roughly a 5 percent midigation increase... Whoops, there goes the Warrior bonus in exp groups. Aura of Hate adds roughly 6 - 10 dps to each melee including you, pretty worthwhile especially with more than 1 melee in the group.
