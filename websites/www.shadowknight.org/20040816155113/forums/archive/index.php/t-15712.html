Boy it seemed like the damn luggald saliva was never going to drop for me. I got 6 bloods and 8 biles before my first saliva dropped.

Just to confirm the locations. If you go past the octopus bridge, take your first right. Follow the corridor to a large room with the blackblood assassin. Kill everything in this room for best results. Blood, Bile, and Saliva seem to drop from all of these guys.

For those looking for organs to finish Blood of Pain (nice spell IMO), the organs seem to drop from any luggald starting at the entrance to the luggald area all the way up to the octopus bridge. Actually got another one I didn't need last night off an incantor of malevolence on the far side of the bridge.
