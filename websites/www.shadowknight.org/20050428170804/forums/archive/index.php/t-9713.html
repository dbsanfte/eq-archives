Originally posted by Tarniq
I went 15 into sta to bring it up to 90 and the rest into str giving a 75.
That's a pretty good breakdown, since you have 108 INT and 85 AGI/DEX. Gnomes have a pretty good stat layout if you spend your points right, plus they taste great on toast ( <-- obligatory gnome-as-food remark ).

I'd get every skill increased, if possible. I find that it is possible to keep two skills maxed until around level 25-27, then you will find it pretty easy to keep three skills maxed, and one or two others increasing, until around level 36-37, when the skill increases begin to really slow down. At level 39, your weapon skills cap at 200, and the caps don't increase again until level 51. So you have all of those levels in which to max them all if you wish.

My 52 SK has 1HS/2HS at 206, 1HB at 200, piercing at 199, and 2HB at 139. My 30 SK has maxed his 1HS/1HB/2HS (155) and has 2Hb at 52 and piercing at 70. Trying keeping them all at some level that isn't too far behind, because it is aggravating to use a 2HB weapon at level 40 with a skill of 5. :)

Put all 20 points into this stat on the character select screen or you will find it somewhat difficult to raise it or keep it above 100 in the early goings.

Strength can easily be overcome by str items (extremely common) or you can always buy a woven frostgiant beard (5 charges 40str buff) in the bazaar for 5-15 plat.

I personally almost always use my 2 hander (real men swing 2 handers!) but i wouldnt neglect the sword and shield approach especially with some of the nice shields and 1 handers that have become available in recent expansions.
