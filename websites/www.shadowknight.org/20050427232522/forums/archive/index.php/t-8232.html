HELLO FELLOW ENTHROPIST!!

To answer some of your questions as a fresh Shadowknight serving Bertoxx:

--The difficulty getting the armor is as hard as getting any other newbie armor. Except there might be a little difficulty getting whatever bits and pieces you need from Qeynos Hills. Holly's going to be stalking you (but come lvl 35 and you can go back there and fear kite her for every time she's killed you ^_^)

--Factionwise, you're about equal KOS in the world as any "good" character is. We can go in Neriak just fine, but don't bother with Oggok and Grobb: They just want you in a pot of stew. You'll be surprised at the places you can travel (You're probably going to be dubious, but it's still safe travel. Con everything just in case). You will be fine in OT as well.

--As for quest related mishaps: There is nothing different about you that other Shadowknights have to go through for your quests (When you go hunt Darkforge, though, watch out for the ogre guard by the CZ isle entrance!)

Anyways: Best of luck to you, fellow enthropist~! Follow the path of bertoxxulous and let all those who oppose you decay and rot! ^.^ Send a PM if you have any further questions!

Congratulations, you have taken one of the roads less traveled. Your early life will be quite hard, the folk surrounding Qeynos hate us with good reason.

The gnolls of Blackburrow our "allies" are your enemies.
The Barbarians of Halas and Everfrost and West Karana will kill you on sight.
The Erudites of Paineel will kill you on site as well.
Even in our home town we must be careful not to get too close to the paladin and cleric guild.

You will be able to enter Neriak but dont expect to be much liked at first.

Grobb and Oggok are KOS to you.
Faydwer is KOS to you, including most of Akanon I think.

Bertox is the most hated god in the game as far as faction goes.
Freeport is not KOS.

I would quest your newbie armor in the Qhills area and be glad that you have such a fine quest to do. Once you reach your 15th season I would consider heading to Paludal Caverns for excellent experience. If you cannot head to the moon, I would travel to either the Warrens or to the Freeport area.

The posters above were right about the low numbers of Priests of the Plague lord as well, when the time comes you will have to seek one out for the special armor.

I play a 48th season Shadow Knight on the FV server. It has been a lot of fun and is a great change of pace from the Hate, Fear, or Glory seeking fanatics.

I am also a human, bertoxx SK. Starting points I put a bunch in stamina and then put maybe 5 in some other random stat, like AGI for some reason. The key is you want all your melee stats above 75 and the rest in Stamina.

Anyway, as for being KoS... this shouldn't really be a problem. Like other people have posted, it's the REALLY good aligned places that hate you, and of course... druids hate you. Surprisingly, I found I was only dubious in Felwithe at the start.

I skipped out on the whole blackburrow train-station fun and went straight to the warrens for those middle years. That gets your faction up with Paineel and the GUARDS in Erudin. The clerics and paladins in Erudin will still hate your guts, obviously...

I don't think I was KoS to Neriak ever. There is that one guard at the bar in the foreign quarter who is threatening for some reason, but he's pretty low level. The ghouls in the necro section were KoS too.

Freeport was not a problem. I didn't ever go searching out the dwarf guy or that drunk high elf paladin chick, but... they're in one building in West Freeport that's easily avoided.

The temple of Solusek Ro which is where you do DF armor quests is indifferent to everyone at the start, even Iksar, for all the good it does them.

I think you start at dubious with the outpost in OT. It is on VS faction, and so are those ogres on the stop-over island for the boat in Timorous Deep.

That having been said... you are now free to ruin your faction as you see fit. Personally I found no use for being non-KoS in feydwer, so I killed a few elves here and there, hunted in PoG eventually, etc. I would NOT suggest killing orcs in CB or gfay as it ruins your faction with the Dark Elf warrior guild, which could cause you problems if you ever want to go there.

I trashed my faction with VS, so now I'm KoS in the OT outpost, but I figured it was a good tradeoff for being able to hunt in KC and HS. Plus I did a ton of faction work so I could do the Greenmist quest in Cabilis which eventually requires killing Drusella Sathir.

In general, I would say you have very little faction trouble with a bertoxx human SK. Especially now when you can just go up to PoK from just about anywhere to buy, sell, train skills, etc. And you will have the advantage of being loved by the SK guild in Qeynos, where you have to do the turn-in for the decrepid sheath. .... of course, if you want to do that stupid Qeynos badge quest, you might be out of luck, but....
