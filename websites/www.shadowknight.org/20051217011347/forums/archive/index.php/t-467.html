Hello,
Science of War is a guild in formation on the Stormhammer server and is now recruiting shadowknights who know their stuff.

(The reason I've come to this forum is because I believe that class boards are full of people who know how to play their class really well or are at least interested in improving on their skills by learning from others. Those are exactly the people that we are looking to recruit).

I'm convinced that there is a core of extremely talented players on the Stormhammer server, and we aim to get them into this guild so that we can do things that are not done on other Everquest servers. Isn't it time that the "Legends" environment lived up to its moniker? Let's create a great guild.

If you are interested in applying, please visit http://www.scienceofwar.net and get to know us. Read the FAQs, evaluate our goals, familiarize yourself with our recruiting requirements, and spend a little time thinking about whether or not you'd also like to apply for a position of officership (we primarily need class recruiters and class leaders right now (we don't have a Shadowknight class leader yet, and class leaders are the foundation of the guild -- so please apply if you think you're up for the job!).

I think that you'll appreciate the fact that one requirement for getting in is that SKs demonstrate knowledge of what it means to play a their class really well. We therefore ask that all applicants post a guide on SOME aspect of being an SK to these boards as part of their applications. Maybe we'll see a few interesting guides come out of it, at the least. =)

Thanks, and good luck.
