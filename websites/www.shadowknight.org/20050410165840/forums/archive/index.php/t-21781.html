Dakkin
People who parse a lot say ripostes are a small proportion of incoming damage from mobs while tanking.

Basic rule is avoid it anywhere you don't want to give the mob a chance to get the occasional free hit on you.

So when tanking heavy hitting named you're not sure you can survive, you might turn attack off and use spell aggro only. And when raiding heavy hitters, avoid the front of the mob so you don't pick up the odd 2K riposte and create a nuisance for your group healer.

Oh and 1 handers are supposed to be worse for ripostes than 2 handers like Slein says.

Vengyr
Mobs will Enrage when they reach low health (usually around 11% or under). It doesn't multiply the damage they deal, what actually happens is that the mob gets an automatic riposte on all attacks that hit him from the front. Basically, against any mob that Enrages, you should stand behind it, and you will be safe. Be careful though if you choose to continue attacking from behind an Enraged mob, as the main tank will have switched attack off, so you might get aggro (though as SKs are not exactly a high DPS class, that's not something we have to worry about :rolleyes: ) You'd be surprised how many level 65s still eat Enrage death on raids, even in Elementals....
