Here is the walk through that I have.

Talk to Harla Dar who can be found in the Western Wastes. He has lost a hatchling named Frakadar and will reward you if you can Frakadar for her.

Well as it turns out, Frakadar was eaten by Zlandicar, a huge dragon who lives in the Dragon Necropolis. Zlandicar will sopmetimes drop Frakadar's Talisman when he's killed. Return this item to Harla Dar and you will be rewarded with the Shroud of the Dar Brood.
