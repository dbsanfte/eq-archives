Josahd
Max hit is solely dependant on the weapon, not your atk.

The distribution of hits is dependant on your atk.

Max hit with a WMBS IIRC is 218. With low atk, you won't hit for that often, but you'll still get it now and then.

With high atk it just means your average hit goes up, and you hit for max more frequently, but you still can't hit for more than that.

The only way you'll hit over 300 with a WMBS is with a critical, it doesn't matter how much atk you have.
