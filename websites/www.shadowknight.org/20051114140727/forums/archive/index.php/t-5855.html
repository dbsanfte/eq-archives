I think most of your stats are low for being 57. I a 55 Iksar Shadow Knight, but I've been playing for 3 years and have better than average gear.

I think my unbuffed stats are as follows

STR 179
STA 132
AGI 95 (or close)
DEX 120 (or so)
INT 127

HP are close to 2800. Max buffed with Aego and Shaman buffs is around 4k I think
AC is close to 1100. Max AC has been 1182 with buffs

Really, stats don't mean a lot i nthe high end of the game though. What matters is Hit points and haste. Stats can be buffed up so much by your local Cleric, Shaman, Druid (sometimes stackable with Ranger and Beastlord) that all our stats are close to max no matter what we started at. And as SKs, we will only be taking hits if we are a few levels higher or our gear is FAR superior to a warrior (of the same level). So it comes down to how much damage you can dish out, and being able to taunt the mobs off the MT in the rare situation it's needed. Or as the puller.

Concentrate on getting to 1200 AC (buffed) and do whatever you can to increase hitpoints without sacrificing any stats. Don't sacrifice haste for anything and be a good damage dealer (best damage/delay ratio you can get.)
