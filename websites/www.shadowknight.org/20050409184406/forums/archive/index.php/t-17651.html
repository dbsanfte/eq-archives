Agarwaen
Both have a lot of merit. My current and former main are enc/sk, which I often two boxed, even as mt and puller for small raids, and I commonly 2 box a friends's 62 shaman with one or other of them.

Advantages of the enchanter: lulling, fast crowd control, clarity line, superior haste, ghetto heal rune line, tash, gmr, highly desirable in groups for most levels, gear is very cheap


Advantages of the shaman: dots, regen, sow, canni, heal over time, heals, pet, ghetto cc, more durable than enchanter, highly desirable in most grp situations for all levels, easier to 'fire and forget' than the enc


My view: to two box, provided a single pull, the shaman is significantly better at all levels from 24 on.

Best asset if you want to form larger groups: enchanter. Once you are used to a enc/SK combination for pulling, tanking and cc, you pretty much just need to add a healer and any other 2 classes and you have a 6 person grp capable of anything xp wise in game.

The power of the enchanter is well known and is highly scalable. I find myself constantly astonished at how potent a shaman is in small groups though. Provided there are no CC issues, the shaman sk combo is capable of far more potent kills than enc SK. I suspect necro-shaman is lethal.
