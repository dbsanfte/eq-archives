Aye, experiment with pathing and get a rough estimate on how long it takes to kill so and so mob, using how much mana. When fear breaks move them back towards your camp a little if they are roaming too far. If you don't have much room to work with use regular fear instead of Invoke.

Amphitryon
When I was Fear Kiting in The Grey, I found it useful to stay up on the zonewall but what I found most useful was the terrain itself

Ofter what I would do is look for large boulders out in the area I am pulling through and would pull the mobs through said boulders to my camp, then I would snare/fear them. When they headed back out across the boulder, they have troubled climbing back up them and get delayed, giving you and your pet extra time to do damage.

Doing this kept the range the mobs would run very small, and reduced the chances for an add.

ANother tip, is to find the static trap locations and camp those. (there is one in the SW corner of the zone that gets little traffic. YOu can just trigger it at will whenever you are ready for a mob, then pull it up closer to the zone wall (lots of room for kiting in that corner)
