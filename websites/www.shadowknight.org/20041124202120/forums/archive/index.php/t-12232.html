Not gonna use quotes, but I should be able to answer your question(s).

BTW, I'm 64 Iksar working on 65.

Your pet, no matter how high level you are (64 pet is very nice), it will never be able to solo a mob that can get you experience. Not even your FIRST pet. Your pet after the 20s is green to you, right? (I've been 50+ for over a year now, I can't remember). So, it's the 'same' as a warrior, but it's green. So it's not going to be able to kill anything on it's own that is even lt blue.

About the 61+ arena. I don't know how much level experience it takes to get one AA point. What I do know is that mobs that con dark blue to you in 61 (that you can solo ie flies near the entrance of PoD) will con lt blue to you at 62. The REALLY bad thing is that the amount of XP you get from it GREATLY diminishes. At 61, I was getting 2-3% AA from each kill (solo). At 62, I was getting 1% or less (that's with 100% AA). That's a pretty big difference for just one level.

So, you think "I'll just stay at 60 and rack up the AAs!" But, this is not the best way to get powerful. First thing is that the higher level you are, the better you are at tanking, resisting (such as AoE spells), hitting, and you get more and more powerful spells as you level. AAs give you nifty things like criticals and mana regen. These all have their place, but levels will always be more powerful than any AAs.

Second thing is that it will be hard for you to get to the Teir 2 planes being level 60. It's not impossible, but it will be MUCH easier for your group to flag you if you are above 61, the higher the better. Once you get to Teir 2, the XP is MUCH easier better. I solo the GY frogs in PoS all the time. I get 1-2% per kill (and it's about 9% AA I've heard). It takes me about 15 minutes to kill a frog, but it's better than sitting in PoV shouting "64SK LFG" for an hour.

Hope this helps you in deciding how you want to allocate your XP :)
