I load Shroud of hate and torrent of hate on every raids, and cast em on every mobs.

_Shroud of hate is magic based and extremely low resist, if a mob resists it, then this mob is 100% MR and will resist non-disced HT.
_Torrent of Hate is disease based and has extremely low resist as well, if a mob resists it, then this mob is 100% DR and will resist disced HT.

Cast both spells on mob to know wich HT he would resist, so you don't burn it for nothing.
I know a very few mobs that resist both.

Resists on HT are not a problem for me.
