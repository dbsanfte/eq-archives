That's right yes, bow and arrow range add together.

As far as I know, the best easily made arrows have about 150 range (eqtraders.com describes how to make them) so with your bow your total range would be 370, about twice your spell casting range.

Bow pulling is very under-rated.

No we can't do jack-shit dmg with our 75 archery skill cap, but if you want to tag a nearby mob and have time to FD almost instantaneously without a spell refresh getting in the way if things go wrong, then bow pulling rocks.
