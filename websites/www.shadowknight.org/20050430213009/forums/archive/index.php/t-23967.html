Did this zone as my mage with a shaman partner years ago, liked it than, fairly enjoyable now.

Target's Legionnaire's, spot I like is by bank. If you zone in from twilight sea's, hang a right jump off first bridge, in the Water is a emblem on the wall- click it ports you in center of town- At the bank. How nifty can you get.

At the wall that overlooks the water is where you pull. Mobs are single pullable at 58, I think 55 is what you need to be to guarantee singles. No trick, otherwise you get to split with FD ect.

Darkness, use lowest lvl fear, few pathing errors if you can find them. Invoke lasts too long, can get you adds. Fear lasts long enough where 2 and they're dead.

minor points-
*-*I used Mental Corruption at all times, no downtime what-so-ever buffless.
*-*No loot, what-so-ever also. 1-3plat maybe, drops armor that's sellable but if you're already kos to the town you can't sell it back to them.
*-*58 pet helped quite a bit, waitin on mob to reset when fear wears off to back him up he did quite a bit of overall dmg
*-*1% normal exp every 3 DB kills, 1% aa each kill
*-*Out of the way to get to, I personally spent 150p for Potion of Twilight Mists to be basicly outside of the town. Than use my OT hammer to gate back to outside of PoK book-OT hammer for the win.
