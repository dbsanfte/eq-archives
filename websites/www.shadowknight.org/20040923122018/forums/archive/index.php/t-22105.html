The original poster's spell lineup looks fine for his level, though maybe I would switch out the lowest spear for Aura of Hate. Personal preference wins out on that one. I posted my lineup at level 65 without any Gates spells because the question was what do other SKs use for exp grouping. For raiding, I often drop the spear and replace it with blood of hate, and I will ditch the pet buff / self buff slot for Harmshield and just rotate out other spells when I need to buff.
