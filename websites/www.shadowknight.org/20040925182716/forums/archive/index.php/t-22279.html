At its core the focus itemization of GoD SUCKS. The majority of the SK dilemma as it relates to foci are a result of that poor general design.

Make a Magelo of what you would wear as a tank. Then compare that to a 'focused' Magelo. Then notice the shortcomings in shielding, avoidance, ac, and hp LOST trying to improve us in the all around fashion that should take place.

Focus 'splintering' has broadened. While still affecting SKs more than Paladins the latter are by no means happy. I have gone from needing:

Healing mod, distance mod, beneficial preservation mod

To needing:

Targeting healing mod, group healing mod, beneficial distance mod, detrimental distance mod, and preservation mod.

The 'best tank' setup for us now has me with Targeted healing mod, a 30% beneficial haste mod, a 23% all spell haste mod (irrelevent for me) a magic DD focus (irrelevent for me), and general preservation.

At the moment I am overjoyed that there are no great upgrades to RZ visor in GoD, lest I have to replace that general distance mod with yet TWO more 'bed/det' mods.

As tanks:
We are penalized by skill caps
We are penalized by the lack of specialization
We are penalized by lower value mana regen on the clicks we can use
We are penalized by raw hp
We are penalized by HP AAs
We are penalized by AC tables
We are penalized by DI mod
We are penalized by lack of effective tanking disciplines

But apparently that is not YET ENOUGH.

Now we are to be penalized with itemization. We either have to choose to keep our durability up and let our spells suffer, or we are play 'scavenger hunt' to bolster our spells and fall even further behind in tanking.

Personally... I think they should nerf the crap out of endurance regen and the various 'pure melee abilities'. Then place the regen and 'foci' they need to reach their full power on a slew of items and let them feel the sting of equipment selection.
