Talk to Holly Longtail who can be found in the Plane of Knowledge. She will tell you that she collects stuffed animals and would love to obtain some replacement parts as a couple of her animals have been damaged. She will then ask you to speak to Marri, and obtain a Collector's Box. Obtain an Undead Froglok Tongue, a Cockatrice Beak, a High Quality Cougarskin, and a Tiny Rockhopper Eye. Place all four items in the box and hit combine. Now return it to Holly and you'll be rewarded with the Fine Antique Ring.

other quests similar in the same room, this one is best though, and all can be gotten in the bazaar.
