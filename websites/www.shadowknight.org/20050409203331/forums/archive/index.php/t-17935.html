Oggim
You get more adventure points per win based on your level at the time you complete the mission. For example, if you have a group of all 65 players everyone will get 51 points if you win (on normal). However, if you die back to level 64 during the adventure, you will get either 47 or 49 points (I forget which), while everyone that is still 65 still gets 51 points.

I can't tell you just how big of a power boost 60-63, although I imagine it would help quite a bit, but if you want to get ldon points more quickly I'd suggest getting levels so as to get more points per adventure.

I wouldn't be afraid of the level 65 adventures either, even if you have a total jack-ass in the group setting off traps etc, it is pretty easy to win most adventures on normal. Too bad there isn't a medium level between normal and hard, ah well.

I used to think AA's were better than exp, but have since completely changed my mind. You'll honestly be best off if your entire group hits 65, then does AA's. Don't worry about a 65 adventure being hard. The only requirement to beat basically any adventure is to have some form of healer (cleric, druid, shaman) and some balance between CC and DPS. There is a huge flexibility in what makes a "good LDON group". I've seen 3 necros, 2 mages and a cleric do awesome. I would hazard to say that the ONLY group you do NOT want is a group of 5+ wizards and no tank. Since you're an SK, and you'll always be in your groups, just make sure you got a healing class and otherwise, try to keep a full group of 6 peeps, no matter what classes they are, so long as they're good players, you'll have little to no problems.
