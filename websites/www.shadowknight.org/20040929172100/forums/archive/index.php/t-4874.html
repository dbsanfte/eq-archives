Bulvyne
I am a dark elf, so it's a little different for me, but based on the other advice people have listed here, I'd agree: 5 points into AGI, then the rest you can put where you want.

Looking back on my early-days as an SK, if I re-created my guy, I'd probably have put as many points as possible into STA. But as you gain levels, and find more gear, STR/STA gear isn't that tough to find, while DEX/AGI stuff that compliments other stats/AC/HP isn't as plentiful. Plus, when you get REALLY high up there in levels, STR/STA will be maxxed out pretty easily anyway.

I know some SK's who put a lot of points into INT, and they are quite happy! It's all really up to you! :)
