Jaggnar
I have to second the recommendation to practice your abjuration. Feign Death is a powerful ally - it allows you to get out of trouble in tight situations, and considering you get no pacify/lull spells for living mobs -- it can become a useful way to split-pull a camp. It's boring to sit and cast Endure Cold on yourself over and over again, but trust me - you need it. If you ever make it to 54, you get Banshee Aura too - and if you're abjuration isn't 185 you're not going to be able to reliably cast it. *don't ask me how I know that, lol*

Another recommendation is to practice all of your weapon skills, starting with 1HS and 2HS, and if you have time - work piercing, 1HB, and 2HS as well. Many melees only work on one or two weapons skills because they can take a long time to develop - but when the day comes that you get a nice weapon in a skill you do not possess, you're going to regret having to head out into the field looking for dark blue mobs with a weapon you can't hit a tee-ball with. *heh*
