Rugs Darkbane
The only thing you cant fix is racial advantages and the look of the character in this game.

Get a race that you first like the looks of.

Then weigh the advantages of the other classes (dont look at the stats if its within like 10 points either way).

If I hated the way ogres look, I wouldnt be one just to get the unstunnable feature. If iksars drove me crazy, I wouldnt choose to be one for the ac bonus or regen.

Late in the levels all things come to equality (or close). With buffs and decent gear, everyone maxes their melee skills except maybe dex, agility, and intelligence. If you have primal going, then intelligence is the only one that wont max from buffs alone.

Regen is nice for soloing, dueling, and FD'ing while dotted...its not going to make the cleric any happier when your gettin hit for 500s though.

The unstunnable thing is great if your tryin to get off a spell and are having bad luck, but with the sk spells you can hold aggro even if stunned half the fight.

Slam is really handy but can be cured with 6 aa's.

Raid buffed humans usually have better stats than me since my intel is pathetic (ogre), but then again, nobody ever has the exact same gear so its all relative.

*Note: Just dont put points into str or stam...you will regret that one later unless your in banded armor your whole life :) *
