Oagan
Earthseer is a static spawn above the king's camp. Actually, you can do the king's camp and still kill the seer/ph. The drogan earth totems drop off of Earth Seers. They are RARE. After a shitload of hours (well over 100), I have looted one. I have looted six depradator blades during that time (for aged earring of aptidude).

Named Earth Seer is just inside a small alcove above the king's room. Just inside that alcove is a safe camp spot that will not aggro the other mobs in the outside room, and the roamer that paths by. In this spot you will encounter all the quest piece mobs (cave guardian, dust hoodlum, earth seer, bone depradators, ice masters). Quest drops are just a matter of putting in the time, and killing the mobs.

The only decent thing is that these two camps drop viable bazaar loot (Bloodsworn blades and copper hammer of striking). They also drop up to about 20pp, goblin skins, Mt. Death Salts, and gems up to diamonds. They are all also 59-61lvl mobs, as well, and with the xp modifier in Droga, the xp isn't too shabby (below PoP, but still decent).

The old BL site used to have a great map. The LoY downloadable map has all the spawn points of the named listed, so follow that.

Invis works all over Droga.

Never go to Nurga much, because I can two box all the named in Droga quite easily.

Tukka
I've spent a lot of time Droga, and the drop rates for the rare items you need for the quests, as near as I can tell, is 1/40 to 1/60. This comes from parsing over multiple hunts (comparing the number of kills of the appropriate tier mobs to the number of items looted).

Here's my parsed breakdown:

Blade of the Depredator: 1/29.75
(119 killed, 4 looted)
Sash of the Cave Lords: 1/74.5
(149 killed, 2 looted)
Hoodlum Symbol of Dust: 1/17
(102 killed, 6 looted)
Fetish of Ice Mastery: 1/53.5
(107 killed, 2 looted)
Drogan Earth Totem: 1/60
(120 killed, 2 looted)
Total Drops/Kill Ratio: 1/37.31
(597 killed, 16 looted)

I think the hoodlum symbol might be more common than the others, given that the drop rate I experienced for it was much better than the drop rate of the other quest items. The hoodlum symbol is for the quest which results in an all/all ring, so it makes sense that it might be a more common drop, since there would conceivably be more demand for it.
