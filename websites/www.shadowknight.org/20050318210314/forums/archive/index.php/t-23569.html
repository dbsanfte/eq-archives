So, you never bothered to get your tailoring up to 100 and need to for the epic? You have a few k to burn, but don't want to get ripped off?

Well, here's my quick and dirty method to get 100, thought it requires a decent level of brewing and smithing....but brewing and smithing were always easy to get up...(ol tugem's fierce brew to 135 baby....)

First step, use find for the alchemists in plane of tranquility. buy a bunch of mandrake root, and then find yerself a loom and make woven mandrake to 66. Then, picnic baskets to 76.

That ends the easy points. Next is ribbons to 100+. ribbons require farming (and over 100 skill in brewing)... but the parts in the bazaar can frequently be found for much lower than high quality pelts, and non-existant greyhopper hides (search for "cursed" and "steamfont springwater" combinables). In the event that there are none, farm the crap out of kobolds in steamfont for springwater. 15 plat per ribbon plus 1-10 plat per foraged/farmed part may seem like a lot, but it's cheaper than the 30-60 plat per hq hide you get charged in the bazaar. Ok, so the shears mold costs 75 plat, but that's just a one time charge, and the oak staff from the fletchers doesn't cost much.
