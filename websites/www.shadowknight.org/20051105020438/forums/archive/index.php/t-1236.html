Too many players take the 'exp. highway' from 1-50 (Newbie -> NRo -> Oasis -> LoIO -> OT -> DL -> Karnors -> Velks -> Sebilis) are rarely have 2 coppers to rub together.

I am hoping to gather together the wisdom of the SK community to share those sweet spots to level quickly, earn money and/or 'phat lewts' at the same time in this thread ....

I'll start by adding a few of my favorite cash/exp. 'secrets' to this thread...

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
L12-17 Erud's Crossing (Wisp Isle). Bring a magic weapon and head along shore to the left between the dock and the shipreck, hunt those Wisps and take the greater lightstones to the Gypsy's in NK(good) or SRo(evil) for amazing cash and even faction.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
L12-17 Lesser Feydark. 2 camps of bandits dropping bronze weapons with a nearby woodelf Inn to sell at.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
L15-19 West Karana (good alignments). Near the second guard tower is a farmhouse hiding some bandits, the nearby hill houses 3 more 'camps' of bandits. Collecting the sashes (to hand in at S.Qeynos) and bronze weapons will yield good coin, exp. and faction. Evil alignment players will not be able to turn in the sashes to the Temple of Thunder in S.Q.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
L33-48 Crystal Caverns. The Geonids (10 or so) here are on a 14 minute timer, drop chipped gems (worth major pp) in a very safe hunting location. Fast exp. and 1000s of plat for levelling here. Note: Int. casters... the faction you lose will hurt your chances of doing the Pheno Crystal quest.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Well Garb, I really couldnt puzzle out a single word in that post. However, I have my own advice to add:

1-4 Usually your local newbie zone is the best, however, if you wish to start gathering coin at an early age, I suggest the Paineel Newbie area. Hunt the decaying skeletons. Skeletons are on a near instant respawn timer, so there are always plenty around. They drop the common rusty weapons (couple gold) and cracked staves (1p), along with those valuble bone chips. You can eaither start saving the bonechips for your Cabilis faction, or you can sell them to those who are doing their faction.

4-10 If you do your 1-4 leveling in Paineel, consider moving to the Warrens for the next few levels. This will gain you valueble Paineel faction (if you dont already have it) which will allow you to later purchase a key to the Hole. It will also give you a city zone connected directly to a level 50+ dungeon with a 20% exp bonus should you choose to fight here at later levels. In the Hole, collect the Kobold Molars. These can be turned in to a High Guard in the Overlords Palace for additional faction and a random piece of ringmail armor.

10-20 Tough call on this one, however, I would advise doing Gnolls in Blackburrow. It will gain you valuble Qeynos Guard faction along with Qeynos Merchants, Rangers, and others. The only faction you lose is with the Gnolls themselves.

20-30 Paudal Caverns. Now considered to be the fastest exp out there for this level range. I find it humorous how everyone complains about grinding exp soooo slowly in LOIO, yet they continue to go there. I took an alt from 27 to 29 in little more than an hour with a full group at the bandit camp, and walked away with over 500p.

30-40 This is where everything starts to break up. OT is still nice, but finding a group can be a pain and exp can be very slow. I suggest Marus Seru, where you will net a blue or more per solo greyhopper kill.

40-50 DL is still your best bet. You'll find a group without much trouble (unless youre a paladin) and youll get to 50 in due time.

50+ Gotta go with the Hole. Youve got a full service city one zone over, 20% exp bonus, and little traffic.

Best haul was around 50pp in coin and loot after one particular two hour stretch.

Only downtime is to med/rest, there was never a shortage of mobs. The guards are not very far away in case you get in over your head and decide you want to bail. The zone was empty about 75% of the time that I was there. The rest of the time there was one other PC (usually a low level char hunting in the newbie area), or a couple of higher level characters on their way to Trakanon's Teeth. Otherwise, no one there, even though there's a very good newbie area just past the guards.

Do Not Be Conventional. Avoid LoIO and OT. Reasons below.

From 15 to 24, there is NO faster exp in EQ than Paludal Caverns, right by Shadowhaven.

20-26, Netherbian Lair is ok. I got my BP from there.

25-32, Marus Saru - Tremendous exp, no loot.

33-42, WC guards, good exp, +NFP (bank) faction, -WFP, -EFP factions.

33-44, WC druids, you'll need a friend at the lower levels. The best loot/danger ratio you'll see until 50. My SK banked 1800pp there while leveling in not much time recently. In the 30m waiting for the cleric to log on, my SK alt can make 100pp. Maybe more.

35-41, Crystal Caverns. Pharm the velium. It sells to smiths for a lot. Make SURE your invis and FD skills are maxed before you go here. There is a neutral banker at the bottom in Frostone. Dump all the loot here, and sell it when you return the Thurgadin.

35-?, The Goblins outside of Droga. I like the exp I am seeing there. Goblins only assist like types. At 39+ the adventurous can move into Droga.

Why you should not hunt in LoIO or OT. Sarnaks, is why. They are on a faction called "Brood of DiZok". If you don't damage this faction, you can kill Droga-area goblins and improve this faction in short order. Once you're warmly in Chardok, you can do some quests there that yield superb rewards. In addition, you can drag failed raids out of Chardok for plat. You can scout for your guild. Coffins are 70pp each. You should be able to drag for 50pp :-) So 900pp for a 18-corpse raid would be reasonable. Muahaha.
