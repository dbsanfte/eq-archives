Ramgar SK
My best hotkey is tash. It's set up to stand, assist and cast.

looks like this:
/stand
/assist Ramgar
/pause 5
/cast 4

The /pause has to be 5, on /pause 4 I would tash myself 1 out of 5 times.

You could do a similar key with Malo, or slow.

Another useful macro is a double cast:
/stand
/cast 7
/pause 10, /cast 8

Note that /pause # needs to come first in a line for it to work. Also keep in mind longer casting times, and spell recovery.
