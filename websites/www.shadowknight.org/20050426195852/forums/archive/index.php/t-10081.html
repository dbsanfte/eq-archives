Slam is extremely effective against pure casters if you time your slams correctly. At higher levels most casters can channel through your regular hits rather easy making slams/bash more useful.

You will be happy you can slam every time you prevent a caster from gating to safety or blasting you for 700 points of damage.

If you are planning to go high lvl there is always the option of 2h bashing AA points though it is a lvl 59 ability and rather costly :)
