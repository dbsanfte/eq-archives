Not sure what server you are on, but on Xegony, I did 50 to 55 in PoJ. About 9 times out of 10 I would get a group when I did a /shout LFG. After dinging 55 I started having more difficulty, but usually I could get a group at exit, Captains room, and rarely the basement.

The times I couldn't get a group, I would try and form a group from others that were LFG. One time we had a full group without a healer but did ok till we could get one. Also, I found that xp went best if we had 6 in group, so if you get a group of 5 and someone is LFG but you don't need the class, grab them anyway so you can get the best xp.

Nambkab
If I remember correctly you can single pull at 52 without worrying about social aggro. If social aggro is a problem on nobles or bards, what I did was to tag one with dark, other comes faster, fear the non-snared one, then fear the snared one and kill it, may need to refear each one once, and it can be kinda hairy, but once you have done it once, you will be able to do it again. Once the spawn is split, you can pull as soon as one pops and not have to worry about the other one (it won't have spawned yet). For some reason I think the bards always came single for me, but I didn't kill them much because the spawn time is so long.
