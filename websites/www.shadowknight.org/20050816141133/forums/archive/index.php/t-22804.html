Only 58 here, so my targets might not be blue to you.
You should be able to solo tsetian flies easily in POD, go to island straight across from zoneline. Park at shore on the right side, near the 3 trees, actually they are giant hair follicles, and guard pet there. Pull 1st fly, with spear, run back to pet and fear kite. There are at least 4 easy single fly pulls in this area. This is a safe area with no adds, just very boring after a while.
I have also soloed the prisoners in the begining of POJ, just step in to the jail cells and engage them. Just fight them straight up. Only ones that are tough are the healers and the one iksars. The healer dont put out much damage but are very annoying. After 57, the rats and guards wont aggro on you, when you are in the cells. The back jails cells can be done too, but they put out enough damage to me, to not make it worthwhile.

Last thought, I had was Katta castellatum. Not sure of the cons there, but there are some nice fear kiting areas.
