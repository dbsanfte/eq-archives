Klotar AngedeMorts
Although some people disagree with me, I maintain that there are certain mobs in some zones that react differently to spells, proximity, melee, etc.

Mobs that react differently (harder to gain and maintain aggro in my opinion) from the rest of the mobs in LDoN zones are:

MM: Spiders

Tak: Bugs

Mir: Bears, cats, dogs

Ruj: Bears, dogs

Guk: can't remember any offhand but NOT spiders.

The above mobs I have a harder time gaining and holding aggro on. This is after ~325 LDoN successes that I base this on (not bragging just saying I've had plenty of time to decide if it's my imagination or not). *IF* I am puller or *IF* I am breaking mez on a mezzed mob listed above, there are zero issues with aggro. If I am MT and I have to tag off of the puller (doesn't matter what class or what they used to pull with), I have a harder time peeling and holding them than I do any other mobs in the same zone. These mobs may ping pong between group members who have hardly done any damage; whereas any other mobs in the zone don't even look at anyone else but me.

This is for all-65 normal setting adventure groups. While I open myself to a bit of "you suck at aggro" criticism by posting, I believe this to be true dispite anyone else's experiences to the contrary. Staple spells for me = CD, CD and oh, CD.
