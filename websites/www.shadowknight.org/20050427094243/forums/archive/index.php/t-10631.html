I know it may not be seen as a justifable topic or whatever but this is how I see it.

Soloing: People say Unstunnable is useless in Soloing when a person is fear kiting but I think of it as this. If i am low on health or need to get this fear out ASAP. What will help me more regening 2 hp a tick or making sure that I am not stunned and therefore knocked out of the spell? I would definately choose the Unstunnable. Even though i may have to heal up more later it definately makes me take less damage since the target will be feared and not bashing my brains out.

Grouping: If you are grouping you will probably have a healer who should know what he/she is doing. I do see the worth in the regen as it shortens the downtime but being unstunnable from the front allows me to just dish out the damage. Not to mention to keep casting my taps, aggro spells, etc. so that I stay high on HP and my group doesnt have to worry about ping pong.

Raiding: Being stunned on a raid is a biatch esp. if you are the SA or TA. If you are just starting to aggro a mob being stunned will give the the mob the chance to run back after the other raiders esp killing a caster or less importantly a ranger.

I still think that looks is more important when you choose your race as any class and details on stats and whatnot can be ironed out later. I just thought id give my gnomish 2cents in. Ya maybe i am biased and ya im inexperienced in the ways of the SK but I was just giving my input as a third party.
