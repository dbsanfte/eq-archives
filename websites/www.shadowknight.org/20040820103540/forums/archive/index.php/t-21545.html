Just finished this for the FT1 earring with 70hps/stats. It took about 2 weeks getting the blades from the king area and earth seer area. When I went to get the "Mark of Imprisonment" from Jailor Maufan, I wound up getting two more blades in about 4 rounds of spawns.

I know these things are rare (the blades), but I would say that perhaps there's a better drop ratio in the area where the named piece drops, since there's usually more of the quest piece mobs in this area.

BTW, with a 65 Shaman/65 SK 2-box, this was not an easy spawn to break for the named area. Without Virulent Paralysis 3 on the Shaman, it would have probably been a wipe. If the prison warden is up, you're going to get 3 mobs (all lvl 60) in both rooms. You're going to get HT'ed or nuked hard by the mobs here (unless one's a spider).

Recommend a full buff before you camp this spawn if you're 2 boxing. With 3 or more I'm sure it's easier. Maufan drops a very decent monk/bl weapon (17/23 with stats) as his rare, which should fetch some good plat in the bazaar (or nicely twink either if you have an alt).

Of course, I would have to get all the pieces after a patch, which will require a re-freeing of the slaves. Quest NPC's had been up for weeks prior to this latest patch.

Oh well, at least it's ready when they are freed again.
