Groff Gorespiller
If you can get your katta faction up a bit, there is a quest there requiring 3 shissar organs from the revenants around the temple that will net you an EB neck. Don't know what the faction level required is, as I bombed katta faction hunting there long ago.

I duoed there with a shammy partner at around 55, we were trying to get him a Golem's Jawbone. Lots of clears, very rare drop, we never did get it, but it was fun enough. EB requirement and traps and wanderers are annoying, especially the worms, but they're not terribly deadly unless you aren't paying attention. Snakes are easy to Feign split, and golems rarely double if you have your mob rotation down correctly. Golems don't hit hard (well, niether do the snakes, really) and once slowed are cake. Just keep in mind that the golems proc some whirl-till-you-hurl type stun spell that is annoying as crap.

We really only hunted at the temple and at the revenant lake in the north east corner, the rest of the zone just has too many wanderers and traps to have been worth our time at that level, but you might find it more rewarding at a slightly lower level. /shrug
