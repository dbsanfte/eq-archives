Hydian
The best spot for bone chips is the Paineel newbie zone. There is a spot where 2 sets of 3 skeletons spawn. Each set of 3 is instant repop with one caveat...there is anti camping code. This actually makes life easier as you suppress the spawn while looting and then make them pop at will.

When Kunark was first released, I got my bard's faction maxed out on bone chips and farmed them all in that spot. I've also done it for other people. The real limitation is how long it takes you to loot all of those skeletons.

To start it, you will need to kill all of the skeletons in the area as they wander randomly and there is no way to tell which is which, so just kill them all until you see the 6 skeletons wandering away from their spawn.

To find the spot, go into the newbie area until you reach the bottom of the hill directly opposite of the tunnel from the city, then follow that to the left. There is a large rock on the hill at the end of it. That rock marks the radius of the anticamping code. If you keep going straight towards the wall about halfway from where the hill ends, that is where the skeletons spawn. Noclin's pet is a rare spawn in place of one of the sets of 3 as well.

Once you get them spawning you kill them, loot, back up to the rock, rush in and kill the next 6. Rinse. Repeat.
