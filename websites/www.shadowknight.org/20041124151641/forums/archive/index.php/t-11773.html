A breakdown of calculations on all SK spells to date (2/28/03) that do damage, via direct damage or damage over time.

Nukes: # of times you can cast the nuke in 6 min (360 seconds) x max nuke damage = Total damage

Nuke dps: Total damage ٪ 360 = Damage per Second

Nuke Mana Effeciency: Total mana ٪ Total damage = Mana per Damage

Nuke info: The # of times you can cast the nuke in 6 min is rounded down (can't cast a Spear if it's refresh is 20 seconds from when the parse ends...)

Dots: 60 (# of ticks in 6 min) x Damage per tick = Total damage

Dot Mana Effeciency: Total mana ٪ Total damage = Mana per Damage

Dot info: The # of times you can cast the dot in 6 min is rounded up (can't have 60 ticks

of dots if the dot doesn't round evenly into 60)

Innate hybrid haste IS factored into the dps of nukes.
