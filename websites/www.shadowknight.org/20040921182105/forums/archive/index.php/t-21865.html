Kaledine
Basically.. I'll sum it up since you have limited web time.

The quest is long.. you need to get your Emperor Ssraeshza key first (I think it's for the best)

Go to SSRA get a taskmaster pouch from the basement, Idol off of Commander Zazuah, Key off of Commander Zerozosh (or however you spell it) and Insignia off of General, Arbitor, or Advisor in the portal room (usually done by a raid)

Combine idol, key and insignia inside the pouch and receive Ring of the Shissar.. that's your emp key.

Now go to Akheva Ruins with a Wisp Stone and Essence Emerald and do the turn ins to get a Shadowed Sceptor Frame.. now.. go to The Grey, Scarlet Desert, Fungus Grove, Sanctus Seru, Ssraeshza Temple, The Deep, Akheva Runs, Maiden's Eye, Dawnshroud Peaks, and Acrylia Cavarns and get a Lucid Shard from all 10 zones. Combine all 10 in the and get the Unadorned Sceptor of Shadows

Now Kill Emperor Ssraeshza and loot a Planes Rift from the mini snakes that spawn from his death. Then kill any boss in Luclin and loot A Glowing Orb of Luclinite.

Combine Orb and Rift in the unadorned Sceptor and get the Sceptor of Shadows.. your Key to Vex Thal which is located in the Umbral Plains.

You need a raid to do Vex Thal.. and if you dont have numbers or gear or good people with good AE abilities... then expect a 7-10 hour clearing of the whole zone.

Good luck :)
