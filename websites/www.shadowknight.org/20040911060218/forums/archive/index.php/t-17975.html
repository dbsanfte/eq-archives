Oberyn
.....I dislike large races, they take up too much room in tight areas, and staying illusioned or shrunk is a pain in the ass. Also, unless they have killer gear (not an option for me) their manapool is pretty slim. Hell, mine as a DE is barely adequate a lot of the time.

.....Gnomes weren't around, and frankly, had they been, I still wouldn't pick one. How can people respect and FEAR a bald twerp the height of your knees? Also my friend plays a gnome and I can't count the number of times he has been stuck somewhere due to his lack of height.

.....Erudites and Humans are night blind, and though there are items and spells to change this, they take up a buff slot, or an inventory slot for a weak item. A good item with truesight or the like is not available to a low or mid level unguilded char, without loads of plat. I like to SEE, call me strange. A Human SK was a close call, but the vision tipped me towards DE.

.....Iksar... good choice. It was a hard choice between DE and these. The lack of good plate armor at the time I made my SK swung my choice. I think it may be better now. Also, there's a lot of REALLY annoying Iksar players on my server *shrug* may have had something to do with it.

.....Dark elf has the manapool, the middle size, the vision, the gear choice, and they actually look half decent - like they might be smart and evil. The STR disadvantage is annoying, but STR is the easiest stat to get as the gear comes in, and through buffs. Hide from day one comes in handy as well.

This subject comes up all the time, and I dunno how many times its been said: ALL RACES WORK, if you are a half ass decent player. Its just a personal choice depending on what a person likes and values. Those were my criteria......so sue me.
