Hello to all shadowknights who reads this post. I have created a shadowknight after many days of trying to figure out which race to choose. I tried ogre but didnt like the huge frame or the look. I also felt the same way with trolls too. I tried Iksar but after seeing the exp penalty they receive and the fact that they cannot wear certain plate armor I decided to pass on them as well. Dark Elf was another choice but I see too many on my server (Solusek Ro) and I wanted to be just a little different. Gnomes I was against at the beginning because I didnt want to be a short character. Erudites have a huge mana pool but with such low str and sta I passed on them too (though they do make excellant necromancers). So after going through all the choices, I decided to go with a human shadowknight. Now dont get me wrong, I don't think one race is better than the other, I just wanted a choice of race that suited me the best. I was just wanted to get other human shadowknights responses on how well they are in combat. Have you ever been the MT in a group? Can you hold your own with the large race shadowknights or warriors in a battle? These were just a couple of things on my mind and wanted to get comments on them. Thanks for all who read this and happy hunting.

My damnation is of my own doing. Lord Soth
