5-6% aa solo is easily doable in the basements of HoH, on the humans of course. Just aggro kite them w/dots while your pet bashes them from behind. Be sure and grab a KEI or VoQ first, mana regen is the key to killing mobs back to back to back... without worry of downtime.

Takes about 6 minutes for me to put them down, isn't really efficient, but it is something to do while /lfg.

Just snare with festering, and DoT with Ignite Blood and Arystole. Keep your pet augmented as well. Heck, grab some pet items from a mage and kills will go a bit quicker. Oh and don't forget to turn pet taunt off hehe.
