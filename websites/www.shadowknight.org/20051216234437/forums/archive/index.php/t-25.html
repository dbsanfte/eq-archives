The goal is always to have good AC, what that is at your level is your own decision. The whole AC vs. Stats argument is really not saying get rid of stat armor but just that AC has a higher value than stats.
For example:
12 AC 6 dex 5 str arms VS. 16 AC arms

I think most people would tell you to take the 16 AC arms.

Probably a bad example but sometimes it's good to make your own stat VS. AC table value wise

for mine

1 AC =
7 HPs
15 mana
2 STA (sta = HPs)
10 STR
5 AGI
20 DEX (i personally feel its worthless)
3 INT

WIS and CHA both worthless to me

factoring in resists also makes it even more confusing but most resists tend to be situational except MR which is always the check used for doing something that pisses you off (charm)

Most stats are situational. For example if you're on a raid and you're not taking any type of arggo from melee mobs then theoritically it doesn't matter if you have 0 ac or 5k ac because you're not getting hit at all. MR and other stats are still important. Say when you're fear kitting while soloing, STR and DEX are the most important stats while soloing because you're not getting hit much and they faster you kill the mob the less mana and HP you have to use but when you're tanking, all you need is enuff str to keep arggo and let the rogue do the most damage. This is were AC is most important (saves your cleric lots of mana)
Also when you're trying to do tradeskills most important stat is Int or wis and Dex for tailoring and str for smithing.... etc. ac is usless in that case.
