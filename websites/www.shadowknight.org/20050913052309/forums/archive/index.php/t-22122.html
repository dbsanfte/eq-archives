It is mostly luck with how much he casts, and how lucky your mitigation is. A couple tips:
1) Place your back to a wall and don't be levitated to help with channeling.
2) Do not spend mana on anything but Touch of Innoruuk
3) Use a 2hander with a Guard3 Aug (5% mitigation that stays up will heal a lot over the course of the fight)
4) Bring Healing Items, Throughout the 6-8 months I've farmed this guy, I've done him once without having to resort to heal potions.
5) Save some mana and/or HT for when Zlandicar runs or when hes low, having to chase him into Chetaris is never good.

Stuff that Helps
~ Vanazir - Many times when nightmares lands on Zlandicar, his next instinct is to dispell it
~ Damage Shield - If you can buff your damage shield to the 60-70 range, the majority of Zlandicar's hits fall in the 96-130ish range. He fits fairly often so the DS will sum up to a hefty amount.
~ FT/HP regen - hes got about 70-80k HP, don't expect a short fight
~ Black Shroud - use it over Mental Horror because well, ToI has a recast and BS is resisted less

Theres prolly more shit that will come to me
MR DD Focus and Crits will save your ass.

Anyhow, the hardest part is finding him up, especially with the two new VP quests involving DN. I haven't found him up since some time in late June or early July.
