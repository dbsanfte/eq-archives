I got my Vxed flag as tank too, with charmed pooka and only 1 healer (cleric). When you're on the lower end of the stat scale like me you have to really "use" the pet to your advantage. Chanter has to be able to control the pet and pull as well- and the group has to aid in pet control and be quick to stun pet on charm breaks.

Chanter pulls with a tash, I tag off chanter on incoming with fear, he sends in pet. In the few steps it takes me to run up and land a taunt off the pet, the mob's already taken a beating. I rarely turn on attack so I take as little riposte as possible, and use fear and CD to keep it agro'd on me while pet and whoever else downs it. I do have full defensive AA and keep AoP on me as much as possible. WoG is a must as well.

I will say that the chanter I've done these trials with is excellent (the best charming chanter I've ever seen in fact), and he frequently does Tipt with 8Kish hybrids as his tanks- so he understands sending in the pet in between my tag off him and my landing of my second agro spell. Even if you just let the pet chew on it for 2 seconds, it's done a nice amount of damage and hasn't taken much itself. By the time a heal lands on me, mob is slowed and it's a bit easier.

True, it's not the most ideal situation and you can start to feel a bit like a speedbump only. But unfortunately this is what Sony's done to SK's for these Trials.
