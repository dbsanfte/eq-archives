If you didn't know already, Drusella Sathir is on a 65-79 hour spawn timer. She is still a contested spawn because she sometimes drops the coveted spirit wracked urn for the spirit wracked cords quest in Chardok. Your best bet at getting Drusella is to have a friend come in periodically to check track in hopes of catching her up. Also finding out when Drusella fell really helps cause it is definately so much easier knowing when she might be up.
