Darko
First of this is more of end game solution so it mostly focuses on pop encounters.

The idea that verant had was, 2h for damage and 1h plus shield for tanking, however there is big problem with that.

Problem:
When using a 2h u do quite a bit more damage, but ALSO u get riposted less due to lower delay on your weapon. So even with the extra stats gained from using a shield you may still take more damage when tanking using the 1h+shield combo.

In fact many warriors have started using 2h when tanking for that same exact reson, mobs that hit pretty hard, like most mobs in pop, are just not fun to get riposted on.

Solution:
This idea can be most easily implemneted through aa skill.

The AA skill would give an increased % chance to parry a riposted attack (meaning when a mob riposts you and gets that extra hit, this skill would kick in and add better %to parry that 1 hit). The skill will be obviosly passive, and will only work when having a weapon or shield in the offhand, this skil will be availbale to war, sk and pal. The skill could have something like 3 levels, at the increasing cost of 2 4 6, each train raising the change to parry a riposte by 25% 50% 100% respectivly, so when the skillis maxed the war/sk/pal will be immune to riposte damage when using 1h+ shield or dual wiedling.

IMO this is not overly over powering, mobs in pop do very high damage and its not cause of the riposte, however this will make it quite worth it for tanks to switch out their 2h when tanking. Also this is seems like a much more reasonable fix for the 1hander problem compared to the more popular "block skill with shields" approach which imo would seriosly trivalize the game.

So what ya think?
