You can get the newbie khukri one of two ways. 1) Get your faction up (which you'll need for the quest anyway) and then talk to someone in Cabilis (I forget the name) to get a weapon that will proc a summon thing. You proc it 52 or so times, hand in what you get, and you'll get your newbie khukri. 2) Do it the easy way, get your faction up, and when you're ready to start the quest, make a newbie Iksar SK. Log him in, turn in the newbie khukri, log in your main SK, and hand in the rest of the first part of the quest. Now you're on your way. And yes, you must finish all 7 (? I think its 7, i forget.....) of the khukris before you can start on the actual Greenmist.
