Anyway for those others that are afraid to say how clueless they are...

When you activate a discipline, it starts a timer and brings up a "buff" symbol on your Alt-C screen. This symbol shows up nowhere else that I am aware of. If you click the "buff" symbol while the timer is active, the discipline is turned off.

So, why is this important? For those who do not yet have disciplines, they have made Unholy Aura a 5 minute discipline. This means from the time you click it, for the next 5 minutes an HT is impacted by Unholy Aura. Now, in many fights that are going badly not only will you HT, but you will Leechcurse as well.
