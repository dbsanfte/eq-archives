Kizzestra
You are not experiencing a bug. What you are experiencing is melee timing. There is a delay between the time a mobs swings and the time a blow connects. If your FD goes off and the mobs blow was already triggered before the spell actually went off, then the mobs blow still comes in. If it hits you, the mob realizes you are not dead because you still took damage. It then is not fooled by your "successfull" FD.

This is a pretty old issue really. Especially with mobs who hit multiple times, the second, 3rd or 4th blow in an attack round can come in well after the first attack is triggered and break FD.
