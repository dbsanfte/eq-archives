Calamity_Daemon
Seeing as how there are more and more people coming and playing Everquest for the first time or returning from a long leave of absence. I have decided to write a scope on all the races. I will try and remain neutral through my little critique :). Please if you have any problem with it please feel free to post. As long as it is helpful please post it up, but refrain from mindless babble.

Human: The final race that the gods created. This race has grabbed a strong foothold in the world of Norrath. But where there is good there is always one willing to do ill. These are the human shadowknights. They have very basic stats 85 str 80 sta 75 agi 75 dex 75 wis 85 int 80 cha and +20 bonus points for you to blow into your character :). As they have very well rounded stats they can never be bad and can only get better. They dont't have any experiance penalties which is really nice to have when making an SK. They do not have nightvision which can be a pain until later when you get Deadeye at level 22 I think it is. All in all they are very formidable especially later on when you get high damage items and bonus equipment (Just like any other race)

Erudite: These I believe were the last of the human race. Led by Erud they left the humans (Who they had grown discusted with) and sailed for Odus. There is a long story of Maragul and Necromancy and everything. But as a Erudite Shadow Knight you will start in Paineel which is one of the BEST newbie zones in the game (On the map it will say you start in Erudin but it is actually Paineel). Followed by Paineel you get to go to Warrens which is a VERY nice little place to level up. I dont know their stats for a SK at this time feel free to post it though! Like humans Erudites have no Night Vision which again is a pain in the butt to me, however you get Deadeye at 22. Erudites are often times scorned for being a physical and not as much magical by their peers (And in forums like this they get slamed a lot ) They have little strength and little sta from what I remember. But you will really never have to leave Odus until you want to go to Luclin! I have seen very few Erudite SKs around but from the high level ones I have encountered they are a nice race. Oh yes the Intelligence is nice to have to learn stuff quickly.

Dark Elf: The Dark Elves were created by Innoruuk as a mockery of the Elven race. Dark Elves are very quick witted and have good intelligence. They make fiersome Shadow Knights in battle as they are swift and very dexterious (Which makes your weapon proc). I believe their stats are 70str 75sta 90agi 75dex 83wis 109 int 65cha and +20 bonus points. All in all a nice race to make an SK and very nice to Roleplay (Which I see very little anymore sadly).

Ogre: Ogres are the children of Rallos Zek the Warlord. At one time they, along with Zek leading them, made war with the gods themselves. They are what fairy tales explain them to be. Big powerful masses of flesh with little intelligence or kindness. The smarter of the race's crop become shamans or Shadow Knights. their stats are 140str 127sta 70agi 70dex 67wis 70int +20 bonus. Ogres can not be stunned from the front and have an experiance panalty but less severe that the trolls or iksars. If strength is what you crave then go ogre.

Troll: No one truly knows how trolls were created but the most resonable explaination is that they came from Kunark. Trolls are big, green, stinky, and some of the ugliest and stupidest creatures in all of Norrath. (It's a wonder they can be SKs at all) Their stats are 117str 114sta 83agi 75dex 60wis and 62int along with +20 bonus points. Alas the trolls have a 20% xp penalty laid on them due to their regeneration. But trolls can become the fiercest Shadow Knights in all of Norrath. After the frogloks attacked Grobb the trolls moved to Neriak making the newbie zone Nektulos forest. The recent patch up'ed the spawn rate so there is more to kill now )

Iksar: Iksars were created by Cazic-Thule and were slaves for the longest time. Not a single race accepts Iksars but themselves. This means much faction work on your side (Even more because your an SK) but there will be little need to leave Kunark for a while. The Kunark area around the iksar city can easily take you to 20. I am not sure at their stats but I know that some of the biggest duel nightmares I have are with Iksar Shadow Knights. There is a 20% XP penalty due to their regeneration. Iksars can not where plate armor because if messes with their scales which kind of stinks but you get some nice culteral and the Khurki (I can't remember the name ) quests which eventually lead to the Greenmist quest which is a very nice weapon.

Gnome: Everyone has problems with gnomes. Gnomes are the short folk who live in the Steamfont mountains. Their urge to touch and experiment gives you the ability to Tinker. You can tinker fun stuff like Fireworks which are fun at guild meetings. They have little str but are swift and very smart as well. I think these guys are fun to Roleplay (I have a gnome as my alt SK) and even though they are weak physically they can really pack a punch with the right equipment. I have outdamaged and ogre warrior with my gnome shadowknight. The newbie area is quite nice as well.

I leave with that. Shadow Knights are very very powerful and should be roleplayed to KNOW they are powerful and be as evil as you can be regardless of race. Maybe in a group cackle when your friend dies and you live. Or if you are a troll or ogre in the group look at the halfling with a grin and make threats to eat him/her. All in all they races are balanced and should not be taken lightly in any fight. you never know when HT is coming. Play your SK with pride!
