I didn't read that entire guide, but browsing over it i noticed snare pulling. Shadow Knights are great at this technique and it is a great way to pull singles out of a group that aggros. You just cast darkness to aggro the group of mobs that you are pulling and then FD so that they will return to their spawn point. The one that is snared will take longer to get back or not move depending on what level of darkness you use. Just stand up once the others have returned to their spawn point and the one that is darkend will come back for you. You have to play with this way of pulling for a little while to get it down because sometimes the snared mob will still be to close to the group after you stand up and they will all reaggro. Also sometimes darkness gets resisted on the first try so you have to quickly fd and let them re-set before you give it another try.

Another way to work fear pulling (depending on the level of the mobs you want to split) is to fear one of the mobs. Again depending on the level the only thing that will happen is the feared mob will run away leaving the rest of the mobs at their spawn points... Follow the feared mob a ways out (so that when fear breaks it comes to you instead of going back to spawn point) and then pull it back to your camp spot... This was a successfull tactic at around 47 when I was doing gobos in LOIO, when I could not simply single pull from spawn point..
