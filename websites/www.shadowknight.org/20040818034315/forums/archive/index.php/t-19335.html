Menon Lifeblight
You receive two types of messages when completing a flagging event:

You have received a character flag!

This is the good one, meaning your stuff is in order and you have completing all the pre-flagging.

You have received a checklist flag!

This means you haven't done all the pre-flagging, and you don't really have the flag yet. You need to go back and fulfill your requirements, and then unlock memories to get the full effect.

Without PoJ trials, AD will give you a checklist flag. So once you do the trials, you'll get an AA. Then you can unlock memories to get the HoH flag from your AD kill, and you'll get another AA.
