Malize Mikage
Actually, what you're seeing now is the way the Luclin models were *intended* to look like all along. It's just that until this last patch, the /dopropertinting toggle was defaulted to OFF and you had to manually toggle it on. The patch must have set the toggle to default to ON.

Do proper tinting does exactly that. Instead of an armor's color or an armor's dye covering an entire body area (for instance, that leather bustier high elf females are known for. Before, with the toggle off, you dye it blue and the entire chest from the collar around the neck to the waist would be blue, including the elf's supposedly bare chest), the armor color or dye only covers the actual material of the armor, leaving the skin its natural tint.

That means all that chain mail that used to be lime green links over skin also dyed lime green and looked like a solid mass now shows only lime green on the links themselves, letting ogre ass show through.

This is how I've seen the Luclin models for well over a year because a long time ago I turned the proper tinting to on. So in reality, they didn't mess up the graphics. They *fixed* them.
