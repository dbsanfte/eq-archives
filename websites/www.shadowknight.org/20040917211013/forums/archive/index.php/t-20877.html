Life Leech - Lucy (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=692&source=Live)

See the field "Resist: Magic"...that means the roll is made against magic resist.

See the field "Resist adj: -200"...that means that the MR of the target is effectivly lowered 200 points, thus making the spell easier to land.

look at these spells for comparisons...

Touch of Innoruuk (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3413&source=Live). Also magic, but a -500 check.
