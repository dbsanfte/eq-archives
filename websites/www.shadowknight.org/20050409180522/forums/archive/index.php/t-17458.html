Q: Aggro? My aggro spells suck!!!

Hmm....Did you not buy clinging darkness? By 51 you've got your two staples of agro- CD and HF. Shouldn't need much more than those two for some time.

Spell lineup is going to vary- there's a thread here somewhere on it in the spells forum. You have only 8 gems and need to be ready for many situations. You'll want FD up at all times of course. Some people like to keep HS up too, I'm one of those. If you're not MA you might still want to keep your CD or a terror up in case you suddenly become MA. At 51 my lineup was usually

a lifetap
cd
terror
dooming sometimes
spear
varied
fd
hs

at 52

a lifetap
cd
terror
varied
varied
comatose
FD
HS

As for pulling, that varies just as it does when you are solo'ing. Sometimes if you're just getting a single you don't want to snare because the mob's slower movement may mean more chance of a repop in pull path. So instead just pull with a str tap or something small. Sometimes you have to split so adjust accordingly.

Rarely did I use a bow but that was mostly due to archery sucking and never having a bow up.
