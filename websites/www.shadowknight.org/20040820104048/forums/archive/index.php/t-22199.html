These two posts explain it the best. Neither are in the Dirty Deeds forum so that'd explain why you probably couldn't find much info on it.

In a nutshell, don't bother trying to faction for this quest. Factioning up will help with various stages of the quest and some fights that take place in Katta, but will NOT help you for the final turn in.

So to save time and disappointment, it's recommended that you do the quest via MQ because of the three strikes you have against you: an evil race, evil class and worshipping an evil diety. Illusions helped me when most of my Katta factions were not yet maxed (and most of them are now) but did not help one iota with the final turn in.
