Tanking:
Warriors: Have higher mitigation and hit points, defensive disc, Furious disc and the new Warlords Tentacity(insta heal with a weak DoT as the backside). Not to mention the extra hp's they get from Gates AA.

Paladins: Have good self heals, LoH.

SKs: Have some nice lifetap-procs, but that really only counts as a random regen. Lifetaps heal to little.


Dps:
Warriors: Have a higher dps meele-wise, fellstrike disc. weapon skill caps at 252.

Paladin: About the same melee dps(SKs aren't even close when compared to undead).Weapon skill caps at 225.

SKs:Use a 50/tick DoT(and snare) to take aggo. Have more DoT's too. Pet is nice if mobs don't AE much. Weapon skill caps at 225. Spear/Scythe line of spells are nice.


Aggro:
Warriors: Have the best taunt skill(252), have tons of EB weapons, have AE taunt, Incite line of taunts, a 15% aggro mod on the Time BP, Furious Bash shields and Anger III from LDoN.

Paladins: Stuns, best snap aggro in the game(fast to cast, low mana and a usefull effect) 220 taunt skill.

SKs: Have the Festering Darkness spell that is really good, if it hadn't been for the DoT in it. Some say Fear is good too, but it didn't look like it to me. Have 220 taunt skill. The 12% aggro mod is nice but annoying (takes spell and buff slot). The EB spells taunt way to little to be usefull.


Emergency situations:
Warriors: Combination AE-taunt + "defensive disc"or "riposte disc" and most situations should be under control pretty fast.

Paladins: Use the non DD-stuns(so the chanter can mez) + root. Wind of marr, LoH, aa group heal.

Sks:Can't help much really to taunt off. if we use the DoT on every mob, i doubt the chanter will love us much afterwards. And the Fear spell... Well it has a recast and isn't that good aggro. The lifetap HT only heal for 2300 when maxed aa, and is not affected by the "Unholy Aura"
Best option then: Feign Death.

Pulling:
Warriors: Pull with the new taunt ability that has some range on it. Can't single out mobs tho.

Paladins: Have pacify and can root a mob to get singles.
Sks:Can FD-pull, but it is normally much to time consuming(Especially for the LDoN adventures). Can, however, do a warrior pull and FD if it goes really wrong. Can use pet too, but if you think FD is time consuming...


Equipment:
Warriors have it a lot easier when it comes to equipment. just need ac, hp, resists, melee stats, cha, Vengeance and regen. EB on weapons.

Paladins need the same as warriors + mana, Wis, FT and a ton of Focus effecs. Need runes, scroll and lots of points in LDoN to get spells. Just need one 1handed weapon tho and EB on weapons is not a must.

SKs have the same as pallys, but Int instead of Wis and some different focus effects.


Misc:
SK’s and Pallys have to use a 1-handed weapon and a shield to get the most possible hp, ac, etc(and Furious Bash). We don’t have any bonus attacks with 1-handers(with a 2h, we have increased 2x-attack and a chance of preforming an extra attack each combat round).


Summary:
Warriors out-tank everything by far. If a warrior has high end equipment, nothing can take aggro from him/her(/it).

Paladins are best in getting snap aggro, but can’t keep up with a warrior in the long run, unless the pally jumps on the stun button the entire fight. Far best of the tank classes to keep crowd control.Have very nice healing spells and skills.
