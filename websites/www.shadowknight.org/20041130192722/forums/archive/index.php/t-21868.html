Slein Jinn
Virtually every meaningful SK spell aside from lifetaps is disease-based.

Currently, disease is by far the most resisted spell category in the game, and is the only resist not regularly debuffed in raids/groups. Malo coveres everything BUT disease, even.

Forcing SK's to rely on numerous disease-based spells with no/low resist mods when it's the most resisted game-wide and the least debuffed game-wide isn't quite fair. It's not like our spells are so powerful they need to be balanced by resistability.
