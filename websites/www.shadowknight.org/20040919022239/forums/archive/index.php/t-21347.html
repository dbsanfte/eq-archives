Catharsis
Rampage is a second agro list that is built in the order that you do anything agro inducing to the mob. Rampage hits the person at the top of this list unless they currently are attacking them. It never changes (theres exceptions, but in practice). So to control who's RT, that person must engage first or right behind the MA. Usually have the RT pull the mob when ready to engage.
Dyrkk
You can also use Harmshield as rampage tank, which is great for situations where original RT has died and you are next on the list and need the healers to catch up...let them know you have HS up for 18 seconds so that they dont waste mana while you're invulnerable.

If you are RT and FD for some reason, the next person on the rampage list will become RT until you stand back up again, and then you will resume being RT. FDing several times as suggested above might clear you if you get one of those rare 'mem-wipe' FDs, but generally FDing is a bad idea because there is no way to make sure that a slower/healer/bard isn't the next one after you on the rampage list.
Brael
If you need to avoid a round or two its best to back out of the mobs rampage range rather than FD. Generally what I do is pull the mob into camp using something extremly low aggro like an arrow, the tank gets the mob and the mob will now start hitting me on rampage, let it land one round if you're full hp, then invul for 18 seconds, get your heal while invul from divine arb so you'll be good when it disappears. Now just stand there and get hit, keep your aggro low but don't FD. Optimally you would want every non RT to be higher aggro than you so in the event that the first tank dies you can be sure the RT doesnt become the focus of the mobs attention and get someone else doing rampage. Use whatever you can to lessen damage intake to yourself - lifetap procs, casted taps, aura of pain, torrent of hate, etc... don't build up alot of aggro though as FD'ing is risky, get heals while you're hit and thats all there is to it. Also, don't let your guild know what a Divine Hammer of Consternation is or you'll never RT again.
