What, at this stage, do shadowknights need?

We're excellent in groups and decent soloers.

What we need is to stack in raids. This will increase our desirability to guilds.

If a guild has 4-5 tanks in MT lineup and 4-8 others for TMing, there is no real need to add more.

If a guild has 20 wizards, it can add another 20 and they will all stack, to infinity. Similar with all the offensive classes (though melee offensive can get crowded a bit).

Make an AA skill, should be fairly expensive in terms of aa points, and accessible at 62+ or perhaps 65, that makes Zevfeer's Bite Recourse an AoE spell.

If you want to make in in stages (1, 2, 3) make level one with AoE range of 25, level 2 with AoE range of 50 and level 3 with AoE range of 100, all other effects should be the same.

Net effect? For group - nothing. For soloing - nothing. For raids and guild desirability? Awesome. If a guild has 3 SKs, it can add another 3 and they will actually be useful.

What do you think? Any SOE developer lurking these boards? :)
