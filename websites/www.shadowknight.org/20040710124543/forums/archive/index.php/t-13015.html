Platinum Disc goes in the charm slot. I recently obtained one and tested it by moving platinum between my account and my wife's account. Here's what I found.

With this amount (in thousands of platinum pieces) in bank, it had these stats:

0k no stats
5k +1 hp +1 mana
10k +3 hp +3 mana +1 fr +1 cr
15k +1 dex +4 hp +4 mana +1 fr +1 cr
20k +1 dex +6 hp +6 mana +2 fr +2 cr
25k +1 dex +5 hp +5 mana +1 fr +1 cr
30k +3 hp +3 mana +1 fr +1 cr
40k no stats
60k +1 dex +5 hp +5 mana +1 fr +1 cr
80k +1 hp +1 mana
416k +1 dex +3 hp +3 mana +1 fr +1 cr

For an item that says "improves as you accumulate more total wealth" it seems to work just fine as you go from 0 plat to 20k plat.. but at values over 20k, seems to be broken and assign a fairly random value to the charm for a given wealth level. Clearly bugged, IMHO.

I'm guessing that there is some programming variable that doesn't have sufficient length in the definition to allow for numbers much over 20,000 plat, and that above those values of wealth it gives a random result.

There's no way that you should have decent stats at 20k and no stats whatsoever with twice that much money if it's supposed to IMPROVE when you get more wealth.

Please fix this bug Verant!! Thanks.

-Bolas

Lame.

Sounds like verant is suffering from a "variable out of bounds" type problem, where their algorithm used to calculate the stats of the Platinum Disc is buggy when it gets values over 22949672 total wealth (measured in coppers). Time to fix the bug. Bug report filed.
