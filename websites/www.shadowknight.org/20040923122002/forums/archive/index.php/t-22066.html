Not sure where you want to post it, am assuming you want to add it to your signature

this is what you do, go to User CP at the top, then on the left hand side you'll see a link called Edit Signature

Now what you want to do is type this

(a href="http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=######")Mehatesu 40 SK Drinal(/a href)

On the above, replace the ( with < and ) with >

Replace ###### with the number of your Magelo profile, some have 5 digits, some have 6

click on save signature at the bottom, and thats should be it, whenever someone clicks on your name, it will take them to your magelo profile

That's just an easy way of doing it, for images and such it's pretty similar, but you'll have to get an image first...

Finished signature would look like this

<a href="http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=123456">Mehatesu 40 SK Drinal</a href>

It's simple really
