high hp regen and mana regen are a lot more important than total hps and mana pool in pvp imo

Hmm not so sure of that. Large mana pool is pretty important in PvP I think, you can burn better. Usually fights don't last that much more then 10 minutes unless you are just 'kiting' people. Of course mana regen is nice as long as it is more significant then +mana during 10 minutes (FT1 equates to 100 mana during that period, so a 250 mana item is better then a FT1 item in that case). HP regen is not as important as mana pool because spells do so much damage you hardly notice it when fighting people like wizards. Your taps heal for much more then a bit of regen during the fight, with a larger mana pool you can tap for a lot more then you would have gained with regen items. Of course when fighting melees HP regen can be more important then mana pool since they will interrupt your casting (with bash/kick) and hit you often during casting if they pay attention, so it all depends who you are fighting.
