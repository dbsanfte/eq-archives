Groff Gorespiller
Having a 60+ necro helps. Their undead slows and mezzes are nice, charms are nice as well. The golems are flagged as undead, IIRC, so it makes Drusella's room a bit easier.

Basically, the crawl through East wing is pretty simple, you don't absolutely need a roge if everyone can get a lev and a SoW/SoB/SoE. Kill the golems at the entrance to the south wing, crawl on top of one of the fire pots there outside the door, then lev/run with speed buff across to the top of the East wing false stairs. East wing door isn't locked.

XebarisTP
I had lots of fun collecting the pieces for the master key (iirc finger, thumb, forearm, glove). The south part was hardest to get (without a rogue), as you need to progress through north and west wings first, and it took us like 6 attempts to spawn the first boss.. I actually did this over the course of two days, and didn't log between or I'd lose my no rent keys.

There's a trick to get to the east wing without the previous no rent keys, so I only worried about the south wing. With sow+levi you can float from west stairs to east stairs, avoiding the trap. The door isn't locked. However, if you get a group to HS, someone is guaranteed to not be able to do the float. Tried with 3 groups, and there was always one or two that couldn't do it. That's why I decided to work on the master key. Heck, I didn't even know I'd need it if we didn't have a rogue in group. After doing the combine, the door unlocked, though.

Still waiting for Drusella to be up when I have time to do something about that. Can a druid track her from entrance? I figure you need a minimum of 4 people close to 65 to do east wing without risking a wipe. The spawn rate is quite fast, so if you linger, you're likely get adds. I strongly suggest a pacifier to single pull.
