Every now and then we wind up having to fight one of these bastages with the 3 player group we do the lesser dragons in WW with (62 cleric, my 65 shaman and me 64sk). I wouldn't want to try to fight these toe to toe without a slow...most likely you'll lose. They dispell like crazy, so plan on being buffless.

Compared to the other loot available in WW, they're just a royal pain in the ass. Invis, and go for the better mobs. One greater dragon head will fetch you a Frostbringer (if on Kael faction) that will sell quick for 6k.

Compared to dragon loot (drops and quest) they're not worth it, even though their silks and hides sell for a fair amount. It's a long pain in the arse cr if you wipe.

Honestly, these guys are about 2-3 times the hps of a guardian wurm in SF.
