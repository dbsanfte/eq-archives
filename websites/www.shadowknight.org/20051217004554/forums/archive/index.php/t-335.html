Between AP and SoG, AP is a far superior weapon in my opinion. As everyone else has mentioned, 4% increase in damage output is not trivial.

Regarding the question of AP (42/49) vs Etched Velium Lance (30/35), this gets pretty interesting. They both have identical dmg/delay ratio, so which ever has a relatively higher damage bonus at your level would be a better choice for pure melee damage. By relative damage bonus, I mean the one that gives a higher bonus/delay. For example, say at your level, the AP gives 10 dmg bonus and the Lance gives 8. In this case, the Lance (with a relative bonus damage of 8/35 = .229) will give you higher damage than the AP (10/49 = .204). Of course, the damage output difference between two identical ratio weapons should be minimal unless one has an insanely high delay (like Weighted Axe).

With 2 weapons of identical dmg/delay, there are a couple other things to consider, however.
1) The faster one will perform more consistently over every fight. For example, missing 10 times in a row with a 150 delay weapon is not going to hurt as much as missing 10 times in a row with a 50 delay weapon. (winner - Lance)
2) Reposte would do much more damage with a slower weapon.(winner - AP)
3) Casting in between swings would be more difficult and most likely lower your damage output more with a fast weapon. (winner - probably AP)

Of course there's stats to consider when all else is equal. I personally would prefer the lance over AP at level 52 because of #1 and because none of my offensive spells take over 3.5 seconds to cast.
