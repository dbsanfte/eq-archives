Here is the problem sometimes...there are many times I only have about 30-45 minutes to jump on line and play do to other real life responsibilities.

LDONs = 1.5 hours (considering getting group formed, buffing, etc)

CAMPING = Long time to get to location, get group, get the camp, etc....not really an option if on for 45 minutes...works for when you have longer time availablity

RAIDING - takes 45 minutes just to get raid force together and organized.

FARMING FOR TRADESKILLS - ugh, no thanks. I might as well go scrub the kitchen floor.

I wish there was some kind of advancement process for those that can only get on for short periods of time, but could still get some xp, points. loot, even soloing.

SWG (I know there were major drawbacks but...) had the mission terminals where you could get 2 missions, and complete them both in 30 minutes which was awesome. Then when you have longer time to stay on line, you could get a group and go for bigger targets.

Somehow SOE needs to find something to entertain the casual limited time player to keep there interest. Maybe the Tribute Master could give quick missions that must be completed solo, and as you build up enough credit, it could be used towards a loot pool, LDON adventure points, tribute points, etc.

There is so much focus on the "power-gamer" big raid stuff, how about some love for us limited time people?
