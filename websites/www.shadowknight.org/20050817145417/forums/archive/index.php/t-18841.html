I found the lower hall further past where Ramgar kites was way easy. Not many resisted spells and good fear pathing. Nice little safe spot to park bones. If you have very good mana regen you can prolly do all 3 rooms (12 mobs or so) and keep the hallway clear.

Just watch out for the fake floor in the one hallway and do not attack any of the mobs standing around a small pool behind the small door as they will put the hurt on you.

Should net you 1 aa or better, depending on mana regen, per kei. Coin can be ok too if you get lucky with the belt buckles and other small drops. Lots of cash weapon drops.

Lineup was DP, Festering, Fear, Ignite, Zevfeer's(this slot flexible), Spear, Pet buff, Tap. Regular mobs were almost dead by the time festering / ignite wore off. They like to flee under 20% though so keep that in mind.

Most common named pop is the Defender. Can drop a nice priest / caster FT neck that was going for 60k or so last I saw on Lanys. Common drop is the Defenders Cloak, semi rare is some Defender's something blade, 2 HS. Can also drop a charm that increases stats with more money you have on you.

Named are immune to fear so you will have to kite them back and forth in the hallway. Make sure you have serious agro otherwise your pet will get a beatdown and you will need the pet for all the extra dps you can get. Named don't hit too hard ( 180ish) but they hit fairly quick so head to head tanking won't work unless you have 6500 hps or so and a bunch of taps memmed.
