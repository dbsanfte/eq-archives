Yes, "search" here at sk.org is your friend, but unfortunately if you don't know what to search for then I suppose your back to square one. I would recommend:

http://lucy.fnord.net/ --- For class spells, familiarize yourself with the sk spells to begin with.

If you've played eq before, which it sounds like you have then you know that sk are good at tanking because of the aggro that we can generate and keep. You will hear many people argue whats the best aggro spell we have. we have the "terror" line spells (terror of terris, terror of thule, terror of darkness) which generate decent close range aggro. We also have "Clinging Darkness" and "Fear," both of which generate good aggro. I personally use clinging dark unless I am with a chanter that is mezzing, in which case I will use fear.
I would suggest getting pickup groups in the Plane of Valor and be dps to start with (using slash, spear of plague, festering darkness, as well as the torrent spell lineup if you need extra stats, and whatever else you want to use depending on mana). When you feel that you want to be puller / MT go to the right or left castle out in pov and learn how to pull singles. I personally pull with festering darkness and then hit with clinging dark before it hits the camp. That will be enough aggro generated that the slower will NOT get aggro, even with a malos/tash + slow. I would suggest overkill on the clinging darkness at first. Cast alot so that you don't lose aggro and let the mob ping-pong damage in the group which will just make you look bad. Overkill on the aggro and then lighten up untill you find whats perfect.
