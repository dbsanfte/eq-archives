Drakonn
At low levels it's pretty much impossible to max all your combat skills, simply because you ding to next level before they're all capped.

Once you reach about 30 however, it's simple to keep them all maxed, provided you have 1 weapon from each type to rotate between - most groups won't mind you doing that, as long as you keep agro, assuming you're tanking - if you aren't, you don't even need to mention it to them, it's unlikely they'll be parsing everyones damage to make sure they aren't slacking.

Used to be easier with Hell levels, but it's still doable.
