Noctix
I am wondering if people are using Lifetaps in LDoN groups.

I'm MT for my group and we have both a cleric and a shaman. Between regrowth, CH, and torpor, we really have plenty of healing power.

I don't have time to med very much during an adventure so I need to be conservative in my spell casting, usually casting 1-2 spells per mob. My biggest lifetap (I'm level 56) is 150 hit points, which is like 3% of my total hp when I'm buffed. Is that even worth casting?

Everyone always talks about how lifetaps are a main feature of the SK class but I really don't find them very useful. I use them for gaining health when I'm soloing but I hardly ever solo.

Is there some group tactic using lifetaps that I'm missing? Also, do we get any lifetaps later on that are more useful?

Ramgar SK
With plenty of healing power, load your spears.

Grab a kei in pok before you go adventuring in LDoN, or group with a chanter. Bot9, and spiritual dominance add mana regen too (druid and BL spells). With all those I have a very tough time using all my mana up unless I use all the big dots.

Taps are our power because we can make it a lot easier on the cleric by loading up the tap proc, tapping, and heal over time with Vampiric curse (at 57 or bond of death at 62). That just gives the healer more flexibility.

I love doing this and getting a druid in place of a cleric. Druid/chanter combo > shammy/cleric cobo in LDoN IMO.
