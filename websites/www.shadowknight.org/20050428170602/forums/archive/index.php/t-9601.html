Originally posted by krikk
and best newby zones. (Cept' Swamp).
The Swamp of No Hope has a small but useful newbie area, which is usually quite empty. Not a bad place to level from 1-4 at all.

But yeah, Iksar make a good non-twink SK race. Regen makes getting through the early levels much more tolerable. You have your choice of newbie hunting grounds, and they tend to be pretty good.

You have the armor quest for Curscale armor, which is one of the few times SOE got newbie quest armor right-- you get the pieces from mobs that you can kill at level 1, the mobs and drops are plentiful, and thus you can level up while getting a set of newbie armor.

You have the Khukri quests, and the first one is also very nicely done, you can start hunting the sarnak hatchlings around level 3 or 4, and should be able to get exp and the necessary components for your first weapon upgrade.

There are plenty of skeletons in each of the newbie zones as well, so you can start building up that bonechip collection. And the weapon drops aren't bad at all. The rusty shan'tok is something like 8/40, they make really nice pet weapons too.

By the time you're level 10 or 11 you can go to Kurn's Tower and get excellent exp and decent loot. You can get to 30 or so without going very far at all. Granted, PoP makes this a moot point, since you can get just about anywhere in a short time nowadays.

So you get a good bag of tricks there. Regen avoids a lot of early deaths, you get a shot at some decent newbie armor and weapons before you are even level 5, and you get a variation in hunting grounds right around your home city. It is a nice combo for a non-twink.
