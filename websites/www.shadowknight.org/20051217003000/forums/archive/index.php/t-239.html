If you go digging through the official eq site (eqlive.station.sony.com) and check you will find a pretty good explanation of how bane and extra damage works.

My understanding is the first example that you used. And from my own limited testing of these types of weapons (using the summoned cleric hammers) the bane damage or energy damage adds directly to the damage stat of the weapon.

Your guildies seem to be confusing that with the bonus damage that is listed on the weapons. That (unless they have changed it) is the amount of extra damage added to each swing....thereby upping you minimum hit and maximum hit.
