Bulvyne
Well, you pretty much have the info you need to decide, you just have to decide what's most important to you! :)

If your main concern is NOT being "a dime a dozen", don't be a DE. DE's are most likely the most common SK in the game.

However, even though the human might start out with a couple extra points in STA and STR, don't let that fool you. After a few levels, stats start to even out between humans and DE's when you figure in spells, equipment, and other factors. Same goes for Int, as well. I have known some Ogre SK's who at 30th level had equal INT scores to some 40th level DE SK's I've known. It all becomes a matter of gear selection as you gain levels.

Both "look" cool in my opinion, but if "looks" are another important factor, then use that to help you decide.

Yeah, humans are night-blind, but as Shad just mentioned, after a short time that won't be a problem.

So, really, which race REALLY interests you the most? :)
