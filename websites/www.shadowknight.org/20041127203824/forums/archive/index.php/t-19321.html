Probably because, at 35, the DL skellies are probably a bit rough...

They were still decent xp for me until 52, and an easy solo. Around 49 it becomes a lot simpler because you get Dooming Darkness, though I've heard that they've been patched to no longer run on low health. I don't know if you could solo it yet or not, but if you have a shammy friend/guildie around the same level, you might want to hunt City of Mist stables or Pyramid room (aka temple) for your Greenmist scrolls and his epic scrolls.

You could also probably do fairly well at the Fort in Gunthak, or even around the entrance to Dulak and the first boat or so.

I did DL skellies and CoM from around 45 to 52 or so, then started on Dulak boats and moved slowly up to the Armory (start at the Stormwave, move up to the Dandolak as they start to light blue out, then move to the Armory). At around 55/56, I started xping in PoN at the hedge most of my time, did 55 - 57 there. I also had a Shammy partner the whole time, and we've learned each other's playstyles pretty well.
