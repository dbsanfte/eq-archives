Vezax
Pretty much all are solible, but some arent worth the time. Note all of mine were soloed at 65, your milage may vary.

-Easy solo-

Seru- took forever, but easy to solo. Nice thing was I was able to pad my pockets a bit here, stuff is light blue mostly, if you do the cat walk, some are rarely dark blue. They drop off "legos of the hand" or "lego <name>". There is also 2 that spawn by the zones of MS and DSP. Watch out for the roamer, he can be a pain. His name is "something of the Eye". I found this place to have the 2nd worst drop rate.

Grey- rock guys/ sun revs, didnt take long at all. Blue / lt blue. I heard this shard can take a while, but I got it off my second kill.

SD- kill every thing on plateau, stuff is green. Got it in 10 kills of some caster.

DSP- Kill every thing in NE caves, stuff is green. Got it on my 8th kill.

-Duo with healer-

FG- soloible, but a pain, easy peasy with a healer. stuff is all blue, drops a shard roughly once every 5-8 kills. Agro radius are small for shikar warriors, so they are easy solo pulls, royal guards are easily snare split. The place I camped was a tunnel between where 4 shikar warriors spawned and 2 royal guards spawned. A named priest spawns in the 4 warriors room. make sure to clear him too. with me and a 65 shammy, we were able to clear this 6 spawn of mobs easily.

ME- soloible if you do the 3 spawn camp by the goranga fort coimg from DSP. But its best to get a couple more and clear that 3 spawn, and the 3 spawn almost directly south of the 1st 3 spawn. In those 6 spawn, any of 3 named can pop. This is a shitty ass camp, and all the goranga are blue. PH are called Goranga scouts. This camp sucks ass. Easily the worst camp.

Acrylia - soloible if you want to clear the room near zone in where the grimling primalist pops. But that takes forever, but you will make a ton of pp w/ vendor loot and acrylia. Head to inner acrylia and kill mobs there if grouped. about one shard per 8-10 kills. I suggest grouping it, it goes quick.

Akheva - soloible I guess, had a healer. Go kill stone guys by davyns room. Drops about one in 4-5 from them. Pretty easy stuff.

-Group-

SSra- /shrug If youre doin VT keys youre gonna spend a lot of time here. You'll eventually get one. Basically I logged on and had a group of guildies farming green metals and snagged myself a shard.

-Unknown-

Deep- still havent done it yet.

The shard hunting isnt that bad besides ME. took me 8 days (6 of which I used actively to shard hunt.) to get 9 shards, and only 3 of those day was I actively shard hunting for more then 3 hours. I spent 5 hours in Seru, and 9 hours in ME (split between one 6 hour session in a group and one 3 hour session soloing, from which I got my shard.)

Basically I set it up the way I recomend doing it, as I found it most efficeint. I'm guessing the deep shard will be like the acrylia/akhevia/FG shard, once im set up with a healer or group, it will take about 30 min roughly for each shard.
