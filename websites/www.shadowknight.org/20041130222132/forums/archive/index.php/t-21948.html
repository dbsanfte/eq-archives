Maybe I am odd in my thinking, but I actually think that mana regen has created a problem in the game....

for a moment i would like to forget hybrids even exist in the game.. yeah I know they do but it is easier to explain if I can just ignore them for a while.

In the old days pure casters HAD to med, these days how often do you really see an int caster on a raid having to med (wis casters slightly different story as they tend to burn their mana buffing, but even so its not often they need a real med break) other than after a named mob where they have gone all out for 5 mins. I know from playing a different character I rarely if ever had to actually med. Sure I would sit down every now and then but not out of real need, more out of habit.

What this zero downtime has meant for casters , is that their sustainable DPS has risen considerably and has left the melee pretty much left for dust as far as DPS goes, burst DPS its an even worse situation..but then it always has been and that was the balance - burst DPS v sustainable DPS

For hyrids it gets more complicated as their oppertunities to med are limited, but honestly, with FT15, bard song, c5, 9, SP how often do you even run close to running out of mana and having to actually manage your spell casting ? I know even with my FT1, C5 and SP I have to go pretty insane with lifetapping to chew through my mana and so if I avoid lifetapping I know I can pretty much cast what I want for around 1 1/2 hours before i need a med break.

Upping the FT cap can only serve to further shift the balance towards pure casters in terms of sustainable DPS and that can only be a bad thing for all melee's (pure and hybrid) desirability both in a raid situation and in a group situation
