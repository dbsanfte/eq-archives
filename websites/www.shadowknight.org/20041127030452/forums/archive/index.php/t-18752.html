Keevel
The other issue is regarding unlocking items, which I can really only coherently explain with an example.

For example you have 150 points in MM. So you've unlocked all the items up to ~ 175 pts. you spend 120 pts. Now you have 30 points left. You go back to MM and earn 60 more points and your back up to 90 pts, but nothing new has been unlocked.

The unlocked items are based on the highest number of points you've had in a dungeon at one time.
