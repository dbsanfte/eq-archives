Maelins Guild Scene is way different than the other servers.

I moved from Prexus and it is like night and day.

On Maelin ya have 5 or 6 Uber guilds and a couple mid range Guilds and then the rest.

Uber Guilds-Have to be VT Keyed and mostly Time able and even then they aren't really recruiting.

Mid Guilds- Want to become uber so if ya can't raid 5 times a week minimum forget it.

The Others-1 or 2 ok Guilds but I never see them doing much Raid wise. Maybe they group but if they don't, then you back where ya started.

Here is what I do. Aggro Kite in PoJ, Gaolers, and some Sentries if bored. Use best pet, you will NOT get ANY Adds. You can do this until 65 (I did with Necro altho I added HG's). Just Fester and load up all your dots, sick pet (taunt off) and med in corners until mob dead, rinse,repeat.

Also can Fear Kite Flies in PoD but can be pain if a Bard or Wizzie pops in.

Velks can be good if ya have desent gear and can toe to toe Spiders.

See ya around Mahb.
