Ramgar SK
Yes, the WMBS has slightly better DPS at 55+. That alone should be enough to upgrade to it. The huge price difference is just gravy. WMBS are going for as low as 5k on a good day on Lanys. And WB sells for as high as 28k, and sells easy for 25k. Check out Slein's post in The Armory for more info on dropable weapons:
http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=13456

Also a very good spot is the Shrine Bruisers in Nadox. They drop weapons frequently, gems sometimes, and plat. Named can corpse pop there too. Good loot, safe, easy fear kite, single pullable. Set up camp at the TM zone in Nadox (outside the temple). You can get there easy with invis, or easier with a druid (evac from lava when you zone in to Nadox from Gunthak). Oh, and don't worry about falling in the lava on the bridge, you can't. :) I've stood still on the gaps, and didn't fall through.
