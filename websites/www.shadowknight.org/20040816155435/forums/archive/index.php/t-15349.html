Carshail Shel'rac
Duoed them all with a 65 cleric.

Set up camp in the first room on the left on the 3rd floor. It's usually empty except in the Eye building, there's a spawn inside, just kill it, respawn is like an eon.

If you're out to pull, stick to the wall. Gotta be careful there. Look carefully around the corner, target the mob as fast as possible and hit em with a spell, I don't recommend heavy nukes or you'll get summoned. Then run back to your camp spot as fast as you can, kill, proceed with the second mob.
Getting mobs on the 4th level is easy as a SK. FD near the porter, hit it and look around for the roamer. If the floor is clear, stand up, hit the mob at the porter, port down and head to your camp.
This is risky though, you might end up with an add.

Only thing you really need is patience. Took me 1 week to get all reports. At least I made 3 AAs and lots of cash.
