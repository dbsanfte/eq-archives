PoFire----
For Knight items first when you zone into fire hug the right wall to the Jopal Village which is easy to clear, decent XP and drops Knights Gleaming Mace.

In Castle 1 Captain and Vicar of Fire spawn which drop basalt bulwark and charm of defense, both nice knight items.

In Field 2 which a lot of people don’t clear nameds spawn frequently and drop 85 hp mana haste cloaks.

In C2 in fire the nameds drop a 90hp/mana ring with regen 2 and a 115hp earring

All mini named in Eps are one groupable easy, the hard part isn’t killing them its finding them up. Your best bet is to zone in after a patch and track every EP zone.
