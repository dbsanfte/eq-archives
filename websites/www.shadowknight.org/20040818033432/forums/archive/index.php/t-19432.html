Here is another interesting tidbit to add. Silvershadow may have mentioned it but I couldn't tell if he was talking about the portal in the east wing or the one in the south wing.

Guildie found out there are two portals that lead outside to the Overthere. The first, as Groff pointed out, is past Drusella's Room and indeed requires the master key, east wing key, or a rogue with 200+ picklock skill. However, not many people know this but there is another portal. The portal in the south wing is located just before the invisible wall you go through if you were heading to the east wing.

So looks like there are 2 ways to escape Howling Stones without a porter or a gate potion/OT hammer. I do not know for sure though if there are any locked doors on the way to the portal besides the entrance door to the south wing.

My old main was a rogue and I spent tons of time in HS (long before the master key was in game and since wing keys were no drop and no rent we never bothered - didn't have to).

There is a locked door before each of the portals in South and East wing - I don't remember what the keys are. Don't get these portals confused with the ones in North and West which just bring you back to zone in.

The door to go into South wing is locked, but you can get in using the key you get from West(?) which you get a key to from North(?) which you get a key from killing at zone in. Sound fun yet?

Now of course you can get into the East wing by leving up (those stairs are fake) and running over - the door is not locked. BUT, you can't backtrack to the South wing from there. The false wall in that first room only comes IN from South and NOT back to South. Follow so far? Good.

Now, while you are trying to hunt for the keys don't forget that there are some trapped rugs. One in each wing that drops you down to the basement. If that happens you need to find your way to the teleporter in the middle to get back to zone in. Going through the teleporter does NOT clear aggro.

Ok, so with all that said, just get a rogue friend to lead you or better yet a porter friend to port you. Otherwise you will most likely spend many hours trying to find keys and get through.


p.s. Undertakers HT for 731 so it only takes a few of them to turn the tables on a fight - depending upon your gear of course.
