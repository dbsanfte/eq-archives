On a side note, I saved all my skins and salts for my wife's rogue. She had never killed a sarnak and was a bristlebane worshipping halfling. Although she was scowling it was just barely scowling. With just a few stacks of skins she went from scowl to indifferent.

On the Saryrn server the skins and salts sell for 30pp-50pp each quite often in the bazaar. If you want fast faction this is the way to go but it will be somewhat spendy.

Good luck.
Bolas
Fastest is to buy 3000 Mt. Death Mineral Salts and 3000 Green Goblin Skins in the bazaar at 50 plat each, then turn them in for faction. If you are paying that much each for them, they should flow to you reasonably quickly. Meanwhile, while you buy those as fast as your bazaar can supply them, kill stuff in droga and save the salts & skins. Turn those in also. After 40 hours of killing and thousands of salt & skin turn-ins, you should have the desired faction.

And that's the quickest way to do that. You get more faction from turning in salts/skins than you do from killing.

-Bolas
