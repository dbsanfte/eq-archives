Source the 9 items required for the Earring of Solstice in the bazaar, you should be able to put it together for around 3k these days. Just combine them in any forge and take the result to Rebeca Cabbage leaf in Fungus Grove to turn into the all/all earring.

Edit: omg do the draconic pauldrons!

Other quests you might want to look into starting, even if you can't complete them just yet you'll be glad you put the ground work in:

Sigil Earring of Veracity
Starts with http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1667
result is (one of the) best earrings in the game http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2299.

Ring of the Shissar
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1999
resulting in http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=11836,
a nice ring even if you never plan to see the Emperor. Compared to Valorium rings of Galantry, fully raid buffed the Shissar Ring gives me more HP and AC, and it's a good thing to get out of the way. You'd need a raid to get the 3rd floor comms though.

You might even want to look at gathering lucid shards for a VT key, if you think you might ever be in a high end guild. You'll be getting decent xp now and you'll thank yourself later. If nothing else you'll be the envy of other shard hunters ;)
