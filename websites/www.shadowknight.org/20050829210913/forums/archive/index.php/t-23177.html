go there with your SK.

I dont see it being practical at 59 to tank there with just your shaman and I dont think you could do named. Some of the one group named are still red at 70 although most aren't.

Best thing to do is just to go there for yourself and get into a full group with your sk and see about tanking a mob. This should give you some indication as to how difficult it may or may not be for your level of gear and AA.

Then once you know how much healing you had to have with a full group present decide if your shaman could do it.

On Test server I have yet to see anyone under 60 in that zone. But then again we consider it overcrowded with 28 in zone :D

Syfon has a point, a good one, but I suggest an alternative.

Go to the Ruined City of Dranik, off the bloodfields, with a group, and single pull the noc's right at the entrance called "golem maintenence workers" They hit about the same as WoS's South zone wall. It's just a much nicer, easier, safer camp. Use that to test whether you can tank in WoS.

Then, when you are comfortable with that, try WoS. The mobs may be the same but you'll learn the word "SHadowhunter" fast.
