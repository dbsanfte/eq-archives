For level 20 to 35ish the DS will be sufficient, assuming she can do damage to all the mobs, on a place like the beach in Gunthak (Dead don't run). Infectious Cloud will help for this, if she can get it off, but as targetted AE is probably only going to affect 4 targets at once. Don't forget to use potions for added DS.

After she moves through that stage, another option is to use bob and flop with the SK on significantly higher level mobs. Take the SK and the shaman (with the enchanter for tash) to someplace like Dawnshroud. Tash, melee to almost dead with the SK, and malo and lay on dots with the shaman. Then flop and stay down right before it dies. With practice, she will be able to "kill" anything that she can land dots on. Remember not to group the enchanter and SK (or enchanter will get credit for SK's damage). Your goal is to find mobs that shaman can dot easily, with low enough HP for the sk to kill very quickly, but high enough to make the effort worth it. You can use this method to camp for the shamans group cure in Dulak as well.
