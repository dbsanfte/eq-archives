I searched in Nektulos forest for days for a Calcified Sternum, without finding one. Apparently they don't even drop from calcifieds, only greater skeletons drop them, and only extremely rarely. Given the slow spawn of greaters, coupled with the rarity of the drop, and the fact that every Sk in the zone was looking for one, I decided to try the skeletons in Toxx forest. I managed to get one in there in just about an hour and a half. I don't know if I was simply lucky, but I don't think so. They were dropping all manner of calcified bones, maybe 2/3 of the time, so even if the sternum is kind of a rare drop, it'll still come up fairly fast I recommend having sense the dead hotkeyed.

Good Hunting.

I can confirm that it does not drop off of only Greater skeletons, hence my post. It drops from Greater skeletons in Nek forest. It will not drop from Greater skeletons anywhere else, that I'm aware of.
It will, however, also drop from normal skeletons in Toxx Forest. Kill all the decayed's that you find, and you will start to find more normal skeletons, using 'sense the dead'. They drop calcified bones at about a 2 out of 3 ratio. You will probably get a Calcified Sternum in about 90 minutes.
Good Hunting.
