Rukog DethBryngr
Good point. Sidestepping the haters, here's my direct input on the GM.

It is probably more of a status thing for me at this point...even when I got it last spring.

The stats are ok...certainly not something that can compete well with current weapons, but then again...there's not a lot that came out of Kunark that still can.

If you are a masochist and like to feel pain and desolation...do the Greenmist quest, especially if you aren't an Iksar. I did faction work when it was still possible to park a pet and agro the Warslik Woods mobs all day...and factioning STILL drove me nuts. You've got an even bigger hurdle now.

Is it "worth it"? If you are fighting only Kunark mobs/old planes now, then probably...especially if you have a decent shield to make it worth it. With the big AC on Ssra mobs, it can't compare to big 2handers that will shine with their damage bonuses, but you do get kinda tingly seeing 100+ point hits on a 1 hander.

The proc helps a little bit - but I wouldn't say it's an all-out reason to go for it on its own.

All in all, if you like questing, then it might be something you'd enjoy working on. If you don't anticipate getting better anytime soon, then it might be worth it. One of the most group-focused item, the Greenish Metal Shard, can even be seen pretty regularly in the Bazaar these days. And there are probably lots of Mirror MQ's sitting out there for you too. But for most at this stage of the game, it's a "personal triumph" or status symbol.
