Old system
The exp penelty was take out before the group exp was divided. This system screwed the whole group and was completely unfair.

New system
The exp is divided for the group and then the experience penelty is removed from that individual. Only the individual suffers for selecting the more powerful race.

Due to the harsh exp penelty given hybrids and the additional penelty give certain races experienced EQ players shunned those hybrid/race combinations. Once people realized what was going on sony changed the way this worked so people would not have issues about getting a group.

To answer your question she should get roughly 20% more exp than you not 20% more levels than you. This may only account for 2-3 total levels by 60. Rest assured you will not be causing your friend any undue camping because she is grouping with you.
