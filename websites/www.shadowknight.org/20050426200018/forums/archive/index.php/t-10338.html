Hello all,

After taking a break from EQ for a few months I am thinking of starting up an Erudite SK. Although Erudites are quite an unpopular racial choice for SKs I am interested in them for the following reasons:

- In terms of appearance and from a role playing perspective Erudites seem to fit the evil persona of SKs perfectly.

- I want soloing to be a viable option for me, therefore in my view an Erudite’s higher mana pool would be beneficial. As I became more adept at fear kiting with my main character (a 53 Ogre SK) I began to notice that I rarely got hit at all. The thought occurred to me ‘Whats the point of having an Ogre’s tank stats if I am hardly taking any damage?’ Frontal unstunability also seemed a waste in these situations.

- The spear line of DD spells seem very useful for adding significant damage between melee attacks - another reason why more mana would be an advantage.

On my server 30+ Erudite SKs were completely non-existent (although there were a few 55+ players, but they were in uber guilds so they do not really count). Having never encountered any Erudite SKs above level 25 I am pretty unclear as to their role in high level raid/group situations. My Ogre SK was quite a respectable MA on a few guild raids, however, due to their poor tank stats I very much doubt than an Erudite SK could fill such a role.

I would be very glad if any seasoned Erudite SKs out there could let me know what to expect. Some advice on gear and starting stats would also be much appreciated.

Cheers :)

Lunaticfringesk
As an ERU SK, you would probably want to focus your point allocation in one of three ways:

1. 5 AGI, 15 STA
2. 20 STA
3. 20 INT

Between the first two, flip a coin I guess. Personally, Id take option 2 over 1 because you will get gear that adds 5 to AGI pretty fast, so that problem essentially goes away. Many, however, will argue that you have to have 75 AGI no matter what, so that is an option I suppose.

As far as option 3 vs 1 or 2, Id ask yourself one question: Do you plan on taking this ERU SK to lvl 65 and raid high-end (by high end I mean SSRA boss mobs and beyond) and playing with this character a *ton* or not? If so, then 20 into INT is definitely arguable. If not, Id go option 1 or 2. As a matter of fact, Id probably take option 2 over option 3 regardless, but that is highly debateable (heh--we have this discussion on these boards many times), so I make no conclusion as to such.


One other thing worth mentioning...

Mana pool is basically *worthless* when referring to an xp-grind. In general, an ERU SK isnt going to solo any better than a ogre SK. I dont know if I will ever stop hearing this, as someone always brings it up somewhere, somehow. So what if the ERU SK has a larger mana pool then an ogre SK....all that essentially means is that he will have to med longer to get it back to full.

Want to make a difference for soloing? Get something that affects your mana REGEN (c3/4/5, FT items, etc). That is where the difference is at.

A large mana pool, many will argue, only really becomes important at the high end raid game; NOT the xp grind.
