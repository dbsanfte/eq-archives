Yeah, you can start Greenmist at 49. The only tricky parts really are the Mirror (You'll need a pretty damn good group, or 2 maybe if they're low 50's), the Greenish Metal Shard (same thing, might need a couple groups depending on level), and the Glowing Cliff Golem (one group will do it... might even only need a friend or 3 depending on level). The rest you'll find you can likely solo or duo with a healer type.

As to the tracker, if I'm remembering the right guy he pops in the city then runs out and over to a rock near the cliff. He'll reach the rock and wait there for a bit. Uninvis from a distance and Conn him. If he's Indifferent (he'll be in the form of a wood elf as I remember) then he's the guy you want. Then just take the quest from there.

Look in Dirty Deeds for the quest on Khukri's, it's all listed in there along with map points and specific locs.
