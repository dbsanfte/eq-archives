Well appearantly many of you have missed the oh-so-simple point. Let me make it more clear so that there isnt any future spam-posts of unintelligable bleeting.

Cleric gets Rez and its hybrid, the Paladin, also gets rez.
Necromancer gets Rez, and its hybrid, the Shadowknight does not.

Now, my point is, paladins are currently the same in tanking ability as a shadowknight. Therefore, why should the paladin gain the the edge of having a useful rez off the cleric, and we dont gain the same useful benefit off the necros?

Cleric's get HoT's, Paladins are about half the power. Clerics can imbue, paladins can't. Cleric's can DI as well as rez, pally's get a lesser rez. Clerics get the summoned hammers, paladins do not. Clerics get the Vie line, paladins do not. Clerics get the mark line ((the reverse DS's)) paladins do not. Clerics get spell haste, paladins do not.

.... Any of this starting to sink in? We are NOT supposed to be carbon copies of our parent classes. We're supposed to get some abilities that suit our purpose. Paladins are a defensive/healing tank. We're an offensive/leech tank.

Let's look at what we DO get, shall we? Snare, which is damn useful and got me groups in MPG when I couldn't tank it. Why? A) They needed a snarer, B) They also needed a puller, which we can do DAMN well combined with! Ta Da! Feign Death. One of THE most useful abilities in the game.
Only four classes get it, necros, monks, Shadowknigts, and Shamans, and shamans need to complete a quest to get an FD ITEM, with charges.
We get a pet, which up until recently hasn't been very useful, but now is. Hell, the other day in MPG, my pet with virtue and shammy buffs was shielding me now and again when my health dropped. Would it have saved me ass had I been in REAL danger? I dunno, but it might have. Same pet, unbuffed, was being used against Thought Horro Overfiend when we did him with 4 people, and barely needed a heal, maybe one every.. 15 minutes?
We get lifetaps, not only direct ones, but bonds, proc taps, ToTC. ANd that adds up, quickly.
We also get some decent DoT's. We're not pumping out necro damage, but it helps.

And just as paladins get some things clerics don't have: We get Theft of Pain, which I believe is CRITICAL in the paladin/shadowknight balance. We get a zeefer's line without a recourse buff. A self HP/AC/DS buff with an extremely long duration given the mana cost. We get other power taps, like attack and resists and Stamina. We get Hate spells that make us Snap aggro kings. I've kited in Air for a ring event and tried to have a pally tear the mob off me. I had barely cast on it and the paladin was chaining stun and getting nothing.

We.... are... getting... what... we... NEED. Let's not FUCK that up by whining about shit we "want".
