Rhumba_Kween
Generally SK lvl 65 spells are pretty rare. My reccomendation would be to pick up any of the big 3 CoL, ToI and AoH as soon as you see them for sale. On Tunare ToI is super rare for some reason.

You also may want to vary it depending on who you group with mostly.

If you group with a BeastLord much, you can skip AoH since it does not stack with Ferocity. If you group with a Ranger, BeastLord or Pally much, you may want to skip CoL since they all have buffs which because of either better hps or atk are better.

Either way they are all great spells. Good luck finding them.
Kyfe
ToI >>>>>>> all. This spell is far and away the best of them. It lands on near anything and is exceedingly helpful in most situations. With ToI & ToV up my survivability in AE situations went way up. It also helps for gap healing when a cleric is a bit snoozy.

AoH is nice but because of the retarded degrade and effect < recast it isn't a HUGE average atk upgrade over ToH and it is like 8x the mana. Still a nice spell though.

CoL is actually better for tanking than BSS/SOT/SV but any of those is fine, so it isn't vital.
Squigle D'Muerte
Personally in with other melees I get a lot of use out of Aura of Hate. 125 to ATK will make rogues your best friends. The other night with Shaman buffs my ATK was 1500.

ToI is a nice upgrade but not a spell I cast much. I keep it up at all times but only cast in emergencies. Like all taps it eats a lot of mana, so for extra life I tend to stick to BoD. Of course when healer dies and mob is below 50% you will be glad you have it.

In 4 months I still haven't seen Cloak of Luclin for sale at any price. Since it is just an upgrade to CoA not critcial just would be nice to have.

Voice spells (and Terror spells) are pretty useless. Only use I have ever had for a voice spell is to cast on warrior MA in raid.
