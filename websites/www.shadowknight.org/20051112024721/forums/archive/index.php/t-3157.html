The Graveyard is not in front of the castle.

It is the area, directly past the waterfall on the way to the castle, filled with Gravemasters and skeletal berserkers and champions. A Couple of Nec/SK drop (mostly) mobs spawn in this location.

The Herbalist is no longer found behind the locked door with the pit trap, he is now in an entirely different part of the dungeon.

The guy with the greenmist book does spawn in the library, which is past the former "safe hall" and down a second flight of stairs. Every raid to royals I have been on in the last 3 months, I have seen him up on the way thru to get to the King and Queen. The book is NOT no drop, so it can be traded if your guild picks it up for you.
