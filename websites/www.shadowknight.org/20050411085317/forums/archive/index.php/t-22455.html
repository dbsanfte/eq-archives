Getting a tank switch in on Xegony shouldn't be looked at as a real option, 1 tank needs to go the duration. Getting and keeping her ATK debuffed by a necro will help.

SK role is more in pulling her and then kiting and perhaps tanking the waves of spawns. The encounter is about keeping the spawns under control effectively, the tanking on her should be maintainable for hours with a solid CH rotation and BRD/Necro maintaining the cleric mana.
