Groff Gorespiller
The guy in Neriak is "a Tier'dal Correspondant" and he stands right on the path to the Ogre/Troll section of the FQ.

The turn in is three "Troll Pirate Head"s, combined first in the bag the quest NPC gives you. You'll get "A bag of Troll heads" or something like that when you do the combine.

I'm pretty sure they've upped the drop rate on these heads significantly in the recent past, as the first time I went and tried to do this quest, my Shaman duo partner and I cleared all the huts around the fort and many of the mobs in the fort for hours on end and never saw a head.

This time, it took us two evenings of easy killing to get 5 heads (he's got two in case there is a different reward based on class). The plunderers, marauders, pillagers and brokenskull warriors all have the chance to drop a head. The mobs inside the fort (mostly shamen of some sort) do not seem to drop heads, and they are generally of higher level. Just stick to killing the mobs outside the fort, in the little huts. We worked our way around, starting at one side of the entrance tunnel and pulling to that spot until we had a clear path to the next open spot along the wall. Between the two of us, we could get in three full circle clears before his bags were full of weapons and gems and I'd have to start looting. In the span of an extended KEI, we cleared the place around 6-8 times at a very casual pace, with no worries. We also made between 400 and 600 plat each in the period of a KEI off of the weapons and gems.

Took me about 4 hours to solo this at level 53. Everything was green or light blue, so it made for easy pulls, but they still hit hard enough that I was using huge amounts of mana tapping health back and still had to go through a couple stacks of bandages.

Problem is, I did it the hard way first.

Originally, I just camped a half dozen huts or so, pulling anything there and waiting for respawn. This wastes a lot of time.

Much easier to just invis and scout out the trolls, as they're the ones dropping what you want. Target, run back to the wall, and pull with a dark. When it comes lumbering over, knock it down, invis and go find another one.

They're uncommon drops I'd say (I think I killed roughly around 20 trolls for my 3 heads). It does go much faster if you just concentrate on trolls whenever possible, rather than pulling every damn gnome, inkie and erudite that hangs out there.

Just be aware that some of the trolls and basically all the ogres are shadowknights, so expect to eat some HTs on a couple pulls.
