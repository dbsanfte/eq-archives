Improved harm touch is unresistable, and as such will not work with disease-based focuses, magic-based focuses, or any other focus except for ID1-4, or the Solusek line (all spell damage). Even using disc + IHT, it's still unresistable.

As for focuses, can't really tell you what to go for, as I have no idea what stage of the game you're at. Personally, I have a magic focus, and a War Crested Ring that I switch in for 3 seconds while I blow HT, then pop it back out. There is a mask from RZ with a solusek focus on it, as well as a few pieces in Time I think. There are a couple in GoD, it all depends on where you are and what (if) you raid.
