Keevel
No matter why you start you will fall in love with your SK. I made mine just to keep a friend company. My friend no longer plays that char but Keevel keeps rocking on. You may find yourself switching mains.

Play style: You will not be the most physical tank. But adapt to an Erudite style, you mana pool is huge (for a hybrid) you can control agro well, enjoy it.

Gear: With an Edurite sk, if you want to twink him a bit. Park your SK at the ent of the Warrens. Switch to your mage and go pull the mugglewump to the entrance of the warens and kill him there. The mugglewump will sometimes drop a cool shield with RuneII and and Erudite Sk only helm. Switch back to your SK and loot. If he drops any of his other items, all of which are very class specific, just ooc to see if anyone wants it, though you may want to keep the Mage only fishing pole for yourself.

Weapons: You'll want to keep all weapons skills well trained. With your high int, you have an advantage there. Get weapons with low delay for better training. The MoSS is good. For 1HS I use either a Wakashi of the Frozen Skies or a Sharpened Flotsam not great but speed and nice for holding agro.
