Be very careful about such spoilers. First off, always look WHEN they were written. Someone writing about how easy, say.. Rallos Zek is after OoW is released is a lot different than a person doing so from the original perspective.

And especialyl keep in mind that any strat/spoiler that does not include the gear/progression level of the number doing the strat should be taken with a grain of salt. A Qvic guild writing a start to Sir Elmonius Falmont, for example, is NOT going to be the same as one from an EP level.
