nimic
I'd say, as an ogre, stay away from being an Ogre unless you really, really want to be an ogre. Getting stuck all the time sucks, taking up the space of 3 normal toons sucks, getting stuck sucks, and getting stuck sucks :D. Lower intelligence means your skills will advance more slowly then our intelligent breathren, and you will not have as large a mana pool. Last but not least, there is a hefty experience penalty for playing an Ogre - about 15% I believe.

On the positive side, we look intimidating as hell, have very high innate stamina, and are unstunnable by melee from the front.

End game, you are left with one pro and one con. You will still be unstunnable from the front by melee, and you will still be a fat ass. The Ogre unstunnability is not really a big factor at 60+... I still get stunned by the LDON traps, and it seems like every other POP mob procs a stun with their melee attacks.

Play an ogre because you want too, as they have little advantage end game. If your looking to PL your way to end game, the 15% penalty might hamper your progress a bit.
