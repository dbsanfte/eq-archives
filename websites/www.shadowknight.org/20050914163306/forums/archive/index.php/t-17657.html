Originally posted by Zaros Trainstopper
Warriors are better tanks than us. I accept this as a given.


Im not so sure I agree with this sentiment. As many WAR love to point out, the fact that they can take a better beating means little if they can't get the mob to hit them. Translation? Agro control *is* a part of tanking.

This leads us along the lines of one of his main question which was can a SHD tank a boss mob?

The answer is certainly (as can a PAL). The only *significant* difference between a WAR MT and a SHD/PAL ("hybrid") MT is the use of /defensive. That's pretty much it. Without defensive, I would take a hybrid tank over a WAR every single time due to nothing more then better agro control, which is pretty much everything against boss mobs.

Hell, its heavily debated in my guild right now between who will act as MT and that includes even WHEN /defensive is up. A hybrid tank really allows the other DPS classes to *open-up* to a noticable degree and drop the mob faster. The drawback, of course, is that the heals must generally come faster, as /defensive will reduce the damage received (which on most PoP boss mobs, is a scary thing).

At the end of the day, WAR in my guild end up tanking most of the time, but it isn't vastly accepted as the *best* way.
