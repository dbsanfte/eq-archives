I still think a slow / haste tap would be a good spell to add to our line-up. Nothing near the ability of Shaman or Enchanters, not even near the ability of Beastlords.

Level 50 = 10% slow / 10% haste
Level 60 = 20% slow / 20% haste
Level 65 = 30% slow / 30% haste

This spell would be overwritten by any slows / hastes caste by Shaman, Enchanters, Beastlords. It would have a low duration (about the same amount of time as our auras), high aggro modifier, and fairly low mana cost.

Why do I want this...

1) Great for soloists in our class. Or those that maybe group with 1 other person.

2) We know that slows generate alot of aggro, so maybe this could be our new aggro tool? Maybe even add a aggro multiplier to it?

3) Pulling - it would give us a reason to be pullers. Wouldn't it be nice if the mob came in already slowed 30%, giving the real slower the time required to "wait" to cast that big slow?

4) As a "non-damage" high aggro tool it would be used to generate aggro on mezzed mobs.

hmmm, why not?
