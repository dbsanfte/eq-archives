I've done the Loyalist Shield via MQ about 5 times now, the most recent being about 6 weeks ago. My druid did the MQ's on all of them (including my SK, who was the first one I tried MQ on -- and 3 of the 5 MQ's were for KOS-to-Phenic characters).

I've never had item(s) handed back to my druid, so I'm a bit at a loss; but again, my last MQ was 6 weeks ago. The only thing I can think of to watch for is that the Badge of Service "changes" at various parts of the quest. I'm at a loss for words how to describe that but basically the NPC's know if you have the 'right one' somehow (it gets flagged or has a different ID or something). For example, if you screw up somehow along the way, and decide to shortcut and do the rest of the quest but invariably skip part of it because you think you have the right badge already (visually it's indistinguishable) but it hasn't been flagged or exchanged by Nathyn yet. Remember, sometimes you hand the item in and you get it back but it may not be identical.

That would explain the badge being handed back but not the token, because you get that from the robes hand in near the end and it's not cycled like the badge is.

Did you hand in the scroll to Phenic before handing the badge and token in? It might be the scroll hand in which 'flags' those two items as the final hand in items. I am guessing of course, please keep that in mind. Worse comes to worst, you can't go wrong as long as you don't hand in the ashes until you are sure. If in doubt, I would do everything with your shaman again, destroying every item related to this quest first. The only thing hard about the quest, as you know, is Zevfeer, and then the shades fight; so you can repeat everything else easily in a single night.

Good luck.

Just wanted to post that I was indeed able to finish this quest yesterday for my Iksar Shadowknight, despite being KoS to everything in Katta.

I ran through the entire quest except the final turn-in with my wood elf druid and got the Badge of Service and Magus Conlegium Token. I then camped Zevfeer (6+ hours), and looted his Ashes with my SK.

I then had a 65 chanter guildmate (thanks Haary!) accompany us up to Phenic and Pacify him. My druid turned in the Badge and Token, then my SK dropped invis. and slowly inched closer until the trade window opened. My druid then cast invis. to make my SK indifferent, he clicked trade, and got his shield!

A few interesting notes on the Zevfeer fight:

I setup my 65 necro, 60 SK, and 51 druid on the balcony, in the middle near the edge closest to the mountains. I had no problems single pulling and killing mobs here without adds. I was there killing for 6 or so hours when Heratius Grolden finally spawned on the north side of the balcony. After medding to full, my toons pulled and killed him. Not a hard fight, just kept him slowed.

Once he died Zevfeer spawned right in front of my toons i.e. his spawn point is in the middle of the balcony along the railing. We wiped on the first try with Zevfeer at around 80 percent health. Fortunately, a kind 65 chanter and 65 cleric (THANK YOU again Stonehearted and Adarank(sp?)) offered to help me with round two. I can't thank these guys enough for the offer to help. Some nasty people would have brought in friends to gank the mob, but they offered to help with another try instead /cheer good-hearted people.

By the time my necro and SK got back in the zone - BOTH Zevfeer and Grolden were up! The chanter was able to pacify/mez Grolden while we pulled and killed Zevfeer. It was a long fight, but he went down and my SK got his ashes. After looting him, we killed Grolden again and pop! another Zevfeer. Sometime during the course of the second Zevfeer fight - *another* Grolden spawned. Killing him, however, did not spawn another Zevfeer.

The cleric got the second ashes drop and the chanter got Grolden's nifty 2-hand Int caster drop, so everybody won out. Looooong night, but what a great finish. Good luck to all those embarking upon this quest. It's definitely worth doing if you have the patience for the final camp.
