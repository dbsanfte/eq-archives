Ok, the following info will be mostly for untwinked, poorly geared SK's that wanna level up.

I'm from Alk'Kabor server, the gear is not the greatest no matter where you go, and these zones will only be up to PoP.

If your on a PC server, I would probably expect you to be unguilded to have similar gear to the 5 SK's on our server, anyways heres the path I followed.

1-8 hometown (solo)
8-15 Kurns tower (solo + group)
15-23 Paludal caverns (group)
23-35 Highkeep (group for goblins to 30, guards after that to 35 solo)
35-40 Tae Ew lizards in the feerrott(sp?) (solo)
40-46 Echo caverns (solo fear kiting bulks)
46-48 PoJ for a bit, learn the planes (group)
48-50 Echo again, taskmaster room (Solo, undead fear)
50-55 HHK again, upstairs nobles (solo, fear kite, kill guards before pull)
55-56 PoN, some PoV maybe (was toughest level for me)
56-60 PoJ (solo, fearkite prisoners/undead) PoV, PoS (both group) other T2
60-65 Tier2, and 3.. BoT, PoV, HoH, PoS (mostly duo's, and trio's)


theres my list, hope it helps any of you, probably crammed zones for you guys, not sure though, but that was a pretty fast path for me, even though i have yet to complete the last bits lol.

by the way, PoJ at 56+ is very nice AA xp, up to 5% a kill on some guys, fast kills to, and a GREAT fear path, you will see if you try it ;)
