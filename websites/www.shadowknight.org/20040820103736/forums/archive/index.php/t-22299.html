On allahkazam in some of the quest descriptions will say that mobs holding an item for the quest will appear to be glowing. Lufan
First of all, you have to go to Options (before logging in your account) and check the option that says: Dynamic Light

Coldain Shawl Quest # 5 is one example. You need to kill the Wurms in GD to get the biles. At night, you can notice a significant difference that some wurms seem to carry a lightsource on them.

Another example, the Reet Knights/Shamans in Sebilis. I was soloing one and I noticed one of them is a lot brighter compared to the others. I went for that, killed, and got the Reet Crown.

Smidgit
Fire beetles glow when they have a fire beetle eye.

But seriously, very few quest items give off light. Look up the item in Lucy, and see if it says anything for "light." The entry under "light" will tell you just how strong of a light source the item is. I think torch is the weakest (2), and greater lightstone is the strongest (11).


Sample which gives off light (for silverwing quest) (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=69325)
Sample quest item which does NOT give off light (greenmist) (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=3889)
Darkmourn D`Estrellas
More than likely he's referring to the new VP quest items that drop from glowing mobs. As far as I know, every drop for the different quests, whether it be "inscribable words" or blood or power source etc. is torch level illumination. I can personally confirm that the *one* time I've managed to get a quest piece, the mob was glowing quite noticeably. It was in the Deep, and if you've ever spent much time there you'll know that there are a ton of glowing blue rocks all around. I spent a lot of time killing mobs without getting a drop near those rocks just in case I couldn't see the glow because of the rock, and then when one spawned I knew instantly that it was going to have the piece. It's very hard to explain, but unless the mob is standing right next to a torch already, and you have dynamic lighting on as described above, you'll know. Of course, in zones like Torment there are freaking torches all over the place :(
