Okay Enter the hole and proceed down till you see a large drop off and a bridge across to a building. go to the left and drop down.
Across from where you drop down is a door. Go in and to the left. You will go down a long tunnel with lots of turns and stairs.
At the bottom is a large room with a pool. Directly across from where you enter is another tunnel going down.
At the bottom you will see a gate into the city. Go through it and to the left to a pool.
From there you go straight out past what looks like a fallen chunk of ceiling and a bit to the left. There will be a small doorway into another section.
Just throught the doorway you can go right to a pool, or to the left with an immediate turn to the right.
Just past the turn is another doorway into another section of the city. If you go straight through that doorway and between the two buildings ahead you will see a building with a broken door on the left. This is the bank. There is a fake wall through a door back there to steps leading down to the chests that drop the key.
