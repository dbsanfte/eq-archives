Nurga is ton of exp and money but you can get adds

You've never 2 boxed and haven't listed any experience with playing a druid.

With those assumptions in mind

The druid will only be used as a healer, buffer, and root parker for the ghetto CC.

The heals will get a ton of agro and since you don't have a slower you'll need heals frequently, so keep agro holding in mind when picking your spell line up and use your SK to snare as you don't want druid getting too much agro.

Don't use the druid pet, it's green as shit and will get killed instantly and get druid agro.

Look through zone and you'll see several ramps where you'll be out of agro range and only have to deal with wanderers every 10-12 minutes, most of the zone is on a 12 minute pop timer.

Find what are PH's for named, park near there and enjoy, the loot will either be upgrades for you or sold in bazaar for 200-2000pp (with a few higher and extrememly rarer exceptions like the fists that I've been offered 20K for)
