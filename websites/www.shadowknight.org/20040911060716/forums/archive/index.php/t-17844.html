Auronan
One team is best for this, and you'll want either 1 cleric or two non-cleric healers for when you take on the Princess. With the personnel you mentioned, you will have no trouble clearing the Thunder Spirits. Just remember to do a few things:

1) Clear the Thunder Spirits first. If you attack the Princess with the Spirits still up, all of them will come over to assist.

2) No AoE type spells. The Djinn that live on the lower level of the 1st isle will agro if you use AoE spells and when they all come out together, its gonna be lights out for you and your team.

3) Snare the Thunder Spirits and Princess as you battle them since they are all runners and the last thing you want to do is have them run over to anything else and get adds. Let the SK do that since the mobs disease resistances are fairly low.

4) Do not attack the Dwarf Merchant there as you engage the Princess as he is bad news. And likewise do not attack or even speak with Sirran the Lunatic once he spawns upon the Princess' demise.

5) Your best bet is to go to each Thunder Spirit and fight them where they float as to avoid the chance they run and bring adds if you pull them to a different location.

The Thunder Princess will have the Blade of Abrogation visible in her hand if she has it. So you will know going in if you are going to be SoL or not. And last time I was there, the spawn time for the Princess and her underlings was 8 hours.

Hope that helps.
