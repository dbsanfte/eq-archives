Squigle D'Muerte
Exp is definitely better in groups. Best fear kite I have found gives about 2 AA notches (not bubbles, notches) per kill. This aint great exp but if you have less than an hour it sure beats trying to get in a group for that long. Personally I hate aggro kiting (too much down time) unless mob is going to drop something good.

You can still Fear Kite for exp straight up to 65 in a few spots if you can find mobs just below the immune to fear level (54 or 55) in a zone with a good exp modifier and room to kite. For example the goos and feeble rats in PoD can be single pulled to the Gateway and kited there assuming some Necro isn't training the area. There are few spots like this though and you basically have to find them yourself through trial and error. That is what I did.

Sadly when there are so few choices no one is going to post the one they found here and risk seeing it camped all the time. Lower level camp sites are fine to give away because you are done with them, you are never done with a level 65 spot though. Good Luck.
