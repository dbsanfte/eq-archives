Venae
Pretty sure bracers are one sheet, unless they've changed smithing again while I wasn't looking.

Make sure you're using a "Forge", not an Oven or Kiln or anything else. Also, make sure it's not a cultural forge; it should just say "Forge" on the window when you open it up.

Other than that, make sure you're using a regular smithy hammer, not a velium or combine or cultural hammer, use store-bought flasks of water (summoned and foraged don't work), and make sure you're got the right mold.

Also, make sure you put each item in its own slot, not stacked (but I'm guessing you knew that one already)
