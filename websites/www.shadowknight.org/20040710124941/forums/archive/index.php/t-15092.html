Thacker
I took a couple years off the game, and I've been playing about 3 months and until I read this post last week, I had completely and totally forgotten that I even had hide. Hehehe.

Here are the places that hide is completely awesome.

1) Out of mana/health and need to sit down to med and recover a bit. Tummy starts grumbling a bit and you want to go make a sandwich. Well, you could feign, but then you have super slow regen for health and mana. you could invis, but then when it wears off you get attacked, probably while you are pouring your milk and you spill it all over dashing to the computer to save your already damaged SK. Hide doesn't wear off, so you can sit in dungeons like Droga forever and not be attacked.

2) As mentioned earlier, you can hide while feigned, which allows you to stand up and then if needed use it for #1 above. If you feel really daring you can do it under a mobs nose even.

3) You want to pretend that you have the old unnerfed circlet of shadows, so you just hit the button and are invis. :) Ok, so it only works if you aren't going anywhere. Glad I got mine before the nerf. Even with me having the awesome old one, I am using hide a lot when I need to be AFK and don't want to use feign for some reason or another
