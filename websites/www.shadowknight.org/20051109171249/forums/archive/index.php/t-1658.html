Actually the camp where the FT III boots drop is not that bad. You don't need 6 60s, as long as you have an FD puller (which you obviously will). 56+ would be a good idea though. Chanter should be at least 56. I'm 60, so not sure how much worse the aggro range is if you are lower.

This is one camp where SKs really shine IMO. Most of the stuff you have to pull is Slimes. There are 3 classes -- Oozes, Swirling Ooze and Pool of Slime. Never bring back more than 2. And never bring back more than 1 PoS. The PoS are pretty nasty. They hit pretty fast, max at 210 and Enrage.

Bonus is the Slimes do not cast. They are also static spawns, so are very easy to split. As an SK, you can pull and MA. With your Taunt skill, you also let the chanter Slow the MOBs. Most chanters aren't willing to try slowing with a Warrior MA down there.

Your problem will be that there are two types of drops here -- the Boots and the Mosquito Leg Earring. Both are uber items. (There is one Mosquito PH in the camp that you will have to clear as well. Basically the equivalent of a PoS.) Typically, groups go NBG -- casters roll on boots, while melees roll on the earring.

The Named MOBs that drop the items are pretty rare. I've spent a total of about 14 hours down there and have seen 2 Boots drops and one Earring (I won that one:)) Bonus is that XP here is unreal. I have yet to find a better XP camp.

I don't really solo that much any more, so am not interested in the Boots. But they aren't unobtainable, if you can get a solid group together.
