Squigle D'Muerte
If you are trying to keep all weapon skills maxed I would consider getting yourself a two-tined pitchfork. They sell for like 800pp or less and with a 26 damage 22 delay have one of the best damage/delays in the game. Only disadvantage is they have a poor plus.

As far as shield goes it depends how you are playing. If you are fear kiting definitely lose the shield. You want the biggest damage weapon you got out for fear kiting, screw defense you shouldn't be getting hit. Otherwise you have to do what works for you but only 1 handed weapon I have used in a long time was the shard of night which makes rogues drool but is kind of expensive.

If you have the expansion, The Gulf of Gunthak beach has crazy good exp at your level and mobs have fairly low hps. Just go to the beach. Living and undead mobs. Not much room for kiting though so best to bring a healer.
