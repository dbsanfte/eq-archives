The penmaster and penkeepers have been moved to the goblin caves in Frontier Mountains! And this is a good thing because theese goblins are green to my 58th level SK and I can solo them forever!

The "Contraption", however, has not been moved and is deep in the new Droga!

To get to it takes a long and perilous journey past many a dark blue goblin! These goblins do not see invis, so you can hurry past them while invis. Once you get to the jail you will be in for a treat. Entering the jail requires a key from the janitor!

When I undertook this quest the other day and arrived at the jail I discovered that an old friend was ensconced within! He opened the doors for me and gave me his key! This was a lucky turn of events! Otherwise I would have had to enlist a team of stalwart companions to undertake this journey with me!
