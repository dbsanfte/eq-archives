Drakang
If you can make it to the city you have plenty of power for the chests and the ratman guard. I know a 65SK who just did the solo FD flop run down to solo chests. The ratman Guard is tougher but not that much harder than the other mobs around.

When you give the key to Caradon run back up the stairs and FD. Wait till you are sure Caradon has pathed back to his cell (or have somebody stand there invis to watch) and then get up. If Caradon has forgotten you then you can kill Kryenna alone. This also leaves him up for other SK's.

Did Kryenna back when I was 60th and she was tough. At 65 with defensive AA's she hits like a wimp. However she is still almost completey magic resistant. Couldn't land spears although lifetap line worked. She aslo runs on low health and can't be snared. So you usually need some melee power to take her down. With a druid and shaman for healing I would suggest a rogue for the extra DPS to drop her when she starts to run.

Getting to Glohnor is another thing. The mobs get harder down near the undead tower. Had 2 groups and had one almost wipe there this weekend due to a bad pull and repops. (gotta keep moving). But 1 good solid group could clear it. You only need to clear the left side not the right. Glohnor pops in the same place each time now or did for us.
