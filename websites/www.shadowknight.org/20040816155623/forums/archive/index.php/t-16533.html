Moghedred
I did the war relatively recently (1 month ago). I only did the last 5 or 6 missions though.

What I do know is that that cleaver didn't drop for us.
Also, the final boss mob was Claw of Khati Sha who dropped a very nice medallion (Medallion of the Hero of Shar Vahl, I believe) and a 100% weight reduction 10 slot giant bag.

I can't remember seeing the High Commander but it is entirely possible we killed him. However, if he used to be the boss mob (i.e. last encounter) then he's been replaced by Claw of Khati Sha coz that was the last mob. Claw of Khati Sha isn't listed by Allakhazam btw and looks like a naked Vah Shir (a traitor hehe).

There's also a trick for the last encounter. As you may know, you have to occupy every tent in the last camp (i.e. have someone standing inside it) to avoid re-pops (which happen really quickly) and to get boss mob to spawn. The trick is that it is enough to have a pet in a tent. The pets master will have to stay in a tent as well, because if he's aggroed pet will come to help (except with /pet hold command). We did this with 7 people in the end (had a beasty join later on) though we started the last encounter with 6. Cleric died twice, but I managed to tap myself healthy enough to take down the Claw.

And for you d00d-speak nazis out there: it was gr8 fun!
