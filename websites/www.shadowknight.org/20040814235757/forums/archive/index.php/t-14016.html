Easiest way to wiggle inside the Hole without the key is:
From water and facing the Hole entrance, float above ent rock and to the right. There will be a small opening. Move towards the opening until stopped. Face left and down with mouse hitting duck repeatedly.

Just be cautious to make sure you are not ducking after you get through and making your short swim to Hole zone. It will slow you down considerably.
