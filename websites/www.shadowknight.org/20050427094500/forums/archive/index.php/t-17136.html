Yes yes, it's all about the hp/ac bro. Check out my magelo for some ideas on mid-high end droppable stuff. I can't think of anything other than my Haste belt and Fungi that cost me more than 6k, and most is sub 4k, yet I can tank fine.

It can be hard to get good up to date gear with your main in the 50s or lower, don't worry too much, just upgrade as much as you can. Like was said above, if it seems like everyone else has better gear it's because most of them have at least 1 (often several) other characters that are post 50 if not post 60.

Also, I'd like to reiterate the need for slows. Even with my gear (which I consider to be good, though not cutting edge by any means) I have trouble tanking some mobs when they're not slowed.

In short, just look around at some of the gear that's available and set some goals. Since about 45 I've been wanting to hit 3k hp unbuffed (almost there) and 1200 ac unbuffed (could be a bit closer >:P), so I just keep upgrading towards that end when I can afford it.
