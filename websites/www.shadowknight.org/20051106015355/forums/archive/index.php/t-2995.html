1. Fear (the low level one)

Originally posted by Kellisanth
(the only spell casting area I really suck at is Evocation - it's in the teens I think - which means my "Ward Undead" fizzles A LOT! Everything else is mastered... What's the easiest way to get this one mastered? I'm just _now_ wanting to go about and use it -at 30, *duh me* )
Summon your level 9 pet and chain-cast your level 22 undead nuke. The pet won't take damage, and you'll slowly get your evocation skill up. Mind you, if you are 30 and your EVOC is under 20 you are in for one painfully long process, as you will fizzle so often that you will get off one or two casts per bar of mana. I don't know how long it took to get my evocation to 50 or 60, which is when fizzles become less of a problem and skill increases started to occur more often. But it felt like it took forever.

If you have some spare training points you might want to use enough of them to get your skill to 30 or 40. Getting to 50 was a nightmare and even at level 41 my EVOC is 168. For comparison's sake my Alteration is 207, Abjuration 198, Conjuration 191, and Divination 200. I used to train them all up each level, but now I use most of the skills so they go up on their own except for Divination (use a CoS for invis) and Evocation. Maybe when I start hunting some undead spots I'll finally break out the undead nukes, until then I'll try and max out Evocation by blasting my poor pet over and over and over . . .
