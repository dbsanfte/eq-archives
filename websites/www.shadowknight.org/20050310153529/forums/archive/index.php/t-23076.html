killed all 4 today.

Silithis Sisnta in TD - took him down with a single group. 70 CLR, 69 BRD, 66 RNG, 66 WAR, 70 SHD, 66 SHM. the poison-based AE dot can become dangerous if you are light on DPS and allow the fight to go on for any length of time. his melee was not that impressive compared to say mobs in Vxed or MPG.

Gubblegrot Smashfist in OoT - nearly identical fight to the first one. it is not listed on any of the walkthroughs, but he procs an AE dot, this one is a snare called Corpse Rot if memory serves me. again, good DPS will prevent the dot from becoming a serious factor.

Skurza Slickutt in SoNH - this was the easiest of the three SKs which i used a group to kill. the 1500 lifetap is easily resisted with a bard and decent gear. his melee is identical to the first two. (all 4 have the exact same melee skills). he does not use an AE, making him an easy kill if you have a cleric that is sharp to cover the occasional damage spike.

Giligattabus Igglebix in IC - the gnome was easy, i soloed him w/ just festering + blood. i used the 68 pet at first, however the gnome has such an insane amount of HP that eventually Boner would do enough damage to gank aggro and get killed. get a c3, go and solo him. he never resisted a single festering or blood, it did however take me 30min of kiting to kill him. i was more concerned with mana management than a fast kill.

hope this helps you. i had just over 1800ac and about 9750hp with the buffs from my group. i am taking down the WW mob tomorrow to finish up. it's nice to see so much cooperation and information being shared here. Innoruuk's Voice was solved long before many other epics thanks to that behavior.

did them all with me and a guildy, both of us boxing 1 other toon.

all 4 quad mostly around the 200-500 range on average with a few spikes.

used DI but it never needed to fire [except when i was pet pulling the pally's it went off once on my pet :P]

with just the 2 pair of boxed toons it wasn't that hard, but not a walk over either, good fun.

also use harm shield to pull and you'll avoid their HT's and anyspell they might cast on inc.

the gnome guy was just root dotted because we could ;)

the 6 pallys are even more gimp, often 1 or 2 HoTs was enough for each, when they start healing themself move around behind for better hits ;)

stick virt/DI/RUNE on your pet and he should survive getting stunned on the pull.
