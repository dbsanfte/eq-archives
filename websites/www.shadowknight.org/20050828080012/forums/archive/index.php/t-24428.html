A nice spot to duo the shaman and shadow knight would be in Draniks Scar at the Bloodfields zone line. I spent a couple levels there with my SK Shaman duo. Park the shaman on a mount at the bend in the tunnel and pull into the tunnel with the SK. Lots of single pulls and go nonstop, if things go sour the zone is right behind you. When things start to turn Lt Blue/Green you can move over to the Nobles Causeway zone line in Draniks Scar, mobs are a bit tougher and you will have to split some of them.

All in all Draniks is a fun spot with a chance at the odd named spawn.

03-23-2005, 07:17 AM
You can give the Bloodfields a shot, the spot I am using is just at the zone in. There is a little alcove to the left of your zone in spot, directly across from the setup alcove is the zone out line to Draniks Scar. I started here with with my SK at 61 and the shaman at 57. You will need to split the mobs in the first room, 4 spawns come 2 at a time. Once they are all dead start working through the various wings of the tunnel until the 1st room starts to repop. Just be careful of the High Priest that can spawn in one of the side tunnels. He can be a handful, mitigates slow, 500-600 hitter, 675 nukes, C heals, summons.

The best I could get was about 3 kills = 1% reg exp for the shaman (Iksar). The 50s are brutal for exp especially level 59 and unless you are in a solid exp group the best you can hope for is steady exp. My SK is currently 63 and the shaman is 58 I see myself staying here for a bit, it is steady exp, a quiet zone, and ok coin.
