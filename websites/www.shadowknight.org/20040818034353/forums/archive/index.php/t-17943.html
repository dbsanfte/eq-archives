I got calcified bone pieces from skeletons when they conned green in Toxxulia Forest, though they did seem to be a lot more common on light blues.
It did take me a while to get all the pieces.
Just a few pointers for anyone who is going to do the quest in Toxx Forest (it might be faster in Nektulos, I dont know since I did it all in Toxx):

1) Skeletons spawn more often on the Erudin side of the river, so it seemed. Be very careful though, esp. if you've done the Heretic Kobold Molar quest several times. Phaeril Nightshade, a paladin of the Deepwater Knights, hunts this area and was KOS to me. Hes about 25th lvl or so, so he'll drop you pretty fast at low levels.

2) Buy the Sense the Dead spell (assuming you are lvl 9, of course). This is invalubale in Toxx Forest. Dont wander around aimlessly on the Erudin side. Take careful steps and cast this till you sense a skeleton. This spell makes it a lot easier to find skeletons and, as a bonus, is a nice way to raise your divination skill.

3) The nice thing about Toxx over Nektulos is there isn't many players searching for the pieces. Usually I was the only one in the zone. If it turns out to be too difficult, it may wise to heed Shadow Lore's advise. I never tried Nektulos myself, but it quite possibly could be better.

Good luck to any future Knights of Paineel. It may take a lot of work, but the reward is worth it. It may not be the best armor stat-wise, but it looks damn good and there is, of course, the pride factor.
