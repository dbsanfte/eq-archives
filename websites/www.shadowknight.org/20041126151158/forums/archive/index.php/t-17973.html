Since you are duoing, you might try the initiates in Gunthak or the back of the armory in Dulak. The initiates are extremely easy to fear kite, there are 6 of them and easy to single pull to the side. None are undead but being able to fear kite them makes them very easy. The armory is doable but has more risk.

At 54 I did some duoing with a necro in Maiden's eye. There are undead in areas of the zone. Again fear kiting is doable.

In early 50's I also did some duoing with a necro in the City of mist. There are groups of undead there, stable etc.

Good luck.
