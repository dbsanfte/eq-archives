Izzy
Way back in the beginning of this thread people were talking about Gnome STA versus Ogre STA and was one treated differently from another. Here's some numbers.

Using the testbuff feature on the test server, I was able to compare the HPs for different SK races at level 1 and level 25, butt-naked and armored.

Tested Gnomes, DE's and Humans at 90 STA and all three had identical HPs at level 1 (32 HPs) and level 25 (687 HPs).

Tested Erudite at 95 STA and got 32 HPs and 696 HPs. Armored at level 25 with 138 STA he had 771 HPs.

Tested Troll at 115 STA and got 34 HP's and 731 HPs. Armored at level 25 with 151 STA he had 794 HPs.

Tested Ogre at 145 STA and got 36 HP's and 783 HPs. Armored at level 25 with 176 STA he had 838 HPs.

The math. Ignore level 1 and just look at level 25 - Human, DE, and Gnome are identical STA and HP's at a ratio of 7.6 HPs per point of STA (687 HPs/90 STA). The Erudite's 5 INCREMENTAL points of STA netted 9 HP's, or about 1.8 HP's per point of STA at level 25, and that 1.8 ratio applies whether the STA is "base" or is added through armor.

The Troll's base ratio at level 25 is not as good. The Troll only gets (731/115) or 6.38 HP's per point of STA. The incremental HP ratio is the same as Humans, DE's, etc, or about 1.8 HPs per point of STA, again regardless of whether the STA is added to base or through armor (Troll has 20 more base STA than Erudite and has 35 more HPs, about a 1.8 ratio, and armor STA shows the same ratio).

The Ogre has the "worst" use of base STA, netting a 5.4 ratio of STA to HP's (783 HPs / 145 STA). Incremental STA points get applied at the ~1.8 ratio, just like all the others. However, if you "scale back" the Ogre's STA to match the Gnome's STA (90) using the 1.8 ratio, you see that the Ogre and Gnome would have exactly the same HP's. So there is no difference in the way the game treats Ogre STA and Gnome STA.

In addition, contrary to what was said earlier in this thread, small races actually get MORE HPs per point of base STA (at least, up to level 25). Incremental HPs are added at exactly the same ratio, regardless of race. Based on this data, an argument could be made that the Ogre's STA "bonus" is not really a bonus at all, since the smaller races can have the same HPs with STA buffs.

Even though they track one another up to level 25, it's possible that the races have different curves after level 25. I know my level 51 Erudite SK nets about 4 HP's per point of STA, so the curve definitely changes as you level; what sort of HP/STA ratios do the rest of you have?
