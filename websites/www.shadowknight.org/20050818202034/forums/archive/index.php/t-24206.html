I posted this on the SOE site and have received no traffic, so posting here.

Two of the trials in particular have been giving our groups some great frustration lately.

1. Trial of Power. Easy enough trial - kill ~21 mobs in 15 minutes and the MoD is targetable. Kill him, win. The problem has been in the spawn rate. We'll knock off the first 4 mobs then end up waiting 1-2 minutes for the next wave. By the time the 1 minute to 30 second warning has fired, we've only killed 17-18 mobs bc that's all that have spawned! Sometimes, at the 30 second warning, we finally get 2-3 more mobs, but all at once, therefore failing the trial. When we have not had this 'spawn lag' we have won easily, with plenty of mana, ready for the MoD ( who hits decently but is rather easy tbh ).

2. Trial of Fear. Same problems as above, but additionally, we get waaaay too many fearless mobs. On one run, from the start, we had 9 fearless in a row, with 13+ fearless total, and only 3-5 fearable mobs. Again, not many mobs spawned, but the number of fearless really wore down our kill rate. On times where we have won, spawn rate was not a problem and we'd get between 1-2 fearable mobs a wave, sometimes 3.

Has anyone else had problems with 'spawn lag' or just an extremely high number of fearless mobs in these trials? It sucks to line up a good group, fight down to a trial, get all prepared and then to get screwed over by something you can do nothing about ( mob type spawns or no mobs spawning at all ), then to be locked out for 1:45 before we can attempt the /random the trial again. =/
