I play more of a spell oriented style as an SK. There is clearly a drop off at 200, but its not as severe as it once was. Target mana pool, IMO, should be 3k mana. Thats good for most fights where you are chaining spells to last you an entire fight. With KEI I can go over an hour in an XP group using most of my arsenal before I run my mana down enough to warrent a med break. Soloing, I can do 4 to 5 mobs on a full bar of mana when fear kiting, 2 toe to toeing it. I have 197ish unbuffed with 600ish in +mana gear. That is not too difficult to hit as a Dark Elf SK these days, especially with cultural armors out there (the bracers and pauldrons in particular are excellent buys) and the Kael plate cycle.

A large mana pool is basically good for two things: Boss mob fights where you want to maximize damage/lifetaps and having a reserve for when the shit hits the fan. With plentiful mana, you can offtank a slowed add with little to no healing assistance, which is really helpful in certain situations (and takes pressure of the healers). Being at 3k mana seems to be that comfortable level, imo. Enough to do a nice long sustained chain tapping, but not so much that you will never call on that mana.
