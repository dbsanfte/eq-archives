Starting anew with a untwinked toon on a server your not familiar with is very valiant seeing as you had a lvl 57 well-geared sk in your possesion. I would suggest to just level up and get gear as it comes; playing the sk class to the best of your ability. Gear really doesnt matter a whole lot at any stage of the game except for boasting rights ( the way I play anyway) and if you learn all the tricks of the trade you will be 50+ soon. I'd recommend darkforge tho for starters, as the quests aren't very hard and the armor is pretty good. after that you may want to look into tradeskills to earn yourself some money, or just farm. either way gear will come and leveling will come easier. I do recommend more intelligence though...:p

UnholyWarrior
Using hides from bears and silks from the various spiders et al in Neriak, make yourself hide armour (ups your tailoring skill while you are at it); should keep you busy for a few more levels...

At lvl 11-ish, quest the newbie armor (Dark Bashers armor); should keep you busy until 15 or so (with some long waits for some crucial peices). Wouldn't recommend the dark forge until well in your 20's (unless you want to buy the no drop peices)...
