Bulvyne
When I created my SK, I divided up my points between STR and STA, and haven't regretted it.

After having been 60th for quite awhile now, I've noticed that the availability of geat to supplement STA and STR is quite abundant.

Also, at 55-60th, if you're raiding regularly, you're going to be buffed to max STR and STA anyway.

So basically, I'd suggest you enjoy your current SK and save yourself the time of levelling again. I really don't think you'd notice too much of a difference if you went back and rebuilt your character.

Of course, if you really enjoy the levelling-up process and the lower levels, then by all means, remake your character and enjoy levels 1-52 all over again! :)
