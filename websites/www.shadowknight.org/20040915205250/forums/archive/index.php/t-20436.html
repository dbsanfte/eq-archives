Croaakk
There is no best race. It all depends on how you are going to play your toon and who you tend to group with.

If you anticipate tanking alot, make an Ogre for the stats and non-stun from physical stun ability.

If you plan on aggro kiting make a Troll or Iskar for the regeneration abilities (helpful with pact of hate)

If you plan on casting more and fighting less, make a dark elf, gnome or Erudite for the higher INT.

Otherwise, look at other players and decide which graphics you like the best an go that way. Also, if you have a 63 SK and you think "he sucks" perhaps you would be better suited playing another class. You may not be happy with any SK race. (Btw, I don't think 63 DE SK's suck - I have seen some pretty good ones even without "ubah" gear)
