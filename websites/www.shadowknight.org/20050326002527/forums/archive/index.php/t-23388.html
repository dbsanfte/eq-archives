Leafweir
There was a time, back before Luclin when resistances really mattered. You could and would swap out AC and HP gear for resist gear when fighting Vox/Naggy, Trakanon, ST Warder's, etc. However as the resistance value on standard gear has grown, and the resistance value on "resist gear" has stayed the same, this is no longer really possible. You are no longer forced to make a choice, your best resist gear is also your best HP and AC gear. I'm not exactly pleased with this change.

Under the current spell syetm, unless you can climb at least 200 points above the resist modifier for a spell, it's pretty much going to hit you. Even at 200 points above you're still going to get tagged a fair amount. For example Gelaqua's Embrace (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5760&source=Live) this spell has a -400 Cold Resistance on it and the NPC that casts it fires it off once every 10 seconds or so. Why is this resistable at all? So that we may have the false hope of resisting?

What is the current cap on resistance with all PG trials and AA done? 5xx? Does it even reach 600? Even with max resists (which not many have) the above spell is going to hit you frequently. Why bother putting a resist value on it at all? Worse still are the dots that cast every 12 seconds and last for 24 with -400 or -350 resist on them. Even asuming you resist half the time you're still going to be dotted for the entire fight.

What they need to do is either just own up, make these spells unresistable, eleminating the farcical notion that resists matter. Either that or start keeping the minuses and refresh rates at a reasonable level and create some new resist gear to fill the current void.

How about an AE with -300 CR, 1:30 duration, 1:00 recat, -99 curse counters. Reduce the frequency of testing so that failures are not entirely assured. The 99 curse counters makes it nearly incureable, and 300 is reasonably resistable with current caps, get to 500+ and you're going to be mostly ok. Then create some gear with 50/hp mana, sub standard AC and stats, but like 75 CR and make it level 70 required.

Since they have "charms" now you could even make something like a "snow suit" that confers reasonable, but lower than normal raid quality gear stats, but confers 300 CR as long as you are wearing every piece.

As it stands resists don't matter very much if at all. Either remove em all together and stop toying with us or make the system meaningful again.
