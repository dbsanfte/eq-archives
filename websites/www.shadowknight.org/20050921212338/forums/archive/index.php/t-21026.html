Endride is the easiest tower boss.

Should be easily doable charm or no charm.

Have the healers CH from max range, nukers stay at max range as well. Pin him against the wall at his spawn point... sorta walk him back and forth in the 'U' shaped area he spawns in.

1 healer CHing once he was slowed seemed like it was plenty to me, but of course that'll vary with gear. Another priest to help bridge before he gets slowed is a definite plus. Doesn't have to be a cleric.

Build a lot of aggro early, as unless you have a bard in your group you're gonna be OOM rather quickly.
