Seriously, I know it seems like a vague question, it's something I've been thinking about.

IMO I think there's lack of creativity on Sony's part. There was a time when a dru/shammy/cleric could all perform their function, as well as sk/war/pal. I honestly think SoL was a great expansion, not because of all the farming involved, it was more tactics oriented. The boss mobs didn't hit too hard but they had creative procs and or abilities, and an assload of HP that made you have to use different tactics.

PoP had more scripted events that made it interesting. Again, it went away from the "marathon" fights like in SoL, but you still had to sometimes think on your feet.

Now fast-forward to the state of the game today. Are mobs that quad for ungodly numbers the way to make it harder as we grow in power? To me it seems very noncreative. There has to be other ways (none that I can think of off hand), to make things harder w/o just making the mobs hit harder and faster. Things are going to be eventually too watered down. We all picked our class based on the style we wanted to play, but since there are 3 classes that are supposed to tank, 3 classes that are supposed to heal, it seems to be the only way to balance the current game is to make all the classes so close to each other there will be no "class defining" abilities.

I don't know, I've been playing since 99, and I hate the path this game is taking. I'm just looking to see what you all think, and if anyone shares the same feeling on this. If so, what would you do to fix things w/o making the game a "watered-down" product of what it once was.
