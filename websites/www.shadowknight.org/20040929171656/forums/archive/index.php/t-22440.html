Sesshou
Life Leech isn't an awful spell for its level, and if you're not going to level up quickly to get the higher level, better spells, I see no reason not to do it. I know I used it quite extensively in my low- to mid-50s and I certainly didn't regret getting it. It's not a terribly difficult quest, so I would say just go ahead and do it. Besides, it's always neat to have something other people don't.

-edit- Besides, it gives 280 HP and you won't get a single lifetap to beat that until level 60. Granted, it has a 2 minute recast, but only a 2.5 second cast time. It's not overly mana efficient, but our lifetaps never have been.
