I could write way more than I need to but here's some suggestions. I'd recommend donating for nice buffs before venturing out solo.

Old World:
Lower Guk. Lord Hall is nice and blue. Faction hits given for Crown of Deceit quest. Decent exp and coin. Downside, lots of 65s farming faction / Arch Mage and other named.

Sol B. Decent exp and usually empty anymore. Can be dangerous if you get 1 too many LDC adds. You get to see what Boil Blood does finally.

Kunark:
Chardok entrance is nice if you're real sharp. Downside is you can get adds easily there if you don't time repsawns. Bonus for Chardok is the random drops can get ya some cash.

Howling Stones isn't a playground. There is no zone out close so you need evac and getting a rez in there can be big trouble. If you can get a necro, pally, and cleric with keys to join ya that place will be cake. This zone was revamped so the mobs hit a little harder than they used to (mid 150s) deeper in the zone. The pit is good fear kite once it's broke. Loot usually comes from named mobs only and coin is so so. Experience is very nice.

The Hole. If you're an sk and haven't hunted here you may want to reconsider your class selection. This zone also revamped and a little more difficult now. You could prolly handle the zone in elementals though.


Velious:
Kael bank area. Faction and possible armor drops. Mobs should single easy. Kill the roamers first in the hallway that comes from EW to bank. Then start pulling. Just watch out for Bvellos. Coin and exp are so so.

Velks entrance. Haven't been in here in ages and heard they fixed the fear pathing. Exp is so so. Coin comes from silks / velium / rare quest gem.


Luclin:
Try the Gray. Once you figure out what spot to set up in that zone is cake. Will be decent exp and coin for you til 58. Can usually hop a ride out to pok with the druids root / dotting mobs near Ssra.

Maidens Eye. Can be done at your level but will take forever fear kiting. Experience is ass. Cash is ass. Don't bother.

Paludal Caverns. If you don't care about Shadowhaven factions, there is a camp of miners deep in the zone. Nice exp and coin. Downside is they are on top of each other and very social. Also hit fairly hard.

Scarlet Desert. Hit the plateau and fear kite for days. Mobs have a nasty proc but overall it's not that difficult. Research pages and the occassional VT key shard are it for loot. There are a couple of named that spawn but might be tough at 55. Experience not that bad for an outdoor zone.


Planes of Power:
Justice fear kiting was nerfed. Get a shammy, chanter, beastlord partner. Or join a group there.

Disease - Rats are semi easy fear kite due to streaky resists.

Innovation - grouping is your best option.

Nightmare - go back at 61 when you get Ignite Blood and Festering Darkness and agro kite. Yardtrash won't touch you.


Ykesha:
Dulak. Nice coin, easy fear kiting, decent exp.

Nadox. Miners might be tough due to social proximity. Better off zoning in from Gunthak than Dulak. Head toward the bridge and kill the ogres. Experience is nice.

Torgiran. Go back after 61 solo.
