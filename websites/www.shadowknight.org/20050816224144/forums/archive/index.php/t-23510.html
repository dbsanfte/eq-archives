Stoneworker Stonetooth

From zonein, pass through the Murk caves to the open field with 2 bridges on it ( one where the Bridgekeeper spawns and the other where Ukun Fleshthing roams ) and a hill to your right ( looking from the Murk caves ) where the 3 Sharpshooters are. Straight across from the Murk caves is a small hill with a golem by it and a dragorn next to the bridge. The Dragorn is a Prodigy PH and the golem is the Stoneworker PH.

Ungi the Swift

If you setup camp on the small hill by the Stoneworker, facing directly into the Murk caves, you can see the hill that leads to the Sharpshooters from the caves out in front of you. The first ukun *on* the hill ( not at the foot of it by the kvy ) is said to be the PH, though I have not seen Ungi pop.

If your group is decent, you can clear both PHs and grab the Ukun roamer as well. We got a ft2 face piece and a ft2 hat off the roaming dog, and 3? basically ER5 detrimental only focus augs off of the Stone dude.
