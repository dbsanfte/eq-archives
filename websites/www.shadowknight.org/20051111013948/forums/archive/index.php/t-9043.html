OK clerics are difficult to level most of the time due to the lack of their offensive power.

My best suggestion would be to play them properly until he/she gets lvl 45 or so and then just duo them with your sk. If you don't fancy grouping i would suggest sticking to undead mobs such as specs, or frogs in dead side lower guk. That way you get the best offensive power for your mana. If you do this, use your sk to pull... that way you get singles and can jump in if things all go horrendously bad.

Stick to old world mobs rather than going to an expansion zone. Old world mobs have far fewer hp and hit for far less. High turnover of mobs=big xp.
