I just wanted to post about my first duel I had the other day. I have few friends on EQ, and the ones I have are all 55+ and off doing guild stuff. So, usually, I am off adventuring and learning on my own.

I was sitting in HighPass Keep the other day looking for a group. At the time, I was a 27 DE SK and this 29 VahShir BeastLord asks if I want to duel. I ask if he wants to use pets... "of course" he says. I stand up, summon a skele and put a weapon on it. Hit the 'pet guard me' macro and accepted his duel.

He sicks his kitty cat on me and gets an initial hit in on me. I fear his pet and my skele is on him like white on rice. I kick off the HT, disease cloud, engulfing darkness, siphon strength and lifetap while I'm swinging and bashing the hell out of him. His pet comes back and starts in on me doing minimal damage. I take a glance at the life bars and notice I'm a bubble under full and he's got 2 going on 1.5 life left. He was attacking my pet still! LOL! My skele drops so he finially turns on me. I ignore his pet and keep swinging, bashing and lifetapping. He got down to one health and tried to kick off a heal on himself - HOW FUTILE! *BASH* And his heal was interrupted. I actually gained 0.5 bubbles of life back from him from lifetaps. At the end, I was down 2.5 bubbles of life. He died and comes back to get his stuff.

"Were you buffed?" he asked. "Only with the spells my class has and my gear. Nothing extra." I reply. "Damn, I'll think twice about dueling a SK again. I thought I would be able to beat you, especially being two levels below me. You owned my ass. Good duel" he said. "Thank YOU" I reply.

I love bragging rights, especially when afterwards, I noticed that almost everyone in HighPass Keep came up to watch the fight. A friend watching says that someone set out a shout or ooc message saying a 27 SK was dueling a 29 beastlord and was barely taking any damage.

I have no problems finding groups now in HighPass Keep.
