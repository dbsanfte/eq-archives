100 hits after weapon afinity

If you have a lifetap proc and you use SoC, you'll probably be better off with WA. What people don't point out when they talk about how WA is so bad, is they don't focus on the extra damage done by WA and compare it to the damage lost. Every extra time your WA makes your weapon proc, it gives you guaranteed damage, compared to a small chance that you would have your self proc fire. If you take any of the math people have done showing how much less you could have self proc go off, and then figure out how much damage would be done by weapon and aug proc, you would get a very significant increase in damage. Yes, it is true that you will have a very small decrease in self procs, but the overall increased damage makes up for it IMO.

Originally posted by Thack

so why haven't you done any of the math to show how "very significant" this increase in damage is?

in actuality, weapon procs are a very minor source of DPS unless you have a very nice weapon proc. anyway, if you use the formulas (http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=20180&highlight=formula) you can see just how much you'd really benefit from WA5 or an augment. for fun, lets run through an example.

vanazir, auged with 150dd, and using mental horror fighting undead.

to adjust for resist rates and the damage done with 150 mana from mental horror, i'll rate vanazir at 95, the aug 150dd at 140, and mental horror at 700. (270 base, 430 damage from 150 mana).

at 19 delay (vanazir 100% hasted) this is what i'd expect in procs/min, each column increasing in WA level:

primary..: 2.00 | 2.20 | 2.40 | 2.60 | 2.80 | 3.00 |
augment: 1.87 | 2.05 | 2.22 | 2.39 | 2.55 | 2.72 |
self-buff : 1.75 | 1.73 | 1.71 | 1.68 | 1.66 | 1.64 |


buff with no augment: 1.87 | 1.86 | 1.85 | 1.84 | 1.82 | 1.81 |

so at WA0, damage/minute equates to 2* 95 + 1.87 * 140 + 1.75 * 700 = 1676.8
at WA5, damage/minute equates to 3 * 95 + 2.72 * 140 + 1.64 * 700 = 1813.8

so WA5 in this scenario would up your estimated proc DPS by 8%, or 2.3 DPS.

without the augment proc, WA5 does even less.
then you're looking at 1499 without WA compared to 1552 with WA5, a 3.5% improvement in proc DPS.
