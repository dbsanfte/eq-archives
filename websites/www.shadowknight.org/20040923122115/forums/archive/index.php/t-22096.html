Jovec
1. Increase to Knight tanking power bringing us closer to Warriors and seperating our tanking capabilities further from non tanking classes.

Issue number 1 is complex, and probably deserves a more thorough description. There are 3 areas of SK tanking that need to be balanced: War vs SK, SK vs Pal, and SK vs the remaining classes. I'd suggest something like the following. Comment as you see fit.

-----

1. Analyze Shadowknight tanking in the following areas to ensure that the variances are as intended and within desired levels.

1a. Shadowknight tanking versus Warriors: Tanking is the primary role for Shadowknights, and therefore we must be able to perform that role at a level suitable enough to maintain grouping and raid offtanking desirability. Analyze the current situation with AC, HP, tanking mods, and skill caps to ensure that the warrior advantage is not now, and will not become, too great as to reduce Shadowknight tanking desireability to unnacceptable levels.

1b. Shadowknight tanking versus Paladins: The Shadowknight community believes that both knight classes should mitagate evenly. On stunnable content, this is not the case and Paladins have a clear advantage. Adding a Fear2 line (or modifying the terror line to act as Fear2) would bring knight class mitigation even, with the differentiating factors being the Paladin's spell focus on healing and the Shadowknight's focus on damage.

1c. Shadowknight tanking versus Rangers, Monks, etc: The lack of an immunity disc, coupled with the High DPS/Low HP concept taken to the extreme in GoD content, means that too often, guaranteed immunity is a greater advantage than the higher mitagation, AC, and HP of Shadowknights. Address this situation to ensure that knights are always preferred ahead of the aforementioned classes as primary or back-up tanks in the new GoD paradigm where bosses and minis routinely die in the 30 to 60 second range.

-Jovec
