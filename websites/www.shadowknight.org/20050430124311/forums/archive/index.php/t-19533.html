Just wanted to post an update on a couple of place I have tried.

We went and did the flies in PoD and the camp was definatley doable. There were alot of people in and out of the zone, but it seemed that there were always mobs to pull.

Also, on a wild hair, I realized that I had not gone to Dragon Necropolis for a while. I figured we would head over and kill the Phase Spiders and see if we could get a PSC for my BL. This camp was very easy, not to mention safe. Setup camp right by zone out and pull spiders as far in as you can go until repop. Even 3 boxing, I would commonly run and tag 2-3 spiders to bring back. The magic resistance was a bit high on the spiders, but with Tash it was not a problem for a 53 enchanter to keep 2 mez'd and everything slowed.

On a side note, we did get the PSC, and it was refreshing to get some loot from a camp for a change.

I still haven't gone to Acrylia yet, but plan to try there soon.
