PE improves CA, CS, ND.

If you dont have them to atleast 1 then you dont get the increase PE provides until you get them to at least 1. Once you get the increase, that's it, it doesn't scale and it doesn't go away.

You could do PE first, but it would improve 0 because you dont have the others yet.

The reason you should do it after getting them to all to level 1 is also AA cost vs improvement.

Aye, this is to maximize the value of the AA's you purchace. To buy PE earlier would mean you bought something you can't fully utilize yet.

Buying the others first doesn't lessen them in any way, you get the full benefit while you are saving for the next purchace.
