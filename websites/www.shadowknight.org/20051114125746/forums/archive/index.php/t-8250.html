We were mostly gimped because of unexpected side effects on our spells.

We ended up having a very high agro through spells that never were designed that way, we could increase our DPS through spells originally designed to heal...

These abilities did raise our overall efficiency and prevented us to get real upgrades for a long time, which of course led to an immediate loss of efficiency compared to other classes when the circumstances didn't allow us to use those tricks due to a lack of mana regen, interrupts....

With experience we were managing to lessen this lack of efficiency and were pulling our weight.

VI nerfed our instant agro, and through new design eliminated the side effects they didn't want, as a result we were able to gain abilities actually fitting our class and that we can use in all circumstances.

That is mostly what brings us ahead of what we were, AAs only come after.
