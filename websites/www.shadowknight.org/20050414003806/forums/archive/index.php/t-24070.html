I widdle it down to about my last blue bubble, and try to keep it at that.

personally I try to keep aggro, and the ac tap at the top of my casting list. if I have any mana at all, it goes toward these two spells(or three, in cast you need to use a no damage spell, like fear. otherwise I just use Clinging darkness for now). if I have anything else to work with, I lifetap.

in xp groups I never have that much mana to waste. it generally stays at around 10 or 20 percent for hours, because eventually you know that good xp groups dont stop! as a cleric I magine you're generally at FM if the group is going good, at least at 66.

it's true that sk's almots never stop pushing a button during an xp grind, even at lower levels.

so dont be too upset about never having that much mana to throw out at anything.
Slein Jinn
I'd much rather hover at 80% than 20%. If you're hanging at 20% mana, and something goes wrong, you don't have the mana to deal with the situation. If your healer goes down, you've only got a few lifetaps in you, or whatever the situation may dictate. If you've got 80%, you're in a much better position to deal with an adverse situation... Blow your mana at the end, not the beginning.
