Just getting a tap off while tanking is painfully unreliable, as is synchronizing a tap so as not to be overlapping with incoming heals...your first mitigation priority (aa's aside) is having high AC in order to take fewer max hits. Our healing isn't meaningful mitigation even if it crits; spikes of dps from a mob in GoD are not reduced by reactive self-healing (i.e. they will still drop you dead before you can get a tap off).

Our real issue continues to be, as ever, our inadequate innate mitigation capacity, which would scale if it were ever improved.
