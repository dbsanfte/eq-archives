Jabbuk
From the way I understand it, the potion only helps slightly. Iksar illusions will certainly increase your faction over what you'd have as a non-iksar, but you'll still need to do a little bit of faction work. Mainly I believe they're used so people don't have to raise the necromancer faction to non-kos. The necro's aren't directly involved in the quest, but they are right next to a few of the folks who are. The Ikky potions just reduce the amount of faction you have to do.

Personally, i just went ahead and did all the factioning. It doesn't really take all that long. Just go to places like OT and kill sarnaks while you're xp'ing up. That'll raise your Iksar Warrior faction high enough to do Knives and Bone Chips (although you have to keep an eye on the Crusaders wandering around while handing in chips).
