Although I'm sure the scythes do 'rock', IMO I don't feel they rock enough to displace a piece of Mir armour or half a piece of Tak armour or a nice purchased augment.

What I mean is, your gear, your ft, etc. will be working for you all the time while you are tanking; whereas your scythes will only be used on undead (although admittedly doing ~40% more damage than Deny Undead). If you are happy with the gear that you have and have no weak spots that could be improved by getting some Tak gear first, then by all means a scythe may be the best option.

IMO, it all depends. Depends on your gear, guild, and future goals. If you are in a raiding guild, doing luclin -> PoP, then FT gear should come in a reasonable time. If not, then you may not see FT gear for quite a while, and depending on your normal play, the Scythe may be best put on hold. You've got FT7, so you should be pretty good in FT, but, on to my next point...

Group makeup - for instance, my 'normal' group at this time consists of a ranger, rogue, wizard, shaman, cleric, and myself. So that's DPS, DPS, DPS, slower, healer, tank. Average mob lifespan in LDoN is 30 seconds. Sure, with Scythe I could up my personal DPS, but the benefit to the group is next to null in comparision. FT is more important in that scenario, as there is only ghetto CC, so using my mana to heal myself at times is more important than an occassional nuke, as the cleric may have to heal multiple group members.

It really depends on your play, what you plan on doing in the near future, and your common group make up. There is no blanket wrong or right choice, just the best choice for you and your group.
