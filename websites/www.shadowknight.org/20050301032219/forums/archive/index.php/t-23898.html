Boomjak (a pally) wrote this up:

(Ghoulbane Quest)
The Dusty Blue Stone

*Talk to Vida Sweeps in Nettleville Hovel to obtain the quest.

*Visit The Baubleshire and talk to Tawli Whiskwind in Baubleshire. She will give you a broom.

*Go back to Vida and she will give you the Dusty Blue Stone.

*Examine the stone and you will be given the quest.

*Go to Greystone Yard and talk to Gruffin Goldtooth.

*Head to Thunderous Steppes and mine 40 rock samples. You only get credit from samples mined from Windswept Stones. Stonecrest Ore will not give you credit. You need 90 skill in mining to obtain the samples. (You can get to 20 in Oakmyst Forest and to 90 in Antonica.)

*Once you have your rock samples head back to Greystone yard and talk to Gruffin again. He will tell you to seek out a mage who understand minerals.

*Head to South Qeynos, enter the mage tower and use the red portal to visit Aristride Calais. He will ask for the stone and send you to Thundermist Village in Thunderous Steppes. The village is somewhat hard to find. Follow the main road from Antonica into Thunderous Steppes. It will eventually make an "s" turn. You will see some wagons, etc., barricaded up to your left. That is the entrance to the village.

*Talk to Olden Highguard in Thundermist Village and he will send you back to Aristride Calais.

*Aristride Calais will send you to find a priest.

*Head to the Temple of Life in North Qeynos and talk to Toranim Skyblade.

*Toranim will send you to StormHold to kill 29 ghouls. You don't actually need to kill ghouls. I killed Defiled Sentries and it worked just fine.

*Head back to Toranim and you will be given two choices. You can end the quest, which I did not do, or you can continue on. If you continue on, you will receive the Restoring Ghoulbane quest.

*A Key to the Past Quest. You are asked to complete the access quest for Tomb of Valor.

*Head to the Library in Stormhold. You may require a group depending on your level. The mobs were blue (group 2up arrow) mobs to a level 25.

*Talk to the Librarian and he will give you the access quest.

*Kill Defiled Squires until you receive all three pages that the Librarian has asked for.

*Return to the Librarian.

*Kill Defiled Knights until you receive two more pages. [Thanks to P.Calvert]

*Return to the Librarian.

*Head to the chessboard and Kill Lord Andros. He is a rare spawn so you may need to kill the priest placeholders and wait. He is a level 20 group mob with two guards.

*Head towards the spiral staircase and into the barracks. Guard Captain Hess is another rare spawn who spawns at the very back of this room. You need to kill him. He also has two guards.

*Head down the spiral staircase to level 2 and into the Atrium. Go South, and you will find a room with Defiled Cruasder(s), A Locked Strongbox and hidden in on the right two Zombie Handmaidens. Kill the Handmaidens to finish this part of the quest.

*Head back to the Librarian who will tell you to go find a key. Why didn't he tell you all this in the first place? =)

*Head to the entrance of StormHold and look by the statue beside Sir Valinayle for a key. Grab the key and you have access to Tomb of Valor.

Tomb of Valor
*ToV is an instanced zone. You can enter it once every 8 hours if you fail the mission or once per week if you succeed.

*You need a minimum of three people to enter the zone.

*Get your ToV group and enter the zone. Do NOT touch anything. Touching the coffins, etc. spawns mobs.

*The sword should be on the wall to your left as you enter the zone. Go and grab the sword. Finish the instance or not as you wish.
*Head back to Toranim Skyblade in the Temple of Life and get the Weakened Ghoulblade.

Reward: Weakened Ghoulbane: Primary Slot, One-Handed, Skill 160-190, Damage 9-27, Delay 1, Health +24, Wisdom +1, Power +24, Strength +5, LORE, NO TRADE.
The quest continues from here, though I do not yet have the information.

Final Reward: Ghoulbane: Primary Slot, One-Handed, Skill 160-200, Damage 11-34, Delay 1, Health +28, Wisdom +5, Power +28, Strength +9, LORE, NO TRADE.

Bulvyne
Finished my GB up last Friday, and have a few quickie comments.

First, I am somewhat disappointed in the stats on the sword, to be honest. I don't consider myself a powergamer, but the sword is only marginally better than a feysteel flamberge (in my opinion), yet requires enormous effort to acquire. The flamberge (and other similar weapons) are useable at a lower level, and are far easier to get (lucky on a lotto, or 1g at a broker). I am still happy I got it, and I don't mean to sound like a complainer. The guildstatus points were appreciated, of course. ;)

The final portion - going into Nek Castle and defeating Lord Everling - was enormous fun in a high 30's party - and not even a full one at that. We had a 41 Shaman, 39 Druid, 37 Roge, and 38 SK, and it was a romp. Most of the mobs in the castle were grey to us, but the end fight was pretty fun, being a decent challenge. Just a few short weeks ago my full group of friends trudged slowly and carefully through the castle in about 4 hours. Friday night, we were down to Everling in under 2. It was still great fun though! :)

If there was one part I wish they'd change, it's the tiny portion where you have to go into the Tomb of Valor. This part is so hard - but so easy. You have to do the access quest for ToV (a pain in the arse), and then once you get access, find 2-5 more people to enter with you. Once you enter, it takes 10 seconds to grab the sword... and you're done. :mad: You leave ToV and never have to go back. I wish they'd remove this part totally, or make the interaction with ToV meaninful in some way. :/

Anyway, thanks again to everyone for taking their time to post on this thread, and others, to help those of us seeking this fabled blade! :)
