Elhrairah
I was "afraid" of fear as well before i got my amy eye. In reality, this is the easiest part of the quest so far. You have a better chance of dying in Nightmare kiting glooms or in PoG from random adds than here. All you have to do is:

1) Mem harmshield
2) Invis, get on horse, zone in
3) if aggroed at zone in, harmshield, break west on horse, FD at wall, wait your 2 minutes, stand up, invis, creep north towards temple.
4) If necessary clear NW corner or just train/FD the mobs away.
5) To pull, simply run towards the NW corner of temple, ull chain aggro everything almost as soon as you cross the fire. Just run back thru fire, hop on horse, break to corner, FD.
6) pretarget your amy, stand up, snare, FD. FD timer and festering last same amount, so wait until u see ur FD message (***NOTE:VERY IMPORTANT***, if you stand up b4 u see that message, thats how noobs get DT'ed or x-loc from random aggro)
7) Stand kill Amy, toe-to-toe or kite if u have too, a lvl 65 sk can solo these guys with snare alone, they have crap for hps
8)loot eye, gate.

The only issue I ever had was with parking the stupid Wraith of Shissar guy. If he adds, just treat him like ur next amy. Single him the same way, satnd up and snare him, kite him south then when he gets far enuff south, break to NW corner and FD. Hell stay where u parked him for 20+ minutes at least.

This part is, like the PoG drop, is all about FD tactics. Bring your blood ember as it will save u alot of mana here. Have HS up for emergencies, about all thier is to say.

I wouldnt bring a group for this camp, they are a liability to you here, not an asset.

Nasiir
I did this solo when Fear was fully popped. A group would definitely be a hindrance to a SK trying to do it.
I ran in, ran towards the NW corner and FDed (actually FDed once prior to reaching to top corner, just because I was worried about chain aggroing a golem). ***Always wait til you see 'your enemies have forgotten you!' message before getting up! (if you don't expect DT)*** I set up camp in the far north of the west most side of the temple, against the zone line.
When ready, I walked to the temple, used 'target closest npc' I was able to pull the Amy's out, then I waited til I could see the first Amy, cast festering, then FDed. At the 2 minute mark, just as all mobs had forgotten me, and when festering was just wearing off, the darked Amy was still just where he was when I FDed, so I'd pull him back to my spot, straight melee him down, then do any resting that I needed to, and started over.
A few times, I had to run NW to recast festering for resists. I also had an roamer when I was fighting in the NW corner (which is why I moved to the spot I mention above - which was completely safe from all roamers when I did it). I also was very careful not to pull the Amy's when CT was near the area the Amy's path out and around to the back - consulting Ms. Cleo is highly recommended.
I think the piece dropped on the 4th or 5th Amy.
