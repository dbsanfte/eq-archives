I'd say you should have no problem, Amph. I duo'd mostly in PoN from 61 - 63 in Treant Valley (between GY and river towards the hedge), where there are 3 static treants plus one static raven/treant/spider all within view.

This was right after PoP release when I did not have access to any Tier2 zones (didn't pass trials until lvl 65) and back then my gear was not that hot (not that it is now).

I duo'd with my druid, who although he was 60, did not have the better lvl 63 partial-CH nor any heal-over-times -- so yes he did aggro a bit more than I'd liked to have but nothing unmanageable. Aside from self-buffs, I would have had PoTG and druid DS/regen.

That said, the world has changed in the last year and it's easier, for the most part, than ever to duo in PoN. My 60 monk twinkie tanks duo now at the same spot; although the druid is now 65 which does make it easier for the monk to tank, although there is still no heal-over-time, slow, etc. but has better buffs, DS, regen, etc. than he did at 60.

I've also duo'd with my cleric there (although with the monk, not the SK), and the only disadvantage there was no snare for runners [and they do run], so I rooted with the cleric, but as a SK you have snare covered. With heal over times early on and CH for when you need it after aggro is established, you should be good there.

Moongarm
Something I always try to look for if I want to go off and duo with my SK and Cleric for exp is a good population of undead. Clerics can deal a respectable amount of damage on undead mobs which can shorten the fight and increase the experience rate.

You'll see a point with the duo where you can tank the mobs all day long without straining mana supply so your cleric will be nuking more as time goes on. At that point hunting undead is your more efficient route to good exp.

There's a good spot in Nadox with a big population of undead that will give you about the same experience rate but also give you decent coin as well.
