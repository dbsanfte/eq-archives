I want to add a different spin on this a bit.

I agree that you need more HP and AC. On top of that you need more DPS out of your guild.

Your going to run a 4 - 5 sec Cheal chain and then beat on Vindi. In essence the guild calculation has to run like this...

Vindi's HPs/Guild DPS = Secs to kill Vindi

(Your HPs/Vindi's DPS) * Secs to kill Vindi = Number of Cheals you will need

It looks like you will have a 3 person Cheal chain (leaving one for spots/Rampage healer) so, basically people will be chain casting Cheal. Depending on there mana pool, you can give a good estimate of:

Lowest mana pool Cheal Chainer/Mana per Cheal * Length of Cheal Chain = Max number of Cheals/Fight

So, then its a pretty easy comparison.

Why I am saying this is even if you up your stats, the guild itself can not make that equation work at the moment. So, what I would do is find a copy of the old Mob Tier Chart (Vindi is a Tier 3 mob iirc) and see where you stack up as a a guild on Tier 1 and 2.

So, can you guys do the Kunark Dragons including Trak? If not, then Vindi is out of reach. Set a plan to work your way up the chart (increase guild size, up levels of folks, add stats to your main folks [FT for healers, HPs/AC for MAs]) and you will get there soon enough.

One last thing. Expect at least 1 add during the fight and have a plan on dealing with it.

I guess the others are posting in my vein, which is this. Any main raid mob for a guild at any level is not an individual issue its a group issue.

The main elements are:

DPS, Healing, HPs of MTs

If you want to grow your guild capability, you grow these stats of your characters.

Raising your HPs alone don't fix the other elements and with Vindi create a Rampage tank.
