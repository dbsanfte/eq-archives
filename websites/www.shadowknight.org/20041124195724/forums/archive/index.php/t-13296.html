Keyan Aubaine
On to something more realistic...

Out of the two you mentioned my favorite would be the swarm pet, mostly for RP reasons, i don't exactly see what in our class would be leading to such a skill. As far as game mechanics as concerned, since neither would bring more than a situational thing, i would rather have something i can use to help push mobs where we want them, or increase a little our burst DPS with the swarm pets, than add to our ranged combat because i don't exactly see what it would bring (other than a slightly higher DPS when we cannot fight in melee obviously).

Personally i'm even more fond now than ever of the death peace AA that never went past the luclin beta, the number of our spells that cannot be swapped in due to long recast (harmshield, zevfeer's bite, the spear line, the voice line) vampirizes our spell slots quickly.
Swapping in DoTs or buffs is not an issue, but these take up to 10 minutes to load after the moment we mem them, which makes it required to keep them up permanently or not at all.
A death peace AA would allow us to free up that valuable spell slot to fit one more of them, and since it would keep the same mana cost and cast time, it wouldn't modify the balance for agro control/pulling (the 100 mana of DP is about irrelevant anyway these days).
A VERY important bonus would be to totally remove the "spell recovery time not met yet" issues with death peace, at this point i consider this bug to be most likely a part of EQ in a permanent way, and DP moved to an AA button would at least make it recovery time-proof, and would take away a lot of frustrating deaths.
Additionally this AA is already coded in the game and wouldn't require much work at all.

I don't think allowing us to have two timers for IHT and leech touch would be a good thing, it would open the door for much higher burst dps which imo is not something that would benefit the game (we are already ahead of any other class in that area).
On the other hand maybe changing IHT so it's a default 7200 with or without the discipline unholyaura would be more interesting, it would remove a good part of the nightmare of timing the disc and the skill since both have different refresh times and are most of the time used together (since the discipline doesn't affect leech touch). It would make the discipline effectively irrelevant at level 65, but honestly i don't think that will be an issue, and is way worth removing the timing difficulties.

The AA version of cloak of luclin wouldn't be bad at all, giving it the exact stats BSS+CoR have would make it worth using over other classes buffs. The issue though is that it's a level 65 spell (ok the worst one of the bunch, but still), and it would be hard to justify it.
