Catharsis
1. Open with a CD or two
2. If you lose agro (and only if you lose agro) taunt and cast CD again in that order. Taunting when you have agro is useless.
3. Resists or immunity from spells doesnt change their agro generation.

That should be all you need to know in an exp session at 49
Trysbrina67
It doesnt matter if clinging darkness is resisted, it still generates aggro.
Spears (when not resisted) give a nice chunk or aggro and damage.

My suggestion would be to use Clinging, highest terror and a spear to help keep aggro until you hit 60+(to be honest 2 clingings + melee should be enough aggro for 99% of fights). In the 60+ game the mobs you fight are too high level to be affected by fear and fear spells generate more aggro than the terror line.

Taunt is a skill check, when you successfully taunt you gain the aggro on the mob + a small amount of hate. Taunt has the best success rate on dark blue mobs and below. Using taunt on evens, yellows and reds tends to have a lower chance of success.
