Androsulach
You're a little low, but you might be able to handle cave basilisks in Jaggedpine in the caves from the Blackburrow gem. You'll need to watch out of the cave basilisk genitors, since they will most likely be white at 34. I did 37 to 44 killing basilisks there. You will want to con the basilisks before you pull one, but you should be able to kill them. Depending on your AC, HPs, and DPS you shouldn't have too much trouble with them at 34. You can probably add genitors starting at about 36 or so. The genitors drop eggs, that you can bake with or sell in the bazaar for 5pp a piece.
