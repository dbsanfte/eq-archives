Rhowne
There's a way to bring him down into the pit, but it does require killing the other giants on the ring that connects to his platform. Best bet is to grab a FD class (SK's work best for this imho), and aggro him - go around to the right side of the ring (if you're facing the platform in the middle) and from there you have to just work it.

FD when he runs too far around the ring. Move closer to the ledge before he gets to you and after he's run past you. Pull back when he's near you on the ledge. You have to "feel" it, though - but it is very possible to bring him down into the pit.

A group of guildies took out the Air tower boss with one group. In any case, I highly recommend taking a competant enchanter along to use a charmed giant to up your DPS.

Water tower named would be about the same difficulty - just invis/paci your way up to his room and a single group can get to him in no time.

Sand is a short tower, but Skygge's make for super rough going. Lightning respawns are very fast considering the distance and number of mobs to kill. I have no doubt someone will single group each BoT tower, but for Sand and Lightning you won't be able to do it if you're looking to hit those named for gear upgrades (unless it's for an alt).

18 skilled people can hit all 4 Towers with a fair amount of ease. The number can drop to 12 depending on classes and gear. Less than that and Sand/Lightning become very difficult unless you have a high percentage of healers and charmers present.

Ghismonda
One grouped all the BOT minis except Kuanbyr so far. Not exactly normal group make-up though.

Me (sk), mage, enchanter, enchanter, cleric, druid.

We usually pacify our way UP to the bosses (if pacify is resisted, mez/gtf out of the way, mem wipe), but have never actually attempted to pacify the rim mobs for the Brynju pull. Fear of resists and a shitload of adds, since they'd be pathing back through mobs who's pacify wore off a long time ago... but anyway!

I don't know about DPS in your groups, but with the DPS of three charmed elementals we can kill the respawns without worry.

To single pull them from the pit, tag whichever and get close to the rim in the pit. Run with the giant as he paths back and forth, FD if they get close to another giant. Do not let them enter the tunnel. Eventually they'll have enough aggro built up that they'll warp or jump off the rim instead of running toward the tunnel. With each mob that you clear, the next pull is easier because you have more room to let them path back and forth and build aggro without having to FD as they get close to other giants.

Easiest one for our group has definately been Icestorm, and Chaoswind a close second. Still working on the strat for Hailstorm.

Good luck!
