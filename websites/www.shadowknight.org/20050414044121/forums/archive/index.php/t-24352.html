Klotar AngedeMorts
Well, I was hesitant to post in case it was a secret -- nothing game breaking here but possibly unintended.

It would appear that summoned items, such as food/water, bandages, etc. can be stored in the Guildbank and remain there, even through instance shutdown. Of particular interest are that pet weapons, such as muzzle, girdle, fireblade, etc. also remain too.

While this doesn't affect large guilds where the mages summon up stuff based on need; it can be handy (moreso if the mage or you are a designated guild banker) for smaller guilds or ones where mages are scarce, because the mage can whip up a bunch of pet toys which can be deposited in the guildbank where they won't poof. Or if you get two pet packs but 'lil boner didn't die today -- and you don't feel like giving your extra pack to n00bie_00 in PoK, you can deposit your spare set in the bank (this is where it's handy to be guild banker status, so that you don't have to ask someone else to flag the items for you). Note though, that you can't put the entire pack into the guildbank, each item must be taken out of the pack and deposited separately.

Disclaimer: although this has been tested and confirmed, it may be changeable at any time without a patch (although the guildbank is technically separate from the guildhall instance but can only be accessed from the guildhall).
