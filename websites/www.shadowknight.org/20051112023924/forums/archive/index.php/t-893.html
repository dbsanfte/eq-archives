Sorry but even for soloing mana still shouldn't be your first priority. Do you know how little mana you use when you solo? When you get deadly temptation or the CoB proccing sword from yelinak you will NEVER run out of mana. You can use dooming darkness and panic the dead and never run out of mana against undead. For live stuff use BE Gaunts and invoke fear and you won't lose mana. This is WITHOUT AA. Even without those mana regen spells or sword I use almost NO mana. The more ATK, Haste, AC, and Regen you have the less mana you have to use. With a fungi, I'll tank the last few rounds of combat. Hp's regen back next fear kite. More ATK = faster kills = less mana used. And truthfully are u really gonna solo ALL the time? How do you expect to get that kael gear? You can farm armor in kael with 1 group. And guess what? lotta times you are gonna be the FD Puller AND the best tank there. Guess what you need for FD pulling? HP's and AC.

If you REALLY wanna be mana conservative go for BE boots. 6second cast time of fear. Pull with darkness run back, do fear from max range, thats totally manaless fear kiting.

With FT items, Deathly Temptation, Yelinak Sword, or AA skills your mana regen will be off the hook, considering for soloing we use DoTS. We're not about total mana pool. Very subtle difference. Aim for mana regen not for mana pool. Go Solo in Burning woods at your level, use blood ember gauntlets and undead fear (which one depends on your level), it used to take me about 1/3 of a bubble of mana to kill a mob. If it takes you 1/4 thats grea but guess what, you kill more mobs but you also med longer. It takes me less time to kill 1 mob, i don't kill as many mobs, but then again, I don't have to med as long.
