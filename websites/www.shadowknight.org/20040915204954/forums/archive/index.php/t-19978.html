Critical Affliction - This ability grants damage-over-time spells a chance to have a critical effect each tick they are active.

I ran a parse today to get the data on this AA. A critical tick is dmg of normal tick x2. I used Festering Darkness for the test, which does 50 dmg per tick (100 on a crit.) This is done with all 3 levels of this AA. The cost was as such: 5/10/15.

Number of ticks of Festering Darkness total: 1325
Number of 50 dmg ticks: 1204 (90.9%)
Number of 100 dmg ticks: 121 (9.1%)

Granted, this parse could use a larger data sample. But from what I see now, it looks to be a 9% chance to crit at level 3. I did a shorter parse on the beta with just CA1 and got 8 crit ticks out of 437 (~1.8%.) Knowing all that, I'd guess that the % to crit per level is 2/?/9. Probably 2/5/9.

One of the things I found out with this parse is that crits interact with focuses differently than with SCF:

[Wed Feb 11 14:26:16 2004] A Shissar Stalker has taken 130 damage from your Festering Darkness.
[Wed Feb 11 14:26:17 2004] Your Symbol of the Planemasters feels alive with power.

That's a 30% focus adding the 30% bonus on the accumalitive damage after the critical. If it were following the same rules as SCF for example, the max crit would only be 115.

SK specific info

Our bread and butter DoT is Blood of Hate. Not worth it to be using anything else on raids. It does 160 dmg per tick.

Here's the dps increases for it before and after CA3:

Unfocused: 26.7 dps --> 29.1 dps (+2.4 dps)
Burning Affliction 4: 29.4 dps --> 32.1 dps (+2.7 dps)
Vengeance of Time: 30.1 dps --> 32.8 dps (+2.7 dps)
Vengeance of Eternity: 30.8 dps --> 33.6 dps (+2.8 dps)
