Jovec
SKs are the DPS tank.

The above comes into play quite often when tanking issues between warriors, paladins, and shadowknights are discussed(for example, this thread (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=7633)). Generally speaking, the SK position is that War DPS is greater than SK DPS currently is, while the War position is that SK DPS is greater. Those TSW DPS parses are still fairly vaild, as least as the melee ratio is concerned between Warriors and SKs...a ratio that showed that SK melee did 80% of Warrior melee DPS. The variables those parses don't take into account are spell/pet DPS, and the DPS spell versus Aggro spell tradeoff that needs to be made when tanking. I think it would be extremely useful to have recent, accurate parses of the DPS differences, both when tanking and when not tanking, which can be used to rebuke the "SKs are the DPS tank" arguement (or possibly prove it true).

These parses are something I would do myself if I could, but I don't have the gear or the weight that some of the visible end game SKs like Brael have in the EQ/Dev comminity.

-Jovec

I'm saying that there is a significant number of warriors and paladins who are of the opinion that since they believe SKs do the most damage they should be the worst tanks.
