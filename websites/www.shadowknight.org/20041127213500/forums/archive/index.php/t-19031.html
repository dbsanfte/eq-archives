Lizarde
If you're after fast-track exp, I'd head to Sol A at 25 or so, especially since you've both got fungis. The exp there is lightning fast until your low-40s if you decide to go from killing goblins to killing gnomes. You'll ruin your Ak'anon faction, for what it's worth, but the exp is extremely fast.

From your low 40s to level 50 or 51, head to the City of Mist. The mobs will be dark blue until 49 or 50, and they have both low DPS (max hit ~120 for entrance mobs, often less) and low HPs (around 1200 to 1600 for the entrance mobs, a joke). With your pet and the SK pet and haste for everyone, you'll be killing very fast. Plus, your shaman gets his pages for his epic quest. You can get a decent wrist and waist items for your SK from the Ravenous War Boned Skeleton, and a MoSS from the Mighty Golem, both rare-ish spawns that are only slightly tougher than the average entrance mob.

After that, get LDoN groups, the adventure points aren't so bad at 51 (11 to 13 per win I think) and the exp and coin are okay. You have a good start for a group with the SK/shaman combo. Build up your points and you can start looking for armor upgrades from the merchants, along with any augments or items that you manage to get in the dungeons themselves.
