The Ghost is somewhere at the Undead Tower. I realize the before post said he was at #4, but I beg to differ. Of the last five times I have gone after him since the Hole revamped, only twice has he been there. Once he was in the East courtyard, once in the undertower accesed by the West courtyard, and once he was almost on the top floor of the tower.

Each and everytime so far the mummy did not auto aggro from the "basement" and we had to go pull him.

No.


Little secret: Paladins and Clerics using DB and Knights and Necroes using Harmshield can jump to number 7 on the map above by fallling into the Hole immediately after acitivating their DB. From there a CoH mage's dueled no XP loss corpse can be summoned from the Hole entrance and the rest of the raid CoHed down. Saves you a lot of time especially if you have a bigger raid going for Kirn and the Ghost at the same time.

Doing it this way allows you to have to fight only 8 mobs to get to the Tower.
