Oberyn
...there is very little in GoD for the soloer. Theres a few places where you can solo for decent xp, but due to the nature of GoD mobs, its always risky. They hit hard. A couple stuns or an LD guarantee trouble. I have found PooP or Ykesha of far more value to the soloist, and I do a fair amount of soloing during the week.

BUT, If you have a good regular group, there is a lot to make GoD worthwhile, even just touching on Qinimi, Barindu, Riwwi, and Ferubi. Augments, some nice weapons, and the odd very nice piece of armor. Not to mention a replacement for the Fungi tunic with no negative stats and lots of HPs/stats. Overall SK itemization is spotty, but theres a few high points here and there.

Plus (always assuming a competent group) GoD is FUN. Mobs are nasty, theres the lure of some nice drops, theres some good xp when you start mowing down the mobs quickly... its a challenge. Tanking there as an SK is spooky though:) Thus the fun factor. Also, IMHO the mob graphics are the best part of GoD, theres some creative ones:)

So for the soloist, not much, for the person with a group of friends, a challenge, is my take *shrug*
