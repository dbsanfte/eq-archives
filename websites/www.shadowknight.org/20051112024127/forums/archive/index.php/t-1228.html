Spectral Courier in CoM
Dark Blue at 45, highest mob was around 42ish
I got lucky at 45th and went with a guild monkie that was 51 that needed 1 page to drop. The path to the stables was clear cause he had been pulling it all day long with a 52 Shaman and a 40? warrior.
After 3 hours, I had 2 scrolls and the Monk and I both needed the same 3rd scroll. It finally dropped 4 spawns later, another 2 hours, and he got it.
A week later a RL bud and I went to CoM to try to get his pieces for the Shaman epic, and hopefully my last CoM scroll. The guild monkie was working on AA points at the entry and helped us with the Stables. 5 hours later, the Shaman had his epic drops and I had the last CoM scroll.

Mistmoore Librarian and the Assistant
Both were Light Blue (teal)... high 20s/30ish
Remember Mistmoore has 2 spawn cycles.
Day = Living (invis will get you everywhere)
Night = Undead and Living mix (can't get too far with just one)
So travel in the day hours in-game.

The assistant is in a room with a red conopy over the bed near the throne room. The PH / Assist spawns directly in front of it and a Gargoyle spawns in the right corner as you enter.
At 40ish, I went in invis and smacked the PH, started my timer and took out the Gargoyle. The mob in the adjacent room agroed and I had to FD for 11 minutes while it stood over me. It left and I hid/med/healed for the next 10 minutes (yep, 25-26 minute timer) and the Assist popped 2 cycles later.

The Librarian is in the Library, upper tier. 2 other mobs spawn there on the lower level as you enter. The room is incredably small. Be sure to pull from the upper landing, to the right of the door as you enter or you will get the 3 mobs from the adjacent room. If the 3 mobs are casters, wait until the night/day cycles and hope you get all melees. :)
I took out the PH and the Lib. spawned 25ish minutes later.
I had a 49 druid with me and only lived cause of him. :)

Here is another solution you can try.... I hope you live ;)
This requires SoW or Wolf Form, and regen....
1) Shout to the zone you are pulling out the castle main floor to the zone. ;)
2) Tag the PH/ Lib. of your choice and run to the top of the ramp near the zone and look down the ramp... KEEP THE PH TARGETED
3) When your train arrives at the lake, cast FD
4) The mobs will all hang out for a few minutes, maybe 10... this is why you want a regen up so you can heal more while feigned
5) When they thin out, or the PH starts to move, stand up and cast Darkness on it
6) FD again
7) The rest will wander back before the PH and you can kill it at your leisure

This isn't fool proof, but worked when I got the Lute off the Gypsy for the Bard quest armor when I helped someone get it.
