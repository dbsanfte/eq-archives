The snakes you'll be mez-tanking are immune to snare/darkness. As stated already, the key is to hold aggro and not kill the mob because it will only respawn and slaughter any unsuspecting guildie nearby.

My method is the following: Equip my Ashenbone Shield and my Journeyman's Walking Stick (extra AC/HP from shield helps, and I get enough damage from the JWStick along with the proc for aggro, but not enough to cause a problem). I then gain initial aggro with Darkness (which is resisted, but aggro is still gained). As the snake comes to me, I'll AC tap and start to position. The mob will then be slowed and then I get comfortable for a long fight.

Continue to keep aggro after the Blood and Emp are dead and loot is being done. Your crowdcontrol/lockdown snake should never die. If you win loot, keep aggro and go loot - the snake will stay with you. At some point, the Raid Leader will call the raid complete, all crowdcontrol/lockdown tanks should position their mobs near each other. The rest of the raid will join you and then an AE TL will be cast by a Wizzy.

Spells to use: AC tap, lifetaps, Lifetap over Time, Darkness or some other aggro boost.
