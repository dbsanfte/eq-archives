Firstly as far as the flagging goes, to enter KodTaz everyone needs to have completed both mountain trials (vxed/tipt) and have either Smith Rondo flag or all 4 sewer flags. For sewer and mountain trials only the expedition leader needs to be flagged for the specific expedition requested - so totally unflagged peeps can be added to do vxed or tipt for example. For progression to KodTaz and beyond you will obviously need to flag everyone eventually.

As to whether you should continue on through Vxed and Tipt I would say definately yes. The yellow mobs hit for no more than the blue mobs in my experience and if you have 2 competent healers with plenty of mana you should do fine. I box Vxed a fair bit to gear alts and use a bard to pull so its easy to always get singles, and I ensure they are slowed on incoming (bard slow is better than nothing if you don't have an enchanter for insane pet dps). Speaking of enchanters you don't mention if you have one available - if you do I would say Vxed is a done deal for you, and Tipt also, though both can be done without - just substantially more of a grind. For flagging in Vxed if you can get a bard in you really never need to leave the zone in - I flagged numerous characters by killing at the zone-in until the npc popped. Then levi and invis, and throw up bard speed. Run high on wall like the devil is on your ass to end room, target npc (invis usually off by now cause of aggro anyway) hail (can't remember if you say anything to him, been a while since I've done this) and zone out if you can on clicky stone - or die. FD classes can just fd at npc and then jump up for flag - have done this successfully with wizard, monk, bard, cleric, warrior.

For farming named in Vxed most have low hp and only Chavoor seems to put out mega dps (though I havent parsed them or anything, he's just the one I die to most if shit goes wrong). There are a number of upgrades you could get and prolly for the rest of your guild also. Also a rare drop earrring off trash you could get in Barindu that would be a nice upgrade for you - ac 12, 100/100 + nice stats.

How we do the flag is we try to clear to it.. we wipe out.. we do our CR's.. but we've killed enough mobs that the named is up.

We get on our unholy steeds (or bard speed drogmor's / horses for the non-knights), run from the entry making a nice train (or have bard help ya with selo's). Once you are there at the flag mob, you hit your Shiny Brass Idol that you farmed ahead of time. Bingo, 18 seconds to hail the mob and have a nice chat with her about "tend". Meanwhile a HUGE train gets to bounce off you. When you're done hailing her and talking to her about "tend", click the portal stone to exit.

Alternately, pull and kill all mobs at entry, then selos/horse, levitate, invis, train to quest mob, hit shiny brass idol, do the hail and so on, zone out with new flag. Don't even have to try to clear if you farm shiny brass idols ahead o' time.
