Josahd
focus is still applied when you crit, it's just applied to the base damage portion only. it's not base * 2 * focus, it's (base * focus) + base... or (focus + 1) * base after factoring.

Assuming you're using ID4 and Touch of Inny:
No focused, max touch of inny would be 940.
With focus, max touch of inny is (2.2) * 470 = 1034.

If focus was applied after the crit, it would be 2 * 470 * 1.2 = 1128, which you'll never see.

It basically results in focus being half as effective on spell crits than on non-crits.
