Hi All,

I have an idea for a group/raid life tap like spell/discipline which allows for a percentage of each hit we take to be mitigated and distributed to the group/raid that we are in (say 30% of the damage, mitigated by a further factor to be assigned).

I have already mentioned this in another post, but some people really disliked it saying that no guild would have an SK if he he had this ability. Whilst they may be right (I have only recently started playing a SK, my main is a mage), I am still not sure why that would be.

Assuming a raid of 70 people, each other player would receive 30%/70/mitigation factor (say 5) of the total damage from each hit, i.e. 0.08% (so if the mob hits for 1000, then each player takes 8 damage). This could be further mitigated by group regen spells. Admittedly this would stack up in the long run (either through the increased use of healing mana, or in terms of raw damage), and would not really be viable for main tanking (where even 1% of the damage could create significant problems for casters), but would give an immediate boost to the SK as an off-tank.

The aim is to provide a tanking ability (in this case a damage mitigation of 30% as far as the tank is concerned) which scales with the mob and that can make the SK a more desirable off tank in exceptional situations. Furthermore such an ability would be unique (not another stun, or stun-like ability), and would be in line with the SK ethos.
