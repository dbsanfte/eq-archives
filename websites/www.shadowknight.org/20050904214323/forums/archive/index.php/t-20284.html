Just some ideas:

As much DS as you can get, including shammy potions, on your pet too if it is targettable (I don't remember).
Dispell the hell out of him using items or Nullify.
Get fully buffed prefight, and get your pet fully buffed prefight. He won't have the same hp as you if he's buffless and you are raid buffed, and a raid buffed pet won't be so easy to kill.

Your goal will be to kite him, either by running or with shadowstep, and never really engage him in melee.

Shadowstep, dispell, shadowstep, dispell, until it seems clear he's lost his buffs, then just shadowstep, ToI until he's low enough for HT to kill him, then kill him. Meanwhile he'll either be chasing you around or trying to kill your pet, if he does attack your pet, melee him from behind. If he turns to melee you, run or SS away.

ToI will definitely land, use ToV too if it tends to land.
