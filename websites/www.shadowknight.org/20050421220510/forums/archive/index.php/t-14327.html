My two cents (which means nothing to anyone except me).

From a roleplaying point of view I believe that only an Iksar should be able to wield the Greenmist. Outside of roleplaying I do not really care. I am an Erudite and I have begun working on this quest because it is an incredible weapon that I would like to wield. I am playing my character in a manner that I believe this game was originally meant to be played. Every single item I own has either been quested for or obtained from the corpse of an enemy that I have slain. That is the style of play I prefer. That is the style of play I respect.

Having said that anyone who has completed the quest for the Greenmist deserves it. The faction work alone (which is what I am working on at the moment) is extremely tedious. Some may think it is easy. Those who do are probably not limited to two hours of play time a night like I am (work and family prevent me from playing longer).

If you own the Greenmist I congratulate you. If you earned it I respect you. However everyone pays the same amount of money to play this game so if you bought it than you deserve it just as much as the next guy. If you resent those who did not put as much work into it as you did all you are going to do is become a bitter person. I learned that myself the hard way. So now I go about playing the game the way that I like and do not begrudge those who play the game the way they like. Of course I do not understand why they like to play it that way. In my opinion it is kind of like winning at Monopoly when you allow yourself to take out loans from the bank without having to pay them back. Sure you win but you have not really accomplished anything. If that gives you a sense of satisfaction though than so be it. Far be it from me to rain on your parade.

I like to do it the hard way though. At the end of the day I like being able to look at my character and know that I have earned everything that he has. Truly earned. No farming for platinum to purchase something in the bazaar.

That is just me though. If you are different I say more power to you.
