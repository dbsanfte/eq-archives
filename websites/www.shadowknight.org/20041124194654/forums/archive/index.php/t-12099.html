Mace of Shadowed Souls (19/29 10 INT 1hb SHD only) and Clay Guardian Shield (25 AC 50 HP)never cease to amaze me. they're both perfectly good pieces of equipment, and are both less than 100p in the bazaar. Once you're upper 40's Low 50's, you can use Weighty Pole arm, which is like 29/35 10 AC 15 AGI and some other stats maybe? It's 2hs, and like the 2 items listed above, despite it's badassness, it's so common that it's rarley above 200p. As for armor, Crustation Shell is a good source of STA, while Sentient armor is good for INT, both are important stats, and both suits are cheap. Low AC however. If anyone's ever killing Dwarves in Icewell, armor drops there which I still use at 51, and it rots most of the time. So see if you can tag along. Cold Steel, from Velk's, is alos REALLY good AC, and really cheap. this is not because it's so common, but because it's so heavy. But if you don't mind the weight, Cold Steel is inexpensive and good AC (no extra stats tho) if you can find it in the bazaar.
As a SK, I did LoIO (17-27) Then FM (27-32) then random places including the guards in WC, then OT (36-45) which I solo'd alot in the 40's, then DL/KC then on. get Experience wherever you can find it, and don't stay in the same place if it's getting boring. Making the game enjoyable for yourself is better than leveling fast in a boring zone, so do whatever you like best. It IS your $12.50 a month, anyhow. And remember, it's not too late to make a Troll/Ogre/Iksar :) Enjoy your SK, hope that stuff helped

Best way to make money period, is with tradeskills. You could buy low, sell high buy why spend so much time in the bazaar?

Get your tailoring to 115, or even 88 and make 10 slot backpacks. They have a 10% weight reduction and every is always wanting them. You could also sell tailored quivers (gives haste to archery) for a decent price also.

They sell between 65 to 85pp, you can either hunt the HQ bear skins, or buy them off of newbies for 25pp or so depending on your server.
