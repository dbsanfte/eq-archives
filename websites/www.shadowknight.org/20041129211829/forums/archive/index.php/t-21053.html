Androsulach
You can also do cave basilisks in Jaggedpine Forest in the caves. not far from where the TP gem from Blackburrow will bring you. Just hang a right when you get to the gnoll fort, it's not hard to miss. You can single pull the basilisks, just watch out for the genitors, since they will aggro if you get too close. Genitors also drop eggs which sell well in the Bazaar.

Oagan
Killing in Kaesora will hurt your VS faction, if you are planning to quest the OT Hammer, which is one of the best quests for our class. At your level, you can gain decent xp killing the Sarnaks, and the Bezerkers drop the braids for the hammer. There's no reason not to be in good with the outpost by level 40, and have the hammer, unless you're a lizard SK, which will make the quest difficult.

That hammer is is still great, and saves making gate potions with my Shaman. Those puppies still cost about 160 plat to make. I just duel him in a zone to gate, or use it on a mob.

You didn't say which race, but unless you're a lizard, there's no reason not to get the xp killing the Bezerkers at your level, and get the hammer as well. Later on, you can dump the faction and invis when you land on the boat, or just train the PoK book, which always makes friends with the HE cleric or pally medding next to it.
