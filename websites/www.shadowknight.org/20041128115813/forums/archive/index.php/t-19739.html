Old Spell Clean-up

I have a couple old spell issues, that I think would be useful to fix up. Some honesltly appear to be oversights, while others are my hopefully balanced suggestions.

1. All "Aura" Spells to the short buff box.

Aura of Hate
Aura of Darkness
Aura of Pain

Should all go to the short buff box. It would make these spells more desirable on raids, where buff slots are at a premium. Not everyone can have a bard in their group and these spells are bard-like functions IMHO.

2. Aura of Pain to the group

SK is the only one that benefits from this particular aura. The other two benefit the group, it should probably all work the same in that regard.

3. Brell's + Aura of the Crusader v. Cloak of Luclin

The essentially 300 hp advantage that Paladins get built in because of their spell stacking is excessive. I think its appropriate for Paladins to have more hps OR more AC but not more hps AND more AC. The simplest fix to this would be to make Cloak of Luclin stack with Brell's. We'd still have lower hps and lower AC, but the gap would be narrowed.

If a new spell is required, then make "Aura of the Dark Crusader" a hp/ATK/mana regen buff rather than a AC/HP/mana regen buff. Same stack pattern as Aura of the Crusader and Blessing of the Nine (i.e., you can have 9+7+Symbol+Cloak of Luclin or Virtue+7+Aura of the Dark Crusader+Cloak of Luclin).

GoD Spell Issues

My only real gripe is with the new spear. Spears kinda suck. No offense. In Luclin they were good, because almost everything was vulnerable to DR spells. In PoP they suck because too many things are DR resistant.

The high recast times on these really prevent me from wanting to use them.

I'd much prefer that you provide an upgrade to Asystole, such as Cessation of Corr.

Or, an unbalanced lifetap, such as 400 DD + 800 Recourse. Obviously, you have concerns about raising our dps too high, but I have a concern that my lifetaps -- even my best lifetap -- barely moves my health bar. I've got to be able to use a lifetap to rescue me from a late heal.
