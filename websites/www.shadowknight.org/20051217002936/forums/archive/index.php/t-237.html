To consolidate this some:

Many of your spells are available from the Necro Merchants near the Arena in Cabilis. However, 4 Spells are not available: Summon Dead, Shadow Sight, Invoke Fear, and Breath of the Dead. These four spells can be purchased in Grobb or Oggok OR you can ask a friendly necro mancer to create them through research. I think Oggok would be difficult to do on your own, but Grobb can be done fairly easily. As stated, the merchants in Grobb will sell to you (indifferent) but the bashers do not like you. Use invis to get there and time your de-cloak for when the wandering guard leaves that area; purchase spells; cloak; then depart - simple. Have FD memed just in case you happen to draw the guard's attention - like I did (invis dropped before he wandered away :( ). Good Luck!!

Also, remember that Life Leech is a quest spell.
