The wine quest will only work if the Ogre is on one of three factions:

Dreadguard Outer (DE guards in Nektulos)
Dreadguard Inner (DE guards in Neriak)
Neriak Merchants (don't remember what their faction is called)

Wine quest can be done while KOS to these factions. Get your bags full of wine and an invis, and find Lothar in (I believe it is) the Third Gate. He was indiff to my IKS SK when I did the quest, and the only faction I had at the time was dreadguard outer, so I assume he would be indiff to you. He is far enough from other NPCs that you should be able to drop invis and be safe, as this is what I did.

Hand him enough red wine (8 stacks?) and you'll be ally with merchants and dubious with DG inner/outer. You will still be KOS to some Neriak factions, so invis on your way out. This allows you to bank/buy/sell in Neriak without any trouble. Dunno if this makes finding that Ogre safe, though.

And of course, if the ogre is on another faction you'll need to check which one and fix that one as well.
