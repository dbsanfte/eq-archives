Being very familiar with 2 box sk/enchanter combo myself, using a 58 Enchanter, I can give some advise.

Set up the enchanter with hotkeys for assist and tash, mez, root, debuff(I use 2), and slow - to make your life easier. Be ready on your sk to steal aggro (like we do so well) just in case.

I found the flies in PoD cake. At your level, straight up tank them, they are whimps. You won't get much exp, but the enchanter will do fine. If you can get both the front (safe rock) and back island (Verti Fly), there are plenty of flies there to keep you busy. All should come single at 64. Watch for Rats in the water. Expect them to add when you are lom. :) Do NOT pull A Diseased Malarian. They summon and are nasty casters, necro I think.

If you aren't worried about getting exp, just PL'ing the enchanter, go to Velks and try for all the spiders you can. You shoud be able to rip through them no problem.

If you want blues (to your sk) for better exp, try PoN or go to Torgiran Mines, or even Nadox. TM by the Nadox zone is my favorite spot right now. Ok exp and good cash drops.
