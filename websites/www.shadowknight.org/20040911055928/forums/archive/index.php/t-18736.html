It could be done, but you'd want to do it as such:

Change to race with xp penalty (or penalty greater than your old race): Lose the appropriate amount of total experience. Meaning if you had 100,000 experience points at level 50, and were an inkie and got a race change to iksar, you'd immediately drop to (100k/1.2)xp and likely delevel a few times.

Change to race with no xp penalty (or less of a penalty): Nothing happens.

Factions: Your factions become the worse of your current factions, and base starting factions of that race. Factions never go up.

New race can't use some of your gear: too bad.

Change races enough time and get to be KOS to everyone for free! ;)
