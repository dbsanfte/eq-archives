Hmmm....an actual roleplayer? Well I'll be damned.
As to your question, it all depends on what factions you want to lose...I.E who will eventually attack you on sight. Killing druids hurts druid factions, killing good city guards hurts that city's factions and so on. If you're never going to use the old-school druid rings, or enjoy being attacked when you port into certain places, go right ahead. Just make sure there are no quests involved with the factions you're destroying, so you dont have to go thru a tedious process of building them back up. Have fun roleplaying while you can...most of us have left it behind, except for a Mayor Gubbin kill once in a while when we feel like slaughtering halflings :D
