Lizarde
In regards to your question- as I understand it, DEX will not affect your hit rate at all, that is based on other factors, particularly your weapon skill.

DEX is said to help with increases in offensive skills. I've heard that AGI helps with defense skill-ups, but that seems to be a less accepted notion. The consensus seems to be that AGI is all but useless, just make sure it is at least 75 or you take a big AC hit at higher levels.

DEX is said to help with procs as well. Sure, 10 DEX won't make much difference, but every bit helps. If you put some starting points into DEX, I see no reason to re-roll. You're quite right that most stats are not hard to get into the 200-250 range with armor and buffs (and higher as you level up and get better gear).
