Rukgut
Making $$ - I'd say go with making and selling your own prismatic dyes. Those seem to sell quite well on many servers. Also, with a little legwork you could also make your own Grobb's liquidized meat and sell that.

Upgrades - LDoN's are your friend here. Try putting together your own group, and make sure that you're set up as DPS rather than MT for the group. At 52 you'll probably earn 38 points or so per adventure. Work on the BB adventures and for 150 points you can equip yourself with Death Embracers armor.

ex. Breastplate: AC:+40 HP:+25 MANA:+25 STR:+6 STA:+6 WIS:+6 INT:+6 FR:+5 DR:+5 Slot1:type1 Slot2:type1 Slot3:type3

Once you have a few pieces you should be in a better position to do some MT duty within your own groups.

What I'd personally do (not saying it's the best or easiest or whatnot) if I found myself without gear, be it at lvl 50 or lvl 70, is I'd head over to Eastern Wastes and first stock up on Ry`Gorr chain armour... just to get beefed up and fill a few slots to improve on my naked AC (I'm assuming zero plat available). You can fear kite a few Ry`Gorr around the fort and pick up a few random pieces of chain. Get a few bone chips beforehand so that Jabobner can help out. The pp from the orcs isn't that shabby and you might be able to pick up some cheapo gear in the Bazaar afterwards. Now if I recall, some of the orcs in the fort were DB at 60 so you might start out on roamers outside, they can still drop Ry`Gorr.
