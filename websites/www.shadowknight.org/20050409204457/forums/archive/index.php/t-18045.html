Noctix
As the others said, try pulling with cancel magic. Also, several caster classes have higher level versions of cancel magic that strip more buffs than cancel magic will (I think some strip 3-4 buffs at once), so you might ask your group members to strip off the damage shield. In my group, we always strip the damage shield off first thing.

Getting rid of the dmg shield will benefit your whole group as the dmg shield isn't just hurting you, it's hurting every melee character in your group. I dual box a SK and a rogue and those dmg shields can just eat my rogue alive given her fast dual wield and low hp.

Dakkin
Get a slow one hander with a decent ratio (I'm guessing completely at your gear and circumstances here, apologies if I'm on the wrong track), so you don't have to switch between weapons so much.

If you're both MT and puller in LDoN, it's tough to pull with cancel magic because it's really low aggro, and ideally you want that first cast to be clinging darkness or spear.

Noctix's suggestion is best though; if you can get a caster to hit the mob with nullify magic as soon as possible (has to be very early or it dispells slow) you've saved a lot of healing for the group as a whole.

If the DS is taking 1/3 of an SK's hitpoints away, then a dual wielding rogue with decent haste is in danger of killing himself on it.
