I think the best thing that SOE can do to regain some balance and to instill some new life into EQ is to introduce 5 or 10 more levels for characters.

There are just too many people all at level 65 with very little room to improve thier characters anymore. There is not that much difference between have 100 AA's and 150 AA's.

This would also help the casual / non-raiding players. As a non-raider / light raider I could think to myself...well, I can't kill these now, but with 5 more levels I bet we could single group kill mob X.

The power gamers would all charge up to 75 and have fun in that nasty GoD content (instead of them lowering the GoD content).

Also, maybe then a level 70 sk could tank as well as a level 65 warrior.

Would it make old content obsolete - no. It would make the older content the domain of the casual players and family style guilds while the power gamers would not need / want the drops from the older stuff because the are getting bigger/better drops from GoD.
