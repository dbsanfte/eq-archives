I would figure the gnome would be less constrained by faction issues than the iksar. I've not checked the starting stats for a gnome, but I know that in general they're higher int and lower melee stats than other races.

Due to high int (more mana) and easier moving around due to faction, I would say the gnome would probably make for an easier soloing SK. However, the iksar's regen and AC bonus, not to mention the 15 level advantage, are certainly not to be discarded lightly. Note I say I think the gnome would be easier to play, not necessarily better.

When it comes right down to it, it's how you play the class that will make you succeed or fail, the race of the toon is pretty much just style. The different races may go about things in a slightly different manner but they're all using the same spells, weapons, etc. There is no right or wrong choice, play the one that you like.

Personally, I'm guessing you'll stick with the gnome, because if you had a 30+ iksar and were able to play a gnome to 15, I'm thinking you like the gnome.
