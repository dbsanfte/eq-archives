My main is a 61 Ranger, SoN and SoT have no DS component...

Rangers have 4 spell lines with a DS component:

1) Druid type DS - Level 62:
24 point DS (does not stack with druid/mage DS)

2) Call of Rathe - Level 62:
34 AC, 10 point DS (does not stack with Force of Nature, 8/9/10th rings effects or shammy potions)

3) Force of Nature - Level 49:
15 ATK, 3 Regen, 3 point DS (does not stack with Call of Rathe, 8/9/10th rings or shammy potions)

4) Bladecoat (self only spell) - Level 63:
37 AC, 6 point DS (Stacks with all other DS spels and potions)


So Ranger spells really wouldnt help you max your damage shield because 1) Druids allways have a higher level pure DS spell 2) Shammy potion overwrites CoR and FoN 3) Bladecoat is a self only spell.

You could pick up an item with the Bladecoat line as a right click effect. I believe there are an item or 2 (from Grieg and Tunare) with Spkecoat on them, which is a 4 point DS that should stack will all other DS.
