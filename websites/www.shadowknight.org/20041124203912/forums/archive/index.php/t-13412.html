Lejina Bloodbath
Unwanted agro is bad on raids.

A SK casting 2 spears, Blood of Hate and Touch of Innoruuk is maximizing his dps but also has to deal with a good load of agro.

Spell Casting Subtlety makes it easier to control your agro on raids when being in a damage dealing role.

Does this ability is "dangerous" to the SK training this?
Hardly.

Currently the SK have the ability to taunt much more than what is needed. Unless you are being very cheap when it comes to cast agro spells when tanking, you would hardly notice a change once you train this when you are trying to hold agro.

Im not saying this ability is great and thrilling or anything, but its far from being useless.


Does an affliction haste or quick damage ability be of any use to the SK class? No.
At lvl 65 we are naturally spell hasted by 45% on all detrimental spells. The haste cap is at 50%. Virtually all our spells are detrimental (at least, those we cast more often then once every hour), so a SK has really no need for any kind of item, spell or ability that enhance our spell haste.
