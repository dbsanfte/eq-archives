Nocifer Deathblade
Hey dark brothers,

Right now, we all know that aaxps, drop rates, power level of 2h weapons are overly weighted against 1h/shield option for knights and I am 1h/shield knight all way due to RP reason and still use the combo.

Last summer, VI was working on upping bash damage and block ability. Increased bash damage went thru that used n dmg/60 delay where n is AC of the shield up to 25 max. 25/60 is the max you can go. That was a significant upgrade.

Unfortunately, block ability didn't go thru cuz VI didn't like what they saw on test server and shot it down.

Basic idea of 1h/shield over 2h is:
1. More damage mitigation.
2. Block ability of incoming blows
3. Reduced offense power
4. Best defense against missiles

Right now, only #3 is true for most cases. #1 is reversed. You have higher damage intake by using 1h/shield combo (see below). #2 and #4 are non-existent.

Often, 2h offers good AC bonus/stats attached to it that could be better than 1h/shield combo.

Sad but true, You get more damage done to yourself by using 1h/shield if you are tanking over 2h especially in POP! That is disgusting.. Know why? You will get more repostes in same time frame over slower 2h would and you ended up losing health quicker than using 2h. For example, 1h of 20 delay vs 2h of 40 delay. That 1h of 20 delay will get repostes from mob twice over what 2h would receive. Ripostes of 600 hps 2x more against 1h/shield knight hurts. Warriors solved that somewhat by having tactical combat aaxp to ignore some defensive power of mobs.

Common sense shows that you should have more defensive benefit by using 1h/shield. Right now, you get better defensive benefits by using 2h while enjoying good offensive power of that same type!

For some reason, VI shot down block ability. I thought up of one great solution that should not affect game in any way while upping defensive power of shield in certain situation such as tanking only.

I propose that wielding shield should grant BLOCK ability only against ripostes of mobs significantly! VI tested block against all type of attacks that can be overpowering. Blocking only against ripostes should be best solution and I strongly believe that VI would agree with that. It should solve the glaring drawback of using 1h/shield. My goal is to use 1h/shield exclusively by tanking so you can enjoy better defensive benefit and reduce damage intake over 2h in exchange for reduced dps which was supposed to be. That way, knights can have more viable choices..

I am just sick of getting more ripostes by using 1h/shield over 2h knights and I still refused to have 2h cuz of RP. I don't even have 2h in my arsenals cuz I, as raidleader, kept giving 2h weapons to all other knights first before me.

That's all about my proposal. :) If you all like, I could send it to VI..
