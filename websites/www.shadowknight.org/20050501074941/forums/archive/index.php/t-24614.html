yeah, that is a great place - rarely anyone ever goes there unless they're passing through. There are a couple other good places to set up camp for nice, long kite paths with minimal chance of adds. If you don't like the little dip, then check out the far wall almost straight out from the steps, slightly to the left - follow the ridge to a flat, triangular spot at the stone wall. There is a drog somewhat nearby but you'll never agro it unless you're really sloppy. The other spot is nearly identical. As you face the steps, turn sharply left and run parallel to the building until you hit the stone wall. You'll find another triangular flat area for your camp around there. This is a great place to practice different methods of kiting, and eventually, with a level or two, you'll be able to start working your way along the long runway at the top of the steps and be able to mess with the luggalds up there. Just keep that snare up - I always just counted the ticks.
