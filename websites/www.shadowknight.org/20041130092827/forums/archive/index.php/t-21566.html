kiztent
It depends on where you are going. The shopkeeper that drops the belt is pretty much perma camped by a 65 necro, so good luck with that camp.

The goblins can be tanked by a 65 shaman pet, I expect you'd do ok there with 1 or 2 other people (just watch for gaters).

The next set of camps is... lord sasil and champion someone or other. You should be ok tanking there, but none of the drops are useful for you (and probably don't sell for much cash).

The next set of camps is the rotting peasants, and the kylong legs drop there, which would be an upgrade. You could do that with a full group, but it might be a bit dicey.

Past that is the golems (no loot) which I seem to think are about 55 and hit for 250ish, then the shopkeepers (soulscream belt) which are perma camped then highborns (kylong darkmail gauntlets) who tore the crap out of me at 64 but became doable once I got 65 and started filling in CA/CS/PE and bumped my AC/HP over 1250 and 4000 (duo with a shaman).

Oagan
I 2-3 box here quite a bit (all the way to Synthrax). If you're the tank, looking at your magelo, you won't be able to handle the Highborns. They will rape you. Not a slam, just where you're at for the time being. Some hit in the 400's, and at your level, you're going to get feared, and that's going to mean trouble with 61-63 adds.

I'll try to find the link for the map (.jpg) that I use. There are some upgrades available here. Toughest mob outside Synthrax is the Highborn Crusader...fairly nasty mob damage-wise, and the only one that consistently fears me (which can be a bit scary when you're surrounded by 61-63 mobs).

I've done the highborns quite a bit, but no drops on the gloves yet. I have got the shopkeeper a few times, as it's not as heavily camped on TM.

I would say if you have a regular group of your level, you should look at some spots in Droga for farming for plat, and possibly some upgrades. No mob sees invis. They don't fear, and at most you are getting hit for 200. A bit less hp's as well. Plat drops are 10-20pp/mob on avg. There is some decent stuff, and possibly some upgrades for you here or member of your group. The named seem to pop a little more consistently here, too. At the very least, you'll be able to farm some drops for bazaar plat.

At your level/gear, I'd say you have a bit to go before you could successfully handle Veksar. It's tougher than Droga.

Oagan
It's not complicated to get through that tunnel. SK's have breath of the dead (requires fish scale) and bobbing corpse, so we don't really need water breathing items. When you come out of the tunnel, there will be one undead mob in the room with you. There will be 4-5 in the next room. The ph'er for the Feral Lord is the animist. This is a nice camp to try your skills and drop is the plate legs ( Kylong Shinguards - 39 AC, and will be a good upgrade from your crusty).

Mobs all summon. Get your magic resistances up over 100, or you're going to get feared a lot. That can be a problem, and result in a death. I believe Veksar is considered a city. So you can bind at the zone-in area, when you come out of the water from LoIO. That will avoid a long run back, in the event of a death.

Eggborn
Around your level, or it might have been later come to think of it, when I couldn't get a group I solo'd the entrance mobs.

Cast a pet, memorize your baby fear. The first few are a pain to break, but after you can single pull them you are good.

Snare the mob, send in pet if you hold aggro. Dot when you can. When the mob gets to you fear kite it to where the room opens up. Then /pet back off, run back to the wall at the bottom of the stairs till fear wears off. Repeat till dead.

I got about 4 to 5 percent AA per kill there till around 58 or 59, and a few nice drops from the named that spawns.

Mostly people wont train you, cause it's all sk's and necros in the zone who fd or die first. If you see other classes in there, watch your butt and hide in a corner or zone right away, or the golems they will bring fast will just mow you down.
