i just did some hanging around the uticle as a level 12 troll shaman with a level 10 necro partner, we handled it fine, both a little twinky though
your wood elf friend might find nek forest a little hot at level 9, make sure you keep an eye out for the guards, they will not be well-disposed to him, on the other hand it's a nice challenge
have him bound in west freeport and you should check for guards wandering near zone-in for him, go immediately left along wall and follow zone wall round, carefully avoiding sundry nasties (orcs, shadowmen, undead,etc), the piranhas in the river are nasty watch out for them when you cross, keep heading north until you hit the uticle
there is the lavastorm zone nearby but it is guarded, also it occurs to me the inkies at the fire might chase your friend too
anyway i really like the new nek forest, lots of variety and things to do, best of luck
