In the commonlands you can get a drop of "Bootstrutter's Field Guide to the Commonlands" read it and accept the quest. The quest has you run around to many places in the commons (14 in total) and then take the boot to the academy of aracne sciences in NFP. Reward is listed below.

I was able to do this at level 11 without dying but I had previous knowledge of exactly where to go from beta. I almost died 5 times so be careful if you aren't sure if you can make it and dont want to leave a shard in some retarded place wait til level 15. Quest cons red at 11.

EDIT: Start at the red X at the purple #2 Graveyard marker!
