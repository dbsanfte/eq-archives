Morganti
The primary tank classes in EQ have each had their advantages and disadvantages in the past. I believe the current problems associated with Shadowknight tanking is due to improper scaling of our advantages in comparison to Paladin/Warrior.

It is unclear to me when this problem really kicks in, perhaps elementals + unless the SK in question is equipped in gear surpassing the content they are tanking...but lets have a brief review of how things USED to be.

Warrior: Mediocre DPS, best mitigation, best hit points, no snap aggro.

Paladin: Less DPS than warrior, snap aggro and mitigation via stun, moderate healing ability for self and group.

Shadowknight: Slightly better DPS than the above, capacity for pulling with no CC present, self sustaining lifetaps which could be quite effective at reducing burden on healers against slowed mobs.

This is of course a highly simplified list.

Currently, we have warriors who have aggro on command, and I believe this is a good thing. We have paladins who can stun up to what, level 68? I believe this is also a good thing.

Shadow Knights, however, have no further progression besides the tools they had to begin with...our strength was our better dps combined with lifetaps...
To simplify again: The mob dies a bit faster, we can sustain ourselves fairly decent, thus we have a balanced trade off.

I believe with a substantial lifetap upgrade this could help our survivability in higher end content. Now, I am not thinking say, huge DPS upgrade here, just a slight boost...however I think the recourse of lifetaps should be larger, think theft of life AA on a greater scale...

For example a 750 point lifetap, which has a base recourse of 1250 health gained, which theft of life could also affect...and of course the super rare crit-lifetap-TOL.

Now I have not given any thought to the actual numbers we should look for in this spell/ability line, I just used the above numbers as example.

Perhaps another Theft of Life AA line (Advanced Theft of Life) which could triple the healing recourse instead of double?

Creative implementation of this concept would be steps towards restoring the high points of having a SK tank.

On a side note, while I understand many SKs do not like the feeding mana concept, I do wish Zevfeers and Mental Corruption line would stand a chance to gain double mana back also...heck make it another AA, Theft of Mind.

The damage for Zevfeers and Aura of Hate/Pain should probably be bumped up a few notches as well...its a huge trade out to keep buff slots open in some cases.

In my mind this is all in a Beta stage of thought, lets hear your thoughts!
