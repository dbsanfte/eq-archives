Paradox
Wait until 7:00pm game time when the Dragoons leave the Outpost to slaughter mobs and/or players. Darkness pull Rottgrime (he WILL resist it, but it's the aggro timer you want), marines will add, FD when Rottgrime is close (you'll get hit several times), wait for marines to leave...

Here's the tricky part, but with practice, it works (did for me):

With Rottgrime solo, set up a hotkey for sit/stand and one for hide. Put a braid on your cursor, quickly hit the stand hotkey, then initiate trade with Rottgrime with one braid, then hit the hide hotkey. If all goes well, you'll be standing in front of him, hidden with a trade window open. If he attacks you, hide did not work, hit escape and FD again and retry the hotkey combo. If he did not aggro you, put three more braids in, click give. You get faction. FD, try process again.

It's imperative that you wait until 7:00pm or later game time. If the Dragoons are still at their spawn points, they WILL aggro you off Rottgrime and will beat the hell out of you. Since you are going to get beat on a bit by Rottgrime during the method I described, get some HP buffs before attempting.

This does work, I've been doing it off and on for the last few weeks during free time to raise faction to finially get my hammer.
