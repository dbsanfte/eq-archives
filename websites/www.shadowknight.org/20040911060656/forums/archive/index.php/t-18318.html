How will the person get the appropriate faction with Duriek?
Covi
To MQ the hide, have the person your with and yourself bring up the trade window with Teydar with all the correct items, but DO NO HIT TRADE. Have a third person cast invis on both of you, this brings you both to indifferent faction. Tell the person with the MQ to hit trade once invised and then let you know, so you can immediately hit trade yourself.

Wholla, you just got a decrepit sheath.
