CotS does not affect Leech Curse at all. With CotS fully trained it adds 600pts to the base 900pt damage of Leech Touch, so you will be able to fire off a Disease based (with a -150 adjustment) spell called Life Curse which will do 1500pt dmg/heal.

Leech Touch is -not- affected by our Unholy Aura /disc due to it not being affected by -any- form of Improved Damage. For the first year or so after it went live LT -was- affected by Imp. Dmg items, but then an unannounced nerf went in that made this change:

06-11 11:12 Removed SHD/1 from Classes

Which of course made LT not function with Imp. Dmg. item like it had been for so long. I think that change ranks pretty high on my list of Idiotic Things the Dev's Did scale.
