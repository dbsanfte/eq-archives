Siilvir
This is not a game breaking issue, but it is something that has irritated many of us since the line's incarnation.

Terrors.

We can argue back and forth on semantics as to whether or not the line is 'broken', but the fact of the matter is that in every given situation for aggro, there are better alternatives. Specifically:

1) Non-damage snap aggro (Fear)
2) Non-damage sustained aggro (Fear and Invoke Fear)
3) Very fast snap aggro (Clinging Darkness with Twinging Pain 1)
4) Sustained aggro (Clinging Darkness and Fear)

What that leaves us with is a useless line of spells. Useless in that we always have a better solution and cannot afford to waste an additional spell gem on Terror.

My suggestion: Fix the entire Terror line to provide massive snap aggro at a distance, or replace it with another spell line entirely that is actually useful. As it is we have five spells that essentially are a waste, even after they were 'fixed'. It feels like we were sold a spell line that worked great in theory, but failed in application.

Would appreciate your comments. (And let's not turn this into a "well this one time Terror did fine for me...". It CAN work, especially at lower levels, but Fear and CD work better.)
