Heck at 55 in Katta I get a blue of AA per kill...which leads to an aa in 2 hours or so considering down time (assuming I have KEI).

So you definately haven't hit the sweet spot.

Understand that 1 AA is approximately the same exp as leveling from 51-52.
Exp gotten is determined by exp per mob, your level in relation to the mob, ZEM, group size and any penalties you might have.

Basicall, for best exp, you want to find a mob within 5 levels of you, that is soloable and is in a Zone with a descent exp modifier.

Zones with good exp modifiers seem to be more common at 60+ (though this includes areas that require you to earn access) that for 50-60.

While solo exp at 70 might be hard to find at the rate of 1AA per hour, finding a zone to solo for 1 AA in less than 3 hours will only get easier as you progress.

The sweet spot for you might be found at 60 or 65 and you might want to pause to grind a couple, but you'll only have and easier time getting AA (even solo) after you're done with dragons and stuff and return to leveling.

If you're looking to solo while only tanking, not fear kiting or anything, your choices will be much more limited, however.
