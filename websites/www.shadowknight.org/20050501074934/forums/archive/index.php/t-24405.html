Eggborn
Greig's End, 3% or so AA per kill. Personally I think this is the best for down time vs xp. DoN (Stillmoon and I think, Tirran's Delve if you don't mind a few FD's here and there.. good xp) and Fire are great zones, but for me , they drain my mana faster and I have down time. I don't seem to get down time in Griegs End so much.

Go as deep as you can. The named will keep you in tribute items, and the spell components will keep you in small cash in the bazaar.

Harbringers Spire, less xp, but good stuff to farm if you are needing some things. You can kite in the hallways, Two things spawn there, so watch for that second mob to get ya. Don't mess with the doggy names, they cast a nasty dot that lasts an hour, but you wont with it.

Otherwise see above posts, they are all good too.
You can probably kite some in KT too. The GoD tipt trial is very good xp. If you have a group that takes the full 6 hours to do it, you end up with a good bit of xp... but well that is group.

I never realy solo'd real xp, just AA's. Fire is good for a smal group or solo for real xp. I like the Ruined City of Dranik the best for group or less real xp. Find the "Bowlord" Camp. It's a bitch to get to, but simple camp, no trains, steady xp once you get started. I level'd quite a few friends from 66 to 70 there. It felt about a million times faster than WoS. Don't let anyone leave once you get a group there haha.

Muramite Proving Grounds. If you aren't the most equipped person, pull the Blind/Task camp.. Extremely easy. Someone should be able to show you how.
