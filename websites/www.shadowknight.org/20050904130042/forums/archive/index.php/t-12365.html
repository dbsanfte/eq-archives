Hide can also be used to complete some quests with faction lower than was originally intended. If indifferent is sufficient faction for the quest then Hide will let you do the quest with faction as low as dubious. Simply give the item(s) in question to the NPC, but don't finish the transaction. While the trade window is open use the Hide skill. Be sure to con the NPC to make sure the Hide was successful; if it was finish the transaction. I used this trick in Paineel to do the Kobold Molar quest at dubious faction even though you normally need apprehensive for the quest.
