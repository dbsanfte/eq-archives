You best bet for exp is get into a fast pulling PoJ group.

Try the flies in PoD. They are equivalent to PoJ mobs. Plenty for a duo to pull. They hit light, and have few hitpoints, and are pretty whimpy once slowed. This has my top vote for getting you to 60 quick just 2boxing. You could probably fear kite them with your sk, and just have the shammy back him up.

Velks should be fine for you, lots to pull and easy kills. The lack of exp here is made up for by the sheer number of kills you can do. Spiders start going light blue at 61 I think.

Droga has great loot but a lot of casters. Level ranges 55-58ish. You shouldn't have any problems here, just invis and move to where you can find a good spot.

PoN should be good for you, but depending on your tanking skill, you might need to heal a lot even with slow. The mobs hit pretty hard here.

The Veksar goblins were pretty easy for me at 58 2 boxing my enchanter. I just tapped for heals and was fine.

Torgiran Mines is another of my favorite spots, but kinda out of the way. Zone in from Nadox and set up in the hall outside the fake wall. Lots to pull and several named here. Exp isn't that great, but it's ok. More of a loot spot than anything.

In PoN, try the hobgoblins at the ledge at the east side of river.

You can get 1-2% normal Troll xp per kill if you leave shaman out of group. Even at 59, you get 1% xp per kill. They also drop diamonds and ethereal parchment which will be helpful once you get to 61.

Shaman should be able to keep up with healing with just Quiessence. Play around with offensive spells, I could get the SK xp with 90% consistency if shaman only casts one blood of saryn and epic.

Note: Getting there for the first couple of times can be tricky, especially 2-boxing. So get SOW and try with SK first. Also, if you see people crossing the river, get ready to run. Most people are unable to cross the river without training you.
