Here are just a few things I noticed, feel free to add more to it.

Three types of npc's that I saw. Goblins, Statues, Drakes there may be more but this is all I saw.

99% does not see invis, but it seems that an occassional npc has a chance of seeing invis. I died 1 time there but I scrolled up and saw that my invis didn't drop until after it attacked, and not before prompting the attack.

I haven't found any npc's that summon. So any class that can kite or root/rot will be ok, at least in the wide open areas. Mounts are NOT summonable in that zone.

Melee/spell dmg isn't so bad. I didn't encounter anything that hit for much higher than 300ish. With Virtue/CoL I only have about 8k hp and 1600ish AC, 200+aa and was able to tank 2 mobs fine before having to sit to med up. With my cleric bot I was able to tank non-stop.

I didn't notice any special attacks from the mobs or any serious spell casting. So SK+Cleric or SK+(any slower) would be fine. Although I think SK+Shaman would be unstoppable there.

I noticed some rooms with 3-5 mobs in it, and others with only 1 mob. So there are multiple places to break and hold camp relatively safely. Everything was dark blue to me at 65 and was giving me about 3% aaxp per kill solo (i didn't group with my cleric so i'd get the aaxp).

Imo I think that 65 might be a bit overpowered for that zone. I think that would be a great zone for 55+ in full group or 3person group for 60-63. 70 is WAY overpowered for most mobs there.

There is at least 1 Wayfarer quest there. I belive it's called either "overpopulation" or "population control" i forget.

ok hope this helps.

Klotar AngedeMorts
Being the slowpoke that I am, I'd never gone into Stillmoon except in an instance in a full group. Due to pathing, etc. I found that for the instances at least, Stillmoon seemed to be a harder zone than the Ascent zone, even though the devs rank Ascent as being more difficult than Stillmoon.

Anyways, I decided to try a Wayfarer's task (collect 7 meats) and headed to Stillmoon. At 70, most mobs are still db with a third or so lb. Maybe it's my unuberness showing but although I didn't find it hard soloing, neither did I find it easy. My worst pull/add fight was 3 and by the time it was over I was dispelled all to heck, lom, pet dead, etc. and figured it would be prudent to head out and rebuff.

All sorts of groups were at the ZI so I headed further in. I agree with the posters above, range is hard to ascertain at times, and FD on statics that return to spawn point seems funky. I split a single out of a room of 4 goblins; although I did not wait 2 mins to clear aggro, I was very generous in the time I allowed for the mobs to return and reset. But after a few seconds into my fight, I had all 4 mobs plus another respawn drake and I had to pull the plug.

The best task for this zone IMO is as was mentioned earlier "population control" (kill 20 stillmoon creatures) rather than collects. Plus a bonus for this population control task is that unlike collects where drops in instanced missions do not count towards your task -- killing stillmoon creatures within instanced missions DOES count towards your task. So, to speed up faction and for a little "free" xp, I'd recommend that even if you're not actively doing tasks, that you try to get that one if you can, for the reason I stated above. Not sure though if a similar mission is available at the actual faction camps or just the wayfarer camps yet.
