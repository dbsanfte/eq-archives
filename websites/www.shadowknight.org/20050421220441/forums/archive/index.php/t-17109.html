Did her with 2 L65 Druids, 65 Monk, and 65 Chanter. (I was running the druid at the time to just MQ the mirror later)

When you appear in zone, the door you need to go through isn't locked. The others still are. Use same method as always to get to the door without falling through pit in the stairs.


As you can see on the map, it's a pretty short run to Drus and from what I remember we found the mobs pretty much where they should be, It was undead all along the way. Pits in carpet spots are still the same. Pretty much don't step near carpet squares.

I had a different map than you have linked but it was also very badly marked. Thinking back to where we pulled to, I *think* she's the red dot in the East Wing that's closest to north. We set up at the corner before and monk pulled her.

I didn't exp there before the patch so couldn't give you a comparison of the difficulty with regards to pre and post patch-but as with many old world fights , you do it and go wow they were kinda gimpy. :/
