Remember folks...I can dig up the thread, but there was a lengthy discussion about class penalties and bonuses as they relate to AA/Regular exp splits.

The basic point it came down to was: If you are a RACE with a penalty, you are throwing exp away by having your bars set to ANYTHING other than 100%. Go one way or the other, even changing them kill-to-kill if needed, but under no circumstances should you split them.

Races with a BONUS (Asslings), seem to get BONUS exp from out of nowhere if they split their exp. They actually get more TOTAL exp from splitting than if they were to go 100% exp.

It has something to do with the apparent calculation of exp bonuses and penalties BEFORE it is split into AA and Regular, if it is split.

They were right when they said a long while back that AA Exp doesn't have racial penalties...it seems...but only in the strict sense. There doesn't seem to be a penalty (or bonus) if you go 100% AAExp. Split it in any way with regular exp., and the game seems to calculate the exp modifiers first.

My point? Maybe some people are seeing some discrepancies when comparing their exp because of these racial penalties and bonuses.

Or maybe they just snuck Class penalties back in on us, and we need to collect cold, hard data that shows VI how screwed up it is.
