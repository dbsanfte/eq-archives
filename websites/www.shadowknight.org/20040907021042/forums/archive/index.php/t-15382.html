I had a blast going solo to get my cell key. I read some posts here and thought it would be exciting and I went into it expecting to die.

I think one thing that was left out is GET SOW. I would recommend getting a 5 dose sow potion. This came in handy when I was FDing my way to the bank.

Also, I would recommend making a level one Rogue and making sure you know the way to the bank.

I zoned in and fd my way to the bank with little problem. The sow keeps the mobs so far behind you that you dont have to worry about them bashing you.

I did /quit about twice on the way down.

Once you get past the secret door then FD near the bottom. /quit

As for pulling, if I just got close to the chest room the middle one would charge me without the others. Casting all the spear lines, life leech, and Greenmisting him, it was not too hard a battle. VERY IMPORTANT! Do not forget to land Doomdarkness. The one time I either did not realize it did not take and when the mob got to 14% it ran and pulled like 4 golems. I FD with 1% health! Thats no lie! That was the only one I had run on me.

Once you kill the middle one, med up and then just walk along the left wall slowing. The right chest will agro you without pulling the other.

Then just med up and repeat.

Do not let it run or pull it up the stairs or you will pull the golems.

It was an easy camp except for the one runner. I got my key after 6 chests. I forgot a gate potion so I used my gate hammer from OT. That was a scary battle too!

Good luck and it can be done! Im glad I dont have to drag my whole guild down there now.

Okay, I was kind of curious about getting to the mimics so I did a little checking around...

DMF at the top of the large 'hole' up by Paineel entrance.... slowly drop down until you reach the FIRST 'lake' area, there is a dock here, make sure to stop here or you are doing the same fighting but going up instead of down...

This area is on the same level as the bank, and only about a 50 yard run. Yes Harmshield is your friend again.

Move to the back wall of this area, there is a hallway, pass through, strafe right until you hit the wall, go forward, tada the bank.

NO fighting your way down through masses of crap or /q multiple times (although once reaching the bank you're going to want to do the quit option as noted in the above postings)

But hey, look how much time and effort you have saved yourself.

I really dont see how anyone could call this a cheat, I mean, you have the DMF, you now have more direct access (they did design the zone this way), is it not a Shadow Knights perogative to use any advantages you have...

Try it, I think you'll like it....
