neferaza
A lot of people probably feel that after picking a cool race and of course the most fun class (SK) that after several levels they picked the wrong stats. A lot of people picked STA or INT after reading this board, which are good choises. But some picked AGI, DEX, STR... or maybe even WIS or CHA when they created their char long ago and regretted it ever since. The effects of most stats often became apparrent much later, often much too late to start all over again. For some a simple primal proc capped most of their stats, for others equipment they had no clue existed when they started did the same for them.

There is already a service where Verant will change your characters name or even your server if you pay them. Why not your stats? I am sure there would be a great interest in this even if it costs a little. I am also sure that this is probably less invasive then changing someones characters name.
