I had (untill recently) a 65 sk and a 65 sham, and i played with the pets a bit.

There is a few important things to remember with shaman pets:
1 They do not dual wield without weapons
2 When summoned they have very crappy stats

Whereas the sk pets "a la natural" have much better starting positions.

I have extensively tested the various combinations etc, and I can say conclusively the sham pet will double the DPS of a sk pet when set up.

In a dual when buffed etc, the sham pet will kill the sk pet with about a 30% loss in hit points.

Importantly, when tanking mobs, the sham pet has about 3 - 4 times the tanking ability of a sk pet.

Off the shelf sham pets have very low ac, but buff them a bit and they really do get a lot tougher.
