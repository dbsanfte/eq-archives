Cloak of Luclin is seriously broken compared to Pally spells. Brells Stalwart Shield and even the Rangers Strength of Tunare are group spells, CoL is self only. I can live with that even though it makes no sense. The problem comes with Pallys self only spell Aura of the Crusader. AotC stacks with Virtue and BSS, giving the Pallys a significant HP bonus that we cant achieve. That gives them a significant leg up in tanking and grouping over us.

Cloak of Luclin - SK
HP: 279
AC: 18
DS: 10
Self only

BSS - Pal
HP: 330
Group

Aura of the Crusader - Pal
HP: 350
AC: 30
Mana Regen: 3/tick
Self only

So in a raid situation, a SK would have the following configuration:

KM: 910hp
Po9: 618hp 32 ac
Fo7: 544hp
CoL: 279hp 18ac

That gives the SK a 2351 hp bonus and 50 ac. Of course in most raids SKs have BSS on instead of CoL to maximize HP, so that would be 2402 hp and 32ac bonus. Now compare that to a Pally in the same raid:

Virt: 1405hp 72ac
Fo7: 544hp
BSS: 330hp
AoC: 350hp 30 ac

That makes the Pallys configuration 2629hp and 102ac. Thats a 278hp and 51ac difference over us. Thats a significant difference.

Either CoL should get a bigger hp and ac bonus as a self only buff, to put us on par with Pallys, or it should finally be made into a group spell. And can we finally make it so Call of Rathe doesnt over write CoL?!

We should really fight for this, since Pallys are also going to a have a big advantage over us in LDoN undead dungeons. We are getting left behind.

Now.

As for Aura of the Crusader, yeah this bothers me a good bit. Basically, a Paladin is going to be 200hps greater than an SK, given the same or equivalent gear.

200hps is a lot of hitpoints. You might not think that at the Time/Elemental level 200 is much, but you would be wrong.

Hps are like a sea wall. If you are fighting whimpy things, it doesn't matter whether you have 9k or 8.8k hps when fully buffed, but when you are fighting things that can drop you in 2 rounds (or worse 1 round) every single hps makes a difference as to whether you can live or not. A free 200 hps is a big advantage. Its like having an extra equipment slot and then filing that slot with a Plane of Time god loot.

This needs to get resolved. The easiest thing to do is make Cloak of Luclin stack with BSS in the same way that Aura of Crusader do.

Basically, a Paladin can have BSS and Aura of the Crusader up, but not Protection of the Nine -- so they have Hand of Virtue instead.

In the end they trade a small amount of mana regen for a large amount of AC and HPS. If they want to (and most do). We should get to make that same choice. 200hps advantage is a little unreasonable I think.
