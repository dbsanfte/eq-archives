With maxxed dex SA3 using SoC adds about 4.5 dps... however,
if you're really aiming for DPS you should be using MC instead of SoC, which lowers that a little.
that's about .25 DPS per AA.
mental clarity 1 should add about .5 DPS per AA, and MC2 should add about .25 DPS per AA.

CF1 increases your melee DPS by .5% per AA, so if you do 50 melee DPS normally, it would give you .25 DPS per AA.

SoTK & KA combo give you about a .35% increase in DPS per AA, and are the next best things after CF1.

combat AAs in the most efficient order are:
CF1, KA1, SoTK1, SoTK2, SoTK3, KA2, KA3, CF2, CF3, double riposte 1, FoTA1, FoTA2, FoTA3
after KA3 and CF2, the DPS gain has significantly dropped though.

so... all together, the ordering is SCF3, MC1, CF1, SA1, SA2 & SA3, MC2, KA1, SoTK1-3, KA2-3, CF2, other junk
