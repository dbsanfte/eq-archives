Dyrkk
Fire soloing at the tables area is probably still the best solo AA per kill, in fact more so with the new pet and additional DoT upgrades. The thing that is so annoying about soloing in Fire is that you can't use your FR based DoTs, which are pretty much our most mana efficient. But I have found that with the 68 pet and the full complement of mage summoned toys that the time per kill there (and the mana regen downtime) has gotten a hell of alot faster. You can now complete a single kite in under 6-7 minutes, for example...and I think that the first time I soloed there it used to take me 10 to 12 minutes...

It isn't something I'd want to do for more than a few kills to cap off an AA, but it is nice to go there when you need to do precisely that.

Vxed isn't really worth soloing for AA if you don't wish to make a real commitment of time getting new instances when a summoning mob repops in your path...add in a zoning crash or two and the downtime won't justify the AA gain rate. But it is fun to do a few times for variety.
