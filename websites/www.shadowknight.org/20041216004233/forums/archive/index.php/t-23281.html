Yes, unless monk available destroy the sand...

Mortar, Splinters, Cornerstone plus Plans (drop of a Girplan Mason) will complete a quest for a nice belt with (I think) 15atk for an SK. Non-hybrid casters get same belt with FT2 replacing the atk bonus...

You will not accidentally run across the Mason but invis will give you a nice opportunity to search.

Found mine by going to the end of the zone, turning right, climbing staircase, turn left up a wooden ramp, take 2nd left on ramp and the mason is in a camp of other guys who look like him, but are not him.

Wish I could remember the name of the belt, but by searching quest info in harbingers spire you would find some of the above information.

Good luck.
