Let's get a list of ideas going of spells for the new expansion in one definitive place (feel free to copy your ideas from other threads).

No problem to constructively critique ideas or give them praise, but let's not have a huge back and forth that destroys the intent of the thread. Most importantly get your creative ideas posted.

Since none of us know the power increase level 70 will bring, concepts of spells will be more important than the details, which can be fine tuned later. Some people's ideas may look over powered by today's game, and that is ok for now. The idea is to brainstorm.
