Heres the lowdown on PoGs/faction/fd; although this is all info posted in other threads.

When u zone in, the PoGs start runnin. You have less than 3 minutes from zone in till first PoG gets to u.They come in pairs. You kill your horse, then FD.

If a healer heals you while you are FD, but before you get your "enemies has forgotten you" message, that healer has just aggrod the PoGs. They will, repeat will, be on thier way towards your healer to eat them. If for some reason u still want to have your healer do this, than use a druid who can egress themselves. Egress will allow them to heal you and prevent faction/aggro from becoming an issue if they eggress b4 mob dies. PoGs regen very quickly when they arent aggrod, so no, you will never be summoned after hurting a PoG if u wait the full 2 minutes and get the message. It should be at 100% by then, hence wont summon.

Personally, I dont see why you would want to kill the PoGs. There are never that many horses up, and it only takes 45 mins to clear em all anyway; PoGs are a waste of mana and just a way to get yourself killed in this zone. Treat them as a distraction.
