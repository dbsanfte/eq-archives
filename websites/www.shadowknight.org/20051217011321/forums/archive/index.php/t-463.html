Kinda a lame question...

But if you have a choice of any class in the world to twink ya 57+ ... and aaxp and beyond... which would it be?


My top 3 are..
Shaman
Enchanter
Druid


My logic.....
Xp grinding comes down to kill rate and down time...it really doesn't matter how MUCH hp you have.. but matters more how fast you regain that hp, or how slow you lose that hp. If you're in a fight that the quantity of hp matters ... then you're probably picking a target too tough for xp grinding.


That being said..
Shaman can increase your kill rate dramatically with focus + celerity and a pet for extra damage, and epic free dot, jbb might KS ya :P. Damage mitigation can't be beat with slow, along with regrowth.. you really shouldn't have any down-time on the appropriate mobs. Also they're probably the fastest in mana downtime control with can4 + torpor + regrowth + fungi. Also with a soul defiler that extra hp regen = mana regen.

Enchanters can increase your haste even more.. and with C3 is a lot of free sustained damage from sk spells. Yesterday in velks, I was paired with a UBER 60 isk monk, but I had C3 on, he had aego on... and I out tanked him because I was regaining hp faster due to taps, vamp curse through C3. If you get one with dire charm that's a nasty pet to help out in killing faster too. They also have slow as well, and I think with slow + hp from c3 you'd have no downtime either.

Druid's damage shield at 60 is about 32 dmg per hit? that's pretty solid damage output, free DoT from epic, regrowth?, and PoG to help with mana regen, no where close to C3 though. Damage mitigation may become a factor here because mobs are going full speed at you.



What's your favorite?
