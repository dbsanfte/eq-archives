Just keep killing all the drakes on and around the hill by the southern Sphinx. Eventually Rharzar will spawn. You'll end up killing Darkwing a few times (drops item for bard epic, just ooc that he's dead and a bard will come).

Rharzar does not see invis. His pathing, if not aggro'd, will take him around the hill, but not out of that area. Get a soild group of guildies there to help you kill him, he's a little green drake, looks harmless - but he's nasty as hell.

It can be a long camp, I spent several hours each day over several days trying to get it to spawn. Finially, it did and I had 3 guildies show up (shaman, monk, necro - all 60+) and the 4 of us barely lived (mainly because we weren't buffed).

Hope this helps - have fun!
