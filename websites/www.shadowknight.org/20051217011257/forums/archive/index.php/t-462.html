Well, pet pulling is what the name says, pulling with a pet :)
It's very useful if you want to agro something, and not be on the Line of Sight of the mob.
It also allows to be far ahead of the mob when it aggroes you.
It's also good to eat Harm Touch(es).
And last but not least, you can buff up your pet with aego focus and DS, send it on a mob, pull another, bring yours to group, then FD. It's called Pet splitting and it's a very secret technique ! *grin*

Hope this helps :)
