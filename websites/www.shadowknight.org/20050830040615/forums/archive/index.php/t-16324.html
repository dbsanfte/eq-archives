I have to agree and disagree with Jabbuk. Killing Sarnak's in OT is good for the war braids so you can get in the outpost for the hammer, BUT this can be done later. I am a DE SK, I got from really bad Cab faction to starting the quests in a week, here's how:

1. Kill the Sarnak's in the Sarnak fort in Lake of Ill Omen. I was 34 when I did this, I would have gotten stomped so I brought along my friend who was a 53 Pally. We were grouped so I wasn't getting XP, but faction came quickly. In order to complete the Greenmist you need the mirror from Howling Stones...to get that you need the key...to get that you'll need the Jade Chodakai prod from Chancellor Of Di`Zok who happens to be in the Sarnak fort. The Weaponmaster who drops the hammer of 1406 (also needed for Greenmist) hangs out in this fort too, so you're killing three birds with one stone, so to speak. After clearing the Sarnak fort about 6 times, including the chancellor and weaponmaster, my faction with the Guard who you turn the knifes in to (on Legion of Cab faction) was at dubious.

2. Turn in ~100 explorer knifes to that lone Guard in Lake of Ill Omen. This got Legion of Cabilis to Amiable.

3. Head to Kurns and buy bone chips off the newbies there for 2 - 5pp a stack, massive genocide of skeletons. 2500 chips later my Crusader faction was at dubious. A note on the crusaders, there's a crusader named crusader d-something who's faction is one higher than the other crusaders. When you con him he'll show as dubious when the others are still threatingly...make sure you con all of them to be safe. Also, you'll want to turn in chips as fast as possible and hang towards the corner so if a crusader creeps out he won't attack you (hopefully). ;)

4. Invis yourself and run through the goblin caves from Lake of Ill Omen to zone into Warsliks Woods. You'll be in the tunnels with the outcast Iksars, kill kill kill! This is the most tedious part, getting the Brood of Ko`tiz (necro faction) up to dubious. I cleared these tunnels probably about 100 times, maybe more. I spent about 4 hours a day for 3 days.

After doing all that you should be able to start the quests. Should take a week of 4 hours a day + 10 hours on Sat & Sun. Pain in the butt, but having the newbie Iksars stare and follow you around Cabilis confused is worth it. :D

The Iksar tunnel is the tunnel you take to zone into Lake of Ill Omen.
http://www.eqatlas.com/kunark/warslikswoodsmap.html = Location B
The mobs here are level 18 or so, killed easily solo at 30. You'll need help for the Sarnak fortress, but not here.

Here's a cheesy illustration (done while on the phone at work :p) that shows the best path to clear the Iksar tunnels:
http://www.drmobius.com/images/map.jpg
Start at the green dot and follow the dark blue line all the way around back to the camp, then follow the light blue line up, around, and bafk to the camp. The mobs are static spawns except for one wonderer who you'll usually find along the light blue path. Once you kill the fist mob glance at the clock, they respawn exactly 12 minutes later. If you follow that path it should only take about 4 minutes to clear the tunnel, so camp on the green dot for the remaining 8 minutes. Soon as the first one pops kill it and follow the same path again.

A note on illusions -

Illusions will only fix your RACIAL faction. That is, if your God is hated among the race you're trying to infiltrate, then you will still be KoS.

This is why an evil Erudite can Illusion to an Iksar and infiltrate Cabilis unharmed, amiable, at that, and also why an Iksar can do the same to Paineel if he's turned into an Erudite -- They both follow Cazic-Thule.

To answer the question about the OT hammer, it's a MUST have for any SK.

The hammer is worthless as a weapon, not only because of very very shitty stats but because of the proc. When you equip the hammer and proc, you are ported to the Outpost in OT. Very useful for getting out of raids, annoying far away zones, an emergency port when you're about to die (although you better have made some big offereings to the luck gods here...), and if you are in a PoP encounter where an NPC ports you (a la PoN Maze event) and it's bugged, you can /duel a gate class caster and port yourself.

To do the quest you must be Amiable faction (if memory serves) with the Venril Sathir faction of OT outpost. Simply turn in the braids to Rottgrime to raise the faction, 4 at a time. Then, once you're high enough faction take a single Jade over to 'An Undead Foreman' by the beached ships in the Outpost and give it to him. He'll give you the hammer. You can then proceed to screw up your VS faction, just understand you'll be KoS in Outpost and have to run pretty fast when you gate.

On quests that can be MQ'd, you have everyone that has part of the quest hand in their stuff, the person to hand in the last needed part will get the reward.

Your_Friend_1: Hands in item 1 & 2
Your_Guildmate_1: Hands in item 3
Some_Guy_You_Paid_Off: Hands in item 4
You: Hand in item 5 and get the reward.

Everyone turns in the items sequentially, not at the same time.
