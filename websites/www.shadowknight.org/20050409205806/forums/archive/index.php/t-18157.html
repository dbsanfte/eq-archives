Catharsis
I just breezed over the guide, so this is probably redundant.

The fight is easy. Seriously, if you can kill anything in Ssra, this will be cake. The second the butterflies spawn, everyone gets off TT and congregates at the enchanters. Everyone except the MA must be near the lockdown. AE them down. If any straglers dont make it into the lockdown, have your knights/rangers pull them in (get used to this, this is an important role we have. positioning mobs).

Back on TT. Take note: DA/HS will protect you from the AE dispell. If you have this spell up, might as well use it. Anyways, re-MGB the important stuff (clarity, haste, virt, focus) for the lame classes that dont get invulnerability. We would assign 4 knights to kite the gargs. They pop, agro and run around (do a drive by HT on Terris =P). Bring them in one at a time after TT dies. Hail PP.

Butterflies and dispell are the only two considerations. Thats how we used to do it, handle them how you will.

Ashrai
Yeah assign a kite team to the Gargs (with an extra person in case a kiter goes down).

Precall your targets of course so that no one grabs someone else's Garg.

Tell your raid to watch their health. Low HPs brings the gargs wading into the raid.

Grab em and bring them outside, kiting back and forth, until it's time to bring yours in.

Have a 5th out there keeping an eye on things in case someone goes down so they can grab their Garg if the raid isn't ready for it yet.

Make sure your kiters get an SOE after dispell or have them make sure it's buried on slot 15 or 14 or something so they don't lose it.

Did this last week and went very smoth.

To add whats been said about the gargs, have each kiter assigned to a garg and be watching it after the AOE dispell. It will begain to move when it becomes targetable so you have a few secs to target and throw an agro spell on it if you are a kiter. We kited inside the room along the wall in a clockwise motion. If you got mana you can toss some dots onto your garg as well to help eat it down while you are kiting. (Mine was the next to last one to be pulled in and I had it down to 90% life with only ignight blood, vampiric bond and pet after TT went down)

One other pt is that the only AOE that TT does is dispell so you can use your pet, mine lived through out the entire event from zone in till TL out.
