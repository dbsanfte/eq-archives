LoganHex
Did the ring quest.

Pretty easy.

Cockatrice beaks are pretty common drops in the Overthere. Go snag yourself one.

The tiny rockhopper eye was simple too. Head to Shar Vahl and drop down into the pit area. Wander around killing the little rockhoppers until you get a tiny eye. These are pretty common too.

I heard nightmare stories about obtaining a high quality cougarskin. All of the posts that I read say they drop more frequently in the Eastern Wastes but I did not want to wander around the entire zone looking for the cougars so I went to Iceclad where there is a camp of them. Not sure if I just got lucky or not but I got the high quality skin on my sixth kill.

I went to Lower Guk for my undead froglok tongue just because I had never been and always wanted to go. No need to though. You can hit the Qeynos Aqueduct system and get it too.

Or you can just buy every item in the Bazaar. None of it is no drop.

Is it worth it? I think so. As for how much mana it actually preserves I would say about one bubble per full mana bar. No serious research done to back this up but that is what I tend to notice. Aside from that I think it is worthwhile for training spell skills. I am one of the few losers who still maxes out my spell skills as soon as I gain a new level and the Fine Antique Ring makes the process much faster.
