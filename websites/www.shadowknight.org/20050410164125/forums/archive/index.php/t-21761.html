Catharsis
Its been a while, but this is what I remember. I did most of them solo in NToV and Ssra gear. It really is very straightforward.

Ssra - You'll get a ton when your guild clears the mines for green ore. Should get more than enough to cover everyone in your guild. Otherwise, its very straight forward. Single pull, tank it till dead.

FG - Set up on one of the cave walls and pull the Royal Guards (any of the mobs near that dead end in the caves really). Bring a healer partner.

Akheva - Flop to to SW corner of the room just before (east of) the Vah'Dyn's room. There is one alien there. Kill it and set up camp. The triggered trap mobs can drop the shard, and any of the rock dudes in VD'd room. Kill the trap near the arch to VD room, then pull mobs from the room to your corner. Easy to straight up tank. Warning: the name escapes me, but theres some rock dudes in the VD lair that have about 50x more hp than the rest.

Grey - Revs, kite them or whatever. Easy.

Seru - Mine came from the gaurds right at ZI, dawnshroud side. Some people say the drop rate is better if you go up to the catwalk, but the adds would be a pain solo. Just take 15 mins to clear the ZI halls both sides while you head to another camp, eventually you'll get the drop.

DSP - easy, pick up the rot from someone farming hides.

Deep - Flop to the bridge. Actually, iirc, since the revamp the mobs on ZI side dont see invis anymore. Whatever. Horrors are easy to tank solo. Might want to bring a couple friends to make it go faster.

SD - Easy, kill the revs or pick up rot shard from exp'ers.

AC - Might need a heal partner for this. Move to the inner area. You hvae to click on a porter to take you in. Sit at this spot and kill bloodguards. The drop rate is good. To get out you need to go up some stairs and click on another porter thing. Sorry I dont have a map to reference directions.

ME - Kite gorangas until named pops. I dont remember if nameds summon. If they do, you'll need a healing partner at least. This would be much less painful with a full group because of the rarity of nameds.

Again, theres no special tricks to any of them. Just go and try them out.
