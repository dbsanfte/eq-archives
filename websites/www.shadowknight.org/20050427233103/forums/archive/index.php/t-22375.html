At the beginning of the game, go with whatever you want. Endgame, the only real difference is the fact that Ogres can't be stunned from the front, and Iksars have a slight AC advantage (compared to say a DE with the exact same gear). Mid-level gear for iksars can be hard, but once you get higher they are allowed to wear plate so it doesn't much matter.
