Originally posted by Bloodragon
What is the name of the quest armor we can't wear?

Darkforge is the quest armor that Iksar cannot use.

Iksar cannot use Blood Ember armor either, that is the Kunark class-specific armor that drops in Karnor's Castle and Sebilis.

Iksar cannot use the Umbral armor that drops in Plane of Fear, but there is an Iksar-only variant (Greenmist armor).

Outside of those (and basic plate armor), Iksar can use most other SK-specific armor sets, like Noctivigant that drops in PoG, or the Velious quest armors.
