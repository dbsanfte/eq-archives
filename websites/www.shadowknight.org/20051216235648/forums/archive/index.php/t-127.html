Granted, I am only 44th and haven't been pounding the pavement, but...
Dalnir can make you some serious cash.
Just wander around killing anything with a weapon. For the most part it is all FS, but the female erudites and the male DEs occasionally drop the Tower Guard's Blade and the Charred Thulwar, each sell with a 60 cha for 27 plat. The female gnomes drop the Sap of Piety (13/36 1HB), that sells to a merchant for 3p, and the iksar monks drop the Iksar Stave. The other nice ones are the Kly Imprecators(grrr!) that drop Bo Stick (14p) and the Robe of Dalnir, Earring of Station, etc. The one that pops the most in on the 2nd floor in the shackle room, and the Coerced Crusaders (live version is a PH in shackle room, undead version is on 3rd floor in the broken table room past the one-way door).
After you clear the ones with weapons, clear the rest. They occasionally drop emeralds(10p), golden medallions(11p), opals(?p), and up to 4p in cash.

Without really trying (I was camping the Imprecators on the 3rd floor) I made 150-200 plat a night, which isn't bad really for level 44. If you went in to sell FS and get some of the monk/bst only 2HBs, you can make a large amount of cash.

Lumpies drop the Batons that you can sell to players if you wish, but I'm not sure of the market for those anymore.

Hope this is of some good, if not to you, then to the rest of you that are in your 30s and 40s.
