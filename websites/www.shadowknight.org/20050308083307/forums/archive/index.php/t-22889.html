Valarkin
The PH's in PoH are all the same mob type...ie in tier 4 a warrior will always be the PH for Warlord, etc.

The Warlord is snareable and slowable and doubles for around 800.

There is 1 mob downstairs in his building, and 3 mobs upstairs including his spawn.

There is a roamer nearby the building which you should clear, and you can also clear the downstairs mob in the adjacent building which houses the SK named PH to give yourself room.

The upstairs room with the Warlord PH is pretty cake to break, as the stairs down break caster LoS and staggers casters from the melee, ie if you snare the Warlord or PH its not difficult to work him out of the room. The additional advantage is that even at 65 the aggro range in tier 4 hate is surprisingly small.

If even a casual SK, ie about 1400 to 1500 ac and 5 to 6k unbuffed hes easily duo'able with a shammie without issue. Just make sure while breaking your shammie doesn't creep up on you into aggro range and you get a Q or such prior to engaging and slow.

Spawns like most in EQ run in streaks but hes not exceedingly rare, about 1 in 8 or 1 in 10 from my experience in the zone while hunting for Marsinger drops some time back.

Hope this helps

Edit: Forgot to add the Paci works on all the mobs in tier 4 except the named rather handily, so it adds additional ease in breaking spawns for different group types.
