If you see the message "XYZ was slashed by flying debris" then the spell landed. If a mob fully resists the proc you won't get the message. There really isn't a way to know if the spell landed for full or partial damage though. Most magician's will cast malosinia on a mob to drop it's resists then assume all proc's land for full.
