Something else that hasn't been mentioned are the mage summoned focus items.

Although pets don't use no drop items, they do ignore the lore tag. One jewlery bag will give 2 bracers but if you want to bug mages more, ask them for a second ring and earring once you give the first ones to your pet. Total AC gain is 57 and 160hp. I'm not sure if the recommended level on the focus items affects the pets or not (with lower level pets that is) but even the lowest level pet would get some benefit from the items if the recommended level does decrease the value of the stats. Also remember that if you give your pet the 2 focus bracers, you don't need to give it the two summoned phantom plate bracers.
