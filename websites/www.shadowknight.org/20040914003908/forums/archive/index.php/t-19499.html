Short answer:

Disc = Discipline. Extra hot keyed abilities that you can activate based on specific timers. You receive your first Discipline, Resistant, at lvl 51 (I think, don't remember exactly, but it's either 51 or 52).

For a list of SK useable disciplines, check eq.crgaming.com under the Shadowknight class heading.
