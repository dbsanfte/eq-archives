Keevel
The difference between farming aa's at 61 (usually 62 is the example used) and 65...
You are fighting the same mobs at boht levels, but at the earlier level they con higher to you so you get more exp. Since AA's exp in a set amount needed, you get that amount quicker.

So yes you can farm out aa's quicker at any level (62-64) faster than you can farm the same aa's at 65.

Why isn't this the generally accepted practice then?
because: power increase from levels > power increase from aa.

Summary:
Not an awful idea to farm out a few at 64, but don't waste too much time just because you can farm an aa 3 minutes faster.
