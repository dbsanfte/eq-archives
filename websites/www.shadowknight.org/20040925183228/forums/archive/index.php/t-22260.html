unless your PC is athlon t-bird or sub 2.0ghz P4 level...I would hold off fomr buying any motherboard until the BTX format goes live.

BTX has tons of nice features, like eventually phasing out 32-bit PCI, floppy controllers, IDE, serial/printer ports, and PS/2 ports. Basically legacy-free. Instead you have usb2.0, SATA, IEEE1394, PCI-E, and a special chassis and system board design that's built around optimal cooling. Intel has the spec sheets for it somewhere on their site.
