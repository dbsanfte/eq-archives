Concerning the question about running: there are situations where running is a good idea. Generally, a few conditions have to be met: you need to be fairly close to a zoneline, you need to be able to do it without training other people, and the group has to actually run when run is called. the reason it can be a good idea to run providing the above conditions are met is that many experience groups break up after a group wipe, or, at the least, it takes quite a while to get everybody back and fighting again. It can be particularly handy to get your cleric out alive (in cases where cleric is in the group).
Once you have FD, often you will not be running in many cases. But it's important to communicate to your group: I'd have the healer be in charge of the decision, but also get everyone to agree that when run is called, everyone run. If you are near the zoneline, zoning and re-zoning takes a lot less time than dying and rezzing etc.

It sounds like you're using Mace of the Shadowed Soul, which is a good weapon, but do keep your eyes open for a 2-hander.
