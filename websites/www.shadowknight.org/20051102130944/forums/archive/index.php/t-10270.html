I play on TZ, so I can help you.

A lot of the prices have dropped since PoP, which means that most SKs on our servers no longer have to wield shitty weps like Wurmys and MoSS's till 50.

Here are a few weps to check out.

Tantor's Tusk: 50/60 50 hp 10 SV cold 2hp. A great jousting wep at 30, but pretty sorry until that level. They used to be about 24k, but now they are more like 8-10k.

Argent Protector: 42/49 10 ac 10 int 10 wis 10 str 2hs. I currently wield this and I imagine I'll have it for a while longer. the prices vary greatly, but they are about 16-25k

Black Acrylia Halbred: 40/48 4 magic dmg with an 85 lifetap proc 2hs. Don't bother with this wep till level 46, cause the proc won't work.

Worn Sha'din naginata: 37/45 awesome graphic 2hs with nice stats. These used to be about 17k, but have amazingly dropped to about the price of the Tantor's. The polished Naggy's are more expensive, but have better stats than the worn.
