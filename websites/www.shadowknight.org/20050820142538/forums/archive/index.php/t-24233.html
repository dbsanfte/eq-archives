A Simple Task:

Kill the Gardern and loot the pot

Kill 20ish or so mob (maybe less) from inside the Garden building and the Master will spawn, kill him and loot the dirt.

Pick up the ground spawn ( looks like small shroom) in the garden and combine with the dirt.

Now to play it safe and assuming you don't have a kick ass group or any form of CC just pull all the mobs outside to you instead of you moving in to them. They are easy to split anyway. Once you think you have hit the 20kill mark move your entire group(wont work unless they are all in there with you) inside to one of the halls and kill a mob. This spawns the Master and usually not on top of your group. Move your grp back outside then pull him to you. Also the doorway isn't considered INSIDE, I know trust me.

Hope that helps
