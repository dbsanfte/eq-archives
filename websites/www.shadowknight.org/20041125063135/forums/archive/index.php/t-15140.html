Some things I think are rather over due for us

Pets:

SK pets are pretty worthless until lvl 52 or so, even then they are nothing to write home about. I feel they should move up the lvl pets we get a level by one rank. There's no reason why they should be half our level, and so far behind our Necro parents when we get them. The 49 pet should be our 58 pet and at 64 we should get our own pet. Something with approx 3k hp, lvl 60, quad 61, and 100 lifetap. Not a huge upgrade from our current pet, but would be more durable and would benefit from Mage proc weapons / lifetaps on higher lvl mobs.

Noob Spells: ( already mentioned making pets better )

The power scale of an SK from low lvl to high lvls is HUGE. There are many lines that are introduced only at the high lvls, that I feel lower lvl SKs should benefit from. Spells I feel younger SKs should get are the following:

Low 20s: 15 to whole group atk buff
Low 20s: 50 hp and 25 mana group tap
Low 20s: 35 ac, 50 dd tap

Low 30s: Proc buff that gives 30 hp / 30 mana per proc

Low 40s: 30 to whole group atk buff

High 40s: 70 ac, 100 dd tap

Low 50s: Improved Pet buff

Mid 50s: 125 hp and 75 mana group tap

High End SK Spells:

While SKs are very powerful in the high end game, combining a good mix of Defensive, Offensive, and Group abilites there are a few spells / AA that I feel will add to our class without overpowering.
