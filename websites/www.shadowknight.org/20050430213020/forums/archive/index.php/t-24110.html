Croix VorAbaddon
Stop at 52. Get Run III, Innate Regen, Innate metabolism.

Level to 62. At 62, do Combat Stability, Combat Agility, Natural Durability, then Physical Enhancement, 2H bash, and Unholy Steed. At that point, you can also pick up IHT and Soul Abrasion. Soul Abrasion is also highly recommended.

Level to 65, pick up Innate Defense, Lightning Reflexes, PLanar Durability, Planar Power, and Theft of Life.

From there, up to you.

Eggborn
At level 51 I got AA's in the Great Divide at the giant fort on top of the Hill. You can fear kite them back and forth, be rdy for a wolf add every so often. Sell the weapons, decent vendor cash.

Rathe Mountains can provide some decent AA xp at 51 as well.
Both areas give you pp.

Later when you get to 52 to 57 head to Veskar and use baby fear (not your long one) and fear kite the Goblins. Once you can single pull them it is easy. FD if a train is called, cause it is hard to make it out in time.
You can also use baby fear to fear kite in Chardok at the entrance.

My favorite spot for solo regular xp at about 53 to 56 was the Burning Woods. Fear kiting at the giant fort on one side is the fastest, but the undead at the ruins are nice too. After they start getting light blue head to Skyfire Mountains. The burning woods zone in is ok, but always prefered near the overthere. When feared they path along the wall. You may wish to wait until your late 50's unless you have good mana regen and some haste.

Another favorite spot of mine for many, many levels was the Guards in Felwithe. great xp, sell the swords they drop for 20pp, you can't fear kite, have to straight up tank. You can do either zone of Felwithe. Check, I think you need to do this later.

Also worth checking is kiting the guards in Katta. Go to the edge of the graveyard once you snare them, cast fear and watch for a few adds coming from the soil.

On red servers, the conventional wisdom of levels>AA falls a bit flat. AA's are RELATIVE power gains, something that is actually of importance on PvP servers. Once you find that "sweet spot" grind location, where you enjoy killing the critters and don't feel vulnerable after the kill, work your aa's there, and kill anyone who tries to snag it from you.

My sweet spot was level 58 at katta costellum graveyard, killing guards, with deathly temptation and the new pet. At 59, some of the guards turned light blue, which ruined it, so I'd recomend snagging all your pre class aa's there at 58 before hitting 59 and getting the rest. The place is worthless at 60.
