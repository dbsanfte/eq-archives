One Terror of Discord is roughly 1.7 or 1.8 Clinging Darknesses. It is the most potent aggro spell we have, and also the most consistent (nothing else, from mob type to SCS AA's affects it). Clinging darkness is slightly more mana effecient, however. Fear's aggro depends somewhat on the target, but it is a less potent spell than either ToD or CD; the reason it was attractive in the past was aggro without a DoT component for tank mezzing. It is no longer needed in this role, however, due to the superiority of ToD.

With ToD, you don't ever really need another aggro spell (Dread Gaze aside, but that's another monster entirely.) - ToD is the most potent and is usable in all situations. CD is more efficient, and can be further improved by AA's and affliction haste, however, though neither spell is exactly taxing on mana, so the added efficiency isn't a big deal.

What nobody has mentioned are spear spells. They're obviously all but worthless for sustained aggro, but spears are certainly useful for snap aggro. They produce a decent amount of hate and with only a 0.5 second cast time can be quite useful when a mob turns on someone.

Procs are about 2/3 the value of the casted spell from what I could tell but thats nowhere near exact... that would also mean that at 15k it would max on a proc.

The rangers are due to hp scaling, as the mobs max hp goes up the amount of hate generated increases. You'll cap around 10k hp unless a proc which would put it more at 15k instead.

Clinging Darkness 1-1200 from snare component+125% of dot damage/tick as aggro (6-17 depending on crits/focus)

Festering Darkness 1-1200 from snare component+125% of dot damage/tick as aggro (62-175 depending on crits/focus)

Asystole 1-1200 from debuff components+125% of dot damage/tick

Dark Constriction 1-1200 from debuff components+125% dot damage/tick

Fear 1-1200 from fear component

Seeing a pattern here?

Spears 2/3 base DD value (no added aggro from focus/crits)

Taps 2/3 base DD value+healing aggro.

Terror of Terris 510

Terror of Thule 600

Terror of XXXX check lucy values

Terror of Discord 1800

Dreadgaze 1800

Oh, in addition to this Terror of Discord and Dreadgaze are the only two aggro spells in our arsenal which bypass the 110 initial aggro cap. When casting any of the previous spells on a mob while it's unaggro all you'll get is 110 hate, when casting one of these two you'll get the full value.
