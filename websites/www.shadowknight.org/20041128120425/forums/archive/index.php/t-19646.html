Much ado has been made by both knights and warriors about balance.

The biggest problem is that our roles seem to not be defined.
Warriors, Shadowknights, Paladins, and now perhaps even Berserkers seem to share the same overall team roles.

This is attempt to form a plan.

This is what SHOULD be different, not what is currently so.

WARRIORS

Positives:
-Offensive AND Defensive melee skills maxed.
-Most HP
-Innate Mitigation
-Damage entirely through melee (both advantage and a disadvantage)
-Should have some nice melee abilities which show skill above and beyond that of the other classes.
-The ability to use all fighting styles (1H/Shield, Dual wield, and 2H) with skill and thus be able to handle different melee situations the best.
-Master of reliability and consistent melee damage and melee mitigation.

Negatives:
-Shortest RANGE on aggro (this is to say they SHOULD be able to aggro in melee range quite well)
-Small Utility/Flexability (specialist)


PALADINS

Positives:
-Defensive melee skills, Defensive Spells
-(should be) best combat healer (speed) second only to Clerics
-most flexible defensive tank (roots, stuns, Heals)
-Highest melee damage to undead (should be overall best tank vs undead due to the positive power coursing through them.)
-(should be) Second best ranged aggro in the game
-Master of defensive shining moments (Paladins should be able to defend their groups/raids for a short time when no one else can...)

Negatives:
-Lowest overall damage output in the game for a damage dealer (except undead, where warriors should be the lowest)


SHADOWKNIGHTS

Positives:
-Defensive melee skills, Offensive Spells
-(should be) best burst damage dealer second only to mage/druid/wizard. (note this is BURST damage due to spells and abilties... not sustained damage like rogues/monks/wizards/mage)
-Most flexible offensive tank (feign, nukes, lifetaps, drains, debuffs, damage shields, LIFETAP DAMAGE SHIELDS)
-(should be) the tank that hurts the mob the MOST to hit
-(should be) Overall best at causing hate in the game period.
-Master of Offensive tanking shining moments. (beating on a shadowknight should cost the mob dearly for a time)

Negatives:
-little to no friendly buffs or group/raid affecting things without combat.
-The most dealy of Shadowknight abilities shoudl have costs above and beyond the mana cost (or exceptional mana cost)... but should be proportionately powerful for their cost. (right now they aren't)


BERSERKERS

Positives:
-Offensive melee, Moderate defensive skills
-Best burst melee damage dealer in the game bar none
-Decent sustained melee damage slightly below ranger melee output.
-Master of the endurance bar (for short bursts of melee offensive and defensive feats)
-Most overall endurance and endurance regeneration.
-Some decent utility due to melee feats.

Negatives:
-Lesser tank than knights or warriors.. better than rangers
-When Endurance is gone, is "winded" and thus mediocre in everything.


Thats the plan. Now how do we get to it?

AA's
Shadowknights are getting horribly left behind on AA's that seem to focus on our PET, and horrible copy/pastes from other classes' PoP AA lists. Where is our tanking AA's?? If mobs are going to hit for 5k, can we at least scale with the other tanks please?

AA's like the Dot-crit one are actually nice... and maybe adding Fury of Magic.. yank the pet AA's please or perhaps simply add a swarm AA of HT'ing pets... never mind adding piles of AA's to our little pets as we will see very little actual improvement.


here are so ideas for Shadowknight-Only AA's

-If we must have a Damage shield AA... then let it be this one:

Let me suggest a substitute for the SK-only AA Intense Hatred (the one that makes DS on us do more damage.)

Vampire's Spite
3 ranks
Cost 3 6 9
Requires Soul Abrasion 3

When activated, VS creates a lifetap damage shield, mitigating part of the damage done by converting it to life. This is cast much like Harmtouch in that you cannot use it while stunned or silenced, but that it "casts" instantly.

Level 1 20pts lasts 30 seconds (5 ticks, recast 60 minutes)
Level 2 40pts lasts 60 seconds (10 ticks, recast 50 minutes)
Level 3 60pts lasts 90 seconds (15 ticks, recast 40 minutes)

Too weak? mob hits you for 600, you take 60 life from him and only take 540 damage effective. Thats a 10% mitigation. If the mob hits harder its less, if the mob hits softer, you get a larger percentage.

Too powerful? Warriors mitigate 5% innately... this doesn't count defensive... which both lasts longer than VS, and mititgates more damage. All of this doesn't count the new HP increases warriors are getting and we aren't. Considering it will cost an very large amount of AA to get, it SHOULD be powerful and useful. Its also in OUR theme of doing things... the way of draining our enemies dry... while dealing damage. For fast hitting mobs that hit lighter, we would be quite useful... for mobs like Vallon in Time, (3K hits) we would not.

Stacking will be important... it should stack with all other DS, but NOT lifetap from them. (hehe bards would make this a bit too powerful -- 150pt lifetap DS might be just plain wrong.)


This is just one way.... Any way we could have a vampiric damage shield seems the way to go to me... it improves our tanking and offense all in one shot... and would be unique through ALL the classes. Make it an AA, or a spell, or a combination of the two that stack...

If you are going to give us the THIRD upgrade for an ability nearly no one buys before their 200th AA point, could it actually DO something now?
