I also posted this on the official sk forums.. came up with ideas for oow aa what do ya think?

General AA: (Everyone gets these!)
Bubble (10) - Allows you to breath underwater permanently [Requirement = Lasting Breath 2] Make those lung capacity AA's worthwhile!

Improved Convalescence (5/5/5) - Increase your Hit point regeneration by 1 per rank [Requirement = Convalesence 2] More HP regen is never bad!!!

Hasten's gift (10) - Increases your run speed by another 10% [Requirements = Swift Journey 2] Hasten the jboots guy

Power of Omens (5/5/5/5/5) - Each level increases the maximum you can raise your base statistics by 5

Armor Affinity (5/5/5) - Increases the ammount of AC you get from your equipment I'm probably wrong but I think SK's get like 1.9 x the ac on their gear? (ex. you put on a 10 ac item your ac goes up by 19) So this would raise it by .1 for each rank so instead of 19 ac from that 10ac item you would get 20, 21, and 22 etc....

Tanking AA: (WAR PAL SHD)
Enhanced Durability (5/5/5) - Increases your maximum hit points by 2% per rank [Requirement = Natural Durability3] Works just like ND.. based on sta etc..

Flowing Agility (5/5/5) - Further Increases your damage avoidance by 2% per rank [Requirement = Lightning Reflexes 5]

Fortitude (5/5/5) - Further Increases your damage mitigation by 2% per rank [Requirement = Innate Defense 5]

Innate Stun Resistance (3/6/9) - Reduces the chance of being stunned and the duration of stuns by 5%, 12%, and 25%. This ability does not affect stuns that result from spells.

Mirror Stance (3/6/9) - Gives you the innate ability to reflect spells cast at you back to the caster by 2%, 5%, and 10%

Hybrid AA: (PAL SHD RNG BST)
Battle Channeling (3/6/9/12/15) - Allows to melee while casting a spell. The first level at 5% accuracy and damage, second level 15%, third 30%, fourth 50%, fifth 75%

Battle Focus (3/6/8) - This ability Improves your chances of channeling a spell without getting interupted by 5%, 15%, and 30% [Requirement = Channeling Focus] Hybrids only not pure casters.. it makes alot of sense since hybrids spend their whole lives casting spells while in melee combat... naturally they would be the best at it

Shield Defense (3/6/9) - Grants an innate shielding bonus when using a shield by 1%, 3%, and 7%

ShadowKnight Specific AA:
Fury of Hooves (8) - Improves your unholy steed ability, giving you a different horse that is faster. [Requirements = Unholy Steed] Would be at run speed cap.. Maybe give us skelle horse model or flaming horse. Paladins should get the same thing cept their horse could be maybe a wrulon or the flaming horse model from pofire just change the horse color to white and the flames from red to sky blue.

Improved Spell Casting Fury (3/6/9) - Further Increases your chances of Landing a Critical Blast [Requirement = Spell Casting Fury 3]

Improved Theft of Life (2/4/6) - Further increases your chances of landing an exceptional heal on lifetaps [Requirement = Theft of Life 3]

Stonewall (3/6/9/12/15) - Same as monk aa that lets you stay feigned if casted on

Bonewall (5/10/15) - Greatly increases your chance of Channeling Feign Death, Death Peace, and Comatose without getting interupted by 15%, 40%, and 75%. This ability also makes it impossible to fizzle these spells.

Dark Power (10/10) - Further enchances the damage done by your lifetap procs by 400% and 500% [Requirements = Soul Abrasion 3] SA was 300% if im not mistaken

Dual-Handed Harm Touch (8) - Puts your Improved Harm Touch and Leech touch abilities on separate timers. [Requirements = Improved Harm Touch ,Leech Touch] Also leech touch should auto fire theft of life much like IHT auto fires spell casting fury

Dark Shroud of the Living (5) - insta cast invis (this would balance sks that dont have a cos cuz its such an overpowering item)
Dark Shroud of the Dead (5) - insta cast IVU

Dark Familiar (3/6/9) - Greatly improves your pets hit points, armor class, and resists. Level 1 - 500hp 30ac 10all resist, Level 2 - 1000hp 80ac 25 all resist, Level 3 - 1500hp 150ac 50 all resists Or just something to make our pets not die in one hit or one ae
