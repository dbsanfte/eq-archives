I've been very very surprised with the experience and platinum I have been getting in ToFS. At level 53, once I get a rhythm going, I can get a blue bubble in about 40 minutes or so, which I'm guessing isn't bad exp, worst case, and at best is really good exp for my level. I camp the 6th floor area with shades. When you zone into the 6th floor, go straight ahead through the first intersection to the deadend, then head right, as if you were heading to the incoherent spirit, but instead of going right at the fork, head left for a room full of 5 shades. Once I kill all of the shades, I move towards the exit, just nearby (from the zonein, head to the deadend and head left instead of right), and there are around 7 more shades to pull. I'm not sure, but due to the <ahem> favorable pathing when mobs are feared, you might even get better exp from pulling the gnoll looking things to the zonein spot and fear kiting them (you can definitely get insanely quick exp doing this as a necro). However, I think taking advantage of said pathing is tantamount to exploiting. In addition, each shade can drop as high as 10plat, plus gp/etc, certain spells, gnomeskin, crystalized shadow, and crystalize weaponry so in a day of hunting, I racked up about 1k worth of plat (probably 85 percent in plat and gold). I hit 53rd the night before last, and even though I haven't really been taking full advantage of my weekend to put in a lot of time, I'm already half way through 53rd.

*edit*
Also forgot to mention, that if you want to just make tons of plat semi-easily, you can always farm RM for Hill Giants, who can drop 30 or more plat per giant. You can easily make tons and tons of plat doing this for a long time.
