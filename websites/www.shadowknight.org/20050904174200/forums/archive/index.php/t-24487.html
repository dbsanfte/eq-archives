Stacking your buffs right is really important too. Certain debuffs/dots will overwrite buffs so you can quickly dispell them if you have your order right. Like for example, Malos overwrites bobbing corpse and many different dots overwrite aura of battle.

The best is to put 2 crap buffs in your top slots and then proceed with your normal buffs. When you engage in pvp, click the top 2 buffs so they are open for any dots/debuffs they might cast, then dispell them right away.

Aura of darkness is a great spell to cast in pvp because all debuffs have a 50% increased effectiveness in pvp. Assuming you're on a pvp server, from what I understand all spells with recourses don't work in pvp on blue servers. I would ditch the cascading darkness and go with engulfing darkness, it's lower mana and cast time.
