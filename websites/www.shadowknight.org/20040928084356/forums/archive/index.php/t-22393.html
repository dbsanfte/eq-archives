Ok a n00b question I know but what is the difference between Knights Advantage and Speed of the Knight? One gives an additional attack w/ 2h weapons, the other gives a chance to double attack w/ 2h weapons. :confused:
Menon Lifeblight
One increases your double attack rate, while the other offers an extra attack (which means you will occasionally triple attack.)

Each rank of these abilities increases your melee dps with 2h weapons by a little less than 1%. Get the cheapest one first.
