Do you really think Warriors need high end agro weapons/augments to keep agro in fights now with their added agro abilities? They don't.

Warriors and SK at level 65 have comparable DPS, although the SK is admittedly higher, but not by that much. Not exactly a necessary role for an SK... but looking good compared to the Warrior! Woohooo!

Monks or Bards would pull - SK's are a distant 3rd (or lower) now. An SK can still do it in most places, but rarely by choice, and I sure wouldn't look for one to do the pulling. Bards and Monks have both gotten SIGNIFICANT pulling aids given to them since PoP - SK's have gotten nothing - but apparently that is not our *primary* role, so no one seems to care. Me personally - I liked pulling, and I think this is at least as lame as the Warrior changes... but I think I'm in the minority.

In Kunark we weren't totally gimped compared to Warriors for tanking in groups, because high agro classes like chanters and rogues loved having SK tanks so they could cast/damage without worry - now they can do that with Warriors too. If the SK worked on it they could be a puller for any group/raid in the Kunark days, but now this is really innefficient. SK's were starting to define the agro/offtank role in Kunark, although this wasn't widely accepted (I know my guild at the time was pro-Warrior 100% of the time in Kunark still). I think most (including me) thought Kunark was the dark days of being an SK - I got groups and leveled and had fun - but it was due to my personality and friends, not due to my role. Do you think the improved damage spells we got with PoP actually make up for the fact that we now have *EVEN LESS* than in the Kunark days to bring to a group or raid in comparison to other classes?

I don't think the question is about what exactly non-uber SK's need, it is a question of why don't they need the same things? They need to be comparable with Warriors (and Monks/Bards, but that is another topic) so a good SK can actually fill a role - not take the role outright, but fill the role without a significant drop in efficiency. I acknowledge that a 65SK *COULD* tank/off-tank in a group or on a raid - even many older expansion boss mobs - but the question is really: WHY WOULD YOU WANT THEM TO? A Warrior would do it more efficiently every time - that is not class balance, regardless of whether it *can* be done or not.
