DrMobius
There are 4 main factions inside Cabilis: Legions of Cabilis, Crusaders of Cabilis, Merchants of Cabilis, and Cabilis Residents. Then many of the classes have their own faction, but the only one you have to worry about is the Brood of Kotiz for the Necro's. Doing the dagger turn in, then bone chips, and then clearing the tunnels will raise the Legions, Crusaders, and Brood of Kotiz so you can do the Greenmist quest. I have never heard of the ring quest, to get my faction up I did the dagger quest once I was at dubious with the guard in LOIO. If the ring quest is similar to the dagger quest it only raises your faction with the Legion of Cabilis, the Crusaders will still not like you. There is one specific Crusader who patrols (Crusader D something) who always has one faction level above the rest, so if he is at dubious then all the other Crusaders are still threateningly.

I needed about 2800 bone chips to get the Crusaders to amiable, and I cleared out the tunnels with the outcast Iksars about 200 times (3 hours a day for a week).

As I said though, I've never heard of the ring quest so I may be spouting off info you already know. Hopefully you can find this useful. :)

Lastly you need to get Brood faction wich you can get from killing the outcast Iksars or making bone granite powder.
