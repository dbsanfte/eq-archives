Bane damage is added to base damage straight off against the creature type.

The other types, such as disease, are added to base damage after a resist check. Partial resist are possible so anything from 0 to 6 is added to base in your case. I did some log analysis using a weapon with 6 cold damage. On normal mobs, about 2.5 damage was added to base on the average. With malosini in, it was very close to 6 added all the time.

Okay, here's a little rudimentary testing I did with my Hoarfrost Hammer (15/20 COLD DMG+5) in Droga.

The results are based off analysis of magic number hits. so will be slightly inaccurate because there is no way to distinguish between an actual MN hit and a non MN hit based on how much of the cold damage was resisted. Example, the hammer could hit for 45 points [MN for 15+2 dmg] even when its damage was 15+1 [MN of 43].

First, MN hits for this weapon are 41, 43, 45, 47, 49 and 51. The base MN being 41 for a 15 dmg weapon, then incremements of 2 up to a max of 51 for a 20 dmg weapon.

Out of 1041 swings, here's the result.

Hits for 41: 62 (26.7%)
Hits for 43: 40 (17.2%)
Hits for 45: 31 (13.4%)
Hits for 47: 36 (15.5%)
Hits for 49: 30 (12.9%)
Hits for 51: 33 (14.2%)
Total MN hits: 232 (22.3% of total hits)

There were noticeable peaks at all of those numbers, indicating that the elemental damage is indeed added to the base dmg, not total damage. It also shows that there's a fairly high resist rate, even with green con mobs.

If the elemental damage were added to total damage and not base damage, then the MNs would be 41-46, which is clearly not the case, as demonstrated by these hits.

Hits for 41: 62
Hits for 42: 15
Hits for 43: 40
Hits for 44: 12
Hits for 45: 31
Hits for 46: 13

As mentioned earlier, not all of the MN hits are true MN hits, but that shouldn't skew the results too much.

My conclusion is that my Hoarfrost Hammer sucks, especially compared to Doomshade's other drops. The only way elemental damage would be worthwhile, is if the weapon already had a good ratio or if mobs are being debuffed, since without debuffs the elemental damage will be resisted the majority of the time.
