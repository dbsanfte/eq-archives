Slein Jinn
I'm a big fan of SCF3 early in your AA path (possibly even your first 12 archetype AA's if you're not hurting in the tanking department) on the grounds of it effects so many things for an SK.

On defensive AA's (luclin era) - you get the most for your AA on the first and 3rd levels, so doing CA2 isn't the most effecient. Stop at CA1 or go ahead and do CA3 for the best gain/AA. That said, I personally prefer CS over CA - same damage reduction over time, but less spikey. In the end, you'll want both, so it's not a big deal, but something to think about.

CS/CA > ND also from a tanking standpoint, but one can't argue with the usefulness of more HP - again, you'll want them all fairly early in your AA path.

SCF3, CS3, CA3, ND3, PE, horse, SA3, ToL3 > the rest.
