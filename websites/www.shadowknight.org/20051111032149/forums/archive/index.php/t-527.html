You never know what weapon your going to get, so train in as many as you can. I recently was awarded a Shard of Night, but my piercing was a lowly 43. It sure does suck trying to get it maxed out when you are level 52. The good side is, piercing goes up a lot faster than the 2h skills, due to the low delay.

I would suggest at the least, getting your 2h skills maxed, as the longer delays on 2h weapons causes them to go up very slowly.
