Attyla
Nod, lvl 30 has a lot of spells that you will end up using alot for some time to come. Initially they are pretty mana hungry.

Some tips would be to pick up some int gear to increase your base mana pool. In groups, you'll likely have some form of mana regen (which is far more important than base int). If you are fear kiting I would suggest items that increase your pool alot (either int or raw mana). I keep 3-4 items on hand to increase my mana pool when soloing, sacrificing AC in the process. Usually on the jewelry/low AC slots.

I would suggest you start saving soon for some BE gauntlets. They are typically very cheap and for a troll are excellent. I still use them when soloing, to keep my mana usage down when fear kiting.
