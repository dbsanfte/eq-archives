In mob terms it is kind of like an aoe rampage that hits everyone within a certain range of the mob.

Its used in some of the ikkiniz trials on some of the bosses. Basically the mobs seem to infuriate at around 50% health and will kill anyone within melee range. So we just back up when the mob gets close to this level. They seem to do it again around 10 percent health the ones I have seen anyway.
