Found myself working on Darkforge and wanting to know what else I needed along with the decayed, so here it is. I am probably going to work out a spawn map of the pyramids when I get time.

Darkforge Boots
11AC +3 sta
Need:
Decaying Left and Right boots
2 Faerie Dusts (Greater Faydark or Lesser Faydark)

Darkforge Bracer
9 AC +3 dex
Need:
Decaying Left and Right Bracers
Broken Minotaur's Horn (Steamfront)

Darkforge Breastplate
20 AC +10 sta +3 sv magic
Need:
Decaying Breastplate
Decaying mail
2 bars enchanted platinum

Darkforge Gauntlets
12 AC +2 dex +5 sv magic
Need:
Decaying Left and Right gauntlets
1 Bar enchanted platinum

Darkforge Greaves
13 AC +9 ata +3 sv magic
Need:
Decaying Left and Right greaves
2 Melatite (Sol A)

Darkforge Helm
13 AC +5 int +5 sv fire +5 sv disease +5 sv cold +5 sv magic +5 sv poison
Need:
Decaying Visor
Decaying Helm
2 damaged freeport Militia helms

Darkforge Vambraces
11 AC +4 str
Need:
Decaying Right and Left Vambraces
Qeynos Guard Kite Shield
