05-17-2004, 04:40 AM
StPatrick, buy a ring of goblin lords. It'll reduce your CD casting time to around one second.

My fear casting time is well under 2 seconds. That's normal for an SK with cheap focus and innate 60+ level spell haste.

SKs going for maximum aggro do not chain cast fear; they drop their highest level darkness on the incoming mob to get a couple more spells on it before it reaches camp, then alternate fear and clinging darkness. Fear is noticeably better aggro than CD but has a longer recast time so alternating with CD gives better aggro than either on their own.

Terror is a poor aggro spell due to being configured to basically (a) not work at range and (b) generate much less aggro than snares or fears.

Incidentally it's best to avoid doing aggro tests on undead or animals, which are often configured to react oddly to proximity and other effects.

I hope this helps, and thanks for the input.

Another thing I've lerned on these boards:

The agro generated by the terror line is a static amount while the agro from our other spells (fear CD, etc.) scales with the Mob.
