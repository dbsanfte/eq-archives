Save yourself some trouble and do this:

Before you try the combine again go to Marus Seru and Farm Greyhoppers for an hour or two. You should walk away with 5 billion LQ Hopper pelts and maybe 20-40 Greyhopper hides. Then go to the Feerrott and hit the NorthEast corner of the zone. Make sure you have your tailoring kit with you to save time. Farm the spiders and take their silk, once the mobs get sparce you can make silk threads until they repop.

Basically you combine 2 spiderling silks to get a Silk Thread, then combine 1 Silk Thread with a LQ Hopper hide to make a Leather Padding. Do this until you hit 56 in tailoring and then leave the Feerrott and hit the Bazaar. Buy enough Mask patterns to match your inventory of Greyhopper hides and combine them (1 of each) to make masks until you run out. This should put you somewhere above 60 or 70 in tailoring which will make your next attempt at the 1st ring combine much easier.

Then just sell off the extra LQ Hopper hides and Leather Paddings in the Bazaar and go do your ring combine. Greyhopper hides can take you to 95 in tailoring if you want to go that high.. but the pelts for it drop somewhat rarely so don't spend too much time on it.
