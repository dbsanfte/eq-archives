Learn to pull the temple yourself and it'll be a lot easier to convince them to stick around for another 15 minutes to clear it. Good luck convincing anyone else to step up and eat a bunch of HTs for the lone SK on the raid.

There are other ways, but I usually grab myself a Shiny Brass Idol, some JBoots (sometimes a Glare Lord will be parked inside with the Amys), and a tagger...click the Idol, approach the door until you see the HT messages, run around a bit and kite them around Cazic's hill while your tagger pulls them off a few at a time for your group to kill, or bring them all in yourself and AE them down.

You'll probably die halfway thru your first time or two until you get it down, but it's damn fun and can be rewarding for younger SKs. I make sure it gets pulled when someone needs it because I remember the frustration of seeing it go unpulled when I was collecting Umbral. ;)
