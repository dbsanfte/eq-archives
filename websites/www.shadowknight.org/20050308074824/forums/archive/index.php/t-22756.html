Use this map as a reference: (( Kudos to the mapmaker, by the way.))

Look at the bottom left corner, ignoring Inny's section. Look for the Tier 4 number 6. That's Archon G'uvin's spawn, clear it. Start there.Head west up and around Overlord R'Gahbsha's building ((number 3)), and take out that named/PH camp, then Dread Lord M'Noxin ((4)) right next to it. I HAVE seen the essence drop from him.

Here there is a deviation of the path into three routes:

Route A) This is if you want to camp Marsinger pieces as well. This is the longest and most DPS heavy route.
Head North to the number 6 from Tier three, Evangelist W'Rixxus. North of that building is Tier 2's number 8, Master D'Samni ((lich model)). Head South to Tier 3's number 4 Dread Knight T'Kamax. Southeast to the Spy master I'Kavin camp ((Tier 4's number 2)). Then east to the last Tier 4 mob of the route, Arch Lich T'Vaxok ((5)). Then head down to the Archon spawn and start again.

Route B) This also includes some Tier 3 named that drop marsinger items, but is slightly shorter and less DPS heavy.
Starting this from M'Noxin's spawn, Head North to the Evangelist. Then South to T'Kamax. South east to the Spy master, and back to the Archon.

Route C) This is all Tier 4 named. This route may or may not be useful. I personally think the Essence may drop from any Tier 4 named, and so this route was designed to camp them all. Since I and my groups were also after Marsinger drops from varying Tier's, I used Route B or A mostly.
Starting from M'Noxin, you go Northwest to the Spy Master. Then west to the Arch Lich. Then Southeast to the Arcanist V'Gimis spawn ((Tier 4, # 1)). Then back to the Archon. Likely, however, the Archon/ph may not have respawned. If this happens along any of the route, adjust accordingly.
