today i was considering spending adventure point to acquire one of the new spells that dots you and increases your atk. i wasn't really sure how much difference atk made so i decided to do some parsing with different ATK lvls.

the easiest way i found to do this was by fighting first with grim aura and second, without it.

i ran to kael and used the quest giver mob at the entrance as my dummy. he's great since he regens so fast you can't kill him and he doesn't really hit hard.

i parsed around 800 swing using grim aura and then 800 not using it. my initial guess as to how atk worked was that it affected accuracy. however my parse results showed me something different and frankly strange.

Without Grim Aura - ATK 1005

DPS: 56.11
Max Hit: 169
Min Hit: 47
Avg. Hit: 112.23
Accuracy: 60.51

With Grim Aura - ATK 1018

DPS: 56.00
Max Hit: 173
Min Hit: 48
Average Hit: 113.62
Accuracy: 61.39 %

these numbers come from Yalp from the Damage Tab. Grim Aura seemed to enhance my max hit , min hit and accuracy. That makes sense.

The DPS number just doesn't. How could lower accuracy and fewer hits not add up to a higher DPS? Anyone have any possible explanations? Should i try a different parser?

Also am I right in assuming that your ATK value increases your chance to hit as well as your overall dmg or is one of these numbers statistical noise?
