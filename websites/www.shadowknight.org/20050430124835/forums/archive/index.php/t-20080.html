Dyrkk
Soloing for aa when you have elemental flags is really innefficient, but it can be a fact of life if you play odd hours.

Fire is the best solo aa exp for aggro kiting, and you can also add a duo partner there to speed things up. Mana pool and mana regen are essential, as is mana efficiency-- the tribute system makes focus effects very easy to access, so FT/Mana Pres 4/Burning Affliction 4/Additional Mana Pool/ID4 might be a good bunch to consider. BA 4 tribute buff was broken at first according to guild necros/druids...might have been fixed by now. Also, equip your best weapon set for mana/int-- that is the primary concern for aggro kiting...if you end up meleeing anything, you've probably fucked up...

Mage summoned haste mask/stat belts/weapons for your pet make a huge difference in his dps, especially over time...so does our pet buff. Make sure his taunt is off too. To state more of the obvious, Kei or better is a good call (manapool/int over Pact), along whatever other buffs you can get -- Pot9 being among the most usefull.

PoF zoneline area is a good spot to test yourself. If you feel like it's too easy, then you can try the picnic table area by the Fennin script setup spot-- 6 solo kills per aa, but ofter camped and very very very fucking long solo kites. Conversely, there is a decent spot in Tactics up ontop of the pit on the left side, and finally there is also the HoH graveyard area.
