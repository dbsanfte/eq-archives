In short, to set the correct time in the BIOS you need to enter it while your computer is booting up. Typically you'll see something along the order of 'Press Delete to enter setup,' or F2, etc.; depending on the BIOS manufacturer.

Once in the BIOS, the focus should be on the Time/Date and you can make the changes accordingly and then choose 'Save settings and Exit' or similar.
One thing that could be affecting it is if your CMOS battery is low and needs to be replaced.
