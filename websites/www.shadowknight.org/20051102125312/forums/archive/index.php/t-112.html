Ok the mobs that spawn right in front of the bank entrance (where the Grand Herbalist now spawns) are some of the PHs for the Alchemist Acolyte. I would suggest just camping that area around the bank and killing everything and that is your best bet to get the Acolyte to spawn quickly (worked for me). If the Acolyte does appear, you will see the Shard in its hand as a dagger like graphic.
