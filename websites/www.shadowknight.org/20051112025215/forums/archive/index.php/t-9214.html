Harmshield drops you to the bottom of your groups aggro list, however you will still maintain aggro.

Cleric Divine Aura and CH. It's very easy for a cleric to take aggro using CH. Cleric get aggro and then casts DIVA. As long as DIVA is up, the mobs would rather go after anyone else in the group and the cleric is now dropped to the bottom of the aggro list.

18 seconds later, DIVA fades...Cleric sits down to med...BAM! Sitting aggro jams the cleric right back to the top of the list and the mobs go right after the cleric.

So that's how HS works. You get dropped to the bottom of the list, but your actual number of aggro points are NOT reset. It's almost as though the mob temporarily forgot you even existed.

However IF you have far more aggro points than, say a warrior taunting and EBing, when HS fades and you attack the mob, you will have "reminded" the mob how much it hates you and the mob WILL turn on you.

Normally speaking, a tank can over-ride the aggro difference in 18 seconds...
