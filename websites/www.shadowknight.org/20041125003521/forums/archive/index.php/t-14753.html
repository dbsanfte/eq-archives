I realize this topic has been discussed before, however I think its time to resurrect it once again.

After the utter disgust of watching my toon run like a special olympic’s marathon runner I have decided to give the old models a try. The original reason I switched to the luclin char models is due to the fact that my unholy steed does not work on the old models.

I spent 1day so far using the old models and the change is huge. The animations are smooth and life like, emotes are visible, armor textures look better and are far more plentiful allowing for a more unique look, the old armor textures look even better now with the dye system in place, and I just prefer the art style. The biggest change was the reduced lag, I run a very decent PC, 2400 p4 512pc2700 ram gf4ti4200, yet in quite a few zones on raids I still get lag. TimeA is especially bad, even with particles and spell effects completely off, with the old models however, with full spell effects/particles I was getting basically no lag.

I know for fact that the majority of my guild either uses all old models, or only has enough models turned on to use a horse, horses are the only thing keeping people from enjoying the old models. I for 1 spend 5aa, and this was in luclin, for my horse, and for it not be usable is a crime. I don’t care what the horse looks like while using the old model, I only use horses to get from point A to point B which doesn’t take much time, infact make the horse invisible if that is easiest, but I would want the horses to be available when using old models. I am sure this is not a difficult bug to fix, so it NEEDS to be done.

At the very least I would like my 5AA back.
