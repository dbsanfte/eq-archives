Tier 2 and 3 will be too difficult without a slower unless you have good gear since the mobs will eat you alive and your lack of dps wont drop them fast enough.

What you could do for decent xp is Plane of Diease left or right courtyards. They hit for 300s but give almost 1% xp per kill and not too many hps.

Another option would be Nadox in the undead area. The cleric could even land a few undead dots to speed up the process.

Best option is to get into a BoT or HoH group. The xp will fly.
