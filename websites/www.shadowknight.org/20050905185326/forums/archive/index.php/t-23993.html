Kurns Tower hands down is your best zone. You can stay there a while too, as the basement and subbasement mobs are pretty hard.

Time to ramble:
I leveled my Iksar SK on Kunark only until level 30, so here’s a mostly soloer’s guide to Kunark:

Kurns tower—nuh said
Close by in the Field of Bone is a very nice place to level your swimming, as the path is very long, long enough to trade skill while you do it safely. I do suggest you don’t take the pass through the cliffs though to the beach as an Iksar named may find and kill you, also there’s a ghost that roams on the beach. Go up into the woods behind Kurns Tower carefully clearing or avoiding if you are below 19 and get into the water there. If you swim just off of the shore you should be safe. I don’t suggest doing too much exploring on the island out there with the wall, big epic monkey out there.

Back to Leveling:
When you are bored with Kurns Tower try heading to the Overthere (especially if you can get group) or for solo, Warsliks Woods. In the Overthere the area between the PoK book and the Warsliks Woods zone is appropriate to your level below 20. Gather your troops just across the water in the Oasis, but don’t take the boat there from the Overthere, as the outpost will probably kill you. The mudpits are fun with a group, even a vendor is there who will sell to any race.

I spent most of my time between 18 and 30 in Warsliks Woods when I solo’d. The Giant Fort will can sustain your loot and xp needs solo from 18 to 25. Sneak around to the Cabilis side to enter, watch for roamers and try not to get involved killing skellies on the hill outside, giants can roam and the respawns of the skellys are frightening. Yes the caves can take you to the Lake of Ill Omen, but don’t do that unless you are feeling the adventure and die. Watch for goblin roamers and just say no. They are awlful. Once inside the giant fort, a safe camp is up on the hill. Snare the giants. There will be down time. Later in levels try the Brutes outside of Dalnir in the cave. If you have some friends to hang out with, try the goblin fort , nice earring can drop there.

If you have a healer friend go to the Swamp of no hope in your late twenties and camp the skelly camp near FV. If you are an earlier level try the skelly camps closer to the Field of Bone. This zone is great!! and too overlooked. I just duo'd my 28 druid with my hubby's 24 necro there and we had a blast!! extremely good xp, wish we had gone there sooner. Solo'd it's kind of hard as you can get adds, but you will learn to hate frogloks.

Grouping in Kunark, the Frontier Mountain Giant Fort or the LOIO Sarnak fort can be fun. The Timerous Deep is also worth exploring at any level. Watch when you get there. If you have to take a telelporter, get an invis first. Go via the Oasis. The sweet solo spot in your Late 20’s is Gorla Island. No one will be there, and you have plenty of mobs till level 35 to get good xp on and fear kite.

If you get a group together, hands down the best place is Dalnir at level 30 and above, especially if you have an enchanter. It blows the best ldon or raid out of the water.

This is not every zone, but a few that I did mainly. Post is long enough as it is.. good luck!
