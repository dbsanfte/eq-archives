Elkar
More details on spawning the babies:

They don't all spawn immediately. It takes 2-3 ticks of agro per baby to spawn them. They way we did this was to set up camp on top of the exit of the tunnel. There is a nice flat area with no mobs around to set up and buff (this is assuming Breeder is up and you are assembling for a kill). Cleared our way down, set up in the first cubby to the left inside the tunnel. Cleared the wanderers.

Then had an SK with harmshield (suppose a bard would work best here) agro the breeder, HS and run back toward the zone. Rule #1 helps for after HS wears off, but if you get far enough on HS, you'll make it past the heaviest concentration of adds. Once out in the open near the bridge you can run toward zoneline, but by this point all 6 babies will spawn. Mage CoH at this point is best.

Once breeder reset, we singled him (easily) to the cubby where he was tanked.

Took us a while to get this all done, it's a laggy zone and many folks were struggling with it. The spawns stay up for a good 15-20 minutes like said above. Once they depop or are killed, breeder will spawn them again if agro'd.
