If you have both a mage and a beastlord there (assuming both 65), have the warder tank, as it's procs have more aggro than the mage pet, although less HP. It WILL get aggro over the mage pet, so be sure its fully buffed, have the mage summon a pair of weapons, a 500hp belt, a haste mask, a bag of Phantom Plate, and focus items, and hand it to the warder.

If no beastlord, use the 65 earth pet as tank over any necro pet, it tanks better than the gheylord warder, its just a question of aggro.

Dont waste time with pull strategies, just Joboberize them into the ground.
