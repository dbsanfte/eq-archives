I did this part with the hide trick....as soon as the chick spawns, she and her brother are instantly aggro on you.

So, your options are to FD and /q out to clear aggro, or you wax em on the spot. If you have a mezzer there keep her brother mezzed, kill her, loot your stuff and leave him up so another sk can repeat that part of the quest.

If they wipe your group she won't despawn for sometime, and they will both be non-aggro on the second try.

Other than that make sure you have atleast 2 healers, cause these 2 bastages can cause some trouble.
