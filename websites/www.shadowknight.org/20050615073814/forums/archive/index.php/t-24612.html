on topic, since people keep posting misinformation...
you can proc multiple times per swing, the only thing that can't go off at the same time is primary & aug... so you can get the following combinations in one combat round:
primary
aug
SoD
TotC
primary + SoD
primary + TotC
aug + SoD
aug + TotC
SoD+TotC
primary+SoD+TotC
aug+SoD+TotC

with WA5 and around 290 dex, using a 30 delay weapon with 41% worn haste, over a 317 minute period (8691 combat rounds) i got:
941 primary procs (2.97/min)
843 augment procs (2.66/min)
634 SoD procs (2.00/min)
630 TotC procs (1.99/min)

so...
the number of procs per minute is
primary: 2 + 0.2*wa
augment: primary* (1 - primary * delay / 600)
SoD: 2
ToTC: 2

for example for the augment proc rate:
with my 30 delay weapon and 41% haste, the delay was 30 / 1.41 = 21.277
so expected proc rate was 3 * (1 - 3 * 21.277 / 600) = 3 * (1 - 0.1064) = 2.68 expected ppm, which is just about what happened over the 5 hour parse.
