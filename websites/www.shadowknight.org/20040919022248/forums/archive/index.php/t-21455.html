Slein Jinn
I'd definately go ahead and do Swift Journey.

KA/SotK are your best source of improvement for melee DPS, but being limited to 2h starts to become more problematic as you progress and 1h/shield becomes more and more required for tanking. Overall, it's a good AA choice for you.

Weapon Affinity is nice, but it's expensive and has a negative (albeit not large) effect on innate procs.

MC is good - it's a cheap way to get mana-regen not effected by the cap - always a good thing. It's got deminishing returns, however, being a progressively worse value after each level.

I'd probably do something like Swift Journey 2, MC1, KA/SotK3, and go from there.

pet hold is by far the best AA you don't have, unless you never use your pet... and if you never use your pet, then i'd have to assume you don't really care about efficiency, and recommend you max out the finishing blow and the double riposte lines as soon as possible.

other than that...

swift journey 1, possibly 2 (just because PVP, otherwise its really not _that_ useful for the cost considering we have unholy steed for outdoor areas)

i personally did and recommend MC1 and MC2 before starting the KA/SotK line.

if you use clinging/fear for agro, spell casting expertise 1 is definitely worth the 2aa. not fizzling those is useful.

immobilizing bash might be useful in PVP... i don't know if anyone has done any calculations on how much of an improvement it is though, so i really can't say if its worth the AA.

critical affliction 1 is only worthwhile if you consistently use blood of hate... overall i'd still rank it below the SotK/KA line assuming you use both a 2h and blood of hate all the time. with SoTK/KA you're looking at about a 0.33% per AA increase in melee DPS with a 2her all the time (so if you do 50 melee dps (low for elemental+), that's about 0.166 DPS per AA). critical affliction 1 is about 0.16 DPS per AA only when blood of hate is running.

intense hatred 1 is actually an okay deal for DPS when tanking, especially if you do stuff like tanking multiple unslowed mobs at a time in regular ldons. 2nd level is half as good of a deal, so stick with first level.

weapon affinity 1 is an okay deal (depending on how good your weapon proc is). with a sews i'd go ahead and get the first level before KA/SotK. WA2 is half as good of a deal, WA3 1/3rd, etc, so i'd just stick with the first level until you have KA/SotK/CF2 done.

based on proc rate formulas... (http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=20180)

heavy bludgeoner with black shroud comparison at full haste:

proc dmg per min:

wa0 wa3 wa5
siphon life 2*75 2.6*75 3*75
black shroud 1.93*270 1.91*270 1.90*270
total 671.1 710.7 738
dps 11.185 11.845 12.3


so, WA3 cost 12 AA total and netted you 0.72 DPS max (id4 included)... wa5 from WA3 cost 18AA and netted you 0.5 DPS max. the last levels of WA are not much improvement for a steep cost, unless you have a very very nice weapon proc.
