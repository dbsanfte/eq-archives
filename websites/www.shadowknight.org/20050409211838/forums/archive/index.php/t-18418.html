Catharsis
Search around.

In a nutshell: each individual weapon has a proc rate. Calculate procs per swing over a long time frame. Take off your haste and count it again, the number went up. Put the haste back on and get spell haste, the number went down. The average number of procs per unit of time remains the same. The average number of procs per swing varries depending on current delay. The latter would be the constant one if the weapon simply had a % chance at a proc with each swing. It may be stored this way in the item database, but in practice the number gets scaled with the inverse of your delay modifier (haste or slow) such that proc rate remains constant over time.
