Atlis
I recently finished the Greenmist quest from start to finish. As a Rallos Zek Ogre on Quellious, I spent uncountable hours faction hunting the Iksar bandits. Many a newbie now have a Bracer of Scavenging.
To get my khukris from the Crusaders of Greenmist, I maxed out my indifferent faction, and proceeded to target Lord Giczik. Then I used my Ornate Velium Pendant for its Alliance benefit. At amiable, I was able to acquire my Crusader's Khukri (after, of course, Penance and the work thereafter).
When speaking to Lord ?Korzin? to get the Hero's Khukri, he said I was not ready for him, and to perform tasks, or some such similar speech.
I was gutsy, however. I gave him the Crusader's Khukri and the Visceral Dagger. Although he did not see me fit for the task, he still rewarded me with a Hero's Khukri. Proceed to Arch Duke Xog.
Again, I was amiable to Arch Duke Xog as I was the other Crusaders. I spoke with him, and he requested the Stupendous Tome and my Hero's Khukri. I promptly handed him the items, expecting my Lord of Pain's Khukri. He laughed at me.
After filtering Tells to a separate window and sitting AFK for a day and a half, I finally got a hold of GM Brewko, who took the tome and khukri away from Xog and let me try again.
I was not completely idle, however. I went into Dulak and hunted a few Iksar sailors. After taking their meat and paying the merchants in Gunthak, I found my local shaman who, after a failed attempt, made an Illusion:Iksar potion.
As an Iksarized Ogre of Rallos Zek, I was still amiable, but Arch Duke Xog managed to hand me the Lord of Pain's Khukri. I then proceeded to the Greenmist quest, which gave me no trouble, even as a fat Ogre.

Long story short - don't bother getting Ornate Velium Pendant to trick the Iksar. Go kill some lizards, instead.
