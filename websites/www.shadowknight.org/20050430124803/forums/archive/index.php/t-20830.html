Oagan
A group of 55's would do better in Nadox or Droga. There is respectable loot available in either from the named that randomly spawn in both from ph's. Some could even be upgrades for your groupmates and yourself. You'd be fighting lvl 53-60 mobs that don't hit like PoP mobs (except for the bosses). Nadox also has undead to help get your evocation up, in case you didn't do so as you leveled.

Both are fun zones/dungeons, and invis works all through droga, and most of Nadox.

If you're still wearing less-than Velious era armor, then it could be worthwhile for some of the no-drop pieces (PoG). However, there is better armor available at reasonable prices in the bazar that's better than PoG stuff now. A few sessions in Droga, and you could buy a full suit of cultural, which is comparable or better than ToV/Kael armor.
