AA points are a bit of a discussion point. Personally I would put yourself on full aa till you get run 1 and then turn it off till you reach 60.

Why...

Levels > AA xp.

Your attack, hp, ac all go up, and you get new spells, some of which are class defining like the torrent line.

Why run 1. For pulling in dungeons pure and simple. Run 1 will let you stay ahead of most mobs without sow, which if your pulling is invaluable.

After that, you should have a better idea of where you want to go AA wise.
