Howling Stones is expensive if you don't have an overthere hammer. I'm an Iksar, and I usually go there until I can get a port out, I hate drinking gate potions just to get out, and there's no way in hell I can fight through solo, or anywhere deep.

At 54 your undead lull spell will work on most mobs, and I mostly just tanked things down, speared, tapped, sometimes DOTed, but mostly speared and expulsed undead for the low cast time with spell haste. It's good XP.

When you zone in, find a corner and FD. Check the spawn layout and see what's floating around the center. If you can lull one of the entrance mobs and kill the other, then the lulled one. Under you is a pit full of good XP skeles, and directly under the zone in platform is a portal up to the entrance. There is no way you could get out solo without some form of gate. Just a few rooms away there's a mob that I have a hard time dropping with a trio (some big named sperm with teeth). Get a map and watch out for pits, they are a PITA when trying to progress through the dungeon and ultimately lead to the big zone in pit, which I recommend you stay at if solo.

Do what you will, I've had a few CR's here, and have drank a few gate potions to get out, but I keep going back...

Depending on the class and level of your friend, you might try duoing at the Hedge in PoN, if you're up for duoing. Mobs range in level between 53 and 55, and it can be challenging when they're even con. Xp is pretty good; loot is, of course, pretty craptacular.
