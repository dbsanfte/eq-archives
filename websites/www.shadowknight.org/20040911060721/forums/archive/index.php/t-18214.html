Thakalas
It doesn't matter what your base stats are really, since any buffs/gear that brings it up behaves identically to having the base be that. The that's easiest to see is the penalty to AC for having under 75 agility. Assuming your gear brings agility up above 75, it's all good.

Dex and agility have nothing to do with hit/miss. Depending on what you're attacking (ie red con/blue con/green con) you'll miss about half the time.
