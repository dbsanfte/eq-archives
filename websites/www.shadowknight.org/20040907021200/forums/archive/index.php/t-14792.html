Ogres get Slam, which is great to help keep taunt and for interrupting casters. Frontal stun immunity is only helpful in specific situations so I wouldn't worry too much about it.

Iksar can't wear most plate which is often a bad drawback.

If you're twinking heavily then race is almost secondary, since your stats will come from your armour.

But it sounds like you're not, in which case you'll probably find the ogre better.

Incidentally, off topic I notice that your group doesn't include a secondary healer (i.e. druid). If you're planning on mostly staying to the exp groove in Paludal, HHK DSP and DL then it doesn't matter. If you want to spice things up a little by roaming around dungeons a lot then you might think of a druid instead of the wiz.
