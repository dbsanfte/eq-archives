Btw just for info: you dont loose aggro when you "miss" hits.

You attack with a 42 dmg weapon and miss = you gain 42x aggro
You attack with a 42 dmg weapon and hit for 200dmg = you gain 42x aggro
You attack with a 52 dmg weapon and hit for 70dmg = you gain 52x aggro (more than the 200dmg hit from above)
