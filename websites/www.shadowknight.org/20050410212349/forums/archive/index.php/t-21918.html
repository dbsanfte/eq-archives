kassdax
Here's a couple of suggestions for plat farming.

1. Kurns Tower - kill the stuff and collect bone chips. On Saryrn, bone chips sell for 20pp a stack if you sell them in bulk (get 4+ stacks before putting them up in the bazaar). Stay here until level 20 or so.

2. Level 20-25 - head to East Karana and kill spiders. These things drop silk, lots and lots of silk. Mainly go after the red and black spiders, they drop the most silk. On Saryrn server, the silk sells for 200pp a stack. Once you get several stacks, head to the bazaar and put them on your trader overnight.

These two things outfitted me very well for my level. Prices on your sever will probably vary but one thing in common with pretty much all servers is people want trade skill stuff.

G' luck.
