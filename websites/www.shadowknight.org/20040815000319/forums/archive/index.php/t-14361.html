I did a few of the Darkened Knight's quests after I finished my Darkforge. Trust me, they're not worth the trouble. The mobs that drop what you need are rare, and unless you can find the items in bazaar or on a vendor, you'll have some serious issues with getting everything for a single piece.

Each Darkened Knight's piece requires at least 3 items, the Greaves and BP require 4 each.

I'd say go do the Darkforge Greaves, if you need/want to quest the items. No faction issues with the turn in mobs (unlike the katta mobs that you'll have to deal with if you try the Darkened Knight's set) and much easier to gather the components. Otherwise, look into buying some Acrylia plate or Engraved Royal Velium. Decent stats, and not nearly the PITA that the quested armors are for lesser items.
