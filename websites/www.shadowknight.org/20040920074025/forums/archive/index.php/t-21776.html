... follow the pillars out into the lake till you are between the two towers jutting up from the water (roughly lake center).

Submerge yourself and you will find a large dome not far under the water. Follow the curve of the dome deeper, till you hit vertical wall, follow it down to sand level. Swim around till you find a door into the dome.

Thats Veksar.

The zone entrance has been there since Kunark. Was dissapointing to find an obvious zone entrance there, and no zone. Glad they finally put it into game.

Excellent dungeon, well worth the time to find it.
