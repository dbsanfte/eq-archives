The gate potions are nice to have.
It's not often that I use them but when I do, I make another so I always have one on me.
Keeping one on you guarantees that you always have a way out, no matter where you are.

OT hammer is great, too but can get hairy if you're relying on it as an escape from the mob you're trying to get away from.
Much nicer if you can use it against someone else's mob so you're not getting splattered while awaiting the proc.
