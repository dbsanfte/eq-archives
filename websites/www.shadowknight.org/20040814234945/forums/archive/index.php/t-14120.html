Leyl D`Korrosin
Obviously not common enough :D

There are Realm of the Heros npcs in the old world cities and in Cabilis (never bothered to check Shar Vahl, and they're only in PC starting cities). They all have rather long stories they'll tell you if you hail them, but they all boil down to 'My god decided I was special, and wisked me away to the Realm of the Heros. I've been sent back to the material world to reward others who have been deemed worthy. Give me the coin, I'll give you your reward.'

Entirely too many people engaged in conspiracy theory-esque discussions of what 'The Coin' was, and how to get it/them. It all turned out to be the new (this was what? Kunark era added content? I forget exactly) way VI had set up GM event rewards. They put one in or around the starting cities to make faction not an issue for people who had been involved in the events. The stories the guys tell you are actually pretty interesting if you feel like sitting through them.

As for whether I have first hand experience; no, I've never won anything from a GM event. I'll spare you the boring 'I know a guy' stories, but yes, people have confirmed their function. Of course, what was really the last interactive GM event? The Malevolence thing, I'd guess... and that's a good while back now.
