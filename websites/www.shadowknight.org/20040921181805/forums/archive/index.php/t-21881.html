Dyrkk
As you level from 51 to 65, its fine to go 100% AA for a LDoN adventure or two occasionally in order to hit Run 3 and perhaps Regen 3.

But although Level Exp may seem to be slow and frustrating, it is more important and powerful than any AA offered to us in many many ways. The spells gained themselves are some of the best pieces of casting progression that we are given. You will gain AAs very fast once you level up and have access to the better exp areas, so do not overvalue cheap short term rewards over meaningful long term progress-- you will end up in the same place more efficiently if you stick to levels first.
