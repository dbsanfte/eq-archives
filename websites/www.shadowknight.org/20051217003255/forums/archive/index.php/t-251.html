the damage bonus doesnt add to raw damage (a 6 damage bonus on a 23/40 weapon does NOT make it a 29/40), but rather, it adds damage on the final hit (e.g. if you normally would hit for 46 with that weapon, the actual damage would be 52 and the minimum damage you can hit with it would be 7, 1+6)

For all you Iksar, Gnome, DE, Eru and Human SK's.

As far as I can tell the damage bonus you get is only applied after the calculations are done for including your STR and weapon skill. Also there is a thing called modal damage which is kind of like the usual amount of damage that you do. So if you actually connect with the mob...

Minimum damage is 1 + damage bonus.
Modal damage is (2 * weapon damage) + damage bonus.
Max is (weapon damage * ((STR + weapon skill) / 100)) + damage bonus.

Note that the max and modal damage could actually be higher than that if you have an item or spell such as Grim Aura that adds to your ATK. However, these formulas will get you pretty close.

The above information was derived using the information I found at
http://www.monkly-business.com/articles2.asp?ArticleNum=66

I've observed that SK one handed weapons follow very closely to the primary hand calculations. 2HS and 2HP weapons also appear to be the same 2HB formulas in Xaas Swifttails article.
