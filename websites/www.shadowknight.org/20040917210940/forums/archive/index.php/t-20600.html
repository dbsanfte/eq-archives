Lynya Shaddowfury
For those of you who aren't EP/Time geared and running around pwning the world (we do exist)...

Take 2 solid groups 60+ or more if the peeps are lower level. DPS melee, many of the creatures are high MR and lvl 56+. Once you've worked around to the castle front, paci gargoyles up top and pull the spirits. There's also a pair of wandering constructs, take them out, kill the gargoyles and keep the pulling at a good rate; a necro is invaluable for feeding the cleric(s), if necessary. Have your tracker check which side the ghost is on; left or right. You can actually get just inside the door and not agro the second pair of gargoyles if your raid group is small or smart enough to stick close to the wall (shrink is your friend). Since the mummy will pop on the left side basement, move your raid to the left side GY, paci or kill that gargoyle.

From what I read here that the mummy would run out of the basement, he DID NOT for us, after several minutes of twiddling our thumbs we tried to pull the spirits there and got them all, which was all right, the enc mezzed, no big deal. Everyone pounced on the mummy and was beating him down, but when he started to run (15%) he was too close to the repoped front yard.

Ouch.

Somehow he managed to die at the feet of the ghosts outside the front entrance. The necro rezzed the cleric who rezzed the rest of us. We weren't able to clear to it in time and the corpse rotted. Immediately after the depop, ghost of glohnor was up, this time in right side (he had been in left when I turned in).

Many of the posts above mine hadn't yet been made when we went; but now the lessons are learned: sufficient DPS for quicker pulling; just pull the basement before turn in; move into the basement or handle push better.
