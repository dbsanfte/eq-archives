Ruuti
A lot of good points have been made here but the one that I find most pertinant is the relationship of xp to level, it is true that it has never been easier to get experience, SOE has geared it that those in a group fighting mobs as close to their level or above would get the best xp. Sure I read a post on it, but the aim was to get people in full groups fighting mobs that were all their level or one to two levels below them. Then they made the xp drop off sharply for those who are fighting mobs significantly below them. Taking into account the High Zem of the planes and the relatively high level of the mob most people fight per level the experience will also remain high. Raising the ZEM of the rest of EQ to match the planes would spread out the population some, but then the creators of the game are faced with a population that grows out of control and will be harder to please (keep in mind we all seek an immersive environment). The other option would be to lower the XP in PoP to match the rest of EQ then you would have people as we have seen in this thread crying Nerf. SOE is up against a wall, and the question they have to answer is how do we keep the most people happy while providing an environment that they can control and an economy that is not any more out of control.
