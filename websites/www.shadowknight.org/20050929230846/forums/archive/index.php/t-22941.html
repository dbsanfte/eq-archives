Hopefully you have your Fearless AA. If you don't, there's no real reason to use you over a warrior. Also, you probably don't want to hear this, but consider using a Fearless paladin to tank him the first time. Aside from his damage dealing AE, he also has an AE silence which will make getting (and maintaining) aggro a real sonofabitch. Seems like the many times we killed him, I was always hit with the silence right off on inc. Paladins have a discipline (Santification, I think) that makes them immune to spells for a few ticks and thus they won't get silenced on inc.

Tank him in one of the two back corners that you zoned up into P5. He and Bertox aren't leashed to their little areas like the other gods in Time are.
