Bulvyne
Since this idea is a purely role-play aspect of EQ, you can in fact create ANY reason that you want for being an SK.

Someone could argue why a DE would "make a better SK" than a human, but I bet you could find just as many folks who could argue the point the other way around.

The important thing is: what feels 'right' to YOU? Do YOU feel more "shadowknightly" being a human, or DE? It's really a matter of personal choice.

Elric and surprisingly enough Vader both represent good ideas of 'variants' of a shadowknight. You can see most of the EQ SK qualities in both them. (Personally, I think the Elric series is one of the best-written fantasy series of all-time). But, both of these villain-heroes are much different from each other... just like humans and DE's in EQ. ;)

Here is the way I have looked at it:

From a roleplay aspect anyway:

DE make good shadowknights, they are the children of Innoruuk and are born into the darkness.

Trolls and Ogres are evil by nature. They are big and strong and evil, though they may not fit the "knight" aspect like others would, they got the "shadow" part down.

Humans and Erudites CHOOSE to be evil. Their races can (and mostly are) good classes, but these few decide for themselves to be evil, they don't start that way.

That's what I like about my human. I wasn't evil because mommy and daddy were, it was MY decision to turn to the side of hatred.
