dagelbauer
Dot/pet kiteing is possible for a skilled Shadowknight in Vexd, i've done it many times myself.
A few things to remember,
1) Use Lev to take advantage of the ledges for extra running space
2) The mobs here hit very hard so HP/AC buffs are a big help
3) Its not a half-assed kite, lag in this tight area with mobs that hit for 1k is bad
4) Last but not least watch out for the little bugs they can be hard to see and they do SUMMON, if you have a hard time single pulling without getting the bugs exit and start a new one.

Good Luck and "Don't be a fool, wrap your tool"
