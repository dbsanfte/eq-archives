Klotar AngedeMorts
Thacker, it depends on your race I think (of the race/religion/class combo). Humans/Erudites/Gnomes may need less factioning, I'd guess.

Generally, illusion will boost you one level, and the scalp will boost you one level. As a DE (evil race) SK (evil class) worshipping Innoruuk (evil diety), I maxed at Amiable. With scalp, Kindly. With illusion, Warmly. Warmly is needed for the quest.

To be a bit more clear on the 'one level', I'm told that it's a set number of faction points rather than purely faction level. That is, max kos without scalp may still be max kos with scalp, rather than going from Scowling to Threatening; this is due to the high number of points in the Scowling range.

So, your goal is Warmly. How you get there via combination of faction work, scalp and illusion doesn't matter as long as you achieve Warmly. I'd guess that unless you are Human/Erudite/Gnome, you will still have to do a lot of faction work in order to complete this quest. You can, of course, try an illusion with the scalp on to see what your faction is without doing the faction work -- but the bottom line is Warmly or no woodie for j00! :D
