For Skard, SolA. Great xp modifier in there. And get the hammer while you can gain the xp. You won't always be playing with your cousin. The reason people pick shaman/sk duos is for the Shaman slow, haste, and melee buffs. On the Shaman boards, SK's are becoming one of the favorite 2-box choices, because our dot/snare creates more aggro than slow. You can duo easily to 65 with a shaman, because the shaman can slow the PoP mobs for you. Druids start having a little harder time in PoP duos, because of the lack of slow.

For Maelyn, I would say maybe give the wyverns a try in CS. You could also try Crystal Caverns. Good xp modifier in there, as well, plus some velium for selling. When you get to 50, give Velks a try. You could also try Solb (Nagafen's Lair). The higher you get, the deeper you'll be able to go. I'd say Kaesora, but you're probably wanting to keep your VS faction at this point for spell purchases. Lizarde
Sol A- as mentioned already, great exp modifier and very good cash/sellable loot. As long as you're not concerned with gnome faction you'll be fine (and why be concerned with gnome faction? pfft). There are casters among the bunch and they can nuke for 200 or 300+ as I recall, don't get too confident after you easily kill the warrior types. :)

Crystal Caverns- decent exp, again very good loot, pretty fun zone once you get used to it. The orcs are pretty weak but if you're not careful you can create one hell of a swarm of them.

The Great Divide- may as well start working that Velious faction now. Frost giant scouts should be pretty easy at 35+ and are dark blue right on through 45 or 47. They can hit harder than most level 30 mobs, but are easily fear-kited. Berserkers are tougher, and stay away from Elites, they will ruin your day.

The Dreadlands- mobs have low HP and low DPS and can be fear-kited. Can hunt here until your low 50s, assuming you want to go completely crazy.

Firiona Vie- the valley in the Northeast corner of the zone, that leads to the Swamp of No Hope. Lots of mobs, easily pulled into an area with no wanderers, decent exp. Lots of shaman mobs, though, and they can be a real pain in the ass. But as long as there is no one else there you can pull all day long without a break if you want. Good exp until 39.

Dawnshroud Peaks- Yeah, everyone goes there, but with LDoN you might find a nice corner or two to pull to. Might not be worth it if its crowded, the /ooc and /shout arguments are entertaining for a while though.

And if you want to be old school, the basement in Unrest could take you to 39 or 40 pretty fast as well. Fun zone, tons of undead, nice atmosphere, and a very good exp modifier.
