As an Erudite you'll only have two major problems. First: nightvision, or actually the lack of it which isn't much of a factor until 22 when you get Deadeye. The other isn't that big a problem but do yourself a favor and put some of your starting points into Agility which is the major score Erudites fall behind in. Oddly enough Erudite SK start with a little more strength than Dark Elves.

In regards to faction don't worry about the ghouls. The ghouls everyone is talking about are in 3rd Gate in front of the Lodge of the Dead, the Burnished Coin (bank) is in Neriak Underground in Neriak Commons.

WHen you make your character do yourself a favor and worship Cazic so you can start in Paineel. Mainly because the n00b area there is one of the best around. N00b are practically spoonfed there first level hunting undead there.

Just my thoughts. I'm still Tier'dal all the way.
