Go down the middle hall from entrace, when you hit the wall, turn left, take first right. You should be in a hall with 8 snakes. There's a hidden wall on the left. That's rhag 1 spawn spot. He's probably the easiest source of good sol 60 spells.

Pretty sure rhag 1 is zhezum. All of rhag 2's and arch lich's guards are rhags as well, so don't rely on just knowing that rhags are up, look for zhezum specifically.
