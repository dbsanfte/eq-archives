Jodgrim
That's interesting, according to SoE after they made changes to the experience system, adding a sixth person should not change the exp modifier for the group.

As I understand it, the xp modifier goes up when you add members to your group from 2 - 5, and 5 it is 80%(I think). What that means is that when you kill a mob you get an extra 80% xp to spread among the people in your group.

When you add a 6th person the modifier does not go up, meaning that you are still getting mob xp+80% but the number of people you have to spread that number among goes up, so you get less xp per kill with a 6 person group than you would with a 5 person group.

What got me was Nyrvel getting the same amount reg xp per kill as AA xp in a full group when there is a difference of 2% in a 5 man group. Seems like there wouldn't be. (I think that was what Savoline was asking about too.)

The exp per person is the same from a 5 person group to a six person group (not taking into account the affect of your level posisition relative to the rest of the group).
