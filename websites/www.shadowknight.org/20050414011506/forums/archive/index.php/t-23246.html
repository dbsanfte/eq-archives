Pinepath
Pet weapon priority is just based on delay, nothing else. Also, pets that natively dual wield won't equip two handers. If the pet has a weapon in primary, it won't equip a primary only 2nd weapon that is slower, since that would mean swapping out the faster weapon it's already using primary. Similarly, if it's already using a slow primary only weapon, and you give it a faster weapon that is pri/sec, it will swap out the primary and still not be dual weilding, since the old primary won't go into secondary.

The graphic bug is when you give a pet one weapon, and then give it a 2nd that is faster, making it switch hands. It won't replace the graphic in primary when it switches, but it's just a graphic bug.
