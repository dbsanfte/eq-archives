My theories:

Similar to the health/hp/mana updates, there seems to be a latency for xp and aaxp for server vs. what you see on the client. In an effort to speed up aspects of the game, I think SoE has been tweaking/tinkering with the frequency at which these updates and synchronizations occur.

For example, in a very recent patch, they announced that spending AA points in STA/AdvSTA would now update your hp immediately. Before this patch, spending a point in STA/AdvSTA would take several minutes before an increase in current and max hp would show up on your display.

There also seems to be some anomalies when playing with the slider bar for XP and AAXP. On two of my characters, I decided I would max out my 65 xp bar instead of hanging at 20% like most people do (don't ask, I hate staring at a non-full xp bar I guess). When I maxed my xp bar at 100%, I wanted to see if I could "fill out that 100%" (i.e. it took x kills to go from 98% to 99% to 100% so I wanted to fill the 100% so that the first time I die, I'm not at 99% again). Obviously, since I didn't ding lvl 66 nor was there any indication that the xp bar was "moving beyond 100%, I slid the bar to split between xp and aaxp. I had to med up a bit for a couple minutes, and then when I was fm and ready to pull, I glanced up and my xp bar was at 99% and my aaxp bar had jumped 10%+. While I acknowledge that this one is explainable (similar to pre-Kunark and pre-Luclin), it was still somewhat evident that the server had made some recalcs and transmitted them to the client with some sort of delay.

I have no answer, just thought I would pass on a few observations and theorize at what might be happening.
