and for playing SK, some tips:

use clinging darkness for agro with affliction haste 1. there's no faster more mana efficient way to gain hate, and it does snare the mobs enough so they walk from 20-10% and stop moving at 10% hp. if you use festering or terrors for agro you'll either run out of mana or have the mobs ponging to others. with clinging you can cast and recast all day long without running oom.

Marruk
My mana pools sucks, and always has sucked.

If you are running out of mana, and you are MT, cut back on the dps spells. The extra dps you can squeeze out by using our spears and dots eats a lot of mana, and as MT you should be worrying about holding aggro first, and dps second.

Using just CD for aggro, and mental corruption for mana (no kei), you should be able to hold aggro indefinately, while still putting out some decent dps (you are using your pet, right?). If you have a shammy in the group, then your dex should be well buffed, and mental corruption should be proccing fairly consistantly.

If I am the only snarer in a group hunting mobs than run, I can maintain mana for a long while if I cut back on other spells. I can Fester and spam CD for aggro without mana buffs for a pretty long time (long enough for a LDoN, at least).

In a decent exp/LDoN group, there should be little to no reason to lifetap if your healers are even remotely competant. Zevfeer's mana drain is negligible, as it is roughly 100 mana every minute (less if you have focus effects). Spears aren't worth it unless you have mana to spare, which is clearly not the case.

It sounds like you are burning too much mana. Pact of Hate is nice, but it probably isn't going to solve your mana issues.

Lifetaps and Z bites uses a lot. QUOTE]

Lifetaps aren't mana friendly. Unless you can increase your taps with AAs and focus, then it'll always be more mana friendly to let the healer heal you. MC + ZB should equal themselves out. Even with crappy dex, you should get 1 proc every 45 seconds or so with MC. That would replace the mana lost, give everyone else in the group 100 mana, and do 250 dmg to the mob, all costing you nothing.

Clinging Darkness with nothing but MC to keep you afloat should have more than enough mana regen to keep aggro. Even a resist is great aggro for you. Like others said, spears aren't worth using unless mana permits. 600 dmg every minute won't make or break any encounter your group would have.

[quote]I don't use the pet lots of times. I never use it when pulling and i rarely use it when there is a mezzer for adds because the pet tends to sick the add

You do know that pets cannot attack a mezzed mob. If the mob they fight gets mezzed, they immediately stop fighting it. Your pet is free damage. If you position the pet behind the mob (which is easy to do if you tell it to guard the entrance to your camp, and run past him everytime you pull a mob), then it should survive quite well. A whole LDoN even. The only thing that makes it a pain in the ass is when pulling and you get hit, it runs to your aid.. well until you get Pet Hold. If it dies, big deal. Better your pet die than you. It takes an extra iota of thought, but it helps a little.
Ashrai
A poorly controled pet is one of the most annoying things

Yes, it is. Which is why you should launch little Jabober every chance you get and learn how to let him do what he needs to do. Your pet will not, can not, and never ever will break a mez. It's no longer possible. He will also whine at you telling you that he can't wake it up.

In fact learning to use him before you get /pet hold will make you a pet controller supreme. Once you parse him and see what a nice little dot he is (solution to mana probs right there) you'll find him well worth the bone chip. If you solo as much as you are saying you do, you should be pretty adept at using him, and pulling your mob to where he's behind it should be second nature by now.

Ditch the spears, even at his 50whatever per hit, Jabober is outdamaging your spear very quickly especially with the long refresh on them. Never rely on something for agro with that long a refresh, it's basically taking your mana and throwing it on the ground.

You should need two spells and two spells only in PoV. Festering on the mob on incoming to give everyone time to debuff and slow it, and hit it with a fear and it will come right to you. 2x CD negates Tash + slow off a chanter, test it and you'll see.

If you can get the cleric to toss a virt on Jabober he can take care of splitting if you get two unsnareables that need to be split and you're worried about not surviving to get FD off. Jabober with a virt and a rune can survive singling stuff in sol ro tower, so he's more than capable of PoV. Get used to your /pet back off, and /pet follow hotkeys, practise somewhere safe like PoN splitting stuff near zoneline, and you'll get the hang of backing him off the instant he gets agro and then tagging off him.

If your groups are so low in DPS that you are being put in the position of blowing your mana on DPS instead of agro then form your own groups and put a rogue or something in them. Higher DPS = mobs die faster = SK needs less heals. Sounds pretty obvious but you'd be amazed at how many people will put their level 59 friend who wants PLing in the group instead of that level 65 rogue who's slumming in PoV for some easy AA before bed.
