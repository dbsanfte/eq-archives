Lejina Bloodbath
Slam act as a 5/60 off hand weapon

Bash act as a *ac of your shield, up to 25*/60 off hand weapon.

So, if you use a Sarnak Battle Shield (ac 25) it will act as a 25/60 weapon when you will use bash.

Bash/Slam also have the ability to interrupt casters up to lvl 55.

Bash and Slam both use the same skill.

Totally and completely incorrect. Slam is simply a racial version of Bash Skill which does not require a shield to be equipped. In other words:

I hit the slam button. The combat readout is something like, "You bashed MOB_001 for 19 points of damage!"

I equip a shield, hit the slam button. The combat readout is something like, "You bashed MOB_001 for 29 points of damage!"

The slam button will activate a "bash" if a large race has a shield equipped. Due to some of the bash changes with shields, an actual shield bash does more damage than a slam, slightly. This is not, however, due to the skill progression. It is simply because they coded it to make shields give a damage modifyer to bash skill.

When you are using slam, skill in bash goes up, but you have to train a single point in the skill, IIRC.

I've done some asking around in the past, and I know that large race shamen are not able to increase their slam skill beyond 1 damage. I'm not sure about warrior class, as the only Troll warrior I know never bought a shield (he also didn't play very far on that toon).
