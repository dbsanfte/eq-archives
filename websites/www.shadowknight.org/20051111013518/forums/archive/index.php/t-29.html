Another alternative is the Horned Beetle Mask from Hollowshade. It drops off Gorehorn (low 30s), who is spawned by mass killing the Rhino Beetles (level 10ish) near Shar Vahl. Gorehorn will usually spawn within 30 minutes or so of doing this. Stats on the mask are:

6ac 4str 3sta 6sv-magic 6sv-poison
effect:ultravision (triggered, 5 charges)

Charges used to be unlimited but Verant deemed that too uber for the level needed to obtain it so it was smacked with the nerf-stick.
