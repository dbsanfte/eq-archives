Squigle D'Muerte
Best way to increase Evocation:

Get C3. Go to Paineel Newbie area.

Using lowest level undead nuke kill all the skels you see then run up on hill straight out from entrance.

Once all the skels are dead (like 5) they will start to repop at bottom of hill provided you stay far enough away. Range on nuke reaches bottom of hill so you are cool to just stand there.

As long as you stay on hill, every time you kill a skel another will immediately pop in same place. You can kill piles of skels this way AND you can get piles of bone chips for doing it if you want. Beats beating on your pet.

Only thing is if you see a newbie Necro/SK in area let them have it. While it is normally empty they need it more than we do.

I do this every so often and it has kept me in bone chips and got my Evocation skill to 227. INT is only 147 with C3 so skill increases hard to come by at this point.

Also when sitting around wayfarers camps keep your low level undead nuke up. Most of them have undead wandering through camp may as well do something while you are sitting around.
