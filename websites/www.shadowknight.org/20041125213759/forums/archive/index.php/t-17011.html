Aelali
Well, maybe I should explain my idea a bit more. If we had an AA that would effect fear then I think it should be one that we could use to turn "on" or "off" fear's ability to land on higher lvl NPCs.

So, for example, you want to fear kite, so you hit the hot key made for that ability, and now your fear spells will effect them. If you want to use fear as an agro spell, then hit the ability again and the spell no longer would effect NPC that now are immune to fear because of their lvl. They of course would have to work in a refresh time (hopefully very small) between when you turn it off and when you turn it on, but basically my idea is to be able to toggle it as you want it... with the ability staying in effect until you turn it off. Basically an on off switch for fearing higher lvl NPCs.
