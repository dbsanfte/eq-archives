Now if you are hoping to go into VP, my first question is, what part of the game is your guild at? If you are still at hall of testing phase or lower but can kill Trak. There is alot of REALLY good loot in VP. Robe of Azure Sky is the best robe your caster will see until they start farming vulak/emp/AHR. Also this is noticeable better exp then PoV(Idiot free exping is great).

There is alot a guild can gain from farming VP. Its a great piece of game content that is often over looked or ignored.
