There's a few factors at work.

First, a parse taken by yourself is never 100% accurate for those around you. It's closer in an exp group (people are less likely to be out of range of your parse), but the parse can still miss things from other people. For example, dot damage from other people is never factored in.

Gear gap. I've parsed myself at about the same damage output as a rogue appy before.... because he was using BoT daggers or something similar, and I had epic 1.5 and max worn atk.

AA gap.... things like touch of the cursed, soul abrasion, etc add a lot to our sustained dps.

A bit of effort in gear and aa's can make up a sizable gap in the difference between a 'dps' class and us.

And finally, SK's straight up are not all that bad of dps. We'll usually edge out warriors, always outdps paladins... depending on if we have decent mana regen, and the mob's resists, we can often knock on the door of other classes, too.
