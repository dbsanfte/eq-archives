My brother and I have done a bit of talking about new classes being added and what we would like to see. The only one we could both agree on was a nija type class that would have been a hybrid between a monk and a rogue with a bit of magical power sprinkled on top (mainly geared towards stealth).

They would eventually get critical throws (with shurikens and the like much like rangers get critical shots with their bows) and critical strikes (with their monk like abilities). They would be able to apply poison like a rouge and have a line of ninja specific weapons such as the sai and nunchuk.

Their magic powers would be geared around stealth (as mentioned before) and feature something like a smokescreen which would wipe aggro as the ninja was escaping.

They would be able to hide and sneak as well as have a backstab type of ability which would only work when performed while hiding and sneaking.

Of course we have to have some drawbacks to offset all of those advantages so ninjas would only be able to wear cloth based armors (making them the weakest damage absorbing class there is).

They would have the same weight restriction that monks do only going over it would drop their armor class more drastically.

That was about all we came up with but I think that would be a pretty killer class (naturally my main character is a monk while my borther plays a rogue as his main character which is probably the only reason we came up with this combination).

Sometimes that's the case. However, most of the time, it's because of shit like this. Once a month, without fail, we get a "Hey, guys! I heard from this guy who heard from his sister's cousin's brother's roommate that a high end guild on Xegony knows this uber guy on Zebuxoruk who knows this other guy on Vallon Zek who has completed every quest in the game and he says that he found a super secret quest mob only up on a full moon when the planets are in alignment who gives you the option to begin a quest for Epic 2.0!"

We've heard it all before. It's not true. There is no Epic 2.0. Period. Never has been. Probably never will be. Chances are you're just catching the month or two old tail end of a rumor that was started here in the first place.

Case in point, probably the beginning of this rumor:
