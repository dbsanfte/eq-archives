Dyrkk
Soloing an SK is very doable at any level. But it takes time and patience, and it is often very innefficient. I'm talking primarily about aggro kiting to solo, because fear kiting ends once the mobs you can kill for exp are L55 and up.

Aggro kiting is pretty simple. Snareable mobs that don't summon are really the only thing you need. Get your pet up and haste him. Basically, the idea is that you snare the mob and keep aggro on it while it chases you, and your pet chases that mob (with taunt off) and hits it. You refresh the snare when needed, and drop some DoT spells on it to speed the process, perhaps even using the occasional DD spell. Eventually, the mob dies...then you sit and regen mana so you can do it again.

There is no scenario normally where an SK can make more exp solo than they would killing similar content with a group.
