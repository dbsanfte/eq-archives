Well if I understand this right... all the mobs will aggro the pet anyway if he's " not green " to them or one of them has a pet of their own.

What I used to do on a few pulls that included a lot of running room ( Like Rumblecrush ) is park the pet out to a side. Run up to mob and cast your favorite aggro spell... get some distance and do it again. Now send your pet after the mob. Have a " scouter " ( preferably a rogue ) tell you when the pet attacks the mob and /pet back off or hold. Wait a sec until your pet runs back to his guard spot and FD. All the mobs will camp you a sec, and the target mob will immediately make a mad dash at your pet. Have your scouter tag and reel him in. Note this works best with bard speed, but isn't impossible with sow / aa depending on the mobs you're trying to pull.
