When I first started EQ I got in a discussion one night in HHK about what class selection says regarding the personality of the player. This came to mind again reading these boards, but in a new way. Within the SK community what does race selection say about the personality of a player?

Dark Elf - Image is very important to this person. Stats may or may not even out, but looking good is where it's at. Generally these people tend to be haughty bandwagonesque posers who try to hard to be macabre and hope all the effort makes them cool.

Erudite - While looks and style are important, it's also important to have a signature style. Brains are more important than brawn, but brawn has it's uses. These people also tend to find foreheads sexually arousing.

Gnome - High energy, curious and often confuse annoying with entertaining.

Human - Experiences conflicting urges to be both average and different. Believes that having no specialized strengths equates with having no specialized weaknesses, and did I mention they are different. The like to blend in to the crowd, but please notice that by doing so they are then different. They like to be different.

Iksar - Attitude, lore, skills and presence are very important to these folks. Too bad you can't tell what gender they are in RL either.

ZarrosLivinglight
For me I love the iksar mentality in general. To play an iksar you need to get into a very alien mindset, as the iksar are not about hate, or fear, or love, or lust, or anything of the sort. The iksar are cold-blooded beings, with little or nothing in the way of an emotional response. Imagine a race of sentient velociraptors, capable of forging an empire, and wielding the necromantic arts, and you're getting close.

Iksar think in terms of "prey" and "iksar", with perhaps the distinction that some prey might make useful slaves before being made food. Unlike the brutish trolls, or the loutish ogres, they have keen minds capable of effciently wielding the arcane magics they will need, and have a good balance in their melee stats. They are the ultimate survivors, and their entire culture is devoted around loyalty to the empire and personal honor. Rogues are expelled and become outcast, and traitors are hunted down ruthlessly. Nothing screams "dark knight" more than the iksar.
