A custom helmet is from the Velious expansion. If you are able to complete a plate quest helmet for Thurgadin, Kael, or Skyshrine factions, you can "customize" your helmet by handing it in to an NPC. The stats don't change, but you get a semi-unique graphic.

As far as I know, I am the only female iksar on my server with this look, so I get a lot of inspects, and I love the attention. :) Whether or not the helmet is actually attractive is up for debate though LOL.
