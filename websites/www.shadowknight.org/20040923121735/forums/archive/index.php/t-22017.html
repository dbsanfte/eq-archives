Syphedius
Having just returned myself after about 1 1/2 years off I can tell you that there are good things about coming back and bad. For one, everyone it seems is lvl 65 or has a 65 alt. Maybe it's just because I'm 65 and you only "hang" with other 65's.

It was lonely at first coming back. The Nexus is fairly empty now. POK (Plane of Knowledge) is bustling with activity and it is much easier to get KEI or Virtue (which is the new Cleric HP buff everyone loves). Just hang out near the POK bank.

The Luclin zones are pretty empty except for the occasional twink getting PL'd by some unknown shamen. The real action for 60+ is in the PoP zones.

You should spend some time doing some of the Planar progression raids. Hang in Plane of Disease or Justice or Storms until you get to lvl 65. That should be your main concern. xp flows much richer than before. I have met tons and tons of people with 300+aa. It's easy to get AA. Just do 3 Adventures and you'll have 2aa.

The LDON expansion is fun if you can get into a good group. It offers good consistent experience and some nice rewards.

I haven't done much with the GoD expansion yet. Not too many people have so you aren't missing much.

The interface has some new features. I recommend you go through the commands reference and the online help.

As for my experience it has been fun to get back into the game but it will again quickly become a timesink.

I looked at your equipment. You may want to buy some plat from one of those online sites and re-outfit yourself in the bazaar unless you have 100k saved up. The amount of good equipment in the bazaar has increased and the prices on a lot of nice armor have come down dramatically. Also, check out the new Dye process. You can now look cool when you are out-dps'ing those silly Pally's.

Good luck.

Speaking as (well, someone who is currently taking a small break from eq but will probably come back soon, to be honest) someone who was actually in the business of rating sk recruits, here's the deal:

Step 1: level 62 and zevfears.
Step 2: run3,whatever 3
Step 3: work on aa untill you have all the tier 1 flags...PoJ, Grummus, Hedge... spend them on defensive aa, cs1 (combat stability), ca1 (combat agility), then nd3(natural durability). Yer gonna be spending buttloads of time in LDON, because that's where sks can still tank without getting laughed at...Speaking of which:
Step 4: level 65
Step 5: Horse, PE, pet control, not necessarily in that order. In fact, I'd recommend pet control first, as that's what allows you to pull singles once again in LDON hards.
Step 6:finish off cs3 and ca3.
Try and snag an sk weapon from the BOT mini's.

At this point, you're now worth looking at by a raiding guild, and you don't go nuts bitching about how lame your tanking stats have become. (level 62 in BOT does NOT cut it). Believe me, going for the super ht before going for defensive aa is an exercise in insanity. You also have at least one peice of decent equipment via ldon.

Once you've gotten this far, while yer apping for yer guild, you can decide whether to go for super ht or just finish off yer defensive aas (lr5 pd3 um, that other defensive aa5) Bottom line, the minimum acceptable defensive stat keeps going up, so yer best bet is to just keep working on them defensive aas.

Good luck.
