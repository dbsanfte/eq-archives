Dug up an old log and found this since I couldn't remember:

A mysterious voice whispers to you, 'Kwilrz Vn`Ycxa in the Cuisine Excelsior has just joined the Wayfarers Brotherhood and has some information about them, and how you can start doing odd jobs for them. You looked like the heroic sort, so I wanted to contact you . . . discreetly.'

After talking to this guy, I eventually ended up hailing Vual Stoutest in Butcher Block.
At this point, I said, "favors," then, "Morden Rasp," then, "farstone magic".

At this point I got the Adventurer's Stone.

ADD: Oh, Kwilrz Vn`Ycxa is in 3rd Gate.
