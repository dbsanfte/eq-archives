The Greeks, after taking the small trojan towns etc arrive at Troy...

At this point Achillies, whose mother dipped him in the river styx (there are a few variations) made him immune to all attacks (except for his heel) is the major warrior. But Agammenon is the overall leader, and remains so bar for a brief period.

The temple of Apollo resides halfway between both armies, and is the major source of fresh water. Both sides consider it neutral ground (this is important for later).

As the war develops...

Patrocklies is killed by Hector, who thinks he is Achillies.

Achillies then kills Hector, for slaying is lover.

Achillies kills the Amazon champion (who is fighting for the Trojens to atone for killing her sister), after he kills her and removes her helmet. He falls in love with her, and kills one of his men who makes some remark about loving a dead body (he slays him with a single punch)...

Achillies sees Polyxana (daughter of the Trojan king) at the Temple of Apollo and falls in love with her.

Trolius steps up to the plate (another son of the King) and starts kicking ass left right and center.

Trolius awaits Achillies in the Temple of apollo, Achillies takes him and kills him in a frenzy of man love.
