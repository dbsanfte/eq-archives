I can honestly say from reading this post that I can understand your reasoning .. though your methodology is flawed.

First .. a SK is just that .. a SK ... not a warrior ... not a necro ... a combination of both classes in order to obtain a class that can tank for exp groups and smaller PvE encounters and a class that can survive on its own with little outside help. While the SK isn't the king of the soloing world, or the tanking world for that matter, they are probably one of the more self sufficient classes in EQ for all aspects of the EQ arena.

At the high end forum of the game as a SK you DON"T want agro. Agro should always remain on the MAIN TANK during a raid encounter. Taking agro from the MT at any given moment would get you killed and more than likely blow the raid. No, a SK's roll at the high end game is definately NOT the roll of a Main Tank, leave that roll to the warriors.

I also wish that the SK's role in the ' LARGE ' PvE arena were extended to give the SK a broader role on raids and such .. but SoE just doesn't see things that way. We are forced to sit back behind a mob, watching a 11k hp warrior slash and hack away ... while the rogues next to us are stabbing for 1.5k ... the wizzies behind us are landing bombs for 5-7k ... rangers bowing away for 500's on nearly every arrow ... while we slowly tinker away with our 200's and 300's and watch nearly all of our spells being resisted. Only to get the opportunity to push 1 button at the very end to release a 9k kilo-ton BOMB that we have to wait another 63 minutes to use again.

Sure while spells like Zevfeers Bite and Aura of Hate / Darkness have their usefulness as well as Voice of T .... these aren't really all that instrumental in the final objectives that guilds are trying to reach toward the ' end game ' . Especially when most of your Aura spells are resisted by the high end mobs you will encounter in the EP's and The Plane of Time.

I always thought about an option of the SK getting a large ' mana feed ' type spell where a SK could ( for example ) feed 1000 pt of mana for a 2k mana cost. Of course, this spell would have to have a HUGE refresh timer on it to keep things balanced and not take away from the content of the game either ( say like 60 minutes ).

This could also be spun into something like a reverse pact of hate ability where a SK could be given the ability to feed 1 pt of mana per every 4 pts of HP sacrificed by the SK but subsequently suffering a loss of life in the process ( no DI type spells can save you from this type of death lets just say for the sake of argument ). So in theory, a SK with 10k hp could give to a fellow cleric 2500 pts of mana ( like a giant ass mod rod ) but you loose your life in the process. This would keep things even since you would subsequently loose the SK for the fight. To keep this from being exploited you would have to put it on the same refresh timer as the Harm Touch so you couldnt just rez them back and let them run in and re-engage to use the Harm Touch 1 minute later.

Another option would be something similiar to the Paladin's LoH but instead of a no cost CH ... it would be like a reverse manaburn ... to say like 1 hp per 3 pts of mana so in theory a SK with a 6k mana pool could heal someone for 2000pt of life and still having the detrimental effect of sacrificing all of the SK's mana for said heal sense everything about a SK is giving up something for something else. This particular ability also having a large refresh timer much like the Harm Touch.

I think you're undead ability would significantly over power the Shadowknight in both PvE and PvP. I can understand you're frustration though. Not being of significant use ( especially on a Blue server ) isn't much fun at all. Good thing I play on a PvP server so my roll in PvP is very important and gives me a very active roll on that side of the EQ coin.

Just my 2cp
