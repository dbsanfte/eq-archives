Jaggnar
I'm not sure what Imgrunk is smoking, but I want some of it. The green mobs at the entrance don't hit for much, but by the time you get down to the mobs that are all dark blue at 55, they are doubling in the 75-100 range and have approximately 4-5k HP each. Even if you're not having spells landed on you, you can run into trouble with adds if you're not careful.

What works for a BST may not work for us SK - our pets are considerably weaker, and the 55 BST has a pet buff that is very effective at stunning/interrupting casters.

Nurga still is excellent XP though - no doubts about it. If you can handle the dispellers, the slows, and can provide healing for yourself that's effective (regen) then you're in good shape!
