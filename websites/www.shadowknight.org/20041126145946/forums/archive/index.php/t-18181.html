Wozan
Defense skill-ups are gained in inverse proportion to your AC. If you have great AC (i.e. twink/PL/3rd toon) you will gain Defense slowly. If you have crustacean shell armor battling yellow mobs you will get Defense skill-ups like mad.

Defense will even out eventually. Don't bother worrying about it. You can't train it with a trainer. The only reliable way to train it is to take off half your armor, grab a healer you trust, and pull yellow mobs until your healer is oom. Defense++
