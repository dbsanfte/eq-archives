The named at the end of sewer #1 would be tough. Vxed and Tipt named are out of the question. You can tank everything in sewer 1 right up until the end, and you can tank everything in sewer 2-4.

You can be successful in GoD if you put the right group together. You'll either need to be the puller, and bring a warrior to tank, or you can bring a ranger for WS tanking on named and a chanter to charm pets.
