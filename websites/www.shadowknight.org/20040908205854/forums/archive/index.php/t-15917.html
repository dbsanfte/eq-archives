Just do the Hole parts of the raid walkthru - kill mimics for a cell key, give cell key to Caradon & kill Kyrenna, give her stuff to the appropriate NPC's, give shroud to Ghost of Glohnor, give the head and wrappings to the right Kastane. Make sure you do all turn ins invis or hidden, check /con first. Enjoy your new BP/Earring.
