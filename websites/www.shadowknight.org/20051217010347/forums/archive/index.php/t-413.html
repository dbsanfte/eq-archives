I'm a 28 DE SK, started playing EQ in Feb 2002. This is my first char...

I cast Grim Aura and Vampiric Embrace before I start. I usually use lifetap to pull a mob and once it's on it's way, I pop off disease cloud. This gets the initial damage going and disease cloud usually forces/keeps agro on me. Once it's in front of me, i hit lifetap to make up for the initial hit it may have gotten on me then I cast siphon strength. While swinging on it, I'll cast lifetap after I get in a bash because sometimes bash will stun the mob. I'll continue with swinging, bashing and lifetapping until the mob is dead - casting disease cloud again if it wears off.

Be careful using DoT spells like engulfing darkness when in groups. Casters cannot mez a mob with a DoT spell on it because the DoT spell breaks the mez right off the bat.

My big pet peev : TAUNT THE MOB BEFORE BREAKING A MEZ!

It's really annoying to have to chase down a mob who is chasing a caster who is running away, sometimes causing more mobs to join in on the battle. It's a big mess that wastes a lot of time, mana and hitpoints that could have been avoided with a few clicks of the taunt button.

Some of this may be basic info, but being a newer player to EQ, I had to find out the hard way with people yelling at me in group chat or in tells sent to me. Hopefully, a newer SK will read this and keep it in their mind.

If you have found better tactics that can be used at this level, please let me know - I'm always open to new ideas for causing mobs to bleed in larger volumes quicker.
