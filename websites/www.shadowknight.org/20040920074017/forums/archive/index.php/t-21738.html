logbash
Can you load up a different character on your account? Might be due to the port EQ is using for the particular zone you're camped in. Alot of internet providers block "commonly used virus ports", just out of practice. Bad thing is, that occasionally, EQ will use one of those ports for packet transfer. ISP may be blocking that port for you. If that's the case, say goodbye to your character until the next patch/server down. Or, you can beg a GM to put you into another zone.
