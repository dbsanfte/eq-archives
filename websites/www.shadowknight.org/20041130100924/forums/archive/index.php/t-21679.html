Vedark
Frontal stuns have gotten more and more frequent as the expansions have come out and levels have passed. Being a darkelf, when I was 50 it was not this way. I'd like to think that is the stick by which to measure how much they intended us to be stunned.

As it is now, the initial hit by any mob is usually a stun. During combat its not unusual to have between 3 - 4 more stuns over the course of a minute or so. I think this is getting just plain out of hand. With the hard hits and number of them in gates, interrupts are already quite numerous. Being stunned constantly is just getting plain annoying. I know I don't play a race that is supposed to have the benefit, but I don't believe the hindrince was intended to be this punishing.

At anyrate, I'd like to compile some information on the topic that we could present to the class balance boards for the retuning that is coming later this decade.
