Caziel
I just got done reading the thread from general discussion about the warriors pissing and moaning about how we need to be nerfed so that they dont suck anymore. In the course of the posts are Voice/hate spells were brought up a bit and that gave me an idea.

This may very well have been posted before so bear with me.

The voice line makes the actions of the recipiant of the spell cause more aggro with his/her actions correct ? Warriors want better aggro control for raids, exp groups, etc. Why not just give warriors an innate "voice" effect for their attacks ? With that innate ability they would be causing more aggro with their attacks then any other classes (straight) melee and would therefore be able to hold aggro as well as was needed in said raid or group. Now then, they may argue that "a knight can still pull aggro off of us" and that may well be true but on a raid where the knight is not the MT why on earth would he want to purposely grab aggro ?

All in all I think this (if possible) would make a happy median for the classes. The warriors can now hold aggro for their coveted tank position on raids or groups. While we can still hold superior aggro skills and have our utility spells.

Warrior: high ac and hp, melee dps, plus can hold aggro with the change.

Knights: Supperior aggro but less hp,ac (as it is now) , with wide array of spells that make us unique.

Voila. Everybody is happy and nobody gets nerfed.

Ideas , suggestions please. :)
