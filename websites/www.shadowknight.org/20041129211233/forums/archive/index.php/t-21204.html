This is a repost from some ideas I posted on another thread here. I got no feedback so I thought to try it here ( I guess I should post in the right forum huh?). Some of my ideas are a little different but maybe different is the solution we need.

Here are some ideas I've had. Some good some maybe not.

-A single target mez. I know, were not chanters. But make it with a high recycle time so the SK isn't mezzing trains and zero memblur so that the SK will have aggro when it expires.

-Slow. Yet again were not chanters or shaman. A slow that is not as effective as shaman or chanter but make one that stacks with theirs. Maybe make it a tap to let us add to our DPS like our AC/ATK/Sta taps

-Drain HT. An AA that on command will add half the HT's damage to your HP kinda like a heal.

-Fix spell aggro. I know this sounds nuts but this would get a big warrior bitch off our backs. However improve our terror line of spells so that we use that to get aggro. After all why were they put into the game in the first place?

-Damage aura. Instead of giving SK's defensive. Give SK's an ability to reflect melee damage or a portion of it back to the attacker. Sort of like an ultra high powered damage shield. The SK would still take the damage and need healing but would be doing more damage to the mob. This might be ideal in the case of SK ramp tanks taking over in case the MT falls, the extra damage would create the needed short term aggro to keep healers and high end DPS from getting summoned and eaten.

-Animate shadow. A triggered AA ability similar to the pet swarm AA's that are out there. This would create a shadow duplicate of the SK that can fight for a brief period of time. The emote would be "Soandso's shadow comes to life". It would have similar raw stats to the summoner. If the SK has a pet already it is destroyed. Duration would be 30secs to one minute tops. Think of Grom Hellscream's power in Warcraft 3.

I admit some if these ideas may not be the best but maybe something different is in order. We're not getting defensive. We're not going to get new spells until the next expansion (if there is one). We do not have the numbers to even some close to causing the uproar that warriors did. Hopefully warriors won't be fighting tooth an nail against us like they have been in getting these changes.
