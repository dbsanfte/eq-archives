Drakonn
No real need to joust a rogue... hit em with spells and keep at a distance, and make sure to have all your damage-shield spells / right clicks up.

Make sure you keep facing them so they can't get a full backstab in - Chaotic Stab makes them harder in duels but a full damage backstab is a lot more dangerous.

In general it's very hard for a SK to lose to a rogue unless the rogue is much better equipped than you.

Which spells depend on their gear - usually you can tell by their guildtag or reputation... if you know their resists are good, just use low resist spells (ToI)... if you know they aren't uberly equipped, try to land a snare... once snared charge in and melee the hell out of them.
