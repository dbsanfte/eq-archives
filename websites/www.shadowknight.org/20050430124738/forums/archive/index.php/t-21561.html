Ramgar SK
I hate to knock something stickied on this great board, but that hunting grounds list needs some serious updates. Many zones have been revamped, Nurga and Droga have changed in level quite dramatically. Veksar, Nedaria's Landing, and all of GoD is not even on the list.

The best exp 56+ is grouping in PoP. Soloing at this level is painfully slow. Try PoJ exit groups, PoI exit group, and Nightmare waterfall groups.

Try the flies in PoD. Depending on your gear, you should be able to fear kite these from the safe rock. Watch out for the rats in the water. Keep up bobbing corpse if you need to truck it to the zone. Great exp though 61.

Nadox Shrine Bruisers are great, single pullable. But geting there is a pain now that you can't run though the big trapdoor, go though Dulak or bring a druid for succor. /flip directX9.

Natimbi is good for fear kiting, they have low hp.
