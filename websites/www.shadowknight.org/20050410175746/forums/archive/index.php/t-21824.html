tanadil
My guild did a Ldon raid last night.

To put in perspective we are a medium sized guild, doing lower tier Pop atm. Many equiped uto that level and with some decent Ldon gear all round.

We chose MM (normal risk).

We went in with 28 people all lvl 65. We had 4 clerics, 3 tank types(inc me), 1 bard, 1 monk and 3 chanters with the rest made up of DPs types.

Despite the warnings from most evry forum I read, it actually wasnt too bad at all.

The trash mobs hit hardish (ldon hard standard) with some of them rampaging!
The mini bosses hit very hard indeed. But a CCH of 3 folks on the tank was OK. However we all reacted too slow to enrage at one point and ended up with a pile of dead people around the foot of the miniboss:P

The end named's were OK, but there is a nice twist we which we figured too late and ran out of time:) But having worked out the script now, we will go back and complete it soon.

Loot was non existent.

However made a different change to the usual raids, and I would agree that its a great training ground for raiding.
