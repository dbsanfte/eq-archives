well, i don't know of any "neat package" these boards are about as close as you come... for example taunting a mezzed mob is easy (these spells depend on loeve) at level 10 str tap, start casting disease cloud and hit attack while it's casting (that way you'll hit it as soon as the spell goes off for that extra bit of agro) at 55 i do shadow vortex, shroud of hate, high level str tap and attack (this can be shortened depending on how many times the mobs been mezzed, and how good a chanter you have) it's really just something you pick up lol not much advice on PvP from me, only ever dueled about 20 times
