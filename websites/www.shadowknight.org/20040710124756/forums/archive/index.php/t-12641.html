Not to go off on a tangent, but someone asked where Jupke got those numbers from. What I think Jupke was trying to explain was more how SCF in general works then specific to the UT/SCF abilities.

Without UT you can crit HT for:

SCF1 - 2% chance to crit - 33% more damage
SCF2 - 4% chance to crit - 66% more damage
SCF3 - 7% chance to crit - 100% more damage

With one level of UT trained you will ALWAYS crit HT if you have SCF trained. The damage dealt will of course scale depending on what you SCF is.
