Rhumba_Kween
If I were you:

1) Get level one of Spell casting Expertise, if you use Fear or Clinging Darkness for aggro. No more fizzles on those is very nice and cheap. No real need to go past level 1 though
2) Weapon Affinity is real nice, and although it hurts self procs. It does not hurt them badly, and certainly makes up for it in increased weapon proc rate. This one gets too much of a bad wrap, although, I still would not reccommend it till well past 300 AA's.
3) After that is done, and you do Swift Journey, I would start on the Leech Touch line. I'm working on it now, and although I use it seldom, it has bailed me out occasionally. Super over priced for what you get, but still it is useful occasionally. I'd put it in that 400 aa range.

Other than that you are at the same point I am. I think you have Pet Crits (which seems worthless to me for the cost), and a couple level of Damage shields (which I may get after I finish up the leech touches).

Good luck,
