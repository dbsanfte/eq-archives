Hey all.

Read this if you wanna to ensure a guaranteed single pull of a mob out of a mob group to solo it out..

We know that we can have 100% mem wipe from FD if we wait for 2 minutes. We know that our festering darkness last just 2 minutes as well. Everytime if you wanna snare pull a named out of a room with many mobs and FD. Rest will go back but snared one would stay for 2 minutes but snare usually goes off before mem wipe occurs. Usually, mob goes back fast after snare goes off and you would probably redo it over again.

A trick to fix that.. Get Extended Torment IV. It adds nice 12 seconds to your darkness. Voila! A easy guaranteed single pull everytime after 2 minutes as long as darkness sticks on mob you are pulling. ;)

Old method is to snare pull then FD several times trying to shake off aggro on adds. It worked well so far but sometimes takes a while to accomplish it. Extended torment IV is lazy way and ensures 100% success on single pull if you just wait 2 minutes which can be very handy in some situations. :)

I just realized about it when I struggled to split the camp full of so many mobs in very close space of othe room that I pulled into and snare kept going off before full mem wipe occurs that sucked. That made me wished for longer darkness duration to make it work alot easier. Then it was how I realized about usefulness of extended torment IV. Hehe.


Just thought to share some new tips. ;)
