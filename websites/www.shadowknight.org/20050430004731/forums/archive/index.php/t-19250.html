Paw is listed (Splitpaw Lair). However, this list was before some additional patches that inreased some of the zones even more. This was on a related thread at the crucible....

Droga increased by 12%
Nurga increased by 12%
Solusek's Eye (SolA) increased by 13%
Najena increased by 13%
Befallen increased by 13%
Paw increased by 13%
Permafrost increased by 13%
Kaesora increased by 18%
Qeynos Catacombs increased by 20%
Runnyeye increased by 20%
Kerra Ridge increased by 20%
The Hole increased by 25%

I believe you would add these to the original ones posted on the above list. The Hole was not listed, but to my memory it is a 25% ZEM.

Another thing to consider is that there is also additional modifiers for additional people (grouping), which I believe adds 10% for a 2-box team, and up to 80% for a full group of six people. After group modifiers were added, it took away some of the incentive for soloing.
