Dark Elves - high starting int, ultravision
Erudite - High starting int
Gnome - High starting int, infravision, tinkering
Human - Average all around stats
Iksar - AC bonus, increased regen, infravision
Ogre - High melee stats, unstunable by melee attacks from the front, infravision, slam
Troll - high starting melee stats, increased regen, infravision, slam

No stat is really all that hard to raise, regen 60+ is moot, vision is easily corrected with spells/items. The only things that can't be compensated for with spells and gear is ogre no frontal stunn, and Iksar AC bonus.
