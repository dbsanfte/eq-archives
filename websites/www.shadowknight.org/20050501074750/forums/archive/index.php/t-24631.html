Rhumba_Kween
My favorite spots before BoT were:

1) Plane of Storms - In the desert area there are various mobs. All easier than those frogs. The thing you really want here though is the medals off of the Diaku. After you collect 4 of these medals (or whatever they are) you can alternate flag one full group for Tactics, which is a great XP spot at 64-65.

2) Plane of Valor - Either Caves or Other side of zone from zone in are great xp. The xp in Valor is a bit better if memory serves, but both are great spots and a nice step up from PoN.

3) You might also try Gargoyles in PoN. They were my favorite spot until we moved to Storms. You find these around the castle area. They are simmilar xp to Goblins, but seem lots easier.
