the OT hammer as it is usually referred to. the quest is the new worker, and all you have to do to complete the quest is give a jade to the foreman. the problem is that without faction work, you don't have the needed amiable faction to complete the quest.

to get your faction up you can either:

1. kill Kunark dragons.

2. kill sarnak berserkers. collect their no drop braids, and hand them four at a time to captain grimrotte. usually takes about 88-92 braids.

pain in the ass, so i suggest doing it at a lower level where you still get xp for it, since the hammer itself doesn't proc till 50.
