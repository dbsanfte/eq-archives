Xikuliz Litecleaver
Echo Caves, off Shadowhaven on Luclin is the place to be at 49. Kill the hell out of gnomes. Kill the 4 guards, theres plenty of room to fear kite, they path well. Leave the casters on the right alone, they're annoying. The next mob is in the upper room on the right, kill him. Then farther on the back right wall, closest to you, is a rogue mob, fear kite him as usual. The spawn time is 20 minutes to so, and you'll establish a good pace after a couple runs.

The King of Above and King of Below are tricky. First you have to know the zone, which isn't hard. It's not echo caves where the evil trainers are, it's another entrance and place altogether. It the one that leads to the deep if you wanna check eqatlas. I've never killed the king of above, but to split them you have to pull the king of below (you know this because he'll scowl at gather shadows, when your faction craps out). Pull with dooming darkness, and run like hell. A cleric, the other king, and 2 other melees higher level than the guards will join in. Run to the Deep with the king of above not far behind. Try to zone when he's in the middle of the big chamber so people like me can avoid him. Run back and you'll see the King of Below sitting whereever he was when you zoned in. The Kings don't move from where they deaggro. If you try to split with FD, you drop, and they sit down until you get back up again. If you /q you'll log on and they'll be right there unless there is a patch. I'm not sure if the king of below is fearable, but he's snarable. You'd have to try to straight tank him, and i'd wait til 55 so you can DS yourself.

Anyway, at 49 the levels flew til 52 here. Echo caves rocks. Generally feared mobs flee toward the zone out to Shadowhaven.
