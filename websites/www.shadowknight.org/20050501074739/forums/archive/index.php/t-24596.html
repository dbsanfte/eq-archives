I'm gonna focus on the Shaman gear for the simple reason that you'll be able to do much better if the shaman is better off. OoW and DoN have a ton of SK/Shaman duoable things.

1st rule of shaman gear is that HP>all, especially with Cann 5. HP = living and HP = Mana.

Strategy: Look at all the shaman peice and replace everything with NO direct HP, then replace everything under 25HP, then raise the bar to 40 HP and so on until all items have good HP.

Sta doesn't matter too much, because he should be able to max sta with self buffs (PE + FoS or, worse case scenarios Altuna + IoS + Boar + PE)

You're good on wis and should hit max easy in bear form with or without crack(bear form also gives good regen and again since HP = Mana for a shaman both HP regen and Mana regen help).

Problems:
Back: that lodi has no HP, moss covered (cape or cloak) OR Iksar would be better back item.
Range: What you have is good for buffing but needs to be switched out when not buffing - Engraved War Totem is about what you'll need
Wrists: Rust Encrusted has NO HP, ans Spiritcharmer is really low HP, get pre-DoN cultural (Artificer's ringmail for Barb's) for both slots
Neck: No HP, Watchman's Wrought-Iron Neckband should be cheap

Another thing that would help:
Head: get a Wallwalker's helm (should be about 1K)

You'll be surprised at how much more effective this makes the shaman and opens up your possibilities for exping.
