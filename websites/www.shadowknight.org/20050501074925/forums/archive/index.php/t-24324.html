Klotar AngedeMorts
I'll go out on a limb and possibly suggest 'Death Comes Swiftly' mission in the Ascent. Disclaimer: I don't 4-box, so I don't know first-hand but have done ~20 of these missions grouped with others (w/my boxed dru or clr) so might have some helpful info/advice.

First, it's an outdoor zone, so mounts do work here (along with FoE, etc.). Secondly, and perhaps more significantly for your setup, because it's an outoor zone, your druid can harmony using the 67 Nature's Serenity which will allow your SK to single pull. There is a 'boss' at the end of the mission which is triggered by killing 10 weaver spiders and 5 warder spiders. This boss doesn't hit overly hard as boss mobs go, but has a nasty AE dot -- the problem possibly here being that it's the other group member's health that you have to watch. Spirit of the Wood/Grove really helps here, as does Pureblood (LoY dru/clr quest spell but shm version is group -- shm will need to cast twice to cure entire group).
