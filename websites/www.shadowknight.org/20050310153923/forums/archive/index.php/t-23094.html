Avengent
Fastest way i know of to get to 100 in Tailoring is

Farm Crystalline Silk form Crystal Cavers (or buy the silk in the bazaar if you can) Gonna need 2 10 slot bag fulls. With those make swatches (2 crystalline silks unstacked in sewing kit = 1 crystalline silk swatch)

From Thurgadin buy shoulderpad patterns (going to need about a 10 slot bag full) And Silver Thread also from thurgadin (going to need about a 10 slot bag full as well)

Combine 1 swatch + 1 thread + 1 pattern = Crystaline silk mantle. Trivials out at 118 and if your int is maxed it goes really fast.

spent about 200 plat on the silver threads, 20 plat per stack. The patterns were inconsiquential as far as money goes.
