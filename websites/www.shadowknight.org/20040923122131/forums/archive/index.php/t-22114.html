The details of the raid are sorta sketchy, but a friend of mine who had moved to Veeshan several months back reported that his guild fielded 49 people to give it a shot. A drake outside VP has several guards around him, they killed all the guards (guardian wurms I think) and the drake becomes targetable. Hail him and he has you run to each of the 4 dragon statues in Skyfire and has someone click on them, he also gives each hailer a 4 slot tome for a combine for the key. Once this is done a Named will spawn somewhere in Skyfire. When hes killed, he drops 48 dragons bloods that supposedly are in chests. One dragons blood and your old VP key = augment and new key. As far as the new key I dunno, Kiz had his old one. From some of the stuff he linked, it seems very on par with elemental level loot, the raid force didnt wipe once inside, but it did struggle to do the named, apparently hes pretty tough.
There is a thread on it on the shaman page, has some good links to other sites that are working out the details.
