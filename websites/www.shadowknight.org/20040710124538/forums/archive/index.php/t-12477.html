Just another update. Alan had asked me on Mobhunter to email him the details of the problem. I sent the following letter to Alan, straight copy/paste follows:

Hi Alan. I am the fellow who posted up on Mobhunter concerning Comatose. I am a long time player of EQ and have a 57 SK main and a 39 Wiz alt.

Comatose is the only LoY quest spell which remains unsolved more than a month after LoY was released.

The pieces in general for both Blood of Pain and Comatose are insanely rare. How rare? Killing a massive number of luggalds in the hidden cave in Dulak (where this stuff drops) with two 65 wizards for 6+ hours yielded zero drops. Some people have spent literally weeks killing in all the LoY zones and still do not have the drops for either spell. I don't believe doubling is enough. This quest should be brought more in line with the other quest spells in difficulty.

Blood of Pain, while ridiculously time consuming, has been solved.

For Comatose, the NPC mentions that he needs Luggald Blood, Bile and Saliva. I personally have the Luggald Blood, so I know that exists. I have seen reports of the Luggald Bile dropping as well. I have not been able to find a single report of Luggald Saliva EVER dropping since LoY was released. And trust me, I have done extensive leg work trying to find it. Posting everywhere, checking every SK and Necro board, asking repeatedly in both serverwide.sk and serverwide.necro, asking every Necro and SK I know. The drop does not exist.

So, in summary, Luggald Organs, Luggald Bile, and Luggald Blood need DRASTIC drop rate increases. Significantly more than double. It should not take a few days /played to obtain a 44 or 52 spell. Luggald Saliva needs to be added to the drop table of some mobs as it clearly is not in the game at the moment. Surely the combined efforts of every Necro and SK serverwide would have produced a single confirmed drop if it existed.

Realize that there are a fair amount of people out there who are pretty angry about this. For many SKs (who are pullers), including me, this spell was one of the chief reasons they bought LoY. It is a massive pulling upgrade for the 52-59 SK. Having it still be MIA a month+ later is quite upsetting.

Thank you in advance for your help.

P.S. As an entirely different subject, are you aware that SKs are the only class in the game who needs a different class to make certain spells for them as their ONLY way to get the spell? I am speaking concerning Necro research only spells (Harmshield, Bond of Death, others). Every other class can get every single spell via their own research, drop, PoP Quest, or vendor. Even the new Int-caster research for priests is an alternate way of getting spells which can otherwise be gotten via drop. Please add SK only versions of all SK spells which are currently Necro research only to some vendors.
