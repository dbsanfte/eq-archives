This is an idea I have about "rebreathing" life into epics (I consider the Greenmist as an "epic weapon")

Let's face it, anymore getting someone's epic seems a bit trivial, maybe not in the effort, but at least in it's value. Truely as it stands the only epic that is really and truely "worth" getting is the cleric epic. All other epics eventually get bagged in some way or another, which to me seems wrong to do with a weapon that is suposed to make you one of the most powerful people of your class.

So, on to the idea:

First, I would add 3 more augment slots to epics (which would include the Greenmist, and possibly other classes weapons that are similiar to the Greenmist). Now, the first slot would stay the same, a type 4 for a proc augment (this way nothing is changed if you decide to not do the quest(s) ) The other 3 slots would a type that is epic only (like type 8 are time only). The "epic only" augments that could be placed into these slots would all be quested, and there would be 2 types of them.

1) General epic augments: There would be quests to get general epic augments, ones that can be added to any epic of any class. The would be similiar things to the type 2 augments on LDoN merchants, just general stat/utility augments of some type, add damage to weapons, lower delay, add stats, etc.

2) Class specific augments: These would be class specific types much like the effects that are curently on epics. These would range from added damage types, procs, clicky effects, skill mods, etc. All very class specific things.


Also, the quest should be semi-epic like, or even epic like, taking anywhere from a whole group to a raid to get some of the pieces. Obviously, the general augments would be easier, maybe even letting some of the weaker ones be soloable, and the class specific ones be more full group/raid oriented.

Inaddition, there could be special solvent that will take off the augment without destroying it. This could either be done is a kit you could carry around, which could let you change your aug to the situation (nothing is worse that spending points on a cold based DD and then find yourself fighting cold immune NPCs). That might, depending on the types of augments you could collect, be overpowering, so maybe just make it so you can only change the aug at the augment sealer in the camps maybe.

Also, I know this puts the total slots on an epic to 4, which is more than any other item that is in the game, but then these are special, extremly magical and powerful weapons we're talking about. There would also be enough of the quests that each epic could basically be unique, or at least enough that there wouldn't be the same 3 quests everyone would do. So that instead you can pick which 3 augments you would want on it, and not be forced to do a specific quests for an augment you might not want. (i.e. you can still fill the 3 augment places while not *having* to get augments you might not want)

Well, it's just an idea and I thought I'd share and see what people think about it (and maybe point out flaws in it that I don't see atm)
