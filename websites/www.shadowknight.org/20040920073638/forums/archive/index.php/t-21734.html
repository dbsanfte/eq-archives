Groff Gorespiller
It's an easy quest. I did it solo (and drunk) in about an hour at 65; two or three 50-ish toons should be able to pull it off fine. Come into the furthest room in the Luggald tunnels (room with a door) and turn left, go to the corner, set up camp there. Pull left to right around the room, sweeping through the middle as you get the mobs cleared out. Simple FD splits, mobs on the other side of the door don't seem to aggro.

The only thing I'd offer as advice is make sure that you have a slower and that you keep snare on. They hit a little fast if you're lower level and your gear isn't excellent.
