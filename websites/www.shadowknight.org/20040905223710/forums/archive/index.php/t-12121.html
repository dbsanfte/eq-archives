Wedge
I was just reading through the latest incarnation of the "do gnome SK's suck?" thread, and it got me thinking. If they were going to introduce another race/class combination (not another new race or class, mind you, just added another race to the list that can play a certain class), what would you want to see, and why?

I'm not looking for comedic value here, although I'm sure some people will simply post the goofiest thing they can think of, but I'd prefer if people thought up some things that made some RP sense.

My favorite?

Iksar Rogue

Something about this is just really attractive. They have decent rogue stats, as well as regen, AC bonus, etc., but that's not the real reason.

The Iksar don't like rogues, hence the "Forsaken" mobs outside Cabilis, all the outcast rogues. They could start you in some crappy corner of Cabilis, or in the tunnels, and give you apprehensive faction to all of Cab, and have some merchants not sell to you.

I've had some friends tell me that Iksar just wouldn't become rogues, since it's so frowned upon, but think about it: can't you see someone being part of an isolated race that's hated by the rest of the world, and thus becoming all bitter and hateful and turning to theft and murder as a way of taking out his aggression?

What do you guys think? What would you like to see?

Ok...I thought about this a little bit during practice. (I have a lot of time to think then) and here it is:

Mancers: Hybrids of the Magi class each specializing in one aspect of the Magi's job

Pyromancer: Attuned to the world of fire. Able to use swords and axes (All these mancers will be able to they are melee and get watered down pets and spells but they work). Races: High Elf, Erudite, Dark Elf, Human. Born ability is Fireball which is the classic "BURN!" attack.

Geomancer: Attuned to the world of earth. Yes your thinking of a druid but no. These are melee casters who can only summon earth pets (Like the pyro can only summon fire pets) they get crippling earth magic like the druid but also get powerful earth dd spells. Races High Elf, Erudite, Human, Dark Elf. Born ability would be to manipulate surroudings which would take anything around them and use it to do massive dmg to the enemy

Aquamancer: Attuned to the world of water. They will get minor healing spells because water is the symbol of health and purity and they get water pets. Get severe water DD's and choke spells. Races: same as above. Born ability would be to evelop in water and slows movement and does a DoT depending on level and AA's.

Aeromancer: Attuned to the world of air. Utility hybrid with spells that levitate and throw things and hit with all manner of air. Get choke spells and minor illusion spells. Air pets. Same races as above. Evac spells, Born ability would be a move like Whirl Till You Hurl only with massive results.

All offshoots of the magi class and can be roleplayed well, just played well, and have some interesting side effects in groups. I think it would be a great class. They could wear up to chain mail armor and are meant only for damage not for taking it. (A lot like rangers in a way). Would be interesting to see if they could solo and all. Dunno, you decide
