Winkles, think about it...

If you were full health, every time LC healed you, you'd be healing whatever the guard damaged you for. He hits you for 53, then you whack him for 160 - Net result? You get healed for 53. In other words, if you're at 4500/5000 hps and LC, once you hit 5000/5000 hps, it'll look like the disc shut itself off. If you get pinged for 1 damage, you'll see a message saying you got healed for one damage (not the amount of your swing) as soon as you hit something.
