With dots that doesnt happen apparently.. Instead, look at the line saying how much your dot does.. something like "Your festering darkness hits %t for 50 damage"..

I have crit dots 3 on my shaman. Not very ego satisfying to not get a pretty message on your DOTs, but oh well.

Still nice to have. And for what it's worth to SK's, I and other shamans I've noticed talking about it DO seem to notice that we get more aggro with crits on our DOTs than we normally did before we had DOT crits AA's. Enough so that when stacking DOTs on mobs at tables in Plane of Fire, I can occasionally pull aggro off necros who have been running around kiting the mobs with snare for a while and stacking one more DOT than I cast -- and their DOTs are better. That's a lot of aggro to overcome. And the crits on my DOTs aren't even very high.

So ... you'll definitely get a little more DPS and a lot more aggro when you get critical affliction AA's. Any SK at the point where he's starting to think about putting AA into critical affliction will probably not have been worrying about trying to get more aggro for a long, long time. It's sorta OK for DPS though. Don't think I'd put it very high at all on the list for most SK's.
