The week before the VP upgrade, I cleared VP with a few guys and know for sure my faction was max KoS RoS when I learned about the new quests.

Solution? AFK pet factioning.

Your 64 pet is level 41 and with mage summoned items and weapons will be able to solo any of the 6 to 10 spawns in MM without buffs or haste. If you have a stable connection you can simply stand in an out of the way location MM before going to sleep at night with low risk of trains. The mobs will agro on you when they spawn and then your pet will kill them. Maxing RoS faction was longer than any other faction I've ever done this with - took 7 days of afk pet factioning to get max ally RoS this way. Getting ally Chardok from max kos took only about five days standing outside Droga. Getting highest possible Cabalis faction (including Iksar necro faction) for Greenmist only took about 4 days. This is just something very easy to do when you aren't doing something else on your character. I've completed a few of the VP quests now, the effort was very much worth it.
