There is lots of misinformation about on this subject.

Yes, the quest was previously possible using clay golem potions. The potions boosted your existing faction a bunch, but even so, you could be so KOS that it wouldn't work. For instance, I knew dark elves who got too KoS hunting in HS to regain their faction using the potions (ie, even in ee form, they were still kos). It all depends on what faction hits you have taken. To illustrate the boost of the ct potion, I was able to raise my faction, still was scowling normally, but with the form, I was amaible, so I got my OT hammer when I normally was scowled at.

Iksar start out only lightly kos, but it takes between 280-300whips to get from scowling to dubious. 280ish if you haven't taken any neg outpost faction hits, while 300, which is around what it took me, seemed to cover a couple times in kaesora (not something like farming spiders though, since they primarily give the hits I believe).

I believe that the necro forms won't work. I can't confirm them, other than by 2nd hand info, which I happen to have heard from a number of reliable sources. The only other way to do it would be to charm the captain, or if you have sneak, as is the case with monks, you can do the sneaking while partner hails trick to turn in whips.

If verant puts the clay golems in feerot and has them drop the earth elemental potions (I heard they no longer reside in CT), or if you can somehow find 4 or 5 of them (if you load your entire bank with whips and your person, maybe only 2 potions or so would be needed. It took me 5 potions, handing in 76~whips per time), then it is doable. If not, then I don't see how nonmonk iksar will ever be able to get faction in the outpost anymore, which also means no hammer. In theory, if all you want is the hammer, and you haven't had any neg faction hits, then 1 potions might be enough to get the hammer if you load up your person and bank to the brim with whips.
