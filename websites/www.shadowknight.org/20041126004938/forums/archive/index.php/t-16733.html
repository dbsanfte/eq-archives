Basically my point is the HP differential arguement is a non arguement Pre VT levels. Equally equiped warriors enjoy a 900 hp advantage over knights, which equates to a 20% adavantage in HP. The major problem is the HP margin stay relatively the same (read on FoH boards that the differential of Wars vs knights hp is about 800) but the advent of all/all 100+ hp items reduces warrior hp advantage to something that was sizible (20%) to something thats marginal (10%). All in all HPs between knights and warriors seemingly stay very balanced up to VT level.

The purpose of this analysis was to show while at the ends of the game Warriors may have an arguement about a diminished tanking advantage over knights because of small HP differential, they still enjoy a fairly massive one for almost the rest of the game.

So if you see any under VT level war whining about HP differential, tell them to STFU.
