Voice spells are aggro modifiers.

Voice of Terris is 10% aggro modifier to the person you cast it on.

Voice of Thule is 12%.

I have a hotkey specially made to keep Voice of Thule on a shaman friend during guild raids, last Fennin raid I counted 5 deaths before he sent me a tell begging me for mercy :D
Brael
Not quite 12%, it divides the aggro mod by a decimal when they converted how some stuff works. what was previously *1.12 is now /.88. While it's still close to 12% it's actually a bit higher in some cases. As far as melee goes (spells I havent figured out exactly yet, but it should be similar) SK's are at .7 aggro mod with subtlety, .8 with subtlety+voice, .82 with nothing, .93 with voice and no scs. If it was still *1.12 it would be .7, .78, .82, and .92 it's not much of a difference but its something, basically 13.63% aggro increase instead of 12%. Aggro increasers got better from that change aggro decreasers got worse.
