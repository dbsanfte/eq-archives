Resists max at 500, and Touch of Innoruuk has a -500 MR modifier, meaning that the paladin's effective MR would be 0 when making a resist check against this lifetap. I PvP many times every day and I have never had full or partial resists on Touch of Innoruuk. The only thing is that enchanters and other people with a damage absorb shield will indeed take the damage (which will be absorbed) but you will not be healed from the spell.
