First of all, it's an 8 point difference in Intelligence, not 15.
117 - 109 = 8. That's the math you're talking about.

Let's try a different approach. Spend 8 of the Dark Elf's bonus points on Int, and the rest on melee skills. Put all 20 of the Erudite's points into melee skills. The Dark Elf now has a 12 point melee advantage, and even Intelligence.

A 3rd way is spend both race's 20 points all on Int. The Erudite would have 8 more points of Int, but the Dark Elf would have 20 more points in melee skills.

My math gives the edge to the Dark Elves. But no matter whose math you use, the differences are so small (especially after you factor in gear) that it's ludicrous for you to bash others for choosing a race that you didn't.
