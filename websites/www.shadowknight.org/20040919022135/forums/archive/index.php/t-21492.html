Yes. It increases the damage done by 0-20% and as a result, it increases the amount you are healed because of the way the lifetap works. The damage you do with it is how much you are healed.

ID does increase the amount Zeefer's Bite does, but does not increase the mana gained.

ID does not increase weapon proc damage, nor the damage done with self buff procs such as Mental Clarity or Shroud of Chaos.

Gash
Anger of Druzzil only works on Magic-based direct damage spells. There's no "chance" for extra damage; extra damage is guaranteed. HOW MUCH extra damage is random.

Improved Damage IV works on all damaged based spells, including Improved Harmtouch.

So, you may want to use Anger for your lifetaps, and ID IV for Spears and undead nukes.

Hope that clears it up.
