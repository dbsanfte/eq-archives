Plubius
I still believe an ability that truncates the damage distribution is better than a defensive copy with a lower mitigation percentage (though I am a big fan of the short time burst defensive suggested by slein).

IMO, the big problem with GoD is that they specifically designed encounters with SL/HL in mind. Instead of designing damage/hp ratios for 10 second heals, they designed them for the new, faster heals. This has had the unintended side affect of making the defensive ability much more important from a survivability standpoint because the original U shaped DI distribution has less random draws to average damage over.

Having a disc that truncates the damage distribution gives us an ability that has the same limiting distribution as defensive (ie same as defensive at extremely high AC) but is siginifcantly worse in other situations (~40% the damage mitigation in most situations). The advanatages are as follows:

1) The scaling of the truncated distribtution would be the same as defensive - thus there would be no balance issue in the next expansion.
2) Mobs could be adjusted in a way that allows high end SKs and Warriors to win without elemental warriors winning (large DI, low Attk). Mobs could also be adjusted to make warriors superior to SKs (Medium DI, high attk)
3) It is not strong in the lower end game - thus it wouldn't have the same unbalancing issues as a mitigation disc.
