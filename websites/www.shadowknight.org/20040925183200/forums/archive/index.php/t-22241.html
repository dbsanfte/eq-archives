the problem isn't tanking, it's perception. joseph goebbles said it best: "a lie repeated often enough will eventually become the new truth." sk's can't tank as well as war's, but they were never meant to. sk's tank marginally worse than paladins on stunnable mobs as a trade off for increased dps. personally, i haven't ever been told i can't tank because of my class.

to be honest, i have had problems tanking in the past, but that was almost always due to either trying new zones with mainly red mobs (hello PoV at level 55) or a gear deficiency (hello BoT with 3.5k unbuffed).

at the level of progression i am at, as a bazaar/ldon sk progressing through PoP, i feel that tanking isn't a problem at all given that you are appropriately geared. if siilvir is being honest about tanking at the high-end, then i'm not sure what the "tanking problem" is. i know the squeaky wheel gets the oil, but running around telling all the other classes that you "can't tank" isn't very productive in my opinion. leave those complaints to the official class board or the sk.org suggestions forum. soe is slow to fix, but other classes aren't going to help "fix" us, as they have their own issues to deal with.

i can see, however, that the tanking heirarchy could use some tweaking, but if that is the case then i think it should be a global-type change to mitgation tables. a mini-defensive as some have suggested would be a nice toy, but that alone without any sort of class damage calculation adjustment would be missing the mark (and not to mention infuriate those greedy warriors).

the most pressing issue i deal with personally with sk's is that agro is fugged. the fact that no truly informed sk's use terror's unless given no other option is ridiculous. also, i havent so much as looked at a voice spell in months. why am i using level 15 spells for agro? i see that as a high priority fix. i don't care if i can't tank as well as a war as long as i can get the mob to hate me more on demand.

maybe i just play on a server where people aren't so ignorant as to blindly believe what they're told without experience, but damn, some people need to get a grip.
