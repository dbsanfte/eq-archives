The Community Team would like to take a moment to thank everyone who applied to be a correspondent. We received many wonderful applications and the decision was very difficult, but the decisions have been made. Should the need arise in the future for new correspondents we will ask for your applications at that time.



Before announcing who was chosen to be the shadowknights correspondent, I would like to tell you a little bit about what we see as the role of a correspondent. The role of a correspondent will be to help us gather information; unfortunately we may miss posts on each board so these folks will be our extra eyes and bring posts we may have missed to our attention. They will float posts that they feel the community should comment on, and we may also rely on them to start posts to gather information, and to bring posts regarding bugs to our attention. Each correspondent will have access to a correspondent only board to ask questions, have discussions and bring information to our attention.



Now without further ado, please join me in welcoming Brael as the correspondent for the shadowknights.
