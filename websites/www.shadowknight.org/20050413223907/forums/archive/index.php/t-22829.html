Scabben
In a nutshell:

Kill spiders for 5-10 minutes... do this however you want.

At the end of those minutes, blur any spiders and let them pass.

Prep your raid force for the fight:

Everyone at east door.
Healers are to the left as you face the door. They are to hide around the little jutted thing there. They need to pin themselves in that jut as close as possible to the door.

Take your enchanters and spread them back out to CC spiders.

MT Group:

Tank 1, 2, 3, bard, healer healer (should be suffice np)

Healer group:

Healerx5, bard (a bst for paragon if you have one lacking MGB works in here also)

Add a healer to all other groups if available.. use pallies, etc for group type heals.

MT goes in (or weaponshield ranger works, along w/ a pally w/ pacification -- don't use DA). MT pulls to inside left corner (from east door), generates his agro (use an SK to build if you're worried). Raid force follows him in after like... 6 seconds or so, burns down Mr MB -- fire off ANY AE heals ... if 2 can go at once, make sure your raid party knows to have like 3-4 buff slots open for heals/mb ae dot effects.

Did I mention burn? Yes.. you wanna be full dps on him... which means tank needs damn good agro because if MB turns.. you're only hurting your efforts.

Once he is dead, clear out the room. Find a monk to pull Giwin to raid. As long as you keep Giwin agro'd.. he'll last as long as 30 minutes for you (which allows for things Sony wouldn't like). Enchanters blur and let their spiders go back into room. Once the dust settles and everyone is rezzed, get a link on loots, assign them, and keep an eye on spiders, and let people in to loot.

Not really all that hard.. just pay attention -- doable w/ 24 no problem.

As an alternate strategy,

- Set kill spider teams at each of the 4 corners leading to the MB room. These will consist of Knights, Beasts, Shamans, Monks, Rogues + a few others (chanters included) who can neither heal effectively nor range attack effectively.

- Set a MT and a ST (just in case) with a bard.

- Create healing groups.

- Create Ranged DPS groups.

1 - Kill Spiders and keep killing them through entire fight.

2 - When MB pops, MT engages and healing begins. ST with some healing engages to build aggro.

3 - Range DPS engages, burns down MB

4 - 1 corner at a time (or more if you have enough chanters) get the spiders cleared (so there is 0 aggro) or spiders memblurred (so there is 0 aggro)

5 - Hail and loot

This fight strategy has some advantages and disadvantages against the burn it down fast strategy. It takes longer and requires a bit more control (something that will be useful later). It simplifies the healing job in some ways, and if you have an alt clicky cleric (like I do) one can recover from a wipe of the DPS/Healers pretty easily. If you don't have lots of chanters, and have good DPS (particularly Ranged DPS) this strategy can be effective.

Croix VorAbaddon
Here's the strat used by my Alliance:

This strat needs 4 decent tanks for corners, MT and ST/RT, 4 chanters AT LEAST, the more the better, and 4 druids, again, the more the better. Also, as many TS rangers as you can snag. 10 or so will be perfect.

Create four mez groups. These should container a chanter, perhaps a second if available, and a druid snarer. Rangers will NOT suffice, you'll see why in a minute. Also in this group should be a tank, and a healer. The rest are DPS gravy. Assigned these groups to corners by direction: NW, NE, SE, SW. Use the map to get it right.

Once those groups are in their corners, the rest of the DPS/Tanks head to a corner that seems to need people and waits.

At this time, the MT, RT, and ST stand by a door to MB and ready themselves. Clerics stand at the wall looking at the door.

At this time, the Raid Leader calls for spider kills to begin. At this point, everyone grabs a spider and the slaughter begins. After a few minutes, the MT should be able to target MB. At this point, the RL calls to finish the current spider, and mez the rest. Once this is call, tanks and dps OTHER THAN rangers finish the mobs. Rangers report to the MB door. Either one will work, since this won't last long.

HoT the MT and RT, and they charge. Soon as the MT is ready, he'll call something like "RANGED ATTACKERS! ASSIST ME NOW!" at which point, the ranger move into bow range, preferably IN the room so as not to draw MB out if they somehow get aggro, and begin plinking away. Soon after this, the MT calls Trueshot. All rangers Trueshot at once. MB should be mowed down before he knows what hit him.

If things begin to go to hell, or the TS barrage doesn't kill him, the MT calls for an all-melee assist. All melee's rush in, and go all out, full offensive discs.

As long as aggro is managed well, he's toast.
