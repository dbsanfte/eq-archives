Morgrist
Okay. Here's what you're going to do.

First,you MUST go to the top of the stairs in the Spurned building. Talk to the Enchanter GM up there. He'll tell you to go to the Ebon Hand.

Next,when you get there (in 3rd Gate) walk in the door,turn right immediately and you'll see a rogue. Say to him "I need to assassinate someone." Go through the text. You -are- getting flagged for stuff as you do. You'll get a slimy mortar and pestle to do the combine. Two parts you get from a poison-maker. Susceptible Essence and Mind Melt. Two parts you'll need from killing a named in Dagnor's Cauldron and South Ro. Bring a group. They're mean enough that you don't want to solo them.

Go to Grimpl in DC FIRST. Hand in the note,track Jagged Fang and kill it,then go back to Grimpl with what you get and get the note for the South Ro Teir'dal and Gargantua. Get the poison component from Gargantua and head back to the rogue with the combined result. Then go back to the enchanter. You'll get a 1-charge item, ALL/ALL with Illusion:Guktan. He will recharge it for you if needed.

If you're not an enchanter,you can stop here and enjoy the toy. Or,take the stone to Gukta,zone in and illusion up. Find the right guy and hand him the poisoned mug of ale you now have.

Watch him suffer and die. You'll end up with a medal off his twitching corpse. Bringing it back to the enchanter will get you the enchanter Illusion: Guktan spell (NO DROP I believe.).

http://www.attrition.org/eq/hosted/gsq/ has all the text if you want it spoiled utterly.
