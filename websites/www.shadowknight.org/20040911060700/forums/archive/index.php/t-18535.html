That all depends on your supporting cast and the time you have to put into it. At 52 I've completed all but the 3 planar drops and the Glohnor kill. It's been solo or 1 grouped up to now. I still have all the toughest parts to go, obviously, but this should give you an idea. You can easily do most of the quest at 51 with a solid group. If you have a group/guild that is experienced enough to do Fear, Hate, and Sky then there is no reason you can't do the rest of it either.

Keep gaining levels to make it easier on yourself, but really you are at a level where you could pull it off if you want to. Again, it depends on your supporting cast.

I found that going along on other people's epic raids often helped me get my stuff - i.e. if they are doing the ranger guy in the Hole, they often don't mind killing a few chests for a cell key on the way.
