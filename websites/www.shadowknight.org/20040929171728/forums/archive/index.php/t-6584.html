While I do like Paineel, I actually like Neriak the best. Sure, I am biased: I am a DE. But right from the start, you know in the city of Neriak, no one messes with the Lodge!

Ghoul guards keep out the riff-raff! Indigo punks and all the other silly pajama-wearers KNOW they are in the wrong part of town when they see these guys!

Blood-red walls beckon the initiated, and frighten off the uninitiated. Who can count the number of screams these walls have heard? Who can count the number of rituals these hallowed walls have witnessed?

Upstairs, various experiments (fools would erroneously call it 'torture') have been undertaken through the ages for the eternal study of the miracle of undeath. Tables perfectly tilted to provide maximum blood-flow are blood-stained from the countless number of victims disected there.

Below, the leaders of the Shadow Knights and Necromancers gather in the main chamber to discuss the guild's course. Resplendent in their robes and armor, these leaders represent the most intelligent and powerful masters of death that the world has ever known.

Conventiently located in small alcoves you find various merchants prepared to buy and sell the goods you need, whether it be the trinkets of gold, silver, armor and weapons collected during your conquests, or the left over remains of your latest kill.

Yes, the very air of this fine guild is permeated with the aroma of sweat, blood, and death. No finer scent shall you find in all of Norrath!

Leaving the guild always lowers my spirit, for when I leave it, I leave a place of ultimate reverence and inspiration.

Now, my journeys take me far and wide away from my ancestral home, and often I long to see the blood-red walls again.

And of course, I miss /con'ing the ghoul guards, ever present and ever watchful.
