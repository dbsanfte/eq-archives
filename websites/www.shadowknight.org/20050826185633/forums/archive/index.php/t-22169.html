The simple answer is that KC, in my opinion, is not a good place to hunt.



The complicated answer is this:

KC used to have a good experience yeild (IE: In Kunark/Velious days) however it was also packed 24/7 with every idiot to ever follow the "experience highway" and was thus known for adolescent behavior and amazing trains. You had to weigh the risk/reward to decide whether or not to hunt there. Today, it is largely abandoned because there is better experience to be had elsewhere - not to mention that its reputation preceeds it and people tend to avoid the place.

(On that note however, if you planned on taking friends there instead of going LFG it might be worth checking it out. You may be able to get the zone to yourselves at the cost of less EXP/Kill. Some people prefer to have a zone to themselves even for less EXP; you might be one of those people.)

Now, as for it lowering your faction in the OT outpost, that is somewhat true. Not every mob will lower your faction, but some will. Its your choice whether or not to ruin your OT faction. Your epic is started there, however the Epic mob is on Truespirit faction and not OT faction. He is also not located very close to any OT faction mobs. Therefore, while it will be a little trickier, you can still complete your epic with bad OT faction. Also, search in the Epic section of these boards for information on "Seal Running". It will allow you to skip the first part of the epic quest, including the part in the OT outpost. Seal running is too complicated for me to recite here, especially because there are already many great guides on it located on these boards.
