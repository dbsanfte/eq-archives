HS has the same cast as death peace, but I channel HS almost 100% of the time and Death peace gets interrupted a ton more (my observation). So for a bad pull I would use HS in conjunction with FD.
Think of HS as a "get out of dodge" spell. You can't attack or cast during it, so it's not like you can finish off a mob while invul at 10% health. It's basically to get to a safe spot to FD.
