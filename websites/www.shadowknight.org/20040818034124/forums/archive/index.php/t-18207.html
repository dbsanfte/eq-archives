Thanks for the feedback. Just finished this quest a few minutes ago. For anyone else who would like to take it this was my experience:

First of all every spot in the Wakening Lands looks pretty much like every other spot in the Wakening Lands so be prepared to get lost if you are not skilled in the arts of navigating.

My first stop was the raptors. I hit the area right outside of Skyshrine (if you are facing the entrance to Skyshrine they are literally just a few feet away to your right up on a ledge amidst some trees). I had heard that the strong raptor gut was a rare drop so I am going to consider myself very lucky here as I got mine on my first kill. It dropped off of a raptor (did not bother with the saurus) which was single pulled out of the group of about five of them. Pretty easy kill. Raptor did not have a lot of damage output.

Thanks for the tip on Rolandl by the way. Almost had a heart attack when I ran across him. Thankfully I was invisible at the time.

Next stop was the holgresh. My experience with these pretty much reflect what the other two posts said. Tough bastards. I found the elementists were easier to fight than the conjurers as I resisted more of their spells. Even so it was not easy by any means. In total I fear kited three to death and was killed twice by two others. Never even found the cave. Luckily a friend of mine (level fifty eight beastlord) popped on around this time and came out to help me. He found the cave and proceeded to route it. One clearing of the cave yielded the strong wooden branch.

Went to Iceclad and did all of the necessary running around. Maybe my gear is more chump then I thought but I found the enraged walrus battle to be somewhat difficult. I did not fear kite him for fear of what might be in the water if he ran that way (completely unfamiliar with the zone) and he hits pretty hard (consistent eighties). Conned dark blue at fourty three though. Managed to put him down with twenty eight percent health left (did not have to drop harm touch).

All in all the quest was more difficult than I was expecting it to be from the posts I read but definitely worthwhile for the item it yields. I would advise bringing assistance for the holgresh part of it if you attempt it at the level I did. They are soloable but it is pretty much a crap shoot.
