To answer the basic questions, you fight him in the water if you turn left at the entrance to AC. He is in his room alone, although you might drag the odd Grimling with you to the setup area (if these worry you don't do the encounter).

He Flurries and Enrages. He does not Rampage. I don't remember what he hits for but if you can tank in NToV you can tank him with ease. He has no AE.

Whenever he is up my guild kills him, because we are at a stage where his drops are an upgrade for many. Farming him 2 box or 3 box will not go very far unless you are a) just trying to see if you can do it or b) like to sell Luclin era spells or c) equipping an alt.
