I would recommend making them the same race, if possible (like troll or ogre). That will allow you to go to the same cities for spells, etc. But others are right. With a good shaman, you can go just about anywhere a full group of equal level can go. It will just take longer to kill things.

Get to lvl 10 asap. Head to Paludal until about lvl20.
Head to Mistmore - Is now a "hotspot" with xp modifier the same as paludal. Stay there till 25-30.
Head to Splitpaw - another hotspot. Stay till 35.
Head to Gunthak - another hotspot till 40
Head to Dulak's - another hotspot till 50
Head to Nadox - go through the luggald temple in Gunthak to avoid the lava. Another hotspot. As a two-box, this will give you great xp until 60. At about 55, head down to the undead, or do the luggalds on the other side of the bridge.

Post 60 - PoP
