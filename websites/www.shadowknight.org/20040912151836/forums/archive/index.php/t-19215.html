Thacker
Pet Disc all the way!

Honestly, this is one of the best AAs I have ever spent points on, and you will especially love it if you are in LDoN. Cast a pet, have a cleric virtue it (since they are wussy little buggers). Make a hotkey for /pet attack, and then make another hotkey with /pet back off on the first line and /pet hold on the second line. When you have a room that has a few mobs in it, target one, hit hotkey to send pet in to attack, then as soon as he hits mob you hit the hotkey to bring him back, and Viola! single pulls. In LDoN you can single pull consistantly. Not even a bard can single pull better than a SK with pet disc. I even use Mr Wussy to pull in BoT and he does it flawlessly. The only time you have problems is when there are greens around. They will aggro pets sometimes.

I only have about 60 points, and I think I got pet disc around 50. I think if I had to do it all over again, I would have got it around 30 instead because of how awesome it is for pulling. It's also nice to have just so that your pet is useful and won't run off and get himself killed when you get hit by something tough and he provides extra DPS.

I think you should also just keep your mood in mind. Sometimes saving up six AA seems painfully slow and you just don't have the stomach for it. When that happens, get the AA that cost 2 or 3 or 4 points and wait till you feel patient to top out SCF3 or ND3 or the like. At other times, you've got a few saved and it's not eating you alive, and you feel like it's worth it getting one of the big AA levels done with, maybe -- so rack up a few more and get the big ones done.

AA's can burn a hole in your pocket just like money. Sometimes it's more fun just to spend them whether it is or isn't the best idea. It's just a game.
