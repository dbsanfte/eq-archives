In a couple levels (35 or so) pack up your crew and head to the Tentacle Terror tunnel in Crystal Caverns. That was where I practiced indoor FD pulling. Plenty of mobs for you at that level with a couple of groupmates, they hit fast, and occasionally hard, so you have incentive to get it right, and they're scattered around the tunnel in unfortunate configurations that make getting singles a real pain in the ass, but when you've figured it out, you'll be set for just about anywhere.

Youll get a feel for it. Kind of like the way you know a mob is in spell range before you cast. Its mostly a matter of room, you need enough room for your spell bar to refresh so you can cast FD after dark. But of course you wanna get your drop n flop off before the unsnared add gets to you as the regular feign death is a bitch to cast when mobs are beating you. Theres other things, like pets cause problems sometimes if the mob gets a hit off on you. Theres a few basic things like that and the rest will just come naturally. Make sure that your casting spells are maxed.
It will become second nature.

Just choose a spot with alot of room to start out and as you get better you can move to tighter quarters. Good luck to you.
