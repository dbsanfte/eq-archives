SorishMT
Due to the knee-bends in the calculations, you should try getting your 4 primary combat stats above 75 to start (makes things easier later when that's 5 less points you have to worry about).

Ultimately, you're going to want to boost STR/STA as high as you can go with gear, and get AGI/DEX over 100 (more is nice, but you won't miss it much if you don't get much above 100). So, in the long run, starting points really don't matter much.

My personal preference when doing picks is to get all of the 4 combat stats to 75+ (preferably as close to 100 as possible).
