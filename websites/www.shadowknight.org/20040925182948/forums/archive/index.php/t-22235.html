vilesoul
port triggering, ports 1024 to 700, app name Everquest.

Read your manual to learn how to use port triggering.

I've had two linksys routers, and this worked on both of them.

BTW..this is all due to how linksys routers firewalls work...namely they treat any incoming traffic froma IP/port that was not started internally, it blocks it.

How that works is Sony sends a special "ping" to you, and if you get that ping, you send backa special "pong". What's happening is the router is blocking that ping, so the server never gets your pong, therefore it assumes your LD, and cuts your connection....therefore making you LD.

I know for a fact that Netgear routers don't have this issue though, if that is of any relevance whatsoever here.
