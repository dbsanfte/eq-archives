natus
Sol A entrance area to bar, maybe a little further in but can be tough without feign. Definitely won't be boring solo.

Mobs have low hps since its an old zone.

Downside is you may have to compete with farmers/pl'ers.

You can make decent coin and experience there 23 - 30.


Around 30 try Dalnir's. Downside there is the casters can suck if you're not ready for them. Bright side however, invis and FD will get much practice here. Not many mobs see invis in here.

If you can swing it group with a cleric and chanter for some trio fun in these zones. Hell, duoing with just about any class would be good for either zone.

Good luck.

A good place for the mid to late 20's is Marus Seru, though it's not a favourite of very many. No bonus, but not many hunt there, and I like that because no one bothers me. :)

Zelniaks and lightcrawlers are agro, and the Zelniaks have a long range. Rock dudes, greyhoppers, Recusos, and beetles aren't agro until you've killed a lot of them, and they're social.
