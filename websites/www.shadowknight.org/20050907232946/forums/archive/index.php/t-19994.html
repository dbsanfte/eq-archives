... pull Stoneberg and you will be all right at dorfs. They hit for a max of 70 and that rarely. With good regen, and a good DS you can straight tank them endlessly. They drop Heads too:) Back in Velious my friend and I estimated we made about 40K from the heads. *cheer Dorfs*

That said, don't neglect Ykesha. A lot of people trash it (usually high end raid types) but the soloing there can rack up xp at a fast rate to 65, if you have the balls and the time to get off the beaten track. At 62 it seems to me the Luggald caves at the very back were very good XP. Use the short fear, and some ingenuity, and its very good. You can rack up 6 or 8 blue diamonds and multiple other decent loots in a session as well. Or you used to be able to - its been a while.
