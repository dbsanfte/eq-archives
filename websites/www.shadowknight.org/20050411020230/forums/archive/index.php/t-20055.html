Nambkab
Str does indeed raise attack, but there are two facets to attack, and raising both will raise attack, but in different methods. Raising Str allows you to hit harder and/or raises max hit if your str was really low. Skill mods and +accuracy rais your chance to actually hit the mob. Raising either of these will show higher attack on your screen, but will have different effects. Raw attack bonuses from what I've seen and parsed, just seem to raise chance to hit, maybe chance to hit for max.
