Rueeliar
Biggest bottleneck for running two instances of EQ on the same HDD is that the cache on the drive has twice as much data to manage, so it will cut corners and repeatedly drop info from one instances in favor of the other, then drop that in favor of the first, back and forth ad infinitum. That, and constantly being told to access two different locations on the drive at the same time.

Howlitoezis
Raid 1 is mirrored. It is the slowest of raid setups. It is not for speed but for redundancy, so in case 1 harddrive fails, you have the complete image on the other drive.
You are thinking of raid 0.

The separate harddrives will show minimal performace gain in normal operations, but for two boxing, it should show a reasonable increase in performance.
