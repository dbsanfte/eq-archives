DrMobius
The mob levels in Droga were bumped to 50 - 55. If you get SoW'd or SoE'd before you go in the zone, then shrink yourself and invis, your invis should last you all the way down and back up. However, there are no mobs in the cell with the pottery wheel, nor are their any mobs in the adjacent cell. There is a mob in the third cell, but he's a quest mob that won't agro on you when you re-cast invis.

There are 2 goblins outside of the cells, but they won't agro on you either. Its really a safe, fast, painless run. If invis happens to drop on you then keep booking it to the cells and FD or DP before you enter the cell. Wait it out, the mobs will eventually all return and then you can stand up and squeeze through the bars to the pottery wheel, FD or DP just to be safe, then do your combine and put invis back on.

DrMobius
Medium sized characters (DE, Human) need to take one swig of ants potion, fat-assed characters take 2. You run against the bars moving from left to right and you'll just run right through the gates. No need to kill anything or get keys.

Before entering Droga get SoW or SoE.
Enter Droga.
Take 1 or 2 swigs of ants potion.
Cast Gather Shadows (or click CoS).
Run down to jail area.
Run against bars to squeeze through them.
Combine in pottery wheel.
Re-cast Gather Shadows (or click CoS).
Run like hell out of there.

Its cake :D
