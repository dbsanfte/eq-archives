in terms of sustainable mana usage, mana preservation cuts all spells by 10%, so the more mana regen you have the more you will be helped by mana preservation. if you want to compare it directly to FT, divide the amount of mana you're regenning on the average tick by 10, and it's better than that much FT.

lets say on average you're getting 45 mana/tick... you'd be able to spend 50 mana/tick worth infinitely with mp4, because you'd save 5 on every 50 you spend.


at a minimum i'd say mana preservation 4 is slightly better than ft4, and the more mana you're getting the more beneficial it is. just using pact of hate, mental corruption, and zevfeer's bite, you can get 40+mana/tick.

During exp group runs, I have found the most economical and efficient use of a mana pres item is the Ceramic Gavel of Justice. Only affects DoT type spells, but reduces cost by 25% on those, so makes Blood of Hate a LOT more desirable. Just cast early and it's usually about to wear off or worn off by the time the mob dies. I only have FT1 and I cast BoH at least once, and CD at least twice on every mob, and I never go OOM even when chain pulling, so long as I have Pact of Hate and MC up.
