Ramgar SK
My last 4 levels have been all over. I'm currently 64.

At 60 LDoN seemed to drop off and lose popularity. It was still fun, but got old. I have done many things, mostly 2boxing, to get me from 60 to 64.

Torgiran Mines and Nadox are still great exp for me and would be good at 60. Set up near a zone, make a pet, and pull what you can handle.

PoV is really good exp if your group has cheal and slow. I've still died several times, even with a good group. There is little room for error there. The golumns just hit too hard to allow for mistakes of any kind.

Velketor's Lab. is still great exp for you. Get the named camp and pull like crazy. They die fast, don't hit hard, and there are lots to pull. Might even make some cash.

Veksar entrance would also work well for you. Plenty of goblins to keep you busy, and a couple named can spawn there.

PoP exp can't be beat though. A good planes group will set your exp rolling.

Don't sit LFG. Make the trips, talk to groups in person (not shout to zone). I've found this to be the best way to get a good group.

It is possible to fear kite in PoD by the courtyard. Pull feeble rats, wandering biles and goos back but be VERY careful and patient doing so. They have added a butt load of higher level wandering mobs now so if you get a spider DONT go back to Cleric LOL. I hate those spiders.
