Don't assume, it's bad practice. Dex definitely affects proc rate, Str definitely affects attack rating (and therefore average damage per hit), Agi affects AC. Double attack is determined by a skill check that is independent of Dex. If anybody has any proof, or heck, even evidence, to the contrary, please inform me.
