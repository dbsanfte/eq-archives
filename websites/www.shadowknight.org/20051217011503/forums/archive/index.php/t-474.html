Fizzles depends on:

*Your skill in the magic type of this spell (ie 200 skill = less chances to fizzle than 150)
*Level of the spell, if you try to cast a lvl 28 spell with 10 in the skill, you will fizzle it at least 90% of the time, if you try to cast the downgrade of this spell wich is a lvl 9 one you "should" not fizzle it alot
*The single spell by itself, I noticed that some specific spells (like 54 banshee aura for example) fizzle alot more than most of lvl 60 spells, even with a maxed skill, I don't have proof of it tho, thats just an "impression".

About the interrupt thing, it usually happens when you run/walk and start to cast spell just after you stop, solution is to stay still for about half a second before cast, it also "seems" to happen sometime (rarely) when there is alot of action near you.
You're interrupted when you "move" while casting, ie a mob pushes you, you're slipping on an icy floor etc...

I HIGHLY suggest you to turn a little bit before casting. For fear kiting. I just run away from darked mob and immediately turn a little while stopped then cast right away.
