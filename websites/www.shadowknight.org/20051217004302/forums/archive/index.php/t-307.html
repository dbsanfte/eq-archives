Originally, there were two versions of the Vampiric Embrace proc to handle our lower power version. For some reason, due to the coding of that one, all the new lifetap proc buffs they've added have two identical proc effects, one for SKs and one for everybody else.

Blade Dance
Decrease Dexterity by 25 (L1)

Blade DanceSK
Decrease Dexterity by 25 (L1)


LifetapEffectNormal
Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 5 (L1) to 35 (L60) and adds it to yours

LifetapEffectSK
Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 5 (L1) to 35 (L60) and adds it to yours


Trickster's Torment
Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 70 (L1)
Lowers you on the target's hate list by 100

Trickster's TormentSK
Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 70 (L1)
Lowers you on the target's hate list by 100


VampEmbraceNecro
Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 13 (L1) to 72 (L60) and adds it to yours

VampEmbraceShadow
Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 1 (L1) to 31 (L60) and adds it to yours
