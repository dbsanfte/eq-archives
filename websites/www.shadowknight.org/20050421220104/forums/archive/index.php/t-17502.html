I have never made a focused effort to raise Cabilis faction, and yet now I am not KOS to any faction there that I know of (guards are warmly and crusaders are dubious). Here's how I did it:

Hunt in Veksar, collecting all the steel weapons you can find. When done collecting a few bags full of them, run back out of Veksar to LOIO and there's a guy along the western shore of the Lake that takes them in sets of four. Turn in to him for faction, coin and exp. I did that for about 3 levels (from 60 to 63) and am now non-KOS everywhere. I don't consider this out of my way at all, because I was hunting in Veksar for exp and fun not faction, and just discovered the faction quest later and it turns out the exp it gives you isn't that bad either (I think I average about 1% of exp for a full complement of weapons (ie. about 50 weapons). Granted you can only do this if you can hunt in Veksar, but I think it's a very reasonable quest. I hadn't really planned on doing my Greenmist (have epic already) but considering I have this easy way to get the faction, I am seriously considering the Greenmist someday when I get bored.
