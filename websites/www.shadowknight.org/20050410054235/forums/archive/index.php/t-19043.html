Dakkin
I have a couple of hotbutton banks so I can activate them by pressing the number keys in combat.

Fairly standard is the following (I have another setup with range attack instead of assist for when I'm pulling outdoors):

Button 1 - assist
/assist <pullername> or if needed /assist luller or chanter

Button 2 - aggro
Clinging darkness spell gem

Button 3 - caster interrupt
Slam

Button 4 - Call assist
/doability 8 (taunt)
/doability 7 (slam)
/attack on
Assist me on %t
/cast 1 (clinging darkness)

This is actually the most useful macro I have, and makes a huge difference in fast groups and for breaking mez; if I'm tanking, I rarely turn attack on except through this button. I have a similar one for offtanking.

Button 5 - taunt
/doability 8 (taunt)
I'm taunting %t
/cast 2 (terror)

This works a LOT better than standard taunt for pulling mobs back onto you, and can be used for taunting mezzed mobs. Considering adding a disarm in front of the taunt too subject to some checking; Brael says it improves taunt effectiveness.

Button 6 - HT
When you get higher, you'll want to change this to a macro to pop discs and stuff prior to HT.

Button 7 - feign death
/stand
/attack off
/cast 8
/cast 8
/cast 8

This will cast through fizzles to get a successful FD almost all the time.

A couple more buttons I've kept for clickies; when I hit 45 I found the BE gauntlets incredibly useful for saving mana on aggro, and I still use them surprisingly frequently for various things.

One other general macro I've found useful:

Incoming
Incoming %t
/cast 1 (clinging darkness)

I poured scorn on this when someone suggested it for pulling in LDoN then tried it and embarrassingly enough found it incredibly useful.

That's about all I can think of offhand; I have a ton of macros and hotbuttons for raids but those aren't worth going into here. I hope it's useful anyway.

lol Putrid. True. True.

I've only found one set of hotkeys to be useful. My second set of hotkeys has the ones I seldomly use but need to be repeated a lot, such as /corpse, /loc.

Most important key: Assist. Type out the MA's name, don't use %t, you might not be in his/her group. The only time you don't need it is then you are the Main Assist.
Secondary assist key is nice also. Very useful in raids, edit the key any time MA changes:
/gsay Assisting Ramgar
/assist Ramgar
/pause 5
/gsay on %t


Pause is useful in macros for assisting and casting a spell on incommng, when I'm not the puller, but I am MA:
/stand
/assist Ramgar
/pause 5, /cast #

I've found that /pause 5 works 100% after assist, and /pause 4 works about 4/5 times. The latter resulting in castings on myself. :) /pause has to be first, followed by a comma, then /doability or whatever.

It's a good idea to always have an "Incomming" key that tells the group and or raid what is coming, or added while fighting:
/gsay INC ---> %t
/rsay INC ----> %t

I also have an "Assist Me" key, for raid tanking, breaking mez and multiple incomming:
/gsay ASSIST me on a --- %t ---

Unholy Aura Key:
/disc unholy
/em Ramgar becomes ENRAGED!
/doability # (# = harm touch ability number) (can also use /alt activate)
