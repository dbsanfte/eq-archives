Soloing is very slow experience for SKs from my experience, but if you really are set on going the solo route, you're probably going to have to find either something you can fight head to head that gives you experience, fear kite mobs that barely give any experience, or aggro kite mobs that don't summon while your dots and your pet beat it up. None of them are particularily good options, but you can at least aggro kite anywhere with a decent amount of open space. Just in case you don't know, aggro kiting is where you snare a mob and run from it while your pet (with taunt off) runs behind the mob hitting it. If you find your pet stealing aggro from you (it shouldn't), you can throw other DoTs into the mix like Boil Blood, which is our most efficient DoT besides the pet, to do damage faster and generate a little bit more aggro.

Some places that you can aggro kite in include Plane of Nightmare, Plane of Disease, Plane of Storms and Plane of Valor. Once you get access to Halls of Honor you can do it there, too.

You can still fear kite in PoD if you just follow the left wall around by where the spiders spawn (they can't be feared BTW). Just pull the rats and goos back to said wall and fear it using your shortest duration fear, but be careful as some of the rats will be 55+ and can't be feared either. The XP is about half of what you'll get in a PoV or better XP group. Also, if you're completely against group XP then duoing with a shaman (you can tank tier 1 with a slow and light heals easy) or aggro kiting with a good ranja (i know it's an oxymoron) can be decent XP as well, but the best XP post 59 is always group XP.

I've spent alot of time soloing in both Pod (ratz and goos) and in Pon (werewolves). At your level the Ratz and goos were decent exp, but don't compare to a PoV or BoT group. My Pod solo fear spots are near the bone wall or along the north wall, west of the Malarians. With Proper placement I used highest level fear, with snare always on fear runs out before they can aggro others (experiment and find where to stand). The PoN spot is along the zone wall, and it can also be done with highest level fear spell, but adds sometimes occur.
At 65 While /lfg I can get an AA in about 3 hours in Pod, and Pon is a little better, but almost always camped. Still the best thing would be only use these spots while /lfg, exp will be 2-3 times better with a decent group.
