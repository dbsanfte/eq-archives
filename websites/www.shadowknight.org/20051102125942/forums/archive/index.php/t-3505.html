I am not sure what PvP server or what race you are, but you should break this down into two tasks: getting from 9 to 15 and from 15 to 20.

At level 9, you should be able to solo in places like Field of Bone and depending on your server, East Commonlands. Other good spots would be The Warrens. You should be able to milk these until about level 12, or in the case of Field of Bone, 15 or 16. At that point, at level 15, you get Fear and Clinging Darkness. You should be able to Fear kite at that point and you should be able to go until level 20 doing that. You can go from 15 to 20 by hunting at the mudpit at the NE corner of The Overthere zone. All you have to be careful there of is the Sarnak fort and PKers.

Hope that helps.
