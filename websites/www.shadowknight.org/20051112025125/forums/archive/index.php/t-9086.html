ATK taps/debuffs have next to no taunt value. Lifetaps are low value too. Disease Cloud had it's aggro castrated ages ago. Stick to Clinging Darkness. Use it early and repeat a couple of times as necessary.
It sounds like you are using an awful lot of mana per fight, cast the ATK tap once only when it fades, cast lifetaps only when you're getting into the bottom end of the red bar and the cleric is low.

The damage output is not the only thing that controls aggro. Its also the range at which someone is attacking.

In this case I would just get the monk to back off occasionally so the aggro passes. True that you will loose some dmg output on the monk, but at least he wont die.

I was once told that even in melee, the closer to the target you are decides the aggro. I'm not 100% convinced but as a 50 SK I can take aggro from 50+ BSing rogues and taunting warriors without using aggro spells just by getting closer to the mob. Especially if the mob is rooted.
