The quest starts in Cabilis in the Tower of Terror, which is why you need the faction. In addition, you need amiable faction with Crusaders of Greenmist to even begin the quest, and there is a necromancer not more than five feet from the first quest NPC, so you'll need to be out of KOS range with them.

As for the no drop vs tradable pieces, there are too many to list. There are several walkthroughs and a very good checklist in the section of the same name on this board, so just check there for the required pieces.

As for buying the item, well, we each play the game the way we like, and occasionally we're forced to do things we wouldn't normally do because of lack of time or assistance, but I'd recommend doing as much as you can yourself. The reward isn't so great that it's worth buying the whole thing. Most of the benefit of the quest is the feeling of accomplishment from doing it.

Important: Greenmist is an extension of the Khurki quests. "Beginning" it is finishing the Lord of Pain's Khukri.

You want to do this because as an SK you should strive to be fucking sexy (Pardon the language, but it's the real reason) whenever possible. Also, if you are a gnome, you want to do it to piss off the Iksar and other "pure" SKs.

Begin here to see the work you have to do to really BEGIN the Greenmist: http://forums.shadowknight.org/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1412

Buyable peices:

THEORETICAL: Hero's Khurki -- Droppable, but I've never ever seen one for sale. To get this you'd need another SK that gave up on the quest essentially.

THEORETICAL: Lord of Pain's Khurki is MQable with someone who was willing to camp the Stupendous Tome.

PRACTICAL:
Greenish Metal Shard (Bazaar)
History of the Greenmist (Bazaar)

Mirror from Drusella is MQable.



It procs at 45 according to lucy.fnord.net.

At level 30 you SHOULD be looking at this quest, because it will not be as painful for you to camp CoM and other parts while you get experience from them. However, it is excruciating.
