While you're mounted on Unholy Steed, you cannot get flags for events by Hailing mobs, at least on two encounters.

1) We're doing Xanomech and fighting our little hearts out. We kill him and as I was low on mana, I had summoned horsie towards the end of the fight. The gnome for the flag pops, and I ride over to him and hail him. I had him targeted, I wasn't invis, and I was nearly standing on top of him. I hailed him two times, no response. Thinking maybe I would log out, come back, and try again I dispelled my horse. On a lark, I hailed him again, and got my flag.

2) We're doing Behemoth event in PoI. I run in with my raid and hail the first gnome before leaving the first chamber in the Factory. I'm not mounted, and I get my pre-flag. We finish the fight against Behemoth and I and many others died due to dots. I left my corpse at the Graveyard after I'd ridden from bind point, and dashed into the Factory. I rode all the way through naked and reached the gnome where he'd been stopped right outside the Behemoth room. I was still in raid, I wasn't invis, and again I was right on top of him, but on my hail, I didn't get the flag. It didn't occur to me it might be the horse, so I left the area (we had a pretty big raid) and got my rez. On a lark, I went to the Seer in PoK and 'unlocked my memories'. I received my flag then, getting the message from the gnome that he'd 'forgotten' I'd promised to 'test the machine'.

This is only two instances, but in both cases I was mounted and it's about the only thing I can possibly think of which could link both together. In both cases I did exactly the SAME thing as everyone else on my raid, but I had issues getting the flag, while they did not. If you're having trouble getting a flag where you 'hail' something, dismount and give it another shot, or go 'unlock your memories' with the Seer in PoK.

Just a heads up, make of it what you will. :)
