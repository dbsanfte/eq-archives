I wouldnt discount a BL as automatically easy to beat in a duel..especially if they are ready for you..specially the high lvl BLs (and if they know how to PvP)

remeber they can resist buff their pets AND of course the pets use the resists of their master. if that BL has resists worth anything..fear wont stick and the pet will be gnawing your ass off. (and of course all those DD procs going off in your face)

and at many stages of the BLs life the pet is the same lvl as the BL himself. Also dont forget the BL can S*L*O*W..which is bad news for your dmg output. The BL can also buff a lot of his own resists making life tougher for you. and he has a sow and his HP+Mana regen spell

The Sks advantage is that the BLs, heals arent worth spit and they have generally low AC and their HPs arent that great. the BL is a hybrid so his mana pool isnt great either so using your mana tap could force him into straight melee sooner.

so get in his face fast..if you have good resists you nullify a lot of his defenses..and id burn HT quickly on him..use the unholy aura too. and lifetap him between attacks
be mindful of the pet though..dont let the pet sit there and attack you in the back..make sure you postion yourself so both the BL and the pet are in front (so you can parry/riposte)

if he gets slow to stick on you, yer in trouble though (lvl 60 BL has 50% slow and they can 40% haste themselves and their pet)..at this stage counting on fear is a bad idea

oh yea dont forget..Fear cant be cast on PCs on any server anymore ..besides having high resists make fear a undependable solution at best
