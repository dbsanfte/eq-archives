On the Dire Wolf Stalker...Quest writeup on Alla's is wrong, it's on the island with port ring.

I decided it would take forever to do it with just the sk, so I pulled out my 63 druid, went to around n2000,p6000 in Iceclad, near a dragon skull stone, and would ensnare every wolf I could find. Would gather up between 15 and 30 wolves, circle em together like a quad, and PBAoE them with Catastrophe. Doing this continuously for over 6 hours, killing hundreds of wolves, eventually spawned a dire wolf stalker. Tracked him, dragged him to where sk was parked, murder, log and loot.

You definetely need a tracker for this. Get some friends and just hang out at that loc killing wolves and eventually you'll get him.

Don't know why Bloodmaw did that...What I did was to go up behind him, fd since I'd get aggro. Put meat on cursor, hover it over Bloodmaw, and quickly hit 1(stand up), click meat on Bloodmaw, 2(hide). Took a few tries since hide didn't always work and I'd have to fd again, but eventually I hid and was able to get him to poof.

I remember Lost Pirate not responding either. Tried lots of things and unfortunately didn't log what worked. Keep trying stuff in the dialog he gives you, something with pieces/parts/finding.
