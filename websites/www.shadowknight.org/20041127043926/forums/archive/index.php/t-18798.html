Your level range indicates that you'd get a level 55 adventure, meaning you'd face monster levels of 49-51. None of those are within 4 levels of you which is the sweet spot for experience. Could be you randomly just got a lot of low level pops so that a majority of what you faced was level 49. I'm not 56 yet so I don't know how experience flows at that level, but 3% shouldn't be out of the question if you're just killing the minimum number of mobs, being on the high end of the curve (i.e. closer to the break point to the next dungeon tier), and getting an adventure with low level pops in it. If you want more experience, bump up a tier (by getting a hard dungeon or get in groups where the average level is greater than 57.833) then you'll be in a level 60 adventure and face monster levels of 54-56. All close blues and a few whites should be sweet experience for you. You said it yourself that the adventure was won easily.
