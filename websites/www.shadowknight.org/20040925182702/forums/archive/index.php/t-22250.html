Yes, your old macro should work, except you might have to change or check the exact wording behind the /disc word. Looks like that's been covered and I'm guessing it's working for you again since you didn't mention it in your second post.

To turn the unholy aura disc off, so that it doesn't continue 'running' long after you've done your HT (which prevents you from using any other disc), use CTRL-C to bring up the combat abilities screen and left-click on the icon that appears there. That will enable you to use leech curse.

Yeah, it'd be nice to kill unholy aura with a macro and a pain to do it this way but at least if you already have the combat abilities screen up to click off the icon, you can just hit your leech curse disc button right from there.
