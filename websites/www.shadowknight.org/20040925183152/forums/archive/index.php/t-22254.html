Noctix
A) SOE is going to evaluate issues largely on their view of how they fit into overall game balance and how easy they are to code. Something like "fix our tanking" is going to take much longer to evaluate than something like "add a toggle for SCS". Therefore, they probably aren't going to solve issues in order.
B) It is quite likely that SOE has more than one developer working on the various class problems. Our spell issues probably go to one developer, our mitigation issues to another, etc. Therefore, issues will be evaluated in parallel so ranking won't matter.
C) If we require them to solve issue #1 before moving on to issues 2-10, that is likely to mean that they either decide not to fix issue #1 because they want to move on to other things or they never get to issues 2-10 because issue #1 takes a long time.

I believe that if we include our tanking issues on the list, they will get as much attention as they would if we ranked them. Having multiple tanking solutions that might go to different teams (a spell based solution, an innate mitigation solution, and an AA solution) might speed things up by allowing multiple developers to evaluate the problem in parallel. I don't think ranking things will help.
