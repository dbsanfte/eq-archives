I'm fairly convinced that most of the oddities with feign are a result of lag, rather like the bug where people died after being completely healed, but before the healing was recorded by the server.

For instance, I have never once feigned out of a melee situation and had a monster come after me once the fight was over. Likewise, I have never been feigned and had something attack me as it walked by.

If I was running away from a train and feigned successfully (i.e. no fallen message) without mobs catching up to me, they have never attacked me unless they break feign with a cast in progress.

What I have found though, is that if I'm in a high lag situation, it becomes almost impossible to feign with multiple mobs on me. If I turn my field of vision towards the ground to decrease my lag, I have far less problems.

Personally, with the exception of a few bugs in the past, I think feign is intended to work 100% of the time, assuming that...

You don't fall to the ground.
You don't get hit with a spell while feigned.

I recall Verant stating that some things had a chance to see through feign, but I get the distinct feeling that was just bullshit intended to cover up the fact that they just didn't know why it failed some times that it shouldn't.
