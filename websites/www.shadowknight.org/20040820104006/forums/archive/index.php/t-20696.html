It's for Lost Dungeon of Norrath. You talk to someone in your home city (the person in the text), and then talk to someone in one of the Adventurer Camps. You're then given an Adventurer's Stone that allows you to be teleported to the various camps.

Just follow the bracketed text if you go to the person the text is talking about.
