synnoira
Starting from Freeport you have lots of good levelling all around you, plus they've added in those sk armor quests. I've heard they're a pain but they might keep you occupied. I went to nek forest at level 6 and hunted there. It's been revamped since and looks quite fun. Orc and derv camps will keep you occupied in EC/WC/N Ro/Oasis plus there is Befallen for a dungeon.
I suggest you work you're way through High pass and then go through the Karanas to the Luclin spire and do some xping on Luclin. Above all else don't rush it and try to go new places and try new things, especially dungeons whenever you can. Oh and start forming your own groups as much as possible, one advantage of being an sk is you can make an effective group with just about anybody.
Putrid
Do E. Commons or neriak till around 8. Get in an orc group till 12. Then I would suggest taking a trip to the Warrens. Good beginning dungeon with good loot, easy to reach zone line, and close bind point. That will get you up to 18 or so. There have also been some threads on Paludal caverns being a great place at these early levels also. I have not hunted there but you may want to do a search on this forum for it to get the details.

Just remember dying is a learning experience.
