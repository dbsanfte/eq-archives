Were you pulling the mob that was pacified? :p Assuming I read your post right, you're not using lull spells correctly.

Pacifiy is one of the lull spells. When it lands on the mob, it has a reduced radius at which it will both notice you are there, and a reduced radius in which is will bother doing something about you. Thus, you lull one mob, and you pull the other mobs that are not lulled, and the lulled one does not come(barring some strange mob pathing through the middle of the lulled mob.)

Skill with a bow is irrelevant to pulling.

Creates less aggro on the mob than some spells (clinging or fear), about the same as a lot of our others (like abd. of str).

Advantage of bow pulling is longer range, no recast time, instant 'cast', can bow on the run. Skill with the bow is irrelevant to its usefulness as a pulling tool. If I'm the groups main puller, I normally use a bow, depending on what I'm pulling and where.
