Here's the Casters Realm info that Evilangel mentioned

Last week there were a lot of changes made to pets, especially in relation to casting times and reagents. The current changes on Test Server have almost all pets with a 1.0 second casting time and only 1 reagent required regardless of pet. Previously pets could take up to 10 seconds or more to cast, and use up to 3 reagents per summoning.

Could you provide a little insight into the current pet revamp on test, what your aim is with it, etc?

The current Test Server changes to pets are mostly regarding casting time. I wanted to see what the impact would be if pets were all fast casting, pretty much guaranteeing that a character could summon his pet almost anytime. There are concerns about things like chain-casting pets and the effect it could have on soloability for instance. The changes on Test should allow us to see the worst case.

I'm not sure whether this particular change will go live or not. I'll see how it looks, and continue experimenting with other aspects of pets as well. We already put in a feature where pets are almost never riposted to increase their durability, and I'm looking at increasing some resists on pets, as well as their chance to hit against 50+ mobs. We've got some other things we're looking at doing for pets also, but I'm not willing to discuss them until I'm more sure they'll make it onto Test.

- Rich
