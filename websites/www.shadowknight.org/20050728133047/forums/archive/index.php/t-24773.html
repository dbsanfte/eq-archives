Izabadman
I don't know how much it will help you in an all the time PvP world, but I can give you some tips I learned from Arena and /duel on a PvE server.

1. Shadowstep is your friend when rooted or snared.
2. Diamond Skin is actually useful against nukes from other hybrids, clerics, mages, and even to an extent Necros, as the rune portion shortens their return heal by equal amount.
3. Your lifetaps are hybrid hasted. They may hit for less, but they can hit more often and really, that necro has less hitpoints than you, he's just regenning mana like mad and tapping for more, so you have to offset that.
4. Your pet is GREAT at disrupting casters.
5. Harmshield at the appropriate times can really wreck a caster's day.
6. Get more Magic resistance.
7. Your horse is still pretty fast when snared.
8. Snare pets.
9. OoW poison dots seem to land VERY well, and do substancial damage over short term. (Blood of Inruku, Blood of Discord) Both stack.
10. Nullify Magic or Cancel Magic will clear up a snare or root.
11. Liching or dotting self will prevent mezzes from sticking for long.
12. Cure disease will clear up shaman and beastlord slows.

As I said, some of this could be incorrect on a red server, but test it out, play around with it, whatever. Eventually you will figure out what works best and what doesn't. From what I hear though, Shadowknights took a giant step down on the PvP food chain with the last major PvP changes, so you'll have to work harder :p

Because if you find out that you've got the wrong spells mem'd (ambushed, rooted, no shadowstep, OUCH) you can hit HARMSHIELD and have 18 seconds to sit down and memorize a new spell that you actually want.
