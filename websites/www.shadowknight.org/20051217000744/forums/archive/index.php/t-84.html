Neriak factions include:
The Dead: Kill the 29 High Elf Chanter in Najena around 30 times (6 minute timer) and you should be Amiable to The Dead and can train there.
Clerics of Innorruuk: Iksar are KoS to them. Only way I have seen, other than doing their guild quests, is to turn in the 4 staves for the Blued 2-handed hammer quest.
Indigo Brotherhood: Warriors... no need to raise, easy to avoid.
Hall of the Ebon Mask: Rogues.. see warrior
The Spurned: Non-necro casters. I beleive you start out dubious as an Iksar SK.
Dread Guard Outer: Nektulos Guards. Halflings in Nektulos, & Red Wine.
Dread Guard Inner: Neriak city guards. Red Wine (480 bottles should do it)
Troll and Ogre side: Never been on that side as an Iksar.

There is a guard in NFQ near Smacka (Ogre merchant for IC quest) that is on Cleric faction, but green at 39 and did not attack. There is another in 3rd Gate at the pool of water past the Cleric guild (wanders to the 2 guards you pass to get to the Library) that is also on Cleric faction, that did attack me at 39. I forgot to invis, heh.

Wolf form to do the red wine won't work as it is indoors.
For the Red Wine, you need to be Indifferent to Lokar To'Biath. If you aren't, I was as I recall from just being evil, pay a lowbie chanter (level 4) to invis you for the 1st few trades. Or have a guildie/friend do it.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Another place to bank is in Runny Eye near the well on the bottom floor.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Paineel took a ton of kobolds, and once to indifferent, you turn in the Kobold Molars to get to amiable, took around 30-40 molars. This will allow you to get a key from the guards, and train here.

~~~~~~~~~~~~
Halflings, if you want to bother with it...
give the halfling in Misty Thicket 4 stacks of 4 bandages, will need someone to invis you. Turn in around 1k bandages.
You will still be KoS to the clerics, but you can bank easy, and avoid those. Not sure where they put the halfling Pally guild though.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Qeynos Blood Sabres:
Kill the kolindil (sp?) fish in the pool under the UFO. There are 2 and they are a 6 minute timer, I think. This will make you non-kos to the Bloodsabres and alllow you to get to the evil banker in the sewers without getting attacked. Not sure how many to kill, I haven't done this yet but need to for the IC.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~
That should do it for banking. Any other you want to know about, just ask.

DO NOT put a stack of 20 in each of the slots..you will only be wasting wine.

4. It is impossible to get OT faction because we start off scowling and you need dubious or better to hand in sarnack braids.

This is pretty much all we have pre-velious. The Paineel and the Quenos we need anyway to work on epic. Good Luck

The 'Hide Trick' works as follows.

Have the 'Hide' button hotkey'd. With the item on the curser click the item on the NPC, a split second (almost simoltaneously) after you do this hit the 'Hide' hotkey. If you did it right the item should be in the trade window and the 'Hide' hotkey should be depressed(grayed out). Now to check if you are indifferent, just hit the 'c' button, and that will con the NPC. IF he cons indiff, then hit trade. You can do this as many times as needed.
