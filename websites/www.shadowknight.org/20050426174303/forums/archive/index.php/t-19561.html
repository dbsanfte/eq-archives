Wienaman
Far and away the easiest way to get all iksar faction is to go to Veksar, solo any mob you see with a weapon, and /ooc about every 20 minutes that you will buy any and all weapons that drop. Then, once you have about ten sets of 4 (sets have to be of the same weapon), go out and turn them into Syrik Iceblood in LoIO for a great faction hit. About 25 or so turn ins should get you non kos with all 5 factions (think its merchants that doesn't get affected, can't remember for sure)
