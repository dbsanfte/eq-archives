Your faction in Neriak/Freeport should be pretty much a-ok. I found I was dubious to NFP from the start (Although you might wanna kill a few DF Orcs just to be sure). Watch out for the Gnome inside the jewelry shop tho. You will be indiff to the Coalition of Tradesfolk (Faction most of the merchants are on). You will be apprehensive to the Milita, and Aimiable to the Human Shadowknights of Freeport. In Neriak, you will be dubious to a couple factions, but indiff to The Dead (Dark Elf SK/Necro guild), so you can train. HOWEVER, The Ghouls who guard the entrance to The Dead Guild Hall will be KOS to you, although once you get level 9, you can just cast invisibility to undead to get past them.

As a bonus, you will also be non-KOS to the Orcs of NRO, Oasis, and SRO, and non-KOS to Desert Madmen.

The only incredibly annoying thing is that commonsresidents will be dubious, so you won't be able to buy from all the merchants in that area, but even that is surmountable with enough Orcs.

Possibly now more than ever it's a good time to be a evil Erudite. With the Luclin portal only a stones throw from Paineel its a short trip from your new home to the rest of the world. As long as you have somewhere close by to a) Bank and b) Train. The world is yours...but you might need to invest a little time in obtaining it.

;)

What I did with Choz was start him in the Paineel newbie yard until 4/5 for 2 reasons. One being it's simply the best newbie yard bar none and Two. Bone chips. You will need some when you hit level 9 and get your first pet and higher level Necros are always buying them. Sometimes as much as 5pp a stack.

After the newbie yard greened out, Duck inside the Warrens and go on a puppy slaughter. It's fun and once again. The exp is dandy considering its so rarely used. at level 5 or 6 you can easily break/control the first 4 spawns.

At level 10. after getting the first set of spells. I managed to get a port out to North Karana. I wanted positive Qeynos faction and to date. That's been the roughest faction to obtain. To start you are KoS to everyone there. Solution? Blackburrow...and another puppy slaughter. Once you get to the point where you can hand in Gnoll teeth to the Captain. You are golden. You can bank, buy/sell with most of the merchants and all the other "perks" of a light city. Save for the Casters. I never discovered how to improve faction with them, but even that is workable. Just avoid Nostahla Westfend as she wanders and never go past the bank towards the caster guild houses and you should be fine.

After level 13, It's time to go to Freeport/Neriak and do the dreadful Ro/Oasis time investiment. Yeah. it's a pain but it goes quickly. Add to that you are apprehensive to both West/East Freeport and Neriak. Amible to the Church of Rage (SK Trainers in FP) and the fact you can start to Window Shop in EC. You will reach level 15 (and new spells) in no time.

At 15 is where you need to make a choice. Do you want to carve out a new adventure for yourself? Or do you want to do the questionable but now commonplace practice of the loio/FM/OT/DL/KC/Seb grind on Kunark.

That's boring. :p

After being reltitively pampered in FP/Neriak, I wanted another faction challenge. So Highpass hold I went. And man..the fun you can have there.

Once in Highpass. Get your self set up on the Gnoll side. Its trickly at 15/16 but you and your new pet "can" solo here. If you get jumped by another mob, you are right by the zone. Maybe you will luck out and score a PGT or 2 to sell to some Halfling. :D

Orc Groups are also nothing sneeze at. The exp is incredible, and the faction +'s are for both Karana residents and Highkeep as well. Shouldn't take long until you are able to turn in Gnoll/Orc scalps and even more exp/faction goodness and you will be 22 before you know it.

If I knew then what I know now..I would have keep Choz in Highkeep all this time instead of going to Luclin when it came out. Yes the exp in Neb Lair and MSeru is decent. But sometimes near impossible to get a group and the Downtime from soloing is brutal.

So, at 22. With your new spells and your first damage cap removed. enter Highkeep and begin to whack goblins in the Guard Room and Lookout Room. at 26. You are primed for Warrior and Raider room groups. Watch out for trains though and you will hit 30 in no time at all and you will have aquired enough faction to go just about anywhere you want.

...

Except Rivervale. Slaughter the Halflings in Nek Woods as much as you possibly can. Because 1) It's fun and 2) Halflings suck. >: )
