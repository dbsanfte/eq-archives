Heh, not quite, EQ's patcher like all patchers works in a much more efficient way. The patcher will look at all the eq system files on your computer, then queury the hard drive for either the creation date or the file version. If it finds a file thats is out of date or missing it will add it too a list to be updated. Once its finished analyzing the files it will request the files on the list from the patch server, thus making every eq file up to date in one patch instead of downloading every patch on top of one another.

Now on the subject of it taking so long, it could be a few things. Even though you are using a 56k modem, you could still be connected to your ISP at 36.6 or worse. On top of that some where in the connection from your house to the eq patch server there could also be another weak link causing the download to slow down further. Also, keep in mind when the patcher says 2 minutes it usually means 30 secs or less.....my 2cp :D
