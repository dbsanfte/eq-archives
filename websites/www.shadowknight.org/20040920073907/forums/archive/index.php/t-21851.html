Try this and post if it still doesn't fix it.

Goto your control panel and goto edit options (http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/profile.php?do=editoptions) From here scroll down to Messaging & Notification the third choice under this category is Default Thread Subscription mode, change that to whatever you would like to have (Do not subscribe, No email notification, Instant email notification, Daily email notification, and Weekly email notification). Next goto your thread subscriptions (http://www.shadowknight.org/forums/subscription.php?). From here you can adjust existing ones individually either deleting subscriptions, changing how often you get emailed, etc. In addition to this, when you make a post under Additional Options (below the first Submit Thead button currently) you can set your subscription at the time you post.

Post if none of this works to fix the email spam.
