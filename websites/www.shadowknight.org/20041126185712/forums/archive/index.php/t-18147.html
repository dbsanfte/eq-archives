You can solo in Crystal caverns, just takes some practice. The orc miners can be pulled singly from the entrance by EW or from the dwarf town. Just learn where the spawn points are and look for wanderers coming. I used to grab one just past the dwarf city and run back to the icy room with a some water at the bottom and just fear kite in there. You could alway go to guards if all hell break loose, but it shouldn't.
You can also solo the terrors at the broken bridge room, but that is a little harder, due to there pathing, and some mobs in the water. There is also a guard that will kill steal you.

CC has an exp bonus, I believe, and has good loot.
