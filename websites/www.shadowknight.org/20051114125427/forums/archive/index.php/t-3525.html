Shar Vahl and Shadow Haven for sure. You'll have to do a little faction work (easy quests) in Shadow Haven. The Bazaar is also easy to use.

You can also do some banking in Fungus Grove, and the SK trainers in Echo Caverns.

Avoid Katta Castellum and Sanctus Seru, as you will be KoS in those 2 cities. While you might survive zoning into Katta, zoning into Seru is hazardous to your health.
