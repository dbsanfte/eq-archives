Nyrvel
Weapon Skills is what I concentrated on the most and as a consequence, keeping my skill maxed was a fast and easy process. Here's how I did it:

1) Invest in a decent Weight Reduction Bag, many sell for under 400pp.

2) At the early levels, look for weapons for each Weapon Skill with fast speed, keep them in the aforementioned bag.

3) Once the skill is maxed, then you can keep them maxed by switching through as you fight in group. Groups usually don't mind you maxing your skill by 5 points. Its when you switch from a maxed weapon to one that has 6 points that pisses them off.

4) Now that your Weapon Skill is maxed, you can upgrade weapons as you level and can use them instantly...instead of going somewhere to specifically grind up the Skill.

Since Level 30, I have maintained that practice and had excellent Skill Ups, often switching during a fight as I maxed each weapon. Now I sit at 62 with all my Weapon Skills maxed, including Archery (different method to level) and Hand-to-Hand (hard to do). :p Just takes a bit of discipline...don't forget your Casting Skills and Offense/Defense Skills.
