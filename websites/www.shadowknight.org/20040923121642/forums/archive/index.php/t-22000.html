Prathun wrote:
I realize that the time investment for camping epic pieces and the unreliable nature of the drops can be frustrating. I realize that the relative values of the original epics vary greatly, and some of the weapons have aged better than others. Right-click abilities and effects tend to hold their value much longer than stats or weapon ratios. On top of that, the difficulty between epic to epic varies greatly. There is no doubt that these epic quests could benefit from a thorough top-to-bottom retuning.

That said, there are infinite aspects of the game that, with enough time and resources, we would love to change for the better - but the catch is we are dealing with a finite amount of time and resources. Since the epic quests are fully functional and have been working as is for the past several years, there are less reasons to tinker with them than other more pressing issues.
Not often I get to quote myself. We're not going to revamp the epic 1.0 quests anytime in the foreseeable future. You will not need the original epic to work on or complete the new quest.
