Ultimately SOE will have no choice, in my view, but to provide knights access to a moderated defensive or equivalent. I say equivalent because it is conceivable to make a spell that is scalar and achieves the same effect.

For myself, as posted elsewhere, IMHO knights should get full defensive, while warriors are given improved situational combat abilities and itemisation. Thats based on my view that warriors should achieve x level of tanking for less xp than knights, but knights should be able to match that tanking ability overall given x+y xp. Similarly wars should gain access to gear more readily than knights. Its radical, would give warriors apoplexy, and result in quite a lot of work for soe to actually make worthwhile abilities and itemisation for wars.

Ultimately it would make the game better for warriors (more abilities to challenge themselves with, same progression advantage) in the ability sense, while removing their constructive monopoly on raid MT, but I am the first to acknowledge SOE would never have the guts to commit to such a far reaching option.
