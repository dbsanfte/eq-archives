I have considered switching off from my troll, and even tried some alts because the XP penalty was getting me down. After a lot of looking around, trying stuff, and just plain trying to decide what it was that I really wanted, I'm still playing my troll.

The races really are well balanced in terms of usefulness, or we wouldn't be having these discussions. After 4 years we would have figured out conclusively that brand x is the best for shadowknights, and there ya go... have fun.


If you have not played an ogre before you will find that ducking everywhere is very annoying, shrinking is less optional than you may have thought, and the mana pool really is pretty small compared to what your darkelf has. Even if you think you can level back up to 42 pretty quickly, you could level you current char that much farther during that time and still come out ahead. You have a lot of time invested already and a level 50 darkelf is going to be a lot more powerful than a 42 ogre in any case.

MANA REGEN is the key, its the only thing that will allow you to solo faster.
