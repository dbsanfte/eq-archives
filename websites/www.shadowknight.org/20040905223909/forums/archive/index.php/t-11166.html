Meriboh
There really is no right or wrong way to distribute your starting stats, as you should custom your character as you see fit and how you wish to play.
If you wish to know what the recommended placement for starting statistics are, it is as follows...
All points should go into Stamina. Stamina will determine the amount of hit points you have as well as how well you continue to fight after your stamina is drained. More than 100 points in stamina will ensure that valuable statistics such as strength and dexterity do not drop when stamina bar is drained. Stamina also determines how long you can hold your breath and the length of time you can swim without tiring.
Intelligence is one of the easiest statistics to raise using items in the game; that and strength.
You are correct that wisdom and charisma have no impact on our class.
If you want a comprehensive explanation on the placements of our starting statistics, visit: http://eq.crgaming.com/creationguides/Shadowknight.asp
