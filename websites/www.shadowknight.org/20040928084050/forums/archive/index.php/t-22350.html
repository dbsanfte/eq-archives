Getting back to the original post--

BA4 and DoT crits aren't especially significant in terms of increasing aggro for us, as they don't amount to any huge dps increase. Even then, dps is only a relatively minor component for SKs in terms of generating points on a mob's Hate List.

Think about it. Hate is generated more from the snare component of the Darkness line, whether it lands or not. Debuffs, Stuns, Slows, and Fears are the same story. Weapon augments are another example...the additional stun component of the Ykesha augments is much better aggro than a straight dd proc augment.
