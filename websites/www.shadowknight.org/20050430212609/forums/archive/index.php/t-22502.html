Well, I started a hobby twink a couple days ago myself. Although the new toon isn't an SK, at these lower levels and with the availability of cheap gear, where I am now and going next wouldn't change whether I played a melee or hybrid for a while anyways.

I'm using a 65 druid to PL (of course, have to use 49 and below spells to buff and PL with) and I went to OT at lvl 15 and hit lvl 30 last night. I do plan to go to GD next, to kill wolves and try out some FG scouts. However, I just got my OT hammer last night (oh, this is a BST twink) so I'm thinking of staying in OT a bit longer so I can get to non-kos at the outpost so when I hammer in I don't get aggro'd and such.

Although I am not planning to PL the absolute fastest possible way, I prefer to get the same quest items I have for my other characters, and therefore I try to PL in zones where I can faction and xp at the same time. I plan to do the OT Hammer [got], 8th Ring [GD], Bracer of Benevolence [GD], Woodie Crown [Seb] -- so I'm heading to those areas so that I can get decent xp, even though they aren't the BEST xp/PL zones. I have about 10 hours played, including time spent working on spell skills.

I guess I am saying, I'd suggest planning ahead on certain must-have quests and try to hit those zones so that you can get faction plus decent xp at the same time; possibly rather than hitting the absolute best xp zones and later going back to faction for quests when the mobs are now green.
