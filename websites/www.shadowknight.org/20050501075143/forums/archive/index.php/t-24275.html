Heh, I've done a decent amount of soloing in droga at various points... Always considered the casters to be the "relax and take a break" mobs though, and the melee's the annoying ones. ;) Not that anything in droga is particularly hard - stuff hits for like... low 200's max and doesn't have a lot of HP. Pretty good XP for how easy the stuff is.

You should be able to toe-to-toe in droga - there are three areas in the zone of different level ranges that should cover 60-70. Many pulls come single, those that don't can be pet pulled using your normal pet until you get the 68 pet, at which point you'd have to swap out. FD pulling works too if you're good at managing casters.
