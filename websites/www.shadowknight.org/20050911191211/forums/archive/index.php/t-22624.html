There is a pillar type support leading up to a drianage pipe on the left hand side of the room. You can run up this pillar, then jump on to the top of that pipe. From there, you can run and jump on to a ledge, that leads all the way around the room. If you position your cleric/support team here, and then position yourself further towards the back of the room you are able to pull the ancients up out of the water on tothe ledge with you. This should be a ranged only fight unless you have plenty of healing power and your group is able to take an AE rampage fight.

Pull with something high aggro like Asystole or a few chain CDs in order to let slow hit early, and you'll be fine. The funny thing abou this particular encounter is if you are in the water with the mites you will not be able to cast on them. So you must be out of water to cast on them. This ledge is the only way weve ever been able to succesfully do it, and seems to be a widely used tactic.
