Once the mirror is handed to the Tradesman to spawn Dalnir, you're done with it. You were never supposed to give the mirror to Dalnir. You get it back the first time (as the cracked mirror) because you really shouldn't know what Dalnir wants ahead of time and therefore you might need to respawn him again at a later time. Of course, since we've all read the walk through, we are prepared the first time Dalnir is spawned, so the cracked mirror can later be used to help another SK.
