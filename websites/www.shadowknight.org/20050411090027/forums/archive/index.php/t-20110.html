Kazadeth
You can do the UA HT and Leechcurse now.

To get it to work, do your UA HT then open your combat window (ctrl+c). There will be an icon showing that UA is curently activated. Click it to turn it off.

You can now activate Leechcurse.

They are no longer on the same timer but you can only have one of them activated at a time which is why you click off UA in the combat window. If you don't, you have to wait the 20 mins or whatever it is until the UA effect fades, then you can use LC.

Hope that helps.
