Depends on the fight:

Varns - in the air, due to the revamp
Olidan - Nasty, but we did it with a raid force of about 20. Tank was decently geared, but not uber warrior. If you can take the named Ocean giants that drop medallions, Faruek the Bold and such, you can take him.
Hate - Camp camp camp away!
Fraga- Get him slowed and crippled ASAP, and spam quick heals. Best to have two clerics. Once does HoT, quick heal, HoT, etc. Other just spams quick heal. Bring a tank with about 7.5k buffed HP, mayb 1500 or better buffed AC.

That's about all I have
