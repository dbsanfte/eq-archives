Groff Gorespiller
I can confirm that information is correct, Vici.

When Night's Dark Terror is earlier in the buff list than MC (or any of our other proc spells), it's proc takes over, giving a 35hp lifetap proc. When one of our proc spells moves higher in the list than NDT, our proc takes over.

Most of our chanters will put NDT on the melees in LDoN Groups or Raids and this has been the case for me every time. Typically what I will do to keep NDT from rising higher in the buff list is to junk buff myself with clicky grim aura before the rest of the buffing starts, then have the chanter do NDT last (has to wait until inside the zone/dungeon anyway), then click off Grim Aura and put MC in that spot. Occasionally, as buffs wear off and are re-applied, NDT will rise higher than MC in my list, and I have to either click it off or wait until it's about to wear off and do some quick re-structuring in order to get the correct line up again.

Edit:

Oh, regarding AoP, if any member of your group is out of buff slots when you cast any of the group recourse spells, it will give you the "Your spell did not take hold" message. Did you look and see that the AoP recourse icon was indeed not in your buff list? Chances are you might have just received the message because it wouldn't stick/stack on one of your group members. I get this quite frequently when I'm not MTing and we have our tank buffed to the nines including Voice line. I'll cast and Aura of Darkness and his won't hold because he's got his slots filled. In cases where one group member's buff slots are filled, it is usually better to just tell them to click off the recourse anyway, as not having a slot open for HoT's makes the cleric angry... shammies too. ;)
