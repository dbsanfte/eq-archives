Don't touch AAs until level 65, except for (and this is argued, its up to you) getting RunSpeed3 when you can, if you pull a lot, etc. (Though you could easily get the 3 AAs from planar progression if you're in a guild that is doing any of it, or even with the single group alternate key quests). For 1HS, I honestly couldn't say, I forget what all has level requirements; Adjuctant's Saber (sp?) is rec.56 I think, and is a decent little weapon, there is also a nice 1hander out of Riwwi I think, but I forget if it has a req. or rec. level on it.
