After bailing out of Frontier Mountains (hunting goblins) I passed the place holder for the VP skeleton in Dreadlands. He con'd even to me so I thought I'd give it a try. No pet and only grim aura in my buff box I figured it'll be a FD fight. As luck would have it, straight up duke it out. ED and a heat blood, tap tap tap......

Finished the first one off with zero mana and abou 25% health.

Made a pet, recast grim aura and waited until I was full health and mana. Loaded undead fear then pulled the next skeleton with ED. Sent Jobeker in, landed fear on it and proceeded to bash it, smash it over and over. Fear and darkness broke a couple of times, easy enough to reapply as necessary. Using this tactic I finished the fight at about 50% health and 30% mana.

This is a 6 minute static spawn, never wanders. I killed 6 of them for a total of 22% xp at level 34. Reasonable gear, nothing stupendous (see magelo in sig). All were even, yellow and red con's.

Hope this helps somebody.
