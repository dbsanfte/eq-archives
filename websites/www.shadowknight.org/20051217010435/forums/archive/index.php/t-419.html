With the chanter hastes, shammy buffs, and 3 damage dealers, you are ready, BUT...... as stated, the Black Reaver can insta-spawn multiple times before Lord Ghiosk (or a ghost) pops. With that in mind you will probably need to have another healer (preferable cleric for Complete Heals) to take him on. If you are lucky, he could pop first time and your group could handle, but you really need to prepare for worst case.

In my experience, Lord Ghiosk pops from the Black Reaver just outside the castle, so a rogue should not be necessary. I may be wrong here, so it wouldn't hurt to have one on standby.
