Shield Mastery:
Three Levels. Gives you a shielding mod equivalent to the shields AC/10 while wearing a shield at level 1. Gives you an increase to your parry skill equal to the shields AC at level two. Gives Parry the success rate of Block and a 2nd success check if the first parry check fails at level three. Also for Warriors only add half the AC of the shield to the damage bonus of their main hand weapon at level 1 of the ability. War/Shd/Pal.

Strengthened Bash:
Increases the damage bash can do while having a shield equipped. Three levels of the AA each removes the cap by 1/3, so for example if you have a 55 AC shield with no AA your bash uses 25 AC for damage check, with level 1 1/3 of that ac over the cap would be added giving you 35 AC for damage check (10 or 1/3), level 2 would be 45 AC (20 or 2/3), and level 3 would allow you the full AC of the shield for damage. War/Pal/Shd only, prereq of shield mastery level 3 for knights, no prereq for Warriors.
