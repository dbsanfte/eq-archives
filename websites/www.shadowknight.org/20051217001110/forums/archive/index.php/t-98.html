#1... Don't pull the queen, fight her where she stands.
#2... Dark/FD is good for splitting her guards out of the room.

Get a few guards out, rush in and do damage fast while enchanters handle the adds. You can't resist the AE, so go for hp gear, have clerics use group heals, but the important thing is to do damage as fast as you can. If memory serves, normal HT works well after tash, UA/HT normally got resisted without a disease debuff. She has like 16k hp but you have to take her down in about 20-30 seconds or the AE will get you.

My guild sends in a DA Pal to make her waste the first AE, buying you a few seconds before it recharges. If done right, only one AE should ever hit you.
