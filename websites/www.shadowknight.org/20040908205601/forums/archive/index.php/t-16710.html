I'm pretty sure the ikky ac bonus is 50ac. From level 1 to level 65. If you were put up to an equally equiped sk of another race, yes you could equip your fungi and still be 50ac more than them.

The reason why people trade from fungi to a BP with more ac is because eventually you will get to the point to where 40+ more ac will do a lot more than 15hp/tick. Once you start tanking in pop type zones the 15hp/tick will mean nothing compared to the mitgation a 50+ac BP could give you.
