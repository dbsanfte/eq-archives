Why did I do this quest?

1. This sword might not be all that great for you extensive bazaar shoppers or higher level ppl out there, but the stats were slightly better than the Noctivigant Blade I've been using for the last few levels.
2. The proc. This thing has already saved my backside when soloing more times than I can count.
3. It's a 1hs. I like being able to still weild a shield and bash those pesky casters.
4. The satisfaction of having completed the quest, particularly as a dark elf. Parts of this quest took my places I might otherwise never have gone, and parts were very enlightening. Admittedly, tho, some parts DID bore me to tears.
5. The graphic. Seriously, how many swords do you see in-game that have this graphic. I have personally only seen one, and that's the Greenmist.

Also, while some of you may say that roleplay is dead, I find that I enjoy my play time much more if I'm not in such a mad rush to level-up. Taking time out for quests allows me to meet new people, go to new zones, flesh out my skillset, and otherwise have more fun and become a better player. If this is not your thing, don't worry about it, but don't knock those who are into it.
