Ok For the Muckglider Breeder here are a bit more information

I tryed to times tonight to kill him..on the second time we dropped him easy.

First Try we learned this: 6 Spawns are Comfirmed agian and can be pulled torwards zone and left there. (Corpse campers) Way we got them out (died 4 times doing this) I aggro'd by standing torwards the ent of the room form zone in side (I was on the stairs) I used a quick spear to get aggro and then instant invis and ran for the zone in. as I ran had a mage do a coh (12 sec of running) and FDed once Coh landed. did this about 4-5 times to get all six out then trained them all down the hall and away from her.

Next we got her to 7% on the first try...would have won but first 45 secs we learned the resist of the mob which are as follows.

Disease and Posion is basically Nerfed...never got any to land with shaman or SK after doing Malos and Decay.

Has a really high fire resist....it was resisting about 60% of everything spell.

Has almost no Magic resist (unsnarable though) Bane, Black Steel, and Aura of Pain landed all the time.

Also has no Cold resist either

Hope this helps....btw the tank I had was a warrior with about 9.8k buffed and about 1600 ac had no real healing problems.

Murkglider also has a AE called Tendrial Strike (I think that's the name) has like 32 disease counters and does 500dd and 200 per tick dot that lasts a long time. Large range too


Last thing...I am not a supper powerful SK and nor was our crew most where not even EP and had no VT gear on casters. I buff to 9.1k and have 1700+ AC (mostly VT gear and mix of quest and other times)
