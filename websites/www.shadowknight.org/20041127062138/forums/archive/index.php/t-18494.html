Rather, it functions as a kind of limiter on what we can accomplish with those snares. Yes, it's a small limitation, but it's also one that's been relatively well balanced since the beginning.

I do like the idea of a delayed effect spell. Confusing Shadows:
Introduce 10-15% spell failure rate on target.
