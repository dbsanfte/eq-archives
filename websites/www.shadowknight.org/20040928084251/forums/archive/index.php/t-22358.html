Noctix
With a warrior you get unquestioned tanking superiority, a well defined role, and the benefit of an entire player base who thinks warriors are better tanks.

With a SK you get the flexibility to be the 3rd-8th best tank, puller, or dps. You can solo inefficiently in a pinch and have near agro-lock on trivial low con mobs. You have to fight against a player base that has no clue what your role is supposed to be and generally isn't interested in finding out.

It is possible to play a SK well and accomplish a lot of things in EQ. However, when they say that Shadowknights are a high difficulty class, they aren't kidding. SKs can be powerful if played creatively but they don't fit the stereotypical EQ group/raid mold well at all.

So, if you're mainly looking for a well defined role, you should listen to what everyone else said and make a warrior.
