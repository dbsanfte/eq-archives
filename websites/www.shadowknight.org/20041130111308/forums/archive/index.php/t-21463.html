Noctix
I've been thinking about the problem that SKs don't have any way to mitigate burst damage like most other tank classes do. While giving us a "mini-defensive" and/or a set of parry/riposte/etc discs would fix this, that doesn't really seem to be a very good fit in a role-playing sense.

I have been trying to think of a good way to mitigate burst damage that would fit with the SK class. Here are two ideas I've had. They're similar ideas and could probably use some tweaking but I thought I'd throw them out there for discussion.

First, my idea in spell form:

Spell: Breath of Death
Level 66
Mana 1000
Duration 20 min
Cast 10.0
Recast 10.0
Description: This is a self only buff. When the SK reaches zero hit points, this spell automatically complete heals them, then the buff disappears.

Second, here is a similar AA idea:

AA: Afterlife
3 Tiers, 4, 8, 12 points
Levels 66, 68, 70
Type: Automatic
Duration: 2, 4, 6 seconds
Description: Upon death, the SK can fight in invulnerable specter form for a brief period of time. If a heal lands during this time, it brings the SK back to life. If no heal lands, the SK dies as normal at the end of the effect.

The idea of both of these is that it would give SKs a way to mitigate burst damage by giving a "second chance". I like this as an alternative to a mini-defensive because it is more class defining and gives a sense of the Shadowknight relationship with death and the undead.

As I said before, the numbers could probably be tweaked. I wanted to get the idea out for discussion though, so feel free to debate, tinker, and flame.
