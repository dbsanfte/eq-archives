I'm Nolarr of Maelin Starpyre, currently level 64 ogre sk with the most horrid exp penalty possible. To get by level 63, I did chain pulling in BoT most of the time. Although you may get 1% every 3 kills, the kills come way faster than in Valor.. the grind should not be that bad.

Short of leeching off high level people in MPG or whatnot, the best way to get exp while making a significant contribution in a group is in WoS. And if you can hit 6500 Hp and 1350 AC, you can tank with 0 AAs. And if you can tank, just grab yourself a cleric and slower and you're good to go. People might call me insane, but I have done it no problem. But stay either on East Wall or West Wall.

The exp is considerably better than BoT, at somewhere around 50% regular exp per kill for me. Here's how to tank WoS, which is the best way you can get exp IMO, with 0 AAs. You gotta have SoW, or Spirit of eagle or something like that. When you pull, pull with Clinging darkness. This does negligible damage so you don't get summoned. But if it hits, it snares the mob slightly, so it can still get back to the group in a reasonable amount of time. After the first cast of CD, cast it again. With 2 casts, you now proceed to run in a circle and kite the mob until it is slowed. With this technique, you will never have to fight a mob unslowed. A slower will be able to malo/tash, and land slow without aggro ever going off you. If you feel the need, cast a third clinging darkness in case of lots of resists of slow. I'm not sure how many people are aware of this technique, but it works extremely well and has enabled me to out-tank warriors and make slowers very happy when they don't get beat on after their slow lands.

Go and experiment in WoS and you'll be surprised how fast you can rack up exp here, while dispelling the notion that newb SKs like us can't tank. Also from what I'm able to gather, you will eventually need to stop and collect AAs.. because trying to level up to 70 in WoS will take ridiculously long, and you will need the defensives/HP AAs to get to MPG. Good thing though, the AAs come fast. Hope this helped.

No, it was 40% for hybrid. the 20% penalty was for monks.
Class bonus/penalties used to be:

Warrior/rogue 10% less xp per level
Healers 0
Int casters 10% more xp per level
monks 20% more xp per level
hybrids 40% more xp per level

Racial penalties
humans 5% less per level ...well, was was SUPPOSED to be humans, but they fucked up and gave the bonus to halflings instead.

Barbarians 5% more per level
Ogres 15% more per level
Trolls/iksar 20% more per level

Whats worse, those penalties were MULTIPLIED by each other, so troll and iksar sks had to make not just 60% more xp than everyone else, but 68% more xp than everyone else.

Then came the xp change.

CLASS penalties were removed, however the rogue and warrior class bonuses were left in...because rogues and warriors were little bitches.

RACIAL penalties were left in. It is unknown and untested whether they removed the halfling bonus, or whether they gave that bonus back to the humans, where it belongs...(god being human sux0rs!).


These days, we get the backhanded xp penalty of having to buy both melee AND caster aas to match the same power upgrade that a melee can gain with pure melee aas and a caster can gain via pure casting aas.
