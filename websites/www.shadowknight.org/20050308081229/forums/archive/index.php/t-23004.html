TinyDiabla
Group with a healer and move them towards the zone out 2, near the plateau. There are usually no mobs there. Now get to beginning part of tunnel and get on your horse with bobbing corpse. Invis and run like hell to the plateau area and FD. Pull the horses so that you are close to your healer. You will find that your healer's spells can reach you even though they are not on the plateau with you.

did this with a four box and a friends sk...ran my sk through the caves...did a run and flop every few mobs...and regen a bit before next stage...as i dont have aa horsie...and then had others in group down below edge...

could have soloed them...but was easier/faster with heals from below...

couple more pieces of info...

1. anyone with a pet class can assist you from down below...just assist and pet attack...and pet warps up to help...did this with mage and shammy pets to speed dps...

2. normal heals work...but quiesence did not...may just simply be further away...or could be a block on heal over time spells...

3. mage could re-damage shield me from down below....but shammy could not cast regen on me...

4. others in group could not cast spells (slow, nuke, etc.) on the gloom nightmare from down below...

5. on the last one after i pulled it...i fded and let the other sk down below pet pull it down to group...it worked just fine...mob came down off of plateau and was promptly slowed, tanked, nuked and dead. so if you need a group to do this... anyone with a pet in group (doesnt need to be sk pet) can pull the mob down to the group once targeted from up on the plateau.

6. there is no restriction on going off the plateau vs getting up on it. on one of my pulls i was not watching were i was going...and went off edge...and had to go all the way back trough the caves again to get the next mob.

got 1 to drop on 4th horsie....guildie got it...ill go back with my bot team again...as we had to go to raid...
