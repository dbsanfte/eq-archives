Dead right Stasus :)

As a gnome, you can't Slam (= bash with 2 hander equipped) till you get that AA.

Once you do, you're on a par with large race SKs and can get the greater damage output of 2 handers without losing your bash.

Till then I'd be inclined to use a good one hander and shield by default, while keeping 2Hs up if you can.

Bash is handy for interrupting caster mobs, and spamming it on non-casters adds significant extra hate.
