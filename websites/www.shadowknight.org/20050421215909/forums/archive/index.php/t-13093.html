Easiest way to start the quest is this: Get your factions. There's NO RUSH here since you're just starting out getting your main up - so unlike the rest of us, you can kinda XP as you do this, which is nice, instead of it just being a drudgery thing. You can get the factions however you want... thousands of bonechips, killing the Forsaken Iksar in Warsliks and LOIO, Explorer Knives - however you want. Take a look at what each bit entails and decide what you wanna do. You're going to HAVE to kill the Forsaken Iksar for the Necro Faction and honestly, I think the Knife Quest is the highest amount of faction hit per turn in for the rest - maybe you just wanna do those. Test it and play with it a little.

As to when it comes time to get the Khukri Quest started, you don't even need a friend to help ya...

You've got max faction with Crusaders now, so just go up to the top level of the tower and talk to the guy behind the Arch Duke. He'll lead you into a quest to gather Sarnak Hatchling heads - and he wants four. He'll give you a bag. Go outside into LOIO and stay right around the complex of buildings. Kill every Goblin of any kind you see, and eventually, Hatchlings will spawn. Kill them until you have four heads. Put the four heads in the bag and combine it. Return to Arch Duke Whatshisname.

Log out at the feet of the Arch Duke's friend behind him and make a noobie Iksar SK. You'll spawn right there at the Arch Duke. Hand him the Note, he'll hand you a lame Khukri. Turn around and give it to the other guy. DO NOT HAIL ANYTHING. Don't say anything, don't talk to them, nothing. If you do this right, this character will be deleted without ever speaking. Once you've handed in the Khukri, log out and back to Main.

Main hands the combined sarnak bag thingie to the guy near Arch Duke, and poof - you've got the first No Drop Khukri.

Now you just have to do all the rest of it. :)
