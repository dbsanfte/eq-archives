Tilelyanea
I find myself enjoying the task system in Omens, perhaps just because it gives me an excuse not to deal with idiots, but I was wondering if anyone else had done any tasks and what they have thought?

I've done 1 short, 2 medium, and 2 long tasks all taking place in the temple of Droga or the Mines of Nurga. Having soloed there it was a great help knowing a little about the zone.

I can say that at lvl 65 a medium task will get you 1 gold AA exp and 116 plat, whlie a longer one will get you 8 Blue AA exp and 175 plat.

Although I tend to get distracted by names and things, the typical longer task i have been doing involves visiting 2 or 3 locations ((invis)) and killing about 20 light blue to dark blue mobs.

Being as how we are the only tank class that can self invis, it seems to me that knights are built very well for the new task system, we can invis past everythign we dont need to kill, and assassinate what we do need to kill. In addition the aggro radius of gobbies in droga/nurga is fairly small, so you can usually get single pulls, with the occaisional add only causing you to lifetap a little more.

As i do a few more tasks I'll try to get an average time spent to complete, so far the biggest problem is finding where what you need to kill can spawn, and a little knowledge of the zone can solve that.
