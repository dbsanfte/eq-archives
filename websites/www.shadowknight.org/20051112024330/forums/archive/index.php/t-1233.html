Seriously, there are some nice things to be said for all 3 of the factions, so it primarily depends on what you want out of the game. If you want to go for the High AC armor with minor stats and the Collar of the Storm, then Giant Faction is the way to go. If you want decent armor with stats or you plan to quest for the Coldain Rings/Shawls, then work on Coldain Faction. If you want moderately high AC armor with stats and resists, then you will need Dragon Faction - which means you have to kill giants, especially Storm Giants. And if you are killing giants for the Skyshrine (Dragon) armor, then you will probably want to maintain your Dwarf Faction. But all of this assumes you plan to work on the uber high-end game.

If you are a casual player and plan to hunt alot in Velious, I would recommend you stay on Coldain Faction for the access to the Thurgadin Merchants. I personally would not trash my Coldain faction just because it seems like the way to go for the sake of Roleplay - after all, for RP, the Giants already HATE you. Just my opinion!! Do the research and determine your goals, then curry the favor of the faction you need to achieve it.
