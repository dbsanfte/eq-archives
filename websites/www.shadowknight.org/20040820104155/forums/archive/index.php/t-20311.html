A couple things for people who don't bring trackers:

1.- Kazel wanders in the large valley directly to the right and a little down on the map. If ya can't find him run up on the large middle hill and face the small undead camp there. He normally stops in front of it for a short bit.

2.- Xenyari runs all over the damn place around that druid ring, and she's a half-elf. So if ya don't see her, just wander around a bit and you'll eventually see a half-elf running around.

3.- Oh and if you accidentally attack kazzel during the fight, just continue fighting the zombie. I had virtue, and the zombie was mostly on the cleric while i was screwing around, lol, but kazzel didn't really do all that much damage. Just loot teh heart and evac or run to feerott zone, and you are good for hand in.
