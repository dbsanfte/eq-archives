Joining Clan Blackwatch

There are some simple and painless ways to join the Clan. Below is how it works...

How to Join

1. Hunt and generally get to know members of the guild

2. Read, Understand and agree to the Code of Conduct clanblackwatch.com

3. Find a full member to sponsor you

4. Fill out our membership form (located below)

Once you have been invited:

5. Register at Guildmagic.com and add yourself to the Clan Blackwatch roster

6. Anyone not following these steps will not be invited. Anyone not registering at Guildmagic will not be a full member until they do.

Requirements for Initiates and Sponsors

Once an applicant is invited into the guild, he/she becomes and initiate and begins his/her probation period. Your probation will last up to but no longer that one month at which time the leader and officers will discuss and vote on your membership. If you recieve a majority vote, your status in the guild will change from initiate to full member.

During their probation, initiates will NOT be treated any differently that any other member. They will have the same right to raid loot as any one else. The only purpose for the probation period is to ensure that the initiate's personality is compatible with others in the guild.

A sponsor is responsible for making sure the initiate has read and agrees to the Code of Conduct, knows where the message board is AND reads it. You must also be sure that the initiate adds themselves to the guild roster at www.guildmagic.com. A sponsor must be available to answer any questions the initiate might have.

*** Note: Returning members and New members from DAoC will not require a sponsor.
