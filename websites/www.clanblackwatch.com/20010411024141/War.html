EFFECTIVE GROUP COMBINATIONS - 4 Warriors and 2 Shaman

There are many group combinations that work but one that I have found extremely effective is a 4 warrior 2 shaman combo. After personally using this combo numerous times I can say that it results in hardly any down time and good exp. The warriors deal with all the offensive combat action while the shamans buff and heal. The shaman that I have done this with often find themselves with more mana than they know what to do with and the warrior's hardly lose any health. Keeping the warriors fully buffed makes them able to deal out between 250-500+ points of damage a round on the low side and upwards of that if the warriors have good weapons (BIBs or Exec Axes). The occasional DOT from the shamans can also prove useful. The mana they would normally use for DD spells though is better spent healing and rooting runners.
