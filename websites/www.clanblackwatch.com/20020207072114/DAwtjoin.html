Updated Policies:

Clan Blackwatch is always on the lookout for new members. We accept members of any race and class as long as they have good tendencies. We have a wide range of members from the 20-55+. As a rule we prefer members we've grouped with before mainly to make sure potential member's attitudes fit in with the guild. If you like what you see here, are interested in joining and play on the Morgan Le Fay server in the Midgard realm you can reach us by contacting one of the officers.

New Membership Policies

From this point on, any player wishing to be in Clan Blackwatch will require a Invitation* into the guild. When you are invited in you will be rank 9 and retain the status of Clan Initiate. Initiates can do everything except bear the Clan emblem. After a two week period there will be a re-evaluation and a promotion to Clan Defender if everything has worked out well.

Invited Applicants: Anyone who hunts with us on a regular basis can speak to us in game and arrange a Invitation.

You Must Read and understand our Code of Conduct.
You Must Fill out our Membership Form and group with our members.
After joining, you will be expected attend events for at least a month (Level permitting)
or until you know all members and we know you.
Applicants in Need of a Invitation: For those who want to join but don't have a Invitation.
We want References from Members you have hunted with or Allies (if member of Allied guild).
You Must Read and understand our Code of Conduct.
You Must Fill out our Membership Form and group with our members.
After we receive your Membership Form our officers will consider you for Invitation.

There are a few additional rules involved when inviting new members into Clan Blackwatch:

All new members must agree to the Code of Blackwatch.
You must be sure they filled out our Membership Form.
Any player not providing this Information will not be invited.
No player is to be invited until at least 3 officers approve the Invitation.
Any member breaking the Code of Blackwatch will be disciplined or removed from Clan Blackwatch

Following these rules is a requirement of being an Officer. Any officer who repeatedly invites members without getting this information will lose their Officer status.

*Invitation Defined as: Player has met all membership requirements, and 3 Officers and a Recommending Member find the applicant to be a well rounded, informed and fair minded player with a main Char no lower then level 20

**The rules and regulations of the Clan can be modified without notice or notification.**

Please read our code of conduct before applying.
If you feel you would like to apply for membership please fill our Membership Form.
If you have any Questions contact a officer in-game.
