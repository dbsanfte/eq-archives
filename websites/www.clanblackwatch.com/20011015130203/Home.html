Faydedar Falls - July 7, 2001

After three previous battles and one false start the mighty Faydedar finally fell. There was not one death as a result of the battle. It was a perfectly executed dragonslaying. The forces fo Praxium, Holy Fire, Servants of Mystery, Path of Valor and Clan Blackwatch assembled on the beach. After a quick and decisive fight Faydedar crashed to the ground dead and broken. The victory was a triumph to teamwork and organization - yes it does work and yes victory is a result. We fought, we lost, we learned and the reward was victory.

 

Velkator's Labyrinth Raid a Success - June 25, 2001

Members of Clan Blackwatch, Praxium, Holy Fire and Qestors Moy stormed Velkator's Labrynth on Sunday. The adventure took us past our previous trips stopping points all the way to Velkator himself. A battle was engaged with Beldrak a golem that when defeated spawned the smaller golem Bled. At the end of the rough fight we visited the imprisioned Coldain nad then headed out.
