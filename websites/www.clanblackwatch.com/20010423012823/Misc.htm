Miscellaneous Rambling

Here is some miscellaneous & personal opinion stuff that I just wanted to write down. 

Finding Directions

Many people think that we druids always know our way.  Now, as someone who has travelled most of Norrath, and has almost 200 skill in sense heading, I can tell you that is not the case.  As someone recently mentioned, possibly I can get a gps soon, because I still can't tell east from west, or find my way directly from the Misty Thicket druid circle to Rivervale...

Buffing & Teleporting

As a druid, you will often be asked for buffs (especially SoW) and teleports.  How you choose to react is up to you, but as usual, I would recommend being nice.  It is easy to forget how difficult it can be to get around Norrath.  In fact some druids remember this well, and make a decent living teleporting for money.  I guess I am not a great salesperson, because I usually don't make much, but often just "throw around" some buffs and teleports here and there.  Some people can be quite obnoxious, but most are nice.  Just ignore the annoying ones.

My usual policy is to take money for teleports when it is inconvenient (going somewhere I don't really want to go, and I was doing something else, and I have decided to actually port somewhere).  Often I port for free, just depends.  These days there are lots of high level players around, so the obnoxious spamming types usually find their ports pretty fast from someone wanting money.  A suggestion: if you have been hunting in a good group, and the group is breaking up, take your group members where they need to go.  This is usually much appreciated and really not much inconvenience for you, especially if you are logging soon anyway.

As for buffs, if you have some extra mana, SoW the people nearby.  If someone sits down next to you mostly dead, cast regen on them if you can.  If somebody is running from a terrible monster and seems to be in trouble, snare (or fear) it for them.  Obviously, there are some limits to this.  Don't try to snare a griffon if you are level 10 or something.
