Clan Blackwatch Titles

 

In order to achieve a title in Clan Blackwatch you must participate in a Quest which might involve retrieving items or facing a dangerous opponent in combat. If an item is retrieved it must be brought to a Clan officer or the Clan leader to gain the elevation in title. If defeating an opponent is the objective then a screenshot of your heroic battle or a Clan officer as witness to the event is a requirement. Each title must be completed in succession, no title can be skipped. Higher level members who have completed quests before the title system was in place are awarded titles based on their previous deeds.

In the future special distinctions and medals of honor will be introduced to acknowledge certain special quests, actions above and beyond the call and for those who have been proven to have special knowledge (a Clan master jewelsmith would be a good example of special knowledge)

Clan Initiate

Every Clan member begins as a Clan initiate. The initiate title is "free" and requires no special objectives. It represents the basic Clan member.

Clan Hunter

Clan Hunter is proof of a Clansman's ability to stand against danger. To receive the title of Hunter one must defeat the Gnoll Commander in Blackburrow.

Clan Defender

A Clan Hunter must prove their expertise by retrieving Dvinn's Heart from Crushbone. They must engage in combat with members of like level , slay Dvinn and gain the heart.

Clan Crusader

The Clan Crusader must brave the dangers of Castle Mistmoore and return with the fabled Mistmoore Shield. The quest is not for the faint of heart as all the cursed legions of Mistmoore will be pitted against the strength of the would be defender. Return with the shield and others will know of your courage.

Clan Hero

Evil is always present in the lands of Norrath. In the bowels of Cazic Thule lies the Avatar, a creature so foul that it must hide for fear of detection by the forces of light. From below the soil of Norrath it seeks to pervert all that is pure to decay and filth. It requires a hero to defeat this incarnation of the god Cazic Thule and banish it back to its domain. Defeat the Avatar of Fear and the title of Clan Hero will be yours.

Clan Legend

Only the most dedicated and heroic Clan members will be able to achieve the title of Clan Legend. To become a legend one must face the most fearsome terrors of Norrath. Victory over the dragons Vox and Nagafen or the slaying of Cazic Thule or Innoruk qualify a member for legend status. A Legend's tale of valor, strength and perseverance against the forces of darkness will ring throughout the history of Clan Blackwatch.
