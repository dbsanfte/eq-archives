Two Dreams of Technology

1.

I xerox the words
of a great leader's speech
and left the copy in a newspaper box
wondering if anyone would pay
or call it news.

2.

Soon the telephone wire
will around the peach tree
and we will sit underneath
on pine needles
which the gardener
has delicately picked out
from all the messages
that a tree
gives to a whisper.

Francis Raven
