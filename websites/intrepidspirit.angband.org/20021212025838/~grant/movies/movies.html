Grant's Movie Collection


So, I like movies. Hey, who doesn't? Well, my enjoyment of movies kinda took off when I started being a movie critic for The Technique, Georgia Tech's student newspaper. Further helping this little addiction along was my purchase of a DVD player. When I started seeing all the neat little features of DVD's, and all my friends started buying all their movies in DVD format, I knew it was time for me to jump on the bandwagon. Anyway, movies are pretty damn expensive, so my movie collection hasn't quite bloomed like my CD collection, though I think that there are a fair number of decent movies in my collection. Well, without further ado: my movie list.
