Welcome to the home page for Mark Yohannan's Shadowrun campaign. In this web site you'll find profiles on important characters (player and non-player), player reports for each mission, news from the world, and other interesting information.

In the campaign the players are not shadowrunners. They are members of a special investigations team for Knight Errant Security. They investigate strange happenings at facilities and in cities which have security contracts with Knight Errant. In addition, they are occasionally hired as outside investigators by those who use other security contractors.
