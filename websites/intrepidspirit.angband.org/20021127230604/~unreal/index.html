- - = = issue 9 = = - -


Welcome to the ninth issue of Curiosity's Escape. It's been a while, and I've been a very lazy editor. But I am going off to study in Spain tomorrow (july 1st) so it is a relief to get this zine out before I leave.

As always, this issue is chock full of goodies. I did a last minute look over all the stuff, so there may still be many errors. Just let me know and I'll fix them as quickly as I can.

oh, and another announcement, Curiosity's Escape is now hosting a sub-site called A Boy And His Pet Heart. This is a site that my friend iAn runs as a homepage to his shoutcast internet radiostation. Go check it out; he has a very cool CD collection full of independent stuff you have probably not heard of.

And before I leave, I want to thank Chris for helping out with the editing of this issue. Without his generous aid, this issue probably would've taken even longer to get out (believe it or not). Thank you!

If you would like to recieve news about our zine in your e-mail box, then please subscribe to our newsletter. Just type your e-mail address in the box below and hit the button. We don't send a lot of mail, so don't worry.

Well, happy reading, people!

-Jimmy.
