Directions to the Ghetto

The Geeky Way

The ghetto lies at North 34.02127, West 84.25690 at an elevation of 1075 feet. The above coordinates have a margin of error of +/- 14 feet, so people beaming or using teleport should be aware.

The Boring Way

As everybody will live south...

Take GA 400 North until exit 9. When you exit 400, head east ( right ) along Haynes Bridge Road for about 3.5 miles. When Haynes Bridge Road terminates at Old Alabama Road, you will need to turn left onto Old Alabama (if you continue straight, you will go into a subdivision). At this intersection there will be a BP and Publix on the left.

After you turn left onto Old Alabama, go straight through 2 lights. You will pass a large church (St. Bridget's Catholic Church) on the right, just before the 3rd light. Turn right into the Preston Oaks subdivision at the 3rd light. To clarify, the first light is at a firestation, the second light is at Brumbelow Road ( or something like that ), and the 3rd one is my light.

After entering the ghetto subdivision, take the third left, and follow it until it terminates in a cul-de-sac. My place is the one directly opposite the road, number 475.
