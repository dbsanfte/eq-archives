 Rahl's Language Training
1) Left-click the SOCIALS button 2) Right-click any of these you won't need(how about the Duel one?)
3) Re-name it to something(I suggest SPAM) and type in whatever you would like to say while teaching, it doesn't matter what it says, but use all 5 lines available.
4) Place it to to right as a HOT-KEY(mine is set to the # 2 in this example)
5) Left-click on OPTIONS
6) Left-click on CHAT FILTER
7) Left-click on GROUP CHAT [ON/OFF] to switch
8) Follow these instructions(5-8) again to turn it back on; these are the only steps(5-8) to follow if you are LEARNING ONLY!
9) Right-click in the bottom/middle of your inventory window where all the speech and commands show up.
10) Left-click on the Language until you have changed to the one you will be teaching
