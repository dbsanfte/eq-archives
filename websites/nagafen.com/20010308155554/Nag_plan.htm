Things Not To Do On A Vox Raid

  • Never use any offensive AE (area effect) songs, weapons, or spells, during, before, or after the attack. 
  • Do not evac. Either camp out, or die and let the CR team take over. 
  • Do not complain if you die multiple times, this aggravates others and lowers the morale of all groups. You knew what you were signing up for, so take it in-stride.

Notes

  • Dragon Fear spell is the biggest inhibitor to a successful dragon raid. Due to this, only level 50 players will be in the first 4 groups, and level 49+ players in the remaining groups. These players have the highest chance to resist the Dragon Fear based on their level, thus it is logical that they should be the ones in the raid.
