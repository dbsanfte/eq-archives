The Loot List

The loot gained from Nagafen will be rotated among the guilds in the following order. The guild abbreviations marked in green represent the current item that a guild qualifies for. Non-guilded members particpating in a raid must be sponsored by a guild to be be considered for loot. Over consecutive successful dragon raids, as the guilds get items on the list, the guild beneath them in the list will qualify as next for loot (and be marked in green).
