Loot


Loot is unimportant, fighting in a multiple guild organized event is probably the most fun you can possibly have in EQ. That should be all the reward you should need. BUT, since loot does exist, and there is not that much of it, it has to be distributed fairly. Below you will find links to loot conduct and loot tables.

 

Looting in dragon raids:
 

The loot gained from dragon raids will be rotated among the guilds in the following order. The guild abbreviations marked in green represent the current item that a guild qualifies for. Non-guilded members participating in a raid must be sponsored by a guild to be be considered for loot. Over consecutive successful dragon raids, as the guilds get items on the list, the guild beneath them in the list will qualify as next for loot (and be marked in green).

Looting in the planes:
 

Loot will be distributed in the following order:

  1. By need - Determined by guild masters/guild officers.
  2. By mutual decision or random number among applicable classes - It will be decided who gets the item based on class need, then by random number.

If the majority of people object to this method, we will fall back on distributing loot by random number.

Note: only the group leader should first check the contents of the mob - unless the mob has a probability of carrying an item the group leader may be eligible for.
