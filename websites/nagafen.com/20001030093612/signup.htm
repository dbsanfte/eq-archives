If you are not guilded and would like to participate:

You must get a sponsor from either CIW, MFH or KEG - using the Request Sponsorship forum. If you are sponsored, you will be treated like one of our own. 

WARNING: If you are not invited and decide to "tag along" in any of these events, you may consider yourself on your own. Often these trips mean dying - sometimes repeatedly over and over. Sometimes dying is part of a tactic to "break in an area". Sometimes it will take extraordinary tactics just to perform corpse recovery - or help you in a precarious situation. If you are not invited on a trip, we discourage you to follow for your own  well being as well as our own.
