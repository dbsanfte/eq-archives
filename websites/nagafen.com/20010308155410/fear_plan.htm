The Fear Plan

Disclaimer: This is a proven strategy if executed properly. Special thanks to BotS guild on the Rathe for discovering the north wall camp spot. Here is what you must be prepared for in order for it to be successful:

  • You will die at least once during initial setup.
  • Be prepared to die about 4 times throughout the course of the evening. If this price is too high to pay, please notify me and you will be removed from the Fear raid.
  • Have a newbie character ready.
  • Know who your group leader is, so that you can send them tells as to when it is safe to log-in/zone/etc. during initial setup.
  • Be on time. The raid starts at 3pm EST. If you are late, you will miss the opportunity to run to camp, die, and be resurrected. If you are late, your next opportunity to log in will probably be much later, when the area around the portal is cleared.

The Plan

  1. The CR (corpse recovery) group will be logged into Fear and camped at the North wall Camp (1390, 500) prior to Fear spawning. After camping, they will be reentering the game as newbie characters until it is time to log in and start resurrecting.
  2. At 9pm EST we will start sending in people one at a time. You will be fully buffed. You will have invisible or superior camouflage, see invisible, resist magic, dead-man floating, and spirit of the wolf, in addition to any health buffs available.
  3. Upon zoning in, you will immediately run Northwest toward the Tentacle Terrors. Then run due north towards the wall of fire along the path shown on this map. Do not run blindly. If you see a spinechill spider or shiverback, try to steer clear of it. They can see invisible. You probably wont see Scarelings, but they will see you, so be prepared for a quick death.
  4. If you fail to reach the camp spot, we will recover your corpse once that area is secure.
  5. Once you reach the camp, let whatever mobs are on you (80% chance for rookies to be spotted :>) kill you. That's right, die there.
  6. If by some stroke of luck nothing saw you. Camp out. Log in as newbie.
  7. Send your group leader (or Shomari .. aka Snaggle, if group leader is unavailable) a message that you are "in". At this point we will send in the next victim.
  8. Once everyone is in and safely dead, we will begin ressing the Scout group and the Mez group. Any members of these groups whose bodies are currently irrecoverable will be temporarily replaced by members of other groups.
  9. The initial three groups will clear a relative safe spot around the camp. We will then start dragging corpses that didn't make it close enough in for rezzes.
  10. We will rezz the remaining groups. During this time we will try to minimize fighting due to the threat of packet loss and linkdeath.
  11. Once all groups are in we will begin to pull from the west, then make our way south.
  12. Once the zone is clear, we will clear the temple and make it safe to zone out.
  13. If Cazic Thule has spawned, we will attempt to kill him after we clear the temple.

Contingencies

  • If Cazic Thule and Draco have spawned the zone will be much more dangerous. To take down the Dracolich, we may reorganize groups to mirror dragon-raid type groups.
  • If everyone dies horribly due to someone training while CR is being performed. We will take the remaining groups and fall on the backup plan.

The Backup Plan

  1. Zone in invisible with three groups in rapid succession and secure the West Wall Camp.
  2. Once camp is secured bring in additional groups (if any).
  3. Kill any roamers first then work our way north then east. This plan is our traditional Fear plan, and usually involves much death and evacing. Eventually, it does work. But it hurts like hell.

Guidelines

  • Assist Snaggle. Make a button that types /assist Snaggle. Use this button whenever a pull is incoming. Once the target is dead, wait until Snaggle (that's me) engages the next target then mash your "Assist Snaggle Button." As the night progresses you may be changing your button to "Assist Yukitaro", or "Assist Vardamin", etc.
  • In the case of a pull with multiple mobs that are mesmerized, do not engage the mesmerized mob until the tanks have all taunted it at least twice (ideally three times). This keeps our enchanters/bards from dying.
  • If you find yourself in the position of being swarmed by mobs away from the group, die there. Do not pull a train into the group. There will be one official puller at all times. Anyone else who pulls, be prepared to die.
  • The group leader will be responsible for looting. Do not loot a mob in the midst of battle. Wait for the battle to cease so that your group leader may loot.
  • Do not, under any circumstance, ask a GM to respawn the zone. Doing so is bad. Very, very bad.
  • Do not invite GMs or Guides along to watch us. Although we love them dearly, they have been known to aggravate mobs and train parties in Fear. Furthermore, they usually observe our tactics, then report them to the development team. The development team likes to tweak things with the next patch to make them much harder. You are in OUR zone now. Grah!
  • There will be a pop quiz in the morning. This is not an open book quiz and grades will not be curved. Study your Fear Guidelines.

Loot

Loot will be distributed in the following order:

  1. By need - Determined by guildmasters/guild officers.
  2. By mutual decision or random number among applicable classes - It will be decided who gets the item based on class need, then by random number.

If the majority of people object to this method, we will fall back on distributing loot by random number.
