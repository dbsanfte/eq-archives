Report of Solusek's Eye, June 20, 2001

Our covert operations in the mines of Solusek's Eye were, unfortunately, not as covert as we might have liked. Initially, I had planned to lead a small team deep into the mines to retrieve some magical items that I require for my research, but we were quickly beset upon by hordes of goblins. These goblins bore a remarkable resemblance to the picklaw goblins which infest HighHold Keep, although they appeared to be allied with the gnomish miners and clockwork mining machines employed in the mines.

Once our team discovered the extent of the goblin presence in the mines, a messenger was sent out to bring forth the guard of the Cursus Honorum to distract the goblins, while a small team snuck deeper into the caves in search of precious minerals, metals, and relics for my research. The guard was able to penetrate and hold a large portion of the upper caves, thanks to the unflagging efforts of Regent Ardour and squad leader Lady Splendor. Alas, the goblins (or perhaps their gnomish allies) had secreted away many of the items we saught once they learned of our plans, although we did manage to relieve them of some rather nice looking goods.

The speed at which the goblins set upon us and their seeming cooperation with the gnomish miners makes me wonder if they had somehow been tipped off to our secret offensive... I will have to consult with Vice Regent Benedain to see if he can scry any insight into this matter.

Count Elyar Klennix
