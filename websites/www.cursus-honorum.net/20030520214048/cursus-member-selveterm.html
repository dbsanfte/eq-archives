Character History

I, Selveterm L'Vol, am the product of a brief indiscretion of my mother Sendariu and a half elven paladin whose name I have never known. Mere hours after my birth the Tier Dal stole me from my mother as a way of punishing her for her defection. She must have thought me sacrificed to Innoruuk, and truly I am surprised I wasn't, but instead they raised me in the Temple of Innoruuk and when I was of age my training as a Shadow Knight began. After I had reached majority and completed my training I was given a letter from my surrogate mother Ei-Vienne. It detailed my past and who I really was. Ei-Vienne was actually a follower of Tunare, a rare dark elf renegade who had turned back to the mother after the perversions of the father. She made me see the sins and lies of Innoruuk and to accept the fact that from the moment of my birth I have been a slave. I cursed Innoruuk that day and cast off my Holy Symbol, Ei-Vienne just smiled as she saw my faith in the Father crushed in one revelation. I left Neriak that day and went forth to find my mother and who I really was...
