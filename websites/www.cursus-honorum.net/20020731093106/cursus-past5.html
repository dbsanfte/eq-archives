Low rank training session

With the ever increasing threats against Norrath and our depleated armies, Rohwin has ordered that our younger ranks be prepared for the fights ahead. The Warrens will provide excellent training grounds to test our skills. The Kobolds that inhabit these caves are a simple lot, but they have dedicated themselves to fight against the higher beings of Norrath. The threat they pose is slight, but we must keep them subdued or someday they might grow to the power of their greater brothers in Nagafen's Lair.

OOC details

On Wednesday, June 27th, Rohwin will be hosting a training session for our lower ranking members. The Warrens will be an excellent place to learn the skills of combat tactics, crowd control, communication, and dungeon strategy. It will begin at 6pm PDT. Points will be awarded for levels 10 to 19 who can attend. Higher levels are welcome to come to share the fun or to help run the event. Bring your friends if you like. Please refer to the general message forum for comments and questions.

Results

The Kobolds took a hard lesson from our galant forces. They offered heavy resistance when we infiltrated their lair, but we quickly moved through their defenses and established ourselves in a chamber near their prince. They attacked in wave after wave but their attempts proved futile. The Kobold's repeated assaults left little time for training for our younger ranks. However, the lessons of battle are always best learned through direct experience. Before our supplies ran thin we fought our way back to the entrance of the lair and made our exit.
