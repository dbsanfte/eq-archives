Character History

Xxaiver was accused of murdering his own family in Felwithe. He evaded capture from the royal guard and found shelter among Cursus Honorum. When we discovered the accusations against him, we intended on returning him to Felwithe. However, the Hammers interceded and sent us on a quest in the pursuit of justice. We hid Xxaiver from Felwithe while we searched for the true killer. Kolvaran was the first to return proof of Xxaiver's innocence, and King Thex issued Xxaiver a pardon, clearing his name. Look further into these tomes for more information about Xxaiver's Redemption.
