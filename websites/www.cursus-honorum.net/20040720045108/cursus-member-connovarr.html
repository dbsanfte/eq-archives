Character History


Awards

A promising Barbarian Warrior, Connovarr was plagued by dreams that he did not understand, and grew to suffer bodily from. Fearing that it was somehow a curse laid upon him by a vengeful gnoll witch, he went finally to a village shaman, and was increasingly perturbed that the shaman could not tell him what he needed to know. After a very loud discussion on what he should do next, a name was mentioned of one who could possibly help him decipher his dreams, and overcome the ills that had befallen him since their arrival.
