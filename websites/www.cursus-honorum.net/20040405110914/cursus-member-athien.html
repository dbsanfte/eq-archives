Character History

Middle child of a very influential Felwithe House, Athien has been schooled nearly from birth in the cavalier arts, expected to follow his Father into the path of the Paladin. He has done so with surprising aplomb, his skill at arms showing promise rivaling that of his Father. He does not speak of his family, now that he is out to build his reputation. He strives to avoid the shadow which might preclude his own doings. He strives to be the epitome of the Holy Knight.
