Prof. Klennix, currently on loan to the AAS from his Felwithe, Faydark high council position, arrived in Freeport in 3190. Born in Felwithe to parents Elsyd Klennix, noted politician, and wife Reyna, Prof. Klennix spent his formative years studying under prominant academic Tarker Blazetoss of Felwithe.

Upon completion of his training, he was asked to take the position of Ambassador of Felwithe to then warlord Artillian Vincere, a placement which gained him some animosity amongst the High Elves, but suited Klennix's tolerant personality well.

After Vincere's disappearance, Prof. Klennix returned to Freeport after a stay in Felwithe, to take a position with the Academy to teach his now famous Thaumaturgical Archaeology class. He is still active in field work, and continues to campaign with Vincere's alleged regent, Paladin Commander Rohwin Ardour.
