Character History

I am Khala Dun. In Shar Val, that said would be enough. But this is not Shar Val, and you are not Vah Shir. So I will explain. I was bred, born, and raised for the defense of Luclin. I know not my parents as Khala Dun are raised by all Khala Dun. My earliest childhood memories are of fighting in the Arena at Shar Val. When I came of age, my masters sent me to Arelian as an emassary for my people. Though from different worlds, the fate of Norath is intertwined with that of Luclin, especially with the opening of the portals and the Plane of Knowledge. So I have been sent to fight evil lest it contaminate my home.
