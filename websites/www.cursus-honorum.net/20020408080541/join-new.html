How to join Cursus Honorum

Before applying to Cursus Honorum you should be sure that this is the right guild for you. You need to read through all the rules and understand and agree with them. There is a lot of information on this site so take your time reviewing us. The pages you should read are as follows:

1. Hierarchy if Nobles. This page outlines the power structure of the guild. It explains the branches of power, the different names given to our ranks, political points, and noble titles.

2. Code of Nobility. Our code is a list of the do's and don'ts. Most of these rules are common sense. This page also explains our recruitment standards and how to be discharged from the guild.

3. Guild History. Our guild history is the basis of our roleplay. It is important to be familiar with our storyline to interact with your guildmates. Our story grows and changes as we play. There may be important information on the recent history page as well.

4. "Why Join a Guild?" This editorial outlines some of the basic reasons to join a guild and some of the things to look for in a guild. It isn't required to read, but if you are looking for some guild advice it may be good to look it over.

Summary of our Requirements

Note: Some additional rules have been added to our application process. Please refer to our forums to read the updates. -Rohwin

1. The evil races of Ogres, Trolls, and Iksar will not be admitted. Tier'Dal may be invited if they role-play in a good manner, however, in addition to our regular recruitment procedures we will require a written account of the character's history to justify their good nature.
2. No Shadow Knights or Necromancers are allowed to join. It may be possible to make an exception to this rule, but it will be at the discretion of the High-Council.
3. No evil deities. Such as Bertoxxulous, Cazic-Thule, Innoruuk, or Veeshan. Exceptions to this rule would also fall under the discretion of the High-Council. Note: If you roleplay a deity other then the one you chose during character creation then please say so in the "charcter history" or "other comments" box.
4. All members should enjoy to roleplay.
5. All applicants need a voucher. Any officer in the guild may vouch for you. The vouching officer must be sure that you will enjoy your time in our guild. He or she may screen you as he or she sees fit. It may require grouping with them or simply talking at length with you. Every officer is different and may have different requirements. Once they do give their approval for you then it is time to fill out the application.

If you do not have a voucher you may apply anyways. In the voucher box you may state that you are seeking a voucher. Also, if you do not have a recruiter when you apply, then when you are invited into the guild you may declare a recruiter at that time. You may tell the Regent on-line or E-mail the Regent who you wish your recruiter to be on the day of your recruitment. If you have not declared a recruiter after a couple of days then the Regent will become your defualt recruiter.

Note: The voucher and the recruiter may, but not necessarily be different people. The voucher is which ever officer in the guild has given their approval of your joining. The Regent will speak with the voucher to make a final decision on who is admitted into the guild. The recruiter is who ever you feel is most responsible for your recruitment into the guild. You may only put down one name for your recruiter. If you truly can't decide between two or more people then whoevers name you put down first will be your declared recruiter. If you leave the recruiter box blank then the Regent will become the default recruiter. Who ever your recruiter is will earn the political point for your recruitment.

Oath of Fealty

I swear by my honor that I have read, understand, and agree with all the rules of Cursus Honorum. I have truthfully answered all the questions on the application. If I have purposely misled anyone from the truth then I expect to be removed from Cursus Honorum and have my name added to the list of previous members without honor. I will serve Cursus Honorum to the best of my abilities. I will fight and if necessary die for our cause. To serve Cursus Honorum will be my honor.
