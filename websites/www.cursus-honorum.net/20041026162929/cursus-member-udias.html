Character History

I am Udias, a lone wood elf and a druid of Tunare. My history spans years in which half I cannot recall and the other half I choose not to. *chuckles*. Ahem. I came to know Cursus Honorum many years ago through a mage associate of mine named Filivaa. His stories of chilvary, gallantry and honor within his guild drew interest from me. I recall the nights he would weave tales of battles including when Lord Regent Rohwin sailed with Artillian to Faydwer and fought to rescue its inhabitants and the cities in which they dwelled. Or Benedain and Elyar's journeys into that most foul of Gnoll lairs, Split paw. After one night of his story telling though, Filivaa vanished. I have been in search of him ever since but to no avail.
