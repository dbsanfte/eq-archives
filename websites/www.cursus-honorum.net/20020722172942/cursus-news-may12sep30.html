June 13, 2001

A warm welcome to our newest members. Splendor is a 19 Cleric of Tunare and Avekin is 10 Cleric, also of Tunare. I'm sure their contributions will be welcomed by everyone.

Also, the High-Council has voted on some new rules.

First, it has been determined that Rogues will be allowed to join our ranks. Their skills in clandestine operations will be a valuable asset. Those that act with nobility and hold the greater good of Norrath dear will be considered for invites.

Second, reformed Tier'dal will also be allowed to join us. Those that can prove to us that they walk in the light will be treated as equals. In addition to the regular inviting procedure, we will require that Tier'Dal offer a written account of their past that justifies why they prefer to leave their kindred behind and follow our banner.

Those who still follow evil deities are not yet allowed into our guild. However, the discusion continues on the subject of redeemed minions of Innoruuk and the like. If it is truly possible for previous minions of Innoruuk to forsake their past and join our cause, then it may be possible for those who study the arts of Necromancy and the Shadowknights to join us. I see a grand oportunity to study this question. Please read the post by Sendariu L'Vol in the general discussion board. Speak with her and judge for yourselves if we can ally with her, then post your thoughts so that we all may know. It is logical to think that intelligent beings will understand that our cause is rightous, and that they would choose to support us. Erollisi demands that we judge her with our hearts, not with our eyes.

We will be accepting applications from rogues and most Tier'Dal currently. I am in the process of updating the web-site, until it is finished please make any corrections to applications in the "other comments" box.

Rohwin

June 6, 2001

The High Council has voted on some rule changes. The ban against twinking has been lifted. We still encourage people to earn their own equipment, but everyone may play as they see fit. After all, only you can decide if you are having fun with EQ. The new rule in the "Code of Nobility" is as follows:

7. We discourage twinking, but you may play your characters any way you see fit. Use your own judgement on what is acceptable twinking. Also, it is not the funtion of this guild to twink its own members. No one will ever be obligated to give away equipment, or to sell at extremely good deals. Please do not ask your guildmates for free equipment. Remember, it is much more rewarding if characters earns their bounty on their own. Twinking is hard to define, but I have written an editorial that attempts to define twinking. I hope it helps.

Also, the "message of the day" has new rules. It has been added to the "Code of Nobility" as such:

16. The "message of the day" is reserved for the Regent and Vice-Regent only. Also, it should only be used to notify the guild of important matters of concern to everyone. The only exception is if there is an emergency situation where the guild needs to be informed in less then 24 hours, then any officer may post a "message of the day."

Journey safely everyone,

Rohwin
