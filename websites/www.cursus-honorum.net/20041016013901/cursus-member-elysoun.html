Character History

Born on a farm in the Karanas I lived a small sheltered life untill the age of five when my adopeted woodelven brother had left home to train as bard in the the forest of Greater Fay. Two weeks passed and a war raged in the Karanas near the farm I lived on. My father was a warrior and took up arms. His wounds suffered him till death. My mother died shortly after that of a broken heart I am sure. Neighbors brought me to the cleric guild in Qeynos in hopes I might find piece there. I trained in healing the soul and listening to stories of travlers and one day hoped to venture off. I later met Drallien, a proud warrior of Tunare and Ranger hailing from Surefall Glade. I fell in love. He left A year later to travel giving me his word he would come back for me. Another year passed and very little if any word had reached my ears of Drallien. I decided it was time to take my skills to the field and quest to find him. Upon adventuring I came across a wounderfull and dashing Paladin named Rohwin who sought aide in healing. I bound his wounds and asked the Prime healer to see that they healed and to bless this child of good with protection so that he may fight with honor and glory. We quickly became friends.

Recent events have led to distrubing news. The Temple of Life has excommunicated Elysoun for charges of allowing life to perish. By her own request she has been stripped of any title of cleric or healer. She now seeks the power of her arts from within herself. By result of these turns of events she has been placed under Earl Elyar Klennix's observation.
