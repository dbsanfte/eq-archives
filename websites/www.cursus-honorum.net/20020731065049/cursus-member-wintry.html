Character History

Abandoned as an infant, this waifish monk was miraculously found by a Coldain scouting party seeking shelter in some caverns during a most furious storm. The baby was taken back to Thurgadin, and given over to the care of a childless couple. This is where she spent the first few years of her life, and became a devout follower of the goddess E'ci. As a young girl, it was decided best to send her back among her own kind, and she was fostered with the monks of Freeport. She has learned discipline, humility and subserviance. Wintry abhors the southern climate, and looks forward to the day when she can return to Velious, and repay the kindness of the Coldain, and her adopted parents.
