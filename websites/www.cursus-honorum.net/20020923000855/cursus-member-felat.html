Character History

I was birthed in Rivervale by me Mama and Pappy Taggelfoot. They are wonderful parents and raised me proper and humble. Me heritage folk were corn growers for many generations. Me parents still live on the corn farm in Rivervale.

Schooling wasn't a big concern in my house so I took it upon myself to go to the library at a young age and be lessoned in reading and writing. It wasn't really a library then. It was just a shack where old hermit lady boobybop lived. She kept a book shelf with about 337 books on it. She taught me the reading and writing mostly because she wanted someone to talk too. Me pappy called it a fruitless waste of good time, however I kept learning. Me brothers and sisters made fun of me for it and I was kinda considered a trouble maker by the towns folk for not listening to me Pappy and helping more with the corn. I think me Mama secretly liked me learning to read, because she would always tell Pappy to leave me be about it.

In me teen year’s lady Boobybop died; very sad for me it was. Her books and belongings were left locked in the shack for months. A town meeting was held about the books. That’s when I was appointed head of the Library Committee. This was kind of a joke by the town’s folk, because there was no Library committee until they put me on it. Since they made a Library Committee and I was the head of it, I rebuilt the shack and made it in to the Boobybop Town Library. I was quite proud of the place. Many townsfolk were outraged, because they either wanted the Library torn down and the books burned, or the wanted it named after them. They held a meeting and tried to remove the Library Committee form the books, but the laws wouldn’t allow it to be removed until three years passed. I was the Head of the committee and they couldn't do a thing about it.

One day, I suspect someone; they burnt down the Library and all the books in it. I was hurt badly by the smoke, trying to save the books, and spent many days under Mama's care in bed. I didn't get any books out, it was just too hot. Many of the town’s folk felt bad about what had happened. That’s when they appointed me to the corn growers committee. Partly because of me heritage and partly to cheer me up. It was really a prestigious position with only 15 members on it. The meetings were full of squabbles and opinions, but we did manage to accomplish some things. I personally wrote the new detassaling handbook for the farmers press.

One day, Bly Babbinsbort took me into his cellar and opened a big dusty locked chest. IT WAS FULL OF BOOKS! Old books and scrolls about Adventures and Magics. The Babbinsborts were known for adventures and such, but it was all stories, so I thought. I read them all and leaned them as much as I could. That gave me a new dream. No more boring corn meetings for this adventurer. I decided I was gona be a Druid and explore the world. I resigned my corm committee positions and spent my time studying and exploring the Misty Thicket. But those are other adventure stories to be told....

I haven't told me parents, that I've been over at the Babbin's cellar reading and learning about magic’s, and when I'm away long they worry about me. But, I want to use my magic’s to explore and help others. In time I will become a powerful Druid explorer and helper of many.
