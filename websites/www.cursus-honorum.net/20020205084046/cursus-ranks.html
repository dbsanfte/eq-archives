Ranks

The members of Cursus honorum are organized by class and level and position in the guild. The highest ranking member is the Regent. Below the Regent are the members of the High-Council. Next are the members of the House of Lords. Then the high, mid, and low ranks follow with guild vassels and initiates ranking last. Each collum represents one branch of power. Each branch of power has its own representitives in both the House of Lords and the High-Council. As members for each class grow in numbers seperate braches of power will form until eventually each class will have its own brach of power. If a class or group of classes do not have at least 4 members then they are put into the liaison branch. See the previous members page to see a list of those who left the guild with or without honor.
