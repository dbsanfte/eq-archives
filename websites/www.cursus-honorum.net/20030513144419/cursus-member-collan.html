Character History

I was born on the outskirts of Kelethin, in the northeastern parts of Greater Fay. My father died shortly before my birth while on a raid in Crushbone. My mother was a priestess of Tunare and she imbued in me a great love for the gentle protecting goddess. I was not magically gifted enough to become a druid, so I slowly found my way into the ranger’s guild. I quickly learned to live off the land, and had a knack for tracking. When I was in my middle teens, our house was raided by the orc’s of Crushbone. Fortunately, neither my mother nor I was at home that day. It did, however, leave us homeless. We moved into the inner city of Kelethin and moved in with my uncle. After becoming a full fledged ranger and mastering all the skills my guild could offer me by my 35th season, I set off to explore the many hidden corners of Norrath.
