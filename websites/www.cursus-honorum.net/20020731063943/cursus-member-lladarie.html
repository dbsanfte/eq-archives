Character History

Lladarie is still naught but a young girl. Though she has been wisend by her brief relationship with the warrior Lanserial, she knows very little of the world outside the boundries of Faydwer.

Lladarie feels somewhat bound to the forest of her birth, and has no desire at all to veture away from it. The concept of being away seems rather frightening, and she hopes to be able to avoid such a fate.

Currently her skills are limited, but she is a talented youth and under the guidence of future tutors she shall advance in no time.
