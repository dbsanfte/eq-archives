Character History

Wutz Mighlyne fought in East Commons for most of his life, yet he longed to see those dark and eerie lands he had heard about from the adventurous who had traveled through East Commons. After reaching level 20 he found that he had grown weary of the fight and left the lands for safer and more domestic grounds for about a year. He took the platinum pieces he had won as a warrior and bought a small farm about ten miles outside of a small midwestern town. He grew corn and cabbages and tended to his goats, thinking that he could assimilate into the simpler life of a peasant. But, as a warrior, his life could never be that simple. He soon grew restless and knew that deep down he was destined to swing a sword, not a manuer shovel. He did not know if he would be able to return to the land in which he had fought after so long an absence, but to his great surprise he was able to do just that! So he has returned and is ready to fight once again. He currently seeks to become a master archer and fletcher.
