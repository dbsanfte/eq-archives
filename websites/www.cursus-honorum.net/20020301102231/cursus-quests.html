Quests

Quests of all kinds call to us as adventurers. Some are rather short, designed to simply gain the favor of a group of people. Others can be quite extensive. The prizes for such quests are too often too slight for the effort involved, but it is the true adventurer who will complete these lengthy and dangerous quests, and sometimes, the prize is well worth the effort. So then, if you seek high adventure in Norrath, then look below at the accounts of our bravest comrades. Here you will find the details of how specific quests have been completed. Read them in their entirety, or just look for clues, however you see fit to explore Norrath in the most noble manner.
