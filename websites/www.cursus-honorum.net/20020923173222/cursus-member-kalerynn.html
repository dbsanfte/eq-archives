Character History

I was Born a Farmers Son, at the age of ten my father took me to the local lords castle and asked that I become a squire. I became one to my mothers disdain but I was excited to see the world and fight evil. My Lord and Master Morad Solinari took me under his wing and trained me as he would have his son. Sadly he was without family. But that is another story. Well one day my Master and I traveled to the far reachs of Everfrost to slay a mighty dragon. I was afraid be he reasured me by saying "Once the beast has seen me he will run for his life lad" My masters words helped me cope with the fear. As we neared its lair we decided to camp for the night and attack the beast the following day. When I awoke I was alone my master was gone. I wandered aimlessly searching for him until my food ran out. I had thought of death but thought my master would never aprove of me dying. I gathered my small things and picked a direction and began to walk. I wandered for a whole year traveling from town to town helpign when I could, Finaly I reached the Butcher Block Mountains home of the hardy Dwarves. I met Ciranaxil the kind druid and that leads me to all of you.
