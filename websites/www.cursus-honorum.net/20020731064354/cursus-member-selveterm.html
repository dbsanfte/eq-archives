Character History

Selveterm is the Son of Sendariu through a one time encounter with a high elf paladin of Mithaniel Marr. He was abducted by Dark elves asa way of retribution against Sendariu. To complete their vengeance they brought him up as an unholy death machine of Innoruuk. When he had reached his majority he was given a letter by one of the women who brought him up. She was herself a secret renegade and very much so respected Sendarius courage and felt that this travesty that was my life must be ammended. She made him see his sins against the innocent (with a spell) and forced him to rerad a letter that explained his background. When all was said and done his faith in Innoruuk was shattered and he soon came to revile him. Selveterm now searches for his mother and for his place in Norrath and has heard rumor that his mother has joined a league of true hearted heros and now he is trying to follow in her footsteps....
