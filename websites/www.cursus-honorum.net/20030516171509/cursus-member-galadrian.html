Character History

I grew up fairly standard for a young high elf noble, my mother was sister to the Archmage Elyar Klennix and my father was a noble of a slightly lesser house. I was to be a Klennix though for the simply pragmatic reason of the weight that name would carry. At age twelve, like all high elven youths, i was taken to study the course of magic that was to be my gift. I studied hard and well, discovering along the way that i was far more adventuresome and free spirited than my more stuffy kin. I also discovered that Tunare was not the goddess that was closest to my heart. Erollisi was though. I kept it very quiet though for although my faith is not heretical it would land me with some explaining to do. For these reasons and the fact that I am tired of hearing stories of my Uncle Elyar second hand i have decided to join your fight against the darkness of Norrath.
