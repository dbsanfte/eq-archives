Longing for Jezeb

A letter has been sent to the nobles of Cursus Honorum from Regent Rohwin Amoure Ardour...


Fondest wishes are sent to you, the nobles of Cursus Honorum, may Erollisi provide all that you will ever need, from Regent Rohwin Amoure Ardour, the keeper of Artillian's rightful place.

Our military victories are certainly something to rejoice over, yet I have not felt such uplifting spirits as of late. It has been too long since I have seen my beloved wife, Lady Jezeb Amoure Ardour. She has been serving as our liaison to the Knight of Freedom. Their expeditions have kept her far away, too far for any correspondence. I have asked their officers of her whereabouts, but they told me that the missions she was accompanying were delicate, and I could not be told where she was for her own safety and the safety of the Knights of Freedom.

Please seek her out, I must know that she is well. She could be anywhere on Norrath. If you can find her, then please tell her that I miss her dearly, and if you could deliver a flower to her, then you would earn my gratitude. I patiently await your news from abroad.

May Erollisi guide you,

Rohwin Amoure Ardour Regent of Cursus Honorum

***Results***

Many adventures sought out Jezeb at my request. In the end, three had found her safe and well, and they did present her with a flower and my best wishes. Congratulations to those who completed this quest, Splendor, Ciranaxil, and Selveterm.

Rohwin Amoure Ardour
