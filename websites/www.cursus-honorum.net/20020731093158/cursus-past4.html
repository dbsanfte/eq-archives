Kolvaran's Wedding

Erollisi be praised. Viscount Kolvaran has proposed marriage to Dacia. She has accepted his invitation and they have set the date of their marriage for June 24th, 5pm PDT. The ceremony will be held in East Karana at the waterfalls. Follow the river south of the wooden bridge and you will find the falls. Everyone is encouraged to attend. I'm sure everyone will have a good time. Hope to see you all there.

Congratulations to Kolvaran and Dacia. They are now bound by their hearts and souls in holy wedlock. Dacia Angelborn is an honorary member and shares her husband's title as Countess of Cursus Honorum.
