Character History

Born to a guard and a druidess in Kelethin, Ciranaxil was culturally impressed with a hatred of the Orcs. Early in her youth, she took up the defense of the forest against the orcish hatchets, and this progressed until she and her friends would ambush small parties of the orcs, raid the camps, and even venture into the Crushbone lands themselves for preemptory strikes.

One such strike led to disaster, while Ciranaxil was at home in the trees, her love participated in a midnight raid on the slave cabins, and tragically, did not come back. Rescue attempts freed few of the captured party, but her love was not among them. It was this that drove her to leave the Faydark and see the world.

Adventures and misadventures came and went, and Ciranaxil has returned to her home bearing a measure of wisdom and power. She will revisit the Clan Crushbone, when the time is right...
