Character History

I am unable to recall the particulars of my earliest days in Norrath, for at a very young age I was taken away from my home and all that I knew and held dear. I have lived my life thus far with mirth and merrymaking as I have struggled to survive the only way I have know - through swift thinking and perhaps less than lawfull means. I am always seeking for adventure and enjoy the journeying. For many seasons I have been questioned as to why I have no surname - my only response was that I had not found my prince yet. However, a short time ago in the depths of Nagafen's sinister lair - I met (much to my surprise) a very intriguing adventurer. His strength and valor protected me from the brutal attacks of the vile creatures that inhabit that dungeon - and his gentle voice and playfull manner drew me to him from the first moment. My history ends with the joyfull news of our engagement for now my future begins, but I cannot tell you of the future - only know this, that where you shall see him - this gallant Lord of my heart... there you shall also find me, and I am the happiest little rogue that there ever has been or ever will be in all of Norrath's fair lands.
