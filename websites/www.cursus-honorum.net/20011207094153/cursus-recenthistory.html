Recent History

In Recent times, events upon Norrath have been shaping Cursus Honorum. Amongst our tomes, logs, scrolls, and diaries are the details that describe how Cursus Honorum is being effected, and what effect we have had on the rest of Norrath. Study them carefully, for within these writings are the clues and secrets to Norrath. (It should be noted that the information here is different from that of the news page. News is for any kind of guild annoucments, while our recent history is reserved for events that are related to our roleplaying storyline.)

The Offical Log of Cursus Honorum has many guild-wide annoucments and developments that shape our storyline as we play. Below are important issues from a variety of sources listed in chronological order. Updates to the offical log will also be listed below. I will use modern dates to avoid confusion with accelerated game time.
