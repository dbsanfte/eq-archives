0.10: Is there a downloadable version of the FAQ?

If you would like a copy of the HTML version of the FAQ to keep on your own computer, there is a zip file you can download. There is also a text version of the FAQ which you can download. You can download either version for personal use only. This means that you can keep the files on your personal computer and look at them whenever you want. You may not distribute it. Among other things, you may not put it up on a web site. If you are interested in maintaining a mirror site for the FAQ, contact P. Korda and Matthew Hunter.

If we find bootleg copies of the FAQ on the web, we will reconsider our decision to distribute the FAQ in this way. Don't ruin things for everybody else.
