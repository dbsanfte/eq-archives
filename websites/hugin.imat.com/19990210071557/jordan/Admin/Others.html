Other Resources

Non-net stuff:

  • Issue 4 of the Waldenbooks 'zine Hailing Frequencies had a little interview with RJ. (I believe this was pre-FOH)
  • Nov 1994 Issue of the B. Dalton 'zine had an interview with RJ.
  • Fan Clubs: If netting isn't fannish enough for you, there is a RJ-sanctioned Wheel of Time Fan Club.

    The Robert Jordan/Wheel of Time Fan Club is a snailmail club. They have a bimonthly newsletter, Threads. For more information, write to:

    RJ/WOT Fan Club
    P.O. Box 463
    Mansfield, MO 65704

Net Stuff:

The RJ ftp site, reachable by anonymous ftp to hugin.imat.com, directory /pub/jordan/ has many amusing and informative things, such as old versions of this FAQ, The Dark One's Dictionary, some fan art, a glossary of the Old Tongue, etc.

The WWW Jordan homepage, at "http://hugin.imat.com/waygate/jordan.html" (maintained by The Cabal {TINC}).

The Wheel of Time Index at http://student-www.uchicago.edu/users/kor2/WOT/WOTindex/ (every WOT-related thing on the 'Net, maintained by P. Korda).
