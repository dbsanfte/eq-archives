Trolloc Tribes

(TEotW: Glossary, 798, entry "Trollocs", 668):
Ahf'frait=Afrits, Bhan'sheen=Banshees, Dha'vol=Devils, Dhai'mon=Demons, Dhjin'nen=Djinns, Ghar'ghael=Gargoyles, Ghob'hlin=Goblins, Gho'hlem=Golems, Ghraem'lan=Gremlins, Ko'bal=Kobolds, Kno-gmon=Gnomes, Al'ghol=Ghouls (Arabic guhla, or some such).
