Where can I contact Robert Jordan?

RJ is not on the Net.

You can send mail to RJ in care of his publisher, Tor Books. You can send e-mail to torbooks@panix.com, or snail-mail to the address given on the copyright page of the books:

Robert Jordan
c/o Tor Books
175 Fifth Ave.
New York, NY  10010
USA
