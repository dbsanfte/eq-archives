2.7.2: How tall is Everyone?

Heights given in English feet:

Info from a post-LOC book signing (reported by Erica Sadun)

  • Rand: 6'5" to 6'6"
  • Perrin: 6'2"
  • Mat: 5'11"
  • Aviendha: 5'8" to 5'9"
  • Elayne 5'6"
  • Nynaeve: 5'4"
  • Egwene: 5'2"
  • Moiraine: 5'0" to 5'2"

Info from another post-LOC book signing (reported by Greg Gruber)

  • Rand: 6'6"
  • Moiraine: 5'3"
  • Egwene: 5'5"
  • Nynaeve: 5'5"
  • Aviendha: 5'10"
