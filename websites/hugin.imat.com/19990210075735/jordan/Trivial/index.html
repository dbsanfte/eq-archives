3.0 Trivial Pursuits

References, Sources, Allusions, Parallels, etc

Robert Jordan says:
"There are elements from Norse, Chinese, Japanese, and American Indian mythologies, to name just a few. I think it adds resonance to the story, although I've taken great care not to follow the older material in any slavish way. Occasionally, I will add in details here or there, and then discover that I have done something that is absolutely authentic to the myth I was working from." (From Waldenbooks 'zine Hailing Frequencies, issue #4, reported by Edmund Wong)
