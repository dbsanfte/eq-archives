99.1: When is the next book going to be out?

When will TPOD be out in paperback?

The mass-market paperback edition of TPOD is due out in August, 1999.

When will Book 9 be published?

There is no official projected release date for Book 9. At a post-TPOD book signing [San Jose, CA, 25 October 1998, reported by Rick Moen], RJ said that he'd start working on Book 9 after Thanksgiving of 1998. From the amount of time taken between the last few installments of the series, we can guess that Book 9 will come out in mid to late 2000.

What will Book 9 be called?

We don't know. It's doubtful RJ even has a title for it yet. From a Barnes and Noble online chat with RJ [19 October, 1998]: "I have to do a little writing before the title becomes clear to me. I don't start off with a title. That always comes to me at some point during the writing. Something that seems to fit the specific book."
