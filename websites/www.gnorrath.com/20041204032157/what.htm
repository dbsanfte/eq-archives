What is a Heroic Opportunity?

Heroic opportunities occur when a character uses certain abilities. This will start a chain that can lead to several types of events. (i.e. Direct Damage, Group Heals, Buffs etc.) Found on this site are breakdowns of heroic oportunities and their end results.

Heroic Opportunity Icons

Opportunity icons are the building blocks of heroic opportunities. Each spell or combat art used by players has an opportunity icon associated with it, which can advance a heroic opportunity. The icons are divided into four groups, one for each archetype. With the recent patch changes, heroic opportunity "skills" have been added. These are now shown on the table.
