The Guild Rules:

1. Have fun
2. No begging of items, powerlevels, or money
3. No fighting in guild chat, if you have a problem get an officer or send personal tells.
4. Respect your fellow member, they may save your life.
5. Please remember you have a tag on your name with the name Storm Legions on it, when they see you and your actions they see more than just you, they see all of us! So, always strive to make us proud and be good to our guild tag, it speaks volumes of you..... even tho you may not realize anyone notices.
6. Would like to have new members of no less than level 25 unless special circumstances. This is for the good of the whole guild, ie: more able to set up events for everyone.
7. Any new member who joins Storm Legions will be put on a 30 probationary period for us to see how they are and for them to see how we are. During that time either party can decide to end their membership with no hard feelings.
8. Would like to have all new members sponsored by at least 2 members, having had some time to group with the individual and seeing how they play and general attitude. Then the sponsors could recommend membership at that time.

Treat your fellow guildie the way that you, yourself would want to be treated! We are all here to play and have fun, so no childish or rude behavior will be tolerated! We all want to find a place to call home, and work on skills and fighting abilities, this can be such a place! With fair play and a bit of kindness we can all find that here amongst the ranks of the Storm Legions!

It is understood that disputes will arise between people. Try to resolve the disagreement so that both parties are satisfied. If you are unsure of what you should do in a particular situation, contact an officer for guidance.
1) If you have a dispute with another member, please try to resolve it in tells.
2) Do not air grievances against another guild member in guild chat, regardless of whether or not that person is online at the time.
3) If you are unable to resolve the dispute, contact a guild officer for help. If a situation arises which involves another guild, contact a guild officer immediately

Good communication is essential to the process of evolving as a group. This includes the feedback of ALL members - ingame, at meetings, and on the Guild Forum Site - and ALL Guild members are responsible for participating in the decision-making of the guild. We may disagree at times, but we must and will continue to discuss and resolve the issues.
