Our History

Formed at the very conception of the Stormhammer server, Stormlegions was created as a way to gather friends, both old and new, together for the new adventures that awaited us on Legends.

Hawkonen Spiritweaver, a Barbarian Shaman of questionable skill and leadership abilities, although making a fine drunk, stood before the new Gods, raised his voice to be heard above the din of folks from across many lands, gathered a few together and spoke the words the Gods must hear. They granted him the power to gather like folks amoungst himself and called them the Storm Legions.

Hawkonen's rule was short lived, as he was not happy in the role of a Leader. Perhaps because the "spirits" (maybe even those that can be found at any local pub), had spoken to him in regards to his true role as heyokah. It was time, in any event, for him to place the mantle on someone else's shoulders. In his wisdom he sought out a leader who had the intelligence and forethought to command and, after a long and arduous search he found an old friend that agreed to take the role.

Soloras Wardancer, Wizard of many seasons and a great thinker with varied leadership skills, was well able to lead and accepted.
Hawkonen then became General of Legions, to guide the armies of Storm Legions on their journies.
Soloras took stock of the situation surrounding his leadership, and being great of mind and power took the stead with vigor. Soloras too came to another crossing in his life and chose to follow the ways of the Wizard.

Leadership was then passed to a trusted member whose job was to simply hold the mantle until a great druidess could be brought from her adventures to take up her role. Soloras, in his great ways of foresight, agreed to become Guild Advisor. A role more suited to him and his abilities.

Our beloved Teke Tufang, great Druidess and Queen of Legions, then became our leader. What glorious days followed! Our expansion was vast. Friends gathered, warriors and healers, those of spirit and mind, even evil came to be lead by her! Our story really began with the induction of our Queen as our Leader. Many great events and adventures are here for the telling now, but this being a recount of history I find I must limit myself to the facts regarding our Guild growth.

During her reign we saw many of great honor and ability join our ranks, and others in our midst were recognized with titles and duties.
Linlu Spiritweaver was promoted to Master at Arms.
Kuriel Sureshot was promoted to Guild Herald.
Rakyrn Shadowbane, later to become our Queen's betrothed and loving husband, was made Concubine to our Queen.
Others were named officers and given titles, a full recount of which can be found here and there.
Seeing as how there is no Guild Scribe that I can bring to mind, it may be time to try and find one...

Even the greatest of Leaders has it in them to carry on the duties and needs of their own choosing and it came to pass that Teke had need to move on. A sad day this was! A great search was put underway to find one who may allow the Storm Legions to evolve and develope.

One was found, although the ramifications of which are most likely to be far reaching. Change has a way of interjecting itself where it may, and Lyrak De' Corrupter slithered, er, stepped into the role. Ah, but what a way change has of stirring the pot, eh? Evil incarnate, Lyrak now leads Storm Legions. His reign has yet to be written, and the ramifications of his leadership are still being developed, but his evil ways are a twist of fate that's neither good nor evil.

Change is like that in most cases, and the adventure begins anew. What stories will there to be told now?

We shall indeed see...
