About Storm Legions

Storm Legions is, simply put, a place for friends to gather and play Everquest.

Our Guild is located on the Legends server, Stormhammer, and was created within a day of Legends going live. We strive to create an atmosphere of friendly role-playing, with diverse stories being created and changed on a daily basis. Ongoing plots and twists are an every day occurence in our Guild chat. A friendly place just to talk about EQ and give advice to others, all within a Roleplaying atmosphere first created by Teke Tufaang (Please see the History page for further information). General discussions almost always include skills and experience advice, as well as just general knowledge being passed along from one player to another.
We've had a diverse existence on Legends, even with it being such a new stomping ground for most, and our goal is to simply generate a Guild for both those who are simply seeking a fun group of people to associate with and those who are ready to hit the next phase of gaming. Going more in depth into the world of Norrath and becoming more serious about our adventures while still maintaining what some might term as a 'newbie atmosphere'. All too often, larger or older Guilds forget that Everquest is a game somewhere along the way, and it becomes more of a chore. A hefty goal, to be sure, but we are hopeful that we can accomplish this.
Our Guild roster is one of the larger on Stormhammer, albeit alot of the characters in our Guild still maintain a presence in other Guilds and/or on other servers... we still have a great number of players associated with us. Degrees of experience amoung our members is quite vast, and the diversity sometimes plays itself out in very interesting ways to be sure.
Simply, we just wanna have fun while experiencing Everquest for all it's worth. It IS a game, after all.
