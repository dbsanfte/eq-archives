    Sadari was born in a little house in the deep forest of Kiticor. One time a man tried to resurrect his brother who died cause to a fire in the house. He failed, He opened a gate to hell where the great amount of undead invaded the forest, this place where no longer safe.

      The undead army invaded the house and slayed her parents but they didn't see little Sadari, totally terrifying by the undead in the house and kept quite.

    The next day an Paladin, who ran an errand from the great hold of High Pass to Freeport telling about the hordes of undead stumbled across the house and heard the children crying and yelling, he entered the house and saw mother and father dead, in great agony he turned away from the corpses and saw Sadari lying there, He picked her up and proceed his journey.

    Sadari was now an orphan and was taken care by the Paladin guild in Freeport, raisin to the belief in Mithaniel Marr. She swore the holy oath that she would help in the quest to clean the world from all evil.
