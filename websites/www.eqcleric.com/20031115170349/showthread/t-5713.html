Jarvis
Greetings fellow Clerics...

I'll be putting together a wish list of updates to be done to our Spell System and this is YOUR chance to get your 2 cents in...

Here's what I've planned so far:

  • Possibility to add Spell Location / Merchant Info
  • Easy Updating of the Spell Database from Lucy to reflect the most recent changes.
  • Updating of Spell Info to include information regarding Researchable Spells
  • New Searching feature to isolate and filter only Researchable Spells
  • Preparation of Spell System for the future release of EverQuest 2 so both EQ platforms are covered.
  • Some sort of admin interface for Clavigers to easily update spell info manually if needed. (For those rare occasions where Lucy's info doesn't seem to match that of the game.


Now that you've seen my list of things up for change I'd like to hear what you might suggest.

Please post a reply to this thread with possible changes/additions/updates you would like to see to the spell system. Please be as detailed as possible regarding your suggestion and don't be shy to suggest anything.

If you're suggestion actually makes it live into the system I'll be making sure to give you a little extra attention on the site in ways of a graphic emblem and some extra pp :) You might even make it on to the Site Credits page being written these days! ;)

Now I'm looking forward to reading some solid feedback here. Several users have made subtle suggestions on how we can make the spell system better...Now is the chance to make those ideas heard and considered.
