I think you'll find it difficult at best to find a PoP-raiding guild at 53. You mention Solusek Ro in the subject, and at least from the guilds I've seen doing that level of PoP you will need to be 65 and with quite a few AA (never mind spell requirements!) before even being considered.

This is not to put you off to it or shut you down, just suggesting that you may still have a long road ahead until you can even apply to many guilds. Again, this is not meant at all as an insult, but given the types of AoEs the boss critters at that level can produce, you would be absolutely zero use to a near-EP guild, and if you applied now they would probably think you were just trying to grab upgrades. Just pound away exp in PoP, and you can get 53-65 in no time!
