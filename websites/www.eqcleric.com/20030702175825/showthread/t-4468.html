Keep in mind, testing this is hard, because the server judges ticks as 6 seconds, and server lag + your lag can and will sully any testing)

On horseback, it's the same as sitting, as long as you are sitting still, and not melee'ing.

FT adds 1 tick per point of FT, so FT 15 = 15 more to the base total
Mental Clarity adds 1 tick per level, so MC3 = 3 more, that stack with FT.

Now, Enchanter mind candy breakdown~

Breeze = adds 2 per tick.
Clarity = 4 per tick@ Level 29, and goes up to 7 per tick@ level 48.
Clarity 2 = 11 per tick
Boon of the Clear Mind = 7 per tick@ Level 52, to 9 per tick@level 65
Gift of Pure Thought = 11 per tick
Tranquility = 16 per tick (Also adds to mana pool by 260 and raises int/wis by 26)
Koadic's Endless Intellect = 14 per tick (Adds 250 to mana pool, and raises int/wis by 25
Voice of Quellious = 18 per tick (Adds 275 to mana pool, and raises int/wis by 28)

Now, Yaulp 5 and 6 add mana regen, too, and those add 10 per tick, and 12 per tick, respectively.

Also, there are Mana regen items, that have a castable buff, so they stack with FT, MC3, and Enchanter mind buffs. (Basic rule of thumb; If it has a buff icon, it stacks.) These include Emp Shawl, Burrower Talisman, Shadowed Sphere, Twilight Shroud. There are also items that add to mana pool, but they have no actual mana regen.

If someone can find a definitive, uncontested amount for the base mana regen we get from plain ole sitting, I will love you forever. I searched for 2 hours, and still found nothing :(

Pen-

Riding a mount is equal to sitting - you're sitting on your horse...so long as you stand still.
Armor of Protection, Blessed Armor of Faith, and Armor of the Zealot provide mana regen rates of 2, 6, and 8 per tick. Druids have Protection of the Grove and Protection of the Nine which do not stack. They give I believe 6 and 8 per tick.
Enchanters cast clarity line. There's 6 spells in this line, starting with breeze, that vary from 2-18 per tick.
Beastlords have their "spiritual" line as well, that grants a regen of 3, 5, 7, and 9 per tick as well as equal HP regen.
Flowing Thought, other worn items, and the AA ability Mental Clarity all contribute...FT states its regen rate in the name and MC is 1 per skill level. The net sum of these, called your UMR (unbuffed mana regen), cannot exceed 15.
There are a select few other items that have right-click effects that will also contribute, like Talisman of the Burrower which has an effect of Lunar Whispers (2/tick)

I think a couple of my numbers here are off, but I'm doing this off the top of my head, and at work. I'll edit, as per correction replies.
