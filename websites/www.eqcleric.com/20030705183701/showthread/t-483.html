G'day!

I thought I'd drop a note to introduce myself given that I'm going to be a resident clerical newbie for a while.

By day, I'm a mild mannered IT manager. By night, I'm a level 8 froglok cleric named Kiji in the service of Mithaniel Marr on Torv.

My EQ life started in March 1999 with a high elf cleric on Bristlebane -- a poor, abandoned girl who never quite made it past level 20. She was left for an enchanter who I've been pretty faithful to over the last 60 levels. I felt like I needed a change and a challenge -- so I headed off to Torv and created a cleric that I couldn't powerlevel. I still have my newbie club, and am working my way through the newbie frog quest armour. WOOT!

Let me just say this: I'm scared and loving it! :) :) I might be comfortable nailing down mobs and speeding up tanks but when it comes to handing out the bandaids, I have no confidence whatsoever. I'm spending all my free time scouring the net looking for good sources of happy cleric-ing. :)

Anyway, that's me in a nutshell. I'm sure you'll see inane, lowbie cleric questions from this frog -- just wanted to say hi before I inundate y'all. :)

And, hey, if you're on Torv and have any suggestions for lowbie guilds, or just want to say hi, FEEL FREE :)

-Kiji, who hopes she'll be fine in the long run as long as she doesn't go chasing a passing fly in the middle of casting a CH
