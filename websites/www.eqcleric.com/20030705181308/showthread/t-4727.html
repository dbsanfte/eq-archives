Mattress
The Ancient Cyclops (AC) spawns in two zones. One being South Ro. In that zone he is a completely random spawn and can spawn from any mob in the zone. Therefore, the best strategy for that zone is to slaughter everything in the zone until he pops.

The second zone he spawns in is Ocean of Tears (OoT). There he has a static spawn spot (but random spawn time) on the island across from Sister Isle.

The AC in South Ro is noticeably easier (lower level) than the one that spawns in OoT. Anyone can get the AC ring and Multi Quest the boots for you though.
