Gericht
I may be completely wrong here, but couldn't you transfer the Cleric to another account?

As for the other two options,
Beastlords are quite self-sufficient. They are, obviously, one of the best soloing classes in the game. You might enjoy the change of pace from a cleric who are limited in soloing with ease. Despite popular opinion, they also have a fair amount to offer a group (dps wise, utility spells, and slows) and in higher levels offer very respectable slows and healing abilities.

Enchanters, however, are a different breed. They can solo in low levels, but it takes more strategy on your part. This is basically a support class, like a cleric. They get a lot of fun abilities, illusions, jewelcrafting, (if you're a gnome-tinkering), charm, haste, and mana regen. You may have some trouble getting a group in lower levels (because of your low damage in early levels), but you'll find you have a vast array of options and duties once you are in one. Go to dungeons crawls like Befallen, Unrest, etc. and you'll find yourself really shining.

Both are fun. It really depends on what you're looking for.
