Shadowfrost
The dark elf "snare" item that we hear so much about is the Regent Symbol of Innoruuk which casts Clinging Darkness. Bear in mind that we're talking about a so-called "Snare" that lasts for 36 seconds, takes 6 seconds to cast, and only slows down monster movement speed by 30%... it really doesn't help you all that much, and I certainly prefer to have a snarer in the group when fighting critters that run.

In any case, the only reasonably effective or efficient way of soloing that I've found past level 60 is to root-rot, in which case critters running is less of a problem. :)

Restandre, I can think of about six places where a level 65 cleric can solo dark blue critters using the root rot technique:- Valorous Spirits in HoH, various critters in the Castle in PoD, Tortured Iksar Miners in Chardok, Sun Revenants in the Grey, some of the Disturbed Elysian Remains in UP are dark blue to 65, and the robes in ME.
