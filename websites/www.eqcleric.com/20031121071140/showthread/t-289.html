AanyaLorien
There are only 4 spells from the Kunark era included in the turn in distribution. At various places in Kunark you will find drops of certain spells(Sebilis, CoM, Charasis, TT, EJ - any appropriately leveled zone).

The turn in spells are: Death Pact (51), Upheaval (52), Yaulp IV (53), and Reckoning (54). These spells can be turned in to NPCs: Frist Furtun (a dwarf in FV) and Brinaa Darkpact (in Overthere). You will receive (random): Sunskin (51), Word of Vigor (52), Heroic Bond (52) or Unswerving Hammer of Faith (54) for the turn in.

Honestly, though - most if not all of these (at least on Zeb) are for sale in Bazaar for minimal cash.

Improved invisibility to undead is a Velious era spell, it drops only in Velious - but again, is probably for sale in the Bazaar.
