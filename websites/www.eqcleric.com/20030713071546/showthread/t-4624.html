Shadowfrost
Most of the real upgrades are kind of expensive.

Velium Jasper Ring -> Shadel Bandit Ring/Valorium Ring of Gallantry
Both Bracers -> Cultural Bracers
Acrylia Studded Cloak -> Hierophant Cloak
Coldain Skin Gloves -> Hurricane Plate Gauntlets

I can't think of a leg slot upgrade which would be realistic in price. :/

Earring of the Solstice is a decent earring but personally I feel that you have two reasonable earrings already and you'd be better off saving the pp for a slot that needs upgrading a little more.

Lodi shield is a decent back slot but it's overpriced for an item no HP or MANA, and sacrificing the 75hp/75mana on the equally-priced Hierophant Cloak strikes me as an error. If you watch how high end clerics select their equipment, you'll see that their slots tend to fill up with the highest HP/MANA items they can find, and there are good reasons for that.

Once you join a raiding guild and you aren't relying on the bazaar for upgrades any more, a whole new world of equipment opens up for you!
