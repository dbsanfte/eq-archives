Sydian
I'd agree with 1,2,4, but you could probably forgo symbol as well. If you're not that well off then you aren't going to be able to afford to symbol much. IMO symbol isn't worth the mana/pp cost until you get to Pinzarn (lvl 34). Through most of your early levels I would only buy the very essential spells, which are:

1. Healing line (lvl 14,24,34)
2. HoT line (lvl 19,29,44) - Expensive but worth it. I notice many lower clerics don't use these, they're more efficient until you get the next heal circle.
3. Ressurection line - cheap so worth it every time
4. Stuns - good in ldon now with healer mobs
5. Courage line - always worth the next upgrade
6. The misc. spells like self armor at 34, gate, root, fear, cure poison/disease

In most groups you won't have mana to nuke until C3 (lvl 45) so you can hold off on most nukes until then if you want. Unless you like to solo a lot, of course. Evoke can be caught up later on by casting the lvl 1 dd on yourself.
