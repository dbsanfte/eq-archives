mantrius
Kaesora is actually a GREAT zone, I usually level all of my characters there, be they melee or caster. It can be a bit of a tough zone due to the fact that there are tons of mobs and quite a few of them are casters. However the vendor loot and some drops are still very sellable. 35 to 45 or 46 is no problem in this zone, I usually hunt the library area to about 44, then hunt the Xalgoz side of the zone until about 46 or so.

Most of the library area is undead, only a few non undead mobs. Your best bet is not to solo here, I would reccomend at least duo with a decent tank of any type.

direhand
Thanks everyone for all of the suggestions, but I've found by far the best place to go from lvl34 to my now lvl40, and beyond. GULF OF GUNTHAK. Everything on the beach is practically Undead. And at 36 a cleric can carefully solo there, but a duo is recommended. With the new patch coming out wednesday the exp should be even better for pairs. I highly recommend this zone if you want to go up about 1 level every night. :D So, anyway thank you for the suggestions! If you don't know how to get to the gulf of gunthak it's fairly easy. From POK you go to the paineel stone. From erudin, you will have to run into the nearest cavern, I forget the name of it. But once in there just hang some lefts. or actually use your map...hehe. From there you have to run through the warrens, if you do it at least once you'll know the way, besides, they don't attack you after lvl32 i don't think. After going through the warrens is a short walk to get to the boat that takes you to the gulf of gunthak, just click on the doors. Once there, if below lvl34 stay on the beach with a friend or two, once you hit about 36 you can solo there. The loot is good. about 4pp per drop on the skellies and the undead trolls drop mucho good loot including various expensive jems such as opals, periodots, and i've even seen one lucky bastard get a diamond!!!/grr So anyone between lvls i would say 34 and 45 you are in for a treat there. Have fun. L8rs.
