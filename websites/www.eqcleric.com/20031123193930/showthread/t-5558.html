Brokan
Nope, haven't noticed anything like that. Can't see how that would be the case anyway, all Pure Blood does is remove both Disease and Poison counters. Even if some effect would give you BOTH Disease and poison counters, you should be able to remove them with cure disease and cure poison.
Still believe Pure Blood is a handy spell to have, saves you a spell slot, since you will find both disease and poison traps in the dungeons.
If cure poison and disease don't remove an effect its probably a curse (or a fire dot or something) and then pure blood won't help either.
I could be wrong tho :D

I found I waste so much mana by casting cure poison and disease, that I gave up and would use the group heal instaid. Now, at 57, the upgrade also removes a few poison and disease counters.

The other thing to do, is to get the puller to pull through those rooms with traps to an untrapped room, so you can med without jumping ever time someone springs a trap. When cleared just run past the room with the traps and pull to the next untrapped room. A lot easier to heal only one dotted puller, than a whole group.
