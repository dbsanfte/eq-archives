Parklyn
I was going to offer that you move the group and yourself target windows in the middle of the screen (bottom middle) so that it is near "where the action is"!

I tend to float heals also since I usually am near FM (with KEI or VoQ, Spiritual Radiance or Purity and Yaulp 5 on mana is always freely available it seems.) Floating a heal is casting the heal on the group member most likely to take damage (tank, puller, enchanter, slower or yourself) anyway and interrupting the cast if you need to quickly change this decision (cast on a different group member change spell to cast, etc.) Using our Heal over time spells saves your aggro too!

I do not have my UI configured for the middle (I have everything in the bottom left together) simply because I sit facing that direction towards my monitor (kind of at an angle.) I do keep all the windows together though like others have suggested. Using the mouse to point and click around the UI is handy, but you really have to get used to using keyboard hotkeys or just plain using the keyboard commands. Remember that using the mouse does take more time!

Restandre
Placement of your windows is completely irrelevant. If I'm typing, I'm going to not notice no matter where the health bars are. You get trained enough to just see mostly-empty health bars from anywhere on the screen eventually.

Just know that your elixir is on 1, your remedy on 2, your CH on 3, your open assist key on 4, etc., and you can just move your left hand into place easily. The nearby function keys target your group members. Very often you will have to delete what you are typing (shift-down, enter) or resort to mouse clicking, but I find that in general using the keyboard exclusively works FAR FAR BETTER than the mouse.
