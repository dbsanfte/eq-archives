I'm a lvl 32 cleric on Rallos Zek. Basically, good gear is hard to come by on this server and once you get it, you're constantly watching for pks. Anyway, a friend of mine wrote a list of gear that is good for my level range, so I'll put it up here for you to see.
Fingers - 2 Velium Jasper Rings (fairly expensive, fortunately a guildmate helped me out with these, +7wis each)
Wrist - Bracers of the Reverent
Ears - Forest Loop (At your level, go to the giant camp in Warsliks Woods and camp the giants, you'll get exp as well as the loops)
Neck - Black Alloy Medallion
Head - Shissar Scale Headband (No wis, but a good item to have)
Face - Velium ruby veil (guildmate also helped me out with this item)
Shoulders - Adamantite Epaulets
Back - Acrylia Studded Cloak
Arms - Nathsar Vambraces
Legs - Guardian (No drop, would be hard to get at 25)
Chest - Crustacean BP
Waist - Smiter's girdle
Gloves - Gauntlet's of the black
Primary - Dark Ember
Secondary - Book of Obulus
Range - Gantru charm
The most expensive item on this list would be the book of obulus, about 3k. If you have about 6k lying around (which a lvl 25 cleric will never have unless you've got highend chars helping you out) this is what I would suggest. Yes, there is better gear for a cleric, but for lvl 25, this is perfectly fine and actually very nice.
