Rawn
Whew, I feel like one incredibly lucky little dorf.

I've made several attempts to start my epic, but got frustrated with waiting for hours and hours under Lake Rathe for Senoir Begurgle. Well.. last Sunday I got ticked off and decided I was going to get him once and for all, and spent a good majority of the day camping the lil bugger. Lo' and behold, he finally makes an appearance! Crown in hand, I go check on Shmendrik, who happens to be up, do my turn in, a little keeling, and voila, Step 1 complete!

So after a little running around with one of my guild buddies, we visit to the Temple of Sol Ro, smack down the Plasmatic Priest and loot a bloody little robe. Yay! After some more running around, I swing by Sol A that evening, which I expected to take quite a bit of time. Well, lucky lil dorf me, I nailed him in the first 45 minutes. Woot! Bedtime!

Monday night - Now, the guides here say Naxot spawns in Burning Woods at 6pm gametime and then despawns 12 hours later - apparently this has changed. I arrived at his spawn point at around 5am gametime (and he was up) and waited to see if he despawned.. he was up until 1pm and I finally had to camp out for a bit - by the time I logged in later he was gone again. Soo.. later that night, some guild buddies and a couple good friends gather in Burning Woods (a grand total of 9 of us), and prepare to take down the big ugly Fire Elemental Ixiblat. We get buffed up at the pull spot not far from where Ixi spawns and I head over to where I saw Naxot earlier in the day. After all our buffs faded and I nearly dozed off, I see him pop! woot! wait.. oh hell, something's attacking him! ack! After panicking, I proceed to lay the smack down to the skeleton bullying Naxot, but not before he's down to 20% health (eep). I do my turn in, head back to group and we lay waste to said ugly-arse elemental. Yay! Sceptre of Ixiblat.

Fast-forward to Saturday. This part made me a little nervous. Most of my (very small) guild is on Chardok faction. However, two of our non-kos folks are a high-level enchanter and mage. After some research, we decide that getting our mage down to the hall outside the king room and CoHing the rest would be the easiest approach. So we sweet talk some more good friends (including a high level rogue with the ultra-sneaky-AA), and lead our mage down to the hall. Summon enchanter, who pacifies all the wanderers in the hallway, and then CoH the rest of raid force down and, to make a short story shorter, slaughter the King. Woot! Singed scroll is mine! I do my turn-ins to Omat and Natasha and call it a night.

Now, Sunday (today), time to camp Zordak in Sol B, ugh. I've heard horror stories about this one. Strategies for evading the Fire Giants when they aggro while you're waiting for days, and so on. So, anxious lil dorf that I am, I cruise down to the Sol C zone bridge, dash by a couple bats (who decide to chase me anyway), and .. dam.. fire giants are up near the very first bridge. So I root the stupid bat thumpin on the back of my head and camp out nearby, figure I'll check back a little later. So.. about an hour later I log back in and hey! a group is just now heading in to clear giants! I follow them in and --- holy crap --- Zordak is up!! I quick hand him my pearl, get the Box of Bindings and Swirling Pearl, and gate outta there. Wooooot!!

Sunday afternoon, time to start camping shards in Skyfire. I figure my luck can't hold out, and plan on this part taking weeks to finish. Five friends and five hours later, I have four shards in hand! holy crap!!!! I'm one battle from my epic!! Battle scheduled for 9:00 pm tommorow night! Wish me luck!

I'm one lucky, psyched little cleric!
