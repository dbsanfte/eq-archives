MlainDreamthief
Mlain Dreamthief here...

Keep in mind.. They only upped your melee damage capability.. they have done nothing to make you absorb it any better... you still take considerable damage as a cleric tanking. I would not recommend being main tank, although i feel you will see more dual cleric grps now that clerics can output a fair share of damage.... you will also begin to see the battle clerics emerge... as well as hasted clerics.

I toyed with the new skills a bit. I can solo the entrance mobs in kael with minimal difficulty. I recommend pulling with mark of retribution. soon as its cast find a corner and back into it... this makes casting further spells easier... whip out your pet asap and get it on the mob. With you tanking the mob your pulling the pet will take no aggro from you... That hammer procs like a bitch. I need one or two celest heals on myself tanking in kael. However i have 1152 ac pre buffs.. so plan your tanking strategy accordingly. I also have an additional 8pt damage shield i can instaclick on myself for tanking...

all in all i havent toyed much with yaulp 5 ... should make u prock more and swing faster with more accuracy.

All in all.. i think it took about a bubble of mana per mob for me to solo in kael. Much better than wasting 70% of my mana root blasting them.

Nothing else... we shouldnt be cought with our pants down running through a zone without root loaded or oom with a green mob killing us.
