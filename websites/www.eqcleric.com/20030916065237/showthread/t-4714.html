Restandre
There are a lot of things I'd like to see. I'd like to see double attack, I'd like to see invis, I'd like to see completely outlandish things like getting death touch. But to be semi-realistic...

We are a defensive class. We are THE defensive class. Plate class mitigation is not an impossibility. I understand why we don't have it and am appreciative of the mere extended range of equipment wearing plate allows, but it would be nice. That notwithstamding, a more viable option would be better defensive skills. Parry and riposte don't make much sense, and block doesn't mean shield-blocking, but would it be impossible to have a higher dodge cap?

IMHO we need a couple more things that deal with undead destruction / control. The one AA ability (not counting innate IVU) that is at all undead related is one that absolutely nobody takes. It's an interesting ability, but it's not terribly useful, considering that it has a fear component, and so doesn't work effectively against mobs above 55. (And who needs 10k damage against an old world undead mob?)
Revamp Turn Undead - shorten it's usage, correct the innate level limit associated with it, maybe make it exceptionally more useful and then limit it to 4 targets seeing as it is a targeted effect.
Other options would be the undead snare listed above, or some *real* undead dots. I think a dot that has worse efficiency than a nuke is a joke. You can't say giving clerics some slightly stronger dots is unbalancing considering necros solo their entire lives with dot stacking, and I'm not hinting that we should go that far. But stacking two dots for less than 2k damage for roughly 600 mana is obscene.

I think "full complete heal" should be an AA option. Increased chance of critical on CH, a CH ability with a reuse timer or something. I realize that 7500 is more than plenty; it just doesn't sit well with me that a spell called complete healing has been changed to not do that. I realize there's little practical benefit except against a mob that uses balance.

Just a couple other ideas for consideration~
