Yes 132 seems awfully low for your level. Instead of spending that whole 1.6k on armor, there are some more free quest items you might consider...

Paw of Opolla - 9 wis (other stats too), primary secondary
I was able to camp this without difficulty in my early 30's. Had a low (24?) druid friend that camped with me to help with some root bombing. Sells for 3k at bazaar, but camping is free.

Prayer Cloth of Tunare - 3 wis, shoulder
Kill the Orc Warlord in Crushbone, then turn in his Bracers of Battle to the elven priest at the slaver camp. Casting Wrath on him once would probably be close to finishing him. Not much wis actually, but fast, free, and easy.

Bracers of the Reverent - 5 wis each, wrist
In North Karana, kill Zahal, then Regis spawns, so kill him too. Turn in Regis' drops and 2 enchanted plat bars (about 250pp) for each bracer. They're not no drop, but they sell for 600+ at bazaar.

Cheap stuff to buy...
Acrylia studded mask - 5 wis, I paid 50pp
Savant's cap - 5 wis, paid 50pp for this too, but have been meaning to upgrade to othmir
Ancient sensate shield - 5? wis, mine's been collecting dust in the bank since I got the testament actually

Not so cheap stuff to buy...
Adamantite epolets - 7 wis shoulder, 500pp (or drops in Guk if you and some pals want to camp it)
Cultural armor - depends on race, but good stats

That's all I can think of off hand. I equip the paw and testament while grouped, then while running around I usually equip a springwood club (6 wis?). I occasionally get a jab about still wearing newb quest armor, but I'm level 34 now and have 170-180 wis depending on what I'm equiping.

While boring, you can cast minor healing on yourself repeatedly during down time to increase specialization skill an reduce mana cost of heals. Something to do when you just missed the nexus port.

Just my 2cp.. hope it helps

Basalic
Sandals of the Skeptic drop in Scarlet Desert on the Plateau. I got a pair fighting there with my Bard, he was 45 and was grouped with 50-51 Mage and Wizard.

Don't know if this is an option, but try the Kromrif Military Leggings Quest. Low cost :D but good leggings to see you through till the 50's.

Change the Mask to a Sebilite Scale Mask, about 500-900pp on Morell Thule and get a pair of Gaunts of the Black (cheap but not sure on price but shouldn't be more than couple of hundred plat as they are so common).

Belt of the Cenobite should cost you 700-800pp but is a much better item than the Black Steel Wrap.

Also get two Bracers of the Reverent. Reasonable AC and good WIS, personally i'd ditch the RMB as I think they are a hugely overrated Bracer for Clerics.

This should spend a lot of your 3500 but will give you a good WIS and AC increase.

After this I would suggest saving up for a better BP, Froglok Scale is good and sells for around 3000pp last time I checked. Then swap out the earrings for Forest Loops, the BP will offset the loss of WIS here.

From there on in it's all about making purchases that keep your WIS around the 200 mark whilst increasing AC.

Items to watch out for are Cobalt Drake Hide Belt, Cultural Armour Bracers (if you can get these as a Human), and Donals Vambraces/Helm and boots.

My own setup is based around an Emerald Dragonscale BP which gives +20WIS. Cost me 13500pp some time back but it's been worth every cp. Many groups get wiped because the Cleric gets killed, but few groups get wiped because the Cleric is OOM.

If you're going to wear high wisdom, low ac gear, why not just make a druid and be done with it? Especially since they have CH now.

As you get into your mid-50s, you will find that being a wimp who dies after 4-5 hits from a blue mob is more than just annoying, it's downright embarassing. Clerics are allowed to wear plate, take advantage of that and wear some ac gear.

Rather than the velium veil, get acrylia studded mask (5ac 5wis). Nightshade wreath? That thing has what, 1ac? 2ac? Get something with wis & ac instead. I'd suggest the old cultural stuff if you were a dorf or HE, but I'm sure you can find something in the bazaar for a human too. Get 2 quest bracers instead of quest/RMB, the ac/wis combo rocks. Platinum armband is 0ac, that's a slap in the face - get giant scalemail arms, 11ac & same amount of wisdom. BS tunic only had 13ac, use the gold cleric quest bp instead for the ac until you can get a seb scale bp or that green banded-looking bp I forget the name of atm. Cloak? Get acrylia studded, 7ac 10wis, or get a friend and do the 10ac 10wis shield quest out of qeynos, that thing can go on your back. Gatorscale legs are druid gear... it's tough to get good wisdom legs, I'd say go with the golden quest ones for now until you get a nice drop (jarsath legs from KC basement are a good upgrade, 15ish ac & 5wis). Boots, also a tough call because the +9wis is quite a bonus. You may as well get them and tough it out until you get something nicer like quest boots or donals.

You're not a druid. Don't be one of those embarassing clerics who can't channel a heal or can't survive a mob beating on them for 10 seconds because their ac rivals that of the group's enchanter.

It is true that mana regen is more important than mana pool in most cases, but gear is relatively important. Levels are the easiest way to upgrade yourself, but a cleric really does need a decent mana pool to spit out those mana-intensive heals.

All of the items listed so far are decent items, and they will help you out enough. You really should hold off on min/maxing your gear until you hit 60.
