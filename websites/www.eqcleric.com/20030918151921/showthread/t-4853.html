Gericht
38 is a fine level to go to DS Peaks. The zone can be laggy and boring, so be forewarned. I'd definately consider some dungeon crawls or some other such fun at your level. Still, as requested:

Here's a link to a map of the region: http://eqlive.station.sony.com/comm...ts.jsp?id=53736

The zone is fairly easy to figure out. Basically groups tend to camp the various entrances:

NL: Netherbian Lair
ME: Maiden's Eye
SS: Sanctus Seru
GE: Greig's End

The GE and ME entrances are both near areas that tribals frequent. The SS entrance is the lowest level hunting ground, it tends to be where small zelniaks and lightcrawlers abound. NL entrance is camped for shrooms, zelniaks, large zelniaks, etcc.

You'll see wandering groups or soloists in the middle of the zone hunting large zelniaks or zelniaks (and occasional annoying wolf).

And, then the Rockhoppers (or Tribesmen, depending who is up) will also be camped. They are fairly high level. I can't recall any abbreviation used with them.

There are also a number of stonegrabbers spread throughout the zone, from my experinece they are undercons and, as such, seldom camped. Wolves are also extreme undercons but can be hard to avoid. Chances are most groups will have to tackle some.

At your level, your best bet is to get a tribal group. Again, those are either wandering groups or at ME or GE (usually the latter) entrances.

As I said, the zone is much simpler than HHK. You should have it figured out in a night. Have fun.

Penecillin
I've played DSP from 33-36 and the groups at ME, GE and in the center are consistently good.

Its a bit of an XP factory setup and I'm still getting about 1 blue per white con in a group of 5-6. If you're interested in loot then make sure there is an agreement on the Acrylia ore when you join a tribal group. Most times its distributed Alphabetically.

You can also quest the lightcrawler armour (see Alla) and some Fine Antique quests take in Mobs and items in DSP (again see Alla). You can sometimes sell the lightcrawler bits, armour and other quest stuff in the bazaar for good prices too.

Braeaelin
I soloed in DSP throughout most of my experience gained from 36 to 43. This was mainly to work on all of my skills. If I was still to group, I would have either tried for a rockhopper group or would have gone elsewhere because most mobs groups were fighting around the zonelines were light blue with the occasional dark blue. I setup camp just east of the mushroom fort which is west of the GE zone and near the zone wall. The only mobs that wander that area don't aggro (unless you're fighting a tribal and another should pass by). I fought only zelniaks and later large zelniaks. It might be handy to keep cure blindness up in case you're aggro'd and blinded by a lightcrawler. I used snare all the time so if you're a dark elf make sure to obtain your Regent Symbol of Innoruuk.

Avoid the bandit camps like they are KOS to you! They may only con dubious if I recall, but should there be a wolf in the area that aggro's you the whole camp will assist the wolf and you can pretty much consider yourself dead. My drogmor saved me a couple times fleeing with less than 5% health from full.

Nice looking zone but can be very boring. Good luck!
