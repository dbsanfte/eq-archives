Lucretia
You can press just the 'L' button to get the Leadership window to come up and then press 'L' again to get it to go away. CTRL-U brings up the tribute window so that you can see what tribute buffs you have and their upkeep as well as upgrade (at the Tribute Master only) or downgrade them.

Btw, there did seem to be some good Group Leadership abilities. There were two I remember specifically. One was to increase the mana in your group and the other was to increase the hitpoints of your group. I think they each had 2-3 levels. I'm not sure if it actually increases the mana/hp or increases the pool of each. I can't remember the description at the moment.

There were others to see where party members are on a map, to see the buffs of your group members, to 'mark' mobs, to see information on marked mobs, etc.

Edited to clarify I was writing about Group Leadership, not Raid Leadership in the second two paragraphs.
