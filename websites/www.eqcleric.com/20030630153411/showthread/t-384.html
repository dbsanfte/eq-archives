Dowel_Fearn
I did this quest about 2 years ago with my first EQ Charecter, a Paladin. IF memory serves me correctly, in order to recieve the Aegis of Life, you need BOTH versions of Grimrot; the Undead version and the Living version. Each drops a piece that you need for turn in.
Now the trouble comes into play in the fact that Grimrot back then was set on a 6 hour Real Time spawn timer AND a 50/50 chance of getting one of the versions of his lordship.
Look for the Skelli that spawns inside one of the wooden frames. Spawn breakdown is: 1 in 4 chance: PH Skelly
1 in 4 chance: PH Skelly
1 in 4 chance: LIVE Grimrot
1 in 4 chance: Undead Grimrot

So after looking at that breakdown, making note that its 6 rl hours between spawns.. and getting first one form of Grimrot, then the other.. the quest is VERY frustrating..
It took me a full week to get the first version of Grimrot, and a helpful guildmember sent me a tell a few days later that the other version of Grimrot was standing arround SK waiting for me.. hehe
Good luck and dont loose hope or patience..
