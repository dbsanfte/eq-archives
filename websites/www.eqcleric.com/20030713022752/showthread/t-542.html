Well, Temperance is a boon for younger clerics. I really wish this spell had been around back in the day;)

Temperance gives 800 Hit Points, 48 AC.

Let's take a look at some of the numbers for the other buffs combined:

Level 50~

Symbol of Naltron = 525 Hit Points @ Level 50
Resolution = 250 Hit Points @ Level 50, and 16 AC.
Shield of Words = 31 AC @ Level 49

Level 52~

Heroism/Heroic Bond = 360 Hit Points, 18 AC
Symbol of Naltron = 525 Hit Points
Shield of Words = 31 AC


So at Level 50, you are still able to use Temperance, knowing that while they aren't getting a huge AC upgrade (Only 1 point difference), the hit point difference is 25.

Also, Mana cost to cast the 3-spell combo = 725, Mana cost to cast the Single Buff spell = 550. So Unless you had Donal's Legs, Temperance is still the better spell all around.

At Level 52, however, we see that using Symbol/Hero/SoW gives 885 Hit Points, 49 AC. Mana cost for the 3-spell combo here is 755, unless you use the group Hero spell.

Conclusion: I'd switch to Symbol/SoW/Heroism at level 52, and possibly pick up some Donal's Pants to save the spell slot.
