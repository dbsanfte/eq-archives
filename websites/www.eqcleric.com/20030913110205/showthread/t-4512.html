Shadowfrost
I play in 1200x900 resolution. However, the viewing area of my screen is smaller than that; there are black spaces around the edges. My various UI windows (chat windows, casting bar, group window, raid window etc.) are arranged over the black spaces rather than over the viewing area, so that they don't get in the way.

In order to make the viewing area smaller, what I did was to type /view 200 100 800 600. In other words, my viewing area is 800 pixels by 600 pixels and there are black spaces 200 pixels wide below, to the left of, and to the right of my viewing area. The space above the viewing area is only 100 pixels wide.

Dyschord referred to Caleal's UI's above; Caleal P`Terak, who wrote several UI's, is apparently a confirmed /viewport addict and as I remember, he has written his UI's specifically with the /viewport command in mind.
