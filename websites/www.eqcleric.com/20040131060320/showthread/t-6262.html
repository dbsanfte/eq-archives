Skinsear
I've made about 4k in the last week just buying and selling stuff in the bazaar (thanks to the great advice from the people in this forum!). I always check the prices of rockhopper hides - they sell like hotcakes and if you can find a good deal on them, buy them and mark up the price! High end players with loads of pp will pay a lot for these items.

Also, use your alts to check out vendors in your home cities - buy any spider/spiderling silks you find, as well as HQ hides etc. Easy way to make some good money. It takes a while, but by leaving your character in the bazaar overnight, you'll sell your stuff pretty easily.

Every time I find one of my chars low on plat, I go to BB Mountains (as stated in the discussion that I linked above). I go to a goblin camp right by the "T" in the path and there are 6-7 goblins that pop there (heads & blood are stackable and worth 4pp to nearby vendor), not to mention, a Wandering Greenblood happens by every once in awhile whose medallion is also worth about 4pp to the vendor. Not the most exciting way to raise plat, but it works for me. I just grab a book, read a couple of pages, kill pops, repeat.
