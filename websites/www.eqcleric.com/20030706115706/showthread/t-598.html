My mage has lives in FG, and has since about December. On Druzzil it has to be the best 50+ zone to hunt. Since PoP noone, and I mean noone goes, unless its for a quest related item in the town.

I didnt go there however (soloing) until 59. At 59 i was able to single the shrooms. I was able to pull with malosi from quite a distance. I think it would be an excellent zone to exp in for your levels. There are a couple places in particular that would be ideal to hunt in, the first suggested one would be the mushroom pod by the lake that you pass on your way to Twilight Sea zone. The other would be past the pod mentioned above heading to tw sea zone. Take a sharp left at the first deep cavern lotus you run into. Continue running left and hugging zone wall, and you will come to some Fungal beasties... there is a shroom pod off to the right of them. This is my new favorite spot in FG. So long as you dont get too close to the Fungi beasts or the lotus, its a super great spot. Heres what i do..

There are two lotus that spawn tot he left of the shroom pod. The Lotus cast like a wizzy somewhat and can/will summon you/root you. Sending my pet in on one, ever since 59 I can get away with singling them so long as I dont use an AoE /dead . If you get aggro from the second, they cast fast and hard. btw the lotus stay stationary, they do not move. These drop sap and other parts to make sweet lotus pie (or whatever its called) and can net you very nice ching to a vendor, even more to someone tradeskilling. (Also looted a 9 wiz 45 manna neck. lotus vine whip) Once the lotus are dead (15 min repop) I pull the shroom pod. (So long as you dont get within the lotus aggro range they dont have to be killed, but i hihgly recommend it, you dont need lotus aggro while fighting the shrooms) There are about six or seven shrooms to a pod. Have your group pull out their MR gear and keep them buffed against magic. The shrooms cast stuns, slows, and they have a 1-200 pt Stun called Shreikers Curse... turns your tank into a shroom while slowing them. The shrooms drop fungus clumps, and stems. The pristine stems sell for 20pp while the lower end ones sell for about 5pp. They drop some of the new words, and for rares i have looted three Mushroon veils and three 5 dose potions of shrink. Once the shroom pod is clear there is a shriknar forager againt the wall between the lotus spawn and the shroom pod. (There is another to the far rright of the shroom pod) These are melee only mobs that have high ac/hp and hit fast. Really helps for these to be slowed. These will drop chitin plates (11pp) eyeballs (18pp) and spells. I have looted several 54 shammy/chanter/necro spells and even a few mage spells off these. (The same spells will drop off of the Fungal beasts and the Shadel bandits. ~~also looted a SK spell)

You can of course, if the zone isnt crowded on your server as well, fight closer to the zone in from the city. Just as you enter the "hunting ground" from the town there is a nice pod, although heaveily lotus infested (which is a good thing loot wise hehe) Also on the hill in the corner here is a shadel bandit camp. These can *only* be pulled if pacified. We had a level 53 Pally pacify the camp, then we pulled one at a time until clear. Then moved right into their camp. They are DB at 62 and very good AA exp. Dropped alot of spells and we got three bandit rings 55hp 55manna (save cold i think) 12 ac

NOW.... the pod by the lake.... (on your way to TS) Nice spot to camp at against the back wall *away* from the one Lotus to be found here at this camp. There are mutant dregs. These too must be pacified to be singled, but the exp and potential loot is worth the trouble. Once you get a spot clear the repop is plenty of time to med/rest. I have looted A putrid belt 8int 8 wiz other misc stats, and some nice shoulders I gave to my druid. 6wiz 6int These are melee only mobs, hit near 140ish. You could also pull shriknar to the TW sea zoneline

One more note about the shrooms. While two boxing with my 52 druid, when attempting to cast snare I got thay annoying message that I was unable to alter the mobs runspeed at all, I was also unable to root. If you have a mezzer, (assuming they can be mezzed) you're ok. I am sure that to start with, lest you pacify them first, you will get adds on pulls, until your party gets it down.

There are no wandering mobs in this zone. I have gone afk in here for literally hours. You occasionally have someone who cant get an inviz in town and brings a hellacious train to TW sea zone, but unless at the lake, I have been unaffected.

Having said all that..... I would recommed bringing a druid/wizard for evacs at least until your group gtes the hang of things. Its almost impossible to make it to zone out if you get a bunch of shrroms aggrod on you, you will run by a lotus and be summoned anyway.... best to just die the best spot you can retrieve it in.

If/when you go, let us know how things went. Good luck!~
