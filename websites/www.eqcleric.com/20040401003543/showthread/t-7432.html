Crovan
Name: Elembri
Class: Cleric
City: Felwithe
Did it go anywhere: Level 53, maybe higher someday.


When my roomie and I decided to start EQ, she got the game like two weeks before I did, so I settled down to watch her play. She made a high elf mage right off the bat, just by looking at the info on the character creation screen (and she still refuses to change mains to this day, 4 years and 65 levels later). Anyway, when she first logged into the game, the gamma correction was all the way down, and of course we didn't know anything about what was happening or how to change it -- all we knew was that the screen was so dark she could barely see. She kept falling into the water and drowning... it was so awful and frustrating that we almost gave up. Fortunately a kind soul came by and explained things to her, and once we could see what was going on, we were both hooked.

I researched on the net for weeks before I started, trying to decide on my class, and ultimately decided on cleric. Originally I wanted to be a gnome, but my mage friend pointed out that I would start in a different city, and how would we ever find each other in game?? :) So I made a high elf instead.

I originally enjoyed being a cleric very much, but eventually, my patience with the class waned -- I felt stagnant and trapped in my role. I tried a variety of other classes, keeping the cleric as my main... until I made my beastlord. I soloed him up so fast, and had so much fun doing it that it was only slightly painful for me to change my main to the bst without a second thought.

Crovan came into existance when I started a second account and wanted to be able to rez my beastlord. At the time, character moves between accounts weren't available (or maybe I just didn't know about them, I don't recall now), so I started a brand new cleric, a dark elf this time because I had discovered that I like being evil. Elembri only gets played when I need to rez or buff or PL a toon on Crovan's account... sadly, he was my very first toon, and it shows in his terrible gear. Crovan had better at level 1 than poor Elembri is wearing at 53. I've always said that when I have plat to burn, I'll buy him some nice stuff just because I still have a soft spot for him.

Now I've got two 50+ clerics, and I don't regret either one of them at all... I'm enjoying being a cleric again when I play him solo or group him with others besides my own alts, recapturing a little of what I liked so much about the class in the beginning. It's still not where my EQ heart is set, but I appreciate the class more for what it is now, and hopefully am a better cleric after playing and understanding the DPS and Tank roles that I fulfil in different situations on my beastlord.

-Crovan, Cleric of Hate
