Agmyleez
While downloading today's patch I noticed that one of the files downloaded referred to Nedaria's Landing. So I took off to go see what kind of changes had been made.

First thing I noticed was that I could no longer enter Nedaria's Landing from the cave entrance on the east side of Jagged Pine. After some asking in zone I was finally able to learn that you had to enter the "Good" Gnoll cave thats on the West wall, go through the castle gate, go past the king and under a waterfall.

Once I got into Nedaria's I ran to the stream that runs towards the Ocean and immediately noticed some new buildings.

After exploring the new dock and boat I went to the Adventurers camp to see what sort of NPC's they had there. Some of them were giving out quests that could be done from within the zone and some just gave Lore of what was happening etc.. However, I noticed there was a Magus in the camp...


http://webpages.charter.net/rnbecky/nd4a.jpg
Click thumbnail for larger image


I had to give ole Magus a try and guess what?.. He ports you to any of the other Adventure camps. And to my suprise, by saying "Nedaria" to any of the Magus's in any of the camps, will return you to Nedaria's Landing.

Once I had returned to the Adventurers camp of Nedaria's I turned around and directly in front of me stood Nedaria herself, with her lover Callinav.


http://webpages.charter.net/rnbecky/nd1a.jpg

If you haven't been to this zone yet I suggest a quick trip from a local Magus just to see how the waves crash on the shore and the schools of fish that swim in the ocean. The graphics in this zone rate among the best in the game and is well worth the trip!

Scura
This might be a silly question.. but if it's an adventure camp, does that mean all races are safe there?

Agmyleez
I can't say 100% that everyone is not KOS because I have not been there with any races except my Cleric who is evil and my Druid who worships Tunare. Niether of them were KOS. However, I will say that it is a safe bet that no one would be KOS.

I might add though, that there is a very good possibility you could be KOS in Jagged Pine which is the connection zone to get to Naderia's landing. So I would recomend that if you go, go to one of the AC camps and use Magus to get there.

Also be aware that the Turtles and Frogs seem to be KOS to everyone. The zone is a 30ish level zone, so the mobs are near that level. I had a lot of problems getting agroed by the turtles and frogs to the point it was driving me crazy. I stayed invis most the time just avoid them.
