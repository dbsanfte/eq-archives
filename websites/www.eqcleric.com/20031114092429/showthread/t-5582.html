Milletoux
Also, the dungeon seems to scale for the number and level of people who exist when you get your adventure, not the number and level of people who enter the dungeon. So if you get an adventure for 6 people and one (or more) of your number splits or goes LD and doesn't come back, the dungeon is still scaled for those intial 6 people at the levels calculated at that time.
