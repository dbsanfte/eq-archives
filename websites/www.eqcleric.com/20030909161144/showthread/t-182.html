Shadowfrost
Not sure how you'd use Hammer of Souls, these hit for 508 so I wouldn't want to melee one. I think Traves means Hammer of the Sun. And no, you don't need a Hammer of the Sun - although it does help considerably. If you're a dark elf, a Regent Symbol of Innoruuk also rocks. What you DO need is level 65, a reasonable mana pool and a decent flowing thought count.

Personally I would go for the Valorous Spirits, which are white in colour and they aren't actually in the SE basement. To find them, first get invis, then zone into HoH and follow the left hand wall until you reach the three Wrulons. Just past the three Wrulons, move into the building and set up in one corner; nothing will path to the corners there.

Load up Pacification, Sound of Might, Tarnation, Petrifying Earth, Sermon of Penitence, Epitaph of Life, a heal, and gate for if things go wrong. Use pacify to single-pull one, root it, and then stack up the DOT's until it dies.

I've also done this camp duo with a necromancer, which trivializes it totally.
