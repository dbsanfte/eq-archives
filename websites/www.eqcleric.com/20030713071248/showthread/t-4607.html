Shadowfrost
There are five kinds of Velious armour: Plane of Growth, Thurgardin, Kael Drakkal, Skyshrine, and Plane of Mischief.

Of those, the Plane of Growth armour is equivalent in quality to Thurgardin quest armour, but harder to obtain because of the sheer rarity of the drops in PoG.

The Thurgardin stuff is easy to obtain by hanging around with a few friends in the place variously known across different servers as the Riff House or the Plate House. It also drops behind the Arena in Kael Drakkal, although rather more rarely.

These two kinds of armour might be considered the `basic` Velious armour and in my view they are the least desirable.

The Kael Drakkal armour quest pieces drop in the Western Temple of Veeshan. They are fairly common, and the armour that results is of medium desirability.

The Plane of Mischief armour quest pieces theoretically drop in the Plane of Mischief, and the quests to obtain the armour are quite complex. I say "theoretically drop" because in practise, the drop rate is so low that you need to clear the plane completely several times in order to receive the quest parts for a single piece of armour. Obtaining it is impractical.

The Kael Drakkal and Plane of Mischief armour pieces have better stats than the PoG/Thurg armour and might be considered "middle of the range" in desirability.

The Skyshrine armour which drops in the Eastern Temple of Veeshan - the area known as the Halls of Testing - has the best stats for a cleric, and if you're in a guild which can raid this area, then it drops very frequently compared to the other Velious quest armours.

So if you're in the fortunate position of being able to choose, go Thurgardin at first (while you build up faction), then Skyshrine when you have the necessary faction and levels.

You also need to know that the plate armour made by tradeskillers is, in some cases, as good as or better than the Skyshrine stuff.

In particular, cultural plate bracers are notably better than Velious quest gear, and the hurricane plate gauntlets are also considerably better.
