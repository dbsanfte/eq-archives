Galv
Definately try to get the scarab armor--it's very very cheap and isn't too bad for low level players. In terms of making some cash, one of the better and steadier ways to do it in BB is simply by doing the bone chip quest:

1) You get XP for killing the skellies.
2) You get some change and an occasional piece of armor off them.
3) When you hand in the chips (4), you get faction, coins, xp, and an item that can be sold.

Also, sometimes people are looking to purchase the bone chips for upwards of about 10-20 pp a stat, which isn't too shabby at your level.

Because our charisma is so so low, it's important to get as much faction in Kalidim as possible, that way when you are selling items you are making maximum profit. Also, try going to the Bazaar and picking up some Split Paw gloves and certain pieces of Ulthork Armor. On Xev, the pieces sell as low as 5 pp each, which is an excellent deal. For another 50 - 100 you can get yourself a bloodstained tunic or rygor tunic, both of which will help your mana pool/wisdom among other stats.

Just my 2cp though...
