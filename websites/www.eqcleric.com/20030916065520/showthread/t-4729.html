I've never been part of a failed ring war myself - though I've seen the wreckage of Thurgadin after one. It was ... incovenient, I went to Thurgadin to buy something that's only sold there - I waited around for a while (this was before books - ugh) and finally just gated out. Long respawn. That's the one flaw of the event IMO - it's really unfortunate that anyone wishing to go to Thurgadin is penalized - at least for as long as it lasts. I could see 20 minutes; it's kind of funny to see the gored guards and murdered merchants... for a little while.

What happens, Battering, is the quester hands in the 9th ring to an NPC outside of Thurgadin. That despawns all of Great Divide, and spawns the Coldain and Kromrif armies. Your mission... to prevent wave after incoming wave of Kromrif from reaching Thurgadin. The Coldain armies can be led by the quester; they help. Needless to say, quite a number of people are needed - and all must be non-KoS to Coldain.

If you're ever in GD and the zone depops... watch. It's an awesome thing.
