Greetings to you all! My name is Elliandra Soothingsoul devoted Priestess of Tunare (ok well preistess in training anyway ;) ) I have currently seen 27 seasons in the lands of Terris-Thule and can usually be found in the company of one Gooptar Quagmire, my husband and ever-present meat shield. I have traveled many of the paths of Norrath, Luclin and have most recently discovered the wonders to be found in the Plane of Knowledge. After a long and trying journey wading through the bodies of the neatly slain orcs in the Faydwer I made my way to the continent of Antonica, where I met my aforementioned husband. *sighs dreamily in rememberance* He was shiny and green with a large sword and an enticing "Ribbit". He was slowly hopping through the Swamp of Innothule in search of mightier prey when we fell to hunting together and now are always found in one anothers company. We banded with a group of friends into the guild Divine Union of Souls and are currently the youngest members, age-wise anyways (as the others have surpassed our experience by moving on to bigger and better hunting grounds). We prefer to meander through the different lands taking in all the new and wonderous things around us and of course saving those in need and punishing those who are also in need /smilewink .
