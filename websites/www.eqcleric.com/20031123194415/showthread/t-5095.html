I guess my questions are where is the best place to get this drop, how rare is it (can't be THAT rare I have heard it sells for about 100pp in the Bazaar which is no help cuz I only ever have like 17pp) and what lvl/group arrangement would be best to hunt whereever I would need to in order to get the drop (I've given up on buying it...I kinda feel like it takes the fun out of getting the spell).

Rauri
As you progress much more in levels you will find two things:

1. You will NOT be able to camp for all the spells you want / need, and
2. You WILL get better at making money

If you want the good spells be prepared to buy them in the bazaar. Hunt places like Tower of Frozen Shadows and sell the velium in the bazaar. One brick of velium sells for way more than 17pp ;) and you'll soon be able to buy your needed spells.

And Celestial Healing is very much worth it, get it as soon as possible!
