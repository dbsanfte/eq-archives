Gurm
For those of you thinking this is a useless spell, think of it this way:

For every caster in the group/raid with BoR, a mob encounter goes by faster. Let's take for example a group with 2 melee's and 4 casters with MoR. Basically that means that the kill goes by about 4/6 * 10% = 6.67% faster.

Sure you might not notice the difference with one mob, but when you're killing 50 mobs that each takes 4 minutes to kill, the above group would finish the feat in 185 minutes instead of 200 minutes.
