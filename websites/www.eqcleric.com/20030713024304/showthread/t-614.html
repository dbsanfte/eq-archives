Mojoejojo
Throw a high level chanter together with a complete healing (True CH, not the 7500), and there is not much they cant do. I didnt realize this till I saw a cleric CH (7500 hp) a charmed mob, and only healed it for a bub and a half of life. Rough estimate says the mob had around 25k hp. I hate to say, but situations like that make player tanks trivial.
Even with CH caped at 7500 hp, I cant really say I have ever seen a MT not be fully healed when it lands. When fighting mobs that throw out enough damage to quickly take down a well equiped 65 warrior, (Usually raid type encounters), there are usually numerous spam healers, or even the clerics are spam healing simply due to CH's long cast time.
True, some MTs are now buffing to over 9k hp, but it is by far still the exception, not the norm, and the encounters where damage received by the main tank is steady and predictable enough that a cleric can cast and have CH land on the MT with one bub or less of health are not as common as they used to be, at least in the planes.
