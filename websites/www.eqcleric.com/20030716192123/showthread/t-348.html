Jarvis
Welcome back to the world of clerics!!

Well for starters you'll have some zones to level up in.. Netherbian Lair, Bandits camps in Paludal Caverns, Maru Seru and Dawnshroud Peaks. bandit camps in paludal as well as Maru Seru/Netherbian will take you to 30+ quickly, and then it's on to Dawnshroud for a while for some good experience.

One major addition has been the Bazaar, and reason for mentioning this is you know have a market for buying and upgrading gear at a lower cost. Possilibility of comparing prices on same item has made trading more real to RL trades. I bet you'll be surprised.

As a cleric you have a few new spells to play with for sure...

You do in fact have a new rez spell to look for, Reparation, which will restore 20% exp.

There's Blessing of Piety which is a 10% Spell Haste spell at level 19, and it's upgrade Blessing of Faith at lvl 39.

Some combat enhancements, like Bash, will soon be available to you, and Yaulp line of spells just got an update to use less mana. Expect to see more enhancements to shield and how it works in-game soon.

Then there's the new UI. You can customize it now, and play around with it. Takes some getting used to, but you'll love it once you get it how you want it. Visit http://www.eqinterface.com for new skins and a lot of eyecandy and useful mods to enhance your cleric experience.

There's a lot I'm forgetting here especially if you have the latest expansion, Planes of Power, but this will get you startet.

We'll be launching our spelldatabase shortly, so stay tuned for a great way to check the spell updates!
