Galv
Here's some suggestions for making some money, just within the newbie zones--

Butcherblock (Dorfs!)
Kill the undead and save their bone chips. You can sell them in the bazaar for anywhere between 5gp and 1pp each, or you can turn it into a quest npc near the palladin guild in Kaladim for xp, coin, and a minor item.

Skunks drop an item worht a handful of pp. I think it is the skunk eye, but not sure.


Everfrost (Conan's people)
Kill the undead and save their bone chips. You can either sell them in the bazaar as mentioned, or take the PoP book down to Butcherblock to do the quest. Kill the spiders and save ALL the silk. Sell it in the bazaar for 2-5pp each. Kill bears and save pelts that are low quality and higher. Ruined pelts are no good.


Stonebrunt (I "think" it's stonebrunt--the zone for newbie gnomes!)
Kill everything and hope that iron oxide drops. You can sell that for 20pp each (maybe more, but I forget).


That's about it for making easier money as far as I can think of in a brief time. I'm sure others on the board can add their thoughts.

Dowel_Fearn
