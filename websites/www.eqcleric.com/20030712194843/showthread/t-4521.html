Bonra
Make sure there are no large electronic devices next to your network card antenna or your router, especially any speakers cause they have large magnets in them. Your computer its self doesn't affect the signal because it's in a metal case(E&M, don't get me started) but a stereo, speakers, television, nuclear reactor, etc. near them will.

Battering your problem may actually not be due to the wireless but due to a setting you need to change on Linksys routers involving port triggering. SOE has finally provided "router support" on their tech page, basically a compilation of all the fixes players have found for them here: http://eqlive.station.sony.com/supp...t/ts_router.jsp
