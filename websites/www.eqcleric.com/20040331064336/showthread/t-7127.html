Suzybabe
Was in a funny group last night. Only had an hour and a half to play so I headed over to KC as I knew there would be people there for a group. Sure enough as soon as I zoned in there were about ten people sitting around the entrance and within a minute I got asked to join a group. Only three of us, one of whome I'd grouped with a few times before but never met the other two. Anway the person who invited me suddenly said that we were waiting for her rl husband who was a high level mage as he was going to PL her. Well she was 49 so I thought it was a bit strange to be PL'd at that level and KC probably wasn't the place to do it anyway but I thought what the hell I've only got an hour or so. So after a minute this 62 Mage turns up and joins the group and we got another pick up person so the five of us head off into the castle and up the stairs to the first level.

Well for the next hour it was like being back at school, the Mage was standing at the back issuing orders just like a school master, watch out for that skeletal guardian, I'd mez that drovalg sentry if I was you, stun that skelly. The worst thing though was when he said, "Step back and watch me solo this one." If we were on a pvp server I would of attacked him right then! The thing was I could of understood it if we were all in our teens or twenties but we were all upper 40's or lower 50's so in theory we all had a good idea of what we were doing anyway! On top of that the xp wasn't flying so why they chose to PL in KC I have no idea.

After 30 minutes of this I was getting pretty annoyed with him but I bit my tongue as I only had a while to go and I thought it best to just get what little xp I could and then leave nicely which I eventually did. I have no idea how long the others lasted but they seemed a pretty meek lot !
