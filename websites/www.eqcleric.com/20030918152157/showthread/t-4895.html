I have to admit, on the scale of things that annoy me in EQ, this is fairly low...but I'm bored at work and figured I'd share.

Whenever I read a post that starts, "I'm thinking of making a cleric, and I have 52k to spend on him/her....", it twists my tail a bit.

I'm not really sure why, though I think it has to do with the fact my cleric was my first character. I've never had even 10% of that at any one time...heck, I've had maybe 4% of that at any one time, and that was when I was saving up to buy a drogmar.

Is it jealousy? Could be; I think of all the nice gear I could have had on my cleric at level 1. /shrug

On the one hand, I'd have loved to have that kind of plat available; on the other hand, I take a perverse pride in the gear I have. Some of it (though very little any more) are pass-alongs. Most of it I've looted, quested or bought with plat he's earned. He's been the "sugar daddy" for my other alts, no the other way around.

Do I think any less of people that do this? Absolutely not. This is just a means for me to get this off my chest.
