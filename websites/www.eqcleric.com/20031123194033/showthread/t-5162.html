Doohickey
The Gulf Water isn't a common drop, but it is hardly a rare one (compared to other drops).
I've camped for it twice now near the fort in Gunthak, and got it within 20-30 minutes each time.

The first time, two of us needed it...it still didn't take more than 30 minutes.

I don't remember if it was a specific mob we got them from..probably a Pillager.

As you run into the tunnels from the lighthouse, just take the first left. Set up camp at the entrance to the area (note: Not a different zone) and start pull-pull-oulling away. With a Harmony or Pacify Caster, singles are easy, and doubles really aren't that big a problem. The only mob to seriously pay any attention to is the wandering Shaman who will buff the mobs and, of course, cast Nasty Things at you. If you move in to the left and pull from the back side of the outside area of the fort, you won't even have to deal with her.

I haven't done the Nadox part yet...I've been waiting for Duhikae to be old enough so that the both of them could go in and duo it. Both Shaman and Cleric need the same drops, with the Shaman just handing back the Cleric spell to get the Shaman version of it after turn in.
