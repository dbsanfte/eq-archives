Dyschord
Nadox is pretty simple. When you enter the zone from Gunthak, just follow the left wall, through the lava. Follow it till you see the bridge, and you can shimmy up the wall near the bridge. Be invised.

From there it's just a matter of picking what part you want to camp. The Luggald Room is the middle Entrance on the left (if facing the bridge, with the Gunthak zone to your back) and they drop the Pure Blood item.

For the Vial of Blackend Blood, you want to go across the bridge (from the Lugglad room), and take the first entrance on your right. You'll go straight in a bit, and you can find a few safe spots for camping. Then just pull the needed mobs to you.

You can invis around in there for the most part, and camp times may vary, but I did it in a fairly short amount of time.
