Galv
You need to get a smith to make it for you, and generally it will set you back 1000s of pp per piece. I'll give you an example for cleric cultural armor--dorf of course! however, I am sure that the other cultural armor is about the same.

There are three levels of dwarven armor out there:

Enchanted
Dwarven
Underfoot

The enchanted and Dwarven armor are somewhat reasonable in price, as the pieces don't generally run over a couple of thousand. However, for the underfoot armor, it's going to run roughly 5k for a bracer and up to about 20k for a bp or greeves.

The enchanted armor does not (if memory serves correct) have much--if any--wisdom or mana bonuses, as it's primary stats are physical. The Dwarven armor has the wisdom and mana bonuses AND some physical AND some resist stats too. The underfoot armor dwarfs (pun intended) the previous two sets, as it has mad AC/HPS (BP for examp tops 50 AC and hps) and very solid mental and physical stats. I also think that underfoot has some resists in it (could be wrong).

So, if you want cultural, do one of three things:

1) Raise your smithing to 250.
2) Get a GM smith buddy to make it for you.
3) Hire a GM smith to make it/buy it from the bazaar.

On Xev, there is always at least a couple of different pieces of each type of cultural armor available.
