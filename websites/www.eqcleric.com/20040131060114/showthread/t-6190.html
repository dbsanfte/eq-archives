Shadowfrost
Extended Enhancement IV only works on spells with a minimum duration of 48 seconds. Therefore, it simply does not affect our Heal Over Time spells.

According to Lucy Extended Blessing IV works on spells with a minimum duration of 24 seconds. Extended Blessing/Enhancement type spells, unlike most focus effects, have a fixed rather than a random effect, and therefore you can say with certainty that Extended Blessing IV will add 2.4 seconds to the duration of your Heal Over Time spells.

Heal Over Time spells work every tick, i.e. every 6 seconds. Therefore, mathematically you would have to time your spell to land during the last two fifths of the tick in order to get the 8th HoT tick.

It's hard to time such spells so precisely because there are two sets of lag to take into account - lag from your client to the server, and then lag from the server to your target's client.

So although I haven't experimented, I think that you need to view Extended Blessing IV as giving you a 40% chance at an 8th HoT tick. My own view is that Extended Blessing IV isn't worth it.
