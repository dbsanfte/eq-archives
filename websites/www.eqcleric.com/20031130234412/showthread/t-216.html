Cerrick
Its amazing,

The plane of knowledge huge.. The central library is monstrous, people reading from the shelves here sell spells for every class.

I found 2 new cleric spells:

Blessing of Piety, level 19 spell.

Blessing of Faith, level 39 spell.

These appear to be mana regen folks :)

spell message says when you cast it on yourself : Your thoughts race as reverence fills your mind.

These are targetable and they stack. and I kept them up all night. Its not KEI.. but it did seem to help.

Lots of other things in the plane.. its not just a library ofcourse .. Vendors and trade skills are all here. Check it out for your new planar fletching kit or sewing kit :)

The look of the place is great too there seems to be alot more variance in the shape of the trees and rocks. The Sounds are awsome.. footsteps echo when inside.. a pub has lots of singin and carrying on goin on when you approach .. and it fades to crickets chirping as you draw to more open areas.

Plane of Tranquility .. heh this is basically the portal to all the other planes.

It sucks.. to quite.. the ocean drove me nuts.. the seagulls.. ACK!!.. hee jk its nice and serene for sure.

Seems like from here you can enter the Plane of Justice Plane of Innovation Plane of Disease and Plane of Nightmares right off the bat. there are portals to all sorts of other realms.. most said that i did not possess the will to enter.. something like that. Lots of quests here ect. so keep and eye out for what people say.

Its been said that you must do quests to gain access to the rest of the planes.

Plane of Innovation.. Rampaging automatons Smashin the bejeebees outta yer tank. Killed a few things here before moving on .. this is no theme park.. Everything echo's in your ear(spells ect) and gets to ringing pretty loudly. The monsters are nasty.. take a good group.

Plane of Justice.. its a huge prison with no way out except for ports. I met the tribunal.. :) big dudes.. lemme tell yah.

Plane of Disease: Looks like a magnified image if the inside of someones nose.. Huge hairlike things.. the ground looks like skin .. and there is a green ooze lake. Music is awsome and frightening. Stuff is tough here too..

That was my tour of the planes.. Didn't make it to nightmare.

Blessing of Faith (39 Spell) is a 10% Spell haste buff.

Check your cast times on your spells, then buff yourself with this and check them again... you will see "Your casting time" next to the regular casting time and it will show a shorter number.

It's a fantastic buff and it works with ALL spells even level 61+ ones! (Currently there are ZERO focus items in the game, Mage summoned or otherwise that work with 61+ spells.)

Be Oober and cast this on all the casters on your raid while you can. All my Wizard, Mage, Cleric friends request it now.
