Rauri
There's nothing wrong with being a Erudite cleric. They have a nice base wisdom, on par with the other more popular clerical races, and that's what really counts for most of a cleric's career. But what are the secondary considerations:

Erudites offer a superior int score, which is about worthless to the cleric class. Other races offer night vison, strong physical stats, better charisma or skills like Hide, all useful to the cleric.

It is these secondary factors that make Erudites less popular.
