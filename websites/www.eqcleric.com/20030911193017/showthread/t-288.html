Calisan
Grimrot is pretty easy once you've figured out the camp. The PH for Grimrot has a spawn time of around three hours and 20 minutes +/- 10 minutes. An EQ day is an hour and five minutes approx. Knowing this, you are now ready to camp grimrot.

Time your kill of the PH so that the next spawn time will make the PH pop after 7pm and before 7am for the undead version. Kill the PH so that it will pop again after 7am and before 7pm for the live version. Generally, it is best to time your kill of the PH so that the repop will occur in the middle of your time span.

So you did the above and Grimrot didn't pop huh? Well, here is the real kicker, he is a random mob. It is completely random if a Grimrot will pop when the PH pops. Tried and true method, I kid you not. I've been able to spawn live Grimrot twice, and dead grimrot twice using this method. I just killed PH to coincide with spawn time, and came back in about 3 hours to check results, and it does work. It might just take a few times, so be patient.

Now for Gimblox, another random mob. Could pop on the next spawn after PH is killed, could be 50 times you have to kill PH. When I set out to do my epic, I spent 27 hours camping Lord B, and 1 hour camping Gimblox, so I suppose it all worked out in the end. Good luck brave, bored, and berated clerics.
