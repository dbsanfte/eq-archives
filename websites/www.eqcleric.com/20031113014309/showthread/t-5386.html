Savyrass
First thing I usually do when I get a new spell is to cast it on myself and see exactly what it does (yes even harmful ones, hehe).
In the case of buffs, Ill load up every buff I have, cast the lower ones first and then see which ones the higher level ones overwrite. Obviously in some cases this isnt needed so much (like an upgrade to resist poison or something), but normally its a big help.

After all that....Temperance will not stack with your level 44 or below HP or AC buffs. But Ive found its worth buying those buffs anyway (except symbol) because they are good for pets and such. I say except Symbol, because Symbol uses a peridot anyway, so if I am going to spend a Dot on Symbol, Ill just spend a Dot and Temp instead. I use the lower Symbol on pets (uses Jasper and that stuff is cheap enough to waste).
