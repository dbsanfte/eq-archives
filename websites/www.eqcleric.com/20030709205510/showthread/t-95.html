Sure thing Jalad. I'm doing my epic right now to be able to write up an updated epic walkthrough. Hopefully I can get that done as soon as possible once complete.

Here's a quick lowdown on the next steps in your case though.

  • After giving "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer" and "Singed Scroll" to Omate Vastsea you should receive "Orb of Clear Water".

  • Find Natasha Whitewater. She's in the Inn behind Omat, and give her "Message to Natasha" which you received when handing "Ornate Sea Shell (II) to Naxot Deepwater in Burning Woods. You should receive "Shimmering Pearl" from her in return.

  • Now head to SolB. You have to camp for Zordak to spawn. He'll spawn under the banner next to Fire Giants Castle. He's on a fixed timer it seems between 21 and 24 hours. This spot is usually highly camped so get there early. Hand Zordak your "Shimmering Pearl" and he'll give you "Swirling Pearl" and "Zordak's Box of Bindings".


    Zordak will spawn under this banner.


  • Now you go to Skyfire and farm 4 "Pearlescent Fragments" which drop from random mobs in zone. Put in "Zordak's Box of Bindings" and hit combine. If you didn't receive the box for some reason you can get it from "Warder Cecilia" who you also turn in the box to after combined.

  • Turn in combined box to Warder Cecilia. She will give you "a Seal" and Zordak Ragefire spawns in human form. (Still lvl 55). Kill him and Zordakalicus Ragefire, the lvl 60 dragon spawns. Once you kill him, loot his "Impure Heart of Ragefire" container. It's NO DROP. Place "Swirling Pearl" and "Seal" inside it and hit combine to receive "Heart of Ragefire".

  • Head to Timorous Deep again and give Omat Vastsea "Heart of Zordak Ragefire" and receive "Orb of Vapor" in return.

  • Run down on beach and find Jhassad Oceanson at the far end. Give him "Orb of Frozen Water", "Orb of Clear Water" and "Orb of Vapor". You will receive "Orb of the Triumvirate".

  • Wait for Avatar of Water to make his way to you from the lake. He will come all the way up to you, and wait till he stops. Hand him "Orb of Triumvirate" and receive "Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh"

    I wish you the best of luck, and I'll get working on the revised epic walkthrough as soon as I'm done.
