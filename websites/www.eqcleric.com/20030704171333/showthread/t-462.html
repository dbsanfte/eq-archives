In Lucy, if you put "improved damage ii" in the "spell search" box, it will return the names of the items that have that effect. Doesn't say where to get them though, well, unless they're tradeable - I've always preferred looting stuff to paying for it... but then I didn't grow up with a Bazaar.

1. Book of Tactics, AC: +5 Dex: +6 Int: +6
Will certainly fit your faction, as it's droped by a Kromzek in the Arena area of Kael: Gkrean Prophet of Tallon (he's a cleric... he gates). (Tradeable)

2. Polished Mithril Mask - this is likely right in your guild's range of stuff to do. Magi Rokyl, Nagafen's pet mage, drops this. Fire Giants in SolB, fun dungeon crawl raid as long as Naggy's not up :p (Mask is AC5, Wis 7, Int 7, all/all). You won't find Rokyl until the end - he hangs out in Nagafen's Lair itself. (No Drop) This crawl can net your guild several nice items.

3. Imperial Wardog Collar (AC: +10 Sta: +9 Fire Resist: +7 Disease Resist: +7) from someone named Deathfang in Chardok... no clue where. (Tradeable)

4. Wurm Scale Coat (AC: +8 Magic Resist: +10 Fire Resist: +20). Another good possibility - this drops in Burning Woods from named wurms Nezekezena and Phurzikon (Tradeable).

5. Heavenfall Girding - new item, but it's for Froglok Wizards only.
