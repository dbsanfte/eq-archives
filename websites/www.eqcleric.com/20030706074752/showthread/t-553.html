Shadowfrost
I've seen research by a cleric who tanked one of the HoH critters for a while at varying AC totals.

The conclusion he came to was that, at ~1500AC, he took around 3% less damage overall than he did at ~1100AC.

That tells me that AC is practically worthless. If you would like to get hurt less in combat, my advice is to buy Lightning Reflexes. :)

I look at Natural Durability like this ~

It's an extra 200 + hit points, that only require some time spent exp'ing. No ridiculous amounts of money spent in the bazaar, no insane camping for "that special item". It's just free hit points for minimal effort.

Also keep in mind, that having maxxed
ND adds to each item you do obtain. So a 140 hit point bracer, is really 154 more hit points for you.
