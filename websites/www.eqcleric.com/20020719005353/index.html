Welcome to EQ Cleric



Welcome to the future site of www.eqcleric.com! As you can see the site is not even close to finished.

We're working on the design of the page which will include images of the different cleric classes in EverQuest.

If you want to be featured on the site, please send us a screenshot of your cleric character to jarvis@eqcleric.com. Please try and get a screenshot without the interface around it, and make sure every part of the character shows and is in clear view.

We will pick the screenshots we feel best represents the class for which we receive pictures. All pictures used will be featured with name, level, server, guild etc so please include this in your e-mail and we're looking forward to "seeing" you! :)
