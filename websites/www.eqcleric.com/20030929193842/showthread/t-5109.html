Doohickey
Well, just so I don't have to start a new thread...I'll hijack this one.

Dinged 39 last night!

I arrived in Dulak, set up camp in 'My' corner, and was just having Shazzog buff me when I see someone run by and down the gangway there. I figured someone was trying to snag the mobs there before I could, and shrugged it off. I had just arrived and hadn't announced my camp, so I wasn't going to worry about it.

I slapped Johny the Fisherman upside the head by way of saying "Hello" and, making sure .Yaulp really was Yaulp and not Gate , went to town on him.
Once he was down for his first nap, I checked the gangway to see if the Mysterious Stranger had taken them all...or gotten a dirt nap of his own for his troubles.
MS was still there. He had taken down Robert, but Fred apparently was a little rough for him.

I asked him if he was holding the gangway and he said, "Yeah, looks like that's about all I can handle solo at the moment."
Nothing makes me feel sympathetic like hearing that someone is trying to solo and getting their butt handed to 'em. Especially when they are level 52 monks.
I offered to have Shazzog buff him up, and he happily accepted. I figured Shazzog could make him a bit tougher, toss Chloro and Alacrity on him, and he could pull from the corner with me so that when they wore off, Shazz could refresh 'em. He offered to group up, instead.

Now normally, I try to avoid having anyone join my little duo setup. Especially non guildies I have never met. This is because I usually regret it when I do.
I was thinking that adding another char that was 14 levels higher than me was going to send my XP gain into the Abyss. Still, he seems nice enough...sure, why not.

I forgot how nice it could be to have an excellent monk as a puller. Yes, I got far less XP per kill than I would have with just Shazzog and I. It didn't matter, because he was pulling them into camp so quick, I barely had time to loot, and only got to meditate when he had to Feign Split (which was fine because the only mana I was using was for celestial heals). Shazzog was tossing off so many slows he had to start doing the Canni Shuffle in spite of having KEI and being on a Drogmor. Between my Battle Cleric self, Old Yeller and MS, the mobs were dropping almost before Shazzog could actually land the sparklies.

I turned autosplit off and just set all the gem drops aside for him. By the end of the night, I had about 350pp in coin and vendor trash, and he had 2 star saphires, 2 fire opals and an emerald ring.

At some point along the way, we were joined by Screall, one of the SK's in my guild. I dropped Shazz from the group, put Old Yeller to bed, and just had him toss off the Sparklies of Slowness.

Wasn't too long before I was calculating how many things still needed to die that I might have Complete Heal.
Shazzog is only 18% away from 52, himself, so he’ll probably go on an all AA diet pretty soon. He has a Mage to babysit when I am done with him, so I don’t want him getting too far ahead.

...

So tonight or tomorrow I will start camping Lord Gurgle'n'burp, then move my bivoac to Skyfire for shards.

Shazzog has just gotten to the point where he has forgiven me for Lord Gimpy, so hopefully the swimming around won't go on too long. I will just keep reminding him that while he may not get much XP in Skyfire, some of his spells drop there as well. That should, I hope, keep him focused for that long camp. I'll only be 39\40 to start, so XP should be decent for me.
