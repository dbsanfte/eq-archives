Funny thing happened to me last night. I was wandering around the various adventure camps trying to find a group. As I was using the magus a lot I kept myself in the say channel. I arrived at the North Ro camp and asked if anyone wanted a 53 Cleric. After a few minutes I get a tell saying "I do." So I think to myself great I've got a group and I look them up and find out it's a level 8 Cleric! I assume they are joking and use the magus to port to BB. A few minutes later I get another tell from the cleric saying hold on I'm on my way!

I'm a bit puzzled by now and send back a question mark and again there's a few minutes delay while I'm zooming around different camps and then I get a tell from the cleric saying "I have to come to you don't I?" Totally puzzled I send a tell back asking what he means and I get the reply, " Your giving away your 53 Cleric arn't you?"

I nearly fell off my chair! I sent a tell back explaining I was asking if anyone wanted a cleric for their group, not to give my account away :) He replied apologising and saying he thought it was to good to be true :)

I thought it was pretty funny and it shows how sometimes you have to be careful what you say :)
