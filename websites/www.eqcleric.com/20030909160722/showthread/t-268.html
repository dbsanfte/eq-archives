Gaelenn Purehea
A very easy camp for a lvl 50 cleric, upto lvl 52, is the Taskmaster camp in Echo Caverns.

The undead slaves and the Taskmaster, a shissar mob, are all undead, can be stunned, rooted and mez'd.
1 small note of warning tho, always leave 1 undead slave standing. THis is due to the fact that if the room is cleared and all of the slves have been killed a lvl 55 enchanter mob called Xilniov spawns. A MR of 110+ is needed to tackle him and whn he stops casting he melees fast. But leave 1 skele up and he won't pop :)

From lvl 49 it is easy to single pull these mobs.

Slaves hits for 17 to 69, double attack now and then and also bash.
Taskmaster Torkazh hits for upto 105, double attacks often and can also bash, he also has a very large melee range.

All loot from these mobs are lore but sell from 7pp to 45pp at the gnomes camp also in Echo.

I went from lvl 49 to 51 in 5 days with this camp, maybe not very fast but sufficient enough for a solo camp.
