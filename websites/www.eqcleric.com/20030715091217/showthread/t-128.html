Jaryn
We all know how that great pet of ours goes away after the mob is toast. I am sure many of you know this little trick to turn your pet into an Enchanter pet that hangs out with you for a long time.

Cast your pet on Mob_01.
Have Enchanter cast Visions of Granduer on your pet.

Mob dies, pet stays.

You will have no control over the pet, it acts like an Enchanter pet. Will only attack when you get hit.

This was working last week but not sure since the last patch as I have not tried it.

Just thought I would post that for those that didn't know this little trick.
