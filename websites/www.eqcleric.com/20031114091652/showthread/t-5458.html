Aanya
I played it, so did Alcanian.

Basically there are 5 camps with an Adventure Recruiter (will give your party their quest), Raid Recruiters (for raids - only 36), Teleport Mage (will teleport you between Adventure Camps once you've done a quest - which I didn't do, so no info), Adventure Merchant (the one you can buy stuff from for adventure points), and a Lore Master (stories).

The camps are in:

North Ro (between the tunnel and Freeport) - this goes to the Takish Ruins which you get to by zoning into a sand pit (shades of Return of the Jedi). Very cool new MOB graphics (not only here!)

East Commons (haven't been to this one, so don't know where zone in is - BUT, your adventure screen will tell you which zone, and once you reach the zone, the direction is handily marked on your compass, so you can't miss it.) I think this is Orcs, it's called Rukujarn Hills or something).

South Ro - Deepest Guk, Frogs, of course. Very cool dungeon graphics. Very.

Butcher Block - Mistmore Catacombs. Very goth. UNDEAD!

Everfrost - Miragul's Lair (I didn't do this one either.)

There seem to be three types of adventures:

Kill Bob
Rescue Bob (lead Bob out of dungeon)
Find X number of objects

You have 90 minutes to complete the adventures.

You can choose the difficulty of your adventure. Adventure groups must have 4 - 6 people. No one can join an adventure after it's been accepted, you have to be grouped when the leader clicks "accept".

Oh my the traps... ok, you'll have to find out about them yourselves. I will mention one, however. It spewed beer. Yes, dwarven heaven. If you have little to no alcohol tolerance (as my betabuffed cleric did) you're going to be messed up for a considerable time.

I enjoyed it and am looking forward to planning "play dates" with friends :)
