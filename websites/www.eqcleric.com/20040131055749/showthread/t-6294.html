Ayreon
Pros:

The new nukes are awesome. Sun Vortex particularly (mage spell) looks like a small nuclear cloud. Our Judgement line now consists of blue-white lightning arcs (very cool).

They seem to use less resources. I was able to keep spell effects full on during a Xanamech and Behemoth raid last night.

Some of the effects seem to match the spell now and make more sense (poisons are puffy green clouds, even the new druid dots have been revamped and look like a swam of nasty insects)

Bard notes flying around are pretty cool and not spammy or annoying in the least finally.

Cons:

The bard damage song (Rizlona/Call of the xxxx) are terrible. They now pulse a noise along with the spam. So now you hear the old "conjuring" noise constantly. It's terribly annoying!! I had to play with the sound turning down.

Some of the spells' sounds are mixed up. Casting BoR sounds like a nuke now. I swear the sounds changed.. I first casted BoR and it sounded like a normal buff, and later I noticed it make the fire nuike noise.

The healing spells blind the target, or so I have heard from the tanks. It's disorienting.

A lot of the old sounds are no longer the same spells. When rooting, I heard the druid dot sound (sounds like a bubbling froth). 4.5 years of recognizing sounds and spell effects down the drain. I no longer know what's being cast.

Stuns (some of them apparently) appear as a large blue-white column, much like one of the level 58+ pally spells (not sure of the name). That's just silly. The red blotches, to me, looked like what a stun should have been.


Overall, I like the effects, but they're broken in some cases.. sounds don't make sense and the bard song is so terribly annoying, our normal bard buddy is ready to take a break until they're removed.
