Galv
A couple of suggetions:

1) Make sure your LFG tag is on. Because of PoP, it is common to be asked to group in a zone completely different from your own.

2) Create your own group. Do a /who lfg to determine how many, if any, people are looking for a group in the zone in which you are hoping to get some experience. Then, do a /who all lfg level # level # (e.g., /who all lfg 35 40). Or, if you need a specific class, do a /who all lfg class level # level # (e.g., /who all lfg shaman 35 40). Of course you can always use the new loy grouping tool. :D
