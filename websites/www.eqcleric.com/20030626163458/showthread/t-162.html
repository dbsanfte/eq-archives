Well here my first story enjoy..I know that something may not be VI's version of the story but I think I did pretty well. Chapter 1: The Start.The Young Dwarven Cleric heard clamoring in the streets.A Young High Elf Mage was shouting about a ghost that was of Dwarven shape.Jalad being a bit for friendly with outsiders decided to approach her to see what the matter was."Hello there Miss.Now what seems to be the trouble?And What is your nae Lady?" She looked at him with hope in her eyes and a fire in her soul."Doriea,I come from Felwithe and I did some research that let me to Unrest...While I was there a Dwarf was attacking everything in sight."Jalad lead her down the hall to the Cleric's Guild to have a chat with Priestess Ghalea about it.The Priestess was always willing to help anyone and had a great deal of information.However no one ever asked except Jalad,and he was well...odd for a dwarf."So Jalad are you back again..and I see you brought company." She looked at Doriea with a mother's eye."Now dearie what seems to be the trouble."Doriea told her the story of the dwarf and Unrest.The Priestess stood and walked to her bookshelf and pulled out a dusty book with the Words "Exiled" On the front.Being a Exile was the most cruel punishment the Dwarves have for a crime.Not ever being able to feel the soil and earth near you.Every dwarf on Norrath would shudder at the thought.Death being more preferable.Slowly she opene the book and read aloud."Torklar Battlemaster,Exiled for assisting a Necromancer of Gnomish origin.Torklar was found guilty of luring young Dwarves to their deaths while the Necro would transfer the life force from the victim to himself thus keeping him alive.Once known as a Powerful Warrior who was almost considered for the Storm Guard Ranks.He was a brewer by trade and could make just about any drink known in the world.BEWARE of this Dwarf.His Hammer is Legendary and could split a ogre's head in two with one blow.Report filed by Loremaster Reghar."Jalad looked at Doriea.She looked at him."I'll help you." he said. Chapter 2:The Journey
Opening his pack Jalad put some gems for his spells,his Armor he had made in the Temple of Solulek Ro,and the usual thing one takes on a quest.Closing the packs and grabing his hammer.He made his way to the inn to pick up his charge Doriea.He thought she was lovely..(For a High Elf) And that he couldn't bear to see her in pain.When he arrived at the Inn he found a message from her saying she also would be back but left a friend to wait for him to arrive.The Innkeep looked strangely at him and said"It's in there" "Okaaayyy"Jalad replied.And stepped into the common room.There in the center stood a Earth Golem..Mistress Doriea has commanded me to bring yo to her at once.With that it grabbed Jalad and started out the door..

Ok On we go then... As Jalad was being carried ou tof Kaladim like a pig in a blanket,he had to stop the guards more then once from attacking.He would smile and say He's with me.Jobobaner was the creature's name and he (for an Earth Golem)was a lo of Dwarven in nature.Jalad decided,Ol' Jobob could be counted on and left it at that.Doriea was waiting at the crossroads for Jalad and Jobob to get back.She was sitting next to the tree stump where she could feel more at ease.Her mind was calm as she was master at meditation.But she could escape the fact of the Ghost's image in her mind.Looming over her like a flyswatter.If Jobob hadn't have been there...she shuttered to think about it."Doriea,We....are.....coming...."Jalad said with being bounced around he sounded like a gnomish machine. He saw her.And she saw him.."Shall we be on our way Mistress Doriea?" Jobob asked."I'm ready if you are,Jalad." Jalad stopped look in the direction of Dagnor's Cauldron and said "To the Defeat of Evil...." And with that and Jalad taking a swig of Ale.They set out for the Estate of Unrest. As our heroes got there through the tight passage of the walls.They felt a drop,then another,and soon a downpour.Karana must be testing his powers again.An Old Norrathian saying goes.And the Morning was no exception.That meant they couldn't see the cliffs ahead and had to rely on the sounds and memories of previous journeys here.Fumbling in the pouring rain.Jalad heard footsteps and High pitched cackles and laughter.Goblins.Jalad reached for the hammer and moved Doriea behind him.Jobob senseing danger joined Jalad at his side.Ready for whatever threatened his Mistress."My Lord...Someone is near... Ack a Dwarf...Only they are so foul to be around." The Tidal Lord agreed."Yessss,Seek him out and bring me his flesh..."..The Goblin sniffed and looked around."Sire....they are near....THEM..." "Well THEY are always about causeing some form of chaos here...The Outlanders will die this day.ATTACK!!!" Jalad heard the call and prepared to fight as he was taught with a weapon in hand and a spell on his lips.The first goblin made it to Jalad without so much as a whisper.Jobob pushed him aside and puched his fist straight into it's skull.The next Jalad was ready for a struck a full blow on it's kneecap allowing Doriea to finish it off with mighty firestrike.The Third and last goblin charged the dwarf.His senses reeling with the sound of battle. The goblin struck Jalad full on his chestplate and knocked him down.Jobob attacked it with no remorse,no guilty and more importantly,no fear.With every stroke Jobob was getting weaker and Doriea knew it.She called him off and attempted to fight it one on one.First incasing it's feet in the rocks.Then as she started her spell to finish the creature off.It broke free and went after her with a rage unlike most goblins.It struck her forehead and as she was falling to the ground she could have sworn she heard the number four.
