Bakanar
Im a dorf cleric, and when I distributed my points I put all the points I could into wisdom, and the rest into stamina. Why stamina? Stamina give you health, and a cleric needs that when he gets aggro from healing a tank. Having just a little bit more stamina can help save your life by having just 12 health left after the mob dies or enough time to get that heal off. As for choice of race, the dwarf is good because you start out with a lot of strength and stamins already so you can easily put those points into wisdom for more mana, and alos because they are short and can do that awsome dorf roll! ;) High elfs are a good choice too because they have the highest starting wisdom, and wisdom means more mana which means more heals, and everybody loves that. Darkelf, they start off with decent wisdom, but are weak, but on the other hand have a cool look to them and are unique.

The cleric class is a fun one to play and glad you are considering it :)

Welcome to the board!!

Restandre
I agree with the inky above me. Pick who you feel comfortable with. Dwarves have good physical stats, elves have better wisdom which is more directly related to your performance. The other races are perfectly valid options as well.

For starting stats, make sure your strength is a little higher than 60...dwarves, humans, and halflings don't really have to consider this. Then split between sta and wis depending on how serious you are about the character. Wisdom comes with time, stamina doesn't quite as much so, but as a caster your mana is more important IMHO. Put your points into wisdom to as much as you anticipate not being able to cap your wisdom. (It's hard for some and easy for others.) Put the rest into stamina.

Hannorel
Welcome to one of the most rewarding AND frustrating classes to play. As a few others have said, you really can't go "wrong" with whatever race you pick. Know your weaknesses and compensate for them.

I have three clerics, 1 high elf, 1 dark elf and 1 erudite. PoP or not, there are still some faction issues to be careful of, depending on whom you choose to worship. Obviously, not every race has an option for deity when it comes to clerics.

Halflings, just based on stats, are a very good race to pick. Believe it or not, you will come to truly appreciate Sneak and Hide. While Invis vs Undead spells are useful, being able to get past regular mobs without worrying about aggro is an incredible boon.

And, I gotta say, the Luclin models for female halflings are pretty durn cute. :D
