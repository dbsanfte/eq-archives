Isig
Yes, even without any dyes in your inventory, you can "check" any color you wish to by simply pressing the Dye key and pretending like you were going to dye the armor.

To be honest, I'm a little disappointed with how it works. It dyes an entire body part. This means that if you have a bracer on your arm that has a "half shell" look (for instance, just belt fasteners on the underneath side) your skin turns the color of the dye too. I guess they just did a good job covering that fact up on Luclin models, but it's really obvious with the new frogs that areas not covered by armor still get tinted. No way around it really. Overall I think it's neat, but holy cow, 50pp per body part? No thanks, I'll take that 350pp and buy a better [insert item here].
