Parklyn
The news from the Testserver has the new spell management system adding lists of often used configuration of spells...your corpse retrieval list, your grouped list, your solo list etc.

I absolutely love this feature and really hope it is one of many that they have been testing that gets put on the liveservers!

"On the test server recently there have been a number of changes to improve aspects of the casting class from a technical standpoint. Two of these relate to memorizing spells.

The first change is that if you right click an empty spell gem it will bring up a selection of spell types such as Heals, Buffs, etc and then appropriate usb-categories leading to a spell. This makes it much easier to quickly find the spell you were looking for in that big book. It even indicates the level of the spell and whether it is group, aoe, enemy targets only or friendly targets only by the color of the spell name.

More exciting though is the addition of spell sets.

Spell sets are a collection of 8 spells you save. You may save a set for particular raid encounters, a set for soloing, a set for travelling etc. To load a spell set you need to have all your slots empty, this makes the system very useful for people who have just died.

To save a spell set right click on the book icon at the bottom of your spell gems once you have the 8 spells you want in your set currently memorized. Then choose Save Set, enter a name and you're done.

To load the set later just right click on the book and choose load set. Note however that each spell still has to load individually, there is no instant 8 spell memorization of any kind.

There is no limit to the number of sets you can create but they are stored locally on your machine so they won't be there if you go to a friends house and log in.
