Dulcinea
Banishment of Shadows is very rarely usable and requires a star ruby to cast. I would skip it for now. Divine Intervention is really a raid spell and isn't reliable until you have maxed the AA Unfailing Divinity. The Ancient spells are rare, unresearchable and no drop. Word of Redemption is pretty useless: mana costly long cast timeand very high aggro. I would skip this one as well, we get a decent group heal at 63, until then use Etheral Elixer or make friends a pally. Marzin's Mark is stupidly rare and will be replaced by Kazad's Mark at level 62. Again, Hammer of Souls is stupidly rare and is replaced by a specteral parchment spell.

I would buy Aego, on my server it's cheap and common. I would buy or research BoA. Of the spells remaining, Etheral Elixer is only one I still use often -- it is a must have, irreplaceable spell. The rest I woudn't worry about. If you every decide to move to a raid oriented guild, Divine Intervention becomes important but it really is a raiding only spell.

Varyx
The problem with a lot of 60 spells is that they're completely replaced shortly by ethereal/spectral parchment spells. If you're short on coin I wouldn't spend money on anything but ethereal elixir and maybe aegolism (depending on the price). Given the fact that 60 is maybe half a level and 61 can go so quick in LDoN/PoP... virtue will become available soon and totally replace aegolism =/

Hammer of Souls is nice depending on your playstyle. However, it's not a huge upgrade over the 58 hammer and at 63 you get Hammer of Damnation (which procs more and finally has improved stats over the other hammers).

So to recap:

1) Ethereal Elixir

And then coin permitting....

2) Aegolism
3) Hammer of Souls

Banish: I don't have it and have not seen it yet.
DI: Raid spell...rare...just got it 3 days ago when some guildies cleared VP...3 dropped and gave me one. We as a raid guild use it every time before and during a named encounter.
Marzin: dont worry about it as Kazad is better and will be asked for anyway.

Good luck with your spells. I only had Boa, Aego, EE for most of the time and picked up a few as I saw them. Save your plat for 62+ spells.
