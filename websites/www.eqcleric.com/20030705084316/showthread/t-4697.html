Nyvn
I started looking for items with +mana after I broke 200 wis, I acually lost a good bit of wisdom but overall gained more mana. The reasoning for this is that you get less mana per point of wisdom thats past 200.

The mana calc for level 60 is

12*(wisdom up to 200) + 6*(wisdom from 201-255) + raw mana = total mana

Lets see how this works in game.

You have 200wisdom(for the sake of the discussion you have an empty ring slot), and have the choice of either;

Platinum Jasper Ring
Slot: FINGERS
AC: 0 WT: 0.1
WIS+6
Class: ALL
Race: ALL

or


Shadel Bandit Ring
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
Slot: FINGERS
AC: 6 WT: 0.5
HP+55 MANA+55 SV DISEASE+10 SV COLD+5
Effect: Summoning Haste II (Unlimited Charges)
Class: ALL
Race: ALL

Platinum Jasper Ring: 6*6=36mana

Shadel Bandit Ring: =55mana

Further more you have a wisdom cap, past which any wisdom you have is wasted. This cap automaticly raises 5 points each level past 60, and aa's can raise it further.

So if you have more wisdom than your cap your not getting any mana for it. For example you have 255 wisdom at level 60, you have a;


Crystal Chitin Shield
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM
AC: +20 Wis: +9 Mana: +40
Weight: 1.0
Classes: Beastlord Ranger Shaman Druid Cleric Paladin Shadowknight Bard Rogue Warrior
Races: All Races
Inventory Slot: Secondary Melee


and are thinking of "upgrading" to a;


Shield of Bane Warding
LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM
AC: +25 Wis: +20 Cha: +8 Magic Resist: +12
Weight: 6.5
Classes: Beastlord Ranger Shaman Druid Cleric Paladin
Races: All Races
Inventory Slot: Secondary Melee


The Sheild of Banewarding has more wisdom, how ever your already at your cap and you'd be wasting at least 11 points of wisdom, and you'd be loosing the 40mana from the Crystal Chitin Sheild; so you'd actually be loosing mana.

Also keep in mind that you get less mana from each point of wisdom at lower levels. This can make +mana items even more valuable than +wisdom at lower levels. This is the reason that you gain mana as you level, even if your gear remains the same. You're not actually gaining more mana from leveling. The calc that you're using to turn wisdom is changing. Giving you more mana for each point of wisdom.

Restandre
If you want to be perfectly accurate, the mana you gain from wisdom is 1/5 your level. So at level 60, that's 12. At level 40, that's 8.
Once you break the soft cap, that figure is halved. You can say it's 1/10 your level if you like. At level 40, you would get 4 mana per wisdom post-200.
If my Magelo is to be believed, every point of wisdom after 255 is worth 5 mana. I added a level of Innate Enlightenment, which raised my cap by 10 and gave me 50 more mana.

So if you want a formula...
(Wisdom * level / 5) - (Wisdom-over-200 * level/10) - (Wisdom-over-255)


I'm not exactly certain how the formula works with stamina, but I know that 1 sta is roughly 3 HP at upper levels.

Ok I found the formula Magelo uses.

Mana from WI at or below 200 WI:

Mana = [ ( 850 * level ) + ( 85 * WI * level ) ] / 425

[(850*65)+(85*200*65)]/425= (55250+1105000)/425=2730

Mana from WI at or above 200 WI:

Mana = [ ( 33 * WI * level ) ] / 425

[33*57*65)]/425= 122265/425=287.68

Mana = Mana from WI at or below 200 WI + Mana from WI at or above 200 WI + Raw Mana from items

2730+287.68+995=4012.68
