Jarvis
There are no "uber" clerics, just people who think they are better than the rest.

True, some of us have put in a lot of time developing our characters, but there's always room for improvement, not by gear and AA points necessarily, but in gameplay, techniques etc.

This site was developed to fill a void in the cleric community. There's always been a breach between the upper level clerics and the lower/newer clerics, and it's our mission to bridge the two, sharing our knowledge with you and at the same time learn from you guys.

We're always in the pursuit of the early days. Trying to recreate the excitement and joy of being completely new to the game, adventuring, making mistakes, being goofy and having a great time while we admire our newly acquired "uber" bronze bp.

I miss those days, and I can never have them back, but I can certainly do what's in my power to help you guys enjoy that time while drawing the experience and assistance from those of us where that time is long passed.

I'm glad you guys enjoy the site. I know it's fun to run it, and it's fun to see it being used, and that's the number one driving force for keeping up with it!

So I should be thanking you guys for making this a website worth working on!
