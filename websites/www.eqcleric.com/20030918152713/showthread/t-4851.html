Hannorel
If you're set on soloing undead, the graveyard in Katta Castellum is doable starting around level 42 or 43. It becomes easier at 45 when you get Banish Undead.

All of the spawns in the graveyard are similar in nature to the wolves in Dawnshroud Peaks. They are "trap" spawns, triggered by you walking over their spawn point.

You will see a message along the lines of something bursts out of the ground to attack (don't remember the exact wording). When you see this, if you start running for the gate in the low wall surrounding the graveyard, you can wait for it to lumber towards you.

Tactics are fairly simple; root, DoT, med. If you're in a hurry, drop some nukes on them, but that obviously risks breaking root. They con from blue to yellow around level 42, and most were blue to me by level 46. The highest level mobs are the skeleton-looking ones...rotting corpses, maybe?

They drop various pieces of glyphed skin, all NO DROP. They are part of a quest for a weapon with "Bane Dmg +12 Vampire" as the final reward. They also drop some plat and minor coin.

The easiest way to get to Katta is by getting a port to Twilight Sea, and then running East.

I hope this is of some help to you.
