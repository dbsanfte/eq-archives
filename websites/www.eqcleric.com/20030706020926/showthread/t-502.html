Phixin
Since we turned this into a nifty new place to hunt thread, Let me tell you about the abandoned zone I have just started playin in. KEDGE KEEP. Underwater zone. What fun. Exp is ok, even with LB mobs. I have been there 3 times now and have yet to see anyone there besides my group. The mobs dont hit overly hard, so I was able to melee some, and low enough hp that my nukes actually did some good. Thing to remember here is to put your back against a wall if you want to cast a spell because if something even blows bubbles your direction you move and get a spell interrupt. Oh, and dont forget, this is a 3 dimensional zone, so when looking for mobs, you must look all around, above you and below as well. Not sure what levels are but there was a LB in the first room to me at LvL 52, he was sorrounded by greenies, but still. Swim down one level and some mobs turned DB. Dungeon bonus applies, but I am not sure what level bonus it is. Had a blast there and highly recommend it.
