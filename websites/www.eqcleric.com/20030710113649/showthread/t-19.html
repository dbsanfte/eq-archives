Pet's been around for a while. It's a level 54 spell and is called Unswerving Hammer of Faith. It's a 100hp hammer. You have to have agro from a mob to make it stay up and it poofs once mob is dead unless you have agro from other mobs. (non-mezzed that is)

You can buff and heal your pet, but with the recent changes to the spell, lowering mana required being one, it's more mana efficient to re-cast when it dies.
