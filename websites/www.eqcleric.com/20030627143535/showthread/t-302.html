Jarvis
There is in fact such an item, but it's not a quest at all. It's a tradeskill item...a pottery item. It's called Faithstone...and there's one for every deity. The dwarf one is called Faithstone of Underfoot.

From EQtraders: Faithstones (cleric) are weight 0.1, and fit primary and secondary only. Both can also be activated from inventory. Cast time is 30.0

I would check the bazaar for a potter selling them, or check www.eqtraders.com for the ingredients and find a potter to do it for you. Keep in mind that these Faithstones are recommended level of 51, and Trivial >250 to make.

I'm sure such an item will set you back 2-5kpp, but merely guessing here. I know Cerrick has one for his deity.
