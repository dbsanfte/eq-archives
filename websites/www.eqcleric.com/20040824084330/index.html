Welcome our Community Correspondent!
Posted by Jarvis 07-05-2004 at 10:11 PM - 1 comment
Congratulations and Welcome to our Cleric Community Correspondent, Hurk!

Some of you might not have known, but on June 29th it was announced over at the EverQuest Live Forums that Hurk would be taking the role as our Community Correspondent.

The role of Community Correspondent entails as per SoE's definition:
quote:
The role of a correspondent will be to help us gather information; unfortunately we may miss posts on each board so these folks will be our extra eyes and bring posts we may have missed to our attention. They will float posts that they feel the community should comment on, and we may also rely on them to start posts to gather information, and to bring posts regarding bugs to our attention. Each correspondent will have access to a correspondent only board to ask questions, have discussions and bring information to our attention.


So jump on over to Hurk's Hello thread and give him your best wishes.
