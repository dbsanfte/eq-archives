Ghidorah
Pacify (and later pacification) is great for pulling. However, there are a few strategies that you can add to be more effective with it.

1) Get a charisma buff. Sometimes your pacify, wot or pacification will critical fail and train you. A higher charisma will help lower this possibility.

2) Learn to use F9 to see mobs hidden around corners. Hit F9 three times and rotate your mouse until you are looking at yourself from inside the room. This is great for LDoNs with those annoying doors. Remember that pacify and pacification do not require line of sight to cast.

3) Pull with a low aggro spell so the tank can easily remove aggro off of you. Strike works fine, as do MoK and MoR. Do not pull with a stun as you will keep aggro much longer.

4) If you overpull and know you need to channel a heal on yourself or die, you can DB, ofcourse. However, I find it pretty effective to wedge myself in a corner and channel right through the hits. When you are in the corner, the mobs cant push you. (Also great for AEing 20-50 greenies /wheee)

Good luck to you. Pulling is a lot of fun. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do.

Sniperwolf
as Ghid said the biggest mistake you can make as a cleric puller is pulling with something that will over-agro the mob and cause your tank problems for pulling it off you. At lvl55 you should already have Mark of Retribution and at 56 you will be able to get Mark of Karn. I strongly suggest using one of those two spells to pull. If you are in Tak or DGuk i would strongly suggest using Mark of Karn, if you are in any of the other three dungeons pulling with Mark of Retribution (imo) is the better choice, since in Dguk and Tak there are FAR more DS's than in the other 3.

Also as Ghid said the pacify line DOES NOT require LoS to cast, use this to your advantage. If you have a rogue have them go ahead (invis of course) and use them to target any mobs you may need to pacify and /assist them to get target on them.

Hope this helps somewhat, good luck on your pulling quests.

Milletoux
A couple good spells to pull with are Mark of Karn (for the 56+ set) or your highest Cancel Magic spell if you are not 56+. Neither of these are high aggro spells because they don't do actual damage, but Mark of Karn, if successful, will wipe out a mob's damage shield. Your highest Cancel Magic if you are not 56+ has a chance of doing the same thing, before your party members start laying on debuffs.

If you pull a lot, you might also check out getting a Green Ceramic Band. It's player made (pottery 222), works from level 20 onward, can be operated out of inventory, and casts a mana-free Strike.

...Mille
