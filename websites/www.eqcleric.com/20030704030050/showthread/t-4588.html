Sadly, most of the *really* easy EE4 items are all ranged, for cleric use. The only ones that seem to differ, are not really in the *easy to obtain* catagory.(Wristband of Judgement, for example, is the only pre-PoP distance focus I know of that works on PoP spells)

There are other items with Druzzil's Distance, but the mask seems to be the single most common drop of them all.
