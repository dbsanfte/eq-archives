Torwin has it right... keep in mind that the first letter of people's names is always capitalized, and other letters (with very rare exceptions where a GM intervened) are all lowercase. That means that any letter that appears like a straight line is a lower-case "L" unless it is the first letter of their name, in which case it's an uppercase "I".

I hear your pain... I find this annoying as well. I use /ttell or reply (note... if you have several conversations going on, hitting "r" for reply, then using Tab and Shift-Tab will allow you to cycle backwards and forwards through the different people who have been sending you tells)

At some point, if they created a name with a bunch of I's and L's where it isn't pretty plain which is which, I assume they did it on purpose to avoid a bunch of /tells, and I leave them alone ;)
