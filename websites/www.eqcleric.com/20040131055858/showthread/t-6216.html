Shadowfrost
quote:
Does anyone know why everyone loves MMC so much?


It's because MMC is the easiest theme. I won the first 50 of them I tried.

In order of difficulty:-

MMC (easiest)
Ruj (easy, lots of large open spaces; concentrations of the casting goblins can be more challenging)
Everfrost (mid-range; concentrations of the casting goblins can be quite tough)
Takish Hiz (harder, monsters more clustered, casters more frequent)
Guk (hardest, monsters sometimes very close together, highest frequency of casters)

I'm pretty sure this will be constant across all of the servers.

Pickup groups tend to like MMC, Tak (for the plate armour and the pet aug), and EF (for the chain armour, the anger proc aug, and the FT augs). I'm pretty sure that someone relying on pickup groups won't get very many adventures in Guk at all.

Restandre
/em has more adventures in Guk than anywhere else.

Mistmoore can be easy, but I personally feel that they're really all about the same. Guk is slightly more strenuous to pull because of the room design and stuff, but the rest are pretty well in line with MMC as far as pulling goes.

The reason people do Mistmoore, aside from the purported claims of it being easy, is that it is the place casters want to go for their top LDoN armor, and because there is a high concentration of undead. It used to be even higher, and I got a group there once with the top two paladins on the server who basically just ran around and killed everything and cleared the whole dungeon with plenty of time left to wait for the timer to reset before we could try again.

Tak, imho, is popular because of the plate armor, and because mages can do CC there. Almost everything there is summoned. It's also very easy to pull once you understand the room designs. I only normally get unintended mobs when they're behind a door.

Everfrost is about as hated on my server as Guk is. Poor rangers and rogues are always pulling teeth and donating organs to go there. I only hate it because the zone hurts my eyes. Hands up if you spent too much time in Velks when you were leveling~

Ruj, while obviously people go there, because there's a high concentration of people with 30+ wins, is the one dungeon I NEVER do - and not because I choose it that way. I just never get groups that want to go there. I have 20 wins in all the other dungeons and I think 6 in EC. My experience with the zone is that the mobs are mostly pretty easy, but there's too many healers, and its really easy to get a train if you're not careful.

Guk is my personal favorite just cause its my private playground. I was once ranked third on the server in Guk wins, am still first on percentage, and I've never been higher than mid-60s on overall wins. Everyone tends to go there once, have a bad experience because it caught them unprepared, and never go back. But it's also the place the type 7 20/20 augs drop, as well as the home of a bunch of useful spells.
