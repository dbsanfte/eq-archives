Galv
Buy a faithstone.
Bind in front of where he spawns.

Gate there before you log to see if he is up. Camp there. When you log on again, you'll be able to check if he is up. Use your faithstone to port back to your home cleric guild. Use the PoK books to get you where you need to be from there.

Rinse and repeat until he pops.

1. If you sit way way way at the end of the little ramp he spawns on you shouldnt get any agro. Note this is where I would bind myself after I've bought a faithstone.

2. Yes he does spawn right under the banner on the lower tier. I do not know the direction.

3. He should respawn after a patch.

4. Yes if there is a giant opposite of that ledge he will agro and bring the whole castle down on you.

5. No, its not always 24. I've heard give or take 3-8 hours

6. Just keep trying. Dont try to camp it. Waste of time and quite dangerous. Invest in a faithstone or make thurg potions. Get there, bind, and use another way out.

7. Your welcome :)
