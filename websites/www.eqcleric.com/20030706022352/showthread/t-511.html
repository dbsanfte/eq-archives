Sarii
Cleric and Paladins come from the same line of spells. Depending on what you want out of your playing time will help you decide more...

Clerics spend ALOT of time medding only to get up, heal, and sit back down. In the later levels ( 56 + ) we get hammers we can summon and Yaulp 5 has mana regen so its possible you can still melee and heal efficiently. I've heard plenty of people say clerics can also solo all the way up to 50+. I have a hard time believing this but people do it. I'll say this for now, it would be very difficult to solo, so as a cleric you are very group dependent.

Paladins (although i've never played one beyond 21) are the melee versions of clerics. They get the same spells (plus more) like the cleric but later in their life. So, its possible to melee, keep yourself and group healed. I would think its alot easier to solo as a paladin as well. As a side note, Pallys DO get the rez line as well so you will be able to rez yourself.

IMO, it sounds like you are leaning toward a paladin more. However, level them both up to 30 or so and see which one you like more.. what do you have to lose?

Good luck :)
