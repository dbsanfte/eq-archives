Galv
Cone of mystics or Paw of Opolla for your primary--yeah you lose the ability to melee, but definately worth the trade off in terms of ac/wis/mana.

Rygor oracle shield or crystal chitin shield for your secondary. It's a huge upgrade that will last a long time.

Acrylic gear for your back and bp. There are two different types: reinforced and studded. Studded has more wisdom, but is generally more expensive.

After you push your wisdom close to its max, start looking for gear with more mana, especially in the ring/earring department. Shadel bandit ring has 55 or 65 mana in it and goes for 2k. Silver hammer earring has similar mana + wisdom and goes for about 3k. And don't forget about the earring of the solstice, which has ft1. This can either be quested or purchased for 4-5k.

I know this equipment is either expensive or difficult to obtain, but by keeping a tight budget, the PP piles up surprisingly quick.
