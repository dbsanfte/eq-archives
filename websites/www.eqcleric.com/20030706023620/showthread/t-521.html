Erronn
Regarding Bergugle, I was one of the lucky ones...only killed a single placeholder. I did the PH solo - didnt' want to tie up someone else for the camp, as you mentioned .... lulled the placeholder and single-pulled him to the wall underwater (north wall in my case) to kill him....my back against the wall to help avoid interruptions. You are far enough away at that wall to avoid heals from his friends.

When Bergugle himself popped, I called down some help from a druid and a paladin. Did the same lull single-pull to the wall, and pally and I chain stunned him while pally and druid wasted him...fairly easy.

You definitely don't want to fight him or his PH in-place at the tower...the others will heal and attack. Also, make sure you are only killing the center spawn (near Deep, the dark elf mob) as this is the only PH. I believe the other one or two in the tower are PH's for the druid epic mob...you don't need them for your pop.

As for EB, I do not have an EB item, but my necro friend gave me a fairly long-lasting Dead-Man-Floating which has EB (lasts about 2-1/2 hours I believe), good for a few PH's.

Good luck to you! (I'm currently needing the Lord Gimblox....camped him a couple hours for a few sittings...I'm too impatient for more than that....oh well, someday).
