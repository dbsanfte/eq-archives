Shadowfrost
The possible specializations are: Evocation; Alteration; Abjuration; Conjuration; Divination.

You can dismiss conjuration and divination out of hand; nobody ever ran oom from summoning hammers, food or drink, and nobody ever ran oom casting flash of light and invis to undead.

Abjuration would reduce the mana cost of your most expensive spells (e.g. Hand of Virtue at 2.5k mana a cast) - but the truth is that most of the time when you're buffing, you have the chance to med up again afterwards. And in any given hour of playing you generally use a lot more mana healing than you do buffing. So that's out.

Evocation would reduce the mana cost of your nukes. But if you made a cleric in order to nuke people, I really suggest switching classes. Nuking is something we do when we have surplus mana - it's not our core role. Evocation's a good one to choose for your Secondary Forte but it's not a good choice as your core specialization.

Alteration reduces the mana cost of your heals - including CH. (Specifically, if you specialize in Alteration as opposed to Evocation, CH will cost you 8% less mana per cast once your specialize skills are maxed out).

I believe that all players of clerics should choose alteration as their specialised skill.
