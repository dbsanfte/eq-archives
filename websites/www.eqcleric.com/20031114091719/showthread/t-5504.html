Keyera - To start the cursed cycle, a guild must kill all the taskmasters in the Ssra basement in a select period of time. (30 minutes I think.) If a guild wants to perpetuate the chance of doing that event while putting off another guild, they can just kill one Taskmaster every 30 minutes, since they don't respawn for 2 hours. When they're good and ready, they can tell that group to stop killing taskmasters because they'll be ready in an hour. Any other guild there by then will probably get frustrated and leave, but you can't really petition it because there's other reasons to kill the taskmasters. (Drops a part for emp key.)

When my guild would raid in Ssra, there was another guild that would do this ALL the time. On a patch day they would get one group to do this while the rest of the guild killed things. When my guild left, they would stop and let them all respawn. Fortunately Cursed isn't a flag/key mob, but emperor is for VT key, and the HoH trials and Coirnav can have similar things done to them.
