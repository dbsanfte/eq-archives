Alcanian
What spell is 20k? You could group in PoV now, with a decent group it wouldn't be a problem. When "hell" levels were removed, so to speak, the evened out the exp needed per level a bit. Like someone said above, you still need the overall same amount but a little more or little less in certain levels. 60 is only 1/2 a level compared to most others so when you die, its the loss of 4 blue, where in 59 you lose like 1.5 blues +/-. Aego single is pretty cheap, BoA is more but seems pretty rare these days. I would recommend shooting for 62 and then doing AA unless you are wanting 65. At 62 you will get single cast virtue, and the only time then you would need to use BoA would be for groups or raids. Everyone in your groups will want virtue though so decide who you are going to give it too (Tank, Chanter, puller(?)). PoV you can make back your dot money. I always did so would buff everyone with virtue, spread out so it wasn't need refreshed all at once. For money, I just left my trader online and sold Halas Pies and Misty Thicket Picnic Baskets while I was building my baking skill to 250 and saved 60k now. Takes a while but is a steady income. Also, just be sure to loot enough to cover dots, though PoD is tough for pp. Rat livers sell pretty good though.
