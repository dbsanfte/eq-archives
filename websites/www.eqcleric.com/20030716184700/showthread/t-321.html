nanashi
Been using one for a few months, and have figured out a few pros and cons.

Pro's
* You med simply by standing still, no sit agro
* You can cast spells without getting up (good if you're lazy)
* You look cool
* Nothing cooler than a flying horse
* Nothing cooler than a flying cleric on a horse

Con's
* You cannot duck or jump to instantly interupt your spells. For those times where you want to interput that CH that ended up not being needed, or if you need to interupt and cast a faster heal, you need to click off the horse and then jump. Takes too much time.
* Sow has no effect on horse movement.
* You don't stop instantly, or start going fast instantly. You slide to a stop, and need to pick up speed when you start moving.
* People will beg from you, thinking you have lots of cash.


There are a few more of each that I have noticed, but can't remember them right now. Hope this helped =)
