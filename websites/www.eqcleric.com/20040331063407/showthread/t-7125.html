Sydian

Shadowfrost
The things which limit you are: No slow, and no snare. Snare is probably the more important of the two, at level 56.

Sebilis is good advice, although the cash flows less freely there than it used to. The only way of ensuring you don't get runners is always to have two mobs in camp, which might prove to be a bit too much of a drain on your mana.

You'll certainly get much better xp and loot if you can get a full group together. If you're still determined to duo, I suppose the best suggestion I can make is the camp known (across different servers) variously as Upper Dogs, Upper Kobolds, or Velks Village situated in Velketor's Laboratory. The kobold paws that drop there sell for decent money and there are also various bazaar-sellable loots. Be aware that if you pull a named, your tank will need decent gear and probably will also need to use a discipline.
