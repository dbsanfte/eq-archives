Varyx
What is your favorite pickup group composition? I start all my groups from scratch so I was curious where everyone stood in their preferences.

Here's mine:

---

Bard - great for pulls, fading memories is insane, songs tend to move things along quicker

Beastlord - slows, decent shaman buffs, SD is sweet, great dps, paragon of spirit

Tank (SK preferred) - i'm partial to the mana/hp proc and agro on the SK, but any good war/sk/pal will do =)

Ranger - track, insane dps, a couple of decent buffs that stack nicely, snare

DPS - no real preference here... wiz/magi/rogue/ranger/bst

Cleric - me!

---

I know a lot of clerics love to pull (myself included), and I've started swapping in another dps in for a bard depending on what I feel like doing in the group. However, the bard has a few abilities that really make them shine. First, fading memories (if they have it) is just ridiculous to avoid bad pulls. Second, the ability to mez let's them run another mob into camp and if the current one is still alive they can pop a mez on their add and then start twisting away. Third, while we can pull with a DS, bards can pull with a debuff or pre-slow (52% at 65!).

A lot of people love to have 2-4 mobs in the camp at once, but with a good bard these adventures are little more than the PC's meat grinding through mobs until success. =)

Of course, I'd always rather sacrifice group composition to add friends, so my groups only aim for the above composition. But the above group is the one I've noticed running through missions in under 45 minutes the most frequently.

Anyway, what are your thoughts?
