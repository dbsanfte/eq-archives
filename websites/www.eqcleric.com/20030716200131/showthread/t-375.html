Erronn
Get over the whole money issue?!?

Not sure what you mean here....takes plat to buy food, water, gems, equipment upgrades (unless you can actually hunt for them), etc. Money is a major part of this game. If you mean the "greed" issue, then yes, I agree. Like in RL, some people are just greedy and don't know when to be cheritable.

I also subscribe to the "fellow clerics get free rezzes". It's a "brotherhood" thing for me. I also rezz fellow guildmates and good friends for free, and of course groupmates. I charge strangers a "free-will donation"..that is, whatever they choose to donate, no set price. I feel that asking for a donation is not greedy...a set price is. I've received anywhere from 5pp to 200p, which is great. A 90% rezz saves higher level people many hours of experience, and is comparable to a multi-hour PL session. It consumes my mana, which takes my time to regain. I think it is reasonable to receive some compensation for it.
