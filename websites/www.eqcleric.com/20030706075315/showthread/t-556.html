Shadowfrost
Lodizal is generally killed for a melee mask quest: the Eyepatch of Plunder, which has a clickable haste buff. That means that he is a very, very heavily camped critter and is generally killed within a few minutes of spawning.

A level 65 shaman could certainly kill Lodizal by himself. In fact, a level 60 shaman can do it. The problem is finding him up.

He is theoretically on an 18 hour +/- 1 hour spawn time, but there's some wierdness about this and apparently he shares a spawn window with Stormfeather; I'm told it can be up to fifty-four hours between pops.

He doesn't spawn at the gnoll camp, but you can see his spawn spot from there. Sitting at the camp, look out to sea across the ice floes. Between one minute and fifty-four hours later, you'll see a great big turtle appear in the water, just to the left of one of the nearby patches of ice.

My advice is to forget about the shaman and just go to Iceclad and find out which melee character is camping Lodizal - because somebody will definitely be camping him - and then come to a deal with that person where you help them kill Lodi, they get the map piece for the Eyepatch of Plunder quest, and you get the section of Lodizal's shell if it drops.

Once you have your Lodizal Shell Shield, I recommend selling it at once and purchasing a Hierophant's Cloak, which is around the same price on my server but has hugely superior stats for a cleric.

Do not trust in AC or resists, young grasshoppah! Seek out instead the elusive hit point and that rarest of delights, the mana point!
