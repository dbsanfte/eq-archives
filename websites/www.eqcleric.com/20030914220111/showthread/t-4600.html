When you installed the SB live value did you disable the sound chip from the motherboard in the BIOS?

Know this too that I have heard of problems with the value edition of that card. An audiology is cheap and works very well with the game.

Years back I had a similar problem and this is what took care of it.

1) disable the onboard sound chip in the BIOS
2) install a new sound card that is 3-d capable. Some of the onboard chips just wont handle the 3-d sound from EQ.
3) deleted EQ and make sure the EQ folder is also deleted from the harddrive so no extra files remain.
4) reinstall EQ and download all of the patches. This new install will look at your new sound card and set itself up for what it thinks the card can handle.
6) if you have problems with the new card you can also try swapping to a different PCI slot. Some motherboards share IRQs of other devices with certain PCI slot.
7) check your IRQs to see if the sound card has its' own or is shared with another device

8) if all else fails try sliding down the sound from 3d to 2d. It may just be that your motherboard and soundcard dont want to work that well together.
