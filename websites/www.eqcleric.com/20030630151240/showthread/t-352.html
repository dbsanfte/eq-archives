the key is DPS. You have to make sure you only take 25 seconds or less to kill each mob. I agree with Jarvis, there's very little room for error, and that stinks. It took me at least 12 tries to get it. We made it to the Executioner about half the time, but never had enough mana left to kill him. One time we even got him down to like 4%, and the server went down (we're on the Test Server so this happens alot).

We finally got it done with a 63 Warrior, 65 Rogue, 63 Wizard, 61 Necro, 58 Enchanter, and me 58 (at the time) Cleric. And I finished with like 50% mana left. It's like hitting a home run in baseball, when you finally do it, it feels so easy, like you didn't even have to try.

one tip, instead of remove curse, I used Word of Restoration. It heals the whole group as well as removes that damn insects spell from the whole group. It's a little mana intesive, but worth it if you use it at the right time.
