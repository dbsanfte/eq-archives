One thing you can do to help minimize those occurences is to include something in your healing macro that asks them to stay in range.

For example, my Celestial Healing macro is: "Celestial healing [[ %t ]] - - stay in range!"

This is my Complete Heal message: "Complete Healing [[ %t ]] - - stay alive and in range for 10 seconds!"

I've found that putting their name in brackets helps to emphasize it and call their attention to it.
