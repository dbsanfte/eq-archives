Hail fellow Clerics!

Just wanted to pat myself on the back after my first CH chain, and a successful level 65 named kill ! First of all, I dinged 56 yesterday (yay, 96% rezz!!!) and of course I now feel like a bigshot :D . Anyway, with my new enlarged ego, I decided to jump into Plane of Storms to get a group. I got invited to a small raid that was forming to take out a level 65 named Giant, Paruek the Strong. There were 3 of us clerics, the other two being 65's (boy, I felt small again). We wiped on the first try, as the puller brought the mob too early, while one cleric was out of the zone...doh! We reformed, added a 4th cleric (who just did spot heals) and us other 3 clerics set up a Complete Heal chain with a 4 second delay, and kept the MA alive the whole fight!

This is a big deal for me, as it was my first CH chain. It was exciting, to say the least, knowing that the raid's success rested much in part to us clerics timing our heals properly. The only confusion we had was that one cleric did not put the /pause in her macro, but the others did. Ack! So we sorted it out and relied on manual pausing instead of the built-in pause. I know there has been great arguing over the pause or no-pause issue, but personally I prefer the manual pause.

Another issue...how do we know if we lose a chain member? Just by noticing that they do not give the cast message? But by then, it's too late, right? Not sure how to deal with that one yet, any pointers is appreciated.

Anyway, for you veterans this is old hat, but for us younger ones just emerging into the raid scene, it's a kick!

Dulcinea
I always try to use alpha order for the rotation because it's easier to keep track of in case of deaths, LD or emergency afks. In any event, watch the rotation being called in the chat window. If something goes wrong, heal first, then figure out how to adjust. If the MA is OOR for your heal toss a fast small, then tell the healer channel OOR. If you see a cleric down and it's their turn in the rotation, take their turn if you can, then tell the channel why you went out of turn.

Gratz on your first rotation. It is fun to be an important link in killing a big mob.

Sherazade
Grats on your first chain!

About the pauses, I did some testing a while back and found out, that not every computer handles pause the same way. /pause 40 seems to be somewhere to be close to 5-6 sec, this would mean /pause 10 equals more or less 1.2-1.5 sec and not 1 sec as many people think. It also depends how much lag there is, how much people are zoning in an out and how much spam in zone is. I tested with 3 computers, all 3 connected to the same router for DSL connection:

1. mine - good one, raid capable
2. BF's - good one, raid capable
3. my old one - crappy, luclin models off, cliping plane down etc. I would never use it on raids, but you never know what computer people are sitting at, unless you are a hardcore raiding guild and ask for firstclass computers.
