Ok, want some funny. And that will give you a flavor of whats over there.

I was on deployment to Oman back in the late 70's. We had been there for a few weeks. We were deployed and living in these huge (like can cover a couple of football fields) tents. Several thousand of us and pretty well packed in.

A group of us decided that since we had to work at the air field we were going to live out there also. We'll one night one of the local creatures came calling. There lives in the region a small bat-earred fox called the Fennic Fox.

The Fennic fox is very very curious and about fearless. We found one wandering around on the flight deck of a C-130 one morning. One other time, we found fox poop on top of the wing of another c-130. (the crew had pitched thier sleeping bags up there and the fox had followed)

Anyway back to the story. We knew there were some nasty spiders on the ground so I took a 4X8 sheet of plywood and stuffed it in the back of a range rover. Then layed out my sleeping bag, and that where I slept.

Well one night, I had this strange dream. In the dream I had a hard time breathing, like something was standing on my chest. Well I woke slowly, and some where between sleep and fully awake I relized there was something standing on my chest. It was dark, moonless night and I couldnt see what it was. And I was scared spitless. What ever it was walked down my chest across my abdomin and stepped on .... Then turned around and walked back up. About the time I was fully awake this thing reached my upper chest. And I opened my eyes to see this fox staring at me. I don't know who was more suprized me or it. But when I moved, it hit the ceiling of the rover and took off straight, smack in to the wind shield. As it comes back like a rocket it hits me in the face just as I sit up. And again smack in to the wind shield. I got my hands on a flashlight just as he hit the ground running. Gut feel he didnt stop until he hit Yemin.
