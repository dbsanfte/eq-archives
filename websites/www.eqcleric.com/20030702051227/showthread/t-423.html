This is the walkthrough for the quest to receive the Pure Blood Spell.
********SPOILER ALERT********

In a brew barrel combine A Blackened Vial, Troll Skull Blood (both found in Crypt of Nadox), and Gulthak Gulf Water (found in Gulf of Gunthak, can also be foraged(?)) This will give you a Black Potion. Combine this and Pure Blood (found on Luggald Initiates in Crypt of Nadox) in a brew barrel. This should give you a Glowing Black Potion. Give this to Grennik Neltrin.

All of these combinations are NOT no-fail so be careful I do not know when they become trivial.
