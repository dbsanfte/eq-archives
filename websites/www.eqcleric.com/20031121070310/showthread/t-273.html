Then it will automatically help its owner, you, by attacking the mob that had just hit you. So I will wade into the second mob (if there is one) and attack it when the primary mob is getting very low on hp's. I get smacked by the mob taking some damage, I root it, and then step back. Now when the first mob dies my pet makes a beeline for the second mob. You can keep this up as long as there are mobs and you have good timing. If there is another, or safer way to keep my hammer pet alive I want to know about it!

I can F-Key to anyone who needs a heal, but that way I can stay at close to 100% mana all the time, and the mobs drop so fast it's crazy. I mean with both your's and the pet's DPS combined you are in effect adding 2 more melee to the fight. And besides, it's so much more fun than just sitting on your ass healing people all the time. This tactic works best with a combo of KEI, P9, F7 (or your own self buff shield), and Symbol. Much better ac/hp/mana regen than KEI and Aego/HoV combo.

Eleena
One quick note. Some chanters use their mez line to stun mob when regular stuns won't work. This will kill our pet, since it won't attack a mezzed mob, and suicides if it has no target.

The other case of poofing is when it doesn't have Line of Sight to target. Consider the pet like a mage's bolt spell. sometimes just a slight bump between you and the mob will cause the hammer to not see it and go poof.

As for making it stick around... it used to be if a chanter hasted it, then as long as the haste buff was on it would not suicide. The bug was fixed and the pet now poofs when its hate list is empty.
