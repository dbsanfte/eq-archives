I've been playing a necro since the game came out, but recently I've been trying to find another class that I would have fun with. My options so far are cleric, rogue, or shadowknight, but I think cleric is at the forefront.

So anyway, I find that I have no idea about clerical equipment. Being an intellegence centered caster, I don't know what's good for wisdom users. So I was hoping that someone could help me out with some items that would be good for a beginning cleric. Dragonkid239
Get him this stuff:
Weapon: Dark Ember/Cone of the Mystics/Paw of Opolla(later lvls)
Offhand: Charred Guardian Shield/ Shield of Ry'gorr Oracles (cheap and +wis)
Range: Griffon eye (might be expensive)
Shoulders: Adamantine Epaulets
Chest: Chestplate of Constant (Sol Ro)
Back: HP/Mana cloak or +wis cloak
Waist: Belt of cenobite
Legs: Solusek Ro armor
Feet: Golden Efreeti boots/ MQ for Journey's
Rings: Wisdom or +saves
Wrists: Sol Ro, Mithril Runed Bracer
Arms: Sol Ro, Nathsar
Gloves: Sol Ro, Coldain skin
Ears: Wisdome/Str or +Saves
Necklace: good wis amulet
Mask: +saves
Helm: Sol Ro/ more expensive substitute

Depending on the deals you find, you could be spending anywhere from 10-25k. If you look for better stuff listed, expect to pay more. I don't know what your economy is like but this stuff will outfit your cleric for quite a while.
Hope it helps! :D
