Well, as I type...this is my last night in my own home for a very long time. Tomorrow night, at 8:45 PM, I am expected to report to the Best Western in downtown Memphis. The next morning I will board a bus for a short trip 2 miles away to MEPS, Military Enlistment Processing Station, for the Memphis area. I will take a short physical to ensure I have not gained/lost excessive weight in the last year (I've gained 25 pounds working out, 6'0" and 175lbs), and to ensure I'm in proper health. A quick swearing in ceremony. Then I will wait...wait...and wait some more until my plane leaves. I will be given a ride to Memphis International, and take a ride to Atlanta. A short layover and a $20 lunch courtesy of Uncle Sam later I will be boarding another plane to South Carolina. From there I will board a bus to Paris Island. Once there, for the next three months I will undergo recriut training to become a Marine!
Y'all have been great, I'll come back....whenever. Pray for me, wish me luck, or be an ass and dont even click/reply this thread...I probably won't be reading this thread anytime soon....sooo...yea.
Goodbye and don't do anything I wouldnt do. I expect to hear of no halfling mercy during my stay of absence.
