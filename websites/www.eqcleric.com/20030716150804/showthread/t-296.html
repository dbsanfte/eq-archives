I have to agree Sullah, and even in places like Plane of Storms when you have a good group you can still hop in there and melee, quick heal and mok, mor.

I've found the key to playing a melee cleric in more dangerous places is always take you role as healer as the number 1 priority. ( no duh right?) But that doesn't mean you have to sit down and flatten the bottom side of your armor. If you assist the tank as soon as possible.. and keep you eyes still on the health bars of your party.. you can get in there early do some useful damage and effecitively keep your group Healed.

I made some hotkeys that help me melee and heal more sucessfully

my assist Key is number 3 hotkey, so the mob is targeted while its still incomming.

my number 2 hotkey.. casts Yaulp 5 and also does Bash. so i can stand in there.. cast this highly important spell (gotta have it goin to be medding and proccing as much as possible) and not have to pay attention to when the spell gem pops for yaulp or when bash is up. I just hit it every few seconds.

After that.. I just keep my eyes on the health bars of my group. The level 61 Heal helps this out alot too.. because it is SO fast and heals a substantial amount. After that mob si slowed.. there is no major healing goin on.. and often buy the time the mob is nearly dead.. time to stop attacking and CH the main tank.

I enjoy my cleric more now than I ever have. Constant mana regen helps but if I get low i just sit out a couple mobs and am ready to dive back in there. My hammer procs way more than the melee characters Crit.. so i know i am doing a reasonable amount of damage standing in there. and the faster tha mob dies.. the less people need healed.
