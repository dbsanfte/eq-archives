Jabbur
Okay, I hope I do not regret bringing this topic up but I am seeking others opinion on whether it is time to look at our incomplete Complete Heal spell.

With top geared tanks hitting 12k and even decently equipped approaching 9k hp, I find it disconcerting to not be able to fill the tank, pardon the pun. I understand that the raid versus xp group heal may need to be addressed as two separate issues. Yes, I do use the whole variety of spells that are available to do my job in keeping folks alive, but it seems that for clerics as a class to remain the master of healing, sooner or later the devs will need to look at CH.



If you are in favor of bringing CH back to a true CH how would you implement it? An AA ability? A level 65 spell? Would you implement a true CH for a slightly (emphasis on slightly) higher mana cost? Of course, being a cleric if it were to be upgraded I believe it should continue to be a cleric only thing.

*** Edit - In light of griping about the number of clerics needed for certain encounters, feel free to also address the issue of allowing CH to be modified by spell haste or perhaps AA ability. ***

Hopefully this post does not draw too many flames but provokes an open discussion of the issue. Thanks.
