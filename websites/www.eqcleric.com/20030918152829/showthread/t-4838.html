I have had good and bad experiences with players of every class, but I would not let that ever stop me from taking someone of a specific class into my group. Sorry for the long post, had to get that off my chest! :)

Okay, then...guess not.

The Ranger is leading, and heads up the bank out of the lava river. Chanter climbs out, Mage climbs out, Cleric climbs out. Me and the Ogre...we can't climb out. In the end, I brought Duhikae on and had him run to Nadox to do 20% rez at zoneline.
