Qyadar
Has a chance to save the target it was casted on from death. When the player dies it will have about a 25% chance (?) to go off and heal 300-400 Hps. Cost 1 pearl to use per cast and the duration is 1 hour.

Divine Intervention is the upgrade that only last 10 minutes but will complete heal on a successful check. With our AA upgrade DI and DP will never fail but will only do partial heals on a unsuccessful check.

As far as its uses go, when say your fighting a mob like Mithaniel Marr that can double for 2000 and procs a nasty 3000ish DD, DI can give you a lil head way toward failure. Its literally a life saver in some situations.

One thing I have noticed about it is when you do "die" and then are healed, it drops you from the hate list of that particualr mob. Sort of a fresh start.

Also Charisma does affect the chance of this spell going off when the player dies.

In a nut shell there ya go.

Karnor
Death pact is a buff such that when the recipient of the spell first gets between 0 and 15% health the buff has a chance to heal about 400 hp. Whether it heals or not the buff wears off at this point. The chance of a heal actually occurring depends on the charisma of the target.

Very roughly the figures I have seen are about 40% chance of a heal with <75 charisma and 80% chance with a 300 charisma. But these figures don’t really have a huge amount of data to back them up.

Essentially given the general uselessness of a mere 400ish hp heal when someone is about to die, you will find it quicker and easier to simply destroy a pearl every time you would cast it. You will find it takes less mana and saves on a spell slot this way, while still providing approximately the same chance of saving a life.

On the other hand the level 60 upgrade, Divine Intervention, is a very powerful spell indeed because if it is successfully triggered it will heal the recipient to full, which can certainly help save someone’s life. Since it only lasts 10 minutes, and uses 2 emeralds the uses of it are generally restricted to raiding.

Even with the AA upgrades (UD3) the spell can still fail completely. The AA upgrades turn some of the failures (not all) into partial successes. A partial success for Divine Intervention heals 1500 hp for every level of UD. I’ve never bothered to test it for death pact, but the thought of a partial success for a 400ish hp heal is pretty funny :)


PS: The spell has no effect on the hate list, so it won’t magically take you off it when it successfully goes off (a good thing since otherwise it wouldn’t be much use for tanks). It may seem that way if takes you from frenzy health to a nice safe health level.
