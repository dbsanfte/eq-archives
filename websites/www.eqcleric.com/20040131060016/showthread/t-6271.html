Just affirming what's been said. No two HoTs or in the same line will stack, same cleric or not. I also believe that if you're the same level and same spell, yours will not overwrite the other cleric's (both level 65's cast supernal elixirs). I've gotten the does not hold message numerous times after failing to cancel my HoT when another cleric cast theirs (same level).

SotW and Paragon can co-exist with both HoTs and CR. I've had all three going before in a rough adventure setting.
