Well, it depends on what you want to improve past your WIS. While mana is a good one, AC should be another factor. Also keep in mind that once you hit 61+, the hard cap raises 5 for each level. Plus, if you do Planar Power AAXP, that raises it another 5 for each level. So, you could conceivably have a WIS hard cap of 305 down the road.

I've been wearing Thunder Etched Gauntlets for I don't know how many levels now, and I actually opted to keep them instead of HoT (Akkirus) Gaunts.

The higher end gear HP and Mana Is usually Hand in hand on an item so I usually look at stats this way.....

Wis (I am maxed so do not look for Wis that much)
Mana/HP
Sta
Resists
Str (I am a HE and DE)
Dex

That is the General order I look for when looking at Stats. Wis with PoP Can get to 355 so Do not completely disregard Good Combinations that put you over your current Wis cap.
