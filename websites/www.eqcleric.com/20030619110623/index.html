Exciting NEW Features!
Posted by Jarvis 06-11-2003 at 05:47 PM - 2 comments
We here at EverQuest Clerics have been eagerly anticipating the changes that went live with todays patch (06/11). Especially of interest is the new spell management changes making the life of a cleric that much easier.

We've snapped a few screenshots of these new features to show just how easy spell-management now is.






In addition to this we also have the new naming feature for NPC's to better identify their role in the game. This is particularly helpful for newbies as well as veteran players. I'm sure we all wouldn't mind saving the time finding that trainer or vendor we need.

Another great feature we were anticipating was the extended spell descriptions. It's great having this feature in-game and a testament to SoE working hard to make EQ a more userfriendly game with many helpful features.

To get the details on a spell you can hold ALT and left-click with your mouse on a spellgem you want information on. Notice the added text you now have.

Spell System is LIVE!
Posted by Jarvis 05-18-2003 at 09:46 AM - 3 comments
The Spell System is now LIVE! Yeah, that's right...it's working! John's (Sepodati) been doing some serious work on the code, and it's only been getting better and better. We decided to open it up for you guys to try so we can get some feedback about functionality and the sorts.



Hope you like it. Of course it's not completely done yet, there are some features and bonus candy we haven't completed yet, but it's not vital to test driving it. The commenting feature is active on all spell details. You have to be a registered member and logged in to post comments, so if you're not registered yet run and do that :)

Also we're not entirely happy with the way the system handles AA spells/abilities, but we're working on a better way to filter it.

Have fun and enjoy!

Welcome back!
Posted by Jarvis 04-16-2003 at 08:31 PM - 2 comments
Brells blessing, the site is back on a new, stable host! Sadly the website went down without warning March 31st due to reasons unrelated to eqcleric.com. It seems hard to find a good webhost these days without paying an arm and a leg. Especially if you're pushing a great deal of traffic, and eqcleric.com is already moving quite a bit.

Now that the site is back on we're going to spend a few days ironing out bugs from moving the website. There's always something not right when you change the entire environment the site lives on. So don't hesitate to send me a PM if you find a problem with the website.

Donators, please read
This site exists today thanks to those who have been generous enough to donate to eqcleric.com. We thank you for that, and we would like to give you a little extra attention. If you're registered with these boards and you have donated to the website, please send me a PM or E-mail with the following information:

  • The PayPal Transaction ID (located in your confirmation email)
  • The E-mail address donation was sent from
  • Your eqcleric.com Forum Username

    That way we can tack your account with a "Site Supporter" graphic that will appear in your profile and on all posts you make on this website. This is a little courtesy we would like to extend to you as a thank you for supporting the website and helping it stay online.

    Now that the site is back online we can start working on features for the website. We hope to have spells, quests and guides available in the near future.

    We're glad to be back!
