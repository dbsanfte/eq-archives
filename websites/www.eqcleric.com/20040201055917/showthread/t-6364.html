I have a bazaar character, not because of how long it takes me to get back and forth from the bazaar, but because I can't spare the inventory space on any of my characters or in their banks for the items I'm only hanging onto to sell. All of my bazaar toon's inventory slots are filled with trader satchels, which are not very convenient to be carrying around, because they're very heavy, and don't have have any weight reduction to them. It's easier to just outfit the trader with the satchels, and transfer any items I want to sell, and then leave the trader parked in the bazaar all night, while I'm asleep.
