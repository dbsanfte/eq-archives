Most tomes for hybrids require lev 50+ however some of War/Mnk/Rog ones begin earlier (in fact the first one is at level 10 - a short burst hp regen ability).

I'm not sure if it's a bug but when you hand the tome to your GM you have to zone before you can create the hotbutton in the combat window.

They haven't fully implemented the new system so we should be seeing more disciplines in a while.
