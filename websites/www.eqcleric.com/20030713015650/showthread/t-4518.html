Rauri
I'd say Faithstone first. The Faithstone can actually allow you to make money faster by binding at your farming location and using the Faithstone to return to your home town to sell. When you're done you Gate back to your farming location and immediately resume collecting items and cash.

Also, you can provide banking services for people in "cash rich" zones by trading plat for gold, silver and copper and a modest fee ;) . Some people will also unload velium and acrylia at excellent prices in zones where they drop...you could quickly bank and Gate back for more, eventually selling your haul in the bazaar for a profit.

And there's also the obvious point: Faithstones are MUCH cheaper than any kind of mount so if you're into instant gratification the Faithstone is clearly for you!
