AanyaLorien
Tailoring Bites. I'm considering a collection for a contract on the person who thought up "heady kiola" - anyone care to contribute a few plat?

I didn't use skins at all to get my tailoring to 158, and it took me two days of serious work.

It means Heady Kiolas and vials of viscous mana, but that still is easier/cheaper to come by than skins.

For recipies see: EQ Traders

I did: Wu's to 88, Crystalline Silk to 131, and Wu's Fighting to 158.

Spider silks, even the crystalline kind, are much cheaper and easier to farm than hides. Heady kiola might cause repetitive stress injury, but it's still cheaper. Viscous mana is a level 12 enchanter spell. If you don't have a chanter friend who is willing to do this for you... it sure doesn't take long to get one to level 12. The Viscous mana is some expense, however - you need a poison vial and a pearl for each cast.

Here is the best walkthrough for the shawl I've ever seen: Coldain Prayer Shawl, EQ Traders
