Very difficult to change patterns of behavior but it can be done. EQ was not created for the casual log on for an hour in the evening type of play that would be considered reasonable to the outside world. It is a huge time sink type of game. Playing "too much" is easy to do!

I believe that once you have backed off from the game and you replace your computer time with what you used to do before EQ (organize photo files, surf internet, post to message boards. etc. or whatever it is.) you will find the craving subsiding I think.

I know I absolutely love the game and have changed how I play like night and day! I have been leveling up another cleric (Triksie is level 63 now) but only doing about 10 hours a week on her. I am tradeskilling another character...so can log on for 30 minutes and still enjoy the game. Granted I do miss out on our guild raids a lot more now, but face it 8 hour days playing EQ are hmmmmm. Well whatever.

I am reading a lot more now. I am spending more time on other hobbies (drawing, web sites, etc.) While family is not an issue to me (girlfriend plays EQ too) I am finding that we are doing things at times when I used to play EQ (like going to the library to study instead of doing it in front of an EQ screen!)

We have all played games we no longer play...just consider EQ another one!
