Shadowfrost
As a very rough guide, when your WIS is over 255, each additional point of WIS is worth approximately 3.5 mana. (The precise formula for wis-mana conversion, as far as we have derived it, is featured in the FAQ.)

Laume Elon
I would say that it is always good to have more mana, currently my mana pool is insanely disappointing (personally) even though at 300+ wis I don't usually have a problem with needing to med. On those long CH cycles or difficult raids, every little bit helps however. Definatly upgrade your wisdom as high as you can with AA and items.
