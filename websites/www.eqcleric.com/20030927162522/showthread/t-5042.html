First of all, welcome to the boards!

Secondly, regarding the CelHeal vc CHeal-
It has been my experience that it really depends on your group and the situation. If your MT's HP bar doesn't drop off a whole lot during the fight, whether due to regen, good AC, a slower, whatever, it might be better to throw the occasional CelHeal on him/her. Aside from the obvious benefit of lower aggro, it allows you to "top off" your MT.

I'll also throw a CelHeal sometimes before a CH, to allow some healing to be happening during those interminable 10 seconds. True, it's not as mana efficient as a CHeal by itself, but a bit safer, IMO.

Remedy is a nice, quick heal, for when your target needs that health RIGHT NOW. Chain casting those is one way, though not the most recommended, to work on your Defense skill, if you catch my drift.

Regarding the avatars-
I have generally made mine from screenshots. I'll touch them up one way or another with Adobe Photo Deluxe(TM). I think Photo Shop offers a lot more toys/tools to mess with, but I'm not sure. You can add an avatar under the "user CP" area of the board; same with a signature. Adding a signature on this board can be done right away; to add an avatar, you will need to accrue 100 pp (board plat) from posting. If you look at the top of the forums when you're looking at the whole board, you'll see a line that tells you how much pp you have, and a link next to it thats says "spend". Click on that, and it will show you the options.

Hope this helped some, and see you around the boards.

I agree that healing methods vary per group - though when I'm in an experience group (unfortunately increasingly rare) celestial heals are my staple heal. Yes, I also use CH on the tank when necessary - and yes, it does draw agro at times.
