EverQuest - Live Update - 08/11/05
----------------------------------

*** Headlines ***
----------------------------------
Gukta is no more. The Trolls have retaken their ancestral home and torn down the remembrances of the Frogloks. The Guktans, suffering more than just defeat, have retreated into the Rathe Mountains. They have built a tent city to accommodate their needs, but there is something obviously wrong. Many of their friends and family were touched by the curse laid upon the battlefield by the Warlord Ykesha. These poor souls have been corrupted. They have been torn from the breast of Mithaniel Marr and grasped by the clutching claws of Innoruuk and turned to evil. Yet they are cousins and brothers to the remaining Guktans and so they live together, trying to make the best of their fate.

*** Items ***
----------------------------------
- Ancient Mist Root Stone, Refracted Dusk Amethyst, Ancient Life Root Stone, and Refracted Dawn Amethyst are now usable in the hands and wrist slots.
- All potions that were recently introduced now have a required level instead of a recommended level.
- Lightning Singed Mantle now has 38 AC.

*** Missions ***
----------------------------------
- Changed the required count for the kill and loot elements on the Dragons of Norrath mission "Diseased Pumas". This change was to fix a discrepancy between the number of items required for task completion compared to the number of kills and items looted that the task said you needed.

-- The EverQuest Team

EQCleric went Boom!
Posted by Jarvis 05-16-2005 at 07:57 AM - 6 comments
As I'm sure many of you noticed EQCleric.com was offline for a few days.

We had an unexpected hardware failure and the last few days have been spent getting the site back to where it was before the server exploded.

I have to give a huge Thank You to Auroralee and Bullard for their hard work in getting the server back online! Without you guys the site would be nothing!

Sadly we probably lost about 18 hours of activity leading up to the crash, but that's a small price to pay considering we are back in action!

We'll be looking at overall site performance and functionality over the next few days to make sure the systems are behaving.

Thank you for your patience and understanding. You've all been greatly missed and I'm glad to have you back!

New Banners, Staff Needed, Future Plans
Posted by Jarvis 03-02-2005 at 04:32 PM - 11 comments
New Banners
I'm sure those of you who are not logged in contributors have noticed the new banners on the site. I understand most of you don't like them, but sadly they are a necessity to offset the fact that people hardly donate to the website anymore...

So far in 2005 we've gotten a total of 2 donations to the site, so please bare with us when we try these new banners to ensure the website stays online.

We will still keep offering site donators a banner free website, so anybody who donates to the site will not see banners anymore as long as they are logged in. We ask for a minimum of $5 if you want to donate to the website.


Future Plans
I understand many of you are not playing EQ that often these days, and trust me, we are working on an EQ2 section which will be complete with guides, spells, quests and of course forums.

The current forums will be split into 2 sections, EQ and EQ2. Before that happens we want to upgrade the software to the latest v3.x out there which requires a re-write of the theme etc.

The other plan is to get the new frontend out and working, but right now we're stuck on the code that is suppose to hide the banners from contributors. We want to carry that over to whatever new software is being used.


New Staff Needed
Currently we are also looking for a few new admins aka Claviger of Lore as well as moderators and content contributors for when the new frontend goes live.

If you are interested in any of these, please contact Jarvis via PM/Email through this forum.


Programmer Needed
In addition to needing content managers and admin we also need a programmer. Someone solid in PHP/MySQL who can help us with a few concepts and ideas. It would be a non-profit job, but naturally available as portfolio showcase.

If you want to help and you can show me some of your previous work, contact Jarvis via PM/Email through this forum.


Give us your input
This website has always made it's changes based on peoples comments and feedback. We are always open for constructive criticism and would like to hear your ideas on how we can make this place a better website for you and your fellow peers.
