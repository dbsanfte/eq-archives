The idea was great. The implementation was completely horrible, though.

Some spells should be rarer than others. The staple buffs and heals should be reasonably common and fairly easy to get; but, all classes also need rare, strange, and powerful magics to obtain, preferably through tracking down rare critters, slaughtering mighty bosses, and completing obscure quests. Sony had a sense of this with their Ancient spells, but those are almost obsolete nowadays.

I'd have preferred the ethereal/spectral parchment system for obtaining Virtue, Hand of Virtue, Symbol of Kazad, Mark of Kazad, the Supernal heals, Condemnation, and Yaulp VI. There should have been two versions of Armor of the Zealot: one that's a short quest aimed at a single group of average level 65's which is tradeable, and the other (with more hp and mana regen) that's a long and challenging quest aimed at three groups of strongly-equipped level 65's with lots of AA - and no drop. The rest of the spells should have been quested in otherwise underused zones; I'd love to have an intelligible reason to visit the Plane of Torment.
