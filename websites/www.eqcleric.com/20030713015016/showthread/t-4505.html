Shadowfrost
You need Mana Preservation III. It will save you, on average, 10% of the mana cost of each of your spells; it's more important than Flowing Thought imo, since it directly increases the number of spells you can cast per minute before running oom.

Fortunately, you can camp a neck slot item with Mana Preservation III. It's called the Crystalline Torque and it drops in the camp known (depending on your server) as Upper Kobolds, Upper Dogs, or Velks Village. There are reasonable items for several classes from that camp, so you shouldn't have too much trouble finding people to help you camp there, as well as reasonable cash drops in the form of Icepaw Kobold Paws which, if sold to a vendor, will more than cover your peridot expenditure. For anyone in your group who needs Velium, the golems at the back of the camp drop blocks of velium fairly frequently too.

You need to be level 60 in order to single-pull Upper Kobolds, unless you have a FD puller. My advice is to bring a couple of level 60+ or a balanced, well-rounded group around level 55 which includes slower and plate tank. Be prepared to spend quite some time there before the Torque drops.
