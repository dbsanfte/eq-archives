Once you've spent 6 AA in General AAs, you receive the title Baron/Baroness. IIRC, once you spend 12 in the next tier (can't think of the group name off the top of my head), you get the next title, and then 24 AA in the 3rd tier gets you the class title (Sage, Sensei, etc).
