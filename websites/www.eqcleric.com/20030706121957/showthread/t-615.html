Now let's take a look at minimum requirements...shall we?


Operating System: Windows 98/2000/ME/XP
Processor: 1GHz or greater
RAM: 512 MB
Video Card: DirectX 9 compatible. Pixel shader and vertex shader compatible hardware with 64 MB of texture memory
Sound Card: DirectSound compatible audio hardware


Honestly I think this will be the biggest problem for players at first which might slow down registrations at first. Speaking from experience we all know minimum requirements for EQ can surely be doubled before you can really enjoy the game in all it's glory.

These are pretty steep, demaning minimum requirements.

Now here's my suggested recommended requirements. Keep in mind this is NOT from SoE, but my own, personal opinion.

Operating System: Windows 98/2000/ME/XP
Processor: 2GHz or greater
RAM: 1 GB or more
Video Card: DirectX 9 compatible. Pixel shader and vertex shader compatible hardware with 128 MB of texture memory or more.
Sound Card: DirectSound compatible audio hardware

So we double most of the hardware requirements to simulate how EQ works today with all expansions. Gee, this will be fun! /dead
