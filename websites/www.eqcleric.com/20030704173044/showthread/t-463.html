AanyaLorien
Ethereal Mist is purple. You can start getting that at 46 in Hate.

Velious armor colors:

Plane of Growth (dropped) is gold.

Thurgadin quest (pieces drop in Kael) is silver. You get the armor by giving a quest dwarf in Thurgadin the appropriate three quest gems and the armor piece.

Skyshrine quest (pieces drop in Halls of Testing in ToV) is a lovely shade of - yeah, blue-purplish. This armor is quested in Skyshrine, give the quest dragon in Skyshrine the appropriate three quest gems and the armor piece.

Kael Quest (pieces drop in West Wing ToV (and rarely in North ToV) is a deep purple. The quest giants are Kromzek (Storm Giants - the ones with no beards). They stand in a big room next to King Tormax. Each class has their own quest giant, you give your piece of armor, along with the appropriate three quest gems and receive your piece.

(More detail on that one cause that's the stuff I used to wear - well I still wear the legs)

For each type of Velious quest armor, ally faction with the quest givers is needed.
