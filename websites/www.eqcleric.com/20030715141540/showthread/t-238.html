Galv
I'm not sure if Luclin was out when you were playing. If not, then obviously that is a huge addition to the game. Luclin provides a new world and (more importantly to an extent) the Nexus, which is a hub for teleporting PCs around the EQ world. Off the top of my head, I do not know all of the new spells that have come out, but a good sight to visit is castersrealm.com (pretty sure that is the correct url), which will give you a low down on all cleric spells, levels 1-65. In addition to Luclin, Planes of Power was just recently released, which I won't even get into.

As to your question: Where should you hunt? Well, there are a million places to go for your level (check out Eqatlas.com) for a listing of them, but since you are "new" to the game again, I'd recommend just going to Dreadlands. No, it isn't challenging. No the xp and loot isn't anything to write home about. BUT, one or two days worth of grouping there will certainly get you back into the groove, so to speak.
