Filadan
Just to clarify, Seb has more than "only a few items worth looting". Several highly-sought after items can be found here, and require less than a group to get. In fact, several high-dollar items can be obtained here solo, so don't be so quick to label the zone.

Juggs is arguably some of THE BEST exp in the game in the right group. Not to mention the fact that many high-dollar spells drop here (RotG still sells for around 10k on Mith Marr).

To get good exp in The Hole, you need to go relatively deep, and it requires a certain group makeup, so it is only an option on some occasions.

KC is horrible exp in almost every camp, with "ok" exp in the rest. Even just 2 people holding a camp is barely tolerable exp.

Kael is actually great exp if you take a solid group and trigger the ring cycle over and over again. Not to mention all the plate MQs you end up getting. I'd have to say that it is only slightly slower exp than Juggs.

The Plane of Growth yields three things.
-- Class-specific armor, various pieces being equivalent or better than some of the Velious Quest armor.
-- On the loot side, you can easily walk away with 10-15 blue diamonds and 10-15k a night in vendor gems.
-- On the exp/arms-legs-bp side of the river, you have your 55+ mobs, and the exp is good.

The fact that the loot side of PoG can be done by 2 characters (druid and monk works well), that is a lot of dough.

Pomaikai
Places I've been grouping since Level 50 for great exp:

Sebilis: (Best loot I've ever had. Tons of Gems and Jewelry)

PoI (Really excellent EXP) Entrance unless you get in a seriously good group. Loot is prett good.

PoN: (Very good EXP) Loot is next to non-existent in the groups I can get at my level.

PoJ: (Exp is pretty decent, especially at Jail) Have fear up for doing Jail and you should be fine. Loot can be very good, but comes in dribs and drabs. A low-mid 50's group can do the hallway with no problem. Jail requires a puller of at least Level 56.

PoD: (I've not had a chance to do PoD yet, but hear it is quite excellent.)

Starting at level 50, do not hesitate to hit the First Tier Planes. Just hit a zone and throw up LFG. Clerics can be hard to find there, and you can get a group quite easily. Want to ensure getting a group? Bring a Shammy or Chanter with you... You'll be everyones new best friend. /smilewink

Dots: Bring lots. Also do NOT hesitate to ask for either cash or loot reimbursement for your Dot use. Same goes for Pearls if casting Death Pact. Folks want and need Symbol, but you don't need to go broke giving it to them. If you're up front about it, you will usually come out ahead.

Good luck fellow Clerics!
