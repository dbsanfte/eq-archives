Hail Clerics!

I have now completed the "ornate sea shell #1" part of the epic quest, and wanted to pass on a little info I learned. Most of you that have done this or have studied this quest know that early in the quest you have to kill A Spirit of Flame, and that there are two different ways to do it. I used option 2, which is to kill Shmendrik and then kill A Spirit of Flame to get the Damaged Goblin Crown....THEN wait for Shmendrik to spawn again to hand Lord Bergugle's Crown to spawn Natasha to hand in the Damaged Goblin Crown (safer since supposedly Natasha will kill Shmendrik and even maybe kill A Spirit of Flame, which will lose your drop).

Anyway, when I did hand-in to Shmendrik, Natasha indeed spawned, but inside the building...and DID NOT aggro Shmendrik, so she did NOT kill him to spawn A Spirit of Flame and kill it which would keep you from getting the drop. Thus, it looks like Option 1 (hand-in to Shmendrik, hand-in to Natasha, kill Shmendrik, kill Spirit) is safe after all. It eliminates having to spawn Shmendrik twice (3hr spawn time).

Just my observation....differed a bit from some walk-thru's I've read.
