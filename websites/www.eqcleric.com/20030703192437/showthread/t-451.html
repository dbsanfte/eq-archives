Galv
Here's a quick ToFS rundown before I go out. /cool

To get the key for the 2nd floor, what looks like an undead Dolv... something (they look like the dogs) will spawn when you kill the PH. Unfortunately, as far as I know, there is know specific PH.

The second floor is a library...

To get the key for the 3rd floor, you need to kill the angry libarian. Again, kill everything until he/she pops... no static ph as far as I know.

The third floor is kind of like an undead barracks or something. Essentially, however, it's a maze.

To get the key for the 4th floor, kill everything (again) and another undead doggie looking thing will pop.

The fourth floor is a flat out undead/shadow men maze.

To get the key for the 5th floor, you have to kill Cara Omica. Cara has a placeholder, however (yeah!). And they are the following: Amonhtepha(sp?) and the maggot infested flesh. If, neither of those two are up (and Cara is not up), then it's time to start killing Ancient Mummies. Those, as far as I know, serve as PH's for the PH's of Cara, and sometimes it will cause Cara to spawn also.

The fifth floor is the wedding floor...

Sigh... like the previous floors (excluding the 4th) there is no PH for the mob with the key. So, just hack away at everything. Eventually, the Enraged Relative will pop (and sometimes, not all the time, he has the helm you are looking for). That get's you to the 6th floor.

And, that's it for my "expertise" on getting keys in ToFS. Perhaps someone else can fill in the blanks from here, or make any necessary corrections.
