Hehe ya I can relate. I was unguilded until level 65, no raids or anything other than pickup groups along the way.

Does your guild use /pause macros or manual count ?

/pause macros: You set up a macro with a /pause in it for CH. When you see your name, you hit the button and the macro makes you start your CH in X seconds.

manual count: You have a CH + Heal macro like in a normal exp group, only with a soandso next attached to the end. When you see your name, you wait X seconds and then start your CH.

Seems a little unclear from your post what your guild uses. If your guild uses /pause macros then they should have helped you set it up, maybe they just assumed you had one. Otherwise if they used manual counting with a heal message they should have explained how the timing involved.

The toughest thing is probably that you didn't have a clue what to ask, and they assumed you were fine. Raids really are very different, instead of 1 cleric keeping 1 group alive it's many clerics mainly keeping your raid main tank alive, and a few healers patch healing as needed. The pressure often is a lot higher as your missed CH could wipe the raid, but that's all part of it. Nothing beats taking down something you were sure the raid was going to die to, but pulled it off anyways.
