Nyvn
MGB buffs taek the same nunmber of components. They require that you have at least the minimum amount of mana recuired by the scroll the cast the spell, but will use 2 times the mana. So BoA will use 4 peridots and you won't be able to cast it if you have below 2k mana, spec checks aside, and will drain all your mana if you have less than 4k mana total.

Myrrha
So if you're buffing 1-2 people with Virtue, buff them first and BoA the rest of the raid later if you don't have HoV, then you only have to med once. :) The MGB timer also starts counting down when you press the MGB button, not after it's used, so you can hit the button when you enter a zone, use MGB an hour later and only have 12 minutes until refresh.
