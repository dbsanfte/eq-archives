Kamiff
Yes the Daiku quest is not part of Planar Progression. It just grants you access to the zone for exp purposes. MB in POI, as others have stated is the requirement for Planar Progression.

The flags do NOT need to be done in order, but if they are done out of order you will need to see the seer in PoK to "unlock memories" with "guided meditation" :p

There are many sources available listing all of the flags needed, including some already posted here. Allakhazam has a good "map" as well" And do not forget all of the pre-flags and such which usually involve hailing and talking with an NPC in PoTranq or PoK.

One of the BEST guides used to help fix your flags if done out of order when you are visitting the seer can be found here:

Seer Information and messages.

Also, one thing many are not aware of: You do NOT need to have all flags for access to Plane of Time. If you are in a guild and do not have all flags done, and they use the "will of the raid" function to get you into a raid in an elemental zone, all you need are the four shields from hailing the NPC that spawns when an elemental god dies, combine them, and enter Time through PoI. Our guild just got Time access on Friday, and we have quite a few people that have Time access but do not have elemental access.

Restandre
There is an order you are supposed to do them in...at least certain ones before others, but if you get out of order, the seer will help you if you say "unlock my memories." The seer is in the neutral section of PoK, in a building near the PoT stone.


To be specific though, you need the 4 EP gods to get time access. PoFire access requires killing Solusek Ro. Solusek Ro requires Rallos Zek and access to the zone. (And you should have the 5 minis as well, but those just get you access to Sol Ro's chamber, which can be virtual-flagged.) Access to Tower of Solusek Ro requires Saryrn, Agnarr, and Mithaniel Marr.

Saryrn requires the Keeper of Sorrows (in Torment,) Terris Thule for zone access (which requires hedge maze in PoN,) and Bertoxxulous. Bert requires you do the High Priest aka Carprin cycle in Crypt of Decay, and you need Grummus from Plane of Disease to get in the zone.

Mithaniel Marr needs HoHb access via the three trials in Halls of Honor. You need to kill Aerin Dar to get into the zone in the first place, and to do AD in Plane of Valor, you have to beat one of the trials in the Plane of Justice.

Agnarr needs a key to enter the area via the towers in the courtyard. Entering those takes another key made in BoT. You enter BoT via Plane of Storms, doing the quest Askr the Lost gives you. PoS access requires a PoJ trial as well.

Killing Rallos Zek the Warlord does not count until you also get flagged for Saryrn, for Mith Marr, and also for Tallon and Vallon Zek, who are both in Tactics with their dad. You enter Tactics by killing the Manaetic Behemoth in Plane of Innovation. RZ flag is the key for entering the three EP zones besides fire, in addition to allowing you to reach Solusek Ro.
