Evanya
My problem was, I was never scared *enough*. I got turned onto EQ by a couple of RL friends at work, who played from Beta. I resisted for about a year, despite their constant ravings, but finally started playing. The graphics just blew me away from day one (yes...the pre-Luclin graphics...hahahaha) and I was constantly running around from zone to zone, just sight-seeing, and taking screen shots. That came to an end when I died in Innothule Swamp one day. After several failed attempts at a corpse recovery, I sent a /gu tell and asked for help. One of my friends was on, with her Level 12 cleric, and she volunteered to help.

"Where did you die, again?" "Innothule Swamp." "Ummmm...I'm not sure where that is. Show me." And off we run, from Nektulos Forest, to EC, Nro, heading South toward Oasis.

Friend: "Wow...you were a long way from home. What level are you up to now?"

Me: "4".

Friend comes to screeching halt..."4?!?!?!?!?!?! What the hell were you doing outside of Nektulos? You shouldn't even be *here*!!!"

Me: "Oh, it's not a problem. I do it all the time"

Friend: "oooookaaaaaay...but I've never been down this way...I'm not sure I like this." And on we continue, to Oasis, where we were both immediately squished by a wandering mob, camped at the zone line. *grin* This was repeated a couple of times, now with *her* corpses piling up alongside of *my* impressive piles. She finally got pissed and logged on her mighty Level 22 Wizard, and we blasted our way through Oasis and South Ro and into Innothule, where we finally found my corpse under a couple of feet of water. I thanked her profusely, and she warned me not to go to zones over my level any more.

A couple of days later, similar situation. I'm out sight-seeing again, snapping screen shots of mobs, just before they kill me, after which I run back and get my corpse...good fun for me, until I again get my corpse stuck where I can't retrieve it.

Back to /guild..."Ummmm...anybody available to help me with a corpse run?"

Helpful uber Level 30 Guild Officer: "sure...where's your corpse?"

Me: "Ummmm...I'm not sure what you call it...the zone where you ride the boat."

Entire guild, screaming in unison: "What? You went to OT? I thought Lyraic told you never to go to Oasis again!"

Me: "Hey...I didn't go to Oasis, okay? I took the other boat. The one that takes you to Vellious."

Entire guild: "*Groan* OMFG, you are such a noob."

The entire guild was assembled, to drag my level 4 butt out of Iceclad. Mind you, this was a guild where the Uber-mains were mid-30s. Not *too* many people died on the rescue mission. *grin* (But, *man* I got some great screen shots! hahahaha)

After that, I was given a final warning to stay in Nektulos until I hit 10, or I'd be deguilded. Only took me another month. *chuckle*

Edit: (by the way...this story is about the early days of my main, who is now a level 54wiz, who was created about the same time as Vellious came out. I was rereading this, and suddenly realized how odd it sounded, to hear a level 37 cleric talking about the good-ole-days before Luclin. hehehehe)
