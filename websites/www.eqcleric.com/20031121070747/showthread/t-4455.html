Parklyn
Hotkeys are definitely your friend in situations such as this, if you are in it hot and heavy. The automatic casting of the hotkey will negate any misclicks, reaction times, etc. that you might have by manually typing or mouse clicking.

Hunting with monks is a touchy thing anyway. Most know their advantage for bad pull situations and would not blame a cleric like this case unless they are just assholes.

Galv
And, if you want to really be safe, when you get the second version of it, Divine Barrier, load that up too. I'm one of those types of people who have both of them setup as I've been in more groups than one where I've needed to fire off my DA's either within the same pull or within 1-2 pulls of each other.

Also, you might want to add text to the hotkey to the effect of "I am casting Divine Aura/Barrier on myself and will not be able to heal for 18 seconds, etc." Two reasons for this: 1) it makes the group aware that you are in trouble 2) it makes them aware that you will not be casting for a significant period of time (obviously, however, you can click off the icon earlier than this).

Be aware, however, that many individuals do not know about/understand exactly what DA/DB is, so you might need to explain it to em.
