Here's a hint: If a group is not asking for symbol, don't cast symbol. You're going to have a hard time getting money from them when you are casting unnecessary buffs. In some cases, it is wise to use symbol, but I recommend saying, "I am not comfortable with your hitpoints and am going to be casting symbol. I will need to be reimbursed before split. Is that OK with everyone?".

If they say no, then don't do it. Remind them you need more downtime due to the lower hps (if that is the case). As far as guild raids, they should be providing any components from the guild coffers. Most guilds on MM keep any cash and gem drops from raids, which they then sell for the sole purpose of refunding peridots, pearls, emeralds and coffins. Try suggesting that to your guild on the next raid, and if they disagree, tell them you will not be casting any spells that require components. It is not your responsibility to fund guild raids. If you feel uncomfortable confronting people, then just tell them you could not afford the components and that may give them the hint.
