Gwenevieve
I would start saving up for Koada Dal armor (high elven cultural) I had almost a full set of it till I was in my mid 50's & started upgrading. I
f you canot afford to buy it, you can probably get a guildmate, or friend who is 170 or higher in smithing (must be a HE) to make it for you.
You will need an enchanter, to enchant the mithril blocks (for the imbued armor.. gives you + wis on each piece) You will also need Moonlight temper. Essence of moonlight (can be found in mistmoore of the mobs there.. good place to hunt at your level), as well as Morning dew (foraged in Gfay, & lfay) for the morning dew, you can either have a friend go forage it for you, or what I did, I created a Wood elf ranger, & when I was bored, or didnt' have alot of time to play, sat in gfay, & foraged till I had a stack or two. (gets kinda boring, & takes a while, but worth it) Swirling Mist is also needed for the moonlight temper, I got a few off the steam elementals in steamfont, and also in Najena (also good hunting at your level)
You will also need Imbued Emeralds, & as long as you're a tunare worshipper, you can do the imbues yourself once you get to 29.

Galv
You're fine for your level right now.

While cultural is great, it is very, very expensive. A less expensive route for you is to pick up Acrylia armor, as it has nice AC/Wis/Mana and other stats. It's generally cheap (pieces cost no more than 1-2k each) and available.

Gwenevieve
Not sure about other cultures, but high elves have two "grades" of cultural. the Koada dal is the lower grade (still awesome armor), and the Blesses full Mithril is the higher grade, & about 10 times more expensive. The Acrylia plate pieces, only one of them has any wis on it, & it's only +2 wis. the koada dal Armor (the Imbued kind, tunare only) will give you 57 wis, 48 dex, 18 agi, 31 str, & 60 cha, along with quite a bit of AC. I'm not sure about your server, but on mine, the Koada dal is about the same price, if not sometimes a bit cheaper than the acrylia, and if you have high level friends, they'd probably help you gather the components for the armor.
