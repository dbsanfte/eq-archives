Arlessa
As playing a 65 Ranger most of my time until I got a second puter I found the Ranger epic useful up to a point.

The slow on Earthcaller is always usefull when soloing or you need a mob slowed and you don't have a slower in group, and Swiftwind is good if you don't have another worn haste item.

But once we started getting into higher tier PoP zones and Ssra to VT is when i've found my Rangers epics near useless.
The only time I unbag my epic is for the slow proc or a screenshot. I still see a lot of clerics in our guild with thier epic in their primary, but a lot of them are starting to bag it for PoP, Ssra, VT loot, and has mostly become a tool to use on the endurance fights (HP, Emp, etc.) or for CR recovery.

I still have an SK friend that uses his epic sometimes but he generally bagged it after PoP loot.
I still see monks with epics equiped in their hand slot right up until about elemental planes.
Warriors seem to quickly surpass their epic for High end Velious, PoP drops with high agro procs or hate mods
A lot of shammy's bagged their epic after HP of Ssra and ST loot.
Rog's bagged theirs after a better haste item and a good dagger from NToV and up
Enchanters bagged theirs for better stat weapons after getting Speed of Vallon as a spell
Mages bagged theirs for stat weapons after getting the better pets.
Don't have many druids in guild, but still see a few druid epics up until tier 3 tier 4 planes
Bards seem to use it till they get their hands on some thunderous drums of karana and other PoP loot.
Necro's seem to drop theirs around tier 3 planes too.
I haven't really noticed any wizzards in guild with theirs anymore as i think they go after the same stat loot the mages are.

So basicly I guess every epic has it's usefulness up to a point, clerics just seem to have a higher usefulness as the stats tend to still be good in the higher lvls and the effect is priceless.
