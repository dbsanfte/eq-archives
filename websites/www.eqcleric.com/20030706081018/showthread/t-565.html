Dyschord
At level 54, I wouldn't bother with Temperance. You'll have better spells available to you that , while not all neat and tidy in one buff, will give your groups more hit points.

Do, however, get Pure Blood. Very neat spell, very easy to get.

As for your specialization, yes, you can go to Sol Ro Temple to change it. When you do it, all your specs get reset to 49, so just cast your alteration-based spells till it "levels" up.

Look for Ostrom, in the Temple. He will ask you for 50 gold pieces, and a ruby. When you hail him, he'll start talking about erasing memories, and he'll prompt you along the text.

Rinse and repeat until you get lucky... or, you could just bite the bullet and camp him sporadically over the next handful of weeks.
