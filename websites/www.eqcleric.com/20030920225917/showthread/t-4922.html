Hannorel
I spend most of my time grouping; I try to mix it up between grouping with guildies and outside the guild. While I know I can rely on my guildies and know their competence level, that also limits my opportunities to meet new folk.

We don't do what most of the upper tier guilds would probably consider "raiding", but we do have several nights a week set aside for different events. Different folks from within our guild and allies organize hunts to different zones, usually with a particular goal. The goal may be XP, it may be tradeskill materials or might even be some of the epic camps.

The large raids are fun, because they are a challenge in organization and cooperation.

The standard group hunts are also fun, because you're a lot more flexible in where you can go and what you can do.
