Ldon rant AND rave - Click HERE for Original Thread
norvik
I finally did my first ldon with my cleric. We had 27 cleric(me), 21 cleric, 27 beastlord, 27 rogue, 20 shammy, and a 20 wizard. We took a mistmore collection adventure. First thing I though was from what every body had posted here, the collections seem to be the hardest to complete. Well, I wanted to complete it but not as much as I just wanted to do it. Everybody got ants in their pants and did not want to wait for me but buff the rogue and beast before we went in. I remembered someone saying to do so and med to full before entering so you could go in buffed and fully medded, oh well. We entered, I buffed and healed as we killed. Almost forgot, the wizard left the group as we entered the adventure. I guess he had to go to bed. The other cleric was on poison/disease removal and undead nuking. After buffing and fighting a bit I was at 35% mana. We seemed to be doing well and my mana was starting to grow so I was not worried. When my mana got to 65% we came to a room with a chest. I saw the rogue standing near it and I knew it was trouble. I started to type that we should leave it for later when the thing went off. It was a strong acid trap. The rogue must not have detected traps enough times or it was not one he could detect. It wiped the party out. We come back in still having 40 min left to finish the adventure. We are too low to worry about rez so we just loot our corpses and are about to move when boom, the rogue sets off the same trap. I throw a greater heal on the beast and try to do the same for myself seeing that we are the only 2 alive at the time. He lived but my heal did not make it in time. I figure what the heck, I have made plenty of mistakes before so hopefully we all learned from this one. We all get back into the adventure. We all loot up our corpses and move on. The Rogue makes a joke, "atleast we are all full mana now, heh". The other cleric says "Actually I am at 50% now". I look up at mine and I am at 45%. Must be all the wis and mana gear. So we lost time, exp, and mana. I check the timer and we have 28 minutes and have collected 15 of the 30 we need to complete the adventure. We decide we can do this and we press on.

I got to admit, it was all very cool. The graphics are nice on the mobs as well as some of the traps. Anyways, we kept killing and collecting. We had 29 of the 30 to go and still had 12 minutes on the counter. We were getting into some tough fites and mana was becoming a concern. I had not had the time to med any up really with the way we were mowing them down. I was at 25% mana and the other cleric was at 10. The rogue wanted to push for the last seal we needed to collect so the beast pulled. When I saw 4 mobs coming all I could do was smile. I knew I was going to die again. I threw a heal on the beast and rooted one of the mobs that was one him. That combo drew one to me so I could not med any further. Well I know I am no paladin but I can take a punch. I take my armor seriously and right now I wear Imbued Koada'Dal Mithril in nearly every place it is made. My resistances are no good but I have fantastic stats and ac. I tank one and beat him to a pulp with my mace. I throw one more heal on the beast just to see him say run and he does. Root breaks on the one, he comes to me as do the other 3. I knew I was dead so I just meleed them till I died. While at my bind point I got a message that we failed the mission. I wanted to see if we could get partial points for getting the last seal so I headed back. One note, get a loc on the adventure zone in if you are not going to bind there. Once it is a success or failure you can still re-enter for a short period of time. But the blue line is no longer on your compass.

I was the first to get back. I zoned in and found the beast corpse as well as the other clerics corpse at a new place. They ran right into a weak poison trap. It did about 400hp to me while I was rezzing them to get them into the dungeon so we could get the last seal. I could not find the rogue for quite some time. The shammy disbanded and logged off. The beast looted his body, disbanded and logged off. This left only the other cleric and I in the adventure. The rogue was lost in fay somewhere. We found his corpse and as i was targeting his corpse for the rez we got booted out of the adventure. I rezzed him outside.

I died 3 times and did not rez myself at all. I still made 12% experience over all and about 30 plat. I would have made a lot more if I had stayed at paw but I am glad I did it. I had fun and that is what playing a game is all about.

My rant is we should have had a chanter/bard for crowd control or maybe another tank to keep the aggro. And oh yeah...silly rogue.

My rave is it was very fun to do. Well done Sony. And a big thanks to all of my group members. Especially the other cleric. He did well.
