Nexes
With the advent of PoP and Luclin faction issues really aren't that much of an issue anymore. Mostly faction just comes into play when you want to buy/sell off of vendors, and quests. The major factions in the game currently are giant/dwarf/dragon in velious. It all depends on what armor you want to quest (if any). At lvl 27 you really don't hunt much of anything that hits these factions, so no worries there. OT seems to be a great place to find a group, as does FM and Lake of Ill Omen at the sarnak fort. Unrest would be a great solo spot for ya being a cleric, lots of undead there.

Faction can always be fixed later on through quests and killing greens, I personally don't worry about faction much, heck, I've killed Tunare a few times.. she denied me healing spells for weeks! hahha

restandre
My druid killed a lot of bards in the early levels. It got to be annoying being attacked by the mail bards in every single city.

The treants in the plains of KARANA have nothing to do with the mobs in TUNARE'S domain. Only people that intend on questing, and HIE clerics that want to use their faithstone safely should be concerned with PoG faction; treants will hurt you at druid rings and in Surefall and Jaggedpine.
Don't kill Sarnaks if you ever intend on doing the quest from Chardok. Probably not, but tossing it out there.
Pick a faction on Velious and stick with it. Until you can get into ToV, Coldain is your best pick, and lets you do the shawl quests.

There are very few factions you can't fix. Sometimes it's very painful to do so. (Thank you for the bone chips, thank you for the bone chips...) But it generally can be done.
