Impudent
As magelo is blocked from work, I cannot see your profile.

However, I can suggest a rough guideline, good for most all clerics, but may vary depending on your finances and play style.

Prior to lvl 27-30'ish, you want to consider +mana item instead of +wis items. You'll get more mana from it.

At 27-30'ish, you want to start concentrating on +wis, until you reach about 200'ish wisdom. At that point, while adding more +wis, you ALSO want to start adding +wisdom.

Prior to lvl 50 or so, more than likely, you will be sitting on your duff the majority of the time. Although the rare group that affords you the opportunity to melee while retaining mana for heals+ may be hard to come by, don't expect them. Expect to have to sit and med, and who knows, maybe you'll be pleasantly surprised.
