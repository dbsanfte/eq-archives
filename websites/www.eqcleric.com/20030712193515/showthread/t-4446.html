"Well I have to say - this is one AWESOME spell. A cleric friend of mine just got it and we have been playing with it to see what it does and how it works.

After fiddling around with it, it appears to do this:

Wherever the Cleric is standing when he casts the spell an invisible "sentry" is placed. Then whenever anything crosses within its radius,which appears to be 40ish feet, the caster is told that "Such-n-such" has triggered your sentry.

The cleric does NOT have to stay within that radius in order to get the triggered messages. Although, we didn't test yet just how far away he could get, we did get up to about 75 feet from where he had placed the sentry.

The times we tried it, it seemed to last about 5 minutes, and a level 9 Cleric was casting it.

I should add too that the messages for it don't seem to be working. And when the spell is cast, there is no visual effect with it. You can't see sparks from the hands, he didn't pull his arms back like casting, or anything. In fact, we weren't sure if it had gone off at all or when it faded other than we were constantly running in and out to test it. And it doesn't put any sort of icon in the caster's effects window. So be forwarned, it could wear off without you realizing it has done so.

Also, even though the above description says that it can be cast on others, it didn't seem to work when he tried to cast it on me. It would tell him "your target is to far away" even when he was standing literally on top of me at one point.

Even so, the possible uses for this are endless, and I am totally excited about our cleric having it!
