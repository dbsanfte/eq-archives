Falelar
I solo'd my cleric from 1 to 54, and occasionally to 60. Some good camps are:

Cynthia around lvl 30. (the gypsy in Rathe Mtns near Lake Rathe zone) Just nuke and stun and shes pretty easy, but she has nasty nukes.

Spectres in Feerott. EXCELLENT exp at around 39 i think. I hunted there even when Roror was tweaking out and killing people. I died buy him alot, but the xp was still too good to give up the spot. It takes a bit to learn the aggro managing in the cave. Just root and undead nuke and these pansies drop like flies.

Cyndreela at Plane of Fear portal. Hardest part about her is breaking her with her pet. Great exp as soon as you can kill her (50 if you're taleneted). To break, just fear her pet and root/nuke/stun her, the pet wont aggro the spectres amazingly, but i believe she will. She packs a hard punch, so dont let her get near you, and DONT jump in the fear portal! :)

After that, spirocs in TD are decent at around 50.
