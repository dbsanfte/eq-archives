AanyaLorien
SCM3 I believe to be the single most beneficial AA there is, at least for a caster's mana pool. I know the feeling is shared by most, if not all, casters of my acquaintence. Yeah, it takes a total of 12 points, but they are well spent points.

After that... I think it just depends what you are doing with your character. I went for MGB, as it was far easier on large (50 - 70 people) raids to have to use 8 peridots and a half bar of mana to Aego the raid than simply doing it by the group - or *shudder* as was the case before anyone got Hand of Virtue, individually.

I went for reinforcement then, Aego that lasts 4 1/2 hours is far superior in my mind than Aego that lasts for 3 hours - I got so buffed out in the days before Aego, casting three buffs on everyone for 60 levels.

Then I chose healing gift, as at the time PoP was on the distant horizon and I had no idea that the PoP abilities would so outshine the class abilities as to make class - well, worthless, IMO - I won't be an Exarch until that point in time that I feel I have 7 points just to toss away.

In PoP, I'm working on adding to my wisdom pool, 3 points at a time /rolleyes - while also adding other really useful abilities:

Radient Cure: I have this at level 3; it is amazing. It cures the group. It cures virtually everything. Curses from the top Luclin mobs, diseases from the Crypt of Decay; it even cures Malo.

Divine Arbitration: Wow. No time for a CH to hit? Mash that hotkey - unbelievable. The dying one's life goes up, everyone else goes down - toss on an EE and get back to healing the tank. Game altering ability.

Unfailing Divinity: Increase the percentage that your death-pact type spells work. You say "death pact is a waste of a pearl," and I agree completely. Divine Intervention, however, I've seen save the same tank's life twice in one encounter. Our MTs even get charisma buffs to help their chances. At a cost of two emeralds per cast, and a duration of 10 minutes, it is well worth the chance to not have to switch MTs mid-fight in some of those endurance tests that Verant has given us.

That's just what's in my immediate game-plan. So many abilities... so little actual experience time :p
