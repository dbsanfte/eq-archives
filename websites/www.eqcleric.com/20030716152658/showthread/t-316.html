I started and completed mine at level 54. I did it during my winter break. However, I had already had the singed scroll (a major piece) from a previous guild raid.

Some parts of the epic, however, you might want to start earlier--for example, the Lord Grimblox in Sol A (???). Don't do it at level 54, do it at a level when you get xp from camping the sucker. In addition, the aqua gobs at Lord B conned light blue to me, so you might want to do him a little earlier too so that you can get some xp. The other components are just delivery service quests, with a big mob thrown in here and there.
