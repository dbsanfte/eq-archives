I am a huge fan of Celestial Regeneration. I first picked it up in Luclin, then expanded it PoP.

Some info~

It's an insta-cast. It's manaless. It takes 72 minutes to refresh. I *believe* it overrides mana-using HoT's, though I cannot say that with 100 percent certainty.

The base "spell" heals for 500 per tick, for 4 ticks.

Yes, Spell Casting Reinforcement, Mastery, and Item focus for Extended Buffs do affect it. (So you can get about 3 extra ticks on it, on average, though I have seen it go 4 extra ticks)

Celestial Renewal adds to the actual heal amount, while still keeping the base amount of ticks it lasts.

Level 1 of Celestial Renewal heals for 650 per tick.

Level 2 of Celestial Renewal heals for 800 per tick.

Usage~

This has a multitude of uses! I originally purchased it back when mod rod weren't 1 use per 5 minutes, for a fast mana-free heal on myself in CH chains, or other clerics in the chain.

Another similar use is to have a fast patch if dot'ed, till you can get a cure. (Some AE"s are evil)

Yet another use, is making dangerous runs through nasty zones. Keep yourself targetted, then when you get close to a zone or if things look like it could be nasty, just Celestial yourself, hit target DA (both instacasts), and book it to zone!

With PoP, I use it primarily on kiters for our guild's pulls. Seems to draw no aggro, so I feel safe in popping it off on a kitter who gets close enough, thereby allowing them, and us, a little more time.

I also use it to save my ass, if I'm being beat to crap, before I DA myself on raids, so I don't get frenzy aggro when DA wears off.

It's also nice for when you have 0 mana, after a ress, into a potentially bad area on a raid.

Also, nice for when you again, have no mana for a heal, but a group/raid member needs a heal.

With AA, Item Focus, and maxxed PoP skill in Celestial Renewal, you have a mana-less insta cast heal that can potentially heal for 6400 hit points.

Hope some of this info helps!;)
