Gericht
SKs and Paladins have similar abilities of Aggro Control and general tanking skills. Both can lock down aggro fairly easily and they each take a blow about the same. SKs have an easier time losing it on themselves (FD), but that's rarelly good when they are MT. With HT, spears, life taps SKs can do more all out damage when needed too over paladins and FD can be handy in pulling. Still, Paldins tend to have an easier time getting a mob off a groupmate or protecting them somehow (i.e. with stuns, loh, root, or a nicely timed heal), they can cut the healing load on the cleric, and they can rez if the worst happens. They also tend to fit with an role playing vision of a cleric better (with all races except dark elf, of course). So, all things considered, I'd probably go with paladin. Though, both knight classes are comparable in abilities.

Warriors are obviously the better tanks for difficult battles, as they are the better defensive tanks. While they lack the powers of snap aggro (as they rightly should to make knights a viable choice for tanks), they still are solid exp group tanks (with slightly different tactics) and the only good choice for particularly difficult battles.
