The Cleric hall in Kaladim has your 34 rez spell. I do not know the name but I can tell you where he is at exactly.

Once you enter the Cleric guild hall go straight back. He is the second to last Dwarf standing on the right hand side of the stage or platform that is in the center of the room.

Another way to look at it is like this. There is a dwarf trainer with a glass eye on your right hand side after you enter and walk toward the back a bit. The dwarf selling the rez spells is on the right hand side of the trainer with the glass eye.

Enjoy.
