restandre
Regen is the classic choice from before the mod rod nerf. A lot of people think it's pointless now, but I will still support it.

Wisdom is good if you need it. You apparently don't.

Stamina is good for 6 HP each, so 18 total before you can get to archetype. I'll let you decide if that's worth it. Even with advanced stamina 10, that's still only 45 points...50 with ND3.

Dexterity is a stat I like to support. I've heard people say otherwise, but I believe channeling is dependent on your dexterity. It's also nice to see your summoned hammer proc more.

Last option in New Tanaan Craft Mastery, but that's only for tradeskillers. I'm not touching tradeskills with a 10 foot pole.

Someone will probably say get Metabolism. I think it's a waste, but I only ever use summoned food/drink, so it's not really a big deal for me to just make more. Understandable if you want your hero sandwiches to go farther.

I can't survive runs to Ssra naked, so one point in lung capacity is helpful for that reason alone. Otherwise a waste imho.

Those are your options.

Draphen
Phixin,

You must spend 6AA in Generals before Priest type AA. So you can't go from Run3 to SCM. Also SCM will cost 12AA to get. SCM1 - 2AA, SCM2 - 4AA, SCM3 - 6AA.

You need 6 General AA to do Priest AA and you get a Baron title.
You need 12 Priest AA to do Cleric AA and you get a Venerable title.
You need 24 Cleric AA to get a Exarch title.
