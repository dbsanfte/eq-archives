Good luck with that Sniper, but I think it may wind up being a little more complicated than you expect. As I understand the way the tribute system works, the values of the items turned in differ between classes and levels, from some as-yet-undetermined equation based on how useful the donated item is to the donator.

In other words, a Level 50 cleric donating a high-level cleric spell would receive more Tribute Points than a lower-level cleric would for donating that same high-level spell, and that lower-level cleric in turn would receive more than a warrior would (if the warrior received anything at all for an item he couldn't use). And, vice versa, a warrior donating an uber-sword would get a lot more TPoints than would a cleric who turned in the same (useless to him/her) sword.

I hope it works out for you. It'd be useful information to have.
