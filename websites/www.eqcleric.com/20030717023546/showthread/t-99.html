Galv
Well, since we had started a thread on rezzes sometime ago, I felt it appropriate to ask how individual clerics handle casting symbols on people in xp groups.

Here's my spin, although it fluxates. First, I rarely cast the big $$$ symbol (the periodot) because at 10 pp a pop (give or take), it is just too expensive. Usually I will cast a symbol that is one step lower (the jasper) and that saves me considerable expense.

There are exceptions of course. If we are in a particularly nasty zone where we are constantly pulling and the tank is slowly turning into a mana sponge (or ranger:D ) I will cast a big fat symbol on him. Regardless of the symbol used, I always expect to be reimbursed--and I make that fact quite clear--especially if I am casting a periodot symbol. If I find I am not getting adequate cash items and/or donations to help out, then I will loot a little bit more frequently than I usually might.

What about everyone else?

Brevinlokai

leucol
I never carry symbol gems. I keep them in the bank for those times I'm grouped with guildies or other friends, those that I know will ensure I'm compensated for the cost of those gems.

If people in a pickup group start asking for symbol buffs, I tell them that I used up all my gems on the last group I was in and didn't make enough loot to replace them. This strategy works very well. It also indicates, to me anyway, that its usually the cleric alone who is really concerned about symbol buffs. (Obviously, full-scale raiding is a different situation.) The melee types don't seem to care enough to make a special trip to a vendor and spend their own plat on gems for symbol buffs, so why should I?

On rare occasions, I've had a melee-type pass me a gem for a buff after I told my sob story. I wonder how many melee types would make it a point to carry gems if ALL clerics refused to carry gems?

Dramadon
As a caster, I definately appreciate the extra hit points a symbol gives me. It often means the difference between a wiped out party or a safe evac. However, I do understand the costs.

In some zones, it's tough to symbol everyone because the loot drops just blow. Especially on the moon. However there are others out there that a cleric will more than make up their expenses in drops. Especially seb where gem drops are plentiful. If a group can't handle the cleric taking a few extra gems to cover dot costs, well they better get used to soloing or dieing a lot because those people aren't going to get much lovin from the cleric. I agree, there should be compensation, but there's no need to be overly stingy with them if you are going to be compensated for them.

Filadan
I wouldn't say that I "always" buff anyone with symbol. If the situation calls for some additional HPs, I usually buff the puller, the main tank, and the crowd controller (if any). Depending on the quality of the group, I may or may not symbol myself.

As far as compensation, it depends on the situation. If I take it upon myself to symbol (just because I would like a small HP buffer), I'm usually using Pinzarn, and I simply pay for the jasper myself. If I find that I *need* to use symbol, I will be using peridot for Marzin, and will require compensation. When selling vendor food, I instruct the ML to purchase as many peridot as I used that night. They are given to me, and the remaining cash is split between all group members.
