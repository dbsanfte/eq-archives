MSN Messenger 6 Preview - Click HERE for Original Thread
Jarvis
For those of you using MSN Messenger there's an exciting new version coming out in the new future, MSN Messenger 6. This is indeed a nice upgrade over v5.0 with some nifty, new features and long awaited upgrades.
  • Enhanced webcam features
  • Define and use your own emot-icons
  • Customize Messenger's look with new backgrounds
  • Play online games through Messenger
  • Buddy Icon!!! Finally


MSN Messenger 6 - Buddy List
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6buddylist.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif Sporting the same, familiar look there's not much hinting to a new version here.



MSN Messenger 6 - Chat Window
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6chatwindow.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif Things are starting to look good! The chatwindow is refreshed and looking smooth.



MSN Messenger 6 - Custom Backgrounds
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6background.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif One of the new features in MSN Messenger 6 is the option to define your very own background aka light skinning of the chat window. The default one is still very nice IMO.



MSN Messenger 6 - Custom Emot-icons
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6customicons.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif An exciting new feature is the possibility to define your very own custom icons for use in chat.



MSN Messenger 6 - Emot-icons
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6emoticons.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif The emot-icon dropdown sports the same, well known icons as well as some new ones. Can you spot the nerd? A great feature is the inclusion of your custom defined icons at the bottom for quick insertion into the conversation.



MSN Messenger 6 - Buddy Icon
http://www.eqcleric.com/images/msn6/msn6myicon.jpg

http://www.eqcleric.com/images/arrowup1.gif It's finally here! The option to define and use your own custom buddy icon like in AIM. This has probably been the most requested feature in MSN Messenger and Microsoft listened.


So there you have it! A well worth upgrade to look forward to for those of you using MSN Messenger.
