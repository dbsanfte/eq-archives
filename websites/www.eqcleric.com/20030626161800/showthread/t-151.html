Shadowfrost
Never set foot into Paludal Caverns with your first character. It's fine for powerlevelling those alts or whatever, but Paludal Caverns teaches you nothing about how to play EQ.

Theoretically, the best exp for the least risk and the easiest pickup grouping path would be:-

Paludal Caverns
The Overthere
The Dreadlands
Karnors Castle
Velketors Laboratory (spiders)
Plane of Nightmare
Plane of Valor

You could level to 65 and be doing serious numbers of AA in those zones in a few scant weeks of play... but your equipment would be horribly bad, and you'd be seriously lacking in game experience which groups will start to expect that you have.

Get thee to a dungeon, my child! Dungeons are Good. EQ is at its best when you're crawling a dungeon.
