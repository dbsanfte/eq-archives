Did some research on Cancel Magic last night with the help of a friendly high level character. Both Cancel and Nulify magic don't work on other players. Its considered hostile, you need to be in a duel, Or... in a group!

It could also be used in a hostile fashion. Invite, cancel magic, disband.

I'm certainly against casting hostile magic on unsuspecting people. Its just something I found interesting given the excellent job done dealing with PvP problems in general. A hole SOE missed perhaps?

Hannorel
Not sure how many of you folks are aware of this, but there's a safer way to get rid of Tash and Slow spells on your party members.

Cure Disease (and its upgrades) will remove a Slow type spell, and Cure Poison (as well as its upgrades) will remove the Tash line of spells.

Only other time I've used CM on a party member was a ranger that didn't understand the concept of "med breaks".
