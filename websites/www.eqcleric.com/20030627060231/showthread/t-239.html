Aye, patience is the key word. I have every piece of the newb armor but it took me quite some time to get it. The Mature Arbs do spawn near orc hill and they do drop arb amber although that is the most rare drop that they have. The Giant Sylvan bats also spawn near orc hill and rarely drop their furs. The hardest part of this for me was trying to get a sprout twig to drop off of arb sprouts. The armor itself is great for newbie armor or if you're like me and are one broke cleric. Just have patience with it, hang out at orc hill, and the items will eventually drop. :D
