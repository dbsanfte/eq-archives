Braeaelin
I was curious as to what spells are available to clerics with LDoN so I made this list. If I've missed anything or made a mistake, please make a note.

Level 9
Remove Minor Curse
Cleanses your target's soul, removing minor curses.

Level 14
Iony's Lesser Augury
Attempts to sense the presence of a cursed trap on the targeted object.

Iony's Lesser Cleansing
Attempts to destroy any cursed lock present on the targeted object.

Iony's Lesser Exorcism
Attempts to disarm a cursed trap on the targeted object. Consumes a lesser scrying stone when cast.

Level 24
Iony's Augury
Attempts to sense the presence of a cursed trap on the targeted object.

Iony's Cleansing
Attempts to destroy any cursed lock present on the targeted object.

Iony's Exorcism
Attempts to disarm a cursed trap on the targeted object. Consumes a scrying stone when cast.

Remove Lesser Curse
Cleanses your target's soul, removing lesser curses.

Ward of Vie
Places the Ward of Vie on your target, absorbing a portion of incoming melee damage. This spell absorbs a set amount of damage before dissipating.

Level 34
Iony's Greater Augury
Attempts to sense the presence of a cursed trap on the targeted object.

Iony's Greater Cleansing
Attempts to destroy any cursed lock present on the targeted object.

Iony's Greater Exorcism
Attempts to disarm a cursed trap on the targeted object. Consumes a greater scrying stone when cast.

Level 39
Circle of Cooling (Group v1)
Surrounds your group in an aura of protection, warding away fire for %z.

Level 44
Circle of Warmth (Group v1)
Surrounds your group in an aura of protection, warding away cold for %z.

Guard of Vie
Places the Guard of Vie on your target, absorbing a portion of incoming melee damage. This spell absorbs a set amount of damage before dissipating.

Level 49
Blessing of Temperance (Group v1)
Cloaks your group in an aura of temperance, granting them increased hit points and armor class for %z. Consumes two peridots when cast.

Level 54
Protection of Vie
Places the Protection of Vie on your target, absorbing a portion of incoming melee damage. This spell absorbs a set amount of damage before dissipating.

Level 62
Bulwark of Vie
Places the Bulwark of Vie on your target, absorbing a portion of incoming melee damage. This spell absorbs a set amount of damage before dissipating.

Level 64
Aura of Reverence (Group v1)
Bestows your group with a divine blessing, allowing them to cast spells faster for a period of %z. This blessing effects spells up to level 65.

Well, Lucy doesn't have the info in a concrete manner, so the info I have is merely from testing on Beta/Live Servers.

The Vie Line is a Damage Mitigation Rune of sorts. The Highest one we get, mitigates 10 percent damage, but still unclear as to how much damage it will take. It's assumed to be low, though.

The Wizard and Enchanter lines of similar spells do not stack, but absorb more, and I believe it's the wizard version that also deals with spell damage.
