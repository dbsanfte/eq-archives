Well, as a general rule in an exp group I have the following spells up:-

1. Complete Healing
2. Supernal Remedy/Light (varies)
3. Yaulp VI
4. Supernal Elixir
5. Condemnation/Destroy Undead/Buff
6. Stun/Pacification/Buff
7. Petrifying Earth
8. Divine Aura

From my point of view, it's pointless to have Gate memmed, because I can hit Divine Aura, sit down and mem gate, and by the time DA has worn off, Gate is up and ready to cast.

I won't gate unless I'm the last person standing in the group.

That's easy for me to say, because I have spellcasting reinforcement mastery and my DA lasts for 27 seconds. I can't remember for certain sure if you can mem gate under an 18 second DA - but I'm prepared to bet that you can!

I never have gate memmed in an experience group - it seems discourteous and cowardly - but I always have it up while soloing or travelling.
