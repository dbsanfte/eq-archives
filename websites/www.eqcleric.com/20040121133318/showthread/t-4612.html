For screen shots, Print Screen only copies the viewable screen to the clipboard while ALT + Print Screen copies the active window to the clipboard.

Then it's just a matter of pasting the image into a graphics app like Photoshop and saving the image as a jpg or gif to be viewable over a web page. MS Paint actually works as well, but first open it and set Image > Attributes > Width & Height to something small like 2 x 2. When you paste in your screenshot, the canvas area will expand to fit your image. Save it as a jpg or if that option isn't available, save as a bitmap then open & save it through another app that does support jpg (if that's easier).
