Galv
Earrings-- Forest Loops (about 250-300pp each). Dropped from Giants at Frontier Mountains (that's how I had gotten them many, many moons ago)

Neck--I'm thinking Goblin Soul Caller here or Blackened Alloy Medallion for Mana Pres 2 (about 500 - 1000)

Face--Fine, or you can get the +8 wis/str player made one (under 500), but has no AC, which is a big draw back

Rings--those are fine, or you could go with the +6 wis ones (like 250 each I think?)

Bracers--fine

Arms--Would go with Donal's Arms. No stats, a nice little buff effect (not till 45 I think though), but more important is the 20+ AC you'll get. This will allow you to free up other AC slots

Shoulders--Fine

Chest--OK, but invest in the Acrylia one. There are two varients: one with High Wis (like 10 or so) and the other with higher all around stats (anywhere from 1k - 1.5k)

Back--Would definately go with something Acrylia here, or at the very least, higher AC and forget about the extra 1 pt of wis.

Waist--Can't think of anything, but I picked up the belt of cenobite rather cheap (like 500 or so), and it has +7 wisdom I believe. AC is 7 or so I think, but it has 10 vs. cold.

Legs--Gatorscale with +4 wis and decent AC I think (should be about 400 or so)

Feet--How about crystal lined slippers, which have 3 wis I believe and run anywhere from 150-500

Primary--If you are going offensive, go Dark Ember. If you aren't worried about fighting at all, check out cone of mystics with it's +5 wis and +40-50 mana and... AC to boot. Forget the specifics (saw one selling for 300). Or... you could go with the paw of opolla--like 9 wis and a bunch of other stats (seen em as low as 500 but average between 1k and 1.5k)

Secondary--Nice shield. Another good choice is Shield of Rygor (name might be off a little)--good wis/mana/AC or, more expenisive, Crystal Chitin Shield (about 1500-2k these days) with 20+ AC 9wis and 40mana or so

Now, I know you don't have that money to purchase everything, but these are just items to look out for. In addition, perhaps you aren't into the bazaar shopping, which is fine too. YOu can find where all of these drops by doing a search on http://everquest.allakhazam.com/

hammong
I know the stuff is very expensive for some, but the Dwarven Plate items shouldn't be overlooked. The "Enchanted Dwarven Plate" items have +STA and +STR on each piece, with very good AC. The "Dwarven Plate" (note the drop of the word Enchanted) items have the same stats PLUS +WIS on each piece, and are restricted to Brell worshipers. It's hard to beat this cultural player-made armor until you reach level 45 and can use the click-effects of the Donals pieces.

My 26 High-Elf cleric is wearing mostly Koada'Dal Mithril armor, which has +DEX, +CHA, and +WIS on it. Not as nice as having +STR and +STA, but the +CHA is useful to increase effectiveness of my Calm/Pacify lineup.
