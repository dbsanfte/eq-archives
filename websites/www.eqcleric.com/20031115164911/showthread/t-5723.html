Ycarus
As I've been lurking a bit here, I thought I'd make myself known:

Having played my character since May of '99 pretty consistantly, you might be surprised I hit 65 about two months ago. Ycarus is somewhat of a rarity as a halfling cleric. (Not as rare as Erudite or Gnomish clerics, but I digress). He belongs to a guild called the keepers and finds that he adores the raiding experience. (Public system on the server has gotten many, many people into Elementals, Ycarus is only 3 flags from solro+air,water,earth). Ycarus posseses the cleric FA weapon, which just so happens to contain the spirit of an individual from New Jersey named Clarence. He has a horse called Mr. Rodriguez (as in slowpoke) who seems not to like where Ycarus takes him, and makes it quite known.

So, that's a very short synopsis of Ycarus
