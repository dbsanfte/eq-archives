Naeron
Unfortunately, you will never have the pleasure of porting up into Hate invis and running around lost, hoping you're running the same way, to the safe spot. :)

I'd still suggest you go to Hate - it's a fun, enjoyable place. I'm kinda curious what exactly you mean by hints to help you out? Raiding one place or another generally doesn't change too much. The fights are a lot of mobs over and over, and a boss every now and then. But you're not forced to fight fast. Still, you want to make sure you and your group are always buffed, without having to slow down the raid. Heal quickly, but try your best to be efficient.

The most important tip I have for raiding is do your best to always pay attention and stay focused on the raid. If you have to go afk, let your group, raid leader, and fellow healers know - but don't go AFK for a long period of time. :)

Hope that helps!
