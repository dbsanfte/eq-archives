Suzybabe
I successfully completed my first high risk adventure last night:)

It was an adventure to! I got recruited into a group with two level 60's in it which was a first for me as I've just hit 53. As we were waiting to fill the last spot I was asked if I minded doing a high risk as they all wanted too so I said I didn't mind and then the leader told us he had a beastie coming. A few minutes later the leader told us that the beastie didn't want to do a high risk so with a bit of moaning everyone agreed to do a normal. The beastie turns up and we all run to the zone entrance once the adventure is chosen only for the leader to go LD. The beastie then tells everyone to leave the adventure as he's not going in with only 5. Well there's a bit of bitching going on as he was the one who didn't want to do a high risk and then he tells us to leave the adventure as soon as someone goes LD.

Eventually he runs off to find a replacement and comes back with a warrior and while he's doing that we all leave the adventure and replace it with a high risk one! Now I'm pretty easy going but I could tell that there was a lot of friction in the group by now, the beastie wasn't happy we'd got a high risk one but eventually he comes back with a replacement warrior and finally after a lot of agitation we all zone in and start the adventure.

So I'm in my first high risk and I'm a bit wary but everything seems to be going okay apart from the fact that the MT needs at least one CH every fight. We're on collection and we get six of the required 21 without to many problems at all and I'm thinking that this isn't so hard when the level 60 necro decides to go and open a chest! Now I'm not the cleverest girl in the world but even I know thats a dangerous thing to do! So all of a sudden the room is full of fire and everyone is singed but only take about 350 damage. So I think to myself ok that wasn't so bad until I hear the druid scream that his kei has gone and as I look I see mine has gone too! Now I know you hardened players will give me some grief about being able to manage my mana without kei etc etc but we had no chanter with the group and we were only quarter of the way in to a high risk mission with no mind buffs at all and a light weight tank!

Well to cut a long story short we finally made it with 4 minutes to spare and it was hard going. I was never over 40% mana and neither were the other casters,the MT was pull happy and as I said needed a CH every fight with a smaller top up heal aswell. But we got through it and I got 26 points which was very nice:)

At least I've done a high risk one now so won't be to scared about doing another, provided nobody opens a chest!
