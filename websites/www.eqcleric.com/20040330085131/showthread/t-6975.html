No great point to this post but just thought I'd let you all know about yesterday.

It started with two LDON adventures, both of which failed, the first one our very first pull bought a whole room of mobs onto us and there were screams of zone straight away, and I made it out with 1% of health left. I was about to say I'd just got out in time when pain and suffering hit me and I died! Then my corpse disappeared! The rest of the group then spent 30 odd minutes looking for my corpse even though I'd only taken two steps outside the zone entrance before I died. They finally found it but by then we only had 57 minutes of the adventure left and we decided to quit. First adventure I've been on where we quit without killing a single mob :)

Second one we all quit about half way through as two of our group were insulting each other! So I head over to DL and get a tell asking if I wanted to group inside KC. It was my first time in there and even though I died three times I had a lot of fun. Actually had to pacifiy rooms for the first time believe it or not, with about a 35% success rate!

Later on I get a tell asking if I wanted to group in POI. Never been there before so I said yes and joined the group there. Actually had a 60 warrior in our group, first time I've ever grouped with a level 60:) The guy who sent me the tell turned out to be a guild leader and he asked me if I fancied joing their guild so I did and I'm now a proud member of Antaean, probably wont be able to spell it to often though! The POI group was great fun, especially as most of the mobs were red to me which was a bit of a shock after mainly fighting blue mobs!

Finally that group splits and the warrior who I grouped with in KC turned up and asked if I wanted to go to POJ. Never been there before either so off we go. I thought I'd see if any of my new guildies fancied grouping there and two did which was great.

So all in all I had a great time in three zones I'd never been to before and joined a new guild which looks like it could be a good one. It bought back some happy memories of the early days when I was in zones I'd never been to fighting all kinds of wierd and wonderful mobs. I didn't even mind dying, the fun was more important than the xp for once.

As I said no real point to the post but I'm, still feeling happy about it all:)
