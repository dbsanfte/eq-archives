I went on a raid recently and we spawned him over and over.... but I usually have to look at the ground due to lag, so I will answer this as best I can.

There are 3 tiers of mobs. Each tier will keep respawning until their 'bosses' are dead. I can't remember which order they go in, but if you kill the bigger ones, the little ones just respawn as soon as they are dead.

We did a massive pull and had everything mezzed. One cleric was there to keep the enchanter alive incase something broke a mez (which wasn't done - phew!). Then we picked through the mobs we wanted while the rest remained mezzed. *Edit Info* The bigger guys may not be able to be mezzed, it was awhile ago and I just stared at hitpoint bars LOL. But as said above, they don't hit too hard so they can be split tanked easily.

There is the Lord, and then the boss mob left on the island after that. We kill the lord and because his boss mob is still up he repops about 30 seconds later.

The trick is to not pull the boss in the first pull. If he gets aggroed the puller either FD' or dies.

I hope that helps!
