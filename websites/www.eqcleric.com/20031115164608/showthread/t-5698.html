it all depends what your playstyle is really. if you already have a set group of friends with whom you regularly xp, and your guild is happy with your current level/gear, then do whatever makes you happy.

but from my perspective, level is much more important than aa's - especially for clerics. SR and SL are crucial for any planar xp group. not only that, but having hov will save you a ton of money in peridots (unless you have ancient:aego of course).

if/when the time comes that you should choose to pursue joining a more hardcore raiding guild, they will most definately tell you to turn off aa and go to 100%xp until you hit 65. full access to your spellbook is a much bigger asset to a guild than div arb could ever be.

good luck in whatever you decide to do. the important thing is that you have fun.
