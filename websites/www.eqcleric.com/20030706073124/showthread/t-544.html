Dyschord
This site :PoP Flag Progression Order has the best flagging map for PoP I have found.

As for checking what flags you have, if you should somehow forget, go visit the Seer, in Plane of Knowledge. She is in a building, in her own seperate room, near the Plane of Tranquility stone, in Knowledge. (She shares the hut with an Alchemy Vendor)

"Parchments" are what you need to collect, and turn in for your spells, post-level 60.
Ethereal Parchments, are for level 61-62 spells.
Spectral Parchments are for level 63-64 spells.
Glyphed Runes are for level 65 spells, and can only be obtained in specific areas, off specific mobs.

Since you are jumping into PoP late, you may be able to find many of your spells for sale, in the Bazaar, so always keep an eye out in there.

The Cleric turn-in person, for spells, is located in Plane of Knowledge, in the Library, with all of the caster-class spell vendors. Vicar Ceraen is his name, and he sells our only vendor-buyable spell, Supernal Remedy. It will cost you maybe 200 plat average, and you'll want to get that asap.

If you have old Plane of Hate/Plane of Fear armors, rotting in your bank, you can take them to any cleric Guild Master, in Plane of Knowledge, and turn them in for some exp and occasionally gems.

The best thing for a cleric new to PoP is this : Mobs are going to hit hard and fast, in alot of zones. Until you get comfortable with the "lay of the land", you'll want to be on your toes!

Alot of clerics find cash hard to come by, in PoP, and you'll be dishing out regeants left and right. PoP does have cash loot, but it really varies on where you hunt. Mobs do not drop plat.

PoP has alot of storyline to follow, so if your'e into that, go around and chat with the various NPC's you'll find in Plane of Knowledge/Plane of Tranquility. Many are connected to the many flags you'll need/aquire in the days ahead, and the new story window function is great for keeping track of what they tell you.

Hope that helps some!

Dyschord
Simply put, the "solo" flag quests, only allow you access to the zone. It doesn't, however, "flag" you.

Example : Say you have the Halls of Honor solo flag. Your guild decides to do the 3 trials, to get flagged for Halls of Honor B (Mith Marr). You go through the trials with your guild, yet...you cannot zone into HoHB.

Reason? HoH B only recognizes the actual Aerin Dar Flag, to have the trials (in HoHA) flag you properly.

Same thing occurs for the Crypt of Decay solo flag. You cannot access Bertox's Lair without actually having killed Grummus.

Yes, Virtual Flags do get around this, of course, but you'll never be flagged till you do the events in the "main" PoP storyline.

I assume the storyline is why the "flags" were set up the way they are. (Having to save some poor saps soul, and eventually save Zeb)

I'm unsure if they will change this problem in the near future (Very well could be "working as intended"), but as I've personally seen from some of our apps with solo flags, it's still "broken"
