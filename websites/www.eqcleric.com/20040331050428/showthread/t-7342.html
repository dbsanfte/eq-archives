Q. What difference is Virtue vs Protection of Nine+Focus of Seventh + Kazad?

A. The difference is 217hp in favor of Nine/Seven/Kazad for clerics, 123 for everyone else. Do note that Protection of Nine and Kazad got a base duration of 63 minutes compared to Virtues 2h 30 minutes. Exact layout below.
Given:
Blessing of the Nine - 618hp, 32ac, 8mana/tic
Mark of Kazad - 910hp
Focus of the Seventh - 544hp
Ward of Gallantry - 54ac
Virtue - 1405hp, 72ac
Armor of the Zealot - 450hp, 36ac, 8mana/tic

We have the combo's of:
Virtue/Seventh (non-clerics) = 1949hp, 72ac,
Virtue/Zealot (usually clerics) = 1855hp, 108ac, 8mana/tic
Kazad/Nine/Seventh/WoG = 2072hp, 86ac, 8mana/tic

So the change for clerics is +217hp, -22ac
And change for others is +123hp, +14ac, +8mana/tic
