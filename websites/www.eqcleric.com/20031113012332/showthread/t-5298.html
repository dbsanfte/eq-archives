Savyrass
I had such fun last night, I just had to share :)

First let me say that I dont normally go for pick up groups, never have...even on my Ranger, Id rather go to a less exp solo zone, than you join a pickup group.

But last night I took the plunge. Myself and a 48 Enchanter friend of mine, went to Plane of Tranquility and /ooc 48 Cleric and 48 Enchanter looking to join a group.

Well, we got a tell from a Paladin saying that he is making a group for Plane of Disease.
Hmm, thinks I...could be tough, but lets try it...after all the worst that can happen is we all die, hehe.

So we join the group, the pally and shaman are both 55+ and we are soon joined by a wizard and Warrior...also both 55+
Hehe, then there is me and my 48 Enchanter friend :)

We go into PoD and set up to the left side of the courtyard...This is my first time in the Planes (my ranger had been in PoJ once, but thats it).
I am being extremely careful with heals and all is going well. When suddenly the Palladin (he was pulling) calls that he has 3 on him...."Dammit!" I thought, this could be tough and I am spamming HoT trying to get in early - but he is OOR...then suddenly the Palladin calls "No heals, Im just going to die here"...and he does...."WOW!" I thought "well done my man".

I res, we all get set up again and start again. Eventually I get comfortable healing and we have less downtime and all is smooth as silk. These guys are fair with loot (well, hehe, the two partchments that dropped anyway). The pulls are good, each class knows what to do...it was very smooth and really well executed.

I had a blast, both the enchanter and I made over 2 yellows of exp and we met some really nice and talented players.

Eventually (after about 2 and half hours) Enchanter and I had to leave, but replacements were found.
We said our goodbyes and then the tank thanks me in group say for healing well....I puffed out my chest with pride... To know that I at 48 can hold my own in a high zone, with high players, who all knew their stuff.

Well, it was a blast and I wanted to pass it on. I guess it wont mean much to you all. Except maybe, dont be turned off trying something tough, it could be really rewarding.

Pomaikai
PoJ = Ok drops. You can jackpot on gems there at times. Exp so-so until you can get groups in the tough areas.

PoN = Average drops. Lots of dark powers pages from trees there for quest cloak. Exp very good.

PoD = Parchemant drops. That's about it except for some quest drops, but exp is the best.

PoI = Great drops. Not money, but all that vendor junk is tradeskill stuff for Tinkers. You can clean up in the Bazaar with those drops. Also some really good armor upgrades for you level will drop there as well off random yard trash mobs. Sometimes you get pages for quest cloak. Always check the Bazaar before you sell the vendor loot... You may be VERY suprised! :D Exp can be quite good here, especially if you can get into a good fast pulling group. I'm a huge PoI fan.
