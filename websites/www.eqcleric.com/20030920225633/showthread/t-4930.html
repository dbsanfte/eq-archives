Hannorel
There are a few determining factors, in my opinion.

How much cash do you have to equip your new toon? If not much, then go with a High Elf, as they have the highest starting WIS. If, on the other hand, you'd actually like to be able to wear plate armor, go with a Dwarf.

It's been said that SOE loves Tunare...and so it would seem, with all of the Tunare-specific items out there. With all of the dawrven and high elven cultural smiths out there, there shouldn't be a lack of cultural armor available.

So, if you're bound and determined to play either a Dwarf or High Elf, those would seem to be the major game mechanic factors. What can't be determined from any set of numbers or items is what "vision" you have for yourself as a cleric. Exactly how do you perceive yourself as a 'holy man'?

You haven't said why you've narrowed your choices down to the two you have, but if you want to be different than the majority, look to play a cleric of an uncommon deity. How often do you see cultural armor for Karana, Prexus or some of the others?

As 'small' an impact as your religion choice seems to have on the rest of Norrath sometimes, keep in mind once you pick it, you can't switch.

Now, the reason I chose a High Elf had nothing to do with any of the above, to be quite honest; I based mine off a DnD character I had played.

Best of luck on making your decision.
