EQleric.com is definitely a website in continued growth. It's very exciting to see how many people are signing up and participating on the message board.

There is no doubt most of you have great knowledge of the game, EverQuest. Watching you help others coming to the website, asking questions, I can't help but be impressed.

EQcleric can be a great website of diverse content, but we need your help. Player Guides, Quest Overviews, Game Content are all sections we need to fulfill.

Another area is Player Fiction. This is not a new concept by far, but one that is fun and entertaining to read. Do you have a great story from your journeys in EverQuest? Do you like to write fiction? Well, we'd love to show your work on the site.

If you are a programmer, with extensive knowledge about PHP/MySQL, and enjoy the game of EverQuest -- we want to talk to you.

As you can see there's a lot of sections on this site needing help. I would write them all up myself if I had the time, and if the websystem we run itself was up to par, I'd let you all submit the work through it...That's just not the case, and it's questionable how good the site would be if only me and Cerrick produced the content...

Together we stand strong, and together we can show the world of EQ what clerics are all about, and at the same time give new clerics a fighting chance to survive throughout the leveling and question that is needed to become a High Priest.

If any of this appeals to you, please don't hesitate. Send me a PM on the board, or email me; jarvis@eqcleric.com. Even send me a tell in-game if you want (;tell prexus.jarvis). I'm looking forward to hearing from you.
