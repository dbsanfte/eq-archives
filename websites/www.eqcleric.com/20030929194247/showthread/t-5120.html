Aguth
If you are picking a race purely because of racial traits I would go with Halfling or Dark Elf.

Dark Elves get a nice clinging darkness necklace, can hide which has helped me many times. They also have access to the overthere hammer which is another gate item which never hurts. Problem is you have to live with bad faction and no invis which used to be more of a pain than before.

Halflings get racial hide and more importantly racial sneak which lets you buy from vendors that you normally couldn't and lets you do some quests you normally couldn't do. They also level a slight bit faster than other races. Only real downside is that sometimes being short is a real pain and they eat twice as fast as other races.

All of the other races are more or less useless compared to those 2 from a racial trait standpoint at high levels. At low levels stats really help though so don't be afraid to pick because of them, especially if you plan on spending a lot of time at low levels.

As others have said though the best reason to pick a race is because you like the idea of that race, I'd pick Dark Elf for my clerics even if they were the worst race in the game for it.
