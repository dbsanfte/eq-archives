Well, since you've been gone, Clerics have gotten New Summoned Hammers (These have actual stats, as well as a decent DD proc). We've also got alot of Alternate Advancement Skills, which start becoming available at level 51, if I recall. (Not all are available at that time, but you *can* start gaining AA points then)

We've gotten Undead DoT's, Lower-level Heal over Time Spells, Lower-Level Ress Spells, Player-Made "Gate-Stones", Self-Buffs that add to our AC/HP, and at higher levels add mana regen. Newer Yaulp Spells, that also add mana-regen at higher levels.

As for "what reigns" in terms of stats, Hit Points are the new "big" thing. There are clerics hitting well over 7k in hit points raid-buffed these days. Armor class naturally follows suit, as cleric armors have alot more AC as time goes on, for the most part. Wisdom can go to 355 max now, with Alt Advance skills, and of course mana pools are hitting well over 6k these days.

Granted, alot of this is based on end-game level of play, but you get the general idea. For someone returning from the Kunark Era, there is a plethora of armors that are player-made, that are tons better than what was available then.

Your best bet would be to look over this site and others, and take a look at what's available, in terms of getting updated gear. There is alot of neat items available from the most recent expansion, Legacy of Ykesha, with decent focus effects on them. Also, it's pretty good exp for a group around your level in most parts of it.
