firm
In terms of self-sufficiency, a cleric/beastlord combo is going to be hard to beat due to the utility of high-end beastlords.

Despite being a leather-class, a beastlord's tanking ability is fairly respectable and with slows, mana regen spells, spell taunts (spam drowsy or sicken) and high dps when combined with a pet as well as an AA snare, I chose to start one up to 2-box with me when I really have nothing at all to do. This unfortunately is rare - raiding typically takes up 90% of the time I'm able to spend online.

If I was expecting to take my twink to along to raids though, it'd have to be a casting class that can solo well, such as a magician.

cujomatic
I have two boxed bard/cleric to 50. The bard can melee pretty well with cleric heals and chain stuns, and bard speed makes moving nice. Bard can provide a bit of added mana regen as well, but that will be nerfed a bit in the near future. If the cleric wants to go cook dinner, read a book, etc., the bard can swarm kite nearby and pl them both, lol. Doing this in the planes might be quite a challenge. A stunning/rooting/nuking(even minor) partner makes a powerful ally for a bard.

I have two boxed bard/ranger to 52. I had to spend some major pp on the ranger just recently, because at 50, 30 ac bp is a little thin. Two rooting classes can make for some interesting crowd control. Again, a Snare of Wolf partner can be handy. I am not sure what happens when the cleric can no longer chain stun mobs (above 55). But before then, a kei'ed ranger backed by a chain stunning, healing, nuking cleric is a pretty powerful combination.

I have two boxed warrior/cleric to 22. If they fix warrior dps, this would be a viable combination, but no porting/sow can be a hindrance. At 22 the warrior dps and taunt is just fine, a little bit twinked, but later I understand warrior dps is not that great.

A think a cleric shammy could duo and be a great group core, as well as cleric/enchanter.

Any class can benefit from a cleric parner (probably a druid, non pet caster the least). Having a cleric to be the core of the group, should you want to form one, especially with LDoN should be awesome.
