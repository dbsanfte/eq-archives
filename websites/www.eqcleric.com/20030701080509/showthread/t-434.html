A "BOT" cleric is just a cleric. From what I understand, it's the same as "2-boxing" if you know what that is. Basically, a person uses two accounts (with two computers) to duo two characters or to keep an extra player in (like a cleric) for heals, buffs, etc. It's also a way people PL (power level) newer characters by themselves instead of getting help from another.

I use 2 box, and its relatively easy to group with yourself. The key is to macro everything and memorizing your keys. Being in a full group is a different story. Just being cleric in a small raid, you always have to be alert, and sadly, rogues needs also lots of attention ( Backstabs every now and then, evade, and positioning yourself correctly ). I am upfront about it when I group with others, I tell them about my attention shifting between 2 chars, some ppl are nervous cause they like a fully concentrated cleric.

Anyhow, at first it is very disorienting, trying to heal with the wrong character... But it comes natural rather quickly.

Huge benefits is that I can go far without anyone helping me. With my cleric Celestial Healing my rogue all the time, he is an ok Tank, and cleric meds the mana back she used to cast Celest H.

Capien
A key thing I've found in helping to 2-box is having the 2nd monitor side by side, and cordless keyboard/mouse as far as physical hardware goes. In game it's all about the macros like was mentioned above, you can do alot with macros and so much of EQ can be broken down into fairly repeatable sequence of actions. Clerics are fairly easy to run, you can watch health from any character in the group and a series of targetting hotkeys (if not in group) or the F# keys and the heal macro works good :)

I don't like to 2-box while grouping for exp, I have sometimes been asked to 2-box during events though. My favorite use for it is in PLing alts. I've seen a guy manage up to 4 accounts at once in Cazic at the 4 corners camp and do fine all by himself so to speak.

If you are going to 2-box in a group I suggest telling them upfront, you making a mistake is much higher and some people rightfully don't want to group with 2-boxers.

PLing with a cleric and boxing one as an equal or lower level character are two different things. A bot is a cleric that falls into the second category, often kept ungrouped to max the melee's exp. Or a bot is used on a raid as a miscellaneous healer/buffer/rezzer, once in a while included on a chain but with the attention on the other character at any other time.

I personally don't think I'd do well with this, but I can see how other people do it. That monk 2-boxing in different groups must have been pretty incredible if he could truly keep up with both groups. (I'm betting his druid was standing there for quite some time though.)
