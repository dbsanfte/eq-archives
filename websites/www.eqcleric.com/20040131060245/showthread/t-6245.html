Shadowfrost
Before I start, I'm going to take a moment to offer a massive pat on the back to eqcleric.com's main man: Jarvis! Let's hear it for the dorf!

/em cheers wildly.

From very humble beginnings, Jarvis has created this amazing site, which is both an excellent resource for the cleric player, and also a valuable community of like-minded people. I can't congratulate him enough.

The success of eqcleric.com has been astounding really, when you think about it. It doesn't advertise for new posters, and it's free; it relies on people like you telling other people that it exists. And yet it's worked... I've looked at the statistics for numbers of users and numbers of posts, and there's been a steadily-increasing trend for a very long time. Right now, it's phenomenally successful. Far more so than I would ever have predicted!

This has led to two things happening.

First, the costs of the site have been steadily increasing, and you need to know that Jarvis has been meeting many of those costs from his own pocket. There have been generous donations from our Keepers of the Flame, of course, and these have certainly helped, but we're still at the stage where we're costing Jarvis a significant amount of money.

Now, the dorf is adamant that there won't be popups or advertising links on the site to help pay for it... and I respect that. I wouldn't want to see those either. So at the moment, we're thinking about ways to raise the money the site needs, perhaps through merchandising - it's possible that you'll see a range of items like eqcleric coffee cups and T-shirts and stuff to buy, and maybe a call for help with designing appropriate products.

I promise not to let Kayanaa design a thong, though... some things don't bear thinking about :\

Second, Jarvis has plans to increase the breadth of what the site has to offer, in terms of articles, guides, and other resources. We're looking for people who can write and compile these; people who contribute in this way will be recognized with specific titles, forum pp, possibly their own forum and other privileges. Effectively, we're looking to establish a new group of people, like the Keepers of the Flame but for those who contribute site material rather than money.

So what am I coming to?

I'm coming to this. Everyone who does anything for eqcleric is a volunteer, and we need more people to volunteer. If you've benefitted from eqcleric.com, you make use of the site and you think it's valuable and worthwhile to you, we need YOU to help us make it better!

If you're in a financial position to dip your hand in your pocket and donate a couple dollars or so to help Jarvis with the costs, that would be a wonderful thing. But equally, I need to recruit new contributors of site material.

If you have the knowledge, ability and enthusiasm to write articles or news for the site, I need to hear from you. So for anybody who's looking to help out, you can either post below, or if you'd rather, send me a PM. You can also email me: shadowfrost@eqcleric.com

Not everyone who applies will end up adding material to the site, of course. Like anything to do with publishing, what you write will need to be checked and possibly edited for accuracy and use of language... It takes strength of character as well as knowledge and language skills to write for a website.

And don't forget to appreciate Jarvis! He's the man who's made all this possible.

Shadowfrost
I've been truly overwhelmed by the response from the site readers to this post!

A lot of people have asked me what they should write about - and the answer is, I don't know precisely. If a player of an EQ cleric character might want to know it, then it belongs on this site.

Here's a good way of thinking about this. If you've recently completed a quest, why not come up with a writeup? We're looking for stuff like which monsters to kill, what drops what, listings of NPC text, that sort of stuff - and locs and screenshots would be amazing! If you've recently killed a raid critter and you're prepared to share details of the techniques you used, again, a writeup would be wonderful. Stuff that you've research is also cool - e.g. an article on how to get hold of all the spells in a particular level range would be great.

It doesn't all have to be massively high level, this site is aimed at clerics in all level ranges and newbie quest guides are as useful to us as a complete guide to killing Quarm.

It just has to be about clerics, and about EQ. Apart from that, all we need is for people with decent communication skills to write up something that you know about, as accurately and specifically as possible.

I want to finish by apologizing to those people who've sent me emails and PM's who I haven't replied to personally as yet. I'm not ignoring you, and I appreciate the response, which has been pretty amazing. I will reply to every message I get, but it might take a little time. :)
