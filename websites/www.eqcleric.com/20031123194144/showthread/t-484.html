Shadowfrost
According to research done on Graffes, specialisation has only one effect, which is to reduce the mana cost of your spells.

Graffe's concluded that specialise skill of 50 reduces the mana cost of spells in that category by 3% and specialise 200 reduces the mana cost by 11%.

If this is correct, with Specialise Alteration 200, Complete Healing costs 354 mana to cast rather than 400; this can be further reduced through the Spellcasting Mastery AA skill to a minimum of 316 mana at Spellcasting Mastery 3.

Specialisation has no spell haste effect that I am aware of, and neither does it have any effect on fizzle rates as I understand it.
