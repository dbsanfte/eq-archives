Hannorel
Well, I had the pleasure of going on my first "big named" mob raid the other night. While Vindi may be small fry to a lot of you, I hadn't ever done anything like this before. I might still be waiting on that "first time" if it wasn't for some noble folks on my server.

An old guildmate, now a member of La Catena, inquired as to what I was doing, just as my guild's hunt in Kael was drawing to a close. I told him that, and he said "great! we need another person for a CH rot for Vindi." Well, I was a bit nervous, having never done a CH rot before. The other two healers on the rot, Miyabi and Pako, both were patient and took their time walking me through what I was needed to do.

I am grateful to these folks for taking in an unproven outsider, even if one of their own did vouch for me. I am also grateful to the other healers for not taking an elitist attitude, or saying anything like, "omg, you don't know how to do a CH rot?" I am truly grateful for all guilds and players like this, the ones willing to teach and pass on knowledgeand help those of us just entering the top layers get the sort of play experience we need to function well.

So, hats off specifically to La Catena, and also to Bassam, for getting me a spot, and to Miyabi and Pako (who says druids can't heal?) for giving me guidance and instruction.
