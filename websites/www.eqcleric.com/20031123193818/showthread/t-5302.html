Hi Gang,

Not sure where changes on test are typically reported, but wanted folks to see this.

First Divine Barrier is getting a bit of a change. The DS component is removed, and the heal component got buffed a lot. Used to heall for 153 @ lvl 65, now heals for 976 @ 65. Gives a cleric a chance to live after it wears off :)

Second, hammer pet had its mana cost reduced (again) to 175 from 250. Personally, I still want a lvl 65 version that wields the lvl 63 summoned hammer /smilewink

Third change is the big one. The celestial regen aa line got buffed up. Big time. They increased the duration from 4 ticks to 8 ticks, AND its now group instead of single target. Which means it should be MGB'able. Now instead of spending 1900 mana to mgb elixer (300 hp for 4 ticks) we have a no mana raid regen of 500-800 a tick for 8 ticks. Going to make dealing with non-dispel ae's a good deal easier.
