Well, the update for LDoN didnt finish until 11:00 PM last night for me (Central European Time) and I didnt have time to do any of the adventures, but after logging on this morning i managed to nag a group with a monk, 2 chanters, a beastlord, myself, and a bard. We signed up for the Mistmore Catacombs adventure and off we went. First impressions of the dungeon were "Awesome graphics!" the mistmore catacombs were very nicely textured and the adventure part was a nice change from your average "Pull Mob, Kill Mob, Get XP" we were constantly faced by new challenges of traps, trying to pull only 1 mob at a time, and trying to find our way around. We did manage to get lost and go around in a circle which set off a trap that poisoned me, hehe.. Because we were lost for about 10 minutes, we didnt get the quest done in time for any adventure points. But, nonetheless everyone still enjoyed it very much. It was a major relieve to be able to go dungeon crawling and was very exciting! I admit that this expansion was really awesome! Cant wait to try oujt some of the other dungeons! So! You folks post your first impressions.
