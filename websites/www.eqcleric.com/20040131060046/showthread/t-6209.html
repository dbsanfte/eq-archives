First off, I don't hate beggers. I do however dislike beggers who persist! Usually when a new person to EQ asks for help it isn't for gear, it's to ask how to do something or get somewhere. Anyone with any Experience at all should be able to spot the TRUE Newbie over the Newbie who begs to save PP or is to laxy to earnw hat they need.

I have helped new players in many ways. Some with guideance, be it showing them a place to hunt or how to use their interface to giving them some PP to buy spells or bags.

One of the tests I use to judge wether the person truly needs PP or not is rather than just giving up my earned PP, I will suggest a way to earn PP of their own, be it a place to camp for an item they can sell in the bazaar or what mobs to kill that give the best PP return. If they in turn make some sort of statement other than "thank you" or "how do I get there" I immediately know this person is just a lame begger who does not deserve anymore of my time.

Recently I was in Thurgadin working on my shawl quests, when out of no where I get a tell from a guy who says
"Hi, could you help a fellow cleric?"
to which I replied, "Maybe, what ya need?"
He then says "I need some PP to finish my jewelcrafting skill"
so I do a /who on him to find he is /anon. So I ask him what level he is and he replies "19". Ok now I'm thinking to myself, level 19 working on jewelcraft? He obviously has another character who has earned some PP somewhere or he probably wouldnt be working JC. Then all of a sudden he pops up in front of me and types /plead. The guy is twnked with some expensive gear. Now Im thinking no way your getting any PP from me.
So I reply politely "Sorry man, I am very busy and don't like to offer PP to beggers, however, I can give you some advice on where to hunt to earn some PP to help you finish your JC skill."
He then replies "No, I don't want to hunt I just need some PP, please please please give me some" at this point he went on my ignore list and off I go to finish my quest. The kid follows me all over thurgadin, getting in my way etc.

I am a very polite person IRL and reflect the same qualities in Everquest. But I will not be taken advantage of, nor will I stand around and let you harrass me.
