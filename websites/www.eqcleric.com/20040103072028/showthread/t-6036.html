[21:10] We're getting a lot of questions. So it will take us a moment to sort through them.

[21:14] There's several aspects of the melee revamp, one aspect of which is the new opening system. There's plans on addressing other specific problems with melee in addition to the new system.
[21:15] The goal is to create a balance where knights and warriors both have equal desirablity in different situations.
[21:16] We're really excited about all the new changes we're making and we're anquious to get them out on test where we can tune them and see them in action.

[21:16] *Coronis* When are you anticipating these changes going live on regular servers?
[21:16] Our changes are expected to go live during the December 16th update that'll be happening next month. We also plan on introducing them to the Test Server very soon. [21:18] We decided to do that because it's really an entirely new system, and has very little to do with how the old stamina bar worked.

[21:19] You will be able to take advantage of the new melee opening system when you begin recieving new combat abilities at level 20.

[21:21] You will be able to take advantage of openings that you create. Some openings will work for everyone, but there's no link between your opening and someone elses. There are no combinations or dependencies on other players.

Most of the basic skill swill be available on vendors, while a few high level ones will be drops.
