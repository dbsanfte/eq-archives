Mattress
When you dye armor you are effectively dying the armor slot - not the armor itself. So whatever piece you place in that slot is going to be tinted to the color you have selected. If you wish to change the color back to it's original color - dye the slot using 255 255 255 (max white). Then it will be the color it originally was intended to be.

You can dye a slot as many times as you have dyes to use. If you want to be purple one day and yellow the next - so be it. You'll just need the appropriate number of dyes.

I am a regular of the Den Mother's at Traders Corner ( www.eqtraders.com ) she has the recipe for making the dyes using alchemy or brewing so if your brewing is above say 70 you can make it you just need to get some unfired vials (pottery skill) here is the recipe from EQ Traders:

Armor Dyes
dye tints are applied to the player's armor slot (not directly to the armor) by use of the "dye" button on your inventory screen. This is the point at which you select the color, with no need for making different shades of dyes, etc

dye vial unfired vial (vendor-sold) - fire in kiln with quality firing sheet -- trivial 54

prismatic dye prism shard (vendor-sold), glaze lacquer - combine in brew barrel using brewing skill, or combine in a medicine bag using alchemy skill -- trivial 54

vial of prismatic dye prismatic dye, dye vial - combine in brew barrel using brewing skill, or combine in a medicine bag using alchemy skill -- trivial 68
