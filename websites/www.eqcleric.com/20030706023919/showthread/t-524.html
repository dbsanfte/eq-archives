not realy a quest, but i treated it as such, since i "quested" the items for it:



i just got a great shield for 50pp and some change. almost all solo, only got help in CT, and help with final combine.

the tae ew shield. i colected everything in one night.

1. goto dalnir, get a iksar blood. (i got 2 in about a hour, plus 200pp and another 200pp in vendor trash)

2. goto sarnak fort in loio for sarnak blood. (lol first sarnak i killed dropped the blood, woot!!)

3. goto CT entrance, killed 5 lizards got 2 tae ew blood. (i had lvl 62 necro friend help me with this, gave him first blood that dropped, i got one right after, i pulled with pacify)

3. spent hour and half in lower guk, no frogloc blood, so did a /ooc in PoK got one for 50pp.

4. went to east freeport purchased 2 metal sheeting, got shield mold in PoK.

5. i used brew barrel to combine iksar,frogloc and sarnak blood, then combine that finished product with the tae ew blood also in brew barrel, both combines trivial at 135.

6. found a friend who has a friend that is a master smith, had them hit combine for me, poof, i have awesome shield.

this shield sells for over 10k usualy in bazaar, sensless to pay so much when is so easy to get the stuff for it, i soloed everything except the tae ew blood. you can also buy the blood in bazaar for around 3k.

replaced my agis of ice with this shield
