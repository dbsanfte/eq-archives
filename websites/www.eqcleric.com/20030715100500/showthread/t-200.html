Jarvis
Unless you've been living under a rock the last week :D I'm sure you've heard that tonight, Friday Oct 18th, 48 Hours Investigates did a special on Addiction.

Included in this show was a segment about Everquest vs. addiction to online games.

The story was controversial in that it featured the story about 21-year-old Shawn Woolley who comitted suicide last year after apparently playing EQ.

So they opened a huge can of worms here obviously. Is EQ addictive? What kind of addiction are we talking here? Is it an addiction that goes beyond the average persons control? Is EQ such a life altering game that the end result for a fragile mind is ending his life? Would that person have chosen the same outcome if a similar/identical crisis happened in RL or through a different medium?

There are many questions, and few answers. I'm hoping we can open a discussion on this, and I'd really like your honest opinion on the matter.

A link to the show is here: http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/1998...main22761.shtml

Should have a link to a video in case you missed the show.

Looking forward to reading your replies.
