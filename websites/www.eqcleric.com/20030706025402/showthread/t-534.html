Numo--

The Ragefire in Sol B needs to be handed the Shimmering Peal, and then you get the Swirling Pearl and Zordak's Box of... something (forget the name).

Then you take the 4 pearl fragments looted from various/random mobs in Skyfire, put them in the box and combine. Give the box to the Warder Cecilia who is near the big dragon statue in Skyfire (she gives you a Seal) and then the human form of Ragefire spawns. Kill him and then immediately the dragon form of Ragefire spawns.
