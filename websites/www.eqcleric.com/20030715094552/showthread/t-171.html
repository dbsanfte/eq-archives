Following are changes that are being made in anticipation of the release of The Planes of Power expansion pack. These changed are intended to dramatically change the game world, and fix several long-standing balence issues. Please see eqlive.station.sony.com/c...p?id=52118 for more information.


** Wizard **

- Manaburn has been changed to require a 1 minute delay between casts on a single target by multiple Wizards.
- All Wizards who currently have trained Manaburn should find themselves with 5 AA points, and the skill untrained.
- The effect on the Staff of the Four has been changed to Manaskin.


** Magician **

- Rod of Mystical Transvergance now summons a Rod containing a single charge of 360 mana for 450 hit points. It can also be cast on a target.
- The Orb of Mastery is no longer lore.


** Cleric **

- Complete Healing now heals for a maximum of 7500 hit points.
- Word of Redemption now heals for a maximum of 7500 per target.
- The effect on the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh has been changed to Ressurection.
- Blessing of Ageolism and Ancient: Gift of Ageolism have had their duration shortened to 1 hour 20 minutes.


** Monk **

- Defense skill cap for monks has been lowered to 175.
- The number of hit points granted per point of stamina for a Monk has been reduced.
- Monks will now recieve a hit point penalty similar to their AC penalty when a monk weighs over 15 stones at level 50. This scales per level to 5 stones at 65.
- Reuse timer for Feign Death has been changed to 30 minutes to prevent it from being used as an offensive ability.


** Paladin **

- Valor of Marr has been changed to a 150 mana sieve + stun.
- Paladins have had their innate casting haste removed.
- Improved Lay on Hands has been changed to heal a maximum of 7500 hit points.
- Slay Undead has been changed to do significantly less damage per level.
- All Paladins who currently have trained Slay Undead should find themselves with 3/6/9 AA points, and the skill untrained.
- Brells Moutainous Barrier has had its duration lowered to 1 hour 30 minutes.


** Necromancer **

- Changed all lifetaps to heal half the value of the damage of the lifetap.
- The mana regen component of Mind Wrack has been changed to 25 mana per tick.
- Emissary of Thule has had its component changed to an essance emerald.
- Feign Death and Death Peace have had their recast timers increased to 30 minutes.
- Dire Charm has been changed to do a break check every 5 minutes.
- All Necromancers who currently have trained Dire Charm should find themselves with 9 AA points, and the skill untrained.


** Shadownight **

- Changed all lifetaps to heal half the value of the damage of the lifetap.
- Vampiric Embrace has been changed to grant the same hit points as the Necro version.
- Feign Death and Death Peace have had their recast timers increased to 30 minutes.


** Ranger **

- Endless Quiver is now an activated skill, with a duration of 5 minutes and a reuse of 30 minutes.
- All Rangers who currently have trained Endless Quiver should find themselves with 9 AA points, and the skill untrained.
- Archery Mastery has been changed to be 50% of the effect it was previously.


** Rogue **

- All Rogue-usable weapons that are 16 damage or higher will only be equipable in the offhand slot.
- The Rogue Escape AA has had its recast timer brought in line with the Monk Feign Death skill.


** Warriors **

- The duration of the discipline "Defensive" has been reduced to 12 seconds to bring it in line with other disciplines.
- The duration of the discipline "Evasive" has been reduced to 12 seconds to bring it in line with other disciplines.
- The duration of the discipline "Offensive" has been reduced to 12 seconds to bring it in line with other disciplines.
- The duration of the discipline "Precision" has been reduced to 12 seconds to bring it in line with other disciplines.
- Area Taunt now has a chance to fail similar to normal taunt.
- All Warriors who currently have trained Area Taunt should find themselves with 5 AA points, and the skill untrained.


** Bards **

- Effect on the Singing Short Sword has been changed to Solon's Song of the Sirens
- Attempting to move while singing will cause a missed note.


** Beastlord **

- Beastlords now recive the Feign Death skill at level 30.
- Beastlords have had their defense skill cap lowered to 175.
- Beastlords will now have the same weight penalties as Monks.


** Shaman **

- Plauge of Insects has been changed to perform a MR check.
- The Cannibalization AA has been removed from game.
- All Shamam who currently have trained should Cannibalization find themselves with 5 AA points, and the skill untrained.
- Khura's Focusing has had its duration decreased to 1 hour 20 minutes.


** Druid **

- Druids have had their defense skill cap increased to 175.
- The effect on the Nature Walkers Scimitar has been changed to Drones of Doom.
- Dire Charm has been changed to do a break check every 5 minutes.
- All Druids who currently have trained should Dire Charm should find themselves with 9 AA points, and the skill untrained.


** Enchanters **

- All illusion spells now have a recast of 30 minutes.
- Koadic's Endless Intellect has had its duration lowered to 1 hour 20 minutes.
- Dire Charm has been changed to do a break check every 5 minutes.
- All Enchanters who currently have trained Dire Charm should find themselves with 9 AA points, and the skill untrained.
