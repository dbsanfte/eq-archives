Laucus
Find out from your higher level bard friends about their lulling skills. You might honestly be better getting a solid group of 8-10 (just to be safe) and fight your way down. If you are careful, have the rogue disarm the traps and have the bard lull (and I'm thinkin maybe a high level druid would work for this too??-not too versed in the druid area though) You should be able to make it to the king area.

The most important thing to remember is to lev once you're in the library and cling to the left wall down the hallway to avoid the trap. If you can pull 2-4 at a time with a chanter and a bard, you should be gtg.

key classes are what are vital here though-rogue, crowd control (preferably chanter AND bard), and uber shaman helps (slowers are AWESOME), an FD puller if you're worried about big trains, yourself and some dps. Be very careful and dont' rush things and you should be able to get to him for the kill.

Good luck with your epic!
