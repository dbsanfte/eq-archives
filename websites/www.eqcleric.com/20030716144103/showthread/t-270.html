Illi
I have all but two pieces of Akkirus' armour (just lacking the greaves and vambraces; I'm not going to try to get the crown because I have better), and what surprises me is that at level 60 with a 255 Wisdom cap, I have only 1 point of mana less than Jarvis does at level 62 with a 265 Wisdom cap (compare my Magelo profile to Jarvis').

It looks like the Akkirus' armour has better stats than the Templar's when it comes to mana (although it has lower AC). I'm not trying to brag, I'm just trying to highlight the differences between the two sets of armour (at the risk of bring up the age-old Kael vs Skyshrine debate, which is not my purpose here).

It boils down to this: I was told as a young Gnome trying to make my way through the world that +Mana items are generally better than +Wis items, especially after reaching the 255 cap. I have tried to follow that, since any Wisdom over 255 is basically wasted below level 61.

In any case, I prefer the Skyshrine armour (Akkirus') to Kael for two reasons: 1) It's a prettier colour :D, and 2) I will never ally myself with a bunch of people who are as so grossly wrongsized as the giants are. ;)

Anyways, Jarvis, I'm not trying to slight your equipment, I wanted to suggest working on getting more +Mana items. :) It's too bad you're on Kael faction, because the Talisman of Benevolence (my range item, +55 mana) is very nice, but it's a Skyshrine quest item. Also, the belt I have (White Dragon Scale Sash, +90 mana) is dropped by King Tormax. I don't know if there are Kael equivalent of these, but hopefully you can find Kael equivalents.

Other suggestions I have for somewhat easy-to-obtain gear are the Large Smoothmetal Torque (neck slot, +45 Mana drops in PoI) and Silver Hammer Earring (ear slot, +55 mana, drops from a named in PoJ in the Executioner's trial).

I hope I don't come across in the wrong way, I'm just trying to help a fellow Cleric be the best he can be. :)
