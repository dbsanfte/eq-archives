Hate's Fury End-Boss - Click HERE for Original Thread
Dyschord
We ended our daily grind on the Rathe Council last night early, and decided to have a little fun. A guildmate of mine, had spent 100+ hours camping the key parts to set off the final event in Hate's Fury (LoY), so we grabbed 24 people and decided to give it a go.

It's a spawnable event, but not a typical "ring" event. When you hand in your "key" (Only 1 key per attempt, key disappears on hand-in), the Assistant you hand in to, despawns, and 4 Luggald-model mobs, as well as Captain Krisnok(sp) spawn.

The four "Gaurds" that spawn are non-mezzable, slowable, hit in the 200's. Easy to off-tank. We killed them, and found they do repawn, while the Captain lives, so we tank-mezzed them for the remainder.

The Captain is your main target. He's level 65, he has 2-3 AE's at his disposal and uses them all throughout the fight. I found that his AE's were cureable through use of Pure Blood, and Remove Greater Curse, as well as Radiant Cure. RC seemed to cure them all in one shot, at level 3 of the skill. The Captain hits for approximately 600-800, and I do believe he is slowable.

The fight is pretty easy, overall, and I almost feel like we had overkill there, with 24. When the Captain died, we killed off the gaurds. There is a very unique death graphic, for the Captain. One that I won't ruin for you here /eek

He dropped some decent items, though I would like to see what his other drops are. The gaurds also seemed to drop items, of a similar level.

Of note, he dropped a mace, which has the side effect of proc'ing imp pets. Useable by most melee, as well as priests. Decent stats, though for us was mostly a toy. Also, an nice Mana Pres 3 earring dropped, with stats that were about equal to the Solstice earring, useable by preists/int casters. Off the gaurds , there were some shoulders for hybrids, and a melee bracer, with decent stats. (Sorry I don't have any loot pics, though I might if our monk posts them up for me;) )

The Assistant, who starts the event respawned about 10 minutes after we were done, so if multiple people had keys, this could be done again and again.

We'll be heading back to do him a few more times, as we want to see his full loot table, and in hopes of possibly seeing some good charms off him.

All in all, was a great time, and for those that have a few good balanced groups, and some time to kill, this is a fun way to do it;)
