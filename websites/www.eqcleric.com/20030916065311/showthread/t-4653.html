Bakanar
My friend plays a magician, and I find that pet classes are very nice to partner with, since the pet can tank and do damage, while the caster is another damage source. With a normal tank, you only have one damage source, and in the higher end of the game, a good magician pet will do just as much or more than a warrior or paladin. Also pets can get magician summoned items to improve their hp, on top of the normal buffs that you can give them. If you're in trouble, you can have the pet tank the mobs while you run to a safe place or zone, and then you can cast a new one when you come back, while when a tank class dies you have to go back and drag them to a safe spot and rezz them and wait for ressurection effects to wear off and all of that other good junk.

In my opinion, a pet class is one of the best classes to partner up with.
