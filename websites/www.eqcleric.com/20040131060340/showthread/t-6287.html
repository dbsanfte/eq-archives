Design your casino correctly and you will be practically guaranteed a huge profit. With a 20kpp house, you should set the max bet to about 500 pp. The problem is, however, that most of the people who run casinos have no background in statistics, so they believe that simply giving themselves a higher than 50% chance to win will cover all their losses. The fact is, with 2 to 1 payoffs for landing in the upper 40 percentile, the house is guaranteed a loss. If you make the payoff over 100 runs less than the gain over 100 runs (i.e. a roll of 60 or over will win you 1.4x your money) then you are guaranteed to win. This is how a real casino should be run. However there is no way anyone would play in your casino with those odds given that there are so many others out there that offer double payoff at the same level and TRIPLE payoff at around the 95th percentile range, which is ludicrous, and I guarantee you that unless they are incredibly lucky, they suffer huge losses.
