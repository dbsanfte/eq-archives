Actually, there *IS* a way to reduce the casting time of complete heal, but it's kinda a trick, and kinda not as great as you'd think.

Use 'Cog Encrusted Epaulets' (from Plane of Innovation) and cast 'Blessing of Reverence' on yourself (cleric buff). Now mem and check out 'Word of Redemption' (think that's the name of the group CH spell)- and be surprised that it's cast time is like 8.5 seconds. :)

The downside is this- it costs 1100 mana to cast instead of 400. On the up side, it also heals everyone else in the group who's close by.
