AanyaLorien
I do my casting macros like this:

Line 1: /cast 1 (for me, this is supernal remedy)
Line 2: /g casting SR on %t
Line 3: /2 (guild cleric channel) casting SR on %t
Line 4: /cast 1

/cast 1 tells the game to cast the spell in your top spell gem.

The two lines of text inbetween the cast allows for fizzle and recovery time, and then casts again.

I also keep 4 heals up at all times, depending on the situation 5... sometimes 6.

My lineup is:

To make a hotkey from an item such as jboots, or a clicky from your inventory:

Hold your left mouse button over it until it "duplicates" the picture. Then you just drop it into an empty hotkey slot. From there you can either right clicky the item or just type to corresponding number to use the effect.

Erronn
Actually, Galv, you need to turn off the "attack when assist" setting by doing "/assist off"...then the /assist function will just target, not attack. I use it like this on all my characters, even my warrior....if I want to attack, I hit attack....if I want to target, I hit /assist...simple as that. If I want to attack the target, I hit /assist then hit attack. No more accidentally hitting a mezzed mob by just seeing who's targeting what...
