Shadowfrost
Zone: Nedaria's Landing.

Connects to: Jaggedpine Forest, any LDoN Magus, Abysmal Sea (by boat); Natimbi (by magus).

Comments: Jumpoff point for the Gates of Discord. No encounters of note.


Zone: Abysmal Sea.

Connects to: Nedaria's Landing (by boat); Natimbi (by boat).

Comments: City area for Gates of Discord. No encounters of note, but quite a few quests found.


Zone: Natimbi, the Broken Shores.

Connects to: Abysmal Sea (by boat); Nedaria's Landing (by magus); Qinimi; Kod`Taz (if flagged).

Comments: Outdoor exp zone seemingly aimed at 55-65. Only encounter of note is the Cragbeast Queen, which apparently has yet to be killed on any server.

From a cleric-specific point of view, it's worth noting the Restless Souls, which are a near the magus. They are undead, dark or light blue to 65, and a relatively easy solo; with run speed 5, I found I was able to kite them. It's not amazing xp or loot but it's something to do while you're lfg. These have a very small chance to drop a quest part for a 14/23 hammer, but this hammer is of no interest to clerics.


Zone: Qinimi, the Court of Nihilia.

Connects to: Natimbi; Ferubi; Riwwi; Barindu.

Comments: Outdoor exp zone seemingly aimed at 60-65. There is a 6 player encounter called the Chamber of Shadows which I have yet to explore; there's also an NPC who will summon your corpse from anywhere in the zone.


Zone: Riwwi, the Coliseum of Games.

Connects to: Qinimi; Ferubi; Barindu.

Comments: A zone I have yet to explore.


Zone: Ferubi, Forgotten Temple of Taelosia.

Connects to: Riwwi; Qinimi; Barindu.

Comments: Indoor exp zone aimed very much at the level 65 crowd. Mobs hit for over 1000 and proc a 650dd; some rampage, some flurry, some gain huge amounts of AC when they hit 20% life meaning that they need to be finished off with a nuke. At least two confirmed drops which apparently come from any critter in the zone, which are similar to VT or EP loot. The one I saw was a range slot item with 100hp, 125 mana, a load of saves and assorted stats, and Mana Preservation IV, and yes it was cleric useable.

Deeper in there are single-groupable named with some very nice drops, including augments. There is also a raid encounter, Smith Rondo, which is important for progression since it is the raid route to obtaining the first four flags.


Zone: Barindu, the Hanging Gardens.

Connects to: Riwwi; Ferubi; Qinimi; and the six instanced zones listed below.

Comments: Contains four noteworthy NPC's that I've found: The High Priest, the Scribe, the Apprentice, and Ganesh. All of these NPC's relate to the instanced content.

Ganesh gives access to the instanced content; you can hail him and choose which of the first four instanced zones you explore. Then you run to the entrance and you can explore the zone of your choice.

The High Priest does the same as Ganesh, but if you complete the trial in the instanced zone, you can also gain a character flag. This is the single-group route to obtaining the first four flags.

The Scribe is the character you hail after completing a trial in order to get the permanent flag for that trial.

The Apprentice is like the High Priest, but gives access to trials five and six.


Instanced content for Barindu:

1. Purifying Plant. (Can kill Smith Rondo in Ferubi instead of doing this.)
2. Emanating Furnace. (Can kill Smith Rondo in Ferubi instead of doing this.)
3. Lair of Trapped Ones. (Can kill Smith Rondo in Ferubi instead of doing this.)
4. Pool of Sludge. (Can kill Smith Rondo in Ferubi instead of doing this.)
5. Vxed, the Crumbling.
6. Tipt, the Treacherous.

The fifth and sixth instances are noticeably tougher than the first four. In all of the instanced areas, monsters can hit for up to 1208. There are also named, both randomly-spawning and as a part of the trials; these named can drop EP quality loot and also GoD spells.

All of this instanced content is limited to six players.


Zone: Kod`Taz.

Connects to: Natimbi; Yxtta; Qvic; and an instanced zone called Ikkinz.

Comments: I have yet to explore this zone and the areas beyond it.

What I do know at this time is that Yxtta leads to its own instanced zone called Uqua. Qvic allegedly leads to an instanced zone called Inktu`Ta; passing through this apparently takes you into Txevu, the Lair of the Elites, which has its own instanced zone beyond it.


Further information as and when it becomes available! :)
