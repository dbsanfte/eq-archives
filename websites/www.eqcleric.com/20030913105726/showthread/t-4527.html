Penecillin
My first character was an Iksar Monk so I spent a lot of time sitting on the edge of the Pit in the field of bone, bandaging and healing. As I sat I watched the Mobs and thought 2 things...

1. "I've got all this training in safe fall. Without it you take a lot of damage running off the pit wall. What if I train a Mob over the pit wall?"

2. "What if I became a caster with nukes? I could stand on the pit wall and nuke the Mobs in the pit and they probably wouldn't be smart enough to find the ramp and get me. I could laugh and sit and nuke all from the security of the pit wall"

Well. SOE seem to have given all the Mobs parachutes. None take falling damage even from the height of Kurns.

As for my nuking from orbit solution. The first Mob ran to the bottom of the cliff and started laying waste to me. I couldn't see them to attack back and I couldn't cast without interruption. I was too inexperienced to extract myself safely so I ran. It tunnelled underneath me all the way to the guards hitting me all the way. Then it finished me off right at the foot of the guards. Who did nothing to the Mob in the safety of its tunnel.

Two brilliant theories come crashing down to nothing. /sigh
