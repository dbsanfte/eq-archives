When I was a 90% rezzer, it amazed me how many people would pass it up looking for a 96% rez for the next hour. Here is some simple math:

The rough difference between 90% and 96% (remember, it's the percentage of LOST xp, not total xp) is about 0%-2% total difference in actual xp AFTER the rez. I say 0%-2% because it does truly vary (I often wonder about the accuracy of EQ xp bar). I have rezzed people to have them lose no xp, and other times they lost up to 2% (usually lower level people seem to lose more for some reason).

Now what I don't get, is that people will look for over an hour for 1% more xp back. When they can kill a few mobs and get it back in under 5-10 minutes (at high levels). To me thats just dumb. Take the rez, get going, get more than your lost xp back in the same time fram e rather than sitting on butt looking for a rez.
