Shadowfrost
How are clerics as undead nukers? Well, at 65, Destroy Undead has a ratio of 4, a resist check of -100, and can be affected by an easily-obtained (LDoN) Anger of Druzzil focus for a random bonus of 1-23%. It lands for full against most undead up to level 65.

Most clerics specialize in alteration rather than in evocation, which means that a cleric suffers a -8% mana cost penalty relative to an evocation specialist for this nuke. Nevertheless, clerics and necromancers are still primary anti-undead nukers.

Restandre
Your undead nukes are not a great deal better than your live ones except for two key factors: resists and recast time. You can cast instantly, and you get resists - partial or total - far less frequently. I never appreciated this in lower levels and banished (no pun intended) those spells to the back of my book and stuck with Wrath, Reckoning, etc. At the least, it helps keep you from overnuking.
