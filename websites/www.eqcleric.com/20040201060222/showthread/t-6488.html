You must train all specialization skills in order to get one of them to go over 50. So go train the rest, then cast a spell that uses alteration until your Specialize Alter goes to 51.

SubRock
If by chance you accidentally train your specialization in the wrong area, as I did, you can go to the Temple of Solusek Ro and do an easy quest to correct your mistake. But, if I'm not mistaken you only get one chance to get it right.

For most clerics, the obvious choice for specialized skill is alteration.
