LADYGUINEAPIG
Ok, this is what I know for sure about wis/int mana and caps.
For every five levels you go up each wis/int point is worth an extra mana. Example:

LVL 1: 1 wis = 1 mana
lvl 10: 1 wis = 2 mana
lvl 25: 1 wis = 5 mana
lvl 50: 1 wis = 10 mana

This is why for the first 20-25 levels of your life, you are much better off with mana gear than wisdom gear; except for when you are working on a trade skill of course.

This progression is "capped" at 200 wisdom. So any wisdom over 200 does not follow that formula anymoe.

Not too long ago, any wisdom over 200 was set to a 1 to 1 mana ratio. Meaning 1 wisdom equaled 1 mana regardless of your level. But since the lvl cap has been raised many times since the early days from 45 to 65 and gear has gotten much better this has been changed.

I am not 100% on this but I beleive wisdom between 200-250 now follows a 1 to 5 ratio. i could use a confirmation on this but I've seen this posted in a lot of places. I also don't know how your level may affect this; wether it's regardless of level or you have to reach a certain lvl first for the 1 to 5 ratio. After all, there are a lot of mega twinks out there. I've seen lvl 10's with 200+ int.

As far as the calculations for mana above 250? No clue. My guess is there are all sorts of variables there, I haven't reached that high yet myself and won't even attempt to.

Other stats become very important at later levels, and besides your max mana only counts when you start a fight. Generally if you are in a good group gaining exp you will never be at full mana. Keep in mind the bigger your mana pool is, if your the type to med to full, the longer that down time is going to be. So who cares wether you have a 3000 or 3200 mana pool? You'll never actually see it all.

I'm sure someone on this board has those answers for you though regarding wisdom over 250.
