Kayanaa
The website forum tells you what to do. Clothe your character & hit Shift + Print Screen. This saves it to your clipboard. Then you open whatever photo program you have (I use PhotoShop but there is PaintShop, etc as well). Open a "New" item & Control + V or paste. then crop the part you want & save. Tada ~~
