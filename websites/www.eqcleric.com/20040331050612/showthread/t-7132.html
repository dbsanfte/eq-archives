Shadowfrost
quote:
the Healing over time spells, while they take a good chunk of mana, the Celestial Remedy healed a lot more than my healing spell - does it also generate appropriately more hate or would I be better off tossing a few Heals on?


No. Heal over time spells generate less hate than direct heals, and at your level are more mana-efficient.

Don't get too addicted to heal over time spells, though. As you level up, more and more often you encounter situations where you've got to heal a lot of hp NOW and deal with the consequences later... taking aggro is not always bad, and you need to maximize your defence skill at every level. You'll thank me for that advice later. ;)

quote:
is it a good idea to keep symbol on everyone or just the MT, myself and the enchanter (if one) while keeping the non-component HP buffs on everyone else?


AT LEVEL 23 don't symbol anyone who doesn't need it. As you rise in levels, you'll find that everyone needs a cleric hp buff eventually, but for the moment, hang onto your gems.

quote:
I currently have 2 +6 wis rings, would it be better to drop one of them in favor of a Moonstone ring with the extended range on there? (current wis is 222)


Definitely.

Never mind the Extended Range... the moonstone ring gives +30 mana. At your present level, that's as much as you'd get from wearing a +15 wis ring.

All you need at 23 is 200 WIS, and less than that would be fine too (I was at about 140 WIS when I was level 23). 200 WIS is the "soft cap" beyond which there are very diminishing returns for having any higher. Look for items with direct +hp and +mana on them in preference to +WIS and +STA - one of the best is the jewelcrafted 65hp/65mana necklace.

quote:
And would a book of Obulus (provided I can find and afford one) be a worthwhile investment for me at 23 or is there something else that can increase the amount of HPs I heal per cast?


Focus items are good. For a level 23, get them in this order: Mana Preservation II (most important); Improved Healing II (less important); everything else (hardly matters at all.)

quote:
Then later on, group members started leaving and I was made leader. I got some Leadership experience...is there a place I can see how much I have and what does it do for me or the group? (I'm betting I missed a URL that explains this...)


From the default UI, press "L" for an explanation. At level 23, though, I'd say you want to turn this off and keep it switched off until you're in your 50's or 60's.

I'm going to jump on the "get your defense up now bandwagon". You can only get skill ups from mobs that are blue to you. The more you level, the harder blue mobs will hit. Defense gives you an AC bonus, the lower your AC, the more damage the mobs will do to you, thus making it even more difficult for you to skill up in defense. It's a nasty cycle.

If you try some zone line skilling up, not having a hammer will make no difference at all. Although if you want to try to get your 1hb skill up, just summon the lowest summoned hammer and beat on them. When your health is at 20% zone out and heal yourself.
