I have been spending this week going through 3 accounts that my brother used to play checking out the characters and I found a lvl 12 necromance named Peelain.

See more about Peelain in this thread.

Well I got out maps etc of The Warrens and decided to see what experience I could gain.

Most stuff at zone in from Paineel was green (scouts, runts, rabids) but there were light blue and some blue kobolds mixed in. I basically had a Heroic Bond and KEI buff and went to town killing everything!

The Kobolds dropped quite a bit of loot with some plat. What surprised me is how much cash I had after selling what I could hold on me between 2 trips to town and as long as KEI lasted. I had nearly 300pp after about 4 hours!

That is nothing to laugh at for a lvl 12 character!

Mattress
The Warrens is full of interesting items for low characters. It is an under-utilized zone IMHO. I used to farm Muglwump there for invis potions (until they nerfed his spawn time from 6 min to over 40). There are a few Stonebrunt quests that involve this place as well (ex: Pouch of Bone Dice from Prince - turn in for 4 charge snare item). Last but not least, this place improves your faction in Paineel as well as Erudin. Farm city sometimes, but oh well.

The Warrens has always been a zone with poor press to me. Because it isn't a convenient zone, and there aren't any "uber" drops, people shun it, except for the few that enjoy hunting there or faction farmers.

The molar quests are decent for beginners, especially if they can wear ringmail armor. I mean, c'mon, what other quest easily doable at level 5 and up will potentially net you ringmail armor?

Even more underutilized (possibly changing somewhat since the advent of the ports to the zone) is Stonebrunt Mountains. Rare is the occasion that the zone population goes above 10 at peak hours.
