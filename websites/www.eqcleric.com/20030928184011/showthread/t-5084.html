Restandre
Well, every time I read a post about the epic, it's either I hate this camp, or omg I finally finished this camp and got my epic two days later. I think there's been a thread on this before, but I'm curious. What is the camp you've had the most awful experience in? You know, the sort that would make an epic monk think Raster was a breeze.


I anticipate most people here will bring up an epic camp. Begurgle, Gimblox, old-schoolers would say Ragefire. I can think of a quest that was far worse for me -- Veeshan's Peak key.
There are 9 things you have to do for this key. Two are groundspawns and one is a simple turn-in, and the other 6 are mob kills somewhere in Kunark. Fortunately I had a Trak Tooth on MQ, and most of the others are easy. But there were two, as anticipated, that were.

The pained soul was a nasty camp. Has a PH on nearly a half hour timer and the average time camping it, from people I've talked to, is around 15 hours. I got lucky with this one and was only there 6 or 7, but there was one rogue in my guild who took over 30.

I wasn't so lucky with the camp in Karnor's though. There's a skeleton named Verix Kylong's Remains in the basement that you have to spawn and kill. I went down there one day to join a couple guildmates who were working on it. After maybe two hours, one dropped and I let one of the people there before me take it; they had been camping for a while. After that, nothing but placeholders.

I spent the next six hours there partnered with a monk named Chewee who killed anything and everything and never spawned Verix. I camped in the safe room there since I can't invis my way in. I came back the next day and Chewee came over a couple minutes later. We spent four hours or so before a raid was called without luck. I came down after the raid, cleared the camp twice, and went to bed. I came back on four successive days, averaging six hours each day. Often Chewee was there. Sometimes a could other guildies came. Sometimes I was solo and could barely make it to the PH if at all. I was getting ready to give up at this point.

I returned two-three days later on a Saturday to find there was a group killing in the basement. They were all people of a level that belonged in KC and none in a major guild. So I asked them; if I help you with your camp, can I loot Verix if he spawns? They agreed, so I gave them all Aego and started to pull for them. They all stayed in the room while I brought stuff to them. After a few hours and right before I was about to leave for a raid, Verix finally spawned.
