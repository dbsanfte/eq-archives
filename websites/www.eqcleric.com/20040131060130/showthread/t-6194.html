Heh. Doesn't sound like you'll have any problems with gear. Snag a horse for sitting med.

Temperance quest is easy. Have your shaman go to Dulak, clear stormwave for a couple hours until "a Deepwater Emblem" drops, and pick it up with your cleric (it's no drop)

Then you turn in the emblem to an erudite spirit who may or may not be up. Full details can be found in the Temperance Spoiler thread on the "Quests and Spoilers" forum here.

AA FAQ attempt and AA Discussion

Yes, at the moment your Orb of Delightful Magic is going to have slightly sucky stats, because you're using a 15k item on a character who might normally have just scraped by buying his level 9 spells with the last pp he snagged off a rat.

I'd just stick with that orb though. You're not going to find something much better. YOu could buy a gem-encrusted sceptre and a charred guardian shield if you just want short term < 1kpp solutions for wisdom.

As far as the rest of your gear, it depends. But if you can afford Ornates, buy ornates for when you can use them. Anything you don't currently have tricked out can be filled in with Sandblasted steel for a fraction of the cost of some other stopgap.

Enjoy being a cleric, and try reading the rest of the boards. Most questions you can think of are already answered. But we love getting more!

Satene
For the lower levels forget stats and go for hp and mana gear. the amount of mana you gain from wis at your level will pale in comparison to actual mana gear. As for the items you already have, they stats will scale up as you gain levels..ie if the required level is 60, when you hit level 30 you will have half the stats you would at 60.

Ideas to look at since plat doesnt seem to be an issue...
Primary-Guard captains mallet for skilling up, a cone of the mystics for normal group activity
Secondary-- Orb of Infinite Void
Range-Sarnak Prayer beads
Feet--Coldain skin boots
Legs--Donals, or grey flesh legs
Waist--Girdle of flayed iksar
Wrist--Bracelet of Sense
Hands--Coldain skin
Fingers--Shadell bandit ring
Arms--I havent found a really solid set of arms yet. I am wearing old human cultural atm for the plate look.

Chest--Emerald dragonscale

The above assumes that you seem to have a ready supply of plat available based on what you have already purchased. My best advice is do some reading. A informed consumer is a happy customer when twinking.
