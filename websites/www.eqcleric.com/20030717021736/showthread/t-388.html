__________________________________________________

To receive the Pure Blood Scroll:

In a brew barrel combine A Blackened Vial (drop in Nadox off the Blackhands) + the Broken Skull Clan Blood (drop in Nadox off the Blackhands) + Gunthak Gulf Water (Foraged and dropped in Gunthak).

You get A Black Potion.

Then combine A Black Potion + Pure Blood (dropped off a Luggold Initiate in Nadox) in a brew barrel to receive A Glowing Black Potion.

Give the potion to Grennik Neltrin and receive the scroll of Pure Blood. It is no drop.
