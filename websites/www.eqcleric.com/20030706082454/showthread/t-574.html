Anyone got any good tips on how to keep the 1HB and 2HB skills up to cap? I'm currently lvl 12 with weapon skills around 19 in both 1HB and 2HB. I just don't seem to be getting the bumps in skill and I'm loath to spend training points on them.

Tonsofrezzen
Offensive skill raise quicker the high level the mob is compared to you. Also, you will only get imps if the mob cons DB or higher to you.

I found that I got the most imps when a high level dru came by and buffed the hell out of me. I just went and meleed till they were gone. If that is not an option, then when I am 80% or higher mana, I would get up and melee with the tanks. Just stand behind the mob to avoid rampages, riposts, and flurries (sp). For me, it works well.

Braeaelin
If you're looking to improve your offensive skills only, you'd probably be best assisting while grouped. Otherwise I'd be more interested in keeping up my defensive skill.

What I've done is solo mobs and just let myself get beat on while also fighting back improving offensive skills. Midway through a fight, I'd root the mob and back off. I'd spend this time to heal, med and let my stamina naturally replenish. As I pray to Innoruuk, I'm able to use the Regent Symbol of Innoruuk to cast a snare on mobs (which is mana-free of course). I usually pull with this and once I've rooted a mob during a fight, I'll lay this on them to keep their health from restoring. Otherwise you'll probably have to hit them with a DD.

When the mob is running, I'd snare it and remove my weapon to improve H2H :)

restandre
Agree with what Braealin said.
If you want to max your skills, obviously get low delay weapons, and appropriate stat buffs. I would recommend only working on one skill until you have it maxed, and then switch as you have time to. I kept my 1hb near-maxed most of the way up, but I never used a 2hb until I got a drop that was better than my prim/sec combo (but definitely isn't anymore!) Nonetheless, I still keep the 2hb for fun, but I find it to be not very worthwhile, and my 2hb is still a few points short of max. Now if I could just get my h2h up...

Bukkiah
My method for keeping skills maxed, defensive, offensive, weapons, etc. was to pull a guard in West Freeport to the zone line to North freeport. I'd buff myself up and hack him for a bit, zone, heal, zone back and start again. It was slow and there was nothing to be gained other than the skill-ups but it works. I did not kill the guard, just lumped him up until I had to zone to heal. 1hb is maxed, 2hb is near max, H2H is half way there, and defense and offense are maxed, for what it's worth.
