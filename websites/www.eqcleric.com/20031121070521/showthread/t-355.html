Cerrick
All of the imbue/enchant spells are directed at trade skills. Clerics get 2 imbue(to grant an item godly power) spells. Enchant spells(to grant an item magical power) are enchanter only.

Each cleric gets the imbue spell for their dietie's holy stone ie: Plains Pebble for Karana; Emerald for Tunare; Ivory for the tribunal(shaman only); or Jade for Rallos Zek (shaman only).

And we also get Imbue Valor at some point later in our career (I think 64 maybe) and this is for the high end trade skill stuff of Planes of Power.

Maelarr K'Stan
The only reason druids and shammies get imbues is because that they are regarded as the clergy of their diety. Thus they are all able to imbue the power of their diety into their dieties chosen gem. As a priest of Innoruuk I can ONLY imbue sapphires. I cant imbue plains pebbles, or jades, or emeralds, and Druids/shammies cant Imbue our stuff.

Spell SCROLLS are diety specific, I.E. I can (and have) scribe the 'Imbue Sapphire' Scroll, But not the 'Imbue Plains Pebble' Scroll of the droods, and Vice versa, a druid can scribe imbue plains pebble, but not imbue sapphire.

Kendricke
Greetings,

There are two lines of Imbue Spells.

The first line is religious in nature. These imbue spells are all level 29:

Bertoxxulous (black sapphire) - Gnome Clerics, Human Clerics
Brell Serilis (ruby) - Dwarf Clerics, Gnome Clerics
Bristlebane (peridot) - Gnome Clerics, Halfling Clerics
Cazic Thule (amber) - Erudite Clerics, Iksar Shaman, Troll Shaman
Erollisi Marr (star rose quartz) - Human Clerics
Innoruuk (sapphire) - Dark Elf Clerics, Human Clerics, Troll Shaman
Karana (plains pebbles) - Half Elf Druids, Halfling Clerics, Halfling Druids, Human Clerics, Human Druids
Mithaniel Marr (diamond) - Froglok Clerics, Froglok Shaman, Human Clerics
Prexus (black pearl) - Erudite Clerics
Quellious (topaz) - Erudite Clerics
Rallos Zek (jade) - Ogre Shaman
Rodcet Nife (opal) - Human Clerics
Solusek Ro (fire opal) - Wizards of any race except Frogloks
The Tribunal (ivory) - Barbarian Shaman
Tunare (emerald) - High Elf Clerics, Half Elf Druids, Human Clerics, Human Druids, Wood Elf Druids
Veeshan (fused dragon crystal) - Potters of any race or religion

The second line of imbue spells are often referred to as "raw diamond imbues" or "Planar imbues". All imbues require the components in brackets as well as a raw diamond. These spells are all level 61-65, and must be created:

Gray Diamond of Storms (storm rider blood) - 62 Druid or 62 Enchanter
Red Diamond of Fire (fire mephit blood) - 65 Magician or 65 Enchanter
Green Diamond of Disease (bubonian blood) - 61 Enchanter, 63 Shaman or 63 Necromancer
Yellow Diamond of Valor (metallic liquid) - 63 Enchanter or 64 Cleric
White Diamond of Justice (alkaline etched stone) - 61 Enchanter or 62 Shaman
Black Diamond of Nightmares (nightmare mephit blood) - 62 Enchanter or 62 Necromancer
Purple Diamond of Torment (putrescent blood) - 62 Enchanter or 63 Necromancer
Orange Diamond of War (war wraith blood) - 63 Enchanter or 64 Shaman
Cloudy Diamond of Air (air mephit blood) - 64 Magician or 64 Enchanter
Brown Diamond of Earth (earth mephit blood) - 62 Magician or 62 Enchanter
Aqua Diamond of Water (water mephit blood) - 65 Magician or 65 Enchanter

=================

Lady Lillyy,

Personally, I think you're missing out. I've made more money from my abilities to imbue than with any of my spells - including Aegolism and Reviviscence.

Imbued Emeralds and Plains Pebbles are especially lucrative to tradeskillers due to the Protection of the Cabbage quest items. Imbued Diamonds, Rubies and Sapphires are particularly useful if you happen to be a blacksmith of sufficient level to produce some of the higher end cultural items (i.e. - Heraldic, Underfoot, Regent). Imbued Black Sapphires and Peridots are particularly rare and have a certain demand. Imbued Black Sapphires and Rubies are in particular demand right now since they are used in the new Blue Diamond Tinkered Armors.

The paths aren't always easy or obvious. You can't always find them on a "website". They won't always drop in your lap. It will be difficult and tedious. However, those who are committed and dedicated will prevail.
