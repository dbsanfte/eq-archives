If you find the clerics who sell the spells in the library just hail them and it's pretty obvious which one takes the spells. He's the high elf guy at the end. He has the Blessing spells. The spells sold there are no drop, the parchments are no drop, but AFTER you turn in the parchment the spells from the vendor are NOT nodrop. IE a warrior with a parchment can turn it in to the corresponding NPC for a spell of that class. A friend could get you one of your spells. They are given out randomly afaik.

In the castle in PoI parchments drop alot. You have to do the gnome quest and kill the dragon to get in tho.
