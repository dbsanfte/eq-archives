Qyadar
I feel your pain. I spent a solid 7 hours doing nothing but raising all my skills to above and beyond 170 last nite. It turned my brain to goo doing it. Alot of people told me that it is a waste but Im a perfection freak and couldnt let my self attempt a half @ss combine with 20 less then the trivial when what I needed for skills was right next to me at the vendor.

Smithing and Tailoring am teh Debil as we say in my guild. For tailoring I went the Hopper/Acrylia route. Both crazy expensive on Fennin, up to 80 a block and 200 a skin. Aside from being a semi perfectionist Im also a penny pincher and I realize most others are, usually not by choice.

So what I did was I told my chanter friend that I wouldnt rez him again if he didnt come to help me in DSP. We camped the hopper caves when they were to crowded, he grabbed one of those NPCs over in the near by camp. Dragor Fuax I think is the best one to charm IMO. The neat thing about these lil NPCs is back in the day I guess people were tearing them apart for exp and they are also quest NPCs. Well VI couldnt have that so they gave them an ungodly amount of hps well over 15k. So with this lil bad boy hasted and mini buffed I would proceed into the cave and grab every last hopper exit and DA. When about 30 of them were on me, my chanter friend would start chain stunning. I would drop a few AEs myself to soften them up while The False Dragon (I guess thats his name translated) would tear them a new one. Doing this we could clear the cave in a matter of minutes and I would get about roughly 6 superb hides and plenty of other qualities, a cave load. Do this for several hours and you will have several stacks.

Acrylia is another story. People say TM is a good place to solo but the drop rates there are literally next to nothing. I suggest gathering up some friends and heading over to Acrylia Caverns. If your under lvl 60 you can still get exp, although not good exp, exp none the less. About a 2 to 3 hour session in AC with good killing speed can yield enough for 20 combines. Also i found that the best camp is to the left of zone around the RBs pool. The mobs are easier and only one DB (deathbringers HT for up to 600).

With a Wisdom of 285 at the time was able to get my tailoring to 176 from Wus doing this in less than 2 weeks. With a geerlok and a tailoring skill of 176 I was successful on my first attempt of the 6th shawl and I beleive Im also over the trivials for the other 2.

The Royal kromif blood I found was incredibly easy to get with only a group of above lvl 60 characters. 3 kills and 3 bloods, not very rare. You can invis all the way to the house then single pull the giants to walk inside. If you have a slower and a moderate lvl tank its a cake walk. When we arrived there we were prepared for a decent camp but got all the blood we needed right off the bat so we decided to stay and do the cycle for some Thurgadin Plate drops (Also easy with a decent group).

Well I have to appologizes for whoever just read this post. I left my computer several times while writing and I might have veered off the topic. Oh well... it is the rant and rave section. /smilewink
