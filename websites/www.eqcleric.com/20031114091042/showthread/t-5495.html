Doohickey
about 10 minutes if you go through it quickly.

Go to your home tonw, a message will tell you who to see and where. Go to that person, follow the conversation. This must be done before going to the WayFarer camp, as far as I know.

From there, go to the person they tell you at the camp they tell you (I went to the ECommons camp near the Shady Seller in the tunnels)

That person will ask you questions.

The answers are, not necessarily in this order: Adventure, Favor, Mordin Rasp (spelling?) and farstone

The name, Mordin Rasp, is the guy who started the watfarers and discovered the farstones. All this information is given to you by the person you talk to in your home town...just watch for teh spelling of the name to make sure I didn't muck it up.
