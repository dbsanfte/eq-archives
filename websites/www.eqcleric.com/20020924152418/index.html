    Epic quest nerf in upcoming patch? Posted by: [Jarvis] on 09-14-2002 @ 02:12 AM
    I was told today to expect an "update" to the new addition of the cleric epic quest. Wednesday next week is scheduled to change the spawn time of the Skyfire warder to 5 hours.

    It's also said the shards, dropping from random mobs in Skyfire, will be made an even more rare drop.

    I was expecting some change to this part of epic sooner or later. It seems a bit easy now, compared to before, and perhaps this will level out the difficulty of the epic quest.

    These rumors haven't been confirmed by Verant yet, although I'm sure we'd all appreciate if they stopped by to share any changes to our epic. We'll just have to wait and see if this happens, but it's an interesting nerf...I mean...update.
