KEYERA
at lvl 51 you can turn aa exp on. what it does, instead of normal exp, you decide what % you want to go to aa exp.

there are all differnt aa you can chose from. the first aa most people go for is inate run speed. this makes you run faster all the time, sow will still make you run faster, but with 3 points spent in inate run speed, you now run almost as fast as journymans boots aka J-Boots. and this is permant. even in doors, when sow don't work, run3 still does.

even tho you are lower lvl you can still open the aa exp windows and look at the different ones. if you have the old UI press "I" it will bring up your inventory, then press alternate advancment. then you can view the different aa available for your class.

another thing about aa exp, is once you earn aa exp, even if you die, it don't go down. it also lvls up faster than normal exp.

hope that helps.
