Jarvis
Here's the quick and dirty walkthrough to receive your copy of the Temperance spell...

First of all. These spells are nodrop, and you have to be a cleric for the quest guy to even talk to you. The quest seems to take place only in the new zones, so make your way out there.

The quest guy is named Millivs Darkwater and he hangs out near the lighthouse in Gunthak. He's an Erudite Paladin.

He will give you the quest when hailed.

What he wants is "a Deepwater emblem". It looks like a ring. The drop can be gotten in the Dulak Harbor zone, and it's assumed random drop from any mob in the zone, but I have heard it drops off "a Stormwave deck hand" in this zone. Just kill everything until you get it. /cool

Once you have the Deepwater emblem go back to Millivs Darkwater and hand it in to him. He will give you "Alina's Emblem" and tell you to take it to alina's spirit.

The spirit you seek is actually called "an Erudite Spirit" and it spawns randomly on the beach in Gunthak. You get to the beach by jumping in the water and swimming into on of the caves. I belive this is a random spawn and it will not be KOS to you. Everything else is though, so watch your back. All greenies to me at lvl 63, but very aggressive and it's easy to cause a train, so move with caution.

Once you turn in "Alina's Emblem" to "an Erudite Spirit" you will get your copy of Temperance and a tad experience!

Good luck to you clerics, and let us know when you get your spell!!

Rawnlissa
I wrote this up for my guild, a couple of days after LoY was released. I hope you all do not mind me sharing it:
I have had many people comment that this spell is useless. /boggle Therefore; I thought I would put all Temperance info in one place.

At level 44, you can use a spell that cost one peridot to cast. This spell will give you an additional 800 hit points to your total hit points, heal for 800 HP and add an additional 48 to your armor class. Yes, if you cast Resolution, Symbol of Naltron and Armor of Faith you would have a few more HP, but less AC.

You would use 3 buff slots, instead of just one. When you are grouped with a druid and/or a shaman, your tanks will love you for saving more room for other buffs.

The only spells that over write this are the spells from the Aego line. It will stack with other HP/AC buffs from other classes up to 1100 HP total.

You can use it on anyone and not have to worry about a group version or it taking more than one Peridot.

No, it will not stack with our symbol line, but why would you want to use 2 gems to buff one person at one time?

Yes to the 54 + Cleric, this spell *may* be useless. But when you look at the fact that it uses only one slot instead of 3 it still has its merits on raids and in the right groups when buff space is important. After all, you want your celestial heal to land, right?

With a duration of 1 hour and 40 minutes, it is worth every bit of mana it takes to cast it.

I have not tried casting it for donations, but I think it might be worth my time to try. I will keep you posted on that.

The quest, itself is pretty straight forward and easy. Just time consuming.

1) Head over to Gunthak and speak to the Eurdite Paladin Darkwater.

2) Swim to the south from him to the beach. Follow the right wall to the tunnel. Have someone with you to cast invis or have invis potions. Some of the mobs see invis, so be careful.

3) Follow the tunnel, until the fork. Take the first fork to the raider village. There seem to be more of the dark old world races at this village, but when we were there, it was heavily camped. We killed mobs at the entrance for about 30 minutes. XP was slow. One deepwater emblem dropped during that time. Yes, this was in Gunthak, not Dulak’s. I know the emblem also drops in Dulak’s but I cannot say exactly where.

4) Take the emblem back to Darkwater. He will give you Alina’s emblem.

5) Take it to the beach at approximately neg 847 neg 675. There is a skeleton place holder. Keep killing it until a female erudite spirit pops. Give her the emblem and she will give you the spell and some XP. At 48, I dinged 49 off killing light blues for amusement, and got a little over a blue of XP from the turn in. The hull that she pops by has pops on both sides. The other side has random pops, the side she pops on only has skeletons and her. As you are approaching from the beach, she is on the left side of the hull. (I camped the wrong side for a while. /blush)
