Galv
Here's some easy ways to make some pp and xp with your cleric, regardless of race (so long as they are one of the good guys). Go to halas and kill the bears, skellies, and spiderlings. Save the pelts and silks and sell them for the appropriate price in the Bazaar (which range from 1-20pp or more, depending on the server). Save all your bonechips.

Now, with the bonechips you have a choice: 1) sell them for a pp or a little less in the bazaar or 2) use them for xp.

If you choose xp, do the bonechip quest in kaladim. I forget the npc's name, but he is near the pally guild. He asks for 4 chips and in return you get faction, xp, coin, and a minor item. You can very easily shoot your cleric up multiple levels by this quest--up to about level 10 or so. While I didn't do it with my cleric, I did do it with my Barb warrior. Pop made it very very easy with the books. And, by doing a ghetto port (remember, you return to bind with all your equipment at 10 and under) travel is easy.
