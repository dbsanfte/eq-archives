salvatorus
Well if you want to lvl fast bro, my suggestion is to head to Plane of Justice. You will proabbly be able to find groups there quite often. If no groups there then you can Try Plane of innovation, nightmare, or diesease. At these levels thou a Slower is a must and 55+ tank HIGHly recommended. So i would lvl up in PoJ till 53 then move on to the other zones. Only bad thing is theres no reall lewt in the stuff you will be fighting. Theres the occasional diamond and a nice necklace in Plane of innovation. If you looking for lewt well theres lots of places you could go. Seb, KC if your despreate, Madiens eye which has some nice items in it(got a +15 wis range item there), and if you can bring your own group you can do places like Acrylia caverns, the deep lots of places at this lvl. Me thou i spend most of my time in the planes not really intrested in lewt atm and the Exp is super nice there and Lots of groups looking for clerics. I spend on average 5-10 min LFG in planes. Good luck and have fun.
