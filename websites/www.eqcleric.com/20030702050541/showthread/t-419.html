I have an RL Friend that just reactivated after a 15 month layoff.

First I would take a serious look at whether to keep this toon or start over.

When you left Wis was capped at 200 and it was semi difficult to get there. So the setup for the cleric was pump all points into Wis. Leaving us very vunerable in some other areas. Currently Wis is capped at 255 and can go above that with AA points. There are a lot of very cheep items and it's much easier to to get your wis capped than it used to be. OH AN THERES A NEW DRUG CALLED "KEI", think of it as "Uber Clarity". It adds a bunch of Wis. Not sure how much because it pushes my Wis to the cap, but I think its around 25 wis. As a cleric it's going to pretty much expected that when you join a group, that you have a relitivly fresh Extended KEI. And with PoP, its a pretty quick run to go get a refresh. Make sure you are getting enough loot to pay for the KEI.

****edit****
If you are intent on soloing, you will want to seriously consider spending the PP for a KEI, at your level, it just about means no downtime to med. You will make more in cash in a much shorter time.

Isig
I'd say keep the toon. No use in rerolling to attempt the "perfect blend" of abilities. Let's face it, maxing out isn't easy until you get to the high-end game. If you plan on going all the way, maybe you reconsider. If you're not sure, just play what you've got. I have not found it that easy to max out WIS at lower levels. I have a 25 cleric who has 165 in WIS. I don't have money, I don't have higher level mains that can twink me, nor does my guild work hard to twink their lower level members. If this is the case for you, aquiring WIS is your top priority.

I'd suggest maybe researching your newb quest armors for specific pieces (rarely will a full suit be as beneficial as mixing and matching). I got a set of the arm guards (good WIS and decent AC) and boots (same reason) if you are a dorf. Get yourself a pair of Guantlets of the black. I saw a pair in the bazaar just last night for 150pp. Not a bad price. Get an Othmir fur cap (+6 WIS for about 50pp?). See if you can get a set of Adamentite Pauldrons (shoulders, +7 or +9 to WIS, can't remember even though I'm wearing a set and price might be too steep, but not sure). Basically, take a look at my magelo. I'm not saying I'm optimal, but except for my bracers, everything else is achievable for you. Oh, and look for anyone selling Acrylia studded/re-enforced stuff cheap. Every once in a while you'll see someone dumping their tailored items. It's not common for anyone to dump them, but if they do, most of those pieces have good WIS too and fair AC.

That's my take on it.
