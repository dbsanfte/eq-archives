Jarvis
Having a steady moneyflow is important for the average cleric. Once you get up in levels you need money for dots, emeralds, ports, KEI ... you name it.

So unless you fall butt first into money all the time, spend mad time selling off master tradeskill items, or get lucky some other way, you're gonna spend some time farming to keep that pp stash at a tolerable level.

So I started this thread hoping we could share some farming spots and help out our fellow clerics in need of money...:)

Penecillin
In my recent past (Level 7-19) I farmed spider silk in the Field of Bone. The pit has two spider Mobs: Bonecrawlers and Bon Binders. And there are yet more Bonebinder spawns outside Kurn's Tower. Both drop from 0-4 Spider Silks each plus the occasional poison sack. It certainly doesn't take long to farm up a stack.

Also there are lots and lots of bone chips Mobs in the FoB and Kurn's Tower.

Further back, (Level 1-6) I farmed around Kaladim newbie area. Pristine Skunk Claws go for 4pp each with the local traders as do Goblin heads. These are both quest drops too though and the goblin heads only seem to drop once.

Level 19-22 I've been in Paudal Caverns bandit camping. They drop 0-2pp each.

Every cleric seems to love Tower of the Frozen Shadow (ToFS). 5th floor. Farm the ushers for weapons, pp, and Velium (blocks sell for 12pp or so to vender or 50pp in bazaar, pieces sell for 15pp each in bazaar). Farm the wedding party for weapons, pp and the ocassional Ring, which are lore and sell for 1500pp or so in bazaar. Crystilized SHadows stacks and sells for about 32pp per stack. I can average about 200pp an hour here easily, with C3. Recommend LvL 49 for 5th floor. 3rd floor is doable at lvl 44 solo, just be careful of the Armored Shadows.

SolA.. All Greenies. Most goblins drop some kind of FS, pp and alot of them drop HQ Ore. HQ Blocks sell for 10pp to vender or 30pp in bazaar. You will ocassionally get some named drakes, or the Kobold Predator, which drop bazaar junk. I can average about 140pp an hour here with C3. These turn green around lvl 39 I think, but please dont quote me on that.

Crystal Caverns: Geonids if not being farmed. I can solo them with hammer pet fairly easily, every named one drops a gem worth between 40 and 180pp. I have made as much as 1k in 3 hours there before, solo. I also do Rezzes there for an average of 30pp each donation. Everyone wants rez there because of the long CR to get there. I make them drag to me so I dont loose the camp. I was unable to effectively solo these at 44, but at 52, I could effectively lay waste to the run, but they would already be respawned at bottom. At 54, I have med time between respawns.

PoK... Rez farming. I have made probably 5k doing rezzes while doing tradeskills. Best was when a group needed Chardok rez.. They summoned to entrance, and I did 7 rezzes for 600pp. Then buffed the group and gated back to Pok.. just to go out for another 3 man rez at CT Entrance for 200pp.. not bad for never being in danger, if you ask me. Most in PoK want 90% or better rez.

Galv
Here's an easy one that doesn't put you in danger at all: make arrows. It seems as if there are many high level warriors out there who do not have a fletching skill, so the lowbies can make some very good pp by simply making arrows. My alt tank, when he was low level, was running quite a market by making class 2 wood arrows with 150 range. He sold em for about 3-5gp each;therefore making a handful of pp per stack. While it is not the fastest way to make pp, for a low level toon, it is very easy and very safe. BTW, he never had a buff on him and easily raised his fletching to the mid 80s in a handful of hours.

After you get your fletching up to an acceptable level, spend about 15 minutes or so an evening making some arrows, leave your toon on in the bazaar and watch as you slowly--but steadily--bring in 100s and eventually 1000s of pp over the course of a couple of weeks.

Phixin
Another thing I do is to take my baking along with me when I go farm a single mob. Mobs usually take about 2 min to kill, that gives me 4-6 min average of downtime to regen mana and work on combines. I grab my spit (havent gotten a collapseable smoker yet) and all the stuff I need to make 80 Halas Pies, then go camp some slow spawn mob, like AC in OOT, Gornit in OOT, Oowomp in TD, or Lord Begurgle (amazing that you can bake underwater,,, havent figured that one out yet), or any number of single spawn mobs you can kill for either loot or Exp. Between kills, I do my pre-combines getting ready for the final combines in the oven. It averages out to about 9 combines per attempt at one Halas 10 Pound Pie combine, so you are looking at 700+ combines just to get ready to make 80 Halas pies attempts. 80 attempts has the possibility to make 480 Halas pies, which I sell for 4pp each, or 55pp a stack.

You could do this with fletching, tinkering or Tailoring as well. Dont know if there is any portable pottery wheel or brew barrells.. sorry.

Oh, for Tailoring.. Make yourself TONS of heady Kiolas (brewing, triv (42?)), take a sewing kit, and as many stacks of Mask patterns as you have heady Kiola to Upper Guk. Camp the spiders there. They are on I think a 20 min spawn. They drop lots of silk. Combine 2 spidersilks for a swatch, then combine 1 swatch, 1 mask pattern, 1 heady kiola. Fail, rinse repeat. I destroy any masks I actually create. They take up too much space and sell for very little. If you run out of silk and are FM again, and alone in zone, run to the spider bridge, kill the four there, gather a few more silks, go back to your camp and start over. Cured silk masks will triv around 82(?) or so. You can also get Chitin Shield's to give away while you are at it.

AnsalonFV
In Qeynos hills, there are some NPCs around a campfire near the SFG zone. One of which gives a quest in Blackburrow that requires you kill a gnoll courier (roughly level 35 - doesnt aggro unless attacked). The courier drops 4 lore items (cant remember the names of the items) - but he spawns at the entrance in Blackburrow (at the zone leading out to Qeynos hills) every 13 minutes. Collect all 4 items and return them to the quest npc and receive a 1hb Gnollbasher that sells for 200p each.

The quest can be done in roughly an hour with virtually no risk - take some tradeskill stuffs along and buff/heal the newbies hunting inside blackburrow while waiting the spawn.

Granted this isn't the most ideal farm - but it is easy, allows you to tradeskill, chat, etc.
