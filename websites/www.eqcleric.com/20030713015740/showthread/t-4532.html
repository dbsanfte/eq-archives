Phixin
My experience is that some weapons do Proc more than others, and yes, Dex has a major factor in how often it procs as well. The differance is very noticable. I go from procing maybe once a mob, to 2 or 3 procs per mob, with my opening swing seeming to proc OFTEN.

Dex does help, as it's a plus to the base proc amount.

Not maxxed on dex yet, so I generally proc Avatar first, if I am meleeing, then either use Hammer of Zek, or Dark Mace, depending on the mob.
