Restandre
Keys go onto your keyring if the use of them directly takes you somewhere else. Befallen keys open doors, the Paineel key operates the elevator. ToFS keys warp you to a different part of the zone, so they are keyed.
The only exception to the rule that I can remember finding is that the Mark of Justice, which you get from doing all six trials in PoJ for access to the Seventh Hammer, does not key. I can count on one hand the number of people I know have this though.
