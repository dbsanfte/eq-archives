Hannorel
I just recently went through this ordeal. It may be coincidental, but both times I was after Begurgle (more on that in a bit), he spawned shortly after the tainted/corrupted had been killed.

The way I did it was have my puller swim down and pull the gobbies up on land. An EB item obviously preculdes having to worry about getting a mage to summon you some items, or bringing along an EB casting class. I lent the puller my Fishbone Earring; if you can get a ranger (self wolf form) or a druid with group wolf form to help you, wolf form does help reduce adds.

The first time I attempted this camp, I got lucky. Lord Begurgle spawned after about 2 hours of pulling and waiting. Unfortunately, Shmendrik Lavawalker wasn't spawned at that time. The next day, I checked on ole Shmendrik, and sure enough, he's up. I gather some guildies, and we get set up. I give him the crown, and Natasha spawns on schedule. She walks over, they have their verbal spat, and she procceds to wax him. Unfortunately, she was the closest to the spirit when it spawned, so it aggroed on her. Our group did the most damage, but she got the final kill. ;(

Soooo, I had to do the Begurgle camp again, and of course, this time it took closer to 5 hours to get him to show his ugly mug. Once I got that crown, I gathered some more guildies, and tried the method whereby you attack Shmendrik, then kill the spirit after it spawns. We killed Shmendrik easily enough; we hit a glitch when the spirit simply despawned about a minute into the fight. I'm guessing SOE fixed that loophole in the quest.

The next time I attempted that portion, I once more gathered some folks, and I gave the crown to Shmendrik. I made sure I was standing pretty much right up on him as he died. The spirit aggroed on me upon spawning, and Natasha stayed out of the fight. I got the damaged crown, gave it to Natasha, and, well, that's all the pertinent info for Begurgle.

Schmendrik takes a few hours to respawn, so if you do this, kill him before the camp so he'll (hopefully) be back up when you get back.
