Nosk
Personally, AA progression is dependant on a couple factors..

#1 Play Style - As Every one of us has differant play styles it's important to think in the terms, "I want all my aa's but wich do i want/need first"?

#2 Focus Items and Equipment - Do I need to spend 12 AA's on Healing Adept? or do I have an IH 4 Item?

#3 Common Spells Cast - Am I a CH Only Cleric? or perhaps A Heal over Time Junkie? or a mix?

#4 Do you prefer - NPC Pet groups or Dules, or regular 6 person groups. or even solo xp?

With a combination of these 4 basic things you can paint your own picture as to whats important and what order should be done first, Because in all truth everyones differant.

In My case, at the time, I had an IH 4 item, As well as an extended buff item. However I prefered to solo with chanters and necros, as well as raid duitys. I Lived in CoD playing with Undead and well had my fair share of deaths. So In my case after SCM. I got MGB, MC 3, Divine Arb 3, Radiant Cure 1, Innate IVU, HG 3, AHG 3, CF 3, Radiant Cure 2, SCR 3, SCRM, HA 3, Divine Rez, Radiant Cure 3, LR 5 ID 5. Ect Ect.

So To answer your question, You will need to decide on what abilities you can wait for, what ones you will really use and what order do you want them in.

The most important thing when spending AA's is DONT WORRY ABOUT TITLES!!! Be smart with the aa's you train for, dont just train in Archtype or Class to get Exarch at 42 aa's Get the important stuff... then worry about what your name is.. :) In my case I had 125+ aa's befor I was an Exarch. And I know atleast 2 clerics with 200+ aa's that are still not 3rd title.

Most Highly Desired AA's imo

Mental Clarity 3 - To increase FT to 18.
Divine Arbitration 3 - Sweet "Oh $hit spell" It WILL save your life!
Radient Cure 3 - Get rid of nasty dots ect.
Planar Power / Innate Enlightenment 5 - Raise when you get the equipment to raise your stats from 280-305 / 355.
Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery - 50pct extended buffs, 41 second HoT's, MGB 41sec EE.
MGB - Why bother with Group spells when you can gass the country side.. :)

Medium Desire

Combat Agility / Combat Stability - Reduce the amount of damage taken, Great if you get beat up.
Healing Gift / Adept - Also Very nice, Although can be substituted with Focus Items.
Celestial Regeneration - After changes rank 1, = 250, 2 = 275 and 3 = 300 point HoT that is MGB'able.
Divine Res - After changes this is a great ability and basically gives you one free accident per day.
Innate IVU - Very handy if you like to play in undead zones.
Unfailing Divinity - personally dont like, would rather not let my tank die then spend 12 aa's but most like for Death Pact spells.

Most Useless AA's

Anything thats says "Hastened XXXX" - Divinity, Purfication and Turning.
Purify Soul - Not much point with RC 3 if you keep Words active. Else its not bad. but useless for me.
Turn Undead - Granted I dont have this ability, the fear part makes it quite useless against "raid" mobs.
Bestow DA - Comes in handy about once every 3 weeks... especially after changes to DA spells.
Natural Durability - Because this is based on BASE HP and well cleric HP's suck, your 12 AA's will net you about 300 extra HP... Whoopie.. put those 12 aa's into something else first :)

Most Underrated AA's

Channeling Focus - This is a GODLY ability for us clrs that get beat up once and a while, IE: when raid mobs rampage on your but, you can take quite a few hits and still get your CH off. (personal best is 17 hits and still got CH off) granted I died, but MT lived so alls good :) Also Extreamly good if you Tank.

Spell Casting Fury - Granted cleric nukes suck compaired to most classes, with this maxed you can do som pretty nice damage in excess of 3k.

New Tanaan crafting - Believe it or not, by completing all 6 ranks and upping all of your trade skills to 220+ you can do some pretty NICE quests.. IE +25 to all/all +150 mana/hp earing with FT 3 and 15 pct MP. If your board, drop some booze and up your trade skills. (not to mention shawl quest as well)

Lightning Reflex 5 - This is a godly AA ability, If you can afford the 12 points for CS and 15 for LR 5 you will notice a very nice differance in the number of "hits" taken.

Innate Defence 5 - This reduces the amount of damage taken per hit.. Again costs 27 aa's but is WELL worth it.

Again, Everyones choices will be differant. However I can tell you with AA's such as Channeling Focus 3, LR 5, ID 5. and a good mix of Equipment and skill clerics can TANK level 65 mobs in HoH or Sol Ro.. (and I mean TANK.. not just get hit, but dish out some damage and HOLD agro. With 2 stuns and a nuke you can hold agro about the same as a pally. (granted best to do with a sub healer) it makes for a nice change :)

imo the break down is:

Equipment 10pct
Skill and ability 60 pct
AA abilities 30 pct.

Hope this helps, GL in what ever choices you make.

I stuck the following post in our long-standing AA discussion thread. It was an edit of an old and useless post of mine, in the middle of the thread, so I doubt anyone has even noticed it, but since it seems to be appropriate here, I'll cut-and-paste it:



As the post after this states, you need to figure out what matters to you, so I got rid of the ordered list that had been here. Instead, I'm rewriting this to state my values of abilities.


General
Innate Run Speed: 10.
Innate Metabolism: ? - for people with baked goods, which is not me
Innate Regen: 4 - what anyone else should take for 4-6 imho
resist increases: 3 - after you have *everything* else you actually want
Innate Lung Capacity: 1, unless you can't run to Ssra safely
stat increases: 1
Tanaan Craft Mastery: ZERO - friends don't let friends do tradeskills (imho )

Archetype
Spell Casting Mastery: 10
Mental Clarity: 1st level 8, 2nd level 7, third level 6
Natural Durability: 8
Combat Agility: 7
Healing Adept: 6
Spell Casting Reinforcement: 6
Healing Gift: 5, or the value of a class ability you need it for
Combat Stability: 5
Spell Casting Fury: 1st level 7 for fun, 4 otherwise
Channeling Focus: 1

Class
Bestow Divine Aura: 9
Mass Group Buff: 9 for raiding, 1 if not
Celestial Regeneration: 7
SCR Mastery: 6
Innate IVU: 4
Purify Soul: 2
Divine Resurrection: 2
Turn Undead: 1, unless you think it will be fun (and it can be very rarely)

PoP Adv
Innate Enlightenment: 8 while your wisdom is capped or might be soon, 1 when not
Planar Power: See above. Hopefully you won't have other stats capped before your wisdom.
resist increases: 3
stat increases: 1

PoP Abil
Radiant Cure: 1st level 9, later 7 if you ever needed to remove a curse and 4 if not
Divine Arbitration: 1st level 9, later 5
Lightning Reflexes: 7
Unfailing Divinity: 7 if you cast DI for raids, 0 if not
Celestial Renewal: 6
Adv. Healing Adept: 6
Adv. Healing Gift: 5
Innate Defense: 5
Fury of Magic: 3
Hastened Purification: 1
Hastened Turning: 1
Hastened Divinity: whatever you yourself rate BDA 1-10 after you have it


Summary:
10 - RS3, Regen 3, SCM3 (18 points)
9 - BDA, MGB, RC1, DvA1 (+20, 38 total)
8 - MC1, ND3, IE5, PP5 (+39, 77)
7 - MC2, CA3->LR5, SCF1, HG3->CR1, RC3, UD3 (+72, 149)
6 - MC3, HA3, SCR3->SCRM, CR3, AHA3, HD3, TU1 (+65, 204)
5 - CS3->ID5, DvA3, AHG3 (+42, 246)
4 - Meta 3, SCF3, IIVU (+16, 262)
3 - resist increases (+75, 337)
2 - FoM3, stat increases (+123, 480)
1 - ILC3, CF3, TU3, HP3, HT3, TCM6 (+60, 540)



This is what I wrote a couple months ago. I've found need to reprioritize this slightly; I've skipped some stuff to do resists, and a couple abilities have been changed, but I think it's a reasonably decent list. (Others' arguments to follow, no doubt. A lot of this is personal opinion.)

And fwiw, I don't follow my own advice too well. 250 AA and still no healing adept or adv healing gift!
