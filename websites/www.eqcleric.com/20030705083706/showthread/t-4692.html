Shadowfrost
I can see a lot of places where dungeons can be added.

For example:

The blocked tunnel in the Nexus next to the Plane of Knowledge book has finally been broken open! If you do the quest for the Mighty Shovel of Brell, you can click on the rockfall with the shovel and be taken to a new place, although nobody knows as yet what you will find there.

Behind the throne in the King Room in Lower Guk is a teleporter. At the moment, it takes you back to the surface; but a froglok wizard has discovered the secret of its operation, and now it takes you to a new place called Lost Guk. At the moment, Lost Guk is stuffed with horrid undead creatures and the Guk frogloks are crying out for adventurers to help them clear it out...

Meanwhile, in the depths of Sebilis is another teleporter which is presently called Lower Zone Out. Using the same techniques on this teleporter you can move to Lower Sebilis, where the Deep Frogloks are hatching a plot to expand out onto the surface...

At the top of the tower in the Oasis of Marr, a third teleporter has been discovered. This teleporter takes you to the ruins of the ancient Elven city which is buried beneath the Desert of Ro.

In the depths of Neriak Third Gate, there is a tunnel which apparently leads into another zone but has been blocked off by a wall. (Yes, really there is, you can see through the wall if you sit down in the right place.) Recent mining has removed that wall, which takes you into the Palace of Queen Christianos Thex.

Get the idea? :)
