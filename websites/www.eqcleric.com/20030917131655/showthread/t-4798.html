Dowel_Fearn
Oh I certainly Agree. I was just curious to see if most others concidered this just another EXP race Game, a dungeon crawl/ Diablo type game or more?

They have done, IMHO, a great job in giving the individual player alot of different "paths" to take while playing the game. I know some players who simply play to "Beat" the game (gain as much exp as fast and as much as posible).. others who play soley for the Role-play aspect and others who simply like to perform the quests and the social interaction.

Personaly, I take advantage of ALL of those aspects and figure I get everything I can from playing.

Braeaelin
Since Galv's posting on the Sleeper, I have become quite fascinated with the whole story line. I've spent some time digging up information from other's experiences and looking at that. Then you look at all the other back stories that exist for other zones/races/classes or whatever throughout the game. It's all pretty in depth and very interesting!

To me, experience is only a small portion of the game. Then you have tradeskills and recipes, exploration of zones, quests, friends to speak with. I could go on but you guys know where I'm going.

The thing is: there's always something to do to suit the mood you're in. I was going to go out and get a group tonight, but I ended up falling asleep by accident on the bed. So when I got up I was too groggy to play so I just explored some zones and chatted with a friend.

Oh, so throw exploration in there:) When I first started playing that was the number one thing to do for me.
