I remember Speed Racer, Battle of the Planets, Ultra Man (true, not a cartoon), Starblazers and Robotech. I also remember Thundarr the Barbarian, Space Ghost (before he went "Coast-to-Coast"), the Herculoids, Super Friends, etc, etc.

I also know why the little kid that talked funny on Battle of the Planets talked that way...think deleted expletive and you'll have the picture.

Starblazers was my absolute fave, and would rush home from school to catch it. I was totally bummed that only two of the series made it to American TV. I remember being thrilled when I got to see some of the scenes that were cut from the American release at a Sci-Fi/Comic convention in Philly. My brother, cousins and I had our own nicknames for the main characters: "Childstar", "No-bra", "Eager Beaver", "Sandbox" and "IQ Zero".

Robotech also rules, but I could gladly have put up with a lack of Minmay in that series...what an irritating little....

All of the cartoons mentioned are/were classics, but none are shown on Cartoon Network (which is what was being polled about).
