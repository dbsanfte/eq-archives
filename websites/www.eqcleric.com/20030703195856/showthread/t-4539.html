Phixin
Cool. Too bad it wasnt the Teachers Sylibus.. but still something for twinking an alt or selling in bazaar or something. 3rd floor is a blast. I had a lot of fun killing that floor off.

My strategy if you are interested is to go right from the entrance. Kill the Armored Shadow that is waiting there for you. He is not undead, darnit. About half way thru the fight, the second will probably add.. so be ready..ie... full mana before clicking on the mirror. Pacify doesnt work on them as there is no room to aviod bumping into them as you walk by. Once they are dead, still moving right from 3rd floor entrance.. hug the left wall so as not to agro the gnolls in the next room you come to.. follow the wall to the left at the corner, then go to 2nd door on the right. Kill the zombie butler dudes there. The doors on the back of the room lead to two more zombie's. Kill them and set up camp. The mirror in this room is your way out. From the room where the 2 butlers were, there is a hidden passage to the 2 rooms with the gnolls you passed going in. Those passages help avoid Armored Shadow adds. The enraged cook can pop out of anything on the floor.

Happy hunting.
