Braeaelin
In a raid that goes sour and the raid leader tells the clerics to camp so they can log in at a later time to resurrect the fallen, is there any standard time to wait before logging in again to make sure the area is clear of MoBs so you don't end up getting killed? Is EQIM used at all for the clerics to communicate between each other while out of the game? Does the main cleric tell the other clerics how long to wait before logging back in while camping? Is it just a straight forward risk to log back in?

I would assume five RL minutes would be a reasonable delay, but I wanted to hear what clerics that have experienced this situation have to say.

Taroith
Usually for me in a raid type atmosphere when dealing with large numbers if a cleric is told to camp, he does so then logs on an alt. The alt char then waits for the FD types to let clerics know its ok to log back in.

One thing I have noticed is that they would rather wait a couple extra minutes because if all your cleric force gets wiped then its a pain in the arse to get them back to said spot, especially if your deep in a dungeon or zone.

Just my 2 cents.

Shadowfrost
With pickup raids, you generally get clerics camping either to alts or to a chat channel. With guild raids, I've found you tend to get the clerics talking to each other by phone. :)

Restandre
I've only ever witnessed players camping to alts waiting for the go-ahead. Problem is, sometimes nobody tells you anything and you give up waiting and just log in, and then completely screw things up by reaggroing the mobs just as they were leaving or some such thing.
The thing I hate about camping is that unless something goes horribly wrong, rarely is a call made to camp; you have to use your judgement. Oftentimes I've not camped when I thought I should because I wanted to try to help and hoped somebody else did it and then we wipe, and sometimes I've camped out when I mis-perceived the situation, and I hear people carrying on with the raid as normal without so much as a rez-rebuff period and I look pretty stupid.

Heelar
When told to camp, log on an alt and rejoin the chat channels, and wait for the FDers to tell you when it's safe to log back in. Nobody's gonna forget about you hehe. As for when to camp.. that's the raid leader's call. Camp as soon as he says, don't wait a single second, or, if the raid leader is down, then it becomes a cleric-judgment call. If you know there's no hope, tell the raid you're camping and do it.. unless they yell at you not to of course.
