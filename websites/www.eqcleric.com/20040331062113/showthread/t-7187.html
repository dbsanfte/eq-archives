I've been doing alot of LDON mission in Mistmoore, trying to get the flowing thought II item with my AP (getting close now). I have seen radical differences in the amount of loot received on various missions. In my opinion, the best chance you have to get stiffed on loot is to take a low number collect mission. On those, it seems that the only thing that drops is the collect item. No plat and very few augs.

But, I'm not sure that there is a general nerf that has taken place. Just last night I did a mission with a full group and the split was 160pp each. We did stay until we got kicked out by the timer. That might be the deciding factor. Many groups run through a mission in 40 minutes then leave the dungeon. In that situation, I suspect that the split would be on the lean side.

Even though I've been ripped off by a few MLs in LDON adventures, I've found that most people who are willing to take the thankless job are good folks who are honest and want everyone to have the maximum split. Unless it was obvious that you were had by an unscrupulous bastage, reserve judgement on your ML and give them the benefit of the doubt. You can always monitor the battle spam during the mission to see what the ML is looting. If you see 6 blue diamonds being picked up, then the split is only 25pp each, you know you have a thief on your hands.
