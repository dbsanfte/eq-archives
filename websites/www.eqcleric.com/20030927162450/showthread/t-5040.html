Shadowfrost
The entrance to Chardok B is situated between the bank and the vendor, very close to the waterfall, on the same side of the waterfall as the herbalist house. It is a small lever in the wall in an alcove.

Points to note:-

* Unlike the chokidai in Chardok A, the chokidai in Chardok B are undead, except for the domesticated ones.
* Faction hits at zone in are + to Brood of Di'Zok; deeper in, on live critters, they are - to Brood of Di'Zok, and rather buggy (sometimes giving positive and negative hits to the same faction).
* The chokidai are not an easy solo (your target is immune to changes in its run speed).
* The other undeads can be soloed with ease, dropping minor quest parts, a few pp, and 1%-2% AAexp.
* We encountered nothing in the whole zone which was remotely a challenge to a reasonably geared group of level 65's.
* Experience was decent but not as good as a good planar group.
* Out-of-whack drop for the day were Spirit Masters War Beads, from a Rabid Chokidai, stats: MAGIC LORE, ALL/ALL, recommended level 55, neck slot, HP25 Mana 25, Flowing Thought II. The critter that dropped them was no tougher than the standard yard trash.
* The same critter also dropped boots, all plate/chain/leather classes, AC8 STA15 HP40, no recommended level (the boots seemed a far more common drop).
* There are rooms with Sarnak Ressurectors which appear to be a ring event. We failed to figure out details of the event.
* There are three named skeletons which are quest critters. One of them accepts four staves of suffering, one accepts four parts of a sword, and one of them accepts four pages; discussions of the quests are on the Safehouse.
