Aguth
Others have given good advice on spots already but just going to give you advice on how to solo more efficiently. Get your AC up, get a nice high dmg high delay 1h blunt and a haste item, pull with DoT then melee and nuke. Don't waste mana on roots or stuns. Hunt undead as much as posible as your nukes are more mana efficient that way. Until level 50 you can solo this way just fine if you build yourself on AC rather than wisdom. After 50 it gets a bit rougher.
