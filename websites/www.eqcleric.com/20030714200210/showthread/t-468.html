Mattress
You can always try the entrance of Befallen. Just hang in the hallway next to the zoneline and grab any skellies in your line of sight. Only thing to be cautious of is a Rat add. Worst case, just zone out.

LADYGUINEAPIG
Kurn's Tower. PC might be the fastest exp in the game, but this is the easiest. I sent my necro there at lvl 9 and my cleric at lvl 10.

Unless you go to the basement with the big teddy bears you will be facing all undead mobs!!! So just root and nuke. This is also a good place to work on your defensive and offensive skills. The monk skele's hit you often to help defense and there are lots of spots where you won't get adds if you feel like swinging your weapon a bit.

The loot there is pathetic, but at lvl 10-15 loot should not be on anyone's mind yet.

Phixin
Lady is all over it. Kurns baby. Kurns. With my necro, I did lvl 10 to 20 in one day...under 9 hours. All solo. Agreed on loot, it sucks. But, save your bone chips. Sell to necro's in PoK. At 5pp a stack, you will probably sell them pretty quick. Not great money, but you will leave Kurns with alot of bone chips.

Phixin
Kurns is in Field of Bone. On the north east end of the pit. Take the Cabilis stone out of PoK, run north to the pit wall, then either run the edge of the pit around until you see the tower, or just run north passed the dragon skeleton until you see the tower. Entrance is outside the pit. Take the ramp up the side. You can bind at the top of the ramp. Then zone in and commence destruction. There are usually a bunch of Iksar in there, Monks, SK's, Necro's and warriors. You can either partner with one, or just solo the crap out of the place. With KEI, you will have little or no downtime, unless you have massive +Mana twinkage, which is excellent until you hit level 20 when +wis starts to count more.

Maanen
Just a quick add to the Kurns directions. Run to the pit, then follow outside pit to your left. At lvl 10, theres some nasty suprises to the right if you arn't careful, which if you knew the zone would not be so bad, but for first time there, better to go left to kurns. Also dont jump off rim of pit onto ramps. Only due to dmg, since the pit rim is elevated and the ramps are declinated, the amount of fall dmg increases subsatially, the closer to the rim.

I was going to run through to Kurns but the Overthere is so awesome for xp imo that I stayed there and got another 3 levels in last nights sesh :)

I have been killing yellows and reds quite easily using this method and it maxed out all my combat skills.
