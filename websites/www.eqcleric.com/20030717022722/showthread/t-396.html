Solved this quest this morning.

Millius Darkwater needs proof of what happened to Alina and mentions that clerics were required to wear a Deepwater Pendant.

A Deepwater Symobl: Found off ranodom pirates (marauders, pillagers, etc.) at the fort in Gulf of Gunthak, and also off the crew of the boats in Harbor of Dulak. Got mine off the crewmen in Dulak as they seem to drop it more often.

Turn in Deepwater Symbol to Millius Darkwater and he gives you back the symbol now named Alina's Ring.

He then tells you that she has definately perished and that her spirit needs to be freed and mentions her spirit is nearby.

On the beach with the wrecked ships at loc neg840, neg 675 "An Erudite Spirit" spawns. Run there and turn in the ring. And you get Spell:Temperance.

Good luck all. :)
