Phixin
Kurns Tower. Fastest EXP in game from 16 to 20 I think. I did 10 to 20 with my necro twink(minor) in just under 9 hours. Yep, one long session and I gained 10 Levels. I did use C3 the entire time, but exp was amazing fast. A cleric with C3 should be able to solo there extremely well, but if you can get a monk or SK partner, you will move up the level ladder like mad. Buy a stack of agies to keep Center and Symbol buffs going.Bind just outside the zone. Load Expel, stun, heal, DA, Root, and Fear and Gate. If you go upstairs, that is all you need. If you go down, load Smite cause the badgers are not undead. They are Necro's or sk's, so buff endure magic, disease, and fire. Save your bonechips and sell them in PoK, Nexus, or Bazaar. Necro's often come in there looking to buy them for 10pp a stack as well.

Wow, that turned out longer than I planned. Woohoo. Have fun. I had a blast in there with my necro. Wish I had done it more with my cleric, but I didnt know anything about it and just farmed bonechips there a time or two for PP. IF you dont have better, use your summoned hammer. Remember it has undead bane damage.

NoxCruor
Hmm...as far as fighting undead goes, then yeah...Kurns is the way to go, no doubt about that. But if you're just looking for raw exp, the best you'll get is Paludol Caverns. At 16, a person can easily solo near Hollowshade Moore entrance. The mobs there don't have very many HP's, and they don't hit that hard either. That can take you up to 18 or so, and you just move in the caverns a little bit more. All you really need is a disease removal spell and you're set.

PC can generally take you to 26 or 28 if you're soloing. But for soloing undead, there are Kurns, Najena entrance, and....*blinks*...can't think of the name of the friggin zone, but the entrance is in West Commons in the sand area. It's where Lanys held her "base of operations" so to speak for being a pain in Inny's side. :P

Just my two coppers...
