Calisan
In kael, there is a quest in a building called "The Icehouse" (Loc neg 850, pos 1150) that is needed for the 9th Coldain Ring. You must clear the giants from this room, approach the throne and spawn Doldigun. He can be spawned by saying "the Dain shall be slain for the peace we must obtain" You must then kill all the giants that spawn until the last is killed, then Doldigun will spawn and you MUST IMMEDIATELY kill him, if he runs off, the quest cannot be restarted for two and a half hours.

By now, you are probably asking what this has to do with corroded plate. Well, much to your luck, these giants have the ability to drop various weapons, and corroded plate armor. In fact, Corroded Plate is the only armor they will drop, and they generally drop a lot of it. Once the giants and Doldigun are dead, you can immediately start the encounter over again and farm more plate.

Not the easiest encounter in kael, but 3-4 groups of level 50 plus characters can generally take this enounter fairly easily.

Oh yes, a quick little edit. Your chanters cannot charm/mez any giants, or else the quest canbecome bugged thus stopping your farming. Slows are ok.

What Cali is referring to is known as Riffs in Kael (spelling might be wrong). And, this is what is better known as the cycle. As for the charming/mezzing, we've never had any problems with this when our guild has done Kael. In fact, that's one of our major tactics: get one of the giants on our side.

Beware, however, as the armor drops are somewhat rare, and as mentioned, some of the equipement is not exactly in line with traditional stats that a cleric might want/need (eg., the boots). However, another place where you can get very similar equipment (but better) is in the Halls of Testing, which seems to have a much more generous drop rate and drops a variety of pieces as opposed to just plate.
