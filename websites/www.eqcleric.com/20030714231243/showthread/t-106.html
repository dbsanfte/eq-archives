Filadan
I'd like to adress two of the comments made above...

First, casting KEI does not consume 1/3 of a level 60 Enchanter's mana pool, unless they are SEVERELY under-equipped. If an enchanter had 2700mana at level 60, they would not be in a guild capable of obtaining the KEI scroll, nor would they have money to spare to purchase the scroll, as they should be purchasing non-newbie equipment. In addition, most intelligent enchanters specialize in Alteration, and thus the mana cost would be even less.

Second, it is a very bad idea to get into the habit of chain-healing. You do not need KEI at level 12. You do not NEED KEI at level 60. What you need to do is learn to manage your mana pool efficiently. Don't worry about melee overpulling. If you have warned them, let them die. They will learn soon enough. Eventually level will catch up with you, and KEI will not save you if you have not taken the time to learn how to play a cleric appropriately.

And, as a previous poster said, 5pp is more of an insult than a donation. Nothing aggravates me more than someone asking to buy a service, then showing how little they value my time. This is why I do not respond to any /tells, or /ooc unless someone comes right out and says what they are willing to pay.
