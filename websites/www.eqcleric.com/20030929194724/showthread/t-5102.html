Thorarrin
I can suggest a place to purchase a video card or any component, and that is by checking out www.pricewatch.com and finding a vendor with the best possible price... that is if you don't mind waiting a week for shipping. It's alot cheaper than getting gouged at BestBuy, CompUSA or other retailers in your local area. As far as deciding which to go with, or maybe what your problem is, I couldn't help you there. I have been getting ones from the competition, NVidia, with GeForce 3 64 Mb and they have done fine until I started playing EQ back in June. I'm not sure which cards provide the best performance, but I know that I do not like the lag or choppiness that seems to come from the graphics being too intense. Hope this helps a little.

I currently have a GeForce FX 5600 128mb. It runs great (much better than my old GeForce II). It may be a bit on the pricey side, although I haven't checked on what the price is on it now. norvik
I run a Geforce 4 TI4600 128mb card at home. That card is last years model basically but it runs flawlessly. I only experience lag in the bazaar. At work I have a machine set up for playing on as well. It has an ATI 8500 in it. I deffinately feel the difference. The modeling looks about the same but spell effects, fire based, can slow me down and make the video studder a bit. I just set up another machine for a friend with a Geforce 4 TI 4200 in it. He has not loaded EQ on it yet so I can not comment on the actual performance of the card. What I have heard is that it performs quite well in EQ as well as other games. It is also considered a value card. You can get a boxed version for $100-$200 depending on the card manufacturer.

I dont think they are stocking the ATI 8500 anymore...they went to the 9000 series. It has a better engine. Some of the Geforce 4 cards may be hard to get aswell because of their new chipset release. If you can get your hands on one of the Geforce 4 TI (titanium series) it most likely will not be too expensive and will deffinately give you the performance that you need.
