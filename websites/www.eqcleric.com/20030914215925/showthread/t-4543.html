restandre
Rats in CoD. Rest of zone hasn't been too great, but I've had REALLY good exp at rats.

Pit in Tactics used to be the classic spot before they nerfed the drops. It's still great exp. Nowhere else in the zone really to go though, maybe the ogres up top if you've got a strong group.

HoH is solid. Not great, but at least competitive with PoV. Which camp you do is a matter of preference, although most people camp the upper floor of the trial halls.

BoT is the easy access zone, so you can find pick-up groups there. Deep South there is the best camp to grab, but Deep East isn't bad either and camping anywhere is respectable.

And there's also Torment, although exp isn't too good there. It's a fun zone just to mess around in though. I'm not really sure why, but I like it there.

Shadowfrost
The largest exp per mob that I've found, not counting minibosses, is probably the giants in Storms; these are lovely critters because they drop a few nice trinkets as well as experience and BoT access quest drops. However, they hit for 716, mitigate slow, and proc an additional effect which varies according to type, so not every pickup group can handle them; and you get more exp per hour fighting easier things.

For soloing or unusual groups, try the undeads in SE in Halls of Honor where you can use a necromancer for slow. Add a Slay Undead paladin and it's an amazing place for a trio; if soloing, stay upstairs, where there are six undead that can be single-pulled with Pacification and root-dotted - but you'll need a decent size mana pool and some flowing thought gear, KEI, and ideally a Hammer of the Sun in order to pull it off.

If you just can't find a slower at all, try the courtyard in Bastion of Thunder. Those bee things are fine unslowed if you have a decent tank; you'll need full mana for the statues though.

For those standard old-fashioned grind group days, Bastion of Thunder represents a reasonable compromise between loot, the chance at a few parchments, and still getting reasonable experience. Read what Restandre has to say about South Top and East Top; the camps which are called South Middle and South Bottom on my server also represent decent exp per hour.

My personal favourite for pure exp, though, is the Hero Shrine in Halls of Honor. Perhaps I've just had good groups there.

Valor never did anything for me really. It's miind-numbingly dull; and the best exp is from the golems in the Wasteland or the Razorfiends in the Caves, and there are never enough of either of them to go around, which means that Valor suffers from what I sometimes call Velks Lab Syndrome.
