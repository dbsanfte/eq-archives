restandre
Unless something changed very recently MoK remains the same. I haven't used MoR for a while now since 15 points just isn't that much vice mobs that hit for over 600.

You'll get rampage if you put aggro on a mob before anyone else does. Different people have different explanations on rampage, but bottom line is if you aggro early, you're on rampage. It's not the person with the second-most aggro, it's simply the second person TO aggro. (or something very close to it, depending on who you ask.)

Shadowfrost
The main use of the Mark of Retribution is for clerics to use in conjunction with Pacification in order to pull. Mark of Retribution is superbly well-adapted for this because of it's 200 range, low mana cost, relatively short casting time, and very low amount of hate generated.

Mark of the Righteous is worth using on old-world raids, since a 34DS can add up to a lot of damage.

Mark of Karn and Mark of Kings are, as far as I can tell, useful only for stripping damage shields.
