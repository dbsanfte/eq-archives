Shadowfrost
Don't go to PoV at level 55 unless you're very, very sure of your tank.

Personally, I was level 60 when PoP came out and I dinged 61 with the first kill (in Plane of Innovation, it was). I was level 62 before I even got access to the Plane of Valor, and during the time I was levelling there, everyone in the zone was level 62-65 and originated from one of the big raid guilds.

The thing about PoV is that several camps (particularly the caves camp and the golem camps) offer exp that's as good as BoT or HoH. So you do still see level 65 tanks in elemental gear with 200+ AA there; get a tank like that and the exp will just flow. You can start a CH on a tank like that at under 30% and still be early.

However, nowadays any level 55 tank, who's wearing gear they bought in the bazaar yesterday for 20k, can zone into PoV and try to tank critters that hit for 500+ and proc nukes. You can start a CH on a tank like that at over 70% and they'll still go SPLAT like a froglok in the Troll SK guild in Neriak.

I recently went back to PoV for a couple of evenings, and encountered this new breed of tank who's decided that now they're 55, they won't settle for less than Tier 2 exp. With Divine Arbitration, Cele Regen, Supernal Elixir and Supernal Light I could keep everyone standing, although I did get smacked about a bit by the golems. But it took all my skill to keep a 55-ish group standing at the Western Wall.

My advice is: honestly, don't be in too much of a hurry to rush to PoV if you can't find a big tank. You'll get more exp fighting in Nightmare, Disease, or even Justice than you will while you're waiting for the corpses to repop in Valor.
