Sorvani
Well ladies and gents, even if they require PoP for you to be able to use the new raid system, you can still be in the raid and get xp if you do not have PoP.

The only people required to have PoP are the Raid Leader and the group leaders. the other people in the groups get auto-joined to the raid when the group leader joins. ORQ tested this last night on our Kael Arena raid. Now the people who do not have PoP can NOT use /rsay but they DO see all loot messages, get XP, get cash if split on, and do see other people's /rsay messages. Do to the /rsay issue we just ignored that feature and continued to use our normal raid chat channel.

We killed a bit slower than normal last night do to raid classes, and Quellious crashed and was rolled back /sigh, but for the night with my xp set to 100% towards leveling, my xp bar moved one tick. We had 26 people in the raid i believe and i feel that one tick of xp was better than the normal none. And it was definately better than the groups all fighting to be the KS group. we aren't here for xp. we are here for thurg armor pieces. This function leveled the playing field.
