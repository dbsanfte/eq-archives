Shadowfrost
Clerics have a legitimate combat role between levels 1 and 20, and again between levels 56 and 65. From 21 to 55 you're pretty much better off not fighting, and using any spare mana to nuke. In the thirties and forties it'll be pretty rare for you to have spare mana.

Copperhead Fang
What shadowfrost said. If you're worried about not getting in and being active/doing damage/etc, cleric may not be the best class for you. However, this is a social game and as a cleric you enable your group to do many things they could never do otherwise by standing back and healing. You might consider a shaman if you're interested in both doing damage and some healing too.

All my melee skills are maxed, and have been for quite some time. I am a BattleCleric(tm).
My usual Warrior partner once said he had thought I must have played a melee before my cleric becuase I wasn't afriad of getting hit. It's not that, and I have never played a melee. I just know what I can take, and what I can channel a heal through, and I make sure I always know what I am going to do if things go pancake shaped.

There will be times that, yes, you will be sitting on your butt and medding when you don't stand to heal (get a drogmor. ASAP). Those times, remember that the XP flows because of 2 or 3 people (usually just 2): The slower\CC person(s) and YOU.

I just did a LDoN in Everfrost. The puller died once (got a little too anxious and rushed too far ahead), and was back pulling within 2 minutes...because of me. He was able to (aside from that one event) control exactly how many came into camp because of me. I used an Extended Range item and used /assist to Pacify pull with him.

We never, ever took a med break...because of me. The times the chanter and druid were oom, they just sat out a few fights...because I had everything under control.

Of course, whenever I was over 80%, I would either tank along with them or nuke...but I sat more than I would in a normal XP group...but we finished the dungeon in nothing flat because everyone did their job perfectly...and there was a cleric to make sure no one died.

What I am saying is, yes...you can be a Battle Cleric...get decent armor, make sure you have a shaman or enchanter, and away you go. Don't worry about the times you will have to sit and watch...that sitting and watching, if you are doing things right, is just as productive as the melee types in their whacking on things.

Any dumb grunt can make things die...we make sure our group lives. Everything else is just an amusing diversion.

Just don't play a dwarf...you will get sick of looking at Troll Butt, trust me. Dark Elf, all the way!

Nyvn
Yes, you can melee. Keep in mind that you'll do low damage compared to any real melee. Also you'll take more damage than any melee class will. Keeping your group alive is your primary concern; so you should only melee when you're good on mana. Personally I prefer nuking over melee, but thats just me. I believe that my mana is better spent on more efficent large heals on the tank than on smaller faster heals on myself.
