You can indeed use the epic at level 52.

As far as a higher level cleric being the "main" cleric at a "raid", well that would make sense. They have a (potentially) larger mana-pool, and more powerful buffs/heals. Furthermore, I don't really see a difference between main/alt. As an example, most of the members of my guild 2box (or 3box) characters, and know more about game dynamics than 95% of the people I have met in pickup groups. When you have multiple high-level characters, the definition main/alt (as you are seeing it) doesn't really apply anymore.

Personally, I cannot 2box as I only have one computer and one account. I level all of my characters the old-fashioned way, and the idea that another cleric plays better than I do (because their cleric is their "main") is laughable.

If your guild treats you poorly due to level, I suggest you leave. If it is all in your mind, and you just get mad because you do not get all the attention, I suggest you level, or again, leave the guild and find a lower level guild.
