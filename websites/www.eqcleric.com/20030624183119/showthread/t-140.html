I am up to the RF fight and so far nothing has been no-rent. I know the shards in SF and the pearl from RF is also not no-rent, nor is the heart of RF you take back to TD, so I am guessing none of the epic involves no-rent items.

In Temple, The seeker is indeed male, blue to level 60, and spawns to the left of the throne. A female seeker is the PH during the other half day. To pull him, hand in and sprint to the zone (jump off balcony...he runs around). I was told by many to have stuns loaded for his CH...but, he's immune to stuns, so don't bother.

He will agro the others in the area if he casts his AE Tremor spell. Just pull him into the zone tunnel to LS and you should have little problem. I did the fight with me (60 Cleric), 54 Warr, 60 ranger, 60 Enchanter, and 60 monk. It was WAYYY overkill. Dropped in 20 seconds tops.

As for level for the epic effect, I have known noone below 56 getting epic since RF was such a mess. But other items with an effect which is a spell normally received by that class at a given level can not use effect until that level is attained. So my educated guess would be Level 56 to use epic effect.

McMrLenale, Zordak merchant is yes 20 to 24hour spawn real life day. He spawns upon a patch and when sol b crashes (becomes unavailable).

If you are having trouble getting the seeker to the zone with him beating on you do this. Dont turn it in. Have someone (a tank is best but anyone with hp will do)aggro the seeker and run him to the zoneline. You should not get touched really. Have that person zone with the seeker at the zone in. Once aggro is zoned, turn in and kill him. No getting lost, or dieing and having to redo your hard work.

Also if you are having trouble with Ixiblat just a bit of useful information. His ae proc is front side only. So get all your melee on its back side. If not your going to do a lot of healing that could be going to your Main tank.

Hope some of this helps out at least one person.
