Ayreon
What I did:

Run3
Wis3
SCM3
MGB

From the raiding aspect:

HG > HA. Even maxed AHA3 only kicks the spam heal up to 2200 or so, whereas a standard critical heal will hit 3k just at the base. Sure, it's not every cast, but with HG3 done you'll notice it does kick in a lot, and a lot of times, it saves a caster or the MT's life. Neither of these affect CH really (the 15k+ crits are more for show than anything ;) ) Once you get some IH4 or better foci (I have Marr's Gift), then HA really shines, IMO.

CR is usefull if you have MGB. I don't use it very often in raid groups, I use it a ton in LDoN, especially when coupled with DvA. It's really nice when you tack on SCR3 or better as well.

It also depends where you raid. If you raid a bunch in PoP, you'll use MGB/CR a good deal, especially on the Tier1/2 mobs (MB, Grummus)

DvA is cool with CR as I mentioned, but not used a bunch in raiding. It's helpful when you're too busy to switch healing targets (CH rotation) and you wanna save someone.

SCR is also a nice compliment to MGB especially if your guild does po9/kazad buffing, since Kazad only last 1:16 by default. You can get it to 1:45 or so with AAs alone, which makes it worth the MGB cost.

So, basically, for raiding, aside from direct stat boosts/resist/etc (stamina, the defensive AAs, etc), MGB, CR, and SCR are major. SCM is a requirement for any caster, so that should always come before anything else in Archetypes.
