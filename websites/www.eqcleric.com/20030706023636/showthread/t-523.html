restandre
With dueling ANY class, the very key thing is the other person's MR. If it's low, you can go to town. If it's high, your options are slim to none.

1) Make sure you have see invis.
2) USE DISPEL. Try to have a right-click item to fill in for a junk buff if you get dispelled. Conversely, try to dispel the dots that get cast on you.
3) If your opponent has low MR, root them, nuke them, blind them. Only stun them if you need an interrupt. You keep them locked up, but you also keep yourself locked up casting until you go oom.
4) Don't be afraid to melee, except against a slowing class. Shamans are arguably the absolutely worst class to duel. Use a weapon with a noticeable proc on it; your straight melee DPS will not be exceptional. Use mark of retribution if you can land it.
5) Heal yourself. You're the best healing class in the game, use your mana to keep yourself alive unless your opponent can drain your mana. I dueled a paladin once for over an hour...his better DPS << my better healing.
6) Keep both DA and DB up at the beginning. At some point you're gonna say "gee I wish I had this spell up." Cast DA, demem it, and put that spell in its slot.

Kain
Coming from a PvP server. A few tips for you... Get your resists as high as possible for fight (especially MR). With a high MR you hopefully will resist the snares a lot and spend a minumal time dispelling them and focusing on combat. Use junk buffs in front (leave blank spots at front of buffs and bury your HP buffs at the end so they last to get dispelled) so that when you do get snared and DoT'd you can dispell them without having to strip all your buffs away. Liberal use of dispel's, scrub that sow, DS (perhaps use mark of Karn to overwrite if he has low MR), HP buff... Use that shield bash to interupt as much as possible.

When I say get high resists... I mean over 150+ (200+ for MR).

On PvP servers nukes do 66% of their regular damage, and no spell can do over 75% of a players current MAX life. Also you cannot fear another player (prevents fearing someone in say Hate and watch them die a horrible xp death in hard to get to spot).
