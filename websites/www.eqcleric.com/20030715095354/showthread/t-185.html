A good deed - Click HERE for Original Thread
Alyaa
I was in OS one night grouping with my husband's war. Our enchanter had to leave so I shouted for a replacement. We got a new chanter with in a few minutes. He was very nice but seemed a little off. I figure maybe he is ebay, never did ask if he was. But since he was a nice guy I never complained when he was a little slow on mezzing. Well after a while I noticed he was wearing a black crystalined robe. I was thinking, man thats not very good for a 56 enchanter. So I asked him if I could inspect. I was shocked. He had cloth sandles no shoulders legs or hat, most of his things didn't even have int or char on it so I asked him what his int was. He said 128. When he said that 3 of us in group ask him if he was looking at his wis not his int. I felt so bad for the guy. Later that night obulous death shroud dropped and 3 of us wanted in on roll (was a NBG group) and he won, and I was very happy. He had no clue it was worth 20k. Well anyways, I asked him why he didn't go buy some good stuff in bazaar and he said he had no clue what to get himself so I said I would go there with him. The next night we went and he hands me 6k to get him some gear. For 3k we got him to 200 int and out of the kindness of my heart gave him my husband's old GEB's. He wanted new shoulders that had int on it so I told him we would find him a set. Then I told him the shroud was worth 20k. He was shocked of course. I was expecting him to sell them right away but he didn't. He gave them to me. He also tried to give me 1k just for helping him. Of course I refused. I told him also I would only borrow the shoulder until I could get my own or something I liked. He thanks my husband and I profusely for 20 minutes and finally I had to go to bed, with a grin on my face. I was thrilled to help someone so in need. He now groups with us every night almost and my husband is helping him with everything he needs to know. He is getting much better and even is confident enough to pull himself! Where as before he was afraid to do almost anything. Not sure why I posted this. Figure it would be a nice little story to read. I helped someone and made a good friend. Enough to make EQ worth playing when you want to quit. :)

Alyaa Phiscon
60 High Priestess
