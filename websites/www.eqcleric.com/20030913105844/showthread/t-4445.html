Jarvis
Ok, let me explain how you earn platinum (pp) on this board right now.

/forums/images/orangestar.gif Register as a member: 100pp
/forums/images/orangestar.gif Starting a new thread: 10pp
/forums/images/orangestar.gif Making a reply: 5pp
/forums/images/orangestar.gif Voting on a poll: 10pp
/forums/images/orangestar.gif Owning a thread that get's X replies according to below:
  • More than 5 replies: 10pp
  • More than 10 replies: 25pp
  • More than 20 replies: 75pp
  • More than 30 replies: 150pp
  • More than 50 replies: 250pp
/forums/images/orangestar.gif Owning a thread that get's X views according to below:
  • More than 10 view: 5pp
  • More than 50 views: 25pp
  • More than 100 views: 50pp
  • More than 200 views: 150pp
  • More than 1000 views: 250pp

Now keep in mind that this Store system is very much an untested feature that just went live, so we might be adjusting this feature at any time, adding new actions to buy or removing some. We already removed the option to change your username since we couldn't come up with a valid reason anybody would actually spend 5000pp doing it.

We're also planning to give donators pp to their account for the obvious reason. We're thinking of doing this somewhat based on donation to make it fair. Some sites use different graphics to represent donators of different amount values, but we're as appreciative of $5 as we are when we get $30+ so we don't want to discriminate between users by giving out different graphics.

However we can easily make a level system for donations within different brackets and give out pp based on that, so that's in the works.

Hope this clears things up a bit. And as 'someone' found out earlier; there are already several perks to buy in the store right now.

Question is. Would you guys want a spend link on your posts in the message board, or is it easy enough to track back to the forum index?

* Remember: Keep checking this post for an updated list of incentives. Don't be shy to test the new "Rate" graphic on posts.

Jarvis
Well, I don't want to rewrite the system just for the rating system. Speaking from years of experience with websites and message boards; not many people will end up using the rating system. Heck, i couldn't even get people to rate threads before we got the store installed, and I even moved the rate thread right above the text field in the reply window and made it a different color etc, so I'm not too worried about it at this point.

Let's test this system for a month or so, then we can make adjustments based on usage etc. This is just a fun feature installed to give you members some incentive for posts you're already making.

Oh, and keep in mind. Starting to spoof threads on existing topics will be grounds for a warning and removal of thread, merging it with the original thread and giving the original thread starter credit for your hard work trying to earn extra pp. Also, on repeated abuse of the store system you will be banned from the store. You can continue to post and participate on the message board and website but will not be able to accumulate pp or spend your pp in the store. That should be enough to discourage people from trying to gain extra pp.

Trust me, those of you who have contributed greatly to this website up until now will receive plenty of pp. Just look at some of the past threads receiving 50-60 replies and 1-2000 views. Those will come again, and the creator will make a lot of pp.

Like I said...this is suppose to be fun, so dont' take it too seriously. We're not trying to build a strong economy here or set up trading posts etc. It's just a system created to reward the most active users in ways, as well as motivating the less active people to participate in the system.

I appreciate any feedback of course, so keep playing with the system and tell me what you think.

As for the rabid dark elf's question about the pp accumulation being broke: It works on all your threads, so even though you just clicked on one post and saw your pp jump up quite a bit, that can come from people replying to/viewing other threads of yours etc. If people are working with 4-5 threads/posts of yours, then surely it is going to jump a bit.
