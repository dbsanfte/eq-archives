grevan
Yes the bard should get all of the experience.

In this case Bard Dmg Output to Creature X would be > Cleric Damage Output to Creature X as damage and reverse damage shields are non attributable damage (ie...non agro producing, no damage is actually assigned to a player and thus no agro is assigned to a player).

As long as the bard does at least 1 damage he should be fine.

Aguth
You should be able to pull 6-8 blues-even con mobs. Have the bard plays his AE songs and melee and just heal the bard when needed with the cleric. As you get higher levels the bard can plays his damage shield song to speed it up and eventually the cleric will even be able to throw an AE nuke here and there to help with damage output, as long as the bards AE's are doing more than the cleric does. Bards are one of the easiest classes in the game to plevel up because of their AE's/DS's and ability to take a hit at low levels.

Doohickey
BTW, for anyone wanting to speed level (not quite PL efficiency, sadly) an enchanter:

Temperance and Mark of Retribution are your friends.

My little Gadjette is 19 now, and would be 20 had I not stopped to do rez runs and chat with a young cleric with questions about our class.

Temperance to let the enchanter take a few hits, then pull a red mob with Mark of Retribution. Mob obligingly comes over and starts beating on you. Enchanter tash's said mob, then tosses DoT's and DD's until something sticks or until the mob moves to beating on the enchanter (at which point the pet starts assisting).

I had her in OT until 17, then moved her to M Seru yesterday. average fight was probably about a minute, every Hopper was at least yellow, with most being red. Between 4 and 9% per kill.

Plus, her defense and offense are pretty close to max pre-50 now. Of course, Abjuration, Evocation and Alteration are also getting a work out. Still leaves Divinationd and Conjuration to chain cast, but oh well. She isn't even turning on the attack (no weapons equiped) so those skills still suck, but I don't exactly plan on meleeing her anyway.

There are probably ways to get XP faster, but so far everything else I have tried requires a lot more mana and effort, whereas this one is pretty painless, and is moving her along fast enough.
