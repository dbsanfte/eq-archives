I don't think you're too low for Sebilis - after all, when Sebilis was first entered most people who got in first were 51 or 52, as the level limit had been raised to 60 with the same expansion that brought Sebilis. It was tough in those days (though really fun - I love exploring new zones, I saw the first oh-so-uber Lamentation drop in Sebilis on the Rathe and that Guardian... well, he was simply breathtaking to encounter with no idea of his presence).

Don't limit yourself to that though - Jarvis points out some great zones in Luclin; and you're also a good level to start any Velious faction work you think you might wish to do (upgrading to Thurgadin armor is something that's possible with small numbers, for instance - and giants in Kael arena would be great experience at your level. Then the Geonid caves in Wakening Lands to find some quest gems to go with any armor pieces you may find. Careful of Wuoshi though until you've killed a lot of giants... I don't know that too many people kill that PITA any more.
