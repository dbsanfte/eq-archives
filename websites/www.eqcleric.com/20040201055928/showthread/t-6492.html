Piqueno
Piqueno walks slowly to the front of the room and the poduim that stands there. Standing behind it, he quicly realizes that he cannot see over the top. Quietly clearing his throat and muttering something about over-sized goofballs running the show, he walks around to the front of the poduim and address the milling crowd that is assembled there.

"Ahem, greetings fellow priests and clerics. My name is Piqueno, and I'm a follower of Bristlebane on the Rodcet Nife server. As you can see, Im taller than a gnome but shorter than a human, so that must make me a halfling. Though 'half' of what I've never really been able to understand. Anyway, I have completed my 31st level of training and am progressing through the ranks of my temple and wanted to come here and introduce myself. So, hi!

Stepping back off the poduim, Piqueno notices the rather large refreshment table set up at the back of the room, and even more quickly notices the even larger keg sitting beside the table. Never one to pass up on a chance to concecrate some ale back to Bristlebane, the stout halfie quickly saunters to the back of the room and quietly draws a pint.

Over the rim of his mug, Piqueno searches for a kind face amid the confuion of strangers....

Just wanted to drop in and say hello everyone. As stated, Im a 31 halfie cleric. My main is a 65 Barb shaman that has become more work than fun and so I decided to dust of Piqueno (who has actually been around since June 15 of 2001, according to his born on date) and give him a once over. He was 24 when I started playing him again about a week ago, and Id forgotten how much fun actually talking to the people I group with is, a luxuary not afforded me on my shaman who is constantly casting something. Im really enjoying my time as a cleric (and not constantly getting bugged for SOW!!!) so I wanted to come here so that I can learn a bit more on how to be a better cleric (though as a halfling, Im already halfway to perfection :p )

So I guess thats it. I'll be seeing ya around!
