Hadley
Auroralee,

The four pearl fragments are combined together in "Zordak's Box of Binding," which you can get either from Cecilia or from Zordak himself in Sol B. When combined, you get combined pearlescent shard. Hand this shard into Cecilia, and you she will spawn Rage, and return the pearl to you.

When Ragefire dies, loot the heat, a two slot box. Place into this box the pearl Cecilia gave you, as well as the one you got from Zordak in Sol B. Combine them to get Zordak's heart.

Hope this helps!

Hadley
