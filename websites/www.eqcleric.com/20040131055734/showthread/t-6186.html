Ok, I hate to start another post on this subject and this will be my final post on this matter.

However, I felt this deserved it's own thread for a couple of reasons.

Jarvis has graciously setup a forum specifically for our Clan hunting group. You can find the site at Clan EQcleric. Thank you Jarvis from all of us!!

Secondly, I wanted to make one more invitation to anyone interested in joining us. This started out as me seeking to get a couple people together to hunt once a week for a couple hours, to it being something many people were interested in doing.

The rules of the clan are still in the works, but the basics are as follows so far and are subject to change:

No twinking of any kind, period!
No leveling on non-clan hunting days.
Leveling will be done "Old School Style" where we earn what we get.
You must be a registered member of this site Eqcleric.com

At some point we may form a guild out of this just for the mere purpose of making communication easier in game. However, this guild will not be a replacement for your primary server and or guild. If a guild is formed from all of this, it will still only be a once a week event.

We are also looking for writers. People who can write a weekly story about our adventure and post it in the "Halls of Lore" section of this forum. We need people with writing talents not a secretary to keep notes. :p

Now let me make something clear. The forum as mentioned above is not a Cleric informational site nor is it to be used as a replacement for this GREAT site. All clerical information is to be directed here on this site. Our forum is for communication organization and entertainment purposes for the clan only. Everyone and anyone is welcome to visit the site and see what's going on regardless of wether your a member or not.

If you think this is something that interests you then by all means go visit the site, register and post the appropriate information so we can add you to the list. We may add more groups at later dates as more people show interest.

Once again, let me say a BIG Thank you to Jarvis for his help and support!
