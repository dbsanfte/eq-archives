Galv
The general rule of thumb is that you want to get your natural resists over 100 (i.e., unbuffed). Specifically, the most important resists to have up are MR, FR, and CR. The reason for those three being most important is ToV, in which you'll be fighting dragons that primarily cast those type of 300 pt AoE breadth weapons/spells.

Blue diamond gear is great, but it will severly cut into your mana pool since it has little mana and no wisdom. So, when you are shopping, try to be cognizant of the resist too. Here are three "major" pieces that can up your resists very quickly with little or no trade off:

Tae Ew Shield (goes for about 5-7k on xev now):
AC: 35
STR: +8 WIS: +8 INT: +8 HP: +50 MANA: +50
SV FIRE: +25 SV DISEASE: +7 SV MAGIC: +10 SV POISON: +7

Crown of the Froglok Kings (goes for about 300-500 on xev)
AC: 12
SV FIRE: +20 SV COLD: +20

Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh (your epic! priceless...)
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
STA: +10 CHA: +15 WIS: +25 MANA: +100
SV FIRE: +10 SV DISEASE: +10 SV COLD: +10 SV MAGIC: +10 SV POISON: +10

Those three pieces alone will push your MR by 20, FR by 55, and CR by 30. Certainly nice upgrades for any cleric. Admittedly, the epic is a little out of your reach right now and the shields has a required level of 51, but it's never too early to start planning.
