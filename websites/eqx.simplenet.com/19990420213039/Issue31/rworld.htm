To my Astonishment

I can't believe I have been around this long without a release of a game. I have never been so involved with a pre-beta, prerelease of a game in my life. It's almost been a year now. Now I know some of you are saying you have been here longer than that, but I'm just saying a year is a long time for me. I mean when Ultima Online was released I didn't even play it for three months, and yet here I set at about the year mark and I'm still writing, talking, and watering at the mouth over EverQuest. All I can say is it better be good or I'm gonna go postal!

My New Games

I bought a new game Friday. Its called "Death Trap Dungeon". The same company that made "Tomb Raider" created this game. Although I never liked the camera angles they use, the game is not bad. I get annoyed with that interface and have to stop for a few days before I can play again. They took some of the sword slashing from "Die By The Sword", and implemented it into the game. I'm like on the second map. It's not the greatest 3dfx graphics I've seen, but there're pretty damn good. These little blue dudes that throw knifes at you stick in your legs and arms until you heal. It's really cool. You also get the arms, legs and head severs which are fun to watch.

I also bought the game "M1 Tank Platoon". Now if you're into military equipment and platoon warfare you're going to love this game. It supports 3dfx, making the graphics very realistic. The interface is the best I have ever seen, and very east to use. Actually using the mouse in the cockpit can access most of the switches. No more annoying keyboard overlays with this game. I was in the military for five years, and have been inside an M1 Tank during operation. I can tell you it's the closest tank simulation I've seen that compares to the real thing. All your missing is the ringing of your ears when the main gun goes off, the smell of hot casings, and the compression of your chest from the blast.

My Son bought "Final Fantasy Seven", which was also done in 3dfx. The graphics during the fight scenes are cool but the rest of the graphics sucked as far as I was concerned. You spend most of your time clicking the continue button to see the next character speak while watching very boring, uninteresting cut scenes. Don't waste your money on this one.

I'm happy to report I'm still playing Unreal. I'm stuck on this new level. It's like a city in the clouds and there are these tombstones everywhere down under the ground. I'm not sure what I still need to do to move on.

Well not twenty-four hours later they called her on the phone advising her she was staying on too long, and thus taking away online time from other users. When she hung up I called them back and commenced to bring this ISP up to the 1990's. I advised them that they advertise 24/7 access to the Internet, and by GOD we are going to get it! I also informed them maybe it was time to upgrade the huge T1 connection they currently had, and a few other choice words that I won't repeat here. Well we found out the next time she logged on her account had been deleted. I guess they were not too amused with my lecture about modern day remote access networking.
