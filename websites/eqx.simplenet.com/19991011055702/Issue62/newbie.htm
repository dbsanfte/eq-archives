ISSUE # 62

January 20th - February 26th


The Newbie's Lament

It's here! It's here! It finally came!
Phase 4 is in my grasp!
Another week and I'd have gone insane,
I get to play at last!

I slash at the mail, the paper rips away,
As if 'twas touched by Death.
I jam the game in my CD tray,
And then I take a breath!

The sounds blast and the pictures glow,
I've been waiting for this so long!
Even with my new graphics card,
I'd been getting bored of Pong.

Into the night I gaze at the screen,
As I play and play EQ.
Into my veins I inject caffiene,
Every half-hour or two.

Although I'm just a newbie,
I will still boldly go,
And thrust my lethal dagger
Into the heart of every foe!

I admit I've never played the game,
But I'll learn as I go.
If foes were wise, they'd run away,
I've practiced in UO.

Into the wild I'll bravely tred,
With my knife and some food.
Off the land I'll learn to live.
I will be a kewl dood.

I can't be killed, I'll never die!
I'll show them who is boss!
And if I fail, I'll start to cry,
And turn my PK switch off.

I choose a path and start to walk
To start a daring day.
As I go, I shout gangsta talk,
So others can't role-play.

As I travel throughout the land,
What'd you suppose I see?
I come across and Ogre band,
They start to laugh at me.

Shut up, you punks! I yell at them,
They tremble in their fear,
But they wield swords and start to grin,
As soon as I get near.

I have no sword, but I can bite,
And kick them in the shin.
I'll sure as heck put up a fight,
I'm certain I will win.

Despite my brilliant fighting skills,
I fail to stay erect.
Fatalities don't work on them…
Must be a game defect.

I fight and fight to stay alive,
They club me in the head.
I won't give up, I must survive,
Dang! It looks like I'm dead!

They killed me once, but I don't care,
It doesn't cause me strife.
I'll get them back, I won't play fair,
When I come back to life.

My newbie friends gather 'round,
And we get set to kill.
We'll pound those Ogres to the ground,
To prove that we have skill.

We hunt and search to find those tramps
Who killed my yesterday.
But when we come across their camp,
My friends all run away.

Well look who's back,” they say to me,
Ya want to play some more?
Before I can turn 'round to flee,
They pound me to the floor.

I'm dead again, that wasn't nice,
For them to fight like that.
I should have taken some advice,
And practiced on a bat.

Once more I'll try, with newbie friends,
To kill those Ogre fools.
My two past deaths must be avenged,
Or how can I be kewl?

Before too long we see our foes,
Near the horizon line.
Anxiety for battle grows,
A chill runs down my spine.

I have some leather armor now,
So I am not afraid.
Their Orge heads will surely bow
Beneath my mighty blade.

Who cares that they were in Phase 3,
I'm much too kewl to lose.
My newbie friends will protect me,
Cause we are awesome doods.

As they approach, I start to cuss.
This doesn't look like fun.
The problem is that they've got us
Outnumbered 3 to 1.

Now wait, I say, on second thought
I'm not so sure I'll win.
In truth I think I'd rather not
Get slaughtered once again.

The enemy's upon us now,
And I don't feel so brave.
My friends and I shout “Holy cow!”
And run into a cave.

So in the shadows we will hide,
Until the threat is gone.
Deep into the night, we all cried
And shouted for our moms.

    Lament by Gary Gardiner
