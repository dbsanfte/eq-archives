Shadow Dancing

I was in bar in Rivervale when I learned that my beloved twin sister was dead. I was sitting across from a Halfling who thought he could beat me in poker when he invited another person to join our game. The young man sat down, and from the way his eyes never left the pile of platinum on the table, one could tell that he was a rogue...

    Tale by Maesyn Shadowdancer

Malice
 A Study in Villainy

The guard at the door to my cell fidgets nervously. I understand the task of guarding me is handed out as punishment. It used to be that only the most highly trained and the most alert were assigned to be my wardens; I am a dangerous figure after all. But now the unruly apprentices and unfaithful Paladins are my keepers...

    Tale by Ramos


Raevenchylde
Vol. II

Months had passed since my brother and I had first set foot into the Toxxulia Forest and began our adventures. We still tended to our schooling between ventures, showing up long enough to breeze through exams and dazzle our classmates with our above average grasp of the arts. Any time I did not spend in that forest I spent in the library poring over all the tomes I could read...

    Tale by Ammudana
