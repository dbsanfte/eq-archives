30th Issue Contest!

To introduce our newest column we are giving away an EverQuest poster that Wynn Orill brought back from E3. EXPRESSions will be a column written by our staff on topics that our readers want to hear about. In issue 29 we will be setting up a form for you to send in topics that interest you. Each week one of our staff will take your suggestions, pick one, and write a column about it. As for the contest, each reader that sends in a topic from the time the form goes up until Friday, July 10th at 6 pm. pst (that's 9 pm for you in est.) will be eligible to win the poster. We will choose one of those folks randomly. For the sake of this contest you must provide a valid e-mail address, and duplicates will not be counted (though we still encourage you to send in as many topic ideas as you want.)
