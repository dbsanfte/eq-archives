ISSUE # 22May 16th - May 22nd

A Long Day

Blackness, emptiness, soothed and consoled her. The void of nothingness, where no pain, no anxiety, reached her. Slowly the darkness took shape, blurring into a dull grey image, and Lilan became aware of the first sounds to reach her since the ordeal had begun. It came rushing back in bits and pieces; each new noise and new blurry, confused image that pierced her mind brought a flash of the painful memory back. But where was she going when it happened? What was she doing? She could not recall so many things for some reason, and she had no idea how much time had passed.... She became aware of how cold her face was. Cold and sore. She cracked her eyelids open to reveal a dark stone before her, against the side of her cheek. As she blinked to focus she became acutely aware of the scent of incense and the stench of burnt flesh. What is this? "Arise, servant!" shrieked an odd voice. Lilan summoned the strength to lift her head and gazed at the source. It was an odd creature, thin, pale, and wrinkled... "In accordance with the Rituals, I bring thee forth to do my bidding!" It had no hair on its head, but only a thin few strands along the sides of its head, which was so obscene... There were no horns! No scales or plates, only a grossly smooth, tan skin. Lilan shuddered in horror as she looked into the thing's eyes, which blinked in fury. It has round pupils... she thought in disgust as it spoke again. "Zsikthian Demon, I bring you forth on this plane to do my bidding for a term of no less than 24 hours!" This was going to be a long day...

Tale by Amoroth.
