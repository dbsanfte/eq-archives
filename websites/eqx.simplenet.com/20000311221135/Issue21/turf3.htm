ISSUE # 21May 9th - May 15th

Territory

All this said and suggested, I still sit back and see dimensions yet seen for this classic battle, and EverQuest in general. I wrote here about races fighting for ground and loot, but I think the greater battles might not be based on turf alone, but the other factor in many wars: religion. I sit here, writing my last lines and think to myself, "good lord this game has potential." I will leave the theories about religion effecting this battle (and game) to someone else, and in the meantime invite you to look again at the atlas and wonder.

Article by Ian Ash.
