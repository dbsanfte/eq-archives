A Wizard's Note

The breeze that remained behind the thunderstorm swept the low hills of the Plains of Karana. Wolves silently padded out of their dens as the breeze ruffled thick grey fur. Further west and away from the two-legs a bear stood and flexed powerful muscles as he too welcomed the new day. The wind swept east to where an old farmer rubbed his unshaven chin as he surveyed the flood damage to his crops. There was much work to be done if they wanted to survive the winter. The only other two people out at sunrise in the Plains slogged down the muddy road at a slow pace.
