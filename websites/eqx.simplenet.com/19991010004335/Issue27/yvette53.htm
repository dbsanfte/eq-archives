ISSUE # 27June 20th - June 26th

Yvette's Tower - part 5

"Message by falcon told me that the ship will arrive on the shores of the Southern Karana plains to collect before sailing around to Freeport for the journey home." Bastion informed him of the details. The Paladin turned to his hunting party without delay and addressed the gathering.

"You have heard the final word men, be prepared for a trap. We leave for Freeport at dusk." With that he left, motioning a dark haired Priestess to chain Bastion. 		"But, they are meeting us in Karana" Bastion shouted after him. He chuckled as he disappeared into the evening shade.

"Consider yourself a prisoner of war," the woman warned him. Bastion glanced at her neck and the neck band she wore, covering from collar to jaw. 		"You bear the mark," he murmured to her. She glanced at him as she locked the last chain. She called to one of the hunters and instructed him to take care of the prisoner.

"You should have more respect for women," the hunter said as he kicked Bastions legs out from underneath him. Bastion smiled to himself.

Tale by Ian Ash.
