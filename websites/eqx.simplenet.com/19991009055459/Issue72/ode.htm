ISSUE # 72

May 1st - May 7th


Laughing Kaelib

Laughing Kaelib, favored of Brell,
Warding us from death's grim knell
As oceans of enemies swirl and swell
You are the vessel that keeps us well

Amidst fierce battle above the din,
Forsaking his below bound kin,
In dire straits, our outlook dim
Kaelib prays and hope begins

No undead, mummy or skeleton
Shall mock us once his chants begun
In fear they whither, shriek and run
For 'longside Kaelib, we fear none

Though far from Faydwer, I honor him
This jolly son of Kaladim
Great Lord Serilis, let him win
Till once more I behold my friend

    Ode by Remido
