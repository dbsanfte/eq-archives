The Troll Song
 (Sung to the tune of the Beverly Hillbillies)


Come listen to a story 'bout a bar-shin' troll,
From the swamp and to the city,
Barshin' away at all the gnolls,
One day he was afightin' and alookin' for some food,
And out from the shadows came a dark elven dude.
(Necromancer that is. Lean mean undead summoning machine.)

The elf said, "Hey watcha think yer doin' here,
"You're a troll, this is mad, don't ya even have no fear,
"Them guards is gonna get ya, you wont have no place to run,
"So get back to Grobb, that's where yer killin' should be done."
(Good killin' that is. All kinds of evil stuff to barsh.)

The troll set out for Grobb, but was stopped by a guard,
Who saw the troll scratchin' his backside like a dumb retard,
His faction wasn't too bad but it didn't really shine,
So he said, "You've ruined your own lands, you'll not ruin mine."
(Good lands that is. Ain't no swamps out in them parts.)

The troll tried to kill 'im but he didn't stand a chance,
He had to stop slashin' 'cause of the itchin' is his pants,
He looked for the dark elf but the guy was long gone,
So he turned full around and headed for the zone.
(Safety that is. Guards won't chase you through them.)

Well he finally saw more trolls near his native habitat,
Who said, "We no see you in long time, where you been barshin' at?"
He told them, "Me not know," and headed into Grobb,
A little older, a little wiser, still a butt itchin' slob.
(Troll that is. Stupidest creatures ever to come out of Norrath.)

    Tale by Mideon, Wood Elf Ranger (turned bard) of Prexus
