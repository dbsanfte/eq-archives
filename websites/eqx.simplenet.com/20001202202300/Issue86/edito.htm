ISSUE # 86

August 7th - August 13th


Editorial - Oghma

As can be expected from last week's editorial, I went on to play on a Race War server this week. I was lucky enough to be able to get in right away when Verant brought them up earlier than expected, and so got in with the first rush.

And what a rush it was! I created a wood elf rogue to play and was in the chaos that was Greater Faydark with 200 newbies and 3 black wolves. I chose this race/class combination because I had seen in the chat room how players were mostly planning on being dark elf enchanters, and I didn't want to do that. I debated playing a gnome wizard, but decided not too, since I knew that spell casters would be common, and I have a bit of a stubborn streak.

After doing the guild quest, I realized that kills were just not going to happen for me - I have a slow processor, and the zone was just too full to find a kill at all. I decided then to quest for levels and fun, and had a blast! I also wound up avoiding the many zone crashes suffered by those in the Greater Faydark, though I'd be lying if I said that I'd planned it.

SLIGHT SPOILER ALERT! DO NOT READ THIS PARAGRAPH IF YOU DON"T WANT A QUEST SPOILER. I decided to do the mail quest from Felwithe, Butcherblock, and Kaladim to get experience and cash. It was easy slipping into Felwithe and a tad more scary sliding into Butcherblock to run to the docks, but I had two letters on me when I tried to sneak into Kaladim.

How did I sneak into Kaladim? Well, by practicing Sneak I already had it to 10 by the time I hit the docks, and wood elves naturally can hide as a racial advantage. Since they enhanced Sneak to allow you to move invisible with a successful Sneak and Hide, I was sneaking straight past dwarves engaged in slaying skeletons and goblin whelps- I could have picked their pockets if I was higher level. I taunted them with says and emotes, then slipped into Kaladim - and my first death.

While invisibility and camouflage does not disappear when you cross a zone line, Hide and Sneak both do. I zoned into a Kaladim and raced to the wood elf bard in town, and ran straight into a dwarf who chased me all over Kaladim. I zoned and he was there waiting for me, and I was not able to get a successful Hide in before dying.

That night I did the Kaladim run four times and made it twice. Once a cleric saw me duck into the guard towers inside Kaladim before I was successful with Hide, and he targeted me with Smite and then got me outside of Kaladim. Twice however, I completed the quest (with the three letters) and I made 2nd level having fun while the other new players argued and fought over spawns.

I'm going to invade Steamfont next, and steal into Ak'anon for the quest there! Hey Verant! Make it so I can zone and stay hidden! J

    Editorial by Oghma
