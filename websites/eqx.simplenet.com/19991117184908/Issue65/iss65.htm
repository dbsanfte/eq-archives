Creating a Guild

I decided to write down some things about how the F'leurs DuMal came about to chronicle the creation of a role-playing idea and how a guild formed around it. The best guilds out there are the ones that have a central theme the can be agreed upon by all members. It's immaterial whether the theme is a lofty one or an evil one, as long as the members all agree on it and act accordingly. These notes aren't really a how-to guide to guild creation, more of a how-did-we-come-up-with-this narrative. Your mileage may vary.

    Article by Oghma
