Roleplaying Religion in EverQuest


Rallos Zek, the Warlord


Rallos rules the Plane of War with an iron fist.  His skin is jet black with visible pulsating lava-like veins.  His world is a scene of constant strife and battle, corpses littering the ground, and the air dark and filled with smoke. 		What would you do if you wished to worship Rallos Zek?  How would you approach his sphere of influence as a character?  Is this a deity you wish to worship whole-heartedly, as a cleric, or as a lay person?  Is this a deity you want to try to influence in your favor?  Or a deity you wish to placate, and ignore you?  How do you figure this out?

Any description of a deity give you clues to temperament, interests, "alignment", and areas of control.  From these indicators you could develop an ethos and style of worship for your character.  There are a few more deities at Everquest to look at, but I chose Rallos Zek to start with. 		Let’s start with "alignment". For lack of a better term, alignment refers to a general world view which guides action and temperament. In most rpgs, alignment has three categories along two axis. One is the order/chaos axis, usually ranging from Lawful through Neutral to Chaotic. The order/chaos axis shows whether you are aligned toward individual over society (chaos) or society over the individual (order). Thus Spock’s quote: "The needs of the many outweigh the needs of the few". The other axis is the good/evil axis, which determines the means you use to meet the ends of the order/chaos axis. It runs from good through neutral to evil. Determining a gods alignment, then determines for a good part their worshippers world view.
