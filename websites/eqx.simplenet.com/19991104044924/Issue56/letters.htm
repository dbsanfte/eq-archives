ISSUE # 56

January 9th - January 15th


Letters to the Editors

The Travelers Tales were I believe one of the best things about this newspaper! I have read all the stories but none have kept me coming back waiting on the edge for the next issue. I really have to hand it to Ozymandius they are great stories. I find myself now sitting in the middle of the week thinking about what will happen next to Winter, Maclir, Ozy, and the rest of the gang. Each tale has kept me in great suspense. Each time you think you know what will happen there is a new twist and you go back to guessing.

They have now been gone for 2 weeks! I thought maybe the first week Ozymandius just didn't have time to write one or something but now it has been two weeks without it. I know it isn't a regular feature but there has been one every week for the past eight weeks. I was just wondering what happened and decided I would ask. Any kind of reply would be greatly welcomed. I thank thee for taking the time to read this.

Your Devoted Reader,

~~Mightyraven~~

[Ed. Ozy took a break for the holidays. I guess that's fair... But the tales continue this week.]
