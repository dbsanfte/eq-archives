ISSUE # 88

August 21th - August 27th


Nine Hours

I learned a lot in the land of Norrath on this day. If you have a moment you could sit down a listen. I only hope you receive a sliver of the knowledge I have gained. Today in the land of Norrath I died six times. How you ask? Oh, no worries, I will tell you how my poor child!

    Twice I drown
    Once to a mob of Orcish brutes
    Once to a mob of Dervish Cutthroats
    Once to a Hill Giant
    Once to the poison of a Darkweed Snake

During nine hours of play this day I spent six hours within the last bubble of my 12th level.

I spent four of those six within the second half of the aforementioned last bubble.

After nine hours of play this day I reached the 13th level.

After nine hours of play this day I learned the basics of Duel Wield.

After nine hours of play this day I have memorized almost every word to every good Garth Brooks song.

After nine hours of play this day I learned the two most basic rules of life:

    1) one needs air to breathe. Come up out of the water every few moments lest you drown

    2) don't pick fights with a numerically superior foe. They will win every time.

After nine hours of play this day I realized that girls are bad like my older brother has always told me. They distract us from the important things, like breathing.

After nine hours of play this day I learned kindness when I donated little things to the young ones of the land.

After nine hours of play this day I learned anger, at those who would kill my guards in the name of experience, and unknowingly hurt the young ones whom those guards were protecting.

After nine hours of play this day I realized it takes a man to sacrifice and it takes only a boy to kill.

After nine hours of play this day I realized how truly comfortable my chair is.

After nine hours of play this day I realized that being a dark elf isn't all bad. Right before this realization a Dragoon killed my comrades. I had the same revelation an hour later when the Dragoon returned.

After nine hours of play this day I realized I should have put my computer on the other side of the room. My legs got cold from being above the air conditioning vent.

After nine hours of play this day I learned satisfaction, as I logged out, still of the 13th level.

After nine hours of play this day I came back to reality and odds are I will die tomorrow and lose the 13th level again.

After nine hours of play this day I left the land of Norrath, and I came back to the real world.

After nine hours of play this day I wished all I really had to worry about was that damn hill giant.

I hope you learned something from my nine hours of play this day.

    Article by Kardde
