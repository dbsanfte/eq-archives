Plot

Personally, I would like to see the world of everquest filled with complex power structures and plots.   Mainly, I would like to see Everquest be plot-driven.  I would like to see Everquest have a focus, where for example, Meridian does not.   Rather than inject events into the gaming environment on occasion, I would like to see continuous plots active in Everquest, alterable by players, and not just when the staff suddenly  declares   "ok,
there is an opportunity to change stuff now".

I have yet to see an OMG that had delivered a game based around plot.  Usually, like with Ultima Online, they just shove in game play at random; "Hmm, this migh tbe neat to do".   No.    I hope that the EQ team has created a plot and created game play to represent, express, continue and change that plot.  I have always found

this  kind of  game to  be  more fun: just look at the Final Fantasy series (it was done that way).

Sir Thomas Moore, author of "Utopia" expressed that a society founded upon and developed according to good principles would become a good society.   A game is the same way, and one of the single greatest principles a game needs to be based around is plot.
