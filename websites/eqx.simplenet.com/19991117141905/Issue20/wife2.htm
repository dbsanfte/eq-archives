How to Geektify Your Wife 101

I slammed the mouse down on the desk in an attempt to punish my computer for my obvious lack of genius.

"What are those little things for?" My Wife said as she smudged her fingers on the screen of my monitor. 		"Ammo Cells for energy weapons" I replied, as I wiped off the screen.

"Why didn’t you pick it up?" she questioned.

"I didn’t need it" 		Now starting to get annoyed my twelve-year-old walked in. Pulling up a chair on the opposite side me, she sat down. A moment of silence passed as everyone took in the action on the screen. Adrenaline was pumping hard in my veins as they began to swell on my forehead.
