ISSUE # 65

March 13th - March 19th


Letters to the Editors

To the Staff at EQExpress:

Thank you for your time and devotion to this wonderful site. Although and avid paper and pen role-player in my younger days, the time and connections to continue playing fell by the wayside. Imagine how excited I was to hear of a game that combined the setting and opportunity to re-experience those games on my own. I bought a copy of UO a little over a year ago, having never played an online game before, I was lost. I love video games so believing I could survive in this world I set off to enjoy my new found freedom. Little did I know.....

I learned about Everquest a long time ago, while still playing UO. I would look at the site and long expectantly. I applied for beta testing. (Although I was accepted for phase 4 I was not able to get my response back in time) So I must wait.

Being also a new comer to the net ment I was surprised to see a site like yours. I had become cynical about the web, due to my lack of knowledge and lack of apparent substance of the web.

Upon discovering your site one night I was amazed. I then proceeded to devour everything. The stories are incredible, I personally think they should be compiled and published. I would encourage you to motivate those that have not finished there's to do so. I have learned so much from reading this site, NOT just about everquest but role-playing in general. You have also made me more aware of the issues surrounding online gaming.

Now for my question. I have read on the official Everquest site about planes of existence. Other than the material plane of Norrath, I have never heard one word about these planes on this site. Maybe you could shed some light on the subject for us. I am mearly curious about the subject and would like to know more about it. Since it seems like it could be a large portion of the game a few comments on this subject are more than justified.

Well I know you are all busy and I do not want to keep you from your excellent writing I will close for now. Once again thank you for your time a nd please keep it up. There are still those out there that are not playing YET.

Sincerely David L Dobbs
