Q: How do I successfully teach a language and what are the requirements of doing so?
Kilroy

A: The essential answer here is that you can only teach someone a language if you have a higher proficiency at it than they do and you can only teach them up to the level of your own skill. Before you can teach a language, then, you need to gain as much skill as possible in it. To do this (after you practice the language at least once at your guild), you can group with an expert in the language you want to know and through some friendly spam, raise your skill level. To turn around and then teach the language, you find a student, group with him or her, and then send friendly spam to them in the language you wish to teach. Of course, if you wish to become a professional language teacher, it’s a good idea, through diplomacy with all races, to become as proficient in as many languages as your skill list will allow you.

Q: I'm a necromancer. I noticed something about my pet the other day, he had an orc pic once, a rusty dagger the other time, and he did 14 damage! Does it actually matter what weapon you give your pet? I would have a lot more money if I could sell all those rusty halberds and 2 handed swords.
Baloo, lvl 7 necromancer, Follower of Innoruuk

A: You can thank the Pillar of Wisdom, Teranil, for helping me out with this one. It can matter what weapon you give the pet but whether it does or not depends on what the base damage of the pet is. To determine how to equip your pet, first figure out how much damage the pet does on its own, without a weapon. Then, if the weapon will do more damage than the pet, you can give the weapon to the pet to wield. If the pet’s damage is already higher than the weapon, then of course you won’t need to equip the pet. It is important to consider the type of weapon too. Teranil says that if you have a lower level pet, it’s good to give them a 2h high damage weapon, while if you have a higher level pet, you’ll want to give it 2 1h weapons so the pet can dual wield.

Q: Do you have any bardic theories on what REALLY happened to the Combine Empire since Verant isn’t talking? There are rumors flying around that there is a Combine city in Kunark, but I'll believe that when I see Kunark in a week.

A: Of course there is a bardic theory on what really happened to the Combine Empire. There is a bardic theory for everything, and usually more than one actually. My own pet theory is that the Combine Empire was destroyed when some arrogant high elven tailor decided that half-elves should never wear dresses. Immediately after all soft, beautiful, comfortable dresses were removed from every half-elven wardrobe, the entire empire crumbled leaving only the shells of the teleport spires found throughout Norrath as a reminder that the power of the Empire was a mere illusion compared to the power of half-elven females deprived of their favorite accoutrements. Now, if there proves to be a Combine city in Kunark, perhaps what rightfully belongs to the half-elven wardrobes will be restored and an additional small measure of Combine influence will return to the realms. Should anyone be willing to brave the terrors of the unknown in search of a dress, please let me know so I may accompany you.

Q: After reading Brackiss's tips on keyboard setup, I decided I'd bless you with my own. *Smiles*. My setup is optimized for using both the keyboard and the mouse at the same time. I have W as move forward, S as back, A as left, and D as right. Then right underneath, I have X as autoattack, and Z as crouch, and C as Consider. With this layout, your left hand has access to all the hot keys, all the speaking related keys, such as R and T, and even your overlay display key ~. Also, I often use the mouse to move, too, and I wasn't sure if many knew this but if you have mouselook on, and hold right click, and then hold left click while still holding right click, you can move in that direction!
~Wyndorn Windchaser

A: Thanks for writing. I think your suggestions will give some good ideas to players seeking ways to modify their keyboard assignments.
