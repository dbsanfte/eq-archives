Charles Flock always places .-Friar or ?-Friar at the end of messages. I think we can read it like .-F riar (some spelling error in "riar", I think it's "rear"). And I can decode this sign as: I'm a one-eyed vampire with a broken tooth; I show you my rear. And I can suppose only, how to interpret ?-F riar (I think %-6 for .-F) I have one question only: Does he show us his rear, or have mind there?
