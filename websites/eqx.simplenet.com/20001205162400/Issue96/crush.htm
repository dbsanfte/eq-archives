ISSUE # 96

October 16th - October 22nd


Crushsong

From atop a ragged pile of skulls
The Orcish King did reign
The sound of armoured footfalls alerted him
To an intruder to be slain

Swiftly did he put aside
His cup of evil brew
"The day you swine came to my Isle
Shall be that day you'll rue."

He armed himself with wicked haste
And donned his chainmail bright
With lust and hate for Good did he
Anticipate the fight

And then around the corner
Came a figure clad in Plate
"I come to slay thee Orc-Lord
Prepare to meet thy fate"

And deep in Crush's heart awoke
Unbridled hate unreasoned
"I'll slay thee upstart silver elf
And serve thee up well seasoned"

"For Tunare and the Koada' dal"
Was all the Knight replied
But softly drew his blade and stood
The battle to decide

In dark and evil glory
The Orc-King then did sneer
"Give my regards to your Elven Bitch
For soon you'll be quite near"

With a clash of steel they came to blows
For hours they did strive
Mighty wounds were dealt by each
'Till one was left alive

Then down upon his fallen foe
The wounded Knight did gaze
And slowly home he made his way
Though wounded for many days.

Yet despite his valiant struggle
And his prowess on the field
By blessings of the darker sort
The Orc-King did not yield

Then did Crush's hell-spawned master
Refuse to end the strife
And thus by dark communion
Returned his flesh to life

And so sits the Orcish Monster still
Upon his carved plush throne
And slays all those who intrude within
His Kingdom of Crushbone

    Tale by Corius of Kelethin
