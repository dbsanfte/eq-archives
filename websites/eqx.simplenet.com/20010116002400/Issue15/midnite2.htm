"Hurry... Ana!"

Ana was rather content, dozing off; all the exitement of watching her apple pies cool down wearing thin, after the first min; more or less...

"ANA... ana.. oh NoO... here they come..."

Somethin’ scurrying around the corner catches Ana’s eyes. Rubbing her bleary eyes, she squints to see through the midmorning sun, into the darker corner of the storage hut; where she and the freshly baked apple pies had taken refuge... 		A company, numbering some two score Holy Warriors, had marched into her village, early this morning. They claimed a war party of barbarians was last seen in the vicinity; so, the Garrison marched down from their Outpost nearby that they liked to call SevenorEight, to guard the village.

Of course, Ana knew THAT was just an Excuse; she was rather convinced they came for her Apple Pies, that won her Red Ribbons every SpringFest for the last three years. How they knew she baked some, she had no o idea... But nothing could be beyond those Holy Guys...

There it is...again.. This time, feeling less sleepy, Ana got up, or somewhat. And crawls to that corner... and sees a small dog crouching...

"hi.. Small Dog... wat ‘tcha doin’ back there?" 		or rather, a large rat...
