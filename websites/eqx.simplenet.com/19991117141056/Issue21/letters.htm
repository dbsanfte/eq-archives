Razor 		I am not sure why, but the general idea on the irc and message boards is that people are going to magically roleplay in EQ. Well time to wake up, it's simply NOT going to happen. Let me tell you why:

1. The same kids (I say kids because the fact is most people in UO that just wanted to exploit/inflict misery upon others were 12 yearolds) that were assholes in UO are going to be assholes in EQ. The kids people assholes in UO are bored of the game just like us and are looking to EQ as an answer. Let's face it UO was an experiement to see if Online gaming at a mass scale was possible, and from that perspective it was successful (however very unsuccessful in the gameplay perspective, but this is neither the time nor the place). People's general feeling towards EQ is that these kids will be gone, well this is not the case they will be there, not roleplaying, being assholes.

2. Some people just prefer not to roleplay. I am one of these people. I just don't get any enjoyment from roleplaying. I know alot of you RPers are saying, "it's a roleplaying game, if you don't want to roleplay, don't play the game". I could give a shit what you think I should do in this game that I am paying $10 a month for. Bottom line is it's a game and I want to have fun playing it and I will play it as I so choose to.

I will say, however, that EQ has the tools to roleplay MUCH easier than UO. The race concept is being handled beautifully and I really like the idea of differenet languages for different races. EQ has more traditional RPG elements than UO did and I believe SISA will encourage roleplaying, but this does not mean that the majority will roleplay. The minority will be the the roleplayers, I believe.
