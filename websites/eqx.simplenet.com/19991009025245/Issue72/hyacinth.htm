ISSUE # 72

May 1st - May 7th


Transcription of a conversation with: Hyacinth, Troll-Singer

Yu! Hummie! Wait! Me no bash you! Yu no run, me no bash you!

You funny hummie with fether and sheepskin?

Gud! Me got use for yu! I got story.

Me purdy Troll lady. Me called Hyacinth. Me saggy in gud places.

Trollies keep eye on Hummies. We keep eye so we no wen dey want to bash us, or we get hungry.

You no worry, me not hungry, ate nice Plumpling wile lookin for yu.

Me hear Hummies have big party. Me hear big Hummie called Ji-Tree make songs and purdy lights. Me make purdy lights too so me want to see Ji-Tree, even tho he a Hummie. I tink maybe he lurn me de songs before I bash him. But stupid Hummie guards, dey no let Hyacinth see Ji-Tree. Dey bash me gud.

Me go talk to smart Troll-lady. Ask about Troll-singing. She say big gods no make Trolls for singing. Me say "Dat stupid! Me bash big gods!" Smart Troll-lady bash me gud.

Me not stopped. Me not smart, but me stubborn. Me be first Troll-singer. Me make funny words and purdy lights for yu. You show world gud Troll songs.

Me start with poems. Dey short, but dey gud. Me sing poems to Troll-boy and he say me poems hurt more dan me bash. Me tink dat gud.

You scratch wit fether. I no bash you.

Redy?

Dis me first poem. Me tink of it when bashing frogluks. Me sing it loud all night. I stop when big Basher bashed me gud.

Me bash frogluk
Club go Crack!
Frogluk go Splat!
Me bash frogluk

Dat gud for first poem ya!

De Hummies have a poem dey call 'eye scream'. Eye scream? Eyes don't scream! Stupid Hummies. Me change it gud.

You bash!
I bash!
We all bash for Hummie mash!

Dat gud poem. All Basher Trollies like dat one.

Dis gud poem too. But it long. I pound it in sand for you.

I bash
and I crash
I smash
and I mash
I bash and crash smash mash and thrash.
I whump
and I thump
I bump
and I clunk
Dis poem's too long I thunk.

Dat gud ya! Dat Ji-Tree, I bash him gud wit my poems! Dis nekst one is tuffest ever. Hyacinth sat tree days to tink it!

I tromp
and I clomp
in purdy swamp,
de frokluks and snakes dey all cringe
and run at me passing
cuz de air I gassing,
cuz I on a dwarf-pickle binge!

Dat funni poem! Dwarf pickles make big ripping noyzes in Trollies. Dat why poem is funni.

Dat all I tink. You scratch gud on sheepskins, tell wurld of first Troll-singer. I tink more poems later. I find you agin. I no bash you gud.

:end transcription.

    Tale by Scarmig
