Saturday, May 29, 2004
 

A good friend of mine named Erik threw a party this Saturday as a half-house warming half-labor day shindig. He invited a few people from work to make the trekk out to his new place in Leander (quite the drive!). Mark and Darren cooked up a storm on the BBQ as we indulged in carnivorous activities mixed with a bit of beer and PS2.

The area where he lives is absolutely beautiful hill country, I couldn't help but take a calander shot of my ride parked out front. Erik shares the pad with his critters Sassy (bird) and Kenobi (dog). Towards the end of the evening I shared a good conversation with Erik and his brother who are both extremely knowledgable in the realm of martial arts... I believe I'm going to search for a good Wing Chun school here in Austin if possible :)



- George @ 5:00 PM
