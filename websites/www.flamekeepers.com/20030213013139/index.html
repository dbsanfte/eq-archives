First Featured FlameKeeper of 2003

 

Posted by Jacquara on Wednesday, February 05 @ 04:13:09 CST (50 reads)
Topic FKoN News

Collecting the last of the month's Featured FlameKeeper nominations from her mailbox, the wee hobbit priestess places them neatly in her knapsack with the other votes.  Clicking the heels of her Journeymen's Boots together, she sets off at high speed to the meet her sylvan sister in the treetop city of Kelethin...

Arriving at the base of one of the city's wooden lifts, she is relieved to see that the lift is already descending to meet her.  "'Tis a good thing... I can barely reach the plungermajiggies that makes these dem lifts work!"  She smiles at Guard Brookrock, humming a happy tune as she boards the lift that will carry her up to meet Faélicia.

"HALT, MISS TIGERSBANE!" exclaims Guard Brookrock as he recognizes her.  "I have a scroll for you."
