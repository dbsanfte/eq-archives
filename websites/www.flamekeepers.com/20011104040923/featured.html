Featured FlameKeeper

Each month, guild members are asked to pick amongst their clan and choose one whom they feel has been an exemplary member. In a silent vote, every member of FKoN explains why that particular person has earned his or her admiration. To these members, we shout our congratulations and thanks for being who they are, a burning torch who we strive to be!

August 2001 – QuestMaster-Elder Sentanta Ulsterman

Because Sent is on LOA, I was unable to catch him for an interview. However, I am able to say with pure heart felt emotion that a finer friend cannae be found.

Sent roams the lands with an open heart, easy smile and kind word for all. Not to mention offering aid whenever he is able. If I can be half of the good soul he is, I count it a day well done.

Thanks to you Sent, for being you are, a treasured brother and esteemed friend - Burn Bright, Keeper!
