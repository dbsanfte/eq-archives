How To Join FKoN

Well met, Seekers all! FKoN is glad to see yer interest in our fine clan. It be not an overly easy route to join but it be not an overly ‘ard route. Please make sure ye read these guidelines thor’o’ly an’ ye unnerstan’ ‘em. Ifn ye dinnae, ASK! **grins**

We be open to all race, class an’ alignments. We ‘eartily suggest ye do be of the 20th season however. Those o’ younger seasons are still warmly invited to get to know our members but we cannae offer ye a tag until the attainment o’ yer 20th season.

Our request then be as follows:
Get to know our members. Travel wit’ them, hunt wit’ them, spend a moment in tavern tossin’ back an ale an’ chattin’, become an active visitor on our boards.

Once ye have gained friendship, ye must obtain 3 letters supportin’ yer admission to FkoN an’ ha’e them sent to the Council o' Elders. These letters may be written by anyone exceptin’ Elders an’ Apprentices.

Upon our reception o’ these 3 scrolls, all officers will be notified that they may tag ye upon their earliest convenience. Ye will be tagged as an Apprentice fer a period o’ 90 days – time ye will use to complete yer assigned quests an’ get to know the Keepers!

As an Apprentice ye will be treated as a member of FKoN an’ by wearing our tag, ye will behave accordingly – meanin’ ye will follow the rules o’ the guild, hae access to our boards, an’ be welcome to all events.

Ye will be given two quests that will be posted in the Seekers Forum. Ye will have 90 days to complete these quests (which will be based on your level upon tag). When yer 90 days ha’e been completed, the guild leader will be notified she can search for ye. She will ask ye a few questions o’ FKoN, minor ones to ensure ye’ve reviewed our website an’ are familiar wit’ our policies. Upon answerin’ these, ye will be granted Initiate Status, a full member o’ FKoN. This means ye will be asked to pick a “newbie” location an’ the guild will join ye there for an Initiation Day Party on yer behalf.

Initiation Day Party ye ask? Aye, tis so! We will ask ye to perform a “leap of faith” along wit’ joinin’ ye at yer chosen “newbie” locale. While there, we will ‘elp younglings as we can, be it food, water, buffs, gear giveaways. And we will do it on yer behalf – shouts will ring out that “YOU ha’e become an Initiate of the Keepers. In Celebration, YOU have invited us to bring ‘elp an’ fun to this zone fer a time!”

At any time ifn ye have questions, please feel free to post them in the Seekers Forum.
