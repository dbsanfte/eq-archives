Featured FlameKeeper

Each month, guild members are asked to pick amongst their clan and choose one whom they feel has been an exemplary member. In a silent vote, every member of FKoN explains why that particular person has earned his or her admiration. To these members, we shout our congratulations and thanks for being who they are, a burning torch who we strive to be!

Well this was interesting..interviewing myself..…

Talk to yourself alot?

Heh, jes for this.

*slams ale in one gulp* I plead tempporary insanity..no wait, erm, that isnae right - OH! The FIFTH! I plead the fifth. Ayep. *orders more ale, blushing*

Anything you’d like to say to your family and friends?

Aye, when I started this guild over a year ago, I had no idea it would turn out even better than I imagined. Thanks to all who've ever held the Torch. Burn Bright, my lovelies. *Smiles and waves*
