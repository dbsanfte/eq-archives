How-ta Potter wit' Dwerin Dankwatre

Arr, matees! This 'ere's a little advice on usin' yer pottery skill as best ye can. If ye don't care fer then a ye can show yerself the door er it'll be shown to ye. Fer those o' ye who'er stayin', yer in fer a treat, arr har har!.

Let me start by sayin' that pottery isn't exactly the most lucrative trade skill ta invest in…but that's not sayin' it's a bad one ta invest in either, arr. Thing is, pottery be useful in the developin' o' other trade skills more than bein' used on it's own. Most o' the things ye'll be makin' through pottery are used fer either cookin' er fer makin' poison, arr. 'Owever, ye can make a pretty penny through the construction o' wisdom deities, which can be sold fer anywhere from 100-200 platinum while costin' only a quarter o' that ta make.

An overview o' the process is thus, matees…ye'll need ta have a pottery wheel an' a kiln, one sketch o' the item ye want ta make, a block (er more) o' clay o' the right size, and a water flask (summoned an' foraged water don't work) fer each sketch you combine on the pottery wheel. When ye've successfully made sumthin' on the wheel ye can put it in the kiln with an' appropriate firin' sheet ta get a finished product, arr.

Most items require a high quality firin' sheet. There be a skill roll with the use o' both the pottery wheel an' the kiln, 'owever, the kiln will tell ye yer item is trivial long 'afore the pottery wheel does, so don't mind the message from the kiln. Also, ye almost never fail at firin' an item ye've just made on the pottery wheel….arr, expect most o' yer failures ta come on the wheel.

Well now, about getting' started….ye'll need 35 plat er so in order to become a master at pottery. 'Ere's whachee do. Go ta yer guild an' put one practice inta pottery. Ye only need one 'ere cuz pottery raises rather quickly on its own, matee. Arr, then go buy yerself a Basic Pottery Book (which shows ye yer recipes), 50 small jar sketches, 150 bowl sketches, 200 flasks o' water, an' 200 small blocks o' clay….don't worry none about the firin' sheets, ye won't be needed them ta raise yer skill, arr.

Then, go blow the rest o' yer platinum on brandy, arr har har! Ye'll be wantin' it after sittin' in front o' that wheel fer four er five hours, matees. Next, find yerself a pottery wheel ta use, there be many throughout the lands ta use, in fact, there be one in most every city on the map, arr. Now, ye put one flask o' water, one small jar sketch, an' one small block o' clay on the wheel an' combine 'em. Use up all yer small jar sketches in this manner an' then move on ta the bowl sketches. Ye'll prolly be makin' several runs back ta a vendor what sells small blocks o' clay simply cuz they don't stack, matees….so's I suggest ye get yerself a number o' backpack so ye don't have ta make so many trips, arr.

Continue workin' with the bowls till yer skill reaches 102 Congratulations matee, yer a master!….ye may have ta go buy a few extree bowl sketches an' flasks but ye shouldn't need more'n 30 more….I followed instructions fer 200 bowl sketches an' found I had very many leftover, so I suggest ye start with 150 an' buy more as needed, arr.

Once yer skill is 102 ye'll need ta start makin' other things ta raise yer skill….my personal suggestion bein' poison vials (there are three types, start with basic poison vials). Arr, ta make these ye'll need an animal skin each time ye use the pottery wheel in addition ta the sketch, flask, and block o' clay. Most any skin er pelt'll work, though not all so be careful…I find that zombie skins work best cuz they stack an' none o' the other skins do….the best place I know ta get zombie skins is the Estate of Unrest south of the butcherblock mountains an' dagnor's cauldron. Make these poison vials until ye acquire 148 skill.

This will take much longer ta do than it took ye ta reach 102, even though it be less far ta go, arr. At this point small wisdom deities will be trivial ta ye and ye can make an' sell 'em fer a fair profit. The wis deity recipe requires a sands of ro which, from another player, can run ye 25-50pp….they also drop off sand giants in the Oasis of Marr an' Deserts o' Ro. Beyond 148 skill the only items that'll raise yer skill be more advanced poison vials (i.e. lined poison vials an' finally, sealed poison vials)….the lined vials will get ye ta 168 skill an' the sealed vials can raise ye ta 188…beyond that, there be nuthin' this pirate knows o' ta raise yer skill anymore save mebbe new velious recipes which I 'aven't tried yet, but feel free ta try yerself, matee.

Where? Workin' in North Freeport is prolly yer best choice. The vendor named Winoyn sells all the none poison vial sketches ye'll need. She be found next ta the freeport bank in a white plaster an' wood buildin', arr. Also in North Freeport be Groflah's Forge, where Kyrin Steelbone sells blocks of clay o' various sizes. Merra Clayfinger be located at the easternmost inn in Eastern Commonlands. 'E sells yer blocks o' clay, vial sketches, glass shards (needed fer some recipes), firin' sheets, an' the Basic Pottery Book ye'll likely want (there be a number of similar vendors located throughout the whole o' Norrath). Ta find a pottery wheel in NFp, head fer the bank, arr. As ye enter the Freeport Market turn right, ye'll see a halflin' wench an' an open grassy area…there be both a pottery wheel an' a kiln there.

Finally, drink that brandy I telled ye ta buy, matees! ARR Har har! Here be a table courtesy o' eqtraders.com which ye may find useful.
