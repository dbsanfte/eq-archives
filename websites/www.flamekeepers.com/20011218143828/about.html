FlameKeepers of Norrath
A Lil' About Us

(A short female dwarf stands afore ye, grinnin'. Her cheeks are ruddy, her eyes merry and a sassy smile adorns her face. She bows…)

"Hail and well met, wanderer. I be Taylah Triskelion, Guild leader o' the FlameKeepers, at yer service. Tho I claim little talent in wordsmithin', it falls ta me ta give voice ta our past. I hope I do it justice."

(She sits in a large chair next to a fireplace. She beckons to an empty seat across from her an' points to the table next to it. A tray o' beverages sits fer yer enjoyment. Ye choose yer favorite an' settle back to hear her tale.)

The Founding

Many moons ago, two heart-sisters, Selkye Stormwillow an' Taylah Triskelion, wandered the land o' Yserbius. This well-peopled land nestled near an awe-inspirin' yet allegedly dormant volcano. It came ta pass that the Powers That Be re-ignited the mighty 'Cano. Chaos ensued an' their homelands were destroyed, its people dispersed like leaves upon the wind.

So they traveled far an' near, together an' apart, always seekin' a new home an' others with the same belief they held dear. The younger sister, Selkye, arrived in Norrath, in the county o' Rathe, an' found the lands an' its people ta her likin'. She summoned her elder sister, Taylah. Upon her arrival the two traveled the width an' breadth o' Norrath. Finally, they resolved ta create a new home here, in this bountiful an' populous land.

(Tay stops fer a moment, sipping from her own drink wit' relish, then begins again…)

In their wanderin's they met many, but one who particularly embodied all that they believed. This was the wise an' affable Benecleod Shinkicker. Quickly he won the friendship o' the lasses.

The trio decided ta create a fellowship based on five simple beliefs. Friendship, Laughter, Adventure, Mirth, and Entertainment. Thus began the search fer others who shared their hearts-code.

(Ye lean forward, askin' if there be more ta the tale. The cleric smiles, a wistful expression on her face an' nods. She answers…)

"Aye, neighbor. I can tell ye more but be forewarned, this is a sad part of the Keeper history…"

The Flame Flickers

Alas, almost a year after the formation o' FKoN, a time o' great turmoil an' hard choices came about. Our lands were ta be split by the Powers That Be. Some Keepers chose ta remain, some ta depart. Sorrow an' nostalgic trips were plentiful as the days neared 'til family an' friends would be parted.

The CoE chose not ta keep a presence in both the new county (Zebuxoruk) an' the old (Rathe). This meant those who remained were no longer Keepers - except in our hearts. Unfortunately we lost many fine members, from Initiate ta Elder. Yet the FLAME still burns an' so shall we continue ta feed it.

(Here Tay points to a closed door on the far side of the room. Beautifully carved and well polished, it wears a small silver placard.)

"Thru that door is the center o' our Compound, the Pyre. The walls have been turned into memorials - the Walls o' Remembrance. An eternally burning torch hangs in a sconce, burnin' fer each Keeper who has ever passed thru these halls."
"An' now, m'friend, let me bring ye up ta date fer we have chatted over-long an' I have tasks ta attend ta…."

Relighting the Pyre

The Powers That Be were successful in the split an' now the Keepers sought ta put down roots in the new county o' Zebuxoruk yet familiar land of Norrath. Many Keepers came through the split an' with this core, we began anew ta rebuild.

Steady She Burns

An' now it has been many moons and seasons since that split, an' it be pleasin' ta see that indeed the Keepers o' the Flame ha'e held their torches aloft, thru it all. We ha'e grown in numbers some, learned much, an' worked to build relations wit' several other guilds.

We have done much an' hope to do more, wit' others of like mind at our side. I hope ye shall wander the Compound an' learn all ye can o' us!

"Now then m'friend, I must be off. A pleasure ta meet ye an' may Blessed Brell watch over ye. Burn Bright!"

(The dwarf stands, her drink an' tale finished. She bows to ye afore she disappears thru the Pyre door, closin' it ahind her.)
