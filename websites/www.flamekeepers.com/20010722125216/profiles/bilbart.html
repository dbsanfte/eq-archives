Bio

At the vigorous age of 62, Bil is just a wee lad compared to his adventuring bucko, the wizened ol’ rogue Giggen (who we all know is really just a softy at heart). The bloody mayor kicked Bil an’ the ol’ rogue outta town, so young Bil, bein’ not exactly well off, took learnin’ the ways o’ the lands an’ drivin’ a taxi to pay the for expenses incurred (mostly repairs caused by the illustrious Gigs an’ his lady friends at the inn). Now, to make up for a guilt complex caused by the ol’ rogue, Bil searches the lands for the perfect hug….with Karana’s blessing he shall one day succeed.
