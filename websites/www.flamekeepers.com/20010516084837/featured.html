FlameKeepers of Norrath
Featured FlameKeeper

Each month, guild members are asked to pick amongst their clan and choose one whom they feel has been an exemplary member. In a silent vote, every member of FKoN explains why that particular person has earned his or her admiration. To these members, we shout our congratulations and thanks for being who they are, a burning torch who we strive to be!

May 2001 – Elder Kazho Icehawk

Congratulations Kaz, on a well-deserved honor.  I caught the barbarian lug frisking with some wood elves and here is what she had to say…

What is it about wood elves anyway??

Jeez…haven’t ye ever seen one frolicking thru the lands with little to nothin’ on?  Asides, leather becomes them…especially collars ::evil grin::.

Two words to describe your guild family?

Freaks and friends.

Which came first – the chicken or the egg?

Well, the egg of course….right??

What one thing would you change about being a shaman, if any?

That we could purchase wood elf slaves right there at McDaniel’s’ in Halas.  Well, that and we could memorize more spells at once.  The Tribunal graced me with all these spells ..why can’t I use more than 8 at a time?  Guess I would have to talk to The Nameless about that one…can anyone get me an appointment for that? ::chuckles::

Anything you’d like to say to your family and friends?

No.

Heh, really though, I’d like to say that this month I share this honor with the noble, ale-drinkin’, shorty, shrimpy dorf known as Taylah.  Without her, none of this would be. (Ye better put this in or I will go back and add it myself ::smiles::)

Secondly, this is the most noble clan I know in all the lands of Norrath and I feel graced to be within its ranks.

Thirdly, do I get woodelves as a prize now???

Burn Bright!! Elder Kazho Icehawk
