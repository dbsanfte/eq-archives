How To Join FKoN

Well, wanderer have ye learned enough o' our clan ta want ta join? We are open ta all races, classes, alignments, an' levels. Our standards are high but fair.

Firstly send a scroll (in character) ta the CoE stating yer name, level, class, who referred ye (if'n anyone) an' any additional information ye'd like us ta know about ye. THIS IS MANDATORY. No applicants will be considered unless a scroll is sent from the link below. If it is temporarily unavailable, please make sure ta visit the message board an' post in our Recruit Forum.

Upon receipt o' this missive, a response (within 3-5 days) from the CoE will direct ye ta the Seekers Forum where a RP quest will be given ta ye (based on level/ability). Ye must complete the quest an'd show proof ta an Advisor or Elder in-game. At that time, ye'll be instructed on what the next step is (dependin' on quest) an' given a probationary tag (Apprentice).

Apprenticeship lasts 30 active days. At the end o' this time period, the CoE will vote ta induct or detag. In yer 30 days as an Apprentice, ye must also have two full Keepers submit a letter ta the CoE (in character) supportin' yer full induction in the pyre. Durin' this formal induction ceremony, the two keepers who sent a scroll in yer support will also be asked ta stand before all the Keepers in support o' ye becoming an Initiate o' the Flame.

Lookin' forward ta hearin' from ye soon - please send yer scroll ta contact the Council o' Elders. Ye will be contacted within 3-5 days. Until then, Burn Bright!
