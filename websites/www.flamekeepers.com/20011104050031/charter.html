FlameKeepers of Norrath
Our Mission & Charter

"..Tis the desire o' this Fellowship that these five words (Friendship, Laughter, Adventure, Mirth, Entertainment) combine ta create our code o' belief an' that our members come together as family ta uphold this code."

Here be some general knowledge o' our guild…

We are a role-playing guild, and while not mandatory, feel it adds to the ambience of the game if characters are publicly role-played. By this it is meant that all public actions, shouts, etc be kept 'in character' and use ooc, private tells, and personal conversation for non-role-playing discussions.

Blind to class, race, religion or levels, FlameKeepers of Norrath also disregards alignment. 'Evil' and 'good' characters are both welcome to join. Members are encouraged to work with the Council of Elders and fellow guild mates to create and schedule events, quests, and role-play scenarios for guild and non-guild members. While we enjoy all aspects of EverQuest, our primary goal is always to feed the FLAME. If you are strictly a power-leveler or Phat Lewt player - this is not the guild for you.

Policy & Procedures

We have very few 'set' policies and procedures at this time, though the assumption is as we grow, our need for certain rules will as well.

1. Any member engaging in foul ethics will immediately be removed from the guild. Foul ethics are unacceptable and include kill-stealing, abusive or excessive foul language, and disruption of other player's game enjoyment. "I was role-playing." is not a reasonable excuse.

2. Members may have as many characters as they wish tagged - names may be any they wish and need not be similar.

3. Members may belong to other guilds as well as FlameKeepers.

4. If a member will be absent from game or guild for extended period of time, FKoN asks that notification be made and member will be put on LOA, unless a request is made to be removed from guild ranks. Members not seen for one month (without notification) will placed on MIA for one month, moved to LOA for one month and then removed from roster. You may be re-inducted upon return.

Guild Meeting Protocol

1. In circular formation, Elders will sit together with Advisors to either side of them and all Keepers helping to form the circle.

2. Each Keeper shall bring & hold a torch during all meetings. Extra torches will be available to those without one of their own, but cannae be provided for all. Please bring yer own if at all possible.

3. Guests are always welcome to attend meetings. Prior notice is welcome so that the ArchPriest/ess is aware and can greet appropriately.

4. The center of the circle is reserved for Seekers(recruits), Initiates who will be formally inducted into the guild. Following inductions, the center will be used for promotions/titles, and for award distribution. At all other times the circle will remain unoccupied.

5. Meetings will generally consist of the following:

- Opening

- Formal Inductions

- Promotions/Title Bestowment

- Awards

- General Session & Announcements

- Closing

- Event - if scheduled
