Featured FlameKeeper for March 2003

 

Posted by Jacquara on Sunday, March 16 @ 02:07:45 CST (24 reads)
Topic FKoN News

The wee hobbit priestess comes stumbling out of her hobbithole, squinting in the light of the early morning sun as her bleary eyes adjust. She yawns and stretches, breathing in deeply the still dew-damp air of dawn.

She ambles along lazily to her mailbox (as she does every morning), opening it sluggishly. Reaching in, she fumbles about to check its contents, and draws out a single letter. She rubs her eyes to wipe away the sleep, noting who it is addressed from.

"Mmmystyre Woodsoooonnng," she reads as she yawns again. She stops suddenly, her eyes wide and her sleepy state gone instantaneously.

"MYSTYRE WOODSONG!" she exclaims. "Oh my, this be about the Featured FlameKeeper interview!" With that, she quickly scampers back into her hobbithole to set about the task of editing...


Ahem! Allo friends, 'Keepers, an' gentlekin! I be verily pleased to present to ye the most recently selected winner o' the Featured FlameKeeper elections! This month, let's be all givin' a hearty congratyelations to one o' our kin that we all know an' love--the one, the only...



Congratyelations, sis! Ye verily much be deservin' the recognition! An' now, gentle readers... the interview!

(Side note: I'd also be likin' to thank Mystyre for conductin' the interview for me while I 'ave been busy with RL things for school.)

Arriving at the base of one of the city's wooden lifts, she is relieved to see that the lift is already descending to meet her.  "'Tis a good thing... I can barely reach the plungermajiggies that makes these dem lifts work!"  She smiles at Guard Brookrock, humming a happy tune as she boards the lift that will carry her up to meet Faélicia.
