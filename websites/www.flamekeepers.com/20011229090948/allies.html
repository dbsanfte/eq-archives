FlameKeepers of Norrath
Allies

There be those that wander the lands lending aid in all manners be it healing, donating time or effort, assisting in battle or any manner of help. To those friendly & helpful souls, we raise our torches high & cry, "Burn Bright and Eternal!" Here we shall note any who earn such esteem.

As there are allies, so are there foes. Find out who hath made the Black List.

Guild Friendship Allies - Lone Wolves of Norrath, Nefarious Individuals - FKoN

Burning Flame Alliance(BFA) Allies - Nefarious Individuals, Evil Artists, Fates Messengers - FKoN

Faithful Knights - havin grouped wit da Faithful Knights on more'n wun occashun an havin made friends in particular amongst der crew, matees Baldrick, Downyhill, Grimnirx, Xabiktik (metinkin dat be mispelt but ye get the idear), an Astramaria, who i feel all be a good repursintashun o dat fine crew i be likin ta have dem added ta tha list o allies an perhaps in tha future ta have a more formal guild alliance wit em tho i cannae say i know much detail o their policies at this point, they be quite kind an enthusiastic those i have met an know their places in a group, arr har. I do encourage me matees ta group wit dese Faithful Knights if'n ye git tha chaynst so's we kin all have more matees in that big sea called norrath, arr har har! - Dwerin Dankwatre

Concreto & Chumlee - they are good friends and can be relied upon to do their parts in each battle. Plus, who doesn't want to have an ogre warrior and an erudite necro on his side? - Bilbart DragonRider

Gryfynclaw - For always taking time out of his busy schedule to /tell us the most interesting things. For not being afraid to be the butt of the joke. For continually making us laugh, no matter how much we try not to. For his continued support of the FlameKeepers, particularly as we just started out. - Benecleod & Selkyee

Khervek StormHammer, Clan Icewind - For being a shaman supreme. For his organization of raids and events for FKoN - our 'unofficial' raid organizer! For going way above and beyond the call of duty. For instance, saving me from a certain watery grave after going linkdead in Kedge Keep. Not because he had to, but because he wanted to. That is the kind of attitude missing from a lot of players. - Benecleod

Kolwyn, Evee, Belin - A trio from Clan Icewind who are kind, intelligent, and fun. We value their friendship highly. - Selkyee & Taylah

Unrest Hero - Unrest is one of my favorite zones, though lest ye have a competent and trustworthy group it can cause much anguish. Recently I journeyed there for three days, it is the first day only I shall speak of for the latter two were the opposite of this scenario. I grouped with a well-oiled and communicative group - but one stuck in my mind as excellent in his skills, friendly, and honorable. Zidden Zothe, a high elf cleric whom I was gladdened to meet (a friendly high elf - imagine!) and honored to work in concert with. To him I hold a special salute for jobs well done and new friendships formed - Taylah
