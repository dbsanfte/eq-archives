Welcome! I have attempted to cull information from various sources off the internet to provide you, the viewer, with a "one stop shop" for all your EverQuest needs. All of the links take you to the original locations of these files, this site is merely a compendium of links of quality EQ content to hasten your quest for information. Read on and enjoy, and remember, these may be considered spoilers to your game. If there is anything else you'd like to see on this site, please email your request to eqic@fullbyte.com and I'll try to find the information you need. Also, please stop by and visit our Guestbook if you have any feedback you'd like to offer about this site.
Thank you!! EQIC had over 47,000 page views for the month of December as compared to 24,000 page views for the month of November. We hope this means we're doing something right and our site is beneficial for the EQ players. Thanks again for your support!
As you know, EQIC is a web site unconnected with any major gaming corporation or web presence. This site is created and maintained merely for the needs of the players. We do not get paid for what we do here.
