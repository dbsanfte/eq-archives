Welcome to EQIC!

I have attempted to cull information from various sources off the internet to provide you, the viewer, with a "one stop shop" for all your EverQuest needs. All of the links take you to the original locations of these files, this site is merely a compendium of links of quality EQ content to hasten your quest for information. Read on and enjoy, and remember, these may be considered spoilers to your game. If there is anything else you'd like to see on this site, please email your request to eqic@fullbyte.com and I'll try to find the information you need. Also, please stop by and visit our Guestbook Bulletin Board if you have any feedback you'd like to offer about this site.
As you know, EQIC is a web site unconnected with any major gaming corporation or web presence. This site is created and maintained merely for the needs of the players. We do not get paid for what we do here. In order to support the costs of maintaining a web site on the internet, we have associated ourselves with several online stores where you can find some great products. If you find yourself in need of computer equipment, software, books, music, movies, video games, tools, model airplanes or kids stuff, please consider buying those products at our stores. It will help defray the costs of this web site, and eventually allow us to move to our own domain name site. Thanks for your support!
