"This wide expanse of sand lends itself well to the construction of sand castles - one could even call it a huge beach! Numerous buckets, shovels and other utensils of serious castle building can be found in the picturesque fishing village. Enjoy the surf!

The Inn in the North is one of the few places that sells large sewing kits - probably because some of the giant customers in the south are so close?"
