Steamfont Mountains
last update: August 21,1999
 
 
Surveyed by the late Samwisen, Gnome Enchanter

"This area - my very first - is just sooo beautiful and diverse. Little rats and spiders infest the southern areas whilst majestic drakes swoop in the skies of the north. The beautiful windmills set a very pictoresque background for all kind of slaughter - even your own at the hands of 'the Hero' or any other minotaur...

The only thing really bothering me are the rats - they are of about the size of us gnomes, which really depresses me. Nothing to look down on except from a hilltop."

- Thank you Samwisen!
