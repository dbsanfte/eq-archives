I quit Everquest in November 1999.

So welcome to what I kept of my little Everquest pages.

As a fanatic halfling slayer I used to suggest that members of that race to go elsewhere - my kitchen for example - and make themselves comfortable in my spacious oven. But those days are past ;o)

Yes, I played a Dark Elf shadow knight with a travelling itch that pulled me hither and thither and a mapping urge that got me killed regularily while bent over the parchments. For what I was gaining ever so slowly was a growing collection of perhaps the finest maps in all of Norrath. And that was my ambition: mapping all of Norrath.

But when the game lost it's appeal I had to roll my parchments, pack my pens and move on.

I'd like to thank all the folks from all over the real world that I had the joy of sharing the fun with.
