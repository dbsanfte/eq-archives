Nektulos Forest
last update: August 28,1999
 
 
Surveyed by Soulitaire, Teir'Dal Shadowknight

"Nektulos Forest 'lurks' in almost perpetual darkness - a fitting home for the Dark Elves whose hometown of Neriak lies here. Skeletons and spiders abound and trappers will marvel at the pelts carried by wolves and bears. Halflings grovel along the western side, but I have yet to kill one that leaves me with some halfling meat.

Enjoy the gloom of our home!"
