The Feerrott
last update: September 22,1999
 
 

Surveyed by the late Graggak, Troll Shaman

"The forest of Feerrott is dark and green. Night falls swiftly here, so bring torch. You be quite safe on East side of River. Try not to step into Lizardmen camps if you alone and weak. They do magic on you and kill you. West side of River be much more dangerous. Skeletons there and Lizardmen Warriors. Pyramid and Castle too! You go look.

Oh yes - big fat ugly Ogres live here too. But them be servants of Innoruuk too, so dont hit 'em too hard."

- May you rest in peace Graggak. I hope I continued mapping with your spirit of adventure!
