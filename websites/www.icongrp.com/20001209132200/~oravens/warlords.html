Warlords

Warlords.....include all forms of hand-to-hand fighters. These brave soldiers of the land look death in the eye everyday. The classes in this division are warriors, paladins, monks, and shadow knights. The ranks of this division from highest to lowest are: "Grand Raven", "Great Raven", "High Raven", "Master Raven", "Adventurer Raven", and "Page"
