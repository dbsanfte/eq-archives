I'm a level 64 Mage. Lately I hunt pretty exclusively in PoP. I have the 62 water pet so far. No, no epic unfortunately. Still need the staff from Hate, like anyone still goes there. Anyway...

I used to be able to solo very well, but not in PoP.

PoP mobs eat my little Jebober before I can blink twice. Doesn't matter how well I equip and buff him. Heals are not enough or fast enough.

Ok, I never have chain cast pets. Never had to, never practiced it. My bad, anyways... even if I did these are unbuffed, un equipped, in essence weaker pets. Seems very inefficient to me.

Hopefully in a week or two I'll be 65. Hopefully I'll get the 65 earth pet. I'd really like to be able to solo again. Even like the trees, ravens, and such in PoN. Or maybe some of the frogs in PoS. Which I watch Druids and Wizards kiting around all the time.

Think back (4 years) to the original EQ manual. Which is what I read when I decided to play a Magician. Which described Magicians as one of the strongest solo classes.

After level 60, it just isn't so. Necromancers I will not complain about, they were also described as strong soloers. Wizards were not, although they can solo circles around us at these levels in PoP. Druids too. All of these classes can, due to the snare types of spells.

I don't care if I ever get root or snare, although snare would solve the immediate problem. I could play like a weakened Wizard

I want a pet that can tank the mobs I need to kill to make decent experience, at my level. Increase pets hp's and defensive capabilities. Increase Magicians healing capabilities on there own pet. This would make me very satisfied and be in line with the original description of a Mage.

51 was cool, I could send my earth pet at a blue raptor and my pet would win. Even if I didn't help

I'm not exactly feeling powerful lately. Oftentimes it's hard to find a group as a Mage. Give me back my solo ability.
