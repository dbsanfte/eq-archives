Bott first of all it does sound like you do not fully understand the paladin class. If your pally is only lvl 40 than this is not your fault. This is a class that changes drastically every few levels.

Agro is a HUGE deal in the upper zones. It took me playing a cleric in a group to really understand this. With a warrior MT I was consistantly healing the slower at the beginning of a fight. During the fight the ranger often took agro from the MT, and cost manna to get him back up to full health. The combined effect was more down time for medding. When a paladin MT took over I healed him, nobody else. We never had downtime for me medding, and the overall exp was much better.

As far as taunt goes, there is no way a warrior can hold taunt better than a pally does. I have pulled mobs off a warrior without ever turning attack on (mainly just to see if I could do it). With 7k hps buffed there isnt much out there I cant tank in an exp group, and I have many HP AAs to go.

If you want to know what class makes the best MT for an exp group just ask the clerics and slowers you know.
