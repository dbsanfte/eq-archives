Its a very multu class to play. You do about everything, but your main 2 areas will be buffs and slows. Once, shamans were slow masters, but like clerics, we lost our main appeal. Tho, not completely, we're just not the only ones now.

Shamans give out most stat buffs, we have a easier time with our group spells. Conderning MGBs, we're there with the Focus line of buffs.

We're secondary/patch healers, so it doesn't hurt to have us in a group.

With soloing.. its possible for us to solo many mobs, especially after our 60lvl pets come about. Not as kick ass as necros and mages, but we hold our own.

Hope this helps!
