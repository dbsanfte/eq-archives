Str, is how hard you hit for.

Sta, Helps stamina up in running, fighting, and adds hit point bonuses.

Agi, Agility adds to AC, and also adds to chances of a susessfull Riposite, dodge, parry, or Mob missing you. Ac is Just Armor class, but having good Agi is 2 fold, with better AC, and ability for ^..

Dex, Is how often you hit your enemy, it brings down on your misses.

This is basicly the primary function of each of those Stats. I play an Iksar monk and recently leveled him to 31, and saving up my monies threw my travels, was able to purchase better armor. Taking my
Str 132
Sta 100
Agi 135
Dex 145

In each of these stats I notice a differance in my skills. I think Agi, and Dex are Very important, but when you get each to around l40 Str should take over your stat concerns (at least till higher levels). Thow Sta gives a big Hit point bonus to monks over 50.

Wu's Armor is great begining monk armor for decent Agi, and all resonable priced. But as I said I recently purchased many pieces, allowing me to get my stats to thier levels, and I notice a signifigant differnace.
