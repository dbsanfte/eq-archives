i don't know how many of you this is going to help, but...
i used to play a racing game called Viper Racing, at super high res. and graphic options completely maxed out. it ran smooth, but for one problem. After playing for about 20 minutes or so, the computer would lock up and have to be completely rebooted. i figured out what it was immediately. when a graphics card gets too hot, it simply locks up. to solve this problem, i installed a 486 fan on the heatsink side of the card, and on the side with the ram chips, i just set a power-supply fan on top of it and let it blow down onto the chips. since then, the card as never locked up and runs at a very cool temp.
i hope this helps with some of your lockup/framerate problems. you'd be amazed at what high temps do to your system!
