As a human your natural abilities are fine. I'm a high elf and the only concern I ever had past intel and mana were my str, which is basically 65.

Work on gaining intel gear first and then mana gear. The mana gear is actually something you try more for when you have intel capped out and you also own a set of resist gear. Once you start hitting the planes, you'll find that there will be times when you will sacrifice your intel for the resist gear, which is fine since planal gear should provide you with both resists and intel items in the long run.

To find out what is available for each slot, try this site:

http://eq.crgaming.com/data/items.asp?Action=ClassSelect&ClassSelect=Magician

It's part of Everquest Casters Realm, and what is nice is you can set search paramiters to look for items by alpha, mana, intel or whatever order you are most interested in.

Good luck

Boots of Blade Calling
( 2 WT, 10 AC, +55 MANA, +12 INT, +9 STA, +10 SV MAGIC, EFF: SHOCK OF SWORDS)

Boots of Flowing Slime
( 0.1 WT, 7 AC, +65 MANA, +20 HP, +10 SV MAGIC, EFF: FLOWING THOUGHT III)

The +12 Intel is very nice, but by the time you can get to Velk, you will most likely have well over the 255 Intel cap and want items that are high in mana, not intel.
I had a pair of Coldain Skin Boots with ( 2.5 WT, 3 AC, +35 MANA, +8 INT, +8 WIS)
and traded them in for the Flowing Slime.

The effect of Shock of Swords, again nice; but you are talking about a lvl 44 spell and if it falls along the line of most right click effect items, will take anywhere from 15 to 20 seconds to cast. Not effective.

We, as casters, rely heavily on our mana pool and therefor, if the intel is capped out, you won't want to waist your time on items that will increase it to no advantage. Increase what can be done, your mana pool and the ability to regen that mana quickly.

With the Flowing Thought boots, Transom's Phantasmal Protection shield and Earing of Solstice, my mana regen is at 7 per tic. May not seem like a lot, but in many a raid, it has proven to be a major blessing.

No single item can be classified as the ultimate best, it all varies according to the level and needs of your character.

At lvl 45, yes the Velk's boots would be awesome, but at present, I would pass on them.

There is one pair of boots though that I would dearly love to have, I doubt anyone could blame me.

White Dragonscale Boots ( 1 WT, 15 AC, +110 MANA, +85 HP, +17 INT, +17 WIS, +20 SV COLD, +5 SV DISEASE, +5 SV FIRE, +10 SV MAGIC, +5 SV POISON, EFF: FLOWING THOUGHT II)

For the added HP, and Mana, I'll sacrifice one point of Flowing Thought
