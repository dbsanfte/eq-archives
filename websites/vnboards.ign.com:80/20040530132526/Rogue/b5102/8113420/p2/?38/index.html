I solo'd until 14 and I paid the price for it. I had to save points and spend PP to get my backstab skill anywhere close to being max'd for my current level at 16. I'm still a good 20 points behind where I should be.

I'm not twinked for regen but I use decent weapons and some decent armor (better than level 15 but less than level 30) and I was doing fine.

My trick to soloing was to watch mb pathing while hidden and after learning the timing I could position myself to hide on the path, BS when they walked by and then retreat to a safe place to kill them. I was doing blue/black/the occasional yellow without losing more than 60% of my HP.

My trick is to keep a single SoW potion for emergencies and use my level 11 mage to summon me bandages before I head out
