Start with something simpler to make. Fish rolls "go trivial" at baking skill 82 (which means that you will receive the message "You can no longer advance your skill making this item").

visit www.eqtraders.com for an extensive listing of recipes.

With a skill level as you described, I would recommend making sandwiches (loaf of bread and either wolf meat or bear meat). Then switch to batwing crispies (batwing, frosting).

A recent patch reduced the price merchants pay for baked goods, such that making items (fish rolls in particular) to sell to merchants no longer makes about 40-60p per hour profit, and now loses money. The "powers that be" were offended that someone on Sullon Zek had plate armor 3 days after the server opened; as a result they punished everyone.
