You cannot complete the epic until you are 50+, however you can start it at any time. In fact, by getting the lower level focus items, you are working on your epic already.

You should start on the Quillmane cloak as soon as you cans solo in SK.

You should start on the Hate staff as soon as you hit 46.

You likely will not find the PoA crown attainable until 50+...but, if you have enough friends or guildmates, you can get the crown at level 1 (it is tradeable).

You should start on the Phinny staff as soon as you can find groups that will take you!

You should start on the COM torch at about 45 to 50.

All other items are fairly easy to get. You can completely ignore things like the pages from Najena until later. Then, just waltz in and take them. You'll easily be able to solo them.

There are a couple of fights like Rottgrime, Slixin and the BW wurms that require help. While you technically do not have to be 45+ do go after those mobs, you should be or you're just relying on others to do the epic for you. Also, these fights are a pretty "sure thing". The problem with things like the staves and torch from COM is that they do not always drop. They're rather rare...

I'm not sure if it will or won't rock the epic since nothing is yet known on the attacks and such with the earth pet.

However, for a long time people did insist that a lvl 60 elemental would, fully buffed, would be able to wipe the floor with an epic pet. Or that the lvl 60 necro pet would do the same, didn't happen in either case.

The epic pet's major advantage is it's DS of 50. I haven't actually sat down and tested the amount that my epic hits for, but I did find this statistic done at the Mage Compendium (please note this is out of date and does not take the current upgrade to pets regarding lvl).

According to Mage Compendium, if a lvl 41 Epic is summoned it does the following amounts of dammage:

2160-air attack, 2659-earth attack, 1786- fire attack, 2219-water attack, 56-mele damage.

I think any other pet would be pretty hard pressed to beat those. I would also be pretty damn peeved if after all the work done to gain this pet, is going to be easily replaced by a dropped spell.
