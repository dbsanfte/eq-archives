Freezing up like that sounds like a hardware problem...Especially since it comes after (in your case) 10-15 mins of play...Since it happened with both cards, I'd say it is probably the motherboard. If it was a PCI-card I'd try another slot, but its more likely you'll have to change motherboard.

Lockups after a certain time are also likely to indicate a heat issue...Is your computer ventilating enough? Try removing the cover and maybe even putting a fan next to it to see if this is the case.
