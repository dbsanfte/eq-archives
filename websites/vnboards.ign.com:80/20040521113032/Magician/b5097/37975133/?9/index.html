After having read the recent nerf, I truly want to throttle the verant developers and scream all at the same time.. "What part of STUPID MOVE do you not understand?"

First point: Okay so Verant says they don't want mages to be turned into mana factories, I can understand that. However, modrod2 was a way that I could actually regen my own mana. Something that wizards, necros and even shaman have been able to do since the dawn of time. For me to even consider regening my own mana would be insane since it costs me more to summon the damn thing than the return of mana I would be given.

Second point: Verant says they want mages to be what was intended, second most highest damage dealers in game. Um.. hate to point out something here but in regard to damage, we are actually third in line. Wizards are obvious, then druids, and then mages.

Third point: Verant also sounds like they are whining when it comes to mage pets. "We give you these awesome pets that we worked so hard to make better and you don't use them waa waa waa" (off-color-quote). Yep, it's true, Verant really did give us some nice upgrades for our pets and I for one do like them. But as most of us already know, leave the malachites and don't right click that orb during a raid. Pathing in most places sucks ass and any AE spell will cause a pet to run rampant. So until I earn the full 24 AA points to get /pet hold, my pet will not see many a raid.

Oh and one more point and purely just an irksome one. When they made the pet control box for us, why didn't they actually ask which icons we wanted? What intelligent goober decided that /pet sit was more useful than /pet report health?
