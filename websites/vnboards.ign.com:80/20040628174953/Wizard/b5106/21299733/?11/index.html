I spent my pts this way so far, but I wouldn't do it the same again....

General:
3 run spd (would do it again)
1 stamina (wasted this pt)
2 regen (would do 3 if doing over)

Archetype:
2 clarity (alot of pts for small regen, but we are all about mana)
2 Fury (and we're all about damage)

After I got the title of Master, I maxed Fury and have put 1 pts into Fury Mastery which seems to have had no effect whatsoever.

Crits happen on AE and even PBAE spells as well as single target DD. Once you max Fury, you will crit for up to double damage. Fury Mastery does not increase the damage done per crit. I'm guessing it increases the percentage chance that you actually score the crit.

As I mentioned in another post, I've heard the Improved Familiar skill is broken and has no effect. I'm not going to grind for 9 pts only to find out I wasted em, so I'm holding off on that. It sure would be nice to have a familiar up when doing AE mobs though, especially for long fights like Vulak. That 4 mana/tick would come in REAL handy.

I'm rather upset that our final title is Sage. Sage is the 2nd stage Necro title (whose final title is Lich). So at our best we're as good as a mid level Necro? I think not. Every wiz needs to send /feedback to Verant about this great injustice being done to our class. Master is a fine title for 2nd level, but Sage is weak anyways. We deserve better. So, I will only spend 23pts in Class specific skills until this is fixed. That will keep me at Master, but I can spend all I want in General and Archetype w/o being demoted to Sage.
