Depends, as always on your supply of platnium.

I would suggest 1'st off, to get a higher damage weapon for you off-hand. A decent cheap-Mid range Price iteam would be Cane of the Tranquil (COT), with a crap load of phat lewt platz a Stave of Shielding (SOS).

As for armor, thier are not many sets out thier for a monk that really really good, most monks have a good mismatch of iteams. With Luclin BLACK PANTHERSKIN line of armor this is decent, but is pricey also.

It would help to know the price range you are looking at for iteam suggestion. Then I could suggest iteams for the best bang for your buck.

A monk likes STR, STA, AGI, DEX.
STR- How hard you can hit
STA- Hit points
AGI- How often you get hit
DEX- How often you will hit (special Attacks)

With that said, STR AGI are really good, but then again DEX is good to, he he. With my monk I always got really paranoid, so all my stats are relativly close togather all my stats (In the 4 listed above) are between 140-170. But having Good STR, DEX, AGI I will say I can noticibly see a differance in my damage output, and ability to take damage.

But good luck.
