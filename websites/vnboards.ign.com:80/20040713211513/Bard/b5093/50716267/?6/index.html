The 2 classes are almost black and white... EQ Bards have some much more utility than DAoC Bards... you can go offensive, defensive or supportive.

People complain DAoC Bards are hard to play due to instrument switching etc... They have nothing on EQ bards. EQ Bards can 'twist' their songs like a Paladin can twist their chants, running up to 4 songs at once (if you are good at it) to maximize the amount of buffage you give a group. That plus switching out instruments makes for some pulse pounding typing

Groups will love your downtime killers (Regens and speed) and you'll find Bards are in demand even more than they are in DAoC. EQ Bards are jacks of all trades, with the difference being they are pretty good at virtually everything they can do (compared to DAoC).

Combat wise, an EQ Bard would murder a DAoC Bard. An interesting difference in EQ is a bard can do better damage with their songs than they do with weapons...

Songs include DOTs, Health Regen, Mana Regening, Mezzes, Charms, DDs, Invisibility, Speedsong... gotta love em.
