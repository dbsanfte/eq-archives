Take it from someone who has had 2 level 50+ human paladins. Restart and put your stats into Agility.

Most the armor you will get in your career provides ample strength, dex, stamina and wisdom but lacks in charisma and agility.

Its not a bad idea to put 3 into dex, 5 into stam and the rest into agility.
