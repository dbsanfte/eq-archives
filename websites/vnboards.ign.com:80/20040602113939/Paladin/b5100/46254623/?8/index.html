day in and day out i search the boards for information that i can use while playing my my beloved little dwarf pally and still there are non beleivers about the effectivness and usefullness of this class. Here is a top ten list og why pallys rule, granted they have their postive and negatives but who doesn't.

reason 10: Pallys have a great AC on most of their gear and also can generate alot of hitpoints through sta which is a fairly easy stat to raise.

reason 9: crowd control, dont expect us to shut the party completly down but a well placed stun followed by a root is priceless in its self. Not to mention you could always use flash og light send the mob running then root em.

reason 8: swords!! on nearly every major 2 HS wep that has alot of dmg the pally can swing it. They are a nice damage dealing class all in all. Rouges and bards may out damage us over time but i like to see them double hit something for 200ish every 3.7 seconds or so.

reason 7: easy leveling. It has been my experience that despite what has been said about pallys they are forgeting one thing that being that pallys do not require all that many kills to get sweet exp and level up like a hero.

reason 6: soloing ability. From what I have heard people are always dogging the pally out because they like the SK or warrior better. A soloing warrior will turn out to be eaten like mommas meatloaf. As of SK's ability to solo I yield to the comparison and say Ska re about equal. Granted pallys are not best at soloing but being able to walk into GD or rathe mnts at lvl 36 with a C3 soloing giants by root and step back and heal is not bad either.

reason 5: special armor. Pallys get some of the best and most useful armor designed just for them. check out the deepwater helm. Which can be used for mana free healing, also look at the Thurg armor they get a very usefull BP indeed.

reason 4: undead abilities. Pallys own the undead. They are many great zones along Norrath which they can play in and get good stuff. Unrest, Lower guk, najena, TOFS, and the new harbor zone from LOY all great exp leveling undead zones. Imbue your blade with instument of nife and proc 230ish while swinging like a hero and hitting them with a 94 DD nonstop. use some AA points get the skill clense a buddy pally of mine hit the main vamp in the KV when he was considered undead for 9 K with it !!! NASTY all I have to say.

reason 3: HORSEY!! spend your AA points correctly and you dont have to worry about buying your horse or ever have to get mowed down by a mob in open zones. You get the holy steed! A great addition in times of panic and in times when you have to get some where quick. No cost to you just click and ride away.

reason 2: healing. We are not a cleric!!!! with that being said w still can hold our own LOY as given us a 400 hp heal over 24 sec. Not bad. I feel sorry for the cleric who just stands, heals, sits. Pallys allow the clerics to have some fun by DD and summoning their little hammer buddy to help in the fight. Pallys who pay attention to their group can aid the cleric alot by randomly casting 300 heals now and then. A group of 3 pallys can keep the whole party alive of they work together and cast heal on one another.

Finally reason 1 : LAY OF HANDS. With exception to FD perhaps the most useful skill in the game. I do not know any other class that can get a second chance like this one. When the battle turns sour nothing makes a pally prouder than telling the oom cleric just to sit and med and worry bout the others, because you can handle yourself.

Thats it folks 10 reasons why pallys are sweet. Not to mention the fact that they can keep aggro off any other class using stuns and taunt, and not even mentioning the ability to rez themselves and others when clerics are scarce in the zone.
Please post your comments I want to know what every one thinks.
and for all you PALLY HATERS LAY OFF US!~!~

I agree with all your points, but not in the order you have them. I would put agro controll as the #1 reason. We save cleric manna by keeping others from getting hit. This is THE major reason why paladins are wanted for MT exp groups. Forget a warriors hps, if they cant keep the enchanters and shammies from taking damage they cost more healing manna than they save. SKs are great at agro controll also, but they dont have near the HPs and AC that paladins do with equal lvls and gear. I dont use flash of light at all. I load a fast casting low lvl stun and Yalp to maintain agro. Its not too often I hold agro, no matter what other classes (and lvls) are in the group.

Healing power I would put at #2. Healing Wave of Prexus can make a real boring day for clerics on HoT raids. NToV its possible to put 2 pallies in a group as healers, and leave it at that. Touch of Nife at lvl 61 is great for spot heals on the MT in higher end encounters.

#3: Weapons. I challange you to find any paladin of a decient guild that does not have multiple weapons to use. I cant count how many paladins I know who have banked their Primals for better items.

#4 HPs/AC: We will never match warriors in this catagory, but we are number 2 on the list. I know several pallies in the 7-8k hp range. The only reason we do not MT more mobs is a warriors disc. We are quite capable of MTing HoT and WToV, and we are a better raid MTs than warriors in many cases.

#5 Undead: Yes with Slay Undead we own on damage vs undead. I have seen a group of paladins outdamage a group of rogues on the same undead mob (was a lil test we did to check it out). The only problem here is the 30 AA ponts you will spend getting SU3. Its a nice toy, but I think other AA abilities are more important (ND3, 2H Bash, Holy Steed, Improved LoH) I havent messed around with the newer AAs yet.

Not gonna go through them all, but these are the ones that top my list.
