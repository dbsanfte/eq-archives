Ok, the only time u loose the man is if he dies from being out for 10 hours, or some mob does 400 HP damage to it. If the pet is dieing due to being up 10 hours (it will blink), CLICK IT OFF!!! you won't loose the mana, you won't die, nothing, he just poofs then. Also, if your fighting a known AE casting mob, common sense would tell you not to have the pet out, but then it looks like your lacking that :P As for the necro pets, there is no comparison. For instance, our pet cannot gain aggro, cannot attack, does not attack, can freely move through a zone and NEVER gain aggro. Not only that but our pet zones with us. You need to think of the pet as a buff, and NOT as a pet. Necro's rely on their pets, if they zone, they loose the pet.
