ok, I'm assuming your comming from DAOC and are not american or speak english. I'll do my best to try and explain what I can cause I'm not quite sure what ya mean

>>Wizards are the Arch Type of MAgic Class Right? <<

In everquest, there are 3 types of casters, Wisdom based (Druids, clerics, shamans), Intelligence based (Enchanters, Wizards, Necromancers, Magicians), and Hybrids (ShadowKnight, Ranger, Beastlord, Paladin,). Note, I do not count bards as they are rather unique when it comes to magic.

Wizards, as you can see, belong to to the Pure Intelligence group. Wizards are very frail and cannot take punishment (i.e. Damage) yet they can deal out more damage than any class.

>>Are they fun to play? <<

This is a rather difficult question to answer due to everyone being unique and wanting different things. For me, I love being a wizard. I love being able to do well over 1000 points of damage a nuke, or watching a mob's health fall instantly with a wiggle of a few fingers. But don't let this lead you on, Wizards are very difficult and very frustrating at early levels. Only at level 49 and up do they truely come into their own. Getting to level 49 is a long path and is not for the faint of heart. Wizards require alot of patience and persistance.

>>Is playing a DE accpetable? <<

Again, this is personal preference. Every race can make a great wizard. Depending on the server you play on will make a big difference on how "accepted" you are. If on a Team PvP Server, all the "dark races" (Trolls, Iksars, Ogres, and Dark Elves) will love you where all other races will hate you and do everything in their power to exterminate you. On "blue" servers (non-PvP) race makes no difference other than what you feel is the most fun. Best advice I can give is play what makes you happy.
