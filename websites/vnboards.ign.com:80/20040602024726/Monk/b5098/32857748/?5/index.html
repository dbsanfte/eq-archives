I'd suggest 29/30+ group for the 2nd floor, which is where the Crescent will be dropping, but watch out - it can get really tough.

But I'll tellk you this now, just wait - Crescent really doesn't seem to be worth it (unless you were like me and wanted it for the looks). It's pretty rare and you'll have a VERY HARD (or at least I did) time getting together a good 29ish group to get down there, it's out in the middle of nowhere and pick up groups will be few and far between.

If you're really determined, I'd suggest you grab a ton of invis potions and every time you're in Dalnir check to see if there are any other high levels camping the lower levels, ask 'em to let you know if any Crescent drops that no one in their group (or they themselves) need and then just pop a potion and head down there to loot the body as fast as you can, it usually takes 2 to get there and back.


Anyway, that was just about the time Velious was coming out - so it is waaaaaay out of date. But that's what worked for me.
