is the 8 gig hd the old one with the original windows OS you were using before on it? you probably already have, but you might want to try to play around with the jumpers on both hd's making the ME one manually the "master" drive and the other manually the "slave" drive.. (in case you have them set to auto-detect ON the HDs)
also try switching the settings in your bios from user defined for both, to auto detect, etc.. i always have quirks with installing and recognizing hard drives, but i have 3 hd's in mine, 1 to the mobo, and 2 to an expansion card (ultra66) haha, wanna talk about playing with settings? with switching jumpers, cables, hd's, bios settings too all diff. combos. that was almost a pain! they work fine now.. and there *shouldn't* be a compatibility issue with ME and '98.. but, then again we are talking about a microsoft product here...
funny story about my recent upgrade/HD connecting experience... after connecting all the HD's and rebooting to see what i needed to change in the HD boot sequence, i was surprised to see upon exiting the POST..
"LILO booting.." seems that a previous Linux installation was still hanging in there on a win 98 dominated HD... (windows 98 overwrites the master boot record of any HD it's installed on, without telling you)
