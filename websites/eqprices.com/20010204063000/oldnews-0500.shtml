Price Rounding
Date: Tuesday, May 23rd at 10:50 AM EST

     I made some updates to the item listing script so it rounds prices. If it's less than 20 pp, it leaves it alone. Otherwise, it will round it to the nearest 10 or 100 depending on what range the price falls into. It's a lot nicer seeing 15000 pp than 15034 pp (at those prices, it's not broken down to a single plat anyway).

Kunark and Code Part II
Date: Wednesday , May 10th at 1:39 PM EST

     Another daily Kunark item update. This time around, we have an amazing haste belt (the Runebranded Girdle). I don't have an image or location for it, so I can only assume it is real.
     I added weapon damage/second and "effective percentage" calculations to the weapon listings. The "effective percentage" is based on the most damaging weapon currently known (the awesome Tranquil Staff), which has a damage/second ratio of 19.667. All percentages are based on this number being 100% (until a more powerful weapon is discovered!). Please note that these calculations do not account for any stats ont he weapon (including haste), so they may be a bit off.
     A site like this will always be visited by the terminally bored, who feel it is there job to enter bad prices and screw up the database. What fun. I have put in scripts to maintain the quality of the database, and they will flag prices that deviate too far from the average so I can remove them if necessary.
