System Software Overhaul
Date: Tuesday, August 29th at 9:40 AM EST

     I may not update the main page everyday, but I spend hours on the site itself. Over the past week, I have been converting the entire database over to a new format, and rewriting every single piece of code on the site.
     Why? This new database will be much faster, and allow powerful searches. Want to know what items give more than 3 DEX, more than 5 WIS, have an effect, come from Trakanon's Teeth and cost between 3000 and 5000 pp? This is possible in one easy search on the "new" site. No more limiting searches to particular slots. Look for the launch soon!
