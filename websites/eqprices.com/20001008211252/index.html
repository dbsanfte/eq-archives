A Whole New EQPrices.Com!
Date: Thursday, October 5th at 7:33 PM EST by Soliptic D'Ception

     Although the site may look the same (at least I hope it does), it is completely different from a code perspective. Over the past few months, I have been rewriting the site from scratch to take advantage of a more powerful database engine. Right now, it's live, but it has a lot of work left to go.
     The first major change you'll notice is the default "Any" search category. This was the #1 most requested site addition, and it's finally here. No longer are you limited to particular item categories. Go ahead, click Search Database with just "all" selected and you'll get 2022 items to look at. That's everything in the database!
     I had to temporarily remove the item price range search, but it will be back. It just requires a bit of coding and I wanted to get the update live since it's so useful.
     I tested this out for many hours, and it seems stable. Of course, as with all major overhauls there will probably be bugs. If you see anything that isn't working quite right, please e-mail me at feedback@eqprices.com and let me know!
     Look for an advanced search option soon. For now, enjoy the new EQPrices.Com!
