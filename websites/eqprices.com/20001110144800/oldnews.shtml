The "effective percentage" is based on the most damaging weapon currently known (the awesome Tranquil Staff), which has a damage/second ratio of 19.667. All percentages are based on this number being 100% (until a more powerful weapon is discovered!). Please note that these calculations do not account for any stats ont he weapon (including haste), so they may be a bit off.
