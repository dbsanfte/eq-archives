Average Overhauls
Date: Sunday, November 19th at 2:26 PM EST by Soliptic D'Ception

     Karl, an staff member here at EQPrices.Com, has been going through every item on the site and making sure the averages are in line with reality. We have removed 500+ prices in the past week to get these averages back to where they belong, and more is on the way. This includes cross-referencing items with each other, to make sure the prices listed make sense when compared with prices on similar items. With as many items in the database as we have, its hard to keep track of whats going on with every average for every item. But Karl is very familiar with averages for many items on various servers both old and new, so this should help make EQPrices even more accurate. Remember, if you see an item price entry in the Price Details section that's not correct, report it. And be sure to enter the correct price for your server, so the averages are accurate.
     User-submissions are next... there is a 300+ item submission backlog right now that I'm working on getting through. It should be done either tonight or tomorrow, so if you submitted a new item or an item correction, look for it then.
