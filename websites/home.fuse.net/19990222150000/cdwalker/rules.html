Solaria's Unholy Alliance
Guild Guidelines
 
We play in game B on server 2 and soon server 1 as well.

We will not attack other guild members at any time.

You do have the option to cancel alliances with them and not back them against thier enemies if you feel they have done something wrong.... such as attack someone you have now declared peace with.

Try to go to war only when attacked first.  We do not want to upset a player with strong allies.  If you are attacked try to hit them a couple of times and then declare a truce with them, if they accept you MUST stick by it.

Aid countries in the alliance when someone much larger than them attacks....  Maybe add them to your defensive alliance or hit the enemy with a nuke or cause dissention.... you would want them to do the same for you in times of need.
