Erudin

Peacekeeper staff - Cleric's Guild. is a staff useable only by erudite clerics and paladins. The staff does 5 damage and has a delay of 28. The only real advantage of this staff is that it grants +10 charisma when held. If you accept the quest you will be sent to the Tox Forest to search for an NPC. The NPC is in the first hut to the right after you leave the city gates. He will give you a staff and directions on how to continue the quest. Basically you will be required to kill a Treant and bring back the treant shards to the NPC. When you do so he will grind them into treant resin and hand them back to you.
You know must get the other required item to combine in with the treant resin to create the staff. Go to the vault in erudin(the bank) and talk to the banker on the far left. Tell him you are in need of some vasty deep water. He will tell you that you need a guild coin in order for him to give it to you. Now return to the clerics guild and tell them that this NPC sent you to get a guild coin. You will be given the coin. When you give it to the banker you will receive the vasty deep water in return. Combine the water and the resin in the staff and the end result will be a peacekeeper staff.

KARG Polar and Lupine Armor - Karg Icebear in Everfrost will make you polar or lupine armor provided the right materials and some compensation. Polar armor requires polar bear pelts of some quality and provide protection from cold. Lupine armor is special armor derived from lycanthropes(werewolves). Some lupine armor can be directly found on lycanthropes such as the lupine armbands. Others must be gained by questing for Karg. Lupine armor is excellent armor for all classes but especially for monks and pure casters. It can be worn by all races and all classes and it is extremely light. It is the same armor class rating as leather but it's magical bonuses usually more than make up for that. Lupine armor all gives magical bonuses. For example the armbands grant +3 strength and +3 dexterity.

Crushbone

Screaming Mace (8 Dmg and 35 delay light equal to greater lightstone and casts yaulp 18 sec +10 STR +22 AC).quest is one of the most fullfilling quest you can do between levels 14-25. This quest can be done by a full party of level 14 characters, and can be done solo at around level 22-25 depending on your class/race combination.

First thing that you need to do is go see the dwarven smith. He is located in the small building that is near the mine area. To get here from the entrance you must take a left and go across the bridge. Once you cross the bridge just basically follow the path. After you go down the hill you will see a building. Inside this building is a jailor who you will have to kill. Then you will see two dwarven smiths and an elven priest. Talk to the middle dwarven smith(actually you can probably talk to either smith but I always spoke to the one in the middle, so for safety purposes speak to him). Tell him that you are here to set him free and he will begin rambling about how he wants his freedom but the taskmaster scares them, yada yada yada. Then ask him about the king or emporer and he will speak of the high quality ringmail that is worn by this beast.

There are several parts of this quest but you really only need to concentrate on the final two(bracer of battle and dwarven ringmail). Each piece that you find can either be kept or turned in for the item that it returns depending on your class, race, and needs.

The first mob that you might want to worry about is the Taskmaster. He hangs out to the right of the castle near the slaves. He seldomly spawns but when he does he has a chance of carrying some magic +2 AC earrings. You can either wear these earrings or return them to the dwarven smith. If you return them to him he will give you a blade mold which can be used for blacksmithy.

The second mob in this quest is the Instructor/Trainer. This is a large orc who can be found training other orcs on top of the large hill in the middle of the zone. He very rarely carries a Shiny Brass Shield (10 AC +10 Vs Magic and light source). You can elect to keep this weapon(a wise choice or a poor choice depending on your class) or you can turn it in to the dwarven smith in exchange for a sludge mold.

The third item that you will need is the one that will be the most dangerous. You now need to kill the Emporer/King and hope that he is sporting the Dwarven Ringmail Tunic (strong as chainmail AC 12 lighter and grants +8 vs. heat, cold, and magic) If you are a small race you might think about keeping this item to wear on your person until you get a second one. However you can turn this item in to the dwarven smith as with the items above. This time he will give you a Dwarven Mace (Dmg 8 delay 35). Not a bad weapon, however it's just a stepping stone. The smith will also send you to go speaking to the elven priest standing in the next stall. The priest can enchant this mace for you(and turn it into the screaming mace) but first you need to do something for her. She wants you to kill the Warlord and bring proof of his death.

The fourth item that you need is the hardest one to get. You need to find the Warlord. He can be seen patrolling the area from time to time. He is a level 14 or so mob, but that isn't the problem. The problem is if you attack him he is going to run and bring help. So you must be careful to root him in place and then try to take care of him before root runs out. If you are lucky he will be carrying a Bracer of battle(same AC as leather but also gives a bonus of +3 strength and +3 dexterity) This is a fine piece of armor for this level, however it can also be turned in. This time you will turn the bracer into the elven priest rather than the dwarven smith. The priest will give you a Prayer Cloth in return for the bracer.

Many players get to this step and think, what? What did I do wrong. Well it is important to NOT give the priest the mace at the same time as the bracer of battle. You need to give her the bracer alone first. Then after she hands you the prayer cloth you can give her the mace. When she asks, "do you have something else for me?" then you can hand over the prayer cloth and voila you get one shiny screaming mace.
