Making Advanced Leather Armor

Reinforced armor
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Just had a friend make me some today.. You need both a smith and a tailor. Have the smith make steel boning (i made that). Then the tailor uses the boning and high quality pelts to make reinforced. the stats aren't that great, and it's quite difficult to make. Out of 30 attempts (tailor was 24 skill). We got one hat (2 more ac than leather) and one sleeve (1 more ac than leather).

Hats and sleeves require 2 steel bonings, and one high quality pelt each. Boots, tunics and legs require 3 bonings each.

Cost is crazy for the quality of the stuff (figure is cost me 50pp for the 30 attempts). And it takes forever just to make the supplys. combine ore to make bits, bits and molds to make file, file and small brick to make boning.. Amazing waste of time, and my tailor friend didn't even gain any skill.

Might be better if you can find a higher skill tailor.

Hope this helped
Camili (19 druid, 137 smith) Cazic Thule
