COMMANDS

Chat Commands

/Tell <name> Hello? - Whispers to that person "Hello?"  /T works also

/Shout Hello - everyone in a large area hears you

/Gsay hello - everyone in your group sees hello /Gtell and /G work as well.

/OOC hello - Out of character appears in green and says hello

/Who will list everyone in the area and their class/race/level

/Who <name> gives same info for just one person the /who <name> all is for the entire world rather than just area.

/Bug - opens bug report screen

/Cheer - cheers

/Panic - panics

/Wave - waves

/Cry - crys

/Rude - give a player the finger

/Bow – bows

/Consent <name> gives someone permission to loot your corpse if you died and are too far away to get your stuff.

/Corpse Summons your corpse if nearby to your feet.

/Follow Will have you auto follow the person targeted (not implemented yet 6/6/99)

/Petition I am stuck under the bridge HELP ME! tells the GM's "I am stuck under the bridge HELP ME!"

:touches himself - emotes in color it will say <your name> touches himself

Right click in chat window to change your spoken language.
 

Other commands

Shift 1-6 changes hot key menus while 1-6 uses the hot key on current menu.

Quick press of Spacebar is jump This is handy for out running monsters.

Hold Spacebar to crouch.

Hold Forward arrow then combine with alt, release alt, then release forward to auto-walk/run This is good for typing while on the run.

Hit the backward arrow to end auto-walk/run

Hold down right mouse button on item in inventory to see who can use it and its properties.

Pgup Pgdn look up or down while in water.

Drop Beer on face slot in equipped items screen to drink.

Pet Commands

/pet attack (or /pet kill) - Pet will attack your current target

/pet guard me - Pet guards you from anything that attacks or that you attack

/pet guard here - Pet guards an area

/pet follow me - Pet follows you (not through zone boundaries)

/pet back off - Pet will cease fighting

/pet as you were - belay previous orders

/pet sit down

/pet stand up
