*Specials:

Buff=buff target (make stronger)
petBuff=buff pet only
deBuff=debuff target (make weaker)
Heal=restore hps
OD=outdoor only
SO=self only
AE=area effect
COL=Column Damage
DD=Direct Damage
DoT=damage over time
Root=hold target in place
DS=damage shield (mob dealing damage to target recieves damage)
Removed = Spell was planned, but not fully implemented.

Note on the Succor flavor of spells:
These are quick and dirty versions of the Circle flavored spells. Useful when your party is in WAAAAAY over it's head. There is a 2% chance per party member that that character will be left behind (failed teleport).
