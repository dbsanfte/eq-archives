Mit diesem Erfolg reiht sich Magna Charta in die illustre Reihe derjenigen Gilden ein, die den kompletten Tempel des Veeshan entvölkert haben.

    Aber wir wollen euch nicht länger auf die Folter spannen, die folgenden Bilder sprechen für sich!

    The past weekend´s theme was "We won't leave North ToV until the last dragon is dead!". And we kept our vow. Shortly after 11 pm on sunday evening, Vulak'Aerr, master dragon of the Temple of Veeshan, fell dead to our feet. Before that, Eashen of the Sky, Ikatiar the Venom, Aaryonar, Dagarn the Destroyer, Cekenar, Jorlleag, Lady Mirinella, Lady Nevederia, Lord Feshlak, Lord Koi'Doken, Lord Kreizenn, Zlexak, Sevelak and Lord Vyemm fell before us, all those dragons with additional guards. With this success, Magna Charta enqueues with all those illustrious guilds who depopulated the whole of the Temple of Veeshan.
    The experience of conquering more than fifteen of the hardest dragons (Vulak'Aerr in the first run) of Norrath is hard to express. A minute-long storm of jubilation arose when Lord Vyemm and Vulak'Aerr made their last gasp.
