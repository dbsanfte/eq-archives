Fate's Companions...

...was founded with friendship as a base. Our aim is to keep this spirit of friendship in all of our members and all that live in Norrath and Luclin.

...aims to spread fun and laughter across Norrath and Luclin, to bring people together against the beasts that threaten our land. The emphasis is on enjoyment rather than levelling quickly or getting the very best equipment.

...is open for all who have an active interest in each other and each other's goals.

Guild members are expected to keep to these rules. Also they should know and keep to the spirit of the Charter.

All guild members must obey Verant's code of conduct. (i.e no KSing, no intentional training, no harrasment, no fake trades). We support Verant’s “play nice” rules and encourage all of our members to peacefully and equitably work out camp sharing or grouping issues. If this is not possible, then contact a Guild Officer or Guild Leader. Always keep in mind that the guild's reputation is dependant upon YOUR actions.

This guild was created to help everybody have fun and enjoy Everquest. If the guild isn’t going in the direction you had in mind, please talk to someone in the guild about it and make your feelings known. If it’s a matter involving a particular guild mate inform a Guild Officer of the situation. Part of the reason why people are chosen to become a guild officer is because they are willing to listen and ready to do something about any issue the guild and its members may face.

The guild is open to characters of any race or class, but you must have proven your dedication by reaching level 30 or higher. Any guild member is free to bring in new members. The new, want-to-be member needs to be introduced to any one of the officers. The potential member is also asked to introduce him/herself on the Guild message boards. After all Officers have decided, the new member will be asked to join for a trial period of two weeks. After the trial period, the member is either welcomed into the guild or is politely asked to leave. Reasons of denial will be given in a personal and private way to all persons involved.

No profanity in Guild Chat, OOC, Shout, Auction or anywhere. Everyone is human and that at times we all need to vent, but our guild is one with a range of ages and backgrounds, so foul language is not needed. Remember that not all guild members speak, write or read English as a first language, so always try to be as clear as possible so everyone understands.

The guild is here to help one another, but do not expect guild members to provide their services whenever you ask. If you are looking for a port, a buff or other assistance, please ask politely in guild chat, stating what zone you are in. Do not spam - if you get no reply, don't ask again for at least 10 minutes. If someone can help, they will send you a tell. If they are busy, then respect that. The people you are asking have just as much right to play the game with no interruptions as you do.

We are not a Twinking guild. Do not expect the guild or any of our members to twink you or you lower level alts. We are aware that twinking is tempting but its hard to prevent. Also is it hard to determine what amount of twinking is fair or unfair.

Items can be given to you or an alt by the guild but this will be done on a 'Need before Greed' and 'Can Use, Will Use' basis. You are expected to replace the item from the guild with a similar item that then can be handed down to a other guildmember.

Any item given to you by the guild is to be handed down to another member of the guild or returned to the Guild Bank when you outgrow it. It is in no way to be sold inside or outside the Guild. Items given from the guild bank will be appropriate for the character's level. Any item given to the Guild for use in the Guild Bank will remain property of the guild. However, if you inform us when donating it, we can and will return the item to you if you ask for it back.

Do not use "/anon", use "/role", this is so we can see you online by doing "/who all fate's companions" command.
