Please read the following in order to become a new member:

 The guild is open to characters of any race or class, but you must have proven your dedication by reaching level 46 or higher.

Any guild member is free to bring in new members.

The new, want-to-be member needs to be introduced to any one of the officers.

The potential member is also asked to introduce him/herself on the Guild message boards.

After all Officers have decided, the new member will be asked to join for a trial period of one month, during which time you will not be entitled to loot on any raids.

After the trial period, the member is either welcomed into the guild or is politely asked to leave. Reasons of denial will be given in a personal and private way to all persons involved.
