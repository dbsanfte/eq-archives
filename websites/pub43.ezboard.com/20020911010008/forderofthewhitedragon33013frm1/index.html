Welcome to the OWD Event Calendar!

If you need to make an inquiry, or post anything that is NOT an event, please post to the General or General (Public) Forums.

If you're here to post a new event, please create a new thread with the title in this format:
Zone (Date, Day) [Time and Time Zone]

For example:

City of Mist (Friday 1/11) [5PM EST]

This should make it much easier for everyone to glance down the list and see OWD's upcoming raids. And remember, if your raid is on Rordin's, put a link in your post to make it even easier for everyone to sign up.

If you have multiple raids, please create a thread for each. Events are removed one day after they happen, to cut down on clutter.
