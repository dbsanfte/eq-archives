Welcome, prospective members!

These are some basic guidelines for all new applicants. This is not the official recruitment policy, that can be found here. Please read the following rules and be familiar with the type of people we are looking for.

  1. Loot is not OWD's primary goal, so if loot is your only concern, you will not fit in.
  2. Though we are genuinely excited when an OWD member gets his or her epic, priority is typically given to members who have been around the longest. If you want to join OWD because you are looking for help quickly getting your epic, you may want to try elsewhere.
  3. OWD is a firm believer in the "Need Before Greed" philosophy. If you do not believe in the NBG philosophy, do not apply.
  4. We also believe in respect - if you do not respect others and their ideas/beliefs do not apply.
  5. If you are looking to join a family, have fun, have adventures, and help one another, then please continue below with the application process, and we look forward to meeting you!
If you feel that OWD is the place for you, please do the following:

  1. Post on the Membership Applications section your name, race, class, level, and WHY you want to join OWD.
  2. It is recommended that you group with OWD members on a casual basis.
  3. Please try to attend OWD raids that are listed on our event calendar to meet OWD members (and have fun, too!). You will need to attend at least three raids as an applicant.
  4. You will need to be interviewed by one of our recruitment officers listed below.
  5. You will need to have a sponsor, if you do not know anyone from the guild, do not worry; we can find someone who will get to know you.
  6. Be patient! The application process will not happen overnight; sometimes it will take a matter of weeks. Though we will, of course, go through the process as quickly as possible, please realize that issues (both in-game and RL) do come up. If you truly think OWD is the guild that you'd like to spend some time in, the extra week or two will be a drop in the bucket.
Thank you very much for showing interest in Order of the White Dragon. Please feel free to speak with any of the recruitment officers (Eldorath, Trulea, or Warg) with any questions you have about our guild or applying.
