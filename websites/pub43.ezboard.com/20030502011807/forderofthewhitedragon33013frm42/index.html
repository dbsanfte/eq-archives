Welcome, prospective members!

These are the Order of the White Dragon recruitment guildlines. Please read them and be familar with the way we recruit new members into our family. This section is for those who have already read and agree with our charter. If you have not read it yet, please follow this link: OWD Charter

  1. Order of the White Dragon is a supporter of the Bristlebane Event Calendar. OWD is looking for similar-minded people on this subject, so if you don't agree with the EC, you need not apply. If you are not familiar with the EC, please click this link: BB Event Calendar.
  2. Loot is not one of OWD's primary goals, so "Loot Whores" need not apply.
  3. Though we are genuinely excited when an OWD member gets his or her epic, priority is typically given to members who have been around the longest. If you want to join OWD because you are looking for help quickly getting your epic, you may want to try elsewhere.
  4. OWD is a firm believer in the "Need Before Greed" philosophy. If you do not believe in the NBG philosophy, do not apply.
  5. We also believe in respect - if you do not respect others and their ideas/beliefs do not apply.
  6. If you are looking to join a family, have fun, have adventures, and help one another, then please continue below with the application process, and we look forward to meeting you!
If you feel that OWD is the place for you, please do the following:

  1. Post on the Membership Applications section your name, race, class, level, and WHY you want to join OWD.
  2. It is recommended that you group with OWD members on a casual basis.
  3. Please try to attend OWD raids that are listed on our event calendar to meet OWD members (and have fun, too!)
  4. Once you have done this, a current member of OWD will sponsor you for membership and we will vote on you on our guild message board. It is recommended that you attend some raids and group with as many OWD members as possible otherwise you will get neutral support.
  5. If at any time during the application process you want to know the status of your application, please send a tell to me first. If I am not online, an officer can tell you where you stand in general, non-specific terms.
  6. Be patient! The application process will not happen overnight; sometimes it will take a matter of weeks. Though we will, of course, go through the process as quickly as possible, please realize that issues (both in-game and RL) do come up. If you truly think OWD is the guild that you'd like to spend some time in, the extra week or two will be a drop in the bucket.
Thank you very much for showing interest in Order of the White Dragon. Please feel free to send me a tell in game with any questions you have about our guild or applying.

Webling

Recruitment Officer
