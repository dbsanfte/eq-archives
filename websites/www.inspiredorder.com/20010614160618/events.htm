Kael Fest

Start Time:  Sunday, June 3nd at 8:00 PM EST
Assembly Point:  Kael Drakkal just inside zone point from Eastern Wastes
Duration:    2+ hours
Level Range: 39+

This Sunday night we're going to raid Kael Drakkal to work on our Giant and Dragon faction. Kael is one tough place so we won't be able to just go and wipe it out. The intent for Sunday is more to carve a nice swathe of giant corpses from the Eastern Wastes entrance to the Wakening Land exit. We'll probably wander up towards the arena some as well depending on how many people sign up for the raid. After the raid, a group of us will probably also wander over to the Wakening Land to do some Holgresh since Dart and others need spell drops off of these silly monkeys.

Mobs here can be pretty serious. Some of the stuff in the zone is well into the 50s so we'll probably spend most of our time fighting Guards, Watchers, Watchmen, Sergeants, and some of the Sentinels. Here is a complete list of the Mobs in Kael. A loot list is also available, but we're really in it on Sunday for fun and faction so don't expect too much.

As usual, EQ Atlas has a guide to Kael which gives a high-level overview of the place. They don't have a map, however, EQMaps has a good map of the center of Kael and also another which is less detailed, but more broad.

Transportation to Velius will be provided, but it is a good trek to Kael from either Iceclad or Great Divide and then through the Eastern Wastes so plan on starting out early or binding nearby prior to the event. If you need a ride, be near a druid circle or a wizard spire at least 45 min before the event. Melee can actually bind just inside the entrance to Kael so use your judgment in that respect.

A sign-up sheet has been posted on the Raiding Forum. Please make sure you read the Raiding Policy prior to the event. See you there.

Vertix Ironpaw
Raid Leader

 

 

The Mugging of Najena

Start Time:  Saturday, June 2nd at 8:00 PM EST
Assembly Point:  West Commonlands Druid Ring
Duration:    3+ hours
Level Range: 18-34+ (higher levels welcome to help out)

This Saturday night for the lower level members of the Guild, we are going to raid a classic - Najena. We plan on a general dungeon crawl of the place killing everything as we collect the necessary keys and end the night with Najena herself for those who wish to stay and camp the evil wench.

Najena is an evil dark elf magician, who drops the Flowing Black Robe and the Black Tome with Silver Runes. Other interesting drops include the Bloodstained Coif and the Enchanted Fine Steel Bastard Sword. A complete loot list can be found here.

The dungeon itself it fun to look at (read very evil). It is full of undead along with regular monsters and some summoned creatures as well. A complete listing of the Mobs can be found here.

Please read and get familiar with the map and guide at EQ Atlas. Transportation will be provided as much as possible, however, as always, plan ahead and listen to guild chat about a half hour prior for a ride. Melees can be bound in Neriak if they are evil, otherwise, they can be bound in Freeport.

A sign-up sheet has been posted on the Raiding Forum. Please make sure you read the Raiding Policy prior to the event. See you there.

Fryne Wilwarin
Event Coordinator

 

 

Tower of Frozen Shadow 2 - The Return (Muhahahahaha!!!)

Start Time:  Sunday, May 27th  at 8:00 PM EST
Assembly Point:  West Commonlands Druid Ring
Duration:    2+ hours
Level Range: 35+

Last week at Frozen Shadow, we had a blast, we got the keys, and we basically decided that there was a LOT more to do in that Tower than we had time for in one night. So guess what ? WE'RE GOING BACK BY POPULAR DEMAND! Come one, come all, bring your can of undead whoop ass, and generally get ready to stomp our way on up the tower one level at a time.

You know the drill:

Loot is pretty good here. There are too many drops to list, but of particular interest to many is the Student's Homework, the Shield of Shadows, the Diamond Wedding Band, the Prophecy Scroll, and Abram's Axe of the Stoic. A complete list of loot can be found here.

As usual, EQ Atlas has a good guide to the Tower of Frozen Shadow. Surprisingly enough, however, they still haven't posted a map yet. At EQ maps, I was able to locate a low-quality map of the entire tower and also a slightly better map of Level 4. Mobs are primarily undead. A listing of them can be found here.

We will assemble at 7:45 PM EST at the Druid Ring in West Commonlands and then port over to the Iceclad Ocean where we will run to the Tower itself. Melees should find their own binds since Thurgadin is the closest spot to the tower. If you're having trouble finding a bind, give a shout in guildchat around 7:15 or so. As usual, transport to the event will be provided. If you need a ride be at a Druid Ring no later than 7:30 PM EST and listen to your guildchat.
