JOINING INSPIRED ORDER

 

BEFORE YOU SUBMIT AN APPLICATION TO JOIN INSPIRED ORDER

You should get to know us some before you make your formal application to join Inspired Order. Joining a guild should not be done lightly - we want to make sure that joining our guild is a good fit for potential new members and for ourselves.

Take a look around our site. Probably the first thing to look at is our Guild Philosophy. This document probably best describes what you will find here and what will be expected of you if an offer to join Inspired Order is extended. Take a moment to also read our Charter and our Raiding Policies. Together, these three documents provide the core structure for how we run things in Inspired Order.

We are a very active guild and tend to have frequent raids and events. You can check out a sample of the activities we do by browsing the Events and the Calendar sections of our website. You should know that Inspired Order is on Coldain and Claws of Veeshan faction in Velious. In addition, we require that prospective members have and play a level 49+ primary character - alts, however, are entirely welcome and can be of any level. (If your main character does not meet our current minimum level requirment, you may apply anyway and we will consider your application.)

The majority of our message boards are for members-only and are not visible to the general public. You can, however, visit our public forum and see a little of what is written there or post something of your own. (Note that we do not use EZ-Board so you will need to set up an account if you wish to post something.)

Finally, the best way to get to know us is to group with us. Our Roster lists the current membership. Look for these people online and get to know them. Tell them you are interested in learning more about Inspired Order and ask them questions about the guild. If you are offered membership, these will be your brothers and sisters so make sure you also enjoy their company. Naturally, our Guild Officers are available to help match you up with groups and answer questions themselves.

If you've done all this and would like to receive formal consideration as a potential member of Inspired Order, please feel free to submit your Application to Join.
