Website Back Online!

As you can see, the IO website is back online after going down for about 36 hours. Basically, Danadarr hosts the site from his home and his T1 service was temporarily suspended for this period. For those of you interested in a more detailed explanation of the events, you can read the thread which Danadarr posted in the Member Discussion Forum. We're taking steps to prevent this from happening again.

In the meantime, this has thrown a wrench into our raid planning with respect to signups for this weekend and other stuff. Accordingly, we're not going to have any formal raid and will instead have a few pickup groups going each evening. We'll resume our regular raiding schedule next week. Sorry for the inconvenience and thanks in advance for your understanding.

May 20, 2001

Inspired Order is Officially Recruiting

At last night's officer meeting we discussed and concluded that recruiting was an important priority for the guild over the next month or so. While we are off to a tremendous start, there are still level pockets within the guild that sometimes prevent members from being able to find a guild group. We find this unacceptable and intend to recruit across all levels so that IO members can always find other guildmates for groups.

While the officers are leading the charge on this effort, members can help us by keeping their eyes open for good candidates. First and foremost, we want mature individuals that are interested in actively participating in a guild family. Direct potential recruits to the Guild Philosophy page - if they don't like and agree with what is written there then we aren't the right guild for them. Also have them look at the site generally to get a feel for how we operate and what we are like. Finally, make sure they are people that you feel would fit into our family and people that you like to play with.

The best way to recruit new members is to group with them. If you think you've found someone good - send a tell to one of the officers and we'll be happy to help them take the next steps towards joining our guild.

 

Guild Banker, Librarian, and Event Coordinator Announced

We also filled some guild positions at last night's meeting. Dartan will be in charge of the Guild Bank, Arkalen/Shadra will be responsible for the Guild Library, and Fryne is our new Event Coordinator.

If you have items or plat you wish to donate to the bank, talk to Dartan. He will also be posting a list of bank items on the boards in the near future. As guild banker, Dart will also be spending time in EC checking out the auctions for good deals or possible trades that will benefit the guild.

If you have runes, words, or pages, give them to Arkalen/Shadra. Arkalen/Shadra is maintaining the library in Rivervale, but if you can't get there an officer can help get your stuff over to the mules Shad created for the library. A catalog of library items will be posted in the near future as well.

Finally, if you have suggestions for special events or want to get more involved on that front, get in touch with Fryne. Vertix will still be leading raids, but Fryne will be coordinating all other non-raid events such as parties, recruiting events, fairs, contests, quests, etc. There was also some discussion of adding another event coordinator since this is a fairly large task so you may see someone sharing these responsibilities with Fryne at some point. At any rate, look for new threads in the Events Forum soon.

Calendar Section Added to the Website

We've recently integrated a Calendar with the forum software to facilitate scheduling raids, events, pick-up groups, and other activities of interest to the Guild. If you're planning on doing something specific on a given night that you'd like other Inspired Order folk to join you doing, this is the place to schedule it. Hopefully, if we use this feature correctly, we can eliminate anyone ever looking for a group and feeling frustrated.
