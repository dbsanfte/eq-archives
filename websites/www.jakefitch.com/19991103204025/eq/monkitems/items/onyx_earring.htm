The Onyx Earring is found on the Gnoll Commanders in Black Burrow.  There are two locations that I know that Commanders spawn in BB.  One in the large Elite/Commander room, which is all the way at the top of the ramp, and near the snak pit.  The other location is also at the top of the ramp.  Just after you come through the door at the top of the ramp, there is another small ramp to the right, that leads to three small rooms, the gnoll commander spawns in the room to the left as you walk toward them.
