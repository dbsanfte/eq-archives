The headbands and sashes are received by doing quests for the monks at the guilds in Qeynos (headband) and Freeport (sash).  You can do the quests for both guilds if your faction is high enough.  Freeport monks can generally automatically do the Qeynos quests, but the Qeynos monks need to increase thier faction with the Freeport guild before they can complete the quests.  There are a few ways to do this.  One is to kill ALOT of orcs in Freeport, but only a certain type of orc will raise the faction (can't think of the name right now).  Another way is to complete the Qeynos quests multiple times.   A few tips for turning in the items.  First, make sure you are giving them to the right NPC.  Go through the text clues until they list the items needed for the headband/sash.  Always turn in any stackable items in one per item slot, not stacked.

White Headband - you need to give Phin 4 Gnoll Scalps for the White Headbands.  They come from Gnoll Pups in the Qeynos newbie area and the Qeynos Hills.

Yellow Headband - give Phin 3 Putrid Rib bones and your White Headband.  These come from Putrid Skeletons which can be found in the ruins in the north part of Qeynos Hills.

Orange Headband - give Phin 2 Gnoll Pelts (unruined), 1 Gnoll Skin and your Yellow Headband.  The Gnoll Pelts come from Elite Gnoll guards in Black Burrow.  The Gnoll Skin comed from the Gnoll Commanders in Black Burrow.

Red Headband - you need to turn in your Orange Headband (but not to Phin, a different Monk and the guildhouse now), the head of Shen, Dareb's skull, and Drah's head.  All of these items are found in Split Paw, which is in South Karana.   Phido has Dareb's head, Phido is a rare spawn in Ren Rex's location.  A rare spawn Grisok, a high shaman, that is in Lenrel's location has Shen's head.

Purple Headband - turn in your Red Headband, the Dagger of Marnek which comed from third level Befallen on the necro theumaturgist, the skull of Jhen'Tra which comes from the Gnoll Embalmer in Lake Rathe, and Zaharn's Coronet which comes from the level 31 High Priest Zaharn in Permafrost.

Blue and Black Headbands - the quests for these headbands are not it yet, but you can get them from killing the monk guildmasters.

White Sash - 1 bone chip which comes from pretty much every type of skeleton, 2 orc scalps, and 1 snake fang.  All of these items can be collected in the newbie area near the west gate in Freeport.

Yellow Sash - turn in your White Sash, a Giant Snake Rattle which come from Asps and Darkwood snakes, a Chitin from a Desert Tarantula in North Ro, and a Deathfist Slashed Belt which come from Orc Centurions in East Commons.

Orange Sash - your Yellow Sash, a Legionnaire Bracer from an Orc Legionaries, a Greater Lightstone from a Wisp, and a Cutthroat Ring from a Cutthroat Dervish in Ro.

Red Sash - your Orange Sash, 2 pieces of Marnek's wand (Blackened Wand) which is found on third floor Befallen on Priest Azim, and the Sapphire of Souls which is found in Najena.
