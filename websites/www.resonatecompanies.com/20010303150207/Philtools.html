My Wealth Plan and Philanthropic Legacy Questionnaire

 

The value driven questionnaire called "My Wealth Plan and Philanthropic Legacy" helps people further crystallize their values.  The completed questionnaires are returned to you.  Then, you create, or have Resonate create, the person or family's "Philanthropic Philosophy." When the Philanthropic Philosophy is complete, schedule an appointment with the client. Give them their document and leave them alone in the room to read through it for the first time.  Join them in about 15 minutes and ask for their response. Be prepared for "Well, it's close, but it doesn't quite reflect accurately..." This is a perfectly normal part of the process because people often continue to develop their thoughts while you are constructing the written document. Offer to revise this to the satisfaction of the client.  Once it is "right" for the client, then let them know that you have some ideas on how they might accomplish what it is they have identified as being of such importance to them You can show them how to effectively align their financial resources with their goals and values. All you need is some information now about "what they have" so that you can integrate that with "who they are."

Making Peace with Money Workshop

 

Designed for clients or prospects aged 30-60.

 

In spite of the fact that we live in the richest nation in the world  - or perhaps because of this fact – many of us are hurting around money in some way.  We may feel angry that we don’t have enough or guilty because  we have too much; we may be depressed over spending too much and incurring credit card debt; we may be afraid of running out of money and becoming a burden on loved ones.   All feelings attached to money stem from our basic beliefs about money and wealth.  The combination of these beliefs and feelings are powerful and actually control our behavior around money.  We may pursue it relentlessly – never knowing when we have enough; we may give up – believing that we don’t deserve to have more; we may acquire it dishonestly or at the expense of other people.

 

Whatever your clients or donors past experience around money/wealth has been - or present experience is – the all day interactive workshop, Making Peace with Money provides the opportunity for them to create the relationship they want to have with money.  You will help them:

 

·         Learn how to change old beliefs and create new ones;

 

·         Answer intriguing questions about money which lead to a new  understanding of it;

 

·         Define what role money is to play in their life.

 

Comes complete with a detailed Facilitator Guide and 10 Participant Workbooks.
