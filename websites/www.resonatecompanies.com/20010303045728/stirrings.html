OVERVIEW

S tirrings of the SoulTM is a coaching, marketing and training program designed for professional advisors who want to move beyond traditional, transactional planning.

 

It is a transformational journey shared by advisors with their clients, prospects, and/or donors of not-for-profit organizations.  Stirrings of the Soul invites the attendee to move beyond planning just for the tangible side of our wealth - the part that provides a certain dollar value - to explore the intangible side of wealth - the part represented by our core values.  The training includes everything needed to integrate life planning into your organization or practice – questionnaires, new interview techniques, marketing that works!

 

The complete course training also includes twelve separate workshops (7 secular and 5 Christian versions) that can be randomly conducted with a single client or in group settings. The workshops are actually facilitated experiences - rather than a lecture or traditional seminar format.  While the centerpiece of each workshop is money/wealth, the specific subject matter explores the psychological and spiritual aspects, the purpose, and the meaning of money/wealth in our lives.  It further emphasizes living our legacy rather than simply leaving one.

 

The target markets are families, anyone over age 55, mothers and daughters, fathers and sons, donors of not-for-profit organizations . . . essentially anyone.

 

The complete course consists of an initial 2-day training session with a concluding 2-day training session approximately 6 months later.  Between sessions, you will have the opportunity to join in a telephone conference in which you will be able to share your life planning experiences, ask questions to the program leaders, receive valuable coaching and complete your own Personal or Family Mission Statement.

You’ll learn how to:

 

·         Incorporate values-based planning into your existing practice or organization.

·         Shift your approach from being transactionally driven to transformationally driven.

·         Effectively facilitate workshops.

·         Create Personal and Family Mission Statements.

·         Build successful relationships with not-for-profit organizations or other
professional advisors who will sponsor you to present workshops for their
best donors/clients.

·         Think more globally about money/wealth.

 

 

You’ll also discover:

 

·         Why your practice must change to meet your clients’ needs as our country enters the Age of Self-Transformation.

·         Why your clients need a new advisory team to compliment the one they’ve built.

·         Who should be members of this new team.

·         How to position yourself as the liaison between the two teams.

 

 

What can you expect as a result?

 

·         To lead clients into 21st Century planning - which is a comprehensive, not compartmentalized approach.

·         To grow your practice by recognizing your client’s values and responding to the needs of the whole person - not just their financial needs.

·         To distinguish yourself within your community for Life Planning services - not simply financial, estate or tax planning.

·         To dramatically increase fee revenues which have no correlation to assets under management or document preparation.

 

 

Each class is limited to 15 enrollees. Casual attire is encouraged.
