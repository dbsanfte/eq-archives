Hear Barb Culver speak on
Seven Survival Skills for the New Millennium
The seven survival skills for the 21st century appeals to all audience types-whether left or right dominant. It is an amazing blend of technical financial planning strategies, insights into the psychology of planning and changing demographic trends which are impacting our practices today. This information is accompanied by the Six Emotional Reasons Why All People Plan and The Four Dangerous Belief Systems currently present in most financial planners. The program then offers sound reasons why these dangerous belief systems need to be released. Replacement techniques are offered which are more congruent with the sociological trend in this nation, which involves a shift from the Age of Materialism to the Age of Social Transformation. The Seven Survival Skills includes innovative marketing techniques and creative practice management ideas to increase both production and personal satisfaction.
