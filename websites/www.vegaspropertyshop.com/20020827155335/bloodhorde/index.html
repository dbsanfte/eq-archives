When the Bloodhorde was first congealed, it was determined that the world of Norrath needed to know that the power of evil would one day reign supreme over all the land. High Elf, Iksar, and Troll alike would all know the truth and bow down before the evil ones.

To that end, it was written upon the first Wall of Blood that those of the Horde would greet each other and also let those unbelievers know of the coming scourge that would sweep them away. This was to be known as the Bloodhorde warcry, and upon entering a new land, each Hordeling was to shout it for all to hear, and for the other Horde nearby to answer in kind.

This Bloodhorde mandate, handed down from Zhokaii, Kolu and Runja - the Most Ancient and Powerful
