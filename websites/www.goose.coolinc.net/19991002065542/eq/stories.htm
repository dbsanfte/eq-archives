Welcome to the EQ Stories page.
Here is where anyone who likes to write stories can come and
publish their greatest works.

Info: I read all posted stories. Stories should be EQ related. I will delete any
story that I feel is not fit to publish. These stories will be read by all ages.
Previous writers asked for a different format so that others could post about
their story. So with my limited resources I decided to use a message board
format. This way the story will be the main thread and comments, etc. will be
listed under.
