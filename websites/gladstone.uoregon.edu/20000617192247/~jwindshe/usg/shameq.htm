Shaman Equipment Guide

Ok, changed this a bit, hopefully a bit more informative. As a shaman, our primary role is a caster, not a tank, so if I leave out special equipment, it's probably because I didn't feel it was worth mentioning, as you should have one of the other items. Items with Wisdom/Mana bonuses are highlighted. There may be some errors, if you see any, LET ME KNOW, please :)


General Armor Sets
ClothBetter than naked, but not by much. Don't waste any money on it, just find it on corpses.1-12 Mobs, NPCs
PatchworkGood stuff. If you don't want to make it yourself, you can buy from other players. Get this as soon as possible.Made by players
Raw-HideStep up from Patchwork. Set yourself up in Patchwork, and replace Cloth slots with this when you get a chance.12-18 Mobs
LeatherStore bought Raw-HideNPCs
LupineLeather AC, nice stat bonuses, mostly meant for druids, int-based casters, and monks.Werewolves
RingmailThis is the cheap Chainmail. It has the same AC, but it weighs more.16-24 Mobs, NPCs
ChainmailWeighs less than Ringmail, but quite a bit more expensive, not really worth it for us.NPCs
Blackened"Magic" armor, same AC as Chainmail, weighs more than Ringmail, has no bonuses, but hey, it looks like black chainmail.Runnyeye
BandedExcellent Player-Made armor. Lightwieght, AC between Chainmail and Plate. Get as much of this as you can, around level 20.Player-Made
RubiciteAC 2 points better than plate, looks like shiny red plate, lightwieghtCazic-Thule
Rune EtchedAC equivalent or better than Rubicite, huge stat bonuses, looks like grey plate, shaman only.Fear
Lustrious RussetAC better than Rune Etched, similar stat bonuses, not sure of color.Fear
