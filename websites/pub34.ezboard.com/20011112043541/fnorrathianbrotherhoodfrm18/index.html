THE NORRATHIAN BROTHERHOOD

 

YOU GO, WE GO!
The Norrathian Brotherhood is a group of people dedicated to one another and making Norrath a better place to be. We have sworn loyalties to one another. As The Norrathian Brotherhood we pride ourselves in making our guild one of the strongest in Norrath by ensuring that each member is always meeting its full potential. This is achieved by
other members helping out with class quests, difficult levels, or just moral support. The Norrathian Brotherhood also shares ideas and tactics to help out in the development
and playing abilities of younger members.
Come, join our crusade!
