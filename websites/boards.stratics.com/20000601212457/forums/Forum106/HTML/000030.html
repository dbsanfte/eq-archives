Skill gains did not take insane amounts of maps until the mid to upper 80's. Up until that point I could gain .1-.2 every map... then it started taking 3 or 4 maps to gain .1 in the 70's... then it got to 20-30 maps in the low 80's. After 88.0, I started to actually write down the number of maps it took for me to gain .1.

The worst .1 for me was 99.2, which took 678 maps to get. However, it was offset by 99.4 and 100.0, which took 2 and 8 maps to gain, respectively.

The key to gaining Cartography is *do something else at the same time*. Fight your friend's summoned elementals, work your swords, tactics, resist, and do Cartography at the same time! Just standing in the NPC mapmaker's shop makes the skill go slower, I think.

And, most importantly... you only ever need to make DETAILED maps. Don't mess with the other levels, as they don't sell as well, and deplete the NPC of his money.
