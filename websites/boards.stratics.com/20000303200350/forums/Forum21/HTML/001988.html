Here is what I have found in getting to 50th.

The strength bonus is doubled on 2H weapons when compaired to 1h. That was posted by Verant some time long ago, or perhaps it was a change made in an early patch, I dont recall.

Stregth Bonus is a percentage of weapon damage. Max hits will tend to be in the range of 4 to 5 times base damge (max hit with a Ykesha is 42 (8/24), Ex Ax 100 (25/50), dont know what it is with the Mith 2H). My mode with the Mith 2H is 50.

2H vs 1h is entirely dependent on the weapons you are using. Using the released formulas and my personal skills (200 Duel Weild, 205 Double attack) on average I will get 1.51 attacks around with 2h and 2.14 with 2 1h weapons. YOu can now take the efficancy your weapons and see which comes out better. For figureing out 2 1h weapons which are different use 1.51*efficancy for the primary and 0.63*efficancy for the second weappon. For 2h weapons use 1.51* efficany. This is based on a 50th level charicter.

Criticals seem to be based on the following: minimum hit is 2x damage +1. There is a bonus factor of 0-7 (or 8 this is off the top of my head). The formula for the extra damage seems to be (level-12)/5.
