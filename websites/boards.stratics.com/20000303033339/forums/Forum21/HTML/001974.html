I'm soloing and grouping in Cazic now with my L26 Cleric.

You can pull and kill Defenders and Radiants from the entrance to the maze. Every now and then one of them pops his head out the door and generally is alone when you pull him.

I've also killed Warders, Proselytes and Pages, pulling from the hallway that leads to the crater.

This type of soloing is not all that profitable here though. You tend to get trained out fairly often and there's some really funny mechanism at work near zone which causes an Alligator and a Page to pop into your battles fairly often if you try to pull to zone.

One other thing to be really careful of is Bouncer Hurd who occasionally gets pulled to the Feerot side of zone and will instakill you if you zone out with a bubble or two left.

Find a group in the crater or CY and you'll profit very well in Cazic during the 20's.
