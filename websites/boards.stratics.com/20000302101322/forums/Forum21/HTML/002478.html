It's not your strength, it's just the way the combat system works. An EBW is 7 damage, therefore at level 43, you should hit for exactly 20 about 30% of the time, follwed by hits under 20, then hits above 20. Your strength only increases the maximum damage you can do with the weapon, but not the frequency.

With a 5 damage shark tooth you should hit for 16 damage 30% of the time. With only a 0.3 second difference in delay, it's clear that the EBW comes out ahead, though in the long run they may equal out due to the fact that the faster the weapon the more chances you have to double attack in a battle.
