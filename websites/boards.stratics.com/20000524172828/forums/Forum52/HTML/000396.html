I read from a post somewhere else that someone was able to tailor a whip with the same formula, except that a medium quality cat pelt was used. I decided to try it out, and to my surprise, it worked -- same item, same stats. Since the trip from Freeport to Qeynos is both absurdly dangerous (Kithicor comes to mind) as well as incredibly boring, I wasn't interested in risking life and limb to go and get some wolf skins. But after finding that the cat pelts would work, I made a few in North Ro -- nobody wanted them anyway. Freeport is just a bad place to sell items of that level, I suppose. Especially with all the twinks about..

Overall, I think that the trade skill weapons aren't a real money maker. Minotaur Axes, Dwarven Axes, Fine Steel swords, Forged Bastard Swords, and the Tailor-made whip all compete in the same price range. With all the Axes and Fine Steel about, it's rather easy for a newbie to grab one for a couple of plat, or for free. I think that the trade skill weapons, while certainly not bad, just don't have a big enough edge over the preferred mino axes and such.
