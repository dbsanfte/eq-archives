Only a DE Enchater lvl 8+ or any other Enchanter lvl 12+ can quest for Stein. The quest starts in Freeport, at the docks, in Seafarer's Roost Tavern. The bartender, Gregory, talks about exotic drinks. He also describes where each component can be found. Of these 4 components, 3 are LORE, and 2 are NO DROP. 2 items are obtained via subquests. One item can be obtained two ways, both at a cost of SEVERE faction hit. Employing a mule is advised. After you get the item from Gregory, go to Oggok, and give item to appropriate Ogre in exchange for Stein.

Jewelled Skull quest starts in West Karana. Involves killing a lvl 30+ mob I heard.
All NPC's you interact with must have faction indifferent or better.

I did the quest yesterday, with no problem. Maybe they patched the group method - a quest runner groups with the mule, mule kills fish, quest runner loots. The method 'kill with mule, camp, load quest runner, wait till corpse lootable, loot' works fine.

The other way to get the fish (wolf Frostbite) precludes mule usage - Frostbite cons blue to lvl 14.

Faction hit severity: I usually make a Human Wizard who worships Rodcet Nife as a mule, and gauge my faction on Eric Rulkin. I start at amiable. I put all practie points into piercing, and go kill rats and snakes until level 2. It is only 15-20 min, and is really worth it. After killing 2 fish, faction dropped to apprehensive. After 5 - dubious. After 8 - threatening. If you want only one stein, killing one fish might be ok. But not every koalindl yields a fish - yesterday my mule killed 4 of them before getting a single fish.

Here are ways to 'patch' this:
1. Give faction hit to BOTH killer and looter.
2. Make fish level 10.
3. Post an extra couple of guards in the pool.


But here's something I wonder about. What if:
1.bind at qeynos pond
2.nuke yourself till you have 1 bubble of health left.
3.enter water right next to Rulkin.
4.Target Koalindl.
5.Cast a Suffocating Sphere (Choke is too strong)
6.Get killed instantly by Rulkin - step 2 ensures an instant kill
7.Respawn
If I remember correctly, if you die before fish dies, you take no faction hit.
Your Suffocating Sphere should finish off the fish AFTER you die.
I also believe that the corpse of fish will remain.

I don't want to risk my Enchanter - but if someone has a throw-away char with a DOT spell,
it is worth a try.
