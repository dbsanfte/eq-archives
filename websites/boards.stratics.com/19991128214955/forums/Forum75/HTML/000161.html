Hey Everyone,

I've been lurking around the AC boards on various sites for awhile now, and I finally have something to say! Actually, I'm not going to say much, but I hope you all will.

Basically, what I would like to do is have anyone who is interested to give me your opinions on what would make a good game. AC specific, or just a generic wish list.

The caveat however, is this...I would like you to justify your ideas, by backing it up with how it would function...in other words, balance the idea! No 'I want huge swords that do 100 points of damage to everything', as that would obviously not be too balanced.

The areas I really want to hear about are...item system ideas, skill / class issues (which is preferred), and more importantly, how would you design your own magic system and combat system...

Hopefully this will spark some good discussions on what we really want to see in a game like AC. It's kind of a hobby lately for me to see how I can design these systems...and I want to know if others have thought about this also.
