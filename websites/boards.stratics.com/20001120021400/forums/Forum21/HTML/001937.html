Hello fellow warriors!
Some of you on the Rodcet Nife Server may know me as Gravid, a 14th lvl warrior who has recently been hanging around Crushbone alot. Well, first off I will admit, I am twinked, but I roleplayed it out. If anyone were to view my character, they would read a description saying that my characters father was a member of the wall guard for 22 seasons, and upon his untimely retirement, he offered his equipment to Gravid in hopes he too would become a hero of the wall someday. And this does hold true to my characters, I retired a 22nd lvl warrior, though human, because of a bad starting decisions, and created Gravid, whom I am very happy with so far. I love the reactions I recieved when people call out for tank to group with and I show up, standing all of 3 feet tall taking on Orc Emmisaries and Legionaires. I have properly developed Gravid with maxxed skills in 1h and 2h slash, and I have also maxxed out his sense heading, and bind wounds, which was more than I did for my retire warrior. I have been very happy with the outcome of my character thus far, and I must say that I can stand toe to toe with many other races of warriors. I think halflings as a warrior are superb because they start with a high agility, which helps in dodging and avoiding attacks, and with a point placement dispersed between Dex and Str, you can have one tough little guy to work with. I think what I most enjoy, and what other warriors in grousp enjoy is that fact that we naturally lead, and I love the idea that people twice my height "look up" to me. I have very good knowledge of the lands on Norrath and I have traversed many places since the lands were created. I must say that I knew I enjoyed the Warrior class best of all before I created Gravid, But I would have to say that us Halflings are the most entertaining, and role-play valued races of warriors I have played. I want to thank everyone on this board for providing me with very intuitive information through out my playing of this great game, and I look forward to learning more as the months go by.


Gravid
Halfling warrior of the 14th seasons
