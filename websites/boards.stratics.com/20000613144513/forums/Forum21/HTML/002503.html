Well, my advice is to bitch to VI about it on their official board. Gordon already said that they would look into it. That is a step in the right direction. In case you missed the post he bascially said that they decided to have a set number of drops per day come out of the planes and then tuned the drop rates to match that number based on the theoretical number of plane clearings that can be done. Suffice to say that they totally missed in their estimation of how many times you can clear Hate in a day. They even admitted this.
