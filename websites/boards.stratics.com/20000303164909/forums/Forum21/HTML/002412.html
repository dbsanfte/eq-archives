I've started a Barb Warrior (level 5)---one of the two classes/races I always wanted to play but was cautioned against. My other characters are a level 26 Human Druid and a level 18 High Elf Paladin. One thing I've noticed about my dim-witted barbarian is he seldom gains skill in either 1HS or 2HS. My defensive and offensive skills go up at a nice clip each level but I am lucky to get 2 skill increases per level with my weapons. I am not a mega-twink but I am twinked with banded, a langseax and a BBC. My Paladin was also twinked with banded, a BBC and a FS scimitar (now a silvery war axe). The high intelligence of the High-elf Pally allowed me to max out both slashing melee skills within the first two bubbles of experience after making each level. I have to spend training points to see any real skill increase with my Barbarian. Is this something I need to get used to or is it just a statistical oddity I am going through? I expected the skill increase to come slower with a 60 int, but I didn't expect it to be this bad.

I've started a Barb Warrior (level 5)...but I am lucky to get 2 skill increases per level with my weapons.

You've got 3 things working against you here.

1) Some skills (notably weapon skills and sense heading) are a real bear to raise before you reach a skill level of 20.
2) The first few levels are fast, so you have a limited amount of time in which to get enough hits in to raise the skill.
3) Barb INT is low, making it hard to raise skills.

When you get to a higher level, you'll start maxing the skills out because you'll get more successes per level, giving you more chances to increase the skill.

Let me suggest that if you want to work piercing or 1H Blunt after level 13 is just to put the weapon in your off hand. YOu can gain skill while using a different weapon in your off hand.

For example, At level 20, I realized that I wanted to be able to use any weapon that I picked up. Never know when you are doing the long naked walk if you need a weapon.

I was maxed in 1H slash, DW, and DA. I just bought a FS Dagger (delay 19) put it in my off hand and went to town on the blue cons. Very slow going til about the skill number 20 and then the fast speed of the dagger really caused a climb. For 1H Blunt, I used a Screaming Mace, not the fastest, but the proc made up for it.

For 2HB, go to Lake Rathe and find the shovel in the orc camp. Delay 27 Damage 4 will help raise skill a lot.
