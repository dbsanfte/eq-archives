Spectres are good experience at 40 in a small group (2-3). They are usually camped however. If Oasis and Feeroot are both camped, you might try the Spectre Island in OOT.

I know you said SolB was camped, but does that include the beetle rooms?

If you get a full group of good players (need 6 40+) you can camp the death beetle room and rake in the experience. The group I've been hunting there lately was 39Cle, 43Dru, 49pal, 49Mag, 45Pal, 44Enc (me). Do *NOT* attempt this with poor players, you will die horribly. I'd also suggest not attempting it without a 39th+ Cleric and Enchanter. There are 4 beetle spawns in the room, plus one down the ramp to the Imp/Efreet area (don't cross the lava). You can also pull as many bats as you want while waiting for the beetle to spawn.

If you'd rather not fight beetles, the bats are great exp and you can pull the roaming LDC/Nox placeholder. I expect the bat rooms are camped, however.

Hope this helps.

Kedge Keep is fantastic at your level. Camp the room under the golden haired mermaid (nothing spawns or agro's there) and pull from all directions. You will usually get 1, or at most 2, at a time. When helping friends level there I usually pull mermaids as well, but if you are short on casters you can stick to fish.

Seafury isle is ok... the cyclops don't give great XP in my opinion, but they have a very fast respawn and drop lots of plat and gems. The dinosaur is lousy XP (too much downtime), but he drops some very nice gems (black sapphire, diamond, jacinth) and level 49 research items.

Sol B is camped silly, except for bugs and bats. Guk can be good at certain hours, but again, it is usually camped silly (100+ people in it on weeknights on Innoruuk.)

I'm annoyed by guard-killers a bit, too, I can attribute at least one death of my then-low-teens Troll SK to players camping the bouncers in front of Ogguk. Then again, if Hurd chases you to Cazic or the spectre cave, what are you gonna do? I don't see a problem (aside from aesthetics) with camping guards just inside a town zone, or even with wandering guards, but guards posted specifically to protect newbies I agree should be (a)made insanely difficult (ie. level 53 bouncers) or (b)go poof like a vendor. Freeport Militia by the newbie zones would be the only exception IMHO.

As far as giving guards weapons, hand them two one-handed rusty things. As I understand it, an NPC does no different with one weapon as they would barehanded, but a second one hander will let them use Dual Wield, which of course will make them tougher.

The respawn on the Ice Giants outside Perma is pretty quick, so I would say that's a good spot even if there are one or two competing groups...only when a Vox party was congregating had I ever seen the pickings particularly thin there.

I finished level 40 in the Raider room in High Keep. It was too much for me to solo, though yes the individual green warriors were quite soloable then, but teamed with a level 40 Wizard we did just fine. I was rarely above half health, but never needed more than that once the spawn was broken. If you have a healer or two, it can be slow going there.

My experience with the Sol B bats/bugs has been if the King's camped, so probably will bugs. Fortunately the bug area can support at least two independent 40-50th level groups (there's a zone to Sol A for this area that can be a safer option than the bat room by the trap). The "tiger room" and the Noble are good options for 35-45, but tend to get pretty crowded during peak times.
