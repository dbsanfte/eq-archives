There are many types of treasure hunters people like to play. It is all about style. Basically, it boils down to whether or not you want to use magery. It seems that you do. Forget about 50 magery/50 resist. Its all or nothing. If you don't believe me, use the resist calculator at this site and you will be astonished as to how worthless 50 resist is. To counter the little bit of extra damage you might take, drink greater heals while fighting.

Also, do you want to fight the monsters from harder chests? The only way to succusfully do this on your own(in my opinion) is with a bard treausre hunter. Either through peacemaking or provoking. The only problem with provoking is that the first monster spawn might be a problem if it is only one singular, tough monster. Peacemkaing is handy too.

Furthermore, as far as I am concerned don't GM Detect Hidden. Get it to about 50, and don't worry about it after that. After all, you *know* the chest is trapped, just use it until you are successful.

Also, since OSI is undoubtedly going to nerf telekinesis, they are probably going to do something about everyone knowing the exact locations of treasure spawns as they appear on the map. Random treasure chest locations is not that hard to implement. Besides what sense does it make having buried treasure in the same spot. What pirate would bother to bury it in the same spot if it were gone from there since the last time he tried it in that location? Anyway, what I am trying to get at here is that Mining may become a little more important again soon. Just keep an eye out.

Good Luck
