Well if you want to kill a LOT of lower level creatures EASIER, try a War Mage first. I have experimented with one and at level ONE I was able to kill any 2,3, or 4th level creature with ease (just blast them at a safe distance). My main character who's kind of a hybrid warrior and life magic (cleric type) is level 4 and still has a HARD time killing the Level 4 Drudge Slinker/Sneaker classes (he can do it but almost always has to heal or wait for HP to go up after the fight). I don't know if this obvious advantage to the warmage character lasts longer into the game (I encountered a skeleton in the desert last night that ran so fast he was upon me before I got the 2nd spell off), but early on they are devistating.
