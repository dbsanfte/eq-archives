OK, first question, what is twinked. There are several theories, but basically it is players using there higher lvl character to aquire items that a lvl 1 - 10 prolly should not be able to get. Another is money. They give lower character money to allow them to get going through the low lvls. That is a basic twink.

Second question. What price to sell Raw Silk at. Depends on the server, I started on Vallos Zek a 1pp per AC. I found that people just were not going for that. So I refined it to, 5 gold per AC. Which for Robe would be 3 plat, Leggings 2 Plat and everything else 1 Plat. So I was making good money and exp, killing the large and giant spiders.
