A comment on the best source for HQ Bear Skins:

They only come from black bears and brown bears. or at least I have never found one nor heard of anyone finding one on any other sought of bear.

So if you have some your people looking for hq bear skins for you tell them to only kill black and brown bears [BTW brown bears are only found in the Queynos area, it is black bears almost everywhere else].

Also warn them that they will only get 1 skin in every 20-25 kills, and with bad luck they may go 60 or more without a single hq bear skin.

NEVER PAY anyone less than 20plat for a HQ bear skin, if you do you are ripping them off. Charge 100plat or more when you sell them, the high level players who want these items can afford it, the newbies who you give 20 or plat to can really do wonders with this money. If you feeel like you are making to much profit, don't drop the price on the back packs, raise what you offer to newbies for HQ bear skins.

BTW if you are the first to raise prices to 30+ plat per skin on your server you can make life very tough for your competition.

Try this trick ...
auction for hq bear skins at 20plat. But give them 25plat and tell them if they bring you more you will give them 30plat for every additional one.

Your competition will not believe you are offereing 30plat per skin because they have never heard you shout it out. Now lots of people will save their skins for you.
