They've already nerfed Death by making it so other people can't loot you until you decay. You've got quite a bit of time to locate your corpse, so the only time you're going to lose items is if you were somewhere you REALLY should not have been in and decide to write them off, or you weren't paying attention to your position when you died.

I understand that it is frustrating to lose items, but rather than have them add additional code to make it even easier to find your body, just try to remember to check your map if things look bad.

If they do much more to make Death less bothersome, they might as well just do away with item loss entirely.

It would, however, be very nice if they would allow us to give looting rights to people. While this might make Death less inconvienient as well, it is idiotic that I can guard my vassal's corpse, but not return their gear to them - If the real reason that they did away with looting was to discourage anti-social behavior, the least they could do would be to allow social behavior.

Give us a "@loot [playername]" command at least - though in a perfect world we could have a "@loot add [playername]" to add people to a looting list.
