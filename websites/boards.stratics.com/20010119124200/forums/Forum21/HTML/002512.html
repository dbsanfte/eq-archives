I've found literally dozens of pieces of medium bronze in Sol B over the last week or two. I have a full suit of medium bronze in the bank, which I'm keeping with plans of twinking. The rest I sell or give away. A nice level 1 bard on my server got med bronze arms, breastplate, gloves, and boots last night because he didn't ask for them and looked like he needed help.

The beggars, of course, get nothing from me.

As a follow up, go to lavastorm if you are able to, and check the vendors there. Often you'll find bronze for sale, since the stuff is heavy and many of the Sol B hunters are tired of lugging it around and just dump it on the vendor. The prices are very low, since a bronze bracer sells to a vendor for about 1.5 plat. If you also auction for bronze you may find people willing to sell to you for reduced rates, though there are always the opportunists who'll try to get every last cent out of you.
