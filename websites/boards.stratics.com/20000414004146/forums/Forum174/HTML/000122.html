I firmly believe the reason Verant doesn't want Vox or Nag to be taken in a group of six is simply because of the squabbles that would occur. The dragons only spawn every seven to eight days now, at random, but they pop every time after a server patch or reset. Imagine now twelve groups of six all racing to Sol B the minute the server comes back up to get to Nagafen and the KSing that would occur. It would be chaos. Verant likes it as it is where it requires 18 to 24 very good players to take out a dragon, since by default on any mature server there are usually only one or two guilds that can do it with success. Voila, dragons are "uber-mobs" and there is very little competition for their very nice and sought-after loot (except within the group itself). It's waaaaaaaaay easier to assemble a group of six or eight 55's for a dragon raid than a group of 18 or 24 50s. Thus unless they twinked the dragons up, these mobs would suddenly be accessible to many, many more players and chaos would reign.

The solution? Up the spawn on the dragons to three times a day or so. Drop their loot a bit if you have too (frequency and quality) but not too much. So what if in six months most 55's are walking around in a cloak of flames? Right now most 50 casters have runed cowls/SMRs or plane gear and most 50 warrs have mith 2H and some plane armor. God, we've all seen level 15 twinks dual wielding yaks LOL, and yaks used to be talked about in hushed tones as if mentioning the weapon of God Himself. I realize dragon/Fear/Hate gear right now is considered elite and tops, but as soon as Air/fill-in-name-of-new-zone-or-plane-here gear becomes available, it will be considered routine. Sure, Vox and Nag or draco will no longer be the ubermobs they once were but again, so what. A week into first Beta Emperor Crush was considered just about unkillable hehe and we all got over that.
