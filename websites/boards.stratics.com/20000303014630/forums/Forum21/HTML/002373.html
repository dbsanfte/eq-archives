Hi all-

Here's a tip I picked up recently, of relatively limited use but kinda fun and VERY nice for that one particular spot.

Warriors can use "pets"!!
The cave entrance to the POF in the Ferrott is guarded by a flock of spectres- one outside on hill, one in entrance, ~2 hall patrol, and maybe 4 hidden back in the left branches of the cave. They ALL seem to be linked tho, so attacking one at lower levels (I'm 44) tended to pull the whole cave out, which is a big mess even with pally / necro / cleric fear kiting. Makes life as a warrior unfufilling since even careful pulls can end up as a disaster.

So here's the trick: just north along the west zone wall is a ruin with a couple skeletons or zombies. Pop an arrow into one of them, but do NOT melee or even face it afterwards (a single riposte will generally kill a skele, tho the zombies tend to be a bit more durable). Now, with your "pet" following you (and beating on you incessently for 0 or 1 pt of damage!), walk over near the secret entrance to the cave. POOF, a spectre or two will warp in and attack- pull it over to the SW zone corner (very close) and put the smack on it w/ yer buddies. Lather- rinse- repeat. Keep moving farther in the cave as you denude the supply... fairly fast respawn tho, so make sure to watch yer watch. I've cleaned out the entire cave with a particularly lucky/tough zombie before, one or two specs at a time.

Why it works: beats me. I'm GUESSING that having the skele/zombie attacking you increases the aggro radius of the spectres dramatically, and since they attack you instead of you attacking them, an "alert" signal doesn't get sent to the other ones it's linked to.

Note: a LOT of people will say "you're aggroing the whole cave", etc. Simply isn't true. To get some of the specs to aggro (NEVER attack them directly first), you have to walk almost up to their face, even with a skele beating on you. If someone doesn't believe you, invite them to come into cave with you and watch you play shadowknight-
in-crafted armor.

I've used the same tactic to pull freeport guards. Though it requires some assistance in this particular spot. Basically, I give 1pp to a newbie to lure mobs within guard aggro range. Same trick in CT - using a broodling, you can pull the outter throne room without dumping the whole throne room on ya. Just tag a broodling and bring him up to the steps. Works like a charm.
