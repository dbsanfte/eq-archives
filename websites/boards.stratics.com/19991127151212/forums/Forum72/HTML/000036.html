Sorry for the long absence, folks. It's been the usual brushfires around here.

Nayt, the problem with the the inconsistency lay in Turbine's own specs, and I just happened to fall victim to it. There is a Monsters-General doc which gives a small amount of information on each species in Dereth. This doc mentions in a single sentence that Banderlings, Mosswarts, Drudges, and, yes, Tumeroks, all come from the same world.

On the other hand, there is the Tumerok-Dungeons doc, which goes into considerable detail about those critters, and which says absolutely nothing about the other species they supposedly share a homeworld with. It also says, "The Tumerok homeworld was dry and barren... The predominant colour of the vegetation there was mottled violet, which explains the creatures' garish coloration." If this is true, it makes no sense to think that green, swamp-dwelling Mosswarts also originate from this barren, purple world.

For what it's worth, which probably isn't that much, my original title for "On Drudges" was "The Nocturnes." Fortunately, they didn't change any of the text, just the title.
