Time to replace the lizzie scale cloak, yeah. Best options are a Hooded Black Cloak (10 ac, 5 str, 45 hp) or a Cloak of Shadows (12 ac, 13 dex, 10 vs disease). Personally, I'd go for the Hooded. While it's almost as rare a drop as the Cloak of Shadows, it's a much easier and less competitive camp. You don't need the Dex on the Cloak of Shadows, though it does have more AC.

Of course, a Cloak of Flames would be fun. =)
