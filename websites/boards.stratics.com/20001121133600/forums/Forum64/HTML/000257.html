This isn't the Oaken Scimitar quest...the resulting pre-magical sword is a Longsword. Few things that I seem to be the only one to have noticed:
1. He doesn't say "One-Eyed", but that it needs a gnoll eye stolen by a white gnoll.
2. The Gnolls of East Karana (Reavers) are all white gnolls.
3. If I'm wrong and it IS a one-eyed white gnoll, the Undead Reavers are one-eyed also. Same place.
4. The gnolls of Splitpaw are also white, but he says "In the Karanas" not "In Splitpaw". Might want to check faction hits for when you turn the kid in...the thief's faction will probably drop. If Splitpaw faction drops, go there to check it out.

I've heard of a named White Gnoll named Splitclaw (not to be confused with the dungeon) who spawns on Reaver Hill in EK when you have killed a lot of reavers. He's supposedly level 20 or so and is a necromancer. I've been hunting him a while on the chance that he's the one, but no luck so far. Then again, considering who I am, no wonder.
