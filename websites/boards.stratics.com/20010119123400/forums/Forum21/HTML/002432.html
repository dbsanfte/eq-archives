Max hits can be approximated with this formula:

(Level + Weapon Skill + Strength)/100 * Weapon Damage

It appears to be *slightly* inaccurate, i.e. no more than 1-2 pts off. Anyway, a 50th level warrior with 200 strength wielding a 35 damage gleaming bastard sword should be max hitting for around 157, with the most common hit being about 78. Still quite impressive.

If I could have ANY item and use it (regardless of class/race restrictions) it would be a Butcherblock Hammer. It was given out in a GM event and is Cleric/Paladin only, but I can dream. 34 damage, 45 delay, 10 strength, uncharged effect of superior heal that procs at level 50. Wow.

If I could have any warrior usable item, I would want either a Belt of Contention (plane of sky haste belt for warriors, 10 AC, 5 str, 5 sta, 5 sv fire) or an indicolite breastplate. Both are the best piece available for their respective body part, so it's a toss up. There are so many really good items usable by warriors 46th level and above.
