Obviously, I enjoy tank classes. But the other thing I noticed: I hate casting spells. Not just mildly dislike, I absolutely hate casting. I really don't know why but I really like just running straight up to something and just laying into it.

Maybe it's the simplicity of it. Now don't get me wrong, there is strategy for playing every class, I just find the warriors strategy the most fun and entertaining and it just seems to come very easily. Which isn't to say that warriors are easy to play. Far from it. If warriors were easy to play then you would see a ton of powergamers playing warriors not necros and druids.

Warriors, having no spells and fewer skills than anyone, have to become the best at game mechanics. No on I know is as good at the mechanics of swimming as I am, or of cliff-running, or zone-dancing, or gliding at full run, etc.

Now which is more fun? Turn yourself invisible and hit the num lock through the Gorges of King Xorbb? Or cling to the cliff...time your descent when it becomes perpendicular...watch the minos running by underneath you...now run run run to the next cliff and up as fast as you can...time your descent...blitz for the zone with mino horns blowing in your ears.

It's like we're level 1 characters until the day we hit 50. We just become STRONGER level 1 characters. If what you really like is just playing the game, then I think we're the best class of all.
