Apparently the current steps the moderators take to manage the forums are failing in some regard. I've gotten several emails or messages on the staff forums regarding the number of personal attacks that have been getting through lately.

Now they stop.

Effective immediately, on all forums, the Moderators have been directed to follow the following guidelines for handling personal attacks.

Personal Attacks
As the population of the forums has increased, the number of personal conflicts is on the rise. To deal with the increasing count of personal attacks, we are implementing a strict warning policy.

1) When a user demonstrates that they are in the habit of making personal attacks in their posts, you should issue a Warning to that user that Personal Attacks are not allowed on Stratics, and that they should refrain from such attacks in the future. If the attack is particularly offensive, it should be removed - otherwise the user can be encouraged to edit it. The offensive message, the Logged IP assosciated with it, and your Warning, should be sent to me. (greebo@stratics.com)

2) If the user then posts a second attack created after the first warning is given, the user should be given a Second Warning, with the understanding that a Third Warning will result in a one-week suspention from the forums. Again, the offensvie message, the Logged IP assosciated with it, and your 2nd Warning, should be sent to me.

3) If the user makes a third personal attack after the second warning, then you should inform the user that you are sending me a request for Suspension, and send me the 3rd attack, the Logged IP assosciated with it, and your notice. Upon receipt of the 3rd warning, I will evaluate the attacks and the warnings, and if warranted, I will suspend the access of the user to these forums for one week, and send an email to their registered address explaining such. I will mark my calendar to lift the suspention on the designated date.

4) If the User returns and resumes making personal attacks, or if the User bypasses the Suspension by creating a 2nd account under a different address, the evidence of this (4th Attack or post by the Bypass account) should be sent to me, as well as the Logged IP assosciated with it. The User will be permanently banned at this point under both Email addresses and IPs of these points.

5) It remains the judgement of the Moderator to determine whether a Personal Remark qualifies as a Personal Attack. However, I will continue to personally investigate complaints about any user/thread which are sent to me directly. With the institution of this new practice, hopefully the number of emails I receive should decrease significantly.

Greebo
Stratics EverQuest Interractive Coordinator
