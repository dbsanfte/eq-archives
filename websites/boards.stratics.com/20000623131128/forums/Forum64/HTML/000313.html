First up he has TWO placeholders. Brother Zephyl and I quote: " A Drake ".
If Brother Zephyl is up you must wait for him to despawn ( this takes 2 hours from when he has first spawned )and then either Hasten or " A Drake " will spawn. HOWEVER if Brother Zephyl is stuck on that bugged rock, he will NOT despawn. Your only chance is to petition and hope the GM will make him die. Just sit at that bugged rock, ( junction before the named beetles, second up from feerrott zone ) and wait for " A Drake " or Hasten to run past. " A Drake " usually always gets stuck on the rock, kill it whenever it gets there and eventually Hasten will run past. Dont go chasing all over the mountains for Hasten, he runs past the rock and he is very fast, you will not catch him unless you have Spirit of the Cheetah, or the bard song Selo's Accelerando. Goodluck
