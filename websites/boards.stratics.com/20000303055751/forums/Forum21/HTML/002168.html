Don't bother with the PGT. It is relatively slow and the damage shield it procs is pretty useless after about level 20. You'd be better off with a Dragoon Dirk or a Langesaxe. Silvery War Axe is a slightly better 1hs. You can't get 2 because they're lore. Other good options within reach would be an Ashenwood Short Spear or an Enamelled Black Mace.

As for armor, other than a few exceptions go for crafted. Head, chest, wrist, and shoulders are the only pieces I see any debate against crafted (SSB, EB BP, Hero Bracer, Carnal Pauldruns being the favorite counterparts). Oh yeah, don't tell your priest, but your true god is AC

Your warrior will be better off with 1 handed weapons til he hits 20 (bye bye damage cap). Then he'll be better off with 2 handed til about 30 (off hand weapon starts getting double attack). Then it's a toss up that depends on the weapons. A good choice for a 16 warrior would be a Langesaxe of the Wolves or equivalent.

Actually if you want to work both slashing and piercing at the same time you might want to go with a Dragoon Dirk instead of a langesaxe or maybe an Ashenwood Short Spear. They're fast so you'll fairly easily max both 1hs and pierce.

The PGT is good until about level 20 or so but the higher your level, the less useful it becomes. The damage shield is relatively small and you take an AGI hit for it. Plus it's slower than a langesaxe or equivalent. As mobs start doing more and more damage, the PGT proc becomes a liability because of the AGI hit and the difference in delay becomes more important.
