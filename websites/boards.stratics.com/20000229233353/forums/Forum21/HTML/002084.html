It's quite possible to max out all of your weapon skills, especially as a gnome or a dark elf. I'd recommend working on your 2hs and 2hb as early as possible or it'll be hell to raise them later. The 1 handers raise considerably faster than the 2 handers so it's not that big a deal if you let those lag behind a bit.

I finally got an EB mace right around the time I hit level 42 and now that I'm about to hit 43 I've raised it from a skill of about 15 to a skill of around 120. Given the fact that the only 2hb worth using for a warrior is dropped by a god, I doubt I'll get around to maxing 2hb unless I get the Ashenbone Warhammer sometime.
