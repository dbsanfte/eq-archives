I've stood up for PvP many times, but there is a lot more to AC than a single player game with a chat-room, even if they scrapped PvP altoghether.

The focus of AC is multi-player. A lot of people get more enjoyment out of a game if they can play with their friends - even if they can't kill one another. The most fun I've had in AC is in fellowships, fighting back-to-back against hordes of AI nasties. Would I have had even more fun if those opponents had been other players? Maybe...maybe not. But I still had a lot more fun than I would have had in a single player game.

I've Given The PvP Mess A Lot Of Thought Lately Due To The Fact That I've Been Getting Slightly Annoyed With People In Dereth. Also, When You Can't Lay A Hand On Someone, Even As A Joke (Haven't We All Wanted To Slap Our Friends Upside The Head For A Point Of Damage or Two For A Laugh?) It Takes Some Realism Out Of The Gameplay. There Are A Lot Of Ideas Out There To Work Through This, Most of Them Need Too Much Game-Changing Work To Be Done, Though. My Favorite Idea Would Be To Have The Switch In Your Settings... This Makes You Available To Be Hit By Other Players And To Hit Other Players That Are Willing To Be Hit. This Could Be Coupled With A System That Would Include Duels And Wars. There Could Be Some Code Added For You To Make A List (Like A Friends List) Of Persons You Can Fight With. This Would Make For Friendly Sparring, Heated Duels, And If You Want To be That Organized - Guild Wars. Plus, You Could Reach Over And Knife Your Friend Who Happened To Leave The Computer To Find His Notebook Full Of AC Notes..... Anyway, I'd Like To See Something Done About It. The Way We Have It Set Now Just Doesn't Seem To Fit In With The Mentality Of Most Of The People. You Don't Have To be Evil To Be A PKer... You Just Have To Be Good (At It)
