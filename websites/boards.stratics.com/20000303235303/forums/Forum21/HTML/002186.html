The only method I can think of that would eliminate the huge disparity in power between melee and casters would piss off so many casters that they'll never do it. There are 2 things they'd have to do. They'd have to make it so all mobs had a way to do significant damage to whatever was at the top of it's hate list no matter what movement effecting spells it was up against (either through ranged attacks, self affecting spells like sow or cancel magic, etc..)

They'd also have to change it so that pet damage counted against the CASTER on a mob's hate list. From what I've heard this is already the case for dragons and gods, but they'd have to globalize it to all mobs.

This would put casters in the same league as us warriors for soloing because anything nastier than high blue would kill them before they could inflict their massive damage. A PC tank would be REQUIRED to fight anything nastier than a blue and in some cases green con, just as a caster is REQUIRED to support a tank fighting anything higher than low blue. I'm talking about at high levels here not level 15 or so when anyone can solo.

A more popular method for fixing the disparity that is proposed by many tank playing people is that tanks should do more damage. This would be fine for soloing, but the consequences for group play would be ridiculous. Every mob in the game would have to be drastically upgraded to give any challenge to a group that included buffed up tanks that put out casterlike damage.

Both methods would essentially be a huge nerf to casters. The method I mentioned first should have been the state the game was released in in the first place in my opinion. There would be much more cooperation and grouping going on than there is now because it would be required. The game would be more fun because it would be more challenging and because grouping with people is just more fun regardless of whether it's less efficient.
