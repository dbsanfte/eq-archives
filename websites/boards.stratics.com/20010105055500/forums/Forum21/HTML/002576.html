I subscribe to the theory that, given relatively equivalent weapons, go with a faster weapon in the secondary too. Why? Each time the weapon delay expires, you will get a skill check to see if you can swing that weapon (DW). Over the short term, low delay = more skill checks = more swings. This should even out over the long term, though.
