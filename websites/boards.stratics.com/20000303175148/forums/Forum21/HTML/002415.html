Someone said to post more on this, from mention of it in another thread, so here goes - as best I can... With this little "trick" you can setup your jboots - and maybe other items if you don't have them, to be activated by right clicking on your health meter in the upper right hand corner of your screen. So, here's what you need to do..

1) Place your jboots in an inventory slot, NOT a pack.

2) Drag that slot icon into your hotkey(3) menu slot #3 (I'll have a cheesey diagram)

3) Now go back to hotkey(1)

4) Right click your health meter, the jboots should fire off

Hotkey Diagram (Numbered by slot):
1 2
3 4
5 6


Hope that makes sense, if not - I'll take some screen shots of it this weekend, and put up a better description this weekend.

I never bothered testing other charged items, but it might be something worth playing with. Might be able to replace the jboots with any charged item. This would probably be pretty beneficial for you PvP types out there if you can do this with pumice and such.
