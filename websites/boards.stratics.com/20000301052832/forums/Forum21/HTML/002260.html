Well Povar broke Fear sometime this weekend (I think Friday) and kept it broken atleast though the Superbowl Sunday. I have never been to Fear (I think it is was the only zone I had not been to) and though it would be neat to help out.

Well there were only 2 warriors there who did not have Amydgalan tendrils and shields. One other guy and me. We dropped a fair number of Amydgalan warriors. I get a sheild (beating the other warrior who had camped for 12 hours for a tendril with no luck). Little later we get a tendril which he gets (I already have a peice of planar gear and am happy to give it to him). THen another shield drops- he gets my. My group starts to root for me to get a whip.

On the last pull in the plane (before the next repop that is) we get an amydgalan knight. He drops SK stuff. We kill him, and low and behold he has a whip, which is now mine. So for 24 hours in Hate I have nothing, for 2 hours in Fear I have all the warrior drop stuff (except god swords ).

Amy whip: 9/25 +5 str +4 dex
Amy Shield: 20 AC +5 str, stm and dex

Yes I am a very happy camper!
