Hi there,

Here is my opinion of melee skills to learn.

You should keep all skills up.

2HB = some really nice staffs that are WIS boosting and do a lot of damage. Even though the delay is higher, it's well worth it.

1HS - I have the obsidian scimitar and a charred guardian shield. This is an AWESOME combination. It's very stylish, and the delay on the scimitar is pretty nice (I think it's like 19 or something, can't really remember off hand). However, the damage on most 1HS weapons is low (usually 5-7), so it can be kind of depressing not to hit a meanie for 19+. Still, it's handy to have this skill high, especially if you want to use the neato scimitars later on in the game.

1HB - I admit, my only experience with this skill is the screaming mace (which I LOVE!). 1HB weapons usually have a higher delay, but also higher damage than 1HS weapons, and they look fun too. You can also weild a shield with these guys.

What I typically do is trade off weapons during a fight. Since I can cast a STR buff on myself (for when I'm dungeon crawling, and collect a lot of money), I will carry around a shield, 1HB, 1HS and 2HB. This will usually consume about one backpack worth of slots, but trust me, it's worth it.

You will get skill raises off of blues, evens, yellows and reds. I have had considerable luck with evens and skill raises.

Never think that it's too late to raise your skills. Just don't try practicing a skill you're "awful" or "bad" in while raiding a dungeon, or fighting in a particularly bad spot. If you can, get a few friends together and fight some blues, and inform them that you're going to be practicing with different weapons to raise your skills. Most people should understand.

Hope that helps.

1 hand weapons are really the way to go for druids for most of the druidic life. There are just too many 1h things that can boost wisdom much more than any of the common 2 handers (Paw of Opolla, Testament of Vanear, CGS, Tainted/Ruined Battleworn Morningstar, etc). With a 1 hander, its not too tough to get 10-15 extra wisdom (up to 19 with a Paw/Testament combo) and more AC - and at high levels, your weapon damage isn't all that significant.

However, I *DO* suggest you keep 2h bashing up, even if its NOT your primary weapon. Most Godly weapons that are Druid usable are 2h bash - and you'll be kicking yourself (as well as spending a ton of money and practice points) if you get a Staff of the Earthcrafter without having used 2 hand bash in 35 levels.
