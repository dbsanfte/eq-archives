I'm enjoying my DE lady warrior tremendously, and I also like being unique. The only time I feel a twinge of jealousy towards my larger friends is in the HP department. The higher stamina and base hp of the larger races are an advantage, I think--at 33d level, I have a base of 1152 HP unbuffed; that's with 81 stamina (+6 comes from the charred BP I'm wearing until I can swing a crafted BP). And I would like slam.

Still, I love the ultravision, the look of my character, the high intelligence, and the mobility--I've never had a worse reaction from anyone than "glares threateningly" regardless of where I've been (I've never conned the Faydark elf guards, though; not sure if I'm KOS there or not). Only once have I actually been attacked by one of these glarers--the little cleric on the way into Rivervale. So we can move around fairly well.

Oh, I'm a Rallos Zek worshipper, by the way. I'd steer clear of Innoruuk when choosing your deity.

As a dark elf, I put most of my points into strength, myself, to get it up to a base 90; this seems to be about standard if you're wanting to run a class that's armor dependent. My others I put into raising my agility to 100 because at the time it was thought to raise your AC more than it evidently does. If I had it to do over, I'd probably put the points into dexterity to help a bit more with procs and critical hits.

Anyway, I currently have a modified strength of 116 when I have my LoW equiped. I don't use any stat raising items that don't also give an armor bonus (with the exception of earrings--I'm still sticking with a couple of onyx ones as I have other spending priorities at the moment). That's enough for an almost full set of crafted (not resized--never know when I might want to create a new warrior that takes medium or large), my LoW, my two one handed slashing weapons, a bow and some arrows, and a bag with 20 each of food, water and bandages. I can't carry a huge amount of loot, so I just hit the bank a little more often.
