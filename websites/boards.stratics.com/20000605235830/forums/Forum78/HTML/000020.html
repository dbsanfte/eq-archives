I started a War/Life mage based on someone's suggestion. Seems to be ok so far but dunno:

Start stats were: 40/60/10/70/80/70

Specialized in Mana Conversion, Run and Arcane Lore.

Trained in War and Life magic.

Strength seems to be perfect for me, as is Quickness because I hate running slow. The mana isn't a problem (yet) because I cast Stamina to Mana all the time and Stamina recovers really fast. Even though my coord is low I don't take that much damage because I can usually fire off 4-6 bolts before the MOB ever reaches me. If it does, I don't (yet) take a beating unless it's twice my level.

I'm sure this will change, but so far the character is lots of fun to play. There ain't much variety in War magic so far, but there's lots to play with in Life. I'm looking forward to getting Creature magic or Item magic at level 10.

My starting scores were something like:

SEL 95, FOC 95, COR 10, and a subtle balance of STR, END, and COR depending on what you're looking for.

I specialized in War, trained in Life, Mana Conversion, Arcane Lore. I trained Creature at level 9 and will train Item at level 26.

I am currently level 17 and my war skill is at 130, my Life and Creature are both at 100, my Mana Conversion 101, and my Arcane Lore 75.

I sepent quite a few points early on to bring health up to 50, stamina up to 60 and run to 90. After that I worked on mana for a long time (and still have a LONG way to go). Recently been working on Arcane Lore and boosting my skills to 80 health (with a goal of 100) stamina to 100 (with a goal of 120) and run to 130 (with a goal of 150). Currently my FOC and SEL are both at 118 and I will be pumping more points into those by my early level 20's.

If I had a chance to do it over, I might train only in war (since I use it all the time anyway) and specialize in Mana Conversion. I'd bring my FOC and SEL to 100 in the beginning. Finally I might specialize Run with the last few character points.

Hope this helps.

You can start a War/Life/Creature mage pretty easily. This is the formula I used:

STR-30
END-40
CRD-10
QIK-50
FOC-100
SLF-100

Specialized-Run
Trained-War, Life, Creature, Arcane Lore, Mana Conversion

This works well, as you can kill stuff at range using War magic, buff yourself to run faster and have high endurance using Creature magic and then use Life magic to turn STA into mana.

Fortunately the power curve decreases as you level higher due to the lack of specialization in any school of magic. If not, the game would be extraordinarily broken.

For you EQ vets out there this hybrid mage combines many of the best features of a Wizard, Shaman and Enchanter. At L1 you have good DD, a +33% Spirit of the Wolf and the equivalent (between mana regeneration and STA to mana) of a 100% Clarity. And as lot of other very disgusting stuff that I'll let you discover for yourself : )
