Check your faction with Renth in Halas (he's a barbarian in steel in a tavern). It needs to be high amiable or when you give him the murderer's head he'll just take it and give you a message about not being in good enough with the Wolves of the North.

Be careful when chasing frostbite. I've done the langseax quest over 30 times (unfortunately) and the majority of the time frosty will lead you directly through either Froon and Choon, or the ogre camp. You could outrun everything with SoW, but the ogre shaman in the camp dispelled my SoW as I was running past and needless to say, I died soon after.

If you do the quest too many times you'll become KOS with the barbarian rogues. This includes the banker in Halas. I can no longer bank there due to having done the quest so many times.

Not to be rude but I LOVE the fact that the faction requirements are so high. It keeps the light hearted from attempting the quest which keeps prices high.

I have gotten 4 LoW's so far and sold two LoW's now for 750pp each. The other two went to guildmembers. And my Druid has made just as much money selling the plain 1HS Langseax's all over the world for 30pp.

The XP simply rocks. I went from 19-24 nearly exclusively doing this quest. Paglan is blue to me at 24 and Basil still gives off XP.

Sure, I put hours and hours of gnoll teeth hunting and (shh...) doing the candle quest which uses wax from the beehive bixies in the Rivervale newbie zone (which are NOT no-drop). But, I got 1pp per jar of wax that I turned in plus the same faction adjustment as the gnoll teeth but, in less time, cause you just buy the wax from newbies or kill the bixies yourself (2nd level mobs).

Aye, you do become KoS to the banker in Halas but, eh... who cares!

If you put the time into the quest it *IS* worthwhile. But, it does take time to get that LoW. You will get 30 Basil's before you see one Paglan.
