He's L50, and basically a Guildmaster type NPC (e.g. over 20K hp, hits for 200+, jumps over tall buildings in a single bound, etc). He'd eat every HG in Rathe Mtns, pick his teeth with the Sphinx, and then come after you.

Best suggestion was above - since he gets stuck (sometimes) on the spinning rock, which won't allow him to despawn, petition a GM to come by and /kill him to get the spawn moving.

Without him in the picture, Hasten is pretty easy to force. The a_drake is definitely his placeholder, spawns every 10 minutes like clockwork, and goes from the HG area (where he spawns), down to Zaza's hill, back up to the HG, thru the Lizard camp, past the necro tower, up by the Feerrot zone, left to the spinning rock, crosses the spinning rock, then back to his original spawn point where he goes 'poof'. If the monk isn't up, so far, I've been able to force Hasten to spawn every hour to hour and a half of trying (6-7 placeholders or so).
