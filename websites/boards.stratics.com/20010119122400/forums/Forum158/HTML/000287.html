To get any item to "float" do the following:

  1. Stack logs (or any item that is one unit high) to the height you want the item. At a height of 16 units (not sure what else to call it), you can walk under the items.
  2. Remove two of the logs (or whatever you stacked).
  3. Place a body part on the stack. This must be a body part you get by carving up a human body, since they decay.
  4. Place the item you want to "float" in a small crate, then place the crate on the stack of items. (Some items can be stacked without the crate, others won't go on the stack and need to be in the crate)
  5. Smack the crate with an axe, this causes the item inside to fall on the stack.
  6. Wait for the body part to decay.
  7. Once it has decayed, lock down the item you want to "float". Then remove the logs (or whatever you stacked)

A bit involved, but you can get some neat affects.
