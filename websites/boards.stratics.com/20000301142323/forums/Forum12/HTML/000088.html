Brad has said in the past that they have opted not to do character transfers from one server to another because of game balance issues, having to deal with same names, economy changes, ETC...

But Fennin Ro is going NUTZ. Last night was over 2400 players and tonight over 2500 players, not to mention the people (including myself), who were trying to log in, but couldn't.

Unfortunately I think the Fennin Ro problem is getting worse partially because of the Christmas purchases. People are signing on for the first time and choosing the "most popular" server, not knowing that it would be in their best interest to chose a less crowded one.

The servers to my understanding were intended to serve between 1000-1500. Well upon lauch they've shown that the servers can handle up to 2000 players tolerably.... But 2500+ is getting a bit much.

The solution seems quite obvious... Split Fennin into 2 servers. Let people choose if they want to go to Fennin Tau (whatever) or stay on Fennin Ro. Allow people to transfer until there are roughly equal numbers on the servers. After such a transfer there would ideally be 1250 players on each server at peak times - something more in line with what was initally intended for server population. AND this provides some room for Fennins to continue to grow (as all of the servers have been gradually doing).

By allowing one server to split, instead of allowing people from a crowded server to transfer to a less crowded server... this completely bypasses all of Brad's reasons why not to allow transfers (something I presume legistically possible, otherwise he would have said it was not do-able in previous discussions).

Please consider this suggesion. Thanks for your time.
