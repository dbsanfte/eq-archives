Ok so Crafted isn't level 50 gear. We already knew that though. But how many of you high level warriors like me are almost all decked out in this "sub-par" gear?

The answer is probably almost all of you. Seeing as the Plane of Hate is bugged to hell, and the drops there are insanely rare compared to Fear now, what are we to do?

My suggestion would be for Verant to put in a "Crafted 2" quest.

Warriors are the class most entirely dependent on AC for our tanking abilities. I would like to see a new armor quest for high level warriors with AC less than Indicolite, but better than Crafted (Like an AC 30 BP, or AC 15-17 Arms and legs). Perhaps make them statless so that Indicolite is still an attractive reward. We need AC! The leap between Crafted and Indicolite AC is just crazily dramatic that there is no sense in not putting in something between the two.

But literally, we warriors are suffering under this sub-par Crafted armor. If there was a high level quest to get a new set of armor that didn't involve loitering around in the Plane of Hate, I bet every warrior would jump at the chance.

What do you think about this?

Alright =) I posted this under the Gameplay forum on Sony's site. It's already gotten some interesting ideas going.

I'm glad I'm not the only one who noticed the disparity between our plane armor and the "mid-level" crafted. The problem is, there really is nothing better AC-wise for a high level warrior to wear in most places until they /do/ get Indicolite now.

In some armor locations, this isn't a bad disparity. Indicolite bracers are AC 12, crafted are 10. Crafted gauntlets are AC 12, Indicolite are 16.

But in most armor locations, the jump is unacceptable. Crafted BP is AC 22, Indicolite BP is AC 35. Crafted legs, arms and helm are AC 14, 12, and 14 respectively. Indicolite legs, arms and helm are AC 20 each.

There's a lot of room for something inbetween here.
