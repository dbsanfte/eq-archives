The answers below all assume sitting regen rate. It's slower when you're standing.

1. What is "normal regen" for non trolls?

2 points per tick.

Warriors between 20th and 50th get 3 points per tick. At 50th they do 4 points per tick.


2. What is troll regen rate.

I believe it's 4 points per tick. Might be 6.


3. Does troll regen rate increase with level?

For Warriors, yes. At 20th they'll do an extra point, and at 50th they get another extra point.


4. How useful is this at higher levels? (I know it can't hurt.)

Not very, unless your primary healer is a druid, a shaman, or a paladin. If you've got a cleric, Regen doesn't do a whole lot.

It's a huge advantage at lower levels, though.

To clarify:

2. What is troll regen rate.

4hp per tick. +1 at level 20, +1 at 50, max 6hp per tick. With Rubicite breastplate at level 50 on a troll, 8hp per tick.

4. How useful is this at higher levels? (I know it can't hurt.)

I guarantee you will NOT be soloing beyond 42 unless you do some really whacked out things (see my thread: The Flip Side of Crippling Blow: Encouraging Soloing)

Also you'll usually be in a group at high levels with either a Shaman, Cleric, or Druid, who can either regen or do some serious healing.
