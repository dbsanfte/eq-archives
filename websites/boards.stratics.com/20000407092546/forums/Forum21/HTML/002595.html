I had a recent experience in one of the nastiest rooms in the game that really made me feel like throwing it all in. My warrior is a lvl 47 ogre with all the right equipment etc and im a fairly good tank. I've gone on record here before with warrior tactics and opinions on various topics and i figured that it would be nice to have your opninions.

Anyway heres the scene.
We had a full group camping the frenzied ghoul in lower guk, myself, a lvl 47 necro, lvl 48 mage, are the key players here. I had been pulling all night and we were getting along fine when bam the magician hits 49 and summons up his new pet. After 5 mins of just watching this thing smack everything we pulled we decided to try a test. The next blood thirsty ghoul i pulled we let the two pets solo, both wielding nothing more then the ol 3 dmg 23 dly fs daggers we found on the mobs there. For those that dont know blood thirsty ghouls smack hard in the hundreds time and again so we figured we'd just see how far the pets got then step in........like yeah step in....

they kicked the living s**t out of the ghoul. Myself and the lvl 50 pally in our group (the other tank) were pretty unbelieving of this both pets were blue to me at 47 but the earth pet of the mage had over 2000hp's of that i am sure.

Not content to be belittled by two pets wailing upon one of the elite mobs you will find in norrath we tried it with the actual one the only named spawn the frenzied ghoul.....I remember one necro and one magician sitting in a corner giggling their nuts off as their two pets destroyed it without any other member of the party raising a finger.

Now i understand my role in a party and my worth as a taunting masheen and the skills i bring to the table but i worked literally hundreds of hours to get my character to where he is now, and it made me feel like a POS to see 2 guys friends of mine BTW who could summon a pet stronger more efficient and able to take more damage then i can after working my guts out for all that time.

Its a whine people it wont stop me playing and yes i can do things that the pets cant but bear this in mind neither the necro or the magician had to lift a finger or cast a spell the entire battle. I left the room soon after not disgusted with my friends but despondant that after all the claims of balance and gameplay improvement such a blatant and obvious thing would be allowed to occur. The group in there spent the rest of the night pulling to room and just sitting down while pets worked the frogs over. I could not be a part of it it literally made me sick
