Just when you think you know everything about being a warrior and tanking. I got this tip from a high level warrior.

Carry your own jaspers and peridots!! For those of you that don't know, these are the material components of the cleric symbol series pizarn and naltron. Jaspers are approximately 1 pp, while peridots are about 10-11 pp depending on your charisma. Although the peridots are pretty expensive, i regularly give all the plat on my body for a 49 cleric res (usually 20-80 plat). Compared to that, the 10 plat for a peridot isn't that expensive. I'd only request the naltron series if we're breaking a particularly dangerous room (like the king room in lower).

Your cleric will thank you.

And to those of you that knew this already... please excuse my denseness. Being a shorty i get bopped on the head a lot. Luckily, my head is my strongest body part (and one of the largest) ;p

I keep 20 jaspers and 20 periodots on me at all times. Here is another solution if you are in a stable group going off to camp something. Ask ahead of time if it is ok for the cleric/paladin/mage to be compensated for the stones used before the loot is split. We try to do this as much as possible. Just get the cleric to keep up with how many he/she uses, and give them that money before the loot is split. Works great!

I usually team with the same clerics, and generally make regular donations to the "rock fund" so that I don't have to delve into my stash much.

Ever since I found out these spells take components I always make sure to have some with me!!! You should ALWAYS carry a nice hefty supply of Cat's Eye Agate (20's), Bloodstone (high 20 - low 30), Jasper (mid 30 - 40) and Peridot (40+). (Choose the two that bracket your level.

Not only are these used for the Cleric HP line of buffing...they are the component for casters Shield Skin line as well Enchanters Rune Series. You never know when a party member will be in need, and there is nothing better for breaking a tough spawn than a Rune III on a few tanks =) And casters love you when they run out of components for their shield skin line...watch their eyes light up when you mention you have a few extra gemstones.

It is always nice as well to have some extra bat wings and fish scales since you never know when you might need a levitate or enduring breath =)

This is excellent advice. I usually play with the same people so all of us tanks carry the stones needed at all times if we can and our cleric has but to say the word to be flooded with them. It always feels weird noe being in a pickup group and not getting symbols before tough fights.

Also this has helped in one thing: too many coins. I'm sure everyone has left a dungeon incredibly overloaded with silver and gold. I usually destroy the copper but save the silver and gold if it can be helped - then head to the nearest gem store (my favorite for this is C-T with the store so close by in Rathe mnts) and stock up on gems for those useless coins. This has proven especially invaluable for giant killing in rathe since I almost never have sow for pulling the giants and need to be as unencumbered as is possible for pulling.

We usually have symbols for all tanks on all but the easiest fights and I'm sure my group has won a lot of fights we couldn't have done without those buffs. It may seem expensive but when you can kill faster and more effectively I believe it's quite worth it. Since most of us also carry at least 20 fish scales and bat wings always we're ready to go anyplace at anytime without having to go find a place that sells spell regs :-) And it's true, clerics really do love it when tanks think of these things since clerics are poor for a looong time with both armor and spell buying.
