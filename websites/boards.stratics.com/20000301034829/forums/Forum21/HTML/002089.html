The Avenger Axe is a very good weapon for your level. I also believe that it's easier to get than the LoW in my opinion.

Here are a few tips that may help you...

1) Faldor spawns about every 2hr 26min. There is a trick to this though. Both Faldor and a beggar will spawn in the room. The beggar will spawn 26min AFTER Faldor. So if you catch the beggar in the room you have about 2 hours to wait for Faldor.

2) You cannot camp Faldor inside the bar. I've waited over 10 hours on two different occasions inside the bar and he never showed. Wait outside the bar near the pond and check every few minutes for him. I've successfully camped him 4 times and this has held true every time.

Other than that it's a pretty easy quest. After a friendly warrior told me those two tips I have done the quest four times solo between levels 23-24. If you're lower than this you might want the help of one friend.

One final note is that the exp for this quest is great. Not counting the exp from killing Slaythe and Faldor you get almost one full bubble of exp for the quest. If you have good friends that can TP you between NK and Faydwer (I do) you can amass a good bit of exp rather quickly if you're in your low 20's.

This info has worked for me. Hope it helps!
