A good place that I have seen to get silk is from Giant Spiders in West Commons. I know it is a long ways from Fay, but I stood at the toll guard booth thing there and with in about 10 min, I saw 7 or 8 Giant spiders stroll bye. They con Blue, and yellow to me at level 10. Only thing you have to watch out for what is spawning around you, because it can be a tough zone. Also sometimes the GS like to gang up, but it's good exp. and you get silk too. Just know were the guard is,and what's going on around you.

I spend all day killing spiders for silk. Still only have tailoring to 36, but I've made enough heady kiolas to last me a long time and I work on it constantly. If I have all day I MIGHT be able to get my skill up 10 points. THis is the most tedious skill I have ever had to raise.

The best place for spiders, outside of Guk, is East Karana. At your level I'm not sure I would recommend it, but when you get bigger or have a group, crag spiders and carrion spiders and all can sometimes have 4 silks on them. Just watch out for those cyclopses and Hill Giants.

I've done the spiders on Faydwer, but there simply aren't enough of them.
