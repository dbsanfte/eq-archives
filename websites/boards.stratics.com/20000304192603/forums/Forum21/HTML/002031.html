Cyndi in Mac's Kilts is looking for the white polar bear cub skins. She'll give you gold and patchwork (large) for these.

Karg is looking for white polar bear skins. Give him 5pp and a white polar bear skin and he'll make you a Polar Bear Cloak (AC5, SV Cold 15).

You'll need to be about L10 or 11 in decent equipment before you can solo a polar bear. They give good XP though and you can resell the PB Cloak for up to 15pp so if you're willing to become a haberdasher you can make decent money doing this.

I've played three Barbs at this point (Warrior, Shaman and Rogue) and I honestly think you'll make levels faster and significantly more money fighting in Blackburrow but it might be interesting to try to get L30 without ever travelling south of Everfrost. You could do this in EF and Permafrost I think.
