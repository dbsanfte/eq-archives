You dismiss the Unicorn shield too quickly. It has great AC and an addequate damage/delay. When run through a spreadsheet using the newish damage formulas (see other thread), as and offhand weapon the SoSU clearly is adequate at high levels and the AC makes it one of the best off-handers in the game, if only because offhanders add so little to a fight compared to what I think you think they are.

Here is how I've set up a spreadsheet to find the best combinations in terms of damage per second.

Column A: Weapon Name
Column B: Damage
Column C: Delay
Column D: The Warriors level (it makes a difference, if you play with this numer you will see that different weapons will swap importance as you gain level)
Column E: ((2*B)+((D-25)/3))/(C/10)
Column F: .42*(2*B)

Column E is your modal damage per second in your primary hand (or for a 2-hander)

Column F is the damage per second for your off hand which receives no level bonus and has a modifier because it doesn't swing as often. .42 seems to be a good number and came from someone around my level (40), it may be higher or lower, but I think it's good.

Now to figure your damage over time, just add your column E results for your primary weapon to your column F results for your off weapon. You will see that your primary weapon is the lions share of your damage and that anything around the 1/4 damage delay is all about the same in your off hand. The little bit of difference between at 6/23 and a 10/40 is practically negligable, and the AC is a respectable bonus. In fact there wasn't anything you could put in your offhand, including an EBW that would make up for the AC of the SoSU.

This of course doesn't count for Proc'ing weapons. A proc'ing SSoY may be better in your off hand than a SoSU because of the Proc', but it really depends on how often it goes off vs. how much the extra AC of the SoSU protects. Also, you'd be nuts not to put the SoSU in your Primary hand if you only have 1 and it's your best weapon.

I think the best combination over level 40 (and it gets better as you go up) is an EBW in your primary and a SSoY in your off hand. That little boost in speed of the EBW makes a bigger and bigger difference as your level-bonus increases.

My 2 cents.

The little bit of difference between at 6/23 and a 10/40 is practically negligable

Um.

This is due to the fact that they have almost the same DMG/DLY ratio: 0.260 for the Langseax vs. 0.250 for the SoSU.

I agree that the SoSU is worthless unless you're twinking. If you're not twinking (I'm completely untwinked), then by the time you're of sufficient level to get one for yourself, you're going to be wanting a weapon with a bit more punch than one with a damage ratio of 1/4.

Remember, the DMG/DLY ratio of 1-handers approaches 0.333 (1/3rd) for non-planes items as you get better and better weapons. By the time you're in your mid-30s, the Langseax is going to be getting a bit long in the tooth and you're going to want something with a bit more oomph than a DMG/DLY of 0.250.

But for twinking, it's perfectly fine.
