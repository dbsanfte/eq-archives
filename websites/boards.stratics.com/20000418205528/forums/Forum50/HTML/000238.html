You don't actually have to kill the brownies yourself. Rangers need arrows dropped by the brownies for their quest armour. If you get lucky and find a ranger hunting down the brownie outcasts, just ask nicely if you can have the brownie parts. In fact, you should bring something to trade for forage too. The ranger will be there for quite a while.

Rangers need six different arrows from the brownie outcasts. That means a lot of dead brownies. Unfortunately not very many brownies will have meat
