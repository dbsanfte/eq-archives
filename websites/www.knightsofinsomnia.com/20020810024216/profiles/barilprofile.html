"As I travel throught the frozen wold of the tundra that I like to call my homeland... the beast that I come across and the spells and the potions I make, inspire the heart of a Knight to Be Just and Fear Not. I would like to see our guild a powerful and mighty guild, with a close family feel, with people waiting to join us and be part of the Great Knights of Insomnia. A guild where we help and greet each other with the respect we deserve. Remember, Knights of Insomnia is not just a guild tag, it's US."

As Field Marshall, Baril is one of three leaders of the Knights of Insomnia. Knights of Insomnia Field Marshalls are there to enforce the rules of the guild charter, uphold the goals and ideals of the guild and serve as the backbone of leadership.

As Guild Leader, Baril reserves the respect of a great leader and holds in his power the fate of the guild. Only the Guild Leader may truly command his army, promote officers, initiate guild alliances, wars and truces.
