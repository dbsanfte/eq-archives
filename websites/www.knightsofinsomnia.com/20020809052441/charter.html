Our Charter

 

Mission Statement

Knights of Insomnia has been established in order to provide a support system that shall not only sustain its members, but also to reach out to all of those in Norrath who are in need. It is our goal as the Knights of Insomnia to be a positive and protective society within the expanse that is Norrath; furthermore, to be of service to any and all who reach out to us. As a Knight, members are required to abide by the rules of the council of Officers and Guild leader as listed below. Please note that any and all rules are subject to change as our guild matures and develops. Please visit the Guild Forum Boards or attend guild meetings as posted on our homepage to have the opportunity to give your officers and leader any input you might have concerning our charter or anything else.

All Knights are expected to be familiar with the Articles of the Guild Charter that pertain to them. This Charter is posted so everyone may view it freely; ignorance of the Charter is not an excuse for violation of its terms.


THE ARTICLES
Entrance into, Membership, and Removal from the Knights of Insomnia.

Any character, regardless of race or class, having attained their 15th level or previously joined KoI with an alternate character is eligible for consideration for invitation. Prospective members are required to apply for membership by submitting an application through our guild website or by clicking here.

Former members who were in good standing when they departed will always be eligible for readmission. Former members who were not in good standing when they departed will be allowed to re-enter only after a vote of the officers. The vote shall be conducted by each rank of officers, with a majority vote needed by each rank for reinstatement.

All Knights are expected to follow the rules and guidelines as stated in this charter. Any Knight, regardless of rank, is subject to probation and/or removal if they do not comply with our charter.

 

Chain of Command

Knights of Insomnia has three ranks of leadership: Field Marshall, General and Commander. The order of seniority is as follows- Guild Leader (also a Field Marshall), the remaining Field Marshalls, Generals, then Commanders. For a list of our officers please click here.

In some situations an alternate of an officer may also have the commands required to perform officer's tasks. In this case, the alternate officer is referred to as an Officer Lietenant. Officer Lietenant's have the same authority given to their main character in instances where they must make a decision as an officer. In the event an officer's vote is needed, only one character per person may place a vote.

There shall be a set number of officers per rank. Currently the restrictions are 3 Field Marshalls, 8 General's and 6 Commanders. Field Marshall positions may only be elected by our Guild Leader Baril Barontundra. Generals may be elected by a unanimous decision of Field Marshall's. Commanders may be elected by majority vote of Field Marshall's and Generals in attendance of said vote. Once an officer is elected, he or she may chose to accept or decline an offer for promotion.

However, if an officer of Knights of Insomnia resigns from their position as an officer without prior notice to our officers, they may be placed on probationary status for a period of up to 30 days during which time they will not be eligible for promotion within the guild.Should a vacancy arise in any rank, the position will be filled from the next lowest rank in the voting method described above.

 

Guild Positions

Guild positions are non-officer posts that are given to or volunteered by individual persons as needed. Any Knight may hold a guild position, regardless of rank. In a voting situation, a member holding a guild position will vote with the authority of their default status or rank within the guild. An example of a guild position is Guild Historian or Webmistress.

Knights of Insomnia's Code of Honor
How a Knight conducts Himself in and out of Norrath

All Knights are to refrain from profanity, name-calling and all other non-roleplaying outbursts on public channels. Public channels include, but are not limited to /shout, /ooc, /auction, guildchat, independent chat channels and on the forum boards.

All Knights are to respect the Chain of Command.

Knights of Insomnia members are encouraged to help those in need, especially other Knights if in the area and available. Unreasonable requests including constantly begging for ports, items, pp and powerleveling. Such behavior will not be tolerated and may lead to a member being placed on probation or removed from the guild.

Any Knight found to be intentionally killstealing (as officially defined and stated in EQ policy) shall be immediately placed on probationary status and could face removal from the guild following proper removal protocol.

Roleplaying in public chat channels is strongly encouraged.

 

Discipline

In the instance a member of Knights of Insomnia has been determined to have violated the charter in any way, they may be placed on probation by any officer. At such time, the officer will be expected to notify the member via /tell of their violation and status. Once a member has been notified of their probationary status, the officer responsible for the decision has sole discretion over whether or not they feel the need to announce the probation in guild chat. Regardless of this decision, it is this officers responsibilty to post the decision on the officers board located on our guild forum boards, contact other officers online and make a report to our Guild Historian. Each instance of probation will be documented as a Guild Incident by our Historian for future reference.

The default amount of time for probation is 30 days, although if the instance is severe a vote may be cast for a members removal before this probationary period has ended. Any Knight with a probationary status mark on their record will not be eligible for promotion or other positive guild advancement until the mark is "expired" or the probationary status period has ended or been terminated. Once a mark has expired, the incident is forgiven and does not count against a member for future promotion or advancement. Should a member require more than one mark in any given time, or repeatedly require marks for the same infraction, they will be subject to removal.

A decision to remove a member of Knights of Insomnia must be made during an officers meeting and requires a vote. A Field Marshall must be present to make the final decision of removal. Should a Field Marshall be unavailable, the decision must wait until one is available. Officers meetings shall take place in a private chat channel created for this purpose. Not all officers must be in physical attendance to vote, should they have access to this private chat channel. In some instances a vote may be conducted on the officers forum boards.

Removal of a member may only be performed by a Field Marshall. Should an officer remove a member without following proper protocol, they will immediately be placed on probation.

Ammending our Charter

Any Knight may call for an ammendment or repeal to any part of the charter at any time. Should a member decide to apply for an ammendment or repeal, they must provide the exact wording for presentation to our officers. Once such wording has been reviewed, a 2/3 vote of all members is required to enact the ammendment. All votes may take place in public channels in Norrath, on the Forum Boards or via our website. Documentation of all meetings, votes and decisions must be made to our Guild Historian.

 

KoI Loot Code
During raids, it is important each Knight understand our loot code. Our policy is as follows:

  • All items of worth shall be rolled on.
  • If a member of our guild needs a specific item for quest or epic, that item is defaulted to the member in need automatically.
  • If more that one member is in need of the same item, they shall roll between themselves for the item.
  • Remember, need before greed... and we are here to help each other out!
