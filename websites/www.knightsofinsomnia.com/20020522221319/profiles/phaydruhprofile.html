Phaydruh Phylicity

As Phaydruh prepares for her upcoming wedding to Baron Granmorte Commes, it is clear to all around her that she is joyous finally, after what has been a trying and heartbreaking year. Despite her losses, because of her triumphs- she has become tempered against injustice, strengthened for future hardships.
