Nerull the Reaper

Bringer of Darkness

Greater God: God of Death, Darkness, the Underworld, Evil, Covert Activity

Holy Symbol: Skull and Scythe on black field

History:

Nerull is rarely worshipped in the open in dwarven society. As such, it is hard to tell how many worshippers he has but, most tend to be thieves, assassins and murderers. His clergy can call up many undead, are adept at necromancy spells (both clerical and wizardly) and can become liches if they attain enough power. Of all the dwarven gods, only Abbathor is on good terms with Nerull and even that is strained as best. Cults of Nerull are hunted down with great dedication among the dwarves and they place dwarven worshippers at the highest priority to kill due to their "corruption" of the dwarven spirit and way of life.
