Charter of the Order of the Mithril Warhammers

CHARTER

The Order of the Mithril Warhammers
 

WE, THE MEMBERS OF THE MITHRIL WARHAMMERS, being determined to keep our goodly ways and traditions alive for our children and the good races of Norrath, to reaffirm faith in the power of the Light over the Darkness, in the dignity and worth of good people regardless of race, in the equal rights of male and female and of groups both large and small, to establish conditions under which justice and respect for the obligations arising from treaties and other sources of  law can be maintained and to promote better standards of life in freedom have formed The Order of the Mithril Warhammers.

To these ends we pledge to practice tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbors, to unite our strengths to maintain peace and security to all and to ensure, by the acceptance of these principles and methods, that armed force shall not be used, save in the common interest of that which is just and right.

We have resolved to combine our efforts to accomplish these goals this day in the city of Kaladim. May the Light guide and protect us all in all our journeys.
 

 PURPOSES AND PRINCIPLES

Article 1

1. Maintain peace and security. To that end, we take collective measures for the prevention and removal of threats to that peace.

2. Develop friendly relations among clans/guilds based on respect for the principles of the Mithril Warhammers.

3. Be a force of the Light as shown by the actions of members in the attainment of these common goals.

Article 2

The Order and its members, in pursuit of the purposes stated in Article 1, shall act in accordance with the following principles.

1. The Order is based on the principle of the sovereign equality of all good races.

2. All members, in order to ensure to all the rights and benefits resulting from membership, shall fulfill in good faith the obligations assumed by them in accordance with this Charter.

3. All members shall settle their disputes by peaceful means in such a manner that peace, security and justice are not endangered.

4. All members shall refrain in their relations from the threat or use of force against the independence of any group or in any other manner inconsistent with the purposes of the Order of the Mithril Warhammers.

5. All members shall give the Order every assistance in any action it takes in accordance with this charter and shall refrain from giving assistance to any clan, guild or persons against which the Order is taking preventive or enforcement action.

MEMBERSHIP

Article 3

Membership in the Order is by invitation only. In order to apply, one must first be sponsored by a current member of the Order. Then, one must pass the tests of Friendship, Honor and Goodness as witnessed by a member of this Order. Finally, one must read, accept and sign this Charter as their personal law and live accordingly. Failure in any or all of these things are grounds for disciplinary actions as deemed nessasary by the leadership of the Order up to and including removal of the offending person from the Order.
 
POSITIONS

Article 4

The leadership of the Order is as follows:

Chief Elder (1)
Mithril Council member (4 plus Chief Elder)
Minister of Trade (1)
War Council (3)
Keeper of Records (1)
Ambassador (as needed)

No two positions my be held by the same person at the same time.

 Positions may be join occupied should the current occupant be unable to fulfill their responsibilities until a replacement can be found.

Additionally, the member must have attained a minimum rank before filling the position. Ranks needed for a position is as follows:

Chief Elder (Rik Drungi)
Mithril Council (Orc Krami)
Minister of Trade (Hazkul Grobkuli)
War Council (Hazkul Grobkuli)
Keeper of Records (Ock Kaldari)
Ambassador (Ock Kaldari)

To apply for a position, a member must contact the Chief Elder or a member of the Mithril Council and be seconded by a member of the Order.

POWERS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

Article 5

Chief Elder:

This position is the head of the Order. There is no higher authority save as listed below. The Chief Elder’s words are considered to be Order law.

Mithril Council:

This group along with the Chief Elder dictate the policy of the Order. The Chief Elder can over rule (Impress) Council decisions at will. However, if three (3) of the members of the council vote to Impede the Impress then the council shall hold sway.

Additionally, this body votes on any and all treaties, disciplinary and membership issues by majority vote.

Minister of Trade:

This position holds the responsibility of obtaining and maintaining of trade treaties, collection of guild dues, storage of all coinage for the Order and recording the trade skills of members and their current proficiency.

Council of War:

This group has been charged with obtaining and maintaining military treaties, collection of armor and arms for the Order and storage of same. Additionally, they will keep a record of each member’s primary choice of weapon type and give wise council to the Chief Elder in times of war.

Keeper of Records:

This person will keep the minutes of any and all meetings in addition to obtaining a copy of the lists kept by the Minister of Trade and the Council of War. These will be sent to the Chief Elder on a bi-weekly basis for his review. Finally, the Keeper of Records will be responsible for keeping the member rosters with the Name, Race, Class and Email of each member. These will be sent to the Chief Elder once per week.

Ambassador:

This is the contact position for a group the Order either desires to have a treaty or interactions with. The primary responsibility of this position is to attend meetings of the group and carry out dialog between the Mithril Council and that group’s leadership.

***
 

IN FAITH, I have freely agreed to and signed this Charter and all parts therein. And shall fulfill to the best of my abilities the goals and purposes of The Order of the Mithril Warhammers.
