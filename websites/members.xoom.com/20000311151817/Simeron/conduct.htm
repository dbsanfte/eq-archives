The Warhammer Code of Conduct

To be a Mithril Warhammer is a special thing. Only the finest and best examples of each race are allowed this title. While this makes our numbers small, it also makes us strong. We share a kinship with each other that will keep us alive in the dire times ahead. On the stone table in the Shrine of Light is our code. I will tell you in more plain words here what it means.

We, the Warhammers, serve the Light in all our ways, with all our life. We stand as a beacon in the darkness of the night, shining as hope burns, telling that someone keeps watch. We stand against the Darkness which grows with the passing of time, as Guardians and Protectors of the good races of Norrath. We are unfailing in our Justice and strive to keep our Honor clean. Our Loyalty to one another and the Light is renown in the land and our Courage is well known. But most of all, our Spirit is strong. Stronger then the Darkness which surrounds us. Let our Light shine out even brighter in these dark times.

We aid the good races of Norrath in many small ways, such as a helpful heal or shielding spell and possibly giving of a small item to someone in need. We do not give money or items to anyone that begs under any circumstances. Instead, tell them you will help them for a fight or two and then go along on your way if you have time and desire to do so.

We follow the way of Light at all times. Spirit is the base of this. Always keep your guild and guildmates dear to your heart. Upon this rests Loyalty and Honor, which are the walls of our fortress. They come from your spirit and will serve you well in life. Finally, from those come Justice and Courage which will make your name known throughout the lands of Norrath. One spoken with pride by those who call you friend and fear from those that name you an enemy.

To promote the kinship within the Order and to better be prepared to serve the Light, there shall be Strike Teams created. These Strike Teams will be used for hunting the more powerful evil minions of the Darkness and in times of war, against our enemies. PvP training will be on Sunday afternoon before and after our hunt.

Makeup of the strike teams will be as follows:

Ork Krami or higher for leader
Minimum two Hazkul Grobkuli
Lower Ranks to fill in as needed.

Each strike team should consist of the following members:

Two "Tanks"
One "Medic"
One "Nuker"

I would recommend adding one healer then one more tank or nuker to round out the full six member team but, this basic set up will serve you well starting about level five to seven.

Promotions are loosely based on two things, availability of positions and personal combat ability mixed with loyalty and devotion to our guild. If you show yourself willing to devote not only game time but real world time to make the Warhammers special, you will recieve promotions. As of right now, the highest level of promotion is Ork Krami. Here is the breakdown of the  ranks.

One Rik Drungi for the entire Order.
One Lok Trommi for the entire Order.
One Ork Krami per strike team (4 to 6 members).
Hazkul Grobkuli and lower are unlimited.

Donation of coin and items to the Warhammers are gratfully accepted. These will be of use in setting up new members who have proved themselves worthy of the title of Hazkul Grobkuli or higher with suitable weapons, armor and spells. It also will provide for a common store of food and drink along with a reserve of coin for trading and funds to make our guild hall the grandest in all of Norrath. Only rare, special or expensive (5pp or higher) items need to be donated and only gold or platinum coins. Contact an officer or myself to donate.

I need from all members these things to be sent to either my email address in the study or Cestus's email on the Roster:

Character Pic
Character Bio
Character Name (Full name)
Character race
Character class

Lastly, I will be having many small quests and special events for the Warhammers as I can. The more members and help we have, the more of these will be possible.
