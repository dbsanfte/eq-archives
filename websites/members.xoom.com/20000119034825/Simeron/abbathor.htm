Abbathor

Great Master of Greed

Lesser God: God of Greed, Evil, Spite

Holy Symbol: Jewelled Dagger

History:

Abbathor is the god of thieves and those that serve evil. Swindlers, cutpurses, rouges and the like are the normal worshippers of Abbathor. Abbathor opposes Dumathoin and Berronar, usually secretly. Many priests of Abbathor are more thief than priest and it is not uncommon for the leader of the thieves guild and/or assassin guild to be a worshipper of Abbathor. Worship of Abbathor is not normally accepted in dwarven society but, not unheard of.
