The Warhammer Arena

You hear four long horn blasts as you enter the arena and see Warhammers paired off for combat training. Amid the crash and clang of metal upon metal, you see older, more seasoned veterans called Long Beards, training and advising the younger warriors. While visiting Warhammer Hall, the visitors may spar and practice with each other here to keep their battle prowess and skills in top form. The Warhammers have a wide array of arms and armor on display here.
