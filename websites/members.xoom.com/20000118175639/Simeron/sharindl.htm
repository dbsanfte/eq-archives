Sharindlar

The Lady of Life

Lesser goddess: Healing and Mercy.

Holy Symbol: A flame rising from a steel needle.

History:

Sharindlar works with Berronar as the goddess of courtship. Her worshippers tend to be healers and alchemists mainly, at least those that seek to aid people against injury and disease. Her clergy are renown for their abilities with the healing magics and few dwarven strongholds do not have at least some of her clergy within their walls.
