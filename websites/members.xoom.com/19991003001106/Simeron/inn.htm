Welcome to the Rusty Steel Tavern

There are quite a few patrons in the tavern right now, but that isn't unusual given the time of day. A minstrel group plays a lively tune while fresh loaves of multi-grained breads, meats of all kinds, cheeses, eggs and many strange foods and drinks are being served. A group of the Warhammers sit by the hearth and sing of fine orc hunts, the glory of honorable victory and the great warriors of the past and now. The inn keeper brings you some fresh bread, a piece of cheese and a large mug of Dwarven Cider. She tells you Lord Simeron has said all travellers may stay for the night in the guest room in the upstairs of Warhammer Hall. She also points to some bulletin boards in the corner where you can leave messages to the Warhammers and to the Mithril Alliance. Also, a newsletter is posted on the board of events and happenings.
