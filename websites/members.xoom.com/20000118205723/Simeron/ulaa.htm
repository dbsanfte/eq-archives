Ulaa

Lady of the Hills

Greater Goddess: Goddess of Hills, Mountains, Gemstones

Holy Symbol: Mountain with a ruby heart

History:

 Ulaa is the god of nature for the dwarves. Shrines and churches must be in hilly or mountainous regions and be roofed or underground. Services involve low, resonant chanting and the rhythmic pounding of hammers on stone (with careful care to keep the sounds pleasing and musical). Ulaa is one of the elder gods. She is thought to possibly be one of the First Dwarves. Ulaa is most commonly worshipped by Sundered Dwarf tribes that live above ground mostly and farmers. Her worshipers do not honor Moradin though this does not appear to concern Moradin however, as the two gods are on good (if not amicable) terms with each other. Priests of Ulaa are only rarely adventurers, preferring to stay at their homes and protect them. Her worshippers are most commonly found in areas with significant humanoid and giant threats. Her priests are expect to lead their people into battle (when necessary) and to preserve the faith in the face of adversity.
