Age of Empires and its expansion pack The Rise of Rome are computer games developed by Ensemble Studios.  The theme of Age of Empires is the rise of the first great civilizations over the 12,000 years that followed the ice age.  Your goal is to build your tribe into a mighty civilization that can vie for world dominance.  As you advance through the ages by exploring and gathering resources with your villagers, you can build new buildings, research new   technologies, and create military units to conquer your opponents.  Age of Empires is a Real Time Strategy (RTS) game.  All rights reserved.
