Lord Simeron Steelhammer

This is me history and who I am. I was born to a noble family in the Misty Mountain Clan. Me father, Fulgar Ironhouse, was a powerful trader within the clan. He regularly took me mother, meself and me younger brother into the trading village near our caverns. We would pack up metal ingots, weapons, armor and crafts from our shops to sell at the town and then buy furs, scented oils, feathers and various foodstuffs from the town for resale once we got home. I was apprenticed to a weapon smith for several winters so that I would have a better knowledge about metals and weapons and to get accustomed to humans and their ways. However, trading is in me blood and Dad made quite a bit of gold at it so, I returned to him fairly quick. He taught me the value of knowing how to read a person. I'm not as good as me father at it but, I'm a good trader now too. I think the greatest thing me father taught me was how to read and write in both dwarven and human. It helps ever so much when reading contracts.

Early on, I knew me life's path was pretty much mapped out for me. Clan tradition said that I must join the army once me beard touched me chest. This came to pass in me thirty-second winter. A bit young to be sure but, I grew fast. Thus, I left home to become a dwarven infantryman in King Wintergreen's army. However, as fate would have it, I was directed by the Longbeard to a new branch of service, the mounted scouts. Here, I learned many a hard lesson, and I got the scars to prove it. If anyone ever tells you that mountain goats are easy to ride, tell them I said they're a liar. Goliath, my goat, was as temperamental as they come but, I was just a bit more stubborn than he was so, we have come to a mutual understanding. I ride his back and he gets fed at least twice a day. After I completed me training, I was then entered into the advanced courses where I learned mounted as well as ground combat, studied stone fortifications and the rocks themselves so that I would be a better scout, how to set snares for food and defense and the best ways to tie and untie ropes on people and things. Me father had a special graduation gift for me when I finished me training, DwarGlum, the Vermin Bane. A rune covered dwarven hammer of exquisite make and fantastic power. It is because of this that I dedicated me life to learning the dwarven hammer better than anyone else. I use DwarGlum in preference to my battle axe or crossbow whenever I can and am quite deadly with it when I need to be.

Me holy life is simple. I pray to both Mordain and Clangeddin every day for wisdom  in life and guidance in battle. I follow the Dwarven Way and live by a code few can follow. I believe that how someone lives is just as important as how someone dies. I also don't believe in wasting resources or lives in any case. Through good tactics and training, both will last long and through many fights. I have a very strong hatred of ogres and take great pleasure in knee capping and nut cracking them with me hammer. Nothing is as funny to me as seeing one of them lumbering hulks trying to hold his groin and run with two busted knees.

I have traveled far from my home now and seen many worlds. Some were beautiful while others left much to be desired. It was in those travels that I came across a fellow dwarf named NemesisKnight. He took me to his small, but growing clan and introduced me to Dergan the Foe hammer, chieftain of the clan of Odin's Hammer. After a brief discussion and alot of fine dwarven ale, I decided to settle in with them for a few weeks before moving on in me journey. That "few weeks" became many a winter and I came to command me own warband within the clan itself and sit on the Council of Elders. We have since set out on our own as is the Way of Odin's Hammer Clan. I have molded and shaped me warriors into the best fighting force any army could ever want for. Know that when we take the field, evil will tremble with fear at our approach and die by our hands. A greater friend you cannot have nor a worse enemy find. We are the best of the best, slayers without equal. We are the Mithril Warhammers.
