OUR CODE OF CONDUCT




    		•

    We all may have the same goals in Norrath; to achieve what is laid before us, Quests, seasons, and FUN!!


    However, there are other reasons to be here and those are what we as a Guild look for in prospective members.


    1. Honor - to watch out for those around you, to stand to the bitter end and YES, even death. Such things happen when in honorable battle.


    2. Justice - this may take many forms, such as protecting those weaker than you from others who may try to exploit them, escorting a younger adventurer thru a dangerous new land, or defending a small band from a train dumped on them by an inscrupulous warrior, or providing any special services (such as bindings, enchanting metals, etc) at a fair and reasonable fee, in a timely manner.


    3. Loyalty - to sum this up, friendship. To stand beside your friends to the bitter end, whilst knowing they will be standing beside you; for this is what friends do for one another. And who knows, maybe you will live because you fought rather than ran away. Not that we live to die; if we see that the odds are to great we will flee only to fight another day!!


    4. To always have someone to adventure with, no matter what circle you may have achieved in your travels, and to have assistance, willing and able, to help you achieve a goal that is still slightly out of your reach.
