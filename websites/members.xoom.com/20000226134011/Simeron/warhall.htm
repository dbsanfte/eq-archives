Parlor of Warhammer Hall

You have entered the Hall of the Mithril Warhammers.There is a group of musicians playing in the parlor while around you, members are busy doing various tasks. You can tell right away that this is a Dwarven Hall. The tables and chairs are of the sturdiest wood and a good stock of ale and mead is readily available. Upstairs holds several doors, a study with books and chessboards to the right and a trophy room to the left. A rune covered, stone pillar near the stairway has many fine gems set around a golden plaque.
