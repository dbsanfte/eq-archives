Becoming an Initiate of the Amber Order

1) ONE member needs to nominate the person, and ONE Officer needs to evaluate that person.

2) TWO officers must agree on the admission of any new members.

3) Individuals playing characters who are normally "evil" in game will be required to have the approval of all officers, and will be required to verbally admit to the officers that the chaotic ways of their brethren are detrimental to the Amber Order's goal of the spread of knowledge. Furthermore, they must promise not to pursue such dark goals themselves.



Initiate testing period
(policy developed by Mordrick Stoneshadow)

1) The period during which members are "Initiates" will serve as an evaluation period of the potential scholar. This means all scholars and above should make every attempt to interact with the initiate and get to
know him/her as much as possible.

2) The normal initiation period will be 14 three hour play sessions or two weeks, whichever is greater.

3) When the initiation period has passed, initiates who show the characteristics desired by the Amber Order will be admitted.

4) If any member of the Amber Order has some reason to doubt an initiate, they should report this to the officers via email. Depending on the severity of this report, the initiate will either be immediately disbanded or will be required to undergo an additional initiation period during which the officers will more carefully scrutinize the initiate.

5) If at any point during this process an officer has due reason to doubt the behavior of one of these initiates, they can disband the initiate from the guild at that point.
