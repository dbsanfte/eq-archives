The Autumn leaves crunch under the hooves of your mount and the chirp of birds can be
heard over the crisp wind blowing through the barren trees. Winter is approaching and you see squirrels gathering the last of the nuts and berries they will need for the long snow-filled months to come. Ahead, on a hill, you see Castle Steelhammer. Within lies Warhammer Hall and a good nights rest. You can smell the aged brews, fresh breads and smoked meats the dwarves are preparing along with the sounds of smithing. As you draw near, the deep, throaty sounds of dwarven voices raised in song mixes with the sounds of hammer and anvil. They sing of battle, smithing and thier history with a pride renown in The Seven Nations. As you come to the gates, a patrol of Mithral Warhammers passes you by, thier hammers and armor gleaming in the morning sun. Seeing these mighty slayers, you know why the area around the castle is free of those that would do evil. You are met at the gatehouse by a young dwarf, who takes your mount to the stable. Upon entering the courtyard within, a dwarf clad in shining mithril armor bids you a hearty Well Met. Hanging on his side is a warhammer of exquisite make engraved with many Dwarven Runes of Power. This can only be DwarGlum the Vermin-Bane which means this must be Lord Simeron Steelhammer himself. He stops before you asking where you would like to go.
