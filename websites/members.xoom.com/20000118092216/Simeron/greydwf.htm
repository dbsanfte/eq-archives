Duergar (Gray Dwarves)

Grey dwarves, or Duergar, are the evil side of the dwarven family. They too headed deep within the earth, like the deep dwarves, but their souls were perverted by some dark power and they now serve evil. They are normally bald with beards of white or grey with pale, pasty skin. They are four feet tall and weigh 120 stones normally. While they do still smith and mine, their crafts are not as good as other dwarves. Having lived underground for so long, sunlight is painful to them and they dislike open areas. So, their try to avoid the surface as much as possible. Deep dwarves are in a constant state of war with duergar due to limits on living space and resources underground.
