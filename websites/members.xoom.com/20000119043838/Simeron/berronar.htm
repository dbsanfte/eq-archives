Berronar Truesilver

The Mother of Safety

Intermediate Goddess: Goddess of Safety, Home, Truth, Marriage, Love

Holy Symbol: Two linked silver rings

History:

Berronar is the wife of Moradin. Only priests and preistesses of Berronar can perform dwarven marriages within dwarven society. The only exception to this rule is when no priest is available to do the wedding for some reason. Priests of Berronar make frequent use of quests. Intolerant of thieves, they will not willingly travel with them for, according to them, they usetheir talents for evil. Being the dwarven goddess of safety and marriage (some cynics note that the dwarves place safety first), she is loosely associated with healing and record keeping. Her clergy perform all weddings in dwarven strongholds and also serve as arbiters in disputes (marital or otherwise). Dwarven marriages are expected to be for life and no priestess of Berronar would ever consider shattering a marital bond. Dwarves that abandon a spouse or dissolve a marriage are anathema to
Berronar and cannot be healed by her followers. There are no know cities that follow Berronar, although she is of great importance to all of them.
