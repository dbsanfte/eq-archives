Dumathoin

The Keeper of Secrets Under the Mountain

Intermediate God: God of Secrets, Earth, Mountains, Metals, Gemstones

Holy Symbol: A faceted gem set against a silhouetted mountain.


History:

The god of mining and underground exploration. He is called the Silent Keeper, for he never speaks, but may guide his faithful through fate and happenstance to the richest veins and the greatest treasures. Dumathoin worshippers are mainly miners and those dwarves that rely on the ores, gems and stones mined and quarried from the earth. His priests and priestesses can call upon powerful earth elementals, mud creatures and such in time of need. Dumathoin is said to guard the treasures placed within the ground for the dwarves to find. He rests on the Mithril Pinnacle upon which the whole world spins. The clergy of Dumathoin is on very good terms with most of the dwarven gods save Abbathor, due to Abbathor's followers stealing of gemstone and the like. Priests of Dumathoin also tend be extremely tight-lipped, particularly with information that might aid non-dwarven races.
