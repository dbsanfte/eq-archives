Titles within the Order of the Mithril Warhammers

Rik Drungi - Lord Vanquisher

This title is held by the leader of the Order and grants complete authority over the governing affairs within the Order.  The Lord Vanquisher determines policy, Codes of Conduct and the role each strike team plays in the defense of the Order. The Lord Vanquisher also serves on the Council.

Lok Trommi - Praiseworthy Longbeard

This title is held by the second in command of the Order and grants the duties of insuring the orders of the Lord Vanquisher are carried out, as well as overseeing the proper training of new recruits within the Order. If the Lord Vanquisher is overcome in battle or is otherwise unable to fulfill his duties, the Praiseworthy Longbeard assumes command of the Order until the Council votes on a suitable replacement. It is viewed in the eyes of this Thane that the holder of this title should be an excellent fighter.

Ock Krami - Skillful Wardrum

This title is held by a seasoned veteran who's duties require him to assist in the proper training of new recruits within his strike team as well as the restocking of the finest provision and supplies for the Order. If the Praiseworthy Longbeard is overcome in battle or is otherwise unable to fulfill his duties, the Skillful Wardrum assumes the duties of the Praiseworthy Longbeard until the Lord Vanquisher can fine a suitable replacement. It is viewed in the eyes of this Thane, the holder of this title should be a most capable fighter.

Hazkul Grobkuli - Young Goblinstalker

This title is held by members of each strike team who have proven their worth by defeating an enemy of the Order in single combat or performing a heroic deed in the defense of our delvings. It is viewed in the eyes of this Thane, the holder of this title should be a good fighter.

Ock Kaldari - Skillful Warrior

This title is held by members of each strike team who have performed nobly in the defense of our delvings, but has not yet defeated an enemy of our Order in single combat.

Barazi - Oath Taker

This title is given to all new members of the Order. All will be schooled in the Customs & Traditions of the Order.
