So you want to be a Mithril Warhammer
 

To become a Mithril Warhammer requires several things. You must be a good race that follows the Way of the Light at all times. You must commit yourself to the Order fully. You may pick your own weapons and armors but, as far as trade skills, I ask that each warhammer specialize in no more then two. I expect those of the Order to conduct themselves with the proper respect for all goodly creatures regardless of race or class. Our sole purpose is to stand against all evil beings. We are the first into a fight and give others a chance to escape before we withdraw from battle. Above all, we are loyal to our Order and all members of the Warhammers.

Please read the oath of the Warhammers that is carved on the stone table within the Shrine of Light. Search deep within your heart and soul. If you decide you are willing to join with this band of brothers and sisters, read the Code of Conduct and the Charter. Then send me an email with all the information required.

I will get back to you within a fortnight (10 days), normally sooner. I am currently limiting the number of members to 60.
