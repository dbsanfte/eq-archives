Deep Dwarves

Deep dwarves have gone deep into the earth and rarely ever come to the surface. No one knows the exact reason why they chose to do this but, there are many ideas. Curiosity, protection from monsters or a cataclysm or some other such thing has been pondered. They are four to four and a half feet tall and weight 120 stones. Their build is slighter than their cousins and hair color runs from firey red to corn yellow. Their skin is pale brown to light tan in color, normally with a reddish tint to it.
