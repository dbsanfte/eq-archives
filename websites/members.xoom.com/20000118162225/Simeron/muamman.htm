Marthammor Duathal

The Watcher

Lesser God: God of Wanderers, Sundered Dwarves and Travel

Holy Symbol: An upright mace superimposed over a dwarven boot trimmed with fur.

History:

Clergy of Muamman are unique among the dwarven gods in that they do not build a permanent structure for their services but instead have a portable tent-like one they can move with them. The main worshippers of Muamman are sundered dwarves, dwarven adventurers and those dwarves who move among strange and alien peoples as opposed to the remaining within the safety of their halls. Clergy can never own anything they cannot carry with them nor can they "settle" in any one spot for longer than one winter's cycle (1 year). After that time, another member of the clergy must take up residence or the community left with but a small leanto shrine. Muamman is probably the most neutral of all the dwarven gods but, he does tend to side with good more than evil.
