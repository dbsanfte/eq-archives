Sundered Dwarves

Unlike most dwarves, sundered dwarves live totally above ground. They once were hill or mountain dwarves but, something cut them off from thier traditional way of life. This could be natural like a volcano or earthquake or supernatural like a gateway to a hellish plane being opened. Whatever the reason, they have abandoned the subterrainean way of life. Communities of sundered dwarves are mostly found along side human and elven towns where they still practice their smithing and mining skills. They are four and a half to five feet tall, making them the tallest of the dwarves, and weigh about 155 stones. Their skin is light brown to pink in color and hair is normally black with blue tinges.
