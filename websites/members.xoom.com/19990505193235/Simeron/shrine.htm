The Shrine of Light

You hear across the dark moor the sounds of voices in song. They sing about days gone by. A time when honor and glory had meaning. They sing of the great sacrifice made by the Warhammers to keep the world safe from the great hoardes of evil that once plagued this land. Slowly, you reach five stone monoliths set down by the First Dwarves of the Mithril Warhammers.

Here is one of the most sacred of the Warhammer places. It is a spot where every Warhammer comes to pay homage to thier fore fathers and pray to thier gods. Before someone can be given the honor of becoming a Mithril Warhammer, they must come to here, search deep within thier heart and soul to find the things carved into the stone monoliths before you and take up the Warhammer cause. If they cannot do this, then they are sent away.

In the center of the monolithic circle, lies a stone slab with torches set to either side. On this is written the code of the Warhammers.


 

Our devotion to the Art of War is renown
throughout many a land.
Our dedication to the Light
is well known.

   Our bodies be hard like the bedrock of Mother Earth we were created from.
Our minds be sharp like the thunderbolts of the Heavens that gave us form.
Our souls be full of fire, like the Eternal Flame where we were tempered.
Our movements be swift like the Waters of Life where we were born.

 We are strengthened by battle, tempered by discipline and grow wise with
experience. Always remember, a Warhammer's memory is a long one.

We be the best of friends you can have.
And the worst of enemies you can find.

We are elite of the good races, slayers without equal.

This be the code we follow. Mark it well, for evil everywhere will know great fear and trembling when the Mithril Warhammers enter battle against them.

I have no:

Parents: For the Earth & Sky are my parents.

Home: For the Order is my only home.

Divine Power: For honesty is my way.

Magic Power: For my attitude is my magic.

Law: For the Order Oath is my only justice.

Life nor Death: For I live and die for the Light.

Miracles: For my Honor is my miracle.

Tactics: For seizing opportunities in battle carries the day.

Enemy: For I am ever vigilant.

Armor: For Loyalty is my shield.

Will: For my Spirit is strong as the raging river.

To this code I pledge all that I command.

My heart, my soul, my body and my spirit.
 

Lord Simeron Steelhammer - Rik Drungi - Order of the Mithril Warhammer
 

LET EVIL EVERYWHERE KNOW GREAT FEAR AND TREMBLING. FOR THE DWARVES ARE UPON YOU!!!

Warhammer Member Conduct
