       5-26-99 I start this journal not as a young mage, but afollower of the 5th circle of spells. I hope to jot down the details of my experience as a young mage.
Level    1-3 I started my travels outside my hometown Ak'Anon home of the gnomes ( the soon to be rules of land). I woke with my master gone and a note to give to my guild master ( who i had never met) and two scrolls, a book which i have yet to look at. I have placed the book in the bank for later use. I have talked to several other new adventures and they have said the book contains advice on fighting with my own brethren, and the other races in our world. I have found the book somehow returns to me if i die, and it has to be replaced in the bank. After several fights i should have know better to engage in and replacing the book in the bank i have decided to keep it on my person for now. The first thing i did was  sit down and look at my scrolls i had, since i knew they had to be scribed into my spell book i did that. 
I noticed as i scribed the spells to my book that the words i wrote disappeared off the scrolls as i wrote them. the spells were: 

Burst of Flame- It seemed when i cast this spell when i had a monster targeted it ignited the air around it. I found this spell worked well on the first monsters i encountered. The range was enough i could target and pull back to a safe distance and fight at my convince. I did find the spell would attract any monster close to the one i had targeted, mayhaps the grunt from the targeted called its brethren to help it. 

Minor Shielding-When i first cast this spell i felt more life in myself, and i noticed monsters seemed to have a tougher time hitting me. As i this seemed to help me in battle, i kept this spell active at all times. 

After I left my home city i took the pass to the left straight out my home towns tunnel. I found a stone monument with a Human female ( at least this is what i have been told by others, terrible looking thing, so tall its a wonder they dont tip over when they are hit) i found she would by my loot  which saved me considerable time going back to my hometown to sell. I attacked Rats and Gnome skeletons, making sure i healed and rested so my manna was full. Soon i felt i had reached a new level, my health seemed to increase. I noticed that sometimes the skells had weapons, these i attacked with vigor after finding most of these weapons gave me a gold back from  shops. I soon came upon a skell with a great staff, after i had killed it i held the staff in my hands, it felt good, and after being hit with it i thought i would try it. I soon learned it did some great damage but was very slow, still i felt after growing stronger i could take more damage so i had the time it took to deal out damage with this staff. I noticed as i used it my skill moved very slowly, i decided when i trained with my guild master i would put some points in 2 hand blunt, and after this my skill with it seemed to increase faster, I believe that the initial learning curve of any level is best to train a few points with your guild master in a skill , it "seemed" to raise quicker , this is my belief, others may tell you not to as your skill will go up no matter what with use, i leave this up to you. I soon saw a skell with another staff, one i had not seen before, so i attacked and looted the corpse. A cracked staff, i noticed i could hold it in one hand, and that it was considerably faster then the huge two handed staff, but now i needed to learn how best to use it. After using it awhile i decided i would switch off weapons , 2 handed staff, 1 handed staff, and my dagger, so that my skill would be equal with all. 
Something just seemed to go off in my head when i got better, so i knew when yo switch off when i felt i had learned all i could for awhile. I stayed away from the rats that seemed to be green colored as they seemed to carry some nasty desiease. I continued to clobber skells and rats till i reached 4th level. I bought a few spells from the guild along the way. 

Summon food- This summoned me some black bread, i noticed i could trade it to others, but the shop keepers wanted no part of it and would not buy it. After an extended time away from adventuring  i noticed when i came back and picked up my adventuring pack that it was gone. It seems this is only good till i sit and camp. 

Summon Drink-This summoned me some water which i could trade, but also no shop would buy. It also seemed to disappear when i camped. 

SummonDagger-When i first cast this spell and got a dagger after a death i knew how handy it was..I need not walk back to my corpse unarmed anymore. I would always have a weapon. I have also heard form other adventures that since i made it from magic it can hit those creatures that are immune to normal weapons.This i have yet to test as i am not daring enough to fight anything of that level yet, besides i have seen nothing that a good rap on the skull with a stick wont injure. 

Flare-I have not bought this spell as i have been told by others it is just a pretty light. They have said it is useful for people finding you, just shoot it and it shoots a bright orange flare  to pinpoint your location.This may or may not prove useful, i myself found no use for it so i have not bought it. 

Reclaim Energy-I bought this spell and wondered what i would use it for, a fellow mage of  some considerable circle higher then me said if i had alot of food, or a dagger i had summoned i could use this spell to suck the manna back into my reserve. he also hinted i may use it on a friend i would soon be traveling with, he said i could suck the manna out of him. I am wondering if i was talking to a loony or what.. I hope to find out what he was talking about soon. 
 
Level    4-7 As i felt the surge i feel when i have made some progress i headed back to the mage guild, as i got there i noticed i could use more spells. I asked a fellow mage and he told me to get a pet. I thought this a rude comment, but after examining the spells i could now use i seen what he meant. I quickly bought a  new spell. 

Elementalkin Fire-I knew from the excitement in other mages eyes as they bought this spell that it must be something special. I quickly cast it. Hmmmm it didn't work, it just seemed to be missing something, i asked around and was told i needed a green stone called Malachite ( spelling?). I bought a few stones and went to my old stomping grounds to see what this spell would do. I cast it and heard a low rushing of air behind me and turned around. I jumped back as there was a flaming molten fiend standing right beside me, i turned and ran as quickly as i could, the beast followed. As i ran out of breath and had to slow down i could almost feel its flaming fists slamming into my back, i gave up running and turned to face the beast. It stopped and stood by me. After much testing i learned it followed my commands. I had it follow me and soon seen it would attack anything i wished. This was the life, sit back and let my pet do the work. I soon learned if i helped the pet with a BURST OF FLAME and also helped with a staff now and again i could whittle monsters down  much quicker. I also noticed my new pet healed quit fast. 

My next spells where all bought soon after. I also learned there were a total of 4 Pets you could get, i would suggest to you to try them all now when the spells cost little, and pick a favorite. I have noticed this about the pets: 

Fire-- It seems to have a small amount of health, but i noticed every monster that strikes it takes damage from its       flaming body. 

Air-- This pet seems to be harder for the monsters to hit, and seems to do average damage. It also seems to disappear when not fighting. 

Water-- This pet seems to be about average at everything. It -seemed- to heal quicker then the others, but i cant be sure of this. 

Earth--My favorite pet, he seems to be slow so he gets hit alot, but this seems not to bother him much as he seems to have alot of health. He also seems to hit much harder then the other pets, so as long as i hammer along with him i can take much harder monsters. I have also heard tell he is better at pulling monsters off of me if i am there target, i cannot tell you if this is true, but i hear him taunting the monsters alot whilst we fight. 

Burn- This seemed to work alot like Burst Of Flame but it did alot more damage. 

Fire Flux- As i was attacked by more then one monster at a time i learned this spell did damage to al the monsters close to me. The damage wasn't great but it hit all in its range. 

Gate-This spell when i cast the first time took me back to where i started. I figure this will be good for getting out of tight situations. I did notice it takes awhile to cast so one would need to be prepared to cast it for quick get aways. 

Summon Bandages-This spell summoned bandages to use with the Bind Wound skill, i used this skill often when i felt i was under half of my health. It seemed to do little but i was assured it would be a great help latter. I also heard that the better i became as a mage the more bandages it would summon. 

Sense Summond-I have heard this would point me to the nearest summoned creature felt i would use this little in my adventuring so i did not buy this spell. 

Summon Wisp- I cast this spell and found i had a stone in my hands. It seemed to give off light. I wondered what good it would do. Soon i was told not all of the inhabitants of this world see as well as gnomes at night, i was told this could be given to those who see poorly at night to aid them. I will find out one day, but right now i useit not. I would imagine if you could not see well at night this would be a handy spell. 

I noticed that with a pet i could fight tougher monsters and i moved to some of the spiderlings in the area, they gave silk as loot when the body was split. I found that spider silk was a profitable seller. I also noticed alot of spiders from home, but i noticed they followed no orders, and soon found they were rouge. I smashed a few, and soon found they carried broken parts, gears and such, some i could sell, others i could not. I felt they were a waste of time for me, i needed money to learn new spells,and i got exp and loot from others. I soon noticed a 2 tents a short distance from the rats and Skells. It was populated by Kobolds. I tried the camp, as my spell hit the guard out front  2 more emerged from the tents. THis was going badly, my pet was getting beat from all sides and i didn't have the manna to take the Kobolds out fast enough. After this fight i realized i would have to be careful and kill them one at a time. I did notice they would sometimes venture off form the camp where i could single them out. I also noticed there were Kobold Pups running around which i could easily take out. the larger ones sometimes carried a bag which was quite useful for storing my loot, and sometimes they carried a drink. After awhile i decided to venture to another area. 
I soon found another Kobold camp which was much larger, and much more dangerous. I Found some help and we attacked the camp with vigor, soon i was feeling stronger and stronger. I must warn you the Pumas, and Regular Skells are much to powerful so unless they seem to be an even match ( size them up) i would stay away. I also found that the large Plague rats seemed to help the skells out if you attacked them. I would recommend not to do this till later levels. 
 
Level    8-11 As i reached the third Circle of magic i quickly made my way to the guild and bought spells. 

Flame Bolt- This spell did good damage but unlike my other spells i found i must actually aim it. Right when i tried this spell for the first time i thought it was a manna hog, for the damage and it took a long time for me to feel i could cast another. After a few tries i found i used this spell very little as i could miss, others tell me it is quite effective, so i leave this to you. 

Lesser Shielding-This spell made me feel stronger and more immune to attacks, it seemed to be the same as MINOR SHIELDING but better. I found myself using it all the time. I tried casting this on another but it only seems to work on me. 

Shock of Blades-When i first tried this spell on a Kobold i was amazed, the Kobold died before i could let my pet hit him. I find this spell to be of great use, and have noticed as i feel stronger it increases strength also.I use this spell as my mainstay attack. 

Shield of Fire-I find casting this on myself or my pet during a longer battle is a wise thing to do. As i notice it seems to make anything that attacks you pay for it buy being burned. this spell seems to last through combat, and then fade away.  

Invisibility-This spell is very useful as it makes whoever i cast it on fade away. I have used this many times to travel areas i felt were much to dangerous before. I have noticed that for me to cast it on another we have to be in a party. 
I have also heard it does not work on the Undead, or some monster i have yet to see called a Evil Eye. 

Eye of Zomm-I have heard this is a spell that makes you a scout to check areas you dare not go. I have not bought the spell so i can tell you no more about it. 

Elementalings-I noticed as i looked at the spells that there were new spells for the 4 Pets, and i asked the guild about them. I was told they were more powerful then the one i had. So i bought my favorite Earth.I have not tried the others but have been assured they are much like there smaller counterparts. 

After getting my spells i went out and started attacking the Kobold camps with a renewed vigor. They fell much easier with my new pet, and a Shield of Fire spell on him seemed to make taking on more then one much easier. I also Joined forces with others to attack Pumas and Full size Skells. this made me learn new things much faster. I also ventured around more in my home(Steamfont mountains) and found there were some amazing things to see. There were 3 or 4  Observatories located around the mountains and there were the amazing Windmills. I also found a cave that contained Slavers with Bull Heads. I dared not venture to close as there were tales of a Bull head that was so fearsome that the guards were slaughtered by him. I heard his name was HERO but i know not if this is true. I also saw my first flying drakes, they were all black and most called them Young Ebon Drakes. i stayed away from these also. And the HUGE rouge Clockworks, i had seen the Cargo Clockwork at my home city but i didn't know the gnomes had built so many, and they had changed to rouges, yet another thing i stayed away from. 
 
Level    12-15 As i felt i was ready for the fourth circle of magic i headed to town to buy my spells. 

Rain of Blades-This spell seemed to rain down fire from the sky on a group of attackers. I use this spell only when there are more then one attackers. 

Column of Fire-This Spell seems to do a large amount of damage as it centers a flame on the my target. I ma not sure if it is better then SHOCK OF BLADES, but i can tell it does more damage, but it takes alot out of me to cast it, and a long time for me to cast another. I fell this spell is to slow, but wheni am helping a party and need to do damage quickly i use it alot. 

Bind Affinity-I find if this spell is cast on me i can camp anywhere and always start there again. I have also found that if i have somebody in my party i can make them Bind to the ground in any city have learned this spell is very tough to cast and i practice it any chance i get as it burns alot of manna and when it fails i feel very weakened, i must practice this to get good, maybe i will train with my guild master so i can get better faster. 

Cancel Magic-I have not bought this spell, but have heard if my party has a magic spell cast on them it would remove it. I must buy and try this sometime. 

Burnout-I have found when this is cast on my pet he does more damage and seems to get hit alot less. But after battles it seems to take him longer to rest and get back to health. I do have some questions on this spell, i have no idea how long it lasts as my pet will not tell me. And im not sure it is a wise spell to use as my earth elemental gets hit regardless of if this spell is used or not. and the slow regain is tough to wait on. I feel this spell would be much better with one of the other elemental, but as i prefer the Earth i have not had a chance to test this. 

Summon Blade-I have not bought this spell, but have heard it summons a sword of some type to hit magical creatures, as i cannot wield a sword i felt i didn't have a use for it. BUT i have heard i can wield this weapon from others, and i have also had party members ask me to summon them a blade when we are attacking magical monsters so sometimes i wish i had it. mayhaps i will buy it soon. 

Ward Summond-I have not purchased this spell as i have seen very few elemental there are some around the Slaver mines, but i have not seen the need for this as they seem to be little tales of them elsewhere in the world. I may be proved wrong but i have not bought this spell as i see little use for it now. 

Elementals-Again new pet spells, i have bought the Earth and the Water at the urging of another to see if the Water is as good as my Earth. After much testing i still use my Earth almost all the time. I feel my new pets are quite weak compared to my old ones. Maybe they will get better down the road. I am much more careful about what i attack with them now. Fighting  monster that seems above my level has been left out of my daily activities, where the old pets could handle the tougher monsters at there levels this new one seems to have much tougher time going at it, i must help him much more. Mayhaps trying some of the other pets is a wise choice now.But as i can ill afford to buy spells i may not use i will wait awhile. 

I ventured towards the Slaver cave and took on some of the Young Ebon Drakes, they are quite tough but i am beating them with my pets help. Sometimes i must attack with my own blade or staff, but i know this is a wise thing as i need to increase my ability with my weapons for that day when my magicv fails. I also feel i am getting better at attacking and defending my self the more i do this.I have fought a few Elemental they give very little loot , most times nothing, but sometimes a gem. The skells in the area by the mines seem to give good coin. I have also attacked a harpy or two, they seem to be very tough, but the loot is ok and they sometimes yield a gem . After getting used to my spells i went into the caves and fought a few Bull heads, i found that my pet with my help could take 2 if i was lucky but if more showed up i ran and let my pet hold them off, yes a cowards tactic, but it was working and keeping me alive. I joined with others in  the mines and found it much safer. I also noticed that sometimes the Minos ( as i now call the bull heads) carried a much sought after axe that sold well to other adventures. I found that if you take these to the main land they sold much better then in the lands around my home. I also heard tell of some truly evil thing in the mines that would make you attack your friends. And yes i found out the hard way the roumors were true, i could not control my body and i attacked many of my fellow adventures, they fell like chaff as i sent spell after spell at them. Soon they released i was under a spell and took it upon themselves to beat it out of me, well they beat a bit to good, and they sent my soul back to the city so i could come back and pick my corpse clean. 
 
Level     16+ I have just reached the 5th circle and have began my hunt for spells. I have not seem my new pet spell in Ak'Anon so have asked around and heard it was in the High Elf city. Well after traveling to it i find the High Elves do not like me much, as i approached the gates the guards ran out and attacked me . As i was not expecting the attack i was unprepared and fell quit swiftly. After i came back to my body and collected my things with my summon corpse command and Invis. i started taking with the locals. Soon one offered to go into the city and get the spell for me, he seemed a nice lot so i gave him the plat  ( which i might add gave me a sick feeling in my stomach as he could have just ran away with my money). he soon found the spell  at a vender outside the Magic Library there, he was tough to find. 

Summoning-Lesser-This summons a pet, and there are yet again 4 variations, once again i have picked a earth. the Spell isn't the big disappointment the last one was, my pets seem slightly weak, but they are good enough to do the job. 

Phantom Leather-This spell required me to have a cats eye stone on me, it seems to increase my defense against monsters. 

Shielding- This is yet another variant of the shielding spell it seems but much better. 

the other spells have yet to be purchased as i have just reached the 5th circle and have yet to make the money for others. 

UPDATED: I have finally made the money to buy new spells :

Summon Heatstone- This produces a item that you can give to players that cannot see in the night. 

Summon Throwing Dagger-(may be renamed) This produce a dagger for throwing..some classes use this, though i have seen little call for it. 

Identify- I am not sure what this spell is good for...As i seem to just "know" what things are in the world. 

After getting stocked up on spells and the stones needed to cast my pet. I headed out to see what the world held. 
I traveled through a rocky mountains area called ButcherBlock mountains. Here i found another race much like my own in size, but they seemed of a stocky build. I found they didnt much like me, some such shouts about my beliefs and my god, i have heard that some of my gnome brothers can go near them but i cannot. As i travled the land i came to a large gameboard, Chess i think they call it. At this place there were many undead. I soon heard there was a ship that traveled to another land, the home of the Humans and other such lower lifeforms. Upon hearing this i quickly invis'ed myself ( i ahve become fond of being invis while travling, makes it go quickly) and headed for the docks. After passing the Dwarf Guards i snapped my fingers to remove the invis spell and waited for the Ship. As i waited i heard many say they left all their belongings in the bank for the trip , after asking why many said the ship was know to travel on rare times to a different plane, and when one made the trip all ones belongings were lost and rentry into our plane was normally without the boat...and that a swim back to an island was sometimes quite hard. After Reaching the new land and travling through a town called Freeport i moved into a area called  the common lands. I examined the beasts in this area nad seen little of intrest. As i traveled through the land i heard shouts of "Griffion to 2nd Inn" as i had passed an inn earlier i started l;ooking for this "griffion" and as i creasted the hill there was a HUGE bird, lion thingy with wings...I seen  a guard rush to meet it...the battel was fierce and the guard went down. I felt it was best to pass this beasty by. As i travled fartehr i came to a land called West Commons. there was a dervish camp here (bandits im told) and a larger Orc camp. I soon came to a large forest. I set up camp at the entrance.
