Clangeddin Silverbeard

The Father of Battle

Greater God: God of War, Battle, Bravery

Holy Symbol: Two Crossed Axes or Warhammers


History:

As the Father of Battle, Clangeddin is the resolute dwarven war god in the eternal struggles with the other races. HIs worshippers are mainly warriors but, in time of war, all the dwarven nation turns to him. His priests fight as well as warriors and many a high priest leads a dwarven army as its general. Clangeddin teaches that a battle must be just and a triumph obtained through valor or nothing is gained. Cowardice or Stupidity in battle (strategic withdrawls are allowed) is punished by the loss of all granted powers until the offender atones by defeating a feared enemy, the quest must be performed by the dwarf alone, with no assistance other than his own arms and armor.
