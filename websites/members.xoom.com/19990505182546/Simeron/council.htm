Council Chambers of the Mithril Warhammers


 

You enter a grand council hall for the Mithril Warhammers.
The table is carved from a single piece of sturdy oak while the chairs are carved out of solid granite. Armor, weapons and tapastries adorn the walls of the chamber. It is here that the Council meet with Lord Simeron to discuss matters of guild policy and defense. All visiting emmisaries, be they of dwarven clans, other races, merchant guilds or similar organizations, are met here when they are in this land.
