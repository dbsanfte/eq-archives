The Library of Lore

From times long since past, these tomes have been
kept safe here. Within these pages lie a history of the dwarves, their gods, culture and customs. The Mithril Warhammers began as an all dwarven warband within Odin's Hammer Clan and has grown into the multi racial Order of today. You may browse among the works, follow the link to the Dwarven Delving for more dwarven information or get an overview of the lands that Lord Simeron and the Warhammers have explored.

Also, for some good stories and tales, please go to Simeron's Study.
