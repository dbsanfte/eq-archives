Warrior Warriors are the masters of armed combat. They are at home on the battlefield, and are trained to take punishment as much as dish it out, so their H P are the highest of any of the other classes.
Ranger Ranger is a mix between a Warrior and a Druid, sharing some skils from both. They are at home in the outdoors.
Paladin Paladins are the very picture of a holy knight, fighting for good in all aspects of his life. They share some of the power of a Cleric, and the fighting ability of a Warrior, but have a few powers all their own
Rogue They are the shady class of the world, who sneak about in the dark. Some say they are assassins or thieves, but they are capable of both. They are good with weapons, but they cannot stand that great of a chance against a Warrior, and prefer to make the kill by  attacking from behind ( at which they are good at ). They  prefer not be discovered. If they are, however, they will make sure to not be there long enough for their opponentto cause any damage.   Picking locks and stealing are known trademarks of this class.
Shadow Knight To the polar opposite of the Paladin class,you have the Shadow Knights. they obtain all of their power from evil gods. They are a mix of Necromancer and Warrior, and share their abilities and spells.
Druid A Druid is one with nature. He/she will never be attacked by an animal unless thy attack it. A Druids spells involve nature.
Monk Monks are dedicated to shaping their bodies into a pure weapon. They shun most weapons and armor. They are very religious and the gods are known to bless them by magically enhancing their attacks
Shaman Similar to the Cleric, but more of a witch doctor, they are typically found near the more primitve races. Their primary focus is healing and other Cleric like spells, but they have a good deal of offensive spells.
Magician Magicians are summoners, and have to power to call anything from dirt to demons. While they are not as good at offensive spells as Wizards, Magicians still obtain reasonable power in this area as well.
Enchanter If you have ever played a game like Final Fantasy, you would be familiar with the Enchanter. The Enchanter can make his/her enemy thier slave, having them take the damage for them. They can also enchant weapons to give them stronger attack, etc. not as good with offensive spells as a wizzard.
Necromancer A Necromancer has the abillity to reanimate the dead, using them as thier soliders. They can also use spells to drain life from thier victims.
Cleric The Cleric is the holy man, with the abillity to heal and call apon his Gods to destroy his enemies. they use prayers to regain his/her abillity.
Bard The Bard (like the pied piper) play's songs. Thier songs are more like spells, and their effects last as long as the Bard sings and plays the correct instrument. They have some knowledge of defensive skills, but they will never be as skillful as Warriors.
Wizard Wizards seek knowledge, and their spells are aimed on this goal. They use powerful spells to see things from far away, and to teleport themselves as well. This thirst for knowledge has also made them the true masters destructive offensive spells.
