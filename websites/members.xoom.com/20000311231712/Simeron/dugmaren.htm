Dugmaren Brightmantle

Keeper of Knowledge

Lesser God: God of Knowledge, Scholars and Invention.

Holy Symbol: Opened book with eye in middle.

History:

Dugmaren is the keeper of the histories and knowledge of the dwarven race. His clergy maintains a written history for their clans for use by future generations as well as confirm new inventions owners so that proper credit can be given to the inventor. His worshippers are mainly scholars, inventors and tinkerers and every one of his temples doubles as a library. It is said that if you cannot find out about something dwarven from one of his temples, it didn't happen.
