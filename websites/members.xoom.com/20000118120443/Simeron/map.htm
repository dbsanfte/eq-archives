The map above shows the three land masses for the land of Norrath. Be advised that Everquest is continually evolving and growing and that there will always be suprises out there for you.

Below you will find a better pic of each continent and some general information about each.

FAYDWER

Land of the Elder Races, Faydwer is home to Dwarves, Wood and High Elves and Gnomes. Kaladim is the capital of the Dwarves, Kelethin is the Wood Elves home in the trees, Felwithe is the stronghold of the High Elves and Ak'anon the home of the Gnomes.

Some general advice is to stay close to the main entrances of your city until you progress several levels and remember, what you kill will effect your reputation sometimes. That can and will come back to help or harm you. Places to check out are the Giant Chessboard in the Butcherblock Mtns, The Crushbone Clan stronghold (Orcs) in Greater Faydark and the Windmills of the Steamfront Mtns. But, as always, be warned that there are many creatures that will hunt you as you hunt them.


 

Antonica

Antonica is a HUGE place that is by far the biggest of the three continents. It is the land of the humans, half elves, barbarians, ogres, trolls, dark elves and halflings. Again, stay close to your home city until you gain a few levels before you venture out into the world lest you become dinner for a griffon or giant. For goodly races, the lands of Neriak (Dark Elves), Grobb (Trolls) and Oggok (Ogres) are hostile. Evil races have the other problem in that Freeport, RiverVale, Qeynos and Halas are hostile to them. A boat to Faydwer leaves Freeport while Odus is reached by boat from Qeyons. For sights to see, Hill Giants and Griffons in the commonlands cant be beat. And Sand Giants in the Desert of Ro are even more impressive. Just make sure you keep a healthy distance. You will be able to see them I assure you.


 

Odus

The land of Odus is home to the High Men. Dark skinned and highly intellegent, they tend to be spellweavers of extreme ability. As before, stay near your home city until you have leveled several times. I loved the city of Erudin. Sites to see are the town library, hall of magics and the city hall. I was truely impressed with the details within these buildings. I plan on visiting there again soon.
