So you want to be a Mithril Warhammer
 

We of the Order have high standards when it comes to who we allow to enter our fellowship. Only those people who show us that they are commited to our principles, willing to accept our ways and be a honorable member of our order are even concidered for membership.

Once someone has shown this, a Proven or Founding member of the Warhammers will accept the Barazi (Oath Taker) Pledge from them. This member will bring this Oath to the Mithril Council and the Chief Elder for consideration. Each one will then give a task for the Barazi to preform from the five stone tablets that are the Warhammer's Strength.

When all five of these tasks are completed by the Barazi, they will bring to the Chief Elder the Oath below and swear it before the Shrine of the Warhammers.

At that time, the Barazi will be:

Accepted into the Warhammers as a Proven Member
Given the Title of Orc Kaldari
           Given FIVE platimum coins to symbolize the five tests they have passed
           Given full rights and priviledges of Sponsership
