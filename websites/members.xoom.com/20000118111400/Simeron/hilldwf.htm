Hill Dwarves
 

Hill dwarves make their homes in the rolling hills of a land. Thier strongholds are mostly underground with a castle or walled settlement above that. Hill dwarves are normally four feet tall and about 150 stones in wieght. Their build is stocky and muscular with skin of dark tan to light brown. normal hair colors are brown, black and grey. They prefer earth tones in clothing and wear little jewelry at all. Hill dwarves are the main type of dwarves other races trade with for dwarven items and crafts.
