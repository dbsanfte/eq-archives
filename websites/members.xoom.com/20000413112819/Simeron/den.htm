Hall of Honors

You enter the Hall of Honors within Warhammer hall and are amazed at all the things within. Pelts and heads of animals, weapons, armor, tapestries and cups adorn the area. A book on one table has heroic deeds done and enemies slain written within its pages. But, all this is nothing compared to the object in the corner. A sign reads "Portal to Other Worlds".
