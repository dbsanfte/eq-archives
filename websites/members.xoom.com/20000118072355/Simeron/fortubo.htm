Fortubo

The Stone Dweller

Lesser God: God of Stone and Metals

Holy Symbol: Warhammer with glowing head

History:

 Fortubo is worshipped in small communities of dwarves that live on the edge of the two main areas of habitation. These communities live niether above ground or below ground all the time but in both areas off and on. Fortubo's temples are always of stone construction with metal bars placed in the stone for added strength. He is a close ally of Moradin, Berronar and Dumathoin. He has found these dwarven gods to be much more to his liking than the others. His main worshippers are mostly metalsmiths and miners (to a lesser extent). Fortubo demands a rigorous respect for all peoples of his priests, and they are sworn to never take life without cause. He will defrock any clergy who knowingly commits an evil act, though he will spare them their life. Fortubo's clergy are expected to act as spiritual guides to their peoples and to lift arms only when the actions of the more evil races demand it.
