WHAT WE STAND FOR




    		•

    The Circle of Ronin was formed based on the premise that we must aid the weak, protect the innocent, and fight to eradicate evil from the Realms.


    Thus the goals of the Circle are simple: we are dedicated to assisting those weaker than us in whatever way necessary without interfering in their innate right to grow and learn in their own way thus keeping their gaming experience both enjoyable and challenging; while never acting in an imperious, overbearing manner, the Circle protects those that find themselves under unjust attack by others, attempting through mediation and adult discussion, to uphold the rights of ALL individuals within the realm; the Circle will do what it takes to meet and defeat the evil spreading over the realms, including joining forces with another Guild/Clan to accomplish our goals.
