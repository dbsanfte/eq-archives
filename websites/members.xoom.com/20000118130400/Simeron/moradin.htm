Moradin

The Soul Forger

Greater God: God of Smithing, Forging, Dwarves

Holy Symbol:  Hammer and Anvil

History:

Moradin (and his priests) despise evil dwarves. Moradin is the husband of Berronar. Worship of Moradin always takes place at forges or hearths.
A Greater God, Moradin forged the dwarven race from iron and mithril then breathed life into them as they cooled on the Eternal Forge. As the
creator, he is revered by all good and neutral dwarves regardless of clan. Priests of Moradin are charged with upholding the dwarven virtues and
with guiding their people in the arts the dwarves hold dear. They often hold leadership positions within their clans. In fact, more than one high
priest of Moradin is a king or prince of his people. Most dwarven cities hold one god above all others, with Moradin, Clangeddin, Ulaa and Fortubo receiving the lion's share. The morals and arts of the city reflects their patron god, with Moradin's cities tending to be the most moral (some would say intolerant) of the lot. The metal working of his strongholds are second to none.
