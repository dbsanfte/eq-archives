SuperCross Circuit™, from 989 Sports and the makers of Cool Boarders 3, bring you the most realistic motocross and supercross action with pure "MotoPhysics™". Now you can experience the incredible physics engine that will exhaust gamers who make it through each grueling, hard-fought race. Compete on 9 authentic stadium supercross courses and 12 motocross tracks as you maneuver through tell tale signs of wear and tear like tire-ruts and bikes accumulating mud. If you win and stand out at the "Local Tracks", you’ll move onto the national outdoor tracks and the supercross circuit where you will do battle against the best riders in the world including: LaRocco, Albertyn, Button, Dowd, Emig, Henry, Hughes, Lytle, Tortelli, Ward and Windham.
