A hacker is not someone who just finds an exploit and uses it. This is a cheater. (I guess by my own definition I was a cheater)

A true hacker, in my opinion, are the ones on the forefront of technology. They spend most of there time researching. A true hacker gets to the core of how things work, what makes them tick. They know there OS's as well as the developers, if not better. A true hack takes patience. It can take months or even years to silently probe a system, looking for cracks.

The Hackersquest team has been probing EQ for over a year now. They are studying the nuances of the game. Through this study damage formulas have been determined. Experience tables have been discovered. There is a deep understanding of the game mechanics due to the work and tools provided by Hackersquest.

In this study, sometimes bugs and exploits are discovered. Then they are studied and reproduced.

Just because you use a memory editor to change a value doesnt make you a hacker. Have you truly probed the memory space of the game to understand it all? Do you understand the structs in place? Or are you just changing a value someone else has told you to change.

Show due respect to those who are the pioneers. Those you spend the hours upon hours of their time in uncharted waters learning so that we may follow easier.

Hackers are the explorers, they go where no-one has gone before. There are few true hackers in the world.

There are those of you who will take the knowledge hackers discover, and build on those at times. Most who think they are hackers are usually this type. You may are taking roads less traveled, but not undiscovered before you. Your work is often great, and knowledgeable. Just dont forget those who blazed the trail.

Then there are those who just want to use xploits and cheats. You dont discover much on your own, you use others work. You might even plagiarize their work, claiming it for your own.

Tzung (not a hacker)
With respect to those who have discovered.
