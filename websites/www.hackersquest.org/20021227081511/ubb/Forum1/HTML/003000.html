A few comments on Guide accounts. /kill and /zone work on any full guide account. A Senior Guide or a GM has to enable your account for it. Once you are promoted from an apprentice guide to a full guide /zone and /kill work. You can zone to any zone, cshome, planes, etc. Once you have become a full guide your billing is erased untill the account is banned, meaning, that if you lose your guide status, your account will not be billed. I am speaking from experience here. If you lose your guide status, you still have your guide character, still guilded, with all the armor, but you cannot use /kill, or /zone, and the petition queue will always appear blank to you.

While I have never really played the guide character since the guide status was lost I have heard that people have gotten accounts suspended for playing their guide chars after being kicked out of the guide program.

A recent guide article
-----------------

/kill (will KILL ANY MOB or Player)

/zone zonename (teleports you to that zone's succor spot)

/searchcorpse playername (tells you what zones they have corpses in)

/find whatever (targets and shows loc of any player, npc/pc corpse, or npc in the same zone e.g. /find Dain_Frosreaver00, This will show LOC and zone that PCs are in if used inter-zone however cannot target them for obvious reasons, good to find anon GMs)

/goto whatever (will goto your target after using /find or type a name after the command to goto it /goto Innoruuk00's corpse0 for example)

/summon (will summon any player/corpse to you, works through zones as well(except on corpses))

/hideme (hides you, cannot be seen with seeinvis, also makes you anon int he same command)

/who all (same as player command except that it shows info on Anon players and also shows their PID(a number that signifies your account, will also show anon guides, but will not show anon SGs or GMs unless you are one)

/query CHAR PID (Shows soulmarks on that persons account, this is used mostly for notes, The ABUSE database shows ALL warning)

/warn CHAR PID reason (Adds a soulmark that the player was warned, However this does not officialy warn a player, you must also warn them in the ABUSE database wich is a massive database that keeps up with warnings ON all accounts in EQ, can be searched by Character name, Server, Warning type, PID. The abuse database info is sent to the GM of the server the person plays on and if the person has more than 1 warning a suspension or ban is inimate)

/praise (same as warn sort of, Warns and praises are never official as they are just soulmarks, usualy used for keeping notes. For example the GM is offline and you are talking with a player about lost items you can /praise CHAR PID Note: player said he had this..that..the other..blah blah blah for the GM to read)

/Toggle (will not receive tells, only used when you are in a quest)

There are a few more guide commands but those are the main ones, Alot of guide powers are in their armor...
