Just some corrections. If you have someone else making the account (eg, brother, friend, ) they are technically allowed to bid on those items to "pad" the auction. As long as you both agree after not to sell it to him. If you do it yourself that is illegal.

A tip too. Never let a level 1 buy stuff..

Last thing, your not actually violating the user contract when you sell/buy on ebay... There is at least 5 loopholes which allow you too legally. And, if you DO get caught its not anything bad, the WORST thing they can do is ban your account. (OH DEAR GOD NO!!!)
