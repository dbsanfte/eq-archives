Scout's Cape

Kelethin - Crushbone

You say, 'hail Geeda'

Geeda says 'Are not the woods of the Faydarks a lovely sight. Tunare has truly blessed us. It is unfortunate that the Orcs of [Crushbone Citadel] have chosen to invade our land.

You say, 'What crushbone citadel?'

Geeda says 'Within the Greater Faydarks can be found the entrance to Crushbone Citadel, home of the Orcs. They have increased their raids within our land. We do not know why. The High Elder of Kelethin has instructed us to keep tabs on the Orcs movements. Hmmm.. How would you like to [assist the Scouts]

You say, 'I will assist the scouts'

Geeda says 'We trust this operation to High Standing Rogues of the Scouts of Tunare. Easy it may be, but the reward is a Scout Cape. The Scout Cape is meant for Scouts of Tunare only. Have you [contributed to the Scouts cause]

You say, 'I have contributed to the scouts cause'

Geeda says 'So we have heard. Here then. Take this coin. Venture into Crushbone and find our Scout Kelynn. He is posing as a slave to gain information. He tries to work very close to an opening in a cave near the moats. Find this point and wait for him to appear. Give him the coin and he shall give you the Information you are to return to me.

[Receive a coin to give to Kelynn inside of Crushbone Slaves area]

FInd Keylynn inside of the slave caves. He's a relatively rare spawn. Giving him the coin will prompt him to give you a note inreturn. Return note to Geeda the Scout's Cape.
