How to use Safe Fall

Tips submitted by Arie, Naeen, Google and others.
Edited by Zato

Level: 12
Activation Type: Automatic
Usage Delay: None
Advised Practice: 1 pt to prime
Gain Difficulty: Easy to Medium
Cap: 94


It's not how far you fall but how often you fall. An easy way to do it is go into a place with steep cliffs like northern ro. Walk up the side of a pillar or cliff and just
walk forward, should hit about 3-4 times each landing allowing you to increase skill in safefall.

It doesn't matter how far you fall at all. If you jump off a spot where someone without safe fall would take damage, your safe fall will increase. I got mine up to 70 by hopping
off the wall in Misty thicket. I stopped taking damage once I got to about 20.

You will still take damage for long falls, but the amount of damage is greatly reduced depending on your Safe Fall skill.
