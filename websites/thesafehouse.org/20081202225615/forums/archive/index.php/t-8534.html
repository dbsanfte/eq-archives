EZ_the hawnz
12-03-02, 02:08 AM
ya, it's like 5 medals every kill off lords and named.

you should start the quest when you have the head and the 3 items.

tell askr "what problem?". then turn in your head off any of the giants in POS. then run through his quest dialogue (pretty easy to follow). eventually he'll tell you about the pieces he needs from each giant. turn them in to get first flag.

you need:

head - random off any giant
sash - forest giants
bone - desert giants
beard - seashore giants

these are all pretty common, but random drop items off the respective giant tribes.

when you turn all this in, he'll give you more text. you can stop here and do the rest later if you want, as you are flagged for it. the next step requires 2 medallions. the medallions drop off of the lords of each tribe as well as the named.

to kill the lords, you have to kill all the giants in the area and he will become targetable. they all have their own little special abilities, which i'll let you find out on your own. lords seem to always drop 5 medals and 1 loot item. their loot items range from really nice to eh.. the named also seem to drop 5 medals all the time, but are easier to kill.

you need 2 medals to complete the BOT key quest from this point. all the medals are named the same thing, but they're not. you need any 2 different ones. you will need to kill at least 1 of the lords.

possible medal combos.

any lord medal + named medal
any lord medal + either one of the other two lord medals.

it's pretty much a waste to use 2 different lord medals though since it's a waste as the lords are harder, and have set respawn where as named are easy and faster respawn.

go back to askr and tell him "what bastion of thunder?" and he will ask you for 2 medals. give him the 2 medals and you will get another character flag. the first time you zone into BOT, you have to do it from POS. in the desert area there is a spinning cloud in the sky by a waterfall. next to the waterfall is a cave full of KOS frogs.

invis up and go in the cave and there is a small lake with a tornado in the middle of it. on the edge of the lake is a white stucture. click on it to zone into BOT. you will receive another character flag when you do this, which will let you zone into BOT from POT now.

the BOT zone in in POT is right behind the POS zone thing. it's a stone with lightning etched on the side of it.

hope that helps.

Ok, recap here...

A lot of his dialogue is convoluted so if anyone has exactly what you say to him, post it here if ya could please. Not even the info on Allakhazam's was right last I looked.

Anyways, to start the quest, bring Askr a Storm Giant Head, which drops from any giant in PoStorms.

When you give him the head you will get a 4-slot bag in which you combine a Sash (from Forest Giants), Bone (from Desert Giants) and Beard (from Coastal Giants). After ya hit the combine button on the pack and hand it to Askr, you will get yet another pack (2-slot if I remember correctly).

Now take a fairly good raid force and head towards any of those same areas you got the beard, sash and bone from and kill things until named giants spawn. The Giants you need have 'Lord' in their title (i.e.- Jabawhatzhisnuts, Lord of the Srependi). You need a total of TWO medallions from any of the three Giant forts there. Yes they are lore, but since you need them from different camps, you should never have to worry about getting a duplicate. As far as I was able to tell, the Lords can spawn at any of the three encampments. I myself got my medallions from the Forest and Desert lords (mainly because fighting those annoying coastal giants is annoying with how buggy they are and how they make ya FD in the middle of the fight with their procs).

Combine those two medallions into the pack again and hand them back to Askr to get a necklace. Put it on then zone into BoT from WITHIN PoStorms there (head into the center area with the tornadoes and frogs). You must enter BoT from this point the first time; after that you can use the PoT zonein outside.
