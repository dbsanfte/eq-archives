Marble Mortar

# Produces Master sketch which does ?

Master Sketch is a re-useable poison component used for several 1st-tier and 2nd-tier poisons.


GMAVial is discussed here: www.eqtraders.com/secrets...ophies.htm
Basically it's an Artisan's Seal combined with a Sealed Vial in Mortar &amp; Pestle.

GMASeal requires GMAV, Eyerazzia and the following 8 old-world poisons: Mage's Bane, Soul Burn, Susceptible Essence, Spirit of Sloth, Warlord's Rage, Rending Swiftness, Twisting Disorientation, Iron Dimethy
GMASeal produces Coffin Bottles, which are NO DROP LORE 10-slot containers than are used to make 5-charge and 10-charge poisons. The Coffin Bottle is used up in this combine, it will disappear (but you can always summon new ones).
A Coffin Bottle is used to get on of the 4 sketches for the Master Sketch. Veteran ShadowCross Bladesong
