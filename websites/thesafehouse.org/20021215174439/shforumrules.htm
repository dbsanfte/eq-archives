The Safehouse was founded around its Forums. They are a great place for both knowledge transfer and social interaction. Friendships have been born here and memories forged that some of us will never forget.We pride ourselves in the unique community we have all built, and because of that we have become very protective of it. In hopes of preserving what some call a "second home", we ask you to follow a few simple rules.

General rules:

Watch your language. The Safehouse is visited by people of all ages and backgrounds, please keep it clean. This includes religious swear words.

Please post in the appropriate forum.Don't be upset if we move your post to a more appropriate forum. We vary rarely delete posts. In the event you find your post “missing”, please attempt to locate it in what might be a more appropriate forum.

Avoid personal attacks. Debate opinions, not the people behind them. This includes posting images as a representation of your attack.

The Moderators here at the Safehouse, have a monumental task watching these forums. Due the vast number of members and the incredible number of posts per day, we sometimes do not manage to immediately catch all rule violations. Please try and avoid taking matters into your own hands when you see someone violating any of the rules. We would respectfully request you simply email us at shmods@thesafehouse.org and we will take action ASAP.

The Safehouse Staffs decisions on locking threads or banning members is final. If you have concerns regarding in incident, please feel free to discuss the matter with us via email. We can be contacted individually or through the shmods@thesafehouse.org mailing list. Do not create a new topic simply for the purposes of questioning a moderators decision.

Do not post links to pornographic or other inappropriate sites.

Do not use the Safehouse forums for advertising purposes.

"Spamming" is not allowed in any fashion. The repeated posting of the same topic across multiple forums, or even within the same forum qualifies as “Spam”. This also includes posting simply for the sake of increasing your post count, bumping threads, or posting “I like Pie” 1000 times.

If you receive a week ban do not rely on us to remember when its up. It is your responsibility to send us an email once your ban is over. Simply send an email to the shmods mail list stating your ban is over and we will remove it.

Attempting to bypass a temporary ban by accessing the forums with another account will result in a perma ban.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Above all, DON’T BE A DINK!!
