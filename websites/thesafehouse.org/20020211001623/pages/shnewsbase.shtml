Event at Dallas Fan Faire! Submited on Friday, January 18, 2002  posted by  Zato

This just in from Cindy...

Announcing The Intel Arm Yourself for Adventure Game Room.

Intel will be sponsoring a player room for the Dallas Fan Faire where you and your friends can play EQ together! Finally, you can have a chance to play together in the same room! Not only can you play with your friends, but you'll also get to check out some great top end
Intel based PCs.

You will be able to sign up for time in the computer room when you check in. Space will be allotted on a first come, first serve basis. In addition, the guild with the greatest number of members present
will be given a prime time spot so they can all play together!

If you plan to take advantage of this opportunity, please be sure your characters are where you wish them to before you come to the Fan Faire. Playtime will be somewhat limited and it would be best if you
did not have to waste your time travelling to meet up with your friends!

Our thanks to Intel for bringing this new and exciting activity to the Fan Faire!

Remember that registration for the Fan Faire closes on Monday, January 21st. There are still some spots left for you to attend!
Visit http://everquest.station.sony.com/fanfaire/ for more information!

Cindy Archuleta
Community Relations Manager
Sony Online Entertainment
