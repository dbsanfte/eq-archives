    Berserkers and Rogues need to be Equals (DPS wise)

    10-04-05, 02:32 PM
    It's my belief that both classes have very little raid utility. Up until DoD Berserkers had ZERO raid utility. In fact a raid/guild leader would have to be either a very kind person, curious, or just plain stupid to recruit a Berserker over a rogue (BEFORE DOD), or even a wizard.

    In DoD my class got some raid utility, as little as it is...it's still something to us. We appreciate anything we can get these days. Many a berserker has been turned away from high end raiding guilds, being told they didn't need any Berserkers, period. And they were right, at the time.

    In the end it almost seems as if the Dev team has heard our plea for help and wants us to be closer then ever to rogue DPS, being that we are supposed to be a pure DPS class.

    I truly hope that rogues and Berserkers play on the same level of DPS up until the point where the person behind the toon can either cause the character to do more, or less, DPS. So that the skill of the player takes over and puts them out in front, or behind. Prior to that, and gear selection, we should be on the same level...or so I would hope.

    It may be hard after all these years of being top dog for your rogues but I don't think it is a bad idea in the end. Call me biased if you wish but prior to these recent changes, lets face it, we brought nothing but SECOND BEST dps to the raid scene. Now, depending on how you play your Berserker, and how the encounter goes...we can bring either the #1 dps, or #2 like we always have been.
