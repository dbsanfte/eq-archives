The Safehouse Resource Pages

 

The Safehouse Resource pages have been totaly revamped. Some of the new features include:

  • Drop down navigation menus for the resource pages.
  • Searchable Armor database.
  • Searchable Weapons database.
  • Sort options in the database to sort armor and weapons by AC,Weight, Damage, and location.
  • Faster access to guides.
  • More content for your rogueish pleasure.
