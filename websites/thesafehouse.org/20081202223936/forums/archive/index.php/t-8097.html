I just finished my quest for the Grandmaster Assassin's Seal. Finally, I can make multi-dose poisons. Yay!

However, I'm very displeased that this item is a light source. Yeah, the particle effect is nice. But the fact of the matter is that lightsources create a halo of light around you, whether you're hidden or not, thus revealing your location. It bothers me from a roleplay point of view, as well as from a practical one. It would *really* bother me if I were on a PvP server.

I've been keeping my GM Vial in my ranged slot most of the time lately, for the stats. If I don't need throwing/archery, and don't need the slot for resists, the strength and dex are nice to have in combat. And I've enjoyed the freedom of not having to swap in gear for charisma any more, every time I deal with a merchant.

I really don't want to have to remake my GMAV and bank my Seal. I like having it as a mark of my accomplishment, as well as for the stat benefits. But for me, the lightsource effect is too big of a sacrifice; I won't keep it equipped except when I need it, while the lightsource remains.

Is anyone else bothered by this? If so, please speak up. Perhaps the people at Verant will be willing to change it, if enough people feel the same as I. After all, Verant listened when we asked to have the lightsource effect removed from the Burning Rapier, a long long time ago. Bayler - 59th rank Swashbuckler - Xev
