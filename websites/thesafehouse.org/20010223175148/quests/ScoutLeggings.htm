Scout's Leggings

Kelethin - Dagnor's Cauldron - Qeynos

Previous Quest: Scout's Cape

You say, 'What is the scout silvermesh legging'

Tylfon says 'Scout Silvermesh Leggings are part of the garb of the Scouts of Tunare. Other than greater protection in battle, they offer a protection against magic and their unique powers boost the wearer's agility. They are meant for the Scouts only and as such are not just given away. Are you [willing to earn the leggings].

You say, 'I am willing to earn the leggings'

Tylfon says 'A former scout named Faldor Hendrys has stolen our [Gem of Tunare]. He has fled Faydwer and we have been unable to track him down. Perhaps if you go and speak with his brother [Elmion Hendrys] and ask him of Faldor.

You say, 'What gem of tunare'

Tylfon says 'The bright green Gem of Tunare was discovered by the Scouts of Tunare in a trunk of a great tree. The lightning streaked down and split the tree in two and there was the gem, Tunare's gift to the people of Kelethin. It has no magical properties, but it represents the glory of Tunare. It appears as a small Emerald Shard. Alas, now it has been stolen by Faldor Hendrys and only his brother [Elmion Hendrys] could know of his whereabouts.

You say, 'Who is Elmion Hendrys'

Tylfon says 'Seek out the Feir'Dal, Elmion. He was last heard telling the local bar patrons that he was off to do some adventuring at the lake near the Estate. What that is, I do not know.

[Find Faldor in Dagnor's Cauldron]

You say, 'hail Elmion Hendrys'

Elmion Hendrys says 'How are? Please rest. Have you [been wounded by the beasts] of this region?

You say, 'Who is Faldor?'

Elmion Hendrys says 'Ha!! You seek my foul brother, Faldor Hendrys?!! He is my half brother and has left to live with the vile half of his ancestry in Qeynos. No doubt he has decided to lend his talents to the local thieves guild. Good luck finding him.

[Find Faldor Hendrys in Crow's bar on a relatively rare spawn]

You say, 'hail Faldor Hendrys'

Faldor Hendrys says 'What do you want?!! Can't you see I am busy drinking here? You don't even look like you belong here. You can have my seat when I am good and ready to leave, which won't be for a while yet If I were you I would get lost before I got myself hurt.

[Attack and kill Faldor Hendrys]

Faldor Hendrys says 'That's it swine! You are outta here!

You have slain Faldor Hendrys!
Your faction standing with QeynosCitizen got worse

[Loot a Emerald Shard]

[Return the emerald shard to Tylfon]

Tylfon says 'You have done well my friend. The elves of Kelethin are grateful. Please take the Scout Silvermesh Leggings as a gift. Every Scout of Tunare shall find them useful. Serve the forest well.

Your faction standing with TunaresScouts got better
You gain experience!!
