Book: Miner Maters Guide to Mineral Toxicology

LEAD PEROXIDE

This lead powder was discovered by a fortunate accident. While experimenting with LEAD SULPHIDES, I spilled a bit of my favorite dwarven brew, the TUMPY TONIC into my mortar and pestle. Much to my surprise the result was a yellow lead that is absorbed through the flesh and attacks the bloodstream much quicker than other forms of lead. The result of contact with this toxin is severe head pains and nervous agitation.
