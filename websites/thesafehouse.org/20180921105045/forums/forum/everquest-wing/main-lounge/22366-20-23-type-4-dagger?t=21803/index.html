                          if it is Lannal the Beast Tamer in NC, then below are the significant notes about the encounter from here and here. Basically it is a ring event triggered by the death of Lannal, with another named later on that drops the loot.


                          ----------------


                          "First, I had a group plus 1.. included me, sk, cleric botted, druid, bard, wiz, rogue. We killed the beast tamer first, nothing happeend. Then we pulled and kill both guards seperate. After that, this is the emote I got

                          [Sat Jan 01 17:00:29 2005] A voice bellows from deep inside the compound, 'Quickly, release the bazu! Release the beasts!'

                          At that point, it was hard to see inside, so I sent in the rogue to look inside the area. A beast handler (dragorn model) came out to the entrane and we beat him down quickly, but then two of the bazu's came out. We could not get them below 98%, their regen was too high. They hit upwards of 1500 and rampaged.

                          After we messed around with the bazu for a few minutes, this is the emote that we got

                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.
                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.
                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.
                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.
                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.
                          [Sat Jan 01 17:03:06 2005] The bazu have been freed. You have failed.

                          So, I talked to the rogue a bit, and he said that there was a few beast handlers back there, and a dragorn that was releasing the bazu's, and I think that he was referring to the controllers. So, suggesting that you kill the controllers and the beast handlers as soon as you can. I don't know how many you will honestly need for this, but it looks like something that's going to need a fairly good amount.

                          At the end, I really have no clue how many of each type of mob was up, other than there were a total of 12 "A Rampaging Bazu". I think prob up to maybe six each of beast handlers and controllers, too hard to know for sure right now though. What I suggest maybe is have the raid force pull the final mob before the event triggers into the area behind the beast tamer, and then finish whatever mob is left off and then immediately agro whatever you can in the area, especially the beast handlers and the controllers. When the beast handler ran over to the entrance (we were standing outside of it) I agroed it using Nihil, and it took a second for it to come over. I did have the bard with me and no clue if he tried to mezz it, but it wasn't an automatic thing. This event seems to be something that needs to be done quickly, and people have to be in the right spots to agro stuff.

                          ... I looked up some info and no one mentioned anything about it, other than the fact that when they killed the tamer, they had an empty corpse."


                          ----------------


                          "Lannal the beast tamer in NC. This might be streching things a little, but to my knowledge from what Ive been able to find posted anywhere, this event has never been completed, people talk about killing him and nothing happens, .... Took about 25 people there, killed the gargantuan guardians then killed Lannal the beast tamer. BOOM. big fat emote, RELEASE THE BEASTS. Dragorn controlers and some other dragorn poped, bunch of them, they started running around and releaseing all the chained bazus that are back behind Lannal the beast tamer. The bazus seemed unkillable, so we focused on the dragorns, we killed quite a few of them, but with only 25 to many had run around releaseing bazus. All the bazus that were releases rampaged. High hit from rampage on me was about 1500. Im hopeing to get a full raid force. 54ish there to try it again.


                          ----------------

That is as far as I have gotten. Would probly need a couple of groups been awhile since I have tried this but a lot of dragorns try to run into castle that you have to agro or else they will just run right by you."
