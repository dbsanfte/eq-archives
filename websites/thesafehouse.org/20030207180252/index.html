This morning at approximately 7am Eastern time, about 140 Hibernians on the Guinevere server banded together, broke into Caer Excalibur and walked off with Albion’s relic, the Scabbard of Excalibur. How to become a Jedi
2003-02-01 04:34:47
Story by - Ruccus


Over at the Star Wars: Galaxies website, they've gone into more detail in their FAQ as to what it will take to become a Jedi.

Instead of your main character becoming "force sensitive", certain actions of your main character will open up a new character generation slot. A new character created in that character generation slot will be force sensitive.

These force sensitive characters will suffer permanent death after they are killed a certain number of times (I read it as three). When force sensitive characters reach "Jedi Master" status, they do not permanently die, but become "blue glowies" upon death. You will not lose the force sensitive character generation slot, but you will have to create a new character from scratch if your character perma-dies.

They're promising that the Jedi path will be by far the hardest path in the game. As developer Holocron wrote on the SW:G forum, "Most of you will never make it to blue glowy. You'll die. Trust me. If we do it right, most of you will never make it to Padawan."
