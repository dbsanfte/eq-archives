          My guild spent a few days with a few groups running through these...
          It was actually a lot of fun especially once we got the rhythm going..

          It's best to get a grp of 6 togteher and do each mission 6 times.
          as you only get one piece of the seal each mission.

          Also, in the second dain mission a nice +65 HP aug drops with +5 to stats.... on Dain 3.. it's a 65 HP with +5 to saves.... a ring and a shield drop in the first one... a melee mask in second and a caster mask in the third... sorry i dont have the links or info atm


          On the third mission.... leave the dain and a cleric at the general guy... have the dain set hotkeys for all 4 armies to attack/retreat

          Have the other pally pull the giants to the awaiting dwarf army ... the 4 others in grp kill named when the giants are close have dain send troops to attack. soon as giants dead retreat. Dain and cleric stay there b/c on a rare occasion a runner will spawn and make a beeline for thurg.. if he makes it you lose.. also this keeps dain out of harms way
