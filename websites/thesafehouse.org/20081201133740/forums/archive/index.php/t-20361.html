Bleach is another of your typical Shounen Jump anime. The manga runs in Shounen Jump Weekly in Japan. It has been running for the last year, quickly approaching it's 52nd episode. It is very similar to Naruto and One Piece, but is the more action-oriented of the three.

There are more fights in this series than the other two, and it has more emphasis on sword fights rather than hand to hand / special ability combat of One Piece and the Ninja Technique combat of Naruto. This allows for some very cool scenes.

Of the three, Bleach has the most "so... cool...." factor. As in, the author likes to draw everyone in very awesome poses, impratical as they may be. The fights are intense and heavily animated. The spirit force gives a very neat effect on the fights and brings the intensity to a new level.

There is no real running story in Bleach, so it does not look like it will last very long. The Anime is already very close to the manga, which does not bode well in general.

But, the anime is still VERY worth checking out. It's a fun action show for the guys to watch (and girls too, if you're into that). Does not take much brain work.

There are many places to get Bleach, the best place to follow it would be Animesuki, as it is still unlicensed (not for long, most likely, unless people went broke licensing One Piece and Naruto).
