Bard Epic Quest "Singing Short Sword"
  1. Baldric Slezaf asks you for 4 items
    1. Page 24 Top and Bottom (actually in two halves)
    2. Page 25
    3. Lute made by Forper Fizfla.

  2. To get top part page 24 go to West Karana and see Konia Swiftfoot. He gives you a torch and starts a race. Take the torch to Fajio Knejo in Misty Thicket (close to the wall guards). He gives you another torch. Then take that torch to Andad Filla in South Ro (close to innothule zone) who gives you another torch. Then take that torch to Misty Tekchita in Lake Rathe (by arena zone). This will get you a ring. Finally, take the ring back to Konia Swiftfoot and you will get the top part of page 24

  3. To get the bottom half of you need to give Konia Swiftfoot Mahlin's Bongos. Talk to Braenar Swiftsong. He will give you an invoice after you ask "what doll". Go to Solusek A and give the gnome vendor, Marfen the invoice and you will recieve a doll. Take the doll to the Estate of Unrest and give the doll to Serra, who is next to the gnomish speulunker. Serra will give you a note. Then take the note from Serra back to Braenar, who will give a new note for Maligar. Take the note from Braenar to Maligar. Maligar's Enraged Doppleganger will attack at that point (bring a group). Kill the Doppleganger and loot his head. Take the head back to Braenar and recieve "Mahlin's Bongo's". Take the Bongo's to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana to get the bottom of Page 24

  4. To get page 25 go to South Karana and talk to the woodelf bard Kelkin Mekia. She wants 3 items: 1) Guts of a named chromatic drake in skyfire (Edrik the Old) 2) guts from the new named wrum in Burning Woods (Nezekezena); and 3) guts form the Huge Oynx Drake in Rathe Mountains (Blackwing) Give these to her and you will get page 25

  5. Now you need Forpar's Lute. This is rewarded as head, neck, body and strings. First you need to get horn from Ocean of Tears (Alluring horn from Quag Maelstrom). Take the alluring horn from Ocean of Tears to a half elf bard in Butcherblock, by the docks (Vedico). Take the note she gives you to Forpar. He then gives you a note and a list of items he needs:
    • Backbone of an Ancient Fishman - Phnagel's Spine from Kedge Keep
    • Amalgym Tentacle - Amygdalan Tendril from Plane of Fear
    • Petrified skull of a Lycanthrope - petrified werewolf skull from Karnor on Drolvarg Warlord

     

  6. Take all 3 items and the note back to Forpar give them to him and he gives you back the Head and Neck of the Lute and the quest for the body, which requires the following items:
    • Large red Dragon Scale (Talendor of Skyfire's scale works. Nagafen's scale also works)
    • Large white Dragon Scale (Gorenaire of Dreadlands works, Vox's scale also works)
    • Two Metal bits (smithed metal bits)
    • Finally, get the strings for the Lute - Trakanon Gut Strings. These drop from Trakanon himself.

     

  7. Once you have all four, give following to Foplar to recieve the mystical lute:
    • Head and neck
    • Body
    • Gut strings

     

  8. You are almost there.To get your singing short sword give following items to Baldric Slezaf:
    • Top of page 24
    • Bottom of page 24
    • Page 25
    • Mystical lute from Foplar

He will reward you with the singing short sword.

Cleric Epic Quest " Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh"

 

Head off to Lake Rathe, and talk to Schmendrick Lavawalker [3600, 0]. He spawns near the cabin where the Boots of the Reliant quest npc is. He will tell you to get him Lord Burgurgle's Crown.

Now get a small group and head into the underwater tower below the Erudite Isle, in Lake Rathe. Enter the Deepwater Goblin Tower [2800, 150] and head to the top. Lord Burgurgle spawns on the top floor, in the middle and has a placeholder. Kill him to loot Lord Burgurgle's Crown.

Head back and hand Schmendrick the King Burgurgle's Crown, and you will receive the Oil of Fennin Ro. Natasha Whitewater will then spawn nearby and she will proceed to kill Schmendrick. Once Schmendrick dies a Spirit of Flame will spawn in his place. Kill the Spirit of Flame and you will receive the Damaged Goblin Crown. Hand this crown to Natasha and you will be rewarded with the Ornate Seashell.

Now head off to Timorous Deep and talk to Omat Vastsea [-12000,-200]. Hand him the Ornate Seashell, and he will give you instructions on killing the Plasmatic Priest, which spawns in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He will then hand you the Coral Statue of Tarew.

Now head off to the Temple of Solusek Ro and locate the room with the throne [326,38]. At 9am a Seeker will spawn on the left. Say the following line to make sure it is the correct spawn, "what plasmatic priest". Hand him the Coral Statue of Tarew, this will cause the Plasmatic Priest to spawn in the seekers place. Kill him and you can loot the Bloodstained Plasmatic Priest Robe. He uses Tremor and AoE spells so make sure you pull him to zone, otherwise the other npc's will kill him.

Next head over to Solusek's Eye to hunt down and kill Lord Gimblox who spawns near the King room [-800, -366]. His placeholder is a Large Fire Goblin. Once you kill the Lord, you can then loot the Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring. Head back to Omat and hand him the Bloodstained Plasmatic Priest Robe. He will reward you with the Orb of Frozen Water and once again Natasha Whitewater spawn. Hand her Lord Giblok's Signet Ring and she will give you more quest details and will then hand you another Ornate Seashell (which when identified is shown as Ornate Seashell II).

Now we head off to the Burning Woods to talk to Naxton Deepwater. Hand him the second Ornate Seashell and he will in turn hand you back the Message to Natasha. This will also cause Ixiblat Fer to spawn. He will tell you to kill the Ixiblat Fer and to obtain his Scepter. He is a God type mob and will require many groups to take him down. Charming some or the wandering wurms and using Fire Elementals works well, as well as pulling him to the Skyfire zone to fight him is suggested. Kill this elemental and loot the Scepter of Ixblat Fer.

Next we head off to Chardok and search out Overking Bathezed. He is the boss of this dungeon and uses AoE spells which will aggro mobs in rooms above him causing many trains. Make sure the room above is empty before attacking him. Kill him to obtain the Singed Scroll. Now head back to Omat Vastsea and hand him the Scepter of Ixiblat Fer and the Singed Scroll. He will reward you with the Orb of Clear Water, and will ask you to head over to Lord Nagafen's Lair to kill Zordak Ragefire and return with his heart. Natasha Deepwater will spawn once again and if you hand her the Message to Natasha she will give you a Shimmering Pearl which she tells you to hand to Zordak Ragefire to block some of his powers.

Head off to Lord Nagafen's Lair and locate Zardok. Not much is certain on how to make Zordak spawn, but make sure that all Fire Giants are dead, as well as Lord Nagafen. Once you locate Zordak bring him to Nagy's lair and hand him the Shimmering Pearl, which will cause him to attack you. Kill him and he will spawn once again into a second form, Zordakalicus Ragefire, a nice red dragon. Kill this beast and you will be able to loot the Heart of Zordak Ragefire.

Bring this heart back to Omat and he will reward you with the Orb of Vapor. This will cause Jhassad Oceanson to spawn. Run over and hand him all three orbs that you have received so far, Orb of Frozen Water, Orb of Clear Water, and the Orb of Vapor and he will hand you back the Orb of the Triumvirate. An Avatar of Water will spawn nearby, it does not respond to hails, nor any commands. Just hand it the Orb of the Triumvirate and you will be rewarded with the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh.

Druid Epic Quest "Nature Walkers Scimitar"

The Start

- Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods: ask "what action" and you will get a note. if not, then your faction is too low. Faction can be built up fast by doing the muffin-quest in Freeport

- Faelin Bloodbriar in Greater Faydark: give her the note and you receive a ring. Faelin roams the northern part of the zone near Crushbone. She is a rare spawn and can have orcs as placeholders. After giving her the note she depops.

- Giz X'Tin Kithikor: give him the ring, get a dark metal coin. Giz roams along the path. After u hand him the ring a lvl 50 Darkelf Reaver spawns and attacks but u can run away with SoW.

- Go back to Telin Darkforest and give him the Coin. He will give it back to you as worn metal coin. He tells you to find Athele.

- Athele in East Karanas: give her the Coin and receive a worn amulet. give this amulet to Sionae, then to Nuien and finaly to Teloa. The last step is the trigger for the Dark Elf Corruptor and two Darkwlf Reavers (all about lvl 50) to spawn.

- Find the Corruptor and kill him fast He and the 2 reavers spawn on top of the hill east of the river (loc -1500, -1000). He will depop after 10 seconds if not attacked. The 2 Reavers will head to the 4 Druids and kill them. You dont need to kill the Reavers. Just kill the corruptor and loot the tome. If the Druids die, never mind....Athele spawns again in about 1 hour. Give Athele the tome and get an earthstained Note. Bring this to "she who walks the land in service blabla..."

- Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket is this wanted women that wanders the zone. Give her the earthstained note and she will tell you that she needs a mixture. She will give you her mixing bowl. It is a Container so you need a free slot in the Inventory. She also mentions some kind of ancient bowl that she needs.

The Hardened Mixture

- You must forage 4 items that you have to combine in Ella's mixing bowl.

- Frozen Tundra Root from Everfrost Peaks

- Sweetened Mudroots from Misty Thicket

- Speckled Mushrooms from Innothule Swamp

- Heartfruit from Greater Faydark

- This can take several hours. Combine those 4 items in the bowl and you get a Hardened -Mixture.

The Runed Bowl

- Go to the Chess Island (succor will take you there when you have enterd the zone) in Timorous Deep and ask Alrik Farsight
"how do you make an ancient bowl?". He gives you a crushed pot and sends you to Felwithe.

- Talk to Farios Elianos in Felwithe south. If he dont respond to you then your faction with Keepers of the Art is too low. You can build up faction fast with the batwing-quest in Felwithe south. If Farios talks to you, give him the pot and you receive a grocery list. He will give you the receipt for the bowl if you do a job for him.

- Bring this grocery list to Merchant Nora in Felwithe north and she gives you in return a bag of Provisions. Bring it to Farios and he gives you the receipt for the ancient bowl.

- Head back to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep . He will give you an ancient pattern when you give him the receipt.

- The pattern says that you have to combine it with a piece of echanted clay and platinum speckled powder in a pottery wheel. NO skill in pottery required.

- Platinum speckled powder can be obtained through another subquest. Forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie and give it to Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glades. The old wooden painting that you receive has to be brought to the human skeleton that roams the western part of Frontier Mountains. Head back with the silver chained locket you got from the skeleton to Surefall Galdes and give it to Niera Farbreeze. She hands you the platinum speckled pouder.

- Enchanted clay can be obtained through Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim's Everhot Forge. If you ask him "what mud" and "do it for me" he will tell you that he would like to have a colored axe for his service. This is a Jade Reaver that can be found in the City of Mist. Hand him the axe and you get the enchanted clay. I think that if you are kos to the dwarfs then you can do this part invisible. Just go to the Everhot Forge and hand Kinlo a Jade Reaver to get the clay.

- Now combine the pattern, clay and powder in any pottery wheel to make the Rune Crested Bowl

The Stones

- Go Back to Misty Thicket and hand Ella Foodcrafter the hardened mixture and the rune crested bowl. She will make for you a softly glowing stone from it.

- Ella tells you to find one such as yourself. For the Druids this means to seek out Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie.

- Ask her "are you one such as myself?", "what answers?" and "what about Venril Sathir?". She tells you to bring her the stone you already have from Ella and the gem that can be found on Venril Sathir.

- Kill Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle and loot the gem he drops. Venril is a very tough level 55 Necromancer that can only be beaten by very strong guilds.

- Give the gem and the softly glowing stone to Foloal Stromforest and get a warm pulsating stone. Now this is the time where the quest splits up into the Druid and the Ranger parts. Foloal tells you to take the stone and walk your path.

- The Druid's path leads back to Ealla in Misty Thicket. Give her the stone and you will get the Elaborate Scimitar (10/30 9wis 10mana 5sv poison and disease)...now you have one of the four final pieces. She asks you to collect the Cleansed Spirits of Antonica, Faydwer and Kunark and hand them along with the scimitar to Xanuusus.

The Cleansed Spirits

- The withered treant in North Karana tells you to find the corruption in the land. He means the Corrupted Mammoth (below lvl 30) in Everfrost Peaks (pops near Permafrost, Mammoth Calfs are PH), the Corrupted Aquagoblin Shaman (mid lvl 30) in Lake Rathe (PH are the sidespawns on top of the underwater-tower. - Give the 3 items looted from them to the treant to receive the treant heart. Go to Yeka Ias in South Karanas and give him the heart. He will give you the Cleansed Spirit of Antonica.

- The Unicorn will give you a gleaming horn for the 3 items. This has to be handed to the gnome Silox Azrix in Ak'anon to get the Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer. Enchanter Epic Quest

 

You: 'hail Stofo Olan'
Stofo Olan: 'Good day, I have discovered something truly wonderful! If I only had the materials required so I can copy my notes and send them to my teacher.'

You: 'what materials?'
Stofo Olan: 'The materials I need are a mechanical pen, ink of the dark, and white paper. Bring me those and I will give you a copy of this information.'

You: 'who is your teacher?'
Stofo Olan: 'My teacher is Jeb Lumsed. He was last seen in the regions of the newly discovered lands. If you wish to find him that is where you must seek him out.'

You need to get 3 items, and return them to Stofo, who will then give you Copy of Notes which is needed by Jeb Lemused to get to the meat of the Epic Enchanter quest.

The Ink of the Dark

In Qeynos, talk to Reania Jukle

You: 'what is the ink of the dark?'
Reania Jukle: 'Ink of the Dark, you say? That isn't an everyday item, you know. In fact i can't remember the last time someone requested it. I have given up keeping any here with me. You are going to need to find your own supply now. Sorry.'

You: 'where can I find the ink of the dark?
Reania Jukle says: 'The ink is the blood of a dark scribe. Tempt him and give him this vial. He should cooperate.'
You recieve an Empty Ink Vial.

If you charm the Ghoul Scribe in Lower Guk, and hand him the vial, you will recieve the Ink of the Dark.

A Mechanical Pen

In Akanon talk to Rilgor Plegnog

You: 'What do you know about a mechanical pen?'
Rilgor Plegnog says: 'Why do you want one of those contraptions. You will spend more time repairing it then you will writing with it. But if you insist on getting one, you are going to have to do a small favor.'
Rilgor Plegnog tugs at your robe and grins evilly. Mighty nice outfit you have there. Sure beats these drab robes us three are wearing. Hmm...my favorite color is purple, in fact a shiny metallic purple would do nicely. How bad did you want that pen?'

You give Rilgor Plegnog a Shiny Metallic Robe.
Rilgor Plegnog says: 'Very nice!! It is perfect! Here take this pen. Have fun with it.'
You recieve A Mechanical Pen.

A White Paper

In West Karana, talk to Chrisln Baker

You: 'what writing materials'

You turn in the Paper and Quill
Chrislin Baker: gives you a hug. 'Thank you so much. I did some cleaning after you left and found this behind a barrel. It may have been left behind by the person who took my materials. You can have it.'

Receiving the sash will spawn Thracken Griften, a level 55 very agressive Bandit at around loc 800,-11570. You need to kill him to get the white paper.

Paladin Epic Quest "Fiery Defender"

 

To complete this quest you will need to locate three pieces of armor, and then three items to purify the armor, so that you may be able to release a tormented soul and lay him to rest. The three pieces of armor you will need are the Armor, Sword and Shield. The three items of purification are the Light, Gem and Water.

Head off to Kaladim and talk to Jark who can be found near the Mines. Tell him "some" and he'll tell you the he's waiting for his dinner. Tell him the "I will get your dinner" and he will tell you to run and find Nella Stonebraids. She is standing right outside the Temple. Tell her about the "dinner" and she will hand you Jark's Dinner. Now run back to Jark and hand him his meal. He will tell you that he just found a very tiny pure gem and he would like to give it to you for showing him kindness. Receive the Gem.

Now we travel off to Freeport. There is a peasant woman who can be found near the Monk guild in West Freeport. If you approach her and say "what water". She will tell you that she has to bring some water to her brother Joshua. Tell her "I will take it" and she will hand you a Bucket of Water. Bring this bucket to Joshua, who can be found in the Bakery near the Monk guild. He will in turn hand you back the Pure Water. The Tainted Darksteel Breastplate is dropped by a Thought Corrupter in the Plane of Hate.

The Tainted Sword is dropped by the Keeper of Tombs who lives in the Hole. The Tainted Shield is dropped by Kirak Vil, who can be found in Nektulos Forest.

Now head off to Erudin and talk to Reklon Gnallen who can be found in the Temple of Quellious. Hand him the Tainted Sword and the Pure Water, and he will bless the items and return the Gleaming Crested Sword to you. Next you will have to travel to Felwithe and talk to Elie the Pure. Hand her the Tainted Shield and she will return to you the Gleaming Crested Shield. The last step is to head back over to Erudin. Talk to Reklon Gnallen again and this time hand him the Tainted Breastplate and the Pure Crystal. He will hand you back the Gleaming Breastplate. Now hand the Gleaming Shield, Gleaming Crested Sword and the Gleaming Breastplate back to Reklon and he will reward you with the Mark of Atonement.

Now head to the Plane of Fear and talk to Irak Altil and hand him the Fiery Avenger and the Mark of Atonement and you will be rewarded with the Fiery Defender.

Shadow Knight Epic Quest

 

This quest begins in the Overthere and results in the shadowknight's particle weapon.

This quest begins at an NPC named Kurron Ni who spawns near the docks in Fort Danak. This NPC is a rare spawn so don't be discouraged if he is not there the first few times you check for them.

Do not worry if you are killed on sight at the fort. Kurron spawns near the docks and you will be able to swim or invis yourself to get to him. He is also far enough away from other NPC's that you do not have to worry about them aggroing you when you are there.

If you ask him what he seeks(do not ask him what his mission is or he will attack you) and then agree to his terms(tell him "I agree") then he will tell you that he seeks three pieces of darkforge armor(helm, chest and leggings) and 900pp.

Before turning in these items you need to gather up a good party and have them accompany you. Two groups would be best.

Handing these items in to him will simply gain a raise in TrueSpirit faction and he will say that his mission is nearly complete. He will reward you with no items. If you ask him what mission he will tell you of his treachery and then attack you.

At this point you must kill him. Upon killing him you will find a "Note to Duriek" on his corpse. You will need this for the next portion of the quest.

NOTE: You MUST first hand in these items or he will not drop the note. This has been tested and confirmed.

Duriek can be found overlooking the newbie area in Paineel. He is upstairs in the newbie area looking out through the glass. If you hand him the note he will tell you a story of his apprentice. He will also tell you to "take this for your efforts." but no item will be given. I'm not sure if this is broken or not. I have contacted Verant about it with no response. Someone has told me that Verant responded to this on a public board saying that no item is supposed to be given but I was never able to find the thread. Either way you receive TrueSpirit faction and are able to continue the quest.

If you ask him what apprentice he will give you further background. Tell him that you will take his apprentice's place and he will ask you to prove your worth first. Duriek is aging and is in need of a cough elixir. This is obtained via a side quest in Neriak.

Upon returning the cough elixir to Duriek he will inform you that he was trying to create a corrupted ghoulbane. Unfortunately he is no condition to gather the components. His previous apprentice was searching for them when he met his demise.

He needs you to find a tome for him. This tome can be found in The Hole on Ratman Guards. The best place to find these guards is by taking a left upon entering the first fortress and then take another left and pass down past the pool. Between this area and the old city beneath it Ratman Guards spawn fairly commonly as patrollers.

Beware that these rats are extremely tough. They hit for 370s, are very magic resistant and have a ton of hit points. The tome is a rare drop on them so you are likely going to have to kill several of them to obtain one. Our first encounter with one we had 7 people and no deaths but we had a high level party and Turgur's stuck on the first attempt. In later raids we had as many as 25 people and might lose 5-8 people each attempt. A lot of it depends on if you can get Turgurs or another speed reducing spell to stick early.

Handing the Tome to Duriek will grant another faction adjustment and will reveal what items he will need to complete the corrupted ghoulbane. He requires:

1) Blade of Abrogation from the Plane of Air
2) Soul Leech from Cazic Thule in the Plane of Fear
3) Ghoulbane from upper guk.
4) Decrepit Sheath from a Qeynos side quest.

Returning this items to him will result in a corrupted ghoulbane(dmg 16 delay 34 effect dismiss undead sk only 1hslash) and will send you to seek out Lhranc in the City of Mist. This item is not much use to a shadowknight of this level but it is required for the next portion of the quest.

Lhranc spawns approximately at location 0,0 in the City of Mist. This is just before the Reaver area near the large hut with all of the Goos. He spawns in the middle hut right past the big goo house. He spawns at the bottom and the two NPC's who spawn upstairs are not placeholders. Upon speaking with him he will reveal what he needs to forge your weapon.

Each of these steps is accomplished by completing a sub-quest. These sub-quests all tie in together. The heart of an innocent and the will of innoruuk are wearable items that are among the best that shadowknight can attain. As a result these sub-quests are listed with their own seperate pages that are cross-linked together. They are listed in the neccessary order of completion.

Heart of an Innocent
The Will of Innoruuk
Head of the Valiant

Return these three items along with the corrupted ghoulbane to Lhranc and he will reward you by turning in to a living version of himself wielding the almighty Innoruuk's Curse. Killing him will allow you to loot the weapon.

Head over to Kargek Redblade and hand him the Spiroc Wingblade and the Note to Kargek and you'll be rewarded with the Red Scabbard.

Put all four items into the Scabbard, the Ancient Blade, Ancient Sword Blade, Finely Crafted Head Hilt, and the Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt. Hit combine and you will receive the Jagged Blade of War.

Note: It can be split into two one handed weapons, called the Blade of Tactics and the Blade of Strategy.
