The Safehouse was founded around its Forums. They are a great place for both knowledge transfer and social interaction. Friendships have been born here and memories forged that some of us will never forget.

We pride ourselves in the unique community we have all built, and because of that we have become very protective of it. In hopes of preserving what some call a "second home", we ask you to follow a few simple rules.

  1. Watch your language. The Safehouse is visited by people of all ages and backgrounds, please keep it clean. This includes religious swear words.

  2. Please post in the appropriate forum. Don't be upset if we move your post to a more approriate forum.

  3. Avoid personal attacks. Debate opinions, not the people behind them.

  4. Do not post links to pornographic or other inapropriate sites.

  5. Do not use the Safehouse forums for advertising purposes.

  6. One of the great things about the Safehouse is all the GREAT sigs. But with all the sigs, threads can get bogged down, take forever to load and look really cluttered. So we ask that you follow these simple rules:
    • Sig MUST be under 15kb in size - this includes Flash, animated gifs, etc
    • Only post your custom signature ONCE per thread. Simply uncheck the "Include custom signature" box when posting more than once in a thread.
    • Personal photos MUST be under 15kb if no sig pic is used. If sig pic is used, the combined size of both pics must be no greater than 15kb in size.
    • Don't post "test sigs", use the "Preview" checkbox to see how it looks.
