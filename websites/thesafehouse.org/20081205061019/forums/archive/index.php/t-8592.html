What likely happened is that you got hit with the semi-old hide/sneak trade bug.

Around about the time that the new UI became the standard UI, SOE changed how the secret handshake works. You used to hide and sneak behind a mob and open the trade window. Click trade and *poof* trade at indifferent faction.

Now a-days if you still have hide active when you open the trade window, you are unhidden and unsnuck. Your faction goes to KoS and you get turned into fertilizer.

To correctly use the secret handshake you MUST click off hide before opening the trade window. So long as you are still behin dthe mob, you still stay snuck and indifferent. Complete your trade and then reapply hide.

This is not only for the OT hammer, it also applies to any other KoS hand-in... like you know... that General guy.
