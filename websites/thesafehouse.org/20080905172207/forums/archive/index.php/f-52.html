The Safehouse, Vanguard, and the Affiliate Fan Site Program
Story by: The Safehouse Staff
Thu, 17 May 2007 @ 05:23:00
Discuss this story

Unless you have been living under a rock, you have recently learned that Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) has acquired Sigil Games Online (SGO) and its assets for Vanguard: Saga of Heroes. As a result, SOE is dissolving the established Vanguard Affiliate program and have opened official forums to centralize the community of active subscribers and developers. Their intent is to give credence to all fan sites making an effort to promote the game. As Alan Crosby (Brenlo), SOE Director of Community Relations, states in a recent post:

…We will put up a list of fan sites but it will not be "An official" list.  We are not going to recognize any one fan site over another.  This is how we treat our fan sites on all of our titles.  Now we may not offer exclusives, dev posts, or even link to sites that are filled with frothing hate or anger but neither will enforce any standards beyond that.  We realize that each community is different and each will have its own flavor and style…
