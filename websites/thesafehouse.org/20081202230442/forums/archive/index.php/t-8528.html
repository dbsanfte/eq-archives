Nevron - you might want to explore Seru a bit if you're not kos to any houses yet, including the Katta spies. The mobs are placed exactly the same in each house: 2 on the 3rd floor, and about 7 or 8 near the Praetorian's chambers (1 on each teleporter). Eye, Heart, Shoulder: all level 60. Eye contains Ranger NPCs, Heart is Enchanter and Paladin, Shoulder is Mage (pretty sure) and something else, Hand is level 66 Warriors and Paladins, who can Enrage. They hit fast for about 180's, and their high level makes them hard to chant. Having Tash and a Shaman help a lot.

One of the mobs right inside Eye house is level 62 and sees through invis. There are also multiple class Guild Leaders in each house - they HURT, beware..

Hope this info helps. I strongly suggest you mess around with the zone and learn it's pathing. There's a lot of glitches that can either save your ass, or get you dead (hi stacking mobs near Praetorian's chambers).. stacking mobs is cause by running into a side room while aggro on a certain floor, or clicking in and out of the Praetorian's chambers. Take a few days and mess around with a monk (if you have access to one) or a rogue, get to know the zone well, it will be your home for a few weeks, maybe months. Good luck~

Heh; lots of confusion surrounding this quest

Quote:As for the jail the Praes banish to, there is one in each house and it's not a far run back to the fight.

Not sure what this mean... The banish-spot is the same for all 4 prae fights, and is down in the jail area in the southern part of the zone (near the arena, where they have some of the alien guys captured). The bind/TL trick is the most mana efficient way of doing it. CoH is expensive, and these fights tend to be fairly long. If you're very lucky, you can get the Prae into a port/summon mode where he'll port your MT then summon him directly back, but that's fairly rare.

Rhugol -- Eye, slowable
Bikum -- Shoulder, not slowable
Vantorus -- Hand, slowable
Mapta -- Heart, slowable

(I might have the last 2 reversed; doesn't matter much). All flurry, and all hit for 500-ish if I remember right. Vantorus seems to have more hps than the others (fights tend to be longer). The all banish to the same jail location. And I've been banished when I was not hit, through a wall. Their banish is even interruptable; it's not a proc.

Report drops are very streaky. I've seen 4 drop in one clearing of a house, then none drop in like 10 clearings of a house. The third earring is by far the most annoying to get =P Just take a group up there and move around clearing the top floor. Have fun with the pathing.

As for what type of mobs drops them... I forget the exact names (they were posted previously in this thread), but you can go by this guideline: Each house has 2 teleporters up to the 'top' area where the Prae spawn. Each teleporter has a guard. Those guards have a name. That name is the type of mob that can drop the report for that house. Eye house is the worst; it has very few report droppers (2 outside, 2 right at the portals, and 4-5 other random ones). Others in that house don't drop reports. Those names can drop the reports regardless of where they spawn in the zone.

Quote:The fron 2 house leaders are slowable, back 2 aren't and has higher damage output if i am not mistaken

Not exactly. The Bikun (Shoulder prae) is not slowable. The others are. All can randomly drop silk horses, but it seems fairly rare, and each has their own unique piece of poop they drop.

The guards in the Arx building only drop Shoulder reports, rarely.

You need good Katta faction to finish this quest, and it helps to have good Seru faction to start it, but it's not required. You can do this quest while KOS to Seru, but it takes some tricks for the first earring (since it requires talking to a Seru-aligned guard). The rest of the stuff in the Seru zone itself can be done while KOS too; the people you have to talk to are *not* on Seru faction. After the second earring, you need good Katta faction since it requires talking to Tilbok in Katta. You'll likely have good katta faction after getting the 4 reports; if not, you definitely will have good katta faction after killing the 4 Prae. And one or two Seru kills will have Katta shining your boots when you walk into the zone.

Long annoying quest. 5th earring is sweet, tho. Edited by: Niin at: 1/22/03 4:49:07 am
