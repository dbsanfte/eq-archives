Go to the Emerald jungle and clear the the northwest area, on top of the plateau, of mobs.
A iksar outcast will spawn with the name 'an Iksar tomb raider' as very rare spawn. He is
approximately 33-35th and seems to spawn about one in every one hundred kills. I was unable
to identify a placeholder/spawn cycle. He will wander that area back and forth, I didn't see
go off the cliff to the lower areas or despawn but I can't be sure of either. You will likely
need a group at 35th level to do this, but the experience will be good if the group is capable
of chain pulls and sets up outside the various mobs pathing along the north wall.

Kill him and he always drops one book, which can be one of several Iksar history books. The
common drops are the same books as sold in Cabilis. His uncommon drop is a No Rent version
of Venril the Chief, it is tradeable. This is similar to the ones sold in stores in that
it covers the rise of Venril Sathir and his creation of an Iksar Empire.

Once you have that book, you bring an Emerald to Dom K'Perl in the Dark Elf outpost of
The Overthere. Handing it to him will result in a tradeable No Rent book called 'Before Green'.
If you are Kill on sight at the outpost and worried about being attacked by guards, you can
have someone hand in the Emerald and trade the book back to you.

Take the two no rent books and a group to Kaesora's library and clear it until the Tortured
Librarian spawns. He is on Brood of Kotiz/Legion of Cabilis faction and is quite possibly
Kill on Sight to some without good faction in one or both of those.

You say, 'Hail, tortured librarian'

tortured librarian says 'Bring to me that which was taken.'

Hand him the two books and it will result in:

tortured librarian says 'All must be returned!'
tortured librarian fades out of exisistence. The Air smells of death. Something has appeared in your hand.
Your faction standing with Brood Of Kotiz got better.
Your faction standing with Legion Of Cabilis got better.
You gain experience!!

The result is a Large Tassle Bookmark which identifies as a 'Tassel of Flaxen Cord'


Speculation:
The identify having 'Flaxen' in it looks like its part of the Flaxen Hilt quest in Cabilis West. It's likely that this is on of the items to be joined with the hilt.
