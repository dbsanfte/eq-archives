The Safehouse Network Account FAQ

Q: What does it cost?
A: There is no cost to view any of the Safehouse Network sites. Although we do offer extended services, they are not a requirement for gaining access to the site.

Q: If there is no fee then why require an account?
A: The requirement for an account is to allow us to provide services that we otherwise would not be able without knowing who you are.

Q: What do you do with the information you collect from me?
A: All information we gather for the basic account access is for the purpose of the Safehouse staff maintaining your account. We do not sell any information we collect to advertising services or marketing companies. You can read the entire "Privacy Policy" here.
