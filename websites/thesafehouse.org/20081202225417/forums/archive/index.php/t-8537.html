the Broken Skull Armsmaster location is as follows:

From the zone, go down the ladder on the left, you land between a rat under the ladder and an undead mobs. You are now in the room with the Luggald Interrogator.

If you exit that room thru the only other exit, you will be at a hallway, and facing an entrance to another room across the hallway.

The Armsmaster spawns in that very same room across the hallway, he is the spawn at the anvil with an axe in it.

If you proceed down the hall, there is a large room on your right, and the hall turns and proceeds to the left and then turns again at another rat. The large room on the right is the one with the Quartemaster and the drunken buccaneer.

----

For the Captain event, all the the mini-spawns have a chance to drop an item. We killed several of them before we were sure about the respawn, and got two different items (a neck and something else, I will have to check my logs).
