Here I was going to say something witty... but since I can't always be witty, and it is rather hard to act witty when you're not, I won't say anything witty.

There is currently no software out there that I know of that would allow the DMing of a 3rdEd D&amp;D game, with the possible fuzzy exception of Neverwinter Nights.

Note that this is in the Pen &amp; Paper Rogues forum.
Basically, what Icebreaker is looking for, as I understand it, is people who know or can learn the tabletop / pen and paper version of D&amp;D and play that online, theoretically through some chat program.

So there's no real way to download D&amp;D 3rd edition online. You'd have to travel to your local gaming/comic store or order the 3rd Edition Player's Handbook online.

Some dice would help too, unless you can find a script that will generate random whole numbers in a specific range with a specific chance.

If I recall correctly, Wizards of the Coast upped the price to $29.99 or somesuch for the PHB.
