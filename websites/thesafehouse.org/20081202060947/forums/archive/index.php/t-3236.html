Aidden bursts into the room, sweating profusely. He looks around at the unruly crowd, and starts to shiver. He slowly reaches behind his back and pulls out a small, leather cylinder. He carefully removes the top and slips out a piece of parchment. Grabbing the top, he rolls open the scroll.

The Safehouse NWN game Sever

Overview:

The Safehouse staff, in an attempt to continue its support of the MMORPG and Rogue communities will be providing a Neverwinter Nights gaming environment. We will be providing a general, persistent module that will represent a virtual Safehouse as well as weekly/monthly adventure party Modules. All attempts are being made to provide an enjoyable gaming experience in a fashion you have become accustom to from the Safehouse.

Details:

All access to the Safehouse NWN game servers will be granted to the individual by providing the individual with a server password.

Anyone wishing to participate on the servers must sign up for an account through the Safehouse NWN web server. By requiring this, we can keep tabs on those we have given access to, and keep everyone informed of updates through email and Web site posts.

We will only be allowing server vaulted characters. This means that you must create, and level up your character on the Safehouse Neverwinter Nights server. We will provide enough content to allow you to both advance your characters as well as have fun doing so. We wish to stress that this environment is being designed for fun. If you wish to grief other players, do not even bother applying.

The Server is not a public server. Access is granted as a privilege. If you are found to have given out the access information without permission you risk losing your access along with those you gave the information to. Please respect this rule.

We will be providing a method of Module submission to allow Safehouse members to create and DM their own modules. The details for how this will work are still being ironed out, but there will be a review process. We do this as to guarantee a quality experience for all those participating.

There will be a calendar with scheduled events, and sign up sheets for each. As each encounter is specifically designed for a certain number of people, we must provide restrictions for events. Based on the popularity of individual modules, we will allow extended running to allow all members to participate.

A detailed rules and regulations list will be posted on the Website once it has gone live, for your viewing pleasure.

As we near the opening of the Safehouse NWN Web and Game servers, we look forward to seeing you all there with anticipation.

The Safehouse Staff

Default port for connecting is 5120..

of course the Safehouse is on a different port..

The problem is the first port is used to connect, but there are an additional 200 that could be used..

So open UDP ports from 5120 to 5320 for regular, and open the Safehouse ports from the port you use for connecting + 200 more..

Make sure you are opening UDP and not TCP..
