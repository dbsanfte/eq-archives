Frequently Asked Questions

What classes can make potions with alchemy?

Alchemy is a shaman-only skill.

When can I start working on alchemy? When should I start working on alchemy?

You can start once your shaman is level 25. However, many shaman wait until they are level 29. See our guide on Beginning Alchemy.for more information.

Is there a skill cap on alchemy? What is the formula?

Yes. The formula for alchemy skill cap is:

(Your level minus 24) times 5.

Also, see our guide on Beginning Alchemy.for more information.

Do the recipes for "Old Alchemy" still work?

Only a scant few, namely:

Dulsehound (made with Hill Giant Toes and Balm Leaf),
Stinging Wort (made with Ice Giant Toes and Balm Leaf), and
Graveyard Dust (made with Fire Giant Toes and Dragonwart)

Note that all three of these potions involve the combining of "Giant" toes and herbs available only from various gypsy camps.

See our Recipes and Effects page, our Herb Vendors page, and our Herb Prices page for more information.

Where do I find the level 50 spell, Rain of Sickness?

This spell was only on test and was removed prior to patching. Therefore, it does not exist.

Where do I find the shaman spell, Growth?

This spell was removed from test pre-patch and does not exist on live servers.

What are the best potions to make in order to gain and/or improve my alchemy skill?

Try to find a balance between "potion difficulty" (ie, trivial level) and "cost to make" as per our Trivial Chart, taking into consideration those potions that sell best to players (Blood of Wolf, Invisibility to Undead and Shrink in the pre-100 skill area . . . Greater Aura of Heat and Greater Null for Vox raids, Greater Aura of Cold for Nagafen raids, Greater Aura of Purity for Trakanon raids, Stingwort and Gate potions in the post-100 skill area).

Also, see our guide on Beginning Alchemy.for more information.

What attribute has the greatest effect on alchemy success rates and skill increases?

Wisdom.

See our guide on Beginning Alchemy.for more information.

Where do I find the herbs to combine with various "Giant" Toes?

Balm Leaf, Dragonwart, Elf Leaf and LarkSpur can be found at gypsy camps noted near the bottom of our Herb Prices page or our Herb Vendors page.

Where do I find the various "Giant" Toes used for certain alchemy potions?

First and formost, from killing the giants and looting their toes. Some vendors (especially those near giant spawn points) may carry giant toes from time to time as a result of other players selling their looted giant toes to that vendor. There is no known vendor that commonly stocks giant toes in their regular inventory. See the bottom of our Herb Prices page for more details.

What the recipe for regular Invisibility potions?

There is currently no known recipe for making regular Invisibility potions in alchemy. However, such potions can be vendor purchased in virtually every city throughout Norrath at a potion merchant.

What is the recipe for Clarity potions?

There is currently no known recipe for making Clarity potions in alchemy. However, such potions have been known to drop from goblins in Frontier Mountains (very rare).
