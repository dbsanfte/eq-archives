Combinable Potions

"I'd rather have a potion and not need it, than need a potion and not have it"

Various Stat and Aura potions may be combined for compounded effect so long as the stats adjusted by each potion in the combination do not overlap. In the chart that follows, the combinations are restricted to "like potencies" for each combination example, although dissimilar potencies could be combined to produce a myriad of stat adjustment possibilities. However, these are the ONLY Stat and Aura potion variety combinations possible.

Utility potions may be taken with any other potion, except as noted below:

Utility Potion Combination Possibilities

As far as can be surmised, utility potions can be combined with virtually any other valid potion combination (Stat, Aura or Utility) with the following exceptions:

Rejuvenation Line: No potency of Rejuvenation potion can be combined with any other potion that effects either Strength or Attack Speed. Therefore, Rejuvenation cannot be combined with the following potion varieties: Stability, Vigor, Power, Heat, Purity, Tamed Mercury, Adrenaline Tap, Dulsehound, Stinging Wort or Graveyard Dust.

Unlife Awareness: As this is an invisibility to undead potion, any attack, spell casting or drinking of any subsequent potion will immediately remove the invisibility effect.
