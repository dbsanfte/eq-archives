Drinking Potions on the Fly and Other Hot Key Tricks

Ever wonder how to drink a potion quickly when it absolutely, positively must be there overtongue?

Simple, just create a General Inventory slot "Hot Key" for your potion, then right click on the hot-keyed potion when you need to drink it in a hurry.

The same can be done with "Effect" items. Just pick up the item (by holding down your left mouse button on it) from your wrist or other body part and drop the copy it creates into a "Hot Key" slot.

Then just right click on it when you need to use it.

Spell "Gem" Icons can also be "Hot-Keyed" in similar fashion.
