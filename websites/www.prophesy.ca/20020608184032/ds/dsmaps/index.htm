Wednesday, June 5th, 2002

UPDATE: Added a new tutorial, seems as though some ppl have been having problem with making doors that are activated by switches so here it is, SWITCH ACTIVATED DOORS. Enjoy!

It seems that the closer I get to completing the Spider Caverns, the less I want to do it, lol. I am so close it isn't even funny, I could be done in like 3 hours, tops. Only problem is, it's all mostly placement of scenery, and that consits of large amounts of cobwebs and mucosas and spiders. Trust me, it isn't as fun as you'd think it would be! I do plan to take my friend's advice and "suck it up, bitch!" and just finish what I started. From there, I will be able to create the transition forest from valley to swamp, which will then connect to Blight Bog. That in turn will connect to The Ancient City of Tenesia. Expect "unusual" things to happen to the storyline there. I won't give anything away, suffice to say it will be things you will not be expecting...

I have ideas for a final boss too, everything is starting to fit together as I write more lore books. I'm really quite happy with the storyline so far, and I hope it will only get better. The lore books I make will simply be supplimental knowledge to the history of Athesia, and may go so far as to include short stories/accounts written by the residents of my land. I hope to make an experience that adds just a bit more "character" to the area which you journey, as I found that lands such as Ebb and the Urtaen Penninsula were magnificently designed, but failed to really convey the "feeling" of a land that had any sort of history, other than the scattered bits of ruins and whatnot that you discovered as you journeyed through them.

I recieved a very good suggestion from a visitor to the site, about alternative modes of transport between cities. Things like boats, hot air balloons, etc.

I'd like to at LEAST make boats, like you take a boat from one town to the other, as opposed to using things such as the HUB. I will still have Displacer Platforms, as these make it easier to join up with people who are scattered around the land, and are good for heading back to town for supplies every once in a while. True, as of right now Frostdale DOES have a HUB platform, but I think "certain" cities will not have them, and will only be reachable by other means. One of these cities will most definitely be the Dark Elf city of Drak Vilerran, which is deep within the Underworld, and will require a long journey to reach. This town will be a pivotal area in the storyline, so expect it to be nothing short of spectacular. Same goes for the city of Qwithnairn, the city of the elves. It's gonna look WICKED. I would not do any less for the magnificent elves, as they have been around since before the days of man.

Suffice to say, things will only get darker and more opressive as the storyline moves on, and you will be fighting some truly "colossal" foes. You think rock beasts are big? Mwa ha ha, you ain't seen NOTHIN yet ;-)

- Mike, Website Administrator
