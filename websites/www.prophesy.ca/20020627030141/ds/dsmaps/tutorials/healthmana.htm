These things are really easy to make, it just takes the placement of a few objects and one node, and BLAMMO! Instant (almost) health or mana shrine! Enuff with the blabbing, let's get it on with the tutorial!

First, place the shrine node of your choice, "t_grs01_heal-area-8x8" for a health shrine or "t_grs01_mana-area-8x8". These nodes are located in "terrain nodes/grass1". Place a "t_emt_sound_act" object, located in "game objects/gismos/special", in the middle of the shrine, and open it's properties window. In the template properties tab, there should be the value "start_on_creation". Change it to "false". Below that is the value "event_sound". Enter "env_shrine_health" if you are making a health shrine, or "env_shrine_mana" if you are makking a mana shrine. Copy the SCID of the "emt_sound_act", you will need it later.

Next, place a "shrine_health effect" or "shrine_mana effect", whichever you are making, above the "emt_sound_act", making sure it's still in the middle of the shrine. The only reason we put it above is to make selecting each object easier. Go to the object properties window of the "shrine_health/mana effect" and adjust the following 3 values: heal_amount, health_left, and health_regen. These explain themselves. If you are making a mana shrine, be sure to set the value "heal_life" to "false". Lasty, copy down the SCID of the "shrine_health/mana effect" object. You now have 2 SCID's, one for the "env_sound_act" and the other for the "shrine_health/mana effect". Now for our last object.

Place a "trigger_health_shrine" or "trigger_mana_shrine", once again depending on the type of shrine you are making. Open the properties of the "trigger_health/mana_shrine" object, and select the Trigger Properties tab. Click on the word "trigger_0" at the top left. In the "Actions" area, paste the SCID of the "shrine_health/mana effect" into the "Send to Object" value field. Click on the word "trigger_1" at the top left. First, in the "Conditions" area, change the value "Boundary Check" to "While Inside". Next, in the "Actions" area, paste the SCID of the ""shrine_health/mana effect" into the first "Send to Object" field. Paste the SCID of the "emt_sound_act" into the other two "Send to Object" fields. There should be red animated arrows pointing from the "trigger_health/mana_shrine" object to the "shrine_health/mana effect" object and the "emt_sound_act" object. That's it! Your shrine is DONE!
