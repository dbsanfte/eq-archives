In the dark times of Norrath, the Great Guilds began to lose sight of honor and loyalty and plunged the world into chaos.  It was in this chaos that the Gods saw fit to spread their presence around the lands and with it, turmoil for the peaceful citizens of Norrath.

From these dark days, a scripture was written, that fortold of a group of adventurers drawn to a single goal.  To form an alliance, and band together to fulfill the Divine Prophesy and challenge the Gods themselves.

So it was written, so it shall be...

Welcome to Divine Prophesy, an Everquest Guild. Our guild resides on the Povar Server and welcomes a wide variety of people from all over Norrath.  This site contains information on our guild as well as our members.  We also have several items of interest for non-guild members such as our databases of items and people.

We hope you enjoy your stay and perhaps send us a note if you’re interested in becoming a fellow member of Divine Prophesy.
