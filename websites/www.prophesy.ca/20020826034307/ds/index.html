Site Opens

May 22, 2002

Welcome to my Dungeon Siege WWW site.  This fan site is dedicated to the sieglet entitled “Island of Aracania” which is currently in development. My goal is to create a fun filled adventure available for anyone who is willing to download it and play.  Geared primarily for multi-play use, this adventure I hope will inspire others to share their creative thinking with the rest of the gaming community.

Because this site is quite new, I dont have much content on it yet, however I do have several screenshots of one of the towns on Aracania.  Please feel free to drop me some comments or suggestions.   My address is: seranne@prophesy.ca
