Tales From the Dark Side - High Hold Pass
by Talyan of the Undead Lords

Many moons had come and gone since I had been home in Nerriak. How I longed for the familiar smell of inscence in the halls of the Temple where I grew as a boy. The chants of hate, pain, and suffering, echoed through my head. But, all of that was long gone. I had been sent upon Norrath to roam endlessly to spread the word and make sure the message of Innoruuk was felt.

As of late I found myself in strange company my temporary residence found me at High Hold Pass a mountain city built like a maze. Many travelers have been lost forever there. However to my advantage they also have a pest problems at both entrances to the city. Seems nasty bands of orcs are controlling the traffic to the city from the south, And another band of gnolls controlling the entrance from the North.

Now I know what you are saying what would a dark brother even care if the pitiful humans had a problem ‘tis there own lose and let the foolish mortals clear it themselves. However, it had been long since I had been able to store some coins in a bank, my head ached for a bed and my lips were parched longing for the sweet cold taste of mead.

So reluctantly out of mutual need a pact was made the residents would offer me their services and safe passage in and out of the city, in trade I would lower the orc and gnoll population. Seasons had passed and say in and out I would hunt the savage half races killing them with my mighty moring star till the morn of today...

I had been in a deep slumber. Dreams of mead and ale amongst good kin filled my slumber. <Thwack>

A Sharp mind shattering pain arcs though my bones. Awaking me, in shock and blood blurring my vision. Find my self-stumbling around with A gnoll soothsayer and 2 brawlers trying to make me into there packs next meal. Half-naked grabbing my shield and morning star, ‘twas all I could do to swing around to surprise the gnoll with a blow of my own Fine steel. Too no avail though I was only able to inflict a mere flesh wound. Now I knew that this fight was not for Innoruuk of the residents of High Hold this was a fight

For my life. A gnoll begins to cast, green bubbles surround, my feet have become rooted to the path. Now I had no obvious way to retreat and unable to grasp an edge on this Battle. The crimson pool surrounded my feet. In hopes to gain an advantage in the battle, I called upon all the power of the father Innoruuk God of Hate. A red sparks flew from my hands, striking the gnoll. The gnoll lets out a blood-curdling yelp. However, the spell was not enough the gnoll brawlers came in with fierceness, their claws hit their mark tearing flesh from my arm.

Not yet ready to die, but knowing that the battle was futile to try....

Death was surly in my future but at that moment as if the last blow had awakened some lost spell. Quick I chanted the chant taught to me as a boy. Clear spheres surround me

Surround the air, the gnoll sticker, misses, and again.

Then...aloud rush fills the air and the world is dark.... Then again the spheres surround me to find myself, surrounded by Lava in front of me a large entrance, with the sign of the dark elf on one of the giant doors the other door lye on the ground.

I had heard of such a place, could this be, The home of Najena, throwing my armor over my head, wiping the blood and sweat of my brow I make for the entrance and whatever adventure and treasure I might find....
