Races in EQ2

In EQ2, your alignment matters. Depending on whether you choose a good or evil start, your city of origin will change. Evil races start in Freeport; good races start in Qeynos.

What race should I choose?
All the races have their characteristics outlined for you on the official SOE EQ2 site and when you start a character in the game. Depending on how much role playing you want to do, you might choose your race because of the racial characteristics defined by the game designers. In EQ2, racial characteristics seem to play a much greater role in the game than in EQLive. The Archetype you want to play will also determine what race you choose, since some races are better equipped for some jobs over others.

The betrayal quest, which allows you to change cities and factions, is long and involved, so it's best to choose a good or evil race that you can live with when you start your character.
