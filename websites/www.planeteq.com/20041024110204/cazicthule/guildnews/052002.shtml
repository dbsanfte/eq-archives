Fungus Grove Rumble
Posted by Agilent On May 17 2002 At 10:59:41 AM
Last night we decided to get some exp so a few groups headed to do the Caller spawns in Fungus Grove. At first the spawns were too slow so we decided to trigger the biggest wave. The highlight of the event was when after 40 min or so the spawn timer went crazy. We had 10 or so mobs up and two named up at the same time. Needless to say we dispatched them. Grats to Allegory on a rogue cloak and everyone else that got loot.

After FG I mentioned going to the Deep to try to get some levitation spells. It was pretty late but managed to get a group of a 60 enc, 52 bard, 2 warriors (50, 60), 56 shaman and a 58 Mage. The fight in was rather tedious but we eventually reached our goal. After 30 min of hunting at camp the named mob spawned and dropped levitation. Grats to me, ilent, on finally getting the componentless levitate. After a few spawn cycles a few of us were tired and it was late so we had to start fighting out as we had no porter and Verant managed to nerf CoH in the zone.

We were almost half way to the exit when I got the strange gut feeling. I told Aaneck one of our enchanters to check the Verina Tomb spawn for the enchanter epic because my feeling was that one fo the mobs was up. He checked and she wasn't, but then I said well then it's Cazel (Aaneck needed this piece). Moments later we see a guildie in Oasis saying "Cazel is up". After our late night Deep crew zoned to saftey we headed to Oasis for some Cazel smack down. Our main tank was Cabrine (bard), with 2 mage pets and a chanter pet to help tank. Toasted the menace in under 2 min. The newbies in Oasis were rejoicing. Congratulations to Aaneck on another epic piece. Only four to go and he has all the hard ones out of the way. Not much longer now till we have another snake stick. Was a very fun and rewarding night all around...

Shadowed Soul
Sunday, May 19th, 2002
Lord, I am *SO* Tired, How Long Can This Go On?
Cuz we've been workin in a coal mine, goin downtown...

Erp, wait, that wasn't it, but it's been close.

This past week, SS & DA have been busy as hell running about killing stuff. I would recount it all, but honestly, with the sleep I am losin trying to keep up with all these hoops VI wants me to hop through, I can't remember what I did last night. The only thing I am sure of is that it wasn't sleep. If anyone reading this saw me this week, please email me and let me know what I was doin, so I can write something snazy about it for our next update.

On a related note, I think I will share my own silly views on Luclin. The new VI high end model does one thing amazingly well....it insures that only the truly insane will be there. If you are only mildly crazy, or a low volume player, in the back of your mind you could always think that under the right circumstances, when the planets are lined up in just the proper order and you have sacrificed the required 3 Chickens and drank the rum....you might get in on the slaying of some Uber Mob. Nagafen, Trakanon, Cazic-Thule...somebody. If you just play your cards right, someday you can get there.

Welp, no longer. There is now a pretty firm ceiling on where you can go and what you can do in Luclin if you don't want to prove you are nuts. It's nasty and long, and HARD to get to some of this stuff, and frankly, it is starting to bother me less. As time goes on, the competition for any mob gets tougher, but unless VI changes the design of the expansion, I can't see that really happening in Luclin, or at least not as bad as it has gotten in every previous expansion.

So, is this a good or a bad thing? Well, in the end it is just a thing, but like I said, it is starting to bother me a lot less.
