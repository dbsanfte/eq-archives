Namik's Adventure
by Namik - Prexus Server

It was a frosty night as cold swept Anotonica, but the Commons where the worst. As Namik looked at the moon and trudged on carrying her short walking stick, being a Halfling is quite small. She had just killed a Zombie of a human; she pouted,"Why can't Humans kill their own undead, you don't see and Halfling Skeletons..." she sighed and her breath showed in the night air. She continued walking next to some sand covered hills and noticed a small Human building standing near a tree on grass and decided not to go, who knows what a Human would do to me at this ungodly hour. She walked on and she noticed that in the sand hill there was an entrance to something! She walked stealthily being a rogue and walked inside the entrance to this some kind of dungeon. "So this is Befallen..." and walked deeper in.

As Namik walked though the damp dungeon, she saw no one and only heard the aggravating noise of skeletons. How she hated skeletons and realized that with Skeletons come Necromancers and with Necromancers come more Necromancers etc. any how, Namik was getting quite up set about now and decided that she was going to turn around a leave. As she turned around she noticed she was at the ankles of a Ogre, ready to catch her. She ran quickly deeper into the Dungeon realizing that the Ogre wouldn't come in here, but it did. The ogre was in a hunting party with 5 other Ogres. Sigh though Namik, i guess im staying the night here.

Namik started to explore the dungeon behind the ogres back, running in and out doors they opened and closed looking for whatever they were looking for. suddenly from behind a corner, a Dark Elf Shadow Knight walked out, not noticing Namik, but soon would. Namik said under her breathe, "for the honor of the Wrath" and backstabed the Dark elf right in the back leaving a huge gashed hole which was leaking the Dark Elves blue blood. He turned around and saw her, holding her Dagger firmly in her hand ready to attack again, which she did. She got him right in the knee, making the dark elf fall in pain. And to finish the dark elf she stabbed him in the back to the heart. she started searching the Dark elf for any valuables and found a sack of money holding 3 gold, 6 silver, 8 copper. she quickly ran back from where she had come from hearing the scream of a Ogre from the other side of the Dungeon. "If something is that big to kill an Ogre, i don't think im welcome here" and she ran back to the desert outside of the Dungeon and thought, "Namik, that was some good hunting, you even got the hear a Ogre Scream like a Human Girl. What a well spent night" as she looked to the horizon and saw the sun rise.
