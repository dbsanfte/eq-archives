Interview with Al'Kabor

Recently, I had the opportunity to meet with the famous wizard Al-Kabor, who granted me an exclusive interview to talk about his latest research. We met at his modest house outside of Erudin. --Kwill Heartfyre

KH: Thank you so much for meeting with me, Mr. Al-Kabor. I am very curious to learn about your latest research I have been hearing so much about.

A-K: Well thank you, young lady...it's always a pleasure to talk to you gals in the media...and don't you look fetching today....take off that robe, get comfortable...ale, my dear? I hate formality, don't you?

KH: Um, no I am fine...now, about these new metals you seem to have discovered..

AK: Oh, come on...you are as bad as that Firiona, always serious! You high elf girls need to get a sense of fun...head over to Kelethin sometime and take a gander at what those girls are doing. I told that Thex to marry Firiona off years ago, she wouldn't have had all that truck with the dragons, and then being kidnapped! Shameful, I tell you! Why, I even mentioned marrying her for her own good, but Thex wouldn't hear of it. Hmph. Let me tell you about this one time when I went to Neriak in disguise....

KH: That sounds very interesting, sir, but could we get back to your research?

AK: Well that was research, sort of... but all right. Just sit so I can see you, okay?

KH: If you insist. Where are my glasses? Here they are...

AK: Glasses! What does a pretty young thing like you need to see for? Those gnomes never could set their priorities straight...

KH: Mr. Al-Kabor! Please! Now, are you the one who discovered something new about the Combine Empire?

AK: Yes, yes I did. You know, lately I have been able to unearth many old tomes from the time of the combine empire, and contrary to popular belief, they did not construct the wizard portals. No! In fact, they didn't do anything but take advantage of someone else's work! I can very well understand how people can be taken advantage of. I have had some of the worst assistants in years, lately! Anyway, they built the portals to take advantage of the magic from the "quadroliths" as I like to call them.

KH: What are the quadroliths exactly?

AK: They are the sets of four tall spires found all over Norrath. They emanate some sort of magical energy. The most interesting one is the one in Velious, which does not have a pyramid associated with it. I am not sure why, since all the others have been used by the Combine Empire. I did experiments where I scraped off parts of the portals and combined them with ores, and made spells that could translocate people to other places with the help of wizards. But I was betrayed, as usual...that idiot assistant of mine sold all my spell formulations and now they are as common as Blackburrow stout. Let me tell you something, young lady...

KH: Ahem, have you discovered anything else of interest that you could share with us?

AK: Well yes, I have, now that you mention it. I am still continuing my experiments. Of course it's still a proprietary secret, but I have found a new mineral ore on Kunark that I have not yet learned the properties of. I can't seem to find the secret to unleashing it's power. Once I do, who knows what magics I can create from it. And now that I have gotten those new tomes from those bloodthirsty gypsies, I am even closer to discovering what happened to the Combine Empire. It's my life's work, you know...to find out what happened to those people. It's possible that there could be someone among them that I could actually talk to, unlike the cretins here on this planet! I don't trust those gypsies, though--despite the fact they are ignorant fools, they can be devious. Take that Cynthia--what a vixen! One time when I was in Rathe Mountains....

KH: Yes, well...some people are suggesting there could be life on the moon. What do you think of those theories?

AK: Heh, the likelihood of that is about the same as seeing another race of people that look like tigers! But if anyone could get to the moon, it would be me.

KH: So what is Firiona Vie like?

AK: Well I think she could be more fun, but her father just beat all that honor and paladin stuff into her. It's tough being an avatar, too...gods can be so demanding. I still think she has some issues with the kidnapping...I told her not to have anything to do with that Iskar Danak, but she wouldn't listen to me. She should listen to me more! Did I tell you I wanted to marry her? That Thex is an idiot.

KH: Okay, well that's all the time I have. Thanks for talking with me.

AK: Sure you won't have an ale? And I would love to show you some of my old books over there...I may be old, but I'm not dead yet! I am a very powerful wizard!

KH: Um.... I think that wouldn't be such a good idea. Can I get a port to West Commons please?

AK: You want a port? Sure, I'll give you a port. Cheeky reporters!...
