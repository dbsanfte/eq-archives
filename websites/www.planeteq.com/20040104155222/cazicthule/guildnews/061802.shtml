I hate cabbage

Posted by: Druss on Friday, June 14th, 2002 @ 1:23 AM

So...the week in review.

First, and most importantly, we've changed the name of the guild. Our logo has changed accordingly. I'm sure Flare will be updating all titles on our site very soon.

In addition, our new Guild Heraldry: [picture of a cabbage!]

So, whats the deal? Well, the past week, we've pretty much done two things: Jack, and cabbage quest earrings. And Jack left town, he's allergic to cabbage.

Yes, cabbage earrings have pretty much taken the guild by storm...to the exclusion of nearly all else. Our guild is full of tradeskill geeks, what can I say...

The strain of endless foraging took its toll on our druids and rangers. Hours of pushing buttons, hoping for that one special drop to appear on their cursor, caused many to teeter on the brink of madness. Take Dinuvian for instance:

You see? Irrefutable evidence that Dinuvian has completely lost his proverbial marbles.

A few rare times, we managed to pull our cabbage crazy members off to kill a mob or two: Zlandicar died twice, Vindi once, Dain once, Itraer Vius once, and Tunare, in what was the fastest zone clearing and smoothest Nature Ho smackdown (4 minute fight, on the nose) ever.

We also managed a shot at the High Priest in Ssra. We went, knowing absolutely nothing about him, going into the fight. No, we didn't kill him, but we pieced together the strategy needed to take him down, and we'll get him the next time we go after him.

Oh, in the not-so-Ubah portion of the update, we managed to blow the AoW spawn this past week. I must say, the barrage of errors we had were truly spectacular. We screwed up multiple times, and still got him to 20%, which was about 80% better than we deserved. In retrospect, we really didn't prepare for the raid correctly - everyone had stocked up on fish rolls, and not Iron Rations...

Thats all for this week. It will be a while until my next update - Yours Truly is on vacation till the end of the month, and I'll be far, far away from a computer. Maybe the guild will have worked through its cabbage fetish by then.

Aloha!
