Council of Grey
Death to the White Dragon Lady
Posted by Grisnalg On Apr 10 2002
It was a cold, windy night in the land known as the Dreadlands. Snow swirled all about obscuring the vision and playing tricks on the mind. In the distance the defiant roars of the Elder White Dragon could be heard. Across the land in a voice laced with rage Gorenaire, the Dread White Lady, cursed those foolish enough to tempt her wrath.

Bravely the intrepid adventurers of Project Eon and Council of Grey marshelled thier forces under the cover of a nearby forest. Under the direction of the Mighty and Fell Wizard Redracer the eager blades and spells of the adventurers raced forward to slay the Lady Gorenaire. Countering with her own magical art the Dread White sent many of her would be slayers running in fear. Alas for Gorenaire there were too many, too strong for her to escape. With blade, axe, spear and rending spell did the adventurers wound the dragon till she fell limply to the earth.

In the end the haunting beauty of such a dread creature could not be marred by the many terrible wounds she had taken. Honor was paid to such a noble beast by those who had slain the Lady Gorenaire. A worthy opponent was heard to issue from many lips. Within her carcass, secreted in a hidden place, were many treasures that were shared by the victorious.

Congratulations to PE on Sunstrike, Battleskar & Clawhammer on Voice of the Berserker, Mancinia on McVaxius Horn of War, and a White Dragon Scale, Legacy of Thorn for the CoG bank. Hail to the Victors!
