History

The Iksar are a reptilian race which calls the recently discovered continent of Kunark home. The Iksar were once slaves of the extinct Shissar, until a plague killed the Shissar. The Iksar then waged war with the Ring of Scale, and in the process their city of Sebilis was destroyed. 

The remaining Iksar have rebuilt in the city of Cabilis and the fallen Empire is once again recovering its fortunes. The Iksar are a war like people and are suited to the rigors of battle. All Iksar regenerate at an extraordinary rate and have a natural armor in their lizard like hides. 

The Iksar commonly become Warriors and Monks, and many follow the dark paths of a Necromancer or Shadow Knight. Through their tribal heritage some may also become Shamans or even a Beastlord.  

The Iksar are friendly with no races, and hated by all. Some believe the Iksar are simply a misunderstood people and the other races hatred of them prevents them forging valuable future alliances, so far, none have tried.

All Iksar follow Cazic Thule, though some are less 'devoted' than others. 

 

Race Specific Tips

Acrobats

Iksar have very good Agility and Dexterity and many will trade on these alone, spending all their bonus points increasing these attributes. 

If you prefer to have a more rounded character it would serve you well to increase Stamina and Strength, both of these start out fairly low, especially Stamina.

Body of Steel

An Iksar's armor class will always be fairly high because of the natural armor class bonus, the benefits of this will be greatly enhanced with a high defense skill and good armor. Investing in a highly protective suit of armor would be a wise choice.
