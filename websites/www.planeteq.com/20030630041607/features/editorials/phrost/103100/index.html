I See Dumb People
By Phrost (10/31/00)

Can I tell you a secret?

::Looks around::

I see dumb people.

And most of them have characters whose names have the word “wolf” in them. I see them as I pass through the many lands of Norrath. Warrwolf, Wraithwulf, Treewolf, hell, I once saw a guy named “Wulfwolf”.

This just in:

Your names suck.

Now don’t get me wrong, I feel your pain… really, I do. The name filter limits creativity. Those Nazi bastards at Verant have us by the balls. I remember when I made my first EQ character, not 10 minutes after getting home from the software store. I’d carefully allocated my stats to where I thought they’d serve me best in the battles to come. I chose the face that suited the character I wanted to portray in my adventuring. I had even started to come up with my character’s backstory. Then I hit submit.

“Your name has been rejected, pick a new one.”

I was crushed! How dare the wicked tyrants at VI keep me from naming my character “leethaxorpkdood”!

(For those of you too dense to detect sarcasm: No I didn’t really name my character that… I’m making fun of you idiots that think names like that are acceptable in a fantasy setting.)

o Meesta Phrosty, why are joo being so mean and nasty today?



Simple, I’ve had enough of you beet farmers running around on my server with stupid names. I swear to god If I see another derivative of Drizzt, Gandalf, or Raistlin, I’m going to reach through my monitor, grab your character by the throat, and choke the crap out of you until you’re at your bind point.

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

/brittneyspears

Oops, I found your bind point… I killed you again!

/brittneyspears

You have lost a level! Welcome to level 0!

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

My server has a wonderfully diverse collection of such idiots. In the past year I’ve seen a “Drizt”, “Drzzit”, “Dzirtt” and my personal favorite, “Tzzird”. We also have a mage named “Ganddalf”.

“What’s that, Mr. Tolkien? You’re coming back from the dead to bitchslap this guy?”

We’re graced with characters like “Hackavelli”, “Dymix Ruffrider” and “Bigtymer Eminem”.

::Turns on PA system::

Attention: Will whoever let the dogs out please sic them on these retards?

We have the brigade of twelve year-old girls (or forty year-old men pretending to be twelve year-old girls) that have taken their character names from Pokemon™ like “Flareon MacEvee”, “Pikaroofy Chu” and “Bullbasuar”.

Swift blow to the head with a blunt object wielded by a disgruntled roleplayer, I chose you!

Speaking of head trauma, I visited the whineplay boards on EverQuest.com the other day and read a thread by someone that was genuinely upset that his Ogre Shaman’s name was forcibly changed from Mrmojo Rison.

Hello, McFly? This isn’t the Sixties, you are not Jim Morrison, and just because you “Retardify” (aka. Ogrespeak) Doors lyrics, doesn’t mean you’re roleplaying. I’d like to “show you the way to the next whiskey bar” and slide your brain dead carcass across it into a dartboard.

Now I know some of you mouth-breathers are going to come back at me with your fingers in your nose saying “But how can you bash people for their names... look at yours!” So I guess I should briefly explain how my little inky was a slave to a Barbarian family in EverFrost until the day he broke free… bla bla bla bla, nickname given by rescuer bla bla bla, came back for revenge, bla bla bla. Satisfied?

I’m pretty sure some of these trouser pilots could come up with a story about their druid growing up in the ghetto of Qeynos Hills, watching Holly and Cros do drive-by nukings, and drinking 40 of fish ale to justify being “Dymix Ruffrider”. Or how they broke free of this large red and white ball and got a badge by defeating the trainer at the Freeport gym. Or how their Ogre ate a lot of mushrooms in the Feerrot outside of the Temple of Cazic Thule and started to think he was the “Lizard King”.

Sorry, but there’s a distinct line between suspension of disbelief…

...and bullsh*t. And that is exactly what some of your names are.

So next time you want to name your character “Heeman”, “Toopak”, or “Charizard”, consider one of the following options:
  1. Use the random name generator.
  2. Go play in traffic.
See you on the freeway.
