The Spear of Fate is a remarkable weapon, enhanced by shamanistic spirits to grant its wielder almost unrivaled benifits. In the process of this quest, you will recieve 3 items.

This guide is split up into a number of sections and subsections.

  • Shield of Falsehood
    • Beginning the quest
    • 'You can buy booze'
    • Test of Patience
    • Test of Wisdom
  • Black Fur Boots
    • Test of Might
  • Spear of Fate
    • City of Mist reports
    • Lord Ghiosk
    • High Scale Kirn
    • Completing the quest

As a side note, DO NOT hand items in when prompted whilst under the effects of any illusion, as it may mess up the faction involved.
