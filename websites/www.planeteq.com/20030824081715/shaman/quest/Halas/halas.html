Tribunal Initiate

Give Jinkus a piece of Mammoth Hide Parchment (from Cindl - Ask her 'what mammoth hide parchment?'), and a bottle of datura ink (from Greta outside Smokes and Spirits - Ask her 'what ink?').

He will give you a wanted poster. Take this to one of the Bank guards in Qeynos. They will give you a List of Qeynos' Most Wanted.

Return this to Jinkus for Initiate Symbol of Justice, exp, and faction.

Shaman Armor of Justice

This is the new newbie armor quest for Barbarian Shaman.

You need to follow the text from Balgros McMichael until he gives you an assembly kit.

Combine the following items in the kit to get the material needed for the armor. Combine the armor material and the appropriate mold in a forge (non-fail) to get the Armor. The molds are obtained by saying 'I want to craft the [piece]'. Where [piece] is one of the following:

  • Breastplate 3 Small Pieces of Ore, 2 Polar Bear Skins, 1 Spider Venom Sac
  • Armplates 2 Small Pieces of Ore, 2 Polar Bear Skins, 1 Patch of Gnoll Fur
  • Boots 2 Small Pieces of Ore, 1 Polar Bear Skin, 1 Spiderling Legs
  • Bracer 1 Small Piece of Ore, 3 Bone Chips, 1 Ruined Wolf Pelt
  • Gauntlets 1 Small Piece of Ore, 4 Bone Chips, 1 Polar Bear Skin
  • Helm 2 Small Pieces of Ore, 2 Bone Chips, 1 Gnoll Pup Scalp
  • Legplates 1 Small Piece of Ore, 2 Polar Bear Skins, 2 Bone Chips

If you ask him 'What other task' and you will be prompted to bring him four Wraith Orc Wristbands. Do so and he will give you the Cudgel of Justice.

Gavel of Justice

Tell Shamus Felligan, 'I will hunt the Goblin Casters'

He will tell you to bring him some Ice Goblin Beads. These can be gotten from Ice Goblin Casters at the 2 igloo camp in Everfrost. They are a rare spawn, and they only rarely drop the whole Beads. However you get exp if you hand the shattered beads into Shamus as well.
