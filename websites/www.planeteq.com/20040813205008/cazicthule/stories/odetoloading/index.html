Ode to The LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...
by Diggler

=============================

Levels one to ten,
Time and again,
Were much, much fun,
We all killed, but then

Call it fate,
Or what you will,
Because that giant,
On that hill…

He loved to maim,
To pound and kill.
And with his rage,
He roared with skill.

I tried to run,
I tried to gate,
But then I saw,
LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

   ------------

Ten to twenty,
Showed me many.
Much things to hunt,
And Orcs ‘a plenty.

To the younger ones,
I was parenti,
With lots to teach,
I was cognoscenti.

To get away,
From that hoard,
I ran, I zoned,
Oft’ times /ignored,

But in the end, I’d simply say,
“I have to go, I’m very late.”
And then with that, with no more words,
I'd be blessed with, LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

   ------------

Twenty to thirty,
Sure was “purdy.”
Unrest for me,
Was my Cape Verde.

I grouped, I healed,
And buffed with shield.
But in that zone,
Our fate was sealed.

People died,
I tried to abate,
But the Knight crept up,
LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

   ------------

It was also fun,
Being thirty to forty,
With my shiny quest armor,
I was lookin’ quite sporty.

The babes would flock,
To see me flex,
To suck my cock,
And then have sex.

But I’d decline,
With a static line,
“My dear, I’m a priest.
You can’t have this beast.”

To my surprise,
They’d ask to /duel,
How little I knew,
They’d be so cruel.

I’d accept their challenge,
With martial valor,
And meet their glare,
With so much pallor.

Then, before I could state,
My deepest regrets,
Amidst their threats,
At their upsets,

They plastered me good.
With, I think, some wood,
And instead of their mate,
I was LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

   ------------

Forty to fifty,
Oh what bliss.
No time to be thrifty,
No time to dismiss.

The money was flowing,
And so was xp,
I showed no slowing,
As I yelled out, “whoopee!”

I went to Kunark,
As a big lark,
And spotted a mark,
To attack in the dark.

I stalked up to it,
To get the first hit,
And the next thing I knew,
I was jumping to /quit.

I started to type,
While that mob took a swipe,
But alas, let me restate,
I was LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…

   ------------

Now, fifty to sixty,
Holds so much promise,
You’d better believe me,
I’m no doubting Thomas.

The world is my oyster,
That I’ll enjoy in my cloister,
As I watch others die,
While they do a train-by.

When they ask for a “res,”
All they’ll get is, “I says…”

“…You thought you would train me,
You thought I’d be killed,
But instead, now you find,
That you’ll be billed.”

“My services aren’t free,
For those, you now see,
Who thought they’d be saved,
When they misbehaved.”

“What? You don’t like it?
You think it’s unfit?
You think I should res you for free,
So you plea?!?"

An /ignore from me,
Is what happens right now,
And I’m sure you’ll agree,
I’m as right as can be.

They probably won’t stop,
Those damn trains, I do rate,
But they’ll sure as hell be used to,
The LOADING, PLEASE WAIT…
