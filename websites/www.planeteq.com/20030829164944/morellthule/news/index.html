   Friday, August 29, 2003

Gameplay Update
8/29/03, 3:25 PST | Muskie

From EQNews.txt:

August 28, 2003
------------------------------

- Two Bastion of Thunder keys and one key in the Plane of Air that were supposed to open up a location for your entire group were only taking the key holder. We have fixed that problem. Also, the Enchanted Ring of Torden will now attach to your key ring the next time you use it.

- The "base" population in the Tower of Solusek Ro now has the ability to summon. Please be aware of this the next time you enter the tower of the god of flame.

- We have corrected a pathing issue in South Karana that was causing the NPCs to all travel to the same point.

- Cloth caps will no longer drop off NPCs in Siren's Grotto.

- We have reduced the number of characters in a raid required to access the Plane of Time from 40 to 18.

- The Burrower has been modified back to its original difficulty.

- A tether has been added to Fennin Ro.

Nothing Much here, Just a few Gameplay bugfixes serverside. No Core Engine patches today.

Wayfarers Check Update
8/28/03, 18:26 PST | Muskie

A Travelling Ranger by the name of Elinas Sian Lann (On Torvonnilous) Sent me this Report from the Field:



Although I was unable to locate on track with my ranger but in West Freeport down between the Arena and the Warrior Guild I distinctly heard Calliav say:

"Zebuxoruk! Guide me I don't know all of what I see"

followed by

"Dark doom a war like we've never known...strange beasts...so powerful"

and then

"Make the visions stop how do I make them stop? Why is this happening to me?

and finally

"Entranced...dazed...forgotten purpose...shimmering sand"


So, knowing that there was a tunnel outside the city from my earlier wanderings...I surmised that I was hearing voices drifting through air vents in those tunnels. I promptly ran outside to those tunnels and found Calliav Giniuar and Morden Rasp sitting in those tunnels (loc -594, 173)

Hailing Calliav produced only more rantings of a coming darkness...

"Something dark and ill seeps along in the slime. ...foul air..."
and "Time is short...we must learn...we must move...into the dark. Back away! Please!"

Hailing Morden Rasp I was told "Quite please, if you would Elimas. Poor scrapper 'ere is 'aving a bit o' trouble sortin' out 'is head. I'm waiting for Nedaria to get 'erself 'ere so we can 'elp 'im and make some sense of the babblin'. Seems Calliav is a wee bit weak too so we're goin' to get 'im rested as well -- bein the fair and kind sort we are in the Wayfarers Brother hood. He he. Poor lad. Screams in pain a lot, aye. Not much I can for now. So, keep the racket down, eh? Be well, friend.'

Further prompts and questioning produced no response since he was obviously wanting silence to help his friend. So I'm off to search for Nedaria...

   Tuesday, August 26, 2003

Changes to Tribunal and Tallon Zek
8/26/03, 9:09 PST | Kwill

Relocation of The Tribunal and Tallon Zek can affect character settings. We are relocating the Tribunal and Tallon Zek Servers. This won't affect access to these servers once the move is finished, but the "number" of these servers has changed. This will mean that if you have characters on those servers you will need to rename the .ini files for those characters to retain your settings (like hotbuttons and such).

Renaming those files is easy. Here's how.

1) Open My Computer and locate your EverQuest directory.
2) Find the .ini files for the characters you have on The Tribunal or Tallon Zek. The file will have your character's name, then the server number - like this:
Absor_84.ini
3) If the number after your character name is 84, change that number to 56.
4) If the number after your character name is 85, change that number to 55.

Once you've done that, all of your specific setting should return.

We're sorry for the inconvenience.

Alan

Wayfarers Brotherhood Check
8/23/03, 12:46 PST | Muskie

And now a New Mini-Feature for PEQ in the leadup to the release of LDoN:

The Wayfarers Brotherhood Check!

For the people who apparently don't read their story windows(Shame on you!), the Wayfarers Brotherhood is the "Guild" formed by Morden Rasp, an NPC Barbarian Warrior, Tondal Di'Xevar, a Dark Elf Necromancer ( I think, the story doesen't elaborate on his actual class, he could be a shadowknight), and Nedaria, An erudite woman of an unknown class, apparently in a relationship with Morden. The adventures of these three characters and the Wayfarers Brotherhood are documented in the lore that is available through the (enormously useful if you're into tradeskills, etc ^^) Story window, activated by hitting alt+N ingame, or viewing the text files in the storyline foler in your root Everquaest folder. To generate a little more ingame leadup to LDoN (unlike some previous expansions, whe it was suddenly "Grobb has been taken over by FROGS! Ha ha! *points at trolls and laughs*), The characters documented in the stories are actual NPCs ingame, And the purpose of the wayfarers Brotherhood check is to Document the Position of the "guild" at all times, as they move around quite a bit.

At last I saw of them, Tondal and Morden were waiting near the Freeport PoK book for Calliav Ginuar, But the latest storyline update ("A Path with Purpose") Indicates that Tondal has been sent to Lavastorm to fetch Nedaria to Meet Morden and Calliav in the Commonlands. If any players manage to spot these NPCs, please Email me so I may update their exact locations.
