Friday, October 11th, 2002 -
Posted by: [Kyrthor] on October 12th, 2002 @ 04:28 PM
I know, I know.

Y'all been wonderin what we have been up to. Pretty much just the one thing. Vex Thal. Couple days over a month ago, we whacked Emporer for the first time, and started getting completed keys. Took about a week to get enough keys completed to look around. Poked about for another week and then put in a serious push to learn the zone...and learn it we did.

We took the grand tour of VT from start to finish, and I have something important to share with you. Years ago, I visited a friend attending a major university, and something about it struck me. Every damn building for this entire school (for 30,000 + students) looked like it had been designed by the same guy and built from the same load of bricks. No matter how interesting the folks I met were, every place I went felt monotonous. I kinda wondered to myself what kind of moron would design a university that way, and who would hire such a moron?

Heh.

We found him. He built Vex Thal.

So, while trying our best to ignore the initially cool looking and then mindlessly repetitive architecture....we took our alliance through the zone to test our mettle. There were wipeouts and some wasted time from respawn, but every boss we lined up died, and that is what it is all about. A couple bosses won the initial encounter as we learned them, but nothing took more than a second attempt leading all the way to mini Aten and Aten herself, which both dropped first time.

It has been a DAMN fine couple weeks learnin this place, and as always I am proud like all get out to be runnin with the folks I am...the finest bunch around.

Now, of course, that clears the expansion for us but for one guy....Seru. Comin for you shortly, white boy.
