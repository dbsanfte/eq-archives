Legion of Cabilis

Iksar Warriors enlist in the Legion of Cabilis for their training, the Legion are the protectors of Cabilis and are often found outside the city guarding the fortified entrances.

The young Iksar are often thankful for this protection and many have been saved by the swift blades of the Troopers falling on the enemy. The lowest rank of the Legion is as a footman, from this a strong and noble warrior can earn his pay as a Trooper and even become a Warlord. The Warlords lead individual 'teams', responsible for guarding the city from the dangers of the area or the training of new warriors. 

While not as feared as the Crusaders of Greenmist the Troopers of the Legion are no easy foes, many have attempted, and failed to enter the Iksar stronghold, paying with their lives. The Iksar will accept none other than their own kind entering their cities.
