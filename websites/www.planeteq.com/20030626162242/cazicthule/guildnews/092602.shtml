Today it was time for pansy snake to die again. We sized him up, stepped in, and laid into him. VI decided the lag spike flurry Kung Fu was wasted, so they got rid of it. Bad move on their part. We had around 70 folks again this time, and without any kind or special help from the lag, the Emporer proved once and for all what a joke he can be. Damn, that felt good...mostly.
