Test of Might

Head to Emerald Jungle, and in specific, loc +1250 +300. You will find there a Sentinel Spirit.

'Hail, a sentinel spirit'

    Give the spirit the gem

'I will continue what I have started'

'I am prepared to walk the path of might'

'What like the wasichu?'

'How do I find him?'

'What dark point ear?'

Next, head to Mistmoore and kill the Black Dire. He requires at least one group of levels 50+. If he is not up, he can be triggered by killing the Advisor.

    Loot the Black Dire Pelt

Head back to the spirit in Emerald Jungle.

    Give him the pelt

You are given your second epic item, Black Fur Boots! (I like to call them 'E Boots' like J Boots and T Boots)

Black Fur Boots
AC 9 Str+3 Sta+3 Wis+3 Agil+3
SV Disease+3 Sv Magic+5 SV Poison+3
Effect: sow, self only, no casting time

'What is Heyokah?'

'How can we rub it out?'

'What about the city's demise?'

'Tell me how it all began'

You are handed a combinable book with 6 slots.
