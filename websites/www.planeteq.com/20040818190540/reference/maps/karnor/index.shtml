Karnor's Castle


Karnor's Castle
By Questers of Norrath

Location: Karnor's Castle is a dungeon, the entrance to which, is located in The Dreadlands.

Continent: Kunark

EQ Version Needed: Ruins of Kunark

Bind: Bind self only

Creatures & NPCs: Decayed Prisoners, Decayed Soldiers, Drolvarg Bodyguards, Drolvarg Captain, Drolvarg Guardians, Drolvarg Pawbuster, Drolvarg Sentries, Drolvarg Warlord, Froglok Zol Knights, Guardians, Hangnail, Human Skeletons, Knight of Sathir, Sentry of Sathir, Skeletal Berserkers, Skeletal Captain, Skeletal Caretaker, Skeletal Guardians, Skeletal Jailor, Skeletal Lookout, Skeletal Scryer, Skeletal Warlord, Skeletal Watchers, Skeleton Warriors, Spectral Callers, Spectral Curate, Spectral Knights, Spectral Protectors, Spectral Turnkey, Undead Jailer, Venril Sathir, Verix Kyloxs remains, Venril Sathirs' Remains and Wulfware Bodyguard.

Zone Type: Indoor - Dungeon

Factions: Venril Sathir

Level Range: 45 - 60

Miscellaneous:

  • The faction for killing almost anything in this dungeon are negative for Venril Sathir. Fighting in this zone will quickly make it impossible to visit The Overthere outpost ever again.
