Monday - September 16, 2002 - Demana Azulene

Sometimes I wonder what the secret of our success is. We don't have enough clerics, we don't have enough enchanters, and Garthebe and Hidemi are some overworked mages. I thought about this for a long time, and I finally came up with the answer. I could give you the quality over quantity bit, and that's partly the truth, because the players in the DA/SS alliance just plainly own. However, there's more to our success than just plain old l33t skillz. I present to you the true secret to our success:

Yes, we're lemmings. We blindly follow our brave pullers into whatever spawn infested areas they happen to wander into, and somehow, things just die. We also follow the pinkness that is our fair leader, because, honestly, ya really can't miss a dude in bright pink robe.

On to the news...

Indulge me, if you will, in a bit of time travel. We're not going back too far, only 8 days or so, to last Sunday morning. Once again, the lemmings were sleepy, but they came, they followed, they even made all the correct turns and at last, the lemmings arrived at the feet of the King of the Snakes. And, as usual, the lemmings performed well, in spite of some seriously laggy conditions that resulted when they brought the zone down to "fix" certain encounters.

OK, let's now travel to the very recent past, this weekend to be specific. Two emperor kills netted us a significant amount of rifts, which meant VT was on the horizon. Saturday night's server crash meant we had some stuff to kill, and that stuff happened to be in VT.

First named in VT for DA and SS down, grats to Wildaan and Kaisi on their items of ownage.

Sunday was just something amazing. The pictures don't tell the whole story.

I know Itraer isn't uber, but shit, it died to the steamroller we were yesterday. There's some other stuff we killed (stuff that makes Cursed spawn, High Priest, Insanity Crawler. Yes, the Insanity Crawler. I've taken enough pictures of things that look like the stuff I clean out of my cat's litterbox to last me a lifetime, so no more pictures of nasty looking worms for joo. I think some Rhags died too, but they are poosae and really don't count).

Anyway, we came, we saw, we conquered, blah blah blah. And of course, people said funny stuff that I'll be posting right about...now.

Oh yes, Chadd is back, which explains why I now know about the Left Nut Award.

And this is the shit you get when you fall asleep in Vex Thal.

Wednesday, September 18, 2002
Day 177 & 178 - Knife Edge
Monday was another exp,shard,key,ore love fest. So I'll say no more.

Tuesday was a scheduled attempt at the Emperor now that he was "fixed" and spawned again. Since blood was killed earlier for some reason, we got to wait around a little while (ok, a bloody long while) until the Place Holder blood spawned. That was around 11:30 I think, and we had already spent 3 1/2 hours up on the 3rd floor waiting around killing stuff just to pass the time and get some insignia's. Well, suffice it to say, Blood spawns, we get buffed and move and kill and slaughter and hold and kill and slaughter and Blood dies pretty much like he always dies. Then the sekret tactics of sacraficing a ranger failed! Because I lived through the weaponshield, lived to get off the rampage list, and then proceeded to go LD everytime I tried to log back in. I think the no death made the Gods unhappy and they saw fit to keep me out of the fight one way or the other ;(

Anyway, it was a very bloody kill for us, but those are the most exciting, and you balance on a knife edge, death on one side, victory on the other and we kept falling to deaths side, but somehow through sheer willpower and determination, we did our balancing act well until the final percent where we tipped to victory. If you think fights in everquest are ever boring, you missed a great one last night, I think its why people play the game, to get to witness a momment like that, sure there are other momments you want, flawless kills, perfect execution, but sometimes its refreshing to know that you might not win. Most of the fights we do other then Emp are fights that we pretty much walk through, even cursed now.

Anyway, great fight, great victory and a few more lovely vex thal pieces for all, soon you'll see us in Vex Thal, I'm still willing to give my soul or 10k (which ever is deemed to be worth more) for a Maidens Eye Shard, send me tells if they drop, I'll come running!

Recruiting Level 60 People Pretty much everything but warriors (special circumstances .. we have alot of you already) ... If you like to play, we'd like to have you. Send a tell in game to Bonecrusha, Cerritus, or Effix and make a post in the "Everyone Forum" and seek us out.
