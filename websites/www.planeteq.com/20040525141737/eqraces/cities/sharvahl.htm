Shar Vahl

Shar Vahl is the city of the Vah Shir.

The city is built on top of a crater, with the edges forming a 'dry moat' around the base of Shar Vahl.

It is heavily fortified and guarded with only defended bridges offering a way out of the city. Shar Vahl is connected to Shadeweavers Thicket and Hollowshade Moor, the first of which is ideal training grounds. The edges of the city are also suitable for levels 1 to 8.

Inside Shar Vahl you will find warrior, shaman, beastlord, rogue and bard guilds, the Vah Shir consider training very important. The quests from the guild masters often reward with experience and useful items. 

All Vah Shir are expected to register as a citizen of Shar Vahl, once citizenship is secured the Vah Shir may then enter the guild of their choosing.

Merchants for all trade skills can be found in Shar Vahl along with the city bank, arena, Kings Palace and even a prison.

Vah Shir NPC Guilds

Beastlord - Elder Animist Sahdi 

Rogue - Master Taruun Rakutah
