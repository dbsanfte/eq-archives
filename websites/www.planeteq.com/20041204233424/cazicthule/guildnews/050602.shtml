What we've been up to....

Posted by: Druss on Monday, April 29th, 2002

Yeah, no pictures, no big list of names of the lucky recipients of phat lewtz. Deal with it. I'm pinch hitting for Mirax. Us Barbarians aren't exactly known for our eloquence or reporting skills.

Ok, what have we done since the last update...oh, a couple things.

Lets start with the AoW - After several attempts, we finally took this bad-boy down on 4/21. About a smooth as killing as you could imagine. Congrats Taraddar on his Ring of destruction, Shaofung on his Flayed Barbarian Leggings, and Talons on their Ring of Destruction and their flayed barbarian skin mask.

Amazingly, within hours of his death, our strategy on how we killed him was leaked on to CTC. With this supa-sekrit-ubah-strat, every guild on the server will soon be knocking down Rallos Zek's avatar. Missed the post on CTC? Afraid you missed your chance to be Uber, too? Well, out of the goodness of my heart (and that $50 check "Lords of the Ogre Dance" sent me), I'll repost it here. Ready?

ALL YOU HAVE TO DO IS HIT CH

Revolutionary? Yes. We had a team of over 50 trained chimps, brainstorming for months, before we came up with this radical new concept in raiding. Complete Heal. Combined with our patented "Bind-point-rush" tactics, the avatar of war was no match for the forces of Zerg. "

On a side note...the one chimp that suggested pulling the AoW to Tormax's room, then kicking him off the bridge into the wolf pit, has since been fired. Though I hear he found work with another guild, and that the "WHY PUSH IN WOLF PIT WHY WHY WHY" Strategy has worked out well for them.

Moving right along...

Well, a month has passed, and it seemed it was our turn for ToV North again. Cleared out 7 or so dragons on the 24th. Congrats to all who received phat lewtz. Server came down two days in a row, so we ended up going back to clear it again on the 26th and the 27th.

There was a Tormax killing tucked in there somewhere too.

And then on the 26th, we went to Ssra. Killed Rhag 1, The Glyphed, Exiled, and the Creator.

So...thats what CC/Talons got done.

Throw in the tedium of Luclin's key farming, a wee bit of AA, and a bunch of small stuff that died, and you have a week in the life of a CC member.

Anyway..hurry back, Mirax - bring back normal news page updates!

Tuesday, April 30, 2002
Can I get some friggin options please?
You know I read quite a few of the websites and message boards of the large guilds and see their rants and quietly nod my head. Almost immediately after I shrug my shoulders because more often than not not a damn thing changes. Here's one example out of a hundred. How many posts were there about fixing the Ring of Fire? How many were blatantly ignored in light of the 'Vision'? RoF 1.0 was FUN after the first 3 cycles and pretty challenging in that laggy room. What did Verant do though? Nerfed it into non-use effectively removing an option for people. Ring of Vulak same deal, option removed and whats this crap about load on the server screwing up the trigger? Run a load generator or better yet the login server off of test if you need to QA your code under load since anything is better than the login crap you have now. If you absolutely cant figure that out download Seti@home or open a porn server on it to simulate load for cripes sakes.

We the people who have playtested Verants content for the last 3+ years probably have a fairly good idea of what is fun and what is broken. Well Im here to tell you that Luclin is pretty damn broken. Yeah I know that Tigole and Furor and all the other Guildleaders have been saying that for quite some time but that has been a little bit more about the encounters themselves, risk vs reward and the number of timesinks in the game. I agree with them mostly but Im talking about being even more fundamentally broken. Here, I'll show you.

Kunarks end game zone was Veeshans Peak. To get there you had to build a key that was built from drops across the continent with a bottleneck at Trakanon. Ok fine you dont want everyone there so you have one PREDICTABLE spawn as the bottleneck, something everyone can count on. If there were several groups of people who wanted him rotations werent that difficult to work out since there werent that many who could do him at the time due to a younger server population.

Velious's end game zone was Sleepers Tomb. While the zone itself was broken with its 'dynamic content' crap and the warders should have been just a WEE bit more difficult than the first brood dragons you had to kill just to get into the zone, the key to get to it was alright. You had 5 options to gather keys for your guild and get in there. On top of that Verant had the start of a good idea in having 3 different lines of faction to keep an aging server population (more lvl 60's) from bumping head consistantly. Granted it would have been pretty damn nice if they had worked the storyline to allow for the Kael and maybe even Thurgadin ubers to be able to drop key pieces as well so we werent forced to kill our faction of choice but I'm asking for too much there now and in the end we had 5 options so thats good enough.

Enter Luclin, simply put the most poorly thought out expansion yet. It had potential when we started. There were some nice new ubermob abilities that we had to work out tactics for, a fairly in depth storyline and what looked to be 3 seperate factions (read options) on how we might be able to get into the end game zone of the expansion. Boy were we stupid, or maybe just deceived. Can anyone explain to me which brainiac decided that it would be a good idea for the Emperor of the Shissar to be the only uber to control access to Vex Thal? Couldnt you have had Seru conspire against the shissar and steal part of their sacred celestial power source so we didnt have to fight over the Emperor? Could you also explain to me why Lcea is such a puss? Why not make her on par with Seru or the Emperor so we have another option or make her trigger Katta's awakening/resurrection and have him awake with knowledge from the afterlife about the celestial power source?

As it stands right now you have an extremely aged server population all fighting to get to Vex Thal. On CT, which is one of the original launch servers, we have 3 alliances who have all killed AoW/Tunare with a 4th coming up pretty quickly. All are quite capable and all of us realize that the road to Vex Thal runs through Ssra. Before Tritons Emperor slaying you would find maybe 60 people in zone when a raid on the Arch Lich or High priest was in progress and maybe 20 people when there wasnt. Now the zone AVERAGES 110 people in zone all fighting over RANDOM SPAWNS. WTF!

Not only did you turn the zone into Sebilis 2.0 you made it impossible to establish any order since the spawns we are sitting there waiting on for 1-5 hours can spawn anywhere. As soon as trackers pickup a Commander, 50 people are rioting and stumbling over each other to make it dead. Add to that this bullshit new "We dont want to deal with spawn disputes" attitude from our quite lacking server GM's who would rather run mini best of the best tournaments than do anything else and you are just asking for alot of pissed off customers ready to tear each others throats out. I dont blame my competition for trying to monopolize the bottlenecked spawns to Vex Thal. I understand they want whats best for their people just like I want whats best for mine. I blame Verant for forcing us into the situation with poor planning.

The 3rd floor and basement portions of the Emperor key quest are fine due to their decent drop rates. The Commander portion of the key quest needs some serious help. Here are my suggestions to give people more options in Luclin, I'll even give you a couple different options to fix it. ;>

1) Use the Seru faction. Fix Seru's sword so that it summons power sources the same way the Emperor's sceptre does. If you insist on leaving his sword in its present broken state include a SMALL NON TIMESINK quest to extract the power sources from it. Charge his key quest to either require the reports portion to be done to get a 100 charge key or better yet unlimited charge key. That quest sucks so many hours out of our playtime I think you could justify it. If you want to leave it so we can get the key by only killing the minibosses make them not SUCK ASS. Yeah its real fun to kill those mini's, I think I'd rather shoot lead directly into my blood stream. While you're at it FIX THE DAMN PATHING AND WARPING.

2) Use the Katta faction. Buff up Lcea to Seru or Emperor level, RE-ITEMIZE her loot table and have her glean knowledge of the power source through some contact with the now dead/in stasis Katta. OPTIONALLY allow her to ressurrect/awaken Katta so we can fight him instead and have him drop an item that summons power sources. Make him on par with the Emperor or Seru and add Katta banes if you want or make him harder and dont add banes it doesnt matter.

3) Ok, ok I know you're lazy and dont want to put all that work into the game that you are now charging us even more for even though we're getting less support than ever before. That and I understand you are far too busy working on PoP to fix the bugs and completely unfun content in Luclin. Here's a suggestion for you though. Instead of raising prices to keep your profit margain well ahead of your costs, how about make your game better and attract new business? Its real nice of you to tax your loyal customers that have stuck with you through all the other games that have come out in EQ's lifecycle. Here's another suggestion. How about kick back PoP, take those developers and add them to the 'seperate crew' working on Luclin and get shit fixed THAT MUCH FASTER! Hell you could get the heal bug fixed in 2 weeks instead of a month and a half maybe. Anyway back to the point.

You're lazy. Fine, make some minor changes to the Emperor key quest and move the bottle neck for Vex Thal somewhere else. Increase the spawn rate of the commanders to be on par with the 3rd floor or basement portions of the quest. Lets be frank. Your precious Emperors cherry has been popped so you can quit worrying about delaying us from killing him. It obviously didnt work and the whole key quest within a keyquest is pretty lame anyway. Trust me the Emperor isnt just going to keel over and die to 20% of your subscriber base even if anyone could just walk up to him. Its going to take the best of the best to make him dead so lets just make him easier to get to and this whole ugly situation will go away and be replaced by nice happy rotations. If you really want to be asses, and I know you do because you like timesink mezzed customers, make the shards more difficult to obtain. THAT DOES NOT MEAN MAKE THEM MORE RARE. Instead make it take more tactics, smarts, organization, whatever to gather them instead of just randomly dropping off of Joe Q. Mob.

I know what you're thinking. You're thinking we cant possibly understand why you must design things the way you do. We cant possibly understand the business ramifications or difficulty in coding things the way I've suggested. You'd be surprised just how much we do understand. We're not stupid people just because we have 200+ played days in your game. The sad thing is everyone else probably understands it as well and someone, somewhere, is going to make a game that includes what we want and EQ is going to go the way of AO, AC, DAoC and all the other failed or failing MMORPGs. Hope you enjoy rubbing elbows with M59 in the not too distant future. Remember that game? Me neither......

I just gave you 3 options to fix Luclin and make it better than it currently is. I just gave you 3 options to retain customers and maybe even attract new ones or lure back retired ones. Its your choice to take them or not and Im sure you wont but maybe, just maybe, the kinder gentler Verant will consider them since 'the Vision' seems to have left with changing of the guard.

Oh and while I have you listening. FIX the bane/mitigation bullshit. Is it really necessary to force us to farm and waste time on your lame tradeskills just to have our melees gimped worse than us casters were in Kunark? I wouldnt wish that on anyone and let me tell you what, while I really enjoy the changes you are making to casters I wouldnt trade it for an inversely gimped melee crew in a million years. Bane weapons are a cool idea. I like not being able to damage a mob unless we have special weapons but once we get those weapons we shouldnt be reduced to 10dps. They are called BANES for a reason, look up the definition in your local dictionary. Let the banes be the only way for melees to hit the mob but let them hit for regular damage or *gasp* even more damage and increase the mobs HP or regen accordingly if you want a longer fight. Let EVERYONE feel like they are making a real contribution on these epic fights not just the select few. As it stands right now people are just going to bypass your timesink because its lame. Im sure some guild with an army of nukers is marching on these ubers right now. Oh and make bane arrows please so our rangers who have put in the hours to get AM3 dont see it wasted on the ubers.

Anyway, enough rant, Im wasting my breath.
