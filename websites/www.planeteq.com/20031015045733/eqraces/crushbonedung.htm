Hunting in the Crushbone Dungeon

The Crushbone Dungeon is the stronghold of the Orcs on Faydwer. It is constantly being attacked by the Elven and Dwarven races, it is believed that the Orcs will soon have to flee. Occasionally the Dark Elf Ambassador Dvinn can be found in The Throne Room in meeting with Emperor Crush. The Dark Elves have forged an alliance with the Orcs if the rumours and the evidence are taken as true.

The Crushbone Dungeon can take you up to level 17-20. You can hunt here from level 5, if you stay near the entrance. 

The most important thing to remember in Crushbone is to watch out for trains. If you hear someone shout TRAIN! and you are near the entrance, be prepared to have to zone. (If you are the one leading the train it is polite to shout train to warn others) 

The second biggest drawback with Crushbone is that it is almost always overcrowded. The Dwarven, High Elf and Wood Elf races all hunt in it. 

If you can find a suitable camp for your level (see below) and a good group you will have an enjoyable time, and the experience you receive won't be bad either. The loot isn't anything to write home about but Crushbone Belts and Pads can sell for anything between 1 and 3 platinum each. (They are also good for using yourself, hand in to Canloe Rusback in Kaladim for experience, gold and small armor)
