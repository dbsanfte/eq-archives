Hunting in Greater Faydark

Greater Faydark is the home of the Wood Elf and High Elf races. In the center of Gfay (as Greater Faydark is known) is Kelethin, the Wood Elf city. South East of Kelethin is the city of Felwithe, home of the High Elves.

The common place to hunt for a Wood Elf at level 1 to 5 is around the 'newbie' lift. (ask someone politely to take you there) The other alternative is just outside Felwithe Entrance (south east), this will also take you to 5 or 6.

You can hunt bats and wasps from level 1. At level 3 you can kill pixies, spiderlings and sometimes orc pawns. When you reach level 6 it is time to move on to Orc Hill.

Orc Hill is a collection of small tents, Orc Oracles, Centurions and Pawns guard these tents and will attack anyone who comes close enough. On top of Orc Hill the training High Elf, Dwarf and Wood Elf groups fight against the endless reinforcements the orcs bring to battle.

When you reach level 9 at Orc Hill you can fight just outside Crushbones Entrance. When you reach 11 or 12 you will want to move on inside Crushbone.

Camp Locations:

Bottom of Newbie Lift (Kelethin) (Levels 1-5)

Felwithe Entrance (Felwithe) (Levels 1-5)

The abandoned druid ring (Kelethin) (Levels 3-6)

Orc Hill (Kelethin) (Levels 6-9)

Crushbone Entrance (Levels 8-11)
