Testament of Vanear

Head to North Qeynos, to the Pub next to the pond. Hanging around there will be an Erudite called Moodoro Finharn. If you give him 2 gold pieces he will give you a book (container, lore item). Then he tells you that two pages are missing from the book. To get the first page you play cards with Moodoro. Give him 4 gold piece and he will give you a playing card. if it is the Jester, give it back to him and he will give you the first page. Morodoo sometimes hangs out at one of the sewer entrances to Qeynos, this makes it possible for evil races to complete the quest with little or no faction work.

Next, wander off to High Hold Pass. Head toward the orc side, you will come to a lake. Jump in and swim down through a tunnel (its short, no need for Enduring Breath) until you come to a hidden camp of smugglers. There is only one named NPC there, Dyllin Starshine. He is on a 7 hour spawn timer (some say 12 hours), level 16 and a very easy kill. The other smugglers WILL assist him and some conned red to a level 40. Faction hits when you kill him are: -Corrupt Qeynos Guard
+Guards of Quenos
-Kane Bayle
-Circle of Unseen Hands
-BloodSabers
The faction hits for him are pretty large, so be aware after that of potentially slipping to KOS in Qeynos.
