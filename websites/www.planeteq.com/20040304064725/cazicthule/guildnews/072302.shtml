The night started off rough, having to airdrop Woushi on the way in, which now, looking back at it, seemed a harder task then taking down AoW himself. Not many people were on the same page, which was a worrysome thing at first, only because of the huge task ahead of us.

We got together, pulled our collective heads out of our asses, and set up for the fight. It seemed like seconds passed and the Idol was pulled...and again, in my opinion, struggled with the mob. Things weren't looking too good from my perspective - for good reason. And with just enough time to take a short breath and in the blink of an eye, the Avatar of War was charging our camp.

Now, personally I have high expectations of my guildmates, and our earlier performance wasn't our best show...but nothing could prepare me for what I was about to witness...

The Avatar of War was completely owned - FLAWLESSLY. Superb tanking by the TRULL himself, Foulbreath (only tank to take beats the entire fight), flawless healing by the BEST FUCKING CLERIC CREW IN THE GAME, and no deaths.

Relentless 1, Avatar of War 0

Loot was monk legs, ring, and sword, huge grats to Mintern, Thornsong, and Rabrak on your phatty stuffs. Congratulations to every member of this guild on that AoW kill, you are truely Relentless.

So....you might think somethin in there is what I am feelin good about, but you'd be wrong. What has got me happy? My pavlovian conditioning has finally broken and I can spill the beans on how we kill what we do....


Ya see, back long ago in ages past, Piro saw the movie "A Clockwork Orange". Might seem innocent enough but it was a sad, sad day for us. Ya see, it gave Piro an idea that lead to the secret behind DA/SS.

Every person that joins the alliance is given weeks of intense response/reward conditioning. Then a small appliance is attatched to the members PC. It's really quite simple.

Each time the person does something they are supposed to do (heal, taunt, cast DS on a ranger) a small bell rings by their monitor and a Cheeto rolls down a food dispenser next to their keyboard and the member is allowed 1 second to eat. If we DON'T do it, we don't eat. Remarkably effective.

They are likely gonna cut off my supply for squealing, so if ya don't mind, please save any Cheetos ya come across and email them to kyrthor@shadowedsoul.com, I could really use them....
