Quiz 27: Plane of Fear
posted by Kwill |September 20, 2002

Written by Rinerdar Smyth, Ranger of Prexus.

The Plane of Fear...do you know it and love it? or just know it and hate it? Find out...



1.Who rules the Plane of Fear? a. Tunare
b. Cazic Thule
c. Bristlebane
d. Innoruk 2. Where can the portal to the Plane of Fear be found? a. Ferrott
b. Oasis of Marr
c. Lavastorm
d. Toxxulia Forest 3. What MOB drops the ranger's Thorny Vine armor? a. boogeyman
b. glare lord
c. tentacle tormenter
d. a gorgon 4. Which of the following is NOT a demi-god? a. Terror
b. Fright
c. Horror
d. Dread 5. Which of the following is part of the wizard epic? a. An enraged golem
b. Amygdalan knight
c. Ikssar broodling
d. Wraith of a Shissir 6.Which of the following is NOT a MOB found in fear? a. Amygdalan warrior
b. Amygdalan shadowknight
c. Irak Altil
d. phoboplasma 7. What race can walk up to Cazic Thule and not be attacked, regardless of class? a. Erudites
b. Gnomes
c. Dark elves
d. Iksars 8. Which of the following does a Dracoliche drop? a. Beserker's Ring
b. Ball of the Everliving Golem
c. Bloodfire
d. Staff of the Earthcaller 9. How do you get to the Plane of Fear? a. wizard port only
b. wizard or druid port
c. have to have a key
d. walk in 10. What MOB drops greenmist armor? a. Nightmare
b. Boogeyman
c. Amygdalan knight
d. Tentacle tormentor 11. Glarelords are hated by a raid party because of their ability to ______. a. Area Effect Damage Over Time
b. Area Effect Debuff
c. Instantly kill
d. Area Affect Dismiss Pet 12. Fright Fingers drop what armor? a. Umbral Platemail
b. Carmine
c. Anthermion
d. Vermiculated 13. The druid headpiece in this plane is a type of what? a. Skullcap
b. Hood
c. Crown
d. Helm 14. What is the name of the druid armor found in this plane? a. Carmine
b. Thorny Vine
c. Anthermion
d. Vermiculated 15. Scarelings look similar to a... a. Imp
b. Zombie
c. Ghoul
d. Wilowisp 16. A nightmare is a type of ... a. specter
b. horse
c. golem
d. skeleton 17. Which of the following is NOT a MOB with a pet? a. Amygdalan Knight
b. Frightfinger
c. Glarelord
d. Worry Wraith 18. What line of spells do not work against the Dracholiche? a. Stun
b. Area Affect
c. Magic
d. Undead 19. Most monsters found in Fear are... a. Undead
b. Naturebound
c. Animals
d. Enthralled 20. Cazic Thule is a ... a. Necromancer
b. Shadowknight
c. Cleric
d. Enchanter

That's it! You're Done!
