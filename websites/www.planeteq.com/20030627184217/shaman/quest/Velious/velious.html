The continent of Velious, released in the expansion pack 'The Scars of Velious', bought with it 4 new armour sets for the Shaman class. Three of those armours were quested, requiring a rare armour drop and three gems. The final armour set was identical to the easiest to obtain armour set of the first three, other than it's name. That one was obtained on the Plane of Growth.

The armour sets were:

  • Spirit Weavers (aka Growth)
  • Rune Crafters (aka Thurgadin)
  • Spirit Callers (aka Kael)
  • Wolf Callers (aka Skyshrine)

To get the Thurgadin armour, you need 3 gems and the correct piece of Corroded Chainmail armour. (from Giants in Kael Drakkel)

To get the Kael Drakkel armour, you need 3 gems and the correct piece of Anceint Tarnished Chainmail. (from Temple of Veeshan, Non-Testing halls)

To get the Skyshrine armour, you need 3 gems and the correct piece of Unadorned Chainmail armour. (from Temple of Veeshan, Halls of Testing.)

 

 

 

 

 

 

Some of the items have effects that can be activated by clicking on them.

  • Boots: Extinguish Fatigue
  • Bracer: Ultravision
  • Legs: Abolish Disease
  • Breastplate: Ritious Health

I am not going to spell out the exact stats of each item here, but I will say that the Kael armour tends to have higher AC, whilst the Skyshrine has better non-AC stats. The Thurgadin/Growth armour have lesser stats than the other two sets, but are easier to obtain.
