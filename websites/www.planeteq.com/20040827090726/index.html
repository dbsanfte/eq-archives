   Wednesday, August 25, 2004

Developer Chat
8/25/04, 9:28 PST | Kwill

The entire transcript of the Developer Chat hosted last night by EQStratics can be found on their website. Some highlights include:
  • Content will be available for all levels, but the expansion is geared toward level 47 - 70. There is only one keyed zone in the expansion.

  • Task system: you will need to be level 3 to do a task, but if you finish the tutorial you should be level 5 when you are done, and ready to do a task. The task system is designed for everyone and is a nod to the casual player.

  • No solo instances will be added at this time.

  • AA pricing will be lower than Gates, and the price of AAs for Gates will be reconsidered.

  • Pets are getting a boost in the new expansion, particularly mage pets.

  • Backflagging will be replaced with something more user-friendly.

  • For non-gating classes, there will be a "gate to friendly city" AA, or you can use a "stone of marking" to activate a gate to a bind point elsewhere. There will be bind points in OoW for binding and gating classes.

  • There will be a new title feature that allows you to earn titles and choose which one to display.
    • There is much more in the chat, so check it out.

    Exclusive Omens of War NPC
    8/13/04, 13:41 PST | Kwill

    For our screenshot of the day, we have direct from SOE a glimpse at a Pyrilen:
    These elementals of heat and flame are the minions of an even greater planar race that have been granted to Mata Muram for travel between worlds. Their true masters intend to seed Discord into as many realms as possible. The Pyrilen have the ability to hone in on rifts in the fabric that separates worlds. They then use their magic to open and sustain these portals.

    These creatures are chaotic and often enjoy tormenting their employers. Keeping their allegiance is difficult even under the best circumstances. And if they are crossed, there will be trouble. The elementals and their superiors have a deal with Mata Muram -- when he invades new worlds, these elementals are meant to be given full access and power in any of the planar realms that are found in trade for their services.

    Mata Muram would likely have moved to new worlds by now, but the pyrilen are no longer cooperating. They believe Mata Muram planned to betray them and have Norrath's planes and pantheon for himself. Both Mata Muram and the pyrilen are now waiting in their most recent acquisition, the realm of Kuua, while maintaining a tenuous relationship.

    See the actual NPC in these exclusive screenshots of one in her natural environment in the Omens of War expansion.
