This is just a checklist, for full details please refer to the walk through on this site.

Orb of Frozen Water
      Kill Lord Bergurgle in Lake Rathetear, loot "Lord Bergurgle's Crown".
      Turn in crown to Shmendrik Lavawalker spawning Natasha Whitewater.
      Kill the "Spirit of Flame", Loot the "Damaged Goblin Crown".
      Give the "Damaged Goblin Crown" to Natasha Whitewater. Receive "Ornate Sea Shell (I)".
      Turn in "Ornate Sea Shell (I)" to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep and receive "Coral Statue of Tarew" in return.
      Give "Coral Statue of Tarew" to 'a seeker'.
      Kill Plasmatic Priest and loot "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe."
      Kill Lord Gimblox in Solusek A and loot "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring".
      Give "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe" to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, receive the "Orb of Frozen Water".
      Give "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring" to Natasha Whitewater, receive "Ornate seashell (II)".

Orb of Clear Water
      Give "Ornate Seashell (II)" to Naxot Deepwater in Burning woods, receive "Message to Natasha".
      Kill Ixiblat Fer and loot "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer".
      Kill Overking Bathezed and loot a "Singed Scroll".
      Give "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer" and "Singed Scroll" to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, receive the "Orb of Clear Water".
      Give the "Message to Natasha" to Natasha Whitewater, receive a "Shimmering Pearl".

Orb of Vapor
      Give Zordak Ragefire in Solusek B the "Shimmering Pearl", receive the "Swirling Pearl" and the container "Zordak's Box of Bindings".
      Kill random mobs in Skyfire Mountains, and obtain 4 "Pearlescent Fragments".
      Give Warder Cecilia "An Assembled Pearlescent Shard", receive a "Pearlescent Seal"
      Kill resulting Zordak Ragefire, and dragon form, Zordakalicus Ragefire, loot "Impure Heart of Zordak Ragefire".
      Combine the "Swirling Pearl" & the "Pearlescent Seal" in the "Impure Heart of Zordak Ragefire", to make "Zordak Ragefire's Heart".
      Give "Zordak Ragefire's Heart" to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, receive the "Orb of Vapor".

Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
      Give the "Orb of Frozen Water", the "Orb of Clear Water", and the "Orb of Vapor" to Jhassad Oceanson, receive the "Orb of the Triumvirate".
      Give the "Orb of the Triumvirate" to the Avatar of Water to receive the "Water Sprinkler of Nem'Ankh".
