Quiz 4: Monks
 by Aracnakat | March 6, 2001



This test asks you all there is to know about the flipping, kicking and punching monks.  How much do you know about them? 



1. How many races can elect to become monks? a. 1
b. 2
c. 3
d. 4 2. According to Verant, what are the four primary statistics for monks? a. Strength, Stamina, Agility & Dexterity
b. Stamina, Agility, Strength & Charisma
c. Dexterity, Strength, Charisma & Wisdom
d. Agility, Stamina, Strength & Wisdom 3. At what level are monks able to first use the round kick skill? a. 1
b. 3
c. 5
d. 7 4. If you were to create an agnostic human monk, what town would you start life in? a. Freeport
b. Qeynos
c. Surefall Glade
d. All of the above. You get to choose. 5. What is the name of the monk guild master in Freeport? a. Asnod Marnok
b. Reyia Beslin
c. Velan Torresk
d. Puab Closk 6. What name does the monk guild in Cabalis known as? a. Allize Tae Ew
b. Ashen Order
c. Silent Clan Fist
d. Swifttails 7. Which one of the following skills do monks first get at level 3? a. Round Kick
b. Safe Fall
c. Sneak
d. Tiger Claw 8. What "creatures" would an Iksar monk need to kill to obtain the Shackle of Stone (this question assumes that the monk already has the first two shackles)? a. Goblins
b. Froglocks
c. Iksar Bandits
d. Sarnak Bezerkers 9. Above what weight do monks begin to lose their additional 1 AC per level (over their normal AC gained from equipment and stats)? a. Above 14.9
b. Above 19.9
c. Above 29.9
d. Above 34.9 10. At what level do monks first gain the feign death skill? a. 10
b. 13
c. 15
d. 17 11. Which of the following is not a monk skill? a. Dragon Punch
b. Tiger Claw
c. Monkey Bite
d. Eagle Strike 12. How many disciplines do monks get? a. 8
b. 9
c. 10
d. 11 13. What title does Verant give a monk at level 60? a. Disciple
b. Grand Master
c. Master
d. Ninja 14. What is the name of the monk epic item? a. Blessed Fists
b. Fists of Fire
c. Celestial Fists
d. Fists of Whup Ass 15. Which one of the following skills do Iksar monks get that their Human counterparts do not? a. Dragon Punch
b. Reptile Sweep
c. Tail Rake
d. Tiger Claw 16.Which of the following disciplines "hastes" a monk? a. 100 Fist
b. Ashenhand
c. Inner Flame
d. Whirlwind 17. In which zone would a monk begin their quest for the Golden Star Arm Wraps? a. Kael Drakkal
b. Plane of Growth
c. Skyshrine
d. Thurgadin 18. Which of the following choices is not a monk discipline? a. Stone Stance
b. Thunder Kick
c. Silent Fist
d. Lightening Flash 19. What is the name of the monk only armor found in the Plane of Fear? a. Apothic
b. Silver Moon
c. Resplendent
d. Shiverback 20. What is the major difference between the hand to hand combat ability of Iksars and Human monks? a. Iksars do more damage per level.
b. Humans have a faster hit delay at later levels.
c. Iksars gain an extra hit because of tails.
d. Nothing, they are both identical.

That's it! You're Done!
