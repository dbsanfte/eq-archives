History

Wood Elves are probably the most common of the Elven races. Created by Tunare they love the wilderness and even the Wood Elves city of Kelethin is built in the trees. 

The Wood Elves can often be found as Druids or Rangers, protecting nature as Tunares Guardians.

Wood Elves are much less ordered and noble as the High Elves, they are a fun loving, playful and sometimes mischievous race. Through their love of music and art many Wood Elves often become adventuring Bards. 

In general they are a kind, tolerant race and it is Wood Elves and Humans which Half Elves are born from.

 

Race Specific Tips

Home sweet Home 

The Wood Elves city of Kelethin is in a very good starting location. The nearby Dungeon of Crushbone, and the cities of Felwithe and Kaladim give the Wood Elf plenty to explore. 

Train at Home

The Crushbone Dungeon makes a good area to train from level 8 right up until level 17 or even as far as 20, fighting in the Throne Room.
