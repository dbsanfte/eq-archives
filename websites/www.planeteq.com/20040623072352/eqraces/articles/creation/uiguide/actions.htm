Actions Window

The Actions Window has four tabs, General, Skill, Melee and Social. The general tab displays 6 preset (can't be altered) buttons. 

 

Find opens up a window which lists locations that may have mystical paths open to them. Mystical paths are essentially route markers and you can follow the swirling path to the location you want to go to.

 

Invite invites your target to join your group (only available if the target is a player and you are not in a group, or are the leader of the group.

 

Disband disbands your character from the group.

 

Camp (Exit) only works when sitting down and not under attack. It begins a count down of 30 seconds and at the end will log you out of the game.

 

Sit will sit your character down, sitting characters will regenerate hit points and mana faster than they would standing.

Skills Tab

 

The Skills Tab allows you to customise the 6 available buttons to activate a chosen skill.

 

When you click on a button it opens up the Choose a skill menu. All your available skills appear and by clicking on one, the button becomes the activate button for that skill.

 

 

Melee Tab

 

The melee tab gives you two buttons that cannot be changed and a further 4 that may become activate buttons for melee skills.

 

The melee attack button will set your character to attack your target, your character will continue attacking with its melee weapons until the button is pressed again or your target dies.

 

The range attack button will set your character to attack your target with ranged weapons. Your character will continue attacking with its ranged weapon until the button is pressed again or your target dies.

 

You can't attack with both range and melee at the same time, you can have only one activated during combat. Though you can change which is activated during combat.

 

When you click on one of the 4 buttons it opens up the Choose a skill menu, only melee skills will be available, such as kick or taunt. By clicking on a skill the button becomes the activate button for that skill.

 

 

Socials Tab

 

The Socials Tab gives you access to 10 sets of 10 buttons. The first set of 10 is preset and cannot be changed.

 

AFK sets your character to AFK mode. (If anyone sends you a tell an AFK message is displayed, next to your name AFK will also be visible on zone /who commands and for anyone near you)

 

Anon sets your character to anonymous mode. Any /who commands will result in your name but not your guild, level or class.

 

Split enables automatic loot splitting.

 

Bug opens up a bug reporting window.

 

Consider will use the /consider command on your target.

 

Duel will invite your target to begin a duel (only applies to players)

 

Feedback will open a feedback submission window.

 

Hail will say Hail, Target, where target is your current target's name.

 

Played will display your played time in total and this session (session starts when you log in, finishes when you log out) and also your characters birthday (creation date).

 

Time will display the current in game time and the real time according to your system (computers) clock.

 

GM List will do a /who command searching for Game Master or Customer Service Representatives.

 

Wave will make your character do the /wave emote.

Setting a Social Key

 

By right clicking on an empty social key (button) you will open an Edit Social window. Here you can change the buttons name and what the button does.

 

You are given 5 lines to write commands that will be executed when the button is pressed. Usually it is best to keep these to two lines.

 

If you don't specify a command and just type text then it will become the /say command by default, and when pressed that text will be said by your character. Some other commands include:

 

/gsay (Group Say)

/who Bob (Searches for players called Bob in your current zone)

/who Bob ALL (Searches for players called Bob on the entire server)

/tell Bob Hi (Sends a private message "tell" saying Hi to Bob)

/r Hi (Replies to last tell sent with message Hi)
