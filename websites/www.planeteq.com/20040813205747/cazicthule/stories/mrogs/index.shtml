Mrog's Stoopid Brudders
by Mrog My Momma cawled me Mrog when born. Mrog was firstest borned from Momma. Momma sez dat Mrog be priddiestest Ogress in da whole of Oggok. Mamma away right, so Mrog da priddiestest Ogress for sure!

Momma is dead now. Nawt know why momma was kilted. Before Momma dies she had thre more childrens. Dey is Flog, Phrog, & wittlest brudder Taog. Dey all stoopidestest brudders in all Oggok. Momma sed dey stoopid awl da time. Mamma away right, so Flog, Phrog & Taog is da stoopidest Ogres for sure!


Left to right: Bouncer, Flog, Mrog, Taog, Phrog, Bouncer - Outside of Oggok


Before Mamma was kilted she ask mes Mrog to takes gowd cares of da stoopid brudders. Mes agreed cause Mamma cried dat dey stoopid an need Mrog to wook out for dem.

Now Mrog, Flog, Phrog & wittle brudder Taog travel da lands. Someday we be biggest and strongestes Ogres in Oggok, for now wes just da piddiestest and stoopidestest.

Da other day wes was travelin an Zugtess (a very ugwy Ogress) asked me brudder Phrog and Mes to hewp her. She had awl her stuffs stolen by da nasty Ogre Ohumim an dey was gowna duel. She asked us to heals her while dey battles. Phrog an mes hewped as much we could (we is Shamans). Zugtess is ugwy, but she is nice an we helped and she win da duel but da nasty Ohumim would nawt give her da stuffs back. Mes remember dat Ohumim and sez to da stoopid brudders dat whens we biggestest Ogres dat we hunt and kills him for da nice Zugtess. Also, Mrog finks dat da stoopid brudder Phrog lik da Zugtess an want ta mates wif her. She too ugwy for da Phrog so Mrog will crush him skull so he nawt so stoopid.

Later dat same days wes met wif da cutestest Ogre Kogg. Kogg is cutestest an he wants mate wif Mrog but da stoopid brudders scareded him aways. Mrog mak da froggie stu for Kogg but he no come to cave for eats. Dis make Mrog sad an she smash brudders in face for scare da Kogg.

Den after later we mets da nice Snagy. He nawt as cutest as da Kogg, but he asks to cum join us and da stoopid brudders sez no. Dey sez no 'cause dey jelaous of da Snagy probwly stwonger den dem an 'cause dey stoopid. Maybe Snagy mates wif Mrog? Mrog no ask, she forgots.

Brudders gots losts many times dat day but we finaly found da Trwolls swamps. (Dem Trwolls is UGWY wif da biggestest noses!) We kilted many froggies in da swamps which maks for da gowd stu for da eats times! Brudders gots lost in da swamps and Mrog had to find - Dey so stoopid!

We mets some nice Ogres mens at da swamps. Der was Thudd & Tuggaq who was nice and cute too. Stiwl nawt as cutest as da Kogg, but dey nice an stoopid brudders nawt let Mrog ask to mates. (Dey always won't leave mes alone!)

Many times dat nite we hads to go homes wif our foods an to twain at da guilds. We is getting bigs an Momma would be prouds of us. Me brudders is stoopid but mes luvs dem anyhow. Dey okay brudders for stoopid poopie heads me guess.

Mrog
Priddy sisah of Phrog, Taog, & Flog

At this point, Mrog and her brothers are all around level 7. More adventures to come as they grow in their disfunctional family!
