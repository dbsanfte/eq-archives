Quiz 22: Estate of Unrest
by Aracnakat | July 31, 2001



When you enter this dungeon for the very first time you get the message, "You have entered the Estate of Unrest." That message always brought a chill to my bones as this dungeon is without a doubt my all-time favorite haunt. Come with me now .. and enter, if you dare.


1. When you exit Unrest, what zone do you find yourself in? a. Siren's Grotto
b. Rathe Mountains
c. Dagnor's Caldron
d. Butcherblock Mountains 2. How many exits/entrances are in Unrest? a. 1
b. 2
c. 3
d. 4 3. What types of creatures are commonly found in this dungeon? a. Frogloks
b. Orcs
c. Undead
d. Kobolds 4. There is a ghost named Garanel Ruksif found deep within Unrest that sometimes drops some nice boots, what race is he? a. Dwarf
b. Gnome
c. Ogre
d. High Elf 5. What creature would you need to kill to obtain a Bloodstained Tunic? a. Festering Hag
b. Undead Barkeep
c. Zombie Of An Unrest Noble
d. Undead Knight 6.Approximately what level are the Festering Hags? a. 10
b. 20
c. 30
d. 40 7. Of the following, which is the highest level? a. Jack O Lantern
b. Torklar Battlemaster
c. Festering Hag
d. Khrix Fritchoff 8. What two types of "common" armor are frequently found in Unrest? a. Banded & Cloth
b. Cloth & Bronze
c. Bronze & Ringmail
d. Ringmail & Banded 9. Khrix Fritchoff is a nasty little fellow that lives in Unrest, what race/class is he? a. Dwarf/Warrior
b. Gnome/Necromancer
c. Dark Elf/Shadow Knight
d. Halfling/Cleric 10. There is a Quest related NPC which spawns in this dungeon named "Serra," what does she look like? a. A Werebat
b. A Zombie
c. A Skeleton
d. A Ghoul 11. Which creature in this dungeon drops the Thick Leather Apron? a. Undead Barkeep
b. Dark Boned Skeleton
c. Crazed Ghoul
d. Undead Knight 12. What class are the Festering Hags? a. Clerics
b. Enchanters
c. Necromancers
d. Wizards

13. There is a high-elf enchanter imprisoned in Najena named Linara Parlone. She asks you to kill someone in Unrest and bring her back a key. Who does she ask you to kill? (This quest results in the Mystic Cloak) a. Garanel Ruksif
b. Khrix Fritchoff
c. Priest of Najena
d. Lesser Blade Fiend 14. What creature found in Unrest drops a 5 damage, 21 delay, piercing weapon known as Pugius? a. Mortuary Fungus
b. Dark Boned Skeleton
c. Lesser Blade Fiend
d. Tormented Dead 15. Approximately what level is the Gnomish Spelunker? a. 10
b. 20
c. 30
d. 40 16. From the following, what item does the Reclusive Ghoul Magi drop? a. Dusty Bloodstained Gloves
b. Festering Cloak
c. Gladius
d. Ivory Bracelet 17. In what zone, closest to Unrest, would you find a druid portal? a. Steamfont Mountains
b. Dagnor's Cauldron
c. Butcherblock Mountains
d. There's a druid portal right in Unrest 18. Serra is a part of what classes Epic Quest? a. Cleric
b. Bard
c. Mage
d. Warrior 19. When EQ first opened, Festering Hags had a special ability/spell which they no longer have. What was it? a. They could charm
b. They could stun
c. They could summon
d. They were invisible 20. What type of creature, found in Unrest, must a Mage kill as part of their Epic Quest? a. Tentacle Terror
b. Crazed Ghoul
c. Reanimated Hand
d. Werebat

That's it! You're Done!
