The Captain Makes It Happen!

Posted by: Druss on Monday, September 30th, 2002 @ 11:41 PM

The guild alcoholics came up with a revolutinary strat to take our friend the Emp down. We would invoke a the aid of a powerful, mystical pirate ally to help take him down...the infamous "Captain Morgan".

The summoning ritual consisted of every member of CC downing a 5th of Captain Morgan's spiced rum prior to the emperor fight. Low and behold, he appeared...

Other than the nifty goatee and moustache, though...he didn't seem to do a whole hell of a lot, and the emp ended up kicking our asses again. Sigh.

Yes, each time, we get closer, learn more, etc. We know many guilds took 10-15 attempts to get him down, and due to the crowds on our server, we're sitting on attempt 7...but its still frustrating as hell to be stuck at this point.
