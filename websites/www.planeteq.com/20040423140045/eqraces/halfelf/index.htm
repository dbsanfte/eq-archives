History

 

Half Elves are born from the union of Elves and Humans, but are not considered to 'belong' to either race. The life of a Half Elf tends to be lonely, as an outsider, though many find a home within the guilds and societies of their professions; often becoming Bards, Druids, Rangers and Paladins. 

 

The Half Elves are a kind, warm people to those who show they can see beyond the 'Half Breed' prejudices.  

 

The magical energies of the Elves seem to have deserted the Half Elves and they tend to be stronger and hardier, similar to the Humans. They are however, more nimble and dexterous than Humans and seem to have retained the Elves natural agility, as well as their ability to see in the dark.

 

Race Specific Tips

Easy Access

Half Elves get more choice in starting cities than other races, take advantage of this and take the time to explore and find the benefits of each city.

Land Ahoy!

A Half Elf starting in Kelethin will find that the docks near Kaladim can take them to Freeport, a popular choice for starting characters. 

Best of both worlds

Half Elves receive the strength and stamina of a Human, the agility and dexterity of a Wood Elf, and even night vision thrown into the bargain too. A popular choice for someone who is interested in playing a Human but would like to be able to see at night (a big disadvantage with Humans at low levels) and look that little bit unique.
