Sunday afternoon rolled around, and we realized something was amiss. There was great imbalance in the universe. A "good" god had fallen, leaving the Evil gods free reign over Norrath. Only one thing could restore peace and balance to our souls...

Or maybe we were just bored on a sunday afternoon...
