Beginning the quest

The quest can be started in a multitude of ways, but they all involve killing something. The things you have to kill are listed below, but you only need to kill one of these items.

  • Captain Surestout (loc +800 -5400, Ocean of Tears)
  • Blinde the Cutpurse (on cyclops hill, Rathe Mountains)
  • Peg Leg (loc +260 +1650, Butcherblock Mountains)
  • Iksar Outcast/Manslayer (marauder caves, Field of Bone)

Once you have killed one of these, a lesser spirit will spawn. The conversations will sometimes be long and drawn out, so I will only put here what you need to do, and actions of the spirits, or what you need to do.

'Hail a lesser spirit'

'Yes'

'He threatened my life'

'Who are you?'

'I wish to know more'

    The spirit will give you a gem

     

'You can buy booze'

This part of the quest takes part in North Freeport, and starts in the sewers in the north west part of town. Find Bondl Felligan:

'Hail, Bondl Felligan'

    Give Bondl the gem

Follow him when he moves and when he stops he will speak to you.

'You can buy booze'

    Go to Jade Tiger's Den, 3rd floor middle room

'Hail, a greater spirit'

'I follow Justice'

    Go to the next room and speak to another spirit

'Hail, a greater spirit'

'What Heyokah?'

    The spirit will give you a gem

'I will walk the path of the Heyokah'

    The spirit will despawn

 

Test of Patience

This is arguably the most annoying part of the quest, but it is a good idea.

Head to Eruds Crossing (wisps island) and find Ooglyn. If she isnt there, her respawn is about 2 hours.

This part is best done with a druid, or a ranger and wizard, as you'd be best suited to have evac and track. You also need Enduring breath, sow and levitate up.

'Hail Ooglyn'

    Give her the gem

Ooglyn will run off. You should now head to loc -1600 +2400 and head underwater. Down there you will find a number of spirits, if they arent there just wait, they will be. Remember, this is all about patience. (The loc MAY be -1600 +4200, please post if this is the case)

'Hail, Abe the Abandoned'

'Who are you Abe?'

'What is the Combine?'

    Wait a few moments and he'll speak again...

'I will give them the arrow'

A few minutes he will say that he has found the answer, and tell you to follow him... DON'T!

    Wait there and sooner or later a greater spirit will spawn.

'Hail, a greater spirit'

'What is the next path?'

You recieve another gem

Test of Wisdom

For this part it is best to take your Druid/Ranger with you again. Head to West Karana, get your Druid or Ranger to track the wandering spirit (looks like a wisp) near the locaton -500 -5300. Once you find it Hail it untill it stops.

'Hail, a wandering spirit'

    Give the spirit the gem

Head to Rathe Mountains, find Tabien the Goodly (loc +6300 +1550) and kill him. Loot his shield (Marrs Promise) note that this shield is GIANT in size, so it is advised that you should have a spare inventory slot, with no bag in it.

Also in Rathe Mountains you will be able to find Glaron the Wicked (loc +2900 -1900). Kill him, he will drop two items, Woe and Envy.

Take Woe, Envy and Marrs Promise back to the wandering spirit in West Karana.

    Give the spirit all three items.

'What shield of falsehood?'

You will be given your first epic item, the Shield of Falsehood!

Shield of Falsehood
AC16
STR+3, WIS+5, INT+5, DEX-3, CHA-5, MANA+30

'What last path?'

You recieve a gem
