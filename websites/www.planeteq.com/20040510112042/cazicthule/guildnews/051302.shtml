Patches and Snakes...

Posted by: Druss on Saturday, May 11th, 2002

So, because we all haven't seen enough of the zone lately, we decided to kill something other than Commanders post patch.

On Thursday - we killed the Glyphed, Exiled, and took a shot at the Cursed. I think we might have actually moved his HP bar, but hell, it was a learning exp. Killed Rhag 2, as well /yawn.

So...with another patch on Friday, we returned to Ssra, and killed the Glyphed and the Exiled (in what could quite possibly be the sloppies kill I've ever seen on that mob...blah). Took another shot at the cursed, refining our strategy somewhat, and got him down to 45%. Next time, he dies. After wrapping up in the basement, we moved up and smacked down the Creator. The increased spawn rate of his guards make it a fun fight - the adds actually killed 3 of us during that fight, but he's still pretty damned trivial now.

Loot? Well, CC broke a longstanding tradition and actually got our Casters some loot. In fact, thats pretty much all the mobs dropped the last two days.

Kenji, our fearless GM, picked up a copy of Speed of the Brood, and the newly upgraded Memory. Sehria picked up a copy of "Mask of the Hunter" and a Geomantic Focus Crystal. Uzir picked up the new shawl from the Glyphed. Brendayan picked up the newly revised and Uber "Willful dirk of domination" (Thanks VI, for actually fixing some of this loot). Murf received a "Headband of Many visions". Tikana got some shiny new Golem greaves. Boanerges received a Hammer of the Sun (again, updated, and soon to be re-updated).

Our Allies in Talons picked up a pretty neat new bow from the Glyphed, a Serpent tooth choker, a headband of many visions, a Hammer of the Sun, a Geomantic Focus Crystal, a Rei's Battleworn Dirk, Gloves of Control, a Jade Skull Ring, a Black talisman, and probably some other things I'm forgetting.

I'm crossing my fingers for more patches. The more VI patches, the less time we spend farming keys. GO PATCHES GO!

Keys, Keys, Keys, Tormax, Keys, and Keys

Posted by: Druss on Monday, May 6th, 2002

Dropped King T on the 4th. Congrats Flareon and Vanelar on their rings of lightning, Talons on their head (Congrats on the Ever-so-L337 Clawed Griffon Sword, or whatever caster crap that might be made into) Bracer of lightning, and Axe of the Kromzek Kings.

Truth be told, its a pleasure when we get the chance to knock down mobs like old king T. Not for the loot, though of course thats nice. More because it gets the guild the hell out of SSRA and away from the tedium known as "Commander Camping". Pretty soon, I'm gonna get a wild hair, and start doing daily news updates about key camping. They'll all look pretty much like this:

5/5 - Camped keys all day. 120 ppl in ssra. Fought with other guilds over commander spawns.

5/6 - Camped keys all day. 120 ppl in ssra. Fought with other guilds over commander spawns.

5/7 - Camped keys all day. 120 ppl in ssra. Fought with other guilds over commander spawns.

And so on...

The real question here is...how many days could I do that before Mirax and Flare had simultaneous conniption fits...Ah well, I'll try and stick to things remotely interesting...like...other key quests!!!

What else gets us away from key camping? Why, a 4 AM trip to Acrylia at 4AM in the morning to free the stupid cat from his Richard-Greico-look-alike captors, the Grimlings. Honestly, the Acrylia key is probably the most "fun" of the Luclin keys to do, as it actually has a storyline to it, beyond VI's usually formula of "Kill ubermob on a 7 day spawn, and loot key" (well, maybe not beyond that, more like it has a story AND a 7 day bottleneck, so you have something to think about while you wait for the mob to spawn). Its a shame that with the emperor being the only Luclin key quest that leads to Vex Thal, that more than likely noone is going to bother with Acrylia until well after PoP.
