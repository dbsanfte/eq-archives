Quellious

 
Initate Symbol of Quellious
Speak to Laraena in the Temple of Peace(Quellious) in Erudin. Say to her "what more powerful shamen?" She will then give you a pouch and instruct you to gather 8 "odd bone necklaces" from lesser shamen (kobolds) in the Warrens. Fill up the pouch, hit combine and return it to her. Reward: Initiate Symbol of Quellious The necklaces drop about 1 in 10 times.

Disciple Symbol of Quellious

You must first do the Quellious Initiate Quest. After completing the Initiate Symbol quest, say to Laraena (Temple of Peace in Erudin): "I am ready to deal with the greater kobold shaman." She will give you a pouch and instruct you to fill it with 8 "odd bronze necklaces" which drop from greater shamen in the Warrens, and from greater kobold shamen in Stonebrunt Mountains. Fill the pouch, hit combine, and return it and your Initiate symbol to Laraena to recieve the Disciple Symbol of Quellious


Regent Symbol of Quellious

After completing the Disciple Symbol of Quellious, say to Laraena: "I am ready to advance." She will tell you to return with the symbol of the shaman high priest. High Shaman Drogik spawns in the Warrens (King room) and will sometimes drop an "odd cold iron necklace". Return this necklace to Laraena. She will return the necklace and a sealed note and tell you to seek out Glyssa Sonshaw in West Freeport. Glyssa is located in the courtyard area under the caster guilds in W. Freeport. Hand her the necklace and note; she will return the necklace and another note. Glyssa tells you to seek out a friendly kobold in Ak'anon. The kobold is named Drigaz Grumblegrowl and is located in a bar near the bank. Hand him the necklace and note and he will tell you a little bit about himself, give you yet another note, and tell you to seek out a kobold missionary in Steamfont mountains; he wants you to retrieve the "doctrine of Rolfon Zek." The kobold missionary spawns in the area around the Minotaur caves, and seems to spawn at night. Kill him and loot an "encrypted document" -- which he drops every time. Return to Erudin and turn in the note from the kobold, the encrypted document, and your Disciple Symbol to Laraena to receive the Regent Symbol of Quellious. Note: all items used in this quest are NO DROP.
