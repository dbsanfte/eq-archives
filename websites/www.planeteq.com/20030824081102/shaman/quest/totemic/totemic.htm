Basic information

In Lake Rathetear zone there is a group of Barbarians, two of which offer to make you some special armour. The two people are called Vrynn and Kyralynn. The quests also require a Dufrenite, worked in some way. Dufrenites are a rare drop from Carrion Ghouls in Unrest and from Clockworks in Sol A, but can be bought from a magic shop for about 180pp. Take this stone to the non-kos ogres in the same zone to get it crushed, powdered and so on...
