Bloodforge Hammer

This is fast and easy if you have good faction with Stormguard. All you have to do is go to South Qeynos and kill Trumpy. He is easy, but if you try to kill him in the bar you will be jumped. To get him out in the open you can either buy him some drinks and talk to him a bit until he mentions S'hrag. When you ask him about that, he will deny everything, and start walking. Follow him and kill him when there is no one around. Guards will help him, although he is not very liked in Qeynos. Take the head to Byzar Bloodforge in the warrior guild in South Kaladim. He will give you the Hammer if your faction is good enough. If you are a dwarf this should be no problem at all.
