Jun 22 2002 3:14AM - Some NToV action... Deacis
So...Here we are again, on our merry way to NToV to gank some wicked dragons, hopefully winning, getting some fairly decent loot...

Should be fun, and enjoyable, laughs and kicks for all...But...

Some people just have problems with their long term memory, I know there is a month between each of our slots in rotation but...That isnt a reason to go blank each and every time we go there...

Things went well for starters...Eashen went down without any trouble, except for perhaps the worst positioning I have ever seen, despite a heapload of guiding from the raidcoordinators...Died though, so not the biggest fudge up...Grats Sicon and Leafric on their new bracelets, and a monk from PE, whose name escapes me atm on a pair of nice claws.

Ikitiar hadnt spawned yet, so we decided to give Roy a go...Shouldnt have done that...Got his guards though, but was amazing...Took about 2 minutes of shouting in caps before guard was disposed of in the appropriate manner, which then again left us with an angry unslowed Roy and some oom clerics...But we've been in worse...So we fought on...then our AE of the hatchlings came back at us, landing 9 hatchlings on us...Oh well...

Regrouped, and decided to gank the newly spawned Ikitiar, to at least accomplish something on what was a very long friday...So that we did...Wasnt the prettiest fight, again positioning was way off, just one death though, so wasnt total loss, just seen better before :p Was the big monk day, and Grats Evade on his new tunic and boots, and Scoff on a new pair of leggings from PE...

So people started logging, but so what? Somehow people pulled themselves up from the big puddle of mud we were crawling in, and executed a perfect kill on Roy...And at the second he fell dead, at the rigth spot, our 9 friends from before came back...:p So welcome CR, courtesy of 9 very angry hatchlings...Actually worse CR on these than any of our previous flurry wipes...Was actually so bad, that we had to end the night after a good load of summons, and some good effort done by a solid group of people. Infestalia got a new amulet, and Hermi got a new mask, Meldinor from PE got an amulet as well.

So wasnt a total loss...Just not very pretty... ;)

Would like to thank the peeps on this picture for staying up, helping people getting their corpses, and in general showing excellent support. :)
