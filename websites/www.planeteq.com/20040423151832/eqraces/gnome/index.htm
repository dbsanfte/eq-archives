History

 

Gnomes were created by Brell Serilis; the cousins of the Dwarves.

 

Gnomes are very intelligent, and are known throughout Norrath for the gadgets and devices they create. Gnomes are also the only race capable of tinkering. 

 

The Gnomes live in Ak'Anon a little clockwork city, full of mechanical treasures. The Gnomes also have a large library visited by many scholars and apprentices. Gnomes often take up the magical professions, and because of their high dexterity and intelligence make very powerful examples.   

 

Similar in nature to the Dwarves, Gnomes are considered a 'good' people, though some have become Necromancers and more recently, Shadow Knights. The religion they follow tends to be Brell Serilis but many will follow religions more in keeping with their profession. 

Race Specific Tips

Tinker tinker

Gnomes are the only race capable of using the Tinkering skill, take full advantage of this. Only your race will be able to sell tinkered items so expect demand to be high and supply to be low.

Little People

Gnomes are the smallest playable race on Norrath, this means you can get to places others can't and you won't have any problems in small cities.
