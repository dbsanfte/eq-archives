City of Mist reports

Head to the City of Mist, located in the Emerald Jungle.

Off random creatures in the zone, one of five pages drop. They are:

  • Personal Diary Page
  • Crier's Scroll
  • Merchant's Letter
  • Written Announcement
  • Priest's Diary Page

The final (6th) page you need can only be found on a Spectral Courier is called the 'Students Letter'

Place all six pages in the book, and hit combine.

Give the resulting book to the Sentinel Spirit in Emerald Jungle.

Lord Ghiosk

This part is short, but is the first main boss mob you need to kill.

Bring a raiding party to City of Mist and kill Lord Ghiosk. Reports suggest you should bring a raiding party of about three groups, levels 55+, but the more the better.

Ghiosk will drop three books when killed.

Take those books to another spirit, also in Emerald Jungle. Not to be confused with the Sentinel Spirit, the Spirit Sentinel can be found at loc +3684 -640.

    Give the spirit the books.

High Scale Kirn

Before you go and see Kirn, you will need the High Scale Icon from the City of Mist, here is where to find it.

Go into City of Mist.

Go across the bridge.

Open the gate.

Turn right.

Go straight forward through 2 arches.

In the last room you will find a temple with a waterfall running down its side.

Stay in that courtyard of temple, and pull skeletons, etc., from the first door you see at the base of the temple.

Open the door. Inside is another door directly in front of you, do not open this door, it leads to top of temple. Look instead to the right, where three skeletons spawn. Pull these skeletons to courtyard and kill them.

Go back into door, head right and ye will see a room set below ground level with three phantoms in it, standing on a raised dais. Pull and kill these phantoms. There are two doors beside the dais, one opening to the left and one opening to the right. Choose the left door. Inside is a small room with no monsters inside, and a small bag on the floor in the left corner. Click on the bag, you now have the High Scale Icon. Gate out.

Using Invisibility to Undead potion will get you past the last three skeletons and phantoms. They will still scowl at you, ready to attack, but it is a bluff. They will NOT be able to see you.

Be warned, however, as the golems of the area can see through Invisibility to Undead, and you may run across a couple on the way to the temple area.

There were no rogue-locked doors between the zone and the High Scale icon.

Once you have the icon, you can head to High Scale Kirn, who resides in The Hole

Bring another raiding party with you to The Hole, and Kirn can be found in the tower beyond the city.

    Give Kirn the icon.

High Scale Kirn will agro you immediatly.

    Kill him and loot his ring

Completing the quest

Return to City of Mist and give the ring to Neh'Ashiir, she will agro you.

    Kill Neh'Ashiir and loot her diary.

Take the Diary to the Spirit Sentinel (the second one) in Emerald Jungle.

The spirit will tell you to go to the Plane of Fear.

Head to the Plane of Fear and kill the Iksar Broodling (level one). Sound easy? It isn't. The Broodling is a triggered spawn from one of the Demi's in the Plane, so this isnt a walkover.

    Loot the Iksar Child's Tear.

Go to the City of Mist with a large raiding party and Lord Rak'Ashiir

He will not agro anyone until the tear is handed to him

There have been many reported ways to do this, but this one seems the best...

    Questing Shaman and Magician Fire pets are placed near Rak'Ashiir

    Other people as far away as posible without leaving the room

    Shaman gives Lord Rak'Ashiir the tear

    Other raiders stay back and watch, they shouldn't interfere, if other mobs want to disrupt them watching the fun, let them teach the mobs a lesson, but NO area of effect spells.

    The shaman will probably die, but as soon as possible, should get a rez. The shaman should then loot the Iksar Scale.

Take the scale back to the Spirit Sentinel at loc +3685 -640

    Give the spirit the scale
