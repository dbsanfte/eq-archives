The reason for black
At first I did not intend this site to be black. This however has proven to be a bit more difficult than first planned. Jpeg compression tends to dither the colors a bit so backgrounds do not come out as they should. I know there is a way to work around this, but I have not yet had the time to figure it out yet. So for now it will be black, and I will just contintue on the more important aspects of the site.
