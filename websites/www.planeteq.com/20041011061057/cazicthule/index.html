       Monday, October 4, 2004

    lfgzone.com
    10/4/04, 13:40 PST | Kwill

    A new website has been started to help you get a group together, even before you log on. Check out http://www.lfgzone.com/. From the website:
    This site offers tools that will allow you to organize and schedule hunts that other people who visit this site can search for and join. You can even designate what types and classes of players you want to join your group. Want to send an invite for a specific person to join your hunt? No problem, you can e-mail your unique hunt code to friends or guild mates so that they can quickly find and join your hunt.

    We offer the ability to create either public hunts or private hunts. A public hunt is one that anybody can search for and join. A private hunt is one that you must send out invitations to using your unique hunt code.

    Don't feel like creating a hunt? No problem, use our searchable database to find hunts that fit into your schedule. You can narrow your search using a variety of parameters so you can find hunts that are exactly what you are looking for.

    So end the hassle of standing around in game with an LFG tag up for hours, and start using LFGZone to join and schedule all of your hunting groups!

    Sounds like something interesting for the casual or non-guilded player.

       Wednesday, September 29, 2004

    SOE Worlds Magazine
    9/29/04, 11:59 PST | Kwill

    SOE has announced the creation of a new magazine for its game players:
    Introducing Sony Online Entertainment Worlds, the ultimate magazine for the players and communities of EverQuest®, Star Wars Galaxies®, EverQuest® Online Adventures™, PlanetSide®, Champions of Norrath™ and EverQuest®II. In-depth information, interviews and columns from the creators of your games, insider scoops on new games and expansions, as well as articles and features written for and by players just like you.
    For EQ players specifically:
    SOE Worlds contains crucial insider info on EverQuest for players of all types. For new or returning players, we’ll cover gameplay through the first ten levels and offer suggestions for finding that perfect guild. Expert players will learn high-level raiding secrets and develop new trade skills. And Omens of War, the latest EverQuest expansion will be peeled open for all to see.
    See more and get the magazine pre-ordered at http://soeworlds.station.sony.com/.
