Crushbone Orcs

The Crushbone Orcs are the only real serious threat to the Elven races on Faydwer. The Crushbone Orcs intend to take over the continent for themselves at the earliest opportunity and have no problems with doing this by force. Of course they must first end the siege of their stronghold before they can begin to think about anything else.

The Crushbone Orcs are rumoured to have 'friendly' relations with the Dark Elves but other allies are largely unknown. It is thought the DeathFist Orcs in Freeport would be the nearest allies but Orcs aren't known for their 'comradeship.' The enemies of the Crushbone Orcs are far more numerous, Dwarves, High Elves and Wood Elves are all among them. Many other races can often be found assisting in the fighting. 

The Crushbones stronghold will from time to time have visits from Emperor Crush before he is chased out by the hordes of Elves and Dwarves training in the Throne Room. The Emperor is usually guarded by Royal Guards. Emissaries can also often be found close by and inside the Throne Room.

The most common of the Crushbone Orcs ranks are Centurions, these guard the entrance to the stronghold and can be often be found with Oracles in the encampments outside in Greater Faydark. The weakest Orcs are Pawns and these can be seen anywhere in Greater Faydark and often in other areas. Inside the entrance the next most common are the Legionnaires, these protect the stronghold itself and cannot be found outside, this is fortunate as they are formidable fighters and would make an inexperienced fighter flee in terror. 

Effected Factions:

Crushbone Orcs (-)

Indigo Brotherhood (-)

Emerald Warriors (+)

Faydarks Champions (+)

 

The sign enclosed in brackets is the result of killing a Crushbone Orc, minus represents a worse standing, plus represents a better standing.
