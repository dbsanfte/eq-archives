Dark Beginning
by M. Frank Spencer

You struggle through the murky, muddy swamp around Grobb. The mud pulls at your armor boots with each step. In the distance a bullfrog croaks. Your once proud and shiny armor has been covered with mud, and the red cross you wear on your shield is barely visible in the darkness. Stumbling over a hidden root you barely catch yourself from going face first into the muddy water below. A stench nearby is overwhelming. To the left a rotting body of some unknown Troll lies, surrounded by dried blood. In the distance your ears pick up talking, it is hard to understand from this distance, so you move in the direction from which it flows.

Nearing the noise, a glimpse of a campfire can be seen through an eyeslot in your iron mask. Praying to your God, you move forward as quietly as possible. Several figures are huddled around the fire. A light mist begins to fall in the swamp, further hiding your presence to them. Thunder cracks overhead as the lightning flashes. Three skeletons surround the outer area around the fire, weapons at the ready to protect whatever lies in the middle.

Blue skin can be seen against the light of the fire. A dark elf sits quietly as two bigger creatures stir near him-Trolls obviously. Clutching your sword tighter, you move closer. Off to the left by the fire, a light flickers and grows larger. You duck as low as you can to avoid detection, all the while keeping an eye on the dark creatures gathered around the fire. The dark elf sits calmly, knowingly by the fire as both Trolls mutter in alarm and reach for shields and weapons.

"On time, as expected." The dark elf says cooly yet menacingly. "How far along is progress?"

"Right on schedule, many of the sleepers are in place, and our forces build even as we speak. Innoruk will be proud." Came the reply.

Innoruk? Who is this to speak of Innoruk. Eyes squinting as hard as possible, you stare into the darkness outside the light of the fire. A familiar cloak is worn by this-man? Where have you seen that before? An Erudite in Freeport. An Erudite...but that is no necromancers cloak. That’s the one their mages wear. Surely the Erudites are beyond the reach of Innoruk. Aren’t they?

"And your friend here, where will he be when the darkness falls on all of Norrath?" Friend? There is another? You peer even harder into the darkness for the second man the Dark Elf referrered to.

"Wherever the Prince of Hate needs him, and he can get us into the most sacred of places." With a smirk the Erudite’s friend stepped towards the fire light revealing himself. This man was human, and with a cross painted on his armored chest plate. A paladin then. Corruption is deeper in Norrath than you thought.

Rage consumes your body and your mind alike, you stand to make a wild charge-SNAP!-a stick underneath your foot cracks. The Erudite’s hands light up as the mage begins to cast a spell. A burst of light explodes in the swamp. Shielding your eyes from the light while waiting for them to adjust, you hear the rattling of bones and the clinking of metal coming towards you in a hurried pace. Sliding on the helmet that protects you from evil, and beginning a pray that may bring you from this encounter alive, the first encounter of blade and armor happen. In the muddy landscape you are knocked off balance and fall to one knee. Swinging wildly with your trusty sword, your blade encounters flesh and then deep down, bone. As you struggle to peel the now red covered sword from the leg of a Troll, your eyes clear...just in time to see your last vision. Another paladin’s sword coming straight towards the eye slot in your armor mask.
