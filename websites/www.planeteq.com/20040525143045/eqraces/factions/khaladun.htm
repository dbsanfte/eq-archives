The Khala Dun

The Khala Dun are the warriors and guardians of the Vah Shir. They enlist after their registration as citizens of Shar Vahl.

The training begins with fighting small, weak creatures such as grimling skeletons, beetles and xakra worms. As the warrior builds up his rank through to apprentice, journey man, and beyond, he will begin to combat harder and more powerful creatures, slowly advancing in his skills and building experience.

The Khala Dun will always stand a watchful guard over the palace of the King and the two bridges over the crater of Shar Vahl. When veteran Khala Dun warriors receive full honours they are often granted the title of Royal Khala Dun. Some become the kings personal body guards, or are given the responsibility of protecting the Vah Shir outposts which hold back the Grimlings in their territories.

A fully trained Khala Dun warrior is a powerful opponent, but none will ever use their training for evil purposes, they are taught to fight with honour and to respect both their allies and their enemies.
