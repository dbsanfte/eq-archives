Phrost's New Favorite Word
By Phrost (08/16/01)

"F%$ktard": Yep ladies and gene trash, that's my new favorite word.

So to celebrate this, I wanted to share with you a few examples of how this new word can be used to discuss things in EQ and everyday conversation. And along the way, we'll discuss such wonderful topics as, Vulak raids gone bad, arcade games that can determine your sexual persuasion, and even add a few more names to the Hall of Shame.

Let's start with an in-depth examination of the word "f%$ktard".

The first part of the word, "f&%k" we're all familiar with. If you're not, make sure you've disabled the /filter option and go hand mod rods to newbies in Greater Faydark. (Thanks to Jipp from Cazic who was the first to do this) The latter part of the word, "tard" comes from the word "retard" which is a derogatory term used to describe people with mental disabilities. So, this word was obviously created by someone who wanted to express his displeasure at a person who displayed traits similar to the mentally challenged, yet wanted to walk that oh-so-thin line of political correctness and avoid offending people who genuinely suffered from such handicaps. So being that this word is not only expressive, but also ethically tactful, I propose we all start using it every time the opportunity presents itself.

For example: Your guild/alliance just pulled Vulak and is engaging him. Random_newb00 who invited himself along unbeknownst to anyone, aggroes a drake and trains the camp, causing the attempt to fail and Vulak to level off your fellow guildmembers. Now that you're versed in the finer points of this word and its history, feel free to ask in /gu "WHO THE HELL LET THAT F%$KTARD COME ON THE VULAK RAID?!??!" I guarantee your use of this word in the above sentence will help effectively communicate your point to the rest of the guild.

Or a better example: you're sitting on the couch after a long day at work (http://www.dictionary.com/cgi-bin/dict.pl?term=work for some of you monitor-tanned, Velcro-assed shut-ins) and the softcore porn flick you want to see on Skinemax isn't going to start for a few minutes. So, you flip channels looking for something to pass the time when the batteries in your remote die and the TV's set to A&E's presentation of Lord of the Dance 2: A Tribute to Liberace. Here's another opportunity for you to use this wonderful, versatile word, as you exclaim to whomever might be able to hear you shout "WHO THE HELL IS THIS F^%KTARD ON MY TV SET AND WHY IS HE MAKING ME WANT TO SING SHOW TUNES?" If you live in a dense urban area when you shout this, even better! Your neighbors will be afforded an opportunity to use the word for themselves when they shout back, "SHUT THE HELL UP YOU F%^KTARD, BEFORE I COME DOWN THERE AND FEED YOU MY FOOT!" Everyone wins!

Speaking of Liberace, Show Tunes, and Dancing, I wanted to express my feelings about a new phenomenon in arcades across the country. Dance Dance Revolution is the newest thing from the people that brought you Yoko Ono, Animated Porn, and "all your base are belong to us". Thousands of young girls can be found crowded around one of these new dance-simulator games.

Oh, you may have noticed that I said "thousands of young girls" in the above sentence, instead of "thousands of young people". This wasn't an oversight, stay with me.

Now before you get in an uproar about where I'm going with this, I should state for the record that no, I'm not a homophobe, nor do I have any real issues with the gay lifestyle. In fact, I have a few good friends that are gay, and I even went so far as to confirm my suspicion that this game resided in their neck of the woods. So I have it on good authority that putting quarters in this machine is much like showing your hindquarters on dollar night at your local Blue Oyster Bar. In other words, it'll bring you that much closer to wearing panty hose and being picked up by Eddie Murphy when you're strolling around town late at night.

Follow me here, because I'm still not sure some of your brains can process what I'm saying. There's absolutely NOTHING wrong with being gay. But there IS something wrong with trying to be heterosexual (which isn't very tough), and failing at it miserably. And that's exactly what is happening to every person endowed with a penis that steps onto a Dance Dance Revolution machine under the misguided notion that what they're doing is cool.

If you're one of these poor, deluded souls, do yourself a favor and spend your entire next pay/welfare/disability/stolen check putting quarters in Tekken to "virtually" beat the crap out of someone else.

It's what Chuck Heston would do.
