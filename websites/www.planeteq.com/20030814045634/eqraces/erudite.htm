History

 

Originally hailing from Humans, Erudites fled to Odus after being disillusioned by the war-like natures of the Humans and Barbarians. On Odus they have built a large, magical city, famed for its libraries and texts.

 

Erudites are perhaps the most magical of all the races, all Erudites have some control over the magical forces and are not found in other professions. Not all Erudites are considered good and a group of Erudites left Erudin to study the dark arts.

 

Erudites, similar to the High Elves, believe themselves to be superior to the other races and are often disliked by the other races because of their attitudes. While intelligent, they are not a wise people.  

 

Erudites are fairly diverse in their religions, but most follow the good or neutral gods. 

    

Race Specific Tips

Genius

Erudite intelligence is the best you can get for a caster class, if you put all your bonus points into it, you have a fireball machine.

The other option is to put your bonus points into raising your weak physical stats but this may be better served by obtaining items in the game as your high intelligence will reach 200 fairly easily. Of course, no matter how many points you spend your physical stats will never be of much use.

Careful of the Mummys

For a learning Erudite disease resistance can be a problem, Erudites have the lowest disease resistance of any other race, some resist enhancing gear would benefit you greatly if you intend to fight undead or fight on Luclin. (Unless you can cure the disease anyway)
