Felwithe

Felwithe is a beautiful marble city, home of the High Elves.

It is suited to those in magical professions, with many spells and reagents. The city is home to the Clerics of Tunare and also houses Enchanter, Magician, Paladin and Wizard guilds.

The Jewel craft skill is well catered for in Felwithe with several merchants supporting it.

Merchants for leather, cloth, and weaponry can also be found in North Felwithe.

High Elf NPC Guilds
