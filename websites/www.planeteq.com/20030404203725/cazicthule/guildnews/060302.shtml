The Care and Feeding of Clerics
Posted by: Druss on Tuesday, May 28th, 2002 @ 7:46 PM
So, the week in review.

CC/Talons hit the Avatar of War Wednesday. An encounter that has been joyously described as simply "have enough button pushing monkeys hitting Complete Heal, and you'll win". So, not wanting to miss out on an AoW "Hat Trick", we started looking for this key component. Though we searched far and wide, the only trace of a button pushing monkey that we could find, was this:

The AoW kill was in jeopardy. People began to panic as his spawn date approached. For God's sake...Monk loot was at stake. Somebody had to do something!

And somebody did. Our fearless leader Kenji, never to be rattled, came up with a brilliant solution. All Clerics in CC were given an X-Box and the following game.

After days and days of cross training, we felt we were ready to engage him again. All the hard work paid off, because he dropped like a rock. It would have been a flawless kill, other than a little issue with him turning around while enraged....

Anyway...Grats to Bukow on his Flayed Barbarian Skin Mask, and Gristle on his War Bow of Rallos Zek, Talons on their Flayed Barbarian skin leggings and Flayed Barbarian skin mask. Also that evening.

In other news:

Did another Ring war this week, congrats Whopper on his Ring of Dain Frostreaver, and once again, all 5 heros survived.

Also killed by the alliance:Statue of Rallos Zek, Vindi, and Lord Yeli.

And finally - CC got bored on Memorial day, and killed the Dain and King Tormax in house. Weeeeeee.

Oh, yeah..and welcome to Areia. Cleric, heal thyself of your illness! More news soon.

Schism
Tuesday to Sunday :

Umm, I think I should be taking actual notes for these updates.

The normal First Broods died, though I don't think we got Zlandicar or Lendi the Keeper cause I know we missed Zland, but someone must have killed Lendi in the middle of one of those nights, because she never spawned for us at the expected time. Kind of sucks when the server gets so crowded that dragons that most guilds don't normally kill do it out of boredom ;(

Anyway, we killed Vindi a bunch of times! Though, we did get a chance at the Statue, so thats probably the high point of the 6 days. In a 20 Second tanking flurry of epic proportions, I kept the statue busy with weaponshield while the chanters/shaman did their work tashing/malo/slowing him all before he ground me up into paste. Sure I didn't get heals, but I was supposed to die. 1100 points of damage he hit me for during his final round. HEHE! Anyway, he fell, some stuff dropped and all were happy and rejoiceful.

We were supposed to go to VP this weekend, but like I said, the server was REALLY REALLY bored and all the dragons were killed before we had a chance, so there was no use in going.

Most of the weekend was made up of people exping, doing trade skills to quest things or doing little quests around the place. I made some more avalanche ale, 2 for 2. *glee* I also found my new triple boxing spot, fungus grove. Which is fine if you don't get blasted by an AE's overpulling train right over top of you. Nothing like seeing 10 to 15 blue mobs come flying at your little party of 3, where the cleric/chanter are dead before you can switch to them to gate. Anyway, 4 points to Veteran. Then its only 9 (cry) short points to Endless Quiver, then 18 (double cry) to Archery Mastery 3.

Oh, we did some sleepers again, I think we got down to the FA once, but again more guilds came in and picked off the MoTG cause he was up. We got some stuff, but it wasn't that bountiful.

Oh! I guess the 2nd Big news of the past 6 days was a little North ToV Exploration Run on Friday night. Since like every dragon was dead, we made it all the way to the PoM junction that leads to the 3 dragons that are splittasticly difficult. Well, not the splitting, just getting the one you wanted. Everyone was afraid of Zlexak for some reason, so what if he had a 1250 disease AE with slow, we'd have killed him good! Anyway, we played around with the flurry drakes and rampaging wyrens and lots of other drakes and a few wurms, but kind of just learned the lay of the land, so to speak. Anyway, we then went to PoM, and fooled around for a while, got lost, got more lost, then I got 10k'd down a trap that has no bottom, well it looked like it did, but it made you fall into the floor, then 10k out of the floor. Nice GM rez and I gate out! At least I can say I've been to every zone but Vex Thal now, whee.

All I can say is thank goodness for the patch this week. The spawns are all over the place, everything is super staggered and every guild is all over the place instead of the places they should be. So hopefully a nice patch day maddness will cool everyone's jets for a bit.

Shadowed Soul
Tuesday, May 28th, 2002
I Kicks The Flavor, Like Steven King Writes Horror....

Did not suck. Not at all.

Tonight, we decided to put a hurt on some snakes just for bein scaly bastages. The targets? Arch Lich and High Priest. Arch lich we have taken before since hes been buffed, it's not an easy fight, but we knew the score and expected to see him dead. Evidently he knew the score as well, since he rolled over and played dead nicely. From there, we were lookin for a little challenge, so we set our sights on a mob we hadn't killed yet, oh wait, I already told you....yah, the High Priest. In fact, since they buffed him, I don't believe anyone has killed him on CT and doing him with 55 was damn fun.

The fight certainly does get your blood pumpin right from the initial 'break' of his room (I used the word 'break' advisedly, ever since we figured out the Druid/Wizzy solo pulls outa there, it got a lot easier). Then you step into the room and lay into a mob with more hits than a dealer on friday afternoon. And then you fight. Then you fight some more. Keep fightin, and fighting, and fighting....27 minutes for us this time. At one point his cronies spawned and caught us off guard. Before we could react they decided to heal him from 20% to 40% and visions of a mass CR from the zoneline started floating about the room. Anyway the HP fight is a very different fight from AoW, but it brings a high level of difficulty and a large intolerance for screwups. I can dance to it, I give it a 9.

That's all for now, time to kill more snakes.
