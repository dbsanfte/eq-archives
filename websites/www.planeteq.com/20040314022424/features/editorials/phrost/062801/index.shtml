Verant Redefines "CS", Now Stands For "Customers Suck"
By Phrost (06/28/01)

Hey boys and girls. Uncle Phrosty here. Yeah I know some of you paint-huffing rocket scientists have been wondering why I hadn't written anything in a few months, but I guess you're just going to have to get used to being disappointed, much like your parents already have.

Anyway I wanted to relate a wonderful little tale of my recent interaction with SOE's CS team, add a few more idiotic character names to the hall of shame, and give the grammatical equivalent of the finger to gaming's own version of Boo Radley, Furor from FoH.

First of all, I'd like to make one thing perfectly clear. I'm not Lum. I have no real issues with Verant/Sony/BMQ/etc, nor do I have any intentions of making a "living" crawling up their asses and looking for things that smell bad. While I dig what Lum does, that's just not my style. I actually agree with a lot of the policies VI has in place. I empathize with how difficult it must be to deal with hundreds of thousands of crack addicts and maintain one's sanity. Were I a member of the Verant staff, I'd have to log in my avatar and DT at least three or four people per server, per day, just to cope. I know the CS team must deal with more retards every day than you'd see at a Dave Matthews Band concert. However, when their jaded attitudes and mouse-trap reactions affect me personally, I get irritated. And you really don't want me irritated… ask around.

The story goes: I'd gotten booted offline with my character and was sitting in Chat, waiting for him to time out so I could log back in. For those of you mouth-breathers who have never actually been in EQ-Chat, I invite you to try it out. It's much like going into a kindergarten class where the teacher(s) throw chalk at you for saying things they don't like. In this case, the teachers didn't like my use of the word "twit". I actually got warned/booted out of Chat for using the word it. No, not "Tit" (wasn't a discussion about Tomb Raider), not "S-H-I-*"… just "twit". So I was baffled. And folks, Phrosty doesn't get baffled often. So I reconnect, and sincerely ask what is wrong with the word "T-W-I-T" (I even spelled it out for them). Guess what?

What, Meesta Phrosty?

I got warned/booted again.

At this point, it's war. So I log in on another account I happen to be paying on. (I have no problem sending VI $20 a month if it'll help them get laid by buying sports cars. The way I see it, the more VI execs get laid, the less BS us gamers have to deal with.) I return to Chat to get the name of the GM on duty that removed me (GM Slainess). I also make a comment, referring to recent CS policies they've enacted (Insta-Bans for losing your password) that "Verant must enjoy negative publicity", who I was, and that I'd be writing this up for our beloved website. (And you'd damn well better be loving it or I'll come to your house, strap you to your chair, and make you go through our new weapons and armor database item by item.) This results in a third warning/expulsion from Chat. Now I can't log in and access Chat from either of my accounts. They've disabled it for me.

But just wait my little mush-brained zombies, it gets BETTER!

I get back on to play my character (no, you gets no love here, I don't want to speak to you in game) and petition that I'd like to speak to a GM (not guide) about the incident and have it explained to me why I was warned/booted. I feel that it's a fair request.

Eventually (30 minutes or so) I get a response from a guide who tells me that the server GM isn't online and he can't escalate the petition.

I disagree. I know the petition can be escalated to a GM that's on duty to cover the times when the regular server GM isn't on. I have enough friends in the Guide program to be aware of these things. However, Guide Retardo .. err Trovalor denies that he has any capability to do so and attempts to end the conversation by telling me to send an email. Well, I'm not about to let him off so easy. I know better.

[Wed Jun 20 19:24:07 2001] Trovalor tells you, 'If you have a problem with the chat GM, then write your complaint to eqcs@station.sony.com. Otherwise there is nothing else we can do. And this is the end of our conversation, Good Day.'

[Wed Jun 20 19:24:08 2001] XXXXX says, 'GoB plz'

[Wed Jun 20 19:24:08 2001] You told Trovalor, 'Yes, but there is a GM queue that matters can be escalated to an on-call GM. If I cannot rejoin the chat server how am I supposed to get any resolution?'

[Wed Jun 20 19:24:10 2001] XXXX says, 'sure XXXXX'

And so, the guide gets snotty:

[Wed Jun 20 19:25:52 2001] Trovalor tells you, 'Any more tells to me will result in a warning on your account, plus I will seek out the Chat gm for possible further action due to repeated offences in such a short time'

[Wed Jun 20 19:25:57 2001] XXXXX tells the guild, 'Some stupid D0rf in full Valorium standing on Throne, damm he looks like Dain =/'

[Wed Jun 20 19:25:58 2001] XXX saved.

[Wed Jun 20 19:26:06 2001] XXX tells the guild, 'haha' [Wed Jun 20 19:26:41 2001] You told Trovalor, 'Warn me then, if that's what it will take to speak to the chat GM. I thought you said there was no way for me to speak with Him/Her?'

He's caught in a blatant lie. GG Verant, your volunteer staff is certainly doing a bang up job.

Of course, my modem dies right at this point. Wireless on a Laptop may impress teenage chicks at Starbucks, but it's worthless for gaming.

So yes ladies and biological rejects, Phrosty gets the Rodney King treatment from VI's Customer Support Gestapo. It was a wonderful experience; I imagine it must be fairly similar to prison rape. At least I had the satisfaction of exposing a member of my server's guide program as a liar.

When "CS" in your company stands for "Customers Suck", you're not going to be around very long. The zombied masses will find another game to shuffle off to, and you'll be left with 25 servers populated by maybe 300 people each who just can't play any other online game because they can't afford the hardware requirements.

I know there are smart people at VI. It's not hard to think when your head is up your ass, it's just hard to see what's going on outside. We're outside Verant, and you're treating us like the enemy. You're the only game in town right now, but that won't be the case forever.

It's cheaper to hire more people than it is to lose customers.

Bah, I'm done with this I've no doubts it's been said before by someone else anyway.

On to the HALL OF SHAME

This month's new inductees on my server:

Mightyjoxer
Brotherdoe
Kidz

You're all recipients of the Wilfred G. Crabsinbeard Dumb Name award for the month of June. Congratulations!

And finally, I'd like to give a /rude to Furor from Fires of Heaven. This pretentious TWIT has the gall to "write an essay" to Verant, explaining why ALL warriors should be immune from frontal stuns. Don't let him fool you kids, it's not because he gives a damn about you non-Ogre warriors. He's just a spoiled brat who chose a Human warrior so he could level faster, and is now wetting his diaper because he's not as good of a tank as the level 59 Ogre in a non-uber guild. God forbid someone is better than you at something.. oh wait, that's 99% of the earth's population.

Furor, if you wanted to be immune to frontal stuns, you should have picked an Ogre. Nobody gives a damn who you are, or how close to VI's headquarters you live. Perhaps in the expansion they'll add this in as a class upgrade choice you can make, but for now, you'll just have to stew in it, quit whining, and go powerlevel Furrorr the Ogre if you want to have this ability.

I'm done. Heading out to Carl's Jr. for a Western Cheeseburger. Furor, make sure you don't undercook it, k?
