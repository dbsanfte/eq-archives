The Sleeper Round 2
11/18/03, 8:28 PST | Nelixic

As a followup to the unsuccessful attempt to slay the sleeper, the following was posted on the official Sony Everquest Developers Corner.
The Sleeper (11-17-03)

Over the weekend several guilds gathered on Rallos to fight with the Sleeper. Unfortunately, their encounter was cut short due to an apparent bug.

I wanted to take a moment here and apologize to those that were there, and to those that have heard about the event through their friends. The bug concerned an NPC in the zone that appeared to have been causing the Sleeper to not focus on the player characters. The decision was made at the time to end the event. Further investigation has only served to make it unclear if this was a real issue or not.

I, on behalf of the company, apologize for any consternation this may have caused during your play time. If anyone is going to defeat the Sleeper, it should be done without any question about the validity of the event. We're very sorry that this first attempt was halted, but at the time it seemed like the best thing to do.

We have resurrected and restored those that participated. We have corrected the potential problem, and have reset the encounter. Other than that one potentially problematic NPC, nothing else about the encounter has been changed.

We want to wish those on Rallos that are planning to tackle the Sleeper the best of luck.

Send me some screenshots of all of you standing around the corpse, I'd love to post them on the site.

Thank you and thanks for understanding,

Alan
Although there is considerable dispute over the reasoning behind the de-spawning of Kerafyrm, it appears that the players involved on Rallos had another pop the following day in which they achieved their goal (although it apparently did involve more character ressurection than is healthy).

Razzinel of Wudan wrote, "The Sleeper is...uum was a QUADING for 7k mob which deathtouches (20k) every few seconds. Immune to ALL spells but Harm Touch and Manaburn. Only melee damageable with HP that make any other mobs hp dwindle in comparizon. About four hours of combined DPS of 2 time enable farming Quarm guilds and one elemental...180 people."

So congratulations to Magus Imperialis Magicus, Ascending Dawn and Wudan guilds on their making of Everquest history. It was both a job well done and an impressive showing of persistence.

Sleeper Battle
11/17/03, 11:59 PST | Kwill

The talk around Norrath today is the battle on Rallos Zek that got The Sleeper (the dragon Kerafyrm) down to 27% health before he despawned in the midst of the battle. The Sleeper was supposedly unkillable, and had been buffed up since he was last released on various servers.

According to various guild message boards, three major guilds, Wudan, Ascending Dawn and Magus Imperialis Magicus in a 170 people zerg went for three hours and ten minutes against The Sleeper. During the fight, 60-70 people at a time were maintained in the zone, with the melees engaged on The Sleeper constantly being ressurected in. Many players followed along as the fight was heard "round the world" on at least 9 ingame server wide channels.

To give you some idea how hard this guy hits, here's a piece of a log from a fight:

[Sat Nov 15 21:24:23 2003] Kerafyrm hits Kryptopholus for 5199 points of damage.
[Sat Nov 15 21:24:36 2003] Kerafyrm hits Kazug for 6819 points of damage.
[Sat Nov 15 21:27:00 2003] Kerafyrm hits Dupre for 6999 points of damage.
[Sat Nov 15 21:42:51 2003] Kerafyrm shouts 'Begone insect, I have much slaying yet to do!'
[Sat Nov 15 21:42:51 2003] You have taken 19200 points of damage.
[Sat Nov 15 21:42:51 2003] You have been knocked unconscious!
[Sat Nov 15 21:42:51 2003] You have been slain by Kerafyrm!
Players were reporting that he hit for a maximum of 6999, and along with death touch procs. No resistable spells landed on him. Pets and arrows provided damage per second, along with a constant influx of melee characters.

Some players were sure that the GMs depopped The Sleeper as he got to low health. Rumor is that he is not supposed to be killed, and the script is broken, so they had to intervene. There is no evidence that the GMs deliberately despawned him -- it could have been written into the script itself that The Sleeper would despawn when he was down to a certain level of health. For their efforts, players involved was awarded 2 AA points. The zone was reset as if nothing had ever happened.

Ornate Orb of Mastery (Mages)
11/13/03, 8:39 PST | Kwill

Mastering the orb does including making your pet better:
Ornate Orb of Mastery and pet focus augmentations.

If you are the owner of an Ornate Orb of Mastery, please do not place an augmentation on it with the purpose of focusing your epic pet. The Ornate Orb must be equipped in the primary hand to focus the pet, but the Orb of Mastery must also be equipped in the primary hand to summon the pet. Obviously you can't have two items in the same hand, so if you place such an augment (specifically the Diamond Prism of Companionship) on you Ornate Orb, you will not be able to focus your epic pet.

If you currently have the Diamond Prism of Companionship bonded to your Ornate Orb of Mastery, you may petition your GM and they will reimburse the item for you.

There's some history behind this problem, but the message above is the important thing.

When the Diamond Prism was introduced, it was not intended to focus the epic pet, and some Mages placed them on their Ornate Orb of Mastery. Recently we changed the Prism to focus Mage epic pets, and thus this problem occurred.


Expanded Character Transfers
11/13/03, 8:26 PST | Kwill

You can now move your characters between servers with items intact. Here are some of the details:
The EverQuest community has continued to grow each day. This community has evolved from the many fan sites, message boards, guilds and from playing the game. Many times when a new friend is made in the community, that friend does not always play on the same server that you do. Because of this, we allow the transfer of characters to different servers and have received many requests to transfer characters to different servers while maintaining the character's items and coin. Although we understood the desire for the EverQuest players to play with their friends and families on the same server without the need to reequip, established policy did not allow for such transfers to take place. This policy was established in order to maintain game balance, prevent fraudulent activity, and to eliminate the heavy burden and cost it would put on the Customer Service Department.

Due to the many requests to transfer characters from one server to another server and from one server to another server on a different account, we have implemented a value added service and have enacted a new policy to meet these requests. If you desire to transfer a character or characters from your primary account to your secondary account on a different server, or wish to transfer to a different server during non server split opportunities, the following service is offered. This is a value added service which requires a fee that can be billed to your credit card.

Fee Schedule

For moving characters on the same account from one server to a different server:
1 to 2 Characters $75 (total charge)
3 to 8 Characters $150 (total charge)

For moving characters from one of your accounts to one of your other accounts and changing to a different server:
1 Character $112.50
2 Characters $187.50 (total charge)
3 to 8 Characters $375 (total charge)

In addition to the fees described above, if you are a resident of an European Union country, you will also be charged a VAT equal to 17.5% of the applicable fee.

Guidelines and Restrictions In order to process a transfer from one account to a different account, the destination account must have access to the same expansions as the source account.

Guild affiliations: We will be removing any guild affiliation as part of the character move process.

Characters will not be moved to servers that are less than 6 months old.

Sanctioned Server-Splits offered by SOE/Verant will be at no charge. If you were not able to perform the /movelog command at the time it was offered, you will need to fill out our character move request form and all applicable charges and conditions described in this offer will apply to you.

When moving characters between accounts, billing information must be identical on both accounts and you must be the original owner of both accounts. You may use the credit card on file or submit a different credit card using the Billing Information fields. If the credit card holder is different from the holder of the card on file, please enter a short explanation in the bottom field. If you have only paid for your account with a Game Card, you must submit a credit card using the Billing Information fields.

It's pricey, but if you are tired of your old server politics, you can now move to another server with different politics!

From EQNews.txt, Nov. 5th, 2003:
November 05, 2003 3:00 am
------------------------------

** Quests, Adventures and Encounters **

- We have added one more new LDoN raid to Stormhammer. This raid was created with Elemental capable characters in mind.

- Changed the minimum required number of group members to request an adventure from 4 to 3.

- We have increased the consolation prize on level 65 hard adventures from 19 to 26 to match the increase in reward points for success (raised from 76 to 105 with the last update).

- Verrell Cogswin can help convert red dragon scales into green dragon scales.
(Whats he gonna do? paint the damn things? :P -Muskie)

- Fixed the Undead and Fire event in the Plane of Time. There was a bug that could cause some of the minions to fail to spawn.

- Raotin Teawel will always give out the correct robe for completion of his quest.

- Thought Destroyer has returned to the Plane of Hate.

- Check with the Town Crier of your home town for news. There is information about the recent activities of the Wayfarers that should prove interesting.

- We have made some improvements to the Plane of Air avatar rings. We have added a random interval to the respawn time of the Plane of Air avatar rings, placed apparitions at the avatar spawns so that it is possible to tell when the event is available, and the Avatar of Smoke will now respawn properly.

- The Mujaki event in Plane of Nightmares was completely rewritten.  It should now be a lot more intelligent about where it sends players.

- Increased the number of items found at the end of a successful LDoN raid.

** Skills and Alternate Advancement **

- Channeling Focus was not giving bards the proper reduction to the chance of missing a note.  It is now fixed and gives messages to indicate when it is has helped.

- Corrected a problem that was causing Alternate Advancement buff duration increase abilities and duration focus effects to negatively affect lull spells.

- We have fixed a bug that could cause pick pocketed items to not show up on your cursor until you zone. This could also sometimes cause the client to crash.

- Added /stoptracking command so that you no longer have to re-open the track window and hit cancel if you want to cancel your tracking quickly.
(There you go, Rangers. I told you that SOE gave you something. -Muskie)

- Activated AAs from Planes of Power that were not working if the user did not have Shadows of Luclin registered. This has been fixed.

** Spells and Pets **

- The Aura of Pain Recourse can no longer be removed by Radiant Cure.

- When the character changes size (through illusion), other players will no longer see the mounted player separate from their horse.

- Magician Pets will no longer attack the caster who summoned them.

- Hitting your own pet while in a PVP area or while you have the PK flag on will no longer kill it.

- Cataclysm of Ro has had its movement rate bonus removed to fix a stacking issue with the Selo's line of movement rate songs.

- Cassindra's Chant of Clarity and Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity now lasts 3 ticks instead of being instant.

  - Cloak of Luclin, Cloak of the Akheva and Frostreaver's Blessing will no longer be overwritten by Call of the Rathe.

  - There are still some issues with Frostreaver's blessing being overwritten by some spells.  We are aware of the issue and hope to get it resolved soon.

- Protection of the Cabbage, Protection of the Glades and Protection of the Nine will block Temperance, Aegolism and Virtue. (This is going to impact the Buffing community.. -Muskie)

- Ice Flame of E`ci now has the standard 2.5 second recast time.

- Reclaim energy should now function properly on the pet summoned by Child of Bertoxxulous.

  - Beastlord warders focused by Summoner's boon should now look as they should.

** Interface **

- We have added the consider color to the Target window. The art used to display the consider color can be removed or edited via XML by custom skins.

- We have also added a 3D target indicator.  The ring color indicates the con color of the target.  This can be turned off in the Options window. A new settings file in the UI folder can be edited by players to customize the appearance of the indicator, including different animated textures for each con color and other interesting settings.

- Added /fontface command (usage /fontface , e.g. /fontface Courier). This will change, on the fly, the fonts used by the interface (and over character's heads). Fonts can also be selected in the General Options page.

- Added /tell windows. Each time you get a "tell" from a new character, a new chat window would open. That window will only display "tells" from the particular character, and any message sent from the chat window would automatically be sent as a "tell" to that character. You can turn this option on and off in the Options window. If you have the maximum number of chat windows open when a new tell is received, the tell will go to the main chat window.,br>(I can see it now, for all you social butterflies --"I had so many tells it went to the main chat window!" --Kwill)

- Temporary pets will no longer send their buffs to the pet window. Generally they have none, and they would replace the buffs of any standard pet.

- The buff display portion of the pet window will now fade.

- The Auto-inventory area of the Inventory window now fades with the parent window.

- The Dump button in raid window now creates the proper, non-blank, file.

- We have also added time-stamp to Raid Dump output file.

- Pop-up dialogs no longer close when you press ESC. This will prevent the rez dialog from being closed accidentally.

- Lay on Hands will no longer "gray out" the Inspect and Open hot keys in the Hotbutton window. This was fixed earlier in the Actions window, but now it is fixed in the Hotbutton window as well.

- Alt-tabbing in and out of EQ should no longer cause mouse issues.

- Fixed a bug that sometimes left charmed pets health bar in the group window after the owner of that pet is killed.

- Find trader can now be cancelled in bazaar.

** Items **

- The Copper Medal of Rapture now gives a bonus to Int. instead of Wis.

** For the New Player **

- Left-clicking on an NPC prints out a message indicating what type of NPC they are and indicating that you should right-click on them. This message can be turned off in the Options window.

- New characters will now start with a bag.

- Added a tip window that pops up when you invoke the Quantity window that explains the use of Shift and Control when selecting items.

- All characters will gain a Sense Heading skill of 200, if it was below 200. All new characters will start with a Sense Heading of 200.

** Miscellaneous **

- Explore mode works properly again.
