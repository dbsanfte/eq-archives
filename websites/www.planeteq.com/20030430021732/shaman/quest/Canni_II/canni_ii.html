The items you need:

1) Crushed Diamonds:

In Timourous go to the Gorla Island (location: -8840, -6080) Invis and lev up, Jump off the boat and run to the Gorla Island. Gorlas dont see invis so run in find a bag on the ground at the above Loc pick it up.

2) Strange ochre clay:

Drops from Army Behemoths in the City of Mist in Emerald Jungle. I found it easiest simply to politly ooc that you are here for Ochre Clay.

3) Greyish bone chips:

Drops from the Skeletal Warlord in Karnor's Castle - Dreadlands. I found it easiest simply to politly ooc that you are here for greyish bone chips.

4) Shaman Powder: Must be the Black powder from Yun shamans in Trakanons Teeth. There are other frogs that drop shaman poweder in other zones but it will not work.

Finally:

Return the 4 items to either Marlyn McMerin on the docks in Firiona Vie, or Great Oowomp at the ogre camp in Timorous Deep, and you will be rewarded with the scroll McMerin's Feast (Cannibalize II)
