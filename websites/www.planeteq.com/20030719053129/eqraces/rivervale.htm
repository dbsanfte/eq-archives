Rivervale

Rivervale is the home of the Halflings. It is a fairly small, but has all the supplies an adventurous Halfling will need to start their journey.

The town itself has some similarities to the Elven cities of Kelethin and Felwithe, where they appear to be 'integrated' into the natural environment. Halflings feel a great draw to the outdoors and feel right at home surrounded by grassy fields, trees and streams.

Though the size of the buildings would make it somewhat difficult to a barbarian to explore them, Rivervale is fairly easy to navigate. It has two entrances, one from Misty Thicket and one from Kithicor Forest, both lead into the main 'green' of which the Fool's Gold tavern is the centre piece. (Also the Rogue Guild) From here the Bank is a short walk North (The Warrior Guild is stationed inside which aids bank security) and the Chapel of Mischief (Cleric guild) can be found to the South East. Other places of interest for those new to the town (or to Norrath) are Bobick's Boads for fishing supplies, Kevlin's Gear for armor which is to the South of Fool's Gold and the farmstead where the Druid and Ranger Guilds reside. (East of Fool's Gold) 

There are also two small 'settlements' to the North and to the South. The South has an oven and forge, and the North has a pottery wheel and Kiln. There is also a Brew Barrel behind Fool's Gold.
