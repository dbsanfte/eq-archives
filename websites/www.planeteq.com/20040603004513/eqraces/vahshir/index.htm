History

The Vah Shir are a tribal, community orientated race. Vah Shir rely on each other and have a strong bond with others of their kind. The Vah Shir originally are the descendants of the Kerrans, a race of cat people who arrived on the moon of Luclin due to an Erudite magical experiment. The Kerrans original home was taken by the Erudites long ago, it is believed that for this reason, Erudites will not find themselves overly welcome in their cities. 

The Vah Shir are also a very tolerant people and many races are accepted in the city of Shar Vahl, even Trolls, Ogres and Dark Elves will find a home, if they are willing to prove themselves. The Vah Shir are considered a good people and many races in return will accept the Vah Shir in their homelands.   

Honour and Trust are very important in the Vah Shir society and those highly respected are often great and noble warriors. 

All Vah Shir have an affinity for nature and are commonly found as Beastlords and Shamans.   

 

Race Specific Tips

Huuunnggrry

Vah Shir consume food and drink very fast and an adventurous Vah Shir would do well to stock up before exploring Norrath. 

Friendly

Vah Shir are accepted in many cities and don't usually have problems in finding supplies, an experienced Vah Shir can use this to their advantage; hunting in the best dungeons and locations for their level.
