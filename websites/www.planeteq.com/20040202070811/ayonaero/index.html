    Exclusive Interview on Leadership Exp
    1/28/04, 8:48 PST | Kwill

    Jamey Ryan, EverQuest designer, answered some of my questions over email about the new leadership experience feature coming out with the Gates of Discord Expansion:
    PEQ: What was the idea behind implementing Leadership Experience?
    JR: The goal was to provide another venue to advance your character and to add another dynamic to the game while in groups and raids.

    PEQ: Was this aimed more at raid leaders or experience group leaders?
    JR: We have created a system for raid leadership experience and group leadership experience. You earn each independent of the other. Along with this, you acquire raid leadership abilities and group leadership abilities respectively.

    PEQ: What are some of the point award abilities that you think are most interesting? How will they work?
    JR: There is an ability to mark up to three NPCs with a special graphical symbol, each being unique, so groups and raids can identify attack order. This also helps mezers to be able to distinguish which NPC should be mezed and in what order.

    We have also provided a array of stat bonuses that a leader can acquire for his group or raid. This includes a Mana Enhancement, Health Enhancement, and an Offensive Enhancement with varying levels.

    PEQ: Some people feel that a group leader does not always take on a leadership role. Did the dev team take this into consideration when they designed the Leadership AA system? Will everyone benefit because just because a person formed a group and is the "leader" doesn't necessarily mean they "lead" the group?
    JR: We did consider this and designed this system to help group and raid leaders become better at their roles.

    PEQ: What about soloers and people who play with just a partner? Will this affect them?
    This feature is designed for groups and raids. There is no solo aspect to it, although you can start earning leadership experience from level one.

    PEQ: Do you think that people will now be more inclined to start a group than to just wait for one?
    JR: We definitely hope that with the introduction of this new system, that players will be more inclined to be proactive in regard to forming groups.

    PEQ: Will these abilities only be "on" when you're in a group? Or will they always be active on you once you've purchased them, whether or not you're the leader?
    JR: All of the leadership abilities, both group and raid, are only "on" when you are in a group or raid.

    PEQ: So if someone has max level exp and max AAs, they can still only put 20% towards leadership exp? with the other 80% wasted? At least offense, mana and health enhancement sound interesting. Might these be more attack, mana and hp?
    JR: We are providing the ability to turn leadership experience on or off. If it is turned on, then you will get 20% less regular experience. Yes the offense, mana, and health enhancements are more attack, mana, and hp.

    PEQ: What about on raids when you're not getting any noticeable xp anyway?
    JR: When you are on a raid, the raid leader will gain raid leadership experience and the group leaders will gain group leadership experience.

    Thanks Jamey!

    Guilds FAQ
    1/27/04, 8:58 PST | Kwill

    Questions about forming or changing a guild? SOE has created a Guilds FAQ. I find these to be two of the more interesting questions and answers:
    'I can get my 10 members together, but not during GM hours. What should I do?'

    We no longer require that 9 people be present with you at time of guild creation on server. All you need to do is petition on server after you have received your approval email. Please remember that the guild leader will be held responsible for having at least 9 other people in your guild aside from alternate characters when we do our guild maintenance. IMPORTANT: You will still need 9 other people in chat for initial guild registration.

    'Our Leader quit/left EQ/is not around and we need a new leader.'

    You will need to /petition your server GM for this matter. When you /petition, you will need to have at least 50% of your guild members present. When the GM arrives, they will take a vote as to whom the guild members present would like as their guild leader. After the vote is taken, the person selected as the new guild leader will be granted the leader flag. We recommend that you create a new guild and move your current membership to that new guild. This would prevent any ownership disputes if the original guild owner returns and requests leadership of his/her guild back.

    Lots more info on the FAQ, a good thing to know about!

    Leadership: NPC Marking
    1/27/04, 8:07 PST | Kwill

    One of the more interesting additions to the Gates of Discord expansion are the new skills being added to the game. One of the most intriguing is the leadership series. SOE has sent us an overview of the NPC marking system:
    Leadership Overview

    One of the exciting new features in EverQuest: Gates of Discord is the addition of leadership experience and abilities. Leadership will be available for all levels and adds some very nice features for the aspiring group and raid leaders.

    This week we will highlight the NPC marking system.

    With this new system a group or raid leader can highlight up to three NPCs for the group or raid to see. This can allow the leader to designate attack order, mez order or just plain identify the mobs for the group/raid. With the initial ability you can mark one NPC, with the ability to mark up to three as you spend more leadership points on this skill. The NPCs are noted by a number before their name as well as a new targeting circle at their feet.

    This is only one of the great new abilities coming with Gates of Discord. We hope you like it.

    With so much of EQ revolving around groups and raids, the addition of specific leader abilities makes working with a group easier, more productive, and a lot more fun.

    SOE has sent us some exclusive screenshots of how the new NPC marking system will appear when in use (plus we get to see more new monsters!)
