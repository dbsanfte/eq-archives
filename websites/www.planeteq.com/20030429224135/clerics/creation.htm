DARK ELF
Dark Elves resemble the High Elves in many ways, but follow the dark paths. They thrive in the dark and they worship evil gods. They come from the underground city of Neriak, where they first learn the ways of darkness and evil. Dark Elves are very intelligent and possess great potential as spellcasters.

DWARF
Dwarves are proud and strong. They come from Kaladim in the Butcherblock Mountains. They are very physical and make good paladins. They are known for their predominant facial hair, which even leaves some of their woman with a beard. Dwarves love food and a good pint of ale...or twenty. They are wise people, which makes them great Clerics.

ERUDITE
Erudites are the most intelligent race of all that walk the earth of Norrath. They live in the secluded city of Erudin. However it is known that some followers of a certain deity, not to be mentioned here, live in the dark city of Paineel. Erudites are generally social beings, but believing themselves to be superior to all other races, tend to socialize only with their own kind.

FROGLOK
Frogloks are a proud amphibian race, devout followers of Mithaniel Marr. Frogloks have no time for jokes and play, taking their duties very seriously. They are agile, dexterous and wise, and accepted by the other good aligned races of Norrath, which makes them a suitable choice for the path of a cleric.

GNOME
Gnomes are the smallest playable race that inhabit Norrath. They come from the city of Ak'anon. They mostly live underground, and they rarely mingle with the other races. Gnomes are intelligent and therefore you often see them becoming great spellcasters. Due to their size, you will never have any problems getting in anywhere, which can prove to be a very good thing.

HALFLING
Halflings are joyous people. They are very social and they love to travel, which is usually done walking on their haired feet. They are short but very graceful. They live in pact with nature and it is in nature that they feel at home. Halflings excel at most everything they do, and the classes available to them, are almost perfected in the usual Halfling manner.

HIGH ELF
The High Elves of Norrath come from the beautiful city of Felwithe. They are frail in body, but in the ways of the mind, they surpass most of the other races. They are excellent spellcaster, as they spend most of their time on the study of Arcane scriptures and knowledge.

HUMAN
Humans are the most predominant race of Norrath. They come from Freeport and Qeynos. They are all-rounders, but they are poor spellcasters. They are not that intelligent, but they are liked throughout Norrath, having almost no enemies. They are one of the only two races to be able to become monks, which is where they have their forte.
