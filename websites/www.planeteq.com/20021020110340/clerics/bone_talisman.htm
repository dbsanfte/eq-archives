Bone Talisman

This item goes into the off hand.

Collect a Bone Talisman from Errak Thickshank on Kerra Isle. A Giant Snake Fang off a Giant Snake (common in newbie zones). A Tiger Skin, from a Tiger, the one in Ak`anon zoo seems to be the easiest to get to. An aviak charm, drops in South Karana off an Aviak Avocet. Turn them into Errak for the Bone Talisman.

The Tiger skin is also dropped other places, but it seems that the Tiger in Ak'Anon zoo should be the easiest to camp
