Drunkards of Norrath
It's Official: We Suck
09.04.02

Earlier today, in a press release by Verant Interactive, the game developers' stance on "guilds whose only common link is the desire to be drunk and lazy" was finally revealed.

"Seriously? Screw those people. In-game drinking, alcohol, and debauchery are all things we wish we could 'un-include' from this game. I'm told there's even a guild on our Cazic-Thule Server that does these things almost to the exclusion of ALL other things. To them we officially say, 'Get a damn grip. How many of you people even have your epics? Why even play the game? You freaking suck.'"

Twyl, the leader of the Drunkards of Norrath, could not be reached for comment, probably because he was praying the technicolor psalm to the porcelain god, or scrubbing Emotion Lotion off of his fat, sweaty body.

Bullies - Stormhaven (9/5/2002 5:44 AM)

So here it was patch day and the normal race began. We knew we had to get going quickly cause if we didn't all the good eats would be gone, and we'd be left with nothing but creamed spinach and bean sprouts. No one wants that to happen.

There we were standing in line for our meal when this ass, the Avatar of War, does the whole "I'm bigger than you" routine.

We had to make sure he understood that we don't take kindly to that kind of guff around here. We smacked him around until we shoved him two-thirds of the way through the brick wall. We figure he won't be bothering us again anytime soon. Despite his rep, he was a wuss. In the past, he had been known to put several players into traction - well we don't know where he got that rep from, cause he went down without a single casualty. Even Booboo, the lame druid bear pet, survived.

Well, he was soundly beaten, and we did what every normal 60+ raiding party does when someone like the Avatar of War tries to take your lunch...

We stole his stuff.
