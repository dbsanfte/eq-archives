 Thursday 23th January 2003
Legacy of Ykesha

The new Everquest expansion has been announced, Legacy of Ykesha. It will be aimed primarily at characters level 35-60, with a new playable race, and other interesting new features.

The new race will be Frogoks, and they will be able to play as Clerics, Paladins, Warriors, Wizards, but more importantly, Shaman. They will be a good alligned race, worshipping Mithanel Marr, with the following starting stats, and innate abilities:

    STR 70
    STA 80
    AGI 100
    DEX 100
    WIS 75
    INT 75
    CHA 50

    Swimming begins at a skill level of 125
    Ultravision
    Amphibious Breathing (ability to hold their breath under water for long periods of time)
    Higher Magic and Poison Resistance
    Able to wear small and medium sized armor

The other features are there, as usual, new zones, new spells and new creatures, but there are also new ones, such as a Cartography system, a new LFG tool, a Guild Management tool, new armour dyes (which may involve Alchemy) and a new slot: Charms. Among the new spells may be Stoicism and Pure Blood. (see below)

To learn about each feature, go to www.legacyofykesha.com and see for yourself.

 Sunday 5th January 2003
A Sad Day but a New Beginning

EQ Shaman's creator, Maddcovv has decided to call it quits. He will be leaving the website up, but it will not be updated any more. Here is his post from the Shaman's Crucible Forums:

    "I'm calling it quits, the game has become more work than fun and I dont really miss it when i dont play. Thank you all for helping me build Eqshaman.com . I will leave the site up for some time. If anyone wants the site let me know, I'll have to decide what to do with it one day.

    For now slow em fast and dot em up boys!

    Good Journeys!

    John Reese
    Maddcovv
    Eqshaman.com"

I will be increasing the scope of the Shaman of Justice website to fill the lack of up to date Shaman information that the loss of that great website will leave and Shaman of Justice will become 'The Online Guide for the Shaman of Any Race'.
