Blood of Nadox

In a brew barrel, combine A Blackened Vial, Troll Skull Blood (both in Crypt of Nadox, and Gulthak Gulf Water. (dropped & foraged in Gulf of Gunthak) Then combine the resulting Black Potion with the item 'Pure Blood' (drops from Luggald Initiate in Crypt of Nadox) also in a brew barrel. You get a Glowing black potion, which you hand it inro Grennik Neltrin. As of note, these combines are now no-fail combines, making it impossible to fail the combine.

Stoicism

An Impassive Sea Spirit is underwater in Gulf of Gunthak. Hand it an Empty Flask (from Drunken Pirates in Dulak's Harbor) and you will get a stone. When you hand it the stone (be ready for a fight) two more spirits will spawn at the enterance of the upturned boat. You need to kill Flotsam & Jetsam & loot their essences. Handing them into the original spirit will give you the spell: Stoicism.
