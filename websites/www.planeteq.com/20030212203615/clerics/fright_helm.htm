Fright Forged Helm

Talk to Sern Adolia and ask him about summoning the avatar of fright.

He requires some special components for the ritual. Fetch him the following: zombie flesh (dropped by various zombies), the dust used in the process of mummification (Embalming dust - dropped by mummies), charred bone chips (drops from Obretl the skeleton necro - a rare spawn in the buried tower in SRo) [no drop]. IMPORTANT: Charred bone chips from Kunark skeletons will not work! a vial of Tunare's Breath (you need to kill a druid named Kalayia Woodwhisper in Lesser Faydark, who is an ultra-rare spawn)[no drop].

When you hand him the items, he will give you a mundane helm and the chanters nearby will summon the avatar of Fright. Give Dread the mundane helm to receive the Fright Forged Helm.
