Froglok Specific Tips

 
History

Frogloks have been around in Norrath for a long time, but have been confined to small groups and dark, secluded locations. Their city in the Swamp of No Hope is perhaps the best example of their isolation.

With the victory over the trolls in Grobb the Frogloks gained a valuable stronghold where they are able to begin establishing themselves. The Frogloks are really only getting started and in time may hold the same influence over Norrath's fate as the fast evolving human race.

They are generally a truthful, honest people and are proud followers of Mithaniel Marr. The other good or neutral aligned races will accept them, but are indifferent at best, the Frogloks have yet to prove themselves and many view them with suspicion.

The evil aligned races, think of them just as an annoyance, and would never allow them inside or even near their cities and outposts.

 

Race Specific Tips

Land Ahoy! 

 

Frogloks are excellent swimmers and can hold their breath for long periods of time, this skill can come in handy if you need to evade a tough monster or move around quickly.
