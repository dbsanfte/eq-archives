High Rolling with the AoW...

Posted by: Druss on Monday, August 26th, 2002 @ 10:46 PM

Random drop tables are part of the excitement of EQ. Sure, sometimes, its a real raid-downer when after you've been fighting Ubermob_02 for 20 minutes, he drops 3 BST weapons. But every once in a while, the drop table comes out...pretty nice.

Case in point: On passing through Kael Drakken, we dropped a coin in this fella and look what happened.

Yeah, no joke, 4 blades of carnage. For CC, Congrats are in order to Rulak and Warlor on their new suicide sticks...err, weapons, yeah, thats what I meant.

So...what else have we killed this week?

Well, along with the good old AoW, we got Vindi and the Statue of Rallos Zek. Prior to that, we did the Rhag "hat trick" in Ssra (Rhag 1, Rhag 2, and Arch Lich). Killed the High Priest, Velketor (yeah yeah, go retro crap. Anything to get Angturil his "pretty-in-pink" robe, I guess...), Rhag1 & 2 again, Blood, and had a pretty decent Emperor attempt as well. Busy busy busy busy.

Crimson Talon
AUG 23, 2002
Trakanon's lease on Seblis expired and we went into his lair to evict him. Instead of throwing him out, we decided just to slay the foul beast. Drops were: Donal's BP [Bormer], Cloak [Kilwean], and 4 Teeth [Physst, Silvercaster, Proetus, and Ceeni].

AUG 21, 2002
This night's raid was Hate in order to get a ranger stone. We quickly cleared our way through the first level and proceeded to work on the second. Drops on the second floor were very light (as always - that zone really needs a better drop rate). After dozens and dozens of kills, we managed to get a Darkmetal Falchion. Innoruuk seemed to be aware of our presence and just before we pulled him, he ran out of the building to another spot! We rescouted and then fought an epic battle with the god. Overall great fight and only one death. Drops were: Eye [holding], Icebelt [Denor], Pauldrons [Karrzem-guest], Triumphant Mask [Machedor], Ring of Pure Blood [Awll], and a Shattered Emerald [Gilthanus].

AUG 20, 2002
Started the night in ToV, but another guild was already present. We did the right hallway for a bit, until we decided to try Vindicator. We ported other quickly and barely paused from our sprint to the arena to kill the giants in our way. Then, a flawless pull and a well executed fight later, we divided up Vindi's loot: Living Thunder Earring [Signarius] and Head [Dunskye].

Our posse then moved on to Fear to kill all the minis in the hope of getting a SK epic piece. The mobs seemed to drop everything else except what we were looking for including: Shield, Claws, Puppet Strings, and a Trunk.

Tue-08.27.02-Ouden-
-Kael, Maiden-
Sunday night we took one group to Maiden's Eye to get some experience and some loots. Not much dropped though, couple of people got some upgrades, but nothing major. The pullers dropped a couple of times each though. But overall wasn't too bad.

Monday Night: Kael: Mission: Kill Stuff: Mission Accomplished. We grouped up with LoD to take out the Tormax area in Kael. I must say they were excited.

Hawk and Animal were bodyslamming and piledriving their way through the giants left and right. Wait...what? Not Legion of Doom? Oh, the guild Legacy of Darkness. Forget all that I just said then. Legacy of Darkness got the following: Plate Bracer, Plate Gaunts, Coldain Gloves, Mask of War, Plate Arms, and Silk Boots. Grats to all who came away with something. And to the others you need to start cybering us officers better if you want some loot. Nobody gets something for nothing! Anyway, here's the happy party.
