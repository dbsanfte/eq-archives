Karnors Castle



Have you braved the haunted halls and rooms of Karnor's Castle to gain experience and treasure? The ghostly wall hangings from the parapets speak of a grander time in the history of the world. Home of the infamous warlord Venril Sathir, both the living and the dead want to make sure you stay out of their castle! Test your knowledge of this dungeon, where even the brave fear to tread.
