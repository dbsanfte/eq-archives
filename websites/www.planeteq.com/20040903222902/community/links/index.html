  • Guildboss ($20 shareware): GuildBoss is a guild and clan management program for Windows 9x+/NT. Here's a list of just a few features that GuildBoss has to offer a frazzled guild or clan boss: Bulk emailing and ICQ paging with option to send custom, pre-generated messages. One-click HTML roster report generation with customizable page layout. Automatic promotion/demotion scheduling and notification (optional). Automated "billing" of dues and/or rank tax (optional). Automated flagging of "inactive" members (optional). Innovative member evaluation program based on custom Deeds and incentives (optional). Unlimited custom ledgers and HTML ledger reporting. Roster exporting.
  • Guildmagic: GuildMagic.com is a system for helping Everquest players and their guild masters to keep useful and accurate lists of their guild members, their levels and their skills.
  • Guild Portal : GuildPortal provides a fully-functional community management system for free, with additional content available for the price some other providers charge for just a forum. These are some of our features: Customizable page appearance with style templates to get you started. Control of the types of content on your site, where they appear, and security based upon member settings. Content Type offering a variety of functionality, including forums, events, voting, web-based e-mail, rostering, news, info sections, custom HTML/text, and depending on the game, even content that integrates with the publishers. Free, though content restrictions and advertisements are placed on non-subscribing guilds.
