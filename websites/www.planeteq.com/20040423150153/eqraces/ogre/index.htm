History

Ogres are incredibly strong, created for War by Rallos Zek. Originally Ogres were also an intelligent people but were punished by the Gods for assisting Rallos Zek in attempting to take over the planes of power. Their punishment: Stupidity

The Ogres that walk the lands now are very dumb and stupid, but they have retained their ancient strength and stamina. Most Ogres become Warriors, a profession which their strength benefits them greatly. Some also become Shamans or Shadow Knights, Ogres are still at heart an evil race. 

The only races which will not look upon ogres as the enemy are Trolls and Dark Elves. Trolls are considered the Ogres 'best friends' and can commonly be found hunting the smaller races unfortunate enough to come across either's home city.  

 

Race Specific Tips

Size Matters

Ogres have one big advantage over all other races and that is size. Ogres are stronger and have more stamina than any other playable race. They also cannot be stunned from the front.

All this makes Ogres very good Warriors, the one drawback of this size is they are incredibly slow. An Ogres agility and dexterity is poor by anyone's standards and it would often be wise to spend all your bonus points beefing up these two stats, some stat enhancing items wouldn't hurt either.
