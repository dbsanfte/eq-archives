Sites helpful for a newbie

(General)

 

Planet EQ - http://www.planeteq.com

EQ Races host, all the info you could need on starting a new character including descriptions of the classes and races, commands and emotes, tradeskills, maps, and a very helpful newbie forum.

 

Casters Realm - http://eq.crgaming.com

Covering many areas of EQ, including some very useful creation guides and spell guides.

 

Allakhazam - http://everquest.allakhazam.com

A site better suited to people who know what they are looking for, but has some helpful info on newbie armor/weapon quests.

 

(Specific)

 

Shaman of Justice - http://www.planeteq.com/shaman

An excellent site for anyone interested in starting in EQ as the shaman class (and for the more experienced shaman), has many guides for spells, tactics and a great section on the alchemy skill. 

 

Clerics of Everquest - http://www.planeteq.com/clerics

A great resource for new clerics, includes information on creation, abilities, skills, spells, cleric quests and a very active forum.

 

EQ Weapons and Armor DataBase - http://www.planeteq.com/eqwadb

One of the largest item databases for Everquest and the perfect site for finding those upgrades for your rusty broadsword and leather tunic.

 

Saathy's homepage - http://homepage.ntlworld.com/michaela-jane/

The personal site of Saathy Silversoul, includes some great info for anyone starting or currently playing as a Dark Elf.

 

Lucy - http://lucy.fnord.net

Lucy is another excellent database covering both items and spells. Note that some of the information here is from the Test server and may not apply to the live servers.

 

If you would like a link placed here from your site or a good site you know please contact me at nerishau@planeteq.com and ill see what I can do. Please note that only sites with Everquest related content can be linked to. Guild sites can be included but only if the guild makes special effort to accommodate newbies, or provides helpful information.
