Level 61/62 Spells

To obtain these you need to hand an Ethereal Parchment to the Otter shaman spell merchant in the Plane of Knowledge library. He will hand you a random level 61 or 62 spell in return. The parchments drop off random monsters in all Planes of Power zones, however are rare, and more commonly found on higher level monsters, and even bosses. Be ready to compete for these parchments, as every other casting class needs them for their own spells.

 

Level 63/64 Spells

To obtain these you need to hand an Spectral Parchment to the Otter shaman spell merchant in the Plane of Knowledge library. He will hand you a random level 63 or 64 spell in return. The parchments drop off some monsters in the higher planes in Planes of Power zones, however are rare, and more commonly found on higher level monsters, and even bosses. Be ready to compete for these parchments, as every other casting class needs them for their own spells. The exact drop rates and monsters are unknown at this time.

 

Level 65 Spells

Planar Imbue Spells

Not exacty a quest, but to make an imbue spell you need a scroll of Imbue Diamond (North Freeport Cleric Guild), a Raw Diamond and a third item. The third item determines what spell you get. You combine the three items in a Planar Jewelers Kit

  • Imbue Disease: Bubonian Blood
  • Imbue Justice: Alkaline Etched Stone
  • Imbue War: War Wraith Blood

 

Planar Armour

The shaman guildmaster in the Plane of Knowledge (he's in the building behind the soulbinder, along with the Beastlord guild master) is willing to reward you for every piece of old world planar armour you return to him. He is on about the Plane of Hate 'Rune Etched' armour. For every piece given to him you will recieve noticable experience, and a gem. Some people have recieved peridots, Diamonds, Raw Diamonds or even Blue Diamonds for their old armour. Unfortunatly, there isn't a quest of this nature for Iksar Shaman and their Scaled Mystic armour, also from the Plane of Hate.

 

Infusion of Spirit

This spell is obtainable via a similar quest method as the lvl 61-62 spells, however, you need to hand the parchment in to the Beastlord spell quest.
