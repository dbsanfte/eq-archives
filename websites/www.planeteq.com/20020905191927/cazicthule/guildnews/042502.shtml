ToV day Four
Posted by Ztalten On Apr 22 2002
Another incredible day in HoT... rocky at the start but once we got past the doors everything started clicking. I don't think many would disagree that we need to spend more weekends in ToV again sometime soon as we got a total of 56 drops (30 plate, 18 leather, 4 chain, and 4 silk.) After ToV ended we took 10 people up to the Plane of Air to kill Gwan for Sasanaz's epic (no casualties.. luckily, because we had no cleric to rez them heh) and picked up a Symbol of Marr (15ac 5str 5wis necklace) for Sterling off the island one pixies. Big huge grats to Sasanaz on getting another monk epic!!!! Grats to the following who got loots from ToV today:

Chain bracer for Picket, plate bracer and gloves for Bregga (thanks to Nirrissa), plate legs and boots for Varner, plate boots for Bromm, plate helm for Barbed, plate boots and bracer (thanks to Ssinjinx) for Sereta, plate boots for Corus, plate helm for Samn, plate gloves for Sterling, plate arms for Bladestrike, leather legs for Nikolete, leather hat for Mythika, leather bracer and legs for Wutai, leather arms and legs for Tyelia, leather boots for Silini, silk crown for Drokaten, and silk bracer for Duco.
