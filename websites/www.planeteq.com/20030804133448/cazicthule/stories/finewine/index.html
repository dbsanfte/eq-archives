Fine Wine
by Delrachnid (Twelve Year)

The rain fell hard as a small figure ran clumsily through the trees. Panicking and without any knowledge of what lay before them they looked back as if being chased by some unconceivable horror. Slipping in the wet earth, the high elf fell to her knees then hurriedly tried to regain her balance. As she stood up she took the time to again glance behind her. Seeing nothing she took a small breath and fumbled in her pack for some water. As she raised a small flask to her lips she looked over its rim cautiously. The water was cool as it slid down her throat and she relaxed a little, tilting her head back and taking in its refreshing quality. Then she heard it. An unmistakable foot step. Dropping the flask in reckless abandon she turned to run just as she caught a glance of a dark figure gliding through the trees.

The figure was covered in a dark cloak and swiftly ran between the forests’ branches. Seeing the high elf he instinctively withdrew one of his daggers from its sheathe. Before the high elf had time to bolt, Delrachnid flicked his dagger in her direction. Embedding itself in the elf’s calf, Delrachnid delighted in hearing her scream in pain. As the high elf fell to one knee the assassin slowed his step and removed his other dagger.

The pain was unbearable. She tried to reach behind her and pull the blade from her leg but the poison had already begun to take its draining effect and her hand fell half-heartedly to her side. She arduously turned her head to gaze upon the figure that approached her. His eyes glowed red from beneath his cloak, but all other features were mysteriously shrouded. She could not help but think how elegant this one looked, even as he approached her with a blade in his hand.

Delrachnid stooped at her side and looked at her smugly. Reaching behind her he watched as she winced while he removed the dagger. Cleaning its edge on her sleeve he held it close to her neck.

"I do enjoy a chase, but it would have been a lot easier on you if you didn’t run," Delrachnid stated calmly.

Parting her lips the high elf spoke with hesitation, "Just do it and get it over with dark one. Know this…Neriak will fall."

"I plan to do just that and of course…a little more for the comment and amusement’s sake."

Holding both daggers in his hands Delrachnid bent low and looked deeply into the high elf’ eyes. Concentrating on keeping his glare he noticed a pale shine in the pupils as his blades entered her throat. He watched, as the eyes became large and full of life with the elf’s body twitching at his side. Then nothing. Removing his own flask from his belt pouch the assassin began to fill it with some of the blood that fell from the wound of the dead elf’s neck. Standing to his feet Delrachnid looked down and spat as he raised the flask to his lips.

"Now let’s see about this Elven wine I’ve heard so much about."
