Sebilis


Old Sebilis
By Elsbin


Old Sebilis
By Questers of Norrath


Mushroom Area
By Questers of Norrath


Trakanon's Lair
By Questers of Norrath

Location: Old Sebilis is the home of Trakanon, the entrance to which, is located in Trakanon's Teeth.

Continent: Kunark

EQ Version Needed: Ruins of Kunark

Bind: Bind self only

Creatures & NPCs: Arch Duke Iatol, Baron Yosig, Blood of Chottal, Brogg, Crypt Caretaker, Dar Ghoul Knights, Emperor Chottal, Frenzied Pox Scarab, Froggy, Froglok Armorer, Froglok Armsman, Froglok Bartender, Froglok Bok Knights, Froglok Bok Shaman, Froglok Bok Wizards, Froglok Chef, Froglok Commander, Froglok Dar Knights, Froglok Ilis Knights, Froglok Ilis Shaman, Froglok Ilis Wizards, Froglok Jin Shaman, Froglok Jin Wizards, Froglok Kor Shaman, Froglok Krup Knights, Froglok Krup Shaman, Froglok Krup Wizards, Froglok Ostiary, Froglok Pickler, Froglok Reet Knights, Froglok Reet Shaman, Froglok Reet Wizards, Froglok Repairer, Froglok Zol Knights, Gangrenous Scarabs, Gruplinort, Harbinger Freglor, Heirophant Prime Grekal, Iksar Necromancer, Imperial Crypt Guardian, Krup Ghoul Knights, Krup Knights, Leperous Scarabs, Myconid Adepts, Myconid Priests, Myconid Reavers, Myconid Spore King, Myconid Warriors, Necrosis Scarab, Pox Scarab, Sebililte Juggernauts, Sebilite Golems, Sebilite Guardians, Sebilite Protectors, Sepsis Scarabs, Skeletal Baron, Skeletal Champions, Skeletal Crusaders, Skeletal Duke, Skeletal Harbingers, Skeletal Hierophant, Spectral Commander, Spectral Harbingers, Spectral Revenants, Tolapumj and Trakanon.

Zone Type: Indoor - Dungeon

Factions: Trakanon

Level Range: 45 - 60

Miscellaneous:

  • A key is required to enter this zone.
  • You can not exit from the same place where you enter this zone. Use caution when entering due to this fact.
  • Deep within this zone lives the dragon, Trakanon.
