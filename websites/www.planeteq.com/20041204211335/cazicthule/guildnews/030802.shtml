Cazic-Thule Guild News

March 2- March 7, 2002
Compiled by Kwill on 3/08/02

Note: unless news is emailed to me, I check all guild websites on cazicthule.com for news, and post recent news I find there.

If you would like to have news of what your guild is doing around Norrath posted here, please email kwill@planeteq.com. And, if for some bizarre reason you would not like your guild featured in this report, let me know that too!

News from Guilds Around Cazic Thule:

There were 28 days in February.

2 days no-one has a clue what we did

Soel fell asleep at a raid once in Feb.
We attempted Creator 3 times, Arch Lich twice, and killed Rhags twice.
Tunare felt our wrath the most, we killed her 5 times.
Browny sent me the most ‘<3’ after receiving Shissar.

Shadowed Soul
Wednesday, March 6th, 2002.
Workin In A Coal Mine, Goin Downtown....
Last week, we kack'd the Thought Horror Overfiend (new version) one day, and the Burrower Parasite the next. Well.....doin it that way again is so....well....last week!

This week we hadda kill both on the same day, and kill them we did. We zoned into the deep, plowed to the bridge, leaped off the bridge, dragged some corpses, leaped off the bridge again, waited while the late arrivals showed up, laughed at them as they almost died making it to the bridge only to watch them leap into space and miss the bridge, and then for good measure we fell off the bridge again. After that stupid *@^%@$#@^ing bridge, the Parasite and the THO are cake in comparison.
Not buying the cake theory? Bah! Ok, so they ain't cake, but they ARE fun as hell (Note to VI, more like these, plzthx). They are a sort of beautiful chaos of a fight, and if you can't react, you get wiped....AWESOME. You don't need 80 people to do them (hell, we had 37 fot THO....well....36 if you don't count me from fallin asleep) and they are certainly not based around main tanking, are MTs exploits yet?
Welp, kiss kiss, time for Rangers to go sleepy-bye with dreams of big nasty Avatars...
