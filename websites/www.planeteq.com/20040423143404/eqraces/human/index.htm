History

The Humans are probably the most numerous race on Norrath, they have no special abilities, no preferred religions or professions and each is noticeably different in nature and opinions.

Humans have several cities and depending on their profession and religion tend to start out in either Qeynos or Freeport. Both are orderly cities, open to all of the good races. 

 

Humans are found throughout Norrath, commonly found trading with the Dwarves and exploring the continents. The Humans have much in common with the Barbarian and Erudite races and share the common gods of Mithaniel Marr and Erolissi Marr.

 

Race Specific Tips

Work that candle

Humans have very poor night vision, use that candle and don't destroy it!

Likable

Humans have good faction with most good and neutral races; Human Shadow Knights have more choice in where to trade than say an Ogre or Iksar Shadow Knight. 

Levelling Up

With no special abilities Humans are allowed to level quite quickly, if you want to play a Hybrid Human ones won't be such a chore to gain experience with. 

Can't go wrong

Humans stats are average across the board, some careful allocation can make a good example of any class. Expect to use items to raise your classes primary and secondary attributes.
