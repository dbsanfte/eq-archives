Officer Apprentice Application
This application is for members interested in becoming an Officer of Huntress Lair.
Please review the requirements prior to completing the application.

If an applicant chooses to complete and submit the information once the requirements have
been reviewed and met, their application will be reviewed by the entire Leadership Council for an
Officer Apprentice position. The position will be for a trial period of 60 days, at which time Leadership and
Officer Apprentice will review the possibility for promotion.

Requirements

~ Applicant may only be evaluated for an Officer position if they have been guilded with Huntress Lair for 60 days ~
~ Applicant must play EQ for a minimum of 12 hours each week ~
~ Applicant must be able to organize and lead raids ~
~ Applicant must be willing and able to handle conflict resolution ~
~ Applicant must have all characters guilded in Huntress Lair ~
~ Applicant must participate actively on the Message Board ~
~ Applicant must participate actively in Guild Chat ~

If you choose to complete and submit the application below, you have agreed that you have met the above requirements.
