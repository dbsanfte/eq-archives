Leadership Roles

Aulilea

Alliance Coordinator:

  • This would involve inquiry and discussion with alliance guild leaders to determine the potential for relationship or ensure a smooth relationship with a current alliance guild. This includes, but is not limited to researching a guilds reputation, however she may be successful in doing so i.e. attending raids, making inquiries, etc.

Bank Coordinator:

  • Responsible for obtaining and maintaining donations and sales to and from the guild bank and illustrating that on the website.


Ragord

Raid Coordinator:

  • Coordinate one-two raids per week following the guidelines of our Raid Education, unless otherwise determined unnecessary i.e. Holidays, attendance, availability etc. This role in itself holds plenty of responsibility.

Officer Coordinator:

  • Coordinate a meeting with the Officers monthly and report log back to LCM with any thoughts, ideas and issues there may be.
  • Deal with minor issues related to the Officers behavior and/or performance.
  • Major issues that need to be dealt with, such as serious performance issues resulting in the potential loss of position can be discussed on a case by case basis to determine the appropriate LCM handling.

Wrensong

Meeting Coordinator:

    • Coordinate all aspects of a guild meeting once a month: Scheduling, Leading, Topics of Discussion, etc.

Social Event Coordinator:

  • This would involve coordinating an event once a month to improve guild member interaction and morale i.e. Bar Room Brawl, Naked Noobie Duels (LOL just made that up! We should do this), etc.

While we are all responsible for the ultimate success of these areas, any one of us should expect to be called upon to assist and provide input in that area, when needed. In addition, we should all feel free to contribute to these areas, to make this an awesome guild.
