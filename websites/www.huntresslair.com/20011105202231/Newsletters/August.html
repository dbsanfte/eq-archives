RECRUITING

I have been doing some research on the needs of our members. As you all know Huntress Lair has grown both quickly and responsibly. I would like to continue to grow, however it is most important that we continue to do so responsibly. I'm sure you are all aware of a few races that are rare when grouping. i.e. Cleric, Bards, Bards, Shammy's, etc. If you take a look at the member roster, you will see that we are in need a few of these races in order to become more well rounded. This will help optimize grouping capabilities and ensure that future raids are better organized. My reason for bringing this to everyone's attention is due to the membership quality we wish to maintain. Huntress Lair prides itself in the maturity of it's members and would like to continue that goal. I think everyone can agree that the best way to find quality, mature, fun loving people for the guild is by word of mouth. Your friends are our friends. My friends are your friends. If any of you know someone that would enjoy a family such as Huntress Lair then bring them to our attention.

I have recently been communicating with Obsidian Guard to determine the feasibility of forming an alliance between our two guilds. I think we each have unique benefits that we can offer other and this will be a big turning point in the growth of Huntress Lair. As we progress into the upper levels, our needs as individuals progress as well. While Huntress Lair is growing fast, we need to consider the needs of those members now rather then later. Epic Quests and various other activities are important factors to consider when reaching a 50+ level. I believe a guild of Obsidian Guard's nature can help us in those areas.
