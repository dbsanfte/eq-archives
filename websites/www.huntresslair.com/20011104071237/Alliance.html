REALM OF HONOR www.realmofhonor.com
Leader: Tugna Trueheart
Officers: Namila, Artagel and Drewt
Much like ourselves, Realm of Honor does not mirror the common goals of getting the best loot, the most loot, having certain level requirements and obscure rules to obtain permanent membership. Items and loot are only side-effects of teamwork by good, decent players who enjoy the game.

Instead, we step away from the politics and red-tape often associated with most popular guilds. The guild tag will be more than just a bunch of words next to your character name.

This is a place where we can be with most of their best Everquest friends to have fun and have help in adventuring, fighting, questing, searching, auctioning, etc. The guild will foster a type of structured, sincere, organized, honorable, easy-going, small, effective, respectful, and fun environment.
Characters of any level, class, and race will find the freedom to be "uber" in their own way and at their own pace without feeling heavy demands and pressures of having to live their Everquest lives the way others want them to.
We believe that to become a good player, you need guidance when you first begin, and that only through teamwork will you become great for the rest of your EQ lives. Most of all, the key words here are LOYALTY and HONOR.
