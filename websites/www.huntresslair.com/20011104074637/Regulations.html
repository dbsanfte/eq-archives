Huntress Lair
Code of Conduct for Guild Members

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you are admitted into the guild, we are assuming you have read these and will abide by them. If you see any members in violation of conduct it is YOUR responsibility to notify a GL or Officer.


1. RESPECT AND MATURITY

Be a good representative of Huntress Lair to fellow members as well as people outside of our guild. It is very important that we all treat each other with respect and do our best to keep everyone happy at all times. Most importantly.....You must have fun . This is a game, not work or school.  All members must have wonderful, playful, yet mature personalities and enjoy roleplaying. A sense of humor is preferred.


2. MEMBER ASSISTANCE

It is all of our jobs to create the environment that makes us want to help people in the guild. Help out when you can, however, you will never be forced to. We also do not want anyone to feel bad if they cannot give a guildmember what they need or want. We've been there before and in the end it all works out somehow. Don't take advantage of people. If you have things to give away, consider the guild first.

3. HUNTRESS LAIR BANKER

A guild member can sell anything to the guild for a reasonable going rate price. Pricing can be determined by going to www.eqprices.com. Any donations that you wish to make to the guild should be given to our guild banker, HuntressMule. Any items donated to the guild banker will be sold to members highly discounted. While they will not be given away, this is due to the future needs of the guild as it progresses into raids. We will soon have a list of items available for purchase through HuntressMule soon.


4. SECONDARY CHARACTERS

Consideration will be given to those members who wish to have a second character join the guild, under the provision that their main character has reached Level 45. This provision has been made, in order to encourage main character growth. This provision does not apply to Officers in the guild for a number of reasons:

1. Officers have a huge responsibility in the success of Huntress Lair.

2. Officers need to take whatever necessary steps they need to in order to make themselves readily accessible to members of Huntress Lair.

3. Officer assistance will be much more flexible to a wider member population if they are able to maintain a variety of skill sets.


5. CAMPING

If another Guild or Guild member (regardless of level) is camping an area, please move on to another area without a fight. Norrath is a vast land with plenty around for all. Somtimes! *Snicker*

6. NO FIGHTING

This never solves anything and will be put out immediately. You can resolve conflict by talking to that person in /tell or talking to an officer. If that doesn't solve anything, feel free to come to the guild leader. If there are any outbursts in guild chat, you WILL receive a tell from a GL or Officer.


7. NO BEGGING

Please do not demand or ask that guild members powerlevel, port, res, give you equipment, camp spawns, give you money, or use their mana/tradeskills. While we are all here to support each other and we do our best to assist with the needs of our members. Help create the environment that makes people want to do this for you and don't expect it. Make friends. Do NOT ask repeatedly in guild chat for the service. In other words NO BEGGING in or out of the guild.


If the above conduct is not adhered to, you can be let go by any GL or Officer at any time. Please refer to the Guild Charter.

We can't rid the land of evil if our group has evil among us.
