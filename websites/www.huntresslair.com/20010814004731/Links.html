AllaKhazam's Magical Realm

One of the top EverQuest information websites available. They have huge quest listings, class guides, links, maps, and much more. Well worth the visit.

Caster's Realm

Caster's Realm is a good website for the magic casting class. They have news, spells, and much more.

EQ Atlas

EQ Atlas is collection of Maps and Guides for all areas of Everquest. A must have! For those of you who haven't gone to EQ Atlas to print out every map that exists...I definitely recommend that you do so. Get yourself three ring binder and start printing. I cannot tell you how often it saved my life. Thanks Syntar!

EQ HeadQuarters

EverQuest Headquarters - An EverQuest news resource which offers EQ information, forums, data, EQ fan site hosting, and special sections such as their mailbag.

Maximum EverQuest

Great information on everything regarding EverQuest. Especially good item lists, sortable by type/location, class, race and damage output/AC.
