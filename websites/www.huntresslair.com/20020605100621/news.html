In order to remain active in Huntress Lair, it is strongly suggested that everyone register their character on both Guildmagic and the Message Board. Some of you may have already registered on Guild Magic and not the message board or vice versa. The message board is key to receiving up to date information regarding the Guild. Council mailings regarding upcoming events and important information will be managed using the message board. Guild magic is also key in maintaining up to date information on guild members in order for us to plan raids and market ourselves as a strong guild.

It has been noticed that many of you were having trouble logging into the message board, so we have provided details on how you can. Please click here to reach the Huntress Lair home page:

http://www.huntresslair.com

In order to access the Message Board, click Message Board from the navigator menu. You will see the Message Board appear in a separate window (with annoying ads that will be going away any day now). Scroll all the way down to the bottom of the page (past all the ads) and you will see the "Register an account" link. We have also include this link below.

http://pub59.ezboard.com/BBSMessageBoard.showRegistrationChoice?boardName=huntresslair

Be sure to create a global account, which will give you access to hop around to the many message board that can be useful to playing EQ. This is completely free and only takes a minute. Once you have completed the registration process you are then ready to communicate with the guild by reading and sharing posts. The board can be reached from the home page at any time or simply by clicking:

http://pub59.ezboard.com/bhuntresslair

In order to register your character with Guild Magic, from the Huntress Lair Home page click Guild Members from the Navigator menu. Proceed by clicking "click here to create user". This link has also been included:

http://www.guildmagic.com/create_user.cfm

Once you have created your user you will be able to log into that user and add valuable information your user 'profile'. An important thing to do immedicately is to add the Huntress Lair guild to your Guild Membership. This will notify one of the five administrators of the guild account, who will accept your submission. Once your submission has been accepted your information will appear under Huntress Lair Guild Members. :)

Your user information can be updated at any time. It is a very easy way to manage your information and level, simply by clicking the DING button.

Thank you for your time in reading this email and helping keep Huntress Lair up to date!
