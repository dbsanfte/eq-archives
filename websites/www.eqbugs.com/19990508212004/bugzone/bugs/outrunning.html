    • Outrunning Nasties

    Since EverQuest resolves movement in full vector quantities, the effect and usefulness of this is if you strafe while running, you run faster, and at a bit of a angle, getting you ahead of the chasing party.

    Another method of losing monsters is the tree'ing tactic. You simply run a tight semicircle around a tree trunk as you are fleeing, and if you are lucky, and leading them by a decent amount, they will run up the tree allowing you to flee.
