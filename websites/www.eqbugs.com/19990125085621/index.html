    • January 16th, 1999 - Ick. Don't you hate the flu? If you have a extra Phase 4 account and want to score a point with the big guy in the sky, you can donate it to the EverQuest Center which will be a new EverQuest news site done by MGOnet. It looks like they are keeping a database of sorts for people that didn't make it into Phase 4.

    Also, like every Saturday, the EverQuest Express has a new issue out. Go check it out.

    I still need a couple more writers to work on a EverQuest strategy guide. If you want in, or know someone that does, e-mail me.

    January 15th, 1999 - Breaking News! It looks like there is a new class in the game for Phase 4. It's called the "Lamer" class. Geez people, the game isn't even ready yet but if you take a peek at the various EverQuest message boards, people are already whining and bitching because they didn't make it into Phase 4.

    First of all people, it's a beta test. If you think that your life depends on you playing the game 1 month ahead of everyone else, so be it. But that's not an excuse to be bitching and moaning on the various boards about not being able to play cause you live outside the U.S. or to be starting 30 message long threads just cause you don't have a credit card, or you don't think the signup site is secure.

    One word for everyone : Chill.

    Later,
    Baal

    January 8th, 1999 - Whoa! We finally get a date for the release of the game, it's March 16th, 1999. Phase 4 of beta testing will start sometime during the first two weeks of February giving those testers a good month of playing.

    I've been getting a few e-mail's from people that don't like this site or what it is for, I would hope that they would read this page so that they can start to understand this site better.

    Later,
    Baal
