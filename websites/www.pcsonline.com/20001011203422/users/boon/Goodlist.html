The Rathe Druid Council's "Good List"

These people have consistently proven themselves to have consideration for others on The Rathe Server. The Druid Council asks that all Druids, and any others with a concern for justice to help these folks in any way. Let them know you have seen their names here and you look forward to helping them. More than likely, they are on a mission of mercy anyway.

Names are not casually added here. There must be multiple substantiated reports. That means that once we get several reports, our agents will observe the person. Are we the Moral Majority? No, but we can make sure people are rewarded for how well they treat others since the gods can't/won't. 

Want to tell us about a good person someone? 
Send an email here and it will be documented. Please state your character's name, the other character's name, other characters they play if you know them, and give specific detail of the good deed. Screen shots and log files are appreciated.
