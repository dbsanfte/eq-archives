My Resume
Hello Net Warriors and welcome to my home page. I'm not sure how you got here, but since you're here, let's get to know each other, me first. Born in Dalton, GA, Feb 1st, 1964 where I was raised largely on a farm until middle school. Much of my childhood was spent in the mountains and forests. When we moved to Bradenton, FL it took some time for me to adjust to the beach and ocean. Since then I've been a lifeguard, swimming, sailing and windsurfing instructor, recreation therapist and school teacher.

This was the martial arts school I owned for many, many years where I developed and instructed a program based on Tae Kwon Do, Hapkido and Kum Do (a Korean version of sword fighting). I still train a few days a week, but it was too difficult competing, business wise with all the belt factories and martial arts schools that were really day cares, so I decided to do something that would allow me to build for a family and still live the Warrior Ideal. I am giving some consideration to starting a private martial arts school only for those dedicated to real martial arts education.

I just started a new, great job as the Sr. Customer Service Engineer for Teltronics.  Prior to that I was Manager for the Data Network Services Department of Sarasota Memorial Hospital.  The 2 years prior to that, I worked as a Network Client Engineer for RISCORP (Now owned by The Zenith), a workers comp and health care insurance company.  Before that I worked as a computer technician for IBM / TSSC. I sepcialize in Windows NT (4.0 & 3.51), Windows 95/98, SQL, all Microsoft products, PC hardware and I am a Cerified ATM LAN Engineer. I am currently learning UNIX as well as increasing my knowlege with SQL.
