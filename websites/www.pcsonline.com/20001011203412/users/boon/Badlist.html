The Rathe Druid Council's "Beware List"

These people have consistently alienated themselves from their fellow hunters on The Rathe Server. How do they get here? Some think it's from Kill Stealing, that may be part of it, but mostly folks end up here because they seem to have either missed or forgotten the lessons from their parents about how to treat others. I think the anonymity of the Internet make the more insecure people strike back against all the bullying they endured as children, even if they are becoming bullies themselves. I cannot stand idly by and watch these jerks victimize others within this society. This is the stand I make, with the power of the Druid Council and all supporting guilds. 

Are you on here? Don't want to be? Easy enough, quit treating others as if they are NPCs! Your actions create REAL feelings of victimization and that makes this virtual world something real, ugly, but real. Instead, lets make it real by creating feelings of friendship and caring and kindness. 

I have seen children misbehave and apparently feel no shame for their actions, they only felt bad that they were caught. If you hurt someone else, you should feel ashamed of your actions, whether you are online or not. If you don't feel ashamed, please call your parents and explain to them that they failed and then seek therapy.

The Druid Council asks that all Druids, and any others with a concern for justice to do your best to not assist these folks in any way, this includes grouping and trading. Let them know you have seen their names here and you will not help them. Names are not casually added here. There must be multiple substantiated reports. That means that once we get several reports, our agents will observe the person. Are we the Moral Majority? No, but we can make sure people consider the way they victimize others since the gods can't/won't. If a substantial number of  reports come in for a person for the "Good List," a vote will be brought before the Council to remove a person's name from here.

Want to report someone? 
Send an email here and it will be documented. Please state your character's name, the offending character's name, other characters they play if you know them, and give specific detail of the incident. Screen shots and log files are appreciated.
