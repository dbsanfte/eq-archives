The Keepers Guild Gathering!
These seem to be an every Friday Night thing now.
Thanks everyone for the hard work and great fun!

 
 
This past week:

Fun for everyone. I took everyone's' name and level, set up a T.A.G. (The Assassination Game) List and assigned everyone someone to hunt down, duel and hopefully kill (or make them yield). Only the High level person had no one to hunt, and only the low level person had no one hunting them. Here I am setting up the list.
As with all T.A.G. events, it boiled down to two people, usually both pretty tired from the process to get here. This one was no different. The Duels were done at various locations and I got no pictures. All fought bravely and seemed to enjoy this very different event.

The final one ended up being between Daveian and Tashim. Tashim waited on him in the Arena, this might give him an upper hand. 
There was a lot of mesmerizing and some blasting whenever Daveian could break the Mez. Although the fight seemed slow, with a lot of sitting while Tashim medded and Daveian was mezzed, it was an edge of your seat fight.
Tashim chose a strategy of mezzing and trying to blast when he could, instead of just power nuking him. Being higher level, that might have been a better strategy, but Daveian's patience was indomitable.
A few of us got to the Arena to watch the fight, it was very exciting.
In the end, it turned into a blast party on both ends, each blasting and praying they got the other first. Both got very low in hit points and it looked as if they both might go down, but Daveian got the final blast that brought the brave Tashim down.

It was an amazing battle and both do their class, race and this guild proud. 
Afterwards they discussed their strategies and commended each other on a great fight, they even discussed what they had learned from each other.

In the end they headed off to Paw to hunt together, with a hearty congratulations from all involved.
   
Several weeks ago:

We hunted Sand Giants, some seriously pissed off goblins from the lake and even killed several specters at one time, Thanks to Tashim mezzing them. Now that I think about it, I believe we were trained there, but with a good enchanter, no one knew..
Great exp. and fun for everyone.
