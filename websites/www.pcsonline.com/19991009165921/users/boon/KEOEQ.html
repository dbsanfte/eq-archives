Tis a glorious day as the world of Norrath is being built. Ye may find us maintaining the balance of the world on the Rathe Server. The members of The Keepers of the Everlasting Oak are of many different races and professions. We believe your  actions dictate your heart, not your race. Although many Trolls, Ogres and Dark Elves are evil, there are many that join us in our war  against the Gnolls and Orcs.

    I am Brennos the Druid, human by birth, Guardian of the Goddess by choice. Although we spend much of our time exploring and hunting, I love to fish and just spend time with my friends. We spend a lot of time socializing and having fun and hope that others will join us and call us friend from all across the globe.
