The Wedding!!

 
 
The Wedding of Wonarn and Sandralay was the nicest wedding I've seen in Norrath. Mammoth was there which is always a good sign.
I was honored to be asked to preside over the handfasting. We all said a prayer together to the gods, and the forces of nature. Then the couple jumped a fire together, walked past all their friends and family, and stood before me for the ceremony.
As the couple got ready for the first married kiss, everyone got ready to make some fireworks
The Fireworks begin.
This looked like the sentinal spell off of a Observer Staff, even the trees were rejoicing and involving themselves in the fireworks.
And there was silence, and awe. The forest blessed them too.
Everyone exchanged gifts with the happy couple, and they exchanged them with each other.
What then? Well, maybe we drank a bit. *wink*
