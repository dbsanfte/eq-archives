Want To Be a Member of KEO??

It's easy, first we ask that thou check Our Beliefs Page and see if ye think KEO is the right guild for ye.

Once thou has't decided that we are the right guild for ye, grab our carrier pigeon (ICQ) numbers from our Member's Page and see which of us is available, then send us a message.  We will meet with ye and work on your entry.  If you don't use carrier pigeons (ICQ) or we aren't available, then send Brennos a message via e-mail and make an appointment to meet and work on yer entry.  It's that easy!

What are the benefits?

  • Several of us are Grand Masters and can help ye to get to that point as well, through training, hunting and experience.
  • Have problems with thieves or murderers?  We have found that they are less of a problem if ye spend time with others, so come hunt or explore with us. We also can teach thee the best tactics to discourage them.
  • Maybe ye want to hunt in some of the more dangerous places, but know that no matter how good ye are, yer nay a match for the beasts that roam there.  We hunt in those places often and find them invigorating, lucrative and surprisingly clear of thieves and looters. Going as a team makes this possible, come join the team!
