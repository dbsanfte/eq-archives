This is not a Guild, but instead a Gathering of all Druids to make decisions on how best we can seek balance for the animals, trees, rocks, streams and people of Norrath.  We  ask that ALL responsible Druids on the Rathe please attend our meetings and follow our guidelines, thus the people of Norrath will know what to expect from all Druids.

Thursdays are "Duty Free Thursdays!" This means all Druids are asked to charge nothing for their services and to position yourselves throughout different zones and announce your services. Here is an Example: Stand at Oasis Dock and shout that you are casting SoW free of charge at the Dock for the next 20 minutes for all polite adventurers, Sponsored by the Druid Council. Another example is to place rusty, and bronze weapons you've collected on low level beasties in Newbie areas and announce that the Druid Council has used it's power to increase the loot on many newbie creatures. Maybe offer to make patchwork armor at no charge for anyone bringing ruined pelts to you at the second tower in West Karanas, I will have a hundred or so patterns for this one, of course shouting that it is sponsored by the Druid Council. You may want to have the URL ready for those who ask what the Council is, as you won't have much time to explain it.
