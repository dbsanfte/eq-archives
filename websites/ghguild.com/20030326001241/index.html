Let the Legacy Begin
Posted by:Hammeron on:2/28/2003 at:5:42:10 PM PST
For the last three years, Everquest has been about great achievements and turning points for Grey Hawke. From our first killings of Nagafen and Vox, to Fear, Hate and Sky, then Trakanon and Veeshan's Peak and Phara Dar. The Dain and Yelinak, Sleeper's Tomb and Ventani, then Temple of Veeshan and Aaryonar, Vyemm, Vulak and the Avatar of War. On to Luclin and Arch-Lich, High Priest, Cursed, Lord Seru, the Emporer and finally our long awaited arrival in Vex Thal. Last night was another amazing night and a big turning point for us.

Last night we went into Vex Thal with a purpose and a plan. We had yet to make real progress past DXXT and we wanted to change that. It truly was an outstanding display, and after 6 long hours and 3 dead mobs, and surprisingly no wipe outs, the goal was in sight. Aten Ha Ra was before us and it was the time of judgement. Needless to say, this ugly bitch was found unworthy and at 3am she died in a near-perfect first time execution. It had been a long, exciting, and stressful night, filled with the monotony that is moving around in VT and the rampant suicide pulling that becomes almost second nature. Our monk team had died about 50 times collectively, but I have to say, it was well worth it, it was another great turning point, and we can close the book, in a sense, on another expansion:
