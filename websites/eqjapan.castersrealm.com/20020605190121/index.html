Wednesday June 5, 2002

GRAPHICAL DISPLAY PROBLEMS

If you are having problems with your inventory display or other graphical related issues after the patch the following advice is being given in game:
    Players that are experiencing inventory icon issues or garbled interface graphics should turn off the texture caching option from the front end options screen and then delete all .txc files from their EverQuest folder.
If you are still having issues you can always contact the live chat technical support located here between 9am and 9pm PDT.

SMALL NOTE ON FOCUS ITEMS

With the new focus items on Test players should be aware of the following:
  • Focus Item I effects (e.g. Mana Conservation I) only work on spells up to Level 20
  • Focus Item II effects (e.g. Mana Conservation II) only work on spells up to Level 44
  • Focus Item III effects (e.g. Mana Conservation III) work on all spells up to Level 60
In the next patch it appears only Level I and II focus items will be patched in. Level III items will most likely be brought in during subsequent patches.

PETS WONT TOUCH THE MES'D

Alan corrected a statement made earlier today regarding the new pet AI:
    I made a mistake when I said:

    Pets will still attack who you tell them to. They just won’t run out and do it on their own.

    With the new changes, pets will not attack a mezzed NPC, no matter how much you beg, plead or cajole. You’ll have to break the mez by some other means first.

    I’m sorry about the mistake.

    - Alan
