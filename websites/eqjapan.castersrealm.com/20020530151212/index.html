Wednesday May 29, 2002

FREE ACCOUNT KEYS

Be sure to check your e-mail. Sony Online is offering an extra account key to every current subscriber of EverQuest who signed up for their Newsletter!

The key will give you a second account with access to Kunark and Velious, but will also require you to pay an extra monthly subscription after the first 30 days.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Baelish, 9:37 PM EST
 

PATCH FOR JUNE 5

This from the Scheduled Downtime page:
    On Wednesday, June 5th, at 3AM PDT, (11AM GMT) all servers will be brought down for a scheduled patch. The estimated downtime is 4 hours.

    - The EverQuest Operations Team

Submitted by Baelish, 6:13 PM EST
 

SOE AND UBISOFT EXPAND

Sony and Ubisoft have expanded their existing relationship for MMOG's to provide new servers and better localization for european players.

The deal, while specifically referring to EverQuest, could also mean better services for other SOE titles in development such as EverQuest Online Adventures for the PS2 and Star Wars Galaxies.

Click here for details.
