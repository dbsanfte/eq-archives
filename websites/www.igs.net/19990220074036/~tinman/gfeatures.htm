Features

Engine

Uses a real-time true 3D engine specifically designed to take advantage of 3D accelerator cards such as the 3DFX(tm).

Advanced lighting engine that supports color lights and accurate shadows for breathtaking environments that look pre-rendered.

Procedural models. This allows all creatures in the game to be unique. For example, in a horde of Goblins, each individual creature will look and act differently.

A unique magic system where the combination of different elements allows for scores of spells.

Vastly different gameplay depending on character choice.

Collect many different types of items including armor, weapons, range weapons, and magic. Items will change the look of your character.

Advanced combat system with accurate collisions and physics.

Single and multiplayer.
