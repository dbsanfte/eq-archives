The Game

Gothica is a world forged by ancient magic. It is a time of beautiful elves, loathsome goblins, treacherous humans, and powerful magic. A time where the ancient struggle between the essence of good and evil prevails. Nobility comes in the form of questing for an allegiance -- either good or evil.

Elf folk, born from long dead ancient powers, struggle alongside humans for survival in an increasingly hostile world. The forces of evil, sensing a time to strike, infest the world like bugs in a rotting tree.

From a humble beginning, you must build your allegiance and standing in a world of mistrust. Journey from the dark, metropolitan cities of man, to innocent, magical treetop villages of elves in order to uncover the secrets of this forlorn world.

Gameplay

Gothica features a realtime 3D third person environment with unlimited camera movment. The world is highly interactive and sensitive with hundreds of tasks that can be accomplished. These range from the simplicity of a swinging door, to the complexity of setting fire to a wooden barrier and watching it burn to ashes.

The world of Gothica is filled with danger and excitement. Reward awaits those well trained, unspeakable horrors await those ill prepared. Are you brave enough?
