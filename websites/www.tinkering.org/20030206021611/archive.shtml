August 30, 2001 - Gemcutter news

Thank you very much, everyone who has cut and pasted the story from Everlore about gemcutters. It is appreciated. Here is the note, in case you missed it.

Since the discovery of the Gem Cutter gnomes have wondered its uses.

The gemcutter can be used in place of the Darfumpel part of the shadow night quest containing a faceted Hyacinth as a quest componet.

Take one gemcutter and one uncut hyacinth and put into a jewelry bag. Have a high-level jeweler combine these items and you receive the faceted Hyacinth.

Darfumple (npc) charges 1000pp to facet the stone so using this, shadow nights could probably get it done cheaper from pc sources. This is the only quest I know of that it works on but it is surely compatible with other stones...

Lots of you sent that to me, so I figured I would put it here, even though the news originated on our message board.

In related news, Stonebrunt is now open. My iksar talked to every cat NPC he could find, and no mention of gems of any sort was made. If anyone has any news that gemcutters have a use there, please email it to fizzwizz@tinkering.org. Thanks!

August 7, 2001 - A Personal Victory

It's almost amazing how good a single click can make you feel, no matter what had happened before. Last night, I attempted two vanishing devices, and failed both. Ah well, was kind of expecting it. I pulled out the holgresh furs I had just bought for 50pp each, and stuck them in the toolbox with all the other stuff. Click one, failed and got my fine plate arms back. Do it again, and........

Yay! Rebreathers are now trivial, and I have something to show for all of my hard work and spent cash... My first piece of Watchman armor! I'm very happy about this! Can you tell?

In related news, and MUCH more impressive, Igbert, a regular poster on the boards, just got skill 200!!! Congratulate him!
