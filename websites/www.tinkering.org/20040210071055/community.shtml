WARNING!!

This is a WISHLIST of tinkering ideas! This list doesn't actually WORK :) Tinkering is expensive enough without people going out and trying to make these things. They are NOT in the game. Please save your coppers for real stuff :)

The Tinkering community is an inventive bunch. Many are constantly at their workbench, inventing new gadgets. Every piece of platinum they make goes to buying just one more gear or metal shaft for the latest contraption. Many non-Tinkers don't understand this obsession, and scoff. But the Tinker doesn't hear the remarks, being so absorbed in the work at hand...

This page is dedicated to those Tinkers that have put in the effort to invent new gadgets. A lot of thought has gone into ensuring that the items remain balanced within Norrath. The reason: a lot of us would like to see these items in Norrath one day.

Most of the information presented here has come from the replies to Post 16681. Now that that campaign is complete, please post your invention to the Tinkering Forums. Currently, you may reach the forums from here: Community Forums. From time to time, I will post these new inventions to the official Wishlist forum with the hope that some of them will make it into Norrath.
