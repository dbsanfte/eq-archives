EverQuest
Index > Rebreather > Assembly

Once you buy all the things shown here and put them in the toolbox, take a moment to put your int/wis gear on, so it's back up where it should be. You want that extra chance for a skillraise, don't you?

Making the Aqualung:
Metal Rod 8gp Bom Knotwood, Rylin Coil, Mechanical Merchant
Marchap Biddinight, Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Metal Twine 1pp Bom Knotwood, Adinel Jailbar, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Gnomish Bolts 3pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Silk Lined Steel Helm 16pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Fine Coral Mesh 189pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Fresh Fish 3sp Common drop off fish
Sold at merchants near fishing areas
Produces: Aqualung, Slot: Head, Charges: 50, AC: 1
Effect: Breath of the Dead, Wt: 10.0, Size: Giant, Class: ALL, Race: ALL

When you click combine, you should either see "You fail to combine these items to make something new" and everything will poof, or You'll successfuly combine them, and maybe even gain a skill point! Put the aqualung in your second toolbox or other giant bag. You'll get it out later.

You can sell these for five or six hundred, and accept them for 400 off the cost of a rebreather if you want. It doesn't matter how many charges the aqualung has when it's combined later. Some people would rather just use the aqualung, and that's fine... as long as they know that the breath of the dead can be dispelled, which can lead to drowning in places like Kedge Keep. The effect on the rebreather is permanant, and can never be removed, as long as you're wearing it.

The aqualung goes trivial at 148. If you are not quite there yet and have some cash, you should make a whole bunch of aqualungs and store them until they are trivial. At that point, you can go back and turn them into rebreathers, and have a better chance of success, and a better chance of gaining skill.

Nevermind-- I know you're going to ignore that advice, so let's move along to making firewater.

Making Firewater:
Water Flask 1sp Any standard food vendor
Gnomish Spirits 6sp Bar in Ak'Anon and East Freeport
Also can be brewed
Produces: Firewater

Chances are very good that this was successful. If not, curse at the Random Number Generator for a minute, and try again. Next stop, putting it all together!

You're almost there! Just one combine left, and then you have a real money maker... or you have an empty cursor and 900pp gone. Put your charisma gear back on before buying the components you need here. Prices range from 17pp to 156pp on these last 3 things. Put the Platemail helm, the firewater, and the aqualung in your toolbox, and buy the other three items from Bom. Put your int/wis gear on again, pray to Bristlebane, and click combine.

Making a Rebreather:
Aqualung 210pp Tinkered
Firewater N/A Tinkered
Medium Platemail Helm 400pp Platemail Vendor
Metal Fastening 16pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Sharkskin Tubing 142pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Lime Coated Meshing 157pp Bom Knotwood, Marchap Biddinight
Trace the Tinker, Blaylok Geerlok
Produces: Rebreather, Slot: Head, AC: 1
Effect: Enduring Breath, WT: 10.0, Size: Giant, Class: ALL, Race: ALL

Congradulations, you may already be a winner! If you made the rebreather, put it in another bag, and go for a second one. Don't forget to swap out your charisma and int/wis gear when you need to!
