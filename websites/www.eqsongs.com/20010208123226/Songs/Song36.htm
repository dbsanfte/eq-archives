VILIA's VERSES of CELERITY

Level: 36

Effect: Increase agility and haste

Skill: Singing

Target: Party

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Other attack speed buffing songs, and enchanter/shaman attack speed buffs. 

Taunt: Low

Quantified: Agl: Integer (lvl/2) + 5
Attack speed: +20.23
Delay change: -16.83

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: Increases the parties attack speed and agility, both are very good things.

Strategies:

Stacks nicely with Chant of Battle, to increase the parties DEX, STR, AGI, and attack speed.
[Noruas] The attack speed component will stack with item based attack speed boasts (FBSS, Exec Axe, etc..)

Cautions:

The attack speed component does not stack with Enchanter/Shamen attack speed spell lines, but you will still get the AGL bonus.
