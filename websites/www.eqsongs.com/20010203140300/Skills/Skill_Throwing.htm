RANGED COMBAT SKILL - Throwing

Available at Level: 1

Effect: Increases chance to hit with thrown weapons

Maximum skill: 200?

Quantified: (lvl+1)*5?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: A very neglected skill, also an expensive one (at low levels) to work on.

Strategies:

Thrown weapons have a limited range, and are mostly useful for pulling from a crowd after lulling the NPCs you do not want to pull.  I prefer this method as opposed to running in and clubbing the thing or using Bellow, as both those methods take more time and expose you to additional risk of pulling unwanted mobs.
Stackable throwing weapons will auto-feed into your hand form inventory or a container of any sort.  All you need is one in the range slot and one in the ammo slot for auto-feed to work.

Cautions:

Stackable throwing weapons have a very short range, non-stackable throwing weapons tend to have a much greater range.
[Curanto] Many fine weapons with stat boosts (e.g. Dragoon Dirk, Thex Mallet) can be equipped in the throwing slot, allowing the wearer to get the stat benefit even when not wielding the weapon.  HOWEVER, the weapons have a range rating, meaning you can accidentally throw them if you're not careful. Throw it, and it's gone forever.
Some players have reported that when charmed they have lost stat boasting items from their throwing slots do to being ordered to throw them.
