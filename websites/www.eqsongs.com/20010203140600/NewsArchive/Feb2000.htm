2/23 8:00am - I did some testing last night with Niv's Melody of Preservation and found some interesting things:
-The Gypsy Lute adds +5% to songs just like the Lute of the Gypsy Princess.
-Holding an instrument of the same type you are playing an instrument will nullify the instrument bonus to the song you are using (ie Niv's with a regular Lute adds +20 Str, if you hold a Mandolin in your mouse pointer Niv's only adds +10 Str).
-Some Bards have reported that having a lesser quality instrument in an open inventory slot will degrade song performance (ie Having a Mistmoore Battle Drum equipped and having a regular Hand drum in an open inventory slot).  I could not reproduce this effect.
-Holding an instrument of a different type while you are playing a song will not nullify the instrument bonus to the song you are using (ie Niv's with a regular Lute adds +20 Str, if you hold a Horn in your mouse pointer Niv's will still add +20 Str).

From: FLEX DOLPHYNN

Subject: [eqbards] SHARKSKIN DRUMS
Date: Fri, 11 Feb 2000 05:38:55 -0800 (PST)

From: FLEX DOLPHYNN

Got my SharkSkin Drums tonight.  Many thanks to the Twelve Year Guild on
Cazic-Thule. 

The SharkSkin Drums are found on a named Mermaid called Coralyn Kelpma. She
spawns past Estrella, just to the right of the 5 StingTooths in the Ceiling
and right outside of the Seahorse Caves. A normal Gloomwater is her
placeholder. We came upon her by accident as she spawned 20 min after we
killed her placeholder. I had never heard of her before. Now I wonder what
her rare loot is. Grin. She looks like she is equipped with a lute when she
has these.

I have tested the Drums. They are in fact better than the Mistmoore Drums. I
do not have Selos drums to test against.

Here are the test results from a 47 bard singing Elemental Rhythms. The
number represents the point increase for the cold/fire/magic resist.

+28 = no instrument just singing
+50 = normal hand drum
+58 = Mistmoore Battle Drums
+61 = SharkSkin Drums

Judging from this Mistmoore drums give a 16% increase over normal drums. And
SharkSkin Drums give a 22% increase over normal drums. Testing with a couple
other songs also shows a slight increase when using the SharkSkin vs. using
the Mistmoore.

In Addition Selos speed seems slightly enhanced (actually easier to see a
slight slow down when going from Sharkskin down to Mistmoore). I feel very
happy running with these. A visually subjective test with Encumbrance seems
to indicate they are at least as good as Mist Drums in dealing with
Encumbrance and Selos running.

All in All great Drums and ultra-rare. I shall keep these over the Selo's
Drums, even though Selos is likely better.

The Icon looks like a lute. When equipped it looks like a lute as well. The
nice thing about this is that when running with selos I actually strum a
lute instead of a flute. Unfortunately I may mistake the icon for the Lute
of the Gyspy Princess sometimes when switching instruments as they appear
the same. 

I Don't really read this onelist.  Just posting for a heads up and to place
in the Archives. 

Good luck getting yours. I love Kedge!

RADIA DELFIN

2/4 7:00am - GZ published the Bard edition of The Arch-Magi Answers.

 

2/3 12:15pm - Patch Day (Bard items bolded)
Patch process complete.
******************************************************************************
February 3rd, 8:00am
--------------------

The following changes have been implemented on the live servers:

*Spells*

- Lay On Hands: Enhanced to heal more hitpoints per level.
- Enveloping Roots: Casting time reduced to match Immobilize
- Skin Like Nature: Small Damage Shield added. This will stack with other shields.
- Insidious Fever: Casting time reduced.
- Insidious Malady: Casting time Reduced.
- Burnout series: Negative HP component removed to bring it in line with the Necromancer's Intensify/Augment death series.

*Songs*

- Syvelian's Anti-magic Aura: Potency increased.
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding: Now also grants See Invisible. Party members can now see each other while under the effect of this song.

Additionally, the following songs have been given a slightly larger radius:

- Selo's Accelerando
- Agilmente's Aria of Eagles
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding
- Tarew's Aquatic Ayre

*PvP-Teams Change*

Customers on the PvP-Teams servers have been plagued by a tactic known as "Bind Rushing". Casters would bind themselves in a hostile area or dungeon, and attack everyone of an opposing race in the area. Sooner or later the caster would die. They would then respawn, memorize a few spells, then jump back in to attack their wounded opponents.

As a first step in combating this practice and improving life for those on the PvP-Servers, casters will now respawn with zero mana after being killed by another player in a duel or open combat.

This code is new for those on Tallon and Vallon Zek. It has been in place for some time on Rallos Zek and works very well there.

*The Plane of Sky*

All spells that grant levitation will no longer function in the Plane of Sky. This affects spells cast by Wizards, Enchanters, Shamans, Druids, Necromancers, and Bards. The Plane of Sky is designed to be completed in certain fashion, and the existence of these spells allowed the islands to be completed out-of-order.

A teleporter will be placed on the first island in the next patch to allow people access to the room below.

- The EverQuest Team
