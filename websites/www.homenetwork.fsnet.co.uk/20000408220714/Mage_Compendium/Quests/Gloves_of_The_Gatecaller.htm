Gloves of The Gatecaller

 

 

This item is obtainable by completing a quest in the Erudian Mage Guild House.

 

What you do is hail Vasile Jahnir and Say "The power of the gatecallers is Summoning".

 

If you have good faction with the Gatecallers he will tell you to bring him 2 summoned food and 2 summoned Daggers.

 

Give these items to him making sure the food is not stacked when you give it to him.

 

Once you do this, you will receive the Gloves of The Gatecaller.

 

Note : If your faction is not high enough, kill Eliah Brooks (A Necromancer near the docks in Toxxulia Forest, Loc +140, +1200).
