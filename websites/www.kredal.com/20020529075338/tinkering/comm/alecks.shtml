   Alecks Stevansun of Innoruuk

Tinkering Journal: Alecks Stevansun of Innoruuk
Gnomish Gun
First I place the METAL ROD and attach it to the METAL SHAFT with the GNOMISH BOLTS. I then attach the GEARS to the product using the METAL TWINE, to ensure maximum potential I GREASE the inside. Tah-dah...your very own 20dmg 50dly Gun, with range 150 I feel it is a pretty nice weapon.

Ingredients:
1 Metal Rod
1 Metal Shaft
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
! Set of Gears
1 Metal Twine
1 Grease.

Gnomish Bullet
I first go to my local Jewelry store and buy a very cheap HEMATITE, it seems to work the best. I then douse the bullet in GREASE, this makes it run smoother, and not wear the gun out.

Ingredients:
1 Grease
1 Hematite

Gnomish Microphone
Attention Bards! By wearing this item in your melee or 2nd melee slot, you can increase the effectiveness of your songs by a decent amount (5%), find a Gnome who is willing to help, you Gnomes first need the STATIC ORB. Using the METAL ROD, and METAL FASTENING you can make the base of your project. By attaching the GNOMISH BOLTS and GREASE to your project you will have it.

Ingredients:
1 Static Orb
1 Metal Rod
1 Metal Fastening
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
1 Bottle of Grease

Goggles of the Sightless
The constant killing of our guards and entry of invisible Trolls and Ogres into the inner chambers of our city must be stopped. I have devised a new invention to stop this problem. These goggles worn on your face can be easily made. With 1 SCROLL OF SEE INVISIBLE, 2 METAL SHAFTS, 1 GNOMISH BOLTS and 2 REFLECTIVE SHARDS you will be able to see not only the invisible, but the hiding as well.

Ingredients:
1 Scroll of See Invisible (Sold in Magic Stores across the world)
2 Metal Shafts
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
2 Reflective Shards

The Hazemaker
First, you must get a medium LEATHER ARMOR. By cutting the REFLECTIVE SHARD into 4 parts you have very little left to do. Place the shards on the armor, and begin to wrap the LIME COATED MESHING around the armor. This keeps the shards locked in place.The armor is AC 8, +6 agi, and Effect: Haze (AC raising Enchanter spell). When worn, the opponent averts his eyes at the sight of your bright, shining Hazemaker.
