   Babkin of the Nameless

Tinkering Journal: Babkin of The Nameless
Stun Grenades
Use throwing skill to use will explode on target, and cause a area effect stun kinda like the low level enchanter spell, "color flux"

Ingredients:
1 Bottle
1 Firewater
1 Diseased Rat liver
should cost ~10pp

Swimming Fins
Fits on feet slot and it increases your swimming speed when worn. The item would increase swimming speed but have an AC of like 2 with a weight of 8 or something. Maybe it could break depending ou your swimming skill. So if your skill was 5 you would be thrashing around so much that you would break your swimming shoes fin thingies. But if you were a good swimmer they would have a rare chance of breaking.

Ingredients:
1 Set of Leather Boots
2 Sheets of Metal
1 Set of Fish Scales
1 Metal Fastening
cost should be ~200pp

Sprint Shoes
Will give you 5 charges of a short duration of sow (blinking icon). They are worn in the feet location and can be used to get a way to escape a battle, but since only 5 charges and would cost a medium amount of pp would not be used all the time.

Ingredients:
1 Set of Chainmail Boots
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
2 Bottles
