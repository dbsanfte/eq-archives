Tinkering Journal: Liet Spivak
Gnomish Trip Flare
This is a modified firework that can be dropped, and not picked up. It would remain on the ground until a MOB passed near, then it would explode like a gnomish firework. Once it exploded, the MOB that tripped it might agro on the user.

Game balance: You can, with a decent sound card, hear MOBs coming most of the time anyway. This would even things out for those people with crap speakers.

Gnomish Food Processor
This would cost a fair bit to construct. It would take a PLAYER MADE food to charge it, 3 player made foods for one charge, and each charge could produce one food or drink in the field. Charge limit could be 80 or 100.

Game Balance: Sort of a way of carrying more weight, but should be impossible to exploit. Because of the cost of charging, it's really just a way of rendering food more portable for those long hunting trips. Bonus : it also would add some value to the cooking skill, since it can only be charged with player-made food.

Gnomish Army Knife
This would be a magical weapon with different modes, one for each weapon type. A class that could not use a given mode (2HS, for instance) could not equip the knife in that mode, and you would have to de-equip and right click to change modes.

This would be very expensive to produce. Due to the lousy stats below, it would only be useful for players to practice a given weapon skill without having to carry around 5 different (and heavy) weapons.

Game balance: Should not hopefully be a problem - this is a worse weapon than even the newbie ones in each class. Its only use should be to practice weapon skills.

Stats:
1HB DAM 2 DLY 35
2HB DAM 4 DLY 50
Piercing DAM 1 DLY 30
1HS DAM 3 DLY 35
2HS DAM 4 DLY 50
