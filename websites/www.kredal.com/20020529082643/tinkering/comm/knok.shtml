   Knok Verst of Karana

Tinkering Journal: Knok Verst of Karana
See Invisible Goggles
I have seen a recipe from a fellow Gnome about a tinkered device to "See Invisible" people stalking our guards and city.

However, my research has taken me along a different route as to the proper ingredients for such a device. In the interest of an opening a discussion, I have forwarded my Formula.  ;)

I recommend the following:
1 Silk Steel-Lined Helm
1 Flameless Lantern
1 Metal Fastening
1 Reflective Shard
1 Twine
1 Firewater
1 Metal Shaft

Attach the Flameless Lantern to the Helm by using a Metal Fastening. Wrap the Twine about the Reflective Shard to hold it to the Flameless Lantern. Use the Metal Shaft to feed the extra amount of Firewater (attached to the your belt) to the Flameless Lantern. Proper placement of the Reflective Shard next to the Flameless Lantern combined with the extra heating from the Firewater allows Invisible objects to be seen.

Unfortunately, I have found that heating up the shard with the extra firewater causes it to crack after about 5 uses.

Gnomish Medical Kit
The Gnomish Medical Kit applies each bandage correctly and perfectly to a wound, and as such, are more effective than bandages applied normally. Each Gnomish Medical Kit has 20 bandages.

Effect-  Increase effect of Bind Wound Skill.

Ingredients:
1 Metal Rod
1 Sprocket
1 Gear
1 Grease
20 Bandages

Divining Rod
Lost in the Desert, our Expedition was running low on water. A Wood-Elf in our group had already used up the last of his Spring Crystal. I tinkered a Divining Rod up and it kept us alive till we found some gypsies and bought enough water to last us to get out of the desert.

Effect - Abundant Drink, 10 charges

Ingredients:
1 Static Orb
3 Metal Rods
Metal Twine

Snake Bite Kit and Flash Bomb
When assembling the group of adventurer's, they asked what skills I could add to the group. I said, "I am a Gnome. I will Tinker."

A couple of them laughed and I think they invited me along more for fun than what they thought I could do.

Their opinion of me changed for the better after we wandered into a nest of snakes and all members were snake bitten! I surprised them all by pulling out my Snake Bite Kit and was able to cure all those poisoned.


Snake Bite Kit-
Effect- Cure/Counteract Poison

1 Asp Sac
1 Flask of Water (To distill the poison into Anti-toxin)
1 Firewater (Also for the Anti-toxin)
1 Bottle
1 Sprocket (To meter the appropriate dosage)

The Asp Sac provides enough for 10 doses of Anti-toxin.

I have also experimented with using deadlier poison components which provide more charges (20) of a stronger cure (Counteract Poison).
