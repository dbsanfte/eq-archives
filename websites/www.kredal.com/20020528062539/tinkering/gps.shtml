  GPS: The Gnome Positioning System

Welcome to the Gnome Positioning System. This page has been set up to allow you to find a Tinker on your server. Tinkers that have been awarded a Golden Gear have been indicated. If you wish to be added to the GPS please use one of the following methods:

  1. Update Using the Online GPS Form

  2. Update by Posting on the GPS Section of the Forum Boards

  3. Email your character name, server, current tinkering skill, email address (optional) and any comments to kredal@tcainternet.com
