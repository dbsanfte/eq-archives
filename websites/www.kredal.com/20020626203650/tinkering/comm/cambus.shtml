   Cambus of Cazic Thule

Tinkering Journal: Cambus of Cazic Thule
Manna Attenuator
AC: 0. Head slot item. Will increase meditation rate like clarity, but only 33% as effective. This effect will stack. While in use, it will decrease stamina by a like amount. When stamina hits zero. The user dies. Weight: 10.0

Ingredients:
1 Expensive Helm
40 Tiny Daggers
1 Metal Disk
1 Metal Twine
other items to bring the cost to 1000pp

Firework Mortar
This is a container that would hold up to five components. Four must be regular fireworks, the fifth is a gemstone. Each gemstone corresponds to a particular aerial burst. The Aerial bursts are regular spell effects, but are executed 20 to 50' in the air. Aside from looking pretty, the mortar has no practical purpose. (Maybe agro?). To really make it exciting, the Mortar breaks like a fishing pole, causing the owner to take significant damage (50% of hps?). Weight: 8.0

Ingredients:
2 Metal Rods
2 Metal Twine
1 Grease
1 Metal Fastener
2 Gears
cost should be ~50pp

Gnomish Pickups
Increases effectiveness of bard songs by 3% when equipped in Ranged weapon slot.

Ingredients:
2 Lodestones
2 Static Orbs
1 Power Source
2 Metal Disks
1 Firewater
1 Metal Twine

Gnomish Accordion
A musical instrument usable by bards. This instrument could be used for songs that normally are enhanced by either percussion OR wind instruments. -8% on all songs. (Break's like a fishing pole)

Ingredients:
1 Piece of Ivory
1 Set of Wood Splinters
1 File
1 Metal Twine
1 Harmonica
1 Rat Liver

BioLocator
This is a container that accepts one item. Once an item from a specific creature is placed into the container (Beetle eye, lion meat, etc), and "Combine" is pressed, the user will receive a tracking type message for one creature that (roughly) corresponds to the item used. Lion meat would point you to the nearest lion. Bone chips, the nearest skeleton (or undead). The item used is destroyed.

Ingredients:
1 Static Orb
1 Firewater
1 Backpack Pattern
1 Metal Sheet
1 Chipped Bone Rod
1 Spyglass
1 Compass
1 IR Goggles :-)

Gnomish Exploding Land Mines
Drop them on the ground, and when something moves within their detection range (after a certain "arming period" has elapsed), they explode (make a loud sound), and cause a minor amount (<10pts) of damage. They will disappear after a while (typical of dropped objects). One use would be as an alarm while meditating.

Ingredients:
2 Stacked Bone Fragments
2 Firewaters
1 Pixie Dust
1 HQ Wolfpelt

Flamethrower
AoE, DOT weapon. 40pts per beat, for 3 beats. 20 charges. Can break like fishing pole, causing an AoE, DOT against player (and group) for 60pts. Ranged weapon, uses Firewater as ammo.

Ingredients:
6 Stacked Bottles
2 Drakes Breath
3 Sharkskin Tubes
1 Static Orb
1 Metal Rod

Crossbow
Weight 5.0. Useable by all classes that can use bows, EXCEPT PALADINS. 2x as powerful as a composite bow. VERY slow (ideally, used once, maybe twice per battle). Breaks like a fishing pole.

Ingredients:
1 Short Bow
1 Metal Twine
2 Metal Rods
1 Set of Wood Splinters
2 Cams

Remote Viewer
Works like Bind Sight. 10 charges.

Ingredients:
2 Refractive Crystals
2 Agates
2 Metal Rods
1 Sharkskin Tubing
1 Firewater

Multi-Bow
Makes a bow that does 2x damage. Breaks like a fishing pole. Uses 4x the number of arrows of a regular bow. Weighs 8.0

Ingredients:
4 Bows
2 Metal Rods
2 Cams

IR Goggles
Provides the user with IR vision. 3 charges, weight 0.5.

Ingredients:
1 Reflective Shard
1 Bloodstone (a Sunstone(tm?) would be more appropriate)
1 Firewater
2 Bottles

Starlight Goggles
Provides the user with UV vision. 3 charges, weight 1.0.

Ingredients:
1 Reflective Shard
1 Ruby
1 IR Goggles

Gnomish Purple Death Ray
Despite it's name, it actually causes 1hp of damage per round, and makes a real loud noise. Gives the user a headache.
Weight 20.0

Gnomish Engram Transfer Helmet
This device is a container. When a spell scroll is placed into the container, and a victim, er, subject is targeted, the spell will be tranplanted directly into the recipients mind (recipient has a chance to accept, like Rez). The spell randomly appears in the spell bar, and once used disappears. The user of the device has all spells wiped out on the spell bar. Cost should be ~1000pp

Gnomish Metabolic Exploding Canobolizer
Worn as a belt, only by gnomes. When placed on the gnome (victim), it cannot be removed. If the gnome dies, everyone (including those grouped with the gnome) receives 50pts of damage.

Gnomish Yonderblast
Provides one charge of the following: Random zone teleport, and random location in the zone. Landing in molten hot magma or at the feet of Cazic-Thule is entirely possible. Forget about corpse recovery. This is a device only a monk might love, er, like... um... tolerate... Should cost ~200pp

Gnomish Holographic Projector
Creates a non-moving temporary replica of the user. The replica is transparent. Aggressive monsters will attack. Effect lasts for 1 minute. Cost should be ~150pp

Gnomish Fanciful Balloon Fabricator
Produces an enormous, floating transparent balloon the floats away. The balloon could be a Gnomish Head, a Gnomish Rat, or a Clockwork Spider.

Gnomish Coin Bank
When placed in ammo slot, automatically allows the user to use his own money to distribute the actual looted value of a creature evenly. For example, in a party of five, if 3pp coins are looted by the user of this device, each player would get 6gp. (The user would get the platinum). Cost should be ~40pp

Gnomish Icecream Churn
Container that allows bakers to make icecream. Requires Ice. Cost should be 10pp.

Gnomish Collapsable Crutch
Placed in an arm slot, will allow a severely wounded individual to move slightly faster than they would without it (can never move faster then they normally would). Cost 30pp

Gnomish Emergency Air Supply
One charge. When right clicked, moves the "Air Remaining" bar to full. Cost 8pp

Gnomish 8-Ball
When right clicked, produces curious random messages. "Future hazy, ask again later". "The signs say, Yes", etc. Cost should be 12pp.

Gnomish Manna Indicator
10 charges. When pointed at the individual PC (you are grouped with), this device will report the amount of manna (as a percent) the individual has. Cost ~180pp

Formal Gnomish Gardening Hat
This is actually a Tailored item, but its very gnomish... And I couldn't think of anything just now. AC 2, head slot. It is a pointy cone shaped, red felt cap. Weight 0.1. Chr +2. Gnome only.

Portable Gnomish Faux Campfire
When dropped, this item will appear like a camp fire. It does nothing but mark a spot, and illuminate an area. Good for marking a spot. It will eventually disappear, like most dropped items. However, if you recover it every once in a while, and drop it again, it will remain useful. Cost 30pp.

Gnomish Festive Outdoor Lighting
Improvement to the Portable Gnomish Fax Campfire. Creates a blinking line of colored lights (like Christmas lights) scattered across a large area. Cannot be recovered. Cost 50pp.

Gnomish Whoopie Cushon
Ranged weapon (1 charge). When the recipient is hit, they will be inflicted with a "Whoopie Cushon" effect (for 2 game hrs). If the afflicted user sits down, a loud rude noise sounds. 15pp

Portable Gnomish Chime Clock
Chime's out the time every hour. Cost 200pp. Weight 5.0

Gnomish Shades
Makes day look like night. However, lets you see beneath the surface of lava (Molten hot magma). 40pp

Gnomish Hide-ur-loot Rock
Looks and behaves like a standard store-bought 8 slot backpack. However, when dropped on the ground, it looks like a rock instead of a bag. This disguise keeps passer bys from picking up your stuff while your enjoying extended camping. Now, you can just drop them on the ground, with a slight chance of concealment. Obviously, anyone can pick up your hidden rock (and the loot inside). So its not a security item. However, it beats being over-encumbered during combat. 15pp. (Tailors should be able to make this item too)

Gnomish Flying Probe
This is Stalking Probe version II. Better, faster, stronger version of the old stalking probe, plus it can fly.

Gnomish Field Tool Kit
Worn like a belt. AC2. Works just like a toolbox when right-clicked. However, it only has 6 slots. 30pp (requires something from a tailor?)

Gnomish Portable Catapult
This is a container that allows you to put 1 item into it. When you press combine, the item vanishes, but any targeted creature suffers a little damage, based on the weight of the item sacraficed. Cost: 300pp Weight: 7.8

Gnomish Automated Blender
This is a container like a Medicine bag... Increases success rate of Alchemist by some very marginal amount (2%)

Gnomish Sewing Machine
A sewing kit that increases Tailors chance of success by 2-5%. Weighs 50% more than an ordinary sewing kit. 200pp

Gnomish Rollerblades
When worn on the feet, allows the user to glide around on flat surfaces like ice. Increases speed very, VERY slightly (far less than SOW). They would also break, just like a fishing pole. In which case, the user cannot move until they are removed. If worn in an unpaved area (desert, forest area, etc), the user would move at a walking speed. Wearing these in a city cause loyal guards to agro. Cost 30pp.

Gnomish Combat Rollerskates
This is an improvement over rollerblades. These are usable in all areas. Cost 100pp
