Tinkering Journal: Joe D
Fishing Net
Works like a fishing pole, except you get 4 chances to catch something every cast, and it doesn't take bait. Could be 4 fish, could be 1 fish, 1 sandal, and 2 daggers, etc.

Ingredients:
2 Collapsible Fishing Poles
2 Ruined Bear Pelts

Swim Goggles
I'm sure you've all noticed that fine point when swimming, where the water level is exactly at eye level, and you can see under the water just as far as you can above it. The goggles will give you that effect when worn in the face slot, making the water transparent. This would be good for hunting stuff on the bottom, or visiting areas like Kedge Keep.

Ingredients:
1 Leather Mask
2 Bottles
1 Set of Fish Scales
1 Grease

Improved Compass
Since the compass as currently implemented doesn't do anything that the Sense Heading skill does, there's no reason to get one. Maybe be a bit more precise on the direction, and give a numeric heading?

You are facing North (3 degrees)

Folding Boat
Basically, it'd just be a canoe that you can carry, but when you drop it, it makes a canoe that you can use. When you get where you're going, just pick it up.

Change Machine
Lets you change 10 copper to 1 silver, 10 silver to 1 gold, 10 gold to 1 plat. Keep people from getting encumbered by all that loose change. Make it cost around 50pp or so to build.
