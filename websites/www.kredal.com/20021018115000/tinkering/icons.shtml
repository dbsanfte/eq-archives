Like a "unique" icon for under your name on the forum board? Grab one of these.
  1. Find an icon you like and "right click" and "save as" to your computer.
  2. Find a place to store that image online. Please do NOT link directly to these icons.
Q: How do I link to my new icon?
  1. Goto the Tinkering Compendium's Forum Board
  2. Click on "My Control Center"
  3. Click on "Edit Profile"
  4. Enter new URL (http://) for your icon under "Personal Photo URL"
  5. Make sure there is a check in the box for "Use personal photo"
  6. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom of the page
