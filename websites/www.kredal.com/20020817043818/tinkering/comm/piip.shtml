  Piip Ignatz of Innoruuk

 
Tinkering Journal: Piip Ignatz of Innoruuk
Blinking Light Cap
The blinking light cap was made as a way to warn other gnomes that this particular gnome had firewater in the making. It has now also become a popular party article. Its light is as strong as a flameless lantern, and it blinks.

Ingredients:
1 Flameless Latern
1 Metal Sheet
1 Spricket
1 Gnomish Bolts
1 Grease
1 Silk Lined Steel Cap

First the spricket is mounted inside the lantern, at the top. Then the metal sheet is curved to fit inside, and fastened to the spricket. The spricket makes the sheet rotate, making the light blink. Finally the blinking lamp is bolted on top of the steel cap, so that the gnome has both hands free.

Gnomish Throwing Disc
The gnomish throwing disc is a throwing weapon (dmg 6, delay 40, range 40), that is able to hit creatures only affected by magical weapons. It consists of a disk with sharpened, silver tipped edges.

Ingredients:
1 Pie Tin
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver
1 Sharpening Stone

Fog Goggles
The fog goggles lets you see quite a bit further in the fog that so often covers Norrath when it rains.

Ingredients:
2 Reflective Shards
2 Topaz Gems
1 Silk Lined Steel Cap
1 Metal Wire
1 Gnomish Bolts
1 Sharkskin Tubing

Pill Maker
This machine is a 4 slot container (7.5 wt, all, all) that produces small pills (0 wt, all, all), from various other foods. Each pill will suffice as nutriment in line with rations and the like.

Ingredients:
1 Medium Bowl
1 Spricket
1 Sprocket
1 Gear
1 Metal Rod
1 Metal Fastening
1 Dagger

Pill recipie:
A loaf of bread + rat meat + a flask of water + spices combined in the pillmaker produces 2 pills.

Scary Mask
This mask looks real scary, with moving parts and glowing eyes. It has 20 charges of "Instill doubt" or some fear-spell, and needs to be right-clicked to be activated. It then will make the mob turn away and flee for a short period of time.

Ingredients:
1 Grotesque Mask
2 Fire Beetle Eyes
2 Rat Teeth
1 Gear
1 Metal Rod
1 Metal Fastening
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts

Mechanical Mandolin
The mechanical mandolin will play the lvl 1 bard song "Chant of battle" when activated (right-clicked), and will need to be re-clicked every minute to keep on playing. It will, like the bard song, affect the whole party, when in range. The mechanical mandolin can be used by any race and class.

Ingredients:
1 Mandolin
1 Sheet of Metal
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
1 Gear
1 A6 Sprocket
1 Metal Fastening
1 Clockwork Spring (new component)
1 Music Sheet with the Bard Song "Chant of Battle"

Skin Improver
This is a device for tailors. It comes as a container, with four slots. When you place 4 low quality pelts in it and combine, you end up with a medium quality pelt. When you place 4 medium quality pelts in it, you end up with a high quality pelt. The success rate depends on your tailoring skill, and it need to be HIGH.

Ingredients:
1 Functioning Force Module
1 Needle Mold
1 Metal Sheet
1 A4 Gizmo
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
1 Metal Rod
1 Diamond Dust (to keep the needle sharp)
1 Ruby Inlaid Coffin

Water Collector
The water collector is worn in your back slot, and has a wt of 6.5. It will collect and produce drinkable water from the humidity in the air, and from your body as you sweat. The collector will not take away your need for bought or summoned water, but will reduce the rate at which you drink it to half. Due to certain hygienic factors it gives the user a saving throw VS poison -2. It also provides an AC of 2.

Ingredients:
1 Pot (made with smithing or pottery
2 Sheets of Metal
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
1 Sharkskin Tubing
1 Belt (to attach the apparatus to your back)
1 Diseased Rat liver (to filter the water)
1 Bottle (where the water ends up)

Catapult Boots
The catapult boots will make you take jumps, at normal speed (or faster if you are buffed), levitating you for a short distance before landing again, for a new jump. For each time you hit the ground you will take between 1-10 damage, since this is considered a fall (excepting rogues and others with safe fall). Levitate will nullify the effect of the boots.

Ingredients:
1 Pair of Medium Chainmail Boots
2 Springs (new component)
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts
1 Batwing

Alarm
The alarm is ideal for casters under 35, which can turn it on when they meditate. When right clicked the alarm is activated, and stays activated for 2 minutes, whereas you must activate it again. When any creature enters the normal visual field of the caster, it will sound the alarm. The alarm will not detect invisible creatures.

Ingredients:
1 Griffon Eye
1 Spricket
1 Gear
1 Metal Wire
1 Undead Froglok Tongue

Tinter
The tinter will tint any piece of visible bronze or steel armor into a white kind of color (very different to any existing armor types), that emits light like a fire beetle eye. The piece may of course be lost if you fail.

Ingredients:
1 Piece of Bronze or Steel Armour
1 Cask (to contain the acid bath)
1 Diseased Rat Liver
1 Firewater (for making an acid bath)
1 Lightstone
1 Functional Power Module (to power the brew)

Heated Boots
The heated boots can be worn by all races and classes, AC 5, wt 8.0, SV fire +8, trivial at skill 124. Fuelled by firewater they will keep your feet warm, even in Everfrost.

Ingredients:
1 Set of Iron boots (bought at vendors)
1 Firewater
2 Metal Rods
1 Sharksskin Tubing
1 metal wire
1 Set of Gnomish Bolts.

Periscope
When the periscope (all, all, wt. 5.5, agi -3, trivial at skill 181) is placed in the face slot, the viever will see his/hers surroundings from an altered height. Small races will look at things as if they were medium, medium races will see things from the height of a tall race, and tall races will see things from a height two heads above their normal.

Ingredients:
2 metal rods
2 reflective shards
1 iron mask
1 Set of gnomish bolts
1 Shiny Brass Shield
1 metal wire

The metal rods are bent into the right shape, then the Shiny Brass Shield are cut into pieces that functions like mirrors, that are placed inside the hollow rods. The reflective shards are placed in the end of the tube to protect the users eyes from dust and insects. The metal wire ties the two rods together, and finally the tubes are fastened to the iron mask. The user will find it a bit uneasy to manouever with the apparatus on, and suffers a -3 penalty on agility.

Magnetic Rod
With a wish going out to Verrant to introduce magnets to the world of Norrath, I present the magnetic rod. This device is used for disarming opponents, like the disarm skill, but with a lower chance of success. It is placed in the primary melee slot and does no damage. A successful check makes the user disarm the opponent. Dmg 0, rng 25, delay 38, effect of disarm, wt. 1.0. Trivial at skill 162.

Ingredients:
1 magic stone of Kunark (magnet)
1 metal wire
1 collapsable fishing pole.

Gnomish Pot Helmet
The Gnomish pot helmet is pretty heavy (too heavy to wear for anyone except large races), but it has superb AC. The spikes on it also gives the user a slow damage shield. The stats are: AC 16, wt.25, effect of rotating rapiers (dmg 1-5 every 6th tick). Class War, SK, Rog. Race Barb, Ogre, Troll. Trivial at skill 168.

Ingredients:
1 pot (made with smithing)
2 metal sheets
2 rapiers
1 spricket
1 gears
1 gnomish bolts.

The spricket is bolted on top of the helmet, and gears connects it to the rapiers. The spricket makes the rapiers rotate and do damage to any monsters targeted. Finally the metal sheets are cut and bent to cover and protect the mechanical parts.

Wind-up Rat
The wind-up rat was made as a toy, and it is a toy. When you rightclick the rat in your inventory, a mechanical rat will appear in front of you and move at normal walking speed, in random patterns, for a short period of time, before it stops, and can be picked up again (by anyone). Weigth is 6.0, it can not attack anything, and have hitpoints like a lvl 10. Animals and unintelligent creatures will aggro on it. Trivial at skill 160.

The rat is made in several, separate steps:

Components for 1 rat leg:
2 metal rods, 2 gears, 1 metal wire, 1 bow cam.

Components for 1 rat body:
1 rusted blackbox, 2 metal sheets, 1 A4 size gizmo, 1 gears, 1 gnomish spring (new item), 1 metal shaft, 1 gnomish bolts

Components for 1 rat head:
1 metal sheet, 2 rat whiskers, 2 rat ears, 2 opals (for eyes), 1 gnomish bolts

To make the rat:
Combine 4 rat legs, 1 rat body 1 key no 22 (from Crushbone) and 1 rat head in your toolbox.
Repeater
(wt. 2.0, all, all)
When activated (right-clicked) the repeater will repeat everything you say in SAY and GROUPSAY for a minute or so.

Example:
You say, Have you seen my new, tinkered device?
Repeater says, Have you seen my new, tinkered device?
Panticles says, I thought you had given up tinkering?
You say, Aye, I had.
Repeater says, Aye, I had.
Tallis says, But now Verrant finally read the tinkering community post on the board, and decided to use one of her suggestions, she even got a job at Verrant now.
You say to your group, And they were choosing the repeater )
Repeater says, And they were choosing the repeater )
Panticles says, How great...

Besides being an annoying toy, the Repater will have no real function.

Ingredients:
1 zombie skin
1 metal shaft
2 springs (new item)
1 pie tin
1 gears
1 spricket

Levitating Coffin
(container, capacity 2, wt.0.0, all, all, agi -3):

This small coffin will levitate on top of your backpacks. It is a container with a capacity of two large items. It will reduce the wt. of these items to zero, but has a tendency to get in the way, so you get a penalty of agi -3. The coffin is made in two steps:

Components for levitater:
1 spricket, 2 griffon feathers, 1 metal fastening, 1 gnomish bolts, 1 gears, 1 grease.

Components for coffin:
3 metal sheets, 2 metal shafts, 1 metal wire, 1 wooden shards, 1 gnomish bolts.

Then the levitater and coffin is combined in the toolbox. Trivial at skill 192.

Gnomish Canon
4 slot container, wt.8, range 75, usable only by the most intelligent races: Gnomes, erudites, high elves, dark elves, all classes. Trivial at skill 177. Effect: Rain of metal (50 dmg, area effect).

To use, first load the cannon (put the right items inside and click combine to produce a charge), then aim (target) and fire (right click).

Components for cannon:
1 pot, 2 gears, 3 metal rods, 1 metal shaft

Ingredients for charge
1 firewater, 2 metal bits, 1 gnomish bolts
(The charge is LORE ITEM: you cannot produce several charges in advance. This makes the delay of the cannon naturally low).

Remote Door Opener
(wt.2.0, all, all, trivial at skill 192)
This device will open any unlocked door or gates from a distance. Are there several doors all will be opened. It can be to some use in dungeons, or used elsewhere just for fun.

Ingredients:
1 ruby
1 firewater
2 sprickets
1 gears
1 metal wire
1 A6 gizmo
1 gnomish bolts

Gnomish Strider Pants
AC 4, wt.8.0, all, all, effect of lessen fatigue, agi -3, trivial at skill 166.

These pants are made in such a way that they support your movement in an elegant and comforting way, reducing the stamina loss when moving, jumping or fighting into half. They also reduce your flexibility a bit, and you suffer a -4 agility penalty for that. The pants are made in three steps:

Components for pants chassis:
1 medium cloth pants, 1 medium banded belt, 4 collapsable fishing rods, 2 gears

Components for spring device:
2 sprickets, 2 springs (new item), 2 gears, 1 grease

Finally the pants chassis and the spring device is combined in the toolbox.

Mechanical Snake Charmer
AC 3, Wt.2.0, all, all, effect of charm snake, cha -3, trivial at skill 141.
The snake hypnotizer is a mask that will charm one snake targeted. The snake will remain charmed for 1-4 minutes, and will follow you around. If you get attacked it will defend you, but cannot be commanded in any way. When the charm effect wears off the snake will attack you.

Ingredients:
1 iron visor
1 metal fastening
1 spricket
1 gears
1 sprocket
3 spiderling eyes

Parachute
Charges 5. Wt.3.0, all, all, effect of safe fall, trivial at skill 88.
The parachute will automatically open under falls where you would normally take damage, and one charge will then be used. It will work as safe fall (meaning it will not remove all damage, but reduce it considerably). It is worn in the belt slot.

Ingredients:
3 silk swatches
1 cloth cord
1 medium bowl
1 pie tin
1 leather belt
1 metal fastening

The silks and cord are sewn together to make the parachute. It is then packed inside the medium bowl, that is fastened to the belt. Finally the metal fastening is used together with the pie tin to put a lock on the bowl. Due to the fragility of the bowl the parachute can usually only be used 5 times.
