   Mildikin Quartzworth of Tunare

Tinkering Journal: Mildikin Quartzworth of Tunare
Gnomish Bomb
This item will look like a black ball. You place the bomb in the desired center of the explosion. When you are ready to ignite it, throw a throwing weapon at it (like a throwing dagger or shurinken) to make the spark to egnite the twine which will burn a split second to the core (filled with the excessive amount of Firewater), causing a massive explosion... Area of Effect damage of 150-200 damage to ANYTHING in the proposed area. Trivial at 120.

Ingredients:
1 Static Orb
2 Firewater
1 Metal Twine
Additionally you'll need a throwing weapon of sorts, not to be combined.

Dimensional Hammer
This item will look like a Fine Steel Warhammer. The stats are:
1 Hand Blunt MAGIC ITEM, LORE ITEM
Attack: 30 Delay: 15 Weight: 0.5
Effect: Stun
Effect: Gravity Flux Charges: 5
Races: ALL Classes: ALL

Ingredients:
1 Metal Rod
2 Bat Wing
1 Functional Force Module
1 Small Power Module
1 Blackbox B Series
1 Gnomish Bolts
1 Metal Disc

Mistletoe Cap
In traditional custom of the mundane world at Christmans time, when a mistletoe is hung and a man and a woman stand under it, they are supposed to give each other a small kiss. It this tradition, the Mistletoe Cap was invented. As such, this cap will give +20 to charisma (CHA). It also provides AC of 2 and can be worn by anyone. Such item would be trivial at 80.

Ingredients:
1 Mistletoe
1 Cloth Cap (any size will do)
1 Metal Twine
