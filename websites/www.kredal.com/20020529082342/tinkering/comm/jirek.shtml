   Jirek

Tinkering Journal: Jirek
Gnomish Infrared Goggles
Right-Click and it will either blind you for 15 seconds and not be useable for another 4:45, or it will give you ultra vision for 5 minutes. Chances of blinding you are 1:4. Used in head slot. Effect: infravision, weight: 2.0, races: all, classes: all

Gnomish Jet Pack
Right-Click on it, and it will consume 1 firewater from your inventory and casts a 5 minute levitation. Only works while in your BACK slot. Effect: levitate, weight: 5.0, races: Dwarf, Halfling, Gnome, classes: all

Gnomish Radar
Weight: 5.0
Effect: Radar
Race: All
Class: All

Ingredients:
1 Ruby
1 Compass
1 Large Black Lodestone
1 Metal Rod

"... King Ak'Anon put on a royal game of hide and go seek in the city today. In order to find the king, I made some slight modifications to a previous invention of mine. I call my new creation, the GNOMISH RADAR. I took my regular compass, and replaced the disply with a ruby for a more readable, accurate display. Then, by having a metal rod attatched to the compass pin, it would send out a sound vibration by hitting the large black lodestone I had implemented. All creature-shaped entities would then bounce back the sound, and the device would receive and display the data. Needless to say, I won the competition..."

The device could be made at 125 skill, and would never become trivial at 160.

At 148 skill the item would fail 5 out of 6 times, and at 160, it would still fail 3 out of 4.

Right-click on it, and it brings up the tracking display that druids and rangers get, but equivalent to 80 skill. One would *NOT* be able to track; only to see what it detects.
