ABOUT

The Iksar are the oldest race in norrath.

They were created by their lord Cazic-Thule long before the fleshy races came to inhabit their lands.

They saw many great wars and triumphs in their time of glory but over time their empire was driven out and forgotten. The other newer races of Norrath spread and prospered while the Iksar remained forgotten in their historical homeland of Kunark.

In recent times, they have been rediscovered by the fleshy races who have come to Kunark for exploration. Angered at this intrusion into their ceremonial lands, a young group of Iksar in Cabilis have banded together calling themselves the Champions of Cabilis.

Their goal: push back these intruders and to spread the power of the Iksar once more to the four corners of the world.

Beware the Champions for they are the pride of Kunark!
