Saturday September 29th, 2001
  • Today was the wedding of Ishy Drackos and Srarnu Shadowcast (now Drackos). Was a great time for all who attended. Took a while to get the GM to come for the ceremony but it was a good chance for Champs old and new to hang out and meet each other. Tipsy Bartender dusted off his armor and came out as well as our old pal Sasraaz. The wedding was held on the island beside the Tangrin and he watched the whole thing. After the ceremony which included a lot of weeping Iksar we celebrated with booze, food and dueling. Cakaris was the Arena Champion schooling myself, Xakanis and I think one other.
     Wednesday September 26th, 2001

Hope Cazic-Thule doesn't see this shot Sslarr, for shame!
     Monday September 24th, 2001

This should be a fun event. If you've ever wanted to see the Champions create a newbie Iksar on the Nameless to join us in our celebration. There will be booze, fireworks, and general merriment. Don't miss the Cabilis social event of the year!!!!
     Monday September 17th, 2001
  • Updated the picture of the day today. It shows Scalis in the Stonebrunt Mountains. Scalis has moved from a new recruit to part of the gang right away with his generosity and maturity. Thanks for the pic Scalis. Cleaned up the members page a bit today too. It needs some serious work.
    Friday September 15th, 2001
  • 30,000 hits for this website today. Well not all today of course but over the course of a year mostly. I'm pretty impressed by that. I started this website with zero knowledge of HTML and no real concept but it came together. Thanks to all who have contributed and all who visit. You make me proud!
     Thursday September 13th, 2001
  • We lost one of the great Champions today. Chable Bloodyfist was one of the longest standing Champions. He originally got me into this game as we played together on a clan in Team Fortress Classic. We spoke at length about his feelings with EQ. He has joined Brothers of Darkness our allied guild and will still be around. As with many of us post-50 EQ can become a drag at times. He was looking for something to spice things up and found he was grouping more regularily with those guys. He will always be around to help out any Champs and will still be around for the joint BoD/CoC raids. /Salute Chable we will miss you over here.
