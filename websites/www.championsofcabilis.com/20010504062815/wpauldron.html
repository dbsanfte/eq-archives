Trooper Scale Pauldron
By Scarlax, Lueenyen, and Hempire


Starts in: The Overthere
Who: Xlixinar Arcut

Xlixinar Arcut sends you with a note to Prime Hierophant Vek. He will tell you of a Potion he needs you to make.

The potion is Gray Ice and 2 pondfish spines. The Gray Ice is from the highest peak in Frontier Mnts by the Mountain Giant Fort. The Pondfish spines are from Pondfish. They spawn in the small pond that feeds the Lake of Ill Omen coming from Firiona Vie. You combine the 2 pondfish spines along with the grey ice in an oven along with a pot. I was able to do it no problem with my Baking skill at 25.

Then you get some "Hopeless Willow Moss" which is a difficult forage in Swamp of No Hope. Combine it with a bottle, Flask of water, and the vial of poison in a brew barrel. This is also trivial around 20-25 in skill. You then get your potion, give it to a froglok slave (located somewhere in Cabilis normaly near the FoB entrance). The poison will kill the frog and you must bring back the remains with 2 Fire Emeralds. He will give you the proof you need. Take what he gives you and Banded Pauldron to the Skorpiki for the "Pauldrons"
