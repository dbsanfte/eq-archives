Mystic Scale Bracer
By Cradossk
To receive your Mystic Scale Bracer Zazlan asks for a Coercion Implant, a Gooey Adhesive, a Ruby, and a Banded Bracer.

The Coercion Implant drops randomly off any coerced MOB in Dalnir's. Hang out there for a while killing and you'll get one. Same goes for the Gooey Adhesive. It's in Dalnir's as a rare drop off of any Goo in there.

Buy yourself a Ruby and a Banded Bracer and turn this in.

This is the only item that isn't lore so you can do this twice and get a bracer for each arm. It's a decent bracer, pretty much like the Imbued Cabilis Scale Bracer in terms or stats. This isn't the be all end all item for your arms though, Chipped Bone Bracers give much more WIS and Jarsath Scale Bracer will give you AC and WIS and can be looted fairly easily at the Karnor's zone. Still considering you'll want to fight in Dalnir's at some point, these are good for their difficulty in getting.
