Shackle of Dust
By Kwanlei

   Upon your birth as a brave Monk of the Swiftail caste, give your guild note to Grand Master Glox. He gives you this shackle. It is recommended that you place this item in your bank until you reach your 6th season. If you choose to equip it, be aware that if you die, you only have 30 minutes to retrieve it or your corpse will decay.
