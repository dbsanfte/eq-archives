Crusader's Khukri
By Reptilo
   Alas young knights, the gods have decided that this khukri will be not granted for a period of time. Speak to Lord Qyzar about a matter of betrayal. He will ask for a stolen book of charasis, a copy of the same, and your zealots khukri. Stay tuned for an update to this quest as the gods see fit.

    Side note: The quests are fun, if not sometimes frustrating. Do them at a level which yields you xp, so they are not in vain. The rewards are not super great, but remember the zealots khukri is meant to be done at level 25ish, just imagine the 1hs we will get when we obtain the Greenmist at 50ish. IMO it is well worth it to get the Zealots khukri and stash it in the bank and wait and see what happens. Once again stay tuned for an update to these matters.
