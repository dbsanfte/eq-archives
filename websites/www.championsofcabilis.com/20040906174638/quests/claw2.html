Claw of the Scout
By Ishy Drackos

The second quest starts in east cab in the temple of terror. Elder Patriarch Ixthal is the quest giver.

He says this:

Elder Patriarch Ixthal says 'Hello to you as well. I am Elder Patriarch Ixthal and I continue training when the Prime Patriarch becomes overwhelmed with duties. I will train you if you seek to one day [become a Patriarch of Cabilis].'

You say, 'i seek to become a patriarch of cabilis'

Elder Patriarch Ixthal says 'I thought you might. I can sense your spirit is strong. For your first mission, you will need to use the spirits to guide you to collect some [rare items] we need on a daily basis in order to keep up with the demands of the city. This mission will also be great [training] for you.'

You say, 'what rare items?'

Elder Patriarch Ixthal says 'We gather several items that we trade with the Hierophants. These items include a cracked femur, creeper cabbage, and lichenclover. Once you have all of these items, return them to me with your Claw of the Cub and you shall be rewarded.'

Cracked femur drops off greater scalebones. lichenclover is a foraged item. foragable in field of bone and Firiona Vie creeper cabbage drops off man eating creeper plants in swamp of no hope.

A level 14 could probly kill most of the mobs if careful.
