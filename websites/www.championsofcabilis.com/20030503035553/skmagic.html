The Grades given to the spells reflect my own personal opinion, this can be affected by zone and play style, someone who solo fear kites will likely have a higher opinion of the pet spells than I.
 

Your First Spell

Harm Touch
  To dispell some misconceptions of Shadow Knights, Your soul is your sword (or mace, or in Ashikaga Daikoku's case a spear) Not your spells, your spells act as a force multiplier, increasing what we can do, but not being our life. This includes the holy grail of Shadow Knight misconceptions, Harm touch. You will find that HT is an enabler, enables you to hunt further out, try that slightly tougher MOB, cut the downtime, the deaths or the mad train to the zone on a bad pull. As opposed to Many Shadow Knights, the Ashikaga-Ryu teaches one to learn to recognise situations where your HT may be needed, and use it early on. Very rarely (only to save the life of a groupmate) will you want to kill with HT. Hit it early, the MOB will flee/die sooner, causing you and yours less pain.
 

The Joy of Mana, Your Level 9 Spells

In the long term, no other class gets the useful spells a Shadow Knight does in it's first group. No 40+ Enchanter is going to waste a spot on Minor Shielding. No Cleric will mem Courage. Only Rangers are close, with Snare, Glimpse, and Lull Animal being really useful in later life. Only two spells will be wastes of book space in your 40s(Lifetap/Sense Dead.) Yes even the pet spell will have its use in later life.
Name Mana Spell Type/Grade  
Disease Cloud 10 Conjuration/A+  
In a group, this spell is your best friend. As a Primary Tank this will be key to holding aggro, as Secondary, it will speed up your ability to pull a mob off a caster. One of the two level 9 spells that level 50+ Shadow Knights keep memed, in groups.
 
Invisible to Undead 40 Divination/B  
Makes your target invisible too most undead. Not the most used spell in the world, but critical when needed. It is also one of our only usable on others spells. Will be used in your 40s as a way to run across the Lake Of Ill Omen with out green skeletons annoying you when doing the Dreadlands/Cabilis runs.
 
Leering Corpse 40 Conjuration/C  
The Wimpy Midget. These guys are not TOTALLY useless, but our pets role is completely different then that of any other pet using class. Our pets are not tanks, they are very long duration DOTs. Always use /pet no taunt, tank yourself. At level 22 Leering Corpse, gets a new lease on life: Target. Many a Shadow Knight has bored themselves to sleep nuking their pet with evocation spells for skill practise. Why use the mana for "Animate Dead" when this will do?
 
Lifetap 9 Alteration/B+  
Drains 5hp from the target, gives it to you. The Life-X series of spells is our general purpose DD 'nukes' 'tap' is quick and fast, when paired with a slow, high damage 2 handed weapon, can be cast between swings, increasing your damage and your endurance though the fight. By lvl 12, you'll find this spells best use though, the first aid kit. Find a scorpions or wolf pup, then life it to death. Really cuts down the down time soloing as Mana regens faster than HP.(Once we get Meditation)
 
Locate Corpse 5 Divination/B  
"Everyone Is Your Friend." Points you in the direction of the targets nearest corpse. This spell will make you friends, save you from fret when you died without a /loc. Ashikaga Daikoku came unto the notice of his current Daimyo because of this spell. Be nice to people, I've made friends, got a Guild invite, and earned up to 20pp with this spell, never even asking for a donation. Help people and help yourself.
 
Sense the Dead 5 Divination/D  
Points you towards the nearest undead. Pretty pointless after level 12, when you can go to Kurns and kill skeletons to your hearts content. But useful helping lower level friends hunt Brittle Iksar Skulls.
Syphon Strength 5 Alteration/A  
Drains 10 STR from MOB, adds 10 to you. Pull spell of choice when without Clarity, good for a STR boost any fight, when encumbered on the way to sell target a green and then drain and kill it to reduce your overload. As with Disease Cloud, a spell that will still be used daily when you are level 50+.
 

Filling Some Holes, Your Level 15 Spells

Besides some upgrades two spells stand out at 15, Fear and Clinging darkness. With these you begin Fear Kiting.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Bone Walk 80 Conjuration/C-  
Pet II, same rules apply as with the first pet, but without a use after you get the third.
 
Clinging Darkness 20 Alteration A  
A short term DOT/Snare. The spell to use when pulling SoWless. In many cases superior to the upgrade Engulfing Darkness (Faster cast time/less mana cost.) Can also be chained with Disease Cloud for Uber taunting.
 
Endure Cold 20 Abjuration/C  
The Practice Spell. Use this spell to keep your abjuratation skill up. It is useful in the Swamp of No Hope when fighting Froglok Shamans or Tizmark shamans in GD. If you go Shardwurm hunting in GD, be over 40 and have Resist Cold available. Keep practicing with Endure Cold.
 
Fear 40 Alteration/B+  
The Spell Of Death, but who's? Used right, you can hunt yellows solo, used wrong and die in a train of greens. This spell can save you if you cast it and can flee to zone out. With Clinging Darkness (Later Engulfing Darkness) this spell can lower your downtime tremendously. Good spots for it are Pulling skeletons up the ramp one over from Newbie Ramp, then Fear, and chase it around the greenies, the walls of OT Specifically the ledge area. Also a great area for the mid thirties to solo, the Tizmarks in GD. Pull the Tizmark Down the hill with darkness, then fear him, then you attack, pet (if you have one) will join in. pull it fairly close to the dwarfs, they wont assist, which is good, and no wanderers, good too.
Lifespike 18 Alteration/B+  
The Lifetap upgrade. Slightly more mana efficent, slightly slower cast time.
Numb the Dead 20 Abjuration/C  
Calms the dead, causing them not to agro when you pull their neibhor. Nice spell obvious uses for it, but with the Kurnak Skeletons, no real use until much later in life.
Shadowstep 10 Alteration/D  
Short range, random teleport. Is good for a pure caster, but as a close in, with it's cast time, not much use.

The Swiss Army Knife, your Level 22 Spells

Nothing that flashy this level, with the possible exception of Vampiric Embrace being cool/flashy, these are still useful.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Convoke Shadow 120 Conjuration/C-  
Pet III, you should be familar with these guys by now.
Dark Empathy 20 Alteration/B  
I feel your pain. Transfers Some of your HP to your target. It is more useful for building guild faction with lower level players for you and your guild then anything else. Only we, the Iksar, (and those lowbred Trolls) can regen, so the younger members of other races will always be asking for heals. Good evil Iksar fun, when armed with a Stalag Terror Spine Spear or other spear, heal a newbie, and silently laugh in his face as explain to him how you can not SoW him. Well, we are evil. Dark empathy does have its more serious uses. Partnering is expanded to you as a Greenmist Kishi, these wont be the most efficient partnerships, but in the end, it's not plat, uber loot, or XP, it's about fun. Ashikaga Daikoku has partner on multiple occasions with a lower level High Elf Wizardess, she roots/snares and nukes, he pulls and spears, life drawing as he can. XP is low, fun and good conversation while resting is high, Dark Empathy can keep the caster at full hp without affecting down time much if any. Also remember when partnering with enchanters, their pets wont attack unless they are. DE will allow you to heal them afterward, you are the tank, you should take the damage.
Deadeye 35 Divination/B-  
Self ony See Invisible. Not that useful until level 30 and you get Gather Shadows.
Engulfing Darkness 60 Conjuration/B-  
Upgrade to Clinging Darkness, DOT/snare. Would be far more valuable if there were more spell slots. The short cast time of Clinging makes it preferable to Engulfing in a standard spell layout when you first get it. It is very helpful in specialized camps/groups.
Spook the Dead 10 Alteration/C  
Preferable to fear for fear kiting undead. Finding a good spot for fear kiting undead is another story.
Vamperic Embrace 50 Alteration/A+  
A lifetap proc for your weapon. This spell won't seem like much when you first get it, but as you grow, the better it seems, procing more, when you'll need any heal you can get.
Ward Undead 30 Evocation/B+  
Undead only DD. This is your first evocation spell, get friendly with an enchanter because you will need breeze/clarity like you wouldn't believe. The obvious nickname for this spell is "Pet Nuke" as everyone has spent a thrill charge hour or two casting this on their pet. You will ant to keep Evocation up for your later spells, especially Dismiss Undead, where you can be a more effective caster in Lguk, CoM, and KC.

Stepping Into Midlife, Enjoy the World

With these spells you leave the world of a broodling, ready to face the world, things are going to change.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Endure Disease 20 Abjuration/D-  
Resist disease Buff. Only useful for casting on Newbies in N-Ro, or in healer weak teams, would only be mem'd for buff breaks, then replaced.
Feign Death 60 Abjuration C  
A spell that mimics the Monk discipline of the same name. In the eyes of the Ashikaga-Ryu this is the most overrated spell in your entire carrier as a Greenmist Kishi. Helpful friends will just stand there watching you die yelling at you to FD, while you are getting chain-stunned. For pulling this is great, unless the mob is eating you alive from the back. When acting as a tank, it's not that good. Before deciding to place your life in it's hands remember the cast time, remember it does get interrupted. 
Gather Shadows 35 Divination A+  
Self Only Invisability. The Ashikaga-Ryu's one rule of travel: Always travel invsible. Another piece of advice is to travel in /anon (Or /role if your in a guild.) Traveling so not only stops random attacks, but when traveling through newbie zones, if they don't see you, they don't beg for plat and gear. One downside to this method is that you will get asked if you're a cleric or a porter, just reply "Sorry, I'm an SK" and you are clear. Shadows is not infoulable, undead can see through it and many higher level mobs can see through it, so don't hit numlock and go get a coke. In the end this is a very enabling spell, allowing you to move through zones where you are KOS. to get to groups you want to join, camps you want to do.
Heat Blood 72 Alteration/D  
Heat Based Dot: The most useful of your 3 dots when you're in a group. Only will Necros overwrite this spell. Also seems to be resisted less than Heart Flutter.
Lifedraw 63 Alteration/A-  
New Lifetap Spell 45 point transfer. A moderete Nuke/Heal when you first get it, later on do not dismiss this spell, the healing you do yourself, your party healers, or regen during down time won't.
Reckless Bones 160 Conjuration/C-  
Pet Spell: You've been here, done that.
Wave of Enfeeblement 40 Alteration/D  
Area of Effect Syphon Strength. Can be useful for power leveling broodlings, as it will keep aggro on you, for a crowd. Otherwise, not worth the slot space.

Last Drink Before the Drought, Your Level 39 Spells

Enjoy these spells, they are the last you'll see until level 49.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Animate Dead 200 Conjuration/C-  
Pet Spell: More of the same.
Cancel Magic 30 Abjuration C+  
Removes Spell Effects on Target. Not something regularly used, but can be used to cancel mobs Damage Shields or Sow, or a Darkness cast on you.
Expulse Undead 60 Evocation/B  
Upgrade to Ward Undead. Undead Nuke 91 points max damage.
Heart Flutter 60 Alteration/B  
Disease Based Dot: This may be perferable to Heat Blood when soloing as the mana cost is less and has 17 AC debuff effect. Druids, Shaman, and Necros all have Disease DOTs superior to this, so save your mana in Root/DOT groups.
Resist Cold 50 Abjuration C+  
Upgraded, usable on others, resist cold buff. Can you say Vox? How about Shardwurm camps?
Shadow Vortex 40 Alteration/C+  
12 Ac point transfer. Ac is always good, combined with Heart Flutter you have a -29 AC effect, pets are more effective when soloing. A strain on your mana pool, even with clarity, for every MOB use, so it is recommended for use as a buff.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Shieldskin 41 Conjuration/C-  
55 hp Forcefield. Good for Soloing and PLing others, costs money to cast, use sparingly.
Name Mana Spell Type  
Shrod of Hate 60 Alteration/C+  
20 Atk point transfer. Basicly acts as a 2 level attack boost to you, with a lowering of your opponents by 2 levels. A strain on your mana pool, even with clarity, for every MOB use, so it is recommended for use as a buff.
