OCTOBER   NOVEMBER

Monday December 4th, 2000
  • VICTORY! The Champions of Cabilis were victorious in their defense of our fair city yesterday versus the 7th Legion. This was an excellent event I'm sure for both sides. We had a force or near 40 Champions, the most online at one time ever. We gathered our forces by the large Iksar statues in the Lake of Ill Omen. After some wrangling, war was declared on both sides and the battle began. It was fast, furious, and extremely laggy but a heck of a lot of fun. Each group had a target and executed well. I was taken down by Fortenbras wolf but he too would fall soon after. One of their druids did escape and try kiting us but in the end The Legion conceded victory to the Iksar. Congratulations to all those who came out, it was a great job by all. Cazic is proud of his chosen ones on this day. Pics of the event can be found HERE Thanks for those who sent them in. Now we must not grow too content, it is time to plan our next event.
