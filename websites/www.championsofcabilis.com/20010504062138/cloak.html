Mystic Scale Cloak
By Cradossk
To receive your Mystic Scale Cloak Zazlan asks for a Forest Giant Hammer, a Worn Charbone, a Star Ruby, and a Banded Cloak. The Forest Giant Hammer is found randomly off Forest Giants in Warlsick's Woods. The Worn Charbone drops off Charbone Skeletons in the Lake of Ill Omen and The Swamp of No Hope. Buy or make a Banded Cloak and buy the Star Ruby from, you guess it, the Vendor in the building to the Southwest of the bank.
   Give Zazlan these items and he will reward you with a Mystic Scale Cloak pictured below. This is a great cloak until you are able to get a Mammoth Hide Cloak or a Kunzar Cloak.
