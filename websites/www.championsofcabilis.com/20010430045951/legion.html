LEGION LAGER QUEST
By Cradossk
   This can be a fun quest to do if you are into the brewing skill. The Legion Lager you make is also used in the Restraining Order quest, read about that one here.
   In West Cabilis near the Warslick's Wood entrance there is a Trooper named Neozite, hail him and he will tell you of how he loved to drink Legion Lager. Unfortunately the master brewer can no longer make this. He longs for a drink of this legendary booze and would be so happy if you could get him some.
   Legion Lager is made with the brewing skill. The recipe is as follows:

   Bloodwater - Can be bought at a vendor near Swamp of No Hope entrance in East Cabilis.
   Deadbone Barley - Can be foraged in the Field of Bone.
   Giant Leech Blood Sac - Found on Giant Leeches in the Swamp of No Hope.
   A Bottle - Bought from Brewing Vendor by the Bank.

   The Trivial Range for Legion Lager is about 40 so don't try to brew it without a brewing skill of 30. Bring him the Lager once it's brewed and he'll be very greatful. You'll get faction, small amount of experience and small amount of coin.
