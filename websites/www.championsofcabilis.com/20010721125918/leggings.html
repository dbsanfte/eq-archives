Mystic Scale Greaves
By Cradossk
   To receive your Mystic Scale Greaves Shelzie asks for a Sarnak Recruitment Letter, an Exiled Iksar Head, Banded Legs, and a Fire Emerald. This is a fairly straightforward one. The Letter drops randomly off Sarnak Recruits in the Lake of Ill Omen. As well the Exiled Iksar Head drops randomly off Iksar Exiles in the Lake and Warslick's Woods. Buy yourself some Banded legs and buy a Fire Emerald from the Jewel Vendor southwest of the bank.
   Hand in the items and you are rewarded with Mystic Scale Leggings pictured below. These are a good pair of legs to keep around if you ever need the SV resists but you'd be better off with a pair Gatorscale Leggings if you can get a hold of some.
