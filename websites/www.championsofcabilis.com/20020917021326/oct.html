     Saturday October 21st, 2000
  • Today was a day that will stick in the memory of those hated half-breeds-The Sarnaks, for time. Although we had a small number of Champions turn up to raid the Sarnak fort, those who came showed their courage and strength and our task was accomplished. Seven Champions went into the Sarnak Fortess, I believe only 3 came out without giving their lives. We had some help from an elf friendly to our cause. I guess all elves aren't so evil! After killing countless Sarnaks we took on the Chancellor of Di'zok himself. He was immediately aided by a see of lesser Sarnaks which made it difficult to wade through. Fortunately we had the strength of Belfior and Hempire dealing damage to him and he went down. Unfortunately he didn't drop the Jade Chokdai Prod :( Afterward there was some good dueling (Hemp and I dueled to a draw after my pet turned on me). Then it was prize time. A Foremans Tunic, Lumberjack Cap, Iksar Stave, Sarnak Emblazoned Tabard, and a few other nice items were given away. For those who couldn't make it, you missed out. Time to start planning our next event, toss your ideas on the forums.
