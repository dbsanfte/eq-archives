Mystic Scale Gauntlets
By Cradossk
To receive your Mystic Scale Gauntlets Shelzie asks for Venomous Parchment, Warlord Drawing Plans, Banded Gauntlets, and a Ruby.

The Venomous Parchment is a random drop by Venomous Lampreys which spawn near the Bandit Camp in the Swamp of No Hope or if you're really brave in Firona Vie.

Warlord Drawing Plans are actually a typo on Verant's part. What you actually want is a Warlord Drawing Quill. This drops randomly off of Goblin Warlords in the Lake of Ill Omen, quite possibly in Warslick's Woods too. Banded Gauntlets I'm not sure where to get those heh heh, j/k buy a pair then buy a Ruby from the Jewel Vendor southwest of the Bank in East Cabilis.

Hand in these items to the lovely Shelzie and she'll reward you with: Mystic Scale Gauntlets, a nice pair of gloves these are but the plus INT is kind of a waste for a Shaman. I'd try to grab a pair of Splitpaw Hide Gloves but I'm a WIS Shaman myself. The AC on these is pretty nice.
