ARCHIVED NEWS

Tuesday December 4th, 2001
  • Today marks one of the biggest days in Everquest since it's release. Hundreds of thousands of fans globally are looking forward to Shadows of Luclin. This expansion promises to upgrade the entire EQ game engine with new graphics, spells, skills, etc. Should be damn exciting. It also has a certain significance to the Iksar people. We get a new class to play - The Beastlord! Should be an interesting new class that hopefully will be able to help us in the future. I believe they get track which could be useful in a lot of situations where we'd need a tracker. The new screenshot of the week features Al'Kabor, the Erudite Wizard who discovered the portal to the Moon Luclin. I spotted him in Dreadlands last week and snapped the shot.
     Thursday November 29th, 2001
  • The Champions of Cabilis will be hosting a raid on Kael Drakkel on December 12th at 5PM PST/8PM EST. This raid is open to all friends of CoC and members over lvl 44. It should last about 4 hours tops and hopefully will result in some good drops and faction for all. Sign up on the events board. We haven't been raiding a lot as guild as of late so it's time to get that part of our game going again.
