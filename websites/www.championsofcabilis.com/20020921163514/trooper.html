Trooper Scale Armour
By Scarlax

   This armour was introduced to the game in the patch on Nov 15th, 2000. The look is a darker green version of Cabilis Scale. It is the Iksar Version of Crafted Armour. The quest begins by Hailing the new NPC Skorpikis, Drixiv and Xlixinar Arcut. They are close to the Chasm in The Overthere.
