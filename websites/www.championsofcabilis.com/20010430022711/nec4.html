Dark Binder Skullcap Rank Four
By Shaozin
   Minimum Level 13. Ask Master Kyvix in the Temple of Death about the New Components that he needs. He states that he requires an apprentice of the third circle. He hands you a Brood Sac, which he wants filled with Creeper Cabbage, a Heart sting Telson with venom, Brutling Choppers and a Scalebone Femur. In return for returning this combined sac, you will receive a Dark Binder Skullcap and your Fourth Rank.
