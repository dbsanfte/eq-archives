Mystic Scale Boots
By Cradossk
   To receive your Mystic Scale Boots Zazlan asks for a Rhino Hoof, Spirit Caller Beads, Banded Boots, and a Fire Emerald. The Rhino Hoof drops off of Kunark Rhinos in Overthere. Both the mid teens ones and the high 30's ones. Keep killing them and one will drop. The Goblin Spirit Caller Beads drops of of Goblin Spirt Callers. They can be found in the Lake of Ill Omen or in Warslick's Woods, fairly common spawn they are. The Boots are bought or made and the Fire Emerald is bought at the Cabilis Gem Vendor southwest of the bank.    Hand these into Zazlan and he gives you: Mystic Scale Boots AC9 +5STR +5VS Dis +5VS Poison. That's a lot of AC and Strength in a pair of boots, I'd use these till you can get your hands on some Imbued Cabilis Scale ones, these are nice for protecting your feet.
