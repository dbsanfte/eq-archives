     Monday March 5th, 2001
  • Our Raid on Castle Mistmoore went off fairly well yesterday. About 12-15 Champions were in attendance. We ported from Field of Bone thanks to Tipsy's Wizard Aandiwen. In tow we had a full group from Harbingers of Death. Took a while to get everyone organized and melee's bound in G Fay. After scaring some elves we made our way to Mistmoore and fought our way through elven bodies to the spot where Black Dire spawns. While we killed random mobs some Brothers of Darkness showed up to join in the fun. By the end we had about 4 or 5 full groups of high levels. Unfortunately that damn Black Dire took forever to pop. At around 7PM he did show his face and was swiftly dispatched to his makers. Tsumar of HoD won the roll on the pelt he drops as Ishy and I looked on in jealously. The plan was to get 3 pelts one for each of us. I took a break for dinner as did many I suspect. Upon logging back in I found that Ishy was the only Champion to remain along with a handful of HoD guys and a good Iksar named Labbats. We tried once more with a much smaller group and at that point I lost my internet connection due to some personal issues. I believe it was unsuccessful. There was much good to take from our first raid off of our home continent but also some dissapointment. For a guild of 120 members it's too bad we can never muster more than 15 out to events.
