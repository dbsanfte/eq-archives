Zealot's Khukri
By Reptilo
   If you have arrived at this point then you are truly not a hatchling anymore.
    In the next test offered to you Speak with the Lord Qyzar. In response to his question answer him the year of the birth is 371 ag and the name of the father is Rile. The Lord has dispatched a knight named Grizzle to seek the Kromdul fortress named Chalp. Grizzle was supposed to map out Chalp. To obtain the Zealots khukri you must obtain the Chalp diagram from Grizzle and return it and your knights khukri to the lord. Grizzle may be found sitting near the guards in the Warsliks Woods. To obtain the Chalp diagram Grizzle requires you to retrieve 3 spells in the Woods. 2 of them are found in the giant fortress close by, I believe off of sapling giants. They are labeled a misscribed Gate and misscribed lifetap if I recall correctly. The third is located at the lake in WW. Follow the river to where it opens into a lake, swim out North and West and dive to locate the last spell. If you must the location is: +4332/+4215. Take the 3 spells and purchase a shovel in Cabilis, hand the 4 items to Grizzle who then rewards you with the Chalp diagram. Take the diagram and your knights khukri and hand them in to receive your Zealots khukri, a mighty blade indeed.
