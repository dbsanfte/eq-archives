Curscale Buckler
By Scarlax

Starts in: Cabilis East
Who: Drill Master Dal
Estimated Time: 3 hours
Minimum Level: 5
Quest Items: Bone Shard, Giant Blood Sac, Goblin Scout Beads, Sabertooth Kitten Canine
Additional Zones: Lake of Ill Omen, Swamp of no Hope, Warslilks Woods

Talk to Drill Master Dal in Fortress Talishan and Mention that you are a new recruit and he will hand you a Partisan Pack, a 6-item backpack.

Fill it with one Giant Blood Sac of the Giant Leech, Scout Beads from Goblin Scout, one Sabertooth Kitten Canine, and three Bone shards. Combine and return it to him and he will reward you with a Curscale Buckler.
