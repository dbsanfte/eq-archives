Shackle of Rock
By Shaozin
   Note : You can start this quest whenever you like, but I found Feign Death was necessary before I could finish it. Approx lvl 17.

   Talk to Master Niska in the Court of Pain about the Shackle of Rock, and she tells you that she will give you the shackle if you bring her back the fists of Talon Southpaw. You may want to buy the book about Talon from the Court Chronicler if you have not already, and go ask the Bar keep in the Tink and Babble about him. Seems he was almost impossibly-fast with that left hand. Here's the catch ; he had two left hands, explaining his lightning speed. Time to get those fists.

Fist One
Dropped off a Sarnark Courier in the Lake of Ill Omen. Normally spawns near the windmill and along the lake area around there. Just OOC in the zone and let people know that you are looking for one and it should go pretty smooth.

Fist Two and Three
The third fist is held by the Skeleton that the Great Oowomp will summon if you give him five gold. The Great Oowomp is on the Ogre Island, where the boats stop in Timorous. Here is the catch though, the hand is only the thumb. The thumb is a container, in which you must place and combine, his fingers. His fingers, named 'A Withered Finger X', where X is a length, drop off the Fingered Skeleton in Kurns. I hope you hunted here and managed to pick some up. Camp the Fingered Skeleton until you have all four fingers.

Note: The fingers are 2.5, 3, 3.1 and 4. The fingered Skeleton spawns in the basement of Kurns Tower.

Now to head out to Timorous. Get a map of Warsilk Woods, if you don't already know, and head to The Overthere (safest way is to head north along the zone line then when you reach the beach go west along the shore, keeping the water to your right. When you reach a wall swim around the wall hugging it until you reach the other side of the beach. Stay there until you see the boat dock and swim to it. Make sure you get on away from the dock. This boat will take you to Timorous. After you zone in, stay on the boat until it arrives at the Ogre Island.

Note: While waiting in The Timorous Deep, try to forage a riles sand coin which is needed for your shackle of bronze quest. It will save you a bit of time and you will knock out parts of 2 quests in one shot.

Arriving at the Ogre Island, don't get back on the boat straight away. Head towards the Great Oowomp, using sneak and trying to avoid other Ogres. I did this at level 19, and everything except the Oowomp conned blue. Get as close as possible to the Oowomp, and then Feign Death. Then, get five gold ready, un-feign and quickly give it to the Oowomp. If you un-feign with sneak on, you will have a few seconds before he and the other ogre near by aggro. Give him the cash, and re-feign. He summons a skeleton. After the skeleton as popped up, un-feign again, with sneak on, and ask him who Talon Southpaw was. Follow the cues and get your fist. Feign again. These guys are weak, and if you are fast, shouldn't lose much health. Get up, run to the docks, FD to get them off you, and rise and repeat until you are on the boat back to The Overthere. Stick the four fingers into the container you just got from the Skeleton and combine them. You will have the second fist after combining. On the way back to Overthere, keep your eyes open for the Bandit shipwreck, when you see it, jump off the boat, and then head for approx -5000, 3000, and the Iksar Master . Find him, ask him what are the Fists of Talon Southpaw, and then offer to return it. Take the bag, put the gloves, claw, mandible, and sash in it, combine them, and give him the bag back. You will have the Third fist after combining.

The four items are:
Excavators Claw - This drops off Burynai Excavators in Kurn's Tower, and in the Pit.
Excavators are fairly easy for their level. Around 16ish. Intact Mandible - This drops of Skulking Brutling, in Warsilk Woods. Around level 12, easy kill. Drop is semi-rare though, it may take awhile.
Sparring Gloves of a Goblin Pit Fighter - These drop off Goblin Pit Fighters, a semi- rare spawn in Warsilk Woods. Head to any set of Goblin ruins and kill everything that spawns (you may need some help for this) until you get the Pit fighter.
Torn Sash from a Young Ronin - Head to the Oasis, I have found him here more often then Southern Ro. Once again OOC the zone and let them know you are looking for him.
