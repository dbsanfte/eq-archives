Claw of the Scout
By Ishy Drackos

Now onto the next quest in the series. Talk to Elder Patriarch Ixthal and...

You say, 'i wish to continue training'

Elder Patriarch Ixthal says 'That's the spirit. For your next task we will require you to show your skills at locating a [missing member] of the Patriarchs of Cabilis.'

You say, 'what missing member'

Elder Patriarch Ixthal says 'His name is Welgaz and he tends to roam the continent in search of a perfect training area. We have not heard back from him in a while now and we grow worried. You must now go forth and find him. Tell him I have sent you to find him.'

Our lost little lizard is hiding out in warslik woods. Trotting over to Location -983.07, -1483.17, 275.60 you find the hungry lost iksar.

You say, 'ixthal sent me'

an injured traveller says 'Oh, has he now? I can't return yet, I do't have the strength. If you were to [help] me I think I might be able to make it back though.'

You say, 'i will help you'

an injured traveller says 'Good. I have not eaten in a while so I will ask you to get me some Legion Lager and Noodles for my journey back.'

The recipes for the items in question are:

Legion lager: blood water, giant blood sack, deadbone barley. and bottle.

Noodles: water, egg, and flour in an oven.

After feeding his face our lizard says: 'You have returned with what I asked for. Here, take this note back to Ixthal and let him know I am safe and will be making the return trip soon.'
