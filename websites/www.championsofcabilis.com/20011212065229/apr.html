APRIL

Monday, April 30th, 2001
  • Was a good weekend for the Champions. On Saturday we took a couple gropus into Droga for some good old fashioned Goblin killing. For many it was there first experience on a dangerous dungeon crawl. I think they learned a lot. There were a few casualties at the end sadly. Thanks to Zardossk and Bigole for sucking up their deaths without complaint. Sunday saw myself, Bhayne and Xakanis joining a pickup raid on the Dragon Lady Vox. We joined about 30 other adventurers and stormed the beasts lair. Unfortunately her waves of AOE damage of 500HPs quickly reduced our forces and people began to flee. Bhayne and Xakanis boldly gave their lives. I ended up gating to avoid death. I was without ftp access to our site for a few weeks but am back taking care of things again. Cleaned up the members page a little today, added a few new members. If you aren't on the members list please let me know and I'll add you. Also I added Sasraaz' Quest writeup for Leech Life, the lvl 49 Shadow Knight Spell Quest. Good reading for all you SK's out there.
     Thursday, April 26th, 2001
  • Today marked the one year anniversary of Cradossk's creation. It is also the one year anniversary of Kunark. I'm sure many of our older members will be seeing the anniversaries of their characters in the next little while. Congrats to all Iksar on the first year of our existance in Everquest. We have come a long way!
     Wednesday, April 25th, 2001
  • The past few days were glorious for the Champions of Cabilis. Tuesday night saw a group of Champions 10 strong and made up of some of our mightiest members slaying Dragoons. That's right the hated soldiers from Overthere who had dispatched so many of us in their time would meet their makers on this night. Do to some excellent pulling by Kodmid and Chable we were able to get them in singles. With 10 Champions ranging from high 30's to low 50's we dispatched them with little casualties. A great time was had by all! Welcome to new members Jawz and Msisy! I updated the 6th Cudgel Upgrade Quest with some new info today as well, this is still unsolved but I'm working hard on it.
     Tuesday, April 24th, 2001
  • My apologies for the lack of updates over the last few weeks. I'm back for good now and should have time to work on the site again. Big Thanks to Scarlax for his Fishing/Gambling event on Saturday. Turnout was decent at about 20 Champions. Congrats to Chabasrn for winning the fishing tourney. He caught 2 - 17lb fetid bass. YUM! YUM! He was rewarded with a 10 shot sow potion for his efforts. Some great duels took place also. Hempire and myself squared off and I was able to prove the might of the Shaman. Thanks for taking it easy on me Hemp! I missed out on the gambling and still haven't heard who won that.
