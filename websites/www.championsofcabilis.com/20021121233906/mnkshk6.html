Shackle of Bronze
By Kwanlei

This quest originates in Cabilis, but has more involvement with "an Iksar master" in the Timorous Deep.

In order to initiate the quest, go to Master Niska (in Cabilis, located near Master Glox) and state that "I will be your personal courier." She will hand you a Tin Box which weighs 8 pounds, and request that you take it to Master Rinmark, who in fact identifies as "an iksar master." Master Rinmark is located on the chess-board island in the Timorous Deep. He stands meditating atop a mountain peak on the island (you should have no problem finding him as the island is quite small).
Once the Tin Box is handed in to "an iksar master" (Master Rinmark) the following reaction will be triggered:
"An Iksar master opens the box to reveal a variety of pebbles. How grand!! My pebbles!! Now I can continue with my rock garden!! Would you please [assist old Rinmark in completing this rock garden]?" You say : "I will assist old Rinmark in completing his rock garden" "An iksar master pours the pebbles from the box into his pocket and hands it to you. Take my box, Fill and combine within my box the following: a Radiant Meteorite, Frontier Fools Gold, Petrified Redwood, Rile's Sand Coin, Sulfur Stone, Fragment of Tektite, Plains Pebble, Jade Magma, Heart of Ice and an Unholy Coldstone. Return the Full Tin Box to me and I shall reward you with a Shackle of Bronze."

The components are easy. This will probably be one of the most tedious and boring quests you will ever do.

Radiant Meteorite - Foraged in the Burning Woods. This item is not no drop so anyone can do this and give it to you.

Frontier Fools Gold - There are indeed 2 different forageable gold nuggets. Our desired nugget is will identify as Frontier Fools Gold. The one we DON'T want and WILL NOT combine in the box is Genuine Gold Nugget. A good way to check is to try to sell it. If it sells then it’s the wrong one.

Petrified Redwood - Foraged in Warsliks Woods. This item is not no drop so anyone can do this and give it to you.

Rile's Sand Coin - Forage in the Timorous Deep. This item is No Drop.

Sulfur Stone - These drop from Drolvarg Scavengers. Scavengers can be found roaming the middle of the zone. Walk straight outwards once you zone in from Lake of Ill Omen. They're really known as "Scarlet Stones," but with the identify spell these are indeed Sulfur Stones.

Fragment of Tektite - Pick up 2 carnelian gems. Grab a friend to cast the invisibility spell on you, or buy an invisibility potion in west cabilis. At the entrance to the evil outpost (coming in from overthere) you will see two gargoyles. Run into the tunnel with invisibility and at the other end of the tunnel will appear two more gargoyles, with one of having the name "Tektite." He wants a replacement for his eyes, and seeks two Carnelian gems. Run up behind him, feign, and hit sneak until it works. Get back up (DO NOT HAIL HIM) and hand him the two gems in separate slots. You will receive a piece of "Tektite."

Plains Pebble - Foraged in any of the Karanas. This item is not no drop.

Jade Magma - Kill burynai miners in Frontier Mountains. This item is No Drop.

Heart of Ice - In Lake of Ill Omen, these drop from "Ice bone skeletons" near the lake shore. This item is No Drop.

Unholy Coldstone - In permafrost caverns, a goblin priest will drop them. Priests are random common spawns deeper inside permafrost. This item is No Drop.
