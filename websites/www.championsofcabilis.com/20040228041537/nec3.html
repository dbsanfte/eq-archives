Apprentice Skullcap Rank Three
By Shaozin

   Minimum Level 10. Ask Master Rixiz in the Temple of Death about acquiring the Third Rank Skullcap. He hands you a Glass Canopic and asks that you place inside of it the brain of a Sarnak Crypt Raider (found in the Lake of Ill Omen), and immediately combine it with Embalming Fluid. This requires the brewing and smithing skills. To brew embalming fluid you need: 1 rubbing alcohol (can be bought). 1 bone granite powder (combine in a forge 1 bone chip + 1 bone chip + 1 granite pebble + 1 forging hammer) and 2 bog juices (brew snake scales + water flask + bottle). Combine it to get a preserved Sarnak Brain (no drop, lore), and return it to him, and he will reward you with the Apprentice Skullcap - 3rd rank.
