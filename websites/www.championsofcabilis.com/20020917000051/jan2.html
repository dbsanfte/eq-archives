Tuesday January 22nd, 2002

  • Sasraaz is now one step from completing his Epic! After a good turn of fortune and the guidance of Cazic I'm sure, he ended up only needing to kill the final nasty mob. We have a tentative date of Sunday Feb 3rd at this point as the day that BoD will be available to assist. There are no Iksar Shadowknights on the server with their Epics and Sasraaz shall have his! We are all super proud of the guy, WTG Sasraaz! Krikk from I'm not sure which server has done an excellent Greemist walkthrough so I added that to the quests section. Updated the Pic of the Week and the members list a bit too.
     Thursday January 17th, 2002
  • Had quite a successful raid on the Hole last night. I good turnout by the Champions as well as a good turnout by Ssizzel's guild Controlled Chaos and we had ourselves a raid. Sasraaz got one of his epic parts and the raid hit a stall not long after when many softskins ran in fear of the single cleric that was staying. Well in true Iksar spirit we pushed on to High Scale Kirn and I was able to loot the ring and move on to within 2 steps of my epic. Thanks so much guys!
     Monday January 14th, 2002
  • The Kaesora raid was quite a success from all accounts. 3 groups went down and laid waste to the place. Congrats to all who came out. Our recruiting drive has really paid off as of late. Some great new members have joined the ranks of the Champions, welcome new Iksar. This week's pic of the week is courtesy of one of those people, Vasudan Broussard. I got a good laugh out of it, enjoy.
     Thursday January 10th, 2002
  • Was a quiet Kael raid last night though there were a couple drops including Silk Legs. A small but powerful group of Champions and Allies did the bank area and the chanter house. Perhaps doing Kael every week burned people out a little bit, not sure. We have a Kaesora raid on Saturday at 3PM PST/6PM EST for mid levels. The mid levels have been crying out for a raid for some time now so Ishy has put a good one together. Try to come out regardless of your level as this will be a fun event. Kaesora is a great dungeon.
     Friday January 4th, 2002
  • I hope everyone had a great New Year's. Mine was great, I partied my ass off and as a result the page didn't get updated this week. Bad Crad! Was a good week for CoC. Sasraaz put together an amazing Raid on Kael. More than 40 people turned out to beat up Giants in the Arena. Sasraaz and I are planning a raid on the Hole on January 16th at 5PM PST/8 PM EST to get farther on our Epics. Please come out and help the brethren. We have had a lot of new faces join over the past couple weeks. I won't try to name them all but looks to be a good crop of recruits. Veinos, the funniest Iksar alive, sent me the pic of the week. It's the Osama Bin Laden MoB, lookout!
