Mystic Scale Sleeves
By Cradossk
   To receive your Mystic Scale Sleeves Zazlan asks for a Frenzied Gnawer's Tail, a Froglok Treated Plank, Banded Sleeves, and a Saphire. The Frenzied Gnawer's Tail drops off the Frenzied Gnawer in Kaesora, the dungeon in the Field Of Bone. Zone into Kaesora and get yourself a map from EQ Atlas HERE. Go to the first drop, levitate down to the 2nd floor (not the 3rd!) then continue south. Head into the room labelled #7 on the map. It has a caved-in floor. Drop down to the bottom of this room too and take the southeast exit from the room. Follow the path till you come to a circular ramp. Follow it and you'll be heading North. Follow this till you come to a circular room keep going North past a second circular room keep going and it will lead back up to the entrance to Kaesora. The trick is to work this route over a couple times killing all the spawns and wanderers you see. Within a couple circuits of this you should fight the Frenzied Gnawer. Grab his tail and Gate out.
   The Froglok Treated Plank drops in the Froglok Village in the Swamp of No Hope. It drops of a Froglok Repairer. To get him to spawn set up a camp at the first platform there. Kill every frog you see and he'll spawn eventually. Be careful to root running frogs here because even though they may be low 20's MOBs, they gang up like you wouldn't believe if you let them run. The Champions found this out on our raid of Frog Town. The Banded Sleeves can be bought or sold. The Saphire is bought off the Jeweler in East Cab.
   Hand in these items to Zazlan and he rewards you with: Mystic Scale Sleeves AC 8, WIS +2, STA +5, AGI +5. This is a SWEET item! It's a bit tough getting the components for this one but these sleeves will do you fine into your late 40's.
