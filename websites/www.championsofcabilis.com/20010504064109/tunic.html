Mystic Scale Tunic
By Cradossk
   To receive your Mystic Scale Tunic Shezlie asks for Watchman's Spectacles, a Nectar of Isolation, Banded Tunic and a Star Ruby.

The Watchman's Spectacles are found on Raider Watchmen, level 20 Ogre Warrior types, in the Timorous Deep. The glasses are a semi rare drop and they can be found on the main island the boats from Overthere and Oasis go to.

The Nectar of Isolation is also found in the Timorous Deep zone. Thanks to Miracht Riversfriend for the heads up on this one. It is bought off a vendor on a semi-hidden island. The location is n12000 n2000 approximately. The entrance is underwater you go through 2 tunnels then you should see the inn. He's the Dwarf Vendor. The Nectar is NO DROP so don't bother getting your friends to buy you one.

The Banded Tunic can be bought or made and the Star Ruby can be bought in Cabilis.
   Overall this is a bit of an improvement on Cabilis Scale but for my money I'd wear a Bloodstained Tunic with it's +3 WIS over this one.
