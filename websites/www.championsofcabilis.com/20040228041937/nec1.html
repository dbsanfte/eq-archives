Apprentice Skullcap Rank One
By Shaozin

   Minimum Level 4. Ask Master Xydoz in the Tower of Death about the Apprentice Skullcap. He wants you to prove yourself by bringing him the brains of four Sarnak hatchlings. After you prove yourself, he asks for an additional 4 brains, and he will reward you with the Apprentice Skullcap - First Rank. You get good experience for this quest.
