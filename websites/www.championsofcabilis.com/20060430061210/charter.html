We are those that call Cabilis our home. Once a forgotten race, we have now arisen to claim back our lost honor. With the recent incursion into our lands by the other races, we can expect many perils on our journeys as we venture forth into the lands...........We are: The Champions of Cabilis!

RULES:

  • The Champions of Cabilis Reside on the Nameless Server. Your character must be on the Nameless
  • Except in special cases prospective members must be of the 15th rank to be considered.
  • Obide by Verant's 'Play Nice Rules', ie. no KSing, Ninja Looting, etc.
  • Help your brethren when they call for help, provided you are able.
  • Conduct yourself as a 'Champion' would at all times.
  • No swearing in the guild channel, if you wanna /tell your friends stuff like that fine, but not in the public window.
  • Share information with your brethren, quests, strategy and so forth.
  • No begging.
  • Do not use /anon. Use /roleplay instead. You should be proud of your guild. /roleplay allows people to see your guild tag but not your level and class.
  • No account sharing! You were invited into the Champions because of you, not your friend who knows your account info. If you are showing someone the game with your character go anon.
  • Have fun when you play !
  • We Shall only accept members of the one true strong race - The Iksar
  • We shall try to RP in a manner that shows that we feel our land has been stolen and we wish it returned. This doesn't include senseless insults to players of other races beyond the point of good fun. Use your judgement here, if people are getting generally pissed off then relax, apologize and move on.
  • Whenever possible we shall provide discounts on items to fellow members. This can be done through trade skills or surplus items. Overcharging a guild member is a serious crime if you are in the know when you do it.
