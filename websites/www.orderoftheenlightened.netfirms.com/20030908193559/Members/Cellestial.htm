Cellestial's Profile

Level 24 High Elf Enchantress
.

Level 24 High Elf Enchantress

 

Some years ago in Felwithe, a local Jewelry maker & his wife were happy to announce the birth of a beautiful daughter.  The little family was happy in Love; they wanted for nothing & were content with their lives. All seemed pleasant & perfect, & the child Cellestial knew only that she was Loved & happy.

As often seems the case, their happiness was not to last.  A raiding party of Orcs from Crushbone put an end to their idyllic scene in a wave of blood & horror.  When the attack had passed little Cellestial found herself alone.

The good Clerics of Tunare took her into their care & raised her best they could, but they could not return the light to her eyes nor the sparkle to her heart, though she was beginning to laugh & smile once more.  Upon investigation they found the young girl only had but one dream: to become a great Jewelry Maker like her father & perhaps refind elsewhere the happiness she lost.  Alas, Cellestial was a pauper child, & could not hope for the funds that would enable her to become a master at an expensive trade such as Jewel Craft.

Being kind as the Mother of All, her clerics took Cellestial to be apprenticed as an Enchanter in their guild.  This, they reassured her, was the best way for her to achieve her goal in being a Jewelry Maker.  As an Adventurer she would be able to get the funds she required, & the Enchanters gifts would enable her to make baubles that ven her esteemed father could not.  Amid a mix of tears of sorrow & happiness, the clerics bid the now bubbly Cellestial anon.

Cell wished to thank Tunare & her loyal followers for their love & support by herself following the Mother of All, but she found that she could not.  Her heart had led her too much already, & she new she followed the Queen of Love in her essence.  Although her faith holds to Erolissi Marr, Cell always keeps close ties to those of Tunare... which can often be seen in the company she keeps.  For Celles is often found in the companionship of Tunare's Cleric, Shanahan Everheart.
