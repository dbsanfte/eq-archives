Tarril Arrowstorm- Ranger of Tunare

"He's a hopeless case."-Maesyn Trueshot, Master of the Ranger Guild, said while watching Tarril turn a rabbit into finely shredded powder.

 As a Ranger of Tunare, it is Tarril's duty to protect Nature and all who walk within her domain.

As Tarril, he blatantly disregards his duties and will attack Orc, Goblin, and Snow Bunny alike with wild abandon.  Tarril has a love of swords, swords, bigger swords, explosions, and slightly larger cutting devices known as swords.  Since his first experience with his guildmaster's Shortsword, Tarril has been on the lookout for bigger and better cutting equipment.

 It is this love that lead him to explore the lands of Norrath.  While on his journies, he discovered that making people laugh was often a good substitute for a good sword, and, since he was stone broke, decided to run headfirst into a brick wall in order to give himself brain damage so he could freak out other people and make them laugh.  His love of swords didn't go away, but something happened then..... Tarril started acting strange.  Really strange.

 Later, after several seasons of lonely adventuring, with only his Blackened Alloy longsword to keep him company, Tarril came across what he had been looking for his whole life.  A small group of friendly adventurers were forming a permanent guild, and Tarril, inquisitive as he is, decided to join them.  And since then it's been good times and fun adventures (not to mention big swords) for him.....
