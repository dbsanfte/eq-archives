Here is the information I have so far on the Mage Epic Quest. It is in the order that you should complete it.

The quest starter is in Lake Rathtear. Speak to Rykas (apx loc 1000, -700). He will start you on the path to many quests that lead to the final reward. I have made the rest of the quest in a flow chart style since it is vast and requires many sub-quests.

==================================
Legend:

[NPC] (Location} - Quest name

or

Subquest parts:
[Item] (Location) - Who/What/Where
