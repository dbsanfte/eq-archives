Bastok Mines Quests

Beauty and the Galka
Client: Cornelia & Parraggoh
Location: House, Bastok Mines [H-5]
Items: Zinc Ore
Reward: Bronze Knife

This quest starts by talking to Talib at Warehouse 2, Port Bastok [F-6]. When you talk to him for the first time, Cornelia shows up. After the intermission, give Talib a Zinc Ore [which drop from any worm type mob] to get the Palborough Mines log. At this point, you will want to go talk to Parraggoh in the Bastok Mines area.

Blade of Darkness - incomplete
Client: Gumbah
Location: House, Bastok Mines [J-7]

Talk to Gumbah then go to Palborough Mines. Once here, head to the boat dock and ride the boat to get to the Zeruhn Mines. When you get off the boat, there will be an intermission where you are given Chaosbringer.

You should level up the Warrior job some now if you have not already done so to at least level 5 as this is the only job you will have that can equip the weapon and it isn't that good. You will need to kill at minimum 100 mobs with Chaosbringer, which must provide the killing blow. Also the game isn't going to tell you when you have killed enough enemies with it, so you should keep track somehow using either /echo or /p.

Fallen Comrades
Client: Pavvke
Location: House, Bastok Mines [I-7]
Items: Silver Name Tag
Reward: 550G

Silver Name Tags drop off random mobs in the Palborough Mines. Also this quest seems to require some level of fame in order to do so you most likely will not be able to do it immediately.
