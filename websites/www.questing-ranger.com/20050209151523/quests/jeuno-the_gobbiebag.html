The Gobbiebag Part I
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: none
Items: Dhalmel Leather, Linen Cloth, Peridot, Steel Ingot
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 35 Items

Summary: Bluffnix promises to enlarge your pack if you bring him the following: some dhalmel leather, a steel ingot, a square of linen cloth, and a peridot.

All items needed for this quest can be obtained through crafting or purchased at the auction house.

Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part I
Items: Mythril Ingot, Ram Leather, Turquoise, Wool Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 40 Items

Summary: Bluffnix promises to enlarge your pack if you bring him the following items: some ram leather, a mythril ingot, a quare of wool cloth, and a turquoise.

The Gobbiebag Part III
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part II
Items: Gold Ingot, Painite, Tiger Leather, Velvet Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 45 Items

Summary: Bluffnix promises to enlarge your pack if you bring him the following: some tiger leather, a gold ingot, a square of velvet cloth, and a painite.

Of the gobbiebag quests, this one will cost the most if you are buying the ingredients from the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part IV
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part II
Items: Cermet Chunk, Darksteel Ingot, Goshenite, Silk Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 50 Items

Summary: Bluffnix promises to enlarge your pack if you bring him the following: a cermet chunk, a darksteel ingot, a quare of silk cloth, and a goshenite.

If you managed to complete the third quest in this series, the ingredients for this quest are significantly cheaper at the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part V
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part IV
Items: Bugard Leather, Moblinweave, Paktong Ingot, Rhodonite
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 55 Items
Title: Greedalox

Summary: Bluffnix promises to enlarge your pack if you bring him the following: a square of bugard leather, a paktong ingot, a quare of Moblinweave, and a rhodonite.

Like with the other Gobbiebag quests, the items for this can all be found for sale in the materials section of the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part VI
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part V
Items: Balloon Cloth, High Quality Eft Skin, Iolite, Shakudo Ingot
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 60 Items

Summary: Bluffnix will make your pack even bigger if you bring him the following: a square of balloon cloth, a shakudo ingot, a high quality eft skin and an iolite.

Like with the other Gobbiebag quests, the items for this can all be found for sale in the materials section of the auction house. Unlike other gobbiebag items, these ones are all mob dropped, and there are no known crafting recipes that will create them. Balloon Cloth drops from Diremite Dominator in Pso'Xja. The High Quality Eft Skin is dropped by Efts and Tartarus Efts in Bibiki Bay. The Iolite is drop by Arch Corses in Attohwa Chasm. The Shakudo Ingot is dropped by Fomor Ninjas in Lufaise Meadows, Misareaux Coast, Phomiuna Aqueducts and other Chains of Promathia zones as well as by Goblin Junkman in Newton Movalpolos.
