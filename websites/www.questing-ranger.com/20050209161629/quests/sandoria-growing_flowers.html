Growing Flowers
Client: Kuu Mohzolhi
Location: Phoenix Perch Inn, North San d'Oria [G-8]
Items: Marguerite
Reward: Moghouse Exit Upgrade [San d'Oria]

Summary: Give the tiny Mithra some flowers so she can plant them in Queen Leaute's garden.

This quest does not actually show up in the quest log until you complete it. Just give Kuu Mohzolhi a Marguerite after talking to her. This can be grown with gardening or purchased from the Auction House under Alchemy Materials.
