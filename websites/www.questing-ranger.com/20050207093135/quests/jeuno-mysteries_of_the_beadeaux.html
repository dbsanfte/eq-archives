Although these are really two quests, both need to be completed to gain access to the Qulun Dome.

Mysteries of the Beadeaux I
Client: Sattal-Mansal
Location: Tenshodo, Lower Jeuno [J-8]
Requirements: Tenshodo Membership
Items: Quadav Charm
Reward: Coruscant Rosary

Summary: Bring back a Quadav charm and Sattal-Mansal will trade a coruscant rostery for it. Only then can you enter the depths of Beadeaux.

The Quadav Charm drops from theDe'Vyu Headhnter in Beadeaux. The reward for this quest gives you one of the two key items you need to enter the Qulun Dome in Beadeaux.

Mysteries of the Beadeaux II
Client: Sattal-Mansal
Location: Tenshodo, Lower Jeuno [J-8]
Requirements: Tenshodo Membership
Items: Quadav Augury Shell
Reward: Black Matinee Necklace

Summary: Bring back a Quadav augury shell and Sattal-Mansal will trade a black mantinee necklace for it. Only then can you enter the depths of Beadeaux.

The Quadav Augury Shell drops from theDe'Vyu Headhnter in Beadeaux. The reward for this quest gives you one of the two key items you need to enter the Qulun Dome in Beadeaux.
