The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Wizard epic quest for the Staff of Four.

Quest Start

Hail and talk to Solomen in the Temple of Solusek Ro to start the quest. Say 'I agree' to him to get a note.

Story of the Four Wizards

  1. Go to Erudian. Give the note to Camin.
  2. Give 1000PP to Camin.
  3. Go to Halas. Buy Ro's Breath from the merchant Dargon.
  4. Go to Erudian Give the Ro's Breath to Camin. He will change the potion then return it to you.
  5. Go to Halas. Give the potion to Dargon to spawn Arantir Karondor.
  6. Hail Arantir Karondor to get Arantir's Ring.
  7. Go to Felwith. Give Arantir's Ring to Challice to get another ring.
  8. Go to Halas. Give the ring to Dargon to respawn Arantir Karondor.
  9. Hail and talk to Arantir Karondor to learn his story and hear about the other three wizards. Say 'Who is the gnome?' to get a letter.

Blue Crystal Staff

Go to Kedge Keep. Kill Phinigel Autropos to get the Blue Crystal Staff.

Gnarled Staff

Go to Karnor's Castle. Kill Venril Sathir to get the Gnarled Staff.

Staff of Gabstik

  1. Go to Butcherblok Mountains. Give the note to Kandin Firepot.
  2. Say 'what oil' to get the Golem Sprocket.
  3. Go to the Plane of Fear. Give the Golem Sprocket to the Broken Golem to spawn the Enraged Golem, which argos.
  4. Kill the Enraged Golem to get the Green Oil.
  5. Go to Butcherblok Mountains. Give the Green Oil to Kandin Firepot to get the note and the Staff of Gabstik.

Staff of Four

  1. Go to Halas. Give the ring to Dargon to respawn Arantir Karondor.
  2. Hail and talk to Arantir Karondor, who will ask for the three staves.
  3. Give the Blue Crystal Staff, Gnarled Staff and Staff of Gabstik to Arantir Karondor to get a pack.
  4. Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro. Give the pack to solomen to get the Staff of Four.
