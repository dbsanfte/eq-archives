The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Enchanter epic quest for the Staff of the Serpent.

Quest Start

Go to Erudin. Hail and talk to Stofo Olan to start the quest.

Copy of Notes

  1. Go to Qeynos. Talk to Reania Jukle and say 'Where can I find the ink of the dark?' to get the Empty Ink Vial.
  2. Go to Lower Guk. Charm the Ghoul Scribe. Give the Empty Ink Vial to the Ghoul Scribe to get the Ink of the Dark.
  3. Kill A Ghoul Archmage [in Lower Guk] for a Shiny Metallic Robe.
  4. Go to Akanon. Talk to Rilgor Plegnog.
  5. Give a Shiny Metallic Robe to Rilgor Plegnog to get A Mechanical Pen.
  6. Go to West Karana. Talk to Chrisln Baker.
  7. Give Chrislin Baker Paper and Quill to get a Bandit Sash, which spawns Thracken Griften.
  8. Kill Thracken Griften to get the White Paper.
  9. Go to Erudin. Give the Ink of the Dark, A Mechanical Pen, and White Paper to Stofo Olan to get the Copy of Notes.

A sarnak imitator

  1. Go to Burning Woods. Give a Sarnak Imitator the Copy of Notes to get Jeb's Seal.
  2. Say to a Sarnak Imitator 'I need a sack' to get an enchanter's bag.

Modani Qu'Loni: Master of Illusions

  1. Go to The Overthere. Give Jeb's Seal to Modani Qu'Loni, and he will return it to you.
  2. Say to Modani Qu'Loni 'I need a sack' to get an Enchanter's Bag.
  3. Go to Chardok. Kill Prince Selrach Di'zok for the Head of a Prince.
  4. Go to Felwith. Give the Head of a Prince to Joren Nobleheart to get the Chalice of Kings.
  5. Go to East Cabilis. Kill Vessel Drozlin to get the Xolion Rod.
  6. Go Ogguk. Talk to Bozlun to get a note.
  7. Give the note to Brokk Boxtripper to get the apology.
  8. Give Brokk's apology to Bozlun to get the Snow Blossoms.
  9. Go to Neriak. Kill Verina Tomb [in the Cleric Guild] to get Innoruuk's Word.
  10. Place the Chalice of Kings, Xolion Rod, Snow Blossoms, and Innoruuk's Word in the bag that Modani Qu'Loni gave you and press the combine button.
  11. Go to The Overthere. Give the combined item bag to Modani Qu'Loni to get the first piece of the staff.

Mizzle Gepple: Master of Enlightenment

  1. Go to Ak'Akanon. Give Jeb's Seal to Clockwork VIIX, and he will return it to you.
  2. Say to Clockwork VIIX 'I need a sack' to get an Enchanter's Bag.
  3. Go to the Oasis of Marr. Kill Cazel for the Spoon.
  4. Go to The Overthere. Kill a Mesa for The One Key.
  5. Go to the Crypt of Dalnir. Look behind the bed for The Lost Scroll.
  6. Go to the Plane of Sky. The Book of Charm and Sacrifice can be found on a table.
  7. Place the Spoon, The One Key, The Lost Scroll, and the Book of Charm and Sacficice in the bag that Mizzle Gepple gave you and press the combine button.
  8. Go to Ak'Akanon. Give the combined bag to Clockwork VIIX [Mizzle Gepple] to get the second piece of the staff.

Nadia Starfeast: Master of Charm

  1. Go to Firiona Vie. Give Jeb's Seal to Nadia Starfeast, and she will return it to you.
  2. Say to Nadia Starfeast 'I need a sack' to get an Enchanter's Bag.
  3. Say to Nadia Starfeast 'What diamond?' to get the diamond.
  4. Go to Kaesora. Charm the Spectral Librarian. Give the diamond to the Spectral Librian to get the Enchanted Diamond.
  5. Say to Nadia Starfeast 'What sapphire?' to get the sapphire.
  6. Go to Skyfire Mountains. Charm Faelia Goldenwing. Give the sapphire to Faelia Goldenwing to get the Enchanted Sapphire.
  7. Say to Nadia Starfeast 'What ruby?' to get the ruby.
  8. Go to the City of Mist. Charm the Wraith of Jaxion. Give the ruby to the Wraith of Jaxion to get the Enchanted Ruby.
  9. Say to Nadia Starfeast 'What emerald?' to get the emerald.
  10. Go to The Overthere. Charm Impaler Tzilug. Give the emerald to Impaler Tzilug to get the Enchanted Emerald.
  11. Place the four enchanted gems in the Enchanter's Bag that Nadia Stareast gave you and press the combine button.
  12. Go to Firiona Vie. Give the combined bag to Nadia Starfeast to get the third piece of the staff.

Polzin Mrid: Master of Phantasms

  1. Go to The Hole. Give Jeb's Seal to Polzin Mrid, and he will return it to you.
  2. Say to Polzin Mrid 'I need a sack' to get an Enchanter's Bag.
  3. Go to the Plane of Fear. Kill the Wraith of a Shissir to get the Head of a Serpent.
  4. Go to The Hole. Kill the Ghost of Kinde to get the Essence of a Ghost.
  5. Go to the Plane of Hate. Kill a Forsaken Revenant for the Essence of a Vampire.
  6. Go to The Field of Bone. Kill The Tangrin for the Sands of the Mystic
  7. Place the Head of a Serpent, Essence of a Ghost, Essence of a Vampire, and Sands of the Mystic in the Enchanter's Bag that Polzin Mrid gaive you and press the combine button.
  8. Go to The Hole. Give the combined bag to Polzin Mrid to get the fourth piece of the staff.

Staff of the Serpent

Place the four pieces of the staff into the Enchanter's Bag that a Sarnak Imitator gave you.
