A Candlelight Vigil
Client: Ilumida
Location: House, Upper Jeuno [G-8]
Items: Candle
Reward: Flower Necklace
Title: Activist for Kindness

Summary: Ilumida wants to light a candle in memory of her husband. Bring her one from the Temple of the Goddess.

Talk to Rouliette in the Temple of the Goddess to initiate the "Candle-making" quest then complete it to get a candle [key item.] Return to Ilumida and talk to her.

Candle-making
Client: Rouliette
Location: Temple of the Goddess, Upper Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: A Candlelight Vigil [started]
Items: Lanolin Cube
Reward: candle
Title: Believer of Altana

Summary: Bring a lanolin cube to Rouliette so she can make you a candle.

Lanolin Cubes [ex] drop from Battering Rams in La Theine Plateau. Once you have one, trade it to Rouliette for the candel [key item]
