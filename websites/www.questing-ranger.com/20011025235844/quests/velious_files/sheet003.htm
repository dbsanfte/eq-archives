Forest Stalker's Armour

These quests are all given out by Gragek Mjlorkigar in Kael Drakkal. You need to have ally faction with him to do these quests.

Forest Stalker's Coif

  1. Go to Temple of Veeshan. Kill dragons [the ones not in the Testing Hall] to get the Ancient Tarnished Chain Coif.
  2. Obtain 3 Crushed Coral from random Velious Mobs.
  3. Give Gragek Mjlorkigar the Ancient Tarnished Chain Coif and 3 Crushed Coral to get the Forest Stalker's Coif.
