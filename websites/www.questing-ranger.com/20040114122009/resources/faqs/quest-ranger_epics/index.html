Swiftwind and Earthcaller: Introduction

A great evil is corrupting both the lands and the animals, who are tuning on people in some cases. Both rangers and druids are working together to stop this. While the druids work to cleans both the lands and the animals of the corruption, your task is to sever Innoruuk's link then make a strike against the god of hate himself, then restore the balance of nature.

Although it is possible to do portions of the quest out of order, this walk through assumes that you complete each part before moving on to the next section. Also, if at any point in the quest you find that an NPC does not want to talk to you, you most likely have a faction issue with that NPC and need to raise it. You must be at minimum Level 45 to start this quest and to perform the final turn ins to receive the rewards.

The "ID" text that appears on item screens is what is shown in the chat box when the spell "Identify" is used on the item. Items which have this as a right-click feature include the Silvery Mask and the Star of Eyes. This text as been added to appear here through the use of image editing software.
