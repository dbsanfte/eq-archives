Learning a Skill: Cooking

This section is a work in progress as I am still learning the culinary trade myself. Also you cannot fail the cooking tests. Just give the NPC the appropriate food item after talking to him, and you automatically pass it.

Hard-Boiled Egg [0-6]
Crystal: Fire
Ingredients: Bird Egg, Distilled Water

If you start out cooking early in your character's life, you can get most of the ingredients yourself while you gain EXP thus making this recipe one of the more inexpensive. Fire Crystals drop off the orcs around San d'Oria [just make sure you have a signet up] and Bird Eggs drop off the bird-type mobs in La Theine Plateau that are common exp mobs before going to Valkurm Dunes. The only thing you should need to buy is the Distilled Water. Alternately, Lizard Eggs may be used in place of Bird Eggs but the trivial raiting when using them is 4 instead of 6.

Orange Juice [7-10]
Crystal: Water
Ingredients: Saruta Orange x4

Saruta Oranges can be purchased from the regional vendor that controls the Sarutabaruta Region, which may be Windurst in most cases. While this may seem inconvient for those living in San d'Oria and Bastok, you should consider that the Culinary Guild itself is in Windurst and you will need to make regular trips to it. Orange Juice generally does not sell enough to make it worthwhile, and it doesn't stack. If you are playing a mage job and are at your younger levels, you can still get a use out of it. Stock up on a stack or two of water crystals and enough oranges for amount of crystals you have. Now go join an exp group and make juice as you need it.

Cooking Test #1: Salmon Sub [8-10]

Sliced Sardine [11]
Crystal: Wind
Ingredients: Bastore Sardine

While this recipe often gets recommended sooner, I'm not that fond of it. Wind crystals typically sell for a lot on my server. The amount of gil I get for selling Sliced Sardine doesn't cover what I could have made had I just sold those wind crystals instead. It is however a rather convient recipe though as you buy Bastore Sardines at the Fishing Guild in Windurst.

Selbina Butter [12-20]
Crystal: Ice
Ingredients: Rock Salt, Selbina Milk

This is a relatively cheap recipe, but you will fail a lot at first. Don't worry about this too much as you will have more butter than you know what to do with even before you hit 20 in the skill. I highly recommend having a storage mule for this one just so that you have room for other things. Ice crystals are among the cheaper ones to buy. Rock Salt can be purchased at the grocery store in South San d'Oria. Selbina Milk can be purchased either at Selbina or from the regional vendor of the city that controls the Zulkheim region. When you hit the 18-20 range, go do the cooking test.

Apple Juice [17-20]
Crystal: Water
Ingredients: Faerie Apple X4

Similar to Orange Juice, but with a bit more sale value. Even then I general only make it when I want want juice while I am in an exp party. Faerie Apples can be purchased at the grocery store in San d'Oria when the city is in first place. I remember there being other vendors but I forget off the top of my head. This is more of an alternate recipe to do if you find that you really don't have storage space for yet more butter.

Cooking Test #2: Pea Soup [18-20]

Insect Ball [21-29]
Crystal: Earth
Ingredients: Distilled Water, Little Worm, Millioncorn

I recommend doing this one all the way to 29. Insect Balls, also known as "Insect Paste," sells fast. You do not even need to price it low in order to clear it out fast.

Pineappel Juice [29-30]
Crystal: Water
Ingredients: Kazham Pineapple x2

I normally don't like using a receipe for one level, but the next one just sucks that much. I cannot say how much you would get for these on the AH as I blew them all on a BCNM40 trip.

Cooking Test #3: Vegetable Gruel [28-30]

Meatball [30-35]
Crystal: Earth
Ingredients: Distilled Water, Hare Meat, San d'Orian Flour

You will want to do this receipe in San d'Oria and not Windurst [for the imaging synth] if you want to save some time here. San d'Orian Flour is not stackable and the best places to buy it is either from the Regional Vendor of the city that controls Ronfaure [most likely San d'Oria] or from the Grocery Store in San d'Oria if the city is not in 3rd place. The Culinary Guild Store sells it slightly cheaper usually but does not carry Distilled Water, which the Grocery Store in San d'Oria always carries. Hare Meat is also almost always available for sale at the San d'Oria AH due to a quest. The part that makes this receipe suck though is that although you will get a profit on this, Meatballs sell very slowly.

There are some recipes that you can make for the upper 30s, but most of the suck for one reason or another. Melon Juice is expensive to make and you'll be needing every Fire Crystal you can get soon for later receipes. I'd jump ahead to Pie Dough at this point, but don't forget the cooking test.

Cooking Test #4: Meat Mithkabob [38-40]

Pie Dough [35-42]
Crystal: Water
Ingredients: Rock Salt, San d'Orian Flour, Selbina Butter

Hopefully you have been saving the Selbina Butter as you're going to start to need it now. You will also most likely need at least one mule character if you do not already have one.

Apple Pie [43-48]
Crystal: Fire
Ingredients: Cinnamon, Faerie Apple, Lizard Egg, Maple Sugar, Pie Dough

Make sure you get imaging support for this one. You will fail a lot and get minimimal skillups without it. This is not fun. Also make sure that you use Lizard Eggs and not Bird Eggs when you first start making this as doing so will push the trival up to 50.
