Altana's Sorrow
Client: Virnage
Location: Bat's Lair Inn, Bastok Mines [J-8]
Reward: Scroll of Teleport-Dem
Title: Pilgrim to Dem

Summary: Virnage needs a bucket of divine paint to restore the statue of Altana to Her former glory. He advised you to look for it in a citadel on the Sauromugue Champaign.

Head to Garlaige Citadel to the area around G-8 and H-8 to look for a "???" spot. Once someone examines the spot, it immediately despawns but will respawn somewhere else in the same area. I wasn't sure if it was respawning immediately but even if it is not, the wait was not long. Once you have examined it, return to Virnage and talk to him again. You will now want to go to the Cathedral in North San d'Oria to talk to Eperdur, who will give you the spell.
