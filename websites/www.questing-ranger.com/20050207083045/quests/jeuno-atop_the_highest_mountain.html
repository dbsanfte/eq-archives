Atop the Highest Mountain
Client: Maat
Location: Grand Duke Palace [H-5]
Requirements: Level 51+, In Defient Challenge
Items: Round Fragicite, Square Fragicite, Triangle Fragicite
Reward: Level Cap of 60
Title: Summit Breaker

Summary: Collect three stones of frigicite from fiend-infested caverns in the north. Bring them to Maat to further expand your innate limits.

This one requires an alliance and all takes place in Xarcabard. You will need sneak to get around everywhere in Xarcabard, and invisible as well when you get to the area with the demons. To complete this your alliance must work your way to three different caves, kill the Notorious Monster inside, then inspect the ground at the back of the cave to get a Key Item:
- Round Fragicite: Boreal Tiger [I-5]
- Square Fragicite: Boreal Coeurl [J-4]
- Triangle Fragicite: Boreal Hound [G-10]
