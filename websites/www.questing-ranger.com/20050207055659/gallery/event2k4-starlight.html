Special Events: Starlight Celebration 2004

Event Activities

Moogle's Activity: Letter Delivery
This can be done in any of the three starting cities. It does not matter which you do it in as it can only be one once. Talk to the moogle and it will give you clues as to where to find three children that need your help. Moogle will only tell you how many children you need to find in each zone, and there are three total. When you find a child, you will be given a letter to deliver to the Smilebringer. You will need to make note of the name of the child and the letter as it is completely random as to which children you are assigned. Once you have three letters, give them to the Smilebringer [NPC wearing a Dream Hat] and correctly identify which child gave each letter to get the Dream Hat.

Holiday Tree Activity
There are three holiday trees, one for each of the starting cities, and it is possible to get all of them. However this activity can only be done once per game day for any city. For this activity, you need to talk to an NPC in the port section of one of the towns:
* San d'Oria: Farmaoquaix [near Airship Dock Entrance]
* Bastok: Tree Talker [near the Circus Tents]
* Windurst: Rhi Perejha [near zone to West Sarutabaruta]

The NPC will then have you go talk to one of the Market Moogles that can be found near the Residential Area. For the San d'Oria and Bastok ones, I was able to run from the Quest NPC to the Moogle and back in plenty of time. For Windurst though, I found it necessary to change my homepoint to the Port Windurst Residential Area, then Warped, talked to the Moogle and ran to the NPC. The guy I was grouped with whiled doing the Windurst one used flee and he barely made it in time. If you do not make it back in time, the NPC will have you make more deliveries though and give you more time to complete it.

Smilebringer's Activity: Present Delivery
If you talk to the Smilebringer, you will be given a present to deliver. Equip the Dream Hat and give it to any child in the NPC. Once per game day, you can get a [rare/ex] present for free from the Smilebringer to delivery. Once your Event Fame reaches a certain level, go to your mog house and talk to your moogle. A Special Present under one of your holiday trees. If you leave the Mog House and use the Special Present, you will get the Dream Hat +1.
