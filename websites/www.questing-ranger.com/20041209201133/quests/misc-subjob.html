Subjob Quests

There are two subjob quests and completing either will allow you to have a subjob. The mobs that are fought for Elder Memories are easier in level and the drops can be obtained while in exp parties in Valkurm Dunes. While the mobs for The Old Lady are noticebly harder to kill, the items needed drop more frequently. The Magicked Skull in paticular for the Elder Memories quest can be rare, even with a thief treasure hunter in your party.

Elder Memories
Client: Isacio
Location: Waterfront, Selbina [G-10]
Requirements: Level 18+, The Old Lady not started
Items: Crab Apron, Damselfly Worm, and Magicked Skull
Reward: Able to set support jobs

The Mhaura version of this quest is "The Old Lady." You can only do one of these two quests but not both, nor can you have both quests active. While the three drops for this quest can be obtained while experiencing in Valkurm Dunes, the Magicked Skull can be rare. The alternate place to obtain the Magicked Skull is Gusgen Mines. However you will need to bring your own group if attempting to obtain it from here instead.

The Old Lady
Client: Vera
Location: Outside Warehouse, Mhaura [G-10]
Requirements: Level 18+, Elder Memories not started
Items: Wild Rabbit's Tail, Cup of Dhalmel Saliva, Bloody Robe
Reward: Able to set support jobs
