A Discerning Eye (Kazham)
Client: Swift
Location: Airship Dock, Kazham [G-7]
Requirements: Airship Pass
Items: Dropped Item
Reward: 500G
Title: Discerning Individual, Very Discerning Individual, Extremely Discerning Individual

Summary: You have been asked to return dropped item to its rightful owner. The passanger should be on the next airship flight.

Swift gives you the Dropped Item [key item] when you start the quest. He then shows you a random npc and you need to memorize the race, gender, face type and armor. Get on the next airship and look for the npc you were show. When you found the correct one, talk to the npc and choose the option to give the item. If you selected the correct NPC, you'll get the reward.

The title you normally get from doing this quest is Discrning Individual. If you do these quests enough times, you can get the other two titles instead.
