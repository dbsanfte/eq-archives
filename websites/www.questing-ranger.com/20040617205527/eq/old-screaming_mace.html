Screaming Mace FAQ
by Crydee Ossiriand, Torvonnilous Server
http://www.questing-ranger.com
Version: 2004.06.01

This quest takes place entirely in Crushbone. You will first need to get a Dwarven Ringmail which drops off Emperor Crush and a Bracer of Battle that drops off the Orc Warlord. Once you have both of these, you'll want to head over to the slave section of Crushbone.

You say, 'what about ringmail?'
a dwarven smith says 'The king was the last person I heard of who was wearing what sounded like my ringmail. Return it to me and I will reward you with a fine weapon I forged myself.'

*Give Dwarven Ringmail to a dwarven smith*
a dwarven smith says 'AHA!! My ringmail!! My, you ARE a resourceful one! Here is your reward as promised. It is exquisite, if I do say so myself. You might also wish to tell the priest in the cell over there that the king is dead. I am sure he will be pleased to hear that and might even be able to help you further. Farewell! It's time for me to make my escape!'

You say, 'the king is dead'
an elven priest says 'Dead you say? My goodness! Did you manage to slay the warlord too? If you provide me with proof of his death, I can enchant the mace the dwarf gave you as payment. But, I will require the proof first!'

*Give Bracer of Battle to an elven priest*
an elven priest says 'Erollisi be praised! You slew the orc lord! Well, here is a token of my appreciation! Should you wish to have that mace enchanted, just hand me the mace and my token and I shall uphold my end of the bargain!'

*Give Dwarven Mace and Prayer Cloth of Tunare to an elven priest*
an elven priest says 'As promised, here is your mace. Let it not fall into dark hands as its power is hidden until combat when it will reveal its true nature. Fare thee well!'
