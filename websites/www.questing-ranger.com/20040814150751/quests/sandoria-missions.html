San d'Oria Missions

1.1: Smash the Orcish Scouts
Mission Orders: Hunt Orcs lurking outside San d'Oria and bring back one of their axes.

1.2: Bat Hunt
Missoin Orders: Exterminate bards from King Ranperre's Tomb, and bring back proof of your deeds.

1.3: Save the Children
Mission Orders: Rescu the children held captive in the Orcish camp in Ghelsba outpost.

2.1: The Rescue Drill
Mission Orders: Participate in the Temple Knights' rescue drill out on La Theine Plateau.

2.2: The Davoi Report
Mission Orders: Retrive the latest scouting report from the Temple Knight scout somewhere in Davoi.

2.3: Journey Abroad
Mission Orders: Visit the faraway nations of Bastok and Windurst and receive official recognition as an adventurer.

Journey to Bastok
Mission Orders: Survey Bastokian mining technology and bring back some mythril sand out of Palborough Minds. An official named Pius will instruct you further.

Journey to Windurst
Mission Orders: Our the giant monster that dwells deep inside the Yagudo realm of Giddeus.

3.1: Infultrate Davoi
Mission Orders: The scout in Davoi needs the help of an adventurer.

3.3: Appointment to Jeuno
Mission Orders: Head to the Embassy of San d'Oria in Jeuno to fill your appointed post.

4.1: Magicite Mission Orders: Seize the three types of magicite from Davoi, Beadeaux, and Castle Oztroja to prevent the Shadow Lord's return.
