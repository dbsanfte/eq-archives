Special Events: Summerfest 2004

Event Activities

Moogle's Dungeon Test Part #1: Onoko Yukata / Omina Yukata
You need to form a party with someone that is not the exact same race/gender combination as yourself. Both you and your partner need to talk to the moogle. After doing so, both need to talk to the moogle again to be transported into an random dungeon. You both will be level caped to Level 1 and be given an teleport key. You will also have perminate invisibility and sneak. Your objective is to find any "GoalPoint" hidding somewhere in the dungeon. After locating it, drop invisibility then activate the GoalPoint. It is not necesary for you and your partner to find the same one. At this point, you can use the teleport key to return to town then trade the item you got with your partner for the item he/she got. Now give your partner's item to the Moogle to get the Onoko Yukata [if Male] or Omina Yukata [if Female].

Moogle's Dungeon Test Part #2: Lord's Yukata / Lady's Yukata
This works exactly like the first test, with a few exceptions. You will need to have four turn in items, each from a different race. You cannot use your own race/gender combination nor can you use the race/gender combination that you used for the first test. Other than this, the dungeons function exactly the same. You also need to be wearing your robe from the first test.

Moogle's Dungeon Test Part #3: Summer Fan / Festive Fan / Chocobo Ticket
After you have obtained the second robe, you can go through the dungeon with any race/gender combination except your own. Also combinations that you used previously are valid again. You only need to turn in one item at this point and the reward will be a random amount of either Summer Fans, Festive Fans or Chocobo Tickets.

Goldfish Scooping
The Moogle quests are no longer available at this point. Merchants have been added to San d'Oria, Bastok and Windurst that sell Super Scoops and will give out Goldfish Bowls. The bowls are equiped in the range slot and the scoops in the ammo. Goldfish Scooping itself works just like fishing, but don't appear to need actual fishing skill. There are three kinds of fish that can be caught, all worth different points:
* Tiny Goldfish: 1 point
* Black Bubble-Eyed: 3 points
* Lionhead: 10 points

Points are accumulated and can be exchanged for prizes as follows:
* 5 points: 1 Super Scoop
* 15 Points: Bag Of Fireworks [Random]
* 30 Points: Colored Drops [Random]
* 50 Points: Mystery Bag [Sprit Mask Fireworks]
