Give a Moogle a Break
Client: Moogle
Location: Mog House
Requirements: Bronze Bed in House
Items: Beetle Ring, Power Bow
Reward: Mog House Safe increased to 60 items
Title: Mog's Kind Master

Summary: Your moogle wants to take a power bow and a beetle ring to his family for the holidays. He sure would be happy if you found them for him.

The Bronze Bed must be placed in the house using Layout. Also you can only get this quest on Sundays after the weekly conquest update. Additionally your moogle needs to be at home and not at a rent-a-room while the update is going on for you to get the quest.
