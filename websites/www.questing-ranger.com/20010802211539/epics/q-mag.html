The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Magician epic quest for the Orb of Mastery.

Quest Start

Talk to Rykas in Lake Rathetear and say 'I wish to learn of the tale of Magi'kot' to get a Token of Mastery.

Words of Magi'kot

  1. Go to West Commonlands. Give the Token of Mastery to Jahsohn Aksot.
  2. Talk to Jahsohn Aksot and say 'What task' to find out what you need to do.
  3. Go to Kithicor Forest. Kill either a Dread Wolf or an Enraged Dread Wolf to get the Torn Page of Magi'kot pg. 1
    [Note: These mobs only spawn at night time.]
  4. Go to Estate of Unrest. Kill the Tentacle Terror for the Torn Page of Magi'kot pg. 2
  5. Go to Lower Guk. Kill the Bloodthirsty Ghoul for the Torn Page of Magi'kot pg. 3
  6. Give the three Torn Page of Magi'Kot to Jahsohn Aksot to get the Words of Magi'kot.

Power of the Elements

  1. Go to Butcherblock Mountains. Find Walnan, who is a rare spawn. Hail and talk to Walnan to find out what you need to do next.
  2. Go to Castle Mistmoore. Kill the Gypsy Dancers for the Power of Wind.
  3. Go to Lesser Faydark. Kill the Faerie Guards for the Power of Earth.
  4. Go to Solusek's Eye [Sol A.] Kill either the Blazing Elemental or Lava Elementals for the Power of Fire.
  5. Go to Kaesora. Kill either the Enraged Ravener or Famished Ravener for the Power of Water.
  6. Go to Butcherblock Mountains. Give the Power of Wind, Earth, Fire and Water to Walnan to get the Power of the Elements.

Words of Mastery

  1. Go to Najena. Hail and talk to Akksstaff, who will tell you what you need to do next.
  2. Kill the Female Dark Elf Magicians in Najena to get the Torn Page of Mastery Water, Torn Page of Mastery Earth, Torn Page of Mastery Fire and Torn Page of Mastery Wind.
  3. Give the Torn Page of Mastery Water, Torn Page of Mastery Earth, Torn Page of Mastery Fire and Torn Page of Mastery Wind to Akksstaff to get the Words of Mastery.

Power of The Orb

Give the Words of Magi'kot, Power of the Elements, and Words of Mastery to Rykas to get the Power of the Orb.

Power of Fire

  1. Go to Skyfire Mountains. Hail and talk to Jennus Lyklobar to find out what you need to do next.
  2. Go to City of Mist. Kill Neh'Ashirr to get the Torch of the Elements.
  3. Go to Burning Woods. Kill the named wurms to get the Burning Embers.
  4. Go to Kedge Keep. Kill Undertow to Blazing Wand.
  5. Go to Skyfire Mountains. Give the Torche of the Elements, Burning Embers, Blazing Wand, and Power of the Orb to Jennus Lyklobar to get the Power of Fire.

Power of Earth

  1. Go to Firionia Vie. Hail and talk to Tiblner Milnik to find out what you need to do next.
  2. Go to Plane of Hate. Kill Magi P'tasa to get Staff of Elemental Mastery: Earth.
  3. Go to Burning Woods. Kill Slixin Klex to get Dirt of Underfoot.
  4. See below if you do not have the Broom of Trilon.
  5. See below if you do not have the Shovel of Ponz.
  6. Go to Firiona Vie. Give the Staff of Elemental Mastery: Earth, Dirt of Underfoot, Broom of Trilon and Shovel of Ponz to Tiblner Milnik to get the Power of Earth.

Power of Water

  1. Go to Dagnor's Cauldron. Hail and talk to Jinalis Andir to find out what you need to do next.
  2. Go to Kedge Keep. Kill Phinigel Autropos to get the Staff of Elemental Mastery: Water.
  3. Go to The Overthere. Kill Captain Rottgrime to get the Tears of Erolisi.
  4. Go to East Karana. Kill Trabul Earthstrider to get the Rain of Karana.
  5. Give the Staff of Elemental Mastery: Water, Tears of Erolisi and Rain of Karana to Jinalis Andir to get the Power of Water.

Power of Air

  1. Go to the Plane of Sky. Hail and talk to Kihun Solstin to find out what you need to do next.
  2. Kill mobs on Island 7 to get the Crown of Elemental Mastery.
  3. Go to The Hole. Kill the Elemental Warriors to get the Elemental Binder.
  4. Go to South Karana. Kill Quillmane to get the Pegasus Feather Cloak.
  5. Go to the Plane of Sky. Give the Crown of Elemental Mastery, Elemental Binder and Pegasus Feather Cloak to Kihun Solstin to get the Power of Air.
    [Note: The Master of Elements will spawn at this point, so make sure that you have the Power of Fire, Earth and Water before you turn the items in.]

Orb of Mastery

  1. Hail and talk to the Master of Elements, then say 'I am the one' to him.
  2. Give the Power of Fire, Power of Earth, Power of Water and Power of Air to the Master of Elements to get the scroll Summon Orb of Mastery.
  3. Scribe the spell into your spell book.
  4. Cast the spell to summon the Orb of Mastery

Sub-Quest: Broom of Trilon

  1. Hail and talk to Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro to start the quest.
  2. Buy a Star Ruby from a Jewlrey Merchant.
  3. Go to East or North Karana. Kill a Griffon to get a Griffon Feather.
  4. Get a Broom from a Dark Elf Magician [in Najena] or alligator [in Cazic Thule.]
  5. Go to Rathetear Mountains. Kill a Cyclops for Cyclops Toes.
  6. Give the Star Ruby, Griffon Feather, Broom and Cyclops Toes to Vira to get the Broom of Trilon.

Sub-Quest: Shovel of Ponz

  1. Hail and talk to Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro to start the quest.
  2. Buy a Ruby from a Jewlrey Merchant.
  3. Go to Castle Mistmoore. Kill the Gargoyles to get a Gargoyle Eye.
    [NOTE: These are more common inside the castle.]
  4. Get a Shovel from a Dark Elf Magician [in Najena] or alligator [in Cazic Thule.]
  5. Go to Rathetear Mountains. Kill a Hill Giant for Hill Giant Toes.
  6. Give the Ruby, Gargoyle Eye, Shovel and Hill Giant Toes to Vira to get the Shovel of Ponz.
