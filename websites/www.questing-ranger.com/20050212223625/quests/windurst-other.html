A Crisis in the Making
Client: Ranpi-Monpi
Location: Culinarians' Guild, Windurst Waters [D-9]
Items: Off Offering
Reward: 400G

Summary: When preparing the food offering as a sign of Windurst's friendship with Giddeus, Ranpi-Monpi accidently ixed a liquid that Yagudo hate. Quickly recover the tainted offering from the alter at Giddeus before ruffling some Yagudo feathers.

Head to the underground treasure room in Giddeus, and look for the alter. Inspect it to get the Off Offering [key item] then return to Windurst and talk to Ranpi-Monpi.

A Discerning Eye (Windurst)
Client: Pygmalion
Location: Airship Dock, Port Windurst [M-7]
Requirements: Airship Pass
Items: Dropped Item
Reward: 500G
Title: Discerning Individual, Very Discerning Individual, Extremely Discerning Individual

Summary: You have been asked to return dropped item to its rightful owner. The passanger should be on the next airship flight.

Pygmalion gives you the Dropped Item [key item] when you start the quest. He then shows you a random npc and you need to memorize the race, gender, face type and armor. Get on the next airship and look for the npc you were show. When you found the correct one, talk to the npc and choose the option to give the item. If you selected the correct NPC, you'll get the reward.

The title you normally get from doing this quest is Discrning Individual. If you do these quests enough times, you can get the other two titles instead.

A Pose by Any Other Name
Client: Angelica
Location: Hostelry Room, Windurst Waters [F-10]
Item: desired clothing based on job
Reward: Ancient Blood, Angelica's Autograph
Title: Super Model

Summary: Angelica wants you to model for her next painting, but your taste in clothes doesn't match her current mood. Go find the clothes she likes and come back wearing them... before she changes her mind.

When you get this quest, Angelica will tell you what she wants you to wear. There is a time limit on this quest, so you will need to do it fast. The Ancient Blood is a painting that you can put up in your mog house, and Angelica's Autograph is a key item.

Creepy Crawlies
Client: Illu Bohjao
Location: Dhamel Farm, Windurst Woods [I-6]
Items: Crawler Calculi x3 or Silk Thread x3
Reward: 600G
Title: Crawler Culler

Summary: The dhamel farmer wants you to cull the crawler pests that eat up all the dhamel food. Bring her three silk threads or three crawler calculi as proof of your efforts in order to gain a reward.

Silk Thread drops from any crawler type mob, and Crawler Calculi drops specifically from those in East and West Sarutabaruta. Because of the high value for Silk Thread on the Auction Houses for clothcrafting, it is not recommended that you turn in the silk. Instead turn in all Crawler Calculi to Illu Bohjao and sell the Silk Thread to other players.

In a Pickle
Client: Chamama
Location: Rarab Tail Hostelry, Windurst Waters [F-10]
Items: Smooth Stone
Reward: Bone Hairpin, 200G

Summary: The weighting stone used to make the Rarab Tail Hostelry's specialty dish, "Rarab Tail Pickles," was broken. Chamama wants you to find her a replacement stone that is just the right size, shape, and weight.

Smooth Stones [rare/ex] drop from Cawlers in East and West Sarutabaruta. When you get one, trade it to Chamama. However she will usually not accept it as being good thus forcing you to go find another. The first time you successfully complete this quest, she gives you a Bone Hairpin and 200G every time after it. [It is necessary to talk to Chamama before each turn in.

Mihgo's Amigo
Client: Nanaa Mihgo
Location: Cat Burglar's Lair, Windurst Woods [J-3]
Items: Yagudo Necklace x4
Reward: 200G
Title: Cat Burglar Groupie

Summary: If you want to befriend the infamous Cat Burglar, then you'll have to give her something she likes.

Yagudo Necklaces drop from random Yagudos in Giddeus. While the financial reward is small for what you turn in, this is still a good quest to do as it gives both Windurst and Tenshodo Fame.

Something Fishy
Client: Tokaka
Location: Fisherman's Guild, Port Windurst [C-8]
Items: Bastore Sardine
Reward: 70G

Summary: Tokaka can tell the flow patters and temperature of the ocean currents by looking at certain fish. Bring her any Bastore sardines that you catch so she can help keep the fishermen informed as to where to drop their lines.

Although this quest is repeatable and Tokaka gives you more gil from Bastore Sardines than what they cost from the Fishing Guild, you must zone between each turn in and talk to her before doing the turn in on top of it. The combination of these two things makes using this quest to earn fame rather tedious.

Star Struct
Client: Koru-Moru
Location: Manor, Windurst Walls [E-7]
Requirements: The Postman Always K.O.'s Twice
Items: Meteorite
Reward: Compound Eye Circlet

Summary: Koru-Moru wants you to find him a rough black stone, for some spaced-out reason.

Meteorites [rare/ex] drop from Crawlers in East Sarutabaruta. Once you find one, trade it to Koru-Moru.

Stardust
Client: Baldric
Location: Darksteel Forge, Metalworks [G-9]
Items: Sundsand
Reward: 300G

Summary: Baldric wants you to bring him a pinch of Valkurm sundsand for one of his experiments. It is said to only appear during dust storms.

Sundsand can be found on the Siren Sands beach near the boat during sandstorms. Look for the "???" and examine it. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Other Miscellaneous. Once you have the Sunsand, just trade it to Baldric. This quest can be repeated but it is necessary to leave the zone and return, then talk to Baldric again to reinitiate the quest.

The Postman Always K.O.'s Twice
Client: Ambrosius
Location: Auction House, Windurst Waters [J-12]
Items: Damp Envelope, Muddy Bar Tab, Odd Postcard, Torn Epistle
Reward: 50G - 500G

Summary: The auction house's postman was knocked out by a monster (again). Ambrosius, who runs the auction house, wants help recovering all mail that was lost.

Turning in one of the lost main will result in a 50G reward and the amount increases as more lost mail is turned in simultaneously. The different pieces of mail can be found as follows: - Damp Envelope: Canyon Rarab, Tahrongi Canyon
- Muddy Bar Tab: Savanna Rarab, East Sarutabaruta
- Odd Postcard: Canyon Rarab, Tahrongi Canyon
- Torn Epistle: Savanna Rarab, West Sarutabaruta
