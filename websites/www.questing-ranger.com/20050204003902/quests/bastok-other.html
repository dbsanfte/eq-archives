A Discerning Eye (Bastok)
Client: Grin
Location: Airship Dock, Port Bastok [G-7]
Requirements: Airship Pass
Items: Dropped Item
Reward: 500G
Title: Discerning Individual, Very Discerning Individual, Extremely Discerning Individual

Summary: You have been asked to return dropped item to its rightful owner. The passanger should be on the next airship flight.

Grin gives you the Dropped Item [key item] when you start the quest. He then shows you a random npc and you need to memorize the race, gender, face type and armor. Get on the next airship and look for the npc you were show. When you found the correct one, talk to the npc and choose the option to give the item. If you selected the correct NPC, you'll get the reward.

The title you normally get from doing this quest is Discrning Individual. If you do these quests enough times, you can get the other two titles instead.

Beauty and the Galka
Clients: Cornelia & Parraggoh
Location: House, Bastok Mines [H-5]
Items: Zinc Ore, Palborough Mines Log
Reward: Bronze Knife

Summary: Bring a chunk of zinc ore to Talib of the Tenshodo in Parraggoh's stead. Monsters in Gustaberg are known to drop ore.

This quest is initiated by talking to Talib in Port Bastok [F-6,] then selecting yes during the intermission when Cornelia asks if you will help. Zinc Ore drops from any worm-type mob. It also can be mined or purchased at the Auction House under Goldsmithing Materials. Trade one to Talib to get the Palborough Mines Log [key item.] Now go talk to Parraggoh in Bastok Mines [H-5.]

Fallen Comrades
Client: Pavvke
Location: House, Bastok Mines [I-7]
Item: Silver Name Tag
Reward: 550G

Summary: Bring Pavvke the name tags of the Galka who lost their lives in the Palborough Mines twenty years ago.

Silver Name Tags drop from Quadav in Palborough Mines or can be puchased from the Auction House under Neck Armor. Once you have one, trade it to Pavvke.

Gourmet
Client: Salimah
Location: Trader's Home, Port Bastok [L-8]
Items: Sleepshroom, Treant Bulb, Wild Onion
Reward: 100G, 200G or 300G
Title: Mommy's Helper

Summary: Salimah is looking for a special ingredient o spice up the dish she is preparing.

If you talk to Rothais, in the same building, she will tell you which ingredient to turn in at the moment for the most amount of gil.

Treant bulbs 6:00-11:59 (200 gil) Wild onions 12:00-17:59(350 gil) Sleep shrooms 18:00-5:59(200 gil)

Shady Business
Client: Talib
Location: Tenshodo Warehouse, Port Bastok [F-6]
Requirements: Beauty and the Galka
Items: Zinc Ore x4
Reward: 350G

Summary: Talib of the Tenshodo is buying zinc. Bring him four chunks of ore to receive gil.

Zinc Ore drops from any worm-type mob. It also can be mined or purchased at the Auction House under Goldsmithing Materials. When you get four, trade them to Talib. The significance of this quest is that it gives both Bastok and Tenshodo fame.

The Quadav's Curse
Client: Corann
Location: House, Port Bastok [E-6]
Items: Quadav Backplate
Reward: Bronze Subligar

Summary: Corann wants to shut himself away from the rest of the world. Find him an item to hide in -- something big enough to hide his whole body.

The Quadav Backplate can drop from any Quadav in North Gustaberg, South Gustaberg or Konschatat Highlands. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Miscellaneous Beastmen-Made items. Once you have the item, trade it to Corann.

The Stars of Ifrit
Client: Agapito
Location: Air Travel Agency, Port Bastok [G-6]
Requirements: Airship Pass
Item: Carrier Pigeon Letter
Reward: 2100G
Title: Star of Ifrit

Summary: Agapito wants you to find out the meaning of the following words: "Under a full moon, toward the stars of Ifrit, a bird delivers a letter to the ship in the sky." Also, try to bring back this "letter" to satisfy Agapito's curiosity.

For this quest, you need to ride the San d'Oria - Jeuno Airship either way, during a full moon (90%+) and at night time (18:00 - 04:00.) When you meet this requirement, go up on the top deck and look for the "???" on the rail of the airship. Examine it to get the Carrier Pigeon Letter [key item.] Return to Agapito and talk to him.

Welcome to Bastok
Client: Powhatan
Location: Steaming Sheep Restaurant, Post Bastok [E-6]
Item: Shell Shield
Reward: Spatha
Title: Bastok Welcoming Committee

Summary: Great Powhatan's guest at the air travel agency. Wear a shell shield so he'll know who you are.

For this quest you need to get a Shell Shield, which drops from Young Quadavs. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Shields. You will then need to equip the shield, which can only be worn by L7+ warriors, red mages, paladins, beastmasters or samurai. After you equip the shield, enter the Air Travel Agency through the Arrivals door and talk to the person behind the counter. An intermission will play where you meet with Evrain. Return to Powhatan and talk to him.
