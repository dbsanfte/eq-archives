Threnody Guide

Renki [Skill Chains]:

With renki, the appropriate threnody is always the one that will debuff the mob for the element that the mages will be magic bursting on. For example on Liquefaction, you use Fire Threnody before the mages cast their spells. While the official hit book says to use Ice Threnody in my example, this would be if you wanted to magic burst with the threnody itself. Since threnodies themselves do no damage, there is no reason to try to magic burst one. Since level 2 renki are multi-elemental, it is best to ask the mages as to which element they intend to magic burst on then debuff for that. [If you don't have a good renki chart, I recommend the one available at Destiny Seekers.

Renki Level 1:
- Liquefaction: Fire Threnody
- Induration: Ice Threnody
- Detonation: Wind Threnody
- Scission: Earth Threnody
- Impaction: Thunder Threnody
- Reverberation: Water Threnody
- Compression: Dark Threnody
- Transfixion: Light Threnody

Renki Level 2:
- Fusion: Fire or Light
- Distortion: Ice or Water
- Fragmentation: Wind or Thunder
- Gravitation: Earth or Dark

Renki Level 3:
- Fusion/Fragmentation: Light
- Gravitation/Distortion: Dark

Songs & Spell Resists:

Threnodies only apply to a few of our own songs. If you find yourself needing to use them often, it might be time to work on instrument skills and equipment. Whenever possible, use a high quality instrument and make sure your skill for it is maxed out for your level. Secondly check around and see if there is additional charisma increasing gear that you can get, and change your subjob to white mage as this will give you more charisma than other worthwhile subjobs. Finally if L51+, try investing in the appropriate elemental staff for its hidden effect and add a equip line to your macro for the song to equip it.

They can also be used to help other casters to reduce the change of a resist with their debuffs! This should be mostly a factor when dealing with mobs that are resistant to the element type.

Ice Threnody:
- Bind
- Paralyze

Wind Threnody
- Gravity

Earth Threnody:
- Battlefield Elegy, Carnage Elegy
- Slow

Light Threnody:
- Foe Lullaby, Horde Lullaby
- Magic Finale
- Foe Requiem I - VI
- Maiden's Virelai

Dark Threnody:

- Sleep, Sleep II, Sleepga
