The Sand Charm
Client: Celestina
Location: Goldsmiths' Guide Salesroom, Mhaura [H-8]
Items: Sand Charm
Reward: Map of Bostaunieux Oubliette

Summary: Retrieve the sand charm Celestina's father carried. Your one clue: a sailor saw a pirate wearing it.

Talk to Blandine then talk to the two sailors on the dock Grimr and Zexu. It will be necessary to pay for a boat trip to get to them. Talk to Blandine again, then go to the Goldsmiths' Guild and talk to Celestina to get the quest. You'll now need to take boat trips between Mhaura and Selbina until pirates attack, which can only occur at night time. The Sand Charm [rare/ex] is a rare drop from the Crossbones. Once you have it, return to Celestina and trade the Sand Charm to her.
