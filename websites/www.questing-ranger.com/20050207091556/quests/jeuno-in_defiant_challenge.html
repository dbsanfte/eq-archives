In Defiant Challenge
Client: Maat
Location: Grand Duke Palace [H-5]
Requirements: Level 50
Items: Ancient Papyrus, Bomb Coal, Exoray Mold
Reward: Level Cap of 55
Title: Horizon Breaker

Summary: Apparently, your innate limits can be breached by acquiring the necessary items.

For this you need to fight three different mobs in three different zones for a rare/ex drop: - Ancient Papyrus: Lich, Eldieme Necropolis
- Bomb Coal: Exposure, Garlaige Citadel
- Exorary Mold: Exorary, Crawler's Nest

Of these three, the Exorary Mold is going to be the easiest. Although the drop rare seems rather low, even with a thief in the party, it is the one that you are most likely to be able to obtain while gaining exp. Both the Ancient Papyrus and Bomb Coal tend to require special genkai hunting trips.
