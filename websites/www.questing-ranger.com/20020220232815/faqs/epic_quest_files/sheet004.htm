Creating A Hardened Mixture

You will receive a four-slot container from Ella Foodcrafter to combine the ingredients in. Combine the following foraged ingredients in the Shiny Tin Bowl to get a "Hardened Mixture":

  • Everfrost: Chilled Tundra Root

  • Greater Faydark: Ripened Heartfruit

  • Innothule Swamp: Speckled Molded Mushroom

  • Misty Thicket: Sweetened Mudroot

There is no special method for foraging any of these items. You will need a minimum skill of 100 in foraging to be able to get them, but it will be easier to obtain them if your skill is at or close to 200.
