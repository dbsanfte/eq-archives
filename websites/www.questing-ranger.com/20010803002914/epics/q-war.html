The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Warrior epic quest for Blade of Tactics, Blade of Strategy and the Jagged Blade of War.

Quest Start

Talk to Kargek Redblade and Wenden in East Freeport to start the quest.

Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt

  1. Go to Lake Rathetear. Swim to the Flame Tower Island, then swim underwater and find the tunnel going underneath the island.
  2. Pick up the Unjeweled Dragon Head Hilt.
  3. Obtain a Diamond, Black Sapphire and Jacinth.
  4. Give the Unjeweled Dragon Head Hilt, Diamond, Black Sapphire and Jacinth to Wenden to get the Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt.

Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt

  1. Go to Timorous Deep. Go to the Chessboard Island.
  2. Pick up the Damaged Dragon Head Hilt that is at the Chessboard.
  3. Go to Plane of Fear. Kill Fright, Dread or Terror to get the Ball of Everliving Golem.
  4. Go to Dreadlands. Kill all the giants at the castle until the Mountain Giant Patriarch spawns.
  5. Kill the Mountain Giant Patriarch to get the Giant Monocle.
  6. Go to Frontier Mountains. Give the Giant Monocle to Mentrax Mountainbone in the giant fort to get the Rejesium Ore.
  7. Go to East Freeport. Give the Damaged Dragon Head Hilt, Ball of Everliving Golem and Rejesium Ore to Wenden to get the Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt.

Ancient Sword Blade [First]

  1. Go to Ocean of Tears. Talk to Denken Strongpick and he tells you the items he wants.
  2. Go to almost any merchant selling alcoholic beverages to buy a Keg of Vox Tail Ale.
  3. Go to Permafrost. Kill the Ice Giants to get a Block of Perma Frost.
  4. Obtain two Rebreathers from any player Tinkerer.
  5. Go to Ocean of Tears. Give the Keg of Vox Tail Ale, Block of Perma Frost and two Rebreathers to Denken Strongpick to get the Ancient Sword Blade.

Ancient Sword Blade [Second]

  1. Go to Chardok. Kill the queen to get the Ancient Sword Blade.

The Red Scabbard

  1. Go to East Freeport. Talk to Kargak Redblade. Say "I will do you a favor" to get the letter.
  2. Go to The Ferrott. Give the letter to Oknoggin to get the Tiny Lute.
  3. Go to East Freeport. Give the Tiny Lute to Kargak Redblade to get the book.
  4. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the book to Tenal Redblade to get the Frozen War Totem.
  5. Go to Permafrost. Kill the Goblin Wizards to get the Frozen Goblin Heart.
  6. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the Frozen War Totem and Frozen Goblin Heart to Tenal Redblade to get the Totem of Fiery War.
  7. Go to Emerald Jungle. Kill Severlious to get the Green Dragon Scale.
  8. Kill either Nagafin [SolB,] Ragefire [SolB,] or Talendor [Skyfire] to get the Red Dragon Scale.
  9. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the Totem of Fiery War, Green Dragon Scale and Red Dragon Scale to Tenal Redblade to get the Mark of the Sword.
  10. Go to Plane of Hate. Kill Maestro to get his hand.
  11. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the Mark of the Sword and Maestro's hand to Tenal Redblade to get another note.
  12. Go to Plane of Sky. Kill the Spiroc Lord to get the Spiroc Wingblade.
  13. Go to East Freeport. Give the note and Spiroc Wingblade to Kargek Redblade to get the Red Scabbard, along with instuctions on how to combine the swords.
