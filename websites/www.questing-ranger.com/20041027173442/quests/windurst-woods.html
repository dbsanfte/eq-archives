Windurst Woods Quests

Creepy Crawlies
Client: Illu Bohjaa
Location: Dhalmel Farm [H-6]
Items: 3 Crawler Calculi or 3 Silk Thread
Reward: 600G
Title: Crawler Culler

If you are killing the crawlers themselves as opposed to just buying the crawler calculi at the auction house, I recommend turning in the calculi to get the fame but sell the silk at the auction house as it goes for way too much to be used as a quest turn in.

Paying Lip Service
Client: Tapoh Lihzeh
Location: Apothecary [I-8]
Items: 3 Beehive Chips or 2 Remi Shells
Reward: 150G or 200G

This quest gets pointed out to me a lot as if I didn't know about it. However I just do not think the reward is worth the effort. I'm not sure about Remi Shells, but Beehive Chips go for a lot at the auction house on my server... especially when sold by the stack. Much like the Silk Thread for the Creepy Crawlies quest, you are better off selling Beehive Chips than using them for turn-ins.

The All New C-2000
Client: Kopuro-Popuro
Location: Manustery [H-10]
Items: Insect Wing, Rabbit Hide, Two-Leaf Mandragora Bud
Reward: 200G
Title: Cardian Tutor

The two-leaf mandragora bud is a rare/ex item so you will need to specifically camp for it. The rabbit hide and insect wing drop more commonly off their respective mobs, but can also be purchased from the auction house.

The Fanged One
Client: Perih Vashai
Location: Vashai's Residence [K-7]
Requirements: Level 30+
Items: Old Tiger Fang
Reward: Ranger's Necklace, Ranger Job
Title: The Fanged One

For this quest, you need to journey to the cave in the Sauromugue Champaign at [L-10]. If you enter the zone from Meriphataud Montains, as if coming from Windurst, follow the wall to your right until you reach a point that will allow you to go to the upper level area then head to the cave at [L-10]. Also the best way to do this part of the quest is to come here by chocobo as the tigers in and around the cave will not agro you will you are riding one. The chocobo will enter this cave, so take it to the back to where there is a tiger skeleton on the ground.

At this point, you will want to wait for all the tigers to leave then get off the chocobo. You may want to check the bones now to see if you can get the Old Tiger Fang. If not, just invis yourself and wait for the named tiger to die naturally. When you do get the fang, invis yourself and leave the cave.
