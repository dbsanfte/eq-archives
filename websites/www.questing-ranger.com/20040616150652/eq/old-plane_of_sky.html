Plane of Sky FAQ
by Crydee Ossiriand, Torvonnilous Server
http://www.questing-ranger.com
Version: 2004.06.01

These quests take place entirely in the Plane of Sky. The zone is sometimes referred to as Plane of Air due to the zone abbreviation for it being airplane. However these two places are not identical. Plane of Sky is the domain of Veeshan, and you will see references to the Wurm Queen in the zone. Plane of Air on the other hand is ruled by Xegony and is part of the Planes of Power expansion.

Quest items will drop off of mobs randomly, with some being rarer than others. These quests are completed more out of luck then anything else, so try not to get too discouraged. At the same time, I wouldn't put great hope or expectations of completing them as the rewards are more trophies than anything else at this point in time. After you have obtained all the items you need to turn in, visit the Quest NPC area to receive your reward.

Ranger Spirit

The Ranger Spirit can be found in the Quest NPC area of the Plane of Sky. To get to him, buy a Veeshan's Key from the Key Master on Isle One, then use it on one of the portals to go to the Quest NPC area. Keep in mind that once you go to this area, you will not be able to go to other isles.

You say, 'Hail, Ranger Spirit'
Ranger Spirit says 'Greetings, Crydee. Are you a true servant of the Pine?'

You say, 'I am a servant of the Pine.'
Ranger Spirit says 'Very well, my friend. In order to reach your true potential you must pass many tests. Relinin Skyrunner and Gordon Treecaller are here to perform these tests. Please choose one of these worthy teachers.'

Gordon Treecaller

You will first need to have the Ranger Spirit summon Gordon Treecaller for you.

You say, 'I choose Gordon Treecaller.'
Unfortunately the dialgue for this portion is missing as I had misplaced it at some point. The Ranger Spirit would say some things in reply to you then give you a book. You return the book to him and the Ranger Spirit summons Gorden Treecaller. The two NPCs great each other before the Ranger Spirit disappears for a while. When you hail Gordon Treecaller, he lists off the tests he has available for you to perform.

Thunderforged Earring

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the element of thunder.'
Gordon Treecaller says 'One of my greatest allies indeed. Thunder can be very powerful if used properly. Bring me a djinni statuette, a spiroc thunder totem, and a white gold earring. Then you will know the true power of nature.'

Items Needed:
- Djinni Statuette [Isle 5]: an Essence Harvester, a Soul Harvester
- Spiroc Thunder Totem [Isle 6]: Spiroc Expulser, Spiroc Revolter, Spiroc Walker
- White Gold Earring [Isle 6]: The Spiroc Guardian

Arydryidriyorn

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the blade.'
Gordon Treecaller says 'Very good choice, my blade is my best friend and yours will be as well if you can complete this task. Retrieve an efreeti long sword, an emerald spiroc feather, some bitter honey, and a circlet of brambles. Be careful with the honey - if it is tainted, the blade will not come to life.'

Items Needed:
- Bitter Honey [Isle 7]: Bazzazzt, Bizzazzt, Bzzt
- Circlet of Brambles [Isle 8]: Sister of the Spire
- Efreeti Long Sword [Isle 2, 5, 9]: Noble Dojorn, Overseer of Air, Hand of Veeshan
- Emerald Spiroc Feather [Isle 6]: Spiroc Arbiter, Spiroc Banisher, Spiroc Caller

Windstriker

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the art of the ranged attack.'
Gordon Treecaller says 'Many of the fools on Norrath don't realize how powerful a good bow can be. Many dread wolves have met their fate from my bow and you can have the same power if you can bring me back these items. Efreeti war bow, thickened nectar, sphinx tallow, and a shimmering pearl.'

Items Needed:
- Efreeti War Bow [Isle 2, 5, 9]: Noble Dojorn, Overseer of Air, Hand of Veeshan
- Shimmering Pearl [Isle 9]: Eye of Veeshan
- Sphinx Tallow [Isle 8]: Fatestealer Drake, Greater Sphinx, Heartsbane Drake, Windrider Drake, Undine Spirit
- Thickened Nectar [Isle 7]: Bzzazzt

Gordon Treecaller says 'You are a walking legend, Crydee. Please take Windstriker. It needs one such as yourself to wield it.'

Yannikil Trade-In

You say, 'I am looking for things to trade.'
Gordon Treecaller says 'I am searching for the sword called Yannikil. It seems when I gave this weapon out the blade did not perform as well in combat as it should. I wish to fix this embarrassment and replace the blade with one tested and proven by my own hands.'

This allows Yannikil to be traded in for the current reward of the test of the blade: Arydryidriyorn.

Relinin Skyrunner

You will first need to have the Ranger Spirit summon Relinin Skyrunner for you.

You say, 'I choose Relinin Skyrunner.'
Ranger Spirit says 'Relinin Skyrunner, you say. True to Tunare is she. Take this book and read it. When you feel you know it well, return it and Relinin will appear to test you.'
*Ranger Spirit gives you a book: Sisters and Life*

Day 327, Sisterhood Island.
Again my brother came to me. He asked that I return to the ways of Tunare and again, I refused. I have no desire to follow a god who carries with her such inequality between the sexes. For generations, the elves have always show favor to the male elven rangers. They ignore all of the good we had done for the forest of the Faydarks. So we left. We left for very good reason. He were are able to accomplish very much the same thing, with one exception. All of us are equal under the sun. Each of us knows our own worth and appreciates the freedom we have been given. Know thyself and know thy abilities so says the Mistress of the Island. Live by it.

*Give Ranger spirit the Sisters and Life book*
Ranger Spirit says 'Relinin Skyrunner, I summon thee.'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'I, Relinin Skyrunner, bid you good day.'
Ranger Spirit says 'Good luck, my friend.'

You say, 'Hail, Relinin Skyrunner'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'Greetings, Crydee. I can only assume the reason for my being summoned is that you wish to take the tests. Would you like to be tested in the body, in defense, in the element of earth or would you like to trade?'

Griffon Talon Necklace

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the body.'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'Very well. You must scour this plane and bring back to me these components: an auburn tessera, a ysgaril root, and a griffon talon. Make haste, and good luck.'

Items Needed:
- Auburn Tessera [Isle 1]: a thunder spirit
- Griffon Talon [Isle 4]: Gorgalosk
- Ysgaril Root [Isle 3]: Protector of Sky

Dark Cloak of the Sky

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the defense.'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'Defense it is. You must prove yourself worthy enough for the spiroc spirits to guard you. Bring back to me a mithril disc, a harpy tongue, and a fine velvet cloak. Good luck to you, Crydee.'

Items Needed:
- Fine Velvet Cloak [Isle 5]: Keeper of Souls
- Harpie Tongue [Isle 4]: an Avenging Gazer, a Gorgalask, a Sprited Harpie, a Watchful Guard
- Mithril Disk [Isle 3]: an Azarack

Earthshaker's Mantle

You say, 'I wish to be tested in the element of earth'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'Elemental earth you say? When adeptly controlled, the elements can be among our best allies. Retrieve a gridelin globe, a dragon hide mantle, and a spiroc earth totem. Only with these items can you master the element of earth.'

Items Needed:
- Dragon Hide Mantle [Isle 5]: an Essence Carrier, an Essence Tamer, a Soul Carrier, a Soul Tamer
- Gridelin Globe [Isle 4]: a Crystalline Cloud, a Gust of Wind, a Shimmering Meteor
- Spiroc Earth Totem [Isle 6]: The Spiroc Lord

Spiroc Healer's Cloak Trade-In

You say, 'I wish to trade.'
Relinin Skyrunner says 'I am currently interested in trading for a Spiroc Healer's Cloak. I will even trade something of a better value for it.'

This allows the Spiroc Healer's Cloak to be traded in for the current reward of the test of defense: Dark Cloak of the Sky.
