Outpost Teleportation

To use the outpost teleportation service, you first need to complete a Supply Run Quest to the region's outpost. These quests can be done regardless of if you meet the level requirements to teleport there but are only available to regions that your country currently controls. Once you have completed the quest, you can teleport to or from the outpost regardless of if your country controls the region or not [but it will cost more when controlled by another nation or the beastmen] provided that you meet the level requirement and have enough gil to pay the teleportation fee. Additionally teleportation is one-way [to the outpost only] when beastmen control the region as there will be nobody present at the outpost to teleport you back. Finally if you change your nation affiliation, you will need redo all suply quests.
