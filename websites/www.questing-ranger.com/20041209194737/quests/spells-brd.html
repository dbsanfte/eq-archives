Bard Quest Songs

Introduction to Teamwork [Mage's Ballad, prerequisite quest]
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Items: Party of 6 from the same country
Reward: Shell Ring

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all from the same nation: San d'Oria, Bastok or Windurst. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward. While this quest does not actually reward you with a song scroll, it is required for the quest that does.

Intermediate Teamwork [Mage's Ballad]
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Requirements: Introduction to Teamwork
Items: Party of 6 from the same race
Reward: Scroll of Mage's Ballad

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all of the same race: hume, tarutaru, mithra, elvaan or galka. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward.
