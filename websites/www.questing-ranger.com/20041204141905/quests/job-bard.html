Bard Job Quest

The Old Monument [Part 1]
Client: Lewenhart
Location: Song Runes, Buburimu Peninsula [F-9]
Requirements: Level 30+
Items: Parchment
Reward: Poetic Parchment

This quest actually starts with Mertaire at the Merry Minstrel Meadhouse in Lower Jeuno. After you talk to him, talk to Bki Tbujhja who mentions the Song Runes. You will now want to travel to the Song Rune in Buburimu Peninsula. On the Khoonta Dunes, there is a cave at [G-9] that leads to another beach area. You will need to check this beach for a second cave [F-9], which is where the Song Rune is located. Click on it to meet with Lewenhart, then trade the parchment to the Song Runes to get Poetic Parchment. If you didn't bring parchment with you, it can be bought at auction houses as well as the Tanners' Guild in San d'Oria.

A Minstrel in Despair [Part 2]
Client: Mertaire
Location: Merry Minstrel Meadhouse, Lower Jeuno [I-8]
Requirements: The Old Monument
Items: Poetic Parchment
Reward: 2,100 gil

Once you have the poetic parchment, return to Mertaire and give it to him.

Path of the Bard [Part 3]
Client: Lewenhart
Location: Song Runes, Valkurm Dunes [C-6]
Requirements: A Minstrel in Despair
Items: none
Reward: 3,000 gil, Bard Job
Title: Wandering Minstrel

Talk to Bki Tbujhja again to learn of the second Song Rune in Valkurm Dunes. You will now want to travel to Valkurm Dunes and head to [C-6] and look for a cave. On the other side of the cave is the "secret" beach. Follow the wall to the left to find the second Song Rune. Click on it to unlock the Bard job.
