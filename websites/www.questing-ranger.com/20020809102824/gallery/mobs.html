Gallery: Mobs [Introduction]

This section covers many hostile beings in Norrath.

ranger epic: Covers all mobs that appear as part of the ranger epic quest.

noteworthy: Covers popular to kill mobs that guilds um... fight over, excluding dragons and epic encounters.

dragons: Covers all dragons, drakes, wurms both named and insignificant. Planar dragons are also covered here.

planar: Covers all mobs that reside in one of the planes. This excludes dragons, noteworthy and epic-only encounters.

other epic: Covers mobs that are for epic quests.

other: Random mobs throughout norrath. Although some are named, none are of major significance.
