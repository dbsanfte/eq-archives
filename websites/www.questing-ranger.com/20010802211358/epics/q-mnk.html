The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Monk epic quest for the Celestial Fists.

Quest Start

Talk to Tomekeeper Dahl in Erudian and say 'What rare finds.' to start the quest.

The Book of Celestial FIsts

  1. Go to Skyfire. Kill any of the named mobs to get the Book of Immortals.
  2. Go to Erudian. Give Tomekeeper Danh the Book of Immortals, and say 'Who is Lheao' to get Danl's Reference.
  3. Go to Timorous Deep. Say to Lhaeo 'I have found the book Immortals' to start the dialogue with him.
  4. Give the Robe of the Whistling Fists and Dahl's Reference to get the the Celestial Fists Book.
    [Note: see Robe of the Whistling Fists Sub-Quest below for information on obtaining it.]
  5. Go to Trakanon's Teeth. Hail and talk to Kaiaren.
  6. Give the Celestial Fists book to Kaiaren to trigger Kaiaren [the true form.] Kaiaren will argo and you will get the Celestial Fists book back.
  7. You can either kill Kaiaren or Feign Death.
  8. Give Kaiaren [his true form] the Celestial Fists book, then say 'I got it from Lheao' to start dialogue with him.
    [NOTE: The Celestial Fists book that you have is different now.]

Celestial Fist of Fire: Eejag

  1. Go to Lavastorm. Find a Fire Sprite and say 'I Challenge Eejag' to it to spawn Eajag and the Fire Sprite will despawn.
  2. Eejag will shout across the zone and challenge you to come to the large fire pit near the Temple of Solusek Ro.
  3. Go to the large fire pit and you will see an Eejag in the fire pit.
  4. Kill Eejag to get the Charred Scale.
  5. Go to Trakanon's Teeth. Give the Charred Scale to Kaiaren's true form. The scale is returned to you and you are given more information.

Celestial Fist of Air: Gwan

  1. Go to the Plane of Sky. Give the Charred Scale to the Presence [on Island 2] to spawn Gwan.
    [NOTE: Island numbering can differ a bit. You need the one with Noble Dojorn.]
  2. Say 'I am prepared' will cause him to argo.
  3. Kill Gwan to get the Breath of Gwan.

Celestial Fist of Earth: Trunt

  1. Go to the Mines of Nurga. Give the Breath of Gwan to a Sleeping Ogre to spawn Trunt.
  2. Kill Trunt to get Trunt's Head

Celestial Fist of Water: Deep

  1. Go to Lake Rathetear. Go to the island with the Flame Tower, then search underwater for a tunel going under the island. Enter the tunnel to find Deep.
  2. Give Trunt's Head to Deep. She returns the head to you, despawns and tells you to meet with her master.
  3. Go to The Overthere. Give Trunt's Head to Astral Projection to initiate dialgue with him. When he asks if you are the one who has been killing his students, say 'Yes.' When he asks who you are, say 'I am the one,' Astral Projection despawns, and you will get the Eye of Kaiaren.
  4. Go to the Lake of Ill Omen. Astral Projection is on a platform in the middle of the lake. Give Astral Projection the Eye of Kaiaren, Astral Projection despawns, Deep and Vorash both spawn and argo.
  5. Kill Vorash to spawn Xenevorash.
  6. Kill Xenovorash to get the Demon's Fang.

Celestial Fists

Give the Celestal Fists Book and the Demon's Fang to Kaiaren to get the Celestial Fists.

Sub-Quest: Robe of the Whistling Fists

  1. Go to Brother Balatin in Dreadlands. You will need to Feign Death and hail him as you stand or he will argo.
  2. When asked what discipline do you follow, say 'I follow the brotherhood of the lost circle' then 'I am ready for the true drape.'
  3. Obtain the Robe of the Lost Circle if you have not already done so.
  4. Go to Chardok. Kill an Iksar Betrayer for the Metal Pipe.
  5. Go to Karnor's Castle. Kill the Drolvarg Pawduster for the Metal Pipe.
  6. Give the Robe of the Lost Circle and the two Metal Pipes to Brother Balatin to get the Robe of the Whistling Fists.
    [NOTE: I've been told that Brother Balatin needs to be charmed or he will eat your items.]

Sub-Quest: Robe of the Lost Circle

  1. Go to Brother Qwinn in South Karana. Talk to him to start this quest.
  2. Go to Nagafin's Lair. Kill Targin the Rock to get the Code of Zan Fi.
  3. Go to South Karana. Give the Code of Zan Fi and a Purple Headband to Brother Qwinn to get the Needle.
  4. Go to Lower Guk. Kill Brother Raster to get the Idol of Zan Fi.
  5. Go to Rathetear Mountains. Give the Idol of Zan Fi and Red Sash to get a Tailoring Pattern.
  6. Get a Swatch of Shadow Silk from any player tailor.
  7. Buy a scroll of Jonathans Whistling Warsong from a Bard Guild.
  8. Combine the Needle, Tailoring Pattern, a Swatch of Shadow Silk and scroll of Jonathans Whistling Warsong in a Sewing Kit to get the Robe of the Lost Circle.

Sub-Quest: Purple Headband

  1. White Headband:
    1. Go to the Qeynos. Hail and talk to Togahn Sorast in North Qeynos to start this quest.
    2. Kill Gnolls in the newbie area to get four Gnoll Pup Scalps.
    3. Give the four Gnoll Pup Scalps to Togahn Sorast to get the White Headband.
  2. Yellow Headband:
    1. Go to the Qeynos. Hail and talk to Togahn Sorast in North Qeynos to start this quest.
    2. Go to Qeynos Hills. Kill the Putrid Skeletons to get 3 Putrid Rib Bones.
    3. Give the White Headband and 3 Putrid Rib Bones to Togahn Sorast to get the Yellow Headband.
  3. Orange Headband:
    1. Go to the Qeynos. Hail and talk to Togahn Sorast in North Qeynos to start this quest.
    2. Go to Blackburrow. Kill Blackburrow Gnolls to get two Blackburrow Gnoll Pelts [cannot be ruined.]
    3. Kill Blackburrow Gnoll Shaman or Elites to get a Blackburrow Gnoll Skin.
    4. Give the Yellow Headband, two Blackburrow Gnoll Pelts and a Blackburrow Gnoll Skin to Togahn Sorast to get the Orange Headband.
  4. Red Headband:
    1. Go to the Qeynos. Hail and talk to Togahn Sorast in North Qeynos to start this quest.
    2. Go to South Karana. Kill Gahnex Drah's to get Gahnex Drah's Head.
    3. Kill High Shaman Phido to get Brother Dareb's Skull.
    4. Kill High Shaman Grisok to get Brother Shen's Head.
    5. Give the Orange Headband Gahnex Drah's Head, Brother Dareb's Skull and Brother Shen's Head to Togahn Sorast to get the Red Headband.
  5. Purple Headband:
    1. Go to the Qeynos. Hail and talk to Togahn Sorast in North Qeynos to start this quest.
    2. Go to Lake Rathetear. Kill the Gnoll Embalmer to get a Skull of Jhen'Tra.
    3. Go to Befallen. Kill the Necro Thaumateurgist to get Marnek's Dagger.
    4. Go to Permafrost. Kill High Priest Zaharn to get Zahern's Coronet.
    5. Give the Red Headband, Skull of Jhen'Tra, Marnek's Dagger and Zahern's Coronet to Togahn Sorast to get the Purple Headband.

Sub-Quest: Red Sash

  1. White Sash:
    1. Go to West Freeport. Hail and talk to Velan Torresk to start this quest.
    2. Kill an Orc Pawn in the newbie area to get two Deathfist Orc Scalps.
    3. Kill a Decaying Skeleton in the newbie area to get the Bone Chip.
    5. Kill a Snake in the newbie area to get the Snake Fang
    6. Give two Deathfist Orc Scalps, the Bone Chip and Snake Fang to Torresk to get the White Sash.
  2. Yellow Sash:
    1. Go to West Freeport. Hail and talk to Velan Torresk to start this quest.
    2. Go to East Commons. Kill the Orc Centurion to get the Deathfist Slashed Belt.
    3. Kill a Darkweed Snake to get the Giant Snake Fang.
    4. Go to North Ro. Kill a Desert Tarantula to get the Desert Tarantula Chitin.
    5. Give the White Sash, Deathfist Slashed Belt, Giant Snake Fang and Desert Tarantula Chitin to Velan Torresk to get the Yellow Sash.
  3. Orange Sash:
    1. Go to West Freeport. Hail and talk to Reyia to start this quest.
    2. Go to East Commonslands. Kill the Orc Legionaire to get the Legionaire's Bracer.
    3. Go to East or West Commonslands. Kill the Willowisp to get the Greater Lightstone.
    4. Go to West Commonlands. Kill the Cutthroat Dervish to get the Cutthroat Insignia Ring.
    5. Give the the Yellow Sash, Legionaire's Bracer, Greater Lightstone and Cutthroat Insignia Ring to get the Orange Sash.
  4. Red Sash:
    1. Go to Befallen. Kill Priest Amiaz to get the Blackened Wand.
    2. Go to Najena. Kill Ekeros to get the Blackened Saphire.
    3. Give the Orange Sash, Blackened Wand and Blackened Saphire to get the Red Sash.
