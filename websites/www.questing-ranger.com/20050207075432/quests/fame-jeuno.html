Jeuno Fame

Any quest performed in Jeuno as well as the average of your fame for San d'Oria, Bastok and Windurst will result in Jeuno fame. Tenshodo fame is not counted towards Jeuno fame. Since Jeuno does not have easy repeatable quests on its own, the best method is to work on fame with the other three cities perferably that which is the lowest.

Only the Best
Client: Melyon
Location: Weavers' Guild Salesroom, Selbina [I-9]
Turn-In: 5 La Theine Cabbage, 3 Millioncorn or Boyahda Moss
Note: If you have bad fame in San d'Oria and Bastok, this quest gives fame with both... which will help your Jeuno fame, but it lowers Tenshodo fame.

Fame Checkers:
- Mendi, Merry Minstrel Meadhoues, Lower Jeuno [H-8]

Fame Levels:
1: Unfortunately, I have never heard of your name before.
2: Hmm... That name is vaguely familiar...
3: Yes, I heard some travelers in a tavern talk about you. It seems you've a reputation.
4: I hear your name mentioned quite often these days. You've done well, my friend. Keep at it.
5: A good deal of people here in Jeuno know about you!
6: We hope your already substantial contributions to our humble city will continue to grow with time.
7: It is my pleasure to inform you how impressed we are with your service to Jeuno.
8: To all citizens here, your name is synonymous with courage and sacrifice.
9: You have emerged as a hero to the people of Jeuno. Just the sound of your name strikes courage in their hearts.
