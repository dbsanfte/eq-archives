Chateau d'Oraguille Quests

Her Majesty's Garden
Client: Chalvatot
Location: Queen Leaute's Garden [F-7]
Requirements: Rank 2
Items: Defland Humus
Reward: Map of the Northen Regions

There are two intermissions that you may experience here. One involves talking to the princesses [which does not start the quest] and the other involves just talking to Chalvatot about the garden [whih does start the quest.] If you get the first intermission I mentioned but not the second, then your level of fame in San d'Oria is not high enough. Also citizens of Bastok and Windurst will need to be Rank 3 and have talked to their respective embassay in San d'Oria before gaining access to the Chateau. Defland humus can be purchased at the auction house.
