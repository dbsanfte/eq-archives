QUEST ITEMS

Complete list of quest items:

  • Ancient Longsword: given by Telin Darkforest; used to get Refined Ancient Longsword
  • Ancient Pattern: Given by Alrik Farsight; used to create Runecrested Bowl
  • Bag of Provisions: Given by Merchant Nora; used to get Receipt
  • Braided Grass Amulet: Given by Althele; used to get Frayed Braided Grass Amulet
  • Chilled Tundra Root: Foraged in Everforst; used to create Hardened Mixture
  • Crushed Pot: Given by Alrik Farsight; used to get Grocery List
  • Dark Metal Coin: Given by Giz X'Tin; used to get Worn Dark Metal Coin
  • Dwarven Smiths Hammer: Given by Mairee Silentone in Erudin; used to get Hammer of the Ancients
  • Earth Stained Note: Given by Althele; used to get Shiny Tin Bowl
  • Enchanted Clay: Given by Kinlo Strongarm; used to create Runescale Bowl
  • Faelin's Ring: Given by Faelin Bloodbriar; used to get Dark Metal Coin
  • Firefly Globe: Summoned with the spell Dance of the Firefles; used to spawn the Spirit of Venril Sathir
  • Fleshbound Tomb: Looted from Dark Elf Corruptor's corpse; used to get Earth Stained Note
  • Frayed Braided Grass Amulet: Given by Sionae; leads to spawning of the Dark Elf Corruptor
  • Grocery List: Given by Farios Elianos; used to get Bag of Provisions
  • Hammer of the Ancients: Given by Mairee Silentone; used to trigger Usbak the Old and get Small bit of Mithril Ore
  • Hardened Mixture: Created by combining Chilled Tundra Root, Ripened Heartfruit, Speckled Molded Mushroom and Sweetened Mudroot in the Shiny Tin Bowl; used to get Softly Glowing Stone.
  • Jade Reaver: Looted from a Black Reaver in City of Mist; used to get Enchanted Clay
  • Platinum Speckled Powder: Given by Niera Farbreeze; used to create Runescale Bowl
  • Pusling Green Stone: drops off Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle; used to get the Warmly Glowing Stone.
  • Receipt: Given by Farios Elianos; used to get Ancient Pattern
  • Refined Ancient Longsword: Given by Usbak the Old; used to get Swiftwind
  • Refined Mithril Blade: Given by Kinlo Strongarm; used to get Earthcaller
  • Ripened Heartfruit: Foraged in Greater Faydark; used to create Hardened Mixture
  • Rose of Firiona: Foraged in Fiona Vie; used to get Wood Painting
  • Runecrested Bowl: Created by combining Ancient Pattern,Enchanted Clay, and Platinium Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel; used to get the Softly Glowing Stone.
  • Shattered Emerald of Corruption: drops off Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate; used to get Earthcaller
  • Shiny Tin Bowl: Given by Ella Foodcrafter; used to create a Hardened Mixture; give to Jaeil the Wretched so you can fight him and get the Hammer of an ancient dwarf
  • Siver Chained Locket: Given by Human Skeleton; used to get Platinum Speckled Powder
  • Small bit of Mithril Ore: Given by Usbak the Old; used to get Refined Mithril Blade
  • Softly Glowing Stone: Give by Ella Foodcrafter when you give her the Hardened Mixture and the Runecrested Bowl; used to get the Warmly Glowing Stone
  • Soulbound Hammer: Looted from Jaeil the Insane's corpse; used to get Dwarven Smiths Hammer
  • Speckled Molded Mushroom: Foraged in Innothule Swamp; used to create Hardened Mixture
  • Spell: Resurection: This is the L49 Cleric spell that brings back the dead. It can be purchased from Cleric guilds.
  • Sweetened Mudroot: Foraged in Misty Thicket; used to create Hardened Mixture
  • Swirling Sphere of Color: Looted from Essensce Tamer in Plane of Air; used to get Hammer of the Ancients
  • Warmly Glowing Stone: Given by Foloal Stormforest; used to get the Ancient Longsword
  • Worn Dark Metal Coin: Given by Telin Darkforest; used to get Braided Grass Amulet
  • Wood Painting: Given by Merdan Fleetfoot; used to get Silver Chained Locket
  • Worn Note: Given by Telin Darkforest; used to get Faelin's Ring

Items that must be obtained to complete the quest [not given by quest NPC]:

  • Chilled Tundra Root: Foraged in Everfrost
  • Firefly Globe: Summoned with the spell Dance of the Firefles.
  • Fleshbound Tomb: Need to kill the Dark Elf Corruptor in East Karana [triggered spawn]
  • Jade Reaver: Dropped by the Black Reaver in City of Mist
  • Pulsing Green Stone: Dropped by the triggered version of Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle
  • Ripened Heartfruit: Foraged in Greater Faydark
  • Rose of Firiona: foraged in Fiona Vie
  • Shattered Emerald of Corruption: Dropped by Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate; also a rare drop from his sub-bosses
  • Soulbound Hammer: Dropped by Jaeil the Insane in The Hole [triggered spawn]
  • Speckled Molded Mushroom: foraged in Innothule Swamp
  • Spell: Resurection: Purchased from Cleric guilds.
  • Sweetened Mudroot: foraged in Misty Thicket
  • Swirling Sphere of Color: dropped by Essensce Tamer in on Island Four of the Plane of Air
