QUEST WALKTHROUGH [SHORT VERSION]

This is a striped down version of the faq. It will tell you exactly what you need to get and only phrases that must be said to complete the quest.

It does not however provide any of the following you with any of the faction checks or tips that the Complete Version will provide nor will it give suggestions on correcting or dealing with faction problems. You will need to remember to con NPCs on your own.

It also does not provide information on finding NPCs or mobs [unless they can be difficult to find] or item descriptions.

Part 1: Obtain the Shiny Tin Bowl

  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Target him and say 'what action should I take' to get the "Worn Note"
  • Go to Faelin Bloodbriar in Greater Faydark. Give her the Worn Note to get "Faelins Ring"
    [Note: She spawns at POS 1500, POS 500; placeholder is an orc pawn.]
  • Go to Giz X`Tin in Kithicor. Give him Faelin’s Ring to get the "Dark Metal Coin"
    [Note: A Dark Reaver will spawn; just run from it.]
  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Dark Metal Coin to get the "Worn Dark Metal Coin"
  • [Note: The following part must be done as one sequence and all takes place in East Karana]
    Go to Althele. Give her the Worn Dark Metal Coin to get "Braided Grass Amulet"
    Go to Sionae. Give her the Braided Grass Amulet to get the "Frayed Braided Grass Amulet"
    Go to Nuien. Give him the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and he will return it to you
    Go to Teloa. Give her the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and she will lead you to the druid gathering
    Kill the Dark Elf Corruptor and Reavers that spawn and loot the Fleshbound Tomb
    Go to Althele. Give her the Fleshbound Tomb to get the "Earth Stained Note"
  • Go to Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket. Give her the Earth Stained Note to get the "Shiny Tin Bowl"

Part 2: Create the Hardened Mixture

  • Go to Everfrost and forage "Chilled Tundra Root"
  • Go to Greater Faydark and forage "Ripened Heartfruit"
  • Go to Innothule Swamp and forage "Speckled Molded Mushroom"
  • Go to Misty Thicket and forage "Sweetened Mudroot"
  • Place the Chilled Tundra Root, Ripened Heartfruit, Speckled Molded Mushroom, and Sweetened Mudroot in the Shiny Tin Bowl. Hit the Combine button to create the "Hardened Mixture"

Part 3: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #1 [Ancient Pattern]

  • Go to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Target him and say ‘I will take the artifact' to get the "Crushed Pot"
  • Go to Farios Elianos in Felwithe [B]. Give him the Crushed Pot to get the "Grocery List"
  • Go to Merchant Nora in Felwithe [A]. Give her the Grocery List to get the "Bag of Provisions"
  • Go to Farios Elianos in Felwithe [B]. Give him the Bag of Provisions to get the "Receipt"
  • Go to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Give him the Receipt to get the "Ancient Pattern"

Part 4: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #2 [Platinum Speckled Powder]

  • Forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie.
  • Go to Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glade. Give him the Rose of Firiona to get the "Wood Painting"
  • Go to Human Skeleton in Frontier Mountains. Give her the Wood Painting to get the "Silver Chained Locket"
    [Note: Spawns around NEG 2100, POS 2000]
  • Go to Niera Farbreeze in Surefall Glade. GIve her the Silver Chained Locket to get the "Platinum Speckled Powder"

Part 5: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #3 [Enchanted Clay]

  • Obtain a Jade Reaver from City of Mist
  • Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim [B]. Give him the Jade Reaver to get the "Enchanted Clay"

Part 6: Create the Runecrested Bowl

  • Combine the Ancient Pattern, Enchanted Clay, and Platinum Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel to make a Runecrested Bowl.
    [Note: This requires no skill in pottery to do and any pottery wheel should work.]

Part 7: Obtain the Ancient Longsword

  • Give Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket the Hardened Mixture and the Runecrested Bowl to get the "Softly Glowing Stone"
  • Kill Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle to spawn Venril Sathirs remains
    [Note: Venril Sathirs remains now spawn randomly after Venril Sathir is killed]
  • Cast the spell "Dance of the Fireflies" to Summon a Firefly Globe
  • Give the Firefly Globe to Venril Sathirs remains to spawn the Spirit of Venril Sathir
  • Give the Spirit of Venril Sathir the Level 49 Cleric Ressurection spell to respawn Venril Sathir
  • Kill Venril Sathir to get the "Pulsing Green Stone"
  • Go to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie. Give her the Softly Glowing Stone and the Pusling Green Stone to get the "Warmly Glowing Stone"
  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Warmly Glowing Stone to get the "Ancient Longsword"

Part 8: Obtain Swiftwind & Earthcaller

  • Go to Jaeil the Wretched in The Hole. Give him the Shiny Tin Bowl to spawn Jaeil the Insane.
  • Kill Jaeil the Insane and loot the "Soulbound Hammer"
  • Go to Mairee Silentone in Eurdin. Give her the Soulbound Hammer to get the "Dwarven Smiths Hammer"
  • Go to Island Four of the Plane of Air. Kill the Essensce Tamer to get the "Swirling Sphere of Color"
  • Go to Mairee Silentone in Eurdin. Give her the Dwarven Smiths Hammer and the Swirling Sphere of Color to get the "Hammer of the Ancients"
  • Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim. Give Kinlo Strongarm the Hammer of the Ancients, which causes Usbak the Old to spawn, who gives you a "Small bit of Mithril Ore"
  • Give Usbak the Old the Ancient Longsword to get the "Refined Ancient Sword"
  • Give Kinlo Stirngarm the Small bit of Mithril Ore to get the "Refined Mithril Blade"
  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Refined Ancient Sword, which causes Faelin to spawn. Telin gives you the Ancient Sword back.
  • Give Faelin the Refined Ancient Sword to get Swiftwind.
  • Kill Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate to get the "Shattered Emerald of Corruption" [Note: This also a random/rare drop from any of the boss mobs.]
  • Go to Xanuusus in North Karana. Give him the Refined Mithril Blade and the Shattered Emerald of Corruption.

The quest has now been completed!
