Part 6: Create the Runecrested Bowl

Item Checklist:

You should have the following items:

  • Shiny Tin Bowl: see part 1 to obtain
  • Hardened Mixture: see part 2 to obtain
  • Ancient Pattern: see part 3 to obtain [required]
  • Platinum Speckled Powder: see part 4 to obtain [required]
  • Enchanted Clay: see part 5 to obtain [required]

Creating a Runecrested Bowl

Combine the Ancient Pattern, Enchanted Clay, and Platinum Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel to make a Runecrested Bowl, which identifies as "A meticulously decorated bowl." This requires no skill in pottery to do and any pottery wheel should work. Make sure that you hit the Combine button on the Pottery Wheel after you place all items in it. If you close the Pottery Wheel without hitting combine, you will lose all the items and have to re-do parts 3-5 of the quest.
