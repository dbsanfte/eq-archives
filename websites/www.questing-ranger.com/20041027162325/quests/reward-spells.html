Spell & Song Quests

Healing the Land [Teleport-Holla]
Client: Eperdur
Location: Cathedral [M-7]
Reward: Scroll of Teleport-Holla

You'll want to go to Gusgen Mines. You mostly need just sneak to get around here if you are doing this at or around L37 as only undead will agro. Your goal here is to work your way to [G-8] then look for a "???" spot that is behind a shed. Examine it then return to Eperdur.

Introduction to Teamwork [Mage's Ballad, prerequisite quest]
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Items: Party of 6 from the same country
Reward: Shell Ring

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all from the same nation: San d'Oria, Bastok or Windurst. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward. While this quest does not reward you with a spell, it is required for the quest that does.

Intermediate Teamwork [Mage's Ballad]
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Requirements: Introduction to Teamwork
Items: Party of 6 from the same race
Reward: Scroll of Mage's Ballad

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all of the same race: hume, tarutaru, mithra, elvaan or galka. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward.

Mean Machine
Client: Unlucky Rat
Location: Cermet Refinery, Metalworks [G-7] Items: Slime Oil
Reward: Scroll of Warp

Slime Oil is a drop from slime-type mobs. However this is at L18 spell and slimes might be a bit difficult still at that point and even with two treasure hunters in the party, the are a rare drop. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Alchemy Materials.

The Rumor
Client: Novalmauge
Location: Bostaunieux Oubliete [F-8]
Requirements: Rank 2
Items: Beastmen Blood
Reward: Scroll of Drain

This quest requires a lot of fame to even start it. If Novalmauge tells you that you do not belong here, this is why. Also you may get a different intermission that involves a prisoner if trying to do this while L30 or higher, which is associated the Dragoon job quest. If this occurs, try talking to Novalmauge again. Also citizens of Bastok and Windurst will need to be Rank 3 and have talked to their respective embassay in San d'Oria before gaining access to the Chateau. The Beastmen Blood you need is a random rare drop off leeches. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Beastmen made items.

After you give Novalmauge the Beastmen Blood there will be an intermission where he asks if you believe the rumor or not. I picked no and Novalmauge said that I'm a kind soul... no idea what he will say if you picked yes.

Truth, Justice and the Onion Way [Blaze Spikes, prerequisite quest]
Client: Kohlo-Lakolo
Location: Warehouses, Port Windurst [G-5]
Requirements: none
Items: Rarab Tail
Reward: Justice Badge
Title: Star Onion Brigade Member

Rarab Tails do not drop off of rarabs but off of bumblebees. Even if you are killing the right mobs, getting the drop can take a while. These are also sold at the auction house. While this quest does not reward you with a spell, it is required for the quest that does.

Know One's Onions
Client: Kohlo-Lakolo
Location: Warehouses, Port Windurst [G-5]
Requirements: Truth, Justice and the Onion Way
Items: 4 Wild Onions
Reward: Scroll of Blaze Spikes
Title: S.O.B. Super Hero

Rarab Tails do not drop off of rarabs but off of bumblebees. Even if you are killing the right mobs, getting the drop can take a while. These are also sold at the auction house.
