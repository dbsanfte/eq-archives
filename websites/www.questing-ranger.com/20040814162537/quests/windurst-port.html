Port Windurst Quests

A Discerning Eye (Windurst)
Client: Pygmalion
Location: Airship Dock, Port Windurst [M-7]
Requirements: Rank 5
Items: Dropped Item
Reward: 500G
Title: Discerning Individual

For these quests, you will be shown what the passanger looks like after you agree to deliver the dropped item. This is the only time you will be shown what the actual passanger looks like, so the most successful way to do this that I have found is to make note of the following: race, gender, hair color, hair style, head equipment, body equipment, hands equipment, legs equipment and feet equipment. If you give the item to the correct passanger, you will gain 500 gil, which more than covers the cost of the flight itself. Also if you have completed all other Discerning Eye quests, you will get the title "Very Discerning Individual" instead.

Know One's Onions
Client: Kohlo-Lakolo
Location: Warehouses, Port Windurst [G-5]
Requirements: Truth, Justice and the Onion Way
Items: 4 Wild Onions
Reward: Scroll of Blaze Spikes
Title: S.O.B. Super Hero

Rarab Tails do not drop off of rarabs but off of bumblebees. Even if you are killing the right mobs, getting the drop can take a while. These are also sold at the auction house.

Something Fishy
Client: Tokaka
Location: Fisherman's Guild, Port Windurst [C-8]
Requirements: none
Items: Bastore Sardine
Reward: 70G

This quest is repeatable and one of the easiest ways to gain Windurst fame, but at the same time can be extremely annoying. Bastore Sardines can be either caught with fishing or purchased from the nearby Fishing Guild. However in order to repeat this quest, you need to change areas then come back and talk to Tokaka again. She says a lot of stuff and pauses during the conversation, both when you talk to her and after the turn in.

Location: Warehouses, Port Windurst [G-5]
Requirements: none
Items: Rarab Tail
Reward: Justice Badge
Title: Star Onion Brigade Member

Rarab Tails do not drop off of rarabs but off of bumblebees. Even if you are killing the right mobs, getting the drop can take a while. These are also sold at the auction house.
