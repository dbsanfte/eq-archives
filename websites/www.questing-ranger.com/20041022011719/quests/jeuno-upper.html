Upper Jeuno Quests

A Candlelight Vigil
Client: Ilumida
Location: House [G-8]
Requirements: none
Items: Candle [Key Item]
Reward: Flower Necklace

This quest is done in conjunction with Candle Making, but you must start this one first. After starting this quest, you will need to perform the Candle Making quest to obtain the reward for this quest.

Candle Making
Client: Rouliette
Location: Temple of the Goddess [H-9]
Requirements: started A Candlelight Vigil
Items: Lanolin Cube
Reward: Candle [Key Item]

This quest is done in conjunction with A Candlelight Vigil, as it must be started afterwards and provides you with the turn-in item for the other quest. The Lanolin Cube is an EX drop from Battering Rams in La Theine Plateau.

Crest of Davoi
Client: Baudin
Location: House, Upper Jueno
Requirements: Rank 4
Items: coeurl meat
Reward: Crest of Davoi [key item]

This quest is actually a subpart of the Rank 4 Mission and also leads into the "Save My Sister" quest. Coeurl Meat can be purchased at the auction house.

Chocobo's Wounds
Client: Brutus
Location: Chocobo Stables [G-7]
Requirements: Level 20+
Items: 4 Gausebit Wildgrass
Reward: Chocobo License [ability to ride chocobos]
Title: Chocobo Trainer

This quest takes period over 6 days Vana'diel time [5 hours], so make sure that you have enough time to do it before starting it.
