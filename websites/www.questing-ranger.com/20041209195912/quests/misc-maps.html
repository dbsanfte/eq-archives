Map Quests

A Smudge on One's Record [Fei'Yin]
Client: Hariga-Origa
Location: Optistery, Windurst Waters [F-8]
Items: Slime Oil, Frost Turnip
Requirements: Chasing Tales, Glyph Hanger
Rewards: 3000G, Map of Fei'Yin

The two prerequisite quests may be completed in any order, and it is also necessary to zone after completing both. Without doing both of these things, you will not get this quest. Once you get the quest, just give Hariga-Origa the two required items.

An Explorer's Footsteps [Crawler's Nest]
Client: Abelard
Location: Mayor's Residence, Selbina [G-9]
Items: Selbina Clay, Clay Tablet
Reward: 800 - 10,000 gil per Clay Tablet, Map of Crawler's Nest

Abelard wants a copy of all the Stone Monuments that an explorer left behind. He gives you a piece of Selbina clay that you then trade to a Stone Monument to get the clay tablet. Once you have a clay tablet, you then need to return to Abelard and give it to him in order to get gil [amount depends on location] and another piece of Selbina clay. The monunments are listed and covered in chronological order, but you can find them in any order. Also after you have turned in 15 of the 17 monuments, you will receive the Map of Crawler's Nest and will have the option to go after the two remaining monuments.

South Gustenberg [K-10]
This one is easy to find. Just head to the area around the lighthouse and this monument will be in plain view along the edge of the cliff.

North Gustenberg [F-7]
For this one you need to actually head to South Gustenberg then go to Dangruf Wadi. Once in here head towards the upper right and you'll find a guiser. Stand on the guiser at the spot where water comes out and it will shoot up to the higher ledge. You may need invisibility at this point as the mobs are higher level in the next part and will still agro at Level 30. Continue heading towads the upper-right and you'll come to another guiser. Again stand on it to get shot up to the higher ledge. This higher ledge leads to a secret area that will take you to North Gustenberg. Once in North Gustenberg, you will be in the area below the waterfall. Go straight ahead then cross the bridge to get to the left side of the river. [There are goblins here as well that may agro.] When you get to the waterfall, enter the cave behind it to find the Stone Monument.

Konschtat Highlands [G-7]
Look for a cave along the North side of the mountains.

East Ronfaure [G-11]
This one is not far from King Ranperre's Tomb. From here, head to G-11 and you will find it against the far wall of this area.

Junger Forest [H-12]
Head to the area of H-12 inside Jugner Forest and you should see a cave, which has the stone monument inside. While I did read of many warnings about tigers being in this area thus creating a danger, I did not see a single one anywhere near the cave. I did however see someone healing in this area that most likely was killing them.

Rolanberry Fields [K-11]
For this one, you'll want to ride a chocobo to get the the monument. Just head towards K-11 and when you get to the white barrier, you can cross it from the left side [when entering.] Along the far back wall, you'll find the monument. To get out, you'll want to use invisibility and sneak unless at a high level. Just cross back over the barrier from the same side then work you way back to the road. [Alternately, you can use an instant warp scroll to get out.]

West Ronfaure [I-10]
This one is in a corner of one of the building ruins in this area.

Valkurm Dunes [E-9]
The mounment is in a cave along the beach.

Pashhow Marshlands [F-6]
Unless you are of a high enough level that the malboros in this zone will not attack you, you should ride a chocobo to get to this monument. If you look at the [F-6] are on the map, there is a small dead-end. The stone monument is here, but it is being guarded by a malboro. Wait until the malboro is looking away then dismount from the chocobo at the monument. You will need to use sneak to get out safely. However should you be detected anyway, gravity works well on slowing it down enough for you to get away to safely. Other than this one mob, most others are easy enough to avoid getting agro from by not getting to close to them.

La Theine Plateau [J-7]
For this one, you need to go into Ordelle's Caves. Once inside, take the passageway to the left that leads to a room full of fungars. There will be two more passage ways here. One leads too the pool room and the other back out to La Theine Plateau. You'll want to take the one leading back out to La Theine. You will now find yourself in another one of the pits in the zone. The Stone Monument is easy to find from here. Just keep running straight ahead and eventually you will come to J-7 where it is located.

Meriphataud Mountains [J-10]
This one is fairly easy to locate as it is out in the open. The only noteworthy thing is that it is in the lower section and there are a bunch of raptors in the area.

Tahrongi Canyon [E-5]
Look for a cave along the edge of the wall in this area.

West Sarutabaruta [G-9]
This one can be found on the raised cliff area to the west of Windurst. You'll find the slop leading upwards towards the north side of the cliff. Once you have found it, just follow the wall until you come to the Stone Monument.

East Sarutabaruta [K-6]
Another that is extremely easy. Just head to K-6, and the Stone Monument can be found near where the river ends.

Buburimu Peninsula [J-5]
This one is along the boundry wall for the area and out in the open so shouldn't be too much trouble to find.

A Foreman's Best Friend [Gusgen Mines]
Client: Gudav
Location: Construction Site, Port Bastok [H-5]
Items: Dog Collar
Reward: Map of Gusgen Mines

The Dog Collar drops from a Black Wolves that spawn in North Gustaberg at night time. It can also be purchased at the Auction House under Armor then Neck. Once you have the Dog Collar, just give it to Gudav to get the map.

Glyph Hanger [Horutoto Ruins]
Client: Hariga-Origa
Location: Optistery, Windurst Waters [F-8]
Rewards: Map of Horutoto Ruins

After you get this quest, leave Windurst and talk to Ipupu, who can be found in a tower around [I-8] of West Sarutabaruta. Once you talk to her, return to Hariga-Origa for the map. This also completes one of the requirements for the "A Smudge on One's Record" quest. The other requirement [Chasing Tales] is given out by Tosuka-Porika at G-8.

Exit the Gambler [King Ranperre's Tomb]
Clients: Aurege and Guilberdrier
Location: Carpenters' Guild, North San d'Oria [E-4]
Items: 5 gil
Reward: Map of King Ranperre's Tomb
Title: Daytime Gambler

Varchet can be found in South San d'Oria in Lion Square [L-6], not far from the residential area. When you talk to him, he'll agree to go back to work if you can beat him in a dice game. Trade him 5 gil to play, but he will give you 10 gil when you win. [Should you not win, just keep trading him 5gil until you do] Once you have beat Varchet, return to Aurege and Guilberdrier.

Her Majesty's Garden [Northen Regions]
Client: Chalvatot
Location: Queen Leaute's Garden, Chateau d'Oraguille [F-7]
Requirements: Rank 2
Items: Defland Humus
Reward: Map of the Northen Regions

Defland humus can be purchased at the auction house.

Rycharde the Chef
Client: Rycharde
Location: Sailor's Stay, Mhaura [H-8]
Items: 2 Dhamal Meat
Reward: 1500G

You'll need to talk to Somo Asstsula first. Now head to the govener's office, talk to Numi Adaligo, ask her about work and she will suggest trying the tavern. Talk to Take on the first floor of Sailor's Stay, and she'll refer you to Rycharde, who can be found upstairs. When you talk to Rycharde, you will get the first quest. While this does not reward you with a map, it begins a series of quests which will eventually lead to obtaining a map. Also there does appear to be a time delay between quests, so just try back the next day after completeing of these quests to get the next one.

Way of the Cook
Client: Rycharde
Location: Sailor's Stay, Mhaura [H-8]
Requirements: Rycharde the Chef
Items: Dhamal Meat, Beehive Chip
Reward: 1500G

You now need to give Rycharde a Beehive Chip and Dhamal Meat. While this quest does have a time limit, this limit only impacts your reward, which will be less if not completed on time.

Unending Chase
Client: Rycharde
Location: Sailor's Stay, Mhaura [H-8]
Requirements: Way of the Cook
Item: Puffball
Reward: 2100G

Puffballs drop from Walking Trees in Jugner Forest. They can also be found for sale at Auction Houses.

His Name is Valgeir [Toraimarai Canal]
Client: Rycharde
Location: Sailor's Stay, Mhaura [H-8]
Requirements: Unending Chase
Reward: Map of Toraimarai Canal

Rycharde gives you a pizza [key item] to deliver to Valgeir in Selbina. He also arranges to have the costs of riding the ship payed for you, so just hop on the next boat to Selbina and talk to Valgeir. Return to Rychard afterwards to get the map. Also there are additional quests that Rycharde can give you after this one.

Northward [Zvahl Castle]
Client: Radeivepart
Location: Peace Monument, Ru'Lude Gardens [H-9]
Items: Flame Degen
Reward: Map of Zvahl Castle

Flame Degen can be purchased from the Auction House [under Weapons then Swords,] can drop from Napalms in Ordelle's Caves and can also be crafted.

The Antique Collector [Delfutt's Tower]
Client: Imasuke
Location: Gate to Qufim, Port Jeuno [E-6]
Items: Kaiser Sword
Reward: Map of Deltfutt's Tower

Kaiser Swords drop from any of the Dancing Weapons which can be found on Qufim Island. You can also buy one from the Auction House under Weapons then Swords.

The Bare Bones [Dangruf Wadi]
Client: Degenhard
Location: Near Galkan Bridge, Bastok Market [H-10]
Items: Bone Chip
Reward: Map of Dangruf Wadi

Bone chips drop off undead mobs and can also be purchased from the auction house under materials then bonecrafting.

The Rescue [Ranguemont Pass]
Client: Thunder Hawk
Location: Mayor's Residence, Selbina [G-9]
Item: Quadav Charm
Reward: 3000G, Map of Ranguemont Pass

After you get this quest, you'll need to head to Beadeaux. Head to [H-7] and into the tunnel. While inside, go to [F-8] to come out in another area. You'll pass by an two machines outside, "The Mute" and "The Afflictor" before you will see a ramp leading to the upper area. Go up the ramp then work your way to [I-9], which is where De'Vyu Headhunter spawns. You'll need to kill him for the charm as it is Rare/EX. Once you have the charm, go through the gate and head North into the next area. You'll need to drop down into the lower area. The jail door can be found at [I-8.] After the intermission, return to Selbina and talk to Thunder Hawk again. Also Quadav only detect by sound, so you should only need Sneak to get around Beadeaux.
