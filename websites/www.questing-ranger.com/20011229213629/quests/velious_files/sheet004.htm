Golden Leaf Armour

These quests are all given out by Nalelin Fe'Dhar in Skyshrine. You need to have ally faction with him to do the quest.

Golden Leaf Helm

  1. Go to Temple of Veeshan. Kill dragons [the ones in the Testing Hall] to get the Unadorned Chain Coif.
  2. Obtain 3 Crushed Coral from random Velious Mobs.
  3. Give Nalelin the Unadorned Chain Coif and 3 Crushed Coral to get the Golden Leaf Helm.
