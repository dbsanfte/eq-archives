Her Majesty's Garden
Client: Chavatot
Location: Queen Leaute's Garden, Chateau d'Oraguille [F-7]
Requirements: Rank 2+ if San d'Orian, Rank 3+ if from other nations
Item: Derfland Humus
Reward: Map of the Northen Regions

Summary: Bring some Derfland humus to use as fertilizer so that flowers might grow in the late queen's garden.

Derfland Humus drops from Doom Scorpions in Crawler's Nest. It can also be purchased at the Auction House under Other Miscellaneous. Trade it to Chavatot to be rewarded with a map.
