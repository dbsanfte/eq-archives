Language Translations: Japanese

The following information was reprinted from various forums as I do not speak a word of Japanese. It mostly consists of courtesies and greetings which are not necessarily easy to say with the autotranslate feature.

greetings:
good morning: ohayo gozaimasu
good afternoon konnichiwa
good evening konbanwa
nice to meet you: yoroshiku

thank you:
thank you very much: domou arigatou gozaimasu
thank you: arigatou gozaimasu
thanks: arigatou (or domo)
thanks for hard work: otsukaresama deshita

congratulatory:
congrats: omedetou
grats: ome~

goodbyes:
end [as in leaving party]: owaru
party breaking up: PT kaisan desuka (add a ? at end to ask about this)
leaving in Xminutes: Xpun >> ochimasu ^^; (where X is the number if minutes you have left)
goodbye: mata ne
goodbye, nice to meet you: mata yoroshiku
goodbye, hope to do it again: mata onegaishimasu

other courtesies:
away from keyboard: riseki shimasu
bathroom break: wc

language related:
i don't speak japanese: nihongo o hanshimasen
do you speak english?: eigo wo hanashimasu ka?
please use the auto translator function: jidou honyaku kino wo tsukate kudasai

mob difficulties:
difficult challenge: tsuyo (or tuyo)
tough: tote
very tough: totetsuyo
incredibly tough: totetote
incredibly tough with high something: totetote+
incredibly tough with 2 high somethings: totetote++

mob types:
crab: kani
goblin: gobu
orc: ooku
lizard: tokage
fly: tonbo
rabbit: usagi
worm: mimizu

skill chain related:
skill chain: renkei
I'm sorry, what's the renkei order? who>who>who?: gomene, renkei wa 'order' nandesuka? dare>dare>dare kudasai.
first or one: ichii
second or two: ni
third or three: san

other:
may I check your equipment?: soubi wo [/check] shite mo kamaimasen ka?
[note - use autotranslater for [/check] to emphasis that you are asking for permission to use an in-game command.
