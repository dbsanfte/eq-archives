Rallic Pack FAQ
by Crydee Ossiriand, Torvonnilous Server
http://www.questing-ranger.com
Version: 2004.06.01

This faq is divided into two parts. The first deals with the actual quest portion to obtain the pattern needed to make the pack, while the second half deals with the crafting of the Rallic Pack. This faq is not 100% complete in the sense that I do not give a skill up route for tailoring nor do I intend to. The trivial raiting for this item is so low that you can do it just by sending points with one of your character's guildmasters.

Rallic Pack Pattern

Rallic Aristos, Katta Castellum:
Rallic Aristos can be found in the upper section of Katta Castellum. He is inside the large castle surrounded by a wall that has the nocturnal garden in front.

You say, 'Hail, Rallic Aristos'
Rallic Aristos says 'Well met Crydee! I am Rallic Aristos, Governor of trade and economy here in Katta Castellum. I know it sounds like a lot of parchment pushing and coin counting but maintaining good trade relations with the various Houses of Shadow Haven and the haggling Vah Shir of Shar Vahl is not as easy at it may sound. Then there is the issue of the [acrylia mines] in the Tenebrous Mountains.'

You say, 'what acrylia mines?'
Rallic Aristos says 'The Tenebrous Mountains are rich with veins of the valuable acrylia ore. Unfortunately the mines were dug and controlled by a tribe of savage grimlings from the forests to the south. Negotiations have been attempted with the grimlings but to no avail. Several of the other governors and I have been developing a [plan] to take the mines or establish mines of our own. In either case the mines will have to be defended from attacks by the grimlings unless when placed in that predicament they finally resort to trade negotiations.'

You say, 'what plan?'
Rallic Aristos says 'The full plans are not at my disposal but one of my concerns that may be instrumental in our success is the acquisition of grimling documents carried by a grimling courier who is seen frequently running from the forest to the mines. If one of the Validus Custodus attempts to obtain the runners pouch the grimlings will surely retaliate, if adventurers like yourselves obtained it however and quietly delivered it to me we may be able to move forward with our plans.'

Grimling Runner, Tenebrous Mountains
A Grimling Runner is a random spawn in Tenebrous Mountains. He will spawn in the cave that is closest to the zone to Grimling Forest, then start wandering the zone. This mob is about L35, so will be rather easy to kill. Once you get the Grimling Runner Pouch, return to Katta Castellum.

Rallic Aristos, Katta Castellum
*Give Grimling Runner Pouch to Rallic Aristos*
Rallic Aristos says 'Excellent, the contents of the pouch shall be examined immediately. Take this pattern for a crafting a pack that will be sure to lighten the burden of its contents. I'm afraid I do not have the rest of the [required materials] at hand.'

Rallic Pack

Shade Silk Cord
You will first need to go to Shadeweaver's Thicket and hunt the silkworms and shades for Shadeling Silk. Combine two Shadeling Silk in a Sewing Kit to make Shade Silk Thread. Once you have 3 Shade Skilk Threads, combine them in the Sewing Kit to make the Shade Silk Cord.

Perfect Owlbear Pelt
The best way to get this is to hunt Owlbears in Hollowshade Moor.

1 Rallic Pack Pattern
1 Shade Silk Cord
1 Perfect Owlbear Pelt

Once you have all the ingredients, combine them in a Sewing Kit to make the Rallic Pack.
