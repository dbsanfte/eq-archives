San d'Oria Fame

Any quest performed in either San d'Oria or Selbina will give you fame with San d'Oria. Selbina quests will also give you fame with San d'Oria. However there are several quests that are repeatable. With the exception of Warding Vampires, all turn in items for these quests can be purchased at the auction house.

A Discerning Eye (San d'Oria)
Client: Eddy
Location: Airship Dock, Port San d'Oria [H-6]
Requirements: Rank 5

Fear of the Dark
Client: Secodiand
Location: Laborman's Way, North San d'Oria[E-6]
Tun-In Items: 2 Bat Wings

Only the Best
Client: Melyon
Location: Weavers' Guild Salesroom, Selbina [I-9]
Turn-In: 5 La Theine Cabbage, 3 Millioncorn or Boyahda Moss
Note: Also gives Bastok Fame, but lowers Tenshodo Fame

Starting a Flame
Client: Legata
Location: Lion Springs Tavern, South San d'Oria [K-6]
Turn-In Items: Items: 4 Flint Stones

The Merchant's Bidding
Client: Parvipon
Location: Tanners' Guild, South San d'Oria [E-8]
Turn-In Items: 3 Rabbit Hides

Warding Vampires
Client: Maloquedil
Location: Parade Grounds, North San d'Oria [J-8]
Turn-In Items: 2 Shaman Garlic [Ex]

Fame Checkers:
- Namonutice, Lion Springs Tavern in South San d'Oria [K-6]

Fame Levels:
1: No, I never heard that name.
2: Hmm... I might have heard that name before. Then again, Maybe not.
3: That is a name I often hear. People speak well of you! Your deeds for the kingdom have earned you much honor.
4: You have become well known in these parts! I hear much of your accomplishments.
5: You are famous in our kingdom! Of you no ill is spoken. Give to the kingdom and she will give to you, no?
6: I would venture that much the kindgdom has heard your name! And your reputation sparkles.
7: Practically all of the kingdom has heard of you now. What's more, your reputation is stellar.
8: People are talking about you! Every infant in his cradle knows your name!
9:There isn't a soul in the kingdom that doesn't consider you a hero!
