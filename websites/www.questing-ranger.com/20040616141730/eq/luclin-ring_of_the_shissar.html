Ring of the Shissar [Key to Emperor Ssraeshza's Lair] FAQ
by Crydee Ossiriand, Torvonnilous Server
http://www.questing-ranger.com
Version: 2004.06.01

This quest takes place entirely in Ssraeshza Temple and does not appear to have any real starting point. The four items needed to complete this quest may be obtained in any order.

Taskmaster's Pouch
This is a semi-common drop off of any of the named taskmasters in the basement. Some of these are melee-orinted while others are casters. The mobs you are specifically looking for are Taskmaster Kavamezh, Taskmaster Keuzozh, Taskmaster Mikazha, Taskmaster Revan'Kezh, Taskmaster Vezhkah, Taskmaster Zerumaz and Taskmaster Zhe'vozh.

Zazuzh's Idol
This is a guaranteed drop off Commander Zazuth on the First Floor. After you go through the first door, go up the ramp and through the door. Commander Zazuth spawns in the room at the end of the hallway. There's a few other spots he can spawn, but this one is the easiest for a single group to do.

Zeruzsh's Ring
This is a guaranteed drop off Commander Zherozsh on Second Floor. While I don't have directions to him, he appears to spawn around the ellites.

Ssraeshzian Insignia
This is a guaranteed drop off of General Kizuhz, Arbiter Korazhk and Advisor Zekuzh on Third Floor. These mobs can be encountered on the way to Emperor Ssraeshza himself.

Ring of the Shissar
Once you have all four items, place Zazuzh's Idol, Zeruzsh's Ring and the Ssraeszian Insignia into the Taskmaster's Pouch then press the combine button to get the Ring of Shissar.
