Bard Artifact Quests

Painful Memory [Bard AF1]
Client: Mertaire
Location: Merry Minstrel Meadhouse, Lower Jeuno [I-8]
Requirements: Level 40+ Bard
Reward: Paper Knife

You'll need to get a party and head to Rangemont Pass, then go to [D-5]. You'll find a pool of water here, and clicking on the "???" will spawn the notorious monster Tros. You'll need to fight Tros. Both Barwatera and Water Carol will help due the the water-based AE that he does. Once Tros is dead, inspect the "???" again. Mertaire will show up and give you the Paper Knife.

The Requiem [Bard AF2]
Client: Bki Tbujhja
Location: Merry Minstrel Meadhouse [I-8]
Requirements: Painful Memory, Level 50+ Bard
Reward: Choral Slippers

After you talk to Bki Tbujhja, get some Holy Water then talk to her a second time. You'll now need a full alliance. You can get away with about two parties instead of the full three if you have some high level people helping. You'll now want to go to Eldieme Necropolis, but must go to the area which requires you to travel to Beaudice Glacier. Once here, sneak your way to [D-4] and clear all the mobs in the room. Put sneak up again then inspect all the coffins in the room until three NMs spawn. You only need to kill the Owl Guard to pass this, but this depends on if you manage to pull him by himself. With this mob [or all three] dead, inspect the coffin again for a Star Ring. Return to the Merry Minstrel Meadhouse and talk to Bki Tbujhja again to get the Choral Slippers.

The Circle of Time [Bard AF3] - incomplete
Client: Imasuke
Location: Qufim Tunnel [E-6]
Requirements: The Requiem
Reward: Choral Justacorps

Imasuke shows interest in the Star Ring [key item] that you obtained from the spirit towards the end of "The Requiem." You'll need to take the ring to Xarcabard and bury it in the snow at [I-10.] There are several high level mobs in this tunnel, but you can sneak past them easily. You'll now need to wait until the next real life day to dig it out. Once you have the ring back, return to Imasuke and talk to him again.
