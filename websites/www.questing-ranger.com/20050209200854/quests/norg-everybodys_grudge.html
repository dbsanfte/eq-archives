Everybody's Grudge
Client: Magephaud
Location: Near Central Staircaise, Norg [I-8]
Requirements: having fought and killed a Tonberry
Items: Gold Beastcoin x3
Reward: Tonberry's Key, Ability to Reset Tonberry Rancor
Title: Honoary Doctorate, Majoring in Tonberries

Summary: Bring back three gold beastcoins if you want to learn how to rid yourself of the Tonberries' rancor.

Completing this quest does not reset your tonberry hate. It only gives you the key that is needed to reset tonberry hate. This is equal to the number of tonberries that a party you were in has has killed, excluding any that the party called for help on. This quest is necessary specifically for completing Zilart Mission 4 so that Everybody's Grudge doesn't become an instant kill move.

To rest Tonberry Hate, go to the sealed door at [F-11] on the first map in Temple of Uggalepih. Inside this room, you can reset your accumulated tonberry hate to zero by paying a fee to the priest inside. The cost depends on your current hate level.
