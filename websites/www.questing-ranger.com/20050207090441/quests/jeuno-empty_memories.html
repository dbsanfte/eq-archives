Empty Memories
Client: Harith
Location: Grand Duke Palace, Ru'Lude Gardens Items: 2000G, Recollection of Fear, Recollection of Guilt, Recollection of Pain
Reward: Hysteroanima, Psychoanima, Terroanima

Summary: The mysterious "society" that Harith works for is supposidly performing research on an strange life form known as the Empty.

You only need one of the three possible types of recollections to complete this quest. Recollections drop from the zone-specific empties: Thinker [Holla,] Gorger [Dem] and Craver [Mea.] Trade a recollection and 2000G to Harith, and he will synth it into the corresponding anima for you. These used to require a 24 hour wait, but now occur instantaneously:
- Recollection of Fear: Psychoanima
- Recollection of Guilt: Terroanima
- Recollection of Pain: Hysteroanima

Each of the animas has a special effect when used that will make the Promyvion boss fights easier.
- Hysteroanima: disables enemy special attacks for 20-25 seconds.
- Psychoanima: disablles physical attacks for 20-25 seconds; does not stop special attacks
- Terroanima: makes the enemy run around self-kiting for 20-25 seconds, and it will not attack
