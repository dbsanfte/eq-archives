Tenshodo Fame

Any quest performed for the Tenshodo or Norg. Additionlly quests for the Tenshodo also count towards fame with the city they are performed in.

A Job for the Consortium
Client: Portaure
Location: Cargo Room B, Port San d'Oria [H-9]>
Requirements: Rank 5 Airship Pass
Item: Brugarie Goods [given by npc]
Notes: Also gives San d'Oria fame. If unsuccessful with clearing customs, airship pass revoked until 7am PST or 11am EST.

Shady Business
Client: Talib
Location: Tenshodo Warehouse, Port Bastok [F-6]
Requirements: Beauty and the Galka
Items: 4 Zinc Ore
Note: Also gives Bastok fame.

Fame Checkers:
- Vaultimand, Norg [H-8]

Fame Levels:
1: I have never heard of your name before.
2: I wonder if I have heard of your name before somewhere...
3: I have heard of your name before.
4: I often here of your name.
5: Your name has become fairly known by people.
6: There are very few people that don't know you.
7: No one is unfamiliar with your name here.
8: I have heard of your activity showing again!
9: Your existence is heroic for all the people living here.
