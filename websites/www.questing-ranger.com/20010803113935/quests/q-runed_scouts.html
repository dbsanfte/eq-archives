Runed Scout's Helm

This quest is given by Argash in Thurgadin. You need to have amiable faction with him to do the quest.

  1. Go to Kael Drakkel. Kill giants to get the Corroded Chain Coif.
  2. Obtain 3 Crushed Coral from random Velious Mobs.
  3. Give Argash the Corroded Chain Coif and 3 Crushed Coral to get the Runed Scout's Helm.
