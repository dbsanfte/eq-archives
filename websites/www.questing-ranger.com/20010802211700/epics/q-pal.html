The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Paladin epic quest for Fiery Defender.
Note: This quest requires that you have completed the quest for Soulfire and after that Fiery Avenger. Information on these two quests has been provided as well.

Quest Start

Hail and talk to Irak Altil in the Plane of Fear to start the quest.
Say 'what sword', 'what shield' and 'what breastplate' to him to get information on what you need to obtain.

The Gleaming Crested Sword

  1. Go to The Hole. Kill the Keeper of Tombs for the Tainted Sword.
  2. Go to West Freeport. Go to the Peasant Woman at the Monk Guild and say 'I will take it' to get the Bucket of Water.
  3. Give the Bucket of Water to Joshua [near the bakery] to get the Bucket of Pure Water.
  4. Go to Erudian. Give the Tainted Sword and Bucket of Pure Water to Reklon Gnallen to get the Gleaming Crested Sword.

The Gleaming Crested Shield

  1. Go to Nektulos Forest. Kill Kirak Vil for the Tainted Shield.
  2. Go to Felwithe. Give the Tainted Shield to Elia the Pure to get the Gleaming Crested Shield.

The Gleaming Crested Breastplate

  1. Go to the Plane of Hate. Kill the Thought Corrupter for the Tainted Darksteel Breastplate.
  2. Go to Kaladim. Hail and talk to Jark Stonebraids, who can be found in the mines. Say 'some' to him, then say 'I will get your dinner.'
  3. Go to Nella Stonebraids and ask her for Jark's Dinner to get Jark's Dinner.
  4. Give Jark's Dinner to Jark Stonebraids to get the Pure Crystal.
  5. Go to Erudian. Give the Tainted Darksteel Breastplate and the Pure Crystal to Reklon Gnallen to get the Gleaming Crested Breastplate.

Mark of Atonement

Give the Gleaming Crested Sword, Gleaming Crested Shield, and Gleaming Crested Breastplate to Reklon Gnallen to get the Mark of Atonement.

Fiery Defender

Go to the Plane of Fear. Give the Mark of Atonement and Fiery Avenger to Irak Altil to get Fiery Defender.

Sub-Quest: Firey Avenger

Go to the Plane of Sky. Talk to Dirkog Steelhand and give him 500pp to spawn Akera, then talk to Akera on Island Four to find out what you need to obtain. You can also collect all the items needed for the quest and then see Akera to do it all as one trip.
[Note: You need high Deepwater Knight faction for this quest.]

The Blessing of Nobility: Soulfire

If you do not already have Soulfire, see below for information on this quest.

The Blessing of Sacrafice: Ghoulbane

Go to Upper Guk. Kill the Froglok Shin Lord to get Ghoulbane.

The Head and Robe of Miragul:

  1. Kill Lord Nagafin, Faydedar [non-triggered], Talendor, Servelious or Ragefire for the Burnt Book
  2. Kill Lady Vox, Faydedar [non-triggered], or Gorenaire for the Frost Book
  3. Buy about 200 Red Wine from any merchant.
  4. Go to Neriak 3rd Gate. Give all the Red Wine [not stacked] to Lokar To'Biath.
  5. Go to Neriak Commons. Go to Rysva To'Biath in the Blind Fish Tavern and /con her to make sure she is warmly.
  6. Give the Burnt Book, Frost Book and 1000PP to Rysva To'Biath to get the Book of Scale.
  7. Go to the Ocean of Tears. Give the Book of Scale to the Oracle to get Miragul's Philactry.
  8. Go to the Everfrost Caves. Give Miragul's Philactry to the liche to spawn the true Miragul.
  9. Kill Miragul to get his robe and head.

Fiery Avenger

Give Soulfire, Ghoulbane, Miragul's Robe and Miragul's Head to Akera to get Firey Avenger.

Sub-Quest: Soulfire

The Soulfire quest technically starts with Kalatrina Plossen in North Freeport. However, much of the beginning portion of this quest can be completely skipped. As such, I have left those parts out.

Testimony of Truth:

  1. Go to North Freeport. Merkos Quentos to start the quest for the Test of Truth.
  2. Buy a Cloth Shirt from a merchant in Freeport. It must be size medium. You can check by seeing if Humans can wear it.
  3. Go to East Commons. Give the Cloth Shirt to Altunic Jartin to get the Token of Generosity.
  4. Go to North Ro. Kill a Desert Tarantula to get a Spider Venom Sac.
  5. Go to North Freeport. Give the Spider Venom Sac to Merkos Quentos to get the Token of Bravery.
  6. Give the Token of Generosity and the Token of Bravery to Merkos to spawn Guard Willia.
  7. Enter the sewer near the Hall of Truth and you will see Guard Willia to the right. Kill Guard Willia to get the Token of Truth
  8. Give the Token of Truth to Merkos Quentos to get the Testimony of Truth.

Brother Hayle's Letter:

  1. Go to East Freeport. Go to Seafarer's Roost, buy a Drom's Champagne and wait for Tykar Renlin to spawn.
  2. Give Drom's Chapagne to Tykar Renlin.
  3. Keep buying Drom's Champagne and giving it to Tukar Renlin until you get a blanket from him. Since Drom's Champagne is Lore, you have to do it one at a time.
  4. Get Edible Goo by combining a Fire Beetle Eye and a Rat Ear in an Oven. You can also have a player Baker make this for you, since it is tradeable.
  5. Get Bog Juice by combining a Snake Skin, bottle, and water flask in a Still. You can also have a player Brewer make this for you, since it is tradeable.
  6. Go to West Freeport. Enter the Jail under the Arena and you fill find Zimel in the first sell.
  7. Give the Blanket to Zimel.
  8. Give the Edible Goo and Bog Juice to Zimel to get the key H.K. 102.
  9. Go to Highhold Keep. Give the female banker the key and she will give you a note to give to Brother Hayle.
  10. If you do not already /con Warmly to the Priests of Life in Qeynos, go to Qeynos and find Nashun Novashine. Give him 2GP to gain faction with the Priests of Life, and repeat this until he /cons Warmly to you.
  11. Go to Splitpaw. You will find Brother Hayle in the Jail. Open the outer jail door by using the wall switch. You will find Brother Hayle in the last cell. Give Brother Hayle the note that the banker gave you to get a letter from him.

The Brilliant Sword of Faith:

  1. Go to West Freeport. Kill Sir Lucan in the Millitia House to spawn the undead version of Sir Lucan.
  2. Kill the undead version of Sir Lucan to get Lucan's Testimony.
  3. Go to North Freeport. Give Lucan's Testimony to Valeron Dushire to get the Brilliant Sword of Faith.

Glowing Hilt:

Go to Castle Mistmoore. Kill Xicotl, who spawns in the Bathroom to get the Glowing Hilt.

Soulfire:

Go to Splitpaw. Give Brother Hayle the Testimony of Truth, Brother Hayle's Letter, the Brilliant Sword of Faith and the Glowing Hilt to get Soulfire.
