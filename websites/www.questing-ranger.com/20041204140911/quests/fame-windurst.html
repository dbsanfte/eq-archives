Windurst Fame

Any quest performed in either Windurst, Mhaura or Kazham will give you fame with Windurst. However there are several quests that are repeatable. All turn in items for these quests can be purchased at the auction house. While thre are many other repeatable quests, most have turn-in items that are rare/ex drops from mobs.

A Discerning Eye (Windurst)
Client: Pygmalion
Location: Airship Dock, Port Windurst [M-7]
Requirements: Rank 5

Creepy Crawlies
Client: Illu Bohjaa
Location: Dhalmel Farm, Windurst Woods [H-6]
Turn-In Items: 3 Crawler Calculi or 3 Silk Thread

Something Fishy
Client: Tokaka
Location: Fisherman's Guild, Port Windurst [C-8]
Turn-In Items: Bastore Sardine
Note: You must zone and talk to her again after each turn-in.

Fame Checkers:
- Zabireo-Hajigo, Timbre Timbers Tavern, Windurst Waters [F-10]

Fame Levels:
1: Hmm... Sorry, but I have never heard that name before.
2: Now was that the name of the ...? No, that was some other guy.
3. I've heard pretty good things about you.
4: I heard some guys talking about you over their dinners at the eatery last night.
5: There aren't many Windurstians who don't know that name. We're all proud to have you on our side. Keep up the great work! 6: You'd have to be living inside a hole to not know of you.
7: There isn't a soul in all of windurst that has yet to hear of your tales.
8: A day doesn’t go by when I fail to hear a tale of your deeds.
9: I am honored to have the hero of Windurst in my presence.
