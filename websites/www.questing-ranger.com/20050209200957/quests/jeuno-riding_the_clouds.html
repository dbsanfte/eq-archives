Riding the Clouds
Client: Maat
Location: Grand Duke Palace [H-5]
Requirements: Level 61+, Whence Blows the Wind
Items: 4 Kindred's Seals
Reward: Level Cap of 65
Title: Cloud Breaker

Summary: Use Maat's hints to search out four people who will trade you a strange stone for a Kindred's seal. Only then will you know your true limits.

For this quest, you will get clues that describe four random NPCs in Vana'dial. These NPCs are either just random NPCs or associated with quests and missions. When I did the quest, I got one NPC in each of the starting cities plus one of the other areas. A complete list of known NPCs and clues can be found at Allakhazam at the following link:
http://ffxi.allakhazam.com/db/quests.html?fquest=320
