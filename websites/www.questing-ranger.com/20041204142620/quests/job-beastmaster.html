Beastmaster Job Quest

Chocobo's Wounds [Part 1]
Client: Brutus
Location: Chocobo Stables [G-7]
Requirements: Level 20+
Items: 4 Gausebit Wildgrass
Reward: Chocobo License [ability to ride chocobos]
Title: Chocobo Trainer

This quest takes period over 6 days Vana'diel time [5 hours], so make sure that you have enough time to do it before starting it. More than likely you will have completed this quest long before reach Level 30, but it is included here just in case. ^^

Save My Son [Part 2]
Client: Dietmund
Location: Merchant's House, Lower Jeuno [G-11]
Requirements: Chocobo's Wounds, Level 30+
Reward: Beast Whistle, 2100 gil, Beastmaster Job

You'll want to head to the area around F-8 in Qufim Island. Look for a ramp that heads downward along the edge of the cliff. Once here, go down a bit but not all the way. You will now want to wait until the time is somewhere between 20:00 and 4:00 as this is when the flower will be in full bloom. You will need to use Sneak at this point in order to get by the Kraken safely. Double-click on the "Night Flowers" to start the intermission. Afterwards, you will want to head back to Dietmund's house, then talk to Brutus at the Chocobo Stable in Upper Jeuno.
