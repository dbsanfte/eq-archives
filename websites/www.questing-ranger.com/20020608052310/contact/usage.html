Contact Information: Content Usage

Philosophy:

Questing Ranger was started under the phiolosphy that questing related information should be freely shared. Such information should also be present in such a matter as to be complete, accurate and easy to read. Each FAQ document at this site represents hours of researching, actually doing the quest in most cases, writting up the FAQ and finding links to the best maps available. Whenever information presented is unverified or missing, this will be stated.

Using FAQs - Preliminary Versions:
In all cases, do not reproduce preliminary FAQs. Although the information that is present should be accurate, is has yet to be proofread for errors nor is it necessarily complete. These FAQs will also be missing one or more sections. They are presented here as works in progress.

Using FAQs - Final Versions:
Non-profit web sites and web pages are more than welcome to reproduce my FAQ documents in their entirety without making any modifications to the document.
If you wish to modify one of my FAQs to fit the look and feel of your website, please contact me by email first before doing so. I am not going to say no, but there will be sections that I will want to have remain intact. This will vary depending on the FAQ.

Gallery:
You may freely take and do what you want with any image that contains your character. There is no need to even ask me or tell me that you are doing this. The same applies to guilds in regards to images that containes one of their members. I do ask that if the image is being presented on a site that you do host on your server and not just link to the file on my site.
If you wish to present an image that does not fit into one of the above situations on your site, please ask me first. In most cases, I will not care. However a few images are of friends that no longer play and will not want to have these images reproduced.
