Painful Memory
Client: Mertaire
Location: Merry Minstrel Meadhouse, Lower Jeuno [I-8]
Requirements: Level 40+ Bard
Reward: Paper Knife

Summary: Take Mertaire's bracelet to Ranguemont Pass and immerse it in the Waters of Oblivion.

Mertaire wants your help disposing of a ring, which has painful memories of his lost love, so he cannot bring himself to do it himself. The spot he has chosen is a pool of water inside Rangemont Pass. However this is not as easy as it sounds as you will encounter a notorious monster, so gather a party then head to Rangemont Pass, then go to [D-5] to get to the pool of water.

Clicking on the "???" spot at the water will spawn the notorious monster Tros. Both Barwatera and Water Carol will help due the the water-based AE that he does. Once Tros is dead, inspect the "???" again. Mertaire will show up as he realised he should throw the ring in the water himself and gives you the Paper Knife as thanks for your help.
