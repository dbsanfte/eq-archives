Moogles in the Wild
Client: Moogle
Location: Mog House
Requirements: The Moogle's Picnic!, Noble Bed in House
Items: Raptor Mantle, Wool Hat
Reward: Mog House Safe increased to 80 items
Title: Mog's Loving Master

Summary: Your moogle wants to take a raptor mantle ad a wool hat to his friends in the Outlands. He sure would be happy if you found them for him.

As with the previous mog quests, the bed needs to be placed in the house using Layout. The quest will only occur on Sundays after the weekly conquest update if your moogle is home during the update. If you any other bed in the house, it will not impact your ability to get this quest. The raptor mantle can be purchased at the auction house, and the wool hat from either the hat shop in Windurst or at the auction house.
