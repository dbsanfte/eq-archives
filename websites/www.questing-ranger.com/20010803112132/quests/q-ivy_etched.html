Ivy Etched Helm

This quest is given by Gandari in the Kithikor Woods Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger the quest is 'I am interested in Helm.'

  1. Go to Mistmoore Castle and kill the Deathly Ushers for the Rune of Ivy [Top.] These mobs are female Dark Elf Warriors.
  2. Go to Rathetear Mountains and kill Tarskuk for the Cyclops Charm.
  3. Buy a Saphire, which is available from most merchants that sells Jewlrey Craft supplies.
  4. Give Gandari the Rune of Ivy, Cyclops Charm, and Saphire and she will give you the Ivy Etched Helm.

Ivy Etched Sleeves

  4. Give Gandari the Rune of Ivy, Heart of Fire, and two Star Rubies [not stacked] and she will give you the Ivy Etched Sleeves.

Ivy Etched Leggings

This quest is given by Leaf Falldim at the Kithicor Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger this quest is 'What about Ivy Etched Leggings.'

  1. Go to Gornit Isle in Ocean of Tears and kill Wiltin Windwalker for the Quiver of Kithicor.
    This item is a container, so you will need to have a General Inventory slot free when you loot it. If you do not, it will poof as the item is no drop and will not be able to fall to the ground. Also, Wiltin Windwalker's placeholder is a Buccaneer. Do not attack the Buccaneer as you could end up waiting two hours for a respawn. If you camp Wiltin Windwalker without attacking the Buccaneer, the Buccaneer will despawn after an hour. Either Wiltin Windwalker or the Buccaneer will spawn a few minutes later.
  2. Go to Lesser Faydark and kill the Brownie Outcasts for the six named arrows: Acknowledgment, Bravery, Defiance, Gallantry, Fortitude and Recognition.
  3. Place one of each arrow in the Quiver of Kithicor, and combine the quiver to get the "Quiver of Kithicor - full."
  4. Go to the jewlrey shop in Erudin and buy a Star of Odus.
  5. Give Leaf Falldim the Quiver of Kithicor - full and the Star of Odus to get the Ivy Etched Leggings.

Ivy Etched Boots

This quest is given by Leaf Falldim at the Kithicor Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger this quest is 'I will earn the Ivy Etched Boots.'

  1. Mistmoore Granite
    Go to Mitmoore and kill Fouting Gargoyles, Jeering Gargoyles or Leering Gargoyles for Mistmoore Granite. The granite drops very rarely off of the Gargoyles outside of the castle. You have a better chance of getting the Granite inside of the castle. You need two pieces of Mistmoore Granite to complete this quest.
  2. Bar of Ronium
    Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro. Talk to Lord Searfire to get the Cauldron.
    1. Mistmoore Granite: You should have this item already from the above step, so just place one in the cauldron.
    2. Sands of Ro: Go to the Oasis and kill Sand Giants until you get the Sands of Ro, then place it in the cauldron.
    3. Soil of Underfoot: Kill faries until you get four fairy dust. Give the four Fairy Dusts to Priestess Ghalea in the Cleric guild, and she will give you the Soil of Underfoot. Place it in the cauldron.
    4. Melatite: Go to SolA to the Gnome area. You can kill the gnomish robots in this area for the Melatite, which is a rare drop. However, some people will kill the robots for whatever reason, sell the Melatite to the Gnome merchants, and you will sometimes them selling it. Place one Melatite into the cauldron.
    5. Enchanted Bar of Platinum: You can either by these from a player enchanter, or you can buy two Bars of Platinum and have an enchanter enchant them for you. Place two Enchanted Bars of Platinum in the Cauldron, and make sure they are not stacked.
    6. Hit the combine button on the cauldron and you will receive a Bar of Ronium.
  3. Give Leaf Falldim the Mistmoore Granite, a Bar of Ronium and 2000 Gold Pieces to get the Ivy Etched Boots. [Note: Giving him the equivalent amount using other coins will not work.]
