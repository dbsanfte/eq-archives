Warder Petition

Reprinted with consent from Aeril Crimsonhawk.

What's in a Name?
Assassin. Grandmaster. Sorcerer. High Priest. These are the coveted titles that let everyone in EQ know that you have fought the long fight, been in for the long haul. Title that Verant has reserved for players tenacious enough to play through 60 levels on one character, a feat that I'm sure no on will argue is worthy of some recognition anywhere you go. But it is ironic, in my opinion, that the same people who have granted these titles have also seen fit to devalue one of these titles in the most eronious way... by handing the title of a level 60 Ranger to a pet, one that may be summoned up by any Beastlord once he has past level 9. I think it is degrading and insulting to every Ranger that has managed to reach their 60th season, only to find a little kitty cat summoned up by "Joe the Beastlord" and is instantly named Joe's Warder.

Funk & Wagnall's Dictionary defines Warder as: A keeper; guard; sentinel; watchman. Appropriate for either group, to be sure. But inappropriate to be used for both. I find it incredibly disheartening for all of us Rangers who have "get to level 60" on our Everquest life's goal list. I know everyone has one of those.... a list of all the things we want to do some day in Everquest. Mine is pretty long... but it includes getting a fletching skill above 200 (mine hit 202 yesterday) and also includes getting my Ranger his epic weapons, a full set of Golden Leaf Armor (Skyshrine Quest armor), the Helm of the Tracker, and, I think most importantly, becoming the best Warder in all of Norrath, or at least the best on Veeshan server. Those are my goals. But hey, if I want a Warder, why not just make a Beast Lord? It'll be a lot quicker that way.........

I think everyone can agree on this point; I can't see a reason for the pet to not have a name that is unobtrusive to everyone. I certainly also wouldn't support the pet being named "Joe's Hierophant" or "Jack's Crusader" or "Jane's Warlord" Those are simply titles that I respect and would not submit to the sort of debasement that comes of having your title given to a kitten! This showing of the lack of either respect of thought on the part of Verant has me very dissappointed in Everquest: I feel like a child who just found out there's no Santa Claus. The solution that I propose is simple: the Beast Lord pet needs a new name. Something that suggests power and might, but in their own right. Some suggestions: Keeper, Watcher, Sentinel, Guard, Protector. Be creative and give the Beastlord class something of which they can be very proud, but not at the expense of the Ranger.

Aeril Crimsonhawk
40 Ranger
Champions of Hope
Veeshan Server

Aeril Crimsonkawk has started a petition on this issue. If you wish to support it, please send an email to warder_title@hotmail.com. Please include your character name, character class and server name.
