Samurai Job Quest

Forge Your Destiny [Samurai]
Client: Jaucribaix
Location: Near Captain's Quarters, Norg [K-8]
Requirements: Level 30+
Items: Sacred Sprig, Oriental Steel, Bomb Steel, Sacred Branch
Reward: Mumeito, Samurai Job
Title: Bushido Blade

The two purplis you need to talk to are Ranemaud and Aeka, who give you a sacred sprig and oriental steel. Afterwards you have two triggered boss fights to complete. For the bomb steel, you will want to go to Konschtat Highlands to the cave at [D-8] and trade the oriental steel to the unidentified point. This will trigger Forger, who drops the bomb steel. For the sacred branch, you need to go to the east end of Zi'tah Sanctuary [L-10] and loook for a giant tree. Use a hatchet here to trigger the fight with the Guardian Treant. With this out of the way, you can trade the sacred sprig to the unidentified point to get the sacred branch.

Once you have both items, return to Jaucribaix and give them to him. You will now need to wait three ingame days [3 hours] for him to make the sword. Once it is completed, Jaucribaix will give you mumeito and the ability to select the samurai job.
