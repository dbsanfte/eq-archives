Quest Change:
May 8, 2002 Patch

The ranger epic quest has been confirmed to be not completeable if you are under L47. Making the final turn in for Swiftwind will result in not Faelin not giving you the reward. It has not been confirmed at this time as to if Telin Darkforest will allow you to start the quest below if you are below the level limit.

There is no guarantee that GMs will make reinbusements for epic turn-in items being lost due to being too low to complete the quest.
