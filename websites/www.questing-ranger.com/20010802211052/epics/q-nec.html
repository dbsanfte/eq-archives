The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Necromancer epic quest for the Scythe of the Shadowed Soul.

Quest Start

Talk to Venenzi Oberzendi by the river in Nektulos Forest to start the quest.

  1. Go to Lake Rathetear. Talk to Kazen Facae.
  2. Go to East Freeport. Find and kill Sir Edwin Motte to get his head.
  3. Go to Lake Rathe. Give Motte's head to Kazen Facae to get the Symbol of the Apprentice.
  4. Go to Nektulos Forest. Give the Symbol of the Apprentice to Venenzi Oberzendi to get the Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice.
  5. Go to Najena. Kill Najena to get the Flowing Black Robe.
  6. Go to Nektulos Forest. Give the Flowning Black Robe to the dark elf that gave you the quest to get the moss.
  7. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give the moss and Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice to Emkel to get the Symbol of the Serpent.
  8. Go to Swamp of No Hope. Give the Symbol of the Serpent to Ssessthrass to get the Scaled Symbol of the Serpent.
  9. Go to Chardok. Kill the Royal Herbalist to get the Manisi Herb.
  10. Go to Swamp of No Hope. Give the Scaled Symbol of the Serpent and the Manisi Herb to Ssessthrass to get the Refined Manisi Herb.
  11. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give the Refined Manisi Herb to Emkel to get the Symbol of Testing.
  12. Give the Symbol of Testing to Kazen Facae and three mobs will spawn.
  13. Kill all three mobs to get the Symbol of Insanity.
  14. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Symbol of Insanity to Drendico Metalbones to get his journal.
  15. Go to Plane of Sky. Do the Cloak of Spiroc Feathers quest to get the Cloak of Spiroc Feathers.
    [NOTE: See below for the quest.]
  16. Go to Plane of Hate. Kill Innoruuk to get the Eye of Innoruuk.
  17. Go to Plane of Fear. Kill Cazic Thule to get the Blood of Cazic Thule.
  18. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Cloak of Spiroc Feathers, Eye of Innoruuk, Blood of Cazic Thule and Drendico's Journal to Drendico Metalbones to get the Prepared Reagent Box.
  19. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give the Prepared Reagent Box to Kazen Facae to get the Tome of Instruction.
  20. Go to Plane of Sky. Go to Island 3 and give Gkzzallk the Tome of Instruction to get Gkzzallk in Box.
  21. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give Gkzzallk in Box to Kazen Facae to get the Scythe of the Shadowed Soul.

Sub-Quest: Cloak of Spiroc Feathers

  1. Hail and talk to Drakis Bloodcaster in the Plane of Sky to start the quest.
  2. Then talk to Jzil GSix.
  3. Go to Island 3. Kill the Azeraks to get the Silver Disk.
  4. Go to Island 4. Kill mobs to get the Spiroc Feather.
  5. Go to Island 5. Kill the Keeper of Souls to get the Black Silk Cape.
  6. Turn in all three items to get the Cloak of Spiroc Feathers.
