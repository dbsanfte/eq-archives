Bastok Markets Quests

The Bare Bones
Client: Degenhard
Location: Near Galkan Bridge, Bastok Market [H-10]
Items: Bone Chip
Reward: Map of Dangruf Wadi

Bone chips drop off undead mobs and can also be purchased from the auction house under materials then bonecrafting.

Gourmet
Client: Salimah
Location: Trader's Home, Bastok Markets [L-8]
Requirements: none
Items: Sleepshroom, Treant Bulb or Wild Onion
Reward: variable gil
Title: Mommy's Helper

The ingredient that Salimah wants varies according to the time of day:
* 6:00-11:59 Treant Bulb [200 gil]
* 12:00-17:59 Wild Onion [350 gil]
* 18:00-5:59 Sleepshroom [200 gil.]

Also while this quest is repeatable, you need to leave the zone and come back before you can turn in another ingredient. However you do not need to talk to her each time, which does help significantly if repeating this quest multiple times for fame.
