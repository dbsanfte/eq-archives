Inventory & Mog Safe Quests

The Gobbiebag Part I
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: none
Items: Dhalmel Leather, Linen Cloth, Peridot, Steel Ingot
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 35 Items

All items needed for this quest can be obtained through crafting or purchased at the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part II
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part I
Items: Mythril Ingot, Ram Leather, Turquoise, Wool Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 40 Items

All items needed for this quest can be obtained through crafting or purchased at the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part III
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part II
Items: Gold Ingot, Painite, Tiger Leather, Velvet Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 45 Items

Of the gobbiebag quests, this one will cost the most if you are buying the ingredients from the auction house.

The Gobbiebag Part IV
Client: Bluffnix
Location: Muckvix's Junk Shop, Lower Jeuno [H-9]
Requirements: The Gobbiebag Part II
Items: Cermet Chunk, Darksteel Ingot, Goshenite, Silk Cloth
Reward: Increased Inventory Size to 50 Items

If you managed to complete the third quest in this series, the ingredients for this quest are significantly cheaper at the auction house.

Give a Moogle a Break
Client: Moogle
Location: Mog House
Requirements: Bronze Bed in House
Items: Beetle Ring, Power Bow
Reward: Mog House Safe increased to 60 items
Title: Mog's Kind Master

The Bronze Bed must be placed in the house using Layout. Also you can only get this quest on Sundays after the weekly conquest update. Additionally your moogle needs to be at home and not at a rent-a-room while the update is going on for you to get the quest.

The Moogle's Picnic!
Client: Moogle
Location: Mog House
Requirements: Give a Moogle a Break, Mahogany Bed in House
Items: Selbina Butter, Shrimp Lure
Reward: Mog House Safe increased to 70 items
Title: Mog's Exceptionally Kind Master

As with the previous mog quest, the bed needs to be placed in the house using Layout. The quest will only occur on Sundays after the weekly conquest update if your moogle is home during the update. If you have the Bronze Bed still, it will not impact your ability to get this quest. Both items can be found for sale at the auction house. Selbina Butter is a Food Ingredient and the Shrimp Lure is under weapons, ranged then fishing.

Moogles in the Wind
Client: Moogle
Location: Mog House
Requirements: The Moogle's Picnic!, Noble Bed in House
Items: Raptor Mantle, Wool Hat
Reward: Mog House Safe increased to 80 items
Title: Mog's Loving Master

As with the previous mog quests, the bed needs to be placed in the house using Layout. The quest will only occur on Sundays after the weekly conquest update if your moogle is home during the update. If you any other bed in the house, it will not impact your ability to get this quest. The raptor mantle can be purchased at the auction house, and the wool hat from either the hat shop in Windurst or at the auction house.
