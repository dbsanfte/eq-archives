North San d'Oria Quests

Advanced Teamwork
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Requirements: Intermediate Teamwork
Items: Party of 6 of the same job
Reward: Horn Ring

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all having the same main job. Subjobs do not count and are not required to be the same or even there at all.

Exit the Gambler
Clients: Aurege and Guilberdrier
Location: Carpenters' Guild, North San d'Oria [E-4]
Items: 5G
Reward: Map of King Ranperre's Tomb
Title: Daytime Gambler

Varchet can be found in South San d'Oria in Lion Square [L-6], not far from the residential area. When you talk to him, he'll agree to go back to work if you can beat him in a dice game. Trade him 5 gil to play, but he will give you 10 gil when you win. [Should you not win, just keep trading him 5gil until you do] Once you have beat Varchet, return to Aurege and Guilberdrier.

Father and Son
Client: Aibeche
Location: Parade Grounds, North San d'Oria [J-9]
Title: Family Counselor

After talking to Aibeche, you will want to go to Helbort's Blades in South San d'Oria to talk to Exoroche [K-7]. Now return to Aibeche to talk to him again. Exoroche shows up and gives you Aibeche's Willow Fishing Rod. While you can technically keep it, you will not earn the title until you return it to Aibeche.

Fear of the Dark
Client: Secodiand
Location: Laborman's Way, North San d'Oria[E-6]
Items: 2 Bat Wings
Reward: 200G

This is an extremely easy quest that can be repeated many times to earn fame with San d'Oria. Bat wings can drop off any kind of bat as well as are purchasable at the auction house.

Gates to Paradise
Client: O'bergient
Location: Cathderal Manuscript Room, North San d'Oria [L-7]
Items: Scripture of Wind
Reward: Cotton Cape
Title: Pious One

O'bergient gives you the Scripture of Wind after you talk to him. The friar you need to find is Faurbellant, who can be found towards the northeast section of the Crag of Holla. You may want to just walk around the edge of it until you come to him as he will be along the wall.

Growing Flowers
Client: Kuu Mohzolhi
Location: Phoenix Perch Inn, North San d'Oria [F-7]
Items: Marguerite
Reward: Mog House Exit Upgrade

The Marguerite you need for this quest can be obtained through gardening. It is also purchaseable at the auction house under Materials then Alchemy.

Intermediate Teamwork
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Requirements: Introduction to Teamwork
Items: Party of 6 from the same race
Reward: Scroll of Mage's Ballad

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all of the same race: hume, tarutaru, mithra, elvaan or galka. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward.

Introduction to Teamwork
Client: Vilatroire
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [C-7]
Items: Party of 6 from the same country
Reward: Shell Ring

For this quest, you just need to form a group of six people all from the same nation: San d'Oria, Bastok or Windurst. Once you have done so, talk to Vilatroire to receive your reward.

The Holy Crest - incomplete
Client: Oiheaurese
Location: Cathderal Manuscript Room, North San d'Oria [L-7]
Requirements: Level 30+, Rank 2 if San d'Orian, Rank 3 if from Bastok or Windurst
Items: Pickaxe, Wyvern Egg
Reward: Dragoon Job

First you will want to talk to Arminibit and Ceraulian, who are located in Cargo Room A of Port San d'Oria. Once this is done, you need to head to the Bostaunieux Oubliette, take the first right and continue down the hall until you see Novalmauge wandering the corridor. To get to the Bostaunieux Oubliette, you need to go though Chateau d'Oraguille. If you cannot enter Chateau d'Oraguille, your Mission Rank level is not high enough. Citizens of San d'Oria need to be Rank 2. Citizens of either Bastok or Windurst need to be on Mission 3 and have spoken to their respective ambassador in San d'Oria. You will now want talk to Morjean in the Manuscript Room of the Cathedrial in North San d'Oria. Oiheaurese will show up during the intermission and you will be given the quest at this point.

You will now need to get a Pickaxe [only one should do] and head to the Maze of Shakhrami. While most walk throughs I have seen recommend entering from the Buburimu Peninsula side, I recommend that you enter from Tahrongi Canyon. The mobs are lower in level and if you are doing this at Level 30 exactly they will not agro on you. [Whereas if you enter from the Buburimu Peninsula side at Level 30, they will agro.] You will need to find an Excavation Point. [My recommendation here is to print out the maps for this area that are available from the Mining Logging Harvesting Excavition Map website. Once you have located the Excavation Point, use a pickaxe on it to get the Wyvern Egg.

Return to Morjean in the Cathedrial Manuscript Room in order to continue on with the quest. You will now need to go to Meriphataud Mountains and head towards the area labeled "Dragon Spine" the map that is around the zone to Castle Oztroja.

The weapon type mobs around here will agro. I recommend finding a spot away from the mobs where you can put up sneak and invisibility if you have it. Along the white thing in the [K-8] section, you will find a "???" spot. Trade the Wyvern Egg to it, which will result in an intermission.

You now will want to go to Chateau d'Oraguille in North San d'Oria, then talk to Rahal in the Royal Knights area. Once this is done, head to Ghelsba Outpost to the hut at [G-10] the investigate the door for the boss fight. more to come...

The Medicine Woman
Client: Abeaule
Location: Inn, North San d'Oria [F-8]
Requirements: Trade in the Forest
Items: Old Amaura's Prescription, Insect Wing, Malboro Vine, Zinc Ore, Cold Medicine
Reward: 2,100G
Title: Traveling Medicine Man

Amaura can be found in one of the homes in Watchdog Alley, South San d'Oria. When you talk to her after having talked to Abeaule first, she gives you Old Amaura's Prescription, which is a key item that lists off the three ingredients you need to gather: insect wing, malboro vine and zinc ore. These all can drop from mobs or be purchased at the auction house. When you give all threee to Amaura, she gives you the cold medicine in return. Return to Abeaule with it to collect your reward.

The Trader in the Forest
Client: Abeaule
Location: Phoenix Perch Inn, South San d'Oria [F-8]
Items: Supplies Order, Clump of Batagreens
Reward: Robe, ability to buy Batagreens
Title: Green Grocer

Abeaule gives you the Supplies Order when he gives you the quest. You'll want to take it to Phairet, who can be found in the tower at the south end of West Ronfaure. After Phairet receives the order, he gives you the Clump of Batagreens, and you now have the ability to buy them from him in the future. When you give the Batagreens to Abeaule, he gives you a robe in return.

The Vicasque's Sermon
Client: Abioleget
Location: Cathedrial, North San d'Oria[M-7]
Requirements: Waters of the Cheval
Items: 70G, Blue Peas
Reward: Brass Ring
Title: Benevolent One

Blue Peas can be obtained at the Cathedrial for a donation of 70 gil. They can also be purchased that the auction house or obtained through gardening. Once you have them, give them to Andelain at the tower at the souheatern corner of East Ronfaure. After giving him the peas, return ton Abioleget.

Trial by Ice - incomplete
Client: Gulmama
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [E-7]
Reward: Shiva, 10,000G, Shiva Claws, Ice Belt, Ice Ring or Rust 'B' Gone

This is the high-level group fight against Shiva. While you can get the quest as soon as your fame level in San d'Oria reaches Rank 7, you cannot actually do the fight with any hopes of success until many levels later. While I have heard that this quest is repeatable, I cannot confirm this due to being too low to do the actual fight. [No further information on this quest will be added at this time.]

Trouble at the Sluice
Client: Belgidiveau
Location: Sluice Gates, North San d'Oria [G-8]
Requirements: The Rumor
Items: Dahlia, Neutralizer
Reward: Heavy Axe

After talking to Belgidiveau, go speak to Novalmauge who can be found wandering the first coridor to the right in the Bostaunieux Oubliette. [You can get there by going through the Cateau d'Oraguille.] Novalmauge can make the Neutralizer but will need a Dahlia. These are random drops from Goobbues, but can also be purchased from the Auction House under Alchemy materials. Once you get the Neutralizer, which will be a Key Item, just talk to Belgidiveau.

Undying Flames
Client: Pagisalis
Location: Cathedral Reliquary, North San d'Oria [M-6]
Items: 2 Beeswax
Reward: Friar's Rope
Title: Faith like a Candle
Summary: Collct two lumps of beeswax for making candles.

This is a simple quest that makes a nice belt for mage classes. Beeswax can be created through alchemy or purchased at the auction house.

Warding Vampires
Client: Maloquedil
Location: Parade Grounds, North San d'Oria [J-8]
Items: 2 Shaman Garlic
Reward: 900G
Title: Vampire Hunter D-Minus

Shaman garlic drops off orcish cursemakers in Yughott Grotto and Davoi. These are also ex items, so you cannot buy them.

Waters of the Cheval
Client: Miageau
Location: Cathedral, North San d'Oria [L-7]
Items: 10G, Blessed Waterskin, Cheval River Water
Reward: Wing Pendant
Title: The Pure One

After you get this quest, you will need to talk to Nouveil [to the right of Miageau] to buy the Blessed Waterskin. At this point you will want to go to the area of H-5 in East Ronfaure then look for a spot along the river which you can actually target. Once you find this spot, trade the Blessed Waterskin to it to obtain the Cheval River Water.
