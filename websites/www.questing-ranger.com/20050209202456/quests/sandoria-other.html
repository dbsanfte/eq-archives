A Discerning Eye (San d'Oria)
Client: Eddy
Location: Airship Dock, Port San d'Oria [H-6]
Requirements: Airship Pass
Items: Dropped Item
Reward: 500G
Title: Discerning Individual, Very Discerning Individual, Extremely Discerning Individual

Summary: You have been asked to return dropped item to its rightful owner. The passanger should be on the next airship flight.

Eddy gives you the Dropped Item [key item] when you start the quest. He then shows you a random npc and you need to memorize the race, gender, face type and armor. Get on the next airship and look for the npc you were show. When you found the correct one, talk to the npc and choose the option to give the item. If you selected the correct NPC, you'll get the reward.

The title you normally get from doing this quest is Discrning Individual. If you do these quests enough times, you can get the other two titles instead.

A Job for the Consortium
Client: Portaure
Location: Cargo Room B, Port San d'Oria [H-9]
Requirements: Airship Pass, Tenshodo Membership
Items: Brugaire Goods
Reward: 1,000G

Summary: The Brugaire Consortium wants you to smuggle a package onto the airship and take it to the Tenshodo in Jeuno.

Portaure gives you the Brugaire Goods [key item] that need to be smuggled when you start this quest. The only way you can get them to Jeuno safely is by riding an Airship from San d'Oria to Jeuno, where you will need to pass through customs. Should you pass through customs, head to the Tenshodo and talk to Yin Pocanakhu. Return to Portaure at this point to complete the quest.

If you try to enter Jeuno with the package by any other method, it will be damaged. If you get caught, the quest is over and you cannot ride airships leaving Jeuno until the start of the next day in Japan:
- EST 10:00 AM - EDT 11:00 AM
- CST 9:00 AM - CDT 10:00 AM
- MST 8:00 AM - MDT 9:00 AM
- PST 7:00 AM - PDT 8:00 AM

A Purchase of Arms
Client: Helbort
Location: Helbort's Blades, South San d'Oria [K-7]
Item: Order Sheet
Reward: Elm Staff
Title: Arms Trader

Summary: Find the traveling merchant Alexius in Jugner Forest and place an order for weapons.

When you talk to Helbort, he will give you an Order Sheet [Key Item.] Just head towards Lake Mechieume in Jugner Forest, which is where you will find Alexius [I-6.] Talk to him. Getting to Alexius can be difficult for lower level players due to the many dangers in this zone, so you might want to consider riding a chocobo to him. Once you have spoken with Alexius, return to Helbort and talk to him again.

A Timely Visit
Client: Deraquien
Requirements: Rank 2+ if San d'Orian, Rank 3+ if from other nations
Location: Watchdog Alley, South San d'Oria [G-8]
Reward: Medieval Collar
Title: Obsidian Storm

Summary: A criminal suspect has been spotted on La Theine Plateau. You have been asked to asist n his capture.

After you talk to Deraquien and start the quest, go to [F-6] in La Theine Plateau and talk to all the elvaan at the training area then talk to Narvecaint. Return to Deraquien, and talk to him again. Go to Chateau d'Oraguille and talk to Halver, then talk to Deraquien again. Enter the house at [D-7] in South San d'Oria and talk to Phillone, then talk to Deraquien again.

Head to King Ranperre's Tomb and find the exit in here that leads to Jugner Forest. There will be a "???" spot on the ground that needs to be in investigated between 18:00 - 04:00. When this happens, Giollemitte B Feroun and a Skeleton Esquire will spawn. The Skeleton Esquire can be put to sleep with Lullaby, but not the boss himself. Kill both of them then investigate the spot again.

Return to South San d'Oria and talk to Phillone [D-7] again. Go back to La Theine Plateau and talk to Narvecaint [F-6,] then return to South San d'Oria and talk to Phillone to finally finish the quest. While the reward for this quest isn't so great, it is still a nice quest to do for the intermissions as it covers some of the history involving San d'Oria and Bastok.

Cargo
Client: Vuntar
Location: Foot of Central Stairs, Selbina [H-9]
Items: Rolanberry881, Rolanberry874 or Rolanberry864
Reward: 800-3,000G

Summary: Vuntar will pay premium price for Rolanberries, but for only those that are thoroughly aged and can serve as food for long voyages.

Rolanberry881 can be obtained by using a regular Rolanberry to spawn the Guardian Crawler in Crawler's Nest. While it does not spawn every time, it will drop Rolanberry881 every time it is spawned and killed. Rolanberry874 is obtained by using Rolanberry881 to spawn the Drone Crawler. Rolanberry864 is obtained by using Rolanberry874 to spawn the Matron Crawler.

Summary: A child stands crying in the Parade Grounds. Find his father for him.

Ailbeche's father is Exorche, who can be found inside Helbort's Blades in South San d'Oria [K-7.] Talk to him then return to Ailbeche to talk to him again. Rosel the Armorer
Client: Rosel
Location: Rosel's Armor Shop, South San d'Oria [K-7]
Item: Receipt
Reward: 100G or 200G
Title: Entrance Denied

Summary: Deliver the receipt for the prince's cloak to the chateau guards.

When you start this quest, an intermission plays and Rosel gives you the receipt [key item.] It can be confusing as to which prince you are supposed to deliver it to, so you may want to talk to Rosel again as he will remind you. Go to North San d'Oria and talk to Guilerme, who is one of the guards in front of Chateau d'Oraguille. He will ask you which prince to give the receipt to, select the correct one and wait for him to return. Return to Rosel and talk to him again.

Sleepless Nights
Client: Paouala
Location: House on Squire Alley, South San d'Oria [M-8]
Item: Mary's Milk
Title: Sheep's Milk Deliverer

Summary: Paouala cannot sleep, so bring her some Mary's Milk to cure her insomnia.

Stary Mary is a notorious monster sheep that can be found around [F-7] in Konschtat Highlands. If you kill her for the milk, you will earn the title "Mary's Guide." However the big prize from this mob is Mary's Horn, which is worth a lot so expect a lot of competition for this mob. The Medicine Woman
Client: Abeaule
Location: Inn, North San d'Oria [F-8]
Requirements: The Trader in the Forest
Items: Insect Wing, Malboro Vine, Zinc Ore
Reward: 2100 G
Title: Traveling Medicine Man

Summary: A guest at the inn has fallen ill. Call on Amaura in Southern San d'Oria to get some cold medicine.

When you talk to Amaura, she gives you a list [Key Item] that tells you the ingredients that are needed. Insect Wings drop off any beetle type mob or can be purchased at the Auction House under Clothcraft Materials. Malboro Vines drop from Malboros and the easiest of these would be located in Pashhow Marshlands. It can also be purchased at the Auction House under Alchemy Materials. Zinc Ore drops from worms or can be purchased from the Auction House under Goldsmithing Materials. Once you have all three, give them to Amaura to get the Cold Medicine [Key Item,] then return to Abeaule at the Inn.

To Cure a Cough
Client: Amaura
Location: Watchdog Alley, South San d'Oria [G-7]
Item: Thyme Moss
Requirements: The Medicine Woman
Reward: 3000G
Title: A Moss Kind Person

Summary: Bring Amaura some thyme moss, and she'll concoct a brew with curative powers.

Talk to Nenee [F-6] then go next door and talk to Amaura [G-7]. In the house next to Amaura's, you will find a diary. Read all three pages to learn of Thyme Moss, then talk to Amaura again. You will now need to go to [F-9] in Davoi. However going directly to this spot will result in you encountering a wooden barricade. It will be necessary to walk through the water to get around to the back side of the barricade, where the "???" spot can be found. Examine it to get the Thyme Moss [Key Item.] Return to Amaura and talk to her to get the Cough Syrup [Key Item] then talk to Nenee, who gives you a Treasure Map [Key Item.] This map leads you to [H-6] in East Ronfaure. Examine the signpost in this area to get the reward.

Unexpected Treasure
Client: Morunaude
Location: Furniture Store, North San d'Oria [G-8]
Requirements: Cupboard in Moghouse
Item: Mistletoe
Reward: 12,000G

Summary: The small teacup found in a cupboard sold at this store appears to have royal origins. The owner would doubtlessly be grateful to have it back.

To start this quest, you need to purchase a Cupboard [ex] from the furniture store in North San d'Oria then place it in your moghouse using Layout Mode. Talk to your moogle, and a teacup [Key Item] will be found. Go back to the furniture store and talk to Morunaude to start the quest.

Go to the Cathedral and talk to Calovour [M-6] now. He asks for Mistletoe to make tea. This drops from Walking Trees in Jugner Forest or can be purchased from the Auction House under Alchemy Materials. Trade the Mistletoe to Calovour.

Waters of the Cheval
Client: Miageau
Location: Cathedral, North San d'Oria [L-7]
Items: Blessed Waterskin, Cheval Water
Reward: Wing Pendant
Title: The Pure One

Summary: Draw water from the Cheval River in East Ronfaure.

After talking to Miageau, talk to the other friar in the room to buy a Blessed Waterskin [ex] or 10 G. Now head to the Cheval River in East Ronfaure at [H-5].
