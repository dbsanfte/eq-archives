Mean Machine
Client: Unlucky Rat
Location: Cermet Refinery, Metalworks [G-7] Items: Slime Oil
Reward: Scroll of Warp

Summary: One of the factory's machines needs oiling, but Unlucky Rat is out of machine oil. Help him out by bringing him something that can be used as a substitute.

Slime Oil is a drop from slime-type mobs. However this is at L18 spell and slimes might be a bit difficult still at that point and even with two treasure hunters in the party, the are a rare drop. It can also be purchased from the Auction House under Alchemy Materials.
