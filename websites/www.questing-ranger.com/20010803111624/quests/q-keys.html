Cobalt Scar Key: Skyshrine Key

Technically there is a quest for this key, but it is long and tedious and dangerous... so why bother? I like to quest A LOT, and even I will not bother with the quest. Additionally, the quest is broken. You cannot even do it right now if you wanted to.

Instead, go to Skyshrine. Look for a group that is killing Ziglak Whipsewing for fun and exp. Ask them if you can loot an extra key. They will most likely have extra keys rotting away on his copse and not mind you looting one. Always ask first!

Howling Stones Key: Key to Charasis

  1. Go to Kaesora. Kill Xalgoz to get the Xalgozian Fang.
  2. Go to Lake of Ill Omen. Kill Chancellor of Di'Zok to get the Jade Chodakai Prod.
  3. Go to the Swamp of No Hope. Head towards the tunnel to Trakanon's Teeth.
  4. Look along the top part of the wall around POS 1150, NEG 4000 for a passageway.
  5. Enter the passageway to find Durgaz.
  6. Give the Xolgozian Fang and Jade Chodakai Prod to Durgaz to get the "Key to Charasis."

Sebilis Key: Trakanon Idol

  1. Go to Trakanon's Teeth.Kill the Froglok Forager to get the Medallion of Kunzar.
  2. Kill the Froglok Hunter to get the Medallion of Nathsar.
  3. Give the Medallion of Kunzar and the Medallion of Nathsar to Emperor Ganak to get the "Trakanon Idol."

Sleeper's Tomb Key

  1. Go to Dragon Necropolis. Hail and talk to Zlandicar. He sends you on an errand in exchange for the key that gives you access to Jaled Dar's Shade.
  2. Jaled Dar's Shade wants you to kill the five first brood dragons to get their talismans.
  3. Kill Lord Yelinak, Sontalak, Lendiniara, Klandicar and Zlandicar for their Talismans.
  4. Give the Talismans to Jaled Dar's Shade to get the key.

Veeshan's Peak Key: Rune of Scale

This quest is given by Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth. To complete the quest, you need to give him Trakanon's Tooth, Medallion of Jarasth, Medallion of Obulus, and Medallion of Kyong.

  1. Trakanon's Tooth
    Go to Sebilis. Kill Trakanon to get Trakanon's Tooth.
  2. Medallion of Jarath
    1. Go to Timorous Deep. Hail and talk to Xiblin Fizzlebik on Chessboard Island.
    2. Go to Swamp of No Hope. At POS 52, POS 2935, you will find the top part of the Medallion on the ground.
    3. Go to Lake of Ill Omen. Kill the Bloodgill Marauder at the bottom of the lake to get the bottom half of the medallion.
    4. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the two medallion halves to Xiblin Fizzlebik to get the Medallion of Jarath.
  3. Medallion of Obulus
    1. Go to Burning Woods. Hail and talk to Xilxen Klex [at NEG 680, NEG 1120.]
    2. To get the top half, either kill Sselot Dnass on the island in Warslike Woods or give him a Burnished Wood Stave, which drop off the Sarnak Aruspices in Chardok.
    3. Go to Sebilis. Kill the Pained Soul to get the bottom half.
    4. Go to Burning Woods. Give the two medallion halves to Xilxen Klex to get the Medallion of Obulus.
  4. Medallion of Kyong
    1. Go to Lake of Ill Omen. Hail and talk to Professor Akabao.
    2. Go to Chardok. Give a Black Sapphire and a Ruby to Niblek, located deep in the slave are to get the top half.
    3. Go to Karnor's Castle. Kill Verix Kylox to get the middle half of the medallion.
    4. Go to Kaesora. Kill mobs in the Library area to get the bottom half.
    5. Go to Lake of Ill Omen. Give the the medallion parts to Professor Akabao to get the Medallion of Kyong.
