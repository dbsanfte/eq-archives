The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Druid epic quest for the Nature Walkers Scimitar.

Quest Start

Talk to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods and say 'what action should I take' to get the Worn Note.

Shiny Tin Bowl

  1. Go Greater Faydark. Give the Worn Note to Faelin Bloodbriar to get Faelins Ring
    [Note: She spawns at POS 1500, POS 500; placeholder is an orc pawn.]
  2. Go to Kithicor Forest. Give Faelins Ring to Giz X`Tin to get the Dark Metal Coin.
    [Note: A Dark Reaver will spawn; just run from it.]
  3. Go to Burning Woods. Give the Dark Metal Coin to Telin Darkforest to get the Worn Dark Metal Coin.
  4. [Note: The following part must be done as one sequence and all takes place in East Karana]
    Give the Worn Dark Metal Coin to Althele to get Braided Grass Amulet.
    Give the Braided Grass Amulet to Sionae to get the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet.
    Give the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet to Nuien and he will return it to you.
    5. Give the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet to Teloa and she will lead you to the druid gathering.
    Kill the Dark Elf Corruptor and Reavers that spawn and loot the Fleshbound Tomb.
    Give the Fleshbound Tomb to Althele to get the Earth Stained Note
  5. Go to Misty Thicket. Give the Earth Stained Note to Ella Foodcrafter to get the Shiny Tin Bowl.

Create the Hardened Mixture

  1. Go to Everfrost and forage the Chilled Tundra Root
  2. Go to Greater Faydark and forage the Ripened Heartfruit
  3. Go to Innothule Swamp and forage the Speckled Molded Mushroom
  4. Go to Misty Thicket and forage the Sweetened Mudroot
  5. Place the Chilled Tundra Root, Ripened Heartfruit, Speckled Molded Mushroom, and Sweetened Mudroot in the Shiny Tin Bowl. Hit the Combine button to create the "Hardened Mixture

Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #1 [Ancient Pattern]

  1. Go to Timorous Deep. Talk to Alrik Farsight and say ‘I will take the artifact' to get the Crushed Pot.
  2. Go to Felwithe [B]. Give the Crushed Pot to Farios Elianos to get the Grocery List.
  3. Go to Felwithe [A]. Give the Grocery List to Merchant Nora to get the Bag of Provisions.
  4. Go to Felwithe [B]. Give the Bag of Provisions to Farios Elianos Receipt.
  5. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Receipt to Alrik Farsight to get the Ancient Pattern.

Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #2 [Platinum Speckled Powder]

  1. Go to Firiona Vie and forage a Rose of Firiona.
  2. Go to Surefall Glade. Give Merdan Fleetfoot the Rose of Firiona to get the Wood Painting.
  3. Go to Frontier Mountains. Give the Wood Painting to the Human Skeleton to get the Silver Chained Locket.
    [Note: Spawns around NEG 2100, POS 2000]
  4. Go to Surefall Glade. Give the Silver Chained Locket to Niera Farbreeze to get the Platinum Speckled Powder.

Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #3 [Enchanted Clay]

  1. Go City of Mist. Kill a Black Reaver to get a Jade Reaver.
    [Note: This item is tradeable.]
  2. Go to Kaladim [B]. Give the Jade Reaver to Kinlo Strongarm to get the Enchanted Clay.

Create the Runecrested Bowl

Combine the Ancient Pattern, Enchanted Clay, and Platinum Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel to make a Runecrested Bowl.
[Note: This requires no skill in pottery to do and any pottery wheel should work.]

The Elaborate Scimitar

  1. Go to Misty Thicket. Give the Hardened Mixture and Runecrested Bowl to Ella Foodcrafter to get the Softly Glowing Stone.
  2. Go to Karnor's Castle. Kill Venril Sathir to spawn Venril Sathirs remains.
  3. Cast the spell Dance of the Fireflies to Summon a Firefly Globe.
  4. Give the Firefly Globe to Venril Sathirs remains to spawn the Spirit of Venril Sathir.
  5. Give the spell Ressurection [Level 49 Cleric Spell] to the Spirit of Venril Sathir to respawn Venril Sathir.
  6. Kill Venril Sathir to get the Pulsing Green Stone.
  7. Go to Firiona Vie. Give the Softly Glowing Stone and the Pulsing Green Stone to Foloal Stormforest to get the Warmly Glowing Stone.
  8. Go to Misty Thicket. Give Ella Foodcrafter the Warmly Glowing Stone to get the Elaborate Scimitar.

Cleansed Spirit of Antonica

  1. Go to Everfrost. Kill the Corrupted Mammoth to get the Chunk of Tundra.
  2. Go to Lake Rathetear. Kill the Corrupted Aqua Goblin Shaman to get the Clean Lakewater.
  3. Go to Rathetear Mountains. Kill the Corrupted Hill Giant to get the Ancient Rock.
  4. Go to North Karana. Give the Chunk of Tundra, Clean Lakewater and Ancient Rock to the Withered Treant to get the Warm Pulsing Treant Heart.
  5. Go to South Karana. Give the Warm Pulsing Treant Heart to Yeka Ias to get the Cleansed Spirit of Antonica.

Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer

  1. Go to Kedge Keep. Kill the Corrupted Seahorse to get the Kedge Cave Crystals.
  2. Go to the Ocean of Tears. Kill the Corrupted Cyclops to get the Ocean of Tears Seavines.
  3. Go to Lesser Faydark. Kill the Corrupted Brownie to get the Green Heartwood Branch.
  4. Give the Kedge Cave Crystals, Ocean of Tears Seavines and Green Heartwood Branch to the Pained Unicorn to get the Gleaming Unicorn Horn.
  5. Go to Ak'Aknon. Give the Gleaming Unicorn Horn to Silox Azrix to get the Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer.

Cleansed Spirit of Kunark

  1. Go to the Swamp of No Hope. Kill Ulump Pujik to get the Froglok Essence.
  2. Go to the Emerald Jungle. Kill the Corrupted Gorilla to get the Green Tree Bark.
  3. Go to the Lake of Ill Omen. Kill the Corrupted Barracuda to get the Pure Lakewater.
  4. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Froglok Essence to Dolgin Codslayer to spawn Faydedar. He will return the Froglok Essence to you.
  5. Kill Faydedar to get the Pod of Seawater.
  6. Go to Overthere. Give the Froglok Essense, Green Tree Bark, Pure Lakewater and Pod of Seawater to Nekexin Virulence to get the Cleansed Spirit of Kunark.

Nature Walkers Scimitar

Go to North Karana. Give the Elaborate Scimitar, Cleansed Spirit of Antonica, Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer and Cleansed Spirit of Kunark to Xanuusus to get the Nature Walkers Scimitar.
