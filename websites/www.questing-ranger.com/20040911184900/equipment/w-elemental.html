Bard Weapons: Elemental Staves

Elemental stats and their upgraded versions have hidden effects that enhance spells of that type, such as Light Staff or Apollo's Staff for light spells. This hidden effect reduces resists and increases damage. The original Japanese thread on this can be found [here] There is also an [English thread] on this same topic.
