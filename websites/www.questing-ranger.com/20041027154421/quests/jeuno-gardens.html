RuLude Gardens Quests

Atop the Highest Mountain
Client: Maat
Location: Grand Duke Palace [H-5]
Requirements: In Defient Challenge, Level 51
Items: Round Fragicite, Square Fragicite, Triangle Fragicite
Reward: Level Cap of 60

This one requires an alliance and all takes place in Xarcabard. You will need sneak to get around everywhere in Xarcabard, and invisible as well when you get to the area with the demons. To complete this your alliance must work your way to three different caves, kill the Notorious Monster inside, then inspect the ground at the back of the cave to get a Key Item:
- Round Fragicite: Boreal Tiger [I-5]
- Square Fragicite: Boreal Coeurl [J-4]
- Triangle Fragicite: Boreal Hound [G-10]

In Defiant Challenge
Client: Maat
Location: Grand Duke Palace [H-5]
Requirements: Level 50
Items: Ancient Papyrus, Bomb Coal, Exoray Mold
Reward: Level Cap of 55

For this you need to fight three different mobs in three different zones for a rare/ex drop: - Ancient Papyrus: Lich, Eldieme Necropolis
- Bomb Coal: Exposure, Garlaige Citadel
- Exorary Mold: Exorary, Crawler's Nest

Of these three, the Exorary Mold is going to be the easiest. Although the drop rare seems rather low, even with a thief in the party, it is the one that you are most likely to be able to obtain while gaining exp. Both the Ancient Papyrus and Bomb Coal tend to require special genkai hunting trips.
