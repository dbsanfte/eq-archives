Vial of Velium Vapors [Thurgadin Gate Potion] FAQ
by Crydee Ossiriand, Torvonnilous Server
http://www.questing-ranger.com
Version: 2004.06.01

Technically this is not a quest on its own but an item that is created as part of the Prawer Shawl Quests that are based out of Thurgadin. The results of this sub-part will produce a potion that acts like a normal gate-style spell [as cast by any casting class] and gates to the middle of the first bridge inside of Thurgadin. Because this faq only covers making these potions for personal use and not for the shawl quests, the only NPC I am including is the one you need to talk to for making the potions even though there are other NPCs that eventually will refer you to her after completing the first three shawl quests.

You say, 'Hail, Frundle Frenklar'

You say, 'What velium vapors?'
Frundle Frenklar says 'Ahh, you come seeking my family recipe? After all you've done for our people it's the least I can do. First you must fashion a velium vial, then combine the vial with two small pieces of velium in this jar. That's all there is to it. Please keep the recipe a secret.'

You say, 'What velium vial?'
Frundle Frenklar says 'I'm no potter mind you, but if I remember correctly it's a small brick of velium, three crystalline silks, and two glass shards. Then you'll have to finish up by firing it in a kiln with a high quality firing sheet.'
*Frundle Frenklar gives you a Velium Purifier.*

Mastering Pottery

You will need to get pottery up to a skill of at least 122 for the Unfired Velium Vial to be trivial. The trivial raiting for the Velium Vial itself is very low, and the final combine to make the actual potion is no fail. Below are some recipes that will help you master this tradeskill.

Trivial Recipe Ingredients
21 Small Block of Clay Block of Clay, Water Flask
26 Clay/Water Mixture Small Block of Clay, Water Flask
36 Unfired Ceramic Lining Ceramic Lining Sketch, Small Block of Clay, Water Flask
54 Dye Vial Quality Firing Sheet, Unfired Dye Vial
82 Unfired Smoker Large Block of Clay, Smoker Sketch, Water Flask
102 Unfired Small Bowl Bowl Sketch, Small Bowl of Clay, Water Flask
122 Unfired Medium Bowl Block of Clay, Medium Bowl Sketch, Water Flask

Obtaining Ingredients

Crystalline Silks: Spiders in Crystal Caverns [near the bottom]
Glass Shards: Sold by a merchant at the Thurgan Bank
High Quality Firing Sheet: Sold by a merchant at the Thurgan Bank
Small [7lb] Brick of Velium: Ry'Gorr Minors in Crystal Caverns [near the top]
Small Piece of Velium: Ry'Gorr Minors in Crystal Caverns [near the top]
Velium Purifier: Talk to Frundle Frenker in Thurgadin and say 'what velium vial?'

Note: Most ingredients can be collected in bulk. Only the Velium Purifier is lore.

Recipe

Part 1: Unfired Velium Vial [Combine in Pottery Wheel]
* 1 Small [7lb] Brick of Velium
* 3 Crystalline Silks [unstacked]
* 2 Glass Shards

Part 2: Velium Vial [Combine in Kiln]
* 1 Unrefined Veluim Vial
* 1High Quality Firing Sheet

Part 3: Vial of Velium Vapors [Combine in Velium Purifier]
* 1 Velium Vial
* 2 Small Pieces of Velium [unstacled]
