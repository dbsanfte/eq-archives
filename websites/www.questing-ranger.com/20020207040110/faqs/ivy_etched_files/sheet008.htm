Ivy Etched Leggings

This quest is given by Leaf Falldim at the Kithicor Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger this quest is 'What about Ivy Etched Leggings.'

Items for this quest:

  • Quiver of Kithicor - Full
    • Arrow of Acknowledgment
    • Arrow of Bravery
    • Arrow of Defiance
    • Arrow of Gallantry
    • Arrow of Fortitude
    • Arrow of Recognition
    • Quiver of Kithicor
  • Star of Odus

Quiver of Kithicor

The Quiver of Kithicor drops off Wiltin Windwalker on Gornit Isle in Ocean of Tears. This item is a container, so you will need to have a General Inventory slot free when you finally get to loot it. If you do not, it will poof as the item is no drop and will not be able to fall to the ground. This may have been corrected as I have seen no drop items drop to my cursor when I didn't have room for them. I would not depend on this happening and just have the space for it.

Wiltin Windwalker's placeholder is a Buccaneer. Do not attack the Buccaneer as you could end up waiting two hours for a respawn. If you camp Wiltin Windwalker without attacking the Buccaneer, the Buccaneer will despawn after an hour. Either Wiltin Windwalker or the Buccaneer will spawn a few minutes later. The Buccaneer is very common and Wiltin Windwindwalker is rare. Camping him is more of a test of patience than anything else.

The Six Named Arrows

The six named arrows are random drops off the Brownie Outcasts in Lesser Faydark. They all spawn near the pixie tower, which is not far from the fairie village. The pixies in this area are place holders for the Brownie Outcasts so be sure to kill them as well.

The arrows are lore, so it is not possible to accidently get two of the same arrow. However, the arrows you need are: Acknowledgment, Bravery, Defiance, Gallantry, Fortitude and Recognition. Place one of each arrow in the Quiver of Kithicor, and combine the quiver to get the "Quiver of Kithicor - full"

Star of Odus

Go to the jewlrey shop in Erudin and buy a Star of Odus. This is not sold anywhere else in Norrath.

Ivy Etched Leggings

Give Leaf Falldim the "Quiver of Kithicor - full" and the Star of Odus to receive the Ivy Etched Leggings.
