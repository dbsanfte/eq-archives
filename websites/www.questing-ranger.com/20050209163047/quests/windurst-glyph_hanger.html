Glyph Hanger
Client: Horiga-Origa
Location: Optistery, Windurst Waters [F-7]
Reward: Horutoto Ruins Map

Summary: While transcribing an ancient manuscript, Hariga-Origa chanced upon a glyph that he cannot read. He wants you to track down Ipupu, a specialist in paleopgraphy, and hand her a note that contains the unknown letter.

Leave Windurst and go out to the towers around J-8 to find Ipupu and talk to her. Return to Horiga-Origa and talk to him.
