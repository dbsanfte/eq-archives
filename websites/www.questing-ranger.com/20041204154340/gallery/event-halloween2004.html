Special Events: Halloween 2004

Event Activities

Trick or Treat:
NPCs in the towns of San d'Oria, Bastok and Windurst are dressed up on costrumes for Halloween. Give them a sweet treat, either a piece of fruit or a player crafted sweet, and they will randomly give you one of the following:
- Costume
- Jack-o-Lantern
- One of eight random prizes

The costumes NPCs give you can only be worn in the city area that you are currently in. Once you leave to another part of the city or leave the town completely, it wears off. Also costume duration is not infinate and will wear off on its own after a while. The Jack-o-Lanterns are the same as the ones that can be crafted. As for the eight random prizes. Rumor has it that the warp effects of the staves all get deactivated after Halloween. ; ;.
- Trick Staff: DMG:1, Delay:356 HP+1 MP+1 Level 1, All Jobs
- Treat Staff: DMG:1, Delay:356, HP+5 MP+5, Latent Effect: Warp, Level 1, All Jobs
- Trick Staff II: DMG:2 Delay:356 HP+1 MP+1, Enchantment: Warp, Level 1, All Jobs.
- Treat Staff II: DMG:2, Delay:356, HP+5 MP+5, Enchantment: Warp, Level 1, All Jobs
- Pumpkin Head: Enmity-1, Level 1, All Jobs
- Horror Head: Full Moon + Darksday + Nighttime: Enmity-50, Level 1, All Jobs
- Pumpkin Head II: Enmity+1, Level 1, All Jobs
- Horror Head II: New Moon + Lightsday + Daytime: Enmity+50, Level 1, All Jobs
