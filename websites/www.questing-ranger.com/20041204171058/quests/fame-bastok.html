Bastok Fame

Any quest performed in either Bastok or Selbina will give you fame with Bastok. Selbina quests will also give you fame with San d'Oria. However there are several quests that are repeatable, with the expcetion of Beauty and the Galka as it must be completed in order to perform another quest that is repeatable and uses the same turn-in items. All turn in items for these quests can be purchased at the auction house.

A Discerning Eye (Bastok)
Client: Grin
Location: Airship Dock, Port Bastok [G-7]
Requirements: Rank 5

Beauty and the Galka
Client: Cornelia & Parraggoh
Location: House, Bastok Mines [H-5]
Items: Zinc Ore
Note: Non-repetable, but required to do Shady Business.

Gourmet
Client: Salimah
Location: Trader's Home, Bastok Markets [L-8]
Turn-In Items: Treant Bulb [6-11:59], Wild Onion [12-17:59] or Sleepshroom [18-5:59]
Note: You must zone between turn-ins.

Fallen Comrades
Client: Pavvke
Location: House, Bastok Mines [I-7]
Turn-In Items: Silver Name Tag

Only the Best
Client: Melyon
Location: Weavers' Guild Salesroom, Selbina [I-9]
Turn-In: 5 La Theine Cabbage, 3 Millioncorn or Boyahda Moss
Note: Also gives San d'Oria fame, but lowers Tenshodo fame.

Shady Business
Client: Talib
Location: Tenshodo Warehouse, Port Bastok [F-6]
Requirements: Beauty and the Galka
Items: 4 Zinc Ore
Note: Also gives Tenshodo fame.

Fame Checkers:
- Flaco, Steaming Sheep Tavern, Port Bastok [E-6]

Fame Levels:
1: Level 1 What is that, some kind of snail? Oh, that's your name.
2: Hmm...sounds familiar. Who was it again? Oh.
3: Yeah, I've heard of you. You're not doing bad for an adventurer.
4: Quite a few people are talking about the things you've done for Bastok.
5: A lot of people know about what you've done for Bastok.
6: Most everyone in this country knows your name by now
7: Don't look so surprised, as everyone knows your name and what you've done for Bastok.
8: You are a hero to the people of Bastok! Everyone here knows your name and what you've done for us!
9: You've become a househould name in these parts. You're even considered a hero by some... like me.
