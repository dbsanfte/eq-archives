Metalworks Quests

Stardust
Client: Baldric
Location:
Items: Sunsand
Reward: 300G

Sunsand can be found in at the derelict ship on the beach in Valkurms Dunes during a sandstorm. It can also be purchased at the AH but considering that Valkurm Dunes is a common exp spot between Levels 10-20, it is easy enough to just get it yourself. While this quest is repeatable, you do need to zone out and back then retalk to Baldric before he will take another Sunsand.
