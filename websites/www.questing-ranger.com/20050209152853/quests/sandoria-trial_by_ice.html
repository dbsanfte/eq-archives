Trial by Ice
Client: Gulmama
Location: Watchtower, North San d'Oria [E-7]
Items: Tuning Fork of Ice
Reward: Shiva, Shiva Claws, Ice Belt, Ice Ring, Rust 'B' Gone, or 10,000G
Title: Heir of the Great Ice

Summary: Ring the tuning fork of ice upon the protocrystal in Fei'Yin. A path will open to the trial that awaits.

When you talk to Gulamma, you will receive the Tuning Fork of Ice [key item] which allows you to enter the Shiva Prime avatar battle in Fei'Yin. This is the high-level 6-person party avatar fight. I can't give you strategies on this as it will depend on your party. Since I'm a bard, I did use my two-hour ability for Ice Carol which neutralized Shiva Prime's two-hour move. [She used Diamond Dust on us.] Once you have defeated Shiva Prime, return to North San d'Oria and talk to Gulmama. During the intermission, you will get the option to choose your reward. [I chose to make a pack with the avatar, which may be where the title I got came from.
