Notes

Cazic-Thule
With the upgrading of the gods, Cazic-Thule's drops have been moved to his three sub-bosses: Fright, Terror, and Dread.

Faerie Guard & Undead Sprites
With the recent changes to Lesser Faydark, the faeries have all become undead sprites. All items, including the Power of Earth, which used to drop off of Faerie Guards now drop off of Undead Sprites.

Innoruuk
With the upgrading of the gods, Innoruuk's drops will also drop off of his sub bosses and very rarely will drop off of him as well.
