Area Access Quests

Kazham Airship Pass [Elshimo Regions]
Client: Guddal
Location: Kazham Agency, Port Jeuno [I-7]
Items: Ghelsba Treasure Key, Giddeus Treasure Key, Palborough Mines Chest Key
Reward: Kazham Pastport

Guddal will also sell you the pass for 148,000 gil, but it is better to collect the three keys. These keys drop randomly of specific mobs in their respective zone:
- Ghelsba Treasure Key: Orcish Curesmaker, Orchish Fighter or Orcish Serjeant
- Giddeus Treasure Key: Yagudo Priest, Yagudo Theolgist or Yagudo Votary
- Palborough Mines Chest Key: Brass Quadav, Copper Quadav or Old Quadav

Tenshodo Membership [Tenshodo HQ in Lower Jeuno]
Client: Ghebi Damomche
Location: Neptune's Spire [F-6]
Items: Tenshodo Invite or Tenshodo Application Form
Reward: Tenshodo Invite, Tenshodo Membership Pass

This quest is rather easy. If you do the application form method, you will need to go to Bastok then return to Jeuno. If you have a tenshodo invite, you can just turn this in. Whichever method you do, you will be given a tenshodo invite along with membership to give to a friend.
