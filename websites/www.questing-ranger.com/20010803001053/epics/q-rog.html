The Epics: Quests

This is a summary of the Rogue epic quest for Ragebringer.

Quest Start

Hail and talk to Malka Rale in the Qeynos Aqueducts to start this quest and get a pouch.

Ragebringer

  1. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the pouch to Anson McBale to spawn Stanos.
    [Note: If he doesn't spawn, try talking to Lendel Deeppockets in Rivervale.]
  2. Hail and talk to Staons.
  3. Go to Kaladim. Pickpocket Founy to get a piece of parchment.
  4. Go to Neriak 3rd Gate. Pickpocket Tani N'Mak to get a piece of parchement.
  5. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the two pieces to Staons to get the Combined Note.
  6. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give the Combined Note to Eldrath.
  7. GIve two Bottles of Milk and 100PP to Eldrath to get a Scribled Note.
  8. Go to Steamfont Mountains. Give the Scribled Note to Yendar Starpyre to get the Completed Note.
  9. Go to Plane of Hate. Pick up the Book of Souls, which spawns in Maestro of Rancor's Room.
  10. Go to Steamfont Mountains. Give the Completed Note and the Book of Souls to Yendar Starpyre to spawn Renux, who argos immediately.
  11. Kill Renux to get the Translated Note and sometimes the Jagged Diamond Dagger also.
    [Note: If you did not get the Jagged Diamond Dagger, see the below sub-quest.]
  12. Go to Highpass Hold. Give the Translated Note to Stanos to get the Box.
  13. Go to Kithicor. Give the Box to the Dark Elf Advisor to spawn the General.
  14. Kill the General to get the General's Pouch and sometimes the Cazic Quill also.
    [Note: If you did not get the Cazic Quill also, see the below sub-quest.]
  15. Give the General's Pouch, Jagged Diamond Dagger and Cazic Quill to Stanos to get Ragebringer.

Sub-Quests

All of the sub-quests are given out by Vilnius the Small in West Karana.

Spawning Vilnius the Small

  1. Make sure that all bandits are in the bandit camp.
  2. Keep killing the bandits that run from the camp to the road until a Brigand spawns. Leave him alone until 11pm [EQ Time] and Vilnius the Small will spawn in the Brigand's place.
  3. Hail and talk to Vilnius the Small.
  4. He will give you three quests. Complete the ones that you need.

Jagged Diamond Dagger

  1. Give the Painbringer, Fleshripper, Mithral Two-Handed Sword and the Giant Zwiehander to Vilnius the Small to get the Jagged Diamond Dagger.

Cazic Quill

  1. Give the Robe of the Kedge, Robe of the Ishva, Shiny Metalic Robe and Robe of the Oracle to Vilnius the Small to get the Cazic Quill.

General's Pouch

  1. Give the Burning Rapier, Eyerazia, Martune Rapier, and a Well Balanced Rapier to get information on how to meet Stanos and receive the General's Pouch.
