Your Crystal Ball
Client: Kurou-Morou
Location: Fortunetellers, Lower Jeuno [I-7]
Items: Ahriman Lens, Divination Sphere
Reward: Ability to have Fortune Told
Title: Fortune Teller in Training

Summary: Create your own crystal ball to have your fortune told.

The Ahriman Lens [ex] drops from Ahrimans in Castle Zvahl Baileys. These are the same mobs that drop Coffer Keys for Artifact Armor, so you might want to just join a key hunt rather than make a special trip just for the lens. Once you get the Ahriman Lens, trade it to Kurou-Morou and he will tell you what to do next. [This step cannot be skipped.] You'll want to head to the Maze of Shakhrami and go to [G-5] on the first map when entering from Tahrongi Canyon. Here you will find a pool of water called Rockwell. Trade the Ahriman Lens to Rockwell. At this point, you need to wait one real life day then examine Rockwell to get the Divination Sphere [ex]. Return to Kurou-Morou and trade it to him.
