Sunday February 28, 1999  (C&S Real Audio)

12:30 PM EST - Spub (spub@xsploitz.com)

The end of the month already, wow.   I don't know about you, but for me, it just zipped by.

I'm hearing that the new tailoring bug is causing a bit of frustration for OSI.   They have supposedly disabled tailoring altogether, or just the hats section on certain shards.

And I have a unconfirm for the Gain Resistance in Town bug from yesterday. It simply did not work, even at low levels of magic resistance.

I don't have too many bugs for you yet, so I think I'll share a little idea I've had.  Remember the old Fred VS Bob wars?   The one where everyone who participated would either name themselves Fred or Bob, and they would have a huge battle to the death, until one man was standing?  What if something similar was organized on Abyss Test?   It would be nice to give the people something to do, instead of just running around killing each other for no reason (the colour wars are getting pretty boring too).  I don't think it would be a weekly event because it would get pretty stale after a while so many just once a month.

Send me your thoughts and opinions on it.
I can be reached at spub@xsploitz.com.

Also, I need a couple volunteers to help plan the event if it takes place, though I see no reason why it won't.  So if you're interested, send me an e-mail.
