Font Bug

This is not an exploit but it is a bug. In the options window, where you select your speech font, try clicking and holding the mouse on a font selection, dragging the mouse above or below the font selections (until none of the three fonts are highlighted), and then releasing. You will now have no font selected. BE CAREFUL WITH THIS ONE. The first time I did it, my speech appeared as a crazy pattern of dots that looked really cool. After that, I began to get crashes. Now, I crash every time I try to enter Britannia and I will have to reinstall UO. Maybe there is more that can be done with this bug, I haven't been able to experiment very much or get screenshots because of my current crashing problems... let me know.

I have this idea, that could help a lot of people that are pissed from PKs killing them all the time. It is some sort of payback where you might even get your stuff back after being PKed. What you have to do is:

  1. Get two runes to a place in town, where there are no players, but where there are always NPCs.
  2. Name those runes "House" and "whatever you want".
  3. Get an old house key, that you don't need.
  4. Get a gate scroll.
  5. Put the "House" rune into your backpack and the other one into the bank.
  6. Put the gate scroll and the key in your backpack.
  7. Always carry those 3 things with you. If a PK kills you now, he'll see the rune called "House", a housekey and a gate scroll.

He will most likely gate to that location. Once in town the PK will get killed by the guards, leaving your and his stuff on the ground. If you're fast you can get resurrected and recall to the PK with the second rune in your bank. Now you not only have your stuff back, but you also got the PK killed and you got his stuff.

From Nicole
