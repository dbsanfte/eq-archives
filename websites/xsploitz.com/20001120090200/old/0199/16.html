Tonights UNCONFIRMED Update(s):

  1. Cast Metor Swarm on a Door. The door will not die (duh), and you'll gain MAD Magery!! It's that simple!!
  2. Simply punch a dummy that is in your home, and get it swaying, then while it is  swaying, attempt to move it and place it a few spaces away. Since you cannot move a house add-on after is has been placed you will recieve a message stating that the item could not be moved ,however, the dummy will keep on swinging!  This obviously isn't a useful bug, but it is very neat to see the dummies constantly swaying steadily along. Hope you like it!!
  3. Find a house where player is macroing....cast earthquake killing the player....then use telekenesis on their keys...they will appear in your pack....and you can loot all you want....
  4. Feel like killing NPC's without getting a murder?  Just attack them,lead them back to your house and you can let them in and due to Origin's new patch they will be flagged under 'Texas Law' and you can kill them at will with no repercussions.  Chop em up take their belongings all without any penalty.  Great fun and a great way if you are looking to lose karma fast, just cut em up.
  5. Go town....bank works well.  Drop a stack of 100 gold next to you, invariably someoone will run up tot the gold.  Make sure you have your mouse button held down on the pile.  As he starts to drag it, quickly drop it into your pack (You have control since you had the mouse held down first), if you time it correctly and it hits the interior of your pack before the other persons client says "You cannot pick that up" he is flagged grey and hit your guard macro.  Boom dead 'thief'.  Loot away and repeat.
