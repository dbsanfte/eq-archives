Bugs- 

The EASY way to get house ownership to all your characters if your characters already have houses of their own :

  1. Take the character that needs friend a house to the house he wishs to be friended.
  2. Stand on the spot where the owner of that house last log out.
  3. Log that character out of the game.
  4. Now log in with the owner of that house, you will be outside of the house since the character you wished to have friended to the house standing on your log in spot.
  5. Go into the house and double click on the house sign to bring up the friends menu, select add a friend to the house, then target to your char!

(Retro)"Bad Circle" in a spellbook:

  1. Get a book in your hand.
  2. Cast cure and target the book.
  3. Now, the book is supposed to be minimized at upper left of your screen.
  4. Double click on it to open it, then click to go 1 circle before the first one.
  5. Then you see it: Bad Circle ((but no spells in it)) really just for showmanship

(Retro) Skill Gain

Find a rabbit or some other little creature and trap him in between 2 beds in the healers or in a corner in your house. Tame him then release him and keep taming hima nd releasing him and you will gain lots of str and dex and a bit of intel. You can macro something else like snooping as well, just get the healer trapped as well and snoop him while you tame and release the little creature. I found that rabbits work the best and it will cap your Taming skill at 20-30 and you will gain no more taming from it. Once your taming hits 20-30 your stats will still go up but taming will not since its a crappy little creature and barely needs any skill to tame.
