"UOP/UOE House Loot"

You need UO Extreme or (my favorite) UO Plugin...So watch your ass and the clock! In your UO.CFG set your trans cirle to max (see uoss.stratics.com for details there)  Walk up to a house...single click on an item Equip that item...it is now on you, probablly invisible if it is not normally an equipable item Unequip - goes into your bag! Beware - If item weighs more then you can carry or put in a packpack you are screwed - I do not think a GM will help ou get your character unstuck!  Try having a friend kill you, then loot the stuff!
