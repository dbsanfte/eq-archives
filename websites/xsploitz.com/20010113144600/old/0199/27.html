Today's Unconfirmed Bug(s)

  • Abyss Shard - Meditation: First you start meditating
    while you're at full mana, and then, without moving, you cast any spell. You wont stop meditating.  After that you are free to move around and you will still be meditating.   As long as you don't put on metal armor or use meditation again, you will keep meditating.  As one could guess, there are quite a few advantages to having your mana come back at warp speed constantly.
  • Block Poison: This is a interesting bug, I had it demonstrated to me using the following technique, but I haven't been able to make it work.  Give it a try anyway.  Get two poison bottles and use the poisoning skill.  When the cursor comes up, select a weapon.  As the weapon is being poisoned press alt-p, and you will then do you will notice that it looks like you are casting and a cursor comes up again. Select the weapon again, and you should get a message something like, "Poison not accessible"  To test it, drink a poison potion.
  • Hiding: Simply cast Explosion and click on your target, and quickly hide. Since the explosion 'lags' a little while, you won't get the "You are attacking someone!" message until it goes boom. So, before this, you could hide, recall or run quite a long distance before the target
    even knows you attacked!
  • Blocking Monsters in dungeons: Get the following items: 2 wooden shields, 2 flour sacks, 1 back pack, 1 orange chest. Stack those items in the following order: Go 1 sheild, 1 sack, 1 sheild, 1 sack, 1 pack, 1 orange chest. Chest must be placed so it sits north to south on top of pack.
