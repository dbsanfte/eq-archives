Today's Unconfirmed Bug(s)

  • Monster Killing: If you are in your house and monsters come to the front door to try and attacks, you can ban them from the house and they will become trapped under the house sign and cannot move...all you need to do is go outside, cast flame field and boom they are dead. 
  • Weight Limit: When you try to pick something up and you get that "That container cannot hold more items" message... drop the item on the nearest person to you and then cancel the trade window... The item will go into your pack without checking for the ammount of items you have in it.
  • Town Kill: Cast a poison on a person (or any other spell which is harmful) If you don't die, walk up to the person and begin auto attack.  Have a buddy hang onto your sword or bow, and if you do not have the guards called, equip the weapons.
