Well, you may be wondering why I didn't update yesterday. The thing is that I had a family emergency and wasn't at home most of the day. I was also told that the server was down most of yesterday, so I couldn't have updated even if I was at home. =)

To start off, I'd like to show you part of a post made by Runesabre on the COB Dev Board:

The hard part of my job is doing things that I feel are better for the game as a whole even in the face of player anger and resistance. Why have we been silent on the whole fishing fiasco? Because we have already made the changes we feel are necessary. Its time to move on to other areas.

With this said, I will no longer be supporting the fishing protest. Why? Because I feel that it will do no good. Gold = fun, and we all know that OSI doesn't like us to have an easy way to make money and have fun at the same time. Anyway, here is today's update. It's a good one =)

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Backpack Bug #2: Another way to carry a large quantity of items without using an npc vendor. Take a lockable chest and put your items in there, it can hold 125 items. Lock the chest, now put it in your pack. The chest only counts as one item now.
  • Report Your Attackers: Ok, you know how they fixed the thing where if someone attacked you, all you have to do is recall away put everything in bank and kill yourself and then report that person...well there is a trick/bug where if someone attacks you, instead of recalling away you pop up a gate walk through to a bank, put stuff away then suicide yourself and report the person =)
  • Double Hit: There is still a way to double hit, it can be done without an autoarm/autodisarm program (UOAssist, etc.) but it is much harder. First of all, remove the persons shield and then paralyze then, follow this up with an explosion.  Right after you targeted the explosion, run up to them and equip your weapon, if you do it just right, your weapon will hit twice, and then the explosion will happen.  This can be very useful with a halberd of vanquishing, since it will kill most people.
  • Cross Dressing: ok what you do is you cast polymorph in to a female and put on armor that only a female can ware and wait tell it wares off and you are a male cross dresser!
  • Town Kill: This works best if your guild is chaos/order, but will work with any guilds that are at war... first off, when u see opposing guild at town, be it chaos/order or rival guild, get them to notice you... sometimes insults will prompt them to attack:) when you get the message "so and so" is attacking you quickly resign from guild... (helps to already have this typed) and bam, there dead and grey ready to be looted... i made 23k in 2 hours in vesper and killer reagents...
