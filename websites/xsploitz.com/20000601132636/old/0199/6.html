Tonights Update:

  1. Training with wisps- you can safely train with the wisps without worry of there spells, heres how.trap a wisp in your house ( note you can use a single large crate to do this and they will not fly over it) open the door to the room the wisp is in, attack it ( it will cast a spell at you), step out of the wisps room and let the door close by itself. come out of war mode while your waiting for the door to close. once the door has closed re-enter the room with the weapon you want to train with. move close to the wisp so it can attack you and you will taking damage from it. as long as the wisp is hitting you your skill will improve.(NOTE: healers be sure to allow your hit points to go to 10 or less before applying bandages, this will allow you to gain skill up to about 60)
  2. Instant kill an attacker: When player A tries to hide and succeeds, and if player B attacks before the "hide succeeded" notification is reached from the  server (so player A is still visible to player B), B is instantly killed.  It occurs a lot in laggy situations (ie. guildwars) It's probably to reduce UOE exploits, but who knows...
