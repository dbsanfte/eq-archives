Tip - First, get five lockable chests, and line 'em up in your backpack.
Then buy about 70 lockpicks (buy 'em all at once from different cities so they're cheap) Now get a keyring.  Put all the keys for the lockable chests on the ring and all the Lockpicks in one stack.  Go to a guard tower, and hide.  Now all you do is dbl click the keyring once and use Last Object, until you get to the picks.  Do the same for the picks. Now Macro the hell out of it.  Using last object saves around 5 hours of time, no exaggeration.  You should have from 0 to GM in 3 nights. Careful though, your stamina will go from 10 to 98.
