Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Super Attack: 1) You need UOE, UOP or UOA so that you have a macro to equip your weapon fast. 2) Cast a offensive spell, get the cursor up, and be right next to someone. 3)  Target the person, and right when you click on him/her, hit your equip macro. 4)  You should then do super damage to the person. Try using Flamestrike, because if gives you enough time to equip the weapon.
  • Dungeon Walls:  The picture of the wall on the boat you posted is from a floating structure in T2 just off the Titan area in the sea. You get those squares on your boat by having 2 items minimum stacked on your deck, then sail into the structure. The squares sandwhich themselves between the 2 stacked items. If you remove the bottom stacked item the square falls onto your deck. It's useless there, but if you leave the stacked items alone they will hold the square and you can sail away with it.  Just sail to where ever you want to place it and remove the TOP stacked item, THEN move your boat. The square will stay where you put it. You can then get onto the square and mark on it. Great for a recall spot to you far away boat. You can move and remove these squares all you want using the stack method. So put them someplace people can't get to and steal them from you.
