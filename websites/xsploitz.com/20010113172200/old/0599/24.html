House Placement Bug... (unconfirmed)

I found a different house placement bug than everyone is talking about. Find a spot witha ring of mushrooms. Stack two large books on top of the mushrooms [gotta be on them ,on the ground in the midddle wont work!]. Now double click your house deed, and click on the top book. VIOLA, your hosue is placed. I think the deal is that mushrooms, the ones that you cannot pick up, also raise Z axis. this could be usefull for other things also.

Random Crap

I have found that while digging up buried treasure chests that If I have cast a gate right where I entend on standing while digging when the critts spawn they can't see me and I can loot the chest in complete safty. The trick is you cannot move off the spot where the gate comes in and you have another person in the gate with you. The two of you just stand there while the digger digs up the chest, disarms and unlocks it, then you take the loot and recall. Also you can cast non direct damage spells such as blade spirts and EV and still not be seen if you want to kill the critts. This will not work after the chest has been dug up and spawn has taken place. I find the dig spot then mark a rune, move away from the spot, then cast a gate and continue the digging. My self and one friend can do lvl 3 to 5 chests in this manner without having to share the loot with a whole crowd of people usally needed to kill the critts on such a high lvl chest.
