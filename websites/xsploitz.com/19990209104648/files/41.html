"Fletching Bug"

Here's a bug that will help you earn more money from flecthing....It seems that if you are riding a horse you are faceing the right of the screen, when you try to carve a crossbow, an image of the crossbow will appear in front of the hind legs of the horse for a split second whether you suceed in making the bow or not.  If you are quick you can grab that crossbow and its a real crossbow.  Thus, you will never fail at making corssbows and profit more from ur flecthing.  You may not gain skill from making a crossbow this way, but you get to sell it.
