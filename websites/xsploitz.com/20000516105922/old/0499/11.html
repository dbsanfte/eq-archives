12:11 PM CST -  Sal (sal@xsploitz.com)

A couple weeks ago, I posted a bug that would allow you to combine ore from another ship with the ore on your ship. It would seem that this works not only with ore, but anything, stackable or not, that is on a ship you are close by. This is a great bug, and anyone who doesn't want your valuables lost should remove them from your boat immediately. Here's a detailed explanation of how the bug works:

Hi, the old bug about standing next to the boat and dragging things off does work again. What you must do is unlock your plank and open it. Then walk out onto it and make sure you stay on the plank. The plank has to be extremely close to the other boat. Now, OSI made it so you cant see whats on another boat unless your on it. So, if your really close you can see whats on it. And there ya go you can still drag all the stuff off other boats onto yours.

Warning to users of ICQ99:

There is an ICQ security hole out that allows a hacker to get *ANY* file (including your uo.cfg) from your hard drive.

If you are using ICQ99, there is a function called User's ICQ Homepage. When this function is turned on, your ICQ will listen to port 80 (i.e. the HTTP port), allowing a hacker access to all your files. To turn this off (this is disabled by default, so unless you have activated it, you shouldn't have to worry), click the Services button, My ICQ Page, and make sure that Activate Homepage is not checked. (thanks to rabi for this information)
