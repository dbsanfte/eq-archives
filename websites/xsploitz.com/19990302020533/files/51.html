"Armageddon Trap Bug"

Here is how to do it... you make a chest/box and put like 10 GE inside it. Your Newbie/kamikaze is carrying the chest with an extra GE in his bag and an axe. Then when he is close to a group of people who stand still (speaking or fighting), he double click the extra Ge, cancel targeting, drop the GE in the chest/box, and then break the box with the axe... what happen here is when you break the chest, all the 11 GE are falling on the floor, and 1 sec later it makes a chain reaction, and the kamikae and everyone around here die... now it's time to get the loot :) Of course you need a perfect connection to make this. You cant do it with a few lag...   I tried this with a master tinker traped chest, it worked, but i didnt tried with normal chests, but in fact i don't figure out why you would need a traped chest...
