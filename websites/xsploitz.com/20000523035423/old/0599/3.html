NO BUG; Fixing client crashes:

This deals with constant client crashes people may have been having. For the past few weeks I've been constantly crashing whenever i get near civilization in UO it was so bad i could rarely even get to ANY bank without having to reload UO at least 15 times. Not sure how many other people have had this problem. But i found out that if you set all your font settings in your options screen back to the original UO font (the one that looks kind of like a olde english script font) it should fix the problems. I changed them yesterday and have not had even a single crash since, while running through the streets and around the banks for hours to test it.

(Retro)"Bad Circle" in a spellbook:

  1. Get a book in your hand.
  2. Cast cure and target the book.
  3. Now, the book is supposed to be minimized at upper left of your screen.
  4. Double click on it to open it, then click to go 1 circle before the first one.
  5. Then you see it: Bad Circle ((but no spells in it)) really just for showmanship
