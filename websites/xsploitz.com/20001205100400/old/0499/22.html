Safe Item Placing-

For this you need an Altar or a pentagram placed in your house. Then use UOE and click the ignore macro (Ctrl+Delete) in the middle of one of the two, this will make the middle peice dissapear, no you can place something there like gold or something, now when you press resync 0/INS it will place the middle peice back and no one can see it, to retreive it just delete the peice again and get it back. Useful Information from a Skunk-

The area of effect spell meteor swarm does not take down reflection. I tested this tonight. Here is the deal to test. Two people who can cast 7th. Really only need one, lol. One person casts reflect and the other person casts meteor swarm. Here is the deal, the meteor swarm spell is reflected but does not take down spell reflect. Also note that each person in a group who has reflection on when this spell is cast has their damaged reflected back on the caster. Of course a bug is only has good as it's use. I tested all other area of effect spells including earth quake and chain lightning. Suggested uses, have you and your 10 best friends taunt someone from inside a house. Of course when the idiot cast meteor swarm, a great house kill method the spell is reflected back and waa laa free loot.
