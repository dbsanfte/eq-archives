Using UOP's show deeds I have found that some people will leave unsecured chest and other items on the second floor of their public house.  They will block the entry to the second floor with lockdowns but neglect the upper story itmes completely.  Use UOP's pickup thing if you are standing under other objects and lose sight of the target item when you are directly below it.  You have to be standing directly below an item to steal it through the ceiling.  I made 3k in one house just today!
  3. With the new eject and ban commands added to the house system there is a bug envolving you being a ghost. If you are killed in your house somehow or loose your keys to a pk, you can simply go to the house as a ghost and ban them WHILE as a ghost. Just do the same command "I ban thee" and it will ban them even though you are a ghost.
