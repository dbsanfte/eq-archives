Tonights Updates:

  • You all know, a few days ago OSI put in a patch which effectively put a stop to people walking 'up' on certain items. The effect was that the character was elevated above ground level, through walking onto items like long pants.  If you still want to do this, you will need a tame horse in your  possession.   Stack 2 long pants vertically ontop of one another, ride your horse onto that tile. Dismount. Mount again. You're ontop of the pile!  To go up higher, simply stack 4 pants on the pile next to the pile you are currently standing ontop. Dismount. Step through the new pile and have the horse follow you.  It will walk UP the new pile, then you can mount it again and elevate  even higher.  Rinse and repeat and you should be very high very soon.
