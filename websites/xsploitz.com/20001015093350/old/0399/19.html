9:45 PM EST - Spub (spub@xsploitz.com)

I am proud to announce that the rogue campaign was a success! 

Runesabre said this on COB Dev board today:

I have read thru many of your ideas and concerns about stealth. I do realize stealth is not simply a thief/rogue skill btw. :)

Tentatively, here are some changes that I might consider making to stealth. Comments welcome.

- Keep pushing thru a player revealing you... I think that is ok. Limit this only to players though. Allow that you can push thru non-players like rats, monsters, etc without revealing you automatically.

- Reduce the passive, automatic revealing to a distance of one tile instead of what it is now. Basically have the automatic reveal check only work when the stealther is standing right next to another player.

- Make it so you are not auto-revealed by a guildmate. This allows people to stealth in groups instead of being loners.

- If you manage to snoop without being noticed, then you are not revealed.

So there you have it.  I'm still not too keen on the pushing through the person update, because you could have a check against what your stealth and hiding is at, and that will determine whether or not you can keep yourself hidden.  It's great to see that OSI is taking an interest.  If you get the chance, pop over to COB dev board and thank Runesabre for listening.  No I'm not whining still, I would be very happy with the changes above.
