Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Meditation Bug: Try meditating while wearing exceptional plate chest and gloves.
  • NPC Killing: (1) First, Be a War Mode. (2) Cast Explosion to any target (NPC, PC) in Town. (3) Target on, then Recall out of town A.S.A.P(use Explosion's time lag!)   Surely, Guard come point where u cast, but Recall is faster. (4) Keep in  War Mode for 15 seconds, and come back to town. (5) Character who was casted can now auto attack the NPC.  Note: This could be a new town kill for PC's
  • Healing Bug: When the Wizard Hat is put on, STR is lowered,
    along with hit points.  When the hat is taken off, STR returns
    to normal, but it takes a while for HP to return (depends on
    how hungery you are).  You can heal yourself & get skill
    gains up until about 25-30%.
  • Magic Gain Bug: Use evaluating intelligence on the town crier until you gain 1 int, then cast a spell.  You'll go up .3 to .5 even if you fail.
