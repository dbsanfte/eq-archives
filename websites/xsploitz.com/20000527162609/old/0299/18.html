Tip - GM Thief in 9 hours!: First get some bags and some potions (the type of potions doesn'tt matter) then for every 10 skill you have in stealing put in a potion plus one more ie, 30 stealing = 4 potions in bag.   Then fill your friends character or the pack horse* with the bags with potions until they're full.(* if using pack horse you have to have another character on your account log in and fill it) then steal away but don't random steal, you must click on the bag itself in order to gain skill.(you steal the bag containing the potions and skill increases fast) A friend and I did this I went from 6 stealing to 100 in about 9 hour,s and I used the pack horse for half of that time.
