Some of the other bug sites are reporting the vendor dupe thing as a huge get rich quick thing.  The real deal is this.  There was some legacy code that corrected vendors that managed to wander away from their creation point (during the earthquake days of last Christmas).  The code caused vendors to teleport back to their location in a  callback that is triggered at various times.  What is actually happening is that the vendor deed that you get contains your vendor.  When we allow vendor placement, you get to let him out of the deed.  Well, the legacy code was causing him to teleport out of the deed (which is why people actually saw him appear), leaving the character with an empty vendor deed.  

Also the vendor deed is unstealable and unlootable, so that kill exploit posted won't work either. (our vendor fun bug)
