Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Dartboard Fun: You need to be inside a house that you own and have a dartboard deed and one bookcase. Place the bookcase sideways with one edge where you'd like the dartboard to hang. This would most likely be in the middle of a small house or in the middle of the room in a larger house. Now double click the dartboard deed and target on the side of the bookcase. Once the dartboard is "placed" remove the bookshelf. The Dartboard will now be suspended in mid-air. Just thought all you house decorating friends might want to know this.
  • Ghost Horse: Make your mount an object, mount your mount, and go into house you are a friend of.  Say "remove thyself" and use a hotkey for "target mount object".  You will stay mounted on your horse/whatever, but it will be expelled outside!  Dupe, no, because if you "dismount", the horse you are riding disappears.  A Great idea is to do this and stable your horse so if you die it can be retrieved from stables, but this would definitely reveal that you use UOE.  Have fun with those Ghost rides!
  • Secure Containers: Here is a simple way to store things securely. Many people know that there is a bug with armories, if you drag items directly to armoire without open the armoire, the item will be hidden in the armoire, no one can see the item since its invisible, but the item does exist, one way to reveal the item is chop the armoire, whatever inside the armoire will fall on the ground. The trick is that if you lock down the armoire in your house/shop, then only you--- the owner can chop it, no one else can chop and armoire and reveal the items inside, and because items inside are invisible, they will never be able to take it. Now, tones of secure containers can be used in any kind of house!!
