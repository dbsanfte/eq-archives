Tonights UNCONFIRMED DUPE (and other) Update(s):

  1. Dupe: Find a server line map of where the server lines are in Brittania. Look for where lines 1 and 3 intersect (near Orc Fort by Minoc, only 2 that intersect on land). Go to the general area...from here there are 2 tough steps...the first is to find the exact intersection of the lines. To do this walk 1 step at a time north, when weight changes drop a flour sack, the walk east or west depending on where your standing to locate the other (I think 1 runs north south, 3 runs east west-all by radar not by screen) A direct line from each sack will show the intersection. To successfully complete this you must walk -diagonally across BOTH server lines at the CORNER where they intersect-. As you pass over the line drop the item, one will be in your pack and one on the other side of the server line. A friend -MUST- retrieve the item because if you walk across the line back and put both items on the same subserver, one or maybe both will be deleted. The timing of when to drop is by far the most difficult, but once you get it its easy to repeat. To dupe multiple items place them in a sack and drop the bag, it will duplicate bag and contents. You -CANNOT- use this to dupe stackables (dont know why it just doesn't work). Good luck with it...
  2. Non-Dupe:1. Get a Bow 2. Equip it. 3. Use a purple potion (was fixed but now works again)
  3. Non-Dupe:How to make a potted x-mas tree! things you need-a barrel and a deed to a christmas tree  Place one barrel on the square you want the potted tree in, next lift up the barrel and walk onto the square it was in. Now drop the barrel 1 square to far away then you can put it so it pops back on you.  Now lock it down, and place the tree. It should be OVER the barrel giving it the "potted tree" look!
  4. Non-Dupe:You can lock down and secure a chest underneath your porch step. This way if you want to macro in your house with another charactor you can just have him use a key to the house in the chest...also...you can instant log on porches of a house you are owner or friend of even without the keys.
