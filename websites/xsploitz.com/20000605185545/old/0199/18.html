Tonights UNCONFIRMED Update(s):

  1. This bug allows the OWNER of a house to enter it at any time without having to carry a key on their person and risk dying and the possibility of someone else looting their house.  This is also useful if you lose the keys to your house and want to get inside it. First, double click on the brass sign outside the house.  Next, declare the house to be a public building (make sure no one is around that might run inside your house).  Now enter your house immediately and close the door. Finally, quickly double click the brass sign outside your house again and declare the building to be private.  You are now in possession of one key, while the other is in the bank, and for safety's sake, you might want to put the key that is in your backpack somewhere inside your house or in your bank where it can't be stolen, it really doesn't matter as when you declare your building to be public next time you want to get inside the keys will disappear. If this is done correctly, you can enter your house with the same speed as if you had a key on you...
