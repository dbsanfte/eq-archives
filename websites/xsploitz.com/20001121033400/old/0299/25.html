Oh boy, oh boy... I don't know if I should laugh, or be angry.  The magic bug that was sent to me this morning works.

Unlimited Gates: Just use a gate scroll.   It doesn't go away, and you are supposed to be able to gain magery by using it.   Here's a tip so you don't get caught by GM's.  Mark a rune right under your houses sign and then go into your house and keep using the scroll.  The gate will not appear.

10:00 AM EST - Spub (spub@xsploitz.com)

Good Morning, (or maybe Good Afternoon for some of you): Whether you like the thief system or not, it's in and it's here to stay.  I have discovered a neat little technique to get Stealth up.  Don't bother macroing it in your house because it caps at 25.  Go into a building and let stealth macro, once you see that it looks somewhat stuck, move to another building.   I've done this three times already and it looks like it's working very well (I'm just hitting 60 Stealth)

EZ Macro Setup: Have it go HIDE, STEALTH, STEALTH, STEALTH, STEALTH.  That is probably the most efficient way to macro the skill overnight.

UOLoop Setup: If you are Abyss and have 100 stealth and 100 hiding, set macro to either last skill, or stealth (probably a F# macro).  Then set UOLoop to 100, and select the key that executes the macro.   Go and annoy all those pk's who have setup houses, and open gates to Britain from them.  I like to open and close the doors.  If you're fast enough, you can switch around the way you open the door by walking from the inside to outside, or the other way.

Already, I'm having a great time with the new skills.  I'm able to creep up behind people and search through their belongings.   Yesterday, I noticed to be talking about transfering money.  They were agruing whether or not gold will drop to the ground if you place it in a pouch and cancel in the trade window.  Well it did =)  But I wasn't fast enough to grab, I came damn close though.
