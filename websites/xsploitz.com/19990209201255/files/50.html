"Ruining Skill"

Just go by someone who is GM Mage or someother gm (hopefully near stat cap). Now here is the trick. you can learn by standing next to someone. So what you do is have a pretty high skill in  a wasted area. Use UOPlugin to kick out massive skill boosts. The person you stand next to will now gain up to i believe 30 max points. Most people at stat cap will be destroyed by this. I know of a gm mage i did this to during server downtime and he dropped 5 points. and if you know alot, 5 points is a bitch to get at 95. Good luck with this bug, as people will be destroyed by it. poisoning skill is a good one to use with this.
