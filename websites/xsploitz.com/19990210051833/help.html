Tips For Getting a Bug Posted!

  • Make sure all spelling/grammar is correct! It is a big factor on if your bug is posted or not. Xsploitz has had some BAD experience with bad spelling and grammar.
  • Make sure everyone even the biggest idiots could possibly understand your bug. Imagine yourself a HUGE idiot and you don't even know what "HP" or (Hit Points) means, try to explain everything in detail.
  • Be as explicit as possible, if you say "turn left" don't just say that, say "turn left, two steps" be exact in ALL details you give, review the bug once or twice.
  • Don't go "I did" this "I did" that, no-one cares what you did. So save Xsploitz some time and write it like your telling someone how to repeat the bug. We don't really like to hear about what you did and didn't do we just want to do the bug ourselves. If you must leave your comments at the bottom and we'll post that also.
  • Include ANY screen captured images (or screenshots) that are relevant to the bug and you feel would help people to better understand it! If you do this, you will pretty much be assured a posting on Xsploitz of your bug. Anonymity in your screenshots is a big thing and we'll take care of it for you, please try to keep all screenshots unedited if possible. Please also zip compress all included files!
  • If you'd like your bug posted exclusively to Skunk Works please include something saying of your wishes, if Xsploitz sees fit it will only post it to Skunk Works!
