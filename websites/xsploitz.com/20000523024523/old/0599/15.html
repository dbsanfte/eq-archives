Macroing Magery

When macroing magery, instead of having a continuous macro that will keep using your mana when it becomes available, set a macro that first uses all your mana then lets it recharge. This will allow magery to advance at normal speeds, instead of being limited by the "repetition" aspect of magery which causes advancement to slow.

More than you bargained for...

While stacking Lockable chests against the wall I discovered that it is possible to have your backpack exceed the maximum 125 items. I filled the backpack with 125 items so that the chests would not end up there while placing them. But upon releasing them they ended up in my backpack now cotaining 135 items.
