Tonights UNCONFIRMED Update(s):

  1. Vendor Delete: Grab a Barrel (i know they are rare but who cares) and a Small Box (those orange looking things). Now move to the same place the vendor is standing and drop the box into the barrel. The box drops to the floor. Now cast a gate. As soon as the gate dissapeares the Vendor and the Box vanishes. And you are dropped 2 steps away from your former location. There is no actual use for that as the Vendor is just gone and you get no loot or anything but its nice to get rid of some foolish players vendors. (GM's WILL Ban you for use of this!)
  2. Summon an air elemental , name it something then get the master of the house to eject it , this is the funny part you can tell it what to do from inside the house to attack people who are waiting outside , since your inside the person you told to attack cant hurt you. Good for PK or newbie killing, if your that type of person.
  3. Ever get tired of people recalling away when you try to kill them? I have thought up a possiable solution. Snoop their pack then use UOE to create a ghost item in your pack of it. Then when they recall away cast recall on the ghost and follow em. (Needs UOE)
  4. Rumor:IF you have a boat, and you want a forge on it. Double click the forge deed then click the cursor on itself, the forge should now be placed on the ship!
  5. BAD BUG:I encountered this while attempting to stack chests.  In the process I lost around 375 stones of reagents!  If you put a table over a chest (by whatever means), then put a chest on top of the table, then remove the table, the top chest will vanish and cannot be recovered.
