Money Making

You can start an apprentice chef, get some raw ribs, and cook them on a campfire. The raw meat will stay in your pack, but cooked ribs appear from nowhere. Cooked ribs sell for 21 gp each, and since you can make 500 of them in a half hour from only one or two raw, you can get rich quick.
