BTW: How many of you were ever able to get into the bug confirm/unconfirm section?  Just leave a reply to the header in the message board (yes there is one setup now)

The Secure Chest outside of the house is confirmed.   I'll leave it up for one more day.

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Secure Chest Outside of House (Confirmed):   Here is a different version of placing a secure chest outside of your large house (note: large)  Get on the steps of the house and then place a table right underneath the sign.  Then place your chest on top of the table and secure it.  Pull away the table, and it's now on the ground. You can do this without the table, but in some cases it is hard to place the chest in the correct position.
  • Animal Lore: Well, I know all of you need to be GM in animal lore (just kidding)  Here's a way to get to GM in basically one night.   Just macro animal lore on a vendor.
  • Murderer in Town:  Try this one on Abyss. You need two people, a murderer and a person with GM healing & GM anatomy.  Use bandages to rez the murderer.  The murderer will be able to walk around town and the guards won't come. Submitted by: Tamer
  • Friend your characters: If you want your characters to be friends to your house, just make a bunch of key copies.  Drop a key somewhere and have your other character come by and pick it up.  Take him/her to the house and have him/her log out unhidden.  Log back in as the owner of the house.  The other character will still be there, and you are then able to make him/her a friend to the house. Submitted by: PowerPaw
