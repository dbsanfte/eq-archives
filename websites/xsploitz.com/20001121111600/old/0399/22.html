5:57 PM CST - Sal (sal@xsploitz.com)

Hail and well met! My name is Sal and I will be taking over for Spub here at XsploitZ. I've got a great update for you today, so without any further ado:

  • New Gate Scroll Bug: Mark a rune, put a box on the spot where you marked the rune and stand next to it, then use the scroll, the scroll doesn't get used up and magery increases. Note: This will work with town criers as well.
  • Cooking Bug: I found out a cool bug accidentally while using Uoa. (Note:You will need Uoa for this one). You will need to get some fish and then macro cooking, now while doing this, walk around or outside bank, and guess what, your still cooking fish, that is cool, you can talk to your friends outside of bank and cook fish at same time.
  • Town Kill: First you need to find someone in town that is macroing and away from their computer. You will need to have your character be able to cast poison on them. All you have to do is carry one nightshade with you to cast it, or a scroll. After you cast poison on them you might get killed by the guards or you might not. I sometimes run away and the guards don't get me. Now you just have to wait until the person's health dwindles away to almost nothing. Then I give them one shot with an arrow or what ever your personal preference is. They will die and then all of their loot is yours for the taking. This is a really easy exploit to do because now fishing is a favorite and people get bored watching their character fish, so they macro. The town I do this in is Vesper. I imagine any town with water will be just as well. Hope you like it. I have made tons of cash,regs and other valuable items this way!
  • Change the Level of Treasure Maps: Go fishing and get MANY Level 1 maps. Decode them all, but don't get the treasure. Complete a high level map (say level 5) or walk around and look for empty chests that are left behind. From you collection of level 1 maps choose one that goes to the exact same spot where this abandoned chest is now. Dig! The chest will appear while the other is still there, replacing the old one. But now it is not an L1 chest, it has the level of the chest that was there before.
  • Poisoning Bug: When you failed putting poison on a blade you would lose the poison and not gain. With the Bug you use a bread Loaf. You make lesser poisons and poison the bread loaf. The Bug is that whenever you fail on the bread loaf you don't lose your bottle of poison and so you can keep performing poison until you actually gain. This way it costs a lot less to gain poisoning, you save a lot on regs. I got my alchemist to 100 in 3 days using it.

Remember to keep sending all the bugs to team1@xsploitz.com, and if it's posted and confirmed you will receive a free Skunk Works account. =)
