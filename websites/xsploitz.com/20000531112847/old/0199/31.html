Today's Bug(s):

  1. Unlimited Secure Containers: Take a chest, armoir, crate, or any other lockable storage container. Lock it down within your house. Then use magic lock on it. People cannot open it because it is locked. People cannot lockpick it because it is magically locked. And people cannot magically unlock it because it is locked down. This makes the container as good as "locked down and secure."
  2. Perma Head: Cast the spell incognito once your change names kill yourself or have a friend kill yourself (near a shrine or healer because you need to rez before your body decays) once you are dead have a friend rez you or a healer and go up to ur body and cut the corpse....grab the head....instead of the head decaying after 10 min or so...the head never decays...the head is "a head of whoever u died as under incognito" take the head to your house and lock it down as a perma head.
  3. Update to Blacksmithy Bug: It says you can just log out, then back in to reset the blacksmtihing clock.. but actually, you need to wait for the character to time out.. or log in as another character (at the inn, or in your house), then log back in as your blacksmith.
