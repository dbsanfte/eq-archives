7:15 PM EST - Spub (spub@xsploitz.com)

Oh boy, read the new changes to the thief system.....groan.

  • Stealth
    • Shoving past someone while stealthy will reveal you.
    • Snooping will reveal you.
    • You cannot stealth while riding a horse.
    • It will become substantially more difficult to start stealth when in the vicinity of other players.
      • The more players, the harder it will be.
      • They have to be within 10 paces to count against you.
      • Only players affect this, not NPCs.
      • This only affects when you start stealthing, not while you walk stealthed.
    • The number of steps you can take while stealthed has been halved.

The changes might be more realistic, but they are still absolutely rediculous.  Is it me, or has the rogue class just recieved another kick in the balls?  Leave your opinion on the message board.

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Remain Stealthed: Hide, wait the required time go into stealth mode. Now you can recall or cast a spell (which will un-hide you) do not walk or take a step hide again you are back in stealth mode, no waiting, just walk away. If your in stealth mode and you want to recall or cast a spell. Just start stealth again so you will have maximum steps after the spell or recall.

  • UO AutoPilot script for the gate scroll bug.  It casts the spell and then used meditation.  Bug.apf Submitted by: dale65

  • Tinkering Bug: Buy 100 blank keys.  Setup a macro to use one blank key on another.  This way when you are successful the key stays blank
    and you can use it again.  You can go up .1 each time (until you get to high skill levels) and you'll gain STR, INT and DEX.

  • Secure Chest Outside of House (Confirmed):   Here is a different version of placing a secure chest outside of your large house (note: large)  Get on the steps of the house and then place a table right underneath the sign.  Then place your chest on top of the table and secure it.  Pull away the table, and it's now on the ground. You can do this without the table, but in some cases it is hard to place the chest in the correct position. (This is the last day this bug will be on the front page)
