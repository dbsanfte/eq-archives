Accepted Answers [Clues?]

  1. For what reason was Mondain's father killed? [secrets, secrets]
    HE WOULD NOT SHARE THE SECRET OF IMMORTALITY WITH MONDAIN / GIVE HIM THE GEM
  2. What was the name of Mondain's young lover?
    MINAX
  3. From the bottom of which body of water did Exodus rise? [with a capital "G"]
    THE GREAT OCEAN
  4. Who was the Avatar's greatest ally in the fight against Exodus? [Chronoriffic]
    THE TIME LORD
  5. What was Britannia called before it was united under Lord British?
    SOSARIA
  6. From the depths of which abyss did the Avatar seize the Codex of Ultimate Wisdom?
    STYGIAN ABYSS
  7. Who dethroned Lord British and held him captive in a dungeon? [they hid him in the shadows]
    THE SHADOWLORDS / BLACKTHORN
  8. What event caused the destruction of much of the gargoyle race? [see previous question]
    BRITISH'S ESCAPE FROM THE DUNGEON / RAISING OF THE CODEX
  9. Two lenses that allowed one to view the Codex of Ultimate Wisdom. One to Lord British. What was the name of the gargoyle that received the other? [they called him "Big D"]
    KING DRAXINUSOM
  10. Moonglow is the city of which virtue?
    HONESTY
  11. Where might most Britannian fighters go to train?
    JHELOM / WARRIORS' GUILD IN BRITAIN / SERPENT'S HOLD
  12. What do gremlins like to steal?
    FOOD
  13. Where is Mandrake Root most commonly found? [murky!]
    SWAMPS
  14. What effect do the magic words "Vas Sanct Lor" have in Britannia?
    CAUSES MAGE AND ALL IN HIS PARTY TO BECOME INVISIBLE / "GREAT PROTECT LIGHT"
  15. Which powerful artifact generates red moongates?
    ORB OF THE MOONS
