"UOE Potion Bug"

If you set your macro in UOE to equip your weapon in only your right hand instead of both your right and left you can drink a potion no matter what the weapon is...Halberd, 2 handed axe, etc...

Take Note that is someone sees you abusing this they can call a GM and get you banned!
