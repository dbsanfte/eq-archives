      Tonight's Bugs :

Messing with Minoc Mages

Know that spawn north of Minoc where you go and mess with newbies, and sometimes other good people show up? Well if they are a mage and start attacking you, run into guard protection near the cave. The mages will almost always mana dump on you then they are in a bad situation

100INT in a jiff!

How to get over 100 INT ... FAST!! FIRST GET A MAGE TO CAST POISON WALL.... Run fast up and down the poison wall. do this until you get 100 int. then get a mage to cast curse on you while running up and down the poison field. If you get 100 int before the -11(Cursed INT) You will now have 111 INT. You can also add ale.... Gain some more :)) Not sure if it works on OSI servers... but it works on Player Run servers... Suppose it works.

Town Poop

You know how everyone goes into town cause they want to use magery and have it not hurt them. Well try this if you would rather stay away from those thiefs, and beggars around the bank. It works realy well if you own a house or have access to one. Go buy a horse, and get some food for it. Make sure you got your reagents of choice with you. Then go on your paper doll and select options and then macros. Set a macro for last target. Now go to your desired location outside town. Get off the horse and bring up a target cursor. The one i always use is my dagger target because it does not preform an action unless on somethin dead. Then get on the horse and cast a spell. Once you have the target for the spell like ebolt or flamestrike or even fireball, use your last target macro. It will target the horse you are sitting on. It wont anger the horse or hurt it. And it does not hurt you either. The only problem that i have seen is that you go grey. heh so be carefull.

Dirty Thief!

This one is to all those that have a 60 or more stealing and have played long enough with your thief to join the thieves guild. Buy your trusty disguise kit from the same thief guild master that signed you up. Then go put it in the bank. Once you have it in the bank use it but dont hit okay. Just click apply. The menu for the disguise kit wont go away unless you die or recall. You can just drag it to the edge of the screen and steal on. Changing your diguise at will without fear of losing you trusty and expensive disguise kit.
