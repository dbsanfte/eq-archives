Tower Break-In Bug

Get someone who is master healing, master anatomy (well only need 80 of each, but the more the better).  Find a tower that doesn't have it blocked off by tents.  Go under the roof of the tower, and have a ghost stand right next to you (you being the healer).  Use the bandages on the ghost, and then have the ghost walk in the tower, all the way up on top of the overhang above where you are.  Since after the healing delay will occur, it checks to see if the person is right next to you (which he will be, he will be up on the roof, but still next to you) and then he will get a ressurection screen, then he's in and free to loot or whatever.
