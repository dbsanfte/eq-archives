Today's Unconfirmed Bug(s)

  • Taming: .  To tame any creature that you have enough skill for do the following, and you should be successful on the first try. Bring your health close to 0. Start to tame the target creature, while taming, die. Play as a ghost and stand next to the target, you should succeed your tame every time. When you res, your pet should be waiting for you.

  • Boat Break-in: .  Find a boat near a bridge. Once there stand on the bridge so you are over the boat. To see if the spot you have selected over the boat is good, try opening the plank. If the plank is open, but unlocked, your in. Once you open the planks you can also give the tillerman commands. Move the boat around so you are over whatever you wish to take. Then simply drag the item into your pack. (This is sketchy at best, but try it out and tell me what you come up with.)

  • Trade Window Rip-off: .  Basically all it is good for is a TRADE WINDOW RIP-OFF.  But it is kinda a HACK.  You must use GAME HACK, which we all remember was big for a while when the "Gm Newbie MAGE" hack was out and about. Get some X-bow Bolts(10 or so) say you are selling then.  When the window comes up do a seach for one less than what you put in there(note you may need to adjust how many are in there till it will work) then do a search for that actual #. Make sure the second search finds only one match! Now freeze it, raise it,and KABOOM, 10 bolts goes to 10,000 and u make a lot of PROFIT!!  (Note: This is pretty confusing, give it a shot anyway.. but be careful using Game Hack)
