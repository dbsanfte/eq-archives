"3rd Party Programs Present: Quick Kill"

This requires UOA or UOE last target to work properly, and for the fastest responce a macro that first unequipes both hands and then selects last target.

  1. Use and item on some to get them set as last target.
  2. Attack them.
  3. Cast a suitable attack spell, Energy Bolt for example.
  4. Equipe your bow or other weapon that does quick damage. (no comments on what those might be :P  )
  5. Do some quick damage with your weapon, not enough to make your opponent heal but say to perhaps half life if heal let you.
  6. Now unequipe both hands and let loose your EB using your last target macro.

You'll find that this is a nice way to get the quick kill, as even if your EB doesnt kill your oppenent outright it will startle them enough when they see thier life drop to almost nothing to make an error.  I recommend Explosion personally though, because most people dont bother healing until the see the damage and by then its too late if you are casting again or shooting at them.
