10:00 PM EST - Spub, Loric, Lomo

Ok, here is the bug you have been waiting for.

This is a boat break-in, and also can be used for secure storage.  If for some reason you've seen this bug before, remember, for every one of you that knows the bug, there's 50 others who don't.

Get on a boat with a friend, and move to a spot where people usually park their boats.   Cast gate on a rune that leads to land (right on the shore/cliff is best), and then walk through the gate.  Once you walk through the gate, have you friend move the boat so that the gate is now over the water.  Once you see that the gate is over water, quickly walk back into it.  The gate will disappear, and you'll be standing in the middle of the ocean. Mark a rune.

Now you can recall to that spot whenever you want.  The best part is, that if someone puts their boat in the spot where you marked the rune, you will be onboard the boat!  You can also use this as a safety recall (using UOE) and you do not need keys.
