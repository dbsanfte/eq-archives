  • New Meditation Tip:  You can wear bone arms and bone legs with thigh boots, and it will not affect your ability to meditate.  Once you put on gloves or anything else though. It says that it cannot penetrate your armor. 
  • New Meditation Bug: Get yourself a magic trap scroll and a pouch. place the pouch on the floor and use your scroll on it, for some reason the scroll will remain intact!!!! Now, use your meditate skill and gain like crazy without using ONE reagent! btw, as you gain skill in meditate, you will need to start with lower mana , so cast the scroll on the pouch repeatedly to lose more mana and then meditate!
  • Tip: First off, try to get a gate to Bucaneers Den or recall there. Go to the BlackSmith and Carpenter shops. Start beating on those NPCs! They might be tough at first but you will get the hang of it. After you kill a blacksmith take his helmet and sell it to one that spawns. After an NPC dies another one respawns pretty quickly.
  • Tip: You can use the anatomy skill on a ghost, so if your freind is a pk and needs to macro off some murders have him do it in your house so you can macro anatomy on him.  Strange that ghosts would have anatomy. It might also work for evaluating intelligence.

Today's Unconfirmed Bug(s)

  • Meditation: Take a Magic Wizards Hat ( the one that gives you +5 Int ).   Equip the Magic Wizards Hat, use Meditation Skill, Unequip Magic Wizards Hat, Since the hat adds +5 to your int, the meditation skill will be available for use. We all know that meditation can not be used when you have full mana.  This is a great way to macro Meditation without regeants lose.
  • Meditation #2 (Posted as received): First off you dont really gain any skill if you do it when your mana's full so you have to have regs to waste mana (and gain magery) if you dont have regs try and find a magic item of mana drain to use on yourself. (Tip: it seems that the lower your mana the lower your chance of meditating, thus lower mana = more skill gain) Now heres the bug: if you succeed at meditating you gain skill but you also start filling up on mana faster, thats a pain, because you would need to cast more often to keep your mana low, so simply wear some bone armor and when you succeed, you still gain well but your mana regeneration doesnt double(you get a message saying teh regenerative forces cannot penetrate your armor).
