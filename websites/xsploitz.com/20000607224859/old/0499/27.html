Bugs- 

To place an item floating in house, proceed the following steps :

  1. Stack box/log/table to reach the height of the item you want to place.
  2. Place a body part of corpse over there, then the item you want to float.
  3. Lock down the body part and wait for it decay.
  4. The item is floating in the air now, you can walk under it or place a trash barrel under it.

Small, but useful, tip!

This is just something i came upon by accident, should be useful for a lot of players. If you don't like stacking your bandages or using third party programs, just cut up all the bandages you want stacked, get killed in town via daemon, elemental, trapped box, drinkin some poison, whatever. Die, loot corpse, bandages are stacked.

Old, but also useful, bug!

This is the anti-paralyze bug. You can cast magic trap on a bag, and then when being chased by a PK mage who paralyzes you, open the bag to be set free.
