6:10 PM CST - Sal (sal@xsploitz.com)

It would seem that the mini-patch promised to be published sometime this week was put in yesterday. What does this mean? Well, for those of you whose main source of income has been selling your stash of level one treasure maps and SOS bottles from the previous night's macroing, I'm sorry to say you're going to have to find another way to make money. I macroed my master fisherman all last night and only pulled up ONE treasure map (contrary to the previous 10-20 I was getting just a couple days ago). For those of you who haven't seen it already, here's part of the post made by Runesabre yesterday to the COB Dev Board which lists some of what went in the patch:

Fishing Changes
-----------------------

- You will only be able to fish up things other than fish (boots, SOS bottles, treasure maps) if you are in deep sea water. This basically translates to away from the shore on a boat.

- The chance to fish up SOS bottles and treasure maps has been greatly reduced.

- Sea serpents will also only spawn if you are in deep sea. For each sea serpent actually spawned, there will be a small chance it will be a slightly more powerful deep sea serpent. Fished up sea serpents won't last very long either... they eventually disappear.

Pet Guarding
-------------------

- You will no longer be able to command a pet to guard a corpse unless it is your own corpse. Yes, it is possible for a ghost to command his pet to guard a corpse. Comes in handy... not sure how many players knew a ghost could do such a thing.

Misc
--------

- Few exploits were fixed. :)

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Home Security: If there is anything/anyone you wish to attack outside of your house, summon or have a large tamed animal (the scaly kind is best if tamed) and name it something simple such as "A", then ban the creature, once he is banned, you may control it all while being safe inside of your home, the creature can hear your commands threw the walls, so if you have a tamed dragon, you can have a lot of fun with the people living next door trying to get into their homes with your pet taking a chomp out them.
  • Unlimited Space: You can have unlimited space in any house by doing this. Stack 3 chests on top of one another then take the bottom chest and try to put it in a full container. The 2nd chest will fall and will be on the same tile as the 1st chest.
  • House Break-In: This bug requires using UOE with the "remove object" feature. It is quite difficult to do, but worth the time. First, you must mark a rune outside the house in which you would like to break in to. Then, stand about three spaces away from the door, and use the remove object option on it. Now, make sure the rune is on last target and begin to cast gate. After it has been cast, run into the house and while it is temporarily lagged, hit last target and a gate will be cast from inside the house to the outside. This seems to work even though the new patch prevented people from gating from inside of houses. I have only been successful at this while using fast walk, and I assume that it works because of frame skipping and it somehow recognizes you inside the house for a very short period of time.
  • Pillow Bug: I got "a pillow" from a sunken ship today, I can stand on it like you used to be able to do with ribs and get over large smithy counters.

Everyone with a public buildings should take special notice of that last bug. You might want to bank anything valuable you have behind the counter until we receive confirmation of this one, just to be safe.
