Resist Tip

This tip is for building resist, but will only work if you already have a high level mage and a home or boat. What i do is use my GM mage to summon 4-5 elementals, name them all, then command them all to stay! I then log out and log in under my other char. I toggle into attack mode and let the elementals blast me with spells individually!! and heal when I need to. When they finally vanish I log back in with my mage and repeat the process. This has gotte me from 57 resist to 71 resist in about a 1 week period

Box Dupe?

I discovered something very strange while macroing tailoring up. I found the wooden box a while ago and kept it on my as a container. I started macroing tailoring up by making cloaks. When I run out of fabric and sell all my stuff, at the bottom is a second wooden box. I was very confused. I thought it was just a fluke. I buy more yarn and make more fabric and start macroing again. Then once I am done selling it I have a THIRD wooden box that is empty as well. I was wondering if someone could figure out how to harness this to make it dupe gold. Would be very nice to dupe 20k in gold.
