Internet Security, a growing concern:

Credit: Markee http://i.am/uomall

Hacking/breaching other people's computers is becoming a common occurance daily.   This is no joke or exaggeration, I've seen a number of people e-mail's in the last week that have asked me why everything in the UO account is suddenly gone.  No one is safe, and no amount of software will protect you 100%.   You can take percausions to limit the possibilities of a hacker getting into your computer though.

1) Don't accept files from anyone you don't know or barely know

2) Don't give your ICQ # out to people, you can be hacked through ICQ without NetBus or Back Orifice programs.

3) Hide your IP address at all costs.

4) Static IP Address users: Have your ISP change your IP address every couple of weeks, and use a firewall.

5) Read this: http://support.uo.com/faq_sec.html

This is an extremely serious matter and you should not ignore these warnings.  If someone has hacked your computer, call your local authorities and report it, they will help you.

I did a search on download.com for firewall software.  I came across a Netbus/Back Orifice jammer, it might be something you want to check out:http://jammer.comset.net/index.html
