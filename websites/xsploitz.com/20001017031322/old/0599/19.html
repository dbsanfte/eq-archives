1K/10minutes

Once you get the hang of it, you can make 1k in about 10 minutes using this, but it gets really really boring.. Well here's the bug: things needed-any character good or junk, short or tall and about 20 pieces of cooked ribs. ok, now that you have what you need, on to the bug! go to a hide shop(I went to the one in Brit, by the carpenter shop) and find the NPC who sells hides.(the one in the Brit shop is named 'Tanner') Now 'vendorsell' the NPC 5 ribs(you can try experiment-maybe sell him 3 or 4 at a time instead of 5), then 'vendorbuy' all the ribs on him. Now just keep repeating this over and over and over and over..oh yeah there's one exception every once in a while, he won't buy at all, then you just "vendorsell" to him again. You should be selling your ribs to him at 7gp each and buying them back at 3gp each.(double yer money!)

Tip: you can use scrolls left on the patio(if it's on the edge) of those houses with a patio!

Lumberjacking Bug

Lumberjacking has the same "don't check the container first" bug that fishing has. If you are overloaded or already have 125 items in your pack, you will not remove resources from a tree when you succeed the skill check. Unlike fishing however, skill failures still removes resources from the trees. This bug can be used to greatly reduce the amount of wandering about that one does. While testing this out, I raised my lumberjacking from 76.8 to 77.1 on only 2 trees. (I got 37 skill checks on one tree and 22 on the other.) I have tried this with both an overloaded pack (400 stones) and an "over-itemed" pack of 125 items. Both ways work. This was tested on Pacific.
