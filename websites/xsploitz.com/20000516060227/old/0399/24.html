9:09 PM CST - Sal (sal@xsploitz.com)

The mini-patch that I said was published two days ago was apparently put in today (I still only got 2 treasure maps from macroing last night). Along with this patch comes a lot of crap that will ruin the fishing skill for most of you:

  • All special items found from fishing can only be found in deep water.
  • Other than boots, special items such as treasure maps, SOS's, and magical fish are more rare.
  • When fish aren't biting, skill doesn't increase.
  • If you are hidden, fishing will now unhide you.

I strongly urge everyone to attend a fishing protest being organized by Daeron. The event will be take place in LB's throneroom tomorrow at 8 PM CST. I'm not sure of which shard this will be happening on, but as soon as I find out I will post it here.  If you don't have a fisherman, show up anyway to support the cause.

Another thing that went in this patch is a fix for the gate scroll bug (OSI's third attempt at fixing it). Now, when you use a scroll on a rune with the location blocked, the scroll will disappear. Also, a house break-in with flying nightmares has been fixed.

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Recall Bug: There's a bug that lets you get a recall ready while you are recalling.  Using your recall marco get a recall spell ready.  When you get a cursor before clicking on a rune hold down your recall marco.   As soon as you click on the rune it starts casting another recall spell and is ready for you when you get to where the rune is marked.  This makes it almost impossible for someone to catch you as you enter a dungeon and disrupt your recalls.
  • Town Kill: Find someone macroing in town, lets say at the town crier.  (Note they don't have to be macroing its just easier :P) cast explosion on them and then recall away.  Because explosion is a lagged spell, it wont hit them till about 2or 3 seconds after you cast it so if you do it perfectly it should turn out with the guards saying "Thou Shall Regret Thine Actions Swine" but by then you disappear from the recall.  I have done with multiple times, I suggest putting all items in bank except a bow or any kind of weapon.  Recall back to the guy you used explosion on and you will be flagged but you are not tabbed out, so you two can go at it. its best to use a bow if they are macroing you can nail them and they wont move.
  • Backpack Bug: I found a bug that will let u carry more than 100 items and at a time. First, u have to get a empty bag or box. Then u put as as many items as u can. After that if u try to place this bag directly into ur packet, it will say that u can't carry too much items. At this time all u have to do is to find an NPC and drop the bag or box on NPC. After that NPC will said he can carry too many items, THEN the NPC will place the bag or box directly into ur pocket. Keep do the same thing again with another bag. Boom.. u got 2 bags = 200 items in ur packet. The max   items u can carry will become 400 items which is = 400 stones.

One more thing before I go, the poisoning bug where you keep the poison even if you fail has been fixed. Now, when you fail, the poison is used up.
