Enjoy Tonights Updates:

  • Ok this is a bug that will let you recall in one pump. What you do is... 1. Have your recall macro set up for quick use 2. Then have your fingers on the bow macro (ctrl+b). 3. First bow, the quickly recall.(if you are using UOE then use your emergency recall rune) 4.You should recall one pump, makes for quick outtings.
  • HOUSE BREAK IN?---  First, you have to be over weight and have at least 10 purple potions(or enough to kill you). Travel with someone (have him carry some of the stuff with him so you can walk). Get to the front of the door and get as much over-weight as you can. Then try to walk, getting you to 0 stamina. While being over weight and with 0 stamina, double click on all the purple potions at once so they all start counting down. Then as soon as all of them start counting down, keep pressing tab as fast as you can and run towards the door. KEEP PRESSING TAB, even after you die. When you die and the screen asking you if you wanna rez or come back as a ghost comes up, your ghost should be in the house. Just Rez and loot away.
