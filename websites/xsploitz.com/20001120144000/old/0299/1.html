Today's Unconfirmed Bug(s)

  • Teleport Bug: When you need to hide real bad but are getting your ass kicked by something, cast teleport.  While teleport is casting (as soon as you click on the targeting cursor disappears), hit your hiding macro and you will hide.  Seems that when teleporting line of sight is removed from your attackers so you're free to hide!  
  • Lower Karma: Go to bucs den.  Use provoke on an NPC.  You won't gain skill but your karma drops soo fast.  You can hit evil in 15 minutes.   Also, theres a bank in bucs den now (on all shards?)!  It's very useful.
  • Detect Hidden:  Make an arrangement of 8 chests, and stand in the empty corner, and detect the middle. The tinker's skill that made the trapped chests plays a big part, with 60 detect, I still can't detect my gm tinker's traps even 1% of the time, so try to find a newbie tinker with 20-30 or so skill for the first few attempts, then 40-60, then around 80, then gm for the most speed.
  • Fishing Bug: When you go fishing and stack the fish that you've caught in your backpack. If you then use a bladed item on the stack of fish, you get 4 steaks and use up 2 fish. but if you single out the fish and use the knife on each fish by itself, you still get 4 steaks from 1 fish.
