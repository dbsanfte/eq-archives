How to get House ownership to all your characters if you characters already have houses of there own:

  1. Take the Character that needs friend a house to the house he wishs to be friended.
  2. Have that character open their paperdoll window.
  3. Double click on a Usable Object like your sword, so that you get a targeting cursor.
  4. Log that character out of the game.
  5. Log in with the owner of that house. (Targeting cursor will still be on).
  6. Make sure Paper Doll is open to logout later.
  7. Just double click on the house sign to bring up the friends menu.
  8. Select add a friend to the house.
  9. When targeting cursor appears log off.
  10. Relog in with the character you wished to have friended to the house.
  11. Now just click on yourself..

Bingo, your done. It makes NO difference where you stand as long as you are near the sign of the house. Works like a charm.

Keeping your order or chaos shield after death!

Join a Choas or order guild...Get a shield for whichever you are...wear the shield..have the other type of guild kill you and when your about to die drag the shield off so your holding it but not dropped it in your bag or on ground...when your a ghost you can run around with your choas/order shield. Works with weapons also.
