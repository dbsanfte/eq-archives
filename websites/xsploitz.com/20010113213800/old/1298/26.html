Tonights Updates:

  • We all know how well Silver works against undead creatures right?  Well find yourself a silver haliburd or bardiche and go tackle a lich... if you have about 50 resist and more than 75 str you shoudl be able to handle him.  But what about living creatures? Well if you poison a silver weapon, you basically enable the "silver" qualities on living creatures.  I have 80 str and with my silver bardiche i can kill a normal skeleton in 1 shot.  I poisoned my blade while up in minoc and decided to see what effect it had on a troll.  4 hits and he was on the ground.  Nifty eh?
  • Provoke Skill Gain     With the new patch you can't block in monsters or animals because they break whatever is blocking them in.so i figured out a way to get passed this.Here is what you do to get provoke up with new patch:   1.First you get an animal/monster (animal preferred because they wont attack  people and break the the item that you use to block it with)at the outside edge of your house if you have a one-room house or against a wall if you have a multiple-room house, up against a wall so that you can be directly across from it on the other side of the same wall.   2.Next you get it trapped without it going after you and breaking the chests.One way to do it is paralyzing the animal where it should be then quickly trapping it then hiding until it is no longer aggressive towards you or peacemaking so that it is no longer attacking you.   3.Now you go directly across from it on the same wall and start provoking the animal on any item (I used a chest) and your skill will go up.When you fail it will attack you but since you are directly across from it, the animal wont break the chests because you are straight across and the chests are on its sides and behind it so make sure you dont move and hope noone kills it if it is outside.
