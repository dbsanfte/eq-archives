I know that you had a housing pole awhile back, but I think you should bring it back and one of your protests as well.  Recently, talking to Counselor Matrix I learned that "dynamic" objects that prevent you from placing a house include: flowers, grass pockets, mushrooms, and all those really MINOR objects.  What the heck!?  This was verified by GM jakarter, or something like that.  This is insane, OSI is thwarting their own efforts at making an interactive, RP community because you can no longer make cities, guild villages, etc.  PLEASE, lets protest, I will get 3 guilds to join in the protest and I know MANY others will join.

Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • Dexterity: I have done this myself, and it definitely works.  Every so often they scan the servers for people will buffed dex and lower it back down, but you can just do the bug again the next day and all will be back up again. The bug: 1. You will need a full suit of plate armor, female plate chest, and magical wizards hat. 2. Cast bless on yourself. 3. Cast Polymorph on yourself. 4. Equip all armor and magical wizards hat. 5. Cast Curse on yourself. 6. You stats will be the same as normal, except your stamina should say "1". 7. Wait until the bless wears off (ie until your stats change) and then immediately take everything off. 8. Once the curse wears off, you will have 11 pts of permanent extra dex (Assuming you are a GM Mage).
  • Save Space: If you have a furnace/stove thing, you know, that chimney thing, and put a dartboard on the right side (facing south) it will delete half the chimney and you can walk through it! You can still cook stuff on it! This will help those people who train their chars with dummies/stoves/spinning wheels and need to conserve space!
  • Disguise Kit: Before someone recruits you into the guildstone apply your disguise kit to change your name. When the Guildmaster accepts you into the guild the name you had from the disguise kit will appear on the stone. This comes in handy when you come back to kill  the rest of the guild with a different name. (this is helpful because when you are in a thieves guild you cant attack blue people or you will get kicked out ). They wont know who to kick out of the guild because of the fake name. :)

Warning!: Be careful when dealing with anyone who is selling land to place a house!!! If they ask to see the deed, DO NOT PUT IT ON THE TRADE WINDOW! All they have to do is cancel the trade and random steal from you. The deed will always be stolen because it is the lightest item in your inventory. This happened to me at the Britain bank and the thief was off with my deed before I even had a chance to call the guards. =(
