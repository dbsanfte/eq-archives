Unconfirmed Bug(s):

  • New Scroll Bug: Mana Vampire scrolls do not vanish after use. (I haven't tried it yet, so let me know)
  • Update to Making Items Float:  First, you need to have body parts carved off a human corpse, as they decompose. After stacking these parts in a tile, it is possible to stack an item on top of them. As the body parts decay, the item will retain it's current height. You can then either leave the item where it is or lock it down so it can't be moved.

  • Gain Resist In Town: Mark a rune to a house inside of town.  It's best to do this in an empty house.  Run outside of town, until you get the, "You have left the protection of the city guards." message.  Then recall back in, but do not move.   Start casting spells on yourself and your magic resistance should still build.

  • Tailoring Bug: When making Jester Hats, if you hit last object just after you create a hat, a fancy shirt will appear in your inventory.

  • Magic Combo (Again): This combo is great for Abyss Test or on the regular shards.  Cast explosion and target the person.   As soon as you click the person, start casting flamestrike.  This combo is extremely devastating and I have actually killed a couple people (Maxed out on everything) in one shot.  If you get two or three people doing this, no one has a chance at living.
