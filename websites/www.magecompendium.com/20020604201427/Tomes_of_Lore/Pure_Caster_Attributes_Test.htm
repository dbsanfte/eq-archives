Pure Caster Attributes Test

 

 

Note : This test was made only for Pure Casters.

 

This Tests have been run with a level 44 Enchanter and a Guard in North Karana, Who hits for a maximum damage of 65 points.

 

In each test the fight took 20 minutes to get confirmed statistics.

 

The first question is how does agility effect or influence the Dodge ability ?

 

Test One : With 75 Agility

 

Test Two : With 165 Agility (Due to Items and Buffs).

 

During both tests the Armour Class was kept the same. 

 

During Test One the character used dodged 6 times on average (75 Agility).

 

During Test Two the character used dodged 7 times on average (165 Agility).

 

Conclusion : These Tests have been run several times, and the results have nearly always been the same. That means that Dodge is only influenced due to the Skill in the ability one has and probably not due to the Agility of the player.

 

The second question on our minds was how important Armour Class really is ?

 

We used the similar tests as above.

 

We made several tests with a character nearly naked and slowly increased the Armour Class up to its maximum level using buffs and Armour.

 

Overall, after we evaluated all the tests, we came to the conclusion that you get for every 10 Ac 1 Hp (Exactly 0.9) less damage. 

 

Conclusion : That means for every 1 Ac 0.1 (exactly 0.09) less damage due to melee damage.

 

Alright, what does it mean ? 

 

Items which give you any extra hit points are much more important using this information.

 

An Example : One Caster, let's just say he has an Armour Class of 400, the other one only has an Armour Class of 370 but he gets a benefit due to his items of lets say about 30 hp.

 

Both encounter the same enemy and both get hit 10 times in battle.

 

Due to his higher Ac the first one gets 3 hit points less damage than the second one at each hit.

 

That makes at 10 hits a total of 30 hp less damage he will take.

 

On the other hand the second one has 30 hit points more. Ok, now just as a suggestion, the second one looses more hit points overall that means that he has a higher downtime and or needs to be healed up, including the fact that someone else has to spend mana to heal him, that means group downtime increases etc…etc…

 

Counting everything I would say that the ratio between Armour Class and hit points is for Pure Casters 1:1 and maybe for Enchanters 1:2 (because of their ability to taunt).

 

Conclusion : That means 1 Ac overall worth 1 Hp.

 

The Question now is what makes more sense ?

 

Before answering that, another point of view coming first. Imagine you wear a magic item which provides extra 3 Agility. It depends on your level, but let's just say you get another extra 3 Ac.

 

Any other Item which gives a plus of lousy 5 hit points would be more effective (both items must have the same base Armour class).

 

Overall, it looks like that Pure Casters only have two really important skills. Intelligence and Stamina (Charisma for Enchanters).

 

Dexterity is not needed because no Pure Caster will melee at higher levels (procs of weapons) and Agility is not needed cause of the reasons I tried to explain above.

 

I would propose to stick on equipment that provides good Armour Class and hit points (Needless to say also Intelligence).

 

These tests have been made several times from four players, although it is not guaranteed that the results are correct.
