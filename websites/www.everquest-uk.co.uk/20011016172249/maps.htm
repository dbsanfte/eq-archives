Maps

With so many excellent map resources out there, it would be foolish to try and emulate them.

With that in mind, from this page are links to the best sites on the web, IMHO.

The first site I *always* visit is EQ Atlas. I just like the look and feel of the maps they produce and have a full set of his work printed out and by my side at all times.

The other site I use is EQMaps. I tend to not use the maps from here unless I need one that EQ Atlas doesnt have, but the sheer amount of zone, item, NPC etc etc info here is staggering. Well worth a look.

If you know of any other excellent map sites out there, please let me know.
