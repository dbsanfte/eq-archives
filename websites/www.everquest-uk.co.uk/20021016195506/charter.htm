Guild Charter
11 April 2002
This Guild was founded in 1999 by Kaladdar and others who were real life friends living in England. Lucky early members of the guild had holidays together and were privileged to enjoy personal friendships.

I was invited into the guild sometime in late 1999. I was excited to join such an illustrious group of loonies and terrific people many of whom I was and am proud to call my friends. I was the first American member. Circumstances change and life evolves. October 22, 2001, I was promoted to Guild Leader. I am happy to be heading such a terrific group of amusing, fun friends, as we battle critters and spend our relaxation time together. All the members of Charmed Alliance are working adults who choose to spend some or all ( LOL) of our free time adventuring together.

Our guild has held fast to the traditions, legendary repartee, camaraderie, and friendship which has always been the bedrock of our small but well reputed band of adventurers.

We have a few requirements for membership in our little family. They are as follows:

1. The potential member must be an adult over 18.
2. The potential member must provide a valid email address--in case we need them and they haven't been reading the boards regularly.
3. The potential member must be sponsored by a member in good standing. The potential member must accompany the guild on one of its weekend outings so that all guildies have an opportunity to evaluate them for membership.
4. The potential member must possess a terrific sense of humour and an ability not to take the game too seriously.
5. The potential member must be voted upon by all members. The Guild Leader will decide any ties.
6. The potential member is a provisional member when initially tagged. The member or the guild may decide without animosity that the member and the guild do not mesh.
7. The Guild Leader has the privilege of tagging all new members.

The following is a list of friendship rules I received from a good friend. I happily share them here as they seem so applicable to the CA sense of how things should be:


1) When you are sad, ...I will get you drunk and help
you plot revenge against the sorry bastard who made you sad.
2) When you are blue, ...I'll try to dislodge whatever is choking you.
3) When you smile, ...I'll know you finally got off line and got laid.
4) When you are scared, ...I will rag you about it every chance I get.
5) When you are worried, ...I will tell you horrible
stories about how much worse it could be and to quit whining.
6) When you are confused...I will use little words to explain it to your dumb ass.
7) When you are sick.....stay away from me until you're well again. I don't want whatever you have.
8) When you fall, I will point and laugh at your clumsy ass.
9) When your character dies, I will do all I can to get you rezzed and then hound you endlessly about your stupidity.
10) I will remind you it's just a damn game when you get caught up in the moment.
