Spells & Magic Stuff

With so many excellent spell listings and research resources out there, it would be foolish to try and emulate them.

With that in mind, from this page are links to the best sites on the web, IMHO.

The first site I *always* visit is Casters Realm. Total and complete, this site has everything you will ever need ot know regarding spells and their research too.

The other site I use is EQ Caster. They have an excellent utility called EQ Caster, which basically parses your spdat file (the EQ file on your HDD that stores all the spells info within the game) and presents it in a quick and easy to view format. AS its reading directly from an official EQ file, its as close to the real effects as you can get, although it is well known that Verant likes to throw in the odd "made up" spell to catch out those people who like to rant about such things :) Perfectly safe and OK to use, its well worth a look.

If you know of any other excellent map sites out there, please let me know.
