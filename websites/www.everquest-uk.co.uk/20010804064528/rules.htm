Guild Rules
Hail!!

We have only very few set policies as far as new members to the guild go. Firstly however you must be over 18 years old. This rule is not for awkwardness, its more to protect other people as we like to talk as we want in the guild without fear of language upsetting anyone. We are a solid group of friends and all new members are treated as such and this rule means we can all relax together alot more.

Secondly you are better to be sponsored by another member of the guild. Fitting in and taking advantage of the fun and friendship we provide can be tougher without knowing anyone in the guild to start with. Again this is more to facilitate your enjoyment.

Charmed Alliance is not a large guild, nor will it ever be so, however we do want to be part of large events and explore as much as we can. At present w are able to take a good few more members.

Give myself or an officer within the guild a tell if you are interested in joining. A member online at the time will point you at the nearest officer :)
