ME and Faces - Sacrates
Here's a bit of cool info on faces and expressions in Middle Earth! This is very cool info to me, since I think that is the one thing these games lack is way to express one's emotions. When I attack a monster I don't want to run in with some lackadaisical look on my characters face. It should look like I want to shove that Sword right up the beasts ass, turn it, then have a broad smile on my face as I tug it free and spit on the creatures face! Will that happen? Read on.

We will provide an unprecedented number of facial feature options. Besides that, if you change your clothing, it will show up on your character! It's Everquest's use of vertex animation that limits them this way. We're using a mix of skeletal animation and vertex animation to get the best of choice AND look.

EQ and food - Sacrates
Abashi was on the boards again and had this to say on the need for Food in Everquest. I have to agree with him, I think food should be extremely important. Plus it's not too much to worry about. Not to mention you don't need to do anything as it eats automatically in the background.

No, you will not die from running out of food, but running out of food may lead to your death :) Food is important enough that you'll never want to be without it, and it's just one of those things that is expected in an RPG. Nearly every MUD has a need for food, and MUDs are what EQ is based on.

-Abashi, Everquest Guide
