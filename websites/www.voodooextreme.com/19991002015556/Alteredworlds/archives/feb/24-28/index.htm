U9 Party info update
Well it seems that the party did exist in U9 but Da man with Da plan, Richard Garriott chopped it off at the head.

No, the party existed in the game (and boy do I have about 500 pages of conversation code to prove it) until recently. The decision to cut was made by Richard after he joined the design process, saw what we were doing and spoke with the programmers about the difficulty of doing the party correctly. It was a decision of necessity.

Carmack Speaks

Well because everything this man says or does will affect the 3D community down the road and will inevitably affect our RPG's etc! Not to mention I love listening to him talk! I ripped this off VE:

Say, if you toss a grenade under a desk, you'd like to see it modelled so it shoots out from underneath and billows into a big cloud. That´s the type of thing you could do with an expanding particle model"
"The level designers, right now, build with blocks and assign textures to the blocks. In the future they'll be able to to bend the blocks to fit curves to them, and assign fractal noise to theam and surfaces as both displacement maps and texture modifiers." "However, the most obvious immediate benefit of next-generation engine is going to be scalable detail: to be able to have an outdoor area and do it well, be able to look down a canyon, or see a building in the distance and walk all the way up to it"
