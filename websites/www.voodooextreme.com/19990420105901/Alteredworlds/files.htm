Drivers

DirectX v6.1 02/24/99 6,680 Kb
The latest version of Microsoft's Gaming API. The above link jumps you to the download page, where you can grab just the DirectX 6.1 core or the core/drivers pack. You may need to visit the manufacturer's site of your sound/video cards for updated drivers as well.
Publisher: Microsoft Corporation The DirectX Homepage

 

DirectX Media v6.0 02/24/99 6,680 Kb
The Microsoft DirectX Media 6.0 Runtime download  includes updates to DirectShow and DirectAnimation, and adds DirectX Transform. The ActiveMovie control has been replaced with the Microsoft Windows Media Player control.
Publisher: Microsoft Corporation The DirectX Homepage

 

3Dfx Reference Drivers 02/24/99 Varies
The latest and greatest version of 3Dfx's reference drivers for their Voodoo family of 3D cards. They recommend that wherever possible, you use the manufacturer's drivers for your specific card, but sometimes you just have to go straight to the source. You can grab Win95/98 and NT specific drivers here.



Publisher: 3Dfx Technologies 3Dfx Drivers Homepage

 

Specific 3Dfx Drivers 02/24/99 Varies
Lots of times, the company that made your particular graphics board will enhance the reference drivers in some way - adding extra options or giving you that extra little bit of performance. Here you will find quick links to the most popular manufacturer's sites.
Publisher: 3Dfx Technologies 3DFX Drivers Homepage
