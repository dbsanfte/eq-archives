Might and Magic VII Alpha - Sacrates
Just noticed this bit of goodness over at Billy's Love Shack. (VE) That Might and Magic VII has gone Alpha already. These guys really pimp out the games don't they? It seems like just yesterday that M&M VI was released.

And, as if that weren't enough, the highly-anticipated Might and Magic VII For Blood and Honor hit alpha today. Following last year's immensely popular Might and Magic VI The Mandate of Heaven, For Blood and Honor maintains the rich legacy of classic role-playing that made the Might and Magic series so successful while adding technological advances such as 3D acceleration.

Other new features include monster vs. monster combat, fully digitized character faces, multiple races, improved combat movement and automapping.

Might and Magic VII is expected to ship at the end of March

Clans Look - Sacrates
GA Source have gone and slapped up their first look at the upcoming game similar to Diablo (Who would have thunk?) called Clans. Looks very dark and foreboding. Let's hope it plays like that too.

Dragonflight Progress - Sacrates
Noticed over at the fresh scented VN that Kevin Oxland who is leading the Development Team on Dragonlfight splashed a little update our way. Haven't heard much on this gem lately.

The game is still progressing and is looking fantastic. We had to re-write some of our technology but it was well worth it and has brought a whole new feel to the game. Its much faster, so looks smoother. Technically we have many more new features that we can use to bring the world alive and the game/story elements are being implemented continuously, as we speak. As you can appreciate, the game and story elements are the bulk of the work and takes time to fine tune. Its looking like a late summer completion/release now, but it'll be worth the wait.

We'll be updating our web site soon with a new improved look so watch out for that also.

Lack of Zones in EQ - Sacrates
I haven't even left Faydwer yet, and that is not the largest Continent. There is no lack of size in this game so far and i've been playing a month solid. Your gonna have to play this game for quite sometime before you can even go to certain places because you'd be killed in a heartbeat. Check the word from Brad.

I heard that there might be two less Continents, but there are more total 'Adventure Zones' then they had when they were planning on the 5 Continents...

-Esoog

This is true. We are shipping with approximately 70 zones, as opposed to the (much) earlier plan of 40 or so. We decided to pack more zones into Odus, Antonica, and Faydwer, as opposed to spreading them out more.

So, bottom line, regardless of how many 'continents' we're shipping with, the game is a lot larger than we'd orginally planned.

EQ and UO - Sacrates
Here is just a quick example of what misinformation can do to you. Not only that but I think this guy is an AOL user. Not that there is anything wrong with AOL mind you.. (Snicker) Here's Brad dishing the deal out.

a: I don't have the techincal support for EQ. When your poor like me, you can't afford the MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENT of a 3d accelerator. Even with a 3d card, it is going to lag like hell.

Nope -- a game like this being 3d or 2d has little to nothing to do with lag.

Brad, you are so kind. Let me step in here. What are you Nuts?! Can't afford a 3D card?! This, my friends is the perfect example of drug induced posting. Hell, you can buy a Voodoo under $40 now and a V2 under $100. As for the lag issue, this guy is totally uninformed. (He obviously didn't read my Preview) There is little to no lag in this game so far at all.

Right now, in beta, you have what...a few hundred people with a graphic engine near quake II quality? Imagine when it releases 1500 per server (the max...to low in my opinion), with 28.8 connections, low rate 3d card, lag everywhere.

Nope. During phase 3 of testing we had 1700 people on a server, with all sorts of connections, 3d cards, etc. There was NOT 'lag everywhere'. In fact, people are pretty blown away as to how lag resistant EQ is.

This guy could make a living talking out of his ass! (Not you Brad :)) Let's put it this way, just visit a Bank in UO sometime and you will learn what Lag is. Put 20 players on screen at once in EQ may cause a little graphic slowdown but not much lag. 1500 players IMHO is perfect.

: B: UO is a rpg. EQ is a rpg. UO is faster, original, has more of a rpg aspect (Can we say virtual economy? I haven't heard that word from anyone in EQ).

Depends what you mean by a 'virtual economy'. We have a 'player-driven economy' that depends on tried and true principles such as supply and demand.

UO is faster? How can he say this when he hasn't played EQ may I ask? More of an RPG aspect? Yeah I guess PKilling with a few 12 year olds does add to the realism of the game. Virtual Economy? Please, it was a failed attempt at trying to be too realistic. Virtual Economy means squat when it's not fun. This is a game for god's sake not Wall Street!

Plus i've already devoted enough of my time to this great game
: so if you want to go and complain about Uo and how EQ is god, go to www.eqvault.com...we don't want your kind here.

Well, I can respect that, however I can't stand by and let misinformation about EQ float around either.

UO "WAS" a great game. I enjoyed the hell out of it. But it failed to live up to it's expectations. Lag is still an issue. (Not in EQ) Bugs are still an issue. (Yes, in EQ also but it's still Beta and most of them are fairly minor balancing issues) This may sound very Pro EQ and it is, but the plain and simple fact of the matter is UO is outdated old Tech. I enjoyed it while it lasted.
