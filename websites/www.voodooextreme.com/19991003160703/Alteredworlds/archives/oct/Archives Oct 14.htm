Dues Ex Plus - Sacrates
You guys are getting the royal Dues Ex treatment today! Looks like these guys over at Ion Storm aren't shy at all. They just let it all hang out. The Interview was done with Chris Norden the lead programmer. We also have some Dues Ex goodness coming your way, but we won't put it up till later on. Especially considering today is Dues Ex day.

on multi-player: Deus Ex is primarily a single player game, but we will have some multi-player missions as well. They will most likely be special cooperative missions that take place on special maps that capitalize on our unique gameplay elements. I'm not saying that we won't have deathmatch, but it's really not a very high priority at this stage of development. Deus Ex is about character interaction and development, problem solving, and exploration... it's not just a mindless shooter. Our combat system is also very realistic - if you get shot in the leg, you won't be as mobile and dexterous. If you get shot in the arm, your aim won't be as accurate. All of the NPCs will also react according to where they have been hurt. As far as the problems with Unreal's networking, Tim is working very hard to address all of the issues that have surfaced lately. We will integrate all of Tim's new networking code when it is available.

BG site change - Sacrates
Sounds like changes are in the works for the Official Baldur's Gate Web Page. Looks like everyone is changing their pages lately eh? I snagged this from the hairy chimps over at BG Chronicles.

We are planning a major site overhaul to change this site from a 'developer' site to a 'consumer' site.

What this means is that the development areas will be moved to a different area (perhaps a daughter site) and will start to focus on ideas / discussions for other BG products (expansions or whatever).

This site will become more focused against the game AS RELEASED, and we want to focus against playing strategies, technical questions (like script writing) etc. We will also improve the interface (make it prettier and easier to navigate) and basically scrub the whole place to get it ready for heavy traffic...

So any thoughts as to what we should include, or how the new boards should be set up? What do people like about this site currently? What would you change or drop entirely?

More Septerra Core - Sacrates
Well, well, well it looks like we weren't the only ones doing Interviews with the studly guys working on Septerra Core? Next Generation also got into the act too with this bit.

Also I was concerned with the exploration and Non Player Character side of RPGs. A lot of them just end up being filler for a dungeon crawl, and instead we've really tried to create a full world that can be explored by all of the player's characters. Each one of your characters has a different agenda, and a different interest or point of view. So depending on who you have in your party, you'll get different information about the story and puzzles in the world. This figures into the plot of the game as well as how you, as a player, gather information and complete story elements.
