Diablo II Beta - Certis
If there is one game I did not enjoy more than a few days it was Diablo. I finished the single player, got nailed by hackers on battle.net and tried it out over a lan with a few buddies. The constant sequence of fighting for 10 minutes and then turning tail back to town was monotonous. If I wanted the same gameplay over and over again I'd revert back to to tetris, at least that game required some thought. From what the previews have to say Diablo II will be more of the same, much more. If that's the case, count me out.
