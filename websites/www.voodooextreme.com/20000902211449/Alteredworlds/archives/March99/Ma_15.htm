Monday March 15, 1999

Klatu Virata Niktu - Sir Sacrates
Oh yeah, Shop Smart...Shop S-Mart. Sci Fi Channel is once again showing Army of Darkness. Therefore I must pause my never ending quest for RPG news at the moment. This movie is the unadulterated king of cheesy one liners and I must stop to memorize them into my vocabulary once more. This movie is my Bible. 

EQ Trade Skills - Sacrates
So you want to get a head start mastering the Trade Skills in Everquest? A new site called Tricks of the Trades has opened their pearly white doors to the public. I like the idea that sites devoted to various aspects of EQ are opening, although i'm going to go ballistic when I see Little Johnies Site on how to be a Kill Stealer.

WoT mini bites - Sacrates
Again, i'm really starting to dig with a fine toothed comb for any news that I can get. There just isn't much at all. Anyway I stopped by WOD and noticed they dug up a few posts by the boys working on the game. First is Glen on missions.

There will be a few missions in the single player story that use the citadel-construction strategy of the multi-player game, intended to ease you into the process.

The single player game is fairly linear, as it is a story.

And a bit from Sam Brown on Multiplayer.

There is an Arena mode wherein you can face off against others in non-Citadel Editor scenarios.

I must also say that WoT and UT will not appear at all alike, not in graphics, content or gameplay. What will be similar are details you probably won't even notice, such as engine enhancements.

Altered Yak - Sacrates
Typical Monday here, going through the normal EQ withdrawl symptoms at the moment: Hands shaking, convulsions every few minutes, thinking of going to the local EB to see if EQ has arrived yet and maybe pick up a Backpack, a Rusty Sword and some Iron Rations while i'm at it. Anyway, we should have some more news coming your way if it ever picks up. I haven't gotten one response on the Giants thing yet so I assume that's a no on the Giants news issue?

EQ Hits - Sacrates
Just got a few confirmations that EQ has begun arriving in some EB's. Either way we won't be able to play until tomorrow at 9 PST. At least it's finally starting to arrive.

AW and Giants? - Sacrates
I just slapped up a quick question in the Altered Boards asking if you want us to cover a little extra Giants news. As you can see by the pic in my Sac Says column the game has a strong Fantasy theme and has many elements that don't really define it under any particular genre. Stop by and give us a thought or two on the matter. 

Lost Adventure? - Sacrates
You want adventure? Join me for my endless quest for big breasted naked Nymphs...now that's adventure! CGO have poked up an Editorial on Adventure lost in recent 3D titles. Run around killing, killing, killing and maybe a puzzle here or there. Check it.

3D, 3D, 3D. Action-Adventure! Run through the landscape shooting things! Oh, a puzzle? OK, fine. Now let's run some more. Since we're seeing more and more of these hybrid action-adventures, it's obvious that I'm not alone. But what about "the old school" of adventure? Has it really lost its following?

Deus Ex Preview - Sacrates
These guys really like to hug onto all info surrounding this game that's for sure. Looks like Beyond 3D was able to scoop some goods up on this highly anticipated gem. Stop by and tell them Sacrates sent you. They may just smile and look the other way.

Backstage at ECTS, Luke and I sat basking in the glory of the Eidos suite, sprawled out across the plush red leather upholstery of the small three piece suite with our feet resting casually on the delicate antique tea-table arranged in front of us. With a suggestive wink to one of the buxom young wenches who were on hand to serve refreshments, I ambled across the room to begin casually free-loading as many of the canapés as I thought I could reasonably get away with. We were chatting with Mike Breslin, this year's ECTS man of the match, while we waited patiently on Tom Hall - Scary Man of Much Wild Hair as the natives used to call him - to show us the very impressive Anachranox. But as we guzzled the free food and ogled the extremely expensive waitresses, we did not know that a little surprise was in the running for us.

Everquest Beta 4 end - Sacrates
Stop by Everworld.net for some good daily EQ coverage and a recently slapped up Beta 4 Coverage page. This is one of the better Beta 4 spots to hit for all the skinny on what happened that fatefull night. All night...not even one big breasted Nymph showed herself to me. Guess i'll just have to make due with the Dwarven bearded woman instead.

One More Day!!! - Bowmeister
Woohoo!!! I've got that warm fuzzy feeling all over, and there's still one more day to go till EQ hits the shelves. Methinks it's time to put one on hold somewhere, just in case :). Site's all archived and running faster than Intel comes up with "errata" when faced with a PR situation. I've got two days off coming tuesday and wednesday, so look for some site revamping and other treats mid week - assuming of course I don't play Everquest for 48 hours straight :).
