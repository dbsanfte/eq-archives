Saturday December 26, 1998

The day after Altered Spew - Sacrates
Expect not much in the way of news over the weekend. I'll be digging like I normally do, but the day after Christmas and the weekend you can expect everyone in the Industry to be at the Strip Clubs right around now. Either that or they are playing the hell out of Baldur's Gate and Tribes! BTW, Tribes is all the shit. a total surprise on my part. If you like Team strategy games this is superb and will keep you satisfied until TF 2 slaps you in the John Holmes. Baldur's Gate isn't that bad either. :)

EQ goodness - Sacrates
Looks like EQ Vault have slapped up a new Editorial on EQ Entitled "Building a Community". They also have some screenshots of our lovable Guides that have been assigned to the message boards lately so stop off and grab one, two and three of them.

Friday December 25, 1998      Merry Christmas ya Mofo's!!!

Merry Christmas and other ramblings - Sacrates
Have a great holiday everyone. Just a few things before I take a brief respite from the page today. I'll be back on the job tomorrow you can be sure. I just need to put in some time on Tribes and of course Baldur's Gate. These two games have literally come along and kicked me in the Altered Worlds. Although...word to the wise. Baldur's Gate is strictly a single player experience. The Multiplayer is Nasty with a capital N! This game just wasn't meant for it unless you want to go through a lot of dialogue hell. Things get really frustrating when you have 6 players in at the same time. For those that do have it, imagine taking your turns at the Inn getting equiped?!! That is what consisted of my whole MP experience. Otherwise this game is incredible. Although I may be back later to update IF I get some time.,\

Thursday December 24, 1998

Baldur's Gate is here! - Sacrates
Well, I was one of the lucky ones and was able to grab a copy of Baldur's Gate before it sold out. First impressions....this game is simply amazing. It's the kind of game that you only see come along occasionally. Diablo, Half Life, etc etc. We have been covering this gem at AW long and hard for a while now and am happy to say it's 100% worth it. The music is absolutely fantastic and is something that impressed me. Sounds very similar to Conan the Barbarian. I also was able to procure a copy of Tribes for myself too so you can expect VERY sporadic updates. With that said, you have obviously figured out that Baldur's Gate has arrived so what are you doing sitting there for? Go get it if you can.

Altered Holiday Spew - Sacrates
Well, here we are. Christmas Eve and still no hide nor hair of Everquest Beta. Although for that now though, I am thinking mid to late January. That's also depending on if they've got the cd's duplicated yet. Although I don't think they would have duped them just yet, since I would think they would want to get all the info on them rather than have you dload a big mofo of a patch when you get on, especially with this new sound system going in soon in B3. Either way, your going to have Baldur's Gate to pass the time until then, and I seriously doubt your gonna finish that in a month. Not exactly sure what to think of today in terms of news however. Just check back and you'll see it here if there is.

The Holidays either bring incredibly Epic trips to the bathroom or incredibly fun games for us PC Gamers. In the case of this year we get both.
