The Celestial rapture is always looking for Good people to join their ranks.
We might not recruit quick, or recruit often, but there is always room for the right minded player.

How to get in TCR - i.e. our application process:

Group with us and get to know us. Join our chat channel and talk with us.
We are a tight knit group of players who take pride into the guild's advancement.
Someone who knows you and can vouch for you is a good thing to have.

The nature of the high level game requires high level players.
You're welcome to pre-apply anytime, and tag along for exp groups - but keep in mind that it is best if you are lvl60 - with an exception for clerics.

The process to apply to CR is divided in three phases.
When you express an interest in joining, you can hang out in our channel crcount:crcount - that is kept moderated during raids, but otherwise it is the place to start getting to know us.
Do not let the levels you lack discourage you - with the new exp zones in POP one could do 50 to 60 in merely a week.
Group with us, get to know us, we can possibly help you get character flags for the zones we exp in such as POV.

An applicant is any person who has expressed interest to join. They are welcome to group with us in crcount, and getting to know us, and vice versa.
Applicants must ask before coming to raids.

Once the majority of the guild has got to know you, and you satisfy the level requirement (case-by-case exceptions on that can be made, but not often), a decision is made and you have the status of recruit - and are informed of it.
This means that we are actively considering you to be guilded soon. Recruits must come to all raids and show us what they're worth.

Once we've seen you in action, and we know you, and you gradually get to be a part of our team, and we choose you to be with us, then it's probably time for you to wear our tag.

Be patient. Don't expect a guildtag after two raids or three. It's not a quick thing.

Be on at eurotime, and frequently - If you can only play a couple hours a day on weekends, then the guild isn't for you. We raid anywhere from 4 to 6 days a week, depending on

Have a solid Internet connection. If you go Linkdead during raids and fall back in time, then you do little good.

Come prepared to raids. Peridots, reagents, coffins, etc. We help with the costs of reagents, but ask in advance!

Have resist gear appropriate for all encounters, and work on it if you don't. Try to get a bare minimum of 100 in any one resist with 100 mr at the same time. Have an invis-EB-night vision item, or potions

Must know how to listen and follow orders during raids. If you just go around doing your own thing (scouting), and cant follow instructions, then the guild is not for you.

Do not expect loot for some time after you join. We want people to earn their items.

You will only have one main character for CR. Do not expect loot for alts, unless it's a rot item.


SERVER TRANSFERS --

We respect the fact that people who choose to join us with server transfers lose all their gear to be doing so. If you wish to join us, and your character is on another server, I will require an e-mail from you first. Include in that e-mail the reasons that make you move, what server you come from, level-class-aa purchased, your past guild history, and contacts from your past guilds that may let us know more about you. Include in the e-mail your playtimes, your /played, when you started playing, and any information that you would deem useful. Upon acceptance of your application you will be told to move, and will come to raids as a recruit. We will then get to know you in person and help gear you up, and if you hold true to your promises, we'll tag you.
