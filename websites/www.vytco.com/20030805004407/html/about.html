 Welcome.

 This guild is oriented to become a raiding guild. Our membership is limited per class. Total number of active members shall no exceed 64 at this time. This is done to ensure focused attention to members need and our ability to still perform tasks we need to do at this level.

 For distribution of the vacancies between classes please check our message board or roster link. Message board will provide more accurate picture since it is updated daily.

 Our requirements to maintain active status at this time are following:

 - for characters under level 30: to level 10 levels per week

 - for character between levels 31 and 46: to level 5 levels per week

 - for characters 46 and above to reach level 51 by the end of the second week after recruitment

 - for characters between levels 51 and 55: 2 levels per week

 - for characters 56 - 60: get run 3 by the end of the second week after recruitment.

 - No alts (except bazaar mules should be played by members until L60 is achieved.

 - You have to attend 40% of the guild raids to maintain active status.

 

What we intend to do. Well, start from getting everyone to planar level 46+, then complete some important epics and move to obtaining Velious armor. Next step will be getting ready for VT. Long process of collecting shards.

 

Our minimum level requirements will be increasing as majority of us are leveling up.
