March 04, 2005::

Today we found out that our officer and guildmate ESCOSES died on the way home in an automobile accident. Escoses was loved by everyone in Lords and outside of the guild. His mellow and calm personality and  willingness to help people won him many friends in the world of Norrath. With deep regret and sorrow we face the fact that one of the best players and our friend has tragically died.

We love you Escoses! You will be sorely missed! You won place in our hearts and will remain there forever!
