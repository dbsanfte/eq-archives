June 15, 2005 - Past few days...

Raid News:: <Lords of Prophecy> raid force continued it's work. Our current focus slightly shifted. We are still working on gearing up and flagging people with EP Gods drops; however, two new areas have gained our interest as well - OoW raid mobs and 69-70 runes farming. With our strong raid team we were able to achieve good results in those areas as well. In the past few days just from the raids we gained over 20 Greater and half a dozen or more Glowing runes, bunch of drops and added 2 new mobs to our farm status - Battlemaster Rhorious and Lirah the Bridgekeeper. Last kill of the Battlemaster was especially nice achievement. By the time we made our way to the Battlemaster room, Darkblood attempt on him already failed, so we had Battlemaster and all 5 adds already triggered. This presented certain challenge, because we still needed to setup and drag and rez few people who lost their inviso on the way. Additionally, BM and all adds had shared agroe on our kitters, which caused us nearly a wipe with our first try to peel. However, we managed to quickly recover and soon enough all 5 adds, and following them Battlemaster himself were dead. In one day we killed arrowers script, Lirah, and Battlemaster.

May 23, 2005 - Planes & Gods::

Elemental Planes:: <Lords of Prophecy> spent last couple weeks clearing planes of Fire and Air and Earth. We killed several times Fennin Ro, twice Mystical Arbitor (to gain access to PoEb), cleared all nameds in PoE(b) twice and took a shot at Coirnav and Rathe Coucil twice. With Coirnav we feel positive that we can manage this encounter next time we will face it. Rathe Council on the other hand we were stopped twice at having cave +2 under control. Our big problem at this time is shortage in the mez control department; therefore, we have opened recruitment for Enchanter and bard class.

Recruitment News:: If you are an active and capable player, who loves to raid and is confident dealing with big mobs, belong to enchanter or bard classes, and are L66+ - APPLY TODAY! We do not care if you have any flags at all, as long as you know how to play your character well!
