Applicant "To Do" List

If you decided to apply to <Lords of Prophecy> here is what you need to do:

- Register on our message board and send PM to one of the administrators: Pauliana or Flaor with your user name and character name.
Note: Your user name should begin from your EQ character name. You are allowed to add numbers at the end if you wish to do so.

- Once you get e-mail message, that your account has been activated, please go to the message board and join applicants group of users. This will allow you to obtain appropriate user permissions.

- Once and if your application was voted on, you will be contacted by a recruitment officer. This person will inform you whether you passed vote or failed to do so. If you passed you will enter "pledge period" and have to follow instructions bellow.
Pledge's "To Do" List

- Pledge period starts from tagging your character with <Lords of Prophecy>. If you were informed by one of the recruitment officers that you are accepted for "pledge period", you can ask to be tagged right then. However, if you can not meet with recruitment officer right away, you can always do so at the later time. Simply contact any of our officers to obtain your tag.
note: Remember - it is your responsibility to obtain tag for your character as soon as possible.

- Once you have your tag, go to message board and join "Pledges" group of users. Upon administrator approval you will gain wider access to the private areas of the board.

- At this time you need to register your RP (raid points) account. Follow the link called "Lords RP system" on the menu to get there. On the Lords RP system page find the link "Register" on the top and click on it to enter the registration process.
note: As pledge you can earn same amount of raid points as active members during the raids. However, it is your responsibility to register, before you can do that.

- Pledge period is set for 2 weeks at this time. Try to participate as many activities of the guild as you can, make friends, get to know people in the guild. Remember you need to get them to vote for you to become a full member.
New member's "To Do" List

  Once officer contacted you and announced in the guild the end of the pledge vote and congratulated you on being accepted as full member, it is time for you to do some more work.

- Go to message board and join group "Lords members". This will give you full access to the private areas of the board. You will find areas where you have to submit information about your flags, keys, magelo number. Those are must do entries. Do it. Also you will find areas where you can request assistance from other members or from leaders to help you with your tasks, spoilers for some good quests, invitations for off-hours events, groups, etc. Area "Time to Raid" will tell you if there is anything planned or already in progress in the game. Check message board often to keep up with current happenings in the guild.

note: Now that you are a full member of the guild you are allowed to vote on applicants and pledges. If you have reasons to vote "no" remember it is your responsibility to do it. Once poll is closed no one will re-open it for you to complaint if you failed to do that on time. Once applicant or pledge has been accepted any hostilities towards that person within the guild will cease.
