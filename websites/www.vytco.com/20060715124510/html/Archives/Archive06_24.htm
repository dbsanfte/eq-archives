<Lords of Prophecy> is an EverQuest® guild on the Bristlebane (formerly - Solusek Ro) server.

June 12, 2006:

The newly rebuilt LoP marked its official return to The Mastery of Corruption Trial and once again made Time a farming zone. Early in the week we reached a new milestone as we mashed through Time in one night.

We did a few epic fights as well and gratz to those who got a fight completed!!

 Whitesoul, Gromey, Ldaryy, Duggan, and Talain !!

 

If you are a member don't forget to get your Magelo updated so that it doesn't affect the Tier at which you loot.

If you are a druid, cleric or wizard looking for a great guild that is actively progressing through Everquest with a perfect blend of raiding and grouping fun, please click the application link!
