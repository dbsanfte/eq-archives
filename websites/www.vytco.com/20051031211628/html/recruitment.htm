 Lords of Prophecy. Recruitment information.

Recruitment status:: OPEN

<Lords of Prophecy> is a raiding guild. Accordingly your character must be developed to a certain level and have some flags and AAs. <Lords> is not a guild for everyone. We raid 5-6 times a week. Our normal schedule is::

Monday - individual target mobs
Tue/Wed - off
Thursday - GoD/DoN/OoW progression
Friday - GoD/DoN/OoW progression
Saturday - Time
Sunday - Bonus mobs (usually challenging targets where success pays extra raid points)

Raids start at 5-6pm PST and continue until 10-11pm PST

Following is the list of requirements your character must meet to be considered for recruitment in <Lords>

For all classes:

Must have all expansions

Must have minimum of 200AAs unless table below specifies different amount
