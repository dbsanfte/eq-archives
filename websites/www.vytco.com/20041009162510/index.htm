Welcome visitor,

<Lords of Prophecy> is an EverQuest® guild on the Solusek Ro server.

Started August 12, 2004 by a few friends who had this idea of a guild that raids without stepping over the corpses of their own kinsmen. We decided to make principle "no one is left behind" our main goal. We don't loot mobs till everyone is rezzed, we help those who are missing flags, we do our best to make sure our guys are happy. Basically, simple idea - guild for members, not from members.

August 14. 2004 we opened our recruitment and invited some of our friends we knew from joint hunts and LDoN groups.

August 21, 2004 we had our first mini-raid as a guild and had fun in Inner Acrylia.

September 6th, 2004 on Labor Day we had a little celebration by killing our first PoP goddess - Terris Thule with 35 members present.

September 20st, 2004 Foramorm became first shaman, to our knowledge,  on Solusek Ro to complete shaman epic quest 1.5 and acquire:

Congratulations Foramorm !

September 26, 2004 Carprin cycle was complete with only 28 members present. Flawless execution.

October 09, 2004. Aerin'Dar killed with 57 people by the join raid of Jaggedpine Defenders and Lords.

Today, we are building this guild with the intention to create a nice, friendly environment for players who login during US times on Solusek Ro, players who want to enjoy challenging encounters, with a friendly non-confrontational atmosphere at the same time.

We use a Raid Point system to distribute drops during our raids. The link to this system you will find on the left.

Our guild is oriented mainly towards experienced players with well developed characters. At the present time, your character must be level 65 and you must demonstrate the ability to operate this character efficiently in both group and raid environments before you can join our ranks. A well played character, however, is not our only requirement. You must be able to co-exist with other players peacefully, without creating any "drama". More about us, our policies, and recruitment procedures you can find on our forum page. If you have any questions that we failed to answer there, feel free to contact us in game or via the message board.

Our raiding times are mainly between 6:00 PM pst and midnight pst.

This website is still undergoing some construction. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. We assure you, we are working as much as our schedules permit to complete this process as soon as possible.
