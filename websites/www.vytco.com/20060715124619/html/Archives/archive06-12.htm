<Lords of Prophecy> is an EverQuest® guild on the Bristlebane (formerly - Solusek Ro) server.

June 3, 2006:

Welcome Back to QVIC LoP! May 31st marked the return of <Lords of Prophecy> to Qvic. The new raid team did one incredible job leading and the members with quite a few still pledges and others new to the guild were impressive in their determination to follow this through. After a brief wipe in the gas chamber Lords prevailed against the odds and slaughtered Barxt on the first attempt!

Just a couple of days after downing Barxt all headed to Ikkinz4 again to smash The Keeper of the Altar. The results were once again impressive.

LoP is doing a fantastic job upgrading the gear and flags of the newest members and the raid team is working hard to get LoP back to where it was before re-building began. We now have a better balance of classes and a team that seems to work incredibly well together. We are still looking for a few good druids, clerics and wizards.

If you are a druid, cleric or wizard looking for a great guild that is actively progressing through Everquest with a perfect blend of raiding and grouping fun, please click the application link!
