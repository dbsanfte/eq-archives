 Welcome visitor ,
<Lords of Prophecy> is an EverQuest® guild on the Bristlebane (formerly - Solusek Ro) server.

Tuesday November 28th, 2006:

COMPLETED

Since the release of The Serpents Spine LOP has been brutally attacking the grind again. Around half of LoPs hardcore raiders are level 75 now. Thanks to those who grinded hard to gain those hit points, mana and spells that helped make it so much easier to get to where we are today. A lot have reverted to spending half their game time LFG, but in the end its well worth it, hell, we even have the time to give each other RL pointers from time to time.

<Lords of Prophecy> are storming through the Mpg Trials these days with four trials now under our belts. We have turned Rikki, Kess and Yar lir into farming mobs and are ready to break on into Anguish.

 

October 7th, 2006:

On Sunday October 1st we took a trip to see a new mob. Cordagon Boulderfist met his first death on the Bristlebane server at the hands of

<Lords of Prophecy>

Friday October 6th we celebrated our first

DON NIGHT SLAUGHTER-FEST 2006!!!

After a quick re-pounding of Rikki we moved on to Yar Lir's dwelling and quickly made mincemeat of her tough 'lil hide. We still had not had enough so we went and smashed Kessdonna into the frosty ground. Two more first kills for LoP. Filled with bloodlust we ran quickly to the nest and offed T'Shara just for kicks-n-giggles.

   Gratz to all winners!!!

 

If you are looking for a great fun bunch of people who raid and actively progress through EQ without forgetting to stop and have fun along the way then please take the time to look over our requirements and send a tell to  Soulhavoc  with any questions regarding your interest in LoP.
