 Welcome visitor ,
<Lords of Prophecy> is an EverQuest® guild on the Bristlebane (formerly - Solusek Ro) server.

May 23, 2006::

True story about re-building:: It is not a secret that <Lords of Prophecy> recently (month or so ago) lost quite a few members that went long route from Plane of Time to Tacvi. Some of these losses occurred due to retirement of people from EQ, some because people felt need for change of scenery... To name a few: Striding, Krushor, Somlamda, Madoxx, Thwaka, Dluim, Stubar, Ysabell, Botandoro, Humda, Jrantix and several other good old friends moved on... For the record, let me say that there are no bad feelings left behind... With few exceptions (and mostly it's related with people who haven't even being in LoP that long), people who left LoP are dear friends and losing them was painful on both sides. It was, however, time for new generation of players to pick up the leadership flag and carry on.

Surely, such a loss affected Lords ability to raid and continue normally operate daily. Lords were hurt badly, and would it be any other guild from my past experience, result would be degradation to the "family" guild status or simple disbandment. Fortunately, LoP stands for more than just a guild for many of us...  There were still many old timers left who believe in idea that stands behind the tag. And since I mentioned names of those who left, it is only fair to remember here names of those who went out of their way to organize a transition team, and re-build this guild. In no particular order:: Rinrek, Dovi, Dethlord, Bawng, Crillix, Encien, Gnomeater, Danpro, Girthan, Greakgod, Atheka, Mizty, Proceed, Growlin, Xpoc, Zephros, Carraamon, Tasmere, Osser, and several others stayed and made sure that Lords can re-build and survive in such hard times. Transition team was formed and new elections of leaders setup.

At one point LoP couldn't continue backflagging in Time and situation looked really bad. Raids had to be canceled and we did not have enough to even attempt P1 in Time, much less to carry all the way to P3 in order to re-flag new pledges. We had to ask old friends to come and help us form the valid raid. And friends came and helped. Special thank you goes to Yatreny, Regon, Sharedlen, Jdor and many friends from Wolfsclan and Magna Charta who came and helped!

We opened recruitment and started re-flag process from PoTime backflagging. Today it is safe to say Time backflagging is complete and flags and items are rotting in Time once again. Thank you everyone who helped!

Meanwhile, we had our elections and New Leadership was elected. It was amazing to see new and old generation players working together to re-build this guild's ability to raid and function on daily basis. Atmosphere in the guild is even nicer than it was before. Spirit of collaboration and teamwork is stronger than ever.    

During this transition period we revamped few rules. Now days there are no tiers in usage of rps - we started treating rps as true currency. Once earned, it can be spend freely at any given moment. LoP still remains a "private club" though and pledges have to wait till full members are done bidding, but, I guess, this is normal. Our level requirement was reduced to Level 67 and 50 AAs, which is still true for selected classes. However, our roster is now stands at 170+ members and we steadily see low 50s high 40s attendance during raids. With these numbers, not only we firmly clearing Plane of Time, but also defeated most of our normal targets and doing epics. 

Overall, it is safe to say that <Lords of Prophecy> successfully completed re-building and transition team is now off the stage to let newly elected leaders to do their job!

Raid News:: Today marked a pinnacle point in LoP career tonight we beat Ikkinz 4 for the first time with the 'new' LoP. Grats LoP! Round 1 of Qvic backflaggin is well underway. we now have 24 newly Ikkinz4 flagged guild members.
