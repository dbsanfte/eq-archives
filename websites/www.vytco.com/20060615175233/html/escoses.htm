 In memory of Escoses (Scot A. Goodridge)

<Lords of Prophecy> salutes you Escoses. You were dear friend, good guildmate and sweet person! We love you Escoses! You will be sorely missed! You won place in our hearts and will remain there forever!

Friday, March 04, 2005 - 10:34:46 PM
Accident on Chickasaw Turnpike Turns Deadly
 


One Texoma man is dead following a fatal wreck on the Chickasaw turnpike this afternoon.

Scott Goodridge of Ada, Oklahoma was killed just after two this afternoon. Investigators say the 31-year-old was pulling onto the turnpike just north of Sulpher when he tried to make a u-turn.

He pulled in front of an 18-wheeler, and was struck. The driver of the 18-wheeler was not injured.
