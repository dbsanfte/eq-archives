:: News archives ::   


July 23, 2003

 Plane of Hate, hunt started just because we were bored... Results::

Keryen was second priest (after Xerxe) to get Gem of Faith and Huje was second caster (Shaikhy 1st) to get Gem of Sorcery. Many pieces went to the gems fund (we didn't have race/class people to use those), but few found nice spots in the inventories of the new owners.

Grats Rubix on Apothic Robe and Apothic Crown; Huje on his epic piece and FBCR (Rubix gracefully donated old robe and luck pointed on Huje); Relseth <WoE> got Indicolite BP and Boots and Oynx <WoE> walked away with Etherial Myst BP. Some other items dropped as well but i was little distracted to keep records.

 As usual, work well done!


July 19, 2003

 Plane of Hate::
New plane of Hate is entirely different. We had to learn this zone all over again. Naediana did great job pulling some information together and organizing our first tour in the new Hate. Of course, in good tradition of ours before we started Agonar got a new leggings.

 Initial drops were not very promising until we figured out which mobs to pull and which mobs drop what armor. After that everything went very well. Guest from WoE Romba left with 2 iksar warrior drops, Taman got arms piece, Magaen got Staff of Elemental Mastery: Fire, Shaikhy and Timid got their epic pieces:: Essence of Vampire for Shaikhy and Book of Souls for Timid, Noodel grabbed himself a bracer, Timid - helm. Several quest pieces for nice range slot items dropped as well. 3shammy armor drops went to floor.

 Naediana and Xasis gave us a small tour around and without any major problems and few mini-bosses later we left the Hate. Special thank you to Arminius who was patient and worked hard to get people ported up. Too bad several guys had connectivity issue. Overall :: Great work all!

Figura
