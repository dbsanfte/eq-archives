 New Member's "TO DO" List.
Important ! Remember that there are 2 separate databases you need to register for 1) Forums and 2) Lords RP system

Every new member of Lords must complete following steps::

STEP 1. To create account in Lords RP database Click Here
this allows you to get raid points when u come to the raids, track your flags and attendance to guild events.

STEP 2. To register account for message board Click Here
this is a separate registration from Raid Point system, you need to complete this to receive access to the private section of the message boards.

STEP 3. (After your accounts are activated)
To create your characters on the roster Click Here **
you need to complete step above to be able to do this.

STEP 4. (After your accounts are activated)
Update your flags status.  To do this go to the Lords RP database and click on Settings (edit your flags here). Choose your character and put checkmarks against every flag you have in the list.

** - you must have active account with Lords RP system. If you play 2 characters you need to add both characters (profiles) through the same account.

REMEMBER:: Your user name must be the same as your character's name in EQ.
Otherwise your registration request will be denied!
