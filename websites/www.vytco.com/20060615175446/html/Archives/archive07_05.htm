July 30, 2005 - LEEROY strikes back...

Raid News:: There is no other way to describe what transpired tonight in Plane of Time during the Rallos Zek kill. Everything was going on as planned, raid was rebuffing and getting ready after clearing P1 and quickly mauling through CT and Bert... When we witnessed something quite unusual for our raids ... Lkan without any warning charged Rallos Zek and by this confused the living hell out of several people who followed him... Sekai and RInrek, tried to save the situation, and led the rest of the raid in unprepared charge. Unfortunatelly, most of the healers were out of mana or close to that from the get go and we had to pull back with Rallos Zek standing at healthy 40-some pct.

Explanation was due ... and we got it from Lkan ...

Other than this interesting occurrence, we continued on and killed as planned Rallos Zek, Innorruuk, and Quarm.

July 21, 2005 - Epics, epics, and more epics...

Raid News:: Basically, we caught up on few postponed epic fights. Epic 1.5 were complete for Yellowcard, Yagher, Carraamon, Lkan, Mousebane, Somlamda, Trawler. Several people advanced to their last fights, which are scheduled and will be done nearest future.
Also worth mentioning Ikkinz raid trials 1 and 2 were complete with more to come... Remainder of this week we have scheduled for Time (Friday and Saturday).

Recruitment News:: We are back to regular recruitment practice. Requirements are posted on the Recruitment page. As of August 1st, 2006 Council approved increase of minimum required level 68. Current guild members who are behind this requirement need to work on catching up to it. For several classes our need downgraded to VERY LOW. These classes include: beastlords, druids, necromancers, paladins, rangers, shadowknights, and warriors. Most wanted classes at this time are: bards, enchanters, mages, rogues, and wizards.
