September 26, 2005 - HIRING CLERICS! Ikkinz 4 almost - only couple more runs.

Recruitment News:: <Lords of Prophecy> closing recruitment for most classes. Our roster is nearly complete, we have people waiting to get in GoD raids. Time raids steadily have high 60 attendance. In this situation we will be closing recruitment for most classes. Only class that we continue activelly hire people in is clerics. In fact we need 3-4 clerics with high play time. We don't care about your flags, gear or AAs - this all can come as you group with us. What we do care about - is your talent in playing cleric, your skill level and your attendance. If you are good at playing cleric, play a lot, and want to raid in Time and beyond - send us your application.

We will consider candidates L65 and higher.

Please understand that as much as we would like to give everyone a chance to prove themselves, only 3 or 4 best candidates will be pledged. While your performance will be measured against tough challenging environment which we are raiding in, rewards for those who succeed will be adequate to it. Don't worry about your mana-pools, or lack of gear, hp, resists - show us that you can play your cleric like a champ - we will take care of the rest!

Raid News:: With another 33 flags we will do one more, maybe two, runs to Ikkinz 4 and will be done with it. Don't miss these!
