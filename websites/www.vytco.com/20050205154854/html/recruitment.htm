 Lords of Prophecy. Recruitment information.

Recruitment status:: OPEN

We are planning to add approximately 20-30 characters to our roster. We will do 1 full round of backflags once we stop recruitment on Tuesday, February 08, 2005. If you are active player, love to raid and play often - APPLY ! Don't miss your chance. We are not going to re-flag for some time after this upcoming round.

<Lords of Prophecy> is a raiding guild. Accordingly your character must be developed to a certain level and have some flags and AAs. <Lords> is not a guild for everyone. We raid 5-6 times a week. Our normal schedule is::

Monday and Tusday - personal (FFA days),
Wednesday through Sunday - raids start at 5-6pm PST and continue until 10-11pm PST

Following is the list of requirements your character must meet to be considered for recruitment in <Lords>

For all classes:

Must have all expansions

Must have following flags complete:
PoJustice (any 1 trial), PoStorms (Askr quest complete), Hedge quest, Carprin

Must have minimum of 50AAs unless table below specifies higher amount
