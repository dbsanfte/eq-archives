The History of Knights of Velious

Below is a short history of the Knights of Velious with some of the pictures removed from the Tavern during the move.

All facts a fictional, so bear with me.

The beginning

The Guild and the Dream of the Knights of Velious was formed on a cloudy day in the earliest of Norraths time. Starlancer put together a small band of people sharing a dream, and started what since has become known as the happiest and most kindhearted people in all of Norrath. The beginning was easy, ideals where shared and the goals accomplished. Starlancer is no more among us, but his dream lives on. It was during these time that the original doctrine was written. The doctrine that today is still our Charter, and our Guideline.

Awaking world

The times was hard back then. Most of Norrath still undiscovered and travelling was dangerous. The Knights swore to keep the lands safe, and, more important, to help each other survive in a World full of evil forces. The Knights would be the shinning light that guided all others towards a better and safer world.

Passing the Crown

As the World grew older, Starlancer grow old with it and decided to resign. He passed on the crown to (who?), who took it with the fire that always distinguishes a true Knight from the common crowd. Under his ruling, the Knights grew stronger and gained recognition in the world.

The Tavern

The Tavern has always been where the Knights met to discuss the future and brag about the past. It has changed over the years, but always been a home for the Knights to rest and regain strength before continuing on our Quests.

The first Tavern was a simple house. Made to meet and share stories. Many a night, people has told of their victories and defeats. It was also in the Tavern that plans were being made for the next daring attempt on a dungeon or evil nest of monsters that needed to be forced back into hidding. The old Tavern served well many years, but eventually it grew too small and the Knights build a new house for the Guild.
The new Tavern was much larger and held room for everyone, a better kitchen, common room with space enough for the entire guild to meet and stables, rooms etc. The new Tavern also had a permanent Host, Grumm, who kept peace and served the best brew known to all of Norrath.

Nobility and friendship

Many people have met and made friends through the Knights of Velious. Some has even committed to love in Marriage. Other has gone on to Nobility, becoming the Lords and Ladies of our lands. All of these are listed below.

Lady Zarria and Lord Humakt Truesword

Lady Zarria gave her heart to Lord Humakt Truesword on a bright sunny day in the fall in Lake Rathe. They met during a fight against the viscious Orcs in High Hold and has since then travelled to almost every corner of Norrath. Today they live with their cat Yinkinh in House Truesword, a castle filled with joy, laugther and riches donated to their new cause - helping the next generation of warriors along to fight the Evil that still persists.
