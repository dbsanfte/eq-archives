Pasketti's INI File Editor

It was with great trepidation that I was summoned into the presence of King Ak'Anon. What could he want with me? Was he angry about that spider trap I had been experimenting with? No, that couldn't be it. The damage has been cleaned up, and my assistant's wounds are healing nicely. Was it the exploding fishing bait I was testing in the lake? Maybe. The waves it generated did inundate parts of the city, but only temporarily, and they did wash away a bit of grime. Perhaps I should use a smaller amount of Firewater...

As I entered the throne room, I paused to admire perhaps the greatest achievement of Gnomish Tinkerers - King Ak'Anon himself! By constructing him, we freed ourselves from the day-to-day drudgery of running the kingdom, and can now devote our attention to more important matters, like that exploding fishing bait. Maybe it isn't the Firewater, maybe it's the...

"BZZZT-TIK! Pasketti! CLIK! I have summoned you here because I believe you may be able to help. WHIRRRRR!"

Oh yes! I forgot where I was...

"How may I help, Sire?"

"PING! It seems that many throughout the land are having problems editing their INI files to correctly set text colors. CLANK!"

"Sire? I don't understand. It's simplicity itself. Set User_15_Red to 0, User_15_Green to 0, and User_15_Blue to 128 to make your skill messages come out in a pleasing dark blue color."

"PTING! Yes, yes, to us it is. WHIRRR-CLICK! But to those that haven't been blessed with keen Gnomish minds adept at tinkering, it can be confusing. TICK! Therefore I task you, Pasketti! BZZZZZT! Create a way to allow everyone to configure their text colors! TICK-PTING-WHIRRR!"

"Yes, Sire! I'll get right on it!"

I returned to my workshop posthaste, and have crafted this program. It's so simple even an Ogre can use it.
