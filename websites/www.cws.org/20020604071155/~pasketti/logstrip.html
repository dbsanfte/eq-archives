Pasketti's EQ Log Stripper

This program will read your Everquest log files, and strip out some of the extraneous garbage, making them smaller.

It can also save the stripped lines to another file. This is handy for keeping records of conversations, etc. You can do this while leaving the original log untouched if you choose.
