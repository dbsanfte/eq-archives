Strategy

This strategy takes two Necromancers with pets.  It is a great way to practice working with another Necro, avoiding stacking.

 

Fully buff self and pets.  Set pets on hill right next to the west zone wall.  As you look down coast to huts and pier, you will see one large tree.  One Necro stands here.  The other goes in to pull Lars.  Lars is normally at the pier.  Be careful!  Ulrich is nearby.  Pull Lars with Darkness and swim/sprint back to pets.  Second Necro at the tree casts Heart Flutter as soon as Lars comes into range (or Darkness if the first attempt was resisted), then also turns for pets.  Then both necros load on DoTs, Lifedraws, and Fear (which won't last long) until Lars is dead.

 

The camp is good once teamwork works.  It is really important to make sure both pets tank Lars and that Necros cover each other.  Avoid melee at all costs.  If one is being chased, other Fears and Draws.

 

You need to keep an eye out for Ulrich.  It may be possible for Lars to Fear onto him.  If Ulrich joins you need to feign immediately.  Don't worry, you will be coming back for him later :)
Faction Standing

Lowers faction standing with Steel Warriors, Wolves of the North, Shamen of Justice, and Halas Merchants. Raises faction standing with Rogues of the White Rose.

Additional Comments

Warblek (Innoruuk)

Lars is great for experience up to level 23 but tapers off after that.  The loot is average but the experience more than makes up for it and he is very soloable.  I also never found him camped, much to my dismay.  The trick to Lars is keep him in darkness and chasing you. He can kill a L20 pet fast and you as well. Keep one step ahead of him and your pet and dots will do their work.  Fear is risky if Ulrich is around because he has a huge agro range and will kill you very fast.  I cast Clinging Darkness almost every other spell at L20 and 21 just to make sure he stayed that way. L22 gets easier but the exp is still great.  Fill in with Juhn if you are full health/mana and Lars is not back yet.
