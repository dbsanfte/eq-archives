The City of Freeport.  Home to the Humans or the occasional Half-Elf.

Normal humans. The standard race by which all others are judged.

Pro's: Only race that can be monks, levels faster than any other race, can be almost any class

Con's: No night vision, no high starting stats

Available Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Enchanter, Magician, Monk, Necromancer, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Shadow Knight, Warrior, Wizard
