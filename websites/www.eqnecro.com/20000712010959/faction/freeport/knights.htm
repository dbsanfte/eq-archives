Where: Freeport
Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? Tumpy Tonics.  In the seafarers roost, give Groflah Steadirt the tonics in sets of 4.  It takes about 1 bag or so of the tonics to max out all but one of the Tradesfolk factions, the merchants I think.  Even as a Dark Elf, I was able to get the Knights of Truth to love me in about 30 minutes.  Hardest part is getting the tonics, which require you to buy Kiola Nuts on the Sisters Island.  A brewer can make it or Tumpy Irontoe can.  It took 3 bags to fill up a bag with tonics.  Note: You can give the tonics to Groflah as a Dark Elf, but you cannot buy the nuts as a Dark Elf so someone else will have to do that part for you.  Anyway, good quest for faction if you can get the tonics.  The bag of tonics cost 30pp plus though, much for the person to do it for you.

Faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing

Other faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing with Coalition of Tradesfolk I and Coalition of Tradesfolk III

Where: Freeport underground
Who should I kill? -
What quest should I complete? As a low-level gnome necro, I found that Dismal Rage did not trust me.  Finally, I lucked into discovering how to improve my faction.  There is a famale NPC in the first room you hit when you enter from the outside gate entrance who can train necros. She is the one who asks for 4 fire beetle eyes, 4 bone chips, or 4 spiderling silks. Giving her 4 at a bones at a time-unstacked, of course - will give exp and faction increase with Dismal Rage. If I gave her eyes or silks she told me to get bent. Go figure. :)  Once you give her several rounds of bones (I gave 20) I became amiable to them.
Faction standing modifiers: Lowers faction standing
Other faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing with Opal DarkBriar and Dismal Rage
