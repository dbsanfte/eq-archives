The City of Erudin.  Home to the Erudites.

The final variant of the Human race. Being the exact opposite of the Barbarians, the High Man prides himself on his intellectual abilities and social graces, to the almost total exclusion of the physical disciplines.

Pro's: Highest starting Intelligence in the game

Con's: No night vision, low strength, you are not very welcome in your home town and pitiful hunting grounds around your city

Available Classes: Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Paladin, and Wizard
