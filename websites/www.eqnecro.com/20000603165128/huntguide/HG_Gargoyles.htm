Strategy

This is a great spot for those necros nearing level 20.  It is relatively safe, provides decent SP and very good loot.  Best way to get to this spot is to take the boat from  Freeport and just after you zone, the boat will stop at a dock.  This is Sister Island.  It is important that you do not get of at the dock as the NPCs near the dock will KoS most Necros, especially if you are a Dark Elf.  Just jump off the boat at the opposite side of the dock and swim out toward the island just to the right of the island directly opposite Sister Island.  It looks like a big gray rock with little growing on it.

 

Once to the island, find a safe spot near the shoreline to set-up camp.  DO NOT go anywhere near the center of the island as there are three Spectres there that will destroy you in seconds.  If you leave them alone they will leave you alone.  In order to get gargoyles to spawn, you have to kill any greater skeletons in the area to induce the spawn.  The gargoyles will spawn at random spots on the island so keep your eyes out. (Please note that they can only be hit by magical weapons.)

 

A good strategy is to use Heat blood, an occasional Lifetap spell and keeping your pet healed with Mend Bones should be all you need.  The loot dropped is an occasional Gargoyle Eye that can be sold for as much as 9pp with good charisma.  Even the Greater Skellies drop as much as 4gp per kill so this is a nice bonus when waiting for the Gargoyles to spawn.  Avoid using Fear as you could end up chasing a Gargoyle right into the Spectres.  That would be bad....

 

Happy hunting....

Marconitus (Innoruuk)

I have used every different spell combo and have not been able to kill a level 19-20 yellow con gargoyle at level 18.  Even with a level 16 pet buffed with Spirit Armor, Mend Bones (which is too slow to use imho), kiting and using Fear they just won't go down.  Now I am not saying it is impossible but it must be very difficult.  Level 18 white/black con gargoyles are no problem, simply Engulf, pet attack, Heart Flutter but don't Heat Blood for about a minute or he will frenzy on you, with a couple of Lifedraws it should be over.

Boeing (Tallon Zek)

Don't worry about the Gargoyles going frenzy on you.  Use that to your advantage. I usually pull with darkness and lay on all my dots this really makes them mad.  Then I have my pet attack.  Then I just run around the island or in wide circles as the dots and my pet eat away at the Gargoyle.  Make sure to ALWAYS have Darkness on the Gargoyle!  They fly fast so unless you have sow you must slow them down.  If you want you can have your pet not taunt during the fight, but I want my pet to taunt because if darkness wears off and I can't recast cause the Gargoyle is interrupting or resisting, I want him to start to aggro my pet so I can recast.  If the Gargoyle turns on your pet just fear it and life tap away. Don't worry about the Spectres.  If they see your pet and a garg fighting the  won't jump in. As long as you don't attack them or stand near them very long they will leave you alone.  If another Gargoyle jumps in and the pet is hurting a lot, I'd feign or gate.  If the Gargoyle didn't resist much and everything went smoothly I would finish the fight with full health, half mana, and a pet with 90% health.  I started taking these guys out easily at level 17, even level 20 Gargoyles fell easily.  Good luck and if you see me on Tallon Zek killing these guys please don't jump in.  I have these guys well under control.

 

P.S. Another fun thing to do is to watch the Gargoyles and the Greater Skeletone fight each other.  The Gargoyle will win and when he is finished has around 75% of his hit points.  DO NOT AND I MEAN DO NOT PULL THE GARGOYLE WHEN IT IS STILL FIGHTING THE SKELETON! The Gargoyle hates you more then it hates other NPCs and it will aggro you no matter how much less damage you give it then the Skeleton does.  In the end you will waist mana and health and the Greater Skeleton will take the XP!

 

I hope this helps. Good luck!

Ulaa

I headed to gargoyle isle level 22. I had tried hunting all of the usual suspects without much luck. I got to gargoyle isle (before I read this hunting guide) and started hunting.  Set pet cast Darkness, Heat Blood, Heart Flutter and Leech. Once all of the DOTS are on start to Lifedrain as these guys will happily take out a level 20 pet if left alone even with DOTS on. Usually when  they reach about 50% health they will aggro on you, so simply start to run around the outside of the island. Stay away from other Gargoyles while running but ignore the skellies and the Spectres as they will not bother you. About 1 in 4 GGs will drop 1-2 Gargoyle eyes. These are worth 8p8g and they are stackable so if you bring lots of food and stay for a while this can be a VERY profitable spot. I stayed here from 22 to 24 and got two levels in about 8 hours played. After 24 the XP is not worth it, but I walked away with 50 gargoyle eyes, a nice haul for what is essentially an easy camp at level 22+.

Hauteur (Rallos Zek)

I’m level 18 and I have been hunting Gargoyles for quite some time now and I have developed the following strategy.

 

First, only kill 2 greater skeletons so that you are not spawn a heap of gargoyles. Next, since there is normally someone always hunting the spectres, see if you can grab a bronze scythe off one of the corpses. Now if you are level 18 you want a pet the cons dangerous. Give your pet the scythe and a shield from one of the greater skeletons.

 

Okay, now for the fight. Open with Heart Flutter and then send the pet in. Follow up straight away with Leach and siphon strength. Now wait a little while (about 5 sec) and then cast Heat Blood. Now you have to do some dirty work..... jump in and save your pet. Whilst fighting be always casting lifetap until the fight is over. I have kill level 20 gargoyles this way and even when my pet has died I have still taken out the gargoyle. Don’t worry about darkness and fear as it is not the area for these spells to be effective. By the way, take plenty of food and bone chips. Also try not to die on the boat at sister island or it will be a long wait for a GM to get your corpse.
