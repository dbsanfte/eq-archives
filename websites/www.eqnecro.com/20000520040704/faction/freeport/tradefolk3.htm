Where: Freeport
Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? Tumpy Tonics.  In the seafarers roost, give Groflah Steadirt the tonics in sets of 4.  It takes about 1 bag or so of the tonics to max out all but one of the Tradesfolk factions, the merchants I think.  Even as a Dark Elf, I was able to get the Knights of Truth to love me in about 30 minutes.  Hardest part is getting the tonics, which require you to buy Kiola Nuts on the Sisters Island.  A brewer can make it or Tumpy Irontoe can.  It took 3 bags to fill up a bag with tonics.  Note: You can give the tonics to Groflah as a Dark Elf, but you cannot buy the nuts as a Dark Elf so someone else will have to do that part for you.  Anyway, good quest for faction if you can get the tonics.  The bag of tonics cost 30pp plus though, much for the person to do it for you.

Faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing

Other faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing with Coalition of Tradesfolk I and Knights of Truth
