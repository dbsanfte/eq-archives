Mob

Ice Boned Skeletons

Mob Level

12 - 14

Location

Underwater cave

Spells Used

Heat Blood, Numb the dead, Grasping roots, Endure Cold, Ward Undead

Strategy

Look around in the cave until you find a 2 spawn of Ice Boned Skeletons (shouldn't take long).  Go down the hall and bind yourself cause you are going to die.  That is the sad but very true reality.

 

When you are done, summon your level 12 pet and make sure it is level 11.  Now cast Endure Cold on both of you.

 

Now it gets dangerous.  Find the weakest one and cast Numb the Dead.  The other will charge you. Immediately cast Heat Blood and send in pet.  Now step in to take some hits so Heat Blood can start working.  Now step back and Ward Ward Ward until dead.  Now the skeleton that was Numbed should have come after you or stayed there.  Try to work in an extra Numb the dead while you are fighting.  When he comes at you, skip Heat Blood and just Ward Undead until it comes after you.  This is very dangerous but it is the only way.  You are probably going to die if anything goes wrong, but that is ok.  Just work on at least killing one so when you come back, you will only have one to deal with.

 

Now the spawn is very fast and the XP is very good, but it is very dangerous.  I started hunting at 16 and it was easy, and the XP was 1 1/2 bubble per hour.  I can't imagine what it would be at 12 or 13.  Although the loot isn't good, the XP is great.  Hope you like it as much as I did =)
