The City of Kelethin.  Home to the Woodland Elves.

These Elves appear somewhat human at first glance, but their very sharp features and pointed ears set them apart. Elves are weaker than humans, but more than make up for this with their amazing agility and superior dexterity. They equal the High Man in their intelligence and wisdom, but are much more fair and charismatic.

Pro's: Very Agile, infravision, and the forage skill

Con's: Low strength and you will be a wood elf =)

Available Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger, Rogue, Warrior
