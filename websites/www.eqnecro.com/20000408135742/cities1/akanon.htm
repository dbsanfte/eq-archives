The City of Ak'anon.  Home to the Gnomes.

Gnomes spend most of their lives in their underground burrows and rarely come to the surface. They resemble Dwarves to some extent, but have a ruddy brown skin and are more wiry and gnarled. Their hair is white, and somewhat sparse.

Pro's: Infravision, High Starting Dexterity and Agility, High Starting Intelligence, and you get the Tinkering Skill, Better Faction Standing than other Necromancers

Con's: You will have very short legs

Available Classes: Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Rogue, Warrior, Wizard
