Mob

Centaur Archers

Mob Level

21 - 23

Location

All over the zone

Spells Used

Darkness, Heat Blood, Heart Flutter, Siphon Life, Shock of Poison, Allure of Death

Strategy

Cent Archers can be easily be fought after 24 with SOP, but careful use of your DOTs can make soloing these guys at 21, 23 a good fight, with good loot potential.

 

Strategy is pretty basic, just pull with darkness, send in pet. Re-apply dark if it resisted.  You need this one to stick, Cents are still pretty fast even when Dark'd (2 resists when yellow con and I'm hitting Gate).  Then depending on level, start DoTing... With SOP I usually just Heart Flutter after Dark for the stat decreases then SOP and and Siphon till dead ( with allure on).  Pre-SOP I'd usually stick it with Heat Blood for damage, then Siphon if low con, or Heart Flutter if tuff one then Siphon till dead...

 

Multi Dot's work usually pretty good on these guys, when they aggro on you they usually don't just run till they can hit you, they run a bit then shoot arrows (which only hit for bout 15-20 or so, which is tons better than the dual 40 or so they melee for), so when they pause to shoot just start casting your Damage spell...  If you time it right you can still get it off even if they hit you (right after the hit or miss, cast... then run :-) ).

 

Remember one thing though, if ANY other cents come near you, they will join in. Always make sure the pull area around you is clear, and a path to kite away to is clear as well.. Very important. Even though these guys can be harder than Aviak rooks, which are about the same level (mainly because they run faster than Aviaks when Darkness'd),.

 

The loot for these guys can make up for it. I've had 3pp or so drop, average bout 1pp per kill, plus they drop Bronze weapons sometimes, plus Med to Large Ringmail pieces.. not to mention 20ish words.  Plus they are just Great XP ,especially if hunting them when they are yellow :-)

 

Good Hunting!
