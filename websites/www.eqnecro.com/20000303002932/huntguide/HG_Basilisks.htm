Strategy

These are all over Rathe Mountains but I chose to sit by the Undead Towers just off to the left of the Feerrott zone since you rarely see a see anything wander that is tougher then an Orc.  And if it gets close you can get to the zone if needed.  I would bring up your Shielding spell just for extra magic resist first off.  I pulled with Engulfing Darkness and sent in the pet, it may come after you and you may get stunned by its stone breath but your pet will taunt it off you before it hits you more then 2 times.

 

From here on just keep a distance since and add your other DoTs and Lifetap if in needed since your pet is likely to get stunned a few times and it will be after you again but it seemed that I got hit rarely since as soon as I was stunned, my pet would have taunted it off again.  Do this until it dies.  Fear may work well on them too but I never used it until later levels so it is untested on my part.

 

I always meditated on the hill on the zone boundary since it is out of the path of anything big that occasionally wandered (Cyclopes and Hill Giants).  Lizardmen and Orcs wander through too that are more of a pest rather then a threat.

 

Loot is terrible.  All I remember is basilisk tails and tongues.  You get an occasional word.  I did however go from 16 - 19 in a couple days without dieing once.  At 16, 7 or 8 of them was a bubble of exp.  But it dried up a lot at 19.  I compare them a lot to the crocs in the oasis their main advantage is that only classes with pets have a chance against them at this level since they do not have a pet to take the stuns.  So it feels like having the Oasis to yourself with no cutthroat competition to get the pulls.
