The City of  Rivervale.  Home to the Halflings.
 
Halflings are stocky little people that most closely resemble humans, but they are extremely agile, dexterous, and very light of foot. 

Pro's: Infravision, Hiding Skill, and very agile

Con's: Big Feet, Short legs and you are a halfling

Available Classes: Bard, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Warrior
