The KGC is not an entity per se. Its merely an agreement between most of the guilds and alliances on the server to share a limited resource (about a dozen mobs) as equally among each other as possible.

This allows small guilds of only 10 or 12 people to have the same access to some mobs as guilds with 200+ people in it.

The reasons for keeping the rotations hidden to all but officers is many.

The easiest though is that it simply changes too often to be update in more than 1 area. On an average day it changes some 5-7 times. I've seen it change as many as 22 times in one day. That makes it impractical to show in more than 1 place as it will be out of date within a few hours at best.

As for keeping it secret. Well, on most servers these mobs are monopolized by 1 or 2 guilds. They tend not to make their kill times public either. This is no different than that. The only difference is that on Karana the mobs are being shared by everyone and not just a few.
