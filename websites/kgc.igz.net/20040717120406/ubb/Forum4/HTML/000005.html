- The FFA period starts 6 hours before the scheduled downtime.
- Only guilds who arent in the bracket (or UP as for Naggy, Vox and CT) can take a mob as an FFA kill and maintain their spot in the rotation.
- Bracketed guilds always have priority over FFA guilds if both are in the zone at the same time. The FFA guild must wait for the bracketed guild to concede in order to engage. There is no time limit on how long the bracketed guild has to kill the target.

- Bracketed guilds must still take a fail and move to the back of the rotation if they fail to kill a target, even if their attempt is during the FFA period.

- The first bracketed guild must still take a fail/skip if they do not engage the target if it spawned before 8pm local time for that guild.
