Did some testing after servers came up. Grouped with
a bard and necro in skyshrine courtyard for a few spawn
cycles. SK no longer taunt well with spells. One or two
casts of DC or SV before the patch and the mob was mine.
After patch, I sometimes had to cast DC 8 times to get a
mob off the necros pet.....

Spoke to a couple clerics on chat after servers came down.
A complete heal now frenzies the mob on you and noone can get
it off. Dragon raids are gonna be fun now, clerics heal and
die ;P

Oh boy, what to say. Lots of improvements and a HUGE
change in game mechanics. The nerfing of the taunt factor
of debuff spells indirectly nerfed SK's ability to taunt.
Priest classes now face frenzied mobs even after casting
only 1 CH or 2 Superior heals.

We will all have to relearn game mechanics or clerics will
die on every mob. Only way I could taunt well was using
engulfing darkness. Problem is, chain casting that
and you are OOM fast. Good luck to everyone with the new
changes and grats to those who got upgrades.

I, however, believe the couple nerfs affect the game much
more than all the improvements.
