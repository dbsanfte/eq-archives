the AoF is the avatar of fear in cazic thule zone. A mob name cazic cenobite spawns down there and drops cazic gate potion. You out it in an inventory slot. Lizzies in there still aggro though, so if you dont have FD its not the best place to port to when low on health. Still, bettter to die there than in fear.
