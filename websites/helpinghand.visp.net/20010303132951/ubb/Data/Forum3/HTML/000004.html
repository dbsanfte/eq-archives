We simply need more higher level characters. This became painfully clear after I talked with Ronak about that chanter epic mob (and he's not even very tough compared to some of the others).

If you are going to leave the guild, the game, or start a twink, please let us know. The worst thing you can do for the guild is to simply disappear into thin air and join the ever-growing MIA list.

However, I would like to encourage everyone 51+ to really think twice before starting a twink, or worse, quitting the game. We all have real-life responsibilities, and we can certainly accept that, but realize that it's really a whole new game 51+. Imagine what the guild would be like if all of our MIA or quit-the-guild people - Innoxa, Maduyb, Zinker, Tuyet, Kreeble, Runin, Cactus, Moonbeam (sic), Milkdud, Arbok, and all the rest - returned to the guild and were level 50+. We could raid almost anything on the face of Norrath.

Twinks are very time-consuming and may not be worth it. Besides, it's very easy to burn out on twinks. I had quite a lot of twinks whom I all quit on until I finally realized that twinking really wasn't a good idea for me.

If you must start a twink, make a warrior though. We're very short on them

I do think that we should do some more active recruiting, as Lendaor said. I see very few new faces in the guild that aren't simply twinks of guild oldbies. We should announce that we're recruiting members, but, if we do this, we have to tighten our guidelines for recruitment somehow. I've seen too many guilds collapse and fall apart because they announced that they were recruiting and started inviting every Vince Idiot into their ranks.

It's my belief that if we raid something big (Thrackin Griften ) it'll be a good experience for the guild and will show that we actually can do things. It's no challenge raiding mid 30s dungeons, but taking down an epic mob or something similar could spark new interest in our guild's high level raid abilities. Ideally, we should all be ready to give up whatever we are doing (yes, including exp/loot groups in Seb) for any raid that's going on.
