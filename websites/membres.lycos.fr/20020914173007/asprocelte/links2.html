THE DISCIPLINE OF RESISTANT

Level obtained : 51
Duration : 1 minutes.
Re-Use Delay : 60 minutes.
Re-Use Delay at Level 60 : 51 minutes.
Discipline Description : This discipline adds +10 to all resistances.

This discipline lasts for 1 minute, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease
as you level.


THE DISCIPLINE OF FEARLESS

Level obtained : 54
Duration : 11 seconds.
Re-Use Delay : 60 minutes.
Re-Use Delay at Level 60 : 54 minutes.
Discipline Description : Immunity to 'Fear' and all spells that cause 'fear'.

This discipline lasts for 11 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease
as you level.


THE DISCIPLINE OF PROTECTIVE SPIRIT

Level obtained : 55
Duration : 12 seconds.
Re-Use Delay : 12 minutes.
Re-Use Delay at Level 60 : 7 minutes.
Discipline Description : When using this discipline the Beastlord will take roughly 1/10th damage from most physical attacks.

This discipline lasts for 12 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as you level.

It's kinda like the Stone Stance discipline of the monk and it's more like 1/3 dmg reduced


THE DISCIPLINE OF BEASTIAL FURY

u>Level obtained : 60
Duration : 12 seconds.
Re-Use Delay : 30 minutes.
Discipline Description : When using this discipline all hits landed by the Beastlord will be for at least four
times the base weapons/hand damage. For example: If you used a weapon with a base damage of 8, then damage would be calculated
on every hit as if you had a base weapon damage of 32.

This discipline lasts for 12 seconds and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes.

It's about the same discipline named Innerflame from the monk class
