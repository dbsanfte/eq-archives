Guild Laws

These rules are to apply to all guild member please read  them carefully thank you

      Guild members should be known for their fairness, generosity, and willingness to stand by each other in  adversity. In particular:

Guild Raids

Our Guild will participate in two types of Raids

1.    Experience guild raids

2.    Fun guild raids

  1.Experience guild raids.

 These types of raids will consist of a group of similar levels as to allow for exp's to be gained by all in the parties. The chosen destinations for the raids should be appropriate to the levels participating in the raid .For example: Befallen would be a good raid for parties consisting of levels around mid teens. (Old befallen at least)

  2. Fun guild raids.

These types of raids are for the whole guild to participate in. The level ranges in the parties may not allow for all party members to gain exp's .The purpose of these raids is for fun, excitement and overall friendship. Example fun raids will allow low to high level dungeons and also item achievement raids (Item achievement raids are for obtaining quest items and /or specific race /class items.

Rules to Guild Raids

Guild raids are to be run in the most efficient and organized manner as possible. All participants of the guild raid are to remain patient so as to let the raid organizer do his job.

Looting of corpses will be done by a guild raid organizer or by an appointed individual (s) .At the end of the raid all loot will be distributed equally amongst all participating members. Please note All players’ corpses must be recovered before the guild raid ends. It is everyone job to help in the recovered of corpses

       3.organizing guild raids

Each officer should run a low level alt parked in a city or newbie zone. These alts will also be officers so that new members can be joined using them. All alts of an officer will be made officers.  Each officer is expected to run a low level raid, these raids will have the purpose of leveling or training the attendees. Low level means for characters under level 25. Officers are expected to attend at least one low level raid per month and to spend some hours helping the lower level characters gain exp. Exceptions will be made for characters that spend time per month helping guild members in other ways. The main exception to this rule being for any wizard or druid who spends time porting people. For each low level raid they run an officer can call on the guild to help them obtain a quest item or aid with a trade skill or anything else that they need help with. This help may take many forms for example, a raid to kill a particular mob, or a long running camp, or a gathering

All guild raids will be organized and ran by an officer (or a officers alternate char.) of the guild.  Exceptions to this rule will be allowed upon consent of the guild master, in other words if one of the guild members would like to organize and run a guild raid. A request will need to be made to the guild leader for approval. At least a week’s notice should be given before a guild raid is to take place. This will allow plenty of time to prepare for the raid and to update the planned events page on our web site. We will also try to remind everyone of the upcoming raids a few days in advance through the guild motd, so it is everyone’s responsibility to keep an eye on our web site and motd for up coming events  exercise.There should be roughly 10 to 15 members for each officer. Here member means an account and not a character.

 Guild recruiting

          Recruitment procedures the purpose of this list is to out line the proper procedures for inviting new members to our guild, this is intended to maintain a simple process, while ensuring our standards are kept high, and also to be certain that new members of the guild no what they are getting into prior to joining.

            1, A new member will have one week of trial, during this week the guild shall judge them worthy or not.

          2, asking to be power leveled or for items within your first week within the guild, will most likely result in immediate removal from the guild. While we want to help our members we cannot afford to help out every person that signs up out of the blue.

          3, two or more guild officers must agree on inviting a new member to our guild

          4, we will accept any race, class of  level  10 upwards. This is because at this level  they can look after there self  to a certain degree

          5, If for any reason a guild member has been removed from the guild by an officer, the guild officer must give a full explanation on why the decision was made to remove the member to the guild leader.

          6, When a new member joins, will the officer that invites the member announce the member on guild chat, so all members on line may welcome them, and it is that officers responsibility to give the new member the web site address, and ask them to sign in and read the guild

 rules

                                                                 Play nice policy  

a) Guild members will never name call or otherwise run down other guild members in any way except by direct tells. What you call each other in  private is your concern what you say when others can hear is the guild's concern.

b) Guild members will never knowingly overcharge anyone, nor will they underpay others. If you know the price is not the normal one then ALWAYS query it and offer to trade at a fair price. 

c) Guild members will never deliberately kill steal nor will they try to take a camp from players already in place. If other players want to share a camp a guild member already has then the member will do their best to accommodate them

d) When dealing with other guild members always be willing to give the other player a good deal. Benefits of being in Proelia Praemia.

a) Being in the company of friends. Members can solo as much as they wish but the guild is about friendship and members are expected to group together and to help each other. 

b) Support when you have a problem. Someone trying to steal your camp? Or having trouble getting your corpse back? The guild will help.

c) Advice. Want to know where to get something? Where to hunt? Just ask.

d) A shoulder to cry on. Just lost your level? Got personal problems and want a bit of company to take your mind of them? Let us know how we can help.

e) Guild events. Usually called raids. A time to meet your guild friends and often a chance to get something extra, like experience or unusual loot or something for a quest. 1, In our effort to build the best guild possible we are asking everyone to remember that his or her actions, comments, remarks etc. reflect on our guilds name, So please play nice.

          Watch your language and respect others in the game, if for any reason anyone is caught by or reported to any of the officers for abusing these policies guild membership will be reconsidered for that individual, this is not something any of the officers want to have to do, so please just follow the guide lines.

          2, In case of conflict with another player within the guild, either back off and leave it or contact an officer to deal with the situation, that way your reputation is covered and the officer can handle the situation in the best possible way.

            3, if anyone officer votes no to an invite, ( and has a reasonable reason for doing so), then the person will be refused membership to the guild.

   4, Follow the guild chain of command whenever possible, contact an officer (see the officers list on the web site), and if the officer cannot deal with the situation he or she will contact the leader for you, do not contact the leader yourself, unless there are no officers available.

  5, anyone found dealing in trade scandals or the exploitation of other guild members will be warned or removed from the guild.

   6, It would be nice to see members of similar levels group more often, this has it’s advantages, but this is not really a rule just a suggestion.

  7,You should help other guild members out whenever possible, however members must not expect the guild to rush to them every time they call, many times other guild members maybe busy, or not near enough to respond, and this must be understood.                                 8, all officers have the final say over members in dispute.                                                                                                                           9  Due to the demand of resurrections  made by members, and bank funds being low  no resurrections will be granted to members under level 30. All rule changes for the guild must be approved by a majority of the officers. The views of the members will be taken into account. This will be done by posting proposed changes on the web site and dropping any changes that more than ten percent of members oppose by posting a negative reply on the message board. Members will have 7 days to object. Guild members will sell trade supplies and spell research items to guild bankers and/or members at the rates they would get from vendors. In particular hard to acquire things like enchanter faded pages should be a priority for members to hang on to and turn in to a banker character. Other pages, words, and runes should be gathered too. Banker characters should buy crafted items from members at one and a half time the value when sold to a vendor and sell these same items at a 30percent profit. E.g. something a vendor would buy for 1pp should be sold to a banker for 1pp 5gp and bought from the banker for 2pp. There would have to be a limit on the number of items of a single type stocked by a banker
