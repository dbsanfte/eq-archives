We strive  through our conduct to create a reputation of
honor, valor, honesty and integrity
associated with this guild and its members.
We require nothing of our members,
other than to try to uphold this standard.
Our goal is to have the tag 
 ~Proelia Praemia~ 
 become known to mean:
 a player to be trusted in battle and trade.
Although we value a highly developed sense of ethics,
we also value a sense of humor. 
If you are easily offended by a bit of good natured,  behavior
or  you feel that humor has no place in Norrath,
this may not be the guild you are looking for.
We are a group of friends, joined by the common idea of honor.
