Greetings one and all!

We are pleased you have found your way to our guild website. In order to get a better feel for what being in The Council of True Elements is all about...we would like for you to know the following:

We believe in friendship and fun... first and foremost, we are not a raiding guild however we do have raids, but this is not our primary focus...we do plan fun events and help one another to the best of our ability.

We encourage people to play whatever class they enjoy and believe that diversity is a way of keeping fun in the game, therefore we do not support the theory of Mains vs. Alts...instead we feel that whatever character you are playing at any given time is the Main for the moment.

We frown strongly on unethical behaviors such as:

Abandoning a guildmate in a dangerous area.

Luring or enticing other guilds member's away from their guild.

Insinuating that someone is not playing their character well or does not have good gear (in general: insulting behaviors).

Ninja looting.

Kill stealing which includes breaking someone else's camp area deliberately.

NEVER EVER BEG IN GUILD OR OUT OF GUILD! BEGGARS ARE NOT TOLERATED!

NEVER BASH ANYONE IN ANY CHAT TO ANYONE.(in guild or not in guild)! Treat everyone with respect. Treat them the way you want to be treated. This is especially strict on officers. All members of this guild need to remain calm and diplomatic when dealing with an issue. If you cannot handle it without getting upset, ask an officer to get involved. If it is an officer or the guild leader you are having issues with then ask another officer or a group of officers to be a mediator. ANYONE caught bashing someone else will have disciplinary brought again them … Everyone will get a warning. Then a counseling session..... if the temper cannot be calmed... the member may eventually be removed from guild. An Officer's will get a warning, a counseling session, then loose officership....eventually could lead to removal from guild. If the member or officer goes way out of line….. then the guild leader has right to remove that member with out the step down process. All actions should be documented as much as possible via screen shots and /log.

REMEMBER TO TREAT YOUR LEADERS WITH RESPECT. They are there to help you and this guild. They are your friends and they are your family but they are also your officers and guild leader. They are basically your Manager and Assistant Managers … treat them with the same respect. Officers should remember to treat their fellow officers with respect and all Officers should treat the guild leader with respect. And although they are your leaders…. They do not have the right to treat you poorly. And you have the right to seek help when you have issues with one of them. See paragraph above.

We believe in the /random lotto method for fair distribution of loot and need before greed is always the rule.

We do have a disciplinary board and will address grievances quickly. We are not above guild removing a member who violates our ideals. We would prefer to maintain a quality guild even if that means remaining small as opposed to a large guild full of players who do not fit into CoTE.

We do not condone prejudice in any form believing all are equal.

We do not actively recruit for membership into CoTE but rather guild invite those who are sponsored by an active CoTE member.

Each member is expected to view the website daily if possible in order to stay well informed of guild activities and prevent repetitive questions about what is happening in guild chat.

We want our guild to reflect the personalities and ethic's of our players and maintain an honorable guild reputation and we feel each person wearing the CoTE guild tag is a representative of CoTE's fine ideals.

We maintain a family atmosphere and value the input of all our members whether we agree with them all the time or not.

Our focus is to form lasting friendships and have a good time while doing this. Along the way extra rewards are experience, loot and adventure

;-)

Please make your application along with who is sponsoring you, if you feel this is your kind of guild.
