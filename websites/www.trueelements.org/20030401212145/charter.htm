The Spirit Element

This is the Element that defines the essence of what CoTE is. The Spirit Element controls the energy of all the other elements. With out spirit and heart all else fails. We are a family a friends with huge heart and a generous soul. We do not condone anything that would be considered dishonorable. We believe in fairness for all and it shows in everything we do. Treat others how you would like to be treated and expect the same from others. Look out for your friends. Listen with your heart as well as your mind. Trust your instinct.

 

The Earth /Ground Element

This Element represents Strength, Courage and Stability. We have remain strong for a long time while other guilds have come and gone. We survived tough times together... using each others courage to hold us together . Each time we have come out stronger. We have learned to adapt, to listen to each other and we gain knowledge from our successes as well as from our errors. Our loyalty to our family is as strong as the mountain.

 

The Air /Wind Element

Inspiration, Knowledge and Wisdom is what this Element is all about. CoTE 's leaders know that to keep the guild happy they must inspire others, learn new things and teach what they know. We encourage all guild members to do the same. Sharing your enthusiasm and your wisdom benefits all. You can also inspire by leading by example no matter who you are. Behave in an honorable way and people will look up to you and inspire to be like you. Everyone has a voice and everyone's voice is important. You will be amazed by the power of one person.

The Fire Element

Oooh La La The Passion Element. We are all passionate about this game in our own way. Each of us have our own goals. Some of us crave loot, power and to defeat great demons. Some only wish to level, while others only crave friendship and good times. We must understand that there is no wrong endeavor in this guild. Just do not force others to view things your way. Everyone has a mind of their own and the freedom to do what they like.

It is a fine line between endeavoring to be your best and becoming greedy. Never let your passion overpower your sense of honor.

That being said .... We believe in friendship and fun... first and foremost, we are not a raiding guild however we do have raids, but this is not our primary focus...we do plan fun events and help one another to the best of our ability.

 

The Water Element

The Element of Emotion. Since we are a family of friends we understand each person has feelings and their own personality. We love to have fun, joke around and be all around... well goof balls. But there are times when we are not ourselves. We may need a shoulder to cry on, to complain to, to talk to or to help calm us down. We try not to take things out on each other but use each other for support. We do not allow attacks against any innocent person. . .Any misbehaver within the guild will be dealt with fairly and on an individual basis. You will be brought in front of a council of officers who will try to come up with a quick & just resolution.
