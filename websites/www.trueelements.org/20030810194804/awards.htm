Spirit of Cote Awards

These awards are to recognize those guild members who have gone above and beyond the call of duty. They have been nominated by their fellow guild mates who have witnessed their wonderful acts. At the end of each month, those who were nominated are then voted on by their officers the appoint a single winner.

Then at an all guild party /meeting, we recognize those who were nominated and what they were nominated for. And then we announce the winner!

 

The winners receive:

Their toon drawn, inked, colored and posted on this site.

The reason they were nominated will be posted next to their pict.

The get to schedule a day for the guild to help them do whatever they want....This includes but not limited to: Power Leveling any of their toons, getting an item that we can as a guild get for them, money ( Within a reasonable amount), a party or a raid.
