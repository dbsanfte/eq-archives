Council of True Elements Charter

  
 

 
 

CHARTER & STATEMENT OF PURPOSE:

True Elements was founded to bring together adventurers who enjoy each other's company and want to make the gaming experience better for all. This guild is a force of Norathians who are excellent players, be that role-play or simply game play. Our ideology includes that of Verant, in that any bannable offense is grounds for immediate dismissal from the guild. We require that the player be a responsible team player to join our guild. We want to enjoy the game without infringing on others' rights to have a good time, and we want to support each other in their efforts to reach the highest levels possible, and create the most interesting characters they can. We want to be helpful to all whenever possible, and to use our resources to promote events that involve all players on the Karana server.

True Elements is lead by a Leader and his council of Officers, but all guild members have a voice. Should our Leader or Council abandon our cause, we will instantly try to expel him as a subverter of our rights and his own, and we will choose another Leader to lead and protect us. We fight not just for glory, wealth, or honor, but for that liberty which without no virtuous man can survive.
