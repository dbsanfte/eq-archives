The Officers Test Event has been scheduled for 6:00pm EST this next Sunday the 9th. Please post in the Forum if you need to request a different day or time.

We have been getting together for some quick mini raids of places like Ry'gorr Fort in EW and DL. These have been going very well and have been loads of fun. Basically these mini raids have been two groups with one puller, one loot master, and a raid leader. Groups are equaled out as much as possible so that both groups get experience. The main puller chain pulls and both groups focus their attacks on the same mobs. This seems to be a strategy that can work well to allow the two groups to kill stuff bigger and faster then what one group could normally do. The exp over time is probably not as good as one good group because of the splitting of exp between the two groups. This strategy does seem to improve overall safety due to both groups helping each other out though. Besides, it is a blast being in a platoon of CoTE kicking some major arse on the battlefield=). Lets keep working on these mini raids to help us improve our strategies for the big raids coming up. And don’t forget, any member can post raid idea's in our forum, so don’t be shy!
