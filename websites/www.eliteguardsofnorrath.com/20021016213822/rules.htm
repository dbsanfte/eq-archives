Rules?!  We don't need no stinking rules!

Well, ok, if we must have rules, here are some pretty good ones that have
served us well.

1) Above all, NO SWEARING IN GUILD CHAT!  We are a pretty laid back group, but we are also very diverse people from very different backgrounds.  What may not offend you may offend someone else.  Please keep an eye on this.

2) On a related topic, you are expected to be respectful of those both in the guild and those outside the guild.  Ninja looting, intentional training, etc does not paint our guild in a positive light and cannot be allowed.  Be fair in your dealings with groups and avoid offensive language with all.

3) Offer assistance when you can.  We are a pretty helpful group and it is my goal to make us ever more so.  To that end, if you are porting somewhere, give someone a free lift.  Heal someone in passing if you can and they need it.  Offer buffs if you can spare the time and mana.  It only serves to make the game better for everyone and spread our name.  Who knows when you will need a favor in the future?

4) Our guild has a lower level limit.  We do not admit first time members under level 10 in most cases.  It is a pretty low level limit and those familiar with the game or games of this type can meet it quickly.  The intent is simply to require a minimum level of familiarity with the game prior to admitting folks into the guild.   Alternates of current members are allowed in at any level.

5) Officers are required to offer assistance to members of the guild whenever possible and feasible.  The officers are there to take care of issues and problems as they arise as well as to promote the guild and perform duties like planning raids.  If an officer cannot assist you right away with your issue, be assured that they will try to find someone who can or will plan a time that the issue can be resolved.

6) New members are invited on a probationary basis.  Just as you may want to get to know us, we want to get to know you.  If you find that we are not a good fit for you, good luck in your travels.  We do reserve the right to make the same decision (however rarely  =)  ).

That's pretty much it.  We do not have class or skill limitations.  Anyone who wants to relax and have a good time is welcome.  We encourage new members and the recruiting of friends and group-mates that you meet along the way to join us in our activities and to come play with us.  We have ever increasing resources for you to draw on and you should feel free to do so.  So if you are new to our guild, WELCOME!   If you are considering joining us, give it a shot!  We're a pretty fun bunch.

See you around Norrath!
Batal Padfoot
Guild Leader & Giant Slayer Extraordinaire
