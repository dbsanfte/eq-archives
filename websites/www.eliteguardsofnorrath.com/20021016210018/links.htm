everquest.allakhazam.com  - Pretty much anything you want to know about quests and equipment.  They have a paid premium service that lets you do things like find out what kind of equipment would be a good upgrade for you and where/how you can get it.  I think so of the advanced searching is only available in the paid version also.  Probably the best all around Everquest info site.


www.eqatlas.com - Maps, maps, maps - This is the source for the best maps of almost all Everquest lands.  I always research new places I plan to travel here first.  I usually print a copy of the maps so I can find my way around.  (If only I had done that the time I fell in the chasm in Overthere.)


www.everlore.com - This is one of the first sites I ever visited when I first started playing, it's a general purpose site with good information, but I've gotten to where I prefer the other sites more.


www.everquest.com - Duh, who doesn't know what this is.


www.eqtraders.com - How the heck do I make banded boots?  Need a recipe for any of the trades skills?  This is the site.  The most comprehensive listing of recipes for all your trade skills.

eq.castersrealm.com  - This is a great site to research what spells you get at what levels.  I come here often to drool over what set of spells I'll get next and see what they will do.  They also have good character creation guides.  This is probably one of my favorite sites.
