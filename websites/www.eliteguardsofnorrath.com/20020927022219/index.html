Planes of Power
I'm sure most of you troll all the EQ sites like I do, but in case you don't or just haven't thought about it lately, I thought I'd post a little news on the upcoming expansion.  Planes of Power will be released next month.  With it will come new zones and I'm sure more uber loot.  It will mostly be for higher levels though there are a couple of zone (Plane of Knowledge if I'm not mistaken) that will be for any level.  With this expansion the level cap will also increase to 65 and bring new spells for those levels.  The new levels are not supposed to feel so terribly boring and tedious and are supposed to be link to something called planar advancement which is akin to (or perhaps an addition to) the AA system.  Not a lot of details have come out yet since it's crunch time at Verant for getting this puppy out the door, but you can find more info on the Everquest site and also over at Casters Realm.  Anyhoo.  Mines already ordered and it will be interesting to see.
