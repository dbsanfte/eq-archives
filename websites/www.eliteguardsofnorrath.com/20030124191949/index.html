need help getting in forum ?
For those of you who may need help getting around the site... First you click on new users, sign up here, then type your character's name in the user name space, then make up a password...click on create, at that point you can put your character's information in and to update any of it at a later time you just click modify...
To get in the forum just click on the forum at the top of the page, you then set up an account it is quick, free, and you only need to do it once. you can get into the members section with a password that can be asked of anyone in guild chat.
If you have any problems please feel free to email me at ellisen75@hotmail.com, in /t or guild chat my alts are Elexa and Cieran.
