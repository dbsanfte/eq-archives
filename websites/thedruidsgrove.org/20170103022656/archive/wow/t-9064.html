Thought I would share this. Havent tested it myself yet:

Anyway, yea. You don't need to bug people to be able to reset instances, all you have to do is use a simple macro (case sensitive):

/invite personsname
/script LeaveParty()


Here's the deal. You DO need a second person, but they don't have to know about it. You just have to find someone who's in a group already (pro tip: /who stratholme or /who blackrock spire etc etc).

Once you've got your person add them into the macro.

Now here's the steps:

-Go into your instance of choice, and farm whatever you're after
-Once you've got everything, just stop. Don't go back to the entrance, stay where you are.
-Use the macro.
-You will see the following:

You leave the group.
You have invited Person to your group.

then a few seconds later the little box will popup saying "you are not in this instance's group. you will be ported to xxxx in 60 seconds"

Once the box pops up, log off.

Now, log back in.

Guess where you are? Right back at the entrance of the instance. But it's a fresh, NEW instance, and you're ready to continue farming.

Rinse, repeat.

Possible problems:

If you use the macro and it says "Person is already in a group", it didn't work. Press the macro again. Problem solved. It just shouldn't ever actually say they're in a group. Just what I posted above.

If the person leaves their group, they're gonna start getting invites from you, most likely pissing them off. So try to find someone who just started a long instance (scholololo) and milk them for every minute.

Otherwise, it works perfectly.
