Pet problems, Healing upgrades and patching issues, Oh my! 07/25/02 8:51 am
Oh, the joys of patch day. Here's a few updates for you:

VI forgot to tell us about the removal of the healing penalty.

We forgot to include a comment in the patch message about the removal of the 10% healing penalty for non-clerics. The penalty was indeed removed with this patch.

Also, while we’ve included the new ‘old-world’ Vah Shir models in the patch, you won’t be able to turn them on yet. We’ll have to patch another file to allow you to choose to turn them off in the interface. That should happen soon.

If you can't summon a good pet, here's why:

For those of you with characters that summon pets, we are aware of the problem with pets. Currently they are not being summoned at their highest level. Sorry for the problem, we’ll get it fixed as soon as we can. - Alan

And most importantly, if you're having crashing problems due to the new UI, here's how to disable it:

If you are crashing to desktop after using the /newui command and cannot log back in without crashing, open up your eqclient.ini (located in your EverQuest directory - typically C:\Program File\EverQuest, C:\Program Files\Sony\EverQuest or C:\Program Files\Sony\EverQuest Trilogy) file and change the line that reads NewUI=TRUE to NewUI=FALSE. We are making the Dev-Team aware of the issue and we will address it in some way.
Regards,

Victor Wachter Sony Online Entertainment
