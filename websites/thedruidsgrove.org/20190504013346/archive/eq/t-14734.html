If your group is fighting 2 or more mobs at once(not mezzing) then rain spells are great dps.

Rain spells hit up to 4 targets (and you if your too close)
4 mobs and each get hit once
3 mobs and 2 will get hit once,1 will get hit twice
2 mobs and each will get hit twice
1 mob will be hit 3 times

Just be sure the mobs are close enough together and watch your aggro, since the adds may not be getting taunted.

They can be used effectivly soloing if you pull 4 mobs at a time, snare them, bunch them up close, kite and nuke.
