Level 58+ solo times - reworked

This is divided into three sections. However, just because it falls in one section does not mean it does not fit into another one. For example, Timorous Deep - it is common procedure to quad here, but when it was busy it was easy to waste some time root/dotting those that were not in the normal accepted quad circuits. Also some zones obviously fall into multiple categories and where I thought it was warranted I listed them twice. For example, PoM - I listed in charm and in root/dot because it is very different areas of the zone. You can almost always make the assumption that if its quaddable, root/dot is just as appropriate. Ok, on to the lists!

Quading Areas

Timorous Deep:
~raptors

Burning Woods:
~monkeys, skeletons, noncasting sarnaks

Cobalt Scar:
~oshmir or bulthar, harmony, snare, etc
~bulthar have more hps and are better for root/dot imo

Grey:
~golems or shissars and skeletons
~don’t mix the two types as golems are slower than the rest

Maidens Eye:
~toilers, goranga, coterie have a ton of hps though

Scarlet Desert:
~any alien type, revenants/reavers – plateau is the best exps area

Root/Dot Areas

Skyfire:
~rare cycle, very very rarely do they summon just load gate/egress, best area near FM zone

Wakening Lands:
~geonids cave, dot shaman at max distance and move away to prevent their healing
~sentient armor on other side of the zone

Great Divide:
~frost giant elites by fort

Eastern Wastes:
~kromrif guardsmen at Kael entrance - beware Death Squad
~dwarven bodyguards at huts on coast
~manticores and manowars (rarely blue)
~rygoor fort elites are 2/3 blue, oracle and chief also

Lesser Faydark:
~brownie guards at the shop area, boring, easy, safe afk, drop meats for baking

Solusek B:
~fire giants are more a root/dot, thorns, regen, nuke fest
~efreeti run with the rare bugs/bats is great fun solo and good exps just root dot your way up

Velketor's Lab:
~spiders and gargoyles from bottom and all the way up ramp to dog area

Howling Stones:
~zone in top and bottom as well as North Wing – Cryptkeeper summons

Trakanon’s Teeth:
~forager/hunter rare cycle – just pull to a wall

Plane of Mischief:
~shakespeare room, rat maze, forest but mostly green

Old Sebilis:
~zone in, zone out, roam and kill stragglers

Seru:
~mainly those near the zone, Optios summon

Maidens Eye:
~coterie, 4arms guys, nothing in the zone summons

Ahkeva:
~all the toilers from zone to the end of each intersections tunnel

Umbral Plains:
~elysians for armor, stick to the safe trench right past Vex Thall, pull with invis as they are undead

Dawnshroud:
~random blues are trackable

Neriak:
~keep gate/egress up, fun place – kill the males, marry the women

Fungus Grove:
~ bandits, zelinaks at Twilight zone

Acrylia Caverns:
~ beware enchanters (charms no big deal when solo though)

Echo Caverns:
~a scound, named scounds, arisen soldiers – good Shadowhaven factions!

Kael:
~both ramps (EW/WL), Lts drop the armor for Thurgadin quests

Charming
~ there are many other areas that you can charm, I just listed the ones I felt charm was the best option

Plane of Mischief:
~shakespeare room, Alice 1-2, still playing here…

Chardok:
~dogs near the top level make great pets

Western Wastes:
~elephants, rhinos, wolves – watch the CoV stuff most of it summons

Sirens Grotto:
~frontal area up to water from Western Wastes

Grey, Maidens, Scarlet Desert:
~just a few other zones where charming is a viable alternative
