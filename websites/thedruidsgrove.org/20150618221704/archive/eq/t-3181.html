Looks like some explanation is in order as to what criteria I used to select the items on the above list... this isn't really "Every Uber Item You Could Ever Get to Upgrade Any Slot." Rather, it's aimed at the level 50 druids who post here every day asking what to use to upgrade their forest loops and velium ruby veil. (I mean, I figure a level 58 druid who's using an enshrouded veil probably knows that the Mask of War would be a good upgrade if only they could get one.) So I used these criteria to try to put together a list of relatively cheap, available items:

1) Stats (durr).

2) Commonness. For example, the crystal chitin shield is an awesome item, but it's incredibly rare. There's nothing sadder than seeing a poor little druid auctioning "WTB Crystal Chitin Shield / Withered Leather Gloves / Burynai Hide Boots" for day after day. So I chose items that are fairly common and should be buyable with an hour or two of auctioning.

3) Cost. This applies to items like Lodizal shield, Crystal Chitin BP, ES BP, etc.: They're super rare (see above under "Availability"), but if you could buy one, they'd run you over 80k on my server. I'm assuming that your average level 50 druid doesn't have that. Mask of War is 25k on my server, withered leather tunic 40k+. About every item I listed is under 15k on Saryrn, with many under 1k.

4) Forces required to get the item. Obviously a dweamorvine garland or withered leather tunic or tanglewood shield is a great item, but they require a full fledged raid to get. I'm assuming that if you went on a Hate raid and saw a runewood shield drop, you'd know you wanted it. So I'm listing items people could organize one group to go camp, or else auction to buy in NFP/EC/GFay.

5) Bang for the buck. For example, electrum black sapphire earrings are awesome and cheap; same with crown of the froglok kings.

Tek ... Wis is more important than mana until you max out Wis which right now I believe is either 225 or 250 ... untill then 1 wis =

( ( Wis / 5 ) + 2 ) * Level = Mana

Level 15:

( ( 200 / 5 ) + 2 ) * 15 = 630 Mana from Wisdom, so at lower levels Mana is MUCH greater then here you are getting 3 Mana per wisdom.


Level 34:

( ( 200 / 5 ) + 2 ) * 34 = 1428 Mana from wisdom, so the higher your level the more important it is to max out your wisdom, you are now getting 7mana per wisdom.

Level 60:

( ( 200 / 5 ) + 2 ) * 60 = 2520 Mana from wisdom, You are now getting 12 mana per wisdom.


With this formula you can figure out what is worth more, the wisdom from an item or the mana.

Pre-Luclin the Wis cap was 200, and Mana items at High level were highly sought after since 200 isnt that hard to reach.
