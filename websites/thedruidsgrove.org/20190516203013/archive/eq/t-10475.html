I used sneak for the NPC's that would not talk to me or were rude.

When I did 1.0, I had to do three different faction quests. The first one was the oh-so-joyful muffin quest. The second one would've been the batwing quest, but the NPC that takes the batwings didn't want them. Actually she "ate" them but didn't give me any faction raise (grrr....). I ended up doing the letter quest between Jeremy Leafrunner (stands by the fire in Jaggedpine Forest) and his grandmother in Felwithe. I had to do a few rounds of giving a letter to Jeremy, then one back to his grandma. After awhile, my faction was good enough that the batwing lady liked me ;) I'll see if I can look it up and edit this post.

EDIT: Ah, here we go.

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2035

This quest raises Keepers of the Art faction also (which you need to talk to Alrik Farsight in TD), but it takes a bit longer than the batwing quest, which is why I only did it long enough until the batwing lady would take my batwings. When I did this, I bound myself right next to Jeremy's grandma (Elishia Leafrunner in Felwithe) and then would port to Surefall and run to JPF to deliver the first letter, then gate back to deliver the second letter. Actually if you deliver the second letter back to Jeremy it doesn't raise Keepers of the Art faction, but I just kept giving them to him anyway since I was already there. After you do this a few times to raise your faction a bit, you should be able to start giving batwings to Niola. Hope this helps!!
