04-02-2003, 08:47 AM
I did the quest back to back two days in a row. The giants weren't up about 20H after I did the first glove, but they were up about 28H afterwards. BTW, unless you as a druid have VERY good resists, be sure not to pull the gaints or be first on the agro list. It's much better to fight them in place with agro on the resist-heavy character than to pull them. Oh, and especially don't use snare to pull. Snare generates huge agro for these giants. They'll glue themselves to you. I killed my druid on the second day by using her to pull, since the fights were so easy on the first day. :(

Delar is often trackable from the dragon ring, but he roams away from it, too. The best spot to be sure to see them is the lake. I've always had at least one on tracking from the lake. They roam on a rough line from just within track range to the ring on up to the NE of the lake up near the N edge of the zone.
