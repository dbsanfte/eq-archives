Ogre let the cat out of the bag with this patch note:
- Raid groups will no longer receive quest bounties and collections. In the future, we plan to have raid-specific quests that will be possible to complete in raid groups.

So no more throwing 10 people at Scarlet Monestary or 15 at Black Rock Depths. I think this fix will do a world of good and bring some meaning back to a lot of the instances.

The Official Forum Post can be found here (http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.aspx?FN=wow-general&T=402107&P=1).
