Chapter 1
Norrath Overview

The world of EverQuest has a rich history that sets the stage for your role playing. The following introduction to the races and geography of Norrath will serve as an initial guide to help you get started.

Your Role

So what will your role in Norrath be? You must make many choices before you can begin. You must choose a race, class, and deity to follow. For race, you may choose between human, barbarian, erudite, woodland elf, high elf, dark elf, dwarf, troll, ogre, halfling, or gnome. Class choices consist of warrior, cleric, paladin, ranger, shadow knight, druid, monk, bard, rogue, shaman, necromancer, wizard, magician, and enchanter. Some classes are not available to certain races. Your race and class selection will also limit the deities you may choose from and the starting location for your character. See Chapter 2 - I Dub Thee for more information about races, classes, deities, and starting locations.

After you create your character, your future is in your own hands. Will you choose the noble way of the paladin, bringing honor and justice to the land? Or will you choose the way of the thief, gaining personal wealth at the expense of others. The choice is yours - the world of Norrath awaits your decision.

Role-Playing Tips

EverQuest is a role-playing game. The discussion of what role-playing entails is a never-ending discussion among online forums. In the end, there are no set rules as to what is acceptable role playing and what is not. One thing is for sure though - those who are more serious about playing the part of the medieval adventurer will frown upon those who speak in the game about pizza, television, or football.

One of the biggest factors in the ambiance and fun of a role-playing game is how well the participants role-play. Following are some guidelines to help your role-playing in EverQuest, which are summed up nicely by wandering bard Roo, "Anyone can role-play. It just requires a desire to be somewhere else, with friends that want to be someone else, in a time that will never occur anywhere else."
