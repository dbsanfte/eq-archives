Dwarf
Dwarves are smaller and stockier than Humans, but are much quicker and stronger. They are excellent jewel crafters with a long history of mining in Kaladim. Dwarves are considered "small" and wear small sized armor and clothing. They possess infravision and begin play in Kaladim. Their special ability includes an increased Sense Direction skill.

Erudite
Like the barbarian, the Erudite is also a descendent of the Human. The erudite, or "high man", consider their race to be the elite of all races in Norrath. They take pride in their cultured, lavish lifestyle and enlightened intellectual abilities. Erudites are considered "medium" and wear medium sized armor and clothing. They possess no night vision or special powers and begin play in Erudin.
