Prexus - The Ocean Lord
Followers of Prexus believe true power lies in the vast depths of Norrath's oceans. They believe that life originated in the oceans and that one day, the oceans shall rise again to destroy those who are unworthy and to embrace the faithful.

Quellious - The Tranquil
Followers of Quellious seek inner peace above all. They are not strict pacifists though and will fight to defend themselves and those they care about if need arises.

Solusek Ro - The Burning Prince
The followers of Solusek Ro believe in the raw and unbridled power of fire. They believe that aggression is the only way to obtain your desires.

Brell Serilis - The Duke of Below
Followers of Brell Serilis believe that the surface world is a waste of space. They find true happiness in the caves, caverns, and tunnels that perforate the belly of Norrath.

The Tribunal - The Six Hammers
Followers of the Tribunal seek justice. Followers of the Tribunal are methodical, patient, and just, for they believe they are doomed for eternity if they do not practice justice upon all others.

Tunare - The Mother of All
Followers of Tunare believe that we are all Tunare's children and that Norrath is a sacred environment. Followers of Tumare will fight to the death to protect nature in all its forms.

Veeshan - The Wyrm Queen
Followers of Veeshan believe that dragonkind is superior to all other forms of life. They swear allegiance to the her and often sacrifice themselves or their loved ones to dragonkind.

Rallos Zek - The Warlord
The followers of Rallos Zek believe in survival of the strong and death to the weak. They believe in conflict and that the strong shall live off the weak.
