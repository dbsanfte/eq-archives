MAGAZINES

 



Character Status
Down the right-hand side of the screen, the first box you will see lists your character's name and a series of three bars. The red bar is a bar indicating the life force of your character. This bar shows how many hit points you have and how much damage you can take. The second bar is your mana, which will be filled with blue if you have a spell-casting character. It will empty if you have a non-spell-casting character. If you are a character who gains spells at higher levels, your mana will remain empty until the level at which you gain spells, and then it will fill up. The third bar currently filled with yellow indicates your stamina or how tired your character currently is.

Underneath the three status bars, you will see an Effects button. You can use this to toggle special effects on and off.

The box below the Effects button is a list of party members. When you group with other players, you can see their health bars here. You can target them or target yourself by clicking on their health bar or your health bar. Below your party member's bars is a Current Target box. This indicates who your current target is.

NOTE
You select a target by clicking on it or by pressing the F1 through F8 targeting function keys.

Preset Buttons
Below the current target, in the windowed view, is a series of boxes. The top three boxes are selections for menus that appear in the area below. If you haven't clicked in this area, you start out by looking at your main selections. Your main selections give you buttons for Who, Invite, Disband, Camp, Sit, and Run. All these items can be moved over to hotkeys.

The Who button will let you look at who is in the same zone as you. Invite is used to invite the targeted player to join your party, and Disband is used to remove yourself or a person from the party. The Camp command is used when sitting to log out. To log out correctly, you must sit, press the Camp key, and you will log out in 30 seconds. The Sit key will toggle between Sit and Stand, and the bottom selection here is Run/Walk and will toggle between these two modes.

TIP
Set up hotkeys for Run/Walk and Sit. They are used quite often.

If you click on the Abilities button, you see what appears to be six hotkeys reading Skill 1 through 6. By clicking on one of these skills, you will see a list of the current skills that your character has. These are based upon your race and class. If you click on one of the skills, you will notice that it will place that skill in your skill buttons. You can drag any of these buttons to your hotkeys.
