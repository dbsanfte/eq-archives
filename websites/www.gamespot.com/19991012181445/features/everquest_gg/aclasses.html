Chapter 8
Advanced Classes

When you set out into the world of Norrath, there are two questions you should ask yourself before creating a character: "What do I want this character to become?" and "What will I need to get there?" The class system in EverQuest is very complex, with many unique skills and spells for each class. A little thought and preparation before you introduce your character to the wilds of Norrath can produce a much more well-rounded and enjoyable character in the long run.

This chapter contains detailed information for each of the 14 classes. Provided are tips and tricks on surviving and advancing each particular class as well as starting equipment, available skills, and spells as well as any class restrictions. In parentheses next to each skill is a number that indicates at what level it is available. For more detailed information on skills or spells, see Chapter 9 - Advanced Skills and Chapter 10 - Spells, respectively.
