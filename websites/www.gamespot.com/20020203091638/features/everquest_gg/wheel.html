Chapter 3
Taking the Wheel

Now that we've created a character, it's time to set out on our first quest. From the "Select a Character" screen, choose the character you wish to take on this adventure and press the "Enter World" button. After the title screen clears, you're presented with your first view of the world of Norrath.

Your screen will have a view of the world centered along the top of the screen, gray borders down both sides, and a scroll in the center at the bottom of the screen. Some characters start within ten feet of certain death in the world of Norrath so be careful moving about. The learning curve for getting acquainted and comfortable with the controls is approximately one hour. At first you will find you fingers helplessly shooting around the keyboard trying to find the appropriate key but with a little practice you will find the interface easy to use. Let's take some time to learn the controls before moving about.

Game Interface

The screen you are looking at (called the windowed mode) is one of three ways you can view the world of Norrath. You can switch between these three modes by pressing your F10 function key. By pressing F10 once, you can see the half-screen mode, also called the see-through mode. You'll notice that a lot of the areas from the windowed mode are still on your screen, but now they're transparent and your entire view is more full-screen. Pressing the F10 key again will clear all this information off your screen and give you a clear view of the world, which is usually used for taking screenshots or taking a good look at something. Pressing F10 again will return you to your windowed mode. Both the windowed mode and the see-through mode are fully functional as ways to navigate around the world.

TIP
Press F10 to toggle your modes. You must switch modes depending on what you are doing in the game. Experiment with the modes, you will notice that each one works in a different way, we will discuss how to customize these modes later.

The beauty of EverQuest is it's ability to customize the game interface. There are limitless possibilities, and you're free to set up the interface in any manner to make playing easier.
