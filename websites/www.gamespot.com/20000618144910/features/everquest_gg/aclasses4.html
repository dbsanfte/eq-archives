Cleric

Dedicated to the art of healing, the cleric only engages in combat when attacked or when battling infidels. Clerics use only blunt instruments of war, but their real strength is their ability to protect during battle and offer healing after. Clerics draw their magical spells from prayer and their faith, making adherence to a religious creed essential.



Available races

Dark elf, dwarf, erudite, gnome, halfling, high elf, human

Primary statistics

Strength, stamina, wisdom

Starting equipment

Club, Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, five loaves of bread, and two spell scrolls (courage and minor healing)

Skills available

Abjure (1), alter (1), baking (1), beg (1), bind wound (1), channel (4), conjure (1), divine (1), dodge (15), evoke (1), fishing (1), fletching (1), hand to hand (1), jewel craft (1), meditate (8), one-hand blunt (1), pottery (1), sense heading (1), smithing (1), specialize (30), swimming (1), tailoring (1), two-hand blunt (1)
