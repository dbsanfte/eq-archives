Rogue

Tread lightly, but carry an envenomed blade. Rogues embody a large variety of professions in Norrath, from the lowliest of cutthroats to the adepts of political intrigue. Not all rogues are simple pickpockets or blatant thieves. The common thread that binds those called rogues is a focus on stealth, agility, and a combat strategy that emphasizes maneuvering and intelligence over brute force or magical energies. After all, why risk the blows of a troll's massive club when a quick snip of his purse string will fulfill your desire? Rogues can also be found sweet-talking warring faction leaders and can be quite convincing in his arguments for, of course, a profit! As scouts and spies, rogues have the perfect arsenal of skills. Whether scouting an orcish camp, pilfering the loot of a sleeping dragon, plundering the armory of an enemy faction, or infiltrating a rival guild, a rogue is at his best. Also, if you ever lose the keys to your castle, you know who to ask.
