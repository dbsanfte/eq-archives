MAGAZINES

 



Paladin
The soldier of light is both warrior and cleric. Paladins give their very life to honor and goodness and the relentless pursuit of all that is holy. A paladin is truly dedicated to the creed of the knight, combined with zealous dedication to God. The paladin's paradox is that he is constantly trying to reconcile the aloof nature befitting one of his station and the humility demanded by his deity.

Ranger
Although rangers are often loners, preferring the company of nature to that of man, they are a generous lot and often very helpful. Spending so much time within the forests has honed the ranger's many skills. Not only is he an accomplished tracker, but he is skilled in the ways of wilderness survival - and more often than not, a deadly marksman. Rangers are seldom if ever evil, but much like the druid, they too have an understanding of the cycle of life - and will defend it to the last

Rogue
Rogues embody a large variety of professions in Norrath, from the lowliest of cutthroats to the adepts of political intrigue. Not all rogues are simple pickpockets or blatant thieves. The common thread that binds those called rogues is a focus on stealth, agility, and a combat strategy that emphasizes maneuvering and intelligence over brute force or magical energies. Rogues can also be found sweet-talking warring faction leaders and can be quite convincing in his arguments for, of course, a profit!

Shadow Knight
A knight of the shadow is a formidable foe - his command of weapons is great, as is his strength and stamina. Just as the gods of light smile upon the noble paladin, so do the gods of dark grant their follower's dreadful powers. Higher-level shadow knights are fearsome necromancers, able to summon forth undead minions and suck the life force from their opponents. Death and decay are the allies of the shadow knight, and their terrible powers reflect this in the most haunting of ways. Shadow Knights are indeed branded as "disciples of death," feared and hated by most inhabitants of Norrath. They use this fear to their advantage!

Shaman
A witch doctor by any other name, shamans are purveyors of practical and ancient magic that heal and protect. Somewhat like a cleric, a shaman is born of a more barbaric and base stock. Shamans come from cultures that are quite superstitious and sometimes paranoid.

Warrior
The warrior is a soldier dedicated to the art of war. Guided by power, religion, greed, or fame, a warrior's greatest aspiration is always victory on the battlefield. Warriors take many forms, but all find the sounds of battle their theme song.

Wizard
Wizards by nature are weak and scrawny men who have studied books all their lives and have probably never wielded a sword or similar weapon. Although they may be weak, wizards are masters of the arcane arts and specialize in offensive magic. They do one thing, and they do it well: they deal damage.
