Pottery

Pottery is the art of making containers and other useful items from earth, water and fire. Pottery allows you to create many other items that other trade-skill users will need. A pottery wheel and kiln are required for this craft.

Block of Clay

Use one block of clay, four small blocks of clay, or one large block of clay

Small Block of Clay

Use any size block of clay

Large Block of Clay

Use three blocks of clay, 12 small blocks of clay, or one large block of clay

The following are some items and their ingredients, which can be crafted with the pottery skill.

Skewers

Flask of water, block of clay, quality firing sheet, skewers sketch

Ceramic Lining

Flask of water, small block of clay, quality firing sheet, ceramic-lining sketch

Clay Jar

Flask of water, block of clay, quality firing sheet, jar sketch

Pot

Flask of water, large block of clay, metal bits, high-quality firing sheet, pot sketch

Smoker

Flask of water, large block of clay, two high-quality firing sheets, smoker sketch

Cookie Cutter

Flask of water, small block of clay, high-quality firing sheet, cookie-cutter template

Small Bowl

Flask of water, small block of clay, high-quality firing sheet, bowl sketch

Pie Tin

Flask of water, two blocks of clay, high-quality firing sheet, pie-tin sketch

Cake Round

Flask of water, block of clay, glass shards, high-quality firing sheet, cake-round sketch

Medium Bowl

Flask of water, block of clay, two high-quality firing sheets, medium-bowl sketch

Muffin Tin

Flask of water, block of clay, metal bits, high-quality firing sheet, muffin-tin sketch

Mixing Bowl

Flask of water, two large blocks of clay, firing sheet, mixing-bowl sketch

Large Bowl
