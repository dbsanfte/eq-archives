Qeynos
Qeynos is probably the most populated city in Norrath. It was founded as a seaside outpost, and its castle-like appearance makes it look more like a fortress than a town. With the ever-present danger of gnoll attacks, it's easy to understand why it was constructed so. It is a human city, though being a busy seaport, it bristles with many different races. The western shores of Antonica are known for their regular rainstorms, and Qeynos is no exception. The humid climate explains the moss that constantly threatens to overtake many of the city walls, as well as the abundance of rats and spiders.


Qeynos
Qeynos has many highlights, with the tranquil fountain at the Temple of Life topping the list. The clock tower (which was a gift from King Ak'Anon), the arena, docks, and gruesome prison are also points of interest. Nearly every type of shop can be found here, and the Crow's Pub and Casino is one of the busiest and best places to enjoy a frothy ale after a long battle. Regular ferry service is offered between Qeynos and Erudin.
