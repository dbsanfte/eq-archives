Enchanter

Enchanters are seemingly unimpressed with the mundane creations of the races of Norrath. When an enchanter looks upon a sword - he is not concerned with the sturdiness of the blade or even its sharpness. What an enchanter sees when he looks upon such artifacts is a wealth of possibilities. Perhaps he could alter the metal to make it stronger. Perhaps he could take the flame of a torch and bind it forever to the sword. Enchanters take the ordinary things found in Norrath, and turn them into the stuff of legends.

Available races

Dark elf, erudite, gnome, high elf, human

Primary statistics

Intelligence, charisma

Starting equipment

Dagger, Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, five loaves of bread, and two spells scrolls (minor shielding and shallow breath).

Skills available

Abjure (1), alter (1), bind wound (1), channel (1), conjure (1), divinate (1), dodge (22), evoke (1), hand to hand (1), meditate (4), one-hand blunt (1), piercing (1), research (16), sense heading (1), swimming (1), two-hand blunt (1), specialize (20), throwing (1), tailoring (1), smithing (1), fletching (1), jewel craft (1), pottery (1), old erudian (1)
