Wizard

Wizards by nature are weak and scrawny men who have studied books all their lives and have probably never wielded a sword or similar weapon. Although they may be weak, wizards are masters of the arcane arts and specialize in offensive magic. They do one thing, and they do it well: they deal damage.



Available races

Dark elf, erudite, gnome, high elf, human

Primary statistics

Stamina, intelligence

Starting equipment

Dagger, Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, five loaves of bread, and two spells scrolls (shock of frost and minor shielding)

Skills available

Abjure (1), alter (1), bind wound (1), channel (1), conjure (1), divinate (1), dodge (22), evoke (1), hand to hand (1), meditate (4), one-hand blunt (1), piercing (1), research (16), sense heading (1), swimming (1), two-hand blunt (1), specialize (20), throwing (1)

Wizards can deal more damage in a shorter amount of time than any other class in the game. Some other specialties are their travel spells as well as their excellent recon and sight spells. Although wizards do not possess any healing magic, their offensive spells more than make up for that weakness.
