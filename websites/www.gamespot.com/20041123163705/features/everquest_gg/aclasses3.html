Basic Survival

Levels 1-5
Bards can advance to level 5 relatively easily playing solo. After joining your guild, go out and fight until you have saved up enough money to buy a song from your guild's shop. The first song you can buy is Chant of Battle, which increases your armor value and makes you stronger. Use that as your default song; if you can't decide on which song to use, at least sing Chant. Your singing ability will soon rise into the 20s by the time you reach level 5, so sing it nonstop while you run around. There is a song for every level, and you should save you money for them before you advance. At level 5, you have the chance to learn the song Selo's Accelerando, which makes you and your group run faster. This is a must-have song for any bard and can get you out of a fight that may be going badly.

Levels 6-9
Advancing from this point on can be very slow if you play solo. Your best way to advance is to get in a group and fight some monsters that may be beyond your solo skills. Your songs will benefit the group, and the tougher monsters will still provide you with some experience. At level 6, you learn Hymn of Restoration. This is the best bard song, at least in the first 10 levels. Singing this song provides a constant regeneration of health for you and your group as long as the song is played. In a party that changes who is taking damage, this song works well because it lets players take damage, heals while someone else takes the damage, and so on. Once the fight is over, get our your lute and start playing. The increased effectiveness using the lute will soon heal your entire party to full health.

Levels 10+
Much like advancing from levels 6 to 9, your advancement rate from here on out will be largely dependent on your group play. Anthem de Arms, which you get at level 10, increases the group's strength and more importantly - attack speed! The increase in speed is hard to notice at times, but it is there, and you will notice it in groups that have fighters whose hit rate is good. If you are in a group that is fighting a lot of monsters and not taking a lot of damage, you will find this song may work better even than Hymn of Restoration.
