Monsters

While animals can be either passive or aggressive, the monsters you will face are almost never friendly. Some may decide to ignore you, but be on guard, most will decide you look like a good snack.

Many of the monsters you will encounter are intelligent to some degree, and they are likely to carry a variety of items. Depending on certain factors, these critters will typically possess a random collection of loot. In general, the high-level monster it is, the better loot it will have. Typical loot from monsters can include currency, packs, or pouches as well as armor and weapons. If you attack a magic-using monster, it will cast spells on you and will continue to do so if you decide to flee. When you're fighting these monsters, it's best to either have some magic resistance or some friends who can help soften the blows of the battle.

This monster reference was compiled through the blood and sweat of many brave adventurers. For all those who have lived to tell another tale, there are many who have not returned. Rumors abound of vampires, dragons, and devils, but nobody has brought forth proof of their existence.
