Quests

Quests can come in two different types: NPC quests and player-run quests. Player-run quests are those that are put on by players or game masters (GMs). This guide is meant to give you the knowledge and skills that you need as a player to find and enjoy the many NPC quests that have been created in the world of Norrath. The creators of EverQuest have created a network of quests that are specific to race, class, and character level. They make use of special NPCs, secret passages, hidden areas in zones, special tools that are supplied for that quest, and special quest items that are only available as a quest completion reward.

Quests can take hours, weeks, or even months. Sometimes, you must travel clear across Norrath to see them completed. However the rewards are often great and can include items, experience, and better standing in the eyes of others (that is, increased faction standing).

NPC Quests
As you advance through the world of Norrath, you will meet new NPCs. There are three main types of NPCs: vendors, common NPCs, and quest NPCs. Vendors generally do not get involved in quests. An easy way to check if an NPC is a vendor or a quest NPC is to get close to him and right-click or use them. If a vendor menu comes up, he will probably not be of assistance in a quest. Common NPCs are the ones without a special name. For example, a goblin is a common NPC. Some NPCs have special names, with a surname. This is an indication that this NPC might give you some sort of quest.

Generally, it is best to hail an NPC by saying, "Hail ," or by targeting the NPC and pressing the H key. The NPC will generally respond and perhaps make a comment on certain things. You can pick key words out from the NPC's response and repeat them. This will in turn elicit more information from the NPC if he has is any more information to give. It is through these means that you will eventually be given a quest, if the NPC has one for you.

TIP
You must respond to with the exact keywords the NPC gives you, otherwise you may not get any quest information! The keywords are usually put in brackets []. If you reply using the words in the brackets, the NPC will provide more information on the quest.
