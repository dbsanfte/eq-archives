Basic Survival

Levels 1-3
When you must fight the smaller creatures, use lifetap to drain them down to nothing. Unlike any other class, you actually heal yourself by killing others. When you have attained level 2 and accumulated enough money, purchase cavorting bones, your summoning spell. You'll need some bone chips to cast the spell, so kill a decaying skeleton or two to get started. Though this pet will be weak, he'll work for your early levels.

Levels 4-7
At this level, you should purchase leering corpse, which gives you a much more powerful pet. You also get meditate, which will let you spend more time in combat and less time waiting for mana. Since you have adequate pets, you still do not need to join a group to advance.

Levels 8+
At level 8, you can purchase bone walk, which summons your most powerful pet to date. Though it may seem obvious, do not let your pet do all the fighting, as you can step in to take some damage and then step back out to regenerate health. At this level and above, grouping is an option for this dark and powerful class.
