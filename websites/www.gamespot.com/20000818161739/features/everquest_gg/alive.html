Chapter 5
Staying Alive

So you've completed your guild quest and received your guild tunic. Cherish that piece of armor, for it will be a while before you can afford to replace it with something better. You've also explored a little of your hometown and have a feel for the game interface and controls.

Well, enough of the busy streets and idle chat. It's time for some action, and the denizens of Norrath await a chance to get a piece of your warm flesh. If you don't get killed at least a dozen times or so when you venture outside town, you are either very lucky, or you've already read this chapter.

Be Wise as Thou Art Cruel

The land of Norrath is full of danger. Monsters will attack you because they have something against your race or class or simply because you look at them wrong. Guards will sentence you to death for even the most minor infraction, and even small animals can kill you if you're not careful. What follows are examples of treacherous situations as experienced by Burning Heart members who have learned about life in Norrath the hard way.
