Paladin

The soldier of light is both warrior and cleric. Paladins give their very life to honor and goodness and the relentless pursuit of all that is holy. A paladin is truly dedicated to the creed of the knight, combined with zealous dedication to God. The paladin's paradox is that he is constantly trying to reconcile the aloof nature befitting one of his station and the humility demanded by his deity.

Available races

Dwarf, erudite, half elf, high elf, human

Primary statistics

Strength, stamina, wisdom, charisma

Starting equipment

Short sword, Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, and five loaves of bread

Skills available

Abjure (9), alter (9), baking (1), beg (1), bind wound (10), channel (9), conjure (9), divinate (9), dodge (10), evoke (9), fishing (1), fletching (1), hand to hand (1), jewel craft (1), meditate (14), one-hand blunt (1), pottery (1), sense heading (1), smithing (1), swimming (1), tailoring (1), two-hand blunt (1), one-hand slash (1), two-hand slash (1), bash (12), double attack (20), parry (17), riposte (30), brewing (1), alcohol tolerance (1), taunt (1), lay hands (1), disarm (40)

Spells available

Courage, minor healing, cure poison, spook the dead, Yaulp, holy armor, light healing, cure disease, ward undead, sense the dead, reckless strength, center, invigorate, Hammer of Wrath, healing, stun, invisibility to undead, endure poison, expulse undead
