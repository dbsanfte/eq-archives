Creating a Character
Magicians come from the races of the dark elf, erudite, gnome, high elf, and human. Magicians must be intelligent and have sufficient stamina.

Getting Started
You start out with a dagger, the standard food and water, guild note, two spell scrolls (Burst of Flame and minor shielding) and the Tome of Discord.

Your first order of business is to officially join your class's guild. Read your guild note and then search around. You might want to type /who mag to see if there are any magicians around who can point you in the right direction. When you find your guild master, hand him the guild note and click Find to choose a few skills you wish to train in. Don't train in battle skills unless you do not have the skill listed on the right. If that is the case, just spend a single point on the skill. Train once in each of the magical skills that correspond to the starting spells (minor shielding and Burst of Flame; you train once in both abjuration and evocation).

Beginning Adventuring Tips
Magicians are the most well-rounded of the magic-using classes. Magicians can create necessary supplies such as food, water, weapons, bandages, and sacks at will. This lets them save more of their money for other things. They have decent offensive spells and very handy damage shields. Their ability to summon powerful elementals makes them a melee force to be reckoned with. They have the capability of going solo better than most other classes, as they can summon a melee partner.
