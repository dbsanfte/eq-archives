Gnome
Gnomes are the smallest of all races in Norrath and are typically considered uglier than dwarves or halflings. Their white hair and thick brown skin give them an appearance that is unmistakable. The gnomes are the mechanical gurus in Norrath, as their creations show a technology not seen anywhere else in the land. Gnomes are considered "small" and wear small sized armor and clothing. They possess ultravision and begin play in Ak'Anon. Their special power includes the improved Tinkering skill.

Half Elf
Half-Elves are the only race formed as offspring to two primary races of Norrath. Part elf, and part human, the half-elf is a perfect blend of each. They are taller than most elves, yet shorter than humans. Their facial appearance is more human, with the exception of their pointed ears. Half-Elves are considered "medium" and wear medium sized armor and clothing. They possess infravision and begin play in Felwithe, Freeport, Kelethin, or Qeynos (depending on your class and deity selection). They possess no special abilities.
