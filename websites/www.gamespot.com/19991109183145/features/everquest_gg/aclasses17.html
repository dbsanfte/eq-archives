Creating a Character
Monks must be human. Agnostic Monks start in Qeynos at the Silent Fist Clan guild. Monks who follow Quellius start in Freeport at the Theater of the Tranquil. Your primary attributes are strength, to increase your melee damage, and agility, to boost your armor and running speed.

Getting Started
Your first quest is to join the monk guild. Read your guild note and then search around your starting city. The Silent Fist Clan in is North Freeport; the Theater of the Tranquil is in West Freeport. When you find your guild master, hand him the guild note and receive your very cool-looking guild tunic. I suggest spending one of your initial training sessions each in sense heading, hand-to-hand, mend, and kick. After that, (except for putting one point to learn new skills), you will never need training your combat skills, as they will rise nicely in your adventures. It is better to save your points for things like feign death and trade skills.
