Basic Survival

Levels 1-3
Since you have little in the way of armor, the Minor Shielding spell is important. Never enter combat without a shielding spell in effect. Only take on easy prey at the lowest levels, as your combat and casting skills are meek. Target your prey with a burst of flame to weaken it and draw it to you. Kill it with your dagger or staff if you can, reserving your mana to strike it down as it flees or use it against something that wants to eat you. Learn to deal with opponents in a melee and save your mana for when it's most needed. In the future, your pets will fight, but up to level 4, you must do it yourself. Learn how to time your spells so that your adversary's strikes don't interrupt your casting.

Levels 4-7
At this level, you will be mastering the use of pets in a melee. Every pet you summon will be different, not only in strength stamina and damage but in temperament. Some are frustrating cowards, and some are heroes you will miss when they go. At this level, you can gain experience fast by letting your pet kill things for you. Learn to save your mana for when your pet needs supporting fire.

Levels 8+
Lesser shielding, a more powerful version of your early protection spell, becomes available as well as more powerful pets. The period from levels 8 to 12 can be a time of difficulty, as foes in this level tend to run in numbers. At level 12, you finally start to get area effect spells (such as column of fire) like a wizard and can start your career as a mage with a tank.
