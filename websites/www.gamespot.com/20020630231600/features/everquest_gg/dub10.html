Human
Humans are the typical, run of the mill two legged being with average abilities. Humans are considered "medium" and wear medium sized armor and clothing. They do not possess night vision and begin play in Qeynos or Freeport. Humans gain levels faster than all other races.

Ogre
Ogres are huge, ugly and mean. They tower over all other races and would sink a fishing boat with their mass. Needless to say, they are very strong, very tough and very dumb. Kill and eat seems to be the rule of the day for the typical ogre. Ogres are considered "large" and wear large sized armor and clothing. They possess ultravision and begin play in Oggok. They are considered evil by NPCs.
