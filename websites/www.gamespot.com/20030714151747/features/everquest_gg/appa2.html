Faydwer
Faydwer is the crescent shaped island to the east of Antonica. Faydwer is the home of the dwarves, high elves, wood elves, and gnomes. Faydwer is also rumored to be the home of two of the oldest creatures on all of Norrath, Mayong Mistmoore, the elf vampire, and Phinigel Autropos, the last living kedge. The name Faydwer was given to this continent by the high elves of Antonica when they landed on its shores upon their departure from Antonica.
