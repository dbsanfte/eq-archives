Neriak
The underground city of Neriak is as beautiful as it is ominous. There are countless tales and mysteries surrounding the city of Neriak, as many as there are stars in the sky. Home to the dark elves, Neriak also welcomes several other evil races of Norrath including ogres, trolls and a few humans. Like Erudin, Neriak is a very magical city, and there are many colorful phantasms and neon lights spread throughout the city.

Neriak offers one of the most unique taverns in all of Norrath - the Blind Fish. This tavern features a two-story aquarium built right into the wall of the bar. There are also brightly colored tapestries and dartboards adorning the walls.

Neriak
