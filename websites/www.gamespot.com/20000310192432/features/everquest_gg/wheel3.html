Memorizing Spells
To cast spells in EverQuest, they must first be scribed into your spellbook and then memorized. To begin memorizing spells, switch to windowed mode. Above your view button, you will see a button labeled Spells and above that a picture of a book. Clicking on either of these will bring up your spellbook. Once open, you will see your spellbook, a button labeled Meditate with two bars on either side of it, and a picture of your current inventory on the bottom right.

If you are a spell caster, you will see a series of four scrolls in your inventory, the top two of which are your spell scrolls. Holding down your right mouse button on a scroll will show you a description of the scroll's contents. Left clicking on it once will pick the scroll up. You can take a spell scroll to one of the empty boxes in the pages of your spellbook and click within the box to start learning the scroll. The right hand bar will fill with red. As soon as the red has left the bar, you will have learned the spell, and the spell will appear in the book.

Once the spell is in your book, you can hold the right mouse button down on the spell icon to see the name of the spell and a brief description of what skills it uses, its mana cost, and what level it is. Along the left-hand border of your book, you will see a series of eight notches. These are the places where you can place spells you've memorized.

TIP
Scribe spells in your spellbook as soon as you buy them. When you die, you do not lose spells in your spellbook, you merely must memorize them again.

To memorize a spell, you must left click on the spell icon in your spellbook once, and you will create a pill. Take this pill to one of the available eight slots and click once more. The left-hand bar under your book will fill with red and as soon as the red clears, you will have memorized the spell. That space where you put the spell will soon have a button that will let you cast the spell placed on it.

NOTE
Certain spells, in particular Divine Aura, may have a long period between casting times. This period must pass before the spell can appear. Most of your spells will reappear within seconds after initial casting, but in some cases, it may be longer.

Below the book, you will also notice the Meditate button and left and right arrow keys. The left and right arrow keys are used to scroll the pages of your book. The Meditate button is used when you have achieved advanced levels; it is used to get mana back faster. When you're done using your spellbook, press Done at the bottom right-hand corner of the screen to return back to the windowed mode. The spells button, as well as each of the individual memorized spell spots can be made into a hotkey button by placing the cursor over the desired item and holding your left mouse button down on it and dragging it to your hotkey box.

TIP
Spells are cast by clicking on the spell icon or activating the hotkey that contains the spell icon.
