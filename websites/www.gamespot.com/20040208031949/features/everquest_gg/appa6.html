Erudin
Erudin is commonly known as the "High City" and is located on the bluff overlooking Toxxulia Forest. Erudin contains, perhaps, the greatest accumulation of knowledge in all Norrath. The erudites, or high men, are blessed with an innate magical aptitude and appreciate the finer things in life. The city of Erudin reflects such appreciation, with its highly polished marble architecture and elaborate fountains. Erudin is also littered with a unique form of transportation - teleports. There are a number of teleporter gems scattered around the city, making travel a magical experience that can churn the stomach of the magic leery warrior. Toxxulia Forest is one of the darkest forests in the land, so do not venture out of town without a trusty guide and a pack full of torches.


Erudin
The architecture in Erudin makes the entire city a traveler's dream. However, there are still certain places that you should make an effort to see, starting with the famed Erudin Library. The stacks here contain a wealth of information compiled through the ages. The Erudite's appreciation for fine things extends to jewelry and gem crafting as well, so be sure to check Sothure's Fine Gems. Always on the lookout for a fine ale, the Vasty Deep Inn is the place to rest and socialize while in Erudin. Regular ferry service is offered between Erudin and Qeynos.
