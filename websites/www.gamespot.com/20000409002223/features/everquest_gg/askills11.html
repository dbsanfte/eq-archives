Fletching

This skill allows you to construct a wide variety of bows and arrows. A fletching kit is needed to craft the items.

Making Bows
Better bows are made from better components. To make a bow, you must combine the following components in a fletching kit.



String

Use a piece of (in order of difficulty and quality) hemp twine, string, or silk

Lumber

Use any of this lumber (in order of difficulty and quality): hickory, elm, ash, oak, darkwood, steel, and ceramic

Cams (optional)

Most bows are not built with cams. Composite bows use either one or two cams

Tools (optional)

A whittling blade will increase the quality of your bow, while a planing tool will create the highest-quality bow

Making Arrows
Better arrows are made from better components. To make an arrow, you must combine the following components in a fletching kit.

Nock

There are three types of nocks, differentiated by the size of their grooves. The larger the groove, the easier the arrow is to make. The smaller the groove, the farther the arrow will travel

Shaft

The shaft can be made from one of several materials (in order of difficulty and quality): wood, bone, porcelain, and steel

Tip

There are several types of arrow tips that can be crafted onto the arrow (in order of difficulty and quality): pointed tip, hooked tip, and bladed tip

Fletch

The feathering of an arrow influences the distance it will travel. There are several patterns (in order of difficulty and quality): round cut, parabolic cut, and the shield cut
