Basic Survival

Levels 1-5
Stay close to the cities and guarded areas, since you may need the guards' help during these trying times. Once you get past level 3, start adventuring outside the city to see what the rest of the world has to offer. Also, start forming up groups to help hunt down and kill things. Your experience will go up more quickly if you battle more than you stand around. Form effective groups that consist of a mage, cleric, and a few warriors to pound on the monsters.

Levels 5-6
Remember to train in kick. Now that you're starting to get good at the fighting aspect of being a ranger, venture forth and explore more areas and visit a few dungeons. Be on the look out for gold. Find other places to adventure and keep an eye out for things that can do you serious harm. Fight some bears, large spiders, orcs, and so on your own. These will help get your experience moving at a faster rate. Keep a watchful eye on your hit points. When your health dips below half, start looking for help. Use your tracking to find people to help you, and remember that you can move and track at the same time. Remember all the times you were practicing tracking in the lower levels? Now you'll start to see what you can do with it. Sense heading is another good skill to use at this time and can help you get your bearings back if you get spun around a few times when running away from trouble.

Levels 7-8
By now, your kicking skill and offensive and defensive abilities are starting to make you a viable warrior and someone who can start to do some decent damage. You should have a tailor kit and be fully suited up in your own set of leather armor by now. Start finding more difficult beasts to attack and search out groups, since they can help you advance and tackle the larger monsters - even if they are yellow or red. Keep in mind that some animals will attack in groups. When you're out and about on your own, other beasts might show up and help if they are close enough. Do not let your ego get the best of you at this time or you will become dragon bait rather quickly. Start to roam other lands now and venture forth to other cities. You're the one who roams the lands and lives in the woods - now get out and explore!
