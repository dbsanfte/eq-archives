Troll
Trolls are slightly smaller than ogres, but are even uglier (if that is possible). Their green skin and lanky arms often make them more unpleasant than the huge ogre. They are extremely strong, but ugly. Trolls are considered "large" and wear large sized armor and clothing. They possess infravision and begin play in Grobb. Special abilities include the Regeneration and Slam skills. They are considered evil by NPCs.

Wood Elf
Wood Elves are the smallest of the elven races, yet retain the striking facial features and pointed ears. They are not as strong as humans, yet are very lithe and quite charming. Life in the forest has it's advantages, as they are very agile, dexterous and intelligent. Wood Elves are considered "small/medium" and wear either small or medium sized armor and clothing. They possess infravision and begin play in Kelethin. Special abilities include the improved Forage and Hide skills.
