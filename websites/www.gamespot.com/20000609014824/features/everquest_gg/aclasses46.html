Creating a Character
Shadow Knights can be of a several races, including human, dark elf, ogre, or troll. Your race decision should be based on how you wish to role play your character, since a disciple of death can be developed from any of the available races. Strength and stamina are important for any shadow knight, so when adding stat points, distribute many of them there. If you plan on focusing on your necromancy later on (level 9 and higher), adding some points to intelligence will help, but remember that you are first and foremost a fighter. Agility and dexterity will also be of good use. Trolls and ogres are killed on sight in most areas of Norrath, so choosing either will be somewhat of a challenge. The added fear factor these races evoke, however, may indeed offset this disadvantage. Also, both races have a special attack called "slam" that adds to their offensive arsenal. Trolls also regenerate much more quickly when wounded. Human shadow knights, though much more widely accepted by the population, lack the night vision capabilities of the trolls and ogres and the better-still ultravision of the dark elves.

Human shadow knights begin in either Freeport or Qeynos. The other races start only in their races' home city.

When you arrive in Norrath, all you will have is a dagger, some food and water, a Tome of Discord, and an introductory letter to your guild master. The Tome of Discord, if read and brought to the Priest of Discord, shows you the way of chaos and lets you kill (and be killed by) other players. Even if you plan on following this path, bank the tome for now and get some experience first.
