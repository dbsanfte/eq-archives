Other Areas
There are some smaller, uncharted islands that occupy the various seas of Norrath. Rumor has is that some may even be inhabited by religious sects and various assortments of unknown life forms.

Cities

The following tips and maps should prove helpful as you negotiate your way through the twisting streets of the cities of Norrath. You'll find that each city has its own architectural styles and eccentricities. Be sure to slow down enough to capture the flavor each city has to offer, as they are one of the best-developed aspects of EverQuest. It's very easy to get lost, and it's best to know a reference point in each city in the event you get turned around. Try not to get frustrated, as there is always something new and exciting around every corner.
