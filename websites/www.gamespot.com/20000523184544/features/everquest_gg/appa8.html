Grobb
Grobb is the home of trolls and lies in the depths of the Innothule Swamp. Trolls are not one of the more aesthetically pleasing of the races, and their city reflects that as well. The squeamish need not venture into Grobb, as the sparse decorations take second fiddle to the assorted array of body parts strewn around the pathways. Aside from the grotesque decorations, beware of the numerous lava pits that are scattered around the city. One misstep, and you'll be dinner for some lucky troll.


Grobb
Grobb isn't exactly a tourist's delight. In fact, trolls are aggressive toward all other races in Norrath. Trolls would rather kill and eat than build buildings, so there are no architectural wonders to be found here. Grobb does host a bank as well as some of the less-reputable guild halls.
