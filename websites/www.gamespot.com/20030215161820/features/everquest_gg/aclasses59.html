Basic Survival

Levels 1-5
Your first order of business should be to purchase a shield. Kill anything that yields good loot, as you will need it. After the shield, save for leather armor, which you can have a friend make if you save up enough pelts. You start with the kick skill, which gives you a second attack. Use it as often as possible in combat and don't stop until you have mastered this skill (which won't happen until you are quite high in levels). Practice party skills and tactics, since you will be the centerpiece of any good party. Make sure your taunt skill is high, so use it often.

Levels 6-9
You get the dodge skill at level 6, which is automatic. It gets you out of the way of the weapon or monster appendage that is trying to dismember you and lets you avoid damage. You should be fully outfitted in leather armor and saving up for better armor, which is quite expensive.

Combat Skills

Train first on only one combat skill, either one-hand slash or blunt. Use just one training point, as the skill will rise quickly after that with repeated use.

Though not all weapons are known, at lower level, a one-handed weapon is best, since it is much faster than the two-handed weapons. As you attain higher levels, this speed advantage is less of a factor and can even mean your smaller weapon cannot harm a creature - you'll need a two-handed weapon to do any damage to larger monsters.

Taunt
This skill is quite useless at lower levels, but becomes very important at higher levels, once fighting in a party is crucial. Use it during a fight often and keep using it to increase the skill for later.

Dodge
It functions automatically in combat once you've trained in the basics, so you don't need to activate it. Basically, if it succeeds, the monster misses. You gain this ability once you reach level 6. Train only once.

Bind Wound
This is a very useful skill, since it gives you the ability to heal yourself and others. This is not really a healing spell, but it gives you another way to heal yourself and those in need. This skill is available right away for warriors, but you must use the skill to advance.

Double Attack
Double attack functions automatically in combat once you've trained in the basics, so you don't need to activate it. Basically, it gives you the ability to hit twice during a normal battle sequence. You gain this ability once you reach level 20. Train only once.

Disarm
This is a wonderful skill, which you get when you reach level 15. The ability to lessen the amount of melee damage the enemy can dish out can turn the tide of a battle rather quickly.
