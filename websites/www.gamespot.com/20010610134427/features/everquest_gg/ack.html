About the Burning Heart

The Burning Heart is a guild of role-players formed in 1996 prior to the mainstream appearance of the graphic, online role-playing games. The members are hand picked and consist of dedicated role-players, many of whom were spawns of AD&D, MUDS, or other pre-Internet games. We participate in EverQuest for more than just the hack-and-slash gameplay so common in computer gaming.

The guild was founded on the principles of truth, trust, and honor, and we fight for the weak and oppressed. We seek to uphold the eight virtues introduced in the distant realm of Britannia: honesty, compassion, valor, justice, sacrifice, honor, spirituality, and humility. We are a tight knit family and, as is often the case with true friendships, respect and trust are earned - not granted.

The Burning Heart can be found role-playing on the Innoruuk server of EverQuest, and we base many of our operations out of the Rivervale area. Feel free to look us up in-game or visit the Burning Heart web site.

Disclaimer

The information contained in this guide was gathered by the members of the Burning Heart guild, a guild dedicated to the furtherance of good gaming in EverQuest. While we have done our best to be thorough and complete, but we realize some things are not contained in this guide. We don't seek to give out all the secrets, but rather to equip you with knowledge and experience to help enrich your stay in Norrath.

EverQuest will continue to evolve after its release. Stories will be introduced, new lands discovered, and grand battles and wars waged. It is our hope that through all the changes in the game, this guide will continue to be a valuable resource.
