Chapter 7
Creatures - Gnolls, Goblins and Griffins, Oh My!

The world of Norrath is filled with interesting and talkative humanoid characters from cobblers and gemologists to guards and kings. Amid the forests, mountains, plains, and dungeons of Norrath exist a myriad of monsters and animals that you will, no doubt, encounter during your journeys.

This chapter provides a brief look at the living creatures found in various parts of the realm. Many of the animals and monsters come in a variety of forms, from the small, easy-to-kill version, to the spell-casting version that waits to snack on your body. The information provided should serve as a guide as to whether you should even attempt a battle.

Animals

The land is rich with wildlife, and the animals found in Norrath are much the same as you would expect to see in any realm. For each animal, we've included some details including where the animal can be found, its aggression, danger rating (the level at which you can expect to have a chance of defeating it), and what can be found in terms of loot. Norrath has many unexplored territories, and there may exist animals that are not contained here.
