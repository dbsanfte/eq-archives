Buying and Selling with NPCs

Buying and selling is easily done in Norrath. You can browse a vendor by right clicking on it. Make sure you do not accidentally attack vendors, they are exceptional combatants. Having your warrior kicked to death by a barmaid is not something you want to experience.

The left side of your screen will change to show the merchandise available. Use the arrow keys to scroll back and forth through the merchandise available. The right side of your screen will show your normal inventory display. You can get a description of any item by holding down your right mouse button on it. Race, charisma, and faction play an important role in the deals you will get from NPC vendors. Vendors in Norrath sell high and buy low, which has created thriving player-driven economics. Buying and selling from another player will usually result in a better deal, and some players have taken the role of merchant to heart.

Buying
Left click on the item you are interested in purchasing, and the merchant will quote you a price. Click Purchase to complete the transaction, and the item will be moved to your inventory. Make sure you have an open inventory slot. If it is a stackable item, it will stack with like items up to 20. If you need several of the item, click Purchase until you have enough. The vendor will try to take payment is the smallest coins you have available first.

Selling
If you left click on any item in your inventory, the shopkeeper will make you an offer for it. Click on Sell if the price is acceptable. If it is a stack of items, the vendor will offer you a price per item, and when you click Sell, a scrollbar will appear, letting you select the amount to sell. If you sell a large number of items, you will notice that the vendor's math is a bit shy of the quoted price. Think of this as giving him a large purchase discount.
