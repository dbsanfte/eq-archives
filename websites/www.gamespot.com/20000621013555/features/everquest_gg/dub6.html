Barbarian
Barbarians are rumored to be descendents of Humans, albeit much larger and more rugged. They thrive in the colder northlands, and call Halas their home. Due to their relative isolation from other races, Barbarians tend to lack some of the refinement in culture that is found in some of the other, older races. Barbarians are considered "large" and wear large sized armor and clothing. They possess no night vision and begin play in Halas. Their special power is the Slam skill.

Dark Elf
The dark elf is one of the more despised races in all of Norrath. Their inherent evil creation is an affront to the other elves who inhabit Norrath and will typically be attacked on sight. Living underground in Neriak, their eyes are completely adjusted to darkness and their skin and hair reflect their below ground heritage with a bluish tint. Dark Elves are considered "small" and wear small sized armor and clothing. They possess ultravision and begin play in Neriak. Their special power is improved Hiding skill. Disadvantages include being considered evil by NPCs.
