Bow and Arrows
Bow and arrows have the advantage of letting you attack from a distance. If your aim is true, you may never even get within arms length of your opponent. Note that the total range of a bow and arrow is the sum of the range of the bow and the arrow.

Bow and arrows require both hands, thus a shield cannot be used. The following classes can use bow and arrows: rogue, ranger, shadow knight, and warrior.
