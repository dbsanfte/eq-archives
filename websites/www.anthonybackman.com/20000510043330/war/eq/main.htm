As the weeks go by in Everquest many are getting to the point where more than 1 - 6 member group is required to accomplish the objectives at hand. I would like to begin to document these greater tasks and note the tactics that work so that very tight teams can be formed. In a perfect world we can begin to see organized 10-12 groups of 6 operating in a state of order and precision.

NOTICE: This is a reference site. If you are looking for a site that is updated with facts of the day and/or discussions on patches you will not find it here. If you are looking for that verity of information please consult the links section below to direct you to an adequately staffed EQ site that specializes in this type of minutia. If you are looking to join in discussions on tactics and/or want information on what observations have been made on group tactics and mission objectives then you have come to the right place.
