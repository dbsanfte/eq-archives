Being locked up

 

 

Here's the pathing chart again for reference. See at the opening of safe hall where 2 wurms and Hoshkar path? Well, luckily they can be killed until they respawn as non-see invisible. The bad news is that Hoskar is only ripe for pulling at that bottle neck by the camp. At full speed Hoshkar can be gone in about 8-10 seconds. Considering that, look at the situation we have to deal with in the pic below.

 

 

See the 2 red dots and the green one? That meets right at our doorstep. If one or both of the red guys are there, that is a no go. Problem is, it happens a lot, one way or another. The top one will be coming when the bottom ones leaving, Both are walking towards camp when Hosh comes, one turns around… tons of reasons to call a no go for safety. That keeps us out of the element of controlling the environment unless we kill 2 wurms every 2 min, and that's not likely to happen. Take a look at what happens when we move up.

 

 

The blue dot and line is the path we would take to get into position. Dead Man Floating or Levitation would be required for this, obviously for the lava that we would utilize as our safe zone. If we all get invis and run to the lair and the wurm is in the back, as in the diagram, we can skirt right on by him and over the pool. Wurms have very limited agro ranges, you have to be on top of them to agro. Within reason of course, but comparatively speaking you have to be very close to set them off.

 

WORST CASE:

What About Hoshkar!!!

Hoshkar has a long ass rout all the way down to zone in and his lair that we are next to is at the waaaay other end of that route. So by the time he passes us on the way down, he's got to go all the way down and all the way back, a process I say I know takes more than 15 min, but in actuality it's cycle is more in the neighborhood of 20 min. So don't worry about him coming back unless by the time he gets back it won't matter if you are kos or not because we will have beat the puss out of a wurm. At least the fight is on our terms.
