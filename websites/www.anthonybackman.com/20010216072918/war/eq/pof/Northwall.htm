BREAK IN STRATAGY

 

North Camp

 

Depending on the numbers we can get more specific on strategy. Aegis Terra on Tarew Marr (thanks to Thumpa and Saerli) was able to break the patrol with around 16 folks so weather or not it's possible is really a moot point, it's just how fast you can move from there. If you have numbers in the 50's you could make short work of it as long as most of the casters split into 2 or 3 groups and alternate the burn cycle.

 

Feign Death (FD) pullers will be a factor, Enchanters will be a factor, Clerics will be a factor, all are encouraged to come.

 

West wall camp is too dangerous. It may be a possibility if we have 4+ enchanters and a good working grasp of the assist key.

 

The default plan is a north wall camp. This plan calls for a very seasoned crew of Fear terrain at least for the patrol break up. If you don't  know Fear well for the love of God wait until the patrol is broken. If you start at 7pst I imagine that safe time would be around 9-10 pst.

 

    Objectives:

    Kill patrol

    work toward the Gorgan tower

    use gorg tower as a base to kill the 3 mini bosses

    Kill lower Mobs

    kill temple

    work to Cazic

    Kill Cazic

 

If you get a glut of folks I hope that they will cooperate and do rounds in waves non-stop. If this is the case you can assign wave leaders once patrol is broken. If you're not up, sit down, don't move or cast. Every bit contributes to link death after 50 people so be warned.

 

SUICIDE RUN

 

A very fat tank needs full armor, spirit of the wolf, and lots of buffs on to pull the entire patrol with him/her on a wild goose chase to the north thus claering the port for the wave behind. 2 or more may be used but it is better to save extra tanks to be buffers near the North camp in case a cleric pulls some heat and is getting hit.

 

This was the plan I posted for a raid on Tarew Marr that was able to break and hold the zone with minimal casualties:

 

    I will do a suicide run to the north wall and draw fire (patrol mobs) until we have a good core of folks in the camp spot 12:00 of the fire ring at the

    temple. The second wave should have a few clerics and a few tanks, if I miss mobs on my first past the other tanks should grab the mob

    and join me in my suicide circle in the NW. Once the core clerics make it to the north camp, camp. I will die in the NW corner and wait for

    my FD puller to get my corpse. Other groups should follow immediately after the core clerics for 4 reasons.

 

    1) More fodder for the patrol to get distracted on while the clerics camp

    2) More of a start up team to take down the glare lord should he be there when the re log procedure starts

    3) More people to to relog on before the clerics to make sure the coast is clear

    4) The window of opportunity to run in free of a patrol will only last about 1:30 before the patrol closes up again. After that window you

    must die at the patrol to join the break in or wait until the patrol is clear.

 

    If you agro the patrol go die in the NW corner and don't train the camp. Go in invis. because if I do my job right all the

    see invis mobs will be chasing me around the north end of the zone.

 

    If you go in the first wave here is what to expect:

 

    I will get some buffs and wolf and port in. About 10-20 seconds later someone in my group will give a GO! This means zone in.

 

    Once you zone you will see an hr glass to the right of you hovering in the air. Make a b-line for it. When you get close to it (not too close

    mind you, that tower has crap that wants to kill you) head north toward the fire wall. Once there skirt the outside of the firewall to the

    left (clockwise) until directly 12:00. There is a glare lord that cycles through this spot once every 2 hrs or so, but is the only real obstacle

    here. If a scarling happens to get there by negligence run, don’t walk with it to the west corner. Scarling is the only monkey wrench in

    this as they can port you into the firewall and bring the whole Amygalian Army on top of us.

 

    Once we stabilize the North camp we can proceed to take down the shiverbacks and spine chillers first. Then move on to scarlings

    proceeding with extreme caution. Scarlings should be mem blured because once you get high on the agro list they start porting you

    around.

 

    About getting ported:

    You will always be able to see the camp from where you are ported or feared. Don't panic. Look around 360 to check your agro, if you

    picked something up go kite it in the NW corner until we can eat it or you die and we rez you later.

 

 

A WALK THROUGH OF THE ROUTE TO THE NORTH WALL CAMP

 

 

Ta da! The port in! Here is a long shot of the portal just to give you some perspective on the hill it rests on. The next shot takes you into the first person perspective of immediately entering the zone.

 

 

 

OK, you just got invisible cast on you and ran through the port on the Feerrott side and loaded in. This is EXACTLY what you see. Notice that Hour Glass? That is your first visual indicator. Head directly toward it to begin the run to the camp spot. Do not pause; do not look around to see if anything is coming. Assume something is on you and that you only have about 5 seconds to live.

 

 

 

Down the hill you can see the base of the first tower, don't get too close as there are nasties about the base of it. The tree to the left of center is a good waypoint on your run up to the north. See the fire wall in the distance? You're getting warmer...

 

 

 

This is looking left from the same point as you were from the picture just above. That house is the Fetid Frenzy house. Get too close and you pile on 4 more very angry high damage mobs into the death train. Most likely the suicide runner will have caught the edge of this house and the mobs will be chasing him/her to the north already. Keep running north!

 

 

 

A bit further North and you can see the beginning of the graveyard to the right. If you get too close you will pick up a large Tactical Terror, closer still and the Dracolite will be on your tail. Keep the fire wall to your right as you continue running north and it should be just fine.

 

 

 

After clearing the graveyard and the Fetid house keep that fire wall to your right and you will come to a clearing where there is nothing of consequence. This is your running area to kite the mobs if you are the suicide runner. The only obstacles here may be the remainder of the patrol mobs at the very edge of the patrol. Keep heading north and the fire wall will start to curve to the east around the top of the temple. If you are going to the camp, follow it to the east to about 12:00 of the temple. If you are a runner/ shield keep going north into the corner.

 

 

 

 

This is what the North West corner looks like. Be warned that what looks like open space may actually be an invisible zone wall. Find your boundaries and start making a large loop all around the NW corner for as long as you can if you are the suicide runner.

 

 

CONGRATULATIONS YOU MADE IT!

 

 

So once all have camped to clear agro you are now ready to work from the North Wall Camp. Have your FD pullers grab the corpses from the NW corner and rez them into the fold then you are ready to go. Above you can see a panoramic view of where you will be safe on the North Wall. To the left you have photoplasm, nightmares, and Cazic if he is up. To the right you have the outer reaches of the Patrol. If you can get a core group up here you can kill off the patrol entirely from here then send word to back ups that the zone is in fact "BROKEN"

 

 

 

A FEW MORE NOTES

 

Glare Lord

Every so often the North Wall Camp will have a Glare Lord that spawns in the camp. It's hits hard, fears you, and can strip all your buffs very fast. Once you get to the camp be prepared for this fact and always be ready to take him down.

 

Scarelings

When you take down the patrol you want to try and kill them as far northwest as possible. The reason for this is that once you encounter Scarelings they have a chance to port you into the firewall of the temple. This will cause a chain reaction and pull all the knights and warriors from the temple on to your camp. Needless to say... you don't want this. If it happens camp a few clerics for the Corpse retrieval and try to take down the scarling in camp so it can't do it to you again.

 

Cazic Thule

IF Cazic is up and you go too far east you stand a slim chance of agroing him thus bringing not only a death touch to the party that agroed him, but a premature fight that you stand no chance of winning. If Cazic is agroed at this point he will call the ENITER zone to help and since you just began the break in this would be the equivalent of Armageddon.
