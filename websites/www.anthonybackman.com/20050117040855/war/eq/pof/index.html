The Plane of Fear is the neighborhood of Cazic Thule, the God of Fear, and all his minions. This is the zone that has the plane armor drops for 1/2 the classes of Everquest. I do not plan on telling you all the secrets to this zone because. come on... what fun is that? But I will give you some tactics on how to get in. Breaking and clearing this zone is one of the most satisfying accomplishments of the game, I wish you luck.
