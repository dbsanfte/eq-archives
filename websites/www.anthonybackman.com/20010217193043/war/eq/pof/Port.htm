The Port in Feerrott

 

 

Here you see the beginning of a very long night. In a quiet, desolate corner of the Feerrott woods there is a cave guarded by specters. Upon entering the cave, if you take a series of rights you eventually come to an open area with the portal you see above. This will be the first staging area for the initial break in. Do not immediately walk through the port until your team can confirm you have a window of opportunity or you will find swift and immediate death waiting for you.

 

In a well executed raid you will form your groups out here and have constant communication with the recon team on the inside that will give you your queue as to when to zone in, what to expect, and where to go. If none of these conditions are met you could be in for a disappointing evening of corpse retrieval.

 

If you have a good solid group and plan you are ready to advance to one of the 2 most common campsites in the zone. The North Wall camp or the Left Wall Camp (sometimes adjusted a bit to be the South West Corner Camp.

 

Most creatures in this area will be green but just so you know what to expect:

Dry Bone Skeletons

Specters

Cyndy (high lvl Necromancer)

 

If you are level 46-50 and with a partner you should be able to handle obstacles fine. Be sure to warn the folks waiting at the portal that you are on the way and that you may, in fact, train them with a few specters. This warning is necessary as sometimes casters are bound right outside the portal to facilitate a quick re-entry. Freshly killed people tend to be upset when trained with creatures that can kill them in a few blows without armor and/or spells ready.
