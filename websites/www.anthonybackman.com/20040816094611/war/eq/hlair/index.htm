The Known

 

Right now, this is what we got…

 

The red dots are wurms, the green dot is Hoshkar. The lines are the path they take. All of the mobs can stop anyplace on there path and double back. Coming back and shortening a predictable window.

 

Right now, we wait for the green dot to get right in front of us, then tag it when it's clean of the other 2 red pathing wurms.

 

This presents a few problems, the greatest of which is, gating away and summoning. There are also additional timing risks with the 2 patrolling wurms in front of safe hall. We are somewhat at the paths mercy.

 

For the most part we are confidant that with just enough folks, we will always ultimately succeed if we stay in safe hall. Be it timing, or people, or a "luck o the draw", we can make it work because after all, we did it once right?

 

Probably sooner or later.

 

It's proven it can work.

 

But what if there is another way? One that takes out what has beaten us 90% of the time.

 

Just look at the room and open your mind.
