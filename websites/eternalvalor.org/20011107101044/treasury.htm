Guild Treasury Guidelines (revised April 14, 2001)

1) The guild treasury is managed by Kina and Souleach.  Both have alts that can hold items and monies.  Kina's alt is Munnypenny and she is located in Freeport.  Souleach's at is Dabnk and he is located in Kunark.  In addition, Draygar has two alts (Runesandpages and Researchone) in Freeport that hold various words, pages and runes.

2) Donations to the guild treasury (money and items) are appreciated, but voluntary for the most part.  The only time that money will be required is when plane raids are scheduled and special items need to be purchased (ie: coffins, gems for hate teleport, etc).  The amount for these special items will be split among the guild members attending the raid and will probably vary from 50-75 plat each per raid.

3) A list of items in the treasury will be updated as new items are received.  The list will be posted under the "Guild Treasury" forum on our message board at http://pub8.ezboard.com/beternalvalor66674 and each member is responsible for reviewing the list frequently.  If you see an item you would like, please make an immediate post to "claim" the item.  First come, first served. Please make arrangements with Draygar, Kina or Souleach to pick up the item.

4) Members agree that items obtained from the treasury are for use by you or one of your alts.  Members also agree never to sell or give away these items without prior consent from Kina or Souleach.  Members will return items to the treasury when they have found a suitable upgrade.

5) In order to make sure there is room in the treasury for new incoming items, items that remain idle in the treasury for an extended period of time will be sold and all monies returned to the treasury.  Items ready for sale will be noted as such on the weekly treasury list.  Those items worth less than 50 plat will be sold to vendors or the general public on a monthly basis.  Those items worth less that 250 plat will be sold to vendors or the general public every two months.  Those items worth more than 250 plat will be kept for four months then auctioned.  Binnin has volunteered to auction the items in EC.  Again, all monies obtained from selling these items will be put back into the treasury.

6) Please contact Kina or Souleach with any questions regarding the guild treasury.
