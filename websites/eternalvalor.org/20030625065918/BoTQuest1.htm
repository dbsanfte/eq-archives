Bastion of Thunder Key Quest Information

In order to get into Bastion of Thunder (BoT), you will need to collect six items off various mobs in Plane of Storms (PoS).  You will need:

    1.  A Storm Giant Head...common drop off any of the giants in PoS

    2.  A Storm Satuur Sash...random drop off the giants in the jungle area*

    3.  A Storm Taarid Bone...random drop off the giants in the desert area*

    4.  A Storm Volaas Beard...andom drop off the giants near the ocean area*

    5. and 6.  Esoteric Medallions...these drop off the mini boss giants in Plane of Storms (ie: Solnebk the Unruly, Pendubk the Turbulent, etc) and you will need to get 2 of them off different mini bosses

*Note: You need 3 different pieces off the giant races (any 3).  There are 4 different giants.  We just happened to get the 3 listed here.
