On March 4, 2001, several EV members participated in the Drunken Gnome Race.  The goal was to run a level 1 gnome from Ak'anon to Kelethin.  To make it more challenging, the participants ran under the influence of a lot of alcohol and without any buffs or SOW.  Gibberish and basic goofiness flooded guild chat for a bit while we took a break from the worries of Norrath.

BTW, a few prizes and an honorable mention were awarded...
1st Place: Teaken
Prize: 200pp and a 10 charge SoW potion

2nd Place: Vampyyr
Prize: 100pp and the satisfaction of knowing that he can walk those ramps in a drunken stupor.

Honorable mention for being the final gnome to finish the race to: Audam
Prize: 50pp, the Title of "The Little Gnome Who Wouldn't Quit" and 20 bottles of mead so he can practice.
