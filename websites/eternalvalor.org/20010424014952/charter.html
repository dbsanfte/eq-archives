Guild Charter (updated March 22, 2001)


The following information is on an Everquest guild called Eternal Valor which was founded on The Rathe server in August 1999.  On March 22, 2001, our guild moved to the Zebuxoruk server.

Mission Statement
 We are an association of players who are interested in building and maintaining good friendships while helping our members and others during our journeys through Norrath.

Guild Structure
 A. Guild Leaders
The responsibilities of the Guild Leaders (GL) will include managing any alliances, coordinating plane raids, handling the guild treasury and determining the general direction and growth of the guild.  The GL will also assist in coordinating raids or settling disputes involving the guild as needed.  The GL will also make final decisions on any guild related issues.

The Guild Leaders of Eternal Valor are:
Kina Kalliope and Souleach Lifesapper.

B. Officers
Officers are appointed by the GL and approved by fellow officers.  Their responsibilities will include enforcing the rules of this charter and assisting the GL in implementing the direction and continued growth of the guild.

To help the guild run smoothly, the following lists the individuals responsible for certain areas.  If you have any ideas, suggestions, questions, etc on the areas below, you are encouraged to contact the individual(s) that manage that area.

Alliance Coordination – responsible for maintaining a strong guild relationship with the leaders of Intrepid Spirit and works on settling disputes between the guilds.  Kina is the primary contact with Souleach as back-up.

Dispute Resolution – responsible to listening and settling member disputes.  Kolisari and Souleach handle this area.

External Events Coordination – responsible for planning and distributing information on non-raid type events (ie: free teleport day, newbie event, etc) that benefit the general population of Norrath.  May work with guild member(s) suggesting the event to get things up and running.  Kadaru and Elyssia handle this area.

Guild Treasury – manages monies and items donated to benefit guild members.  Kina and Souleach are the current Guild Treasurers.  Click here for more information on the guild treasury guidelines.

Internal Events Coordination – responsible for planning and distributing information on non-raid type events (ie: beach party, fight night in the Arena, etc) that are for the members of EV (and IS as appropriate).  May work with guild members(s) suggesting the event to get things up and running. Kadaru and Elyssia are the Internal Events Coordinators.

New Membership – responsible for gathering and recording the required information (sponsor’s names, character info, email) on those interested in joining EV.  Issues /guildinvite command or requests other officers to do so on their behalf.  Also responsible for posting character info to the member and officer forums to introduce the new member and sending a “welcome” type email to the new member which includes links to our web site, message board (including password info), etc.  Binnen and Drusylla manage this area.

Plane Raid Coordination – responsible for making plane reservations on the Rathe calendar system, researching and distributing raid info to participating members, forming groups, coordinate and record pre-rolling for loot, settle disputes on loot. Kina is the primary contact with Souleach and Kolisari as back-ups.

Raid Coordination – coordinates the researching, planning and distribution of information on non-plane raid events (ie: fire giants, efreeti, CoM, etc). This area will also assist members requesting help to complete quests. Will work with the guild member(s) that suggested the raid to help get things up and running.  Various officers and guild leaders will assist in this area for now.

C. Members
By accepting the guild tag "Eternal Valor" each member is bound to uphold the meaning of those words by always being honorable.  Be proud to be part of the EV family.  Treat others fairly and with kindness and respect.

Policies and Procedures
A.  General
Abide by the "play nice" and other policies set forth by Verant.  Participate in guild events, meetings, etc as much as possible.  Contribute to the guild with positive ideas, information and suggestions for continued growth.  Please check the web site and message board frequently for updates, raid info, etc.  Communication is key to keeping a guild strong!

B. Etiquette
Be courteous and respectful to your fellow players at all times.  Please do not expect or demand your fellow guildmate to drop what he/she is doing to rez, port, etc you.  Be considerate of their time (we all play EQ for fun).  Please refrain from using vulgar or inappropriate language.  Use discretion when doing tells to others.  In guild chat, be mindful of others.  If someone does not respond to you in guild chat, please do a tell to them instead as he/she might have just missed the conversation in battle spam.

C. Disputes
All disputes must be brought to the attention of a Dispute Resolution Officer or Guild Leader immediately for further investigation and resolution.

Membership Requirements - Effective 2/10/01, EV is not accepting new members.  Please check back in a couple of months.  Thanks for your interest. 

Rejoining EV
Those that chose to leave Eternal Valor and then decide to re-join, must email Kina or Souleach with reason(s) why they left the guild and why they want to re-join. Upon receipt of this email, the Guild Leader(s) and Officers will vote on if the person should be allowed back in the guild. The person requesting re-admission must receive a majority number of favorable votes to be able to re-join.  The person requesting re-admission will be notified of the final decision within a week from the time the email is received.
