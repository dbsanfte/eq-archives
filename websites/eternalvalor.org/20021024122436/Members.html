Eternal Valor was founded in August 1999 on the Rathe server and was a guild started by some real life friends who played Everquest.  As we got to know other people, our circle of friends continued to grow.  We traveled together to explore zones like Kedge Keep and grew strong enough to fight in the Planes of Fear and Hate.  We transferred to the Zebuxoruk server in March 2001.  Unfortunately, all good things must come to and end, and our guild became defunct in June 2001.  Some stopped playing EQ (Audam, Bovina, Drakson), some joined other guilds (Kina and Rufis joined Clan Icewind, Daystar and Yazaki joined Dark Breed of Norrath), while others formed new guilds (Lodestar and Stone).  Although EV is defunct, the values and friendships live on...safe travels to all!
