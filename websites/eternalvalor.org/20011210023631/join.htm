Thank you for visiting the Eternal Valor web site...we appreciate your interest in our guild!

Who is Eternal Valor?
Eternal Valor started off in August 1999 by a small group of real life friends.  As many of us traveled the lands of Norrath, we met new people, made new friends and our guild continued to grow.  All members of EV strive to act maturely, respect others and value honor, friendship and fun over greed.  With this in mind, we are selective on who we invite into our family of friends.

We have grown into a guild of real life and virtual friends.  We care about each other's accomplishments or problems in game and do our best to help where we can.  Some members like to roleplay and ham it up every chance they get while others don't.  Some are chatty while others are more quiet.  Some love to adventure, some love to quest.  We're all different, but there's one thing in common...the desire to make EV the best guild possible!

How can I join Eternal Valor?
Membership into Eternal Valor is by referral only.  Here are the guidelines for requesting membership into EV.

1. Potential new members and alts of current members must be level 10 or higher.  (If you're curious about the levels of players in EV, please click here.)

2. Potential new members must visit our web site at www.eternalvalor.org and read our guild charter. They must understand the charter, guidelines and be willing to follow them.

3. Potential new members interested in joining EV, must have at least TWO current EV members sponsor their membership. The two current EV members making the referral pledge to act as a “big brother/sister” and watch over the new member (this would include things like making sure they understand correct guild etiquette, mentoring them to be solid players, etc).  If you do not have two members to sponsor you, please make a post on our message board to introduce yourself so that we may group with you to get to know you better.

4. Once the invite is complete, the new member must provide us with a valid email that we may use to contact them.

5. All members must check the EV web site and message board frequently for updates, raids, changes, etc.  Communication is the key to keeping the guild strong!

6.  Exceptions to the above can be made by a Guild Leader and/or Officer as seen fit.
