The city of Oggok has many merchants, all offering good prices for their wares to Ogres. Anything related to bashing can be bought in Oggok. From weapons to armor to supplies. The only thing not readily for sale in Oggok itself are trade practice items, such as BlackSmithing supplies. However, their is an Alchemy Shop unmatched by any other in Norrath. Look at what each merchant sells, then check the map to find their locations.
