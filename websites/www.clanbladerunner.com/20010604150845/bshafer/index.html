Surfing the Internet has become a serious pastime of all individuals from different corners of the world.  While you surf, you will find many pages that you will either be educated, amused, or disgusted by.  I hope that mine is part of the first two, and not the latter.  I feel I have a few things to offer the individuals that surf this wonderous thing we call "The Web", by giving an educating page about setting up a local area network, by catering to those car enthusiastes with my '78 Pontiac Trans Am page, by either upsetting, or amusing Dallas Cowboy fans with my Emmitt Smith page, and by giving a home to those honest, and trustworthy gamers with a well established, enjoyable clan that they can call "Home".  If any part of my page interests you, or you would like to see any changes, please feel free to email me anytime. 
Thanks for stopping by!
--Billy Shafer
