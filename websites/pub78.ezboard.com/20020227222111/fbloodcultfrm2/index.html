If you want to join the Blood Cult, you will need to match prerequire conditions
- Main character must be level 46+.
- Guild is on Coldain/CoV faction or at least working on it. Don't blame us to not raid dwarf =)
- There are no objections to let you join. You will get invited by an officer or leader. You will become a "trial" member.
- From the date you joins us, you have 1 month to integrate in guild. Its obviously that the more you show up, group with us, join raid the better we know you and the more people would sponsor you. After one month the person who invited you will open a thread on our boards, where all our members can add their opinion about the trial member. To stay in guild, Trial member need to obtain 10 sponsors. but if there is some or many bad feeling from full member, officer and leader will consider deeply the case and can veto the full application. Trial members may not sponsor of course.
[Note to member]: If you sponsor someone, you should be sure to base your decision on facts and things that happened. Just cause you never met the person and had no bad experiences with the person is not enough to sponsor him. This is the tricky part. You all are now responsible for the people who join our guild, and to make sure they fit in the guild.
[note to trial member]: Trial members may NOT roll for BPs, Legs or epic loot pieces unless they would rott of course.
The officer will arrange to meet you at some mutually convenient time and make certain in his or her own mind that you are a suitable candidate for the Blood Cult.
Please note that you will be summarily dismissed from the guild WITHOUT WARNING if you bring the guild into disrepute. I will NOT have members of the guild kill stealing, intentionally training on people, using offensive language in public, or whatever whilst wearing the Blood Cult guild tag.
Be safe and have fun. =)
