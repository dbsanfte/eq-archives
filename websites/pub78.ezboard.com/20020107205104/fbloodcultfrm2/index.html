If you want to join the Blood Cult, you will need to match prerequire conditions
- Main character must be level 46+.
- Find a sponsor (i.e. an existing member of the guild in good standing) who will recommend you to an officer.
- As soon as possible get the Sebilis key, the Wakening land tooth or the Cobalt scar tooth for people 50+.
- Guild is on Coldain/CoV faction or at least working on it. Don't blame us to not raid dwarf =)
The officer will arrange to meet you at some mutually convenient time and make certain in his or her own mind that you are a suitable candidate for the Blood Cult.
Please note that you will be summarily dismissed from the guild WITHOUT WARNING if you bring the guild into disrepute. I will NOT have members of the guild kill stealing, intentionally training on people, using offensive language in public, or whatever whilst wearing the Blood Cult guild tag.
Be safe and have fun. =)
