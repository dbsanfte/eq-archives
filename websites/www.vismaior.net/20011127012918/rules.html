Point System Rules

Some Point Guidelines:

*Player with the most Current Points Wins the Item bid upon

*When a Guild Member is gone for more than 7 days in a row, there points are frozen and marked in blue and remain so until they have been active for 4 consecutive Raids and or have been at 70percent of the raids for 2 consecutive weeks or as decided by the Guild Leader.

*All items bought with Points should not be sold on the open market, any trade you wish to do with a point bought item should be discussed with The Guild Leader.

*Any Issues with Points should be Directed to Darkoor ingame or via the Members Message Board.
