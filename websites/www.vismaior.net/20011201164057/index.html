Keeper, Telk, Gozzrem, Velk -- Wednesday, November 28th
 

I logged on yesterday to find the troops in Temple of Veeshan headed for Lendiniara the Keeper.  Since Lotus Cult was in the process of clearing North ToV (grats LC) we figured hell, let's get another ST key and kill the dragons in the West Wing while we were at it.   The Keeper dropped with ease yielding an ST key for Amanraz, Gloves to Aili and a new Mask to hide Clawgrip's ugly mug.  Oh, did I mention that Rabbid and Serpent like to train us? :P

After that we cleared to Telkorenar and took him down with a double suplex and a few kicks square to the nutz.  He dropped some new Pauldrons for yours truly (Krondath :P) and two Masks for Baaguk and Aspro.  Too bad nothing short of extreme plastic surgery will improve their looks.

Gozzrem was our next target.  Our troops deftly maneuvered into position and proceeded to beat the dragon down using a combination of steel, latex and leather.  Gozzrem dropped two Eyes of Rigatoni (mmm, pasta) for Baaguk and the Rivervale Children's Home and a Wand of the Black Dragon Eye to Khando (who probably has already destroyed it because it has the same icon as the mage epic).

After that we hopped over to Great Desktop, er, Great Divide and converged upon the Lair of Velketor the Wussy.  Feeling lazy we pulled him and his guards together and beat them down.  The mangina sorceror dropped some new Boots for Osgiliath (finally), a spell book for the Dot Fund (tm), and some orb that might have rotted.

Until next time...and hopefully I can think of some witicisms that don't suck...

Cazic Tool, Tormax, Jellosnack, Vind, Statue, Sleepers -- Tuesday, Nov 27th
 

Started the day off in Fear.  Draco dropped a Grotesque Girdle and a Berserker's Ring.  Not sure who got these because my ass was still at work.  I arrived in time for Cazic Tool who dropped easily and gave up some phat lewts.  The walking lump of refuse dropped 2 chokers (Gerwood and Vandall), Halo (Stefy), and Pauldrons (Tipu Aroooo!).

Next we moved on to Kael and waxed King Tormax.  Well, I say waxed with the most levity possible. Seeing as everyone is concerned about faction right now this was our ugliest Tormax slaying yet.  Nonetheless, King Toejam is dead and he gave up a White Dragon Scale Sash (Sumer), 2 Crowns (Dhouja and Donnag), Axe (Amanraz Twink) and he even gave Sphyre head.

Lord Jellosnack was next on the list and died quite easily (amazing what happens when people *want* the faction hit :P).  He gave up ST Key (Stefy), Head (OMG, He spent points, Zenfe), Pal Sword (rot), Sk Sword (Rot), Dragon's Blood Earring (someone, I'm sure).

Next stop was Kael (deja vu!).  Vindicator died easily dropping some new boots for Shinu.  After that we had a pretty epic battle with the Statue.  Rabbid (I'm not sure if it was him but I'm going to give him shit anyway) decided we needed to pull statue while we were still clearing so he did.  Boom, hello Mr. Statue.  Clerics were all about 20 mana and no one was ready but we prevailed!  I have to say this was one hell of a fun fight and was a good wake up call. :P

After that we retired to Sleeper's Tomb to take out the Master of the Guard, Progenitor, and Final Arbitor.  I'm not really sure what happened because I fell asleep at the entrance and woke up 2 hours later as the only person in the zone.  I'm going to assume we killed stuff but I'm not exactly sure what!  

Until next time...
