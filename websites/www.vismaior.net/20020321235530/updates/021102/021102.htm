The New Page

Welcome to the new and updated Vis Maior web page.  Arkinia has moved on to new things in life, and is no longer playing everquest, so the task of maintaining the web page has moved on.  You'll notice some new things this time around...

  • Members have the option to have a member@vismaior.net email address, ftp access to store images for ezboard signatures and magelo profile backgrounds (coming soon).
  • A "members only" section with guild resources.
  • A chat room for server downtime and patches.
  • Screen shot galleries of mobs we have killed

Look forward to a redesigned points page with current item costs and mob values, and a few other things we've been working on.  If you are a guilded member, be sure to visit the Members Only portion of the web page and get registered for when those pages come live.  You'll get emails with your passwords and stuff as soon as its ready to go live.

Tormax, Thought Horror Overfiend, Dain: RIP

On Saturday, we headed into the castle of Kael to kill the king himself, King Tormax.

  • Tormax's Head - Deamien
