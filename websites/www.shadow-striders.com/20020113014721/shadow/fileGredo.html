Gredo Bloodscourge
Troll Shaman
 
History: I am Gredo Bloodscourge, Troll Witchdoctor of the 34th Circle of the Shadow Council and Destroyer of Dreams, decendant of the fierce and powerful Gul'Dan Bloodscourge, tribal shaman of the Blackened Tooth Clan. Conceived by the feared warrior, Medivh whose death was a direct result of my violent birth. In what passed as my youth, I studied dark majiks under the tutelage of Gul'Dan and his predecessor, my aged grandfather, Kiljaeden, who was mortally wounded in a historic raid on the Hairfoot Clan, a halfling village of Rivervale. My aspirations rose higher than those of my peers and masters alike, for I knew that the scope of their vision was limited by their devotion to the advancement of the Horde. I cared little for the Horde and its petty politics. I care only for the chance to fathom the spiraling mysteries of the Great Dark Arts in order to strike fear into all that oppose my Clan. Against the will of the Clan, I left the protection of the Horde in search of new knowledge, wisdom, and power. For years the Horde Clans of Innothule Swamp have been at war with all the races of Norrath including themselves. Chaos and dissidence and struggles between the clans has plagued the Trolls and kept us from being the proven superior race. I believe that alliance is power. It is my goal to end the sensless feuding, and unite the Horde with likeminded clans, races, and individuals. Darkness, fear, and hatred shall rule Norrath and it shall be called the Age of Darkness.
