Journal Three
                
 
Reflections

Malgorm walked the tunnels of Black Burrow listening to the music of death, the screams of pain and pleas for help. The gnolls had grown to fear him as he hunted their leaders. He killed the giant black snakes crawling the halls that were so much like him. Malgorm killed. Sometimes he would just sit and admire the snakes, whose fangs he carried as weapons out of respect for the cold blooded creatures. He just laughed as one ate a halfling, then killed it to prove he was the bigger snake.

Malgorm turned to see the twins Stormangell and Silverann. Ah, how they where drawn to him. As they spoke he knew they were more than just corrupted. Malgorm had spent much time with Stormangell. He felt the boy's pain, but something drew him closer to the twins inexplicably. The answer was not yet to be seen. Silverann seemed to dislike Malgorm; harboring strong hate and love at the same time. Both were following Sugan as Wind Riders, and Malgorm corrupted as many of her followers as he could to hurt her. There was something more about them though. He wanted them. The hunger wanted them, and what the hunger wanted Malgorm was powerless to refuse.

The Trap

Deep in the sewers Malgorm heard Lord Faileas summoning him. It was his time... they had trapped the foolish Sugan. As Malgorm made his way though the maze he saw once again the red haired female slicing at his great Lord Faileas, but this time there was no divine storm. She was being overwhelmed by the power of darkness. Lord Faileas crushed her with the strength of his mighty dark blade as Malgorm tormented her soul with shadows. Sugan's blood stained the sewer floor, and she looked up to see her beloved Canelon. With her eyes she begged him at last for help from the pain. Before the high elf could respond Malgorm started chanting unearthly words, forcing a magickal gate open. Terror spread across both Canelon and Sugan's faces. At the word of Bertoxxulous both twins were caught by the unholy power and swept away into the gate. Malgorm only smiled as he vanished, and the axe of Canelon only hit the wall where he once stood. Lord Faileas had won.......

The Return

Many moons passed as Malgorm grew in power far beyond the normal rate of a mortal. The Wind Riders hunted him, lead by the Knight of Storms, the great Canelon Lightweaver. A blood hunt for his head; and Malgorm merely taunted them. So many spies existed among the Wind Riders that they would never catch him. There were many evil deeds he did during this time. He killed many of the Temple of Life and tortured many of the brothers of the robe. He was feeding the hunger and it grew stronger; a plague that covered the land. Malgorm killed everyday; looking for the light to infect it with his corruption. He had made his way to Freeport knowing Lord Faileas would return soon. Malgorm prepared for the return of the twins, his will strengthened to hold Sugan to the darkness. He worked on the boy Stormangell on a daily basis, infecting him with the plague.

The time had come. Lord Faileas marched in from Bertoxxulous' void like a great god of darkness with Sugan at his side. The Wind Riders' pleas fell upon deaf ears as Sugan called those still loyal to the shadows. The pain upon the faces of her loved ones made Malgorm smile in almost pure glee as he sat watching from the shadows, cloaked by his magick. Their pleading for their beloved Sugan and her evil look as she blew them off with ease... for the first time Malgorm felt true joy. Then to hear Lord Faileas tell Stormangell that he was his father and watch him join the shadows; the plans of the hunger had fallen into place. All was as it was supposed to be….
