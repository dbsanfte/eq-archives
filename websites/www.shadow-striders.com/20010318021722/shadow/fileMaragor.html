Maragor
Teir'Dal Necromancer
 
History: >From The journals of Neogoth, Feir'Dal Druid and Historian

Today I met a Necromancer by the name of Maragor while selling backpacks, he seemed a bit put off when I started asking him questions and most of what I have learned of him since then is from observation and rumor...Little it seems is known of Maragor's early life, what is known about him is the parodox under which he lives. To talk to him he seems friendly enough although a bit closed off if you don't happen to be Teir'Dal. He is evil, of that there is no question yet he himself does not realize it. I have seen him stand emotionless as people are slain all around him (assuming they are not Teir'Dal) and weep openly at the death of one of his own ilk. He treats Teir'Dal with generosity and kindness that is truly pure of selfish motivation yet you will never see him lift even an eyelash to help those of other races without their being something in it for himself...even if it is not apparent to others what that may be. He does seem to have some bit of sympathy for the half ! elves, or as he affectionately calls them, half breeds. The reason for this is not yet apparent, but it is definately there, while camoflauged I observed him save a young half elf from a kodiak and quickly gather shadows around himself before any could know it was him...I believe he bears further observation, I will continue my research...
