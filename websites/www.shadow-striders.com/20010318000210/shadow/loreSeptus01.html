Tale of the Renegades
               
After much travelling, you step into a local tavern. A dwarf lady offers you some ale, and you take it. Near the back of the musty, and thief filled area, a bandit tells the story of a family called the Renegades. Since it will take a while for you to revive, you decide to listen to the bandit's story....

There was once a great, powerful bloodline called the Renegades. Each person in the family would attend the greatest schools of sorcery and wizardry. Each was to marry a loving companion and have one child. Never more than one, because the gods would seek balance if there were more. One, by the name of Arthur Renegade married a fair Tier'Dal called Pryde. Not soon after, they decided to pass on their legacy. It seems that this was the wrong time to do so, as Pryde was cursed with two children in her womb. When the children were born, the parents tried to forget of the god's balance, and pretend it was all lies .They named their children Septus, and Tritus. However, one night, using the Renegade ability to manipulate the minds of the weak, and read those of the strong, they stared into Septus's dreams, and saw three shadowy figures taking him away...The balance was at hand. Reluctantly, they let Septus leave, knowing that there was nothing they could do. Septus was destined to be the Dark Chosen, while his brother, Tritus, the Light. As Septus matured, nightmares plagued him, always telling him to leave...and become a shadow dweller. They tore him apart, and eventually settled, as Septus had become a Rogue. While Tritus began wizardry, learning powerful, yet just arts. The parents died five years later...

After hearing the story, you stand, and begin to walk off, but you hear mutterings coming from her mouth...such as 'It is rumored that the two sons are still alive...If they both fall into the hands of the Shadows, all will be lost...of course, it's just drabble. As if the sons are still alive...the Renegade legacy is long dead.'
- Septus
