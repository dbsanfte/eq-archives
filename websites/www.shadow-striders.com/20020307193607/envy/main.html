Welcome Shades, to Fuath Dileab. Or in other words- Legacy of Hate. My lair.

   The shadowed room is an octagon, with it's many walls roughly forming a circle. You do not recall how you got here, but you sense that this place is deep underground - perhaps miles. The walls are made of small, fitted stones and meet the ceiling far above you. Each wall except the one before you has a door, inscribed with a single word you can almost read. Seven in all. The place is clean despite it's apparent age; except for the bits of moss in the rocks, but even they seem strangely cultivated. On the wall directly ahead is an enormous portrait of a beautiful elven child with dark blue skin and white hair, wearing a strange mixture of silk robes and metal armour. Her skin is smooth as porcelain and her posture flawless, but something about her eyes is unsettling. They shine a cold turquoise from the darkness, of their own accord. The girl's perfect violet lips are slightly mocking as she observes her room's intruder.
   A silvery female voice seeps from the room itself, interrupting your observations of the portrait. "My name is Envy. I am a Knight of Shadow, here to show Norrath exactly what our Lord Innoruuk means when he says, 'Hate them'..."
   As you draw closer to the doors, the strange word on each becomes legible, and the girl's voice dispassionately speaks of the room within.
