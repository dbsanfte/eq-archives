Slavery II

Rounding one of the corners, she was jerked to attention when Faileas' pulled her back. A human woman and a half elf man, both with dark hair and leather armor, stepped in front of them.

The woman spoke in bitter tones. "In the name of Antonius Bayle and the city of Qeynos, I demand you surrender the dark elf wretch to our custody, for trial and punishment of her crimes."

Her half elven companion stood behind her to the side, watching Envy with all the wariness and hate of a trained, leashed hound. Envy returned the gaze with glee. Looking behind Faileas, she spotted two more humans block the way back. Trapped.

Turning to face the leader, Envy realized that Faileas had drawn his sword and was holding it at her eye level. It was not to threaten the dark elf's life though, it was to protect her. The human woman gritted her teeth and grunted, as if she'd dealt with him before.

"Faileas," she growled, "if you insist on opposing my authority this time, you will be arrested and executed as well!" The woman drew her sword. "Cause a scene, and you'll never walk the streets again."

Envy felt him shrug. "Perhaps." Faileas mused, "If you live to tell anyone, that is."

In a sudden movement, Faileas pushed Envy to the side and blocked the woman's sword. He ducked under one of the humans' fists, and kicked the other away from Envy. But the half elf had his target and was quick about it, he drove a punch hard into the dark elf's stomach. She doubled over, and coughed a spatter of blood onto the stone floor. As Envy slumped and passed out, she saw Faileas reach to catch her and miss. Then he turned to their attackers, gripping his sword with both hands in precision. Darkness...
