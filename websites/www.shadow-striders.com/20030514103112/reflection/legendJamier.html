Chronicles of the Obsidian Knight
Part I
 
The horrors of the estate of unrest were almost unbearable. Jamier soul cried for the undead, the souless ones that were once mortal. It was all he could do not to be ill at the scent of decay around him as he brought up his blade and slew them one by one.

With but a moment to himself he sat and rested. His body and heart racked with pain as he sensed something wrong; so very wrong. He looked deep within his heart “what was calling him?”. With great urgency he picked up his blade and made the journy to a high druid. “sir I need Transport”. The druid looked upon the skin of the Dark Elf in descust; but finally agreed to transport the knight for a price. Jamier looked sadly as he gave the druid the last of his inherantance to the man.

With a flash of light and a clap of thunder the young night found himself in the West Commons, his heart raced with the sense of danger that awaited The light of the sun was bright to his Dark Elven eyes. He pulled up his arm to shadow the glare as he made tracks towards kithicor Woods. He didn’t know what was propelling him other then gut instinct.

The coldness of the january weather bit hardly at the Dark elf’s flesh, but for all the discomfort nothing would stop him. He finally came to the piont of his pulling. It was a gathering. A face off between the shadows and the wind. Envy seemed to be the center of attention. Jamier growled under his breath, the wenches endless taunting would have to come to a stop. His hatred for her only fueled his strength. He stood by the side of the ranger ranewae as the Enchanter Sorcere seemed to be mediating between the two enemies.

His eye wandered slowly, as he caught the eyes of laele the she-demon, his eyes softened for but a moment at the thought of her caresses. Shaking himself back to reality he realized what a threat she was, her and malcron. He brandished his holy blade and watched as she slinked further into the shadows. Before he realized it he had already shot off 1 arrow from his bow. Just as a simple warning.. Without warning he was awakened back to his senses as he watched sorcere walk off with faileas. “I will keep my word..it is for the child”….he turned back and said “wait here!”. Jamier could only look confused. “envy’s child?” The Knight could only wonder.

The hair on the back of Jamier’s neck stood up on end as he heard Envy’s vioce..’how do you feel cattiebrie?” he knew there was something wrong, and she had something seriously to do with the lady Cattiebrie’s condition. He just stood there frozen, listening. Out of anger he threatened envy’s undead pet. Whatever he said he couldn’t remember, but it had the striders surrounding him. He sat in the face of danger. Envy wanting him ..daring him to fight her. He could only refuse. No he knew Silverann loved her. Jamier thinks that and her supposed pregnant condition were the only reasons he couldn’t kill her.

He turned away only to see Sorcere walk up the hill with faileas. Jamiers eyes Widened he knew it without words. The laughter that erupted from faileas’s lips was a sound that will haunt his soul for eternity. “I must honor my promise” sorcere sounded weak. Jamiers eyes glowed with the brazen beserker rage of his god. Envy’s silvery ice giggle only feuled it “welcome to the shadows sorcere”. He could hear no more as he reached quickly and pulled envy into him. His blade at her throat. Sorcere screamed “NO!” but the vioce fell upon deafened ears. “Jamier this isnt how a paladin would act!” another vioce added. “I can have Sorcere kill him!” Faileas vioce echoed. “No if you do not step back I will kill her”. Jamier was shocked to hear his own vioce threaten anothers life. He grapped her arm and ran with her.

Before he knew it he found himself deep within the caverns of ro with his prisoner. He just sat staring into her eyes. “you were so brave” he looked surprised at her response. He sat there but a few moments longer. Her death would solve it all….he contemplated killing her there and now. Paladin they call him. But that was not a word he used for himself. He raised his sword..then stopped. He remembered Silverann’s words. “I Love her” he always said in an emotionless tone. Sugan’s orders were to capture her and save the child. Jamier paused. Jamiers hand reached forward and rested upon her belly. She laughed. “there is no child!” For some strange reason he was almost inclined to believe her.

Silverann met them in the caverns, Jamier knew he must make it right. He freed envys hands and turned as Silverann consumated a marriage according to the brutal teir’dal ways. He closed his eyes. He allowed it to happen. Jamier turned and walked away. Blessing it in the name of Rallos Zek.

Suddenly he fell to his knees as he heard a distant scream.. “Ralnia” .. for the first time in his life he sat there and cried … “ooh! GODS! Please no!” His fists clenched as the tears streamed down his face. ‘RALLOS DAMN YOU! WHY! WHY DO YOU MAKE ME FEEL THIS!” he screamed into the Night. “Even the cries of war must have a paladin”…the soft vioce echoed into his head. “I will bring her back! She cant go! She is the ony hope to sorcere’s soul” Jamier staggered in the pain. He cannot resist his calling. He looked down at the axe the gods have given him.. “is he a paladin or an executioner?” for sugan for the four winds his destiny was to be both….. for he is the obsidian Knight.

“I hear your pain ralnia…I feel your tears Sugan, I bear your pain cattiebrie , I share your hate envy!….I am coming…..”
