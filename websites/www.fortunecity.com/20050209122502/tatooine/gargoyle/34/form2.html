To be eligible to receive my Cool Fantasy Award, your site
must NOT be a commercial site and must have an emphasis towards fantasy.
I'll be looking at the artistry, imagination, and layout of the pages
more so than the content or the technical expertise. If your site piques
my curiosity and is fun to cruise about in, then you've pretty much got it
made. A few things that will immediately take you out of the running:

 - If I get stuck in frames (I really hate it when that happens). I have
nothing against frames, mind you.

- If there are a bunch of misspellings or poor grammar.
(I'm not multi-lingual, so if your site is in a
language other than English, you're off the hook.)

- If there are more than a few broken or missing links.

- If there are no controls to start/stop midi files.

If you win this award, all I ask is that you link it back to Ice Haven.
