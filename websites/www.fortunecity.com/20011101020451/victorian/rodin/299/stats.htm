Highest Charisma

198(210 with next buff up, don't have that buff yet)

(This is the stat I most want to Improve)  This is another incredibly important statistic for Enchanters. Charisma is important for increased success with our mesmerize line of spells, charm line of spells, and stuns. Thankfully we have three charisma buff spells: Sympathetic Aura, Radiant Visage, and Adorning Grace. There are also a lot of nice charisma objects out there. This statistic is important for all Enchanter play styles but especially for the pure Enchanter. After 200 charisma there is a cap, the benefits don't get much better after 200 Charisma.  Some say intel is more important, but if three spells in a row fail because your charisma is too low you will use up a lot of mana and might even get hurt. 

 
Highest Intelligence

153(For Mana, Skills)

Intelligence: This is the backbone of your class. Intelligence effects three things. Your mana, your spell fizzles and your skill increase rate. High intelligence means you will have more mystical energy to utilized in combat. Since Enchanters are a pure casting class this is the most important statistic and should always be maxed with your bonus points before any other statistics are looked at. High intelligence will also mean that you rise in skill levels faster, and that you will have more chance of successfully casting your spells. This will mean you reach the peak for your levels within the first one or 2 bubbles of that level, rather than falling further and further behind as you rise in the ranks. Intelligence is also a factor in how often Specialization kicks in.
