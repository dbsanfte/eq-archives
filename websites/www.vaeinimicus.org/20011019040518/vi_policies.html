Expectations


1. You will be expected to support guild raids over your own interests. If you're online, you will be expected to attend the nights events.

2. You are explicitly not allowed to share guild information without the approval of an officer or the guild leader. We don't care who, if they don't have a VI tag, they don't have the authorization to know our business.

3. Help your guildmates. Someone has a question, do your best to answer it for them, someone needs a port, etc, do your best to assist.

4. Conduct yourself in a dignified manner at all times.


Recruitment


A prospective member will be required to have 1 sponsor, I.E 1 person from VI that can vouch for you in saying, 'hey this persons an ok guy/gal to be amongst us.'

• Said prospective member will be expected to follow, and adhere to, our rules and expections as listed above.

• Said prospective member must be at least level 58.

• Said prospective member must have 70% hearty guild approval. This is obtained by raiding with us and getting to know folks better.

• Said prospective member must not have any valid objections from current VI members.

• Said prospective member will be subjected to a trial period of 2 weeks to 1 month depending on play time and availability. If they pass this trial period, said prospective member will be then be considered a part of VI.

Once you have your sponsor, send our GM Morrison an email at jelam@neo.rr.com listing your sponsor and his/her contact information to further the guild application process.
