09/30/2001
Smurfs

So I think I've made it clear that listing a bunch of stupid loot isn't really my idea of a fun website update. When we were first killing things like Tormax and Warders and North ToV dragons, it was at least moderately interesting because in a lot of cases they were the first items of their kind on the server, and listing the loot was a lot more interesting than saying "ha ha we killed some stuff". But now, there's nothing left to kill that isn't repetitive, and listing loot is barely academic, and decidedly uninteresting. Yet every single time something dies, I get a bunch of lamers telling me to update the website. Can't please everyone (and god knows I wouldn't want to anyway, bunch of frigging bastards), but I am definitely left with a dilemma. Do I continue to keep journalistic loot lists here for the sake of reference and filler? Do I change my updates to merely say "ha ha we killed some stuff"? Do I start comparing pictures of Meldar and Vanity Smurf to alert my colleagues to their disturbing likeness? Do I fill the update with screenshots of all the obscene tells I get from Nedden every day? I've already gone the Oregon Trail route, but it's entertainment value is fleeting (although Dagrath and Pelzin would deny that to the grave). So, now what? I suppose I'll be democratic and ask what you, the viewers, want to see. So, in the classic Clue-style tradition, I bring you each possible ending! And I ask that you email me and vote on the format you like best. The winning format will be strictly adhered to for as long as I feel like it, and possibly not at all. Results will be posted as soon as some people vote!
