Vae October Girl - Pena!


Born in Neriak, raised, as any good Dark Elf would be, with
a burning desire to kill things, she now lives in Third Gate when she's not
busy gallavanting across the world, killing large critters.

She enjoys buffing, healing, and letting Promethis die. She has a weakness
for dark elves, rangers, and flowers (though not necessarily in that order),
and she hates, with a hate only a priestess of Innoruuk could manage, tanks
who catch agro when they shouldn't, fighters with no resist gear, and the
healing spell Remedy.

"I'd like to kill Ventani, and I'd actually like to wake the Sleeper, just to see it."
