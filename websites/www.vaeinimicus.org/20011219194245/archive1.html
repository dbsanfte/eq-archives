Didn't we just go through this? Telkorenar, Gozzrem, and Lendiniara the Keeper down again. We're reminded that Shadow Knights are spoiled rotten and yet people still won't play them. But frankly, the day we allow Pelzin to live through even a single raid, I'll take my leave of this band of merry travelers. We may kill each other less, but I say we don't let it tear us apart. I say we take this opportunity to draw ourselves closer together, united in killing Pelzin. Editorial mode over.
