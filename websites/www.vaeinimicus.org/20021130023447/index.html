News - Fluffy Bunnies Reign Supreme - Posted on Sat Sep 28, 2002 1:30 pm    


We killed Aten Ha Ra tonight, first time on Tunare, first time for us (one necessarily follows the other, but not everyone who reads the board is capable of a logical progression of thought), in a fight that was so fucking long that my dinner got cold. As an aside, I think that is the most inherently evil thing about Vex Thal is; you think you're going to have time to afk for dinner, but in the end, you have just enough time to grab something to eat while you're moving, but not actually have enough time to eat it. So it sits there, taunting you, getting colder, while you spend hour after hour clearing the morass of mobs that roam the halls, just waiting for an errant horse to show its face.

But I digress. Frequently.

So it's been a long week. For a while, it seemed like all we were ever going to kill were the mobs on the first floor with a bunch of unpronounceable names and unremarkable acronyms. Then Wednesday, we had a breakthrough, Thursday, we had some amazingly dedicated raiders, and tonight we FINALLY KILLED ATEN HA RA. Does it seem like I've already said that? Tough shit. I'm proud of us.



Our healers were dead on. Our pulling was excellent. Our raiders all sat there and did their jobs until ungodly hours of the day and night. Good job, all of you. You should be damn happy with yourselves right now.

And no one has been more dedicated that our own fluffy bunny gnome Meldar (who will likely kill me for calling him that). He spent weeks camped at various mobs, botting a CLERIC, for fuck's sake, while we frantically keyed mages. He stayed up hours testing, training, trying to establish coth links and figure out pathing. He's saved us from summoning more than once, he's been there day in, day out, when we couldn't get 40 people to show up, and when we had 50 at a time and were making groups in stunned amazement at the turnout.

If you need, I'll sit here for a few minutes while you pick your jaw up off the floor and realize that yes, this is the EvilCleric guildmistress, affectionately known as The Devil, writing these warm and fuzzy things about her guildmates in general, and specifically a GNOME guildmate. But I don’t honestly know anyone who currently plays as part of Vae that gives more to Vae, and asks less in return, than Meldar.

Fluffy bunnies do in fact reign supreme.

They also look really nice with the first Talisman of Vah Kerrath on the server.
