So for your pleasure, some Harpy Poetry.

A Little Lantern

A lantern floats upon the silent stream,
Knowing not where it goes.
Following, just following ahead.

Little does the Lantern know,
That it carries one thing so precious,
Little does it know that is signifies a passing of a life.

Inside you can see memories of a past life,
Some joyous, some painful,
Some of Uncertainty.

Down the stream, down the stream.
Following it’s course so,
Little does the lantern know,
that people shed a tear whenever it passes by.

Four windows does the Lantern hold.

One window holds the memories of
Love.
First loves, innocent crushes, and
Stupid acts done in it’s name.
This window is a doorway to the others,
A key that beckons to be remembered.

The Second window holds the memories of
Pain.
First fights, a slap from a parent,
a fall from a bike, a simple cut from a knife.
This window is a key to learning and also,
A key to Hatred

The Third Window holds the memories of
Hate.
Simple envy, a fight with one you love,
prejudices against a certain color,
the wanting of to hurt someone.
This window a permanent key to life,
Is for-ever around and will never disappear.
This window is your fear.

The Last Window, the Fourth Window,
holds the key to all other windows.
The In-betweens, the other joys,
the simple characteristics of life, the memories of all else.
This window is the key to
the Renewal of Life.

A lantern floats upon the silent stream,
Knowing not where it goes.
Following, just following ahead.

Little does the lantern know that what it contains,
Is so precious, and so important.

Little does the Lantern know that when all have passed away,
Who remembered this Person,
It will be the only one who knows who this person was.
How that person felt toward others,
How that person loved and hated,
How that person lived his life.
How that person died.

When the flame contained within, Blows out.
The remembrance of this person has been honored.
The lantern floats up toward the sky,
where it is kept with other others of it’s kind,
So that when one day when the world has ended,
There will be a record of all who have died.

A lantern floats upon the silent stream,
Knowing not where it goes.
Following, just following ahead.
Little does it know what it signifies.

Never forget those who have made a difference in your life, honor them, hate them, but never forget.
