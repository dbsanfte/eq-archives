Hail Sisters and Brothers of Norrath!

Big news! I am involved in the planning of the EverQuest Gathering 2! Since the last posting on EQVault, I have joined forces with Kevin, the organizer of the Los Angeles EverQuest Gathering (see http://www.eq-event.com/ for more details about the turn out for that event ). We are going to work hard to make this event a spectacular occasion that no citizen of Norrath will want to miss!

Due to overwhelming requests from all that responded to our first posting, the city chosen for this event is Saint Louis. This will be an overnight function at a fine hotel and convention center in downtown Saint Louis. There will be many activities in which to participate, as well as just meeting and getting to know our fellow EQ addicts!  

For further information and to make your reservation please visit our site at http://www.eq-event.com/g2/

Judging from the number of responses received in one day of posting our earlier page and response form, space for this event may be filled fast and is limited. So stop by and RSVP soon if you think you might be joining us!

The new topic up for discussion:

How real is EverQuest to you?

Lots of discussions on our any EQ forum include the phrase, "it's only a game!" Yet it is obvious from many of the postings that real friendships develop and often, real emotions are a part of the EQ experience. I am sure we have all spent some time playing computer games that do not interact with other real live people.

So the question is...

To what degree is EQ more than "just a game" to you?

Let your opinion be known in our Forum.
