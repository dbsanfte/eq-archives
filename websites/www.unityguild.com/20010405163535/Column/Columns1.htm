Modern Art
Fayvren @ 1:50 AM
"From the moment that art ceases to be food that feeds the best minds, the artist can use his talents to perform all the tricks of the intellectual charlatan. Most people can today no longer expect to receive consolation and exaltation from art.

"The 'refined,' the rich, the professional 'do-nothings', the distillers of quintessence desire only the peculiar, the sensational, the eccentric, the scandalous in today's art. I myself, since the advent of Cubism, have fed these fellows what they wanted and satisfied these critics with all the ridiculous ideas that have passed through my mind.

"The less they understood them, the more they admired me. Through amusing myself with all these absurd farces, I became celebrated, and very rapidly. For a painter, celebrity means sales and consequent affluence. Today, as you know, I am celebrated, I am rich.

"But when I am alone, I do not have the effrontery to consider myself an artist at all, not in the grand old meaning of the word: Giotto, Titian, Rembrandt, Goya were great painters. I am only a public clown--a mountebank.

"I have understood my time and have exploited the imbecility, the vanity, the greed of my contemporaries. It is a bitter confession, this confession of mine, more painful than it may seem. But at least and at last it does have the merit of being honest."

- Picasso

Message Board Idiots
Fayvren @ 1:33 PM
It seems that the more I spend my time browsing the internet and looking at various forums whether they are in regards to politics, gaming or society at large the population of people ready and willing to demonstrate their lack of intellect and/or general ability to communicate with the world has been steadily increasing.

I ask one simple question... Why do message boards seem to be areas that invite stupidity? Are people so bored that they must continually post anonymously regarding issues that their obviously pre-evolutionary intellect could not possibly comprehend. It scares me to no end when fifty percent of the responses to a single topic demonstrate the lack of reading ability and or abstract thought in the population of this planet. It seems to me that the anonomity of online message boards condones such behavior. I would seriously consider any message board system that requires a verified name, address and social security # for anyone to post. I would imagine that far less stupidity would be spouted on such forums.

Write something even half opinionated on a message board, and rather than intellectual debate you will get whiny underachieving indviduals often posting nothing more than what I would consider digital drool. For god sakes, if you cant spell more than half the words right in a given sentence you should NOT be on the internet trying to communicate. Your time would be better spent elsewhere... like SCHOOL where you might actually be able to pick up some general life skills. It does not impress me that you can respond to messages in almost realtime fashion, it simply begs me to ask the question, WHAT IN GODS NAME DO YOU DO ALL DAY? Furthermore, those individuals on gaming forums that often begin spouting the "you have no life" type statements... ask yourself what that statement says about you, an individual that has dedicated his/her time to telling others how they have no life. If you rode the short yellow bus to school in which signs were marked not simply in words but with little cartoon pictures, then please summarily disconnect yourself from the internet and save us the trouble. How did you even find the ON switch to your computer in the first place.

I was asked a question in my philosophy class on whether we should tolerate free speech merely for the sake of tolerating freedom. At first it seemed like a good idea, then as I began to think about it more in depth, I realized that there are just some thoughts and ideas that are far to big a waste of time for humanity to even spend time contemplating or tolerating. It is of my opinion that these people need to be locked up or smothered as to save the world alot of time and energy which would be wasted in sifting through the unchecked wastes produced by their overactive but inable minds.
