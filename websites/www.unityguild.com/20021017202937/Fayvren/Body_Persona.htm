Who are we, What is this?

We are Mike, Jon and Dominic...

There is nothing particularly extraordinary about who we are or what we do, but that is perhaps precisely the purpose of this experiment. The world has far too many ordinary people. Ordinary people with ordinary tales. Stories. Ideas. Dreams.

This trial of course is nothing more than to give voice to these tales. Broadcast all aspects of creativity. Or if nothing else share a little bit about one another. The power of the internet, the promise of this age of optic fibers and information is the freedom to explore and express.
