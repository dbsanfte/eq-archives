Introduction

Greetings! And Welcome to our first and hopefully not our last Silver Dragon Clan Newsletter!

First of all, if any of you are unable to view this Newsletter Please contact me and I will send you a text version. We will be archiving this and future newsletters on our main Silver Dragon Clan Website so you can view this newsletter at your convenience.

In this newsletter and those that follow, I hope to bring you news of our Guild and EverQuest. Among our topics will be those of us who are promoted, awards that have been bestowed upon guild members, a calendar of contests, events and raids, and hopefully a lot more! 

If you have anything you would like to contribute to this newsletter, please email me with your artwork, stories, poetry, humor etc... anything you would like to share with your Guildmates!

As of now, I hope to send a newsletter out every other month. Deadlines for submissions to the newsletter will be the 15th of each month before publication.

SDC Guild News

 

SDC in Dark Age of Camelot

As most of you know, some of our Clan Mates have been venturing into a far off land called Camelot. In response to the call of clan mates there for others to join them, Silver Dragon Clan is now officially a presence in DAoC. Under the wise guidance of Gippin as Guild Leader, Silver Dragon Clan now has a home on the Percival Server in the Hibernia Realm. If you should find yourself in that land, please look for Gippin so that you can be among friends. Just don't forget to come home to Saryrn every once in a while, we miss you guys!

Percival Server - Hibernia Realm

Gippin - Guild Leader

I believe other Realms of Camelot are under consideration so that SDC members can explore Albion and Midgard.

Sign Up for Our Guild Mailing List so you can stay up to date on Events happening in the Guild!

Send an email to : listserv@narf.iwhd.net (make sure you use the mail account you want all the list mail going to)

In the subject and body of the email put:
SUBSCRIBE SILVERDRAGONCLAN and your first and last name (you can use your first and last game name if you like or have one, or your real name)

for example:

SUBSCRIBE SILVERDRAGONCLAN Katani Silvershadow

Hit send. You will receive a confirmation mail, and after you are added, you will receive an email with instructions on how to send and use the list. 

 

Guild Message Board

One of the requirements of joining our Guild is to sign up for our Guild Message Board. You will find there a great deal of new and updated information from the guild leaders that will have a direct impact on Guild Members, so please when you get a moment, go sign up for the Message Board.

Please remember to use your Guild Character Name so that Guild Mates will recognize who you are when you post.

Among the topics posted there are new additions to our Guild Policy, Membership Requirements, and information for those who would like to serve the Guild as Officers or/and Advisors.  This is very important information so please make sure you visit our guild message board at least once a month if not once a week. I usually try to check it on a daily basis so that even if I can't get into Norrath, I can at least stay in touch with Guild Mates.

Please Make Sure you visit Our Message Board Soon!!!

Dragon Master Radnor!

In response to our growing numbers, Radnor has graciously accepted a position in our Guild as our first Advisor - Dragon Master! In this new assignment, Radnor will be teaching guild mates how to properly group in preparation for regular hunting parties and Guild Raids. He will be conducting classes which will involve actual grouping in the land of  Norrath as well as moderating a section of our Guild Message Board specifically for this subject. 

Please take a moment to congratulate Radnor. We hope to learn many things from him. 

Also, as soon as classes are scheduled, they will be included in updates to our calendar.

Watch the Guild Message Board for Announcements of New Dragon Masters that will be assigned very soon!

Low Level Hunting Parties

IMHO, Hunting Parties are different from Guild Raids. Usually, Guild Raids are for mid and high level dungeons that usually requires a great number of people. Hunting Parties are small groups, maybe even one group of people who are hunting together, not for major item drops, but for the experience of working together with Clan Mates. In the calendar of this newsletter, I've tentatively scheduled some guild hunting party nights for those of you who have lower level characters that might want to hunt in Crushbone, Oasis of Marr, and my favorite - Mammoth Calf Hunting in Everfrost. Please keep in mind that older players may not be hunting there so we'll only have our selves and our wits to rely on. So No Dying if you can help it! LOL

Guild Raids!

We are hoping to get back on track and host a Guild Raid every other week like we used to do. I've gone ahead and set aside some days on the Calendar below for Guild Raids, but we need to get some plans made! If anyone would like to plan/host a Guild Raid, please contact Katani or Shadmel either in game or in email. Let's get some Guild Raids started up!

Event Rules

  1. Ports to Events should be arranged prior to Event Day so that transportation can be planned in advance. (Porters, please send your name to Katani in email so that you can be listed in the announcements made through EMail and the Message Board and arrangements can be made for ports prior to the Event.)

  2. Please do not ask for Ports during an Event to locations other than the Event until the Event is over.

  3. We ask that you please allow the porters to enjoy the Event, but it is at the discretion of the Porter if they are willing to you port you to the Event after the Event has started. (If this becomes a problem, we will limit these requests to those who have previously signed up for the event by sending their information to the Host (see number 7 below, if your name isn't listed then you won't get a port - this is only in the event that port requests DURING the event starts to interfere with the porter's enjoyment of the event.)

  4. Announcements of Events will be posted on the Guild Message Board and sent through EMail to all members which should include Raid Location Information such as Maps, Loot, Mobs and specific goals of the Raids; Contest Rules, and the Low Level Hunting Information. Please make sure you are registered for the Guild Message Board and that you have submitted a CORRECT and ACCESSIBLE Email address to Katani (see emails for Katani at the end of this newsletter).

  5. Elders of Time and occasionally other Guilds are invited to all Guild Events, so OOC and SHOUTS may be used in the zone for information that needs to be passed along to everyone participating.

  6. If you are a Group Porter (druid, wizard), Corpse Locator (bard, necro), Corpse Retriever (rogue) or Rezzer (cleric and <shudder> necro), please contact the Host of the Event(s) that you are able to attend.

  7. Everyone who would like to attend Events needs to send the following information to the Event Host: Name, Level, Class, Guild (if you or the character you are participating with is not in SDC), Special Abilities, (porter, rezzer etc) (you may EMail Katani if you are unable to send that information to the Event Host).

  8. If you would like to be a Host of an event that does not currently have a Host, please contact Katani; also, we are invited to Elders of Time Events so there might be some additions to the following calendars which will be announced through the Message Board and EMail. 

  9. We are still working on a standard Loot Policy, at the moment it is up to the discretion of the Event Host as to how they would like to handle Loot. Whenever we participate in Events sponsored by Elders of Time we will follow their Loot Rules which can be found on their Elders of Time Website. The same goes for any events we attend that are hosted by other Guilds - we will follow their procedures just as they follow the procedures we set up for SDC Events.

  10. The Guild Leaders retain the authority to change or add to the information provided here so that everyone, members and non-members can fully enjoy the Events sponsored by SDC. Please send your suggestions to Katani if you have any ideas for improving upon these Event Rules.
