UnderLight: The City of Dreams

The Dream can be many things.   It can be a wonderful place that brings you all that you desire; Friendship, Adventure, Knowledge, Respect, Honor, and even Love.  Or, the Dream can be utter Chaos, and in the blink of an eye you can experience; Anger, depression, Fear, Lonliness, Cold, and even Heartbreak.  We must all choose our own paths, though some may walk together.... Others are forced to walk alone.
