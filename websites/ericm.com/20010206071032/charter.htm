What is BAD?

BAD is an old and well established online gaming Clan that creates an environment of fun, honorable gamesmanship, support, loyalty, generosity and good humor for the benefit of its members. As its name implies, the BAD Clan shuns the darkness and embraces the light. The Clan demands honorable play amongst its members, and encourages kindness, nobility and fairness in the online world as a whole.

Who is BAD?

BAD Clan members live all over the world what we have in common is our love of online gaming. We wish to enjoy the online world with our friends and provide a fun, safe and stimulating atmosphere in which to play. Many of our members have been playing together for years and all of our Founding Members are still active in the guild. BAD has a long time reputation for helpfulness and all Clan members share this quality. Fairness and generosity are also common BAD traits, along with a fierce protectiveness toward other Clan members. BAD is a family and we take care of our own. Lighthearted humor and a rollicking sense of good fun are universal BAD qualities, along with top notch gamesmanship and mastery of game knowledge and play.

Structure and Government of BAD

The BAD Clan is a democratic association. Its leaders are elected from among those currently active players who have demonstrated their experience, wisdom, willingness to serve and leadership abilities governmental structure of BAD is based upon a triumvirate. Each tier of leadership consists of three members or multiples of three. In most governmental matters, the majority vote wins.

The Grand Elder Council shall consist of three members elected from among the collective leaders (Grand Council) of all the game-realms in which BAD has an online presence. The Grand Elder Council shall preside at meetings of the Grand Council; shall be responsible for ratifying grand, sweeping changes in the Guild affecting all game-realms; and shall serve as the Court of Final Appeal for all disputes which are not resolved below.

The Grand Council shall be comprised of all those Game Council members duly elected as leaders by the players in each game-realm in which the BAD Clan is currently active. The Grand Council shall be collectively responsible for the maintenance of BAD's philosophy of game-play; shall maintain and ensure that BAD's rules and regulations are upheld; shall periodically review and ensure that the governmental structure and rules of the Clan evolve dynamically to meet the Clan's needs; shall maintain the guild-wide mailing list; shall oversee maintenance of BAD's website(s); and shall nurture the health and growth of the Clan as a whole.

The Game Council shall consist of three members duly elected by all Clan players in a specific game-realm. Elections shall occur on the 1st day of March of every calendar year. The Game Council is responsible for all game-specific issues, including but not limited to: recruitment; maintaining the in-game treasury; maintaining the game-specific website; maintaining the game-specific mailing list; resolving in-game disputes; tagging new members; creating or dissolving formal guild-alliances; providing leadership to new members; ensuring that BAD's philosophy of helpfulness, honor and fun is sustained; encouraging and leading Clan hunts or events; safeguarding BAD's good reputation in the game; and nurturing the health and growth of the Clan in the game.

If a Game Council member resigns, an Interim Election will be held immediately to fill the post. In the case of a Clan Crisis, an Interim Election may be called upon the majority vote of all current Clan members in good standing within a game-realm. A Clan Crisis is simply any situation that the majority of members in a game-realm DECIDE is a Clan Crisis. In a Clan Crisis, the membership may also vote upon the underlying issue which precipitated the Crisis and thereby reach a solution satisfactory to the majority.

The Game Council shall be responsible for Clan discipline and will, in its sole discretion, apply the appropriate disciplinary step to fit the seriousness of the transgression. The disciplinary process takes the form of Warning, Censure, Probation, or Banishment. A Warning shall consist of a private message to the member, in the game or by email, identifying the specific transgression and warning against a repeat occurrence. Censure shall consist of a Public Warning sent to the current game specific mailing list. In the case of serious or repeated transgressions, the member shall be stripped of his or her voting rights, will be placed on Probation and shall have to suffer through the normal Probationary Period as outlined in the Recruitment Procedures below (i.e., find Sponsor, go through probation and be voted back to full membership). Egregious acts and high crimes seriously damaging to BAD will result in Banishment from the Clan. Banishment must be decided unanimously by the Game Council; if the Game Council's vote is not unanimous, the issue will be decided by a majority vote of the members of the game realm.

All BAD Clan leaders and members are trusted to conduct themselves honorably and responsibly both inside and out of the game environment. Loose lips sink ships and irresponsible indiscretion and rumor-mongering on the part of a Clan leader or any Clan member will result in discipline, including Probation or Banishment, to be decided in accordance with this Charter.

Clan leaders have been elected by a majority to guide the growth and betterment of the Guild -- their hard work and dedication deserve the respect of the whole Clan. The chain of command will be respected and obeyed by all BAD members.
