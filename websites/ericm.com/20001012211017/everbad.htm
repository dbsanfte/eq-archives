BAD has entered the beautiful world of Norrath!

After we all roamed our homeland, the endless tundras of Oakvael and the vast deserts of Leng, some of us grew weary of all that walking and discovered a new world where people use far more advanced ways of transportation! They have joined the population of Britannia where they are now well respected and wealthy citizens, riding around on horseback through the well-kept gardens of their castles! They defeat nasty critters and other foes that endanger their welfare in the way only BAD's can do.

Now the Spirit of BAD is brought to Norrath, where we venture and admire the beautiful lands, to challenge the baddies and help those in need. We share our experience and treasures the BAD way, we seek to have fun!

Visitors of BAD, check out these pages and find out who we are. Enjoy your stay!
