Revelry and Honor Guild Charter
Revelry and Honor is a guild that emphasizes the two most important qualities of Norrath:   personal integrity and a desire for fun. Prospective guild members must understand that this guild is not for powerlevers or those focused on acquiring fast wealth and impressive items.  Revelry and honor is devoted to helping create a spirit of community on the Prexus server, and all members are expected to uphold the rules of conduct which are listed below:

Members are expected to contribute to events that create a sense of community on the Prexus server. 
This can include the following:
Server-wide festivals
Server-wide lotteries
Guild driven quests
Language fairs
Buffs, SOW's and binds
Offering trade skill workshops
Mentoring a less experienced Norrathian

Any member who can not contribute to a planned guild event is epected to pay a guild due, which will be used by the guild treasury to supplement the event.

Guild officers will determine a minimum requirement on time devoted to the activities stated above.

Members are expected to regularly attend guild meetings,  which will be held to discuss issues and plan events.

Guild members are invited to attend "happy hour," which will be run on a regular basis. 

Members are expected to practice rules of fair hunting, which include no Kill Stealing and no Training on others.

Members are prohibited from begging, either from other members of the guild or from the population of Norrath in general

Price gouging and scamming are not allowed at any time.

Any item up for auction should be offered to the guild members first, and the general population next.

Members are encouraged to create a character history, including background, family stories, personal stories, etc.
