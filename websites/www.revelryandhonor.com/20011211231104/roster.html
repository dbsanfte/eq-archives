To join you must read over the following things and also read out charter.
  • First and most important you need someone from the guild to sponsor your membership.
  • We require that your main character be lvl 40+. This may be subject to change as we keep growing.
  • If you have been sponsored and you are added to the voting you will need ten active member votes to be able to join.
  • Once invited you will then be set on "probation" which lasts for one month, during this time we look over how well you act with others and with the guild.
For more information on how to join please look on the Member application part of our forum. Thank you and Good Luck.
