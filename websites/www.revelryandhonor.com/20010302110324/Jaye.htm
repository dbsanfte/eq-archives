Jaye Equestrielle

Although those who meet me think I'm flighty, I'm actually a very intelligent Wood Elf Ranger. Yes, I do get lost from time to time, and I sometimes fall off the platforms of Kelethin, but I mean well.

I have two loves: Consumption of ale, and my guild. My favorite books are Mixxy's Delicacies Vol. 2, and Tome of Order and Discord, by the Priest of Discord. I enjoy long walks along the Oasis beach, and evening swims outside the Tiger's Roar.

If you see me, hail me for an ale and a tall tale.

Hail Revelry and Honor!
