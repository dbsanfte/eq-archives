Servant Of Tunare

 

  The air was thick with evil, my senses overcome by the stench of the rotting dungeon. Out of the darkness, through the cold bars, a dark hand reached for me. As I pulled away, I heard a subtle plea:


     "Malachite please, that's all I need"


     I approached the cell to find a tortured soul, a single Teir'Dal collapsed near the cell door. Regardless of one's trespasses, no one deserves an end such as this. I handed him a pale green stone I had scavenged off an elemental moments before. He started to work his summoning arts, as I fumbled with the keys.
    Looking into the cell, still unsure if this was an ambush, I noticed an opening in the ceiling.  Just then, a huge beast lunged forth & effortlessly ripped apart the bars. The Teir'Dal thanked me heartily as he ran out, promising on his honor to repay me. I turned to leave the hellacious place and saw, scrawled on the wall, "Bearron was here". I laughed as I ran, remembering when Alcuin & I found the paladin in the very same cell.

Harkas Seagrims
Servant of Tunare
