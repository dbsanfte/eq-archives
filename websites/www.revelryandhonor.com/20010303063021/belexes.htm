"You acted in accordance with the laws of the land, and the laws of your conscience. We now find you guilty of the needless slaughter of many fine Player killers. That you sought no bounty, but did it for the shear joy of inflicting pain on those just doing what they had a right to do is reprehensible. You are hereby banished to the tranquil land of Norrath for time and all eternity. There you will never be allowed to kill another player in cold blood as you have so freely done here."

    "All I did was serve justice to those who made a conscious choice to prey on the weak and the innocent. Yes I would not wait to catch them in the act. If I new they needed to be killed they were killed fast and hard where ever they were found, and there belongings distributed among the poor and the young."

    "Silence the witch! You will hunt us no more." With a great burst of force the killers and cheaters had won. Her name was removed from the pages of history and her body flung into a dimension she had never seen before. But their minds held her memory, some in joy and some in fear. Had they truly won?.....

.....Months of study in the halls of Felwithe taught the woman much. There were many new schools of magic to learn. She realized that she must devote herself to one as she always sought to master that which she did.

    "You would do well to remember the hunting tricks you learned so long ago. Though players are not the targets, many of the things we taught you apply here as well." She turned to the familiar voice. <hugs> "Darkeagle you too have come! So good to see you here." After a long reunion with an old friend, guild mate and hunting partner, she saw that he too had held to the high code of honor that drove them to ultimately leave their homes and friends.

    "Aye m'lady, I am here. I miss the owning of property but not the endless killing. There is so much more to do here, so much more to see. Follow me, let me show you around a bit while we chat."

Thus begins the legacy of Belexes the Charmer.
