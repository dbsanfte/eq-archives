A fighter with a Cause


    I lack the discipline for all the ceremonies and duties of the church; but my faith is just as strong. I serve Mithaniel Marr, not with the healing skills of the cleric, nor the bloodlust of the hired guards. One of the guards once called me a fighter with a cause.
    I travel Norrath to defend the dominated & vanquish the evil rampant in our lands. This is a long, hard road. Many travelers I have met scavenge Norrath for power. Slaying creatures to build their skills, they kill without honor to speed their ascension. Many innocent Treants & guards have fallen to the fast feet of power hungry druids & the defiling art of Death Magic. I have even seen fast Trolls killing these creatures, if you can imagine such a sight. The innocents' end may speed such shallow beings into power; but it also empties their souls.
    I fight long and hard, many times ending the day having more to overcome than when I started. But, it is an honorable fight, that I will stand and face everyday.

Bearron Brightblade
One of the Fighting Hands for Mithaniel Marr
