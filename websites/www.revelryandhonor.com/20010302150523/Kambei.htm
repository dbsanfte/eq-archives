Kambei Katsushiro

I can remember it clearly...the raindrops that fell were as big as lightstones, and the dark was blacker than Dvinn's heart. I was a wandering Ronin, a Paladin who had failed to protect her leader in battle. I made my way to South Karana to hunt Aviaks, and perhaps get some good plat along the way.

Out of the corner of my eye I noticed a sharp looking Wizard, who I later learned was Alcuin Wizziefingers. Despite his best attempts to blast me, I wouldn't go away, and I even buffed him. Of course, he was stunned by my cool demeanor and High Elf features, and we are like a pair of worn Journeyman's boots to this day.

I also remember the day I met our co-leader, Troval Nobleknight. I was sitting on the tin roof of the SteamFront Caves, slowly healing myself, when I heard a voice ask if I needed assistance. I knew immediately that he was a fellow Paladin, and when I saw that he was a High Elf, it was the beginning of a beautiful friendship. We killed dozens of Minotaurs, hundreds of Crocs, and thousands of Orc Chiefs, and countless Aviaks. One day, we decided to form a guild, so that we could share our great adventures with other denizens of Norrath. Revelry and Honor was born soon after.

I am a High Elf, and because of that I tend to have a strong sense of justice. But I also have an innate desire to help others, and when I'm not baking or fletching, I try to lend a hand to those who follow in my humble footsteps. Hail me any time you need help, or would like a tasty meal. I am a Paladin at heart, and my guild is my pride!

Hail Revelry and Honor!
