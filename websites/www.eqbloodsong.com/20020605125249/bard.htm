Welcome to Bloodsong's History and Lore

Captured for eternity and retold here by Netahn, Bardic Scald

As our ancestors retold to us their story of love and war, so do we set down with pen the task of immortalizing our glorious deeds for those who will come after. Gather 'round and pour a mug of ale. Listen now, and remember. For your children's children will retell these tales forever.

 

Through mountains high

And Time grows old

For Bloodsong

Through Dragons' teeth
And Giants' den
We've come to face our deaths again

For Bloodsong

Friendship

A moment
And it is gone
A breath
Before breathlessness
What will defies the incessant turning of the wheel?
And what remains?

A touch
Yet lingers when the fingers have left

A song
Felt once the words have faded

A deed
That lives once we have ceased

And Friendship...
The magic which turns a soloist into a choir
A tree into a forest
A community from strangers

Sharing our selves
Our love
Our pain
Validating the experience of being alive

Such is the pulse of life
brief and uncertain
Beautiful beyond imagination
Tragic beyond compare

 

 

The Lost Song

The moments heave in endless breath
The turning rhythms of the world
Although each birth betrays a death
So must our bulging sails unfurl

The journey 'cross the oceans wide
The only way the truth is found
The great unknown is e'er my bride
To her my helpless soul is bound

And as the sun begins to set
Behold! The mystic moon does rise
So lest ye ever do forget
Know change is but the great disguise

 

Farewell Fudedan

Our hearts you raised with courage true
Their hearts you cleaved with steel in two
With sword and spell in equal skill
The foulest fiend your deeds did kill
And so my friend I bid farewell
When next we'll meet I'll not foretell
But one last wish I send to thee
May your cup be full and your spirit free

 

What is Love..

What is love that enraptures me so
That it has my heart racing
As it turns my tongue slow
It can fill me with pleasure
As it fills me with pain
And makes pale mystery of the wind and the rain
It sends my soul soaring through the galaxies above
Yet I'm stuck with this answerless question
What is love...

Netahn

And Killed My Heart

My love she left me by the hill
To spill my tears to empty ground
The sound of her still haunts my ears
My fears of loss in love I've found

In battles fierce and painted red
She said she'd stay right by my side
My bride now sleeps in another bed
She fed me honey coated lies

So I do sit upon this hill
And still I wonder what will come
My run with love has taken ill
And killed my heart for another one

Netahn
