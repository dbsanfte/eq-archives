Bloodsong is always looking for people that would like to become part of the Bloodsong family. Most people become interested in joining Bloodsong because of exposure to existing Bloodsong members. Members of Bloodsong are always on the lookout for potential new blood. We are looking for mature, fun loving players of all classes and races who enjoy helping others and just having a great time. Unless you are invited into Bloodsong by special invitation, you mush be at least level 10 to join.

If this sounds like what you are looking for in a guild, send us an E-mail. Tell us how you heard about Bloodsong and tell us a little about your character like your name, class, where you are hunting lately. If you have spent time hunting with any members of Bloodsong, let us know who that was. This E-mail helps us better understand if you would be a good addition to Bloodsong.
