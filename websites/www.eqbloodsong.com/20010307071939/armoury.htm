This armoury is for the sole use of Bloodsong members. If you wish to borrow any of the items please contact the Quarter Masters, Taludorf and and Celi, and they will arrange for you to draw the item from  EQBloodsong (Quarter Master character in the game) who is based at the bank in Rivervale. It will be your responsibility to get to Freeport - please do not ask the Bloodqm to come to you as he is only a level 1 fighter.

Loans are for the personal use of your Bloodsong character, and may not be passed on to other characters. By borrowing equipment you agree to return it to the Bloodqm when you have obtained a better item, so other members can benefit from the same equipment. You must also agree to return the equipment if requested to do so by the Quartermaster or the Guild Leader.

There is a mimimum level of loan for each item, as indicated in the table, and no loans will be given to characters level 7 and under. This minimum has been imposed because Bloodsong does not wish to be seen to be giving inappropriately high equipment to very young members. That sort of Twinking can only give a guild a bad name.

A maximum of 5 items can be loaned by a character.

Loans will be made on a first to ask basis

If you have items which you feel able to donate to the bloodsong armoury, please let the Quarter Master know.

For full information on the items in the Armour visit the Everlore site at: http://www.everlore.com/items/
