Structure

In the Fraterni Lux, the individual is the most integral part of our existence. Therefore our "government" is structured in a way that provides maximum control of the group in the hands of its members while establishing a semblance of organization and direction. We operate in a very democratic fashion, where virtually all major decisions are determined by a majority vote. Any important guild decisions will almost always be ratified and voted upon by ever member within the group, so as to ensure that the guild is maintained in a manner that suits its members and not the wishes of some power mongers. However, we realize the need for some sort of "leader" figures, to make sure that things run smoothly. Therefore, the Fraterni Lux has two "coordinators", who do just that, coordinate. They will be in charge of organizing various activities, proposing plans to the guild, running the web page, and many other guild responsibilities essential for our survival. We also realize that as we grow in numbers, certain responsibilities will need to be delegated to members in order to ensure effective maintenance of a large group. Therefore, when the game finally arrives, certain individuals will be asked to perform specific roles, such as command of a squadron. Once again, the creation of any new positions and election of members to those posts will be voted upon by the entire guild. For now, our emphasis is on organization and we will not even consider creating additional ranks or a hierarchy until the final release or perhaps the beta, when such an organizational system becomes necessary. Thus, our structure will remain as democratic and decentralized as possible, so that this guild will be run by its members, as it always should be.

 

Ranks

In other guilds, ranks are given out usually under favoritism or as a measure of the influence that person has in the group. In the Fraterni Lux, we define ranks differently. A rank in our group is the measure of time, work, and effort the particular member shows. It is not a status privilege, but an added responsibility and a recognition of that person's dedication to the guild. As many may know from experience, true leadership roles are neither fun nor prestigious, as they require a great deal of time from the person's daily life and only the most enthusiastic and capable members receive such demanding roles. The group creates any ranks, and candidates elected to such posts will be selected based on their merit and ability to perform their assigned duty. No one with rank is allowed to bark orders or impose their will on others. Rather, members will not follow them out of robotic obedience, but out of respect for that person's ability and qualifications for that rank. In fact, we won't even institute a hierarchy or ranking system till the final or beta or when the need arises for us to become more articulate and organized.

 

Coordinators

There will be two coordinators in the Fraterni Lux. Their duties include representing the guild in diplomatic affairs, general maintenance of guild policies, organization and planning, and the ability to recruit or dismiss members. In addition, coordinators have the final say in matters that can't be resolved through a democratic process. The coordinators in no way assume dictatorial powers, but simply exist to make sure the guild functions properly. The current coordinators are Zealot and Ian Ash.

 

The Fraterni Lux has created no other ranks nor will there be any until the need arises. Rank and such are not our main focus, and newly created posts will be decided upon by the guild. The last thing we want to become is a bureaucracy and we will concentrate on friendship and family rather than promotions.
