Philosophy

We of the Fraterni Lux may seem complex in our thoughts and procedures, but at the very core of our beliefs is honor. The virtuous life is held in the highest regard, and since we believe honor is the highest expression of virtue, it is a value we cherish most. To live a life of honor is to enrich the soul, and through this enlightenment comes wisdom and fulfillment. Action tempered by honor inevitably leads one down the path of light. Through this general goodness of the heart, we embrace any and all who wish to lead a life of virtue. For we welcome any race and profession who shares our thirst for honor and justice, be it troll or elf, ogre or dwarf, so long as they dedicate their lives to the paths of light, they shall ever have a place among us. We believe that no race is born inherently evil, rather it is how they are reared and taught which shall determine their character and role in life. Therefore, we encourage racial unity, and welcome all that wish to pursue the path of goodness. Just as the noble elf is deemed good in the eyes of many, so too can a marauding troll or scheming thief be accepted in our midst, so long as they heed the calls of honor, recounting their evil ways and strive to use their skills for the cause of light. This we believe in, and only through our devotion to virtue do we move beyond the racial bigotries and mindless slaughters that plague Norrath. Together, we the Fraterni Lux follow the path of light, with honor ever burning in our heart. Virtue our calling, honor our bond, honor above all else!
