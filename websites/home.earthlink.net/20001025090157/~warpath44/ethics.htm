Ethics

Below are the moral principles by which we live by. We do not wish slavish adherence to any of our doctrines, but any member of the Fraterni Lux is expected to uphold the guild ethics to the best of their abilities in all that they do. For through them, we grow only stronger in might and spirit.

-A life of virtue is the goal of any Fraterni Lux. This entails the wholehearted belief in compassion, justice, sacrifice, generosity, kindness, and honor.

-Honor is the highest expression of light and virtue, therefore our thoughts and actions must be tempered by it. Let Honor not fail is our motto and that is the creed by which we live by.

-Racial prejudice or class discrimination in our ranks or towards the world at large is the sign of ignorance and is not acceptable. Judge by character and action, not by skill or trade.

-Wanton destruction, killing, and stealing are contrary to our beliefs and therefore are not permissible.

-PK'ing is allowed as long as it is in self-defense or for a just cause. Remember, use your own discretion when deciding whether a situation warrants action by force. Try thy best to stay away from fights, but if it is inevitable, then may ye prevail and justice be served!

-All must work as best they can to perfect their chosen skills, for we must always be on alert and ready for battle.

-All must be willing to work for the welfare of Fraterni Lux, and provide economic or other resources when called upon to do so, for only the diligent group shall prosper.
