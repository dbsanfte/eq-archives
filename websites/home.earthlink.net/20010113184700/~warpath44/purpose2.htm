Purpose

Our purpose, though intricately bound to our philosophy, is quite simple: to have fun. We try hard not to infringe on the individuality of our members, yet we highly emphasize group activities, meeting, loyalty, and teamwork. In addition, we place fellowship and family as one of our highest goals, and we try to approach all aspects of the game as a tightly knit group. Our purpose is also to maintain the most active, dedicated, and honorable guild around. This we take seriously, and therefore are selective in who we choose to be a member. We care more for Friendship and fun than power, money or conquest (though if we naturally obtain these things, so be it). We will be quite content to have a few members, or even become a powerhouse guild so long as our commitment to family, honor, and fun does not waver. We want to adventure together as much as possible, to role-play as best we can, and set a chivalrous example to others while enjoying the game. To summarize, our purpose is to work as a close family, to adventure together, to espouse our beliefs in virtue, and most of all, to have fun.
