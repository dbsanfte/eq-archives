Motto= "Ne Desit Virtus"

 

We welcome you to our hall. We are the Fraterni Lux, an online guild of Sony's promising game, EverQuest. We are a good group, who strives to uphold the highest standards of honor in all our actions and welcome all races and professions to join us in our quest for justice. Our name, derived from the ancient language of the High Elves, means Fellowship of Light in the common tongue, and similarly, our motto translated into common speech would be, "Let Honor Not Fail". We put a high value on friendship and activity, and look for the most dedicated players, as well as those like to role-play. The Fraterni Lux try to give each member the most individuality possible within a guild structure, being a very democratic unit which focuses more on teamwork and friendship than on guild objectives. And most of all, we take great care to ensure that everyone enjoys themselves, because more than anything else, we want our members to have FUN. We take pride in our commitment to honor, family, activity, and role-playing and hope that anyone else who shares these goals will join us in our many adventures through the world of Norrath. Please, read on traveler, and may the light of virtue shine upon thee, whatever thy race or profession may be.

Flanked by two somber looking warriors clad in heavy armor after your nearly fatal battle with a band of marauding creatures, you are led to a clearing where upon you are given food and drink. "Rest," they say to you, "here ye shall find only peace and friendship, we shall protect thee traveler, for we are the Fraterni Lux!" The name instantly conjures up rumors you have heard from various taverns of a powerful guild composed of many peoples and professions who roam the lands of Norrath to aid weary adventurers such as yourself. But save these few bits of information, the group remains largely a mystery to you, and you decide to take this opportunity to discover more about them.

As you sit and drink the warm ale provided to you by the mysterious warriors, you watch in amazement at the site that unfolds before you. Humans, working alongside elves, without any hint of disgust between each other. Dwarves, gnomes, and Erudites laugh and joke as they engage in various tasks without the slightest hint of reproach. What startles you even more is the appearance of some Ogres, Dark Elves, and Trolls, and wonder why no one else takes notice. As you instinctively draw your sword from the sheath, you hear the warriors cry out to you, "Hold thy blade traveler! We are all brethren of the Fraterni Lux, do not be alarmed at what you see! We here work together, regardless of race and profession, to bring justice to our troubled lands and uphold the highest standards of honor. None of us here hold evil intentions, but follow the paths of light and fight together in the name of all that is good. Come closer, sit near our camp fire, and we shall teach thee our ways and customs." Still amazed at all that has transpired, you slowly move towards them as you notice all kinds of people's staring at you not with contempt, but with warm smiles upon their face. As you make your way towards the campfire, you rub your eyes once more in disbelief, wondering if what you see is merely a dream, ever curious to learn more about this unique group of adventurers...
