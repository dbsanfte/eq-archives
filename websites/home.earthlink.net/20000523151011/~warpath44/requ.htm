Requirements

- All members must have a valid ISP account and mail service (no hotmail or other such programs)

 - All members must have an IRC Chat Client, and we strongly recommend using ICQ. If you do not have either of these programs, please follow the respective links in order to download them.

 - All members must try to attend group meetings or other guild events. We understand the grueling demands of real life, so if you are unable to participate, please notify the coordinators prior to the scheduled event.

 - No spamming, mailbombing, nuking or other conduct deemed inappropriate by the greater Internet community will be allowed

 - All members are expected to uphold the highest standards of honor both in and out of the game in any chat rooms, message boards, or any other forums. This Means no senseless flaming or abusive replies to other flamers.

 - All members are expected to abide by guild decisions and ethics.

 - All members should try and role-play their characters to the best of their abilities as well as work hard to improve their chosen skills.

 - You may not be a member of another guild(if you have a problem with this particular rule, please elaborate in your application)

 - No sharing or distribution of important guild information with ANYONE outside of Fraterni Lux

 - Members must try to be active at all times. If you will be gone for a period of time, please mail the coordinators and you will be placed in the reserves till you are able to resume being an active participant in the guild. Inactivity without a reason why will be frowned upon and prolonged unexplained absences will result in expulsion. We want to keep an active guild, and only through such measures will this be accomplished.
