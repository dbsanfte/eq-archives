Bear Hide Armor
In the Qeynos Hills, near the Surefall Glade zoneline, you can find an NPC named Chanda Mller. She can often be found near the campfire. Chanda can make you bear hide armor if you provide her with a bear skin and some coin. Unless you tell her that her brother brother named 'Baobob' sent you, she will not talk to you. Ask her about her bear hide armor and she will fill you in on details regarding the quality and cash requirements for each piece. (Quite possibly, depending on your faction, you do not have to say anything to her. Just simply give her the items and she will give you the armor in return.)

Here are some of the armors statistics:

Bear Hide Belt   AC 4   Weight 1.0   All Races/AllClasses
Bear Hide Boots   AC 5   Weight 2.5   All Races/All Classes
Bear Hide Cape   AC 6  Weight 2.5   All Races/All Classes
