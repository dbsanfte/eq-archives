Everyone knows how to get the container but the locations of the rings have finally all been found.  The four rings come from Gynok Moltor in Befallen and High Priestess Mitty on Kerra Isle.  Mitty has the copper, silver, and gold rings (mostly it is copper but don't get discouraged), and Gynok has the copper and platinum rings.  The end result of putting all 4 rings into the paw and hitting combine is quite nice, 9ac, +9 wis/agi, +5 mana, -5 cha, -5 sv disease.
