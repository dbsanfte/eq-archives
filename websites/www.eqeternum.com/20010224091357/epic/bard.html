BARD EPIC QUEST

There is a bard (Baldric Slezaf) in the Dreadlands who wishes to perform a symphony like no other. To do this, he requires:

    a. The top half of Page 24 of a composition by "The Maestro"
    b. The bottom half of Page 24 of the same composition
    c. Page 25 of the same composition
    d. Mystical Lute made by Forpar Fizfla in Ak'Anon

1. The top half of Page 24 can be obtained by running a relay race for a bard (Konia Swiftfoot) in West Karana.

Reward: Page 24 Top.

2. Konia will keep the bottom half of Page 24 because it contains a signature and has a high value. She will trade it for a Mahlin's Bongos.

    a. Go to Baenar in South Karana. The trigger for this quest is "What Doll?", but "Real" also pulls up some more information. - Bring tissues.
    b. Take the Invoice he gives you to the Tinkerer's Bag salesman in Sol A.
    c. Take the mechanical doll he gives you to Serra in Unrest, found next to the Spelunker.
    d. Take the note she gives you back to Baenar.
    e. Take the note he gives you to Maligar in West Karana. (1245, -10900)
    f. Maligar and Maligar's enraged doppleganger will attack. Kill both and loot Doppleganger's Head.
    g. Give head to Baenar, receive Mahlins Bongos.
    h. Return Bongos to Konia.

Reward: Page 24 Bottom.

3. Page 25 is received from Kelkim Menkia in South Karana. She has broken her lute and needs new strings for it. The strings are made of the following:

    a. Chromodrac guts: obtained off Eldig the Old, who is found in Skyfire.
    b. Red wurm guts: found on Phurzikon in Burning Woods zone.
    c. "Huge onyx drake" guts: found on Blackwing in Rathe Mountains. You can get Blackwing to spawn by killing drakes by the Sphinx hill.

Reward: Page 25.

4. Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont does not like adventurers, and will not speak to anyone unless you visit his bard friend, Vedico Windwisper, at the Butcherblock docks and help her retrieve her horn by killing Quag Maelstrom in OOT. Loot the NO DROP Horn and return it to Vedico. She gives you a note to give to her friend Forpar Fizfla. When you do, Forpar offers to make you the head and neck of the instrument. You will need:

    a. A backbone of an ancient fishman (Drops off Phingel in Kedge Keep)
    b. A strong tentacle from one of the long lost amalgyms (Amy tentacle whip from Fear)
    c. A petrified skull of a lycanthrope (Drops off Drolvarg Warlord in Karnors)
    d. The note he gives you or he might forget who you are.

Reward: Head and Neck of Mystical Lute (one piece).

5. Forpar will then offer to make you the body of the lute. You will need:

    a. Red Dragon Scale (From Nagafen, Talendor, or Ragefire)
    b. White Dragon Scale (From Vox or Gorenaire)
    c. "Some" Metal Bits (1 Smithed Metal Bits)

Reward: Body of Mystical Lute.

6. Forpar will then require you to gather components for the Strings. You must bring him:

    a. Undead Dragon Guts from Trakanon himself.
    b. The completed Head and Neck.
    c. The completed Body.
Reward: Mystical Lute.

7. Turn the top half of Page, the bottom half of Page 24, Page 25, and the Mystical Lute in to Bladric Slezaf to get the Singing Short Sword.

Reward: SINGING SHORT SWORD
1HS
16/26
Str 15, Dex 10, Sta 10, Cha 15, HP 50, Mana 10, SV Fire 10, SV Disease 10, SV Cold 10, SV Magic 10, SV Poison 10
EFFECT: Dance of the Blade
