EQ Spoilers was created by J.C. Smith, Adrian Bloy and John Paxton. All graphics were done by Hector Diaz. J.C. Smith was the original founder and editor of the original Everquest spoiler web site, EQ'Lizer. Adrian and John were both long time editors at EQ'Lizer as well. Hector Diaz was a founding member of GameZNet.

This site is a collection of knowledge from the collective staff members own experience, from user submissions and from a number of fantastic resources across the internet. Sources that deserve mention include EQ'Lizer, Allakhazam's Magical Realm, Caster's Realm, Ilia's Everquest Bestiary, EQCaster, Monkly Business, Safehouse, EQ Maps, EQ Traders and a variety of other sources.

Other current web sites from this team include Shadowbane Legends, Amazing Hideaways and Hulk Seek.
