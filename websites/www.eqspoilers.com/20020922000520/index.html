Menu Revamp - J.C. Smith

I wound up taking the day to create the first test version of our new main menu. This includes the new quick jump menus and options for our Trade Skills database and some minor changes to other areas of the site as well. I'm going to continue tweaking these areas as I go but I figured I would do things on the fly as I do with most.

The new menu options allow you to quickly jump to a list of items for any particular trade skill instead of having to go through the advanced search. They also allow you to get some new options that were not available previously. For example you may now search for Velious Tailoring items, Cazic Thule trade skill items or for Building Block items. Building block items are those items which are used to create other items.

These changes currently only appear on the front page and the trade skill pages. Once we finalize everything then I will copy it over to all of the other pages as well(hopefully within a couple days). You will notice with these new quick jump menus that there are still a couple of areas that need some heavy data entry(alchemy and fishing come to mind) and we'll be taking care of that this week.

I also wanted to take the chance to direct new readers to EQ Traders for any information that they can not find here. Recently the majority of the major equipment sites have begun to focus more attention to trade skills as a result of the May 8th changes. EQ Traders has been providing great trade skill information for years and have been a major point of reference for everyone involved. Kudos to Niami and their staff.

As always if you have any suggestions, comments or rants you can send them in by clicking here.
