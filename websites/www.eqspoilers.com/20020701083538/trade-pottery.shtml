Pottery is one of the easiest and most cost efficient skills in Everquest, and can be used to make some pretty neat things, or is used to make items needed for other tradeskills.

The point of this guide is to raise you skill as fast as possible, pretty much ignoring the costs.

 FAQ’s:

Where is the best place for creating Large Bowls?

If you do it in the Thurgadin Bank, you don’t even need to change rooms, everything can be bought there.

How do you make a Celestial Essense?

You must use your Poison skill, combining Celestial Solvent and one of many research components in mortar and pestle. 

How do you make Lacquered Gems?

You must use your Jewel crafting skill.  Combine Jar of Lacquer with the correct gem.
