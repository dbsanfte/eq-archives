Bestiary

All Zone Beastiary - This is a list of each zone in Everquest and the NPC's that reside in them.  This list provides you with a NPC name, class, race, level and hp's.

Ultra Spawn Guide - Pre Planes of Power Zones
The Ultra Spawn Guide details the most powerful spawns in EverQuest. From dragons to gods, you'll find them here.

Vex Thal Spawn Guide
The Vex Thal Guide lists the most difficult spawns of the zone called Vex Thal. These MOBs drop some of the most desired items in the game. Only the bravest of traveler's who have quested for a key through many lands, can enter.

Rare Spawn Guide
The Rare Spawn Guide lists the most elusive spawns of Norrath. These MOBs are very rare, some of which might not even be seen in the most seasoned adventurer's career.

Planes of Power Ultra Spawn Guide
The Planes of Power Ultra Spawn Guide will give you a break down of the God's, Goddess', Lords and Kings that spawn in the elemental zones. These NPC's are residents of the expansion known as the Planes of Power and this list can detail various tactics and loot tables.

Old World Planar Spawn Guide
The Planar Spawn Guide is made up of the powerful creatures from the old world nether planes.

Temple of Veeshan Spawn Guide
The Temple of Veeshan, in excruciating detail.

Western Wastes Dragon Guide
A listing of the named dragons in Western Wastes, their drops and important information regarding them.

Quick Reference Beastiary:

Pre-Plane of Power Uber Mob (Quick Reference)
A alpha list of the pre-planes of power large mobs. This is by NPC name and not zone and includes item drops and item details.

Planes of Power Spawn Guide (Quick Reference)
This is a overall list of the large targets (including mini-bosses, demi-gods, gods, lords, etc.) that reside in the Planes of Power zones.
