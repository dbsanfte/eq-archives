News For 10/26/2003

Grummus Spoiler - J.C. Smith

I've decided that the time has come to begin spoiling all of the Planes of Power boss encounters. I realize we have semi-spoiled many of these encounters already by giving vitals and tips to the encounters. I am now planning to slowly give away the complete spoiler info on these encounters. This includes the fine details that were intentionally avoided before. We'll start off the spoilers in order of difficulty, beginning with Grummus. We will also be opening a new feature on the site that will include general raid tactics in the very near future. This will include information on healing chains, weaponshield aggro transfers, feign deathing tricks, assist healing, and the like.

EDIT: After some thought I decided to add a new Boss Mobs page to the bestiary. Currently only POP Tiers 1-3 are listed. Mini-bosses are included as well as bosses. Basically any POP raid content should be listed here. Currently the spoilers link to the information from our bestiary or event guides. We'll be visiting each entry one by one and documenting them over time. I'll add the elemental mobs and some old world mobs also by order of difficulty once we finish guides for each of the tier 1-3 mobs. They are currently sorted in order from easiest to most difficult. If you have any suggestions, information or anything else feel free to email me by clicking clicking here.
