News For 4/1/2003

EQ Spoilers is Now Free - J.C. Smith

Two months ago we brought news that the Spoilers Network(including this site, AC 2 Spoilers, EQOA Spoilers, DAoC Spoilers and Gamespimp have gone free. The majority of our information will now be accessible for free by readers. Advanced searches will still require a subscription. We are hoping to be able to fund the additional server load through advertising. If you are interested in advertising with the Gamespimp/Spoiler Network then you may contact me by clicking here.

If you are already a subscriber and have ads displaying on top of your page then fear not. You may turn off these ads by Logging In. You should only need to do this once.

We are hoping that these new options will benefit the user community. We never WANTED to charge subscriptions. Unfortunately given today's market the only way for a small site to survive is by doing so. Bandwidth and server fees get quite expensive. Using the new partial subscription option we are hoping that we can provide banner free pages for subscribers, while recouping most of the server fees for non-subscribers through advertising. We hope that these changes help expose information to a new set of users that was previously only available through subscription. That's not to say that we are not hoping to expand our subscription base. Ads will not be able to fund our servers on their own, we also need subscriptions. We would also like to give back to the community, however.

Thank you for all of your support over the years. Enjoy the new site. Please excuse today's smallish update as I spent most of the day getting our free options online. If you find any bugs with the system please let me know.

New Bestiary: Captain Taradon.

New Equipment: Crow Footed Lance ~ Club of the Tides.
