News For 6/19/2003

Mid-Week Spoilers - J.C. Smith

This update features some information about the Veksar dragon and items from the Plane of Hate, Plane of Time and Veksar. I also went through about 50 more mobs and added information on resistances, hits, slows, etc. Last but not least is a spoiler for the Agnarr event in Bastion of Thunder.

New Bestiary: Remains of Sythrax ~ Garudon.

New Equipment: Kylong Darkmail Bracer ~ Bracelet of Lost Souls ~ Ceremonial Kylong Shroud ~ Rusted Wicked Faceguard ~ Double Edged Ga'Nak ~ Hopebringer.

New Quests: Agnarr the Storm Lord.

New Spells: Chill of Undeath ~ Light of Marr ~ Strike of Marr ~ Arcane Conservation ~ Marr's Blessing ~ Call of the Mistwalker ~ Reclamation ~ Garudon's Rage.

New Equipment: Band of Prismatic Force ~ Kylong Darkmail Coif ~ Viscious War Club ~ Kylong Darkmail Pauldrons ~ Moss Covered Sleeves ~ Boots of the Kunzar Wanderer ~ Platinum Plated Re`Stak ~ Ring of Exalted Freedom ~ Smokey Brazier of Sight ~ Draconian Idol ~ Anathema.

News For 6/17/2003

Veksar Dragon and Plane of Time - J.C. Smith

I went through the Plane of Time and updated a lot of items with the mobs who drop them. I believe I have been able to do that for all the known drops now. The ones that do not have a mob name are the stage drops. This update features new items from the Plane of Time, Torgiran Mines and from the new spawned dragon in Veksar.

New Bestiary: The Broken Skull Warlord ~ Innoruuk(Time).

New Equipment: Goblin Spear of Dread Tidings ~ Kylong Darkmail Leggings ~ Dukatlos ~ Idol of the Wavecrasher ~ Etched Zraxthril Warblade ~ Tiny Jade Ring ~ Shroud of Survival ~ Necklace of Eternal Visions ~ Protective Sleeves ~ Mantle of Pure Spirit ~ Cloak of Ferocity ~ Armguards of the Brute.
