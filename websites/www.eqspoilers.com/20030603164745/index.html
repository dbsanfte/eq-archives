News For 6/3/2003

Late Night Edition - J.C. Smith

I have my system back up and running, but still a lot to install and retrieve. Expect the updates to be erratic for a few days as I do this. I'll keep them coming in but they may be at odd hours of the day, as I will be updating while in between downloads and configurations.

This update features items from the Plane of Time, Plane of Hate and more. This includes new drops from Innoruuk and an updated Quest guide for the Caprin Deatharn cycle courtesy of some helpful posts at The Safehouse. The respective bestiary guides have also been updated with new strategies and information on these encounters.

New Bestiary: An Agent of Hate ~ A Vile Rat ~ A Vicar of Hate ~ A Vengeance Golem ~ A Templar of Hate ~ A Screeching Banshee ~ A Moaning Banshee ~ A Malevolent Banshee ~ A Knight of Hate ~ A Hatebone Drake ~ A Greater Kiraikuei ~ A Disciple of Hate ~ A Cleric of Hate ~ A Champion of Hate ~ Templar J`Rosix.

Updated Quests: Lower Crypt of Decay Key.
