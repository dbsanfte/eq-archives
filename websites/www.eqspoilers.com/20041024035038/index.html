2004-10-19
Submit to EQ'Lizer, Get In EQ 2 Beta! Posted by : J.C. Smith
I am proud to announce a new contest here at EQ'Lizer, that gives our readers a shot at earning a spot in the Everquest 2 beta test. We've been granted the ability to give away thirty Everquest 2 beta slots over the next three weeks. I've been playing the game myself now for a while, and I've been having a blast. Now you have your chance.

Every user that submits to EQ'Lizer between now and the end of the contest is automatically entered in to the pool. Each week we will do a lotto of those users who are in the beta pool and ten users will get access to the Everquest 2 beta test.

As a result of this, we have a few new guidelines for submissions. When you email us a submission please include your Station Name (not password just name), real name and contact information. Most importantly, your email address. Also let us know if you want a name associated with the item submission or if you want the submission to be anonymous. All forms of submissions are eligible for entry. This includes items, bestiary, quests, maps, guides, etc.

Participants must be 18 years of age or older and acknowledge that their participation in the beta is at SOE's discretion and is subject to the terms of the EverQuest II Beta agreement, the Station terms of service, and other applicable rules of conduct. This is open to North American and European Union/UK residents only.

Let the submissions begin!
