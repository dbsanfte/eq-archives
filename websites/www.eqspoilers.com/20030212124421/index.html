News For 2/8/2003

Quest Update - J.C. Smith

It's 3:06 AM but I promised you a quest update so here it comes. Please note that on the new Troll quests they are listed as coming from Grobb but are actually given in Neriak Foreign Quarter. This was done in case these quests move again so I can simply batch move rather than pick through dozens of Neriak quests to find them.

I also went through and changed a good number of quests which were changed in the move from Grobb to Neriak. A good number of other quests(not listed here) had their quest items changed to include items from Nektulos and some have also changed the originating NPCs. These new quests come from various locations but all revolve around the recent changes or upcoming Ykesha changes.

New Bestiary: Gargh ~ Zok Crikk ~ Dar Forager Lumun.

New Quests: Dwarf Heads ~ Wista ~ Ranjor's Axe ~ Deathfist Slashed Belt(Kaglari) ~ Deathfist Slashed Belts(Treskar) ~ Treskar's Tunic ~ Treskar's Weapon ~ Snake Egg Pie ~ Troll Brews ~ Pelt Upgrades ~ Gandak's Dissapearance.

Breakthrough! - J.C. Smith

After a couple weeks of stagnation we have a Planes of Power break through. Afterlife has obtained the key to Plane of Earth B and posted some details on the new zone on their web site.

In other news Grobb has been invaded by Frogloks. Grobb has been renamed as Gukta and the trolls have relocated to the Neriak Foreign Quarter. This is likely a temporary location until Legacy of Ykesha. As a result I'm going to leave the trolls flagged as in Grobb and begin documenting Gukta separately. The guards are dropping Shields of the Slain Unicorn(the old warrior only shield/weapon from Solusek). This is the only item that I found in my trip there. Gukta has been added to the database and this update features the first bits of the bestiary update for it.

I will have a quest update for you later in the day but I need to run out the door right now. Just a reminder all npc's and quests marked as Grobb are currently in Neriak Foreign Quarter but will likely move again shortly.
