News For 11/30/2003

Solusek Ro Spoiled - J.C. Smith

The last of the pre-elemental gods has been spoiled in this update. With our Solusek Ro spoiler behind us, it is time to move on to elemental mini-bosses. I hope that these guides help young guilds find their way in to the elemental planes.

New Equiment: Bone-Dust Sandals(MMC).

New Boss Mobs Guides: Solusek Ro.

News For 11/28/2003

Rallos Zek and Bertoxxulous SPOILED! - J.C. Smith

First and foremost I hope everyone has had a great Thanksgiving. This update is coming in late as I celebrated the holidays. This update is a very special one, however. With this Thanksgiving Day update we have spoiled the two most difficult bottlenecks to the elemental planes: Bertoxxulous and Rallos Zek the Warlord. These spoilers should give you all the information you need to know to get your guilds or alliances over these difficult humps. Now that these two have been added to our Boss Mob Guides page we have spoilers for each of the encounters required for access to the Planes of Air, Earth and Water. You can find information on the ways to complete the pre/post flags on our Planar Progression page. The last remaining pre-elemental god is Solusek Ro, who grants access to the Plane of Fire. Expect a spoiler for him this weekend.

New Boss Mob Guides: Bertoxxulous ~ Rallos Zek the Warlord.

News For 11/27/2003

Lord Mithaniel Marr Spoiled - J.C. Smith

The elemental spoilers are drawing near... Today we bring you the spoiler for Mithaniel Marr, who I consider as the fourth most difficult roadblock en route to the elementals. We will bring you the top three this weekend. That means Solusek Ro, Bertoxxulous and Rallos Zek the Warlord.

New Equipment: Amulet of Insight (Xex) ~ Ruby Ring of the Darkglow.

New Boss Mob Guides: Lord Mithaniel Marr.

News For 11/26/2003

Nedaria's Landing Special Update - J.C. Smith

New Quests: Shore Basilisk Hides ~ Silverfur Wolf Hides ~ Griffawn Beaks ~ Cove Frog Eyes.
