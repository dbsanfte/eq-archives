News For 6/11/2003

Returning to Subscription - J.C. Smith

Today is a day that I did not enter in to lightly. This site was originally launched as a subscription only site, out of necessity. Having been running fan sites for five and a half years now we realized that the ad market could not support a site such as this. The site was embraced by the community, nonetheless and we reached a point where we were beginning to show profits. As a result we attempted to give back to the community by opening the bulk of the site up for free, and only requiring subscription for the advanced searches. We knew that this would expand our user base and we had hoped that enough users would subscribe to make up for the added bandwidth costs. What we did not anticipate was that 1/3 of our subscribers would immediately cancel their accounts. This coupled with our user base sky rocketing, we are simply unable to carry on with that plan, and as of today this site is once again full subscription. I had previously made two warning posts that this might happen, but they did not have much of an impact on subscriptions.

The cost of a Flashlink subscription is the cost of a hamburger and it is good on all Flashlink web sites, including not only all Spoilers Network sites but Gamespimp, Stratics, EQ Maps and EQ'Lizer. If you are unable to subscribe to Flashlink then I sincerely apologize to you for the need to make this move. Our revenue has decreased signifigantly in the past couple months since we moved to partial subscription, but our server fees have increased and our servers have began to run slower than we would like them to due to the extra traffic. We can not afford to upgrade those servers. The result of this change will be our servers moving at a lightning fast rate for subscribers due to less traffic than was previously the case.
