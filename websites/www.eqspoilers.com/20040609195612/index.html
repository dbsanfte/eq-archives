06/03/2004

Gone for the Weekend
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

My Visa expires in two days so I've got to make a Visa run in the morning. Since it will be Friday here I'm planning on taking the weekend off before I face the rest of the conversion process. Let me tell you right now, this is no fun. I have put in over 30 hours so far and I'm only on the Cs, and it's boring and mind numbing work. So remember who loves you.

While keying this all in today I came across a couple dozen items that were not posted in the GameZNet database. Because so many items are being mass changed though the generators are still broken. The PHP on the right hand side does work for new items, but it only keeps track of ten of each type and we had more armor than ten, so not all of them are appearing in this update. Suffice to say we posted a lot of new items and you can find them in the advanced search or by zone listings. I'll see you all in a few days.
