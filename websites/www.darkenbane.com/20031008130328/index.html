NC organizing NOW for platoons with Liberty at the helm!22-09-2003 14:33

Easily the most organized and respected outfits in Planetside, Liberty has taken the initiative to organize some of the higher quality guilds on the New Conglomerate for the release of the new Platoons by Sony for PS.

Darkenbane will be hosting the Teamspeak server which will be organized by Xfactor {Liberty}. The plan is to invite the top end outfits/players of the NC on Emerald into this Teamspeak channel (which will be dubbed NC Alliance) so that they can work in a unified, organized and more effective manner. The Darkenbane Teamspeak server is capable of handling many folks with great voice communications to allow for this to happen to enhance everyone involved gaming experience in Planetside!

For those interested in setting up Teamspeak just click the link here and follow the instructions! If you are not in Darkenbane or Liberty you will need to request permission from Xfactor or Gray (contact Xfactor or other Liberty officers in game) or Gray on the boards (use PM) to get access.

The NC platoon led by Liberty will no doubt destroy any opposition and will be fun to be a part of! Stay tuned for more info after Platoons are released by Sony!
