The Father speaks to one of his enlightened children
Company Info [ 4-19-00 13:53 ] - Graraige D'Bane

Graraige D'Bane walks quickly past one of the DB compound structures. He hurries, knowing and feeling that the Father prepares to summon him. Before reaching the ominous Keep where he sleeps between forays, he realizes it is to late as the world swims under him. No longer even trying to walk, the feared Teir' Dal simply stops and waits allowing his God to bring him how he will to his presence.

Unlike his usual experience with interdimensional portals, Graraige never has gotten used to being called by his deity. Beyond the sickening wrench to his reality, the fetid odor of death and destruction and the horrifyingly pleasant screams of others in agony, he simply did not enjoy the loss of total control the transfer took from him.

Suddenly, without any fanfare, the control of his body was returned to him and his vision cleared. There, before him, stood the body of darkness known to many as the Father, Innoruuk. ON this visit the Father did not take on a visible corporal form, instead seemingly in a pleasant mood, remained in a vaporous trasparent cloud. His spirit, however, was quite clearly present as he spoke to his child.

"I am pleased with Majstic D'Bane's progress organizing and leading the purge. His chosen officers please me as well. It is time to begin the next...phase of the purge. Offer amnesty to all who pledge fealty to me and unite them under Darkenbane's banner. All houses who pledge loyalty to me THROUGH Darkenbane will be allowed to act on my behalf in the conquest of the mortal realms."

"Advise Captain Majstic to continue the purge destroying ALL who fail to pledge in my behalf through Darkenbane. The time has come to begin increasing your numbers on the mortal plains in preparation for my coming."

There was a short pause and the air became noticeably charged. Graraige knew when his Lord was agitated. The Father's next words were no louder than before..nor was the tone different, but the force behind it left no doubt in the Teir Dal's mind that failure would not be tolerated by the Dark God.

"Although we must unite those who desire my favor, I will not tolerate the pretenders. Bring our numbers higher, my tool, but do NOT allow the infidels to live who continue to use my name for their own reasons. The mortals who serve me will do so through my tools in Darkenbane and if they pledge not to service, they MUST BE DESTROYED! DO not FAIL ME!"

As suddenly as he had come, Graraige D'Bane found himself laying face first in the mud at the doorstep to Darkenbane Keep. He stood up, not even bothering to wipe the dirt from his face as he made his way into the Dark Keep to find Captain Majstic D'Bane.
