Darkenbane hits alliance transport routes! 21‑02‑2005 03:54
Posted by darwoth

In an organized raid consisting of 3 groups of darkenbane saturated with recent pent up rage from an excessive period of instance farming, the alliance transportation routes were hit and disrupted for approximately 5 hours.

We began he evening in dustwallow marsh at approximately 6pm est and immediately made our way to theramore island where we dispatched the group waiting to make the trip to menethil harbor and boarded the boat.



As we arrived it was quite comical to see the unsuspecting alliance waiting on the boat scurry like bugs trying to make it back to town as the force of DB members stormed the docks.



Utilizing elite haxing skills we transformed into pirates and proceeded to kill the windrider to keep the more cowardly folks from running (which is usually what happens when we raid) and then focused on the nonstop influx of high level players attempting to retake the town from both the wetlands entrance, the windrider, and boats. it was nonstop action and a lot of fun with a huge bodycount inflicted on the opposition.



Eventually we pissed off enough people and made enough of a disturbance for the trammel like banksitters in ironforge to actually show up and were met with well over 50 players to combat our 15, the battle raged back and forth for quite some time until eventually enough of our guys were finally downed and unrezzable for the remaining skeleton crew on the docks to be overwhelmed by the sheer numbers of the alliance suffering our only real casualties of the night.



We quickly regrouped and took the town once again and held it for another half hour or so until again we were pushed back due to a combination of the 3 to 1 ratio of alliance to horde and absolutely ridiculous lag, we fought a running battle onto the docks where the boat back to dustwallow popped in just in time for most of us to hop aboard and ride out like champs.



We then made our way to stormwind since the most commonly heard whine among the alliance banksitters is "come to our cities then!" so we decided to do exactly that.



We rode right through the front gates of stormwind, the only thing missing was a bugle sounding the cavalry charge, watching the bewildered alliance stop, look, and run away (apparently forgetting they were not flagged) was priceless.

Making our way to the back of the city we set up camp in the tram between ironforge and stormwind hoping that since it was an instanced zone the lag would not be horrendous, the drawback of course was that we had to wait for the opposition to grow some balls being unable to attack people at will. (we really need an ffa server, or at least a server where you can kill the other faction anywhere)



Eventually, using a few as bait on the other side of the zone we got enough people to flag and zone over into an asskicking where the many victims were forced to call in their friends to help retrieve their corpse or attempt to exact revenge for the costs of the numerous spirit ress'es.

After killing a large number the alliance wised up and decided to wait for their pvp flags to expire, got ALL READY with pre picked targets, counted to 3 and hit us at the same time.



The result was us killing about half in the initial charge, zoning over and killing the other half as they chased in.



After this nobody else bothered to show up, even with us IN the alliance capital city and all the people that could possibly be needed to route us a stones throw away, no form of resistance showed up for 20 minutes. at that point we decided to take a quick stroll through southshore given that it was getting late and people were logging.

Killing the windrider to cut off escape we then ran into the middle of the town killing everyone there and held the town for about 30 minutes as the alliance regrouped inside of the inn as some reinforcements arrived.



This was the result, and with that we opened ports and called it a night.

The majority of our guild is capped and thoroughly sick of gearing up and as such it is time to step up our presence on the battlefield, count on seeing us again real soon.

as a sidenote: thanks to the guild left for dead for putting up the only organized resistance from a guild the entire evening, maybe you can get some of the other guilds to do the same.

Two Weeks of CS Matches 04‑02‑2005 21:22
Posted by Sieges

1.6 News:

Two weeks ago the DB CS team took on =KB-Heros= on the map de_train. Starting strong as terrorists we won the pistol round. Following up with multiple rounds afterwards. We dominated the first half with a 14-1 score.

Switching to counter-terrorists for second half we kept our momentum going and only lost three rounds. Putting us at a 26-4 victory.

I'd like to extend a thank you to Ekimus for letting us use his server for the match and recording HLTV for us. If you'd like to a copy of the HLTV you can download it here:

http://s2n.gamedaemons.net/demos/2005-01-25_DBvsHerosTrain-gdtv.zip

You must own a copy of counter-strike to watch the HLTV of our match.

Heres some screenshots of the lineup and scores.



Our next match was against team Hacks 4 Sure. Starting off as counter-terrorists we grabbed the pistol round and won the second round. Losing the next round to a risky buy round from the other team. We then lost the fourth starting off the half 2-2. With some agressive play we managed to turn it around and win the half 11-4.

Going into the second half with a considerable lead we weren't too worried about grabbing the win. We won the pistol round and the next two afterwards putting us at 14 rounds. We then lost a few rounds and soon it came down to 5-5 in the second half. Team Hacks 4 Sure had some momentum going keeping us from getting our final round. The 11th round came down to a 2v1 against Iffy. With some nice rifle shots he took the two down and gave us the final round. Playing out the game we took the next 4 rounds putting the second half at 10-5. Overall victory of 21-9.



Our next match in 1.6 is against team Blue Blood Serial Killaz on the map de_cbble, wish us luck.

Source News:

Two weeks ago we played against team Lethal Hippes on de_dust2. During the week we we're told by many teams that the team we were playing was very talented and it would be a tough match. And indeed it proved to be a hard match. We ended up losing the game 13-17. We started off as terrorists and took the first three rounds due to some nice deagle shots from Iffy. We traded rounds back and fourth for the half finishing with an 8-7 lead.

Switching to counter-terrorists we lost the pistol round and the next two after. Struggling to keep up with rounds team Lethal Hippies walked over us and dominated whatever site they went to. One round actually they planted the bomb at the A bombsite. We didnt make it to the site and it blew up. The next round when the A team went to their poistions they discovered the bombsite was still on fire, which knocked Blitzkrieg down to 22hp. Due to the fire the A bombsite was unplayable for us. The other team managed to work around the fire and plant the bomb in the site. We were unable to get to it. The CAL admins told us it was an honest game flaw and there was nothing they could do about it. Hopefully by the time source completely takes over they will have these bugs fixed so it doesn't effect gameplay. We lost the half 10-5, overall losing the match 17-13. Good game to Lethal Hippies.



Last week we played against team GD on a new map they chose for CAL, de_tides. It proved to be a beautiful map with flowers and excellent building structures. In fact, I believe it is safe to say we all feel a little, how should I put this, homosexual, after playing the map. Thankfully the killing of people and sounds of guns firing made up for the gayness. In my opinion they put the map in because they didn't have any other competitive maps ready for play yet. Anyhow, we started as counter-terrorists and got off on a bad start losing the pistol round. However, the next round Deo went rambo style and ran straight through the enemie's smoke and pistoled one in the face. He then took the mp5 the terrorist had and mowed down another. We managed to grab the second round putting team GD into a saveround for the next two rounds. Winning the next two we were at 4-1. We lost the next round but had enough money to buy again. With some excellent rifling from Nothix and awping from Blitzkrieg we controlled the majority of the half winning 10-5.

Switching to terrorists we lost the pistol round again, but through a fast rush we won the second. This forced team GD into saving for the next two rounds. We traded rounds back and forth, but with some quick reactions from Blitzkrieg and his no scope awp shots we managed to win the game. Finishing out the half 9-6, overall victory of 19-11.



Our next match is on de_contra against team U DONT LAN, wish us luck.

I'd also like to welcome our newest member to the team, Jonathan Chung. We've been playing with him for a few weeks and all of us get along with him and like his play style. Not to mention he is asian, so he has a natural talent for counter-strike.
