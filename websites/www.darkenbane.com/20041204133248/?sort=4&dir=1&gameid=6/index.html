Patience; the gay virtue. 25‑11‑2004 08:45
Posted by Stoffer

The release of World of Warcraft has been exactly the same as every other game release so far.

Not even close to smooth.

The most populated server have been up and down, ours included. Sometimes everything works perfectly, sometimes nothing works. Currently a handful of servers are down with no eta, and hopefully they will be 100% fixed when they come up. A few of us have been making chars on other servers to kill the time, and although most are up, we haven't been on a server that runs problem free.

But I really don't mind. We have waited about 2 years for this game, and waiting another day won't kill me, because I know what I'm waiting for.

This IS the server where the best guilds, the best pvp and the most people on. It's already being called the "hard core" server and for good reason, because when people mention WoW pvp, they will mention this server. Stratics already did their first interview with an Archimonde guild leader and that's a pretty good symbol of how important this server is.

So have patience, killing will come.

WoW retail server 13‑11‑2004 14:12
Posted by Stoffer

Where the competition is.

Since DAoC, the community has begun to understand the need for sitting down with friend and foe to find a common battleground. WoW is really no exception, and people are working hard to designate one ”hardcore” pvp server. This is where the fame and glory, the winners and the losers of WoW will be found.

And we will be right there from day one.

Actually the greatest hurdle in designating a server from launch is Blizzard. For reasons unknown, they are withholding servernames which is a really big pain in the ass, because it prevents us from organizing all the guilds in one spot. Right now most people are in a limbo as to which timezone and which server to play.

Having a dramatic difference in server quality during open beta didn’t help at all. Most of the competition started on east 4, but that server has become so unplayable that many have left. So now we have two semi competitive beta servers, and no one really knows what’s going on. In all honesty, if I didn’t hate leveling more than anything, I would probably have moved aswell. But since there is 10 days left till release, I’ll just stay.

But back to the point. We have chosen a server, it just doesn’t have a name.

We are going with the competition.

And we are going to destroy it.
