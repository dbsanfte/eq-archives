The night before the campaign speeches were to be posted, Grayrage found out that the majority of the officer core and more importantly, Meter, was going to vote against him, so he promptly threw in the towel and resigned as GM of Darkenbane. He completely brought this on himself as the entire guild except a very select few were fully against the way he had been pushing the issue for the last couple weeks. His plans to rip the guild apart were later revealed by Lesa in which they will create a new guild.

The guild stood in limbo for awhile without a leader as Saradin took the reigns for a day, only to hand it back over to the council of the Overlords. The negotiation period lasted for one week. Before these negotations took place, there was a very hot and nasty conversation amongst the leaders of DB in TS. Clearly, that's someone who should be an officer, right? Hah.

Since this had been the 4th or so time that Gray had resigned from Darkenbane, the council wanted to create a terms of agreement before reinstating him to the overlord council. The first draft consisted of an apology, a 5 man council ruled equally, and Lesa was to be stripped of her Officer status and have to reapply to the guild since she had formerly resigned from the guild. Everyone was pretty apalled by her actions in TS and it was agreed that she should never hold a position of influence again. The ToA was rejected and the council bent to nix the apology by having a 6 man council suggested by Gray where Maule would be instated as the 6th overlord to protect Gray's interest, but we didn't move on Lesa. It was then rejected, again because of concerns of a public application by Lesa. The council then bent, yet again, allowing her to re-apply in private. The next rejection came with resignations and so concluded our negotiations. All the overlords sincerely wanted Gray to come back to the the council and hold the entire guild together. Sadly, this didn't happen because his priorities and loyalty were obviously elsewhere.
