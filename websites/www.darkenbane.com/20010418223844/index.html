    Full report from San Diego regarding PvP in EQ!11-04-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 11-04-2001 14:33

    Thanks to Shiya and Coan who took the time to represent DB at the San Diego EQ Fanfare! For the parts they can recall (they were pretty sloshed from what I hear ;p) Shiya made up a nice report:


    I got to speak with PVP GM "Lady Daegarmo", and Rallos Zek, Lead GM
    "Ozuri", at great lengths, and these are some of the issues discussed:

    1. New "Zek" Server on the way. Including possible Exp loss/gain upon
    PVP death. They seem to be gathering input still for this server.
    Movelog not a probable option for RZ players.

    2. The number one change players suggested to see on RZ, seemed to be
    getting rid of attackable blue/red cons. Having only black/white colors
    as attackable.
    Blue/red cons to become OOR, for PVP.

    3. Egg shaped pumice exploit will be changed, in that, it will cost a
    GREAT deal more, to repurchase them!

    4. De-leveled characters will drop to +1 of maxed skills level, for
    actual current level. No more 300 HP backstabs at lev 20!!

    5. Name cleanup. RZ is 1st server to start clearing the list of
    "questionable/offensive" names of characters. If you have any level
    character with a "borderline" name, you should come up with a lengthy
    list of alternate names, then petition to have it changed. This will
    save
    GM time in selecting a usable name(while their Help List continues to
    stack),and increase your chances to have a decent name. A 55 Rogue lost
    account for a racially offensive level 1 character name...lotta effort
    wasted for stupidity/rudness.

    6.NO NONSENSE POLICY. Do NOT make derogatory remarks pertaining to:
    -Racial hate
    -Sexual Preference
    -Sexual Violence
    This is a good way to lose account without warning! This is Verant
    policy and it is not to be tested!

    7. Enchanters being at a recognized disadvantage, because of all the
    high Magic Resist gear, will get a probable increase in the power of
    Tashania line of spells, unless they can find another way to increase
    fairness to Enchanters, in regards to PVP.

    Intel about PvP from the SanDiego FanFare!09-04-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 09-04-2001 22:43

    As you all know, there is gonna be a new PvP server AND some changes to PvP in EQ soon. Player reports are starting to tricke in regrading info developed in San Diego:


    I was at the discussion with Gary Grobson (aka Lady Daegarmo) regarding PvP. A number of issues were discussed. Perhaps Shiya (whom I had a chance to meet there, and is a nice person IRL - it's hard to believe the transformation that takes place when Shiya enters Norrath) can elaborate on some on this:

    1. The new server will be up in about a month.

    2. The specific rule set hasn't been decided upon - several variants of team PvP are options. Those include pure GvE, deity-based teams, and a three-team setup. I don't know what loot system is being considered.

    3. This affects primarily the race wars servers, but they're looking at not allowing beneficial buffs or heals to be cast on those not on your team. They may do it on one of the race wars servers and/or the new server.

    4. The deleveling issue was addressed. They're strongly considering making it so you temporarily lose the skill when you delevel, so that a level 40 who drops to level 11 will have level 11 (or perhaps 12) skills.

    5. Exploits such as corpse camping, portal camping, and zone line camping were addressed. A suggestion that Guides be in trouble spots 24/7 was quickly shot down. I doubt that anything will be done in this area.

    6. It's possible that newbie PK will be eliminated by making level 1-5 characters "blue". Once you hit level 6, you automatically go "red".
