Darkenbane is now recruiting for WoW 02‑05‑2005 04:46
Posted by darwoth

It has been approximately 6 months since recruitment was closed for Darkenbane. In that time we have suffered some attrition due to people going on extended bouts of inactivity for various reasons and have also cut a large number of people who were simply not making the grade.

As such, it is time, once again, to open our doors and allow new faces the chance to become a part of one of the premier PvP guilds within the community.

We are looking for a few good players to add to our online family.

Applicants must...

1> Have a good friendly attitude and be a team player. A little insight as to how we are - Darkenbane has a very laid back atmosphere with members cracking fun at each other all the time as most of us have known each other for several years. If you are thin skinned and cannot handle a joke, are a perpetually negative person who likes to bitch and complain constantly or someone who does not make an effort to meld with the guild, then do not waste our time.

2> PvP comes FIRST, PvE second. This statement meaning, on a typical week, you will spend the majority of your time in PvP combat with the guild, not in an instance farming. We are a PvP guild and that is why we play World of Warcraft. We PvE when we feel it's necessary to do so as we do understand that it sometimes IS necessary in this game. However, PvP is our guilds nature, our focus has always been killing other players and this will always come first. If you want to be raiding Molten Core or Onyxia 6 days a week, then do not waste our time.

3> Listen to directions and follow instructions at all times. Organization and communication is a big part of the key to success and thus, it cannot be stressed enough. If you are someone who cannot follow simple instructions and thusly make our jobs difficult on PvP and PvE raids, then you will not last long. Basically, if you are incompetent, stop reading and leave our website.

4> Activity and commitment. You need to be a commited player who is level 60 and on frequently with that specific character. Simple enough.


If this sounds like you then the first step is to register on our message boards and introduce yourself. Next, send a private message telling a bit about yourself, your game experience and why you would like to join Darkenbane to a recruitment officer (myself [Darwoth] or Eyepatch). You may also contact myself or Eyepatch in game as a last option under the names Wothdar and Eyepatch, but as we are often out PvPing, we may be busy and postpone the conversation. Additionally, if neither of us are available, feel free to contact Saradin or Toad.


Good luck.

CAL de_train Preseason
Last week marked the beginning of season 14 for CAL. We were matched up against team Silent Fragg for the preseason match. We started off as counter-terrorists first half and dominated the half only losing one round. We entered second half with so much momentum this team couldn't stop us. We ended up winning that half 13-2. Darkenbane over SilentFragg 27 > 3. This team seemed to think we hacked, or at least one of their players did. He couldn't seem to keep his frustration to himself and shouted out many things to us during match play, take a look!













Even with all that trash talk of calling us hackers this team still didn't dispute us.

And heres the screenshots of the scores...



CAL cbble Match
For the first match of this CAL season we took on team Five Star Killers. The first half we started off as terrorists and lost the pistol round, followed by the next two. In the fourth and fifth Byo (Ekimus) had some nice picks in middle with the AWP, which enabled us to take over the sites easily. We won both of those rounds but then lost the next four. It was 9-2 then but with some strong plays we we're able to bring the first half up to 10-5. Entering the second half we won the pistol round and the next three that followed. We didn't lose a round until the 9th, but we quickly picked back up again and grabbed the next two rounds, which gave us the win.
