Hunting season opens! 31‑10‑2005 10:22
Posted by darwoth

For millions of people throughout the US, one of the main enjoyments of the winter months is the opening of the various hunting seasons and the consequent stalking and taking of game both for sport and for the table.

In keeping with this tradition we here at DB have decided to sponsor a hunting contest for our members.

What will we be hunting you ask?





BLUEBIES!


yes thats right, bluebies. the folks who spend every moment they are logged on hiding in an instance to increase their armor by 3 and their crit rate by 0.1%

the folks who lack the same level of PVP ability that even a mediocre old school PK would posess and make up for it with thousands of man hours of gear.


the rules for the contest are simple and as follows.


1> Take a screenshot of any member of the following guilds (with their guild tag viewable and readable), upload it to the website, and you will be awarded one point.

Relentless
Templar Knights
Damage Networks
Oinland
Pantheon


2> Kills must be outside of a battleground, a true hunter knows how to stalk and quickly eliminate his prey and does not need to enlist the help of an army of hunters to assist him.

3> if you are grouped then the point for the kill will be split among however many people are in your group.

4> for ease of keeping track of the totals, on the private boards there will be a thread, post in it, this post is where you are to post a LINK (not embedded picture) of each of your hunting trophies.

5> you must have a minimum of 50 points to qualify for the prize, a winner will be declared february 1st.



The prize will be a sum of gold from the guild bank of around 200g depending upon how much we have in it at the time.

and with that, happy hunting.
