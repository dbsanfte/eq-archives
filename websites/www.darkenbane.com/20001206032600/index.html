    After successfully murdering another soulless swine, I was summoned

    distillery. The wine was composed of fermented gnome blood, and was

    of the Ages and also spoke of the Last Rites of the Tier'Dal. A messenger of

    Brell had intercepted the scroll intended for Janys and replaced it with
    words speaking of less evil intention. The Last Rites spoke of total
    domination of Norrath, and the elimination of all dragon influences. He told
    me that all traitors to Innoruuk must be sacrificed to him at all costs. The
    holiest believers of Innoruuk shall be rewarded with showers of blood and
    riches. Anything less than mass murder would be considered a disappointment.

    I agreed to spread the word to my fellow believers but Innoruuk implored me
    to do more. "True evil is more powerful than The Nameless or any blades of
    rightiousness...this is the only key to controlling Norrath" he said, and the
    truth was beautiful. Bloodlust, cruelty, revenge, and hate are the positive
    traits bestowed upon followers of Hate. False prophets, traitors to Innoruuk,
    and those who strive to defeat him must all be slaughtered with extreme
    prejudice.
