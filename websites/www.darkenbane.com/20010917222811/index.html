    • The "Gray Pact" Disbanded17-09-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 17-09-2001 13:51

    After some careful thought, the "Gray Pact" is being disbanded as it was creating expectations among certain groups of individuals that were causing more troubles than not.

    Darkenbane will conduct their own PvE events, but will still come to the call of other Neutral Guilds for PvP defense of our lands.

    We have found that the lack of organized in game communication causes far to many problems in engaging in multi-guild PvE events. We will NOT necessarily EXCLUDE people from our PvE, but for the most part such events will be DB only.

    Guild Inkara, a new addition to the Gray Pact!23-08-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 23-08-2001 12:24

    We are proud to annouce the addition of the current number one PK guild ON SZ to the Gray Pact! Saradin and Inkara have proven their ruthlessness and desire to see the success of the Neutral Team through their devastating and viscous attacks on the foe. They have no mercy in their hearts and expect none in return. Their addition to the Pact will strengthen us.

    Saradin, as a guild leader of the pact, will have admin on the secure forum as well as voting rights for it's course. All members of Inkara (GF and TDC as well) need to contact their guild leader to have permission granted for access to the board.

    Note to guild leaders: The admin panel for the boards is Here. Please select "set private board permissions" and then "Gray Pact Board" from the drop down menu. Then highlight each person's name from the left menu you wish to add (hold down the control key to select multiple people). Then push add and they will be added on the right. You will then have to give each user permission to actually post and you will see a button near their name that says "grant or remove posting rights".

    If you have ANY questions regarding the admin of the board please let me know, but it is important that we all attend this board to plan raids, organize defense, determine looting and the general course of the Pact.

    New patch message22-08-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 22-08-2001 15:05


    August 22nd, 3:00 am
    ------------------------------


    ** Crashing Bug Fixes **

    - Fixed the problem that was causing zones to crash in some instances.
    - Corrected a bug that would cause some people to hang up while waiting for a sound event between the splash and character-select screens.


    ** Item Change **

    - The Green Flower should be working again.


    ** Spell Change **

    - Fixed a stacking bug that allowed multiple Levitation buffs to stack.


    ** Sullon Zek Changes **

    - The Resist code changes we made with the last patch should be working properly now. Here is the text about that change:

    "Changed the way resists are calculated when a Player Character casts a spell on another Player Character on Sullon Zek. Resist modifiers based on level will now be more smoothly distributed and will allow lower level characters a slightly better chance to land spells on higher level characters. At the same time giving lower level characters a slightly better chance to resist spells cast on them by higher level characters."

    - Cancel Magic spells should now work across teams.
    - Characters should no longer disappear when Mesmerized or stunned.

    Submitted by Grayrage on 17-08-2001 14:02

    Per previous discussions, guilds Gladius Fatalis, The Dark Coalition and Darkenbane Company have formed the beginning of the "Gray Pact". This is a multi-guild event group that will do Planes Raids, Dragon Raids, Epic Events and PvP Events together.
