    The Point system get's a revamp!29-01-2001
    Submitted by Grayrage on 29-01-2001 16:20

    Obviously there was some problems with the points system in Fear this weekend. 3 big ones need to be addressed and HAVE been fixed:

    1. Bidding on an item is NO longer possible AFTER everyone decides to random for it. Thus seeing if u win by random THEN bidding when u lose is no longer a valid way to get an item.

    2. Bidding was to time consuming and seemed unfair. We have fixed this and I will describe the change below.

    3. The system is unfair to the more common classes as they must use points to obtain gernal class specific itmes while rare classes can save their points for God drop items, because they will never have to use points to obtain class specific drops.

    Ok bros. Every event we learn something new and make it better for the next event. The point system will evolve and hopefully be the fairest way to reward those who put in more effort than those who attend one event for 2 hours. WE must reward the people who HELP the guild the most or people will not have incentive to come. Randoming everything is simply not fair for the following reasons:

    Example: Ebula has been to all 4 events (and she has). He has stayed through the entire event as well and has been enormous help to the guild. He is a Shaman and has also received NO loot through ALL four events. Let's say we do Hate Wednesday night and a new Shaman to the guild comes on the run. This time we kill something that drops shammy stuff and the new guy wins the ROLL! Ebula will have gone on FIVE events and STILL gotten nothing while the new guy gets an item on his first event and then quits the next day.

    THIS MUST NOT HAPPEN. That is what the point system is for. Ok let me tell u the changes to the points system and then use the example above under the new points system.

    1. The points will accumulate the same way as described in a previous News post (and as will be posted in the Events folder).

    2. People will NO LONGER bid for items. IF no one is willing to use points for an item (let's say a blue diamond), the item will be randomed. IF the item is a class specific item there WILL be a COST for the ITEM. Class specific items will cost 15 points! That is a SET price. That means our ONLY monk will HAVE to pay 15 points for his class specific armor even THOUGH he does not have competiton for it. The reason for this is so he does not accumalate a zillion points and get all the god drop items simply because he does not have to use points to get items. On CLASS specific items the PLAYER with the MOST accumulated points GETs the ITEM! Once he gets it, he will lose 15 points and that will most likely drop him below whoever had the second most points so that HE gets the NEXT item. UBER items will COST 20 POINTS! Quest specific items will cost 10 points. Also players can USE their points to have the GROUP obtain quest items for them. For example: Rumproaster needs us to go do part of the cleric quest for him. Let's say he has 30 points. He can pay 10 points and the planar group will organize a mini-event to obtain the quest item. He will get his item and be reduced down to 20 points. The people who went onthe mini-event will be rewarded in points for attending the event.

    Under these changes, let's look at the Ebula example above. Ebula has at least 40 points (prolly 45) for all of his event attendance and for not getting any gear yet. The new shammy has ZERO cause he has never come before. A shammy breastplate drops and Ebula is awared the item (dropping his total to 30). A second shammy arms drops, but this time Einnod has 35 points and wins it. He get's the item and it drops his points to 20.

    You can see that the new guy prolly will not get a shammy drop with those two old timers there, but after he has put in an event or two under his belt, he to will begin to obtain items. This will reward loyalty and time invested while weeding out the moochers who just come up for one raid then scram cause' the heat is to much for them in our heavily PvP guild.

    As always, we willbe open to suggestions and will change things to make them as fair as possible. I do NOT wish to discourage new guys from joining the planar group. Many times people with lots of points will NOT go onthe trips and it will leave the item drops open for younger folks. But at the least you can count on your loyalty to the planar group paying off and being reciprocated in the end!
