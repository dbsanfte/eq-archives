Updates, updates and more updates!04-09-2003 13:41

I have been negligent in updating the status of what's been goin' on under the assumption that EVERYONE reads the forums. I forgot the millions of people who have our front page as their HOME page (yah sure!) So lemee go over some recent developments!

World of Warcraft is coming along rather well. Some great new information has become public which includes new races/classes and PvP information. WoW will have two factions (the Horde and the Alliance) which will NOT be able to group and communicate. This leads us to conclude that WoW will be very PvP oriented (team based). They HAVE released some specifics which include many guild based type battles, faction battles, PvP zones and plenty more to make me sure DB picking WoW over EQ2 (already announced not to be developed with PvP in mind) was the correct choice. The WoW Warcry headlines are always listed to your left and have great up to date info. Their FAQ is awesome as well.

I announced it in the past and will again say it now: Darkenbane will enter World of Warcraft as an organized guild with a Charter for the game and web support. We expect to enter this game with probably the best crew we have assembled for any game and will have considerable beta experience going into it's official release. During the beta we will begin shoring up our official roster for the game's release. After the release our Charter will specify recruiting policies for the guild.

While waiting for for WoW beta (expected in the next 1 to 3 months), DBers seem to be playing a plethora of games. We are supporintg Planetside in an organized fashion (and enjoying it). PS is expected to release it's new Outfit ownership of bases which will encourage Outfits (like DB) to take and HOLD bases which will be have their name on it specifying their ownership. In fact, PS has quite a few new items coming out including some new vehicles and weapons (there is a new artillary piece coming out which looks really awesome).

Planetside has proven to be great fun and a groundbreaking game that they strive to improve upon daily! We are sitll keeping our eyes open for new folks who are willing to help DB and the NC dominate on the Emerald server! If you are interested, just hop over to our PS public forum and let us know! We need killers!
