Saturday, July 13th, 2002

What can be said other than the fact the guild has gone through some tremendous changes in the past 6 weeks. However, all we can do is pick up and move forward and restore The Shadow Gypsies back to the glory we once enjoyed.

Gwerdon has taken over leadership. He's an excellent man for the job, and his duties to this guild earned him the position. The officers are 100% behind him, and we will all be working towards changing things about the guild at every level.

Keep in touch through the message boards and watch this site for changes in policies and procedures. Keep your heads held high Gypsies. We will get through this.
