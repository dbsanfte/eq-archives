Kkal Torak
Troll, Shadowknight

Aegis of the "Way"
(deceased)



And the clouds grew dark against the heavens, until even the mighty sun became vanquished by the engulfing darkness.

It was then that the calm hit. No product of nature could produce such a calm. The trees...they did not sway. The wind...it did not move. The birds no longer sang their sweet song, nor the oceans roar of their vast magnificence. No living thing dared to betray the vast, empty silence that threatened to surround them.

Then suddenly the earth trembled violently, and the heavens screamed forth! The winds, no longer content with the blackened emptiness, leapt up and slammed into the trees, threatening to tear them away from the pale ground to which they grasped! And the oceans! They were not without their wrath! Great tsunamis of towering height formed, crashing into the frail earth below. The darkened heavens then began to buckle and lurch forth. And in a blinding instant a single bolt of lightning, pure and perfect, reached forth from the sky and slammed into the tallest of peaks below. Two smoldering red eyes appeared in its terrible wake. They peered out intently as the winds began to slow, and the oceans began to flee. 'Kkal Torak' The earth no longer trembled, and the heavens no longer hid. 'Kkal Torak' The glowing eyes then gazed upon the celestial heavens above, where a star began to twinkle gently. 'Master' Lost in thought, they stared off far into the starry night while the wind continued to sweetly sing its name...
