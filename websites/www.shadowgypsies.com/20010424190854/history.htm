Shadow Gypsies -- History & Storyline

In keeping with our physical history (several years of online roleplaying), the Shadow Gypsies have developed quite a collection of stories, legends, successful (and failed) quests, and roleplay biographies.

"Seek and ye shall find. Quest and ye shall receive. Train and ye shall prevail." You might say that this sums up our present situation, but the reality goes much much deeper. In order to understand where we are and more importantly where we're going, you need to see where we've been and what we've accomplished.

The most recent major historical event, the Disappearance of Drakken, still has many of us perplexed.

What has Come to Pass

It was a new world, a dawning of a new age for those known simply as the Shadow Gypsies, and perhaps a darker, more sinister era for us all.

After thousands of years of following the discipline of "The Way," a war of cataclysmic proportions shook the world, and in the aftermath, only few remained, picking up the ruins of the books and scrolls which held "The Way."

Lan also perished, shouting out in agony as dark magics twisted, contorted, and finally destroyed him, body and soul, as if he never existed, while Drakken, an Aegis of "The Way" watched in horror, unable to help.

The remaining Shadow Gypsies, no longer held by the discipline of "The Way" were scattered throughout a new world that had emerged after the cataclysm, known only as Norrath. Their bodies, and minds changing, growing more sinister, and darker in their thoughts, and minds. No longer were they after justice, for their thoughts and perceptions changed, as they too, aged, and grew wiser. They had learned that no matter how hard they tried, justice would never be served, because people’s perception of justice changed from person to person. What was just in one person’s eyes, was abominable in another’s eyes.

Thus the remaining Shadow Gypsies sought another source of justice; another manifestation of law to guide them….they sought power. Power through strength, mystery, and through upholding a law of their own. They were not evil, yet here was a sinister side of the Shadow Gypsies, that was a storm brewing on the horizon, for they lacked the certain direction of "The Way."
