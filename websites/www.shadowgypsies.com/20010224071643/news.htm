April 9, 2000 - Kedge Results

Frith wrote: Well I sure feel like Kedge was a success. We had a few deaths at the beginning while we were getting everyone in place. Rest assured I learned from this small problem and will do my best to avoid it in the future.

Once we set up however, we pulled spawns and slaughtered them. We moved to spawn points and slaughtered them. Not a single person died during this. We killed fish... We kiled sharks... we killed voracious pirahnas... we killed mermaids. We killed the GoldenHair mermaid placeholder... We killed the CauldronBubble placeholder... and then we killed big old CauldronBoil himself. Tharaver got a real cool lookin sword. We did not have enough clerics or enchanters or tanks to feel good about taken on Estreela but we had to save something for another day.

Thank you to everyone. People listened better (particularly after the deaths) then on any hunt I have ever led. Shanna and Myutin did fantastic jobs leading the parties. Tharaver bravely held his ground as the only tank. Poxxpoxx was very busy with a variety of buffing and mezzing duties. Parrin, Uraken, Hazezon, and Mordnatyr were fantastic nukers and doters. And last but certainly not least was Demay whose healing prowess even managed to keep me alive as the puller. Frith

NOTE: Frith did a remarkable job in researching what was required to survive and pull in that environment. His prep time paid off in a big way. >Shan
