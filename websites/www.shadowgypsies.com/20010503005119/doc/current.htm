Shadow Gypsies -- Current History

There was silence, but only for a brief moment before the reaction to Drakken’s statement. Shouts of "No!"; "It is not possible!"; "You can’t!" were are said simultaneously, along with comments made in languages not readily translated.

What had started out as a celebration for the initiation of new officers of the Shadow Gypsies turned to chaos. Frith, Orcaa, Demay and Drapkin the new inducted officers, were not sure how to take Drakken’s announcement. Drakken had made his comment with no preamble, it was simply stated and took everyone by surprise. His statement was, "I am leaving the Shadow Gypsies and some of you may not ever see me again." The reactions were as varied as the guild membership. Shanna and Whitefalcon leapt to their feet as one, Whitefalcon’s mouth open, Shanna staring intently at Drakken. Parrin stood and watched Drakken almost as if he had expected this announcement and called Medwin and Myutin to him. Alendar nearly dropped one of the knives he was balancing. Mordantyr remained seated but his brow was more furrowed than usual. The new officers, unsure how to react in their new positions given this pronouncement looked at each other and the more experienced officers. The membership was in a state of confusion. Lenerin winced in pain as he hit the table with his bandaged right hand. Mourde, Gwerdon and others were in a state of inebriation and were not quite sure what was going on. Menelvagor missed a note on his lute and stopped playing.

"You can’t leave, we have come far but have much further yet to go", said Shanna. "This is not a decision I have come to lightly, I have thought about the ramifications of me leaving.", Drakken responded. He continued, "Leaving was certainly not an easy decision, but there are things on the horizon, events that will occur that necessitate my departure." Alendar had heard as much as he could before his temper and curiosity got the better of him, "Where will you go?", "What will you do?", "When are you leaving? I will go with you, you will need someone with my scouting abilities!" After Alendar’s outburst, a rasping chuckle was heard coming from the shadows, in a corner. A hooded, robed figured stepped to the edge of the light, "Still questioning the Keeper and his motives, boy? I see you have a lot left to learn." And to Drakken, "It appears that this is the proper time to depart."

The mysterious figure had the effect of silencing all chaos in the room; all eyes and ears were on him...her...it. The figure spoke further, "You have all become complacent. You have discovered part of The Way and now you act as if the search is over. I am telling you it has just begun! The Way is not something to be found on pieces of parchment throughout Norrath, it is more... oh so much more than that. You have much to seek, to learn, to discover. The trials and tribulations of this group, these Shadow Gypsies as you call yourselves, are just beginning. Drakken finally understands this... that is why he must leave. There will come a time when each of you must be ready to drive yourselves beyond your limits, you must face and endure personal trials and challenges. Drakken is ready, he must leave and leave now... at least one of you here knows and understands that. You must grow beyond him, you have depended upon him too much. I have said more than I should or am allowed, Drakken, name your successor and we must go."

Drakken turned to face the gypsies, firelight dancing across his austere features. "This, at least, was an easy decision. There are events unfolding that will have far reaching effects on Norrath and the Shadow Gypsies. I had to choose someone that has been with me long, has been through and seen many of the things I have. Someone that already has the respect of our society... Shanna Mistwolf, you will be the leader of the Shadow Gypsies." With almost complete acceptance, the membership nodded in agreement; with the exception of two who looked at each other with sidelong glances. Shanna stood and began, "I, of course, accept this..." and was interrupted when the door slammed open.

In flew a Dark Elf in an obvious rage, "Where do you think you are going!? I have not given you permission to go anywhere, you have yet to complete what we have started! You are leaving all your work unprotected." Dreyn... several of the Shadow Gypsies knew enough about him from Drakken that even if they had not ever seen him, they knew this was he. Dreyn was breathing hard, Drakken had never seen him this angry... in fact, had never seen him angry at all, just arrogant and sarcastic. Before Drakken could respond to Dreyn, the figure from the shadows spoke; "Dreyn, you really must learn to remain calm, remember your blood pressure." and laughed softly. "You!?", said Dreyn, "What are you doing here? This has nothing to do with you and your kind." Without even seeing the shadowy figure’s face, he seemed to command with his presence, "Has nothing to do with?!? This has everything to do with all we know! You are told only what you need to know by your masters; this is bigger than you, it is bigger than I. There are events that are unfolding that bear close watching. Events that are obviously beyond your ken. Silence, Dreyn, and begone, there are important issues here that you are not meant to be involved in." Dreyn sputtered, he had never been talked to in those tones before. Drakken laughed, yes...laughed as he watched Dreyn; "The decision to leave, as hard as it was, was worth it to see Dreyn react like this."

Drakken stood at the head of the table, "It is time, my friends, I must go and I must go now. There is no time for long goodbyes. I will miss you and I will not forget about you. Be safe, be well and above all... be watchful." Drakken bowed, then the figure came to his side and they turned and left... it was simple and uneventful. All eyes turned to Dreyn, when he realized that he was being watched, he left without a word and slammed the door behind him.

Shanna slowly moved to the head of the table, the place of the Keeper. She raised her head and had a solemn look on her face. Her first words, "We have much to discuss..."
