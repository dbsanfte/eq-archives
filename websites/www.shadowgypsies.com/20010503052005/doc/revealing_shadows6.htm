Revealing Shadows Vol. 6



Drakken's head was bowed as he stood over a large pile of stones, somewhere in the Rathe Mountains. It had been hard, the past few days, as he helplessly watched Kkal slowly die from the poisoned blade that has struck him in his vicious fight to save the book. Questions filled his mind, as he again replayed the events of the past few days, but was interrupted by someone nervously clearing their throat.

"Sir." Drakken looked up, and saw Whitefalcon, then remembered of his missing informant.

"I hate to interrupt sir, but Shanna received this as a result of Alendar's travels...I just received it myself, from Orcaa." Whitefalcon said, producing a note, and a small bag. As Drakken scanned the note, his eyes grew wide, then he smiled viciously. Then, opening the bag, Drakken began to laugh for the first time in days.

"Good, White! We are nearly there!" Drakken exclaimed, slapping White heartily on the back. White smiled, his sun-browned features splitting into a grin.

"I've a friend in the Neriak library, a necromancer by the name of Lims. See if you can find someone to deliver this bag to her and..." hurriedly, Drakken made an additional note on the parchment he had just received, "...give this note to her as well. She will know what to do." Drakken ordered, giving the bag and the note back to Whitefalcon.

"She'll get it by the end of the day, Drakken" Whitefalcon affirmed, before turning and trotting off. Drakken turned back towards Kkal's resting place, and knelt to pat one of the stones.

"Old friend, 'The Way' is very near returning to us...I wish you could be here."  Drakken said, his voice rough with emotion. Standing quietly to his feet, Drakken started towards Grobb...

A New Hope

Lims was deep in study, her brow furrowed as her concentration became even more intense. It had been mere minutes since Shanna herself popped into view, along with Whitefalcon, who handed her the small bag and note. Scowling at the presence of a lightdweller in her presence, Lims grabbed the items uncertainly.

"Uh-huh, just as I thought" Lims muttered, while Shanna and Whitefalcon waited nervously.

"What is it?" Shanna asked.

"Well," Lims answered, turning towards Shanna, "Using this information I've received, I could do a spell of "Revealing," but I'm afraid I am missing a vital component." Shanna raised an eyebrow.

"What is it you need?" She asked.

"I need 4 Kerra Isle beetle eyes." Lims said, closing the reference books she was using. Shanna nodded, then muttering some words beneath her breath, vanished in a bright flash of light, taking Whitefalcon with her.

Drakken jumped, as both Shanna and white appeared before his desk in a flash of light, scattering papers as he instictively groped for his sword. Seeing that it was Shanna, Drakken muttered, then began picking up papers that had fallen to the floor.

"Don't you ever knock?" Drakken said sarcastically. Shanna just grinned.

"No, too much work involved." she replied, laughing softly. Drakken scowled at Shanna, then chuckled.

"What brings you here, Shan?" He asked.

"We need 4 kerra Isle Beetle eyes, but to be honest, in light of recent events...I really am too tired." Shanna said. It was then that Drakken began to notice dark circles under Shanna's eyes, and her slightly famished look.

"I know how you feel." Drakken said,  motioning his hands towards the mounds of paperwork on his desk. "Take a few days off to go to a funeral, and this happens."

"Tell you what, I'll have Parrin gather..hmm...Nelix, Silverbrook, and a couple others to journey there. He can teleport them to Qeynos, then take the boat from there."

"Thank you, Drakk." Shanna said. "I'll go tell Parrin you wish to speak to him."

"Fine, after that, you should go to bed." Drakken said, shooing Shanna out of the office.

Shadow Raid

The raid went as planned, but took nearly 3 times as long as what was thought in order to acquire the beetle eyes.
