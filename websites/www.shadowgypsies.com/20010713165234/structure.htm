Shadow Gypsies -- Guild Structure

The Shadow Gypsies are an old guild, so our structure (at the highest levels) has maintained the same form and function for several years. We had to make some accomodations to Everquest, however, but we feel we've come up with something functional and interesting.

At the highest levels, we have TWO guilds on Rallos Zek. The first is this one, the Shadow Gypsies, and the second, the Shadow Makers, formerly known as the Veiled Alliance in Meridian 59.

The Shadow Gypsies are led by the Keeper and at least one Aegis, plus a council of Knights. The Shadow Makers are led by the Guildmaster and Guardian, but they defer to the judgement of the Shadow Gypsies' council on important matters.

Please check out the Shadow Makers website for a more detailed explanation.

Beyond the leadership in the Gypsies, we have several levels of members, starting with the bottom rung of Squire (new members or those in a probationary period), then Soldier (the bulk of the membership), and a few Captains (who may or may not be officers).

Further explanation will be forthcoming as to the requirements for entry into the guild and eligibility for promotion.
