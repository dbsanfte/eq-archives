Amrosse Bele'dhel
Woodelf, Warrior

Soldier of the "Way"



Not many think of elves as living within the cold, northern Antonican Tundra. I am one of these such elves. I lived many years of my life in the snowy clutches of the Everfrost Peaks' tall, white walls. I would return many a time even after leaving whenever my travels would be so fortunate to take me their way.

I know nothing of who my birth parents were, or what happened to them. I know only that I was abandoned to the cold tundra snow, left to die. I was found quite luckily by a kind Barbarian woman after being in the cold outdoors for only a short time. She took me and raised me as her own for she had had no other children of her own, nor would she ever.

In her earlier years my foster mother, Di as I called her, was a great warrior of the Wolves of the North. They were the great protectors of the Barbarian city of Halas, and the surrounding Everfrost Peaks. The Wolves of the North were kind, just and had to be the strongest, and biggest warriors one would ever see in his life. The female warriors were these, but also some of the smartest and most strategic warriors one might see.

Di taught me all she could of the warrior's way of life. She taught me not only in the warrior's trades but also in other skills used in life courtesy, fairness, justness, strength, and above all to never give up. You were to fight to the very end, and never give up. Whether this be in some quest of yours, or just another fight with an attacking thief on the road. I have always headed this and all her other lessons.

When I was old enough, and there was nothing left that could be taught me, I was sent to Kelethin to train with the Emerald Warriors. Though I had learned much from Di and the Wolves of the North they could not train me to my full potential me being not of the same race. So I had come to train with my blood race the elves. Who I had never met a single soul.

The Emerald Warriors immediately took me in of course me being such a strange type of warrior. My training started the very day after me and Di's arrival. I was sad to leave the only family I had known, but also excited at the same time with all the experiences I dreamt to be in store for me.

After sad goodbyes with Di, and many warm welcomes from the Emerald Warriors I started my new life. There were more new and exciting experiences for me than I had ever imagined. I made new friends, helped protect Kelethin form Orcs and Pixies, fought against an invasion of evil Dark Elves, Trolls, and Ogres, and started numerous new trade skills that I would use all throughout my life.

I soon found that not all Dark Elves, Trolls, Ogres, and even Orcs are not evil. One such Dark Elf being Shanna. A lovely wizardess of the Dark Elven race. Shanna introduced me to the Shadow Gypsies and they soon took me into their guild. There is nothing else to tell for I cannot tell the future. We shall just have to let the story of our lives unravel and see first hand what the future has in store for us all.
