New World



Drakken awoke to find himself in an evil countenance of a Troll, gifted with the combat skills of his former knight and cursed the evil tendancies and abilities of that necromantic Lich. He searched, and fought, and searched some more... but nothing was familiar, and he did not know what to do.

Finally, he located someone who sounded like one of the Shadow Gypsy knights, and another who had the bearing of one who had followed "The Way" for a lifetime... and finally found Shanna, trapped inside the body of a Dark Elf with evil power still running through her veins. He would find others, but it would take some time, and the gods were not as kind to him as they once were.

So he determined that he and the others had been condemned to a hell far less gracious than mere death. Drakken then set about to identify as many of the old Shadow Gypsies as had been sent to this new hell, and gather them together for some semblance of the fellowship they once had had.

So it was that they turned to Drakken, to fill the shoes that Lan had left woefully empty, providing the leadership they sought. Drakken knew he could not fill those shoes alone, for there remained others who themselves were also "Aegis" of "The Way," from the old order. Tirelessly Drakken sought them out across Norrath, from the frosty tundra of Everfrost, to the deadly heat of the Ro desert.

And so here we are: Condemned to a world we did not ask for, cannot change, and have very little power to control. We must group, or die. Stand together, or be torn apart. Kill or be killed.

Thus, the Shadow Gypsies are reborn...
