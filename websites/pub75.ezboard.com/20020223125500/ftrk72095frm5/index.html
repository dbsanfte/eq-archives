To become a member you must do the following:
1. Be at least of the 35th level
2. Agree to The Royal Knights Code of Honor.
3. Group and interact with members to gain 3 recommendations from current members. NOTE: to bring up a list of TRKers on, type /who all "The Royal"
4. After you have posted your interest here your post will be moved to the members area for discussion. A post will be made stating it has been moved. A one week discussion period will follow, after which applicants may join at any time that they have their 3 recommendations.
We will contact you in game or in a post on this board as soon as a decision is made. ;)
