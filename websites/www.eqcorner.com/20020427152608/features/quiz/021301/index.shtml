Quiz 1: Clerics
by Aracnakat | February 13, 2001


How well do you know EverQuest? Sure, you've camped that special item for hours, you've leveled perhaps many various characters ... but do you really know EQ? Well, here's your chance to prove to yourself how much you really know about this virtual world.

Our first test is focused around Clerics. How much do you know about them?



1. What is a cleric known as when they reach level 51? a. Templar
b. High Priest
c. Vicar
d. Prelate
 2. What healing spell, new with Scars of Velious, does a cleric get at level 44 which heals over time? a. Celestial Healing
b. Celestial Blessings
c. Celestial Elixir
d. Celestial Bondage
 3. Which of the following evil Races can be a cleric? a. Iksars
b. Dark Elves
c. Ogres
d. Trolls
 4. What is the name of the goblin which lives in Lake Rathetear which clerics must kill for their epic quest? a. Lord Gimblox
b. King Tahoozi
c. Goblin King Lavish
d. Lord Bergurgle
 5. Which God do all Dark Elf cleric worship? a. Innoruuk
b. Cazic-Thule
c. Bertoxxulous
d. The Tribunal
 6. Which gem is used for the level 51 spell, Death Pact? a. Peridot
b. Bloodstone
c. Pearl
d. Jasper
 7. What is the name of the cleric epic quest weapon? a. Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
b. Holy Flail of the Ankh
c. Ankh of All Gods
d. Flail of Reviviscence
 8. Assuming a cleric has all spells through level 49, how many new spells can a cleric get at level 50? a. 0
b. 1
c. 2
d. 3
 9. Which cleric spell summons a player to their corpse and returns the greatest amount of experience lost after a death? a. Revive
b. Resuscitate
c. Reviviscence
d. Resurrection
 10. What is the name of the purple armor for clerics found in The Plane of Hate? a. Insidious
b. Apothic
c. Imbrued
d. Ethereal Mist
 11. What are the three primary statistics for clerics? a. Wisdom, Intelligence, Charisma
b. Wisdom, Strength, Dexterity
c. Wisdom, Stamina, Strength
d. Wisdom, Intelligence, Agility
 12. Which of the following elven races can become clerics? a. Half-Elves
b. High Elves
c. Wood Elves
d. All of the above
 13. Where would a cleric find the spell, Armor of Protection? a. Any cleric guild
b. Drops off random creatures in Velious
c. Drops off random creatures in Kunark
d. Thurgadin and Iceclad Ocean
 14. What is the faction name given to the cleric guild members in Rivervale? a. Clerics of Underfoot
b. Priests of Marr
c. Priests of Mischief
d. Clerics of Tunare
 15. Which race starts life as a cleric with the most statics points? a. Dwarves
b. High Elves
c. Dark Elves
d. Erudites
 16. How many loaves of bread can a cleric summon by casting the spell Abundant Food? a. 1
b. 4
c. 8
d. 10
 17. Which "Imbue" spell is used for the Dark Elf racial armor? a. Imbue Sapphire
b. Imbue Ruby
c. Imbue Peridot
d. Imbue Black Pearl
 18. Which of the following skills does a cleric get? a. Taunt
b. Block
c. Dodge
d. Riposte
 19. About how long does the spell Symbol of Marzin last? a. About 30 minutes
b. About 45 minutes
c. About 60 minutes
d. About 90 minutes
 20. Which race, when chosen as a cleric, is "born" with the lowest wisdom? a. Halflings
b. Humans
c. Erudites
d. Gnomes

That's it! You're Done!

If you like, go back over your answers and make sure you're ready to have your test graded. When you're satisfied, click on the "Submit For Scoring" button below.
