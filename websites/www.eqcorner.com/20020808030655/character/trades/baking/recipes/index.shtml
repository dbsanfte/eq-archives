Baking Recipe Chart

This chart is listed with the recipes end results in alphabetical order. The majority of baking recipes are made in an oven or spit but some of the items are actually made in a mixing bowl. Ovens are stationary and can be found in most (if not all) towns while spits can be carried like backpacks in your inventory. Most of the utensils needed for some of the items are made by blacksmiths or potters.

The majority of baking ingredients are found on various creatures or foraged within the game, while some items like spices, jug of sauces and frosting can be purchased from any baking vender.

If you know of recipes which are not listed here or have more information, please submit them to be added.
