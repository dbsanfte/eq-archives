Quiz 24: Firiona Vie
by Kwill |October 31, 2001

Firiona Vie is pictured on all the EverQuest artwork, and is the signature character of the game. How much do you know about her, and how much do you know about the backstory of Norrath?



1. Firiona Vie is a: a. Warrior
b. Magician
c. Paladin
d. Enchanter 2. Firiona's home city is: a. Freeport
b. Felwithe
c. Kelethin
d. Qeynos 3. Firona Vie's bard companion is: a. Lorisyn
b. Lyriae
c. Sionachie
d. Sylia 4. Firiona is the Avatar of what God? a. Tunare
b. Bertoxxulous
c. Karana
d. Brell Serillis 5. Firiona was kidnapped by: a. Laarthik K'Shin
b. Danak Dhorentath
c. Lanys T'Vyl
d. Vendela G'Kal 6.Her kidnapper used a ________ to capture her: a. glowing stone
b. enchanted weapon
c. magical ring
d. magical necklace 7. Firiona's Arch Enemy, the "Child of Hate" is... a. Laathark K'Shin
b. Danak Dhorentath
c. Lanys T'Vyl
d. Veeshan the Wurmqueen 8. Lyirae Oakwind, friend of Firiona, is a... a. Ranger
b. Bard
c. Druid
d. Warrior 9. Dweezil, companion of Firiona, is a.... a. Cleric
b. Warrior
c. Druid
d. Rogue 10. Firiona rescued Dweezil when he was: a. Attacked by a dragon
b. Accused of stealing
c. Kidnapped by an iksar
d. Held captive by wood elves 11. Firiona had something taken from her: a. Lost her powers of healing
b. Lost her ability to wield her sword
c. Lost her memory
d. Lost her powers as an avatar 12. Firiona has what item that is coveted by the forces of darkness? a. sword
b. breastplate
c. staff
d. shield

13. The item Firiona had stolen from her is named: a. The Lifebringer
b. The Lifeguide
c. The Lifeseeker
d. The Lifegiver 14. The above item can: a. bring people to their bind point
b. completely heal
c. bring back life
d. bring back memories 15. Who has stolen this item? a. Laathark K'Shin
b. Venril Sathir
c. Klum Machata
d. Vendela K'Shal 16. Firiona was rescued from her dire plight by this person: a. King Thex
b. Lyirae
c. Galeth
d. Dagda 17. This person hatched the plot to kidnap Firiona: a. Laathark K'Shin
b. Venril Sathir
c. Veeshan
d. Lanys T'Vyl 18. Firiona has a truce with: a. The Claws of Veeshan
b. The Ring of Scale
c. The Minions of Underfoot
d. The Crimson Tier 19. Firiona Vie could not stop evil forces from causing the death of her friend: a. Falrazim Zaftamor
b. Lorisyn Oakwynd
c. Lyirae Oakwynd
d. Dagda Icefury 20. The above person was killed by: a. an ogre
b. a dark elf
c. an iksar
d. a dragon

That's it! You're Done!
