Beastlord: Only allowed with the Shadows of Luclin expansion.

Some information from Beastlord message boards:

  • The pet summoning is a skill, which appears in your
    "Ability" box when you reach level 9.

  • Everytime you summon a pet, zone, or log in, or gate, the pet says "Ding!" The pet starts at level 5, and once you gate or zone it will revert back to level 5 and will need to be rebuffed again.

  • Pets have the highest level of any pet class (so far).

  • Beastlords do NOT get double attack.

  • The primary beastlord pet buffs can be purchased in ShadowHaven (along with pet heals)
