Quiz 12: Shadow Knights
by Delrachnid | May 1, 2001


Diabolus of Shadowed Soul


Shadow Knights are the evil counterparts to Paladins. Part warrior and part necromancer, these evil knights use the powers of the undead to aid them in their battle against the forces of good. Shadow Knights work best in group combat but through the use of a pet and their dark magic can solo very effectively. But there's so much more to these dark knights .. how much do you know about them?
