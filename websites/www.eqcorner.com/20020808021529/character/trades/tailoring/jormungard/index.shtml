How to Become a Master Tailor
Submitted by: Jormungard, Iksar Monk of Vallon Zek

Becoming a Master Tailor is a nine step process:
  1. Make silk swatches until skill 17
  2. Make Patchwork armor until skill 26
  3. Make Silk armor until skill 37
  4. Bring your smithing skill up to 41 to make studs and steel boning.
  5. Make Studded Leather armor until skill 57
  6. Bring your Brewing skill up to skill 68 to make Heady Kiola
  7. Make Cured Silk armor until skill 82
  8. Make 10 Slot Backpacks until skill 87
  9. Make Reinforced Armor until skill 108
Step 1

Combine two spider silks in a sewing kit to make a silk swatch.

Step 2

Combine any ruined cat, bear, or wolf pelt with a pattern in a sewing kit to make Patchwork armor.

Step 3

Combine silk swatches and patterns in a small or large sewing kit to make Raw Silk armor.
  • 1 silk swatch: Bracer, Cloak, Collar, Gloves, Headband, Mantle, Mask, Sandals, Sash and Sleeves
  • 2 silk swatches: Tunic and Leggings
Step 4

Work on smithing until skill 41 to make studs and steel boning.
  • In a forge combine rusty weapons and a sharpening stone until skill 27
  • In a forge make metal bits (combine 2 small pieces of ore and a water flask)
  • In a forge combine a file mold, metal bits and a water flask to make a file (trivial at 21)
  • In a forge combine a file, a water flask and 3 metal bits to make studs (trivials at 41)
Note: To make steel boning for Reinforced Armor later on, combine in a forge a small brick of ore, a file and a flask of water (trivials at 41 also).

Step 5

Combine medium quality cat, bear, and wolf pelts, studs and a pattern in a large sewing kit to make Studded Leather Armor.
  • 1 stud: Mask
  • 2 studs: Belt, Bracer and Gorget
  • 3 studs: Cap, Mantle, and Sleeves
  • 4 studs: Boots, Cloak, Gloves and Pants
  • 5 studs: Tunic
Step 6

Work on brewing until skill 68 to make Heady Kiola.
  • Gather snake scales.
  • In a still, make Bog Juice (combine snake scales, a flask of water, and a bottle) until skill 21
  • In a still, make Short Beer (combine barley, malt, hops and a cask) until skill 31
  • In a still, make Mead (combine malt, hops, yeast, and a cask) until skill 41
  • In a still, make Short Ale (combine barley, hops, a flask of water, and a cask) until skill 51
  • Using the fishing skill, gather fish. In a still, make Fish Wine (combine a fish, a flask of water, grapes and a bottle) until skill 62
  • In a still, make Ale (combine barley, malt, a flask of water, and a cask) until skill 68
  • Using a still, you can now make Heady Kiola (two kiola saps, a flask of water, and a bottle) with a good amount of success. Heady Kiola trivials at 46 skill but is still difficult to make
Step 7

Combine silk swatches, Heady Kiola and patterns in a large sewing kit to make Cured Silk Armor.
  • Mask requires one silk swatch, pattern and one Heady Kiola
  • Gorget, Slippers, Bracers and Belt requires one silk swatch and two Heady Kiolas
  • Helm requires one silk swatch and three Heady Kiolas
  • Mantle, Sleeve, Cape, and Gloves require two silk swatches and two Heady Kiolas
  • Pants require two silk swatches and three Heady Kiolas
  • Gi requires three silk swatches and two Heady Kiolas
Step 8

Combine a high quality bear skin and a backpack pattern to make ten Slot Backpacks.

Step 9

Combine high quality cat, bear, and wolf pelts, steel boning and a pattern in a large sewing kit to make Reinforced Armor.
  • 1 steel boning: Bracer, Gorget, Belt and Mask
  • 2 steel boning: Cap, Cloak, Gloves, Mantle, and Sleeves
  • 3 steel boning: Boots and Pants
  • 4 steel boning: Tunic
You will now be a Master Tailor (108), congrats!!!
