Monk

Monks are dedicated to the pursuit of shaping their bodies into a pure weapon, and because of this they nearly shun all weapons and most forms of armor. They are very religious as well, and the gods are known to bless them, not by prayers and spells, but by magically enhancing their attacks.

Monks are considered by many to be the best melee class in all of Norrath. Some of their abilities include: kick, round kick, tiger claw, eagle strike and flying kick. Possibly their most admired ability is feign death. With this ability a monk can trick their opponent into thinking that they are dead. Monks are great in groups as they are known for their distinct ability to lure creatures one at a time. As with most of the melee classes they are also effective at lone adventuring and have the advantage of their god given skill, mend wounds.

At high levels, monks gain special abilities called Disciplines.
