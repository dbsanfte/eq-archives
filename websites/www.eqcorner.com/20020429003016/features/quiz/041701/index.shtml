Quiz 10: Warriors
 by Aracnakat | April 17, 2001


Hobjn, Kalorn & Hungrie
This image was used with permission from Divine Alliance



Warriors are the ultimate tanks and because so many races allow them, they come in a vast array of sizes and colors.  Strength is one of their primary stats and because of their bulging muscles and extensive training they can wield most any weapon with the skill and cunning of a seasoned adventurer.  But there's so much more to this class ..



1. How many races can become warriors? a. 9
b. 10
c. 11
d. 12 2. At what level do warriors obtain the bash skill? a. 2
b. 4
c. 6
d. 8 3. According to Verant, what are the primary statistics for a warrior? a. Strength, Stamina & Agility
b. Strength, Agility & Dexterity
c. Strength, Stamina & Dexterity
d. Strength, Intelligence & Wisdom 4. What title does Verant bestow on all warriors once they attain level 60? a. Beastmaster
b. Myrmidon
c. Champion
d. Warlord 5. Which race, other than Iksar, is not allowed to worship Rallos Zek, the Warlord? a. Gnome
b. Dwarf
c. Barbarian
d. Wood Elf 6.In what city can the warrior guild fondly named "Pit of Doom" be found? a. Grobb
b. Halas
c. Neriak
d. Oggok 7. Which of the following two races are the only two that can not be warriors? a. Erudites & High Elves
b. Gnomes & Erudites
c. Wood Elves & High Elves
d. Gnomes & Wood Elves 8. What kind of gems are needed to complete the quest for the warrior only, Crafted Plate Boots? a. Fire Emeralds
b. Rubies
c. Sapphires
d. Star Rubies 9. Which of the races listed have the ability to "hide" as Warriors?  a. Half Elves, Barbarians & Ogres
b. Gnomes, Iksars & High Elves
c. Dark Elves, Wood Elves & Halflings
d. Ogres, Trolls & Humans 10. What is the warrior guild in Kaladim known as? a. Peacekeepers
b. Storm Guards
c. Soldiers of Tunare
d. Steel Warriors 11. Assuming that they put all of their allowed extra statistic points into strength, which race would start life with the highest possible strength? a. Barbarians
b. Dwarves
c. Ogres
d. Trolls 12. Which of the following disciplines will allow a warrior to automatically riposte every attack landed on them by the opponent they are facing? a. Aggressive
b. Furious
c. Precision
d. Fell Strike 13. Assuming they skip no steps, in which zone would an Iksar warrior start their quest for the "Trooper Scale" armor? a. The Overthere
b. Field of Bones
c. Cabilis
d. Warsliks Woods 14. What is the maximum number of skill points that a level 60 warrior can have in archery? a. 210
b. 225
c. 230
d. 240 15. At what level do warriors receive the discipline "Aggressive?" a. 52
b. 54
c. 55
d. 60 16. How many rubies are needed to complete the quest for the warrior only Crafted Breastplate? a. 2
b. 3
c. 1
d. None 17. Which discipline is this describing? "When using this discipline all landed attacks will automatically be either critical hits or crippling blows." a. Mighty Strike
b. Precision
c. Furious
d. Fell Strike 18. Of all the races that can be warriors, which one has the highest total of statistic points when added up? a. Barbarians
b. Dwarf
c. Ogre
d. Troll 19. What is the name of the warrior only armor found in the Plane of Hate? a. Berserker
b. Indocolite
c. Incarnadine
d. Insidious 20. When the two separate swords of the warrior's epic are combined what is the final two-handed slashing weapon called? a. Blade of Strategy
b. Blade of Tactics
c. Jagged Blade of Mourning
d. Jagged Blade of War
