Quiz 13: Enchanters
by Delrachnid | May 7, 2001


Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


Enchanter spells focus on manipulation of the mind, and this mental acuteness gives them more spells than any other class. With spells like Clarity and Charms it's no wonder that Enchanters are sought after by groups all over Norrath. But regenerating mana and charming creatures isn't all that they do well .. take the test and see how much you know about these mystic snake charmers...

b. 5
c. 7
d. 11 2. What title does Verant bestow on all level 55 Enchanter? a. Illusionist

c. Beguiler
