Quiz 7: Paladins
by Aracnakat & Delrachnid | March 27, 2001
Special thanks to Replay Promisekeeper for his help.


Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


Defending all that is good and true in the realms, these warriors gain special powers from the gods themselves!  Much like a cleric, a paladin can heal and resurrect the dead at high levels but they can do much more then that.  Their gods grant them many special abilities ... how much do you know about these valiant knights?



1. Which one of the following spells do paladins get which clerics do not. a. Divine Strength
b. Holy Armor
c. Shield of Words
d. Valor 2. All four of the mighty paladins listed below were in the final Best of the Best Tournament on the Test Server, but which one of them was the victor? a. Greyreaper - Bristlebain
b. Kojiro - Luclin
c. Willis - Tarew Marr
d. Wyldkell - E'ci 3. How many races can elect to become paladins? a. 4
b. 5
c. 6
d. 7 4. What paladin only sword can not be used by humans and has 45 AC in addition to other beneficial stats? a. Fiery Avenger
b. Fiery Defender
c. Nature's Defender
d. Sword of Rile 5. What paladin (and cleric) quest item has you slaying a living and undead version of Lord Grimrot to complete the quest? a. Aegis of Life
b. Armor of Ro
c. Nature's Defender
d. Brilliant Sword of Faith 6.What are the NPC paladins in the Karanas known as? a. Deepwater Knights
b. Karana's Defending Knights
c. Knights of Thunder
d. Knights of Truth 7. Which of the following gods is not one of the selections available to paladins of any race? a. Bristlebane
b. Quellious
c. Rodcet Nife
d. Tunare 8. Which race starts off life as a paladin with the most statistic points? a. Dwarf
b. Half Elf
c. High Elf
d. Human 9. What is the name of the paladin only armor found in the Plane of Fear? a. Deepwater
b. Indocite
c. Valorium
d. Verdant 10. What is the effect on the Deepwater Breastplate? a. Armor of Faith
b. Complete Heal
c. Lay of Hands
d. Symbol of Pinzarn 11. Assuming that you don't skip parts, which NPC starts the "Divine Might" quest? a. Darak Lightforge
b. Diggs Duggin
c. Gwendolyn Lightheart
d. Valeron Dunshire 12. What title does Verant bestow on all paladins at level 51? a. Cavalier
b. Crusader
c. Knight
d. Templar 13. In which city does a paladin begin life if they choose Tunare as their god? a. Felwithe
b. Freeport
c. Kelethin
d. Qeynos 14. What is the effect on the paladin sword "Ghoulbane" called? a. Banish Undead
b. Dismiss Undead
c. Divine Light
d. Yaulp II 15. According to Verant, what are the primary statistics for paladins? a. Strength, Dexterity, Wisdom & Charisma
b. Strength, Stamina, Agility & Wisdom
c. Strength, Agility, Dexterity & Wisdom
d. Strength, Stamina, Wisdom & Charisma 16. Starting at what level can a paladin cast spells (this does not include their special ability to Lay of Hands)? a. 1
b. 5
c. 9
d. 15 17. At what numerical value does a paladin's archery skill cap? a. 50
b. 75
c. 100
d. 125 18. What is the name of the paladin only "haste" belt which is dropped off a mob in the Plane of Sky? a. Belt of the Divine
b. Belt of Piety
c. Belt of Swiftness
d. Belt of Virtue 19. What does the level 60 paladin discipline, "The Discipline of Sanctification" do? a. Grants the player immunity to most harmful spells.
b. Causes critical hits to undead with more frequency.
c. Makes the player immune to fear or fear type spells.
d. Adds +10 to all resistances. 20. What effect is found on the paladin epic weapon, Fiery Defender? a. Holy Armor
b. Holy Heal
c. Holy Moley
d. Holy Shock

That's it! You're Done!
