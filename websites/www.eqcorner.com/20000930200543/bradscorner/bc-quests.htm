Re: Split Paw / Monk Headband Quest

10/10/99:

Re: Fiery Avenger Quest

rokdim@my-deja.com wrote: If I could add my voice to this request. I don't want hints, clues or any help. All I would like is a solid yes or no from Brad that the Fiery Avenger is obtainable in game at this moment with no expansions, future patches or special GM help. I'll hail every NPC, kill every giant rat, trade with Broon, jum of cliffs, swim down trenches and hug every bixie as long as I know that it is possible. so how about it Brad, just reply to this thread with a "yes" if it is obtainable in the game right now or a "no" if it is not.
