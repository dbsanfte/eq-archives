Mrog's Brudder's Stwil Stoopids
by Mrog

Wez started our nite wif da thoughs of kiwlin da froggies again. Dey is eazy for us to kiwls an dey is gowd eats for da stu. Me nawt no why bu wez lets da wittle brudder Taog led da ways.

Taog is da wittlests and he stoopid lik da odder brudders so he gots losts and wez fights da lizard mans instead of da froggies. Dey were much harder bu we kiwls dem gowd anyways.

As always mes wook for da Kogg bu he was nawt anywhere around us. So mes wook for da odder Ogre mans to mates wif. While wez fights da lizard mans a nice Ogre mans named Wrench come an ask to join us. Of course da poopie heads brudders sez NO so no gowd chance for mes to mates wif da Wrench.

Later as wez fights da hard lizard mans Wrench come over an bash on it an takes it from us. Dis makes da brudders very mads cause dat lizard mans was for da lizard stu. Dey yells at da Wrench to leave us alone. Few times later da Wrench comes wif da lizard mans wif da magix sparkles an asks for help. Da brudders sed NO so poor Wrench dies. Mrog try to hewp hims bu da brudders holds Mrog down an nawt let mes heal him or hewps him. Poor dead Wrench, da brudders spits on hims corpse.

Wez gots hungwy so wez went home so mes cook da stu an wez eats gowd. Dem wez go bak outs again. Dis time wez on da way to froggies. (Froggies taste bedder den da lizard mans me finks). Bu da brudder Phrog lead da way an we gets losted again.

Dis time wez runs into awl kinds of wittle peoples. Dey awl tawlk funnies an da wittle brudder Taog tries to eats dem. Dey did look yummie bu dey fast wittle foods so wez gives up an dey runs aways.

Wez gots to meets a big nice mans name of Dyvim Kincaid. Hims sez wez gots da bad grammer. Wez nawt no wha dat means. Hims was nice an tawlks funnies. Brudders sed hims make der heads hurts wif da funnies tawlking. Wez didn't understand much of wha he sez bu he waz cutes so mes asks hims to mates wif Mrog. Hims was gonna say YES bu da stoopid brudders scareded hims away. Dey sed nawt to mates wif Mrog an dey smash hims face. (Brudders is so stoopids!).

After da nice and cutes Dyvim rans aways we was still losts an some ugwy mean Orcs attacked us. Wez amosts dies but wez is stwong an kills dem awl. Dey gots da nice homes so wez cook dem up in pot an makes da orc stu an wez stay at dems homes for da nites.


Left to right: Flog, Phrog, Mrog, & Taog


While we at da nice Orc homes we hear strange voice tawlk to us. Der was no ones around an dis scared mes. Mes was gonna runs homes when it say it name Fibbly an dat is a halfling tree gawd. Fibbly come up to us an it was a tawlking tree. Wez was scared bu we nawt run. Brudder Phrog try to eats da Fibbly an da tree gawd runs away from us.

Den after da tree runs away more Orcs comes an dey mads cause we eats der odder orcs and dey attacks us. (Orcs is nawt nice!) Wez fights da orcs an dey awl deads too. Mrog cook up more orc stu an da big nosie Bambkak cum to seez us. Wez invites hims to eats wif us. He is nice for da big nosie Trwoll an hims sed wez stwrong to fite awl dez orcs.

After da nice Bambkak leave us da stoopid Taog an Flog gets home siks for da cave an dey leave Mrog an Phrog alones at da Orc home. Wez nawt as stoopid as dem so wez leaves an goes to fight da froggies.

Wez kills many froggies den wez goes home to wif lots of froggies meats for da goodest stu of all.

Mrog
Priddy sisah of Phrog, Taog, & Flog

At this point, Mrog and her brothers are all around level 8. More adventures to come as they grow in their disfunctional family!
