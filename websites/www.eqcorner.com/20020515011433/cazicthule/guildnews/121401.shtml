Cazic-Thule Weekly Guild News

December 6- December 14, 2001
Compiled by Kwill on 12/14/01

Note: unless news is emailed to me, I check all guild websites on cazicthule.com for news, and post recent news I find there.

If you would like to have news of what your guild is doing around Norrath posted here, please email kwill@planeteverquest.com. And, if for some bizarre reason you would not like your guild featured in this report, let me know that too!

News from Guilds Around Cazic Thule:

Blind Faith Division
Nothin much goin on the past few days, people just checkin out the new zones (fungus grove, go somewhere else 105 players at any given hour) and the rest who dont have the expansion are chillin in the old zones grinding out the xp. Me, Mal, Ritah and afew others decide to check out The Grey. The usual crazy drop rates for these new zones, we probably walked off with about 2-3 decent items each and had named mobs about every 5 minutes. I think its something they did on purpose to lure people to buy thier new expansion and as usual when the novelty wears off and they sucker enough people over there they will nerf the hell out of it.

Anyway today we'd like to welcome Aumeshing, Aarorn, Tephlon and Falorm as our latest lackeys. Please purchase coffins at your local merchant.

Cohort Chalybeius
December 14th: Each day there's a patch, CC/Talons moves a little bit further in our quest to clear NToV, and last night was no exception.

First, again, was Eashen of the Sky. Grats to Rulak and Minke on their Claws of Lightning.

Next was Ikatiar. Grats to Soonice on her Ancient Wyvern Hide Leggings, and Murf and Catch on their
Ancient Wyvern Hide Boots.

Then Roy. Not even 3 adds could deter us from taking this guy down without a wipeout, although the
HT from the black drakes hurts! Grats to Ulthumbra on his Katana of Pain and Farun on his Circlet of
Summer.

Lord Feshkar...we didn't have a chance for a good try on him the night before because of time
constraints, so he died easily last night. Oh yeah, almost forgot, Valorath did a great job on the pull of
this meanie.

Grats Hass on his Staff of the Silent Star, Skeezix on his Thunderstone, and Kaylynne on her Vambraces
of Discontent. Dargan..he's a wurm!

Grats Olebass on his Bow of the Destroyer, Saze on his Ancient Wurm Hide Leggings, and Whopper on
his Wurm Claw Pauldrons.

Last up for the night was Lord Kreizenn.

Grats to Gukaz on his Kreizenn's Flame, Warlor on his Silver Disk, and Delila on her Dawncaller.

The random drops for the night were a Ring of Superiority to Heellar, two Shields of Thorns to Sehria
and Twenky, a Bow of Silver Fang for Jozz, Shield of Wurms for Motu, Shadow Fang Necklace for Tegian
and Shield of the Protector for Conanan. Great work everyone! It was definitely a night to be proud of!

December 13th: Server down again, meant Iki, Eashen and Roy again for us.

First Eashen, grats to Shaofung on his Fist of Lightning and Vyce on his Cloak of the Sky. Then Iki...Grats Saze on his Ancient Wyvern Hide Tunic and Pava on his Mask of Venom.

Next was our good bud Roy. The fight went as smooth as it did the night before, thanks Warlor for
documenting what happens when a rogue ends a dragon fight with 8 hps left. Grats to Kinson on his Katana of Pain and Avar on his Rocksmasher.

Assorted drops as we cleared were a Shadow Fang Necklace to Talons, a Silver Fang Necklace to Murf,
a Bow of the Silver Fang to Widowstein, a Runed Fang Necklace to Whopper, a Shield of the Dawn to
Delila and a Shield of the Wurms to Egral.

Grats to everyone that got some new loot to play with, it was a fun night for us all!

December 12th: Thanks to Verant, we got to do it all over again! This time the lag was better, there were no mass LDs and we got the job done!

First up again was Ikatiar. Grats to Tarisk and Tegian on their new Poison Soaked Tunics. Then Eashen, Grats to Kinson on his Ring of the Sky and Whoa and a Talons Monk (sorry, didn't catch the name) on their Fists of Lightning. Set up again for Roy, time was on our side again. One of the mobs along the way decided to drop a Bow of Silver Fang for Sylverblade.

Then, the big guy, Roy....It always feels good to drop a mob on the first attempt! Grats to Spiritsong on his Katana of Pain and Tyriaana on her Circlet of Summer. A very good night all around for CC/Talons, securing our spot on a NToV rotation!

December 11th : CC/Talons made their first venture into NToV yesterday. Connectivity issues and lag was a huge factor, but we still had a good time and managed to kill 2 of the first 3 mobs.

First, was Ikatiar the Envenomed. Grats Murf on his Ancient Wyvern Hide Tunic and Zugshad on his
Cloak of Venom. Grats Talons on their Ancient Wyvern Hide Sleeves.

Then came Eashen of the Sky. Grats Druss on his Twisted Steel Bastard Sword and Darrak on his Cloak of the Sky. Grats Talons on their Bracelet of the Sky.

Aaryonar's guards gave Talons a Ring of Superiority and Shaofung a Silent Fang Necklace. It got to be
too late with the impending patch for an actual attempt on Roy himself, so we'll down that beast today!

Crimson Talon
DEC 12, 2001
Both the Prince and Overking of Chardok were killed this evening. This raid was our second prince killing and our first king, and it was also our first large scale raid that mixed standard pulling and AE tactics. Grats to Mysyn for getting enchanter epic piece and to Skylynn for getting to within a Ragefire of a cleric epic! We are very close to having two more class epics within the guild. Other drops this evening were Argent Defender [Lisner] and a Palladium Axe [Valstorm].

DEC 09, 2001
Attack of the killer Groovs. Today was practical joke time on our resident Sorcerer Groovit. About twelve of us made HE wizards with names similar to Groovit's including: Groovite, Groovism, Groovitsold, Groovstyles, Groovikat, Groovinacht, Gagrgov, Groovitsego, Groovdawg, Groovycajones, Groovieluv, Groovtastic, and Groovthaag. We sent Groov a message over guildchat that Al'Kabor was in Felwithe and needed the assistance of a powerful sorcerer. Groov quickly loaded up port and rushed to GFay as quickly as he could to try to get in on the event. As soon as he ported in though, the 'Groovs' began to spam him with stupid questions and OOC'd/shouted Groovit's typical hotkey setups. Took him a couple minutes to realize that he had been had and even though the real Al'Kabor was not present, a fun time was had by all.

DEC 08, 2001
A number of guild raids have taken place over the last several days in numerous zones on the moon. The Grey, Akheva, Mermaid's Eye, Fungus, and The Deep.

Knights of Eternal Light
Everyone please welcome Hatik and Tarnrik to the guild.

Everyone please welcome: Quizzan Shadowhawk 47th Wood Elf Ranger (main), Draygiin 12th Dark Elf Wizard (alt), Tanniim 5th Vah Shir Warrior (alt), Flaconeye 54th Human Druid.

Pale Riders
Hail Riders! While everyone is getting used to SoL and getting their computers upgraded, we have been doing a few exploration runs in SoL. First off we hit Maiden's Eye, and got some good Medalions and weapons. Then we headed to Arkheda (Or something like that) Ruins. There we picked up some really decent weapons including a 14/27, 8str 8sta sword that has a badass graphic. Lastnight we spent some time in Fungus Grove and got a truckload of spells. We also managed to pick up a few 6ac 55hp 55mana 10DR 5CR rings. We will continue exploring in the next few days.
