Quiz 14: Wizards
by Delrachnid | May 22, 2001


Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


Wizards are the master seekers of knowledge, and their talents and spells are focused on this goal. They use powerful spells to not only see things from afar, but to transport them there as well. This thirst for arcane knowledge has also made them the true masters of the strongest form of defense: offensive spells of pure destruction. In their ability to do magical damage, they have no rivals... but how much more is there to know?
