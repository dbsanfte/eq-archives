Quiz 16: Lower Guk
by Aracnakat | June 5, 2001



Lower Guk ... the home to both undead and alive Frogloks.  This dungeon is arguably one of the most loved and dangerous of Antonica. 



1. If you were to exit Lower Guk, which zone would you end up in? a. Innothule Swamp
b. Upper Guk
c. South Ro
d. The Ferrott 2. From the dungeons listed below, which one also has an abundance of Frogloks? a. Cazic-Thule
b. Tower of Frozen Shadows
c. Old Sebilis
d. Castle Mistmoore 3. What is the name of the rare spawn NPC in Lower Guk which is needed to complete the quest for the monk only "Robe of the Lost Circle"? a. Raster
b. Zephyl
c. Qwinn
d. Sasaris 4. What are the stats of the "Crown of the Froglok Kings" which is a common drop from The Froglok King in Lower Guk? a. AC 10, SV Fire +10, SV Cold +10
b. SV Fire +20, SV Cold +20, SV Poison +10
c. SV Poison +20, SV Disease +20
d. AC 12, SV Fire +20, SV Cold +20 5. Which of the following classes needs to go into Lower Guk as part of their Epic Weapon quest? a. Enchanters
b. Clerics
c. Paladins
d. Wizards 6.Which one of the following items drops in Lower Guk? a. Gatorscale Leggings
b. Serpentine Bracer
c. Runed Bone Fork
d. Collar of Undead Protection 7. What level is the Minotaur Patriarch? a. 30
b. 35
c. 40
d. 45 8. Which NPC drops the Shining Metallic Robe? a. Ghoul Archmage
b. Ghoul Assassin
c. Ghoul Lord
d. Ghoul Savant 9. When you kill The Froglok King, which faction(s) will you be hurting/lowering? a. Undead Frogloks of Guk
b. Frogloks of Kunark
c. Frogloks of Guk
d. All of the above 10. Necromancers have a quest for Shadowbound Gloves which takes them to Lower Guk, in which zone does this quest start? a. Temple of Solusek Ro
b. Neriak
c. Innothule Swamp
d. Freeport 11. What is the name of the rogue only item dropped by the Ghoul Assassin which procs Engulfing Darkness? a. Serrated Bone Dirk
b. Short Sword of the Ykesha
c. Executioner's Axe
d. Dark Reaver 12. By approximately what percentage does the Flowing Black Silk Sash increase your attack speed via it's perma haste effect? a. 5
b. 10
c. 15
d. 20 13. Which NPC rules the Undead Frogloks in Lower Guk? a. The Froglok King
b. Slaythe the Slayer
c. The Minotaur Patriarch
d. The Ghoul Lord 14. As part of the Mage epic quest, what type of creature must they kill in Lower Guk to obtain the 3rd page? a. Deadly Black Widows
b. Granite Gargoyles
c. Bloodthirsty Ghoul
d. Greater Ice Bones 15. What class is the Evil Eye? a. Enchanter
b. Mage
c. Necromancer
d. Wizard 16. Killing any of the "undead" frogloks in Lower Guk will raise what faction? a. Undead Frogloks of Guk
b. Frogloks of Kunark
c. Frogloks of Guk
d. None of the above 17. What quested spell for rangers will take you into Lower Guk at some point to get an item needed to complete the quest? a. Call of Flame
b. Call of Earth
c. Ensnaring Roots
d. Wolf Form 18. Which creature's rare drop is the Short Sword of the Ykesha? a. Reanimated Hand
b. The Ghoul Lord
c. Slaythe the Slayer
d. The Froglok King 19. What class is the Ghoul Lord? a. Necromancer
b. Shadow Knight
c. Rogue
d. Warrior 20. Before this item was removed from the loot pool, which creature dropped the Mana Stone? a. Frenzied Ghoul
b. Froglok Noble
c. Evil Eye
d. Minotaur Elder

That's it! You're Done!
