Quiz 19: Blackburrow
by Aracnakat | June 25, 2001



Blackburrow is not only a dungeon but a passageway from one part of the realms to the other. Danger lurks around ever turn in this dungeon, even entering can be dangerous as others frantically scramble out of the zone whenever the shout of train goes up.



1. You can enter & exit Blackburrow via two zones, which two are they? a. Everfrost & Surefall Glade
b. Qeynos & Misty Thicket
c. Everfrost & Qeynos Hills
d. North Karana & South Karana 2. What type of creature makes their home in Blackburrow? a. Frogloks
b. Orcs
c. Gnolls
d. Kobolds 3. Monks have a series of quests to obtain various headbands, which headband quest would take them into Blackburrow to complete? a. Orange
b. Red
c. Blue
d. Purple 4. What is the name (faction) of the clan which lives in this dungeon? a. Ry'Gorr
b. Paebala
c. Mucktail
d. Sabertooths 5. What creature would you need to kill to obtain a +2 Agility, Onyx Earring? a. Elite Gnoll Guard
b. Gnoll Commander
c. Lord Elgnub
d. Socho Darkpaw 6.There is a quest which originates in Qeynos Hills which asks you to retrieve something from Blackburrow by killing Lord Elgnub. What is the item you need to retrieve? a. Silver Talisman
b. Claw
c. Fine Pelt
d. Baby Joseph Sayer 7. Approximately what level is Lord Elgnub? a. 25
b. 32
c. 38
d. 45 8. If you were trying to raise your faction in this dungeon, which of the following NPCs which are found elsewhere would you kill? a. Starn Bearjumper
b. Hager Sureshot
c. Orcish Mountaineer
d. There is no way to raise this faction 9. Which of the following choices would get you to Blackburrow from East Karana? a. E. Karana to Beholder's Maze to Clan Runnyeye to Misty Thicket to Blackburrow.
b. E. Karana to N. Karana to Blackburrow
c. E. Karana to N. Karana to W. Karana to Qeynos Hills to Blackburrow.
d. E. Karana to N. Karana to W. Karana to Qeynos Hills to Surefall Glade to Blackburrow. 10. A Spiked Collar can be found in this dungeon, it has 3 AC and one other stat, what is it? a. +3 Dexterity
b. +3 Strength
c. +3 Charisma
d. +3 Agility 11. What type of armor commonly drops in this dungeon? a. Bronze
b. Banded
c. Ringmail
d. Cloth 12. The Ruined Totem Staff is sometimes found in Blackburrow, out of the following, in what other two zones can this staff be found? a. The Feerott & Innothule Swamp
b. Lake Rathetear & South Karana
c. Misty Thicket & Qeynos Hills
d. Rathe Mountains & East Commonlands 13. How much mana does the Ruined Totem Staff give it's wielder? a. 0
b. 5
c. 8
d. 10 14. Larsk Juton of Surefall Glade gives a quest which takes you to Blackburrow to retreive Blackburrow Casks, what is the reward for completing this quest? a. Cloak of Jaggedpine
b. A Secret Brewing Recipe
c. Faction & Coin only
d. Elven Charm Necklace 15. What class is Tranixx Darkpaw? a. Warrior
b. Shadow Knight
c. Rogue
d. Shaman 16. Which of the following factions can be raised by killing various creatures in Blackburrow? a. Blood Sabres
b. Corrupt Qeynos Guards
c. League of Antonican Bards
d. Guards of Qeynos 17. In which of the following zones would you find the closest wizard portal to Blackburrow? a. South Ro
b. The Feerott
c. West Karana
d. North Ro 18. Lysbith Mcnaff will give you coin and faction if you bring her teeth from Blackburrow. In what zone can she be found? a. Halas
b. Qeynos
c. Everfrost
d. Surefall Glade 19. What class is the Master Brewer? a. Warrior
b. Paladin
c. Ranger
d. Shadow Knight 20. Where is the Pit Trap located on Level 1? a. A tree
b. A hut
c. In a cave
d. None of the above.

That's it! You're Done!
