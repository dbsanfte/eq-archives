Quiz 5: Druids
by Delrachnid | March 13, 2001

Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


How well do you know EverQuest? Sure, you've camped that special item for hours, you've leveled perhaps many various characters ... but do you really know EQ? Well, here's your chance to prove to yourself how much you really know about this virtual world.

Foraging, tracking, kiting and casting spirit of the wolf aren't the only things these masters of nature can do.  Take the test and find out how much you know about these nature babies?



1. What, according to Verant, are the primary statistics of the druid class? a. Strength & Stamina
b. Wisdom & Intelligence
c. Stamina & Wisdom
d. Wisdom & Charisma 2. How many races can become druids? a. 2
b. 4
c. 5
d. 7 3. What is the maximum point cap of the druid tracking skill? a. 100
b. 125
c. 175
d. 200 4. Where does the "Paw of Oppola" quest start?  a. Freeport
b. Ocean of Tears
c. Qeynos
d. Misty Thicket 5. At what level does a druid receive their first "succor" line of spells? a. 24
b. 29
c. 34
d. 39 6.Which 50+ druid spell grants the caster an improved wolf form with an increase of 70% movement, 62% attack and ultravision? a. Form of the Hunter
b. Spirit of the Oak
c. Form of the Howler
d. Mask of the Hunter 7. In what zone does the druid gate spell "Winds of the North" leave you? a. Emerald Jungle
b. Iceclad Ocean
c. Skyfire
d. Toxxulia Forest 8. Which component is used in the level 60 druid spell, "banishment?" a. Diamond
b. Opal
c. Peridot
d. Star Ruby 9. What is the name of the druid only armor found in the Plane of Fear? a. Elder Spiritist's
b. Ethereal Mist
c. Thorny Vine
d. Vermiculated 10. What is the effect on the druid's epic item, "Nature Walker's Scimitar?" a. Summon Food
b. Wrath of Nature
c. Regrowth of the Grove
d. Nature's Touch 11. What is the name of the Coldain Dwarf who gives the druid specific velium armor quests in Thurgadin? a. Dain Frostreaver IV
b. Daggo Giantkiller
c. Cobi Frostbear
d. Ru Paul 12. What title does Verant bestow on all Druids at level 51? a. Beast Master
b. Heirophant
c. Preserver
d. Wanderer 13. In which zone can you purchase the level 44 druid port spells? a. Kael Drakkal
b. The Overthere
c. Iceclad Ocean
d. Wakening Lands 14. Which spell, requiring an opal, allows the druid to stop rain or snow within the zone they cast it in? a. Breath of Ro
b. Circle of Summer
c. Wake of Karana
d. Glamour of Tunare 15. What animal does the level 55 druid spell "Nature Walker's Behest" summon as a pet? a. Bear
b. Wolf
c. Lion
d. A Ranger 16. In addition to the various gems, what is the other component needed to make druid specific velium armor? a. Corroded Plate
b. Eroded Leather
c. Tainted Cloth
d. Rotted Chain 17. What is the name of the NPC in the Sky Plane that gives the "Test of Nature" quest? a. Fenalla Moonshadow
b. Telin Darkforest
c. Tholian Grasshopper
d. Will Treewalker 18. What item can a druid forage only within the Plane of Growth? a. Branch of Planar Oak
b. Branch of Sylvan Oak
c. Speckled Molded Mushroom
d. Ripened Mango 19. What 60th level druid spell grants mana regeneration? a. Spirit of Oak
b. Bonds of Tunare
c. Protection of the Glades
d. Nature's Touch 20. Which Velious zone runs a high risk of being attacked by the dragon Woushi when you teleport there? (assuming you have negative faction) a. Cobalt Scar
b. Great Divide
c. Wakening Lands
d. Western Wastes

That's it! You're Done!
