Quiz 6: Anniversary Edition
by Aracnakat, Delrachnid, & Korelin | March 20, 2001
Special thanks to Aash, Caleal & Wranglir for their help.


Image taken from The Official EQ Anniversary Page


Two years ago last Friday, EverQuest opened it's doors on a whole new world filled with monsters, treasures and vast adventures.  Many things have happened over the last two years.  How much do you know about the beginnings of EverQuest? 



1. By what percentage has the death penalty been reduced since the release of the game? a. 10%
b. 25%
c. 50%
d. 75% 2. What was the first class to have it's own full set of quest armor? a. Bards
b. Clerics
c. Paladins
d. Warriors 3. What was the name of the first character to reach level 50 in beta? a. Aracnakat (Cleric)
b. Asmonius (Wizard)
c. Kendrick (Rogue)
d. Grog (Warrior) 4. Which of the following items was in beta but has never made it to the released game? a. Eye of the Beholder
b. Green Splint Platemail
c. Sword of the Guard
d. Belt of the Clear Mind 5. When EQ was first released what did the Hags of Unrest do which had everyone scrambling for the zone line whenever she made an appearance? a. Charmed Players
b. Used Harm Touch
c. Summoned Players to Her Lair
d. Summoned All Creatures in the Zone to her Aid. 6.What was the name of the first character to reach level 50 after the game was released? a. Quinda (Cleric)
b. Aash (Ranger)
c. Kalaran (Rogue)
d. Korelin (Warrior) 7. What NPC dropped Journeyman's Boots before it was changed from a drop to a quest? a. Ancient Cyclops
b. Drelzna
c. Hasten Bootstrutter
d. Fippy Darkpaw 8. What was the name of the supposed warrior armor (which by an error could be used by WAR, CLR, PAL, RNG SHD, BRD, ROG & SHM) which dropped in the Plane of Fear before Indicolite was released into the Plane of Hate? a. Kings Armor
b. Lustrous Russet
c. Glorious Chain
d. Ceremonial Guard 9. What was the first alternate plane of existance to open? a. Plane of Fear
b. Plane of Hate
c. Plane of Sky
d. Fear, Hate & Sky Opened On Same Day 10. When the alternate planes of existance were first opened, what was the level limit imposed by Verant to enter? a. 45
b. 47
c. 50
d. There Was No Limit 11. What was the first dungeon to be overhauled and have a significant change in level? a. Black Burrow
b. Befallen
c. Infected Paw
d. Cazic-Thule 12. What ranger only leg armor, which no longer drops in the game, originally dropped off of dervishes? a. Oak Leaf Greaves
b. Petrified Bark Leggings
c. Legs of Nature's Preserver
d. Stone Embued Legplates 13. What was the name of the ranger & druid only sword dropped by Lady Vox which no longer drops in the game? a. Vox's Breath
b. Mistwalker
c. Dragon's Bane
d. Windcaller 14. What event was proceeded by the introduction of high level undead into Kithicor Forest? a. The Release of Kunark
b. The Opening of the Plane of Hate
c. The Release of Velious
d. The Opening of The Hole 15. Before it was removed as a dropped item in the game, in what zone could you find Rubicite Armor? a. Mistmoore
b. Plane of Fear
c. Upper Guk
d. Cazic Thule 16. On the day of EQ's release, what was the scheduled time that the servers would be opened to the public? a. 9:00 a.m. PST
b. 10:00 a.m. PST
c. 11:00 a.m. PST
d. 12:00 p.m. PST 17. How many phases of beta testing were there? a. 4 full & 2 sub
b. 3 full & 3 sub
c. 6 full
d. 5 full & 1 sub 18. Before being removed as a dropped item in the game, where would you find a Manastone? a. Cazic Thule
b. Befallen
c. Mistmoore
d. Lower Guk 19. What item, which used to drop in Lower Guk could change anyone's appearance to that of a dark elf without actually wearing the item? a. Guise of the Deciever
b. Dark Mask
c. Face of Evil
d. Mask of Deciet 20. Before the game was released, what division of Sony Online Entertainment did Verant spin off from? a. 989 Studios
b. The Sony Station
c. Sony Interactive
d. Ubi Soft

That's it! You're Done!
