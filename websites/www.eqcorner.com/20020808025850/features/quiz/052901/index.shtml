Quiz 15: Rangers
by Aracnakat | May 29, 2001
Special thanks to Wranglir Lightheart for his help


Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


Rangers are in tune with nature and as such share the spirit of the druids. With many druidic spells at their disposal they are also fierce warriors, and exceptional with the bow. Rangers are excellent group members and are often used to track, pull or heal in the down time, however; they can also solo as they have the ability to buff and heal themselves.... but how much more is there to know?



1. How many races can play the ranger class? a. 2
b. 3
c. 4
d. 5 2. What title does Verant bestow on all level 55 rangers? a. Outrider
b. Warder
c. Pathfinder
d. Tracker 3. Which "melee" specific skill could a Rangers get critical hits in? a. Piercing
b. 2H Slashing
c. Archery
d. 1H Slashing 4. According to Verant, what are the primary statistics of the ranger class? a. Strength, Stamina, Agility & Wisdom
b. Strength & Wisdom Only
c. Strength, Stamina, Dexterity & Intelligence
d. Strength & Stamina Only 5. The full completion of the ranger epic quest will produce two swords, what is the name of the FIRST Ranger epic sword? a. Wolfspirit
b. Lightning Rod
c. Swiftwind
d. Earthblade 6.Same question as above only what is the name of the SECOND Ranger epic sword? a. Earthcaller
b. Swiftblade
c. Wolfwrath
d. Lightning Bolt 7. What is the name of the NPC, residing in the Burning Woods, that starts the Ranger Epic Quest? a. Faelin Bloodbriar
b. Athele
c. Alrik Farsight
d. Telin Darkforest 8. What is the only level 50 spell for Rangers? a. Call of Flames
b. Call of Earth
c. Call of Heros
d. Call of Nature 9. What is the name of the Ranger only armor found in the Plane of Fear? a. Insidious
b. Carmine
c. Vermiculated
d. Thorny Vine 10. Which two gods are the only ones available to Rangers? a. Innoruuk & Bertoxxulous
b. Bertoxxulous & Karana
c. Tunare & Karana
d. Solusek Ro & Tunare 11. Which of the following two "rogue" types of skills do rangers get? a. detect traps & disarm traps
b. sneak & hide
c. pick locks & pick pockets
d. none of the above 12. Which class is considered the "parent" class to the rangers? a. Necromancers
b. Clerics
c. Druids
d. Rogues 13. What is the name given to the ranger only Skyshrine quest armor? a. Golden Leaf
b. White Lotus
c. Woven Grass
d. Myrmidon 14. What are the four components needed to complete the Trueshot Longbow Quest? a. Snake Fang, Sprocket, Cat Guts & Elven Bark
b. Spiderling Silk, Micro Servo, Dwarven Wire & Treant Wood
c. Bear Hide, Berries, Thislte Wire & Block of Wood
d. Bat Wings, Fish Parts, Thread & Twigs 15. Which statistic point effects Archery critical hits? a. Agility
b. Charisma
c. Dexterity
d. Strength 16. Which of the following is NOT an available sorting filter for ranger tracking? a. Consider (Level of creature)
b. Distance
c. Player
d. Alphabetical 17. What is the name of the quested ranger helm found in the Plane of Growth? a. Helm of the Tracker
b. Helm of the Earth
c. Helm of Nature
d. Helm of the Forest 18. At what point level does the tracking skill top out at? a. 67
b. 125
c. 200
d. 255 19. At what level is a ranger able to cast their indoor invisibility spell, Superior Camouflage? a. 30
b. 39
c. 49
d. 51 20. What spell is a level 49 spell but can not actually be obtained via a quest until level 50? a. Call of Flame
b. Ensnaring Roots
c. Shield of Brambles
d. Wolf Form

That's it! You're Done!
