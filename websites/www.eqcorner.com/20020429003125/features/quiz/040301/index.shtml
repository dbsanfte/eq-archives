Quiz 8: Shamen
 by Delrachnid | April 3, 2001


Battleskar Ragingfury drawn by William White


Healing their tribes with secret herbs, chanting to their gods and looking to the spirits for their powers.  Good and evil alike, these witch doctors protect themselves and their tribes with potions, spells and spirits of the bears, wolves and other animals.  Spell casting isn't all they do however as they are fierce fighters as well ... how much do you know about these ancient and mysterious healers of the tundra?



1. From the following list, which was the most recent negative effect to be added to the spell Torpor? a. Mana Reduction on Target
b. Reduction of Attack Speed
c. Target Becomes Rooted
d. Target is Blinded 2. Which statistic has the lowest total of starting points across all races and also happens to be one of the three primary statistics according to Verant's definition? a. Strength
b. Dexterity
c. Wisdom
d. Charisma 3. How many races can not elect to become shamen? a. 5
b. 7
c. 9
d. 10 4. What title does Verant bestow on all shamen once they attain level 55? a. Oracle
b. Luminary
c. Mystic
d. Plaguebringer 5. At what level can shamen first begin using the alchemy skill? a. 12
b. 20
c. 25
d. 30 6.Which named cyclopse in the Rathe Mountains has been known to drop some pieces of Totemic armor? a. Oculys Ogrefiend
b. Resin Bracereye
c. Groob Skullcrusher
d. Phineous Flamebrand 7. What is the name of the shaman's planar armor currently know to drop in both the Plane of Fear and Hate? a. Ivy Etched
b. Rune Branded
c. Ivy Braded
d. Rune Etched 8. Where would an Ogre shamen buy his/her first DoT spell that would be closest to their home town? a. Shamen Guild in Grobb
b. Gypsy Camp in Desert of Oasis
