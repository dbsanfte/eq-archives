February 27, 2002

HZ and OGS together went to Temple of Veeshan, Halls of Testing this past weekend in hopes of upgrading everyone's armor drops. I am happy to announce that we did succeed two out of the three nights that we were there. This zone is becoming very popular and it was hard to get a spot to conduct a full raid. But we did mangage to walk away the first night with several nice items for HZ. These items were awarded to the following people:

Tattered Silk Crown - Tayzia
Tattered Slik Gloves - Tayzia
Leather Arms - Clik

Chain Boots - Rivka
Plate Breastplate - Kharmen
Leather Bracer - Morts
Leather Gloves - Jasaine
Plate boots - Faerus

Overall the drops were lower than the first trip there. HZ is going to concentrate on higher levels, and epic hunts over the next few weeks, so when we go back to ToV, it will be with a vengeance!

Posted By: Krak Kyllz on 27 February 2002

Been a few days since the last news post so here goes.
Monday we battled with the login server after sitting around waiting for the post patch server down time. When some people finally logged in some stuff died. Some of these mobs were in Sleepers Tomb and had Primal. Gratz to Crestfallen (who now looks even more like a ranger), Duron, and Coldar on their primal weapons.

Tuesday went a little better since the servers were a bit more cooperative. Tormax went down thanks to the amassed healing power of our shaman corps. Gratz Hierl on a white dragonscale sash, eti on getting head, and shizda on the crown of the kromzek kings.

Next Tunare went down, which meant pog had to be cleared. Most of this was done by 14 people and only 2 clerics, nice work guys. Gratz twinks and people missing kael armor on their POG armor drops. Tunare2.0 went down without a hitch gratz Thornsong on his Golden Leaf Earring.

After Tunare we made a fast trip to visit the rhags in ssra temple. Snakes died, people got loot, people got spells, nuff said.

To finish the night off the guild went to the Halls of Testing and power leveled Dozikar. Gratz everyone on looting their own corpses.

Shadowed Soul
Thursday, February 28th, 2002.
Here We Are, Born To Be Kings....
NICE couple days.
Moved in yesterday to take a look at the new Thought Horror Overfiend. The THO now sports VI's trendy new idea to make life hard, the ROOT. Ah well....dumb ideas are meant to be overcome, and overcome they were. The new, faster respawn time on the elders really makes sure to keep the pace moving and keep the blood pumping. Seeying 5 and 6 and sometimes even 7 or more of these things in the camp made for a whole heap of chaotic fun. Add our mass charging to positions that we really couldn't stop in...what ya had was a fight that we made up as we went. Was heavily fun. When the smoke cleared, the overfiend called for momma and ran screamin.

Then tonight we set our sites on a mob that has yet to die on our server, the Burrower Parasite. How did it go? But for Darious, who is pretty convinced we are always going to die (well, at least him), we knew we had the stuff to take this encounter, and we showed it nicely. Worm -> Freeport. The THO dropped a nice belt and the worm dropped a FT3 range item, and NEITHER one had a pony! What gives?
~ Kyrthor

Tuesday, February 26th, 2002.
Hello There Ladies And Gentlemen......Hello There Ladies And Gents
Greetings programs!
I have had a movie stuck in my head this week. That movie is Tron. WHY would Tron be stuck in my head? As is often the case, one silly little line. It's the last line Sark says to the captured programs before they are lead away to play in the game grid. "Failure to comply will result in the standard 'sub standard' treatment which will result in you eventual deresolution". Or at least, that is how I remember the line. Why is this line stuck in my head? Because I am constantly dealing with a company that is pushing the envelope and redefining "Standard 'sub standard' treatment". If slow response, inflexible application of vague guidelines, and ransoming reasonable service for usurious rates were an olympic medal sport? Ok, the French judge would have scored it differently, but VI would be walking away with all the medals.

In regular news...in the past week we cleared NToV and took a swing at the "Ring of Vulak" encounter. The fight was pretty hectic and lots of fun. We didn't come out on top, but have some good ideas for our next time through. We took a swing by Kael and met up with the Avatar of War. Lag was a bit harsh, and he got away. We stopped by SSra, VP, Seb....hell we went everywhere this week. After all my time in this game, I was getting jaded about ever seeing anything really different...well, here's an image I call "When different strikes!"
And one last thing....

I give up. Weeks and weeeks of tryin to get every "oh, I might use this one day" item OUT of my bank and IN to EC to grow the "Kyrthor pony fund" nest egg. I concede, you bastages in EC are too damn cheap for me. So now, I am selling endorsements! Toss me 10k and I will intersperse some in the news for you. I came up with the idea as I was taking a trip on Thund airlines. Ya know, that Thund takes such good care of you, you get a full service buffing and he STILL gets you there on time and for half of what those other 'name brand' Druids cost. What a way to fly!
