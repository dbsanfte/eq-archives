EQ Quiz of The Week

The Quizzes are back! They have a different author now--that would be me, Kwill--but we will keep the fun going by posting a new quiz each week.

How well do you know EverQuest? Here's your chance to prove to yourself and perhaps others your intense knowledge of the game. A new quiz will be available each and every Wednesday so be sure to check back weekly.

This Week's Quiz

Quiz 25: Karnors Castle : Home to monsters and undead, they guard this once magnificent castle. The undead Venril Sathir stalks the halls. Have you dared to enter the haunted halls and rooms, where ghostly banners wave and evil reigns? Test your knowledge about one of the most popular dungeons in Kunark.

Past Quizzes

Quiz 24: Firiona Vie: She appears on all the concept art, and her adventures take her all over the world of Norrath. Some of you have even met her on occasion! How much do you know about our beautiful heroine, and her connection to the history of Norrath? Try your luck naming her friends, enemies, and remembering her adventures.

Quiz 23: The Gods!: The Gods created the world we live in, they even created you! They control many things like love, hate, disease, fire and water. But how much do you know about the Deities of Norrath?

Quiz 22: Estate of Unrest: The Estate of Unrest is my personal all-time-favorite EQ dungeon. I'm sure you've been there before .. who hasn't! Come with me now and see just how much you remember about it, that is ... if you dare!

Quiz 21: Solusek B - Nagafen's Lair: You have entered Lord Nagafen's Lair. All around you Kobolds run free, casting spells and weary eyes at any who dare enter thier sacred halls. This dungeon is home to Kobolds, deadly spiders, imps, giants and of course the great red wyrm Lord Nagafen. This is a prime zone for hunting .. but how much do you know about it?

Quiz 20: Frogloks: Frogloks live all around us. They live deep within some of the most dangerous dungeons of the lands and walk freely in many other zones. They are non-hostile to those of goodly gods and strike fiercly at those who worship the evil ones. How much do you know about these web footed foes?

Quiz 19: Blackburrow: Home to the Sabertooth Clan of Gnolls, Blackburrow is one of the most loved dungeons for lower level players. It's not only a dungeon but also a passageway between two zones. Danger, apprehension and excitement await all who are brave enough to zone into Blackburrow.

Quiz 18: Cazic Thule: This dungeon is actually an ancient temple for the priests and worshippers of Cazic Thule, the God of Fear. Endless treasure can be found by those brave enough to venture into the halls of this mighty god. Many people also claim that the experiences found in this dungeon are second to none. How much do you know about this zone?

Quiz 17: Crushbone: Crushbone ... home to the Crushbone Orc clan who are also allied with the Dark Elves of Neriak. At lower levels this dungeon holds many wonderful treasures and is also quite dangerous. Do you remember what it was like to enter this dungeon? Do you remember all the treasures which you found or quested for here? Now's your chance to prove just how much you know about this orcish prison camp.

Quiz 16: Lower Guk: Lower Guk ... the home to both undead and alive Frogloks. This dungeon is arguably one of the most loved and dangerous of Antonica. Why is that, some say it's the challenge while others claim it's simply the Phat Lewt. Take this test and see how much you know .. or perhaps don't know about this amphibious base.

Quiz 15: Rangers: Rangers are in tune with nature and as such share the spirit of the druids. With many druidic spells at their disposal they are also fierce warriors, and exceptional with the bow. Rangers are excellent group members and are often used to track, pull or heal in the down time, however; they can also solo as they have the ability to buff and heal themselves.... but how much more is there to know?

Quiz 14: Wizards: Wizards are the master seekers of knowledge, and their talents and spells are focused on this goal. They use powerful spells to not only see things from afar, but to transport them there as well. This thirst for arcane knowledge has also made them the true masters of the strongest form of defense: offensive spells of pure destruction. In their ability to do magical damage, they have no rivals... but how much more is there to know?

Quiz 13: Enchanters: Enchanter spells focus on manipulation of the mind, and this mental acuteness gives them more spells than any other class. With spells like Clarity and Charms it's no wonder that Enchanters are sought after by groups all over Norrath. But regenerating mana and charming creatures isn't all that they do well .. take the test and see how much you know about these mystic snake charmers...

Quiz 12: Shadow Knights: Shadow Knights are the evil counterparts to Paladins. Part warrior and part necromancer, these evil knights use the powers of the undead to aid them in their battle against the forces of good. Shadow Knights work best in group combat but through the use of a pet and their dark magic can solo very effectively. But there's so much more to these dark knights .. how much do you know about them?

Quiz 11: Bards: Bards are the jack-of-all-trades of Norrath. While playing magical songs, a bard can fight, heal, charm, damage, run fast and so much more. Bards were created for grouping and make an exceptional addition to any group ... how much do you know about these traveling minstrels?

Quiz 10: Warriors: Warriors are the ultimate tanks and because so many races allow them, they come in a vast array of sizes and colors. Strength is one of their primary stats and because of their bulging muscles and extensive training they can wield most any weapon with the skill and cunning of a seasoned adventurer. But there's so much more to this class ..

Quiz 9: Necromancers: Necromancers are the masters of the dead, reaching beyond the bounds of the grave to magically manipulate their dark minions. With the ability to command an undead being the Necromancer makes an impressive group member but they can also solo most effectively. But how much do you really know about them ...?

Quiz 8: Shaman: Healing their tribes with secret herbs, chanting to their gods and looking to mystical spirits for their powers. Good and evil alike, these witch doctors protect themselves and their tribes with potions, spells and spirits of the bear, wolf and other animals. Spell casting isn't all they do however as they are fierce fighters as well ... how much do you know about these ancient and mysterious healers of the tundra?

Quiz 7: Paladins: Defending all that is good and true in the realms, these warriors gain special powers from the gods themselves! Much like a cleric, a paladin can heal and resurrect the dead at high levels but they can do much more then that. Their gods grant them many special abilities ... how much do you know about these valiant knights?

Quiz 6: Anniversary Edition: Time sure flies when you are having fun! It's hard to believe that we've all been in Norrath for so long. Many things have changed over the course of the last two years. Some of the changes brought leers from thousands of players but overall Verant has made many wonderful changes which have enhanced everyone's enjoyment in their world. Were Frogloks always called Frogloks? Well...yes. Okay that won't be on there. But there's a lot of other questions that will! So have a go at it and see how you fare.

Quiz 5: Druids: At one with their outdoor environment that some might think that they are indeed born from the earth or perhaps raised in the wilds. So attune with their surroundings that nature itself gives them the ability to change their forms and become one with the wild animals. You see them all the time .. but how much do you really know about nature's prophets?

Quiz 4: Monks: The masters of hand to hand combat.  The ninjas of EverQuest.  They need no armor or weapons to defeat their enemies and when the going gets tough they can lay down and pretend they are dead.  You might already know all of this .. but how much more is there to know?

Quiz 3: Rogues: The true sneaks of EverQuest. Rogues hide in the shadows, stalking their victims from afar, waiting for that perfect moment to strike. These masters of the deadly poisons perhaps strike fear in your mind and terror into your heart .. but how much do you really know about them?

Quiz 2: Mages: You see them running all around the place with their elemental protectors. If they are high enough level they might even teleport you somewhere quickly by summoning you to where they are. But how much do you really know about these summoners?

Quiz 1: Clerics: You've grouped with them. They heal you, they buff you and most importantly, they bring you back to life if you should perish to some foul beast. But how much do you really know about them? Take the test and find out.
