PE/Anavon go on a roll in NToV!
After messing around with Roy yesterday we decided to get our act together today and come back with a vengeance. Things worked out well for us as Ikatiar the Venomed fell first. Grats to Zarkoril on his new Ancient Wyvern Hide Tunic and Teeleaf of Anavon on his Ikatiar's Stinger.

Then Eashen of the Sky fell next dropping some Twisted Steel Gaunts for Surreall and a Cloak of the Sky for Infestalia of Anavon.

After Aaryonar's guards were dispatched, Roy took a nice dirt nap. It would have been a flawless kill were it not for some rogue that decided to die. Loot was an Amulet of the Dreadgazer to Iegweian and as well as a Katana of Pain to Blackbadger and another Amulet of the Dreadgazer to Darkfan, both of Anavon.

Then it was time to have fun with the flurry drakes and fun we had with warriors, enchanters, and shamans getting free TLs to their bind spots. It was good practice for Ramoen's tanking abilities, I expect we'll see him as the MT in NToV soon. After that it was on to Lord Feschlak. He dropped a Thunderstone for Lillianan and an Amulet of the Storm for Deacis of Anavon.

Dagarn the Destroyer fell and monks everywhere rejoiced. Grip and Volkaran(Anavon) each received a pair of Ancient Wurm Hide Greaves and in addition to that, Ttizzi received a pair of Wurm Claw Pauldrons.

After that we killed Lord Nagafen with an army of monk twinks and he dropped a Flame Etched Short Sword for both Surreall and Stibitz(Anavon).

As we grew weary from all the dragon slaying we needed a lift so we decided to give Lord Vyemm a try. He gave us all the lift we wanted and then some as several of us ended up in the lava moat. It was a fun fight although we didn't win but in the end we decided to find a smaller fish to fry.

We noticed something fishy around the corner and decided to check it out. It turns out Lord Koi'Doken decided to invite us over for some fish rolls. He dropped a Net of the Deep Sea for Radiation(yours truly) as well as a Siren Song, Dagger of the Sea for Wulane of Anavon.

And to finish up the night we killed Lady Mirenella who dropped a Shawl of Perception for the boss, Blasphemy, and an Ebony Headband for Volkaran of Anavon.

A great day of dragon killing in which we redeemed ourselves and were able to change our name back to Project Dragonkill rather than Project CR. More action tomorrow! And people doubted us, pffffft!

Shadowed Soul
Monday, 18th 2002
After long last the bitch is DEAD…
Nothing has pained us more than Tunare. At first the Tunare v1.0 eluded us because we mistakenly thought you had to clear the whole zone to get her. The night we were clearing to do her we figured that out with 35 people left in the zone and no non-KOS to pull her. Being that it was 3am and 90% of us are working stiffs, we gave up.

A few weeks ago, I spotted the Real Doll v2.0(tm) herself in the zone late one night. We started clearing at 8pm and didn't get done till 5am which resulted in only 48 people - 5 of which were non-KOS and a few more twinks. We made two attempts at it only getting her to 30% even when our strat worked perfectly. We realized that we didn't have the manpower to do her so we crashed hoping to get back at her the next day reenergized and with normal daylight manpower. Wrong. She was poached.

Fast forward to this weekend. She was killed a week before this past Friday which meant she was due by Saturday at the latest right? SS/DA spent all day Saturday finishing a partially cleared zone. We even had time to stop by the Arch Lich and Tormax and come back. She refused to show which is strange because most high elves will show you a little if you stuff an orange down your pants, right Vaclav? Anyway, I digress. So we came back to the miserable hellhole called the Plane of Growth and began clearing a second day. Any guesses? Still no show. Nothing like clearing that place twice just for fun.

This morning we get the call that she spawned. Early afternoon, we start clearing the partially spawned zone (still not done from the previous nights clearing). A bit later we had to defend the zone from some poachers. Finally after the rest of the zone spawned we had Tunare all to herself. We lined her up in the Assling Cannon crosshairs and fired with reckless abandon. Sending lumpy ass-breathed hair-foot ear-encrusted asslings at her one after the other. She was felled easily. FINALLY.

Drops included three belts (Galar, Tritolus, Kerlen) and one earring (Haley). I think with a bit more refinement to the strat we could take her with less than fifty, but we'll see if it ever comes to that. As for those that compare her to the Avatar of War? Pllllease... put the crack pipe down. Tricky, yes, but most of Tunare's problem is just coming up with the strat that works, kinda like the Arch Lich. Performing after that is quite easy. Avatar of War takes absolute perfection from everyone to win and is still our favorite fight, though Burrower ranks a close second for me.

In other news, we felled the Arch Lich again. The first time we tried the Arch Lich, I thought - "This mob sucks!". Now he's a snoozer. Can't wait to get a key so we can try Creator or High Priest.

~ Natysha - [11:16 PM EST]
