Maps of Norrath

Use the menus below to quickly jump to any of the map pages or browse through each continent listed below:

           

Antonica: Antonica is the largest of all continents and is home to a vast majority of the races including barbarians, dark elves, halflings, humans, ogres, trolls and some half-elves. The zones within Antonica can all be accessed with the original version of the game.

Faydwer: Faydwer is the home of the goodly races including dwarves, gnomes, high-elves, wood elves and some half-elves. The zones within Faydwer can all be accessed with the original version of the game.

Kunark: Kunark is home to but only one race, the evil Iksars. This continent also has outposts for all the other races within the game so that banking and shopping shouldn't be much of a problem. The zones within Kunark can all be accessed with the Ruins of Kunark expansion to the game.

Odus: Odus is home to only one race, the Erudites, with two cities available for their good and evil classes. This is the smallest of all continents and is thought by many to be very isolated. The zones within Odus can all be accessed with the original version of the game.

Velious: Velious is home to none of the player-races within EverQuest. However, there are several towns within this continent for banking and shopping dependent on your factions. Velious boasts all mid to high level zones and is accessible only to those with the Scars of Velious expansion to the game.

Planes: The alter planes of the gods are spread all throughout EverQuest. These zones are somewhat attached to the various continents above but in some ways are separate. By virtue of the gods, you may not enter the planes until you have grown to level 46 or above.
