Quiz 18: Cazic-Thule
by Aracnakat | June 19, 2001



Cazic Thule is a dungeon filled with treasure.  Treasure for those with the guts and brawn to go and get it.  This dungeon is home to the warriors and priests of the god it's named after.



1. In which zone will you find the entrance to the dungeon, Cazic-Thule? a. The Feerott
b. Innothule Swamp
c. Rathe Mountains
d. Oasis of Marr 2. What types of creatures make their home in Cazic-Thule? a. Frogloks
b. Orcs
c. Lizards
d. Sarnaks 3. What famed armor used to drop in this dungeon before it was removed? a. Lustrous Russet
b. Mithril-Runed
c. Netted Kelp
d. Rubicite 4. What is the only class that can wear the "Ravenscale" armor which now drops in Cazic-Thule? a. Shadow Knights
b. Rogues
c. Necromancers
d. Clerics 5. What creature would you need to kill to obtain the Bladed Thulian Claws? a. Avatar of Fear
b. Clay Golem
c. Cazic Cenobite
d. Tae Ew Templar 6.Which one of the following items drops in Cazic-Thule? a. Silver Wand
b. Chittin Shell Shield
c. Moss Mask
d. Silver-plated Bracer 7. Approximately what level is the Avatar of Fear? a. 25
b. 32
c. 38
d. 45 8. What faction is the Tae Ew Templar on? a. Allize Taeew
b. Inhabitants of Cazic-Thule
c. Allize Volew
d. No faction 9. Bards need to go into Cazic-Thule for a quest which starts in the Temple of Solusek Ro, what is the end result of this quest? a. Lambent Boots
b. Lambent Bracers
c. Lambent Breastplate
d. Lambent Helm 10. A Lizard Blood Potion can be found in this dungeon, what is it's effect when used? a. Raises Charisma
b. Makes the user Invisible
c. Teleports the user to Cazic-Thule
d. Makes the user invulnerable 11. An odd weapon known as the Abandoned Orc Shovel can be obtained in Cazic-Thule. What type of weapon is it? a. 1H Blunt
b. 2H Blunt
c. 1H Slashing
d. 2H Slashing 12. There is a quest which results in the Shaman only, Totemic Helm, which brings the quester into Cazic Thule. In which zone does this quest start? a. The Feerott
b. Lake Rathetear
c. Innothule Swamp
d. Rathe Mountains 13. What creature drops the Lizard Blood Potion? a. Avatar of Fear
b. Tae Ew Diviner
c. Cazic Cenobite
d. Lizard Archon 14. Gardern in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives a quest in which he asks for an item from Cazic-Thule. What is that item? a. Shard of Golem Stone
b. Radiant Gloves
c. Bits of Golem Metal
d. Silver Wand 15. What class is the Avatar of Fear? a. Warrior
b. Shadow Knight
c. Rogue
d. Shaman 16. Killing any of the lizards in Cazic-Thule will raise what faction? a. Allize Taeew
b. Inhabitants of Cazic-Thule
c. Allize Volew
d. None of the above 17. In what zone would you find the closet wizard portal to Cazic-Thule? a. South Ro
b. The Feerott
c. South Karana
d. In Cazic-Thule 18. The Tae Ew Templar sometimes drops the Lizardscale Cloak, what are the two beneficial stats on this item? a. Strength & Dexterity
b. Intelligence & Wisdom
c. AC & Charisma
d. AC & Dexterity 19. The inhabitants of Cazic-Thule have two prisoners, what races are they? a. Erudite & Gnome
b. Erudite & High Elf
c. Dwarf & High Elf
d. Gnome & Dwarf 20. What God does the Avatar of Fear resemble? a. Cazic-Thule
b. Innoruuk
c. Tunare
d. None of the above.

That's it! You're Done!
