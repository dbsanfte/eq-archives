Quiz 17: Crushbone
by Aracnakat | June 12, 2001
Special thanks to Wranglir Lightheart for his help.



Crushbone ... home to the Crushbone Orc clan who are also allied with the Dark Elves of Neriak.  At lower levels this dungeon holds many wonderful treasures and is also quite dangerous.



1. What continent is the dungeon, Crushbone, found on? a. Antonica
b. Faydwer
c. Kunark
d. Odus 2. Which of the following NPCs found in Crushbone is a Dark Elf? a. Ambassador D'Vinn
b. Retlon Brenclog
c. Lord Darish
d. Rondo Dunfire 3. Which of the following factions will you lower/hurt by killing Emperor Crush? a. Stormguard
b. Faydark's Champions
c. Emerald Warriors
d. Indigo Brotherhood 4. What creature would you kill in Crushbone to get the Shiney Brass Shield? a. Emperor Crush
b. Orc Taskmaster
c. Orc Trainer
d. Dwarven Smith 5. Which of the following classes needs to go into Crushbone as part of their Epic Weapon quest? a. Clerics
b. Monks
c. Paladins
d. None 6.Which one of the following items drops in Crushbone? a. Black Silk Gloves
b. Two Handed Dwarven Axe
c. Hooded Black Cloak
d. Sacrificial Dagger 7. Approximately what level is Ambassador Dvinn? a. 15
b. 20
c. 25
d. 30 8. What class is Retlon Brenclog? a. Mage
b. Necromancer
c. Wizard
d. Enchanter 9. In what zone can you turn in Crushbone Belts for faction and some coins? a. Felwithe
b. Kelethin
c. Kaladim
d. Ak'Anon 10. Many slaves are found in Crushbone, which of the following races is NOT represented? a. High Elves
b. Dwarves
c. Erudites
d. Gnomes 11. Which of the following maces can be gotten in Crushbone? a. Ebon Mace
b. Enamelled Black Mace
c. Ogre War Mace
d. Screaming Mace 12. To complete a quest which will result in gaining the rogue only item, Scout Cape, you must speak with Kelynn, a slave within Crushbone. In which city does this quest start? a. Kelethin
b. Felwithe
c. Freeport
d. Halas 13. Which NPC in Crushbone will give you a 3AC +3 Wisdom "Prayer Shawl" in exchange for Bracers of Battle? a. Dwarven Smith
b. Retlon Brenclog
c. Elven Priest
d. Kelynn 14. What other dungeon in Norrath is home to an Orcish clan? a. Mines of Droga
b. Crystal Caverns
c. Splitpaw
d. Runny Eye 15. What class is the Emperor Crush? a. Warrior
b. Shadow Knight
c. Rogue
d. Shaman 16. Killing any of the orcs in Crushbone will raise what faction? a. Indigo Brotherhood
b. Crushbone Orcs
c. Faydark's Champions
d. None of the above 17. If you kill the Orc Taskmaster, he might drop a Brass Earring. What is the one stat found on this item? a. 2 Agility
b. 2 Wisdom
c. 2 Strength
d. 2 AC 18. In which part of Crushbone would you find the Orc Trainer? (Where does he spawn?) a. At the entrance
b. In the castle
c. In the slave pits
d. On a hill 19. When you kill Emperor Crush, he might drop a Dwarven Ringmail Tunic. This tunic has several nice saves on it, what are they? a. SV Fire +8, SV Cold +8, SV Magic +8
b. SV Poison +8, SV Disease +8, SV Magic +8
c. SV Fire +10, SV Cold +10, SV Magic +10
d. SV Poison +10, SV Disease +10, SV Magic +10 20. What NPC is the highest level in this dungeon? a. Emperor Crush
b. The Prophet
c. Ambassador Dvinn
d. Lord Darish

That's it! You're Done!
