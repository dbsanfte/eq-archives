Quiz 11: Bards
by Aracnakat | April 24, 2001


Artwork used by permission from Goldeaf Galleries


Bards are the jack-of-all-trades of Norrath. While playing magical songs, a bard can fight, heal, charm, damage, run fast and so much more. Bards were created for grouping and make an exceptional addition to any group ... how much do you know about these traveling minstrels?



1. Which of the following instrument skills does a bard receive first at level 5? a. Brass
b. Percussion
c. String
d. Wind 2. Which is the first song that a bard can get that enables them to run faster then normal? a. Selo's Accelerando
b. Selo's Consonant Chain
c. Selo's Chords of Cessation
d. Selo's Song of Travel 3. How many races can elect to become bards? a. 2
b. 3
c. 4
d. 5 4. What is the name of the NPC who is a member of the League of Antonican Bards that resides in the Nektulos Forest waiting for someone to come and deliver his mail? a. Martin Stringsinger
b. Tacor Tissleplay
c. Ton Twostring
d. Travis Twotone 5. In which zone would a bard receive the quest for the Lambent Breastplate? a. Kael Drakkal
b. Skyshrine
c. Temple of Solusek Ro
d. Thurgadin 6.What level 34 song, new with Scars of Velious, provides regeneration for mana, health and stamina? a. Cantana of Soothing
b. Melody of Ervaj
c. Hymn of Restoration
d. Cantana of Replenishment 7. Which instrument "skill" is used for the Invisibility/See Invisibility song, Shavri's Sonorous Clouding? a. Strings
b. Brass
c. Percussion
d. Wind 8. What is the name of the bard only armor found in the Plane of Hate? a. Harmonic
b. Singing Steel
c. Imbrued
d. Melodic 9. Which of the following weapons skills can a bard not use? a. Archery
b. 1h Blunt
c. Piercing
d. None of these answers, they get all 3 of the above skills.  10. In which city would you find the bard guild, the Song Weavers? a. Felwithe
b. Freeport
c. Kelethin
d. Qeynos 11. Which of the following gods is not one of the choices available to bards of any race? a. Bertoxxulous
b. Karana
c. Prexus
d. Veeshan 12. What title does Verant bestow on all bards at level 51? a. Maestro
b. Minstrel
c. Troubador
d. Virtuoso 13. Which of the following skills does a bard obtain at level 20? a. Safe Fall
b. Dual Wield
c. Sneak
d. Sense Traps 14. What components are needed to obtain the Resonant Breastplate from Leifur in Thurgadin? a. Corroded Breastplate & 3 Flawless Diamonds
b. Tarnished Breastplate & 3 Black Marbles
c. Rusted Breastplate & 3 Flawless Diamonds
d. Unadorned Breastplate & 3 Black Marbles 15. According to Verant, what are the primary statistics for bard? a. Strength, Dexterity & Charisma
b. Strength, Stamina & Dexterity
c. Strength, Agility & Wisdom
d. Strength, Charisma & Stamina 16. What effect does the song, Kuzumi's Note of Preservation cause? a. Adds a substantial amount of AC to party.
b. Target's attack speed is reduced dramatically
c. Party takes no melee damage (similar to Divine Aura)
d. Target loses mana, intelligence & wisdom 17. Which of the following disciplines allows a bard to auto dodge for 15 seconds? a. Deftdance
b. Puretone
c. Resistant
d. Fearless 18. Which NPC in Skyshrine gives and awards the bard only quest for the Breastplate of Twilight? a. Fardonad Fe'Dhar
b. Qynydd Fe'Dhar
c. Ocoenydd Fe'Dhar
d. Umykith Fe'Dhar 19. At level 58, bards receive their very last skill, what is it? (this does not include disciplines) a. Parry
b. Riposte
c. Picklocks
d. Track 20. What is the name of the bard epic item? a. Prismatic Turning Blade
b. Singing Short Sword
c. Tijoely's Dancing Rapier
d. Claw of Phara Dar

That's it! You're Done!
