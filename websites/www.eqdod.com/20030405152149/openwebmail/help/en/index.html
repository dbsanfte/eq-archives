Welcome to the Open Webmail Help Tutorials!   Open Webmail is a free, open source project that allows people to check their e-mail -- from anywhere in the world! You can use Open Webmail to simply check your e-mail while you are on the road, or as a complete replacement to your offline e-mail client! Open Webmail can also help you manage your daily activities with its easy-to-use and calendar tools. In this tutorial, you'll find examples, diagrams, and definitions, all to help you get the most out of Open Webmail.
