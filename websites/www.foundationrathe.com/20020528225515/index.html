Tuesday, May 28th, 2002 - Look at us grow

- After 5 days of recruiting, Foundation Rathe is 30 members strong.  We are still looking for members especially enchanters, clerics, shamens, warriors, rogues, paladins, rangers and wizards.  For the time being all classes are welcome however open recruiting will soon be closed and the level requirement will be going up slowly as the guild grows in levels.

Our first guild hunt will be scheduled in the next week or so; location to be determined.

 
Thursday, May 23rd, 2002 - A New Dawn

- After a lot of thinking and soul searching, I've decided that I could not rest without giving Foundation Rathe one more try.  That's correct!  Foundation Rathe is back in business.  We will be recruiting again and depending on the members we get, the guild goals will be laid out.  Old members are welcome to return and recruting for new members will be listed below:

  • Level Restrictions - As of now, there will be no level restrictions.  That does not mean that we will be accepting level 1 players that just started the game.  In general, the level limit will be level 15 or appropriate game experience (higher level characters, experience from other servers, etc.).  This will ensure that the player has at least learned the fundamentals of the game. 
  • Class Restrictions - There will be no class restrictions at this time. All classes are welcome. We only ask that you know your class and are able to play and devote time to that class.  While ability to play multiple classes is not a bad thing, we are looking for those that will stick with one (or two) characters and advance.
  • Guild Goals - The majority of the guild currently is in the 15 to 30th level range.  The immediate goals will be to fill in needed class  and begin hunting together so that we all learn to each other's styles as well as acquire needed equipment for all members. 
  • Guild Structure - For the immediate time, guild decisions will be made by the guild leader.  As the guild grows and progresses there will be many officer spots available. Among those positions, will be membership committee, loot council, raid organizers and class officers.
  • Guild loot - Initially, loot will be given/awarded based on need as well as participation.  A guild wide point system will also be put in place so that when we are ready to tackle harder challenges, awarding loot will be easy, painless and not open to dispute.  A copy of the rules can be found under the Loot Rules link.

To inquire about joining Foundation Rathe, please contact the following individuals (many of which are me), Mithandriel, Meaoldar, Turindal, Gandorff, Baladorn and Aratheal.
