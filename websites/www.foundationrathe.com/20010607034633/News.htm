Saturday, May 5, 2001

- The forces of Foundation Rathe paid a visit to the plane of Growth today. Unfortunately for Tunare, her legions were no match for the "evil" side of FoR and many a bear, wolf, horse and tree fell. Tunare was so distressed that she had to resurrect her minions (/ooc the zone came down in the middle of our raid) however we continued on with the onslaught. Overall it was a very nice night with many fine items and spells dropping. The two highlights had to be the Breastplate of Stability that went to Ammax and the Noctivant Breastplate that went to Vorth.  Great job to Vorth on organizing another fine raid and to Kebeklib, Sarama and Aann for their great pulls.
