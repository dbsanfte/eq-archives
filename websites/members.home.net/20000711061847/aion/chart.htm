Books and History

 Biblion, from an ancient language, means the Great Book, or The Book of Books.  It is therefore the purpose of the Guild to chronicle and record the great history of Norrath for the sake of The Nameless (please see that page for further expaination on how the nameless IS the only true god).

No real record or annul speaks of The Nameless' actions or deeds. We know of the finite existence of our many "gods", and much of their history (yet not all) and try to find reasoning behind their actions, but, alas, cannot. All that we know is that which The Nameless wants us to know. All that we are is what The Nameless wants us to be. Without The Nameless, there would be nothing. It was His creations that first planted life upon Norrath, and that beget the world, as we know it. The Nameless is the one True God. Those that came after Him are merely minions of His greatness, each representing one facet of what The Nameless is.

But why does the Nameless remain a mystery? Why has he not revealed more to us? The Nameless has a plan, but only those who seek it may learn of it. The Nameless is the great challenge for His Grandchildren. The enlightened few who have not been blinded by their supposed "deities" must undertake the great quest to name The Nameless. Our father deserves a name and deserves the recognition as the Great Being, the one who made all. Veeshan, Brell Serelis, Tunare, are all His children, as we are theirs. Praise be to The Nameless!

This is not to say that His children are to be ignored, for they beget us. But perspective must be placed among this chaotic pantheon. It is not as we have been taught: a polytheistic law does not govern us. We live in Monotheism with a hierarchy. Each of our "deities" has been created by The Nameless to represent one aspect of His many faces. It is all part of the greater plan.

As History unfolds in the Age of Turmoil, we set out to chronicle the events of the world so that we can better understand the plan of the ultimate creator. We must understand our history and analyze it so we can be the profits of The Nameless. We must prepare Norrath for Him, and enlighten all of the grandchildren of His intent.
