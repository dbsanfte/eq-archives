This is a collection of some of the links we visit fairly regularily to keep up to date
 
The EQLizer - One of the best online repositories of items.  This site contains many quests and spoilers.
The EQVault - A newssite, updated regularily with happenings within and without everquests.
Stratics - Another newssite, updated daily as well.
Everlore - This site has an enormous pile of info.  If you cant find it somewhere else, you can probably find it here.
EQClerics - A must read site for all the clerics out there. 
Allakhazams Magical Realm - Great info for casters, and Norrath news to boot!
Graffs Wizard Compliation - A site with great info for the casters in all of us.
Casters Realm - Coggs favorite place to visit, formerly known as Everquest Enchanters.
Rallos Zek Chronicles - Keep up to date on the events happening on our server.
