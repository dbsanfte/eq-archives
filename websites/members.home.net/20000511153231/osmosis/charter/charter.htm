The Mithril Hammer Clan : Charter of Rights and Responsibilities
Rights
 
  • All Mithril Hammer members have the right to defend themselves when attacked.
  • All Mithril Hammer members have the right to refuse assistance to another member IF they are indesposed, or simply unable to assist. 
  • All Mithril Hammer members have the right to use guildchat for clan discussions, to determine the locations of members, and to auction items.
  • Mithril Hammer Clan officers have the right to invite new members, as long as there is some concensus about this member, or the officer has it on good faith that the invitee is honorable and would be a worthy addition to our clan.  In the absence of other officers, Mithril Hammer Clan officers have to right to invite other members without concensus, but should inform the other officers as soon as possible.
  • Mithril Hammer Clan officers have the right to remove members who are found to be in violation of Clan morals.
  • Mithril Hammer Clan officers have the right to speak for the guild.


Responsibilities
 

  • All Mithril Hammer Clan members are representatives of our clan, and as such should be GOOD at all times, and try to help out others around you.  Every life saved is a friend made!
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan members should attempt to assist any other member whenever possible.
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan members should attempt to keep up to date on guild happenings and events.
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan members must warn all other members of active PK's whenever discovered. 
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan members should attempt to not steal kills from other people whenever possible, as this is akin to stealing items.
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan officers are expected to be aware of member actions, and should report any unclanlike behavior.
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan officers will share the duty of interviewing prospective members, and to voice their opinions or concerns of any prospective member.
  • All Mithril Hammer Clan officers are expected to attend any guild meeting that is held, barring real life interference.  Mithril Hammer Clan members are free to attend at their own discretion.
