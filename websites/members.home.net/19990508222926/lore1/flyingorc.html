The Flying Orc Tavern
. 
.
You crawl your way out of Wrong in the dark of night, weary, your cape torn, your
armor and weapon gone, stolen from the horrible beasts of the dungeon, your heart
still beating fast from the terrifying sights you witnessed within. An owl hoots in the
distance and you hear the cry of a Troll Lord, you take off running from the canyon
into the darkness. Running swiftly thru the trees, you stop only when you reach black
of the water. You follow the banks southwest a ways until suddenly, a water
elemental flies from the water, attacking you. Low on strength and out of reagents,
you dart into the forest, branches hitting your face as you make your way thru the
trees. Suddenly, a building looms before you. Lights burning bright, merry laughter
from within. You approach hesitantly...people are not always so kind in this land. You
sneak around the building, in the front you see a sign hanging above the door, lights
blinking bright in the night:
.
.
.
.
You push the door open slowly, inside Warriors are gathered, beautiful Ladies, a
merry wench laughs loudly as she walks around the room handing out mugs of ale.
Two men stand behind the bar, talking with their customers. They see you enter, one
waves and the other calls out, "Hail weary traveler, pull up a chair and join our
fires." You sink into a chair near the fire, the wench hands you a mug of ale and you a
feeling of calm consumes you. Relaxed you spend the evening joking with new friends,
playing games and dancing to the tunes of the bards.
Xavoc and DarkThorn welcome thee to the Flying Orc Tavern!
Pull up a chair and join our fires! Thou art always welcome here.
 .
.
 
The Flying Orc is located South of Wrong Mountain. You can either run East from
the Yew Moongate,or West from theMinoc/Vesper moongate. It is a fairly long
walk either way.But if you are at Wrong, then it's just a short jaunt anyway.
