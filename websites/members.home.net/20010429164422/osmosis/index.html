News : Wednesday, August 23/2000

The Time has come

In all things, there must be closure. Sadly, it is time for the closure of our fair clan. We has all walked down this path together for well over a year, and in that time we has built very strong friendships and great memories. Me still remembers the joyously fun (and peril ridden) mistmoore trip, some drunken stupors in Lower Guk, adventuring in Permafrost, and giant falling in the Rathe Mountains... and these is only a few of the memories we built. But, alas, it is time to move on.

For those who still frequent Norrath, it is a difficult thing to let go of a family held for so long. For those who have strayed from that land and exist only in that reality thingee, your memories of our family should not be waned by a dwindled clan. We must hold onto the wonderful things that we all has done, but not let them hold us back in the wonderful things that we has yet to do.

Shortly, this page will cease to exist, but will be replaced with a memorial to our fine organization. No one thing has brought us to this point, but all things, as me said, must come to an end... and me thinks it is better to bid a proud retreat than to wake up suddenly without a family around us. The lord Verant has planned a Guild Sweep in November, and me would not want to see MHC gone in the blink of an eye.

Me asks that on November 4th, thems that wishes to officially be removed from the guild would meet me at the place of our conception... outside of Kaladim. There, I shall wait throughout the day. At the end of this day, me will be closing the guild. If you has other plans on this day or dont wishes to come, dont worry about taking off your own tag, just puts it in a momento box and know that you will always be in my mind.

 

Cogg
