Appointed by the Dragon, relying mostly on input from the Council, these two ranks, in addition to fulfilling their duties as Asha'man and Aes Sedai, have several other responsibilities within the guild.

Traditionally the M'Hael is a male position responsible for leading the guild in combat. He is responsible for assuring that his troops are given combat training, and maintain the experience and readiness needed to operate effectively in a wartime situation. The M'Hael is heir to the Dragon, and acting GM when the GM is not available.

The Amrylin Seat has traditionally been a female position responsible for assuring that the entire guild is updated on current guild issues. Both of these ranks are personally responsible to the Dragon in keeping him informed of guild events. Both of these ranks also assume their roles as Council members and Officers.
