- Champions of Brell FAQ - 
Who are we?
The Champions of Brell are Dwarves, Gnomes and Halflings of any class and with at least 10 levels of experience. While Dwarves and Gnomes are the only races created by Brell Serilis, the Halflings have been given special consideration for admittance into our ranks due to thier kinship and common foe - slaying a Goblin from Runnyeye will befriend you in both Rivervale and Kaladim. All classes, including Necromancers and Druids which follow different gods, are still admitted for reasons mentioned above. Simply being a a lvl 10+ Dwarf, Gnome or Halfling does not mean automatic acceptance.

What is the Code of Conduct?
1) Never beg for items or equipment from strangers or guildmates.
2) Excessive OOC comments in Guildchat or at Guildevents is prohibited. Keep it in tells.
3) Always display and uphold the Guild virtues of Valor and Honor
4) There will be no Auctioning of items in guildsay. Post on the Trade Board or go to East Commons.

Note: Only one warning will be issued if these rules are broken, the second warning will be accompanied by guildremoval from a Warleader or the Guildleader.

What are the Virtues of a Champion?
To become a Champion of Brell a Dwarf must be the epitome of both Valor and Honor. Following these principles is an essential requirement for membership.
 

- Valor -
We stand our ground on
the battlefield when others flee.
 
- Honor -
We keep our word and treat
others with the respect they deserve.

- Enlistment Forum -
How do I join?
If you feel compelled to follow The Duke of Underfoot and have a strong sense of Valor and Honor, you may wish to join us. Visit the Champions of Brell Enlistment Forum to request membership.

Note:
If you are a Druid or Necromancer, you must be able to enter the Underfoot Hall in Kaladim and the Bank in Ak'Anon without the use of magic (ie: invis or form change). Pending change
In addidtion to your main, a maximum of one Alternate character (level +10) will be permitted in the guild. The Main character must be level 35 before you may have an Alt guilded. Players are required to specify an Alt and a Main to the Guildleader of your server. Once you have decided who your Alt and Main will be, they can not be switched. Alts will not be considered for distribution of treasure or items from the armory or obtained during guild sieges.
