m e m b e r   g u i d e l i n e s
  • All members of Black Tower are to conduct themselves with honor at all times. One who walks with honor is one whose words, deeds and actions bring respect and the recognition of respect shown to others.
  • All members of Black Tower will encourage the feelings of friendship and family to all other members.
  • All members of Black Tower will be courteous to all persons of Norrath, even those whose opinions differ from your own.
  • All members of Black Tower will help those in need and offer whatever assistance is available. Special attention should be made to new players. The most valuable help to a new palyer is information. Be patient while showing them this new world.
  • All members of Black Tower will uphold the virtues of Black Tower and represent the guild in a positive way. Each and every member is a representative of Black Tower and the actions of one reflect upon the guild as a whole.
  • All members of Black Tower will protect each other and die to save a brother/sister's life.
  • All members of Black Tower, regardless of guild rank, are expected to uphold the integrity of the Black Tower by adhering to these guidelines as well as an unwritten code of conduct which is representative of being a member of this guild.
  • No member will be expelled without good reason and without consensus of the Black Tower Council.
  • No member of the Black Tower will engage in confrontations (shouting matches, OOC spams, duels) with other members of the server. If a member is continuously provoked or harassed by an individual, report the incident to a Black Tower Council member or available officer, and the situation will be resolved.
  • Remember this is a GAME! Have fun! The Black Tower welcomes all suggested guidelines from its members, please contact your local Council member!
