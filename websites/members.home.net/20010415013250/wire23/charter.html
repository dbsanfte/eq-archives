Every guild has a common purpose or goal which unites members and distinguishes the particular guild from others. The Black Tower's role in Norrath will unfold as the years pass, and thus our charter will change to reflect new aspects of the game as they come into play. The following is our initial charter:

The Black Tower guild is a collection of adventurous individuals bound to one another in friendship and camaraderie. Although each member brings to the guild their own entity we, as a unity share a common set of principles, virtues and moral standards which we seek to promote throughout Norrath. We are a guild of honor in that we uphold strict moral standards to ourselves, our guild mates, and our fellow citizens. We are a guild of leadership in that we seek to help not only ourselves, but fellow Norrathians. All members have sworn to uphold the charter of the guild and its guidelines. We prepare continuously to combat the Shadow.
