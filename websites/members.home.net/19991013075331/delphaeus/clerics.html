Kilravan

"The Calling"
Deep in the bowels of Paw, I battled gnoll after swarming gnoll. "This was a bad idea", I thought to myself in between swings of my Minotaur axe. Rangers like me were meant to be outside in the open where the air was fresh and the sky could be seen. But I had foolheartedly plunged into this Tunare forsaken dog pen in search of the fabled magic snakeskin armor that would help me resist poison. I was fortunate enough to venture into the pit with a cleric named Tearin Purespirit. Judging from his formidable appearance and calm demeanor, it was easy to entrust my health into his hands. "Surely any danger I get myself into, this holy man will be able to deal with", I told myself.
Again and again I felt the stinging blows of the gnoll's claws upon my body. The shabby armor I had come in with provided little protection from their gnashing teeth, I was no where near obtaining any magic armor. It was so easy to get this deep into Paw, but now exit seemed impossible. Like a trap, they lulled me in to their most obscure passageways and then pounced in a huge pack. As I quickly realized that this was a deteriorating situation, I panicked and ran haphazardly looking for a way out. In my panic I did the worst thing possible - I didn't allow my cleric partner to heal me. I had become so lost in the maze of tunnels that there was no way for Tearin to find me. As my life slowly faded, and I crawled desperately away, I ceased feeling the gnashing and tearing upon my body. The deeper pain of humiliation descended upon me obscuring all else.
As Karana has favored me for my devotion , I awoke from death in the town of RiverVale. Like a voice from the mist, Tearin called, "I will help you, don't despair." How could he help? I was alone, defenseless, and blind, a long, long ways away from Paw. "I have helped people in far worse conditions, trust me, I will aid you." I had quested with Tearin for a little while before this misadventure, but only now did I realize how seriously dedicated he was to his calling. Indeed, with his strength and wisdom, he was easily able to recover my belongings, fight his way out of Paw single-handedly, and make his way (heavily encumbered) to East Karana where we could meet. For my part, I was able to enlist the aid of Karl the Barbarian as my guide out of RiverVale and through High Hold Pass.e
Who is Tearin Purespirit? A man who has heard the call and followed it.
