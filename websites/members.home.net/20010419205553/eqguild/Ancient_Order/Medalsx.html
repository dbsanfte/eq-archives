The Ancient Order of Guardians have Three Medals, and various
ribbons which are awarded to it's members, and One Medal which is
awarded to non-members. Below you will find a picture of each Medal,
and a brief description of the requirements to earn them.
MEDAL OF VALOR

The Medal Of Valor is the highest award that can
be bestowed upon a member of The Ancient Order of
Guardians.

It is awarded to those who show extreme bravery
in the face danger and certain death while defending
others from harm.

To be awarded the Medal of Valor, the act of
bravery perfomed must involve combat with an
opponent against which little or no hope of survival is
present. The act must be intentionally undertaken and
may not be for personal gain. The act of bravery must
include the saving, or attempt to save, the life of another.
MEDAL OF BRAVERY

The Medal of Bravery is the second highest award that
can be bestowed upon a member of The Ancient Order of
Guardians.

It is awarded to those who show great bravery in the
face of danger, while defending or aiding a citizen of
Norrath.

To be awared the Medal of Bravery, the act of bravery
performed must be of such that death is very probable while
protecting or aiding a citizen of Norrath. The action performed
does not have to include actual combat, but does include
actions in combat which are of a high level of bravery, but do
not meet the requirements for The Medal of Valor. As with the
Medal of Valor, the action performed must be intentional, and
not for personal gain.
MEDAL OF MERIT

The Medal of Merit is the third highest award that can
be bestowed upon a member of The Ancient Order of
Guardians.

The Medal of Merit is awared to those who consistently
exemplify the ideals and principles of The Ancient Order of
Guardians.

To be awarded the Medal of Merit the Guardian must
exemplify the ideals and principles of The Ancient Order of
Guardians to both fellow Guardians and the citizens of
Norrath. While no great act of bravery is required, the actions
of the Guardian receiving this award must have a
recognizable impact on both the guild and the Norrath
Society.
THE GUARDIAN'S MEDAL

The Guardian's Medal is awarded to citizens of
Norrath who are not members of The Ancient Order of
Guardians. It is used by The Ancient Order of Guardians to
recognize a citizen for an action that would qualify a
Guardian under similar circumstances for an award.

The Guardian's Medal is awarded to a citizen who's
actions might otherwise qualify him to receive an award
that would be bestowed upon a member of The Ancient
Order of Guardians.

When a citizens actions would qualify for a Medal of
Valor, a gold "V" is placed on the ribbon portion of the
medal. When a citizen would qualify for a Medal of Bravery,
A bronze "B" is placed on the ribbon portion of the medal.
When a citizen would qualify for a Medal of Merit, no device
is attached to the medal.

ANCIENT ORDER OF GUARDIANS
SERVICE AWARD

The Ancient Order of Guardians Service Award is
presented to Guardians who have attended FIVE officially
sponsered guild events. These events include, but are not
limited to, raids and guild meetings.

For each additional additional AOG Service Award
that is presented, a gold number device is attached to the
center of the ribbon to indicate the total number of awards.
RECRUITER'S RIBBON

The Recruiter's Ribbon is presented to Guardians who
have recruited THREE new members who all complete their
two week probation period.

For each additional additional Recruiter's Ribbon that
is presented, a gold number device is attached to the center
of the ribbon to indicate the total number of awards.
Campaign Ribbons

Campaign Ribbons are presented to Guardians who
have participated in various guild sponsored raids and
events. Campaign Ribbons will be created for each
individual event.

The Guardian who organizes the event will have a
gold "O" device attached to their ribbon. Designated
leaders of the guild event will have a gold "L" device
attached to their ribbon. In the event that the Guardian who
organizes the event is also a designated leader, both
devices shall be attached to their ribbon.
