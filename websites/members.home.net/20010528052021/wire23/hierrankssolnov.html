All new members are inducted to the guild at this rank. All members at this rank are sponsored by another guild member of 3rd rank or higher. A member at this rank may not sponsor a new recruit. It is expected that members at this rank familiarize themselves with the guild's guidelines, structure and members. When a member of this rank exhibits that he/she has basic knowledge and understanding of what it is to be a member of the Black Tower, and shows a genuine interest in being a long term member of the guild, he/she will be given the opportunity to advance in rank.
