**THE HOLE LOOMS CLOSER**
In a post on alt.games.everquest, Brad McQuaid hinted that we should see The Hole on live servers within two weeks. Here a Map of the Hole for you.
**Testing the hole now**
Hello all,
We're about to begin play-testing the Hole on the Test server. Users logging in to Test will automatically download the zone files. This is NOT the final export of the Hole, so people who do not play on test should NOT launch the test patch program to get the file.

When the Hole goes live, a NEW version of the file will be put on the optional patch.
As a note, please remember that many changes will be made to the hole over the next several days, so expect the zone to come up and down regularly.

What level will the hole be approximately?
Pretty rough. Aiming for about 40-55. As a note, when the Hole goes live, I'd highly suggest that people stop using the "Shortcut" to Tox that everyone in Paineel is so fond of.
- Gordon

  May 22, 2000
**NEW ZONES**
Two new areas tentatively titled The Hole, and The Warrens will be accessible via Paineel and Erudin on Odus. The zones will be free of charge and available via an optional patch. The Hole is a high level dungeon which represents the ruins of Old Paineel, destroyed long ago in a war with between the Erudites and the Heretics. The Warrens is a dungeon for lower level players, and is the original home of the Kobolds upon Norrath. More information regarding these new zones will be made available as their release date nears.
