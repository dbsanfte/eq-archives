The Council is selected from the guild's roster of Warders. They are the balancing factor in guild decisions and are responsible for assuring that the guild operates in a fair and efficient manner. They are responsible for debating all major guild issues, as well as implementing changes to guild structure and guidelines. Original members of the Council were appointed by the Dragon, Asha'man M'Hael and Aes Sedai. Additions to the Council are done by a unanimous Council vote only. The council alone has the power to replace the Dragon.
