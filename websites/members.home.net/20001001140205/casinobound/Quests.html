A Warning:  Quests are constantly changing in the game.  The quest information is compiled from information sent to me by the people who visit this site.  While I do my best to keep it up to date and accurate, due to changes, updates or just plain mistaken information, some quest information can sometimes be in error.

A note on quests.  Many quests are level dependant, and will only be given to a player within a certain level range.  Also, the quests you are given and the rewards you receive seem to depend entirely upon your faction with the NPC giving the quest.  Very few quests are class based. If you can't get one of the quests listed here, it is probably because your faction is not high enough.  One result of this is that if you do the same quest over and over, raising your faction each time, you will start to get better items (at least for those where the reward is not fixed) and may receive different quests as well.  If you have the patience, you can receive some pretty good items by doing a variable reward type of quest 30, 40 or more times.  Also, pay attention to which faction lowers with the quests, as you may be making some dangerous enemies.
