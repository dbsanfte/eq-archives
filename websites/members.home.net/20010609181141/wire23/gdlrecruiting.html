r e c r u i t i n g   g u i d e l i n e s
  • A member must be of the 3rd guild rank to sponsor a new member.
  • A member may not sponsor more then 1 new member at any given time.
  • All new applicants must be of 30th level or higher (exceptions will be on a case-by-case basis).
  • All new applicants will be subject to review by the entire guild in a process starting with a post from their intended sponsor in the New Member Submissions discussions area.
  • All new applicants must be unanimously approved by the Black Tower Council.
  • All new applicants must take an initial Oath before assuming member status. A council member must conduct this ceremonial oath and both the new member and their sponsor must be present. When an applicant has been accepted for membership their sponsor will be informed and responsible for arranging a time to meet with a Council member for the guilding ceremony.
  • All new members are subject to guild guidelines and policies from the moment they are guilded.
  • All new members will start at rank one (soldier/novice) when entering Black Tower. All promotions will be decided by the Council and administered by the Dragon. Promotions are based on one's experience and time with the guild, and how well one represents themselves both to guild members and fellow Norrathians.
  • All new members will retain their sponsor until they have achieved 3rd rank.
  • Sponsors will be held accountable for the persons they choose to sponsor. Abuse of this privelege will result in direct administrative discipline, starting with lose of rank. Thus if you don't know this person you are submitting well enough to trust his/her sincerety, do not submit them for membership.
