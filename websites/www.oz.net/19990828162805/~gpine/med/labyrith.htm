The Labyrith

Mobs of interest.. They're all interesting to newbie groups. I forget most
  of them. Groups in the 8-20 lev range work well enough. Lab is good for
  a group of 2 or 3 in the 8-15lev range for xp. Hidden life, so bring
  orange potions.

Goblin castle is rough for newbies, but good xp.

x - exit/enter to/from wilderness (just a bit SE of Medievia)
a - welcome to Labyrith room
b - up to Goblin Castle!
c - Weapon shop
d - Armory
e - Apothocary (caution; some potions aren't so fun :)
f - Food
1a/b - at 1a you can only go down (goes to 1b) (cannot go back up (1b to 1a))
2a/b - at 2a you will _fall_ down to 2b (ouch) Cannot go up (2b to 2a).
