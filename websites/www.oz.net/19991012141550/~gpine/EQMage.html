This table will show a ratio of damage per mana cast with particular spells. Basically the higher the ratio, the more efficient the spell is. Naturally a spell that does, say, 5 damage but has a high ratio is probably not as useful as a spell that does massive damage, even if it has a low ratio.
Great examples are the fire shields. The level 8 one has incredible ratios, pays off 1:1 (damage:mana) fastest, but damage over a duration isn't so hot. 6dam per slap from a level 40+ beast won't add up to much.
