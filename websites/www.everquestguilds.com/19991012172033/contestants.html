Featured Guild of the week 5/30/99 til 6/6/99

Can you smell the rusty steel? Are you after that rare camaraderie?
Well look no further. Clan Battlehammer has put all of those
important elements to an enjoyable roleplaying experience to the test.
With kings, knights and chivalry at its finest, Battlehammer has indeed
put its players in a fun medieval world. War plays little role in their
charter and helping each other out at all times is a must. Two Elven
Ears up for the Battlehammer. Hail to the King!


Featured Guild of the week 6/6/99 til 6/13/99

Sterling Order of Knights. An online
gaming guild that surely has some history
throughout online RPGs. Experience, no
hierarchies and the EverQuest to uphold
justice, are SoK's Strengths. Members seem
to show great pride in belonging to this
outstanding guild. Two Elven Ears up
for the Sterling members of SoK.

   


Featured Guild of the Week      6/13/99 til 6/20/99

Blood Coven. An excellent guild to join
if one is interested in purifying the land of
Norrath of those "other" races. Dark Elves
prove to be the only worthy race to exist in
the land, and Blood Coven is here to make
sure of it. Looks like an extremely fun guild
to join, especially if you are playing a female
character. The fury of Blood Coven has taken
over, will you succumb? or will you join? Two
Dark Elven Ears up, for Blood Coven!


Featured Guild of the Week 6/20/99 til 6/27/99
The Crimson Rose. An online roleplaying
Guild that has established themselves as
very organized and respected band of players.
Following a series of codes, laws and order,
these players have full respect for each other
and for their cause. With guild quests and other
events, The Crimson Rose easily deserves
Two Elven Ears up for the week. This is how
a guild should be run.


Featured Guild of the Week 6/27/99 til 7/4/99
The Dreamwalkers Nation. Undoubtedly
a guild of many members. A Guild based
on peace and prosperity. Their code consists
of maintaining peace and friendship but not to
ignore the importance of staying strong. I think
we have found Utopia! An enjoyable Guild with
many benefits of a friendly environment. This
Guild has tons of potential to be one of Online
Gaming's future powerhouses. Two Elven Ears
up for the Dream Team.


Featured Guild of the Week 7/11/99 til 7/18/99
Order of Polaris. A guild built on trust and
friendship. With pre-requisites not too high,
Polaris gives even the average player a
chance at ridding Norrath of all its minions.
With some pre screening and initiation requests,
membership is not something one would take for
granted. Be ready for some serious "guilding"
when you are considering Order of Polaris.
Two Elven Ears Up for Polaris, and its quest for
a more "structured" Norrath.


Featured Guild of The Week 7/18/99 til 7/25/99
Sacred Reich. A militant Guild based on
the almighty PP. They are a trained group
of players, out to accept any offers as hired
mercenaries. The code of which decisions about
which job to accept, are done by the current Templar
and the council that surrounds him. A guild based on
freelance work, and for sure is one that praises
destruction as a means to an end. "Die valiantly
and there is no fear" Two Elven Ears Up for Sacred
Reich and its contracts.


Featured Guild of The Week 7/25/99 til 8/01/99
Dragon Guardians of Eternity. A Guild based
on the struggle of holding the balance of evil
and good. Sheer balance is what the DGoE
are trying to uphold throughout the land of
Norrath. With a rich history of events, and
a strong membership base, there is a good
chance that Balance will not only be a dream
in the realms of alignment. Two Elven Ears
Up for balancing Norrath.


Featured Guild of The Week 8/01/99 til 8/8/99
Pretentious Malevolent Sisters. Hmm, Well
P.M.S. for short. Need I say more? Check the
Guild out for yourself, I'm kinda spooked on
writing my review. Can ya blame me? I am sure
they are all nice ;) Definitely one of Norrath's
Guilds not to be toyed with. Two Elven Ears up
for the Women of Norrath.
