The Assembly of Norrath 12 We recognize that in times of war everyone has a specific role to play. Those who would oppose The Assembly of Norrath do not recognize the folly of their ways. Through bigotry and hatred, the peoples of Norrath are splitting themselves up and fighting each other. It is this action that will lead to their ultimate demise. Those of The Assembly of Norrath have recognized this flaw. What good is a fighter without a cleric? Who but a necromancer could help you find your lost body? Would you not take advantage of a Trolls strength, or an Elven archer, just because they are different races? What good is an army comprised solely of fighters when warring against fighters and magi? Would you join an army of only human fighters, or would you put your bigotry aside, and join the army of Humans, Elves, Erudites, Trolls etc.? Using this logic, we intend to unite the peoples of Norrath, using each persons individual strength in order to improve the whole. It is in this way that those who oppose us will fall and The Assembly will reign supreme! N/A N/A
