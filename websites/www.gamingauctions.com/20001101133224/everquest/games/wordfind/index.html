EverQuest Word Search

The World of EverQuest will never be the same! Word Search has arrived, and it is time to use your powerful skills and magics that you have learned in your journeys to complete the EverQuest Word Search puzzles. All those whom complete the task in record time shall be listed upon the parchment of the mighty, for all to view. Hurry forth and complete the quest, as time does grow short.

The following Word Lists exist: LOCATIONS, SKILLS.

Welcome to EverQuest Word Search. The object of the game is to find the listed words that have been jumbled up inside the puzzle. When you find a word, you can select it by clicking on the begining of the word and dragging until you reach the last letter. Once you let off the mouse button, the word will remain highlighted and will appear RED on the list to the right. This means that you have properly located a word.

If you are stuck and cannot find anymore words, you can have the puzzle SOLVED for you. This will of course label you as a cheater and your puzzle-solved time will not be displayed.

If you want your name to be included on the high-scores list, take a screenshot of the completed puzzle and email it to me, jparker@gamingauctions.com. Be sure that the entire puzzle is included in the screenshot.

A large list of screenshot programs for download is available at http://tucows.fastdata.net/scapt95.html
