E'Ci Gnomestock 11:30 AM
Come out and jam with the Gnomes on E'Ci server:

What: Gnomestock '99!
When: Friday, November 26th, at Noon PST
Where: Beginning at Ak'Anon on E'ci, ending in Lavastorm
Who: Any and all EQ bards and their friends, family, and pets
How: Come join us for a day of merriment! Create a 1st-level gnome on E'ci with a gname... err... name beginning with Gn (preferably followed by your bardic name so we can identify each other). We will hopefully have teleports to bring us to Antonica. We'll get bound in Freeport, then begin our lemminglike run, ending in a leap into the lava pits of Lavastorm.
Why: Because we like you!

Gnaloro
aka Aloro Silvertongue (E'ci)

MaximumEQ News 11:20 AM
MaximumEQ has some news for the EverQuest Community:

Greetings!

Two rather large bits of news for the EQ community today!

First off, MaximumEQ and The Maximum Gaming Network are proud to be co-sponsoring the 1999 Games of Encouragement. Games of Encouragement is an idea that was started last year by GameStats News Network. We are incredibly proud that they have allowed us to co-sponsor the event this year.

"Last year we presented the community with an opportunity to come together and give a bunch of great kids some smiles that they might not otherwise have had at Christmas. The program (which we dubbed Games Of Encouragement) was successful beyond all expectations and we received over 1500 donations for Christmas 1998. GOE was so successful in fact that the program benefited Ronald McDonald Houses all across the country!"

We encourage the EQ community to show just how thoughtful they are by helping out this worthy cause.

GoE URL: http://www.gamestats.com/specials/lovegames/goe99/index.html

***

On a far less important note, MaximumEQ has added a Price Check section to our information site.

How many times have you wondered what a fair price for an item is when it flashed by you in Auction? With the help of the EQ community we can answer that question. Our item listings now have an option to "View Going Price". Players can input their latest sales and purchases. Our database will calculate the average price over the last 30 days on the item. Players can see how the pricing trend has changed and compare pricing on servers. It will be interesting to see the pricing differences between mature and infantile servers. This is a resource that relies entirely on the community to succeed. We think it is worthwhile and hope that you do too!

MaximumEQ URL: http://www.maximumeq.com

Thanks for your continued support

MaximumEQ Staff
