D I E T I E S

 
DIETY is derived from the latin root word "diet" and is translated as "food of the Gods". Each diety is the patron saint of certain food groups and drinks, and will favor those characters who consume them. These dietary requirements will be listed in the diety description.

When Selecting a Diety, one should strive to remember the old adage. "Eat, drink and be merry, for tomorrow we diet."

The best time to select a diety is before eating or drinking too much. This selection will greatly influence the player's choice of food stuffs.

Strictly speaking, the selection of a Diety is recommended, but not required, unless the character is of a religious nature (Chef, Connoisseur, Glutton, Guzzler, or Professional Bowler, etc.) For those who do not chose a Diety, life will be "like a box of chocolate…you never know what you're gonna get!", and characters who play without the benefit of devine intervention will often be stuck with "potluck" dinners.

The character will have this Diety for it's entire life, and that Diety brings with it certain food alliances and enemies. For example, choosing Tuna Arg!, the Chicken of the Sea, will most certainly make your character unpopular around the temple of Table-Set Nice, the Prime Gastronaut. On the other hand, choosing Pretzealous will make you very popular with the followers of Corona or Alley Man.

For further information and menu selection, choose from the list of Dieties.
