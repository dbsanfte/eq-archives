Just a note about the skill caps, some of these caps are unproven, but generally the combat and singing skills are 200 and the thief skills are 40.  Also, Wood Elves start with 50 hiding and 50 foraging, so don't bother training with them.

Skill Name Level Attained Cap Description
1h Blunt 1 200 Weapon skill using one handed bashing weapons.   (i.e. Club)
1h Slashing 1 200 Weapon skill using one handed slashing weapons. (i.e. Short Sword)
2h Blunt 1 Cannot Raise Weapon skill using two handed bashing weapons.   (Bards can't raise their 2h skills)
2h Slashing 1 Cannot Raise Weapon skill using two handed bashing weapons. (Bards can't raise their 2h skills)
Alcohol Tolerance 1 200? How much alcohol you can drink without feeling the effects of it.  (blurred vision etc...)
Baking 1 200 A trade skill used to bake food.
Beg 1 >100 Lets you beg for money or items from NPCs, can get you killed, also rumored at high levels to yield special items.
Bind Wound 1 100 Lets you use bandages to help quicken the healing rate of people with less than 50% hp
Brewing 1 165 A trade skill used for making alcoholic beverages, and consequently, lots of friends.  New recipes have been added to get your skill all the way to 200 now.
Fishing 1 200 How well you fish, considered to be a trade skill.
Fletching 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning bow and arrow components into bows and arrows.
Hand to Hand 1 100 Weapon skill using no weapon, also used when holding an instrument, useful for bards who use instruments in battle.
Jewel Craft 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning gems and precious metals into jewelry, sometimes even magic jewelery.
Piercing 1 200 Weapon skill using piercing weapons.   (i.e. Dagger)
Pottery 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pots (amongst other things) out of clay.
Sense Heading 1 200 Lets you sense the direction you are facing, helps you navigate.
Singing 1 200 How well you sing, no need for an instrument, it affects songs that require singing.
Smithing 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning ore into armor and other metal items.
Swimming 1 200 How well you swim, the higher your skill, the faster you swim.
Tailoring 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pelts into types of leather armor.
Throwing 1 200? Weapon skill using throwing weapons. (i.e. Throwing Axe)
Percussion 5 200 How well you play percussion instruments (hand drum), it affects songs that require percussion instruments.
String 8 200 How well you play stringed instruments (mandolin, lute), it affects songs that require stringed instruments.
Dodge 10 125 This skill automatically is check and if successful, you dodge an enemies attack.
Brass 11 200 How well you play brass instruments (horn), it affects songs that require brass instruments.
Forage 12 40 A useful skill that lets you scrounge up food or water, lets you spend more time away from cities.
Wind 14 200 How well you play wind instruments (flute), it affects songs that require wind instruments.
Dual Wield 17 200 A great skill, lets you use two weapons at once, and get two attacks per round.
Sneak 17 75 Lets you walk slowly and quietly so that enemies don't detect you.
Safe Fall 24 40 Lets you fall from heights that would normally hurt you without taking any damage.
Hide 25 40 Lets you hide to avoid being attacked or seen.  You cannot move while hidden, or hide while moving.
Instill Doubt 26 100 Causes your target to turn and walk away.   Can only be trained with Caskin Marsheart, a bard guildmaster on the second floor of the Freeport bard guild.  You can find it in the list of skills just above Common Tongue.
Disarm Traps 30 100 Lets you disarm traps near you, at current, there are only a couple of traps in the entire world.
Track 35 100 A GREAT skill, lets you locate creatures or people who are near you.
Pick Locks 40 ? Lets you pick locks without needing a key, apparently it is rather disappointing as some locks still need keys.
