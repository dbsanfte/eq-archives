    May 26th, 1999  

GuildMaster JetStarr begins to visualize the mission of KGB in Norrath
An article by JetStarr, KGB GuildMaster

Picture at Left: After gaining initial experience in Qeynos and Qeynos Hills, GuildMaster JetStar sets off to the Plains of Karana to hone his skills and to discover the ways of Magic

It is rumored that parallel worlds of Norrath exist. Our is called Rallos Zek. This world is very different from the others. The gods do not allow battles among the intelligent races unless one has declared himself ready to take part is such conflicts. Here is Rallos Zek, you have no such choice. For it is here that my men and I have been called to inhabit. For it is here that murderers and evils run unchecked across the land, killing the innocent and laughing at justice and the laws of the light.

My first experience was disturbing to say the least. When I arrived here on Rallos Zek I quickly realized my new incarnation did not have the combat and magical skills I had possessed in Sosaria. These skills would have to be re-learned, and this is a lengthy process to say the least. I had been given this knowledge from many of our former members that have broken away from KGB to form The Elders and The Arcane Order guides on other parallel incarnations of Norrath. Lord of the Guild Ramirez (known as Lexus here on Rallos Zek) arrived a few weeks before me and was more experienced. Together with former KGB friend Existence by Proxy (known as Nietz here on Rallos Zek) help me with advice as I quickly sharpened my skills.

I spent my first few days near the walls of Qeynos hunting small animals and gnolls. I moved into the hills of Qeynos and started to hunt animals, skinning them and selling the skins for much needed gold and platinum. As my experience grew I knew times would get more difficult. I could hear the shouts of Murderers and their prey in echoing in the distance. I knew it was just a matter of time before I would have to once again face a murderer face to face. I also knew that unlike the situation I was used to in Sosaria, I was not surrounded by scores of talented KGB Knights. I thought of the members of KGB that helped us to success in Sosaria. They had served their time and now pursue their own futures and callings under different disciplines. I had once again been called on a new mission, with a handful of the most dedicated KGB Knights. This time I would have to face the Murderers alone, and most likely would feel the pain of death once again, with little chance of defending myself.

It amazes me to see that with a huge threat of Dark Elves, Ogres, and Trolls, the forces of light still manage to prey upon each other with unmatched greed and lack of compassion and humility. It is a sickening sight to watch brother kill brother for a gold coin or a sword of bronze. This would be our first challenge.

After a night of adventuring with my new found friends Weeda, Lewboski, Kalani, Sapling and Bemuse, I decided to venture out on my own since most of the land had made camp and gone to sleep. It was my intention to improve my skills as much as I could that night. I hunted close to the Guard Tower called T2 by hunters on the plains of Karana. I continued to pass the only other hunter in the area. His name was Xrastlin. I just finished fighting a Giant Beetle and used my new 2 handed bronze sword to carve out the beetles eyes, which I found to be worth something at the local hunting lodge (Fire beetle and Giant Beetles eyes glow even after the death of the animal, and are used by many hunters as replacements to lanterns). I was hurt badly and was just sitting down to rest and heal. With dagger in hand, Xrastlin approached me. I thought nothing of it, and many of the hunters here heal each other when needed. I was stunned to find out that instead of healing me, he thrust his dagger into my already wounded chest. I quickly turned and began to run, still in disbelief that this man could be so cruel. He cast a spell that caused the land under my feet to break open, and large roots grabbed my feet. I could no longer move. As Xrastlin began to cast another spell, I pleaded with him for my life. Without hesitation he finished his spell and I screamed in pain as I was engulfed in flames. Xrastlin then took the bronze sword I had spent days and days saving for. He quickly gated to Qeynos.

Realizing Xrastlin was nothing but a greedy murderer, I shouted as loud as I could to the other people of Qeynos to watch out for him. He denied my claims stating that he tried to heal me and that the well known murderer duo Pheer and Faldor had killed me and he tried to heal. He then sent me a message saying, "Noone will even believe you, I am a well known Anti". I continued my accusations and Xrastlin became more and more upset. He then sent me this message:


Xrastlin tells you, Hehehe im going to kill you again now. maybe until you lose levels I haven't decided

I look forward to the day when my men and I meet up with XRastlin again. This is the kind of scum that I swear to clean up. My mission continues.......

    May 22nd, 1999  

KGB's JetStar (JetStarr) and Ramirez (Lexus) moving up on Rallos Zek
An article by JetStar, KGB GuildMaster

Picture at Left: KGB GuildMaster JetStar (JetStarr) exploring the Qeynos Hills, seen here wearing his new armor and sword provided by Lexus (Ramirez)

After poor luck in Freeport, Norrathian KGB pioneer Lexus (Ramirez) convinced JetStarr (JetStar) to end his incarnation as JetStarth and relocate the city of Qeynos. Freeport is truly the slum of the land. Rampant murder and chaos rule the day. Qeynos is a totally different story. The inhabitants are more kind and seem to be more experienced.

Lexus and JetStarr are the only two official KGB members to venture to the Rallos Zek server. There are rumors that other members have arrived or are just arriving. We will make their names known as we make contact. KGB will require a minimum of nine members to settle in Rallos Zek before we will be recognized as an official Guild.

GuildMaster JetStar has spoken of several good recruitment opportunities, and the recruiting process will begin some time this weekend. JetStar has plans to negotiate with the TAO and The Elders splinters of the KGB. These two Guild splinters have settled on the Mythaniel Marr server and some of the members may want to seek duel membership in the KGB once again to get involved in the original mission of the Guild. This land is in dire need of an organization like KGB to place the murderers in check. Unlike Sosaria, none of the politics or red tape exist here. Murders are constantly reported and innocents cry for help 24/7. Its time for us to get busy.

KGB's Official Return to Norrath Postponed
An article by JetStar, KGB GuildMaster

Members of KGB have postponed the return to Norrath until Tuesday March 30th.

Due to the fact that many KGB members are awaiting their release copies of EQ, we have postponed our return to Norrath. These additional days will give KGB members a chance to install, patch and get used to the EQ Interface.

GuildMaster JetStar has issued the following mandates for KGB membership in the new world:

The following classes are disallowed to the KGB Membership:

Shadow Knights
Rogues
Necromancers

The KGB will continue to abide the official code of the Guild:

As a Member of the Knights of Glory and Beer, my personal integrity is irreproachable. I will never degrade myself by the lying to, cheating of, stealing from, or harming the innocent. I hold myself personally and unequivocally responsible to ensure the preservation of the honor of the Membership and good name of the KGB at all times. I will, to the best of my power, cause the peace to be kept and prevent all offenses against the persons and properties of those of "good" alignment.
