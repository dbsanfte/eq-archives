Everlore - Informational site about EverQuest.  Hints, maps, news articles.

EverBazaar (Norrath Merchants) - A site where you can research, by server or item, anything for sale in the game, or list an item you wish to sell to be displayed for others to look up.

EverQuest Player Photos! - Ever wanted to see the face BEHIND the keyboard?  This site has organized real life photos that players have submitted by which server they play on.   Very cool!

EQ Express - Online "newspaper" concerning EverQuest with articles, editorials, and stories.

EQ Vault - The premiere site for news, articles, hints, and information relating to EverQuest.

Noble Warriors of Norrath - Allies of the Order.  Guild website.

Arcane Knights - Allies of the Order.  Guild website.

The Alliance - Homepage of the Tri-Guild Alliance between the Order, the Arcane Knights, and the Noble Warriors of Norrath.  There will be listed all Alliance events, as well as a message board for all three guilds to do joint posts.
