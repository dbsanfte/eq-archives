Welcome to the Legion of Legends Homepage.

As most of you know we are a very small guild of ruthless killers. Only on rare occasion have we invited new members. They must prove themselves worthy and loyal before we will even consider them as a candidate. Legion is a level 20 to 24 guild only, with almost all members equipped with excellent to supreme gear for our level range.

Most of us have found the day to day slaying of frogs,orcs, and bugs to be a bit boring but still have plenty of fun with PvP. Making lower level twinks is an easy way to get lots of action and be able to have multiple characters to play without spending hour after hour leveling.
So if you see any of us in game, feel free to attack , PvP is the sole reason we play.
