Tyranids Codex

The horror we've all been dreading (most of us anyway!) has finally arrived. Codex: Tyranids is here! This book contains all the information fans of the Hivemind have been craving for some time now. Learn what little we know about the Hivefleets, invasion tactics, and the myriad of creatures that make up the Tyranid race. The army list inside this 48 page behemoth features an extensive standard army list as well as a section for developing your very own personalized swarm list. Create a gigantic mass of lesser creatures scuttling forward or take to the skies upon leathery wings of doom! There is also a huge color section full of excellently painted miniatures that'll surely inspire your painting skills. You'll find a ton of color schemes to gawk at and drool over. Expert and novice modelers will also enjoy the pages and pages of conversion ideas and biomorph suggestions. We're talking easy arm swaps to more complex mutli-piece metal projects here! From start to finish, this book is awesome!
