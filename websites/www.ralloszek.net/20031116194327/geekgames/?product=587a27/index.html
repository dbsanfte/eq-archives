Codex: Necrons

From the depths of ancient and terrifying memory comes Codex Necrons! This highly detailed Warhammer 40,000 army book contains complete details for the horrific new threat the Necrons present to the galaxy of the 41st Millennium. Within its pages you will find a complete army list, background, new wargear and special rules pertaining to how these techno-mechanical terrors perform on the battlefield. The Necrons, are the remnants of an alien race ancient beyond imaginin. Sealed into metal shells these hideous constructs are awakening from their sixty-million year domancy to plague the galaxy once more. Thousands of immortal, soulless metal warriors have risen from dusty status-tombs, intent on preying on the teeming species of the galaxy Necron technology is so advanced that their infamous gauss weaponry can cut through infantry and armor with ease! Marching alongside the Necron Warriors are eldritch monstrosities and terrifying war machines. Some are mercurially fast and utterly lethal and others are as ponderous and inescapable as death itself. Worst of all are the masters of the Necrons. These omniscient beings of god-like power have risen to accompany the Necrons to war and are known only as the C'tan. Inside Codex: Necrons you will find; • ARMY LISTS: The complete Necrons army list allows you to select your forces for tabletop battles. Also included are the statistics for the Monolith, a terrifying Necron war machine as well as ancient wargear, new weapons and special rules. • Hobby Section: Sixteen full-color pages packed with advice and tips on collecting, modelling and playing with a Necron army. • Special Characters: Rules and background information on the Nightbringer and Deceiver - two ancient god-like beings known only as the C'tan. • Background: The story of the Necrons and their mysterious masters, and shocking insights that bore right into the heart of the Imperium.
