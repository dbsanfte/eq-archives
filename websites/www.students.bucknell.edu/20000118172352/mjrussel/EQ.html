Minan - Lord Necromancer

The Beginning:

I crawled out of the earth, clawing at the earth and rocks to reach life-supporting air. Cringing I realized that this air was no longer necessary for me, I had become death incarnate. I could feel the thread of magic that tied me to my soul force deep in the rock beneath me. The spell had weakened me beyond what I had feared. Summoning some strength from within I uttered the words of power and surrounded myself with an invisible force shield. Looking around at my surroundings I found that I had emerged near an ancient ruin. Empty eye sockets that burned with inner fire looked back at me from a group of undead that stood watching me from a distance. Fearing that I was to be attacked I quickly went over the proper motion for a destructive spell. Then I felt their words enter my head. They spoke to me as their master, as one they would follow, as one of their own. A sly grin found its way onto my face for a split second. The melding had occurred; I was master of the undead…but at what cost to my power?
