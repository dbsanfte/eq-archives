Entitlements

As a member, you are entitled to the following

  • Induction into FOR by the founder.
  • A FOR character website - basics can be set up by Merlynus if you so choose. RL name and birthdate is required if making one on Geocities. (If you do not already have a website that you can use and devote space to for your FOR char.)
  • You will be added to member email list.
  • You will be added to the website member list (above). A title may or may not be given to you. The founder bestows titles based on a need within the guild or as a bit of humor. Titles are not required and you may choose not to have one.
  • ON HOLD...You will be given a username and password for entry into the member's section of the website (currently under construction).
  • You will be offered the opportunity to make a wood elf character for our sub-guild WEB (Woodland Elves Brigade).
  • You will be offered the opportunity to make an Iksar or other negative character for our sub-guild IL (Ishtar's Light) unless you wish to add them to FOR.
  • You will be invited to our annual guild gathering held sometime in the late summer/early fall on the official property of FOR, The Crystal Cave Sanctuary in Del Rio, Tennessee USA. Other places for gatherings or not to have one are also options.
