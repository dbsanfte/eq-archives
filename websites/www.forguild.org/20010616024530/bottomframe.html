Welcome to Friends of the Realms! (FOR)

Basis of Guild: An elite harmonious group of a maximum capacity of 30 members, dedicated to the tenets of the Guild.

Tenets of the Guild: Honesty, Integrity, Honor, Loyalty, Respect and Fun

    • Honesty in all written and verbal communications,
    • Integrity in all actions and deeds
    • Honor always in our hearts and minds
    • Loyalty to FOR and what it stands for
    • Respect of others and each other
    • Fun because we all deserve it!

(original descriptions of tenets written by Leafsong)

Thanks for stopping by! All of us at FOR welcome you - please come and visit us soon in any of the realms we currently inhabit. We are a friendly, close-knit group of people who are helpful and there for those in need. May you Travel Safely and By The Light!
