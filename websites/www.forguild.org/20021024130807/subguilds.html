The Woodland Elves Brigade

The Woodland Elves Brigade was started in 1999 as a sub-guild within FOR for the original purposes of allowing all members to stay within the same area to hunt together within Everquest. You must be a member of FOR to be in WEB, but this is not a requirement in our other sub-guild, Ishtar's Light (IL). Most members of WEB are within the same levels and we play on Tuesday nights. The same rules apply towards requirements of a member of WEB, except that unlike FOR, dueling is allowed (but not PvP or guild wars).

The leader has the title of WEB Spider. Following is a list of members who chose to make WEB chars for this specific gameplay night. Name in parenthesis is their main FOR char name.

Ishtar's Light

You do not have to be a member of FOR to belong to IL, unlike WEB. Separate application for IL can be found on the application page and will be sent to the head of the subguild.
