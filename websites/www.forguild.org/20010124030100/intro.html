The Guild...

FOR (Friends of the Realms) was established in September, 1995 on Sierra's Imagination Network. Currently, there are 26 members in the guild, with founding member, Merlynus and four other members comprising the Council. Members can be found in the following online roleplaying Realms:

Everquest by 989 Studios
The Realm by Sierra
Illusia: Quest for the Eternals by Living Mask Productions
Empiriana by Infinite Realms
Diablo by Blizzard Entertainment

(currently beta testing Gryphon's Tapestry)

The guild's basic tenets are: Honesty, Integrity, Honor, Loyalty, Respect and Fun. For more information, please visit our information page.
