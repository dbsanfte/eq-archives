Information on our members...

That which makes FOR unique are not only the people, but its basic constant principles:

1. A small group of close friends who share the same ideals and wish to spend time together.

2. To teach and show others that a game environment does not have to be negative - we can make a difference through our actions, using a strong positive image with a positive char.

3. A governing body of the founder and council with all members having a voice and the right to share their opinions.

4. An adult-oriented guild whereby the members would not have to "babysit" nor censure themselves in a game environment.

5. In keeping with the Truth tenet and expecting trust between members, characters and PBK (person behind keyboard) are to be of the same gender.

6. That we would explore new places to play in order to expand our horizons and keep interest in FOR.

Entitlements

As a member, you are entitled to the following

  • Induction into FOR by the founder.
  • A FOR character website on Geocities - basics can be set up by Merlynus if you so choose. RL name and birthdate is required by Geocities. (If you do not already have a website that you can use and devote space to for your FOR char.)
  • You will be added to member email list.
  • You will be added to the PowWow Community list and given the community name and password in order to attend meetings. You must download PowWow from www.tribal.com in order to utilize the Community and our bulletin board.
  • You will be added to the website member list (above). A title may or may not be given to you. The founder bestows titles based on a need within the guild or as a bit of humor. Titles are not required and you may choose not to have one.
  • You will be given a username and password for entry into the member's section of the website.
  • You may be given gifts on the current home game collected by the members as our way of saying "Welcome to FOR"; on any other game you will also be given gifts that will help start out your char, if available.
  • You will be offered the opportunity to make a wood elf character for our sub-guild WEB (Woodland Elf Brigade).
  • You will be offered the opportunity to make a Iksar character for our sub-guld IL (Ishtar's Light).
  • You will be invited to our two annual guild gatherings, held during the French Quarter Festival in New Orleans, USA (April each year) and again sometime in the late summer/early fall on the official property of FOR, The Crystal Cave Sanctuary in Del Rio, Tennessee USA.
