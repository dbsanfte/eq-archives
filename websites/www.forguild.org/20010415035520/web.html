The Wood Elf Brigade

The Wood Elf Brigade was started in 1999 as a sub-guild within FOR for the purposes of allowing all members to stay within the same area to hunt together within Everquest. You must be a member of FOR to be in WEB, but this is not a requirement in our new sub-guild, Ishtar's Light (IL). Most members of WEB are within the same levels and we play on Friday nights. The same rules apply towards requirements of a member of WEB, except that unlike FOR, dueling is allowed (but not PvP or guild wars).

Fornleaf is the leader, with the title of WEB Spider. Following is a list of members who chose to make WEB chars for this specific gameplay night. Name in parenthesis is their main FOR char name.
