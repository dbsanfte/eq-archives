We would prefer that everyone be in the current Home Realm, if at all possible, while still continuing to play and test in other games.

Not all players play in all games, nor together at any given time; we would like to know about other graphic (not text-based) RPG games, as most of us are testing and searching for other realms to consider.

Representation of FOR by a minimum of two guild members in other online games is accepted as long as the Council is notified and given the opportunity to check out the game site for its appropriateness to the guild. If while testing a game you find individuals who may fit in FOR, please give them our website address for more information.

The guild member must adhere to and abide by FOR tenets when in other realms - show how proud you are to be a member of FOR!
