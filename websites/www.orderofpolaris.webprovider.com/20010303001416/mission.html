Guild Charter

Hail friends. This is the most important room of our guild. We take pride in what is written on these scrolls contained within. Their contents, you may ask? Far be it from such a noble order as that of Polaris to withold information of our purpose in Norrath. We invite you to read our guild charter.


"We, The Order of Polaris, enobled by high and sacred purpose, empowered by the gods, are tireless in our struggle to bring order to the age of turmoil. Preserved by noble, resolute men and women, we instil virtue and exact harmony in the realm of Norrath by defending the weak, assisting the needy, and denying chaos an arena in which to flourish. We are the light in the darkness that shall never be extinguished."
