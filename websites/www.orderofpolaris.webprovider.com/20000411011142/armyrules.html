RULES OF WAR
Modified on 12/02/1999 to clarify the rules of war and engagement. Please submit any requests for alteration or additional rules to ladydamali@hotmail.com

As defined by the council of light and darkness, the rules of the guildwar between the Army of Light and the Army of Innoruk are as follows:


  • All Fighting must take place in wide open, outside zones. Fighting in dungeons or cities is strictly forbidden. For zones such as Greater Faydark and Highpass where charecters can be bound, fighting is allowed.
  • You may attack the opposite side at will. You must offer your opponent the opportunity to yield at one bubble of health. If your opponent does not yield by immediately zoning, you may kill him/her.
  • You may not camp bind points, particularly in the zones of Greater Faydark and Highpass.
  • Once you have been killed, you must wait a minimum of 15 minutes to join back in the battle to simulate the ressurection of your character by your Avatar. You must loot your corpse before rejoining the battle.
  • A mortal may not kill an Avatar, although attacking an Avatar is admissable. What this gives the player is the option to attack an Avatar if they are accompanying a hunting party. Only another Avatar may slay another Avatar.
  • The war ends when all Avatars of one side of the war are slain. The estimated duration of the event has been set at 2 weeks.
  • Any deviation from these rules will result in immediate expulsion from either of the two armies.
