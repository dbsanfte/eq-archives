12/06/1999

 Two huge events to report today. The Avatar of Prexus, Lady Damali Arcanus, was coerced by Innoruk into joining the Army of the Dark. Innoruk kidnapped the last of the kedge, the only living child of Prexus, and threated to kill him if Prexus did not give in to his demands. Prexus, seeing no other way, oblidged. Before entering the abysmal pit of hell that Innoruk was dragging Prexus into, the Avatar told the council of light that she was depending on them to carry on the fight and to destroy Innoruk so she may return to the light. The child of Prexus is still being held captive and is closely guarded by Lanys T'Vil, the daughter of Innoruk.

The Blood Lord of Hate himself, Quinne Polaris, was taken captive by a small, elite group of the Army of the Light last night that included none other than the Avatar of Mithanel Marr. Quinne Polairs is being held captive at an undisclosed location. Intelligence within the Army of the Dark has reported that they are mounting forces on the continent of Odus to plan for their next attack.

12/05/1999

 I would have to say that we have seen both some success and failure thus far with this event. The general consensus has really enjoyed participation. There have been, however, quite a few violations of rules being broken and feelings hurt. A select group of GM's and officers from the guilds involved are working very, very hard to iron out all of the wrinkles that we have seen thus far. We have already seen much improvement between the events that transpired on saturday and on sunday.

I would like to remind everyone once again that this event is intended to be a mixture of both a roleplaying and PVP event. The ultimate goal is to promote roleplaying of The Rathe server while providing a forum for all of you to meet new people and generally have a lot of fun. Adherance to the rules and keeping yourself up-to-date on the storyline and events are crucial in order for this event to be succesful. Again, I greatly appreciate the time, interest, and effort that everyone involved in this event has given. Have some patience with the coordinators, and we will deliver you a very successful, fun event.

12/04/1999

 My head is still swimming from the sheer volume of tells and tags which I passed out and answered last night. The respone for this event has been far greater than what I had anticipated. After reading an email from Kathia this morning, it turns out that we are both missing quite a few people still, including Avatars. I really REALLY want everyone who showed interest to get signed up today. I will be in North Ro handing out tags today. It is IMPERATIVE that one of the officers tag you today.

The sheer volume of people involved has posed a few problems as far as control of the event. I will be calling a meeting with the officers and Avatars of both sides to discuss how best to handle this. There will be ABSOLUTELY no roleplaying at the time of the meeting. This is a very serious matter, and we are scheduled to start the war today, so this must be resolved.

The war will begin this evening, after all of the Avatars and Archons have been tagged, as well as many, MANY other people who took the time to send me their information recieve their tags.

The Army of the Dark has already begun the trash talking and flexing their puny muscles. War is eminent...take us to DEFCON 3.
