Spell Window-Trimmed down and compacted a bit (slight modification).
Hotbuttons-Trimmed down, no other changes.
Group-Trimmed down, no other changes.
Target-Trimmed down, no other changes.
Chat-Trimmed down, no other changes.
Buffs-Trimmed down, no other changes.

NOTE-This removes your ability to click and drag the chat windows and the buff window HOWEVER you can use the 'resize' portions to move if you're clever. I took this as an acceptable loss in the interests of keeping the UI nice and neat.
