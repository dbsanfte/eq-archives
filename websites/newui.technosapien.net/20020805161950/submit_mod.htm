To Submit a Mod:

Include 3 things
1. The Mod
2. A Screen Shot
3. .txt file with what the mod is (what did you do), your email address and who you want to be credited as. (if you want either.. the last part is optional)
4. please send only the mod files you changed.. not the whole directory!!!

Zip this file up and email to: mods@eqinterface.com

NOTE: We no longer will post any submissions that do not follow these guidelines, its causing to much work on us to decipher unzipped mail.
