A Comprehensive Player Window: Untested
Added: 7-26-02
Author: Konatsu Mystere, Luclin Server
This compact player-information window contains all the vital statistics
about my character that concerned me enough that I wanted them on-screen
at all times:

- Player Name
- Level
- Health (gauge and numerical)
- Pet Health (given as a two pixel tall bar overlayed on your health bar)
- Mana (gauge and numerical percentage)
- Stamina (and current weight being carried / max weight)
- XP
- AC, ATK, Saves, Str, Sta, Agi, Dex, Wis, Int, and Cha

I did not yet add alternate experience, because I am so far away from having
any, that its constant presence on my screen would depress me to no end.

I also noticed that having many of these values on this window prevents the
same values from being updated on the inventory window; so I removed them
from the inventory screen. I did not yet reduce the overall size of the
inventory window, because, since it won't be on-screen all the time, I don't
really care if it's not as small as it could possibly be.
