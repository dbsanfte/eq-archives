Slim-line Inventory : Untested
Updated (7-25-02)
Author: Alderian Stormguard - Warlord of Druzzil Ro
Made the inventory slimmer by removing all excess space that I could.
Since there are 21 equipment slots, I put them in a 3x7 arrangement.
Removed the class art because it is a waste if you are looking for slim and sleek. Kept inventory and auto-equip together on left to help skill trade folks.
Put destroy button away from other buttons so there are no accidents but made it large to make it easier to loot and destroy worthless junk on mobs so that the corpses don't stick around.

Update: Fixed AA Exp gauge to be in correct position and removed the label. Fixed the HP numbers so it wouldn't go off the edge of the window.

Known Bugs: Resists > 99 may go ove edge of the window. This will be fixed soon.

Inventory "Pet" Window: Untested
Added: 7-26-02
Author: Geddine of Morell-Thule
You should use both windows together as combined they are the current inventory window. I didn't see the need for the pet window (as the health appears on the player bar already. And the buttons, last I checked, couldn't be dragged as hot buttons (useless). It is now the height of a 10-slot container with a combine button (largest container) as the size of containers cannot be modified. The auto-equip area is any area in the window. The Player Stat window now takes the place of the pet window (ie Alt-F1 is show/hide)
