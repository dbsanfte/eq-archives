All Dreams Must Pass

That night
protected so by your embrace
images of the day descend from the now
all that is becomes
a wisp of silk-like hair
the touch of flesh
a hot whisper in candle lighted shadows
all the while not hearing
mocking cold laughter
rising out of the darkness
forewarning me
all dreams must pass

February 23, 1998
By: The Troll King, Stoney Rose
