Filk Song Index

This is a collection of various filk songs, some traditional, some modern, some humourous, but all, I feel, appropriate to Bardics. Many of these come from the infamous "Black Book of Locksley," although a few are from other sources, and you may even find my name in there once or twice...

If there are any songs you feel I should include, or mistakes that need to be corrected, please send them to me.

The actual songbooks are left unorganized so that, if you want, you can just scan through them in a "random" order. The Fatguy's Songbook is courtesy of Lord Gorlan of the Redlands. The Bawdy Bits are collected by The Lady Aeruin ni Fial, and are presented here, because a certain online service censors the slightly risqué nature of these songs.
