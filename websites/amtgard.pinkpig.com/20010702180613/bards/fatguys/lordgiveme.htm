Oh Lord Won't You Give me an SCA Event

(1st-6th verses by Niall of Stone Ford, 1998)
(6th-8th verses by Lord Gorlan of the Red Lands, 1998)
(Tune: "Oh Lord, Won't you buy me a Mercedes Benz" by Janis Joplin)

    Oh lord, won't you give me an SCA event
    I can't afford travel, I just paid the rent
    Worked hard on my garb
    And made a Viking tent
    Oh lord, won't you give me an SCA event

    Oh lord, won't you give me a woods battle please
    I've practiced my tactics, and fighting on my knees
    I've been exercising,
    I last longer 'till I wheeze
    Oh lord, won't you give me a woods battle please

    Oh lord, won't you help me with my A & S
    My illumination, it just looks a mess
    I've even tried gilding,
    It just adds to the stress
    Oh lord won't you help me with my A & S

    Oh Lord, won't you give me a white belt to wear,
    Ten years as a squire, it's too much to bear.
    Wish I could hit my target
    Instead of hitting air
    Oh lord, won't you give me white belt to wear

    Oh lord won't you help me to last through this court
    Been doing the announcing, awards of every sort
    But I'll bet noone's name
    Was pronounced how I thought
    Oh lord won't you help me to last through this court.

    Oh lord, won't you give me an Award of Arms
    My friends all have titles, I'm full of alarm
    I've worked hard at each event
    Got nowhere with charm
    Oh lord won't you give me an Award of Arms

    Oh lord, won't you let me win the Crown Tourney
    I promise I'll travel, the whole land I'll see
    I'll mortgage my castle
    To raise the money
    Oh lord, won't you let me win the Crown Tourney

    Oh lord, won't you help me to pass my device
    I've done all the research and paid the price
    Sadly the heralds
    Have sent it back twice
    Oh lord, won't you help me to pass my device.

The Story Behind this Song

There had been some discussions on the Drachenwald mailing list about the lack of events this winter. This prompted Niall of Stone Ford to write the first three verses. When I read them, I added three more. There was another discussion going on about Royal visits at remote sites. I know some people who have been waiting for 3 or more years to get their devises passed. The 4th verse I started, and Lord Niall improved. I have come across many folks who have not gotten their AoA's mainly because every one assumes that they already have one.
