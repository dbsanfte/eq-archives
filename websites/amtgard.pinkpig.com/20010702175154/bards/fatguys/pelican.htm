The Story Behind this Song

Based on an old joke that goes "You know you're in the SCA when your co-workers think your crazy because you told them that you fell in love with a Pelican over the weekend." With that joke in the back of my mind, I heard "All Shook Up" on the radio and it fell in place. The last verse was another work in progress, that just didn't further. I thought it would make a great ending.
