The Story Behind this Song

At a recent Crown Tournament, we had to trudge up a hill to get up to the Castle where the fighting was to be held. Fat Guys do not as a rule do not go anywhere fast, unless there is a shield wall and combat involved. So as I was slowly trudging up the hill, Luke the Fool came past singing the first line of the Chorus. I am not sure if he was taunting me or was suggesting we go back to the camp for some beer. You can never tell with Luke.
