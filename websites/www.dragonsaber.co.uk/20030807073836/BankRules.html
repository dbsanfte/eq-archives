The Dragonsaber Guild Bank Rules

Initially the bank will have stuff donated by players in the Guild, and as such probably wont be fantastic. The aim is for those players who arent twinked to both work for the guild by providing the bread and butter stuff (eg words, runes, pages, and items they've upgraded from) and obtain items easier to improve stats and generally strengthen the Dragonsaber Army as a whole. However, for obvious reasons we cannot just let items go as the system will be abused, so here goes...it sounds a lot more complicated than it will turn out, honest!

Saving:

All Members begin with Zero credit and therefore donations must be made to the bank before items are “paid” for. For the lowbies, this isnt that hard, including common drops/components like bone chips/bat wings and other stuff highbies need but cant be bothered to source.

Items placed in the bank will be given the price agreed by one of the DSA Bankers, altho will usually be slightly lower than the current average bazaar price. The person donating will have his account credited with the value. Each Official Raid(as organised on the forum) that is participated in will increase your balance by 50pp, the organising Raid Leader should jot down who turns up and let me know if I was unavailable during it. Research items (Words/Runes/Mage Components etc) are worth 5pp each when handed in this way.

Spending:

Thus once you've saved up a little, and u find an item that your class can use you can "purchase" them and have your account debited by the current value of the item...how much credit u have will be shown on this web site.

Notes:

This is NOT intended as a 'freebie' area for beggers or scavengers but a way of enhancing Dragonsaber Army, allowing us to support our brethren. As you travel and accumulate items you may pick up things you cannot use, think about donating them! Nobody expects to find items worth thousands of pp in here, but in time, as we grow and raid bigger and better places who knows what u'll be able to find!

**No Items will be given to players below level 15. (even alts)**

**Casters requiring research components for spells are to be charged a “cost” of 5pp **
