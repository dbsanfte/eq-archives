Xpac Heaven
Posted Monday, March 6, 2000 - 10:53
by OgreGT

Last week two major expansion packs were announced. One being the Age of Empires II: The Conquerers and the other being Pharaoh Expansion: Cleopatra. First let's start with some new features that the Pharaoh expansion will add to the current title.

Pharaoh Expansion: Cleopatra

1. A new campaign centered on the Queen of Kings and other notable rulers from the New Kingdom and later periods, including Ramses II and Tutankhamen.

2. New buildings added to the game.

3. You'll be able to build monuments such as the Colossi of Ramses II, the temple of Amon-Re at Karnak, the exquisite tombs in the Valley of the Kings and the library and lighthouse at Alexandria.

4. Lamp Makers, Henna Farms, Tomb Artisans and Painters provide new services to your city and its residents.

You can read more about the Pharaoh expansion pack at the Impression's Games website. That is just in case you are like me and can't wait for this xpac. Now let's talk about the Conquerors.

Age of Empires II Expansion: The Conquerors

1. Five new races to be added.

2. 4 new campaigns.

3. Brand spanking new map types, such as Oasis and many more.

4. New multiplayer game types such as capture the flag.

5. A tweaked interface.

6. Farm queues.

Now if this is not enough to excite you, I don't know what is! To find out more about the Conquerors, why not try one of the many fan sites and official sites listed below:

AoE2.com
Ogre's AoK Network
Ager's HQ
Planet AoE
Age of Kings Heaven
Ensemble Studios
Official MS Site

Expansion packs are great in my opinion, but some argue they are not worth the cash for what you get. In some cases that is true, but I don't think it is with Pharaoh and Age of Empires II.
