New HQ Site Launched
Posted Thursday, January 6, 2000 - 14:57
by GG-News

Another project has come to fruition! Allegiance HQ is the upcoming online-only space combat game from the MSN Gaming Zone that offers ship-to-ship combat combined with the strategy and management needed in real-time strategy games.

From small, agile scouts to massive destroyers, you not only fly and fight but also man the turrets as you strive to destroy enemy forces. You can command your entire squad as a Commander and give orders to your team to help them to victory. You can also grab the reigns of Investor and decide the technology route your team will develop in its reach across the stars. Head to Allegiance HQ for more info and cool pics!
