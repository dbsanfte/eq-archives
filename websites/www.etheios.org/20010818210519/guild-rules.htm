Etheios Guild Rules

Attention: When you join this guild it will be assumed that you have read and agree to follow the rules below. If you have any questions about any of the following, please speak to a guild officer.

Guild Charter:

Infinite, Soulbound of Norrath, The Regulators of Norrath and Diamond Circle. We have all taken separate journeys to meet here as one. We join together with a common purpose. To provide friendship, fun and opportunity for all our members. We uphold the values of Honor, Loyalty, Faith and Trust. We Honor each other and others outside our ranks. We are Loyal to our guild and each other. We have Faith in each other to know that there will always be a friend to help us on our way. And finally we Trust that our guild will always work to make us each stronger and better players.
New Member Requirements:

All standing members of the incoming four guilds are automatically allowed membership to this one. (this line can be removed after a period of transition). New members must complete an application for membership. They must have references from current members. They must read our rules and agree to them. And they must register on the forum so we know they are aware of it's existence. Admittance to the guild will be voted on by council after review of the application. Real life relatives and friends of current members will not be required to complete an application. They will however be required to read and agree to the rules and register on the forum. New members will be placed on a probationary period and thier status will be reviewed at the end of that period.

If a member who has passed the probationary period wishes to have alternate characters in the guild, they must request an invite for those characters by posting in the appropriate forums, listing the alternates they wish to have guilded.

Note: In reference to the rule about language. This is verbatim from Verant's Code of Conduct. Please read this carefully "sexually explicit, harmful, threatening, abusive, defamatory, obscene, hateful, racially or ethinically offensive". In other words "hurtful to others". We will not allow this in /say (in public areas), /ooc, /guildsay, /shout or /auction. What you say to your group or in private tells is up to you. What you say to the rest of the server reflects upon this guild. If your language is such that complaints are made against you, you will be warned by an officer. Outbursts of frustration, or innuendo in /guildsay are allowed up to a point. What that point is, is a personal meter of each player in this guild. Everyone has the right to say something if they are offended by what someone else has said, as long as it is handled through the proper channels and not used to attack a fellow guildmate. The officers of this guild will not be the "language monitors" of /guildsay. Only extreme cases and those that foster complaint will be "policed". Some topics of conversation that may be acceptable to the "late nighters" may not be acceptable to those that play at other times. Please handle any requests to curb your language or change your topics with respect and courtesy.

2. The use of anonymous status is discouraged unless on a guild raid. Normal status is the preferred status so the guild is aware where the members are located. This is to encourage grouping and assisting each other within the Guild. If you must hide your class and level, do so with /roleplay so you are still visible to the guild.

3. The selling/buying of characters or items on Ebay or any other "online auction" venue will not be allowed.

4. You will abide by council / guild leader decisions. You will also abide by the Raid Advisor decisions during raids. If you feel that a decision was made that was in error or unfair against you, you have the right to request arbitration from the council on that issue. For further rules in regards to raids, see the Raid Rules.

5. You will bring to the attention any infractions of these rules to the council and/or guild leader. We will not risk the disbanding of the guild over one player's actions.

6. Inter-guild disagreements among players will not be aired on the /guildchat channel. If you have a problem with another guildmate, you have the right to request the guild leader and/or council members to arbitrate for you.
Disciplinary Actions:

We realize that everyone makes mistakes. All infractions against the rules will be reviewed by the leadership to decide action. In some cases, a simple apology will suffice. In extreme cases, a member's actions may result in immediate removal from the guild. In the instance where a member has been warned more than once about breaking the rules, a vote will be held to decide if that player will be allowed to remain in the guild. Ignorance of the rules of this guild will NOT be used as a reason to excuse actions that are contrary to what this guild is all about. Disciplinary action can include private or public warnings, temporary removal from the guild (suspension), or complete removal from the guild. In the event that a member's actions have resulted in suspension or removal, the reasons for such a decision will be made public by the council. Disciplinary action may be appealed by posting on the members board for Council review.
Guild Leadership Structure:

The Council will be comprised of 4 senior officers (one of which will wear the guild leader tag in game) and 12 advisors (3 officers from each of the 4 founding guilds). Additionally , there will be 5 Raid Leader Advisors. They may be members of the council and/or volunteers from the general membership population. In the event that any one of the advisors or senior officers decides to step down, the open position will be filled after a vote has been held.
Guild Leader (Senior Officer) Responsibilties:

1. The Guild Leader cannot, under any circumstances, disband the guild. If the current guild leader wishes to step down, the leadership will be handed to one of the other 3 original senior officers.

2. The Guild Leader(s) will be available to the council and will participate in all discussions concerning the running of the guild.

3. The Guild Leader(s) will have the power to remove any member they feel is not in keeping with the spirit of the guild or that has broken the rules. Such an action will be answerable to the other leadership members.

4. Any Guild Leader who is deemed by the other leadership members to be on a "power trip" and not working in the best interests of the guild may be voted out of office.
Council (Advisor) Responsibilities:

1. The Council members will be responsible for handling the day to day running of the guild.

2. The Council members will make themselves available to all members for arbitrating disputes, answering questions and making on the spot decisions as they arise.

3. The Council members will participate in all discussions concering the running of the guild.

4. The Council members will be responsible for reviewing new member applications and will vote on each new member.

5. The Council members will have the power, in extreme cases, to remove a member from the guild. Such an action will be reviewed by other leadership members. If it is found that a council member has abused this power, they will be removed from the guild themselves.

6. Council members can be voted out of office by a majority of the rest of the leadership or by a decision made by the 4 senior officers.
