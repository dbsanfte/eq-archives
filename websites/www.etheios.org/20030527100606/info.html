About Win2000Hoster.com

history and objectives :
- Win2000Hoster.com has been one of the world's leaders in low-cost and high quality web hosting industry for years.
- Win2000Hoster.com has been established on Feb 20 1999 and since March 2002 it has become a new part of the huge World Class Servers Inc., The world's number one low cost Internet Presence solution provider for individuals and small business.
- Win2000Hoster.com introduces a variety of integrated solutions for web hosting, domain registration and also some e-commerce applications.
- Win2000Hoster.com is currently running a large number of servers for shared hosting provided in highly competiteve low prices to more than 4,000 webmasters, web designers and resellers in 72 countries worldwide.
- Win2000Hoster.com is targeting to exceed the number of 10,000 customers and to reach at least 100 countries by the end of the year 2003.
- Currently Win2000Hoster.com is running two groups of servers, Win2000 based servers in Globix data centre located in NYC and RedHat Linux based servers in VDI data centre located in Jersey City and the two sets of servers are being operated and monitored by 2 major companies in the field of Internet and Telecommunications.
