    The Everquest Fashion Tables

    There are many who feel it is better to look good than to feel good, and put as much stock in how certain armor looks on them as how well it protects.  Below are models demonstrating the various armor and robe fashions and styles.  If you have a full or nearly full set of armor or a robe for a race-gender-armor type not yet listed and want to model it for the community, take a screen shot, convert it to jpg or gif format in a small a file as possible (under 100K preferred) [please do not send bitmaps], and send it to Allakhazam@allakhazam.com. Put in the subject line the race, gender and armor type you are sending in.

    If you submitted a picture that wasn't used, it is probably due to one of the following reasons:

    1)  You submitted a bitmap, or a image that was too large (over 100k or so).
    2)  You submitted an illusion race that normally can't wear that type of armor.
    3)  The picture was not a very good quality to show off the armor (Many pictures that are sent in are too dark -- Try to take the screen shot in the day time).
    4)  There was already an image for the race/sex combination you sent in.
    5)  It didn't get to me or I somehow deleted it by accident.
