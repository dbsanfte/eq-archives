    The Timorous Deep
    Level Range:  20 - 45
    Resources:
    • Timorous Deep Bestiary List
    • Timorous Deep Quest List
    • Timorous Deep Strategy Discussion
    Description:   The boat passes through here on the way to Kunark, and you actually have to switch boats in the middle of the zone.  Much like the other oceans in the game, there are several islands which can be hunted.  The main island has a tribe of spirok aviaks on it, which are basically a higher level version of the aviaks found in Southern Karana.  In addition, there is a shipwrecked raft, with a number of bandits on it.  There is also an island full of raptors and an island with a tribe of primitive Iksar called Jasar.  In addition, there is an island with a chessboard and several NPC's on it, which seems to be for quests.  There also seem to be a number of partially sunken islands that may hold treasure.  There also seems to be some things to find underwater.  First, there is the firepot area (see the quest section).  Second, at -11533, -3744 there is a tunnel leading into a massive stone oval area. Inside is another smaller oval  with another underwater tunnel leading inside at -11336, -1381.  Inside here you will find a beautiful oasis with palm trees, a small pond, a suspension bridge, an Inn of the same size as in East Commons and a beautiful view.  However, the area seems to be unpopulated at the time.  There are likely other areas in this zone yet undiscovered.  Just a warning to be careful when swimming under water.  I have been told at least one sea serpent/dragon has been spotted.
