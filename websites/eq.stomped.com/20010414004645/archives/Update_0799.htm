    July, 1999

    July 30, 1999:  Thanks to the many people who sent in information on the new poison skill.  I think I have a pretty comprehensive guide up now, along with some actual recipes and the pottery vial recipes.  Information keeps trickling in on how the last update affected the quests.  Some have definitely had their faction changed (Ale for Beur will not help you get faction with Freeport Militia anymore).  However, I have also been told that the amount of experience you get for many quests has increased.  I might go out this weekend and experiment a little.  I'm also starting to get some item descriptions sent in for items from the plane of fear.  Wow.  From what I can see, each class has a set of mega armor they can get there.  If you are brave enough to risk going there, it seems well worth the trip.  I have completely updated the Oasis zone description.  I'm going to try to go through the zones over the next couple of weeks and put in better descriptions.

    July 29, 1999:  Verant sure was busy on Tuesday.  More things seemed to have changed than were in the official announcement.  Several people have told me that faction adjustments for several quests have been eliminated or changed, so be wary of the quest faction chart until I get updates.  From the descriptions I was sent, I'm guessing that they got rid of the easy faction raising quests, like the Ale for Beur quest.  If this is true, it makes most quests even more worthless than before and is yet one more blow for doing quests as opposed to just killing things.  Also, I have been told that numerous necromancers lost many of the the research words they had stocked, forcing them to search all over again for these rare items.  Let's hope that gets corrected.

    July 28, 1999:  Well Verant's attempt to stress test their servers yesterday certainly stress tested gamestats.  This site had over 23,000 visitors!  I'm happy to say that there was no noticable slow down with that heavy use.  This is a good update.  Several nice new quests have been added, including what I believe is the start of the wizard's ice comet quest (in Everfrost), the new research quest in N Karana, a lead on the tome of ages quest, and a couple of weapon quests.  Thanks to those who pointed out the the Eye of Stormhammer quest does work.  It must be a faction thing.  Also, the Cure for Lempick quest in W. Karana seems like a relatively easy way for a lower level paladin or cleric to get a decent weapon, assuming it works as I was told.  There is also some limited information on the new poison skill for thieves.

    Remember, there is no way I can learn all of this stuff on my own.  I rely upon the visitors here to submit new information and to correct the information which was wrong, so don't be afraid to point out an error or fill in a missing detail.  I have posted everything I know or have been told, so if you know some additional information, especially about a quest or item or skill recipe, that you are willing to share with the visitors to this site, please e-mail me the informaiton.  Conversely, if you have a question about a quest or item that is not listed on this site, chances are I won't be able to answer your question (though I will try).
