Call Of Flames

Requirements: Must be level 50

  • Find Lily and Devin Ashwood in Kelethin
  • Hail Lily
  • Say “I am a Ranger” to Lily
  • Say “I want to learn from him, how do I prove myself as a forest? (note, not forrester)” to Devin
  • Say “I will take letter ” to Lily
  • Give letter to Ganelorn in east Karana
  • Get snake egg from Ganelorn take to Ranger Master (Kithicor) in Kithicor Forest
  • Get icon from Kithicor and return it to Ganelorn in East Karana
  • Ganelorn requires 3 things from his student and then deliver “a note of Credit” to a merchant.
    • Burnt sash = Dropped by imp protectors in Solusek B
    • Adamantite band = adamantite band is the rare drop from the hand in lower guk
    • Electrum bladed Wakizashi
      • Butler fang = dropped by Butler Senclair in Mistmoore
      • Maid fang = dropped by Maid Issis in Mistmoore
      • Turn in fangs to the Avenging Caitiff in Mistmoore for Electrum Bladed wakizashi.
  • Turn in “note of credit” to Aanina Rockfinder in Kaladim B
  • Turn in all 4 items to Ganelorn in east Karana
  • This will spawn a high level poacher nearby named Vance Bearstalker
  • Kill Vance and take his head
  • Give head to Ganlorn for spell
