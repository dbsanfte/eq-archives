Attributes (stats)
back
Attribute

Effect on Shaman
Strength Your strength is you weight allowance, also, the higher your strength the better the chance you will strike for maximum damage .
Dexterity Increases the ability to hit, and affects you channeling.
Agility Helps dodging and improves AC.
Charisma Helps overcome resistance when "effect" style spells such as debuffs or charms are used on a critter. Also improves faction with NPCs and helps improve prices with vendors.
Stamina Gives a few extra HP per level. Also allows you to fight longer and swim longer without getting tired.
Intelligence Improves the rate of advancing your skills.
Wisdom Determines mana store for priest classes, also affects trade skill failure rate.

Spell Types

Skill Name Level

Analysis
Skill capacity: (lvl x 5) + 5
Abjuration 1 Resistance, AC, and quickness buff spells. Also see the spell list
Alteration 1 Healing and many buff/debuff spells. Also see the spell list
Conjuration 1 Summoning spells and death over time spells. Also see the spell list
Divination 1 Vision spells. Also see the spell list
Evocation 1 Direct damage spells. Also see the spell list
Channeling 4 Increases chances not to be disrupted while casting, even when hit. Eventually you can even run while casting certain spells. You must put 1 practice point into this before it will start going up on its own (Skill cap is 200).
Meditate 8 Allows you to regain mana faster, and increase the speed of memorizing and scribing. You must put 1 practice point into this before it will start going up on its own.
Specialization 30 Allows you to specialize in 1 type of magic which means less mana usage and faster casting spells. Note: Your stat for this skill starts at 30 with your first practice. Be careful when you put practices in this so that you don't hit 51 with a spell type you don't want to be your primary.
