The Quest for Blood of Nadox

This is the quest for the level 52 shaman spell Blood of Nadox. The spell is a cure for both poison and disease. Completing this quest at level 44 will definitely require either some high level help or a very, very strong group. However, the exp is good into the high 50s and even low 60s, and there can be other nice drops, so you have tools to entice some help. You must have the Legacy of Ykesha expansion in order to complete this quest.

The quest is given by Grennik Neltrin at +1600 -60 in the Gulf of Gunthak. She sends you off to gather Gunthak Gulf Water, A blackened vial, Broken Skull clan blood, and Pure Blood. You combine the first three items together in a brew barrel, and then combine the result of that with the Pure Blood to make A glowing black potion. Neither of the two combines will fail. Hail Grennik, follow her conversation until it ends, and then give her the glowing black potion. She will hand back your Spell: Blood of Nadox.

All four of the drops are Magic and No Drop, but are not Lore. Due to the quest design, none of it can be multiquested.

More Drop Details:

Gunthak Gulf Water: Drops off of any of the pirates in the harbor or the fort in the Gulf of Gunthak. (Iksar can also forage it.) It is a rather uncommon drop and may require several hours of camping. To get to the harbor camp from the Gulf of Gunthak lighthouse, put up sow, invis, and levitate and run due south to the beach. Once at the beach, hug the right wall and run back to the tunnel. From there, follow the right wall until you reach the harbor. To try the fort, follow the left wall instead of the right at the tunnel from the beach. Any of the pirates at either camp could drop the water. The levels vary somewhat but should be mostly soloable, if tough, at 44. They do run when at low health, so if you are unable to snare be sure to choose a pull spot where you have some room to manuever. You will have a better chance of getting single pulls if you use nondamaging spells to pull (slow is recommended) and pull those that are closest to you first, so that they do not run directly past other mobs.

A blackened vial: Drops off of Broken Skull adherents or initiates in the Crypt of Nadox, as well as the few named that share camps with adherents and initiates. To get to Nadox from Gunthak, cross the open area south of the harbor and follow the passage further south to the castle. Enter the castle and run all the way straight back into the furthest room. From there, follow the passage in the wall on the right (west) to zone into Nadox. The adherents are much tougher than the pirates and can be difficult to solo even well into your 50s. Resists are problematic even at 61, so resist debuffing will be very helpful. The adherents are shamans, so you will also want to have your own resists up. They also gate and have sow in spite of being in an indoor zone, so when they run at low health (and resist roots) it can be very problematic indeed. Snare would be a big help here. If you are attempting these at level 44, some high level help would definitely be useful. Otherwise, bring a group and start killing at the zone to get a feel for how well you can handle things. The adherents are scattered throughout much of the zone, particularly the areas around the zone from Gunthak, so you will not be wasting your time. One camp further in that you may also wish to try, as described by Islowem on the message boards: "From zone in ... go left. Go up the slope, and past the first room off to the left. Stop at the hall to the right. I set up camp here. There are two guard roamers, and one sycophant ... roamer. Kill those three. Then looking on down the hall, you see three mobs. Two guarding a door, and one roaming back and forth. The two guarding the door can drop the vial. Either can spawn as a named also...."

Broken Skull clan blood: Drops off of furious or vehement blackhands in Nadox. These may be a little lower level than the adherents but still hit very hard. They are also scattered throughout portions of the zone, including one at the camp described for the adherents. From the adherent camp, you can also reach another spot with more blackhands as follows, again described by Islowem: "Invis back up, and go down to the room where you [were] pulling the two door guards. Go thru that room. Just past the next hall to the right, and before the next room, [stop] and set up camp again. Two roamers at most (both sycophants), and the room directly ahead is full of furious and vehement."

Pure Blood: Drops off of luggalds in Nadox. It definitely can come from luggald initiates and abominations but might also come from other luggalds as well. These are every bit as difficult as the adherents but harder to reach. Higher level help is strongly recommended. Some luggalds, but not all, are undead, so neither form of invisibility will keep you entirely safe. A suggested camp spot for them, again from Islowem: "From the last camp spot ... continue thru that room, you will enter a cavern. To the left is a bridge. Go across the bridge. You will see three halls. ... I set up camp by going thru the middle door, straight thru the room full of luggald, and setting up camp in the middle of the next hall. No roamers here, so was a good spot."

For more information also see the thread by Islowem in the Crucible Spells Forum.
