Stat Based Potions

 

Potions Herbs
Vigor - This potion will subtract Stamina, and add to Agility and Strength.
  • Lucerne
  • Birthwart
Cohesion - This potion will subtract Agility, and add to Stamina and Dexterity.
  • Fenugreek
  • Sage Leaf
Power - This potions will subtract Stamina, and will add to Strength and Dexterity.
  • Fenugreek
  • Lucerne
Stability - This potions will subtract Agility, and will add to Strength and Stamina.
  • Sage Leaf
  • Lucerne
Adroitness - This potions will subtract Dexterity, and will add to Stamina and Agility.
  • Birthwart
  • Sage leaf
Accuracy - This potions will subtract Stamina, and will add to Agility and Dexterity.
  • Birthwart
  • Fenugreek
  1. Combine herbs as is for Lesser potions.
  2. Combine with Blue Vervain Bulb for Regular versions.
  3. Combine with Valerian Root for Greater versions.

It would appear that potions are not stackable with each other, however, each seems to be stackable with our regular buffs, and a few potions do seem to interact with each other provided stats do not overlap.    You must be standing still in order to drink the potion.
