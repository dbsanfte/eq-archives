Darkley Moon's Guide to Level 30

This was my choice but I wanted to be a Wood Elf Warrior. I know some of you are saying OMG your nuts.  I enjoy the Wood Elf class and it seemed to fit me best anyways.

Level 1 to 5

Of course I just stayed around town killing, bats, Wasps and  Orc Pawns and the various low level things that  spawned there.

Level 5 to 10

Once I hit level 5 i immedietly stocked up on food and moved to Butcher Block Mountains.  Here I fought  Skunks, and Spiders, and Large Snakes for the first few levels.  Making sure I conned everything before I attacked.  At level 7 I started killing the Dwarf Skeletons, and anything that conned blue to me.  At level 8 I then  moved to the Goblin camps and killed the 'a goblins'  and would continue to run around killing blue snakes and skeletons.  Once I got to 9 I could killing shamans and wizards I killed those and skeletons to level 10.

Level 10 to 14

 At level 10 I move to Steamfont Mountains.  Here I killed the Skeletons near the cog past the Mintour Camp.  They conned White and sometimes Yellow.  Killed those and rogue clockworks and harpies to about 11.  Then I started throwing in slavers and Earth Elementals. Did this for about 4 levels till I could get no more expierence.

Level 14 to 16

At Level 14 I move out to the Nybright Sister Camp in Lesser Faydark.  This place was great for experience and loot.  I killed the sisters for 1 level to 15 went fairly fast. Then I moved to the 'a bandit' camp not to far from the sister camp. These bandits were much hard but with the same nice loot.  I killed these to level 16 when exp
started to slow down.

Level 16 to 22 

From level 16 to 22 I hunted in Dagnors Cauldron. I hunted the Aqua Goblins.  There are 4 types of Aqua Goblins in this zone and they vary in difficulty.  I started with Aqua Goblin Mariners.  They are the easiest and good exp at level 16 and not to difficult.  Around 17 or
18 I moved on to Aqua Goblin Shamans. These ones are a bit more difficult and cast spells.  Level 19 I started to throw in Aqua Goblin Bosuns.  These guys are tougher and hit pretty hard, i fought these to about level 20.   Level 20 I started to try the ocassional Tidal Lord. 
These guys are really tough and hit extremely hard. for most of this hunting I was grouped with a druid the same level)

Level 22 to 30 

Level 22 I moved to Mistmoore, here I started grouping  with people my level all the way to level 30.  At around level 26/27 you can start to solo stuff pretty easy here in the courtyard.  Get some buffs and make sure you always use bind wound thru your previous levels you will need it.
