SLOWING AT HIGH LEVELS.
 A guide to mobs that can be slowed, with thanks to Nixx for the inspiration, Seredith for her years of work on the guide and all the shamans on the crucible for their contributions.

Last Update: September 10, 2003

When posting updates please only include personal first-hand confirmations of slowability.

  • FULL NAME OF MOB
  • NAME OF ZONE
  • SLOW SPELL USED (MAGIC - TURGURS or DISEASE - GRUMMUS)
  • IMMUNITY OR MITIGATION MESSAGE

If you are reporting a "contested" mob is slowable, screenshots are appreciated! It is assumed that magic/disease debuffs are cast - Shaman Malos line (or Insidious Decay), Enchanter Tash line and/or bard debuffs like Harmony/Occlusion of Sound. The confirmed by field contains the name of the last person whose information was used to update the entry.

KEY:
Magic Mob is slowable using magic based slow spells, such as Turgurs Insects, Forlorn Deeds, Sha's Revenge.
Disease Mob is slowable using disease based spells, such as Cloud of Grummus.
Immune Mob is immune to changes in attack speed and is reported as such by client.
Unslowable Mob gives no immune message but is so resistant to spells that it is, for all practical and reliable purposes, immune to slow.
Mitigates Mob can only be partially slowed.
** Possible to slow but very difficult, mob may cure/dispell it's own slow.
*** Possible to slow with turgurs but easier to slow with disease-based slow.
% First floor Elites are slowable, other Elites on other floors are immune.
^^ Denotes a zone that has been revamped, use slowability information with caution.
