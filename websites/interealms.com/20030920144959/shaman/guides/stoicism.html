The Quest for Stoicism

The level 44 shaman spell Stoicism is only available by completing this quest. You must own the Legacy of Ykesha expansion in order to complete it. This is the lower level version of the fabled shaman spell Torpor, and definitely worth the effort, especially given the difficulty many shamans have in acquiring Torpor. You may need help for parts of the quest, but should certainly be able to handle all of it with a group of approximately that level. There are reports that folks in their low 50s can solo the entire quest. This guide is based entirely on the submissions made by folks on the message boards.

Important Note: EVERY time you give an item to the npc for this quest, you MUST hail it first. If you do not, the item may be eaten. Be sure to target the npc and stand close to them before hailing. If it replies to you with another trigger, be sure to follow the conversation all the way.

Quick Overview:

  1. Kill Drunken Pirates in Gunthak for an Empty Flask.
  2. Hail, then give Empty Flask to "an impassive sea spirit" in Gunthak, receive A Large Rock.
  3. Hail, then give A Large Rock back to sea spirit. Two elementals, Flotsam and Jetsam, spawn.
  4. Kill the elementals and loot the Small Piece of Flotsam and Small Piece of Jetsam from each of them.
  5. Hail, then give both looted Parts together to the sea spirit, receive Spell: Stoicism.

The Details:

All of the items involved for this quest are No Drop. There is at least one report that it has been successfully multiquested.

Drunken Pirates are found in the harbor area of the Gulf of Gunthak or Dulak's Harbor. To get there, from the lighthouse in Gunthak put up sow, levitate, and invis, and run due south until you reach the beach. Hug the right wall of the beach and as long as your invis holds you should make it to the back safely. Find the cave at the rear, right corner of the beach, check invis, and follow the right wall to the harbor area. If you are not evil with sufficient faction, you can safely explore this area with the use of Invisibility, as long as it holds. The southwest corner of the town area is a good camp to try. The zone is very close from there, affording a quick escape route. Setting up just a bit inside the path to the zone will also let you avoid the wandering guards. Just be sure to watch who else is around so you can be prepared for trains.

If you want to try Dulak's Harbor, head down the tunnel from that SW camp in Gunthak to zone into Dulak. From there, a safe spot can be reached if you turn right along the wall. There is a row of small buildings with a large building alongside them. Outside of that large building opposite the second to last small building is a small pile of crates or barrels. If you stand a couple steps to the side of those barrels (with them between you and the building), you will be safe from wanderers and will not agro anything from inside the building.

The Empty Flask might drop for you right away or might take some intensive camping. Do not confuse it with the Broken Bottle, which is a completely different item. Reports indicate that this is a challenging but doable solo at 44+. They hit hard so slowing is a major priority. You may be able to get single pulls if you pull with slow and not with damaging spells, and if you pull the closest first. You will only get two if they are standing very close together. You may wish to use invis to find and target the pirates and run back to your camp spot before casting. That does, however, prevent you from keeping a pet up.

The impassive sea spirit is at 700, -300, -150 in the Gulf of Gunthak, underwater in a sunken boat about halfway between the lighthouse and the shore. Be sure to put up some form of underwater breathing before going down. The spirit is very small, so be sure to look carefully. The Large Rock is giant and very heavy, so do not do this step unless you have bags that can accomodate Giant items or an open inventory space. Flotsam and Jetsam are not agro and easily single pulled. Be prepared to root as they may run at low health. They in the high 30s or low 40s and not especially resistant to slow or dots. Reports are that they hit in the 60s or so.

Hail the spirit and hand over the Small Piece of Flotsam and Small Piece of Jetsam, and receive your shiny new spell!
