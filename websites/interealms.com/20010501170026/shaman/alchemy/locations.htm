Alchemy Vendor Locations

 

Grobb
Grabah - Located across from and a little left of the shaman guild, near Carver Caggek
     In Grobb you will find the Herbs to create both the Stat and Aura based potions.
Paineel
Yvonne - Located in the Poison Petal (upstairs shop) in the Erudite Necromancer City.
     Yvonne sells both the new Alchemy guide book, and the herbs to make the utility potions.  She may also be part of a quest.
Halas
Dargon -  Located on the right just outside of the shaman guild
     In Halas you will find the Herbs to create both the Stat and Aura based potions.  There is also a new NPC located in the Pottery shop who is Alchemy related, though it would appear to be a necromancer quest.
Oggok
Garakbar - Located in the room with two vendors and the table of steaming skulls.
     In Oggok you will find the Herbs necessary to create the utility potions.
Kerra Ridge
Melixis - Near the Temple.  Must have decent faction with Kerran to buy.
     In Kerra Ridge you will find the Herbs to create both the Stat and Aura based potions.  The vendor is also part of the new Paw of Oppolla quest.

 
Fironia Vie
Marlyn McMerin - Located on the docks near the barbarian spell vendor.
     In Fironia Vie you will find the Herbs to create both the Stat and Aura based potions.

 
Cabilis
              -  Located in West Cabilis in a tent in the middle of the Canal System
     In Cabilis you will find the Herbs to create both the Stat and Aura based potions.
