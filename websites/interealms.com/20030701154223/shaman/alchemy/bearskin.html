Shaman Bearskin Potion Bag

A tailored container allowing shamans to combine like-potions in quantities of 5 or 10 into a single, multi-charge potion.

Tailoring Recipe:

1) Combine 2 Spiderling Silks in a Sewing Kit to produce a Silk Thread.

2) Repeat step one until you have 3 Silk Threads.

3) Combine 3 Silk Threads in a Sewing Kit to produce a Silk Cord.

4) Combine 1 Silk Cord and 1 Grizzly Bear SKIN in a Sewing Kit to produce a Bearskin Potion Bag.

If you do not have, or do not wish to learn, tailoring skill, ask a local tailor to make the item for you. It becomes trivial at 80-90 skill for tailors.

How to Use the Bearskin Potion Bag:

Note: The Bearskin Potion Bag will only combine identical potions into multi-dose potions in sets of 5 or 10. Using dissimilar potions or numbers of potions other than 5 or 10 will result in losing all potions in the bag.

1) Make 5 or 10 of the same kind of potion

2) Place 5 or 10 of those potions you just made in the Bearskin Bag

3) Hit Combine

There is no skill check for combining potions in the Bearskin Bag, so as long as you use like potions in quantities of 5 or 10, you should never fail.

How to Use Multi-Charge Potions:

Just right-click to use the potion like you normally would. But now, instead of the potion disappearing, it will have one less charge to it. When you use the final charge, the potion will disappear.

Note: You cannot combine herbs in the Bearskin Bag, only potions. You will therefore need to keep both your Medicine Bag (to make potions) and your new Bearskin Potion Bag (to combine potions into the 5 or 10 charge varieties).
