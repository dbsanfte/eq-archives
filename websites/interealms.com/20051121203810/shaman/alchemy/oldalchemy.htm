Trade Skill Information
 
Component placement...

Alchemy is a trade skill available only to Shaman who have reached the 25th Circle of Life.  

To practice Alchemy, each Shaman will need to obtain him or herself a Medicine Bag (usually purchasable either at the herb vendor or nearby).

All herbs and items related to the potion being made need to be put in the Medicine Bag (herb order notwithstanding), and the Shaman must then click Combine.

Alchemy is practicable in a fashion very similar to caster research, that is, initial points may be put into alchemy upon reaching 25th level, however, points may only be allotted at a rate of 5 per level of the Shaman over 25, however trainable points will cap shortly after.

Potions will operate on the user as if they were spells cast by a shaman of the level of the imbiber.

Stat and Aura potions will operate as follows depending on ingredients:

  • Lesser Stat (+5 , +5, -5)

  • Stat (+10, +10, -10)

  • Greater Stat (+25, +15, -20)

 

  • Minor Aura (+5, +5, -3)

  • Aura (+15, +15, -8)

  • Greater Aura (+25, +25, -15)
