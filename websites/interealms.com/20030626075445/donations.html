Keepers of the Realms...


This is a special tribute page to everyone that has helped keep the Glade and all of our community sites going. These Shaman, Rangers and Shadowknights contributed either monetarily, or through overwhelming support to keep this site alive. I'm sorry if I missed someone (will update if anymore donations come in,) or couldn't reply to your donation personally, but the support was simply staggering! Almost 200 people ended up contributing the first time and even more the second! I wish I could simply thank each of you individually, but as many of you that donated, I can't.

I can't thank you all enough...
