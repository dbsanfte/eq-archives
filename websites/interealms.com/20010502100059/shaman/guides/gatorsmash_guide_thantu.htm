Shaman Quests



     Hope this helps all those interested in getting themselves a maul.  Our preferred method is to have an agnostic wizard with a deceiver mask run the quest (good luck recreating that one).  In lieu of that, let me state that Dark Elves seem to always be able to do the quest.  Ogre warriors and some shaman (note you will take Greenblood hits), and Trolls who worship Cazic Thule (not Innoruuk) can run the quest.  Barbarians will not be able to do the quest without serious faction and wolf form (NOTE: Invis will drop when you turn in the hearts, and due to the Kunark expansion, wolf form will now make you KoS to the guards).


Hoakay, let's get down to details:

  1. Go to crushbone, blast the crap out of D'Vinn and get his heart (easy enough). Please be sure that the room is empty or if it is camped offer them a dragoon dirk to loot his heart (you'll get another, no loss here). Ogres and Trolls will have to have someone pull Dvinn from the tower or levitate to it (as Verant seems to not like fat people, crackmonkies).  I have also been told that it is possible for large races to wiggle into the left side of the throne.

  2. Catch a ride or foot it to Oggok (pretty safe these days, guards are camped 24/7).

  3. GET A CRUDE STEIN ( so easy to do, guard killers leave them all over the place). This step is crucial, you must have the stein in hand to do the quest.

  4. The third bottom hole of five (the one on the right) leads through the shaman guild, stick right until you enter a room with maybe 6 hallways leading off from it. You want to go down the one in the middle, should be 3rd from the right I think (there will be a bed in the room).

  5. If you happened upon the room at nighttime, have a sit big guy (start sweating if you are invis, heh). At 8am, the Bouncer in the room should unspawn, and at 10 am (like 5 min later), Ambassador K'Rynn should spawn.

  6. Nuke K'Rynn (do not let him run!). He has a heart and a Goon Dirk every time.  I would say level 29 should be sufficient to take him, though help never hurts.

  7. Put your crude stein in hand and head to the warrior guild. Give both hearts to Lork (we started as dubious and moved to amiable, hehe, little bit of xp too, which makes up for all the time spent getting those mauls, rofl).

  8. Admire new Gatorsmash Maul (worked every single time for god knows how many times now, this would be a good time to run like heck you invis people).

Hope this helps you all get your own maul!  Good luck and Godspeed brothers and sisters.
