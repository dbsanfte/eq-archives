Attributes (stats)
Attribute

Effect

Strength Strength directly determines how much you can carry. It also influences maximum and average damage your weapons will do. Also  influences how quickly you learn many offensive skills
Dexterity Dexterity affects how quickly you will learn weapon skills, also how often your weapons are going to proc.  So the higher the dex the more often your weapon is going to proc.  Dex also affects how quickly rogues will learn some of their skills.  Also Dexterity has a factor on how hard you can hit with your Bows.
Agility Affects how quickly you can learn some defensive skills, how difficult it is to hit you as well as how much damage you take when you are hit.
Charisma Affects amount you will be paid for goods by NPC merchants, and how much they will pay you.  Affects the saving throw on certain bard and enchanter spells (charms in particular).
Stamina Affects how many hit points you have, and how long you can hold your breath.
Intelligence Directly affects how quickly you can learn most skills.  Intelligence is also the base for  the amount of mana for bards and all mages, as well as shadow knights.
Wisdom Wisdom is the base for the amount of mana the priest classes have, also rangers. It also has an affect on how quickly you can learn many skills if your wisdom is higher than your INT.
