GENERAL ABILITIES

These abilities are unlocked for training at level 51.
Spending 6 points in this category will earn you the title Baron/Baroness
All General Abilities except New Tanaan Crafting Mastery cost 1 point per level to train.
NAME COST LEVELS DESCRIPTION/PRE-REQUISITES
Innate Strength 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of strength per rank.
Innate Stamina 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of stamina per rank. 
Innate Agility 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of agility per rank. 
Innate Dexterity 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of dexterity per rank. 
Innate Intelligence 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of intelligence per rank. 
Innate Wisdom 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of wisdom per rank. 
Innate Charisma 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of charisma per rank. 
Innate Fire Protection 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of fire resistance per rank. 
Innate Cold Protection 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of cold resistance per rank. 
Innate Magic Protection 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of magic resistance per rank. 
Innate Poison Protection 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of poison resistance per rank.
Innate Disease Protection 1 point 5 This ability grants an additional 2 points of disease resistance per rank. 
Innate Run Speed 1 point 3 This ability grants permanent increased foot speed. The speed increase is similar to the speed of jboots at level 3.
Innate Metabolism 1 point 3 This ability grants a dramatic decrease in the amount of food and drink you consume. You will consume 10% less at rank 1, 25% less at rank 2 and 50% less at rank 3.
Innate Regeneration 1 point 3 This ability increases your base regeneration rate by 1 hp, per tick for each rank.
Innate Lung Capacity 1 point 3 This ability increases the amount of time you can hold your breath.
First Aid 1 point 3 This skill grants an increase in the maximum you may use bandages to heal a player. You may heal an additional 10% per rank.
New Tanaan Crafting Mastery 3 points 6 This skill allows a player to exceed the 200 skill cap in multiple trade skills. This cap does not apply to Fishing, Alchemy, Research, Poison Making or Tinkering.


ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

These abilities are unlocked after achieving level 55 and spending 6 points in General Abilities.
You must spend 12 points in this category to access the Class Abilities.
After spending 12 points, shamans (as well as druids, clerics and beastlords) earn the title Venerable.
Multi-step archetype abilities generally cost 2 points for the first level, 4 points for the second level and 6 points for the third level.
NAME COST LEVELS DESCRIPTION/PRE-REQUISITES
Channeling Focus Level 1: 2
Level 2: 4
Level 3: 6 		3 This AA increases the chances a player has to channel a spell and recover from movement or damage taken while casting, by 5% per rank.
Mental Clarity
(MC) 		Level 1: 2
Level 2: 4
Level 3: 6 		3 This ability increases your base mana regen rate by 1 point per tick. Mental Clarity does not count toward the Flowing Thought cap.

This is the pre-requisite for the shamans most important AA, Cannibalize. This should be the first AA you do in the Archetype Category, all 3 levels. 
Spell Casting Fury
(SCF) 		Level 1: 2
Level 2: 4
Level 3: 6 		3 Increases the chance of landing a critical hit with a direct damage spell. This spell works on regular nukes, such as Velium Strike and Spear of Torment as well as our rains, Tears of Saryrn and Torrent of Poison. Critical nukes at level 1 will be 133% of normal, level 2 gives 166% of normal and level 3 will double the base damage of the hit and then add any increase from damage enhancing focus effects on top.

This AA does not effect our heal over time spells, Torpor and Quiescence. This AA stacks with all heal enhancing focus effects.
Healing Gift
(HG) 		Level 1: 2
Level 2: 4

This AA works on our direct non-percentage heal, Tnarg's Mending. Our other heals, Kragg's Mending, Torpor and Quiescence cannot critical.

CLASS ABILITIES

These abilities are unlocked after achieving level 59 and spending 12 points in Archetype Abilities.
After spending 24 points in these class abilities, shamans (as well as druids, and beastlords) earn the title Elder.
Multi-step class abilities generally cost 3 points for the first level, 6 points for the second level and 9 points for the third level.
NAME COST LEVELS DESCRIPTION/PRE-REQUISITES
Pet Discipline 6 points 1  This aa give a shaman access to the /pet hold command. This will prevent your pet from attacking unless given the /pet attack command. 
Mass Group Buff
(MGB) 		9 points 1  This AA will allow you to cast any group buff as an AE buff, hitting all player characters of the required level in your immediate vicinity.   For all spells over level 50 the player you are casting on must be level 46 or over. Group spells without an icon, like Blood of Nadox, Radiant Cure, and Tiny Terror cannot be MGB'd.

Recast: 72 minutes.
Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery
(SCRM) 		8 points 1  This AA will give your buffs an additional 20% increase to their duration, for a total of 50% increased duration. This AA will stack with all buff duration enhancing focus effects, with no cap.

Requires: Level 3 of Spell Casting Reinforcement (Archetype) 
Quick Buff
(QB) 		Level 1: 3
Level 2: 6
Level 3: 9 		3  This AA will decrease the cast time on your buff spells, including torpor and quiescence. Level 1 provides 10% reduction in cast time, level 2 is 25% and level 3 is a 50% reduction in cast time. This AA will stack with all buffing haste and spell haste focus effects and buffs, with a cap of 50% cast time reduction. 
Alchemy Mastery Level 1: 3
Level 2: 6
Level 3: 9		 3  Reduces the chance of failure when making potions by 10% at rank 1, 25% at rank 2 and 50% at rank 3. 
Cannibalization
(Canni5)		 5 points  1  This allows a shaman to convert 1924 hitpoints into 1066 mana. This is the most important shaman AA and the path to this skill should be your priority when pursuing AA.

Recast: 3 minutes.
Requires: Level 3 of Mental Clarity (Archetype) 
Rabid Bear 5 points  1 This AA bestows the buff Rabid Bear. It give a shaman bear illusion, increases STR and DEX by 100, ATK by 200, resist cold by 50 and gives the shaman maximum haste. It also gives shaman the innate proc Tainted Bite. It lasts for 5 mins and can be increased with buff duration increasing AAs SCR/SCRM and buff extension focus.
