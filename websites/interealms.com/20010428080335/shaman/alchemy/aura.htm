Aura Giving Potions

 

Potions Herbs
Null - This potion will subtract Agility, and add to Dexterity and Disease Resistance.
  • Fenugreek
  • Mandrake Root
Cold - This potion will subtract Stamina, and add to Agility and Fire Resistance.
  • Birthwart
  • Allspice
Heat - This potions will subtract Strength, and will add to Stamina and Cold Resistance.
  • Sage Leaf
  • Benzoin
Antibody - This potions will subtract Dexterity, and will add to Charisma and Disease Resistance.
  • Maidenhair Fern
  • Mullein
Purity - This potions will subtract Charisma, and will add to Strength and Poison Resistance.
  • Lucerne
  • Nightshade
Rejuvenation - This potions will subtract Attack Speed and Strength, and adds to HP Regen
  • Sage
  • Yarrow
  • Lucerne
  1. Combine herbs as is for Lesser potions.
  2. Combine with Blue Vervain Bulb for Regular versions.
  3. Combine with Valerian Root for Greater versions.

It would appear that potions are not stackable with each other, however, each seems to be stackable with our regular buffs, and a few potions do seem to interact with each other provided stats do not overlap.
