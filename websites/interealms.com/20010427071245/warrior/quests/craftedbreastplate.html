1) Head to Mistmoore and kill a Gypsy Musician. You need to retreive a Werewolf Talon.

2) Buy 3 rubies.

3) Head to South Karana and visit the centaur village. Talk to Ulan to get the quest.

4) Give him the 3 rubies and the talon to receive the Crafted Breastplate.
 
** Make sure you keep the rubies not stacked **  
 
Crafted Breastplate
MAGIC
AC: 22     Weight: 9.9
+5 STA, +25 HP
Classes: WAR
Races   : ALL
