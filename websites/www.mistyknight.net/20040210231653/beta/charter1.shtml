Official Misty Knight Guild Charter

Article I. Guild Introduction
(a) The Misty Knight (“MK”) guild was formed to give “All Races” a family-like group of persons with whom they could go questing in the Everquest (“EQ”) world. MK is not a power-leveling or loot based guild. MK’s mission is to make the game more enjoyable and to assist in questing in a family-like atmosphere.

Member Etiquette

Article II. Member Conduct
Section 2.01 Playing in MK is solely for fun.
Members of MK are not allowed to engage in conduct towards guild members or others in the game that involves harassment or cruelty, swearing or use of foul language, use of racial slurs, or ridicule of anyone simply because they made a mistake.

Section 2.02 New members are recommended to be of 20th level.
Any one below this will need to get approved prior to entry. New members are required to register for the guild forum located in the member’s area of the website within a week upon joining the guild. Members will provide a working e-mail address at all times. Failure to abide bySection 2.02 will be basis for removal from the guild after 1st warning.

Section 2.03 Members will not use the /guild, /ooc, /shout or /auction, command for regular personal conversation.
Continued use of these communication formats in such a manner is improper and rude. Please refer to the EQ official website for information on the use of these different modes of communication. This doesn’t mean you can’t use the commands just use your best judgment on how and when to use it. (a) There will be a little Tolerance for Swearing in the guild. This includes /ooc, /shout, /guild, /say, any chat channel in which is a guild formed channel, /group, Any other guild channel that you are in or /group. If you send a /tell and keep swearing at a guild member and I see a screenshot. Punishment is as follows: If you say the "F" word (You know which word) you are removed without warning. If you swear anything to any person and I get a /log or screenshot of it you may be removed without warning. If you swear anything other than the above you will be given two warnings for this and the third will be a removal. IF you continue to swear because you were given a warning you will be immediately removed. Any officer may give a warning. We are not out to censor every little word, we just want it kept to a minimum.

Section 2.04 Members will not beg other members to twink or power level their character at any time

(a) Members are encouraged to give buffs in the spirit of cooperation.
(b) Use of the anon is not allowed. Not needed in guild or group play. (1) Role-play mode is permitted if needed to avoid excessive tells for ports (etc.).
(c) There will be no required amount of playing time. You must contribute by maintaining the guild or by questing.
(d) “Maintaining the guild” means by management, questing or helping other members.
(e) All loot shall be given out from raids and quests on a need before greed basis. Need of an item between more than one member shall be determined via a /random roll.
