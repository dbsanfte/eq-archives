HoT

It’s time - I have put HoT on the Calendar and our first trip has been scheduled for the last Friday of June... the 27th. You can sign up here.

After months of waiting I have decided to just do it. We can spank Fay, and I am sure by the end of the month we will be able to deal with any of the Kunark Dragons out there - that means it's time for the Temple of Veeshan.

First thing to notice is the Recommended Level Range of 56 to 65. Remember: any guild member can come along, but those that are above level 56 will have the best chance of surviving the AoEs – so if you are close to the edge, try to do a little extra grinding over the next 30 days.

Resist Gear: Your best odds of surviving come with have each of your unbuffed resists above 100. If you lack gear - all you need is cash and Diamonds/Blue Diamonds and Alliki can hook you up... send Kode a tell anytime he is online for more details.

Faction: You need good CoV faction to even make your way in to HoT, you need Ally to benefit from the gear that drops there. If you are KOS you will be asked to die at the entrance so your corpse can be dragged to the ring safely. If this is a problem you need to be arranging groups in Kael to keep banging away at it.

As always meet at the nexus at 8:30 PM by Larren.



- Kode

  Love those Toolboxes!
Posted by: necrro
Posted on: Mon Jul 1 21:31:29 2002

Ok, you started a new character and you have no money and no bags. You are sick of running back and forth to sell items because you fill your slots so quickly. What to do? Can't afford a Backpack (6-7 gp) and you need spell or armor money. The Toolbox is your answer. These can be bought in most big cities (not sure about Kelethin) and have 8 slots that hold up to the largest items in the game. Additionally, they are no heavier than back packs and the best thing is they are super cheap. I still have some in the bank.
