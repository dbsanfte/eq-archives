  A need for Vah Shir
Posted by: Raythor
Posted on: Sat Sep 14 10:39:06 2002
Revised on:Sat Sep 14 10:40:35 2002 by Raythor
Revised on:Sat Sep 14 10:41:34 2002 by Raythor

I'd like to take a moment to thank the guild for making me an Officer. I've been a Misty Knight for a while now and have enjoyed meeting and playing with most of you. Those I haven't had the opportunity to meet yet, I hope to see you at some of the raids I'm going to be planning in the future.

Now to the task at hand.

This guild was formed as a quest based guild. Our charter stats that our purpose as well as having fun, is to explore the various quests thoughout the world of Everquest. With the introduction of Scars of Velious and Shadows of Luclin came numerous new quests designed around progressive themes and rewards. One of these series of quests are the Shar Vahl Class quests. Each Class of Vah Shir, Warrior, Beastlord, Bard, Shaman, and Rogue have a series of quests starting with the citizenship quest and finishing with, I believe, the Hero Medal of Shar Vahl. Along the way you improve your skill in your class' trade skill and get a series of Cloak upgrades.

What is unique about these quests is that it requires the co-operation of each class. The Sonic Bane weapon quests which need to be completed about 1/3rd of the way through, require components that can be manufactured only by Vah Shir of the particular classes. For example, the beastlord can make the toughened sinew lashings, the rogue makes the sonic bane poison, the bard makes ceramc sleeves and so on. This means that in order to progress you need to find a Vah Shir of a particular class that has worked up the particular Tradeskill (to 135 in most case).

Now, we have a couple of beastlords, my own Wylder included. Barend I believe has a Vah Shir Warrior and Xaviour/Coppermouth has a Vah Shir Bard named Kittylark. But we need a Rogue and a Shaman to do this.

These quests were designed for the Misty Knights as one of our major strengths is a willingness to help each other quest. I would like to ask for volunteers to create some characters of these classes and maybe a few more Warriors and Bards so that the guild as a whole can participate in these quests.

Over the next 2 weeks I'll be hard at work assembling some cash, both for my own equipment needs but also for supplies to start working on these quests. Anyone who wants to help me slaughter HG's in Rathe Mountains even if your only providing a pair of legs to make bank deposits, I'll be more then happy to have you. Those of you wanting to create a new character for this, I'll be happy to help you build up the necessary trade skills. If you wish to pursue the quests yourself I'll be happy to help you through the earlier stages as well.

Ideally I'd like to have a group of Vah Shir go all the way through the quests to their completion but I realize that not everyone has the time to devote to another Alt.

In the end I think this will be a fun time for everyone who participates as the quests themselves are very intriguing and the rewards are worth the effort.

Cheers to you all my fellow Knights

Raythor Baneslayer
Paladin of Tunare of the 51st Season
Proud Member of the Misty Knights

AKA Wylder Andwylder
Vah Shir Beastlord of the 38th Calling
Also Proud Member of the Misty Knights

-Raythor
