  Focus Items: Any good?
Posted by: necrro
Posted on: Tue Jun 18 06:31:54 2002

You may have noticed that there are some focus items in the game now. They come in three levels: I - 20th level spells and below; II - 44th level spells and below; and III - 60th level spells and below.

Each items has various effects you can look up on Allkhazam's site.

The question you might have is: if I am 44th or higher level, do I need a Level I Focus item? Is it useful to have on my character?

Here are some things I have noticed with necrro who has: Gatorscale Sleeves: Spell Haste I; Woven Grass Bracelet - Extended Affliction I; Batfang Headband - Extended Enhancement II; Dustscryer's Ball - Mana Preservation II; PBB - Extended Affliction - I (I think these are correct, not really imp to my message).

1. I find myself using my 16th level spirit armor buff for me and pet all the time so the Spell Haste I item is always useful including for a very nice quicker GATE and INVIS spell. A nice effect in a pinch.

2. Naturally, at 53, all my shielding and buffs, including my pet haste buff and DMF, etc. are 44th or lower. Thus, the Batfang Headband works everytime and so does the Dustscryer's Ball. At 54, all I lose is the affect on shielding because of new shields.

3. Dustscryer's Ball also very usefull for decrease mana use on many spells including the main pulling spell: Dooming Dark level 29.

Point of the message is that originally I thought the level I items would be useless and the level II items only marginally so. You may find, like I did, that the level II and even the level I items are more useful, even at high levels than they would appear to be.

Take a moment while you med to think about the utility spells you use on a regular basis. You may be surprised to find that you are casting some low level spells that are benefitted from the Level I items.


-necrro
