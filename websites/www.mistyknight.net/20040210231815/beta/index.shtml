Grats to everyone that came out for this Friday's Velks Raid! We're knocking the big guys off the list one at a time.

With less than 3 groups we made it in to the maze under Velkator's castle and tracked down Lord Bob.

The pull was incredibly frustrating until we figured out how to manage it (poor Tae must have died a least 5 times trying to work that one out). In the end Raythor was able to pacify the 2 spiders who were in close quarters and tae was able to coax him out of his hole.

Actually fighting him was a different story:
Toyman engaged and put up a valiant struggle but was cut down by his flurrys early on, T tried to land a slow for good measure but Bob was immune. Angels and Shey did their best to try and keep our tanks healthy... In the end Bob chewed through Toyman, Raythor, Brastez, Woody, and Tchara. Fortunately for us Angels got safely camped and the nukers and Raybound were able to finish the job!
