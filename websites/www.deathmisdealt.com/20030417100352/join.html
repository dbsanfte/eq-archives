Death Misdealt Recruit Policy
  1. Recruit must be at least level 55.
  2. Recruit must have ONE Sponsor. This sponsor must a permanent menber of the guild.
  3. The Recruit posts on the DM public recruit board that he is interested in joining then looks for a sponsor. Once he has a sponsor, the sponsor MUST post on the same public recruit board that he will sponsor the person. If this is not done, the person will not be pledge tagged. Only permanent DM members may sponsor a pledge.
  4. Recruit is PLEDGE TAGGED. This recruit will not have access to the private boards. They will need to contact guild members and their sponsor to keep up with raids and groups. The Pledge Tagged Recruit is not eligible for loot and does not earn DKP. This pledge period will last for a minimum of 2 wks.
  5. Pledge Tagged Recruit is voted on: This will be an OPEN vote, with majority rules. However Pledge will need at least 75% of yes votes to win. Voting will only be for a 2-day period and will be done on MONDAYS only.
  6. Tagged Probation Member-For the next 30 days, the New Member will be on probation. At the end of this 30 day period, the officers will give a yes or no vote based on GUILD opinion of the pledge. Then pledge will be promoted to Permanent Member.
  7. Recruit must have Planes of Power expansion.
