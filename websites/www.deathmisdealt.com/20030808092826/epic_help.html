Guild Assistance for Epics


1. An epic raid will only be scheduled for mobs requiring 2 or more groups.

2. Epic raids are to be scheduled through the Class Officers, who will coordinate with the Raid Officers. The exception will be when a rare mob pops. If possible, the guild will be mobilized to obtain the item.

3. The player must be a full member of Death Misdealt and must be an active participant in the guild. They must have the required DKP points before the raid will be scheduled. Members who have been in the guild the longest will be given precedence when raids are scheduled.

4. In general, assistance will only be given to obtain one epic per member. There will be exceptions based on guild need. Exceptions will be determined by full vote of the officers.
