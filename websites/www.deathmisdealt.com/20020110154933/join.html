Death Misdealt Recruit Policy
  1. Recruit must be at least level 49
  2. Recruit must have ONE Sponsor. This sponsor must a permanent menber of the guild.
  3. The Recruit posts on the DM public recruit board that he is interested in joining then looks for a sponsor. Once he has a sponsor, the sponsor MUST post on the same public recruit board that he will sponsor the person. If this is not done, the person will not be pledge tagged. Only permanent DM members may sponsor a pledge.
  4. Recruit is PLEDGE TAGGED. This recruit will not have access to the private boards. They will need to contact guild members and their sponsor to keep up with raids and groups. The Pledge Tagged Recruit is not eligible for loot, unless not needed by a present DM Perm or Prob Member. This pledge period will last for 2 wks.
  5. Pledge Tagged Recruit is voted on: This will be an OPEN vote, with majority rules. However Pledge will need at least 75% of yes votes to win. Voting will only be a 2day period and will be done on MONDAYS as well.
  6. Tagged Probation Member-For the next 30 days, the New Member will be on probation. They will be allowed to loot on GUILD Raids with the exception of EPIC peices needed by Permanent DM members. At the end of this 30 day period, the officers will give a yes or no vote based on GUILD opinion of the pledge. Then pledge will be promoted to Permanent Member.

Basically this creates three loot lvls with in our guild
Tagged Pledge / Probational Member / Permanent Member
This was done to help ease the hurtful feelings of "New" people getting loot before people who have been in the guild. Of course more than likely there will always be exceptions, which if all present will need to agree on.

**Please note that Rule #3 has changed as of 12/24/01**
