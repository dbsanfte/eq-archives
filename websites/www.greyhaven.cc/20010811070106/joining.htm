Joining GreyHaven

"We are dedicated to justice and camaraderie. We pledge to cleanse Norrath of injustice, and help those who have been wronged. We derive strength from our bonds and are open to any who hold justice above all, and who wish to celebrate life with battle and festival."

New Members:

  • Initiate - minimum Level 40. (unless a secondary character) All Inititiates will remain in this status until such time as they are elligible for instatement to the guild

 

  • Disciple - minimum level 45. At this point if all other requirements are met the new Initiate will be tagged as a Disciple of Greyhaven.

 

  • During the Initiation period, not to be less than 4 week or from the meeting excepted as an initiate to the meeting elligible for disciple. The new Initiate must obtain required sponsorship, Required Sponsorship will be a minimum of 5 sponsors total who have grouped and posted on the site as such, a minimum of 2 sponsorships must be obtained from officers. These requirements are in addition to reaching level 45. 

 

  • If any requirement is not met at the time of the next meeting, as long as there have been no objections to this person he may remain an initiate until such time that he has obtained all requirements necessary to be tagged as a member.

 

  • The New Initiate must visit the site and review the Charter, and place a post within the Membership Application forum. Nobody else should place the post for them. Simply click on New Topic and place a post with the following information:
    • Class/Level/Race of primary character
    • Class/Level/Race of alts that may join
    • E-Mail Address

     

  •  All current members are obligated to post any protest they may have of that character joining on the member discussion board. If there is a protest, the Grey Council will investigate it and decide whether or not to dismiss it.

 

  • Greyhaven is based on a Family like Environment, because of this any Real Life Relatives and or Friends can waiver normal requirements with approval from the Officers Body.
