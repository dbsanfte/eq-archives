Throughout history every culture has had a military of some sort. Within these Armies there was a structure. Rank and Insignia actually started within Religious Cults. Being extremely Chaotic in Nature these vast hordes needed a way to control internal turmoil.

Originally the use of an insignia was a customary practice of vain religious leaders who wore vast amounts of jewelry to show everyone their superiority. Eventually this became a common method of showing honor and pride for ones personal rank. In recent times this practice has taken on a formal approach using standard insignias to represent ranks within an Army.

The name GreyHaven itself is a 2 part symbolic word of historical meaning dating back to the beginning of time. Grey is an ancient religious Color representing a balance of all things. White, representing purity of essence and the combination of all colors signifying a vibrant soul; and Black, representing complete corruption of essence and a void of colors signifying the darkness of the soul.. Haven is an age old word meaning protected place and signifies Neutral Ground where all are respected and can rest Safely.

Our Guild has carried this tradition into its very Charter and Own Rank Structure giving each Rank a significant Color and Insignia, so that all Members (be they Human, Elf, Dwarf, Ogre, or any other race) can wear their given Rank with Honor and Pride.

Guild Councils - These Councils will be volunteer for the most part and consist of no more than 6 members per council. A Council Leader will be appointed by the Grey Council and chosen from those volunteers.

Additionally all Ranks will maintain a structure of 6, there will be a Captain (Officer) for every 6 members. until such time that we have 5 Captains then the sixth will be a Commander, at this point there will be 36 Guild Members. The Grey Council members are in addition to these standard Officers.

GREYHAVEN AWARDS
These Awards can be submitted by any Member of the Guild. All Awards are reviewed by the Grey Council.

The Bronze Shield This is the GreyHaven Appreciation Award and can be given to anyone even non Guild Members. (If a Non Guild Member does receive this Award, It will be posted in a Special section of Our Roster.)
The Platinum Star This is a personal Achievement Award given to members for Outstanding performance in accomplishing High Personal Goals.
The Silver Heart This is the Award for Heroism. This Award is not common and will almost exclusively be given in relation to a guild event. (What constitutes Heroism? Any petitions for this Award will be Reviewed in depth by the High Council.)
The Golden Griffon This is an Acknowledgement of Leadership, given to someone who's contribution results in the overall success of an Event , Raid or Hunting Exhibition.
The Scarlet Claw This is a Unique Award acknowledging self sacrifice. Normally given in conjunction with a guild event or hunt, but unlike other awards it can be submitted at anytime for someone who has sacrificed themselves as a contribution that benefits a group of people.
