Charter
"Lets develop GreyHaven as a skill-based, guide-oriented, example-setting, group of friends. And above ALL ELSE lets have FUN!"


That being said, on to the unfortunate part of having to place some basic rules of conduct.

 

  • 1. Respect your fellow guild members. And give all non members the respect they are due.
 
  • 2. Always act in a respectable way to represent the guild properly. No whining, begging, calling others names, KSing, shouting obscenities, etc.
 
  • 3. Play nice policy. (PNP) EQ has incorporated this into their policies. Everyone in this game is a paying customer. This is a recreational activity and no one should do anything to ruin it for someone else. Don't treat anyone how you wouldn't want to be treated yourself.
 
  • 4. Our guild has every race and every class represented. We can set the example for not only other players, but for other guilds.
  •  
  • 5. Any items you get are yours, what you choose to do with them is your choice. If lent an item, please agree upon a return time beforehand and return it when the time comes. We all work hard for the things we get, so it is our privilege to decide how we distribute them. Of course anything you decide to give to the guild is welcome, so we have more to distribute to members and friends.
 
  • 6. Try to make sure your /ooc comments are in /ooc and not /shout, etc; ie, not shouting about your computer, et al.  This will help to keep the role-playing aspect of the game more pure.
 
  • 7. Register with the bulletin board on our site, and monitor and post often.  Communication is of the utmost importance to the success of the guild.
 
  • 8. Failure to comply with the guild charter may result in a warning, followed by immediate removal from the guild if the warning is not heeded. Officers have the authority to /guildremove anyone they feel that is showing a blatant disregard for guild rules.
 
  • 9. Try to attend all guild gatherings, they occur every 4-6 weeks.  This is a time where we can gather and enjoy each other's company. It's also a time to discuss important guild news. We also tend to have raids after our gatherings. Once the official meeting time has commenced, disruptions of the gatherings will not be tolerated (ie NO Duels/Pets/Shouts/Casting/Etc unless in Self-Defense). 
 
  • 10. Last and most important one: Have Fun.  This is a game and we should enjoy ourselves while playing.
