  Corrupted Hunt in Wakening Lands and Renux Herkanor in Steamfont

Lythia writes "I want to extend a big thank you for everyone that came to my assistance with the hunt for the Corrupted Animals in Wakening Lands.

With all your help today, I managed to acquire 2 of my last 3 pieces for my Tunarean Paladinic Sword, The Nature's Defender.

The first wave of Greyhaven'ers and friends managed to take down the Corrupted Faun. He was white to me at level 55, and hits for generally about 220 per hit. A small group of me, Oogrre, Bigbug, Rann, Alexsis, Jaramena, Medallion, Kinadwarv, Zass and Kumathain, managed to take him down with minimal losses.

A corrupted faun hits YOU for 231 points of damage.
A corrupted faun hits YOU for 87 points of damage.

Later on more friends and family arrived to assist on the next hardest mob which was the Corrupted Unicorn. After some running around and zoning, we all made it to the north of the zone to set up next campsite.

Unfortunately, along the way, our ravishingly handsome Oogie attracted the attention of Rolandal. Rolandal threw Oog up into the air about 4 times before laying his tattered body on the ground. (Gravity Flux for 1250 hp each I think was what Oog said.) This all happened in a matter of seconds until we realized Oogie was no longer with us. We dragged the limp corpse of our beloved ogre to our camp and proceeded to bring him back to life.

Finally everyone gathered and we tried our first attempt at the Corrupted Unicorn. Hard fought was our battle, but alas our last fighter fell before the Corrupted Unicorn, who was down to 30% life.

A corrupted unicorn hits YOU for 110 points of damage.
A corrupted unicorn hits YOU for 290 points of damage.

He had a 1.2k Harm Touch, Pet, Blinds, Fears, and hits very hard.

After some waiting, rezzing, talking, we finally all made it back to camp and ready for another try. This time even more friends and family came to assist.

Our second try went very well with minimal losses and finally the Corrupted Unicorn fell before our feet.

[Sun Feb 10 18:36:40 2002] a corrupted unicorn has been slain by Rann!

Woohoo Rann on the killing blow. :)

With much rejoicing and much pride some adventurers retired for the evening, while others remained behind.

Unfortunately the Corrupted Panther was nowhere to be found, and he was the last one needed.

The hunt for the Corrupted Panther shall continue some other day, but for now the adventurers had one more thing to take care of.

Swiftly we all ported over to Steamfont to kill "Renux Herkanor" for Alexsis.

Renux Herkanor pierces YOU for 114 points of damage.
Renux Herkanor slashes YOU for 147 points of damage.

Renux Herkanor died.
Your faction standing with Circle Of Unseen Hands got worse.
Your faction standing with Merchants of Qeynos got better.
Your faction standing with Corrupt Qeynos Guards got worse.
Your faction standing with Guards of Qeynos got better.
Your faction standing with Kane Bayle got worse.
You gain party experience!!

Once again a big (((CONGRATS))) to Cyanime/Alexsis.

Also again, a big thank you to all of Greyhaven, Laylana, Deamentia, and Alexsis.
{{{BIG HUGS FOR ALL}}}"
