Joining GreyHaven

  

"We are dedicated to justice and camaraderie. We pledge to cleanse Norrath of injustice, and help those who have been wronged. We derive strength from our bonds and are open to any who hold justice above all, and who wish to celebrate life with battle and festival."

 

New Members:

  • Initiate minimum Level 20. (unless a secondary character)

 

  • Disciple minimum level 25

 

  • Must obtain required sponsorship, exact numbers of which will vary depending on the situation. However, at least one sponsor must be an officer.

 

  • Must Visit Site and Review the Charter, and place a post within the Membership Application forum. Nobody else should place the post for them. Simply click on New Topic and place a post with the following information:
    • Class/Level/Race of primary character
    • Class/Level/Race of alts that may join
    • E-Mail Address

     

  • Once a prospective member has received the necessary sponsorship, he will be inducted into the guild. After a probationary period (normally 2-4 weeks) or upon reaching level 25, whichever is greater, it will be voted on by the Grey Council. 

 

  •  A notification in the news section will be put up, notifying of a prospective member. An e-mail should also be sent to them. All current members are obligated to post any protest they may have of that character joining on the member discussion board. If there is a protest, the Grey Council will investigate it and decide whether or not to dismiss it.
