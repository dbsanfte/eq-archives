Joining GreyHaven

 

"We are dedicated to justice and camaraderie. We pledge to cleanse Norrath of injustice, and help those who have been wronged. We derive strength from our bonds and are open to any who hold justice above all, and who wish to celebrate life with battle and festival."

 

New Members:

  • Initiate - minimum Level 40. (unless a secondary character) All Initiates will remain in this status until such time as they are excepted for instatement to the guild

 

  • Disciple - minimum level 45. Once excepted an Initiate is tagged and becomes a Disciple of GreyHaven.

 

  • The New Initiate must visit the site and review the Charter, and place a post within the Membership Application forum. Nobody else should place the post for them. Simply click on New Topic and place a post with the following information:
    • Class/Level/Race of primary character
    • Class/Level/Race of alts that may join
    • E-Mail Address

     

  • People wishing to become Initiates must be present at a Guild Gathering to be excepted Officially as Initiates. During the Initiation period, not to be less than 4 week or from the meeting excepted as an initiate to the meeting eligible for Disciple. The new Initiate must obtain required sponsorship. 

 

  • Required Sponsorship will be a minimum of 5 sponsors total who have grouped and posted on the site as such, a minimum of 2 sponsorships must be obtained from officers. These requirements are in addition to reaching level 45. 

 

  • An Initiate will be expected to show at the following meeting to be tagged and excepted as a Disciple of GreyHaven. Initiates will be contacted during the One month trial period to group with guild members and get to know everyone, If at any time during this period something should happen making them Ineligible they will be contacted in regards to reasons why.

    •  

  • If for some reason an Initiate is unavailable for the following month or does not meet requirements for some reason, as long as there have been no objections to this person he may remain an initiate until such time that he has obtained all requirements necessary to be tagged as a member.

     

  •  All current members are obligated to post any protest they may have against any current Initiates on the member discussion board. If there is a protest, the Recruitment /Retention Officers will investigate it and decide whether or not it is valid or if it can be dismissed.

 

  • GreyHaven is based on a Family like Environment, because of this any Real Life Relatives and or Real Life Friends of an Established Guild Member can waiver normal requirements with approval from the Officers Body. Any one receiving a "Special Invitation" must still post in the Recruitment section of the Site all required Info but if accepted will be able to become Disciples with out the normal initiation period.

 

  • Note ALL of these Rules are guidelines, they are not laws, they are not set in stone, so if you do not think you will meet our requirements, post anyway, there are exceptions to all rules, get  to know us, get to be our friends.
