The members of Black Thorn are a strong and united group from across the lands of Norrath. Skilled swordsmen and women, talented wielders of the mojo, and crafty thieves with sticky fingers, it's members complement each other to accomplish the many goals this guild has set out to achieve. We are trusting yet sly, smart and nimble, witty and at times naughty. We  travel the lands in search of fun and adventure while sharing a bond of friendship. It tis an honor to be part of Black Thorn for the people within it's halls are by far the finest found anywhere.
