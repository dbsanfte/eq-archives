Greetings



Welcome to the Black Thorn website.

We are a group of players who seek adventure and most importantly enjoyment from our time spent online. Through the game we have developed close friendships with each other and have become a tight knit group.

Many have been in the game for several years which provides for a well established membership that knows their way around the pixels.

We are not a roleplaying guild by any means but have a great time experiencing what ever trouble we can find.
I hope you find your stay here enjoyable and knowledgeable.

Please take the time to sign up and take advantage of all the site has to offer, we are adding features every week.

 BT News: Happy Birthday Black Thorn
Today marks the second anniversary of BT, and what a 2 years it has been I tell ya )

This guild was started by a small band of friends, and ended up turning into one of the best guilds on the Bristlebane server. We have seen alot of turmoil, but our drive and caring for one another has made this guild endure and prosper over the years.

Black Thorn is filled with a variety of wonderful people from all different walks of life and I would like to thank each and everyone of you for making this guild as special as it is, it is very rare to have a family-based raiding guild work as well as we have.

A special thanks goes out to those who have been there since the beginning and believed enough in Black Thorn to stick through the bad times and make something of this guild.

We have built friendships in this guild, ones that will last far beyond this game ever will, some have even found romance within our ranks and that is a true testament of what this guild is about.

I love you all and proud to call you family )

Happy Birthday Black Thorn!
