January 31st, 2002
An prosperous night in the world of Black Thorn as we jumped all over the lands. Unrest and Runnyeye were quick hits, East Karanas for Cerridwyn's epic piece, Plane of Hate, Dreadlands, and Wakening Lands.  

January 30th, 2002
Yet another successful siege on the gates to Chardok entrance as several groups of Black Thorn set up camp and cleaned up the petty sarnaks. A great experience hunt. From there the troops moved back to Dreadlands and Karnors Castle where Drovlags were served up Thorn style.

January 28th, 2002
The younger generation of Black Thorn adventured once again on the shores of Lake of Ill Omen battling sarnaks, goblins, as well as each other. An event hosted by Our Lady Shakai. Congrats to all those that were able to gain a level or two. Meanwhile, our forces continued to slash and burn through the minions of Dreadlands and Lower Guk.

January 27th, 2002
It was Plane of Air day and our Lady Keylistia played host to the events. More details coming soon...

January 26th, 2002
A special thanks to those that assisted in the slaying of Renux for the Jagged Diamond Dagger and parchment. A very impressive show of teamwork and organization allowed us to take down a very tough quest NPC. Great work to say the least. 
Meanwhile, the remaining Black Thorn members owned the Dreadlands and everyone continued to work on leveling. 

January 24th & 25th, 2002
Well we made an attempt at Naggy but it didn't turn out quite the way we had expected. Although we were well organized and prepared the age factor was the most evident issue at hand. On the good side of this it gave many the chance to experience what goes into preparing for such an event. Great job folks, you all did very well. Thank you to those that assisted in CR and clearing Fire Giants on both nights. Your efforts are greatly appreciated.

January 19th, 2002
It was back to Skyfire Mtns. and a great turn out once again by Black Thorn. Numerous spells dropped, the Faerie of Dismay was taken down, and a job well done by everyone who could attend. Welcome Lady Vexed and Lord Smackle to the ranks of Black Thorn. 

January 18th, 2002
Some joined Clan Mackintosh in the City of Mist while others in Black Thorn continued their adventures in Dreadlands, Guk, and other such arenas. The younger generation traveled far from home to the Stonebrunt Mtns. lead by Lady Shakai. 

January 16th, 2002
Several in the guild sought pieces to their epic quest and many found success in their hunt. Lord Bakbal, Lady Cerridwyn, and Lady Demile all got needed pieces. Congratulations. 
Our young cleric Dominich has announced his upcoming wedding plans and the adoption of Lady Shakai. Stay tuned for more on these exciting developments!!!

January 12th, 2002
Black Thorn was joined by Clan Mackintosh in a closed guild raid on the Plane of Hate. A great turn out and fine show of force breaking and advancing through the keep. Special thanks to Elindel, Kloh, Lunna, and the other friends who were able to accompany us on yet another grand adventure. Our Lady Demile did an excellent job coordinating and organizing this event. Notable drops included the Cleric breastplate and arms, Rogue arms and gauntlets, and the Paladin epic chest piece. 

January 10th, 2002
www.iblackthorn.com statistics. Total number of hits for December 2001 were 22,831. Average hits per day 781.
