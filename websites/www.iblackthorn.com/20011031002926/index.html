October 19th, 2001
We arrived in Iceclad Ocean to join forces and affirm our alliance with Norraths Curse. Our combined legions cut through the local snow cougars like butter. Innorruk looked favorably upon us this eve as numerous cougar claw earrings dropped off the wild kitties. 


We decided to test our resolve further by traveling across the frozen landscape to Crystal Caverns. The orc inhabitants poses little challenge for the superb teamwork and battle skills that Norraths Curse and Black Thorn displayed. The infamous Herbalist and Foreman that watch over the mining operation met their match and yielded our forces (2) Crystal Covered Shrouds, and several Frozen Long Swords. 


All Hail the new alliance between Black Thorn and Norraths Curse!!!

Furthermore, congratulations to Lord Razore (36), Lady Paigan (38), and Lady Treom (30).

A parchment was received upon our steps signed by Norrath's Curse. The words upon the paper spoke of a possible guild alliance to benefit our growing factions. To introduce the ranks a joint venture in Ice Clad Ocean is scheduled for Friday October 19th at 9:00pm EST. So "Roll Out the Barrels!!"
