Interested in joining the ranks of Black Thorn?
We currently welcome any new applications to our guild if their character meets the requirements below. 

1. Must be 18 years old or older in real life. 
2. Minimum level of 50. (will allow shamans & druids lvl 45+)*
3. Should attend a majority of guild raids and events.
4. Must have Ruins of Kunark and Scars of Velious installed.
5. Should have heat and cold resist gear or appropriate gems to have jewelry made.
6. SoW potions, Journeyman Boots, or ability to cast SoW.
7. One stack of Bat Wings or ability to levitate. 
8. One stack of Fish Scales or ability to Enduring Breath.
9. EZBoard.com ID.
10. Guild Magic ID.
11. Everquestraids.com ID.
12. Skyshrine Key
13. Master in one in-game Trade Skill.

* Subject to change as the guild as a whole increases in level.

Once accepted members may bring in alternate personas if their main character is level 45.

Black Thorn is a raiding and event oriented guild with it's main focus on strategic multi-group hunting. Whether it be a fast paced experience hunt or higher level single NPC, Black Thorn does things as a group of groups. This environment builds companionship among the ranks both in game and in real life. The guild works together as a team in any arena often fielding 4 or more groups to accomplish a specific goal. Many of Black Thorn raids are closed to the public to ensure the best possible chance that items and experience are allocated to good hands. It also eliminates the worry of dealing with people we do not know...(i.e. complaining about loot, mistakes due to not listening, and impatient raid members)...an all to common event in today's Norrath society. 

If accepted to Black Thorn you join a family, an army, and a game few experience in their travels of the lands. Everquest is a game and our main focus is having fun, loot, leveling, friendship, and sharing experiences...

The members of Black Thorn reserve the right to accept or deny any application without reason or explanation.
