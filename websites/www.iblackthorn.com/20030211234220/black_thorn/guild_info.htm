Black Thorn Guild Information...     

 

Who we are.
Black Thorn is a guild in the game EverQuest on the Bristlebane server. We are a group of players who seek adventure and most importantly enjoyment from our time spent online. Through the game we have developed close friendships with each other and have become a tight knit group. Many have been in the game for several years which provides for a well established membership that knows their way around the pixels. We are not a roleplaying guild by any means but have a great time experiencing what ever trouble we can find.

What we do.
Our mean focus is to improve the characters that call Black Thorn home. Much of our time is spent raiding and seeking profit. We expect our members to fully support their fellow guildmates and perform their duties of the class they have chosen at any given time. You will find the ranks of Black Thorn passing time in such arenas as Temple of Veeshan, The Planes, etc. or racing off to slay a spawned mob such as The Va'Dyn, Rumblebcrush, Vindi, or Lcea Katta. Our members enjoy the the challenge of the game and figuring out the tactics to successfully finish the job.

Raid Information.
If and when a raid is called we expect our members to attend no questions asked. We do raid quite a bit and appreciate the dedictation that the folks in Black Thorn show. There are several events that can occur at any given time. 1. An officer or raid council member can call a raid upon a spawn. 2. A member can schedule an event and call for assistance. 3. An event can be scheduled on the calendar and planned well in advance. 

NO OPEN RAIDING HERE:
All Black Thorn raids are closed to the public. This means that members and special guests are welcome. The reason for this is the ensure that the loot and experience are placed within the guild for our efforts put forth. 

LOOT:
All major loot is assigned by the officers of the guild. Though it may seem unfair on the surface, the distribution is always fairly done. We follow the understanding that "Its not who gets the item...it's who gets the item today." There are no loot disputes in our ranks as everyone knows that in time they will be upgraded to bigger and better things.
