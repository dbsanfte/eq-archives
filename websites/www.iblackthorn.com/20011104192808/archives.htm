September 20th, 2001
We have kidnapped Lady Winie of Clan Mackintosh, grand-daugther of Lord Jardail and Lady Gildanni, daugther of Lord Jhason and Lady Laene. For her safe return we have asked for 500,000pp. Meanwhile, the young shamaness rests in our dark dungeon deep in the lower levels of our keep.

August 22nd, 2001
Our forces have arrived on Kunark and found shelter at the outpost in the land called Overthere. The locals told us of a tower east of our location called Kurns. It yields the bones of once living iksars and it's basement is home to many Burynai, mole like creatures. We made note and traveled east along the shoreline and soon discovered the tower in which they spoke of. We entered it's dark confines and battled it's patrons, successfully taking control of the undead keep. A few of our party ventured down to reep the treasure of the basement dwellers. Wren will be proud of our advance.

August 13th, 2001
Deep in the heart of Neriak we gathered to determine who could dish out the most damage. The contest drew many spectators as both young and old cheered the fighting few.

As fate would have it the last two standing in the arena were Jadarin and his wife Jadelf...*smirks*
