Greetings



Welcome to the Black Thorn website.

We are a group of players who seek adventure and most importantly enjoyment from our time spent online. Through the game we have developed close friendships with each other and have become a tight knit group.

Many have been in the game for several years which provides for a well established membership that knows their way around the pixels.

We are not a roleplaying guild by any means but have a great time experiencing what ever trouble we can find.
I hope you find your stay here enjoyable and knowledgeable.

Please take the time to sign up and take advantage of all the site has to offer, we are adding features every week.

 BT News: New Features
I added 3 new features to the website and would like you to try them out.

First, create a profile in "Character Edit" section, fill out all the information on the form, dont worry if you get all of it on the first pass... you can always come back and edit. The information you supply will be entered into the database.

The "Guild Reports" section not only acts as a guild roster, but also shows where we are as a guild as far as planar progression, keys, levels, classes, etc...

And of course... a new "Guild Roster" hehe.

I am in the process of adding a magelo update to the "Character Edit" form, it will allow you (I hope) to automatically download your Magelo info to your profile.

So check it out and tell me what you think.

I also updated the html version of the roster, please see if your information is correct, if not post on the board and I will change immediately.

Thanks... Enjoy!

 BT News: BT's EQBBQ 3
Well the weather held out and we partied late into the night as we carried on a BT tradition for a third straight year.

The illustrious guest list included Sinadorina, Shakai, Paigan, Magnomus, Razore, Madrigal, Asphoedele, Naminawa, Juriette, Tuben from LoT, and Kurolov from the Veeshan server.

All had a grand time drinking beer, watching me burn Hot Dogs with my new grill, drinking beer and talking over EQ stratigies till the sun went down... Oh... and drinking more beer.

Paigan and I would like to thank all those who came to make EQBBQ 3 a success and look forward to seeing new faces at the next BBQ.
