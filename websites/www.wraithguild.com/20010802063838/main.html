Veeshan's Peak - Day 2 - Desi. June 25th, 2001

What a wonderful, wonderful day it turned out to be. A great day for Wraith and for Quellious. Today, Wraith became the second guild in Quellious to clear Veeshan's Peak, clearing the other 4 dragons, Xygoz, Druushk, Nexona, and Phara Dar. 4 attempts, 4 dead dragons. The day started with a great sense of anticipation.  

Rest in peace - Xygoz

The first pull today was of the golden dragon, with the much talked about AoE Silence spell. Definitely the easiest dragon in VP, it was a fun fight. Samsonn and Batuda managed to land the spears and Faie won the blade. Another shot of him.

Rest in peace - Druushk

On to Druushk, who had wiped us once the night before. His 1700 pt AE hurts, but the nukers had his number. Our shortest fight in the peak, the idiot had the audacity to drop not one, not two, but three necro staffs. Grats Disruptor, Maylay and the destroy button. 

Rest in peace - Nexona

We forgive the pull team for the excruciatingly long wait in getting this dragon, for it's death rejuvinated us for the Phara attempt. We downed the mob on first go as well despite the fact that incorrect resist info caused a lot of casualties. The raid leaders can be forgiven for the lack of research. This was a Silverwing only raid after all Congrats to Easier on the cutlass, Spellquestor on the maul, and Gurunt on the star. 

Rest in peace - Phara Dar

And ofcourse, last, but certainly not least, the hardest dragon in Veeshan's Peak. Or supposedly. We personally had more problems with Hoshkar. Phara died, despite Complete Healing herself, with only 1 or 2 casualties. And if there is any dragon that drops good loot consistenly in Veeshan's Peak, it's Phara Dar. We were lucky to get the rare Robe of the Azure Sky, the second on the server, and I cant thank the guild enough for choosing me to be worthy of it. In other drops Boldair was awarded the sweet shroud, Dachunker the claw, and Jagg the katana. 

Thus concluded the successful raid put together by Thuulsa and Jagg, though the intent at zoning in was to just try Silverwing, with few expectations. After VP was cleared, Thuulsa appropriately responded.

It was a wonderful weekend and Veeshan's Peak's dragons will die again, and again, till we have all the items we need from them. Good day folks.
