Deconstructing Uqua: Phase 1 ..... & Recruiting & Superheroes - May 21st 2004 - Biffins

We've started raiding in Uqua and contrary to all expectations we've found the zone a ton of fun.

To all the guilds who beta-tested it last few months and then got frustrated or even quit, Thank You! We're enjoying the fruits of your labors and are having a fantastic time in the place.

We haven't been in Uqua for too long but have managed to get atleast far enough to get Mr. Enraged pictured above dead. More news to come from subsequent trips.

Recruiting:

We've re-opened recruiting recently after a 2 month hiatus. We feel we've trimmed down sufficiently from the PoP raid sizes to the ones needed in GoD now to recruit a few active players who want to see high-end EQ and want to raid in GoD.

Ofcourse, we do like to be guilded with like-minded people. So if you do not like to raid, do not know your class well, do not like to play with skilled players, do not see yourself playing much in the coming months, do not appreciate good natured conversations, and above all, do not know how to have fun in EQ, then this is not the guild for you. If the above does not apply, head on over to the Recruitment section and follow the guidelines to post an application.

All classes are welcome to apply but admission will depend on our needs.

Men in Tights:

Yes that's the name of the Robin Hood spoof movie but it also happens to be the name of the Wraith / Reviviscence super-group in City of Heroes. I want to give a shout out to all old-timers, inactive and ex-W/R players who I know still check up on this site now and then to hook up with some old pals if they are playing or planning to play City of Heroes. I haven't played it, nor am planning to, but apparently these guys have been having some fun.

Was kind of funny to hear the first thing in /raid chat after Enraged dies was "See you in Paragon City in 10 minutes" hehe.

The Wraith / Reviviscence community will survive EQ. Cheers!
