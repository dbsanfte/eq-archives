Welcome!



So you’ve decided to recruit. Now what?



Step 1:
You should start by reading a little bit about Wraith.
Here you’ll find a short summary of who we are, and how we function. A lot of major questions are answered here, and many misconceptions are addressed.

Step 2:
Check our class needs and minimum requirements.
Here you’ll find a list of classes which are open for recruitment, as well as detailed AA requirements. These requirements are, without much variation, set in stone. If you wish to apply, you must meet these minimum requirements unless directed otherwise by an officer.

Step 3:
Read about what is expected of you during recruitment.
Here you’ll find answers to many questions recruits have, in addition to a general guide on what to do and how to act. They don’t teach you how to be an effective recruit in grammar school, but here you can find out just that.

Step 4:
View our online application.
This is a link to our boards. Here you’ll find a blank application, which you can then copy and paste and fill out with your information. Once you submit this information, your application is NOT complete. Remember, you must contact a member of the recruitment team before your application is processed. Details on this can also be found at the above link.



Good luck,
Wraith Recruitment.
