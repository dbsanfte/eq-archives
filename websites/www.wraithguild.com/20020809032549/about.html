If you are interested in joining Wraith you can do one of a few things.

  • Contact the recruit officers in game. Biffins/Desi, Alzedo, or Ketel
  • If you have a friend within our ranks you can also have them talk to the officers about your interest to join.
  • Post on our message boards on the Recruitment Desk forum.

Level Requirements

These are enforced. But we also make a few exceptions depending on the situation.

Bards - 57 Clerics - 55 Druids - 60
Enchanters - 60 Magicians - 60 Monks - 60
Necromancers - 60 Paladins - 60 Rangers - 60
Rogues - 60 Shadowknights - 60 Shamans - 60
Warriors - 60 Wizards - 57
