CHARTER OF COUNCIL MAGICA

On the twenty-third day of the fifth month of the new millennium, a group of friends sharing like ideals allied power to conquer those who would look to harm them. Council Magica was born of that friendship, and what was to follow is, to this day, their legacy…

The Council is based on the virtue of honor, for those without have no place within the embracing arms that enfold the Council. Without honor, there can be no trust…without trust, there can be no Council.

It is on the basis of trust that the Council is governed, placing in ruling positions those trusted, respected, and above all, loved. Should any of the members of the Council have a problem of any kind, they should feel free to bring it to the attention of any officer within the Council. The guild leader, Manthion Tastran is the father of the Council. All beneath him are brothers and sisters of the Council. The Council is more than an allegiance of convenience. More than a bond between friends. Greater than the sum of her members. The Council is family.

As with any family, certain rules and laws must be placed and enforced to ensure the smooth function and operation of it's self. These laws are in place for the good of the Council and her members.

1. HONOR is the primary ideal driving the Council. Any member found cheating, stealing or otherwise swindling any other in the lands of Norrath, will be summarily dealt with by the ruling body of the Council. Only one warning will be issued for any cheat or swindler within the Council. After that, removal will take place.

2. Any member found to be dishonoring the guild will be summarily dealt with by the ruling body of the Council. This includes all from tarnishing the image of the Council, to misrepresenting the intentions of the Council to others. As a member of the Council, it is your job to protect the image, reputation, and soul of the Council with your every action.

3. Any and all officers of the Council will have the power to allow others access to the greatness that is Council Magica. All new members will go through a 30 day trial period during which time it is their own responsibility to get acquainted with the Council and her members. At the end of the trial, a vote will be held among the officers of the Council. It requires a 51% positive vote for acceptance of the pledge into full membership.

4. In the constant quest to maintain the image of the Council, no member will knowingly steal kills from any other in Norrath. This promotes ire toward the Council and will not be tolerated. This falls in the same category as cheating. One warning will be issued. Then removal will be implemented.

5. Begging will not be tolerated within the hallowed halls of Council Magica. We are not here to help one attain greatness, but rather to become great with the members' efforts. Whether it be wealth or experience one is after, aid may be offered, even requested, but in no way will one beg such assistance from another member of the Council. Should an instance of such behavior arise, it should be immediately reported to an officer so it may be dealth with. Two warnings will be given for this offense. Afterwards, removal will be the punishment. If one is not willing to do something for oneself, how can they expect the aid of the Council?

6. Respect is another attribute of Council members. We treat our brothers and sisters of the Council as we would our own families. Constant swearing, demeaning or other hurtful remarks that would offend the Council will be summarily dealth with. This could result in probation to outright expulsion from the Council.

7. Any member leaving the guild must be interviewed by an officer before being readmitted to the ranks of the Council. Should an officer leave, their status shall not be restored upon re-entry into the Council, as quitters tend to make poor leaders. Should one leave twice, then readmittance will not be possible.

8. In order to remove a member from the guild, there must be a vote taken among the present officers to determine if the offense is worthy of expulsion.

9. There will be a council meeting the last day of every month beginning at 7pm CST in Erudin. All who are able are expected to attend.
