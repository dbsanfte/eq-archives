Roleplay for an RP Snob is a Carebear Bake Sale
by Holly the Admin   View Profile

posted 8/18/99 3:29:32 PM


[Something that has probably happened on Fennin Ro]

Paladin: "I shall not buy a cupcake made by the spawn of Innoruuk!!"

Dark Elf: "Be gone you automaton of Marr. I would not sell you a single baked good made by the Hand of Innoruuk!"

Paladin: "BAH!! The disciples of Marr make the best puddings and pies! Demonspawn such as yourself could never master the fine points of baking!"

Dark Elf: "Begone Paladin before I smote you. You shall not talk about the baking expertise of the Teir'Dal Nation in that fashion and expect to get away with it!"

Paladin: "Ha! Try this baked pudding, Dark One. Try it and revel in the pure pleasure that is Erollisi Marr!"

Dark Elf: "Give me a pudding Paladin. I fear I've not been regular as of late and need a good laxative."

Paladin: "Do not tempt into slaying you, Dark Elf. Do not cast slurs upon my cooking!" (Gives the Dark Elf a baked pudding).

Dark Elf: (Tries the baked pudding). "I must confess Paladin that this baked pudding is indeed delicious. Let us be friends instead of enemies. Let us venture to Unrest and slay beasts together so that we may get to know one another better."

Paladin: "Aaah ... now you speak sense, Dark One. Through understanding comes frienship and peace between our peoples. Here have another baked pudding and let me try one of your cupcakes...."

H.
