"Gods Never Die?"
This is a question posed in respect, not in the opposite.

Now, let's say a God of Kaaos, a member of your guild, is having a good day. Winning every battle he or she comes across (be that a good day or a normal day is not my concern), and pretty much slaughtering anyone who puts up an initial threat towards the God.

But, after a few hours, the God comes across just an ordinary cocky Joe. This guy thinks he will kill you, beat you to pieces, and throw you down without even taking a scratch. So, you fight him. Let's say, in respect, you lose (again, it's not my concern whether you never lose or not--a player unprepared to lose is simply unprepared in general)

So, of course, the guy makes fun of you. Do you, as most would do, seek out the fellow again and attempt to kill him, or try to ignore the fact that he just killed you, and move along with your life? If you chose option number 2, what would you say or do if you were to come in contact with that person again, and he were to taunt you and tell everyone that he already killed you? Lie? Turn people against him?

Let's say you decide to return and attempt to murder him again. Once again, out of respect, you die, and people around you see this. If this were to ever come up in the future, would you deny it? Tell him (and everyone) in confidence that you will kill him in the near future?

Like I said, of course your members are mainly winners, with a rare loss here and there. But it's impossible to never lose or be killed by a lucky bastard, and surely there's a defense mechanism that the Gods of Kaaos all have. How can you create a persona that's godlike and evil if people around you have the memories of your past losses in their heads? Do you train harder to overcome the person that defeated you and reclaim your respect? How can you hold an online personality if, whenever you enter and announce that you're godlike, people say snide remarks and don't believe you? Do you kill them, or stay away from them?

You can't build your image simply around talk...or can you? I'm currently immersed in an online community that likes to never leave your past in the past. Is it different in the realms that you play in?

True, I have asked quite a few questions, but this is a topic that has plagued me for years since I've been in the certain game, and they're all orientated along the same path.
