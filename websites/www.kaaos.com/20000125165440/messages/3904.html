The False Prophet walks through an empty wasteland of burning heat. His paces draw him through league after league of heat.

As his burning thirst grows, he comes upon a strange sight. In this desert of burning sand lies a pool - a strange pool. It is a pool of ice, frozen solid. Here white, there transparent. And around its outermost edges, the heat of the desert melts a circle of water.

Hedron stops and drinks deeply of the ice made water. Smiling, he walks on. Strange sights please him.
