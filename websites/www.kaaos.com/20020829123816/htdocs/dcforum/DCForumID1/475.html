"A fucking rant"
Yea yea... I have to vent a little, no where else to do it, no one else has a clue about how it used to be.

I bought pool of radiance, I was excited, I dreamt of others... of Bits of Moander, hell even Draconians spitting at me. Of days past, my C-128 and some slow ass 16 bit graphics, 1988, a pissed of girlfriend and me an a bud hunkered around a dumb ass computor game, an island in the middle of a river, throw a rock... walk through. I was excited.

I loaded it up and jumped right in, fuck no I didn't read the book it couldn't be that different, I mean D&D rules are D&D rules, right? I bought the expensive version, pinned the map on the backdrop behind the pc, rolled the dice a couple of times and tossed em in the drawer with the multitudes of others, it was loading. I rolled up some charaters... er, stat modified some chraracters, why the fuck would they give you dice and not let the game random roll your characters? I didn't care.... A fighter, a cleric, a mage, and a paladin... all ready to roll, hell I even remembered thier names from 1988, Vincent, Buffy (yea... who'da known)Slayer, and Tank. The game was loaded, I was in... cool, new twist, I'm lost in the ruins of Myth Drannor, orcs directly ahead looting the corpse of those I seek, trying to rescue Phlan from the hands of evil once again. Mage is first... no spells, ack. Cleric is next he gets a swing in, fighter drops em in one shot... in turned based combat yeeeeaaaaaa. I grab a beer, begin to wander, kill some shit and find a place to rest. No grim, wtf? My mage can't cast any spells? Where the hell is my THACO? Why is my god damn armor 18? Ohh no, I pick up the book and begin to read......

Charisma determines how many spells and of what level spells you can cast? Charisma determines chance of turning undead? Armor is figured completely fucking backwards? die roll + modifiers must equal armor to gain a hit and I guess what ever the fuck is left over is the damage? I could go on, but why bother.

The game is cool, I like the turn based combat once I got it sped up a little (all this real time shit has seemed to make me a little impatient, beer helps though). The graphics will do, my rolls still suck but at least they are rolls, the fe is gonna work I guess, looks like the game will probably be a lot of fun in the long run. All I want to know is, Who, who fucked up the D&D rules so badly?

Abs

-oh, and nice itty worship there guys. If the world can count on anyone keeping the memories alive I guess it would have to be thier biggest fans-
