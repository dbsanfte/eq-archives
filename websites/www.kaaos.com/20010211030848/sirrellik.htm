He is over a millennium in age, but few have guessed him to be past his late twenties. His hair is long, the color of the burning sun. His skin is deeply tanned and above his scarred cheekbones his eyes are a menacing steel grey. This GOD's name is RELLIKK, and untold thousands have died cursing his name.

RELLIKK, The Legend of Neverwinter that Bards sing about:

'Heh Heh!' were the only words to come from his lips, as he pulled out his 9 inch serrated dagger. Knowing that actions speak louder than words he finished off his opponent and walked off with a stagger.

Glancing back, he watched the other's blood soak into the ground and with a grin at the sight he yelled "Take it, wuss!" Already turning to look for another to fight, careful to not make a sound, Sir Rellik thought of hot tubs, spritzers and a certain Spiked Boot.

KAAOS knows no bounds in the destruction of all that is good and do not hold any barriers to those that want to join its cause. They proclaim themselves Gods and Goddesses over all and their arrival in your midst is always enough to give you pause."

(Lightngblt - Bard of Neverwinter)

RELLIKK, The winner of 3 straight NWN Quests:

"The DreadLord. Ahhh what a name. I spied him in the woods. His Ebony Armor gleaming in the Moonlight. His blond locks almost hidden under his Basilisk helm. His dagger….the 9 inch-serrated pillager of souls. His very aura swelled with Evil. His steel grey eyes…..Many a woman, I am sure has lost herself within their depths. So this is what a God whom has chosen to walk the realms looks like. I am impressed." (Madam Sin)

RELLIKK, Legendary PVPer.

First Holder of the WPC belt, WPC Quad Champ, Multiple singles and doubles ladder champ. RELLIKK has a 1st and 3rd in the Azuth’s Challenge. RELLIKK is the defending champion of the Mech Joust.  RELLIKK came in third to Slayve and Ldy Spawn in the Singles Ladder tourney:

Seeing Sylphee approaching, the DreadLord ripped out his nine inch long serrated dagger and lunged as quick on his feet as a mountain lion. Unable to avoid the disemboweling thrust, Sylphee stopped in her tracks as pink coils of gore tumbled from her gut to be stamped into the ground by RELLIKK’s steel healed boot. Standing there, her life juices flowing onto the ground, Sylphee heard the God of KAAOS laugh, a laugh that sounded horrifyingly like the sudden, bronze note of a bell in hell.

The God of KAAOS wanted her soul. His frightful brain steeped in lore plumbed from the abysses and forbidden to mortals for millions of years, called forth a blast of lightning that fed her soul to the DreadLord as its shattering power burned the life out of her now soulless body.

RELLIKK, GM of KAAOS:

Entering the chaos known as the Hell Hole, RELLIKK bathed in the evil and horror that radiated from its occupants. The roasted body of a woman lay on a nearby table, skin charred, hair burned away. Her heart lay simmering on a plate before Hedron while the brain was set before Ego Prime. To the side Kal Mirage was performing unnatural acts with the dead women’s spleen.

Laughter to curdle the hearts of heroes spilled from the Son of Bane’s mouth as he left his fellow Gods to their evil pleasures and proceeded to his chambers located deep beneath the KAAOS Citadel.

RELLIKK, Romeo to Vixen’s Juliet:

The night sky lay like a hot, caressing, silken blanket over the Neverwinter woods. Stars glittered in diamond splendor against the black sky as the bright orb of a full moon rode in the west.

Bathed in this celestial light was one of the most magnificent creatures seen in realms today. Vixen, her crimson eyes agleam with anticipation, was on the prowl. The thin black fabric of her favorite hunting dress fit perfectly over the curves of her young taunt body and was pulled low enough to reveal her dazzling ebony shoulders and the ruby necklace that was nestled in her cleavage.

The slender Drow Vampiress, with the sculptured form of a goddess, hurried towards her prey. As she ran through the night her dazzling beautiful silver hair swirled behind her, her supple ebony limbs moving in perfect harmony as she reached her destination.

Standing at the edge of clearing, the rising and falling of her perfectly formed chest slowing, Vixen sighted her prey.

In the center of the clearing stood an apparition of death incarnate. From the soles of his black boots to the top of his basilisk horned helmet, RELLIKK was bespattered with large splotches of dusty, crusty blood, sprinkled with pieces of flesh and chips of white bone. His terrible blade, dripping slow, clotting droplets onto the black spiked platemail covering his left leg was being used to shove hunks of crimson gore into his mouth.

But beneath his grime covered blond hair, his steel gray eyes sparkled and a smile of future delight partially erased the look of death that usually covered his weather browned face. When he sighted Vixen approaching, his smile broadened and he raised his bloodslimy gauntleted hand in greeting.

Her incessant hunger temporarily sated, Vixen Faen Tlabbar, vampire priestess of Lloth, lay by the side of her huge lover, nibbling on his neck. She purred in Rellikk's ear with evil glee, as she pressed her ebony body close to his,

The vampiress slid up on top of her evil lover and teased, "Oh, most wicked son of Bane, your treachery and cunning is exceeded only by your prowess as a lover." Rellikk's deep growl echoed like thunder on the massive stones, as he pulled Vixen's voluptuous body close, ready for more....

RELLIKK, The Legend of Neverwinter that Bards sing about:

"You move across the land of the night with magic and sword and luck and skill; You prepare yourself, ready to stalk and fight little do you know your prey has come to kill.

He holds your body as you stumble to the ground and grins as disbelief washes across your face. You realize that it's Death's Minion that you've found as Darkness falls, Rellikk's Eternal Embrace."
