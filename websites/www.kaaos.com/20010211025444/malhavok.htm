  Malhavok arrived in NeverWinter in his late twenties. Headstrong and arrogant, he stalked the countryside, with the sole purpose of making a name for himself. He found NeverWinter a seething pool of chaos, and evil. Yet goodness and honor were prevalent for a time. In these things he remained True Neutral, allying himself with those he deemed worthy to be his equal. He struggled for a time, gaining acceptance as a free-lance assassin but soon started to accomplish his goal; the name Malhavok invoked either fear, respect or hatred, in those he opposed. He became a street-fighter in Port Llast, he became known to many as "Evil One" as he stalked the NeverWinter Woods and the Lost Hills. Yet through his victories and travels, he lacked a purpose. The blood of hundreds on his battle hardened hands garnered him little except prowess. Then suddenly, at the height of his notoriety and power, Malhavok withdrew from the affairs of the folk of NeverWinter, retiring deep within his Tower. For one year he stayed in his tower. Speculation ran wild; was he dead? Did he lose control of a necromantic experiment? Was he traveling the outer planes?

The truth was, Malhavok took to subversion. He spied on all the Guilds, and their members. He knew almost everything about the principal inhabitants of NeverWinter. He gathered his information for his forthcoming purpose. Formation of a Guild the likes of which the Forgotten Realms had never seen. A Guild whose members came and went as they pleased, seeking their own personal plans of conquest, all for the glory of the Guild. He spied, and learned. Watched and waited. Until finally the day came when he emerged from his tower. He purchased the largest Citadel (later being known as the Hell Hole) in NeverWinter with which to house his Guild. There was only one true way of testing one's power in the realms, and that was through PVP. His mind made up, he would form the greatest fighting force the lands had ever known. With this plan in mind, he once gain he withdrew from the daily life of NeverWinter and recruited the core members of KAAOS.  Over the years, KAAOS' many accomplishments speak for themselves.

Today, Malhavok approaches fifty as the gray flecks of white are scattering liberally through his black beard. He stands 6'2, hairless, and dressed in black and cyan battle armor, with a tunic of white dotting the front. One may mistake him for a duke or baron, as the noble sculpting of his chin dictates but their-in lies the deception. Underneath the sophisticated appearance, lurks the mind of a Neutral Evil man, whose talent lies in deception, power and undying loyalty to his creation; KAAOS. It has become the premiere fighting force in Neverwinter under his careful guidance. KAAOS is the guild to beat in nearly every PVP and Quest event in Neverwinter. He wields no weapon in battle, preferring to thrust his hands forward and unleash arcane energies or bash a skull with his bare fists and natural 18 strength. He worships no God, and professes to be a God himself. He likes taunting his opponents into being careless in battle and then unleashing devastating magic's on the fools. His reputation from old, helps immensely in this task. Lately, he has taken to wearing black boots. The right one has large silver spikes covering it and is usually covered with the blood of those he deemed unfit to become or remain KAAOS Gods. ;)
