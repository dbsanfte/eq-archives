The Deathmatch

The rest of the story... In case you bunch of newbs didn't know it, the Deathmatch (Tm) is a KAAOS creation. While on TSN (ImagiNation Network), KAAOS was constantly plagued by wusses, seeking to test themselves against the best (gee, nothin' new!). At the time, PVP on TSN was fairly lame, and those who won or lost a PVP could really only just say "Nyah, Nyah I won! ", as there was no real message boards to announce your victories. Enter the KAAOS Deathmatch. Created by Merculus of KAAOS, The KAAOS Deathmatch, pitted PVP'ers against each other in a REAL on-line life and death struggle against each other. The winner would live, the loser would have to delete his character, never to show his/her face again. These were extreme measures to be sure, but KAAOS claimed enough souls back then to gain a notoriaty unmatched to this day in the area of Deathmatches. Upon coming to Neverwinter, KAAOS's Deathmatch creation was introduced to the citizens of NW and KAAOS found they were sheep waiting to be slaughtered. As Neverwinter Nights 2.0 was unleashed, the programmers (Neverwinter players themselves) took KAAOS' idea and created the infamous "DEATHMATCH CHAMBER", where it resides to this day, in the Triboar Arena. Shoulda been called the KAAOS DEATHMATCH CHAMBER, but at least now you know, "The rest of the story". };)
