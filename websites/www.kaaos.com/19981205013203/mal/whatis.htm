KAAOS - The Ultimate Evil in Neverwinter.

KAAOS is the Premier Guild of Evil Role Players and PVP'ers in Neverwinter Nights. Our guild of Gods and Goddesses primary goal is the corruption of the innocent and the destruction of all that is good. First, and foremost, KAAOS has no rules. A character's own role-play style and personal plans of conquest are highly treasured amongst KAAOS. We do not lay rule upon rule on you. Truly Chaotic individuals would not thrive in an environment where limitations and constraints disrupt the spirit of free-reigning evil.

KAAOS' GOALS
We desire to promote an atmosphere of foreboding terror, fear, and lust in which KAAOS thrives! We will destroy any group or individuals who stand in our way. This usually entails frequently executing those of Good alignments and those that require or follow Law and order in their lives. We will also attack and destroy ANYone who seeks to hinder our progress and objectives.

Alignment is NOT a concern when our goals are threatened. KAAOS desires your soul...and blood...and we will have it at any cost.

HOW TO BECOME A KAAOS MEMBER

KAAOS is unlike most Guilds in it's recruitment of members. First, you must be of any alignment other than Good. Second, you must be "sponsored" into the Guild. This means you must "catch our attention" on-line and either ask to be sponsored (by ESP or E-Mail), or a Guild member will ask if you'd like to be sponsored.

Your PVP skills and/or evil role play ability will be the determining factors in your worthiness to be KAAOS. Once you are sponsored, you will be contacted on the next phase of admittance, to see if you have the merit (and stomach! or spleen!) to be KAAOS! We take great pride in being the best PVP'ers in the Realms, and having some of the best evil role players the lands have ever known. If you are unwilling or unable to be proficient in both, go join a wuss guild. Quantity of members is not our primary concern.

WHAT KAAOS CAN DO FOR YOU

KAAOS is a team of individual characters banded together to bring glory to the spirit of KAAOS. We invade the realms to enjoy ourselves. Special events such as KAAOS HotTub parties and Hot Dog Steaming contests are legendary in their wickedness! The KAAOS Hell Hole is where our members live and meet to plan out our depravities. The Hell Hole features indoor and outdoor running slaves, free flowing soul spritzers, a large tequila collection, a secret recipe for salsa, the infamous KAAOS Train, and one of the realms most feared objects...the Spiked Boot of KAAOS!
