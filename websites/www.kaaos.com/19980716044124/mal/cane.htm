"THE CANING OF ITB WUSS" (1994)

Of course, when one talks about KAAOS' punishment of House ITB, one event stands out in particular...The Caning of ITB Wuss in 1994!  Yes, just as news hit of the punk in Singapore getting his ass caned for vandalism, KAAOS administered it's own Caning punishment to House ITB for Wussisism, right in the middle of NW Square. It was the trial of the decade in Neverwinter. There was a Judge (Judge666), a Prosecutor (Miss Pros), a Defending Attorney (DefAttorny), and even star witnesses (ITB Mom, and ITB Goat). ;)

The trial started only with a few onlookers but news of the hilarious event quickly spread like wildfire throughout the Realms and at least 60 people crowded NW Square to try and get a glimpse of the historic trial. (60 people was a LOT in those days). It was totally un-rehearsed and adlib'ed. The balif, NW Aerich, tried to maintain crowd control, but with each new charge brought against ITB Wuss, the screen was filled with laughter, and things quickly got out of hand.

Everybody who was anybody in those days was in attendance. Kayn was there waving his Golden Staff, but the Judge quickly ordered him arrested for displaying his staff in public. ITB's were there too...and what a sight THIS was: "This is fake! House ITB does not willingly participate in any of this! This is totally against our wishes!", were their cries, to the crowd. But KAAOS would not be denied.

Trying to be "cool" and hoping to play off the event, ITB Silent (GM at the time) took the witness stand and the crowd went wild! A few well placed jabs by Miss Pros, and ITB Silent fled the stand, never to be seen again that night. Finally, the Judge called for the assembled crowd (the jury of course), to render a verdict. One could have foreseen the GUILTY verdict, a mile away ;> So, as Judge666 took up his cane to administer the punishment to ITB Wuss, ITB Mom pleaded for mercy for her wussy son...to no avail. The Caning of ITB Wuss commenced, and one of the funniest damn sights you will ever see in Neverwinter, was etched into the History books of Neverwinter...and now available for your reading pleasure on the KAAOS web page ;>

(A side note to this little event...the ITB's really weren't amused by the Caning: 3 separate TOS violations were levelled against the Gods that evening for...get this..."Trying to ruin the 'good name' of House ITB". Heheheheh!! WHEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE! Needless to say, TOSAdvisor didn't think ITB was a "good name" to begin with and promptly dismissed all such TOS charges. Worship it ;> )
