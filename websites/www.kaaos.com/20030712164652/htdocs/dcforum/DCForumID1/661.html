3. "RE: Private Planetside forum"
You do not need fast twitch if you plan ahead.
Here are a few methods to help you out if you are kind of slow:

Many times you could outfit your guy with infintration "armor" and get good results with a pistol or knife.

Another method is to be prepaired and stand or crouch facing a door in a room that demands defending such a the command counsel room. You know someone is going to try and destroy this room or hack it.

Always aim for the body since this is the main part of a soldier unless you have a splash weapon where you should hit the ground, it can almost be as damaging as a direct hit.

Have a few guys to back you up and avoid using the wrong weapons.

Having a Jackhammer shotgun and a sniper rifle is my combination with the reinforced battle armor. (holds two rifle guns)

Lastly using a heavy tank or vehicle. Does not matter if you are slow with counter-attacks sice you get 2000 to 2500 armor.
