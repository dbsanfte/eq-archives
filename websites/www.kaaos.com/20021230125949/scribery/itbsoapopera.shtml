Report to Ultima: ITB (In The Bond) - My former home in Neverwinter and still a close tie. A group reputed for Pkilling and exceptional organization in Neverwinter since its inception in 1992. Massive organizational abilities and aggressive instincts give these guys a reputation bar none in NWN communities. This guild can easily operate on name alone and MOST of the Pkillers in the game pay a great deal of attention to them.

For several years the guild was essentially the monsters most challenging to kill and people dreamed of one day either becoming or beating them. Everyone (at one time) wanted to be an ITB and only the best could get in. Once this policy changed the guild started going down hill. Currently this guild is more concerned with internal strife involving a fight over power and a lot of personnel problems concerning old leaders who cannot give it up and young members who don't know the first thing about how to run a guild. There are very good people here though. Exceptionally skill and adaptive players. One is writing his own video game and that takes all his time. The current GM (Guild Master) is a former truck driver who used to show up in everyone's home town from time to time drawing us all into a tight group of friends. Another former leader is so far up the butt of all the current and former staffers in NW that he knows just about everything about everyone.... he is, however, somewhat delusional about his popularity. (Ed. note: ROFL!!) One is a staff member at Lucas Art's, a game manufacturer.

Another proposed a revolt to throw out the 10 top (eldest) members a little while ago sending the guild into a huge tailspin ending only because NWN was announced as closing.
This situation prevails in the current mentality of the guild and divisions are insufferable in such a group. Lots of these people (the guild membership) are, in one way or another, being demoralized because of the internal strife and, quite frankly, I don't see an end to it given the personalities involved. As these people become more and more alienated they will begin to break off and look for independent homes. I have notified every one of them that despite previous political adversity I would make them at home in whatever capacity possible here in UO. This might involve you and my "team" or it might not, we'll wait and see what the individual wants. I feel I should let you know that I will go way out of my way to assist any ITB requesting my assistance (without breaking certain confidences of course) No news concerning an "official move" to Ultima Online and they might be going to another game anyway as NWN shuts down.

ITB Faust
