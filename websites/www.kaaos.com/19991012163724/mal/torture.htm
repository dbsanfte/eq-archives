The darkness envelopes you like a shroud.  You cautiously wind your way through the passages of the Hell Hole, when the faint sounds of screams reach your ears. Shouts, curses, bestial gruntings and pure, unadulterated screaming drives you onward, down the hallway.  You round a bend and see a torchlit hallway with a heavy oaken door at the end. You slowly make your way towards the door as the sounds of agony and torment grow louder.

You know immediately you've made a horrible decision.  The door swings open with a loud creak, and the stench of death greets you.  You quickly turn to leave, as the hideous sight before you scares the shit outta you, but the door slams and locks behind you.  The wide-eyed slave stares at you, even as you feel the bile rise in your throat.  Disgust quickly turns to terror as the slave speaks...

"Stay awhile!! HEHEHEHEHEHEHEHEEHE!  The fun's just begun!" cries the KAAOS slave playing with the knife. You have no choice but to obey.
