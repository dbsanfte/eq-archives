KAAOS' success at PVP in Neverwinter needs no introduction.
Neverwinter's turn-based PVP system has yet to be matched
for sheer strategic depth and planning. 

From the moment KAAOS invaded Neverwinter Nights in November of 1994,
the Gods were feared.  Winning Quests, winning PVP events,
ending lives in Deathmatches, gangbanging goodies and humiliating
the pseudo-evil fools who dared challenge them, KAAOS stood
alone in their dominance.  

Many tried, many died.  KAAOS ruled.

Quests
Game knowledge, PVP skill and role-play skill.  These were the ingredients
of winning a Neverwinter Quest.  Large prizes of magic items, 'pearls' and
bragging rights went to the winners of staff and guild sponsored quests.
Six such events took place in Version 2.0 of Neverwinter.
KAAOS' victory's are listed below.  

'Descent into Darkness' Quest

'Black Guard' Quest

'Night Mask' Quest

'Aramos Manor' Quest

Four out of Six, eh?  Could this be why the NWN staff stopped sponsoring Quests?
