"Imperial Declaration of the Crimson Empire!"
LAST EDITED ON Feb-15-01 AT 09:23 PM (PST)

Here Ye, Here Ye, Here Ye!!!

All gather near to hear the proclamation of Patron the Wrathful, Emperor of the Crimson Empire and chosen of the All-Father, Padarrion!

Let it henceforth be known that from this date forward the safehold known as Carlind and the surrounding region known as Carloon are the property of the Crimson Empire.

Soon, Imperial forces will begin occupying the city of Carlind and securing the surrounding countryside know as Carloon. Those who arrive in these lands with peaceful intent will be welcomed and protected under the Imperial banner. Once our Imperial cities are established, such individuals are welcome to use the various training facilities, mercantile, and taverns, available within our walls.

To those who seek harm or disrespect to Imperial citizens. You will be destroyed and driven from our lands through the realization of our philosophy: Ruthless aggression in the face of Adversity.


Any who wish to negotiate terms for residing within Imperial lands must do so by presenting themselves, or their ambassador to paganpaladin@yahoo.com. There you may ask for counsel with the Emperor or his designated representative."

The time of chatter, and threats is near it's end. The time to tread within the fire is at hand.
Serve, or be Crushed!
