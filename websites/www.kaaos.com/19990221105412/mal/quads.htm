TEAM KAAOS
Slayve - Sir Rellik - Hedron - Azimer
1996 Champions of the Neverwinter Guilds PVP Tourney

The young boy sat next to the old man on the steps of the armor shop in Neverwinter. "Tell us the story again, sir" said the excited young boy, and the gathered crowd of about 15 children echoed his request. "Pleeeeease!" they all shouted. The toothless, sightless old beggar merely laughed, wet his dry lips from his wineskin and sat the lad on his knee and began his tale to the throng of children once more...

"These are the tales of legends, boys", said the old man. "The kind you hear passed down from generation to generation. But we live in exciting times, boys, exciting indeed", the old man took another long sip from his wineskin. The wide-eyed children barely breathed, so that they missed none of the story. "This is the tale of the greatest PVP battle the lands have ever known!" he shouted for dramatic effect and the children cheered wildly, but quickly quieted down when he raised his hands. "These events are happening around us right now, and just barely a new moon ago, BDA and KAAOS fought to prove who were truly the best warriors in the realms. MECH teams, UDL teams, ITB teams, Mask teams, OGS teams, and KEF'ers all fell before the might of KAAOS and BDA. None stood in their way, and when the smoke cleared on all the battlefields, there were two decisive victors, and no doubt the two most powerful Guilds had made it to the final battle". The old man heard the children whispering excitedly, and he smiled as he knew he had their complete attention, as he began his legendary tale...

"It was a cool, cloudless day in the Neverwinter Woods. High noon. After the punishment of the Night Mask team by KAAOS, and the destruction of the once powerful ITB's at the hands of the deadly BDA, the two most powerful PVP forces in Neverwinter would clash this day. The Dreadlord of KAAOS, Sir Rellik, entered the North East woods on his jet black stallion, letting his blonde, flowing mane fall around his shoulders as he dismounted. The Demon God, Slayve, seemed to appear out of the thin air from amongst the shadows of the trees, his obsidian flail at the ready. Azimer rode in on his Arabian steed, dismounted, and with a wave of his hand the creature shimmered and disappeared into thin air. The False Prophet of KAAOS arrived last, fashionably tardy. Cloaked in dour gray and holding his Twisted Cross, Hedron waited impatiently for the bloodshed to begin." Another pull from his wineskin and the old man continued...

"The Drow team of Kh0lari, Sylverion, Modalex, and Sagx seemed to ooze gracefully from the shadows and were ready to attack in the blink of an eye. The fight would be amongst giant scorpions and deadly remhoraz'es. Slayve entered first and quickly ran to the left and Globed. In came Sagx, Sir Rellik, Modalex with no incidents, and each globed in turn. Sylverion appeared in the midst of the Remo's and fate played it's fickle hand. Sylverion's appearance seemed to startle the giant worm-like beasts and one attacked Sylverion and it's deadly maw was wrapped around Sylverion's torso before he could even react. In one long gulp, Sylverion was gone. The Gods of KAAOS laughed! Yet they knew the battle was far from over. Fumbles, Stink Clouds, and holds flew as the BDA'ers tried to overcome their one man deficit. Slayve and Azimer cornered Modalex from the others and quickly set about pummeling him. He was nearly dead as he Dimension Door'd away to catch his breath. Slayve quickly followed with a DD of his own, as he would not be denied his kill. Meanwhile, Kholari came under the effects of Rellik's Hold spell and things looked grim until Sagx freed his Held companion. Hedron cast Lightning at Sagx and Kh0lari, but both Globes were still intact. Sagx and Kholari quickly surrounded Hedron and started battering him with their deadly drow magic.

Hedron staggered free and headed for the gravely wounded Modalex. He would get one kill in before his own demise, he vowed. As Kholari ran back to Hedron, Azimer and Slayve once again surrounded Modalex and repeated their deadly assault on the wounded Drow. Hedron, sensing his death was near, unleashed a final devastating blow to Kholari just as the drow sent him to the gate. No sooner had Hedron fallen, than Azimer delivered the killing blow to Modalex. Kholari now sorely wounded tried to Hold Slayve, Azimer and Rellikk but failed. After an ill placed attack by Slayve, Sir Rellik's nine inch serrated dagger ended Kholari's time in the battle and the Gods smiled, now knowing it was but a matter of time. Sagx sent a Lightning Bolt through Slayve and Rellik, but they retaliated with magic of their own and Sagx ran for cover under their deadly assault. The chase was on! Sagx's guerilla takes proved effective for a few rounds, but once surrounded, he was quickly overwhelmed. Azimer, Rellik and Slayve battered him down and in a final scream of "KAAOS!!!", Sagx's bloody body was sent gateward and the Gods of KAAOS stood victorious!"

The children remained silent for a second more, then let out a boisterous, wild cheer. They jumped up and down and started a "mock PVP" amongst themselves. They laughed and shouted as the images of the legendary battle were still fresh in their minds. The young boy on the old man's lap merely looked at him, "What happened then?" he asked innocently. The old man took the boy from his knee and raised his weary bones to stand. "That is a question which only the future may know the answer to. There shall be other struggles and there shall be other warriors to test the mettle of KAAOS in the future, and other tales shall be told about those battles too. Be patient young one, the best is yet to come." said the old man as he picked up his wineskin. The boy pondered a moment, and jumped up excitedly, "Thanks mister!" he yelled and bolted to where his friends were still playing.

From beneath his hooded cloak, the old man's mouth formed into a sinister grin as he stood upright, dropped the wineskin to the ground and sight returned to his eyes. He pulled the hood back and anyone who would have been watching from the shadows would have seen Doc Krypto there, laughing, and reveling in the glory of KAAOS.

Death by KAAOS
Kal Mirage - Ego Prime - Xylina - War Knight
1997 Champions of the Neverwinter Guilds PVP Tourney

As darkness began to fall over the realms, with it, the sounds of the creatures of the night began to grow louder. The sounds of men hunting the dangerous creatures and beasts, the sounds of the these beasts hunting the men, the sounds of battle, the sounds of pain, the sounds of bloodshed, and most importantly, the sounds of evil deeds in progress.

Amongst these sounds, one other distinct and unmistakable sound could be heard.

The sound of KAAOS.

Their group, consisting of seven Gods and one Goddess, was on the prowl. Whereever they went, death followed close behind. A trail of bloody footprints could be seen marking the soft earth of the Neverwinter Woods as the group moved away from the scene of another of their merciless slaughters.

As they moved away from the carnage, everyone turned to glance back at their work one last time.. a pile of six mutilated corpses.. two of them charred beyond recognition, and one no more than a pile of bones and ashes. Puddles of blood pooled on the ground around the bodies and all around, rocks and trees were spotted with the crimson red droplets of these unfortunate travellers. One of the corpses clothes seemed to be missing also, the result of some perverted act also.

The group of KAAOSers began moving on, but for some reason, Slayve was eyeing War Knight and his face was twisted into a demonic looking sneer. The two had a few words and then Slayve pushed War Knight away and they began heading back to the KAAOS Hell Hole. ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Once back at the Hell Hole, everyone began bragging about their take of Souls for the evening. The Dreadlord was fiddling with his serrated dagger and slashing across the air, reenacting a morbid scene in front of the kneeling slaves, ITB Legion and ITB Silver.

In the middle of the large hall, though, War Knight and Slayve were pushing each other again. They were yelling back and forth about who had rights to the soul of one of the wusses they had killed. As the pushes became shoves, the rest of the room began to break up into two factions around the two bickering Gods.

Kal Mirage, Ego Prime and Xylina all moved behind War Knight, while Sir Rellik, Tyo Ark and Krynal gather behind Slayve. Then everyone began yelling back and forth at everyone else soon enough everyone was pushing everyone. Just as it seemed as if it was about to come to some nice honest fighting to settle the dispute, War Knight raised his hand and spoke in his loud booming voice, "There is only one way to settle this.."

Turning, he pointed to the corner where a large keg of his finest ale sat. Everyone traded looks of confusion until War threw a large mug at Slayve. The demon God snatched the mug out of the air and pushed his way through the mass of KAAOSers, but instead of tapping the keg, he just looked at his mug.. and it filled with a dark murky liquid with tendril of smoke rising off of it.

Everyone began to understand and they all fetched different liquids and substances, mugs, shot glasses, and the like. Within seconds, shots were being downed, new kegs were being tapped, people were yelling back and forth and slamming their mugs down as they called for the slaves to bring them more.

ITB Legion hurriedly supplied the Dreadlord's team with more drinks, while ITB Silver ran back and forth trying to keep all of Kal's team's glasses full. Insults were being thrown every which way, along with empty mugs and other containers.

Then the first casualties began to occurr. Xylina threw down three more shots of a red, glowing liquid, but then she started to fall back in her chair. The sound of Sir Rellik laughing at her, "Heh! Heh!," brought her back to her senses long enough for her to shoot him an icy stare and hurl all three shot glasses, in succession, at the Dreadlord's head. One after another, all three hit Rellik right in between the eyes. Then in unison, the both of them fell to the floor, slipping under the table.

Next to go was Tyo Ark. After having downed another mug of War's ale, he leaned back in his chair, two feet off the ground, then he belched. The walls shook with the might of his expulsion of the gaseous bubble in his stomach. The force of the vibrations caused his chair to rock back and his head slammed against the stone floor. His eyes rolled back into his head and blood started pooring from his nose.

Followed by the vibrations of his belch, was a hideous odor. Everyone else's noses furrowed and tehy shook it off. All except War Knight, who was holding a large keg of ale of his head and chugging it. The foul odor caused his knees to buckle and he fell to the floor. The keg slipped from his hands and came crashing down right on his head, then he flopped to the floor with a thump, his head buried inside the keg.

Slayve started to smirk and called Ego over for a moment. Ego wondered what was going on but followed anyways. Then Slayve bent over and another loud noise rocked the Hell Hole, followed by an even fouler odor. Kal and Krynal dove under the tables to stay out of the direct path of the very deadly cloud of gas that had been created. Ego Prime couldnt escape though. He inhaled deeply and the fell face first to the ground, landing right on top of ITB Silver. Ego pinned her to the ground and held her there as he slipped into consciousness.

Slayve, however, let out another loud sound, however this time it was more than just gas. With a pained look on his face, he ran from the room, howling and swearing in some sort of harsh demonic tongue. As the air cleared and the smell disipated, only Kal Mirage and Krynal remained.

Both of these two gods kept slammed down mugs of ale and shots of powerful soulspritzers. Many times it looked as if Kal was about to fall back in his chair, but he kept holding his own against Krynal. Then when it seemed like neither of them would ever succumb to the liquor, Krynal started to cough and sputter, then ducked his head under the table.

Kal Mirage slammed down one last drink as Krynal continued to puke and vomit all over the Hell Hole floor. When the sounds of his puking stopped, he just collapsed under the table. Kal then stood up and raised his mug, proclaiming their victory to his fallen comrades.

He then walked over and lifted his passed out wife into his arms, then grabbed a bottle of some blue liquid and headed off into the slightly cleaner and less smelling confines of the Hell Hole, leaving a room full of drunken, passed out Gods under the table.

As Kal carried Xylina into his chambers and flopped her down onto the bed, the only other sounds he could hear throughout the Hell Hole were the sounds of ITB Silver pleaing for help and Slayve growling in frustration as he ran back and forth down the hallway every few moments, followed by a disgusting fecal odor. ~~~~~~~~~~~~~ KAAOS Team Two drinks KAAOS Team One under the table!
