The HellHole, the center of nothing, the roiling intestinal tract of the multiverse, where the hapless souls reaped by the eternal KAAOS harvest are shitted out, finally embracing misery-neverending.
The HellHole, abode to such dreams & nightmares that even the very blasted earth that encrusts the toxic plane in grim obsidian hues seems to resonate with its entropic energies.
The HellHole, sanctuary, abattoir, and gathering place for the God’s & Goddess simply refereed to as KAAOS. KILLING AS AN ORGANIZED SPORT.
Within it’s wretched chambers the immortal beings cavort, each trying to out do they’re loose-knit brethren in act’s of depravity that would be enough to bring knot’s to the most callous individuals stomach, bucking and dry heaving with unabashed horror.
Many come through this pocket dimension, some as victims, others as would-be conquerors, and others as worshippers offering supplication to their legendary deities. Yet, in the end each and everyone one is twisted into the infamous meat-puppet’s, empty shell’s crafted for the amusement of the homicidal KAAOS Gods.
Some, a fragile handful try and wrest away from the piles of ripening refuse- Fighting across boundless mountains of rotting, twitching and madly gibbering corpses. They arrive at the HellHole’s gates occasionally, striking against the midnight doors for admittance.
They claim to know KAAOS. They claim to be KAAOS.
Understand this, KAAOS is NOT an image, it’s NOT a style, it’s IS a STATE OF BEING. You just aren’t KAAOS, you ARE KAAOS, those God’s & Goddesses are personifications of evil- each of it’s many facet’s reflected from they’re blood crazed orb’s.
Those that (claim) to be KAAOS, those that (act) like KAAOS, are dragged howling & weeping into the depth’s of the HellHole’s darkness, envying the mindless nature bestowed upon the meat puppets
