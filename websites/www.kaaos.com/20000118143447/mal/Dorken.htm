Darkenbane's Suffering

From late mid-May to late-June of 1999, the EQ "guild" Darkenbane sought to test their
mettle against the Gods of KAAOS in Everquest, and we had a nice lil WAR. The eventual
outcome was the request of a Ceasefire from the Darkenbane leader, Graraige, to the
Gods of KAAOS.  Despite having overwhelming odds in their favor, and enlisting the aid
of at least three other Everquest guilds (Dark Brigade, Blood Coven and The Mercs),
Darkenbane asked us to call it quits.  Of course the usual reply of HELL NO! was given. ;)
