Slayve du Mortere stood at the edge of the cliff. Gulls screeched overhead and dove into the blue waters in search of food. He was dressed in his white ether-armor, as his rank of Patriarch Demi-Demon accorded. Yet, he did not look like a demon. From all outward appearance he was a man, a very pale man, but still, he appeared to be human. He spoke like a man, he ate like a man, he even slept like a man. He was a man... to a point. He was one of the fortunate of the Abyss. He had no physical oddities, such as wings or horns, which many of his brethren possessed. Most of his demon characteristics were simply on areas hidden by clothing. His conception was a strange one. A female demon raping a human male was uncommon to say the least. Normally, it were the males who lusted for human women. This was most likely the reason only part of his stomach and half of his left leg were covered with a thick, scaly black hide. He had even been able to bed several human woman, in darkly lit whore houses, with no notice of his demon qualities. He carrys a black and white +2 Mirrored Shield. His weapon, FleshFlayer, is a totally black, obsidian flail made of magical steel and spiked with short black barbs. He whirls his flail over his head and releases his devastating magics through it.

The wind whipped his black cloak and he pulled it tight about him. The chilly evening air cut through him as the white foam of the ocean beat against the rocks of the shore below. "The upper world is much too cold for my liking" he spoke aloud as he shivered in the early fall air. Slayve hated the cold, but he did not hate these KAAOS Gods who summoned him from the Abyss, as he had in the days soon after his summons. He had loathed the upper world and all who dwelled in it. But soon, after partaking of the bloody offerings, unholy sex and depraived rituals of KAAOS, he began to like this strange, new world. He soon found he had a talent for human and elven magic, and his inhuman ego drove him to compete against these mortal fools in Neverwinter. He quickly gained prowess by beating down any who stood in his way. He soon became the most feared, and most decorated warrior in the entire Forgotten Realms. And, as unlikely as it seemed, he owed it all to this gang of murderers called KAAOS.

A gull whirled overhead. Slayve looked up to see it had it's dinner of fish lodged in it's beak as it flew through the crisp fall, evening air. He blinked and the air around the gull dove up and rammed the creature to the ground. It's body splattered as it hit the hard surface of the cliff. Slayve du Mortere glared down at the broken creature and marveled at it.

"Nothing like you exists in the Abyss, creature. So beautiful... so frail".

He bent down and scraped the remains of the dead bird into his palm, being careful to get every ounce of blood and tissue present, and sucked down the carcass in one savage gulp. He licked the juice from his fingers, and gazed at the sinking sun on the horizon, drinking in it's beauty one last time. He gathered the evening breeze and flew southward, back toward the Hell Hole.
