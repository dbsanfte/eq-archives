Long ago....

Down in the cold, dark chamber, at the center of awareness that enveloped the Fortress that joined with and unsettled the magic, he lay. His blood had been spreading in a large pool for many hours and was beginning to cake into the many grooves between the stones of the floor. The gaping hole in this chest, where the bones stuck sharply out at all angles, had long since stopped sucking at the air. His eyes were open, but he did not see. He did not blink, or breathe, did not smell, or hear. His hair was long and white, his beard the same color and his skin was mottled, wrinkled and pale. Rot had appeared in several places especially around the ragged chest wound. Ego Prime, the last of a line of powerful sorcerers, a sect within the Roman Catholic church, on his last breathe had opened the doors to hell from which emerged the true shadows, demon things born of brimstone and death.

Not so long ago....

The Gods of KAAOS roamed the many dark corridors of the Hell Hole above. Soul spritzers, the hot tub, the screams ot tortured souls, the bone thrown becoming another worshiper, Sol slaves pampering their Gods, Doc Krypto's lab, the Evil within these confines reached out to me like an ITB chasing a save. Thum-tum A trill went out into the awareness and all above felt it, through yards of stone, like a pre-orgasmic shiver. Thum-tum, Thum-tum The newly formed heart rattled along like a freight train. Speeding out of control to do a job it was never meant for. It screamed at the trauma of the pain, but kept drumming as a thin film of muscle spread across the open wound. Around the body, blood that had dried, scabbing the stone floor was wet again, warm again. And just as it had slowly seeped from him, pooling on the floor so now it was absorbed by his skin, through his pores, flowing back into his body, like the tide rolling in. With the evil coursing through my veins and the mystical powers returned, I was drawn to the portal of the God's lair and there I materialized only to scare a Sol slave. A sword entered through my back and out my stomach, and I vomited blood, hunching over. The Gods stood there silently and watched. I turned fast, my movement pulling the sword from the attackers grasp, then reached around him to pull the blade from my body. “That......fucking......hurt!” I said, swinging the blade down into the shoulder of my attacker, a female Sol. The sword sank deep into her body, cracking the collarbone and splintering the ribs. I turned and faced the others as my wounds healed. A smile spread across their collective faces and I knew I was home.
