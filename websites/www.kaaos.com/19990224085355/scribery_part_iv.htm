A BRIEF HISTORY OF KAAOS

Part IV: "The Future of the Gods"

Sir Rellik now leads the Gods and Goddesses of KAAOS into prosperous existance. Having retired as GM, Malhavok now dedicates much of his time to insuring the recruitment process of KAAOS remains intact and the guild's reputation as the "Ultimate Evil in Neverwinter" remains strong. Tuesday night Hell Hole's are still a highlight and a tradition, where soul spritzers, troll weed and the promise of "LIVE EVIL!" bring as many as 20 KAAOS Gods and 10 worshippers (a.k.a. KAAOS wanna-be's), to the private room where KAAOS holds court. Now, Neverwinter is gone...crushed by corporate America. Rest assured the Gods of KAAOS will continue. Wherever they decide to next reign, they will go in full force.
