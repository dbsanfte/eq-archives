1997 PvP Quad Champions

Death by KAAOS
Kal Mirage - War Knight - Ego Prime - Xylina

As darkness began to fall over the realms, with it, the sounds of the creatures of the night began to grow louder. The sounds of men hunting the dangerous creatures and beasts, the sounds of the these beasts hunting the men, the sounds of battle, the sounds of pain, the sounds of bloodshed, and most importantly, the sounds of evil deeds in progress.

Amongst these sounds, one other distinct and unmistakable sound could be heard.

The sound of KAAOS.

Their group, consisting of seven Gods and one Goddess, was on the prowl. Whereever they went, death followed close behind. A trail of bloody footprints could be seen marking the soft earth of the Neverwinter Woods as the group moved away from the scene of another of their merciless slaughters.

As they moved away from the carnage, everyone turned to glance back at their work one last time.. a pile of six mutilated corpses.. two of them charred beyond recognition, and one no more than a pile of bones and ashes. Puddles of blood pooled on the ground around the bodies and all around, rocks and trees were spotted with the crimson red droplets of these unfortunate travellers. One of the corpses clothes seemed to be missing also, the result of some perverted act also.

The group of KAAOSers began moving on, but for some reason, Slayve was eyeing War Knight and his face was twisted into a demonic looking sneer. The two had a few words and then Slayve pushed War Knight away and they began heading back to the KAAOS Hell Hole.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Once back at the Hell Hole, everyone began bragging about their take of Souls for the evening. The Dreadlord was fiddling with his serrated dagger and slashing across the air, reenacting a morbid scene in front of the kneeling slaves, ITB Legion and ITB Silver.

In the middle of the large hall, though, War Knight and Slayve were pushing each other again. They were yelling back and forth about who had rights to the soul of one of the wusses they had killed. As the pushes became shoves, the rest of the room began to break up into two factions around the two bickering Gods.

Kal Mirage, Ego Prime and Xylina all moved behind War Knight, while Sir Rellik, Tyo Ark and Krynal gather behind Slayve. Then everyone began yelling back and forth at everyone else soon enough everyone was pushing everyone. Just as it seemed as if it was about to come to some nice honest fighting to settle the dispute, War Knight raised his hand and spoke in his loud booming voice, "There is only one way to settle this.."

Turning, he pointed to the corner where a large keg of his finest ale sat. Everyone traded looks of confusion until War threw a large mug at Slayve. The demon God snatched the mug out of the air and pushed his way through the mass of KAAOSers, but instead of tapping the keg, he just looked at his mug.. and it filled with a dark murky liquid with tendril of smoke rising off of it.

Everyone began to understand and they all fetched different liquids and substances, mugs, shot glasses, and the like. Within seconds, shots were being downed, new kegs were being tapped, people were yelling back and forth and slamming their mugs down as they called for the slaves to bring them more.

ITB Legion hurriedly supplied the Dreadlord's team with more drinks, while ITB Silver ran back and forth trying to keep all of Kal's team's glasses full. Insults were being thrown every which way, along with empty mugs and other containers.

Then the first casualties began to occurr. Xylina threw down three more shots of a red, glowing liquid, but then she started to fall back in her chair. The sound of Sir Rellik laughing at her, "Heh! Heh!," brought her back to her senses long enough for her to shoot him an icy stare and hurl all three shot glasses, in succession, at the Dreadlord's head. One after another, all three hit Rellik right in between the eyes. Then in unison, the both of them fell to the floor, slipping under the table.

Next to go was Tyo Ark. After having downed another mug of War's ale, he leaned back in his chair, two feet off the ground, then he belched. The walls shook with the might of his expulsion of the gaseous bubble in his stomach. The force of the vibrations caused his chair to rock back and his head slammed against the stone floor. His eyes rolled back into his head and blood started pooring from his nose.

Followed by the vibrations of his belch, was a hideous odor. Everyone else's noses furrowed and tehy shook it off. All except War Knight, who was holding a large keg of ale of his head and chugging it. The foul odor caused his knees to buckle and he fell to the floor. The keg slipped from his hands and came crashing down right on his head, then he flopped to the floor with a thump, his head buried inside the keg.

Slayve started to smirk and called Ego over for a moment. Ego wondered what was going on but followed anyways. Then Slayve bent over and another loud noise rocked the Hell Hole, followed by an even fouler odor. Kal and Krynal dove under the tables to stay out of the direct path of the very deadly cloud of gas that had been created. Ego Prime couldnt escape though. He inhaled deeply and the fell face first to the ground, landing right on top of ITB Silver. Ego pinned her to the ground and held her there as he slipped into consciousness.

Slayve, however, let out another loud sound, however this time it was more than just gas. With a pained look on his face, he ran from the room, howling and swearing in some sort of harsh demonic tongue. As the air cleared and the smell disipated, only Kal Mirage and Krynal remained.

Both of these two gods kept slammed down mugs of ale and shots of powerful soulspritzers. Many times it looked as if Kal was about to fall back in his chair, but he kept holding his own against Krynal. Then when it seemed like neither of them would ever succumb to the liquor, Krynal started to cough and sputter, then ducked his head under the table.

Kal Mirage slammed down one last drink as Krynal continued to puke and vomit all over the Hell Hole floor. When the sounds of his puking stopped, he just collapsed under the table. Kal then stood up and raised his mug, proclaiming their victory to his fallen comrades.

He then walked over and lifted his passed out wife into his arms, then grabbed a bottle of some blue liquid and headed off into the slightly cleaner and less smelling confines of the Hell Hole, leaving a room full of drunken, passed out Gods under the table.

As Kal carried Xylina into his chambers and flopped her down onto the bed, the only other sounds he could hear throughout the Hell Hole were the sounds of ITB Silver pleaing for help and Slayve growling in frustration as he ran back and forth down the hallway every few moments, followed by a disgusting fecal odor.
