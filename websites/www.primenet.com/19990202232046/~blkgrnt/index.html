A Note From the Publisher: Welcome to the February 1998 edition of 256 Shades of Grey. Your Web choice for fiction, poetry and essays. The February 1998 edition of 256 celebrates it's third year in operation. Unfortunately, a paper 'zine copy of 256 will not be available till the latter part of 1998. So, for this month I have a smattering of new poems from around the globe and some new fiction from Prague, plus you can see many of our old monthly editions. I am very happy that 256 remains a global entity as well as a regional (Wisconsin based) 'zine. So all I ask for you to do is to sit back, relax, and enjoy some wonderful reading.... And don't forget to read some of my poetry under the pseudonym C. J. Kurkowski. Let us know what you think. Happy reading.



Clifford J. Kurkowski--Publisher
