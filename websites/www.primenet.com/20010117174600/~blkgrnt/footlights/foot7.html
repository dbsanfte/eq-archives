Días Contados

(Numbered Days)

Installment # 2

Días Contados is a modern Spanish novel set in Madrid. I read this novel for the first time in 1992 when I was traveling in Spain. Its author, Juan Madrid, is famous throughout Spain for his incisive and sensitive journalism which has captured the spirit of change in his country since the death of Franco. Indeed, Spain is a country of great change, especially in the changes that took place during "la movida" in the late seventies up through the mid eighties. Días Contados is particularly graphic in capturing life in Madrid at the end of "la movida." Here is the second installment of our serialization.
-Matthew Cornetta, Translator.
