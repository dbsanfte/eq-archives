HOTDOG BOY


by Amanda Fredericks


Tony boy,
pony boy,
you big ‘ole hotdog
muncher
if I were big and bold
and brave and burly
I would punch her.
You know you would,
you Tony boy
you eat that macaroni
Funky man,
funky you,
you dancing masticiolli
Super dude,
super high,
just a-fingernail bit’n
growing roses in your tambourine
living for the fight’n
