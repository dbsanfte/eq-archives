An Eternal Question

Sarah McPeck


I've always had this notion about the way that things would be.
I've always felt a bond that was special between you and me.
You opened up my soul and haved dared to walk inside;
but is this a place where you learn to look...
or a place for you to hide?
Emotions cease and all feeling quits.
I feel like a speckle of earth
that moves none too fast or none too slow.
As the life of love transcends to the sky, is it me you love-
or
will our love just die.
