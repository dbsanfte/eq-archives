WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO BELONG TO THE CRIMSON SKY EMPIRE?
By Xammer99 of the CSE/EQ Faction

 

What does it mean to belong to the Crimson Sky Empire?

Our Motto is "Warrior to Warrior, yours in life and death". We stand together, and stand for honor, bravery, and loyalty. Further our honor and loyalty extend beyond our brothers and sisters within this corps, it extends to the community around us. Our duty is to provide a shining example of dignity and honor to the world, and for the world to know that when they see the name Crimson Sky, they know that the person bearing it is worthy of their trust.

Belonging to CSE means standing for something bigger than yourself.

It means showing respect & honor towards every person they meet and encounter. Be polite.

It means holding yourself above the greedy & petty people in the world, and do their best to set a better example.

It means showing restraint in times of anger, so as to maintain poise, dignity, and honor. If you are angered, think before you speak, for harsh words leave a deep impression. Speak and act with honor. This is especially true when communicating via guild means (tells, guild chat, message board, or e-mail)

It means showing faith and loyalty to their brothers and sisters in the empire. Do not seek to profit off of them, but do not beggar yourself either.

We should strive be self-sufficient, however there are times when aid is needed. Do not hesitate to ask if the need is there, but do not become selfish in your demands upon the group. We are here to help each other, but our goal is fun for everyone, not fun for one.

We adhere to both the spirit and letter of the laws of Everquest. Kill stealing, ninja-looting, harassment of any sort, and other such offenses will not be tolerated from our members.

We should do the utmost to enjoy themselves, but never at the expense of others.

Only sponsor those they truly think would be worthy of bearing our name and would bring honor too it. Do not sponsor those to whom you have doubts. Further if you are sponsoring someone, be prepared to answer in detail to the guilding officer exactly why it is you wish to sponsor them.

----

That is what I expect from each and every member of this guild. Last night I was VERY proud to hear of what was being done with two class specific items. That made me feel a great deal of pride in my guildmates and knowing that tough choices in the past were indeed the right ones.

I'm filled with pride over the trouble and work guild members are goin through to help each other out, and if they got something another guildmate can and will use, letting them have it. That is great, and I'm a huge fan of taking care of our brothers and sisters. When I see a someone giving a spell (probably the most highly prized thing there is) that someone needs to a guildmate, again I'm filled with pride. Its this honor, generosity, and integrity that makes me proud to be in this guild. That is what I expect from each and every one of you.

When you wear the name "Crimson Sky" you don't just represent yourself, you represent all of us. Bring honor to our name, and make sure that people understand that when they see the guild tag "Crimson Sky" they know that that person is a good person, someone who will go to bat for the team, someone who will follow through, someone who will give their best regardless of the reward. The greatest reward of any deed is the satisfaction of knowing that you made someone else happy.

I know no one can always be the way I expect people to act, however I demand that everyone make every attempt to try their best to live up to those expectations.

Warrior To Warrior, Yours In life And Death.
