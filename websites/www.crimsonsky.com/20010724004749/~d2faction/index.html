The latest Diablo II Faction news

Welcome to the Crimson Sky Empire Diablo2 Faction! | Posted by JaxLarz @ 9:00pm EST

Wind blew through the forest like a wayward stranger caught within a dream of abstract. The sky having gone dusk, glowed a crimson amber hue. Forward in his vision, the cloaked figure approached the gates bearing the sigils reflecting the ominous silence of mages and warriors long since forgotten by the desolate landscape.

As he neared the keep, his mount turned an eye to the tower as a single stroke of lightning divided the night. Were it not for his pressing mission, the figure might have stood to reflect upon his past here. He entered the courtyard and proceeded directly to the door opposite.

From the tower a young man watched as the shadow entered. Though he expected the sage, he was still surprised when he appeared. A chill entered the room as the door opened... the figure all of seven feet stood quietly- eyes penetrating through the young man.

The mage took a step forward and began to speak, but paused as if the words stuck in his throat.

"What have you come to tell me ... has my uncle returned?"

"Forgive me, though i've carried the news for several days, I have not yet spoken the words... and the words weigh heavily upon my heart"

The young man cut his next thought short... this was not the tone of good news.

"My uncle is dead?"

The Mage advanced upon the boy, realizing that he had watched him grow up.

"Not exactly..."

Jaxlarz gazed upon the sphere that the mage held before him. It was clear and dark and seemed to have depth within it, suggesting that it contained much more space than it's exterior offered. It seemed to take in the light of the room, giving nothing back leaving the room dim and quiet.

"Look closer"

He did, and for just a second he could have sworn he saw a lightning-shaped spark deep within.

"We have little time, the energy containing him isn't infinite and our window of opportunity is smaller still."
