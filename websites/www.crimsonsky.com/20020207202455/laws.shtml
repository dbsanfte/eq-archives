LAWS OF THE EMPIRE

CODE OF HONOR

  1. Loyalty and duty to the Crimson Sky Empire above all.
  2. Hold the welfare of the Empire above that of the yourself and the individual.
  3. Protect and serve all members of the Empire with your life.
  4. Avenge any attacks against the Empire or its members, to attack one is to attack all.
  5. Assist your guild and brothers and sisters when in need without question.
  6. Show honor and courage in times of adversity.
  7. Kill not without provocation, or reason.
  8. Never steal, loot, or cheat.
  9. Protection of the innocent and good.

CRIMSON SKY EMPIRE CHARTER:

The Crimson Sky Empire is a guild devoted to fair play within the gaming worlds we inhabit. We ensure the maximum enjoyment within those games, not only for ourselves, but also for the honorable players we coexist with. We are a guild firmly set on a path of upholding the virtues of Honor, Loyalty, and Justice. We do not condone mindless player killing, bug-exploiting, or any other aspects of play that are detrimental to the experience of other players and not in the spirit of the game design. Our members are aligned to that of warriors, fighting for our ideals, and in the name of CSE. The Crimson Sky Empire is a large tight knit family, one where death is honorable, and living only gives oneself the chance to meet such an honorable fate for our cause.

 

Building Policies:

The Crimson Sky Empire and it's respective Factions will always build and maintain a least one structure unique to the game and faction for the use of all members as a meeting place and staging point. If a guild owned building is not feasible due to game restraints, a public building or location must be chosen for meetings and gatherings.

Trust Tax:

Regardless of which game or which combination of games you play, there will come a time when you have amassed more equipment and wealth then necessary. It is request that any items, materials, etc that you may acquire in excess you donate to the guild. We do not enforce a weekly or monthly tax nor do we expect everyone to be able to give. It is suggested however that you help when possible. Your donations help new members, allow the guild to grow, and acquire communal property for the benefit of all.

We call this our "Trust Tax" because there is no set amount or a limitation. We ask that you donate as much as you can, knowing that it is for a good cause. If we notice that you never give to our Trust Fund... eventually, this will creep up on you.

Responsibilities of Faction Treasurers/Quartermasters:

Record all transactions.
Provide notes regarding purchases, loans, etc.
Storage of all monies.
Recommend awards/trophies for exceptional member donations

RULES OF CONDUCT:

Policy on Player Killing and Murderers:

The unjustified killing of other players by members of the Guild will not be tolerated by the leadership, and conversely the Guild will not tolerate any other players who engage in this activity. While we believe that such crimes should not go unpunished, we do not enforce it upon our members to hunt player killers, and neither do we condemn those members who choose to do so. We see this matter as one of personal or group choice, and will do all within our power to see that the hunting of player killers remains so. However the right to chose whether or not to actively engage in PvP, in no way relieves members of the codes they have sworn upon to uphold when they joined the guild which includes defending their fellow brothers and sisters with their own life.

Policy on Looting:

It is simply asked that you exercise good judgment when looting fallen foes, player or computer controlled during a combined effort on the behalf of you and fellow members, or that of a apparent abandoned corpse. It is good practice to give whatever you can to help your fellow members, or those in true need, this is a great sign of compassion and charity, and is highly valued in our ranks.

This Empire is rich in resources, the key to our success is sharing and making sure that everyone is taken care of.

Participation:

The Crimson Sky Empire's reputation is well known to the online gaming world. We wish to keep this so. The only way to accomplish this task is by member participation. We expect that all members will be active participants not only in within the guild but within the community. This does not mean we expect to see you logged in to your specific game thirty-five hours a week. On the contrary, we realize that real life often throws each of us a curve from time to time. If you cannot be active on an in-game basis, we expect for you to at least remain active via the message board, the mailing list, or in some other of guild approved involvement. It is requested that if you require some time away from the online world in general you inform your High Lord of your hiatus personally. During this time, you will be placed into an inactive status until you once again return to your online family. Again, we shall never penalize anyone for issues that occur in real life. As a family, we expect you to take care of the issues that arise and while doing so know that we remain here always, ready to aid you in any way we can.

Leaving Our Guild:

If at any time you wish to be removed from our guild, simply email your Faction Leader, or Guildmaster as listed in "Contact Us". This however is not to be taken lightly, and members who so quickly renounce their membership, only to wish to rejoin again may be disciplined. By leaving you will be giving up all rank, status, access, and privileges within the guild. If you ever wish to rejoin us you must do so as an initiate, providing your leave was on good terms. We will not give you a hard time about leaving - things come up and we will respect your wishes.

Negative Guild Actions:

  1. Disrespect of the leaders, officers, and founding members of the guild.
  2. Personally insulting or slandering of guild members.
  3. Lying, falsifying evidence, to leaders or officers of the guild.
  4. Accepting or giving of gifts for personal gain in status or favors.
  5. Giving information to other Guilds regarding the CSE or its tactics and strategies.
  6. Intentionally causing harm to guild members or diplomats.
  7. Granting anyone access to restricted member areas unless instructed to do so by the Guild Master.
  8. Rude statements, foul language, childish vernacular, around people who do not appreciate it, or are of age.
  9. Improper use or abuse of ingame guild resources (i.e. Housing, Guildstones, Ingame chat, etc.) or out of game resources (i.e. Intranet, mailing lists, forums, etc.)
  10. Disregard or ignorance of guild policies.
  11. Inactivity.

The above actions should be considered guidelines for unacceptable behavior that can result in banishment from the guild. It is the responsibility of the Guildmaster and Faction High Lord to interpret member actions when making judgment.

Interaction with Other Guilds:

Any interaction with another guild in regards to forming an alliance or a joint operation should first be referred to the Factions High Lord, 2nd in command, or diplomatic advisors. At no stage should a member of the Guild make any promises to assist another guild or form an alliance without approval from one of those sources.

Rule and Policy Violations:

Any member found to be in violation of guild policy and/or rules will be first issued a warning. Upon the second violation the individual will stand in judgment before the High Council. The strongest penalty is banishment from the guild.

Rule and Policy Additions:

Each faction of CSE may have additional policies/rules based on the game mechanics of that world. It is encouraged you to read over this policy page, then visit the faction in which you seek to join or are already a member. Review the specific faction policies in depth. If you have any questions about the policies whatsoever, please contact Seira@crimsonsky.com, or the respective faction High Lord.
