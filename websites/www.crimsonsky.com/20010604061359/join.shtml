Becoming an Initiate of the Empire:

Before you become an Initiate of this Guild, many things must first take place. We need you to fill out the Application Form for our review. After that, we ask that you meet with several of our members either in game or in ICQ/IRC. We want to meet you in that chat room, and see what your personality is like. Based on that, we will meet up with you IN game, and see how you behave/act when in different situations. This step can take days or weeks, that depends on the impression you make with our members.

We look for several characteristics in a person AND his online persona. To name just a few, we seek those who are loyal, dedicated, honorable, responsible, professional, caring, brave, honest, giving, and this list goes on and on. If you believe in these things, you are one step ahead of becoming a member to our Empire.

The Pre-Initiation Phase takes as long as needed. It is really up to you. If you are IN game alot, and follow the policies, and strive to better the entire guild, you will find that the rewards are sweeter than you could have ever imagined. By giving your time and loyalty to the CSE, you will find that our members will always be there for you, through thick and thin. If you do not put forth a strong effort to participate, we will drop you from the Initiate Phase, and say goodbye to you. The key to this stage is getting to know our members, and striving to show them that you are a team player. Also, make sure that you ask them to report your actions and deeds to the High Council - this helps us keep track of your good faith and participation.

The only way to become an Initiate of a faction is to be vouched for by other members. People have been known to get vouched for in 3 days, others have taken 3 weeks. This method is one that we believe in, and it helps filter out people who are not responsible enough to wear the title of the Crimson Sky Empire. Please, do not think that we are a conceited guild, I know that it may sound that way. Our members love the fact that they belong to a guild that takes pride in every aspect of the game and our following. When you enter our ranks, you will see that THIS guild is very different from the others.

During the Initiate Phase, you will be treated as an equal. You are considered a member of our Empire, but still need to polish up on Guild Training and your activeness within our family. Simply attend meetings, missions and constantly display the characteristics expected of our members. Within days, you will be assigned the Level One rank, and be removed from Initiate Status.

I wish you good luck, friend! Maybe in time, you too will wear the title of the Crimson Sky Empire. Until that day, I fare thee well.

Seira Black

 

After you are accepted:

Once you are accepted into our family, many things will take place. You will be granted higher privileges in our guild, added to our mailing list, added to our Roster Pages, have the option for creating a character biography, and the private guild structures will be open to you.

If you are interested in learning more about the CSE rank structure and levels - check out our Ranks Page. (coming soon)

Guild Uniform:

Our guild uniform is flexible to meet the needs of many. Our primary colors are: Crimson Red and Black. Uniforms and colors may also vary based on Faction, House, and Rank.

Wearing a uniform is mandatory for all members and prospective members. If on your own time, away from the guild, you wish to wear your own colors, that is fine. Wearing the guild colors is thought to be an honor and should be cherished and worn whenever possible.


Participation is Key:

The most important aspect of our guild and our survival, is Participation. We have created several methods for you to participate such as: IRC, ICQ, Guild Mailing List, Guild Events, Recuiting, Advertising, etc. If you make an attempt to meet others, IN and OUT of game, and show the Guild that you truly care - you will do just fine.

We realize that each person has there own amount of time that can be given towards our guild. Some can play for one hour per week, others have given four hours per day! You simply give what you can, and we will respect you for this.

Out of Game Participation:

Advertising via Newsgroups, chat rooms, ultima pages, newspapers, etc.
Meetings in IRC.
ICQ messages to members and allies..
Mailing List Participation.
Stories, Poems, Songs about our Guild, Members and Adventures.

Guild Mailing List:

Announcements are sent to a mailing list that contains all the members of the CSE. We use this list to inform our members of meetings, new Initiates, promotions, missions, changes, etc. This mailing list is PRIVATE and cannot be joined without going through the List Owners.

When sending mail to this list, if it pertains to a certain faction only, make the subject line appear in this format: <Faction> : Topic

CSE-INIT Mailing List:

The LOA now has a mailing list for our Commoner and Initiate members of the Order. This list is for those candidates to use for anything they wish. To organize events, to share tips, to ask questions to the High Council, etc. It is a tool to help you understand the LOA and to learn our culture.

Using ICQ:

The majority of our Guild members have ICQ. We ask that when you become a member, you add your Unit members to your contact list, as well as the High Council members. This way, we can all communicate with each other and have a better chance of knowing who is online from our Guild. ICQ can be found at http://www.mirabilis.com and is currently a free program and service.

We ask that as you add each member to your list, that you rename the entry to reflect the CSE suffix. (i.e. Rasmon should read: Rasmon_CSE). This process will help you to identify a guild member much faster in the case of an emergency.

Biography:

Biographies and Photos are sent to Seira Black. All members are allowed to have their very own biography page. This helps form your character history and identity with our guild and in our various factions. This is not a mandatory feature. Thank you. (As of 9/15/99 Biography's are not currently being accepted)

Meetings:

Meetings are to be held at least every month by Factions, any Faction with more than one house or over 60 members will be expected to have at least two meetings a month. The Faction High Lord will set meeting dates and times. If you cannot attend a meeting, try to notify your Faction Leader. You will receive a copy of the meeting log via the faction mailing list.

If you have topics or ideas that you would like to see discussed in our meetings, send them to your Faction Leader. Your topics will be included in our Meeting Notes and Reminder.

When a Faction leader is unavailable to perform their duties at a meeting, the senior member of each meeting will be required to HOST that meeting to the best of their ability.

 

RECRUITMENT

We recruit only the best members we can find for the Empire and houses within it. Building the ground up with high standards we hope to maintain the strength built from every previous member before. Such high standards are necessary to protect the Empire from those who would like to harm it.

Prospective recruits must be submitted to a House Master for approval by a current CSE/House member. The very act of submitting a outsider by a member binds them by their honor and position within the CSE that the recruit upholds the laws and code of the Empire.
The House Master will screen and or test the recruit. House Masters can have the member that recruited or a member quest the applicant(s) or process the applicant by any means the Master sees fit to insure the safety of the Clan... You must ask yourself what it takes to make such a strong guild? It takes only accepting the best of people.

Failure by a recruit aka Unproven to uphold the laws and code of the CSE will result in their termination within the Empire and appropriate action against the member who sponsored them. Such actions may include demotion in rank, or in the very dire of circumstances status as Disavowed and termination from the Empire.
