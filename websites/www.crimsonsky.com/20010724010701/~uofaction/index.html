Friday, April 28, 2000

CSE UO Announcement: Bi-Weekly Guild Meetings
Starting on the second week of next May on Friday an hour before our scheduled hunt we will be holding public meetings open to all CSE UO members in IRC (irc.stratics.com #cse) above the rank of initiate.

The meetings will be held to openly dicuss issues, and guild events. They will be held in a organized fashion - all topic items must be emailed to myself or Riva ahead of time. We will then add them to the agenda and an apointed moderator will give each person the "floor" to bring up their issue. Once all topics have been settled we will open up the floor and allow each member a chance to talk about anything not covered. If nothing comes up during the meetings, we can end them early and go start our weekly hunt or other events early.

Attendance is not mandatory, however it will be a positive mark if you can show regular attendence.

Again, if you wish to bring up any questions/topics to be addressed email:

Seira Black seira@crimsonsky.com
or
Riva riva@crimsonsky.com

Please include a summary of what you wish to bring up.

We hope this works out well, and can continue to hold these meetings on a continual basis. The date/time however is not set in stone, if we have a full plate of items to discuss, and can arrange for all members to meet in IRC we can hold them early. More info to come on this later...

The CSE/UO Faction has been disbanded until further notice.

 

Chain of command:

Initiate-This is an entry level position into the guild, a position within the guild that represents that the individual has taken the path of learning the virtues of being "Sky". During this time all initiates will follow the training guidelines to bring their new characters up to a level worthy of being in the Guild.

Squire-This position is for those that have attained the goals given to them during the initiate process. This position will also be used for new recruits that have established characters already within UO and conduct themselves in a manner that reflects those beliefs of the "Sky".

Drake-This position is for those that have proven themselves as a valued member of the Guild. During their Squireship they have carried out the orders given to them and have proven themselves to their Leadership.

Knight-This position is for those that have proven themselves to be both exemplary in following orders and helping their brothers and sisters at all times.

Lord\Lady Drake-This possession is for those that have again, proven themselves to be a valued member of the Guild. They have displayed leadership qualities and they are now on the path to using their leadership qualities towards the betterment the Guild.

Lord\Lady Knight-This position is for those that have displayed the desire to lead and to teach all under their leadership what it truly means to be "Sky".

Regent-This position is for those that have displayed the desire and passion for the betterment of the Guild and the well being of their brothers and sisters. A regent's role is to carry out all orders and policies handed down to them by the Highlord. Also to take any concerns given to them to the Highlord for resolution. This is one of the most celebrated positions within the Guild, and those that have attained this title have proven themselves to know exactly what it takes to make decisions correctly as it pertains to the well being of the Guild.

Enforcer-This position is equal to the status of a Regent title. The Enforcer's job is to act out the laws and code of ethics set forth by the Highlord. The Enforcer answers directly to the Highlord and must understand that an order given to them must be carried out at all costs.

Elder-This position is for those that have been with the Sky for a very long time. Though the days of leading or making decisions might be over for the Elder, please understand that an Elder will be treated with the utmost respect. For their honor has be attained already and they remain in the guild to offer advice when asked, and to act as a role model for all to look up to.

All orders issued from the top will be sent down through the chain of command. All grievances and issues will be sent up through the chain of command. I have selected 2 current members to take their place as Regents of the Guild. They are as follows,

Over the next couple of days the three of us will be sitting down and figuring out who fits where within the Guild today, and what responsibilities and title that individual will be granted. Please take the time to congratulate Riva and Spacial for their promotions next time you see them. For they both have worked very hard over the past few months and both deserve this promotion.

Recruitment policy:

All new members will be given the title of Initiate and then be asked to complete an extensive training program. One that will take a bit of time, but one I also feel will allow both the member to see what being a member of the "Sky" is all about and allow the leadership of the guild to see if that initiate is worthy of being a member of the "Sky". It has always been my belief in the past that we must focus on quality and not quantity. The integrity and reputation that Guild has made for themselves on the Pacific shard would be upheld if this was the case. The brief outline of this training process is as follows.

An effective member of the Crimson Sky needs ALL 700 skill points that has been allotted to his or her char. Yet there is no need to be draconian about producing assembly line formula made members. I will however insist that they clear define themselves as MAGE, FIGHTER or ARCHER. The only exception will be the RANGERS, which primarily focus on animal taming. This character class is highly specialized though, so it does not figure into batch recruitment and training. Makes more sense to just enlist the services of a current Grandmaster Ranger, of which they are a good number of notable one through out the lands. I tend to feel that, if you are going to take up a skill - do it till GM.

Operational Behavior while in the CSE/UO (During Missions Only!):

o In any operation there should be one or two leaders who will issue orders and speak for the group. This includes any unplanned impromptu situations that may arise, some individual MUST take command in a moment's notice and everyone must recognize that person as the leader. People should be aware at all times, even if only standing around outside the house, what the chain of command is. In any sort of emergency situation, that individual must be deferred to.

o Everyone should remain quiet and pay attention to the operation leaders unconditionally.

o If you MUST say something msg it to the leader in IRC/ICQ. Do not be vocal in game it looks bad and confuses the situation.

o If you have problems accessing the private IRC channels fix it yourself. It is your responsibility to know how to deal with IRC. If you cannot enter the private channel and will not be able to due to networking problems (frequent disconnection's, DALnet services not responding, etc.) you should separate yourself from the operation. Orders and intel will be conveyed through this channel and without access you would be without the information needed to participate. This isn't meant to alienate any individual, just a fact of life, you should respect the commanding officer if he/she asks you to detach from the operation. Additionally, if you have questions as to how to do an operation on DALnet, feel free to ask any ops questions, just don't do it during a "situation" or operation.

o Do not convey any operational intelligence to non-CSE unless it is an explicit joint operation, in which case a private channel will be arranged for all participants.

o There WILL BE respect for leaders. This includes any of the High Council, any Team Leaders, any Operational Leaders and anyone who assumes leadership position when no one else is of higher rank or able to assume leadership.

o Respect is demanded from ALL members, at all times, despite rank or current leadership position.

o A character's POWER does not make him/her a leader. Actions, respect and trust are what promotions and leadership positions are based on.
