Faction An online Gaming Faction within the Crimson Sky Empire
   
Shakedown The means in which Guild members accept an applicant. An applicant will learn of their acceptance through faction members. Applicants not accepted will just be dropped with no explanation. Anyone can view their status via the CSE server. Once dropped, re apply and keep trying until you figure out what it takes to be a member. *Hint* Self reliance is a good virtue to have.
   
Applying Applicants to the Guild faction will be "under wing". Once an applicant becomes a Knight they are no longer on trial periods.
   
Applicants Once you become a Child of Light or Child of Darkness you work on the higher positions by and through your Master. Any positions higher than Guardian are granted by the Circle. With recommendation from a Master, Master and Circle position are voted on by the Circle and granted by the High Lord. All positions granted will be reviewed by the Circle and the High Lord before the position is granted. Please note that the positions would be EQ Faction positions. Masters can make or give out any titles they choose or come up with for their clan.
