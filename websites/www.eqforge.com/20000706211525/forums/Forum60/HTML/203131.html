I was in the Test of Flight last night on Fennin Ro. In all about 80+ people attended, not all were shooters, but people watching, and cheering their friends on. I left after I shot (horribly). The accuracy winner was a ranger that hit 18 out of 20 times, I'm not sure of the damage winner, but the leader before I left was an ogre warrior who had a friend beat him down pre-shooting and he crippled the target for 96pts! The GMs stated that they were going to have him reshoot at full health, which started a shouting match among the players (both for and against the Ogre) I myself was for the warrior, as otherwise they should have ignored ranger criticals to be fair to the rogue I saw shoot (damn well too, I think 15 or 16 hits) I had a fun time (until the shouting started.) I do not know what the prizes were. I'm still trying to find that out.

Oh, to answer some questions:

Any bow, any arrows are allowed, you supply your own (but expect player fletchers to be charging disgustingly high prices for some 8damage arrows, kinda like ticket scalpers at concerts)

Contestents are debuffed prior to shooting, so no 60th level shaman friends helping out

The target is a GM stripped of armor and with his level changed to match your own (he is an even /con to the shooter)

Anyone causeing disruptions in the Arena (running out into the line of fire, attacking people attending the event, other general mayhem) are summoned to the side by a GM and if they are really bothersome, nuked. I saw Lady Deagearmo(sp?) Cazic Touch at least 2 people that were attacking folks and being pain-in-the-asses. But overall the Event went very smoothly, although it ran a long time with the number of shooters we had.
