This is a simple two-part quest for a great +10 wisdom item:

  1. Seek out Moodoro Finharn, who is at Crows in North Qeynos. Ask him about The Testament of Vanear. Give him 2 gold and you will receive a book which has two inventory slots and a combine button in it. Then, give him 4 gold until you get the Jester Card from him.   Give the Jester Card back to him and he will give you page 34 of the Testament. Sell all the other cards to a merchant. You now need page 30.

 2. You need to go to Highpass now. From Qeynos you will need to go Q. Hills-> West Karana -> North Karana -> East Karana ->Highpass. Once you zone infrom East Karana head toward the Kithikor zone. You will eventually see water on your right with a bridge spanning it. Jump in the water, swim under the bridge, and go underwater. You will see an underwater tunnel on your right. Swim through there and follow the right wall until you reach another underwater passage. Swim through this short passage [note: being a barbarian I had to duck a few times while swimming to get through this tiny hole] and you will come to dry land on the other side. Run up and into the big open area. At the back of the big open area on the right you will see a forge where the NPC with page 30 spawns, Dyllin Starsine [note: he is wearing a Highhold Pass guard's uniform], a +/- 19th warrior. He has a 7 1/2 hour spawn cycle (learned by camping him). Killing him is easy but the smugglers, who were green and light blue to me at 24, will aid him if they are close by. I just rooted him and used direct damage to put him down quickly. Get page 30 from his corpse. Place page 30 (from Dyllin) and page 34 (which you received from giving the Jester card back to Moodoro) in the Testament of Vanear container and hit combine. You now have a Testament of Vanear.

 This is all you need to do to get the Testament. Of course, realize that your faction has to be good with the citizens and guards of Qeynos to do this quest. I did this quest at 25th lvl.

 Gothakmithan Theladiesman

46th Shaman <The Band of the Broken Bow>, The Tribunal
