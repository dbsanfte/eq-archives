http://www.civfanatics.net/uploads4/DG3RPG_BC4000.jpg

The first symbol is a despot, or ruler (the object represents the object that the despot is known by - a valued possession for example).
The 2nd is land, which is shown as 3 green bars. There's 3 sets of them.
The 3rd is a lake (1 line is a river).
4th is a hand = a possesion.

Translated: The ruler owns 3 pieces of land.

The 4th is a new sentence - for a commoner, or peasent. (villager)
the triangle (5th) is a promotion symbol, and the line in the middle means by 1.

Translated: The villager was promoted a rank.
