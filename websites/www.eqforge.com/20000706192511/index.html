To arms Rangers! - Updated by Caswallawn

The time has come to face this strange, unseen force crippling Rangers in their prime. The time is now to act! We will storm the Fortress of Crushbone and we will not desist until the land is covered in the blood of our foes, or us.

What: Wrath of the Ranger
Where: The Nameless
Zone: Kelethin to Crushbone
When: Saturday, July 1st
Time: 1PM EST
Levels: Please make NEW Rangers and only equip your newbie sword.
Restrictions: Please do not use the zone-wide channels to communicate. We had a great turnout at the last "sit-in" and it was peaceful. Although we are at war, we do not need to act immature here.

This is an in-game, roleplaying event to dispute the drastic role changes Rangers are forced to endure post level 45. with a serious lack of defensive skills necessary for a melee class, and a declining role in groups at this stage, the life of Rangers everywhere are coming to an end.

If we are going to die, we are going to do it with dignity and honor, not to some political in-fighting, misinformation and disorganization within Verant!

To arms Rangers.. to arms!
