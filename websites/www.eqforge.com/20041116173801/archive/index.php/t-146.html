participate in a succession game?

wtf is a succession game?

A game that is passed from player to player.Playing a set amount of turns..say 10.Each successive leader runs our civ as he/she sees fit,saves and sends it on to the next player and so on.

I propose a large random world,deity, raging hoardes,no restart for eliminated civs.Possibly flat map.Open for discussion.

We do it at Apolyton and it is great fun, so check civ2/general for examples if you like.It is challenging as the previous leader may have ruled in a completely different style than you are accustomed to and you want to do as well as you can on your turns.At least not hurt the civ too bad http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/wink.gif

10 players should be the limit per game.I can play either 2.42 or MGE.We won't mix and match though.Seperate games can be started if needed.

To start it may be 15 turns first time thru,then 10 in subsequent turns.Open for discussion.

You need Civ2(duh),an e-mail than can accept attachments, and a compression utility like zip.

So if you're game,post here with your version and ANY game setting suggestions.We'll gather e-mail addresses later.

You will be expected to post some kind of account of each set of turns you play.Creativity encouraged but not required.

mac vs pc compatibilty is NOT a problem.
