Another one I like to do is called assboarding. It works great with ogres, I think it works with trolls too.  Assboarding is the art of appearing like you are skateboarding, complete with sharp turns, jumps, 180s with a hitch. The board is your ass. "There is no spoon neo!"1. 

How to do it:

F9 to external camera mode so you can see yourself. I also made the #6 macro button my sit key so I can press 6 and my character will sit or stand. The trick is to stand and then move at the same time while your avatar is still in the stand-up animation, you can move. If you time it right, you can pretty much stay in continual "sit down" position while moving, thus giving the impression you are sliding around on your ass thus "assboarding". 

Assboarding 101: Press your stand hotkey (6, or whatever), then move forward. You will appear to
slide forward on your ass.

Assboarding 102: Press your stand hotkey, then move forward while holding down a left (or right)
arrow key. Now, sit really quick then stand again while not stopping your forward/left directional key.
You will appear to assboard in a circle!

Freestyle Assboarding: Stand, and use a liberal combination of forward and turning assboarding
movements now add in a few jumps. With the proper timing, you can appear to assboard up the side
of a dungeon wall, do a 180, and flip off. Or, do an assboard 180 above a group member's head.

Assboarding is not just a hobby, it's a lifestyle. Only you can choose if it's for you!
