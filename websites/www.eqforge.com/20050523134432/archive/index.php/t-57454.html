Administrative positions in helenatica
Here the official positions of people inside helenatica are listed together with their responsibilities.

Ruler of Helenatica
Prince Lucentio DiSaaMuel LifeSpanius
Lucentio is the current leader of the Lifespanian Family. He is a wise and charismatic leader who is loved by the citizens of helenatica. Lucentio is also open mided, letting the people live their lives if they do not interfere with the political matters.

General of the Helenatican Army
Princess Victoria LifeSpanius
Victoria is the wife of Lucentio and is a charismatic leader for the army. Details on the organization of the army can be found in the Army summary post.

Grand Vizier of Helenatica
SaaM
The Grand Vizier position was introduced to take care of the full administrative burden of helenatica in a way which satisfies the citizenry.
The Grand Vizier is responsible for distribution of resources (after troop maintenance was payed) and the direct contact to the people. He is also the "lower court" of helenatica, which means he is the court of helenatica and can only be superseeded by the leader of helenatica or the despot of fanatica in his decissions.
He is the primary advisor to the leadership of helenatica, and in cases the leaders are absent he takes full controll of helenatica.
Details and information of the administration of Helenatica can be found in a seperate post maintained by the Grand Vizier.

Resources and Land-Usage
All resources except gold which are gained by helenatica are given to the public and can be used freely by its citizens (after troop maintenance was payed, of course!).
The Grand Vizier will be the contact person for all those things! You can easily use this thread to contact him.

Also, people may build buildings and their homes as well as businesses freely on any tile in helenatica. Just state so in this thread and link back to this thread in your business thread!

Titles in Helenatica
Helenatica reserves the right to give out titles. They are given out according to the following rules:

Archduke (royal)
Leading position in Helenatica.
Represents the leader of Helenatica officially and has freedom to decide things in the name of helenatica.
e.g: Grand Vizier of Helenatica, Commander of the whole army

Duke (high noble)
Commanding position in Helenatica.
e.g: Commander of a corps (lt. general), high administrative position

Earl (noble)
High position in Helenatica.
e.g: Commander of a division (mj. general), lower administrative position

Baron (noble)
Low position in Helenatica
e.g: Lower officer in the Army

Knight (gentry)
Valuable citizen. Given out to honour people for their doings.
e.g: war heroes

A big crowd gathers in fron of the palace district in Gorina. The opening of the new crown-room will take place in a few minutes.
This minute, the prince lucentio steps out of the palace doors on the top of the stairs, followed by the grand vizier and the princess. The crowd is going silent to listen to his speach

LUCENTIO:
"me, lucentio disaamuel lifespanius, the current ruler of your lands, now officially open this fine gathering room for the citizens of helenatica.
the so called 'crown room' will be the official place of contact between the administration of helenatica and the people.

as you all know, helenatica has a long and strong history of confidence and trust between its leaders and the populace. you all here know that you can come and bring your concerns and problems as well as requests forth to your leaders at any time.

now in the future, instead of going through the burden of having to apply for an audience with the leader, you can come to this room at any time. you will find here all administrative places and offices of our fine province.

you will find the offices of the grand vizier and the army contact office, as well as the crowning room and the audience room. the big hall on the portal is serving as waiting room for the populace... there will be served the fine wines of Nur Gisnod as well as other specialities of our home during your waiting time.

Please all come into our new hall and help yourself with the meals and drinks we serve for you!"

Applause is starting in the crowd, and the people start to visit the crown room in amazement and are in awe of the richness of it.
