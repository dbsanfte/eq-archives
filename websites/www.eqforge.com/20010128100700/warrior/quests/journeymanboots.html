1) Talk to Hasten in the Rathe Mountains and he will give you the quest.

2) Head to South Ro or Ocean of Tears and kill an Ancient Cyclops to retreive a ring of the ancients.

3) Kill a Shadowed Man to retreive a shadowed rapier.

4) Go to a bank and retreive 3,250 gold coins.

5) Return to the Rathe Mountains and give the gold and the items to Hasten to receive your Journeyman Boots.
  
Journeyman Boots
MAGIC - NO DROP
AC: 1     Weight: 2.5
Effect: Journeyman Boots
Classes: ALL
Races   : ALL
