This thread is an official registry. Every Citizen must register here by putting down one (and only one) post with the required Data. If there are changes in the Data, that post should be edited. Citizens without a specific city of residence are assumed to live in the capital.

There should be NO DISCUSSIONS in this thread.

Please refrain from posting Democracy Game 1 or 2 information in this thread.

Data should be entered in this format:
MANDATORY DATA:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name: Your Name in the Forum
Chat-Name: Your Name in the Chat (if not equal to Name)
City: The City you live in our Empire
Mandate: Your official elected/appointed position (if any).
former Mandate: for example "former President"

Your City name: The city name you would like when it's your turn to name a city.

OPTIONAL DATA:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Function: Non-official position(s) (for example: 'Mayor of NewCity')
membership: Citizen Groups you belong to
Aspiring: if you want to apply for a mandate or function, you can
state it here. for example Aspiring Mapmaker would lead
the government to maybe call you when they need one

OPTIONAL REAL-LIFE:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
timezone: for example GMT+1 DST for Germany,
meaning GMT+1 with Daylight-Saving Time
country: where you live
age: your age
birthday: your birthday
sex: well, not what you like but what you are ;-)

OPTIONAL FREE-TEXT:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
a free textfield.

Please remember to edit you post every once in a while!
