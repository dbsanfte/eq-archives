Rules of these tourneys are pretty much this...

1) No buffs. They dispell the heck outta you before the fight, then give you full mana and hit points. Can't remember the GM command for em.

2) No rightclickable items. You can't use things that have charges or effects, like using Firestrike on a Rain caller or Ivandyrs hoop or Bladestopper Charges, or effects on Kunark armor. However, you can equip those items.

3) The GMs, on my server at least, do not like to waste peoples time. Bow kiting someone for 40 mins = big no-no.

4) Any equip goes except Artifacts and weapons below 15 delay must go in your offhand. And when I say anything goes besides those, I mean *anything*.
