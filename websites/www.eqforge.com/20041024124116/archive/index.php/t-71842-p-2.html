Update III, May 1935

Korean Tribune

Latvia Has Fallen!
With the full assault of the remaining Russian armies, the Latvians have finally fallen, the first victim of the Soviet army that seems to be on the move to unite Eastern Europe. Little casualties have been reported, though considerable infantry regiments are still not heard of from the First Red Army. They have been marked to be presumably dead.

Germany To Spearhead War Efforts in Europe
German forces continue the invasion of Slovakia, despite the huge diplomatic resistance by the Eastern Europe alliance. The Slovakians are pushed back, almost to the far eastern border. In the Battle of Prosev, the Slovakians are able to repel a German force thrice their size, but are nearly destroyed in the process. The small resistance is all that is left, and is expect to easily be crushed next month. The Slovakian air force continues to harass the Germans, but no casualties due to the enability to effectively attack ground. They have been relocated to the Czech part.

Germany to make TWO major headlines!
German forces easily penetrate the small border forces of Belgium and dive deep into the rich country. Due to massive demilitarization, the Belgium is unable to mount a defense. Most of the men that were still in the army are in Denmark. With the German ingenuity of air combat and blitzkrieg understandings, Belgium falls in one month. The German 8th Korps gains considerable training in this operation, as does the air force.

England to Annex Ireland
English troops land in the southern Ireland region, the second most populated region in Ireland, and took it all without the use of much force. Meanwhile the north is fully incorporated into the empire and the Irish surrender. With the boost of moral throughout the country with the nation’s most valiant rival now under control, huge amounts of men from overseas colonies join the Home Guard, and a grant from the Australian Interim government of 40c has been sent to London.

Denmark Close to Falling
With Danish resistance greatening, and Belgium forces arriving, another Swedish paratrooper regiment has fallen. The lines hold, and Denmark is able to slowly regain its standings. But with the Belgium forces being demoralized, many wanting to return to their occupied country, there is still hope for the Swedish movement.

Albania has Fallen
Albanian forces are smashed as the two Greek forces begin to sandwich them in the center of the country. Fighting a two front war, the sparse Albanians are not able to withhold. The country fell sometime near the 9th of the month. Great celebrations are held throughout Greece in this fantastic movement that has once again brought Greece into the spotlight of world politics and military.

Poland Continues To Invade
Polish troops persist to advance in Lithuania, where city after city falls to the Polish army. The air cover is adequate, and the force begins to achieve air superiority, though, mind you, the Lithuanians still withhold a superb anti-aircraft weaponry system in their bases and cities that continue to shoot down Polish planes. The first battle of this month expresses the want of the Lithuanians for independence, destroying a few infantry regiments of Poland. However, the Polish continue to push, and despite being gunned down, increases training among the troops.

Italy Gears Up For War
The last of the Italian force in Croatia, together with the Germans who have landed in support of them, push out the last of the Yugoslavians and take the country despite Romanian superb artillery bombardment. The Yugoslavian fleets in the Adriatic have also been destroyed. Little to no casualties are taken on behalf of the Italian and German forces. Meanwhile, the forces throughout Italy muster to defend the nation.

World War Two Begins
With the attacks on France by Spain, and the French attacks on Germany and Italy, it is safe to say that the second World War has begun.

---French Navies At War
The French and Germany first fleets engage in combat after the French begin with a bombardment from a distance. The large German force began to encircle the transport while returning fire. The huge battleship was the main target for the two French destroyers, but they were outclassed. The Germany battleship sunk the fist destroyer, while a combination of the battleship and the other two Germany destroyers sent the second one limping home. After evaluating the fleet, one German destroyer was scuttled as it was unable to keep up with the fleet.

---French African Campaigns
The French 4th Army Mechanized Infantries are able to wipe out the Italian paratroopers in the first battles of the campaign. They then rush to the north to aid the fifth army, which is under heavy stress from the strong Italian Colonial Army. They are able to push the Italians back, with slight casualties.

---French Forces Invade Palestine
Combined with the aerial support, the French attack British posts in Palestine from their colony in Syria. Unfortunately for the French, the English must have known about this and have created an army and moved an army to Palestine from Egypt. The English are more then a match for the French forces, and hold them as soon as they invade. At the battle of Tel-Aviv, the British show their willingness to protect their colony and push the French all the way back almost to the border.

---Austria Under Attack
French forces dive into the empty province of Austria, and are able to fully capture the German Fourth Squadron on the ground. The force is destroyed, and the province is taken.

---French Attacks Tus
The province is guarded by the Italian home guard, newly created. However, it is quite small. They are pushed back and almost destroyed by the huge French force; taking refuge in the city that is left. The French army take losses as well.

---French Attacks against Tri
The French hopelessly try to invade the Italian province, but are met with huge forces that are nearly double it’s size, and much more able to fight. The paratroopers are reduced by almost half as they are sandwiched in.

---Spain Counterattacks
On behalf of Italy, Spain launches an attack itself into the undefended southern borders of France. They are able to capture the province without a problem, and the large Spanish force is well adapted to the area that they are fighting in.
