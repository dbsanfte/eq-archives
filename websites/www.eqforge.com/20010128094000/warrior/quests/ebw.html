1) Head to Mistmoore and kill Maid Issis and Butler Synclair to retrieve their fangs. These are rare drops and extremely camped. The butler also drops a Cape of Midnight Mist, and the maid drops a Nightshade Wreath

2) Deliver the fangs to The Avenging Caitiff in Mistmoore to receive the Electrum Bladed Wakazashi.
  
** The caitiff is very camped so its a good idea to let everyone know your doing the quest. **
  
Electrum Bladed Wakazashi
MAGIC - NO DROP
Damage: 7     Delay: 21
Weight: 2.0
Classes: WAR, PAL, RNG, SHD, ROG, BRD
Races   : ALL
