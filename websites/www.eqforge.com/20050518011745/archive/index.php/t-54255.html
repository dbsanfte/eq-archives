This is the thread to post news and info about the Royal Family Line of Lifespania.

The history of our family goes back to the beginnings of our nation. The Current family members as well as their relations to other families of our glorious nation is shown later in this thread.

Our family ownings are claimed to be the province of Hellenatica. A Map of those claims will also be shown in this thread.


Definition of Family Membership
The Family Membership for the Lifespanian Royal line in strictly limited.
A lifespanian Family member marrying a non-Lifespanian will keep his/her family name as second middle-name, but wont give it to the husband/woman as middlename.
The children will be reviewed by the existing Family members, and some of them, normally the firstborn of each generation, will be "adopted" into the family and will hold "Lifespanius" as second middle name from then on.
An example for this is Bellona Lifespania and her husband Caeser Jade.
Duitia Lifespania Bellona married Flavinius Jadius Caeser in 1025BC, and as such Bellona was from then on called "Duitia Jadius Lifespanius Bellona".
Of her first generation of children, only Rubinia was adopted into the Lifespanian Family (as firstborn child). Avita as second born was not adopted.
As such, Rubinia's name was Caesaria Jadius Lifespanius Rubinia and Avita's name was Caesaria Jadius Avita.
