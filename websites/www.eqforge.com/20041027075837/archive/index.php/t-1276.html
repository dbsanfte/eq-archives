Scenario 1: "The Revolution"
starts in 1566 with the "Beeldenstorm". People attack catholic churches and destroy all the windows and statues (=beelden). The Duch (have to) conquer a few cities, while other cities join the "Geuzen" because they want. The scenario ends in 1576.

Scenario2: "The Rift"
starts in 1577, the Dutch get extra armies from foreigners (also calvinists) and almost whole Holland (the province, not the country) is with the "Prince of Orange" (Willem van Oranje). Don Jan is the Spanish "governor" of the Netherlands. The Union of Atrecht is formed, consisting of parts of Belgium (that was considered Spanish Netherlands too). The Dutch form the Union of Utrecht, 7 proinces binf=d together to eliminate a common enemy. The scenario ends in 1588. Willem van Oranje is murdered in Delft (nice objective for the Spanish).

Scenario3: "Military Succes"
starts in 1589. The Armada is on its way to England and the Netherlands. The fleet of Parma wants to join the Armada, but will be attacked by rebellion ships near Sluis (Zeeland) and near the French coast. All fotreses of the Spanish in Holland, Friesland and Zeeland have to be conquered with the first well trained land troops of the Dutch, lead by Van Oldenbarneveldt and Prince Maurits (the son of Willem van Oranje). The
Dutch have to try to conquer Dunqurque and the Armada should be crushed by the combined forces of the British and Dutch. The scenario ends in 1609 with the 12-year peace.

Scenario4: "Consolidation and Victory"
starts in 1621. The Spanish try once agian to defeat the new Republic. Frederik-Hendrik (also an Orange-Nassau) starts a conquest in the East and South of the Netherlands. The Spanish must try to conquer Breda and Gulik.
The second Armada will arrive to defeat the Dutch fleet.

Until now, the scenarios are historical correct. The second objective of the Dutch is to conquer Brussels, and the third to kick the Spanish *** out of the Netherlands. The Spanish second objective is to conquer Amsterdam and the third to conquer all Dutch cities.
The scenario ends in 1648 with the peace of Munster, or earlier when you defeated your oponent and didn't start a new war.
