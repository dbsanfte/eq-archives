I'm going to make an unusual suggestion. I suggest we do away with our system of allowing governers free will of naming provinces.

Why? This always results in a hodgepodge of different names which does get very annoying.

I suggest defining and polling a list of provincial names, to be used each time a province is defined. This system has worked well in the Civ2 Democracy Game, where (last game) provinces were named after geographic areas of Mars.

To that end, I suggest the following names, to maintain a theme originally proposed by eyrei, based upon the names of the Seven Hills on which Rome was originally built:

Palatine, Aventine, Capitoline, Quirinal, Viminal, Esquiline and Caelian

Thoughts?

While it would be nice to have a province named Asphinxia, I feel in this circumstance we must bow to the general will. Regardless of what it is called, I will be running for Governor of the 'Old Egypt' province one it ceases to be a warzone :) I think the combination of Egyptian cities, lots of plains and Lake Asphinxia (be it the city or the lake) will satisfy my nostalgia. It's good to see that people still remember Asphinxia so fondly, testiment to its greatness!! (thnx for the support SAaM, CT)

In terms of Lake Asphinxia city, we don't have any law about naming cities currently, but we do have convention. However, one of the things that has been good about this game is that the nameing of cities has been less personaly orientated (so the names all sound better). I don't think Lake Asphinxia deviates from this, it is quite a suitable name.

So, given that we must determine province names somehow, I would agree with Rik. We have three possible means:

Governor: This is the traditional method where the first governor of a province chooses a name as he/she sees fit.

Plebicite: A name is chosen for each new province by a poll each time.

List: There is a list of province names that we work through (eg the hills of Rome)


I suggest a mix of all three!! (stick with me, this idea may work) I have a feeling that if we put it to a vote, great support would be shown for the idea of naming our provinces after the hills of Rome. In fact, I think we could get approved the idea that our first 7 provinces MUST have those names! However, as there is no real reason to enforce an order to these, the first governor of any new province should be free to choose which name *taken from the list) will be the name of his province!!

See, everybody's happy!!!
