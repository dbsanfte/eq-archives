TOP 10 THINGS TO DO POST 50th LEVEL WHEN BORED

10) Sit in Oasis. Buff newbies until their arms and legs fall off. Make grunting noises and run off without saying anything.

9) Take up smithing (if you haven't already). Make FS armor. Use your alchemy skill to dye it all kinds of fruity colors. Make a warrior named "Cappt Amerika" and give him bright red/white/blue armor.

8) Go to Sebelis. Stand at the zone. Auction off dervish rings/rawhide gauntlets/FS
weapons/assorted piece of banded/and offer to 'sharpen' rusty weapons for free. Act all serious about it.

7) Remove your gear, turn ANON and go to blackburrow. Start a "commander room" group. Cast low level spells and do a lot of piercing. An hour later, put on ALL your 50+ level gear and declare "this place sucks for experience". Gate out.

6) Buy stacks and stacks of junk items (food, batwings, arrows, etc... Find a newbie and follow him around while invisible. Drop items all around him wherever he goes. Two minutes later shout "Hey! I just found a FBSS on the ground!"

5) Go to South Ro. Shout "What's an ancient cyclops?" Thirty seconds later shout "how come the tent-merchants won't buy this ring it dropped?"

4) Find a low-level zone, like befallen. Follow people around. Whenever someone is medding, sit on them. (Troll/Ogre only).

3) Bind yourself in Lower Guk, dead side. Proceed to live side. Aggro the King room, the live tower, and the jail area. Gate instantaneously to the dead side while the mobs cross thru the waterways to get to you (aggroing everything in their path). Shout "Train to zone!". When someone shouts "Which zone?" you shout "Both zones!"

2) Run up to a newbie, preferably a halfling. Hand him 46,760 silver pieces. Say "Shhhh!!! I was never here! You don't know me!" Sit in front of him and immediately camp (or turn invisible and watch him try to "run" to the bank with it).

1) Add to this list!!!!!
