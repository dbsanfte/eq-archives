I don't see any reason to suspect any foul play here, and I may even be able to offer an explanation. Many people who used to be involved in the demogame vote in the presidential elections and no others. It is perfectly normal for this election to receive more votes than the others. Also, there are those (myself included) who may have started voting and then stopped for some reason. Currently, I have been interrupted twice while trying to cast my votes, and still haven't finished.

Further, I have seen this phenomena before in other elections. Particularly, the group that often hangs out in the chat room usually votes earlier than others (because they find out the polls were posted first), and tend to vote for certain candidates. Thus, a candidate sometimes will get a large number of votes early on, and then end up losing the election as the rest of the populace votes. This seems to be the case here...

There is also no way to tell who voted in a poll without running a gigantic search of every poll ever posted, so I wouldn't expect Thunderfall to find anything.
