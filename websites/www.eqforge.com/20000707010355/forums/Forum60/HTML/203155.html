And I must say that no matter what most have seen Elderan is right--we are in need of a Defense upgrade. Heck I would even be willing to sacrifice 10pts of Offensive skills to gain 10 pts of Defensive skills. One will come to notice when your in the deeper dungeons Rangers have the horrible tendancy to get the beat down rather quickly. Now before you hit the back button on this post--it isnt a rant on our lack of ability or skills just a plain observation of what I have been witness to/effected by.

The Zone trash hits for 110 on average but the further you work into the zone the harder it gets. Now with Basic cleric buffs and 3 pieces of Tolons my AC sits at 890(not shabby with 2300 hps with Pinzarn). When you guys have the oppourtunity to fight Krup and Iilis frogloks(most lvl 50+) it is a perfect example of how Lacking our defensive skills are. Put myself up against any other Melee class and the cleric(with druid backup) will be hard pressed to keep me alive.
