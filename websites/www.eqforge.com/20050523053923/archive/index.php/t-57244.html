In rebuttal to the laws point...
I believe that the Judiciary has some of the most expertise on laws, and should certainly help to write them. The principle of separation of powers works well for democracies with thousands to millions of constituents. When only around 20 people are active, everybody needs to lend a hand in the lawmaking system, especially those who know the most about them. Keep in mind that the Constitution clearly states that all citizens are in the Congress, which is a lawmaking body. Therefore, the members of the Judiciary, as members of Congress, have every right to propose new legislation, just as any other Congress member. Therefore, I would be honored to write a Code of Laws in the next term, and I will make sure to do so.

Also, I want to remind voters that I am still a relative newbie compared to many other people who participate in this demogame, with 3 months of demogame experience. Though it is true that my opinions have not been supported as often by the Judiciary as a whole, this is most likely because I hold my own opinions on how to interpret the constitution. Simply because the Judiciary found against me doesn't mean that my judgement is unsound. I have written out detailed reviews and explain my position thoroughly. I believe that having an occasional dissenting opinion is an asset; it shows that you always support your opinion and do not let it be swayed by other opinions. I do not want this to sound like I am alleging that Peri can be bought, but in all of his reviews (except for the dismissal of charges against DZ, of which the JA must post the first review), he has waited for the CJ opinion before posting his. I, on the other hand, have never had a problem with posting a review before anybody else, and I have posted the first ruling on almost every judicial review. In addition, simply because an interpretation of the Constitution was ruled one way doesn't mean that the ruling can't be overturned at a later date.

And DZ, where did I embellish my part in your PI case? I said that I helped to acquit you in the PI case, which I did by acting as defender and by reviewing against it. I also said that I got you not to file a PI against Chieftess in return, which you stated was because of my intervention by a post in the Judicial thread. I do not see what was embellished.
