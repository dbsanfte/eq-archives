The evil Aztecs built Chalco on our side of the bay. Then Montezuma had the nerve to demand that our glorious Legions leave his territory! Donsig's reply was WAR!

Two Legions were lost but Chalco was destroyed. Another escorted band of Aztec settlers was captured. It did not take long for the war to expand. Our former enemy, Cleopatra, has allied with us against the Aztecs. Soon afer this alliance was cemented America declared war on us! Both Egypt and Greece have allied with us against the Americans. We lost two warriors in Greece. they fell at the hands of American swordsmen but not before killing off one of the enemy swordsmen first! We have destroyed an Aztec jaguar warrior while Egyptian forces have captured two escorted Aztec settlers.

Our nation has grown with the addition of four new cities. Both of the proposed canal towns in Esquiline province have been founded. Another city was planted southeast of Regia Doughnutia and a fouth sprang up south of Memphis. We also have three more settlers ready for form cities!

We have mastered the currency technology and have advanced into the middle ages! However, the wars have proved unpopular leading to the downfall of our government: we are in ANARCHY!
