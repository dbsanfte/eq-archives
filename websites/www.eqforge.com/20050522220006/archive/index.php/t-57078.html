I come before you, the people of Fanatica, seeking the office of Domestic Leader. I'm not a great speaker, and anyway think that actions and accomplishments are the true measure of a leader, above flowery speech. Not to mention I'm nowhere near as creative as those who frequent the RPG... ;)

So here is my platform, without adornment, or as little as I can get away with. :)

1). Why are you standing for this position?

This office has stood empty for much of the current term, and our nation has suffered from indecision. I think the key role of the DL is to coordinate the strategic use of resources, and we have sadly been lacking in coordination. By contrast, the President's role (other than as DP) seems more like being the visionary, weighing in with an opinion and providing thought leadership, and the other leaders are tightly focused on their respective areas.

2). Why are you standing for other positions too?

I stongly believe that there should be at least two candidates for each elective office, so that a deputy will be available in the case of a leader's absense. The other position (Governor of Capitolene) had only one candidate at the time I volunteered for it. And it doesn't hurt to have a backup plan... :rolleyes:

3). What are your objectives for the first 7 days of office?

We are at a critical juncture, where the immediate strategic decisions will determine whether we can proceed with the victory condition the people want, or if we are forced into some other objective. It is also a time of tremendous growth. We start this term with three times as many provinces as last term.

My immediate objectives are to lead the decisions on immediate strategy (Education vs. Gunpowder, and Monarchy vs. Republic) if they have not already been made, and then position us to implement those strategies in the most efficient way possible. In particular, coordinating the build queues of the various provinces to get an overall strategic mix needs to be a top priority.

4). What qualities can you bring to this position that your fellow candidates cant?

Let's leave it at the qualities I'll bring to the position, at least for now. :)

This is my 3rd term in the Demogame, (browsed the DG2 forums, saw that the game was nearly over, and waited until DG3 to sign up). As such, I'm still fresh and in no danger of burning out by doing the same thing over again. For me it is the first time, and thus I put my best into it.

5). How will you get more people involved in the game?

One somewhat random idea would be to seek out applicants for a "projections" office, to look at our growth potential and forecast what population cities will max out at, how many shields / commerce they will produce, and maybe corruption levels. This would be producing highly valuable information about where cultural improvements are needed.

6). If you have held office before, what was your greatest triumph and worst disaster?

I hold the Science Leader position now. My greatest triumph has to be getting elected in the first place, as I never expected to even come in 2nd. :D Worst disaster is not having any scientific buildings constructed yet, due to the war effort.

7). Which method of victory do you want to pursue and why?

I haven't won culturally yet on Monarch, so that would be cool.

8). How have you contributed to this game?

Science Leader, member of PSC poll review board, attend some (but not all) chats
