Donovan Zoi
May 27, 2003, 04:01 PM
Donovan Zoi - President

I will post the same statement that I made in the noms thread.


As President, I plan to:

1. Involve everyone in the game as much as possible. Newcomers to the DemoGame can always expect a response from me. Also, I will encourage debate on all of our issues.

2. Hold all leaders accountable for the office they hold. Gaining office is more than just the prestige of having it; there is work involved as well.

3. Stay involved in every aspect of the game. Look at the Citizens Forum during term 1 and you see that I have participated in almost every thread. This will only increase if I am elected President.

There are aspects of the actual game as well, so as DP look for me to:

4. Found more cities. We are trailing all of our rivals in cities founded, and have only one settler in our queues! That is not a good sign.

5. Quickly claim our borders against Egypt and America. If we don't get the hills that separate us from Egypt, we are missing a great strategic opportunity.

6. Connect the Iron now! We are a nation of warriors and it shows, because that is all we have militarily. We should not wait 15 turns for Gorina to build a Temple. We should colonize the Iron just outside its borders now.


That should do for starters. If anyone has any questions, please feel free to address them here and I will answer them.

A special note to those just visiting(and casting votes) from other forums, including the RPG:

You may have been invited here by someone, but please think about your vote and consider supporting a candidate that has proven his dedication to Fanatica's future. Just as in RL, an uninformed vote undermines everything a democracy strives to achieve.

Donovan Zoi for President

Please give me voice so that all shall be heard.

Ah, Shluks SAaM :blush:

I am running for the position of Domestic Leader, as well as that of Governor of Capitoline, roles, you will agree, that follow a certain pattern. I believe that nation-building is the most important role for the Domestic Leader. This may seem self-explanitory, but what it really means is two things.

Firstly, the obvious, making sure everything runs smoothly in terms of the physical development of our nation, the workers, the cities, the provinces. It requires both attention to detail and a whollistic 'vision' of progress. I believe I have these.

Secondly, the domestic department is in an exellent position to inolve new members. It is the department with the most obvious, immediate control over the game. It is in the auspices of the Domestic department that new players can most easily and immediately contribute, all it requires it good knowledge of how our world works, not nessisarialy of our government and laws. I think the Dom Department should play a very active role in encouraging contribution from all citizens. Some of the most comprahensive domestic proposals this term have come from very new players, and yet they don't get the airplay they should.

I think the Domestic leader should be attentive to no only their own thread, but the provincial, and even city based, threads so as to gain the widest possible range of ideas and opinions.
