I have lost the Faith...

We all talk about it, we all think about it and we all never do it. It is time to let Verant know the Ranger class is broken post 50 and we need to let them know in their pockets.

We all agree that not only have protests in game not been effective, but they disrupt the other players of the game. We, as Rangers, know how it is to have a hard time playing the game, so we won't subject our other neighbors with that fact.

On Monday, September 11th, I am canceling the account of Sunmoon Glademaster. It is the ultimate sacrifice, but one that must be made. I do not know if he will be restored at a later date.. it depends on Verant.

Verant has stated that they do not delete characters until a set amount of time goes by, but you can never be too sure. If you are unsure you want to send this statement to Verant, then do not.

Verant has effectively reduced the quality of gameplay post 50 for Rangers, and moreover hybrids as a whole. Until they can see the basic design flaw presented in-game with Rangers post 50 (including lack of defensive skills, some spell issues and some serious agro problems,) then Sunmoon shall walk the lands no more.

I urge you fellow Rangers to make this statement to Verant. If we do not, they will never understand the seriousness of our plight. It is legitimate in every way and the majority of the general EQ public acknowledges and supports the Ranger issues post 50.

It is with a heavey, yet understanding heart that I scribe these words. Yet, each day as I progress post 50, I can see our issues amplifying. Some of the highest Rangers in the land are being excluded from major guild events, being told to back out of their primary role and basically, radically reduce their effectiveness. Moreover, we are expected to adapt to a vision that does not include a position for the Ranger in a group post 50.

The Glade will go in in fellowship, I'm sure as strong as ever, but Norrath will not see Sunmoon and I'm sure many other Rangers until our issues are addressed.

If you want to make the ultimate statement, then do it now. Hit the Cancel button, walk outside and enjoy life for a bit. One day, perhaps balance will be restored and there will again be a use for the Ranger in the post 50 game.

I think that we need to be writing hard-copy letters to Verant customer service/quality assurance and addressing our genuine issues with them in that medium.
