QUEST WALKTHROUGH (SHORT VERSION)

This is mostly just a short summary version of the quest. For a more detailed walkthrough, please see the Long Version.


  1. BW: Telin Darkforest (Near Skyfire Zone):
    Say the following:

    • what are you doing in this forest?

    • what answers?

    • what evil?

    • what do you suspect?

    • perhaps it is Innoruuk?

    • what action should I take?

    receive a note

  2. GFay: Give Telin's note to Faelin Bloodbriar (+1500, +500; rare spawn) & receive ring

  3. Kith: Give Faelin's ring to Giz X`Tin & receive coin

  4. BW: Give Giz's coin to Telin Darkforest

  5. EK: Give Giz's coin to Althele & receive amulet

  6. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Sionae & get amulet back

  7. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Nuien & get amulet back

  8. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Teloa & follow to gathering

  9. EK: Talk to Althele & Dark Elf Corruptor spawns

  10. EK: Kill Dark Elf Corruptor & loot book

  11. EK: Give book to Althele & receive earth stained note

  12. Misty: Give earth stained note to Elle Foodcrafter and receive bowl

  13. Create a Hardened Mixture by combining the following foraged items in Elle's bowl:

    • Everfrost: Chilled Tundra Root

    • Greater Faydark: Ripened Heartfruit

    • Innothule Swamp: Speckled Molded Mushroom

    • Misty Thicket: Sweetened Mudroot


  14. Create the Runescale Bowl

    1. Obtain the incomplete pattern (sub-quest):

      1. TD: Alrik Farsight (chessboard)
        Say the following:

        • I seek an ancient type of bowl made by the elves of old/

        • I will take the artifact.

        recieve a receipt crushed pot

      2. FelB: Give Alrik's crushed pot to Farios Elianos & receive a shopping list

      3. FelA: Give Farios's shopping list to Merchant Nora & receive groceries

      4. FelB: Give groceries to Farios Elianos & receive a recipt for services rendered

      5. TD: Give recipt for services rendered to Alrik Farsight and recieve an incomplete pattern


    2. Obtain the Platinum Speckled Powder (sub-quest):

      1. FV: Forage a Firiona Rose

      2. SG: Hail Merdan Fleetfoot, say 'Who is Niera?', and give him the Firiona Rose to recieve a wood
        painting.

      3. FM: Give the wood painting to a human skeleton (-2376, +1963) to recieve a silver chained locket.

      4. SG: find Niera Farbreeze and say the following:

        • What tune is that.

        • Why are they always there?

        • Where did she find the speckled, flecked dust?

        Give Niera the silver chained locket to receive Platinum Speckled Powder.

    3. Obtain the Enchanted Clay (sub-quest):
      KAL B: Kinlo Strongarm in Everhot's Forge, bottom floor.
      Say the following:

      • what ancient

      • what few tales

      • what hammer hammers

      • what Erudite necromancer

      • what mud

      • do it for me

      Give Kinlo Storngarm a Jade Reaver (from City of Mist) & he will give you Enchanted Clay.

    Combine the Ancient Patter, Platinum Speckled Powder, and Enchanted Clay in a Pottery Wheel to get the Runescale
    Bowl

  15. Misty: Give Ella Foodcrafter the Hardened Mixture and Runescale Bowl to get the Softly Glowing Stone.

  16. FV: Talk to Foloal Stormforest.
    Say the following:

    • are you one such as myself?

    • what answers?

    • what about venril sathir?


  17. FV: Foloal Stormforest wants the Softly Glowing Stone that you have and whatever "conduit of power" of power you
    can find on Venril Sathir's corpes.

  18. KC: Kill Venril Sathir to get the sickly pulsating stone.

  19. FV: Give Foloal Stormforest the stone from Venril Sathir and the Softly Glowning Stone to get the Warmly Glowing
    Stone.


QUEST WALKTHROUGH (LONG VERSION)

Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods, Kunark

Telin Darkforest can be found near the zone to Skyfire Mountains.

You say, 'hail Telin Darkforest'
Telin Darkforest looks at you serenely. 'Hello, wanderer.'
You say, 'what are you doing in this forest?'
Telin Darkforest says 'Mighty this place must have been, once. Now scarred by hatred and suffering. I have been sent here seeking answers to problems not yet understood.
You say, 'what answers?'
Telin Darkforest considers you for a few moments before speaking. 'A great evil walks across the land. The hand of some demon we don't yet know is twisting and polluting forests.'
You say, 'what evil?'
Telin Darkforest says 'If I knew that, I wouldnt be here looking for answers. I would be elsewhere, looking for solutions. For now, I suspect much but know little.
You say, 'what do you suspect?'
Telin Darkforest looks at you and sighs. 'The lands are changing. Beasts, large and small, have turned upon those who watched over them. Druids and rangers, worshippers of both Karana and Tunare, have reported a feeling of hatred throughout their once peaceful lands.'
You say, 'perhaps it is Innoruuk?'
Telin Darkforest raises his eyebrow slightly and a subtle look of surprise creeps across his face. 'Innoruuk? Perhaps. Aye. It must be. Perhaps I knew and didn't want to consider the possibility. It must be this though, and some action will have to be taken.'
You say, 'what action should I take?'
Telin Darkforest looks at you consideringly and then scribbles something quickly on a small, worn paper. 'You seem powerful enough. Perhaps you can help those who seek to protect nature's peace. Take this note. Deliver it only into the hands of Faelin Bloodbriar. Where you will find her, I cannot say. She is a friend of mine and a wanderer. She has been seen from one end of the lands to the other. Seek her out, and quickly.'

Telin Darkforest gives you a "Worn Note" to take to Faelin Bloodbriar.
