In order to help Fanatica place cities in a logical order, we need to discuss, as a nation, where to place the Forbidden Palace. Or if we're even going to build the Forbidden Palce.

There are three possible areas that would be feasible now, or in the near future. 1. If we place it Southeast of the proposed city site of Crecent Mountain, we can benefit cities on both sides of that large bay. 2. A possible plaement on the land mass to the East. Just East of the Dyes that are 5 tiles South of Regia Doughnutia, in the hills and grasslands. 3. The other area would be Memphis, or just Southwest of it, if Memphis razes. We would have to take Cleo out of the picture with this placement.

To me these seem to be the best areas to consider. Monty is moving in from the East, and there's not much land over there so option #2 may not be worth the chance. Option #3 would be great, but would commit us to clearing the area of Egyptians (not a bad idea ;) ) and stabilizing the area. If we expand Westward quickly, Option #1 appears to be the easiest and fastest choice.

What are your suggestions?
