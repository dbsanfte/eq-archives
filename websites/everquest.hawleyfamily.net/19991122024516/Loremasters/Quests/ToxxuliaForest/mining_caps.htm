Mining caps

The NPC necro Talriger Eklorin, who is walking around in the forest on the other side of the bridge, wants to employ a young necromancer to retrieve some mining caps for his skeleton workers in the mine. Apparantly, these skeletons will not continue to work without the caps. The caps are sometimes dropped by kobald scouts (though not often). Providing these caps will result in experience, faction, some nice money and an item. He only gives the quest to those he likes (and doesn't KOS).
