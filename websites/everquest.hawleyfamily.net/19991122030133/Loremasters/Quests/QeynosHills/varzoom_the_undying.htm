Varzoom the Undying

Varsoon of Qeynos Aquaducts and Qeynos Hills is a high level necromancer, and the target of many high-level players. Immediately after you kill Varsoon, a Varsoon Zombie will spawn in Qeynos Hills, who will run in a straight line for the nearest de-spawning site (so the zombie stays in the zone for less than a minute). The zombie is called Varsoon the Undying. I am not sure what happens if you manage to kill the zombie as well.
