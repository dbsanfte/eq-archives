The Millers

The Miller siblings, Baobob and Chandra, will make bearhide and wolfhide armor for you if you provide them with the proper pelt and some gold. Just talk to them and follow their instructions. Baobob makes the wolfhide armor (Belt, boots and cape) and Chandra makes bearhide armor. Chandra Millers' prices for bearhide armor are as follows: Bear Hide Belt - 5 GP and Low quality Bear pelt; Bear Hide Boots - 15 GP and Medium quality bear pelt;  Bear Hide Cape - 21 GP and High quality bear pelt. This also raises faction with Karana Residents, Guards of Qeynos, Priests of Life and Knights of Thunder. Be aware that you need to have a high faction with the Karana Residents to get anything made other than a belt.
