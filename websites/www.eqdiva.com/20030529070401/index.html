Hail fellow Rockstars and Divas!

Camp your weary feet a bit. Both mentally and physically, you are always alert, adapting to every situation. So set your lute down and let your fingers relax...

I am Kocho Divah, Bard of the 58th song. Why do I love the bard so passionately? Bards are hands-down the most fun and diverse characters. Tired of going OOM? Want the freedom to either solo or group? Feel like a little melee action? Want to buff the entire group ten-fold instantly? Need to fill a role your group is lacking? Not getting invites to dungeon or plane raids? Want to nearly eliminate downtime? Want to fly like the wind? Be a rockstar - be a Bard!

Not a bard? Don't leave yet! This site was initially designed for YOU in mind! Please enjoy your visit, and hopefully you will leave with a better understanding and sheer appreciation for the hard-working, multi-talented bard.

~ Kocho Divah, bard of the 58th song

Yeah, I just cleared out 75% of our forums, but I've added a very important one.

I think of EQDiva as a nice community to hang out in. I'm adding an IRL (In real life) forum for us to get to know each other better, and blab about everyday life.

I'm trying to follow my server forums. There, we've gotten to know each other almost intimately. We talk about reviews (movie, book, games, products), opinions, and learn so much from each other. It's a great form of entertainment that can take your mind off of EQ for a few minutes.

If you're EQ retired but addicted to the social aspect of forums, then you've got a reason to hang with us again.
