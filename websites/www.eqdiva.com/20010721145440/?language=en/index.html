Hail fellow Rockstars and Divas!

Camp your weary feet a bit. Both mentally and physically, you are always alert, adapting to every situation. So set your lute down and let your fingers relax...

I am Kocho Divah, Bard of the 53rd song. Why do I love the bard so passionately? Bards are hands-down the most fun and diverse characters. Tired of going OOM? Want the freedom to either solo or group? Feel like a little melee action? Want to buff the entire group ten-fold instantly? Need to fill a role your group is lacking? Not getting invites to dungeon or plane raids? Want to nearly eliminate downtime? Want to fly like the wind? Be a rockstar - be a Bard!

Not a bard? Don't leave yet! This site was initially designed for YOU in mind! Please enjoy your visit, and hopefully you will leave with a better understanding and sheer appreciation for the hard-working, multi-talented bard.

~ Kocho Divah, bard of the 53rd song
