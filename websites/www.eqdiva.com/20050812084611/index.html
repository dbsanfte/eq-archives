Hail fellow Divas and Rockstars!

Welcome to the new and improved EQDiva.com - home to the extremely fun and incredibly versatile Everquest bard class! At EQDiva you'll be able to learn something new each visit. If you're an EQ bard, the site is catered for you. If you're not a bard, this site was initially designed for you, so you can get to know the diverse talents of your bard friends. We have lots of resources to offer bards and our friends such as EQ songs, spells, items, zones, and much, much more.

EQDiva has relaunched on 1/26/04 so browse around the site, there's lots of new features, like the new EQDiva LDoN Search Engine which returns both LDoN merchant and drop items! There are lots more exciting new features planned as well. I plan to have an official "Grand Opening" when the site is all ready to go.

Be sure to register as its quick and free! Registering will allow you to enjoy more features of the site and make your own contributions to the bard class. Upon registering, be sure to fill out your EQ Character profile, this helps us in helping you!
