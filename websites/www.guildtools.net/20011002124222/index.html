Call me Fickle9.28.2001 - Grim

Geten has been whispering in my ear again. We have decided that EQRDB 3.0 just doesn't deserve the 3.0 title it has. You can expect a massive layout change in release 3.1. Forums9.26.2001 - Grim
The forums are back. It took me long enough, but I finally got around to fixing them and boy do they run nice. No script errors, no long load times and best of all NO BANNER ADS! Let me remind you, the forums are free to anyone who would like one for a guild or gaming usage.

Since it was running so well, I added a few mods in to decrease its half life.
* Karma (now when your guildmate says something that irks you, Smite them)
* Sticky Posts (for moderators to keep posts where the guild can see them)
* Topic Rating (I'm pretty sure I installed this... but I can't find it on the board anywhere. It's fun throwing a bunch of lines of code into something and not remembering where you put it. Kinda like losing your keys and getting to go on a scavenger hunt.)

Also, I placed all the current articles in the forums as well. Now you can add your input or comments to them.

I will be working tomorrow to add links to print, comment, and save each article (from the main site).

If you hadn't noticed before, you can see the site page hit totals by clicking on 'Site Totals' under the GuildTools Network section.
