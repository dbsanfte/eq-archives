Guild War

*********
Disclaimer: This is all Leeson's idea, so all credit and all flames should be directed to him. Thank you
*********


The big question is this: What are public opinion towards the guilds creating a 'Guild RP War Team' for roleplaying and fighting versus other guilds ? Leeson's specific words are 'kinda like a guild football team'

The basic idea is for all guilds that want to play along to make a team of level 20 characters (not level 19, not level 21, but 20!) all clad in a uniform (colored Fine Steel or Raw Silk, whatever) and then sent to go to war with other guilds. Leeson has very kindly volunteered to PL everybody who wants to play to level 20 if they can't get a guildmate to do so.

The intention is to make it easier for the average member of the average guild to get into Roleplaying. We have seen that there are tons of people on our server who would actually like to RP, but either don't know where to start, or don't want to join an RP guild because they are already attached to a guild. So the idea is for the guild as a whole to get a bit into RPing, which I have seen a number of guilds express interest in, but also don't know where to start or how to get their members into it.

It might even be an idea to make a league of some sort

Some loose questions and my suggestions:

Why not use our main characters ?
Because if everybody used their level 50s, then it would become a matter of who had the most level 50s and who had the best equips. Not the intention. Intention is for the ensuring fights to be fair so that skill and tactics will win the day.

What about precise rules for equips ?
Leeson has a much better idea of all this. We'll write up a specific rules set.

What about the in-character reason for fighting ?
Each guild would need to think up an identify for their Team, are they a mercenary clan ? A group of knights defending their homeland ? etc.

How many members would a team need at a minimum
I would say 10, but this needs to be thought about. Suggestions and comments welcome.

How would 2 guilds get to fight ?
The guildmasters would agree beforehand (possibly here on this board) on a time and place and then place an ITB (Intend to Battle ) Alternatively, a guild could post a number of dates when their guild would be ready and then other guilds could 'book' the dates. Kinda like the playoffs in football.

What if we have main characters whom we have developed a story for and are RPing with ? This I don't know about. For myself I would very much like to see Elhimac be able to lead his Guardsmen into the fray, even if he himself would not be able to fight (he is level 31). I guess it might be possible for guilds to have Command HQ staff members present on the battle field to lead their army, even though they can't themselves in any way fight or assist their team short of giving tactical advice (So no buffing or anything). This really needs to be thought about.

Comments, ideas, critic, silly replies are all greatly welcome
