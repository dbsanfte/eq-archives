FORUM INFO - Information about Free Forums offered by GuildTools.Net

     Anyone can sign up for a free forum as long as it is EverQuest related. When you sign up please specify the catagory (i.e. Guilds, Misc,. etc.) you would like the forum placed in. The user name you sign up with will be the admin for the forum you create, so make sure you fill everything in correctly. An email will then be sent to you confirming the forum creation.

     We use the YaBB script for our free forums. Feel free to visit them for more information.
