EQRDB Tips

How do I make custom queries?
Match Case: cs=1
Match Whole Word: ww=1
Regular Expression: re=1
Match Any: ma=1
Keywords: keyword=?
Sort By Field Number: sb=? (where ? is the field number (see default.cfg))
Sort Ascending Order: so=ascend
Sort Descending Order: so=descend

The following examples work with the 3.1 version:
List All by Level, Descending (+ to -)
db.cgi?db=default&uid=default&sb=3&so=descend&view_records=1&key=*
List All by Class (a to z)
db.cgi?db=default&uid=default&sb=2&so=ascend&view_records=1&key=*
List All by Time Zone
db.cgi?db=default&uid=default&sb=6&so=descend&view_records=1&key=*
List Shadow Knight Only by Level (+ to -)
db.cgi?uid=default&sb=3&so=descend&view_records=1&ww=1&class=Shadow%20Knight
(%20 is interpreted by the browser as a blank space.)

Can I make a login to the Roster from my webpage?
Sure, here's the code:
<FORM METHOD="POST" ACTION="/eqrdb/db.cgi">
<INPUT TYPE=hidden name="db" value="default">
<INPUT TYPE=hidden name="login" value=1>
<INPUT TYPE="text" name="userid" size="15" class="formtexts">
<INPUT TYPE="password" name="pw" size="15" class="formtexts">
<INPUT TYPE="submit" value="login">
</FORM>

Anything Else?
Plenty. Here you go.
Direct link to signup form:
/eqrdb/db.cgi?signup_form=1
Link to list all skills:
/eqrdb/db.cgi?uid=default&db=skills&view_records=1&key=*
Link to show specific character details:
/eqrdb/db.cgi?uid=default&db=detail&view_records=1&ww=1&name=Character%20Name
