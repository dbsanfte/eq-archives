Broken Image Fix!11.28.2001 - Grim
Hello everyone. It's been a long time, I know.

I recently got an email from Gidono about an image being broken after he got his EQRDB set up and running. When I looked into the problem I realized that I forgot to add the blank.gif image along with the rest of the images. I'm afraid many people have downloaded this version and this has probably happened to them as well. As of now there have been 229 downloads of EQRDB 3.1.

I would like to thank Gidono for his feedback so that I could make the current download better for everyone else.

Broken Image Fix
Redownload the EQRDB 3.1 zip file.
Extract the blank.gif image to your local pc.
Upload the blank.gif image to your /images directory on your server

Hopefully that does it for everyone.

Sorry for the trouble

A Lesson Learned11.2.2001 - Grim
