Forums Closing? ~ Forums ~ 02-02-2004
I sent an email out yesterday that said forums would be closing indefinitely. Some of you got this email, but some were returned as undeliverable. Here is a copy of the email.

--------

I regret to inform you that I will be closing down forum hosting in the very
near future.
As you know, the forums have been provided free of charge, without banners
or ads for quite some time, which isn't very cost effective for me. So, I
have decided to close them down instead of paying even more out of my pocket
each month.

Forum admins will be provided with the entire forum zipped up to do with as
they wish. You can find a conversion tool for phpbb on their site
www.phpbb.com if your host is php and mysql enabled. I'll do what I can to
help, if you need help getting the forum set up on your host.

If you have any questions feel free to contact me.

-Grim

--------

There is one alternative. I've never asked for donations or requested them before. But some of you would like to keep your forum here instead of dealing with the hassle of moving it.

I can't tell you how much to donate, but I can tell you that 1 Gig per month costs me about $2.00. The most active forum I am hosting is pushing 6 Gigs a month currently.

Let me know if you would be interested in donating to keep your forum here and hopefully we can work something out. I would probably set up donations through Paypal.



EQ Profiles Debut ~ EQ Profiles ~ 01-30-2004
EQ Profiles is basically an extension to EQ Crafters that allows players to add more information about their characters to the still sortable and searchable database. One of the best new features this site offers is something called Mageloid. With this utility you can quickly add or update your profile with a few clicks of a button.

EQ Profiles will also offer guild support. This means that anyone who attaches your guildname to their profile will be part of a dynamic roster available to your guild. The myguild page allows an admin for your guild to change the CSS and layout of the roster. At a later date more options will be added (customized fields, html enabled, etc).

Here are two example for MyGuild: Fires of Heaven ~ Afterlife

You will also be able to view other characters on your server or in other guilds with normal site tools. The server and guild lists show you visually the stats, tradeskills, resists, and flags those characters have.

Here's an example of a server list: Mithaniel Marr

You can also access a profile of your character with a customizable background (more features coming soon). I have intentionally made the profile to match Magelo's layout so that you can use your background for that if you wish.

Here's an example of a profile: Grimiz Agaud

I hope you will like this new site, and I look forward to all feedback and/or death threats.

For those of you changing from EQ Profiles to RDB I hope to make the move as worthwhile as possible. Please let me know if there is anything you need added or changed.

Percentages! ~ EQCrafters ~ 01-19-2004
I know a lot of you have asked for this before, and it's finally implemented(ish). You can now add a percentage to your skills to represent a geerlock or another item which affects your skills. For now, it will only show up on your profile page, and it will go above 252 even though that is the cap. I will change the cap problem eventually but I doubt the modified skill will ever be added to the server lists.
