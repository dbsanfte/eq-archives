Frequently Asked Questions

Why did you start a site like this?
Well, to be quite honest, when I started this site, there weren't many (if any) like it. Soon after I got the site finished for the first time GuildMagic popped up and did what I was doing better. Since I didn't have the ability to use any scripting language except CGI, the proverbial bucket had pretty much been kicked. I stopped working on the site for a while after that because it wasn't really going anywhere, and the first time I got the script done not very many people seemed to like it.

I got to thinking, not everyone likes to click on links and load up another site just to look at a members stats/skills. So, I figured by making the script downloadable so that others may use it for their guilds, it would be all that more useful. Now, I have re-modified the script so that it is more useful and is more appealing to the eye. The guilds that use it now seem satisfied with its current state. These guilds can be seen from the main page under the 'RDB In Action' column.

Why should I want to use this script?
Being the Leader of any guild can be stressful whether it be a group of friends or an uber guild killing dragons. I have done my best to modify this script so that you have one less thing to worry about. With this script your guild members have the power to add/modify/delete their character profiles at any moment in real time (assuming your server is working). You, as admin, also have the ability to assign your offices admin status of the roster as well. That way if something doesn't look right, or notes need to be added to a characters profile, you don't have to be the only one 'stuck' with the job. Say goodbye to manually updating an HTML table week after week. Say hello to RDB!

"Dude, not all of us can use .CGI... why not do .ASP as well?"
Well, dude, there are three main reasons. Firstly, I don't have ASP available to me, so I don't feel the need to make an ASP version. Secondly, the majority of webmasters have set up .cgi scripts before and many have not used .asp .php etc.. Thirdly, I didn't even 'make' the perl script, I just modified it (heavily) for use with EverQuest.

How can you afford to do this for free?
I can't afford it! Actually the hosting is fairly cheap since I didn't purchase the ASP/PHP/CFM/ETC options. Also, I never intended for this site to be like all the other 'paypal' sites that want you to "keep them going". If you appreciate what I do then a simple email saying such is all I ask. [edit 1.31.02: after being hacked, I don't see any reason why I shoudl pay for site hosting out of my own pocket. See main page for details.]

Did you write this script?
I wish! Thank the folks over at Gossamer-Threads for this beautiful script.

I downloaded this zip file; Can I distribute it now?
The README.TXT file that came with the script states that you may NOT re-distribute or sell this script without the permission of Gossamer-Threads.

Do I have to do anything to use this script on my site?
I would simply like to know where you are using this script so that I may link to your site in the RDB In Action section.

RDB is missing one feature that would make it much better!
Hit the feedback link and I'll see if I can fit it in somewhere in the next release.

Do you need help with the site?
No, not really. I will always welcome praises and criticism though. If you have any ideas for the site I would be happy to hear them.

Why is your site so barren?
If there are no graphic images on the site, it is because I believe that a site such as this shouldn't be about how 'catchy' it is, but about how quick and easy it is for you to find what you are looking for. I have the ability to make this site entirely in Flash if I felt the need to. Obviously, I do not feel that urge. One other reason is because of bandwidth costs. Sites like EQMaps.com and allakhazams.com have recently switched to pay sites because they cannot afford to keep the servers running. Of course their database hosting costs them a good deal of money, but they also have not taken the steps to reduce the bandwidth available to the public. For instance, how many useless images are loaded on every Allakhazam page? Every time your browser reloads these images, that is money out of their pocket (YOUR pocket now).
If there are graphic images on this site, then they are done by me (from edited SS's or from scratch).

I had wanted to make versions for UO, AC, DII, DAoC, and so on, but I just don't have time to keep track of all those different mods. I have asked for help before with these projects, but no one has volunteered, so I let the issue drop. I don't intend to pick it up so please don't send email saying you would liek to help. Also, Everquest is the only game I play at the moment, so I feel that I can make a better EQ Roster Database than I could a DAoC (RDB).
