GUILD INVITE POLICY
updated : April 27th 2001

Information

So, you`re interested in becoming a member of Eternus Astralis are you?

Eternus Astralis is always looking for exceptional individuals who can bring something special to our guild.

We are a raiding guild and thus, we spend the majority of our time raiding alot. Therefor, we are not interested in casual gamers. If you have the time to dedicate to raiding and the eagerness to do new and fun things, then Eternus Astralis is a good pick. Eternus Astralis raids on average between 3 and 4 days a week, the other 3 and 4 days are spent however our members like. However, if you do not have the time to spend during our 3-4 days of mandatory raid days, then unfortunately Eternus Astralis is not the guild for you.

As we are not looking for one specific class at the moment, the best way to becoming a member would be to make friends by getting to know us by grouping with us, raiding with us and etc. By doing this, we get to know you as well and if we feel you have the right attitude, discipline and skill, we will extend an invitation to become part of our family.

You must let us know of your interest in joining by posting in our Recruitment Requests section on our message board. Be sure to tell us about yourself, about why you want to join EA and how you think you would contribute to bettering our guild.

After posting, get to know us, make friends in the guild, make a name for yourself.

When we feel you are known enough and have the right attitude, we will move your application to our private section for the vote. Our recruiting officer, Linxs, will create a post in our members section where members will have one week (1 week) to vote. A minimum of 25 sponsors are required to pass with no more than 5 votes against. After the 1 week period, Linxs will inform you on the outcome of the vote.

1. If you do not pass the vote because you have more than 5 votes against, that usually means you have some issues to work out with a few members before being able to reapply. You will not be able to reapply for a period of 2 months.

2. If you do not pass the vote because you have less than 25 sponsors *and* you have no more than 5 votes against, this usually means not enough people know you and you must continue to make friends in the guild and make yourself known to our members. You will be able to reapply in 3 weeks time.

Minimum Requirements

Eternus Astralis requires the following to even be considered for membership :

1. You must be of a minimum level of 57 (although the higher the better).
2. You must attend a minimum of 5 raids with EA (raid of 3 groups or more).
3. You must have Ruins of Kunark and Scars of Velious.
4. You must have your Sebilis Key.
5. You must have a minimum of 25 members ready to sponsor you.
6. You must have no more than 5 votes against.

Important Requirement

We oblige all prospects to work out any problems or conflicts they may have with current Eternus Astralis members. Preservation of harmony between guildmates is imperative to our success and achievement of our goals. Thus, if a prospect is seen has having too many conflicts* with existing members, the prospect will not receive an invitation (even if the minimum requirements are met) until the conflicts are worked out.

The "MORTAL ENEMY" clause

If a candidate has wronged an Eternus Astralis member to the point where if that candidate were to join the current member would leave, that candidate cannot continue in the process. Eternus Astralis has built our tight family atmosphere by being, well, a tight knit family. Officers will ALWAYS side with the guild in these kinds of matters. It should be noted however that invocation of this clause requires an investigation into the situation between the candidate and the member, and is not to be used lightly. Abuse of this clause is against Eternus Astralis policy and carries with it heavy consequences (suspension, guild removal).

Provisional Membership

If the candidate has met the above criteria, then the candidate is invited to the guild by either the Admissions Officer (Linxs) or the Guild Leader (Rovell). He/she will have access to our guild tag, chat, and private message board. As a provisional member, they are on a one (1) month probational term. During this probationary period the new member is required to do the following:

1. Disband from their current guild (if applicable).
2. Acquire resistance gear (100 for all resistances, not concurrently)
3. Acquire a water-breathing device (or have the ability to cast the appropriate spell).
4. Learn and follow guild policy.
5. Attend scheduled raids (if they are online).
6. Attend scheduled guild meetings (unless RL circumstances do not allow).

Failure to meet these requirements at the end of the one month term is grounds for dismissal.

Communique

Prospective members are NOT permitted to use the Eternus Astralis guild tag in any public posts during their probational period. They are also NOT permitted to post any derogatory or defaming comments about any player or guild without the express permission of the guild leader. Violation of this policy is grounds for immediate dismissal.

Full Membership

Full membership is granted to a new member at the end of the one month period provided they have met the above requirements.
