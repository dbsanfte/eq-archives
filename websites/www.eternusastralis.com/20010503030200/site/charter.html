Prologue

"It is said even among the most intelligent races that life began upon Norrath with the swipe of Veeshan’s claws. Long before that time though the stars watched the nameless rock for untold eons. No one really knows where the stars came from. Some say they are decorations placed in the infinite space for the Nameless One to enjoy. Some even say they are spirits of heroes from long forgotten ages. Whether spirits or adornment doesn’t really matter, what matters is they are eternal. The stars were there before this world began and they will be here when Norrath is no more. Such is our guilds name – Eternus Astralis. The name comes from a language long forgotten by Norrath’s inhabitants but when translated means “eternal star.” It is a wish for members of this guild to spread the knowledge that we serve a greater purpose in the cosmos than just achieving a level or wearing an item. Living and enjoying life are the paramount focus of this gathered body of people, spirits if you will. When you see someone with the name of Eternus Astralis in your travels, or when you look up some night and see the stars watching you, remember that what you do creates ripples upon the pond of history that echo in eternity.

-Kourath Etoile, Speaker of the Stars, of Eternus Astralis"

Structure of Eternus Astralis

Eternus Astralis is lead by one Leader and a set of Officers who make up the High Council. The High Council is in place to assure a solid leadership, to assure that the goals and objectives of the guild are carried out, to see to the best interests of the guild and it`s members as we all strive to be the best we can.

Goals and Ideology of Eternus Astralis

Eternus Astralis is a raid orientated guild. This doesn't mean we will be elitist or exclusionary. It just means we enjoy pummeling mass quantities of things together and at the same time. The most important part of any organization or guild are the members who belong to it. As we grow in members and in levels, we will strive to tackle bigger and stronger foes, bigger and more difficult challenges.

What is expected of a member of Eternus Astralis

Overall, an EA member will focus on being the best player that they possibly can be. Willingness to help anyone should they need it is paramount. Courtesy and respect need to be adhered to at all times. If things are getting so heated in game and you cannot see straight and feel like blowing off steam do it offline and not in guild chat or ooc. Obscene language needs to be held to a minimum. While I know certain situations may call for a "slip" now and then keep it in-group or in tells not in shout, OOC or guild chat.

Although it is not required for all members to attend every single event we organize, it is strongly encouraged and is paramount for certain raids, etc, to have maximum assistance (ie.: Venril Sathir, Trakanon, other uber mobs). Being on time and showing up when you say you will show up are keys to success and must be taken very seriously.

Your guild should always come first when you are online and are needed, it is your family.

Raids

During a raid there can only be ONE leader and it is expected that when you participate in any raid you respect that leader whether he/she is EA or not. If you have a disagreement with the leader then handle before the raid or after the raid in tells. If you find it too hard to continue in the raid then politely bow out and hold your opinions to yourself and discuss with the leader AFTER the raid has concluded.

Being punctual on a raid is very important. When a raid is scheduled to start at 7pm EST, it is at 7pm EST and not 7:15pm EST. Those who come late should not expect to have a spot on the raid unless otherwise noted, of course there are exceptions, but as a general rule, don`t be late.

As a general practice EA raids will be merit/ looted. The characters that have participated in more raids will be eligible for no drop stuff first. Remember that you may not get anything this time but eventually your turn will come.

Participation is expected if at all possible. If you are online during a scheduled raid and help is needed then by all means drop in. The whole purpose of EA is to have fun with friends so if a friend is in need don't hesitate to drop what you are doing and come help.

Loot

We are currently debating whether or not we should implement a points system for loot. For the time being, loot is being handled by merit. Those who invest the most time will be eligible for an item first. Earn it to own it as they say. Remember that everybody`s time will come, you may not get anything this time, but eventually your turn will come.
