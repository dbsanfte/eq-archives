Velkeltor The Sorcerer takes a dirt nap~
05-04-2001 2:15 AM EST
After being left frustrated 2 nights before when we set out to do Lord and Velkeltor only to find out they weren`t spawned yet.. We went back today after patch for our long awaited revenge on the twisted giant..

First we dropped Lord Dropmonkphatlewts.. hmm don`t have a pic but you all know what he looks like now deaded anyways.. Grats to Acturus on Frozen Efreeti Boots and to Maguus on Glowing Red Orb.

Next, we worked our way to Velkeltor The Sorcerer where he was awaiting to meet his match. We were discussing strategy while we made groups about how we would need to drag corpses quick and stuff to get ready for a 2nd and possibly 3rd rush on him, since he completely owned us when we tried him a few weeks back..
