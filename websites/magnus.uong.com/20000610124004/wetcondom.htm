The world is in darkness. It always seems to happen right around the beginning of an RPG. A whole bunch of bad guys get together and start plotting the apocalypse or something equally as nasty. And while most of these RPG worlds could be completely destroyed and only 6 or 7 people would even care, (Beyond the Beyond for instance) there's nearly always a kid ready and willing to stand up to the super evil threat to the galaxy. Well this time around, that kid is named WetCondom. He's not in it for fame, money or fortune. He wants to see the kick ass ending.
