You take a book and change the name of the title. Then you give the book to the NPC and they will say something like "Thou art giving me -book title-". You all know that when you show me something like that I'm going to have fun with it. So, at the suggestion of Geryn, here are the top ten things heard from NPCs that particular evening. 9. "Thou art giving me a booger? Tis kind of thee but I don't want it."

8. "Thou art giving me a Spice Girls CD? I am not interested in this."
