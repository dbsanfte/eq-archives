Top Ten 8/5

Hail friends,

Now that we are under the influence of the "Armageddon" patch, I'm going to crank out an impromptu top ten list. OSI just keeps on coming up with material for me. Here they are, the top ten things good about the new patch.

10. No carving from corpses. This is the first step in the archery revisions. No feathers = no arrows. This also makes cooking a much more important skill since ribs won't be so widely available.

9. Healing is back and it's here to stay! Healers all up in da house now, biatch!

8. Item decay. DD says there are too many items. They are getting rid of all items! Too many houses? They are taking care of that too. PKers using vendors for key storage? Well, they've got that covered as well. And you thought they didn't test this thing! Oh ye of little faith. Who's laughing now?

7. Skill decay. We've been seeing increasing complaints about the difficulty of raising skill points. Well, with stats in the single digits and teens, you should see your skills rocketing up again!

6. Hey! Count your blessings. Lumberjacking still works! I know this one for sure, I tried it.

5. Who needs the spell bug fixed or the house break in bugs fixed when begging is working correctly again?

4. If half the players quit, there'll be less lag and more resources for those players that stick around.

3. Work continues apace on the creature behavior upgrade! What's gonna happen in that patch? Will we have slimes running around in death robes robbing folks at the bank? Will 'I will take thee' spammers be permantly polymorphed into pigs? Will horses ride people? Will killing cows ever stop hurting karma?(Damn Chik Filet hackers!) Will GMs appear as sloths to the players? Will chickens lay coconuts? (Might bring Cyclone back.)

2. No houses = no house breakins

1. UO Extreme doesn't work anymore. Of course, nothing else does either but why quibble over details?

I apologize for the lack of real humor here but I'm more than a little frustrated this evening. Until next time, I remain...
