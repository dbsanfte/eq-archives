I began writing Ultima Online related material in early 1998. Most of the material here was posted first to the rec.games.computer.ultima.online newsgroup before it made it here. Being such, the UO stuff is often highly steeped with inside jokes that very few people will even remember. Also, given the ever changing nature of the Ultima Online game, many of the jokes are based on in game systems that no longer exist. If you're new to UO and even if you're not, these won't make a whole hell of a lot of sense to you. Hell, I wrote them and I don't understand half of them.
