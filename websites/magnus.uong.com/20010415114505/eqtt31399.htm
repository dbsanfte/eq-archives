EQ Top Ten 3/13/99

Greetings newsgroupies,

Thanks for all the warm welcomes I've recieved. I forgot to mention that I've been writing top ten lists in the UO newsgroup for over a year now. I hope to continue the tradition here. So, here's my very first EQ top ten. The top ten reasons I'm buying EQ. (I must apologize if you're expecting UO bashing, I only do that in the UO newsgroup.)

10. All the cheaters in Solitaire are driving me away.

9. I've finally given up all hope for a sequel to Sensible Soccer.

8. There's no PK switch in Quake.

7. I figure there's a better chance of meeting Lara Croft in EverQuest than in MineSweeper.

6. Sewer Shark is never gonna go online. (Did Sega finally die?)

5. Pong has just lost its appeal after all these years.

4. EverQuest babes are hotter than the ones in UO.

3. I want to see if my old video card will start smoking before it dies.

2. After 57 sequels, the MegaMan series is getting a tad repetitive.

1. I played UO for a year and a half, but not once did I ever inhale.

Could this be the start of something big? Time will tell.

-Magnus (Homeless,EverQuest)
