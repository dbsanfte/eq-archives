King Magnus

On this day, with the aid of numerous NGI members, I took the throne of the Catskills shard. British had not been seen in months and the people were asking for a new king. Who better than I? Well, that was Dundee's reasoning anyway.

To make the event seem as 'official' as possible, many NGI members dressed as town criers and announced that a new king had been named.

The event gathered a lot of people. A counselor and seer showed up, many debates raged, as well as a challenge to a duel for the throne of Britannia.

I was even showered with gifts.

Of course, with as many people gathered in one place as we had managed, there are bound to be a few morons in the crowd. I dealt with snoopers, thieves, and spammers. Beware, the following pictures contain INTENSE stupidity.

My thanks to all the nice people who showed up and especially all the NGI members who helped make this event great.
