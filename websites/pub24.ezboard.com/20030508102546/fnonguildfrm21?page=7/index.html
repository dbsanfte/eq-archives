CHARTER: Nameless Lords

  • Be it hereafter known that within the following words reside the heart and soul, the courage and commitment, and the wisdom and justice of the Nameless Lords:

  • That be ye fledging warrior or wizard or high placed sword master or dreaded dark mage, you swear to place the good of the Guild before thy own personal advancement. Bear in mind that this is not an easy thing for one to accept, for the adventurers of Norrath are ever a proud and independent ilk. But know this - it takes more bravery to subjugate ones own desire for the good
    of the many than it does to face any giant or dragon.

  • That the Nameless Lords will be known throughout the world of Everquest for their graciousness, their willingness to help the less fortunate and their deeds of valor. Woe be he that bears our Guild name and besmirches it by vainglorious acts, by speaking ill of Guild members to outsiders, by cowardly behavior or by ignoring the requests of other Guild members. The
    vile offender shall be stripped of his membership and shunned by all, his name stricken from our roll and forbidden to be spoken. For that name will become anathema to us.

  • That Guild members will treat each other with the respect normally reserved for only the highest nobility. For never was there a monarch who wore a title so cherished and so sought after as Nameless Lords. And while the Nameless will have, like any family, disputes amongst themselves, all of us - high and low - shall handle themselves with the maturity and good sense
    that is expected to be present upon acceptance into the Guild. While the Griffon and the Dragon may not share the same cave, they respect each other and share the same sky; so to will the members of the Nameless Lords accept that while love need not flourish between each and every member, respect is demanded. Open hostility will not be tolerated.

  • That Guild members shall understand that each of us is his own man or woman, even as we are one within the Guild. As such, this one will be a jester by nature and that one will be passionate. Another shall be reserved and yet another gregarious. So goes the world and so goes the Guild. Tolerance for such differences is not an option; it is etched in stone with a permanence
    that the rains of Everquest cannot erode. And the member who cannot exhibit that tolerance shall be driven out. For who would want to live in a world that consists of nothing but tranquil Rangers or one populated only by lurking Necromancers? Conformity breeds boredom and had the gods wanted all of us to think and act in the same manner, they would have made us so. Accept that we different and rejoice in that difference.

  • That Guild members shall go forth and spread the good name of Nameless Lords across the continents of Everquest, always vigilant for like-minded adventurers who can both swell our ranks and bring glory to our name.

  • These basic tenets form the brotherhood that we call Nameless Lords. All who embrace the above ideals are welcome; those who cannot accept what the words above embody must seek a different path.

    So writes Sir Bloodthorn Darkbane and so accepts the Nameless Lords Guild
