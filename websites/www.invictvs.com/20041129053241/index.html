11/1/2004 -- GUILD NEWS UPDATE posted by aradiani on 11/1/2004

I was unable to attend a couple of the raids this week, and therefore was unable to post a detailed account of those raids. I'll attempt to remedy that now. :)

October 25, 2004 - Backflagging Night - In order to reach our goal of having 90% of our members who have a raid attendance of 40% or more fully flagged for elementals before we change our focus from VT, we find ourselves frequently hitting Tier 2-3 PoP targets. This time we killed Lord Mithaniel Marr and completed the HoH Crazed Trial (aka "Virgins"). Congrats to all looters and new flag receivers.

October 27, 2004 - PoP Flagging Night - Another night of flagging saw us in the Tower of Solusek Ro. We took out Arlyxir and Jiva, the two minis we were most in need of, and called it an early night.

October 28, 2004 - Epic Night - We attempted a couple new epic encounters and weren't met with much success. Our numbers were lower than expected and we were a bit surprised by some twists that we weren't prepared for in the final fights we attempted.

First was the paladin final fight in Walls of Slaughter. We learned a bit about this fight, and even in the ensuing chaos of adds joining midfight, we got him to 37%. This fellow is going down this week.

Next was the warrior final fight. Unfortunately, we had a bunch of early loggers, and our numbers weren't anywhere near sufficient for this. Once again, we learned that this fight is certainly not beyond our reach. Hence, we'll make sure this one is on our list for this week.

October 30, 2004 - Vex Thal - Saturday found us once again in Vex Thal for another full clear. Everything died and as always there were lots of loots to go around. Fun stuff!
