    01/02/2006 -- Summoner's Ring down (Guild First) posted by sodonia on 1/2/2006

    Not much to add here today. We went to Natimbi and waited for Cragbeast Queen to show up since our raid time was just 12 minutes before she was due to spawn. But someone must have tipped her off because she mysteriously avoided us. So then we made the move to Kod'Taz to prepare for an Ikkinz 2 flashback when we noticed Summoner's Ring was up.

    Some of the raid leaders made the choice to go ahead and do this event, since we all knew some in the guild wanted it done for Breakdown in Communication, otherwise known as the Quest that makes me go Mad Mad Mad Mad. After a slight mishap with a pet and people moving early inside, we recovered and ressed at zoneline. We went back over the strat, came back to the ring, and kicked some tail like nobody's business. All I gotta say about this event is now it's made # 2 on my list of Ring Events I really hate. Right after that silly Noble's Causeway ring for Mage epic 1.5.

    After we looted the pieces and some nice P4-P5 quality loots, we moved to Ikky2. Things started off badly since we haven't done it in forever and a day and the ring rust really showed as the old duck and jab didn't quite work out. Again, the guild's favorite punching bag (me) took the first 2 Ramp Tank spots with Aradiani thankfully doing the third. Everything was starting to smooth out when we got to the Mr. Splitsalot. We had some problems with the splits combined to the late hour and decided to pack it in since it was already 2am EST. A rarity on a weekday that a raid goes this long, but with learning a new event in Summoner's (2 hours) and relearning an old event in Ikky2 (another 4 hours), things tend to pile on. Next time we come back, time won't be as much of an issue.

    Back to kicking tail on Wednesday. On a sidenote, news posts are back to one post per raid.
