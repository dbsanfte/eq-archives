12/17/05 -- Inky/OoW Fun posted by sodonia on 12/17/2005

Well, it started off poorly but we turned this mess right side up again. First target was Ptav M'sha because she was up and we were going to go to Inky anyways. Hax! 2 hp augs. No <3 for you Mrs. Goat.

Then there's the mess in Inky. First named gave us another silly aug. Trash dropped a few more to rub it in. We got to Kelefred and he tried to alphastrike us off the bat with AEs. Then kept picking on us as we were tryng to recover from the mess. To make it a short story, bugged Kelefred wouldn't become attackable. /rude Kelefred.

So rather than waste the night, we decided to go visit some old friends since the server decided to poop on our trip with a last minute crash/patch. Zun'Muram Volklana greeted us with some nice new toys.

  • Muramite's Shield of Legend - Zyzor
  • Wristwraps of Puresilk - Domaa
  • Glowing Runes x2 - Biixen and Tarlore

    • Next up was our old friend Battlemaster Rhorious AKA BM Fred. He didn't disappoint with more goodies just for stopping by and to say hi.

  • Eye of the Labyrinth Dweller - Sibar
  • Prismatic Orchid Amulet - Heelumup
  • Glowing Runes x2 - Addamiss and Casterof

    • Lirah the Bridgekeeper tried to stop us and rob us of our new toys. We taught Lirah a lesson for picking on us when Souldrainer decided to try and join in the fun. After we killed the squid we moved onto RSS. Feratha however was being stubborn and wouldn't come out to play. Ootoc tried following Skaarz's plan of "Punch him in the face and run to the raid"(tm), but it didn't seem to work. After a few mishaps, the dog and his pack decided to jump us on the stairs and we had enough of his company and packed it in for the night.

  • Longsword of the Bridgekeeper - Addamiss
  • Greater Runes x2 - Azzunoya and Lynnara
