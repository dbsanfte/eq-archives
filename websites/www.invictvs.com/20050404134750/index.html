3/21/2005 -- Epic Night -- Two new 1.5s completed! posted by aradiani on 3/23/2005

Tonight's list of epic targets saw 5 events completed, 2 of which were final fights.

Our quick hit target to start of the night was the warrior fight in Bloodfields for Skaarz. Next we moved on to Wakening Lands to finish off Dark Disciple Master, completing epic 1.5 for Claile. After that we hit up Dark Discordling Animist in Ruined City of Dranik to complete Vulpine's epic. From there, we moved to Mons Letalis to take out Elemental Essence of Earth for Qwezek. And finally we killed Vuranish Xxuro in Aradiani's 24-person Vxed instance.

It was a full night, and we have nothing but successes to show for it. Tune in next Wednesday for another round of epic madness.
