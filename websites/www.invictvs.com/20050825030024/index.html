8/22/2005 -- Making Progress in Ikkinz 4 posted by aradiani on 8/23/2005

Tonight's target was the fourth Ikkinz raid trial. We mustered early in KT and soon found ourselves immersed in this trial. We sailed through most of the instance with small bumps here and there. Ultimately we came face to face with the Alter Construct again, our bane from the previous attempt and the final encounter before the trial boss. After several experiments with positioning and various approaches to this encounter, we lost a good chunk of healing power and were forced to call it a night with the Alter Construct still standing. We learned a lot and will work out the bugs in our strategy in anticipation of our next meeting. See you again soon, Alter Construct!

LOOTS!
Aenadin - Darkglint Blade
Annalice - Taelosian Geomancy Stone Jelki
Claile - Mantle of Mending
Docfred - Carved Stone of Lucidity

All said, this was a fun night of exploration for us and we learned enough to call it a night well spent.


 
8/15 -- Adventures in Uqua posted by vulpine on 8/15/2005

Our first raid in Uqua proved to be an interesting one. With lack of targets on our saturday raid we decided to pop in and see what Uqua was about. We didn't make it far :) But it definitely gave us a step ahead for Monday though. We knocked out the first trap with no problem and breezed through only to find out the Gas Chamber to be very picky about how it works. After a few tries and that uncomfortable feeling you get when in close contact with trolls we made it through! :p

We then ran into problems because as time went on the trash got tougher and our escapades in the gas chambers relieved us of most of our AE blockers so called it with time and numbers dwindling.

Great run for a first real attempt! Uqua will be ours!

08/08/05 -- Epics and More! posted by thannis on 8/8/2005
We started off this round of epic bouts in Umbral Plains. Shortly upon zoning in, Rumblecrush decided to throw himself at us for some reason, but that's besides the point. The Netherbain Swarmfiend made an appearance shortly afterwards, and fell before the might of INVICTVS, granting us another 1.5 epic'd Shaman -- this one by the name of Bampa!

Next up was a trip to the Wakening Lands, home of the Cleric 1.5 epic's final bout, and what a bout it was. Adds were everywhere, spells were flying, but admist the ruckus was more than enough concentration and coordination to take down the icky big bad, the Dark Disciple Master. Getting to the point, the pwn was layed down, and Sanatia joined some of her fellow clerics as a carrier of the coveted Aegis.

The brawl between Venefica and the All-Seeing Eye (with his coveted All-Seeing Aggro Radius) came next in the Plane of Mischief. The XanaCrawl(tm) and the fight itself were as one sided as ever. Invictvs took down the Eye, finishing the last step of Venefica's 1.5 epic!

What was thought to be the last fight of the night was for Budian and the Marsinger Family. While Seana's planting of Nexus Crystals managed to weasle away a couple of the raids, it was nowhere near enough to save them, and Budian walked away with the Signed Agreements he was after -- one step closer to his trip to Plane of Hate and his epic 1.5!

Suddenly, the raid's gaze turned to the Nexus Scion. All that fighting around her, and she didn't do a finger to help us out!! Imagine the nerve! Invictvs and the Scion duked it out for what felt like a good 15 minutes, but in the end, Invictvs came out on top! Not even the Nexus Scion is a match for us!!!

EPIC TREASURE CHESTS
Phearumx - Ring of Hollow Faith
Aenadin - Studded Mace of Contemplation
Felith - Dull Icon of Redemption


Good work, Invictvs!! Three new epic 1.5s! Pictures please! You know where to send 'em!
