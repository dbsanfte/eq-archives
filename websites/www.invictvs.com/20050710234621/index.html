6/27/2005 -- Epic Night - Two more 1.5s done! posted by traiteuse on 6/27/2005

We gathered forces in Kithicor to try to get Neama's final kill, but once again the Blackened Treant didn't want to show his face when she had a raid in tow. We'll figure this out somehow Neama!

Once it was evident Neama's mob wasn't going to show up we moved on to Carme's fight in Dragon Necropolis. Vesthon fell without much trouble (go go kite team!), but Vesthon part II proved to be a little more difficulty. After one attempt (with much learned) we decided to move on and come back with a greater force.

Thannis's final fight was up next in Bloodfields. Marshall Hiestant fell quickly and easily in a very coordinated fight lead by Thannis himself. Gratz Thannis on his Epic 1.5! (send fallo a picture please!)

Next up we went on to complete Aakube's final fight in an instanced version of Plane of Hate and flawlessly killed the Defender. Gratz Aakube on his Epic 1.5! (Again, need a pic please!)

We got the short end of the treasure chest stick it seems with only one drop out of 3 kills:

Venefica - Studded Mace of Contemplation

Overall we were lower on numbers than usual, but extra thanks to the raid leaders and members who stepped up to lead as we have a very productive and organized night.


 
6/26/2005 -- Plane of Time posted by aradiani on 6/26/2005

We began our raid early tonight, with a less than ideal force. Surprisingly enough, this caused us little issue early on, and we cruised through Phases 1 & 2 to have extra time leftover.

PHASE 1
Bampa - Smooth Onyx Torque
Llyle - Mask of Conceptual Energy
Noaj - Pulsing Emerald Hoop
Shimmy - Smooth Onyx Torque

PHASE 2
Bammo - Drape of the Unending
Clouded - Girdle of Restoration
Elliare - Wand of the Vortex
Llyle - Shield of the Vortex
Shimmy - Girdle of Restoration

By the time we reached Phase 3, our raid had begun to fill up some. We brought in the remaining late arrivals, and topped out at 58. This phase passed quickly and was very generous to us lootwise.

PHASE 3
Anarak - Elemental Leather Pant Pattern
Bugran - Necklace of Celestial Energy
Clouded - Dagger of Distraction
Graave - Faceguard of Frenzy
Naleril - Cloak of Wishes
Nekro - Protective Sleeves, Necklace of Celestial Energy
Ootoc - Faceguard of Frenzy
Tadahh - Necklace of Celestial Energy

Next came Phase 4. We took out Tallon Zek with only some minor issues, and set our sights on Vallon. It wasn't in the cards for us tonight for a couple of reasons... errant pets, loose adds, etc. We got him to 2% on the second try, and of course by this time started to lose most of our dps classes to early logging. We opted to move on to Saryrn, and gave her that old college try... suddenly realizing we had no bard in the raid. That would have been ugly. At that point, we cut our losses and called it a night.

Tallon Zek
Narsiss - Cloak of the Falling Skies
Venefica - Tactician's Shield
Vulpine - Mantle of Deadly Precision

Once Time becomes truly instanced, we'll be moving our Time raids back to our regular raid nights. Watch the Upcoming Raids forum for details.


 
6/23/2005 -- Ikkinz Trial of Glorification done.. another GUILD FIRST! posted by aradiani on 6/25/2005

We had so much fun Wednesday in Ikkinz 1 that we decided to go back for more! We formed up in Kod'Taz while I played the invis / hide dance around the instance giver, and then we made for the temple.

We progressed through the trial without too much trouble. We did learn that the combination of key parts is oft buggy, and there's a special trick to make the stupid door open... And a few of us also developed a healthy respect for the trial mobs and just how hard they hit. (Mack Truck comes to mind.)

The boss, too, was an interesting encounter, the pull for round 2 being the most entertaining. Kudos to the bards for an excellent split (the second time, anyway). Ultimately, we walked away with a win and lots of nice loots. Congrats Invictvs on yet another guild first!

LOOTS!
Aakube - Deathwrap of Rites
Bammo - Aegis of Blackscales
Carme - Deathwrap of Rites
Thannis - Darkglint Blade
Traiteuse - Jointed Scale Greaves
Venefica - Shale Shoulderpads
