8/22/2004 -- Plane of Sky epics and PoStorms keying posted by aradiani on 8/23/2004

We ran two simultaneous raids today: one for epic items in Plane of Sky and one for BoT keying in Plane of Storms.

The Sky raid was a full clear, and so it was an 6.5 hour endeavor. (My how times have changed...) In the process, we obtained 4 Spiroc Wingblades for warrior epics, 2 robes for necromancer epic (one rotted), and 5 crowns for magician epic. Quite a day!

8/21/2004 -- Vallon Zek and Mithaniel Marr DEAD! (2 guild firsts!!) posted by aradiani on 8/22/2004

Eager for new challenges, we set our sights on new targets in the Planes. First on our list was Vallon Zek. Vallon was no match for Invictvs, and he fell quickly to our collective blade.


Vallon Zek demonstrates proper form as he lunges with his blade.

Upon Vallon Zek's defeat, we turned to the Temple of Marr's most notable inhabitant, Mithaniel Marr himself. Many paladins felt uneasy about taking on the Truthbringer, defender of all that is paladinly. But what did he ever do for us, anyway?




Marr pauses to survey his Invictvs adversaries as a mischievous ranger flings an arrow toward his posterior.

It was getting late, but we thought we might give Bertox another go, since we needed a few more flags from him. Alas, it was not our night. We went into this 2 for 2 on new events, but Bertox is a tricksome fellow, and he pulled out all his dirty tricks. Tired as we all were, we did make a few mistakes. *cough* ninja afk *cough* pet hold *cough* not enough bard sacrifices *cough* *cough* Where last time we finished with time to spare, this time Bertox ran like a scared dog after 2 hours. All the same, it was a valiant Invictvs effort, and the Plaguebringer will die again soon.
