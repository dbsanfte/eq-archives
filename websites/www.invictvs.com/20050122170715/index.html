01/17/2005 -- PoE: Dust Ring, Mystical Arbitor, and PoEb named posted by aradiani on 1/18/2005

Another guild first: Mystical Arbitor defeated!

Tonight we took on the one ring in Plane of Earth that has proved tricky to us in the past: Ring of Dust. This time we were determined not to leave without a victory, and I'm happy to report that we were not disappointed.

Perfected Warder of Earth
Defrogbishop - Circular Crystalline Formation
Sanatia - Circular Crystalline Formation

Lo! Upon completion of the Dust Ring, Mystical Arbitor appeared on our radar. We quickly cleared to his room, and dispatched him without incident. Rather lacking in climax, actually. All told, I believe there were upwards of 50 people flagged in this impromptu MA kill.

Mystical Arbitor of Earth
Bransback - Earring of Living Earth
Ebonight - Earring of Living Earth

Fresh from the kill, we were all a little excited to see the inside of PoEb, so we zoned over to Ragrax, Stronghold of the Twelve. It was getting late, so we were down to about 30 people at this point. We opted to clear the first room and take out the War Chieftans to end the night with (hopefully) some armor drops.
