10/5/2005 -- Volkara dead to Invictvs posted by aradiani on 10/5/2005

Tonight we hit some new targets for a change of pace. None of our hitlist targets were available, so we opted for the Volkara event. Several members have participated in this event before with varying degrees of success, so we had a pretty good idea of what to expect going in. In fact, we surprised ourselves a bit in just how smoothly this went. If it weren't for the unexpected round of eggs at the end that we didn't respond to in time, this would have been a flawless effort. Great job, Invictvs!

Congrats then to Treyna, Ootoc, Llyle, Leave, Kaylic, Smaq, Docfred, Bampa, and Facell on your Volkara progression pieces.

Volkara
Carme - Ring of Unsung Legends
Zyzor - Summoner's Rune Belt

Next we decided to take on our first MPG raid trial. We didn't last very long, but we learned a thing or two for next time. Be sure to add your comments to the ongoing strategy discussion on the boards!
