2/21/2005 -- EPIC NIGHT! posted by aradiani on 2/22/2005

Epic night rolled around again, with a lot of targets requested. Of course, we can only do so many in a night, so we narrowed it down to the wizard event in PoNightmare, the ranger event in Bloodfields, and the cleric event in LFay.

Annalice was our quickhit target for the night. We dispatched her mob in Plane of Nightmare and then moved on to Bloodfields for Kaylic. The Bloodfields fight has proven tricky for us in the past. We've done this several times for poor Kaylic, and the last time we tried it we actually completed it only to find that the final "hail" guy was bugged. This time he behaved and Kaylic got what he needed.

Next we moved on to Lesser Faydark to do the cleric fight for all of the clerics who were ready for this step. Of those, Claile and Tarlore were present. Congrats to them both on completion of that event.
