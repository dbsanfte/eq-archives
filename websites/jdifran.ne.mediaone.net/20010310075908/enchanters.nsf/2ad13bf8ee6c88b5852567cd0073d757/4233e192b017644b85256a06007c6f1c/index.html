I started as soon as I could afford to buy the supplies! If you can beg, borrow and steal the funds to buy +int stuff, it will be a huge help!! Also see if friends will lend you +int armor to help...Getting to 100 or 150 really doesn't cost much if you are careful, and far less if you take the time to enchant all your stuff first. And...I used to use jewelcrafting as an excuse to get to know people and at the same time...sell my wares for profit. I would sit at a popular corner and advertise free buffs with a hello... You can start making money as soon as you get to skill of 25 to 30..selling the basic silver stuff for 4 or 5 pp in the newbie zones.
Trust me...when you get to higher levels, it pays off big time! I made 7000 pp selling to one person just this past weekend!!
