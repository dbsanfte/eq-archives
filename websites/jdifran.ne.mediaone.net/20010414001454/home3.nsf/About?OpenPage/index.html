About JMD Training & Consulting

JMD Training & Consulting is a Qualified Lotus Business Partner owned and operated by John M. DiFrancesco, a Certified Lotus Instructor and Professional.

JMD Training & Consulting was founded in November, 1996 to provide Lotus Notes and Domino training services to LAEC's across North America, and consulting services to small and medium-sized businesses in northern New England.
