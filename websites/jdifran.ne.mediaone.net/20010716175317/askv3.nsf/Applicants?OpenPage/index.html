ASK Applicants


So, you want to join ASK, do you?


ASK has a great membership policy: ALL APPLICATIONS WILL BE ACCEPTED! Yes sir, we will accept an application from ANYONE.

Of course, we don't guarantee that will will actually APPROVE your membership request.

To be approved as a member of ASK, you need to prove that you possess Yee. To do so generally requires participating in an ASK-sanctioned multiplayer game, joining up with some current members, and demonstrating your "joie de vivre" and, more importantly, your "joie de mort". In order to be accepted into ASK, you will need to be recommended by three existing members who preferrably did not know you prior to applying.
