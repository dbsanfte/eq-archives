Searching this Discussion
...tips on finding what you need...

Searching is View-specific
This means that if you Search from the Spells view, you'll only find Spells posts that match your search criteria. Suggestion: Either search All Posts, or pick one of the Category specific views. Don't search the Recent Posts view unless you want to limit your search to the last 7 days' posts.

Searching
Click on this symbol... Search this view ... at the top of any view to get to the Search Screen.

Search Syntax
You can use the Boolean operators AND and OR in your searches. Enclose "phrases in quotes" if you need to find words next to each other.
