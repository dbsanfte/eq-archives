As long as you get a sow, killing bloodgill maruaders is np.... the hard part is to pull them out of the water, when you done that (air elemental illuison, and sow outruns the gobos when ur over the water) then just charm one (pull 2... hehe) help the charmed one out with some dds and maby a dot, (try to keep it alive) if your pet dies, the other gobo should just have a few hundred hps left, so wasy to nuke down, if you pet lives, he should have low hps, so you can nuke him down easliy....

I have been doing this a lot on lev 32 now (at first i had a 45 druid to pull for me, but then i just killed 1 and 1..)

Note: some of the bloodgills are too high for charming, then just charm the other and let him kill tho highest....

My cha is 150 or so with buff, and my int is 165....

Good luck !!
