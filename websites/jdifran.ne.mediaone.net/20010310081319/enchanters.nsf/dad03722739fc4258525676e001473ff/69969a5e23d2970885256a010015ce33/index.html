Easiest way...
by Keyr Aldron (02/27 10:58 PM EST)



...is to have lots of money and lots of time. Unfortunately, getting a high jewelcraft skill is not easy. If you want to work on it with the money you have, you might be able to get up to 40ish skill level (with enchanting the metal and selling it back to vendors) if you are lucky (been a long time since I started =)).

If it is not mandatory to get your skill up, I'd wait until you were higher level and have more money. I think I started around lvl 16, but I wish I would've waited. In my opinion, jewelcrafting doesn't pay off as much as it used to at higher levels (190+ skill level) and you'd probably be better off leveling and saving up your money for spells. Gear isn't that important until 30ish or so (I had my n00b robe until 24 /duck). Just my 2 cents... =)
