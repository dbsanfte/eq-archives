Chronicles of the House

Within these halls of knowledge, you will uncover the collected knowledge of the House al-Nasir, a vast amount of information gathered from the far corners of Norrath. Know that some consider such knowledge a dangerous thing, and go forward with this warning: Not everything within these tomes is known to be true, and not all that you see with your eyes is to believed. Information, as with all else, is only as good as its source...

Artifacts of Norrath - The collected notations of various scholars on the uncommon and rare items which are often sought after by the citizens of Norrath.

Atlas of Antonica - These small travellers guides have served many travellers well over the years. While the information in these tomes is kept as recent as possible through the tireless efforts of the House members, there is still much to be learned.

Bestiary of Norrath - This well worn classic contains information on all the various beasts which have been encountered by members of the House in their travels, as well as tips and tricks on how best to deal with them should you find yourself on their bad side.

Quests - Information from the known regions of Norrath regarding the adventures that await, the treasures that lie hidden, and the rumours unconfirmed.
