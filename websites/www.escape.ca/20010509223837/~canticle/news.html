April 25th, 2001: Background Updates

Sorry for the lack of updates, but currently most of the work being done on New Dawn is in the planning and design stages. Without the editors, all we can really do is work on story line and mission ideas. There are a few new things to report however.

In the Background section we've updated some of the timeline/storyline issues. It's been decided that Fallout Tactics: New Dawn will be taking place roughly five years after the events depicted in Fallout Tactics: Brotherhood of Steel. We realize a lot of folks are very unhappy with the storyline in BOS and have issues with how the Brotherhood was portrayed. We feel we have come to a resolution which allows us to follow Fallout 'canon' (the 'official' events of the Fallout universe as depicted in the Interplay games) while also addressing some of the more glaring plot holes evident in BOS. I'm afraid that's all I can really say at this point other than 'trust us'.

April 14th, 2001: Fallout Tactics: A New Dawn page launched.

The purpose of this site is to support and maintain an ongoing campaign for the Fallout:Tactics community. The campaign will be built using the Game Editors which are scheduled to be released by Microforte. This campaign will consist of several files which may be downloaded from this site, and the supporting storyline which will be provided. This site is currently in the working stages, and even the title is a work in progress. Until the actual editors are released, most of the work which will be done on this site will consist of storyline updates and background material for the campaign world.

The storyline, obviously, is based on the 'official' timeline of the Fallout Universe, although for obvious reasons anything which takes place during the campaign will not be considered 'official' or 'canon' by any stretch of the imagination.

The Storyline of the campaign is set about a decade after the events which take place in Fallout:Tactics. As the game world the campaign will cover stretches from the West Coast of California to the eastern borders covered in Fallout Tactics, and from Canada to the Mexican border from North to South, this decision was made to provide the maximum possible opportunities to explore the campaign world.

Please note that while there are several community projects currently in the works and are open to submissions from the Fallout:Tactics community, this particular site is set up so that a local Fallout:Tactics campaign, designed and produced locally, can be made available to the players in the community at large. We are not currently accepting applications for artists or editors, and exist simply to provide our works to the Fallout:Tactics players at large. While this may change in the future, it is not currently in our plans.

Welcome aboard!
