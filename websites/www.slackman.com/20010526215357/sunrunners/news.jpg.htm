11/10/2000

-Microsoft Announces Release of DirectX 8.0 is the press release with word that the new API has been released to manufacturing. According to the announcement, "Both the DirectX 8.0 SDK and DirectX 8.0 Runtime are scheduled to be available for download before 12:01 a.m. PST, Saturday, Nov. 11."  (Blues)

-3dfx Gamers has released new Voodoo 4/5 drivers for Windows 95/98/ME and for Windows 2000 (both WHQL certified). They fix several specific problems with the Serious Sam and Hitman demos and a few other games, and add DirectX 8 support, which itself will be officially released in the near future according to this Microsoft press release. (Blues)

-GameSpy.com also has a lonely EverQuest: The Scars of Velious screenshot on display, introducing the new Direwolf model in Verant's MMORPG expansion. (Blues)

-The ultra-close U.S. presidential election -- which could take days to determine -- has made the case for Internet voting as no other political event could have done, online experts said on Wednesday. Instead of a long wait for a recount of the votes in Florida for Democrat Al Gore and Republican George W. Bush to be completed, online experts said Internet voting would only take minutes to recheck. (Hulka)

11/09/2000

-Although it doesn't contain any new screenshots, GameSpot has posted their first impressions of Scars of Velious, the soon-to-be-released add-on for EverQuest. The preview is based on what they saw at the SoV launch party in Las Vegas, and explains in great detail how it connects and improves upon the original game. Another report can be found on EQ fan page Everlore, which contains screenshots that were taken via a camera pointed at a monitor running the game.  (Blues)

5/24/00

-On sunday, Suzail, Monorail, and Artukus, Ransacked the Aviak Isle in the Ocean of Tears, in search of the Elusive Soarin Brightfeather and his wonderous spear.

After an hour of searching, and a lot of Aviak deaths. Soarin decided to show his face. With this Suzail won his Ebon War Spear. After a further hour of death and destruction (on the Aviaks part), we managed to coax Soarin out another 2 times, winning a further 2 Ebon War Spears, 1 for Monorail and 1 for Artukus. With this victory we have decided to rename this Isle Sunrunner Island.
