Once upon a time there was a Monk named Mindert. Mindert had been initiated into the 30th circle for some time now. In fact he was quite close to being promoted. Mindert had been Traveling with Talcey for some time. Talcey was a beautiful buxom barbarian lady with a heart of gold and a flirtatious bent that would make a madam blush. Talcey and Mindert had seen most of the world together and had decided to see the sights in Kunark.
 
    For some time now Mindert had been hearing stories of some fantastic Monk weapons and armor that could be found in a mystical place called Dalnir. This is where the fabled Monk Fighting Batons DA8 DE20 were rumored to be found. So Mindert prevailed upon Talcey to visit this wondrous place. Now Talcey was slightly older than Mindert but mindert was beginning to equal her in power, so together they made a frightening force, but when the got to Dalnir they were daunted.
 
    You see, while they could defeat the crypts denizens singly or in twos. There were literally hundreds of nasty critters in this place. It was at this time that the ever wise Talcey advised caution and proposed a plan to reside near the entrance to this terrible place for a time to learn their habits and patterns. All the while gathering their power for the inevitable strike into the heart of the crypt and the wondrous monk treasure therein.
 
    This satisfied Mindert for a time but as with all younglings this monk soon became impatient for his reward. So one day when Talcey was asleep in her Camp, Mindert struck out on his own. Now remember while Mindert is well known for his fighting prowess, he is not well known for his intelligence, and rightly so. You see for the next two hours mindert snuck his way ever deeper into the Crypt of Dalnir, at one point falling nearly 200 feet through a hole, as it was the only way to continue. Now he was committed to his course. He continued deeper and deeper, every time something threatened him he would pretend he was dead (Which he was getting quite good at) and they would go away. Finally Mindert reached his goal, The Lumpy Goo !!!!
 
    It was this point that Mindert looked around and realized what he had done! He was in the middle of an unfriendly Crypt surrounded By hundreds of creatures that were nearly as powerful as he was, with no way out, pretending to be dead. It was at this point that Mindert got scared! Mindert spent the next 30 min pretending to be dead, reflecting on what he had done, trying to figure out a way to get home and cleaning out his shorts. Now remember when I told you that Mindert wasn't particularly bright? Well he got impatient again. He could have waited for his friend Talcey to come down, make him invisible and the two of them could just walk out. But he didn't!
 
    Mindert decided to work his way out just as he had gotten in. After all he was a powerful Monk! So Mindert stood up and started running........He made 5 steps.
