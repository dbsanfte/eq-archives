And the monk would be a Smith.

This is a short little anecdote for all you monks out there who wondered at the reason you cannot fight all that well wearing your Warrior friends suit of Plate. This is also a warning for anyone who would be a Smith.

Remember our intelligent little friend Mindert? Well one day while practicing the manly art of sewing he realized that the raw silk he was working on had become too easy. (But still quite comfortable, and cool). Mindert deduced that he must find new challenges to his Tailoring abilities so he asked around. He was told that he should try working leather again, but in order to improve his skills he must concentrate on not Tattering his pieces like he used to. Now Mindert, being a bright sort deduced that if he started off with a higher quality pelt then he would get a better looking result, but why would someone pay for something that did not function better, something that only looked better.

Again Mindert asked around and this time he found out that if he added metal studs to his pieces, then people would start to pay the higher fees that he would have to charge. Problem solved right? ….Yea right!…. Mindert being a rather impatient fellow went looking for someplace to purchase these metal studs and found quickly that the price for the studs was about the same as the price for the finished pieces. "No problem" he said, "I will make them myself" he said, "It cant be that hard. There only little pieces of metal" he said. ….Yea Right!…..

So after some research Mindert found the Ore shop in North Freeport and started making metal bits….. and metal bits….. and metal bits….. and metal bits….. and metal bits….. Now you must understand this is the most attention he had ever paid to anything in his life. This was progress. Remember when I told a long time ago that Mindert is a little impatient? Well guess what? Mindert had been told a number of ways to get better at smithing really fast. One of the ways was to make metal sheets. No problem! Metal sheets are made using 2 blocks of ore. No problem!

Mindert went to his ore shop in North Freeport and filled 3 backpacks with blocks of ore. At great expense I might add. He then set out to forge metal sheets. …. He then set out to forge …… He then set ……. "HEY ?!? Why am I not moving ?!?!?" The cry was heard across the zone. It took mindert a good 5 min to realize that he couldn’t pick up any of his packs! He looked at his total weight. 327 ?!?!? "How do I fix this one?"

For those of you who have ever sold anything BACK to a merchant, you know that they are the most ruthless business people alive ! When Mindert finally left the shop he had one pack full of ore and considerably less money than when he went in! He then proceeded to the forge, failed all 4 attempts to make a metal sheet and promptly went back to making metal bits………….
