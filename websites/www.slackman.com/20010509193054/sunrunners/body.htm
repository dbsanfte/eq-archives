In the early days of September 1999 the original 10 members formed the Sunrunners.  Guild leadership was passed to Artukus from Volitic September 20th. In early 2001 the guild leadership was passed from Artukus to Mismort. Mismort is the present leader along with his co-leader Redwing
Our guild is a tight family, whose maininterest in Everquest is to have fun. We currently have a minimum level restriction of 40 to be considered for new membership in our guild. We are a tight group of people and want to keep it that way. Therefore we are picky as to whom we allow in the guild.

Currently we are looking for the following classes:

Enchanter
Cleric
Warrior
Shadow Knight
Monk

To register for consideration in the Sunrunners please post your name, level, and usual play times here:Prospective member forum. New member registration is not limited to those classes. They are just the classes we are the most interested in.
If you are just passing through please feel free to browse. Welcome to the Sunrunners web-site!
