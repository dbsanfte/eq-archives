In the early days of September 1999 the original 10 members formed the Sunrunners on the Erollisi Marr server.

Our guild is a tight family, whose main interest in Everquest is to have fun. We currently have a minimum level restriction of 40 to be considered for new membership in our guild. We are a tight group of people and want to keep it that way, therefore we are picky as to whom we allow in the guild.

Currently we are looking for the following classes:

-Enchanter
-Cleric
-Warrior
-Shadow Knight
-Paladin
-Bard
-Rogue
-Necromancer

To register for consideration in the Sunrunners please post your name, level, and usual play times here: Prospective member forum. New member registration is not limited to those classes. They are just the classes we are the most interested in.
If you are just passing through please feel free to browse. Welcome to the Sunrunners web-site!

The guild has had many accomplishments and a few failures. We have had heroes and traitors.  We shall learn from all our experiences and grow to match the future. We are the Sunrunners.
