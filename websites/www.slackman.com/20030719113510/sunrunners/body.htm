In the early days of September 1999 the original 10 members formed the Sunrunners on the Erollisi Marr server.

Our guild is a tight family, whose main interest in Ever Quest is to have fun. We currently have a minimum level restriction of 40 to be considered for new membership in our guild. We are a tight group of people and want to keep it that way, therefore we are picky as to whom we allow in the guild.

The Sunrunners have RE-OPENED recruiting as of July 1st!

We have had the time to get to know our larger family, and are now ready for more! Come one, come all! It is also great to see so many former Sunrunners of old returning to the flock!

We have also made the decision to raise the level requirements for entry into The Sunrunners to the following:

Level 50+

Clerics, Rogues, Paladins, and Enchanters

Level 55+

Bards, Druids, Magicians, Monks, Necromancers, Shadow Knights, Shamans, Warriors, Wizards

Level 60+

Beast Lords, Rangers

Feel free to post you desire for applicant status, and we will process it, once we get used to our new members.

The Sunrunners appreciate your interest in our family, and will be certain to get back to you all in July!

Thanks!

To register for consideration in the Sunrunners please post your name, level, and usual play times here: Prospective member forum. New member registration is not limited to those classes. They are just the classes we are the most interested in.

If you are just passing through please feel free to browse. Welcome to the Sunrunners web-site!

The guild has had many accomplishments and a few failures. We have had heroes and traitors.  We shall learn from all our experiences and grow to match the future.

The Sunrunners!
