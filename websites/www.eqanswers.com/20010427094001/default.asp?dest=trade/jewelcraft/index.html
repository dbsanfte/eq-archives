Mastering Jewel Craft

Mastering the skill of enchanting precious stones of glistening colors and metals of great strength is one not only interesting, but also of the great powers one can instill into items which did not posses such a power before. In order to master this trade skill you will need a fair bit of money to start out and then time to sell off your items to create more profit. If you want to get from 1 to 200 in skill the fastest, you’ll need a ton of money and not bother enchanting your items; just create and sell back to the NPC merchant, but then again not all of us have that kind of platinum.

Making the items

In order to start your career of becoming a successful jeweler one must have the essential components. However, even before-hand you must at least put 1 point into your jewelcraft skill, which can be learned from any of your guild masters. Because jewelcraft is not the easiest skill to improve in level, I would strongly suggest you put the whole 20 points into the skill before trying to attempt anything further.

This guide is geared towards the Enchanter class because this is the only class that can truly harness the raw powers of enchanting metals. Jewelry is mainly used by this class because of that reason, and the majority of wanna-be master jewelcrafters are Enchanters. However, we do not have limited options here at EQAnswers, and so if you are another class interested in doing some serious jewelcraft, please tell us and we will create another guide. Though at this time we do not see a need for it.

If you are less then level 4, don’t even bother about starting jewel craft. If you are over level 8, you are in prime place to start your trade because you get your first metal enchant Enchant Silver at level 8.

 

Beginner (The Silver Years)

You are at skill twenty-one. You want to get to making Platinum Ruby Veil’s and quickly. Failure is necessary. Start with Onyx; go to the nearest jeweler and buy 40 silver bars, and 40 Onyxes. Sit down and enchant all of your silver bars, this takes a while, but that is ok. While you are medding making rings and auction. Put one Onyx, and one Enchanted Silver Bar in you kit and hit combine. Repeat this until you make skill 28. Along the way you can auction and sell these for about 2pp apiece.

Now you will move on to amber. Amber makes +2 str rings, every tank out their wants them and you can sell them for about 5pp per ring. You will be able to get most people for level 5 to 15 on these rings. Again stock up and do a whole batch at once. Sell everything to PC’s. This will take you to 35.

Here is your poor stage. You will be making Emerald Rings, +5 fire save, +2 AC. Don’t bother enchanting the bars here. Just make the rings and sell them back to the vendors. No one wants these. If you need money, use lower costing gems and sell them to the PCs. The best ones now would be: +2 INT Star Rose Quartz Rings for 3pp, +2 STR Amber Rings for 5pp, and +5 Mana +5 HP Jade Rings for 6pp. This will bring you to 46 eventually.

Apprentice (The Electrum Years)

Now is time to move on to Electrum because it will be cheaper in the long run to use these failures. Start off with Electrum Malachite Bracelets until you hit skill 74. Once again do not bother enchanting the bars, these will not sell. You will fail a lot so go back to silver to make your money.

You will now move on to Onyx pendants, these can be resold for 5pp, not as wanted as some others so once again fall back to the silver. This will bring you on to skill 90. Here you can start making Silver Ruby Vales for your money.

From 90 skill, you should make Electrum Amber earrings until 100 skill, and then switch to Electrum Topaz earrings until 111.

Master (The Golden Years)

Gold is your next step. And here it gets extremely expensive, so start out with creating Golden Malachite Earrings from 111 til 146 in skill. These won’t sell to players very well because of the stats, and it would be suggested to not enchant them and sell the item back to the NPC merchant. If you start running out of money, you should make the occasional Jaded Electrum Bracelet or an Electrum Jasper Earring and sell to the players. Once you reach skill 146 start making Gold Cat’s Eye Rings until the skill of 156 and keep at least two of these rings because they will improve your sell prices to the NPC shops.

Once you get to the skill of 156 it's time to make some serious cash with Gold Onyx Necklaces until 162. These will sell well to players, so make sure to enchant them and start selling. Next will be from 162-170 in skill and these will sell very well (Gold Rose Quartz). At 170 make Gold Jade Bracelets, and these will supply you with loads of cash just because of the supreme demands for these items. Finally at 180 in skill make Gold Peridot Bracelets which should take you to skill 186. Then make Gold Ruby Rings until 190.
