Mastering Baking

Of all the skill within this great land we call Norath, baking is not one of the more lucrative or widely used skills. It may however be one of the more entertaining ones. Just think of the happiness brought on by receiving a rat ear sandwich from a friend for lunch. Or sitting on the bank of Kerra island with a loved one, watching the sunset eating some edible goo. Some things can not be bought with money.

Well for those of you aspiring chefs, please do read on. The best way to start your culinary success is with the to mentioned delicacies, Rat ear sandwiches, and edible goo. What you need for edible goo is simple, a rat ear, and a fire beetle eye. So go shopping, and get a couple of each, make sure they are not too ripe, they need to be firm and fresh. If you are in a hurry and just want a quick bite, you can combine almost any meat you find, such as rat meat, wolf meat, or others with some store bought bread for a sandwich.

Of course the truly great chefs carry a spit with them all the time, it is simply a smaller portable version of an oven.

Once making sandwiches simply becomes to easy for you, you can try making fish fillets, simply combine some fish, with a jug of sauces and you will have two fish fillets. Once that too is simple I would recommend making fish rolls, a bat wing and fish, for a tasty snack. By the time this too has become nothing to a chef like yourself you will be considered a master in my book and can try some other more difficult recipes, some of my favorites have been listed below.
