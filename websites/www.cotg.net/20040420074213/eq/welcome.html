We're back from the grave and ready to party!!!!!!

Well, after nothing for the last 8 months, Khazz "The Ranger of Danger" Zikthule has taken over the reigns of the Companions of the Gate and will be leading it to infamy, yet again. Joining Guild Leader Khazz will the Best of the Best returning, Moonshadow, Dalbo, Cilene, Tarthug, Kudani, and Ronar to help bring the fun atmosphere back to Norrath. Each will be Class Officers in the guild, with final member addition decisions going to the illustrious Khazz. Look for fun events and exciting times coming up. Message Board access will be reset and restarted for freshness.

So without further fanfare, see you all in Norrath and stay tuned for the future news!!!!!
