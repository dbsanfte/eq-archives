Farbain sat against a tree in West Commons feeding himself foraged grapes. He looked very much at rest, but his shifting eyes and keen senses took in everything. The exception was the wood elf ranger sneaking up behind him. With a sudden playful movement she swatted him on the back of the head. The grape he had just put into his mouth exited with force, landing a dozen paces in front of him. Composing himself, he looked up at Elentari with a look of dissatisfaction.

“Am I going to have to tie bells to your boots sis?”

“Nope, but you can try.” She said, the mirthful expression not leaving her face.

 “Instead of just staring at the inn, why not just go in and join the others? The service is better inside.”
