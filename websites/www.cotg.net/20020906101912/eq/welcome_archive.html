It is with heavy heart that I bring this news to you my friends. Moonshadow and I received this new back in March, but somehow we missed the e-mail altogether. On reviewing our mail, this is what we found

It is with heavy heart, that I pass on these unfortunate tidings. You may not remember me, I am a real life friend of the man you knew as Arazhan Runehammer, and we have spoken before, but not frequently. I regret to inform you, that our friend has passed on. Scott died Friday, March 10th in an automobile accident. Scott introduced me to this pastime we have in Everquest, and spoke of you all so highly. Recently he had spoken with me about missing all of you in the guild, as his time had become so limited with his job and family. I am destroyed, as I am certain all of you knew him to be kind and good-hearted, in real life he was a man of the noblest of statures, and I will miss him so deeply. To make things all the more tragic, Scott leaves behind two young children and a wife of nine years. I ask that all of you please remember Scott's family in your prayers. I had debated so long wether or not to post you and let you know, but I decided that you were all his friends too, and have every right to know what has happened to our lost friend.

During his time with us, I had come to respect and admire Arazhan, and have missed his company very much. Now I know why he came to be missing from our home. Arazhan was one of our first non-founder members, he also played the character Cyntax - a Rogue. They joined the guild around June 10, 1999. This message was passed to us by his in-game friend Brittonaliel.

May 09, 2000

There is very little that could ever happen to make me post on the message of the day, but this news hit me pretty hard, and I felt it deserved to be put up on the guild news section. A guild that I have had profound respect for these past twelve months has decided to call it quits. The guild I refer to is Innoruuk's Brood. The Companions was founded on good roleplaying and was modeled partially in the spirit of what Innoruuk's Brood has accomplished. The following is the eulogy for this great organization.

~Lythander Lotus'Root~

Guild Eulogy

Its been just over a year now that seven tier'dal, two trolls and a human joined together to form the guild, Innoruuk's Brood. The guild has grown and shrank in size over time, members have come and gone, there were also those who faded away and were never seen again. Now as time has passed, it seems through all our differences the guild has grown apart. The time has come for those of the brood to form their own alliances, to find our place in the world and serve The Father in their individual capacities. To pass on the knoweldge and teachings we learned in the brood and bring that knowledge to others. There will be those who find their place amongst friends, others may wander a solitary life, either way we will always share a bond, we will always be part of what was once Innoruuk's Brood. Go out and spread HIS hate, children of Innoruuk.

Keziah Tho'tep
