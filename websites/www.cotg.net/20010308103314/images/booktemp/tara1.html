I smile at Elentari as she steps into the small room I keep in Rivervale, small of course to me, I do not doubt that it is quite large to a halfling.

     I half know why she is here, for she has recently become the guilds historian, and histories are never complete without the history that proceeds them. Still I carry hope, that she will not ask me what I dread, but rather that she is simply here to share a drink and perhaps a recount or two of the battles we have been in in the past. My hope fades however as she removes the thick sheath of papers from her backpack and a quill.

"Might you have an inkwell," she inquires, her voice enchanting with its vitality and seeming youthfulness. I give a slight nod, and bring forth one that I had recently been using in my research of spells, the ink still quite liquid from my aborted efforts.
