Badafi sits next to you at the dock bar in Freeport, perched on a stack of pilfered.."um borowed" she informs you, cookbooks, to have a better view.

She leans into you and says, "Whats that? You wanna know how I cames ta be warrior?" Her eyes crinkle with laughter.

"Well now, ya see, I grew up in Kaladim. A rather sheltered life it was"
She then winks at a group of dwarves across the room, and motions the barkeep over. "See those handsome lads over there? Send 'em a round on me, and none of that lousy fish wine, ya hear?"
The barkeep nods, and Badafi turns back to you.
"Ah, where was I?"

"A sheltered life in Kaladim..." you reply. Badafi nods and fiddles with with the top of a brandy bottle.
