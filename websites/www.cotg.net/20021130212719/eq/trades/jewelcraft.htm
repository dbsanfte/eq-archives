OK, for Jewel Crafting do the following.....

1. Go to Casters Realm and print out the Jewel Chart - eq.castersrealm.com/misc/..._table.asp

2. decide if you care about selling to people then mark the things you think you can sell (int stuff, wis stuff, jade stuff, str stuff sell best)

3. Go to Casters Realm and print out the Trivial Chart - eq.castersrealm.com/misc/...ivials.asp

4. decide whether money is an object.

5. if money is no object follow the trivial chart

6. if money is an object, once a given set of materials is more expensive then the cheapest set from the next metal grade up (ie. Jade and Silver is more expensive then Electrum and Malachite) - start the next metal with the cheapest stone

7. Repeat ad nausium until you max at 200 with Gold Fire Emerald (never trivial)


From experience - the higher the wisdom the more successes you will have, the higher the int the faster your skill will progress. Enchanted jewelry sells for more then unenchanted jewellry.

Well, it appears I forgot the most important fact, where to get the things you need. The Enchant metal spells are only found in the ligrary in the 3rd Gate of Neriak. To find where to buy the metals and gems, go to Casters Realm at eq.castersrealm.com/misc/..._locs.asp to find the place most easily accessable to your race/class.
