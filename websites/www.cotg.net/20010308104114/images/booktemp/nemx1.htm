Nemx was born with his brother, Renox, under the foolish care of a drunkard of a druid who didn't do his job too help the family out, and a mother who flirted with every wood-elf male she saw.
As a baby, Nemx was dropped on his head far too often.. from the top of Kelethin, to the forest floor down below.
With his incredible hard head, he survived these falls and lived to become old enough to venture out by himself into the world.

With some very hard times growing up, it was all for the best. Good things were to come upon Nemx during his life.

Bound only in leather armor and wielding minotaur axes, Nemx struck it rich with the discovery of an Abu-Kar box, and quickly sold it for 700pp at the young age of 17.
