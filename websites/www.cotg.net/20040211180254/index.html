Creative Outsource Task Group is owned and operated by Monica Wishnousky.

While working for systems integrators I noticed that smaller clients who needed more personal assistance in managing their projects often had difficulty finding qualified assistance.  After many months of planning and deliberation, COTG was born.  I chose to operate as a Virtual Assistant in order to reduce costs for my clients, and to have the opportunity to work with people around the world.

Why hire a Virtual Assistant?
Virtual Assistants can be of great assistance to small businesses. Small businesses both home based and small offices can accomplish so much by utilizing a VA. If you think about it you are probably getting bogged down in busy work. Things that are taking you away from promoting your business. How much wasted time and energy are you putting forth just answering routine questions, bookkeeping, filling out tax information, mailing out brochures. Your time is much more valuable if you are promoting your business and not slaving on tasks that someone else can do for you. What projects would you love to complete but just don't have the staff time or skills to devote to it?

When you hire a VA, you only pay for the time you asked your VA to work, either by task or project. You do not have to pay unemployment insurance, sick leave, vacation pay, health or dental insurance, nor do you have to sponsor a retirement program, and there is no requirement for extra office space, furniture, computers, software, telephones, printers, your normal employee staff requires.


How can you work for me when you're not here?
You can communicate with COTG via Email, telephone, fax, online meetings, overnight mail, whatever makes the most sense for the particular project we're working on.
