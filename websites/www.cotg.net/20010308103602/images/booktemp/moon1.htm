Running from the wizards pyramid in West Commons to the Druid circle, I pace impatiently waiting for Lythander and Elentari to arrive. Even thinking of them makes me smile, Lythander, the charming druid (rogue that he is) and Elentari, first sister of my heart.

There is a shimmer in the druid circle and they appear before me, Elentari giggling and Lythander holding his ear. Hmm, I'll have to ask for THAT story.

I run forward and embrace them both, I am always happiest when surrounded by my chosen family. Though I love my birth family... but that is for Elentari's story.

With a quick hug and a wink Lythander departs for another adventure... strange though, he seemed almost... embarrased.
