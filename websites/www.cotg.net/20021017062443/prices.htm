Prices

We have a variety of pricing plans to accommodate each client's specific needs. Below is a list of our billing options, prices, and policies.

By the Hour
We recommend this plan for small, time-based projects.
Project Management hourly rate is $50.00
Office Support hourly rate is $30.00
All rates are billed in 1/4 hour increments.  A 25% deposit is required before work begins. The balance is payable prior to the delivery of the completed project.

By the Project
You may prefer to pay for our services on a "per project" basis. With this option, we provide you with an quote for the projected cost. A 25% deposit is due before work begins. The balance must be paid prior to delivery of the completed project.

Discount Retainer Packages
Our discounted retainer packages are the ideal option for clients who require our services on a recurring basis. Package prices are paid monthly in advance, and additional hours are billed each month end.  Statements are emailed monthly. Unused amounts are not refunded.

- Save 10% off our standard hourly rates.

- Silver - $500
- Save 15% off our standard hourly rates.

- Gold - $800
- Save 20% off our standard hourly rates.
