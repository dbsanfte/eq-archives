OLD SEBILIS DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to June/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

When something is noted as being a rare drop, it's quite rare. We're talking drops of 1 in 20 kinda thing, not like a rare drop in zones like Solusek B or Lower Guk, this is MUCH worse. In the case of Trakanon, you're usually guaranteed 1-2 pieces of class specific breastplates dropping, but which ones is completely random.

Old Sebilis and Karnor's Castle are host to the Kunark sets of class specific armor. In each case, class specific armor is noted in the tables below using a three letter prefix (eg. "Tolan's Darkwood Gauntlets (RNG)").

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Raid / Quest MOBs" table) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.

Some items have been known to drop in this zone but don't appear in the tables below. No databases I've seen out there seem to say what drops these items, but they are from Old Sebilis. Most of them are items that drop randomly off any common MOB in the zone such as the various frogloks. These items include Peacebringer, Siblisian Berserker Cloak, Stormwood Battlestaff, Bone Mask of the Jarsath, Iksar Hide Boots, Blood Ember Vambraces (SHD), Blood Ember Bracers (SHD), Granite Face Grinder, Jarsath Battle Sword, Worm Cloth Mantle, and the Slime Coated Harpoon.
