BURNING WOODS DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to Feb/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

When something is noted as being a rare drop, it's quite rare. We're talking drops of 1 in 20 kinda thing (usually worse, like 1 in 70), not like a rare drop in zones like Solusek B or Lower Guk, this is MUCH worse.

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Raid / Quest MOBs" table) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.

Any MOB in the zone that is level 45 or higher has a chance of dropping a level 51-54 spell.
