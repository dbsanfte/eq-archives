WESTERN WASTES DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to June/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

I really don't have a good idea of whether certain things are rare, uncommon, common, etc. off MOBs. I also don't know what specific faction adjustments are attached to each MOB. I do know that many of these MOBs are on Claws of Veeshan and Yelinak faction. Killing much of this stuff will increase faction with Kromzek.

This zone is best known for being full of named dragons. Specifically, named dragons that aren't so hard to kill that you need six groups of people to take them down, which is cool. They drop a variation of dragon armor pieces including: Dragonhide Breastplate, Dragonleg Breeches, Dragonspine Vambraces, Dragonhorn Boots, Dragonskull Helm, Dragonhide Gloves, Dragonhorn Bracer, Dragonskin Mask, Dragonwing Cloak, Dragonhide Belt, and Dragonwing Mantle. These armors are further subdivided into grades of excellence, with "matchless" being the best and "worked" being the worst. The types of grading [going from worst to best] include: Worked, Reinforced, Heavy, Peerless, and Matchless.

The First Half of Vin'Pekir's Tome has also been known to drop in this zone, off various random named dragons, but not one in particular. This is an item connected with the Ancient Tomes quest in the Plane of Growth. The Second Half of Vin'Pekir's Tome also drops in this zone, but possibly only off named dragons in the Sapara brood, such as Zil Sapara, Rak Sapara, Mav Sapara, and Jen Sapara.

Various dragons will also drop lesser dragon heads and greater dragon heads. The greater dragon heads can be turned in for the Frostbringer in Kael as part of a quest. The lesser dragon heads can be turned in for the Cloak of the Maelstrom in Kael as part of the same quest.

Please keep in mind that a lot of the levels listed here are estimates. They are based on numbers reported on EQ'Lizer and Illia's Everquest Beastiary. If anybody has better listings or updated information, feel free to e-mail me with your suggestions at the following address: tashimon@hotmail.com. As a result, many of the levels listed below for dragons may very well be wrong or not static values. Dragons listed as level 57 might actually be level 54-59, for example.

Recently we've added in information on dragon resistances, attacks, whether they roamers or statics [meaning they don't roam but spawn and stay in one place], whether they gate, where they gate TO, and whether they see invis [most don't]. Knowing this information goes a long way to helping defeat them in advance. Also included [see below after tables] is a map that's been floating around of the static spawns. It doesn't include all static spawns and it does claim some dragons as statics when others say they're roamers, but it's the best thing we've got right now.

Named MOBs [unless appearing in the "Named Dragons" table] are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.
