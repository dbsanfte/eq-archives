A few things to note before looking at these tables...

Named MOBs have been separated into their own table, listing them by wing, from hardest (East) to easiest (North). Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time. Also, named MOBs have placeholders, namely Crypt Guardians and Skeletal Watchmen.

Also listed is a Key Components table. I'm not sure what the right "cocktail" of components goes into the glove container to make the master key people talk about, but the stuff that spawns on the floor pops up about every 20-30 minutes or so. I think there may be one or two items that also are found in the zone on the floor that don't open doors but may be key components. I haven't seen any, just heard that there were some, but I could be wrong.
