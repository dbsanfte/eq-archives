KEDGE KEEP DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to June/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

Kedge Keep is Everquest's first underwater zone. It was plagued with problems when the game first came out. First there were no MOBs. Then they populated it, but the MOBs weren't itemized. Still, people came to exploit bad pathing. The pathing problems were cleared up (sort of) and the place was itemized finally. Years later, some new MOBs were added to coincide with epic quests, but the zone is still pretty much the same. In order to fight here, you need to be able to breath underwater, which means having Enduring Breath cast on you, using Waterstones from Mages, or items that allow you to do the same thing, such as Fishbone Earrings, Rebreathers, etc.

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Raid / Quest MOBs" table) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.

It should be noted that the Swirlspine Belt that drops off the Swirlspine Guardian seahorses is EXTREMELY rare. We're talking about in 1 in 200 drop rate here - it's very bad. When Kedge Keep first got itemized, they dropped like crazy, so Verant nerfed the drop rate into the stone age. The rest of the zone has drop rates on rare items more in line with other first generation EQ dungeons like Sol B, Lower Guk, etc.

Smaller fish that dart around the zone were not included in these tables. These include: Cerulean Sailfin, Cobalt Sailfin, Crimson Sailfin, Shimmering Sailfin, and Emerald Sailfin. They are level 1-5 and are basically put in the zone to dart around between rooms looking pretty, not to mention making targetting a pain in the ass and speading aggro to other rooms - how nice. When killed, they do drop the occasional fish scale though, if you're desperate for reagents for your Enduring Breath casters. Also ignored were the level 11 Aqua Goblin Prisoners that drop nothing of any note.

The Ferocious Hammerhead is sometimes also known as the Ferocious Cauldron Shark. Not sure which it is now, but the name was changed at some point and it's one or the other now. I'm not sure, 'cause catching it is tough now since they nerfed the spawn rate on this MOB fairly heavily.

Also of note is an item for the Paladin epic quest that isn't dropped by a MOB, but is lying on the floor of Phinigel Autropos' lair. At the bottom of his lair is a star shaped platform. The epic piece is on that platform. Getting the piece means taking out Phinigel pretty much.
