THE PLANE OF FEAR DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to June/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

In March of 2001, the Plane of Fear was re-itemized. As a result, the drops off Cazic Thule and Dracoliche were moved around somewhat. Cazic Thule and Dracoliche now drop all new items. What they previously dropped has been moved to the "demi-gods" of this plane [Fright, Dread, and Terror]. The "demi-gods" have also seen some new changes in that they're all level 55 and about as hard to fight as the old Cazic and Draco used to be. Some pieces have yet to be found, such as several items off the old Cazic Thule [Bladestorm: Katana of Steel Sleet, Bone Clasped Girdle, DawnFire: Morningstar of Light, Puppet Strings, and Shield of Rainbow Hues] and Dracoliche [Bone Razor, Staff of Undead Legions, Withered Totem of Withershims]. The popular theory is that all three "demi-gods" can drop any piece of old Cazic / Draco loot and that we just haven't seen it all drop yet.

The Phoboplasms [aka. Jello] are also somewhat of a mystery. In the past they've been known to drop a few things, such as rogue bracers, ranger greaves, etc. but with the recent changes, we're not entirely sure of what they drop. The common theory is that they drop one of each type of class armor found in the fear and hate planes, but only one type for each piece [eg. ranger greaves, but greaves only].

Various MOBs in this plane are well known for dropping a series of valuable gems, including Diamonds, Blue Diamonds, Black Sapphires, and Jacinths.

A lot of information is still coming in concerning changes to Fear. As a result, some of the information you see below may be wrong. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.
