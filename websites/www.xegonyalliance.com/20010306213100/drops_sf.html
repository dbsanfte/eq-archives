SKYFIRE MOUNTAINS DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to Feb/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Raid / Quest MOBs" table) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.

Any MOB in the zone that is level 45 or higher has a chance of dropping a level 51-54 spell. Level 60 Guardian Wurms have are known for dropping level 55+ spells.
