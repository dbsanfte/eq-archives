KARNOR'S CASTLE DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to Feb/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

When something is noted as being a rare drop, it's quite rare. We're talking drops of 1 in 20 kinda thing, not like a rare drop in zones like Solusek B or Lower Guk, this is MUCH worse. In the case of Venril Sathir, he's usually guaranteed to drop 1-2 pieces of class specific greaves, but which ones is completely random.

Karnor's Castle and Old Sebilis are host to the Kunark sets of class specific armor. In each case, class specific armor is noted in the tables below using a three letter prefix (eg. "Tolan's Darkwood Gauntlets (RNG)").

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Raid / Quest MOBs" and "Named Sathir MOBs" tables) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time. The Drolvarg Warlord is an exception and is NOT noted as named because you can have several of him up in the room he spawns in.

Some items have been known to drop in this zone but don't appear in the tables below. No databases I've seen out there seem to say what drops these items, but they are from Karnor's Castle. These items include Blood Fork, Brokenskull Club, and A Club Claad Blackjack. Flayed Skin armor also is well known for dropping randomly off MOBs in this zone.
