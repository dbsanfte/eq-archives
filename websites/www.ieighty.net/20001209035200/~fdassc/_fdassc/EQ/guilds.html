The Guilds of EverQuest

This is a list of  all of the known player (PC) guilds in EverQuest.  The PC Guilds are sorted by the city in which they are based.  Note that some guilds can be found in more than one city.  All listings are in alphabetical order.  Below the list of PC guilds is a list of NPC guilds, which is far from complete.  Please send any NPC Guild names or corrections to anything on this page to rossf@ieighty.net.
- Auric, Tarew Marr
