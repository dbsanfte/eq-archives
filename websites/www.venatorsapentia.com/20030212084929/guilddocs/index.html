VS Constitution

Venator Sapentia Everquest Guild Constitution

RATIFIED 2/9/2001

Purpose

 Venator Sapentia (VS) was founded by a small group of friends with a common interest in Everquest. Our mission is to have fun and kick ass. Venator Sapentia, roughly translated, means Seeker of Wisdom.

Officers

Restrictions for Officers

No member may hold more than one office.

Officers must remain active in the game. If no member can recall seeing the President in game within the past 90 days, (determined by polling on the message board) it is considered an automatic resignation and the Vice President succeeds him. Other officers who become inactive are replaced by the President as needed.

President and Vice President

The President and Vice President are elected once a year by majority vote. (See the section on Election of Officers.) The Vice President will take the role of the President while he is temporarily absent and succeed him if he is permanently removed from his post or becomes inactive. If the office of Vice President becomes vacant, the President will appoint a new Vice President immediately.

The President will appoint all other officers at the start of his term, and appoint a replacement should any post become vacant. All appointed officers serve at the will of the President, and the President can demand the resignation of appointed officers at any time.

Headmaster

The President appoints the Headmaster to run the guild's recruiting program and to prepare candidates for their induction. The Headmaster judges the outcome of all membership elections. This is probably at once the most onerous and rewarding officer post in the VS guild. The future of the organization lies with the Headmaster. He must be willing to devote a significant amount of his time to his duties.

Herald

The herald is appointed by the President and is responsible for maintaining the Venator Sapentia discussion boards and for running elections. He also provides technical assistance to other officers and members as needed.

Webmaster

The Webmaster maintains the Venator Sapentia web site. He also provides technical assistance to other officers and members as needed. The webmaster will provide an area of the web site to record the current membership roster.

Election of Officers

Officers will be elected once a year, starting on the first Saturday in January.

On the first Saturday in January, the Herald will start a thread on the private message board for the nomination of President.

Members then have one week to post nominations for President to the election thread, and may not withdraw a nomination once posted. Members cannot nominate themselves. Nominees who accept their nomination before the week has elapsed become candidates for the office. Candidates must name their vice president when they accept their nomination.

On the second Saturday in January, the Herald will hold an election for declared candidates for one week. The Herald will use a poll-type message board thread for officer elections. On the third Saturday in January the candidate with the most votes will take office. In the event of a tie, the Herald may extend or rerun the election at his option.

Membership

Candidates for membership must be nominated by a current member of Venator Sapentia by creating a nomination thread on the Venator Sapentia membership board naming the candidate. This thread must be a normal message thread and not an anonymous poll thread.

To vote, members must post Aye or Nay to the voting thread, along with their experience with the nominee. Members must have some experience with the nominee before voting.

If a member wants to vote anonymously, they can discuss their vote in private with the Headmaster. If the Headmaster decides the vote is valid, he will post a vote to the voting thread on behalf of the member without revealing the member's identity or reasons.

To be accepted, the candidate must receive at least 10 Aye votes and no Nay votes within 28 days. After 28 days if there are fewer than 10 votes the election ends and the candidate is not accepted.

The candidate can be accepted after only 14 days if the candidate receives at least 20 Aye votes and no Nay votes. As soon as these conditions are met, the election is closed and posting a Nay vote has no effect. This provision allows candidates who are already well known to be guilded quickly. However, all membership elections must last at least 14 days.

Members can change their vote up until the election end. Thus, ongoing debate may affect the results of the election. Note that if a candidate is accepted before the full 28 days have elapsed because there are at least 20 Aye votes, the election is closed and changing a vote has no effect.

The nominator can withdraw their nomination before the election ends. This ends the election and the nominee is not guided.

If a membership election fails for any reason, the candidate may be nominated again after 90 days from the end of the election.

The Headmaster will determine the outcome of all membership elections, and will decide if votes are valid. Nay votes based on a lack of time with the nominee will not be counted. The Headmaster will only count votes that are unequivocally Aye or Nay. Abstain or "maybe" votes will not be counted.

The Headmaster will exclude votes if the voter does not describe a relevant experience with the candidate that is the basis of their decision. In other words, members must explain their votes. Voters who know the candidate well need only describe this in general rather than provide an account of recent groups, etc. The Headmaster will use the following guidelines for determining what is relevant experience:

  • Friendship with the nominee.
  • Grouping with the nominee within the past year.
  • Participating in a raid or other large event with the nominee within the past year.
  • Any significant negative experience with the candidate, regardless of how long ago it occurred.
No person can become a member of VS without a successful membership election. Any member who transfers a guilded account to another person must remove all guild tags from characters on the account.

However, a member who is actively playing may extend temporary membership to a spouse or loved one who shares their account(s). If the member stops playing or transfers their account(s), the temporary membership is automatically revoked and the guild tag must be removed. However, there is nothing to prevent temporary members from seeking full membership.

Discipline and Decision Making

A member of VS can be removed from the guild by a 2/3 majority vote of all active members. Any member can initiate such an election, but it is a measure of last resort and all members are encouraged to seek reconciliation first. Members can also voluntarily resign from VS by declaring their resignation on the private board. Any member losing VS membership will be removed from the web site and private boards and will no longer participate in elections.

For all other matters not otherwise covered by this constitution, the guild president has broad powers to act as he sees fit.

However, VS is a democracy and any majority vote of all active members is considered a binding decision and becomes law. (Except for decisions to remove a member, which require a 2/3 majority.)

For example: any decision of the President can be overturned by a majority vote of all active members. The President can also be removed from office by a majority vote of all active members.

When determining the total count of active members for the above elections, the Herald will use the list of members posted on VenatorSapentia.com but exclude those members who have not been seen in game or on the boards within the past 90 days, unless those members vote and notify the Herald that they voted.

Adoption of this Constitution

This constitution has taken effect as ratified by a majority of VS members listed on VenatorSapentia.com as of 2/6/2001.

Changes to this Constitution

The constitution may be changed by a majority vote of active VS membership as described in the above section entitled "Discipline and Decision Making."
