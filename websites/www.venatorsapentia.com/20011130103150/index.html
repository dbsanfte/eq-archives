  10/19/2001  Spreading the Rumors!

A few new spells/skills have shown up on test. Most of them are in the spell database just like the Paladin Lay Hands. These could possibly be skills that could be gained by the new advancement mechanisim in SoL.
I would just like to remind everyone that these are things on TEST and does not necessarily mean that they will or are meant to go live. I just thought it interesting enough to share.

Cleric

  • Divine Resurrection: 100% experience resurrection without mana cost.
  • Invisibility versus Undead: Fixed duration self only Invis Vs. Undead.
  • Celestial Regeneration: 4000 HP over 48 second heal.
  • Sacred Barrier: 36 Second short range single target divine intervention.
  • Cleansing Light: Single target complete poison and disease cure.

Enchanter

  • Dominating Gaze: Fixed duration charm.
  • Gather Mana: Self only complete mana refill.
  • Mana Regeneration: Self only mana regeneration +2 per tick
  • Focused Mind: Possibly targettable mana bonus

Ranger

  • Dead Aim: Self only attack bonus

Necromancer

  • Lifeburn: Unresistable huge life tap.
  • Words of the Undead King:  Undead only mesmerise with memory blur (42 Seconds)
  • Wave of Revulsion: 42 Second lure AE fear
  • Restore Companion: 1981 hit point pet heal.
  • Undead Pact:  Fixed duration undead charm.

Druid

  • Servant of Nature: Fixed duration animal charm.
  • Strong Root: Lure based root.

Wizard

  • Manaburn: Unresistable huge direct damage

Magician

  • Frenzied Burnout:  Short duration pet buff spell: +100 ATK, +17 AC, +100 Strength, 195% Haste.

Beastlord

  • Restore Companion: 1981 hit point pet heal.

Shaman

  • Masochism: Huge instant cannibalize.  Convert 1924 hit points into 1066 mana.
  • Rabid Bear: Self only bear form with +200 ATK
Unassociated Classes
  • Resistance Tonic: Self only +10 against all saves
  • Growth: Self only increase size
  • Disease Touch: Disease based direct damage (Possibly SK Harmtouch upgrade)
  • Poison Touch: Poison based direct damage (Possibly SK Harmtouch upgrade)
 This was found over on CastersRealm.
