  9/14/2002  What is this, Cyber, you speak of?


Klaget's been hard at work trying to figure out why I, Damman, surveyor of all I can see, couldn't upload for these wonderful updates. Finally my quest is completed, and I thought I'd share with you my journey to this update.

The day began as any other ordinary day would, I log in, check a few spawns, swim through the lag infested molasses that is now Sanctus Seru and I thought to myself, I've heard this before, but what is this thing called Cyber? I didn't know much about it earlier, and I had come to a conclusion. I would find this mysterious Cyber no matter the cost.

My first step in this mystery was to discover what this so called Cyber really was. I turned to my trusted bard. Knowing them to be jacks of all trades,


perhaps he knew, but he was very distracted with... something... under his desk. I didn't know what at the time, but I had my suspicions.

Still completely baffled, I sought the council of the oldest living creature in existance, Norl.
Much like every worthless old person, he rambled on for good 3 or 4 hours before I was able to escape, continuing on my journey.

The next day, I found myself no closer to knowing anything about this Cyber. I asked if anyone would explain who or what Cyber was to me.


Perhaps just a hint?


Thus, the revelation. It was indeed something sexual. In the interest of science, I simply had to know what this mystery would reveal. I turned to my friends to see what they knew of it, trying to grasp any straws I could.



Sadly, I accidentally said sex in guild chat, and this went on for several days. However, hope was not completely gone. A shadowy figure stepped forth from his bazaar merchant stall, and whispered some advise to catch this tiger by it's tail. Playing it cool, and lots of cool music.



I had the music down, sure, no problem. Still, I knew there was one critical step missing. To find Cyber, I needed something to lure it in.

Well, maybe I didn't afterall. Back to the music! I decided to find the best sexually explicit music I could to lure a Cyber into my trap.

Sadly, that got my no where, as I learned this song only motivated the *real world* sexual fantasies.



Now I knew I was close. This alleged Cyber was for online only. There had to be a way I could capture it.

The next day I knew. I journeyed far and wide to find the choicest seat, a throne of Cyber, if you will. Others knowing what I was doing stared on in revered awe.

Perched from atop my seat I began playing the coolest music known to man, ever.

Oh, they flocked towards me left and right. At first, it was just a small amount, a piece here, a piece there.

Being more proactive I hummed louder, until finally, amidst the chaos of internet~


Success was mine, and I come forth knowing maybe too much of this online web of intrigue we call, Cyber.




Tomorrow, with a little rest, and maybe a bucket of ice, I'll share with you our phat lewtz.
