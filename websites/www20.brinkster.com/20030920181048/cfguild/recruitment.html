Special cases may apply, contact guild management for more information.

All candidates must be subjected to a first screening session by the people in charge of recruiting, afterwards if judged potential candidates they are placed on the guild board, name, class, level and some considerations written.

Every Celestial Fury member has the choice to express his feelings on the candidate, after a certain period of time the candidate is either inserted in the guild or told that his membership has been refused. Any negative vote on a candidate by a member most likely will void the membership of that applicant.

Every new member must pass through an initiation period in which he will participate with the guild, during this period initiates will be also subject to be removed from guild if it is noticed that they do not fit the Celestial Fury mold, Officers are in charge of taking notes about the initiates.

Every member that returns to Celestial Fury, after an out of guild period ( Removed themselves ) must also pass through the initiation process again. No one will ever be re-invited in Celestial Fury after a 2nd /guildremove.
