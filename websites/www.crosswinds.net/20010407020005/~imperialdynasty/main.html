Imperial Dynasty News

Well, I for one am getting excited! We have tons of high-level firepower at our disposal, and I think it is high time we opened some cans on some of these top mobs around Norrath. Tell Patryn you are locked and cocked and ready to rock!

If you have suggestions as to what designates "minimum equipment", tell Patryn your ideas.
