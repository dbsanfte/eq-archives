Light Conclave is a guild founded under the guiding influence of Mithaniel Marr, The Lightbearer. We are not a religious guild devoted to Marr, specifically. Rather, we welcome members of all good races, classes, and religions, though in a rare circumstance we will allow those of the darker races to join if they have forsaken their evil ways and proven themselves worthy. We prize the virtues of truth, honor, and charity, and are champions of the downtrodden and are the most noble of warriors and mages. We will not rest until the day that all Norath is cleansed of the darkness.

We have no requirements on members other than that they be able to participate in the guild peacefully and help us pursue our goals. We should enjoy the friendship of one another and thus be selective in choosing fellow members. We must believe in "All for one and one for all" in all our prusits, helping fellow guildmembers and fellow worthy Noratian Citizens, when the time is needed. At all times we must be ever watchful of the evil ones, alerting others of dangers and if able, destroy these dangers, furthing our ultimate goal.

We will help when we can, when feel that we can do so constructively. We will go to war, or partisipate in vigilante actions, when the time is needed, sacrificing ourselves for the greater good of all Norath

Finally, (out of character) The Light Conclave expects that its members will conduct themselves as "good citizens" in the context of an Online RPG. We will avoid of foul language (since minors may be playing), shun the use of "cheats," be prudent of our use of "broadcasts" (/ooc & /shout), and generally try to enhance everyone's enjoyment of the game, rather than seeking fun in disturbing others.
