Purification of the Unrest Estate

Rotela, our guild friend Frakis and myself were traveling through the Karana plains a fortnight ago hunting those marauding Giants, when something horrible happened.
The sun was setting and we where preparing to make camp when Frakis muttered, "We are being watched, be sharp!". Frakis blended into the foliage, not to be seen, when from behind us a soul piercing scream filled the air.
We where ambushed by a most foul creature, it appeared to be a former humanoid, who's soul and mind where warped by many a foul incarnation. The once proud human had been transformed into a shriveled prutrid, disgusting being.
I quickly muttered a powerful rending incarnation while Rotela called upon the forces the land to poison the creature, my spell igniting the skin of this ghoul. Frakis then quickly fell the creature with several well placed arrows.

Upon inspecting the body we found a tattered up note reading:

"We must set our army free from the estate, we must vanquish the living and rule as our lord Innoruk has foretold. I amy gathering an army here on Antonica, I beseech you to join me with your powerful force gathering in Unrest."
"I have sent my most horrible creation to deliver this message, he is your now to command, we will be in further contact."
- Nocutnus

The three of us quickly retreated to safety and started to plan...

Guild members! We must gather a force and Purify the Estate to protect our brethren from the impending doom!
