[News] August 14th


Mutual Efforts

A couple of months ago a large amount of tradeskill recipies were added in one of the patches. Along with the recipies was an interesting quest involving tradeskills, which results in a powerful Druid Spell and an earring for Druids, which can be traded in for one for other classes (useful mainly for casters as it has Flowing Thought I)

Belrain, Uzeldin and myself thing the guild has sufficient tradeskill ability to be able to do this quest, and hopefully do it multiple times. I also think it is something we can get everyone involved with as it requires a lot of component farming, forraging and collection which everyone can help with. There is a good guide to the quest on EQ Traders which goes into great detail. I am also going to start a thread for the quest on the forums, so everyone can say whether they want to be involved and which parts they think they can help with, the eventual goal of course being to complete the quest once for everyone involved (probably twice for the druids).

A goal we can achieve relatively easily I think, so I hope you'll join in the fun.

[News] June 18th

The Forums are back!

But, before you get too excited, they are currently locked so all you can do is create your account (see below) and send private messages to others that have already done so. The reason for this is that I want to fiddle a bit and make the look of the forums a bit more in-keeping with the main site before they go live and people get used to them. I have set myself a deadline of Sunday to have them fully functional - lets see how well I stick to it!

Account creation is pretty simple. Use your Sullon Zek character name so we can identify everyone, anyone who doesn't do this will find their accounts deleted, and provide a valid email address please. The reson for the latter is that there is a nifty little feature that allows me to mail the entire members list should the need arrise, which may prove useful on occasion. Other than that please be patient, everything should be back to fully working order soon.

On another note, the members list now needs a lot of work with the influx of people joining over the last week, so I'll need some help getting details and pictures. Details by e-mail or on the forums please as I always loose the little bits of paper I try to make notes on during play! If you want to take a picture and send it to me... great! The trick is to use the '/shownames off' command, switch to fullscreen mode (press f10 till all the windows disapear) then press the minus key on the numeric keypad to take the picture. I find it is actually more difficult that taking a real photograph cos getting the positioning right is a bit tricky. Pictures are saved as .bmp files in your EQ directory, so convert them to .jpg (you may need to zip them as well) and then send them along through email. Easy huh?

[News] June 12th

After a wee bit of time (and a bind death loop boredom experience) we have finally got Umonstore up and running. Umonstore is an alternate character of mine whos sole purpose in his EQ life is to store ALL of your old items and save up donations from guild members. The idea behind this, is to store any items which you no longer use, so that we can hand them out to some of our newer guild members. The donations side of things is to handle the considerable cost of getting stones for future raids to the planes.

If this all goes to plan we should have a system where by our guild members hand down any item(s) which their character has out-grown, or replaced, to someone else who may need an improved or totally new item. Not only that but Umonstore can store all of our resist based items, which you yourselves may not have room for, and then when it comes the time for a raid to the planes we can hand out whichever item is appropriate (FR, PR, MR, CR, DR etc)

Umonstore is currently located at the Bazaar on the moon, so grab all your old items, and sift through your old things and get them up to Umonstore.
Cheers
