The Laws Of "The Heroic Elite".


Respect the guild. Your guild members are your support, your strength, and your family. They come first in all things, whenever possible.

You should treat all guild members, as you would wish them to treat you. You must act in a responsible and an honorable fashion at all times. Treat your guild members with respect in actions as well as deeds. Speak to them with respect and expect the same. Be honest and fair in all your dealings with them.

Do not do anything that would embarrass or dishonor the guild in others eyes, and refrain from annoying behavior, act as a responsible adult at all times. While you wear the guild title, you represent the entire guild. What someone thinks of you is reflected upon all of us, so respect other players, but if you need help in a situation use guild speak, that's why we are here.

All members above the rank of 9 can recruit. Your are expected to have hunted a time or two with the people you bring to the guild for membership, but remember you are putting your credibility on the line when you bring them. All prospective members will be expected to spend time with us prior to acceptance. Recruiting is a privilege, not something owed to you, and can be something taken away from you. We would rather have a small guild that was a close knitted group then a large guild full of problems, such as greedy people only interested in personal gain instead of camaraderie.

We are here to provide you with groups to hunt with which will give you insight on how to play your character, and gain experience, without power leveling you, even thought at times it may appear you are being power leveled due to the experience you are receiving.
Any variation from the above rules will not be tolerated. Disrespectful attitude or actions against other guild members may and will result in removal from this guild, so please guide your game play accordingly.

Above all remember we are all here to have fun. Any problems will be swiftly dealt with and can result in immediate dismissal from our guild.
