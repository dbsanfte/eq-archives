Cleric Spells
Level 1
Spell Name Mana

Reagents

 Description
Courage 10 Courage the first in a line of buffing spells for the cleric class. This line of spells adds both hit points and armor class and can be cast on yourself and on others.
Cure Poison 20 This spell will counteract poisons.
Divine Aura 10 This is perhaps the most important spell you will receive until level 29(when you receive Divine Barrier which is surprise an upgrade to this spell). Divine Aura will grant you a temporary invulnerability to damage. This is a great spell to use to escape from those hairy situations and avoid what would otherwise become certain death. Use it sparingly however as the duration is short and you can only cast this spell once every 15 minutes.
Flash Of Light 12 This spell will temporarily blind your opponent.
Lull 10 This is the first in the line of spells which are aimed to calm your foe. This spell can be used to lower your opponents rage and(hopefully) prevent him from attacking you.
Minor Healing 10 Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.
Sense Magic 5 This spell will give you information on magic items.
Spook The Dead 10 This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with lull as you can lull one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one.
Strike 12 Strike is a direct damage spell. 
Yalup 5 This spell endows the caster a brief surge of strength which not only adds to the casters strength but also to his armor class. The duration is very short but the spell also takes very little mana.

Level 5
Cure Blindness 20 This spell is used to cure blindness.
Cure Disease 20 This spell can be used to cure disease.
Furor 20 This is a direct damage spell. It is a high damage high mana spell and is the best offensive spell a cleric will receive until Smite at level 14.
Gate 70 The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.
Holy Armor 20 Holy armor is the first in the line of armor spells. This spell adds additional armor class to a player.
Light Healing 25 Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.
Reckless Strength 30 Reckless strength gives a burst of strength which slowly dies out.
Stun 60 This spell stuns your opponent for a short period of time.
Summon Drink 10 This spell allows you to summon some water. Summoned water is no rent meaning that if you log out of the game the water will dissapear.
Ward Undead 30 Ward undead is the first in the line of direct damage undead ward spells. These spells only affect undead. Ward spells are important because at the level which you attain these spells they are the most efficient direct damage spells in the game at this level.  Does 35 +1 per Lvl of damage to Undead.

Level 9
Center 40 Center is an upgrade to the courage line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.
Endure Fire 20 This spell will increase your resistance to fire.
Endure Poison 20 This spell will increase your resistance to poison.
Fear 40 Fear will cause monsters to turn and flee for a period of time. Beware however they will return when the spell duration ends.
Hammer Of Wrath 50 This spell will summon a magical warhammer.The statistics are dam 6, delay 32 and it can be used to damage magical monsters. It is a no rent item meaning that you cannot hand it out to anyone else and it will dissapear after you log out.
Invigor 20 Invigor will restore a players stamina.
Root 30 Root will prevent a monster from running away for a period of time.
Sense Undead 5 Sense undead will point you in the direction of the nearest undead foe.
Soothe 30 Soothe is an upgrade to lull and the predecessor to calm. It will make a monster less agressive.
Summon Food 10 This will summon no rent food.
Word of Pain 57 Zero ground AoE  Spell.
Level 14
Bind Affinity 100 This allows you to change the point where you will spawn in event of death. You can bind yourself anyplace. You can also bind other people in your party but they must be bound within city limits.
Cancel Magic 30 This is a very useful spell which can be used to cancel a magic effect cast on you or one of your party members. This is great if someone in your party is charmed or rooted in a deadly situation.
Endure Cold 20 Endure cold increases your resistance to cold based effects.
Endure Disease 20 Endure disease increases your resistance to disease.
Expulse Undead 50 Expulse undead is an upgrade to ward undead. It has higher damage and a more efficient damage:mana ratio.
Halo Of Light 40 This spell summons a glowing headband which lights as well as a greater lightstone.
Healing 60 Healing is an upgrade to the heal line of spells. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.
Invisibility To Undead 40 This spell renders you invisible to undead beings.
Sense Summoned 5 This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.
Smite 75 Smite is a direct damage spell. It is your best direct damage spell versus non-undead until the level 29 wrath.
Symbol Of Transal 55 This is the third line of cleric/paladin buffing spells. It does require a component and adds additional hit points only.
Level 19
Calm 50 Calm is an upgrade to the lull->soothe line of spells. It will lower a monsters aggro range allowing you to do things such as calm a 3 monsters in a 4 monster location and then pick off the non-calmed one.
Daring 60 This is an upgrade to the courage->center line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.
Endure Magic 40 Endure magic will increase your resistance to magic based attacks
Extinguish Fatigue 35 This will restore a players stamina and also has a duration where it will prevent them from tiring.
Holy Might 90 Holy might is a stun spell which also does a small amount of damage.
Spirit Armor 75 Spirit armor is an upgrade to the holy armor line of spells.
Ward Summoned This spell allows you to damage summoned beasts(elements). Its damage:mana ratio is identical to the ward undead spell.
Word Of Shadow 95 Word of shadow is an upgrade to the word of pain line of spells. It is an area effect damage spell which centers around the caster.
Yalup II 15 This is an upgrade to the yalup spell. It grants additional strength and armor class for 18 seconds.

Level 24
Bravery Bravery is an upgrade to the courage->center->daring line of spells. It adds additional armor class and hit points.
Dismiss Undead Dismiss undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It is a high damage low mana spell for use against undead foes.
Greater Heal Greater heal is an upgrade to the heal line of spells. It heals roughly 300 mana for 150 hp.
Radius Of Fear This is an area effect fear spell which can be useful in sticky situations.

Level 29
Abundant Drink This spell will summon 5 different drinks. It is useful only in that its a one shot deal rather than having to cast summon drink 5 times.
Divine Barrier Divine barrier is an upgrade to the divine aura line of spells. It not only renders you invincible for a period of time but it also regenerates your hit points and stamina during that period. The weakness is that it uses much more mana than the original.
Enstill Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. This spell has a better chance of success versus higher level foes and a longer duration.
Expulse Summoned Expulse Summoned is an upgrade to the ward summoned spell.
Guard This spell grants additional armor class. It is a seperate line of spells so is a welcome addition.
Panic The Dead Panic the dead is an upgrade to spook the dead. It is a more powerful fear spell which has a longer duration.
Revive Revive is the first in the line of res spells. This spell will teleport a dead player to their corpse.
Word Of Spirit This is an upgrade to the word of pain->shadow line of spells. It is an area effect spell which centers on the caster.
Wrath Wrath is the upgrade to the smite line of spells. It is a high damage high mana spell that can be used against all monsters and not just undead.
Level 34
Abundant Food This spell will summon 5 pieces of food. It is an upgrade to the summon food spell.
Atone This is an upgrade to the calm line of spells. It has a longer duration.
Blinding Luminance I believe this is a blinding spell.   Not Sure
Expel Undead Expel undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss line of undead spells.
Force This is an upgrade to the stun->holy might line of spells. It stuns for a good period of time and also does a small amount of damage.
Frenzied Strength This spell is an upgrade to the reckless strength spell.
Resist Fire This spell adds to your resistance to fire.
Resist Poison Resist poison adds to your poison resistance.
Superior Heal Superior heal is an intermediate spell between greater heal and complete heal. It is a cleric only spell which heals 600 points of damage in a very efficient manner.
Symbol Of Pinzam This is an upgrade to the symbol of transal line of spells. It requires a reagent and adds about 250 hit points.
Tremor Tremor is a wide range area effect spell. It does not do a lot of damage for the amount of mana it uses but it can affect a larger number of monsters than the word of pain line of spells.
Valor This is an upgrade to the courage->center->daring->bravery line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.
Word Of Health This spell is an area effect heal spell. It is useful in situations where several party members have been damaged at once.
Level 39
Armor Of Faith This is an upgrade to the holy armor->spirit armor line of spells. It grants additional armor class.
Complete Heal This spell will completely heal a player. It does require a large amount of mana and is only useful on players with a lot of hit points.
Dismiss Summoned Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the ward summoned line of spells. It has the same efficiency as dismiss undead but it useful on summoned beasts.
Invoke Fear Invoke fear is an upgrade to the fear line of spells. It has a longer duration.
Nullify Magic This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.
Pacify Pacify is an upgrade to the lull->soothe->calm line of spells.
Rescucitate This is the second in the line of ressing spells. It will not only teleport a player to his/her corpse but also restores a bit of the experience lost upon death.
Resist Cold This spell adds to your resistance to cold.
Resist Disease This spell adds to your resistance to disease.
Word of Souls Word of Souls is an upgrade to the word of pain->shadow->spirit line of spells. It is a person based area effect spell.

Level 44
Banish Undead This is the last in the line of ward undead spells. It is a high damage low mana spell which will bring undead to their knees.
Earthquake Earthquake is an upgrade to the tremor spell. It is a wide range area effect spell.
Hammer of Retribution Hammer of Retribution is an upgrade to the hammer of wrath spells. It summons a magical warhammer for use in combat.
Resist Magic This spell adds to your resistance to magic based attacks.
Resolution Im not really sure what this spell does. I only know that it is a defensive spell.
Symbol Of Naltron Symbol of Naltron is an upgrade to the symbol line of spells. It adds additional hit points.
Yalup III Yalup III is the last in the line of yalup spells which add both armor class and hit points.

Level 49
Expel Summoned Expel Summoned is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss line of spells for use against summoned beasts.
Immobilize Immobilize is an upgrade to the popular cleric stun series of spells.
Ressurect This is the mother of all res spells. It teleports a player to their corpse and restores quite a bit of the experience lost upon death.
Shield Of Words I have no information on this defensive spell.
Sound Of Force I have no information on this spell other than it is offensive in nature.
Word Of Divine Word of divine is an area effect divine aura spell. This is incredibly useful for getting out of those sticky situations and will hold the monsters off long enough for the wizard or druid to get off a group teleport spell.
