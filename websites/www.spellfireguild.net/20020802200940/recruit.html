Recruitment
The following is a list of our class recruitment levels:

  • Paladin: 58
  • Shadowknight: 56
  • Ranger: 57
  • Bard: 56
  • Monk: 56
  • Beastlord: 56
  • Warrior: 56
  • Rogue: 55
  • Druid: 58
  • Cleric: 56
  • Wizard: 57
  • Necromancer: 57
  • Enchanter: 56
  • Magician: 55

These levels are based on when a particular class becomes most useful to the overall goals of the guild. There may be some acceptions, but we'll follow this recruitment outline overall.

Recruitment Process:

  1. All recruits must be directed to an officer for sponsorship and raid information.
  2. All recruits must be unguilded during recruitment. (Mainly for tagging during recruitment)
  3. All recruits must attend a minimum of three (3) raids/guild events and group with members whenever possible to enter the final phase of recruitment.
  4. During the final phase of recruitment the recruit will remain tagged and be treated as a full member. As such, the recruit must adhere to all guild poicies and function as a part of the team. If after 30 days the recruit shows the qualities the guild requires he/she will be instructed to keep the tag and be allowed the benefits of a full member.
