Recruitment Process

All recruits must be directed to an officer for sponsorship and raid information.  Check with online members for a recruitment officer.

All potential recruits must obtain a sponsor in order to move to the 2nd phase of recruitment.  To obtain a sponsor, request it from someone you know, or group with members whenever possible to make a member feel comfortable enough to want to sponsor you.  (Note:  If you harass members for sponsorship, or lack thereof, your recruitment will be terminated.)

All recruits must be unguilded during recruitment.

Recruits are entitled to full DKP during recruitment, but cannot use DKP points against current guild members.  All DKP rules can be viewed here.  It is strongly recommended that all recruits read and understand the loot policies and guild rules prior to applying for recruitment.

To apply, fill out our Recruitment Application.  Please make sure you've read and understand all of our recruitment and DKP rules before applying.

All recruits must attend a minimum of seven (7) raids*/guild events and group with members whenever possible to enter the final phase of recruitment.  (Note:  A raid is considered a night of raiding, not a single target mob.)

During the final phase of recruitment, the recruit will remain tagged and be treated as a full member (with the exception of not being allowed to spend DKP points acquired against current guild members).  As such, the recruit must adhere to all guild policies and function as part of the team.  If after 30 days the recruit shows the qualities the guild requires, he/she will be instructed to keep the tag and be allowed the benefits of a full member.

Also during this phase, it is required that all recruits of any race other than Dwarf, Gnome or Halfling obtain or possess a right-click shrink item (charged item or potions). All recruits must be able to get all resists over 100 unbuffed.  In additions, all recruits must obtain a minimum of PoStorms/PoValor flags.
