06/29/03 Recap of a Busy Month !

Tormax died to us for the first time ! We finally managed to find him up and solved that as soon as possible. Great Job Folks !

Tunare was slain finally! Another guild first ! After several hours spent clearing PoG and watching Tk get charmed and led around by an overgrown willowisp, the goddess of nature was slain ! Grats on a great job folks !

Cleared NToV a few times (yipeeee!) and played around with Ring of Vulaak a little. Shouldn't be long before we have that conquered as well !

We killed Bastion of Thunder's tower named several times, netting several people some sweet upgrades !

I saw a lot of awesome teamwork from you guys recently. Thanks for all the hard work. It's really paying off for all of us ! Also, Grats to all those that made it past the final phase of recruitment. Welcome to the family !
