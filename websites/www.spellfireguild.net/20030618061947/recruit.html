Recruitment Process:

All recruits must be directed to an officer (Check our member list for a list of officers. Sort by rank.) for sponsorship and raid information.
All recruits must be unguilded during recruitment.
All recruits must make a post under our "Recruitment" section on the message boards.


All recruits must attend a minimum of three (3) raids/guild events and group with members whenever possible to enter the final phase of recruitment.

During the final phase of recruitment the recruit will remain tagged and be treated as a full member. As such, the recruit must adhere to all guild policies and function as a part of the team. If after 30 days the recruit shows the qualities the guild requires he/she will be instructed to keep the tag and be allowed the benefits of a full member. During the final phase of recruitment, it is REQUIRED that the recruit obtain or posess a right click (anti dispell for indoor raids) item, obtain PoV/PoS planes flags(we can normally help with this), and be able to get all resists over 100 without buffs.
