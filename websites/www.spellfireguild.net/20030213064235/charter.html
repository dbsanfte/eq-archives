Spellfire is a guild founded with the concept that as a team anything is possible. We are dedicated to excersizing our abilities to obtain the highest possible level of play. We require every member to act as a part of the team in order to obtain our goals.

 

Leadership

Our leadership is broken down into three (3) levels:
Leaders: The leaders of the guild are responsible for the primary functions of the guild and helping to formulate changes in policy and procedure to help support and obtain our goals.
Officers: The officers of the guild form a panel of people who act as a support team for the leaders and handle the day to day operation of the guild. Officers have as much authority as the leaders and should be treated as such.
Members: The members are the core of the guild. As such, it is the responsibility of every member to uphold the reputation of the guild. It is also the members responsibility to act as a part of the team and do whatever is possible to help us reach and maintain a higher level and standard of play.
