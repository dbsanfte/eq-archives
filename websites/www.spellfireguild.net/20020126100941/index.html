11/19 Icewell Keep is no match for our skillz!
Chamberlain Krystorf
Tonight after an intense guild meeting several of us banded together and headed to Icewell in hopes banishing our restlessness. Icewell proved to be good for several loot items and also proved that we are an awesome team. While we didn't manage to take out Seneschal Aldikar we did manage to take out Chamberlain Krystorf who is known as the 2nd hardest monster in the zone. He yielded a Gown of the Chamberlain which will go to our good cleric Nickelsen. Kryssanah was meritted a pair of Royal Velium Frosted Greaves, Venganse and Versifier came away with a Royal Velium Frosted Helm and a Royal Velium Frosted Gauntlets each, as well as various other loot. It was an awesome evening for us, and maybe next time we will be able to kill Seneschal Aldikar. Then the only mob left for us will be the Dain himself. Great work guys!
