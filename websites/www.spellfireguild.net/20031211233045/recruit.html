Recruitment Process:

All recruits must be directed to an officer (Check with online members for a recruitment officer.) for sponsorship and raid information.
All potential recruits must obtain a sponsor in order to move into the 2nd phase of recruitment. To obtain a sponsor, request it of someone you know, or group with members whenever possible to make a member feel comfortable enough to sponsor you.
*Note if you harrass members for sponsorship, or lack thereof, your recruitment will be termintated.
All recruits must be unguilded during recruitment.
All recruits must make a post under our "Recruitment" section on the message boards.

All recruits must attend a minimum of seven (7) raids*/guild events and group with members whenever possible to enter the final phase of recruitment.
*A raid is considered a night of raiding, not a single target mob.

During the final phase of recruitment the recruit will remain tagged and be treated as a full member (with the exception of not being allowed to spend DKP against current members) As such, the recruit must adhere to all guild policies and function as a part of the team. If after 30 days the recruit shows the qualities the guild requires he/she will be instructed to keep the tag and be allowed the benefits of a full member. During the final phase of recruitment, it is REQUIRED that the recruit obtain or posess a right click (anti dispell for indoor raids) item and be able to get all resists over 100 without buffs. In addition, all recruits must obtain a minimum of PoV/PoS flags. Also, recruits are entitled to full DKP during recruitment, however cannot use this DKP to vote against full members. All DKP rules can be reviewed under the DKP link from any page within this site. It is strongly recommended that all recruits read and understand the loot policies and guild rules prior to applying for recruitment.
