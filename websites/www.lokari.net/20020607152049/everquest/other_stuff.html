Lesser Faydark Map

This is an original map of the Lesser Faydark zone that I created. Watch out for the Brownies in the north west corner, and the Dark Elves along the south wall!

Runny Eye Map

The original map is a scan of an absolutely wonderful hand-drawn map by Phingers, Raxthorne and Drakkaenn of Mithaniel Marr. These people clearly spent a lot of time on their map, and their work is greatly appreciated! My variation on their map was done strictly to make it a little more computer and printer friendly, and to appeal to neat-freaks, like me.
