Trivial List

The "trivial" levels for items made in Everquest indicate the point at which you can no longer increase your skills by making that item. You can still experience failures making trivial items, but the further beyond the trivial point you get, the fewer failures will occur.

Some of these trivial levels are pretty certain, some are just estimates, based on conflicting information. Unfortunately, I can't test out trivial levels without starting up a new tailor every time an item is in question! Just bear in mind that these are close estimates, and some items may be off a few points.

  • Silk Thread - 15
  • Silk Swatch - 17
  • Silk Bandage - 21
  • Silk Cord - 26
  • Patchwork Armor - 26
  • Tailored Wrist Pouch - 26
  • Leather Padding - 31
  • Tailored Small Bag - 31
  • Raw Silk Armor - 36
  • Tailored Whip - 36
  • Tailored Large Belt Pouch - 37
  • Tailored Large Bag - 41
  • Studded Leather Armor - 57
  • Bearskin Potion Bag - 68
  • Cured Silk Armor - 82
  • Wu's Gauntlets - 88
  • Handmade Backpack - 88
  • Crystalline Silk Garments - 102 to 131
  • Reinforced Leather Armor - 108
  • Mithril Studded Leather Armor - 108
  • Tailored Quiver - 115
  • Imbued Vale Studded Leather Armor - 122
  • Imbued Mithril Studded Leather Armor - 122
  • Wu's Fighting Armor - 128 to 158
  • Ice Burrower Silk Garments - 230 and higher
