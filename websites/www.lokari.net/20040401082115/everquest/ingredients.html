Items Made With The Brewing Skill

Acorn Oil

Combine a Misty Acorn and a Flask of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Misty Acorns are ground spawn in the Misty Thicket. Becomes trivial at a brewing skill of around 103.

Aviak Egg Oil

Combine an Aviak Egg with a Flask of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Aviak Eggs can be foraged in Butcherblock. Becomes trivial at a brewing skill of around 105.

Cod Oil

Combine a Cobalt Cod with a Flask of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Cobalt Cod are caught with the fishing skill in the Cobalt Scar zone of Velious.

Drake Egg Oil

Combine a Drake Egg and a Flask of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Drake Eggs can be foraged in Skyshrine and the Temple of Veeshan.

Heady Kiola

Combine two Kiola Saps, one Flask Of Water, and one Bottle in a Brewers Barrel. Kiola Sap and Bottles can be purchased from merchants who sell brewing supplies. Becomes trivial at a brewing skill of 46.

Oak Bark Tannin

Combine a Piece Of Oak Bark and a Flask Of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Oak Bark can be foraged in Kithicor and Warslik's Woods, and also occurs as a ground spawn in Butcherblock. Becomes trivial at a brewing skill of 103.

Yew Leaf Tannin

Combine a Yew Leaf and a Flask of Water in a Brewers Barrel. Yew Leaves are ground spawn in the Wakening Lands.

Items Made With The Smithing Skill

Fier'dal Sewing Kit

One Mithril Bit, a Needle Mold, a Thimble Mold, and a Flask Of Water. Must be combined in the Fier'dal Forge in Kelethin. Creates a ten-slot Fier'dal Sewing Kit that only works for making Fier'dal cultural armor, and can't be used for making conventional items.

High Quality Metal Bits

This can only be made by Halfling Smiths, and must be made in the Rivervale Forge. Combine two pieces of High Quality Ore and a Flask of Water. The High Quality Ore is occasionally found on Runney Eye goblins. It can also be found on goblins in Permafrost. This becomes trivial at a smithing skill of around 80.

Mithril Bits

Two Small Pieces Of Mithril Ore, and a Morning Dew. Must be combined in the Fier'dal Forge in Kelethin.

Mithril Bonings

A Small Brick Of Mithril Ore, a File, and a Morning Dew. Must be combined in the Fier'dal Forge in Kelethin.

Mithril Studs

Three Mithril Bits, a File, and a Morning Dew. Must be made in the Fier'dal Forge in Kelethin.

Skinning Knife

Combine one Small Brick Of Ore, one Dagger Mold, one Hilt Mold and one Flask Of Water in a normal Forge. Skinning Knives become trivial at smithing skill 41.

Studs

Combine three Metal Bits, a File and a Flask Of Water in a normal Forge. Makes two Studs. Studs become trivial at smithing skill 41.

Steel Bonings

Combine a Small Brick Of Ore, a File and a Flask Of Water in a normal Forge. Steel Boning becomes trivial at smithing skill 41.

Vale Sewing Kit

This can only be made by Halfling Smiths, and must be made in the Rivervale Rorge. Combine High Quality Metal Bits (quantity unknown), a Needle Mold, a Thimble Mold and a Flask of Water. This becomes trivial at a smithing skill of 162.

Velium Bonings

Combine a Small Brick of Velium, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper in a Forge. Coldain Velium Temper can be bought in the Rogue's Guild in Thurgadin.

Velium Studs

Combine two Pieces of Velium and a Coldain Velium Temper in a Forge. This creates stackable Velium Bits. Then combine three Velium Bits, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper in a forge. This creates two Velium Studs, which are stackable. Coldain Velium Temper can be bought in the Rogue's Guild in Thurgadin.

Items Made With The Tailoring Skill

Crystalline Silk Swatch

Combine two Crystalline Silks. These Silks drop from the spiders in the Crystal Caverns and also the spiders in the tunnels under Thurgadin. Can be combined in either a Coldain Tanners Kit or a conventional Small or Large Sewing Kit. Has a low trivial, probably below 30.

Ice Burrower Silk Swatch

Combine two Ice Burrower Silks in a Sewing Kit. The Silks from from the level 60 Ice Burrowers in the Western Wastes.

Silk Cord

Combine three Silk Threads, unstacked, in a Sewing Kit. Becomes trivial at skill 26.

Silk Swatch

Combine two Spider (not Spiderling) Silks in a Small, Large or Deluxe Sewing Kit, or in a Loom. Swatches become trivial around skill 17, however, the combine never fails.

Silk Thread

Combine two Spiderling (not Spider) Silks in a Small, Large or Deluxe Sewing Kit, or in a Loom. Becomes trivial at skill 15, but never fails.

Terrorantula Silk Swatch

Combine two Terrorantula Silks in an Erudite Sewing Kit. The Terrorantula Silk drops from Terrorantula, a rare giant spider in the desert of South Ro.

Enchanter-Made Items

Vial Of Clear Mana

Uses the level 29 spell "Clarify Mana", one Poison Vial and one Emerald.

Vial Of Distilled Mana

Uses the level 39 spell "Distill Mana", one Poison Vial and two Sapphires.

Vial Of Purified Mana

Uses the level 49 spell "Purify Mana", one Poison Vial and four Rubies.

Vial Of Viscous Mana

Uses the level 12 spell "Thicken Mana", one Poison Vial and a Pearl.

Other Miscellaneous Items

Imbued Amber

Shaman - Cazic Thule worshippers of this class can cast an "Imbue Amber" spell (level 29) on an Amber. Amber can be bought from merchants.

Imbued Black Pearl

Cleric - Prexus worshippers of this class can cast an "Imbue Black Pearl" spell (level 29) on a Black Pearl. Black Pearls are dropped by monsters.

Imbued Emerald

Cleric/Druid - Tunare worshippers of these classes can cast an "Imbue Emerald" spell (level 29) on an Emerald. The Emeralds are bought from merchants.

Imbued Plains Pebble

Cleric/Druid - Karana worshippers of these classes can cast an "Imbue Plains Pebble" spell (level29) on a Plains Pebble. Plains Pebbles are foraged in the various Karana zones.

Imbued Topaz

Cleric/Druid - Quellious worshippers of these classes can cast an "Imbue Topaz" spell (level 29) on a Topaz. Topaz can be bought from merchants.
