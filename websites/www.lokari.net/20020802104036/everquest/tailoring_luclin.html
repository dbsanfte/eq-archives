Notes About Luclin Tailoring

Luclin vs. Old World

A number of the items that can be made in Luclin duplicate existing items, but use a different set of ingredients.

Stackable Items

When combining any stackable items in a sewing kit, don't stack them! Put each item in its own slot.

Skinning Knife

A Skinning Knife can be a very handy tool for a tailor. If you have a pelt of a higher quality than you need, such as a Medium Quality when you want a Low Quality, you can reduce the quality by combining the pelt with a skinning knife in your sewing kit. You'll get the knife back, and the pelt will be reduced in quality by one step.

A Skinning Knife is made with the Smithing skill - Combine one small brick of ore, one dagger mold, one hilt mold and one flask of water in a forge. Skinning Knives become trivial at smithing skill 41.

Sewing Contraption

Tinkering Gnomes can make a Sewing Contraption, which increases the chance of success when combining items by five percent. A Sewing Contraption doesn't improce your chances of getting a skill improvement, however. A Sewing Contraption is made using the Tinkering skill, and requires the following: Sewing Contraption Parts, Gear, Sprocket, Gnomish Bolts, Chitterling Barb. It becomes trivial at skill 215, and goes in the primary slot.

Basic Luclin Silk Recipes

Shade Silk Thread

Combine two Shadeling Silks. Shadeling Silk drops from Lesser Shades, Xakra Silkworms in Shadeweaver's Thicket and Xakra Larva in Vah Shir City. Trivial at skill 15.

Shade Silk Swatch

Combine two Shade Silks. Shade Silks drop from Shak Dratha in Shadeweaver's Thicket. Trivial at less than 55.

Shade Silk Bandage

Combine two Shade Silk Threads.

Shade Silk Cord

Combine three Shade Silk Threads. Trivial at skill 26.

Leather Armor

Leather Armor Ingredients

  • Studs

    Smithing - Combine three Metal bits, a File and a Flask of Water in a forge. Makes two Studs. Studs become trivial at smithing skill 41.

  • Steel Bonings

    Smithing - Combine a Small Brick of Ore, a File and a Flask of Water in a forge. Steel Boning becomes trivial at smithing skill 41.

  • Acrylia Studs

    Smithing - Combine two Small Pieces of Acrylia with a Flask of Water in a forge to make Acrylia Bits. Then combine three Acrylia Bits, a Flask of Water, and a File in a forge to make Acrylia Studs. Acrylia is dropped by Grimlings in the Tenebrous Mountains. Trivial level: Unknown.

  • Acrylia Bonings

    Smithing - Combine a Small Brick of Acrylia, a File and a Flask of Water in a forge. Acrylia is dropped by Grimlings in the Tenebrous Mountains. Trivial level: Unknown.

  • Payala Tannin

    Brewing - A Flask of Water and a piece of Payala Bark, combined in a brewing barrel. Payala bark is sold in Shadow Haven by vendor Lander, location 172, 330. Trivial level: Under 120.

Shaded Hopperhide Armor

These are a bit more complex than conventional leather armors. To make these, you must combine a tailor made Tattered Hopper armor item with a matching Xakra Kel armor item, and a Claw Needle.

A Claw Needle comes from the merchant Tiamon Aroo (who sells patterns) in Shadeweaver's Thicket. Hand him four Needle Thin Claws, which drop from Needle Clawed Hoppers, and in return he'll give you a Claw Needle.

The Xakra Kel armor pieces drop from Lesser Shades in Shadeweaver's Thicket. These armor pieces are No-Rent, which means they'll disappear if you log out of the game. So, you must be prepared with the other raw materials to make this armor right away.

Luclin Quest Recipes

Leather Straps

Combine a High Quality Rockhopper Hide with a Small Stone Blade. This is used in a Beastmaster quest. Becomes trivial at around skill 37.

Rallic Pouch

This quest starts in Katta Castellum. Talk to Rallic Aristos and he will tell you to bring him a pouch from a Grimling Runner in Tenebrous Mountains. In return he give you a pattern (LORE) to make the bag but says he doesn't have the rest of the materials.

The other materials you'll need are a Perfect Owlbear Pelt, which drops in the Owlbear cave in Hollowshade Moor, a Shade Silk Thread and a Shade Silk Cord. Combine the pattern, pelt, thread and cord in any tailoring kit.

The final product is a Rallic Pouch, which is a Lore container...
Weight - 1.0, Slots - 10, Holds - Giant, Weight Reduction - 85%, Trivial around 14 - 17.

Champion's Cape

This quest originates with Sentry Ferrin in Shadeweavers Thicket - he wanders the path between Shar Vahl zone and the Gor Taku camp. Hail him and reply to his bracketed text, and he will give you a Venerated Cask. Fill it with poached hides looted from Loda Kai Poachers and Traders. Combine the full Cask and give it back to Ferrin. He then gives you a Hero's Shroud (AC 5, STR 1, SvCold 5, NoDrop). You need to do this part of the quest twice to get two of these Shrouds.

Next hail Kojinn Kaa in Shadeweavers at around -2590, -2860. He's looking for a hero, and needs you to show him something so that he knows you are one. Give him one of the two shrouds you got from Ferrin. He gives it back along with a pattern for a Champions Cape, and tells you to make it then take it to High Priestess Best, and have it blessed with three drops of Loda Kai blood (which drop off Loda Kai Traders). High Priestess Best can be found hidden between rocks at around -2590, -2990.

The recipe for the Champions Cape is two Hero's Cloaks, one Shadeling Silk Thread, and one Champion Cape Pattern. Combine them in a Sewing Kit. This is trivial at between skill 37 and 45. In its unblessed state, the Champion's Cape is AC 8, STR 2, SvCold 10, with a faction modifier.

Give the cloak and three Drops of Loda Kai Blood to Priestess Bast - she gives back a Blessed Champion's Cape, which is AC 8, STR 2, CHA 2, WIS 3, SvCold 10, Lore, and NoDrop, with a faction modifier. It can only be worn by Warriors, Rogues, Bards, Shamans and Beastlords of the Vah Shir race.

Silk Collection

Part 1 - Find Jala in Shar Vahl, upstairs in the building at location 300, -360 or so. She goes into a rambling discussion of Xakra weaving (see Shaded Hopperhide Armor above), then asks you to collect four Shadeling Silks for her. You get some experience and Guardians of Shar Vahl faction for doing so.

Part 2 - She then suggests that you might want to try your hand at weaving with a nearby loom. Combine two Shadeling Silks to create a Shadeling Silk Thread. Give her four of the Threads. You'll get experience and some random piece of cloth armor.

Broker Fahaar's Quests

Give Broker Fahaar in Shar Vahl your Personal Acrylia Slate. If you don't have a Personal Acrylia Slate, speak to Registrar Bindarah in Shar Vahl to do his quest first. This would be a good idea anyway, as a Personal Acrylia Slate is required to do most of the quests that start in Shar Vahl.

Broker Fahaar will give you a four slot Young Hopperhide Sack, in which you must combine four Young Rockhopper Hides. The Hides drop off Young Rockhoppers in the pit surrounding Shar Vahl. Return the combined sack to Broker Fahaar, and he gives you a Leather Cord and a Stretched Hopperhide. You must do this four times, to get four Leather Cords. Finally, give all four Leather Cords to Broker Fahaar, and he will give you a Vah Shir Skinning Knife.

Next, combine a Medium Quality Rockhopper Hide with your Vah Shir Skinning Knife to create a Rough Belt Strap. This is trivial at around 37. Give the Rough Belt Strap and the Vah Shir Skinning Knife (which isn't destroyed) to Broker Fahaar, and he will give you a Vah Shir Needle Set.

Combine a Rough Belt Strap, a Scorpion Shell (which drops in the Shar Vahl pit), a Shade Silk Thread, and the Vah Shir Needle Set to create a Black Chitin Belt. The Belt is trivial to make at about 56, and has the following stats: AC 3, STR 3, Wt 0.7, wearable by Vah Shir WAR BRD ROG SHM and BST classes. Note that in place of the Vah Shir Needle Set, you can instead use an Acrylia Needle or an Acrylia Thimble And Needle Set. Keep the Belt for now.

Combine a Rough Belt Strap, a Grimling Fingerbone, a Shade Silk Thread, and the Vah Shir Needle Set to create a Bone Beaded Belt. The trivial level for this Belt is greater than 65, and it has the following stats: AC 4, STA 3, SvMagic 3, Wt 0.8, wearable by Vah Shir WAR BRD ROG SHM and BST classes. Again, note that in place of the Vah Shir Needle Set, you can instead use an Acrylia Needle or an Acrylia Thimble And Needle Set.

Next, give Broker Fahaar the Black Chitin Belt and the Bone Beaded Belt you made, plus your Vah Shir Needle Set. In return, he gives you an Acrylia Needle. He then asks you to make him some Owlbear Feathered Leggings. Do this by combining an Owlbear Feather, a Sonic Wolf Pelt, two Shade Silk Threads and an Acrylia Needle. As before, you can substitute the Acrylia Thimble and Needle Set for the Acrylia Needle. The Leggings are trivial somewhere between skills 90 and 120, and have the following stats: AC 6, STR 2, STA 2, SvMagic 5, Wt 0.3, wearable by Vah Shir WAR, BRD, ROG, SHM and BST classes.

Give Broker Fahaar the Owlbear Feathered Leggings and your Acrylia Needle. He will upgrade your upgrade your Acrylia Needle to an Acrylia Thimble And Needle Set and give you a six slot container to fill with Owlbear Tunics. To make an Owlbear Tunic, combine two Owlbear Pelts, two Shade Silk Threads, a Tunic Pattern and your Acrylia Thimble and Needle Set. This is trivial between 130 and 160.

Combine the six Owlbear Tunics in the container Fahaar gave you. This time, do not give the results to Broker Fahaar. Instead, you had it to Elder Hortitosh in the Bard guild in the Shar Vahl Palace. Hortitosh will give you a receipt, which you take back to Broker Fahaar.

Finally, Broker Fahaar congratulates you on your efforts, and gives you a Sleek Sonic Wolfhide Tunic to wear. The Tunic has the following stats: AC 16, STR 5, STA 4, CHA 10, wearable by Vah Shir WAR, BST, ROG, BRD and SHM classes. The Tunic is also no drop.
