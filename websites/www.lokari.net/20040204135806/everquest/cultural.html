Halfling Cultural Armor

Vale Studded Leather Armor

  • Ingredients Tailoring Pattern, Brute Hide, Acorn Oil and Studs. You can optionally include an Imbued Plains Pebble, which will give the items additional stamina and wisdom enhancements
  • Combine Can only be crafted by a Halfling Tailor using a Vale Sewing Kit
  • Drop Info Brute Hides drop off Voracious Brutes in the Dreadlands and Skulking Brutes in Warsliks Wood
  • Classes (Non-Imbued) BRD CLR DRU MNK PAL RNG ROG SHD SHM WAR
  • Races (Non-Imbued) DEF DWF ELF ERU GNM HEF HLF HUM
  • Classes (Imbued) Karana worshipers only BRD CLR DRU MNK PAL RNG ROG SHD SHM WAR
  • Races (Imbued) Karana worshipers only DEF DWF ELF ERU GNM HEF HLF HUM
  • Trivial The non-imbued variety becomes trivial at around skill 115, the imbued variety at skill 122
