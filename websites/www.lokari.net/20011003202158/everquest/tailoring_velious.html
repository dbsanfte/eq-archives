    • Important Note about Velious Recipes

    All Velious tailoring recipes listed here require that the components be combined in a Coldain Tanners Kit, with the exception of the Crystalline Silk armor, which can be made in either the Coldain Tanners Kit or a conventional Large Sewing Kit.


    Ingredients for Velious Recipes

    Crystalline Silk Swatch

    Tailoring - Combine two Crystalline Silks. These silks drop from the spiders in the Crystal Caverns and also the spiders in the tunnels under Thurgadin. Can be combined in either a Coldain Tanners Kit or a conventional Small or Large Sewing Kit. Has a low trivial, probably below 30.

    Ice Burrower Silk Swatch

    Tailoring - Combine two Ice Burrower Silks.

    Yew Leaf Tannin

    Brewing - Combine a Yew Leaf and a Flask of Water in a brewing barrel. Yew Leaves are ground spawn in the Wakening Lands.

    Drake Egg Oil

    Brewing - Combine a Drake Egg and a Flask of Water in a brewing barrel. Drake Eggs are foraged in Skyshrine.

    Cod Oil

    Brewing - Combine a Cobalt Cod with a Flask of Water in a brewing barrel. The Cobalt Cod are caught with the fishing skill in the Cobalt Scar zone of Velious.

    Velium Studs

    Smithing - Combine two Pieces of Velium with a Coldain Velium Temper (which can be bought in the Rogue's Guild in Thurgadin) in a forge. This creates stackable Velium Bits. Then combine three Velium Bits, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper in a forge. This creates two Velium Studs, which are stackable.

    Velium Bonings

    Smithing - Combine a Small Brick of Velium, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper (which can be bought in the Rogue's Guild in Thurgadin)in a forge. This creates one Velium Boning, which is stackable.

    Clear Mana

    Enchanter - Uses the level 29 spell "Clarify Mana", one Poison Vial and one Emerald.

    Distilled Mana

    Enchanter - Uses the level 39 spell "Distill Mana", one Poison Vial and two Sapphires.

    Purified Mana

    Enchanter - Uses the level 49 spell "Purify Mana", one Poison Vial and four Rubies.
