If you want to be a Talon: 1) A current guild member should be your sponsor. Find one, make friends, get them to sponsor you. They should strongly support your application 2) That sponsor is responsible for getting you into groups, and keeping your application moving forward. Talk to them. They will decide when you have been "exposed" enough to put your membership to a vote, and they will ask the guild leader to do so. 3) If you don't have a sponsor and can't find one, your chances of becoming a Talon are slim. If your sponsor doesn't get you into groups and support your membership actively, your chances are also quite slim. 4) its a long process, but be patient, it's really better this way for both you and us in the long run!
