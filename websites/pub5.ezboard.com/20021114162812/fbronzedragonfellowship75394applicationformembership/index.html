Click here for instructions on HOW TO JOIN
It will require 2 Members to sponsor new applicants into the guild. You must have the sponsors post on your behalf before filling out the application. You may then fill out an application and submit it for approval. You will also need an Officer to post that they have talked with you and verified that you have taken the proper steps to become a member. Email will be forthcoming letting you know if you have been approved or denied. Once you have recieved an approval email you should contact an officer to be invited.

All applicants that applied on the board and have not met the requirements of sponsorship in 3 weeks will be automatically DENIED.

NEW MEMBERS: A sponsor must be a Bronze Dragon Fellowship member that has been in the guild for a minimum of ONE MONTH. A new member can post on the candidate’s behalf to vouch for the integrity of the individual, but this will not be counted toward sponsorship requirements.
