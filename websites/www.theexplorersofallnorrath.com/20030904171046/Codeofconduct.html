Code of conduct
                   General

1) Alts are allowed but only 1 alt per person. Nobody wants to be in a guild with 50 alts. If you are caught all of your alts are subject to exile.

2) Prejudice whether by race, creed, or sexual disposition will not be tolerated.

3) Uphold the honor of the guild and all its members.

4) Greed leads to anger, anger leads to the Dark Side and the Dark Side leads to exile. Whether grouped with guildmates or non-guildmates remember noobody wants to fight and die for nothing. If you are caught breaking this code you must pay a fine to the Treasurer that is determined by the Council.

5) The Use of /Anno will not be allowed we ask that all members use the /roleplay to hide character info.

6) Try to group with guild members as much as possible get to know all members. This is more along the lines of a suggestion than an actual Code.

7) All Problems, Inquiries, and ideas are submitted to the council for reviewal. And please understand that it may be sometime before the council gets back so have patience.

8) Finally...The golden rule. Do unto others as U wish others do unto U.
