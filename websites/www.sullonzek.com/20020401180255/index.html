The Reapers Creed

ecognizing that I volunteered as a REAPER, fully knowing  the hazards of my chosen GUILD, I will always endeavor to uphold the prestige, honor and high “esprit de corps” of the REAPERS.

nergetically will I meet the enemies of MY GUILD. I shall defeat them on the field of battle for I am better trained and will fight with ALL MY MIGHT.

cknowledging the fact that a REAPER is a more elite PLAYER who arrives at the cutting edge of battle by SoW, sea or PORT, I accept the fact that as a REAPER my GUILD expects me to move further, faster, and fight harder than any other PLAYER.

roudly will I show the World that I am a specially selected and well trained REAPER. My courtesy to other NEWTS, willingness to help, and care for the GUILD shall set the example for others to follow.

ver shall I be vigilant of danger and never shall I fail my comrades. I will always keep myself mentally alert, physically strong. I will shoulder more than my share of the task, what ever it may be. One hundred-percent and then some.

eadily will I display the intestinal fortitude required to fight on to the REAPER objective and complete the mission, though I be the lone survivor.

URRENDER IS NOT A REAPER word. I will never leave a fallen comrade to fall into the hands of the enemy and under no circumstances will I ever embarrass my GUILD.

REAPERS LEAD THE WAY!
