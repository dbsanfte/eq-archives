Holloman is correct. I have concentrated on my piercing and 2h blunt weapon skills by usage and through my guild master. I am currently level 6 moving to 7 and I use a combination of auto attack and repeated shock of frost attacks. When you get to level 4, you will be able to purchase additional spells like shock of fire. Then I used auto attack (with a worned great staff), repeated usages of shock of frost (the reason I use this spell is that I can recast it very quickly but it does only provide you with about 6 pts of damage each time), then when the object of my attack (bat, wasp, skeleton, pixie trickster, orc pawn, and now orc centerion, shaman, and oracle) turn and run, I hit them with the shock of fire spell. This usually finishes them off and I can now loot the body for items to sell for money to buy needed armor and supplies. When you get to the point of attacking orc centerions, shamans, and oracles, you will need to use this technique if fighting alone. Then run like the denizens of hell are chaseing you to a safe spot (usually by a posted guard) and practice meditating. This will increase your meditation skills and bring back your spell points quicker. Good Luck?
