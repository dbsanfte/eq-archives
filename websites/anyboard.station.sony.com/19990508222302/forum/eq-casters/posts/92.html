I've been playing an enchanter since game went commercial.

I have found it to be VERY disappointing. The Official Guide says great in group. This simple is not the case(when was the last time you saw a shout for looking for enchanter to group with?).

Their spells really only work on /con green and sometimes blue. Hardly what a group of 5 is looking to fight. Charm is worthless with it's 30 sec duration. The Mist line of spells is too small a change in ac to be worthwhile(I have lots of player ask for strength they tell me I can skip the mist).

Enfeeble(lev4)(ebbing strength(lev12)actually appears to do less) tested against monsters (100 hits) showed no damage change, if enfeebed or not.

Aliance seems to do nothing. Illusion would at least be fun if it lasted a little while and didn't disappear at each zone change(some cities have multiple zone changes).

As it currently stands, I would not recommend anyone play this class.
