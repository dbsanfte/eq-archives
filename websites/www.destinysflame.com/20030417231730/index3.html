News! Posted Thursday, April 10, 2003 by Aminna
Some big promotions were the order of this week.

Kiyallmane has graciously picked up the mantle of Guild Leader. The guild has been Council run for about a year and we decided it was time to pass the torch on to someone who would lead in the spirit of our founder, Beradin Mistwolf /salute!
