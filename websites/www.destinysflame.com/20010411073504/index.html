March 14-Well since I'm on vacation and EQ decided to patch today I thought I would do the same. I have spent most of the day doing major updates to the website. One of the biggest things I'm working on is spring cleaning on the roster. We will be placing an asterick after a members name if it is a 2nd character of a member. I also added new members to the roster, some of which have been members for a month now, sorry for the mistake. I'm also removing members that we haven't seen for awhile in the game. If you are not listed on the roster send me an email to Beradin and I will post it correctly.

March 9 - Guild picnic date has been set. We are going to do a few things differently. First, if you wish to participate in the PvP tournaments, you must RSVP and include your level so that the teams can be divided ahead of time. Second, a list of items that will be auctioned will be posted in the auction area of the message board . If you have items you wish to donate please send Beradin an email.

March 9 - Two mid level raids are in the works. A raid to Split Paw on March 24th, and a raid to Runnyeye approximately a week later. These raids will require RSVP's as they will be limited to two groups ( a total of 12 for each raid). Thank you to Aminna and Kiyallmane who will be hosting these raids.
