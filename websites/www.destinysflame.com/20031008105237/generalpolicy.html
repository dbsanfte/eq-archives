Honor, Unity, Loyalty, Strength


       As the population continues to grow on Norrath, we, the members of Destiny's Flame, have chosen to band together to thwart the growing number of evils that plague our world.  Growing outward from the forests of Greater Faydark, we welcome all members of all races who are willing to stand by their brother and sister and assist them in their quests and journeys.

     Members of Destiny's Flame follow a few simple codes to better assist us in meeting the standard of our motto:

Honor, Unity, Loyalty, Strength

  • Assist those in need, but be knowledgable of the true needs of the individual.

    Sometimes in our quest to destroy the evils that inhabit our lands, we forget that there are others with a common cause.  Just as it is dishonorable to take away one's destiny, so is it dishonorable to take away the small victories of others.  Make sure that your help is truly needed before providing your service, unless experience and previous knowledge show otherwise.  Thus we are tempered in the Flames of Honor.

  • The bounty of the kills goes to all who participate, no matter their rank in life.

    Our destiny is made more likely by our ability to gather resources for growth, and to work with others with different skills and abilities.  Members who choose to seek only their personal growth when gathered in groups show a lack of unity necessary and will be burned in the Flames of Unity.

  • The goals of the guild before the individual.

    The guild is our way of defeating the challenges we face, and support of the guild is a necessary part of this.  Just as the guild supports us in our times of need, so to do we support the guild's need and are then warmed by the Flames of Loyalty.

  • Respect the members of the guild, regardless of rank, class, or race.

    Our strength lies in our numbers and our specialties.  We have gathered together to assist each other and use our special talents and skills in a common need.  Respect for our brothers and sisters special talents and weaknesses enables us to withstand the blows of our enemies.  By this we are empowered in the Flames of Strength.

MEMBERSHIP PROCESS

  • As of June 1, 2003 all applicants must meet the minimum level requirement of 45, all races and classes are welcome.

  • All applicants must read through our guild rules and policies and post their application to join the guild on the message board.

  • All applicants must spend a fair amount of time adventuring with our membership.

  • All applicants must have 3 non-officers or 1 officer sponsor their 45-day trial membership.

During the 45-day trial membership applicants actions and involvement in the guild will be evaluated. Membership can be revoked at any time during this trial period if a new member fails to abide guild rules. At the end of the 45-day trial, officers vote on whether or not to extend full membership to the applicant.

PLEASE NOTE

Before applying to Destiny's Flame take a good look at our raid schedule and loot rules. Then strongly consider your core goals within the game. If you are looking to hit lvl 60 and own an epic in the next 3 months within this guild, Destiny's Flame is not for you. Even though a large portion of our membership is plane and dragon-capable and we schedule a fair number of endgame raids we are not an uber guild nor do we maintain that kind of raid schedule or attitude.

We like to adventure together and are always willing to help eachother out BUT we will not tolerate abuse of our member's time or person. Members are expected to find a balance between what they receive from the guild and what they give back to it. Imposing on fellow member's play time by sending harassing /tells, /emotes, unreasonable requests (i.e. for ports, rezzes, powerleveling, etc.), and spamming /guild with price checks and auctions are grounds for disciplinary action and possible guild removal.

This is a guild made up of friends and family that like to play together for the fun of it. Our goal is to play the game to the end while enjoying the ride.

ALTERNATE CHARACTER POLICY

The minimum level for all alts to be guilded is lvl 20

The number of alts allowed in guild is based on the main character's level:

  • 45-49 - 2 alts in guild
  • 50+ unlimited alts in guild

UNGUILDING

Members choosing to leave the guild with main characters are respectfully asked to remove alts. We are a family oriented guild and have fierce pride in our membership. Our goals are to advance our membership as a whole. We do not look kindly on departed members attempting to use our guild resources to level/gear-up alts/twinks to meet the application requirments of full-time raiding guilds.

APPLYING FOR READMISSION TO DESTINY'S FLAME

Ex-members wishing to rejoin are asked to post their interest on the general message board. The request will be brought before the council and within a 1 week timeframe a vote by all Officers (not just the Council) will be taken. A majority of the vote will be required for readmission. If four or more votes are against readmission, the ex-member will not be reinvited. An Officer abstaining from voting will not count as a negative vote.

This is the basic policy we have concerning guild raids.

 

Any member sponsoring a raid is considered the "Raid Leader." The raid leader has final authority during the raid. The Raid Leader is responsible for posting all information regarding the raid on the relevant raid boards and calendar. This information will include:

A. Start/End time, Meeting place, Required Supplies, Target Mob(s) and Raid Strategy

Members are expected to be at the meeting place with the requisite supplies on hand at or before the start time. It is understood that exceptions may occur, but these must be approved by the leader of the raid.

B. Groups

The raid leader will assign groups and post them on the board beforehand, if possible, and announce them at the beginning of the raid. Guild members will be assigned as group leaders, and will be responsible for looting their group kills (this helps facilitate gathering of pp at the end and the proper distrobution of items).

C. Loot

The Raid Leader will post Loot Rules on the board(s) and announce them at the beginning of the raid. Most raids have specific goals in mind, and these goals will be the priority in distributing the loot.

1. Specific raid looting rules such as NBG, no NBG, or flavours thereof, are exlusively the realm of the raid organizer. Reservations for items are neither frowned upon or encouraged. However, if items are to be reserved they MUST be announced on the board and in the gathering locale BEFORE the raid gets underway.


2. 100% of the pp and sellable drops go toward paying for buff/transport gems with the remainder going to the guild bank to finance future raids. Please bear in mind that the guild bank is not accessible 24/7 and if funds are needed, it must be put into the planning for a raid.


3. SoV quest gems go to the guild bank to be distributed as needed to individuals completing their quests. Resist gems will be rolled upon as they drop.


4. If an Epic item is a possible loot item on the raid, those interested should make that known to the organizer beforehand via the message boards. Epic items will go to those on the raid closest to completion of their quest. If there is no clear leader in that context then they will be rolled upon by all those doing the quest. If finally there is no one present doing the Epic, the item can be opened to a normal loot roll or given to someone in the guild in need of the item.


5. Should a member feel that the policies are not being followed, they may contact an officer who will bring the subject to the Council. Members who abuse the raiding rules for personal benefit will find their membership revoked.
