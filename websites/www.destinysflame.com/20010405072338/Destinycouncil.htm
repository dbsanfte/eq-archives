COUNCIL OF THE FLAME

All officers of the guild are members of the Council of the Flame

Rankings:
Lord Champion of the Flame
Champion of the Flame
Guardians of the Flame

The Lord Champion and Champion are the voting members of the council, with the Guardians having full advisory abilities.

All members of the council can recruit new members into the guild.





Time Zones:

PST-Pacific Standard
MST-Mountain Standard
CST-Central Standard
EST-Eastern Standard
