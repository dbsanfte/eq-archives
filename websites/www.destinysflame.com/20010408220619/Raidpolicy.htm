Guild Raids

 

This is the basic policy we have concerning guild raids.

 

  


 To eliminate potential problems on guild raids we have established a guild policy concerning all guild related raids. The survival of our members during a raid requires us to be organized and prepared for unforeseen encounters and situations.

First, there will be one individual assigned to be the leader of the raid. His or her authority during the raid is the final say as to what each individual does during the raid.

Second, raids are planned events which includes the time of the event and not just the location. A starting point will be set and all members who are involved in the raid need to be at that point at the proper time. Transportation to a raid will be provided before the raid. Once the time of the raid has arrived, the event is officially active and all members are expected to be in their places. It is understood that exceptions may occur, but these must be approved by the leader of the raid.

Third, there will be a planned method of distribution of the rewards of the raid. Most raids will have specific goals in mind, and these goals will be the priority in distributing the loot.
