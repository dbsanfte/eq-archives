What Is New ?

 

   » As you might already know after a period of quick improvement, our guild faced some recession. Some of our high level members left the guild with various reasons and this crippled our hopes of forming balanced high level groups, thus planning more serious action. Due to those seperations plus some uneasiness within guild, guild officers and some other high level members would leave the guild in some close past too. But after that much time within BG, all those friendships, memories both good and bad and still-burning determination to struggle for better days kept us from leaving. But we also decided to take some certain actions like strictly applying Blind Hypostasis, improving some of the rules and adding some guides to prevent corruption within guild and make us a better guild. Either this had to be done else BG would never make the guild we once set out for. So, the guide will begin with telling what our guild aim is and go on with some new rules plus revised rules. All members say they are fond of BG and like to be a part of it. So please show this with actions too; actions prove while words suspend in the air. And this is the time to make actions. There are many ways to show actions and going along with the rules and auxiliary guides is a good way of acting, a very good one indeed.

    Guild Aim

   Our general aim is to be a good guild. This includes many aspects and might vary according to each player's thoughts. So here we make it clear what our understanding from a good guild definition is. We want to be one of the best guilds of server , doing serious action like planar raids, other high level zone raids and epics  etc. So we set out for our best. While doing those we always aim to have fun too but we shall never forget that discipline and seriousness will always accompany our aim as well.

    General Issues

    To achieve our aims, we need to be more organized, disciplinized and our members should apply al the rules. This means we will be more strict with rules and any member contradicts with the rules will be removed immediately. Please read Blind Hypostasis, which you can find at that site. It contains the general guidelines of our guild.

    I need to know the email addresses of all the guild members. Please send me an email unless you have not done so up to now and I will keep a DB. You can create a new account at one of the widely-used free email account provider sites if you want to keep your own email address privite. You can find my email address at contact page.

   All members should have a means of knowing EST time at any time given. We will be doing all our plans with EST time. You can know EST time with many ways and buying a simple and cheap clock and setting it to EST time can be the easiest way.

    Our current level limit for recruitement is now level 35. First lets explain why we need to set a level limit and then why it is 35 now. Having a wide level range creates some serious problems between level blocks. Being unable to join guild raids with higher level members, lacking the items higher levels members have and being unable to group and hunt with high level members disturb lower level members. And requests that are almost impossible to fulfil from lower level members frustrates higher level members. Everyone begins his/her career at level one and passes through all steps untill reaching his/her current level.So we all know what each level means to person. We neither scowl at lower level players nor despise them. But to keep balance within guild we have to determine a level limit for recruitements. Level 35 seems fine , although a lvl 35 member will unlikely join raids, untill he/she reaches the level needed , he/she can learn more about BG and improve his/her friendship with guildmates.

    This level limit will also affect the existing guild members. All guild members who are below level 35 are expected to reach lvl 35 as soon as they can manage it. At least they have to show the endevour.

    Recruitements will be more strict from now on. The candidates will be asked a group of questions and there will be a period of knowing the candidate. The candidate will be recruited only after he/she groups with guildmates , preferably guild officers. We may devise an application form for our new recruitements.

    All guildmates should group with each other. This improves friendship within guild and gives a great chance to see what other players's strong and weak points are. High level raids require precise coordination between raid members and this can be achieved by grouping with guildmates.

    Reliquary system will be put into use in a different style. In almost every raid, at least one of the items that are looted will be put into reliquary and this will certainly be a serious item. Donations from members , both money and item, are welcomed and this shows member's attitude towards BG. Speaking of items and loots, all members are expected to be humble when it comes to looting and sharing. All members should remember that this game is more fun only when you share. So, when raid leader informs the party that  "this item"  will be put into Reliquary, there must not be any objections. We even hope to reach a point when and where raid leader will announce who will get the item just looted without any rolls at all. This will certainy improve the relations within the guild and lead members to trust each other more and more. Items gathered in Reliquary can be given to the members , or they can be used in other ways. They can be used to gain "very important things" for the guild. A very important spell that will apparently be very helpful to guild , a critical item that will improve the damage output of a tank, exepnditures for raids, giftd for marrying members etc. can all be provided with that fund. Lets say someone sells a very good haste item at market. We can try buying it with our funds for a worthy guild member tank. Level limit to have acces to Reliquary is currently level 40. This limit is put to encourage members to reach high levels and it is obvious that 40 + members will be adding to Reliquary more. All requests to use Reliquary will be made to Dracillion , Lord of Treasury. Requests will be discussed and decided by officers. All the information about Reliquary will always be available at our website with precise data.
