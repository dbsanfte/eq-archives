  Blind Guardians is a neutral good guild located at Bertoxxulous server of game EverQuest. We try to help people when situation arises and forging good relations with other players is one essential rule of our humble family. If you are looking for a warm society that will embrace you with a warm welcome and continous fun this is the place where you need to be. Evergoing guild chats , fine grouping chances , questing together with some possible high level guildmates, in-guild quests , guild raids, new cool friends are just some of the aspects awaiting you in the future. If you wish to join our evergrowing sociaty please contact personally to me or any of the guild officers whom you can learn their names in administration section.
