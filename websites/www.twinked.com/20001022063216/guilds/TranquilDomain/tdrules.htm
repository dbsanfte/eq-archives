Tranquil Domain's Rules & Regulations

1. Guild members must work together to better themselves & the guild

2. recruiting will be done only after the recruit has:
     a. visited our web site & posed an introduction to himself/herself
     b. has been approved by the majority of the guild members
     c. undergone a 1 week trial period in which he/she had participated in all
          guild events

only after all (a-c) have been successfully met will the new recruit be tagged as
a member of TD at which point he/she will undergo a 30 day probational period in
which he/she will be assigned an officer to oversee his/her actions.

* we should refrain from limiting recruiting to a specific level(s) because good
new players are a scarcity, instead we should improve on our initiation process.

3. Participation in guild events is voluntary but required.  An event organizer has
the authority at his/her discretion to call upon members for posted events who are
online but did not register for the event to join regardless of the amount of time
that member has to play or what he/she is doing at that time.
     a. those who will be called upon to join an event they did not register for
          must show up to the even for the greater good of the guild.  In the event
          that you fail to show up you will be:

     I. 1st time issued a warning
     II. 2nd time will be dismissed from the guild

** guild events are held for the benefit of the entire guild & therefore participation
in them should be the highest priority for all.

4. All events should be:
      a. posted on our web site preferably 1 week prior to its occurrence so that
          members will have an opportunity to sign up for the event.  It is ok to
          bring friends who are not members of TD to an event as long as they register
          for the event and follow our rules.
     b. People will have 30 minutes to assemble at a predetermined place at
          predetermined time otherwise the even will be called off.  Even organizer
          has at his/her discretion to extend the 30 minute time limit.
     c. Transportation to as close as possible will be made available by the event
          organizer.

5. If an event has been called but not posted:
      a. participation is appreciated but not required, members will be asked to join
          in but cannot be ordered to do so.
     b. people have 30 minutes to assemble otherwise the even will be called off.
          Event organizer at his/her discretion can extend the 30 minute time limit.
     c. Transportation to as close as possible will be made available by the event
          organizer.

6. Looting:
     a. looting order for the entire guild for guild events will be posted on the
          officers chat site
     b. starting on 5/9/2000 1 person will be completely outfitted at a time (will
          receive all items his/her class can use unless he/she already has that item
          in which case it will go to the next person on the looting order in that
          class).  The looter has the opportunity to pass up the item to the next
          person in the looting order but not to a person of his/her choosing.
     c. Anyone who loots an item who is not told to do so by the event organizer or
          by an officer assigned to distribute loot will be dealt with in the following
          manner:

     I. 1st time will receive a warning
     II. 2nd time will be dismissed from the guild

*** For those who cannot be happy for the person who got the loot or feel that the loot
has gone to the wrong person, please keep it to yourself because you will do nothing
except disturb other members & the guild.

7. Guildchat is to be used for productive purposes only.
     a. There will be no cursing in guildchat
     b. Guildchat should be kept to a minimum during an event so that members of the
          event cant see what is being said.
     c. Guildchat is not to be used to discuss or settle disputes

8. In event of a guild dispute:
     a. 1st all parties will be given a chance to settle the dispute among
         themselves, if that fails then
     b. 2nd an officer should be brought in to settle the dispute in a fair & an
          unbiased manner, in the event that an officer cant settle the dispute, then
     c. 3rd a Guild Leader will attempt to settle the matter

**** Disputes are not to be discussed in guildchat or in the open chat on our website
