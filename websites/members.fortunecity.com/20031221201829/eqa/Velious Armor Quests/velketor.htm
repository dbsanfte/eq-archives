VELKETOR'S LAB

A lot of gems tend to drop here in Velketor’s Labyrinth. The most common drop places are around the kobold area. Around here there are numerous named kobolds including Venar Icepaw, Tpos Icepaw, Jelek Icepaw, and many many more. For more information on these named mobs you might want to check out Illia’s Bestiary for Velketor’s Lab. Also in these kobold villages there are some regular mobs that include an Icepaw Champion, an Icepaw Cleric, an Icepaw Kobold, an Icepaw Kobold Priest, an Icepaw Kobold Warrior, and an Icepaw Prophet. All of these drop crushed gems, although very rarely. They range is level 48-51 while the named Icepaws range from level 50-59. This is also for the most part a raid oriented zone or a high level group zone. Meaning that you shouldn’t come here alone or with a few of your friends. Either get a full group of 50+ that includes a cleric, enchanter, and warrior or organize a raid to get together and kill kobolds here.
