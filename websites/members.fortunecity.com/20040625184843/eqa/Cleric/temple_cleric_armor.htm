TEMPLE OF SOL RO CLERIC ARMOR

The Temple of Solusek Ro Cleric Armor is given out by Blaize the Radiant and Gavel the Temperant in Temple of Solusek Ro.  They are right at the entrance to the zone.

 

Chestplate of the Constant

You say, 'Tell me of the constant.'

Gavel the Temperant says 'Althuryn the Constant was brutally slain by two aqua goblins. The one called Sludge fled to Runnyeye, the one called Dwigus lives in Dagnor's Cauldron. Bring me the ingot of the Constant, the icon of the Constant, and 2 galvanized platinum bars and I will forge them into a reward for you.

For the Chestplate, you will need an Ingot of the Constant, and Icon of the Constant, and two Galvanized bars of Platinum.  For the Ingot of the Constant, you must kill Sludge Dankmire in Runnyeye.  He is a level 25 goblin and spawns at the very bottom of Runnyeye in a well.  There is also a Gel Cube there, around level 20.  You might want to kill him also so that you don't have to deal with them both at the same time.  The Icon of the Constant is dropped off of Dwigus in Dagnor's Cauldron.  He is a  rare spawn (usually every 5-6 hours) but is only level 35.  He roams inbetween the goblin camps and the Unrest zone.  He is very easy to kill if you use magic and fire based spells.  Also, it might help to have a tracker with you, so that you don't miss him.  The two Gulvanized bars of Platinum can be obtained by giving Genni two Enchanted Bars of Platinum.  The bars of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

 

Vambraces of the Fervent

You say, 'Tell me of the fervent.' (Vambraces)

Blaize the Radiant says 'Jovan the Fervent was always prone to vice. He lost his ingot in a game of King's Court with an aviak named Gull in the Ocean of Tears. After that he bacame a drunkard and it has been said he lurks around the bars in Highpass Hold. Bring me the Ingot of the Fervent, the Icon of the Fervent, and 1 vulcanized platinum bar and I will forge them into a reward for you.

 

For the Vambraces, you will need Ingot of the Fervent, Icon of the Fervent, and one Vulcanized bar of Platinum.  For the Ingot of the Fervent, you must kill Gull Skytalon in the Ocean of Tears.  He spawns on the Aviak Island at approximately 2000, 4000.  He is an uncommon spawn and can spawn anywhere on the island so you will want a tracker with you.  He is level 35.  If you are like me, and can't stand long spawns, try logging on right when the server comes up.  When servers go down and come back up, all the monsters respawn.  If you are lucky enough, and Gull's placeholder didn't spawn (usually a regular aviak) then you might get to kill him.  The Icon of the Fervent is obtained by giving two Tumpy Tonics to Jovan in Highpass Hold.  Here is how you get the Tumpy Tonics.  First, get on the boat at Freeport heading to Butcherblock.  You might want to get SoW before you go.  Anyways, on the first stop, which is the Sister Isle, run out and straight into the house right across from the docks.  There should be a woman behind a counter selling kiola nuts.  You might want to check a map sight to make sure you are going into the right house.  The nuts cost about 2 platinum and you will need two of them for the quest.  The icon of them look like chick peas.  Once you have them, race back to the boat.  You do not want to miss the boat or else you will be waiting the awhile unless you can port yourself out.  Get off the boat at Butcherblock and go to South Kaladim.  There is a store here called Tumpy's Place where you will find Tumpy, standing behind a bar.  Give him one kiola nut and one regular water flask (not foraged or summoned) for your Tumpy Tonic.  Give him another kiola nut and one regular water flask for your second Tonic.  Now, find Jovan in Highpass Hold on the second floor of the Lumberyard Inn.  Give him the two Tumpy Tonics and he will despawn, leaving you with the Icon of the Fervent.  The Vulcanized bar of Platinum is obtained by giving Villisia in Temple of Solusek Ro, one enchanter bar of platinum. The bars of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

 

Bracers of the Reverent

You say, 'Tell me of the reverent.' 

Gavel the Temperant says 'Regis the Reverent fell in love with a gypsy girl named Lianna Ferasa who lives in the Rathe Mountains. Ask her what has become of him. Bring me the ingot of the Reverent, the icon of the Reverent, and 2 enchanted platinum bars and I will forge them into a

reward for you.

 

For the bracers, you will need the Icon and Ingot of the Reverent, and two enchanter platinum bars.  For the Ingot, you must kill Zahal the Vile in North Karanas.  He is a ghoul, around level 30.  He spawns around ever 4 hours at location -1800, 200.  After Zahal is killed, Regis the Reverent will spawn at  1450, -1560.  You must now kill him (level 28) and he drops the Ingot of the Reverent and a flute.  Take the flute to Lianna Ferasa, a gypsy in Rathe Mountains at 4000, 1500.  She will give you the Icon of the Reverent.  For the enchanted bars of platinum, you will need to buy two bars of platinum from a jeweler store for 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  Then you will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

 

 

Greaves of the Penitent

You say, 'Tell me of the penitent.' 

Blaize the Radiant says 'Astrid the Penitent often visited the frogloks of Upper Guk. She once told me her ingot was hidden underwater there. I found that strange because she couldn't swim. She was also a good friend to the Kerrans of Kerra Ridge, where she lost her life fighting against the Erudite heretic Maugarim. Bring me the Ingot of the Penitent, the Icon of the Penitent, and 1 magnetized platinum bar and I will forge them into a reward for you.

 

For the greaves, you will need the Ingot and Icon of the Penitent and one magnetized platinum bar.  The Ingot is found in the right-hand Water Pillar in Upper Guk in the Scryer Room, at loc -209, 458.  The Icon is found off of the Necromancer Heretic, Maugarim in Kerra Ridge.  She is level 27-31 and spawns in the room behind and to the left of the High-Priestess' castle.  She spawns every hour but because of the little amounts of people that hunt here, she is usually always up.  She uses fear and hits hard with nukes and melee.  Make sure to use save magic before attacking her or else when she fears you, you will run out to the Kerrans and they will kill her.  Loot the Icon of the Penitent off of her.  The Magnetized bar of Platinum is obtained by giving Ostorm in Temple of Solusek Ro, one enchanter bar of platinum. The bars of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

 

Gauntlets of the Ardent

 

You say, 'Tell me of the ardent.'

Blaize the Radiant says 'Theo the Ardent was in constant conflict with a gnome necromancer in Befallen named Babbinsbort, but he never could defeat him. Poor Theo drowned on an expedition to Everfrost when the ice of the frozen river gave way beneath him. Bring me the Ingot of the Ardent, the Icon of the Ardent, and 1 galvanized platinum bar and I will forge them into a reward for you.

 

For the gloves you will need the Icon and Ingot of the Ardent and one galvanized platinum bar.  The icon is on a named gnome necromancer on the 3rd floor of befallen, around level 25 named Boondin Babbinsbort.  For the ingot, you must swim to the bottom of the Everfrost Frozen River and head to location 2996, -5711 where the ingot should be.  You will need enduring breath for this long swim.  For the galvanized bar, you must give Genni one platinum bar.  The bar of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

 

Boots of the Reliant

 

You say, 'Tell me of the reliant.'

Blaize the Radiant says 'Turgan the Reliant lived in a cabin in the Kithicor woods until there was a great fire. Searching the burned cabins there may turn something up. He always loved Lake Rathetear as well. Bring me the Ingot of the Reliant, the Icon of the Reliant, and 1 enchanted platinum bar and I will forge them into a reward for you.

 

For the boots, you will need the Ingot and Icon of the Reliant and one enchanted platinum bar.  The Ingot spawns in Kithicor Forest on a tree stump near the left wall.  The location is -70, 3275.  To get the icon, you must retrieve something else from Kithicor forest.  In a burnt cabin at loc 1700, 1100 there is a diary on the ground.  Pick up the diary and bring it to Turgan in Lake Rathe at 3626,112, left of the gnoll camp.  This is most likely the easiest Cleric Temple of Solusek Ro Armor Quest due to the fact that both the items you have to get are very close to each other and you don't have to kill anything.  Although you must be very careful.  I suggest that you wait for it to be day time in Kithicor forest before going to get the items on the ground.  At night it is infested with all sorts of undead.  Invisibility vs. Undead works against some of them but not all.  For the enchanted bar of platinum, you will need to buy one bar of platinum from a jeweler store for 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  Then you will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.  Turn in all the items from the Boots of the Reliant.

 

 

 

Shield of the Devout

You say, 'Tell me of the devout.'

Gavel the Temperant says 'Nebbletob the Devout was once a slave in the mines of Meldrath. The Minotaur Sentry there was particularly cruel to him. He was in the expedition to Everfrost when Brother Theo drowned. When trying to rescue Theo, he came upon some caves under the river. He also narrowly escaped death when a great white beast attacked him. He never saw what the beast was, but it shredded his pack where he kept his icon. Bring me the ingot of the Devout, the icon of the Devout, and 2 vulcanized platinum bars and I will forge them into a reward for you.

For you shield you will need the Icon and Ingot of the Devout and two vulcanized platinum bars.  The Icon is dropped from a Minotaur Sentry in the Minotaur Caves in Steamfont.  He spawns in the same room as Meldrath.  If he is not up when you get there, you can try killing the guards and this will eventually make him spawn.  He is level 30-32 and has a lot of hit points for his level.  The ingot drops off of a glacier bear in Everfrost.  The glacier bear is a very rare spawn in the caves of Everfrost.  To get to them.  Go through one of the holes in the ice river and swim left (if coming from Halas).  You will come to the entrance to the caves.  If you are cautious like me and can't find your way in water very well, I suggest getting an enduring breath.  All the times I have killed the bear it was in the morning or at night, in real life.  So, simply bind yourself in the caverns and in the morning and at night, go check to see if it is up.  Or, even more conveniently you could have an alternate tracker logged out in the cave and log him on every morning and night to see if it is up.  Make sure you have a map of the caverns so that you don't get lost and so that you know where you are going.  Nothing here is very dangerous as long as you stay out of the large caverns.  These are usually occupied by high level NPCs such as Miragul and Sulon McMoor.  Also, the place holder of the bear is an icebone skeleton.  You can know which one is his PH because it walks around the corridors clockwise.  Once you find the glacier bear, it isn't too hard to kill.  You might want to have a friend tanker come along with you when it is up to help you kill it.  The Vulcanized bar of Platinum is obtained by giving Villisia in Temple of Solusek Ro, one enchanter bar of platinum. The bars of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.

 

Caduceus of the Sacrament

You say, 'Tell me of the sacrament.' 

Gavel the Temperant says 'The ingot and icon of Sacrament are believed t be just legends, but I sometimes dream of what I could forge with them and 2 magnetized platinum bars!

 

The Ingot of the Sacrament is dropped off of a Giant Skeleton in Rathe Mountains.  This is no ordinary Giant Skeleton though.  It is a special one, different from all the others and its bones are slightly darker than the rest.  It spawns in the ruin at -600, -1600.  It is level 22-28 and not hard to solo.   The Icon spawns on the floor in Mistmoore.  In the back of the Graveyard, you will find an illusionary wall.  On the other side is four doors.  Open one of the doors and they all will open.  There are monsters behind the doors that are blue to level 35s so you will need a group to do this.  In the center closet, is the Icon, on the floor.  The Magnetized bar of Platinum is obtained by giving Ostorm in Temple of Solusek Ro, one enchanter bar of platinum. The bars of platinum cost around 100-110 platinum depending on your charisma.  You will need a high level enchanter to enchant the bars for you.
