CLERIC EPIC OVERVIEW

Orb of Frozen Water

Kill Lord Bergurgle in Lake Rathe and get Lord Bergurgle’s Crown.

Take the Crown to Shmendrik Lavawalker in Lake Rathe to get Oil of Fennin Ro.

Kill the Spirit of Flame which spawns after Shmendrik dies from Natasha and take the Damaged Goblin Crown.

Give Natasha the Damaged Goblin Crown for an Ornate Sea Shell.

Take this Ornate Sea Shell to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep for a Coral Statue of Tarew.

Take the Statue to the a seeker in Temple of Solusek Ro at 326, 38.

Kill the Plasmatic Priest that spawns and loot the Blood Soaked Plasmatic Robe.

Kill Lord Grimblox in Solusek A and get Grimblox Signet Ring.

Take the Blood Soaked Plasmatic Robe to Omat Vastsea for the Orb of Frozen Water.

 

Orb of Clear Water

Take the Signet Ring and give it to Natasha in Timorous Deep for a second Ornate Sea Shell.

Take this Sea Shell to Naxot Deepwater in Burning Woods for a Message to Natasha.

Kill Ixiblat Fir in Burning Woods and loot Sceptre of Ixiblat Fir.

Kill Overking Bathezid Di'Zok in Chardok for Singed Scroll.

Give the Singed Scroll and the Sceptre of Ixiblat Fir to Omat Vastsea for the Orb of Clear Water.

 

Orb of Vapor

Run to Natasha in Timorous Deep and give her the Message to Natasha.

Get a shimmering pearl.

Camp Zordak Ragefire in Solusek B and give him the shimmering pearl.

Kill Ragefire, and then his dragon form, and loot Heart of Zordak Ragefire and give this to Omat for the Orb of Vapor.

 

Final Hand-In

Give the Orb of Frozen Water, the Orb of Clear Water, and the Orb of Vapor to Jhassad Oceanson for the Orb of Triumvirate.

Give the Orb of Triumvirate to the Avatar of Water for the Water Sprinkler of Nem’Ankh.
