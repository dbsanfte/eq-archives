LAMBENT BARD ARMOR

 

All Quest Givers for the Lambent Armor are found in the Temple of Solusek Ro off of Lavastorm.

The Helm

You say: "What lambent helm?"

Cryssia Stardreamer says: "I can give you a Lambent Helm, but you will need to fetch me a few items that I need for later trades with the followers of Solusek Ro. I require an Opoline Helm from a Deepwater Goblin and a Mudwater Rune from a Lizard Fanatic of Cazic Thule. Finally, you will also need to see Garden about getting me a Lambent Star Ruby. Bring me these items, and I will give to you a Lambent Helm."

The Opoline Helm is dropped off of Deepwater Goblins in Oasis of Marr.  The goblins spawn in an underwater tower close to the specters.  The goblins are level 33-37.  If you get a group of 40+ you can clear the tower easily and a helm is almost guaranteed to drop.  The next item you need is a Mudwater Rune.  These drop quite frequently by Lizard Fanatics in Temple of Cazic Thule.  The are around level 32-35.  If you get into a Throne Room group, and you pull from the top floor, you will get lots of fanatics and one is sure to drop one.  Just make sure your group knows you want it because they are No Drop.  Next you need a Lambent Star Ruby.  These are obtained by giving Garden, an NPC is Temple of Solusek Ro, two Star Rubies and a Lambent Stone.  The Star Rubies are purchased by jewelry vendors for approximately 60-70 platinum depending on your Charisma.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.

The Bracers

You say: "What lambent bracers?"

Cryssia Stardreamer says: "Lambent Bracers are nice armor, and do not have the same level of complexity that other Lambent Armor possesses. If you can fetch for me a Dark Boned Bracelet from a Greater Dark Boned Skeleton, a Griffenne Charm from a Griffenne and a Lambent Fire Opal then I will give you a Lambent Bracer."

The Greater Dark Boned Skeletions in Unrest, which drop the Dark Boned Bracelet, are level 25-30.  They drop the Bracelet rarely, but if you camp them, (usually in the basement), you can get one quickly.  The Griffenne's, which drop the Griffenne Charm, spawn in North and East Karanas.  They are level 24-28 but hit as if they were 33+.  The Charm is uncommon and will take 15-20 kills.  If you are a higher level, as in above 40, and are doing this quest for a alternate, you can also kill the Griffons for the Charm but they are much higher level.  Finally, you need a lambent fire opal is created by getting a Lambent Stone and two Fire Opals and giving it to Garden in Temple of Solusek Ro.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.  The fire opals can be bought from Genni in Temple of Solusek Ro for 550 gold pieces.

 

 

The Vambraces

 

You say: "What lambent vambraces?"

Cryssia Stardreamer says: "I will give you Lambent Vambraces if you can acquire the following items for me - Fiery Vambraces from a Solusek Champion and the top portion of the Rune of the One Eye from a Cyclops named Choon. You will also need to see Garden about getting me a Lambent Sapphire. Acquire these items for me and I will give you Lambent Vambraces."

 

The Solusek Champion, which drops the Fiery Vambraces, is level 25-28 and spawns in Solusek A.  He spawns near the King Room and near the River of Fire.  When he gets low on health he runs away and brings back many friends.  You will want to snare him or root him when he gets low on health.  Choon, which drops the Rune of the One Eye, is a giant in West Karanas.  He has lots of hit points so it will take a while to kill him unless you are a high level.  He spawns near the ogre camp.  Finally, you need a Lambent Sapphire which is created by giving Garden in Temple of Solusek Ro, a Lambent Stone and two Sapphires.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.  The Sapphires can be bought off of Jewelry Vendors.

 

 

The Breastplate

You say: "What lambent breastplate?"

Cryssia Stardreamer says: "Lambent Breastplates are not cheap, but I can get you one if you bring me a few items that I need for later trades with the followers of Solusek Ro. Bring me a Basalt Carapace from a Basalt Gargoyle in the Caverns of Guk and a Gypsy Lute from the Castle of Mistmoore. You will also need to see Garden about getting me a Lambent Ruby. Collect these items for me, and I will give you a Lambent Breastplate."

The Basalt Gargoyles, which drop the Basalt Carapace, are around level 30-34 and spawn in Lower Guk.  They can be found at the bridge close to the Assassin Room and are often pulled by the Assassin group.  The Carapace is  rare and will take a while of camping unless you are lucky.  The Gypsy Lute, which is found off of Gypsy Musicians, drops in Mistmoore.  They are level 30 and can be found in a special room with three other gypsy.  Do not confuse this with Lute of the Gypsy Princesses.  Finally, the Lambent Ruby is obtained by giving Vilissa in Temple of Solusek Ro, a lambent stone and two rubies unstacked.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.  The rubies can be bought off of Jewelry Vendors for approximately 140 platinum depending on your charisma.

 

 

The Gauntlets

You say, "What lambent Gauntlets?"

Walthin Fireweaver says: "Lambent Gauntlets are exceptionally well made gauntlets. If you are interested, I will give you a pair - but you will have to bring me the following items that I need for a trade with the followers of Solusek Ro. I need Shin Gauntlets from a Froglok Shin Knight and Black Silk Gloves from a Deathly Usher in Castle Mistmoore. You will also need to talk to Garden about getting me a Lambent Star Ruby. Bring me these items and you will earn Lambent Gauntlets."

The Shin Gauntlets drop off of Froglok Shin Knights most commonly found at the Dead Side 1 entrance to Upper Guk in Lower Guk.  They can also be found when hunting in the Bedroom.  They are around level 33-35 and the gauntlets are uncommon by these Shin Frogloks are found everywhere.  The Black Silk Gloves are found in Mistmoore dropped off of Deathly Ushers in the graveyard or jail.  They are around level 30 and drop the gloves rarely.  Just an experience group going and you should get some gloves eventually.  Next you need a Lambent Star Ruby.  These are obtained by giving Garden, an NPC is Temple of Solusek Ro, two Star Rubies and a Lambent Stone.  The Star Rubies are purchased by jewelry vendors for approximately 60-70 platinum depending on your Charisma.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.

 

The Greaves

You say: "What lambent greaves?"

Walthin Fireweaver says: "There are no finer leggings for a Bard then Lambent Greaves. Collect for me two lesser pair of greaves and I will give you some. Bring me Icy Greaves from an Elite Goblin Guard in the Caverns of Permafrost and Shin Greaves from a Shin Ghoul Knight, as well as a Lambent Fire Opal and I will gift you a pair of Lambent Greaves."

The Elite Goblin Guards are about level 25-30 but they spawn deep into Permafrost.  They can see-invisibility and they will aggro you.  Once your invisibility comes off, others mobs will aggro you.  Because of this, the only way to get this deep without dying, is either with a high level group, or in a mid-level raid.  The Icy Greaves drop 1/10 of the time.  The Shin Ghoul Knights drop the Shin Greaves.  They are an uncommon drop but if you can get a group in the Bedroom, you will definitely get a pair because of the massive numbers of Shins that spawn there.  Finally, buy two Fire Opals from Genni, in the Temple of Solusek Ro for exactly 550 gold pieces, and then give the Fire Opals and a Lambent Stone to Garden.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.

 

The Boots

You say: "What lambent boots?"

Walthin Fireweaver says: "Lambent Boots are the boots of choice for most Bards. If you can bring me Firewalker Boots from a Solusek Mage and the middle portion of the Rune of the One Eye from a Cyclops named Froon then I will give you a pair of Lambent Boots. Oh - I almost forgot - I will need a Lambent Sapphire as well."

 

The Solusek Mage, in Solusek A, which drops the Firewalker Boots, is a wizard around level 31.  He can be found wandering around the King Room and Jail Room area, rather deep into Solusek A.  He nukes hard so make sure to have a healer or a stunner to interrupt his spells.  Froon, who drops the Rune of the One Eye, is a level 30 cyclops that spawns near the ogre camp, just like his brother Choon (see The Vambraces.)  Finally, buy two Sapphires from any jewelry vendor, and then give the Sapphires and a Lambent Stone to Garden.  The Lambent Stone can be bought from players for the low price of around 30 platinum, or you can get them off of giants and griffins.
