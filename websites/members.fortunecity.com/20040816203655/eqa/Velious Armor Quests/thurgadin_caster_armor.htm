THURGADIN CASTER ARMOR

The Torn Enchanted Silk Armor, needed for this quest is dropped off of mobs in the Arena area of Kael.  The best way to get to this area, is to have one group of 55-60s with the necessaries (enchanter, cleric, warrior, evacer).  Or, even better, you can lead a raid.  Many people on Dragon or Coldain faction need to raise faction and are willing to come to these events.

  • The Wizard Armor Quest giver is Mauren Frostbeard and is found is the shop labeled Frostbeards Furs.  If you go left from the entrance then you will eventually hit it if you follow the wall on the right.

  • The Magician Armor Quest giver is Kyla Frostbeard and is found is the shop labeled Frostbeards Furs.  If you go left from the entrance then you will eventually hit it if you follow the wall on the right.

  • The Necromancer Armor Quest giver is Lorekeeper Zorik who is found in the Temple Library.

  • The Enchanter Armor Quest giver is Lorekeeper Brita who is found in the Temple Library.

The Boots

For the boots you will need torn enchanted silk boots and three crushed jaundiced gems. The crushed jaundice gems drop off of ulthorks and bulthars in Sirens Grotto and sentients in Wakening Lands.

The Cap

For the Cap you will need a torn enchanted silk cap and three crushed flame opals. The crushed flame opals drop from ulthorks and bulthars in Siren’s Grotto, randoms deep in Velketor’s Labyrinth, and sentients in Wakening Lands.

The Gloves

For the Gloves you will need torn enchanted silk gloves and three crushed topaz. The crushed topaz drop from wyverns in Cobalt Scar, ulthorks and bulthars in Siren’s Grotto, and randoms in Velketor’s Lab and Wakening Lands.

The Pantaloons

For the Pantaloons you will need torn enchanted silk Pantaloons and three nephrite. The nephrite drop from wyverns in Cobalt Scar and rats in Dragon Necropolis.

The Robe

For the robe you will need torn enchanted silk robe and three pristine emeralds. The pristine emeralds are one of the three rarest gems in Velious. They drop off of the high level rats in Dragon Necropolis although rarely. The other place is in Siren’s Grotto off of Siren Seductresses. These are around level 56 and like to charm and mesmerize. They are enchanters which are one of the most annoying mobs because of their crowd control and area effect spells. You will want to have many stunners when taking on these.

The Sleeves

For the sleeves you will need torn enchanted silk sleeves and three flawed topaz. The flawed topaz drop off of wyverns in Cobalt Scar, rats in Dragon Necropolis, randoms deep in Velk’s Lab, and geonids in Wakening Lands.

The Wristbands

For the wristbands you will need a torn enchanted wrist band and 3 crushed onyx sapphires. The crushed onyx sapphires ulthorks and bulthars in Siren’s Grotto, mobs in the Kobold area in Velketor’s Labyrinth, and sentients in Wakening Lands.
