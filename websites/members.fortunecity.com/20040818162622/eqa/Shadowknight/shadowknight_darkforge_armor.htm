SHADOWKNIGHT DARKFORGE ARMOR

The Darkforge Armor Quests are given out by two Undead Knights. There has recently been another Undead Knight added so they split up the Quests. Be careful that you give the components to the right Undead Knight. Make sure of this by talking to them and making sure that the one you are giving the components to offers that piece of armor.

The Decaying Armor

For each of these quests, you will need pieces of Decaying Armor. The rareness is completely random. You are just as likely to get a breastplate as you are to get a bracer.  The Decaying Armor drops from Lizard Crusaders in the Temple Area of Ferrott. Get in a group on top of the temple or behind a temple and you shouldn’t have trouble getting pieces of Decaying Armor. If you are a high level, (47+) then you can also camp the Crusaders in the Avatar room where they spawn very commonly. You can also get the Ravenscale Breastplate off of the Avatar of Fear for a Rogue alternate or to sell. Beware however that these are shadowknights and they do use harm touch and other shadowknight spells such as Lifetap. The Crusaders have a pet so another easy way to find them is use your spell, Sense the Dead.  The Crusaders are level 33 and can be soloed at around level 35. This is very risky but if there are no groups you can join, this is a definite alternative. The crusaders spawn on the four corners of the first level of the Archon Temple. Their placeholders are zealots. Make sure that you are invisible until you are about to attack. Once you are ready and you made sure no other mobs are around you, send in your pet. The crusader will use his harmtouch on your pet. His harmtouch is the major attack so you don’t want it to be cast on you. Once your pet dies, which is most likely will, cast your DoTs on the crusader and then start meleeing him, using your Lifetap. If the crusader starts to run, then use Engulfing Darkness so that it does not get away. If the crusader happens to get away, get out of there. Either use gate or camp because if you don’t then you will die. It will start a very huge train. There is now a new source of getting Decaying Armor.  The Crusader Ritualists do drop it.  They level 29-32 and also spawn on a pyramid located in the maze.  They don't have any placeholders and their spawn time ranges from 1-3 hours.  Also, when you actually get to loot the Decaying Armor, there are Right and Left pieces to the armor which make it even harder to do the quests.

The Boots

For the Boots you will need the Right and Left part of the Decaying Boots, and two fairy dusts. The Fairy Dusts are dropped off of any type of fairies. Yes, this means the level 10 ones in Greater Faydark. If you want to do this quickly, then get a tracker to help you find the Fairies. Or you can just wait on orc hill and fairies wander by there very often.

 

The Bracers

For the Bracers you will need the Right and Left part of the Decaying Bracers, and the Broken Minotaur’s Lord Horn. This is dropped by the Minotaur Lord in Steamfont Minotaur Caves. The Minotaur Lord is level 28-30 and hits for around 35-60 hitpoints per attack. He is faster than Spirit of the Wolf so if you want to try and kite it, you will have to use a spell that slows him down. If you want to do this quest twice then you will have to repeat the quest once you has finished it the first time. This is because the Decaying Bracers are Lore so you can not have two of them.

 

The Breastplate

For the Breastplate you will need Decaying Mail and Decaying Breastplate, and two enchanted bars of platinum. Platinum Bars can be bought at any jewelry store for around 110 platinum depending on your charisma. Then you must get both these bars enchanted by an enchanter that is level 34 or higher.

 

The Gauntlets

For the Gauntlets you will the Right and Left part of the Decaying Gauntlets, and one enchanted bar of platinum. Platinum Bars can be bought at any jewelry store for around 110 platinum depending on your charisma. Then you must get the bar enchanted by an enchanter that is level 34 or higher.

 

The Greaves

For the Greaves you will need the Right and Left part of the Decaying Greaves, and two melatite. The Melatite drops from CX Clockworks in SolA which are around level 30.  The drop is semi-uncommon.

The Helm

For the Helm you will need a Decaying Visor and a Decaying Helm and 2 Freeport Militia Helms. The Freeport Militia Helms drop from Freeport Guards. The best one to camp is Dreiken, who spawns in East Freeport close to the West Freeport zone. This is also near the named beggar. He spawns every 10-15 minutes and drops the Militia Helm about 80% of the time. This is also a good spot because there are no other guards around and you don’t have to run around the zones killing guards hoping that you get a helm because most other guards have a lower chance of dropping the Helm. Also, this will give you a huge faction hit, but if you camp Dreiken the you will only take faction hit to the Freeport Militia about twice so it won’t completely ruin your faction.

The Vambraces

For the Vambraces you will need the Right and Left Decaying Vambraces, and a Qeynos Guard Kite Shield. The Qeynos Guard Kite Shield is what is asked, but it is really called the Qeynos Kite Shield.  The Kite Shield drops from the two Guards at the Guard tower in North Karanas.  There are two guards here.  You can either have a druid harmony them, or just wait until one of the guards runs off after some monster, and then pull the other.  The guards both conned blue to a 35.
