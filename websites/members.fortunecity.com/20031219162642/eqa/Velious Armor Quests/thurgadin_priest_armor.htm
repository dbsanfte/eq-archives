THURGADIN PRIEST ARMOR

For the Priest Armor, if you are a cleric you need Corroded Plate.  If you are a Shaman you need Corroded Chain, and if you are a Druid you will need Eroded Leather. All this is dropped in the Arena of Kael

  • The Cleric Quest Giver is called Lormaster Dorinan and is located in the library of Thurgadin.

  • The Druid Quest Giver is Kobi Frostbear who is locating in Frostbeard Furs.  Just keep following the path to the right if you are coming out of the bank, and take all rights.  You will find it.

  • The Shaman Quest Giver is Terman Underbelly.  His location in Thurgadin is unknown.

The Boots

For the boots you will need the appropriate type of armor for the boots slot and three crushed flame emeralds. The crushed flame emeralds gems drop off of randoms in Icewell Keep, ulthorks and bulthars in Siren's Grotto, randoms in Velketor's Labyrinth, and sentients in Wakening Lands.

The Helmet

For the helmet you will need the appropriate type of armor for the head slot and crushed onyx sapphires. The crushed onyx sapphires drop from randoms in Icewell Keep, ulthorks and bulthars in Sirens Grotto, randoms in Velketor's Labyrinth, and sentients in Wakening Lands.

The Gloves

For the Gloves you will need the appropriate type of armor for the hand slot and three crushed lava rubies. The crushed lava rubies drop from haunted sea chests in Cobalt Scar, randoms in Icewell Keep, ulthorks and bulthars in Sirens Grotto and randoms in Wakening Lands (geonids and sentients).

The Leggings

For the Leggings you will need the appropriate type of armor for the leg slot and three chipped onyx sapphires. The chipped onyx sapphires drop from wyverns in Cobalt Scar, rats in Dragon Necropolis, ulthorks and bulthars in Sirens Grotto, and sentients and geonids in Wakening Lands.

The Breastplate

For the breastplate you will need the appropriate armor type of the chest slot and three black marble. The black marble is one of the three rarest gems in Velious. They drop off of the high level rats in Dragon Necropolis although rarely, and mobs deep in Velketor's Labyrinth. The other place is in Siren’s Grotto off of Siren Seductresses. These are around level 56 and like to charm and mesmerize. They are enchanters which are one of the most annoying mobs because of their crowd control and area effect spells. You will want to have many stunners when taking on these.

The Sleeves

For the sleeves you will need the appropriate armor type for the arms slot and three jaundice gems. The jaundice gems drop off of wyverns in Cobalt Scar, rats in Dragon Necropolis, randoms deep in Icewell Keep, and geonids in Wakening Lands.

The Wristbands

For the wristbands you will need a torn enchanted wrist band and three crushed opals. The crushed opals drop off of ulthorks and bulthars in Siren’s Grotto, randoms in Icewell Keep, and sentients in Wakening Lands.
