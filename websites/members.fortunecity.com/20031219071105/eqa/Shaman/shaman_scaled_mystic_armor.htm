SHAMAN SCALED MYSTIC ARMOR

The two people who gives out this quest are in the Shaman Guild in Cabilis.

The Boots

For the Boots you will need Banded Boots, Fire Emerald, Spirit Caller Beads, and a Rhino Hoof. The Spirit Caller Beads are dropped off of the Spirit Called Goblins in Lake of Ill Omen. There is a goblin cave/mine here and if you go deep enough you will find the Spirit Caller Goblins. They are level 22-25 and are classified as casters. If you are high enough, and have invis than you can probably get in there and solo one. You can also try and get into a goblin group and then you shouldn’t have any trouble at all getting the Beads. The Rhino Hoofs are most frequently dropped off of Rhino’s in Overthere. These are level 33-37 and moderately magic resistant. Again, you can either get in a group which would be more efficient because you will kill more mobs. Or you can solo which is also good experience up until level 42. The Hoof is a rare drop. The Fire Emerald can be bought off of jewelers for around 80-100 platinum depending on your charisma. The banded boots can either be made with the smithing skill or bought from players for about 10 platinum.

 

The Gauntlets

For the Gauntlets you need banded gauntlets, ruby, a Venomous Parchment, and a Warlord Drawing Pen. The Venomous Parchment dropped off of a Venomous Lamprey which spawn in both Firiona Vie, around the spider area, and in Swamp of No Hope. If you hunt them in Firiona Vie, which I recommend because there is a zone nearby (Lake of Ill Omen). They are also not too hard to find here if you have a tracker whereas in Swamp of No Hope, they can be anywhere. If you are not able to take the Lamprey, but it attacks you, then do not run to the guards. They will not kill it, but only stand there and watch you die. The Parchment is an uncommon drop. The Warlord Drawing Pen is a semi-common drop off of Goblin Warlords in Lake of Ill Omen in the Goblin mines. These are mid 20s level and are quite common. If you are high enough, and have invis than you can probably get in there and solo one. You can also try and get into a goblin group and then you shouldn’t have any trouble at all getting the Pen. It should take you 30-45 minutes at max. The Ruby can be bought at any jewelry shop for about 140 platinum depending on your charisma. The banded gauntlets can either be made with the smithing skill or bought from players for about 7 platinum.

The Bracers

For the Bracers you will need one Coercion Implant, a Gooey Adhesive, a Banded Bracer, and a Ruby. The Coercion Implant drops from Coerced creatures in Crypt of Dalnir. These creatures range from level 25-32. They include Crusaders, Dwarves, Erudites, Gnomes, Goblins, Penkeeper, Tier’Dal, Revenant and Iksars, all with Coerced before their name. Next, the Gooey Adhesive drops from Gyrating Goo, Smoldering Goo, and Lumpy Goo. The Gyrating Goo are the easiest things to kill because they are just melee and don’t hit very hard. Because Dalnir is a dungeon, you probably will need a group to come along with you. The Ruby can be bought at any jewelry shop for about 140 platinum depending on your charisma. The banded bracer can either be made with the smithing skill or bought from players for about 5 platinum.

 

The Breastplate

For the Breastplate you will need a banded tunic, a star ruby, Watchman's Spectacles, and Nectar of Isolation. The Nectar is bought off of a dwarf vendor named Portik Grolam in a secret beach in Timorous Beach. Note that this is also where the named dragon for the Druid Epic, Faydedar, spawns. He is a triggered spawn so there shouldn’t be any trouble. To get to this beach, you first have to get to the inner, underwater tunnel through a cave. Then you have to go through another cave in the inner, underwater tunnel to get the abandoned inn. The location of the first tunnel is -11500, -3700 and the location of the second is -11340,-1370. The Nectar is sold for around 5-10 platinum. The Watchman’s Spectacles drop off of Raider Watchman in Timorous Deep. They are level 19-21 and have around 500-600 hit points. They have two spawn points: (5801,2583), and (5965,2934). They spawn once every 15 minutes and are place holders for a named ogre, Ugruk. The Star Ruby can be bought at an jewelry store for around 80 platinum depending on your charisma. The banded bracer can either be made with the smithing skill or bought from players for about 15 platinum.

 

The Cloak

For the cloak you will need a banded cloak, a star ruby, a Forest Giant Hammer, and a Worn Charbone. The Forest Giant Hammer is dropped off of Forest Giant’s within Warslilks Woods. There are regular giants which are level 18-22, evergreen giants which are level 23-27, greenwood giants which are level 19-23, and sapling giants which are level 15-18. All of these giants drop the Hammer as an uncommon drop. You can tell if they have it because they will be equipping a warhammer. If it doesn’t have the hammer there is no point to kill it unless you want to experience. The Worn Charbone are dropped off of Charbone Skeletons within Lake of Ill Omen. There is a skeleton house in Lake of Ill Omen where a lot of these types of skeletons spawn. These are level 22-25. The Worn Charbone is a rare drop from these. The Star Ruby can be bought at an jewelry store for around 80 platinum depending on your charisma. The banded cloak can either be made with the smithing skill or bought from players for about 10 platinum.
