BULTHARS AND ULTHORKS

The bulthars in Siren's Grotto are a pain in the ass.  They are level 51-55 and hit extremely hard and fast.  The ulthorks are also very annoying.  They are level 53-55 but don't hit quite as hard as the bulthars.  They still hit hard for their level though.  You do not want to attempt to hunt these unless you have a full group of level 50-60s which includes at least one cleric, enchanter, and warrior.  A nuker will also help.  The other alternative is plan a raid in Siren's Grotto with your guild or try and join one that another guild is running.  This zone is not a great place to get experience because it is a raid oriented zone and the main focus here is loot.  In Siren's Grotto, a great place for gem drops is at the Western Wastes zone.  To get there, simply have one 52+ druid or one 52+ wizard in every group.  Have the wizard cast Abscond and the Druid cast Egress.  This will take you from the Cobalt Scar zone to the Western Wastes zone where you can begin to hunt.
