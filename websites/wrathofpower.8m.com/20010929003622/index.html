An apology - Lyphe 11/7/99 11:45 PM
I want to apologize, I have been having everyone fill out the guild member join form to get their information, well as it turns out I collected the info for about the first 2-3 days and after that, it was too much, it was cut & pasting 30-40+ information slots a day, that is a time consuming task, 10-30 min for some days, when I could be doing other things..so in order to save everyone headaches, and to make the site MUCH more useful (not to mention kick ass) I am going to be redoing the site in ASP(Active Server Pages..just like everlore.com). Here is a list of things I plan to change:
  • Dynamic News page (The site will read all the news posts from a database, this way we can have multiple posters...as a matter of fact all officers General and above will have access to post news to the site)
  • You will be able to 'login' to the site, and once you do this you fill out your info, you will also have a members biography that you can fill out.
  • Everything on the site will be much easier to update, news, events, adding members etc.

So until this new system is in place DO NOT submit any more information to the join form, unfortunately for the people who already did submit theirs..all of you will have to re-submit it once the new site is up. This new site will have the same basic layout and design, but will display more info on each page. also we will have a couple cool new features that we don't have now. The ETA for this new site is 2-3 weeks, I will during these weeks keep posting new things as far as news and events and such to the site, but as for stories and screenshots they will not go up,you can send them and I will get them ready for  the new site...but you will not see the results for at least 2 weeks.

Thanks - Lyphe

Guild Meeting - Lyphe 10/31/99 1:25 AM
There was a guild meeting tonight in the tunnel to North Ro. This location was decided upon after some confusion and a mishap here and there. Many people were in attendance, and the basic hierarchy of the chain of command was established and guild officer positions were announced. 

Chain of Command:

  • Guild Leader - Olav
  • High Council (2nd Commanders) take control of the guild in Olav's absence.
    Caorn, Chulita, Cornisolis, Grendir, Lyphe
  • Generals - They are head of their class within the guild. They also organize events, hunting trips etc, for their respective class. Also they recruit people into the guild.
  • Lieutenant - Does some recruiting, but should mainly focus on helping members below them in the chain.
  • Sergeants.
    Feebo, Benvolio
  • Members - The general public who does not hold a position in the chain of command.
