Chain of Command

Here is the hierarchy of the guild.

The following is a description of what the different levels of leadership do.

Guild Leader
Leads the guild, starts and ends guild wars, talks to other guild leaders on our server.

High Council
Runs the guild while Olav is away, will make decisions based on in-game events, also can remove people from the guild if need be.

Class Generals
Organize their class, events (such as bazaars, dueling contests etc.), and solve any problems that may occur within their class. Also Generals appoint lieutenants and Sergeants to run their class.

Lieutenants
Interact with members, and try to help solving problems before consulting the their General, also puts the Generals order into place.

Sergeants
Sergeants recruit new members constantly, and also help carry out their Generals orders.
