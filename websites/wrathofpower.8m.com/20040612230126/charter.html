Wrath of Power Guild Charter
On a cool, misty, September morning, on the Tribunal server, the Wrath of Power was born. In the Commonlands, on the outskirts of Freeport, we quickly grew in strength and power. It was founded on the basis of helping each person in the guild realize the full potential of it’s character. We will help each other to complete our Quests and achieve our goals. We believe in Honor, Respect, Helping others, and Learning. We are open to all classes and races. Our Guildmaster, Olav, has formed a guild of many Races, that commands respect from their allies and from their enemies...fear. We are an Elite group, quick and intelligent. We will awe you with our teamwork, friendship, and skill in any battle. We strive to be the best of our respective classes. Always working to be as well versed in the ways of our race and class as possible. We are looking for players of any class and level. We plan to help support the lower level players with experience and items to try to bring them up as soon as possible and help contribute to the glory of our Guild.

Each member shall influence the way the guild grows and lives; every voice will be heard and none will be silenced. And every member has the inalienable rights to life, liberty, and protection in the pursuit of leveling. These rights shall stand as long as the guild remains a part of Norrath. Long Live the WRATH OF POWER!!!
