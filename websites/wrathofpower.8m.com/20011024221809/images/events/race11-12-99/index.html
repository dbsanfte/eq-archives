Freeport to Qeynos RACE!!!
Guild Event: Freeport to Qeynos Road Race
Sponsor: Olav
Prizes: TBD
Eligible Members: Less than level 10 ((9 and below)
Meeting place: Freeport's West Gate
Time: Friday, November 12, 1999 9 PM EST

This event is a race from freeport's west gate to qeynos's North gate. The first person to cross INTO Qeynos's north gate will be declared the winner. All higher levels are urged to collect small prizes (derv rings, etc) for the lower levels just for them attending. Officers are urged to recruit as many players below level 10 as possible to introduce them to the guild. Players below level 10 participating in the event should think their strategy carefully and plan ahead. I will be running with the leader so magical abilities of any kind are not permitted with the exception of SoW and Invisibility. Casters with these spells are urged to cast these spells upon people for donations. Donations will go to Me (Olav) and will be collected for advancing specialists in our guild (smithing, jewel crafting, etc). Pleas send me a tell if you have any questions.
