Qeynos Chapter Opening
Event: Qeynos Chapter Opening
Time: IMMEDIATELY after FreePort to Qeynos race
Eligible members: ALL

officers All officers are urged to attend our grand opening of the qeynos chapter of the WRATH OF POWER.

This will entail recruiting lieutenants of each class (possibly some generals) to run the guild in Qeynos. This will allow members to get free/low cost binds, sow's, etc from our fellow brethren. All non-officers are encouraged to come to answer questions and to stir up some excitement about our guild. Higher levels are urged to collect some prizes to give away as incentive for joining our guild and to look for some people who you think are or would be good candidates for generals and lieutenants etc.
