Announcements: Membership Drive!
Posted by Ailuvan on Mon Nov 03, 2003 9:49 am
The Honor Guard is sponsoring a membership drive!!!

Starting today, we are offering 2,000pp, a guild award which will be attached to your character's name in guild magic, and recognition on the website, for the IS member that recruits the most new members!

Rules:
1. All recruits must follow the guidelines for application... registering on this website, signing up in "Ye Olde Recruiting Board", attending at least 2 guild sponsored events, and officer approval. *must be at least level 20*

2. All recruits must mention the name of the IS member who persuaded them to submit their application.

3. All recruits must stay IS members for one month to qualify... This means, all recruits need to become members before January 1st and still be members by February 1st to count. This gives you from November 2nd until December 31st to recruit people into the guild to qualify for the awards.

4. No advertising in /shout, /ooc, /auction, etc... These should be people you group with and honestly think would make good IS members.
