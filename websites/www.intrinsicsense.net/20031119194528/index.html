Announcements: Now offering: YOURNAME@members.intrinsicsense.net
Posted by Ailuvan on Wed Sep 17, 2003 1:05 pm
Yes, the new incarnation of my mailer script works. And even better, You Control Your Own Mail Addresses!

If you go to the Your Account module, and then into Your Info, add a line to your Extra Info that looks like this:

Code:
mailto:Ailuvan


That's it, all on one line, no spaces, nothing fancy.

All mail to a addresses @members.intrinsicsense.net is passed through a filter that checks to see if someone on the board "claims" that address. So, for example, Bough might have, in his Extra Info:

Code:
mailto:Bough
mailto:Gaturz
mailto:Forje


All mail matched to a user in this manner is automatically forwarded to the Real Email Address shown in the Your Info page. Int his case, mail to any of Bough@members.intrinsicsense.net, Gaturz@members.intrinsicsense.net, or Forje@members.intrinsicsense.net, would all be sent to Bough's real email address, transparently to whoever is sending the mail.

Any questions, post below, or send me a email to ailuvan@intrinsicsense.net (or ailuvan@members.intrinsicsense.net )
