August 2, 2002

What a day! We started with Rhag Zhezum & Rhag Mozdezh this afternoon with an off chance of getting Arch Lich Rhag'Zadune on our kill list and boy did we get more than we thought we would. After the two Rhags were killed we moved on to High Priest of Ssraeshza which after the lag mob hit us for massive amounts was just obliterated. After we destroyed the HP we moved on th the AL and he was killed in just about record time and so smoothly it was like butter. With over 50 people still be on it was time for that Worm to give us some Tunics, but after clearing all the way to the worm the server crashes. On a personal note I love what you are doing VI, this getting to repeat kill mobs every day is helping some of us out. Thanks guys, keep up the steady quality work.

 

With the server coming back online and being unlocked after half an hour we still had a wild number of people on for 3am and we headed back to Ssraeshza Temple to go ahead and grab our Cursed cycle. We tore new holes in A Glyphed Covered Serpent, Vyzh'dra the Exiled and Vyzh'dra the Cursed. I think we lost maybe 3 people on all 3 mobs. Was the fastest clearing of them yet and the run is getting almost to the flawless point.

So it was 5am and people were still on and just to clear out the night we decided to go ahead and meet Xerkizh the Creator for a night cap. He wasn't much into Cappuccino with us, but he was into dropping dead and giving us some nice stuff.

This was a kick ass night VM, mobs just don't stand a chance anymore!!!!!

--Zupe Blastemded
