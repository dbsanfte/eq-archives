July 31, 2002

Now, on to our night in Luclin. We were originally going to go at that pesky Emperor Ssraeshza again, but seems a lot of people are out of town this week so we settled for Blood of Ssraeshza and he was killed flawlessly. He dropped 2 Maces, gratz to Shaekee and Oaktree, and a few spells that were not any of the rares. Oh well, it was fun and we got additional Emperor room practice. His time is coming soon, just need those people blinding others on the beeches with their pastey skin back in EQ.

We then regrouped and headed over to our friend High Priest of Ssraeshza to see what treasures he would give us. VERY smooth run and a lot of fun.

It wasn't too late so we decided to have a little fun in Umbral Plains as we have not spent enough time killing Rumblecrush or Doomshade. These are fun laid back fights and a great way to end the evening.
