Fennin Ro Dead! 2/2/2003
Kaitlynn

So we've been playing around in Fire a lot the last couple of weeks. Anything that had a cool name - we killed. Finally somebody decided "Hey, let's try Fennin Ro!" Three late nights later, he lay dead at our pixelated feet!

Of course Verant doesn't just let you waltz into the boss' room. Try it! Typing /waltz just gives you "No such command." So we had to fight to him.

And so it begins...

First we mowed through 4 of these guys. They all looked the same, and are easily dispatched. Not worth the space to show all of them. =)

With the peasants dead, the Council felt compelled to try and stop us. "They tried and failed?" They tried and died!

Fennin Ro shouts, "When you want something done right, you have to do it yourself!"

It took us a couple nights, but we stuck with it and learned another new mob. Well done all!

Some of the loot that dropped from him and his little friends.

Gardner

Xcal

Chitara

Nedden

Everyone

Southern got some new booties and Wynde also picked up some Elemental pants. I know I missed a few loots, and I apologize to those people who aren't mentioned.

Ok ok, so you want to actually SEE Fennin Ro. Well, after we got it all looted, our resident necro decided he'd have some fun with the corpse. We were glad he just meant he wanted to chase people around with it.

I'm sure Fennin's mother would be so proud he was outside in his Spiderman Underoos.

Unfinished Business 1/21/2003
Kaitlynn


Click the pic if you like corpse shots.

Quick update tonight. After spending nearly every spare minute with Rallos Zek, we hurried back to the Tower of Sol Ro to tie up some loose ends. (See "You're in MY World Now" two updates ago).

We had heard he was easy so we only gathered up a few groups and used our elite "Go Get 'Em" strategy. Well ... we had to come back hehe. But come back we did - and got him dead. Congrats to Phallax and Sensa for their new loot!

With Solusek's death opening up the final Elemental plane for us we took our first steps into the planes of Earth Wind and Fire (and water too). Our first forays left us staring in stunned amazament at the most incredible boss mob gathering ever seen.
