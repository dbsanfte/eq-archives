On the Road to Anguish 11/5/2004
Kaitlynn
 

Not a lot of Big News (tm), but in recent weeks Talisman has been working towards Anguish. No, no, not anguish over the upcoming releases of EverQuest 2, World of Warcraft, or even Halo 2 and what those releases might do to our raiding numbers. No we have been actively working towards the zone of Anguish.

This has required a multi-pronged (not pr0ned) approach in that we have to keep 70-ish people motivated enough to break into groups to work on a signet quest, and then also raid together enough to beat several raid-level trials for access.

Well, after repeatedly beating the trials for Adaption (not Adaptation curiously enough), Corruption, Endurance, Foresite, and Specialization, we did finally beat the trial for Hate. Hate took us longer than most because we were actively trying to find a way to beat it *without* gimping it. We tried it over and over and got down to the final mob "the good way," only to find out from 'sources' that we are SUPPOSED to gimp the trial. Wow... what a design. Woo.

 

Anyway, we have decided for the moment to hold off on Anguish until more of our numbers are flagged/keyed/cool enough to go. So tune in soon for our adventures in Anguish!

Mixed in among all those trials, we of course continue to beat down the Riftseeker's Rabble whenever we see them up. We also make a point of setting aside time each week to knock out the raid-ish level encounters for our folks' epics. We're so sweet. :)

That's about all I have to say about that.

So now, we turn to the Talisman Opinion Page...

Well the holiday season is fast approaching and with it comes the impending release of EverQuest 2. I won't comment on the state of the game but as a good-will gesture, let me offer SOE something to make their marketing and financial people very happy - perhaps distracting them from pushing EQ2 out the door until it's finished... /cough

What is it that every little kid likes? What is it that computer geeks and geekettes secretly adore? And further, and perhaps most importantly to The Suits, what is it in the Anime/Manga world that is all the rage?

Why, Plush Toys of course!

SOE has wholly overlooked this segment of the market. They've tried action figures of about 6 types. They've tried cell-phone EQ (WTF???), and they've done the back-pack. They even custom-made an EQ fanny pack for Beldon, but we're not sure what he paid them with, nor do we really wish to know.

But what they really need are EQ plush toys.

Talisman Takes Queen, Mates King for Win! 10/10/2004
Kaitlynn

After a few weeks hiatus from raiding following the release of Omens of War, Talisman is Back In Action!


(Click for larger pic!)

Queen Pyrilonis in Riftseeker's Sanctum had the honor of being our first 'real' raid target. We warmed up with a few other events (below), but she was The Chosen.

The first night was experimentation, and getting back into the swing of actually following directions. And considering what 'following directions' means for us, you can appreciate what we might be like when rusty hehe.

We prevailed, winning on the second night! Our efforts were well rewarded, with Sardonis getting Kira's Slippers and Glori picking up a shiny new Crystal Breastplate. Congrats!

But wait - there's more! Riftseeker's crashed the following day, which afforded us a Bonus Round! On that one, Kerial snagged a Totem of Pyrilonis, and Sanam got a Crystal Dagger!!

Following our defeat of the Queen, we took our forces into the basement where the King lay hiding.

The King's forces have a nice little trick that when if you aren't paying attention, can lead to some quality AFK time. In this pic, you can see our entire raid totally immobilized and waiting for the effect to wear off. It takes a LONG time.

Anyway, we finally did get things moving again and we were able to defeat the King in our first night of trying. Congrats to Qhue for his Crystal Silk Robe, and Majax for Kiranus' Tiara!

Goat Bitch Dead 8/26/2004
Beldon

Well, my lazy ass is finally getting around to doing a long overdue update. Back on August 18th we finally got our first kill of Pixtt Xxeric Kex (or more commonly known as "Goat Bitch"), which yet again proved to be an incredibly difficult encounter to overcome. After weeks of throwing ourselves at the slaughter, we finally pulled it off. Since then we've killed her again as well as another couple slayings of Zun'Muram Tkarish Zyk. Next on our hit list is Pixtt Riel Tavas. We are making decent progress on her and hope to have her (or is it a him?) dead within another week.

In other less exciting news, we've killed the Cragbeast Queen a couple more times and also took down Suled Dar's Shade a couple times to complete our *NEW* Veeshan's Peak keys. Of course, we haven't stepped foot in the new Veeshan's Peak yet, but... hey... we got our keys! Hooray for us!

Other than that, not much worthy of reporting. These days we usually find ourselves doing CR in Tacvi with periodic reflagging/farming in earlier zones. We're pleased to have survived our 5th straight EQ summer slump grinding through what has been the most difficult expansion we've experienced. We have our fingers crossed that the upcoming Omens of War expansion will prove to be a much smoother release throughout ALL of the content.
