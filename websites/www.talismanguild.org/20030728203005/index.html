Push the Limit!!! 7/23/2003
Beldon

This week we got off to a great start in Plane of Time. Tonight we first took down Terris Thule after several unsuccessful attempts on Monday night.

With Terris Thule dead, we moved on to Tallon Zek next. With true inspiration from Blitzberg and his valiant battlecry "PUSH THE LIMIT", we took him down flawlessly on our second attempt keeping Blitzberg up the whole fight. Had he died and we lost our battlecry, we surely would have lost! Like so many of our kills, we RARELY leave the corpse in a sexy position for a nice screenshot, so I leave you with this...

Next we are targeting Vallon Zek to complete our sweep of the Wave 4 Time Gods as we work our way towards Wave 5. The week is still young! That's all the big flashy important news for now. Now on to the smaller, less flashy, but still very important news...

Since we never have cool screenshots of corpses, here's a new mob that looks pretty cool from the Plane of Time that's still alive. It's much easier to photograph living mobs than dead mobs.

I know last update I revealed some of Talisman's top secret leet strategies and said it was for one time only. Well, luckily for everyone, I lied. After many long nights of talks with the guild, I finally got permission to post a couple more leet strats. We really take pride in helping other raiding guilds improve their tactics. If you remember from last time, we recommended using rangers to tank mobs like Vindicator. Well, we've now refined our tanking strategies to make use of other classes as tanks to free up our warriors and other melee to do other important tasks. This first shot we will demonstrate how to properly use a cleric as your main tank. Although often mistaken as a healing class, Clerics in reality make excellent tanks! Here's what you do. You put your warriors in a shielding order, very similar to the much more primitive "Complete Heal" order used in the old days. The warriors will alternate shielding the Cleric while he tanks. We find our clerics hold aggro just as well as our warriors, if not better. Simply chain casting small stuns allows the cleric to maintain beautiful aggro for the duration of any fight. This is a next generation tactic that you can employ today!

Once you master using Clerics as main tanks, which is easy if you follow the simple instructions above, you can begin to use other classes who often find themselves standing around dreaming of the day they too can tank a mob, classes like say....shamans. Therefore, we put this idea to the test and decided to tank uber mobs with a shaman instead of a warrior, ranger, or cleric. The verdict?? IT REALLY WORKS!! This tanking tactic is implemented very similar to the cleric tank tactic. Use your warriors in a shielding order, just like clerics...it IS why they got all those AA's right? Simply have the Shaman stand in front of the mob and do everything they can to piss the mob off. Soon, the mob will be dead. Sure it takes practice, but we got it to work on our first attempt!
