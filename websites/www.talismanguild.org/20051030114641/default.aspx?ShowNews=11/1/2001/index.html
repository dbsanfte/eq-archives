Alendora

We sent Southern out to start pulling the Warders to see if they'd wised up to the old portcullis trick. Nope. The Eye of Southern brought the lambs to the slaughter.

Blitzberg decided not to exploit Ventani, so he did it the old fashioned way by tanking her until she died. Blitz made an offer to us clerics that you're damn right I'm holding him to.

Psssst, Daphni, er...look over your shoulder very slowly...

DB? Nope. As if it mattered with 2k flurries.

Not like anyone got that nervous about the loot...drops were 3 PR stones, cloak/shield, brawlstick, and hammer. Those PR stones come in really handy on Wuoshi! Er...riiiiiiight. YELINAK_00 went down pretty quickly.

And finally, a wistful look at the bubble that for so long held the Sleeper. Quiet reigns in the Tomb.
