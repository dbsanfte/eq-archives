Echoes in the halls of ToV North

August 28th
 
~Alendora

In trying Vyemm, we discovered the definition of pain as we got him to no visible life and adds took us out. Which of course got us more fired up than ever to take him down. After a short strategy huddle, we tried him again. After all, you pay $30 to get this kind of ride at Disneyland.

Congrats to Nedden on his spiffy new dagger, the envy of rogues everywhere. And our lovely Erud High Priestess, Cyprusia, is now wearing Vyemm's Left Eye. As a side note, if I was going to keep a piece of a dragon as a souvenir, it wouldn't be his eye. Ick.

We then moved on to Vulak, and enjoyed our second truly fun fight of the evening.

More congrats to CHUNS! on his uber sword of mayhem and destruction, Barber on the shield that made us all say "omfg" in /gui (and then he equipped it and we all had Blackburrow flashbacks), and Lycanthe on his shiny new arms.

And finally, a wistful sight...the empty lair of Vulak 'Aerr.
   
"6 more dragons before bed"

August 25th
 
~Alendora

This is what our raid leader tells us for inspiration after the first two dragons. No stirring speeches like "FREEDOM!" or "this is our Independence Day!", not for us. After killing all the dragons in ToVN but Vyemm and Vulak on Friday and Saturday, nothing but the thought of sleep could have kept us going.

No astonishing displays of corpse artistry today, but fun nevertheless. Here are the photogenic Mirinella, Koi'Doken, Cekenar, Sevalak, Jorleagg, and Nevederia. Kreizenn and Zlexak are just ugly and this is beautiful corpse week so they don't get pics.

Some happy looters tonight were Kiladorm with a Shawl of Perception, Abatoir and his Frostwrath ("I'm the happiest paladin on the planet"), Llaen on Bracelet of the Deep Sea, Mythra with Greaves of the Deep Sea (there a trend here?); Ashana and a Dragonskin Bracer, Yikten with Silver Chains (who knows what slot he chose for that sucker), Alendora on Earring of the Icecaster, and Chuns with his Cloak of Thorns.

   
 Live fast, die young...

August 24th
 
~Alendora

Well after that lovely Wednesday patch we headed back into ToV North with the hopes that the "YOU HAVE BEEN DISCONNECTED" message would not be visiting our collective monitors again. It's so fun hitting that 'Enter World' button and hoping on your last save you were somewhere that 8 angry ToV drakes weren't.


This trip the artiste in all of us is flourishing. This is 'make a beautiful corpse' week in ToV, and we're off to a good start:

Grats to Grugg on Circlet of Summer, Charnondall our Tide guest on Katana of Pain, Wynde the Grandmaster on Hand of the Master, Shadowed on Shadowfang Necklace, Grugg and Gardner on Wurm shields, Bobertson on Ring of Superiority, Dwyer on his Boots of the Destoyer, Southern on his sexy Ancient Wurm Legs, Lycanthe on his new Pauldrons, Abby on her Bracelet of Sky, Xcal on his Mask of Venom, Necamus on his Cloak of Venom, Berserko on his Twisted Steel Bastard Sword, and Shanora and Tsai on Tskai Watcher's Monocle.

Mobs don't get to have all the fun. Here our druids, Keoki and Shanora, achieve spatial leafblower symmetry:

Here is a bizarre cross of chaos and order. Somewhere around 7 am GMT Ashana went to happy lizard zzzz land after being ressed and Southern orchestrated this lovely Ashana tableau (click on the image to view how this sublime piece of work was accomplished):

Speaking of falling asleep, we were getting ready for Aaryonar, and someone said, "cool, we have 3 dragonsbane wizards here, this will be cake." So the leader says, "pulling Aaryonar" and Cyprusia responds, "er...Bobertson AFK or asleep or something." Sure enough, there was Bob, one of the aforementioned wizards, sitting at the base of Aary's steps. Southern pulls and Aaryonar lumbers right past him. In the end, there was Bob, still sitting, inches from Aary's corpse. A few minutes later...

/cheer our conscientious and responsible wizards, who give 120% every night.


And one last thing, a little update on our epidemic. We hope that Gendal returns from his long fishing trip soon and fixes this little problem we're having...
