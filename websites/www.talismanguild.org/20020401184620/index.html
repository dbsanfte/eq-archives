Extra! Extra! Extra! 3/22/2002
Kaitlynn

The Avatar of War is DEAD!

After a pitched 15 minute battle whose outcome wasn't fully known until moments before it ended, the final Velious mob has fallen to us.

Our fearless warriors lept into battle without thought of personal safety to ensure that the Avatar paid no attention to the rest of us. VERY heroic! /swoon. Ok, ok, in reality the raid leader said "Yo, tanks! Go over there and get agro - we'll stay here until you do. And try to stay alive." The only other sound than the constant din of Complete Healing spells was the sound of the warriors getting pummeled. Ooof! Crunch! Ow! (you get the picture) And with the return of the full-duration Complete Heal, we couldn't let up!

Then the first tank fell. AoW decided that he hated those bastard rogues more than the next tank so he chewed through them like a frog in a blender. Not satiated, the AoW decided to eat a few clerics and casters. That seemed to do the trick so he returned his attention to the next tank and the battle continued. When that tank fell, he decided to pick off a few of the remaining tanks until he found one he liked. Once he did, he never got another. After a few more minutes of frenzied attacks, the Avatar of War lay dead at our feet.

When the cheering subsided to a dull roar, 2 Helmets of Rallos Zek and a Blade of Carnage were liberated from his corpse. Congrats to Ackbah for the Sword, and Blitzberg and Parthiv for the Helmets! (Sorry to Parth for no pic) =(
