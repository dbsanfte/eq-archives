It's *TIME* to Slay the Dragon (Almost)!! 8/10/2003
Beldon

Having spent the previous week in the Elemental Planes, this week was once again our turn to visit Plane of Time. Early in the week a patch was added to make Plane of Time an instanced zone. We were elated to say the least. Best of all, it actually WORKED! No more worrying about guilds leap-frogging content in Plane of Time. This was a much needed change, especially on Tunare which is the only server with 3 Time enabled guilds previously making it a total crap shoot as to what gods would be up during a given raid window. With the zone fully populated and 72 members of the team on-line, it was time to begin our instance in Time and as Ragnrok the Warrior's T-Shirt says here...It was time to slay the dragon!

Day 1 of our instance we moved thru Tier 1, 2, 3, and knocked of Tallon Zek in Tier 4 scoring a total of 19 items! The next night, we killed Saryrn, Terris Thule, and Vallon Zek putting us in Tier 5.

Friday, inspired from the previous 2 nights, we actually were able to muster up an entire raid force (unheard of for months on a Friday!) and first took down Cazic Thule for the first time, followed by our first kill of Rallos Zek! Cazic Thule awarded Kantra with a Timestone Adorned Ring, Edain with a Mask of Simplicity, and myself with a Elemental Robe Pattern. I was so shocked at receiving the robe that I completely forgot to take a screenshot of the kill, so I give you my artist's rendition instead...

Here is a slightly more professional, but far less artistic, shot of our Rallos Zek kill. Blitzberg and Rafather were awarded Visor of the Berserkers and Kaitlynn received a Shield of Strife.

We once again had an amazing turnout on Saturday and with a serious amount of effort we were able to take down Bertox (first time killed on Tunare). As has become customary, our corpse placement flat out sucks. Here are two pictures of this glorious event. The first one is a REAL picture of the corpse, or at least what you can see of it. The second picture is yet again more of my artistic interpretation of the great battle that took place. This gives a great shot for those players who may never get the chance to see Bertox in Plane of Time, now you can say you've seen him! Gratz to Jadii on Leggings of Furious Might, Heydin on Collar of Catastrophe, and Blitzberg on Veil of the Inferno. Most warriors collect weapons, Blitzberg collects masks ;-P


Well, come Sunday night, a normal solid raid night for us and our last night in Time for a week, we gave a final shot at Inny, the only Tier 5 god left standing, in hopes of getting our first glimpse at Quarm. With only an hour to kill, we made a couple nice attempts, but unfortunately have no corpse shot to show for it. We feel confident we will be paying a visit to Quarm our next week in Plane of Time.

The previous week we killed each of the 4 elemental gods again. Here's a rare GOOD shot of a corpse followed by proof that the life of a rogue is a dirty job, but someone's gotta do it!

Prior to raid time, it's often common for us to practice various strategies, many of which you've seen on these pages before. Here we perfect the famous ogre wall.

Finally, another addition in this week's patch was Solusek C. Expecting this to be the new uber zone in the game with an extreme amount of phat lewts, we thought it wise to team up with Divine Crusade to take down Culthor the Gatekeeper. We failed our first attempt, but with our combined power, we took him down for a SERVER FIRST kill on our second attempt. The loot was so outstanding, that we will now be on a weekly rotation with DC similar to Plane of Time so that both guilds can farm the zone without having to race.

That's about it for now, except of course the infamous quote section...
