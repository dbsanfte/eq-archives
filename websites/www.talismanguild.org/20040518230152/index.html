Bye bye Uqua, Hello Qvic!!! 5/10/2004
Beldon

On May 5th, Talisman became the 2nd guild across all servers to defeat Vrex Barxt Qurat, who is, for the uninformed, the end boss to Uqua which grants access to the next GoD progression zone, Qvic. Uqua proved to be an extremely challenging zone, especially with all the various changes to the content over the past couple months. We have always prided ourselves in stepping up to meet challenges and as always, Uqua was no exception.

We have now begun testing out what Qvic has to offer and are very pleased so far with this basically upgraded "GoD" style of the PoP Elemental Planes. Armor mold farming once again begins. I'd post some cool screenshots of Qvic, but I, myself, didn't get flagged due to being stuck in a dinner on Uqua night, but at least I didn't pull a Kable and miss the flag because I fell asleep on the raid. Look for another update in the very near future on our progress thru Qvic.

Medieval March Mayhem!!! 4/1/2004
Beldon

Lots of note-worthy news over the past month. First of all gratz go to Celestias for obtaining the longest name in Norrath upon winning the Defender of Norrath FFA competition. This was certainly not a solo effort. Not one Talisman member died until the very end when only Talisman were left standing. After a bloody battle among guildmates, Celestias pulled off the final kill of Ragnrok with some nicely timed disciplines. Of course no update on such an event would be complete without some Ragnrok post-game commentary...

While we're on achievements, Talisman is glad to report our first level 65 Berzerker with EPIC!!

I really wish all news could be good news, but unfortunately the world doesn't work that way. It's with sincere sadness that I must inform the public of the breakup between Tallie and Faeanor... Many years of togetherness, friendship and love...all ruined by one torid night of infidelity on the part of Tallie. Our thoughts are with you Faeanor, stay tough little camper.

Ok, wipe the tears away, here comes some more good news. Talisman has now defeated the 4th Ikkinz raid, the Antechamber of Destruction several times. We have also once again beaten the Yxxta Riddle Ring and killed all 3 Primal Creatures. I forgot to take a screenshot of the Primal Reptile corpse but I swear we killed it!

We've also managed to take down Pixtt Mindrider as well as make some initial progress in Uqua, well...at least past the zone in on many occassions.

TALISMAN MEMBER THREATENS GM AND LIVES TO TELL ABOUT IT!!
