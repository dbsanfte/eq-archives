Talisman DT's Noqufiel!!! 5/25/2004
Beldon

On March 23, Talisman continued our trend of Sloppy....er....Serverwide Seconds with our defeat of Noqufiel granting us access to Txevu, the next zone in GoD progression. Inktu'Ta was an interesting zone with creative encounters giving us a change of pace from previous pushes to end-game content. Sloppy seconds aside, we did land a serverwide first Qvic Breastplate. Gratz Tallie!!

Tonight we ventured into Txevu for the first time with a rag-tag crew of 43 to do some exploring. We were able to take down both Aneuk Nyrt Ymosa and Aneuk Rilst Uskst scoring Kaitlynn the first Qvic Paladin Breastplate serverwide and Chokidaii the first Beastlord Qvic Breastplate serverwide! I forgot to take a screenshot of one of those guys, can you guess which one?!

In other less exciting news, we finally decided to gank the Cragbeast Queen which proved to be faster, easier, and slightly more rewarding than killing a moss snake.

We look forward to reporting our progress on more unpronouncible zone names soon!! Stay Tuned!!

Bye bye Uqua, Hello Qvic!!! 5/10/2004
Beldon

On May 5th, Talisman became the 2nd guild across all servers to defeat Vrex Barxt Qurat, who is, for the uninformed, the end boss to Uqua which grants access to the next GoD progression zone, Qvic. Uqua proved to be an extremely challenging zone, especially with all the various changes to the content over the past couple months. We have always prided ourselves in stepping up to meet challenges and as always, Uqua was no exception.

We have now begun testing out what Qvic has to offer and are very pleased so far with this basically upgraded "GoD" style of the PoP Elemental Planes. Armor mold farming once again begins. I'd post some cool screenshots of Qvic, but I, myself, didn't get flagged due to being stuck in a dinner on Uqua night, but at least I didn't pull a Kable and miss the flag because I fell asleep on the raid. Look for another update in the very near future on our progress thru Qvic.
