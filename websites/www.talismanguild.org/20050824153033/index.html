Vishimtar 2.0 Finally Dies! 7/24/2005
Kaitlynn

Talisman has spent the last *several* weeks beating our collective heads against Vishy trying to kill him since his retune. I won't lie, quite a few guilds have this dragon on farm status (thankfully not on our server!) so yes we are behind this time. But the truth is, that we have been doing it wrong / the hard way for so long - but getting SO close - that it never occurred to us that there was a better way lol.

When you can consistantly get the event down to the 7% range and sometimes as close as 3%, you just figure that there was an execution error or a crucial LD (and this event seems to create a LOT of LDs, more on this event than entire raiding nights elsewhere). If we were stuck at say 40% or something, we would know that there was something else we needed to try. But < 5% ... we just need to keep at it!

So following days of repeatedly getting his health down to a sliver, we started rethinking everything we were doing. We started trying things that some people had suggested for weeks (/cough) and fleshed out the new ideas. And what do you know - it is SO much easier lol.

Congrats to everyone who stuck it out night after night for the big win. Been a long time since a win was that exciting. Also congrats to the big loot winners. Winlaan got Obsidian Pauldrons. Exquisit and Winlaan both picked up a Skull of Vishimtar. Rafather scooped out the slime-covered eyeball and was able to complete the quest for the Bladed Fang Mantle. And of course 9 lucky folks were able to finish off the DoN faction AA's. Congrats to all!

Unfortunately, our rest will be short as we still have to go after OMM now. More on that later!

 

Ithiasor the Scary Swamp Dragon

Hearing rumors of a big dragon loitering in the Swamp of No Hope, Talisman decided to investigate. What we found was indeed a big new dragon ... with bad breath.

As part of the Surgeon General's new Dental Hygiene program, we felt it was our duty to administer a good tooth cleaning to the beastie. Ithiasor though fought us at every step.

Finished, and tired from the fuss he put up, we requested payment for services. Ithiasor turned out to be a dead-beat dragon and balked. He actually tried to run off without paying! So we beat him up and took all his stuff.

Turns out he coughed up more than enough to pay for his cleaning. He had Fragmented Horn of the Clouded Dragon, Horned Drape of the Clouded Dragon, Fractured Talon of the Clouded Dragon, Desiccated Tongue of the Clouded Dragon, Elixir of Minor Learning, and an Ancient Chest.

It also turned out there was a quest involving the Ancient Chest which I won in the hopes of something unique. (See sales pitch). Unfortunately the NPC jerk had despawned so now I am lugging around an item that so far I can't turn in. :(

Evils of Norrath Conspiracy Uncovered! 6/20/2005
Kaitlynn

 

a_Bikini_Bimbo_01 begins to cast a spell.
You fall into a drooling stupor.

Once again Summer has struck a mighty blow to our raiding numbers. The days are longer. The weather is warmer, and the clothes are fewer. And that's when it hit me. It's a conspiracy to keep us from raiding!

The Gods of Norrath in conjunction with Mother Nature have been conspiring for ages. You see it every year. The boss mobs, who not-so-coincidentally have the most to gain, begin living longer, healthier lives during the summer months when the raiding population dwindles.

The conspiracy is a far-reaching one. It encompasses things like "sports" and "spouses" and "eye-candy."

Your friends tell you that you put on weight during the winter (some friends!) and suggest you might play some sports during the summer. But who are these friends really? They are minions of Norrath I tell you! Working for the evil hoardes to keep their masters alive.

And your new girlfriend / boyfriend? Do they try to lure you away from raids with such lines as these?

Boyfriend: "I'm so sorry Honey, I didn't know that you were going to be on an important raid tonight. Thinking only of myself again, I just wanted to cuddle with you on the couch and watch Thelma & Louise. I even bought Haagen-Dazs."

Girlfriend: "You have another raid tonight? Gee, I bought beer and my girlfriend and I were going to show you our new aerobics routine."

And of course, the "eye-candy." These are the worst of the evil minions. They make no effort to even HIDE the fact that they are a distraction.


So what can you do? Raid! Only by raiding can we kill the evil bosses and their minions. Show them that their foul plots are all for naught! Log in and RAID!

But ... until raid time ... Mmmmmmm Haagen-Dazs.

Tunat`Muram Cuu Vauax Dead! 4/4/2005
Kaitlynn

We can finally say that Tacvi has been cleared! Monkey off our back and all that. After a couple of attempts where we didn't get him much below 80% (if that), we finally pulled it together and realized what we were seeing. Our next attempt not only by-passed that mark, but took him all the way to -10! Congrats to everyone for sticking it out and for a big turnout for the night we beat him.

Loot consisted of Greaves of the Tunat'Muram for Cene, Greaves of the Dark Ritualist for Sardonis, Tunat'Muram's Chainmail of Pain for Alima, and a Jagged Glowing Prisim for Davyboy. Congrats!
