Butterfly Collector 3/28/2003
Kaitlynn


Talisman has added Madame Butterfly to it's trophy collection! And finally something worthy of a site update! :)

With all the shiny new loot available in the planes, we'd gotten complacent in our desire to progress. Learning takes time, and the more time spent learning an encounter means less time available for farming.


Well kudos to the officers for stepping up to make the statement, "We are going to do nothing but Xegony over and over until we get it right. You want to farm loot in other zones? Fine - Kill her and we can leave."


Amazing what a carrot will do for you. We had made a few half-hearted experimental look-sees at the encounter, but once the gauntlet was tossed down before us (and we were told that we couldn't loot it until she died), we buckled down to the task.

We spent two full nights of tactical experiments and trial and error and trial and error and ... well you get the idea. Attempt after attempt either ended IMMEDIATELY with the pull, or we progressed through the first wave only to die at the same point each time.


Finally on the second night, late into the evening, we got her to 60% - a huge leap from the 90-95% we'd been averaging. Victory was within our grasp!

Next pull - oops. Heh. Ok, the pull after that one resulted in a long encounter. Wave after wave crashed against our seasoned forces. There were no more surprises. We just wanted her dead so we could LEAVE! We hadn't gotten her this far before - did she have any more tricks up her sheer, strapless Bob Mackey?

Xegony enraged.... Nothing sneaking up on us from the bushes.... Enrage off.... Still clear.... Xegony does the PixyDive! Aaaaiiiieeeeeee!

WOOOOHOOOO!

Kantra and Southern each got a new monk punchy-thing, and Berryfyne got herself a brand new pair of panties!

Congrats to us ALL!

Fennin Ro Dead! 2/2/2003
Kaitlynn

So we've been playing around in Fire a lot the last couple of weeks. Anything that had a cool name - we killed. Finally somebody decided "Hey, let's try Fennin Ro!" Three late nights later, he lay dead at our pixelated feet!

Of course Verant doesn't just let you waltz into the boss' room. Try it! Typing /waltz just gives you "No such command." So we had to fight to him.

And so it begins...

First we mowed through 4 of these guys. They all looked the same, and are easily dispatched. Not worth the space to show all of them. =)

With the peasants dead, the Council felt compelled to try and stop us. "They tried and failed?" They tried and died!

Fennin Ro shouts, "When you want something done right, you have to do it yourself!"

It took us a couple nights, but we stuck with it and learned another new mob. Well done all!
