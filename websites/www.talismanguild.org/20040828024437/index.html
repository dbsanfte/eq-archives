Goat Bitch Dead 8/26/2004
Beldon

Well, my lazy ass is finally getting around to doing a long overdue update. Back on August 18th we finally got our first kill of Pixtt Xxeric Kex (or more commonly known as "Goat Bitch"), which yet again proved to be an incredibly difficult encounter to overcome. After weeks of throwing ourselves at the slaughter, we finally pulled it off. Since then we've killed her again as well as another couple slayings of Zun'Muram Tkarish Zyk. Next on our hit list is Pixtt Riel Tavas. We are making decent progress on her and hope to have her (or is it a him?) dead within another week.

In other less exciting news, we've killed the Cragbeast Queen a couple more times and also took down Suled Dar's Shade a couple times to complete our *NEW* Veeshan's Peak keys. Of course, we haven't stepped foot in the new Veeshan's Peak yet, but... hey... we got our keys! Hooray for us!

Other than that, not much worthy of reporting. These days we usually find ourselves doing CR in Tacvi with periodic reflagging/farming in earlier zones. We're pleased to have survived our 5th straight EQ summer slump grinding through what has been the most difficult expansion we've experienced. We have our fingers crossed that the upcoming Omens of War expansion will prove to be a much smoother release throughout ALL of the content.

High Priest Nkosi Bakari Down!! 6/29/2004
Beldon

On June 29th, we finally took a step closer to Tacvi with our victory over High Priest Nkosi Bakari in Txevu. This marks one of our most difficult obstacles to date on our GoD progression path as we have been grinding away at this event for the past couple weeks. After quite a variety of strategies, everything came together tonight with an incredible amount of teamwork. The real reason we were finally able to win is all because of Southern. He botted a cleric and a wizard and...well....yeah...that won it, or at least that's what he told me to say, and he's always right....right? Seriously though, it was a very satisfying victory for us and we look forward to moving closer to the end zone of GoD, Tacvi in the days ahead. Of course with GoD comes the never ending task of reflagging. We had to take a break from progression last week in order to get several members and applicants caught up on their flags so we pulled off victories in Uqua and Inktu'Ta. The Inktu'Ta win probably marks one of our longest single encounters in recent history. I believe the fight with Noqufiel at the end was clocked at about one and a half hours. Once completed, we had another handful or two of warm bodies to throw at Txevu.

In this day and age in Everquest, it's often difficult to dig up quality applicants to maintain a steady flow of fresh blood into the guild. Talisman, being innovative as always, has come up with alternative approaches to recruitment by advertising at gas stations in the backwoods of places such as Louisiana. Yes, this picture is real, and yes, we really are recruiting clerics. Next time you pull over to fill up with gas or pick up a Slim Jim and a Slurpy, ask yourself....Do I have what it takes to be in Talisman? There's only one way to find out! Apply! Talisman is currently recruiting quality players with great attitudes serverwide of all classes. If you're interested in raiding bleeding edge content, have high play time, and can follow directions, be sure to check out our Membership Information section in our Public Forums and see if you meet our requirements and learn a little about our application process.

That's about all the news we have to report right now. However, we have been receiving quite a few complaints on a blatant lack of a quote section as well as the new, much anticipated Ask Grunties section. Well, in this update you get BOTH.
