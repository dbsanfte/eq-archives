Talisman Goes Retro!! 11/17/2003
Beldon

In an attempt to do something a little different during our non-Time raiding nights, we decided to bring back some of the old school nostalgia and do some retro raiding. Tired of elemental farming and lacking the motivation to trudge thru a 4 hour LDoN raid, we decided to pay a visit to our old friend Vulak. The usual suspects encountered along the way proved nothing more than minor speed bumps. After waiting 30 minutes for the Vulak ring to respawn after our over zealous ranger Grunties activated it out of lack of control of his excitement, the event began. We quickly determined that the way to avoid a 4 hour LDoN raid is NOT to do the Vulak ring!! That being said, in the end, I'm pleased to announce that yes, Vulak down to Talisman!!!

The raid as you can imagine went fairly smooth. Our biggest disappointment is that Divine Crusade, still, to this day, tries to sabotage and steal our leet tactics, often sending blatant spies. Plz, I mean wtf, can't they figure out how to kill Velious content on their own??
