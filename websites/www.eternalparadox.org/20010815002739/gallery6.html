Galene took these pictures of a GM event in the Steamfonts. It started with Gnome Herald in Freeport.
Galene then went to the Steamfonts and took these pictures of what happened there. There were over
a hundred people there for the event. There is a log of the event posted in the Library.
A remarkable new gnome invention was unveiled in the Steamfonts by Asmoranomar. It took the form
of a huge clockwork spider called the Mechlotyranacon.
Then a huge gnome necromancer named Zivirim Vayshul appeared and shouted something about
the Dark Reflection being ignored by the good races of Norrath. Things got a bit confused after that
as the huge spider started attacking people.
Both the gnome necromancer and the huge spider were killed in the end but not with out loss of life.
It seems that several gnome engineers were killed as well. For the whole story, visit the library.
