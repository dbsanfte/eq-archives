(Freeport) - Hail and well met fellow citizens of Norrath; I am Dr. Winguren your guide to scientific exploration here in our little realm. Well, most of it at least, I don't like Dark Elves. Hmmph, well that is another story all together. Let me proceed with today's topic, the phenomenon of the last bubble of experience.

From many people's e-mails and personal experience, it became quite clear that something was up with that darn last bubble. There just seems to be about three times the experience in that bubble than the other four. So we here at Norrath Scientific rolled up our sleeves and set out to uncover the mysteries of this pesky little critter.

We at first tried to observe the progress of experience in the last bubble with the normal human eye, but where unable to notice anything unusual except for the tremendously LONG amount of time it took for the last bubble to be filled.

As we progressed through the last two bubbles we kept detailed accounts of what was slain and how much experience was gained. In figure 1.a, you can see a comparison of the last two bubbles. Fortunately, on the last kill of the experiment, our test subject, Binko de Blanca, a level 30 Dwarf Cleric, was able to fell Lady Vox with one swoop and gain a decent amount of experience.

Fig 1.a Comparison of the last two bubbles.


With this information collected and analyzed, we determined this definitely called for further investigation. I contacted an old friend from the land of the little people, a gnome wizard by the name of Diskod Poisonwand. I explained the problem we were investigating and asked for any ideas. Well, the little tinkering midget ...er height impaired, fellow came up with a brand new and unique invention that may revolutionize the scientific world.

He explained to me that an aspiring astronomer Gnome had once given him the quest to find a lens from a very mean Evil Eye so that she could complete her new invention called a "telescope", a device that allowed to people to view objects from a great distance. Well, he was lucky enough to acquire another lens from the foul beast. He then flipped the lens over and placed into a rolled up pie tin.

His new invention allows anyone to look at any small object with great magnification!! We named this new device the "Look at Real Real Small Things and Make Them Bigger Device".

With this new contraption, we were able to delve deeper into the mysterious last bubble. What we discovered almost shocked my banded boxers right off my body. We believe we have discovered a force, which we have named the Anti-Levelism Force, that actually pushes against your experience. This force is always present, but becomes greatly apparent near the end of the level where the amount of empty space has shrunk to its smallest amount.

Fig 2.a The main culprit of all those bad things that happen in the last bubble.


Ok, if I am talking over your heads now Trolls, I am sorry but you should at least learn how to read. So, as I was saying, this force seems to be the problem with eclipsing the last bubble with that odd shade of orange.

The mystery still remains as to exactly what this force is and how it can be stopped. Some of the researchers here believe the force is part of a much larger entity of evil that plagues us all. Some believe it is directly related to problems of the first bubble. Namely, the high probability that you will die many a painful death in the first bubble so that you can be sent back to be punished by the last bubble of the previous level.

We may never know exactly, but you can count that the crack team of scientists and researchers here at Norrath Scientific will not rest until the mystery is fully solved.
