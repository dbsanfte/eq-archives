News

02-DEC-03:

     

Well tonight seemed to be "Get Sumatri her Epic night".    We got Sumatri MUCH closer to completing her epic by taking Venril Sathir  for a little ride.  We then headed to the Swamp of No Hope, in search of Ulump Pujluk and ask him if he would be so kind as to hand over the "Froglok Essence" that Sumatri needed.  Well, he wasn't in an obliging mood so,... he died.  We then decided that since we had come this far we might as well head over to Kedge Keep and see what mayhem we could create over there.  After making our way to the Seahorse cave, we decided we would pay a little visit to Phinigel Autropos.  We killed him in short order as well, however he didn't drop anything of notable worth.  It was around this time that Yienka gave out a resounding "Doh!" over guild chat, letting us know that she was no longer in route to Kedge Keep and had instead received a free trip to her bind point.  After this the majority of the "Raiding Party" broke up.  Myself, Sumatri, Robotion (after kindly rezzing Yienka), and 2 members from the original force went back to the Seahorse cave in hopes of getting the second to last piece of Sumatri's epic.  Unfortunately we were unsuccessful as that wily little Tainted Seahorse never came out to play with us.  But after discovering a nice little path to get back there, we predict we will be heading there often to sniff out the little fella!  So stay tuned for more death and carnage...same bat-channel, same bat-time!

- -Fystz O'Malley
