Currently, there are six principle sections to this website.

I. General Section- Includes this Site Introduction, an in-depth analysis of what we know All About Stats, an article about Speed Tricks that shows how to drink potions or use clickable items with greater ease and speed, and another in-depth article about What Potions Do to help all, shaman and non-shaman alike, understand which alchemy potions will benefit them, and which may not.

II. The Cauldron Message Board - A place where shamans and others may ask questions or seek answers from other alchemists.

III. Potion Resource Center - All the nitty-gritty details about alchemy from where to buy herbs and how much they'll cost, which herbs to combine and in what fashion to create a given potion, a 95%+ accurate trivial chart and other helpful alchemy charts.

IV. Spell Resource Center - All shaman spells from level one to level sixty are displayed with details about casting times, effects, durations, disciplines, vendor and mob drop locations and commentaries as well as trade-in quests.

V. Armor Resource Center - Everything you wanted to know about shaman armor from cloth to Wolf Caller's is here in the Armor Resource Center.

VI. Quests - Currently, here you'll find detailed instructions to complete the Cannibalize II Quest and the epic shaman Spear of Fate Quest.
