Wolf Caller's Armor *

Slightly less Armor Class than Spirit Caller's, but has the highest combined Wisdom, Hit Point and Mana buffs with a nice smattering of resistances that add up admirably if you have a full set. In order to do the Wolf Caller's Armor Quests in Skyshrine, you will need to raise your faction with Claws of Veeshan to ALLIED status and possibly your faction with Lord Yelinak to NON-KOS status.
