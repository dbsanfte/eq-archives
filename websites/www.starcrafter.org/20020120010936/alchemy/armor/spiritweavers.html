Spirit Weaver's Armor *

Nearly identical to Rune Crafter's Armor. Although you need no particular faction to obtain Spirit Weaver's Armor, inasmuch as this fine shaman armor only drops on the Plane of Growth from various mobs, you will completely hose multiple Tunare factions, making you "Kill On Sight" to the high-elven city of Felwithe in Greater Faydark, and possibly to other elven NPCs in the game as well.
