As suspected, the new smithing changes are incredibly voluminous and more than I wish to undertake for this website. However, there are already some good sources available for those wishing to learn more about either the alchemy aspects, or the smithing aspects of this greatly enhanced trade skill.

Smithing Resources:

EQTraders Smithing Recipes - Perhaps the best resource for blacksmiths on the net! See their Dye recipes here.

Casters' Realm on Smithing - Excellent source of everything about the new smithing changes:

KC's Blacksmithing Guide - Still provides some great tips on how to start and raise your smithing skill whether you have money to burn, or if you're on a limited budget.

UngaBunga's Guide to Smiffin for Dark Races - As the name implies, special consideration is given to evil races and the sometimes difficult task of finding components when you're KOS to half the world.
