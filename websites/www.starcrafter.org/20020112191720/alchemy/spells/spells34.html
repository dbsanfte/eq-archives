NAME/SPEED MANA DURATION DISCIPLINE COMMENTS
Charm Animal
/ 5.0 sec		 120 Varies Alteration Allows you to charm a non-magical animal of approximately level 33 or lower. Charmed animal will respond to typical pet commands, but may turn and attack you when the charm wears off.
Companion Spirit
/ 10.0 sec		 350 Until you zone (pre-Luclin) Conjuration Congratulations! You've gotten your first wolf pet! This friendly fellow will be around level 23 to 27 and will taunt, bash, kick and double attack for 20 to 30 pts damage per hit. Also, when on "/pet guard here", he will turn and point towards the nearest enemy at a fair range.

PET COMMANDS (and comments):

/pet follow me (pet will follow you like a faithful little puppy, unless it gets curious, wanders off and trains mobs on you)

/pet sit down (pet will rest and regen faster, but will not follow you until told to as above)

/pet guard here (pet will stay in one spot and return there after fights. He'll also "point" toward the nearest mob at a fairly good range.)

/pet attack (pet will attack your target until the target dies, the pet dies, or you issue a different command)

/pet back off (pet will cease fighting and default to it's most recent, previous command)

/pet get lost (pet will destroy itself)

/pet notaunt (pet will stop taunting targets ... note, this is a toggle command that can keep your pet alive longer)
Enstill

/ 2.5 sec		 65 0 to 96 sec Alteration Stops your opponent from moving. Attacking an enstilled opponent increases the chance they'll break the enstillment and be able to move again. Generally stronger than Root and good for "parking" excess mobs when an enchanter is not available.
