Shaman Armor Introduction

Non-faction-sensitive Armor and Quests -

Cloth Armor - Found on low-level mobs in most newbie zones.

Patchwork Armor - Better than cloth, created by a low-skill tailor from ruined pelts.

Banded Armor - Better than patchwork, created by a Master Blacksmith.

Cultural Armor - Better than banded, created by a very talented Master Blacksmith who is compatable with your race and diety.

Totemic Armor - The first major, non-Iksar shaman armor upgrade after banded (other than certain cultural chain and split-mails). Here's all the information you'll need to turn your shaman into a lean, green, fighting machine. NON-IKSAR

Scaled Mystic Armor - The first major, Iksar-only armor upgrade after banded (other than certain cultural chain and split-mails). Here's most of the information you'll need to turn your leezard into a death-dealing Godzilla. IKSAR-ONLY

Rune Etched Armor - This non-Iksar armor is superior to Totemic, but inferior to Spirit Caller's, Spirit Weaver's, Wolf Caller's and Rune Crafter's. NON-IKSAR

Scaled Heirophant's Armor - This Iksar-only armor is superior to Scaled Mystic, but inferior to Spirit Caller's, Spirit Weaver's, Wolf Caller's and Rune Crafter's. IKSAR-ONLY

Jaundiced Bone Armor - High Armor Class, non-Iksar shaman armor that incorporates right-clickable effects from Old Sebilis and Karnor's Castle..

Faction-sensitive Quests - * Velious Required

* Arctic Wyvern Armor - Super-lightweight, low Armor Class, moderately high Wisdom studded leather armor. Created via extremely-high tailoring and moderately-high brewing and blacksmithing skills, you may need to raise your faction with the Coldain to KINDLY or better status.

* Rune Crafter's - Nearly identical to Spirit Weaver's Armor. In order to do the Rune Crafter's Armor Quests in Thurgadin, you may need to raise your faction with the Coldain to KINDLY or better status.

* Spirit Weaver's - Nearly identical to Rune Crafter's Armor. Although you need no particular faction to obtain Spirit Weaver's Armor, inasmuch as this fine shaman armor only drops on the Plane of Growth from various mobs, you will completely hose multiple Tunare factions, making you "Kill On Sight" to the high-elven city of Felwithe in Greater Faydark, and possibly to other elven NPCs in the game as well.

* Spirit Caller's - The highest Armor Class available to shamans, bar none. In order to do the Spirit Caller's Armor Quests in Kael Drakkel, you will need to raise your faction with the Kromzek to ALLIED status and possibly your faction with King Tormax to NON-KOS status.

* Wolf Caller's - Slightly less Armor Class than Spirit Caller's, but has the highest combined Wisdom, Hit Point and Mana buffs with a nice smattering of resistances that add up admirably if you have a full set. In order to do the Wolf Caller's Armor Quests in Skyshrine, you will need to raise your faction with Claws of Veeshan to ALLIED status and possibly your faction with Lord Yelinak to NON-KOS status.
