Quest Instructions:

Two important notes to remember:

1) You'll need to trade-in your medium-sized banded armor for each similar piece of totemic armor. DO NOT turn in small or large sized banded or you'll lose all your turn-ins and get nothing in return.

2) You'll need to trade-in one Dufrenite gem (about 160 pp to 180 pp to purchase at various vendors all over Norrath, but the closest place is the Barbarian Fishing Village along the river at the extreme west end of Western Karanas), however, YOU MUST be sure the Dufrenite is in the proper form (unaltered, crushed, powdered or ground) for the given Totemic piece you are obtaining.

In order to alter a Dufrenite into powdered, crushed or ground forms, visit the Ogre Camp in Lake Rathetear nearest to the Rathe Mountains zone. You'll find several ogres there, and three of them will either powder, crush or grind your Dufrenite for you. Simply hail each one until one responds with the particular dufrenite altering capability you are looking for, hand the Dufrenite to that Ogre, and he or she will instantly return the Dufrenite to you in it's altered (powdered, crushed or ground) form.

Where the Totemic Quest Begins and Ends:

Proceed to Lake Rathetear and, using SoW and Levitate, run to the barbarian camp at positive 240, positive 2200. There you'll find several barbarians hanging around. The two you are looking for will be standing near a shrine and go by the names of Kyralynn and Vrynn.

Kyralynn

When given the proper components, Kyralynn will fashion Totemic Bracers (wrists), Breastplate (chest), Helm (head) and Cloak (back).

Vrynn

When given the proper components, Vrynn will fashion Totemic Boots (feet), Gauntlets (hands), Vambraces (arms) and Greaves (legs).

The Components You'll Need, Piece by Piece:

Boots - 1 Medium Banded Boots, 1 Dufrenite (unaltered), 1 Terror Spines (from Tentacle Terror in Najena dungeon in Lavastorm), and 1 Orc Chief Tongue (from the Orc Chieftan in Lesser Faydark). Turn-in to Vrynn.

Bracer - 1 Medium Banded Bracer, 1 Dufrenite (unaltered), 1 Bull Elephant Tooth (from Bull Elephants in South Karana) and 1 Cauldron Shell (from Aqua Goblin Tidal Lords in Dagnor's Cauldron). Turn-in to Kyralynn.

Breastplate (or Mail) - 1 Medium Banded Mail, 1 CRUSHED Dufrenite, 1 Grizzlenot Bark (from Gizzlenot, an extremely rare named-treant in South Karana), and 1 Lancer Fish Fin (from Lancer Fish easily obtained near the zone inside Kedge Keep). Turn-in to Kyralynn.

Cloak - 1 Medium Banded Cloak, 1 POWDERED Dufrenite, 1 Driftwood Pipe (from the Goblin Chief in Ocean of Tears), and 1 Basalt Drake Scales (from Basalt Drakes in Rathe Mountains). Turn-in to Kyralynn.

Gauntlets - 1 Medium Banded Gauntlets, 1 CRUSHED Dufrenite, 1 Mammoth Ribs (from Wooly Mammoths in Everfrost Peaks), and 1 Griffene Downfeather (from Griffenes in North Karanas). Turn-in to Vrynn.

Greaves - 1 Medium Banded Legs, 1 POWDERED Dufrenite, 1 Permafrost Snowball (from Goblin Wizards in Permafrost dungeon in Everfrost Peaks), and 1 Bone Barbs (from Barbed Bone Skeletons in Unrest dungeon in Dagnor's Cauldron). Turn-in to Vrynn.

Helm - 1 Medium Banded Helm, 1 Dufrenite (unaltered), 1 Glacier Bear Pelt (from the Glacier Bear through the underwater cave near the fork in the river in Everfrost Peaks), and 1 Ogreskin Chamois (from Lizard Pages in Cazic Thule dungeon in the Ferrott). Turn-in to Kyralynn.

Vambraces - 1 Medium Banded Sleeve, 1 GROUND Dufrenite, 1 Ghoul Carrion (from Ghouls in Unrest dungeon in Dagnor's Cauldron), and 1 Charger Hoof Chip (from Centaur Chargers in South Karana). Turn-in to Vrynn.
