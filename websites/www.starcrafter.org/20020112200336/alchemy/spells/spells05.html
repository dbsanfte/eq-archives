Level Five Spells

NAME/SPEED MANA DURATION DISCIPLINE COMMENTS
Cure Poison
/ 2.0 sec		 20 Instant Alteration Will cure weak poisons on you or your party members. Stronger poisons may require casting more than once.
Drowsy
/ 2.5 sec		 20 ~ 1 min Alteration Slows your opponent's attack speed. An excellent pull-spell when soloing. Get used to it now, slowing mobs for your group is one of a shaman's prime responsibilities.
Endure Fire
/ 2.5 sec		 20 ~ 28 min Abjuration + 15 Resistance to Fire Based Attacks.
Feet Like Cat
/ 5.0 sec		 40 ~ 35 min Alteration +10 Agility. Increases your Armor Class, running speed and effectiveness of Dodge and other defensive skills.
Fleeting Fury
/ 0.5 sec		 10 18 sec Abjuration + 15 Strength, + 20 Dexterity, + 6 Armor Class. Cast on yourself if solo-meleeing, or on main tank if grouped. Fast casting, short duration melee buff.
Frost Rift
/ 1.75 sec		 15 Instant Evocation Cold-Based Direct Damage { DD= 13 plus 1 per level ... 15 max at level 7 }. First in the line of frost attack spells.
Gate
/ 5.0 sec		 70 Instant Alteration Teleports you to your bind point. Long casting time and heavy mana cost makes it an ineffective escape spell, but you might get lucky. ;)
Scale Skin
/ 2.5 sec		 25 ~ 20-25 min Abjuration + 6 Armor Class Buff. Requires you to have a "snake skin" in your inventory to cast, easily obtained in most newbie zones.
Sicken
/ 1.75 sec		 30 ~ 2 min Conjuration Disease-Based Damage over Time { DoT= 50 ... (8 + 2 per tick for 21 ticks) }. DoTs are best cast early in a fight for maximum effect, but can also agro the mob on the shaman as the damage adds up. Use with discretion and be sure your tanks are taunting.
Spirit Pouch
/ 5.0 sec		 40 Until 30 min after you camp or go link dead. Conjuration Summons a four-slot bag that renders any contents placed within weightless. CAUTION: The bag and all its contents will vanish 30 minutes after you camp or go link dead.
Summon Drink
/ 4.0 sec		 10 Until 30 min after you camp or go link dead. Conjuration Summons a Flask of Water. Like all summoned items, the water will vanish 30 minutes after you camp or go link dead. NOTE: This summoned water cannot be used in ANY trade skill recipes.
