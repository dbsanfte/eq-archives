Level Nine Spells

Cure Blindness
/ 2.0 sec		 20 Instant Alteration As the name implies, this spell cures blindness on you or a party member. Good one to have hot-keyed (when fighting mobs or players that may blind you) since you can't see to cast if you're blinded (blank monitor screen).
Endure Disease
/ 2.5 sec		 20 ~ 28 min Abjuration + 15 Resistance to Disease-Based Attacks.
Light Healing
/ 2.0 sec		 20 ~ 28 min Abjuration + 25 Hit Points of Healing plus 1 per 2 levels ... (28 HP max at level 15) Upgrade to Minor Healing.
Sense Animals
/ 2.0 sec		 5 Instant Divination Points you toward the nearest animal. Handy for pinpointing a specific mob, like the Glacier Bear in Everfrost Peaks.
Serpent Sight
/ 5.0 sec		 30 ~ 28 min Divination Grants Infravision. Handy for those races that are night blind such as Barbarians, Humans and Erudites.
Spirit of the Bear
/ 5.0 sec		 40 ~ 28 min Abjuration + 12 Stamina Buff (plus 1 per 2 levels ... max + 15 at level 15)
Spirit of the Wolf
/ 4.5 sec		 40 ~ 28 min Alteration Increases running speed significantly. A very popular spell and a good way to make some cash at this level by "SoWing" players for donations.
Spirit Sight
/ 2.0 sec		 20 15 min Divination Grants the ability to see invisible players.
Summon Food
/ 4.0 sec		 10 Until 30 min after you camp or go link dead. Conjuration Summons a Loaf of Bread (Meal). Like all summoned items, the bread will vanish 30 minutes after you camp or go link dead. NOTE: This summoned bread cannot be used in ANY trade skill recipes.

/ 2.1 sec		 40 42 sec Conjuration Poison-Based Damage over Time { DoT= 66 ... (10 + 8 per tick for 7 ticks) }. Stack this DoT with Sicken and watch your opponent's health drop like a stone.
