Patchwork (Tattered) Armor

Patchwork, or tattered, armor is wearable by most non-caster classes.

It is better than cloth armor, and can be fashioned by a Tailor of low skill utilizing vendor-purchased patterns and ruined wolf, cat and bear pelts.

For more information about Patchwork Armor, visit EQTraders.com
