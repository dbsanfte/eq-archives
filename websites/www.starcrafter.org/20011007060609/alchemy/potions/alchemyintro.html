Welcome to the Potion/Alchemy Resource Center

Alchemy is a shaman-only skill in the game, Everquest(tm), that allows the shaman character of 25 seasons or more to combine various herbs in a medicine bag to produce potions which players may drink to enhance certain stats and abilities in game.

To make a potion, a shaman of sufficient level and skill places the appropriate herbs to make one potion at a time in a medicine bag and hits "Combine".

The shaman can then take 5 or 10 identical, completed potions, place them in a Bearskin Potion Bag, and combine them into five and ten charge multi-dose potions.

To drink a potion, a player places the potion in one of their 8 general inventory slots and uses their mouse cursor to right click on it. The effects of the potion last for varying lengths of time from instantaneous to over a half hour, depending upon the potion consumed.

Once a potion's last dose is imbibed, it vanishes.
