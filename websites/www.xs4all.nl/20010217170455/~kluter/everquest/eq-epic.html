Epic Quest Weapon Screenshots


When the new Epic Quest weapons where anounced to enter the world, I prayed to my 'god' to show me some of the weapons. Well suffice to say my prayers where heard ;-)
The following screenshot I put under the name of the class I think they belong to, but of course the only thing Tunare showed me where the weapons, she didn't want to tell me anything about them.

Epic Weapon for the Warrior, 2 Hander?
Huge sword with a blue and red burning effect running from the hilt to the top, the two colors rotate around the blade.

Epic Weapon Warrior, 1 Hander 1?
Epic Weapon Warrior, 1 Hander 2?
The warrior 2 hander is rumored to have a right click effect of splitting into 2 1 handed weapons, identical weapons, one with the red particle effect, the other with the blue particle effect. Particle effect is similar to the 2 hander particle effect, only missing the rotating around the blade part, which is 'obvious'.

Epic Weapon for the Shadow Knight?
Big sword with a dark pulsating aura, 3 skulls spawn from the hilt and float around.

Epic Weapon for the Ranger?
Nice sword with lightning running along the blade.

Epic Weapon for the Paladin?
Huge 'sword', blade not visible, just flames. Could this be another Paladin weapon? Maybe a Warrior weapon.

Fiery Avenger (Paladin weapon)
The first of the Epic Quest items in the game.

Epic Weapon for the Shaman?
Spear with feather just under the tip, these feathers move when the spear moves. Nice 'aura' effect around the feathers.

Epic Weapon for the Rogue ! (confirmed)
Rogue dagger, with a poison dripping from the top of the blade to the bottom.

Epic Weapon for the Monk? 1
Some kind of floating super shurikin like weapon.

Epic Weapon for the Monk? 2
Looks like a hand held Flame.

Epic Weapon for the Bard?
Cool blade with music notes floating around it.

Epic Weapon for the Druid?
Nice scimitar with leaves floating around it.

Epic Weapon for the Cleric?
This morningstar is moving on its chain, a blue sparkling effect is around the ball.

Epic Weapon for the Wizard?
Nice mace-like staff, has a burning fire at the tip, enclosed in a force bubble. The bubble seems to get a feel from inside the staff.

Epic Weapon for the Magician?
This staff looks like it converts its raw power into 4 different types of magical energy, one on each side of the staff, each a different color. The 4 points leave a trail when the staff is moved. Although this item may seem childish at first, it looks very impressive when examined up close.

Epic Weapon for the Necromancer?
This Scythe is some kind of skeletal beak which opens and closes, very nice sparkly effect around the 'head'.

Epic Weapon for the Enchanter?
This is a cobra on a stick, but the cobra is not static, it moves its head and opens and closes its mouth.

Epic Weapon 3? (Probably GM weapon)
This blade has a nice sparkle effect running from the top of the blade to the bottom and back up, had been seen in game wielded by Game Masters.
