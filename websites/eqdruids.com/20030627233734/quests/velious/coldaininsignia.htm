Copper Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain Glacierbane and he will tell you that he's very cold and would like a warm blanket. You will need to combine the following items in the large sewing kit to create the blanket.

2 High Quality Tundra Kodiak Hide and 2 High Quality Snow Cougar Pelts all combined together

 

Silver Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain Glacierbane after you have completed the Copper Coldain Insignia Ring quest, and he will tell you that he would like someone to take his axe to his nephew Boridain, and bring back proof that he is ok.

 

Go to [-2450, 1550] and hand Boridain the axe. Boridain will then try to locate his Rabid Kodiak. He will wander around and will agro a lot of different mobs, and you must keep him alive.

 

Eventually he will locate his Rabid Kodiak. Kill it, loot the rabid pelt, and give it to Boridain. You have to do this quickly as he will despawn shortly after the kodiak is dead.

 

Once you hand him the pelt he will hand you back a Broken Axe. Run back to Garadain and hand him the broken axe and your copper coldain insignia ring and you'll be rewarded with the silver coldain insignia ring.

 

Gold Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain once more and you will find out that he wants you to make him a knife.

 

To create the knife you will need a Flawless Walrus Hide, a Wooly Rhino Tusk, and a Skinning Rock.

 

 Once you have obtained all three items, combine them in a forge to create the knife.

 

Now hand Garadain the knife and your silver ring and you will be rewarded with the Gold Coldain Insignia Ring.

 

Platinum Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain once again, and he will tell you that he is in need of a hammer so that he can smith his new knife.

 

He hands you a note to give to Tain Hammerfrost.

 

Head straight out from the dragon bone bridge in Eastern Wastes and you will soon come upon Tain, a dwarven cleric. Hail him and he'll tell you that he was just ambushed by Frost Giants and that he needs help.

 

Say "I will help you", and he will sit down and tell you that they will be here soon, he then tells you to kill Grhek and obtain his vial.

 

Five frost giants spawn nearby and run up to kill Tain. Defeat them all and you will be able to loot a Blue Vial off the named giant.

 

Hand the dwarf the blue vial and he will reward you with the Coldain Smithing Hammer. Now return back to Garadain and hand him the smithing hammer with your golden ring and he will reward you with the Platinum Coldain Insignia Ring.

 

 

Obsidian Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain once more and he will ask you to go intercept a Ry’Gorr Messenger who is carrying some sort of invasion plans. Garadain will then ask you to hand these plans to the Coldain Lookout in his camp. He also mentions that all of the invasion force must be stopped and their leader, an orc named Scarbrow, must be killed.

When you hand the Coldain Lookout the invasion plans, Scarbrow spawns with 2-3 Ry’Gorr Invaders, and 2-3 Ry’Gorr Oracles. The only problem here is that you must engage Scarbrow before the dwarves get agro otherwise they will probably steal the kill and make the body vanish.

 

Once Scarbrow is killed, you can loot his head and a frozen orb. Return to Garadain and hand in your platinum ring, as well as Scarbrow's Head and you will receive the Obsidian Coldain Insignia Ring.

 

Mithril Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain once again and he tells you that he has to find out what happened to the coldain invasion force that tried to attack the Kromrif a while back. Garadain tells you to return with proof of their deaths, and proof of the deaths of their killers.

The first step is to locate Korrigan, the undead coldain, who spawns at night in the dwarven hut near Icefang. Talking to him will reveal that the Ry’Gorr destroyed the coldain forces. While you are talking with the undead dwarf, Icefang will respawn as a large version, and will eventually walk over to the orc huts near the bridge. When he gets there, 4 oracles or Ry’Gorr will spawn along with Poxbreath Yellowfang, a named Ry’Gorr orc. Kill Yellowfang and you will be able to loot the Note from Kromrif.

Now go to a spot near the Ry'Gorr Keep, close to the entrance of Crystal Caverns. If you stay here a Ry'Gorr Emissary will spawn. Wait another couple of minutes, and then Rodrick will spawn next to him. He is the traitor and you must kill him to obtain his head.

Return to Garadain and hand him the Note, the Head and your Obsidian Ring and you will be rewarded with the Mithril Coldain Insignia Ring.


Adamantium Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Head over to the Kromrif Fortress and kill Warden Bruke to loot the Shackle Key. Hand both the shackle key and your mithril insignia ring to Corbin Blackwell (named mob at the giant fortess).

 

 He will be able to escape and hands you back the ring. You now must protect Corbin and keep him alive until he reaches Dobbin Crossaxe.

 

Along the way four Ry'Gorr and four giants including Commander Bahreck will attack. Kill these enemies all the while making sure no harm comes to Corbin.

 

Once he reaches Dobbin, he will thank you for rescuing him and then despawn. Dobbin will tell you that he will give you a note for Garadain if you show him your mithril insignia ring.

 

Hand Dobbin the ring again and you will receive a note in return. Now run back to Garadain and hand him the note, he will in turn reward you with the Adamantium Coldain Insignia Ring.

 

Velium Coldain Insignia Ring

 

Talk to Garadain once again, and he will give you the last quest in the series. He will tell you that there is a war going on against Chief Ry'Gorr and his troops. He asks you to hand him the Adamantite Coldain Insignia Ring and in return will hand you back the Marching Orders.

 

Run over and hand the orders to Gloradin who can be found in the nearby hut. Once you hand him the note 30 to 35 Ry'Gorr Orcs spawn and begin the assault.

 

You must kill the Chief Ry'Gorr and loot his head. You must also keep Garadain alive; otherwise the quest will be over.

 

Once you have the head, hand it to Garadain and you will receive the Velium Coldain Insignia Ring.
