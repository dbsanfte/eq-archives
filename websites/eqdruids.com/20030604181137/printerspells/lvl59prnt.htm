Level Fifty Nine Spells

Level 59 spells are available either off the Druid Spell vendor in Firona Vie, or are dropped off various mobs. 

Legacy of Thorn: 

Effect:  Damage Shield by 28 (L59)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 60 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 350
Spell Duration: 6 seconds (L1) to 360 seconds (L60)
Duration Formula: 7
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by a thorny barrier.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a thorny barrier.
 Spell fades: The brambles fall away.

Gives everyone in your group a 28 point damage shield in one cast.  Can't beat that!

 

Dropped from NPCs

 

Reported to be dropped in Old Sebilis off the Juggernauts and by Royals in Chardok.

 

Spirit of Oak:  

Effect:  Illusion: Tree
Effect:  Root
Effect:  Regeneration for between 4320 (L59) to 4680 (L60) Hit Points (HP) in 360 ticks
Effect:  Increase Mana by 4 (L59)

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 25
Spell Duration: 360 ticks (36.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
 Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You have taken root.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> turns into a tree.
Spell fades: You are no longer a tree.

Upgrade to treeform.  This spell will increase your healing rate, and also increases your mana regen.  

 

Includes:

 

Regen 4 mana/tick

Regen 12-13 hps/tick

 

You will not be able to move, but can still cast, attack trade etc. 

 

Dropped from NPCs

 

Reported drops in Old Sebilis off the Juggernauts, the Royals in Chardok and Guardian Wurms in Skyfire

 

Wildfire:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 1024 (L59)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Fire

Mana Required: 320
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 6.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 3.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your skin ignites as wildfire courses over your body.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> skin ignites as wildfire courses over them.

Upgraded Direct Damage Spell.  This spell does 1024 damage per cast!

 

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

2: Increase Total Hit Points from 467 (L59) to 470 (L60)
3: Increase Current Hitpoints from 467 (L59) to 470 (L60)
4: Regeneration for 6000 Mana in 1000 ticks

Classes: Dru (L59)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: All
Beneficial: Beneficial

Range to Target: 100 feet
 Area Effect Range: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 500
Spell Duration: 1000 ticks (100.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 8.0 seconds

Spell cast on someone: An orc centurion's skin shimmers.
