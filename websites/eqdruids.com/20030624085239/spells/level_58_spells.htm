Fist of Karana: 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 812

Skill: Evocation
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 10 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 357
Spell Duration: Instant
Area Effect Duration: 5
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> has been struck by the shocking Fist of Karana.

This spell does 812 hps of damage as an Area Effect.  Again, the mobs must be very close to each other for this spell to affect them all.

 

Outdoors only
