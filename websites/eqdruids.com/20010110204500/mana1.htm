+Mana items can be just as good as +wisdom items.  1 wisdom give 10 mana at lvl 50 and as the levels go down, the less mana it gives.  However, +Mana items always give the same amount of mana, making them just as good, and even better than wis items at low levels.
But there are less +mana items than +wis items.  Here's another thing to think about... after you have 200 wisdom, each point of wisdom extra only gives 1 mana!!!!  (at lvl 50)(note that all the numbers are at lvl 50)

If the item has both wisdom and mana (Tunarian Scimitar) then the number reflected is the total  - ie +10 wis for +6 wis and +40 mana at level 50
