Emerald Warrior's Items

 

Talk to Regren inside the Emerald Warriors Guild.

He will ask you to bring him 4 items to prove your worth:

  spiderling eye

  ruined wolf pelt

  bone chips

  and bat fur.

He will reward you with xp, faction, money, and an item (often tattered armor).

Your faction improves with the Merchants of Kelethin, Emerald Warriors and Merchants of Felwithe and goes down with Indigo Brotherhood.
