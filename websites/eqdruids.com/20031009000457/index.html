Important change to some augments on Weds Patch 10/6/03

From EQLive:

Augmentation changes for Update - PLEASE READ

As of our update on October 8th, the Solid Coal of Prowess, Blood Ruby of Understanding, and Ooze-Covered Pebble of Perfection will no longer be valid augmentations.  If you had these augmentations bonded to an item, you will find them on your cursor.  You can take those items to Dible Hedledrap in South Ro and he will restore your adventure points.

IMPORTANT NOTE:  You MUST have some open inventory space to hold these items!  Also, if you zone with those items on your cursor, they will disappear.  So when you log in before the update, make sure you have some free space in your inventory.  And when you log in after the update, make certain to get those items off your cursor and into your inventory.

This change is happening due to an issue with some of the new LDoN items.  These three augmentations will be replaced with three similar augmentations that fit into slot 7 or 8.  Along with that, pre-LDoN items that were given slot type 1 will now have slot type 7.

Essentially, this will mean no change for those with these augmentations in their pre-LDoN items.  It will, however, restrict the use of multiples of these augmentations on LDoN items.  We apologize for the changes, but these items needed to be more restricted to help maintain game balance.

Alan

If you have one of the above augments make sure you are ready for this change!

Developers want your input on collection missions 9/29/03

From EQLive:

Collection Adventure Question

At Fan Faire, through the Dev Corner and on various message boards we have been hearing that some adventurers have had trouble completing item collection adventures.  We've been looking at the data and running tests, but we'd also like some specific information.

 

If you have had difficulty completing a collection adventure (at any level, and within any theme) because you have been unable to collect enough of the required item, please send a comment in to the Dev Corner with the following information:

 

Server and character name.

 

The level of your group members.

 

The name of the adventure zone you were in (the one that pops up on your screen when you zone in) and the theme.

 

The item you were sent to collect.

 

Exactly why you were unable to complete the adventure.  This should include how many items you found and how many you needed to complete the adventure, how many NPCs you defeated (as well as you know), how many were left and any other details about why you were unable to finish the adventure.

  

Please keep the information concise.  Don't ramble on about how bad your puller was our how poor your secondary tank was at keeping agro.  Just the details about the adventure please.

 

Thank you for your assistance.

 

Alan

Using the same augments on item 9/24/03

From EQLive:

Using the Same Augment Twice


You cannot seal two of the same augmentation onto the same item. The duplicate augmentation will not bond. We just wanted everyone to be clear on this point when making plans to spend their adventure points.

Lords of Everquest goes Hollywood! 9/23/03

Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) has announced the names of a slew of Hollywood talent who were brought in to provide voice-over acting for use in its upcoming PC game, Lords of EverQuest.


The real-time strategy game, due out this fall, will offer over 75 hours of single player game play, while also supporting a variety of online modes of play for up to a dozen players per game. The actors used in the title include:


    · Fairuza Balk (American History X, The Craft, The Waterboy)
    · Claudia Black (Farscape, Pitch Black)
    · Kevin Conroy (Batman: The Animated Series, Justice League)
    · John Rhys-Davies (Lord of The Rings, Raiders of the Lost Ark)
    · Hudson Leick (Xena: Warrior Princess)
    · Kate Mulgrew (Star Trek: Voyager, Remo Williams: The Adventure Begins)
    · Ron Perlman (Hellboy, Enemy at the Gates, Beauty & The Beast)
    · Katey Sagal (8 Simple Rules For Dating my Teenage Daughter, Futurama, Married ... With Children)
    · Dwight Schultz (Star Trek: The Next Generation, Star Trek: Voyager, The A-Team)
    · Cree Summer (Rugrats Go Wild!, A Different World)
    · Michael T. Weiss (The Pretender)
