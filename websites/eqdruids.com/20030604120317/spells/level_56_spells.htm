Level Fifty Six Spells

Level 56 spells are available either off the Druid Spell vendor in Firiona Vie, or are dropped off various mobs. 

Bladecoat

Wake of Karana

Engorging Roots

Breath of Karana

Ro's Smoldering Disjunction

Bladecoat: 

Effect:  Increase Armor Class (AC) by 37

Effect:  Increase Damage Shield by 6

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 235
Spell Duration: 2 hours and 48 mins @ lvl 56 to 3 hours 15 mins at lvl 65
Casting Time: 2.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Blades spring from your skin.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> skin sprouts blades.
Spell fades: Your skin returns to normal.

This is a nice spell.  Self only buff for 37 AC and does a 6 point damage shield.

 

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Engorging Roots:  

Effect:  Decrease Current Hitpoints from 178 (L56) to 205 (L65)
Effect:  Root

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 75
Spell Duration: 3.0 minutes
Duration Formula: 2
Casting Time: 1.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your feet become entwined.
 Spell cast on someone: <Target> is entangled by roots.
Spell fades: The roots fall from your feet.

Upgrade to your root spell.  Less mana per cast, and does 178 damage per cast.

Wake of Karana:  

Effect:  Stops the rain/snow in the current zone

Requires Opal as a reagent

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 400
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 7.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 5.0 seconds
Recast Delay: 90.0 seconds

When someone casts: <Target> calls out to Karana.
Spell cast on you: You call out to Karana.

This spell allows you to turn off snow or rain in a zone!  Yes, it is more for fun, or impressing newbies than anything else, but so what :)

 

It will turn off the Acid Rain in Plane of Disease so there is a new use for this spell!

 

It does take an opal per cast.

 

Spell is dropped by NPCs

 

Reported to drop by Named cycle in Skyfire

 

This spell can also be researched

Breath of Karana:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 772 (L56) to 812 (L60)
Effect:  Stuns target

Skill: Conjuration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet
Pushes Target Back: 2 feet
Throws Target Up: 2 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 12.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are slammed by an intense gust of wind.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is slammed by an intense gust of wind.

Nice upgrade wind Direct Damage.  Does 772 hps of damage per cast.  Outdoors only.

 

Spell is dropped by NPCs

 

Reported to drop mainly by Named cycle in Skyfire and in Old Sebilis.

 

This spell can also be researched

Ro's Smoldering Disjunction:

Decrease Armor Class (AC) from 26 (L56) to 27 (L60)
Decrease Fire Resist from 66 (L56) to 68 (L65)
Decrease Current Hitpoints by 150
Decrease Attack (ATK) from 58 (L56) to 62 (L65)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 100 feet

Resistance Check: Fire + 5

Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: 10.0 minutes
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by an outline of cold flame.
Spell cast on someone: Soandso is surrounded by an outline of cold flame.
Spell fades: The flames die away.

Another of the Ro's debuffs.  As with all the Ro's you will almost instantly agro the mob on yourself if you aren't careful

 

Can be found off mobs in the following zones:

 

Ssra temple

The Deep

 

This spell can also be researched
