This list is intended to show a comparison of some armor types.  The TYPE of ARMOR (cloth, chain, plate) has no bearing whatsoever on damage or AC - the only thing that matter is AC rating.  Whether you are in FULL PLATE with AC 600 or butt naked at AC 600 - the AC is all that matters.  Please note that we will be changing this with all druid wearable pieces soon...but that's a mighty task!  Check the Metal Look out for you level 30+ druids...
