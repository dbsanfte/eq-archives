Breath of Ro: 

Effect:  Decrease Armor Class (AC) from 18 (L52) to 22 (L65)
Effect:  DoT for 92 hitpoint per tick for 10 ticks.
Effect:  Decrease Fire Resist from 27 (L52) to 33 (L65)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Fire + 25

Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: 10 ticks (60 seconds)
Duration Formula: 1
 Casting Time: 3.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are immolated by blazing flames.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is immolated by blazing flames.
Spell fades: The flames die down.

All I can say, is this spell ROCKS.  It does 920 damage as a DOT in about 60 seconds time, and also reduces the mobs AC by 19 and does 27 points of fire reduction!

 

You must have a Fire Beetle Eye in your inventory for this spell to work.

 

What more could you want!  Try it on a spider in west commons, its awesome!

 

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Egress:  

Effect:  Evacuate to safe spot in zone

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: Instant
 Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 10.0 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> sinks into the ground.

Ok, this spell is a fun spell to play with.  When you cast it, you are moved to the "safe" area in the zone.  It will do a "loading please wait" no matter where you cast it, so don't panic.

 

It will also clear the mobs agro list, so can be very handy when you are being chased by a large number of mobs.

Foliage Shield:

Group Invisibility

Skill: Divination
Allowable Targets: 41

Range to Target: 100 feet
Area Effect Range: 65 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 85
Spell Duration: 20.0 minutes
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 6.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by mystical foliage.
Spell cast on someone: Soandso is surrounded by mystical foliage.
Spell fades: The mystical foliage vanishes.

Group Invisibility.  People must be in your party to cast spell on them. 

 

Your Divination must be trained high for this spell to not drive you crazy.  Otherwise it will fizzle a LOT!

 

This spell also will randomly wear off party members, it is not a fixed duration invisibility

 

Has been found in the following zones off NPCs:

 

The Maiden's Eye

The Deep

Grieg's End

Fungus Grove

Umbral Plains
