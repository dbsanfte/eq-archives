by Darwyn Autumnwood
56th Preserver of Bristlebane

This is the format I use for my spellbook. My most often-used spells are on pages 1-8. Ring and circle spells are on pages 9-14 and animal spells are on pages 15-16. The rest of the spellbook is where the old spells hang out.

I also have a hotkey panel dedicated to my spellbook with buttons called Buffs, Travel, Combat, Resist, Ring, Circle, Kunark, Animal, Heals, Summon, and AOE. This format works really well for me and I hope it gives you some ideas for organizing your own book.

P.S. With the Velious expansion druids now have 34 ring, circle, and evac spells. Ack!
