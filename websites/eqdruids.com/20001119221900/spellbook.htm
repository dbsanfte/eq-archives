I’ve updated this Spellbook organization to reflect the recent patch that lets you move spells in your Spellbook. Many people on The Druids Grove have come up with a similar arrangement. Basically you reserve the first few pages of your book for your current most-used spells. The rest of your spells hang out further back in your Spellbook.

If you’ve been intimidated by the prospect of rearranging all those icons, I hope this gives you a place to start.

Happy mouse clicking,

Darwyn Autumnwood

Bristlebane
