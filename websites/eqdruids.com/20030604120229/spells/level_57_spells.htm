Bonds of Tunare: 

Effect:  Decrease Movement by 65%

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 30 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 115
Spell Duration: 1.8 minutes
Casting Time: 2.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Dark vines drag your feet into the ground.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is dragged down by dark vines.
Spell fades: Your feet come free.

This is a nice spell.  It's an area effect snare.  Watch your quad kiting abilities go through the roof with this spell.  No more having to cast on each mob single.

 

Dropped from NPCs

 

Drops mainly by Named cycle in Skyfire

 

This spell can also be researched

Succor:  

Effect:  Decrease Mana by 150
Effect:  Evacuate to zones safe point

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 9.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> creates a mystic portal.
Spell fades: The portal shimmers and fades.

This is the group version of Egress.  Takes you to the same teleport points, but allows you to take a group with you.  It will use 150 points of mana from each person if available (if no, it drains all that person has available).

 

Great spell to go places like across Siren's Grotto to Western Wastes zone.
