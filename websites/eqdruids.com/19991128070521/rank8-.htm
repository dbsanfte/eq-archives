1) Beguile Animals. This is an upgrade to the Befriend Animal/Charm Animal line of spells. It is supposed to last longer, have a harder chance to resist then the lower level cousins, but it my opinion it does neither. Spell duration is anywhere from 20 seconds to 5 minutes.

2) Circle of Feerot, Lavastorm, Ro, and Steamfont. These are the most desired spells of druids and their friends. With these spells you can teleport groups (must be in groups) to other far distant zones. You cannot unfortunately teleport someone without you going with them though. These spells take a very long time to cast, longer than any other spell; therefore they should not be treated as emergency escape spells. These spells also take a whopping 300 mana to cast. There is a downside to these spells also. You will be sent many /tells by others asking you to gate them to such and such place. Get used to being harassed often.

Feerot -- This takes you to the Ogre Newbie Zone. It is a short cut to get to the dungeon known as Cazic Thule, but be careful of the Ogre Bouncers roaming around. To get to the dungeon, head south west from the ruins. No vendor is here.

Lavastorm -- This spell takes you to the zone of Lavastorm.It is a short cut to Solusek and Najena dungeons. The vendor here sells the level 49 spells.

Ro -- This spell takes you to the zone of South Ro. It is a medium level zone for trolls that frequent the area and is short cut to the dungeon of Guk. To get to Guk, head south to the zone to Innothule Swamp then hug the left zone wall to the dungeon. No vendor here.

Steamfont - This spell takes you to the lands of the Gnomes. The ruins have a vendor and is quite near the city of Akkanon.

3) Drones of Doom. This spell is an upgrade to the Stinging Swarm/Creeping Crud line of Damage over Time spells. It does about 210-330 pts damage in about 60 seconds.

4) Earthquake.This spell is an upgrade to the Tremor line of area effect spells. The area of effect is quite large so you might accidentally pull monsters that you didn't mean to. In addition thanks to a bug, it also pulls monsters from above and below you. The damage is 300 pts but it consumes a large amount of mana.

5) Endure Magic. This is a very important spell as you will be facing a greater number of spell casters that can seriously hurt a party. This spell reduces the damage that a spell will do. It increases your magic resistance by 30 pts.

6) Greater Wolf Form. An upgrade to the Wolf Form spell, the only difference between this spell and its predecessor is that the attack range increases from 40 to 50. There are no new benefits associated with this spell. As with Wolf Form, it casts a longer, faster duration of Spirit of the Wolf on you, adjusts your faction so that evil races do not attack you on sight, and puts you in a real cool body of a wolf.

7) Lightning Strike. This is an outdoor only spell. It has a small area of effect and does 173-185 pts of damage to all creatures inside that range. The range is about a 2 person radius meaning that the MoBs have to be standing side by side for them to get hit.

8) Regeneration. This spell causes the target to regenerate 6 hps a tick for about 15 minutes. It is great for when you are low on mana and seriously hurt as it consumes less mana then greater heal and lets you regain your mana while you heal. It does stack with the bard healing song and a troll's regeneration.

9) Resist Cold. This spell improves your cold resistance by 40. It is an upgrade to the endure cold spell and is quite useful in lowering the damage caused by cold based attacks. An example of such an attack would be Greater Ice Bone Skeletons which hurt a lot unless you have this spell up.

10) Strength of Stone. This spell will cause the target's strength to increase by 20 plus 1 per additional level up to a maximum of 25.

11) Succor: Butcher. This spell is the druid emergency escape spell that will transport the party to the ring spell butcher. As with all Succor spells, they do not cast faster than gate though it is much faster than Circle Spells.
