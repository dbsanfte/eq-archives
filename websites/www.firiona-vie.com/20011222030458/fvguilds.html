    Guild Name Iron Monkey
    Alignment Neutral
    Website http://www.trafficblast.com/im
    Message Board http://pub50.ezboard.com/bironmonkey
    Comments

    Iron Monkey in it's current state is just a handful of veteran EQers trying to milk all the enjoyment we can out of a video game we've played for over 2 years now =) On the verge of quiting we decided to try the Firiona Vie server in hopes to rekindle some of our past enjoyment on a new server. Although we are not a roleplaying guild we will not back away from roleplaying. We are also not an uberguild and currently have no plans to become one. Iron Monkey has always been small and tactical. If we come across a difficult adventure we work harder to win rather then calling more people to help. Death and experience loss mean very little if anything at all to Iron Monkey members.

    Iron Monkey's current goal is to stop leveling at 52 and become a casual adventuring guild. We'll stick to Dragons, Planes and some other 50ish encounters.

    Guild Name League of Honour
    Alignment Good
    Comments

    From many worlds we gather, strangers all. Join us for an ale, and sit, and let us share our stories and see if we would adventure future together. Forget who and what you may have been in other realms and other dreams, and let us talk of what we would see in one another here and now.

    It is not enough to be brave in the face of dangers, and to lay down your life that your friends might escape some vile and evil beast. It is not enough to be merely honorable in your tradings, and to be a staunch and loyal companion. Nay, it is not even sufficient that ye shall be great of heart and seek always the right action. Ye must also be able to do such with a laugh, and a smile for all.

    As we wander let us say our mission is to be true to our selves, whether we hail from the humble sewers of Qeynos, or the mighty Felwithe halls. In this we mean that no matter what skill, or spell or level of songs we have learned, we will always strive to be better, and to help our fellows realize their potential as well. Truly we seek to master the important skills of kindness, humour and generosity of spirit.

    For is it not the case that the only real treasure that you can gain is, that in the end, you like yourself a little better?
