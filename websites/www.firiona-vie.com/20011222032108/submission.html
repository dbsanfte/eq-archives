Submissions Page
Event Submissions:
Please click Here to fill out an events form. Please be as detailed as possible in your submission. Your event submission must include a contact email, time, date, title, and sponsors of the submitted event.
Character Profiles Submissions:
Please click Here to fill out a character profile form. Include a valid email address so if there is a problem or something needs to be clarified, we can get in touch with you to make corrections. Your email will not be published on the character profiles.
Submit Guild Info:
Please click Here to fill our a guild listing form. Please use this form as well if you need to update information about your guild. Your submission must have a valid contact email listed with the form in case we need to contact you.
Galleria Submissions:
All Galleria Submissions will need to be sent to Chessie. All screenshots, real life pictures, and artwork will need to be sent in .jpeg or .gif format. The pictures will be re-sized to fit for displaying. For all original writings, please do not send any .txt or .doc files. Put a copy of your story in the mail and I will work with it from there. Any type of profanity, or distasteful submissions, whether it is a picture or writing, will be deleted and your submission will not be displayed.
