The Royal Staff

 The Royal Staff is composed of adjunct positions such as Royal Scribe, Ambassador, and Royal Attachés as well as Tradespeople who have attained 200 skill in their professions. These positions recognize members' hard work and desire to help others. Members in need of products (jewelry, pottery, armor, spells) can come to the Tradespeople to purchase products for the cost of the materials, as well as attain advice on increasing their skill in that trade.
