The Royal House

Queen
 Toythunder Stormwalker

Enchantress


 Oversees all guild actions. May induct new full members, promote officers, and head guild meetings. Final approval/denial on any matter that involves the guild. Is the Guild Leader and can bring in new probationary members.

 

Crown Prince
 Tyrek Windwalker

Druid


 Assists the Queen in overseeing all guild actions. Oversees all Elite Guard actions. Resolve any matters of guild defense and counsels the Queen on matters of war. Final approval on all applicants to the Elite Guard. Reports to the Queen. Is a Guild Officer and can bring in new probationary members. May head guild meetings and induct new full members.
