Who: Lanitdan Spellcaster, 44th Level Shaman of the Realm

~Guildie Spotlight~

 Lanitdan Spellcaster, Level 44 Shammy

Inducted into the guild by Fidlo.

 I am honored to intoduce Lanitdan as the new Guildie in the Spotlight. He has been working so hard on all his toons. Sometimes it is hard too tell where he is, since he seems to play from his many toons daily.

 Speaking with Lanitdan, I see a great asset to our family! He has gone above and beyond. I truly believe that he was made to be a Shammy. He's so energetic, loves too pull his own, and his spirits are so high. His great personality comes out tremendously when he plays. He loves to joke around and have a great time. His favorite place to play has been in the Dreadlands. He enjoys the SE ruins mainly due to great loot and the nice experience. When I spoke to him, he was at level 40. I can see him getting too 50 in no time. em/bow.

He is so anxious to get to the higher levels, to see what his shammy can really do! And to experience what Keanow (RL father) has spoken about and shown him. His perfect group consists of a chanter, cleric, warrior, warrior, monk, and last but not least him (shammy).

What made him want to play a shammy. He liked the way that Lardaor was able to help people. Lanitdan really looked up to Lardaor. He enjoys being a member of OoL for the simple reason of how nice the people are and how each of them is always willing to help others!

What he would like to be remembered as, is someone that enjoyed the game and is happy to help whenever he can. His goals are his epics and to help whoever and whenever he can.

 We are so very proud and honored to have him in our family and congratulate him on all his success as a friend, player,and most of all as a brother of Order of Light.  Hats off to you, bro!

 

 

Interview conducted and written by: Francheskia Wolfwhisper
