We are the Order of Light on the Rathe Server in the PC game EverQuest !

Our primary goal is to have fun. However, the kind of people who join the OoL are the kind of people who can only have fun if everybody around them is also having fun. So our secondary goal is to make sure everyone has fun during their stay in Norrath.
Some Comments
  • This is not a "twinking" guild. People join the OoL because they want to help other people, not because they expect to be helped.
  • This is a "Good" guild. We are good-aligned and all members are encouraged to role-play that alignment.
  • This is a "voluntary" guild. Members are expected to participate as much or as little as they like at any given time. Duties, meetings, requirements, etc. are kept to a minimum. We encourage members to be involved with the guild, but not to the point where they don't have time to play the game. Unless, of course, that is what they want to do.
General Policies
Alternate Characters
Players may have more than one character in the OoL. However, only one character may vote or hold office.

Duels
Members of the OoL may engage in duels at their own discretion.

Chat Channel
Since there is only one chat channel available to our entire guild, please treat it with respect.
