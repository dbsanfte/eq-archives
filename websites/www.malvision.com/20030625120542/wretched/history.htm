HISTORY

 

Wretched was founded by a small group of skilled gamers that were drawn together from similar interests and attitudes. We are not roleplayers but we do respect the rules and try to stay with in character in public channels. In private channel we are a bit extreme and give each other hell.

 

Wretched has always been one of the top 25 guilds with 1/3 of the members of other guilds, this was one of the founding principles of Wretched.

 

Over the past year we have seen some core members take time off and some return but Wretched continues to grow.
