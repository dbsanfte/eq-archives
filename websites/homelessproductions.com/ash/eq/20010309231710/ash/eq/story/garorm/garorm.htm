The Beginning for Garorm

It all began 60 years ago. Garnald Bludfyst was a great Barbarian Warrior. He had journeyed to the 4 corners of Norath, and had seen most of the wonders of the world.

About 40 years ago, he met Shalana. She was a beautiful lady of human blood. She was very practiced in the skills of Magic. Shalana was journeying across the Karana plains, and came upon Garnald locked in mortal combat with 6 lions.

She rushed to his aid, and with her powerful magic, and his mighty sword, they dispatched the lions, but it nearly cost Garnald his life. She spent 3 days and nights at his side, nursing him back to health.

By the end of these 3 days, Garnald had fallen hopelessly in love with Shalana, and she with him. They journeyed back to her family’s home in the hills near Qeynos, where they quickly announced their plans to marry. Shalana’s family was well off, and the event was a wondrous sight.

People came from all over to celebrate the occasion. The party lasted for nearly 3 days, and the entire town of Qeynos was drunk from the good ale and wine. After the wedding, they journeyed to Halas, where Garnald’s home was, and settled in. For the next twenty years, they shared a wonderful life of love, and adventure. Between all the grandeur of travel, Garnald and Shalana Bludfyst begat three children. They were named Tavin, Thalliass, and Garorm.

About sixteen years ago, Garnald left on an expedition to Freeport. He was to be gone two fortnights. He never came home. Tavin left to go find his father, and returned a week later, with the sad news that Garnald had been slain by a hill Giant in the Eastern Plains of Karana, on his way home. Tavin was very saddened to deliver this news, and after giving the message, announced that he was leaving Halas and going in search of a new meaning for his life.

Shalana was completely devastated. Garnald had been the whole of her life. After two months, she decided she could not bear the memories created by life in Halas any longer, and announced that she would take Garorm, and return to Qeynos to live with her family. Thalliass refused to leave, preferring to stay in Halas, and finish her apprenticeship in The Tribunal.

So it was that on a cold Saturday afternoon in January, Shalana and Garorm departed for Qeynos. At this time, Garorm was 6 years of age. On the journey through Black Burrow, a band of gnolls ambushed them, and despite a valiant fight, Shalana was slain. They then turned on Garorm, and gouged out his right eye. He would have died that day, had it not been for some brave warriors, who came along and rescued him.

Garorm was delivered into the care of Shalana’s parents, who raised him to a young man. At fifteen, he swore an oath that he would avenge his mother’s death, and declared the Gnolls of Black Burrow as his sworn life enemy. He then announced that he was going back to Halas to find his sister, and make his place in the world. Garorm arrived in Halas late that night, and made camp in the merchant’s cave at Everfrost Peaks. The next morning, he rose, and ferried into Halas to look for his sister Thalliass. He found her at the Tribunal.

She had finished her apprenticeship, and was now a Shaman of the 6th rank. She was very happy to see him after nearly three years since her last visit. They went to the Golden Torch for a glass of ale, and to catch up on times past. Garorm asked her for news of Tavin, but alas, she had heard nothing of his fate since his departure those nine long years ago.

They talked late into the night, and by the time they called it a night; Garorm had decided that he would request apprenticeship at the Tribunal first thing in the morning. The next morning, Garorm awoke, and found a note from Thalliass:

Dearest Brother,

Seeing you again has made me long to renew family bonds. I wish you the best success in your apprenticeship. Here are some goods to help you in the coming months of training. I am departing now at first light on a quest to find Tavin. Wish me a good journey, and I will see you again one day, with our brother.

Love always, Thalliass

On the table, along with the note was about 6 platinum, two backpacks, some rations, and a worn great staff. Garorm ate a quick breakfast, and headed toward town.

So begin the Chronicles of his Life.
