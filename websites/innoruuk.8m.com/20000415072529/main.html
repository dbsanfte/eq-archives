Well I just took a peek at EQVault and found something interesting.

I am interested in having a gathering Saturday June 26 on Innoruuk server for all mages who research to trade sell and give away those hard to find research components and spells that we all find and can't use for one reason or another. I propose a meeting open to all races starting around 4:00 CST at the Swashbuckler tunnel between East Commons and North Ro. I propose the gathering follow this schedule:

4:00 - 4:15 Spells and components that people want to give away or trade
4:15 - 4:45 Spells and components to be sold or auctioned
4:45 - 5:00 Language learning (spamming) [/say only please no shouting]
5:00 - 5:30 Mage Dueling
5:30 - till Mages Unite to take out Griffins, Hill Giants, Swashbucklers or maybe even a priest of discord in Freeport (If some high level mages show)

Notice: there can be free bakers treats and alcoholic beverages if I can find any donors (I will bring as many as I can)

If you are interested in helping or feel another day/time would be better please contact me at ehf@acadiacom.net and if I get enough requests I will change the day/time.

Thanks Solinar and EQVault.
