Ashborne Guild Rules


Rule 1) We rule, we know we rule, end of story.

Rule 2) Play your main character.  Alternates are great but we are all here to contribute to our family here and Alts don't help the guild much.

Rule 3) Invitations will be handled by guild officers.  If you have someone you want to sponsor for membership post their names under the membership forum and have them contact Vyktor in game.

Rule 4) No member will ever exploit or use a bug in the game for their own advantage.  Anyone who does so will not only be /guildremove'd but will be reported by all members of the guild.
