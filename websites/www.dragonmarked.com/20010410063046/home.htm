Welcome, this site was created exclusively for the EverQuest guild, Dragonmarked. On this site you will find information about becoming a member of the guild as well as the guild's history. The site also features an up to date, 100% dynamic, list of all members with complete character pictures and bios. A bulletin board system is provided for all out-of-game guild communication.

The Dragonmarked guild is currently active on two Everquest servers, Prexus and Torvonnilous, so the members and bulletin board systems are divided accordingly.

Please take a look around and enjoy yourself.
