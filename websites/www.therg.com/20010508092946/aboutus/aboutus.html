Therestaurantgame, bringing the industry together. Providing restaurateurs and their suppliers with an environment in which they can communicate, educate and trade. A dedicated editorial team with international, industry-specific experience will produce all editorial and creative content. Chief executive, Edwina Lilley, has opened more than thirty restaurants over the last 20 years. In 1997 the 13-strong Est Est Est group was sold to City Centre Restaurants PLC and subsequently expanded to 21.

How therestaurantgame began…..
While Edwina was researching one of her new restaurant ventures (soon to open in Manchester) she discovered that even the most basic information and services on the Internet were virtually impossible to find. Therestaurantgame is the consequence, a one-stop interactive website catering for the needs of everyone in the restaurant industry. The editorial team has a diverse knowledge base covering all aspects of the catering industry and a comprehensive understanding of the latest business technology. Most importantly, we are committed to providing our members with a vital insight into the fascinating business that is the restaurant game.
