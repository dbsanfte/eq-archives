Welcome to Everquest Neverlost.  Your source for all the information you need to survive and live well in the Land of Norrath.  Our focus is on keeping you found in this ever growing and changing world.  We feature the Lost Atlas 
of Norrath, a compilation of the known combat zones within the game, in an easy to read, standardized format.  Points of interest in each zone are clearly marked, zone location numbers readily found on each page.  Each zone will also have our exclusive travelers routes, tips and tricks to get you there and keep you alive on the journey.  In the appendix you'll find a list of unique items and NPC within each zone.  It'll tell you what the item is, who drops it, and the level of the NPC holding it.   In addition you will find journal pages to which you can add your own travel notes.  So if you've a tip, trick, or a route through a dangerous zone you'd like to share feel  free to send them to us.


Neverlost is seeking contributors.  We are compiling guides and trade skill guides at the moment.  Of those that contribute to the Outfitters Discussion forum in the class to class section we will pick the best contribution from each class.  The winner will be given a free Atlas and will write the Masters Guide for the Neverlost site. The winner may also be used for the printed publication due later this year or early next year entitled Neverlost's Guide to Classes.  To compete simply post your advice and ideas within the Discussion Forum for classes.  Good Luck!
