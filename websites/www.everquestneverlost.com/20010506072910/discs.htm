Bards

Discipline of Resistant
Bards receive this discipline at level 51.
Duration: 5 minutes. Re-Use Delay: 60 minutes.

This discipline adds +10 to all resistances. This discipline lasts for 1 minute,
and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will
decrease as you level. This discipline will only be available if you have
purchased and registered the Velious expansion.

Discipline of Fearless
Bards receive this discipline at level 55.
Duration: 11 seconds.  Re-Use Delay: 60 minutes.

Immunity to 'Fear' and all spells that cause 'fear'. This discipline lasts for 11
seconds, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline
will decrease as you level. This discipline will only be available if you have
purchased and registered the Velious expansion.

Discipline of Deft Dance
Bards receive this discipline at level 55.
Duration: 15 seconds. Re-Use Delay: 72 minutes.

This discipline causes the Bard to auto dodge and auto dual-wield. This
discipline lasts for 10 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 72 minutes.
This discipline will only be available if you have purchased and registered the
Velious expansion.

Discipline of Pure Tone
Bards receive this discipline at level 60.
Duration: 4 minutes. Re-Use Delay: 72 minutes.

This discipline makes it as if you are wielding the best instrument of every type,
whether or not you are actually wielding an instrument. This discipline lasts for
2 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 72 minutes. This discipline will only be
available if you have purchased and registered the Velious expansion.

 

Monks

Discipline of Resistant
Monks receive this discipline at level 30.
Duration: 5 minutes. Re-use delay: 60 minutes.

This discipline adds +3 to all resistances increasing to +10 at 50th level. This discipline
lasts for 1 minute, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline
will decrease as you level. This discipline will only be available if you have purchased
and registered the Velious expansion

Discipline of Fearless
Monks receive this discipline at level 40.
Duration: 11 seconds. Re-use delay: 60 minutes.

Immunity to 'Fear' and all spells that cause 'fear'. This discipline lasts for 11 seconds,
and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease
as you level. This discipline will only be available if you have purchased and registered
the Velious expansion.

Discipline of Stone Stance
Monks receive this discipline at level 51.
Duration: 12 seconds.  Re-Use Delay: 12 minutes.

When using this discipline the monk will take roughly 1/10th damage from most physical
attacks. This discipline lasts for 12 seconds and has a base reuse time of 12 minutes.
The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the monk gains additional levels.

Discipline of Thunder Kick
Monks receive this discipline at level 52.
Duration: Instant. Re-Use Delay: 7 minutes.

When this discipline is activated the next successful flying kick will do additional damage.
The base reuse time for this discipline is 9 minutes and will decrease as the monk gains
additional levels.

Discipline of Whirlwind
Monks receive this discipline at level 52.
Duration: 9 seconds. Re-Use Delay: 60 minutes.

When using this discipline the monk will automatically riposte every attack landed on them
by the opponent they are facing. This discipline lasts for 9 seconds and has a base reuse
time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the monk gains
additional levels.

The Discipline of Voiddance
Monks receive this discipline at level 54.
Duration: 8 seconds. Re-Use Delay: 60 minutes.

When using this discipline the monk cannot be hit in melee combat. This does not in any
way alter their ability to be damaged by magical means. This discipline lasts for 8 seconds
and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease
as the monk gains additional levels.

The Discipline of Innerflame
Monks receive this discipline at level 56.
Duration: 12 seconds .  Re-use delay: 30 minutes

When using this discipline all hits landed by the monk will be for at least four times the
base weapons/hand damage. For example: If you used a weapon with a base damage of
8, then you would receive a bonus of 32 damage on every hit for the duration of the discipline.
This discipline lasts for 12 seconds and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time
for this discipline will decrease as the monk gains additional levels.

The Discipline of 100 Fist
Monks receive this Discipline at level 57
Duration: 15 seconds .  Re-use delay: 30 minutes

When using this discipline the monk will be hasted (e.g. will hit much faster). This haste
stacks with every other haste in the game including haste items and spells. This discipline
lasts for 15 seconds and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this
discipline will decrease as the monk gains additional levels.

The Discipline of Ashenhand
Monks receive this Discipline at level 59
Duration: Instant .  Re-use delay: 72 minutes

When this discipline is activated the next Eagle Strike will do a surprising amount of damage
and under certain circumstances may have a chance to kill the target outright.

The Discipline of Silentfist
Monks receive this Discipline at level 59
Duration: Instant.  Re-use delay: 7 minutes

When this discipline is activated the next successful Dragon Punch (Tail Rake for Iksar) will
do additional damage and have a chance of stunning the target. The base reuse time for this
discipline is 9 minutes and will decrease as the monk gains additional levels.

The Discipline of Evasive
Warriors receive this Discipline at level 52
Duration: 3 minutes.  Re-use delay: 15 minutes

The warrior is hit by his opponent less often but also will hit his target less often. This

The Discipline of Charge
Warriors get this Discipline at level 53
Duration: 14 seconds .  Re-use delay: 30 minutes

When using this discipline the warrior will automatically land all attacks against his
opponent. In other words he will not miss. This discipline lasts for 14 seconds and

The Discipline of Mighty Strike
Warriors receive this Discipline at level 54
Duration 10 seconds.  Re-use delay: 60 minutes

The Discipline of Defensive
Warriors receive this Discipline at level 55
Duration: 3 minutes.  Re-use delay: 15 minutes

The warrior takes less melee damage but also deals less. This will not reduce damage
taken by the warrior from any form of magic, damage shield, or weapon proc. This
discipline lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time
for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains additional levels.  You will take 45
to 50% less damage and deal 25% less damage.
