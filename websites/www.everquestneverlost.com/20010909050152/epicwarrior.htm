Warrior Epic

To start the warrior epic go see Kargak Redblade, who is standing next to
Wenden Blackhammer the dwarf in East Freeport. Enter from North Ro 
and follow the left wall.

             


Head to Lake Rathe to get the unjewled dragon head hilt.  Its lies at the
bottom of the lake beneath the three spawn Aviak fire tower.  The unjewled
hilt can be spawned by hailing an ogre NPC at the ogre camp by the
entrance to Rathe Mountains.  He will tell you about seeing  something in
the water near the tower during is once a year bath. You will also need
three gems, a diamond, black sapphire and a jacinth.  These can be
bought or found on high levels  mobs in the planes or high level dungeons. 
Take the unjewled dragon head hilt and three gems to Wenden, hand him
all four items and you will recieve the Jewled Dragon head hilt.

Head to Timorous Deep, go to the chessboard approx. neg6820,pos2835. 
It lies at the foot of  the King piece, looks like a small bag.  This is the 
Severly Damaged Dragon Head Hilt. Now you will need to head to the
Plane of Fear to get a Ball of Everliving Golem from one of the named
golems Fright, Dread, and Terror.

   
  


These are the personal guards of the God Cazic Thule.  You'll need many
high level friends to take them down.  Now you need to head to Kunark,
Burning Woods or Dread Lands to kill  Mountain Giant Patriarchs, you
need to get a  Giant Monocle, this item is not no drop so you may be able
to buy or trade for it.  Killing giants in Dread Lands at the Giant Fort seems
to be the best way to get the Patriarch to spawn. Take the monocle to the
Mountain Giant fort in Frontier Mountains. Gice the Monocle to Mentrax
Mountainbone, he is at the end of the fortress.



Once you give him the Monocle he will give you the Rejesiam Ore.
Turn in the ball of everliving golem, the ore and the severely damaged
hilt to Wenden and he will give you the finely crafted dragon head hilt.

To get blade one, head to Ocean of Tears and talk to Denken Strongpick.
He can be found on  aviak island. He will tell you he needs supplies for
himself and his friend, Fendlemend. He will give you the rusty old blade he
found on the ocean floor in exchange for two rebreathers, a keg of Vox 
Tail Ale and a Block of Permafrost.  The Keg of Vox Tail Ale can be bought 
from vendors all around the world, average cost is 100 to 150pp.  It can be
bought at the Sandfishers in North Ro and the Freeport Port Authority. To
get the Block of Permafrost head to Permafrost off of Everfrost zone.  It is
said that you can  get the Block from both the Ice Giants out front and the
Ice Giants inside.  This may be true after several patches ago but is
unsubstantiated.  It does drop from Ice Giants deep inside  Perma though. 
While you are here kill Goblin Wizards until you have a frozen goblin heart. 
You will need this later in the quest.




Rebreathers are tinkered by gnomes, you will need two and can expect
to pay around 2k for each rebreather. Turn in the Block of Permafrost,
the Rebreathers and the Keg to Denken, he will give you the first blade.

To get blade two you need to go to Chardok which is off of Burning
Woods, you need to kill the Queen.  You may have to kill her several
times to get the blade.  Bring many friends she is tough. The Red
Scabbard.  Talk to Kargak Redblade in East Freeport again. Say 
[I will do you a favor] You'll get a letter, (waxed sealed note).  Take this 
letter to an Ogre in the Ferrott named  Oknoggin. Hecan befound 
between the stone bridge and the fallen totem. Roughly he is at loc 
800,1160. Give him the note and he will give you a Tiny Lute.



Take this back to Kargak, give it to him and he will will give you
a book.  Take this book to his brother in East Karana, Tenal
Redblade by name, run up the chasm beside the path to High 
Hold, you give him the book. He will give you a totem of freezing
winds, hand this back to him along  with the frozen goblin heart. 
He will give you the Totem of Firey War. 



Now you will need to get a Green and a Red Dragon scale.  The Red 
Dragon scale drops from Nagafen, and Talendor, Talendor can be 
found in Skyfire.  The Green scale comes from  Severilous in Emerald 
Jungle.


 




Once you have a red and green dragon scale turn them in along with the 
totem Tenal gave you back to Tenal. He will give you the Mark of the Sword.

Now head to the Plane of Hate, you need to kill the Maestro of Rancor.
After you kill him the Hand of the Maestro will spawn. Some times after
you kill the hand a Very Unpleasant Hand will spawn.  Be prepared for 
some very hard battles.

   


Go back to Tenal Redblade and give him the hand and the Mark he gave
you earlier.  He will give you yet another note to take to Kargek in East 
Freeport, you will also need to go the Plane of Air to retrieve a Spiroc 
Wingblade from the Spiroc Lord.




Once you turn these into Kargek, he gives you the Red Scabbard
and instructions about  combining the Ancient blade, Ancient Sword 
Blade, Finely Crafted Dragon head hilt and the Jewled Dragon 
Head hilt to recieve the Jagged Blade of War. The Jagged Blade 
of War can be split into two weapons for dual weilding and 
recombined to  form a two hand slashing weapon.
