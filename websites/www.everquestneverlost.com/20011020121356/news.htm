July 26, 2001

Ok, we are now in the home stretch!  We are wrapping up the last of the more
complicated zones, and plan to have the Atlas ready for shipping in early August.
We will start taking orders next week so look for an email with ordering information
if you've signed up to be notified at this link.  

We've decided to do two editions of the Atlas:  The Desktop Edition which is a
fully bound edition with space for your own notes, and the Gold Edition which will
be more of a collector's piece, bound in hand-tooled leather.

We apologize for the delay and look forward to presenting you with an excellent
resource for your adventures in Norrath.

Gigl & Bearog

April 25th, 2001

Hello and welcome to the editorial and news page.  It is my experience that
people   avoid the editorial page like an Enchanter would avoid tanking Venril
Sathir.   Nonetheless you have found it so, while you are here please take a 
look at what Neverlost wishes to become.  

Let me say that Neverlost does not wish to discourage the younger player 
in any way. I will say that our goal is to become the dedicated information site 
for the post 40 world of Norrath.  Our information is targeted to the dedicated 
player who as reached his or her 40th season and wants to continue.  
Certainly younger players can use this site and gain knowledge from it,
just don't  expect to see a strategy guide here for Blackburrow or Crushbone.
You will find Planar Guides, guides to places like CoM, VP, TT, CS, VT  and 
many others.  These guides are currently under construction for publication  on 
this site. 

That brings me to my next topic.  YOU.  I am currently seeking contributors and, 
writers for Class guides, Trade skill Guides, Zone and Raid Strategy, 
Epic Quests and General Quests.  If you have talent, or have no talent but 
have the information, then we can and will use you.  You can take a hand in 
contributing to your fellow player's worlds with the information you possess.  
Here is a chance to be recognized for the EQ Guru that you are, put that 
addiction to work!  I would really like to hear from you, ideas, complaints, 
advice or contributions  my door is always open and I'm listening.  

Let's build the best dedicated post 40 world site on the web.

Regards,

Bear
