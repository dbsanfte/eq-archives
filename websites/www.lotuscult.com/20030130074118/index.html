Solusek Ro Dead!

The Prince of Flame (no, not Salta) was sent off to visit Rallos in Freeport recently, as a rampant mob of cultists stormed his throne room demanding access to the Plane of Fire. It wasn't a particularly outstanding kill, but we ran off with some nice loot and around 60 flags for our final elemental plane, so we were happy.

We've already spent a fair bit of time in here, killing off some nameds and generally running around seeing what we can find. I'll try to get more pics of the drops since I've been slacking off, but for now here are some shots of the mobs we've been killing.




In non-plane-of-fire related news, we also knocked off Rallos again recently - I have no shots since my EQ decided to die on me with no warning, but I'm told it was our cleanest fight to date. And finally.. the banana saga:



Agnarr: Zerged!


(And that was just guild members..)


So there we were, having just killed off Rallos yet still missing parts of our flags for the elemental planes. We needed to knock off Agnarr, but were having trouble finding him up and available. Knowing he would spawn today, and knowing we'd only have one reasonable shot at him, we sat and camped. And camped. And sat. And camped some more. 100 people sitting around the little phallic device trying desperately not to touch it was quite a sight to behold. In the end, of course, we killed him, but with such an amazing zerg force there it was rather anti climactic, especially for a guild first kill. Anyway, we are now flagged for Plane of Earth, Plane of Water, and Plane of Air. They all looked like a lot of fun on first inspection, so without hesitation the LC training machine clicked into action and two out of three elemental planes had big nasty trains waiting at the zone in, ready to catch any unsuspecting sight seers by surprise.

I probably don't need to ramble on any more, instead I'll just finish with a pic of what we got up to while waiting for Agnarr to spawn, as well as some quotes that missed being posted on the last couple of updates.


















Rallos Zek Visits Freeport!

It was always going to be soon, but after having to push it back a couple of days due to patches and random spawn times, Rallos Zek is finally dead on Quellious. After spending days throwing ourselves over and over at the event trying to figure out just how we were going to get this guy dead, it was amazing how smoothly we managed to make it in the end. Zero tank switches, and only 10 deaths for the entire final battle showed just how well the guild performed tonight.
