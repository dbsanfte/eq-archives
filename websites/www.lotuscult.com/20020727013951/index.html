Upgrades Abound


Things have changed a little around the site. Most noteably, our old software was becoming unstable and unwieldly, so we chucked it to the weeds. Therafter a horde of gnomes descended on it and muttered something about it being "a great find!" I think it is safe to say that we can all agree that anything a gnome would be excited about is something that needed to be gotten rid of. Anyway, most of the changes weren't affect you gentle readers. Well, you might find our Roster a bit more up to date, but that's about it!. Enjoy!


Oh, and one more thing... if you were registered with the site before, you'll need to re-reigster. Sorry for the inconveinance.


/auc Paying 20k for looting rights to a lucid shard~

We've been taking it easy after downing the emp earlier this week, spending most of our time sitting around in raptured bliss at the thought of going through yet another luclin timesink in order to get a key. Sunday night saw blood repopping and us back up the top of the temple of snakes for another shot at the big party pooper himself. He ended up at around 90% life or so, similar to other attempts, but it was a much more organized fight and our most stable yet.
