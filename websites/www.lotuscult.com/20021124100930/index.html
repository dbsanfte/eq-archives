You hear the pounding of hooves.



We finally gained access to the Plane of Tactics recently, and immediately set to work exploring the zone. Quite possibly one of the nicest places we've seen so far in PoP, the whole zone looks very well designed and like it will be a lot of fun.. if you can avoid the stampeding boars. Our first target was Vallon Zek, a mean looking fellow and an encounter which we had a blast figuring out:



We spent a couple of days on him with little luck, finally after much experimenting our late Sunday night crew of 46 cultists managed to push him over in a great show of teamwork.




Grats Kataisa and Batto!




Of course, in order to gain entry into Drunder, we had to kill off the Manaetic Behemoth in the Plane of Innovation. After having him warp on us at 50% last time, we headed back with a few alterations to the plan and proceeded to lay the smack down on this robotic contraption of war. We were rewarded with some pretty decent loots, and invited to follow the gnome on into the Fortress of Zek.


Grats Xinae!
Grats Shackmyn!




In a busy few days for the cult, we also managed to take down Ryda'Dar in the Halls of Honor. Much like our first attempt at his relative in the Plane of Valor, this guy turned into something of a zerg-fest, however we emerged victorious with the first of our HoH trials completed.

More stuff dead..


Nothing much new to report - we've been progressing slowly but steadily through the new expansion, with most of our recent focus having been on the crypt of decay and the events it contains. High Priest has died twice thus far, so with any luck we'll be able to spend some time throwing ourselves at the ring of bertox soon. If anyone from VI happens to read this page by chance, please do something about the encounter with the skeleton spawns. I'm sure you know which one I mean (it's pictured on the right there) - I have no problems dying to the actual adds during a fight, but dying because the adds caused so much video lag as to render the game unplayable gets pretty annoying.

In other news, we killed Aerin again to get a bunch more people flagged for Halls of Honor, also got Behemoth to around 50% before he decided to eat us and poof back on to the roof. Knocked over Terris once more, and spent a fair bit of time running around Torment exploring the lower depths of one mob's intestinal tract as well as some oh-so-fun key camping for Saryrn's tower. Finally, we wound up with a first shot at Ryda'Dar in HoH, who looks like he will prove a challenge in the near future (if the trigger doesn't get "accidentally" blown by curious wizards).

Picked up a bunch of random loots from around the place, nothing earth shattering but linked here since I've been slacking on the loot posts recently.

*PoP*

We spent most of the day exploring the new planes, more specifically: Plane of Nightmare. First thing that must be said is a big kudos to the verant graphic designers.. after hours on end of killing either a four armed akhevan or a robed shade, it was amazing to see some of the new models they've put in. Overall the expansion looks very smooth. The raid window is particularly nice (it's about time too), hopefully they will get around to making it a little more intuitive to use soon. Anyway - back to our exploring in PoN. First impression of the zone was that it was much like PoGrowth, just without all the warm fuzzy woodland creatures. We started off by killing some named Treant near the zone in, who proved to be far easier than we had expected and dropped pretty crappy loot in line with the difficulty. After that we decided to roam around a bit, and - after several trains due to people running blindly ahead - managed to find a couple of large, evil looking undead knight type things:



They died easily, and didn't drop anything of note, so we pressed on in search of something more challenging to kill. We came across a city of undead, and, after wandering around for a bit killing random mobs, we found our way up to a boss - Mujaki the Devourer:





Pretty cool looking boss. Pity he wasn't really as hard as his evil looks might imply, although the encounter was certainly interesting.



I don't have loot shots from the above attempt, because shortly after he died the entire plane decided to crash. Some of the guild did manage to get back up there for another attempt once the plane had been revived, with the following results:


Grats Darkil!
Grats Wango!




We didn't find time to try any other stuff, however the general consensus seems to be that PoP is pretty damn cool overall. We still have quite a few members missing the expansion, so once they're all PoP equipped we will likely start attempting some of the harder content out there.





In other news..

Knocked off Aten again: Mask of Judgement - Redlance (Oops!), Great Shadow Platemail - Obfob, Tome of Secrets - Gladorr, Talisman of Vah Kerrath - Grongl, Ancient: Lullaby of Shadow - Poplypop. Also cleared out a bunch of the other VT bosses on the way to her, not entirely sure what dropped or who got it since I was apparently too lazy to make note of it at the time. If you're really interested in the sort of stuff that drops there, check out the past updates since most of the item stats are listed there.
