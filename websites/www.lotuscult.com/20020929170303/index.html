AHR Dead - again!

Chalked up our second kill of Aten recently, in what turned out to be a very productive day for the cult. We started off with a pretty routine emp kill - routine that is, if you discount the pea-soup like lag that had infested the zone. Waiting 10 seconds for a song to start isn't the best of fun, although the lag didn't seem to hamper our lovable necros in their endeavour to contribute any way possible:



It wasn't quite as clean a kill as usual, but the result was the same, and he coughed up some more nice toys for us:

  • Acrylia Handled Broadsword - Grats Cierin!
  • Azaliil's Ring of Analogies - Grats Kaleyn!
  • Gebron's Demented Cloak - Grats Cindyn!

Despite losing a few people to the evil sleep monster, we still had a score to settle with some gothic lookin chicks in Vex Thal, and the remaining 50-odd cultists weren't about to let them get away with that sense of fashion for much longer.

First stop was VXAHR, or mini-aten as she is better known. We (literally) got thrown about the place last attempt at her, but straight off an emp kill and revelling in the relatively low lag of VT, this time everything went to plan and we put her down almost flawlessly.
  • Cloudy Mark - Grats Poplypop!
  • Necklace of Endless Concepts - Grats Azile!
  • Earring of Battle - Grats Dachunker!



With half the raid falling asleep, we pressed on quickly to Aten herself. First thing that must be mentioned is that Aten is soooo much more fun without that lame AE blind. Thanks to VI for actually making this encounter more enjoyable and far less annoying. Second thing that must be mentioned - Our clerics rock! So does everyone else who was there to help, but the clerics really deserve a special mention for just being so damn cool (no matter what our grumpy dorfs might say). I'll probably get yelled at for giving out spoilers here, but keep your tanks alive and most encounters become much much easier. We didn't lose a single main tank for the lagfest of an emp kill, VXAHR, or AHR herself, and the reduction in stress levels when kills go that smoothly is very noticeable.
  • Wristband of Secrets - Grats Desmond!
  • Great Shadow Platemail - Grats Juilin!
  • Aegis of Judgement - Grats Samsonn!
  • Soul Essence of Aten Ha Ra - Grats Binedina!
  • Ancient: High Priest's Bulwark - Grats Brambodius!




Etc.
We also cleaned up the Cursed cycle earlier in the week, I don't have pictures since I was still fast asleep while it was taking place, but Hruga picked up some Braided Strands of Corrupted Mana and a Ssraeshzian Longbow from Glyphed, while Mcorrv scored a Band of Disrupted Aura's from Exiled. Cursed put out a Vyzh'dra's Render of Souls for Dachunker, a Cloak of Mourning for Chancy, and a Wistful Tunic of the Void for our very own gothic chick, Kinma.
