Two days of duplicity

Posted by   on Thursday, March 21 @ 14:26:25 EST (0 reads)

Everquest massive patches provide us with one unwavering constant in this pixilated world: a subsequent repatch! So, the last two days we spent running all around obliterating everything we could get our proverbial hands on before this morning’s patch. Tuesday was fraught with depravity. Our day’s onslaught commenced with sufficiency. We cleared Sleeper’s Tomb and bejeweled Quizer with a primal 1hs, Lbas with a primal 1hp, Mauleaur with a primal 2hs axe (8 of 8 active paladins now primalized thanx to fortuitous 2h primal acquirement), and Batuda picked up a scroll for Primal Avatar. We moved over to Kael afterwards to provide calamity unto the Avatar of War. Given the presence of another guild taking on Derakor and Statue, our momentum repelled.

I think he was smiling seeing how few we had:


Our attempt ensued with 50 members providing 3 perfect tank changes and getting him to near 25% before he began tearing through us like razors into wet tissue. We conducted the fastest CR ever but only got him to about 40% the second time. Alas, this is exactly why I love this battle so much: one minute foible can waste an entire raid of 50.

Wednesday proved to be quite antithetical to our effort’s the day before. Not only did we easily best three notable emissaries of beguilement, but we obtained numerous new items yet to be acquired in our ranks. The day began with a sforzando in the Plane of Growth, culminating with our second slaying of Tunare.

Insert some licentious caption here regarding the arrow placement


Once again, our tactic here proved to be flawless. Not a single death the entire fight, which objectively dissimilates her from her guards, thereby proving Guardian of Tunare > Tunare.


Among her hoard of duplicitous quality, only the branch was a repeat item for us. Pottz disarmed Tunare of her dagger, Nilaaxe unsheathed Tunare’s 2hs, Wynnter walked away with the new FT 5 shield, and Riptar captured the Sap Encrusted Branch. Finally, Trabious was awarded the Breath of Ro!



Before you start to comment about how my next few picture are too dark, remember that all of these people I am showing are evil, and thus only hide in dark places! Indeed, it could not be my complete ineptitude with taking pictures in EQ.

Nilaaxe with her new sword


Pottz with his telluric Dagger


Next on the agenda was a short trip to Kael for another dethroning of its monarch. The fight became interesting when the noble intentions of a Shaman incognito cast a few forays of Torpor on the main tank! Agro was thrown amuck. Nevertheless, we quickly regained control and obtained one of the better hauls from Tormax in some time (i.e., nothing rotted or went to alternates!).

Grongl’s soli


The head for FT 2 booties was decapitated by Darkweb, Rings of Lightning were awarded to Gompf and Shackmyn, the Velium Encrusted Gauntlets were bequeathed to Quixotim, and the Wrist Guard of Thunder is now worn by Colach.

Upon our postmortem review of Tormax, our sleuths received word that Cazic Thule was not only up, but the rest of his minions were already absconded. We quickly sent our troops in and, after the sacrificial killing of a paladin on the pull (despite the fact that he doesn’t Death Touch), we quickly thwarted his reign of Fear.

Apparently he doesn’t know that women don’t like hairy backs, especially spiky hair


We didn’t even believe the first person to inspect Cazic when she told us the two drops were a brain and robe. We’ve never acquired the brain and only had 2 robes in our numerous Cazic onslaughts. It appears as though the probability of these items dropping has increased, as the last 4 Cazics to fall on our server has dropped 3 brains and 2 robes. The brain was awarded to the main who needs another brain more than any other, Dunze. Jaysoun followed by dawning a new robe.

Jaysoun sports the Spring Fear Collection

Tormax, IV, Ring 10

Posted by   on Tuesday, March 19 @ 19:03:07 EST (2 reads)

Let be start out by welcome to new Cultists: Chancy (Axen's favorite woman!) and Shackmyn. Monday was the last day before a patch, thereby creating a necessary depravity in adventures. Nevertheless, a Kael reset provided us with another Tormax.


Tormax’s head was bestowed unto Transkor, the Rings of Lightning went to Valshoon and Menegorn (helm), Crown of the Kromzek Kings to Tattle, and The Horn of Hsagra went to one of Gimily’s alternative characters.

Afterward we headed to Akheva for another quick sortie against The Itraer Vius. This was a hair raising experience for Kinma. Nevertheless, he was quickly turned into corporeal matter.


The Collar of Tavuel Xakreum was awarded to Tunilo and Knosos obtained this new range item for the Cult.


Ultimately, we retired the evening with another guardianship of Thurgadin in order to obtain a Ring of Dain Frostreaver IV for Kailynn.



After a successful quelshing of the Thurgadin insurgence, Kailynn shared her hero-awarded treasures as follows: Eye or Narandi to Ssaseluv, FT 2 Helm to Trabious, Earring to Waken, Neck to Axxan, Mask to Kataisa, and Lance to Quixotim.

Creator, 10th Ring, and Rumblecrush

Posted by   on Saturday, March 16 @ 20:01:44 EST (3 reads)

I have just an update dictated by brevity today – crazy weekend here. Friday evening we went over for an early evening creator cremation.


Quizer walked about with the Shiny Trinket of Speed and we added yet another Mournfollow to our ranks, this time going to Trabious (> Quixotim).

From here we went and completed another epic protection of Thurgadin from an Kaelian insurgence. Dachunker was awarded the Ring of Dain Frostreaver IV this evening. She subsequently allotted raid provisions as follows: Eye of Narandi to Zolina, Mask of Bentos to Shadooh, Earring of Somethingorother to Yenelle, FT 1 Necklace to Gimily, FT 2 uber Helm to Mauleaur, and Narandi’s lance went to Nimol.

On Saturday we made, what I think is, our first attack on Rumblecrush in the Umbral Plains. I managed to get us all killed the first attempt, almost instantly! Afterwards, we quickly recouped and usurped the lands from the overgrown Mage Earth Pet.


The breastplate was awarded to Illuminaeta, necklace to Transkor, and the gloves went Fanielar.
