Day Eighty - Qeynos

I don’t think I have ever been as happy to see a city in all my young years. The smells that used to repulse me now seemed as welcome as scent of flowers in the plains of Karana. It seems ages since we left for the island but now we are back. I will carry the scar across my midriff as a constant reminder of the lessons I learned on the island but I believe that I have grown. I have learned the value of patience and the wisdom of following orders.

As I neared the Fish’s Ale for a bite to eat, a parchment nailed to the wall caught my eye. Jhared had already left to find the woman who had sent us on that godforsaken quest but I wish he had waited a while longer. He would have found this message quite interesting.

Hear ye, hear ye

By order of the high council of High Keep I am charged with seeking out brave adventurers for a most important task. Our Keep is under constant attack from both sides by two different factions. The Mucktail Gnolls have laid siege to our passageway to East Karana. Likewise, the Shralok Orcs have begun an assault on our passageway to Kithicor Woods.

Payment will be made for every orc scalp secured by Captain Ashlan and Volunteer Delharn. Likewise, Captain Orban will be paying for every gnoll scalp turned in.

I had thought we had crushed the orc hordes in Rivervale but it seems a new faction has arisen. Why does the name Shralok sound so familiar to me? Could it have been a name that Fyodor has spoken, or perhaps Grannith? These are questions that perhaps Jhared can answer. For now, eating doesn’t seem as such a high priority; I must track down Jhared and make him aware of this.
