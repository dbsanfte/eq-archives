Y.A.L.P. is yet another log parser for Sony's very successfull game EverQuest. If you don't know what a log parser is, you can think of if as a sort of file viewer.

Much like how your browser displays this web page, Y.A.L.P. will display the information from one of the log files that EverQuest generates as you play.

There are many log parsers available on the web, and each of them is a little different. Check out the screenshots of Y.A.L.P. for more detail.
 
Log parsers summarize the information found in your log files, and present it in a more readable way.

People want different things from log parsers, some people are more interested in finding conversation histories, others are more interested in seeing skill and faction changes, still others are more interested in damage information.

Good log parsers provide a number of these features, but no log parser can provide every desired feature for every user.
 
Y.A.L.P. is a robust little log parser that is extremely fast and provides the most thorough damage information of any log parser. In less than half the time that other log parsers produce statistics for your character, YALP will produce damage statistics for every single party in a log file. Y.A.L.P.'s unique party centric design enables it to provide a complete view of damage from the perspective of any party.
With Y.A.L.P., you can find the answer to questions like; "What was the damage per second that my friend Jonaz did to the Hill Giants that we fought together?", or "Who did the most damage to the Cyclops we ran into?". Other log parsers can't answer these questions because they simply don't have a full accounting of damage to and from every party.
Y.A.L.P. is really designed to be an offline parser for sort of a weekly or monthly review of how your character is comming along. Even though you can use Y.A.L.P. to parse a log file as large as 200 megs, I really suggest archiving your log file once a month or so, and starting fresh. This way the statistics that you see are kept fairly current.
So, Y.A.L.P. is fast, it provides great damage stats for everybody, it lets you view your log in color, it allows you to search your log for any text, and best of all, its free. If you like Y.A.L.P., please take the time to spread the word, and maybe even vote for it on the EverQuest Top 200 site. If you find that Y.A.L.P. does not provide what you really want in a log parser, then check out these other excellent log parsers.
