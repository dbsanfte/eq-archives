September 24th 2002
Version 2.2 released. Big changes for this version include HTML integration for printing and publishing reports, the addition of DPS, AD, and Kills to all damage lists, as well as the beginnings of a new Online mode for YALP. Please help me to make YALP the best EQ log parsing tool around by leaving your comments on the YALP forums.
 
September 4th 2002
I have had a lot of feedback from people using HITSMODE with short messages, saying that they are having trouble with Y.A.L.P. Currently for Y.A.L.P. to be able to find hits in your logs, you MUST use the full message text if you have hitsmode enabled. If you would like to help the situation, please send email to Verrant's Feedback Email address, asking them to always place full message text in the log files. People who dont use log files wont be affected, and people who do use log files probably want the maximum information possible.

 
August 22nd 2002
Test version of Y.A.L.P. made available. This is a test of new DPS and Attack Delay calculations right in the damage objects. Both attack delay and damage per second are produced on each and every damage list. This works at an overall summary level, as well as on a per fight basis. I think that this change will benefit most users, even if it comes at the cost of a little speed.

 
June 24th 2002
Version 2.1 released. Big changes for this version inlcude full third party damage information in the fight list, the ability to customize screen fonts, and the removal of the third party damage toggle. Now Y.A.L.P. will always collect full third party damage information from your log files.
