Hosted By
The hosting service provided by EverQuest Head Quarters is nothing short from the best. Our mission is to build a site that has the community in mind and therefore wishes to encourage the community by offering this service. As a hosted site you will receive a variety of benefits (listed below). You can most likely find eqhq staff in our irc channel on the gamesnet.net servers (#eqhq). Feel free to stop by and get to know us. Interested persons should read this entire page before inquiring about hosting.

  • Subdomains - Subdomains are exacly what they say, a SUB domain. Instead of your site being a directory, http://www.eqhq.com/yoursite, you get a subdomain, http://yoursite.eqhq.com.

  • Unlimited Webspace/FTP Space - You have pretty much as much space as you'll ever need as long as you need it for your site. Also eqhq.com's FTP server is mirrored by CDrom.com, Saix.net (South Africa), livewire.com.au (Australia), and soon the UK, so there are always numerous places for people to download your files from.

  • Our Network - Bandwidth is never an issue with eqhq.com, we have three tier one connections to UUNet (DS3), MCI(2 T1s) and Sprint(T1) and a CISCO 7500 router is connected to our three servers.

  • Promoting Your Site - With eqhq.com your site is listed on every page on eqhq.com & TeleNation! That's right, no messy pull-down system on our main page, but rather a button bar place everywhere, front and center, guaranteeing great promotion. Also our recently updated sites system ensures that day or night people will be able to get news on your site instantly.

  • E-Mail - Your site can have multiple e-mail addresses on our server, so you can use yourname@eqhq.com. We can setup full e-mail accounts or it can forward to your current address. Mailing lists aren't a problem either.

  • The Server - eqhq.com is located on a Unix machine and through the Unix machine we offer Frontpage Extensions to allow easier updates if you so desire it. We also offer Perl, php, postgres database support, and we support SSI (Sorry but we are unable to offer Cgi-Bins to guild sites).

  • Personal Touch - We try to work with the sites we host as much as possible. Whether it's setting up a cgi script, designing a new layout, or doing a logo. We want the sites we host to be the best in what ever area they cover. Chances are if you need something we can get it done for you. Someone is always available to answer a question you might have or to help you set something up. Basically we will do everything we possibly can to make your site the best!

What we are looking for

We look for a number of things in a future eqhq.com web site, some being more important then others.

  • Content - This is very important, if your site has crummy graphics, but great content, you have a good chance of getting hosted. Another important issue is that we’re interested in EverQuest content. If it’s good for the EverQuest community, it’s good for us! If you’re not sure if a site is appropriate, just ask.

  • Commitment - This is another important issue. Depending on the nature of your audience, you might not have a reason to post something every day. However, we expect your site to be updated as frequently as necessary, with enough fresh content to continue to interest readers. Sites that appear to be abanonded/neglected will be contacted via email and possibly removed.

  • Banners - This is a must, and will need to be put at the top of your main pages. Just place our banner code at the top of your pages, and you’re set!

  • Graphical Design/Layout - We consider this last, and we don't place much emphasis on it, however, your site needs to look professional. Keep in mind that you don't need a professional artist for a good page, many excellent pages have few or no graphics.

Now you're ready

Think you have the content we're looking for? Are you ready to be part of eqhq.com? Great! Just follow these simple steps and you will be on your way.

  • Step 1 - We require to see your site before we agree to host it. Have your site uploaded to an account somewhere were we can access it. We rarely accept proposals for sites that don't exist yet; set up an account at Geocities, XOOM, or some other provider and start coding pages so that we have something to look at before dropping us a note.

  • Step 2 - E-mail hosting@eqhq.com and be sure to include an URL to the site.


Again we would like to thank you for taking the time to apply for hosting at eqhq.com. After all, eqhq.com exists because of your sites.
