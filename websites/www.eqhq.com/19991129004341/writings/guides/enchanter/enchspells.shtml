Level 1

Lull

This is the first in the line of spells which calm your enemy. This spell can be used to lower your opponents Aggro and most likely prevent him from attacking you.

Minor Shielding

This is the first in the line of the spellcasters shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It is a self only spell.

Pendril's Animation

This spell will summon an animated pet that can is similar to the pets of a magician or necromancer only and can’t be commanded with pet commands.

Sense Magic

This spell will give you information on magic items.

Shallow Breath

This spell is a DoT that not only damages your opponent but also lowers his/her strength.

Strengthen

This buffing spell adds strength to your strength score.

Taper Enchantment

This allows you to break out of or shorten the duration of an enchantment. This means for instance, if you are charmed, you might snap out of it quicker.

Weaken

This debuffing spell lowers a a monster’s strength meaning less damage.

Level 4

Color Flux

This is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster and is deadly like most AoE spells.

Enfeeblement

This spell will lower your opponents strength, more costly then weaken though.

Fear

Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. A note, he might bring back friends so be prepared.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Haze

This is an armor class buffing spell.

Illusion: Half Elf

This spell will allow you to appear as if you were a half elf to others. It will even effect your appearance if they do a /who on you. All NPC’s will react to you as if you were a Half-Elf. The caster also receives as infravision as the illusion bonus.

Illusion: Human

This spell will make you appear to be a human.

Invisibility

Turns you invisible for a good amount of time. It lets you infiltrate areas to spy or get your corpse, or travel through an area without getting molested.

Mesmerize

Mesmerize is the enchanters version of the calm spell. This spell will cause a monster to begin mesmerized and to refrain from attacking a person during battle.

Suffocating Sphere

Suffocating Sphere does initial damage, then DoT. It alsolowers the victims strength.

Tashan

This spell will lower your targets magic resistance.

Level 8

Alliance

This spell will raise your faction with the particular NPCs Race for the duration of the spell.

Bind Sight

This spell will allow you to look through another being’s eyes. The caster will slowly lose stamina while using this spell.

Cancel Magic

This spell will cancel a magical effect on you or one of your part members, such as charm.

Chaotic Feedback

Chaotic feedback is a direct damage offensive spell with a minor stun.

Enchant Silver

This spell is used to enchant silver necessary in magical jewelry creation.

Eye Of Confusion

This spell will make your target run around aimlessly for a short duration. It is similar to fear except that the creature will run in different directions.

Illusion: Gnome, Wood Elf

These spells allow you to appear to be a gnome or a wood elf. These two illusions give you infravision as well and the races treat you as that race illusion.

Lesser Shielding

Lesser shielding is an upgrade to the minor shielding spell. It adds armor class and hit points.

Mircyl's Animation

Mircyl's animation is an upgrade to Pendril's animation. It summons a more powerful animation.

Root

This spell will root a being in place to prevent the creature from running after you or running away.

See Invisible

This spell allows you to see invisible beings.

Sentinel

This spell creates a sphere around the caster and will alert you whenever another enters the spehere.

Soothe

Soothe is an upgrade to the lull spell. It makes a monster less aggressive.

Level 12

Bind Affinity

This allows you to change your bind location where you appear or respawn from.

Charm

This spell allows you to charm another being and use it as a pet. Charm spells are very unpredictable so use them with caution. The charmed pets follow normal pet commands.

Choke

This is a DoT spell which also lowers your opponents strength.

Ebbing Strength

Ebbing strength is a debuff spell which is used to weaken your opponents.

Enduring Breath

Enduring breath is a water breathing spell which can be cast on yourself or others.

Illusion: Dark Elf, Erudite, Halfling

More illusions, same as previous, except that dark elves get ultravision.

Languid Pace

This spell will cause your target to attack more slowly.

Memory Blur

Memory blur is used to wipe a NPC’s aggro.

Mist

Mist is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Serpent Sight

Serpent sight grants you infravision.

Whirl Til You Hurl

This spell will root a monster and cause it to spin in place, which makes it difficult for him to hit you.

Level 16

Chase The Moon

Chase the moon is an improved fear spell.

Disempower

Disempower is a great debuffing spell which will lower your targets strength, armor class and drain his stamina away.

Enchant Electrum

This is an upgrade to the enchant silver spell.

Enthrall

This is an upgrade to the mesmerize spell. It acts as a calming effect on your target.

Levitate

Levitate allows you to levitate above ground. This is useful for traveling over bodies of water or lava, such as in Solusek. It also prevents you from being knocked from ledges, such as in Kelethin.

Identify

This spell identifies a magical item.

Illusion: Barbarian, Dwarf, Tree

More illusion spells. Barbarian gives you bash, Dwarf infravision, and Tree faster regeneration.

Mesmerization

Mesmerization is an area effect mesmerize spell, it works great for getting you out of other AoE spells you have previously cast.

Quickness

This spell adds to a players attack speed.

Rune I

Rune spells rule in groups. These spells can be cast on yourself or others and allow a player to absorb a certain amount of damage without actually being hurt. The regeant is a cat’s eye agate.

Sanity Warp

Sanity warp is a direct damage spell, and upgrade to Choatic Feedback.

Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor shielding spells. It adds armor class, hit points and magic resistance to yourself only.

Level 20

Berserker Strength

This spell adds to your targets strength, but lowers their agility. It also gives a temporary invulnerability similar to the rune spell.

Calm

Calm is an upgrade to the lull line of spells. It lowers your targets aggro.

Cloud

Cloud is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Color Shift

Color shift is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster, be warned, like all AoE spells, it can be very deadly.

Endure Magic

This spell adds to a players magic resistance.

Feckless Might

This spell is a debuff which will drain a monsters strength and dexteriry.

Illusion: Ogre, Troll

More illusions. The troll gives slam, regeneration, and infravision, and the ogre gets slam and infravision. These work really well among non-evil races.

Shifting Sight

This is an upgrade to the bind sight spell. It allows the caster to see through the target's eyes using infravision.

Sympathetic Aura

Sympathetic aura adds to a players charisma score.

Tashani

This is an upgrade to the tashan spell. It lowers your opponents magic resistance.

Level 24

Alacrity

This is an upgrade to the quickness spell. It will allow your target to attack quite a bit faster and has a longer duration.

Beguile

Beguile is an upgrade to the charm spell.

Chaos Flux

This is a direct damage offensive spell, and upgrade to the Choatic Feedback line.

Enchant Gold

Enchant Gold is an upgrade to the enchant silver line of enchantment spells.

Illusion: Earth Element, Skeleton

More illusions. The Earth elemental adds Strength and faction with elementals, the skeleton adds faction with the dead.

Invigor

Invigor will restore a players stamina.

Major Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor shielding line of spells.

Rune II

Rune II is an upgrade to Rune I. It absorbs more damage than Rune and requires a more expensive component then Rune I.

Strip Enchantment

This spell will allow you to remove all magic effects cast on yourself. It will remove one or two spells cast on another party member as well.

Tepid Deeds

This is an upgrade to the languid pace spell. It will slow your opponent.

Level 29

Ally

This is an upgrade to the alliance spell. It increases your faction with your target’s race.

Augmentation

This is an upgrade to the strength spell. It also adds armor class.

Clarity

This spell increases a players mana regeneration rate. It is one of the best spells in the game.

Curse Of The Simple Mind

This spell is an intelligence and wisdom debuff.

Dynn's Dizzying Draught

This is an upgrade to the Whirl til you Hurl spell.

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. It has a longer duration but requires more mana as well.

Feedback

Feedback is a damage shield.

Illusion: Air Element, Water Element

More illusions. The Air Elemental adds levitation, the Water Elemental adds Enduring Breath.

Listless Power

This is an upgrade to the ebbing strength spell. It weakens your opponent.

Nullify Magic

This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Obscure

Obscure is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Suffocate

Suffocate is an upgrade to the choke spells. It lowers your targets strength and is also a DoT.

Ultravision

This spell gives you perfect nightvision, called ultravision.

Whirl Til You Hurl II

This is an upgrade to the Whirl Til You Hurl spell.

Level 34

Anarchy

Anarchy is a direct damage spell. It is an upgrade in the CF line of spells.

Cast Sight

This is an upgrade to the bind sight line of spells. It also grants you infravision.

Enchant Platinum

This is an upgrade to the enchant silver line of enchantmentspell.

Entrance

This is an upgrade to the stun line of spells.

Greater Shielding

This is an upgrade to the shielding line of spells. It is a self only.

Illusion: Fire Elemental

This allows you to take the form of a fire element, which gives you a damage shield.

Insipid Weakness

This spell weakens the target and also lowers their armor class.

Mana Sieve

This spell, when cast, depletes your targets mana.

Radiant Visage

This is an upgrade to the sympathetic aura line of spells, which adds charisma.

Rune III

This is an upgrade to the Rune line of spells, and requires a more expensive component.

Level 39

Cajoling Whispers

This is an upgrade to the charm line of spells.

Celerity

This is an upgrade to the quickness line of spells which increase attack speed.

Gravity Flux

This is an area effect direct damage and stun spell.

Illusion: Dry Bone, Spirit Wolf

More illusions. The Dry Bone adds undead faction, the Spirit Wolf quickens your attack speed.

Immobilize

This is a root spell.

Invoke Fear

Invoke fear is a more powerful fear spell which will cause your opponent to run away.

Insight

This is an intelligence buff.

Mind Wipe

This spell will make a monster forget that you are on it's aggro list, like a memory blur.

Pacify

This is a more powerful version of the lull line of spells.

Rampage

Rampage is an upgrade to the beserker strength line of spells.

Resist Magic

This will add to a players magic resistance.

Shade

Shade is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Level 44

Arch Shielding

Arch shielding is the last in the line of shielding spells. It is self only.

Brilliance

Brilliance is an intelligence buffing spell.

Color Skew

This is an upgrade to the color shift spell.

Discordant Mind

This is a direct damage spell, in the line of CF.

Extinguish Fatigue

Extinguish fatigue restores a players stamina.

Incapacitate

This is an upgrade to the root line of spells.

Pillage Enchantment

This spell will allow you to break out of a charm spell.

Rune IV

This is the last in the line of Rune spells.

Shifting Deeds

This is an upgrade to the tepid deeds spell. This slows an attackers attack rate.

Weakness

This spell will weaken your target considerably.

Level 49

Adorning Grace

Increases the target’s charisma.

Allure

Allure is the last in the line of charm spells.

Blanket Of Forgetfullness

This is an AoE mesmerize and memory blur.

Dazzle

The spell distracts the target and wipes its aggressor list.

Gasping Embrace

This is a DoT spell in the shallow breath line.

Group Resist Magic

This spell adds to your entire groups resistance to magic.

Paralyzing Earth

This is an AoE root spell.

Reoccuring Amnesia

This is a persistent memory blur.

Shadow

Shadow is the final upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Soul Bond

I don’t know what this spell does J .

Swift Like The Wind

This is the last in the line of the quickness line of spells.
