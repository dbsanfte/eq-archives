You can send a Money Order, Cashiers Check or Personal Check to get your copy of the guide. Money Orders and Cashiers Checks will be processed as soon as they are received in the office, Personal Checks will need to clear the bank before we can ship the guide. Follow these steps to purchase the guide when using a Money Order/Cashiers Check/Personal Check:

Step 1. Have your check made out to RPG Secrets.

Step 2. Fill in the amount for the guide:

  • $24.99 for the email version only

  • $29.99 for email version and on CD ROM within US

  • $30.99 for email version and on CD ROM to Canada

  • $32.99 for email version and on CD ROM International shipping

Step 3. Include your email address and server with the payment.

Step 4. Ship to:

RPG Secrets
239 Vine Avenue
Park Ridge, IL 60068
Attn: Mike Szerlong

Once your check has arrived, you will get an email from us with instructions on how to use the guide. If you use a personal check, you will get an email letting you know we got your check and deposited it into the bank and waiting for it to clear, followed by the information email with the guide.
