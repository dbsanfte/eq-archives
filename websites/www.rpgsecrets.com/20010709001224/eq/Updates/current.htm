Current Projects as of July 6, 2001

Here is a list of all the current projects being worked on, and soon to be out for the The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest! Our Items Database program is now out as many of you know (click here for more information on it) - we are now getting ready to add all the items from the expansions (Kunark and Velious) very soon.

 

Here is the list of current projects already underway at RPG Secrets.

  
 
  • Dungeon Walkthrough's for Kunark (including Dalnir, Karnor's Castle and Sebilis) are in the works.
  • The Class Guides will continue as well once we get this program out of the way, and focus on new power leveling - especially now with the new increased experience in certain Zones added in a recent patch. We will also discuss grouping more due to higher experience bonus's as well. (Enchanter Guide by Ibsen was release on 6/14/01).
  • Adding items from Kunark and Velious into the Items Database Program as well as a new Race search.
  • We have started the work on the Velious guide now - should be out shortly.

Those are the current projects of course, not everything we are planning for The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest!, but this will be a way for you to keep track of what is coming from this point on. If you still have questions, feel free to email me at mike@RPGSecrets.com.
