Old news from last site:

December 16, 2002 - Almost 2 months since the expansion has been released, and it has been a fantastic addition to the game of EverQuest. So far, we have these zones almost complete for the next guide (due to release shortly):

  • Plane of Knowledge

  • Plane of Tranquility

  • Plane of Disease

  • Plane of Nightmare

  • Plane of Justice

  • Plane of Valor

  • Plane of Storms

  • Plane of Innovation

  • Terris Thule's Lair

  • Halls of Honor

  • Plane of Tactics

  • Bastion of Thunder
     

Because of the new increased levels and spells with Planes of Power, we will be updating all the other guides as well. We still have a new website that will launch this week (finally - we spent so much time in PoP, this site was neglected for a while). We also have many updated samples to show (our current samples on this site are no longer up to date). Stay tuned for more information soon!

October 21, 2002 - The Planes of Power is here! In fact, if you have not heard yet, you can purchase one today by going to your local Walmart (yes, they beat EB). Most EB stores will not get them in until tomorrow.

October 16, 2002 - Today is patch day, and this will be the final patch (or should be) until the Planes of Power goes in next week. Our new website was delayed, but will be going in this week. For those level 60 casters with AA points, make sure to check your AA after today's patch - you may have been reimbursed AA points you need to spend. Remember you can not have over 30 extra AA's once the Planes of Power comes out next week.

October 2, 2002 - After many emails, we have decided to post here that we will be coming out with a Planes of Power guide, and we do not expect it to be released until the end of November. We also have a new website that will give much more information about our current guides along with new membership and customer support pages.  We have not updated our samples in so long, our samples are even outdated at this point. We will have more news next week when we launch the new web design.
