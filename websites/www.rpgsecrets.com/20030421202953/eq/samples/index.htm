 The release that started it all - EverQuest Classic.

The first expansion to come out, which increased levels up to 60 and the introduction of the Iksar.

A new icy world that added new Dwarves, Dragons and Giants.

This expansion takes place away from Norrath, and on the moon!

Up until now we could only see a few God's - this expansion changes that and gives you the chance to meet nearly all the God's

The Legacy of Ykesha is the latest expansion for EQ - includes new zones, a new item slot, and of course a new playable race, Frogloks!


EverQuest Classic, Kunark, Velious and Luclin are all in one Guide package. This also includes Class Guides, How to make Platinum Guide, Tradeskills Guide (including new Planes of Power and Legacy of Ykesha info), Resource Guide (including spell information through LoY), and Item information.

The Planes of Power Guide will be the only separate purchase when it comes out due to it's massive amount of information. The Legacy of Ykesha will be included in the Planes of Power Guide. This guide will deal directly with all the zones of PoP and LoY - including all Flag information up to Plane of Time. We are also including guides on how to obtain all the new charms available in LoY. All current members will receive a discount on this guide when it becomes available. The price will be posted when it gets closer to release.
