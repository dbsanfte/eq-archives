Welcome to The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest! (US-EQ) This site is dedicated to bringing you the very best EQ guide around. If you are looking for complete information on the following, you have come to the right place:

  • Power Leveling
  • Dungeon Walkthrough’s (Zone guides)
  • Item Database
  • Walkthrough’s on all Quests (including Kunark and Velious)
  • Detailed Maps
  • NPC information that includes Location, Hit Points and Class
  • Mastering Trade Skills and complete tables on each
  • Faction info on NPC’s and how to change your Factions
  • Melee Disciplines
  • Free Updates
  • And More!

The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest first came out in August of 1999 and started with only 50 pages and a great idea – to finally have a guide that can keep up with EverQuest. As of March 2001, US-EQ is over 800 pages and growing. Be sure to check out our samples for the guide and we hope you enjoy reading and using US-EQ as much as we enjoy writing and keeping you updated!

--RPG Secrets Staff
