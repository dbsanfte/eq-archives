There are 4 payment options to obtain The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest!
  1. PayPal using your credit card (includes some international countries as well)
  2. Billpoint using your Credit Card or Personal Check (international Credit Cards allowed)
  3. Money Order sent directly to RPG Secrets.
  4. C2it Citibank accepts Visa and MasterCard (including international)

Please choose the best option to meet your need. The price for The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest is $24.99 and includes all updates for life of the guide. There is an optional CD ROM that gives you all the same files in the download version of US-EQ as well. 

The charge for the CD ROM is dependent on your location and includes all shipping and handling charges:

  • If you live in the United States, the optional CD ROM is $5.
  • For those that live in Canada, the cost is $6.
  • For all International orders, the CD ROM is $8 

Once your order is processed, you will receive The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest by email (even if you order the optional CD ROM) - we will send you links to download the files, not the actual files. The download is currently just under 40 Megabytes and is broken down into files under 2.5 Megabytes each. The email you use will be added to our list server for all future updates. All updates are free of charge, and this includes EverQuest, EverQuest: Kunark, EverQuest: Velious and any future expansions for EverQuest!
