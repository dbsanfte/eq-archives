The Planes of Power is the 4th expansion to EverQuest and the idea behind this expansion was to expand on fighting more of the God's as we had seen in such older zones as Plane of Hate, Plane of Fear and Plane of Growth. New ways were introduced to enter each zone (known as flags) to work your way up the ladder in Planes of Power. These zones are setup for the 50+ level players - however, the Plane of Knowledge is open to all with a wonderful new portal system to quickly get around the world in no time.
