Welcome to The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest! (US-EQ) This site is dedicated to bringing you the very best EQ guide around. If you are looking for complete information on the following, you have come to the right place:

  • Power Leveling
  • Dungeon Walkthrough’s (Zone guides)
  • Item Database
  • Walkthrough’s on all Quests (including Kunark, Velious and Epic Quests!)
  • Detailed Maps
  • NPC information that includes Location, Hit Points and Class
  • Mastering Trade Skills and complete tables on each
  • Faction info on NPC’s and how to change your Factions
  • Melee Disciplines
  • How to make Platinum Guide
  • Free Updates
  • And More!

The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest first came out in August of 1999 and started with only 50 pages and a great idea – to finally have a guide that can keep up with EverQuest. As of June 2001, US-EQ is over 1000 pages and growing. Be sure to check out our samples for the guide and we hope you enjoy reading and using US-EQ as much as we enjoy writing and keeping you updated!

--RPG Secrets Staff


Current News and Update Information

June 1, 2001 - New Update! We added Foraging and Fishing to the Trade Skills guide today. We also had to update the Platinum Guide since there was a nerf as of the patch on May 30, 2001. You can now only make up to 100 Platinum per hour (still very good for a level 5). (Current members can re-download the Trade Skills Guide to get this update)

May 25, 2001 - New Update! We added 9 of the 12 Epic Quests to the main guide. This includes Bard, Cleric, Druid, Enchanter, Magician, Monk, Paladin, Ranger and Warrior - all complete walkthrough's for each Epic Quest. Shadowknight, Shaman and Wizard will be added in the next update. (Current members can re-download the main US-EQ guide to get this update)

May 17, 2001 - We have started using Adobe Acrobat 5.0 since our last update on May 15th, so be sure to download the latest version of the free reader for use with the guide. The link for Adobe Acrobat 5.0 can be found here.

May 15, 2001 - New Update! We added a small update to the main guide for Power Leveling using a Quest! You will need Velious in order for this to work, but you will level very quickly while making hundreds of plat per hour. If done right, you can level from 1-25 in one day! (Current members can re-download the main US-EQ guide to get this update) (As of May 30th, this quest will no longer produce fast leveling)

April 27, 2001 - New Update! We have added a guide to help you make Unlimited Platinum! All you need is a level 5 character and you can make up to 250pp an hour! (Current members can re-download the Trade Skills guide to get this update)

April 19, 2001 - As many of you know already, Sony announced a new expansion for EverQuest called the Shadows of Luclin. RPG Secrets will start writing the guide for SoL when it comes out in December 2001.

April 2, 2001 - Our new USEQ Items Program and Trade Skill Guide has been released! You can view more info in  Current Projects for now, we will add new pages under samples soon.
