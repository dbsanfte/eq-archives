RPG Secrets Frequently Asked Questions

 

Does RPG Secrets accept PayPal, BidPay, C2it, Money Order or Check?
We accept all of these forms of payment, however if you are using a personal check, it can take up to 10 days to clear once we receive it. We also accept Money Orders and Cashiers Checks by mail and there is no waiting period before shipping. (eBay recently purchased PayPal, so eBay Payments will be replaced by PayPal in January 2003).

When will my product ship?
As soon as your guide is ordered and confirmation of payment is made, RPG Secrets will send you an email with all the current links to download the latest version of the new guide. Customers that use their credit card through any of the payment services we offer will receive the email the same day. Those that opt to use a personal check through PayPal will need to wait until the confirmation has been completed (takes 3-4 days).

If you order the optional CD ROM, it will ship by US Mail after the next update.

I live outside the United States, can I still get the optional CD ROM?
RPG Secrets does accept orders outside of the U.S., please check below for additional shipping information.

What are the shipping costs?
The costs for shipping are as follows based on your location:

  • If you live in the United States, the optional CD ROM is $5

  • For those that live in Canada, the CD ROM is $6

  • For all International orders, the CD ROM is $8

The price also includes the CD and handling as well as shipping.

Will I receive a confirmation that my product has been shipped?
RPG Secrets believes in the importance of good communication with our customers, especially when orders have been placed. After you complete your order, we will send you an e-mail verifying that your order was received and is being processed. We will also keep you informed via email of all action being taken on your order after it's processed. Please be sure to check your e-mail regularly.

How often does RPG Secrets update The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest?
This always depends on the current changes to EQ. Currently, we are working on the Planes of Power and Legacy of Ykesha Guides. Some months you may get 3 updates a week, while other times only 2 updates will come out the entire month.

How do I get updates for The Unauthorized Secrets of EverQuest?
Once you pay for your guide, you are added to the update email list for all future updates. Each time RPG Secrets puts out an update, you get an email automatically with the new links of the updated information. This can range from new guides, updated guides, maps, or new software.

Do I need any additional software to run the guide?
We use Adobe Acrobat 5.0 to ensure everyone is able to read the guide without any problems. Adobe Acrobat 5.0 reader is a free download from Adobe (we give you the link in the email we send you). You will also need the free copy of WinZip (we also supply you with that link).

Can I give this as a gift?
Yes. If you want to give the guide as a gift and would like us to hold the email until that date (or a date of your choice), please contact us before purchase at  info@rpgsecrets.com or 630-778-9118 so we can arrange that for you. We can include a personal greeting within that email.
