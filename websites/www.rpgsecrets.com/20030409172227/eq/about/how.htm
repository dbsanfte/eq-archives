While playing in the beta of EverQuest in 1998, we realized the need for a solid strategy guide, but not just your typical strategy hard copy book - EQ changed everyday in beta, and would always continue to change for the life of the game - a guide would have to be able to do the same.

In early 1999, RPG Secrets was formed and the very first guide was on its way. From phase 4 of beta to the final release of EQ, we had the layout set and ready to go into production. After hitting level 50, it would only take a few months and the first EQ Guide of its kind would come out in August '99 - even though it was only 50 pages, it was a huge success.

Over the three years,  The Unauthorized Secrets of EQ would grow into a huge book - over 1000 pages in fact (and still growing now!). We are implementing new programs to help make this an even greater value. As customers can tell you that bought the guide in August '99, all updates are free.

The founder of RPG Secrets, Mike Szerlong, has been in the gaming industry for over 14 years with experience in writing for popular gaming publications and play testing. His love for EverQuest sparked the new idea for a guide that would be able to handle the amount of changes EQ goes through on a weekly basis.

RPG Secrets will only write for games that are noteworthy of long term play. We played Anarchy Online and Dark Age of Camelot, but did not write a guide on those two titles - fun to play, but we did not see the need for a constantly updated guide. EverQuest II and Star Wars Galaxies are on our list, and we will check out Shadowbane. We will keep you posted as new games come out.
