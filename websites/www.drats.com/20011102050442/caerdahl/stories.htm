Just another day

 

A high elf maid and a high elf man journey to a faraway land.

Setting beneath a mighty tree, a dwarf joins them and they are three.

Standing on the grass so green, fightng all that is seen. Hacking, slashing, casting fire, defeating foes is our desire! Alas! Alas!

Then what is seen?

Ugly, ugly goblins green!

They bite, they kick they blast us too! The group, the three, they are slew!

Sadly, sadly home to mend.

Beware goblins! We come again!

 

By the High Elf Wizard Lillae
