PLAYER Name: Alex Namba

Place: Seattle, WA Occupation:

Software Design Engineer E-mail: katsun@microsoft.com

Profile: I came to Seattle 6 years ago from Japan (so bear with me if I make grammatical errors) :). I have been a role playing game fanatic since Wizardry 1 (never was into Ultima, I don't know why). I even enjoyed playing a wizard in table talk RPG back in Japan long time ago. I always thought I was magic user type so I first started EverQuest as an Erudite wizard on other server, but I stopped at level 4 because it didn't feel right for me. So I started a cleric on Tunare and now I'm loving it. It has been a great feeling that I can actually help out people who are in danger. Of course I sometimes envy all the powers that wizards and other casters have, but I can say I'm totally satisfied with my cleric. Rolant is my one and only character now. I have met great people from EverQuest (Enthalpy, Laradorm, we had fun hunting those zombie sailors!), and I'm looking forward to the future adventures with people I'm meeting from the guild (Moshi, Josalea, I can't wait to go back to Unrest with you!).

CHARACTER Name: Rolant Starfield Race: Human Class: Level 26 Cleric of Rodcet Nife Profile: He was born and raised in Qeynos. His mother died when he was still an infant. His father was also a cleric of Rodcet Nife but was killed by a dark elf when Rolant was 12. He had had hostile feelings toward dark elves since his father's death, but when another dark elf saved his life when he was 18, he realized prejudice does nothing good. He is glad that he chose the way of becoming a cleric of Rodcet Nife like his father, and takes his pride in healing people. He doesn't talk much, but he loves people who travel with him. Though he hasn't been able to do for a long time, he likes to cook when he has some free time. His specialty is fish fillet, he actually used to make some money cooking them in the past. He is thinking about starting brewing because he likes to drink! Learning other languages is one of his favorites, too.
