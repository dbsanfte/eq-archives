January 19th 2000

Sensible shoes with purple buckles.

What could be better? I'm in doubt as to whether everyone knows how to find this site, so ask each other and get the site addy dispursed. Also, I have seen guild recruiting tapering off quite a bit recently. I know there are still a lot of really great people out there that we could help and provide a home for. Let me know when you meet them.. post a message here, get a guild officer. The guild requirements are simple. it has to be someone you have hunted with, someone you want to be friends with, someone you will sponsor. Any guildie can sponsor someone into the guild, but officers have discretion on inducting. That brings up the next point. We have 4 guild officers. Belgarian, the inductor. Laradorm, the sometimes active loremaster. Josalea who does LOTS of the planning and working on problems in the guild. And Bhasher, treasurer retired from the treasury business. The truth is that a lot of guildies have made other significent contributions as well, Elvys is the guild historian, get his e-mail and send him your tales as well. There's more to be done to offer everyone more fun in Norrath. I'd like to have a battle master, a quartermaster, and a few trivia knowlege quests with prizes. And most of all, I'd like your input on what would make this more fun, better for all, and better for you. Thanks again for all the work of putting this site together, it's outstanding.

Moshi,

first servent to the Caer'dahl.
