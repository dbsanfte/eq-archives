Bio of Josalea, gnome cleric

I was born in the far away land of Ak’Anon. It was a lonely childhood at first, being a farely empty land. But mighty beings from distance places came to my aid, and I grew quickly. I traveled from my homeland and explored many new places, only to learn that there was even more to see. I quickly grew in strength and power depopulating the dreaded Orcs of the Deathfist Clan in the Commons Lands. It is an honor for me to be both a member and an Officer of the mighty Caer’dahl, the House of Lords. I take is as a serious and rewarding position and as Officer in charge of Guild Events and New Members and I shall fulfill my duties with a happy heart. Should you need my aid, do not hesitate to call on me.

Guild Officer Josalea

Gnome Cleric
