Rumpus Room

 

The House of Nobles,

the Caer'dahl,

We are the friends that have gathered under this simple banner.

Welcome to our homes, and know that a greeting to us is returned with sincerity and kindness.

Never seeking for glory nor self-aggrandisement, we offer freely of our companionship, knowlege, and skills.

With humility and grace, we wander Norrath, finding humor and laughter along the way.

If you value friendship above platinum, a grand hunt more then a trinket, and believe that life is a game, and games should be filled with that precious commodity, fun, then you will be welcome among us.

The journey itself is what brings meaning to the achievement of a goal.

The people met along the way are a greater treasure then the journey and the goal both.

May you find what you seek, and learn to seek that which is worth having.

 

Moshi

First Servant to the Caer'dahl
