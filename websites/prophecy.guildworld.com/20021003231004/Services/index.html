Services

Syspro’s STARS (Structured Technique to Achieve a Rapid Solution) is a unique multi-volume library that speeds the structured implementation of Syspro’s enterprise software. STARS is also a catalyst to enhance overall businesses efficiency. By assisting organizations in defining business processes and flows, solid and dotted lines of communication and reporting, management spans and other controls, STARS enables a business to quickly reap the benefits of IMPACT Encore and achieve renewed operational effectiveness. Additionally, STARS serves as a central repository of system related data and a foundation for training, future system upgrades and continuity in the event of personnel turnover.

Syspro’s integrated Professional Services Group provides world-class support to meet the demanding needs of customers and channel partners. The group offers a variety of technical support services to assist users, resellers and consultants, including “hot line” telephone support as well as fax and email support.

Syspro’s interactive On-Line Web Support System is an easy to use tool for resolving issues. Three methods are progressively available to resolve problems. The highest level prompts an email to the Syspro support staff. Resolved issues are automatically added to a searchable database of frequently asked questions (FAQs) for future use.

Training is available on-site, at customer locations through local authorized Syspro resellers and consultants and in a classroom setting at Syspro locations. Each participant is provided with comprehensive workshop materials.
