Quests listed alphabetically according to the item rewarded:

 

Acumen Mask Quest

1) speak to Vilissia in the Temple of Solusek then you will be given the quest.

2) go to Lower Guk and get a glowing mask from the skeletal monk.

3) go to Unrest and get a darkbone skull from the darkbone skeleton.

4) go to the Ocean of Tears and kill the Goblin Headmaster to get the bonechipped mask.

5) Kill a Shadowed Man to get a patch of shadow.

6) Take all of these items to Vilissia and she will give you a Acumen Mask.

 

 

 

 

Avenger Battle Axe Quest

1) Head to Kaladim and visit the Warriors' Guild. Talk to Beno. He will give you a letter to take to Highhold Keep.

2) Head to Highhold Keep and give the letter to the Jail Clerk. He will send you off to Misty Thicket and give you a container.

3) Talk to Bronin in Misty Thicket. This will trigger Slaythe to spawn. Kill Slaythe and he will drop a leg that you must return to Bronin.

4) Bronin will give you a key. Give the key to the bank clerk in Highhold Keep. The Bank Clerk will give you a shard of the eye of stormhammer.

5) Head to Qeynos. Go to Crow's Bar. Faldor is a rare spawn but when he finally appears kill him and you will get the other shard.

6) Combine the 2 shards in the container to receive the Eye of Stormhammer.

7) Take the Eye of Stormhammer back to Beno in Kaladim and he will give you the Avenger Battle Axe.

 

Bloodforge Armour Quest

1) Head to Butcherblock and kill Corflunk and Zarchoomi. They are rare spawn Ogres that roam the zone. You likely need to be level 10 or above to accomplish this. When you kill them you will be able to loot their heads.

2) Head to the Warriors Guild in Kaladim and hail Byzar Bloodforge to receive the quest. Ask him, "What about Corflunk?" or "What about Zarchoomi?" to make sure you can do the quest. He will respond with something to the effect of don't mention those names to me. Give him the heads and he will give you a piece of Bloodforge Armour.

 

 

 

Bloodforge Hammer Quest

1) Kill both rare Ogres that spawn in the Butcherblock mountains. Their names are Corfulnk and Zarchoomi. Take their heads to Byzar Bloodforge in the Warriors' Guild in Kaladim.

2) Head to Qeynos and visit the Fish's Ale. Wait for Trumpy Irontoe to leave the bar and kill him. You will need to do it very quick as you will be attacked by guards and bouncers almost immediately.

3) Take his head back to Byzar and you will receive the Bloodforge Hammer.

 

 

 

 

Bonethunder Staff Quest

1) Talk to Runethar Hamest in the Temple of Thunder to receive the quest.

2) Head to Surefall Glade and enter the cave with the Bears. Kill the Grizzly Bear Mammoth to retrieve the Mammoth's hide and a list.

3) Give both the Mammoth's hide and the list to Frenway in Surefall Glade and you will receive Bayle List II.

4) Head to the Qeynos Aquaducts and kill Drosco the Zombie to retrieve the Order of Thunder.

5) Take the Order of Thunder to Wolten Graff in the Temple of Thunder to receive The Thunderstaff.

6) Take The Thunderstaff and Bayle List II to Runethar and you will receive the Bonethunder Staff.

 

 

Bracelet of Beetlekind

1) Hail Blixxin Entopop to obtain the 6 slot box, and the list.

2) You need to obtain:
Spiderling Eyes (from Spiderlings)
Spiderling Silk (from Spiderlings)
Fire Beetle Eyes (from Fire Beetles)
Giant Scarab Egg Sac (from Giant Scarab)
Giant Fire Beetle Legs (from Giant Fire Beetles)
Giant Wasp Wings (from Giant Wasps)

3) When 1 of each of the above are in the container, hit combine, and give it back to Blixxin Entopop.

You will recieve 1 of a few different items.

 

 

Bullsmasher Quest

1) Head to the Mines of Malfunction in Ak'Anon and kill Red V to retrieve the Incomplete Black Box.

2) Kill Obsolete Models to retrieve 4 Blackbox Fragments.

3) Put the Blackbox Fragments into the Incomplete Blackbox and hit Combine. You will receive a Rusted Blackbox.

4) Give the Rusted Blackbox to Manik Compolton and you will occasionally receive the Bullsmasher. You do not receive it every time.

 

 

Brazen Brass Kilij Quest

1) Kill the rare Sand Giant in Southern Desert of Ro. He is a mid-twenties level mob. He will drop the plans of Kilij.

2) Take the plans and 3 enchanted gold bars to Kelethin.

3) Find the Druid Guild in Kelethin and on the same platform find the weapon shop. Outside the shop is an NPC. Hail the NPC and if he will tell you about the Kilij plans if he is the right one.

4) Give him the plans and the 3 enchanted gold bars and you will receive the Brazen Brass Kilij.

Wee Harvester Quest

1) Head to Western Karana and follow C. Miller through the fields until he drops a bail of hay.

2) Head to Feerrott and find Roror. He is usually relatively close to the Rathe Mountains. Hail him, and then give him the Hay and 66gp. He will give you back some Cursed Hay.

3) Head to North Qeynos and visit the Temple of Life. As you head towards the temple there will some NPCs on your right. The one on the far left is the one who gives the blessed oil quest. Hail him and get the blessed oil.

4) Head to a forge and put the blessed oil and the cursed hay into it. Combine. This has been known to fail if you have low smithing skill. If you are successful you receive Writhing Hay.

5) Head to Rivervale and give the Writhing Hay to Shakey the Scarecrow to receive either the Wee Harvester or the Belt of the River.

 

 

Bear Hide Boots Quest



Just outside the Surefall Glade entrance you will find an NPC named Chanda Miller.

If you approach her and tell her that "Baobob sent me" she will open up and talk to you. Follow through the bracketted dialogue and you will find that she will make you a Bear Hide Belt for a low quality bear skin and 5gp. For a medium quality bear skin and 15gp, she will make you the Bear Hide Boots, and for 21gp and a high quality bear skin she will make you a Bear Hide Cloak.

You can obtain these skins in various zones where bears abound. Such places as Qeynos Hills, all of the Karanas and the Commonlands are heavily populated with bears.

You must use "skins" for this to work. this has a tendency to confuse people who may be looking for "pelts".

Broom of Trilon Quest

1) Hail the Mistress of Air in the Temple of Solusek Ro to obtain the quest.

2) Kill a Magician in Najena to obtain a broom.

3) Kill a Griffon to obtain a Griffon Feather

4) Kill a Cyclops to obtain a toe of a Cyclops

The broom is a focus object for a Magician to summon pet which is more powerful than the Magician could currently summon. The focus object must be weilded (read: in one of your hands) for it to be used. The Reclaim Energy effect is a seperate function.

Golden Stud Earring Quest

Submitted by Dommini.

Zamel has been looking for a friends missing child. Apparently the child has
wandered into the local sewers and asks for information on her wareabouts.
He also says he will give you a reward if you locate any information of the
child.

Conversation:

1. Zamel describes the (aqeuducts).

You say "What aquedcuts"

2. Zamel describes scary (Beasts)

You say "What beasts"

3. Zamel describes the missing (child)

You say "who's child"

4. Zamel will then ask you to being back any proff of the childs death.


In the sewers of Qeynos there is a special NPC named Cubert. Cubert
apparently killed the child. If you can find and kill Cubert, you might
recieve a LORE item in the form of a "Tattered Dress". This is the dress of
the missing child.

Take the dress back to Zamel, and repeat the mentioned conversation. If you
are lucky Zamel will reward you with the Golden Earring Stud. His common
drop is a piece of Hemetite, worth a few gold coins.

He drops the Earring approximatly 25% of the time.

The trick to the quest is to kill the placeholders of cubert. Cubert only
spawn in a select location and wanders a VERY small area of about 50 in-game
feet.

His placeholders are Geletanous Cubes, Sewer rats and another named rat that
I can't think of right now.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Grobb Cleaver Quest

1) Talk to the carver in front of the Shamans' guild in Grobb. He will ask you for some good Ogre meat, but doesn't know the name.

2) Tell him it is HEHE meat. He will ask you for 3 pieces and the recipe.

3) Head to Oggok and find Chef Dooga. Buy 3 pieces of meat.

4) Head to Neriak and into the Foreign Quarter. Find an Ogre and hail him. If he talks about HEHE meat you have the right NPC. He will give you 2 quests.

5) Ogres and Dark Elves need to go back to Dooga and bring some Froglok legs back to the Foreign Quarter. Trolls need to head to the Ocean of Tears and visit the rocks that have Sirens on them. There will be a named Troll called Nerbilik. Give him the item that the Neriak NPC gave you and then kill him. He drops the recipe.

6) Take the recipe and the 3 HEHE meat back to the carver and you get the Grobb Cleaver.

Speak to Leaf Falldim in Kithicor Forest to obtain the quest.

the isle with Captain Surestout, Gornit, Dixl Drool, and all the seafury
cyclops'. His placeholder is a buccaneer, and spawn rate seems to vary
between 1 and 2 hours. Witlin has the Quiver of Kithicor, which you
must fill with 6 unique arrows that the brownie outcasts stole.

They
are located in Lesser Faydark, at the Pixie Tower. Killing the pixies
seems to cause the brownie outcasts to spawn, but don't kill the pixie
jongleurs! You will very likely get duplicate arrows, so it's good to
do it with another ranger friend. Put all 6 arrows in the Quiver, and
combine them.

You will also need a Star of Odus can be purchased in Erudin.

Turn in the combined Quiver, and gem and Leaf and you get the Ivy Etched Leggings.

Virtue of the Fervent Quest

1) Talk to Blaize in the Temple of Solusek Ro to obtain the quest.

2) Kill the named aviak "Gull" in the Ocean of Tears on Aviak Island. Gull drops the Ingot of the Fervent

3) Find Jovan in Highhold Pass, and give him two Tumpy Tonics, which can be made with the brewing skill or via a side quest. He will give you the Icon of the Fervent.

4) Take the Ingot, the Icon and an enchanted platinum bar back to Blaize to receive the Vambraces of the Fervent.

 

 

Vambraces of the Fervent Quest

1) In the Temple of Solusek Ro talk to Blaize the Radiant. Simply say, "Tell me about the Fervent." and you will be given the quest.

2) Kill Gull in the Ocean of Tears (Aviak Island) to get the Ingot of the Fervent.

3) Head to Highhold Keep and give Jovan two Tumpy Tonics. You will receive the Icon of the Fervent.

4) Head to Temple of Solusek Ro. Give 1 enchanted platinum bar to Villisia and you will receive one Vulcanized bar.

5) Give the Ingot, Icon, and Vulcanized bar to Blaize to receive the Vambraces of the Fervent.

 

 

 

Wee Harvester Quest

1) Head to Western Karana and follow C. Miller through the fields until he drops a bail of hay.

2) Head to Feerrott and find Roror. He is usually relatively close to the Rathe Mountains. Hail him, and then give him the Hay and 66gp. He will give you back some Cursed Hay.

3) Head to North Qeynos and visit the Temple of Life. As you head towards the temple there will some NPCs on your right. The one on the far left is the one who gives the blessed oil quest. Hail him and get the blessed oil.

4) Head to a forge and put the blessed oil and the cursed hay into it. Combine. This has been known to fail if you have low smithing skill. If you are successful you receive Writhing Hay.

5) Head to Rivervale and give the Writhing Hay to Shakey the Scarecrow to receive either the Wee Harvester or the Belt of the River.

 

 

Werewolf Skin Cloak Quest

1) Kill a werewolf for their skin.

2) Find and hail Karg Icebear in Everfrost. Give Karg 100pp and the werewolf skin and you will recieve a Werewolf Skin Cloak.

 

Wasp Poison Quest

1) Collect 3 giant wasp venom sacs.

2) Give the sacs and 30gp to the dwarf NPC on the island in Dagnor's Cauldron and he will give you wasp poison.

 

 

 

 

Watchman Boots Quest

Talk to Lord Jenork in Ak'kanon. If you have high enough faction he will
tell you to find Watchman Dexlin in the Karanas for a special mission.
Watchman Dexlin is a rare spawn on the Bridge to South Karana. When you
hail Dexlin he will tell you that he is looking for a brave warrior to deal
with some pesky skeletons. If you tell him "I am a brave warrior" he gives
you a box and tells you to find "tiny skeletons" and return it to him when
you have filled it with their bones. After you get the box a number of
tiny skeletons will spawn somewhere between the bridge and the gypsie camp.
They are only upper teens but at level 24 I had problems soloing 3 at once.
Kill four of them, loot their bones and combine them in the box. Return the
box to Dexlin and he gives it back and tells you to give it to Lord Jenork
in Ak'kanon to get your reward which is Watchman Boots and a few plat.
