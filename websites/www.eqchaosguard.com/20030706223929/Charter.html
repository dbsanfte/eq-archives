Guardians of Chaos or Guardians Against Chaos -  the battle between order and disorder on the field and in our ranks rages on.

 

Will you wreak havoc through the lands of Norrath by the scourge of your might? 

Will you bring tranquility by subduing those that would disrupt the peace?

Or will you trip lightly on the fine line in between; riding the pleasures of spontaneous destruction and impulsive pacification?

 

Be you good or evil you have a place in Chaos and it has a place in you.
