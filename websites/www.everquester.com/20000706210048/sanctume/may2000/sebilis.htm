May 5th - 11th, 2000

The Knights of Eternal Good had been visiting Old Sebilis this past week.

Anyone wanting to enter Sebilis must posses a Trakanon Idol which must be quested for in Trakanon's Teeth. KEG has spent the first few nights obtaining these idols by hunting down medallions from the froglok hunter and forager and turning them in to the iksar ghost near the spectral graveyard.
