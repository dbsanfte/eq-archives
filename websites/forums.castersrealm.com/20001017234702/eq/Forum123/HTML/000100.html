I don't "gouge" anyone. I've been known many a time to sell something for less than market to people who were short on cash. The thing you're ignoring is, you buy and sell for profit from bigger twinks than you. That doesn't hurt anyone. Some lvl 50 wants to get rid of an item with average market value of 800pp, they let it go to you for 500pp, you sell it to your average joe for 700pp OR you get an offer from another lvl 50 something who's making a twink and has more money than he knows what to do with, and he offers you 900 or 1k for it. That's how I've made my money, and I don't see the slightest thing wrong with it.
