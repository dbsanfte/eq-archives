IMO iksars are easily the best SKs at low levels because they are the most well rounded, have regen, and can see at night. however, at higher levels, starting in the early 20's, they will start to lag behind the other races because of the horrible exp penalty and the fact that they cant wear plate armor. ugh... no darkforge.
trolls probably come in second because of their high str and sta and because of regen. however they have a horrible exp penalty and have low int, which will start to hurt at higher levels. also when they wear plate armor they look like they put it together with yarn and construction paper. in addition to that they are big which generally means that its harder to find armor and you have to be shrunk to fit in some dungeons.
Now to the DE... i have a DE myself. definitely the coolest looking race, at least out of all the sk choices. they are the only race with ultravision (and trust me, it is far superior to infravision), they have a decent str (90 if u put all ur points into it), and they have enough mana to make it through several fights without medding... but i still do find myself OOM quite a bit, especially in duels, i dont know how the trolls and ogres do it. with 109 int, it might be nice if maybe 5 of the base int could be taken out and put into STR, but with their great natural AGI (90), they are IMO the best race choice, but im probably biased.
ogres are big and strong, stronger than trolls but still without regen, which makes the trolls better IMO. their main advantage over the trolls, like i said, is the STR and STA, also they have a few more INT points than the trolls which would be the main forseeable reason to choose an ogre over a troll (also they look better).
The only reason i could see to choose a human would be that they level faster or the player didn't find themselves using their mana pool to the full extent, they are blind as a bat (although you can use a LS or GLS you will never see as well as any of the other non-humanoid races).
Erudite Shadowknights are few and far between. most people who make them are in it for the challenge or maybe they want to be unique. in all of my playing i have only ever seen one erudite SK. their intelligence far outweighs their STR and thus they often find themselves encumbered. Odus is quite isolated, and you will probably want to get off of it before you really have enough money for a teleport (unless you have good enough faction to get through Erudin, which from what i have heard is unlikely if you have done any of the quests. however, if you make it to a high level you will be admired and you will likely be one of the only (or maybe even the only) ESK on your server. however, this class/race combination is generally only for the most experienced of players.
of course as with most things in EQ you should do whatever feels right to you, trolls and ogres are fun to roleplay, i have some chars of those classes just so that i could roleplay with them.
i have written this mostly off of hearsay so if i have made a mistake then i stand corrected.
