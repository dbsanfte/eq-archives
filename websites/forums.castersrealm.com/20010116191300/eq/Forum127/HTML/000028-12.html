First there is another thread (Part 3) that we should be using, this has gotten quite large


Petoften, and others on multi-questing

I've yet to see ANY quest ingame which you cannot 'share' , the way the quest NPC's work is like a slot machine. You feed them the required items, and it records them simple as having X item TRUE/FALSE, once the last item they require is needed, they spit out the reward to whomever turned in the last item, and then "ALL" of the required items are set to false. (Can't had in 10 of one item and 1 of the next , expecting the net nine of the 2nd item to give the reward).

What most likelly happend to you petoften, is that one of the people who turned in the pages had a 'duplicate' page and thus no one handed in the last item. Turning in all 3 then simply destroyed two items which the NPC already had, and giving the 3rd is what gave the reward. I'd be curious, see if you can remember which placement in your trade window the words were placed (1 being teh top slot, 4 being the bottom) If the words were placed in word1, word2, word3 order then look at your chat window to see exactly when you got the reward (NPC's analyze the items in sequence then act upon them) so if you saw for instance "Thank you, Earned exp message, Thank you" You actually got the reward after turning in page 2 (in this example). Depending on that you might learn which person in the original group had the bad page

Anyway, it's over with now, but as I said, there is no flag on each quest stating "Shareable" (yes/no) unless it's something new that verant is experimenting wiht. Someone even posted a while back to Ester that this ablity on quests was a bug, and she replied that it wasn't, and that it was there to help people work together.
