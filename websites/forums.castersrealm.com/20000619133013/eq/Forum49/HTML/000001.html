The unkempt druids are a band of "vengeful nature preservers." They are pretty much pissed at certain other NPC's or groups of NPC's for not holding regard to nature. One named Clops or HG is the target in one of their quests; they want him dead because he tramples the trees in the forest with "reckless abandon." Pretty much they are the same as normal druids, but they are very protective of nature to the point of seeming evil.

There are a bunch of quests for druids that are (a) all broken and (b) explain a lot of this. Talk to Gerard Woodsoe in Surefall Glade about the Unkempt Druids and he describes their history. The real problem about this faction is that until they added the Temple of Solusek Ro, it was very clear that "good" druids were supposed to battle the Unkempt whenever possible because all of our quests had drops in Unkempt Faction and rises in Surefall faction or Rivervale. Now, they added a bunch of NPC Unkempts in the Rathe Mountains who only look at me amiably because of all the quests I have done. Also this is linked to the Druid's Assassin's Quest triggered by Gerard Woodsoe as well which has been broken since Beta and involves trying to foil an assassination attempt in Qeynos.
