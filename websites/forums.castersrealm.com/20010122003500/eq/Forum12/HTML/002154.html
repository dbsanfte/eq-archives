Wait till higher levels. That is where most people do their comparisons. Meleeing is non-existant for any shaman with a clue past level 49 if they are soloing, or 40 if you are a grouping shaman. Shaman simply DO NOT have enough dmg output to justify not medding for mana instead. This means str is only used to gauge how much junk you can carry, and a Iksar with the right equipment and buffs, can carry 155+ stones of weight. Remember, Iksar is a CHAIN based armor wearing race which means Iksar armor weighs LESS than plate based armors. 155+ stones of weight is a lot of weight. I know I RARELY become encumbered. There are 3 important stats at higher levels imo: regen(cann3 is a huge friend for a shaman, and a spell I NEVER unmem), wis(this is your manapool, how many turgurs can you dishout? resists included, while keeping the buffs going on your group), and finally AC(how many mobs can you piss off with Turgur's, so they beat on you instead of the enchanter on multiple pulls, or waiting for the tanks to taunt them off on single pulls and still STAY ALIVE?)

Those are the questions a shaman has to ask themselves. Then make your racial choice based on your playing style vs. the pros/cons of that race. For me, Iksar fits me just fine.
