Well. I just read about our new item. Been in a plane. (yes, its late).

Anyway. Here are my thoughts on this item, and how it is useful, and how it could be better.

First let me point something out I did not see anyone else mention. Specialize Conjuration. At L50 a caster with 200 int has 2100 mana. I can cast Shock of Swords 10 times, give or take for a bad roll on specialize (I am specialized in Conjuration). That should have cost me 2500 mana. That's a saving of roughly 40 mana per cast. Or 16% less. With that in mind, we are spending roughly 168 mana. Not a huge difference, but worth making a note of.

Ok, I frankly agree this should be a 2 charge item. I can never see it as more than that. It becomes too good. Too effecient. With 2 charges. 250 mana, -400 hp, -168 mana. Net= 82 mana, -400 hp. A manastone gives roughly 170 mana for the same amount of hp loss. Of course, since you would summon this item prior to needing it...250 mana for 400 hp is an _excellent_ trade. Can you see why 3 or more charges is ridiculous? 2 charges would also give a wizard or a druid 250/300 mana they need for an evac (realisiticly a L50 of these classes has 50 mana in a sliver of a bubble).

So how is this useful in its current incarnation? It's limited. It's a panic button. Will I pass one of these out to everyone in the group? Heck no. Will it make us wanted? I do think it will make us more wanted, not a lot more, but more than now. Greater Healing - 150 mana, 270 hp restored. Again, most casters have 25 mana even when they are "oom."

I think the Real problem is that we had our aims set too high. We expected too much. We had our hopes up from what GZ said about it being cooler than a ring of regen (cool, but VERY much out of our domain). I am preparing a letter to GZ, very well written sort. Doing some cost analysis (yeah business classes, hehe). In it I plan on demonstrating how 1 additional charge on this item will make it a viable item, sought after as an added bonus to a group, make us wanted type thing. That it will NOT be unbalancing or exploitable. I think this item is an excellent, very appropraite item. It is right up the alley of a magician as a mana storing device. I just feel it needs a slight modification. You need to look at this from their point of view. Anytime you are swapping hp for mana, it becomes a challenge to insure that it cannot be abused badly. Cleric/manastone/complete heal is a good example. Complete heal, 400 mana. 20 clicks on a manastone, 1200 hps burned to cover the cost of the spell.

Ok, the present incarnation is limited and underpowered. Mages have waited 10 months for this spell. And it is not as powerful as we had hoped. It is not a class power spell (teleports, Ice Comet, Ressurect, Summon Corpse, SoW, Stat buffs, regeneration, Mesmerize/clarity), and I think that is what we were all hoping for. We wanted to be special. Unique. Given something that makes others react to a magician asking for a group (Go get that guy/girl! we need a mage for that awesome spell!!). I am not sure what is the thought behind this. I can honestly say if we make a big enough, well thought out, Non flamming (d3wd that n3w sp3]] suxs! Bite me! blah blah blah) arguement, we may be able to get this looked at again. Anyone else just want 5 minutes with GZ alone to talk? Get his insight on this? I can only imagine. This is his baby, he wants all the classes to be well balanced, fun, and have their role. If you dont believe this, grow up. He does not keep any one class down because he plays a necro (where did that come from anyway? He playes EVERY caster class)

I've said it before, I'll say it again. I love the mage class. This new spell is a good addition (not great, or a great leg up) and stays in theme with our class. If you don't like our spell list, play a necro and solo your little heart out. The Magician class is designed for groups. We group very well. We bring more to a group than a necro does, and with this new item, a bit more now.

If you reply to this message, and I hope you do, reply in a clear intelligent, well thought out manner. Pointing out that if you summon and use it immediately (Completely wrong use of the item) you lose 75 mana is not the sort of reply I want to see. Sorry if I seem bitter on that, but I hate flamming posts as they are a waste of time to read, add nothing to a discussion, and only make people mad.
