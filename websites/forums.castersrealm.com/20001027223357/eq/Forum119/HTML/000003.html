My Troll SK is currently soloing in SRO, and I'm practicing kiting by pulling beetles with bow and spell. Beetles are slow so you can outrun them, stop , spell, bow, run again, . I generally do this till I'm out of mana before pulling them over to the wiz port where my pet is camped. This has been a good way for me to improve my archery and spell skills, as well as softening up my opponent and giving me a little healing.
