Okay, here's what I said I'd come back tonight to tell you all about. I hope you'll like whatcha hear 'cause it's not Enchant Velium.

The last piece is in Burning Woods, on top of the meteor. If you're walking down the path the meteor has dug into the earth and come up to the meteor, it's on the other side of it.
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/burning.jpg

When you submit all three items plus the initial thing you get from the geonid for saying that you'll get the stuff for him, he says ...

"You truly are the Harbinger, take this Focus as a mark of your friendship with us. Now we can construct the Menhir, we need ..,..,the stones speak.., There is a problem. Ones of flesh are coming, the large ones, 3 of them. They must not disturb us while we construct the Menhir! Please stop them Harbinger! bring us proof of their destruction along with your Focus and then we will begin the calling. Our asscention is at hand!"

Your faction increases with the geonids. Then, you have to get outside the tunnel. Memorize the path in before you do the quest. Here's a quick and dirty map to show you the path in. If you're running along the wall from Skyshrine to the caves, it's the second along the wall. The reason you don't want to do this from the other direction is that when we went to do this tonight, it turned out a dragon had suddenly spawned in the zone that wasn't there all week and just decided to pop today. It hangs out near the dragon portal, is 60, has a huge aggro radius, and was pretty pissed off all day, slaughtering people left right and center. If you come from Skyshrine to the caves, you're a great distance from the dragon and very safe.
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/caves.jpg

Anyway, you run outside and start tracking. After a few minutes, two "accolyte of Zek" giants will show up on track. You might have to run out a bit to get 'em. They're both blue at 52. Bring 'em to the cave entrance. Snaring them doesn't help, they run fairly fast, but it doesn't slow them down somewhat.
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/accolyte.jpg

Then around the same time you'll see something called Bloodpriest Loukond show up on track.
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/bloodpriest.jpg

This is not to be confused with Priest Bjek, who is red at 52 and not related to this quest but sometimes runs around and looks much like the Bloodpriest.

Anyway, he's a bit behind the accolytes. Ignore him for now, just finish off the accolytes. Loot them and they drop a lore, no drop necklace called Holy Symbol of Zek. You MUST get both of them from the accolytes. Then once you've done them in, bring in the Bloodpriest and do him. All of these are quite quite magic resistant. You'll get a necklace off him too. When you pop these guys, Phenocryst gives you another focus item (mentioned before). Submit that and the three necklaces to him and you'll get the end result of the quest.

Phenocryst's Focus
Magic - Lore - No Drop
3 AC, 3 DEX, 10 INT, 40 Mana, 3 SV Fire/Disease/Poison/Cold, 5 SV Magic

I believe it's a held item, but I'm not sure, I didn't get the item.

The faction hits you get for killing the giants are Kromzek worse, Kromrif worse, KingTormax worse, and ClawsofVeeshan better.

When you submit the final product (the necklaces and focus), Phenocryst says the following:

"Because of you we can at last speak with our maker. We were once given a gift, and we ask that you accept this gift from us now so that we may come closer to that which we owe our sentience to. Now..,we must construct the Menhir.."

Then things get kinda freaky. A message pops up saying:

"Phenocryst begins to resonate. All the stones about you begin to resonate seemingly at its command. The Geonids all begin moving, all to construct the Menhir."

Then they start walking around, Phenocryst leading the pack. They start walking down the halls, lots of 'em, like they're going to get together and make this thing. Only problem is, at the last instant, a goo aggroed on somebody and they all started running at us and we had to zone.

Note to self here. Even if you're amiable to goo in the caves, if a goo gets on you, it will aggro geonids on you and almost everybody is aggro to goo.

Although I don't know what happened in the end, I don't think the quest continues onward any further from that point as far as items you get in return for achieving a task. When we returned, Phenocryst and the two geonids on either side of him were gone from the caves.
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/chanting1.jpg
http://www.geocities.com/brcalley/quest/chanting2.jpg

There you go. Credit for these last steps also goes out to Dariys and Nuala, as well as the fine people of United Norrath, Jade Dragon, and Black Tower who were there to help out. Dariys got the item.

Ah, here's a few more things, two things I forgot. The item is all races, but it's for the four pure caster classes, not for hybrids.

Also, the first time I did this quest, I was alone and couldn't take this stuff out on my own. The acolytes of zek ended up getting into fights with the various mercenaries in the zone and were possibly taken out by them, which is why pulling them when you see them is essential. They won't end up at the caves most likely so you have to get them. The bloodpriest shows up much later, and if you don't pull him in, he seems to show up at that second cave entrance anyway. When he shows up, he stands outside for a few minutes, then he depops. The second time I did this quest, everybody in the zone went LD when we pulled the acolytes. When we came back, the acolytes were gone and the bloodpriest was close by so we did him. He had just arrived at the cave entrance and was standing there for a few seconds when he aggroed on me and we started the fight. I think he walks there everytime, but probably not the acolytes. They don't enter the caves or attack the geonids at all.

When this happened, I decided to submit the focus and the necklace I got. I got nothing in return. When we did the quest a second time and got all three necklaces, we submitted them all, got the reward from the quest, and Phenocryst made a comment that he needed more proof of the giant deaths. This is because this quest can be multiquested. If somebody submits items for two acolytes but doesn't have the necklace for the bloodpriest, for example, a person who comes along with a necklace for the bloodpriest and submits it will get the reward. The last person to submit the remaining necklace and focus item gets the piece.
