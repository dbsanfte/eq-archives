To my knowledge Root and Enstill have the same chance of breaking on damage and chance of being resisted. Enstill has the potential to last twice as long as Root. That is to say it's maximum duration is twice the maximum duration of Root. It can still drop in a few seconds like Root can I believe.

I used to use it to park mobs in fights, when a mob jumped somebody I'd stun it and Enstill to allow the attacked player to move out of the way. As long as nobody damaged the mob it would usually last until it became the primary target.

Immobilise has the same chance of breaking as Root I believe, it casts faster and has a potential duration slightly longer than Root. But does use more mana.
