Champion,

Norrath is the world, and it doesn't include the planes. It is the planet.

You are right in that the quest involves getting items from the planes. But most of these are to prove you are up to earning the Scabbard, or in the case of the golem metal, fixing broken pieces of the sword.

In the legend, Rallos Zek broke the sword and scattered them across Norrath. 1 hilt piece was in Lake Rathe. Another hilt piece was on the spiroc island in Timorous Deep. One sword blade was found by a dwarf in Ocean of Tears. I think we can safely assume that the final piece is not in a Plane or such.

In fact, the current rumor is that the blade was found in a river and forged into an already existing sword, and the quest is to break up that sword so you can use it again.
