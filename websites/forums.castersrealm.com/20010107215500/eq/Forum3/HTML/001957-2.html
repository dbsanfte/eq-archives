Is there storyline content? No, not readily available. And certainly not in a concise and comprehensible format.

Are there sufficient quests? There are some interesting quests with nice returns, and there are many long and arduous quests that do not provide a reasonable reward. If I am lucky enough to find a few good ones, they usually outweigh the poor ones.

Are there sufficient dynamic events? Ummm, events? There are dynamic events? Surrrre there are. I've seen exactly one in the 18 months I've played the game.

Does it have problems? Sure it does. But it's the best out on the market today. Other games will learn from both it's successes and failures, as will the EQ designers. But they were first to market, and have kept me entertained for nearly 18 months. No other game has ever done that.

No mater how small, hold your own events. A new angle in the game is to be in charge. In conclusion, be different and join every event you can!
