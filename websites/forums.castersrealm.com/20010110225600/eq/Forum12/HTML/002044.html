some of the best shaman weapons in the game are piercing,unfortunately there aren't many low level piercing weapons to get to build up to the good ones... especially for sale around Halas.

if you just wait for a little bit, you'll see someone auction Finesteel weapons, and you can try to get a finesteel spear which isn't bad for a low level weapon. once you hit your teens and twenties you'll be glad you kept up the skill, as you'll have enough cash to get an Ebon War Spear for just a few hundred pp.

the weapon skills i keep up are, in order:

piercing
1hb
2hb

i need to work the whb a little, but i always keep piercing maxed.
