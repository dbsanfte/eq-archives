The Overthere.

I came back to EQ after 4 months and have been from 36.5 to 41 in Overthere.

At 34 you should be able to charm fight easily, or get a group.

Personally I prefer charm fighting right now since I have to take breaks frequently (caring for a sick girlfriend). It's easy and fast exp. I made 3 bubbles in 40 in one day (about 5 hours playtime) last saturday.

I pull with tash (get a Spiked Succulent, a Stonegazer, or a Sarnak Knight) and run till I am out of casting range then charm. Then I pull a target with tash and lead it to the zone wall (a good fighting area) and sic the pet on it. I med JUST out of spell range and have charm ready to recast. When a pet is almost dead I go invis and nuke it and get exp for it as well.

Stay away from Rhinos and Tigers. Both are fast. Rhinos are faster, and tigers have insane HP.

Good luck !

I wouldn't solo at The Overthere at 34, not because it isn't good exp, but because Frontier Mountains is better. At that point everything in FM is blue and you can mow through it fast. You can get your first exp soloing giants. Plus when you want to group the Mountain Giant Fortress is a lot of fun. Just remember the goblin casters are nasty and be sure to do everytyhing you can to disrupt casting (stun, blast, mez).

In a few levels go to The Overthere. Start with the Thorny Succulents, Stoneleer Cackatrice, Sarnak Berzerkers, and Sabretooth Tigresses. Later you can manage the higher level versions. I mostly solo, but finding a group is usualy pretty easy there.

I've tried Spectre's too, and they are great exp, but often find them to be too camped. There a bit higher level than the average mob in The Overthere which makes the fights a bit more chancy. I've tried lots of areas but keep going back to The Overthere when I wasnt consistent fast exp.

As a Followup. I decided to do spectres in OOT and they are super easy. At level 34 I could easily do all 4 at a time.




Strategy


Make Sure you have both SoW and Air Elemental. After you are all buffed up pull one of the spectres with Tash. If you are not confidant in your ability to kill all 4 for exp (Like when I first started). Immediatly charm the one you just tashed and have him attack another spec. This will cause the 3 spectres around him to gang bang him until he dies. Now you only have 3 specs to deal with.


Tash one of the remaining specs and AE Mez him. This will not only put him out but it will put his buddies out too. If one of them happens to resist AE mez he is your new pet. With your new pet pull one of the other two spectres with Tash and Suffocate. Your pet should easily be able to finish off the new spec now.


At this point you have one full hp spec (who you probably had to remez) and one pet spec at 1/3-1/2 hp. DoT the full hp spec and send in your pet. While your pet is fighting meditate, using F1 and F8 to keep an eye on both your pet and his attacker. When your pet starts getting really Low stand and DD.


Now you have 2 dead specs and one mostly dead pet. Have your pet guard here and walk away and hide (or invis if you are another, more feeble race). Immediatly Root And DD. Repeat DD until dead.


This was great exp and the exp bar just flew, by the time you are done meditating there are 4 more specs to play with. (If you wanna kill all 4 for exp after pulling them all with tash AE mez the bunch).
