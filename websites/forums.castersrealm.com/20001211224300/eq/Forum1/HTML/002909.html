Tallon was changed to coin loot first and with growing pressure Vallon changed shortly afterwards, most thought that 8 lvls was too far a gap to be able to kill players with very little risk.

Rallos remains the only item loot server and Abashi has stated several times this is not due to change.

I started completely untwinked on veeshan 9 months ago.

I'd been playing since release on Tunare (and beta before that but those chars got wiped =) and switched servers specifically so I would not twink.

I wanted to recapture what it was like to earn everything I got with that specific character. Those raw silk legs were a badge of honor. Wow, I just purchased my savant cap! and so on...

Along the way people would try to twink me and I would turn it down. "I would love a braided cinch cord, but I don't have 500 plat yet." "it's free" "I'm sorry, I can't take it for free."

Now I'm lvl 58(I'll be 59 by weeks end) and I have everything I wanted (almost) and it was all earned by me through camping, purchase or barter, and every item is special.

I find that when there's nothing left to earn, some of the luster is gone.

The price of twinking is that the luster disappears so much sooner...

Of course, I DO have a twink. She's lvl 31. But nothing she camps is useful to her because she has stuff that's better than that. Knowing that nothing that drops will help you makes the game less fun.

One day, I'll start over from scratch again. On a server with no guild, no friends, no other characters. That is the fun and the challenge to the game.

Enjoy it while it lasts =)

Of course, for now, I'm having too much fun with my guild, my friends and my other characters to move!
