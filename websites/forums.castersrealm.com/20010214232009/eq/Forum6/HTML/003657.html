The only stat that makes any impact at all for a mage is intelligence. Of course, any race can get to 200 int without a lot of trouble; that said, however, there are soem races which might make slightly better mages.

Make no mistake: more intelligence is NEVER a bad thing.

Let me point out that your decision should really be made about levels 30 on or so. Personally I have 135 days or so on my mage. Started when game first started so it took me 45 days to level 50. This leaves 90 days or 2/3rds of my character's life at a high level perspective thus for me what is important at this time is bloating my hps and mana which means high int is useful so you don't have to waste as many items getting your int to 200.

Many people point to starting location or faction as a bad reason for picking a race, but when you really look at the situation you are only low level for a very short time of your character life time so that issue should not be a consideration, similarly KoS should be of little concern. Go for the race that will best benifit you in your later life. To me this is the erudite due to the high int, but whatever you choose make your choice based on the high level game.
