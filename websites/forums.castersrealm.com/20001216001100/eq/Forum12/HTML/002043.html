I am finly beginning to understand this topic.

I am an Iksar Shaman, 30 th level. I have about 650 hps, an ac of 620 (unbuffed, mostly in banded with a few +wis items), 147 wisdom, and an attack value of about 650 (using a Slime Coated Harpoon). My melee skills are at max for my level. I hit for 10-25 pts a shot.

I tank. I tank a lot, because I enjoy it. I have (slightly) more ac than many of the warriors, paladins, and rogues I group with. I have about half of their hitpoints, but I regenerate, so it doesn't matter that much. And yes, I do get hit as much as the tanks do. If I an meleeing, and doting, and slowing, the mob sticks to me more often than the tanks pull it off.

The thing is, the tanks are starting to hit 2 or 3 times as OFTEN as I am. Most of them are still hitting for about the same damage per shot as I am. At 29th I got a bunch of new spells, including a haste spell (Quickness). The problem with quickness is it doesn't last that long, and it takes a lot of mana (about half a bubble per cast). When I cast Quickness on myself and not on the tanks, I find that they are still hitting more often than I am but only a little (3 hits to my 2 hits). If I quickness the tanks as well, the ratio returns to what it was before.

In the groups I fight in, we typicaly have 2 or 3 tanks in the party. We tend to camp an area where we will take 4 or 5 mobs, and then have a few minutes break before the spawn cycle begins again. I buff the tanks with quickness (1 - 1.5 bubbles of mana) and then hit each mob with a slow and a dot (1 bubble of mana per mob, if no resists), possibly rooting if one escapes (negligable mana). Well, If I am not canning and medding during the fight, I am oom by the time the fifth mob pops.

In other words, as much as I like to melee, and as effective as I can be at it -I am finding that I can be of more use to my group by NOT meleeing and being able to throw a couple heals (which curently cost me about a bubble of mana each) if our healer goes oom.

If my group was fighting single mobs, and resting up in between, I could happily wade in and do my damage, cast my spells, and take more than my share of the hits. But we are not. So I sit on the sidelines, and keep my group at peak efficiency.
