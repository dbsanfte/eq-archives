Hi everyone.
I think that there are some things to consider when choosing your race as a warrior. Do you intend of playing low, (1-25) mind (26-47 or so) or high (48-60) levels? What I mean by this is if you are planning to play at higher levels you have to take into account your race and stats MORE than if you are a casual player and dont see yourself getting to High level. I know this from experience and have heard a ton of people complaining about their characters now that they are 49th level or 50th level and they suck, and I mean suck, compared to characters who were planning to get to high level, and chose their stats accordingly!

So how do you plan? Make sure you have access to the things you need... SO if you dont have good friends and a strong guild I dont recommend an evil race as a warrior. Play good and you will need to rely on others less.

Pick a strategy. Look at your racial stats, your equipment selection and special abilities and then look at items that are out there and choose a combination that works. For example if you want to be a tank and take damage, you need stamina. So look at which races have a good stamina and how you can get items to affect that.

I was talking to a group that was fighting Trakanon the other day. The human warrior that was doing some of the pulls to clear the area out before Trak was dying all over the place on the Pull! Yup... he never made it back to the party. But when his friend the Ogre warrior showed up,the pulls were fine. Why? Cause the ogre has 1000 hps on the human... yup 1000. And the ogre was not stunnable from the front... which meant continues damage output WHILE tanking. You will notice that if a smaller race warrior is getting hit and stunned he isnt dishing out the damage anymore.

Another good story. Troll warriors... a friend was counting his regen, warrior, level 55, and he regened over 300hps in under 2 minutes. That takes a chunk out of downtime.

Lastly BASH! This is the skill. At higher levels it doesnt stun mosters anymore cause they are unstunnable. BUT it does interrupt a spell. Barbs, Trolls and Ogres can stun while duel wielding, or two hander wielding. This helps when fighting caster mobs, the humans have to switch to their shields.

So basically I am in favour of either a Barb, Troll or Ogre as a warrior, each with his own strength. Be aware though that Ogre and Troll have some serious exp penalties so you wont level as fast.

Just my 2 cents.
