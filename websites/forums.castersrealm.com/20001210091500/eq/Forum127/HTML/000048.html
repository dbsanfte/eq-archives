For those interested, a comparison to the cleric FA. Yes, I know it's "not much better than item x" for us, but this might clue everyone in to it's relevence:

11/24 vs 20/32 - melee is a null point for both
5 str vs 0 str - nice "gift" stat for more weight carrying

40 hp vs 0 hp - ask anyone with 1100 hps... 40 is NICE =)
10 all vs 10 all - even out
