These folks are right. In most situations the AC is a better choice, but in certain situations, you must go with the right resists or you accomplish nothing but your own deaths. Nothing pointed this out more to me then a failed Vox run one night.

Vox fears and has that damn cold breath thinger spell or whatever it is. There was actually close to 60 folks that kept trying to take her down, and they spent most of thier time running around feared and taking cold damage. After about 8 attempts another guild came in and moved in on her (much to the chagrin of those that had been trying to kill her) and with a total of only 18 people they killed her the first try. Everyone tanked, not many were feared, or at least not for very long when they were, and apparently not much damage was taken, because they didnt lose anyone.

When 18 people can take Vox on the first shot when 60 couldnt take her, that goes to show the difference that the right equipment can make. I have heard that the minimum for magic and cold resist you should have on vox is minimum of 100 each. Anyhow, just a thing to illustrate this. Good luck.
