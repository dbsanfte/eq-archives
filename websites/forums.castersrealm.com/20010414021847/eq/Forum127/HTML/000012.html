Here's the first part of the FA quest for shamen.

It starts in Rathe Mountains. Find a bandit hanging out with his buddies named Blinde the Cuttpurse.

Kill him. A spirit appears (wolf). Hail it.
It will ask you if you killed that bandit.

You say, 'Who are you?'

It responds by saying something about, 'Its not important who I am but who WE are etc etc..'

You say, 'I want to know more'

He gives you a tiny gem (looks like a star ruby) You take this gem to the Barbarian drunk in the sewers entrance in North FP.

This barbarian drunk will thank you, and ramble off some nonsense. Wait..

He walks off then gets all upset about you not letting him buy booze. Wait...

He walks to a bar in the Market, 3rd floor, he spawns a Greater Spirit. Talk to this spirit.

More to come later.
