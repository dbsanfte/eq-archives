ATK is the number under health and ac etc...it has little to do with haste.

It will make your tanks hit more often though. Usually strength buffs etc will influence ATK a bit...possibly haste does as well. But you don't need to increase haste to increase ATK. Offense, weapon skill, strength, weapon dmg...those all factor into ATK.
