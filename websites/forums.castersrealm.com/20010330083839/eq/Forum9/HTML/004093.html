Qin, i'd like to see you solo the efreeti at 55
really i would, well lets just try to analyze this.
There are 2 hall way spawns, lets say for the sake or argument they are broken for you, then there is the Efreeti himself. Well, first of all, i doubt u could get a nuke off, since our nukes are 6 sec cast time and he'll probably hit you a few and disrupt. But for sake of argument, lets say u had rune5 up and channeled thru a nuke and it was not resisted. Ok, Now you begin to chain stun. Well, that basically means you cast shift + flux non-stop and berserker str. in the middle once in a while. Well, lets also assume you got the lv 55 enc pet which hits max for 54 *i think* so, basically only ur pet is doing dmg. Lets calculate how long u can keep chain stunning and berserkering urself.

At 55, you should have around 2400-2500 mana
more if ur using some major +mana items.
Shift and flux cost 60 mana
berserker spirit is 150 mana and provides a 200 pt dmg absorb
*i think the efreeti can max for 130 each hit, not sure thou* so basically 2-3 hits, ur dmg absorb is gone. So, lets assume u have to berserker urself every 3 round combat.
so, every 3 rounds you use up 230 mana
Now our best DD cost 250 mana to cast, so it leaves you with 2250 mana, so you can keep this up for bout 31 rounds. Now 1 round of chain stunning is approx 7 sec. Since flux last for 3 sec and shift is 6 sec. So, that means you have 217 sec, which is approx 3.62 min.
Now, lets calculate pet dmg. Max for 54 *i think* and lets say you gave him a weapon for duel wielding and that you cast AQ on it. The Efreeti has approx 9630 HP *according to eqmaps.com* After DD, it has 8980. Now if your pet has to kill him in 217 sec, it has to do 41.38 dmg per second, which im pretty sure our pet can't do.

And this is assuming you make it into the room solo *since ur gonna solo the camp* You can't solo pull imps at 55, but AE mez and AE mem-blur is an option *although a risky one*

If you think you can root the efreeti and let it kill ur pet then mez him and rest, you have to worry about the hall spawns too, which is about 14 min if you break it perfectly *which is hard to do*

Another route is to charm an imp. While the imp can hit upwards of 130 each and can dmg the efreeti very fast, you have to worry about charm breaking and also, Boltran cost a whopping 400 mana to cast, so i dunno how effective thats gonna be.

All in all, if you can solo the efreeti, i'd really like you to post how you did it b/c i wanna know so i can try it

*sidenote*
I broke the camp w/ a monk and druid and they let me try soloing the efreeti, i did the chain stun + pet method. The hallways poped when i got the efreet to about 50% life and i mezzed it then ran out of mana when efreeti was at 20% or so. I was lv 58 at the time and started w/ 90% mana, hasted duel-wielding 55 pet and rune5. Also tried charming Imps, but they tend to only last 80-90 sec.
