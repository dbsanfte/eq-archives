You are looking for QuestNPC

You go around where QuestNPC spawns killing stuff and you end up killing UselessNPC. Because you did this it will take a much longer time for QuestNPC or it might make it so QuestNPC doesn't spawn at all! This might be the reason people are having problems with Modani.
