One thing that's always missing from these "which race should I be?" threads is: Which server do you intend to play on. The race you choose to play makes a much bigger difference then you might think if you play on a team pvp server. And its much more important then what you look like.

>>And of course female DE are cutiest lady of EQ

No way. The blond gnome gals are the cutest. Period.

Now, to seriously answer the race issue:

Any race can make a decent enchanter. High elves and Erudites have the best base stats. Gnomes and DE's are close behind. Humans have the worst. Most of the others have been listed as well. My advice is unless you are really, really worried about stats (you will get 200+ int as long as you have 120 base int, easy, with some pretty common high end gear, and cha, well, it's not too much trouble to get to 200... and it's value is slim enough for us to bicker about it all the time )... go with the race you like best.

You can be a boring snobby high elf. You can be a boring "I hate snobby high elves" DE. You can be a real boring human. Or a boring "I'm snobbier than a high elf" erudite. Or you can be the only race besides Iksar with an interesting culture, gnomes.

Which you choose is up to you. In the end, you'll matter more than your race.
