Bethene: He is why the ADDED Hp (and Ac) are situational:

Lets say your charater has 3000 hps buffed (with Cha gear). Then put on Hp gear instead of Cha gear. New hps will be (for example) 3200. For the switch to be worth anything you would need to be in a combat where you took >3000 damage (w/o heals) and still took less than 3200 damage (i.e. getting use of the 200 extra hps). So only in this situation will the hp/ac have helped you.

Same goes for Cha. Only if the extra chance to mezz will save a character/group, will it be effective. If it just means that you will have to stun and remez again, it will only have a manaefficientcy purpose.
