The trouble with bards is NOT during the low to mid levels, we're fine then. The problem with bards is how they progress through the levels. They eventually fall so far behind their specialists brethren as to become everywhere weak. The curve for bards is cut too steep, especially upon hitting the ubber levels. That's why high level bards are rare. Though its true that bards should NEVER become as good as any specialist they they shouldn't, nonetheless, eventually end up as nothing but mana batteries. Therefore I made suggestions to correct bards. If even one of these were redressed bards might have a better future to hope for.

Balancing the bard:

1) Improve on how bards overall level.
• Raise or eliminate the caps on what rogue skills we have. Perhaps allow bards to progress as rogues several levels behind. As it stands there are only three doors in all of Norath which can be open by a bard.
• Remove the warrior disciplines but allow bards to fight using the same general hit/damage tables as warriors. Doing this will give bards the neccessary combative skills to do their jobs without giving away the skills that define and make warriors unique.
• Revamp song stats so that they level better than they currently do. In their current watered down state most song effects are meager. Remember, even good bards can only solidly twist three songs at a time. Unlike buffs, when the music stops all benefits end.
• Improve hit points in the same way. Bards needn't be the next in-line to die after the enchanter.

2) Fix the remaining songs needing repairs. Kellin's Lucid Lullaby is an obvious one. In its watered down state it's still a suicide song. Agilmente's Aria of Eagles is another. The bug fix of Selo'S Song of Travel has crippled both songs. Now neither song notifies the recipient that they're about to fall before the levition ends.

3) Add in a create instruments trade skill for us. Why not. The parts are already being sold at the vendors. Let us now make them.

4) Allow the existence of more and well thought out bardic instruments that suit the class. For example:
• a piccolo which would enable the bard to charm like a Pied Piper
• a set of Bag Pipes that would raise the moral of his troops and serve to unerve the enemy
• a lyre that could soothe the savage beast (area effect lull)
• a horn that would summon assistance upon his most dire need
• a metronome to help the avid singer keep pace (lengthen song durations so that a solid fourth can be twisted)
• battle drums that can smite down the enemy when struck (magical instrumental damage)
• bucolic pan pipes that turn friend and foe into happy drunkards (useful when grouped with warriors about to take on casters)
• various instruments that sing a fourth (fifth if he's really good) song into the bard's twist.
• instruments that can function as two of different types (i.e, a chime that doubles as percussion and wind, a balalaika that plays both wind and string, a psaltery can be used for percussion and string, or an oboe that blows as wind and horn)

Saga, a bard from the Solosek Ro Server

P.S. Why is it when ever they talk about hybrids we're overlooked. Are we not hybrids too?
