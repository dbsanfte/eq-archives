〜 Modani Qu'Loniの章 〜
Written Date: 2001/02/26

目的:1st Piece of Staffを受け取る事

OverthereのModani Qu`Loniに、以下のItemをEnchanter's Bagで
Combine(Sack for Modaniになる)して渡す

The Xolion Rod
Innoruuk's Word
Chalice of Kings
Snow Blossoms



Overthereのクレータに入り口が2つ並んでいる所があります。
その右奥にいるのがModani。見た目は蠍です。
ServerUp直後はSpawnしておらず、さらにPHがいるらしい
かなり嫌味なNPCです。
周りの蠍はGoodRaceではKoSになりますが、Invisiが効きます。




まずは以下のItemを収集してきます。
The Xolion Rod：CabilisのLV60SHMをKill
Innoruuk's Word：NeriakのLV60CLRをKill
Chalice of Kings：ChardokのPrinceの頭をFelwitheのNPCに持って行く
snow blossoms：OggokのQuestを行う




そして、これらをEnchanter's BagでCombineすると

これになります。

そして、SackをModaniに渡すと

これが貰えます。

それと同時にTruespiritというFactionが上昇します。
順番にやっていったほうがよさそうです。


Modani Qu`Loni Log

You say, 'hail Modani Qu`Loni'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'The hidden self inside a myriad of magic is one that walks quite softly.
Yes, that is what you must seek.

[Jeb's Seal]を渡します
Modani Qu`Loni says 'Ah yes, Jeb's seal. The time to craft a Serpent must have come.
I will need components collected to craft the first of the pieces.
I will also provide you with a sack in which you must combine the items.
Are you ready to collect them?
[Jeb's Seal]を受け取ります

You say, 'I need a Sack'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'Take this sack and combine the items I requested in it.
Then return it to me.
[Enchanter's Bag]を受け取ります
＃但し、他のMasterと同じBagなので、既に持っていればLoreの関係上消えます。

You say, 'I am ready to collect them'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'Scattered throughout the world are various items.
To prove your worth, go collect these and return them to me.
The Xolion Rod, Innoruuk's Word, Chalice of Kings, and snow blossoms.

You say, 'What is The Xolion Rod?'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'This is the rod of an ancient civilization found on this continent.
Nothing is known of it other than that the scaled ones discovered it years ago.

You say, 'What is Innoruuk s Word?'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'The strict doctrine of the priests of Innoruuk is used as
a material component in the crafting. The power of their hate must not be underestimated.

You say, 'What is Chalice of Kings?'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'The Chalice of Kings is the chalice of elven kings.
Recently thought to be lost to the ages, it was recently discovered.
I need its magical powers to create a magical liquid.

You say, 'What are snow blossoms?'
Modani Qu`Loni says 'These are flowers that are also used in the creation process.
They were once widespread, but now I am told they only grow in select places.

[a Sack for Modani]を渡します
Modani Qu`Loni says 'Excellent, you have done well. Here is the first piece of the staff.
Now you must go seek out the second master; he will clear the path for you.
Your faction standing with Turespirit got better
You gain party experience!!

[1st Piece of Staff]を受け取ります
