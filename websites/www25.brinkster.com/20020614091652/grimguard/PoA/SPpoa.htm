This is basically the path we will be following through Splitpaw. Initially, Group 1 (the Raid Director's group) will enter and clear out the riff-raff to the second room. This will be the gathering area. Once this is done, the other teams head in and meet there. The two areas marked Camp 1 and Camp 2 will be the camp areas. This should ensure maximum loot potential with minimum encounters.

From the setup room, the Main Puller will clear the mobs to the larger room near the water (almost directly to the left of this paragraph). When this is done, the Raid Party will move there, and then the rest of the way to Camp 1 will be cleared. Once the Raid Party arrives here, the surrounding areas will be cleaned out and looted (from #1, #2 and #4 on the map).

Again, the Main Puller will clear the way to Camp 2, following which the Raid Party will move. From this area, the remaining loot mobs will be pulled and killed (from #5 and #7 on the map).
