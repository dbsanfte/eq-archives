  • There have been a few policy changes of late concerning planned events and communication and I would like to highlight some of them for you here:
    • All PLANNED Guild-related events will be posted to the Msgboard, and a sign-up link will be provided. To access a list of currently PLANNED Events, simply click on the "EVENTS" button found on the Guild website.
    • If an event is PLANNED, and you do not sign up, you will not be allowed to attend. If you can get to us, you can ‘hang out’ but unless there is an opening in a group, you will be unable to join in.
    • There will always be ample time to sign up for an event. Most events will be announced on the Msgboard, and in the News Brief (on the main Guild Webpage) at least a week in advance.
    • It is recommended that you visit the Guild Website regularly. If you don’t, you won’t know when an event is coming up.
    • There is a new Website address (collective.dynu.net). You can continue to visit the old link (www25.brinkster.com/grimguard) but I’m not sure how long I will continue to update that page. When I finally decide to stop updating that page, I will put an announcement on the site.
    • Along with the new roster, there is a new gallery feature as well. You no longer have to send screenshots to me to see them displayed, nor will you have to put up with my stupid comments, as you can upload them yourself, and add your own stupid comments!
      • Only able to accept .jpg and .gif files (to keep the sizes down)
      • Keep the size under 500k or it won’t upload and please, if the sceenshot isn’t very easy to see, clean it up first. (use a graphic editor with gamma features like Paintshop Pro or you can try Vicman Pro. It is a nice, simple editor, fully functioning, but with annoying nags and a size limit on the images.)

We hope to plan more events and raids, but we are still experiencing a lack of ideas/participation. It seems the same people are suggesting all the events, and quite frankly, they feel a little selfish to be constantly suggesting things to do that will benefit themselves. It is very difficult to find mid-level events where more than one class will benefit. So, if you are interested in getting an item, or getting a part of a quest, and you need backup, then post it on the msgboard for all to see and then we can plan an event around it.

In closing, I would like to say that we have seen some amazing growth in the last few months. I want to keep up the momentum, and to start doing more regular events. More events will mean more loot and more opportunity for growth, both personally, and as a Guild. People see our Guild tag everywhere now, and they associate good things with our members. Let’s keep it up.

ALL HAIL THE COLLECTIVE!
