WATER SPRINKLER OF NEM ANKH

Cleric Epic Quest CheckList:

 

Orb of Frozen Water

  Kill Lord Bergurgle and loot Lord Bergurgle's Crown 
  Give Lord Bergurgle's Crown to Shmendrik Lavawalker 
  Kill "a spirit of flame" and loot Damaged Goblin Crown 
  Give Damaged Goblin Crown to Natasha Whitewater to receive Ornate Sea Shell (I).  
  Give Ornate Sea Shell (I) to Omat Vastsea to receive Coral Statue of Tarew 
  Give Coral Statue of Tarew to "a seeker" 
  Kill Plasmatic Priest and loot Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe 
  Turn in Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe to Omat Vastsea, receiving Orb of Frozen Water 

 

Orb of Clear Water

  Give Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring to Natasha Whitewater and receive the Ornate Sea Shell (II) 
  Kill Lord Gimblox in Solusek A, loot Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring 
  Give Ornate Sea Shell (II) to Naxot Deepwater, receive Message to Natasha 
  Kill Ixiblat Fer and loot Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer 
  Kill Overking Bathezed and loot Singed Scroll 
  Turn in Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer and Singed Scroll to Omat Vastsea 

 

Orb of Vapor

  Give Message to Natasha to Natasha Whitewater to receive Shimmering Pearl 
  Give Shimmering Pearl to Zordak Ragefire 
  Kill Zordak Ragefire to spawn Zordakalicus Ragefire 
  Kill Zordakalicus Ragefire and loot the Heart of Zordak Ragefire 
  Turn in the Heart of Zordak Ragefire to Omat Vastsea 

 

Orb of the Triumvirate

  Turn in the Orb of Frozen Water, Orb of Clear Water, and Orb of Vapor to Jhassad Oceanson 

 

Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh

  Turn in the Orb of the Triumvirate to the Avatar of Water, receiving Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh 

 

SUCCESS!

  Obtain Orb of Frozen Water. 
  Obtain Orb of Clear Water. 
  Obtain Orb of Vapor. 
  Obtain Orb of the Triumvirate. 
  Obtain Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh. 
  YOU HAVE SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED YOUR EPIC QUEST!!! 

Your god smiles on you, Cleric.
