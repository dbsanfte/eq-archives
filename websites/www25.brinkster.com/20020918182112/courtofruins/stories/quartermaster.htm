Employing Quartermaster

 


Even though he enjoyed working at the bank, he just wished he could get out a bit more, or at all in fact, yet ever since he completed his training as a wizard he had been assigned here, as a bookkeeper. Ok, the work was steady and safe, pay was a little low, but he managed. The door to his office (it wasn't really an office, rather it was more like a large coffin, a necromancers dream to study dark arts in, with nice stone walls) open. He looked aghast completely unsure what he should do next, for there, in front of him (or rather in front of his table) stood a gnome. Must be someone's slave was his first thought, nicely dressed was his second. There was not time for a third.

"You are the one called Quartermaster?" speaking the common tongue, the little gnome used a voice much deeper than he would have thought possible.

Quartermaster scowled at it.

"What do you want, slave and be quick about it, before I call the guards and have you whipped"; Quartermaster had had few dealing with the other races that inhabited Norrath, but he had learned early on how to handle enslaved ones.

Recognizing the good guys from the bad has been a problem throughout history, this was no exception. With a swift wave of his hand and a swirl of green lights, the little gnome rose above the table and stood eye-to-eye with him.

" I will take my own time good sir" the gnome said pleasantly in the speech of the Tier Dal, "for time is something that I have much of and you may not."

The smile that went with that froze Quartermasters heart. " But you are young

and arrogant, so your behavior is expected. You have come highly recommended to me. Your superiors seem to think that you are a capable elf who will soon be going places. Allow me to introduce myself, I am Tyre Dreadslayer, of the

Court of Ruins. Lord Utanadar has seem fit to give me authority over the wizards of the Court and I have come here to ask you to join us, will you do us this honor?"

His entire world turned upside down. Tyre Dreadslayer, the only gnome in the Court of Ruins, a wizard of fame and fortune. At first he thought that his life was over, but now, now this mighty wizard wanted him to join the Court (as it is affectionately known around Neriak)! People to go out adventuring with! Sites to see! And the knowledge, he could nnot find a better instructor! This could not be passed up, opportunity does not come along twice, not like this.

"I would be honored great one, anything I can do, anything at all and I will give it all that I can." Quartermaster could not keep the excitement out of his voice.

"I expect no less of you, nor will we take less, but we will take more"; Tyre smiled, a lazily smile, one that said ' you have been warned'. "Come now apprentice, let us be off, for your boss has already released you".

The excitement was far too much. Quartermaster followed the still floating Tyre Dreadslayer out of the bank, saying his goodby's to everyone as they left.

<six months later>

Even though he enjoyed working at the guild, he just wished he could get out more or at all in fact........
