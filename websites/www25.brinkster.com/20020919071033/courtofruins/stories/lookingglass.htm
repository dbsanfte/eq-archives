Through the Looking Glass

 

Even deep within the mighty guildhall sounds of battle could be heard. Mythas, leader of Maelstrom (first amongst all the guilds of Neriak) looked upon his officers and trusted men. "My friends Neriak is going to fall, of this there is sadly no doubt." The mighty ogre shadowknight, Bhaz, looked down at him; " Neriak no fall, Maelstrom will save!" he thundered.

Asphyxia looked at Bhaz; "While it is true we do have the power to save Neriak, we have committed ourselves across the globe to stop the Fier'Dal, it is thus that we are understrength here. Our allies have already committed thier forces elsewhere and the trolls have refused all requests for aid. We are lost."

Mythas looked upon his sullen comrades. " There is one last hope, Innoruuk himself has pledged to assist us in one final way. At the cost of a single sacrfice, he will open pathways to other like dimensions. In spirit, Maelstrom will survive. I say in spirit, becuase we will not all, if any be in the same places, the decision is put before you all."

An explosion rocked the fabled guildhall....

Bhaz (and a few of the other "lesser races" members) left the great room without a word to give themselves to the battle above. Those that remained bowed their heads in acceptance. Mythas, mightest of the wizards, without a word, stepped into the flames and was instantly consumed. Into their midsts formed a great shadow of nothingness, a hole of the purest black. Bidding goodbye, Asphyxia was the first to step through. One by one the officers and most trusted henchmen of Maelstrom stepped through.

An explosion ripped the great door off it's hinges. A small force of dwarves poured into the room. Kzhaddul, seeing that there was still a fair number waiting to get through sounded the Maelstrom battle cry and charged, calling for others to follow him, they had to hold the dwarves long enough to let as many as possible escape. Loyal Tier'Dal fell by his side, but the dwarves fell faster. A rain of fire, followed by several more cleared out the surviving dwarves. The remaining wizards bowed to him. "My Lord you must go, you must be one of the ones to continue on."

" My place is here"

One of the wizards smiled as he cast another spell. Kzh was stunned, literally. Two of the wizards picked him up as he silently protested and just before he was thrown threw the one who stunned him said; " For Maelstrom you must live". The world became black....

 

Kzhaddul awoke, wounded and sore. As he looked around he noticed that he was in a graveyard, a very old graveyard.Yes, it did look the same, he was in Ferrott, in the graveyard.

"Welcome Kzhaddul, welcome to my graveyard"

Kzhaddul looked at where the voice was coming from. He could barely make out the shadow of a man. A shadowed man, no wait, this was the graveyard, that meant....

"Yes, I am a dark assassin. You have entered the world known as Luclin, I hope you enjoy it here, now please leave my graveyard."

Kzh scrambled up and out as soon as he could get to his feet. It was only then that he realized he was naked. Somewhere deep in his soul he heard laughter....
