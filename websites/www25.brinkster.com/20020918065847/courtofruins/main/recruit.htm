Recruitment Policy

1. Officers may invite anyone they feel would make a good addition to the guild.

2. A new member may be sponsored by two current members to gain entrance.

3. In cases where the new member only has one sponsor, the sponsor must post a recommendation in the Applications forum of the Guild Hall. If no objections are made after 2 days, the new member will be invited.

4. If the new member does not have a sponsor in the guild, they need to post their desire to join in the Applications forum of the Guild Hall. If no objections are made after 2 days, the new member will be invited for a strict probationary period.

5. All new members shall be on a one month probationary period. A violation of the Code of Conduct will result in a warning. If a second violation occures, the member will be /guildremove'd.

6. When an Officer invites a new member, they will obtain the character's pertinent information - name, race, class, level, and valid email address. The Officer will give the new member the link to the web pages and the password to the Members Only section of the Guild Hall.

7. New members are expected to register an ezboard account and post their character info (name, class, race, level) in the Guild Discussions forum of the Guild Hall. They will then be added to the Members page.

8. A visit to the Member Links page to fill out the new member form will result in the new member being added to the guild email list.

9. All new members are expected to read and abide by the Code of Conduct. Ignorance of the CoC will not be accepted as an excuse for violating it.
