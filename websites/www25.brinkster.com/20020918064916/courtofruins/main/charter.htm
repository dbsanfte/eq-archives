Charter

We like to think of the Court as a group of friends who like to adventure together. We help and support each other and try to have fun doing it. We do not want to be an "uber" guild. Our members tell us we have achieved a feeling of "family" in this game and we'd like to keep it that way no matter our size. We realize this is merely a game, entertainment, and we accept it as such. You are not expected to devote your life to it. Our main goal, in fact our only goal, it to have fun.

All are welcome to join the Court. It is suggested that the Nature Lovers avoid the Ogres and Trolls, as you may be mistaken for an easy meal. ;-)
