Firiona Vie was spotted in The Commons this eve. She was heard to give the following speech while being heckled, threatened, and praised.

"As those whom have done harm to me, I bless them this very day. I take up no sword against them that struck me earlier for they shall see the error in their ways soon enough. I have spoke to those whom would listen. They will speak unto you as to what is to come. "

Annubiss interjected, " Lanys will do no harm to us."

Firiona replied, "Lanys serves only herself. As her lust for power consumes her. She will make herself known in due time as she will use destruction for her will."

"In the name of the mother I bless thee all of goodness so that when the day of battle comes we shall all be victorious. Go forth and speak in the name of the mother so that when Lanys attacks we shall be ready."

"Alas I will speak elsewhere. Tunare be with you all."

And with that, she disappeared.
