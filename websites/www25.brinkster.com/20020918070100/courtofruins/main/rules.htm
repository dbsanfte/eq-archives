Code of Conduct

1. The guild tag is to be worn at all times. If you need to be anonymous, please use /roleplay.

2. There is to be no ks'ing. Follow the Play Nice Rules at all times.

3. If you have one Officer character, you may not have another. Exceptions may be made for recruiters.

4. Begging is not tolerated, in the guild or out of it. If you need further explanation, contact your class officer.

5. If you are in a position to help a guild mate, do so.

6. Do not ask the guild to power level you. This does you a disservice in the long run.

7. Dueling should not occur between guild members unless it is an event, a friendly test of skill, or just for fun.

8. Do not lie to, steal from, or attempt to cheat your guild mates.

9. If you have a problem contact an officer. This includes problems with other CoR members as well as problems with other players.

10. Be courteous at all times. No excessive foul language will be tolerated.

11. Do not expect the Guild to "twink" your characters. Items are handed down when and where the benefactor deems appropriate.

Violations of the Code of Conduct will be discussed among the Officers of the Court and may result in anything from a warning to /guildremove, depending on the severity of the violation.
