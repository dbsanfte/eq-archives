  • AgentWeb: Information Retrieval and Knowledge Management - IR and KM resources specifically relating to intelligent software agents. Includes a wide variety of web resources with good descriptions.
  • The Anatomy of a Large-Scale Hypertextual Web Search Engine - The classic paper by Sergey Brin and Lawrence Page on everybody's favorite search engine, when it was still a research project at Stanford University.
  • Arcessa - Develops large-scale information retrieval systems. Features general company information and overviews of markets and products.
  • The Center for Intelligent Information Retrieval - University of Massachusetts research lab focused on efficient access to large, heterogeneous, distributed, text and multimedia databases.
  • Center for Networked Information Discovery and Retrieval - Information on the team, history and projects of this research lab.
  • Delphes Technologies International - Publisher of information retrieval software for personal and corporate knowledge management using natural language technology under the brand name Diogene.
  • The Glasgow Information Retrieval Group - Has a research program aimed at giving better access to multi-media information.
  • Index Data - Services - Index Data offers a wide range of services: courses, software solutions, consultancy aid, and support -- relating to Z39.50, Dublin Core, Metadata, XML.
  • Information Filtering Resources - A comprehensive directory of online resources in information filtering, maintained at the University of Maryland.
  • Information Retrieval - An online book by C. J. Rijsbergen, University of Glasgow
  • Information Retrieval and Information Extraction on the Web - A collection of online resources for research in the field of information retrieval and information extraction from the web.
  • Information Retrieval Research - An up-to-date overview of research in the field of information retrieval.
  • Information Retrieval Systems and Tools - A listing of s to IR software, mostly open-source.
  • Information Retrieval Vocabulary - A useful glossary for Information Retrieval vocabulary.
  • Javaisis 3.0 - JavaIsis is an open source Java application by which you can manage a CDS/ISIS database with any Java Virtual Machine.
  • Knowledge Navigation Suite - A suite of information indexing and classification tools that supports information sharing and textual data mining based on natural language processing, statistical pattern analysis, and neural networks techniques. Supports large-scale terabyte data analysis and visualization.
  • Modern Information Retrieval - A recent IR book, covering algorithms, implementation, query languages, user interfaces, multimedia and web retrieval, and more.
  • MultiCentrix - Software for information mapping, knowledge management, and computer aided thinking.
  • Resources for Text, Speech and Language Processing - A collection of resources in a variety of fields related to text, speech and language processing. These include computational linguistics, information retrieval and machine learning. Here you can find pointers to useful Web sites, as well as lists of relevant books, newsgroups and mailing lists.
  • S.I.R.A (Spidering Information Retrieval Agent) - S.I.R.A (Spidering Information Retrieval Agent) is an application that watches as you browse and suggests pages that may be of interest to you.
  • Text REtrieval Conference (TREC) Home Page - The Text REtrieval Conference (TREC) is an annual information retrieval conference and competition, the purpose of which is to support and further research within the information retrieval community.
  • The unCommon Hub - The unCommon Hub is a suite of components, that when connected and utilized in concert with one another provide you with a powerful information management and data integration tool.
  • Willow - A now discontinued Z39.50 bibliographic information retrieval tool from University of Washington.
