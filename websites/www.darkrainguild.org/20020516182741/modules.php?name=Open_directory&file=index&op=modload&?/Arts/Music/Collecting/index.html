  • The Care and Handling of Recorded Sound Materials - A highly technical paper on the archival maintenance of music recordings. Expanded version of an article initially prepared for the National Library of Canada.
  • A Child's Garden of Record Labels - Scans of labels from 78s and 45s with anecdotes about each record, arranged alphabetically by label name.
  • Cylinder, Disc and Tape Care in a Nutshell - Care, handling and storage of musical recordings in a collections environment, from the US Library of Congress. Well researched and includes an extensive bibliography.
  • Gigposters - Images of gig posters, flyers and handbills from around the world.
  • The Great Brazilian Virtual Guitar Picks Museum - Listing of a collection with pictures, and a picture dictionary of guitar plectrum terminology.
  • International Music Discographies - Large online music price guide, including many rare records and compact discs issued around the world. USA values in dollars; accepts submissions from users.
  • The Internet Museum of Flexi / Cardboard / Oddity Records - Devoted to odd promotional and novelty records, usually printed on paper. Images and RealAudio from a collection.
  • More Music Collectors Resource Pages - British guide to music collecting, featuring many annotated s.
  • Nibble's Record Shop Guides - Guide to record and compact disc shops in San Diego, Los Angeles and Albuquerque.
  • PickNET - Global trading network and club for guitar pick collectors.
  • Rare Record Price Guide - Online guide to UK values for records (and a few CDs), mainly by 80's and 90's artists. Calculated from average prices charged by dealers in the UK magazine "Record Collector."
  • Record Collecting - For collectors of vinyl records, 45s, LPs, 78s, CDs and music memorabilia; presented by the original publishers and founders of Goldmine magazine.
  • Record Collector's Heaven - Yet another site about Exotica, Space Age Pop, Incredibly Strange Music, and weird recordings in general.
  • Record Covers Drawn by Comic Artists - Listing of album covers (CD and vinyl) drawn by comic artists and illustrators. Includes many images. In French and English.
  • Rockbase Plus - CD-ROM database software with data on 337,000 album and single record releases and a comprehensive record collectors music cataloging system.
  • RockPosterCollector.com - History of rock posters and information for collectors.
  • Stoney's Guitar Gallery - Pictures and descriptions of favorite electric guitars posted by their owners.
  • Stylus Shows - US based presenter of music collector conventions; information about their upcoming events.
  • Surround Sound Discography - Listings of surround sound recordings in many recording formats and several encoding systems (Ambisonic, Quadrophonic, Dolby Surround, etc.).
  • Vinyl Bank Record and CD Fairs - UK Midlands based record and CD fair organiser. Find fair dates, venue information, public and dealer information.
  • Vinylville - Information about Usenet groups, dealers, resources and supplies, FAQs and discographies, cleaning and storage, and cataloging software; from a music collector.
