Top: Society: Religion and Spirituality: Atheism: Ethics  (21)
Description




See also:
  • Society: Philosophy: Ethics  (383)
  • Society: Religion and Spirituality: Morality  (30)


  • Against the Moral Argument - Examination and refutation of the moral argument for the existence of God.
  • Are God and Ethics Inseparable or Incompatible? - An in depth treatment of "the moral argument for God" and "the meta-ethical argument for atheism."
  • Atheism and Humanistic Value Theory - Author Michael Martin asks: how are atheism and humanistic value theory related?
  • Atheism, Christian Theism, and Rape - Refutes the claim made by some theists, that atheists can provide no objective reason for not raping people.
  • The Biological Basis of Morality - Scientific fact sheds light on age-old ethical questions. An article by Edward O. Wilson, published in the April 1998 Atlantic Monthly.
  • Does Morality Require God? - An article arguing against the claim that morality requires God.
  • An Empirical Approach to Morality - A paper by Merwin Sibulkin offering an approach to morality which is independent of religious texts and addressing contemporary moral problems.
  • Ethical Oasis - E-zine dealing with ethical and moral issues without reference to god.
  • The Ethics of Belief - Philosophical examination of faith and belief. Three essays: "The Ethics of Belief" by William Kingdon Clifford, "The Will to Believe" by William James, and "An Examination of ‘The Will to Believe’" by A.J. Burger
  • Godlessheathen's Golden Rules Page - Different versions of the Golden Rule that predate Christianity, and citations of empirical research that prove religion is not needed for moral behavior.
  • The Human Basis Of Laws And Ethics - An essay showing theological values, as opposed to human-oriented values, to be the most baseless and arbitrary.
  • Human Ethics and Morality - Exploring the relationship between ethics, morality, evolution, psychology, culture and genetics.
  • Is Morality a Matter of Taste? - An article presenting a philosophical justification for rejecting purely subjective ethics.
  • Kant: The Moral Order - A summation of Kant's attempts to formulate a moral law by reason and human experience.
  • The Limits of Tolerance - An article by Paul Kurtz exploring the need for secular humanists to embrace tolerance, freedom, and diversity, while recognizing there are certain limits to tolerance when actions become harmful to others.
  • Moral Foundations of Atheism and Christianity - Mark I Vultec's response to the argument that atheism provides no reason to behave morally.
  • Morality and Atheism - An essay by Josh Rubak discussing whether ethics and morality require religion. Has feedback area.
  • Morality and Atheism - An examination of the theist assertion that one cannot accept both atheism and objective moral standards and that objective moral standards prove that God exists.
  • Morality Without God - Jonathan Blumen discusses whether morality is evidence of God. From the Ethical Spectacle.
  • The Relativity of Biblical Ethics - Joe Edward Barnhard argues, contrary to fundamentalists and evangelicals, that the Bible itself exemplifies moral relativism.
  • The Secular Sphinx - Michael Schermer explores provisional ethics.
