Top: Games: Roleplaying: Magazines and E-zines  (23)

  • Alarums and Excursions - A&E is a photocopied monthly roleplaying game APA (amateur press association) with contributors from across the English-speaking world, begun in 1975. Probably the best known RPG zine.
  • Atlas of Adventure - Monthly magazine featuring industry news and interviews.
  • B-WARE Ultimate Gamesite - Previews and samples from a roleplaying magazine published on CD-ROM.
  • Carnel Fanzine - Online counterpart of the British roleplaying zine. Includes article index and some new articles.
  • Critical Miss - The webzine for dysfunctional roleplayers. Includes adventures, game advice, convention reports, and free games.
  • The Death Cookie Online Magazine - From Hex Entertainment, Inc., the makers of the role-playing parody, QAGS, comes a sharp-witted online magazine devoted to gamer & fringe culture. Reviews, Fiction, Upcoming Releases & Events, and more.
  • Dungeon Crawler Magazine - Free online magazine covering gaming in all incarnations. Features Articles, Reviews, and an adventure scenario with each issue.
  • Green Dragon's RPG Site - Eventually: webmag, Game ideas, reviews, news, s. So far: a few White Wolf, Atlas Games items.
  • The Guild Companion - Monthly online gaming magazine with articles, fiction and reviews. Focuses on Rolemaster and related games.
  • Imazine - Freeware online and paper magazine - reviews, writeups, letters. Available as zipped HTML and PDF files.
  • Interregnum Roleplaying/Science Fiction Magazine - APA covering roleplaying games and science fiction/fantasy published since 1994. Includes sample articles, sample issues in PDF format, and subscription information.
  • Paradigma Roleplaying Journal - Online magazine "supporting roleplaying games of grim beauty." Multi-system articles, press releases and newsletters from Chaosium, Games Workshop, White Wolf.
  • Places to Go, People to Be - Internet fanzine devoted to roleplaying, and the roleplayers of Brisbane.
  • Polyhedron - Official magazine of the RPGA Network.
  • Pyramid - Gaming magazine published weekly. Annual subscriptions required to access most articles.
  • Roleplaying Tips E-Zine - Newsletter with advice for gamemasters.
  • The RPG Times - A monthly magazine covering different types or roleplaying.
  • Sabledrake Magazine - Monthly webzine.
  • Serendipity's Circle - Features subscription information and sample articles from the quarterly magazine of "horror and weird fantasy" gaming.
  • Solomon's Dungeon Adventures Magazine Index - Unofficial cover gallery and list of published adventures.
  • Thoth's Homepage - Support site for Thoth Libram's gaming `zine. Features excerpts, reviews, art, rulings, and subscription information. Accepts submissions.
  • Twilighttime Roleplaying Zine - Bi-weekly e-zine with articles for game masters, RPG writers, and roleplayers.
  • Wounds Unlimited - Monthly webzine of news, reviews, adventures, and advice.
