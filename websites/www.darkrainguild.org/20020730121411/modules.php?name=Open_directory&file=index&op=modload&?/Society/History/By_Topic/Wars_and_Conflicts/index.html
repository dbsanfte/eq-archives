  • Allexperts Military History Q&A - Volunteer historians who answer one-on-one individualized questions about military history for free. Also includes a discussion forum.
  • AmericanMercenary.com - Selection of s on military history, strategy, weapons and technology, and the influence of mercenaries on modern warfare.
  • The Associates of Military History - An organization founded in December of 1997 dedicated to the study of military history. Membership is free.
  • Documents in Military History - "Hillsdale College's collection of primary sources for military history ... currently focused primarily on European military history before the twentieth century, although we intend to incorporate the American and non-Western military heritages".
  • The Great Wars - Overview of the conflicts in Europe from 1871 to 1945.
  • John's Military History Page - Virtual tours of Revolutionary War and Civil War battlefields and armored fighting vehicles.
  • Leaders and Battles Database - Searchable database of major conflicts, battles and leaders from the Persian Wars to World War II.
  • Lost Wars and Battles Internet List - A military history group for people fascinated with the countries and armies who have LOST wars and battles.
  • Military Lore Project - Website is designed to honor soldiers, sailors, airmen and Marines who had camps, compounds, airfields and other facilities named in their honor after their deaths.
  • The Military Revolution - Military Developments in Europe in the 15th, 16th and 17th centuries.
  • New York Military Affairs Symposium - Newsletters, forthcoming events and a good selection of military s from this academic organisation.
  • OnWar.com - Military history site that includes a database of world conflicts and other military events 1800-1999.
  • The Patriot Files - A collection of articles are dedicated to the men and women of the armed forces and the many people who support them.
  • Rongstad's Worldwide Military History - Huge collection of military history s, with emphasis on the US.
  • The Simonides Group - A non-profit organization devoted to remembering the world's military heritage through providing research, restoration, preservation and archaeological support. Web services description and list of hosted groups.
  • Society for Military History - Devoted to stimulating and advancing the study of military history in the United States. Site describes publications, membership information, conferences, leadership, and SMH-sponsored awards and prizes.
  • Trench Warfare: a written history , 1400 - 1918 - History of the development of trench warfare, from the late Middle Ages through to the First World War.
  • War and Conflict - Past, Present, Future - Well presented site that "aims to provide comprehensive information about the theory and conduct of war". Largely written by high school students, which sometimes shows in the quality of the articles.
  • War Links - A selection of s, organized by war and time period.
  • The War Page - Comprehensive site with information, letters and photos from a US perspective of the US Civil War, Philippine Campaign, Mexican Service, World War I, World War II, Korea and Vietnam.
  • The War Times Journal - An on-line magazine of military history which features original articles and archives covering wars and campaigns of all periods.
  • Wars - Summaries of selected major wars and conflicts in history, starting with the Mahabharata and ending with the Persian Gulf War.
  • Wars of the World - Collection of s to military history sites.
  • Web Page of Durfee - Gateway to information on the American Revolution and on medieval Europe.
  • West Point Atlases - A collection of on-line maps covering wars from the eighteenth century to the present day.
