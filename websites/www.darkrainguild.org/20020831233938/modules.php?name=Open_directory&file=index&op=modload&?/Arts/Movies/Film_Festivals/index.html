  • Cape Fear Independent Film Network - A coastal Carolina based non-profit independent film organization whose goals include showcasing independent film, education and training of filmmakers and students, and a yearly film festival.
  • Creepfest, The Los Angeles Independent Horror Film Festival... and music too - A pagan celebration of all things dark and delirious in film, animation, writers, cyber-creations and childrens horror stories. Awards and showcases all categories as well as bands on soundtracks.
  • Darklight Digital Film Festival - A select mix of screenings, seminars, art exhibition, and live events which explore and celebrate the creative potential of art, film and music in digital technology.
  • DFILM - Digital Film Festival - A travelling and online showcase of digital film made with this powerful new filmmaking technology.
  • The Directors View Film Festival Fairfield County, CT and Westchester County, NY - Site dedicated to film festival held annually in February honoring the best modern directors, independent film, international student films and classics in true Joseph L. Mankiewicz style.
  • The Directory of International Film and Video Festivals - Lists over 500 international film, television and video festivals, giving details on how and when to enter these events.
  • Filmjunkie - Presenter of the Filmjunkie Underground Film Festival and junkie journalists review other Film Fests
  • Flicker Austin - Bi-monthly film festival in Austin, TX that features short films by area filmmakers and all other enthusiasts who dare submit via mail.
  • Over The Fence Comedy Film Festival - Australia's largest touring comedy film festival proudly presented by the Film & Television Institute and Play it Safe
  • Park City from The Onion AV Club - A sideways look at Sundance, Slamdance and other film festivals.
  • ResFest - This festival takes place in several cities and spotlights content that has been produced using dig1tal production equipment. Last year there were lots of shorts and one feature length project.
  • Roze Film Dagen / Pink Film Days - Gay and lesbian film festival of Amsterdam, Netherlands.
  • 36th Chicago International Film Festival - This is North America's Longest Running Film Festival. This is a great festival. Not only is the content good, but all the scheduling information in on their site so it is easy to plan which films to attend.
