  • The Virtual Wall   - Digital, interactive legacy memorializing the men and women who gave their lives in Vietnam.

  • American Heroes - A place honoring some True American Heroes from the Vietnam War.
  • American Heroes Remembered - A tribute to the Veterans of America.
  • American Veterans - Call of Duty - Honoring American Heroes, both famous and obscure, whose values, deeds, and sacrifice define the character of our nation and the heritage of its people. Dues solicitation.
  • Cyr's Military Site - A dedication to all Service Members and their families. Poems, photos and s.
  • Dale and Mary's Dedication to the American Veteran - Dedicated to America's POW/MIAs and Veterans featuring America's Prisoners of War-Missing in Action, Veterans, and USMC HMM-262/Vietnam Flying Tigers
  • Disabled Veterans' Life Memorial Foundation - Commemorates the millions of US veterans who become disabled while defending the United States.
  • Empty Chairs Memorial - Dedicated to the memory of all of our brothers and sisters, from all nations, who paid the ultimate sacrifice in the war in SouthEast Asia.
  • Freedoms Sacred Call A Tribute to our Veterans - Freedoms Sacred Call is a poem written by Orlan Hall to help us remember, understand, value and grow in our appreciation of and gratitude for our freedom and its price. Beautifully graphics, this poem is for sale and to be sent with an introduction.
  • Gwinnett's Fallen Heroes Memorial Committee Inc., Honoring Veterans and Public Safety. - Many heroes walk among us today--Veterans, Law Enforcement Officers, Firefighters, and Emergency Medical Personnel. Some made the supreme sacrifice while fighting for our freedom; While protecting and serving.
  • Huntleys In The Military - Since 1675, Huntleys have been donning uniforms, taking up arms, and fighting to defend this great land and its inhabitants, This site is dedicated to preserving the memory of their actions.
  • Malaya Borneo Veterans - For veterans and others who are interested in the Malayan Emergency 1948-60 and the "Confrontation" between Malaysia and Indonesia 1963-66
  • Medics Listed on the Vietnam Memorial Wall - Names and information on Army Medics who died in Vietnam and were listed on the Vietnam Memorial Wall.
  • A Memorial for United States Veterans - Those who are serving, served, died for their country, and for the families that didn't receive a letter from the government upon the death of their service member like my great uncle, Samuel Bufford LeMay.
  • Memorial Web Park - Complete web-based Memorial services with genealogy support.
  • My Salute To All Veterans - Dedicated to veterans.
  • My Tribute to America's Heroes - Just a small thank you to all the veteran's who were willing to make the ultimate sacrifice for our freedom.
  • New Zealand Artillery - Artillery in New Zealand. A collection of historical articles, images and personal reminisences from professional gunners (serving and retired).
  • The Outpost - Pow-Mia you are not forgotten. To all my brothers-in-arms, and the 212th Military Police Sentry Dogs Long Binh, Vietnam, a dedication to you.
  • Remembering - a Tribute to Veterans - A tribute to all who served their country. Lay a virtual bouquet in honor of a friend or loved one. Links to veteran's organizations and all major US conflicts.
  • Sittingbourne Remembers - The story of one small English town's losses during WWI.
  • Special Operations Memorial Foundation - The Special Operations Memorial at MacDill AFB, Florida is for the Spec Ops Personnel of the Air Force, Army, and Navy around the Globe who, throughout history, have risked and or given their lives for freedom.
  • Tampa Bay Moving Wall 2000 - Tampa Bay 7th Annual Salute to Vietnam Veterans and The Moving Wall.
  • 7th Annual Tampa Bay Moving Wall - A dedication to the Moving Wall and those that serve.
  • 417th Bombardment Group (L) My Dad - A tribute to my dad and the men who served in the United States Army Air Force in the 417th Bombardment Group (L)
  • 44th Infantry Division, 324th Infantry Regiment, Company D - Dedicated to William F. Blaise, KIA France December 3, 1944 and all 324th Infantry veterans.
  • A Tribute To Veterans of the Millennium - From WW1 to the Gulf War. Music and s.
  • Veterans Memorial - A place where you can post the name of a Veteran who has died or is a POW/MIA.
  • Vietnam Wars - Dedicated to the fellow soldiers who participated in the Vietnam War together.
  • The WWII Chinese American Veterans Project - OCA is putting out a call to all Chinese American veterans who were inducted or enlisted into the United States Armed Forces between 1939-1945 to participate in the WWII Chinese American Veterans Project.
