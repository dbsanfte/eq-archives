  • Bookworm's Lair - Site has bibliographies of many authors, with updated bestsellers lists, and reviews.
  • Catharton Authors - An extensive, alphabetically sorted listing of author-related resources on the internet
  • Celebration of Women Writers - " . . . on-line editions of older, often rare, out-of-copyright works. We choose works from a range of areas to indicate the variety of interests of women writers." Includes s to texts both historic and contemporary, hosted both on-site and off, as well as s to websites related to specific authors and their writing.
  • Classics at the Online Literature Library - The Online Literature Library's archived electronic texts available online, indexed by author.
  • Corwin's AuthorWeb - "A large and ever-growing site offering information on authors and their books, as well as an interface for visitors to submit their own book reviews."
  • Dalkey Archive Press - Features interviews with their authors.
  • Eclectics Authors - Official web pages for several published authors of genre fiction including romance, mystery, and western. Includes writing contests and newsletter.
  • Eye on Books - Brief interviews with authors of new and current fiction and non-fiction, presented in RealAudio and Windows Media streaming audio.
  • Favorite Authors Discussion Fleet - Author discussions.
  • Great Writers and Poets - A fairly wide-ranging directory of s to resources on the 'net for numerous authors, including information on Nobel Prize winners.
  • Incompetech's British Author Series - A witty and scholarly take on various venerated British authors.

  • Librarie - A "graphically enriched virtual library with multi-page sites dedicated to various writers and thinkers. Bibliographies and biographies included."
  • The Literary Menagerie - ". . . is a collection of the best of the best author-based sites on the net. Includes mystery poem of the month."
  • Literary Pilgrimages - Visits to homes and haunts of Mark Twain, John Steinbeck, the Bronte sisters and others.
  • The Literature and Poetry Page - Brief author biographies with commentary and s to selected works.
