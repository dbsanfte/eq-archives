Top: News

Please do not attempt to list your site here - please find a more appropriate sub-category. (Only really big multi-national news sites are listed here).

It may be worth looking for an appropriate sub-category under Regional/ or Reference/.

more information (editors only)
News sites provide material informing, explaining, or commenting on the events and issues of the day. In addition, web sites with information 'about' news and about news reporting are within the scope of the category and its subcategories. This might include essays on current events or sites describing news sources in detail.

The Open Directory Project's news section includes a wide variety of news s. The Top: News section is a cross section of some of those s.


Analysis and Opinion

Sites that provide in-depth background and reporting, analysis and commentary on issues. Most s are to in-depth feature packages or archives of media or other websites. Most of those also contain s to current material as well. It also contains s to other categories with background material or commentary and opinions.

Breaking News

Only those sites which update throughout the day will be included.
Find late breaking news stories on a variety of topics from around the world. Features sites that update frequently as news happens.

By Subject

Listings of s to other sections of the Open Directory providing news and information sources focused on particular topics or subject areas.

Chats and Forums

Sites for discussion about news, media and journalism.

Colleges and Universities

Only sites in English are listed in this section of the directory. Sites in other languages should be submitted to the closest World category.

Please submit only sites for campus-based news media to this category. Sites listed here may also be submitted to the appropriate school category in Reference: Education: Colleges and Universities.

Alumni and reunion-oriented sites should be submitted to Reference: Education: Alumni Directories
This category is for campus-produced college and university news media.

Current Events

Submissions are no longer being accepted in the top level of this category. All general news sites should be submitted to News. Any sites with any topical interest or alternative slant should be sumbitted to the appropriate News subcategory.

more information (editors only)
Current Events is a section for people looking for current news and commentary on hot news items such as elections, disasters, and military or political conflicts. For general news about a specific subject, check out News: By Subject.

Directories

Sites containing alphabetical or classified lists of news resources.

Extended Coverage

Sites with archives of various news issues which were covered over an extended period of time. These may include news and commentary, audio and video clips, background information, media analysis and external s.

Internet Broadcasts

Streaming audio and video coverage of news events on the Web. This category is meant to focus on the variety of news providers, much like the scope of News/Newspapers and Arts/Television/News, rather than a directory of topic areas elsewhere in the ODP.

Newspapers

This category contains s to the web sites for newspapers. These are often online versions of print daily or weekly publications containing news and opinion of current events, feature articles, photographs, and usually advertising.

Personalized News

All inappropriate submissions will be deleted.
This category is for customizable web sites or services which allow you to see the news that you want and which filter out what you do not want to see.

Politics

Contains sites for media coverage of political issues, events, and happenings in the United States and around the world. Also contains pathways to related areas for in-depth information related to specific ideologies, parties, or issues.

Regional

Links to news sources whose focus is a particular region of the world.

Satire

more information (editors only)
Humor on or about current news events and/or personalities and issues of topical interest. Includes: comedy, parody and satire.

Services

News Services provide news headlines, stories, photos, and breaking news on a regular schedule, frequently updated throughout the day, for distribution to subscribers of those services. Wire services and news syndication services are typical examples of the news services collected here.
