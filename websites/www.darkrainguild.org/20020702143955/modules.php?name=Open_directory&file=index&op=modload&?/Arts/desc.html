Top: Arts

Sites primarily about arts or artists (actors, painters, musicians, etc.) should be listed in the appropriate Arts category.
Sites primarily devoted to selling products, even artwork or artists' products, should be listed in Shopping.
Businesses that serve the arts communities without directly creating art themselves - agents, casting companies, financiers - are listed under the appropriate category of Business.
Sites about art that is produced or viewed through computers are still listed under Arts. Sites about computer tools or data formats that can be used either for art or non-artistic work should be listed under Computers.
Activities that entertain their participants more than the audience belong in Recreation.
The study of non-artistic communication is listed under Science/Social_Sciences.
Non English language sites belong in World.

more information (editors only)
The ODP Arts category contains English language sites about art, or "the use of skill and imagination in the creation of aesthetic objects, environments, or experiences that can be shared with others." This includes the "liberal arts," concerned with skill of expression in language, speech, and reasoning, and the "fine arts," concerned with affecting aesthetics directly, and especially affecting the sense of beauty. (Quotes and paraphrases from Britannica.com.) Art is an abstract and subjective quality, it can be studied, but can not be objectively measured, counted, weighed, or absolutely compared; it can only appeal to the viewer's or audience's personal senses.


Animation

Before submitting any sites to the Animation category, please take the time to browse through the subcategories to make sure your site is included in a category appropriate for the content of your site.

  • We will not accept any sites submitted to the Animation category which even remotely contain either softcore or hardcore pornography - this includes any adult Anime/Hentai sites. Sites of that nature should be submitted only to the Adult: Arts: Animation category.

  • If you are a dealer in animation collectibles, ranging from animation cels to cartoon memorabilia and related merchandise, please submit your site to the Shopping category.

  • If you have a site pertaining to animated cartoons, animated series or other animated TV programs that you would like to submit, the Cartoons category is the proper place to submit them.

    Digital

    This category comprises artistic works that need a digital media or support to express its essence. Two ways artistic digital works are generally identified are through their multi-media and interactive content.

    Before submitting a site in Arts/Digital please consider these categories:
    • Web Design: Computers/Internet/Commercial_Services/Web_Design_and_Development.
    • Flash Movies: Computers/Multimedia/Flash_and_Shockwave/Animations.
    • Digital Illustrations and Portfolios: Business/Industries/Design/Graphic Design (commercial) or
      Arts/Illustration/Digital_Illustration (non commercial).
    • Personal sites displaying computer graphics work: Arts/Visual_Arts/Computer_Graphics/Personal_Galleries/
    Since the 1960s, computers have been used to create, enhance, or alter art in various ways. With the increasing availability of affordable multimedia equipment computers have become one of the most accessible mediums for art.
    The Internet has developed as the most challenging aspect for this community, and art is thriving on the Net, both in conceptual form and in web-based graphics and animations.
    We know `Digital Arts' is a sketchy term and possibly not the best. But today this seems to be understood by the artists working with digital support. Here we have defined Digital Arts as [Art + Computer + Interactivity + Multimedia].

    Photography

    PLEASE READ THESE GUIDELINES CAREFULLY. FOLLOWING THEM AND THE SPECIFIC SUBCATEGORY GUIDELINES WILL ALLOW US TO ADD YOUR SITE TO THE DIRECTORY AS QUICKLY AS POSSIBLE.
    • Find the single most appropriate category for your site. With few exceptions, sites are listed in the directory in one category only.
    • Once you have found the most appropriate category for your site, please read the specific category description and guidelines.
    • Submit your site using your main URL. With few exceptions, we do not deep sites in Arts/Photography.
    • Format your title as requested in the specific subcategory guidelines.
    • Read the descriptions of other sites in the category you would like to submit to, and try to use a similar style for your own description.
    • Sites which are mirrors of other sites or that redirect to another site will not be listed. Only the main site/URL will be listed.


    All sites submitted are reviewed by the directory's editors. They take the final decision on whether your site is accepted, the title, and the description. The description will be based on the information you provide and the review of the website. Sites will be placed in the single most applicable and highest category based on our review. Sites which are continually submitted to the directory, using either a main URL, mirrors or sub-pages, will be banned.



    All sites are reviewed periodically and are subject to changes in placement, description, and possible removal from the directory, based on this review.



    Please submit sites showing photographic work which is not for sale online to an appropriate subcategory of Arts/Photography/Photographers or Arts/Photography/Techniques and Styles.



    Please submit all for-hire services such as wedding, portrait, commercial-advertising, aerial, architectural or fashion photography sites to the proper subcategory of Business/Industries/Arts_and_Entertainment/Photography/Photographers.



    If you are selling photographic equipment or products online, please submit your site to Shopping/Photography. Such sites will NOT be listed in Arts/Photography.



    If your primary focus is on selling prints online, please submit your site to Shopping/Visual_Arts/Photography. Such sites will NOT be listed in Arts/Photography.



    If the primary focus of your site is selling licensed or royalty free photographs from your archives, please submit to Business/Industries/Arts_and_Entertainment/Photography/Stock.



    All sites featuring photographic services which are available online such as retouching, image manipulation, restoration, custom printing, scanning, and photofinishing are listed in Shopping/Photography/Services. Please submit all such sites to that category, and NOT to Arts/Photography.



    If the text on your site is not in English, please submit your to the photography category in the proper language in World. For example, Spanish language sites should be submitted to World/Español/Artes/Artes Plásticas/Fotografía.

    Thank you.

    more information (editors only)
    Arts/Photography is
    • for sites featuring photographers (amateurs, semi-professionals, and professionals) or photography studios and their work, abilities, and artistic skills.
    • for sites providing information about photographic equipment, techniques, and services.


    The primary focus of your site has to be on Photography. It should be evident that you consider yourself to be a photographer. Collections of snapshots, or sites featuring images of poor technical quality will NOT be listed anywhere in Arts/Photography.



    Arts/Photography is NOT
    • for sites selling equipment, products, or prints online.
    • for sites where professional and commercial photographers offer their photographic services for hire to clients.
    • for sites featuring photographic services which are available online (retouching, image manipulation, scanning, photofinishing, photo labs etc.)


    Such sites will NOT be listed anywhere in Arts/Photography.



    If the identity of the photographer or studio is secondary or of no importance, and the intent of your site is simply to show pictures of a particular topic, please submit your site to that topic's category, and NOT to Arts/Photography.



    For example, if you have a site with lots of photographs of cars and the identity of the photographer[s] is unknown or of no importance, then please submit your site to a suitable category in Recreation/Autos.
