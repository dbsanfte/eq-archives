When we started grouping, Kaedo popped into Fear to scout a bit and said something that made my heart stop.
"There are no Mobs"
Luckily, the mobs started popping as he sat there, so we hurried in to take advantage of the lack of ZI mobs. Dark Rain proceeded to Own Fear. At first, LLuweni and the trackers would call out the numbers of mobs on track as they spawned and the numbers got higher. Then, they started calling out the numbers as they got smaller.
