Top: Business: Major Companies: Company Information  (37)
Description


  • Corporate Information   - Extensive collection of corporate profiles, industry research reports and s to company Web sites worldwide.

  • Annual Report Gallery - Annual Reports listings, online versions in HTML and PDF. Over 1000 annual reports, most of the fortune 500 reports listed.
  • Annual Report Service - Includes online and free hard copy annual reports. Links to annual report sites on public companies trading on the NYSE, NASDAQ, AMEX, and OTC exchanges.
  • Annual Reports & Accounts from Corporate Reports - Company reports and accounts, and financial information from UK corporations.
  • Annual Reports Library - Annual Reports Library has built a collection of over 1.5 million original reports (and proxies) from corporations, foundations, banks, mutual funds and public institutions.
  • Barron's Annual Reports Service - Order hard copies of annual reports online, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Online copies unavailable at this site.
  • BusinessJeeves.Com - Business and Company Information Directory - A directory for both public and private businesses or company information around the world.
  • Carol World - Free corporate online services offering one point access to corporate annual reports and other investor information from companies in the UK, USA and Asia.
  • Compan-e-Report - Following the format of a corporate Annual Report, the Compan-e-Report provides in-depth analysis of America’s top corporations in a straightforward, entertaining, on-demand video webcast.
  • Companies Online - Find company web sites by name, industry, city, or ticker symbol.
  • CompanyDescriptions.com - Database of company profiles and descriptions. Includes financial and address information for each company in addition to a comprehensive description of business activities.
  • The Corporate Library - Central repository for research, study and critical thinking about the nature of the modern global corporation. Special focus on corporate governance and relationships between company management, their boards and shareowners.
  • CorpTech - Data and information on high technology manufacturing, development and service companies.
  • Edgar Database - Database for US Security and Exchange Commission filings required for publicly traded companies.
  • E-line Financials - The all-under-one-roof finance and investment resource for individual investors. Stock quotes, company profiles, portfolio tracking, annual reports and much more.
  • Facts Online, Inc. - Hot Companies with New Fundings - Listing of hot technology-based companies. With this fee-based services, you get address, phones, concise description, CEO, funding history, web site and more. This service is provided on the web and in database files for your personal computer. Site currently has address and phone number information available for free.
  • Fortune Datastore - Download the official Fortune 500, Fortune 100 and Global 500 databases of contact and financial information for the world's largest companies.
  • Global Reports, LLC - Annual report provider and an ever-expanding archivist of international financial information. Catalogs and stores financial information from 30,000 companies in over 84 countries.
  • Guide to Understanding Financials - Provides a basic introduction to reading the financial statements in a company's annual report.
  • Historic Corporate Annual Reports - SCETI - This site contains facsimiles of historic corporate annual reports from the Lippincott Library collection at the University of Pennsylvania. The reports are in Adobe Portable Document Format (PDF) and the full text of the reports is indexed and searchable.
  • Hoover's Online - The Ultimate Source for Company Information. Premium services require subscription.
  • HUGIN - Offers access to press releases for European listed companies via free subscription-based e-mail. Site includes access to annual reports, press releases, and other financial news and information about European companies.
  • Indiainfoline - Indian Corporates - Detailed research reports of 500+ Indian companies, and a database of corporate information.
  • Investfacts - Allows public companies to post their profiles for free. Investors can then browse and search through the profiles to aid their research. Focuses on small cap companies.
  • Investor Relations Asia - Database of financial information on listed companies in Asia including annual reports, interim reports and press releases.
  • Investor Relations Information Network - IRIN - Instant access to companies' annual reports, quarterlies, fact books and press releases in their original format.
  • Investquest - Search for comprehensive investor presentations and financial documents for 10,000+ public companies.
  • McGregor BFA - Supplier of real-time and historical fundamental information on South African listed companies.
  • OnlineAnnuals.com - Resources for the investor relations community, including a Q&A and s to online annual reports offered by the Fortune 500.
  • Plunkett Research, Ltd. - Provider of industry information, market research, trends, company profiles, and executive mailing lists.
  • Public Register's Annual Report Service - PRARS - Provides free annual reports on over 3,600 public companies trading on the NYSE, NASDAQ, AMEX, and OTC exchanges.
  • Researching Company Information - Tutorial on researching company financial data using annual reports and disclosure documents. Sites contains s to annual report databases and Web sites. Service of the University of British Columbia Library.
  • Risk Management Internet Services - Databases - Business and Finance - Domestic and international company profiles and research. Access to financial market databases such as SEC filings and stock exchange quotes. Subscription based service.
  • SEDAR - Access securities related information for Canadian companies. Filing with SEDAR started January 1, 1997, and is now mandatory for most reporting issuers in Canada.
  • Street Investor Information Center - Links to company financial reports, news worthy items, corporate profiles or annual reports.
  • Wall Street Research Net - Your one stop for financial research. Use Research A Company to access SEC documents, home pages, news, charts, statistical analysis and more.
  • 123 World - Listing of official sites of the Fortune 500 companies and other leading industrial establishments of our world. Offers direct s to official corporate homepages.
