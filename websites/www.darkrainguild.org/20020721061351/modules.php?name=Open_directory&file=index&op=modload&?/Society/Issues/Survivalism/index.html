  • Team Delta Authentic Military Experiences - An elite unit of former military personnel that offers realistic military experiences.
  • Thoughts on Being Prepared - Survivalism is just one step beyond emergency preparedness. Normal emergency preparations may be a few days or a few weeks worth of food, while the survivalist will have several months or several years worth of food. It is the difference between being prepared until help arrives and being prepared because help may never arrive.
