  • Sufism's Many Paths   - An annotated and explanatory web directory of Sufism designed for students, scholars, and the general public. It includes sections on major Sufis and Orders; doctrine and practice; Qur'an commentary; definitions of Sufism; Sufism in the West; Sufi women and the relationship between Sufism and Islam.

  • The Abode of the Message - Ongoing Sufi retreat program offering individual and group retreats with trained, experienced guides in a spiritual community of 50 to 100 people in the Berkshire mountain area of upstate New York.
  • Afterhours Sufism Stories - Collection of Sufism stories, including the stories of the famous Nasruddin.
  • Aisha Bewley - An eclectic mix of material on Sufism and fiqh (jurisprudence) from the Maliki school.
  • Al Ghazali - Henry Snyder's project on the great scholar and Sufi Imam al-Ghazali. It includes a short biography; a brief intellectual background section, and a summarization and evaluation of one portion of "The Incoherence of the Philosophers."
  • Al Rashad - Sheikh Youssef Al Hassany's teachings on Sufism.
  • Almiraj Sufi Books - Australian based internet bookseller stocking an extensive range of books on Islam, Sufism and comparative religion.
  • As-Sunnah Foundation of America - Sufi organization website with information about events; publications; a fatwa service; prayer resources and e-cards.
  • Attasia Tarikah - Information about the tariqah established by Imam Abdulla Bin Alawi Al-Attas. It includes information about his lineage, life and teachings.
  • Australian Centre for Sufism - The symbiosis of Sufism and psychology assisting people on their spiritual path to God.
  • The Blaketashi Darwishes - The Sufism of the West - Dedicated to the continual re-incorporation of the Perennial Philosophy into Western Culture, a function performed in the East by the Sufis.
  • Chishti Habibi Order - South African based site with s to fellow organizations around the world; information about tasawwuf; biographies of Sufi saints and reflections on social justice.
  • Egyptian Society for Spiritual and Cultural Research (ESSCR) - Out of the conviction that man is spirit enclosed in matter, ESSCR devotes its efforts to awaken man to that realization.
  • Halveti-Jerrahi Tariqat - An international, traditional Muslim Sufi Order of Dervishes.
  • Haqqani Sufi Foundation - Haqqani Foundation Homepage, information about the Naqshbandi-Haqqani Sufi Order. In multiple languages, from English to Russian
  • Hazrat Khwaja Gharibnawaz - Offers information on the life and teachings of the Sufi saint Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chisty (R.A.).
  • The Heart - British based (Naqshbandi) Sufi site with information and an online store.
  • Ibis Books - A collection of book recommendations on Sufi-inspired themes.
  • International Association of Sufism - American based organization promoting Sufism and providing a variety of events; social services and publishing books, periodicals and AV material.
  • Islah-ul-Muslimeen - Official site of Jamaat Islah-ul-Muslimeen, teaching and propogating Islam world-wide.
  • Islam and the Sufi Tradition of Chishti Qadhiri - Sufism, an intrinsic part of Islam is explained according to the sufi tradition of Chishti Qadhiri with strict adherence to Qur'an and the sunnah.
  • Islam The Freedom to Serve by Sayyid nazim Efendi - About this little book, taken from Sheikh Nazim´s lectures to his pupils, forces us to think deeply about where we are in our psychological spiritual and physical world.
  • Maktab Tarighat Oveyssi Shahmaghsoudi - A nonprofit religious and educational organization devoted to teaching the way of self-knowledge.
  • Meditate and relax at Islami Roohani Mission - A site about meditation, relaxation as well as finding your "maqsood" i.e. your purpose of life here!
  • Moon Over Medina: A Sufi Bookstore - Small collection of sufi books to be ordered via e-mail. Nice text on sufism.
  • Naqshbandi Homepage - Islamic Sufi perspectives from the Naqshbandi way, and way of practice
  • Naqshbandiya Foundation For Islamic Education - Naqshbandiya Foundation For Islamic Education(NFIE) is non-profit Islamic Educational&Charitable Organization dedicated to serve Muslims&non-muslims with a special focus on Tasawwuf(Sufism),the beautiful path to love,peace&happiness.
  • Nimatullahi - Principles and practices of the Nimatullahi Sufi order.
  • Qawwali Page - Information about the Qawwali as performed in India and Pakistan.
  • A Sufi Bookstore - Mail order Sufi Books.
  • Sufi Circle - Chef Zaynah's site devoted to various aspects of Sufism including a lexicon and bibliography; social commentary; and scholarly works on Sufism.
  • Sufi Resources - List of Sufi-related internet resources.

  • Sufism Sufis and Sufi Orders - Gateway for the study of the varieties of Sufism. The site was made by western scholar.
  • The Threshold Society and Mevlevi Order - A non-profit educational foundation which offers training programs, seminars and retreats around the world in Sufism and spiritual psychology.
  • Threshold Society and Mevlevi Order - Representing the Mevlevi order in America, drawing its inspiration from the teachings of Jalaluddin Rumi.
  • The Zahuri Sufi Website - Dedicated to Dr Zahurul Hasan Sharib an emminent Sufi of the Qadri, Chishti, Gudri Shahi orders.Contains Qur'anic commentary, sufi poetry, articles, art, recent publications, reviews and reference library.
