Top: Shopping: Health

Sites selling health and beauty products to the consumer are listed in this category. The beauty subcategories are sorted above the line.


Alternative

Includes sites which offer a "variety" of alternative health products.

Sites which offer a "specific" type of alternative health product, such as herbal remedies, nutritional supplements, aromatherapy, etc. should submit to the more specific subcategory.
Sites which offer a "variety" of alternative health products.

Sites which offer a "specific" type of alternative health products, such as herbal remedies, nutritional supplements, aromatherapy, etc. should submit to the more specific category.

Beauty

This category includes sites that sell general beauty products and sites that sell a very wide range of beauty products online. The subcategories of this category offer sites which sell more specific product ranges.

Conditions and Diseases

Features sites that sell products to treat specific conditions and diseases. Please submit to an appropriate subcategory as no sites are placed in the main category.

Dental

This category is for sites with online sales to the consumer.

Sites providing service and sales to the dental profession should be submitted to Business/Industries/Healthcare/Products_and_Services/Dentistry or one of its sub-categories.

Sites about businesses that manufacture consumer dental products, that are not selling online, should be submitted to Business/Industries/Manufacturing/Consumer_Products/Health_and_Beauty.

Sites offering Ameriplan coverage should be submitted to Business/Opportunities/Networking-MLM/A/AmeriPlan



Sites offering Oxyfresh products should be submitted to Business/Opportunities/Networking-MLM/O/Oxyfresh



Submission to the correct category greatly reduces any delay in listing the website.
No Category description.

Disabilities

Disability products sold online directly to consumers.

Please do not submit individual companies to this level, unless there is no suitable subcategory. Also do a keyword search before submitting as there are other categories for medical equipment and supplies.

  • Submit sites for businesses serving the healthcare industry to Business/Industries/Healthcare/Products and Services/Disability


  • Submit sites selling products to treat specific health conditions to Health/Products and Shopping


  • Submit sites for companies that only sell in local areas to the appropriate Regional category


Before submitting your listing, please first review the Shopping Guidelines and the Shopping FAQ, since these pages apply to all listings in Shopping.

Nutrition

Your site will not get listed if:

1. You are an affiliate. 2. You submit more than one page of your site. 3. You submit to the wrong category. 4.
