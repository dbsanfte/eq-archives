  • Dadashri The Gnani Purush - Words of wisdom on a number of subjects from Ambalal Muljibhai Patel aka Dadashri (died 1988), using the teaching of 'Akram Vignam', the 'Elevator Path'. This teaching now continued by a disciple.

  • On Having a Head by Douglas Harding - Radical advaita mentor of the "Headless Way" shocks himself with the truth.
  • Open Mind, Open Heart - Observations and Answers to numerous questions from students by the late non-dualist teacher, Scott Morrison. Books and Tapes may be purchased.
  • The Open Secret - Introduction to "The Open Secret" and "As It Is", two books by Tony Parsons who writes and leads talks and retreats on the subject of Enlightenment and Liberation in the UK and internationally.

His teacher, Ramana Maharshi, showed him the immovable truth of Self in which Gods, souls and the Universe appear and disappear. Sri Poonjaji stayed true to this revelation and his devotion to his beloved Ramana never wavered.
  • The Published Works of Chuck Hillig - Black Dot Publications publishes books on Advaita from a devotee of Ramana Maharshi.
  • Ramana Maharshi Foundation UK - Monthly meetings are held in London. A tri-annual journal, ‘Self Enquiry’, is produced containing articles by and dialogues with modern Sages, together with a Newsletter and diary of forthcoming satsangs in the UK and Europe.
  • Ramana Way - On-line content of this Publication for 1998 - 99 and details of the activities of the Ramana Maharshi Centre for Learning at Bangalore, India.
  • The Sambodh Society - Details of events organised throughout the US and of publications of the society, formed by Swami Bodhananda Saraswati.
  • Sarva Darshana Samgraha - Comparative philosophies of India - scholarly discussions of the Vedas, Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, Dharmashastras, Heterodox Systems (Lokayata, Jainism and Buddhism) and Orthodox (Vaisheshika, Nyaya, Samkhya, Yoga, Purva Mimamsa, Vedanta, Shaiva Siddhanta and Kashmir Shaivism).
  • Satsang - Schedules of teachers holding satsangs and retreats in the Netherlands, together with s and quotes.
  • Satsang With Vartman - a follower of Gangaji, who now gives Satsang also. Schedule, gallery, poetry, satsang extracts.
  • School of Economic Science - Teaches Samkhya influenced Advaita, with particular emphasis on karma yoga, at locations around the world. TM is introduced after two years. Also a world-class centre for the study of Sanskrit.
  • Shanti Sadan Yoga Centre - Traditional yoga of self-knowledge (adhyatma yoga), based on the spiritual philosophy of non-duality (advaita vedanta). Universal teaching for people from any religious or scientific background, or none. Events, magazine, bookshop.

  • Society of Abidance in Truth - SAT teaches the practice of Ramana Maharshi's self-inquiry. Teaching is done by two self-realized sages, Nome and Russel Smith. Schedules, events, retreats.
  • Spiritual Net Directory - Links to teachings from Ramana Maharshi, Nisargadatta Maharaj, Russ, Nome, Adi Shankara, Yoga Vasistha, Ribhu Gita (read by Papaji in Satsang), Ashtavakra Gita, Avadhuta Gita, Extracts from other ancient texts of Advaita Vedanta, Hsin-hsin-ming, the Diamond Sutra, Bodhidharma, the Sutra of Hui Neng, and Vimalkirti Nirdesa Sutra
  • Sri Ramana Maharshi - Brief accounts of meetings with the Indian sage, Sri Ramana Maharshi
  • The Study Society - Weekly discussion groups, sometimes with invited speakers, take place at locations in the UK, Australia, Canada, Mexico, New Zealand and USA.   • Tyohar Foundation - Tyohar's schedule - satsangs and retreats. Images and sharings from the world of Tyohar.

  • Vishal and Alka's Home Page - Information about India, Hinduism, Yoga, meditation, and of course, lots about our life.
