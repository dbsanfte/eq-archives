  • Career Information   [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Presents potential careers ranging from dancing to accounting based on students' interests. From the Bureau of Labor Statistics.
  • What Do They Do?   [ Kids/Teens ] - Teach about what people do in different jobs or occupations or careers. Each explanation is followed by a short story, which portrays a day on the job.

  • America's Career InfoNet [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Find wage and employment trends, occupational requirements, state-by-state labor market conditions, and extensive career resource library online.
  • Are You Thinking about Becoming a Veterinarian? [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Frequently asked questions about how someone becomes a vet including career information, veterinary medicine, jobs, education, salary, advice, schools, and personal experiences.
  • A Career in Veterinary Medicine [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - The Canadian Veterinary Medical Association provides information for young people who are interested in veterinary medicine.
  • Career Journal [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Articles about all you have to know before you start a new job or after you started your job. A good reference for teens who want to know more about their careers.
  • Career Magic [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - You can find reference for jobs interview and resume writing at here. A very useful page with hints for success.
  • Career Search [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - There are thousands of possible careers anybody can be when they grow up- from truck drivers to funeral directors, find information about them here.
  • Career Trail [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Student-created guide to jobs, resumes and vocational interests.
  • Careers in Earth Science [ Teens ] - Provides a brief list of a few careers in the earth science field.
  • Careers in Oceanography [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Offers a complete list of how to become an oceanographer, what education level is required, what the working conditions are, earnings, and what an oceanographer does.
  • Environmental Jobs and Careers [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Like to be involved in environmental jobs? This site will tell you more about it.
  • The First Year [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Follows five teachers through their first year in the Los Angeles public school system. Reveals both the frustrations and the triumphs of the job.
  • FirstGov for Kids: Careers [ Kids/Teens ] - Directory of sites designed to help children and teens explore possibilities for their futures.

  • Imagiverse [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Features interviews from people in many different walks of life and their answers to students' career questions.
  • Inspiring careers in science and pharmaceuticals [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - Career information for school children considering future employment and career opportunities in the Pharmaceutical and Science Industry - sponsored by AstraZeneca
  • Is the Past in Your Future? [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Tells about career options in the field of archaeology.
  • Jobs Future [ Teens/Mature Teens ] - A Canadian page as a reference tool for teens who need help with education and careers planning.
  • Jobs People Do [ Kids ] - A second grader's project on the jobs that people do. Find out what she learned through her studies.
  • Kids and Community [ Kids/Teens/Mature Teens ] - Describes what it's like to be a city planner.
