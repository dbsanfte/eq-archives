Top: Shopping: Crafts

This category lists the sites of individual craftspeople who create items by hand and sell them to the public. In order to be listed in this category, the site must represent an individual craftperson and his or her products.

Companies that buy and resell handcrafted household items should submit their sites to Shopping: Home and Garden: Accessories: Handcrafted.

To submit your site, please find the appropriate subcategory in Shopping: Crafts. No sites are listed in the top level of this category.
Craft -- an occupation or trade requiring manual dexterity or skilled artistry.

Craftsperson -- a creator of great skill in the manual arts.


Baskets

This category lists sites of individual basket makers selling their handcrafted products online.

Sites offering baskets that are handcrafted, but are bought in quantity for resale from the crafter and then offered to the public for resale should be listed in Shopping: Home and Garden: Accessories.
This category features the web sites of independent, professional basket makers that offer decorative and functional handmade baskets for retail sale.

Ceramics

This category lists sites of individual potters and ceramicists selling their handcrafted products online.

Sites offering pottery that is handcrafted, but is bought in quantity for resale from the crafter and then offered to the public for resale should be listed in Shopping: Home and Garden: Accessories: Pottery.
This category features the Web sites of independent ceramicists that offer decorative and functional handmade ceramics for retail sale.

Children's

This category lists sites that sell kits and supplies for children's crafts directly to the public.
Kits and supplies for children's craft projects.

Decorative Painting

Please only submit sites that adhere to the articles in the Category description.

Do not submit sites for artist's paintings. Submit them to Shopping/Visual_Arts

more information (editors only)
This category is for sites that offer items that have been hand painted by the individual artist and may include furniture, clothing, birdhouses, yard signs and various other decorative painted items.

Directories

This category contains a list of sites which are themselves primarily lists of sites related to crafts and crafting resources. "Online Craft Mall" type sites featuring the work of several artisans will also be considered for inclusion in this category.

Sites whose primary focus is the promotion or sale of crafts by an individual crafter should be submitted to a more appropriate category. Example: A site primarily selling ceramics should be submitted to Shopping/Crafts/Ceramics for consideration.

Dolls

This category lists sites of individual doll makers selling their handcrafted products online.

Sites offering mass-produced dolls for children's play should be listed in Shopping: Toys and Games: Dolls.

Sites offering mass-produced collectible dolls should be listed in Shopping: Antiques and Collectibles: Toys and Games: Dolls.

more information (editors only)
This category lists the sites of individual craftspeople who create dolls by hand and sell them to the public. In order to be listed in this category, the site must represent an individual craftperson and his or her products.

Ethnic and Regional

This category is for handcrafted work made by individual artists or galleries representing those artists. Manufactured, imported, or mass produced "hand crafted" items are not appropriate for Shopping/Crafts categories and should be submitted elsewhere in the directory.

more information (editors only)
Sites in this category sell ethnic and regional products handcrafted by individual artists.

Subcategories are divided by continent, then by ethnicity. Sites selling a products for a variety of ethnicities can be listed at the top level, otherwise sites should be listed in the appropriate subcategory.

Fiber Arts

Please submit your site of "finished products" to the "single" (that means only once) appropriate sub category of Shopping/Crafts/Fiber_Arts/ Handweaving or Needlework and the sub categories under that heading.

Do not include advertisement, hype, or any promotional language in the description of your site.

Submitted commercial, manufacturing, and business sites will either be moved to the proper category or deleted.

Following the above guidelines will usually get the submitted site reviewed and included in the directory faster.

more information (editors only)
This category contains same restrictions as explained under Shopping/Crafts category description.

This category will accept "finished craft products" only!! Items must be made and sold by a craft person and not commercial products.

Floral

Companies offering floral art and crafts.

Galleries

This category is for quality craft galleries, "not" for art galleries or for furniture, hobby, gift, and frame stores. Nor for mass produced items.

Art galleries that exhibit paintings and sculpture should be submitted to Arts: Visual Arts: Galleries if non selling sites.

Sites selling "Art" should be submitted to the most appropriate category under Shopping/Visual_Arts

more information (editors only)
Craft galleries are artisan galleries offering handcrafted items, accessories, and other fine crafts.

Glass

This category lists sites of individual glass artists offering their items for sale to the general public. Manufacturers or wholesalers should submit to relevant categories in Business/Industries/Wholesale/ or Business/Industries/Manufacturing/Casting,_Molding,_Machining/Glass/.

Leather

This category lists the sites of craftspeople who create items made of leather and sell them to the public.

Companies that buy and resell handcrafted items should submit their sites to Shopping: Home and Garden: Accessories: Handcrafted.

To submit your site, please find the appropriate subcategory in Shopping: Crafts: Leather.
Listing sites that sell items crafted from leather, online.

Mosaics

This category lists sites of individual mosaic artists selling their handcrafted products online.

Sites offering mass produced mosaic tiles should be submitted to Business: Industries: Construction and Maintenance: Materials and Supplies: Wall, Floor and Decorative Finishes: Tile. Sites selling mosaic supplies should be submitted to Shopping: Crafts: Supplies: Mosaics.
Mosaics are pictures and decorative objects made by setting small colored pieces of stone, tile, glass and other objects into a surface.

Multiple Craft Artisans

This category is for "individual" artisans who work in more that one crafting medium and cannot be categorized in a single crafting category in Shopping: Crafts.

Before you submit your site to this category, please be sure your offerings do not fit in any of our individual craft categories.

more information (editors only)
"Individual" multiple craft artisans that work in more than one crafting medium producing products in a variety of craft categories.

Nature

This category lists sites that offer items crafted from natural materials such as stone, driftwood, gourds eggs and seashells.

Crafters that work in wood should be listed in Shopping: Crafts: Wood.

Crafters that work in metal should be listed in Shopping: Crafts: Metal.

As with all Craft categories, we only list sites of individual hobbyists or professional crafters.
Nature crafts are items crafted from objects collected from nature such as stones, driftwood, gourds, eggs and seashells.

Polymer Clay

This category lists the sites of individual craftspeople who create items by hand from polymer clay and sell them to the public.



Sites that carry primarily jewelry created from polymer clay should be listed under Polymer Clay Jewelry



If you are an artist that is sharing information, offering tips or project pages, or showcasing your work please submit to the appropriate category under Arts/Crafts/Modeling_Compounds/Polymer_Clay
This category lists the sites of individual craftspeople who create items by hand from polymer clay and sell them to the public. In order to be listed in this category, the site must represent an individual craftperson and his or her products.



NOTE: All forms of handcrafted polymer clay items, except for jewelry, can be listed here as long as prices and ordering information are included on the site.

Soft Toys

Please submit only sites dealing with handcrafted soft toys for sale in this category. Sites that sell other hand made products should be submitted to another category under Shopping/Crafts that most fits its main theme.

more information (editors only)
This category highlights individuals who create and handcraft their own line of soft toys from individual patterns or unique designs, and sell retail or privately. Some soft toys are designed for collectors and as such may not be suitable for children.

Supplies

Please submit only sites selling supplies. No finished products please.

Submitting sites to the correct category will ensure your listing will be listed correctly and expediously.

more information (editors only)
This category is for "suppliers" selling craft supplies only.

If the site sells finished products, please submit it to the appropriate category under Shopping/Crafts.

Woodcraft

This category lists sites of individual woodcrafters selling their handcrafted products online.

Sites offering wooden items that are handcrafted, but are bought in quantity from the crafter and then offered to the public for resale should be listed in Shopping: Home and Garden: Accessories or Shopping: Home and Garden: Furniture: Handcrafted
This category features the web sites of independent, professional and amateur woodcrafters that offer decorative and functional wooden items for retail sale.
