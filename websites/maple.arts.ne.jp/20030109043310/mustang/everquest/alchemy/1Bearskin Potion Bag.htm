Shaman Philosopher's Stone

No, not the Harry Potter thing :) . A dropped item allowing Shaman to increase their current Alchemy skill up to their maximum.

いいえ…ハリーポッター事で…)
Shamanにそれらの現在のAlchemy技能をそれらの最大まで増加させるドロップアイテム.

This item is dropped in Greig's end in the Luclin expansion (mob unknown at this time).

この項目はLuclin拡張(このときのMOB未知)へのGreigの終わりドロップ.
