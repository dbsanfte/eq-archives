THO and Double Dain Friday January 24th 2003


The evening started out in GFay, where the n00bs were asking age-old questions about EQ:



I pondered this long and hard, and wondered: I had chosen long ago between these classes.

I was startled from reflection by a call to arms: THO Up! Every wizard dreams of this call at some point: It is a rare opportunity for this mob, who alone at one time held the second rarest Wizard spell, and one of the great spells of Wizardly power. You can't hope for it, you can only dream.



Paralax stormed through The Deep and the THO went down fast.

Next on the menu was the Dain. Quick mobilization and deadly power had a belt for Hjalluf in minutes. Not to slight the necros, Topaz popped him a second time for the 8th shawl and we killed him again for Vizjeri's belt.

Onlookers prompted a report to some higher agencies:



But everyone knows wizzies have nukes, but no chemical (acid) or biological (disease/poison) warfare: Cold, Fire, Magic only! It's in your EQ beginners manual, sir!

Oh? The answer to the n00b's question?


Especially on this night I would have to agree that it's very fun to be a wizard.



Congrats Pouikyto and Me for this great spell and big thanks to the guild!
