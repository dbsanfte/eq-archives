Aronold

Character Bio:

Aronold was born in Halas but quickly moved to the dangerous city of Freeport where he learned the ways of the warrior and worshipped his god Rallos Zek. Aronold believes that the heat of battle is the only place and time where enlightenment can be gained; that the universe was formed by conflict and in conflict it will end, with the victors feasting upon the remains of their fallen enemy.
