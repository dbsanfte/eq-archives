Rebeca

Character Bio:

Rebeca began her studies in Felwithe, City of High Elves, on the Karana Server. She worships Erollisi Marr.

To Rebeca, the right clothes, and the right hairstyle are of great significance. She wishes she had another Tinker's bag to carry more outfits.

Rebeca also loves, and is faithful to her many friends. Be sure to send her an in-game tell, to become one.

Real Life Bio:

Rebecca lives in Santa Barbara. She is a university student, hoping to one day get a job.

In the meantime, she loves to play EQ, ride her new bike, swim & sun at the beach, fish and ski (near her family's High Sierra Bear Valley home), and travel (anywhere, all the time).

She loves life. Her favorite mottos are:
- "Always look on the bright side of life."
- "Enjoy this moment, for it will never come again."

Rebecca loves music. Her favorite groups are Box Set, and Bare Naked Ladies. She loves to dance to Bon Jovi's "It's My Life."
