On July 7, 2003 Paralax, an EverQuest Guild from the Karana server, has officially disbanded. Many of us have departed for new opportunities and challenges. While some members will continue to thrive in the world of Norrath, others have embarked into newer worlds like Star Wars Galaxies, and real-life.

I encourage you to keep the communication open with your former guild mates with the hope that someday we can all band together again and rekindle that fire that made us all legends.

Paralax has benefited greatly from the dedication and leadership of Velamon the Disciple and the many fine members that made Paralax enjoyable over the years, and it would be remiss not to acknowledge that. I leave you with a video memory from our past.....
 

Take care friends,

Rowbert Borealis
