Friday June 22


Official event in Western Wastes! What an incredible time had by all.

Runestorm Fireheart reporting

Here is what Kaallan had to say,


Well - I guess I earned the name again. I was in WW with the guild on Friday (I believe it was) when we took down Von. I was feeling all fat and happy and rubbing my hands thinking of the cool loot (and the nice dragon steaks we would be eating).

All of a sudden, over the hill, came a wyvern, two cragwurms, and Mraaka (he is a nice purple and pink by the way - very pleasing except for the nasty, nasty teeth).

Well, the monks, necros, and shadowknights started flopping over like chickens at the boneless chicken ranch, the clerics started camping saying "I will camp - someone has to for the rezzes. All the porters started to egress, succor, port, gate and generally get the hell out of there. The bards took out accordians and started to play (Music hath charms to soothe the savage breast).

Everyone else (warriors, other casters, etc.) made a last stand. Me - of course - tried to feign about 6 times - i need to work on it.

Now here is what happened to me. If anyone saw the movie "Airplane" - there is a scene where a woman is hysterical and the priest and stewardess are lining up to slap her. And in the line are several other people with whips, knives, guns, etc.

I was flopped over and the wyvern, wurms, and Mraaka pushed all the warriors out of the way - they ate Danath first I believe and walked over to me. Mraaka made them line up and the carnival and play "Take a shot at the necro"

I was hit - in order - by the cragwurm, wyvern, cragwurm and finally put out of my misery by Mraaka (I firmly believe he was chuckling in his own way).

Btw, grats to Darkform for taking a dive off of the cliff. The last thing I saw was "Darkform plunges to his death". That's what happen when you try to run over the ocean and forget you just had dmf dispelled.

At least it wasnt a cobalt drake.

Kaallan M'arbhdraoi
Heretic
