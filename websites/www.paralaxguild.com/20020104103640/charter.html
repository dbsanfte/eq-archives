Paralax Charter

par·al·lax (pr-lks)
n.

An apparent change in the direction of an object, caused by a change in observational position that provides a new line of sight.

I.e : Paralax Guild aim to change the normal guild's goal. Our position and our orientation is different. Our chosen way is completely fixed to the enjoyments of our high level members. So on, our goals are fixed in a different way, another axis... a Paralax.


1. Main goal.
Paralax Guild was built in the spirit of goodwill, faith and the desire to have fun together in the world of Norrath. Guild leadership has the responsibility to make available transportation and disponibility of events for every member. Members of Paralax must respect elementary rules of social conduct and community life, harsh language and off the hook comments are not tolerated in the guild’s chat. Every Paralax member must accept and respect the guild's laws and other members of the guild, human respect of others is necessary to be a part of Paralax.

2. Official Guild-Event
All events classified under the board and quoted as a guild event will be considered mandatory for Paralax members. All Paralax members are held responsible of the life of the guild and as such must be present during guild events when online.

Guild events will be lead by an event master, a specific person put in command of that event. It can be the requiree of the event, an officer delegated to the task or the guildmaster. Event master will be in charge of handling groups and strategy appropriate to the event's area or delegating to a knowledgeable person. The event master will try to make balanced and equitable group as required by the planned area's strategy. Special demands about groups will be allowed but will be respected within the limits of the possible.

3. Presence and absence from Norrath
Every Paralax member is required to be on guild events if present online in the world of Norrath. Event participation will be taken into consideration by the class officers and will be accountable in the sharing of loot. People leaving Norrath for a Real-life situation must contact the guildleader or an officer prior to do so. Extended leave (be it planned or not) will result in a removal from active roster. Members that warned about their leave will be allowed back with a minor administrative penalty, members that left without giving warning for an extended period will be allowed back with initiate status ( to show us they really are coming back to active status ).

Members present online during an official events and not with the guild will be issued a first silent warning. At the second offence the member will be /telled and asked for explanation, any further offence will get a penalty decided by the guildmaster.


Guests are allowed on a very specific basis. All guests must be registered to the guildleader before the event starts. As a rule of thumb only candidates or needed guests are allowed.

There will always be 3 official events per week + rotation events. There will also be prized FFA kills when available. Official events can be called by the guildleader when its judged possible to take down a notable target.

Events will be held starting at the following hours.
Mon-Fri : 6h15 EST.
Sat-sun : 11h-1h EST

6. Recruiting
Minimal level to join Paralax is level 57.

All potential candidates must talk first to the officer in charge of recruiting. Then be present to a couple of raids and be noted as a good candidate. The officer is responsible to invite only prospective recruits and / or constant guests to Paralax's events.


Recruiting is currently CLOSED

Special cases may apply, contact Velamon for more information.

All candidates must be subjected to a first screening session by the person in charge of recruiting ( Velamon ), afterwards if judged potential candidates they are placed on the guild board, name, class, level and some considerations written.

Every Paralax member has the choice to express his feelings on the candidate, after a certain period of time ( as deemed necessary by the person in charge of recruiting but a minimum of 48 hours) the candidate is either inserted in the guild or told that his membership has been refused. Any negative vote on a candidate by a member automatically voids the membership of that applicant.

Every new member must pass thru a 4 weeks initiation period (or 6 raid points, whatever is faster) in which he will accumulate event points but may not use them ( except in the case of them being the only one interested in an item ), during this period initiates will be also subject to be removed from guild if it is noticed that they do not fit the Paralax mold, Officers are in charge of taking notes about the initiates.

Every member that returns to Paralax, after an out of guild period ( Removed himself ) must also pass thru the initiation process again. No one will ever be re-invited in Paralax after a 2nd /guildremove.



7. Guild's pride.

All members of Paralax should be proud of their guild and as such it will be specially asked to avoid being anonymous. Roleplay will be an accepted mode to avoid being bugged.

Warnings will be issued to members who are repetitively anonymous.


The Loot System

1/ Every player at an event receive 1pt.
We will take note of people always arriving late and leaving early at events.
Common sense rule, dont expect points if you come to the last 10 mins of an event.

We will also take note of people repetitively arriving late and leaving early. Events should not consist of coming to get a group...killing 2-3 mobs then going back off. Thats not the goal of it. Warnings will be issued after a second noted offence.

Points are usually noted when Officers/Guildmaster make groups for events. If you have a tendency to arrive late to events, this may have some consequence on your points bank. If you did arrive at an unusual time at the event, make sure your presence has been noted by the event's leader(guildleader).


People have the responsability to make sure they are noted when they arrive to a raid. Managing everyone's attendance is ungrateful already. When you arrive late to a raid make sure the raid leader ( making groups ) is AWARE of your arrival.

Players with multiple accounts/characters must register their main character with the guildmaster. The MAIN character will gain points and will be enabled to roll on items during events.

For specific events it will be allowed or "asked" to come with a secondary character, in those cases, the main character is granted a point, the alternate character cannot participate in loot but the main character is allowed if it is possible and reasonable to bring him to looting ground.

Only 1 change of this character will be allowed, and all must be agreed with the guildmaster/his assistants before taking effect, points may or may not be kept or certain conditions may be imposed before the transferal happens.

Random events
Big target events (Velketor, Dain, Derakor/Statue, TOV Named) are always registered as official by the guild master and as such participation is REQUIRED.


2/ Items are categorized
( this is general barem of points, set values by loot officers ALWAYS override points written in this list ).
Every items will get its separate value but in case of an unknown item, this is the general list.

- 0.5pt: Jacynth, Black Sapphire, cheap items.
- 1pt: common spells ( level 55-59 ), diamonds Jewelry, Fine armour and weapons. Old planar stuff. Spells level 55+.
- 2pts: Old nice planar stuff (valorium, carmine, etc, if it still drop...), Good armour or weapons. Level 60 spells
- 3pts: Some talismans in WW. Trakanon's tooth
- 4pts: TOV armour(except BP's and robes), old planar BP/Robes, Excellent weapons and armour, (stuff generaly better than planar, but not than boss mobs). Some talismans in WW.
-6pts: Boss mob loot, BP/Robes from Kunark/Velious. Exceptional armour or Weapons

-7+pts : TOV BP's and robes, Exceptional items / loot. ST talismans.

NB : Officers and GM can modify the value of items by +/- 1pt as they see it during an event.



3/ Participation on lotto
Lotto is allowed if you possess the FULL value of a given item for the character present at the event and if the character bidding can use the desired item.

No secondary character may roll for an item if a primary character will use that item. Respect and common sense are required when requesting for participation in lotto for an item. The guildmaster may limit/allow certain classes/persons on specific lotto's depending on arrival time, previous winnings and so on.


After seeing multiple cases of adherent participation in lotto's there will now be a ruling out of participation in lotto's. The guildmaster and officers will discuss any of those case by case on the fly. Gross Greed will not be permitted.

Repeat winners :
Sometimes, random and fates plays some strange ways and some people always seem to get the good pick repetitively. Common sense IS required. If you won a couple of items in recent lotto's or took items from guildbank or ... you know. Step out of a new item's lotto, leave chance to others to win items too. After a couple of wins you might receive a friendly tell by an officer if common sense didn't work.

Multiple Lotto's
Lotto's are exclusive. In the case of Multiple item drops, Participation to lotto is limited only to ONE of the dropped items (unless you are the only one in need of one of the dropped item). Since lotto's are sequential and your point value might influence rolls from multiple lotto's, it is the only viable fair possibility.

4/ Lotto numbers
Lotto on item is done using the current lotto rules.
- MaxLotto is 100+(MaxPoints*10)
- Every player rolls from (MyPoints*10) to MaxLotto
- Higher roller gets the item.

Exemple :
Lotto is done from BaseLotto to (max number*10)+100

There is a maximum point value used to roll, the maximum value allowed on rolls is 40pts. If a player has more than 40pts, he only uses 40pts as his maximum to roll for items.



5/ No drop items exception
Items which are used by no one, and collected only for Secondary equipment (i.e CR Gear) can be looted at no point cost if called as such by the raid leader. Item cost maybe degraded depending on the situation, if only 1 person desires that item.

6/ Returning items
Any item returned to the guild gives back half its value in point to the person who returns the item, only when a similar item is received. Only items received from the guild in an official event and that costs points can be used to get points back. It is highly recommended to return an old item after receiving a new one from the guild.

7/ Guests
Guests looting is limited to items that cannot be carried over or on items the officers and GM agree to let them participate on. Their point total is always 0pt in the case they are allowed to lotto.

8/ Guild stash and bank
Items can be purchased from the guild stash for second characters or personal use at a later date, only and only if that item has no use for a primary character and could not be traded/sold.

9/ Epic items :
Never will any Epic Item be given out with direct attribution if there is competition about it, if all people agree and rejoice on giving the item to one of them, ALL THE BETTER.

The goal is to make it CLEAR and I mean Crystal CLEAR that there is no Attribution and no favoritism and our decisions arent tainted with any external consideration.

If everyone agrees with with the one person finishing his epic COOL, FANTASTIC. If there is competition between two or more members, then there will be lotto for that item like all other items.

The biggest exception to this rule is the following :
An item directly concerned on an epic event called for the benefit of one person goes directly to that person, and she must pay the regular price in points for that item.


The guild doesn't handle epic items attribution. There can be arrangements made between people of classes if this is the case and an item is allocated to someone, great, but the guild will never force the allocation of an item to a specific player. If no arrangement was taken, random lotto will be done in all cases.


NB: Points are received AFTER an event.

NB2: Points are deducted INSTANTLY after gaining an item. Not at the end of the raid.

NB3 : The guildmaster and its assistants, always have the final saying on item point costs and in lotto's participation. There are many items which are restricted on participation in lotto's ,

NB4: Cash items received during events will be kept by the guild officers to pay for expenses.

NB5: Guild item received ARE noted and are property of the guild. A Paralax member must give back an item if another item of the same type and of greater power is acquired during a guild event.

NB6: Items obtained in a guild event cannot be traded or sold. IF these items aren't needed anymore and wishes to be traded / sold, there must be some talks made to an officer or guildmaster before doing so.



IMPORTANT NOTICE : The loot system(tm) only applies during Official Paralax Events. Free events are always done using the general means, free open lotto or direct attribution according to participants.

Fun, Respect and common sense are the most important values in Paralax, any person not respecting those will be warned. Repeat offenders might have to look out for a new guild.
