Ceny

Character Bio:

Ceny was born in the lovely city of Kelethin. Learning quickly that she was distracted by the beauty of the trees and wildlife in the Faydark forests, she soon moved to the Qeynos area where she could study the warriors art under the watchful eye of Ebon Strongbear. Ceny prefers outdoor zones where she can feel the wind in her hair, but will travel to dungeons if an expert guide can be found.

Ceny is quiet and shy, preferring to hold a place in the background in most groups. However, those that are closest to her know that she can be quite a feisty, mischievous wood elf with a witty remark for any situation when the mood takes her.

Over the years, Ceny has discovered that many of the creatures of Norrath enjoy trips to visit different areas of their homes. She can often be seen leading several friendly creatures to see the sights. Unfortunately, these creatures often become very excited and over boisterous and accidentally mistreat their tour guide when she is showing them the sights to be found close to the boarder of the neighboring area.

Real Life Bio:



Kim lives in Nashua, New Hampshire with her husband, two kids, two cats, and a goldfish. A former Air Traffic Controller of almost 10 years, she now acts as an automation specialist maintaining a NAS support system.

Her main hobbies are her family and Everquest. When not online, she enjoys biking, cooking, reading, piano, and playing with the kids. A fan of aviation, she never turns down a chance to go flying with friends.
