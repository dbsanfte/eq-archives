Weekend April 13-15
This weekend we had a long raid in the Plane of Sky. We began at the first island, where we gathered keys to get to the next islands. From there we went to kill Lord Djorn, Gorgalosk, the Keeper of Souls, Overseer of Air, all the way to the Spiroc island, where we killed The Spiroc Lord! We had an amazing amount of loot. Here is a partial list of some of the items we got this weekend: Congrats Merileaf on Belt of the Pine, Raben and Merithment on Belt of Concordance, Craak on Belt of Tranquility, Velamon on Golden Efreeti Greaves, Karean on Symbol of Marr, Bamana on Treant Tear Earings, Topaz, Humingbyrd and Tsal on Weight of the Gods, and finally congrats to Craak, Merileaf, Daementia, Leyrin and Danath on their PoA epic quest item! After we were done at the end of the weekend, we got several boss mobs down in the dirt as shown below :)

Thursday April 12
Tonight we had an unofficial event to the Plane of Growth. This was not our first PoG trip so we were quickly able to setup camp at a safe area near the lake. We started pulling interesting mobs from the east side of the river right away since most common mobs were down. In about 4 hours we killed many mobs and received awesome loot including Shadow Night and Monk leggings, Bard vambraces, and Shaman BP.

Tuesday April 10
Again, we had a lot of great events today! First, we went to Kithicor Woods again when Caramen saw the Coercer wearing a Robe of the Kedge, for the rogue epics. We cleared the house, and were very lucky to get 2 robes! Congrats to both Aerah and Caramen for getting their epics! These are the epics number 8 and 9 of Paralax :)

Then we killed the big fire elemental in Burning Woods for the cleric epic. It went down very fast, with only a few deaths on our side. It also dropped the Mantle of Fire

We finished the night in City of Mist, where we killed Black Reavers to get some epic items and other nice loot. While no epic item dropped, we did get an Ornate Runed Shield from Neh Ashir. Grats to Geaux who won this nice shield!

Today we made another trip to the Plane of Fear to clear up the 3 golems. We got nice loot and many people got epic quest items! Here are some of the items we received with screenshots.

Saturday April 7

We cleared the Plane of Fear today. It took less than 4 hours to clear the whole plane, and several nice items dropped including a druid BP and monk items. We gave a shot at Cazic Thule and got him to 60% before we ran out of tanks :)

Friday April 6
Today we had a lot of events :) First we killed the Cobalt Scar dragon for the second time. We took him down with 16 people, and he dropped spells, shells, a warrior sword and a Bioluminescent Orb.

We also took a quick swim to Kedge Keep to kill Phinny, and the tainted seahorse. Bamana received an epic item, and Sentinel a bone for a spell quest. Congrats!

Thursday April 5
We tried the Severillous dragon, and came very close to a kill at 30% HP. We were rushed, but next time we will get him :) We also killed the General in Kithicor Woods twice for Aerah and Caramen.

Sunday April 1st
Today we had an official Skyshrine event. We killed many cubes and wyverns, as well as The Seer, a high level chicken looking mob that dropped gems :) We received some decent experience, spells and items from the mobs.
