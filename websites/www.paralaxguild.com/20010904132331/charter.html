Paralax Charter

par·al·lax (pr-lks)
n.

An apparent change in the direction of an object, caused by a change in observational position that provides a new line of sight.

I.e : Paralax Guild aim to change the normal guild's goal. Our position and our orientation is different. Our chosen way is completely fixed to the enjoyments of our high level members. So on, our goals are fixed in a different way, another axis... a Paralax.


1. Main goal.
Paralax Guild was built in the spirit of goodwill, faith and the desire to have fun together in the world of Norrath. Members of Paralax must respect elementary rules of social conduct and community life. Every Paralax member must accept and respect the guild's laws and other members of the guild.

2. Official Guild-Event
All events classified under the board and quoted as a guild event will be considered mandatory for Paralax members. All Paralax members are held responsible of the life of the guild and as such must try as possible to be present during guild events.

Guild events will be lead by an event master, a specific person put in command of that event. It can be the requiree of the event, an officer delegated to the task or the guildmaster. Event master will be in charge of handling groups and strategy appropriate to the event's area. The event master will try to make balanced and equitable group as required by the planned area's strategy. Special demands about groups will be allowed but will be respected within the limits of the possible.

Every Paralax member is required to be on guild events if present online in the world of Norrath. Event participation will be taken into consideration by the class officers and will be accountable in the sharing of loot. People inactive for a long period will be asked simple questions to their whereabouts and maybe retired for a period or simply removed from guild as wished by the guild's council and guildmaster.

(16-06-01)
Members present online during an official events and not with the guild will be issued a first silent warning. At the second offence the member will be /telled and asked for explanation, any further offence will get a penalty decided by the guildmaster.


Guests will be tolerated and approved by the Paralax guild but must be registered by an officer on the appropriate forum. Guests will be implicated in the guild's life and must conform and respect to Paralax's rules.

There will always be 2 official events per week + unpredictable events ( Gods, Dragon, some rare epics...etc).

Events will be held starting at the following hours.
Mon-Fri : 6h30 EST.
Sat-sun : 12h-1h EST

There will also be Hard free events during the week for occupying people.

5. Recruit
Minimal level to join Paralax is level 56.

All potential candidates must talk first to an officer or the guildleader. Then be present to a couple of raids and be noted as a good candidate. The officers are responsible to invite only prospective recruits and / or constant guests to Paralax's events.

(16-06-01)
All guests will be automatically refused to events unless registered 24h before the event. Guests should consist of exceptional people or Known candidates.

The Loot System

1/ Every player at an event receive 1pt.
No difference for now if coming late or leaving early, I don't want to put up a police. If its always the same and always happening then there will be adjustments. Common sense rule, don't expect points if you come to the last 10 mins of an event.

(16-06-01)
We will now take note of people repetitively arriving late and leaving early. Events should not consist of coming to get a group...killing 2-3 mobs then going back off. That's not the goal of it. Warnings will be issued after a second noted offence.


Points are usually noted when Officers/Guildmaster make groups for events. If you have a tendency to arrive late to events, this may have some consequence on your point bank. If you did arrive at an unusual time at the event, make sure your presence has been noted by the event's leader(guildleader).

(16-06-01)
People have the responsibility to make sure they are noted when they arrive to a raid. Managing everyone's attendency is ungrateful already. When you arrive late to a raid make sure the raid leader ( making groups ) is AWARE of your arrival.


Players with multiple accounts/characters must register their main character with the guildmaster. This character will gain points and will be enabled to roll on items during events. No frequent change of this character will be allowed, and all must be agreed with the guildmaster/his assistants before taking effect.

Random events such as Lodizal, Kelorek Dar, Faydedar, Ragefire, Golem calls are a special case of events.

As such people aren't required to come for those targets. We cannot require people to be present on a string call.
Although for the benefit of the guild it will be important that people show up. Because of that, no points will be given for participating in those events.


N.B : Special events like Venril Sathir, Trakanon, Severillous, Talendor and other big guys like that can be predicted and will count as official events giving points. As a rule of thumb, every event written on the Guild Event board counts as a point event.

-6pts: Epic loot, Boss mob loot, BP/Robes from Kunark/Velious. Exceptional armour or Weapons

3/ Participation on lotto
Lotto is allowed if you possess at least half the value of a given item for the character present at the event and if the character bidding can use the desired item.
No secondary character may roll for an item if a primary character will use that item. Respect and common sense are required when requesting for participation in lotto for an item. The guildmaster may limit/allow certain classes/persons on specific lottos.

(16-06-01)
After seeing multiple cases of abherent participation in lottoes there will now be a ruling out of participation in lottoes. The guildmaster and officers will discuss any of those case by case on the fly. Gross Greed will not be permitted anymore.

(16-06-01)
Repeat winners :
Sometimes, random and fates plays some strange ways and some people always seem to get the good pick repetitively. Common sense IS required. If you won a couple of items in recent lottoes or took items from guildbank or ... you know. Step out of a new item's lotto, leave chance to others to win items too. After a couple of wins you might receive a friendly tell by an officer if common sense didn't work.

5/ No drop items exception
Items which are used by no one, and collected only for Secondary equipment (i.e CR Gear) can be looted at no point cost. Item cost maybe degraded depending on the situation, if only 1 person desires that item.

6/ Returning items
Any item returned to the guild gives back half its value in point to the person who returns the item, only when a similar item is received. It is highly recommended to return an old item after receiving a new one from the guild.

7/ Guests
Guests looting is limited to items that cannot be carried over or on items the officers and GM agree to let them participate on. Their point total is always 0pt in the case they are allowed to lotto.

8/ Guild stash and bank
Items can be purchased from the guild stash for second characters or personal use at a later date, only and only if that item has no use for a primary character.

9/ Epic items :
Never will any Epic Item be given out with direct attribution if there is competition about it, if all people agree and rejoice on giving the item to one of them, ALL THE BETTER.

The goal is to make it CLEAR and I mean Crystal CLEAR that there is no Attribution and no favoritism and our decisions aren't tainted with any external consideration.

If everyone agrees with with the one person finishing his epic COOL, FANTASTIC. If there is competition between two or more members, then there will be lotto for that item like all other items.

The biggest exception to this rule is the following :
An item directly concerned on an epic event called for the benefit of one person goes directly to that person, and she must pay the regular price in points for that item.

(16-06-01)
The guild doesn't handle epic items attribution. There can be arrangements made between people of classes if this is the case and an item is allocated to someone, great, but the guild will never force the allocation of an item to a specific player. If no arrangement was taken, random lotto will be done in all cases.
