Monday May 30
Today sure was full of unexpected events! First, Tarnelliia recruited a few of us to go kill fire giants in the hope that Ragefire would pop, and pop he did! So we promptly killed him. Grats Tarnelliia on epic number 14! Also grats to Danath on a Red Dragon Scale, Sentinal on boots, and Craak on a box. Then later at night we noticed that Lord Doljon was up in Velketor. Of course we couldn't allow him to live very long, so we rushed in and lay him to rest. He droped a tunic, a caster stone, and a nearby kobold droped a neat bard lute. Grats to all the winners!

We got wizard legs, wizard gloves, SK legs, 2 enchanter robes, cleric boots and I'm sure I forgot some stuff. We spent all afternoon in the plane, and we cleared all of the west part, and many of the eastern part including sprites and some named mobs. Grats all who received items! The most picture-friendly are below :) Oh and we also killed a froggy in Swamp of No Hope for Bamana's epic quest.. We took it easily with 2 group, no death, it lasted less than 2 minutes. Saturday May 28
Today we did a lot! First we raided the hole where we killed Jaeil for the ranger epic, some ghost for the enchanter epic, an iksar for the shaman epic, and some mummy for the SK epic. Then we went and killed Master Yael and his keeper of the scale. Grats to Jull for a Red Dragon Scale! We then went to Karnor and killed VS in less than 30 minutes from zone in to zone out. The fight lasted 45 seconds and we had no death. He droped an enchanter staff and ranger leggings. Finally I completed my Earthcaller epic, the epic number 13 in the guild!
