Through iron, we are strong. Through iron, we endure. With the cross, we are virtuous. With the cross, we overcome evil. Together, the iron and the cross form the essence of our way. We are the Knights of the Iron Cross!

On the 30th day of the month of May, Three Thousand and Twelve of this our 3rd age, we, the Knights of the Iron Cross do hereby voice our intention to band together in the blood oaths of brotherhood. Brothers and sisters from every race of Norrath: we do this of our own free will, knowing the full weight of sacrifice that will come to bear through our oaths. For our brotherhood, our order, nay our GUILD shall be built upon the one true virtue, which bears witness to all others: I speak of the virtue of honor

The goals of the Knights of the Iron Cross: Protect and defend all members of our Guild. Aid those who are in need. Our honor will guide us in who we aid. Seek out knowledge and quest for lost items of lore. Admit only those to our order who would sacrifice one to save all. Establish an order of trust and brotherhood.

Recruiting for the cause
Any full Knight of Ironcross may propose an individual as a potential candidate.  The Knight then becomes the official sponsor, and needs to notify the guild through the message board that they are interested in sponsoring this initiate.

Once this has been done a notice will be posted to all Knights via onelist.  All full members and officers will then have 7 days in which to voice any objections to this person becoming an initiate (all officers must respond).  If there is no objection and at least three Aye's from officers the individual will then become a Knight Initiate for a period of 30 days in which all other Knights can get to know them.  At the end of the 30 day period providing there are no objections the Initiate will be offered the chance to become a full Knight of the Iron Cross.

Any member may only sponsor one new initiate at a time, this means that until your sponsored initiate has gone through the 30 day period and been made a full knight, you may not sponsor anybody else.  So please choose carefully my friends as only the best in intentions and honor are truly made Knights.
