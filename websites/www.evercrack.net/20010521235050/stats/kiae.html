BIO
First meeting

Kiae first met the Soulmenders in his then hunting ground of Everfrost, near his homeland of Halas.
The meeting would have been completely by chance, running across Diae lost and trying to find Permafrost, had he not forseen the meeting and its outcome long ago.  After laughing at how one could get lost in such a simple zone he agreed to lead Diae in the correct direction.  Diae would eyeball every tree we passed and eventually excused himself to "get down to business", which I interpreted as "take care of some business" but was greatly mistaken.  Ignoring the odd noises coming from behind the tree I waited.  Diae came back in short time muttering "Too cold, much too cold."  I did not forsee that and wish even today I hadn't seen it - I can not yet see a woodelf without thinking of Diae and the tree.
Diae later got even for my earlier laughter by having his friends teleport me far from my home so I could get lost in Butcherblock.
