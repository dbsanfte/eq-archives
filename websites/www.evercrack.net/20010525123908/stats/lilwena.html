BIO
Born in the City of Kelethin, a simple girl started her way through life. As time went on, she found her self more and more un-attracted to the art of the word, the
power of nature. She instead found the clash of steel on steel, the rush of physical combat to be her calling, that which made her blood boil and a cry spring from her
lips.

Time passed, and finally she found someone to guide her on her chosen path. Albiet he was of the shady sort, he too was one that lived by his blade. Listening to the
careful guidance of this person, she went forth in the world, exploring it and slaying the foul beasts found within.

Skill and savagry came quickly, and although slight to the eye, underestemation cost many a foe there life. As her power grew, as she became more and more
talented in the arts of war, she decided to not take the last name of the guide she had met so long ago, and upon the correct time, she would be forever known as
Lilwena Bloodletter. The rest of the Soulmender's noticed too that she followed the same path as "The Machine" and there by dubbed her "La Machina"

She now fights on, dilligent in her course ever forward. Skill and prowess growing day by day. And always, the Soulmenders are there, keeping an eye over those
that are still young in the world.
