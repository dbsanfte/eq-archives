Just wanted to drop a quick note about the content of this section of the website. I firmly believe that Quests should be under taken as they were meant to be. Some of the Quests are broken, some people get stuck. When you go to the Quest page by clicking on the links, you understand that the information you are receiving is not in spirit of the Game, but rather, a primer or hint book to assist you and help determine whether the Quests are even working! Paladins of Norrath has sent emails to EverQuest, requesting some information and updates on the status of our Quests, re: broken or not. In the description of the link, the person who launches the Quest is posted so you can start the Quest for yourselves.
