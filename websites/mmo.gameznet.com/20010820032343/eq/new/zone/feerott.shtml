Feerott

Click Here For A Map

Feerot connects the ogre town of Ogguk to the rest of the world. This zone also connects to Innothule Swamp and Rathe Mountains, and Cazic Thule. There is one vendor who sells here and you will want to visit her as often as possible(to save trips to Ogguk). If you are leaving town take a left at the fork and keep heading left. Before the path ends there will be one last stone house and inside of it you will find the vendor.

"Good" races will be happy to know that Ogguk is on the right along the zone wall if you zone in from Innothule. If you are zoning in from Rathe Mountains then Ogguk would be on your left along the zone wall. You will want to avoid Ogguk(the ogre city) until you are higher level. Then you will have the option of using the city as a new dungeon for your party.

To get to Cazic Thule from Innothule you would take a left and follow the ridge until you cross the water. Swim accross the water and Cazic Thule is the large temple that you will come accross. Directions are the exact opposite from Rathe Mountains(take a right instead of a left).

To get to Rathe Mountains from Ogguk you can simply follow the road that leaves town straight ahead. A bit of a ways after the bridge you will come to the zone to Rathe Mountains.

This zone is split into a few areas. The newbie part of the zone lies to the left of the original fork. Over here you will find rats, bats, decaying skeletons, froglok tads, minor scarabs, spiderlings and lizard man broodlings. This part of the zone is great for characters level 1-3.

Low level characters will probably want to targe the froglok tads as they carry netted armor. This armor is the same armor class as cloth but can be worn by all sizes. The gloves are also magical which allows you to punch monsters that require magic weapons to hit.

If you head straight at the first fork you will find a bridge that leads to a trail to Rathe Mountains. Over this bridge is the level 4-8 area. On this side of the bridge you will find lizard man scouts, jungle spiders, skeletons and giant bats. There are several camps in this area that feature skeletons as well as zombies. There are also many lizard men camps.

A great spot for level 8-12 characters is the temples outside of Cazic Thule. The lizard men there carry bronze weapons occasionally. The shamans also carry a mystic doll on occasion which can be turned into either the warrior or shadowknight(depending on which faction you are trying to raise) in Ogguk. These lizards also will rarely contain tails which can be turned into the same NPC for a similar reward(cash/exp/faction/minor item).

The high level area of Feerot is somewhat hidden away from the rest of the zone. If you were entering from Rathe mountains you would take a right and follow the zone ridge. After the skeleton camp and before the water you will probably notice a Spectre. Near this spectre you will see a tree which is next to the zone border. This is a hidden entrance to what is known as the spectre cave. Inside of this cave you will find many spectres and a high level 40 NPC named Cyndreela.. She is guarding the entrance to the Plane of Fear.
