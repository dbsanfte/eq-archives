Clan Crushbone

Location
Clan Crushbone is located on the island of Faydwer to the east of mainland Antonica

Zone Type
Clan Crushbone is the home of the Crushbone Orcs, who are found all over Faydwer. This is an outdoor zone, small in size, with a tiny castle which houses the 'rare' spawns of the zone.


Zone Borders
Clan Crushbone borders with Greater Faydark.


Nearest Cities
Within the branches of the great Oaks of Greater Faydark is the tree city of the Wood Elves, Kelethin, and the home of the High Elves, Felwithe, is also accesable from Greater Faydark.

Kaladim (city of the dwarves) and Ak'Anon (city of the gnomes) are both only a couple of zones away.



Inhabitents
Clan Crushbone is the home of the Crushbone Orcs, a savage race who will attack anything that moves. These Orcs can be found all over Faydwer, and their relatives all over the mainland too. Only the Orcs and their slaves gathered from across the lands live here, with the occasional visit from a Dark Elf Ambassedor.



Route
Clan Crushbone is reached from Antonica by catching the boat from East Freeport Docks, which takes you via the Ocean of Tears, stopping at Sister Island, and then zones into Butcherblock Mountains. You follow the path from the docks and after passing Kaladim you eventually find yourself in Greater Faydark. Once here you need only follow the mountain ledge to your left untill you reach the entrance to their realm.
