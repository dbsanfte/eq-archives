Cleric Spells

Level 1

Courage

Courage the first in a line of buffing spells for the cleric class. This line of spells adds both hit points and armor class and can be cast on yourself and on others.

Cure Poison

This spell will counteract poisons.

Divine Aura

This is perhaps the most important spell you will receive until level 29(when you receive Divine Barrier which is surprise an upgrade to this spell). Divine Aura will grant you a temporary invulnerability to damage. This is a great spell to use to escape from those hairy situations and avoid what would otherwise become certain death. Use it sparingly however as the duration is short and you can only cast this spell once every 15 minutes.

Flash Of Light

This spell will temporarily blind your opponent.

Lull

This is the first in the line of spells which are aimed to calm your foe. This spell can be used to lower your opponents rage and(hopefully) prevent him from attacking you.

Minor Healing

Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.

Spook The Dead

This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with lull as you can lull one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one.

Strike

Strike is a direct damage spell. 

True North

Turns your character to face north.

Yalup

This spell endows the caster a brief surge of strength which not only adds to the casters strength but also to his armor class. The duration is very short but the spell also takes very little mana.

Level 5

Cure Blindness

This spell is used to cure blindness.

Cure Disease

This spell can be used to cure disease.

Furor

This is a direct damage spell. It is a high damage high mana spell and is the best offensive spell a cleric will receive until Smite at level 14.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Holy Armor

Holy armor is the first in the line of armor spells. This spell adds additional armor class to a player.

Light Healing

Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.

Reckless Strength

Reckless strength gives a burst of strength which slowly dies out.

Stun

This spell stuns your opponent for a short period of time.

Summon Drink

Summons drinkable water.

Ward Undead

Ward undead is the first in the line of direct damage undead ward spells. These spells only affect undead. Ward spells are important because at the level which you attain these spells they are the most efficient direct damage spells in the game at this level.

Level 9

Center

Center is an upgrade to the courage line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.

Endure Fire

This spell will increase your resistance to fire.

Endure Poison

This spell will increase your resistance to poison.

Fear

Fear will cause monsters to turn and flee for a period of time. Beware however they will return when the spell duration ends.

Hammer Of Wrath

This spell will summon a magical warhammer. The statistics are 6/32 and it can be used to damage magical monsters. It is a no rent item meaning that you cannot hand it out to anyone else and it will dissapear after you log out.

Invigor

Invigor will restore a players stamina.

Root

Root will prevent a monster from running away for a period of time.

Sense the Dead

Sense undead will point you in the direction of the nearest undead foe.

Soothe

Soothe is an upgrade to lull and the predecessor to calm. It will make a monster less agressive.

Summon Food

This will summon no rent food.

Word Of Pain

Word of pain is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Level 14

Bind Affinity

This allows you to change the point where you will spawn in event of death. You can bind yourself anyplace. You can also bind other people in your party but they must be bound within city limits.

Cancel Magic

This is a very useful spell which can be used to cancel a magic effect cast on you or one of your party members. This is great if someone in your party is charmed or rooted in a deadly situation.

Endure Cold

Endure cold increases your resistance to cold based effects.

Endure Disease

Endure disease increases your resistance to disease.

Expulse Undead

Expulse undead is an upgrade to ward undead. It has higher damage and a more efficient damage:mana ratio.

Halo Of Light

This spell summons a glowing headband which lights as well as a greater lightstone.

Healing

Healing is an upgrade to the heal line of spells. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.

Invisibility To Undead

This spell renders you invisible to undead beings.

Sense Summoned

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.

Smite

Smite is a direct damage spell. It is your best direct damage spell versus non-undead until the level 29 wrath.

Symbol Of Transal

This is the third line of cleric/paladin buffing spells. It does require a component and adds additional hit points only.

Level 19

Calm

Calm is an upgrade to the lull->soothe line of spells. It will lower a monsters aggro range allowing you to do things such as calm a 3 monsters in a 4 monster location and then pick off the non-calmed one.

Daring

This is an upgrade to the courage->center line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.

Endure Magic

Endure magic will increase your resistance to magic based attacks.

Extinguish Fatigue

This will restore a players stamina and also has a duration where it will prevent them from tiring.

Holy Might

Holy might is a stun spell which also does a small amount of damage.

Spirit Armor

Spirit armor is an upgrade to the holy armor line of spells.

Ward Summoned

This spell allows you to damage summoned beasts(elements). Its damage:mana ratio is identical to the ward undead spell.

Word Of Shadow

Word of shadow is an upgrade to the word of pain line of spells. It is an area effect damage spell which centers around the caster.

Yalup II

This is an upgrade to the yalup spell. It grants additional strength and armor class for a short period of time.

Level 24

Bravery

Bravery is an upgrade to the courage->center->daring line of spells. It adds additional armor class and hit points.

Counteract Poison

Cures target of poison effects.

Dismiss Undead

Dismiss undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It is a high damage low mana spell for use against undead foes.

Greater Heal

Greater heal is an upgrade to the heal line of spells. It heals roughly 300 damage for 150 mana.

Hammer Of Striking

This is an upgrade to the hammer of wrath spell. It summons a magical warhammer which you can use to fight with. The stats are somewhat dissapointing for this level however.

Inspire Fear

Inspire fear is an upgrade to the plain old fear spell. It has a better chance of success and lasts longer.

Symbol Of Ryltan

This is an upgrade to the symbol of transal spell. It adds hit points but requires a component.

Wave Of Fear

This is an area effect fear spell which can be useful in sticky situations.

Level 29

Abundant Drink

This spell will summon 5 different drinks. It is useful only in that its a one shot deal rather than having to cast summon drink 5 times.

Counteract Disease

Cures target of disease effects.

Divine Barrier

Divine barrier is an upgrade to the divine aura line of spells. It not only renders you invincible for a period of time but it also regenerates your hit points and stamina during that period. The weakness is that it uses much more mana than the original.

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. This spell has a better chance of success versus higher level foes and a longer duration.

Expulse Summoned

Expulse Summoned is an upgrade to the ward summoned spell.

Guard

This spell grants additional armor class. It is a seperate line of spells so is a welcome addition.

Imbue Black Pearl

Create an imbued black pearl.

Imbue Black Sapphire

Create an imbued black sapphire.

Imbue Diamond

Create an imbued diamond.

Imbue Emerald

Create an imbued emerald.

Imbue Opal

Create an imbued opal.

Imbue Peridot

Create an imbued peridot.

Imbue Plains Pebble

Create an imbued plains pebble.

Imbue Rose Quartz

Create an imbued rose quartz.

Imbue Ruby

Create an imbued ruby.

Imbue Sapphire

Create an imbued sapphire.

Imbue Topaz

Create an imbued topaz.

Panic The Dead

Panic the dead is an upgrade to spook the dead. It is a more powerful fear spell which has a longer duration.

Revive

Revive is the first in the line of res spells. This spell will teleport a dead player to their corpse.

Word Of Spirit

This is an upgrade to the word of pain->shadow line of spells. It is an area effect spell which centers on the caster.

Wrath

Wrath is the upgrade to the smite line of spells. It is a high damage high mana spell that can be used against all monsters and not just undead.

Level 34

Abundant Food

This spell will summon 5 pieces of food. It is an upgrade to the summon food spell.

Armor of Protection (V)

Self only AC/HP buff.

Atone

This is a single target memory blur spell.

Blinding Luminance

I am not positive but I believe this is a blinding spell.

Expel Undead

Expel undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss line of undead spells.

Force

This is an upgrade to the stun->holy might line of spells. It stuns for a good period of time and also does a small amount of damage.

Frenzied Strength

This spell is an upgrade to the reckless strength spell.

Resist Fire

This spell adds to your resistance to fire.

Resist Poison

Resist poison adds to your poison resistance.

Superior Heal

Superior heal is an intermediate spell between greater heal and complete heal. It is a cleric only spell which heals 600 points of damage in a very efficient manner.

Symbol Of Pinzam

This is an upgrade to the symbol of transal line of spells. It requires a reagent and adds about 250 hit points.

Tremor

Tremor is a wide range area effect spell. It does not do a lot of damage for the amount of mana it uses but it can affect a larger number of monsters than the word of pain line of spells.

Valor

This is an upgrade to the courage->center->daring->bravery line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.

Word Of Health

This spell is an area effect heal spell. It is useful in situations where several party members have been damaged at once.

Level 39

Armor Of Faith

This is an upgrade to the holy armor->spirit armor line of spells. It grants additional armor class.

Complete Heal

This spell will completely heal a player. It does require a large amount of mana and is only useful on players with a lot of hit points.

Dismiss Summoned

Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the ward summoned line of spells. It has the same efficiency as dismiss undead but it useful on summoned beasts.

Invoke Fear

Invoke fear is an upgrade to the fear line of spells. It has a longer duration.

Nullify Magic

This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Pacify

Pacify is an upgrade to the lull->soothe->calm line of spells.

Resist Cold

This spell adds to your resistance to cold.

Resist Disease

This spell adds to your resistance to disease.

Resuscitate

This is the second in the line of ressing spells. It will not only teleport a player to his/her corpse but also restores a bit of the experience lost upon death.

Turning of the Unnatural (V)

Single Undead Target DoT.

Word of Souls

Word of Souls is an upgrade to the word of pain->shadow->spirit line of spells. It is a person based area effect spell.

Level 44

Banish Undead

This is the last in the line of ward undead spells. It is a high damage low mana spell which will bring undead to their knees.

Celestial Healing (V)

Group Heal over time.

Earthquake

Earthquake is an upgrade to the tremor spell. It is a wide range area effect spell.

Hammer of Retribution

Hammer of Retribution is an upgrade to the hammer of wrath spells. It summons a magical warhammer for use in combat.

Resist Magic

This spell adds to your resistance to magic based attacks.

Resolution

This is the last in the line of courage spells. It adds armor class and hit points.

Retribution

This is the last in the line of direct damage spells. It is a high damage spell with a medium damage:mana efficiency. Much better than prior cleric spells.

Symbol Of Naltron

Symbol of Naltron is an upgrade to the symbol line of spells. It adds additional hit points.

Yalup III

Yalup III is the last in the line of yalup spells which add both armor class and hit points.

Level 49

Abolish Poison

This is an upgrade to the cure poison line of spells.

Expel Summoned

Expel Summoned is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss line of spells for use against summoned beasts.

Immobilize

Immobilize is an upgrade to the root line of spells.

Ressurect

This is the mother of all res spells. It teleports a player to their corpse and restores quite a bit of the experience lost upon death.

Shield Of Words

This is an upgrade to the holy armor line of spells.

Sound Of Force

This is an upgrade to the stun line of spells.

Word Of Divine

Word of divine is an area effect spell which bases on yourself.

Word of Healing

This is an upgrade to word of health.
