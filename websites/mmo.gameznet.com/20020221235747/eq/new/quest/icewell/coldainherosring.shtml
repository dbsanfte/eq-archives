Coldain Hero's Insignia Ring

Faction:
+Dain Frostreaver
+Coldain
-Kromzek
-Kromrif
Related Zones:
Great Divide
Sirens Grotto
Kedge Keep
Temple of Droga

This is the next 9th ring after the Velium Coldain Insignira Ring.

After you got the 8th ring you need to go to Icewell Keep (Thurgadin B).
Go to Seneschal Aldikar and give him the 8th ring. He will than give you the ring back and go to some other room.
After his speech he will say 'Will you accept this task outlander?'
Now when you say, 'I will accept the task' he will give you a 6 slot box and asks you to put in Rodrick's Dagger and the heads of the 5 traitors.

List of items you need:

1.) Rodrick's Dagger (Eastern Wastes, of Rodrick Tardok)
2.) Murdrick's Head (Great Divide, located in a cave close to VL)
3.) Barradain's Head (Eastern Wastes, coldain camp next to the giant fort)
4.) Peffin's Head (Eastern Wastes, named coldain inside giant fort)
5.) Doldigun's Head (Kael, Doldigun is fiund in the Manor)
6.) Juliah's Head (Icewell Keep, throne room of Dain)


Step 1.) Rodrick's Dagger
Kill Rodrick in EW and loot dagger. 
Every once in a while a Rygorr Emissary will spawn there. If you wait a few minutes and don't kill him (he needs to run into orc fort and back to the small tower), Rodrick will spawn next to him.


Step 2.) Murdrick's Head
Kill Murdrick in Greater Divide. He is located in a cave at same level like VL.
Best way to kill him is when someone pulls him out and train to VL and zone. The others outside will pick him out and kill him.
The tricky thing about this is, once someone is getting "close" to him he will start a dialogue. As soon as he ended his dialogue, he will despawn, no matter if you fight him or not. So be fast.
Loot his head and the recipe for tainted avalanch ale.

Tainted Avalanche Ale Recipe

 - Bottle,
 - Iceball (found in EW or Icelad of Snow Dervishs),
 - Ice Wyvern Stinger (NO DROP, found on Wyverns in Cobalt Scars),
 - Artic Mussels (found in Cobalt Scars in the Water at loc 1170 / -600 and 2 other locs along the coast, they spawn every 20 minutes),
 - Seahorse Scales (found on Seahorses in Sirens Grotto and Kedge Keep),
 - Runed Seashell (Cloud) (found random of sirens in Sirens Grotto)
 - Mind Melt (can be made by a skilled rogue, trivial reported at 248. Need: Quicksilver(made of Gnomish Spirits(Akanon) and Cinnabar(Kaladim)), 2 Mountain Death Salts(Temple of Droga), Ethereal Suspension(Firiona Vie Rogue Guild) and a sealed poison vial(Thurgadin Rogue Guild))

The ale is trivial confirmed at 248 brewing.


Step 3.) Barradain's Head
Make the Tainted Avalanche Ale
Hand it to Captain Barradain in EW. it will take some time but finally the poison will kill him and he falls down. Harmony the Bodyguards and pull Berradin and kill him. Loot his head and the Note to Peffin.


Step 4.) Peffin's Head
Go to the giant fort in EW and kill all giants.
Now hand in the note to peffin (right house, she ist invisible). This works best if you pull her out and memblur her and give her the note, because 3 elite giants will spawn inside the house, they see invis, and will jump you.
Ok after you gave peffin the note she will walk to the fire and 5 elite giants will spawn around her. They all, including Peffin are rooted and have very high resists.
Killing Peffin works best if the tanks will push her to the fire, out of reach from the giants. Do NOT use damage shields, the giants will summon!
Once Peffin is dead, loot her head and the note to Doldigun.
She also drops a nice bow:

Step 5.) Doldigun's Head
Doldigun can be found in Kael at the throne in the Manor at Loc neg 850, pos 1150.
He will spawn when you say "the Dain shall be slain for the peace we must obtain".
Hand him the note and he hands you a Ring of the Coldain Counsil (or something similar).
If you attack him he will despawn and 2 giant guards show up. Kill them.
If one of them dies, another giant will spawn, kill the 2nd giant of the first spawn first tho.
3 more giants will spawn, the last one can be identified because he is lvl 60.
After the lvl 60 giant is dead and no other giants are up, Doldigun respawns and runs towards the exit.
Kill him and loot the head.


Step 6.) Juliah's Head
You will find Counsilor Juliah Lockheart either at the counsil in throneroom with dain. (Counsil starts at 9am)
or in lower floor opposide side of the entrnce to the keep.
Hand her the note and she will attack. The other dwarfs will not assist her.
She doubles for about 380, get a good group to kill her,  when you fail you have to start again with the Ale...

Loot the head and put the 6 items in the Traitors Bane Box and combine it.
Than go to Dain Frostreaver and hand him the Velium Coldain Insignia Ring and the combined box.
He will give you the Coldain Hero's Insignia Ring and the Dirk of Dain.
