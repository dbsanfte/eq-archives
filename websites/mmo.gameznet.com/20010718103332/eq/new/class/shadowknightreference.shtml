Shadowknight Reference Guide

This is a shadowknight reference guide. This is not a class guide. If you are looking for our shadowknight class guide then you can reach it by clicking here. The purpose of this guide is to make it easy for shadowknights to find areas of interest in the game.

Quests

One thing that should be noted about shadowknight quests. Unlike most other classes shadowknights can often only do quests within their own race due to faction problems with other guilds. With some careful work you may be able to raise your factions high enough to do quests with certain other guilds(depending on the guild and on your race). As a general rule of thumb however shadowknights have less quests available to them than any other class in the game.

You will notice that the Freeport and Qeynos quest sections are pretty bare. I apologize for this but I have never played a human shadowknight to any substantial level. If anyone has any further information then feel free to mail us here at eqstaff@gameznet.com.

Freeport

Deathfist Slashed Belts ***
This is a good source of experience and money at lower levels. It also is a good way to raise faction with the Freeport Militia. It will lower your faction with the "good guys" in town. However, no shadowknight should worry about their faction faction with the do-gooders in my opinion.

Grobb

Bone Hunt ****
This is a great low level quest. It gives decent experience and items for a low level player and decaying skeletons are common in the swamp.
Grobb Cleaver ***
The reward for this quest is a pretty good 2h slashing level for the level range in which you can complete the quest. dmg 15 delay 45.
Guardian Fungus ***
This quest rewards a decent lower level magic item.
Helm of Hukulk **
The reward for this quest is a decent helm for a low-mid level character. The penalty for doing this quest is that you very well may become KOS in Neriak, one of the few friendly towns.
Kill The Greenboods *
This quest is really only viable in a PVP environment. For the most part it is a useless quest and the reward is not worth the time to cheese your way through it in PVE.

Innothule Swamp

Lynuga's Rubies ***
This is a mindless quest that rewards a decent reward. The downside to this quest is that the ruby is expensive and that the reward is random.

Mistmoore

Darkforge Armor(Bracer Chestplate Helm Vambraces) ****
Hey say what you will about the stats of the armor and the camping required. This is still the best suit of armor for a level 20-40 shadowknight.

Najena

Darkforge Armor ****
The other half of the darkforge armor quests. Same comments apply as above. The quest is a pain in the butt but the result is a decent set of armor.

Neriak

Fire Beetle Eyes ***
This is a decent newbie quest for experience and a little change.
Thex Mallet ***
This is a semi-interesting quest. The reward for completing the quest is the Reaper of the Dead. This is not a great weapon but it is the only SK weapon we know currently available through questing.

Ogguk

Lizard Meat ****
This is a good newbie quest. It rewards random low level items as well as good experience. Lizardmen are quite common in the Feerott which makes this a great way to earn experience.
Lizard Tails ***
What lizard meat is to level 1-3 shadowknights lizard tails is to level 6-10 shadowknights. The experience isn't quite as good as the lower level variety and tails are a bit less common but its still a good source of extra experience and cash nonetheless.
Shadow Tunic ***
This quest rewards a decent tunic for a low level character. This tunic is sought after more for it's dark look than it's statistics however.

Qeynos

Drosco **
This is a decent way to gain faction for lower level characters. The reward is nothing substantial but faction is always a plus.
