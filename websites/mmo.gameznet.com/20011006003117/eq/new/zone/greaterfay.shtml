Greater Faydark

Location
The Greater Faydark is located on the island of Faydwer to the east of mainland Antonica


Zone Type
Greater Faydark is predominantly a low level zone, housing within its branches the city of the Wood Elves, Kelethin.


Zone Borders
Greater Faydark borders with Butcherblock Mountains, Lesser Faydark, Clan Crusbone and also to the city of Felwithe itself.


Nearest Cities
Within the branches of the great Oaks of Greater Faydark is the tree city of the Wood Elves, Kelethin, and the home of the High Elves, Felwithe, is also accesable from Greater Faydark.

Kaladim (city of the dwarves) and Ak'Anon (city of the gnomes) are both only a couple of zones away.



Inhabitents
This zone is the land of the Wood Elves and the High Elves, because although it is the Wood Elve's city housed within its branches, it is the High Elf guards who protect it. Some Pixees also share this realm, though they are concentrated more in the neighbouring Lesser Faydark.



Route
Greater Faydark is reached from Antonica by catching the boat from East Freeport Docks, which takes you via the Ocean of Tears, stopping at Sister Island, and then zones into Butcherblock Mountains. You follow the path from the docks and after passing Kaladim you eventually find yourself in Greater Faydark.
