Top Ten Multiplayer Games
 13:01 EST - jcsmith

Gamespot did one of those Top Ten Multiplayer Game thingamajigs. Everquest was among the winners...

All this teamwork would mean little if EverQuest's gameworld weren't immersive, expansive, and diverse. EQ's Norrath appeared in our TenSpot: The Ten Best Gameworlds in PC games: Its denizens represent creatures from Western fantasy and folklore, and its diverse settings include deserts, tropical forests, snow-covered landscapes, and creepy underground dungeons. Moreover, the persistent world supports a large number of players (50 players in a raiding party, for example), and its numerous 3D environments provide plenty of adventuring opportunities. Each zone has a unique theme and design, and in the course of your travels, you might never see every unique area the gameworld offers.

Pure Evil - Everquest

Playing a different character for a while really makes you realize just how much stuff there is to do in EverQuest.
