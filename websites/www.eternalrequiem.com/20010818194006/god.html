For God so loved the world

Basically it talks about when Jesus was on earth. His death and resurection were all part of a plan that God had, to cleanse and forgive us of our sins.
Without him sending Jesus to die on a cross, we would probably still be doing Burnt sacrifices, and offering up sheep n' stuff.. that'd suck =p
