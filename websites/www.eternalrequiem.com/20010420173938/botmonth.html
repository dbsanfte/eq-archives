I honestly don't know much about this band.
But I do know they are from Australia
and the only chick in the band plays Guitar.
and she's not bad at all.

I dont know what style of music to call them either
"Dark Something or Other"
or it could be just "interesting."
there are some vocals on this album that take a
couple times of listening to before you'll like them.
An aquired taste I guess.

Training for Utopia is one of my favorite bands.
I really liked "Plastic Soul Impalement",
They had alot to say on the album, and it was carried out nicely
musically as well as lyrically
I was not as pleased with "Throwing a Wrench into
the American Music Machine"
I like chaotic music, but I didnt find anything in the album that I enjoyed
Somepeople call them "New Wave Hardcore"

I call them hardcore with Black Metal influence =p
