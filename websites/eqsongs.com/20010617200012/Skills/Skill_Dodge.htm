MELEE DEFENSE SKILL - Dodge

Available at Level: 10

Effect: If opponent scores a successful hit you have a chance of dodging.

Maximum skill: -50: 125 / 51+: 155?

Quantified: -50: (lvl + 1) * 5 / 51+: 125+(lvl-50)*3?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: This is a handy skill if a tad bit unreliable at our low skill cap.  The skill is checked automatically by the server whenever your opponent scores a successful hit.

Cautions:
