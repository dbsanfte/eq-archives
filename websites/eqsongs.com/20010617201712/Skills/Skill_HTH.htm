MELEE COMBAT SKILL - Hand to Hand

Available at Level: 1

Effect: Increases chance to hit in unarmed combat

Maximum skill: 100

Quantified: (lvl+1)*3,  HtH delay delay is 36 (I think)

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: This skill comes in handy when you are doing the all instrument attack or you are going for the maximum party buffs using your instruments.

Strategies:

This skill goes up agonizing slow, be patient trying to raise it.
If you have magic gloves (they will say MAGIC ITEM in the description) you will be able to punch invulnerable creatures (wisp, ghouls, etc..)

Cautions:

Do not expect to hit for a lot, the maximum I have punched for at level 37 with maxed hand to hand is 11 damage.   It makes my monk friends laugh.
