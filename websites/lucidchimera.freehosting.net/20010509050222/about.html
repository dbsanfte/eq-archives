Introduction
Lucid Chimera is a guild on the Vazaelle server which believes in working with one another and having fun. We are not a powerleveling guild and we aren't about getting items. However, if a guild member needs assistance with obtaining an item, members of the guild should do their best to help if possible. We are to do the best we can to help members of our guild, but also others in need. We strive to be helpful and friendly to everyone.

Policy
Members of Lucid Chimera have guidelines which are expected to be followed. If a serious case is to occur, a member may be removed once the action is investigated.

1.) Kill stealing and loot stealing are in no way permitted. This includes kill stealing/looting to get revenge on someone.

2.) Training is not allowed. This includes training to get revenge on someone. We realize training sometimes happens by mistake, but if we find that it was intentional, the issue will be looked into.

3.) Cheating is not to be done, even if Verant Interactive has made a glitch. Along with breaking our rules, it also breaks Verant Interactive's policy.

4.) In serious discussions and meetings, members should refrain from the use of regional and cultural slang that may be misconstrued by other races, religions, cultures, etc. Slang terminology that is harmless in one segment of society may not be so harmless in all segments of society.

5.) Members are dedicated to play that puts Lucid Chimera in the best possible light. That means avoiding arguments in the shout and out of character channels with other players and guild, and stepping out of groups that aren't exhibiting fair or respectful play ethics. We should do our best to help non-members when we can, and tailor our role within any group situation to show non-members what it means to be a Chimerian.

6.) If problems arise between members of Lucid Chimera, the issue should be discussed privately. If the matter can't be resolved through private channels, then a mediator should be appointed to hear the grievance and rule on it. The decision that the mediator makes is final. If the decision does not satisfy all parties then a guild vote may be held, if both parties agree, to appeal the matter.

7.) Members of the guild are encouraged to keep their guild tag visible, if you wish to stay anonymous then it is recommended that you use the role-play tag.

Our members
The following qualities are what we look for in our members. There are many great traits but these are some we feel are needed.

Honesty- Members of Lucid Chimera are to always tell the truth whether it be to members of the guild or non-members.

Integrity- Members of Lucid Chimera are to say things that they mean and mean the things they say.

Loyalty- Members of Lucid Chimera are to stay with us through the good times and the bad.

Tactfulness- Members of Lucid Chimera are to leave comments that are not necessary to themselves if someone may find them hurtful.

Removal

No one is to be kicked out of Lucid Chimera without prior notice. If an incident occurs that is serious enough to make all members feel that person should leave, they will be notified and asked to leave. The issue will be looked through by the guild leader, as they will have the final decision based upon the feelings of the other members of the guild. If someone feels they do not wish to be in Lucid Chimera anymore, they should contact Kilaya before leaving.



Conclusion

Everquest can be a very frustrating game at times. As guildmates we are here to help make Everquest more enjoyable. We want our members to have fun in their Everquest experience as that is why we play this game. Have fun everyone!
