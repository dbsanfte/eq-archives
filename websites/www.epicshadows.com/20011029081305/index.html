Welcome to Epic Shadows

Epic Shadows is a collection of dedicated fighters, casters and tradesmen who belong to the Vazaelle EverQuest server. We stand for supporting each other, and helping everyone have the best EQ experience on the net. Our officers and others organize raids and guild activities to build strong bonds between each member, and we also contribute our all to the groups that we join.

We search for those who are dedicated to their groups and to their own progression. We support you and invite you to join us. Send us an e-mail if you are considering joining our Legion of Shadows. (E-mail Ludarian)
