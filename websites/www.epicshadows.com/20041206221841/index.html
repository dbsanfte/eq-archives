City Tasks - Nightly
City task groups are always a good idea. Check the forums for info on where to get your city tasks and then check online with others your level to help finish them.

City tasks directly contribute to the guild level. Our guild level determines what city raids are issued to us and, indirectly, what items are available on faction merchants. By doing your city tasks you are helping progress every member of the guild.
