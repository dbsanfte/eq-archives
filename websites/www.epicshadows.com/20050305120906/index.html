Epic Shadows members range from typically level 20 to 35 with the more active players at the high end. We are looking for adventures and artisans of all classes and level ranges, and specifically for people who want to progress and play 3 or more nights a week for a good amount of time. The more time you spend online the better we'll get to know you :)

Please visit our forums and introduce yourself if you would like to meet us in game. We'll set you up with access to our TeamSpeak server and have you come along with whatever we happen to be doing that night. Or if you need help with anything, feel free to let us know and we'll get it done.

Heritage Quests
These are the best way to earn status points for your guild! Typically we will be doing pieces of these quests every night with the focus right now on Strange Black Rock and Hadden's Earring. Please research these quests as much as possible.

Heritage quests directly contribute to the guild level. Our guild level determines what city raids are issued to us and, indirectly, what items are available on faction merchants. By doing your heritage quests you are helping progress every member of the guild!
