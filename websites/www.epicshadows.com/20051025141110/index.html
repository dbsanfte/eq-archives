Heritage Quests
These are the best way to earn status points for your guild! Typically we will be doing pieces of these quests every night with the focus right now on Screaming Mace, Golden Efreeti Boots and the Crown of King Tranix. Please research these quests as much as possible.

Heritage quests directly contribute to the guild level. Our guild level determines what city raids are issued to us and, indirectly, what items are available on faction merchants. By doing your heritage quests you are helping progress every member of the guild!
