LucasArts releases a new group of screenshots from its upcoming Star Wars action game.

LucasArts Entertainment has released a new group of screenshots from its upcoming Star Wars action game, Jedi Outcast: Jedi Knight II. The screens show off some of the detailed character models and environments that will appear in the game.
Jedi Knight II, which is powered by the Quake III engine, will let players assume the role of Jedi knight Kyle Katarn and battle the forces of the Empire. The game is in development at Raven Software, and it is scheduled for release in mid-2002. For more information, take a look at our previous coverage of the game.

By Trey Walker, GameSpot PC [POSTED: 09/21/01]
