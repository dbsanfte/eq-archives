Screenshot procedures
Submitted by: Brutius Kikasius on Wednesday, August 2nd at 03:37pm EST

Hi all, Just wanted to let everyone know a few procedures about sending me (or other staff) screenshots for NPC's.

  1. Try and get all screenshots without other people/npcs in the way.
  2. Try and get far enough away from the monster so that you can see the name, but where the monsters doesn't take up the whole screen (I put all monsters into a jpeg 280X200 big).
  3. Send me (brutius@eqmaps.com) any and all screenshots that you get, I will be happy to add them and give you credit for submission.
Thanks!
Brutius Kikasius, Level 52 Magician Brell Serelis

Win a GeForce2 GTS!
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Tuesday, July 25th at 12:59am EST

Starting today EQmaps.com will hold a two week drawing that ends on Tuesday August 8th for a GeForce2 GTS graphics card from Creative Labs called the 3D Blaster Annihilator 2 delivered to the winner's door! If the winner cannot use the GeForce2 because their computer does not have an AGP slot or they already have the card etc. they can opt for a prize of $250 US dollars instead. The purpose of this drawing is to promote the use of our new Price Details Tool that allows players to post prices they have seen a particular item sell for on their server to help others in their purchasing decisions. The Price Detail Tool can be found using the link in the Additional Info section of every item that is not flagged as "NO DROP". When a player enters a price into the database using the form provided they are automatically entered into the drawing, an e-mail address is also required so we can contact you should you win. The more you submit the better chance you have to win! On August 8th I will pick a random entry from the database off all price entries that have a valid e-mail address. I will then contact that person by e-mail and give them one (1) day to respond. If they don't respond in 24 hours I will pick another entry and contact that person and continue to do so until a winner responds. I will also post the winner in a news post on August 8th in case their e-mail isn't working for some reason. Please read the rules page for a full list of the rules.

Thank you to all who participates. I had a lot of fun making this tool and I hope it will help answer all those "how much does this go for?" questions. Please also note I put a lot of care into making sure all the entries will be as accurate as possible, this meant programming in a bogus price filter. This will keep the cheaters out and make the tool useful for everyone.
