    Win a GF3 or Radeon 8500!
    Submitted by: NiteRaven on Saturday, January 5th at 12:30pm EST

    Starting today January 5th and ending on January 31st 2002, EQ Maps will be accepting entries for a drawing where the winner picks a Nvidia GeForce3 Ti 200 or ATI Radeon 8500 graphics card! Submissions for the drawing will be in the form of valid price entries on any item. To submit a price simply look up the item you know the price of, click "Price Details", scroll to the bottom and add a price. The more prices you enter, the better chance you have to win!

    Price entries will be monitored by myself and I encourage everyone to report bogus prices. If an obviously bogus price is found, ALL of your price entries will be deleted. You MUST enter your e-mail address if you want to win, otherwise I cannot contact you. The winner will be randomly selected and announced on February 1st. The graphics card of choice will then be ordered and sent shortly there after.

    The reason I'm doing this is to encourage everyone to add more prices to the database. To keep the database fresh with new prices and as accurate as possible. This is a small gift I can give back to you, the EQ Maps fan. Thank you!
