Another GeForce2 Drawing
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Friday, July 20th at 12:15am EST

Starting today July 20th until August 5th, 2001 EQ Maps will be holding another drawing for a Creative Labs GeForce2 GTS graphics card! Submissions for the drawing will be in the form of valid price entries on any item. To submit a price simply look up the item you know the price of, click "Price Details", scroll to the bottom and add a price. The more prices you enter, the better chance you have to win!

Price entries will be monitored by myself and I encourage everyone to report bogus prices. If an obviously bogus price is found, ALL of your price entries will be deleted. You MUST enter your e-mail address if you want to win, otherwise I cannot contact you. The winner will be randomly selected and announced on August 6th. The GeForce2 will be shipped to their door the very same day.

The reason I'm doing this is to encourage everyone to add more prices to the database. To keep the database fresh with new prices and as accurate as possible. This is a small gift I can give back to you, the EQ Maps fan. Thank you!

Improved Search Engine
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Sunday, July 8th at 09:21pm EST

I recently upgraded the backend database used for EQ Maps from MySQL 3.22.32 to 3.23.39. What does this mean to you? Well thanks to the newly implemented Full Text Search abilities of MySQL: faster and more accurate search results! Taking advantage of this new feature I was able to create a new search script in a matter of a few hours. Once again you can quickly and easily search through the Item, NPC, Landmark, Zone, and Spell tables. While I have done extensive testing, let me know if you run across any problems.

Fixing Things
Submitted by: Darnavian on Thursday, June 21st at 02:55pm EST

Well it seems as if things have finally calmed down a bit on the equipment front. These items will be in more of a hall of fame, as most of them are unique and very difficult to obtain.
