Four New Map Additions
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Saturday, April 1st at 08:25am CST

Keeping up with the persistent work of the cartographers out there I have 4 new maps from them. Maps of the Veeshan server recently created a great map for the under water dungeon Kedge Keep (set 2), Clan Crushbone (map 2), and the Plane of Fear (map 3). I am especially pleased about the Crushbone map as there wasn't a really good one out there and it's such a popular place.

Kuan of the Cazic Thule server recently came out with a great map for extremely popular dungeon of Lower Guk (map 4). This is the most detailed map I have seen from him so far and the location of the rare spawns along with what they drop is a nice addition. He also made some corrections to his Lord Nagafen's Lair map (2).


Huge South Ro Update
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Friday, March 31st at 12:30pm CST

I want to get back into updating the site regularly so we can get caught up with all the existing maps, items, landmarks, and NPC's before the expansion comes out. As we stand now we have at least 1, many times up to 4, maps for every zone except for the Arena and the Plane of Air. It's safe to say we have no rival in that category. Items, Darnavian has been working feverishly for the last few months adding descriptions, quest information, and strategies to every piece of armor in our database. I think he will next move onto some of the trade skills and then onto weapons. We should be set with that by the time the expansion comes out. At that point by the request of someone I will put our entire item database online so it can be downloaded. Does anyone have a particular format they would like to see that in? I'm thinking tab separated and in HTML format.

Now landmarks and NPC's is something I want to take care of personally. We have a lot of landmarks but many times they don't a picture or location because they were submitted by someone who was just recalling the landmark by memory. I will go through those updating them and also adding a lot more. For the NPC's we are mostly missing the screenshots and descriptions for them. From this day on until the expansion I will attempt to make one zone update per day. Today I added around 12 landmarks and added a large number of pictures to NPC's of South Ro. It's a great zone that I think is way under used, so I figured I'd show it off. I was even lucky enough to run across Scrounge, no claw though :( If you would like to assist me in working on the NPC database we are always looking! Just drop me an email. Thanks!

Three new maps - 160 total
Submitted by: NiteRaven on Sunday, March 19th at 09:14pm CST

As the title says I have three new maps for you all. The first one was sent in by Volgor Grimblade of the Karana server and it's a map of High Hold Keep (map #2), mostly the sub levels where you can find picklock goblins. It's a nice place to fight around level 22 to 30. The next map is of Clan Runnyeye (map #4), one of the many great ones out there. This one is unique in that it shows the locations of the major/rare spawns and has a list showing the approximate level, hit points, time of spawn, and what the NPC's drop. Great stuff. The only problem is you would need a mammoth size printer to print this thing out. The last map comes from our friend Maps of the Veeshan server. One of her latest works is of the Innothule Swamp (map #4), once again another great looking map.
