    Crusaders of Plilo Guild Website created. This is to serve as a central location for guild members and others to view and learn about COP. Here are the sections that were created today tho not all of them have lots of content.

    • announcements is where announcements are made about the guild and about the guild website's content updates.
    • bulletin board is where we have a nice ezBoard for our guild members to rant and chat about EQ.
    • calender is the event calender for COP. Must have some balance with IRL events and in game events.
    • guild info is the section where we have Guild Documents and Member Info.
    • links is an aggregated set of links that we find useful whilst playing EQ.
