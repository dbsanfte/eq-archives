 Breaking Wind

  5.12.2004

 
Tonight we held our first Plane of Air raid. What started out as exploration ended up a huge victory for us. Read on!

We entered the zone weapons swinging. Here you see Xora charging head first into the fray. She really fears nothing.


As we fought further into the plane, it was decided that we should try the Wind Circle Event. Here is the guy that greeted us. The bruise under his eye is from Xora. All we knew was that we had to kill him.


The bee fell like any other bee falls: fast. We didn't have much time for reflection though. We had 20 minutes to engage the Avatar of Wind! First we had to find him. We fought through countless more mobs and located our prey. Here he is taking a serious beating.


It was our first raid in Air, and our first attempt on an event, and we came out victorious! Whats this on its corpse? Thats right, the stuff we've been dreaming about for a year: Our first pieces of elemental armor.


Grats to Flexonin on the silk bracelet and Jeffers on the chain bracelet.

Tonight was a successful first run at Air. We look forward to more.

Congratulations Divine Prophecy!
-Ragnok

 RZ for thee

  5.2.2004

 

Well it was bound to happen sooner or later - and no, I'm not talking about killing RZtW, I mean getting rid of that jinky line of news and replacing it with a full colored spread frontpage spread that people could actually browse through!

Poor Rallos Zek though, he's been complaining to us a lot recently that we never come visit him and his friends anymore for coffee. See how sad he looks above? Taking his frustration out on some poor shmuck. Being the nice folks that we are we decided we'd visit him and take him up on his offer for some evening fun. Tallon Zek greeted us at the door and thought at first we were over to collect on his tab, so called his brother Vallon over to show us the door. Got to love those script bugs when you get a 2nd demi-god to spawn on you while your still working on one. On the bright side of it all, there was plenty of nudity to go around afterwards, and we almost decided to throw a toga party. But being the nice folks we are, we couldn't forget our obligation to Rallos could we?

Boom! He didn't know what hit him. Notice the naked rangers in the photo. The nice guy though, he even left us some toys to play with

War Charm of Crossed Blades for Kyleigha

Black Collar of Boundless Might for Nostruum

Of course, we were hoping he'd drop some slightly pointer, sharper things - but we can forgive him for that can't we? As long as he remembers to keep some in stock for next time

In other less exciting, yet equally important news - the flow of SolRo mini loots just doesn't seem to have an end in sight, and all the while we flag more and more of ourselves to get ourselves Fire flagged. REBB supplies are at an all time low, but hopefully that will change, as practically all our monks and BL's are happily whacking away things now with their floating 7's  anymore and we'd have em rotting, god forbid.

Our sexy blue-bummed dark elf chantress has also recently completed her epic - and thanks to Maner's pulling (or is that training) skills, Chardok actually seemed fun again. Those be warned going to Fear though, not to mess with the freaking Berzerker epic imp unless your really prepared  Congratz Syncope! Your new snake looks wonderful
