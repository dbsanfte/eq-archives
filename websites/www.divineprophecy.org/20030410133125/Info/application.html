Joining Information

To apply to the guild, please follow the outlined instructions below:

1.) Required level 58. Rare exceptions may be made by a vote of the officers for a particularly needed class.

2.) To apply - Read the Divine Prophecy Charter. If you are willing and able to comply with all of these guidelines, post an application on the Application Board. You should include the following: 

A.) Full Name of your Main Character 

B.) Class/Race/Level of your main character 

C.) Any alternate characters you play 

D.) What time zone you live in 

E.) Why do you want to join Divine Prophecy? 

F.) What are your expectations of a guild and your guildmates? 

G.) What other guilds have you been a member of? Why did you leave? 

H.) Do you already have a sponsor (see below)? 

I.) What are your unbuffed magic and fire and cold resistances? 

J.) Do you have your Seb key? What other keys do you have (Howling Stones, etc...)?

3.) All applicants will need to seek and find a sponsor within Divine Prophecy. This sponsor must be a full member (no probationary members) in good standing. To find a sponsor, we suggest that you group with our members whenever possible and get to know them. Once you have developed a relationship with them, approach them about sponsorship. Your sponsor must post their willingness to support you on your application.

4.) After your sponsor has agreed to support you formally on the Application board, there will be a one week incubation period before you are invited to DP as a probationary member.

  • Please note that applications can be canceled by through a majority vote by the Officers

  • Probation will last for 30 days and 4 full guild raids. It will be your responsibility to attend the 4 raids based on posted raids on the Raid Board, which you will be given access to upon being invited. Probation will not expire until you have attended 4 raids (so, if you don't attend 4 raids in 30 days, your probation will be extended).

  • During the probation period, the ability to roll on any raid drops will not be available unless no full member will use such drops (this is at the discretion of the Raid Leader).  Further, "hand-me-downs," discounts, donations, loans, and other similar requests should not be solicited from the guild until probation is completed.

  • At any time during the application or probation period, the consideration of an individual maybe declined should the sum of five full DP members express an objection of membership to any officer. These objections will then be shared only with the other officers to verify and void applications or promotions.

5.) Your sponsor is responsible for your actions during this time. You should check with him/her in case there are any doubts on what is appropriate or inappropriate behavior.

6.) Upon completion of the probationary requirements, your sponsor must formally post on the Application Board their support or lack there of in your full membership. At this time the officers will also discuss your membership. Any reservations or concerns will result in us not offering you a full membership in Divine Prophecy.

Additional Comments:

- Divine Prophecy raids on a regular basis. Member participation in these raids is necessary for the furthering of our guild. If you do not plan on regularly attending, do not apply.

- As DP is a raiding guild, you should own resistance gear. It is strongly recommended that your unbuffed fire, cold and magic resist are over 100 (this means with you and your gear only, no self or group buffs or songs).

- We do not support guild shopping. If you are applying to many guilds on Xev, do not bother posting here. Any applications to other guilds will result in the end of your trial probationary period or the invalidation of your application. Also, if you are currently guilded, we will not consider your application.

- Divine Prophecy will not assist with any epic mobs until sixty (60) days after invite. At this point, the officer in charge of epics will prioritize any assistance based on the benefit of the guild at her discretion.
