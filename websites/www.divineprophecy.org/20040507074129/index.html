Divine Prophecy Weekly News Letter

It was our turn to go through the excruciating experience of Vex Thal last week.  Fortunately, servers were stable for us and we only had to do that jaunt in one go around.  Seems lately Vex Thal has been like the energizer bunny… It keeps going and going and going… Either that or we all are just becoming strangely addicted to the pain and torture of it all.  Of course, many great things happened while we were there… Unfortunately, Celi, Aerialle, Manernoob, Fayrielle, Cam and Seb won’t share the great things that happened but it is obvious from all the Hush money floating around it must have been good stuff… Why is it I always miss out on the swinging groups?  Gah.

 

In other news, Rotlom is no longer beating the dead horse… He has moved on to goats after talking to Talvor about the logistics of goat beating… or should that be bleating.  Never trust a skirt wearing shaman near your heard of goats is all I have to say.

 

Zorean is still the resident village idiot, even if he is deployed right now.  We all miss him and hope he will be home soon so that his wife can play with us again.  We have lost his cross dressing wife to the rigors of everday imps that have been spawned from Tunare.  I know, I saw the picture, the resemblance is scarey.  I guess it could be worse, they could be the spawn of Innoruk… Though, the boy imp has the look of Bristlebane about him.  Probably why his mother’s a cross dresser??

 

Ragnok, after spending umpteen hours ( 24 to be exact) in camping his fabled axe from Guk is having hallucinations of grandeur and thinks that he is the one true king.  Obviously, someone needs to smack him upside the head.  Who ever heard of a dorf being “the one?” 

 

Cami, in an effort to prove that she really is a real person and not a grotchbot, has started an all Pie team.  Please send all inquiries to the Grotchbot… er Cami for information on how to join.

 

OOOhhhh Talvor got a new skirt while we were in Vex Thal, I tried to tell him his old skirt looked better but he has something for pink so…  Him and Olangi get a long well… Talvor has the pink skirt and Olangi has the pink thong.  What a team… what a pair.

 

Our Hairyberry halfling is coming back from the tropics… Which is totally awesome because we all know that he was the Hannd groomer and with him being gone that shaggy bear has gotten so matted… Rumor has it the mats are from ape poop in burned woods (which is rumored to be floating off of Erudin this week) so not only is he matted but stinks.  Luckily, Rene will be here to fix it… if we can just keep both away from our official hair remover service aka Krikey.  Otherwise, both might be sporting the buzz look just in time for the hot summer months.

 

Well that’s about all the news I can remember at the moment… Though in all seriousness I just want to say, Stream and Rigo, we are going to miss you two.  Hurry back as soon as you can.
