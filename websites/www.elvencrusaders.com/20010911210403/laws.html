The Elven Crusaders Laws
     
    and Guild Information
     

    The Elven Crusaders are an aggressive anti PK Elven only guild. We KOS known PK's, PK Guilds, personal enemies of our members and of the Elven Crusaders as a Guild. The Elven Crusaders members are to be honorable at all times. We use the PK's tactics to take them down but we live by a different code. We do not corpse camp (wait till they have their weapon before you kill the PK again). We do not get involved in zone shout wars. Zone shouts only serves to make the PK look bad. If anything at all needs to be said in the zones, let it be "Another PK has been slain by the Elven Crusaders". We do not use foul language or cuss and avoid street slang as much as possible. We are a Role Playing guild.
