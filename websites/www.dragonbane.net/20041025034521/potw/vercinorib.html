Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

VERCINORIB
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Game name is usually Verc-something. Real name is George, male, 33, and currently a student.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live just outside Philadelpia, PA. And am single with a cat and a roommate who is a long time friend of many years

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Impossible in 3, I’m too complicated for that, but I’ll try: Curious, extremely intelligent and maddening

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
I like almost anything that is well prepared. I’ve tried so much there is no possible way I could pick a favorite.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Friendly, highly intelligent and very hard to understand.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
I listen to music that matches my mood at the time. It can vary from classical, classic rock, and all the way to techno.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
I have a cat that lives with me called Puddin (not my choice of name, I’m 3rd human for the cat), and my family has 4 more called Fitch (gigantic cat), Burma, Punkin and RC (short for Our Cat).

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
What breakfast?

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
How people can spend their lives almost totally ignorant of the world around them.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Finish fixing up my house, get a reliable, energy efficient, ecologically friendly car (probably a Toyota Prius) then invest the rest.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
Presidents Abraham Lincoln and Franklin Delanor Roosevelt. Both were people who were given immense responsibility at two of the most critical junctures of the US’s history, saw what needed to be done, and were not afraid to do what was necessary for the nation to survive even though it was wildly unpopular at the time. Yet both men were compassionate and did not lose either their compassion or their humanity in the process.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
If you are not comfortable with how you look you cannot be satisfied with who you are.  The only thing I want to do is get back into good physical shape so I have more energy to deal with life better than I have this past year.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Probably Star Trek, the original version.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
Something that flies so I would never have to deal with traffic again.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Just one? There are far too many. 1) completely lose track of time, 2) when I talk, I talk too much 3) I have both the ability and the tendency to pick up a conversation long after you think the topic has been safely switched exactly where it left off 4) I don’t recognize when it is time to let something go.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
Couldn’t. I’ve been blessed for most of my life with a small circle of close friends that I have known for a very long time. I don’t hold scores of what people do. I think that the nicest thing that someone can do is help out a total stranger in need, because there is no reason you have to help or could ever expect anything back from that person, so it is true altruism.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
The perfect place is spending time with the person I love who understands me soul deep and vice-versa. I’ve thought I had reached that twice so far in my life, only to find it to be illusion

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
I have no desire to be in any movie, I am not an actor

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now?
Lets see, 2 bachelors living in a single house? You bet.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Nirvana is transitory: the strength of humanity is measured by being able to get up when the world has crushed you down, realizing that even the best times will not last so they should be cherished while you have them.
