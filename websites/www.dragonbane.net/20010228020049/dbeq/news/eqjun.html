June 14, 2000
Events
Recent events included: DB/LH Fear Raid last week; Raid on Mistmoore last Saturday Guild event led by Gammbit; Xaviertalon and Rhapsodi (LH) wedding on Saturday; DB/LH Hate raid all this week. We've been busy as usual! We had our first Leaders' Meeting on Sunday which went very well, and a subsequent Meeting Summary was sent to all the members.

New administrative additions to DB include a DB FTP Site (administered by Dinnin) where members can download files they need or are interested in trying, and a new DB Email List Server (administered by Xfighter) whereby all DB can send email to the rest of the group by entering one email address. Two additions that should improve DB's efficiency even more!
