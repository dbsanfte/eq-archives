May 20, 2000
Sebilis Raid
Our DB/LH/SOS Sebilis Raid got off to a shaky start. Everything went smoothly (too smoothly) until we reached just outside the "Disco Room." Then a massive train killed us off and we had a night of corpse recovery. Special thanks to members of Vis Maior for helping with CR.

Our next night went better (we ensured all members had 2 keys this time) and we plan to continue our raid into Sebilis until Tuesday. We did get the Froglok Bonecasters Robe (10 ac, 8 str, 8 int, 10 sv fire and magic) which will be rolled for tonight.

Recovery Status
We received this email from Irone/Corpis:

"FYI: I have been moved from the regular hospital I was at to a physical rehab hospital (which, while indicates improvement I suppose, I'm not very happy about... I want to go HOME!)"
May 16, 2000
Plane of Sky Raid
Our recent POS raid went really well considering it was cut short due to Mother's Day. We were able to clear third island and hold our own. It was a joint Dragon's Bane/Legendary Heroes and Slayers of Slaw Raid.

May 12, 2000
Irone/Corpis
Our thoughts and prayers are with Irone/Corpis. We recently received this email from him:

"Hospitalization of Irone/Corpis
While leaving work last Friday at 5:30 pm, I was run down in the parking lot in front of my office building by a hit and run driver. I was very nearly killed, and almost lost my left leg entirely. Following my 3rd surgery this morning, the doctors are confident that they have saved the leg.

Today I finally got access to my laptop computer, and can at least E-mail (altough cannot play EQ). I will be in the Hospital another couple of weeks, and I will likely confined to a wheel-chair for the next 5 months. I will probabally limp the remainder of my life.

So, I will not be on for a bit, to possibly be followed by being on more than ever..."
Troy
