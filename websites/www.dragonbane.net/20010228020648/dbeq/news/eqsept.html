September 23, 1999
We are looking to put on our first festival Friday, October 1st at midnight! This should accommodate all time zones. It will be held in front of Felwithe castle. (For more information, please go to our "Events" Section). What is being planned:

Newbie give aways - in the next 2 weeks, start saving those small loot items that are of great benefit to newbies (weapons, bags, cloth armour, etc.) We will be giving them away randomly throughout the event. For example, Gamil will shout "first level 4 and under to wave to me gets free backpack!"

Drunken duels - alcohol will be provided to the combattants. Then the dueling will begin only after they are staggering. I need volunteers for this. Prizes will be given.

Beauty contests - both male and female. Judged on dancing and answering a judge's question. Need volunteers for this to be judges. Prizes will be given.

Scavenger hunt - to take place in Greater Fay area. Will be a large event and need volunteers for this. Prizes will be given to the winning team.

Relay Races - needs volunteers for this also. Prizes will be given to the winning team.

Auction/Sales - Any large items you wish to sell, keep until this date and we'll do a general auction as well.

I need volunteers as you can see for all the events. I have emailed most of you with your "volunteer" assignments hehe.
I will be sponsoring this event (prizes, etc.) out of my bank account, not the guild bank account. Drinks and food will be given out during the event as well. This event will be advertised on all the main EQ sites included EQ Vault, Everquest, Everlore, etc. The time and date picked are to accommodate not only all time zones, DB members, but also the other players not in DB that would like to participate. This will be one very cool festival!

Yield Policy
Some news that Sharac has submitted -

Ran across this today and didn't know if you had seen it. Thought I would forward it on to you. Found it at Casters Realm.

This was on alt.games.everquest from Brad McQuaid, Producer of Everquest:

If a higher level player who would get no exp for the kill is camping a
spawn, and a group of players that would get exp (meaning they should be
there) wants to fight there, the higher level player must yield. The lower
level group has the right of way.

As for the other disciplinary information in that post [referrring to banning
if you harrass/argue with a GM], it sounds like the same policy that's always
been in place: if you are warned several times and continue to break the
rules, you will be banned.

And this...

I need to clarify some additional things about this new rule.

First, we didn't officially announce it yet. Apparently, there was some
confusion and a guide or GM enforced it or talked about it, and hence there
was a post on EQVault about it. I've confirmed this and explained the details
because I didn't want any false or half-truths floating about. An official
posting will be made soon, and everquest.com will be updated.

Second, there will probably be an exception made for no drop/trade/lore type
items which could not be traded for or bought by a higher level player.

In any case, the Yield rule is coming, but don't expect it to be actively
enforced until we officially announce it.
