What to get in advance:
Get fire resist gear and magic resist
It is very important you get your resist magic gear around 100, same for fire. That is also done preferably without buff, reason is Nagafen might / will debuff you.  Also, keep it half between god like mana / stats and god like resist. 200 resist and 100 wis isnt so good, but 150 150 is way better. A feared caster / meleer is of no use. So get resist magic high.

Nagafen Looting Policy when DB is host:
Fire giant loot will be randomed as follows--
Crown: All caster classes, bards
Torque: Rangers, Paladins, Shadow Knights
Mithril arms, legs, sword: All tank classes
Fancy Caster Face Piece: Int Based Casters (wis based casters have ruby veil).

Every member must give a /tell to the Leader before the Nagafen killing of what item they wish to roll for should Nagafen drop it. All members may only pick one item. Should item not be dropped by Nagafen, then they don't get a chance to roll for it.

Any item that is dropped that was not chosen by anyone, shall be rolled for by all those that can use and need.

All the people that can roll for the item will roll random 0 200, highest to 200 wins.
