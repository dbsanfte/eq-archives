January 29, 2000
Dragon's Bane becomes Dragon's Bait!
"Hiya all! Just wanted to let you all know (for those of you that weren't there) that our guild event in Permafrost today was a lot of fun and very exciting!

We had 4 groups of DB's and we all camped the King's area and we were on such a roll! We were killing ice giants, diplomat (looks like an ice giant), huge spiders, and magis (they look like ice giants too). We were killing 5 ice giants at a time and lived!

Amun was the puller for all the groups and during one pull he fell in a trap. So he was using FD to get out and found himself right by Lady Vox.

All of a sudden while we were talking to Amun about how he could possibly get out, Lady Vox sent out her priest and more ice giants. As soon as Zasc saw the priest he told everyone to run, that Vox was on her way. So some groups teleported, some ran, some gated. There were a couple casualties at this point. So then we all came back to the gate at the King's area and rested there and we could still see the ice giants through the gate.

Well what happened next? Several of us got summoned by Lady Vox! Amun from his position could see us being summoned to her and killed. [What does it feel like to be summoned? Well you're sitting down doing nothing, then all of a sudden your screen looks all twirly, and 1 second later you see your body on the ground, with the saying "you have been knocked unconscious." You don't even see who or what killed you or summoned you lol and you don't even see it coming!]

Now we thought "omg what do we do?" hehe! (But it was still very exciting.) We got a necro to use his summon corpse spell (which costs 162pp per person) to come to perma and he was able to get all the bodies back!

All DB pitched in to find the necro, teleport him, buy coffins, pitch in money, res, etc. It was truly a group effort to accomplish this and it made us feel proud to be in the type of guild we have :)

All in all, the event was a ton of fun and one day soon, Lady Vox will learn that she is Dragon's bane!" - Pantera

January 26, 2000
Guild Events
Our last two guild events were at Mistmoore Castle and Infected Paw. Both were successful and had a good turn out. This Saturday's Guild Event is in Permafrost. All information, maps and unique items are posted in the Members' Only section on the site.

Group Hunt - Lower Guk
During the last three nights, several DB got together and hunted in Lower Guk. The members included on varying nights, Amun, Tamas, Zasc, Tammuzz, Ettore, Kalene, Aelen, Sharac, Burl, Mato, Dolgare, Pantera and Doloiel. They succeeded in obtaining so far, numerous serpentine bracers, skull shaped barbutes, two ykeshas, two shiny metallic robes and silversilk leggings. This hunt will be going on for the next week as well and all members are invited to join up in this group hunt late at night eastern standard time. Any items not needed by the hunting members are being giving to other members in the guild.
