Some Crafting (Level 800 Quest) Information for you!|by Skaull
Hello this is Skaull and I suck at playing a NS. hehe. Now that that is out of the way I wnated to share my lvl 800 Alchy quest with you all. Hopefully this will save other peeps time if they decide to do this quest. Remember that I have only done the Alchy one. SO if Mythic has the WCing screwed up blame me anyways. :)

First off I talked to my trainer. He gave me an item to make. First important thing. The NPCs ALWAYS want lvl 96 or better. Now the materials I needed were not all in TNN. Too high of a lvl for newb capital. So one needs to plan ahead. I needed Carbide metal and had to go to housing twice. Time sink. If you looking to get lvling tools and you want more than one I suggest making batches of items. The quest appears to repeat. I have
done it twice now and same stuff.

So I made the item and gave to trainer. Then I had to make another item and give to girl in MM. She had me make 2 more items and run them to 2 NPC that were in Ardee. Then it was back to MM to talk to girl and then back to my trainer. I receive a lvling item with 19 charges and 8% add to crafting lvling chance. It took roughly 2 hours to do this once.

NOW this is the reason for this email. TO SAVE YOU ALL TIME hopefully. You want to do this quest and thinking you might need more than one item from it planning is key. Make items in batches. Then repeat the quest after you get your crafting item. The second time took roughly 15 minutes on NS speed. Also all 4 quests are given at once by trainer. So you have to be aware of the thread you chose. Me I pick one and when going to the accept box I back off to stop it. then I get spam telling me which quest I
declined. Tells me the lvl on this. Then I just started it again if the lvl is the one I wanted.

I hope this helps peeps out with the lvling grind. Me I think it is well worth doing the Alchy one at least. Each batch of 11 tries now cost me 343g at lvl 987. And that is with using metal I salvaged. No real cost there for me. Thanks for your time and Happy Crafting. :)

Skaull
