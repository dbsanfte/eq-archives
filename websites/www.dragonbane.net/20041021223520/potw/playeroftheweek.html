Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

CIARO
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Ciaro, Kathi Zelenik, female, 50, currently unemployed but starting as teacher's aide in September.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
Currently in Sterling, Virginia but before March lived in Madison, WI for 26 years. I live with Hubby (Mike aka Shallafi), Son (Erick aka Erichk), Daughter (Megan aka Kardilia) and Grandson (Mike is only 2 mos so not playing yet :/ )

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Smart, Loving, lazy

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Paella, salad with viniagrette dressing and real sangria. My favorite dessert is Cheesecake, but there would be no room after Paella.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Bossy, loyal, smart

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Classic Rock...

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
We have 2 dogs, Nikita is a 7 yr old AKC German Wirehaired Pointer and Kaos is a 2 yr old Who'syerdaddy.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
I rarely eat breakfast...(I know I know no lectures!) but this morning Megan made waffles so I shared one with the dogs.

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
Getting a lame excuse when someone doesnt follow through on doing things they say they will. I hate the insult to my intelligence that I would buy the excuse more than the fact the item didnt get done.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Call my husband and tell him to retire, buy a new car and decide where to build a new house.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
The person I most admire is my sister Sue. She has used her inate abilities to move from a retail sales clerk to a vice-president of a prominent Office Supply manufacturer without a college degree. She has also adopted a special-needs child and provided him with a secure home, specialized education and a hope for the future. She used the opportunities that came her way and has had the pleasure of traveling the world. Way to go sis!

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I am currently struggling with the weight issue, otherwise I figure I am what I am and I can live with that.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Captain Kangaroo

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
A Gulfstream jet. I love to travel but I hate commercial airports and airlines that are never on time.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Make lists of things-to-do that I never get done.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
No one individual thing really comes to mind, but my hubby does little things like letting me off near the door when its raining or very cold, getting me a seat when the pub is crowded, sending me jokes in emails, calling me during the day just to say hi that make me feel special.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
With my husband relaxing and sharing a good beer...doesnt have to be anyplace special.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
I'm not sure if it's a lack of imagination or a vacillating nature but I can't decide on any one movie or even one character from any movie.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now?
Yes, we just finished breakfast and I wanted to check emails before cleaning up.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
If you love someone, let them know it through everything you do; just saying the words isn't enough.
