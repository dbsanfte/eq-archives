What to get in advance:
Get cold resist gear and magic resist
Items include: Mammoth leggings / icy greaves / Gnomish environment suit / dwarven tunic / BS mantle /Peridot ring / diamond ring / jacinth ring / Resist magic bracer (gem?) / symbol of vox / Black iron crown / tranix crown (grin) / frog king crown / barbute / earring of magic resist / cold resist / neck of magic resist / neck from noble in lguk / cloak of icebear / mammoth cloak / seahorse etc.

It is very important you get your resist magic gear around 100, same for cold. That is also done preferably without buff, reason is Vox might / will debuff you. Cold resist gear is usually high and easy to find, magic resist is a bit more hard. Also, keep it half between god like mana / stats and god like resist. 200 resist and 100 wis isnt so good, but 150 150 is way better (imo). A feared caster / meleer is of no use. So get resist magic high.

Vox Looting Policy when DB is host:
Every member must give a /tell to the Leader before the Vox killing of what item they wish to roll for should Vox drop it. All members may only pick one item. Should item not be dropped by Vox, then they don't get a chance to roll for it.

Any item that is dropped that was not chosen by anyone, shall be rolled for by all those that can use and need.

All the people that can roll for the item will roll random 0 200, highest to 200 wins.
