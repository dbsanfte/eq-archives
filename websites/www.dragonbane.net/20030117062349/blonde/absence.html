Absence

After dealing with the holidays and another birthday I haven’t felt much like writing. It was a sad year and I am relieved it is past We updated our severely ancient computer (which was a tech-challenged drama unto itself) and I guess I just got sick of messing with the damn thing. All our games work now but I needed a break from this tiny smoke filled study.

I dislike being ignorant on a subject and will usually try to learn as much as I can so not be AS ignorant. Unfortunately computers go beyond my tiny intellect. I took classes, barely passed, and cried in frustration most of the time. I do not have a head for computers. Let me put it this way, attaching a picture to an email successfully is a big accomplishment for me. Sad isn’t it?

Anyway after a month and a half of online absence I have torn up my kitchen and have plans on the works for both bathrooms. The people who lived here before me and mine were an older couple. Apparently they were as wallpaper challenged as I am computer challenged  because the kitchen walls have been bubbling up for the last 5 years (not to mention being the absolute ugliest design). This past month I lost all control and stripped it off with a vengeance. Now as I stare at the mess I’ve made I want to make it great which means dark rich warm colors (migraine friendly colors in other words).  The ceiling is stucco, which is a moronic choice for a kitchen with a ceiling fan. Stucco is a cleaning nightmare. Standing on a chair trying to vacuum the ceiling with obnoxious siamese cats scrambling underfoot is an experience I would like to leave in the past.

I know the desire to game will come back when I am halfway through the kitchen overhaul. That’s usually how it happens when you take a break from something you enjoy. Maybe it was the shock from being able to play all the games the old machine was too puny to handle. I was able to play Quake for the first time and it was only 10 minutes before I was squealing and screeching from frag overload. It was a huge leap from EQ….lol. I was finally able to log into DAoC as well but I just didn’t feel like being a newbie right at the moment. Larry called my attention to the changed models in EQ which I thought for the most part looked sort of silly except for my cleric. I might play her for a bit to see what else has changed in EQ. Not right way of course. I have a kitchen to conquer.

Stay safe and stay sane J

Cynder
