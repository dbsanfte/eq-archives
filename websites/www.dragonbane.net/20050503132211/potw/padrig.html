Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

PADRIG
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Padrig of Daoc (retired light mentalist) and Sandtooth in Horizons (outfitter/chaos warrior/tinker).
Real life a 43 year old male, who is currently designing, testing, supporting a website to do administration of executive benefit plans (yawn). In past years I have also been a headhunter, financial planner, college administrator, financial reporter, futures trader, bicycle repairman,  plastics factory floorboy, and political campaign organizer.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live outside of Boston in a duplex I bought a couple years ago, with my wife of 11 years and 2 boys. I met my wife in Osaka Japan where we both lived for many years. She is from the southern Philippines originally. The guy playing Odysseus now in Daoc is my oldest son and my youngest son we pretend not to know if he is playing online.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Emasculated suburban prisoner

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Fresh broiled bluefish with lemon, Maine baked potato, garden salad without anything in it a 5 year old can’t name. An ale (Z street Rollstone Red). No dessert except maybe an Islay scotch.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Straight from my kids:  wise,ass,old

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Blues although recently the unwelcome drone of bad pop song renditions on the karaoke machine seem to be more common.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
Coonhound named Spot, everyone should have one. Oh, and a lizard of some sort.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
It is ongoing, coffee and white toast so far.

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
People who don’t stop and help in an emergency. My mind cannot comprehend such lack of civility.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Ok in Boston that would buy a house in a good suburb and not much more. I would open a restraunt/bar, open a day spa for my wife, and start an elementary school in the Philippines.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
I like George Soros, who is a financial market speculator and philantropist. He basically steals money from the rich and gives it to the poor, in a legal way.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I guess I would like perfect eyesight so I didn’t have to wear glasses/contacts.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Mr. Roger’s Neighborhood

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
I prefer to walk.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Short tempered

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
A couple people helped me get along in Japan when I couldn’t speak any Japanese and during a college summer a guy gave me a job and a place to live when I was broke and homeless.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
On top of a mountain in New Hampshire at sunset in foliage season with the temperature just cool enough so you need to snuggle up a bit.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Something like Jack Lemmon in China Syndrome or Gregory Peck in To Kill a Mockingbird.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now?
No.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Celebrate the differences between us.
