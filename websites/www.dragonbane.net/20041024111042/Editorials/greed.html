GREED

Hi all. My apologies for being so tardy with something new here. There was much confusion (and effort from Pantera) getting the site moved. And during that period, I kind of lost the fire. Well, now that the site is up and accessible again, let's relight the torch!

I want to talk about greed. Now there's many different things people are greedy about. There's been much discussed and debated about looting policies, but that's not really what I want to discuss. Specifically now I'm going to discuss greed for experience rather than the more common loot greed.

I'm going to spill the beans about something that's really not much of a secret anymore. There's a severe flaw in the formula EQ uses to determine group aggregate damage and exp award. In situations involving ubermobs such as encountered on the planes, the system used by EQ fails. I'm not going to go into formulas and tables and mathematics. That's boring. But in instances
of mobs with 10,000+ hit points, it would seem that the formula EQ uses effectively just ignores most of those hit points, that the exp award goes to whoever does the most damage in the final 2,000-3,000 hitpoints or so.

A lone wizard can, with a well-timed ice comet, KS a mob that it took 4 groups of people beating on. Not only CAN a lone wizard do this, they DO do this. And brag and cheer about it. In the planes it's very common for a wizard or 2 to be alone or grouped together, depending upon the number of people present. Lo and behold, it's astounding how often that wizard or pair of wizards get the kill and exp for a mob.

Now granted, any group with a wizard could do the same thing, you say. True, true. However, most people just fight to get the mob killed. Then there are a small number who brag about being the "KS group" that sit on their hands while tanks melee, clerics heal, etc. 20-some other people actively fight this critter until this cute wizard judges it's sufficiently crippled for his well-timed spell to finish it off.

In a day on the planes recently, we had 33 people present. Five groups of six, and one of three (the self-proclaimed "KS group"). Would it surprise you to learn that that group of 3 got the exp/loot right on about 2/3 of the mobs? They thought it was a great joke. Thirty other people weren't laughing. Thirty other people were meleeing, healing, and yes, dying, so that these three greedy jerks could laugh and giggle and call themselves the KS group. 30 people fighting, dying, and losing exp to powerlevel 3 people who thought it was a great joke and big fun to steal as much exp from everyone else as they could.

That's just sick and wrong, folks. It's sad that the game's programming lets this happen. Sadder still that people actively seek to exploit it. Even sadder yet that they brag about it even after people have complained. I urge people to not let this happen. Raid leaders, stop allowing wizards to solo. Everyone, file feedback reports to EQ demanding that this formula be fixed to reflect the REAL group aggregate damage done. It can sure ruin a lot of peoples' days to endure a 12 hour day on the planes, dying and losing exp while a very tiny majority publicly brags about how much exp THEY are making. Greedy bastards. (And then some of these people left in a huff because they felt they'd been shafted on loot...THAT was funny)

Stand up, tell these people what you think, and let's try to get this childish, selfish practice done away with.

Tamas
