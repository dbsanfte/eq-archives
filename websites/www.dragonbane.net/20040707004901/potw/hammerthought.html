HAMMERTHOUGHT
 

1)       Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation.

a)       I am known as Hammerthought, Arclight, Firesing and Cadre.

b)       My real name is Timothy Hayes.

c)       I am male, age 38.

d)       I am a Senior Programmer Analyst at a well-known insurance company.

2)       Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?

a)       I live in Omaha, Nebraska, a metro area of about 400,000.

b)       I am married (approaching 10 years), no children, 4 wonderful cats

c)       I have 2 brothers, one sister, all older (yes, I’m the spoiled baby of the family), and a mother still living, all in the Omaha area

3)       List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.

a)       Pleasant

b)       Generous

c)       Impatient

4)      What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?

a)       The Thanksgiving dinner, turkey, potatoes, gravy, pumpkin pie.

5)       List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.

a)       Generous

b)       Moody

c)       Absent-minded

6)       What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)

a)       Classic Rock from the ‘60s ,’70s and ‘80s

7)       Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?

a)       Yes, 4 cats

b)       Midnight – black domestic shorthair

c)       Bailey – brown Tabby/Abyssinian

d)       Merlin – black Manx (no tail, believe it or not)

e)       Duncan – black and white mixed breed

8)       What did you eat for breakfast this morning?

a)       A breakfast sandwich similar to McDonalds Egg McMuffin

9)       What is your #1 pet peeve? (something that really bugs you)

a)       Fellow automobile drivers that seem to believe that the traffic lane that is ending or barricaded is a PASSING LANE!

10)   If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?

a)       Buy gift for wife, jewelry, probably

b)       Pay debts

c)       Take a long trip somewhere

11)  Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?

a)       Stephen Hawking, the physicist.  Nobody would have blamed him if he accomplished nothing after suffering the first effects of his crippling disorder.  However, he has persevered to continue to write and study and research.

12)  If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?

a)       I would be 8 inches taller, I am now 5’ 5”.

13)   When you were little what was your favorite TV show?

a)       SuperFriends, the cartoon.

14)   If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)

a)       Dang, no transporter, huh?  Hmm, it would have to be a helicopter then.

15)   Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)

a)       Bite my nails.

16)   Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.

a)       When I was in junior high/High school, I was often picked on.   One time, a student who I didn’t know personally stuck up for me during one of those times even though he had nothing to gain by doing so.  From then on, I stuck up for myself and I encouraged others in a similar situation to do the same.   I had finally seen that I was picked on because I allowed it.  The actions of that one student improved my quality of life in school.

17)   Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)

a)       Anyplace very high up, like a mountainside with a vista of the valley below.  I’m not too interested in mountain climbing, but I sure would like the view.

18)  If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?

a)       A movie version of Thomas Covenant, The Unbeliever trilogies.   It would have to be a series of movies because the subject is too epic for a single movie.  I would be either Covenant or his giant friend Saltheart Foamfollower.  I believe this series to be much more epic and intricate than the Lord of the Rings series.

19)   Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)

a)       Yep and it wouldn’t be home without it.  It would be a hotel J

20)  If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?

a)       You are a child of the universe, no less than the trees and the stars; you have a right to be here. It is a line from the Desiderata, a poem by Max Ehrmann
