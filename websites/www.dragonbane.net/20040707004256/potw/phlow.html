Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

PHLOW aka VONHINTEN
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Phlow/Vonhinten
Jaymie Blanchard (Jay)
Single Male (24)
Software Developer

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in the state capital of Florida, Tallahassee.  I inflict myself upon several other software developers here at my office.  They’re a great group of guys.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Honest, Lazy, Perfectionist.        

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Oh man, this is a tough one… My taste changes so often, and there’s so much good food out there.
I would have a couple 12oz Filet Mignon’s (medium rare), creamy mashed potatoes and gravy, and a nice garden salad with ranch dressing.  For dessert, a large piece of Derby Pie (found while living in Kentucky)

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Anal, Lazy, Antisocial.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
I love most jam-band type music. The kind of music you’d expect a group of smelly hippies to be dancing wildly too. The likes of Phish, The String Cheese Incident, The Grateful Dead, Keller Williams, Bob Marley

<Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
I have the greatest buddy on the face of the planet.  A bulldog/hound dog mix, named Luther. I love him like a son.  Got him at the animal shelter when he was 2 weeks old.  Out of the whole litter of mostly white bulldog-pug-faced puppies, he was the only blonde one with no-pug face, so I took him.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
I didn’t eat breakfast today, but lunch consisted of a bottle of Coca-cola, and a Twix.  Go nutrition!

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
Horrible people (Liars/Cheaters/Thieves/Telemarketers). These people aren’t worth the space they occupy.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Call an accountant, and figure out a way to NOT have to give 50% of it in TAXES.

Take an extended vacation from work so I could spend a little time with my mutt, and upgrade my lifestyle.

Give some money to my brother (without letting the rest of the family know).  He works as an auto parts dealer in my home town, Oshawa, Ontario… works too much, and doesn’t make enough money.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
I don’t really have a good answer to this. I guess I’ll say Wayne Gretzky, for playing Hockey like no other, with pure skill, at a time when most sports have regressed into “who’s bigger” and “who can hit harder”.  (Oh yeah, and he’s from Brampton, Ontario… go Canada!)

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I’m fairly short, fairly overweight, and generally not handsome… fixing one of them isn’t gonna help much :)  I’m 5’7”, I wouldn’t mind being 6’, or even just 5’10”.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Reruns of Get Smart. Old show, but I liked it a lot.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
Car works for me.  I’d have a high end luxury vehicle if I could.  Something like an Infiniti Q45, or a Mercedes-Benz S-Class.  I drive a sporty Acura RSX Type-S and I enjoy it a lot, but if I could afford a luxury car, I’d have one.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
My body seems to want to be on a 36 hour day.  So I’m either too tired, or late for work.  I’m lucky enough to have a job that doesn’t mind if I come in at 11 or 12, so it works out ok (except that I get home from work late).

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
The nicest thing someone (some people actually) have ever done for me was my parents giving birth to me and raising me in a great family.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
A beautiful, secluded white sand beach, with light aqua colored water and slow rolling waves, palm trees, a cool warm breeze and enough cloud-cover to keep the sun from ruining it all. The day I live with that, is the day I’ve achieved my goals.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Any movie playing the leading man opposite of Ashley Judd.  Mmmmm…

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
A few are soaking. Last week I’d have been embarrassed to tell how many dishes were in my sink…

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
To steal a line from Abe Lincoln in Bill & Ted’s:
Be good to each other, and… Party On Dude!
