VALKARIAN

Please list your game-name, real life name, gender, age, occupation.
Game Name: Valkarian; RL Name: Jim; Gender: Male; Age: 36
Occupation: Communications Officer (Major) United States Marine Corps

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in Valrico, Florida while I am stationed at United States Central Command, MacDill AFB.  Only the Marine Corps knows where I will be next – I sure wish the Marines had a base in the Bahamas.  I inflict myself in RL upon my beautiful wife Grace, and our three lovely children Scott (12), Victoria (8), and Katherine (5). In game I inflict myself on Gelrog (RL LITTLE Brother) and Grinder (RL Dad) and anyone else I happen to be grouped with.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Honest, Caring, Friendly

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Shrimp Cocktail, Alaskan King Crab Legs, Cold Beer, and Hot Fudge Sundae

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Stubborn, Serious, Helpful

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Pop or whatever is on the radio when I am driving.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
Maine Coon Cat (Turbo) and 4 Cockatiels (Jarhead, Runt, Beeker, and Cassie – don’t ask)

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Powerbar – I normally skip breakfast.

What is your #1 pet peeve? (something that really bugs you)
People that are rude and unfriendly – there is no excuse for it.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Take my wife on a cruise to Alaska (I might even bring the kids along!), buy a new Jeep, bank the rest for retirement.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
Dad – he was always there when I needed him most – what more can you ask for?

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I wish I was 3 feet tall, skinny, with white hair and a bald spot – hmmmm sound like anyone you might know?

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Gilligan’s Island or the Brady Bunch – boy did I watch some stupid shows.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
A Luxury Yacht to cruise around the world in.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Tell people how to do things (But hey in the Marine Corps that’s my job – just doesn’t transfer over to the household too well…)

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
I really can’t think of any one thing that stands out from all of the many nice things that my family and friends have done for me.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
My perfect place is camping or hiking in the wilderness, with my family by my side.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Outlaw Josey Wales – Josey of course.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
Well, haven’t seen the kitchen sink in about 2 months, but knowing my wife, she is usually meticulous about that so I would guess - no.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Show compassion and kindness to those that you make contact with throughout your life and the world will be a better place for it.
