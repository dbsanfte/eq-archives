Character Creation Tips

1. During creation, it is wise to choose one of the +10stat/+10max stat traits. For example, ‘Fleet of Foot’ which adds +10dex and +10maxDex for 12 points. In game, you will undoubtedly need more than the basic Max, the +10 max for 2 points is cheaper now than down the road.

2. Most classes do not need certain Stats very much, it may be best to take 5 points out of one or even two stats in order to get the extra ”edge” when starting.

3. Make sure to play with the “character kit” option. It does more than just change the clothes your character wears, it also adjusts the weapon you use. Plan ahead, as you will use that weapon for the first 5+ lvls.
