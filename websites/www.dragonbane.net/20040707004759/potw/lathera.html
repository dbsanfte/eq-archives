Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

LATHERA/ZIGGIN
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Lathera/Ziggin, Chris, Male, 25, Security Operations Analyst.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in Arlington, VA (Just outside DC) and usually inflict myself upon members of Dragon’s Bane daily.  Moved to this area and don’t know a soul, so DB members seem to be who I inflict myself upon.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Emotional, Caring, Reliable

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
20oz Medium Rare Prime Rib, steamed veggies or green beans, mash potatoes, sautéed mushrooms, 20oz (or larger) Draft Beer, finished off with ice cream or Tiramisu.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Patient, Dependable, Loving

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
I listen to just about everything. Yes, even country just not the old twangy stuff. I don’t listen to hard rap or heavy metal really though.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
No pets currently.  Just lost my last pet, Dog named Scooter, to Leukemia.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Bagel with Cream Cheese and a left over grape soda (Flat) from last night’s raid. :P

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
People who move to America to live here and can’t speak English so everything in every store needs to be reprinted in Spanish etc…  Annoying! J

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Yellow Hummer2, new house, Start up a Sport Bar business.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
I admire my father the most. He worked hard his whole life to give us as much as he could.  He has put me through college and is currently putting my two other brothers through college. I hope I could do the same for my children someday.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
Be a little fit.  I’ve lost about 32 lbs. And feel great; just a little more should do it. J

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Saved by the bell.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
Helicopter, traffic in the DC area is insane…I think a helicopter would make it much easier.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Bite my nails.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
My father paying for my college. Can’t think of anything that could top that for me.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
Curled up on the couch with someone I care for and watching a movie.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
The Saint.  I’d be Val Kilmer’s part.  Come on…who’s never dreamed you were a professional thief or assassin?

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
Currently a Pot is Soaking so I can clean it when I get off work.   Usually it’s empty…I hate leaving dishes in there for more than a day.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Why are there so many people in this world that hates another country?  Enjoy life and forget the petty differences.
