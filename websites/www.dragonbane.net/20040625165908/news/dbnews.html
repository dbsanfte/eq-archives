For Aspiring Bards|by Jdeirdre
OK, here's something I ran across, it's about what I try to do in rvr so I thought I would pass it along in case any of you up n coming bards wanted some hints:

The bards role in combat:

1. Initial mez. Try to cast it, if you fail in getting one or two off (however many is needed to hit a reasonable amount of enemies) use your instant. Sometimes you'll want to just always insta, i.e. against a good mid group that has all sorts of instas of their own.

2. Get up endurance. If you have two bards you can skip this happy.gif

3. At this point the majority of posts seem to agree that bards are now useless. Totally wrong. There are now two facts, either your are being attacked, or you are not.

If being attacked: good! that means your druids are free to heal, and your casters are free to nuke, which means your probably not going to die and whoever is on you is probably going to die. Kite who ever is on you in front of your nukers range of vision, kite them to your bodyguarders, kite them to your shield slammers, kite them to pbaoe, kite them to your druids line of sight for root! Kite kite kite! Just don't kite them towards a druid who's actively healing or you just screwed your group over. While your kiting enemy tanks toss out your insta mezzes on enemy healers and casters, use your shout and two lulls to interrupt, you are king of the interrupts, use them!

If not being attacked: good! Spam aoe mez, spam it some more, spam spam spam! AOE interrupts win fights. Use your shouts, lulls, and instas where needed, if you have a non-grayed out timer on your shouts and lulls your wasting time, find a caster and healer, lock them down. If your paying attention you can conceivably keep three or more casters/healers interrupted non stop. Throw out a heal when necessary, you mostly shouldn't be healing, your heals are total garbage, even if you have 33 regrowth, still garbage. Druids heal, your job is to keep enemies interrupted and on you so the druids can heal. Rez, druids generally cant stop healing to take the time and rez someone, not to mention the power it will cost them to rez, you and any local wardens are the in combat rezzers. Demez, hot key this. Most importantly keep an eye out for more incoming enemy groups, your the only person in your group (except maybe a nature druid) who can let your group win consecutive fights in a row, stop incoming groups with a super mez and win that VIP trophy.

The only flaw with bards is that if you mess up your entire group dies and they *know* it was your fault. Any one else can mess up, someone can not pay attention and not heal fast enough, or make a dumb target selection, or nuke a near by torc by accident, and whatever, you still might win. But if the bard misses the easy mez, or forgets endurance, or just gets locked down and killed, then the fight is over, you have lost and its entirely your own fault. So the only flaw I can put on bards is that a group is entirely dependent on them, no CC and no end, and no interrupts / extra rezzers / demezzer = death.

Hello this is Skaull and I suck at playing a NS. hehe. Now that that is out of the way I wnated to share my lvl 800 Alchy quest with you all. First off I talked to my trainer. He gave me an item to make. First important thing. The NPCs ALWAYS want lvl 96 or better. Now the materials I needed were not all in TNN. Too high of a lvl for newb capital. So one needs to plan ahead. I needed Carbide metal and had to go to housing twice. Time sink. If you looking to get lvling tools and you want more than one I suggest making batches of items. The quest appears to repeat. I have
done it twice now and same stuff.

So I made the item and gave to trainer. Then I had to make another item and give to girl in MM. She had me make 2 more items and run them to 2 NPC that were in Ardee. Then it was back to MM to talk to girl and then back to my trainer. I receive a lvling item with 19 charges and 8% add to crafting lvling chance. It took roughly 2 hours to do this once.

NOW this is the reason for this email. TO SAVE YOU ALL TIME hopefully. You want to do this quest and thinking you might need more than one item from it planning is key. Make items in batches. Then repeat the quest after you get your crafting item. The second time took roughly 15 minutes on NS speed. Also all 4 quests are given at once by trainer. So you have to be aware of the thread you chose. Me I pick one and when going to the accept box I back off to stop it. then I get spam telling me which quest I
