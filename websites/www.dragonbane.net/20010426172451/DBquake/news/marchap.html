OGL - DB VS CBD
The modem team was up to bat again tonight against CBD. The map played was Shoot the Rapids and was to be a 4 on 4 match. Well there were a few problems right at the get-go as the two team captains had the times mixed up, but both teams were very cooperative and patient and the match was eventually played. Unfortunately for DB we had to play with only 3 players (Jedi, Sav, Spleen) and against a clan like CBD it was going to be tough. It started off pretty slow for the first few minutes , very little action before CBD ran off a few caps. Spleen snagged a cap about
midway through after having to chase the enemy f/c back to their base, Spleen escaped back to the home base with 3 health left with a man
chasing him, luckily for DB, Sav killed the enemy f/c just as Spleen made it back to the flag, a real lucky thing for DB. Jedi played valiantly as
the only offensive man, he was forced to hit the enemy base alone with no quad control, he grabbed a cap also midway through the match. DB
played a good game but CBD was too much at the end. Thanks to Jedi, Sav and Spleen for the great game and a great effort despite being
outnumbered. Great game goes out to Jedi for a good game on offense despite the lack of backup and to Sav who played extremely well in the
roamer/ defense position. We would also like to say great game to CBD as they played a great game and showed good sportsmanship in their victory. They were patient and waited for us to get everything together.Thanks CBD. A possible scrimmage is being setup with CBD, so that should help both teams with some practice. Good luck to the modem team for the upcoming match next week.also good luck to CBD in the ladder, they are a highly skilled team with great teamwork and should do well in the OGL. - [DB]Spleen

April 16, 1999

OGL - DB VS BEAVIS AND BUTTHEAD
The 2 man lmctf modem team had a match last night against Beavis and Butthead (aka Zippy and Om3n) in the ogl ladder. The match was best of 3 maps with Meltdown being the first map. It started off nicely for DB with Sav snagging 2 quick caps and Spleen playing solid defense. Spleen grabbed a cap a bit later to secure a 3 cap lead, which held up throughout the rest of the map. Flag carriers for both teams did a fine job of keeping the flag (Sav for DB) for the last 10 minutes of the map. The map ended up with a 3-0 victory for DB. The second map (Bloodbath) was decidedly tougher with with the lone cap going to DB, Sav snagged one early on. Once again it was a flag holding contest with both Flag carriers holding the flag (Spleen for DB) for the last 12 minutes of the match. The 2nd match ended in a close 1-0 win for DB. Great thanks go out to Zippy and Om3n for the great match and for providing the server, also Afterlife of =V= for the server and for reffing the match. Sav had severe connection problems on the second match and Zippy and Om3n were nice enough to allow us to pause the game while he got them straightened out. We really enjoyed playing with them and hopefully will see them again on the pubs. Everyone were good sports, and we wish them the best of luck. Congrats go out to Sav and Spleen for their first OGL victory and hopefully more are soon to come! - [DB]Spleen

OGL - DB VS QD
Well, The LMCTF modem team had an ogl match on Tuesday night against Quake Dogz. The map played was Enchanted Kingdom, Quake Dogz had the day while DB drowned in a sea of their own vomit. The solution to this is to practice as a team, but that always seems to be the case in our losses. Hopefully soon DB will be able to get things together and put together a good match soon. We will of course remain active in the ogl and put our best efforts to the match. The team has lost Omenborn, as he is on the inactive roster at this time, but have picked up a skilled player in Jedi. On a more positive note I'm looking forward to our next match and I'm sure it will be a good one. - [DB]Spleen
