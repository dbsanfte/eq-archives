October 26, 2000
Lucan Raid for Ishan | by Evoc

Tonight, the brave guild known to the Quellious server as Dragon's Bane faced the Wrath of Sir Lucan and braved the horrors deep within Mistmoore Castle! We started out the evening by helping Ishan slay Sir Lucan for his Soulfire quest. After the first pull in which Sir Lucan failed to race to his doom, we tried a second time and sir Lucan fell before us! There were no causalities on the raid except for the fair Silanea who was slain by the guards on the first pull due to one keystroke that she did not mean to make =( Our raid upon Lucan was successful, as Ishan got the component he required for his quest! After helping Ishan acquire Sir Lucan's Blade many of us turned our sights on Mistmoore Castle. Upon arriving we quickly dealt with the evil beings of lesser power which blocked out way to our goal, the castle itself. Upon reaching the castle we stormed in full force, felling any that dared to stand before us. Upon reaching the Bedroom, (and restraining Ladwenya from pursuing other interests ;), we continued to destroy all the evil that inhabited the castle. Alas, it looked like the raid would be over before it started, as our brave leader and many of our party were forced to leave. Thankfully, reinforcements were on the way, and the raid continued without Cmitchel and the rest!
The small band of adventurers, consisting of myself, Tammuzz, Blazz, Fistgar, Omen, and Synsear, fought our way through hoards of foul demons until we at last reached the Ball Room. Upon reaching this accursed place we cleared it of all its bloodthirsty inhabitants, and continued our assault upon the minions of Mayong! Alas, our time grew short, and
the evil grew strong, and in the end we decided that discretion was the better part of valor and Evaced. The only person to die during the raid was poor Synsear, who death visited twice. Luckily, the noble if somewhat femininely dressed, cleric Fistgar was there to Rezz her both times. The raid upon Mistmoore was also a success! Not only did we
vanquish the evil within, but we also acquired 4 treasures, including a Werewolf Talon, a Gypsy Lute, a Dimondine Earring, and a pair of Crested Spaulders!

/ooc In short, both raids were a great success and a lot of fun. For those of you who did not attend you missed a wild ride =)

October 1, 2000

Capture the Flag!
Amazing time was had at our 3rd Capture the Flag EQ style DB vs LH. Everybody had fun and a lot of laughter and chuckling was heard resonating throughout.

Special thanks to Omentilan for being Captain of the DB team, and to Vorpax for being Captain of the LH team. As well, thanks to Ellise for being the DB Referee and to Meeks for being the LH Referee.

From Ellise:

"I can tell you some about last night. I played the ref for our side. DB had the East Commons Plague Village base and LH had the North Ro Platform. Meeks was the ref for lh. It was a roughstart, because there were many LD because of router trouble. Teeth was the first on our side to get a flag from me, using his travel song. Made him invis and faster then anyone. We also learned over time that he couldn't be rooted, which was a big plus for us! Kobes got the flag first for the LH side. For the first part of the game, the teams were very even, I believe as each side was feeling out how to do the best strategy. Score was 4 to 3 in favor of DB when game was paused for LDs. When we returned, the flags had not all been gotten by teeth...Kalene and Tanithelas had gotten flags also by then. So we were ahead not only because of teeths song....but it was an awesome weapon!

We started again and DB had used the down time to plan. Teeth again was there for flag. He poked and flag given before LH even knew he was around. As he ran through the tunnel, DB had members somewhere near me at the nro base invis. As soon as flag in was called, I was poked again. Ishan, Brutalone, Tantithelas, Kalene were all people to get flags over time. As I sat at the base , they (LH hehe) discussed ways to stop Teeth with his song. During this time, we racked up the points....it was a wonderful stratigy that worked because all of the guild communicated with each other and worked together. LH had trouble getting a person even to their referee for a flag. LH was killed prior to getting there or just stopped. My favorite flag grab was made by Blazz! It was one of the highlights. Blazz made himself into skellie form and lay right next to me on the platform. You could hear LH shouting who was
in zone from DB, but they couldnt find him. Blazz told me where he was, and I told him to wait till flag in was called. As soon as it was called , Blazz stood up and poked me and I handed him a flag, with about 4 or 5 "guards" from LH around me. Blazz was off long before Kobes and others knew he was there. They even insisted on a scroll back to see if he poked me. Troubles we had were when members were waiting for flag turn in, they would be seen and attacked, but this was minimal. In fact, after the game was over, and suggestions were made, it was said that we needed to promote more fighting next time. We continued to get points. DB worked together so well! Flag carriers told in guild when they were close to Meeks and the next carrier would move close to me. It went like clockwork....like we had practiced this routine for days. At 13 6 favor DB, LH surrendered.

We gathered in the tunnel...both teams....for a group pic and to talk about future sugestions for CTF. Everyone had a great time and had many thoughts for next time. Some wanted to do a dungeon...ACK. But the one thought all liked was next time no buffs or bard speed song at all. Most, as I said before, wanted to promote more fighting and less just running."

From Blazz:

Behind two other players I dropped my levitation and just sat on the ground in skelly form like I was a pile of bones - it was too funny because they had one of their druids saying "Blazz is on track!!" and they all came close to the base to protect it waiting for me to appear but I was sitting there the whole time! Finally I poked Ellise, got the flag and took off running! It was such a great feat for me and I was laughing my butt off. I made it clear back to our base and with the flag - and make a cap!"
