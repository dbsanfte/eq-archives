Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

THAPTIN
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
I play Thaptin mostly but also have Thap, Blewit, and Blackmere. My name is Travis, I’m a he and 31 years of age. Right now I’m a Technical Illustrator. I should say what that is because when I tell people that the response is almost always, “That’s cool. What does that mean?” I draw images for Air Force part and repair manuals. Exciting, no?

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in Sacramento, California and I live with my seven year old daughter, my fiancé (who plays Kahlaan) and my ex-wife. It’s not as weird as it sounds. I swear. My fiancé and my ex are best friends and when she needed a place it seemed like a good arrangement.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Three words! That’s not fair. Hmm… How about funny, lazy and daddy. Yeah, those’ll do.

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Kah makes these kick ass vegetarian tacos that are to die for. I’m not a vegetarian but they are so good. For dessert, just some milk and cookies. Yum, cookies.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
I had to go ask a co-worker. She said, multifaceted, astute and personable. Gosh.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
I like a lot of different music. But if I hade to pick one I listen to more then others it would be folk-rock. Love folk.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
Yup a dog, two cats and a bunch of fish. The dog is Oscar. The cats are Buster and Katie. The fish I call, “Who wants to be flushed next?” The fish aren’t mine.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
I don’t usually. I should but I just don’t.

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
There are so many. Heh. Lying I guess. I can’t stand people who lie.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Quit my job, pay off all my bills and then go back to school.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
It took me a really long time to come up with this answer. I would have to say Benjamin Franklin. He lived his life fully and with great service to himself and his fellow man. He was a successful business man, politician and philosopher. He invented, thought, taught and learned his whole life. Sounds like a life worth living and I suppose that we know so much about his life still is a testament to that. Besides, how can you not love a guy who said "Any fool can criticize, condemn and complain and most fools do."

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
Easy, I would lose weight. I’m a big guy.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
When I was really little it was re-runs of Howdy Doody but later it became GI Joe.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
I dunno. Maybe anything someone else can drive me around in. Traffic sucks. A limo.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Sing. It really bugs some people and I’ve cut down on it but I have a bad habit of singing to myself.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
One time I had to ask for a loan from my grandfather. It was tough because I just didn’t want to put him in that situation and I didn’t like admitting that I was having a tough time financially. When I did he sent me the money and a nice letter telling me how he didn’t consider it a loan but a gift and that he appreciated that I had the strength to turn to him when I was in need. It made a situation that was could have been awkward a nice bonding moment for my grandfather and me.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
Sometimes when we go to bed, Kah lies her head on my chest and put my arm around her and we just lay there and talk. That is my perfect place.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
You ask hard questions. I’ll go with “Somewhere in Time” and I’d be Richard. Time travel AND you get to make out with Jane Seymour. How could that be bad? Oh, right the dying thing.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now?
Maybe one or two. There are usually.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
I know what you’re doing and your mother would be appalled.
