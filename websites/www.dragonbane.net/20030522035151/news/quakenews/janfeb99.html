FAREWELL
DB says goodbye to Omenborn. He wrote:

"Well, after thinking about it for the last couple of weeks, I have
come to a hard decision. I have decided to leave DB for awhile. Even
though I think that DB has really pulled together in the last month, I
think it's time I leave for awhile to do other things. To be honest,
Quake was taking up WAY too much of my time and I want to try and get
my life back on track a bit. Don't get me wrong, I truly enjoyed
playing with DB and it has been a great experience. I will definately be back when Quake 3 comes out and possible even DN4ever. But for now I must say goodbye. It's been a real pleasure." Omenborn
"P.S. I will still be online once in awhile, you can bet on that. :)"

DB wishes Omenborn the best of luck and will stay in touch.

January 15, 1999

VICTORY FOR 2 ON 2 OGL MODEM TEAM!
For those of you who don't know, Dragon's Bane has a 2vs2 modem LMCTF team entered into the OGL. Team members are [DB]Ho-Cake and [DB]Omenborn. We had a match last night against Clan Valhalla, and we came away the victors.

To start things off though, we had several problems, which included
finding a server and then getting a server to work properly. Special
thanks go out to [DB]Gonebad for helping to track down (with his many
contacts) some servers to play on. Extra special thanks go out to
s[POK]Babe (hope I got it right), because she admined the games for us, and for dealing with all the problems we had setting up the match and finally getting it going. Thanks for your patience.

Well now, on to the match which started 1 1/2 hours later :). In 2vs2, the match is a best out 3 affair. So the first map consisted of Wargrounds (lmctf20). Ho-Cake played well on O, capturing the flag 4 times, with some stellar attacks that I don't remember seeing him do before. Must be because I was away for a while. I kept the enemy at bay with my D, allowing only 1 cap for the opposing team while we got 7. Needless to say, we won the map, with style for once. None of this nail bitting crap :).

The second match was played on Battle of Little Flag Run (lmctf14).
Ho-Cake started bitching at me too stay on D near the beginning,
because I would grab the blue flag. What he didn't know was that I would have stayed on D, but the enemy flag carrier was leaving our base, so I
had to chase 'em down all the way back to their base. Strange that he would say something that. Figured I might as well pick up their flag while I was there ::shrug:: :) . Strange that he would say something like that. This game was a bit closer at the beginning when we had 2 caps a piece. But then with Ho's great offense and my D, we managed to pull ahead and stay there.

Thanks go out to Zippy and Lady Die from Valhalla for the match and
their patience when we were trying to get it going. They played a great
match and it was a pleasure playing against them. Very polite and 2 great
people.

The next match the 2 vs 2 team plays will be against MasterKillers [MK]. No date or time has been set as of yet. Stay tuned for more details. -
[DB]Omenborn
