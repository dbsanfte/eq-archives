February 23, 2000
Permafrost
Last night Dragon's Bane along with members of Legendary Heroes, Bootleggers and Peace Conclave raided Permafrost and concentrated on ice giants, diplomats, priests and the king. Lots of players showed up and it was great to see all levels there! At times we battled several at once and did have a few casualties. However, it was those dreaded traps that collected the worst casualties and the occasional "summoning by Vox" didn't help much either! (Then we had a couple players getting stuck in the gate! Thanks to Inka for rescuing them.) And special thanks to Bramdar Teken'Duis, the necro who came to our aid to help summon corpses! But all in all, it was fun again just to hunt together in such a large group and the spirit of camaraderie was alive and well!

Goodbye to Brigan
One of our members, Brigan is leaving the guild. He contacted us saying:

"Hey CM & fellow DB'ers,
Due to new job in rl, I am sorry to say I am going to be quitting EQ, and with that DB also. Lately I have not been around (because of job), and have found I also that I have successfully weened myself from EQ altogether. If this is bad or good I do not know, I used to love playing, but then again, sometimes it was all I was thinking about. I didnt think I was that much of a loser (hehe). The few of you I really got to know I am glad I did. DB and the friends I made worth the game play all that much more. You might see me around the next few days and probley at the bazarr, Im trading for things my friends want. Boy when someone quits, everyone gets grabby for items. Well, hopefully I see you all the next few times I am on, and feel free to give me a tell. Also, CM, I will remove my tag the day I quit, so I can listen to all the chatter on the guildsay. See you all later, well probley not. - Brigan Darkbear"

We wish him the best of luck and he will be missed.

February 2, 2000
Dragon's Bane conquers Efreeti
Last night's Guild event was a DB raid on Efreeti. After battling the spiders and lava duct crawlers, the imps were next. During the night, two boots were dropped and Mato and Karre were the successful "rollers." It was a very well organized, co-operative raid, and showed us, that as a guild, we can conquer anything!
