June 26, 2001| Non-Quake Activities | by Sandman
All Lan Party attendees;
The Lan Party is being held in the St.Lawrence River Valley, located in foothills of the Adirondack Mountains of NY. Anyone driving up from the NY City way up I-87 will be reminded of this has they spend about three hours of their trip driving into the Adirondack Mountains on one side, straight through the middle of them, and out the opposite side to get to party. At the same time, if we should desire to take an afternoon break on one day, from our three-day, all day, all night gaming regimen....... then we could take a short road trip to one of two nature splenders of the region. There are two fantastic places to see that are each about 1 1/2 hours from where Bane and I live. We can surely make it to at least one of them, of your choice, during your stay.
One is Whiteface Mountain in Lake Placid. Lake Placid was the home of the 1980 Winter Olympics, and is a really neat place. Whiteface is just outside of Lake Placid, and is the second highest peak in NY, It was the location of all the ski events of the Olympics. Whiteface offers gondola rides to one of the lower summits for $10/person. A less expensive, and possibly more breathtaking alternative is the toll road which is $4/person for access, and you drive part way up the mountain, and then take an elevator up the inside of the mountain to the summit of the highest peak. You can check out the website at Whiteface.com The other trip is to Boldt Castle in the Thousand Islands Region of the St. Lawrence River, just off shore from Alexandria Bay. Boldt Castle is an impressive, huge, 7 story castle on a small island, and has an impressive history to it. Admission to the castle is $3.75. There is a 2hr 15min boat tour around the islands and stops at Boldt Castle Island for $12.50. A less expensive alternative is a shuttle boat to the
castle's island for $6.75. You can check out the website at Alexandria Bay We can do one trip...... or attempt both....... or do none. So let me know what you think about these short road trips........ and which one you would like to see (if we only do one). Bane and I have seen them, so they don't matter to us. Domocracy with rule, and nobody will be left behind. It'll be a Dragons Bane group thing, if we decide to take in the sights. So if you are attending the party, then cast your votes.

"As Sandman has mentioned, 'we have many splendors available to us here in the St. Lawrence Valley located in Northern New York' or something like that. :-P One of my favorite things to do up here in the summer is a local attraction that is available only to those who know of its location - Allens Falls. I don't know who Allen was, so don't ask. Anyway, it's a local favorite with college students and some of us "Townies" who like to enjoy it in the summer. It's a waterfall on a large granite outcropping with a deep swimming hole at the foot of it. It's easily accessible (10 minutes away) and is a nice quiet place to relax and cool off after fragging (or before). During the summer I usually have the place to myself for the most part and I take advantage of it every chance I get. Just thought I'd throw it out there along with the other major attractions Sand has mentioned. I've attached some pics for everyone to see - I'm not a photographer, so be kind. :-)"
