Fear Looting Policy when DB is host:
Each day will be a new beginning.

Once pulling of creatures has been going on for half hour all of the people in Fear are to be considered Original
Members to the Raid.

If new people show up after this time they are considered wanderers. The first time when their class specific
armor/weapons are dropped these people will not be able to roll for that item, but will be considered original members
for the next drop.

For items that are non class specific such as precious stones all people in Fear may roll for them.

If an item drops, the only people that can roll are the "original members" that can use and dont have the item. The
person may roll for each piece that is dropped.

If at the time there is only 1 person that can use and needs the item the person will obtain the item by default.

If a person is to camp out and return 2hrs later they will be considered again as a wanderer and abide by the rules
above.

Rolling for loot will be done as follows:

There will be no penalities given out if you are entitled to role for the item.
All the people that can roll for the item will roll random 0 200 then an appointed person will roll the same and the
person closest to the roll will win.
