April 18, 2001
Last Saturday | by Evoc
/roleplay

Gather round me members of Dragon's Bane, and I shall tell you a tale of a strange and forbidding tower, and the brave people who mastered it! Upon traveling the strange lands of Vellious I came across a black tower rising out of the newly fallen white snow. Upon exploring the tower somewhat I knew that I would need help to defeat the evil that lurked within, so I called forth aid from my fellow guild. Upon entering the tower we quickly dispatched of the inhabitants of the lower floors, using the magic mirrors to transport ourselves ever upwards. Upon reaching the 6th floor we were at last met my some resistance from a genie by the name of Vhal'Sera. However, he was no match for our combined might, and we destroyed him with ease. Upon reaching the 7th floor, we began to depopulate the evil denizens that resided there, making our way to a huge spiraling staircase. Behind a secret panel in the wall, hidden by a large tapestry, there lurked 4 evil bats, all of which we knew would turn into vampires once killed. Upon killing the first bat, out popped Tserrina, the mistress of the foul tower. What followed was a great battle, with us coming out the victors in the end. Alas, after awhile
people had to be going, but 4 of us stayed, and continued to wipe out the evil that inhabited the 7th floor!

/roleplay off

Wow, what a great time last night was! I want to send out a huge thank you to all those who attended, even those who came late: Lao, Tanit, Lolan, Carla, Aykin, Gammbit, Daarkwood, Tepaloo, CM, Auuar, Raines, and a guild applicant and great Cleric Shauga. There were no deaths this raid (yay) and I think that it helped us learn to work together as a team, after working out the bugs just a bit. Just as a note, after everyone left myself, Auuar, Lolan, and Shauga stayed to give the bats one more try. We were able to kill Tserrina again, with the cleric going OOM and lolan at about a bubble of health! The following loot was won, in order of the floor we won it on:

5th floor:
Velium Spiked Skull Helm, 10ac 5str 5cha 20mana 5sv all, went to Tanit.
Ceremonial Wedding Sword, 5str 3dex 3agi 3sta 6sv magic, no mele stats but an awsome look, went to Daarkwood.

6th floor:
Vhal'Sera Skull Earring, 2ac 2str 20hps 15mana, is now guild property.
Talisman of Vhal'Sera, 2ac 3int 3wis 4cha, it was NODROP and I'm not sure if anyone took it.

7th floor:
Tserrina's Key, a key which will take you from floor 1 to floor 7, went to Lolan and myself.
Tserrina's Whip, 1hs 7/22 4agi 5str 8dex, effect: Muscle Lock 1, went to Lolan.
Tserrina's Symbol, 3ac 5int 5wis 5cha 40mana, went to Auuar.

Thats it! I had a great time, and I hope you all did too. You you at next weeks Saturday Event!

April 10, 2001
Saturday Event Synopsis! | by Evoc

/roleplay
Harken to me member of DB and I shall tell you a tale of evil, a tale of death, and a tale of wonder, such as you have never heard before! Knowing that much evil lurks in the depths of Howling Stone, and wishing to rid the world of that evil, a group of DBers took it upon themselves to go deep into Kaesora, the lair of Xalgoz, and destroy the fowl creature. For it was known to the members of DB that Xalgoz held part of the key to the zone of Howling Stones. The members of DB and a few friends all met at Karnor's Castle, and headed off for Kaesora. Although due to someone's oversleeping, someone who will remain nameless (Mordi), the raid started a bit late, we still made it to Kaesora with plenty of time to destroy the evil within. Starting from the entrance and working our way down, all was going well until a Barbarian, who will remain nameliss (Mordi), fell down a deep dark pit, into unknown terrors below. Knowing that our friend needed aid, we all jumped down the pit after him, not caring about what dangers may lurk. Lucky for us, the pit was actually a shortcut to where the evil being Xalgoz resided. We set about dipatching his minions while waiting for him to make an appearence. At last he did show up, and we dispatched him with much ease, taking his token to make the keys
that we needed. Basking in the glow of a job well done, we decided to stay and rid Kaesora of more of it's filthy inhabitants. Alas, I had to be off and was not able to stay for the fun.
/roleplay off

Thanks to all the DBers who showed up: Myself, Gammbit, Obfob, Mordi, Zenfinite, Corsenn, CM, Sarnath, and Daarkwood being played by Tan. We were able to take the zone easily, although there were a couple deaths due to a bit of overeagerness =P Before I left the following loot was won: Fangs for the keys went to myself, Corsenn, and Daarkwood. 25% weight reducing 8 slot medium backpacks went to Obfob, CM, Sarnath, Gammbit's friend Doktor, and Mordi's wife(?) Aidience. The Blade of Xalgoz went to Mordi. Thank you all again for coming, was fun, although a bit of overkill.

This week's Saturday Raid is going to be on the TOWER OF FROZEN SHADOW. This is a really cool dungeon for those who have not been there, it's set up really nicely. You need keys to get to each floor, but they are group keys. I have keys to the 6th floor, the tower goes to the 7th. I need to know if people will be coming who have keys, so that we can take more than one group up. A cleric is a really good idea, as CR here can be a bitch since the keys are not soul. This should be a bit harder than last week, but just as fun. There are no really good maps for this zone unfortunately. I would like to start at the same time as last week, 7pm EST/EDT. I hope to see you all there!!

Taking on the Tower! | by Lolan
Last night three brave DB adventures decide to take on the tower and try get a little exp. I was running Ualen, and was grouped with the rangerduo, Aykin, and Carlaeni. Using camo we quickly made it to the forth floor, where things started to fall apart quite quickly. After dispatching of three mobs, we where in the process of looking for a safe place to med when we where attacked by two of the shadow things. Being OOM, and all of us a little low on health, these two critters quickly dispatched of us.

Knowing that Laoghaire was on Aol, I asked her if she could lend a hand getting our corpses back. No sooner
was I done with my request I get a tell from Raines, who logged on just to help us out. The goal was to get Lolan on, who has all the keys and get us to our corpses. In the process of doing this, Raines has organized with Cm to bring Pan on for the rezzes, and Gurunt has also come over from EW to help. We also received many offers from other guild members to help with the recovery.

In the end we where all rezzed and back to causing trouble in no time :) Thanks to all those who came to our rescue, and to all those who offered to help.

Lolan (Level 53 Ranger)
Ualen (Level 49 Druid)
