August 31, 1999
Interesting updates for our Ranger members in Everquest: rangers now get Critical Hits with bows and thrown weapons, and their Parry and Dodge Skill caps have been raised!

Tale about Whimsy added to the Members' Tales section - check it out!

Last night was a success on the EQ chat server. We submitted our charter, etc. to the EQ team for approval. Now we're just awaiting the "approval email," then I'm off to petition the GM during the day for finalization of the guild. It's merely a matter of hours now! Unfortunately though, the name Dragon's Bane nor Dragons Bane wouldn't be accepted, only Dragon Bane was. But, hey - close enough!

As soon as we finalize our guild, we will be starting work on our first "guild event" details to follow shortly.
August 24, 1999
More links have been added to the Links section. We're hoping to have the best compilation of EQ links on the net! Check them out.

A Trades section is being added soon where members can place custom orders with other members for items created by their trade. For example, Ladina will soon be posting a section to take custom jewellry orders with a listing of prices for members and non members of Dragon's Bane. She assures that all DB members will get special discounts on enchanted jewellry!

This Friday, all members will meet on the chat server at 12 am est. All members have been emailed about this meeting - be there!

Members are encouraged to send in custom tales, poems, bios!
August 23, 1999
Check out the new section "EQ Members' Tales" starting off with A Story of Tamas submitted by [DB]Tamas. It's an actual adventure of Tamas' and certainly an entertaining read! Any other members who have such stories/tales and wish to share them, please email them to me and we'll post them.

Update by Ladina in her researching of guild quests - "some of the guilds I have talked to don't do the quest things but they do put aside one night a week to help another complete a quest one member wants to do. Also we can ask GMs for quests I have heard. The ones the GMs put together take alot of time and are pretty dangerous. I heard of one GM quest that spanned from NRo to West Karana to rescue some girl and return her to her mother in NRo with level 50 monk NPC and other NPCs tailing you along the way. But from what I am hearing from other guilds is that our server is still quite young so not much is going on right now except a few trade bazaars...some guilds plan on hosting drunk fests
at certain cities as well and teaming up with other guilds to camp certain areas or duel other guilds. I don't think we'd want to get involved with dueling another guild - it seems to be against the code hehe. But I will hunt down some other guilds on more established Servers like Povar and ask what they do." She'll keep us all updated as she gathers information. Great work, Ladina.

Also Dragon's Bane t-shirts are available! If you're interested in purchasing one, see the link on the menu bar to the right.
