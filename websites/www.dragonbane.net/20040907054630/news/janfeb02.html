February 15, 2002
Murmurs of a troubled heart| by Stormheart
Hello everybody, and to those I have yet to meet. My name is Phu Truong, I am a Dragon Bane member, and play a 30th Level ranger on the MMORP Dark Age of Camelot. To those who play, know me as Stormheart. I come to you all today as person, and the face behind the character.

As of February 8th, 2002 my mother was diagnosed ith colon cancer. The extent of the problem is
looking very serious. On Tuesday, she will undergo surgery to have the cancer removed. During the
analysis of the surgery of that day, they hope to discover if it has spread to the liver. At which point
the results could prove fatal. I shall not dwell on the details nor relate any aspects of technical aspects of modern medicine.  Rather I wish to expulse a subjective view of my meandering thoughts. In other words I just need to let loose. So here it goes,....Fantasy and reality are surfaces of the same mirror. A German philosopher once said "I think therefore I am." So what we perceive or think we perceive is what we make of it. On one side are the truths and facts that are taught and learned through life. On the opposite are the dreams, desires, and hopes that life reflects on us. Mirrors, like life are fragile things. To those who know me best, know that I am a happy soul (with a wry sense of humor).

Blame is a bookmark, not a story to go over and over with. We move on. People are so caught up in so many bookmarks that they can detail their lives by it. I for one do not choose to live as such. I find people are so caught up in the moment that they forget all things have a beginning, middle, and end (Be it games, distances, people, and life in general). Why is life not fair? Because, nobody said it was. Why is the sky blue or grass green for that matter (that was rhetorical). Someone said, "If life tosses you
lemons, you make lemonade." My god, I hate that quote! Life never tosses anything. Life is on grand scale that, we as humans develop religions to try to explain away everything. Do I as a person have a right to attach the whys and wherefores to such and such a thing thats happens to me? Do I say envrioment or where I was born or who I was born from make my life any richer than what I can make for myself? And I quote from a Columbia speech article "The race is long and time is short, in the end there's only yourself."

But I digress, where were we? Ah, yes, lemonade. A wonderful euphemisism isn't it? How often is that
misleading? Self delusion is never a remedy to the exact nature of any problem that life would swing your
way. It's one thing to be positive but to ignore the true horror and tradegy of Life's calamities is like
living in a bubble. We all deal with problems differently and there is no one correct way in doing
anything when it deals with the human psyche. But to ignore anything is doing more damage than seeing
things for what they truly are. Okay, done venting,..thanks.

So, I face this challenge. I am supported by a wonderful family and I will count on my friends. How
well will I be, after this is all said and done, will remain to be seen. My mother lives a ways away from where I am. So I will be taking a small sabbatical from the game a bit.If I don't or haven't replied to any E-mails, I apologize.

So, I leave you with these parting words. Ignore alliance chat, believe in one another, and have fun.
This game is worth no more than the enjoyment that you get out of it. So don't put into it anything more
then what you know wouldn't be fun.

Sincerely
Phu T.
Vangnourt@yahoo.com

DAOC Guild Housing | by Strokke
Yes it is true! Guild housing will soon become a reality. In the new state of the game, Mark Jacobs laid out Mythic's new 6 week plan. (for those of you that are skeptical the last plan was about 3 weeks ago and was a plan for the month, which has already been completed, 1 week early).
http://www.camelotherald.com/more/202.php

I'll pick out the good stuff ;)

Housing
This is arguably the biggest item in the list. Implementing a full housing system that goes beyond what is available in any of today’s games is our number one goal. As such, it is not an easy nor trivial thing. The full implementation will take several months. However, in order to make sure that we get it right we are breaking it up into two distinct stages, the first of which we will implement in this update. Contained in this update will be the following.

5) There will be at least three types of buildings that can be purchased by the player and/or guilds.

6) Each realm will have at least 50 home sites set up for guild and higher level housing. These sites will be pre-selected by the world developers to not only fit in with the landscape but also in not a way to clutter the environment and inhibit gameplay.

7) The homes will contain a variety of features including vaults, guild banners, emblems, tradeskill tools.

Please keep in mind that this is only the first stage of the housing system. The second stage will include new areas that will be designed for the majority of player housing (vacation homes, crafter towns, consignment stops, etc.). While we could wait and put them both in together, by separating the two we will be able to focus on the core system over the next six weeks but also then be able to better gauge the impact on the game from a player’s standpoint as well as the real demand for housing.

1 - pre-designate raid leader / rotate through guild / CMs call
