December 18, 1998

VICTORY FOR DM LMCTF MODEM TEAM IN THE OGL!
Kick-ass news - the modem team wins a victory in OGL. See details below.

Well it wasn't pretty, but we got outta there with a win. Our LMCTF Modem team receives their first victory in our 3rd match against AOD. No caps, heh. Game started out nice, DB gets quad then a few flag returns. Eventually Ho-Cake and Justice grab the flags and the rest was DM. Man my nerves are shot, never had to hold a flag for an entire match, heh. After watching the demo, I got some concrete stuff, One of the AOD guys disconnected with 2 1/2 minutes left in the match (seemed much earlier when we were playing) with the score AOD 58 - DB 53. The final score was 62 - 38 in DB's favor. Official score would be 62-58 (adding 20 points for the d/c AOD). Gotta hand it to AOD, they played a very good match. Hard to say who would have won if the AOD didn't disconnect, but those are the breaks with us modem people. Have to admit, after hearing quite a few unflattering remarks about AOD before the match, I was pleasantly surprised with their behavior. Nice guys and a good clan. Can't believe everything you read kids :0 Like to thank Omenborn, Gonebad, and Eramus for playing, you guys did a helluva job. Also thanks to Sned for playing backup, Nagrom from OGL for being our official, and last but not least Kimberly for giving us a server. OMG a server we all ping decent at!!! There is a Ho-Cake vision demo available, some nice (lucky :0) rail shots in there. And of course a screenshot. GG GUYS!!! - [DB]Ho-Cake

To all of DB's LMCTF team - Congratulations on our win last night. This should prove that we can play when we want to. 8=) This game was one of the most intense games I think I have ever had the privilege of playing in. Even more so than our last match against MK(MastaKillers). From the very beginning it was a GAME. Ho-Cake, Eramus, and myself made a dash for the opposing teams flag, when Eramus and I stopped off for a break at the Quad area. We then decided to hang out there for it seemed to be a nice area to hang out at. Ho-Cake got their flag and never gave it up. WHAT A HEART POUNDER THAT MUST HAVE BEEN. Have you ever tried to hold a single flag for an entire 30 minutes. WOW. One of AOD members dropped in the last couple of minutes so I decided to guard Ho-Cake because of the score. We had gotten the lead several minutes before that and didn't want to give it back to them. Being kinda STINGY don't you think, but hey what the heck we needed to win one for DB and get us all back on track. We succeeded in doing so and won DB's first game in quite awhile. With practice and strategy, along with team communication, I don't see why we can't keep this new found high alive. I just don't know if I can take another close one like that again for awhile. 62(DB) vs 58(AOD) and no caps is pretty rough to handle. Good game to all that participated and hope everyone had a good time. Special thanks go out to OGL-Nagrom for officiating the match and to s[POK]babe (Kimberly) for administering and setting up the server for us. - [DB]GoneBad

The Team Leader and Team Co-Leader's responsibility is to organize their team and to co-ordinate the positions and strategies. Get to know each other and add each other to your icq contact lists.

Each Team Leader and Co-Leader will be mailed a Team Strategy for tonight's practice. They are to get their teams organized in an icq chat online at 9:00 pm est (8:00 pm cst) and to tell their teams the strategy and their positions. Each team has 15 mins to discuss the strategy. Note: there will be no "lone fraggers" in these practices. Above all, this is about teamwork.

Even though all members of the clans are listed above, we realize that there will be no-shows. This happens all the time clan matches, so it's good experience to be prepared for that.

This exercise will give us all good experience in strategy planning, communication, teamwork and following procedures. It will foster closer relations between both clans and will give everyone the experience of working as a team with players you don't know.

Both teams are to meet on the server at 9:15 sharp. There will be 2 maps played, 20 mins each. Each player is expected to stay the full 40 minutes. It will be weapon no stay and friendly fire on.

The 2 maps to be played will be lmctf 19 and lmctf20. The Server IP has not been finalized yet. But when it is, you will be told which server the practice will be on.
