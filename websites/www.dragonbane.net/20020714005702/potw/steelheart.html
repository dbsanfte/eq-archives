Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

STEELHEART
Quake Team

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation.
Steelheart, Mike Roha, Male, 28, IT Contractor.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in Plano, TX and inflict myself upon myself without remorse. :-)

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Smart, funny, caring.

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
There are so many, but I am originally from San Diego, so I enjoy a huge beef and bean burrito from a particular hole in the wall mexican stand,
and uh key lime pie.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Caring, goofy, funny.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Everyone says "all kinds" and I have that too, but mostly I like 80's rock (hence the funny game name) and early 90's grunge/alternative.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
Negative, I love animals, but I know that I wouldn't be able to give the pet the attention (and discipline) it would require.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Pop tarts or cookies are usually my mainstay, along with a soda or cappucino if I have time to stop for one.

What is your #1 pet peeve? (something that really bugs you)
Inconsiderate people, this can be in a face to face way, or a driving way...argh

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
First I would pay off my debt, then go on a nice vacation to somewhere tropical, then come back and buy a house.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
That's a very difficult question, but I would have to say Abraham Lincoln. I think that the world would be a very different place if he would have lived to soothe the aftermath of the Civil War. He was very perceptive of his fellow man, but at the same time worked toward what he thought right
and true.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I'm happy with the looks I was given, but would like to lose some weight (doesn't everyone?).

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Again there were many, but I hardly ever missed Knight Rider.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
A nice SUV would work just fine, with 4 wheel drive and all the trimmings.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Hmm...sometimes I get to talking too much, and I just can't stop!

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
There's no one thing, but I would say a friend being a good friend is the nicest thing.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
My favorite place is someplace in the mountains, camping. Ah.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
I would probably play opposite Jim Carrey in Dumb and Dumber...that would be fun!

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
Not currently, but I don't cook, so there wouldn't be...fast food has one advantage.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Don't worry, be happy!
