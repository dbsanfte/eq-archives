QWAYMELQNU
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
QwayMelqnu, Numkene, Male, 25 or 26 (can never remember), Electronics Assembly (I work at a place that makes circuit boards for aircraft of all kinds, military and commercial)

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
II live in Phoenix Arizona with My wife Erica (Kwil) and my 3 kids: Jessica (6), Nathan (4), and Erin (1).

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Friendly, Logical, Weird (yes Logical and Weird go together, lol)

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Pretty much any kind of Fish Fillet (not fast food though), I like fish; Homemade Mashed Potatoes, and homemade biscuits. Dessert would be Strawberry Ice Cream (the good expensive stuff).

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Friendly, Funny, Weird

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
What is the trick to this question? I've read a lot of these Player of the Week things and I've never seen why it's a trick question. Anyway, I listen to pretty much every kind of music except hardcore rap. Name a genre and I could probably tell you a song I like that fits it. And if I can't name one, I'm sure I could find one.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
No pets cause Apartments charge more to have them. First time in my life I've never had a pet.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Either Chex cereal or Yogurt for weekdays. Pancakes, eggs, bacon, etc. on the Weekends.

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
This is a long subject that includes many many people, but since I don't have the time, I'll just generalize it into one group: Stupid People.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Pay off Bills, Give my family some security (buying a house, car, amenities, save for college etc), Build myself a movie theater (haven't decided if it is for myself or for use as a business)

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
Dunno, I'd have to read up on my history, it would probably be a Native American (I'm half)

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
Probably my skin tone, I'd like darker skin, I'm white as...well..something that's really white.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Probably Transformers, or TMNT.

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
I'd like one of those ramp-escalators (you know, the treadmill-like things) they have at airports. It would be nice to just ride one of those wherever you go.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
I joke around too much, it annoys people sometimes.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
Hate to get sappy here, but I gotta be honest. I'd have to say the nicest thing anyone has done for me is when Kwil (Erica) said yes when I asked her to marry me. They only moments I have ever been happier than that is when my kids were born, and they wouldn't have happened if Kwil didn't say yes. She has single handedly given me all the happiest moments in my life. Hands down the nicest thing anyone has ever done for me.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
Anywhere away from the modern world. Camping comes close. I like nature.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Any of the funny Jim Carrey movies, I'd play the lead. It would be fun to be able to have all the freedom he has with improvising stuff. Course half of my movies would be outtakes.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now?
Nope, I just did them before checking my E-mail.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
"Got a nickel (or your Country's' equivalent) I can have?"
