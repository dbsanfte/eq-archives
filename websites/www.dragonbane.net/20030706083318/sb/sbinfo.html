For recruits that want to join DBSB -
Any fee on joining? e.g 50k gold? NO

Are there periodic donations after joining?
We do not currently have a required contribution percentage or amount. We do ask that members try their best, within reason, to sell loot acquired while hunting to the vendors in our city. Our new city standard payout amount is 9% higher than the safehold rates so this should be good inspiration to do so.

No alternate characters allowed in other guilds?
Correct

Exclusive use of town merchants (is this a requirement?)
If by exclusive you mean that only guild members will have access to them then the answer is NO. Any money generated from buying or selling at our vendors is cash flow for the guild to help maintain the city and upgrade. If by exclusive you mean that guild members are required to utilize only guild merchants - see the section above on donations.

No player killing (yes/no) (Pking) unless justified
What is commonly known as "griefing" - killing, stealing, or purposely reducing the positive experience of another player or group that is not currently KoS to the guild/nation WITHOUT PROVOCATION is unauthorized. This is also a regulation of the nation in which we are a member. Guild members should check with IC officers to obtain the political status with another guild before initiating any aggressive action. In situations where you are not the aggressor and are being attacked, the following guidelines apply:

1.) If you are attacked by a player or group with ERRANT status (no guild or nation affiliation), you should defend yourself appropriately and with honor. Report the names of the offenders immediately to officers of the Inner Council.

2.) If you are attacked by a player or group with ENEMY guild affiliation (that guild or nation is currently KoS to our guild or nation) you should defend yourself with honor and show no mercy.

3.) If you are attacked by a player or group that carries the CREST OF OUR GUILD or NATION, give the offenders a verbal warning to back off and withdraw their attack. If they persist after repeated attempts to communicate your friendly status, defend yourself with honor. It goes without saying that a member of Dragon's Bane should be given extra care in this matter. Any attacks of this type are considered high priority and must be dealt with immediately by officers of the Inner Council. In a situation where another guild in our nation is involved, negotations must take place to determine their status.

Any level requirement?
None currently, though as we increase in numbers we may want to set a minimum level (30 comes to mind) for new members that are not prior DB or alternate characters or RL friends of existing members .

What guild type (Wizard’s Tower, Mercenary Company, Thieves Den, Barbarian Clan, etc.)
We are a Military guild. It is officially known as a "Military Legion"

Any preferred Classes?
No, all are welcome. Classes that receive a bonus to exp and health/stamina/mana regeneration rates in our guild type are: Priests, Rogues, Thieves, Warriors, and Wizards.
