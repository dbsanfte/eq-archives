CLASS EFFECTIVENESS
Survey - Canvassing for Responses!

I sat down and started to type up something about class effectiveness. I trashed it all because it was sounding suspiciously like whining to me! So what I would like to do is solicit a little input from readers, and use their comments to make some comparisons. Now, supposedly, the classes are relatively balanced, but that's just not true, some classes are quite obviously more powerful than others. So let me ask a few questions, and I strongly encourage you to take a moment and drop me a line with your input.

I understand that one of the desires of EQ is to promote group play, but we all know that's just not always possible or desirable. Sometimes you just want to do your own thing, sometimes you're forced to solo because you just can't find a group. So let's see who's best equipped to go it alone versus some of the evils that stalk the land.

Anyhow, let me get to the questions. Ideally, I'm hoping to hear from at least one person of every class. Don't be shy! I'd rather be buried in email than get none. The questions will be what level were you able to solo kill this or that. I'd prefer to know when you could comfortably do so, as opposed to when you were able to barely survive the encounter and walk away with 5 hit points thinking "wow that was TOO close!" Pet using casters, please also mention whether use of your pet was required. Also, I'd like to know what color con the mob was to you at the time. Fill in the form below!
