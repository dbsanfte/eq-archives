December 19, 1999
Guild Event
To bring seasonal cheer to the newbies on the Quellious server, all members of Dragon's Bane will, in the next 2 weeks, be forming "Newbie Hunting Parties."

As this upcoming Saturday is December 25 (Christmas Day) and the next Saturday is January 1st (New Years Day), we won't be holding our regular weekly guild events.

However, each member will be forming a "Newbie Hunting Party." That is, they are travelling to a newbie area, and helping newbies form a group, and you will help them. Showing them tips/tricks, buff/heal if you can, etc. The event should take no longer then 2 hours.

After each hunting party, screenshots and paragraphs will be posted on the site about their success.

Hoping this will be seasons greetings to our newbie groups!

December 6, 1999
Dragon's Bane Christmastide Festival
Our Festival was an outstanding success!! We had over 160 players participating in the festival, with many more observing. Even three GM's stayed around to watch and enjoy the festivities! Lots and lots of prizes were given out (including newbie giveaways) which included: 000's of pp, fine steel, bronze and banded armour, magic items, robes, greater lightstones, and much more! To see the details of what happened at the Festival, click here or go to our Events Link. One event, the Greater Faydark Trivia contest, we have reproduced here on our site. Click here to see the questions and see how many you can answer correctly!

Thanks to all DB members who all organized their own events and ensured the success of the festival, and special thanks to all the players on Quellious that participated and helped us welcome in the Christmastide season to Quellious.

Kedge Keep
A few of our members recently went to Kedge Keep to see what is was like, as they had never been there before. They were Tammuzz, Tamas and Pantera. Well, this is a screenshot taken at zone and apparently there were (though you can't see all the people) 29 people pulling to the zone! All this is conducted underwater!
