What's In a Name?

I have always tried to avoid conversations about religion. I am agnostic although I will tell most people (if I am asked) that I am an atheist just to preempt any “spreading of the word”. I admire someone who is solid in his or her beliefs and tries to live a happy life. I despise those who use those beliefs to manipulate, steal,  feel superior, or torture & murder those of different beliefs. That is not my idea of religion. That is just plain ol’ head games with a self serving twist.

Why is it that religion has caused such intolerance between people? I have friends of all faiths. Well probably not all but I have been to different ceremonies, and seen different practices. Basically they revolve around a good deity and an evil deity. If you worship the good deity then you are the good guys. If you worship the evil deity you are bad. I would have to say that the majority of the people who are religious support the good side yet because someone is of a different faith, even if they believe in the good side too, they are bad. They are not in the right club so their faith doesn’t count? That makes absolutely no sense to me. What astounds me even more is the righteousness of those who commit the most heinous acts in the belief that they are doing their god a favor and fighting the good fight. By hurting others who are deemed non-believers? No, I don’t think so. That is just plain WRONG!

You would think that those who thought that their particular brand of religion was the correct and only faith would be content to sit back and simply smirk at all the other religions knowing that they would be the only ones to get to that divine after-earth-life existence. Instead the hatred is strong enough for regular harassment, threats, beatings, executions, torture and murder by people who believe in a good god.

No religion is exempt from this behavior. The horrors that have happened through out history due to difference of religion are countless. As I grow older I have prided myself at learning from my mistakes. Why can’t the world do the same? Open a history book, read the news, ask your elders. We have a vast plethora of experiences to learn from. In the end who wins the my god-your god battle? No one does. They never have. Doesn’t that say something?

Now before I get a whole bunch of emails defending the various religions please remember I used no specific examples. I do not believe religion is bad only the people who use it to conduct their hate and intolerance. I wish the world would see the beauty in each persons’ belief to bring out the best of themselves. That is what religion is to me.

Stay safe and stay sane J
