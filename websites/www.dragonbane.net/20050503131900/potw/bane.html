Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

BANE
DBQUAKE

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation.
My game name is [DB]Bane.  My real name is Jake Roberts, I’m thirty-two years old and I teach high school English.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in the balmy metropolis of Potsdam, NY.  Some of you remember it from the LAN.  Daily, I inflict myself on my cat, if she’s not too fast for me.  (Just kidding).   Daily?  Myself.  Weekly, I travel across town and have pizza and beer with Sandman and Sandho.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
irreverent, intelligent, loyal

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Hmm, just one?  I already mentioned the pizza and beer thing…..  It would have to be prime rib dinner (medium rare), with big steak fries.  For dessert, chocolate confusion cake.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Depends on whom you ask.   funny, caring, friendly.  (I hope).  J

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Trick question?  Then it would probably be that canned musak stuff they pipe into stores.  On purpose, I listen to classic hard rock and some heavy metal thrown in.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
I have a cat named Rowan. 

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Coffee.  And oh, yeah, a small black fly.  The fly was an accident though.

What is your #1 pet peeve? (something that really bugs you)
Carrot-top… oh, and that condescending way Alex Trebek gives the answers on Jeopardy... and people who have more than one #1 pet peeve. Seriously, I have a tough time when people refuse to take responsibility for their actions or in other words, refuse to see the consequences their actions have on others. 

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
Pay-off bills, buy property, invest

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
The guy or girl who put together that great practical joke “Stonehenge.”  People are STILL talking about that one…  Aside from that, Walt Whitman.  He had the ability to see the sublime in the simple, and present the simple as sublime.  His passion and unconventional approach have left an indelible mark on our culture.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
Well, I just noticed I REALLY need to clean my fingernails.   As far as my overall appearance, I don’t think I would change anything.  I think we should be happy with what we’ve been given and make the most of it.  Besides, it’s only the way we look. 

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Salty Sam’s Super Saturday (Local broadcast out of Syracuse).

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
A HUMMER with a hood-mounted rocket launcher.  Well, sometimes old people make me angry.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
I chew my fingernails.  Except right now.  They’re dirty, remember?  Honestly, I have problems taking things seriously sometimes, especially when others feel I should.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
I would say gave me life, but I think Cyberdragon has already said that on here.  Next to that, I would say, feed me every Sunday evening for the last three years or so.  Thank-you Sandman and Sandho!

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
At the table after dinner during a family holiday or get-together.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Tombstone.  I’d be Doc Holiday.  I’m pretty sure I was him in a former life.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
ROFL.  Yes.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
On the count of three, jump -  One, two, THREE!
