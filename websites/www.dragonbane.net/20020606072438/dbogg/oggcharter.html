Purpose
The primary goal of the guild is to enable and encourage our members to have fun playing various online games with each other, in an environment which is respectful, mature, and friendship-oriented.

Expectations
Specifically regarding our RPG teams, secondary characters are welcomed, though members are forbidden to
join their characters to other guilds on our server. We will not restrict members' affiliations in other games or servers unless such affiliations bring negative attention to us.

Participation
Participation is expected of active members; this includes the website, forums, IRC channel, email, and in-game channels. Members are expected to play together when online when possible. Attendance at formal events and meetings are highly encouraged. No one will be harrassed for choosing not to play, for placing real-life concerns over game-concerns, or for going inactive. Inactive members will not be permitted to vote on guild issues, but they will retain access to all private guild communication centers and will be encouraged to continue voicing their opinions on issues of import.

Activity
A Dragon's Bane member who wishes to go inactive or leave the guild must notify a DB Founder. Founders are cmitchel and Cyberdragon aka Icewolf. Inactive members may reactivate at any time with no penalty. Only ex-members who resign in good standing may be permitted to rejoin at a later date; to apply for reinstatement, a formal petition with explanation must be emailed to cmitchel, who will present it to the membership for discussion.

Future Plans
Members are expected to recognize that while the Group is relatively permanent, the games we play are not. We fully plan to explore multiple games, in the present and the future, and when the founders decide to migrate the guild, some teams may be disbanded. Please do not join or stay if you become so attached to games that you cannot leave should the group founders choose to move on.

Conduct
The following actions are considered unacceptable behaviour: excessive profanity and foul language in public; cheating or exploiting game systems; harrassment; hostility to other members and or innocents; disrespect for the team/group or its leadership; regarding our RPG teams - unjustified or unroleplayed griefplaying (player-killing, thievery, training, looting, corpse-camping, ninja-looting); lying to anyone.

These are the rules of conduct that all members have accepted to play by and live by. It is with these rules that a member may game online with any other member with trust.
1. Have Fun. Our main belief here is to have fun at whatever game you are playing.
2. Discrimination and Harrassment free environment.
3. No cursing in large groups. This rule is to curb anti-social behavior in front of members who don't want to be exposed to such behavior. If you swear be very careful of who may be around you and hearing you.
3. No lying about or undermining of another member. This rule mean exactly that. No member may lie about another member or undermine another member to someone else.
4. How to resolve conflicts with another member. First step is to go to that member and address the problem. Most times that person will apologize and the issue will be resolved. If the problem persists, then take the problem to a Leader who will resolve the conflict.
5. How to resolve an issue with the Team and/or Group. Sometimes there will come a time when a member has a problem with an interpretation of a rule or way of conduct in a game. If you have a problem with or question a standing rule; or you have any doubt regarding an action you want to take...then DO NOT DO IT. Instead contact a Leader immediately and ask. Harrassment Free Environment
Dragon's Bane members are of the utmost importance to DB. As such, every member of DB must be afforded an equal
opportunity to become a productive member of Dragon's Bane. Unprofessional behavior destroys trust and confidence
among leaders and members, erodes DB's cohesion and is contrary to our core values.

Dragon's Bane is committed to ensuring that our environment is free from harrassment and discrimination, that harrassment and discrimination will not be tolerated under any circumstances and that action will be taken against any member who breaches this policy.

Note that harrassment includes, but is not limited to, verbal, sexual, stalking, and racial harrassment. Discimination includes, but is not limited to, age, sexual, gender, religious, political discrimination.

All DB members and other players, are to be treated with dignity, courtesy and respect.

What can one do should they feel they are being harrassed or discriminated against or observes incidences of this type?
- Ask the person to stop, where you feel comfortable. While this may be difficult, it can be an effective way of stopping
the unwelcomed behaviour.
- Contact a Dragon's Bane Team Leader, if the harassment continues, or you don't feel comfortable talking with the
person about their behaviour. They can help you in dealing with the problem in a completely confidential manner.

Transgressions
Members who are accused of transgressions against a team or group will be summoned by the Founders and reprimanded as appropriate in private. Extreme offenses could result in team and/or group removal. Note founder judgement is final. Should the accused refuse his terms, he will be expelled from the group and/or team.

Organization
Dragon's Bane is led by it's two founders, cmitchel and Cyberdragon (aka Icewolf). Additional sub-leaders are appointed as necessary to lead teams, oversee specific Team or Group functions. All leaders are expected to work
together to achieve leadership success; they are expected to uphold standards meticulously, and transgressions by officers are handled more severely.

Suffrage
Day-to-day issues and decisions will be left to the discretions of the acting leadership. Members are encouraged to make suggestions to improve the group and/or team; those who do not participate or do not make their voices heard lose their right to complain. Please utilize the forums and official meetings, or private discussions with the officers, to make your ideas or concerns known.

Recruiting Policy
An interested player should post to the forums expressing his intent, get to know our members in game, read over the website (especially the charter) and finally, secure from within the active membership a Sponsor - a Dragon's Bane member who agrees to serve as a guide through the recruiting period. Then, and only then, the player is invited to fill out the application at the bottom of this document, which will be submitted to the membership for review and voting. If the application is insufficient, further interviews by the or reviews by the general membership will be conducted. If the application is approved, the player will be invited to the group and team as a member, on probation for a period of one month and briefed on his/her immediate duties. Members on probation cannot sponsor a new member until their one month probationary period is completed.

Waivers
Former members/allies in previous games and spouses and family members may, at the Group's discretion, be eligible to waive or reduce these recruiting requirements. Please address any affiliations that may apply to the Founders.
