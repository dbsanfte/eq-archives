April 9, 2000
Capture the Flag - Legendary Heroes vs Dragon's Bane
Last night we played Capture the Flag EQ style against Legendary Heroes. To do this, the guild war function was issued. LH defended their base (wiz ramp in North Ro) and we defended our base (Plague Village in EC). It was a great time, with players trying to break each other's defenses to grab the flag and take it back to their base. There was an excellent turnout by both guilds and an awesome time was had by all.
April 2, 2000
Plane of Hate Day 3
It's Day 3 in Plane of Hate for Dragon's Bane and Legendary Heroes, Bootleggers and Peace Conclave. It's the first time we have raided Hate as a guild. The raid has been going extremely well, however for the most part, only runes and words have been dropping. We plan to continue the raid for another few days and maybe even pull Innorruk just to provide a little more action up there :)
