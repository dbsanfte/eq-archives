"Yes, I'm replying to myself. So I called Mythic and told them that if I didn't get a copy of SI pronto, I'd get Shania on the horn and have her move back to Canada. That didn't really work, but then I told them that she'd never wear the full-length leopard skin outfit from the video 'Don't Impress
