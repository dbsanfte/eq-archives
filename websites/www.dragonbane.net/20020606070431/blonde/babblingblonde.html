Catch-Up

Well I think I survived the kitchen overhaul with very little trauma surprisingly enough. Luckily Larry was there. Without his know-how and muscle this could have turned out badly.

Our kitchen was wallpapered by morons. At least this was what Larry and I kept verbalizing every ten minutes or so while we fought with 3 layers of very ugly heavily glued sheetrock disintegrating wallpaper. The kitchen had to be completely (and I mean completely) plastered before any painting could be done. We were not happy campers. Now that most of it is done (a little trim is still in need of touch ups) it is really nice to be able to actually go in the kitchen for the sole purpose of its original use. Believe it or not take-out does get boring.

I’ve been listening to the news (while plastering, sanding, and painting) but haven’t had much online time. Today I checked out some message boards and one thing kinda struck me as a little odd. Maybe I’ve just been out of the loop to long but it seems like no one really gets that people are dying horribly of very unnatural causes everyday. The posts I browsed through talked about games (which is what they are meant for after all) but all the gamers were so caught up in virtual items or ingame tiffs. I know games are a way of relaxing or escaping but shouldn’t what goes on around us in the really-real world affect our treatment of others in any forum.

It’s funny, me being an anti-social agoraphobic and caring about the world around me (it’s funny to me anyway) because in truth I know the reason I am the way I am is because of the world around me. It just seems that we all hear the news of peoples hardships and care enough to say “Isn’t that awful” but in the next breath start bitching about the guy that cut you off on the freeway or the moron who wallpapered your kitchen to death. My solution has been to recluse myself from the outside. It’s not really a solution but it gives me peace of mind that if I die I will die in my own house with it’s beautiful kitchen, dammit J.

It’s nice to be back and babbling DragonBane now I just need for NWN or Shadowbane to be released.

Stay safe and stay sane J
