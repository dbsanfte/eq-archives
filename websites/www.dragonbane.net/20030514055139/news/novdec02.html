Realm Ability Testing| by Omenborn
Just did some quick tests on test server regarding some realm abilities, thought some people might be interested.

Long Wind testing
Normal sprint: 22secs
Long Wind 1: 26secs
Long Wind 2: 34secs
Long Wind 3: 52secs
Long Wind 4: 1:42secs

With Long Wind 4, starting from the north gate, I could do just over a full lap around the TirNaNog loop.
I didn't have enough points to try Long Wind 5 with the char i was using, but I'll probably try with a different one once I get it copied over.

Tireless

Tireless 1: 1 minuscule tick (maybe 1 or 2% of total endurance) per 8 seconds
Tireless 2: 1 minuscule tick per 7 seconds
Tireless 3: 1 minuscule tick per 6 seconds
Tireless 4: 1 minuscule tick per 5 seconds
Tireless 5: 1 minuscule tick per 4 seconds ?? didn't have enough points to test

I found tireless to be useless. The amount of endurance you get back per tick is waaaaay to small to be usefull. And all increased levels do is decrease the length to the next tick, it does not increase the amount of endurance you get back per tick. Useless.

Managed to test Long Wind 5: 1:42secs.

Identical to Long Wind 4. I suspect it's a bug that Long Wind 4 allows you to run 1:42 as this is a nearly a 100%(50sec) increase over Long Wind 3 which is huge in my opinion.
