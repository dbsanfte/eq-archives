Submitted August 31, 1999 by Pantera. This story is an actual adventure.

Tale of Whimsy
Just the other night, after a night hunting in Mistmore, Darbycrash and I were leaving Mistmore and travelling through Lesser Fay on our way to Greater Fay to camp. Darbycrash was leading over the hills and dales of lesser Fay and I was following and everything seemed peaceful enough. Then all of a sudden Darbycrash is attacked, backstabbed for 40 hp in one hit by the dreaded Whimsy Larktwitter! We both yell "keep running" and zig zag like mad fiends trying to lose her. She continues on and on, alternately backstabbing both of us until we've lost over half our hit points each and we're wondering at this point are we going to be killed. Finally the zone to Greater Fay was in site, and we agreed let's try and kill her or die trying. With trepidation we stopped and turned around, and there she was, the dreaded Whimsy!

We consider her - she's green? A couple of hits later from us to her and she's dead on the ground in front of us? OMG we couldn't believe it! Darbycrash then says "that was really dumb running." I replied, "lol yes I know but it was instinct from past experiences with her that made us run."

It was too funny to have run and almost died from a creature that is "green" to us now. Well we certainly learned our "whimsy lesson."
