November 17, 2000
DB/LH/COA/KC Venril Sathir Raid | by cmitchel
We had been planning the Venril Sathir raid for weeks and unfortunately we had a few incidents prior to when we could actually get a chance at the mob.

It was awesome the support of guilds on the server that came from everywhere to support us in our efforts: Wraith, Shadows of the Unseen, Crush and Swill, Greater Druids, Lotus Cult, Slayers of Slaw and many more! Over 120 people came out to support us (guilded and non-guilded). Unfortunately we had to turn all level 52 and under away just to keep manageable numbers, but thank each and every individual that came out.

Venril Sathir did go down and he dropped Eldar and Donal leggings and 2 pulsing greens. Fistgar won the Donal leggings, Lalian won the Eldar leggings and Runicmyst and Garaddor won the pulsing greens. Congratulations!

From Fistgar:

"Well, last night I became one of the luckiest cleric's on the server to get the first pair of Donal's leggings! Thanks to everyone that showed up and stayed to kill Venril Sathir after the first failed attempt. Too bad Mato didn't get his epic quest item, I really feel bad for ya man :( Anyways, attached is a screenshot of the stats on the leggings. I was on the phone with my g/f during the entire raid and thought that I was being stupid for getting so excited when I won the leggings... bah, what does she know hehe, I'm still pretty stoked!" - Fistgar Soulsaver

November 3, 2000
GM Event | by Corsenn
There was a GM event a couple nights ago in Lake of Ill Omen. I first realized what was going on, when i rounded a corner and came face to face with Lady D and a dragon-like critter that killed me in about 3 seconds. When I went back to get my body, I paid more attention to what was going on and followed the trail of bodies to the FM zone, where it appears several dozen high level characters made a stand against the dragon...and failed. It had an AoE fear and an almost line-of-sight summoning ability and tore apart any who got too close. After it ran out of victims, it headed into the lake and stayed there for a bit, while the brave and the survivors attempted to regroup and organize. It then reemerged from the lake by the FV zone and tore apart another dozen or more characters, including myself (I got caught in the fear aura and lost SoW, becoming the closest person to it, it summoned me and killed me and another character in a few seconds. I gave up after that and headed to bed. The creature is the elusive sea monster that occasionally makes an appearance in the ocean zone beyond FV. The first several screenshots i sent were of the initial slaughter by the FM zone, the actual pictures of the dragon were taken near the FV zone, soon before I died.
