GRINDER
 

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation?
Grinder OfBones 50 Hero. 
Occasionally play Amyo, 50 Enchanter. 
RL Name is Jeff Connelly, Male, 63, retired. 
When in business was a large-scale system developer – banking, automated teller machines (ATM) and credit card systems/networks. Now I hunt, fish, play golf and on-line computer games :)

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
Live in Boynton Beach, Florida (SE Florida) with my wife and last remaining daughter, a 16 year old EQ fanatic. With the advantage of technology and on-line gaming, I am still able to annoy three sons, Valkarian, Gelrog, and their older brother Faediin who we have finally converted from EQ.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Reliable, impulsive, adventurer

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
Meal – Steak (thick sirloin cut - medium rare), twice baked potato, asparagus, a 1997 California (Napa Valley) cabernet.  Desert is a great cigar and port or scotch neat depending on how I feel.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Calm, lousy golfer.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
Good old Rock and Roll (heavy metal variety) – Stones, Lynyrd Skynyrd are what I try to listen to, however from a dungeon, I mean corner of the house that is dark, mysterious, and difficult to enter there is much loud Rap and Hip Hop.   This horrible sound also surrounds us when on a quest err I mean trip with daughter.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
How embarrasing – yes two poodles, Cindy and Angel. Please note – Grinder was NOT involved in the breed or name selection!

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
Well it was a Sunday so I grabbed two chocolate Krispy Kreeme donuts when I picked up the paper. Since I retired it’s the only day I know for sure – Sunday is the fat paper day ;)

What is your #1 pet peeve?(something that really bugs you)
People who do not accept responsibility for their actions.  I could go on for hours on this one – about the whole litigation nightmare in the US – fall off a ladder and sue the ladder company etc.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
All 3 things would be packed into one venture. I would build the ultimate guys garage.  3 – 4 bays, two story with office and computers on second floor.  Would include machine shop, compressed air system and paint bay. Obviously it would also have a refigerator, microwave, and bathroom.  I’d have to put a couple of bikes in it – one old Harley and a Ducati 999. Also have a 4WD pickup and a vette that needed restoration.   I think that gets rid of most of the $1mm. The rest I give to the kids.  My wife has everything she wants now :)

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
Probably JFK. Not so much for what he did, but for the fact that he represented hope and provided a kind of leadership that was needed at the time. BTW I am a very conservative Republican.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
Not much – maybe lose 10 pounds.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
Howdy Doody? Black and white TV!

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
Easy – 4WD pickup with a custom Harley in the back.

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Track dirt in the house.

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
When my kids tell me I have been a good Dad.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
Have two spots. In my boat fishing saltwater flats in the Florida Keys and on a mountain in West Virginia – usually deer or turkey hunting – but also a great place to take a nap cause the spawn rate is a killer.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Probably an old sea story with sailing ships – and have to be the Captain.  Similar to Master and Commander, but any one similar would be fun.  No spells to remember, load up the cannon and blast away.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
One glass and two coffee cups.

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Play nice.
