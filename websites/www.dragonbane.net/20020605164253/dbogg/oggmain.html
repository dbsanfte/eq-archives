2002.02.15| Everquest Legends, $40 US a month! | by Strokke

In case you haven't heard, Verant has just recently had a signup for a new type of server, called the Legends server. The list filled up the first day, even though they are charging $40 a month!!!
Why would people pay $30 more? Here are some of the aspects that set this server apart.

1. Character Pages-"Everquest Legends players will have a whole new way to show off their characters and equipment - personal character pages!"

2. Dynamic Server-"Our dedicated team of Customer Service & Development staff will not only be providing in-game support to our Legends players, but also running new quests and adventures for players of all levels to participate in"
