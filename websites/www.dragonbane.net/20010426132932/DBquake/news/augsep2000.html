DB Server Updates
I am glad and anxious to bring the team this info......I had a meeting tonight with individuals from Clarkson University, which is primarily an engineering school in Potsdam, NY where I live (and my Alma Mater). I was enthusiastically assured that our new CTF server will be given a home on campus, and connected to their new T3 line! As soon as it is built and set up, they will hook it in to the T3 immediately. This brings me to the second bit of news, I have the case, fans, heatsinks, dual processor board, two Celeron 466's, 256mb memory, hard drive, floppy, cdrom, keyboard, mouse, and monitor, and a linux o/s. I am lacking a video card, sound card, 100mps NIC, and a ATX power supply. People on
the team said that they had these components to send me, but I haven't received any of them yet. These four parts are now holding up the project, and I have ran out of money to purchase them myself. If you have any of these parts and you are willing to donate these for use in the server, please send them to me. Without scrounging these last few
parts, I will have to wait a couple of months until I have the money to buy them, which will of course delay the entire project at least that long.

Matches/Scrims
As previously established..... matches or scrims will be held on the following nights: Tuesday, Thursday, and/or Saturday, at 8pm EST or 9pm EST (or anywhere in between).
