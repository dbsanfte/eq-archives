Freeze Tag is a modification of team deathmatch and it works with Quake 2, Quake 3, and Team Arena. The two teams
compete by trying to kill the other team. When a player is killed, instead of dying, they freeze over and cannot get back into the
game until a teammate unfreezes them. As the teams play, they can be busy unfreezing teammates, guarding frozen enemy
bodies, or trying to kill the other team. The way to unfreeze someone is simple. Stand near a frozen body for three seconds and
it will break apart. That player is now able to get back into the game and fight the enemy. Teams that do not unfreeze
themselves usually lose, however teams that only unfreeze people usually lose too, because they have no offense. The trick is to
find a balance between the two.
