Katzen Crockett
From Katzen - This rather cheesy song was written by my husband when he couldn't get the Ballad of Davey Crockett tune out of his head. I hope you like it. It is rather loosely based on my history of leveling.


THE BALLAD OF THE SHAMAN
(with apologies to Davy Crockett)

Born at the Shaman Guild near Everfrost,
runnin' round the canyons being killed and getting lost,
thumping on the Vengefuls when their paths she crossed,
till out onto the tundra, her butt was tossed.

Shaman, Shaman woman,
got a wimpy little Burst o' Flame.
Shaman, Shaman woman,
the Goblin Whelps she'll tame.

Off to Erud's Crossing, whacking Wisps for fun,
harvest GLS's in the tropical sun,
trade 'em in Karanas for plat-i-num,
being stomped by Griffs and Giants warned of things to come.

Shaman, Shaman woman,
buy some Banded and a cheesy Bloodforge.
Shaman, Shaman woman,
Movin' to the King Xorbb Gorge.

Six easy levels kickin' Mino tail,
hangin' near the EK ramp in case she has to bail,
launch a Borxx-hunt into Runnyeye and never quail,
yet hide from all the beggars in Rivervale.

Shaman, Shaman woman,
can u spare a SOW, some plat, and a buff.
Shaman, Shaman woman,
I'd kill bats but they're way too tough.

Now the camping and the squabbles are much too loud,
wishing she could solo and escape the crowd,
finally got a Wolfie-pet and is she proud,
'cept she's always in a dungeon where pets are not allowed.

Shaman, Shaman woman,
talkin' armor stats and waitin' for the pop.
Shaman, Shaman woman,
ten hours for some silly-ass drop.

Now she's paid her dues doin' SolB crap,
sittin' still and throwing buffs and trying not to nap.
Yearning for the old days hearing Gnoll Pups yap,
hopin' Kunark opens 'fore she hits her boredom cap.

Newbie, twinked-up Newbie,
crankin' up a fresh Level One.
Newbie, twinked-up Newbie,
Fire Beetles! Now we're talkin' fun.
