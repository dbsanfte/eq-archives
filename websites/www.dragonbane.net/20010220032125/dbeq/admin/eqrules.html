• Must be of good or neutral alignment.
  • Intentional training into a newbie area means banishment.
  • Help out fellow travellers needing assistance when asked.
  • No kill stealing.
  • No pure evil character will be accepted.
  • Honest in dealings with all others.
  • Must be active, play at least once a week.
  • Must have ICQ and IRC.
  • No cheating of any sort will be tolerated.
  • All members of the guild to be treated as individuals and equally.
  • Members of this guild only, and only up to 2 characters on the Quellious server may use guild tags.
  • The Guild does not officially sponsor a members' trade profession. However, should a guild member wish to help out a member's trade, it is done solely on a voluntary basis and all arrangements are made between the 2 members.
  • All DB members in good standing shall pay dues as specified.
  • All new members of DB are on a one month probationary period and may not sponsor new members during their probationary period.
  • All members must have their guild tags showing at all time; roleplay is allowed, anonymous is not allowed.
  • No defaming or slandering the Dragon's Bane name.
  • Members must make every reasonable effort to attend Dragon's Bane Guild Events and Festivals.
  • Any member that leaves Dragon's Bane and joins another guild cannot rejoin Dragon's Bane in the future.
  • Items that are handed to guild members from other guild members as "gifts" are considered guild property and cannot be sold. The item when not used any longer is to be handed down to another member in the guild. That member must also hand the item down. When an item cannot be handed down any further, it is given to the DB "mule" character for storage in the bank until such time a new member may need it, or it will be sold and all monies from the item will be returned to the guild fund.
  • Giving our Your Account Information - If you are to give out your account information it must known to who and it must be only to a DB member. If you need to give your information to a non db member then you are to contact an officer directly and let it be known that someone else is playing your character. This is only for emergency times. There are emergency times that are acceptable like have to play to get your body. If a member suspects a non guild member is playing a member's character without notifying an officer, members are advised to ask the player if "they are who they are." If they aren't the person who own's that account, the member will be untagged.
  • Dragon's Bane does not support or think well of any of it's members "farming" equipment, etc. on EBay or any other internet site.

*A member that does not follow the above Rules of Order may be subject to disciplinary action as determined by the guild.
