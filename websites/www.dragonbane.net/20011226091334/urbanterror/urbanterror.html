Urban Terror is a "total conversion mod" for Quake III Arena based on action and realism. Urban Terror is a completely free add-on to id Software's Quake III Arena. Urban Terror is a TC mod because it adds new weapons, player models, sounds, maps, damage system, physics and many new game play options which make it difficult to recognize the mod from Quake III Arena. It is 100% free.
