Capture the Flag Round #3
Let's do this again! Capture the Flag EQ Style!

Sign-up at our DB message forum
http://pub12.ezboard.com/fdragonsbaneoggdbeqgeneral.showMessage?topicID=45.topic
to show who in DB will be participating.

We also need one (1) volunteer to be a referee who will not participate in the actual CTF but will be stationed at the Legendary Heroe's base. Their purpose is to give the LH flag to the incoming DB member and ensure their DB member makes it back to the DB base with the flag. The referee can help his/her DB teammates in guildchat with instructions, etc.

Date: Friday, September 29th, 2000
Time: 9pm EST
Who: ALL DB and LH players (all levels/races/classes)
Legendary Heroes will defend East Commons Base.
Dragon's Bane will defend North Ro Base.

We need as many members out to this event as possible!! All levels needed!

We are looking to book the upcoming Capture the Flag type event between Legendary Heroes and Dragon's Bane.

1. When and Where
On Friday, September 29th, 2000, 8pm EST, we will be waging a guild war in the zones of Eastern Commons and North Ro. This battle is not one of anger, but a brotherly test of skills.

2. Basic description
War is not waged simply for the elimination of a nation’s people but for specific objectives. Remember this is a friendly exercise between nations aligned as brothers, we will wage this mock war with specific objectives other than to kill those we call friends. Yes, there will be deaths, but these are a factor of war, not the ultimate goal. Each guild will be assigned a zone to protect and a specific base within that zone. In order to “capture” a base an enemy player must run all the way around it and return safely to his own base. Teams will use whatever means it takes to keep them from doing so. We will wage war for approximately 2 hours then consume many beers like friends should.

3. About the zones
a. We will have a coin toss, the winner will pick which zone to defend.
b. The East Commons Team will defend the Plague Village.
c. The North Ro Team will defend the platform.

4. Rules
a. All fighting will be limited to North Ro and to East Commons. This does note mean participants cannot go to other zones, simply that there should be no fighting outside of these zones. If you do not wish to participate do not enter these zones during the guild war.
b. For combat and travel, anything goes except teleports/gates. Please feel free to use all of your skills.
c. The tunnel zone line cannot be guarded. Parties in the tunnel should be in transit to the other side. Tunnel guards may be posted, but should be approximately halfway to the tunnel entrance from the zone line or further from the zone.
d. All participants should be in role play mode. This way the "/who guild all" function will not display team locations, but guild flags will still be displayed on player names.
e. No DoT spells, no pets.

5. Warnings
a. NPCs will kill you if you fight too close to them. Please be aware that you will lose experience if an NPC kills you.
b. Remember that unlike NPCs, we are fighting real people here, zoning will only save you if you leave the field of battle. Zoning from North Ro to East Commons those you were fighting can follow you and continue fighting. If you are in desperate shape you will have to choose another zone such as Nektulos, reeport...etc.
c. This will be battle unlike you have ever waged. The combatants are more talented and multifaceted than any NPC, or single real combatant you have fought. Do not underestimate your opponents.

6. Questions and Answers
a. Why did we chose these two zones? The ability for all members to bind nearby and accessibility was a prime concern. The low level of the zones will also make it possible for a majority of players to participate without fear of being attacked by NPC gangs.
b. The tunnel will become a bottleneck, why not use East and West Commons instead? The problem of binding becomes a problem here. There is no direct route to corpses for the West Commons team, Rivervale is a bit far and level 15 player wandering through Kithicor at night will rue the day they were born.
Also, keep in mind the tunnel is not the only way to get back and forth through these zones, it is only the most logical.
c. Will I lose experience if I die? According to the new release notes there is “a possibility” of losing experience when dueling now. I will make no promises that exp will not be lost, but will suggest that if there is any lost, it will be negligable.
d. Won't the two zone format make this event too long? Why not just play in one zone? The original concept for this was simply a team duel in the arena off of Lake Rathe. This however seemed to lack a great deal of the excitement of this event. Thus we adopted the two zone approach. The “flag capture” format of this event should make it proceed fairly quickly. The two zone approach gives a better sense of spontenaety and surprise to the event. In fact I suspect we will have time to play more than one game.
e. If I get killed can other people loot my corpse? Absolutely not. We tested this when we were checking on the experience loss possibility. Player corpses cannot be looted by other players.
f. How will this be accomplished, do we all have to join in dueling mode? There is an actual "guildwar" function in EQ. This is what we will be using. The leaders of guilds can declare war and end it using this function. Basically it makes players PvP to all the members of the other guild.
g. How many do we expect to attend from each guild? This is a tough one to answer.
