April - June 1998 News Archive
June 28, 1998

Rykhas has announced that plans are in the works for an Internal Clan Ladder for prizes including modems, software, games! All clan members will be invited to participate in this event and it will have considerations for HPB's and LPB's. Watch the ladder section and your email for news on its development.

This ladder will be our "trial experience" prior to DB establishing an open to the public clan ladder which will be sponsored, with prizes!

We are happy to announce that DB has moved to it's new location of members.home.net/dragonsbane1. We have been looking for sponsors of DB as you know, and it appears that it is on the horizon.

Played a cool match against Knights of Insanity Clan. They were a great team, skilled and organized. It was very close, but they were victorious overall. It was refreshing to play this clan after a few "distasteful" matches against other clans we will not mention in our news section. KOI reminded us that there are cool clans out there, clans that are professional and are as ethical as we are. Thanks KOI for the great match - join us on our server anytime for some scrimmages. June 17, 1998

Tonight we played against Chaotic Serenity Clan (CS). They were an amazingly skilled, well organized team as well as great sports. DB players cmitchel, Shik, BigSned, Aros and Cyberdragon fought a tough battle and lost both games to CS. However, DB considers the guys of CS to be friends of DB, especially their leader, No-Game. Any clans interested in participating in an utlimate challenge, should send their challenges to Clan CS. Special thanks sent to No-Game for his organizing of and participation in the match. What CS said:

Clan Wars, DB - Info., http://members.tripod.com/~Nogame/

"SCORES: CS=240 DB=129

MATCH 1: Match 1 was played on the Edge, at DB's server. It was evidently clear from the start of things how the match was going to go. CS started off strong, behind No-Game's quick fragging. Saddam Hussien and Dackman took over in the end to really give CS the win. Cmitchel of DB was a constant threat because he was just sneaky as hell!

MATCH 2: Match 2 was played on Tokay's Towers. It started rather close with Cmitchel of DB giving CS a great game! However, Cmitchel was the only powerhouse for his team and eventually lost the top spot on the scores. CS played with consistancy and all players had a close frag count in the end.

ORGANIZATION: The organization for both matches was perfect! Match 1 started after 5 minutes, and match 2 was instantly played. With 20 minute time limits, the war took 45 minutes total! Organization was the best CS has ever seen!

TECH PROBLEMS: Technical problems were non-existant! Other than a 450 ping for No-Game (which isn't unusual), the matches had no lag, and no problems.

SPORTSMANSHIP: Great sportsmanship was displayed by DB. The only words which were exchanged during the course of both matches were praises of good effort. After the end of the match, "GG" was exchanged by all parties.

COMMENTS: CS has never played a more organized clanwar, and I don't think it ever will. DB is the best all around clan on the net (in my opinion). They play the game well, and are enormously good sports! Also, the organization was unparralled by any other. Without a doubt, Dragon's Bane is the clan to play!"
