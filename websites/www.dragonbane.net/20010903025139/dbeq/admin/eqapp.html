Our goal with recruiting is to increase our membership yet find players with the personality which will fit into our guild.

All members must adhere to our Charter and Guild Rules.

How to Apply
Should a player wish to join Dragon's Bane Guild, they must be "sponsored" by a present Dragon's Bane guild member. That is, the member will recommend the applicant to the guild, and is responsible for the applicant during a one month probationary period. All new members are on probation for a period of one month, during which time all members will be evaluating the new member. During this time the new member will also be evaluating Dragon's Bane to ensure they want to commit to the Guild.

Note we do not accept membership applications from players under level 10.

The Dragon's Bane applicant must provide the following information to the Dragon's Bane sponsor (incomplete applications will automatically be returned):

Real Name
EQ Character Name
Email Address
URL (if applicable)
ICQ Number (if you don't have ICQ, download it at www.mirabilis.com, register (it's free) and get your number)
Age
City/Country
Connection
PC System
Interests/Hobbies
Class/Race
Level
Scanned real life picture (if you have one)
Screenshot of online character
Short paragraph on why this member wishes to join Dragon's Bane and how they feel they could contribute overall to the guild

The Dragon's Bane sponsor will forward the information to the Guild Leader who, in turn, will forward to all Dragon's Bane members for their opinions and a vote. There must be at minimum received 10 positive votes and no negative votes within a one week time period. Should there not be enough votes received, the applicant will be advised to get to know more members of Dragon's Bane and to resubmit their application at a later date.

As well, an applicant must attend at least one Guild Event during this one week period in order to meet as many Dragon's Bane members as possible.

Should the vote be successful, the new applicant will receive their guild tags, a Welcome to Dragon's Bane email and the start of their probationary period with the guild. New members on their one month probationary status cannot sponsor members until their probationary period is over.

We do not accept "groups" of members; that is, a group of people that are all online friends and decide to apply together. Each applicant must apply on their own merit and individually must be sponsored by at least one member of Dragon's Bane.

We feel the closeness and uniqueness of our Guild warrants such close scrutiny to our recruitment.

If you have any questions about our application process or the Dragon's Bane Guild, please contact Sharac, our Public Relations Spokesperson at moreta000@aol.com. Please put in the subject heading "Enquiry."
