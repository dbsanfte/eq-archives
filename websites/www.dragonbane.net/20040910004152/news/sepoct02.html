DAOC RvR Showdown| by Hytstuph
Ao and I were hanging out at the AMG in EMain with a couple of groups of Hibbies. We had just waxed a group of Albs by the APK and were heading back to the MG. We swung up through the treeline looking to bait any Infs so we were a good 100 feet behind our group. As we approached the wall I saw a
yellow Alb caster at the base of the wall in the SW corner. I ran toward him and he bowed. So stopped and targetted him with Ao, ready to root if he ran. Instead he walked toward me and bowed. Then he sat down, apparently OOP. He stood up and pointed at Ao, so I sat Ao down. He bowed at me, I bowed at him. Then I hit sprint and charged him. He started to cast, but got never got it off. I hit him with Fireblade, my Flurry insta, and he commenced to runnin'. Immediately two stealthers appeared and attacked me. I stood Ao up and started healing Hyts. I flipped on triple wield and finished of the Caster for 500+ RPs, then I smacked the saracen and took a big chunk of Albion hide. The 3rd saracen ran for Ao, who got off his last heal (OOP) before she got to him. I killed the Saracen and ran for the first one. I got Ao's insta off before she killed him so I had full health. I smacked her once as my triple ran out, and flipped to my parry/evade combo quick bar. I parried her second attack and fired my three move parry combo, which took her to half life. We traded blows and she was down to a small slice of health when a group of hibbies came rolling over her. I woulda had her. All in all 1000 RPs, 3 dead Albs, and only 1 dead bot. Not bad for a random encounter at the wall.
