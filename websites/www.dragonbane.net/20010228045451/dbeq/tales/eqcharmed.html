Charmed Charmer's Luck!
My Name is Joven Wolfheart, I am druid Nature lover of Tunare. Rumored to be quite a Charmer and ladies man amongst other members of the guild (truth of the matter is I'm just nice). I write you a story of my luck in the field. I am only 24th level and I have been charmed 3 times in my career and not happy road to have to take I might add.

My first time was when I was group with a lovely necromancer name Vina She and I were hunting the sisters about 17th level when we for the first time discovered the ruins behind the nybrights camp. we saw what we thought to be a rare spawn Emperor Drake with a monarch. She conned blue to us not even dangerous at that so we decided to slaughter the poor creature. upon hitting it I got a lesson in humility. I was charmed and used as a pawn against the lovely Vina. After her death I was forced to guard the emperor with my life for 5 whole minutes then my control was regained and nothing else came of it.. I never again hit the emperor since then.

Then at 23rd level Iw as closing in to 24 while hunting in mistmoore with a good group when someone shouted "Train
from the graveyard." Without a second thought I ran like hell to get out Mistmoore and just as I prepared to zone, I found my self back in Mistmoore, this time charmed by the negotiator! And, this time I wasn't just charmed, I was beguiled for 20 minutes!! I was forced to buff everything thing I saw from negotiators to king and princess to vampire and ghoul.  I was taken into the heart of the graveyard and buffed anything that spawned "skin like stone, shield of barbs, see invisible, Spirit of wolf" - all,  I laid it on them, at which time barely aware of where I was I reigned my self and was slapped down dead where I stood in about 0.5 seconds!

After I respawned, I went invisible hoping that "see invisible" had worn off which it had thank the gods! I dragged my poor corpse back to zone and looted it and prayed that nothing like that would happen again. ( I was forced to kill so many people in that zone I felt my good name corrupted beyond repentance. I killed my group and people at whim under the control of that heartless bitch whom is called the negotiator) .

Later, I am level 24 and I have been charmed yet again!! This time it's by Evil Eye in East Karana and it too was blue. It made me kill and buff but it wore off quicker, but not quick enough for I was forced to kill my partner Darcness!

The moral of my story is if you see a druid named Joven accompanying a heartless creature, kill him quick and end his suffering for you will do him a favor in the long run.

Cancel magic is also a good thing to have <hint hint> thank you and peace be with all of you.

Joven
