QUAKE: End of Dragon's Bane Quake Team| by Sandman
Dragon's Bane does not run any Q3 teams any more. As far as I know, very few members even play Quake any more. Membership started to wane about a year ago, leaving the clan with only a handful of active players. Attempts to merge with other clans, recruit new players, and jump-start players with sporadic activity all failed. The last of what was left of DB Quake life fell out of existence about 6 months ago. It is hopeful that if Doom3 is ever released (maybe in another year), then it will spark an interest amoung DB players, but no one knows for sure. It's been a good run, but the time has come to close downt the DB Quake teams formally.
