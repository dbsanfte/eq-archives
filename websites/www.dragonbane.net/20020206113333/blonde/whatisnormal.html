What is Normal?

…..and who is to say what is?

I catch myself listening to someone and not understanding the subject but because I am self conscience and am suffering from low self esteem I pretend to know what the hell they are talking about. It is only after I am deeply emerged in the conversation that I will finally break down and admit I was lost from square one. I don’t learn from my experience with this because I keep doing it.

Is this normal? Is there some medical name? Should I take Prozac (or Serafem, same drug different name after the patent ran out, heh heh)?

I hate my shoes. I don’t hate other peoples shoes but I am a woman and should adore, collect and shop endlessly for my shoes, yes? No, I despise my shoes. Maybe I was tortured with shoes as a small child? Or I could be a foot nudist?  

Is this normal? Is this condition listed in the medical journals? May I have a Valium, please?

My point being with all this (yes, sometimes there is actually a point) is that I think humans have gotten to carried away with examining their inner-selfness. Don’t get me wrong, I understand and agree that there are alot of chemical imbalances and trauma out there in the world (here comes my big “but”) BUT I also think that a lot of the conditions are followed by a psychosis-trendy mass of groupies. They see a news blip about some new ailment, match one of the symptoms mentioned and BOOM they hop on the bandwagon. Remember the ADD (Attention Deficit Disorder) trend of the late 80’s. There ‘ya go. Concerned parents flocked in droves to doctors who readily prescribed drugs for children that no long term testing had been done on.

Or you have the “concerned ones” who are readily available to diagnose, prescribe, and protect us from ourselves if we have been deemed not normal in their standards. Let me add here that I am rarely deemed normal by any group….lol. This group of Helper Helpertons (thank you Jim Carrey for this term) want everyone to live the way they see fit and if you don’t, watch out. You could be considered a radical or a militant or even worse worshipping the wrong god….*sigh*. No worries friends, most likely you can be brought around to their way of thinking in time.

In the end I just want to be left to myself to live the way I choose, shoeless and happy. I don’t expect others to be shoeless and I promise not to form a faction of new breed barefoot conquerors to rule the earth. If the shoe wearers decide to force me to their cause I will have to do some steel toe stompin’, and return to my shoeless state with a cheerful grin. Just let me be me and I’ll let you be you J

Cynder
