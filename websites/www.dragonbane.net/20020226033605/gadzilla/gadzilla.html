Jason and Shelby: thank you!

This is a small story from my life that was affected by another Dragon's Bane member.  A member, that went above and beyond the call of friendship and our code of conduct. A person, that to them the kindness that they showed me was part of a normal friendship.  This member is Tumult, aka Jason Petruno. 

In the past month we have become friends and have learnt a lot about each other.   He also has a girlfriend who is equally kind and generous named Shelby.  These two people took me into their home for a long weekend, without even meeting me first. Where I come from, this isn’t something that is done normally.  Everyone where I live is normally extremely reserved and would never allow a stranger into their house without knowing them first.  Not only did Jason take me in for the weekend but, he proceeded to help me get a job.  The job market as well all know isn’t the greatest, so in order for me to shine above the rest, he allowed me to see some of the stuff he does before I applied for the job.  He introduced me to his boss and helped me rewrite my resume to reflect more of my better qualities.  His girlfriend was willing to help as well.  She is a good listener and made me feel comfortable while I was at their house.

In our clan code of conduct, we don’t have a specific section about face-to-face meetings.  If we did, Jason, aka Tumult has surpassed anything that we could have thought of to write as a guideline.  This little story is to honor him and his actions.

I have found that our clan has been attracting some extremely nice people.   I hope this trend continues and that when we all find that special friendship and that we won’t squander it or allow it to dissipate with age. 

Special note:  Thank you Jason and Shelby for your kindness and hospitality. You have given me hope and courage that I didn’t have before I visited you. The kindness from your hearts is infectious and will be remembered for a very long time.

Glenn MacIntosh
