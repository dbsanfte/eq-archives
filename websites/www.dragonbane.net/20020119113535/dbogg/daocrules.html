Uniform Policy
One of the Dragon's Bane's new traditions is mandatory uniforms, a certain color and style of clothing and armor that sets us apart from others, shows us as an impressive, united force, and encourages guild/team pride. Uniforms will not be enforced until a month into the game. Uniform requirements are generally waived for characters under level 10 (due to financial constraints). Lighter colors of dye may be substituted for the more expensive versions as needed. This uniform policy may change as the game economy fluctuates and pricier dyes become more available. The guildmaster may, at any time, designate a set uniform for a specific event, provided it is financially reasonable. Alternate characters on other servers are not bound by any uniform rules. Our sigil is on a two-toned field of grey and dark blue.

Uniform Specifics
The required uniform for recruits rank 9 consists of orange tunic, orange leggings, orange boots, orange arms, orange gloves, and a blue cloak. Helms are optional; if worn, they must also be orange. Recruits are never permitted to wear the official grey and blue eye-emblemed cloak. The informal uniform for full members ranked 1-8 is the official grey and blue eye-emblemed cloak (or a simple blue cloak if funds/level prevents use of the emblemed cloak), and additional clothing, armor, and colors as desired, OR, dark blue tunic, dark blue leggings, dark blue boots, dark blue arms, dark blue helm (if worn), gloves dyed according to rank (see chart below) and no cloak. Both of these styles are discouraged, but permissable when not in attendance at a formal event or raid. Members may not wear the formal uniform of a higher rank and pass it off as a casual uniform. The formal uniform for full members ranked 1-8 consists of dark blue tunic, dark blue leggings, dark blue boots, dark blue arms; the grey and blue emblemed guild cloak (or a simple blue cloak if funds/levels prevents use of the emblemed cloak); and gloves dyed according to rank. This uniform is encouraged at all times, but only required on formal guild outings or as directed by the Master.

Recruiting Policy
An interested player should post to the forums expressing his intent, get to know our members in game, read over the website (especially the charter) and finally, secure from within the active membership a Sponsor - a member of Dragon's Bane who agrees to serve as a guide through the recruiting period. Then, and only then, the player is invited to fill out the application. Until the application is approved, the recruit will be invited into Dragon's Bane DAoC team as a Rank 9 Recruit.
