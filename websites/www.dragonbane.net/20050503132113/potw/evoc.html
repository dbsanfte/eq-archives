Welcome to Dragon's Bane's Player of the Week featuring a different DB member every week. This week, the Cynder questionnaire probes the background of our member ...

EVOC BLAZETOSS
Everquest Assistant Team Leader

Please list your game-name, RL name, gender, age, occupation.
Evoc, Eban, Male, 19 in September, Full time student.

Where do you live and whom do you inflict yourself upon daily?
I live in a little nothing town called Magalia in sunny CA. While you've probably never heard of it, perhaps you have heard of Chico and the California State University they have there. The school was ranked one of the top party schools in the USA a number of years ago by Playboy magazine, and is where I shall be attending come Fall ;) Anyways, Magalia is about 45 mins north of Chico. I "inflict" myself upon my parents and my brother, although I prefer to think that they inflict themselves upon me.

List 3 words you would use to describe yourself.
Kind, Stubborn, Sarcastic.

What is your all-time favorite meal including dessert?
I'd have to say a really good steak, grilled medium-rare, perhaps a bit of seasoning, and lots of salt. For sides I'd have to pick corn on the cob slathered in butter and salt, and a baked potato drowning in butter sprinkled with salt and pepper. I bet you can tell I like butter and salt =P I really really like sweets, so it's hard for me to pick one for desert, so I'm going to have two, hehe. For firsts I'd have to have chocolate cake with homemade buttercream frosting, and for seconds a really good cheesecake.

List 3 words someone else might use to describe you.
Obnoxious, Lazy, Smart.

What kind of music do you listen to the most? (careful this is a trick question)
I like a strange mix of country, pop, and light rock. I'm really not to picky as to what I listen too, just no hard/alternative rock please, it's just noise.

Do you have any pets? If not, why? If so, what kind? What are they named?
I have a dog, Wrinkles, who is a mutt. Part Shar-Pei, part Chocolate Lab, part Pit-Bull, and we think she has some Dalmation in her as well. Ugliest dog you've ever seen, but she's a sweetie.

What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
A glass of orange juice and a bowl of Grape Nuts, standard breakfast during the school year (in summer school now)

What is your #1 pet peeve? (something that really bugs you)
I can't stand sloppy people. I'm a bit of a neat freak and it drives me crazy when I walk into someone's house and the place looks like sh**.

If you won $1,000,000 (or the equivalent in your currency) what would be the first 3 things you would do?
1. I'd put most of the money in the bank so that I could live off the interest and never have to work, muwahahaha!
2. I'd take some of the money and buy a car. Not sure which kind, but something small that looks good and gets good gas mileage.
3. I'd take some of the money and buy a new computer. It would be a P4 1.4 MhZ, 30 gig hard drive, 256 megs of RAM, GeForce 3 Video Card, 21 inch monitor, and I'd a DSL line to go with it. Ahhh the power.

Who do you admire most from history and why (dead, alive, famous or unknown)?
The man who invented the micro-chip, I don't know his name. I'm not sure if what he did was a good thing, who knows where computers will take us, but I can't imagine life without computers. Something as small as a fingernail has changed the world forever, and I admire the man who was able to invent such a thing.

If you were able to change 1 thing about your appearance what would it be?
I'd have washboard abs of course.

When you were little what was your favorite TV show?
My mom wasn't big on TV when I was kid so I didn't get to watch much. We did have a bunch of tapes though, and while I strongly deny this my mom claims that I loved watching Care Bears and My Little Poney videos =P

If you could have any mode of transportation what would it be? (anything that exists to current date on Earth)
I think that it would be fun to have a cruise ship. I know I would only be able to use it on the ocean, but think of the trips that I could take, and all the babes who would want to go with me!

Name 1 annoying habit that you do constantly. (be honest)
Organize things (trust me, it gets on people's nerves).

Describe the nicest thing anyone has ever done for you.
My parents paying for my college education. I'm not finished with it yet, but I know that it's going to be one of the greatest gifts ever given to me, and I will never be able to thank them enough.

Where is your perfect place? (it can be a chair, a room, a country, a state of mind. Wherever you feel at your best)
Stretched out on my bed in my room with a really good book and a glass of lemonade with the fan on. I let the fan and lemonade cool my body and the book take my mind away to a different place where my troubles don't exist.

If you could be in any movie, what would it be and what part would you play?
Neo in The Matrix, because it would be awesome to be able to shape a world however you wanted. Not to mention the fact that Trinity is a babe, hehe.

Are there dirty dishes in your kitchen sink right now? (if you are not at home make an educated guess)
I'm shocked, but no there aren't. My brother is one of those sloppy people whom I can's stand and he normally has dishes in the sink, dishes on the counter, dishes in his room.......

If you could say 1 sentence that the entire world would hear what would it be?
Always try, for even if you fail at least you'll never have any doubts about whether or not you could have done it.
