TUNARE - The Mother of All

Followers of Tunare believe that we are all Tunare's children. The children of Tunare seek to help Norrath, which they believe to be a living and breathing being, to blossom and grow. Followers believe that the world of Norrath gave birth to Tunare and from her all life has sprung forth. Thus, by worshipping and protecting the land, followers are paying homage and respect to the Mother of their Mother, who in turn protects and provides for them. Followers of Tunare will fight to the death to protect nature in all its forms. Many druids, rangers, and a great many elves follow the ways of Tunare.
- taken from Verant

 

Our Charter

Tunare, the Mother of us all, is Goddess of Creation, Lawmaker, Healer, Hunter, Battle Leader, and Truth Giver. Tunare is Sacrifice; the Children of the Mother are humble individuals who think of others first and themselves second.

The Children seek to weed out that which is foul and noxious in search of that which is innocently beautiful, namely love and reverence for life. We seek to recreate ourselves daily by achieving new successes, not to selfishly or impetuously aggrandize ourselves, but to enhance our friends and the environment in which we dwell.

We know that it is not the destination but the journey that is important.

The Temple of the Mother is the Earth. We do not need stone edifices to remind us of our place in the universe; every step we take is on holy ground. We do not need to shout our virtuosity; our actions are our examples.

Mutual respect is the Golden Rule of our Guild. We take care of each other!
