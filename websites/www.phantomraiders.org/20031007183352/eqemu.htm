March 31st, 2003

As a few people know, the EQ-emu project has been ongoing now for some time, and already have their first server cluster up.

It's not that well known, but behind the scenes, PR has been moving it's characters and items to a new server cluster, which has been named Infra Dromaeus

So, with a sad heart, we bid farewell to Bertox, and set sail on new, untouched territory.
   


We log in at the predefined times, only to discover that not all character data has been ported yet. Not to worry, we create some twinks and start having fun..





Some of us were a little slow to log in, though



So, we moved to West Freeport and started levelling up. Soon ran out of mobs to kill, though



So it was on to East Commons



North Ro



The Karanas



And eventually Dreadlands around Karnor



We look forward to continuing adventures on our new server Infra Dromaeus ,
and for our friends back on Bertox
