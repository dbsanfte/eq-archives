31st March :

Phantom Raiders is recruiting:

We are looking for dedicated players who can attend raids from 3pm (est) to 8pm (est) at least 5 days a week. We do not require you to have any flags, but you must be willing to work, to catch up to the guild's progress, when we give you the opportunity. Most of all we need skilled, mature players looking to have fun (and die) in some of the most challenging zones in EQ.
1. Prerequisites for Recruitment

First, you need to make sure meet some basic requirements:

a) You must be level 65.
b) You must have all relevant expansions (This means everything but Ykesha).
c) You must have a connection and computer that can support 72 person raids.
d) You must have at least the basic AA's critical for your class.
e) A working "Magelo" profile

We will look at any applicant that can meet these modest standards, however, we are actively seeking the following classes:

Clerics, Enchanters, Shaman, Wizards, and Warriors.

If you are not one of the aforementioned classes, but you still wish to join PR, your best chance will be to find someone in the guild willing to help sponser you. Your sponser should be familiar with the recruitment process and willing to invest time in helping you meet other members of the guild. If you are one of the classes we are interested in recruiting, but lack a sponser, you may send a tell directly to Raak to inquire about recruitment.

2. Beginning Your Recruitment

To begin formal recruitment you must speak directly with Raak in game. He will explain, in more detail, the day to day requirements of your recruitment. Your name will then circulated through the guild for opinions and past experiences.

You should receive a final decision on your appeal to become a recruit within a few days. However, please bear in mind that Raak is almost always busy. It is unlikely he will respond right away to your tells (especially during raid times). If you're concerned that he has missed your tells, or have any general questions feel free to ask another officer.

3. Being a Recruit

Once you're an accepted recruit you'll be expected to meet the raiding requirements of a full member. During raid times you'll be tagged into the guild and be expected to de-tag yourself before you log for the evening. Most likely you'll miss some raids during this time while you're getting caught up on flags.

The first phase of recruitment has no definite duration. The more quickly you impress and befriend members the shorter this stage of your recruitment will last.

4. Getting Your Permanent Tag

At some point, most likely after you're flagged and have been attending raids regularly, you will be asked to stay in the guild. This will mark the final stage of your recruitment. Typically people that make it to this stage of recruitment become full members. This will be a final test for membership. After a few weeks you'll be put to a formal vote, by the guild members, and informed of your status.


5. Notes About Recruitment

Cross-Server recruits will be accepted but please contact us and let us know your home server and why you're leaving/choosing Bertox.

We require you to submit a magelo, but gear (or lack thereof) is not as important as play time. The entire guild is constantly upgrading their equipment. There are more than enough nice items to help you catch up.

Recruits often receive nice items; however, understand that during your recruitment you will only be allowed to ask for items that receive no tells from members. Any item meeting this qualification will be clearly noted in guild chat.

While we do not require flags or great gear they do help. Recruits that have had prior guild experience make everything easier for us. The easier you make the process on us, the easier (and quicker) it will be for you.
