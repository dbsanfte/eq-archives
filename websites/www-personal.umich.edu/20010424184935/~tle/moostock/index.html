Welcome to Moostock


What is Moostock?

  • Moostock is an event that everyone can participate in regardless of level. It involves all the participants creating a level 1 dorf(dwarf) with their own character name with the prefix 'moo'. Hence, my name, Glayde, becomes Mooglayde. Bill would become Moobill, and so on. One the Moocharacters gather, they travel from their home in Kaladim, to far places across the land of Norrath.

Who can attend Moostock?

  • For the most part anyone can, the first Moostock wasn't as highly publicized because it was primarily an in-guild event. Future Moostocks will be open to the general public however. All you need to do is create the appropriate Moocharacter and be on when the event starts.

Where and when is the next Moostock?

  • Moostock is based on the Rodcet Nife server. The next Moostock (Moostock III) will be run on Sunday, February 25, 2001 at 3pm EST(noon PST). All characters should log in before that time in order to be given a small amount of platinum to buy beer, and some food and water. Please meet outside Kaladim once you have your beer, food and water. Logging in as much as an hour early is recommended.

Who should i contact for information about Moostock?

  • Contact me, Glayde at tle@umich.edu if you have questions about Moostock, or visit our guild message board, http://a-d.virtualave.net/cgi-bin/Ultimate.cgi and post in the public section.

Are there any rules regarding Moostock?

  • Only a couple, please arrive on time to start the event.
  • Do not overly spam, this includes public channels, and perhaps dueling. Though low level character duels might not even create zonewide messages, if this is the case feel free to duel before the event, but do not duel during the event. It is usually tradition upon entering the zone, for everyone to announce the arrival of Moostock to the zone by /ooc'ing moo, or some form thereof. We will do that, but be warned, the gms/guides usually get petitions from people who are disrupted by our fun-having. Please listen to instructions that I may give during the event to control spam. Please also ignore people following and harassing us as we run, /ignore is a wonderful thing.
  • One thing everyone should do, is to create several hot-key social emotes, that are some form of Moo. Use these liberally as we run through the zones.
  • Most importantly, have fun and invite your friends!
