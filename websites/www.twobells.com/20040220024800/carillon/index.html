Welcome to Carillon. The rules are simple. Respect and caring. The Golden Rule. We are a family. Heading up the family are Raydiance Alive and Patrynn Ironfist, a RL married couple. Membership is by invitation only. If you would like to join Carillon, come and meet Ray and Pat -- get to know them. Get to know some of our other lively members. Visit our forum and read the Welcome speech :) Put your feet up at the fire and chat. (Updated 6-26-03)

 

UPDATE 12-04-03:

Because of the changing interests of Carillon members, we have opened the guild up to other game players as well. These games include Neverwinter Nights (NWN) and Star Wars Galaxies (SWG). We are also considering EverQuest II (EQ2).

We are currently taking a look at the requirements and benefits of members in the NWN section. Since NWN is already a go and since many of our Carillon EQ players are already NWN players as well, it has been used as the testing grounds for this experiment. It would appear that the enthusiasm for NWN grows and wanes. It seems to be more of an outlet for disgruntled EQ players than anything else at the moment. However, it is an outstanding game and we reserve its space on the board for anyone who is enjoying the game at the moment. Our biggest difficulty to date with getting NWN up and going as a guild division of Carillon is the lack of dedicated servers...for some reason Pat and I can't access each other's server, and there were no people at the time who had the capability. This may change, especially over Christmas this year, when Pat has a whole week's holiday *eg*.

The moderators of the NWN section of Carillon is Drakhuul.

The moderator of the SWG section of Carillon is Tooncis (resmiranda). Since the release date of June 26/03, there has been a huge flux in the status and enthusiasm of the SWG players. Many are not playing right now...some are struggling to maintain their enthusiasm. Carillon is no longer the only venue for these players, since they receive more benefits through player guilds within SWG itself. Ray and Pat do not play SWG anymore (largely for financial reasons). However, Carillon is still open to our SWG players, current and former, as a meeting spot, a place to exchange ideas, and also a place for our dear friends in SWG to touch base now and then with EQ.

You can access the message board portions of both these sections through the Carillon EzBoard forums.

You can email Carillon via its leaders -- Raydiance Alive or Patrynn Ironfist.

Some things to note for the EQ portion of Carillon (server is Tunare):

1. We are small -- and are not looking to expand rapidly. It is more important to us to maintain the friendly, helpful and fun atmosphere that has been established in Carillon.

2. For raiding purposes, we will seek out mutually cooperative grouping with our friendship guilds or allies. (We are currently allied with Madcap Elite and Legends of Madcap. Our current friendship guilds are A dileab de Paegen, Companions of Norrath, and Watchkeepers.)

3. You may belong to other guilds. Alts are welcome.

4. You are not required to do anything in Carillon except follow the Golden Rule with caring and compassion (and register your character in Guild Magic - failing to do so is a naggable offense :)

5. We encourage people to do their thing in Carillon. Whatever you enjoy doing, be it leadership, trade skills, socializing, serious play -- you will receive encouragement and backing.

6. The prime motivation in Carillon is a caring atmosphere geared to having fun and sharing our experiences, whether it is in actual grouping, via our reserved chat channel, or simply on the Carillon message boards.
