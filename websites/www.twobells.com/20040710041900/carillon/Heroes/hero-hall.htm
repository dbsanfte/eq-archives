Welcome to Carillon's Hall of Heroes. These people have in some way performed heroic acts or deeds to benefit their guildmates. It may have been courage under fire, or perserverance in CR, or simply an outstanding kindness.

Any Carillon member is eligible for nomination to the Hall of Heroes, including alts or mains of a Carillon member in other guilds.

All that is required to attain a listing in our Hall of Heroes is a nomination by a guild member.
