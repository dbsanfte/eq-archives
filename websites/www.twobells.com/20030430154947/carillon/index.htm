Welcome to Carillon. The rules are simple. Respect and caring. The Golden Rule. We are a family. The head of the family is Raydiance. She is supported by her RL husband, Patrynn Ironfist, a knowledgeable and caring person. Membership is by invitation only. If you would like to join Carillon, come and meet Ray and Pat -- get to know them. Get to know some of our other lively members. Visit our forum and read the Welcome speech :) Put your feet up at the fire and chat. (Updated 3-4-03)

 

UPDATE 4-17-03:

Because of the changing interests of Carillon members, we have decided to open the guild up to other game players as well. These games include, for the time being, Neverwinter Nights (NWN) and Star Wars Galaxies (SWG). We are also considering Shadowbane (SB) and EverQuest II (EQ2).

We are currently taking a look at the requirements and benefits of members in the NWN section. Since NWN is already a go and since many of our Carillon EQ players are already NWN players as well, it will be used as the testing grounds for this experiment. Please bear with us, as we change to accommodate the needs and desires of our membership in these various areas.

The moderator of the NWN section of Carillon is Jasarina (Anarawyn Clan).

The moderator of the SWG section of Carillon is Tooncis (resmiranda). Since SWG is still in beta and not expected to be released to the public until some time in June or July, this portion of Carillon will be relatively quiet. However, we have several enthusiastic beta testers already playing the game...they are bound by a Non-disclosure Agreement, but will let us have tidbits from time to time that whet our appetite for this enchanting game.

You can access the message board portions of both these sections through the Carillon EzBoard forums.

You can email Carillon via its leaders -- Raydiance Alive or Patrynn Ironfist.

Some things to note for the EQ portion of Carillon:

1. We are small -- we intend to stay small -- we are not an uber/raid guild.

2. For raiding purposes, we will seek out an alliance with other guilds. Only guilds that follow our Golden Rule will be considered. (We are currently allied with Madcap Elite and Legends of Madcap.)

3. You may belong to other guilds. Alts are welcome.

4. You are not required to do anything in Carillon except follow the Golden Rule with caring and compassion.

5. We encourage people to do their thing in Carillon. Whatever you enjoy doing, be it leadership, trade skills, socializing, serious play -- you will receive encouragement and backing.
