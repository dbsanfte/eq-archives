Epic Tracking for Clan X Members
 

Let us know how far along you are in your Epic Quest.  Until we come up with an automated process, we will begin posting epic status on the Message Board.  This forum is password protected and for Clan X members only.

1) Go the the Epics Message Board.

2) Go to your specific Class.
3) Copy the checklist template.
4) Create a reply in your specific Class.
5) Paste the template checklist.
6) Answer yes or no to the respective parts of each epic.

If you have any questions or would like to update the checklists (as things can sometimes change), please let Glanois know or post under each forum. Or, if you found a better checklist out there, please let me know. I will copy and paste the info in. I am no expert on Epics !  Contact Glanois if you have questions about this.
