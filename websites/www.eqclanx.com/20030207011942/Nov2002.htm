"Black and Blue, Rixma and YOU"
 What the heck is THAT????!!!!

Once each month we are going to have a Clan X get together - ANYTHING goes at this event. What are we going to do? EVERYTHING!!! Language fair, trading of items and spells, duels, discussions of future strategies, treasure hunts, skill building, races, and of course, ganging up on Rixma so YOU TOO can say you slayed her.  Just show up and bring what you want to accomplish, whether it is to fight, learn something new, discuss strategies or vision, or just get to know your guild family.

November & December are "The Royals" Months

 
As a guild, we need warrior epics and more click sticks. If there are a lot of people on line, especially if we have mages, we need to do what we can to get down there for our clerics and warriors. In fact we should try checking regularly for Chardok status so we can get in there and do these fast.

Please focus on The Royals in the months of November and December.
November 26, 2002 
Congratulations to Chesterr for getting Clan X's  FIRST warrior epic.                                                                                  

     

November 24, 2002
Clan X really stood together this last Sunday afternoon and took out Ixiblat three times for Himeo, Beledian,  and Aurorah.  In the middle of waiting for the third kill we heard Severlious was up, so what does a guild itching for some warrior epics do?  RUN FAST and KILL:  Severlious = 0 and Clan X =1.  Thanks to LoM for letting us take the chance to get on the rotation.  Dropped Green Dragon Scale, 2 Blight, Hammers of the Scourge, Avatar, Torpor, Word of Redemption, Devouring Darkeness, Banishment. Congratulations to Gbab for getting Torpor and Avatar,and the Blight Hammer, he also had gotten Malo recently too.  Gbab = Uber Shaman!

     

November 15, 2002
Clan X took out Ragefire three times in a row for Fazel, Takel, and Gileus's Cleric epics. Thanks to all who came and helped make this such a huge success.

For various reasons, as of November 18, 2002 Clan X will be moving to a new message board system.
The homepage link to the old board will be removed and replaced with the new one. If you have the old message board bookmarked or linked on any sites, please change it to point to: http://www.vvm.com/ubercyrus/xboard

November 10, 2002
It was the weekend for lucky loot for Clan X.
Congrats to FAZEL on collecting all 4 shards he needs for his cleric epic.  We are that much closer to another click stick in the family:
Congrats to CELICOS on his Torn Enchanted Silk Robe in Kael - he turned it in with 3 gems to get Warlock's Robe

Congrats to SITHVICIOIS on his Corroded Chain Leggings that dropped in Kael.  He will turn them in for Rune Crafter's Greaves

 
November 8, 2002
CLAN X ROCKS CHARDOK: Wow, Clan X really showed some style and muscle in Chardok Friday night. We were able to get THREE Cleric epic pieces for Fazel, Takel, and Gileus;  THREE Warrior epic pieces for Shrikeavatar, Chesterr, and Dalak; and ONE Enchanter epic piece for Mystagogue.  It was a night to be proud of for all Clan X.

 

November 6, 2002
Congrats to Gneiss on obtaining his flag for the Plane of Valor.  WTG Bard Boy!  Many of our members have attempted the Trial of Execution, keep it up Xers!

 

November 2, 2002
Congratulations to Calamarr and Ubercyrus on getting their Shaman Epic pieces on Saturday.  The day started out kind of rough for Ubercyrus as he wasn't able to continue with the Hole raid of his epic.  Clan X decided to continue on with the raid and do it for Calamarr.   Ubercyrus was able to get his piece from CoM and the guild got together and redid the Hole again Saturday evening for Ubercyrus.  We also were able to get Kindle for the Enchanter's epic piece for Mystagogue, way to go!  Thanks to all who gave up so much xp that day to help get everyone their epic pieces.
