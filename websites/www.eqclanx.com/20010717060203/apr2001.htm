Lonedor's Soulfire Quest completed!!!!! - 9 April

I finished the Soulfire quest last night. I must thank Rixma, Wynd (BoH) and Kebron (BoH), Aknar (last day on our server), Guldarn, and Alex. I know there were a couple others and I apologize for not remembering their names. With Rixma’s help we got the group together fairly quickly and took out Sir Lucan D’Lere equally fast. There were only 2 deaths on the raid, Alex and mine. I actually dead 4 times but 3 were from my own stupidity. Never bind in a spot you will soon be KoS at. (hehe) Rixma, Alex and myself are all KoS in E and W FP now.

Soulfire
 Dmg 23 Del 45 Str +7 Wis +7 Effect: Complete Healing

Here is some background on the quest. Sir Lucan D’Lere was once a paladin but turned to the path of darkness and now rules E and W FP with an ironfist. The paladins still control N. FP. As a true paladin my job was to take out Sir Lucan and return his Testimony of Truth to the leader of the paladins. At that point the paladins gave me the Brilliant Sword of Faith. My own Testimony of Truth, a letter of introduction, the hilt of Soulfire (gotten in MM) and the Brilliant Sword of Faith have to be turned into Brother Hayle in the prison in PAW and viola he gives you Soulfire, sounds easy right?

Here are a few things I learned along the way. We knew Sir Lucan D’Lere is a lvl 49 guard and he keeps a cleric near by. I pulled Lucan and others to the zone and zoned while members of the group hailed him while the other guards returned to their post. I should have waited before zoning to get Lucan a bit closer to the zone. The cleric ended up sticking around and healed Lucan once or twice before he was killed. Once Lucan is dead it is not over, his undead corpse spawns immediately and that corpse is the one with Lucan’s Testimony of Truth. As we were in the middle of delivering the note to Brother Hayle in PAW 2 more BoH save my butt. They told us I need to have a faction of Warmly with Brother Hayle who is a Qeynos cleric. First if you are invisible the faction is indifferent. Even not invisible I was indifferent to Brother H. I just about lost all the items for the quest. Again BIG thank you to BoH. So we headed to Qeynos to build faction. It was easy, hand the head of the cleric guild in Qeynos (he was outside by the wall) 1600 gp, TWO at a time. That only took about 2 hours to go from indifferent to warmly, hehe. Once that was done, back to PAW, hand Brother H in the prison all the items and BAM! SOULFIRE. Did I mention that Brother H is in a jail cell and the door shuts and locks behind you? Good thing Alex was along, we teleported back to WC. Since all the guards not hate me I will be hunting guards near the FP zones. Hehe.

Your humble servant,
Lonedor Bladewise
