Thank you for taking an interest in joining Clan X, an EverQuest guild on the Rathe server. At this time Clan X is only recruiting on a case-by-case basis.  Because of our needs, here are the required levels per class:
 

37 Cleric, Enchanter
42 Bard, Shaman
46 Beastlord, Magician, Monk, Necromancer, Wizard
55 Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Shadowknight, Warrior
60 Druid
for those that applied before 5/20/02, those level requirements do not apply


The process for joining our guild requires dedication from the people who are looking to become members. If you are serious about joining a guild that values teamwork and FUN raiding experiences, then you must prove yourself before you will receive an official invite. To complete the steps below, it is important that applicants make themselves familiar with our:

Guild Charter
Calendar 
Message Board
 

 

  • Step 1: Applicants must submit an application, which will be reviewed by the officers.

  • Step 2: Acquaint yourself with members of Clan X by posting to the New Recruit MB  and on other areas of the MB. 

  • Step 3: During a 2-week raiding period applicants must seek out current members and group with them. 

  • Step 4: Applicants will be expected to attend at least 1 raid with an officer present.  

  • Step 5: Applicants will be required to have 5 members vote on their behalf.

  • Step 6: Applicants will be required to be interviewed by 3 officers upon completion of the above 4 steps.

  • Step 7: Once all above steps are completed, the final say on an applicant will come down from the Recruiting Officer.

These people will be available in game and through e-mail to help guide you in your application process.  If you wish to be in Clan X, then these are the folks you will eventually speak with. 

Upon guilding, a new recruit will undergo a 45-day probation period. Should during this time, the new recruit break any rules of our charter or act unbecoming of a Clan X member, immediate guild removal will be in order without warning.  Note: These rules are subject to change at any given time.
 

 
 
We accept all peoples, all classes and all races.  Any alternates that you may wish to add to Clan X may be any level once you have a primary in the guild.

 

We realize that the process of getting in to Clan X is not an easy one. If you want easy there are MANY other guilds in Norrath to choose from. On the other hand, if you wish to become a part of a close-knit family that is very capable of raiding the Planes and higher-level zones, then these strict requirements of getting in will ultimately serve you well.

 

Clan X is a guild in Norrath that take pride in the people that we recruit – we invite honorable and teamwork driven citizens to pursue membership in our distinguished guild.
