Clan X has official alliances with
Fraternitas Per Honorus and Guardians of the Faith.
We refer to our joint efforts as  the Rathe Alliance.

Alliances allow us to fight alongside different cultures and make long lasting relationships with a variety of Norrath citizens. They also  make it possible for members of Clan X to obtain their epic quest items due to the sheer numbers in the alliance.  Finally, alliances provide the opportunity for Clan X to form raids on the Planes of Hate, Fear, Sky, and Growth.
