Welcome new member Vaeli (41 rogue)! If you haven’t met her, make sure you say hello. We are glad to have her in Clan X!

Screenshots

 

I updated Screenshots this week with pictures of Clan Xers with their new SoL models =o). Check out Rixma, Merther, Kroeten and others. If you have screenshots email them to me!

 

Meet and Greet

 

Thanks to everyone that came out to the meet and greet in Lavastorm on Saturday, it was neat to see what everyone looked like after the SoL upgrade. It was wonderful to see some of the members from GotF and FpH there as well, we haven't seen you guys in ages! Great to see you there!

 

Plane of Hate

 
Way to Go - TWO planes raids in ONE weekend! We started in the Plane of Hate on Friday. Nice job everyone on staying in camp and keeping the SS tight! Even with the LD and lag issues caused by Shadows of Luclin we did a pretty good job. We picked up some nice drops were good in Hate this week! Congrats to everyone who picked up loot!
 

Plane of Fear

 

Great job to everyone that was at Fear! The break in went pretty well, without a major wipe out. WOOT! Thanks to Paratakin for jumping in and volunteering to handling all points. We picked up lots loot in Fear. Congrats to Kroeten, Numscullidor, Kithmayrn, Humedorf and anyone that picked up loot!

Sunday - December 1, 2001

Screenshots

 

I will update Screenshots this week =o). Check out pictures from Kael, ToFS and Hate. If you have screenshots email them to me!

 

Plane of Hate

 

Great job to everyone that was at Hate! Much better job of listening and assisting! With 6 groups we cleared out the first floor, Maestro and that nasty hand. Thanks to Kroeten for getting everyone up there! Thanks to Gneiss for handling all the loot and Sarkisian for resing us with his fancy click-stick as well!  We picked up lots of gear for clerics and several pieces for the other classes. Slinkity, Deniden and Paackets, who picked up the book of souls, all got items for their epics.

 

 

Dragon Necropolis

 

Well, I think everyone found a new favorite zone. =o) We had two groups in DN on Saturday to play with some spiders. Akithmayrn did an awesome job of pull those nasty things. Their teleport spell was really annoying though, I was considering rooting myself, and not on accident this time. Hopefully next time we can get an enchanter to come with us and tackle some ratmen. 

 

 

Soulfire

 
Congratulations to Nazarreth who picked his Soulfire this weekend. I heard he went LD just before he was trading in his items. Luckily he didn't lose any of them after he came back into the game. He has now completed one third of his epic completed.
