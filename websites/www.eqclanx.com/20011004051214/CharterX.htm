Clan X was born as a group effort from citizens of Norrath who wanted a guild in which they could fight alongside one another, gather great loot, be able to level at a reasonable pace, and above all, HAVE FUN! For these reasons, Clan X has NOT been designed with a complex ranking structure or sets of intricate rules and regulations for membership. Instead, members are there to pursue their own goals while helping their fellow Clan X members to pursue theirs. The following list is the basic outline of how the guild exists in Norrath, with a few important rules for conduct within Clan X. (Please note that all of this information is very different for Dwarven Clerics. Their charter can be found at http://www.Igetlosteverytenminutes.com )
  • This guild was created with the basic premise that every member has an equal say in how our guild operates. The newest recruit has an equal say as the "technical" leader of the guild. One person - one vote shall apply for ALL Clan X members (obvious exception to dwarves).
       
  • All members shall act in a mature way to reflect the true integrity of Clan X. This applies to all forms of communication, whether in guild chat, group chat, or external EQ forms of communication like e-mail and message boards. Remember that you show off that guild tag wherever you go in Norrath! No Clan X member shall use foul, belligerent or disrespectful language in guild chat. Name-calling will not be tolerated.
        
  • Always offer support to fellow members of Clan X whenever one is encountered in transit.
        
  • All alternate characters shall remain known to members of Clan X.
        
  • No Clan X member shall ever put a citizen of Norrath ahead of a fellow Clan mate.
        
  • Anon shall not be used by any Clan X member. Rather use the Roleplaying switch.
        
  • No Clan X member shall kill steal or train any individual, group and or guild. Any such member will be considered for removal from the guild.
        
  • Clan X currently recruits levels 30 or higher, all classes, and all races (the officers insisted on including dwarves here - BAH!). In addition, any of your alternate characters can be recruited at level 25.  Keep a look out for other recruits even if you are not an officer - if you think they are valuable, then most likely the guild will feel the same way.
        
  • A majority vote is required to pass and/or amend the charter.
  • Need Before Greed (NBG) is the standard loot policy for the raids and events that the Clan attends.   Basically this means it is class specific - if you can use the item and WILL use the item (meaning it is better than what you already have), then you are allowed to roll on it.  The only people eligible to roll on this loot are the toons currently attending the raid, and toons that can use the item.  However it does not mean that this system is mandatory.  This will be the default looting system for all Clan X raids and power leveling sessions UNLESS it is agreed prior to the raid that a different system will be used for THAT RAID.
        
  • Officers shall make all attempts to make themselves available when real life permits. While officers do not have more authority than others in the guild, there are specific responsibilities and behaviors that an officer must display.  Please see the responsibilities of an officer.
