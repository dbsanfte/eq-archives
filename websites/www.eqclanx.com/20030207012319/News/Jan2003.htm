Busy week in the world of Clan X. :

January 10, 2003

Congratulations to Clan X as they rallied together and took out Vhaksiz the Shade in Plane Of Nightmare. Steam got the Heart for the caster's book quest, Alexedia won the Flame Engraved Torque (wrist), Chesterr won the Disembowler, and Lonedor won the Etheral Parchment.  Thanks to all who came and helped with the success of this PoN Fun Night.

  

January

Clan X ran and killed Lodi, congrats to all who got pieces off him.

       

Clan X wants to wish everyone a Happy New Year.  And what a way to bring in the New Year,  but to do FIVE epics in ONE DAY!  The guild started out the day with doing three Ragefires in a row for Himeo, Heallary, and Aurorah.  That took just under two hours to do all three. The next stop was the Undead Bard in Sebelis.  Vortigern logged in just in time to take charge of that raid and we successfully helped Marriet complete her Bard Epic. Last but NOT LEAST, Sjena (Marriet's wife) was able to complete her Rogue Epic.  She had a rocky go with it the other day as her final no drop piece off the General was destroyed by an unkown Wizard while she was on her way back to loot it from her turn in. Sjena  kept her chin up and all came out fine, way to go.
