2.  Our Timeline for the next 6 months: PoP Progression as well as guild balance of activities.
 
NOTE:  Clan X is using this timeline as a baseline for advancement.  The overall goal is to make sure we take out each progression mob / zone 3-5 times before moving on to the next mob / zone.  The pace of the advancement of Clan X is based on the capability of it's members and our successes.  So don't be surprised if the dates are modified on a regular basis based on our progress in game.  Thanks.

Priority 1 - Progression
To satisfy our progression-eager members with progressing through more difficult game content. This is primarily PoP focused progression.
(# of times we plan on attempting for the week, spawn time of mob)
Priority 2 - Rotations
To take out rotation mobs that we have previously succeeded on.
Priority 3 - FFA Mob Focus
To satisfy raid-eager members with challenging mobs that continue to drop upgrades for majority of guild.  These will include:
-- Challenging Mobs we have defeated before: DS, RC, Rhags, IV, Evolved Burrower, Va'dyn, Xanamach, BoT Towers, Velketor, Venril Sathir, Vindi, Garudon, etc. etc.
-- Challenging Mobs we have not yet defeated: Tormax, THO, Dain, Shei, Tunare, Seru, and many others.  This includes "New" mobs that the End Game Team (EGT) has planned for us to take out.
Balance - Time Off
Or, time off from raiding.  Time every week made available to members to do what they wish ... LDoN adventures, exp grind, tradeskills, chatting, etc.  Do whatever YOU like to do in game.
