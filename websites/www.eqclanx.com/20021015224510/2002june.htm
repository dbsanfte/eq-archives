Will Clan X win 10k in pp?

We are doing anything we can to help our members gain exp in May and June. This is not about powerleveling, but about doing all we can as a team to level together. As part of providing this, we are going to be hanging out in some of the higher level zones in Norrath: Sebelis, Velketor’s Lab, Howling Stones (Charsis), Chardok, Burning Woods, and The Hole.

10,000 pp will be donated to the guild fund on June 30th if we have in the guild one toon from each class level 57 or higher. In addition, 500pp will be given to the highest-level toon per class if this goal is reached (ties will be random rolled).
News for JUNE

 

June 2002  

Last week was a HUGE week for Clan X.

We had several failed attempts for Gneiss's bard epic kill of Trakanon in Sebelis, but we all know THAT DRAGON WILL DIE.

 

Thursday June 27th, 2002

With the help of Keepers Of Oak the Rathe Alliance rocked Chardok!  We successfully got two Kings for the Cleric epic, one Queen for the Warrior epic, the Prince for the Enchanter epic, and finally we were able to get the monk epic piece. Allurra and Denithor looted the Cleric pieces,  Geoo got the Monk piece, and Selory got the Warrior piece. Congrats to all.

 

Clan X epic's completed during the last few weeks:

Axxe: Beastlord

Nukok: Shaman

Vegi: Ranger Swiftwind sword


Sunday, June 6th,  2002 - GLANOIS

 

Saturday night The Rathe Alliance owned fear again.  Cleared in appx. 5 1/2 hours.  Although still n00bs, we do manage to pull off a feat or two.  Once  again, thanks to Tashim and Slinkity for coming out,  as well as other member of Keepers whom I may have forgot.  Break in was flawless and we litterally had minimal deaths.  We managed to take down Dread and Enraged as well and who knows what else if given the chance.  CT...Well, we talked to his secretary (I used to date her) and she said he couldn't come out to be Pwned because he was on the john, and that that could take ALL DAY!
