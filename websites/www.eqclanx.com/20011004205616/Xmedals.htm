The 100 Platinum Piece Award

This award is given to Clan X Members in honor of their addiction to EverQuest.  To achieve status as an EQ addict, you must have achieved over 100 levels in Clan X between your primary and alternate characters.  The following people have earned the title Hopeless EQ Addict and have all received 100 platinum pieces from Clan X.

Blacksunshine 145 levels
Deevine 103 levels
Gileus 100 levels
Laramie 112 levels
Quickling 122 levels
