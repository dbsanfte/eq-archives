"Black and Blue, Rixma and YOU"
What the heck is THAT????!!!!

Once each month we are going to have a Clan X get together - ANYTHING goes at this event. What are we going to do? EVERYTHING!!! Language fair, trading of items and spells, duels, discussions of future strategies, treasure hunts, skill building, races, and of course, ganging up on Rixma so YOU TOO can say you slayed her.  Just show up and bring what you want to accomplish, whether it is to fight, learn something new, discuss strategies or vision, or just get to know your guild family.

 
Will Clan X win 35,000 in pp?
YES the contest continues through August 15th!!

We are doing anything we can to help our members gain exp in July and August. This is not about powerleveling, but about doing all we can as a team to level together. As part of providing this, we are going to be hanging out in some of the higher level zones in Norrath: Sebelis, Velketor’s Lab, Howling Stones (Charsis), Chardok, Burning Woods, and The Hole.

20,000 pp will be donated to the guild fund on August 15th if we have in the guild one toon from each class level 57 or higher and 6 toons in the guild at level 60. In addition, 1,000 pp will be given to the highest-level toon per class if this goal is reached (ties will be random rolled).
 
August is Also ARMOR QUEST MONTH!
Raids will be set up this month specifically to help you upgrade your gear.  Loot rules are Clan X NBG loot rules until we get our new loot rules established.

What is armor quest night?  The raids will focus mostly on getting Kael drops for Thurg quest armor.  We start there.  If Kael is packed, we will quickly move the raid to Western Wastes to work on Benevolence Quests.  If this also is camped, we will work toward killing priests in Wakening Lands.  Not 3 raids in one day, but whatever is available and a priority for members.
