The following items are listed in order of priority.

1.   Smart Grouping: Use your nightly groupings wisely. Basically we are looking for folks to spend their time leveling in areas that actually benefit in more then just expo itself. Whether this is collecting spell research components, 3rd tier key collecting, getting triggers for PoI Dragon, doing CT for drops that folks still want, etc.....we need to start killing 2 birds with 1 stone.
     Tawro/Radir in UP to increase weaponry.  Good place to do this for rogues, then melee.
     Collecting PoP items for Jewelcraft
     Juggs/Protector/Emp/GuardianWurms for high level spells

Get to level 60. You want to do the boss mobs? We need the levels for us to grow. What do levels give you? Resistance to AoE, opens up new spell lines, more HP/Mana, changes your agro ability, allows nukes to hit better.

Trials will be conducted impromptu as people achieve level 60.  No more scheduled PoJ flagging days.

5.   Raiding: 
     Derakor the Vindicator, Ring of Fire - Every Monday in April and May
     Statue of Rallos Zek, King Tormax, or Grieg - to challenge our guild members

6.   ToV Armor: 
 ToV armor raids scheduled 3 times a month on the first 3 Wednesdays of the month.

7.   Class Needs: 
 Keep an eye out for Shamans, Necros, Monks, Wizards, and Enchanters either in recruiting new people or in leveling our alt toons.

Sky scheduled for End of March - purpose to get Necromancer camped safely on Island 7.

     Vindi / Ring of Fire - 1 day per week (every Monday - guaranteed Raiding Day)
