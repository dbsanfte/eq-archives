Thank you for taking an interest in Joining Clan X, an EverQuest guild located on the Rathe server.

At this time, Clan X is only recruiting on a case-by-case basis.  The process for joining our guild requires dedication from the people who are looking to become members.  If you are serious about joining a guild that values teamwork and FUN raiding experiences, then you must prove yourself before the officers will formally invite you.

  • Let one of our officers know of your interest in joining Clan X. Give us a tell in game, or email us at eqclanxofficers@yahoogroups.com.  Within the email you send us, please list your email address, your class, your level, and why you want to join Clan X.  Please don't forget to include the email address so that one of our friendly staff can get back to you about your application.
  • You must join Clan X raiding and group events WITHOUT the guild tag for a period of 3-4 weeks.  Let us see how you fight, work with groups - we need to get to know you, and you need to get to know us.  To find out what we are doing and where we will be, check out our calendar.
  • You must have a list of 5 Clan X members that will nominate you for an invite at the end of the 3-4 week grouping period.  These members do NOT have to be officers, but you need them all to be able to speak for you.
  • We accept all peoples, all classes, all races.  Our only requirement is that your primary character is level 30 or higher.  Any alternates that you may wish to add to Clan X must be level 25 before they can be guilded.

We realize that the process for getting into Clan X is not an easy one.  If you want easy, there are MANY other guilds in Norrath to choose from.  On the other hand, if you wish to become part of a close-knit family that is very capable of raiding the Planes and higher-level zones, then these strict requirements of getting in will ultimately serve you well.

Clan X is a guild in Norrath that takes pride in the people that we recruit - we invite honorable and teamwork-driven citizens to pursue membership in our distinguished guild.
