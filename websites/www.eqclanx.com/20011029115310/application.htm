Thank you for taking an interest in Clan X!

Clan X is a guild in Norrath that takes pride in the people that we recruit - we invite honorable and teamwork-driven citizens of Norrath to apply for our distinguished guild.  We accept all peoples, all classes, all races.  Our only requirement is that your primary character is level 30 or higher.  Any alternates that you may wish to add to Clan X must be level 25 before they can be guilded.

Please fill out the information below so that we can learn more about you.  It is imperative that you group with some of our Clan X members in battle and have an officer review your application before we can formally invite you into our family.

Once we have had a chance to group with you and review your information, the officers will make their recruiting decisions.  We do formal recruiting every other Thursday, and an officer will contact you once you have been selected for Clan X (see our calendar for information on recruiting dates).  Thank-you for considering Clan X, and safe journeys in Norrath!
