September:
WE DID IT!!!!! We killed Teris Thule on September 29, 2003. Congratulations to Clan X.

 

Last Thursday Clan X went CRAZY and killed SIX FFA mobs...one right after another... wonder how many got whiplash!

 

With the new patches and the new LoDN Adventure the guild has been busy checking out what they offer. Congratulations to all those who have gotten some new gear and some fun experiences.

 

Clan X has been busy lately doing BOT tower raids on the mini bosses, congratulations to those who got the "COOL" loot! 

 

We attempted to do Theris Thule, and got her down about 35%, NEXT time she will not like us coming into the zone as we won't leave her standing.

 

With the new key changes in the Plane of Sky, Clan X took a fast sweep up the islands getting two pally epics done on the way.

 

Congratulations to Joslynn on her Paladin Epic turn in, and on our guest Paladin too.

 

Clan X also took a nice trip into Seru to see what the Lord in that zone looked like.  Will be heading back there soon!
