Chapter 1 The life of a lonely Lizard

My first memories are of fighting my way out of this great big round prison, my teeth were of little use against this porcelain monster. Scratch scratch , with all my might but I couldn’t break free. Then this feeling as if I lost my stomach. What was happening to me? Peck Peck Peck. " hello? help I am trapped in here please let me out it is dark and I am scared I want my mother". LIGHT! What is this foul creature looking down at me? A Stoneleer Cockatrice? ACK why is it trying to eat me? Finally I am out, Run Run pump those little Lizard legs. Heart beating soooo fast. I’ll never make it she is closing in. What is that? He looks like me pump pump maybe he can help me if I can reach him in time. "Another victory for the guards of Kunark" "Thank you I was almost eaten" " Be careful little one this place is not safe for scrawny ones such as you, go see Master Brain he will help you". "Hail Master Brain" "Hello little one do you wish to Learn the basics of fighting?"

He taught me so much in the years following that day. The years seemed to past like days. Soon I was bigger and taller. I was so proud of the length of my tail. My scales were shiny and I wore the markings on my head with pride. I soon made a little fortune delivering mail for the Troopers and doing little errands for the shop keepers. I acquired new clothes and weapons. I was practicing my skills all the time. I made new friends but still felt an emptiness in my heart wondering what had happened to my mother? Did I have brothers and sisters? I met an old wise lizard on day while hunting in the swamp. He said to me "Hello what are you doing out here all alone?" "Hunting" I replied. "What is your name scrawny one?" baffled I couldn’t reply. "I never had a name sir, everyone has always called me little one" "Well child u cannot go through life without a proper name. Hmmm let me see. Something not to common but that will suite you. I know your name will be Jackiet. It is different and I think it suits you perfectly". Wow I had a name I loved that name, I bowed before him and thanked him and scurried along my way. I was tired of being alone, every one hates my kind, why? Just because we are reptiles and cold blooded does not mean we are mean. I needed friends a family I could call my own.

Then one day I meet a male Iksar named Leezord, my breath caught in my throat. He was so dashing and handsome. My face lit up with embarrassment when he spoke to me. I was so afraid, but he was kind and invited me to accompany him to hunt in Lake of Ill Omen. We went together and fought many battles against tigers and Sanark and Goblins. Growing closer day by day. One day while hunting we were separated in a place called Frontier Mountains. I was lost and scared and was being chased by a giant when I ran smack into another person. He was much different looking than I. I was scared I didn’t know if he was going to try and hurt me to. He smiled at me and helped bandage my wounds. He told me of great stories about his family Clan X. I listened with excitement as he told me of how they love all creatures. (Was this it ? what I had been waiting for? To fit in? To be loved? To be accepted?) He finally reveled his name to me it was Gress. He asked me to join his family and promised me I had nothing to fear. So I went with him and joined his clan. Sadly realizing I had forgotten all about my friend Leezord. But I was happy now my new found family was so wonderful. And then one day while I was in a shop in town I saw him. My long lost love. Looking as handsome as ever. I approached him and said "Hello Leezord remember me?" He dropped his bag and began to dance with me. I introduced him to my family and they accepted him with open arms. In December Leezord and I were married ..........that is another chapter.
