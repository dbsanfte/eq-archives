NEWS

March 24 - 30, 2003

  • What's all the Buzz about? Sky Part 2...
    • A CX first happened this week as a small determined group decided to test the bounds of their ability and see how far they can get in the Plane of Sky. Well even with their dwindling numbers towards the end of the Raid, they managed to get to the 7th island in 7 hours with as few as 14 in the end to take down the 6th island Mini Boss.
      AMAZING Work and congratz to those who participated.
  • HoT looses its pants
    • CX decided to leave the underwears for the gnomes and went straight for the pants as the guild picked up 4 plate greaves drops this week along with a Plate and Leather BP.
  • Walking in Circles in PoN
    • CX had it's first Hedge Quest night where over 20 people were sent running around after a lost NPC just to run into this guy at the end...

      But no worries, CX was able to take him down twice and get many their "Crypt of Terris Thule" flag.

 

March 17 - 23, 2003

  • What's that in the Sky?
    • CX was in the Plane of Sky this past weekend checking out the view from above. An over all successful raid which CX made it to the 6th island, the guild managed to pick up many nice pieces including 3 drops of the Wingblade for the Warrior Epic.
  • Questing Success...
    • A large Congratz goes out to Moogg this week for completing his Warrior Epic quest... just don't call him Fatty anymore... it's Jedi Fatty to all of you now!!!
    • Double the fun
      Emberglow and Kinadorea teemed up this week to go after some epic mobs in Timorrous Deep. Kinadorea shattered her second to last mob for her epic. The group quickly followed up for Ember as the guppy dragon known as Fay was taken down to finish off her Druid Epic.

      Congratulations to both of you.
  • Welcome...
    • A little overdue welcome to Slomoe... we welcome you to the family.

 

March 10 - 16, 2003

  • Drops Galore in HoT

    • The drops kept on coming this week in ToV... seems that the halls where in a very giving mood as a total of 25 pieces of armor dropped in one sitting.
      Congratulations go to Kwah on getting the Silk BP which turned into this beauty.

      As well to Sjena for getting the Chain BP...
      Congratulations to all of you.

  • Questing Success

    • Nortlav the Scalekeeper went down to CX this week granting Renalden to get another step closer to his Warrior Epic!!!

    • SithVicious completed 8 months of questing for the "Iksar Shaman Epic". The road was tough, but the 2 rewards at the end of the journey are well worth the trouble.
      SithVicious is now the keeper of Venril Sathirs' Soul which is entrapped in this amulet.

      As well as the main reward given to him as a gift by Rile himself for feeing his spirit

      SithVicious is only the 4th on the Rathe server to have completed this quest, joining fellow guild member Tossen as one of the proud to have this piece.

 

March 1 - 9, 2003

  • Smile For the Camera

    • Seems that a drafty "Lair" has come on the market for any of you interested in getting an apartment.
      It seems that the apartment was recently vacated by the deceased dragon Trakanon and his little undead bard.

      Congratulations to Beebopp on completing her Epic with this kill.

  • Severilous finally get's to see CX again...

    • Clan X's Turn on the Severilous rotation has come and gone quickly as a few groups took him down as soon as they heard he was up.

      Congratulation to Moogg on the green scale for the Warrior Epic...

  • General Revisited

    • Congratulations to Suparog and Gnipgnop on getting a force together and taking out the General ftwo times for the Rogue Epic on Friday night.

  • Welcome

    • We would like to say welcome to Rezzidor on joining the family.
