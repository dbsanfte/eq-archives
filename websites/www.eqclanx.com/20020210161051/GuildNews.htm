WTG Ainwen

 

Congrats to Ainwen who COMPLETED HIS EPIC this week! As reported by Uphoria who was in LOIO for the GM event which allowed Ainwen to pick up the final piece needed for his epic:

 

"It all began when through out the zone there was a ROOOAAR. I was logged on as my alt so when I saw the yell I quickly changed on to Uphoria. When she came on the next shout was "BEWARE of the Lake!" So, naturally the entire zone ran to the lake. Then Ainwen who was also in the zone began to shout
that he could see Fay out over the lake. I ran around and around the lake like a chicken with my head cut off until finally I met up with Nazarreth. I FD'ed beside him it was right then that I saw Fay come out of the water and head away from us! Well she began to eat people and as she ate people she began to GROW! She actually got to be the size length wise of a giant. It was then that BoTS and other high level charaters got together to defeat the dragon. It was quite a sight to see!"

 

I bet it was!! Thanks for the update and congrats again Ainwen.
 

The Hole

 

Thanks to everyone who came out to assist in the Hole. Here's a breakdown of the evening as reported by Glanois:

 

"Excellent work by everyone who worked on Yarrl and Tilamdar's epic pieces on Thursday night.  With a group of about 25, and Slinkity in the lead, we started the raid.  We were a bit late but very effective as we made it down to the past the castle.  We ran into a bit of trouble there and had a near wipe. Fan Faire

 

And now, an update from our man on the streets, Yarrl:

 

"The January Fan Faire was held at the Adams Mark Hotel in beautiful down town Dallas. In attendance were Yarrl, Kristal, and Zarmog from Clan X. Also there were several members of our newest alliance hopefuls, Everlasting Alliance. They were Co-leader Denithor, officers Ryne, Auriea, Crystalweave, and member Sanjac. It was my honor to meet and spend time with the Rathe server GM Vaylin. He is a very nice guy and it was fun to have dinner with him on Friday night. I also met some members of Sacracant Legion including the leader "wrong way Amy" as she is lovingly referred to.

 

Together, all of the attendees from the Rathe came in third place in the "live quest". We were all very proud of ourselves for this accomplishment. As it turns out we only lost by 5gp. It was a lot of fun running around the hotel talking to NPC's and solving puzzles and riddles. It turns out that Denithor is very good at word jumbles and Auriea is quite the riddle solver. In all, it was a wonderful weekend and I am looking forward to the next Fan Fair. Cheers to Verant for a job well done!"

 

More Epic Success

 

Clan X you did an AMAZING job with epics last month - keep it up!!! To continue the great progress on epics, we travel to take down Thrackin Griffen for Deevine and Ssenniug! We were able to take him very quickly. Unfortunately there was a bug with Dee's bind and she had to pass the first loot to Ssenniug.. lucky, lucky ;o). Before the second fight we had a bit of a scare as Dee could not find the NPC she needed, it seemed she had despawned. We waited about 20 minutes and as we were packing up camp Thrackin appeared!! We took him down again and Dee was able to loot the piece for her epic. It turned out another guild had turned in items to the NPC as they did not know we were doing the spawn. Thrackin's third lashing came as we assisted Zondervan from Legend of Marr in his epic quest. Great job Clan X! Congrats to Deevine, Ssenniug, and Zondervan.

 

PoH

 

We traveled to the Plane of Hate on Friday night. With members from Clan X, our alliance, and friends we had about 50 people in PoH. It was a little shaky to begin with as we had several friends with us who had never traveled to the planes. Everyone fell in line though and at about 2:30am we got the first floor cleared! We picked up some GREAT loot in PoH. There's so much I couldn't even keep up with it. The top 3 drops were a new BP for Healary, and the Hand, needed for the warrior epic, for Dubas AND Saleb.
