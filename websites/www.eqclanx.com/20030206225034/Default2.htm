NEWS

January 27- Feb. 2, 2003
 Clan X Headlines...
(Click the Headline to Expand for News and Screenshots)

  • The Raiding Continues!!!

    • POP FLAG NIGHT!!!
      We had 8 memebers get flagged in our first official Flag night. This doesn't even include others that were flagged earlier in the week. Watch out Storms... we're coming to make a mess of your mud piles!
      Please look for other Flag nights coming up on the Calendar.

    • HoT
      HoT had another bad night as CX took the mobs down easily to capture 5 more pieces of equipment.

    • Hate? We still doing that?
      Can Someone say BP?... turns out the loot leader said it a few times. I believe the last count was 6 chest pieces from Hate dropped on Friday. The raid also was rewarded with Epic drops which included:
      Mage: Steam
      Enchanter: Jhayde and Reyena
      Paladin: Ekondo
      No Ranger piece dropped much to the displeasure to some of our memebers... Inny decided to be a bit of a cheap skate..,

  • Welcome to the family !!

    • We would like to welcome Kinadorea to our Family.

  • Questing Success...

    • First the Bad news...
      Sithvicious, who is one kill from completing the Iksar shaman cudgel quest, had some misfortune of not having the turn in NPC spawn for him during a recent Charisis Raid... better luck next time... just lucky it's only a spawn.

    • The Good News...
      Drussela was up for a little beating during that same raid. Gratz to Sulpicious in picking up his last piece of a Chardok quest. We can expect him to be wearing this beautiful item after a bit of faction killing.

    • And the Award for Perseverance Goes to...
      Joslynn who this week, after dragon kill after dragon kill, was able to pick up the Frost book for her Paladin Epic.

      Gratz, keep up the good work!!!

  • Talendor and Inny get Facials !!!.

    • Inny got a much needed facelift as CX rearranged it using the latest in plastic surgery techniques. Well, what ever mess we did make of it, it can't be worse than before!!
      He actually didn't tip very well. We were all hoping to get the Ranger Epic piece from him... looks like we're just going to have to do a better job next time... and you all know what happens to people who don't tip!!!

    • Talendor Who?
      A couple of groups of CXers rushed over to take down the Fire Dragon. Unfortunately, no epic drops this time, but no worries, Talendor can expect a whooping any time we find him up.
