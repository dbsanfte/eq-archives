Clan X first meeting - 22 Feb, 2001

The Clan got together recently and conducted their first meeting.  Several things were discussed at this gathering:

  • Discussions about a one-month probationary period for new recruits to see if they fit in with the Clan X family.

  • Passed amendment to the charter about recruiting - Clan X will only be recruiting levels 15 or higher until further notice. 

  • Discussed the issues around trying to balance the classes within the guild - although nothing formal was decided, Clan X members are encouraged to look for classes we need in the guild.  If someone approaches an officer about being recruited and we are not currently guilding their class, say something along these lines:

    • We are always looking for good people but we are generally looking for specific classes right now - hang with us and let Clan X  vote you in when we begin recruiting that class again <paraphrased from Harrison>

  • Discussed how current members can become officers within Clan X.  At the meeting, three of our members showed an interest in becoming officers:  Gandlug, Garbuk, and Hendrex.  These members must achieve a 75% vote by rest of the guild in order to achieve officer status.  They must also understand ALL that is required of them as mentioned in the project charter.  The current officers will discuss this process further and get back to Clan X on next course of action.

  • Moritune Mystictone was unable to make it for the Clan X photo shoot, so he has decided to take on a project of his own.  He will be wandering around Norrath taking shots of Clan X members and submitting them to the web site - that means he just might catch you taking down that nasty sand giant in Oasis.  On the other hand, he just might catch you doing something nasty or sleazy - EYES WILL BE WATCHING YOU!

Feb 4, 2001 - Crystal Caverns

Our trip to Crystal Caverns did not go so well - in fact it didn't go at all!  Most of the Clan X members died on the way to the raid, and we managed to kill people from other guilds along with us.  Scrillo has contacted Clan X about rescheduling the raid, and will either post the dates on our message board or theirs (Band of the Black Hand).

Feb 3, 2001 - Fire Giants

Clan X and Brotherhood of Honor showed true courage in their battle against the Fire Giants of Sol B.  They all fell to our feet eventually, although the giants did take a few of us out with them.  We had external assistance from Crimsonfist, a 55 monk who helped us in the pulling process.  Thanks to Crimson and BoH for showing us a good time!

Members of Clan X that helped us rid Norrath of these vile creatures:  Quickling, Merther, Harrison, Elgor, Gileus, and Rixma.  In the process of taking them down, Harrison was awarded Mithril Arms - GONGRATS Harrison!

It is after the Fire Giants went down that Sol B started laughing at Clan X and its army.  Lord Nagafen grinned as he heard the battle plans brewing just a room away, a mere 26 person militia was no match for him.  But Clan X and the Brotherhood of Honor do not learn from their mistakes, and were determined to take him down regardless of casualties.  An army of 40 people was assembled to take him out now!  And we would not be defeated!!!!  Lord Nagafen could be heard howling in triumph as he splatted the last of our legion against the wall of his stronghold.
