Why form alliances?

  • To fight alongside different cultures and make long lasting relationships with a variety of Norrath citizens. 

  • To  make it possible for members of Clan X to obtain their epic quest items due to the sheer numbers in the alliance.

  • To form raids on the Planes of Hate, Fear, Sky, and Growth.
