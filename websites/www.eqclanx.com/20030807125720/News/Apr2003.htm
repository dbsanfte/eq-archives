April 21-28, 2003

  • And the Award for Perseverance goes to....

    • Congratulations goes out to Vegi, who after multiple kills of our favorite God of Hate, finally got the last piece of his epic.
      Great work and perseverance... at times of frustration, no one from CX backed down and all pulled together to help Vegi attain this great goal...

  • This Weeks New Faces...

    • This week saw 3 new faces arise as new members of CX. We welcome Thetmes, Kortana, and Chrometalon to the guild.

 

April 14-20, 2003

  • Grieg Meets his End

    • After one of the longest fights in CX History for any 1 mob... Grieg finally succumbed to the might of Clan X.

      This puts CX on it's First Luclin Rotation Mob and Second Rotation mob addition in as many weeks. The whole guild is a buzz with excitement.
      Congratulation CX on 3rd Roto Mob.

  • Welcome

    • A few new members joined us this week... we would like to welcome Senzii, Eldreath, Kayaken, Crystessia and Temptyn.

  • Vindicator

    • After a few new recruits joined the guild, we decided to celebrate and take down Vindi...
      Congratulations to Marriet on the Vindi BP.

 

April 7-13, 2003

  • CX Flushes Mr.Hanky's Brother

    • Clan X did the world a huge favor by getting rid of the dread, fear and terror of. Cazic-Thule.
      This was a huge kill for CX as it showed how well a family guild can perform and how far our guild has come in such a short time.

      Congratulations to CX on our second rotation!!!

      We relieved CT from a few of his unneeded organs afterwards.

      Mr. Hanky has vowed revenge on CX... be on the look out for a 3 inch talking poo.

  • Inny goes down

    • Clan X followed up the CT kill with another visit to Inny in PoH this week.
      And again, we took him down without any incident...
      And again no Ranger Epic Stone...

      He has not heard the last of us...
