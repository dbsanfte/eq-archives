August 2001

Saturday - 25 August, 2001

 

Clan X and friends have been very busy over the last few weeks! We have been all over Norrath…here’s a break down of the zones we have visited:

East Karana

Clan X members and friends complete a major part of a druid/ranger mini-epic. This part requires the destruction of a level 50 Dark Elf Corruptor in E. Karana. The tricky part of this is that the person doing the epic must be in a specific point in the zone to get the DE to spawn while a group must to be standing by in a different part of the zone at the DE spawn point.

Congratulations to people that completed their mini epics, which included: Ainwen - Clan X, Qyil - Clan X, Gebi - Clan X, Kinaki - Secret Order of the Phoenix, Evela - Visionaries of Fire, and Fidlo - (unknown). Thanks to those who assisted: Xanadar - Guardian Lords of Peace, Brannok - Visionaries of Fire, Vligon - (unknown), Kroeten - Clan X, and Lythia - Grey Haven.

Ultimately, the group worked well, each person knowing exactly what to do and how to do it.  Ainwen did a wonderful job showing people where they would need to run, and taking them step-by-step as through what was going to happen.

(Reported by Gebi Whitefir) 

City of Mist

Clan X moved into CoM for two days in mid August. We got off to a great start with approximately 20 Clan X members and friends…and one really big train! We racked up the loot the first night with a MoSS (Mace of the Shadowed Soul), won by Ungreivel, and the books and letters Wardoc needed for his epic.

The second night, we had approximately 30 Clan X members and friends in CoM! We took on black reavers and Lord Ghiosk twice. The final battle inside the castle was amazing --everyone did a spectacular job.

I think Wardoc explains it so well:  “For those of you who left early or were not there... Let me set the stage: 3 groups. 2 full. 1 half full. 1 Cleric, 4 Druids, 1 Shammy. NO enchanter!  We had (all at the same time mind you) not 1. not 2. but THREE Black Reavers...AND Lord Ghiosk... AND assorted greater Warboned Skeletons and such.”
We ended up losing 2 druids and a monk in the battle. but we PERSERVERD in the face of OVERWHELMING ODDS, in the end Clan X and friends stood victorious in battle. I was, am, and always will be, PROUD to be a part of this fine guild we call Clan X and am not ashamed to fight along side you anytime, anywhere.” Well said.

Wardoc got all of the CoM items for he needed for his epic. Ungrievel,  Ubercyrus and Sebastian (a friend of the Clan) were able to pick up items that they needed as well. Way to go guys and great job Clan X!

Trakanon’s Teeth

Ungrievel booked us a vacation to TT for some experience and loot. We had a great time and can back with plenty of both…especially the loot. Loriena and Vitalone were able to pick up the two medals needed to get their Seb keys. Ainwen won a MoSS, Humedorf won a Hang Man's Noose, 20% haste item for the neck, and Grim Pauldrons, 13ac 10str shoulder piece. 

MistMoore Castle

We traveled to MistMoore to pick up a few items on Serlie’s shopping list. While the critter never spawned for Serlie, we still picked up a few items and with two groups, take out plenty of other castle inhabitants. Serlie won a Diamondline Earring, Merther won an Advisor Robe, and Loriena won Werewolf Claws for the Lupine Claw Gauntlet quest.  

Burning Woods

We end the Norrath two-week tour in the Burning Woods to help Mether on items for his mage epic. Jaredd, Alexedia, Gebi, Mether, Ssenniug, Loriena, Humedorf, Elgor, Qyil, Kroeten, Bubas, Soluth, and Gandlug were in attendance. Even after 3 hours of camping Nezekena and Phurzikon (named wurms) for the burning embers Merther needed, they wouldn’t drop. We did take down Slixin Klex and Merther picked up the Dirt of the Underfoot needed for his epic.  

The big battle of the night was best explained by Soluth...”3 wurms, 2 NAMED worms, sarnak, skelie, bees…what else?” Indeed. With just three groups, NO cleric and NO enchanter, Clan X did an awesome job combating the baddies! When the druids were finally out of mana we were down to just the bees…of course, we lost several and others had to flee, but we DID a great job of holding our ground…and one day we will go back for the bees!

That's the News for now.

-- Lori
