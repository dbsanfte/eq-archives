Druid Epic

 

Congratulations to Alexedia, who is getting closer and closer to the completing her epic. She was able to take down VSR this week. Well, not by herself, she had a little help. Also,Jaredd received the second stone but chose to give it to Cobo, a ranger from GotF. Jaredd hasn't completed the DE corrupter yet, so he was kind enough to pass on it. 

 

Plane of Fear

Thanks to Jesters of Fate for the invite to Fear this week. We had a good turn out, 6 groups made up of members from different guilds. We had some trouble breaking in, we started with three groups entering but there were some issues and the groups were wiped out. You know, I used to think the funniest thing was when we would raid CoM with 4 or more groups of nude toons clearing a path to the rez point for the clerics. It's not =o). The funniest thing is 6 groups entering Fear, 3 of those groups nude, carrying weapons looted from specs and skeletons near the portal room trying to clear a path to the camp.

Even better than that is that we actually made it to the camp and were doing pretty well till we got trained 4 or 5 times. Remember guys, if you get TP'ed away from the camp by a scareling: 1) STOP and get a loc. 2) Don't move, just turn and look. Can you see the camp? If yes, get a direction. This will help the poor smuck, err... monk who has to run around the zone gathering corpses. 3) JUST DIE! Don't come trotting back to the camp all happy cause you lived. Because, even though you don't see them, there are probably 3 or more baddies just waiting to see which way you run.

The Hole

This week included a few trips to The Hole for Wardoc's epic! It was the first time for several people. We had a great turn out!  We weren't able to pick up the item Wardoc needed, but we will try again Sunday night. Kedge Keep

Friday we swam to KK to help Stimpsonn kill Phinny. We had about 10 people help camp. We even got free food and beer! We didn't actually kill anything but had a lot of fun! Rixma Completes Her Epic

Congratulations to Rixma for completing her epic. After killing Phinny on Friday she finished up the her epic and is the proud owner of her very own Ragebringer. *shivers at the thought of messing with Rixma*. WTG Rixi!

Who's Idea Was This Anyway?

The long awaited trip to the Plane of Hate came on Saturday. With a crew of about 50, we headed up. Hate was definitely a learning experience. I really hope that those that were there will remember what they learned! The old 'Clan X Learning Curve' was in full effect. I can only compare Hate to when we started in CoM. We used to have a lot of trouble in CoM.. now we kick CoM butt! Same thing for Hate, we started slow but got better and in the end we were doing pretty well, considering this was the first planes raid for most.

Special thanks to Akithmayrn and Falran! Thanks for pulling for us guys...awesome job!

We took down a few named mobs including Magi P'tasa, Grandmaster R'tal, and Maestro of Ran. We also picked up some nice loot! Congratulations to: Drawnir - on a lute from the Maestro, helm and arms; Elgor - BP; Blacksunshine - Arms; Bubas - Whip; Merther - Robe and bracer; Cieladon - BP; Kristale - Bracers; Gandlug - helm; and Akiro - greavers. Wanna know the stats? Me too ;o) so if you see these people in game make sure and ask them!
