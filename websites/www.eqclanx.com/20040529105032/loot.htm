ACQUIRING POINTS ON CLAN X SPONSORED EVENTS/RAIDS

Defining a Clan X sponsored Event

Clan X sponsored events are any raids/epic fights that are placed on the calendar or any event that is considered to be impromptu such as a FFA mob/Rotation or an epic encounter announced openly to the guild and requiring 9 or more Clan X members to successfully kill.

Any and all raids requiring and having  9 or more CX members will be worth points as long as it is announced to the guild and not restricted to a certain number.

Note: If the nature of the raid restricts you to the number of attendance, it is no longer eligible for points. (i.e.: Hedge Quest - max 18 people)


Attendance and Points

Attendance is taken at the scheduled start time of the event, every hour thereafter, and at the end of the event. 

If Final Attendance is within 15 minutes of previous Attendance then the Final Attendance is disregarded and the previous Attendance counts as the Final Attendance.

One Attendance shall be taken for a scheduled raid that is called off due to low numbers, class makeup issues, or server instability. The Attendance for such an event will be taken in the same manner as the first Attendance of a scheduled raid but will occur at the time the raid is called off. The raid should be called off within one hour of scheduled time.

One point will be awarded for every Attendance of a CX sponsored event at which the Member is present. 

Points will be tallied PER USER and that tally will be available to all toons under that user.

For Scheduled Raids - First attendance shall be taken at the exact time the raid is scheduled on the calendar. 

For FFA/Impromptu Epic Mob Raids - First attendance is taken when the FFA or Epic Mob is engaged or the raid has begun to clear mobs.

 

USING YOUR POINTS TOWARD GEAR DROPPED AT CX RAIDS

Loot Coordinator (LC) will be 2 designated CX members per raid.
LC will make announcement over raid channel that gear has dropped.
Designated raid looters are responsible for sending LC's a tell of loot that has dropped.
LC's announcement will have to include a link of the item.
LC will make 3 calls for each item dropped. (initial, 2nd, Final)
If no one has placed a bid on an item after the 3rd call that item now belongs to the guild bank. All Droppable items will be posted on the MB under “Guild Auction”. The post will include the Item (link if possible) and the ending date and time of the bidding session. Each bidding session will be a 1 week duration. This item is now eligible for EVERYONE to BID on (non attendee’s, alts and duel boxers). We expect everyone to bid only on items they plan on using. Any individual found to have bid on an item to be sold in the bazaar for cash, shall be reprimanded with possibility of guild removal. All No-drop items that are not bid on, will be left to rot. Exception is class specific gear that can be traded in for gems. Those will be looted by the LC and the gem given to the guild bank.
Players will submit their bids of points to the LC in /tell.
Bids will be kept blind (No announcement of who bid what) so you may change your bid at any time prior to the Final Call.
Players must bid AT LEAST 1 point. NO free loot, not even when bidding from the bank. Even if you are the only one of your class in attendance 1 point must still be bid to loot the item. (No drop or not)
Your points will be deducted from your accumulative total ONLY if you are the highest bidder for the item.
You MAY win more then once per raid as long as you continue to win bids and not run out of accumulated points.
ONLY points accumulated PRIOR to the current raid can be used.
In the event that you have a ZERO points balance, you are the only one interested in the dropped loot and you are able to use the item, your points balance may go into the negative by 1 point for each piece of loot you obtain.
Clan X members need to take a NBG (Need Before Greed) mentality when bidding on items and may only bid on items for the character that is present on the current raid.

 

LOOT COORDINATOR RESPONSIBILITIES

You must have a points total available for the entire raid - Current Participation Points.
You will have to deduct points from a player on the list at the time they have successfully used their points.
The 2 LC's for the raid must be of opposite class so that the other LC can successfully bid before other bids are placed.
IF there is a case that both LC's can use the item in question, they are to send either the GL (first choice) or another officer their bid BEFORE they announce that an item has dropped and all others have a chance to bid on the item.
ONLY the LC that is taking bids may announce the WINNER over chat after the final call has been made.
LC bids are a one shot deal (this is to not give LC an advantage over upping bid after seeing others).
LC MUST post the results of points deducted IMMEDIATELY after they log off for the day.

 

ATTENDANCE TAKER RESPONSIBILITIES

Must have pen and paper or other form of making hard copy prior to beginning of raid.
Is responsible for tallying points for those in attendance.
MUST post the results for points tallied IMMEDIATELY after they log off for the day.
Is NOT responsible for taking attendance for those that DO NOT send in a tell stating their arrival until they have done so if they join the raid late.

 

POSTING OF POINTS ACQUIRED & DEDUCTED

ATs and LCs will post points acquired and deducted on the message board.
Webmasters for Clan X will track all points on the Loot Spreadsheet.
Will be divided by class so that everyone knows what everyone else has.
An ALL TIME points acquired column will also be tallied.  This will include points used as well as points yet to be used.

 

TWO BOXERS

Points are accumulated as if you only have one toon. Both of your Dual boxing characters have access to the same pool of points.
EXAMPLE: Player1 and player2 are the same person dual boxing on a raid. Player1 and player2 bid out of the same accumulative total pool of points.
Can win bids with both toons and on multiple occasions as long as points are available for that toon to use.

 

IN CASE OF A TIE DURING BIDS

LC will announce that there is a tie instead of announcing a winner
During announcement LC will List the names of those tied.
LC will then /random to a number 1 less then the number of those tied (EX. 3 way tie, LC will /ran 2. Roll turning up 0 will be first name, 1 being 2nd, 2 being 3rd, etc.).
Winner of the LC's /ran will be awarded the piece.

LOOT EXCHANGE

All Droppable Loot obtained during a Clan X event is eligible for exchange of HALF the original bid points (rounded up). There will be a list kept of what droppable items have been obtained, by whom, the date, and how many points they bid for it. Once this item has been successfully exchanged, the item now goes up for bidding (same rules apply as above for banked items).

RAIDING SUPPLY REIMBURSEMENT

SPELLS AND ETHERAL/SPECS/RUNES

Loot Leader (LL) of said raid will loot ALL Spells/Ethereals/Spectrals/Runes that drop. All spells acquired by turning in ethereals, spectrals and runes shall be awarded based upon the Spell Class Lists and then by LIFETIME points. The class at the top of the list, and present at said raid, is eligible for the spell turn in. At the completion of the raid, the LL will immediately turn in all Ethereals, Spectrals and Runes by the Classes present* on the list.

A Spell Class List is a list of classes in the game that are able to use spells/songs. Each of these lists are kept for each type of turn in spell ( Ethereal, Spectral and Rune). These lists will continually cycle around. Meaning, once a class has been awarded the turn in, that class is then moved to the bottom of the list. If the class is not represented it will then move to the next class in the list.

The LL determines who the awardee is by the highest lifetime points. The LL then informs the awardee of the spell/song received and verifies if they NEED the spell/song. If the awardee is not in need of the spell/song, the LL will go to the next player of that class present with the highest lifetime points. This process will continue until the spell/song is distributed.

* Classes present must be of the required level of use (Ethereal 61+, spectral 63+ or rune 65).
** NBG as always we expect everyone to pass on the spectral/rune if they are not in need of those spells/songs.

The raid consisted of 4 Druids, 2 Warriors, and 2 Shamans. The LC will then look at the list and go down it until it one of the classes present at the raid is found. The LC will then look at the lifetime points of the druids present and award the spell to the Druid in NEED of the spell/song according to lifetime points. If a duplicate arises it will then get passed down to the 2nd highest lifetime point druid present at the raid. If no one at the raid is in the need of the spell, the spell/song is then turned into the guild bank where it will be distributed to the one with the highest lifetime points of that class in NEED. (There will be a 2 day period in which ALL members of that class and required level can express their need) After the spell is distributed Druids are then placed on the bottom of the list.
