December - Happy Holidays -

 

Clan X had some fun in the Bazaar Arena at a great "Rixma Black and Blue Event"... we gave out over 60k in prizes to CX members. We also took the chance to give out the gifts from our little secret Santa exchange... It was a great time had by all... a Big Thank you to Takel for helping out with organizing this event.
