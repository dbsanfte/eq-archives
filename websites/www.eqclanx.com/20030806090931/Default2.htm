June 23- July 16

  • General News

    • Clan X has been busy lately doing FFA mobs, rotation mobs, getting those needed AA dings and regular xp while checking out the new zone revamping.  

      We have been busy in Plane of Storms raiding for key components for our guild members.  We have been lucky and done a few BOT tower raids, congrats to those who have gotten new ornate items and some great new weapons.

       

  • Bard Singing a New Tune!!!

    • Congratulations to Lokash on his Bard Epic. Took just over two groups to take down the final mob in Sebilis. Thanks to Gneiss and a couple of his friends who came on short notice to help.

       

  • Grieg...

    • GREIG dies again to Clan X forces without hardly any deaths. Ssassy jumped in and  pulled for the raid without having been to the zone before, good job jumping in and taking up that task. Congratulations to Tarratree on her new FT gloves and to Veir on his new Necro toy.  Sorry if I forget anyone.

       

  • 3 MOBS 3 HOURS:

    • Velks died, Va'Dyn died, and the POI dragon died. Talk about focus and guild paying attention.

       

  • The Kael Connection

    • Vindi/ Statue/ Idol all went down, then we went down on AoW!  We will BE BACK!

 

WOW, as I sit here trying to think of all we have been doing, we been VERY BUSY, just hard to remember it all, but this gives a small view of our Clan X life

Alexedia

  • Sith's Spotlight

    • In this Spotlight, we are highlighting someone who some people call Wonder Woman...
      Uphoria: Selfless, hard working, helpful, and resourceful are just some of the traits guild members have associated with her.

May 5 - 12, 2003

  • When Giants Fall...

    • A Simple little Vindi excursion turned out to be a full blown raid!
      After taking down Vindi, we decided to take on The Statue of Rallos Zek  as he decided to pop up. We had good numbers so we decided to go for the fearsome threesome in one sitting.
       

      The first 2 went down flawlessly. We even decided to take out the Idol un-slowed to test our might... and that went as planned. So we decided to go for it all and try out the big red machine. Unfortunately, due to some technical difficulties, the AoW used an exploit to take us down. But now knowing about this issue, we will have no problems taking him down the next time we encounter him.

      Gratz to CX on their 4th Rotation Mob, The Statue of Rallos Zek.

      Great job SJ ... you're another great example of leadership in the lands of Norrath.
