"Black and Blue, Rixma and YOU"
 What the heck is THAT????!!!!

Once each month we are going to have a Clan X get together - ANYTHING goes at this event. What are we going to do? EVERYTHING!!! Language fair, trading of items and spells, duels, discussions of future strategies, treasure hunts, skill building, races, and of course, ganging up on Rixma so YOU TOO can say you slayed her.  Just show up and bring what you want to accomplish, whether it is to fight, learn something new, discuss strategies or vision, or just get to know your guild family.

October 19, 2002

The guild and recruits went to Fear on Saturday. Congratulations to Mystagogue on her Enchanter epic piece and to Tubo, Vortigern, and Lykor on getting some new gear.  Sorry if I missed anyone, I was going by message board and points list.
 

October 15, 2002

Clan X rocked the Halls of Testing in ToV with an AMAZING total of thirty Clan X members! Congratulations to Takel for his new plate legs, and Khaoticscorpion on his new plate gauntlets.
 

October 14, 2002

Its been a busy week for us, here's a quick recap on what's happened.

Go Clan X nightcrew! Venril Sathir dies! Grats to Elrippo/Battlekat on the Beastlord Leggings, and lucky_guild_wizard_01 on the wizard epic piece. Tossen looted wizard epic piece for MQ as no wizards were present to loot.

In other news, Clan X rolls over the Halls of Testing in ToV for the second time. WTG guys! Grats to Sadori on Chain Arms, Gbab on Chain Coif, Ssenniug on Silk Pants, and Fazel on Plate Gauntlets.

Last but not least, Clan X stomps on PoA, going up through island 5, where we farmed warrior Spiroc Wing Blades for their epics from the Spiroc Lord. Here are some pictures from the trip.
