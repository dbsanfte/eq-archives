Guardians of the Faith

As of 24 February 2001, Clan X now has an alliance with this incredible guild. Guardians of the Faith is currently working on their own website - until ready, here is a little bit of information about this guild.

Leaders:

  • Wulbyan - an outstanding warrior who leads his guild with a firm but gentle hand - supporting his followers sometimes to the point of exhaustion, and obtaining for them armor and weapons when they cannot find them alone.  The members of Guardians of the Faith are dedicated to Wulbyan because he is so incredibly dedicated to them.

Officers:

  • Relkin - Cleric Officer

  • Grimlok - Druid Officer

  • Malacoda - Necromancer officer

  • Tecaro - Ranger officer
