Cleric Epic Mob - Sat, 19 May

On Saturday, many people from Brotherhood of Honor, Guardians of the Faith and Clan X arrived in the Burning Woods to extinguish the flame from a giant Fire Elemental. Actually, it was an epic mob named Ixz-something or other and he dropped a piece needed by clerics to continue with their epic quest.

After slowly (and when I mean slowly, I mean it took us three hours) gathering our troops, we finally got around to triggering the mob. But, as fate would have it, Wulbyan, the person punished with the responsibility of having to pull the mob, died on his way.

Well, we all know that Wulbyan is kinda... you know, weird but, he's a 58th warrior, and he fell to an elemental! (pause for cheering mage's to subside)

This mofo of a mob hit for a max of 575 and has roughly 32k hp. After regrouping, rezzing Wulbyan and having a few more people show up, we tried it again

And we succeeded! Hah! take that you mageless elemental!

So anyways, after killing it once, we killed it three more times (yup, we are goooood), many dispersed and went along there way.

WTG guys

Oh, and other than this mob dropping something for the Cleric epic, he drops a certain item of flame.

These items were all Mage/Wizard only

We got two Cap of Flame's
a Gloves of Flame
and a Belt of Flame

Grats to all the winners
