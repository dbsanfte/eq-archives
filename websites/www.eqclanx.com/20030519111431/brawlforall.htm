Brawl for All

The Clan X Best of the Best Event

In the beginning, the Gods asked for a contest to choose their champions. This has gone on thru eons. Now the time has once come to choose a champion...A champion of Clan X.

These are the lost ancient rules of combat found by a wandering rogue..

STRATEGY OF COMBAT

  1. You will submit an entrance fee to the gods of 25 pp - you are looked upon favorably if you also donate a semi-valuable item of your choice from your personal inventory.
  2. All melee types will be debuffed once they enter the arena area. Then a 30 sec buff session from either a cleric, shaman, or druid of the judges choosing. This to be done before combat occurs and not during or after. Buffing during combat or after combat is punishable by guild death.( i.e. the guild will kill you where ye stand).
  3. Weapons with the following spells will NOT be allowed: any type of fear, knockback, invisibility, shrink or dd spells. Weapons which are classified as artifacts, epic weapons, and weapons with right clickable spells will NOT be allowed.  Weapons which proc naturally will be allowed. Items which proc naturally will be allowed.
  4. Spells such as fear, root, darkness, charm, gate, etc. will not be allowed, UNLESS DIRECTED AT PETS.
  5. Pets will be allowed, however once a pet is brought forth the caster will not be allowed to med. to regain lost mana from conjuring the pet. If you arrive at the arena with a pet you be asked to dismiss it, if it is not dismissed it will be destroyed.
  6. NO AOE SPELLS. We don't want dead lower levels all over the place.
  7. All armor and weapons available to the contestant class will be allowed, except those listed in rule 2.
  8. Leaving the arena during your battle constitutes - lose

****These ancient rules are a guideline and can be altered by the judges discretion.

THE GREAT COUNCIL OF JUDGES

Aye, the gods themselves grew bored with judge us puny servants and created a council of judges from the leaders of the area. Their duties are list below:

Our Judges - CLAN X OFFICERS. Their role is to enforce the rules and see that fair play occurs. They are the word, the last word. No arguments.

The officers take up positions around the arena and watch and judge the contest.

THE FINAL OUTCOME

As in all contest there can be only one ...champion. So, the gods decreed that a battle amongst the champions must happen and the champions where brought to a hidden arena to fight. And only one walked out alive. So it is written so it shall be...

At the end of this glorious battle the champions will be taken away by the judges and stripped of there items and armor to fight each other.
