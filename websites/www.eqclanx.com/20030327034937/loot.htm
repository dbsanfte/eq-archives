ACQUIRING POINTS ON CX RAIDS
  1. Raids will be scheduled for 4 hour increments
  2. A possible 3 points can be obtained at each raid
    • 1 point awarded for being on time for beginning of raid
    • 1 point awarded for being in attendance through middle of raid
    • 1 point awarded for being there for completion of raid
  3. Points will be tallied by the Attendance Taker (AT) at the raid
    • AT will be an officer of CX for each raid
    • The AT will HAVE to take attendance at the beginning of the by using the /who ‘clan x’ command to see who is in the meeting zone on time.
    • If you are leaving a raid before completion time, you will have to announce your leaving to the AT to cease your points
  4. Points will be tallied PER USER and that tally will be available to all alternates under that main.
  5. ONLY Raids that are Posted on The Clan X Calendar will be available to award points unless it falls under one of the below mentioned sections for Epics and Race mobs.
     

USING YOUR POINTS TOWARD GEAR DROPPED at a CX raid

  1. Loot Coordinator (LC) will be 2 designated CX Officers or Loot Team members per raid
  2. LC will make announcement over raid channel that gear has dropped
    • Group Leaders/Raid looters are responsible for sending LC's a tell of loot that has dropped
    • LC's announcement will have to include a link of the item.
    • LC will make 3 calls for each item dropped (initial, 2nd, Final)
  3. Players will submit their bids of points to the LC in /tell
    • Bids will be kept blind (No announcement of who bid what) so you may change your bid at any time in the 3 calls
  4. Players must bid AT LEAST 1 point.  NO free loot, not even when bidding from the bank. Even if you are the only one of your class in attendance 1 point must still be bid to loot the item. (No drop or not)
  5. Your Points will be deducted from your accumulative total ONLY if you are the highest bidder for the item
  6. You MAY win more then once per raid as long as you continue to win bids and not run out of accumulated points
  7. ONLY points accumulated PRIOR to the current raid can be used.
  8. In the event that you have a ZERO points balance, you are the only one interested in the dropped loot and you are able to use the item, your points balance may go into the negative by 1 point for each piece of loot you obtain.
     

LOOT COORDINATOR RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. You Must have a points total available for the entire raid - Current Participation Points
  2. You will have to deduct points from a player on the list at the time they have successfully used their points
  3. You are able to bid on items as well when they drop (must be done prior to announcement to raiding party)
  4. The 2 LC's for the raid must be of opposite class so that the other LC can successfully bid before other bids are placed
  5. IF there is a case that both LC's can use the item in question, they are to send either the GL (first choice) or another officer their bid BEFORE they announce that an item has dropped and all others have a chance to bid on the item
  6. ONLY the LC that is taking bids may announce the WINNER over chat after the final call has been made
  7. LC bids are a one shot deal (this is to not give LC an advantage over upping bid after seeing others)
  8. LC MUST post the results of points deducted IMMEDIATELY after they log off for the day
     

ATTENDANCE TAKER RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. Must have pen and paper or other form of making hard copy prior to beginning of raid
  2. Is responsible for tallying points for those in attendance
  3. MUST post the results for points tallied IMMEDIATELY after they log off for the day
  4. Is NOT responsible for taking attendance for those that DO NOT send in a tell stating their arrival until they have done so if they join the raid late.
     

POSTING OF POINTS ACQUIRED & DEDUCTED

  1. ATs and LCs will post points acquired and deducted here on the message board
  2. Webmasters for Clan X will track all points on the Loot Spreadsheet
  3. Will be divided by Class so that everyone knows what everyone else has
  4. An ALL TIME points acquired column will also be tallied
    • This will include points used as well as points yet to be used.
       

TWO BOXERS

  1. Points are accumulated as if you only have one toon.  Both of your Duel boxing characters have access to the same pool of points.
    EXAMPLE: Player1 and player2 are the same person dual boxing on a raid.  Player1 and player2 bid out of the same accumulative total pool of points.
  2. Can win bids with both toons and on multiple occasions as long as points are available for that toon to use
  3. When bringing both toons to an epic or race mob raid your toons count as two Clan X members present. (See situations where epic and race mob raids qualify for points)
     

EPIC BATTLES THAT REWARD POINTS

  1. 1 Point will be rewarded using the above stated rules for the ENTIRE raid
  2. Attendance for the raid must be kept within reason (ex: 50 people are not required to do Xenvorash in Omen)
  3. One Clan X group alone can not be awarded points for an epic kill.  It must be more than just one single group of guildmates.
  4. You must announce the kill over public channels such as guildchat and cxchat, it has to be available for everyone that is online at the time you proceed to the kill spot.
  5. The person that is Obtaining the Epic Piece (Or a member that he/she as requested) is required to post the attendee's on member's only board for points to be awarded, and also alert a CX Officer that is online at the time that the raid is being done
  6. Success of Raid is not necessary to acquire points
    • 2 Points Maximum can be rewarded per area for any given individual (ex. Druid_01 might take players A,B,C,D,E to Kedge 5 times before success, but they are only able to receive 2 points TOTAL for their efforts. If player D is replaced by player X on 5th attempt, then player X is on their first trip there and would obtain 1 point)
  7. Outside from CX sponsored Raids, Epic Battles and Race mobs are the ONLY exception for obtaining points
  8. Epic Pieces obtained DURING CX Raids will NOT be awarded additional Points (EX. Ball of Everliving Golem, Shissar Head, and Blood of Cazic drop on a fear raid....Yahoo CX! No extra Points)
  9. Epic Battles and Race mobs are the ONLY things that points can be awarded on and NOT posted on the calendar due to some of their "rushing" nature (EX. Cabilis and Neriak Enchanter Mobs)
  10. Foraging does NOT count
     

IN CASE OF A TIE DURING BIDS

  1. LC will announce that there is a tie instead of announcing a winner
  2. During announcement LC will List the names of those tied
  3. LC will then /random to a number 1 less then the number of those tied (EX. 3 way tie, LC will /ran 2. Roll turning up 0 will be first name, 1 being 2nd, 2 being 3rd, etc.)
  4. Winner of the LC's /ran will be awarded the piece
     

EPIC LOOT OBTAINED on a CX raid

  1. Will be determined by the following
    • Stage of Epic Advancement by each Player in that Class
    • ALL TIME points will determine any tie breaks
      These rules only apply to items dropped on a posted raid and not for a raid actually Planned by an individual. (EX. You cannot claim these rules if you are an attendee on a raid FOR someone else)
       

RACE MOB RAIDS

  1. All race mobs that require 2 or more groups of Clan X members to kill will be awarded points for attendance and will use the bidding method to distribute the loot that dropped on the raid.
  2. Race mob impromptu raids MUST be announced to all members of Clan X through the /guild or cxchat forums to allow everyone the option to attend.
  3. Spells dropped from Race mobs fall under the spell section for loot distribution.
  4. Due to the rushed nature of Race mobs gem/dot reimbursement must be done after the raid through the gem banker
     

MULTI-QUESTABLE PIECES left  UNCLAIMED and  DROPPABLE ITEMS that are NOT bid on

  1. This includes either Velious Quest Pieces, NO DROP Epic Pieces, and Loot that is not claimed AND that is droppable.
  2. Will be looted by an officer or guild member that can COMPLETE the Multiquest
  3. Will go into Guild Vault
  4. If you wish to obtain these items (Not Epic Pieces) you must post so and get a hold of an officer online to place bid.
    • 1 Day will pass before you get acknowledgement if you are rewarded piece FROM POST (not /tell to officer of bid)
    • Points will be deducted if rewarded piece just as if you were at the raid
    • Minimum 1 point bid is needed to obtain the non epic loot from the bank
  5. EPIC Pieces in Bank Have to be requested for on Boards
    • You MUST be READY for that stage of the Epic Quest
    • Same Epic loot rules will apply as stated above in the case of multiple requests
  6. Will be reserved for MAIN toons unless request is passed by CX Officers
     

VENDOR LOOT and RESIST GEMS OBTAINED on a CX raid
Will be Collected at end of a Raid and Placed into Bank for future raiding supplies.
 

SPELL COMPONENTS and QUESTING GEMS OBTAINED on a CX Raid
Will be Collected at end of a Raid and Placed into Bank for Future Usage upon request of CX members.
***An officer will be designated at beginning of each raid in which to give these items to at end***
 

RAIDING SUPPLY REIMBURSEMENT

  1. Will only be done on CX Raids
    • If a person doing an Epic battle wishes to replace gems used that is at their own discretion
  2. Will Be Passed out upon request from Gem Holding Officer for that raid
    • It is recommended to have an ample supply going into a raid so that you do not have to request DURING a raid
    • This will be on a TRUST basis. You will be responsible for asking for a reimbursement of the gems that you have ONLY used on the current raid
  3. Request can be denied by CX Officers if excessive usage is determined
    (EX. You decide to chain rune the druid in your group during a raid because he is the lone healer in your group)
     

SPELLS

  1. Raid Spells:
    Spells acquired at any Clan X raid can be distributed to those at the raid that are within 1 level of the spell level requirement or above. If there is more than one person requiring the spell, those who meet the requirements must role on the spell to determine who will receive it.
     
  2. Banked Spells:

    Spells that were not usable by anyone on any raids or spells that were donated to the guild bank will be distributed on a first come first serve basis. If the guild member is of the appropriate level to USE the spell then they may place a request to withdraw the spell at the following link: http://www.vvm.com/ubercyrus/xboard/viewtopic.php?t=407

    To view what spells we currently have available to guild members click on the following link: www.eqclanx.com/ClanXSpellChart.xls

    *New Recruits that are on the 45 day probation period have to wait till their probation is over to be awarded spells from the bank.*

    To view who has received some of our higher level spells, go to the same link and click on the tab at the bottom "Tracking Receipt of Spells"

  

CONGRATS FOR READING ALL THAT....NOW FOR SIMPLE TERMS

Basically raids are set for 4 hours, if you are on time and stay for full 4 hours, you get 3 points that can be used in future raids. This is so that if you have emergency or can only stay for a limited time, your time is still rewarded.

You get to choose what you want to have a shot on for loot, and aren't limited to 1 piece per raid. This is good for if you missed last Kael raid for example....only other leather wearer that was there last time spent majority of their points on gear, now you have a good shot at walking out with more then one upgrade.

You get rewarded for going to Epic battles! In all honesty, a lot of the epic battles give you a taste of what the UBAH mobs can do, and what you need to do succeed.....get good learning experience and rewarded for your time once again!

Little stress for officers! Attendance and point rewarding is basically each member's responsibility, all you are doing is writing name down for attendance. Loot Coordinator...all you are doing is verifying points, and recognizing a highest number and announcing winner!

With MQ-able and Gems being put into bank....you can still get sweet stuff even if you happened to miss the raid all together for whatever reason....and with all gems going to bank...no need to look at a list until you have a piece to request for.

This system that is stated above, is for the most part used by Clan Bashemded. Vortigern/Sulpicious has modified in the Epic Battles and possibly some of the other rules to simplify things in the long run. CB is a guild VERY much like CX - very friendly, very family oriented, and very organized on raids...which they only do maybe 2 times a week.
