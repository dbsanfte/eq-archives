Consigliere - Elgor Stonedblade

Let me make sure that you all have a clear understanding of my position.  I am the Consigliere...Yes just like in La Cosa Nostra (I.E. The MOB)...I am merely a counselor to the Clan. I have no vote on Clan issue's (except ones like the server split).  I am like a third party observer or consultant I make suggestions and recommendations to Clan leadership and I only answer to Rixma (this is no joke its just like the real life mob, I only answer to the God Father....so to speak).  I am not an officer nor do I wish to be one.  We created this role when we established Clan X.

Minister of Commerce - Gress Shadowwolf

Basically if you have an item that you need sold you can give it to our Minister of Commerce.  Set the price you want for it and they go sell it.  When the item is sold for that set price or higher, they get to keep a set amount for providing the service to the Clan.  

For example I have a pair of Gaunts Of The Black I bought for 500PP in EW the other day. I want to sell these for no less than 2K. If they sell for 2K, who ever sold them will get 20% of the 2K or 400PP for their time and effort in selling them. As for the benefit to the Clan...It gives those of us who hate to sell in EC the opportunity to still get rid of items, it gives those who don't mind selling in EC some easy cash.

For those of us that hate sitting in EC, they will buy and sell for us while keeping a small profit for themselves for the service - 20% of the selling price goes back to the Minister of Commerce.  Also if you need something bought they can help you get it - if you need something bought, it works the same way except you give them the cash and they get 20% the sale - they go find or buy the item.
