Drunk Ogre Race Information

This is a race of agility, intelligence, and trying to outsmart your fellow alliance mates.  The winner is the Level 1 ogre to make it from Ogguk to E Freeport first and to shout in the E Freeport zone "I am de maste Ogre of all de Nowwath".

Sign Up Below!!!
When: Sunday, June 24th 7pm ET / 4pm PT
Where:  Start in Ogguk - End in E FP
Who can race:  Anyone in our Rathe Alliance (Brotherhood of Honor, Clan X, or Guardians of the Faith).
How much:  To race, you must give 20 pp to Rixma by Friday June 22nd, plus any "nice" items you want to give away.  The winner of the race will get all pp and items collected.

Details of the Race:
You must sign up below and pay your 20pp by Friday, June 22nd.

Character creation, handing out of ALCOHOL, and GUILDING into Clan X (for communication purposes) will happen from 7pm - 8pm ET (4pm - 5pm PT).  All ogres will assemble at the Ogguk/Feerrott zone at 7:55 pm ET (4:55 PT).

Naming your character - try and pick a name closest to that of your primary character so that we know who you are - for example, Rixma = Rixmax, or Matok = MatokMatok, Wulbyan =LushFromHell, Laramie = LaramieLD, etc.

For those of you who do not want to race but would like to participate, we need 5 race moderators (see below) - contact Rixma directly if you want to be a moderator.

RULES:

  • NO TWINKING - your ogre must only have items on him/her that he/she brought into the world of Norrath.

  • As many lives as you want - if you die and think you can make it by starting over, go for it!

  • No "sneaky" friends waiting for you at different zones to sow or invis, etc.  If you find wandering people to help you, no problem - but let's keep this nice and fair all. :)

  • So what about getting drunk?  Here is the getting drunk part:

    • At 8:00 pm ET (5:00 PT), the race moderator in Ogguk will shout "All Ogres Drink UP!" - each Ogre must drink 1 pint of whatever alcohol has been given to you and the RACE IS ON.

    • Each ogre travels from Ogguk zone in Feerrott to the Inothule Swamp in Feerrott - When you zone into Inothule Swamp it is time for DRINK NUMBER TWO (1 more pint)- a race moderator will be waiting there to insure you do your drinking :)

    • Each ogre then needs to make it to S Ro - after zoning into S Ro, again you will drink 1 more pint - DRINK NUMBER 3.  And again, a race moderator will be waiting there.

    • Each ogre then makes their way to Oasis - when you have zoned into Oasis, you will drink 2 PINTS (yes TWO PINTS) - DRINK NUMBER 4.  This is the last time you will be required to drink - any other drinking during the race is purely for your enjoyment :)
