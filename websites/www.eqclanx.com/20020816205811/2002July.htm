Will Clan X
win 35k in pp?

 

Will Clan X win 35,000 in pp?  YES the contest continues through August 15th!!

We are doing anything we can to help our members gain exp in July and August. This is not about powerleveling, but about doing all we can as a team to level together. As part of providing this, we are going to be hanging out in some of the higher level zones in Norrath: Sebelis, Velketor’s Lab, Howling Stones (Charsis), Chardok, Burning Woods, and The Hole.

20,000 pp will be donated to the guild fund on August 15th if we have in the guild one toon from each class level 57 or higher and 6 toons in the guild at level 60. In addition, 1,000pp will be given to the highest-level toon per class if this goal is reached (ties will be random rolled).

"Black and Blue, Rixma and YOU" - What the heck is THAT????!!!!

Once each month we are going to have a Clan X get together - ANYTHING goes at this event, and it will be held in an Arena of some sort. What are we going to do? EVERYTHING!!! Language fair, trading of items and spells (we will get all banker toons to this location and hand out everything in the bank), duels, discussions of future strategies, treasure hunts, skill building, races, and of course, as the title indicates, ganging up on Rixma so YOU TOO can say you slayed her. There is no limit to what we will do at this event, just show up and bring what you want to accomplish, whether it is to fight, learn something new, discuss strategies or vision, or just get to know your guild family.
