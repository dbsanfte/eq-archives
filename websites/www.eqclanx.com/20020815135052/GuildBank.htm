- Guidelines for the guild bank


Spells:  Check out our Current List of Spells.
Spells in the bank will be given out at no charge to those that are within 2 levels below OR able to use that particular spell at the time. Spells will not be sold off at any point due to the fact that many guildies are not yet at the lvl needed to use the spell.  

 
Armor:  Check out our Current List of Items in the Bank.
To withdraw armor from the guild bank PLEASE DO NOT TAKE GEAR THAT YOU PLAN ON TURNING AROUND AND AUCTIONING.

The armor that is in the guild bank will be given out on a first come, first serve basis. When withdrawing armor from the bank, please make a deposit at that time, of either the old item that you will replacing or something comparable that someone else in the guild can use (i.e. Don't ask to withdraw a MoSS and deposit a Splintering Club). If you do not have any gear to deposit, pp can be used in lieu of gear. 

Any gear that has sat in the bank for a long period of time will be sold by the banker. That money will be placed in the guild fund to cover peridots, hate stones, etc. used on raids.  

 
Gems:  Check out our current list of Resist Gear and Materials - click on the "Resist Gear and Materials" tab.
High quality gems (i.e. Diamonds, BD's, Jacinaths) Can NOT be withdrawn from the guild bank. 

If you have a request for resist gear please let the banker know your requested item and they will have it made for you. Upon completion of the item, you will then have a chance to buy it from the guild. Money from purchased items will be put into the guild fund to cover periodots, hate stones, etc. used on raids.

If you would like to have resist gear made for you and have the appropriate gems, contact a guild banker or Alexedia for a list of jewelers that work with the guild on jewelry requests. You will be responsible for the cost of the metals used in making the items regardless of any fail attempts.

(Keep in mind, jewelers will fail at their attempts. The cost of items will vary depending on the success rate. The higher the success, the less an item will cost.)


Peridots, Hate Stones, etc.:
It is up to the raid leader to contact a banker prior to the raid to get the necessary peridots, hate stones, etc. It is also the responsibility of the raid
leader to return any unused items to the guild bank for future use. Please be considerate and use the honor system on these. By not doing so, you're only hurting the guild.
 
 
Resist Gear:

As decided by Gebi and Alex , the following prices will be for guild resist gear:

  • Silver, Electrum, or Gold/ Diamond gear: 100pp/ea

  • For all Platinum or Velium gear: The cost of the components (what ever the going rate is at that time)

  • Blue Diamond Gear- Due to the lack of donations to the guild bank we can no longer offer price cuts in blue diamond gear. Blue diamond gear will now be sold at the cost of which we buy the components for.
    Please check with Gebi or Alex for these current costs. Keep in mind, that the success/failure rate may affect their prices BUT it will still be less that what you would pay in EC.

For those that wish to upgrade their current resist gear piece:
This service will be available soon. You will be expected to pay the cost in the difference in metal AND donate the old piece to the guild to be re-circulated.  example: Lana wants to upgrade her Plat/Diamond ring to a Vel/Diamond ring. She would pay:

  • 160pp (difference in metal cost) 1 diamond trade her plat/diamond ring.  (in event that the jeweler fails, Lana would not get back the 160pp or diamond)

Again, all these services and prices can only continue IF the donations of these high end gems keep coming in. This is an incentive for everyone to think twice before selling these gems in EC, and a wonderful chance to better the guild.

Velious Armor Quest Gems:  Check out our current list of Quest Gems - click on the "Velious Quest Gems" tab.


Other Rules:

If you would like to take something in the vault.....read the rules listed under the bank thread on our board and GO TO www.guildmagic.com. Submit your request and the bankers will approve it. It is up to you to get ahold of the bankers ingame to set up a time to get the items.
