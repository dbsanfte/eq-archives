November 2001

 

Sunday - November 25, 2001

Welcome

Welcome new members Laeloo (53 Vicar), Resus (51 Vicar) and Tangar (51 Champion)! If you haven’t met them, make sure you say hello. We are glad to have them in Clan X!

CoM Sunday

 

First, who ever left gum on the seat in Lori's Taxi, not funny, you are in big trouble. Allurra, I'm looking at you =o). Amazingly there was a druid shortage Sunday so it took a while to get everyone to the raid. But we did get two groups going at the stables. Later in the evening we were down to one group and everyone moved to goo house. Kakita was doing a nice job of pulling at the goo house. After she had to leave, someone thought it would be a good idea to let Fazel pull. We had lots of fun, and don't forget there are more CoM and Seb raids this month so check the calendar.

 

DE Corrupter

 

Congratulations to Loriena who completed the DE Corrupter part of her epic this week. Thanks to Humedorf, Kalorma, Alexedia, Lonedor, Gebi, Yarrl, Saleb, Prynce and Keenin from Bane of Veeshan for hanging out waiting for the druids to spawn so we could get the Corrupter to spawn. After getting the note, Loriena was able to track down Ella in Misty Thicket and turn in the note to get her bowl. She combined her foraged items and is now completely finished with that section of her quest. 

 

 

Blood Dire

 
Congratulations to Kalorma who picked up the pelt she needed for her epic from the Blood Dire in MM. Thanks to Humedorf, Resus, Tangar, Kiktih, Jarredd, Deniden and Loriena for coming out to MM. The Dire wasn't up so we had to kill the Advisor. Loriena won the advisor robe, we were also able to get a Gypsy Lute for Kiktih and WW Talons for Jaredd.

 

In other epic news...

 
Major Thanks to Alexedia and Lonedor!! They helped Loriena yet again with her epic this week. Loriena was able to complete the Cleansed Spirit of Antonica section of her Epic. This is one of three larger and harder sections of the druid epic. It required you to port all over killing corrupted creatures on Kunark, Faydwer and Antonica.

 

ToFS

 
Kroeten, Slinkity, Numscullidor, Tilamdar, Laeloo, Loriena, Merther, Kakita, Stephans from DoN and Denithor and Ryne from EA headed to ToFS for some fun. It was a blast. We made it to the very top of the tower too. Kroeten impressed us all with her AoE in the library, we crashed the wedding on the 5th, and we played with vampires on the 7th. Slinkity and Merther were in competition all night to see who could go LD the most. We had fun in the sandbox upstairs and waited till 4am my time because Slinkity promised the end MOB was going to spawn "any time now." We got really silly and had lots of fun crammed in that tiny room with no exit. We did kill the end MOB, I forget her name now, but Stephans picked up a nice neck piece. It was 4am, give me a break. You can see her in the screenshots this week.  

 

 

Kael

 
We traveled to Kael this week to step on some giant toes! =o) What started as a large pick up group with several guilds turned into a smaller raid with two group of almost all Clan Xers. With Loriena cracking her whip = ) and Akithmaryn doing an AWESOME job of pulling, we headed right into the arena. We only had one wipe out in the middle of the raid because giants cannot be mezed and don't stay charmed. We were fighting a nasty giant who kept CHing himself and his friend. We got him down to death and he gated. Summoning and scattering people around quickly lead to lots of deaths. We recovered very quickly though thanks to Laeloo's rezing and went back for revenge. Everyone did a GREAT JOB! I can't wait to go back!

 

Screenshots

 

I'll post screenshots from ToFS at the beginning of the week, so look for them. There are some really funny ones. Thanks everyone, it was the most fun I have had in a while!

 

I'll also post screenshots from Kael and for any other raids I have.

Sunday - November 11, 2001

CoM Sunday

 
On Sunday, we started the first of the month long experience raids in CoM. We got off to a great, early start and had 6 people grouped in CoM the whole day. That night, we were joined by another full group. One pulled the temple and another the stables. I know everyone was getting great experience, I got 4 yellow myself.

Thanks to Humedorf for getting groups set up and pulling the temple for us! Thanks to Slinkity for all the clarity we could handle. 

CoM Thursday

 

On Thursday's CoM raid, we were able to pick up two jade reavers for the druid/ranger epic! Yarrl and Semprini both received a reaver for their epic. Congratulations!

We had two groups there for experience, thanks to Rixma and Akithmayrn for pulling. Also, I wanted to say GOOD JOB everyone with assisting. Lord Rak Ashiir death-spawned after we killed a reaver and NO ONE touched him! We all lived and moved to the other side with no deaths. WTG!

 

In other raiding news

 
On Tuesday and Friday this week, we also raided Sebilis. I heard that went pretty good as well save a little wipeout.

Raids for CoM and Sebilis have been scheduled for each week during November. Sebilis raids are on Tuesdays and Fridays beginning at 7pm ET for levels 50 and up. CoM raids are Sundays and Thursdays beginning at 7pm ET for levels 40 and up. DON'T BE LATE!
