Harrison steps down ... Blacksunshine takes over - Thurs, 29 March

Our noble Harrison has decided that his duties as officer would better be served by his alternate ego, Blacksunshine.  Just FYI that Blackie is now available for all of you officer related needs!  Congrats Blackie!

Garbuk promoted to officer status - Wed, 21 March

After agonizing months of not wearing pants and scaring his friends with that very large naked ogre belly, a CLOTHED Garbuk was awarded officer status within Clan X.  Congrats Gar!  And thanks for not being naked!  You do Clan X proud!

Lonedor is successful! - Fri, 16 March

Today, Clan X assisted individuals in Castle Mistmoore to acquire some very needed quest items.  Once we helped them with their battles, we took a walk to the bath and defeated the mob which has been keeping Lonedor from his Soulfire hilt!  Congrats to Lonedor!  What is next Jester?

Clan X votes to stay on The Rathe - Fri, 9 March

As of 6pm, PST on Friday the 9th of March, 2001 - Clan X has voted to remain on The Rathe server.  Here are the poll results:

  • Whole guild goes to new server: 4.7%

  • Whole guild stays on The Rathe: 52.3%

  • Guild splits between the servers: 42.8%

  • Really don't care what the guild does: 0%

If you are a member of Clan X and you plan to move to the new server, we wish you the best of luck in your endeavors.  At this point, Clan X will not start up on the new server.  If you have decided to move to the new server, the dates to do so are Friday March 16th at 6pm PST to Sunday March 18th at 11:59 PST.  To move your toons, you just need to log in one and type /movelog yes.  Once you do this, you cannot reverse it so DON'T DO IT UNLESS YOU ARE CONFIDENT IN YOUR DECISION.

If any of you wish to discuss this decision, please bring it up in guild chat on the game or send the guild an e-mail at eqclanx@yahoogroups.com 

 

Gandlug - Officer of Clan X - Tues, 6 March

Today, Gandlug was officially named officer of Clan X.  Due to his diligence in setting up guild raids and his presence on The Rathe, as well as a tremendous drive to guild new people into Clan X, Gandlug was awarded this position after enthusiastically inquiring about becoming an officer.  Please congratulate him when you see him!  Good job Gandlug!
