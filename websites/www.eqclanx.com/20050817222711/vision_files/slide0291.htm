Flexibility of things to do
Welcoming of lower levels
Weaknesses:
Poor Raiding Capabilities
Less “Uber” Loot

Frustration with guild progress
Lifespan < Raiding Guild Lifespan

Leadership Structure
Frequently, officers and leader as equals
Sometimes, distinct leader with officers
     who have “less power” than the leader.
