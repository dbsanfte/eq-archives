How are points acquired?

An Alliance member or an invite of the Alliance earns one point per hour that they attend a raid.  

What are these points for?

You may use the points to add to your roll, if you meet the looting rules.  If you have acquired 10 points you may say you wish to use all 10 before you roll to gain an item of which you are allowed to roll on. You roll a 50.  You now have a total of 60 for your roll.

Do I lose my points if I don't win?

No you keep your points till you win an item and then lose the points that you said you were using.

So if I used 5 of my ten points and won the item I would still have five to use on the next item I wanted to roll on?

Yes.

Can I use the points from the current raid or event?

No. Only the points from previous raids.

How do I know if the raid or event I am attending is earning me points?

If you look on the Alliance calendar it will stay POINTS or No POINTS on each event. If it does not say anything it will be considered No POINTS. If there is an error on the calendar about points its Rixma's fault :).

Can Non Alliance friend earn points?

Yes anyone who attends the raid/event can earn points to use on further events.

If I show up late for an event will that effect how I earn my points?

No it will not effect how you earn your points. But, if you miss the insertion or start time for an event you may not roll on any loot till you have been there for one hour.

What if everyone in my class has won an item am I still eligible to loot
items if everyone in my class has looted something within that first hour?

Yes. If everyone in your class has won an item in that first hour you can loot an item.

If the roll was /random 200 and I was using 20 of my points and I rolled a 190
would I then have a roll of 210?

Yes.

Do I lose my points after a certain period? Or do I just keep accumulating
them?

You just keep adding them up till you use them. They do not expire.

Are there any items I cannot use my points for?

Yes there are items you can NOT use your points for. These are BPs and Robes. Those are rolled for by one person from each guild of the eligible classes. Invites are not eligible at this time to roll on BPs or Robes.

If I was one of the people to roll on a BP or a Robe could I use my points
to roll on it?

No. Points may NOT be used for BPs or Robes rolls.

If I have two planeable characters and I bring different ones on raids/events
do they have separate points or is it per account ?

You earn your points per account.

So points I earned with my Cleric on a raid I can use to roll on items for
my Druid if they are on the same account?

Yes.
