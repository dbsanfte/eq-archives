Under the sea

Thursday, Clan X was not able to raid CoM as there was another guild there, so we took a trip to Kedge Keep in hopes of getting a robe for Rixma. While we didn't get the robe we did get some great raid experience.  We did have three rounds of death that night, but at least the corpse pile kept getting smaller each time.. heh.  Those seahorses are mean, let me tell you. Phinny was cake compaired to them. =o) It was close quarters with NO air and we were raiding with an unfamiliar guild. We also had no mage so it was up to druids and enchanters with limited scales to keep EB on everyone. I think we learned alot on that raid and we will keep trying till Rixma gets the robe she needs.

DE Corrupter 

Thanks to Lonedor, Kalorma, Humedorf, Niamen and Loriena who came out to help Alexedia complete a part of her druid/ranger epic. This part of the epic requires the extermination of a level 50 Dark Elf Corruptor in E. Karana. Alexedia had to bind in a specific part of the zone and then turn in items to get the DE to spawn. City of Clan X... err Mist

Thanks for the GREAT turnout and the good time!

Picnic by the Lake

Ok, there was no picnic, but there were a lot of Clan Xers sitting by the lake. Thanks to all who come out to West Commons to assist Lonedor in getting his Divine Might Spell.  With a few groups on hand, the level 50 skeleton was no problem for Clan X. The tortured revenant dropped the dust Lone needed to complete his quest and he was able to get the spell. This spell adds a random proc to all attacks, regardless of the weapon, and stacks with any other procs that the weapon has. The proc is magic based and delivers 65 hit points of damage.

ToFS

Gebi, Merther, Loriena, Humedorf, Ssenniug, and Slinkity (friend of Gebi) paid a visit to the Tower of Frozen Shadow, located in Iceclad Ocean. ToFS is an 8-floor tower (counting the roof area), which requires keys to gain access to each level. With Slinkity as our guild we were able to get the keys needed to get up to the fourth floor, our camp for the evening. We were able to pick up some nice loot and experience. Major items picked up were: Gebi – Shield of Shadows, Merther – Sorcerous Bowl, Humedorf – Frost Covered Tome, and Ssenniug – Student’s Homework. We also picked up a Magnetic Dirk of Distraction, several pieces of Gnome Skin Armor, and an Embalmers Skinning Knife.

Sol B

Ungrievel, Kroeten, Yekim, Loriena, and friends took a swing at the King room in Sol B for some fun and experience. The group did a great job as we had no enchanter and the entire King room popped at one time, a few times with the King or Champion as an add. After several rounds of room pops and pulling we got two King pops that evening and picked up two Runed Mithril Bracers, one won by Ungrievel and the other won by Kroeten. The best display of team work was not the AWESOME job of killing and keeping everyone alive during pops, but getting Loriena’s corpse out of the wall =o). Only one death occurred during the evening and it was complicated as it became stuck in the wall of the king room and the group couldn’t get it out. With lots of team work (and a GM) the group was able to get the body and drag it to the zone. Everyone did an amazing job, way to go!
