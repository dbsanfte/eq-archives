The Clan X determination for Journeyman's Boots

How to get your greedy little mitts on these boots:

  • First step - Ancient Cyclops (AC) to collect the rings.  The AC spawns in two zones - South Ro and Ocean of Tears.  In South Ro, you need to kill sand giants and other creatures until he appears.  In the OOT, it spawns on the island between the sisters island and the seafury Cyclops.  

    • If you are camping the AC, BRING BIG FIREPOWER.  When it comes to J Boots, it can get nasty and a lot of kill stealing occurs.  

    • It would be a good idea to split the "group" so that you have a full group in each zone that the AC spawns in.  

    • We need a STRONG nuker, tracker, SOWer, chanter, and some tanks in each group - tankers running around killing everything while the nuker is sowed, levied, and GTG when the tracker or roamers see it.

  • Second step - get the shadowed rapier from a Shadowed Man (poofs when you camp, so get this last).

  • Third step - turn these items into Hasten Bootstrutter in the Rathe Mountains along with 3250 gp (yes GOLD pieces).
