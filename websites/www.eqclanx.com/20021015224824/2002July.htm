Will Clan X
win 35k in pp?

 

Will Clan X win 35,000 in pp?  YES the contest continues through August 15th!!

We are doing anything we can to help our members gain exp in July and August. This is not about powerleveling, but about doing all we can as a team to level together. As part of providing this, we are going to be hanging out in some of the higher level zones in Norrath: Sebelis, Velketor’s Lab, Howling Stones (Charsis), Chardok, Burning Woods, and The Hole.

20,000 pp will be donated to the guild fund on August 15th if we have in the guild one toon from each class level 57 or higher and 6 toons in the guild at level 60. In addition, 1,000pp will be given to the highest-level toon per class if this goal is reached (ties will be random rolled).

"Black and Blue, Rixma and YOU" - What the heck is THAT????!!!!

Once each month we are going to have a Clan X get together - ANYTHING goes at this event, and it will be held in an Arena of some sort. What are we going to do? EVERYTHING!!! Language fair, trading of items and spells (we will get all banker toons to this location and hand out everything in the bank), duels, discussions of future strategies, treasure hunts, skill building, races, and of course, as the title indicates, ganging up on Rixma so YOU TOO can say you slayed her. There is no limit to what we will do at this event, just show up and bring what you want to accomplish, whether it is to fight, learn something new, discuss strategies or vision, or just get to know your guild family.

Faydedar Dead!

Sunday July 28, 2002 our guild with the help of many very nice friends helped  Quickling take out Faydedar.   This proved alittle tricky as the real Faydedar spawned and the whole raiding party had to succor to the chessboard to do the kill there.  He dropped some pretty nice stuff and the item Quickling needed too.  Congrats on your new epic, Quickling!

Makaky got a group together and they were able to get his Shadow Knight epic piece in Overthere.  Congrats for a well planned and executed kill.  Yekim, after some   minor, but costly mishaps, got his Rogue epic this week too.  Way to stick with it and succeed.

 

CONGRATS to all on their new levels.

 

July 10, 2002

 

Epics Epics Epics!

This was a very busy night for Clan X and friends.  First we got together and killed Ulump for Quickling for his druid epic piece. Then we gated to Skyfire and succored to Overthere zone to help Makaky get his sk epic piece, but alas, the spawn wasn't up.  So we got Steam there and helped him kill his mob at the outpost for his mage epic piece.  Ssenniug got his ground drop epic piece while we were there too.  Since things were going so well, we ran to LOIO and took out the monk final epic mob for Geoo..... now this was a very confusing kill, many of us kiting him and some of us dying trying. BUT, again Clan X pulled together with friends and we were able to kill the needed mobs and Geoo looted his epic piece.

 

Monk Epic Achieved!

We got together and decided to do the monk epic piece AGAIN for Sulpicious.  After what happened the night before we were prepared and with only one or two outside help, CLAN X took the mobs down with only one death.  Congrats to both monks for getting their epics finished.
