As part of our alliance discussions on Wednesday January 30 2002, please take a look at the following.  We need to come to some agreements on many of the issues below.

From Manderon Canard, Ring of Dragons:

RoD is in the process of finalizing new raid looting rules, general raid protocols and specific zone raid tactics.  We hope that having these read and understood will help avoid some of the problems, misunderstandings and hurt feelings that can arise during and after raids.  We can make these available to you for your consideration with respect to alliance events.  I suspect you will find nothing really new here and that they will be very smilar to your own approach to the issues.  But, having something similar at the alliance level may help us avoid similar problems on alliance raids.

 

From the Alliance Voting Poll this week - comments from officers and members:

"Should we change the loot rules for big raids?"

  • Looting has always been a bit fuzzy with me, but then again most things are. I think that the leader of raid should decide who is most deserving of loot. I know that the people that participate with guild functions will have a better chance at loot, and that might help.

  • The way we are currently looting is not only NOT fair to those that have been in the guild/alliance a long time, but it is an administrative nightmare. I would prefer to have loot leaders choose on all raids, and loot leaders can be chosen from the alliance as to who is the most trustworthy to distribute loot, or who is the most fair.

  • I seriously think you should give out loot by rolling for loot on raids you are on, allowing non-guildies an equal chance. Otherwise who would come to help us? Also, if you could not get loot unless you have been with the guild for a certain period of time, it would make people leave the guild rather than stick it out for months with us. If people leave the guild, they have their reasons. I don’t think anyone joins our guild just to use us.

  • Loots! This my friends is a tough one. IF we give the power to a loot leader we ARE asking for a fight. End of story. The thing is rolling isn’t always fair, we all know this. Which is the lesser of two evils? That’s the question here. Is there any hope of making a list that reflects fairness? I wonder, and seriously doubt that anyone would be able to handle the discussions that it would take to make said list. I would love to see some more talk about this before we make any changes to the rules that although we might no like, at least we know.

  • The "uber" type of items … BPs, robes … should be ROLLED upon by those that the loot leader feels need them the most. I.e. Deni has a Brig Tunic (17AC 7Str 4Sta) and if an Indy BP drops he should be allowed to roll.

  • As far as loots go, I think some things should be changed, but at this point its kinda hard for me to explain how. Something needs to be changed for those items that are no drop, so that they don’t accidentally rot. Anyway, again only my opinion.

  • Forget points … I’ve personally seen no benefit for them. For Planes Raids … only people who sign up in advance get put on the loot list, then they are allowed to roll on loot. (go loot to be assigned by raid leader). All others get last choice on loot. On normal raids, just a normal roll. Need before greed basis works best. Late people do not get to roll. Once you get an item, you are excluded from rolling until all people have items.

  • Who distributing loot should be decided WAY before any raid takes place.

  • Blue Diamonds – looking at possible resist gear, will Guild loots soon be split with members so they can get some if and whey they drop?

"What should we do about people showing up late for raids?"

  • If someone has a RL appointment, and has to be late for a raid, what then? Do we deduct points for them being late due to RL? This is a confusing matter here.

  • On the late thing, should only be an issue if you late soo often that its an issue. Follow me there? People will be late because EQ does not run RL, RL ownz EQ and to that we are all slaves. Show up late once shame on you, twice shame on me, three … Shoot them/hand Kroe 20kpp. Call it fixing Kroeten fetish faction.

  • If you leave the raid, you are gone, end o the story!

  • Not really sure what to do about this BUT, if someone doesn’t get to loot because they are going to be late, what would possess them to even attend the raid when they CAN get online.
