Guardians of the Faith

As of 24 February 2001, Clan X now has an alliance with this incredible guild. 

Leaders:

  • Wulbyan - an outstanding warrior who leads his guild with a firm but gentle hand - supporting his followers sometimes to the point of exhaustion, and obtaining for them armor and weapons when they cannot find them alone.  The members of Guardians of the Faith are dedicated to Wulbyan because he is so incredibly dedicated to them.

  • Relkin - an awesome cleric who is constantly helping any and all that ask - but put your shades on!  His armor glows in the dark, and you can see him coming from zones away - don't bring him on any stealth missions - he is like a radioactive searchlight!  Hehehe Rel!

  • Jakx - although an incredible magician who will come to the aid of anyone requesting his assistance, watch out for this one!  He is after Wulbyan's throne!
