Training

One of the problems in using information systems is the lack of training provided (or available) from the individuals actually responsible for the installation and up-keep of the systems.

While some companies can provide training, training from actual Systems Administrators who are also trained as instructors is rare and extremely valuable. Utilizing the skills and direction of an on-staff Microsoft Certified Trainer, Nexus ITG provides training services on several levels.

Installed Systems - Instruction on systems installed at client sites is always suggested and available to all clients. This type of training can be performed either at the client's location or in the Nexus ITG classroom and either pre or post installation. The choices are provided based on the differing nature of client needs and desires.

Microsoft Approved Courses - Nexus ITG will provide the Mobile area community with training courses that strictly follow Microsoft approved curriculum and standards. The courses offered include both scheduled and ad hoc subjects, based upon the interest and availability of students. In the planned arena, a "Certification Track" will run over a five-month period, one week per month. This course covers the five classes suggested by Microsoft for an individual to gain the basic knowledge needed to operate their server platforms and is a favorite of persons wishing to become certified in Microsoft products.

Individual - Nexus ITG offers specialized courses on any subject for which Nexus ITG (or one of its affiliates) is qualified to act as a subject matter expert. This service is not common among training facilities and Nexus ITG also endeavors to provide this service at an uncommonly reasonable pricing structure.

By properly using the Training component of provided services, Nexus ITG furthers the goal of bringing smaller businesses the services of a large scale corporate Information Technology Department.
