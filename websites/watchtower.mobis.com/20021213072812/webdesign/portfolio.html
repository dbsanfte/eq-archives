The Day Trading Institute

The Day Trading Institute came to us in a jam. Their ISP, a DSL provider, had fallen subject to the fall of the dot com's. This were also moving their office to a brand new 1mil+ building that they were building. They had three problems. They needed a network in their new building, new Internet service, a new website and an interactive CD-ROM. Nexus ITG was glad to help with all of these problems

First order of business was the new building. Nexus ITG designed and built their network and telephone system from the ground up. Luckily we were got in near the beginning of constructions and were able to put the network requirements into the contractors plans. We were there throughout the entire building process to put in wiring as needed. After construction was complete we finished off our part of that job with the network equipment. The results was a fast network that didn't cost an arm and a leg.

Next was getting fast AND dependable Internet access to this new building. We carefully researched our options and settled on a regional DSL provider. We coordinated the install, set up our firewall and DTI was surfing.

Now while all of this was going on, there were two other issues. They had a website that their old ISP had thrown together and they were very unhappy with it. They were also going to a trade show and wanted to take an interactive CD with them. We had a solution that would get both accomplished in the least amount of time. First we employed V1Digital to come up with a snazzy layout and a 60 second video to put on their CD. Then we took that layout and made a set of html pages and an MPEG movie and put them on a business card CD. All in time for it to be burned before their trip to Vegas. While it was at the printers we took those same pages and created their database driven website. Their site includes a private login system, a place to sign-up for classes, a chat room and a streaming radio program. All of this was done under heavy time constraints, but they were happy with their CD when they got to Vegas and their accompanying website.

 

Doug Houston Outdoors

Doug came to use looking for a site that would compliment his show, Doug Houston Outdoors. Doug wanted his site to be flashy enough to compete with the other outdoors shows, but he also wanted it be intuitive and load quickly. That's what Nexus ITG gave him.

Doug also needed to be able to update his site often. Rather than create a site where Doug would have to come to us each time he needed to make a change or add a story to his site, we build a Content Management System just for him. Now Doug is able to make changes to his website as often as he sees fit. Don't forget to check out Doug's show, as well as his website.

 

 

Animal Rescue Foundation of Mobile

The Animal Rescue Foundation of Mobile was in need of an inexpensive website to show their pets that need a home. Nexus was able to donate a database driven website to them. Pets are entered into the database through a back-end by the ARF. Users are able to search the database by animal type, and will hopefully see a pet they would like to adopt. Nexus ITG would like to thank Mobile Internet Services for graciously donating server space and bandwidth to the Animal Rescue Foundation of Mobile.
