Mission Statement ... ?
While it is vogue to place a flowery mission statement on an informational site such as this one; and while some of our team members have been mission oriented during parts of their careers, we're not salesmen and we won't insult your intellegence here.

Instead, let us tell you who we are and what we believe ...
We can tell you we are a company based out of Mobile, Alabama and have no intentions of leaving. Due to our high level of customer care, we work mainly within 2 hours of the Mobile metropolitan area but go further when needed. We are a group of experienced, certified systems administrators that have been working As a Team for several years taking care of large corporate information systems that often cover the country. Our efforts in this environment and recent advances in high speed Internet access have allowed us to bring smaller businesses the same types of support that used to be reserved for expensive, large scale systems. We believe that many smaller businesses have been unable to afford quality, dedicated IT support and that this type of relationship has hurt the business community and the information industry. We believe it's time to try something new in Information Technology practices.

We Are Not ...
Salesmen. If we can help you, We'll offer. If not, We'll tell you.
Resellers. Making profit on the Systems we tell you to buy is a conflict of interest.

If you're the type of person who would defend themselves in a trial or perform your own appendectomy, we probably can't help you.

If you're the type of business owner who wants to run his business and allow a team of IS professionals to help with the computer magic, contact us. It won't cost you anything and we think you'll be glad you did.
