Diplomatic Corps

The Function of the Diplomatic Corps is to negotiate and establish treaties between the Fellowship of LoRE and other guilds, along with building alliances.

Is also known, as a division for greeting new members and making sure the new members know they are welcome.

Division members must know the allies’ rules and who their leaders are. Assists with conflict mediation when requested or as needed. Each member is required to keep an open line of communications with the allies to make sure that if they need assistance from us they can call on us.

Division members are also responsible for showing the citizens of Norrath what we our guild is about.
