The

Fellowship of LoRE

We are a simple people with a common belief. We have traveled many a mile from our homeland and have carried with us the Knight's Code of Honor.

As ancient as time itself, this code is our foundation...it is our mantra...our mentor. We live by it...we will defend it to the death...it is not just for EverQuest nor any other role playing game...but a code that we live by in real life. And isn't real life the the hardest game of all?

We accept all races...all classes...for no one individual is better than another, except when their actions prove otherwise my friend.

We make our home on the Rathe server! Come Join us in our adventures!
