Function

assets. Will work on getting the extra items needed for different quests and hold what they can for others.

Promotions are based upon one's deeds and actions as well as behavior toward Guild Members and the Citizens of Norrath.  Rate of promotion will be subject to the Pirates' Leaders. At all times you will adhere to the Knight's Code of Honor.

Being a Guardian of LoRE Treasure is not an easy task.  We are constantly on the lookout for weapons, armor, magical items, books, gems, materials, and money.  In addition, LoRE Pirates will train in skills to assist in the replenishment of our hoard.

Duties:
Swabbie through 1st Mate will seek out and acquire treasure but may not store.
Sargent at Arms may store weapons.
Squire through Captain may store weapons and armor..
Silver Knight may store any treasure.
Gold Knight may use resources to purchase required items for LoRE.
Admiral reports to the Duke or Duchess of Platinum.
