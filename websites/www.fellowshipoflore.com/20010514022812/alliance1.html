Non-Aggression Pact

Purpose:

I - To insure that the Fellowship of LoRE and The DragonSlayers treat each other with Honor and Respect.

II - To promote peaceful relations between the Fellowship of LoRE and the DragonSlayers.

Tenets:

I - The Fellowship of LoRE and the DragonSlayers agree not to initiate an act of aggression upon each other and to hold all members of their respective guilds responsible to adhere to the conditions of this pact.

II - It is agreed by both guilds that should any member of either guild be caught violating this pact, a full investigation shall be conducted and the matter will be resolved fully and expeditiously to the satisfaction of both guilds.

Terms and Conditions:

I - This pact may be terminated at any time by either the Fellowship of LoRE or the DragonSlayers when it is deemed that an irreconcilable difference has come between them and after leaders of the two guilds have made a concerted effort to reconcile those differences..

II - This pact shall remain in force as long as both the Fellowship of LoRE and the DragonSlayers are active guilds in the lands.

III - This pact shall be re-negotiated in the event that any two of the signatories of either guild are no longer active in the guilds.

Having read and hereby being in agreement with the terms and conditions, we do hereby sign this document and seal our bond.

Signed on this the 25th day of September in the year 1999
