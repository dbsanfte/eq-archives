The Charter of the Fellowship

We are the Knights. We are the People. We are the Strong...because we gather our strengths through our actions and our bond with one another. 

We co-exist in another world, and we bring our code to the world of Norrath. The code we follow is one we live...the one that we feel...not just the one that we speak. 

That Code is the Knight's Code of Honor. It comforts us. It guides us. It makes us strong.

We of the Lords of Retribution are here to make a new home on Norrath, in which, we must make a new name to distinguish our deeds from those of our brethren in the other land.

From this day forward, we shall be known as "The Fellowship of LoRE (EverQuest)". We shall continue the work of helping people in need through our actions.  We shall help defend the weak from the strong. We shall deal justice in the form of retribution to the wicked and evil in the world, so that those of lessor powers may walk in the darkness, without fear that they will be consumed by it.

Let it be known from this day forward, that the power of evil on Norrath has a new foe! That foe is the Fellowship of LoRE! 

Founding and/or Establishing Members will fill the positions of leadership within the guild.

A. The Regent: The Leader of the guild will hold this seat. This person will have authority over all guild matters, but may be overridden by the High Council.

The Regent will hold a tie-breaking seat in the High Council. Any veto by the High Council will put the issue back to the Regent for amendment.

If the issue is vetoed three consecutive times, the matter shall be put forth to the entire membership for general elections and the result shall become guild policy. The Regent accepts recommendations for advancement through structure and appoints members to available positions.

B. The High Council: These eight seats will be held by those responsible for the implementation of guild policy. Members shall have emergency powers to declare a state of emergency and act on own best judgment until The High Council can convene to discuss matter and vote.

The high council can, by unanimous vote, over ride any policy the Regent sets forth

In event of a war a person shall be selected as a fighting leader during that crisis.

The High Council consists of:

1. Treasurer/Financial Advisor

2. Treaty and Guild relations Advisor

3. Head of Intelligence

4. Head of Research

5. Head of the Events

6. Sword-Master

7. High Priest/Priestess

8. Master Explorer

C. Knights of the Round Table:

1. Comprised of Division Commanders at present time is also the High Council until the guild is larger.

2. Will provide input and recommendations on topics placed before them by The Regent and High Council.

3. Division Commanders report directly to the High Council.

D. Divisions

1. Before a member will be allowed to join a division. The member will be required to send the Regent a letter stating what the Knights Code of Honor means to them. A member has ten days after the guild invite to follow through with sending the Regent the e-mail. If the e-mail is not sent out the member will be removed until the Regent receives the e-mail.

2. To become a member in a division, each member must request to join that division. There is no time limit in joining a division; however, you can not be promoted or assigned things to do until you choose your division. To do this the member needs to contact the Duke/Duchess or Master of the Division they wish to join. (Names are listed on the members page) Once you get in contact with the Division leaders you will go through an interview for that division and, if accepted, will be giving an assignment. The Division Leader has the option to deny admittance to the division.

3. Divisions Commanders will track and assist those in their charge with training, information and in some cases, assist them in negotiating the pitfalls of the world of Norrath.

4. The division commanders will notify the Regent when a member is ready for promotion.
