Pirate Division

Procedures & Protocol

  1. Receipt of assets:
    1. Any member may take temporary possession of a guild asset with the intent to transfer the asset as soon as possible to a LoRE Pirate.
    2. Notation of who made the donation should be provided to the receiving Pirate. If at all possible please document the donation via e-mail.
    3. All Pirates must notify the Commander of the Pirate Division within 24 hrs. or less after the receipt of asset(s). Please inform the Commander of the items and quantities received and the donator.
    4. The Pirate Commander will provide storage guidance to the Pirate who received the asset(s).

  II. Request of assets:

    1. Any guild member may request an asset(s) from the Pirate Division by adhering to the following procedures.
    1. The request must be issued to your Division Commander via e-mail.
    2. The Division Commander will then forward the request to the Pirate Commander with approval and a priority annotation. Please note that the Regent must approve an item with Critical Priority.
    1. Low priority – issue the item as soon as possible when it is in the treasury.
    2. Top priority – issue the item as soon as possible and if not in the treasury then acquire by reasonable means.
    3. Critical priority – use any resource and method to obtain this item. Requires the Regent’s written approval.
    1. The Pirate Commander will acknowledge receipt of the request and provide status reports as required.
    2. Status reports on Critical items will be issued to the High Council as required.

III. Pirates’ Responsibilities:

    1. Duke or Duchess of Platinum has overall responsibility of LoRE treasury and must report to the High Council on guild assets.
    2. Pirates’ Commander has overall responsibility of day-to-day operations and will report to the Duke or Duchess of Platinum.
    3. Gold Knight may store any asset and use resources to obtain items under the authorization of the Pirate Commander.
    4. Silver Knight may store any treasure.
    5. Captain – Squire may store armor and weapons.
    6. Sargent of Arms may store weapons.
    7. 1st Mate – Swabbie may not store guild assets.
    8. All Pirates will seek out assets for our guild.
