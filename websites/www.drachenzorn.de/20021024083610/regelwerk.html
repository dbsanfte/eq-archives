Rules


1)
The mail goal of Drachenzorn is to have fun together in Everquest.


2)
The virtues of every member should be fairness, solidarity and all should be willed to help each other (No insults, no Ninja-Looting, preffering groups with DZ Members).


3)
We are a neutral guild, that's why all classes and races are allowed to join.


4)
To get a second char in this guild, your main char has to be at least level 50. This makes sure that our guild consists of mainly high level chars.


5)
The main language of Drachenzorn is English, but there exists a German channel ( /join Drachenzorn ) where every German speaking member and those who want to learn German are welcome.


6)
Any actions, which hurts the reputation of Drachenzorn are not being tolerated.


7)
The passing on of items of high-lvl chars to lower ones is voluntary and shouldn't be take for granted.


8)
Every member shouldn't be anonymous, except Clerics, Druids and Wizards, who are allowed to play in roleplay-modus.


9)
If somebody decides to leave Drachenzorn he has to give the property of Drachenzorn back.


10)
Behind every char there is a human being, that's why we forbid every kind of insult or being rude to each other.


11)
Drachenzorn is allied with the following guilds:
Nomadic Knights, Impending Desaster and Union Divine.
In order to talk to the members of our allied guilds there is a channel called furyoftheknight.


12)
Every main char is allowed to get items from our guildbank. To get nearer information read the rules of the bank in the bankforum.
