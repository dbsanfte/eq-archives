How to create a Magelo Account


1.) Go to www.magelo.com

2.) Register yourself ( there will be an E-Mail to you after you registered. Just click a link to activated, i think it was )

3.) Login

4.) Choose Characters

5.) Choose Add a Character

6.) Fill out fields ( to get the exactly AC and HP, your Char must unequiped, only a weapon in its hand with no stats on it ) After that is done, click Add a Character

7.) Chooce again Characters

8.) You will now find your Char there. Choose Manage his profile and you will find a table of all Slots. Either you will see your stats on the left side.

9.) To Add an item on your Char select Edit on the slot you want to equiped. Choose your item and click Update. Do this with all slots.

10.) If all slots are filled you have to save it. Type a filename under Save the current profile as and click Save. The saved profile will spawn under Stored profiles. You can make as many profiles you want to do. But i think only 2 profiles are needed. One standard gear u using and maybe one sv gear if you have it.

I think thats all for the beginning. Just feel free to ask me in game for further questions.
