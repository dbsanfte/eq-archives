Planes of Power Impressions --- 03-Nov-2002 by Irongut

First of all, no, this is not our new web site. I have changed the style sheet and formatting, but the new site will be going up on another server sometime in the near future. One thing that IS working is our new members section. Just click the link above to check it out.

Well, where to start. For one, I have to give credit to the devs at Sony Online for releasing, IMHO, the best EQ expansion. The rollout was without major incident, and not the massive bug- laden pos that was known as Shadows of Luclin. I have a few minor gripes though.

For one, the Devs need to address zone stability after boss-type mobs are defeated. We have had NightmareB and Plane of Disease crash multiple times after killing the bosses, effectively rolling back the loot and whatnot. Frustrating, but looks like it wouldn't be hard to solve.

Also, I know where you guys were going by disallowing COTH in some zones. Don't disable them completely in zones, just certain parts (ala Ssra temple) because not having coth in an EXP zone is absolutely stupid. I'm not fighting my group all the way back to the zone because a member our our exp group had to leave.

On to the good stuff. I'm having a blast raiding some of these places. There is allot of content too, coupled with high end zones from previous expansions there is elbow room to spare. I don't see a reason why guilds should feel the pinch and race each other (unless there is one friggen mob or zone with all the best loot we haven't seen yet, I friggen hope not).

The fights are quick and lethal, none of the 20 minute+ marathon endurance contests (at least not yet). This is good. A fight should be about strategy and tactics, and not on counting to 3 or pressing defensive.

We once again took out Terris Thule. The loot is fair, at least for us, but for lower tier guilds that will see her the loot will be great.
