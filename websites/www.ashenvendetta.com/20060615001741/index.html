As the orb turns... --- March 2nd - 2006 by Steelhammer

Ashen Vendetta would like to congratulate StPauli on the first Epic 2.0 for the guild!



Well deserved!

After a nasty and long round of recruiting/re-flagging Ashen Vendetta is once again stomping around in the Fortress of Solitude (aka.. Citadel of Anguish) like we own the place. I for one would just like to say... it's good to be back. After doing the Specialization trial for what seemed like the millionth time, things finally clicked and we KTFO'd the mob with only 2 deaths.

It's funny, looking back on this trial, the first time we did it we won without much trouble or preparation at all... we didnt have a full raid and we didnt even expect to win... but we did. On this round of reflagging this trial was becoming my personal albatross, we just couldn't seem to get things in synch for the win. I was beginning to lose hope. Tuesday night though my guildmates dug a little deeper, after a couple weeks of waiting we were all tired of this trial, I made promises to my guildmates that I wont disclose here... lets just say baby oil, woodland creatures and pop rocks are involved... but I digress. The point is we came through.

After the win spirits were high and the Asskicking wasnt anywhere near done for the evening, no good readers... we were just getting started. We moved into the Fortress of Solitude and started clearing house with a vengance... no, a Vendetta. Chaos Incorporated was back and ready to make some noise. We started off by stomping a muddhole in Tures a$$, and then moved on to see the Warden, killing everything along the way. Ture was old hat for us but the Warden was some new hotness that we hadn't faced before. We left a splatter mark and not much else.

Make no mistake, Chaos Incorporated has been dormant for a while, but we are up and moving again. We only move in 1 direction and that's forward. Get out the tractor and my overalls Ethel, it's farm time.

And the beat goes on...
