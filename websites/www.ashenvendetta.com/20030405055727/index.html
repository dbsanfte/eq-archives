The Witch is Dead --- 13 March- 2003 by Irongut
At long last, the fruits of creating a strategy to defeat a tough encounter pays off. Xegony, Queen of Air is by far the hardest EQ encounter we have faced and defeated. Just getting to her spawn location was hard, but the fight itself is one of the reasons to play EQ... Its exciting: The whole fight you are not sure if you will win or loose.

After many frustrating battles, she was finally put down. The entire guild takes part and everyone was masterful. The margin for error is almost nothing with her; yet Ashen Vendetta triumphs tonight. Very tough, very fun and the whole encounter has a massive pucker factor.

We have two Elemental Deities dead, and we'll soon add number 3. I hope you all like sushi.

Until next time and remember to support our troops!
