Welcome to Qvic! --- April 21st - 2005 by Analia

In the past few weeks Ashen has been focusing on Uqua while clearing time and doing epics and other various fights. Let me be the first to say that it hasn't been easy in Uqua for us. I think we trained ourselves in every way imaginable in that zone. We stuck with it though and tonight it paid off.

We blew through the first half of Uqua tonight looking better than we ever have in that zone. We got to the second gas chamber and like every other trip, it went from great to shit really fast. Someone clicked early and yeah we pretty much all died at the second chamber.

We didn't give up though. We rezed up, moved forward and finally got to see Barxt for our first time. We were not expecting to win, hell I was just happy we didn't end the night in a train. We charged into the room, tossed some dwarves at Barxt and knocked him the fuck out on our first attempt.

Qvic is certainly not the end game of EQ right now but I'm very proud of our guild hanging in there breaking into Qvic. Our goal was to be in Qvic before our merge to the Nameless and we have accomplished it. Now on to bigger and better things!






You can run but you can't hide! --- March 8th - 2005 by Analia

There we were, a group of Ashen in the middle of Lavastorm helping a few gnomes dig their way through a cave. Not knowing what was on the other side was part of the excitement. We worked quickly to gather the parts that the miners needed to blast forward. With one last blast the rock fell and out came a dragon to terrorize Lavastorm.


We gathered our forces and moved deep into Lavastorm to get a better look at Tirranun. The call was made by our fearless leader Steelhammer to charge, so we ran forward into battle with the intent of slaying the fiery dragon. The fight was fierce and before we knew it, the mighty dragon Tirranun turned its tail and ran away.


We didn't forget that day on the hill just above the pool of lava that emptied throughout the mountains. We explored the lands past the caves until we caught the tracks that could only be from such a beast that we fought in the mountains. We tracked him back to his lair where he stood there waiting for us to battle to the death once again.



Ashen charged up the bridge to his lair where Steelhammer gave the same call to charge. Swords were swinging, Spells were casting, and this time Tirranun had no where to run..

Meddling in the Affairs of Dragons --- February 17th - 2005 by Tragas

Well, it's expansion time again - this time with my favorite kind of monster: Dragons. Ashen Vendetta has always had a thing for dragons so naturally, we were pretty eager to check this new ones out.

In case you didn't know, in order to get to the Broodlands before the next day of the expansion there's a quest you need to do to get rid of the rocks. Well, instead of exploiting our way through, we did the quest, and as a result set off a script that opened up the Broodlands and spawned one bad-ass looking dragon:




This guy is by far the coolest looking dragon I've seen to date, so just like Ashen Vendetta would be the ones to wake the dragon (again), we decided since we spawned him, we might as well kick his ass too.

So, we went after our new lava-covered friend and fed him dwarves, paladins and wizards. This was the key to victory, as the effects of Moksau are not as potent as they once were and we were nearly out of gnomes.



So to make a long story short, we knocked this big bruiser down to 75% and he took off running with his tail between his legs. But we're not done with him yet - You can bet that you'll see his corpse on this page soon, then we'll see who is crunchy and good with ketchup!
