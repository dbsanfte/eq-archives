9/9 - Peaks and valleys (and more peaks).

Well its been almost a month since I last updated this sucker, blame it on the end of the summer, Labor Day, and the NFL gearing up. Actually, I was just lazy :)

So what's with the circa 1970's hippie title? No, I'm not quoting Crosby Stills and Nash; this is as good a summary of what the last few weeks have been for the crew. One minute you are kicking much virtual mob ass, the next you are turing into Corky and Webster fighting over the last of the kool aid. I have seen great fights, and I have seen, for lack of a better term, shit.

But hey, that's why we play the game, when you know one ill turn can cause corpse summoning.

For the most part we have been fighting in Vex Thall, Ssra, and a few odd bosses of this zone or that. A Shei Vinitras here, Burrower there, etc. Highlights include trying AoW with 34 people, most of those 2 boxing at 2 am on a weeknight. Got him to 7%. That was the most fun I've had loosing in a long time :)

So the Valleys? Wasting a perfectly good Emperor here and there. Training ourselves in VT.

The Peaks? Wasting a perfectly good AoW. Training ourselves in VT (and living).

After our slump of performing like a pick up Plane of Fear raid, we got back to biz. This latest patch saw us gaining mo powah from our squad of l33t melee clerics. Seriously, get rid of that stupid hammer and make the proc a buff (ala ranger self buff) so our clerics can use much better ratio weapons with much better stats.

Duh VI. Instead of giving clerics Melee power (i do agree that bash was good), you should have instead raised cleric def cap, and gave clerics a more flexible set of new spells instead of the (mostly) shitty new heals and summoned hammer. You are going to give druids and shamans new heals (and I agree both classes could use a bump in healing. Just a bump), toss clerics some love too. AE raid heals? Maybe a lure root. I dunno, ask the clerics I guess.

Ok, enough ranting fat boy.

Also with this patch, VT was upgraded. Excellent,... er um well more like ok.

So what's new? Well for starters the yard trash seems to have less hit points (although this could just be every cleric swinging his new toy and no afk casters trying to super nuke for that phat red con exp). Certain spots are no longer safe, and well, I'm sort of on the fence about this but its true now that you got to earn those named every trip.

Oh yea, no more f'n AE blind. Guess the bazillion "/feedback Hey, AE blind sucks monkey nutz" from every VT enabled guild payed off. Seriously, there were times when we would get 3-5 blinders at once (all non mezzable kind) and you would be staring at your blank monitor for 5 minutes straight until they finally came and lay the smack down. No amount of skill could save you, if you didn't pull that many you'd get caught in the respawn. Fun, eh? ;)

Well, I'm glad to say that VT is now much better overall. Loot that was utter crap is now quite good. Still a tough place, but Aten Ha Ra remains tops on reward for any mob in Luclin. From just this last clearing, look at what we got (and I know i missed a few) :

All in all, was good to get in and clean up in VT. Oh yea, Name of the week!
