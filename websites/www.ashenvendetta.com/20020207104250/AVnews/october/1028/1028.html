NToV Cleared (10.28.01)
 
 
 
 
 

Its feels great to have finally overcome so many obstacles, to have defeated all the named Dragons of the North Temple of Veeshan. There were times during this expedition that all seemed lost. Dealing with bad pathing, huge amounts of lag (thx 150+ multi-guild raids and zone campers), lots of LD and frustrated people, and just some awful luck.

Ashen Vendetta really pulled together, defeating no less than 8 dragons in one night, to set up the final battles with Lord Vyemm and Vulak'Aerr. The rumors were that Vyemm was the terror of NToV; but after one test attempt early in the day and one battle getting him to 10%, Lord Vyemm fell. We confirm he is quite a nasty fellow, but not strong enough to end our push for Vulak.

Vulak'Aerr was next to be defeated. The first battle we found out that he can gate, we though we had him drained... alas at no life he returned home. Our determination was not in question. The next pull, after a long slow fight (if you have fought him you know what I mean heh) Vulak went down. The North Temple fell silent, all of its great beasts slain.

I'd like to thank all the members of AV, and the guests that popped in and out, who put allot of (often frustrating) time into a great trip! You guys are what make the game enjoyable to play!

-Irongut
