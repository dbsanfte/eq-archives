NToV, patch, hijinx!(11.12.01)
 
 

Ahhh patch day, always a joy-filled occasion. Login server is overloaded, bugs galore, and my personal favorite, zone crashes.

The zone crash bug (target group buff on a corpse) didn't effect us as much as most (poor Legacy of Steel from the Nameless server). We got hit only our first night of fighting the named, had to take out Eashan and Ikitar a couple of times. No big deal, few extra goodies.

We ended up clearing the North Temple named without much of a hitch. I'd say the best action of the trip was (as usual) Lord Vyemm. We only had 7 clerics (ok not a big deal), but we killed him with one his guards on the pull. Then two Onyx Drakes decided it would be fun to join in when we had Vyemm to about 50%. What a wickedly fun experience! I even lived past enrage!
