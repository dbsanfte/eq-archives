Walk with God, Odum (1.13.02)

In this game of Everquest, that each one of us log in day in, day out; we get to know those behind the virtual persona. We come to learn about the real person, good or bad, love or hate; every character we meet. It's only human, that strong bonds of appreciation and long lasting friendships are formed in a most unique way... through an online video game.

One such fellow, that went by the name of Odum, crossed our paths some time ago. He was strong in his motivation and determination, was always there for you when you needed a hand or some kind words. He pleasently surprised everyone that came to know him. His depth of character was like a beacon; a great friend, a truley upstanding human being. He represented all the best qualities we aspire too, and we are all much better people for knowing him.

Little did anyone know (that didn't know him in real life), Odum had been battling a terminal illness for sometime. I hope, in some small way, that playing this game helped ease his days along, that maybe being a part of and around virtual friends brought him some comfort.

His playtime began to wain as his struggle, with something most can't even imagine going through, became too much. Last week he logged in and said his goodbye, to spend his last days on this earth with his family.

He lost his fight this weekend, the young man we only knew as Odum, left this world to begin a new journey. Eddie, may that new journey be peaceful and wonderous!

You and your family are in our hearts, thoughts and prayers. I wish I had more time to get to know you even better. The people who have come to know you as friend and family will miss you. God bless you Eddie.

Please feel free to share your memories.

-Irongut
