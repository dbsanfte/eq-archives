Sleepers Tomb Edition (8.25.01)

Twenty-nine brave souls ventured into the depths of the Sleeper's lair Friday night. The first target: Master of the Guard. Result: Victory. Grats Braminaut on your new War Lance.

We then moved quite a bit further slaying creatures as they attempted to keep us out. We made it to the last golem, the Final Arbiter. His goal was to defend the Sleeper's chamber. He found nothing but pain and suffering.


With the Final Arbiter defeated, AV got its first look at the Sleeper's Chamber. I must admit it is one of the more incredible sights one will see in all of Norrath.


We prepared to face the warders. First up, Nanzata the Warder. This dark purple lizard didn't give us much trouble and was taken out. Congrats Irongut (thanks AV!) and Hanadan on theirWarswords , Quazarr and Catarina with new Resist Stones, and Rensak on his Sword.


Next up, Tukaarak. Grats Dealdor on your Brawl Stick, Analia and Resnak on your Masks (Go here for gnome invasion) and Darkhealer on your Resistance Stone, not to mention Kaelic's new Staff.


The last Warder on the night, Hraashna, was killed on the first attempt: like the other two. His corpse had a new Bracer for Motef, Braminaut, and Moonhaven; Allayna and Corvan received Gloves , with Serraghost getting a new Axe.


With the 3 warders destroyed (sorry we had 29 not 60, and don't want anything to do with fubar'd scripting), we moved out killing the Progenitor to finish clearing all named mobs in Sleepers Tomb except 4th warder and The Sleeper. He didn't drop anything, except the satisfaction that all named died on our first trip the first fight. Nice work AV!
