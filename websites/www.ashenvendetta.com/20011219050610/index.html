Dog days of Exp (12.17.01)
 
 
 

With patches being the order of the day, the crew has been concentrating on maxing out innate metabolism and boosting charisma (anything to help get that uber horse!).

So many a day we all hang out in great zones full of friendly and courteous folks, slowly increasing our supply of AA points (not AA meetings, Ody). The joyous /ooc spam (I'M A COOL GUY~ GIVE ME ATTENTION PLZ!), training and camp stealing have set in. The holiday spirit is alive and well, but instead of Santa's list we have the ignore list.

But on to something more substantial. During these dog days of exp grinds and exploring, AV took out some of the sub bosses of Ssra Temple. The loots were decent, and the spells are a welcome addition to raids anywhere. I'm glad there is a bunch of caster stuff in Luclin. Headway was made towards more epic encounters in the new lands.

Last night we went for some mobs a bit tougher, good old Tormax and Yelinak. We dropped Tormax in his own house again, what a chump. Afterwards Yelinak was put down; again easily.
