Maybe I'm too sensitive or perhaps I have just played too long, but I've decided to leave UO and all of it's inherent bullshit behind. It seems as though the quality of the UO "community" is in an unstoppable decline, and I simply cannot tolerate it anymore. From the stupidly named characters who say 'sup?' when I say 'hail', to the bank thieves who try to sell dye tubs with black bonnets on them, to the blockers/looters/noto PK's/kill & loot thieves who camp out the hunting grounds.......I've simply had enough.

Now I've heard that I shouldn't leave cause it only assures that the assholes "win", and while that may be true, I really don't care anymore. As far as I'm concerned they can have it.

I've also heard that all will be better when the "new lands" open up, but as I've heard Lum say, you can't code morality, so nothing will be better. It's just gonna be another area for the assholes to dominate, and a new system for them to work around to continue with their antisocial behaviour (which they WILL). And by the way (just to make it clear), I'm NOT referring to PK's. Because with PK's, you know what they're about right up front. They don't hunt alongside you for an hour, taking your heals and assistance only to stab you in the back as soon as they can (the typical blocking/looting playstyle displayed at the Terathan Keep comes to mind).

To all of the people I've met in-game over the last 2+ years, who were actually decent, I thank you. It's people like you (who understand that the real gold in UO can be found in friendships and adventure), and that made it all worthwhile. It's just too bad that most of you have left as well, and have been replaced by 13 year old boys with the intellect of a canteloupe, and a chip on their shoulder the size of Texas.

Oh well.....perhaps all will be better in UO2, although I expect it will be more of the same only in 3-D.

Farewell all. *bows & exits*
