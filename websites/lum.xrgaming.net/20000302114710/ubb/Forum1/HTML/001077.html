Well, as I think it could be a pretty cool system, even though my character isn't pvp, I thought I would jog on over to Britian and check it out.

After the usual starting confusion people seem to have at starting any speech-related ingame function (at least those who don't know you need to set up crystals, etc.), the conference began. In attendance about 7 evils and a couple of goods. The evils were, I believe, Ta'Kiers (sp?). Also a few oddly colored robes (phone sex counselors I'm guessing), and 4 or 5 smurfs.

The 'good' smurf went on to explain that stratics was wrong, you need to have 40 game hours logged to go good, not 24 as mentioned on stratics. You go to a shrine and say "I am a wicked goody 2 shoes' (or something like that ) to become a hero. This gives you a sphere and 10 lifeforce. Spheres are like circles of magic, lifeforce is like mana. Ok, now to you, mr 'evil' smurf.

The 'evil' smurf went on to explain that stratics was wrong, you need to have 40 game hours logged to go evil, not 24 as mentioned on stratics. You go to a shrine and say "I am a wicked evil cookie' (or something like that ) to become evil. This gives you a sphere and 10 lifeforce. Spheres are like circles of magic, lifeforce is like mana. (notice the similarities?)

Then the 'evil' smurf went on to explain how you must SHOUT teh words to become evil at a shrine. To which an argument ensued that you in fact did not, and all the evils there hadn't. To which I had to tell the 'evil' smurf that to have game items recognize smurf talk, they need to shout. Just like the comm crystals that the Ta'Kiers had to bring, that weren't registering the smurf's voices!

So after all this, it's now an hour later. 10pm is pretty late for my current schedule, so I had to leave. I simply don't have 8 hours to wait to get to something past the basics (which I think everyone in attendance knew anyway). When I left, there was a crowd of 7 left from about 15.

Note to everyone: If you put on a conference, know how to work the room so everyone can hear. And have it done beforehand. And show up early. And DON'T let any slow typers speak! Have them prepare something beforehand and run it through xena's tool or something.

All in all, I say don't let counselor's run events. Let the seers, it's what they do.

(I also saw the counselor there that I had to help out about an hour beforehand. This would mark the second time I have helped a newbie thief in Vesper find the guildmaster. I should start a thieves guild, eh?)

end event rant
