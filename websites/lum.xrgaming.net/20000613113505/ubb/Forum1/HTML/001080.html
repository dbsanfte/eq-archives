Fencing has been nerfed for a long long time.

It used to be that damage wasn't measured until the end of the 'swing cycle', and back then as a fencer I was almost unbeatable in the duels I fought (By doing the 'fencers dance' and stepping back before that halberd could hit me). They changed it so that the damage is dealt at the beginning of the swing nowadays.

Last few melee sessions I've fought haven't been conclusive.

Fencing is *still* fairly effective in PvM, and with a 50 something parry my main character can manage anything short of an ogre lord/terathan.

As far as I can tell, in straight melee against someone with equivalent stats/skills, a katana will win over a kryss most of the time, but the kryss will win over anything else, especially if you can heal.

That said, I still prefer fencing, a Gm Kryss, especially one coated with poison, in the hands of a 100 dex GM Fencer is very effective. A long spear is also very effective against heavily armored opponents. A war fork is a little slower, but does appreciable damage (and only takes one hand.)

Overall tho, I'd say that swords still has the advantage.
