Why do you play the games that you play? I know of people who swear up and down by all 3 of the major games out now. Hell I even know a few people who love 10six already (and even a gimpy person that still likes M59).

I used to be very engrossed at least once for each of those games (M59 lasted about an hr for me though), but different faults dragged me away. Things like exp loss on death for EQ, the virtual dial up lag fest of UO (plus the need to macro), and well the stagnability of AC (and the beta advantage on the PvP server).

I still play EQ somewhat, but it bores me with the lack of viable pvp options (also someone else pays for the account for me). The other 2, I havent touched for awhile. I am a powergamer, and get very addicted and in depth with the games that I play but this might also lead me to the disillusionment eariler than most. Couple that with the fact that I won't pay for a "game" that I don't find to be fun.

This leads me back to my question, "Why do you play the games that you play?" A lot of you bitch about how the game sucks.. but continue to pay and play it. Some people still have an UO account just to refresh their house. If fun is the reason that you have, list why you find it fun. Maybe with enough positive feedback and good reasons to play these games, I can get myself back into at least one of them. Instead of just waiting for the next game that I can find a fault with.
