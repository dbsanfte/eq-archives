For crying out loud, someone please give this guy his account back! Or, at least tell him exactly why he was banned.

You know, on the topic of bannings, I was reading Markee Dragon's site for pricing info, and I saw the bit on Tiberius being banned. I just don't understand why he was banned. I know, he posted bugs. What I don't understand is why this is a bannable offense (and please spare me the "OSI can ban you for anything they want" - I'm talking philosophy here). Why is this in the TOS? If you release a product that has bugs, it is your responsibility to fix them. In the case of Ultima, bugs don't get fixed nearly as fast if they are not publized, and there is no better way to protect yourself than to know what bugs people are using. I've never used a bug, but reading a bugsite and applying the information saved my house from being looted twice.

I don't disagree with banning people who USE bugs. Set up the code to catch them and ban their asses. I do have a problem with the banning of someone who posts bugs. I guess I'm enamored of that free speech thing. It just seems a little fascist to ban someone for something they do out of game.

Yes, I know it's their game. Yes, I know they can do whatever they want. But, where's the line? Verant found out where the line was when they wanted to snoop computers. Where do we draw it for OSI?
