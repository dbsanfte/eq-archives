After taking a break from UO for a bit I'm back playing. I've noticed a lot of improvements, but there are still a few logic al "misses" in the game.

I'm not talking about the immersive fiction aspects. A person being able to call energy out of nothing and hurl it towards his opponent is part of the game. And some things have to be added to make the game more playable...like putting your boat in your pack.

But there are a few things that are just, well....wrong.

To wit:

1.) I can sheer a sheep with a hatchet, but not a pair of scissors. ("Damn, sorry about that....I don't think ears grow back but you never know....")

2.) Ghosts are stopped by trees, and walls, and water. They can walk through doors, but can't open them.

3.) I can be in the middle of several Orc spawns, with an etting beating on me, and several harpies hovering, do tracking and get the notice "You see no sign of monsters"

Here are some things I've allways wondered about.

How do tailor shops stay in buisness? I don't know how many fancy shirts I've sold to those guys over the years, but I want to know where they go?

Why the hell are there so many boots in the waters of Brittania?

Has anyone ever seen an outhouse in all of Sossaria? No wonder people name their kids "PiMpmAZZter". I would be cranky as hell too if there wasn't a place to sit and take a crap.

Why don't town criers have more news? Hell, even some gossip would be better than just standing there for months on end with nothing to say.

Since the banks don't give out loans, and there is no property to purchase, how do they stay in buisness? For that matter how DO they transfer funds from one town to another?

If I can transport my body from one place to another, kill alligators with a single swipe of a sword, why the hell can't I pull up a blade of grass so I can place a house?

Why do all paintings have to be fished out of the ocean? Are art galleries all underwater? How does a painter make any money in this land? (one possibility is that it's an art subsidy program, where the government dumps all art created)

Who pays for the guards? I've never paid taxes, as far as I can tell.

Is there no social security program? Is being unemployeed so bad that a merchant is better off commiting suicide when he's dismissed?

Why don't NPCs who are kidnapped by orcs just recall back to town instead of having to be escorted?

For that matter, who the hell do the orcs expect to pay a ransom when they kidnap somebody? I've never seen them make any profit off kidnapping.

Wouldn't it better a better use of money to just buy recall scrolls than risk your life by being escorted by warriors?

Where do ghosts come from if everyone is resed anyways?

That's a few that come to my mind.
