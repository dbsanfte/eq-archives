Charisma
 Charisma is an important stat for all classes in the game. It determines your natural wit, charm, and attractiveness. It is especially important for certain races such as Ogre's who have a very low charisma to begin with. So what makes charisma important?

Well first off a high charisma will cause NPC's to react more favorably to you. While this does not make a huge difference it is a subtle change that is an added bonus.

Most importantly charisma affects the cost of items when dealing with the vendors in the game. Characters with a high charisma will not only pay less for their items, but they will also get more when selling those same items.

One other aspect that charisma effects is charming monsters. Characters with high charisma are more likely to be able to charm other beings and will be able to control these beings for longer periods of time.
