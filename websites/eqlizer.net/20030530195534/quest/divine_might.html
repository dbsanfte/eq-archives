Divine Might Quest [PAL]
Submitted by: Zenki Shikigami

The end result of this quest is the level 50 Paladin spell, Divine might. It is a self only buff that adds a 65 damage magic based proc to all you attacks.

This post does not included any of the npc text or story line, just the fastest and easiest way to get the spell.

You need 6 dusty bones, a dusty sword and shield, and a gem encrusted coffin.

1. Dusty Leg bone: Go to Beholders maze and find Diggs Duggin. Give him 1 pick( you get the pick from the vendor inside the bank of Kaladim), and 2 dwarven ales.

2. Dusty Skull: Mino caves in Steamfont, inside Meldraths lair

3. Dusty Ribcage: The garden in the Castle Mistemoore

4. Dusty Backbone: The Ghost pit room in Unrest

5. Dusty Jaw bone: Underwater caves in Everfrost

6. Dusty finger bones: Phinigel's lair in Kedge Keep

7. Dusty Sword: Mines in Kaladim, in the water

8. Dusty Shield: Cliff ledge on right side of docks in Erudin

9. Gem encrusted coffin: Go to Ping Fuzzlecutter in Freeport and hail him, then say "Tell me about the coffin.." (Do not forget to inculde the 2 dots at the end or it will not work). He needs several gems inculding a Diamond and a "special Fire Emerald". The special fire emerald you get by giving Drake Mountainstorm in Firiona Vie a regular fire emerald.

10. After you have the coffin put the 6 bones, sword, and shield inside then hit combine.

11. Take the coffin to Simon Aldicott in West Commons by the lake.
-----BE WARNED------: After giving him the coffin a lvl 50 skeleton appears next to you. He hits for about 140 and has around 9000hp.

12. After killing the skeleton loot the ashes off him and give them to Simon.

13. Presto! You are now the pround owner of Divine Might, a truely powerful spell.
