Ranger Spells

Level 9

Endure Fire

This spell adds to a players resistance to fire.

Flame Lick

Flame lick is a direct damage spell. This is a low damage spell but is great for fetching monsters and also lowers their armor clas.

Glimpse

This spell lets you take a brief glimpse at objects far away. Basically it gives you a 15-20 second period where your sight is zoomed in to the distance.

Lull Animal

This spell will can cause animals that are normally agressive to not attack if succesful.

Minor Healing

Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.

Skin Like Wood

This is the first in the line of buffing spells. It adds to both the armor class and hit points of a player.

Snare

Snare is an interesting twist on the root line of spells. While it does not hold a monster perfectly still(and prevent it from fleeing) it does drastically lower it's movement rate and has a much longer duration.

Level 15

Burst Of Fire

This is a direct damage spell.

Camoflauge

This is an outdoor only invisibility spell.

Cure Poison

This spell will counteract poisons.

Feet Like Cat

This spell will raise a players agility score.

Grasping Roots

Grasping Roots is an upgrade to snare. This spell actually roots the monster in place(instead of slowing him down) and also does a small amount of damage for the duration.

Invoke Lightning

This is an area effect spell which centers around your target. Beware however it will also damage you if you are within range. This spell is outdoor only.

Thistle Coat

This spell creates a mild damage shield.

Level 22

Bind Sight

This very interesting spell allows you to bind your sight to your target and view from their perspective.

Enduring Breath

Enduring breath is a water breathing spell. This is important if you wish to go to some of the underwater locations in the game. It is also useful if you get rooted while underwater.

Harmony

Harmony is an area effect spell which reduces monsters aggressiveness.

Ignite

Ignite is a direct damage spell.

Light Healing

This is the second in the line of healing spells. It heals roughly 25 points of damage.

Skin Like Rock

This is the second in the skin series. It may not be stacked with others in the line(see skin like rock). It adds armor class and hit points.

Ward Summoned

This spell is a direct damage spell for use versus summmoned monsters(elements). It has a very good damage:mana efficiency compared to your other direct damage spells.

Level 30

Barb Coat

This spell adds armor class as well as a light damage shield.

Cancel Magic

This spell will cancel out a magic effect on you or another party member. It can be a life saver if someone in your group get's charmed or has one of those particularly nasty Damage over Time spells cast on them.

Eyes Of The Cat

This spell will grant a player infravision. It is really only useful for human rangers.

Invigore

Invigore will restore a characters stamina.

Shield Of Thistles

Shield of thistles is a damage shield.

Stinging Swarm

Stinging swarm is a damage over time spell. This is a great spell to use during combat as it will slowly eat away at your opponents hit points.

Strength Of Earth

This spell will add to a characters strength ability for a period of time.

Level 39

Calm Animal

This spell will prevent an animal from attacking your party by lowering it's agressiveness.

Careless Lightning

Careless lightning is a direct damage spell. It does roughly 99 points direct damage.

Dismiss Summoned

Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the level 22 ward summoned spell. This spell does a good amount of damage to summoned monsters and also features the best damage:mana ratio of any ranger spell.

Heal

This is the last heal spell that rangers will ever receive. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.

Levitate

Levitate allows you to levitate above ground. This can be useful if you are on a thin ledge and the mobs are casting spells which knock you off balance(particularly useful in lower guk). It can also be useful in places where there is a lot of lava or for quickly travelling over bodies of water.

Skin Like Steel

Skin like steel is the third in the skin like series of spells which add armor class and hit points.

Spirit Of The Wolf

Spirit of the wolf is one of the most desired spells in the game. This spell allows you to travel quickly in outdoor zones. It also is used in conjunction with Damage over Time spells in a cheesy tactic known as kiting where you outrun your opponent while the DoT spells slowly eat away at his hit points.

Level 49

Bramblecoat

Bramblecoat is the last in the line of thistlecoat spells. It adds armor class and a small damage shield.

Ensnaring Roots

This spell is a root spell which also does some direct damage.

Immolate

Immolate is a damage over time spell which also lowers a monsters armor class.

Resist Fire

Resist fire is an upgrade to the level 9 endure fire. It grants greater resistance to fire based attacks.

Shield Of Barbs

This is an upgrade to the shield of thistles spell. It is a higher level damage shield.

Superior Camoflauge

Superior camoflauge is an upgrade to camoflauge. While they both render you invisible this one works indoors as well as outdoors.

Wolf Form

This spell allows you to take the form of a wolf also taking on some of it's characteristics(higher ATK and faster movement). Of mention is that NPC's and monsters will react to you as if you were a wolf possibly preventing you from being attacked by certain mobs. It is a self only and outdoor only spell.
