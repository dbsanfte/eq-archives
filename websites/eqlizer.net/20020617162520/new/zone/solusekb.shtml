Solusek B

Solusek B is also known as Nagafen's lair. It is the home of Nagafen the fierce red dragon. This dungeon is well tuned for characters level 35 and up. There are several entrances and exits from this dungeon. All of the entrances are in either Solusek A or Lavastorm.

One of the things that should be noted in this dungeon is that all of it's denizens can see invisible players. You will not be able to invis yourself and explore this dungeon.

There are several distinct sections of this dungeon. The first section is readily apparent when you enter the dungeon. This is the Kobold section of the dungeon. Kobolds range from level 32 to 42. There are several named kobolds each of which drops magical items.

The second section of this dungeon is known as the bug and rodent section of the dungeon. This section includes high level(40 and up) bats, beetles and spiders. There are several "named" bugs each of which carries powerful high level items. Some example of bugs are sonic bats and noxious spiders.

The third section of this dungeon is the Fire Giant section. You must go past the bug section to reach this point. Fire giants are all extremely high level. These fire giants are instant death to all but the highest level players. It is best to bypass the giants all together. You can raise favor with the giants by killing fire goblins in Solusek A.

Deep past all of the kobolds, and bugs and giants is the lair of Nagafen himself. Nagafen is the fierce red dragon shown in screenshots on Verant's website. It will take a minimum of multiple level 50 parties to tackle this beast.
