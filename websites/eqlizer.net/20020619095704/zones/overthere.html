Kunark

The Overthere

Location
The Overthere is located on the continent of Kunark.


Zone Type
The Ovethere is a low to mid level zone. It houses Fort Danak which is the evil races stronghold on Kunark.



Zone Borders
The Overthere borders with Timorous Deep, Skyfire Mountains, Frontier Mountains, Howling Stones and the Warslilk Woods. It links to Oasis as well via Timorous Deep. You can catch the barrel barge from Oasis to ogre island of Timorous. Then you can hop on to the evil vessel from there that leads to the Overthere.


Inhabitents
This zone houses Fort Danak which is the evil races stronghold to Kunark. Thus you can find a fortress full of evil denizens here. You will also find Sarnaks and Scorpiki. Unintelligent beings include tigers, kunark rhinos and succulants.


Route
The Overthere can be reached from Antonica by hopping on to the barrel barge in Oasis. Be forewarned if you are not on good terms with evil races then this route is a bad idea.This will drop you off on to ogre island in Timorous Deep. To the right along the shore from where you are dropped off you will find another set of docks. The boat to the Overthere will land there.
