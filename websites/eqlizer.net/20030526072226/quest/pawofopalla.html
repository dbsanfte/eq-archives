Paw of Opalla
This quest was submitted by Nicholus D'Bouncer:

This quest takes place (for the most part) in Kerra Ridge (called "Kerra
Isle" by most people... located at the western most edge of Toxxulia Forest across the water) but begins in Qeynos Sewers.

1)  Travel to Qeynos and in the North Qeynos zone enter the Qeynos Sewers through a walk-through wall with vines to the left of the gate (if you are facing the gate).   Travel down the hole in the floor until you zone into the Sewers.  Then walk around the Sewers until you find the NAMED Begger (not sure of his name) that walks around down there.  He has the Severed Paw on him (the Paw Container for the rest of the pieces to go into).  In order to get the Container from him you can either give him a Rat Ear Pie (bought somewhere in Qeynos, not sure where), or you can do what I did and just KILL him (he's in the low 20's and you get factions hits).

2)  OK, now you have the Container... all you need are the 4 rings to go inside of it.  3 out of the 4 rings can be found in Kerra Ridge on High
Priestess Mitty (about level 18 and spawns every 20 minutes or so in the Temple and is surrouned by level 14 Priestesses that can make her feel "much better").   These 3 rings are RANDOM drops on Mitty and they are...

The Copper Ring (+1sta -1cha)... is dropped every hour or two... (remember totally random, so your results may vary)

The Silver Ring (-1cha +1wis)... is dropped every 3-4 hours... (remember totally random, so your results may vary)

The Gold Ring (unsure of stats)... I've camped Mitty for over 30 hours and never got one... others get it within 5 hours... guilds have camped and not gotten it in 100+hours... It's supposedly a 10% chance of her having it... but I killed her over 100 times and she didn't drop 10 rings... (remember totally random, so your results may vary)

3)  The 4th Ring is the Platinum Ring (unsure of stats)... This can be found in Befallen (undead dungeon in West Commonlands)... It can be found on a low 20's Mummy that spawns near the enterance inside.  This is a very rare spawn  that has (supposedly) a 3% chance of spawning after killing it's PlaceHolder  (thought to be the Plagued Rat or Dry Bone Skeleton).

4)  After getting all 4 rings put them in the Container and hit Combine and  you have yourself the Paw of Oppalla.
