Tuesday, September 24, 2002

Shrinkage In Every Zone?
 17:55 EST - Meshell

It seems the spell "Shrink", used by the Shaman and Beastlords, might be expanding to include all zones instead of Dungeons and a few other random indoor zones. This is only happening on the Test Server right now, but let's cross our fingers and hope that is will spill over to the rest of the game soon. This can seriously assist in decreasing some forms of lag when doing a large raid, as well as help make room in small raid areas (RoF for example).
