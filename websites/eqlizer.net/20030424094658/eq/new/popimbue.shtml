Planes of Power Imbuing/Misc. Guide

 

These spells are made by players, using the recipe below. The imbue diamond spell needed can be found in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport from Locarme Zennix (92, 287). The spell combine is a no fail combine and can be done by anyone.

spell: imbue ____ raw diamond + spell: imbue diamond + component from below list
  • imbue storm: storm rider blood
  • imbue fire: fire mephit blood
  • imbue valor: metallic liquid
  • imbue justice: alkaline etched stone
  • imbue nightmare: nightmare mephit blood
  • imbue torment: putrescent blood
  • imbue war: war wraith blood
  • imbue air: air mephit blood
  • imbue earth: earth mephit blood
  • imbue water: water mephit blood
Combin in: planar jeweller's kit

PoP Imbues - the gems

You first have a copy of the spell in their spellbook. The recipe for the spell is listed above.
gray diamond of storms raw diamond, storm rider blood DRU (62) or ENC (62) cast spell: imbue storms wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
red diamond of fire raw diamond, fire mephit blood MAG (65) or ENC (65) cast spell: imbue fire wt 0.1, tiny, all/all

white diamond of justice raw diamond, alkaline etched stone SHM (62) or ENC (61) cast spell: imbue justice wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
black diamond of nightmares raw diamond, nightmare mephit blood ENC (62) or NEC (62) cast spell: imbue nightmares wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
purple diamond of torment raw diamond, putrescent blood ENC (62), or NEC (63) cast spell: imbue torment wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
orange diamond of war raw diamond, war wraith blood SHM (64) or ENC (63) cast spell: imbue war wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
cloudy diamond of air raw diamond, air mephit blood MAG (64) or ENC (64) cast spell: imbue air wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
brown diamond of earth raw diamond, earth mephit blood MAG (62) or ENC (62) cast spell: imbue earth wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
aqua diamond of water raw diamond, water mephit blood MAG (65) or ENC (65) cast spell: imbue water wt 0.1, tiny, all/all
