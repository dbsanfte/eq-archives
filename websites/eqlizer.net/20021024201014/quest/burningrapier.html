Burning Rapier
This quest was submitted by Zerg Sentinents from Everlore:

1. Goto Lon the Redeemed in the temple of Solusek Ro. He wants you to quest to find two gems (stamina and rightiousness) which you are to give to Ortalius in Sro. Talk to him, and he sends you with a note to his friend the poisoner.

2. Go find Conium Darkblade in Dagnor's Cauldron(poison merchant). Give him the note, and he gives you an axe called "never stops chopping" and a clue about the "top of the world".

3. Unnecessary step: Talk to Falyn in the back room of "top of the
world" in Highpass, and he sends you to Bryan.

4. Goto Bryan on the deck at the Lumbermill Inn in Highpass. Give him the axe and he gives you a bag to fill with 10 different beverages from around the world. These are:

Underfoot Triple Boch - Kaladim - Tumpy Irontoe
Tunare's Finest - Felwithe - Wela Muselender
Vasty Deep Ale - Sister Island in OOT - Or in Erudin
Lendel's Grand Lauger - Rivervale - Mac Deeppockets
Gator Gulp Ale - E. Commons Tunnel - Bubar
Frozen Toe Rum - Halas - Scon McDaniel
Oblong Bottle (LORE/NO DROP) - OOT @ location neg 1611, pos
2924
Blood Spirits (LORE/NO DROP) - "a hemo enologist", Mistmoore,
garden
Clockwork Oil Stout - Clockwork CX, Solusek A - (Pickable)
Blackburrow Swig -

Need a Blackburrow Cask. Found on Gnoll Brewers/Master Brewers in Blackburrow. A common spawn is at loc 32,32 on level 3. Placeholder is a Gnoll Guardsman. Brewer is rare spawn, and Master Brewer ultra rare.

Combine in the Blackburrow Cask: (no brewing skill needed to
combine)

- 4 BB skins, rare drop of elite gnolls or commanders
- 2 BB scalps from pups
- 2 casks of Ol' TJ's fierce brew

The Blackburrow Cask disappears after you correctly combine the
ingredients to make the Swig.

5. Return the bag to Bryan, and he gives you the Gem of Stamnia.

6. Goto the Freeport Sewers and find Toxdil. Ask him about the gems, and then about the Gem Case. He tells you that he has the key, but will only give it to you for the gem inside the case. He says he last sold it to a jewler.

7. Talk to Noresa Sparkle in West Commons. She asks you to save her from Kiz Gix. She also tells you of a "Gem Case", which you would need two keys to open, and would have to return both box and keys to the "original boxcrafter" to find what is inside.

8. Kiz Gix roams around befallen side of WC. Upon killing him you recieve a note writen in Goblin, telling you of a Dark Elf "Outcast" who stole a sparkling purse.

9. Find the Outcast (Lydia the exiled) in Sol A in the High Shaman room. Killing the outcast gets you the Sparkle Purse.

10. Return the Sparkle Purse to Noresa Sparkle and she gives you one of the keys needed to open the "Gem Case".

11. Find the Gem Case. It is hidden behind a lock door in the slums of E freeport that you must pick. (Approx 150 lockpick skill) It is guarded by a dog named Scrapps. Spawn time on case is unknown.
** Gem Case spawn might be broken or moved **

12. Go back to Noresa and purchase off her a "Sparkling Saphire" (this is the Gem Toxdil wanted) (Cost Approx 500pp)

13. Return this Saphire to Toxdil, and he gives you the other key necesary to open the Gem Case.

14. Give the Gem Case and both keys to Bubar in the East Commons Tunnel to recieve the Gem of Righteousness.

15. Return both Gems to Ortalius, and reap the rewards.
