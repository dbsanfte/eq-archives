Cursed Box
This quest begins with in the Great Divide and was submitted by Vogar.

Vogar: Hail a Gnomish Deserter

Gnomish deserter smiles at you and nods congenially. "Hello there. Nice to meet ya." He moves to shake your hand and then remembers that his hand is currently frozen to a very heavy, very solid hunk of metal shaped somewhat like a box.

Vogar: Is your hand stuck?

Gnomish deserter: "Erm. Well ya see….One day I was on guard duty and a huge white cat knocked me over. It looked like it was running away towards the bridge island. Anyways, I fell into the water. I guess I passed out because the next thing I knew, I was over near the gnolls laying on top of this huge box. It’s then that I made the decision."

Vogar: What decision?

Gnomish deserter: "To leave. I’m really tired of this pirate stuff. Captain Nalot said it was going to be great fun and an adventure but all I’ve seen is a lot of biscuits, slush water, and guard duty. So I’m here."

Vogar: You like it here?

Gnomish deserter: "Yup. This isn’t such a bad spot. It’s nice and peaceful. I can see for miles and every once in a while someone like you drops by to talk. There’s no guard duty and I don’t have to say, ‘Arrr’ any more. That really starts to hurt your throat you know. The only problem I have anymore is my hand frozen to this box. If you find a way to fix it, let me know."

Vogar: Let me see the box

Gnomish deserter: "Huh? Box? Where’s a box? Heh. I don’t see a box."

Vogar: What’s on your hand?

Gnomish deserter: "Oh…..well…..yeah. There is a box there if you want to talk in technical terms. If you want it you can have it."

Vogar: Can I have it?

Gnomish deserter points at the box indicating the large metal object. "I don’t want it anymore. It hasn’t caused me anything but trouble. If I could have gotten rid of it by now, I would have. Unfortunately it’s a wee bit stuck."

Where this quest leads is unknown and we could need get him to speak further.
