Strength

Strength is the primary ability of most meelee classes and should always be a meelee classes higher statistic. Why? Well strength effects a couple of things that are very important to most meelee classes.

First and foremost strength affects how much damage you will inflict upon a monster. This is important because as your weaponry skills raise you will begin to get a damage bonus to monsters. Strength affects this damage bonus. Thus a level 30 ogre warrior with 155 strength is going to far outdamage a level 30 halfling warrior with say 70 strength. This is very important to a meelee type class because the quicker you can finish off your opponent the less amount of time he has to pound on you or for his friends to make an appearance.

The general rule is that 1 strength = 1 ATK. This is very important to remember. Figure every level you gain your offense and weapons skills will increase your ATK by maybe 15 or 20 points. Well the difference between 100 and 150 strength is thus the difference between about 3 levels in ATK rating and that is a huge difference.

The area where strength reigns supreme however lies in how much weight you can carry. A character with a strength of 150 can carry a weight of 150 and a character with a strength of 60 can only carry 60. This is a luxury for every class as it means you will make less trips to town to sell because you are overloaded(and you don't want to be overloaded, you will run slower and your armor class will go way down). However it is most important for classes that can wear armor. If you want to wear platemail you better be sure to have a strength of at least 95 or higher.
