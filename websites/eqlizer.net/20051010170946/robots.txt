Inductees to date are: Nolan Bushnell, Shigeru Miyamoto, Halo, Link (The Legend of Zelda), Mario, and Sonic The Hedgehog. <br /><br />The list of nominees is compiled from a survey of industry members and technology, entertainment, and gaming media.
