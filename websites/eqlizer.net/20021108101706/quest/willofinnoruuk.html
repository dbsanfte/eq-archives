Will of Innoruuk

This sub-quest will result in the Will of Innoruuk. This is arguably the best or second best breatplate in the game for shadowknights. It is part of the Shadowknight Epic Quest but can also be worn rather than turned in.

This sub-quest begins at Marl Kastane in Kerra Ridge. He spawns in a hut near the main town area in Kerra. It is important to note here that there appears to be two spawns of Marl Kastane. You can tell which Marl is which by asking him, "what prophecy".

The first Marl will ask you to deliver a letter to his brother Gerot Kastane in Paineel. Delivering this item to Gerot will grant you a second item which must be returned to Marl. This appears to be important for two reasons.

1) It gives you faction to continue on the quest.
2) It spawns the second version of Marl Kastane.

The second Marl will respond to the keywords "what prophecy" by giving you a background story on Glohnor and Lhranc. Tell him that you will complete the ritual and he will inform you that he will need to create a dark shroud. To do this he will need the blood of an innocent. This can be attained by killing Kyrenna which is documented in the Heart of an Innocent sub-quest.

After handing the blood of Kyrenna to Marl you will receive a dark shroud and directions to hand this item to the Ghost of Glohnor in the Hole.

Handing the shroud to Glohnor's ghost will cause a Mummy of Glohnor to spawn. This mummy is extremely tough so make sure you bring multiple parties to help in the task.

Killing him will result in two items. The mummy wrappings of Glohnor and the head of Glohnor. The head is used for the Head of the Valiant sub-quest.

The wrappings are to be returned to Marl. You should ask "what prophecy" to make sure that it is the second version of Marl and not the first. It remains to be seen if the first version will simply eat your items or if he will reward you properly but I would not take any chances at this point. Handing in the mummy wrappings will result in the Will of Innoruuk.
