Tuesday, October 7, 2003

Major Changes Coming in Patch
 10:50 EST - Chantela

Hard adventures are being upgraded point-wise. I believe they score over 100 points right now on the Test server.

Some augmentations will be changed. Apparently Alan typo'd the names in his previous announcement (still in error on the Developer's Corner). The Broken Chunk of Coal, Cracked Ruby, and Charred Pebble are being removed and will be on your cursor when you log in if you have one. I think these are rare drops, perhaps from Hard dungeons. I wasn't able to find them in a search of the drop database, nor are they listed at Allakhazam's or Lucy. (shrug)

Apparently ALL PRE-LDON ITEMS that currently have a slot type of 1 will be changed to have a slot type of 7. In most cases this won't affect the player, at least in the immediate sense, but it must have some sort of long-term impact.

Caster's Realm has some information here:
Caster's Realm Article

UPDATE: It looks like the Blood Ruby of Understanding (a 40hp Aug) is one of the affected augmentations.
