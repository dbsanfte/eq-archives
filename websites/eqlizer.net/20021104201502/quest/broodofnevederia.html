Brood of Nevederia

Western Waste Dragons
This quest begins with Vkjor the gladiator arena of Kael Drakken.

Vkjor's eldest son was slain by a dragon named Nevederia. He would like to take revenge on this beast but she resides deep in the Temple of Veeshan. He would however take consolation in the death of her three offspring. These can probably be found in the Western Wastelands but he would not leave any clues as to what their names are.
