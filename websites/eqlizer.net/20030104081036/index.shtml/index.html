Tuesday, December 31, 2002

Happy New Year!
 10:30 EST - Meshell


Thank you everyone for the submissions, emails and general support you have given me over the last year. The players are what makes this game so much fun and though things have changed here at EQ'Lizer, most of you have remained so kind and understanding. I hope in the New Year I can help provide you with more information that assists you on your travels in Everquest. I am always open for suggestions!

There will be a new Planes of Power Ultra Spawn Guide up in the next few weeks, as well as new Guides on Game Commands and Controls. Be sure and look for quest or tradeskill information under the "description" area of items that have been added in the last few months. This should cut back on your research time and provide you with fast, helpful and simple details to follow. There will also be more Guides added to the Planes of Power Flag Guide and updates to the Tradeskill Area. Please feel free to email me with any feedback you might have!

I also want to wish a loving Happy New Year to the fine folks at Grey Hawke on Karana and that wonderful Shadowknight (my personal hero) Agymles, who resides on Ayonae Ro! Both have provided EQ'Lizer endless information, screenshots and general support over the past year. And of course, the mighty DenMother of EQTraders, who is forever generous and helpful! Not to forget, of course, you for visiting us!!
