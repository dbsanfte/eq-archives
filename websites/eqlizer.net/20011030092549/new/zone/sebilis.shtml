Sebilis

Click Here for a Map
Click Here for a Map of the Upper Level
Click Here for a Map of the Crypt
Click Here for a Map of the Lower Level

Sebilis is a froglok dungeon which can be found in Trakanon. This zone can only be entered with a key(or be ressed from inside). The keys are obtained via this quest.

Upon zoning in you must travel deeper into the zone to reach the zone out. Everything in the zone can see invisible so you must fight your way to any part of the dungeon.

The path from the zone in is fairly linear untill you reach the bridges. It then splits off to 5 directions. 3 deeper into the main floor in Sebilis, 1 to the zone out, and another which drops down from the bridges leading to the Spore King, Tolmpumj, and Trakanon. Consult the maps for details.

Faction in fairly simple in Sebilis.
