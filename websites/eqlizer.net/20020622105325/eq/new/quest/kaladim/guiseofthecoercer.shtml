Guise of the Coercer

Faction:
Related Zones:
Kithicor Forest
High Hold Pass
Lake Rathetear
Neriak
Steamfont

Anyone familiar with the Ragebringer quest will already know 90% of this quest.

You have to be level 50 to do this quest.

First go to Kaladim and look for Founy Jestards near the rogue guild. Pickpocket him until you get his note.
He wont always spawn with it, but he respawns every day (in game time) at 10 AM.

Then off to Neriak third gate and steal the note from Tani n'Mar also at the rogue guild. He wont have it always either but he respawns in the same way as Founy does.

Next step go to see Stanos Herkanor in High hold pass.
He can be found in the "smuggler area" P10 P325
If he is not up and you have high faction you can spawn him by saying. "I need to see Stanos" to his adjudant Anson.

Give Stanos both pieces of parchment and you recieve the "Combined Parchment".

Next, head for Lake Rathet and find Eldreth in the island tower at location P2600 N550. Give Eldreth the Combined Parchment, 100 platinum, and 2 bottles (unstacked) of milk and you get the "Scribbled Parchment.

Then go to plane of hate and sneak through the zone to the second floor of maestro's quarters. Pick up the "book of souls" that spawn on his bedstand at P60 P320

Next step, head for steamfont and look for a gnome in an SMR named Yendar Starphyre.

Give him the "scrippled parchment" you got from eldrict and you get a note back. Then hand in the note and "book of souls" and Renux spawns. She will attack at once and it's wise to have a solid group of 55's there to kill her.

Loot the "Translated parchment" from Renux. The diamond and ravenscale she drops is not used for the mask so feel free to sell / give it out.

Go back to Stanos in high hold pass and give him the "translated parchment". He will give you a box. Hold on to this for now.

Now here comes to tricky part. You will now have to kill Stanos and aquire his head.

Stanos has 32.000 HP, quad hits for 600's, spawns with 2 magic daggers and is almost completely resistant to any form of spells. When he is attacked he will call for the ENTIRE ZONE to come to his aid including 3 GM's that hit for as much as Stanos does. Unless you have a legion of players. I suggest you use this tactic

It is strongly adviced that you do not try and kill him with anything less than 30 very high quality players. (skill, equipment and level) Position your army near the pottery wheel in the smuggler area. Kill all the greenie and blue smugglers wandering around but be careful not to agro Anson.

The best way to get Stanos alone is to wait for him to despawn. Then have a monk train Anson to the kithicor zone and spawn Stanos from there by saying "I need to see Stanos. Have someone keep hailing Anson to keep him in place once you attack Stanos.

When Stanos is up, pull him out of his cove to the Pottery wheel. He will call for the entire zone to help him but all the mobs will go to his spawn point. Not his current position. It is vital that you get him alone because the 45 adds will take a legion of players to combat.

If you manage to kill Stanos and he drops his head (about 2/3 drop rate) loot it and head for Kithicor.
I should mention the faction hit for killing Stanos is HUGE and your survivors will likely be KOS in high hold for a long time.

In kithicor head for the Dark Elf camp at P800 N2400.
Have 2 people harmony the entire camp thoroughly. Then get a monk/bard to pull a single NPC out. It doesn't matter which but you need a named Dark elf. Sneak behind the NPC without agroing him and turn in the Box you got from Stanos.

This will spawn General V'Ghera in the camp.

Now, reharmony the camp and be quick about it. V'ghera will despawn in 10 minutes if not agroed. Again, pull out the general and try to get him single.

Carefully sneak behind General V'ghera (he is KOS to everyone) and turn in the head of stanos quickly.

The reward is the Guise of the Coercer.

Note, the faction hit with high hold for turning in the head is even worse than actualy killing Stanos.

Reward: Guise of the Coercer.
