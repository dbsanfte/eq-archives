Plane of Air Guide
This information in this guide was submitted by Llamanator D'Alpaca

Top Island - 2 Great Sphinx's (Level 53+) Non-Agro, Empty Windmill.

Sister of the Spire Island - Inside the temple here is Sister of the Spire (Level 53+) Agro, Temple is guarded by 7 Greater Sphinx's (Level 53+) Non-Agro, these seem to be Enchanters. Flying around this Island are many Fatestealer Drakes (Level 51-52) Non-Agro and many Heartsbane Drakes (Level 50) Non-Agro. There are 2 Portals here, 1 to the back left of the Temple and 1 in front of the Temple with a fire burning in the middle of it.

Bzzazzt Island - This Island is covered with Bzzazzt's (Level 50) Agro. There is a huge metal gear embedded in the top of this Island and a portal lies behind it. There is also what seems to be a Giant Bzzazzt embedded in the Island itself, only its backend protrudes from the Island.

Spiroc Island - This Island is covered in a Bird-Type race similiar to the Aviaks of South Karana.

The Spiroc Lord (Level 53+) Agro, seems to be a Shaman as he has a mini wolf pet. He guards the portal ofound on this Island.

Spiroc Vanquisher (Level 53+) Agro.

Spiroc Expulser, Spiroc Revolter, Spiroc Arbiter, Spiroc Caller and Spiroc Walker (Level 51-52) Agro.

Efreeti Island - Noble Djorn (Level 53+) Non-Agro. There is a Windmill on this Island. You can get to this Island from one of the Portals on Key-Master Island.

Empty Island - There is nothing on this Island as of this point.

Middle Island - Huge Temple on this Island with 4 Pillars around it, each of the Pillar points and the point on top of the temple have a Shimmering Meteor on them (Level 53+) Non-Agro. Inside this temple resides 3 creatures: Heart Harpie (Level 53+) Non-Agro, Gorgalosk (Level 53+) Non-Agro and An Avenging Gazer (Level 53+) Non-Agro. On this Island are stationary Gusts of Wind (Level 53+) Non-Agro. Surrounding this Island in the air are Watchful Guards (Level 53+) Non-Agro, Gorgalosk's (level 53+) Non-Agro and Spirited Harpies (Level 53+) Non-Agro.

Azarack Island - This Island is reachable via the other Portal on Key-Master Island. This Island is covered with Azarack's (Level 53+) Agro. There is a Portal here.

Key-Master Island - This is where you begin your journey into the Plane of Air. Here you will find several Thunder Spirit's (Level 50) Non-Agro, when killed they drop the keys you need to get to Azarack Island and Efreeti Island. Also here you can find the Key-Master himself (Level 53+) Non-Agro, he sells these 2 keys as well as a coffin needed for the Summon Corpse spell.
