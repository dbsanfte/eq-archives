06/01/2004

Bestiary.php Finished
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

I'm happy to announce that my main man Matt has finished work on the new Bestiary layout. You can see it in action by clicking here. It is working in all the bestiary layouts and I have converted the last couple of updates to use it. Updates on the bottom of the front page do not link properly to it. They all can use the new view, it's just the news that is borked up for them, and there's too many links to change them all manually.

The one thing you will notice on the new file is that comments are now enabled for bestiary. We hope that you enjoy them. The one area on those pages that isn't working properly at this moment is the Related Quests area. It is currently linking to quest.php which is the new layout for quests, which is not ready at this time. Matt will get that working ASAP. If you have any comments or suggestions on the new bestiary layout please feel free to let us know.

There's will be no long list of new items, mobs or quests today. The massive data entry phase is behind us now, as we've got the site completely up to date. What I am turning my attention to now is fixing any problems that occured with the merging of Spoilers and EQ'Lizer. I am doing that by going through every zone in the game one by one and searching for any missing items and changing any mob or quest names that are incorrect to the new format. As a result I'm doing a lot of batch corrections and it's making it impossible for the generators to give an accurate update as items are changing all over the place. You can still see the latest items being updated on the right hand side bar, but be warned it's not going to be very accurate until I go through all of the zones. I'm putting all of my time and energy in to that right now, so it should not take too long.
