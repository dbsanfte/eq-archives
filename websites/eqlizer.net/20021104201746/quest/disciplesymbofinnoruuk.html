Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk
This quest was submitted by Dethfist Hummiechopper

You must first complete the skinned halfling face mask quest. Go to West Commons near the Kithicor Forest Zone. You will find a Halfling named Wallin Slyfoot. Hail him and then say "I will assist you". He will then give you a note that is supposed to go to Master Whoopal (but of course he is dead). Instead turn the letter into Draxiz N'Ryt in Neriak.

He will then ask you to hunt down this Halfling spy and turn the spy's head and your Initiates Symbol of Innoruuk(for information on how to obtain one click here) over to Ithvol K'Jasn in the clerics guild. You can find the spy in the hidden tunnels of Neriak Commons. Kill the Halfling spy and loot his head. Turn in the head and your Initiate Symbol over to Ithvol K'Jasn for the Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk (Neck Slot, AC 5, +2 wis, +1 int, +7 mana, CLR, SHM, HUM, DEF, TRL, Effect of Siphon Strength, Unlimited Charges).
