Erudin - Find Tomekeeper Danl in the Libaray. Ask him "What rare finds?" and "What is Immortals?"

Skyfire - Kill random mobs until you find a book of the immortals.

Erudin - Go back to Tomekeeper Danl and give him the book and he gives you a letter.

Timorous Deep - Find Lheao a location negative 11500 negative 2000. You will have to swim under the water to get to him. Speak with him and then when prompted, hand him the letter and your Robe of Whistling Fists (side quest). He will give you The Book of Celestial Fists.

Trakanon's Teeth - Find Kaiaren. He is somewhere in the ruins of Sebilis. Give him the book of Celestial Fists and he will attack you. There is no need to kill him, just Feign Death then leave. Go find Kaiaren's True Form at location Positive 300 Positive 2400, he is in a building next to a lake. You say, "Lheao sent me". He tells you to destroy the Fist of Fire, otherwise known as Eejag.

Lavastorm - Fire Sprite Eejag, Target the fire sprite located in the big lavapit by temple of solusek ro, Solusek A and Solusek B. Just click in the middle of the pit and you will target him if he is there. Say, "I challenge you, Eejag" and he will attack you. I've also heard that you can say "I challenge you, Eejag" to make him spawn if he's not in the pit. Kill him and loot the charred scale.

Plane of Air - find a "Presence" on Dojorn's Island and give him the charred scale. The will spawn Gwan, Kill him and loot the Breath of Gwan.

Mines of Nurga - Find a sleeping ogre and give him the Breath of Gwan. This iwll spawn Trunt. Kill him and loot the rockfilled head.

Lake Rathetear - Go to eldrath's island and swim into the cave under the island, find the dark elf female named Deep, in the top of the goblin tower in the center of the underwater cavern. Give her the rockfilled head, she will give it back and despawn, telling you to take it to her master.

Overthere - Find the Astral Projection at location Positive 750 Positive 750 and give it the rockfilled head. He will give you the Eye of Kaiaren for your services.

Lake of Ill Omen - Find the Astral Projection again, this time in the lake of ill omen at location Negative 650, Negative 650 and give him the Eye of Kaiaren. Master Vorash and Deep will spawn. You must kill them both! They will despawn rather quickly so you will need a few groups, one to kill deep and one to kill Master Vorash, when both are dead, Xenovorash will spawn. Kill him and loot his Demon Fangs.

Trakanon's Teeth - Go back to Kaiaren's True form and give him the Demon Fangs and the Book of Celestial Fists. He will give you the Celestial Fists.

 

Reward: Celestial Fists
