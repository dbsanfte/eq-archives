07/07/2004

Shining Star of Light
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

It's amazing the odd holes that I have been finding as I have gone through our database. With two months of data loss I expected to find it in the zones that were recently revamped or in the new quests that were added during that time. But what you forget about are quests such as the Shining Star of Light, which was removed from the game and we removed it from our database. The quest was turned back on at some point and the spoiler was not here. The good thing about doing all of these zones individually is every piece of information we are missing will get placed in during the process. Anyway, Shining Star of Light returns and we have some other miscellaneous stuff that was missing in this update.

New Equipment: Prayer Beads ~ Brown Leather Gloves ~ Iron Shackles ~ Lliana's Flute.

New Quests: Shining Star of Light.
