Thurgadin Wizard Armor

Faction:
+Coldain
-Kromzek
-Kromrif
Related Zones:
Kael Drakkal

This quest begins with Mauren Frostbeard in Thurgadin.

you say 'Hail Mauren Frostbeard'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'Greetins to you. I seek one that calls himself a wizard. What do you call yourself?'

you say 'I am a wizard'

Mauren Frostbeard says ' Excellent. Are you sure enough in your skills as to undertake my tasks? If not then get out of my sight!'

you say 'I will do your tasks'

Mauren Fristbeard says 'I thought so. One should never back down form a challenge. Once you have completed them I will have a cap, a robe, sleeves, wristbands, gloves, leggings, and boots.'

you say 'What cap?'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'For an enchated hed guard bring me a torn enchanted silk turbin as well as three pieces of crushed flame opal.

you say 'What robe?'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'If you wish to have a fine robe, bring unto me a torn enchanted silk robe and three pristine emeralds. Then you will recieve a  robe that of your wildest dreams.

you say 'What sleeves?'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'Bring unto me a set of torn enchanted silk sleeves as well as threee flawed topaz. With these in my ppossesion I can create a set of enchated sleeves the likes of which spell casters like you dream of.

you say 'What gloves?'

Mauren Frostbeard says ' If you seek gloves of great power, bring to me three crushed topaz and a torn enchanted silk gloves. I will use the power of the gems to imbue the gloves.

you say 'What wrist guards?'

Mauren Frostbeard says ' An enchanted silk wrist guard and three crushed onyx sapphires is all that I require to create an imbued wrist wrap of great power. Seek out these items and return to me when you have acquired them.

you say 'What leggings?'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'Three nephrite and a pair of torn enchanted silk leggings will net you a fine set of pantaloons.

you say 'What boots?'

Mauren Frostbeard says 'Three crushed jaundice gems combined with torn enchated silk boots would make a most interesting set of footwear. The latent powers of the gems can be harnessed to create magical effects.

Reward(s): Listed Above.
