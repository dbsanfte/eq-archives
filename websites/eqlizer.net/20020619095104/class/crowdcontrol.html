Grouping With Crowd Controlling Enchanter Guide to Easy Dungeon Fighting.
by Untos Gnomish Enchanter.

This guide is meant to give better understanding of Enchanters' role in a  group.   Most people categorized different class as the following:  Puller,  Tank, Nuker, Healers.  Enchanter (and Bard's in some extent) belong in  another category.  That is Crowd Control.

An example:

When you in Upper Guks, the puller pulls 3 frogs on your team, each of your  member will try to engage each frogs and kill them all as fast as possible.   You and your team are taking damage from 3 different frogs at once.  The  healer is having a hard time retargetting his/her teammate to heal them and  run out of mana real fast.  It is more effecient that everyone in the group  to concentrate on killing 1 frogs at a time, thus reducing the damages you  are taking from 3 frogs to 2 frogs..and then 1 frogs..and then non.  Now,  Imagine the same pull, where the 3 frogs come and everyone engage the first  frog.  Wouldn't it be great if the other 2 frogs would get inline and wait  for his turn and doesn't do anything else but just watch the fight (kinda  like some of those Chinese Kung Fu movie).   That's what Crowd Control is, to
Reduce/turn the tide of a fight from Your group VS 3+ mobs to Your group VS  1 after another.  (or even better.. Your group+1 frog vs 1 frog and other frog is drooling on itself).

That is what a good Enchanter could accomplish (and bards).

We all loves the fact that Enchanter in a group will BUFFs/Debuffs  people(Clarity, Speeds attacks, slow the monster and lower their  Str/Agi/AC..), but the Ability to Crowd Control was given to Enchanter since Level 4.(mesmerize)


1.  Mesmerize (MEZ):

This spell does:  approximately 15sec stuns to a monster (if not resist). 
The stuns will break when the NPC/MOBs is DMG in anyway (spells, DOT, Bash).

This is our "bread and butter" crowd control line. (Mesmerize(15sec)/Entrall(30sec)/Entrance(50-60sec)/Dazzle(not sure about this one))   These spells will put Monster on ICE while you take care of his friend. There's an Additional benefit to this spell.  You can DEBUFFs monster that  is Mermerized. (nonDMG type debuffs). You can Root, REduce Stats, Blind it...etc.

In Addition to those spells we also have an Area Effect Mez spell
(Mesmerisation).  This is what most enchanter call TRAINStopper.  Because it   will stop a trains of 10 mobs in a track.  This spell works like normal AE   spell where an Enchanter will Target someone/NPC and cast it.  This spell can be cast from far away and effect an area near the target.  This spell  does 15sec stuns also.  It has one Disadvantage.  If the Caster is within  the AE area, he MAY get Mez also.  Keep your eyes out for "[Insert your enchanter's name] is mesmerized"

In these situation, it IS important that everyone in the GROUP attack the  same MOB/NPC.  You must use /assist and Reassist to make you sure attacking  the same monster.  There are chances where Monster will resist Mez and will  bee-line toward Enchanter, be ready to (root/Stuns/Taunt that mobs).  Make sure you go back to the original Monster once you got the Resist mobs attention, Enchanter will ICE him soon.   Many Enchanters has die from people who like to SOLO in a group, because when a mob break off the ice by someone, it doesn't hit the one that hits it.  It goes after the enchanter who iced/mezed him.

2.  Charm/Beguile/...the whole series.

You all have seen this "Powerful" spells in action.  It essentially takes
one of the mobs and turn it into Enchanters pet.  (Some class has a form of "charm" like Befriend Animal and Beguile undead).  This will help turn a tide in a fight because you now have 1 more member to your group and they(mobs) has 1 less to theirs.

The Chaos of Charms is very unpredictable/unreliable at time.  First of all, charms can last from 1sec to 1min+.  You can never tell (think INVIS running out at the wrong place).  Second, when a charmed mobs breaks, he is megataunt at the charmed (the enchanter usually) and will takes some taunt/nuking to get the mobs off him.   Third, Charmed COULD wander and cause trains like any of "PET" in dugeon.   Forth, Charmed mobs will not attack anything that's RED to it.

Normal practice for enchanters that use charm is to charm one of the
pull..(and let the group know so they don't waste mana/time on it)  And  finish all the mobs and then...Break charms(invis, Cancel magic) to kill him last for this particular pull.

3.  ColorFlux/Color Shifts ...AE stuns.

This line of spells are not very much crowd control but it can helps.  This spells will do a AE stuns to mobs next to the Caster(enchanter).  The Radius  from Enchanter is very small so not all mobs in the fight will be effected.  Color Flux last about 2-3sec, Color Shifts last about 6sec.  Normally enchanter use this spells so that he/she can reapply AE mez and not getting interrupted.  This is all the "Feet move Fast" tactic.  He can AE stuns and run for the zone, which give you a head start (unless there's resists...)

Notice that Enchanter doesn't really do Damage in a group.  He/she may run up and poke them w/ this toys sticks or pokers but that's it.  Most Enchanter will not DD, but will DOT.  Our DD is megataunt (because of dual effect) and it's not Mana effcient.  We will use it in emergency or finish runners.  The recycle time on it is horrible.  The DOT usually lower STR, AGI and maybe AC, so we use that.   Usually we don't have enough spell slot to mem them anyway. (Charm, Cancel, Tashani[Lower MR], Slow, AE mez, Mez, AE stuns, Speed attacks).  That pretty much covers all the spell slots.

With this Guide, I hope all you adventurer of Norrath will now understand Enchanter's role in a group.  (maybe we get into more groups now ;P.
