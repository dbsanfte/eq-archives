Call of Flames
This quest was user-submitted by Glakov Faythro.

The quest starts in Kelethin from Devin and Lily Ashwood, located near the bank.  You must be level 50 in order for the NPC to speak to you. First, you say to Devin, "I want to learn from him, and prove myself as a forester."  Once you say this, Lily will say something about Ganelorn Oast and ask you to bring a love letter to him, target her and say, "Yes I will take the letter." 

Now take the letter to Ganelorn Oast in East Karana, he roams around a bit, but his location is around -3500, -1750.  Give Ganelorn the letter and he will ask you to deliver some snake eggs to Kithicor, the ranger in Kithicor Forest, located around -500, 2000 (I think).  Once Kithicor has the eggs he will give you an icon which you must bring back to Ganelorn. 

When you give Ganelorn the icon he will give you a note of credit which you have to bring to Aanina Rockfinder in Kaladim B at the Everhot Forge, he will also ask you to get 3 items for his pupil Devin Ashwood: Electrum Bladed Wakizashi, Adamantine Band, a Burnt Sash, and a Gift from the merchant in Kaladim. 

After you give the note to Aanina Rockfinder, she will give you the gift.  Now take the 4 items (Electrum Bladed Wakizashi, Adamantine Band, Burnt Sash, and Gift) to Ganelorn and give them to him.  He will ask one final favor, and that is to bring him the head of the notorious poacher, Vance Bearstalker (a level 53 npc), who will spawn not far away as soon as you hand the 4 items to Ganelorn. 

Once you give Vance's head to Ganelorn, he will give you the Call of Flame spell. This is a level 49 ranger only spell.
