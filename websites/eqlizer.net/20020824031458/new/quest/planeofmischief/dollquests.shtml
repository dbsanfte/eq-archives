Doll Quests

Faction:
Related Zones:

This quest takes place completely in the Plane of Mischief.

You must first get a library card dropped by NPCs in Plane of Mischief.

You then turn it into The Librarian in the Library, who gives you a "Book of Mischief" 4 slot container. 

You then must get the four corners of the missing page found on NPCs thoughout the PoM. Once combined they form "Words of Wealth". 

You turn in your "Words of Wealth" to "Bob The Painter" who will give you a "Pot of Gold", 2 slot container. 

You then must create more words of wealth and items form the Cards Quest By combining "Words of Wealth" with any 2 Throne Card Quest item (including Mask of Melodies - only 1 card quest that combines) you receive Funny Money. 

When you turn in 2 Funny Money to Peaches at the entrance to the PoM castle you will receive a Gift Box (each Gift Box is colored, 1 for each class) which has 5 Charges of gift. 

When right-clicked you produce a Doll (each doll is named for each class). When this Doll is combined with the 5 main Armor of Distraction types (Gloves, Breastplate, Legs, Arms, Feet) you get the Class Specific Doll Quest Armor specific.

Reward(s): 
Cleric: Crazy Cleric Boots, Crazy Cleric Breastplate, Crazy Cleric Gauntlets, Crazy Cleric Helm, Crazy Cleric Greaves.
Druid: Twisted Nature Armband, Twisted Nature Boots, Twisted Nature Bracers, Twisted Nature Crown, Twisted Nature Gloves, Twisted Nature Leggings, Twisted Nature's Chestguard.
Enchanter: Mischievous Dazzler Crown, Mischievous Dazzler Gloves, Mischievous Dazzler Robe, Mischievous Dazzler Shoes, Mischievous Dazzler Leggings.
Magician: Sly Summoners Pants, Sly Summoners Robe, Sly Summoners Sleeves, Sly Summoners Gloves.
Monk: Monkey Hide Arm Plate, Monkey Hide Boots, Monkey Hide Breastplate, Monkey Hide Greaves, Monkey Hide Helm.
Necromancer: Maleficent Robe.
Shaman: Shamanistic Shenannigan Chestplate, Shamanistic Shenannigan Gauntlets, Shamanistic Shenannigan Helm.
Wizard: Wily Warlock Gloves, Wily Warlock Robe.
