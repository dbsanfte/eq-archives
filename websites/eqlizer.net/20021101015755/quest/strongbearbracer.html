Strongbear Bracer
This quest was submitted by Dean:

This quest starts off in South Qeynos, at the Hall of Steel. Go up to Brin   and Hail him, he will ask if you are a young steel warrior or a spectator to   the arena.  Tell him that you are a young steel warrior.  He will give you a  bag to fill with five bat wings and five snake scales.  Once that you have all the items you need, make sure nothing is stacked and hit combine, then   bring the bag back to Brin. 

Once you give the bag back, Brin will give you  a note to take to Axe Broadsmith in Qeynos Hills.  He can be located in a tiny hut near the fishing hole.  When you give the  note, Axe Broadsmith will tell you to turn around and kill a skeleton which   spawns as soon as you hand in the note Brin gave you.  After you have killed   the skeleton, loot his head and bring it back to Axe Broadsmith. 

Axe Broadsmith will give you another not to bring back to Brin.  Once you give the note, Brin will give you another note to take to another person in the bottom of the arena (I can't remember his name).  After you give the note, the NPC will give you a key that unlocks pen number 5.  Find pen number 5  and walk in.  At the end of the hall you will find a very weak Young Lion. 

Kill the Young Lion and bring back the pelt to the NPC whom gave you the key.  He then will give you a Strongbear bracer for your efforts.
