Rogue Disciplines
Introduction to Disciplines

Disciplines are not gained every level. They are spread out over different levels(almost every level). They can not be used over and over again. Each discipline has a base reuse time. These vary depending on the discipline. After using a discipline you will not be able to use any other discipline until the reuse time has expired. This means if the reuse time is 45 minutes you better be careful of when you pick to use this discipline. The reuse time decreases depending on your level. It will decrease one minute per level. The monks Stone Stance discipline for example has a 12 minute reuse time at level 51 but decreases to a mere 3 minutes at level 60.

Level 51

Rogues receive no disciplines at level 51.

Level 52

Rogues receive no disciplines at level 52.

Level 53

Counterattack

Level 53 grants rogues their first discipline of counterattack. This discipline will allow you to riposte all attacks against you for nine seconds. This can be very useful in a pinch.

The downside to this discipline is a 60 minute base reuse delay.

Level 54

Deadeye

During the 14 second duration of this discipline a rogue will land all attacks attempted. This is a great discipline if a fight is getting close and you need to dish some damage in a hurry.

It has a 30 minute reuse delay.

Level 55

Nimble

The nimble skill will allow you to dodge all melee attacks against you for 12 seconds. This is a great defensive skill and will allow you to tank high level mobs effectively for a short period of time.

The reuse delay on this skill is 30 minutes.

Level 56

Rogues receive no new disciplines at level 56.

Level 57

Kinesthetics

The discipline of kinesthetics will grant you instant double attack and dual wield success for 18 seconds. This means all four of your attacks will land every time you swing. In addition this can also allow you to double backstab.

The base reuse delay is 30 minutes.

Level 58

Blinding Speed

This discipline will haste you for 15 seconds. This means that you will attack much faster than normal.

The base reuse is 30 minutes.

Level 59

Duelist

This discipline adds 4x the weapons base damage to the amount of damage you do with each attack for 12 seconds. This means if you are using a bloodpoint for example(dmg 10 delay 21) that you will receive 40 points of damage bonus for each attack.

It has a 30 minute reuse delay.

Level 60

Rogues receive no new disciplines at level 60.
