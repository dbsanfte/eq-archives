Dagnor's Cauldron

Location
Dagnor's Cauldron is located on the island of Faydwer to the east of mainland Antonica


Zone Type
Dagnor's Cauldron is a large lake, and acts mostly as a means of acessing
the dungeons of Unrest and Kedge Keep.


Zone Borders
Dagnor's Cauldron borders with Butcherblock Mountains,
Unrest and Kedge Keep.


Nearest Cities
That of the Dwarves; Kaladim, Of the Wood Elves; Kelethin and the home of the High Elves; Felwithe.






Inhabitents
This zone is over run with Aqua Goblins, Large Rats, and Serpents. There is also a small Orc camp where Scouts and exiled Legionaires can be found.






Route
Dagnor's Cauldron is reached from Antonica by catching the boat from East Freeport Docks, which takes you via the Ocean of Tears, stopping at Sister Island, and then zones into Butcherblock Mountains. From Butcherblock follow the path from the docks, eventually you will reach a T junction, referred to as the 'X' roads. Turn right, and follow the path, it will lead you to Dagnor's Cauldron.

Bestiary
This zone is rarely camped and is more passed through, however
there are many roaming MOBs to hunt, from Aqua Goblins to
Cauldron Rats. There are also 2 camps situated on the lakes shores, one which spawns Aqua Goblins and one which spawns Orcs. These spawns do not roam. There are also a few high level rare NPCs who have items of great power.
