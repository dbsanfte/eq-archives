Plane of Power Flag Guide Checklist



Click HERE for Flag Quest information

ClickHERE for a PoP Progression Chart

    • Plane of Valor

    • Find Mavuin in the basement. Hail him and say 'what information?' Do any one of the trials listed here and loot a mark. Hail the Tribunal who administered the trial and say, 'I have come to plead the case of Mavuin'. Go back to Mavuin in the basement jail and hail him - this results in a flag for Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms.  

    • Completion of all the trials allows access to the 7th Hammer - this is not needed to progress to elemental planes.

    • Plane of Nightmare

    • First you must hail Elder Poxbourne. He can be found in "Sick Bay" (the area of PoTranq near beach with sick elders in it) Then hail Adroha Jezith and say 'tortured by nightmares' - this gives you a preflag. Now find the hedge inside of Plane of Nightmares - it is across the river along the left zone wall. Hail Thelin Poxbourne and get ported up into the maze for this battle.  At the end of this event you receive a flag for Plane of Nightmare B (Lair of Terris Thule). Go back to Sick bay and hail Elder Poxbourne and Adroha again. Zone into Plane of Nightmare B through the gate near the hedge and kill Terris Thule, this flags you for Plane of Torment. You can also gain access to the Plane of Torment through this quest.

    • Plane of Disease

    • Go to Alder Fuirstel in Plane of Tranquility - standing besides the huge pipe that zones you into Plane of Disease. Say 'what ward' to receive your pre-flag. Now go to Plane of Disease and kill Grummus, hail the Planar Projection, and zone into Crypt of Decay through the pipe near Grummus. Return to sick bay and hail Elder Fuirstel at sick bay.
       You can also gain access to the Crypt of Decay through       this quest .

    • Plane of Innovation

    • Obtain a key for entrance to the Factory (from zone go left and not far down on your right is the door to the Factory). Enter the zone, go right and eventually you find a small tinker hut with a gnome inside. and hail the gnome at the start. Say "I will test the machine" - this will get you a preflag. Then kill the Manaetic Behemoth deeper inside the Factory, this spawns the same gnome who WANDERS eventually to the front of the Factory and can be killed by any spawns up,  so be ready to hail the gnome again. You are now flagged for Plane of Tactics. Alternatively you can do this quest  to gain entrance to Plane of Tactics.

    • Plane of Valor

    • Kill the crystalline dragon named Aerin`Dar which resides in the back of the wastelands to gain entrance to Halls of Honor. There is a key required to get to this lair, which you can gain through this quest.
      Alternatively you can do       this questto gain entrance to Halls of Honor.

    • Plane of Storms
      Obtain entrance to Bastion of Thunder through Askr the Lost. Quest information is located  here.

    • Crypt of Decay
      Get through the Carprin/High Priest Ultor Szanvon event listed here and hail the Tiny Pusling to gain access to Bertoxxulous' Lair. Kill Bertoxxolous and hail the planar projection. Then zone out, go hail Alder and Elder in the "sick bay" of Plane of Tranquility to complete flag.

    • Plane of Tactics
      Kill Tallon and Vallon Zek and hail the Planar Projection. Then kill Rallos Zek and get that flag.

    •  Halls of Honor/Halls of Honor B (Temple of Mithaniel Marr)
      Complete the three trials, via this quest to obtain entrance go Halls of Honor B (Temple of Mithaniel Marr). In Halls of Honor B, kill Mithaniel Marr and hail the planar projection.

    • Plane of Torment
      Go to "sick bay" in Plane of Tranquility. Hail Fahlia Shadyglade. Say 'what condition', 'what plane of torment' and 'I will go' and you will receive a flag. Go to Plane of Torment and gain entrance to Saryn's Tower. Kill Mareeq to spawn Tylis. Go through his dream event to kill the Keeper of Sorrows - get a flag - Tylis ports you back down to the room before Saryn. Kill Saryn, hail Planar Projection. Go back to sick bay in Tranquility and hail both Tylis and Fahlia.

    •  Bastion of Thunder
      Obtain key to Agnarr's Lair. Kill Agnarr, go up the stairs to the door next to Agnarr's room and Hail Karana. Say "I will follow the path of the Fallen." for a flag.

    At this point you should have killed Bertoxxulous, Agnarr, Mithaniel Marr, Saryn, Keeper of Souls, Tallon, and Vallon Zek. Now find the Seer in Plane of Knowledge (she's in the building right at zone in from Tranquility). Sit down and say "unlock my memories"  This is for fixing flags if you do them out of order. Now go to Maelin. He is a gnome in the Plane of Knowledge library. To get to him go to the bottom floor of the library and find the elevator (it has a button in the wall) Ride the elevator up to the top and he's behind the table in front of a bookshelf. Hail him and say 'I have new information' and 'I have new lore'. You may need to do this a few times. Eventually he will tell you that you need to go find Rallos Zek.

    If you don't go back through the guide and see what you're missing. Otherwise, you are ready for Solusek Ro's Tower

    • Solusek Ro's Tower
      Kill the five mini-bosses in this zone - Riziona, Arlyxir, Jiva, Xuzl, and Dresolisk. These 5 flags along with the Rallos Zek flag allow you to enter Solusek Ro's room. Kill him to gain access to the Doomfire, The Burning Lands, aka Plane of Fire.

    In addition to Solusek Ro's chamber, killing Rallos Zek also unlocks the way to the other three elemental planes. Once you have killed Rallos along with the other listed flag mobs (Agnarr, etc) Go to Maelin and say, "I have new information" and you should get a mass of text ending in a flag.

    • Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind
      Eryslai, otherwise known as Plane of Air is the home of Xegony. Kill the avatars to gain access to Xegony. Kill Xegony for part of the key needed for Plane of Time.

    • Vegarlson, the Earthen Badland
      Vegarlson is the Plane of Earth, and home or the Rathe. Complete the key for PoEarth B and kill the Rathe for part of the key needed to enter Plane of Time.

    • Reef of Coirnav
      This is the elemental plane of water and home of Coirnav. Kill Coirnav to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time.

    • Doomfire is the Plane of Fire
      Kill Fennin Ro to gain a part of the key needed for entrance to the Plane of Time.
