Dragon Necropolis

The Dragon Necropolis is a dungeon in Western Wastes. The zone has several populations. 

The first population is the phase spiders. They can be found near the entrance and on the top level of the dungeon. The largest of these is the rare Queen Raltaas. 

The second major population is the paebala. They can be found underneath a metal door(looks like a steel plate) near the entrance of the dungeon. Their area also has a population of goo's.

The final major population is the chetari. Chetari are minions of Zlandicar, an outcast dragon. Zlandicar has become an outcast because of his tendency to eat other dragons. 

There are also lava keepers and entropy serpents in rare numbers.
