Everquest Equipment Guide: Tentacle Whip

These whips can be found on tentacle terrors. These whips have a damage of 5 and a delay of 28. You are probably wondering why anyone strong enough to kill a tentacle terror would want such a weapon? Two reasons.

First off these weapons are magical and can be used to hit magical monsters. This is important because players may not have a magical weapon yet, or they might not have two for duel wielding purposes.

Secondly a successful land will occasionally cause a lifetap effect in which the monsters hit points will be drained and granted to the wielder.

WAR,ROG,RNG,PAL,SHD,BRD,1HS.
