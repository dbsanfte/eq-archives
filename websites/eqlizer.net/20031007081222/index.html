New Expansion News, and Patch Coming
 13:28 EST - Chantela

Sources tell me that there will be a patch on October 8th which will increase the drop rate of Collection Mission-related items in LDON dungeons, and possibly clarify the difficulty levels of LDON raids, two much-desired changes. The item change is being done to address the fact that you sometimes have to kill as much as 80% of a dungeon's mobs in order to complete the mission. There may be other features in this patch, which has not yet been announced on the EQ Live site.

I'm also told that a new expansion for the main EverQuest PC game is already fully under development. Few details were available, but some fan faire attendents report that it will be focused on high end content.

Other Fan Faire info:

Assassination Missions: People speculate wildly on what it takes to get the "boss" to spawn. According to developers at the Faire, you must kill 35% of the dungeon's creatures for this to happen. That is the only variable.

Similarly, you must kill 35% of a Hostage Mission's mobs in order for the hostage to spawn.

Site Changes, Updates, and a New Face...Welcome Chantela!
 13:59 EST - Meshell

A few years ago I was approached and asked to work on this site, and I was happy to jump at the chance. Over the years we have gone through some major changes, we started out a banner site (with advertisements all over), moved to one that is subscription only, we have gone from a overall general information site, to one that has focused on items, maps, and game guides. But things in the next few weeks will be changing.

I have been asked to move onto a new project and a brand new MMO site called EGCore (Entertainment Gaming Core) and have found someone to take over EQLizer. Chantela is someone I have known for years and is smart, thorough, extreemly experienced in Everquest, and has hosted forums and databases about the game. I have no hesitation handing the site over to her, and I feel she will only improve the site overall. Please make her feel welcome, don't hesitate to click on her name when you see it appear on updates and news posts, and give any feedback about changes you'd like to see on the site.

Also, the site itself will be getting a new face. Over the next several weeks, Chantela will be beefing up the site in preparations for it to change. We will be joining a larger family, under Flashlink (who we currently use), and will have access to more information, support, news, contests, and gaming guides. Over the month of October, Chantela will be bringing you news and updates about the site changes, we hope that they will keep our current viewers more than happy and bring us a new audience.

I do want to thank everyone over the last few years who has been supportive and truely wondeful to me . There is no better job than working on a site about a game you love!

With Regards,

Meshell aka Hermana
