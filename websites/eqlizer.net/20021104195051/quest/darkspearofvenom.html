Dark Spear of Venom
This quest begins with Clrakk in Iceshard Manor of Kael Drakken. It was submitted by Lantar of Povar.

You say, 'hail Clrakk Blackfist'

Clrakk Blackfist looks down at you.  'What is it you want, Human?  Why do you speak to the great Knight Clrakk Blackfist?'

You say, 'Knight?'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'Yes, I am a knight.  One who walks a dark path of blood and hatred.  In your realms, one such as myself is called a shadowknight.

You say, 'I am a shadowknight'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'Don't make me laugh.  What could a mere Human know about the dark arts?  Perhaps I should teach you about true darkness and hatred.

You say, 'Teach me about true darkness'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'The darkness I know is the darkness of my own heart.  I feed upon my own anger and the anger of others.  I use this as a weapon. When I wish pain upon someone, every word I speak is venom.  It will seep through their minds and eventually destroy them from within.  Killing from within can bring great pleasure to one such as myself.  Outright killing, of course, can be just as much fun.

You say, 'What about killing'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'I enjoy killing the Coldain the most, I believe. They fight on even when outnumbered, however, they make the most wonderful sounds as they die.  A jab of my spear to their necks brings such satisfaction!  Have you ever hunted the Coldain, so-called shadowknight?

You say, 'I have hunted the coldain'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'I have hunted many Coldain in my day.  I long for a true challenge, though.  These Coldain are weak.  Their nobles hide within their city where I cannot bring them strife because of my great size.  I have just had an interesting idea, my little 'friend'.  Would you be interested in bringing strife to the Coldain, strife as great as that which they suffered at the hands of Slagd Frozentoe?

You say, 'I will bring them strife'

Clrakk Blackfist says 'The Coldain have grown complacent.  The defenses of their city are built to keep my kind and dragonkind at bay.  However, their defenses against the smaller races such as yourself are not nearly as sophisticated.  I wish to kill many Coldain, but now is not the time for that.  There is a particular Coldain whose death will strike fear into the hearts of many.  His name is Deaen Greyforge, a Coldain knight.  He is both feared and respected by his kind.  If you can destroy him, it will send a message to the Coldain that they are not safe from the wrath of the giants. Kill him and chop his body into bits.  Bring me a few pieces as proof that the fool is dead.


This dwarf can be found in Thurgadin near the Paladin Guild. When killed he drops the pieces Clrakk seeks. Give them to him and you will recieve the 'Dark Spear of Venom' 17/27, +10 hp, +5 magic, +5 int effect: sicken.
