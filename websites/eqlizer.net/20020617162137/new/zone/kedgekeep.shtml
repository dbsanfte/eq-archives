Kedge Keep

Location
Kedge Keep is located on the island of Faydwer to the east of mainland Antonica


Zone Type
Kedge Keep is a completely underwater zone, beneath the waters of Dagnor's Cauldron. Its a high level dungeon.


Access
Kedge Keep borders with Dagnor's Cauldron. There is only one entry and exit, which can be found by walking out from Unrest and walking/swimming straight down, then once at the bottom take a right and follow the wall. The enterance looks like a ruined temple. Have some kind of underwater breathing equipment handy, or you wont even make it this far.

Kedge Keep is one of the most unique zones in the game. Unfortunatly because of how inconveniant it is to get a group together there its often empty. This leaves it open for guild raids though, and its well suited to them, with items for wizards to warriors. The zone can be cleared with two well trained groups.


Background
Kedge Keep was the realm of the Kedge, one of the most ancient races upon Norrath, now only one lives, his name is Phinigel Autropos. Kedge Keep now houses his guardians, the Swirlspine Seahorses, and a pair of named sharks. Also sharing the Keep are the Gloomwater Mermaids.




Layout
Kedge keep is an underwater city, for anyone to live you must have casters with Enduring Breath, Magicians with water stones, or a Fishbone Earing. It has many vast open areas, as well as the small ducts that join them.

Bestiary

Common

Small fish are all around, whatever you do don't nuke these, some have a suicide attack of about 500dmg AE which may not be too much in itself, but the little fish will KOS you for the rest of the time you spend in the zone, which is a pain if your in a big fight, or recovering from one. There are also sharks and seashorses of varying levels.
