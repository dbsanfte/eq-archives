Druid Spells

Level 1

Burst Of Flame

Burst of flame is the first direct damage spell that a magician receives. It is a low damage spell but will have to do until you make level 4.

Dance Of The Fireflies

This is a light spell which only works in outdoor zones.

Endure Fire

This spell adds to a players resistance to fire.

Flame Lick

This spell is used to lower an opponents armor class, making him more succeptible.

Lull Animal

This spell will can cause animals that are normally agressive to not attack if succesful.

Minor Healing

Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.

Panic Animal

This spell will cause an animal to run away in a panicked state. Beware that after this spell wears off(approximately 30 seconds) the animal will return.

Sense Animal

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest animal.

Skin Like Wood

This is the first in the line of druid buffing spells. It adds to both the armor class and hit points of a player.

Snare

Snare is sort of a limited root spell. While it does not hold a monster perfectly still(and prevent it from fleeing) it does drastically lower it's movement rate which accomplishes nearly the same effect.

Level 5

Burst Of Fire

Burst of fire is a direct damage spell. Basically it is an upgrade to Burst Of Flame.

Camoflauge

This is an outdoor only invisibility spell.

Cure Disease

This spell can be used to cure disease.

Cure Poison

This spell will counteract poisons.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Grasping Roots

Grasping Roots is an upgrade to snare. This spell actually roots the monster in place(instead of slowing him down) and also does a small amount of damage for the duration.

Harmony

Harmony is an area effect lull spell. It lowers monsters aggressiveness temporarily.

Invoke Lightning

Invoke lightning is an outdoor only area effect spell.

Ward Summoned

This spell will do a good amount of direct damage(30+level capping at 42 for only 30 mana) to summoned beasts such as elements. 

Level 9

Endure Cold

This spell will increase your resistance to cold based attacks.

Enduring Breath

Enduring breath is a water breathing spell.

Firefist

This spell will to your ATK rating for a period of time. This will make you hit more often and do a higher amount of damage.

Ignite

Ignite is a fire based direct damage spell.

Invisibility vs. Animals

This spell will render you invisible to animals. It is not very useful as you will usually prefer to use camo or superior camo instead.

Light Healing

Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.

Shield Of Thistles

This spell is a damage shield. It does more damage than thistecoat but has a much shorter duration.

Starshine

Starshine is an outdoor light spell.

Strength Of Earth

This spell will add to a players strength ability score.

Thistlecoat

This spell is a minor damage shield which also adds armor class. The coat line of spells are self only.

Treeform

Treeform will allow you to take the shape of a tree. You can not move but you will regenerate.


Level 14

Befriend Animals

Befriend animals is a charming spell which will work on animals only.

Bind Affinity

This spell is used to change a players bind point. This is the point where a player will return to if they die or gate.

Cascade Of Hail

This is an outdoor rain spell.

Expulse Summoned

Expulse summoned is an update to the ward summoned line of spells which does direct damage to elements.

Halo Of Light

This spell summons a magical headband which casts light as a greater lightstone.

Invigor

Invigor will return a players stamina much like a heal spell returns health.

Levitate

This spell will allow a character to levitate. It is useful for travelling over water or lava and in situations where you are afraid you might be knocked off of a ledge.

See Invisible

This spell will allow you to see invisible beings.

Skin Like Rock

Skin like rock is an update to the skin like wood spell. It adds armor class and hit points.

Spirit Of The Wolf

This is one of the most useful travel spells in the game. It allows you to run at double speed in outdoor zones which is great for escaping attack or for reducing travel time.

Stinging Swarm

This spell summons a swarm beings that will do damage over time to your target.

Summon Drink

Summons some water. This spell isn't really useful since you have the forage skill.

Summon Food

Summons some food. As with summon drink it's not really useful to a druid.

Level 19

Barbcoat

This spell is an upgrade to thistlecoat. It adds armor class and a small damage shield.

Calm Animal

Calm animal will calm animals and lower their agressivenes.

Cancel Magic

This spell will cancel a magic effect cast on you or one of your party members. It is useful if someone is rooted, charmed or has a nasty DoT spell cast on them.

Careless Lightning

This is a direct damage spell.

Dizzying Wind

This outdoor only direct damage spell.

Endure Disease

This spell adds to your resistance to disease.

Endure Poison

This spell adds to your resistance to poison.

Feral Spirit

Feral spirit is a buffing spell which can be used on your pet(a befriended animal perhaps). It will make them much more formidable.

Healing

Healing is the third in the line of healing spells. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.

Rings Of Butcher, Tox, Karana, Commons

These are the first of your personal travel spells. They will teleport you to one of the above locations.

Shield Of Barbs

Shield of barbs is an upgrade to shield of thistles. It creates a damage shield.

Superior Camoflauge

This is an invisibility spell which works both indoor and outdoor.

Terrorize Animal

This spell is a fear spell that works only on animals.

Level 24

Charm Animals

This spell will charm animals and allow you to use them as pets.

Creeping Crud

This is an upgrade to the stinging swarm spell. It does damage over time.

Dismiss Summoned

Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the ward->expulse summoned line of spells.

Ensnaring Roots

This spell not only roots your opponent but also does damage while rooted.

Pogonip

This spell is a rain based area effect spell.

Resist Fire

Adds to your resistance to fire based attacks.

Ring of Feerrott, Lavastorm, Ro, Steamfont and Combines

These are the second set of personal travel spells. They will teleport you to one of the above locations.

Skin Like Steel

Skin like steel is an upgrade to the skin like wood->rock line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.

Spirit Of The Cheetah

This spell allows you to go into super fast sprint mode. It is much like spirit of the wolf only faster and with a shorter duration.

Sunbeam

This is an outdoor only area effect blindness spell.

Tremor

Tremor is an area effect spell which has a large area of effect. In exchange for its effect radius it sacrifices mana efficiency.

Wolf Form

This spell allows you to assume the form of a wolf. It will also raise your ATK and increases your running speed. Some NPC's respond differently to wolves so it can be both a blessing and a curse. It is self only and outdoor only.

Level 29

Beguile Plants

Charm plant spell.

Bramblecoat

This is an upgrade to the thistlecoat line of spells. It adds armor class and a damage shield.

Circle Of Butcher, Commons, Karana and Tox

The circle spells are group teleport spells.

Combust

Combust is a fire based direct damage spell.

Counteract Disease

This is an upgrade to the cure disease spell. It works on more powerful diseases.

Counteract Poison

This is an upgrade to the cure poison spell. It works on more powerful types of poison.

Ensnare

Ensnare is an upgrade to the snare line of spells which will slow a mob's movement for a fixed amount of time.

Extinguish Fatigue

This spell will return a players lost stamina.

Greater Healing

Greater healing is the last in the line of druid healing spells. It heals roughly 270 damage for 150 mana.

Imbue Emerald

Imbue an emerald for use in trade skills and cultural armor.

Imbue Plains Pebble

Imbue a plains pebble for use in trade skills and cultural armor.

Immolate

This is a damage over time spell which also lowers a monsters armor class.

Ring Of Misty

This is a self teleport spell to misty thicket.

Scale of Wolf

Faster version of spirit of wolf. You will lose this spell's effect if you are attacked or attacking creatures, this does include offensive spells.

Shield Of Brambles

This is the level 29 damage shield.

Succor: East

Succor spells are emergency party escape spells. They cast faster than the normal teleport spells and can be used in emergency situations. The weakness is that someone might get left behind in rare instances.

Level 34

Beguile Animals

Beguile animals is an upgrade to the charm animal line of spells.

Circle Of Feerot, Lavastorm, Ro, Steamfont and Combines

The circle spells are group teleport spells.

Drones Of Doom

Drones of doom is an upgrade to the stinging swarm line of DoT spells.

Earthquake

Earthquake is an upgrade to tremor. It is a wide radius area effect spell.

Endure Magic

This spell adds to your resistance to magic.

Expel Summoned

Upgrade to dismiss summoned.

Fury of the Air (V)
Direct Damage and Stun to target.

Greater Wolf Form

This is an upgrade to wolf form it not only assumes the form of a wolf but gives special wolf like abilities. It can only be cast on yourself.

Lightning Strike

This is an outdoor only direct damage spell.

Regeneration

This spell allows a player to regenerate hit points. It is a nice spell for reducing downtime while you meditate.

Resist Cold

Resist cold adds to a players resistance to cold based attacks.

Ring of Iceclad

Teleports the caster to Iceclad Ocean.

Strength Of Stone

This is an upgrade to the strength of earth line of spells.

Succor: Butcher

The butcherblock escape spell.

Level 39

Avalanche

Avalanche is an outdoor only area effect spell.

Circle Of Misty

Group teleportation to misty thicket.

Enveloping Roots

This spell not only roots a player but also does some damage in the process.

Firestrike

This is a fire based direct damage spell.

Pack Regeneration

Pack regeneration will cast regeneration on your entire party. It is useful in battles where several players take good damage.

Pack Spirit

Pack spirit is a group spirit of the wolf spell.

Ro's Fiery Sundering (V)

Single target AC and Fire Resistance debuff.

Share Wolf Form

This spell casts wolf form on your entire group.

Shield Of Spikes

Shield of spikes is the next in the line of damage shield spells.

Skin Like Diamond

This is an upgrade to the skin like wood line of spells which adds armor class and hit points.

Spikecoat

Spikecoat is an upgrade to the thistlecoat line of spells which adds armor class and a small damage shield.

Succor: Ro

The escape spell for Ro desert.

Wind of the North

Group evac spell that takes you to Skyfire Mountains. (Only works in kunark dungeons)

Wind of the South

Group evac spell that takes you to Emerald Jungle. (Only works in kunark dungeons)


Level 44

Allure Of The Wild

This is the last in the line of charm animal spells.

Banish Summoned

Banish summoned is the last in the line of direct damage spells to be used versus summoned foes.

Calefaction

Fire based fast casting direct damage. This spell has a long recast time though.

Chloroplast

Chloroplast is an upgrade to the regeneration spells which regenerate at a higher rate.

Circle of Cobalt Scar, Great Divide and Wakening Lands

Teleports the caster and all group members to the desired location.

Drifting Death

This is the last in the line of damage over time spells. It is an upgrade to stinging swarm and drones of death.

Fixation of Ro (V)

Single target AC and atack debuff.

Form Of The Great Wolf

This allows you to cast greater wolf form on your party.

Nullify Magic

Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Resist Disease

Adds to your resistance to disease.

Resist Poison

Adds to your resistance of poison.

Savage Spirit

I'm not really certain what this spell does. I believe it is a pet buffing spell.

Storm Strength

This is the last in the line of druid strength spells.

Succor: Lavastorm

The escape spell to lavastorm.

Level 49

Engulfing Roots

This is the last in the line of ensnaring root type spells which both root and do damage.

Fire

Fire based direct damage spell.

Ice

Ice based direct damage spell.

Lightning Blast

This is the last in the line of lightning based attacks. These spells are outdoor only.

Pack Chloroplast

Group chloroplast spell.

Resist Magic

This adds to your resistance to magic.

Shield Of Thorns

This is the last in the line of damage shields.

Skin Like Nature

Skin like nature is an upgrade to the skin like wood line of spells. It adds to your armor class and hit points.

Starfire

Starfire is the last in the line of druid direct damage spells. It is very mana efficient and is nearly identical to the level 44 wizard DD spell.

Succor: North

This is the escape spell which will teleport your group to North Karana.

Thorncoat

Thorncoat adds both armor class and a light damage shield.

Level 50

Improved Superior Camoflage (V)

Fixed duration camoflage.

Level 51

Circle of Winter

Group fire resist spell.

Legacy of Spike

Group damage shield.

Repulse Animal

Fear spell that targets only animals.

Superior Healing

575 point healing spell.

Upheaval

Area of effect damage spell.

Level 52

Breath of Ro

Decreases target mob's armor class.

Call of Karana

Upgraded charm animal spell.

Circle of Summer

Group cold resist spell.

Egress

Group evacuate spell to location -1 -1 of current zone.

Level 53

Glamour of Tunare

Poisons target and reduces it's magic resistance.

Spirit of Scale

Group scale of wolf spell.

Winged Death

Upgraded DoT spells. Approx 1280 damage in 9 ticks.

Level 54

Blizzard

Ice based Area of Effect spell.

Form of the Howler

Upgraded wolf form that gives the same effect as Breeze.

Regrowth

Upgraded version of regeneration.

Scoriae

Upgraded direct damage spell. Approx 700 damage for 250 mana.

Level 55

Annul Magic

Upgraded Cancle mage spell.

Chloroblast

Single target healing spell, fast casting time, low hate.

Exile Summoned

Approx 700 points of damage to any summoned creature.

Girdle of Karana

Upgraded strength spell.

Nature Walker's Beast (V)

Summon Wolf Pet

Tunare's Request

Upgraded charm animal spell.

Level 56

Bladecoat

Increases ac and adds a small damage shield that will stack with shield spells.

Breath of Karana

Outdoor only direct damage spell.

Engorging Roots

A root spell with a small ammount of direct damage (175-190)

Wake of Karana

This spell allows you to control the weather, if it's raining or snowing and you use it, it will stop raining or snowing, I've been told that this will clear up fog in some zones as well.

Level 57

Bonds of Tunare

Upgrade to snare and ensnare.

Frost

Ice based direct damage spell.

Natureskin

Upgraded skin like nature. Adds hp and ac.

Succor

Group dungeon evacuation spell.

Level 58

Fist of Karana

Outdoor only wind based direct damage spell.

Regrowth of the Grove

Group regrowth spell.

Shield of Blades

Adds damage shield to target.

Level 59

Legacy of Thorn

Group damage shield spell.

Spirit of Oak

Self only treeform that enhances regeneration as well as giving the effect of clarity.

Wildfire

1024 direct damage spell.

Level 60

Banishment

Destroy target summoned creature. (Requires unkonwn component)

Entrapping Roots

Upgraded druid root spell.

Form of the Hunter

Upgraded wolf form. This wolf form has no duration, it will only remove when you zone or cancle it. It also gives the speed effect of scale of wolf, instead of spirit of wolf.

Mask of the Hunter

Self only spell that shrinks you, magnifies your vision, allows you to see invis and boosts your mana regeneration rate by 3.

Nature's Touch

Single target healing spell.

Protection of the Glades (V)

Group HP, AC and Regeneration buff.
