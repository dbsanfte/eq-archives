Quest Component Database Is Working
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

Conversion wise I am in the Gs, so I decided it was time to put the Quest Component Database online. This database is not complete yet, we're only in the Gs zone wise. As I am going through each zone I'm tagging stuff as Quest Components, and once we have the site in good shape I'll start searching for harder to find ones. In the meantime though you can take advantage of this new tool for the site to help you figure out what quests those strange items you are finding is used in.
