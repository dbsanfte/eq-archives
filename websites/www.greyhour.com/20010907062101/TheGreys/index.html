here was a time when the lines between good and evil were easily drawn in the land of Norrath. Those things good and true were written on the faces of the innocent. Those things chaotic and spiteful were etched in the souls of the dark ones. The war fought over these lines has yet to find a victor, and in it's wake has given birth to a new power that draws like a curtain over the land. This power is the one of balance, the symmetry of good and evil. It collects It's fierceness from all life on Norrath. It courses through the veins of all that know it and speak It's name. Those who understand, call It by It's name… they quietly whisper, "Grey".

he Greys are those who know the true power of Norrath. They sit idly by and watch Good and Evil consume itself, forever knowing that in the end all things will balance. They walk among you, both good and evil, and wear their state of equilibrium like a shroud.
