Member Directory
Echo of Valor is a fun loving guild on the Sayrn server that is built to assure the fun and enjoyment of all who are a part of it.  We strive to be as family oriented as a guild can be and try to make all feel welcome and wanted in our everyday gaming life.
New members please read...
You start in a 3 week trial period, during that time you can be booted for not following our simple rules. You can also leave during the 3 weeks if you wish and come back no problem.  If you leave after the 3 weeks you need two officers to ok your return. 
To assure that these standards are upheld we have a few simple rules.

1.) Absolutely NO flaming of other guildies in  guild chat. If you  have a problem with a guild member you talk to me (Fyreslayer) or one of our officers they are listed on the Members section of this website..

2.) Be willing to help guild members by grouping, and questing whenever possible.  If you wish to do a quest and need help please post on Forum under the Raids and Events section.  This will ensure the most amount of people will be there. 

3.) Our name is Echo of Valor....so  plz don't act in a way that would embarass the guild.

4.) On any guild raids and or alliance raids we will follow Need Before Greed rules.  If your class/race can use the item then you can ROLL on it.  AND you do not plan on selling the item.  This Rule can and has been adjusted for raids but is a basic rule on most.  Please know the loot rules before a raid so there is no confusion.  Not all Raids work on NBG and sometimes some items are requested for epics and questing.

5.) HAVE FUN WITH THE FRIENDS WE HAVE MADE!

Echos of Valor

  We were trudging through the canyon carefully looking left and right so as not to get surprised.  Then our point calls out INCOMING and it's a BIG one.  Around the corner he comes running with all the speed spirit of wolf could give him.  Following closely behind right on his tail, the biggest dragon I have ever seen.  I yell "Let's Get him guys". I  lead in with my sword blazing and make the first blow 120hp's.  "Nice Hit" someone calls.  We keep slashing and kasting another and another.  "Is this guy ever going DOWN", I shout.  Finally with one last blow the mighty beast slowly falls and hits the ground.  "Wheew finally he's down", I say.  When no more I get the words out of my mouth that another attacks us again we kill the beast and again another comes.  Time and time we rise victoriuos.  What a day this has been, as I gaze back at our weary crew I realize that we are the best.  We have risen above the others. "We are the Echos of Valor"
