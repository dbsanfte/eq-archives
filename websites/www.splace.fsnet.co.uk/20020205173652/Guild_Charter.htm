GUILD CHARTER

 

 

Celestial Rising is a community of friendly adventurers whose first and foremost belief is "Dedication to Comradery'.  Generally, a small guild does not remain small because it is incapable of attracting members, but because it wishes to remain small.  Therefore, we are selective in our choice of members.  To ensure a tight-nit group of comrades the guild will never become UBER.


The members of a small guild become close companions and good friends, in a way that a larger guild is unlikely to enjoy.  In a small guild, one can come to know every member so well that one may trust each of them absolutely, with life and with honour.  A member of Celestial Rising will give his/her own life in defence of another member.

 

Celestial Rising will strive to make the most enjoyable environment for its members as possible.  This means that we play EverQuest to be with one another, though it is not mandatory for members to group only with other members it will be encouraged..  We do not farm items, do not hold camps for days, do not conquer.  We play because we enjoy one another's company.  Conquest or wealth may come, but it is incidental to the enjoyment we gather on the way.

When one's guild has but a handful of members, there is nowhere to hide.  If one of us is rude or foolish, we cannot brush off a complaint as being unrepresentative of the guild as a whole.  Likewise, if one of us is noted for kindness, sound tactics, or heroism, we all may take warm pride in our friend, and in ourselves.


All members of Celestial Rising shall be encouraged to set up scheduled raids and events to glean the experience that running these events has to offer, but as a whole we are here to have fun and to make friends.

 

 

Jennya Haelen - Guild Leader
