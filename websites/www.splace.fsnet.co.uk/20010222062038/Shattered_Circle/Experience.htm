Experience

Experience will be gained in one of five ways:

 

1

 For every 'In Character' post that is made on a character's behalf.  If a post includes particularly good role playing I will add a bonus

2

 When a Character uses his or her special skills, I will award some extra experience.  This does not include spells in combat - which is just the equivalent of the fighter's long sword
3 At the end of a combat, I will total the experience points awarded for whatever is defeated in the combat and divide it equally among the participating members of the party.  Participating members don't have to be involved in melee - they may be on look out duty, collecting and healing the wounded or spell casting
4 So long as the party are doing things to carry out their assigned mission or task, I will award extra story line experience occasionally
5 When a character comes up with a good solution to a problem, they will gain extra experience
