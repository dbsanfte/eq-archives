Yanish

 

Appearance:

     Her outward appearance is that of a beggar from the many days living on the land, with a dirty face, that if cleaned would look quite beautiful, and knotted hair brown hair.   Her clothing is not the only thing that the journey has soiled, her temper and sharp eyes, warn those that consider a female halfling easy prey to think twice before crossing her path.   

History:

     Yanish has an extreme hatred of Orcs, as a raiding party destroyed her parents and village.  Only her ability to hide, and remain silent saved her from the marauders hands.  Orphaned she fled her small town and followed the great river that ran near the village.  Following it she came across a large human settlement.  Seeking food and money she enters with hopes for a brighter future.  Smiling and running her finger across the dagger concealed under her cloak she thinks, "No matter what it takes."
