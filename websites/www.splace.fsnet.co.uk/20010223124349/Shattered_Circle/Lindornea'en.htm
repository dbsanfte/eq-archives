Appearance:

 

     The best description for the Elf before you, would be ...  A Decidedly Roguish look, for one of the Fae race, for his hair is pulled back into a tail, except where it decided to fall forward.  A loosely bound cloak hides most of what he wears underneath, but makes no effort to disguise the Longsword sheathed across his back.  His gloved hands carefully hold a shod staff ... but, it's slightly out of place, considering the even cracks that run up and down the thing.  His blue eyes swiftly appraise the scene, whilst his golden hair shines slightly in the light ... 

     Yet the recent dust of the road adorns his figure...

 

History:

 

     A cross between Han Solo, and Robin Hood. With a slight mix of Dirty Harry ...  Quite willing to do things rubbing the law the wrong way, not particularly interested in 'All' the time ... but a Sucker for a Hard Case.

 

     With something to prove, he left his homeland, intent on making a name for himself using the skills taught to him by the Village.  It hurt him that everyone never considered him truthfully, and never believed he'd amount to anything more than a guide.  His Singular nature led him to follow his own path more than listening to others, and the Enjoyment gleaned from angering the Village Elders taught him that Law is only run by those who sit in counsel ...  So, who needs it?  With a grin on his face, and something to prove, he found his way ... only to end up in the wrong place ... at the right time ...
