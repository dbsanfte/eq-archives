Guild News & Information :

 

Friday 12th April - Umbral Planes

Tonight we decided to take a small raiding group to Umbral Planes and see what armour we could pick up.  With a three group force Zeksie went out scouting to find us a safe spot to pull too.  We set up camp on the east side of the zone and pulled whatever mob crossed our path including Dark Assasins, Moktors and Zelniaks.  The armour drops weren't particularly fruitful but GRATS to Klaawz who took home a Dark Assasin Bracer.
 
 

Thursday 11th April - Plane of Hate

Another trip to Plane of Hate is always welcomed by our guild, so when we were invited by Dreams of Darkness we jumped at the opportunity.  It was the first time we have raided with Dreams of Darkness and the team work between the two guilds was exemplary.  We sat in the safe corner and continued to pull the roamers and the nearest buildings.  The trip saw us take down the Warrior and the Mage Miniboss and the following rewards were ours for the taking :

Imbrued Platemail Bracer - Taja  (DoD)

Imbrued Platemail Breastplate - Taja  (DoD)

Ethereal Mist Gauntlets - Rumpel  (DoD)

Sode of Empowerment - Zeksie

Imbrued Platemail Boots - Akapella

Ethereal Mist Vambracers - Jennya

Apothis Boots - Mathematix  (DoD)

Apothic Crown - Mathematix  (DoD)

CONGRATULATIONS to everyone that took home new armour and special thanks to Dreams of Darkness for inviting us along
   
   

Wednesday 10th April - Epic Day

And what a day it was.  It kicked off when Talaz jumped on line to announce that both Phinigel and Ulump were up.  It was quite early in the day so not that many people online and took a while to muster the people, but with help from friends we managed to throw together 2 groups and off we went.  The outcome of this great evening brought Talaz and Priwinn another step closer to their epics and provided Mystyfy with her very own 'leaf blower' the second epic of the week.

Celestial Rising would like to thank everyone that attended to help in the three battles below and a special thanks and grats to Cern of Divine Prophecy who provided us with some more manpower for VSr and looted the second stone for his ranger epic

CONGRATULATIONS to Talaz and Priwinn and *big hugs* to Mystyfy
     

Phinigel - Kedge Keep

With not many people on line we had to call in help from all over the shop (thanks Verant for friend's lists).  It took a while to muster the people but finally we were buffing up in Dagnor's ready for the trip into the murky depths of Kedge Keep.  A slight hiccup at the start with a druid succor taking the second group away from us, but before long we were all down on the ledge pulling the seahorse guardians from around the ugly fish.  Three seahorses later and we were ready.  Zeksie managed to single pull him to the ledge, and Kwanii managed to land a slow which unfortunatly meant she was summoned to her death.  He kept swimming away from us but our sit down tactic soon brought him back and before long he was floating in front of us as dead as a Dodo.

We were very lucky with his loot and congratulations to Zyankaly on the Robe, to Bennyboy on the Ore, Jennya has the shaman rod in case it can be multi quested and special ....

CONGRATULATIONS to Talaz on his staff

     

Ulump - Swamp of No Hope

Next saw us travel to the Swamp of No Hope for a meeting with a certain Froggy  Again we called in a few more friends to help us needing brute force for this battle.  We attacked him on the spot rather than pulling him to ruins as we had done in the past, and this proved no more difficult than usual and before long there he was dead on the floor.  Thanks again to everyone that helped us and

CONGRATULATIONS Priwinn only 3 more kills to go
     

Venril Sathir Remains - Karnor's Castle

After Ulump we all went our separate ways in search of experience or spells or whatever it was everyone did.  A few hours later though it was Mystyfy's turn to jump on line and inform us that Venril Sathir's Remains were for the picking.  Looking at the guild list she doubted our ability, but was told to sit in KC and call the camp and we found ourselves once again on a manhunt.  Between us all we have some wonderful friends and within the hour we were a small army of four full groups.  Mystyfy had discovered that Cern of 'Divine Prophecy' was also in need of the stone so we agreed that we would share one with each of our guilds.  Korudan fronted the raid, and a fantastic job he did too (pick up raids are never easy) and despite the couple of deaths on the way to the prep room we were soon gathered, buffed and waiting on the hand ins.  With Korudan MTing VS the battle went smoothly and VS was sprawled on the dungeon floor.

Special thanks to all who attended and helped grats to Cern on your stone and big huge

CONGRATULATIONS to Mystyfy

only the hand ins to do now and the 'leaf blower' is all yours

     
     
Monk Epic - Final Battle

We were on an epic roll, so why not finish it off tonight.  Putting our feelers out to everyone on our friends lists we managed to gather almost 5 groups in Lake of Ill Omen for the final battle of the monk epic.  Everyone got buffed up and Zeksie went to do her hand ins, bringing Vorash back to the group to be pounded whilst Megwyn (Omerta) kited Deep round until we were ready for her.  I think overkill was an understatement here, but Vorash was soon on the floor closely followed by Deep, and then Xenovorash was soon to follow.

I know Zeksie would like to thank everyone that attended and assisted her in this and she is now the proud owner of Celestial Fists

CONGRATULATIONS Zeksie, those bubbles look good :0)

Sunday 7th April - Plane of Air

Two small groups this evening ventured up to the Plane of Air, to assist Zeksie in the slaying of Gwan.  We managed to get everyone miniature swords and handed them in to get our key.  A quick jump on the portal took us to the next isle and we made camp in the windmill.  Buffing everyone up ready for the battle, Zeksie went off to find the trigger mob and pulled Gwan back to us.  It was a long fight and Jennya I think has won the record for most times summoned in one battle (9 in total), but we succeeded (did you doubt us?)

GRATS to Zeksie only one more kill now till the big LOIO battle
 
 
Saturday 6th April - Chardok Royals

Today saw us gather troops (when the servers finally came back up!) in the entrance to Chardok for a trip down to the royal headquarters.  A good turn out provided us with four full groups, but our raid leader mysteriously vanished, not to be put off, with the guidance of Eldwyn (Blood Cult) we made our way down into the depths.  It wasn't too bad a journey but we lost our MA half way down and ended up having to deal with back spawns, but he came back in the end and we were on our way again.  Once inside we headed down to the prince room and made camp in the room just off to the side.  Clearing the room spawns a couple of times before we pulled the prince, the only problem was with the prince came many friends, and our overworked enchanters as wonderful as they are couldn't cope with all of them.  (let me just point out here though, there were only 2 enchanters present)  The prince went down, but running out of mana fast the other mobs started breaking mez, and before long most of us were lying on the floor.  We had managed to get a cleric to camp out, and back she came and started rezzing, but luck wasn't on our side today as the room started popping, and with rez effects still strong once more we all bit the dust.  Time now to call in the help of a level 60 necro head back to Chardok and begin the long winded corpse recovery.  Today obviously wasn't our day to achieve :0(

Special thanks to Blimp, Trazer, Vexica and Eldwyn, and thank you to everyone including members of Dreams of Darkness and Blood Cult that turned up to help us.

Live in fear Royals, we will be back to conquer
