The Shattered Circle

 

Welcome to The Shattered Circle.

 

The Shattered Circle is a 2nd Edition Advanced Dungeons and Dragons (ADnD) Game that I am DM'ing on www.playbyweb.com. 

 

As my gaming world expands, I will post details on the World pages, and keep the History pages updated too.  I will publish any maps to the Maps pages.

 

For guidelines on Character Generation please follow the links provided.

 

The characters for my current game are now up on the Characters page giving individual details on the characters appearance and background history.

 

I will regularly upload the game history from PlayByWeb, and put it into the Game History pages.

 

I have added in an Experience page which describes how characters can gain experience in the game, and will also have running XP Totals for each of the characters playing in my current game.

 

The Religion pages detail the religions of He Who Provides a common religion throughout the lands of Andletairre.

 

I have also include a Monster and a Treasure page, which will give details of all monsters and treasure that the players come across within the game.
