Friday 22nd February - What a night !

It seems to be becoming a regular Friday night thing now until all our druids are in possession of a 'Jade Reaver', but what a night !!

I don't think anyone could actually believe what was happening today.  After many attempts at reavers and not one dropping at all, this night was the exception.  Four groups made their way to the castle at the back of City of Mist, killing everything that stood in their way.  We shook hands with Lord Rak'Ashir outside and continued to pull the castle entrance hall.  The first reaver as usual was sitting on the throne in there, we cleared the room and then pulled him out.  A few hits later he was lying face down on the floor and we waited for the 'nothing on corpse' call that we were so used to .... but no ... not this time ... our first black reaver produced our first Jade Reaver of the night.  Yes, you heard me ... our FIRST reaver ... the first of THREE believe it or not.  We worked with ease, it was definitely our best reaver raid to date, not just because of the drops, but it seemed so easy, and went so smoothly.  Two hours later we were in possession of THREE Jade Reavers so ...

CONGRATULATIONS to Bellilama (Omerta), Paaw, and Megwyn (Omerta) who are all another stage closer to their Epic

CONGRATULATIONS to Talaz and Kulfi today as they both manage another step towards their epics.  After a long camp in City of Mist for the Human Skeleton Talaz finally got the 3rd part of the Concusion spell - Soul of Mardon.  Being in City of Mist also proved fruitful to Kulfi as she was allowed to loot the three books required for her next stage from Lord Ghiosk which 5 members of The Well had just taken down.

 

 

Thursday 10th January, 2002 - Kael Drakkal Raid

 

We were very kindly invited by 'Omerta' to join them on a raid to Kael Drakkal.  Our target camp was the Arena and without any trouble at all it wasn't long before groups were set up and we were safely there.  The evening was enjoyed by all and we seemed to have the lucky dice as we were winning roll after roll.  Out of these 3 pieces of quest armour were dropped and won as below :

 

Kwanii  -  Silk Bracer

Talaz  -  Silk Legs

Zeksie  -  Leather Gloves

 

I would like to thank Omerta for having us along and to thank everyone that turned up and took part.  Great work guys.

 

 

Wednesday 9th January, 2002 - New boots and a key

 

Well after almost 20 hours Jennya and Zeksie were finally in possession of 'Ring of the Ancients'.  A quick trip to West Commanlands to take down some Shadow Men for the Shadowed Rapier, pick up 3250 gp at the bank, then off to Rathe Mountains with Grabatz and Yinadriel in tow.  We weren't the only ones after Hasten, Bennyboy a monk from Blood Cult needed to kill him for his epic so after bringing him over to us to trade in the bits for our boots we assisted him in doing this.  Jennya and Zeksie are now the proud owners of a pair of Journeyman Boots.

 

It was quickly discovered that our new warrior Badiek was not in possession of the Sebilis key, so a group set out to get this rectified.  On reaching Trakanon's Teeth we found that a Doom Skeleton was blocking up the spawn table so re-enforcements were called for.  With the help of Oana the Blood Cult warrior we took him down without too much trouble and after recovering Paaw's corpse a couple of times from the depths of Trakanon the medallions were soon in Badiek's possession.

 

 

Tuesday 8th January, 2002 - A step nearer our epics

 

What was becoming a long boring Ancient Cyclops camp in Ocean of Tears turned out to be quite eventful.  Sitting and watching the time slip by as Jennya, Zeksie and Paaw killed Aqua Goblins, Ogres and Cyclops in the vain hope that the Ancient would spawn, Paaw announced that the Tainted Cyclops was on track so off we went.  We managed to knock down the Tainted without too much trouble, but then the Corrupted spawned immediately from it's corpse and this proved a little more difficult.  It was down to almost one bubble of health when Jennya ran out of mana, Paaw was having problems casting and soon he and Zeksie had become corpses.  In a rush of panic at losing the AC camp we hurried our way back to the island rezzing the fallen and resumed camp once more.  The Corrupted headed back over to the island and this time we were more successful in bringing him down and Paaw is now one step closer to his epic.

 

Fendel and Trepidation were invited to join Blood Cult on a raid to the City of Mist and within no time at all Lord Ghiosk had been brought down and the three books are now in Trepidation's possession giving us another guild member one step closer to his epic.

 

CONGRATULATIONS guys on a job well done

Sunday 6th January, 2002 - Celestial Rising is born

 

Well it is done, at approximately 10pm GMT on the XEV server, Celestial Rising was born.  After sitting waiting for almost 2 hours for a GM to respond to our petition amidst the duelling that was taking place through boredom a response finally came.  We had a turnout of 15 people which was a wonderful start and I would just like to say thank you to everyone involved in the whole thing.

 

 

Sunday 6th January, 2002 - Guild Approval

 

Well it's 03:00 on the morning of 6th of January and I have just checked my email and we have guild approval ... WOOT !!!!  Our guild has been approved as Celestial Rising, now all we have to do is gather a minimum of 10 people together when a GM is online so that the guild can be born.  I am desperately hoping that later on as we prepare to wipe out Chardok that a GM will be about, but i'm not holding out much hope.  If not then, then tomorrow night for definite.
