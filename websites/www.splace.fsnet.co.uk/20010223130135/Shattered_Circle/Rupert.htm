Appearance:

Rupert is a thin, not altogether unpleasant looking man, with straight light red hair held in a ponytail, fair skin and slightly pointed ears.  He favors a long coat with many deep pockets that he stores things in and is forever losing important items.  He walks with a staff and has a tendency to look quite distracted by the slightest thing.

History:

     Born to his mother Rachel and an Elven father whose name he still does not know, Half-Elven Rupert DeVere-Brown has lived a very sheltered life.  Whisked away by the machinations of his unknown father at a young age, he was closeted in a series of very private tutoring establishments.  It was in these that he discovered his innate abilities as a Transmutor, along with his skills in math, engineering and most surprisingly, cooking. 

     While away, he heard of his mother’s tragic accident and the birth of his half sister Dolores.  He immediately had felt pity for his sister’s heritage and decided he would have to go home to help her when he was finished with his studies.  In the meantime, he wrote home often, not surprised that he received few letters in return.

     Growing into a young man, Rupert began feeling the urge to wander the world.  He returned home to find (to his horror) that his sister was a backroom drudge.  Although she seemed physically able to take care of herself, her older brother decided then and there to take her away.  Perhaps in travel, he could protect her and satisfy his curiosity of the world he had seen so little of.
