Appearance:

 

     Dolores, known as Dolly, is a short woman who looks about 16 or 17 years old  ... unfortunately for  her she has a protruding lower jaw, wide flat nose, small close set grey eyes that always seem to have  red  flecks in the bits that should be white.  She has straight ginger hair that always seems greasy - no matter how often she washes it - she hasn't yet found the right 'product' for it.  

 

     To make matters worse she is short and well built.  Although only 4'11" tall -  she is nearly the same  measurement across.  Her shoulders are massive as are her arms and legs -  they just appear to be pillars  of muscle.  She has well developed breasts, which her armour flattens and hides - but is a bit overweight  as well.  So in many ways, especially when she is armed and armoured, she looks almost as wide as she is tall.  To the uninitiated  she looks a bit like a dwarf while her shield covers her lower face -  but  when the shield is dropped -  boy are they in for a surprise  ...

 

     In villages and towns she will dress in men's clothes, with tight breeches and a shirt -  normally with the top buttons undone and her breasts almost bursting out.   There will always be a dagger hidden discreetly about her  person.  When she is expecting combat she will wear her studded leather armour  and carry her shield and beloved  battle axe.
