Wednesday 29th May - A Couple of Epic Kills & A Kael Raid

It was early evening when the call went out across the guild that Venrina Tomb was up in Neriak.  For 45 minutes we panicked with no chanter on line we were going to miss this opportunity, but just then Azurite popped on.  A 'Call to Arms' was made and before long 8 people were gathered at the zone in the depths of Neriak.  With some very quick faction work whilst waiting for people to arrive Azurite was able to pull her himself and we proceeded to beat the life out of her. 

Being so close to Lavastorm we took the opportunity then to travel to Solusek Temple for a quick meeting with the Plasmatic Priest.  Down to just one group now as others had RL duties to attend we had to wait until 9am game time for the seeker to spawn.  Azurite did the duty again of pulling the seeker to the zone line so Celestial could do her hand in and it wasn't long before the Plasmatic Priest was face down in the temple dust.

CONGRATULATIONS to Azurite and Celestial another step closer to your epics

The evening saw us head back into Kael to set camp up at the arena.  For three hours we kept ourselves busy with constant pulls and believe it or not all we got to show for it was 2 pairs of Gladiator Chain Leggings.  Peni took the first pair and Kalnaery was awarded the second, before the Idol turned up to say hello and caused an almost entire party wipe-out.  Special thanks to Zeksie, Skithe and Ehlana who did a great corpse recovery for us.

Friday 10th May - Two Dragons in One Night

Well what a story this turned out to be.  After the misfortune of having the trigger killed on us last time we decided once more to head back to Timorous Deep for Priwinn.

A fantastic turn out from the guild and a handful of friends from Dreams of Darkness saw us stay as far away from the trigger as possible and get buffed up before going in for the kill.  It was a smooth fight and although clerics were OOM at the end and a handful of corpses Faydedar was lying in the sand.  The picture on the left was taken at this point and then Priwinn looted up, but then it all went horribly wrong .... the zone popped and we were all thrown out of the game only to come back and find that Priwinn no longer had the water in her possession, but that the trigger was up again.

A petition was sent in immediately and after much 'bitching' we decided to make the most of the trigger being up again as Tendarya is also at this point in her epic.  Still no word from a GM we started the buffing cycle again, almost finished the Lead GM turned up.  Although we had numerous log files, it was shouted 'in zone' and various screenshots this wasn't enough for her to award Priwinn with the missing bit of her epic.  We had to take the dragon down again.

NB:  For all our friends that read the website we learnt a valuable lesson here.  /report anything of importance like an epic kill immediately after it happens.  We will not be making this mistake again and most of us now have /report hotkeyed

Wednesday 9th January, 2002 - New boots and a key

 

Well after almost 20 hours Jennya and Zeksie were finally in possession of 'Ring of the Ancients'.  A quick trip to West Commanlands to take down some Shadow Men for the Shadowed Rapier, pick up 3250 gp at the bank, then off to Rathe Mountains with Grabatz and Yinadriel in tow.  We weren't the only ones after Hasten, Bennyboy a monk from Blood Cult needed to kill him for his epic so after bringing him over to us to trade in the bits for our boots we assisted him in doing this.  Jennya and Zeksie are now the proud owners of a pair of Journeyman Boots.

 

It was quickly discovered that our new warrior Badiek was not in possession of the Sebilis key, so a group set out to get this rectified.  On reaching Trakanon's Teeth we found that a Doom Skeleton was blocking up the spawn table so re-enforcements were called for.  With the help of Oana the Blood Cult warrior we took him down without too much trouble and after recovering Paaw's corpse a couple of times from the depths of Trakanon the medallions were soon in Badiek's possession.
 

Tuesday 8th January, 2002 - A step nearer our epics

 

What was becoming a long boring Ancient Cyclops camp in Ocean of Tears turned out to be quite eventful.  Sitting and watching the time slip by as Jennya, Zeksie and Paaw killed Aqua Goblins, Ogres and Cyclops in the vain hope that the Ancient would spawn, Paaw announced that the Tainted Cyclops was on track so off we went.  We managed to knock down the Tainted without too much trouble, but then the Corrupted spawned immediately from it's corpse and this proved a little more difficult.  It was down to almost one bubble of health when Jennya ran out of mana, Paaw was having problems casting and soon he and Zeksie had become corpses.  In a rush of panic at losing the AC camp we hurried our way back to the island rezzing the fallen and resumed camp once more.  The Corrupted headed back over to the island and this time we were more successful in bringing him down and Paaw is now one step closer to his epic.

 

Fendel and Trepidation were invited to join Blood Cult on a raid to the City of Mist and within no time at all Lord Ghiosk had been brought down and the three books are now in Trepidation's possession giving us another guild member one step closer to his epic.

 

CONGRATULATIONS guys on a job well done
