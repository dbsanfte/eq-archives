Aeriel

Appearance:

   Aeriel is a slight girl, typical of her race, with long white hair to the middle of her back, braided, and very large blue eyes.  She has beautiful white wings and a delicate figure, being small-breasted and lithe.  She prefers a light blue toga and high-laced sandals.  She wears one of her own feathers on a chain about her neck, the holy symbol of Aerdrie Faenya.

History:

   Aeriel was a gentle girl, in love with the winds of her birth, given to her Goddess and blessed with magic.  She loves to sing and loves more to soar on the high breezes and drafts of her home in far away High Aerie.

   Her life dramatically changed one night.  While flying alone, she felt a sharp pain in her right wing.  Falling to the ground, she was struck hard upon the head.  For many days and nights she drifted in and out of consciousness, feeling herself on a boat, then on land, sick and drugged.  She felt the darkness of a close space, some panic, but was never awake enough to know what was happening- only knowing enough to be afraid.

   She awoke in a small cage, her wing splinted, her muscles and head aching.  It was a mage that had captured her, an evil man with dark hair and a small black moustache, calling himself Ronarre.  He laughed, saying he had both feathers and blood for spells and experiments.  The young Avariel felt only terror.

   She spent the night in shaking horror, both from her fear and from the claustrophobia of her prison.  A young servant came to feed her and in desperation she charmed him, the only spell left in her.  He let her out in response to her pleadings and she ran as fast as she could, her wings unable to lift her, carrying her few possession with her and leaving a confused young man behind her.

   At daybreak, a wandering monk found her, bleeding and crying along a road.  Will the dark-haired mage find her?  Will she ever find her way home?  She does not know…
