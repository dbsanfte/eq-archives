Dungeon Master:

The chitine captain watches as you all sheath your weapons, and smiles (if that’s what you could call it!) as Karg looks confusedly at his club, before changing hands. As Varistan moves away from the longsword that he has left against the wall, with a few clicks from the captain, one of the guards steps forward and removes it, the web sliding from his skin as he brushes it.

 

With everyone ready, a few noises and clicks and the a few of the guards disappear off down the northern passageway, two stay behind to bring up the rear, but the captain leads you after the disappearing guards. As you round the corner you see three of the guards disappear into a doorway on the left, and one disappears into a doorway on the right. The captain leads you to the Northern end of the passage, where he stops and knocks on a door in the west wall.

 

There is a feint clicking sound from within and the door opens. Inside the room lying on a bunk made of spun webbing, Karg recognises Brother Jarrod. There is no movement from the Brother, his eyes are closed and his legs are bandaged up with makeshift bandages. The door was opened by a female chitine, wearing a dress of spun silk; she carries herself with an air of authority. She and the guard communicate in the noises and clicking sounds that you have heard them make between each other, before she turns to you to speak ….

 

“Welcome, Captain Vald here tells me that you have come in search of this man”, she gestures to Jarrod lying on the bunk. Her common is better than the captains, and you have no problem understanding her fluent speech. “He was attacked, by what we are not sure, but there are many strange creatures wandering here now”, her eyes fall to the floor as if ashamed before returning her gaze to the party and continuing, “We brought him here, and have treated him as best we can. He is currently sleeping, but does not yet have the strength to walk”

 

There is a long pause, before she continues, “I am glad you have come in search of him, and I will admit, that the reason we brought him here was through selfishness on our part, but we need help”, her gaze passes over each of you, as if in search of something. “Twelve years ago we escaped from out masters into the caverns below, and made our home there. Our leader, his name is Caullum, recently gained access into the lower levels of our sanctuary, and has disturbed some evil within, strange creatures from bygone days have begun to move in the dungeon corridors. I have led this small group as far from Caullum as possible before his machinations bring death for all who escaped”, she pauses, “Please, I would beg of you to put right what he has undone, and save our kind from the evil that has overcome this place?”

Karg:

Karg pushes forward and kneels down next to brother Jarrod, giving the words of the spider woman but a small part of his attention. Setting his hands with surprising delicacy on the wounded Jarrod, he makes a strangely penitent plea. "Shyeth hear plea of Karg. Follower of Shyeth lie hurt, lie in pain. Shyeth help follower." [Cure light wounds]

 

After tending the slumbering Jarrod, Karg stands and considers the plea of the spider woman. Then he looks to the others. "Karg take Jarrod home. Evil wait."

Micky:

Mickey steps to the side of the huge healer Karg, raising his hand up so he may rest it upon one massive shoulder. He looks down to the injured Jarrod, then to Karg. The cleric is determined to finish one quest before starting another, but do we have the time? It took half the week just to get here...

 

"Karg, I know how you feel, but here is yet Another group in need of our help, and I fear we have not the time to return to Dagger Rock..."

 

He turns, and unshoulders his pack. Pulling forth some rations and water he sets them beside the bed. He then looks to the Chitine and tries to control his revulsion. He knows what bugs eat, what have they been feeding the shepherd?

 

"I'm sure they can care for him a little longer while we take care of this small matter for them. I mean, it IS the right thing to do considering how well they've tended to our injured friend..."

 

Of course, Mickey is seeing the man after the prayers of the barbarian cleric, but before the slow thinking giant can say anything, he continues,

 

"Let us ask Jarrod if he would not mind a few more days when he awakens. We cannot leave so many to die to save just one man..."

Dolly:

"We were paid to get this guy back to the Monastery, so that’s what we should do. Plenty of time to go off searching for monsters after, if you feel like it. Anyway, I'm not sure I want to get involved in what sounds like a political struggle for power. It needs thinking about." Says Dolly sharply to Mickey, but watching the Healer thing carefully for a reaction.

Varistan:

"I'm with our fearsome girl here," says Varistan, giving her a wink and a grin.

 

"Let's not kid ourselves that we're here for any noble purpose. We're here 'cause we got payed and we're on the job. Now once we've gotten that out of the way, maybe then we can help these things with their little problem."

 

He turns his eye to the spokesperson for these strange creatures, continuing to address Mickey. "If they're willing to compensate us for our troubles, that is. And if we can get the whole story."

Rupert:

Rupert thinks for a moment. "Indeed, our mission is to return this poor fellow to his companions, and we should do that first," he volunteers. "However, these kind... er... creatures have kept him from harm and only ask us to assist in a matter beyond their control. Could we not therefore return the brother and come back ourselves to do this thing they do ask of us?"

Karg:

 Karg looks down at Rupert and nods. "Karg with Rupert. Spider clan tend Jarrod, Karg owe debt. Karg take Jarrod home, Karg then return, help spider clan."

Varistan:

Varistan bristles at Karg's acceptance of the creature's plea.

 

"Debt? These bugs are the reason why Jarrod's jus' this side of bein' dead meat in the first place. You ask me, Karg, they owe you for nearly gettin' your friend here killed and gettin' your ass dragged to this pit."

 

Realizing how angry he sounds, Varistan lowers his voice.

 

"Not that I'm averse to helpin' out those who need it, but it's gonna take a bit more than askin' polite."

Yanish:

Remaining silent and as close to the wall as possible, Yanish listens to the group fighting amongst themselves once again. Yanish's hand slips into her and her fingers wrap around the dragon bone daggers hilt. If the bug lady doesn't like being put off, Yanish wants to be ready.

Karg:

"Karg say they help Jarrod, Karg try help them." Almost of it's own volition, the club moves itself from his left back to his right hand. "Varistan scared, Varistan not come."

Varistan:

Varistan's eyes narrow at Karg's insult, and he strides towards the large man until he's standing with the barbarian toe-to-toe, looking up into the taller man's eyes.

 

"It's nothin' to do with bein' scared. I look scared to you? It's about usin' that brain o' yours that I know is rattlin' around in your thick skull somewhere."

 

Varistan's face suddenly breaks into a toothy grin, and he barks one of his curt laughs as he firmly but gently smacks Karg's shoulder in a friendly gesture. "We're all on the same side here, big man, remember?"

 

And then, just as suddenly, he's serious again, turning away from Karg toward the female chitine. "So what's the story, bug? Let's hear every dirty detail."

Dolly:

Dolly looks across at Yanish and raises her eyebrows slightly at the macho blustering. "Boys Boys" she calls "Time for that later". Then smiles as Varistan backs down

Micky:

There are questions forming, and good points being made. We have a contract, and it must be fulfilled. Perhaps we can find a nearby shelter above ground to place Jarrod before we return to the darkness of the underworld.

 

"You spoke of hiding from more than one group. Not just the monsters that now prowl your home, but from your own people as well? Who is Caullum, and does he know of your hiding place here?"

 

Mickey studies the bugs carefully, trying to resolve whom the real Villain is in this whole scenario. Perhaps the monsters prowling aren't half as dangerous as the bug-man(?) who released them...

Karg:

For a moment, things are tense between Karg and Varistan. Varistan breaks the tenseness with a friendly slap on Karg's shoulder. It's obvious the big priest isn't used to such familiarity, but he nods. It takes a moment, but he manages to relax his grip on the club and let the hardness melt from his eyes.

Lindornea'en:

Lin sighs at the whole display. The elf takes it upon himself to gain everyone's attention by coughing loudly... well, as loud as needed. "Here's an Idea... for those just engaging their brains. One or two of us can take the 'Brother' back, and the rest shall fix the problem that the..." he fishes around for a reasonable word, "people here have."

 

He leans his back against the wall, "As such... It would probably be best if myself, and another took him back. I have my doubts that any of you can move as fast as I over the countryside." He cracks a slight grin...

Dolly:

 "And Brother Jarrod certainly can't." Comments Dolly exposing the huge flaw in Lin's suggestion.
