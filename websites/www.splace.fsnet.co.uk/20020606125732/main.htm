Guild News & Information :

     

Wednesday 5th June - Epic Night

Our first epic battle of the evening saw us gather in the Steamfont Mountains for a meeting with Renux.  After tracking down her trigger and following him on his walkabout we soon spawned the evil woman and proceeded to send her to her death.

From Steamfont we headed over to City of Mist for a meeting with the Wraith of Jaxion.  Climbing all the way up to the first of the sky rooms we got overwhelmed and only a couple of us made it out alive.  With 45 minutes till the server went down CR was hurried, but we lost a corpse.  Not to be beaten we all met up when the server was back up only to discover that the Jaxion was actually on track too.  So with just 8 of us we fought our way back up, taking it easy this time.  Wraith of Jaxion was charmed and Kwanii and Azurite got their epic pieces.

CONGRATULATIONS Fendel, Kwanii and Azurite

     
     
Tuesday 4th June - Tangrin

Azurite is working flat out on his epic now and running around like a headless chicken.  After taking some advice from Kwanii he created a mule to check on the Tangrin spawn only to find the ape was up.  Early hours of the morning for me but refusing to miss out on an ape slaying opportunity, the guild roster was pretty light but we gathered them up and headed over to the Field of Bone.  Lacking some serious melee power our first attempt was unsuccessful.  We picked up the pieces and refused to be beaten so Az called in some friends and we went for him again.  He'd won once and certainly wasn't doing so again and it wasn't long before Tangrin was face down on the sand.

CONGRATULATIONS Azurite that's another tick on the list :0)

     
     
Tuesday 4th June - Plane of Hate

We jumped up to Hate again today and will be doing so once more before we head up to Fear on Saturday.  Our reasons for these short little trips are to get our teamwork perfect.  It is going so well that everyone is having loads of fun and we certainly don't sit around getting bored.  The loot we pick up on the way is a bonus and today what a great bonus it was .....

 Saturday 1st June - Plane of Hate

After voting as to where we play today, Plane of Hate was the clear winner, so rallying at wizard spires in South Ro we made our way up there.  What an absolutely awesome start to the month.

Break in wasn't as clean as we could have hoped but we got all clerics to safety and camped to it could have been worse.  With three fantastic chanters on board we went for the quick clearing technique and pulled several mobs at once.  We moved from safe spot into throne room then wandered around the second floor.  Took a couple of mini bosses out on the way including High Priest M'Kari and the Evangelist of Hate, and we got some absolutely awesome phat lewt ....
