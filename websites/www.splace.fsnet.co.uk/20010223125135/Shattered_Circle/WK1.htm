Dungeon Master:

You each find yourself doing your own thing when a poster catches your eye ...

 

 

Your help is required

 

 

We seek a group of adventurers to assist in finding a missing brother. 

 

For further details please meet me in

 

The Dagger Rock Tavern, Dagger Rock

 

on the 5th day of Yuven at the 20th hour

 

 

 

signed:  Abbot Jengor

 

 

Making your own ways to Dagger Rock, you each find the Tavern in question and ordering yourself a drink at the bar, then take a seat, to see what happens ...

 

You find yourself in a large, comfortable tavern area. There are tables and chairs everywhere, with a large bar area and fireplace to the west. A large gentleman behind the bar who introduces himself as Yuri Kinneron serves you. 

 

You start to get a little unsettled as the 20th hour passes, but 20 minutes later the door slams open and a small, chubby out of breath man walks in dressed in a yellow robe. Behind him follows a tall deeply browned man, wearing a hide of bearskin, although on closer inspection he is wearing civilised clothing underneath. In his hand he carries a large club, and his feet are donned with furred boots. 

 

With an apologetic grin to the room, the yellow robed man hurries to the bar, as the tall man closes the door before joining him. The yellow robed man leans over the bar in conversation with Yuri, and before long Yuri is banging a pitcher on the bar quietening the room. 

 

With everyone's attention the yellow robed man starts speaking, "I am sorry for my lateness, but thank you all for responding to our request for help", he stammers. "I am Abbot Jengor from the Kephian Monastery in the foothills to the East of here. I will get to the point, as I know you have been waiting for me, and for that I again apologise. We...", he briefly looks around at his companion, before turning once again to address the room, "we are missing one of our brothers.  He disappeared 8 days ago.  He was out shepherding the sheep, and has not returned.  The sheep have all been found and returned to the monastery, but still there is no sign of Jarrod.  We have searched the foothills ourselves, but no longer have the time to spare, to find him; this is where we would like to call on your help.  There is a reward of 50gp each if you can find our Brother and return him to us. Karg here...", he gestures to the man who entered with him, "... will travel with you. Please if you have anymore questions, I will do my best to answer them"

 

At this point Yuri places two jugs of ale on a spare table by the bar, and the Abbot takes a seat, before quickly standing again to add, "Oh, I am sorry ... we will also provide those people interested with a meal and bed here at the tavern tonight, so that you may set off at first light". Again he sits, and takes a swig of his ale, as mutterings start around the room.
