Micky Donatelli

 

Appearance:

     Blonde hair, fair skinned, standing only 5'4" and weighing a light 130 lbs., Micky dresses as a professional, always clean, always in tailored clothing. He prefers the light blues and whites of his Mothers heritage, rather than the dark clothes normally associated with his profession.

History:

     Micky was born into a loving family, yet, his Mother scorned by her own parents for marrying a 'human' shipping clerk, so Micky never knew much of his Elfin heritage. His mother taught him what she remembered, but he never met her side of the family.

     As a teenager, he grew tired of the monthly burglaries on his Fathers warehouse, and took it upon himself to upgrade the security, including some new lock designs, and innovative but simple traps.

     Losing the cash flow once gained from the warehouse upset the local Thieves Guild, and they took quick notice of Micky Donatelli, approaching him soon afterwards with an opportunity to train and expand his skills.  The naive young half-elf readily accepted...

     Some months into training, he realized the true nature and profession of his teachers’, they were the same men who had stolen from his family only months before. Enraged, Micky quit the 'school' and went home... Later that same night, his parents were both killed in a horrible warehouse fire that destroyed Micky’s inheritance. By the time the smoke had cleared, it had become apparent that the Guild was now his Familia…
