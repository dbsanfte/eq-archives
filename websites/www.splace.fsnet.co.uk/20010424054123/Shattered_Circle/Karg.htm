Karg

 

Appearance:

 

     Karg is a tall man who keeps much of the look of his barbarian past.  His people were the Gralk tribe of the bear clan.  His hide armor is of bear skin, a dark brown.  Underneath, he gives some sign of a more civilized society in that his plain trousers and tunic are of brown wool instead of hides.  His boots are furred and tied in place with leather straps.

     Ever present at hand is a massive club (two handed) and slung over one shoulder is a sling with five javelins in it.  His long hair is brown and is tied back into a pony tail.  His eyes are so dark a brown that they appear nearly black.  His deeply tanned arms show many scars from his youth.


History:

 

     Born to the Gralk tribes, Karg was raised to be a warrior of the clan.  He was taught to stalk the great bear for his own armor, the use of the small spears (javelins) in use by his people, and the war club.  He proved a hale lad and provided well for his people.

     But Karg grew tired of the relentless warring of his people and decided to travel abroad.  He was jumped by a small band from the clan of the elk and while he fought them off, he was wounded grievously.  Making his way further, he came across a small wooden keep in the wilderness, the monastery of the Kephian order.  While his body mended, he became aware of Shyeth and felt an instant kindred with the fabled Kephan, being a stranger among his own.  And so he decided to remain, to repay the kindness of the monastery with his talents and to repay Shyeth with his service.
