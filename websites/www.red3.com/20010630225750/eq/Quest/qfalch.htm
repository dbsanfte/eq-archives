The Falchion Quest

NB You need to gain enough faction with Lord Jyleel to start the quest. Do this by killing Orc Oracles in CrushBone and giving Illegable Scrolls to him or by giving the Cleric Guild Bone Chips.

1. Talk to the Paladin Lord, Lord Jyleel, in the basement of the Paladin guild in Felwithe.

2. Kill the Orc Runner that runs from the entrance to Greater Faydark in Butcher Block Mountains along the path towards the crossroads. (you must be at least level 8 to solo). A trick to getting him at a higher level is to sit down on the path near the two buildings and he will attack you. Some times he has an assistant and also may not always drop the pouch.

3. Loot for the sealed letter pouch and keep hunting until you get the letter from Dvinn and give it to Jyleel or get really good faction with the clerics and paladins by doing the bone chip or illegible scroll quest.

4. say to General Jyleel "how do I become a Honored member of the court?".

5. General Jyleel will request three items -

  • The Black heart of Dvinn.
  • The head of the Crushbone Prophet.
  • A Supply crate.

6. Head to Crushbone - camp the Tower. Kill Dvinn and get the heart.

7. Kill a prophet in same place to get Head, Book and note.

8. Head to OoT - Find the Sirens Rock on the right from ButcherBlock. Find the sirens without aquagoblins. As dawn breaks the Alluring Sirens will disappear.

9. Kill Brawn the Ogre, near the center of this rock. Brawn drops the Crate.

10. Take the items back to General Jyleel in Felwithe. Place all 3 items in the trade window, and hit trade.

11. receive the Falchion.
