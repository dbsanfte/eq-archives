Holy Partisan of Underfoot

1. Head to the butcherblock and "Hail Dru Razbine".

2. He tells you his story about how he was sent by Datur Nightseer to carry the Chalice of Conquest to a noble band of Paladins in the Mountains of Rathe.

3. After his tale ended he hands you a container with two slots. He tells you that once you find the Chalice of Conquest and the Candle of Bravery, combine them, and return the case to Lord Nightseer.

4. Go to Kaladim and speak with Priestess Ghalea of the Church of Underfoot in the cleric guild inside Kaladim.

5. Head to Lesser Faydark and kill Fairies, and pick up four portions of Fairy dusts and give the dust to Ghalea. In return she gives you some blessed Soil of Underfoot.

6. Head to the Rathe Mountains in Southern Antonica.

7. Go to the Paladin camp in Rathe and find Nicholas, the dwarven Paladin, and ask him about the Chalice of Conquest.

8. Head to Lake Rathetear to the Orc Camp. Kill orcs until you get a shovel and a bracelet with Kunga's name on it.

9. Head back to the Rathe Mountains to the Unkempt Preservers. Kill druids until you hear a druid screaming "Long live the green!!!". This is the one you want to kill.

10. Loot the Base of the Candlestick from the body.

11. Head to Halas passing through Misty Thicket picking up honeycomb for no apparent reason :)

12. Find Dok in Halas in a building aptly named "Dok's Cigars" and ask him about the. "Candle of Bravery".

13. Give Dok the pieces of the candle, the honeycomb and a pouch of Soil of Underfoot.

14. Receive the Candlestick. You are half way there.

15. Head to Dragnors Cauldron on Faydwer (Yes you heard me) and head around to the Orc Camp and kill and maim and vanquish Orcs until one drops A bloodstained note:

"Dis is a notis of the death of Klunga~ He tryd to reedeam himsef by stealig a Dwarv cup~ When he drunk from cup at lake he died~ We drug his body back to Calldrin~ We burnd bodi in fire pit here~ We bury cup here~ We killd gnoyou castor here~ We tink he knows sumtink~ Den all is gud~"

16. Find Ghilanbiddle at the camp at the ButcherBlock side of the zone.

17. He can resurrect Captain Klunga, but he requires a few items to do this task. Give him:

  1. The bracelet klunga had owned.
  2. 100 gold coins.
  3. The bloodstained note.

18. Head back to the Orc Camp and find a skeleton by the name of Captain Klunga.

19. Give him the shovel.

20. Follow him making sure no one attacks him along the way ;)

21. A bag should appear at his feet and he will attack you. Kill him.

22. Receive the Chalice of Conquest!

23. make your way back to Kaladim. Visit Lord Datur Nightseer, the noble Paladin leader.

24. Place the Chalice into the container and combined the two items.

25. Handed the container to Lord Datur Nightseer who, in return, gave you a Cape of Underfoot for my troubles.

26. Head back to the port and Dru Razbine himself. Give him the cloak.

27. Receive the Holy Partisan of the Underfoots.
