The Bloodforge Quest

1. Hail Byzar Bloodforge in the warrior guild in Kaladim.

2. He seeks the heads of the ogres Zarchoomi and Corflunk.

3. Ask Byzar "What about Corflunk?" or "What about Zarchoomi?".

4. Hunt for them in ButcherBlock.

5. Return both heads to Byzar and receive experience, faction, some coins and various pieces of bloodforge mail.

6. He tells you about Trumpy Irontoe and how he wants him dead. (Yes this is the same Trumpy as the one in the Tutorial. Sad huh?)

7. Trumpy hangs out at the Fish's Ale in Qeynos.

8. Travel to Qeynos by teleport or SoW and Hail Trumpy.

9. Ask him about Scagg he then gets all huffy and walks out.

10. Chase after him and then root/attack him when away from the bar soes not to start a bar room brawl with Barstomper. Also stay clear of the Corrupt Guards.

11. Loot Trumpy's head.

12. Go back to Kaladim and just in case hail Byzar and ask about the beads. If he replies that your shifty eyes tell him you are not good. Go do get better faction with him.

13. Give him the head.

14. Receive the Bloodforge Hammer.
