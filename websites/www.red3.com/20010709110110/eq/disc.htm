Cleric Concentrations

Facts:
1. Obtained at level 30 for clerics.
2. You must put a point into a concentration to start it.
3. Whichever concentration first exceeds 50 becomes the primary specialisation.
4. will reduce the mana used on the spell.
5. will reduce the chance of fizzling.
6. will not increase chance of recovering after interruption.
7. Max skill limit on specialisation is 5 + (5 x Level), with a maximum of 200.
8. You complete a Solusek Ro Quest to reset the points you have in a current concentration..


Alteration Heals, cures, roots, and resurrestion.
Evocation Direct Damage.
Alteration is the concentration choosen by most Clerics for obvious reasons. The most common practice for a Cleric is to put a point into the Concentration Alteration and cast Minor Healing over and over until you have reached 51 in the skill. Then put a point into Evocation and set out into the world safely knowing that you have set your Concentration.
