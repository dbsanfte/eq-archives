How abilities effect skills
STR
 Determines how much you can carry; influences maximum and average damage; influences how quickly you learn many offensive skills
WIS
 Determines the amount of mana the priest classes have also affects how quickly you can learn many skills if your wisdom is higher than INT.
DEX
 Determines how quickly you learn weapon skills; how often weapon will proc; how hard you hit with bows.
STA
 Determines how many hit points you have, how long you can hold your breath
INT
 Determines how quickly you can learn most skills.
CHA
 Determines the amount you will be paid for goods by NPC merchants, and how much they will pay you; affects the saving throw on certain bard and enchanter spells (charms in particular)
AGI
 Determines how quickly you can learn some defensive skills, how difficult it is to hit you as well as how much damage you take when you are hit.
