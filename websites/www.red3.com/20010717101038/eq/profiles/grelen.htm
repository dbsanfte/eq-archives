Why a Paladin/Cleric?

I have always enjoyed playing the paladin class, ever since I started playing AD&D as a kid. The noble and honorable holy warrior taking his Faith into battle against Evil. (More of a fantasy-version of myself, actually.) I have ALWAYS despised the concept of Undead; consequently, my characters ALWAYS are Undead-hunters. (Guess I lack the appropriate psyche to play Shadow Knights and Necromancers.) Though I dislike the lost of hard-fought experience, Grelen has given his life in the defense of others of quite a few occasions, including several members of the White Cross. He was honored to do so and will again, should the need arise.
