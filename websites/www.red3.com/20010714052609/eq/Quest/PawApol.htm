The Paw of Apolla (Cleric Only)

This quest starts in the Fish Ale pub in South Qeynos. Ask Micc's about the Paw of Opolla to start the quest.

To receive the mighty paw "simply" attain these items:

Severed Gnoll Paw
 Dropped by Vin Moltor in Qeynos sewers
Platinum Ring
 Dropped by a mummy in Befallen, Gynok Moltor.
Copper, Gold and Silver Ring
 High Priestess Mitty in Kerra Isle drops them
Silver Ring
 May also be attained in Blackburrow at bottom of lake.

Make sure all rings are LORE and that they give a +1 and -1 adjustment to your abilities.

1. Put the four rings in the paw and hit combine.

2. Receive the the Paw of Apolla.

Type: Shield
AC 9
Weight 0.2
Magic Yes
Effects +9 WIS, +9 AGI, +5 STA, +5 MANA -5 CHA, -5 Sv. DIS.

NB You can only carry one at a time. They are LORE.
