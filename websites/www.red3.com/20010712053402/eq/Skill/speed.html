SpeedSmithing (For Paladins)

So you want to become a mastersmith as fast as possible? Look no further. In order to Speedsmith to master level you will need around 300-400pp (depending on luck, int and cha) and you need to be in North Freeport.

1) Visit the trainer behind the Hall of Truth and pump 20 points into smithing.

2) Start making steel bonings by placing the items into the forge as shown and clicking COMBINE. Repeat until trivial.

+ + =

      water   +     file      +      ore     =     steel      
                                                         boning

3) Start making sheets of metal by placing the items into the forge as shown and clicking COMBINE. Repeat until trivial.


+ + =

      water   +     ore      +      ore     =    metal       
                                                          sheet

4) Start making gorgets by placing the items into the forge as shown and clicking COMBINE. Repeat until trivial.


+ + =

water    +    mold    +     ore     =   Gorget

Around 350pp later you are a master smith even with an average INT.
