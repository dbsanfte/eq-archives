BASIC BLACKSMITHING

"Basic Blacksmithing Book":

Blacksmithing is the art of creating tools from primal elements of the earth - Ore, Water, and Fire. Talented blacksmiths can create weapons, armor, and other useful items. Before you undertake your work as a smith, you will need a forge. Forges can be found in most cites. Place you raw materials upon the forge. Begin your work.

Conversion rates:
Two pieces of Small ore = Metal Bits
Three pieces of Small bricks of ore = Large Brick of ore
Three large bricks or ore = Block of ore

You will need a flask of water to cool the ore."

To make all the items in the basic blacksmithing book, follow the same procedure as the above items. Collect the needed components and "Combine" them at a forges located in all towns.
