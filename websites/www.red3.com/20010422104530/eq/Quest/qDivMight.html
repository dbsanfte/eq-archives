The Darak Lightforge Bones Quest (Divine Might Spell)

Obtain this holy spell through this great quest. For roleplaying reasons you might want to start from Step 1, but if you are in a hurry to get the Divine Might Spell you can start at Step 9.

1. Head to Gorge of King Xorbb (Beholder's Maze) and find Diggs Duggin who runs around the maze. His standing /loc is 1250, -550 so check there first.

2. Hail him and repeat "What boon?".

3. Go to the Kaladim bank vendor and buy a pick and two Dwarven ales.

4. Return to Diggs Duggin and give all three items UNSTACKED.


The Quest Giver

5. Receive the Dusty Old Leg Bone.

6. Head to North Freeport and the Hall of Truth and hail Valeron Dushire.

7. Reply "What hero?"

8. Reply "I accept" and head out to find the bones.

9. Now the quest truely begins... You need to retrieve 8 bones and a coffin they are as follows (they are listed in this order to reduce travel time):

a) Dusty Skull - The Minotaur caves in the NE corner of Steamfont in Meldrith's room.


The Minotaur Caves

Meldrith will most definitely attack you and should be pretty easy to kill off, but still quite annoying nevertheless.


The location in Meldrith's room and The Skull.

b) Dusty Ribcage - The Garden in Castle Mistmoore Castle. (Remember this is Mistmoore and it can be very unforgiving... even for a 49th level Paladin). Go through the "Valley" and take the Left opposite the Graveyard and head down the tunnel. Head up the ramp on your left and head into MM. Take the right within the Castle and head under the small bridge structure and take another right and enter the door on your left. Take a right into the garden and another right into the fountain area.


The location in the Garden and The ribcage.

c) Dusty Backbone - Ghost Room Pit in Unrest.


The location in the Pit.

d) Dusty fingerbone - Inside Phin's room in Kedge Keep. Invisible (the mobs are nasty) and with Enduring Breath or a Fishbone Earing (you will drown otherwise) swim off of the ledge in Phin's room and down through the black star on the floor. Swim down and to the left and you should see a rock in a small alcove or a branch jutting out of the wall. (Exact Location is neg 147, pos 145, neg 251). The rock is to the right of branch and the fingerbone is on top of the rock



The location in Kedge Keep (Ed. image brightened because it's really dark in there) and the Finger Bone

e) The Dusty Broken sword is found in the mines of Kaladim in the water (where you might also want to buy the pick and two dwarven ales from the bank for Diggs Duggin).



The position off of the walkway in the mines and The Sword.

f) The Dusty shield is found on a ledge in Erudin from the docks in the most SE corner.


g) The Dusty Jawbone is found in an underwater Ice Cave in Everfrost. Head to 1400, -4600 to the entrance of the Ice with an Enduring Breath on (You could try and swim in... but it might be close if you aren't a great swimmer).

Jump into the water and swim South following the wall on your right. Follow it West and then North up the other fork in the ice river until you find the Cave in the North Wall (Loc 3000, -3900) and swim through and up to dry land again. (I have heard rumour of a Necro down here so you might want to invis undead to stop pets from jumping you.. but I didn't see him/her). Take a right in the large room and keep taking rights heading towards the location of the Jawbone (Loc 4500, -5200).

 


h) Dusty Leg - You receive from talking to Diggs Duggin in Gorge of King Xorbb (Beholder's Maze). (Techinically the beginning of the Quest as I said this is in order of time efficiency)
