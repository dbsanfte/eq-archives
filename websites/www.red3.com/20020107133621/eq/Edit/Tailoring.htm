Tailoring guide to attain skill 158

By Jalinka Sandage Paladin of The White Cross

This guide teaches you how to get a Tailoring skill 158 with the least amount of resources as possible. Since Tailoring resources are so hard to get, a minimal of wasted resources is one of your utmost priorities.

Remember this, I only tailored for skill and haven’t sold much of my creations; I relied on adventure loot to fund my continuing skill.

Follow the numbers to skill 158.

  1. Silk Swatches (2 spider silks in a sewing kit) Trivial at 17 Or
  2. Silk Threads (2 spiderling silks in Sewing kit) Trivial at 15
  3. Patchwork Mask (Ruined Pelt + mask pattern) Trivial at 26
  4. Raw Silk Mask (Silk Swatch + mask pattern in a sewing kit) Trivial at 37
  5. Studded Leather Mask (Medium Quality pelt + 1 stud (smithing) and mask pattern in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 57
  6. Cured Silk Mask (Silk Swatch + Heady Kiola (brewing) + Mask pattern in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 82
  7. Tailored Quivers (High Quality cat pelt +quiver pattern in a sewing kit) Trivial at 115 and Or.
  8. Crystalline Silk Mask (Crystalline silk Swatch + Silver thread (bought in Thurgadin) + mask pattern in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 108 (The silk is found on spiders in Crystal Caverns.)
  9. Crystalline Mantle (Crystalline silk Swatch + silver thread + shoulder pattern in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 118
  10. Crystalline Sleeves (Two Crystalline silk Swatch + silver thread + sleeve pattern in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 124
  11. Crystalline Leggings (Three Crystalline silk Swatch + silver thread + sleeve patter in a large sewing kit) Trivial at 128 Or
  12. Wu’s Fighting Gorget (Silk Swatch + 4 heady Kiola + Vial of Viscous Mana (enchanter) + gorget pattern in a large sewing kit.) Trivial at 128
  13. Wu’s Fighting Cap (Silk Swatch + 4 heady Kiola + Vial of Viscous Mana (enchanter) + cap pattern in a large sewing kit.) Trivial at 142
  14. Wu’s Fighting Sleeves (Two Silk Swatch + 4 heady Kiola + Vial of Viscous Mana (enchanter) + sleeve pattern in a large sewing kit.) Trivial at 151
  15. Wu’s Fighting Tunic (Three Silk Swatch + 4 heady Kiola + Vial of Viscous Mana (enchanter) + tunic pattern in a deluxe sewing kit.) Trivial at 158

 

Locations for tailoring supplies:

Commons = Silks and Pelts

East Karana’s = Silks and Pelts

Upper Guk = Silks

Crystal Caverns = Crystalline Silks

Tailoring Supplies guide

Go to High Hold Pass or Freeport and brew the following to get the skill required to make Heady Kiola’s at almost every attempt.

  1. Short Beer (Barley + Hops + Malt + Cask in brew barrel) Trivial at 31
  2. Ale ( Barley + Malt + Flask of Water (Merchant bought only) + Cask in brew barrel) Trivial at 68

Heady Kiola’s (2 Kiola saps + Flask of Water + Bottle in brew barrel) Trivial 46

Go to Freeport and smith the following to be able to make studs.

  1. Sharpen rusty edge weapons with sharpening stone in a forge. Trivial at 22 and OR
  2. Metal bits (2 Pieces of Small ore and Flash of water in a forge) Trivial at 22
  3. Tool Box (Metal bits + Flask water in a forge) Trivial at 51

Studs (Three Metal bits + File + Water Flask in a forge) Trivial at 41

A Enchanter makes Vials of Viscous mana. (Pearl + Sealed Poison Vial + enchanter spell) There is no trivial level, the Enchanter just needs to be high enough level to cast the spell.

Good Luck!
