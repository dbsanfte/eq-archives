BANDED ARMOR

The Blacksmiths Guide to Arms And Armor

A detailed guide to the construction of arms and armor for the experienced blacksmith. Reference manual needed: The Guide to Blacksmithing.

Required: A Forge, Earth, Fire, Water, Skill

Size does matter (heh!):
Barbarians, Ogres and Trolls wear Large Armor
Barbarians, Elves, Erudite, Half- Elves, and Humans wear medium armor.
Dwarves Elves Gnomes, and Halfings wear small armor.

Each piece of Banded Armor can be sized can be sized to a small medium or large client.

The size of the armor s determined by the mold used. Each section of banded armor requires a sheet of metal and a mold appropriate to the section of the body. each also requires a flask of water to cool it."
