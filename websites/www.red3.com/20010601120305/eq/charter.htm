The White Cross of Norrath Charter


Norrath is a dark place full of evil.

We the followers of the White Cross
are here to spread the light of healing and justice.

He have no qualms in laying our hands
on the hurt and dying or healing the poisoned or diseased.

We fight the fight of right.
In the darkest mists we are there to heal and protect.

In a world of deceit. Trust few my friend.

Feonyx
founder of the White Cross.
