The Test of Truth

1 :  Hail Merkos Quentos in the Hall of Truth.

2. Say "I am a squire".

3. Say "what is the small task" and he gives note.

4. Give note to Altunic in far West of East Commonlands by Inn 3.

5. Squire Narl appears. Destroy him and collect his head.

6. Give head to Altunic.

7. Buy a Medium (not small or large) Cloth Shirt as well as a Spider Venom Sac (for later.) and give the shirt to him.

8. He gives you TOKEN OF GENEROSITY.

9. Go to Merkos give him the Spider Venom Sac and he gives the TOKEN OF BRAVERY.

10. Give both tokens back and quickly run into the sewer.

11. To the left is a guard. Kill him and he gives you the TOKEN OF TRUTH.

12. Give the token to Merko and you recieve your TESTIMONY and 3GP.
