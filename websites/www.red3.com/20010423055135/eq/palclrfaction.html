Factions of the Paladin and Cleric Guilds

Essential Paladin Factions:

These are just some ways to fix your factions, not a complete listing.

The Knights of Thunder (Qeynos Paladin)

  1. Hand in bone chips 2 at a time to Lashun Novashine who is found wandering in North Qeynos.
  2. Hand in 3 gold pieces to Lashun Novashine, who is found in wandering in North Qeynos.
  3. Talk to Captain Tilin in South Qeynos to start the Gnoll fang quest.  Handing in fangs gives you some faction adjustments with other groups and more importantly, you receive moonstones for each fang you hand in.  Hand in the moonstones to Mcneal Jocub in Fish’s Ale for Priests of Life faction adjustments. 
  4. Kill putrid skeletons in the northern-most undead camp in South Karana.

The Priests of Life (Qeynos Cleric)

  1. Hand in bone chips 2 at a time to Lashun Novashine, who is found in wandering in North Qeynos.
  2. Hand in 3 gold pieces to Lashun Novashine, who is found in wandering in North Qeynos.
  3. Talk to Captain Tilin in South Qeynos to start the Gnoll fang quest. 
  4. Handing in fangs gives you some faction adjustments with other groups and more importantly, you receive moonstones for each fang you hand in. 
  5. Hand in the moonstones to Mcneal Jocub in Fish’s Ale for Priests of Life faction adjustments. 
  6. Killing putrid skeletons in the undead camp in South Karana will also help raise faction.

The Priests of Marr (Freeport Cleric)

  1. You can kill Militia members in West Commons located near the tollbooth, or hand in milk to Mojax Hhikspin, located near the Inns and Lake in West Commons. 
  2. Kill the piranhas swimming next to the Hall of Marr in North Freeport.
  3. Kill orcs in East Commons. 
  4. Talk to Serna Tasknon located in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport and do the Potion of Marr quest. 
  5. Kill corrupt Freeport guards, and then hand in the damaged militia helms that come off of them to Theron in the Hall of Marr.

The Clerics of Tunare (Felwithe Cleric and Paladin)

  1. Hand in 4 bone chips at a time to Yeolarn Bronzeleaf located in the cleric guildhall. 
  2. Give muffins to Pandos Flintside on the 2nd floor of the Hogcaller’s Inn in West Freeport.  Muffins can be purchased at the unlabeled building right next to the monk guild.

The Deepwater Knights (Erudin)

  1. Kill the necromancer, Elial Brook in Toxxulia Forest.  He has a 6 minute spawn time, has less than 91 hitpoints, and is located near the Docks.

Miners Guild 249 (Kaladim Paladin)

  1. Hand in bone chips 4 at a time to Paladin Gunlok Jure in North Kaladim.

The Clerics of Underfoot (Kaladim Cleric)

  1. Hand in bone chips 4 at a time to Paladin Gunlok Jure in North Kaladim.

The Temple of Solusek Ro (Paladin and Cleric Quest)

  1. One way is to kill Shadowed Men, lots of them. 
  2. You can also give Ssynthi in Mistmoore Castle the flower in the burial chamber. 
  3. If you have money to spend, you can give money to an Erudite NPC named Rathamana in South Ro.  Give him 3 plats worth of coins each time for a faction adjustment, experience, and a low level prize – usually a spell.  Consider Rathamana, since he has the same factions as the NPCs in the temple.
