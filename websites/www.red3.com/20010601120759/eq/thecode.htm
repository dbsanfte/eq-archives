The Code of The White Cross


As a Follower of The White Cross
I voweth to follow ten codes that make up The Code:

1) I will assist any pleading brother of The White Cross with no question.
2) I will act with Honour, Valour and Truth at all times.
3) I will assist others with my sword if I deem it to be just.
4) I will heal others in need if I have the means to do so.
5) I will assist the lost and confused.
6) I will not lie, steal or beg from player or non-player alike.
7) I will stop injustice by any means possible.
8) I will not duel except when my honour hath been questioned.
9) I will portray the way of the Paladin to be humble, friendly, but deadly when needed.
10) I will walk the path of light and share it with others.
