Tailoring

HOW TO SEW:


1) Invest at least 10 practice points into your tailoring skill.
2) Purchase a small sewing kit.
3) Buy patterns and gather appropriate pelts for each size.
4) First right click on you sewing kit
5) Place with in all the necessary components for the armor. Don't stack components!
6) Click the "Combine" button at the bottom of the sewing kit window.

If you are successful then the armor you were trying to create will be put into your inventory.
Studs must be made see our blacksmithing section for that.
