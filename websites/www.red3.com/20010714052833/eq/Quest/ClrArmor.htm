This is quest starts in The Temple of Ro. Blaize the Radiant and Gavel the Temperant are the quest givings. You need to fetch and return the corresponding icon and ingot along with the bars of the specified type of platinum to get the item. The icons and ingots only spawn a few times each day.

Boots of the Reliant: (Blaize)
You need the ingot and icon of Reliant and 1 bar of enchanted platinum. Go to Kithicor Woods, in one of the burnt houses you will find a book (looks like a bag on the floor). The book will reveal that Turgan the Reliant lived here once, and his brother perished in the fire that burnt the house. It also states that one of the items is hidden in Kithicor. This is the Ingot of the Reliant. Look for it on one of the tree stumps in Kithicor Woods (it is among a group of stumps). It is at loc -70, 3275. You need to jump off a fallen tree to get onto the stump, or use levitate. The book also states that Turgan has moved to Lake Rathetear. There, he is situated off to the left of Gnoll Camp at 3626, 112.. Hailing him is useless, as well as asking him about the Icon of the Reliant (he will shout at you and tell you to go away). Give him the book and he will give you the Icon.
AC 11 and Disease +6.

Gauntlets of the Ardent: (Blaize)
You need the ingot and icon of Ardent and 1 bar of galvanized platinum. You need to kill Borrin Babbinsbort, a gnome necromancer on the third floor in Befallen (approx level 25), to get the ingot. The icon is found at the bottom of a river in Everfrost (loc 2996, -5711).
AC 12 and Cold +5.

Greaves of the Penitent: (Blaize) [AC 11, +5 INT, +5 vs. Poison].
You need the ingot and icon of Penitant and 1 bar of magnetized platinum. The ingot of Penitent is found in Upper Guk at 206, -484 in the right hand water pillar. The icon is held by an Erudite named Maugrim who resides in Kerra Ridge on the necromancer Island.
AC 13 and Poison +5.

Vambraces of the Fervent: (Blaize)
You need the ingot and icon of Fervent and 1 bar of vulcanized platinum. To get the ingot, you need to kill an aviak named Gull in the Ocean of Tears. For the icon, get a Tumpy Tonic (details here) and give it to Jovan who resides in a 2nd floor bedroom in the back of the Lumberyard Inn in Highpass Hold.
AC 11 and Fire +5.

Bracers of the Reverent: (Gavel)
You need the ingot and icon of Reverent and 2 bars of enchanted platinum. Speak with a Gypsy named Lianna Ferasa in the Rathe Mts. Ask her what has become of Regis the Reverent and she will tell you that he was killed in the N Karanas by an evil man named Zahal the Vile. She says if you go kill Zahal the Vile, Regis' body will rise from the dead where he was killed along the NE tree line of the zone. She also asks that if he has her flute on his body to bring it to her. Zahal the Vile is a ghoul around level 30 or so who spawns in the NE part of NK. Use sense undead to find him. When you kill Zahal, Regis will appear at the tree line. He is a zombie. You need to kill him and loot his corpse to get the ingot and the flute. Take the flute back to Lianna in the Rathe Mts. and she will give you the Icon.
AC 9 and Wisdom +5.

Chestplate of the Constant: (Gavel)
You need the ingot and icon of Constant and 2 bars of galvanized platinum. The icon is found on an aqua goblin named Sludge in runnyeye. The ingot is now found on Dwingus in Dagnor's Cauldron.
AC 20 and Magic +5.

Shield of the Devout: (Gavel) [AC 9, +5 STR, +25 Mana, +25 Hit Points, (Looks like Shimmering Orb)].
You need the ingot and icon of Devout and 2 bars of vulcanized platinum. The Icon of Devout is looted from a Mino Sentry in the Mino caves in the Steamfont Mountains. The Ingot of Devout is found on a Glacial Bear in the caves under the river in Everfrost peaks.
AC 15, +5 Strength, +25 Mana and +25 HP.

Caduceus of Sacrament: (Gavel) [1H Blunt, 12 DMG, 32 DLY, WT 8.0, Magic item].
He needs the ingot and icon of Sacrament and 2 bars of magnetized platinum. The Icon of Sacrament is in MistMoore, in the back of the graveyard. There is a room through the illusionary wall that has 4 doors. Opening one of the doors (the only one you can) causes all 3 of the other doors to open. There are things behind the doors that are kinda ugly (mid blue to 34). In the center room (these rooms are no more than closets) on the floor is where the Icon spawns. It is on the floor and looks like a small doll until it is picked up. The Ingot of Sacrament spawns on a Giant Skeleton in the ruins in Rathe Mountains. This skeleton is a darker color than the ones that normally spawn at the ruins.
Damage 12. Delay 32. 1 Hand Blunt.
