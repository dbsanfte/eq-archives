The Staff of the Observers

At the observatory past the windmills in the Steamfont Mountains is a female lens crafter gnome named Brona Frugrin and she wants the retina of Borxx.

Kill Borxx, Level 30th Evil Eye-type monster in Runnyeye

Return the retina and recieve the Observer's Staff

L8 Enchanter spell "Sentinel" warns you of approaching hostiles which is very useful while meditating.
