The Initiation

Lord conducting the Iniation: Do you swear to upholdeth The Code?
Initiate: I swear.
Lord: Doth thou swear to Honour and Obey the Way of The White Cross?
Initiate: I swear.
Lord: Follow the Path of Light and be true young knight.
Initiate: I will.
Lord: Truth is Good.
Initiate: And so is Justice.

The Initiate then has his visage recorded and stored on the members page.

Revelry usually follows the event and the skill of Alcohol Tolerance is usually increased.
