The Screaming Mace Quest

This quest is recommended for level 14-20 groups or it can be "Soloed" at about 22.

1. Find the Dwarven smith located in the small building that is near the mine area. To get here from the entrance you must take a left and go across the bridge and follow the path.

2. Kill the jailer.

3. Talk to the middle dwarven smith

4. He requires three items:

Kill the Taskmaster that carry's some magic +2 AC earrings. Hand them into the smith to receive a blade mold which can be used for blacksmithing.

Kill the Trainer. He rarely carries a shiny brass shield. Loot at and give it to the smith in for a sludge mold.

Kill the Emperor/King. He rarely has a dwarven ringmail tunic. Loot at and give it to the smith. This time he will give you a dwarven mace which is DAM 8 DEL 35.

5. Talk to the priest in the next stall he can enchant this mace for you. She wants you to kill the Warlord.

6. Kill the Warlord. Loot the bracer of battle if you are lucky.

7. Give her the bracer alone first to the priest and receive a prayer cloth.

8. Give her the mace.

9. Give her the prayer cloth and voila you get one shiny screaming mace.
