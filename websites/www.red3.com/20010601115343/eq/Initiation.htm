INITIATION RULES

1) All applicants must be of 25th level unless it is an Alternate of an existing member and then the level minimum is 20.
2) All applicants must be of one of the following classes:

Bard
Cleric
Druid
Enchanter
Magician
Monk
Paladin
Ranger
Warrior
Wizard
Shaman

3) All applicants must be of one of the following races:

Barbarian
Dwarf
Erudite
Gnome
Half Elf
Halfling
High Elf
Human
Wood Elf

NB. Two exceptions to the rule are Dark Elf Clerics and Wood Elf Rogues (Scouts) which in order to be admitted must be guildinvited in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport. The initiate must not be invisible or be masked by any illusions. If the initiate is received well by the followers of Marr they will be received into the guild (if they have met all the other guidelines to membership as well).

Rogues Addendum:
a) They must from now on be called Scouts.
b) They are allowed to use all of their class abilities, but must only use them for Justice and "The Cause". (My thoughts are that I don't care how you kill a spawn of Innothule just as long as you kill it before it harms the path of Justice and her followers).
c) No pickpocketing unless agreed to by all members of a group.
d) They must be able to enter and be in good standing with the Hall of Truth and the Temple of Life.
e) They must regard themselves with the upmost of honour and truth at all times. Not just because they are members of The White Cross, but also because their background is questionable and others must see they share no side with their Rogue cousins.

4) The applicant must be "grouped with" / "shadowed" on no less that 2 nights before consideration.
