The Peacekeeper Staff

NB The peacekeeper staff is a staff useable only by erudite clerics and paladins.

1. Go to the clerics guild in erudin.

2. Hail the cleric guild leader. He will tell you about an NPC in Tox Forest.

3. The NPC is in the first hut to the right after you leave the city gates.

4. He gives you a staff and how to continue the quest.

5. Go to Qeynos give Rogar the Tall (upstairs from the cobbler in Qeynos) an Odus pearl.

6. He gives you treant resin. Now head to the bank in Erudin and talk to the banker on the far left.

7. Tell him you are in need of some vasty deep water. He wants a guild coin.

8. Return to the clerics guild and tell them that this NPC sent you to get a guild coin. You receive the coin.

9. Give it to the banker and receive the vasty deep water in return.

10. Combine the water and the resin in the staff and the end result will be a peacekeeper staff.

The peacekeeper is 5 Damage 28 Delay and you receive +10 CHA we donning this weapon.
