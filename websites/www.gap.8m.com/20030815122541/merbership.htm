Requirements to join:

1) You must read the Declaration, Objectives, and Rules and agree to them before applying.

2) To keep our family tight-knit, we have chosen to enforce a level requirement. You must be of level 46 or higher before you apply.

The requirement is merely to keep members hunting, questing and raiding together in the same areas. This allows them to get to know each other better, thus strengthening the guild. All of our officers are 40+. We want to be able to hunt in Planes of Power for experience. These levels make our objectives easier as well.

If though, you are of lower level and think this is just the place for you, and you have something to offer to the family, then wow us with an awesome app, and we will consider you. We look for awesome personalities.

If you are allowed in and are of lower level, then you will be expected not to beg for PLing, or equipment. You will receive these things much easier by just getting involved with members, and getting to know them. Your personality will get you ahead in the game, not your ability to beg lol.

If you wish to join GAP, please feel free to contact us in the game, or send us an email here Gods Among Peasants.
