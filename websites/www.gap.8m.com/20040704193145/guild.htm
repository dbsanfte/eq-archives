Gods Among Peasants
 


Although comprised of many new beginnings, Gods Among Peasants is among one of the oldest guild located on the povar server. Our guild Leader, Perragon Magnus has seen times of feast and times of famine, and still manages to push the Gods Among Peasants through the next battle and challenge.

Gods Among Peasants if a family guild that enjoys getting groups of members for hunting, raiding or just jumping on top of other members while they are foolishly walking around the arena during an event. We are a light hearted guild that allows all members to speak on equal terms with all in the guild.


As a guild we would never presume to incorporate a terribly strict code of ethics' on our members. We are a family and as a family we try to not impose restrictions or tell members how to act. However there is generally a set of base guidelines that are generally common etiquette.
1) Above all else, before any other rule, remember you are a God. As a God you are part of our family, no better and no worse than any other God. All Gods are equal, we have no hierarchy, we are a family, we are united and we are here to have a good time. Remember to respect your fellow gods and remember to always carry yourself in a manner befitting a God.

2) As a God, please do not forget those not as privileged as yourself and do not be so arrogant as to refuse to take 5 minutes out of your day to help a fellow player in need.

3) We do not discriminate by race, sex, class, religion or membership into other Guilds. Anyone may become a God and anyone will be welcomed the same.

4) When you are hunting remember you are a God, and as a God you have an obligation to fight with both honor and valor. Part of that is remaining fair, and we do not encourage kill-stealing, unfairly looting or leaving your party members behind. Remember, as a God the world watches you and your actions will inspire how the world reacts to us.

Congratulations to Willian Drudear for completing what will be the first in a long line of completed epics. If you need assistance completing you epic please contact our Raid Leader to organize a raid.
