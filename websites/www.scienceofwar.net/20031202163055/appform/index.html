Instructions
  • Register! You *must* register on the Science of War forums PRIOR to submitting this form. All of your work will be LOST if you do not do this. (If you have already registered, make sure that you're logged in.)

  • Read the entire application. Do NOT SKIP any of this application. Read it very carefully and complete it thoroughly.

  • You may want to complete some of this application in notepad or Word so that you don't lose your work when trying to post it! Once you've got it all together, then you can paste it into this form.

  • Incomplete applications won't be considered. If you can't fill this application properly (it's easier than a McDonald's application for third-shift fry cook, as verified by a genuine McDonald's third-shift fry cook!) then you'll probably fail to follow directions on a raid, too. Do it right or go find a guild short on raid discipline and long on corpse recovery practice.
Requirements

  • We're looking for people who know their class well, have a sense of humor, are good communicators, want more out of the game than just another disorganized and chaotic raid, and who have the patience to help us grow Science of War into a top guild serverwide. We're recruiting players who are on Stormhammer for the long term. Though you can apply to the guild at any time, to be accepted to the guild you must meet all of the requirements below:

  • Financial Stability: Poor people get lost. Seriously -- the worst possible excuse to leave the guild (and the server) is "I couldn't afford it no more." If your finances are so tight that another month of Everquest is going to mean that you can't afford rent or food for your kids you should not be playing. Once you're in SoW we expect you to stay in the guild for the long term and people who are living from paycheck to paycheck (or unemployment check to unemployment check) don't make very stable guild members.

  • Out-of-Game Balance: If you are not in control of your life you really shouldn't be playing this highly addictive game. We're looking for members who know how to keep an in-game/out-of-game balance so that relationships with spouses and loved ones never reach crisis levels and so that careers and educational efforts aren't ruined. Though we take our game-time seriously, but we also believe that balance is vitally important for long term mental, social and financial stability of all guild members.

  • Class Knowledge: You should be familiar with what all of your spells do and you should be up-to-date on current issues and discussions concerning your class.

  • Attitude: When people take things seriously they get emotional, but when things go bad that's no excuse to throw a tantrum or fit. We need people in the guild who can keep a sense of perspective and can see the positive, humorous side of even the nastiest wipe out. Not everything will be peaches and cream on every raid. This is part of the game and it makes victory that much sweeter. Keep it all in perspective!

  • Maturity: SoW guildchat can be explicit. You must be 17 years of age or older. You must be intelligent and able to hold a mature conversation.

  • Expansions: You must have all EQ expansions activated.

  • Navigation: You must have 200 sense heading and swimming skill and have all cartography maps installed. You can easily AFK your swim skill up to snuff, and sense heading can be bound to just about any hotkey you commonly use.

  • Independence: You must have your own means of shrinking and invising and running fast. There's no excuse for forcing an entire raid to start late because your invis faded and you died. Additionally, you must be able to develop your character on your own. You are expected to earn several AA per week at the least. Don't expect ready-made groups the moment you log in. Take the initiative to do what needs to be done to develop your character.

  • Click Toys: You must have at least one insta-cast clickable buff item that works indoors.

  • Guildstatus: You must be unguilded (this includes all of your scurvy alts). Don't try to play two sides of the fence at once. We want people who will make a commitment to the guild.

  • Levels: You must be level 65. You must have enough experience buffer in level 65 to be able to take two unrezzed deaths because sometimes despawn timers mean that we must regroup and attempt again before rezzing anyone.

  • AA: You must at least have your Archetype AA title including Run3. If your class can MGB, you must have this skill (rangers excepted).

  • Emperor Ring/VT Shards: Science of War semi-regularly raids the Vex Thal zone. Although not a requirement, it is recommended that Applicants finish this timesink to raid with us when we do these zones.

  • PoP Flags: Applicants are not expected to be fully Elemental Flagged. For exceptional applicants we are willing to flag you.

  • Play Time: You must be available to raid between the hours of 8pm ET and 12am ET at least four days per week. If you can't be online during this time, don't apply. We only raid five days per week max, so we need everyone present. We need reliable raiders.

  • Connection: You must have a stable Internet connection and a solid computer that allows you to play without lag in a raid environment. Candidates who frequently go LD or use "lag" as an excuse for substandard performance will be rejected swiftly.

  • Resist Gear: You should carry around enough resist gear to bring any single resist to over 150 unbuffed. Ideally, you need to be able to get any resist to over 200 unbuffed. Buy shaman potions if your resists really suck.
