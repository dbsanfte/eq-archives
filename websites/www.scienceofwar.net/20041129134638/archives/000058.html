April 10, 2002

Guild Formation, Recruiting, Newest members

  • Lots has happened in the last five days. The best news is that we'll be forming the guild this Thursday at 7pm EST in EQ Chat. The room name will be "scienceofwar" and the password will be "sowplz." If for some reason you can't get into the chat room, check this homepage for updates.
  • Our recruiting threads were a hit at the various message boards. Generally the first responses were flames from the local hotheads at each board, but then the more thoughtful people in various class communities chimed in. The rogue, warrior, and shaman communities win awards for having over-reactive moderators. Hopefully next time they'll be able to control their impulse to censor or shut down harmless threads. Here are some of the better threads (flames included!): shadowknight, mage, bard, enchanter, paladin and warrior.
