Slows Storywindow, Guild Progression Tracker, IRC Launched, and What We Ate for Dinner Last Night

Slows Storywindow: We've recently launched lots of great new content on the site. Katla, kitty shaman extraordinaire, has developed a guide to slowing that includes a simple download you can plug into your storywindow so that you've always got a list of slowable mobs at your fingertips. This should be a huge help to Shaman, Beastlords, Chanters, Necromancers and Bards everywhere. (Wow, a lot of bloody classes can slow.) You can see what this looks like in the first image below.

Guild Progression Tracker: Insipired by similar charts on other servers, Kenal created this chart that shows the progress of tier 8 guilds on the Stormhammer server. If you've got an update to make to the chart, send it to this thread.

IRC: You can chat with the Scientists of War in real time now! Isn't this Internet thing just crazy? Here's a newb's guide to IRC that includes our server information and setup information. It runs on Trillian too!
