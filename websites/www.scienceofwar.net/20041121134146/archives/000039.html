Month-End Wrap-Up

We've had a number of guild firsts in the past month: The Itraer Vius, Grieg Venificus, the GM-summoned Avatar of War and so on. We've also managed to climb two tier ranks during the same period. The future's bright for us as a guild and we're looking foward to a number of new guild-first achievements. It's been a helluva month for the guild, and a sweet, sweet series of victories for the anti-zerg forces of SoW. We prove that it's about quality over quantity every single day that we play. .
