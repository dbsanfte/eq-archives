20020730

Sleeper's Tomb Revamp
GMs held an event a few nights ago to "re-open" the Sleeper's Tomb zone. At the event a number of players received "artifacts" (one-of-a-kind GM-crafted items). Congrats to Ladred on his Tunic of the Disciple!

According to folks at the Stormhammer community forums the ST revamp is full of quests and new items. One poster wrote, "The revamped ST is predominantly a quest zone. NPC's have been re-itemized and now drop Velious armor gems (flawless diamonds, etc) as well as high end spells and, of course, Primal Weapons (now known as Prismatic Weapons). Old warder loot is not dropping from the new Dragons which have replaced them. A lot of the encounters seem to have been set up differently (for example: with the use of untargettable guard NPC's who activate as the bosses get lower in health). From early reports the many new quests in the zone are still being worked on but are nearing completion. One confirmed quest requires 10 different items to be collected within the zone for the reward. First appearances would indicate that this zone will be a hefty time-sink with the number of quests available. Despite this though the revamp does provide new content and playability to a zone which is currently under-played on servers who have awakened the Sleeper. It appears to be a good change to at least make Sleepers Tomb a fun zone both before and after waking the Sleeper."
