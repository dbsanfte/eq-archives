Science of War has gone thru a transition period changing leadership. Fuzzbal aka The Six Million Dollar Man and Talicat aka The Bionic Woman are now leading SoW and just like in the TV show, we are rebuilt, we have the technology. We have the capability to make the world's first bionic guild.... or at least a huge amount of upgrading even during our back flagging push!

Our requirements for most classes are about to make an upward move due to our large influx of applicants at this time, back flagging is slowing down and we are moving up the proverbial ladder knows as Planes of Power.
