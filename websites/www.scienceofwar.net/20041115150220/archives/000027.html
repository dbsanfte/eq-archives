March 20, 2003

Vova Arrested

The Internet police broke into Vova Di'Thule's Park Avenue apartment last night and arrested him for hacking into several websites dedicated to many popular guilds. In conjunction with animal control, the local zoo, and PETA, Vova was dragged out of the apartment and taken downtown for questioning. In a short interview with the preceeding officer on the scene we learned that when they entered the apartment they found the suspected lizard balled up in a corner whispering "gotta get shrunk, gotta watch Wapner" over and over to himself. One of the animal control supervisors was heard saying "It was the weirdest thing, we found a bathtub full of cat shit, and a cat with four burnt paws". Vova's neighbor reports that as they were dragging him out of the apartment she heard him scream "I don't wanna go to jail, I just want BANG, BANG, BANG."
