December 10, 2003

Coirnav, Wru?

Coirnav, The Avatar of Water, rests quietly in his grave. The Scientists of War took a force of 72 people down into the depths of the reef and, for the second time ever, faced the squidly beast with terrific results.

Script Messages:

[Tue Dec 09 23:54:42 2003] Coirnav the Avatar of Water shouts 'Those that violate my domain will pay. I call upon the power imbued to me by Povar! Come forth my minions of vapor and destroy these intruders.'
[Wed Dec 10 00:07:23 2003] The monstrous creature spasms in its last death throes sending shockwaves through the reef. Coirnav the Avatar of Water, empowered by the focus of the Triumvirate, has fallen at the hands of the brave adventurers deep within the reef.




The script absolutely flew by. This has to be one of the best encounters in my opinion. It's fast paced, intense, thinking on your feet action that simply does not come close to the other Elemental God snooze-fests.



Much celebration occured directly after the kill as we celebrated being the 3rd guild on the server to complete this encounter.

4 Hours a night, 5 Nights a week and 1 kill away from being PoTime flagged.

In other news:

We had a very fun month so far, we've been kicking it in the Elemental Planes, doing a touch of backflagging, giving PoEarth Bard Drums to twinks and make some old world content fun again with renewed tactics.



When the call to move to Vex Thal is made it is often followed by various shouts of an explicit nature. We decided to, in anticipation of the great Cat in the Hat movies release, turn Vex Thal into a wonderful raid that I would dare say everyone fully enjoyed.

Recruiting Status:

Recruiting is still open, and always will be, for those of you out there whom believe you have what it takes feel free to drop any member a tell in-game or fillout our online application form.
