PoTime or Bust

Upcoming in October: EP reflagging runs!

Recruiting will be open to all level 65's until the end of September, then in October we’ll be gearing up and getting everyone flagged for the Elemental Planes. Right now is an exciting time to be in Science of War as we're starting to head down the road of breaching the Plane of Time and taking down Elemental Gods more consistently. Visit our FAQs and check out the application form for more information.
