Verant Impliments Name-Change Service
As you probably know by now, Verant has done a 180 by implimenting a name-change service. For $50, regular server players can change their name, while it's going to be free to Stormhammer folks. Predictably, this had much of the EQ community up in arms. Here's a question-and-response that gets to the heart of the issue. Still, this is going to hurt Stormhammer. Why pay $50 for a name change when you can pay $40 and enjoy a month of Gold-server goodness, get a name change, learn to play your ebayed toon, and THEN go back to your old server? This is only going to increase Stormhammer churn and increase the number of undesirables that come to the server for illicit purposes. I feel for guilds that invite people based on level and gear alone -- this is going to bring a whole new slew of seedy players into their rosters.
