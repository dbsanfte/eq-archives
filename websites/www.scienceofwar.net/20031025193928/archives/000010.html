All Brown, All the Time

Thanks to the folks who weren't out pretending to get laid this Friday evening we cleaned up the Plane of Earth and buried the Arbitor of Earth in our first meeting with him. Despite our success at them, the rings of the Plane of Earth are poorly designed. It's simply not possible to down all four of them (Dust, Rock, Mud, Vine) plus Tantisala Jaggedtooth plus the Arbitor in a four-hour raid block. This means it's either designed as a six-hour raid zone (which is a ridiculous demand to place on 70 people) or it's meant to be done over the course of several days (like VT), in which case you've got a situation with guilds leapfrogging each other through a zone in competition for the end-spawn. In short, it's set up for unhealthy guild competition. Guilds that want to avoid this issue MUST force their people to raid the entire zone in one evening, which is stressful and not at all fun, especially given the buggy nature of the rings.

At any rate, we have completely conquered the Plane of Earth A zone and are now working on EarthB progression. (See the obligatory shot of EarthB below.) Once again, we're third on the server (After Township Rebellion and Leviathan) to enter EarthB. We're all looking forward to helping Warlord Gintolaken ding a few times on our way to the Rathe Council.
