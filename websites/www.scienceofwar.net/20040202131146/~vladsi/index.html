January 11, 2004
The Rathe: A distant memory
This evening the members of Science of War and it's applicants defeated the Rathe Council. Not only did we blow this event out of the water, we improved vastly on our last attempt.

If you recall, our last update said we ran but a minute long on killing the last of the Rathe Council members. Tonight :

Rathe Council member #1
[Sat Jan 10 23:30:40 2004] You gained raid experience!

Rathe Council member #12
[Sat Jan 10 23:33:07 2004] You gained raid experience!

We trimmed our time down from just above 8 minutes to under 2.5 minutes. Quite an accomplishment, congratulations on a job well done.

The Avatar of Earth died shortly after the last of the Council had died, giving up shiney new trinkets and flags for all.




This also marked a major milestone for the guild, as The Rathe was the last of the Elemental Gods that we had yet to kill.

Shortly after the kill we all feverishly searched our banks for the shields previously attained and met up in the Plane of Time.




Six Months ago Science of War entered the Elemental Planes, Not even One Year has passed since our first entry into Vex Thall. We're moving at a steady pace and not looking back. Congratulations to our members and applicants for always buckling down and giving it 110% night after night.

December 31, 2003
Plane of Time...Just a minute away.
Last evening the forces of Science of War converged on the battle grounds to Face the Rathe encounter for what was our 3rd attempt ever. All prior attempts had all been executed to perfection of what we knew of the encounter. Each night, after our tacticians went over the happenings of the night, we recalled what could have gone better and what we would change for our next attempt.

I'm proud to announce that after executing flawlessly yet again we've reached yet another milestone in this encounter.


Last night we were able to account for all 12 of the Rathe Councilmen and deal with them. Unfortunately we were 1 minute late in killing them all, and therefore the efforts went un-rewarded in monetary gain.

We learned a ton last evening, the event will fall to us in our next attempt. There is no doubt about it. When Science of War focuses, we achieve. We do not faulter from our goals, we presevere and move onwards.

The Rathe Council: but a minute away.

In other news!

Likely our last major back flagging run before Plane of Time will be commencing during the 2nd week of January. If you are looking for a guild on the Stormhammer Server, are an excellent player and enjoy focusing on strategy rather than overwhelming numbers, perhaps there is room for you in our ranks! Don't hesitate to let a member know, or click on the "Join SoW" link above.
