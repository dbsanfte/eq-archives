October 16, 2002

Guild First: Dozekar the Cursed

We've never had a shot at this hotly-contested dragon, and so we were suprised to find Dozekar alive and crabby upon arrival at the Halls of Testing in Veeshan's temple. After a false start in which Dozekar begged for and received help from one of his buddies, we gave it another shot, dropping him without littering his lair with even ONE of our corpses. Flawless victory! He dropped four tears for us, three of them banked until we've got a complete quest. Grats to Goldynstar on the White Dragon Statue. Because we ONLY allow members to bid on the final drop in any given Dozekar quest, our very first kill of him resulted in a completed quest. Our next kill should result in yet another completed quest. The quest-to-kill ratio will be kept high because of our loot rules as they pertain to this series of quests. Though we killed him without a single death by anyone on our 32-person strong raid force, it was by no means a perfect kill. Guild members should offer suggestions for improvement on the Dozekar post mortem.
