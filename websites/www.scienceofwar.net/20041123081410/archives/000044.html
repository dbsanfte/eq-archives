We gathered at the graveyard in the Plane of Nightmares and planned our assault upon this horror of a horseman. Plowing through the caves we came up some evil smurfs. Talicat found them adorable and decided to play with them. One of them broke her mez and ended up a dead smurf.

Azreal never had it so easy. Lucky we were able to yank this earring off of its cold and rotting corpse, and found this old parchment lying on the ground next to him...

Clearing these nasty horse creatures out to Goldy's dismay, we gather and assemble to take out the Big Horsy rider himself. After much planning, strategy, and cyber... we ATTACK!
And of course in a timely and SoW slaughtering fashion this big bad boy goes down!...
