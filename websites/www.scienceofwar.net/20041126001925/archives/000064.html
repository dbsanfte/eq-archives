Guild Member To-Do List

As far as updates go, not a particularly titillating one. For new and old members alike, here's a list of things you should be on top of ... (besides the guild hotties). Gives ya something to do during patch days anyways.
  1. Make sure that your entry on the guild roster table is up-to-date. If it's not, post a response to the guild roster so that those responsible can make changes.
  2. Make sure that you are keeping your Magelo profiles up-to-date. If you don't see your name linked to a Magelo profile in the guild roster then please post to the roster forum with your magelo links. At a minimum, please try to have a resist profile and an xp profile. For bonus points, you can make a wish list profile.
  3. Please make sure you read and give feedback on prospective guild members.
  4. Get your extra spells and Gems and Items to Shads for the guild bank. Please only do this if you feel completely comfortable with having a gnome oversee our assets.
  5. Make sure that all guilded toons that you control are meeting the level requirement calendar. If your alt can't keep up, then guildremove him and concentrate on your main (be sure to let the roster folks know of changes like this).
  6. Vote on No Obligation Day!
  7. Are you getting ready for the Temple of Veeshan HoT?
