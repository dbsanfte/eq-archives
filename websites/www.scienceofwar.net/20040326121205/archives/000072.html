November 15, 2003

Coirnav, Your time is about to come.

It’s been a busy month here at SoW Corp. We’ve been doing our normal thing, laying the smack down on most anything found alive in the Elemental Planes and have even been able to do an adequate job of back flagging.

After having killed Xegony multiple times and having made Fennin Ro our personal fluffer we have decided to begin the process of reconnaissance into the Plane of Water.

Coirnav, if you are out there; your days are numbered!

To ensure victory we have begun collecting all of the information that we can on this encounter and our trustworthy, yet sometimes shady and downright foul, rogues have come back with a preliminary report. It seems that while sneaking around the bowels of the sea beasts’ lair they have come across a wonderful oasis. While sipping gin and juice they began to collect data about the giant beast.

Curious why the preliminary reports had not been completed by these craft gnomish rogues we decided to send in a private investigator. Here is one of the pictures he returned with.
