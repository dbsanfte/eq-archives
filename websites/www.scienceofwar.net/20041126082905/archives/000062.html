Recruiting Goals Exceeded for the Month of May!

Science of War accepts twelve quality players per month by evaluating applications in the order that they are received. Today, one full week ahead of schedule, we invited our twelfth new member for the month, and so we must close the roster until June 01, when we will again begin to invite the very best players we can find. Congratulate the following folks if you run into them: Ackk, Alanya, Dhyna, Draygen, Graus, Kailyn, Mymic, Resolute, Rixalin, Talicat, Tergant, Xairnn

Candidates who have already filed applications should check their private messages on the Science of War Forums and potential applicants are encouraged to talk to guild members and submit an application as soon as possible. We expect June slots to fill up even more quickly as a number of strong applications have already been submitted for June.
