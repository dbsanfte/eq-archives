The Last Eight Hours

In the last two days we've raided 8 hours total. Here's a list...

Tuesday: (hour1, Tue) Warmed up with Shei. Did you know that vendor shei sells whore's bane? Neither did we, until Freeedom kept dying trying to loot his corpse. (hour2, Tue) Agnarr. Guild Shadowknights, Monks and Necromancers have perversely refused to complete the Agnarr key. Vova is sent up first to the slaughter yet again. (hour3, Tue) Behemoth. Raid is delayed five minutes while guild grammar nazis conduct a pop spelling quiz to ensure that nobody continues to call him the Magnetic Behemoth. DKP Fines are levied against Resolute, who claimed, "I'm confuse." (hour4, Tue) Tallon Zek. Cupid, draw back your bow and let your arrows flow straight to my lover's heart for me. The Bonbon club saves the day with quick thinking. We have got an incredible team of enchanters.

Wednesday: (hour1, Wed) Vallon Zek. Even easier than the first time, if that's possible. (hour2, Wed) Terris Thule. We're only still doing her because the bounce looks fantastic. It's like two puppies fighting under a blanket, mang. (hour3, Wed) Aerin`Dar. Thirty minutes after we zoned into Plane of Valor he was dead. During this time, Vova kept getting tells from a potential app who swore he'd be there in just a few more seconds. When the app finally zoned into PoV, most loot had been handled. Moral of the story? Wear sunscreen. (hour4, Wed) Grummus. Bertox needs to meet a few more of our people. We decided to make this fight harder than usual by placing him in the wrong spot and training the raid shortly before engaging him. He still died, despite our best efforts to flub the whole deal.
