Grieg Venificus

As we led our 5.5 band of merry freaks, cyber-sluts, manginas, and panhandlers through the maze of never ending greenies we finally came upon senor Greig. This battle was legendary and remarkable to say the least.

We were poised patient and buffed in the hall awaiting the pull of this blue floating maelstrom. Then he floats into the hall and the bodies started flying. After a few moments of cunning maneuvers, we finally get him into the proper position.

Pumping the melee with patch heal after patch heal, I glance up to the heal channel and see our last standing cleric announce that she is OOM! I spring into action and start pumping the tank fool with all the ghetto cheal I can muster.

Luckily I had a necro in my group who without regards to how trivial it is started pumping me full of mana. The rest of the patch healers take the initiative and start pumping out the heals on the melee while I ghetto cheal the tank back into rhythm. Our last standing cleric makes his rounds and gets the downed clerics back to their feet, where in which they mod and jump right back into gear laying down some serious healing.

This fool took a terribly long time to plow through, but in the end he died, like the wretched puke we all knew he was.

We came away with some fine spoils, and everyone came away with a sense of accomplishment and had an over-all fun time.
