Patch Message November 4, 2002 3:00 am

Ok, so we are a bit late on the patch information, but honestly, this patch was about as exciting as 2 day old oatmeal. Not much to pick apart here as it is more vague than the plot line of "Eyes wide shut". I guess this is good stuff, but they might as well have made the patch message: "Fixed some stuff" and saved some bandwidth.
------------------------------
- Monk fists as hand-to-hand weapons now scale to 15 damage/20 delay at level 65.
Well, sounds cool to me, but I am a shaman. Monks are still bitching a bit though.

- Corrected a bug with a few spells that were group or ranged but would only work on the caster.

- Corrected faction issues with some form changes (illusions).

- Fixed the Magic and Stealth manual quests in Plane of Knowledge.

- Changed how Force Shield, Barrier of Force and Manaskin interact with Beastlord mana regeneration spells.

There was no mention of slows that seem to be broken in this patch message. Hopefully this will be looked at for the next patch as it is a major problems on PoP raids since it is hard to tell when certain mobs have actually been slowed. Keep an eye on the Safehouse and the Crucible for more information. I will be sure to mention this (as will every other PoP shaman in the world) at Fan Faire.
On another note, Alan posted about the experience bug and what to do in order to fix it:

Please double check to make sure that you have the correct expansions listed when you log in.
If you do not, return to the login screen and try logging in again (and again check to make sure you have all appropriate expansions listed). Logging back into the server should restore your access to the expansions, but make certain to check.
