At the Reservation

[Thu Jan 30 - Sun Feb 02 2003]
Thursday: Having squashed the bugs in the previous night, the guild felt confident in their ability to overcome what new obstacles Mr. The Cursed had in store for us. We lay the smack down on Mr. The Cursed's friend Glyphed and Exiled then proceed to plow into him. Wow! What a challenging fight. Going into the battle with no previous knowledge of what to expect from this bugged event, we lost a couple of clerics and a few other key class members. It didn't take us long to get a grip on what was up. We got a handle on it, and quickly put this fellow in the grave. After owning Mr. The Cursed, we proceeded to take out Grieg once again. How many times do we have to own this fool before he stops taunting us? You can find the loot here, and the post mortem here.

Friday: Guess what? We did it all again, but much quicker this time. We saved so much time that we fit in a kill upon the High Priest also. Check out the loot we walked away with here, and the post mortem here.

Saturday: Our pall Aaryonar was up and waiting to get dead in the Temp of Veeshan. So we packed our bags, crossed the icy plains of western wastes, and piled into ToV. With only one shaman in tow, we ended up taking out not only Aaryonar, but also four other dragons. Some great loot dropped that you can check out here, discussed here.
