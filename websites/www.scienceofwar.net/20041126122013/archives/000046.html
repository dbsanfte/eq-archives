May 21, 2002

Lodi Dodi, We Likes to Party, We Don't KS Spawns and We Don't Bother Nobody

In what (I believe) was a significant milestone for the guild, we keel-hauled that great big named Turtle in Iceclad. The challenge with Lodizal is finding him up and getting to him fast, then fending off the inevitable KS attempts. Lodizal dropped two Knotted Turtlebone Rings (AC 5, STR 3, HP 25, MANA 35, Effect: Enduring breath) -- one each for Dhyna and for Growller -- Cannibalize IV (shm) for the guild bank, Focus of Spirit (shm) for the guild bank, Composition of Ervaj (brd) for Devendar, Monster Summoning 3 (mag) for the guild bank, the quest piece of Lodizal's shield for Elim, and the quest piece for the Eyepatch of Plunder for Growller.

Guild members can and should read and comment on the Lodizal post mortem.
