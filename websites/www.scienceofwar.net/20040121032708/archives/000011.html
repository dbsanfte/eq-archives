Fennin Rowned!

At just 8 minutes past midnight on the 22nd of July, Fennin Ro the Tyrant of Fire had his burning loins doused with ice-cold water. Down boy! It was a flawless kill on the first evening we'd ever attempted him. With the kill we have asserted our dominance over all various and sundry demons in the Plane of Fire, for they have all died to us. Science of War ascends to Tier 10 with the kill, but we've got our work cut out for us: in order to hit tier 11 we'll need to kill the Rathe, Xegony and Coirnav. The warmakers are sharpening their weapons and drawing up battle plans. It will happen.

By the way, Fennin's crotch is the size of a halfling.
