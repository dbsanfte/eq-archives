20020710

Tier One!
After overcoming a number of minor of technical glitches that spell death on a mob like Venril Sathir, we finally triumphed, earning us a spot at Tier One on the Legends ladder. This is only the first step and there are 7 more tiers to go, but we did it on our own terms -- by never giving up on our very high standards for what it means to be a guildmember of Science of War. We did more with less and we'll continue to keep moving up the ladder.

We also gave Tier Two (Zlandicar clone) a shot with our new and improved AE DR resist song, but it seems we experienced a wraparound effect -- most participants report being feared / dotted much more frequently at 350~ MR / DR than they were at 240 MR / DR (our previous attempt). We're scheduling our next tier trial (versus the Statue of Rallos Zek - Tier 3) a week from now.

Guild Member To-Do List
As far as updates go, not a particularly titillating one. For new and old members alike, here's a list of things you should be on top of ... (besides the guild hotties). Gives ya something to do during patch days anyways.

  1. Make sure that your entry on the guild roster table is up-to-date. If it's not, post a response to the guild roster so that those responsible can make changes.
  2. Make sure that you are keeping your Magelo profiles up-to-date. If you don't see your name linked to a Magelo profile in the guild roster then please post to the roster forum with your magelo links. At a minimum, please try to have a resist profile and an xp profile. For bonus points, you can make a wish list profile.
  3. Please make sure you read and give feedback on prospective guild members.
  4. Get your extra spells and Gems and Items to Shads for the guild bank. Please only do this if you feel completely comfortable with having a gnome oversee our assets.
  5. Make sure that all guilded toons that you control are meeting the level requirement calendar. If your alt can't keep up, then guildremove him and concentrate on your main (be sure to let the roster folks know of changes like this).
  6. Vote on No Obligation Day!
  7. Are you getting ready for the Temple of Veeshan HoT?

Hail, Ithiosar the Black
Many in the guild -- indeed, many on the server -- participated in an awesome GM event. Here's a photo montage of some of what went on. I need someone to post a picture of the dragon to our forums so that I can add that to this update. Basically, here's what went down: GMs spawned a big mean dragon as the finale to a series of events that had been occurring for a couple of weeks. This dragon was red to level 60 and was situated in a cave in the Swamp of No Hope and was surrounded by level 50 drakes, level 55 and 56 black ravagers and level 60 guardian drakes.

We assembled a very large pick-up and cross-guild force and managed to drop (Ithiosar the Fallen) who upon death spawned yet another (also red to level 60) dragon named Ithiosar the Black. Ithiosar the black quadded for nearly 900 and had an AE breath that dotted players for eight game hours at 100hp/tick. He could not be slowed and had a TON of hitpoints. Needless to say, in our weakened state after Ithiosar the Fallen, he wiped us out. The open raid lost about 60 people to attrition because of the late hour and because of demoralization, so Township Rebellion came in to give it a shot -- with 25 people. They were wiped out on the first attempt (they got him to about 80 percent). They called in the rest of the guild (bringing their number to just under 50 players) and with the help of the remaining 25 pick-up participants, they wiped again. The dragon then despawned.

Players will be gathering at 8.00pm ET on Monday, June 03 for another attempt at Ithiosar, if he can be respawned.
