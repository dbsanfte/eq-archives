Recruiting Begins

Science of War is now recruiting highly-qualified players. If you are interested in applying, we suggest you read the FAQs to get a feel for our philosophy, familiarize yourself with our recruiting requirements, and spend a little time thinking about whether you'd also like to apply for a position of officership (we primarily need class recruiters and class leaders right now -- explained in our org chart). Thanks, and good luck. We know that there is a core of extremely talented players on the Stormhammer server, and we aim to get them into this guild so that we can do things that are not done on ANY other Everquest server.
