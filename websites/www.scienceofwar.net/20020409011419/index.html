Welcome to the Science of War Website!
We're still in set-up mode, so here's a brief list of what we're working on right now.
  1. Recruiting. The "invite-everyone-who-is-level-60" thing has been done by every "uber" guild formed so far on Legends. The only thing more common than bored level 60s are their bored twinks. This guild was created for those of us who want to create a real legendary guild -- a guild that does things better, smarter, faster, harder. Be a part of a second-generation Legends guild that aims to succeed by following the principle of quality over quantity. We don't recruit solely based on level or equipment, but on skill, knowledge and attitude (how novel!). Would you like to join us? Read about our philosophy and recruiting policy clicking on the link marked "join us" to the left. We're looking for some class recruiting officers to help us build out our roster, in particular.
  2. Website updates. I'd like to build out the website a bit. Any suggestions for content? If you've got a link that should go on this page, suggest it here. If you've got another suggestion, let us know.
  3. Guild Creation. The creation date has been tentatively set for Thursday, April 11th and 8.00 PM EST. Watch the calendar for updates.
