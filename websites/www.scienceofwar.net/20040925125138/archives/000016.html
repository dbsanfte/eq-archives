Solusek Rowned, New EQ Scam, The Birth Canal

The Tower of Solusek Ro is lots like VT, only the loot is less plentiful, the mobs are easier, the architecture is less grand, and the boss mobs have 800,000 fewer hitpoints. All of those differences are minor compared to the similarities: it takes forever to clear it and it's too easy. Solusek is designed to take lots of time. It's a virtual speedbump on the way to the Plane of Fire and a big let-down, especially after the difficulty of some of the events leading up to the zone. (Speaking of which, Mithaniel Marr has been upgraded!)

Even so, there are some nifty things about the zone. For one, the if you rush the monks you can get a few pulls of more than twenty mobs, and that's always good for a few nervous giggles. Another nifty thing is that after you beat Solusek Ro you get to drop into a flaming birth canal. So after you beat Solusek Ro, don't rush to zone into the Plane of Fire. Just sit there in the tunnel of love for a little while and soak it in. If you're really diggin' the vibe, spin in circles with your keyboard and type, "I'm a sperm. I'm a lost little sperm." Then zone into PoFire. It's one of those seminal moments in the development of your toon and well worth the sixty seconds you spend on it, I promise.

While we're on the topic of flaming cunts, look see if you can spot the IP address of the bastard scammer hidden in one of the screenshots below. Be on the lookout for emails like this one, Legends Subscribers! You've been duped into paying more for a low-population server, but don't get duped out of your toon. It seems this scammer operates by emailing people who use the Legends Community EZBoard with a fake "your character has been moved" notice in the hopes that the victim will respond with his or her email address and account information. If you get an email like this, forward it to accounts@soe.sony.com. Maybe this is a job for the beastlord hunter.
