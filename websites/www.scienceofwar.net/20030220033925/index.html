2003-02-18 Update

Paying The Bills

[Sat Feb 15 - Mon 17 2003]

Saturday:  As the merciless teasing from inanimate objects, and taunting from egotistic snakes continues SoW mounts a strike against the Glyphed Serpent, and Vyzh'dra the Exiled preparing the Cursed for a beating by Vexare. After prepping the mobs we proceeded to bleed the Emperor of some of his power. The Emperor was such a close fight it was unbelievable, check out the screenshot here to see just how close this fight was. With such a close fight, we felt we needed to have a sure thing to flex our muscles, so we went and smacked the hell out of Aerin'dar once again. Great night, check the spoils, and post-mortem.

Sunday:  After our previous near miss attempt on the Emperor, we decided it was time to really lay the smack down. We ascended to the farm early, and ended up snagging several insignias for some needy spectators, they were appreciative. We entered his lair, bled him respectively, then owned him. The guild then proceeded to the Plane of Innovation to kill some clockwork. We disposed of the Prototypes IX, X, and XI first, just to get the smell of clockwork fuel on us. Then dispatched the Behemoth himself. Fun filled night, and some great battles took place. See the gruesome details in the spoils and post-mortem.

Monday:  Monday Marathon! We began in the Deep, running through the Burrower event, slaying the parasites with a vengeance. Then we moved over to the Though Horror Overfiend to make sure he wasn't left out of the blood bath. Moving on to Akheva Ruins  we dispatched Itraer Vius in record time, check the parsing here. Then moved to the Plane of Disease to bludgeon Grummus. Lots of stuff died, we got great spoils. Check for more details in the post-mortem.

2003-02-06 Update

We Did It Our Way

I got a tell last night from a former sow applicant who had seen the zone-wide GM announcement about SoW's ascent to Tier 8. The person told me, "Took ya a year to do what [my guild] is about to do in 2 months, quite a feat. :)" First of all, envy is so unbecoming. Second of all, we did this our way. We never guilded someone we wouldn't want representing the Science of War tag. We never let go of our standards in our quest to move up the tier ladder and into Vex Thal. We did this without compromising our core values, and for that reason it was a very satisfying victory for all of us who had persevered while other guilds flashed in the pan. Is it possible to select 89 quality people in a single month? Doubtful. Our victory was our vindication.

Hell, even the snakes of Ssra were talking smack as our team burned a path through the temple to the throne of its leader, the Emperor. We subdued his guards and quickly declared martial law, forcing him to surrender his riches. We've now got 40 people keyed and ready to enter Vex Thal which represents more than half of the guild. We'll have to let some planar rifts rot on our next kill of him (apps finish those rings!). It's pretty nice not having a roster of 100 folks. Oh, and Vex Thal? Here's the first glimpse of the promised land. Exciting, ne?

2003-01-30 Update
Squashing bugs, and killing snakes!

[Wed Jan 29 20:02:69 2003] We got the notification early. Swarming to our location we entered that ugly pyramid with unease of what was to become of us later that night. There was constant whispering about a known bug that was terrorizing this ancient temple. Some whispers mention that this bug had taken out more than a hundred adventurers in the last day. Poised in the antechamber we learned that there was another bug in the midst of this pyramid, and it was nastier than the one in the rumors. This one supposed makes your wounds invisible to those around you, and prevents you from being able to heal yourself. Things were looking fairly grim, until we heard Vova's patented mangina rally call to arms. This pumped our spirits up, and into the High Priest's lair we rushed. Through an almost flawless victory, Vova the banshee Iksar died early on in the battle, we defeated that snake and humbly said farewell to the bug until.....

...The guild roamed the halls behind the noblemen and women of Storm Isle, awaiting any second for the first of the two evil Rhag twins to come into view. Ressurecting their dead, healing their wounded, and buffing the adventurers we were hoping to speed up their slaying of these two adversaries. They plowed through them like true champions, and walked away with some pretty decent equipment upgrades. As the second of the Rhag twins fell, a scream echoed through the structure, and we knew it was our turn. The Arch Lich wanted pay back, and Science of War was just the guild to give it to him. In little time we ascended the pyramid and entered his room. Set up our positioning and flawlessly killed this ugly serpent in record time! What can we look forward to tonight in the buggy lair that is Ssra? Will our adventurers overcome these bugs once again to walk away victorious?

2002-11-15 Update

Mujaki

Gathered at the SoW halls of justice, the league scans Norrath for dirty deeds done dirt cheap. We get word that the evil Lex Luthor is working with Hades to spawn the being known as Mujaki.

The league springs into action, gathering twenty four of our noblest and truest heroes to enter the Alter Plane of Nightmare. We wait at the entrance as our elite strategist work through the plans to defeat our newest arch nemesis.

Finally getting on our way, we come upon some other super heroes ridding the world of some lesser evils. Even though we were just passing through, these others heroes deemed it worthy to summon up a god to do their bitching and moaning for them. I guess these other heroes wanted us to delay fighting our arch nemesis until they could finish conducting their business with the lesser evils.

Well screw that! We had work to do, and no one stands in the way of super heroes.

We continue our movement towards this evil dead summoning crazed lunatic while our fearless leader works out the formalities with the elder god. We enter the graveyard, and search for this evil spawn of Satan.

We prepare and enter the 'back alley' graveyard where he has been hanging out. The force gets into position and Mujaki begins spawning evil dead from grave stones all around us. We hold back the swarm to the best of our abilities but some manage to get to Mujaki where in which he gives them a pretty horse to ride, and informs them to wait and watch in laughter as he spawns more evil dead to his side.

So we end up knocking off about twenty nine of the thirty six evil dead that spawn, and wait for Mujaki to buff up his friends so they can pull us.

It's an uncomfortable feeling being camped, now I understand what all of those mobs have gone through. Sitting there watching a group of adventures about to pounce, without anything that it could do. I felt that pain and fear as did each and every person that was sitting there waiting in anticipation of Mujaki to pull us.

random_pc_01 shouts 'camp check please'
Mujaki shouts 'i'm camping SoW but will be done real soon'

Finally after a hearty wait, he comes roaring from his platform, and we engage him. I estimated that he had about eight friends with him that were promptly pulled away and kited while we attempted to stick it to this animated pile of bones. This creep had an evil life tap and kept healing himself whenever we felt like we were getting the upper hand. I could have sworn I heard and evil laugh erupt from his mouth at one time.
In the end the SoW justice league failed to vanquish this villain, but we will succeed next time. This failure was so frustrating that even the mighty wonder woman, and the quick green lantern were rumored to have had words. OH THE DRAMA!
