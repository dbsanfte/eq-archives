VT Is Good for Your Body but Bad for Your Soul

Can I buy a vowel? After a few days of hardcore clearing the body count was TVX, TVX, KTXAD, DXX, DXV, DXXT, TVK, TXVD, TXD, VXAHR, KTXAHR, KTXAHR, AHR. Loot was impressive. But VT is bad for the soul. You get into this hypnotic clearing rhythm because it's simply not a difficult zone, but that's exactly the danger of it. It lulls you into complacency. It lulls you into thinking all of the game's top content is easy and routine and that's really dangerous when your time in VT comes to a close and you're back in PoP.

Wham! We got our asses handed to us on our first attempt at Mithaniel Marr, getting him to only about 95% before the Wrulons that were accidentally pulled with him dropped a messy load of hybrid cat-dog poop on us. We were still in VT mode, and it was our first attempt ever at him, so we needed to get slammed. We came back and triumphed on the next day. He dropped a Signet of the Advisor and Valiant Belt of Fallen Heroes.

In other guild news, Ladred is the new DKP admin. If you've got a question about your points (apps too!) talk to him. Scroto is the new guild keymaster. He's responsible for tracking flags, reporting on them, and hounding slackasses about getting them done. Congratulate them both.
