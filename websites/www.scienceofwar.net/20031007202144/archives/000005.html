Forums Operable Again

The forums are operable again. If you can see this post, you can use the forums normally. They should be noticably faster now. For those of you who care, we switched hosts from http://www.dixiesys.com to http://www.affordablehost.com. Dixiesys stuck us on a pig of a server that suffered from severe performance issues for months and months. In order to move off of the server we would have had to halve our monthly bandwidth and storage quotas. At that point it made more sense to switch to an entirely new host.
