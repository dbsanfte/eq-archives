Rallos Zek the Warlord Is Dead!

A not-so-wise man and leader of a lesser guild (Vexare) once spaketh thusly, "Vulva you talk too much shit for being a retard, but if you want me to school you again and stick my foot up your ass, keep talking out the side of your head. Because believe me when i say your guild won't beat this encounter EVER so make sure your ass doesn't burn every last bridge your dumb ass can take them over."

As predicted by our critic above, the infidels of Science of War did NOT beat the RZtW encounter today. Instead, we demolished it. And what makes the victory sweeter is that we did it on our own terms on our eighth day of attempts. The kill makes us the third guild (after Township Rebellion and Leviathan) on the server to beat RZtW, though we've now got a bit of backflagging to take care of before we're raiding the elemental planes. Can't wait to bid sayonara to the soon-to-be gentrified lower tier zones!

Thank you to all of you who supported us, and a hundred more thanks to the doubters without whom we would have not been quite so driven to succeed and move beyond you.
