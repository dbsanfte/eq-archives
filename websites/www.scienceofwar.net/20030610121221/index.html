VT Is Good for Your Body but Bad for Your Soul

Can I buy a vowel? After a few days of hardcore clearing the body count was TVX, TVX, KTXAD, DXX, DXV, DXXT, TVK, TXVD, TXD, VXAHR, KTXAHR, KTXAHR, AHR. Loot was impressive. But VT is bad for the soul. You get into this hypnotic clearing rhythm because it's simply not a difficult zone, but that's exactly the danger of it. It lulls you into complacency.
