November 20, 2002

Champion of War

On July 27, 2001 Legacy of Steel with the help of Shock of Swords battled the Avatar of War for over thirty minutes to be the first guilds to ever make him scream for mommy.

About 15 months later, fourty three zealots from Science of War did the same to advance to Tier 6. The fight lasted just under 12 nail-biting minutes. Team power ranger pulled some last minute magic out of their oft-used arses to push us over the top and drop him. How nerve-wracking is this?

After the battle, we took our victory lap around the arena singing the happy-happy-joy-joy song. The Avatar of War clone was our final Tier test in the arena. As we ran the victory lap, Vedan spawned a surprise for us. I think we got 'em to 99% each, thanks to damage shields. We had to teach Vedan a lesson to speed up the rezzes.

Now on to our next target!
