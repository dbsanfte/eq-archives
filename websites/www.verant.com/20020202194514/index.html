Current Events / Announcements  
 

Station Games get Mac-friendly! - On January 8, 2002, Sony Online Entertainment announced that its highly popular Station casual games, including Jeopardy! Online and Wheel of Fortune Online, would be available to more than 25 million Mac users. Over the coming months, with the continued support of Apple Computers, the Station Games Team will be working to make all of SOE's casual games compatible with the Mac platform. Other Station games currently available to Mac users include Yuetan, Empyrean, Bingo, 99s, Checkers, and the slot games.

SONY ONLINE ENTERTAINMENT AND INTEL TEAM UP TO BRING GAMERS THE BEST IN ONLINE GAMEPLAY - Sony Online Entertainment Inc., a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming has joined forces with Intel Corporation to bring the largest community of online gamers the best gaming experience available. The two companies are working together to enhance the graphics of EverQuest®, the most successful massively multiplayer online role-playing game, for Intel® Pentium 4 processor-based systems by offering higher frame rates and smoother animations.

San Diego, CA and London, England - Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) announced today that EverQuest®, the world's #1 multiplayer online game, will be hosted on local servers based in the United Kingdom starting November 1, 2001.

EverQuest Comic Book - Sony Online Entertainment announced today that EverQuest®, the world's #1 online multiplayer game, will be available as a comic book series later this year. Wildstorm Productions, an imprint of DC Comics, will publish EverQuest comic books, one-shots and graphic novels based on one of the largest fantasy worlds ever created.

Luclin Textures - Sony Online Entertainment Inc., (SOE) announced on October 5th that EverQuest®: The Shadows of Luclin™ will launch with an additional CD-ROM featuring a complete upgrade to all textures throughout the original world of Norrath and all previous expansions. Check out the Media Alert for more information.

Sony Online Entertainment announces the release of the EverQuest Trilogy Collection - This collection combines into one box Classic EverQuest and its first two expansion packs, plus a detailed map and strategy guide perfect for the first time adventurer.

EverQuest Game Cards - Sony Online Entertainment Inc., (SOE) announced today that EverQuest® Game Cards are now available at retailers throughout the United States. These cards allow EverQuest fans to play the world's leading online game without the use of a credit card. Check out the official press release

EverQuest Mounts - When EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin is released this winter players will have the opportunity to mount up and head out for adventure on the back of their own steed. For a sneak peek at of what’s in store, check out the official press release and screenshots of this addition of mountable horses to EverQuest.

European Servers - Sony Online Entertainment announced today their efforts to open new EverQuest servers for the European community. Beginning this winter, Europeans will be able to enjoy a localized EverQuest experience. For more information on the introduction of European based servers, check out the official press release.
