Sony Online Entertainment and 989 Studios Invest in New Games Development Studio, Verant Interactive


    Business/Internet/Entertainment Editors
    NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE) Jan. 18, 1999 –

    – The Station@sony.com To Exclusively Distribute Online Games Titles
    To Be Available from Verant Interactive –

    Sony Online Entertainment Inc., a joint venture of Sony Corporation of America (NYSE:SNE) and Sony Pictures Entertainment, together with 989 Studios, a division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc., announced today they will make a strategic financial investment to help launch Verant Interactive, a new independent online games development studio which recently spun off from 989 Studios. The terms of the agreement were not disclosed.

    As part of the agreement, SOE will have exclusive online distribution rights for new titles developed by Verant Interactive which SOE will distribute on The Station@sony.com, its leading online game network. Verant Interactive will now focus on developing original titles for the growing online gaming market.

    "This deal represents a major cornerstone in SOE's business plan as we'll have both a substantial stake in a cutting-edge development studio, as well as exclusive rights to distribute the studio's next-generation programming on The Station," said Lisa Simpson, President of Sony Online Entertainment (SOE). "The new titles being developed by the Verant team will further strengthen The Station's gamer-focused SubStation and continue to make The Station the destination for game enthusiasts."

    "SOE's investment in Verant Interactive demonstrates our long-term commitment to The Station's growth and leadership in the gamer enthusiast arena," said Robert Gehorsam, SOE's Senior Vice President of Programming and Platform Development. "We're hopeful that Verant's future titles will soon transform the gamer market, much as we've done with our JEOPARDY! Online properties in the casual gaming arena. The programming line-up of Verant Interactive titles, combined with our current offerings - Tanarus, ChronX and Fantasy War - will clearly differentiate The Station and solidify its status as the premiere online game service."

    Verant Interactive will develop a slate of four online titles for exclusive distribution on The Station which may include role-playing and strategy games. The first title will be 989 Studios' EverQuest(TM), a massive multi-player role-playing game. The additional titles will be announced at a later date.

    "SOE has built The Station as an impressive and reliable network able to meet the demanding needs of online games programming," said John Smedley, President and CEO of Verant. "With SOE as development partner and publisher, we couldn't be in a better position to lead in this exciting and emerging genre."

    "Our goal is to become the leader of massive multi-player online games," said Brad McQuaid, Vice President of Verant, "and we feel confident that such a goal is very attainable."

    The Station@sony.com (http://www.station.sony.com), with more than 2.3 million registered members, features online games and gameshows including the most played games on the Internet, JEOPARDY! Online and Wheel of Fortune Online, as well as games, including Tanarus, developed by 989 Studios.

    Verant Interactive is an independent development studio that develops cutting-edge online games. Based in San Diego, California, Verant Interactive has a staff of 70 employees who consist of the development teams responsible for the online games Tanarus and EverQuest.

    989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and the third-largest PlayStation(R) software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with a second development studio in San Diego, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay(TM) and Twisted Metal(TM).

    Sony Online Entertainment produces and distributes online entertainment targeted to mainstream consumers and game enthusiasts. SOE's initial site, The Station@sony.com (located at http://www.station.sony.com), is one of the leading online entertainment networks. The Station distributes online entertainment from Sony Pictures Entertainment, 989 Studios and third-party developers. SOE's advertising sales and partnership marketing team also works with Sony Music Online and Sony Pictures Entertainment Online to offer integrated marketing and sponsorship opportunities for online advertisers across the Sony Online Entertainment network of premiere branded entertainment-based Web sites. SOE is a joint venture of Sony Corporation of America and Sony Pictures Entertainment, and is located in New York City and Los Angeles.
