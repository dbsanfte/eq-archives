About Us

Verant Interactive Inc. is the premiere online gaming company.

We design and develop immersive, entertaining, and addictive, massively multiplayer games played over the Internet. Our publicly announced titles include Tanarus and 989 Studio's EverQuest both of which have earned critical acclaim.

Recently spun off from 989 Studios (a Sony Computer Enterainment Group Company), Verant is comprised of highly creative, talented and skilled people with experience spanning years of traditional console as well as online gaming development. We specialize in all aspects of the gaming experience including the games themselves, their Internet gaming platforms, and web presence.

Headquartered in San Diego, California, Verant Interactive Inc. is a privately held company.
