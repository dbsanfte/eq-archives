Local Electronics Boutique Stores To Host Autograph Signing Session With Creators Of The Best-Selling Online PC Game, EverQuest®


WHAT:
Boasting sales of over one million units and an active subscriber base of over 400,000 registered users, EverQuest® has more than 98,000 simultaneous users in the land of Norrath at any given time. EverQuest continues to grow with the release of its most ambitious expansion to date, EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin™. Players will enjoy an all-new playable race, graphically enhanced gameplay and rideable mounts in one of the gaming industry's most anticipated games of the year.

At both EB locations, fans will receive giveaways of EverQuest collectible merchandise and have the opportunity to win an exclusive EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin lithograph from renowned fantasy artist, Keith Parkinson. EverQuest, the most successful online role playing game ever released for the PC, has broken all subscription-based online gaming sales records.
