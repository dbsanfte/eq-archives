Verant Interactive Inc. is the premiere online gaming company.

We design, develop, and operate incredible Internet based massively multiplayer games. Our publicly announced titles include Tanarus and 989 Studio's EverQuest. Our products are known for their quality, vision, and cutting edge technology and are the subject of critical acclaim.

 
 
 
 

With offices in San Diego and St. Louis, Verant is comprised of highly creative, skilled and talented people with experience spanning years of traditional console as well as online gaming development. We specialize in all aspects of the gaming experience from the games themselves, to their Internet gaming platforms, and web presence.

Headquartered in San Diego, California, Verant Interactive Inc. is a privately held company.
