The Dating Game Online™: All The Fun of Real Dating Without The Emotional Scarring

- Non-Stop Flirting and Sexy Attitude Now Available at www.station.com -

SAN DIEGO, CA - August 13, 2001 - Sony Online Entertainment, the leading online entertainment network and matchmaker for a new generation, announced today that Dating Game Online™ is now available at The Station® (www.station.com). The Dating Game Online is an entertaining multiplayer online game based on catchy pick-up lines, flirting and risqué chat allowing players to find cyber mates, potential dating partners and online friends while participating in an engaging interactive game.

"Online matchmaking has become a genuine phenomenon as people are meeting their significant others online in greater numbers than ever before," said Scott McDaniel, Vice President of Marketing, Sony Online Entertainment. "Following the success of TV game shows Jeopardy! Online and Wheel of Fortune Online, The Dating Game Online promises the spontaneous flirtatious moments only the Internet can offer."

Like its popular television counterpart, The Dating Game Online will consist of one chooser and three contestants vying for his or her attention. The chooser will pose questions especially prepared to the reveal the romantic nature of each contestant. Answers are analyzed to determine which contestant is best suited to the chooser's particular taste. The chooser will select the lucky winner, and from there the couple can determine the next step in their romantic online relationship.

Sony Online Entertainment ushers The Dating Game TV series into the Internet age with the goal of creating an online community for singles looking for love. With its best-selling EverQuest® property, Sony Online Entertainment has already created the largest online community of role-playing gamers in Internet history. The Dating Game Online is a step in translating that success to other online arenas.

The Station (www.station.com) is the official provider of The Dating Game Online with updates and news about the game, message boards and other community features. Station members 18 and over are invited to engage in The Dating Game Online. The Station also offers the greatest selection of casual online games such as Wheel of Fortune Online, Jeopardy! Online, Parlor and Puzzle games as well as action games such as Infantry™ and Cosmic Rift™. In addition to its casual games line-up, The Station hosts popular premium games, including EverQuest, the most successful online PC role-playing game in history. Currently, more than 405,000 EverQuest subscribers rely on The Station for the latest software updates, news about the game, exclusive community features as well as unparalleled customer service devoted to providing the best in-game experience.
