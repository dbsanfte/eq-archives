Sovereign is the first ever Massively Multiplayer RTS. It enters the market with a new take on the ever emerging genre and adds new depth and value that has yet to be considered. With games from the past such as Diplomacy, Command & Conquer and Civilization, Sovereign will take the players on an empire conquest that will consume their every waking moment. Construction of cities, air bases, seaports, massive armies, air attacks, to nuclear weapons, chemical weapons and the consequences that come with each decision, Sovereign is a game played on a grand scale. The world, in scale to other RTS games, blows the competition out of the water, and with the engine comes the exceptional gameplay that customers have grown to love from Verant.

Even at its preliminary stage, Sovereign is turning heads. PC Gamer has named it one the Top 10 Games of 2000 in their August 99 issue, and Gamespy has named Sovereign a runner-up in the Best of E3 2000 online category.
