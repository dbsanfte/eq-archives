Game Master

Verant Interactive is looking to hire four Game Master positions. The positions are full time, working Monday - Sunday, 2pm to 11pm. Candidates must be gamers. Customer Service background is highly preferred.

Job Description:

  • Game Masters will provide end-user support, via chat room and in-game channels for all Verant games.
  • All positions must maintain a working knowledge of all Verant games.
  • Position is located in San Diego, California. No relocation expenses are available.

Qualifications:

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Must be self-directed with strong work ethics and high motivation.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Windows 95/98 OS
  • Strong computer troubleshooting skills
  • Knowledge of current gaming hardware a plus
  • Gaming knowledge a plus
  • Preferably experienced in help desk related abilities, such as:
    • Troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
    • Having a friendly customer service attitude

Available: Immediately
