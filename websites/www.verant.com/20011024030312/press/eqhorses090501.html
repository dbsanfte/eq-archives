Sony Online Entertainment Introduces Horses Into the World of EverQuest

- EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin To Offer Massive Gameplay Enhancements -

SAN DIEGO, CA - September 5, 2001 - Sony Online Entertainment, a worldwide leader in multiplayer online gaming announced today that mounted horses will be added into the world of Norrath when EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin ships to retailers worldwide this December. Players will be able to purchase a horse of their own in designated locations and will be able to summon their horse with the aid of a magical whistle. Players will be able to traverse greater distances in shorter periods of time and even battle from their mounts.

With the addition of all new player models, numerous additional monsters, and an enhanced game engine, EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin offers EverQuest subscribers an incredible amount of benefits with no additional monthly fee. Set against sweeping moonscapes, Luclin provides a new look and feel for EverQuest players and an entirely new world to be explored. Players can begin anew on Luclin or find their way to Norrath's moon on their own. How ever players get there, a new adventure awaits.

"We are committed to expanding the world of Norrath, creating new adventures and unexplored territories for EverQuest fans around the world," said Jeff Butler, Producer, Sony Online Entertainment. "We've always prided ourselves on giving great value with all of our expansions packs but Luclin goes even further than previous installments. This is going to be an incredible journey for all EverQuest fans."

EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin ships this December. For more information on EverQuest and EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin, please visit http://everquest.station.sony.com/luclin/

Continuing to grow with top-selling expansions The Ruins of Kunark™ and The Scars of Velious™, EverQuest is the largest fantasy world ever created online. During peak periods, more than 98,000 adventurers and dragon slayers have explored the world of Norrath simultaneously. The active global EverQuest subscriber base is comprised of players from countries throughout the "real" world, including the U.S., England, Canada, Japan, Taiwan, Korea, Saudi Arabia, France, Italy and Australia.

The Station® (www.station.com) is the official provider of EverQuest, with software updates and news about the game, message boards and other community features, as well as an easy-to-use billing system for EverQuest's monthly subscription fee of $9.89.

Sony Online Entertainment Inc. (SOE), the online gaming division of Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming. Headquartered in San Diego, California, SOE develops, publishes and distributes entertainment for the PC and online markets. With more than 10 million registered users, SOE's award-winning website, The Station® (www.station.com) hosts a variety of entertaining games and player communities spanning all genres.
