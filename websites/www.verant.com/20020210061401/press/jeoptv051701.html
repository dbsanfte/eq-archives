Sony Online Entertainment Brings Revolutionary "Synch to Broadcast" Technology to Millions of JEOPARDY! Fans

- JEOP! TV Offers a New Way to Watch and Play With the Televised Show -

E3-LOS ANGELES, May 17, 2001 - Further integrating the Internet and television, Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) announced today that JEOP! TV, a revolutionary new online version of Jeopardy! will be available at The Station® (www.station.com). This Fall, millions of dedicated Jeopardy! viewers can play along from their computers in real-time with the TV contestants while the show is broadcast.

JEOP! TV captures the intense excitement of being on stage as players strive to answer before their televised opponents. Armchair players can measure themselves and match wits with the "how-can-they-be-so-smart" contestants with JEOP! TV.

"In the last few years, various forms of media have quietly converged toward the online entertainment category," said Kelly Flock, President, Sony Online Entertainment. "This blending of media expands the Jeopardy! brand while challenging millions of viewers to outsmart the television contestants."

"After more than 17 years on the air, Jeopardy! has an unbeatable track record as the highest rated quiz show in syndication," said Harry Friedman, Executive Producer of Jeopardy! and Wheel of Fortune, Columbia TriStar Television. "In this relationship with Sony Online Entertainment, we are looking to bring the challenge and suspense of the television show to a whole new audience, and reward our viewers with a new way to play along at www.jeoptv.com."

Jeopardy!, television's #1 quiz show, is in its 17th year in syndication with a dedicated 32 million viewers who tune into the show every week. Jeopardy!'s phenomenal ratings over the years are a testament to its extraordinary popularity. The show has ranked as the #1 quiz program in the Nielsen ratings for 900 weeks and has reigned as the second highest rated show in syndication for 60 consecutive sweeps periods. Since its 1984 syndication debut, Jeopardy! has won 19 prestigious Daytime Emmy Awards.

Capitalizing on the success of Jeopardy!, Jeopardy! Online has become one of the most popular television properties available to interactive entertainment by attracting several million users since its launch. More than 60,000 games of Jeopardy! are played daily on www.station.com. The Station is host to several versions of the game, including: College Jeopardy! Online, Rock and Roll Jeopardy! Online @ VH1.com, Sports Jeopardy Online and now Jeopardy! Online Techno-Files.

Sony Pictures Entertainment (SPE) is a division of Sony Corporation of America (SCA), a subsidiary of Tokyo-based Sony Corporation. SPE's global operations encompass motion picture production and distribution, television programming and syndication, worldwide channel investments, home video acquisition and distribution, operation of studio facilities, development of new entertainment products, services and technologies and distribution of filmed entertainment in 67 countries. Sony Pictures Entertainment can be found on the World Wide Web at www.spe.sony.com.

Sony Online Entertainment Inc. (SOE), the online gaming division of Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming.
