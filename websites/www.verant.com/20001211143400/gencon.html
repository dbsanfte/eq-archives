Gen Con
                   

Verant Interactive showed both the Ruins of Kunark™ (the EverQuest® expansion) and our massively multiplayer real-time strategy game Sovereign™. This was the first public showing of Sovereign.

The GEN CON® Game Fair is recognized as being the largest gaming convention in the nation that offers something for every science fiction, fantasy, or general game-playing fan. This year it was at the Midwest Express Center in Milwaukee, August 5th - 8th.

Verant works together with Sony Online Entertainment and the following products were shown: EverQuest, EverQuest: The Ruins of Kunark, Sovereign, The Station web site, Chron-X™, and Fantasy War™.

Verant, SOE, and 989 Studios hosted an EverQuest Player's Party Thursday at Buck Bradley's Saloon to celebrate EverQuest. Several hundred convention goers came to celebrate EverQuest. It was a big success!
