Sony Online Entertainment Announces College Wheel Online and SoapCity® Wheel Online

- Wheel of Fortune Online Spins Off Two More Entertaining Themes -

E3 - LOS ANGELES, CA - May 17, 2001 - Sony Online Entertainment announced today that College Wheel Online and SoapCity® Wheel Online are now available at The Station® (www.station.com). Building on the success of Wheel of Fortune Online, which currently boasts more than 70,000 players per week, College Wheel and SoapCity Wheel join the Single player and Multiplayer online versions of one of The Station's most popular games.

"Wheel of Fortune is one of the strongest television show brands in the world," said Kelly Flock, President, Sony Online Entertainment. "The Wheel is perfect for online play and we have enjoyed the creative opportunity to develop and host different variants of the game."

"The Station is a true leader in online entertainment," said Harry Friedman, Executive Producer of Wheel of Fortune, Columbia Tri-Star Television. "We work hard to produce a quality television show and I'm thrilled to see it paralleled on the Internet."

Over 50,000 players per day log on to Wheel of Fortune Online and accure over 5,000 hours of gameplay daily experiencing the excitement of player competition. Players can solve thousands of puzzles, either competing with other players for high scores or working to beat their own personal best. Appealing not only to college students, College Wheel Online invites anyone with knowledge or nostalgia about college life with puzzles from academics to social life. SoapCity Wheel Online caters to the many dedicated fans of daytime soap operas with specialized clues such as "Back from the Dead," "Famous Soap Plots" and "Soap Vixen."

The Wheel of Fortune TV Show reigns as the number one series among all genres in U.S. syndication television. Harvesting several top honors, Wheel of Fortune had maintained the highest ranking among all daily syndicated series for nearly 900 weeks, making it the most-watched syndicated series for the 66 consecutive sweeps ratings periods.

Sony Pictures Entertainment (SPE) is a division of Sony Corporation of America (SCA), a subsidiary of Tokyo-based Sony Corporation. SPE's global operations encompass motion picture production and distribution, television programming and syndication, worldwide channel investments, home video acquisition and distribution, operation of studio facilities, development of new entertainment products, services and technologies and distribution of filmed entertainment in 67 countries. Sony Pictures Entertainment can be found on the World Wide Web at www.spe.sony.com.

Sony Online Entertainment Inc. (SOE), the online gaming division of Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming. Headquartered in San Diego, California, SOE develops, publishes and distributes entertainment for the PC and online markets. With more than 10 million registered users, SOE's award-winning website, The Station® (www.station.com) hosts a variety of entertaining games and player communities spanning all genres. In addition to blockbuster hits EverQuest® and Jeopardy! Online, SOE has an array of cutting-edge online games in development such as PlanetSide™, Star Wars® Galaxies™ and Sovereign™.
