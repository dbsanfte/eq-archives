If you are new to Tanarus:
    
1. 
 Download the game. (Free download.)
 
2. 
 Create your personal Station account. You'll need this to log in to Tanarus. (EverQuest players use your existing Station name.)
 
3. 
 Run the game. (Start Menu under Verant, Tanarus.)
 

 
From noon Thursday, Dec. 23, through Sunday, Jan. 2, Tanarus will be open for free play to nonsubscribers. Shareware players will be able to access all tanks and arenas.
