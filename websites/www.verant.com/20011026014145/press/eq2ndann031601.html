Everquest® Turns Two!

In Celebration, EverQuest.com Offers Freebies For All!

SAN DIEGO, Calif., March 16, 2001 - Sony Online Entertainment announced today that EverQuest has reached its second full year of service. Fans of the world's most popular multiplayer online game are being rewarded with a host of free downloads and activities from the EverQuest Web Site (www.EverQuest.com.) For a limited time, Norrathians will be able to download exclusive movies, screen shots, concept sketches, and will be able to participate in online chats with the developers of EverQuest.

Since it's launch in March of 1999, EverQuest has grown to over 350,000 monthly subscribers from all corners of the globe. To date there have been two EverQuest Expansion Packs: The Ruins of Kunark™, and The Scars of Velious™, each selling briskly and continuing to move at retail.

"The growth of EverQuest is unbelievable," says Don Vercelli, Vice President of Sales and Marketing. "Most PC games have a fairly short run on retailer's shelves yet we continue to see demand for this fantastic game every month. To keep Norrath up and continually growing for so long is an incredible accomplishment."

"The EverQuest team possesses some of the best talent in the game industry today," states Brad McQuaid, Vice President of Premium Games. "It's been a great pleasure working on this title and I really look forward to seeing the universe of Norrath grow as technology allows us. Our heartfelt thanks to the fans of EverQuest who have stayed with us and continue to spread the word."

During peak gameplay hours, EverQuest sees over 85,000 simultaneous users traveling across the world of Norrath in search of adventure, riches, and online quests that tell the tale of a continually unfolding universe.

(SOE), the online gaming division of Sony Corporation and Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in developing and publishing online entertainment.
