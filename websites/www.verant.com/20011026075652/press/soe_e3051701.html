Sony Online Entertainment Rolls Out Online Gaming Line-up at E3

-- The Station (www.station.com) Solidifies Reputation as Premier Gaming Destination With Online Offerings For Everyone --

E3-LOS ANGELES, May 17, 2001 - Continuing the company's unequaled success in online gaming, Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) has announced their 2001 line-up today at the opening of the annual Electronics Entertainment Expo (E3). With titles ranging from the blockbuster hits such as EverQuest®, JEOPARDY! Online and Wheel of Fortune Online to PlanetSide™ and Infantry™, SOE is taking the lead in online gaming.

"Our goal is to offer the greatest selection of games possible," said Scott McDaniel, Executive Director of Marketing, Sony Online Entertainment. "At this year's E3, we aim to demonstrate our best and brightest. We have a strong commitment to the online game community and our line-up will appeal to our 10 million and rapidly expanding registered user base."

"We are very proud to stand by our E3 line-up and continue our commitment to the online game community," said Kelly Flock, President, Sony Online Entertainment. "The seamless integration of Verant Interactive this year has enabled us to build truly talented production teams with unprecedented levels of experience and creativity."

"According to Jupiter Communications, the online category is due to make up a quarter of all game sales by next year. Lead by such industry veterans as John Smedley and Brad McQuaid, we intend to lead this category with the most diverse and appealing online games on the market."

SONY ONLINE ENTERTAINMENT'S E3 LINE-UP:

EverQuest®: The Shadows of Luclin™
EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin is the third expansion pack for the best-selling EverQuest universe. EverQuest is the most successful online PC role-playing game in history, attracting more than 375,000 active subscribers. Set on a moon over the world of Norrath, The Shadows of Luclin promises a gamut of improvements for the best-selling game including a complete graphic overhaul. Recently named Multiplayer Game of the Year by the Academy of Interactive Arts and Sciences, EverQuest reaches more than 90,000 simultaneous users during peak hours. EverQuest®: The Shadows of Luclin™ will be available this winter.

PlanetSide™
PlanetSide, the world's first massively multiplayer first-person action title, is set in a persistent world and features a new scalable 3D engine, never-before-seen realism, heart-pounding action and seamless gameplay. PlanetSide will set a new benchmark for depth of gameplay and visual splendor. Thousands of gamers can play simultaneously as soldiers, allying themselves with one of three warring empires. PlanetSide breaks new ground in one of gaming's most popular genres and is slated to be one of the most compelling action titles ever. PlanetSide will be available in 2002 for the PC.

Infantry™
Infantry is a science fiction, persistent tactical combat game where hundreds of players compete on a single battlefield searching for personal glory. Infantry, featuring a wide variety of weapons, vehicles, and player classes offers a variety of excitement filled games. Infantry gives players the chance to invade an enemy base with hundreds of soldiers on the field of battle. With a host of game modes like Team Deathmatch and Skirmish, Infantry is fun, action-packed title with easy to learn controls and top notch gameplay. More than 145,000 gamers have experienced Infantry's free open beta version that is available now.

Cosmic Rift™ Online
Supporting hundreds of simultaneous players, Cosmic Rift Online is the most detailed space combat game of its kind. Set deep in the heart of the universe, players can choose from three alien races and thirteen spacecraft to battle it out in a test of strategy and firepower. Cosmic Rift Online is currently available in Deathmatch mode and will soon support other modes of play including; Capture the Flag, Team Battle and Running Zone. Free open beta version of Cosmic Rift Online is available now.

JEOPARDY Online - JEOP! TV™
JEOP! TV captures the intense excitement of being on stage as players strive to answer before their televised opponents. Armchair players will now have the opportunity to measure themselves and match wits with contestants as they watch and play along with the show. Based on the popular television quiz show, JEOPARDY! Online has specialty games such as: Sports JEOPARDY!, Rock & Roll JEOPARDY!, College JEOPARDY! and Techno-Files JEOPARDY! JEOP! TV will be available across the nation this winter.

Wheel of Fortune Online
Based on the internationally successful game show that airs in over 40 countries and reaches more than 100 million people, Wheel of Fortune Online currently boasts more than 45,000 players per day with a suite of games including Single player, Multiplayer, College Wheel and SoapCity® Wheel allowing gamers to experience the excitement of player competition.

Parlor/Puzzle Games
The Station offers a gamut of popular parlor and puzzle games such as traditional card table favorites, Hearts, Spades, Vegas War in addition to mind-bending inventions such as the Scrambles, No Dice!™, Chain Reaction™, Chain Letters™ and more. Single player or multiplayer, Sony's parlor games provide fun for the whole family. The Station's parlor and puzzle games are now available at www.station.com.

Please visit Sony Online Entertainment this E3, May 17-19th, 2001, Los Angeles Convention Center, South Hall, Booth #746.

Sony Online Entertainment Inc. (SOE), the online gaming division of Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming. Headquartered in San Diego, California, SOE develops, publishes and distributes entertainment for the PC and online markets. With more than 10 million registered users, SOE's award-winning website, The Station® (www.station.com) hosts a variety of entertaining games and player communities spanning all genres. In addition to blockbuster hits EverQuest® and JEOPARDY! Online, SOE has an array of cutting-edge online games in development such as PlanetSide™, Star Wars™ Galaxies™ and Sovereign™.
