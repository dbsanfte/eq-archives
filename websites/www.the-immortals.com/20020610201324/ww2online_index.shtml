Thank you for stopping by and welcome to the new home of the Immortals
Expeditionary Force. The Immortals are a dedicated group of adult wargammers
who compete in the virtual world of World War II Online. Our purpose is to
promote fraternity amongst its members and to enhance the online gamming
experience for all players.

The Immortals, one of the OLDEST ACTIVE ORGANIZATIONS
of its kind, dates back almost eight years! In Internet years, that's a
life-time! Born from imagination, courage and a unique sense of camaraderie,
the Immortals have thrived on the cutting edge of Massive Multi-Player
Online Gaming. We can traces our roots back to Air Warrior, WarBirds, Ultima
Online, EverQuest and now WW2OL. Future plans of new Charters to explore new
worlds such as SHADOWBANE, PLANET-SIDE and eventual STARWARS GALAXIES are in
the works.

Thank you for stopping by and I encourage you to explore around and learn
what we're about. Should you have any questions, feel free to drop me an
