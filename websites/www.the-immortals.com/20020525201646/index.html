Thank you for stopping by and welcome to the home of the Immortals. The Immortals are a dedicated group of adult wargammers who compete in the virtual worlds of Massively Multi Player Online Games (MMPOGs). Our purpose is to promote fraternity amongst our members and to enhance the online gamming experience for all players.

The Immortals, one of the OLDEST ACTIVE ORGANIZATIONS of its kind, dates back almost eight years! In Internet years, that's a life-time! Born from imagination, courage and a unique sense of camaraderie, the Immortals have thrived on the cutting edge of Massive Multi-Player Online Gaming. We can traces our roots back to Air Warrior, WarBirds, Ultima Online, EverQuest, WW2OL, and now the Dark Age of Camelot (DAoC). Future plans of new charters to explore new worlds such as SHADOWBANE, PLANET-SIDE and eventual STARWARS GALAXIES are in the works.

Thank you for stopping by and I encourage you to explore around and learn what we're about. Should you have any questions, feel free to drop me an EMAIL. (sand@the-immortals.com).
