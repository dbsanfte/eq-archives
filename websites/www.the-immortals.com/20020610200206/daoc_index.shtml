The Immortals are still getting organized in the new relam of the Dark Age of Camelot

As a result this site is still under construction. Communication during this time is important so that all of our members experience as short of a learning curve as possible while learning the new game. To address the features of the new and our corresponding organization, we will employ two primary medium for communications: 1) Message Board. The message board will be used by all memebers to discuss ideas, vote on topics and share public information. 2) Server Specific Mailing Lists. As the game features multiple servers and three realms per server, our members will be spread across different "worlds." Each server will have it own dedicated mailing list for its community.
