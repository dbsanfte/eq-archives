Combined Services

The Immortals Expeditionary Force is a made up of four distinct combined armed forces, that being Infantry, Mechanized, Air Wing and eventually a Navy. Each Combined Armed Service (CAS) has its own organization and command structure. Each Commanding Officer reports to the IEF's Joint Chiefs of Staff (JCS), the supreme command of the IEF.

Unlike most similar organizations in our WW2OL community, the Immortals show now alliegence to any particular country. Instead, we rotate between Axis and Allied countries conducting a Tour of Duty -- that being defined as the war, from beginning to end until the server is reset. This agnostic approach to the game is our key strength, for it allows our members to the maximum flexibility of experiencing ALL the featues of WW2OL. It also allows us to build many new freindships and recruit many players from the various countries.
