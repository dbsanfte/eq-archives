You guys need to do some adventuring...

For the sharpening stones, they are worth about 3 copper. That's it. I can therefore get 100 sharpening stones for 3 gold. ore is also not too expensive. You just have to know where to look.

OK if you want to smith, Freeport is the place to be. There is a forge near the Temple of Marr and next to the Bard's guildhall called GROFLAH'S FORGE. Groflah Steadirt is upstairs, by the way, so if you have a quest, don't give the quest items to Ardiflah Steadirt!!!
Anyway, there is a human merchant woman behind the counter who sells the stones, ore, and molds. Buy these things from her. buy a lot of ore from her and put it in a backpack. If you need water, all you have to do from the forge is this: If you are facing the forge, turn left and follow the wall. You will go on a grassy area with walls. Continue and stay left. There is a place called "Freepport office of the people." You can buy water from the men behind the counter, as well as several other useful traveling items. If you are a Paladin, the sewer tunnel leads directly to the offoce of the people.

That's all the components. The lady also sells molds to several armour pieces, and if you find yourself needing other molds, go to East Freeport and check out a place called "Armor by Ikthar." They don't seem to sell ore but you can take some from North to it.
