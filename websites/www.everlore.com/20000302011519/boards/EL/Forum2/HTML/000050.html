This is what I know so far (not sure if any is fact or not):

EXP distribution depends on the following factors:

level, race/class, pets and each player's INT.

The lvl system is set so that you'll share experience in a group as long as your 4 lvls apart from lowest to highest in the group. At later lvls, like 20+, the 4 lvl range starts to grow.

Now for the races and classes, I believe the only thing that really matters is whether the race/class you use is a hybrid or not. As I understand it, hybrid's get a small exp penalty, I play a half elf paladin, so both my race and class are hybrids. I get to share advantages of two races and classes, which is good, but i pay for it by the exp penalty.

If your group is a pair or you're soloing, your pet will share your exp, not sure how much.

Lastly, your INT also determines how much exp you gain as far as I know. It allows you to learn skills faster so you increase all your skills more often with higher INT, so i'm pretty sure it also determines the exp you get.

I have heard that there is also break-down of exp by lvl of people who killed it. These are hypothetical numbers made-up to show the relationship.

If your group is say, a 15th & 14th lvl pair & you get 100exp for the 400HP blue mob you just killed, the 15th lvl char would get 55 exp & the 14th would get 45. This is because the 15th did MORE of the work killing mob (in theory), so they should get more exp.

Now if our duo kills a mob that is too low for the 15 to get exp, but the 14th still gets exp for, the 14th lvl should get ALL the exp.

A much lower friend, lvl 9, joins the group. We still see 55 go to 15th, 45 to 14th & 0 to the 9th (too young to have effected the outcome of fight). Now if the younger friend was 12th lvl, you might see 45/15, 35/14, & 10/12. (The young friend goes off to find group his own lvl.)

Our pair is interrupted by a Kill Stealer. If they can manage to do 201 points of damgage (to 400hp mob), they will still get their 55/45 split; if they can't do any more then 199, the KS'er will get the exp (if he needs it).

This is my understanding of how exp works, hope it helps.
