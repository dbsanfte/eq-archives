To the players of Evercamp--I mean quest...

There is no adventure anymore in Everquest's world of Norrath. When the continent of Kunark finally does appear on the oceans of the world, it will not be a place of discovery...rather a new monster factory filled with high-level explorers destroying everything in sight...packaging and shipping them. It will be Africa when Europe decided to exploit it for its many resources and wildlife. I am quite pleased that Verant has locked away many of the upgrade's secrets, but people complain still. They must know everything about it...everything of this "Kunark". If everyone were role-playing within Everquest...no one would know anything about it. Everything would be a mystery. I mean certainly things cannot be muffled in the real world about this expansion...but even discussing it in the game seems wrong. People rumor everything now within Norrath...no one just stumbles upon anything anymore. Even older items like the Executioner's Hood are "camped" anymore. No one just gathers a group and says, "We will fight today for the endeavor of destroying the undead I discovered in the day of yester within the Froglok caves and claiming whatever treasures await." One travels to Lower Guk, for which they already have a map, quest spoilers, and everything to assist in his or her quest for more power...and arrives there to inquire about joining a group for any given abbreviated room...(Example- The "ass room", the "br", etcetera). Then the waiting list occurs. Everything is ruined. All statistics on every item within the game are known...every monster...everything. It is not an Everquest anymore...it is a Neverquest. New servers do not assist...more people just fill them to the brink, gaining thirty levels within a month's time and fully "camping" it. "GM Events" occur sometimes, but end immediately once any high-level group hears of it. More crises should occur in the world...unexpected things like "Scraps the dog" in Freeport killing people for nothing at all. High Keep, another example of a HEAVILY "camped" area should one day have an accident in the mines...like a cave-in. Unrest should be plagued by Death himself wiping anything from the area. Lower Guk should have a similar incident. Zombies should randomly infest towns from nearby graveyards...a monster of an extremely high level should at rare intervals rise from a great lake or ocean and throw its wrath upon a port or a boat traveling between continents. Stampede elephants or mammoths through their respective areas...Karanas or Everfrost alike. Everyone expects everything anymore. As I previously stated...there is no adventure anymore in the world. All is about power and items...never role-playing. NEVER...role-playing. Those few who attempt this are often silenced immediately by a plethora of abbreviations and improper grammar. That remains as another point I will make about Everquest. Abbreviations, shortened words, improvised words like "Yak" from the "Short Sword of Ykeshi (I may have misspelled it as I have never personally encountered the item)", and lack of capitalization and proper punctuation and grammar have formed this Everquest world of Norrath into a bane of mine. I become extremely tired of people walking up to me, or simply sending a "tell" from anywhere in the world saying something similar to "im omw 2 kill sgs coming" or "do u no what sow is". I always explain to the poor individuals who inquire of the abbreviation SOW's meaning that it is merely a female pig...nothing more. I am submitting this to most every board I can find in an attempt to voice my opinion and perhaps rally enough who think as I do to convince the fine people at Verant to possibly do something about this. I am aware that there is probably not much that can in fact be done toward eliminating improper spelling, grammar, the presence of so many abbreviations and such...however there has got to be a way to end the other nonsense. Thank you for your time...

Respectfully Submitted,
Sinj
