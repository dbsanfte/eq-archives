As much as I appreciate all the replies, I think I didn't get the main point across clearly. Essentially what I feel the problem is, is that mid level characters, say from level 10 through 20, are unable to do any quests, and hence have more fun AND get experience. The reason is threefold.

First, there seems to be only two type of quests. Those easy, rather meaningless quests that any LOW level character can acomploish with ease. Such as delivering mail, or doing the inner-city quests.

Secondly, any quest that is tougher is WAY too tough for a mid level (10 to 20) character to do alone. Yet when the quest is complete (if you do it as a group) only one "prize" or item is the reward. So where is the incentive for people to group AND go on qujests together? There is none, from what I have seen. People group only to go kill monsters and such. They don't group to go questing, because the reward (for all) isn't there.

Thirdly, traveling is, imo, a blast. I've gone from Qeynos to Freeport and back. I've gone from Qeynos to South Karana and back. I've gone from Qeynos to Permafrost and back. But you don't gain ANY exoperience from travelling, only from killing or questing.

Anyway, I'm just frustrated at what is so close to being the perfect RPG, and yet it falls short.

EQ seems to need two more elements. One, the ability to quest succesfully by yourself (or give multiple "prizes" upon completion of a quest, so everyone is rewarded. Secodnly, EQ needs to give experience for travelling to "tough" places that are typically far aboove your current level.

Just my .02 worth. I'll get off my soapbox now.
