Hey Jaroo!

Here is the deal on that issue..

Verant made it so that if you place obstacles between yourself and a mob making it impossible for them to get to you, they will either (1) teleport/drop on top of you, or (2) hit you from afar (like aviaks on the ground can hit you in the penthouse).

Its Verants way of saying "no matter how ingenius you THINK you are, we are 1 step ahead of you". It sucks that, that is how it has to be, but if it wasn't then there would be ALOT of people sitting in out of the way places nuking mobs like a sniper in the trees.
