It seems the Mistwalker sword has been changed becuase of an exploit. Gordon reponds about the issue on the Gameplay Forum:

You are correct. The weapon was changed so that it would only proc 25% as often as it used to. This should have been announced. It's not the damage that this pet does that we had a problem with, that was acceptable since the item is dropped by a God.

3 Months of EQ and The Quest for Quests

Over the course of the month of January, everyone who added new items to the equipment database at Maximum EverQuest was entered into a contest drawing to win 3 months of free EverQuest play. We're pleased to announce that our winner is Kelston from New York!

Peakjumping Contest on Quellious
Peter Yerger [2/2/2000-2:29:27 PM]  Comments[0]

There will be a peak-jumping contest in Everfrost Peaks on Wednesday, February 16 on the Quellious server. If anyone is unfamiliar with the term, peak-jumping is the extreme sport that was created by linkdead runners relogging and finding themselves on the top of the peaks with no way down. The winner is the one who can inflict the most damage upon him or herself without dying. Damage is strictly self-reported on the honor system.

A prize to be determined will be awarded to the winner or anyone who really gets some velocity going before their demise. Entry is free, and all races, classes and levels are welcome to participate or watch the gory proceedings.

The event is strictly BYOB: bring your own bandages (or bind).

Please feel free to contact Celian in game or write Celian@aol.com.

Good luck, and I look forward to seeing you (albeit briefly) there!
