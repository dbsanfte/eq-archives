How to kill Nagafen
Valorin | 11/23/1999 4:49:52 PM

Return to guide listing.

Nagafen is a mean person. He is a dragon if you didn't know. If your used to playing UO, you'll think that dragons will be a cinche, but not in this game. The dragons are very very hard to kill. I've heard stories about 10 people killing Nagafen, but I doubt it's ever happened. If you plan on killing Naggy, read this guide as it will help you a LOT.

Nagafen: Ok. The most important thing to do is to make sure that you kill ALL the fire giants. Never kill them anywhere near Nagafen too. Get someone to pull them to be killed, otherwise you might find yourself fighting Naggy and 2 fire giants, a deadly combo which has ended many hopes of killing Naggy. Ok, so you pulled them and killed them. They won't be back for a long time (I think about 5 hours). Good. Now, heres what you need for each party:
Each party should have 1 magic caster (necro, enc, wiz, or mag) 1 healing type (druid, cleric or shaman) and at least 2 tanks. Buff up EVERYONE. Ok. Now your ready. First thing you have to do is get a level 49-50 shaman to cast Malosi on Naggy. Once it connects, the shaman should back up and shout "MALOSI!". Once that happens, send in the tanks. Keep attacking at all costs. The clerics and others should keep them healed. If Naggy casts fear, theres nothing you can do except wait for it to wear off. When it does wear off send in the tanks again. When Naggy hits about 60% health, have the shaman cast Malsoi again on Naggy. Now, get the necros, mags, wizs, every caster to nuke the hell out of Naggy. Most of them should stick, but keep doing it. A stratgy would be to have the casters bound to an area near Naggy, then suicide to get mana back and nuke again. When the casters finish nuking Naggy, he should be about 20% health. This is what I call the desperation time. The tanks are almost dead, casters have little mana, this is the critical moment. At this time, I HIGHLY recommend sending the tanks back and bringing the casters to Naggy for suicide (the casters should be bound near Naggy). The tanks should get heals from Rangers or Paladins. It might be a good idea to have one cleric not fighting Naggy, but just healing the tanks. Anyway, when the casters come back, they should have their best DD or DOT memmed. Send the tanks back. Now, nuke Naggy. That should be all. If the nukes barely help, the tanks should get the job done. That should be the death fo Naggy. If not, then you have a serious problem and should seriously consider gating out. Don't forget to ressurect everyone!

Anyway, that's all. I hope someoene finds this information useful. The main thing to remember is to not panic ever. You should do fine. Naggy has some of the best stuff out there. If you play during GM hours, sometimes GMs put cool items on Naggy. I've gotten a RARE artifact on Naggy on GM hours. It's a "Golden Knight Helm" which is like AC20 +10 STR STA DEX AGI and has a regeneration effect. Good luck
