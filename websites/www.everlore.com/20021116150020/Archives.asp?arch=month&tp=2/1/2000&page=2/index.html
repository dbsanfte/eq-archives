Cazic Thule Slain on Quellious
Thkeron Doomsayer [2/5/2000-2:44:31 PM]  Comments[0]

Cazic Thule has fallen on Quellious.

On the 27th of August, in the year 3180.

The large group of adventurers sat around the campfires while the clerics was working their magic to ressurrect those who had fallen in the last fight.

Many was seething with anger because of Cazic Thule himself, who had killed so many of their friends by striking them with a power so fierce that they fell down dead at the spot - and while he was doing so, he was shouting taunts all over - threatening to rend their minds with Fright, Dread and Terror.

They had laid waste to the physical manifestations of Fright and Dread earlier, leaving their rottening corpses to be picked at by the crows, as well as the previous day they had separated the Dracoliche from his mortal coil in an attempt to show the God that they were not to be toyed around with, but Cazic seemingly laughed at their attempts and continued to strike people down from his corner in the plane.

One of the Clerics had suffered 6 of the Gods Death Touches during the past days they had spent up there, and while he was in the process of ressurrecting one of the monks, the thunderous roar of Cazic Thule was heard across the plane "BIFFINS!", and he fell down dead to the ground, while they could all hear the God laughing at them from far away.

Asternak screamed with anger over seeing his friend dying yet again, shouting horrible curses towards the Gods corner, and those who looked at his eyes could see the determination in them - it was time to extract revenge on Cazic Thule.

Somehow the God must have sensed this, because all of a sudden you heard the thumps of something enourmous, trees falling and being crushed - and out from the woods came a giant steel creature! Cazic had sent Terror to kill them, and with him came two of the Gods own Amygdalean Knights.

The battle was fierce, and during it the God was showing his cowardice by striking down another group of them with his powers from afar, watching...waiting.

After a few minutes of fighting, Terror fell - the ground shaking under his enourmous steel body, and the adventurers turned their focus upon the Knights who had been mezmerised by the mighty enchanters Valdayana and Sonieli.

They were swiftly dispatched of, no match to the united powers of the group, and after that - nothing was left alive but Cazic himself.

"We are coming for you!" Asternak shouted, and hearing the God scream in angerat that moment made him smile a bit, but the brief moment of happiness they all felt was quickly forgotten when they saw Cazic come running at them, a unformed beast with a sword the size of 3 men in one of his 4 hands.

Asternak rushed forward, casting whatever spells he could to try to catch the Gods attention to lure him away from the rest of the group as they watched helplessly - the wizards and shamans knew what had to be done and quickly cast their invisibility spells upon everyone, screaming for them to run back - out of the Gods range where he could not see them so they could live to fight.

After 5 minutes of hearing the Gods thunderous footsteps, all fell silent - they all knew what had happened and said a silent prayer for the Necromancer who had sacrificed himself to lure the God away.

As the Clerics and Shamans was working to magically enhance everyones fighting abilities for the upcoming fight, one of the monks quietly snuck away, his mind set on retrieving the corpse of Asternak before the God could claim his soul for his own, and turn him into some mindless zombie servant.

By an enourmous amount of skill and courage, he managed to find and retrieve the corpse while the God was busy doing some arcane gestures, speaking curses and preparing himself for the fight he knew was coming - one thing the monk noticed was that the god had not 1 skeleton guardian, but 5!

As he was making his way back to the camp, he heard Cazic grow silent - then a roar of anger as he saw the corpse was missing, furiously shouting for any of his denizens to come to his aid in killing these foolish mortals who had dared to challenge him - but the monk knew what Cazic knew...there was nothing left alive in the plane to aid him, he was alone.

After another 30 minutes of the Clerics and Shamans doing their job, the time was closing up on 3 am in the morning, Asternak told them to gather up and prepare.

The time had come, they all said their prayers to their respecive gods, and started to move up towards Cazics area, one of the monks together with a ranger was already there, making sure that nothing would interrupt them as they made their final preparations.

Asternak handed over the command to Ezeke for the fight itself knowing that he had better tactical knowledge of the close combat.

After 5 minutes of anxious waiting, knowing that they could be struck down by the Death Touch any time, they heard one of the rangers whisper "Look, there he is!"

And there he was, slowly coming up on the hill about 100 meters infront of them in all his glory, standing taller than the trees with a sword big enough to cleave a fire giant in two with a single blow, 5 skeletons swarming around him... Cazic Thule - the God of Fear.

Ezeke looked back at them and shouted "CHARGE!", and all 39 of them ran for all they was worth towards him, screaming battle cries - the God noticing this roared with anger and struck Ezeke down on the spot, in a hope of demoralizing the group by killing their leader.

After 10 minutes of Warriors hacking, Wizards sending ice comets flying through the air, clerics sending their healing powers to the left and right while the God was hacking at them, sending people to their deaths by his blade and spells - he finally fell, taking one of the wizards with him into death by crushing him under his huge body.

All was quiet, with less than 5 casualties they had conquered the Plane of Fear - No more was there a God of Fear - Cazic Thule had been slain.

As they was scrounging up the rewards from the fallen God while the Clerics was tending to the wounded, they all heard a roar across the zone "I will be back!" It was Cazic Thule, who had apparently not been destroyed, merely defeated for a brief time.

Asternak smiled and shouted back "We'll be here, waiting for you".

OOC:

Ouch, that one ended up long. Many many thanks to Asternak for putting this together and everyone present for the kill - we all had a ton of fun, and even though many of you spent a long time up there, im sure we all agree that it was worth it :)

We were rewarded with a Bloodfire, 2 Crimson robes of Alendine and a Barbarian Spritualists Hammer.

Congratulations to Daks, Jubilex, Haywood and Badana for their ew "toys" :)

The guilds present was in no specific order: Glowing Embers, Wraith, Shadows of The Unseen, Vis Maior and Cantus Valorum.

Scribed by
Thkeron Doomsayer
