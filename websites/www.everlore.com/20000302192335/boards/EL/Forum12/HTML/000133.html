Greetings.

Just my 2pp on this.

Specialization can only be done in one of the 5 Magic Schools. (Abjuration, Alteration, Conjuration, Divination and Evocation).

Specialization will complement your Dodge and Chaneling skills allowing you to recover more often when interrupted.

Specialization will reduce the mana cost for the spells of it's school.

In order to Specialize on a given school you must get it over 50 in skill, only one school will go over 50 so be very carefull as to how you train them to make sure you get the right one.

Specialization, like channeling, is automaticaly checked and will increase by casting a spell of the proper school. (Note that channeling will be checked and increased without regards to school)

In order to be able to raise any specialization skill you need to put at least 1 pratice into all 5 of the specialization skills. Put any other you have into the one you wich to specialize in and train it as fast as possible to put it over 50 so that you make sure that's your specialization. This can be done by dumping some 20 pratices, I think this is all you can put into a skill, and then cast your lower cost spell for that school over and over again until it raises over 50.

Recomended specializations for a Cleric would be either Abjuration (Protection and Enhancement spells, AKA BUFFS) or Alteration (Healing), although some clerics like to specialize in Evocation (combat spells like Dismiss Indead).

In my case I once specialized in Alteration, this gave me the ability to have more mana for healing. This was done with another character although I'll probably do it again with this one.
