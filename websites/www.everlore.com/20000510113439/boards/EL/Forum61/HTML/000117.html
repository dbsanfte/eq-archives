Have been following this thread, and have to say, would really like to play with some of the GM's involved. That being said (some of my best friends are GMs ?), have the following comments
Do not have a high level character yet, the highest is level 19. Have seen a GM 3 times, spoken to one once. Have petitioned twice but never been able to wait long enough to be answered. Accordingly, i know none of the involved parties.
Have had the perception for some time that many of the guilds and high level players much more familiar with the GM's than i am. This thread has reinforced that opinion. (witness the person who spoke with a gm who implied to him if he fought well, would be rewarded). Would be astonished to have even passing attention from a GM.
This is the heart of the problem. Events like the one described, that non guild non high level people have no access to perpetuate the feeling of an "insider club". Whether this is true or not is unknown to me, but suspect the guides dont come from a vacuum, but from the membership of the guilds and servers. Accordingly, it seems to me extremely important to avoid even the perception of impropriety. I think verant had to act for that reason. I hope the beginning guides are reinstated (since they might not have realized the importance). I certainly believe all should have their player accounts activated, and would certainly enjoy the obviously intelligent and imaginative natures of the individuals involved (assuming they dont kill me for this post).
