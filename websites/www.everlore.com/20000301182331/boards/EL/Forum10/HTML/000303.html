Using the charm spell, what you want to do is kill the monster(s) before the charm breaks.

Charm the lowest level monster, have him attack the higher level monster, then help the charmed creature win (but not by much - it makes your next job easier)

Since I'm a Dark Elf, I can debuff the pet, use hide to break the charm, then nuke him.

You may have to use cancel magic if you're not a DE.

Don't meditate unless you have to - out of combat, the charmed pet regains hp very quickly.

I've only used charm as a combat spell. If I want something to follow me around for a while, I'll conjure an animation.

If a pet breaks the charm while the other monster is still standing, it's 'yell for help and run for the zone' time

It's just too hard to get off a decent spell while two mobs are beating on you.
