LOL!

The only reason why the early levels would be hard to play is that you can't meditate. Other than that, levelling up a newbie druid should be a cakewalk.

The only thing I can think of is that you may have power-levelled him somehow with quest items, so your druid has poor melee and spell skills? Because I can't imagine how a lvl 5 druid's evocation would suck, given that it should be well-practiced, since it's the only offensive magic skill you have at that level.

In any case, Druids are a little more multi-dimensional than pure blasters like Wizards, so if you are just looking for a character that can injure someone quickly, stick with your Wiz. At lvl 5, you should be able to run around with Skin Like Wood and full patchwork armor (or Leather, since you are a twink), using Flame Lick, Burst of Fire, and melee to kick the snot out of every blue and even con you come across.
