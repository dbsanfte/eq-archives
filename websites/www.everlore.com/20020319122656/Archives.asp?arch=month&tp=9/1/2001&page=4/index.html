GM event on Druzzil Ro - Lorrk the Insane acting crazy.. and Al'kabbor still searchin for eternal life.
Sparaz Matazerak [9/28/2001-12:35:08 AM]  Comments[6]

Last night on thursday a gypsy in North Karana said she needed the eye of an insane evil eye that was running rampant through the zone, Lorrk the Insane was the eyes name. It started out with the eye just talkin to us randomly about how he hated gypsies and such, he kept randomly bashing people and even though he calmed down at one point was too insane to listen to reason.. At first no matter how hard we tried (I had an enchanter of 52 circles haste on me with an fbss and after about 5 minutes didnt touch his hp bar)... Finally after much talk he listened to us and said we kill him and give the eye to the barbarian (one of the people involved in talkin to the gypsy), weird turn out yes, but then we proceeded to beat on the eye with about 15 people (blue to me at 51 btw) until it died. 

Afterwards I followed an old friend who was involved in the quest through out the whole time (flintheart) to NK gypsies. A GM controlled irregular (and better looking) gypsy was waiting around the camp. The rouge who pickpocketed the eye (deeppocket) along with Flintheart & several others invovled gave the gypsy what she needed. The gypsy then gave a dark elf of 15 circles a set of journals and instructed them of their SK destination where they were to meet up with al'kabbor near the pyramids (although i'm not sure if they knew he would be the one first or not).

In South Karana, where I arrived long after everyone else (due to my aimless lagging at the gypsy camp), there was al'kabbor and the other groupies from the event talking to him among the pyramid (portish lookin one with spires). The dark elf gave the plans to him whom then asked whom helped her, she told him everyone involved after which he warped out and back shortly with the classic coin rewards, to which he handed out to everyone involved. I personally offered the thousand platinum on me and my life, soul, services, etc to him all the time while roleplaying humbly before him.. In end he gave me a coin of gallantry cause "I reminded him of a ranger who is and one who is now dead.". He told me as I departed (I was last to leave) to give my regards to Lyeina Oakwynd if I ever came across her in my travels. 

I ended up getting a Silken Veil of Lyiena mask (5ac 5dex 3wis 3int 15mana) for my humble involvment with kabbor. Took me 107 days and six gm events to finally get something from any of them, but was glad to finally get it. Long lasting timeframe for the event, but fun none the less... Hope al'kabbor gets farther along with his research now that he has his journals to help.

 

Ask Brad Update
Korenth [9/28/2001-9:41:19 AM]  Comments[13]

Yesterday, Aradune, updated his Ask Brad column at the offical everquest site. Here's an excerpt:

Dear Brad,

I am just curious about something about SoL. I heard that it won't be like Velious in the sense that it won't take 2985798237598237598237589237598 people to fight in a zone. I, like so many other people, am not in an "uber" guild. I am sick and tired of not being able to do jack in most of Velious because I'm not in one. I don't think just because someone is in a great guild that only they should get the good items. Am I correct in thinking that SoL will be more toward a group or two? I really hope so.

Thanks,

-Leodinus

You're correct - the majority of the content will be geared more for a group or two. But that's not to say there won't be some raid content, and please also remember that loot from raid level encounters will almost always be better than loot from less difficult encounters. Keep in mind that it's out desire that EverQuest ultimately appeal to all sorts of gamers, from the most causal all the way to the most "uber", and everyone in-between.

If you would like to catch his full column update head over to the offical everquest boards.

 

GM Event on Prexus
Traverse Moonstalker [9/29/2001-12:49:41 AM]  Comments[6]

I'm not a very good RPer so I'll just tell it how it went... I heard some talk of a GM event in DL so I headed over there, always happy for an excuse to stop exping. I had read about this one happeneing on a different server from everlore so I knew that it was Firiona vie and her companion Lyriae(sp).

I got the loc of BW zone and ran there, when I arrived the travelers had already gone through. I zoned in but they were already close to Skyfire mountains so I just ported myself there. When I finally found Firiona Vie there were only about 4 people there, and she was very small, like pixie trickster small. Later on she chnaged herself to be regular high elf size again.

Anyways she was talking about how they had to talk to a Dragon from the Ring of Scale about the Lifebringer that Firiona Vie had lost. Her companion obviously hated and the Dragons and didn't trust them at all. After a few minutes Lady Zavo, a red dragon, called Firiona to come to the druid/wizzy portal. We (about 20 people now) followered Firiona and her friend there and the 3 GMs started talking. Apparently the ring of Scale would not help Firiona because of some law about not helping mortals.

My groupmate Levitated me and I ran up the dragon statue, then floated down onto Lady Zavo while she was talking, it appeared the dragon was giving my a ride. Firiona didn't like this too much and summoned me down to the ground telling me to sit still hehe. I went LD at this point and 5 minutes later came back. 1 minute later I went LD again (slow computer + 40 people = LD).

I finally got back to the druid rings to see Lady Zavo and Firiona still talking. My groupmate informed me that I hadn't missed much and that Zavo had said some ogre named Ghargin Bumblok had some info about the lifebringer. She warned he would be hard to get to talk though...

Lady Zavo then departed, bidding us good luck. Lyirae led the way to a different part of Skyfire where we stopped, waiting for Ghargin. She told us all to get behind her or else she might accidentally let loose an arrow and hit one of us in the back of the head (she wasn't a very cheery GM hehe). About 5 minutes later Ghargin showed up, except he wasn't an ogre, he was a mini cazic thule. We killed him in about 20 seconds and Firiona rezed him. He came back as an ogre and Firiona questioned him, threatening to imprision him in Felwithe if he didn't comply.

Before he told us anything, he taunted Lyirae about how he killed her brother. She almost killed him right there but Firiona held her back. Eventually Firiona got the info out of him and Lyirae killed him with her bow. BTW if you inspected Lyirae (she was a PC w/roleplay tag on) you could see she was wearing a Forest stalkers BP. Firiona told us not to loot the corpse, but several people did to see what was on it. I eventually looked, there were 6 pieces of no drop issued leather (what GMs give you if your account is hacked) and a No drop no rent book, hand held, with effect: Harm touch. If you tried to loot anything it just disappeared.

After that the crowd pretty much dispearsed and I left at this point, but a guildmate told me FV went to FV and handed out a few rewards and titles. All in all it was a very long gm event (2-3 hours) and not much loot, nice storyline though, and that line with FV telling me to get off the dragon was funny as hell. oh and at one point, someone with Malevolence challenged Firiona Vie to a duel when she stated that a mortal should not hold a godly item like Malevolence. Kaiten was his name I believe.
