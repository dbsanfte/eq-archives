Battle Royal - Xegony Server
Claud Shadowflare [5/15/2001-8:44:17 AM]  Comments[3]

As you enter the arena, you hear the muted clash of sword on steel... the smell of burning flesh hangs in your nostrils, for a reason you cannot understand until you hear the shout of an Erudin Wizard. This is immediately succeeded by a scream, as the spear of a Shadow Knight is thrust straight through his heart. You collect your nerves; bend on one knee to give praise to your Lord; and with an echoing yell, you dash past the signs that read "Discord Reigns Within", and into combat...

Greetings Xegony! This Saturday, May 19th, at 8PM EST, The Legion of Fyre will be hosting a Battle Royal at the Arena. For those of you unfamiliar with the concept of a Battle Royal, it's a competition in which all combatants begin in the Arena, and fight until there is only one remaining; who is then declared the winner.

This event is open to all players who have, at the least, reached their 9th season. There will be multiple competitions held, based on level. There will also be a fee to participate; but this will go solely to the pot that will be won by the winner. The competition will be divided up as follows, with a fee appropriate to each level grouping:

Levels 9-15: 3pp
Levels 16-23: 5pp
Levels 24-33: 10pp
Levels 34-43: 25pp
Levels 44-51: 50pp
Levels 52-60: 100pp

The winner will collect the entire pot. Let's bear in mind that 2nd place is meaningless; you're merely a dead man on the floor of the arena, under the heel of the victor...

Registration for this event will open at 7:00PM EST, and close at 8:30. It is STRONGLY encouraged that you arrive early to register. Battle Royals will be held in rapid succession; and we will not wait if you have failed to register.

Legion of Fyre members will be providing free Translocates to North Karana from 7-7:45, and low cost teleports from West Commonlands and the Dreadlands.

Rules:

-Anyone in the arena with a guild tag is to be considered a judge, and are thus immune from being attacked. Anyone found directly attacking a judge shall be disqualified. While you will not be disqualified if an AoE spell hits a judge, they cannot be used as a target for these. Any Legion of Fyre members participating as a combatant in the Battle Royal shall have the /anon tag on, and will be fair game.

-Prior to entering the PvP area of the arena for the Battle Royal, you are to have your buffs canceled by a Legion of Fyre member, who will be posted at the gates. I don't think we need any level 9's slipping in with a Skin Like Nature....

-Prior to combat there will be a designated 2 minute buffing period. It will be legal to buff other players.

-No form of invisibility is to be used.

-Other than that, it's all legal!

Violation of any of the above rules will result in immediate disqualification.

If you have any further questions, feel free to send Claud a tell in game. We hope to see you this Saturday for what's sure to be an incredible event!

Claud Shadowflare
42nd Season Wizard of Xegony
Legion of Fyre Officer

Kortar Finds Treasure on Torvonnilous (Player run quest)
Wulfgyr, Barbarian Shaman of 11 Seasons [5/14/2001-1:37:21 PM]  Comments[5]

I was standing around in Qeynos the other day, having just returned from an extended expedition of hunting Will O'Wisps in Erud's Crossing. I was glad to be away from the tropical heat and humidity and was preparing to return to my homeland of Halas; I was on my way to complete an errand for my guild. While standing there I noticed a fellow Barbarian, a warrior by the name of Kortar (a guild member of The Blackwatch Legion). He was preparing to go fishing at his favorite fishing hole.

He invited some of us who were standing there to join him. I hadn't been fishing in a long time, as I foolishly left my fishing rod back in Halas. And the company of a fellow barbarian was welcome after spending considerable time surrounded only by humans, erudites, and the odd, but friendly, Kerrans.

I agreed to join him as did Tanamili (Half Elf Paladin, 3rd Circle), Seneladar (Human Monk, 2nd Degree), Aianna (Human Monk, 4th Degree), and Reain (Human Necromancer, 3rd Circle). In order to speed our trip along I called upon the spirit of the wolf, to make us more fleet of foot.

We quickly ran to the Qeynos Hills to a small lake. It was located in a nice, peaceful valley, a welcome departure from the hustle and bustle of Qeynos.

After a bit of fishing Kortar hooked something odd on his line. A hollowed out bone that was sealed with wax. Within the tube were rocks to weigh it down, and a scroll. The scroll contained some sort of map.

Kortar recognized the locale pictured on the map and lead us to the location that was marked off. It was a small lone tree not far from Surefall Glade. We dug at the base of the tree for a while, and Kortar found a small box. Within the box was a key. Some of us pondered for a bit as to what the key could be for. After close examination of the key Kortar recognized a symbol on it. He thought he knew where we could find the lock that this key fit. We made an excited mad dash back down to Qeynos, and then leapt off the docks and swam into a secret underwater tunnel. The tunnel eventually lead to un underground passage, apparently part of the Qeynos sewer system.

After a bit of searching Kortar found a locked box, and trying the key we heard a glorious "click"! The box opened to reveal several pieces of jewelry within! Kortar, in his generosity, shared the treasure with all of us. Everyone got a nice piece of jewelry in addition to the excitement of going along on a treasure hunt! On the down side, I didn't catch any fish... ah well... I've heard the thunder salmon in the Karanas are good eating... I'll have to go check that out...

I'd like to thank Kortar for the fun little quest! I had a great time and thought that he did a really good job of coming up with the quest and I appreciate the time that he took to run it!

Quellious Players

How much do tickets cost?

Tickets are 1pp each. Buy 5 and get 1 FREE.

The money generated from the ticket sales will be awarded to the 1st place, 2nd place, and 3rd place winners.

  • 2nd Place wins 20% net revenue

You can ONLY buy tickets directly through me...Morthan Anuff. I am traveling through most zones each day shouting hotkeyed lotto messages that contain all relevant lottery information. Once you hear the shouts...come to me quickly. I will not stay in a zone long.

You can't. If you are the type of person that requires someone to earn your trust, then I suggest you do not play this first lottery. There will be more to come. If you don't trust me...don't play. Its that simple.

I have done 13 major lotteries on Bertoxxolous, but this will be my first lottery on Quellious. Once this lottery is over and the winners have received their winnings promptly...people will realize I am for real and have no intention of scamming people out of their hard earned money.

When is the drawing?

The drawing will be at approximately 11:00 pm eastern on Saturday, June 16th.

Where will the drawing be?

The drawing will be in North Ro where most people will be able to attend without any real dangers. I believe there is some sort of temple near the water or dock that would make an ideal drawing location.

Not at all. Many past winners were not even online at the time of the drawing. If you win your friends will have heard and the message will get to you.

I always track down winners and have yet to have even a slight problem locating and paying a winner.

10,000 tickets issued

#3: Second place wins 20% or 1,440pp
#4: Third place wins 720pp
