I have many characters throughout the Everquest system and am always drawn back to Rallos. I will not complain about PK'ers this and that, 'cause that's what this server is all about, realism. I have read many posts, some good, many bad. PK'ing is a fact of life, or death, as the case may be.
I have an enchanter (lvl 11) and a rogue (lvl 7). I have to say that I am a bit disappointed from what I have heard. The rogue class being unable to steal from PC's if true, is a serious, serious, hit in the realism factor in PVP.
I started my enchanter first. As I played, I simply did not carry any large amounts of money around, thinking,"I am not going to have a rogue steal my livelyhood."
I would apreciate any feedback from others as to the ability to steal from PC's, with your thoughts as to the possable ramifications of being able to steal. Or, any reasons why a rogue shouldn't be able to steal from another PC.

I agree, while I went through a brief period hating Rallos and considering jumping to a carebear server I stuck with it and have decided I prefer the realism of PvP (yes, I started a newbie character on Lanys and leveled him to 8)...

That being said, I play a rogue on RZ. I know that when I started playing you _could_ pp from players, because my Paladin was picked for a few silver. I started my rogue later, and for awhile kept practicing PP on my group members (promising to return whatever I got), but it never worked. I later found it'd been disabled.

I dislike that development, and think you should be able to PP players.
