Shadow silk is for Solusek Ro Necromancer quests.

It really has no stats until the items are created.

I beleived this is trivial from 20-50, but I'm not sure.

There are 3 types of Shadow Silk, Inky Shadow Silk, Large Shadow Silk, and Regular Shadow Silk...

Shadow Silk > Shadow Wolf Pelt, Silk Swatch, Scroll of Gather Shadows.
Large Shadow Silk > 2 Shadow Wolf Pelts, Silk Swatch, Scroll of Gather Shadows.
Inky Shadow Silk > Shadow Wolf Pelt, Silk Swatch, 2 Scrolls of Gather Shaows.

Hope this answers your question.
