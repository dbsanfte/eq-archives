Dragnfyre, I believe you've been in the norm. Like any trade skills, even at the trivial limit you can miss from 30 to 50 percent of the time and at 160, the Trueshot bow is far from trivial. I'm not even sure it becomes trivial at 200 so, 3 out of 7, while not good seems to be in the norm. I succeed 3 out of 4 with a skill of 150. That's the problem with randomness, you can have amazing success for a short time but you cannot beat the odds for long. At least, the Trueshot bow is not expensive to fail compared to darkwood.

As for the 3 cam bow, I believe it's an hoax but it would be a nice change. Bows are crappy weapons when their stats are translated into melee standard. Some will argue that they are free possible damage but it should be the main way to deal damage for rangers and so, ranger specific bows should be far faster, especialy for a bow called the Raincaller!

At low to mid level, it is nice but it seems to me that at 50th level, the bow damage is meaningless whatever the bow. The main problem is the speed, not the damage. The dmg vs delay of bows are awfull.
