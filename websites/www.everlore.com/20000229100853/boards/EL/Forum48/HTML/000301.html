Ok ... Verant has done it again to us fellow tailors. This may be server wide or not, on the Nameless its positive.

THICKEN MANA CAN NO LONGER BE BOUGHT FROM ELF MERCHANT IN OOT.

Now the only place in all of Norrath to buy this is the brownie merchant. Obviously, just as jewelery is, they want to make this an enchanter "specialization". When I wanted to make Wus gauntlets I would simply go buy the spell from elf, and donate it to any enchanter who would make 10-20 potions for me, since thicken mana was not something every enchanter would buy. Hey.. a free spell just to spend a few bars of mana.

Now, I need to find a smart enough enchanter to go buy me the spell from brownie without getting killed, and pay him for the favor, or if theyre nice enough they dont charge. I still give 5-10pp for their services. I feel bad when they die trying to cast alliance on a guard and they are not fast enough =)

So, my way around this is this. You may consider this a sort of "twink", I consider it beating Verant at their own game. Make an enchanter, you only need lvl 8 to use alliance and lvl 8 should only take u a few hours, plus you can also use enchant silver if youve always wanted to do jewelry too. No need to hire another enchanter, do it yourself.

Use the "same spot" camp method for your characters, somewhere in a remote corner or empty building noone will see you at. Use your enchanter and enchant your silver and make all the viscious mana u want. Drop the backpacks on the ground, camp. Log on with your main character, which of course u camped with at the same spot, and the backpacks are on the ground for you to pick up. Voila!!

Im sorry, I just dont find it fair that Verant is telling us, " If you want to be profitable, you MUST AND WILL play an enchanter." Bzzzttt , wrong answer. Beat them at their own game. I will play what I please.

DROPPED Items (as with everything else) has a Decay rate, but since you are sitting right next to each other (basically)the chance of it being gone is low. Also, do not drop JUST an item (sword,bars of sliver,etc.), put them in a bag to increase Decay rate. BTW, make sure you either do this in a very low traffic area or (to be really sure) LATE at night (never know when some NEWBIE, who doesn't know squat about the area or game manners, will walk in and take you bag full of cool stuff).
