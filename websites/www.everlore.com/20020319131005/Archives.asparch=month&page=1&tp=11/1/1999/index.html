Innoruuk: Festival of the Ages!
Wyndorn Windchaser [11/5/1999-2:22:24 PM]  Comments[0]

There will be a huge Festival this Saturday on the Innoruuk server:

Greetings, after two weeks of planning, I am happy to announce that the Festival of the Ages has been planned!

This Festival will take place in the Arena in Lake Rathetear (Don't worry, its not all PvP area), Saturday starting at 5:00 PM Central. And now, for the various events(all times are tentative, depending on the length of other events):

5:00 -- Enchanter Classes put on by the renowned enchanter Smith of the Lords of Aesir. She will be covering the following:

  • Enchanter Mentality
  • Enchanter Power
  • Creating an Enchanter from
  • Scratch
  • Enchanter Illusions
  • Enchanter Spells
  • Soloing
  • Grouping
  • Charm
  • Trades

6:00 -- Race around South Karana, hosted by the Knights of Armageddon. I have translated this from Grugah's broken common.

Racers shall make their way to South Karana. Upon entering, Gruguh, guildmaster of the Knights of Armageddon will prepare you for the race.

Guildmembers of the Knights of Armgeddon will be stationed at each point, and racers must bow to them before they can continue to the next point. Of course there will be no magic, gating, "J" boots... or anything else that makes you go faster. Cheaters will be watched for.

The path for this race will be: From the Lake Rathe entrance, to the Bridge to North Karana, to the Centaur Village, to Split Paw, to the Aviak Tower up the hill from the vllage, and finally back to the entrance to Lake Rathe.

The guildmembers will be stationed a little ways away from the the points so people wont get attacked, but close enough to find them.

The prize for this event will be two Barbed Whips!

7:00 -- The Battle Tournament hosted by Dark Retribution:
The tournament will be broken into four categories.

  1. Level 20 and below
  2. Level 21 to 26
  3. Level 27 to 30
  4. Level 31+

We will go in order of appearance at the Arena, and matchup on site. I will keep track of the winners, as I will be there for the whole event. There will be NO buffs from outside parties allowed, but casters may Buff themselves. NO JBoots will be allowed to be used.

Prizes have yet to be determined.

8:00 -- The Dance Contest hosted by Aven du Sora:
The Dance contest held by us, Aven du Sora, will take place infront of the entrance to the main section of the arena. Dancers will be taken down on arrival, and their dances will be judged on originality, humor, and creativness. Dancers are encouraged to use all the new emote actions, if not other things in their performance. Bard dancing will play very little, as we have all seen it... we're looking for something new! To dance using actions, make a few hot keys of ones you wish to use, and "spam" them.

The prize to the best dancer as chosen by the judges will recieve a Stein of Moggok (arranges can me made if one is already owned), and there shall be some minor prizes to second and third place.

9:00 and beyond! -- Poetry reading, story telling, drinking and a bazaar! After the last event, the dance contest, there shall be poetry reading and story telling in one part of the outer arena, and a bazaar, drinking, and partying in another. The exact places will be indicated after the Dance Contest

10:00 -- The Outcasts of Norrath's Outfitting Event:
If you are a very young one, I encourage you to head over to Freeport to partake in the Outcasts of Norraths Outfitting event. They have quite a surplus of items, and are giving them to young ones, in return for small tasks!

All are invited to the Festival, and encouraged to attend. We will deal with troublemakers in the Arena, so you've been warned! Also, bring items to sell at the Bazaar afterwards! Also, please expect to see members of these guilds there, as they have all expressed interest in attending: Lords of Aesir , Wayward Travelers, Crimson Dragons, Knights of Armageddon, Aegis Spirits, Radiant Fire, Iron League, Conclave of the Staff, Lost Souls of Glaxnor, Legends, Socii in Facino, The Talquinn, Dark Retrubution, Coinfist, Deus Vult, and Scorched Heart.

Hope to see you all there!

The Rogue Royal Rumble -Bertoxx
Manjol Nighteagle [11/5/1999-2:35:42 PM]  Comments[0]

Rogues of the Bertoxxulous will want to note this event:

Well folks, its finally here. This Saturday we, the silent hand, will be hosting the first ever rogue tourney in the history of the Bertoxxulous server.

This saturday, the thieves of norrath shall emerge from the shadows to partake in a free for all based on their level groups. The event will be hosted in the freeport pvp arena, and the rules are as follows. All players must have anonymous deactiviated so that your level can be verified, once you have entered the ring, the only way out is death or winning, if you are caught leaving you will be disqualified. No looting is permitted of players corpses except under a single condition. If a player tries to enter the ring when his lvl group is not fighting, or another character that is non rogue attempts to enter and disrupt the activites, all contestants are required to slay the player, and loot his corpse. Otherwise NO LOOTING WILL BE PERMITTED. The cost for entrance will be 5 pp per person, and shall be payed upon arrival. Each group will duke it out until one person is left standing, and out of all the groups, the player left with the highest percentage hitpoints is the winner. The use of poisons is permitted and encouraged, as it is a rogue skill. Hidding is permitted. Having a friend heal you is not. The rogue that is declared champion will be given a choice, either the pot(total collection of money for the event)or the serrated bone dirk. All members of silent hand will be allowed to participate with no entrance fee required(is good to be in de guild).

The previous level cap has been raised and players will be grouped accordingly.

  • Group one lvls 10-14
  • Group two lvls 15-19
  • Group three lvls 20-24
  • Group four lvls 25-29
  • Group five lvls 30-34
  • Group six lvls 35-40

Have fun, and good luck to all rogues that participate(never know, you may have to face me).

-To sum it all up-

Where : Freeport pvp arena
When : Saturday at 6pm Est
Who: Any rogue above lvl 10 and below lvl 40
Server: Bertoxxulous

Manjol Nighteagle, infamous leader of the silent hand, and one day the world

I was running through east freeport after disembarking from the ship from Faydwer when I heard the shouts of Monk Disciple and a Human Warrior with the last name Purelight they were calling on the races of light to go purge the Dark Races from the Desert. I answered the call along with a Barbarian warrior as that area of town was pretty empty, the Disciple then led us to the Inn in the Northern Ro where we collected another 4 adventurers we quickly formed an official party of six so we could comunicate and I could spread the affects of my songs amoung them, we then marched to Dorn's camp and slayed the foul inkie, after that we went to the Oasis of Marr to rid that zone of any of Dorn's foul bretheran, but our war party of 8 were meet by a huge dark army of about 50 just south of the warf we all fell quickly except for the Disciple and Purelight but they too surcomed to the onslaught then the army of the dark made it's way to Freeports southern gate where another small force of good esembled, with the help of the Guards and few archers on the wall the darkness was driven back three times and many of them were slain, thus in the end light triumphed and darkness fled back to the desert.
