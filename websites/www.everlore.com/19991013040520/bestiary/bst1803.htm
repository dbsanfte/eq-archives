An evil bastard, don't even try to tango with one of these until 16, no matter WHAT class you are. Also don't attempt to solo until you are about 20. These are found in desert regions and use those iron hard knobby knuckles to good effect. What's even worse is that they cast powerful attack spells so they don't HAVE to bruise those delicate hands. At any rate, if you see one under 16, run.
