This has been posted once before, but that was some time ago. Therefore, for those who missed it (or have become to lazy to search the archives *LOL* j/k), I am posting the very same information again, with a few revisions and extra information.

Category 1: 18-21

Category 4: 30-33

Firiona Vie GM Event on Tholuxe-Paells
Chyr [6/1/2001-7:26:19 PM]  Comments[20]

It was a quiet day in the woods; I had just returned from Kaladim when Firiona Vie shouted that she required all able bodied Soldiers of Tunare to assist her in Lesser Faydark. She said, with the power of the four stones, she was going to summon The Mother of All, Tunare.

After assembling, she revealed to us that we needed to find the magical unicorn named Equestrielle--only she could reveal how to summon The Mother of All.

After finding Equestrielle, we were guided to the Faerie Ring, the only place in the forest with enough connection left to The Plane of Growth to summon Tunare. After naming four adventurers of Norrath as her chosen, the summoning began. The Fire Caller, Wind Caller, Earth Keeper, and Wave Caller were all given respective stones and with their combined power, Tunare was summoned.

Firiona Vie immediately apologized for failing in her task; The Mother of All was quick to forgive. Apparently, Firiona's stave has been corrupted by Cazic-Thule to such an extent that even Tunare herself could not find the stave, nor alter it's corrupted state.

The Mother of All charged Firiona Vie with revisiting The Ring of Scale. She promised, however, that this time, her memories would not be reaved.

After prefacing that The Ring of Scale had become aloof from the adventuring world and was very hostile to those who attmepted to enter it's dens, Tunare made cryptic remarks about an agent of The Ring of Scale and what would be needed for a peaceful encounter.

Peace was not long lived in this forest: Danger was sensed. Cazic-Thule had made his presence known and was slowly making his way towards the Faerie Village. Tunare bid everyone to flee, but Firiona Vie refused to go and vowed to stay and fight. Nearly 130 supporters of Tunare and Firiona Vie agreed to stay and fight along side.

Many threats were made by The Faceless One. He came from nowhere and immediately the dark forest was alight with fireworks--displays of magick from some of Norrath's most talented wielders. Many suffered death at the hands of Cazic-Thule who dealt damage at a massive rate of 16,000 per spell at times. However, the day was not ordained to succumb to evil. Cazic-Thule was defeated and Firiona Vie was Champion of the day.

The greatest hurt was made to Equestrielle, whose magical horn had been corrupted during the battle with Cazic-Thule. Firiona Vie was forced, crying as she did it, to cut off Equestriell's horn with her astonishingly beautiful magic blade. Equestrielle ran off and disappeared not to be seen again.

The huge crowd, overcome with zealotry, huddled close to Firiona Vie. Understandably, Firiona Vie was quite shaken with the day's events and wanted nothing more than some time alone. The crowd was unforgiving in their enthusiasm, sadly. She left suddenly in an enraged state, but not handing out a few tokens of reward--coins that were to be given to a Lord in Felwithe. The rewards for these coins is not known to me.

Immediately after Firiona Vie left, the sky turned crimson red and there were defeaning screams heard. A horde of undead sprites appeared that looked similar to the guardians of The Temple of Solusek Ro. Cazic-Thule's voice was heard after he was slain and he promised that he would meet Tunare's Champion again, very soon. Perhaps this was an example of how strong Cazic-Thule's hold over the forests of Lesser Faydark really are.

This Enchanter quickly left the scene after the undead sprites appeared and I know not what happened afterward. It was quite a day in Lesser Faydark, one that shall be talked about for a long time to come...

Patch Changes Tomorrow
Xanthus [6/4/2001-4:53:08 PM]  Comments[13]

Absor posted the following about patch changes tomorrow:

When we patch tomorrow (6-5-01), we will be introducing code on Rallos Zek server that will result in faction hits similar to those on the team servers.

Killing a character in any of their races home town will cause you to take a faction hit. This counts for all home cities. So, if you kill a Human in any of the city zones of Qeynos (or Freeport, or Surefall Glade), you will suffer a faction hit, even if that Human was born in Freeport. All of Greater Faydark is the starting city for Wood Elves. There will also be faction hits for killing High Elves and Half-Elves in Greater Faydark. Killing an Erudite in either Paineel or Erudin will cause a faction hit.

Also, for those of you trying to start or complete quests for King Tormax, you need to know that we've made a change. You will now need to be in good standing with him in order to do his quests. In game terms, this means you will need to be at least amiable.

Alan

But if you happen to see a person online within three rungs (rankings) of you, then you can PvP with them, though they are not required to fight you then and there. As was stated before, e-mail makes it official. Any challenges that are beyond three rungs of your rank will only count for your record, not ranking.

2) If you challenge a higher person within three rungs and lose, you switch places with the person below you. For example: #4 challenges #1 and loses, they will go down one rung (place, step..etc) and switches with #5. If you win, then you switch with the person you beat. In this example, #4 would be #1 and the old #1 will be #4. All battles must be e-mailed to TZLadder@hotmail.com. The winner of the battle must e-mail the win, Carbon Copying the loser. A loser stating that he lost is not used for records. (For the mere reason that I could accidentally count one fight twice.) This will be based on the Honor System. The Loser is CC'd for the purpose of disputes. If there is a dispute, and no Screenshots or evidence to back up the claim, the match will be refought with a unbiased referee present.

ALL DECISIONS BY THE LADDER STAFF AND REFS ARE FINAL!

3) Avoidance of Challenges: The bottom line is if you challenge someone, they must make an effort to accommodate your challenge. Avoidance of Challenges is sufficient to award a forfeit, though we will remain lenient on forfeits.

4) After challenging someone and losing, you must wait 1 week before challenging him/her again.

5) After being challenged and defeated you must wait 1 day before challenging the person who just beat you.

6) You can only challenge 3 spots up or down from your rank to affect your ranking..

7) Any challenges beyond 3 away from your rank, will be taken for record battles (I encourage this especially if you are having a hard time finding those close within your rank).

8) After making a challenge via e-mail to me and the person being challenged, that person has 4 days to give a response of when would be good for him/her (or to have the battle). If no response is given, and no post has been made that said person will be away for some period of time, the issue of forfeit will be addressed. Ultimately, after four days of no response, all the challenger has to do is e-mail me asking for the forfeit and CC'ing the forfeitee.

(There will be flexibility given to the person who is being challenged IF they are showing effort to make the battle).

9) When a fight is scheduled and agreed upon by both participants, there is a 1 hour grace-period before a forfeit is awarded. (In otherwords, if A agrees to meet B at 9:00pm EST Wed and A is more than an hour late, B is given the win.)

10) There are no rules in the Fights. Any spell, item, weapon, etc. is allowed. SELF BUFFING ONLY. It will be up to the combatants to see to it that the other party is fully dispelled prior to the fight.

You and the other fighter you are battling may agree on any additional rules. For example, if you and your enemy are feeling a little Fungi shy, you can agree on outlawing Fungis. You both must agree to this restriction however. If one of the players doesn't want the restriction, then there is no restriction.

11) All fights are to take place in an Arena type area. (Preferably the Lake Rathe Arena.) This way there is no interference, no XP loss, and No Faction loss. You can change the battle location from an arena to any other zone, so long as both contenders agree.

12) Four straight forfeits causes you to be deleted from the Ladder.

Spectator and General Rulings

Guides and GMs are considered judges and therefore restricted from being attacked. Anyone found attacking a GM or Guide may be banished from the Arena or death touched without warning. The Arena is off limits to unauthorized PvP 15 minutes before and 15 minutes after the event. This is to ensure that the contestants and spectators are safe.

Spectators shall remain seated and not casting during the match. There is Zero Tolerance for disruption of an Event including Casting, Shooting Arrows or Throwing items, whether the disruption is aimed at a participant or a spectator.

Spectators shall not use /shout for any reason, but use /ooc instead. This is so that those involved in the match can use /shout, and the /ooc channel can be turned off to reduce distraction to the contestants involved. Use of /shout at anytime can result in a silence command being issued, rendering the player unable to use any form of communication, or banishment from the Arena.

VI may limit the amount of people in the zone to control conditions of lag or frame rate for the participants. The event shall start open seating, but may close off at any time. The Event Staff may use any sort of decision on who stays and who leaves to ensure the event is a success. This should not be implemented until a minimum of 150 players in the zone has been reached.

AoE Spells ARE allowed in game. This has the potential of killing off some of the younger spectators. If you are killed by one of these spells, please wait for a GM or Guide to give you a summons into the Arena.

The EULA and Rules of Conduct bind all spectators and contestants as set forth by SOE and Verant Interactive, Inc.

Spectators and Contestants found breaking the rules will have actions taken against their account including suspension or possible banning.

Thank you
The Events Team
