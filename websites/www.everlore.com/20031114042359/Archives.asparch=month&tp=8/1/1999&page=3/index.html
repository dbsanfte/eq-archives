Attention fellows of Erudin
David A Clifton [8/12/1999-1:39:45 PM]  Comments[0]

--Vallon Zek-- Attention fellows of Erudin, The Erudite Senate is now forming. This is a fully democratic body, devoted to bringing together the brothers and sisters of Erudin for the benefit of Odus, and Humanity as a whole. Every Erudian of the 5th Level or above has earned the right to a Seat on the Senate; each and every Senate action is dependant wholly on the supporting votes of the Seats. Note that the structure of the Senate is not yet finalised, and is of course subject to the opinions of the Seats. We urge all Erudians to take up their Seats as soon as possible. As a cursory measure, the Senate will be electing eleven Ambassadors, one to each of the other eleven races of Norrath, which will take place in around seven days time. At the time of guild creation, no other such body exists for the unity of Erudin. It is not the task of the Senate to impose mock rule over Odus, but to peform a political and organisational role, primarily to mobilise effective armies in concorde with our fellow Humans, create truces with other races should the Seats of the Senate decide upon it, and to engage in the trading of otherwise unobtainable items with other nations. Brothers and sisters of Erudin, we look forward to hearing your voices in the Senate. -- Please contact 'Erudal' in-game to take up your Seat, or find your nearest 'officer'. Please note that the official creator of this body, Administrator Erudal, is simply that: an administrator, and not a leader. The Senate is dedicated to allowing as many Erudians as possible to take up their seat, and so will happily give 'officer' status (i.e. the ability to recruit new members) to those members of a reliable nature.

Well you're in luck my friend, because the Trade Winds Fair is coming!
