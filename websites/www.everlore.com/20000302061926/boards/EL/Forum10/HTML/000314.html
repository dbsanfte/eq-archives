I had a level 14 Magician, have a level 25 Monk, have a level 14 Shaman, have a level 12 Paladin, and Have a level 18 Erudin Enchanter.

The Enchanter is a lot of fun if you like being tricky, having the ability to look like nearly any race you want.... and want to have a great line of buffs.

The reason I do not play him continuously is that I love to solo. Enchanters at level 18 can solo High Blue and Even Deepwater Caimen if they have a good pet and have fear handy. With an even pet I took out an even Deepwater Crocodile. So, you can see that soloing is possible. What I didn't like was that I had about 20 minutes of down time after each single battle to regain HP and Mana. I know, I know, that's not unusual when you solo but dammit I don't like it.

My new character, the Shaman... is very fun and yes I solo him.
