First off Clerics do not "suck" no class "sucks" it is all about your preference in the game really. We (all classes) have our roles in the world of ever quest. Furthermore no class is more desireable at early or later levels to solo over another it all depends on where you hunt. You as a cleric if hunting undead will have an advantage over warriors hunting undea. So if you want to solo, dont want to "suck" and want "good stuff" as a cleric go to areas where you can fight undead and you friend will be equal to a warrior in effectiveness.
Far too often people make assumptions that one class is better than another and that just is not true. Recently I had a logn debate with a loud mouthed Warrior on Kartana server about how without him our party of 5 peopel would all be dead in a battle. I reminded him my hp as a shaman Barbarian were nearly as high as his and that with buffs and spells to weaken and slow down my opponent when "tanking" made me even closer to being a fill in for him. Mind you He started this and was bragging else I would have held my tongue. At this point he started spewing his stats (hp,ac,dmg ect) and I again reminded him I never said I was AS good as a tank I said I was nearly as good and he was three levels higher than I. In truth even with those levels we were not far apart in stats. That is with me serving as a tank. I then asked him if he could cast Sow? Can you Bind? Can youheal a party meber who was near death quickly and effectively? ( I know the can bandage wounds but so can I and I can also cast heal and inner fire) I asked if he could cure poison? Provide infravision? I continued to rattle off a dozen things which of course I know he would HAVE to say no to.
My point being all classes have strengths and weaknesses. In later levels (I was 15th at the time) My ability to stand up and tank will eventually fall to the weayside and our differences will be more noticeable. However still I will change and become better in spells and other was (alchamy) and he will just get more powerfull in melee. All classes can solo in early goings and again all classes have advantages over others in cases. Take your cleric to East Karana at later levels and fight undead reavers bythe river in a group mabye till 15th or so then see how bad you "suck". You before all others will get your kil on the things and a good amount of loot as well. Trust me Clerics do not suck. There are plenty of meat head warriors out there who think they are the main reason a group succedes. Not saying much. "I rule cause I can take a beating" Well I think I am a good teamate because I can provide an edge to my friends before a battle, help them live through the battle by healing and weakinging our opponent, and after the battle heal them up to start again. If I manage my casting correctly, and do my job well we will all reap the rewards. It is harder to play a charecter that needs to think, but also more rewarding in my opinion. I could just click on kick,bash, and taunt all day and never have to think for myself. I could count on others to do the truely hard work and manage their mana and casting and to keep me going so my time isnt spent waiting all night. However again...in my opinion a more well rounded thinking mans chaerceter is for me. Thats why I am a Shaman of Halas.
