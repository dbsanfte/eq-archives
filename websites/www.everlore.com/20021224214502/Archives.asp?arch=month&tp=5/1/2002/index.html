Changes to Firiona Vie and Sullon Zek
Korenth [5/1/2002-2:04:09 PM]  Comments[25]

Here is the latest from the Developer Boards

Firiona Vie was designed in an effort to create a roleplaying ruleset on an EverQuest server. It was a valiant experiment, but it seems to have fallen short of expectations. Unfortunately the restrictions to both grouping and communication on this server have prevented the population from reaching the mass required to be a self-sustaining environment. With the tremendous size of the EverQuest game world, too small of a population on server is just as devastating to the health of that server as too large of a population.
So we've decided to change some of the rules on Firiona Vie in an attempt to bring more players to the server. The focus for the server is still roleplaying, that won't change. But we're going to remove some of the rules that make playing on the server inconvenient and restrict roleplaying. We will, however, be keeping the following rules in tact:

* /ooc will remain unavailable to remind people that acting in character is strongly recommended.

* The Common language will remain out, to help maintain the feeling of race-based community and to allow people to continue to roleplay someone with a native tongue.

* The Trivial Loot Code and all of the special rules for no-drop items will remain. This has worked out nicely for the sense of community on the server.


However, we will be removing the following restrictions:

* Anonymous/roleplay flags will no longer be required. They will remain available, but forcing anonymity on characters is causing undue problems finding groups and other such things that are essential to enjoyable gameplay.

* The language learning limit/speed restrictions will be removed. While the idea of limited languages seemed like a good idea, this is another item that was just making it too hard to actually play the game. Roleplayers that don’t want their characters to be overly literate always have the option to play their characters that way.

* /shout and /auction will return to unrestricted use to allow easier grouping and selling. People are welcome to filter those channels if they prefer not to see them, but the benefits of having them enabled is just too great. /ooc will remain disabled on this server. Obviously, acting in character on any channel is strongly recommended.

* Alignment restrictions will be removed. Forced restrictions for grouping make playing the game too much of a chore. Characters, however, may always decide who they wish to group with based on any factors the character perceives. If your Dark Elf won't group with a paladin, he doesn't have to (and shouldn't). If your cleric of Quellious wants to heal a necromancer because healing people is the right thing to do for him, then you should be able to do so. But not allowing these things is a restriction on your ability to play that character 'in-character', and a real hindrance to general gameplay.

* We will be raising the number of characters available to each account to three. We've added a new race, class and a few race/class combinations since Firiona Vie was launched and it only seems fair to give folks on that server a chance to play them.


Again, it's important to understand that this is still a roleplaying preferred server. The rules that we are changing were often as much of a restriction to one's ability to roleplay as they were a help. And the artificial barriers to grouping, especially the trouble created in just finding a group, need to be removed to allow people to actually play the game.

We will also be increasing the number of characters that can be created on Sullon Zek to three per account to allow players on that server to create new characters of the new class and races made available since the server was introduced.

All of these changes will occur with the next patch on Wednesday, May 8th, 2002.

Alan
 

 

Network News: RPG Lore Design Changes
Georgia [5/1/2002-3:56:59 PM]  Comments[23]

Before everyone gets upset or confused, I thought I would post some network news to let everyone know about the design changes, currently taking place across the entire network.

 As you know, we rely on advertising to offset some of the costs for running this site. UGO is now requiring affiliates to follow a strict standard of template requirements.  We are now using this standard to try and be able to cover more of the expenses associated with the Lore Network   and have gone to using their template requirements into our mainframe.

All of the same information is there... the only thing that is changing is the color schemes and overall design. We will sorely miss our old designs, but we must embrace our new, collective designs.

I know some people may be having trouble with the color schemes of individual sites. I will look into each site and adjust colors as deemed necessary, for the greater good. Please feel free to email me with any issues that you are having, color scheme suggestions, etc. You may also feel free to reply with a comment to this news thread.

Again, thank you for your continued support! I hope the transition is smooth!

Priestess Azmirelazela of Darkmoon wrote an amazing GM event review.  Check it out:

Dragoon Xlot's Rampage - Part I - By Azmirelazela Imirie`Loki, Priestess of Darkmoon

Azmire set her ice grinder aside, taking out her tome and shield of Koada`Dal bones. The shield protected little from the rain that had started falling, but the wisdom it contained aided her power moreso for the healing that her brethren needed against the one-eyed giants of the ocean. A binding of Darkmoon's army had set up a small camp on the peninsula of the great isle, and Azmire rested a moment, meditating for more of the Father's righteous power.

Gwythian was the first to contact them, using the guild's crystal, he spoke of a great Dragoon, one of Neriak's Finest, Xlot, calling for a slaughter of the pales of the commonlands. Azmirelazela closed her eyes, a smile of pure Hate's extacy coming to her lips. She looked at the party around her, and stood, rising to rally His Children to the Dragoon's defense, no need though, Darkmoon stood, swords and magics clutched in hand and mouth as Neccron's portal opened to Northern Ro, for access to the commonlands through the vast tunnel in the mountain of sand.

The young Oracle, Shenk, called the spirit of wolves to aid their short but haste-required journey, the Swashbuckler's trading post was the rendevous point, as the Usher of Chaos declared. The march or Darkmoon was crisp, the Commonlands had never seen a slaughter like that which was about to come. Hate pulsed in Azmirelazela as she saw the blood filled sky, the smile of extacy would not leave her lips the entire eve to come.

The rendevous with all Darkmoon in the area ended with orders to keep the Dragoon alive and destroy all pales within range. The Guard set out to seek the Freeport Militia and it's Sergeant and Azmirelazela, Tenyu, and Shenk we placed on post at the end of the tunnel to aid to the Dragoon with heals, summonings, and Azmirelazela with mana relief. A perfect plan, the Dragoon's operation 'would' succeed, Innoruuk's Ancient Army would merge forces to see it through.

Pale blood filled the tunnel as Azmirelazela headed down the long corridor, screams from the fleeing day-dwellers echoed in the dry heat-filled air. Breathing deeply and listening to the melodious howls of the falling, the Priestess of Innoruuk made her way to where Tenyu and Shenk had found a good spot for spying the movements of the forces.

Donning her Regent Symbol of Innoruuk, boldly, she approached the Dragoon, letting him know of her position in the operation as assigned, and that she would not leave his side during the battle. He nodded curtly, eyeing her, the question he placed to all he slew did not form, he knew she was Darkmoon and loyal to Innoruuk's Will. With a salute, the Dragoon and Priestess had an understanding, and for the entire battle to come, the hand of the Father and Creator would be hers to heal the forces of Hate gathered.

The General's Reward - by Synne the Unweildy, General of Darkmoon

(Synne raised her face up toward the afternoon sun. She stood at the entrance of the tunnel that led to the shady swashbuckler's lair and took a moment to just revel in the sensation befalling her. Her eyes softly closed and her other senses opened to take in the other stimulus around her. The first to interject itself were the deathcries of the fallen pales as Dragoon Xlot and Darkmoon gave them their final rewards. She could catch each in turn as the fallen were struck down by Neriak's finest and the dark army she was General of. It brought a soft smile to her lips.)

(Next to her came the sweet and tangy odor of blood and death. It flared her nostrils and she slowly circled her head to take in the aromas. The rusty taint of spilled blood, the singeing aroma of ozone as spells of high magic were flung at their targets, even the bittersweet saltiness of the sweat that permeated the warriors fighting for their lives.)

(Finally, her skin felt the tremors of sensation that came flowing across it. The feel of the sun falling down upon her, the last sight some of the fallen pales would see. The gusts of wind that blew softly across the battlefield and brought her the scent of victory over the pales. And deep down she could feel it, the spread of fear and hate across the lands of the Commonlands. The sensation crept across her flesh as the pales began to sense their impending doom and futility.)

(A young Vah Shir warrior, battered and bloodied from battle, crawled toward the tunnel, trying to escape the Teir Dal onslaught. General Synne peered down at the feline, a soft smirk etching over her lips as it glared up at Synne in a cold last defiance, sure she would strike him down. Instead, Synne picked up a chitin shield and dusted it off, tossing it down before the young warrior.)

"Grow up, grow strong. It would be a shame to have something else kill you before we had another meeting..." Synne mocked at the downed warrior.

(The warrior growled out a cursing to Synne, but the General merely chuckled and turned on her heel, striding away as the victorious battle over the pales drew ever onward this day.)
Screams of Pale Ones - by Azmirelazela Imirie'Loki - Priestess of Darkmoon
Horrified.....Screaming......The Dragoon was unstoppable.....Screaming.......Terror......Darkmoon must die!.....The Priestess, what is she doing!....Noooo!.....Children fleeing.....The great pales were gathering......Dragoon Xlot shouts: Infidel! Let this be a lesson to those the interfere with the Indigo Brotherhood!........Must die!.......He's healed!!

Sweat that would have poured from Priestess Azmirelazela was not seen, her focus and void of all around her but the lifeforce of the Dragoon succumbed that fatigue. When the shouts of the mob grew louder, she would rise, and pour her energies into his life's veins.

Dragoon Xlot suffered no wounds, no fatigue, his blessings of Hate poured endlessly from the Modulation Rod and Oracle enhanced priestess. Completely anihilated was the Freeport Militia of East Commonlands, all merchants out of business, dead by their wagons. The Commonlands was a mirage of blood, and the sky rained blood, over the night sky fell a darkest of moons, black as a silouette. The Teir Dal forces claimed victory, a sweet evening concluded for now.

To Darkmoon, the Dragoon stood in a military stance, his salute briskly sharp to all that were gathered. I shall return, Darkmoon, our operation is not finished! More pales must die!"

Azmirelazela looked exhausted, and Shenk attended to her with refreshing stamina blessings and heals of some power. Before her, Dragoon Xlot kneeled, "I'm afraid I have nothing to show you my gratitude, Priestess."

"It is my duty and honor to protect His Children." Azmirelazela said calmly, I am honored to do so. That is enough." He left soon after thanking the rest of the Teir Dal gathered.

Dragoon Xlot's Rampage - Part II - by Azmirelazela Imirie`Loki, Priestess of Darkmoon

Darkmoon preyed on much more that evenings to follow the original onslaught of the Queen's Dragoon, but Azmirelazela stayed close to him, as close as was possible, he kept his view hidden even from her. Without trying she could reveal him, the pale kin of the deceased followed her, the eyes of them watching for any sudden movement to any location, regardless, Azmire had fun taunting them now, the blood of their fallen on her, the health of the Dragoon made sure of it.

She stayed close to the forest, her instinct for battle and tactics knew he was strongest in the Commonlands, where many pales of all age passed through.

One full day had passed, and before the sun set in desert's sands, the Dragoon had returned. Though Azmirelazela was the first Priestess on hand, she was soon to be joined by Saelru and another Priest of the Spires. Darkmoon was ready for the attack this eve and had every resource available for keeping the warparty alive and battleworthy.

Screams and please of the pales were answered this time by Freeport's "Finest" but not much was left of them to strike down after Adus and Tenyu had a go at them, the pales had no defense, no Priests worthy to stand for them, their deaths were even aided by other pales in bloodlust, perhaps confusion in battle. Azmirelazela gawked at their weakness.

Approaching quickly by foot, The Dragoon Xlot and his Captain now headed steadily towards the Darkmoon encampment, a rendevous they expected, but the smiles on their battleworn faces did not show it.

The Dragoon and Captain had brought a Regent Priestess of the Spires with them, he blessings allowed for Azmirelazela's blessings to override them though, and the Priestess of Darkmoon prepared her incantations of Hate. Resolution and the favor of Innoruuk poured over the Hateful trio, upon sight of the three now needing strength, Azmire silently thanked Innoruuk reinforcements for healing were on their way.

The battle was waged slowly, typical of the pales when gathering both brains and forces. One by one at first they attacked; Barons, Evokers, "great" Heirophants, and Oracles, and one by one witnessed the superiority of the Neriak forces.

The sky rained blood. Another glorious evening of day dwelling pales finding their oblivion. Hate filled the air. Soon the masses of pales realized the link of one Priestess could be broken, and the Dragoon's Priestess began taking damage.

Saelru and Azmirelazela worked quickly, each on one side of the Mage Lord for rods, both making themselves oblivious to all trades from those not in their binding to avoid the silly pales who decided trading was their best defense against the Darkmoon Army.

The Captain! Azmirelazela spun around to face the Captain, he was bleeding badly and headed toward her to put life back in his body. Murmuring, chanting, Father please spare him! The Teir`Dal Captain is completely healed. The smirk on his lips dissapated as he turned to face his human attacker with a new stamina, laughing chaotically into the night's air as the blade struck clear through the human's chest.

The Teir`Dal Captain shouts: Let this death be a lesson to all who would interfere with Neriak's Finest!

Pulling his blade from the chest of quite dead human, the Captain motioned her to follow him, at a pace of terror. She saw why as she came around the corner of the tunnel, the Dragoon had near 20 to 30 pales on his backside and 10 more to his front. Stopping as short as she could of him while maintaining the range of her spell, she quickly cast superior heal and watched his body pulse to life only a moment before chanting the complete healing power of Innoruuk.

Azmirelazela shouts: It is the Will of Innoruuk, Dragoon Xlot is Completely Healed!!

The exhaustion was evident on their faces, as they watched Azmire and the Captain close in on the battle, Darkmoon masses were coming from the rear, and the pales knew their disadvantage.

The Captain licked the blood off his sword, tearing into the Veteran warrior on the Dragoon's backside. Back to back the Dragoon and his Captain fought the mob off, and throwing the pale onslaught into fragments, the Dragoon darted toward Nektulos, acknowledging Azmirelazela and Saelru's low magics and seeing the opportunity to grant his Captain a heal that otherwise would go to him, he knew the Commonlands and felt confident he could evade the mob while the Captain sliced a few more day dwellers down. The plan worked, the Captain regained his health as needed, and the Dragoon slowly healed while watching the pales chase the undead armies he released around them.

Slowly, the Priestess' meditated, the pales were less by the minute, only a few remained standing, but the Captain was slowly losing ground, if the Dragoon did not return to alleviate some of concentration on him, Azmirelazela and Saelru would lose the Captain.

Trying to muster the magics to place her healing hands on the Captain failed and almost a split second before her heal would have negated it, the Teir`Dal Captain fell, mortally wounded, a split second after, however, just as the angry 2 or 3 left standing turned to face Darkmoon, the Dragoon came blazing around the corner, sword raised with two hands clear above his head and in one massive strike took the three would be assailants down.

May 8th Patch Message
Xott the Evoker [5/9/2002-8:28:18 AM]  Comments[3]

Well, I finally did it right. After incorrectly posting the patch message yesterday, here it is

** Items **

- We've made some changes that will cause a few proccing items to be out of charges after the patch. Players with such items will need to petition and ask a GM to swap the item out with a new one
- Focus items using the "Naki's Pernicity" effects should now be working as intended
- Focus items that affect a spell's duration should now work for clickable items
- It should be impossible to equip an item that will reduce your hit points by more than your maximum hit points. Also, hit point reduction items should not lower hit points below 1
- Tinkered Compass now adds the appropriate bonus to the sense heading skill
- Hit point bonuses on food items should be working

** Spells **

- Higher level familiars should once again survive when their masters go invisible, Abscond or Evac
- Fixed a bug that was preventing the Lesser Familiar mana regeneration component from working
- Familiars no longer cause damage to their master upon death
- Familiar buff remains after the caster uses the /pet get lost command to remove the visual representation
- Torrent of Fatigue should be working properly now
- Allure of the Wild has had its level cap raised to make it a useful upgrade
- Umbra has been improved to give a slightly higher AC benefit
- A character can no longer trade with an NPC when invisible. Character must turn visible before starting the trade
- Characters can no longer scribe a scroll that they can't cast due to deity restrictions
- Invisibility should now remain after a link death
- Theft of Thought will no longer give mana when cast on PC classes that do not have mana (in PvP or in a duel)
- Drain/Share spells can no longer be cast on players on blue servers, and can no longer be cast on group members on PvP servers. Spells Affected: Succession of Shadows, Crippling Claudication, Mind Wrack, Degeneration, Zevfeer's Theft of Vitae, Torrent of Hate, Torrent of Fatigue, Torrent of Pain
- Spirit Armor scroll no longer lists Shadowknights as a class that can use it (they couldn't anyway)
- Bard AE songs now have vertical limit of 50 feet. This is also the case for Mass Group Buff
- Gating and porting while in tree form should no longer cause crashes
- Removed all "Begins to cast X spell" text from procs
- Resurrection request box will now reset if you open another window while it is up, preventing a problem where a character is unable to be resurrected if he has the res box up and he loots a mob or opens a trade window
- Fixed a bug with lifetaps that would cause people around a lifetap to see, "You feel drained..." instead of "Fippy feels drained..."
- Issues with Healing seeming to be delayed have been fixed (see Spell Filters)
- Fixed a bug that was causing "soandso's song ends" to show up as "he's song ends" if the bard was targeted, "it's song ends" if not targeted
- Divine Glory received a small upgrade to make it more in line with the spell series

** Spell Filters **

- Issues with healing seeming to be delayed should be fixed. The problem was with filters that allowed players to ignore spells that were hitting them. The following changes were needed to fix this issue
- Player characters will now always see spells that hit them and spells they cast
- Players with the Group option set for PC spells will see the start spell casting messages for people in their group and they will also see when member of their group are hit with spells
- Players with the ALL option will still see all PC spells and spell hits

** Spell Stacking **

General stacking changes: Some buffs were not being allowed to stack which now will stack, and other buffs were being allowed to stack which should not have.

For example, many stacking problems with spells that have a See Invisible or Levitate component are now gone (e.g. Dead Man Floating), while a number of stacking exploits that granted too much regeneration, too much offense (ATK), too large of a damage shield, or too many hit points have been fixed. Here are some of the details:

- Vision abilities (natural or spell-granted) should no longer cause spells to fail to take hold
- Bard songs should in general overwrite fewer spells
- Harness of Spirit no longer stacks with spells that grant Strength or Dexterity boosts. It was never intended to do so
- Harness of Spirit and Avatar should now stack properly
- Call of the Predator and Warder's Protection should no longer stack
- Arch Lich should no longer overwrite innate racial vision abilities
- Levitate and Dead Man Floating should no longer conflict
- Dead Man Floating should no longer conflict with any vision abilities (natural or spell granted)
- Spirit of Eagle should no longer cancel Levitation/Levitate spells
- Fixed a stacking exploit with Armor of Protection and Armor of the Risen
- Focus of Spirit should no longer overwrite Visions of Grandeur
- Focus of Spirit should now stack with the Whistling Fists effect
- "Circle of" Druid spells should no longer stack with their Resist counterparts.
- Shroud of Spirits and the Cloak of the Akheva should stack properly now
- Cantata of Soothing should no longer overwrite Clarity spells
- Cantata and Chorus of Replenishment should no longer overwrite Acumen
- Bladecoat no longer overwrites Clarity II
- Lower level druid group buffs should now be less likely to overwrite higher level buffs
- Avatar and Force of Nature will no longer stack
- Ancient Feral Avatar should now stack properly

** Alternate Advancement **

- Improved Familiar should now be better than Greater Familiar
- Fixed a bug with Permanent Illusion that was causing the caster to see his illusions drop when zoning, even if others continued to see the illusion
- Trolls and Iksar no longer receive double regeneration ability from AA abilities
- The Archetype skill Casting Deftness will no longer supercede the Class specific Quick Buff casting time reducing skill. Note that at this time there is a maximum benefit to casting time bonuses of a 50% reduction
- Casting deftness was changed to reflect the increases presented by the in-game help (5, 15 and 25%)
- Turn Summoned should now work on all summoned creatures, not just pets. The text should correctly say that there is only one level for this ability
- Improved Reclaim Energy now works with the epic pet
- Fixed a crash bug caused by Call Corpse
- Alternate Advancement abilities that affect a spell's duration should now work for clickable items
- Quick Summon should now work for Call of the Hero and Manifest Elements
- Quick Summon also changed to match the benefits presented by the in-game help

** Skills **

- Increased the velocity tolerance for the Hide skill. This may slightly improve the functionality of the Evade skill
- Poison should no longer be interrupted
- Sneak only breaks when a character is hit, not when he hits someone
- Fixed a bug that was causing a problem when Pick Pockets was used before Beg had refreshed. Beg and Pick Pocket use the same timer, so now using Beg or Pick Pocket will cause both buttons to gray out
- A message is now displayed and skills are properly reset when a rogue breaks sneak and/or hide because he is looting a corpse
- Beastlords should function like other hybrids when casting in melee

** Zones **

- The Hole now uses Kunark-level Mez/Charm/Taunt rules
- Petrified humerus should now be available
- Zones where mounts cannot be summoned will now remove mounts when they enter the zone
- High level NPCs can now be taunted in Cazic Thule
- Random frame lag in Guktop and Oasis should be fixed
- Fixed a pathing problem in Tenebrous Mountains
- Fixed a pathing bug in Paludal Caverns
- Fixed pathing exploits in Chardok
- Fixed a pathing exploit in Cazic Thule
- Fixed a pathing exploit in Acrylia
- Fixed a pathing bug in the Tower of Frozen Shadow
- Fixed a pathing bug in Fungus Grove
- Fixed a spot where characters could get stuck in Twilight Sea
- Moved the Permafrost 'safe point' closer to the zone line
- Addressed an issue with an aggressive NPC that appeared outside of the Arx Building in Sanctus Seru
-Modified the Vyzh`dra the Cursed spawn trigger to make the various incarnations more accessible
-Modified several NPCs in the temple of Ssraeshza to address the disparity between melee and caster effectiveness in the zone

** Quests **

- Adjustments have been made to the 8th Coldain Prayer Shawl quest
- High Priestess Alexandriana should be working properly now

** Tradeskills **

- Added over 1,000 new tradeskill recipes for all levels of skill, along with many recipe books. A few of the vendors of Norrath might be able to get you started on your way
- Added Mortar and Pestle to a Thurgadin merchant
- Rubbing Alcohol is now available from a vendor in Thurgadin

** Guilds **

- Fixed many bugs with the guild system, for example: GMs can now rename guilds without the need to destroy and recreate them, guilds can have names that share the first 15 characters with another guild (they couldn't before) and people should no longer experience random guild kickouts when a guild on their server is deleted/reformed.

** Pets **

- A character may now have a horse, a pet and an Eye of Zomm at the same time
- /pet attack range has been limited to prevent its use as a targeting tool. Asking pets to attack targets outside the range will generate a "not a legal target" message
- Pets will once again tell you who they are attacking by sending its master a /tell
- Pet attack animations should now be working for all pets

** Chat Channels **

- Players can specify a chat channel as their default channel using the /channel commands (e.g. /channel chat mychannel)
- The 'old' method for setting the default channel is also now saved in the INI file
- Kick and Invite should be working for chat channels

** Horses **

- Potions now work while on a horse
- Skeletons can now ride horses (meaning: PCs should be able to mount and ride horses while changed into a skeleton)

** Miscellaneous **

- Fixed a bug that was causing the AFK flag to sometimes not be visible to players entering a zone after it was turned on
- Made some adjustments to factions to make Beastlords, Druids and Rangers (as well as small adjustments for Vah Shir) a little better liked by animals (those that don't adhere to a faction)
- No updates are sent when the player character is not moving, saving resources. We've also increased the rate that position updates are made by moving player characters, using up some of those saved resources but making movement a bit smoother
- Stationary NPCs should no longer warp when they aggro/summon
- NPCs who shout names while they Death Touch should be fixed
- Teleports arriving in some zones should spread the arrivals out a bit more. Affected zones are Nexus, Skyfire, Emerald Jungle, Butcherblock Mountains, South Ro, Greater Faydark, Tox, North Karana, West Commons and Lavastorm
- Fixed the bug that was preventing players from seeing the "your spell is interrupted" or the NPCs spell interruption message
- Fixed the issue with boats depositing players at the safe point of the zone after zoning
- Fixed the strange floating NPC bug (seeing NPCs floating in the air after zoning)
- /cry is now working
- /consent %t should allow the consented character to drag your corpse
- Target Nearest NPC (F8 by default) has been fixed.

There were also many changes to Sullon Zek, and Firiona Vie. Click Here to visit Everquest Live Site for more details

Dragoon Xlot and Captain Xli (Part III)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pain searing the Dragoon's face, Azmirelazela reached out with Innoruuk's hands, a pulse of blue light radiating from his very essence, with a hateful grin, he switched his sword from his right hand, to avoid touching her with a bloodied hand, wet with the juices of a pale one...from ripping it's heart our moments before. "You did not lose him, an Indigo Brother is not lost so easily. The Father favor us this day!"

A Week Later~

From dead sleep, the Priestess woke, the Captain's summons to her brought little time for preparation. He was stronger and had resolved in his link with the Priestess Azmire, favor to an Indigo sworn from Innoruuk could only come from a clergy of his worship, and he knew it was Azmire who cried to him moments before he fell, he called to her now, the Dragoon was preparing a defense, and in support of the King's Guard, the Queen's army would follow.

The battle would be in their homeland, too far the pales had pushed out of curiosity towards Neriak, the Dragoon and Captain on post at the Gates held them at bay, but with the border to the Inner Dread Guard's less defensive domain quite near, pales were seeping into a foreign quarter at a ratio the Dragoon and General both felt should be terminated.

"To Nektulos!" The General Synne cried, her calls already answered by those in the surrounding area with haste-driven urgency. Azmire knew beforehand though, and was ready for whatever the Dragoon's orders through the Captain were. Shenk had the Darkmoon Guard for healing, and Azmire was confident the experienced Oracle and nearby Warlocks could keep her brethren well above survival stadards. They were on the offense in their own forest, no pales threatened the sanctity of the Guards present this eve, Darkmoon and the Indigo Dread Guards made sure of it.

Pales swarmed in, herded by the scandalous calls of darkness, the lure of shadows that would bring their fate to it's boil, a rage slowly encompassed them all as they entered, taking cover quickly at Lavastorm, where they had druids and healers waiting on the other side for relief of their health.

The Dragoon took a look around at the helpless pales, so unknowing of their expected fall to his dance of blades. The felt overwhelmingly advantageous, and the prelude to a dance hastily took the arms off of his would be assailant, who soon crawled to the 'safety' of Lavastorm.

His accomplice Indigo sworn was not present yet, and he knew the young Captain had aspirations of challenging the number of pales to fall at his blade. He would humor the Captain. The Dragoon was skilled at day-dweller killing, and had learned from only years of their bleeding, Captain Xli, his loyal Captain, would not win the bet for sheer number this eve, besides the Dragoon had a head start, his Captain was late!

Last night around 11:00pm CST, 2 named Griffons showed up in North Karana causing havok around the spires and the druid ring.
