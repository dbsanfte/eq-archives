allright i have seen posts over and over about smithing and how to do it and what do. so i figure i will share my knowledge with you. if you want to be a smith go to freeport. why?? because everything you need is in two zones. North Freeport and East freeport. yes you can problably buy ore in Highhold pass, but why. you still need molds. if you want a list of where everything is for blacksmithing then go to
www.eqtraders.com

this site will help on your supplies

this is the course of blacksmithing. first off before you start research. meaning get on the web and find out what are the recipes and where supplies are. know a little bit before you start. second smithin is expensive between 75 to 150pp to get to be a master smith. and even at skill 100 i dont recommended doing banded. why?? because failure is expensive and it sucks. so save you money and dont start making banded armor til skill 115.

alright here is your lesson. first off you can spend your time doing finding rusty weapons or and sharpening til it is trivial. i recommend putting 20 points in smithing and going from there. next go to north freeport and buy about 80 sharpening stones. then head back to east freeport and go to hallards resale and buy 2H rusty swords. only buy about 8 at a time. to many and you wont be able to move. then go to the forge and sharpen these. then sell them back. cost per sword is about 3s to 4s per sword. sharpening rustyies will take you to 30 or 31. only 2h rusty sword will do this.

now you will have to make metal bits. they suck. this is time consuming. because you will be making about 60 to 80 at a time. so have about 6 backpacks on you and fill up with water about a full backpacks worth and a pieces of ore and make metal bits. they will fail but as your skill goes up it does not happpen as much. you will use your making metal bits until you start making banded

next is scalers. make scalers till they are trivial. about 40 or 41. sell the files and large scalers molds back to the vendors. at about skill 37 take the large lids molds you make and use them to make large containers. instead of selling them. once scalers are trivial make large metal containers. these are are trivial at 48.

next is cookie cutters. make cookie cutters til trivail these are trivail at 56.

now you make laterns. these are trivial at 65. but i dont recommmed doin that. make them til 60 then move to skewers. why?? because laterns suck. high failure rate and low skill advancement. skewers have high advancement and decent failure rate. you can make these til trivial at 114. no you are ready for banded. it is is expensive to fail because each piece of sheet metal costs over 1pp. and so do some of the molds.

ok molds you need are found in East Freeport. the ore and sharpening stones are found in North freeport. buy water in North freeport at some blue store next to the bank.

btw if you dont have any charisma increasing items. get some. necklace, bracers, earring, ring (but very very expensive). i have a max charisma of 108 and it sure helps. also other stats like wisdom are supposed to reduce failure rate and intelligence is related to your learning rate

good luck.
