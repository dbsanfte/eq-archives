I've dueled a couple of times, but I've not played an enchanter on PvP. Entrance is the key to victory my friend. You could do just that and have them begging to let them free. hehe. "Please let me go, I don't wanna fight you, you're too good for me." hehe Remember that you can debuff them when they are entranced. For casters, take down their buffs first. Strip enchantment works well. That's to take care of their personal shields and sow and such things. Entrance them then. Now use Mana Sieve them 'til they can't possibly have any mana. Entrance and Clarity should ensure you have enough mana to do this. Make sure you Tashan your target too. Then lower their str with Insepid Weakness and other spells of that nature. Make sure they stack though. After that you should have no prob taking them down. Engage in melee and flux them if they try to get of a tp or gate. Beat them down and dd them to death. You can use your dot now which also lowers str making it impossible for them to move. Remember that a stunned target can also memorize spells. Don't let them get cancel magic off on themselves. Easy kill if their friends don't show up. Fighters you handle the same way except you don't need to mana sieve them. Happy hunting.

Be very careful when fight a bard that is around lvl 25. Trying to despell a bards buffs is fruitless because he can have them all back up in a few seconds. Also trying to debuff the bards stats won't work because they have a cancel magic song. I can't really supply the information on how to beat a bard, because that is the character I play, but I know full well how to defend myself as a bard.

Bards also have a song called bellow that does around 12 points of damage, but it also interupts casting. If you have any spells that take longer than 3 seconds, you may have a tough time getting them off when a bard is constantly using below and hitting you with weapons.

If each player knows his class well, a bard/enchanter fight could be a long drawn out one.
