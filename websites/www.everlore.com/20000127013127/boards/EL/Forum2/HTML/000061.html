Forage is limited to Wood Elves, Rangers, druids and bards. To my knowledge at least.

Also, if your racial forage skill is already higher than what your level would give, it will not improve. In other word, if a wood elf ranger put one point in it at level 3,
he will not see improvement until level 8.

The formula for the maximum for that skill, for a ranger is lvl*5 + 5 so, at level 3,
it should be 20 but a woodie already has 40 in it so, don't exept any change yet. At level 8, the maximum becomes 45 and that's when you'll see an improvement. At least, that's what happened to my ranger.
