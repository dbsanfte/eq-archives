For lvls 12 through 16 go to everfrost and fight ice boned skeletons. Deep inside the underwater cave is a house with 3 icebones in it. Thats not the one you want to fight at lvl 12, you'll die too easily. Not far from the house with the 3 icebones(this is also where the rod for the staff of the wheel quest spawns) is another small hut, only 2 icebones hang there. After you break the spawn it becomes managable and they con white to high yellow at 12. The spawn time is 6 or 7 minutes, and the exp is insane. I went from level 12 to 16 in 8 hours of playing.
