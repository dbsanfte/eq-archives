Packet Loss and Server Status
trindalc [4/27/1999-1:58:19 PM]  Comments[0]

Here is what John Smedley, Verant President and CEO, had to say about the recent packet loss problems:

Hello All,

As many of you are seeing we are having some Packet Loss issues right now. These issues will be addressed tomorrow morning beginning at 9:30am. We will be doing some maintenence on our Router here, so the game will be unavailable for about an hour. This solution should take care of things quite handily.

In addition tomorrow we will be moving the Karana server over to CERFnet, as well as setting up the newest server Rodcet Nife. We tried to do this today, but we had some logistics issues with CERFnet and we had to delay this until tomorrow.

I don't have an exact ETA on Rodcet Nife, except to say that it will be tomorrow sometime. You would be amazed how many people were sitting in chat today just waiting for this server to come up ;)

We do apologize for the inconvienence this downtime will cause, however we think that everyone will be a lot happier once this Packet Loss goes away.

John Smedley

Thanks to John and the entire EverQuest Team!
