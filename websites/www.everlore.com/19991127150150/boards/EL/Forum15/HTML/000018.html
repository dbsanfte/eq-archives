You can never 'click' a second gray icon to play two songs at once. You can however, twist songs. For example, start and stop CoD via the gray button and you will notice the effects of the song continue after you stopped the song. As soon as you stop the song, click on another, like Selo's, and before CoD has finished, you have Selo's little boot on the screen... Hence, you are playing two songs at once. You can juggle two fairly easy, and up to three if you work on the timing.

Song duration depends on the song. CoD will keep pulsing unless you turn it off. Some songs will pulse only once and be gone.

Experiment and have fun!!
