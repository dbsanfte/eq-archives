Raid on Monster Island!
Eldrad [10/7/1999-2:14:15 PM]  Comments[0]

Eldrad sent us an annoucement about a raid in the Ocean of Tears.

If you ride the boat through the Ocean of Tears, after passing the island of the Sisters of Erolsi, you will eventually see a hill giant named Gornit (or something like that) bathing in the water near a camp on an island. This island is not only Gornit's home, but also the home of a band of shipwrecked pirated, and the dreaded Seafury Cyclopses!

It is Tunare's will that the Seafury Cyclopses, and other wretched creatures, be cleansed from this area. As the General of Tunare it is my duty and honor to see out the bravest of adventurers to assist me with this assault.

The assault force is to be made up of characters levels 35 - 50. Those under level 35 are welcome to come and offer support, however you must do so at your own risk and bring equipment you can afford to lose if you happen to die. The assault cannot be stopped for corpse retrieval missions.

To get to this island, you must take the boat from Freeport. Coming from Butcherblock will not lead to it, as the captains have changed thier shipping routes recently. You are encouraged to bind at the Freeport docks before coming. Those that follow the ways of evil are permitted in this army, but know that you fight for Tunare that day, not your dark gods.

The goals of this assault are as follows:

  • Destroy six Seafury Cyclopses (levels approaching 50 or so I've been told)
  • Sack the Pirate Camp on the same island.
  • Terminate Gornit with extreme prejudice.

After these 3 goals are met, we shall look upon the carnage and judge our abilities. If we feel we are up to it, the army will move on to slay the dinosaur on his island, far away. We will all need Levitate and Spirit of the Wolf to run there, swimming and rowboats will just be too slow.

We will form as many full groups as possible. There will be no set looting orders, other than whatever your group happens to set up. We aren't doing this for loot, or experience, the object is just to come and do something new that not too many others can say they've done. If any special items are found, and the person that gets it doesn't turn out to be selfish and mean, it will be givin to whoever among the army can (and WILL) use it, will benefit most from it, and someone that has worked extra hard that night prove that they deserve it (ie, be the hero type). I doubt there will be anything special, but I will once again be requesting the presence of the Gods, and who knows there could be a nice reward if we succeed in slaying the dinosaur.

The raid begins at 9 PM EST, on Saturday the 9th, on the Tarew Marr server. That means COME EARLY (like 8) or we'll start without you!

If you have any questions, contact me at eldrad@timkline.com

--Eldrad Stormrider
Officer of the Highlander Clan
General of Tunare

There will be a Picnic on the Fennin Ro server this Sunday.

The Sterling Order of Knights will be hosting a server-wide all day picnic this Sunday October 10th with games, prizes, fun and festivities! We will be offering cash prizes throughut the day including a picks from our notourious 'goodie bag'. We have been collecting really great prizes for this event.More information can be found at http://www.sok.org/picnic.

Our Picnic area will be along the path fairly close (but not so close our KOS people will get killed) to the second huts as you come from West Freeport in East Commons. We will be passing out free food and drinks to all! Please come and enjoy the festivities!

Schedule for Sunday, October 10th- {All times EST}

1:00 - Cross Country Relay Race

This is a 4 person team event. The race will begin in Qeynos at the front gate. The official will hand each team an item to be passed off from player to player. The hand off points are listed below. This race is intended to be run along the roads and pathways, however if the team so desires they may take any route they choose, provided they hand off at the correct points. Anything goes in the line of spells, however the spells may only be cast by the participants. No outside help is allowed.

  • Qeynos front gates - Race start
  • WKarana/NKarana border - Marker 1
  • EKarana/Highpass Hold - Marker 2
  • Kithicore/WCommons - Marker 3
  • Freeport West Gate - Race end
Prizes for this event are:
  • 1st place: 10pp for each member of the team (total of 40pp for the team) + their choice of an item out of the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 5pp for each member of the team (total of 20pp for the team)
  • 3rd place: 1pp for each member of the team (total of 4 pp for the team)
2:00 - Scavenger Hunt (all times are EST)

This is a 3-6 person event. The event officials will be stationed in East Commons, however, you are free to go to any zone of your choosing once you get your list from the official. The event will officially end at 4pm EST/1pm PST. The first team in each catagory that brings all/the most items on their list wins. If a group completes the list before the deadline they must turn in their items to their coordinating official. The groups are broken down as follows:

  • Lvl 10 and below
  • Lvl 10-20
  • Lvl 20-30
  • Lvl 30+
Prizes for this event are:
  • 1st place: 20pp for each member of the team + their choice of an item out of the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 15pp for each member of the team
  • 3rd place: 5pp for each member of the team
3:00 - Beauty Contest

This contest is an individual contest. It may be entered by male or female. The announcer will direct you to do a walk through in front of the panel of judges. Each contestant will be asked one question by each judge. The contestants will then be judged by their appearance and their answers to the questions. The judges will vote in private tell to the announcer and the announcer will tally up the votes and announce the winners. Prizes for this event are:

  • 1st place: 30pp + their choice from the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 20pp
  • 3rd place: 10pp
4:00 - Save the Newbie

This is a two person team event. One can be of any level or class. The other MUST be a level one. (Get a friend to create a character in Qeynos, any class, or pick one up off the streets for that matter.) The markers are listed below. Any spell but invisibility type spells may be used. You will get your newbie from Qeynos to Freeport checking in at each checkpoint. If at any point your newbie dies, you may restart the race from their starting point. There will be no penalty for deaths. The first pair that crosses the finish line with a live newbie wins. The markers you must pass are as follows:

  • Qeynos front gates - Race starter
  • WKarana/NKarana border - Marker 1
  • EKarana/Highpass Hold - Marker 2
  • Kithicore/WCommons - Marker 3
  • Freeport West Gate - Race end
Prizes for this event are:
  • 1st place: 30pp + their choice from the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 20pp
  • 3rd place: 10pp
5:00 - Kelethin Race

This is an individual event. (Because it takes place in Kelethin, transportation will be provided from East Commons for any participating contestants and officials that need it, as well as those who just want to go and laugh.) Each contestant will be given 10 alcoholic beverages that they must drink at lift one. They must run through the city and find lift 3. The first drunk that makes it to lift 3 wins.

Prizes for this event are:

  • 1st place: 30pp + their choice from the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 20pp
  • 3rd place: 10pp
6:00 - Corpse Looting Contest

This is and individual event. The contest will take place in the Freeport Arena. Each contestant will check in with the official so that his inventory can be checked. There must be something in each wearable slot minus shield/secondary weapon, and range weapon slots. The official will line up the contestants and order them killed. The contestants will then line up at the entrance. Upon the word "GO" each contestant will run to their corpses, put on all their clothing/armor and run back to the official. The first contestant to make it to the official fully dressed wins.

Prizes for this event are:

  • 1st place: 30pp + their choice from the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 20pp
  • 3rd place: 10pp
7:00 - Gauntlet of Death

This is an individual event. The contest will take place in the Freeport Arena. Each contestant will receive an item they must give to the official standing near the Captain. They will then receive an item they must return to the starting gate official. It is anything goes between the two officials. First one to return the item wins.

Prizes for this event are:

  • 1st place: 30pp + their choice from the goodie bag.
  • 2nd place: 20pp
  • 3rd place: 10pp

Raid on Kerra Isle
[10/12/1999-2:18:20 PM]  Comments[0]

There will be a raid on Kerra Isle this Saturday. (Rallos Zek Server)

What: A raid on Kerra Isle.
When: Saturday, Oct. 16 (EDT: 9AM - 2PM / MDT: 10AM - 3PM / CDT: 11AM - 4PM / EDT: 12PM - 5PM / ADT: 1PM - 6PM)
Where: Kerra Isle, off the coast of Odus. Rallos Zek Server.
Who: Levels 5 through 25
Why: Adventure. Loot. Experience. Exploration. Fun. Awareness of the isles presence.

What is Kerra Isle?

Kerra Isle is an isle off the coast of Odus. It is reached by swimming a distance off shore. Kerra Isle could be described as being the dungeon of Odus.

I will need several spellcasters for Bindings and Invizs. I advise people to get bound either at the marble platform in Toxxulia Forest (since there is a shop you can gate to and sell loot) or near the entrance to Kerra (so youll respawn there, and if you must sell, you can run back, then gate). Well need Invizs to get the dark races through Qeynos and Erudin. We should be able to get them through smoothly except for two NPCs at the dock in Erudin. New Patch Message
[10/13/1999-3:03:04 PM]  Comments[0]

The following is the latest news from the Patch Server.

October 13th, 7:00am
--------------------

We are excited to announce the implementation of the new "Optional" patch system. This new system will allow players to download new optional files such as zones, music, character animations, etc. After the EverQuest executable is run (everquest.exe), and once the download is complete, you will receive an option to patch the "optional" files. If "Yes" is selected, the optional files will begin downloading, if "No" is selected, you will bypass the optional files and continue. Please note that this optional patch requestor will appear every time everquest.exe is run.

In addition to the optional patch program, the following changes and enhancements have been made:

*Optional Patch*

A new zone, The Temple of Solusek Ro, is now available via the optional patch.

*General Enhancements*

- Experience and looting rights are now awarded for group aggregate damage. This is now true for ALL servers. We still consider this change in 'TEST' and will conduct a poll as to whether it positively addresses the kill stealing situation in approximately 2 weeks.
- As mentioned on Saturday, Rubicite armor will no longer spawn. The new class specific quests to obtain the new armor are now in for each class that could wear Rubicite.
- Journeyman's Boots will no longer spawn where they did but rather are the products of a quest.
- To address trade fraud (players removing items/money immediately prior to trading to deceive the other player), items/money may only be removed by clicking on the "Cancel" button.
- When receiving a text message from either an NPC or PC speaking in a language other than "Common Tongue", if the user's language skill is high enough, the language being spoken will be noted preceding the text.
- The fishing pole will now appear in the player's hand whenever the fishing skill is used.
- A number of quests were both added and enhanced throughout the zones.

*Trade Skills*

A number of new items have been added to Blacksmithing.

*Spell Enhancements*

- Some of the Items that Magicians Summon with their spells have been made better / had their effects increased.
- Fufil's Curtailing Chant, Tuyen's Chant of Flame and Tuyen's Chant of Frost (BARD) have all had their effects slightly increased.
- The Burnout Series (Magicians) has had the negative HP component reduced (improvement) and the stat buff increased (improvement). Because of this, the spells are no longer permanent - they now have a 15 minute duration. Burnout III has also had its casting cost reduced.
- Illusion: Werewolf attack speed component now works as intended.
- Insipid Weakness (Enchanter) will research as intended.

- The EverQuest Team
