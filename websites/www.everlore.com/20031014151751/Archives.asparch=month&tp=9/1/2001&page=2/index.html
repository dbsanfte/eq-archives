An Essay of Hope and Justice to the American People
Brian Hendricks [9/14/2001-10:17:23 AM]  Comments[96]

This essay was originally a school assignment that I expanded. So far it has reached as for as the Washington Post and other local news-papers of the Maryland/DC area. I have to admit that it took about 3 hours of editing and I had to stop every 10-15 minutes so I could let out some tears. I hope you like my essay.

How can people be this mad to do these attacks? A few days ago, a horrific act of terrorism struck the hearts of many American citizens. Two of some of the most respected and valuable buildings in the world were destroyed. The nerve center of the US Army and Navy were damaged. Although much lost, some good things can come from this disaster. The terrorists most likely wanted America to shut down airports, tighten security to create a fear of flying, cancel sports events and most of all a destroy the economy. Also, even if things such as the stock market closing, buildings collapsed, tens of thousands of jobs lost, one of the most favorite citiess in the US turned to a war zone, hope must not be given up. There are so many things that we can do to help get the United States back together.

The first major task is that we all must come together and unite, which most of us are doing now. Before these attacks, there was so much violence, poverty, and crime in the US already. Now, we are coming forth as one country to show that we have freedom. Donations must be made to family’s that have lost loved ones and tributes to hospitals should occur. One thing the terrorists wanted was for the stock market/economy to go down. To backfire this plan, large money holders should buy huge amounts of stock to bring the economy back up. Not only will they be helping the country they will get all those stocks! Finally, memorials should be given and we can never forget the tragedy that has occurred. However, much worse things could happen from this that we can change.

Personally, I think the terrorists wanted to change our lives and to put fear in us. The first thing we have to do is get back on the airplanes. Also, we must show that we can have good security and face our fears. We must help our neighbor, to recover from a loss and to encourage them to go back to work. A time of mourning is necessary, but a year of depression is not. America is a known for its freedom, which supposedly is lost. We must not lose our freedom. Although it was attacked, we must always defend it. Our flag still stands for freedom for they cannot take that away. Great leaps in friendship could occur sooner or later. We must show the world that we are not terrorized because we have heaven on our side. We will stand by as American’s.

The last dreadful causes of these attacks are the retaliation efforts. I understand that we want to get rid of Osama Ben Laden, but we do not want to turn into the murderers that we have encountered. If we bomb, raid, or nuke any country, we will be killing many innocent people. We must not show that we are becoming evil, for that is what they wanted from us also. We must never fight fire with fire because two wrongs don’t make a right. We can learn by doing these actions, and find a legitimate way to deal with our problems.

Why did these things happen we could never know? If it’s great buildings destroyed, lives lost, a damaged economy, or the tampering with our lives, we can never give up. Although good things can become of this, many sad events have occurred. The most important thing we can do right now is to become one and get through one of the most toughest things in American history. If we do accomplish this task, in 50 to 100 years we can pass along to our children and grand-children information about this past couple of days. Of how America came through this and can still remain a great nation.

If you would like to contact me e-mail me at Axhelm@hotmail.com

Brian Hendricks
8th Grader at Mclean School of Maryland
A boy with a broken heart and a shattered dream.

Firiona Vie in Skyfire Mountains on Povar
Drenon Dagiant [9/19/2001-10:12:14 AM]  Comments[1]

On the Povar server a GM event was held on September 17th at about 4pm est. The following is a in character summary of what happened.

While hunting in the Dreadlands of Kunark in what would normally be a deadly and dangerous part of the continent, a high elven and wood elven female casually walked through the desolate area, unaffected by the many wandering monsters. They passed many an adventurer, including myself, but I was not close enough at the time to see who they were. They quickly passed through to the dangerous burning wood, and as often happens my curiosity got the better of me and I followed. The burning woods is a dangerous place, especially for a lone cleric such as myself, but I braved the dangers, certain that something was amiss and that I would be needed. As I began to follow the elven ladies, making sure not to appear threatening I was suddenly startled by a massive amount of ash and fire rising into the air.

As I looked skyward all I saw was the dark figure of a winged creature. I felt fearful and considered turning back but I continued onward. The elven women eventually had traveled beyond the places I was familiar with and into the danger of the skyfire mountains. As I passed into this new area I realized I was lost. I traveled with caution until I came across a hunting party and explained what had transpired. They had also seen the explosion and were curious but had not seen the elven women. Suddenly out of nowhere they were among us. Who would have believed it was Firiona Vie herself, and her companion Lyirae Oakwynd. We all lowered our heads in respect and several people including myself stood up to greet them.

By now many others who had seen these women were massing, knowing that Firiona Vie was always on the quest for justice and were offering thier assistance. From what we were told, when Firiona had summoned Tunare in the forest of lesser Faydark, and Cazic Thule had appeared and caused chaos, he has also stolen her staff call the Lifebringer. She was on a quest to recover it, and was seeking the help of none other than the Ring of Scale. She was informed to meet a dragon representative in the Skyfire mountains and she had arrived to pursue this meeting. A moment later, a dragon flew overhead and landed a short distance away at the sphere of norrath. The dragon there was Lady Zavo'Za, a fire dragon of massive size. She greeted Firiona Vie with respect and dignity though the rest of us she was not so sure of. Many of us had gathered to help Firiona in her quest. After a lengthy discussion with Lady Zavo'Za, Firiona was dissapointed to find out that the ring of scale had decided not to assist her.

She was left with only two choices of information; Venril Sathir himself or Ghargin Bumblok, a priest of Cazic Thule. Realizing Venril Sathir was out of her grasp at the time, we lured out Bumblok. Appearing in the form of the avatar of Fear, Bumblok appeared more formidable than he was. Under our massive force he fell quickly. After he perished Firiona ressurected his body. She questioned him on the location of her missing staff, and under threat of imprisonment in Felwithe Bumblok relinquished that the staff was on the frozen continent of Velious.

It was early on discovered that Bumblok had killed Lyirae's brother in cold blood. She waited till firiona had the information she needed, then as Firiona turned her back Lyirae finished Bumblok with 2 shots from her Trueshot bow. Rombus, Bauldirr and Guanna were all rewarded for exceptional service to Firiona. With this Firiona and her companion stepped into a magical portal, returning to Felwithe to plan their next move before continuing on to Velious. The rest of us headed home, glad that justice had been served.

Another GM Event on Tallon Zek!
Sorsisis Plaguescale [9/19/2001-1:26:12 PM]  Comments[8]

After returning from the GM-Lead darky raid on Freeport, which I missed, seeing as my port got there late, I began hunting at the lake. Suddenly, a guildmate of mine, Nert, starting to shout in guildchat that there was a GM event in East Cabilis! Of course being very intrigued, I headed on over to my hometown. Upon arrival outside the shaman guildhouse, I saw a Hierophant standing nearby named "Hierophant Sakar." He was coughing, and seemed ill. Many of my guildmates poured into the area, and we all sat around listening to what the Hierophant had to say. What he told us was that An evil creature named Lord Lozz Nasae had eaten a friend of his in the Swamp of No Hope, and he was too weak to seek revenge.

We being the honorable guild we are, stepped up to the challenge. Many members of Second Rising, including Nert, Xarthil, Brakish, Xraaz, Rezittik, Rasla, and myself, all headed to the Swamp of No Hope. We were informed that the beast only shows himself when the moons are aligned with Norrath, meaning we had around a half an hour of real time to find this foul beast. We split up, and planned on giving eachother the location if we find him. Nert, using his excellent hunting skills and knowledge of the land, found the beast along the side path leading to Cabilis. Nert was taking some damage when we arrived, and we all jumped in to help. As the beast was being hacked and bashed by my guidmates, I threw an Envenomed Bolt onto the Lord, and he died at my hands as spirits of frost slammed against him. Nert then examined the corpse to find a skull on it, which he looted, and we headed back to Sakar. Sakar had fallen asleep, and when we awoke him, he asked us what had gone on. We told him the mighty Second Rising killed the beast and got revenge for his friend's death. Sakar, a bit unsure, asked for proof. Nert handed the skull over. The hierophant jumped back with shock- "This is the skull of the beast!" he proclaimed! He then cursed Lozz's soul, and threw the skull into the Cabilis waterways.

In turn for helping him get revenge, Sakar handed out rewards to us. Some money to be split between our group, to Xraaz went a Book of Rings, which is a nice shaman/druid item that drops in Nurga. Also given out was a Spiked Goblin Ring, which was handed to Nert(an SK), but given to me as it was more a shaman item, and because Nert felt it only right seeing as I took the beasts life. Thank you Nert. =) The rings stats are ac 2, 3str, 2wis, 3agi, -2int, 5sv poison, and drops in Droga, if i remember correctly. It is a nice momento to remember the event by. Sakar then told us to take care, and that his illness was too great, and dove off into the waterway to "End his suffering." We went in after him, but alas, he was already gone.

All in all it was a great time for all the Second Rising members who participated, and its about time we see a darky gm event in cabilis, it was really fun to attend. Congrats to Xraaz on the nice book, and im glad Second Rising got to help sakar!
