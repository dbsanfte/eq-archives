You know... I used to feel the way you do, Tobie. That this racewar thing should be just that - WAR.

But after several months, I just don't bother anymore. There's no point. You get killed PvP, you re-appear, you go back and do it again. Nothing changes. No lands or cities really change hands, no resources are truly won or lost... nothing.

Sure, it's good to drive the other team out of a profitable hunting area... but that's about it. And the next day, after everyone's logged off for the night, everything's right back where it started.

Conquoring a city is unfeasable. You kill all the guards, and maybe all the (insanely powerful) merchants... and what happens? They all spawn back again. You can't own a city. It's not possible.

So basically what you have is not racewar, but racetension, with frequent skirmishes.

Nobody wins, or looses, anything. Except possibly the few items they didn't get a chance to bag.
