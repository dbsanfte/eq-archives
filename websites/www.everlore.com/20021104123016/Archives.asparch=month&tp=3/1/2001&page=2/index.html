(03/11/01) MMORPGSTUFF.com recently partnered with a design studio named, "Yellow Dog Grafix", under the creative direction of Ariel Wulff. Ariel Wulff has been in the graphic arts business for 17 years and has worked on a number of projects with Barry Blair and Colin Chan. She has had artwork published in ElfQuest, Legend of the Elflord, Demon Hunter and the San Diego Comic Con Program Book.

Anthony Pearson has been in the graphic arts and web design business for 7 years, and a member of the gaming community since 1996. Anthony has worked on numerous projects, including work for XRGaming.net, Ianstorm, Shadowbania and more.

Many people already know about the special program MMORPGSTUFF.com offers to those who submit concepts to our studio. We have recently revised this offer to help offset the cost of professionally illustrating our designs. If a gamer submits a concept that is created without a full-color illustration, he/she will earn $20.00 per $100.00 of profit earned by our company. If a gamer's concept is created by our illustrators, he/she will earn $10.00 for every $100.00 of profit earned by our company. Either way, a player can earn anywhere from $10.00 to $200.00 per month, depending upon sales and interest in the design. Not bad for an IDEA!

MANY gamer's concepts have been archived in our studio. We literally have about 200 concepts pending creation, not to mention the ideas that are submitted almost daily. Great ideas are immediately flagged, then archived, where they will be created in turn by our artists.

Thank you for your time, and interest, in our online-gaming merchandise.

Respectfully,

GM Event on Veeshan- Killer Cacti
Dakkota Darkheart [3/12/2001-12:21:57 PM]  Comments[9]

We had an excellent GM event last night in the Overthere, and I thought I might share this with you all.

While closing in on 40, I captained a small group of intrepid souls at the Skyfire zone. As we pulled various yard trash to our operations area, a young Ranger of some dubious nature ran by, shrieking for assistance.

Now, I'm a Seeker of Lore, and a Lady, both of which generally compell me to assist others. But, this individual spoke doodish and conducted himself in a decidedly unfriendly manner. What did propell me into action was the sight of a large and enraged cactus chasing him down the road.

Giggling in spite of myself, I gave chase and observed rather strange behavior for a plant. It seemed that only one of us was deemed by the GM running the prickly pest as able to attack it. This, for all you dear readers out there, was me. As my group hammered their melee hotkeys with no effects, I went toe to toe with this nasty desert dweller for a long time. Trading blow for blow, I was amazed by the regen that this creature had (akin to a fungi tunic). Cozaar (my trollie private dancer and SK) was unable to attack the monster at all, and my druid was unable to get any spells to stick. As my cleric went oom, my fate began to appear grim. The staff member evidently saw my plight, as he turned his attention to another player, delivering an insta-death 1200 point Harm Touch. Ye Gods...this was a SK Cactus. The "Spiker" then began DoTing anyone near, including shadow stepping constantly while regenning hitpoints. At one point, giggling relentlessly, it bounced around me as I swung with my great stamina drainer (Wurmslayer)to no avail. The "Spiker" managed to murder my druid at this point, who instantly rejoined us in the zone, quite pleased at having lost no exp.

At this point, the fight had gone on for ages, and both me and the Spiker were at very low hitpoints. My cleric (I owe ya Talayfox) scraped up enough mana to heal me one more time, and this proved to be the difference. With the tide turning in my favor, the shadowknight cactus promptly harmtouched itself and died. I was then the winner of a silver ruby veil and three (Thats right..THREE..ph@t lewt!) stacks of shurikens.

At any rate, I'd like to thank Cozaar the sexy dancing trollie, Talayfox the savior of my fanny, Joeraja the Druid ("I'm sitting because I dotted it Dak. Leave me alone"), and Vorvox the Chanter. We need more GM events on Veeshan, and in almost 2 years here, this is the first Ive played a major part in. I would like to thank the server staff for an imaginative and well thought out battle.

Stop by Veeshan and say hi, and please feel free to check us out at www.LoreSeekers.com.

GuildBoss v1.11 released
Xanthus [3/12/2001-12:23:43 PM]  Comments[3]

Received from GuildBoss:

GuildBoss is a guild and clan manager for Windows 9x/2000/Me/NT. Grab it at www.guildboss.com

What's new in v1.11:

* Added 'Custom filter...' option to the QuickFilter drop-down on main screen.

By selecting this option you can filter your roster by choosing values from your custom Drop-List fields. For instance, for an EverQuest guild you might want to see all your "Human Paladins" or maybe your "Dark Elf Rogues",etc..

You can filter on all four drop-lists.

* Added count of "highlighted" members in parenthesis next to the count of members in the currently selected rank.

* Custom drop-list captions (on Member Profile window) now update accordingly when renamed via the Drop-List Manager.

* Added support for "numeric" sorting in custom User Fields. For instance, if you defined a "Level" column for tracking character level, you can sort the rosters by the values entered in this field. Prior to this release, all custom User Fields were interpreted as TEXT fields and thus did not sort properly with number values.

* Fixed timeout error when sending a message where the last recipient in the list doesn't have an entry in the profile (i.e. blank email address or ICQ #).

* Fixed date sorting in roster grid.

* Fixed "overflow" error in Deeds Journal when member's Deed Tally exceeded 32,767.

* Fixed "Can't create DAO workspace" error a few folks had encountered, preventing them from running GuildBoss at all.

* Fixed Cellpadding and Cellspacing on roster reports where they were swapped.

* Fixed extra "

* Fixed "Undefined error [invalid key] populating member property controls!" error when adding a new member to a new blank database. Finally!!

* Fixed "Undefined error 3061-Too few parameters" when sorting roster by Dues Owed.

EQ Quiz 5: Druids
Xanthus [3/13/2001-10:50:10 AM]  Comments[5]

Received from Aracnakat:

This week's EQ Quiz is about Druids. What do you know about them? You've grouped with them sure. So at one with their outdoor environment that some might think that they are indeed born from the earth or perhaps raised in the wilds.
