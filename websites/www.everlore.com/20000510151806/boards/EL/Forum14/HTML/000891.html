Ok I gotta 'lil story for yall.

I was hunting in CB with a nice group. then we headed into the TR and got mobbed by D'vinn and Crush, we took out Crush but D'vinn finneshed up off.

So after we egrouped and started back in, out group started to disband untill only myself (lvl 15 ranger) and a lvl 13 wizard were left. I stayed by the entrace of CB and memmed my spells when I heard a lvl 15 wizard asking for challengers. Having nothing better to do I said I would duel him. He annoyed me by ignoring me untill I had said I would duel him twice more. He finaly accepted and we started to duel.

Before I tell ya what happened lemme tell ya what equipment I had so no one says I was tweaked. I was wearing all Ring and banded mail except for my tunic, it was crushes. My mask was raw-hide as was my cape. I had a bloodstone earring a brass earring a Platinum Engament ring and a Derv. ring. My weapon was a PGT but it NEVER went off during the fight. So my weapon was the equivelent of a Dwarf Ax ( I have one in the bank) and I had a SBS.

So when we duled He rooted me and ran off to nuke me. I guess he did'nt know I had a bow. (I forget the name but it's 14dmg 49 delay) So as he nuked me I pegged him with arrows. I thought for awhile I was gonna loose because I couldnt melee him. But the strain of continuall roots and powerful nuke spells quickly drained him of mana and he came in to fight me hand to hand.

Mistake.

I snared him and began to pummle him ruthelessly with kicks and slashes. He may have hit me once or twice with his punny staff for 20 or so but after he came in close I riped him to shreds.

Anyway the morale of this rather long story is that all you rangers who thing casters are better than us think different!! We can beat them and we CAN rule the world!!

Duels are all about
1. Being prepared
2. Simply outsmarting your opponent

As a L49 ranger I beat a L50 wizard in a duel, we used self-buffs only. I won with over 2 bubbles of health and over a bubble of mana. This was an experienced and smart opponent as well. The fight took between 5-10 minutes before I finally got him. I'll spare you the details, but let me say I got hit with no less than 4 ice comets, gravity flux, multiple force shocks, as well as the root/stun routine. What was the spell he used most? Shadow step, by far.. he had to stay away from me. What were the spells I used most? Cancel magic, healing, and root. I needed to keep him close as well as offset his huge damage potential. It's not a race, it's a duel, after all. Resistances? Cold:121 Fire:130ish Magic:70 something-heh. And yes, Limlit, tracking is invaluable.
