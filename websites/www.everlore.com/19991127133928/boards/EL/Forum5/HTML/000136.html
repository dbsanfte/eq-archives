I am an agnostic Dark Elf Wizard. As far as Neriak, anyone having to do with the Shadow Knight or Necro guild will glower at you, including some of thier merchants. But aside from that and your amiable guild leaders, everyone else will treat you indifferently. Ie 99% of the merchants, the banker, the neriak ogres and trolls (not of the SK guild), and any of the other guilds will all treat you indifferently.

As for the humans they all started with glowers at me except the holy righteous types such as the paladins and clerics who will kill you if you step foot in North Freeport. By killing deathfist orcs I got my faction up with the paladins to indiff.. they are the main problem when stepping foot in n freeport because they patrol that entire zone. The orcs also got me up to amiable with the innkeepers in commonlands and north ro. I actually sell to these guys before going back to neriak cause they give me more money then the indiff merchants in neriak. The Freeport militia (guards in w/e freeport) will glower at you until you slay orcs in highpass. All of the freeport merchants will glower at you as well, but I heard there is a quest in the thieves guild in FP that takes FOREVER (because you have to do it over and over) but will raise your faction with those merchants. The halflings will glower at you except for their clerics who will glare.. which means they will most likely attack. The Barbarian guards hate us because they worship that 'unseen force of honor and rightenous' (forgot the name of that god)and all scowel ready to attack which is nothing more then a minor inconvience easily handled with inviso... we have no business in a barbarians city afterall... only need to go there for the ice comet quest and perma. The trolls and ogres both mostly glower at us from WHAT I HAVE HEARD, although there are a few that would like to eat us for a light snack... trolls and ogres I have yet to experience first hand, thatll be coming up very shortly as I am planning a trip to grobb and feerot this week.

Now on a side note, I have been working on a rather large and extensive guide to being a Dark Elf Wizard =) Once I have finished gathering all the facts I need to post it I will put it in the guides section of this site along with several other sites. Those that have proof read it for me say that its quite informative and even a little humorous. It includes ideal huntings areas at certain levels along with faction notes, merchante notes and lots of other stuff. Ill post on this thread to let you know once I have released the guide if you are interested.

Safe journeys fellow inkie
