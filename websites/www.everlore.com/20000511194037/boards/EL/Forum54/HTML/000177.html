You have to make alliances, even with the other races. Sometimes you might work with the elves, sometimes you'll be with the dwarves fighting the elves. The whole point is that you do what it takes to make your team prosper. There are times when two teams have the same goal and will work together for this, there are times later when those same teams will disagree and engage in war. If all we did was try to kill each other, EQ would become a very long and boring CTF game on half life.
