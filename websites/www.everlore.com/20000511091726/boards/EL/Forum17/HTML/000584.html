I am so ashamed of myself, my bretheren. I was hunting in the Sewers of Qeynos, when I met a 12th level paladin. He talked me into going after the smugglers (a bigger misstake than just the faction hit, as it turns out).

At anyrate, I got killed. On my way back, he tells me that my corpse is being looted by the spawns. He told me that if I gave him permission to loot my corpse, he would save my stuff for me. Now, being a lowly rogue of just 9 seasons, I am struggling to make ends meet, and I was saving up for a decent rapier (15 of the 16 PP needed).

Well, to make a long story short, I have been told that there are _no_ NPC's that can loot a player's corpse. This big dumb paladin conned me into giving him most of my stuff and all my hard earned cash.

What really irks me is that I didn't think of this con earlier. And, I was in a hurry to log off (looking for one quick hunt before I went back to reality) and didn't have the time to find him and extract some sort of revenge... And now I can't remember his name.

At anyrate, this is just a little note to either beware, or to try this scam yourself

DOH! You muttonheads!

Nobody, not NPC's, not PC's... NOBODY on a blue server can loot your corpse without your permission.

If they conned you into giving it to them, I strongly recommend that you /petition a GM and tell them what happened, and more importantly WHO did it it. You may not get your stuff back, but if they keep hearing about the same person doing this over and over, the thieving bastard may get banned.

The consent command works across zone boundaries, so even if you have no corpses in the zone you are in, it doesn't mean you don't have a corpse in another zone.

One further note, NEVER, EVER do /consent yourself, this will allow ANYBODY to loot your corpse.
