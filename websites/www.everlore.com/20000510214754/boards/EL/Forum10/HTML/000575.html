There are no creatures in Nektulos forest that will give you a positive faction hit in Neriak. If you want to improve your faction with the Indigo Brotherhood, kill Elven slaves in Crushbone. If you want to increase your faction with The Dead, the necromancers of Neriak, kill the Gnome mage in the slave pits in Crushbone. I killed him like, 20 times and I went from glower to indiff. that let me do the Thex mallet quest which gets you even more faction with them.
