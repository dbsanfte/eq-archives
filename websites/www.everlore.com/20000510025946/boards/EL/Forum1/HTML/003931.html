Originally posted by Lybernia:
If you have a slow connection/low ram and the zone takes too long you will get disconnected, same as being link dead. From my undestanding adding more ram to your system will speed up your zone times and prevent them from thinking your are linkdead.

Ok, I agree that RAM has a lot to do with how fast you change zones. Let's face it. What is happening when you zone is the old zone info goes away and you download current info (who and what is in zone) on the new zone you are entering. It has been stated in other threds that the number of people in the zone influences the amount of time to download the zone info. I agree: more players then more time to download.

I also believe that your connection speed can vary even as you play. Lag can be caused by the EQ servers being overcrowded, your ISP being crowded and your connection to get to the ISP (i.e. phone company) could be problem.

When it comes right down to it you can do everything right, have a top-end machine with mucho RAM and killer video card RAM and you still get crappy connection speeds. I have a 450PIII with 16MB video, 128MB RAM and I STILL have trouble with choppy play sometimes. Been there; done that; bought the T-shirt.
