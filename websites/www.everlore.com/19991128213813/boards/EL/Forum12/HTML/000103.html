Hard to help not knowing your level. However, the small round shield was mentioned. Skeletons and centurians drop them. Fairly easy to come by. You can go to the Nybright Sister Camp in Lesser Faydark. You need to be around lev 8 or 9 in a group to kill them. Your best bet would be to get a wooden round shield off of a mob and use that for your newbie levels (up to 9th or so). Don't bother wasting your money on vendor purchased shields since they are pretty expensive (4pp +).

Around 10th level you can go to Crushbone and attempt for a Shiny Brass Shield (AC 10 +10 vs magic). There are semi rare drops and the trainer has about a 12 minute respawn and is typically camped. If you have been saving wisely you cna probably buy one for 15-20pp or less (one of my characters got one for 5pp and the shield he was carrying).

Once you hit your upper teens you have 2 options. Either go to Guk and get a Chitin shield (AC 15 + 10 vs poison) or go to Highpass and try for a Cracked Darkwood shield (not sure of the AC but it has a nice + Str stat). The Cracked Darkwood shield is pretty heavily camped due ot the fact that it's a pretty sweet shield and it also spawns in the area where the PGT Mucktail (Granix I believe) gnoll spawns. I've not seen Chitin Shields sold very often and the Cracked Darkwood shields are somewhat expensive.

At around the mid 20s you can probably go for a Crested Mistmore shield (AC 10 +6 Dex and + sv vs all (I think) - also looks pretty cool) obviously located in Mistmore off of the male Glyphed Ghoul. He is probably upper 20s but not really hard. He is camped pretty heavily and you can be in trouble if you get too close to the tower and pull some of the nasties from there (shadowy scriveners, Gypsies, etc).

Around 30ish it may be time to go to Sol A (Solosek's Eye) and try for a Charred Guardian Shield (not sure of AC but it is +9 Wis). The area is pretty tough and you will have to kill a certain clockwork gnome (if you are a gnome or care about Ak'Annon faction don't do this). One good thing about Sol A is that the fire goblins start out indifferent at least to dwarves so you can essentially walk around and kill stuff without a care. This is a pretty tough shield to get and I've seen it sell for anywhere from 650-1200pp. you can also do the Cleric Shield quest as well.

I believe that the Charred Guardian shield is probably the best cleric shield in the game (+9 wis what can be better) at least until you get to the POF.

I actually have used a Shiny Brass shield up to about a week ago when a guildmate let me "borrow" his Crested Mistmore shield. It has served me pretty well. Hope this info helped!
