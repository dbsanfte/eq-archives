Just hang out in Nektulos for a while. heheh. There is an area nust northwest of the bridge that is good for 3 typs of mobs, Spiders, Bears, and Skeletons, Shadow Wolves. Dunno on other servers, but on Veeshan, Spider Silk sells for 5 gp each. Kill a spider and get a plat. (Walk arounf with a sewing kit on ya so you can combine 2 silk and make aswatch, which is stackable) High Quality Bear Skins sell for 10 pp to players trying to make backpacks, Bone chips can sell for 2pp per 20, as long as you hold out for that right buyer, Or you can use them yourself. Shadow Wolf pelts can sell for up to 5 pp each. I can camp in nektulos for 3 hours and come out with 75+ pp. All this info is based from the Veeshan Server. DOnt know if it is the same prices on other servers, but I assume it's pretty close. Have fun.
