New Patch Message
[2/3/2000-2:21:47 PM]  Comments[0]

This recently appeared on the patch server:

February 3rd, 8:00am
--------------------

The following changes have been implemented on the live servers:

*Spells*

- Lay On Hands: Enhanced to heal more hitpoints per level.
- Enveloping Roots: Casting time reduced to match Immobilize
- Skin Like Nature: Small Damage Shield added. This will stack with other shields.
- Insidious Fever: Casting time reduced.
- Insidious Malady: Casting time Reduced.
- Burnout series: Negative HP component removed to bring it in line with the Necromancer's Intensify/Augment death series.

*Songs*

- Syvelian's Anti-magic Aura: Potency increased.
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding: Now also grants See Invisible. Party members can now see each other while under the effect of this song.

Additionally, the following songs have been given a slightly larger radius:

- Selo's Accelerando
- Agilmente's Aria of Eagles
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding
- Tarew's Aquatic Ayre

*PvP-Teams Change*

Customers on the PvP-Teams servers have been plagued by a tactic known as "Bind Rushing". Casters would bind themselves in a hostile area or dungeon, and attack everyone of an opposing race in the area. Sooner or later the caster would die. They would then respawn, memorize a few spells, then jump back in to attack their wounded opponents.

As a first step in combating this practice and improving life for those on the PvP-Servers, casters will now respawn with zero mana after being killed by another player in a duel or open combat.

This code is new for those on Tallon and Vallon Zek. It has been in place for some time on Rallos Zek and works very well there.

*The Plane of Sky*

All spells that grant levitation will no longer function in the Plane of Sky. This affects spells cast by Wizards, Enchanters, Shamans, Druids, Necromancers, and Bards. The Plane of Sky is designed to be completed in certain fashion, and the existence of these spells allowed the islands to be completed out-of-order.

A teleporter will be placed on the first island in the next patch to allow people access to the room below.

- The EverQuest Team

GM Event Report -Innoruuk
Eldraden [2/3/2000-2:36:19 PM]  Comments[0]

A few nights ago I was in Black Burrow with some friends raking in experience and loot. We were fighting under the bridge doing pulls and such. After some time I heard some one by the name of Ironbar do a shout, and he was shouting the same stuff that the gnolls say when they spot you and attack you. I thought it was just someone playing a joke and went on with beating the current gnoll I was on into a bloody pulp. A guy in my group happened to mention that this Ironbar did not appear on a /who all, which made me curious.

Some time later we saw this Ironbar run by the bridge, only he wasn't a player, he was a gnoll! He was accompanied by another gnoll, who's name I could not remember. As I watched in awe, the 2 ran up to a barbarian warrior, and dropped him in no time flat. Myself being a level 16 wizard, attempted to take it upon myself to attack Ironbar. My spell landed, but was resisted however the gnolls did not turn to me. Thinking this was odd I did it again, again a resist, and again no action. I went in to attempt to melee and was instantly engaged and cut down very quickly. The remainder of my party attemtped to defend me, but were no match.

As I respawned in the mouth of blackburrow I quickly memorized my spells and headed back down to get my corpse. As I reached the bottom of the ramp I watched in horror as these 2 gnolls continued to slaughter everyone around them.

I snuck passed and looted my gear, my group regained composure and launched a tactical strike with our warrior going in first casters standing back. They double teamed the warrior, and slaughtered him, and then quickly dispatched of the rest of us. I respawned and waited for the rest of my group to get back to Blackburrow and fearfully saw the naked bodies of those returning from qeynos to retreive their corpses. At one point an all out assault was launched by about 15 people at once, attempting to take down the gnolls, but were thwarted easily, barely scratching them.

When it seemed all was lost, a level 40 magician by the name of Morpherios zoned into BB, and did a shout asking where the gnolls were. He proceeded to summon a pet which was larger than a few people in my party! He marched down the ramp, and as I followed, I saw him begin to cast a spell, which landed on Ironbar, and to everyone's astonishment and delight, his bar dropped by about 1/4th. Morpherios then sent his pet in to hold the evil dogs at bay and casted again, lowering Ironbar's hp to 1/2. The gnolls then attempted to gang him, but his pet cast a green spell that looked similar to my root spell, and they stopped dead. He cast again, and a bolt shot out of his hands this time, and dropped Ironbar to very little HP left, his pet finished the job. Everyone's focus switched to the remaining gnoll, and again Morpherios took aim and sent out to torch the remaining gnoll. He fired like mad and quickly dispersed the last gnoll. He mentioned the loot was nothing more than 23 GP, and gave it to me. He then did nothing more but Bow, and marched back up the ramp with another from his guild, Elerian, behind him, and zoned out. He undoubtedly saved many lives, and we from Blackburrow on Innoruuk thank you Morpherios! Curse those wretched dogs!

New Arch Mage Answers
[2/4/2000-9:59:32 AM]  Comments[0]

Verant game designer Geoffrey Zatkin has updated the Arch Mage Answers at the official EverQuest site. This edition of Arch Mage is all about the bard class. Here is a sniplet:

Q: Why did you make all the weapons in plane of hate for bards pretty much the exact same thing except for its weapon type.

A: There were a few items added to Hate Plane for Bards. Three of them were originally designed to be dropped from the same monster - the three identical Bard weapons. They were made identical so that no matter which weapon a Bard had chosen to train up in (1HS, 1HB or Piercing), they would have a good weapon to chose from. During the tuning and testing phase of Hate, these weapons got split up and placed on different monsters.

Jackalstock Event -Tunare
Aloro Silvertongue [2/4/2000-11:32:59 AM]  Comments[0]

Hello all!

As many of you may know, JackalX, the well-loved bard from Tunare, is retiring. He's been one of the longest-lived 50th level bards, and a constant contributor to the bardic community. As a show of fellowship, and to see that he leaves with a bang, not a whimper, we, the Bards of Norrath, are staging an event in honor of his retirement: Jackalstock.

The premise is simple. Anyone who wishes to come is welcome, be they bard or other well-wisher. We'll all make newbie halflings and meet at the Priest of Discord in Rivervale on Tunare at 12noon PST on Saturday, February 12th, 2000. From there, we'll proceed to High Hold Pass, where we'll be bound. Once everyone is gathered, mandolins will be distributed (so we'll be happy halfling bards) and we will proceed to East Karana. We'll all gather at the top of the gorge, and leap lemminglike into the great beyond.

If you wish to attend, please create a character with a name beginning Jx, followed by the name of your bard or other main character. Thus Aloro becomes Jxaloro, Demari becomes Jxdemari, etc. This aids in identifying each other. Please do not turn /roleplay or /anon on, so we can tell who's attending and find when everyone's in place. Further, please don't PvP so we can bind you. Please be at the Priest of Discord in Rivervale on the Tunare server at noon PST on Saturday the 12th, ready to go.

For those of you who remember Gnomestock '99, this is a similar event designed to offer the Bards from different servers a chance to socialize and have some silly fun, while offering up our lives in JackalX's memory. This is more a wake than a funeral, so expect booze and merriment. Once we've leapt (as many times as we feel appropriate) we'll gather at the Tiger's Roar, one of the finest taverns in Norrath, for frolicking and carousing.

Hope to see you there!

Aloro Silvertongue (E'ci)
