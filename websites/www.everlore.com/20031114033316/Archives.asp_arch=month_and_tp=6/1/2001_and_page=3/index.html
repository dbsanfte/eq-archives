He did unbelieveable damage, I lived but alas my druid friend did not. I made my way back to the fort and went inside. I had looked around and found Scaletail fighting 4 sturdy adventurers and began to tashani him hpoing it would help. He fell, and I was happy. The threat had been overted. There was a cheer somewhere off in the distance Ragnor had fell too, a bit earlier. PvP Panel at the Fan Faire - Clarifications
Xanthus [6/19/2001-1:11:30 PM]  Comments[10]

Abashi clarified a bit of misinformation that came out of the panel reports during the Fan Faire. Here is his post on the Developer's Corner:

Hello all,

Its apparent that as a result of the Fan Faire this weekend in Minneapolis, inaccurate information regarding our PvP plans for the future were released and/or misinterpreted.

That has always been one of the difficulties of having a real-time interactive discussion where one doesnt have the time to think about the precise wording of their answers. However, what we gain through the interactive forum makes it worth the effort, even when things appear to come out wrong like they did in the PvP panel this weekend.

For the most part, regarding the information that has since shown up on web sites, it appears that when it was mentioned that we discussed an issue, it was interpreted as we are doing this. The fact is, we discuss a TON of things when were driven to improve one area of the game. If you name it, weve probably discussed it at one time or another. That doesnt necessarily mean that what we discussed has either been decided or shot down, just that weve given it some measure of consideration in the past.

So, with that in mind, heres the quote-by-quote clarification of the story on Castersrealm (eq.castersrealm.com) and RallosZek.net. (www.ralloszek.net).

Quote from Article: In approximately one week, PvP 'rules' will be as on Sullon Zek. In other words, there are no rules with respect to PvP. Standard blue server rules apply and sexual/racial harassment will not be tolerated. Everything else (training/bind point camping/exp killing, etc) will be completely legal. No PvP related petitions will be handled by the CS staff. The standard blue server type problems such as lost corpses, bugs, lost items, etc will still be handled.

Clarification: Sure, we discussed it, much like we discuss anything. As we were putting together the ruleset for Sullon Zek, someone suggested that we role it out across Sullon first and see how that goes and role it across the other servers if it works out. Will it go across the other servers? We dont know. Well have to see how it goes on Sullon first.

Quote From Article: ShowEQ is actively being pursued to be 'fixed'

Clarification: As actively as bang for the buck allows. We really need to do something revolutionary and complex in our next attempt to address the problem. Its just a matter of having enough time to implement and test that. Timeframe? Mid-term, probably prior to Luclins launch.

Quote from Article: Classes are being re-examined for viability in a PvP environment. Lady Daegarmo specifically mentioned rangers as being one of the classes that is woeful in PvP.

Clarification: with nothing specific planned and nothing beyond minor tweaks, certainly nothing class-redefining planned. Regarding rangers there was talk about improving archery, but that was spoken of with plans to put something out across all servers rather than just as a PvP-related improvement. The truth is, the classes are not balanced for one-on-one PvP, no more than they are balanced for one-on-one PvE (Player versus Environment). If you have to fight as part of a group to be successful in PvE, youll most likely have to do so with PvP. Yes, this does mean that a solo melee player will be at a disadvantage when meeting casters, especially those configured for solo play, and yes, that is part of the plan.

Quote from the Article: Lady Daegarmo is pushing for the return of egg shaped pumice on vendors for approx 200P each

Clarification: Oh its been pushed, but has yet not been taken up by the dev team for consideration. Were kind of like the supreme court that way

Quote from Article: It was noted that Harm Touch and Lay Hands are on the same spell function with opposite effects so there's no way to give Lay Hands back to paladins on death while restricting the same recycle on HT

Clarification: Not entirely accurate. The reuse time is not tied to the spell effect, not to mention that each ability is treated as a skill by the game and uses its own reuse timer. The reason its not being done is not a matter of technical unfeasibility, but rather a specific design decision not to. Both are easy to exploit. The level to which they can be exploited is acceptable on the PvE servers and not acceptable on the PvP servers.

Quote from Article: Cross teaming is coming to an end. Teams (see later notes) will be locked down. There will be no ability to crossteam at all. No grouping, guilding, buffing, etc with non-team members. This lockdown is probably 60 days out. (I stated that the Development team would make an official date on the lock down in which they would give a minimal of 30 day notice. I also stated that I doubted you would see these changes within the next 2 months.)

Clarification: Yep, were planning on addressing cross-teaming, probably through locking down the teams. However, the specifics on how this will be done, along with what if any beneficial abilities will be allowed to cross any lock down is still being discussed. Opinions are welcome.

Quote from Article: The lockdown will entail a guildwipe. All guilds will be deleted and must be reformed. There will be extra staff for the first few days after the wipe to assist in this endeavour

Clarification: If we decided to do the most severe lockdown (no cross-guilding), then this would probably be the case. However something like this wont be decided until we decide what kind of lockdown well do.

Quote from Article: It is highly likely that common (the language) will be removed from the racewar servers for general communication use (i.e., public channels). Training in non-team languages will also receive very low caps.

Clarification: No. While this was one of the many thing suggested as part of a panel discussion, it has only been discussed as one of the many possible options for an RP Server, for lack of a better term, by the development team. The RP Server, by the way, will not be PvP. Those looking for PvP rules similar to an RP Server should look towards Sullon Zek.
