No. thta is not what they were talking about. I think what you are referring to is the part of the patch that dealt with cancel magic. Cancel magic was previously not an 'attack' spell, hence you could cast it on anyone anywhere. Even guys you were about to kill. And they would not attack. Now, if you Strip a guy of his buffs, he will attack you. But, if you mez a guy and cast cancel or strip, it will not break the mez.
