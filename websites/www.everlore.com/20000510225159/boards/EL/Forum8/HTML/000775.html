Zentil, perhaps if you listened in math class, you wouldn't be 1) so arrogant, 2) misinformed.

The spell description posted on eq.castersrealm.com says that it does 10 damage initially, doubling every tick for 16 ticks.

Using simple exponential math, that is:

10 + 10*2 + 10*4 + ... 10 * 2^15.

That sum is equivalent to 10*(2^16 - 1) which yields about 650,000 damage.

Now, having been corrected about the initial damage, I see that it only does 6,500 damage. This makes a lot more sense, but still makes it a kick ass spell. I wouldn't be surprised if 6 necros at level 51 could take out Cazic Thule himself unless this spell gets highly resisted.
