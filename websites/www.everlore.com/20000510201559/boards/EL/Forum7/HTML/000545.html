No offence folks, but it's not exactly like this was a hidden flaw.

We KNOW that the contents of summoned bags to 'poof' when you linkdie. We KNOW THIS!!!

So, you should KNOW that when you put something in a summoned bag that isn't itself summoned, that you are taking a real risk with a real object.

For things like mammoth tusks which are heavy and huge, there's not a lot of options, but WHO would fill a DIM bag with FBR'S? Erud's Revered Name! What were you thinking!?! It may be annoying, but it's like betting your house on a turn of the roulette wheel. Should you REALLY be surprised when you lose it?

(yes it's a pain, but, again, it's a KNOWN pain and there's no excuse for getting caught out by it in this fashion.)
