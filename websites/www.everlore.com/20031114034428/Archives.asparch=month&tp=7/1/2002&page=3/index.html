Gamers Haven has opened its doors!
Skvorkian [7/31/2002-1:21:50 PM]  Comments[6]

New gaming site opens:

After months of planning and creation, Gamers Haven has finally been opened to the public in the form of an open beta test. Gamers is a way to play classic pen and paper RPGs online, what does it have to do with EverQuest? ... Glad you asked! Gamers Haven is not only an outlet for paper gamers to play their games but also a way for online video games to collect and share with each other, be it guild meetings or friendly conversation. The website will cover as much as possible in the gaming community and everyone's input is more then welcome, so pop on over if you get a chance and check us out at Gamers Haven and keep on gaming!

You can email them here.
