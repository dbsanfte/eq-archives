Reminder: EQ Gathering Event!
[9/16/1999-4:04:11 PM]  Comments[0]

Reminder for the Largest EQ event around. "The Real Life Gathering". There will be lots of food and drink plus much more. We from Everlore.com will be attending this event. Currently they have over 183 people attending!

**NOTE** The meet will not take place at the Veteran's Memorial Park. The location is of the meet is to be announced soon.

On Saturday, October 2, a large group of players of the massively multiplayer online game EverQuest will be meeting at Veteran's Memorial Park in Culver City. For most of the attendees, it will be the first time meeting people with whom they have interacted exclusively from within an online world. The game is set in the sword and sorcery genre has developed its own subculture. It has been described as the "digital version of crack" (Hunter Godfrey, quoted in Wired Online), as many players spend as much of their free time as possible playing the game. Since the adventure of the game is completely dependent upon the interaction between the players, EverQuest has put to rest the commonly held assumption that computer gaming is a solitary activity. So what will happen when these online aficionados get together in flesh and blood for a day of merriment in the real world? We will soon find out.

The event began as a casual post by player Kevin Scott on an Internet site devoted to the game, inviting fellow players in the Los Angeles area to "come hang out at a park or something". Over the course of the following few days, the scope of the event grew to include over 150 attendees. Some attendees will be coming from as far as Florida to attend the event. The get together has also drawn the attention of several representatives of Verant Interactive, one of the three companies responsible for bringing EverQuest to life. These members include the Verant CEO, John Smedley, and several members of the game's development team, who will be attending the event.

For a description of the game, please visit www.everquest.com. To keep up to date on the developing plans for this event, please visit: http://www.csunbooks.com/eq

GM Quest -Al'Kabor
Ron Parker [9/17/1999-2:54:00 PM]  Comments[0]

A GM turned into a NPC named Al'Kabor and started giving out a Quest. Unfortunatly, people have spend time in trying to do the quest then Al'Kabor Left.

ooc: Last night a GM possessed the body of Al'Kabor in Qeynos on the Prexus server. This was quite nice I had been waiting for the chance to participate in a GM run event or quest.

I was walking through Qeynos and heard Al'Kabor speaking as only a real person could, so when he did not show up on tell I knew he had to be a GM or an NPC. I am familiar with many of our GMs and did not recognize the name. So, I assumed it was a GM controlled NPC.

After some searching I found an Erudite by this name in a rented room. After discussion about needing privacy for research and how one should lock their doors to obtain privacy, I was graciously given the quest of returning 2 Kiola nuts to him. I assumed this was for his research. I thought this was too easy and enquired of him about it. Being assured that was indeed what he wanted I went on my way. Later others told that this was a hard and dangerous quest suited to my abilities, since I am only of the 13th season.

ooc: I had to leave my computer for a few minutes and left a friend piloting me across West Karanas since I believed time was of the essense. Unfortunately he was accustomed to having camoflague and ran me right past Choon. I reentered the room only to witness my rapid demise.

Not to be disuaded, given the rare chance at a real quest, I started my journey again being resurrected in Qeynos. This time I bypassed my body for the sake of time and made the whole trip with a nearly naked. And a naked dwarf is not a pretty sight. After some time my trip was completed and I had the Kiola Nuts in my possession. I safely returned to Qeynos and attempted to complete the quest with Al'Kabor.

There were many others in Qeynos seeking him out to complete their tasks as well. After a short period of time I located him and upon entering the room he disappeared. Many of us spent the next hour looking for him to no avail.

6 on 6 GM Tournament
[9/20/1999-2:53:14 PM]  Comments[0]

The following event will take place on the Xegony Server.

Today on the Xegony server GM Daegarmo held exhibition matches in the arena too prepare for Sunday's 6 on 6 PVP tourney. This was more or a less a trial and error and testing event too see what rules should be applied so that the tournament is ran well. The rules are as follows; 6 on 6 level 45 and up players, with each team too take opposite sides of the platform in the arena. Each team member must stand in a square on the edge of the platform so this spaces the 2 groups out very well. (The down side too this is classes who often rely on Area Effect spells such as Magicians and Enchanters are put at a slight disadvantage. Not that it matters as AE spell hurt your party also in the arena.)

There will be no pre fight buffs, GM's will dispel you b4 the tourney starts. (If one of the advantages too your class is buffs such as Shamans, Enchanters and Clerics, you will have a disadvantage here).

You can not use Charm, Fear or Pets. (The obvious disadvantage here is too enchanters as their primary function is charming and buffing, and I'm sure necros and shamans would like pets but unfortunately the arena has no pathing so pets poof when summoned)

You can not leave the area of the platform or its grounds for immediate disqualification.

Other than that its pretty much everything goes, I saw a guy use a egg shaped pumice (this removes all buffs but in his case it removed his root , so I'm not sure if these will be allowed in the tournament or not) and journeyman boots so I assume they are ok. In closing the tournament should go well and as long as you're not an enchanter (because you can not charm, fear, pre-buff or use AE mesmerize and AE stuns as they hurt your party in the arena) the tourney should work out well.

For those who are upset that enchanters are very limited in this tournament do not blame Daegarmo, it is not his fault that pathing does not work in the arena, it is not his fault AE spells also affect your party as the arena was designed for 1 on 1 tournaments not groups, and because you can not leave the platform that is the reason you can not use fear spells.

The tournament is next Sunday so start getting your team together and practicing, the grand prize is a Fanged Skull Stiletto.

-Tim

There we will get organized, form groups, wait for late people, etc. Groups for the assault force will be formed of people levels 35 to 50, best combo would be 3 fighter types, 3 caster types, with at least 2 people able to heal in some way also (paladins, druids, etc).

At 9 PM we will march on to Neirak. We'll kill any outside guards along the way, and once inside, after clearing the main gate, we will head for the Arena in the foreign quarter. The main goal of this raid is to take and hold the arena.

Those under level 35 are welcome to come, but you must do so at your own risk. You will most likely die in there if you aren't careful, and if you do die we won't be able to stop our attack just so you can get to your corpse. If you're very low level, beware of NPCs casting area spells, and also come naked so if you do die you won't lose anything but a little xp.

This isn't a raid to get loot. This isn't a raid to get experience. This is a raid to just have fun! I know a lot of evil types want to defend their home, and I think that's great, but I ask you use common sense and don't exploit flaws in the game (ie training guards on us while we meditate, etc) to do so, this will only result in /petitions and /reports against you that could get you in lotsa trouble. Please be creative :) I'm sure there will be a few of us in the attack force that might accept a duel too, and since we'll be clearing the arena, you can defend your home there.

If we don't get enough people, we can still hunt inside Neirak. It's a great spot for high 30's - low 40's xp and decent loot. So, hopefully we can get a full assault going, if not we can still kill a few smurfs and have fun :)

If you'd like to come, email me at eldrad@timkline.com
