And not only do we look the same, but we look stupid. We look like a bunch of goons in hobo clothes. Monks also know the feeling of being limited to leather...we just don't look cool.

I am a level 24 wood elf druid.

I thought my tree-embroidered brown suit was cool for a while, but it sure got old fast.

Realizing this I had set out to get a suit of chain. This would be really, really cool. People would think I was a ranger at first glance! I would look different from every other druid! People would know me as the druid in metal!

Very excited, I began contacting other druids to compile a list of chain items that I could wear, so that I could begin obtaining them.

Some of the items were very good:

Head - Runed Circlet. 4AC 4wis/int.

Wrists - Chipped Bone Bracelet. 4AC 5wis.

Feet - Dwarven Work Boots. 8AC 3str 5dex.


Some of the items were mediocre:

Arms - Bone Armplates. 5AC +5str -4dex.

Legs - Bone Leggings. 6AC +5sta -4agi.

I was willing to make these sacrifices and wear them, for the sake of looking cool. (I'm a superficial guy, what can I say?)

This leaves out a couple visible slots:

Hands - I'm told that there are indeed some griffon-talon gloves out there that are chain look and druid-wearable. The gloves are nothing too special (4ac 2dex I think) but are extremely rare and something I probably would never obtain. I was willing to wear leather gloves anyway since chain gloves on a wood elf look like bare hands with chain on the back, behind the knuckles, and leather looks appropriate even with a suit of chain.

Chest - Nothing! The most important slot and there's NOTHING OUT THERE. Nothing besides vermiculated chain, which is from the plane of fear and no drop. I have no hope of getting this. And if I did get it, it's tinted green and looks stupid with the wood elf two-tone chain.


Picturing what I would look like with all chain except a leather chestpiece, you could imagine my disappointment to learn that verant had implemented druid chain for all visible slots but one.


I really like the look of full chain, especially on wood elves, and I think that it should be a viable look for druids who are so inclined. As such, I have asked verant to implement some form of chain tunic wearable by druids. Be it in the form of a new item, or an old item converted to chain-look, I feel it is a reasonable request, as there exists such a piece for every other visible slot. It would be nice if it were a good item, but I would wear it even if it were AC 1.

If you agree with me, please post your opinion here as well as performing a /feedback in everquest and letting verant know how you feel. (I have)

If you want to add to what I have said, please share your opinion in this forum.

If you disagree with me, I would love to hear a counterargument (I haven't heard one yet.)

-Kline, Povar server.
