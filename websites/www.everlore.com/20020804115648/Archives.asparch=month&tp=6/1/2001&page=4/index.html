Gloves of Fire Animation - Developers Corner
Xott the Wizard [6/20/2001-4:53:24 PM]  Comments[9]

I e-maild Absor about this, just to double check, and the same applies to all animations that are different on different races. Thought it was worth a read. A player wrote:

My problem: Gloves of Fire (some stats, effect, no drop, mag/wiz) that drops in .
If you put these gloves on Hight Elf or Gnome they are awesome looking. The colour is bright, even neon red. The best looking gloves in the game.

Why is this? Why do they look so godly on other races and on dark elf they look like you had been grovelling in mud a moment ago? Is there any way to get them to look on Dark Elf the same as they look on High Elf? Is this a bug or just a bad feature because of some shading in graphics or such? I know, there is a plethora of more important things in the game yes. This one just happened to affect me much. I was looking forward to them and I have red dress and those 2 glowing red globes and the neon red fiery gloves would have been exellent for my look. Now it's not so. Please (on my knees) could you ask quickly about this from the graphics guy? Is there any way to get these gloves look like they should on Dark Elves?

You know the saying: "Style before everything."

Thank you for your time and keep up the good work there.

Sincerely
Dthamilaye K'Tang, 54th magician in Tunare

The answer is a little complicated.

Each race has a look, a texture, to their armor pieces. When tinting a piece of armor that tinting is, basically, placed on top of that texture (blended with it, sorta). So the actual color of the armor after the tinting won't be uniform for each race that wears it. This is, really, a good thing, adding variety to the armors that we create. But it also means that there is no way to get a uniform look across all the races.

Velious Armor Confusion
Xott the Wizard [6/22/2001-7:41:05 PM]  Comments[16]

Hail Norrathians!
I've received a few e-mails regarding Velious armor not looking the way it is supposed to. Please allow me to explain why this is.

First off, you must have installed the Scars of Velious expansion, obviously.

Secondly, in your everquest directory, there is a file named eqclient.ini. Open this file up in notepad or wordpad and verify that the following lines are there, and are set to "true" and not "false":

LoadVeliousArmor=TRUE
LoadArmor17=TRUE
LoadArmor18=TRUE
LoadArmor19=TRUE
LoadArmor20=TRUE
LoadArmor21=TRUE
LoadArmor22=TRUE
LoadArmor23=TRUE

NOTE:Turning on these options will increase the Everquest server load time. (This is the screen that comes up after you select your server). Don't be worried when you notice it's taking up to a minute longer than usual, it's just loading the armour. Well worth the wait IMHO.

If your still confused, feel free to post comments and I will attempt to help you the best I can. Alternatively you can e-mail me at xott@everlore.com

EDIT:

After e-mailing VI's CS department, I got this reply:

Hello,

Thank you for contacting Sony Online Entertainment Customer Service department. My name is Domenico Scaduto and I am pleased to assist you with your problem. After reviewing your email, I understand your issue to be Velious armor related.

These are the command lines that load all the Velious armor files. You should have the following:

LoadVeliousArmor=TRUE
LoadArmor17=TRUE
LoadArmor18=TRUE
LoadArmor19=TRUE
LoadArmor20=TRUE
LoadArmor21=TRUE
LoadArmor22=TRUE
LoadArmor23=TRUE
LoadArmor24=TRUE
These lines must be under 'defaults'.

So there you have it, If these lines do not show up in your EQclient.ini directory, you may want to add them, or contact VI's Coustomer Service at eqtech@verant.com.

Member of the Light of Harmony
