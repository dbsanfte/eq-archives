Faction, not fraction. This isn't math. ;-)

The best way to know is to go wherever you want to check faction standing, then stand far far away from whichever npc it is you want to check, then con them (use c key or /con command). You can tell, by how they regard you, how they feel about you. Ex: "Scowls at you, ready to attack" means they don't really want to see you... on the other hand, messages like "warmly" and "amiable" means they like you.

To be really safe about this, go invis, might have a better chance of living if you accidently stray too close to someone/something that doesn't like ya.

Con everything.

Be aware that it IS possible to con amiable to the GUARDS of a city, yet be KOS to the MERCHANTS of that city.
