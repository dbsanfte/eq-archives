Sadly, by our general appearance we broadcast that we are Druids. Maybe EQ will do something about this and have us appear differently.

Going Anon helps only a little.

I have given myself a set of rules that I stick to on casting spells for others.

1 - If you are in my party, you get BUFs.

2 - If you are in trouble, I heal you without question and reward. A thanks is always appreciated but not necessary.

3 - If you just seem to be a good person, I am more than willing to give out SOWs and Binds.

4 - If you are a newbie I give you spells just to be nice and helpful.

5 - I never give any spells to players who are Anon unless they are in Roleplaying mode. A tythe is required from Anons.

6 - A token of esteem works when I am just tired of getting pleas.

7 - If you just pester me with tells and whine, I remind you that our spells are expensive and we need money to buy those spells. Additionally, they are taking time away from me doing what I want to do which is adventure as well as taking away my precious mana. A good druid can make about 15 to 20 platinum at minimun an hour adventuring when you are at or around level 14. Why not require a token for your your lost experience?

8 - I am not the only druid out there. Do not buddy me and bother me every time you need something. Helping a friend and being nice is one thing. I do not like to be taken advantage of.

9 - The game should be enjoyable to all. Sometimes I sit around heavy area and BUF, SoW, and bind for free when I am not in the mood to play right then. I do not ask nor accept money. I should have the right to play without harassment just like anyone else when I want to. I am a Druid and not a Paladin or Cleric who enjoys the company of others. I am also not bound by duty as a Druid to forgo everything to help everyone as Paladins, Clerics, or Monks should.

Anyway, I did not mean to rant a little at the end, but you all understand my point.

I just wanted to reiterate something in my earlier post. I do give away free binds and SoWs. I will not, however spend all of my gaming time performing these services. I have been given my share of freebies and have given my share as well. I will be giving more.

I do not advertise my services for a cost. Some of you do. If that is how you want to make your money, then more power to you. I am not against someone offering me a tythe, however. But I will not ask for one unless they are being a jerk, overbearing, or whining as I mentioned in precept number 7. I think my reasoning is valid.

As for making the money, Bronze weapons sell for a lot. Does not take long to get a few fighting the Sisters or killing in the Derv camps. Willowisps are worth a ton of money too. All you have to do is find 2 greater lightstones in an hour and boom, you can sell them to a player for 10pp each or turn in for quest and make about 15pp. Of course that does not include the other stuff you get while hunting such as Lightstones, money, etc. Easy money for level 14 and 15 I would say. The experience is not too bad either.

I am very giving actually as anyone who has met and played with me knows that I BUF and heal people for free. I beleive that the game would be more fun for all if we did this. I do this with passing strangers and people who are in need. But alas, as in real life there are people out there who just are not nice. You are not obligated to help, so why not charge. There are also others who understand that you are being nice and taking time out for them.
