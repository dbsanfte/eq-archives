IMHO a bard would be your best choice as I will explain below:

-You get a song every LVL and you don't need mana
-With Hymn of Restoration you never have to sit around for hours on end waiting for your health to recover
-With Selo's Accelerando you never need SoW
-You can wear almost all armor types
-With Larisa's Locating Lyric you don't have to run around in endless circles looking for your corpse
-With Jaxan's Jig o' Vigor you can run for hours on end with no food or water and never get tired

With these songs and many more you can and will be a definite assest to any group your in. Unfortunaty there are some down sides too. You don't have the staying power in heated battles unless well protected by your group and when you play your song while attacking you will become the main target. Also you are limited to 1 handed weapons and the higher the LVL you are I've found that you LVL slower than others.

Anyway enough of my rambling been a long day I hope this will help somewhat.

I must agree with these other posts. I too considered the Druid and/or Ranger. However the issue these other posts did not address is this: The Druid is a fine solo character, like the Ranger. Bards are by nature (and necessity) social animals. Like the Druid, some of our songs do not work indoors, but I think less so. We do not have restrictions on creatures we can kill either. When I was a young tooter, in the Qeynos Hills, I was attacked by a Grey Wolf. I was holding my own (barely) when a Ranger came up to me and threateningly said "Leave that wolf alone!" Being one to appreciate a Role-Player, and Bards being quick with the come-back; I imediately responded "Nay, tell the wolf to leave me alone!" Whatever your choice, roleplay it!
