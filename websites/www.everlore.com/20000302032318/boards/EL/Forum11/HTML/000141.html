From level 6 - 15 I fought on the tundra. At level 6 it was Wolves and Ice Goblin Scouts, then on to Polar Bears, Skellies, Giant Spiders, Cats and Orc Troopers; stick close to One guard.

Once you hit level 9 or so, find a group and head out to North or South Tower and look for higher level Troopers, etc, and start to fight the weaker Orc Mouties and Mammoth Calves.

Around Level 11 you can start to kill Orc Shamen and Ice Boned Skellies. Calves, Mounties, Ice Bones and Shamen can take you right up to 15 or 16 if you so desire (actually Shamen can take you beyond even that, but it would be slow going).

I truly hated trying to level in BB myself, but found the Tundra to be a lot more fun, especially with a good group. I'll be 15 today and in search of greener pastures, but I'll always be happy to return to our snowy home (In my 20's it's Mammas and Permafrost ).

Hope that helped.
