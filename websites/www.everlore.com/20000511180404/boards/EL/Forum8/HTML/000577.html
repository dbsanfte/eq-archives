Ok im a serious roleplayer and i think it would be fun to have a day or more of dark classes fighting light classes. (or the spoils of war) Just maby one day of extremly huge pvp battles then the next day of prizes and back to normal life of questing and adventuring again. You can use any of your characters in the battle and all characters would get a message to come to their hometown to prepare for the fight. Before a battle, binds would be given out, then some sort of signal for the start. Or if you liked, surprise attacks from full groups teleporting with invis on and suddenly attacking.
