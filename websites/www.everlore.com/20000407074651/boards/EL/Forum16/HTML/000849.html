2 Points to make:

1) Silvery war axes sell for around 250-300 plat on Bristlebane; they ARE worth the cash, even for a difference in delay of one point; you don't have to pay plat for one at level 24...go to Permafrost - the exp is excellent for you at that level and if you get lucky you can squeeze into a group in the King room (easier to do at lvl 26 or 27, though) and loot yourself a Silvery axe - they are fairly common drop from the Elites.

2) As far as Ogre and Troll stamina making it easier to deal with heavy weapons: I've seen to posts from people that have researched this aspect of Stat effectiveness, and BOTH stated taht there was NO difference in the rate of stamina being used by high STA and low STA warriors. Seems like Verant blew it here when they were writing this part of the code.

My suggestion for good 1h slash weapons are as follows: get two langseaxs asap. Those will take you far. Wait til about level 18 or so and then camp a PGT.
