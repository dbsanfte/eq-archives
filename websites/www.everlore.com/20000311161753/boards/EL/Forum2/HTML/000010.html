Have been trying to interact with NPC's for awhile now. Especially between fun time in BB.

I came to the conclussion that NPC interaction is very limited. Even down to saying hail to some characters they don't respond.

Understandably there are a number of characters in the game with some background etc, most of the gaurds in Qeynos have a small personality, but other characters don't have any or are limited.

Would it be difficult to attach some personality to characters? A gamesmaster in an AD&D type scenario would try and give NPC's a bit of background, even if it was a gruff exterior.

In my roleplaying days (Way back), I would enjoy the atmosphere that a good GM could produce when entering and sitting around in a Tavern Inn.

You can enter a bar/tavern in qeynos, where NPC's stand around. These are great spawning grounds for stories, and quests. Making things more hustle and bustle at the inns, would entice people in.

If there is anyone on the Povar server in Qeynos, who fancies a sing song and drinking contest, give me a /tell some time
