Site Mostly Down
Xomiach [5/27/1999-2:05:16 PM]  Comments[0]

It appears that for a greater portion of Thursday the 27th the Everlore site was down. We have rectified the problem. However, it has highlighted the fact that with the dramatic growth of the site we need to upgrade our servers. We have two new machines that will be dedicated to Everlore only. They will go on-line sometime in the next couple of weeks. This should dramatically increase the speed of the site. Also, Eldwin has been combing over the code on the site and tweaking things to maximise speed. We hope that our site trouble hasn't inconvenienced you. Thanx for your patience and all the good posts!

 

EverQuest's Live-Chat
Sketsa [5/30/1999-4:49:35 PM]  Comments[3]

Hail to all Adventures of Norrath, I would just like to spread the news of a New EQ Site (EverQuest Live-Chat) dedicated to All Adventures of Norrath! This chat site is new and there is little activate there at this time, but with your help (Citizens of Norrath) this will change soon. I hope this live-chat will turn into a gathering of guilds and players from all of Norrath to exchange knowledge, share ideas and make new friends. We will be adding many EQ Sites to the EverQuest Live-Chat to form a EverQuest Chat Ring. By doing this you can visit your favorite EQ site and join in on a Live Chat with many others. We are in the process of adding new members now. Some areas of the site are still being set up but we need your help in testing the chat areas, so go by and say hail to other Adventures of Norrath.... The website can be found at : http://alterdlogix.com/eq_chat.htm ~Sketsa~
