With all this focus on the new expansion pack nobody has brought this up yet, but I am personally excited for the expansion for this reason:
All those people that have high level characters on other servers, who became bored and came over to Luclin, will be rushing back to their old servers now that they can progress again. They had reached the pinnacle, so came to Luclin to start anew. But now that the pinnacle has been raised (so to speak), their old characters have some new challenges to face.
Yippee!!
I don't know exactly how much of Luclin is comprised of these types of people, but I would be willing to bet at least half of the server is.
That means that at prime time, Luclin should only have four or five hundred people on it instead of 1000. And this means no more lists for spawns and much less camping and play nice issues. For someone like me, who's main character IS on Luclin, this is going to be great!

So let's hear it! How many of you fit in this category? Do you have a higher level character on another server that you are anxiously waiting to take to Kunark?
Or am I way off in my thinking here?
