Kiting is still an option and can be done may ways. The above way of using direct damage is great for mobs you dont want to melee with, but you can still use the dot spells with mobs you can melee. I have been able to raise my offense, defense, and 1hs up considerable with this option. South Karana is great with the Aviaks and Centaurs offering great melee options and no faction hits. Start by using a damage shield on yourself medding the mana back, then starting the fray by a dd strike, then dot and melee. I would recommend more dd's as your mob varies but try and save the mana as much as you can. The nerf on dots changed the way it works but if you can stand and fight with your mob the damage is actually increased by 10% over the old method.
