I received this from Verant today. I did repatch later and noticed that I received no warnings. Alert over.

---------------------------------------------

It appears that we did patch two MS-Word '97 format Doc files that were
infected with a relatively benign macro virus.

These viruses can only infect other files (and then only other Doc
files) if they are opened in your word processor, so people who didn't
open them prior to right now need not be alarmed. We at Verant
proactively work to eliminate the possibility that things such as this
happen, however some safeguards were unintentionally bypassed as party
of the Kunark opening. We've closed this minor loophole and customers
should not have to worry about anything such as this happening in the
future. The files on the Kunark Patch have been cleaned. They will be
replaced the next time everyone runs the "Kunark Patch" option on the
patch server, and they should do so prior to attempting to open either
of those *.DOC files.

We would like to extend our apologies to everyone on this matter.
