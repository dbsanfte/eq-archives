Borrow a Bard! I'm a Bard of 11th level and now make the run to HPass on a regular basis. Bards (or anyone) can do a quest running mail to and from HighPass. Clarification: Molds available in Qeynos hills for Scalers (Best way to improve after Sharpening stones). Highpass Keep is closest supply of ore. You need the stackable pieces of ore to make "Metal Bits" that you will use to make Scalers. I always buy as many as I can afford and lug them around the corner to the bank and RUN back to Qeynos. There is a forge in HPass, but its easy to get jumped by gnolls or bandits while looking for it. You can use just metal bits, scalers and skewers for quite a while. You can make studs from metal bits and (if market supports) sell them to Tailors. Trick is with stones or pieces of ore; buy & bank as much as you can at once!
