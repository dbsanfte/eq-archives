I just recently started Alchemy. I'm only lvl 21, so I didn't get to practice, but my very first try worked.. and was trivial. Go figure.

There are 2 ways to make the cha potion... the easy way (2sp plant and rat ears) or the hard way (8pp plant and undead froglok tongues from lower guk I think ) As far as I can tell they are the exact some thing, except they have different pics (easy = like the skin of milk you get in the begining, hard = cute little purple bottle which I REALLY like ) The hard one MIGHT last longer, but I didn't really care to time them.

You only really need them to buy/sell things, so I just make the cheap ones, and sell them fairly cheap. I mean seriously, who wants to pay a whole lot of money to save a little. Think people.

My main complaint is they are NOT STACKABLE! Which sucks.. a lot of people wanted to buy alot and keep them handy for later, but they didn't have enough room. We would make a LOT more profit if they were stackable.

Potions are just portable shamans. Don't think you can drink 80 cha potions and buy everything super cheap. Potion only cast the spell. Cha potion casts Spirit of Snake. The good thing is they cannot be interrupted!! That rules for gate. Talk about a good get-a-way plan. As well as good healing

My other complaint is the items are not easy to get. I know I'm lazy, but some is just nuts. I have yet to see a HQ wolf skin (yes, skin) and anyways, what would a lvl 25 shaman want to kill a bunch of little wolves for. Spending 5 hours killing wolves for a few pp just isn't my thing. I also have never heard of aviak feathers, but I have yet to check out Lake Rathe.

But if all goes well... bind and healing potions will make us ALOT of profit. Warriors will LOVE healing potions and hopefully give us LOTS of money. WOOHOOO!! But like I said.. they REALLY need to be stackable.

I'll tell you more when I know more. Much love to you all
