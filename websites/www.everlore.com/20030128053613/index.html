Fieldwork Dallas, a market research company is conducting focus groups with males (18+ years) who play Role Playing Games (RPG's) and or Massively Multi-Player Online Role Playing Games (MMORPG's) online. MMORPGs research at CMU - HELP! Comments[15]
 
Doyle [1/21/2003-9:24:29 AM]
 

Kevin Lee sent us the following article regarding a MMORPG research study. Here's the details for those interested in participating:

A research team at Human-computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University is conducting on a project that looks at three major MMORPGs (AO, EQ, DAOC) and studies cscw techniques in MMORPGs. We need active volunteers to go to the following site and fill out the online questionnaire.

At the end of this research project, we will draw players from the poll and reward "three free months in the MMOG of your choice" as a token of our appreciation. The questionnaire is at http://www.homenetresearch.org/massive/

Thank you for your participation.
Any questions? please email me at kevinsanglee@hotmail.com

Thanks again.
