I wouldn't get rid of all of the Bronze if I were you, just a few selected pieces. Try swapping the Bronze Cloak and Boots for Chanda Millers Bear Hide Cape(AC6 Wt 2.5) and Boots(AC5 Wt 2.5) - both very cheap. Then swap the Bronze Girdle for a Thick Leather Apron (AC8 Wt 1.0 - 20pp) and Bronze Pauldron for a Bloodstained Mantle(AC6 +3Str +10 MR Wt 3.5 - 40pp). Now, you are correct, keep your +Wis jewelry and start saving for a Silver Ruby Veil(AC0 +4 Str +4 Wis Wt.1 - 150pp). At this point your Str should be fine and you can focus on the more expensive +Wis items (which usually have low weight). Hope this helps, good luck!

Well, i think that perhaps this is a mistake that most ppl make. I have a lvl 38 cleric and have looked at and made a few twinks. As well as helped my relatives with a few. Really, the armor at most lvl's is better if you stay with what was intended. For instance, get rid of the rings and swap out the bracers. You should be using jade electrum/gold ones for rings and bracers. Until you get into mid to late 20's the +mana ones are more efficient. They have ac and +hp's as well and i believe are lighter. My lower level characters use obsidian bead hoops as well, each +10 mana. Other than those couple items the post above is some good advice. You can probably buy the jade jewelry relatively cheap as people try their hands at jewelry making.

For all the sceptics just go to any mana calculater and do the math.
