Wow, touchy subject indeed.

It's an interesting thread, the age matter. I myself did not even get into the online gaming until I was in college. No-one should have the right to tell anyone what to do with their time and resources (except in some very specific circumstances.)

There are a million reasons that people might spend time here. Dislike of people in the real world, inability to go out and do things (handicap), perhaps they dislike being shunned for their appearance. EQ negates that judgement. Perhaps its a way of escaping a painful personal situation. Perhaps someone likes the feeling of accomplishment that they can't seem to get from a job which brings little or no pleasure. Some may like the feeling of teamwork and camaraderie that just isn't available in their real life situations. This includes people of ALL ages. It may help younger folk get some verbal and social skills they might not be able to acquire outside of this situation. Perhaps due to being restricted to home. You never can tell.

It's easy to say, "Get out there and DO something!" But every person is not the same. What one person enjoys (clubbing, dancing, etc) may set another's teeth positively on edge. I agree that there is more to life than EverQuest. It's good to find a balance, but if this is the one thing in life that brings someone pleasure and enjoyment, I say enjoy!

My apologies for rambling on so, but i've been reading Everlore BBs for a while today and I felt the need to respond to this thread.

Safe and Happy hunting to you all.
