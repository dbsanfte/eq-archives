For fast and easy money try Misty Thicket. I was able to get the 20 plat for my Bloodforge Hammer (which at the price is the greatest deal going in a druid weapon) in a short period of time. The Bixies there drop honeycombs that high level Wizzos will Pay up to 1 plat for each! I am not making that up! The other night I raised 20plat in less than an hour just selling honeycombs! The other big seller is bat wings. 2 plat for 20! Needless to say I have been choking a LOT of bats! I have no idea the exact spell they are using these things for (I am told it is a level 34 spell) but they have the plat and are willing to pay. At my level (5) I am a bixie magnet, so just stand close to the hill just outside the entrance to Rivervale and enjoy.

The other hot ticket was Spiderling eyes, again, I don't know what they want them for, but they were bringing 5GP each.

One of the advantages of being a low level is that you can harvest the honeycombs and wings from the same kill that a high level gets nothing from. Its a good arrangement for both of you. If you find a good high level sponser they will buff you up to the max (almost 200 pts in buffs plus SOW! Amazing!) and then you hunt! You get lots of plat and XP on stuff you normally couldn't take down, and your turn around time with them healing you is lots faster. A good arrangement for all.

I did have good high level friends that SOWed and Invised me in order to get to Riverdale, it has been more than worth the journey. The game and spawn rate there is wonderful, and the overall feeling of green woods and forest appeals to my druid nature. It is a hazardous trip at the lower levels but well worth it, and there is a druid guild right here if you want to play a halfling. Even if you dont there is a ready source of spells. Hope to see you all here!

This has kind of been mentioned above but not very clearly:

(1) There is a quest in the game where a NPC (named gupsy in one of the Karanas) - not making it to easy here - buys Greather lightstones for 9 to 10 pp AND give you expiriense on top of that.

(2) Willow Wisps can be killed throughout a number of relative low levels with very little risk and ok exp.

(3) There is an entire Island of willows in the game and a lot of willows in (other) relative newbee areas.

(4) All other NPC merchants only pay 4 pp for Greather lightstones so players keep selling them in large numbers on auction for 5 to 6 pp.

So I you think money is that important what are you waiting for.

The above is the number one money maker for lower levels because it only requires a starting capital of 5 pp or the level to kill your own wisps. If you have the playing expiriense required to survive travel without getting into fights you can make a fortune before level 5. And the neat thing is that you will not fall behind in levels because the quest gives very good xp at lower levels.

Make a fortune early this way and start keeping an eye on the auctions while playing the normal camping for pp and exp at later levels. Just like in real life it is a lot easier to make money if you have money.
