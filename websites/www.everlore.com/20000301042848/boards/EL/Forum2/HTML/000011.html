Ok here's what you do. When you want to learn languages, wait for someone to /shout about teaching languages, or just /shout "Can anyone teach me a language?" and then someone might be willing to teach you Elvish or Erudian, or another lang, for a price or for free. What THEY then do is, spam you with the language enough times so your levels will raise enough for you to learn the language. Then when you speak it, others who know it will understand you. The languages I currently know are: Barbarian, Erudian, Elvish, Troll, Gnomish, Thieves Cnt, Combine Tongue, Dragon, Elder Dragon, Gnollish, Dwarvish, Ogre, Tier Dal, Old Erudian, Halfling and Faerie.
