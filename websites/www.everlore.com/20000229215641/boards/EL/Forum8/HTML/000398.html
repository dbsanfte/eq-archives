Iksar, or whatever the right spelling is, is the Lizardman race on Kunark, another evil race by the way, Cazic-Thule is their main god. Recently Casters Realm had a post with all the Lizzie Classes along with the intro message you get when you start your character. The classes are, if I remember them all, Shadowknight, Necromancer, Warrior, Shaman, and Monk. Their classes, according to the intros, are determined before their birth. They're supposedly hated and/or feared by all other races and only safe in their home city of Calibus.
