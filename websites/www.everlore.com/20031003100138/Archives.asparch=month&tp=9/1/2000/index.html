possible way to get gloves to proc ABASHI i qoute you in here

These are very different situations, and I'm sorry but I can't explain it much more detail. In the case you mentioned, the hand, foot, shoulder slots are not accessed upon each hit. The ability of a character to hit, kick, slam magic creatures is set for the character upon equipping or removing the item, rather than by accessing the item upon each combat round. PENDING GOD CHANGES AND THE REPRECUSSIONS.

Anybody who did not buy Kunark would be losing a feature they always had, the ability to go on raids on the planes and face down the Gods.

Not really, there is nothing to say that they can't participate in one form or another. But even if we assume that what you are saying is basically true, they are losing the ability to kill an NPC named "XXX". That's basically it.

Here's the situation. The deities and dragons are meant to be top level encounters. We made an exception with Vox and Nagafen and kept them where they were even though the level cap went up. In order to do this, we had to place the level restriction on those encounters.

The same situation is present with the deities, and the solution that we have is a compromise. Vox and Nagafen remained the same yet higher level folks can't attack them, and the deities will increase in power, so the lower level folks won't be able to attack them. At the same time we are taking the loot from the current deities and moving it to NPCs that are equivalent in power to the current deities.

Leaving things as they are now is not a reasonable option. This is the best option available to us.

-Gordon
