Ok, what you need to look for is a brown chitin protector. I am pretty sure we can use it, and i know it doesnt give the leather look. I know of some other pieces, but i cant think of the names off of the top of my head. This is for any newer druids that are still using the leather or rawhide skull cap. If you dont like the stupid beanie look that it gives you, go west freeport, and as you face the front gate from the outside, go to your right, there is a set of tents out there, and occasionally a gnome will spawn that sells wigs. You can get one with the same ac as the skull cap, the only problem is that they are heavy. But carrying the extra weight was worth not looking stupid to me. Hope i helped some of you fashion crazed druids.

Comparing classes is a lot like the apples/oranges argument. If you read the other class boards, you will see all the clases complaining how everyone else's class is better, has better stuff.. better soloing, etc. I can speak from experience regarding Palidans and druids.. as I have a lvl 20 pali and lvl 14 druid. Both are great classes... however, comparing the two is really impossible.

Yes.. the palidan has higher AC and ATK and HPs... but, by lvl 14 or so.. the druid can blow a palidan away in terms of soloing. The versitility of the druid is incredible and makes it a ton of fun (in my opinion) to play.

If you like more melee, then I'd go w/ a pali or warrior type... if you want versatility, the druid is great. Just depends what you want.


Just understand, all the classes have thier pros and cons. I LOVE playing my palidan just cause of his persona. I play him as I believe he should be.. a holy knight, dedicated to fighting evil and helping those in need. In comparison.. my druid is a blast w/ spells like SOW and major offensive and some good defensive spells... as for melee.. I do ok with my RTS (granted.. it does kind of stink.. but it was cheap to buy).. and I have a screaming mace too.. so meleeing I do ok with my druid. WHen I group with ppl, they are amazed at the dmg I can do... then heal everyone and buff em up too.
