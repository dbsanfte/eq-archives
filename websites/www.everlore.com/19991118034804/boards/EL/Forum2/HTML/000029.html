STR = your max dmg with any weapon, your weight, ATK bonus

STA = HP increase, also possibly helps your stamina bar go down slower when running/fighting/swiming alot.

AGI = Natural ac (how much a target will miss you and how much dmg he'll hit you for), also your walk/run speed.

DEX = how many times you do something successfully (with the exclusion of tradeskills and spells if i'm not mistaken), what that means is you'll hit more, weapon effects go off more, more dmg with your weapon per hit, etc.

WIS = for the non-4pure-caster types, it determines how much mana and how you cast spells (cast times, mana usage, amount of fizzles)

INT = same as WIS but for the 4 pure casters only (Wizard, Necromancer, Enchanter, and Magician). Also determins how fast you learn skills and how much exp you gain (not sure about the last one).

CHA = determines how much an npc likes you or hates you, helps in prices mostly.
