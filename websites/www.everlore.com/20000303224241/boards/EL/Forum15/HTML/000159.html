Play Selo's Accelerando with a Hand Drum to get the best effect from it.

I've not acquired any of the other drums (Mistmoore Battle Drum or the fabled Selo's Drum of the March), but I believe the effect of the song is increased with those instruments.

You'll find that as your levels increase, and as your skill in Percussion increases, your speed will also increase; i.e., a 10th level bard using a drum will likely lose a foot race with a 20th level bard using a drum.
