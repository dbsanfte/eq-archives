What possible justification is there for shamen to have Charm Animal?

Shamen are not at one with nature, they already have a pet. What on earth was the rationale behind giving them Charm Animal?

Basically, shaman had charm for a while after release. We then removed it from them and gave them Befriend animal instead, based on the idea that they are a nature type moreso than not. When going through checking caps on certain spells in preparation for the launch of Kunark, we discoverd that Befriend Animal wasn't capped like other charm spells. So we capped it, leaving shaman unable to charm a lot of things that they could.

Charm animal, the upgrade to Befriend, was given to them to give them a portion of that power back.

First, let's sort out some definitions.

A Dynamic Quest is something that is run solely by the GM staff and not assembled specifically at players request. They range from something as simple as a minor invasion of an area (NPCs run in and kill everyone until a force comes to stop them.), or historical epics important for the storyline in the game (often run for weeks with individual phases or chapters that run for a few hours). The latter often contains a good deal of RP content, and is run across all servers.

Events, on the other hand, are typically player-initiated and organized affairs. Most of these can be done without GM assistance. Some of them, and I've seen some pretty complicated and intriguing events, require GM assistance in one form or another.

For "Events", we'll have an email address available soon, along with guidelines for requesting assistance to pull these off.
