IN A COMMUNITY SUCH AS NORRATH, THE BEST WAY TO MAKE IT BY IS TO GET ALONG WITH PEOPLE AND PLAY YOUR PART. THIS POST IS BASED ON ONE THAT I MADE IN THE DEPRESSED RANGER CATAGORY, BECAUSE I WANT TO GET INTO IT MORE.
I AM COMPLETELY OUTRAGED WHEN I HEAR PEOPLE TELL ME TO WALK ON ME FEET BECAUSE I CANNOT TELEPORT. OK I REALIZE THAT ASKING SOMEONE TAHT IS IN THE MIDDLE OF SOMETHING, OR IS IN A TOTALLY DIFFERENT ZONE IS PRETTY ANNOYING. I WOULDN'T DO THAT. I ASKED TWO DRUIDS WHO WERE IN MY ZONE TO TELEPORT ME. AND I DIDN'T TELL THEM TO MEET ME ANYWHERE. I WENT RIGHT TO WHERE THEY WERE HANGING OUT. THE BASTARDS WOULDN'T DO ANYTHING FOR ME. NOW THERE'S A LOT OF PEOPLE OUT THERE THAT ARE ASKING FOR QUICK RIDES ABOUT 10 TIMES A DAY, BUT WHAT DO YOU EXPECT, IF YOU ARE A DRUID OR WIZARD, YOU ARE THE ONLY CLASS WHO CAN DO IT. THE EXCUSE "MAKE A DRUID THEN" OR "MAKE A WIZARD THEN" JUST DOESN'T CUT IT. A DOCTOR DOESN'T SAY "YOU WANT A HEART TRANSPLANT, THEN BECOME A FUCKING DOCTOR." EVERQUEST IS A SOCIETY OF TONS OF DIFFERENT CLASSES THAT CAN OFFER DIFFEREN'T THINGS THAN OTHERS. IT'S NOT LIKE UO WHERE EVERYONE CAN JUST BUY A RUNE AND RECALL OFF OF A SCROLL. DIFFERENT CLASSES HAVE DIFFERENT CAPABILITIES WHICH MAKES THE GAME MORE DIVERSE. A CLERIC IS EXPECTED TO HEAL PEOPLE IN NEED OF REFRESHING. IF YOU MAKE A CLERIC YOU MUST EXPECT TO GET BUGGED ALL THE TIME FOR HEALING, IT'S YOUR JOB! DRUIDS AND SHAMANS GET BUGGED FOR SPIRIT OF WOLF ALL THE TIME, I GET BUGGED FOR HEALS WHEN I AM THE BEST HEALER IN THE AREA (WHICH IS NOT TOO OFTEN) I'VE ALSO BEEN BUGGED TO TANK FOR A WIZARD, AND I DID! IT'S CALLED PLAYING YOUR PART. THAT'S WHAT MAKES NORRATH GO 'ROUND. KEEP THE RESPONSES COMING. I WANT TO SEE WHAT EVERYONE ELSE THINKS OF THIS TOPIC.
