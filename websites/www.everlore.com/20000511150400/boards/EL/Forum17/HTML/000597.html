I've played everquest for a total of 4 days now and I'm going to turn to lvl 21 soon. My friend helped me out by giving me some ravenscale armour, a sbd, a dragoon dirk, and some plat. Here are my thoughts of my past 4 days:

I have never been turned down to group as a rogue. Given when I shout lvl rogue looking to group I don't get any responses but when I personally /tell a partner I always find people to group with. Groups of 2 or 3 are so good. If its just a group of 2, u and a healer preferably work out great. You start with the sneak/hide/BS combo while the healer heals you. Safer I believe is the tank/you/healer combo. I believe that the evade command is very very group friendly. You alone here dictate who gets aggro'd on. Of course you start the fight off either doing a range attack or a BS and get aggro'd take hits until you feel is right, maybe halfway, then evade, now the tank will take the rest of the hits. This way damage is evenly distributed to everyone in your group, anytime you want to take more damage to even things out don't use evade for a bit. It is the ultimate group skill. Your BS will almost always get them to aggro on you so if your group member is dying BS them to yourself. Hardly anyone ever dies in groups with me.

Other skills? I hardly waste my time with a lot of skills. I use the combat ones. BS, dbl weild, dbl attack, etc.. things that come naturally. I've been practising my throwing and bandage wound a lot lately while i step away from a fight, I'll bandage myself or throw things at the mob.

How I lvl'd: started off in westfreeport and got bounded in east freeport. Killed things just outside east freeport for a while. After that I went to EC and went with large groups orc camps or Went to the Plat at North Desert of Row and grouped with people there. Did some orc camps there, then ventured more down to the Oasis to fight sand scarabs first then caimens then crocs then DW caimens/crocs, then while still in the Oasis I went to the orc highway and fought stuff there. After this getting to about 16 or so I walked all the way to the areana and got bound there and fought at lake raith. Excellent experience for Aviax gaurds, I went up 3 lvls today to where I am now well almost 3, 1 bub away from 21. Did this all in 4 days. Of course I've had a lot of time considering they gave me time off to study for exams =p I manage to fit in a lot of starcraft hours too and do ok on my first exam...at least I hope so...damn my housemate never should have introduced me to evercrack =)
