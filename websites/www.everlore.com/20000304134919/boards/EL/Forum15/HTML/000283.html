Songis wrote,
"I am wondering how much damage other bards do while fighting hand to hand while singing. I am only doing about 5 points damage."

At 23rd level, that's my average, too. I've never done more than 8 points of damage when fighting hand to hand, and of course I miss more often than I hit.

"...should we get more monk like fighing bonuses since we sing while fighting alot and using a instrument makes us a better singer ???"

It'd be cool if bards could do more damage in melee, whether it's bare-fisted, like a monk, or with a weapon, like any other melee class. But if we did more damage, we wouldn't be bards. Bards are not monks, nor are they really like any other melee class.

Monks are meant to fight hand-to-hand, but that is because hand-to-hand combat is their main method of delivering damage. (At least from what I can tell, from grouping with them.)

Bards are forced to fight hand-to-hand because they are weilding an instrument. And it's taken me 23 levels to learn that doing damage isn't our main focus; our songs should be our main focus. It is the instrument that does the work, not our bare fists.
