E'ci: So you want to be a Millionaire, Update
Deneiele Icewind [7/7/2000-11:23:53 AM]  Comments[15]

Militia of E'ci will be holding a contest for level 30 and under on Sunday, July 9th at 1 pm CST at the ramp leading to Najena in Lavastorm Mountains.

The contest will be based on the TV show "So You Want to Be a Millionaire!".

Rules:
1) Participants will be chosen randomly from the audience in attendance.
2) Questions will be asked concerning Norrathian History.
3) There will be 3 'tiers' of participation with prizes (items) appropriate for those levels.
Levels 1-10
Levels 11-20
Levels 21-30
4) A participant, after being chosen from the audience, will be asked a question. If they provide the correct answer, they can choose to keep the item corresponding to that question or move on to the next question. If they answer the second question correctly, they can choose to keep the item or move onto the 3rd level. If they choose to move on to the 3rd level and answer that question correctly, they can either choose to keep the item corresponding to the third question OR move on to the 4th question. If the participant answers the 4th question correctly, they keep the prize money for that level as well as ALL of the items they previously passed on. At ANY time a person answers a question incorrectly, they drop out of the contest and receive a booby prize.
5) All answers MUST be given within 15 seconds to avoid outside 'help'.:)
6) Twice during each participant's round they may choose to ask ONE person from the audience to assist them with their answer.
7) All questions will be presented in a multiple choice format of 4 possible answers.

Additionally....
-Members of Militia will be at the East Commons/Nektulos Forest Zone to escort levels 1-11 ONLY through Nek forest to the contest area
-Members of Militia will also be at the zone to Qeynos Hills in Qeynos to teleport/escort levels 1-11 ONLY to Lavastorm and the contest area.
-Members of Militia will also be on hand AT the Lavastorm contest area to provide security from the beasties that roam the area.
Please make your travel plans accordingly if you are lower level and would like to attend this event :)

A total of 2000 platinum will be awarded as well as an array of wonderful items donated by the members of Militia, including:

Whip of Strangulation, Circlet of Shadows, Full set of Totemic Armor, Ravenscale greaves, Eye of Melnor, a Blackjack, Crown of the Froglok King, Runed Mithral Bracer, Rokgo's Sticker, Ice Crystal Staff, Bloodstained Tunic, Shaped Darkwood Bow, Drovacg Mantle, Black Iron Bastard Sword, Black Iron medallion, Zaharn's Coronet, Elf hide Gloves, Charred Guardian Shield, Silversilk legs, Dusty Bloodstained gloves, Serrated Bone Dirk, Sarnak Pitchatka, Grotesque Mask, 8 black pearls, 2 Jacinth, Sarnak Emblazened Tunic, Crested Spaulders, Splitpaw hide Gloves, Fire Emerald Ring, Gloomwater Harpoon, Staff of Writhing, Sarnak Great Cleaver, Burynai Leg Ripper, Skull shaped Barb Helm and MUCH more.

Hope to see you all there!
Deneiele Icewind, 51 Vicar, E'ci

The Scars of Velious -- Feature List
Catalyst [7/10/2000-8:05:04 PM]  Comments[25]

I've put together a quick run down of all known information about The Scars of Velious that was gathered by Everlore staff members from the EverQuest Gathering III.

  • One new Continent - Velious which contains 12 new zones.
  • Brand new character graphics.
  • Velious will be geared towards levels 35-60 players, although the level limit will stay at 60.
  • New Starting City for Barbarians.
  • Velious will focus primarily on new zones, quests, items and more variety in how players appear in-game.
  • Some of the new creatures include White Dragons, and frost & storm giants although there will be no new player races or classes.
  • Official Velious site will be up in about a month or so, will include screen shots.
  • Currently set for a Release Date of December 2000.

Some additional notes:
Viewing the new graphics will be optional.
Velious will not include the Ruins of Kunark expansion.
Velious has been in development for awhile now.
The Original games system requirements will stay the same.
And lastly Jeff Butler had this to say. "Velious is going to kick ass."
