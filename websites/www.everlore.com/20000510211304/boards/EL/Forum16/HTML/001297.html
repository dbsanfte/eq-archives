chapter 1

It had been raining for days, as i layed motionless between some rocks, just under the cliff to high hold pass. I couldn't feel my legs or arms, All i could do was stare at the pale moon light feeling the rain running down my wounds, with thoughts of my comrades, and they're horrific fate. Was it my fault that i lead them to they're deaths? I'll let you decide. My names is Sidas MacAuthor of Clan Darkfire, and this is how it began.....

The birds were singing, and the smell of ale and beer flooded the room. As i sat with a barmaid on my lap, laughing with my childhood friend Truebel. Truebel was a powerful wizard, Although you could not tell by just looking at him. His appearance was quite picular, He always walked about with a stern expression on his face, but as soon as you made eye contact with him, He'd let out a smile. An odd one at that, for the fact that he missed one of his front teeth.

We sat there telling stories of our deeds, It had been awhile since I was able too see him, and we had much to catch up. Just then
we heard a loud scream of a woman coming from outside. I grabed my langseaxs from its sheth, and rushed out the door. Truebel was quick to follow me. The woman, badly injured
was fighting off what seemed to be gnolls, a group of four were attacking her. The peasants that stood about watched in horror as one of the gnolls dug his blade deep within her thigh, as she screamed out in pain. Just then I drew my sword out and rushed the gnolls with all my might and speed. I tackling one to the ground as i drove my langseax deep into its throat. as i looked up, the 2nd gnoll seeing his fallen comrade turned his attention to me. With a great yelp he lept towards , only to meet my blade in his chest. Truebel began mumbling some words, and a huge ball of fire shot at the 3rd and 4th gnoll incinerated them in seconds. The woman layed motionless on the ground, and the smell of smoldering gnolls was still apparent. A young Cleric pushed aside the crowd, kneeling at the woman, beginning his healing powers with her. Who was this woman? And why were these strange gnolls chasing her?.......................
