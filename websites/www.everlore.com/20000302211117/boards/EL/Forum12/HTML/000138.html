I myself am a human cleric, and I made the wrong decision to spread my points between Str, Sta, and Wisdom. I have a base of 95, but I've been greatly enhanced to 126 through my guild.

I'm at lvl 24, and I have thought the same thing about starting over as a different race. The thing that keeps me from doing it is this: You can be a High Elf and dump all your points in to Wisdom and boost it to 142, but you will suffer when it comes to any type of melee (which you will have to do at one point or another) and you will have less HP's. What you have to decided is whether the extra mana (470 difference between 95 wis and 142) is worth it. If you want to be a pure caster do it. If you want to have more strength and not be encumbered all the time, stick with what you got.

You are at a good stopping point so if you want to start over, do it now and don't wait till later. I suggest a Dwarf if you are going to start over. Ultra vision, good str, good wisdom. Kinda dumb but they rock. I started a dwarf, cleric twink.

I have a fellow cleric guildmate (lvl 40) who has a base wisdom of 100. I have not seen her suffer too much, and she seems to be well liked by her friends. If you play your character well, it won't matter how much wisdom you have.

IMHO!!
