1) On xp. Verant is lying. Well, not exactly lying, but using some sort of average over time.

Repeated tests using ShowEQ have shown that pets take 1/2 of the xp that would have gone to the mage, but take it equally from all group members, if they do the most damage. If you are soloing and your pet does more damage than you, he takes 50% of the xp. If you are grouped with one other PC and pet does more damage than either of the PC's, pet takes 25% and each of the PC's gets 37.5%. Of course, if pet is doing the most damage, it is a bargain, but still it isn't free.

2) Fire pets will duel better "unbuffed" than other pets due to the huge damage shield. That is, saying the pet is unbuffed is only half correct, because it automatically gives itself an enormous buff. The onboard fire pet damage shield is bigger than the one given by the mage. At level 48 the mage shield will do about 20 per hit while the firepet shield will do 25. This eats melee players alive. I suspect any of these guys could have beaten an earth pet, despite the enormous increase in HP.

3) Pets cannot tank like warriors. ALthough they may have more HP (earth especially) they do not have as much AC or, in case of the high HP earth pets, ability to dodge, parry, etc.

They certainly do more damage than melee PC's. That is the mage's role, to kill the mobs. Someone has to do it.

But pets cannot generally pull; cannot move around dungeons safely or easily; cannot switch targets like a tank to pull mobs off enchanters and healers; cannot taunt like a warrior/pally; cannot snare; and so on. You have to be sensitive to melee players' pet envy when Jibekn is hitting twice as hard as they are, and many melee players will hate your pets' guts. Most people's egos are more important than the group's results.

I constantly comment on the good job done by melee players. Do not let them underestimate the importance of your pet, and let them know that your job is to kill the mobs, that is why you are there; also let them know that the pet is half your contribution and is like their sword, and just as hard to get (at higher levels especially). But also reassure them very clearly that they are more important to the group than your pet and that a warrior or monk is indispensible to a good group.
