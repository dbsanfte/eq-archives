I have a wood elf druid. This combination is the best combination. Druids are not as easy to play as other classes in the beginning, but make up for it in later levels. Yes, Druids can have pets. Charm animal works on animals and if you charm a wolf, he's your pet. When the spell wears off, though, he will turn on you.

I've mostly soloed this character and have enjoyed a great deal of travel. Be aware that killing bears and wolves can affect your faction within your own guild. I enjoy my Druid most of all my characters. I would recommend the Druid/Wood Elf combo.
