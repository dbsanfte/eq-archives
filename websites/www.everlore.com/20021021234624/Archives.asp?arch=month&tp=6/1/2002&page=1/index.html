We will charged the battlegrounds, and seek entrance into the most dangerous of places to assist those in need; To assist the citizens of Norrath.

We are not, nor will ever be, a military force. We are an elite team of rescuers dedicated to the Ssearch and rescue of those in need within Norrath, Luclin, and the Planes. We are a free service and donations or tips will not be asked in return, but if offered by the individual being assisted, the member will be free to accept it.

We will provide the following services:
-Corpse Recovery in dangerous places
-Corpse Locations
-Guiding through dangerous areas, and providing safe travel
-Resurrections and Revives: for those in grave areas that they can not re-enter or travel safely a great distance to their corpse
-Drag Corpses to the nearest ‘safe zone’

Once the individual has been united with his/her/its corpse, or can reach it safely, or they have arrived at a safe haven, the members of the S.A.R. team will depart.

We will not provide:
-Camping of Quests or Mobs

I thank you for your Time.

IGN Games and Trilogy Accounts
Athelyn [6/25/2002-7:09:39 PM]  Comments[1]

From the Developer's Corner, Absor writes:
It seems that some folks are concerned about an email that they are receiving from IGN offering them a free Trilogy account. There is some concern that this is a scam.

It isn’t.

IGN has indeed been authorized to give away these accounts. If they give you one, take it!

Keep in mind that in order for someone to ‘scam’ you they’ll need to acquire information from you that they can use. If someone asks you for a password on your existing EverQuest account, don’t give it to them. IGN isn’t asking for such information, they’re just giving out accounts.

I appreciate that folks are concerned about the security of their accounts. I just wanted to make sure that nobody misses out on an opportunity here.

Thanks.

Alan

It kept shouting out how its creation was finally complete, while the army of named gnolls ran toward the guards. Event on Zebuxoruk!
TradeMaster [6/29/2002-2:02:59 PM]  Comments[2]

This just in from the Zebuxoruk server:
The other day on Zeb I logged in to where I had camped at the gates of Qeynos. In front of me there was this group of people. I was near by so I commented in /say how it is rare to see so many people in Qeynos. I am told that Bramador Wise has been cursed by some necros in the Qeynos Sewers and must get to Rivervale in order to regain his eyesight! I signed on for the trip immediatly.

Now, I had no intention of leading the GM group the whole way. Three of the other people in the group, Siek the Rogue, Maleka the Monk, and Melchoir the Bard all conned green to me, Baien the Druid conned blue and Bramador himself was an LB con! So I have the position of leader thrust on me. That and I was the only one who knew the way. Everyone had either stayed around the area or always used ports to travel and did not know the way through the Karanas and Highpass.

So everyone in the group fires up /roleplay and we head out of Qeynos. Along the way, we have to protect Bramador from various mobs. He WAS blind after all. As a real fast bard myself, sometimes I wouldn't see if the group was jumped. A whole pride of lions jumped poor Maleka but we fought them off. Later along the cliffs in East Karana, the going was quite slow because a Levitate spell or song was too dangerous for poor Bramador. (As a bard who out runs horses, it was VERY slow going)

Finally we made it all the way through Highpass and quickly ducked through Kithicor over to Rivervale. Bramador passed out some gifts to all of us. I did not get specifics on what the others got but I know some agi and cha items were passed out. I was last and aparently he gave out the bard item to Melchoir. Bramador admitted he wished he had better for me but I still made out with a str earing and a wis earing.

It was a fun trip across the continent even if it was a bit slow. Item turn out was no Halloween Event but it was still much appreciated. Kudos to the GMs who run those fun little events.

Nize Dracobrewer
Brewing Bard of the 35th Sip
Federation of Honor
Zebuxoruk
