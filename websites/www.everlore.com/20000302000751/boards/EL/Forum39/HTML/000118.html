I can answer that question, me being a huge pk'ing level 20+ enchanter on Tallon Zek. First of all, before those "people" at Verant took away our charming abilities, we were the absolute most powerful class on any PvP server (and yes i do believe we could be better pk'ers than druids a.k.a killing machines). Now that our charm spell is no longer affective, we suffer a great handicape, but in my opinion are still THE best pk'ers. Us enchanters still have tashan, great dots, good evocation spells, blind, pets (although they are pretty weak for pets), clarity, quickness, other great buffs like strength spells, useful illusions, can enchant ore for jewelry (this can be used well on a pk server because jewelry adds to stats, with which is good to kill elves with), and we are the only class to get stein of moggok the real way (the quest which i've done 3 times already). The only real thing we don't get is teleports, which i REALLY REALLY wish we did get. At some levels enchanters can out-damage wizards with a DD spell, i know lots of wizzies that hate us for that. I have only named a few things that we enchanters get that nobody else does, on top of all these, there are many many more great reasons why we are so strong on this server. We are the most under-estimated class, in my opinion, and even though we are played by many people, not all that many realize the art of being an enchanter. If played right we could kick the crap out of any monk, shaman, druid, or any other good pk'ing class. I certainly hope this has helped you, and i have answered your quesetions. Good luck, and have fun.
