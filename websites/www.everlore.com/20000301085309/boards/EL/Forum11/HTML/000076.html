I have been hunting in a combination of Unrest and Crushbone's Throne room from level 14. Both are over on the elve's island. Couple requirements however, you'll need to group. Things do too much damage for you to tank anymore if you're a Barbarian. Ghouls & mummies in Unrest both require a magical weapon to hit. Other things to hunt are Dark bones, tormented dead - if you're will a really good group (19s-16s) you can take most everything from werebats down. Excellent xp and loot as well. SOW makes hunting in both Crushbone and Unrest easier on us than many others.

I hunted in Lavastorm until around 17 or 18. Excellent loot and good experience as well. A shaman can solo well here as every creature in Lavastorm has poor cold resistance, and all of our DD spells are cold based. Also, one can make a pretty platinum up there with SOW's and Endure Fire buffs on other players. After 18, I spread myself around the Beholders Maze, South Karanas, and Lake Rathe until 21. Now I find myself mostly in Unrest.

I don't know if I would recommend hunting Unrest until maybe level 19+. Not because you cannot kill anything there, but more because at less than level 20, the tormented dead, death beetles and greater skeletons (aptly named 'yard trash' by the regulars of unrest) will not ignore you as they do for higher levels. Not good when healers are trying to get spells off and a death beetle, even if they only do 10 damage, is gnawing on his leg. I went there also around level 16, and found the constant attacks of death beetles and tormented dead very tedious, as they give very little xp and loot and take about 10 minutes of garbage time after every fight. At 21 though, I find hunting Unrest loads of fun, with much loot and xp to go around for all, and only occaisional encounters with Yard Trash.

Hunt at oasis by the NRO zone wall. If you get in trouble, just zone. Madmen, orcs and tarantulas are good there. I went there at 16 and left at 20. Great exp and some of those orcs carry bronze. You can find people to group with right there. Everybody loves a shaman

While you hunt in Oasis you will have to set up residency in Freeport. While there, occasionally hunt wisps in East Commons. Don't sell the greater lightstones to those guys offering 5 plat for them. Save them, and when you have a bagful, sow yourself and head to North Karana. Take the road after the big wood bridge where the zone is and at the crossroads hang a left to the gypsy camp. See the gypsy named Mylana or something and "Give her a Greater Lightstone". She will give you a box that you will immediately sell to the gypsy to her right for 8 pp if you have the "Spirit of the Snake" buff on. Repeat procedure for each Greater Lightstone.

After you finish selling your bag or two of greater lightstones, you will have enough to buy that Glowing Wooden Crook
