Ok you have a few things you can do to regain factions with the temple of life....

The bandits in west Karana... turn their sashes in to the temple of thunder .... +faction with the temple of life.... Clerics have the same problem, we need(ed) insane faction with the PoL for a quest.

Lashun Novashine accepts 2gp donations for faction
Lashun Novashine accepts 2 or 4 bone chips for faction
Priestess Calria (sp) in the saucer accepts putrid wolf and bear hides for faction (off of the rabbid wolves and grizzlies in QH)
Bandit sashes gallore
Blessed Oil quest works too Ive heard, but these first 3 are the easiest..

Good luck

P.S. - If you turn in Kolandl fish corpses to the pally that patrols around the pool, you get very good faction as well. Have the mule kill them or go kill Frostbite...

Actually you don't have to kill the fish or dog either one if you're willing to do quite a bit of traveling. There is a quest where you get a bottle of nitrates from the guildmaster in Surefall Glade. Take this bottle and give it to the zoo keeper in Ak'anon. He will give you a "regurgitonic". you then take this and give it to Frostbite in West Karana, and he will "spit up" a kolandi fish corpse. I'm unsure about the faction you need (probably indifferent at least) with the zoo keeper and guild master, but it's a doable alternative to trashing you factions.
