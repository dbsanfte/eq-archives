Wednesday, June 19 2002

GM event on Vallon Zek
Athelyn [6/19/2002-8:22:20 PM]   Comments[4]

Xotron Nitewing writes:

Hello I'm Xotron Nitewing 41 monk from guild Trao`lech. I was in Oasis of Marr one night and I heard a shout coming from a person named Muggle Greengs. It was to my dismay that it had the last name in the shout. Then many other people shouted for someone to help them. Apperntly Muggle had lost a barshin’ stick to an NPC Hafling. Muggle called out for an army to help defeat his Halfing foe in South Ro. As we set up a group, and only one group, he told us to follow him to North Ro to find more people to help. When we go to Nro I heard shouts from another NPC. I assumed it was someone that would help us. Of course I was wrong. As we came to a hill, we saw a Halfing with a 1hb that looked like a survival staff. Behind him came many other PCs. I counted 7 total. The fight was not that long, and my group quickly defeated the lighty force of PCs. We lost 2 people in all. At the end of the event Muggle said his thanks and told us we would get a reward of sandwiches and beer. He left for a minute, and when he came back he started to hand out things. Much to our disappointment, we where only given 10 shot sow potions, gnome sandwiches, beer, and around 10pp. We were all disappointed and felt cheated ,but I guess it’s better than nothing. As for the stick, Muggle said it smelled like a Halfing and he didn't want it anymore, so he gave it to a Halfing that participated. We were all mad but he replied, "My stench, my stick." It was not that great of a weapon; it was only 11/35 2hb.

I guess that’s it about it for the event. Not bad for my first one though.

-Xotron Nitewing
