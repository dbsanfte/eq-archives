I honestly dont know of any wizard specific quest in Qeynos. If you go over to Erudin... just a boat ride away... and go into the palace, the magi guilds upstairs have a couple fun low lvl quest you might enjoy. You gotta have the faction for them though, but if you can get up to 8th and find a partner you can make short work of a named necromancer by the dock in Tox Forest. Kill him about 10 times and every magi in Erudin will love you, minus the necros who of course will hate you. But why affiliate with lesser magics?

Here is a quest that i believe doable at any level)
Band of rodcet nife:

You need to get your faction to amiable with the tem[le of Life. To do so, you can give Lashun Novashine 2 bone chips at a time and paying him 2 gp for a cure disease.
I know there are other ways to raise your faction, just do their quests
After the faction is amiable, you will need to talk to Enic Rulkin . He is usually on the pool border guarding his precious fish.
He will give you the story of one of his fish being eaten by some dog and will ask you to return the remains to him. Here is the trick, create a dummy character that will kill the fish and log back with your wizard and loot the corpse. Once you get the scales, return them to Enic and you will get the ring (+3 INT No drop)
I use a dummy character because if you kill the fish (koalindl) you will lose faction with the temple and risk of being attacked by Enic and then lose xp.
