Word of Pain is mana efficient if you're hitting 3 or more people. Don't waste your mana if there's just one or two creatures around you. Keep in mind that the closer the monsters are the more damage they'll take. WOP tops off I believe around 28 or so. Once you get Smite at 14, you probably won't use WOP that often.

Very important, because this is an AOE spell, don't cast this around friendly NPC's or guards. Let's just say this won't make them too happy.

Word of pain is a step back, i hated it It does more damage, but is more Inefficiant than word of shadow.Word of spirit and up is worth it. The damage done to the mobs is not adjusted by range; they are either in range and take full damage(or partialy resist) or they are out of range and are not affected. The word line is replaced by the earthquake line, which has a much larger radius of affect. The key to these spells in not to use them unless you have a damn good reason. I do zone patrol (train killing) in unrest and use these spells to pull all the mobs off the victim and on to me. In most casses, if the mobs are too powerful, I will have to zone too(its hard to cast with six mobs beating you). In the case of lower level trains though, this spell at spirit level and above, can sometimes wipeout two or three, and send others running. I used Spirit in CrushBone the other day, and it was a hoot. Of the 12 mobs brought to the zone a pawn and three centurians died instantly, two centurians and two legioneers ran, Dvinn, Crush, The royal guard, and a Legioneer stayed to fight. After several unsucessful attemts to cast, I finally got a second off killing eveything but Dvinn(others were helping tough). I then had to zone because of the damage I'd taken, and knew I couldnt get a good heal off with Dvinn pounding me. I use these spells when I know there are lots of small mobs Mixed with the ones I want to fight (like unrest). I DO NOT use this spell to fight multiple mobs my own level. Id rather root and back off one or two, or have my enchanter mesmerize the extras. This way, if you are knee deep and you call for help, those that come can actually take the mobs off you.
