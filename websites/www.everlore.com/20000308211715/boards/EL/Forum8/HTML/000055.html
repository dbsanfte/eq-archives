I have seen three quest to get special armor for necromancer in the temple of RO. Me and some higher lvl friends did the quest to get the robes of Enshroudment(+4 INT, +4 AGI, +4 vs fire, +4 vs cold, AC 9, necros only). It wasnt easy to do, I had lots of help. You will need one large shadow silk and three other items to get the robes. As for the other two pieces of armor boots and gloves I have not done them yet. You take the items back to the NPC in the temple of Ro, I think her name was Syllin or something, and give her all four items and she will give you the robes. This will take you some time to get so plane on spending a few days on them.
I dont know about any other armors other than these three. If anyone knows any of more armors please post them.
Oh, all the items but the silks to make the robes are no drop, so you will either have to be on hand or have someone else do the quest for you. I had lots of help doing this and Im sure you will have to be in the high 20's lower 30's with a group to get them.
I was going to try to make some thing with the shadow silks and a patter to see if I get anything, but would be an expensive test. My skill in tailoing is aobut 40 and the shadow silks are not trivial yet.
FYI There is a differnt silk for each of the three pieces of armor, shadow silks, large shadow silks, and inky shadow silks.
Good luck and have fun with your new armor, I am.

Necromancer Shadowbound Armor: Syllina will make shadowbound armor for necromancers. She makes 3 kinds: boots, gloves and a robe. In addition, she will tell you how to make shadow silk for 50gp. This is made through the tailoring skill, so you either have to learn tailoring or have to buy it from a tailor.
ShadowBound Boots: shadow silk, sceptre of tears (from castle mistmoore), eye of shadow (from shadow men), and a skeletal toe (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns);
ShadowBound Gloves: inky silk, sceptre of shadow (Lower Guk), hand of shadow (from shadow men), and skeletal finger (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns);
Robe of Enshroudment: [AC 9, Int +4, Agi +4, Sv Fire +4, Sv Cold +4] large shadow silk, werebat wing (from unrest), mask of shadow (shadowmen), and globe of shadow (Nokta Shaman in guk).
