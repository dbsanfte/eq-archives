Mezmo's Creation - GM Event on Saryrn
Tim Checketts [8/1/2001-10:55:30 AM]  Comments[10]

There is an unknown dark secret hidden underneath the sands of the Oasis of Marr and only the respectable Veil of Tears guild of Saryrn last night grasped the threshold of what is to become...

It was a normal guild event for the Veil of Tears, we had planned to meet up in NRo to learn exotic languages from distant lands. It seemed like a normal event until a wondering Gnome of the name Mezmo Lil'Thumbs stopped by our little camp and asked if we had any Tarantula Chitins, immediately curiosity struck the faces of my fellow guild mates and we offered to find him some.

We inquired why he was in need of such an item, his responded by saying that he was a scientist and needed them for his research, he feared to tell us the type of research as his bretheren would not like such information in the hands of the public, although he did accidentally slip some information that the research was for a clock or something similar.

Chitin after chitin was handed to him, after about 7 or 8 he then announced that he had enough for his research and disappeared running towards the Oasis of Marr. I tried to run after him and after a little exploration my guild reported that something had appeared back in Nro and was attacking one of our guild members. I quickly ran back to discover that a clock-work metallic spider was on the rampage, attacking two of our lower levelled members. My attempts to assist were futile as some magic force prevented me from attacking or sending my pet into battle.

After a short fight Holdan, one of our lower levelled guild members, successfully slayed the metallic beast. The contents? Cogs and metal pieces. The mysterious Gnome was no where to be seen..

We were later hinted by a reliable source that the Gnome had retreated back to his secret lab under the oasis, cooking up more sinister creations that would perhaps later be released onto the unsuspecting public.

OOC- We had a great time and I'd just like to thank everyone involved with this event.. I just wish that Saryrn would see more events like this!

Al'Kabor Comes to the Great Divide on Xegony
Kione [8/2/2001-1:46:05 PM]  Comments[11]

I'm running through Great Divide to Thurgadin to sell and camp when suddenly I see an emote to the zone "The ancient wizard contemplates the non-functional Combine Spires," or something to that effect. This piques my curiosity, as well as most of the zone, so I camoed and ran to the broken spires to check things out. When I arrive, I see a few other players also loitering about curiously. Then Al'Kabor breaks his silence, wondering if his invisibility potion has failed as there were so many people about. I cast "see invisible" on myself, just as he drops his shroud (oh well.)

He then addresses the small crowd, telling us of some experiments he's been performing lately. Clearly, he was quite excited about the possibilities of these investigations, as he spoke freely and at some length. He's been experimenting with the properties of certain metals, particularly a property he calls "Fusability." He said he has discovered three different types of ore that respond well to these experiments, each one corresponding to a particular element (I recall Water being found in Kedge Keep, Fire in Skyshrine, I can't recall where he found Earth.) He had reason to believe the fourth ore could be found in Velious, a Kaladim dwarf of his acquaintance had given him some information that led him to this. He then claimed to have obtained some and left it in the Wurm caves in that zone, and asked us to retrieve it.

Always eager to assist in the experiments of the wizard who brought us Translocation, we of course agreed. Perhaps, I thought, he would even find a way of repairing these broken spires. We then went off to the caves, following his directions (second tunnel on the left, then first right.) I grouped with a friend, a bard named Nikolette and a high level wizard of Havenlight. We arrived at the caves, fought a few Wurms that assaulted us, and encountered a "Guardian Monell," a very large Air Elemental equipped with a transparent staff.

The Guardian spoke, though not very much. It asserted that it would protect the land with its life, and that it only wished to guard the ore. It more or less answered any question with one of these two answers, clearly not a being of great intelligence. Eventually, it became entirely clear that the creature would not yield the ore without a fight, so fought it we did. It defended itself ferociously, helped by the Ancient Shardwurm that it had charmed before the battle. I played support, until eventually the Guardian bolted, and everyone persued it. I followed, and in the ensuing confusion, I'm sorry to say our wizard died. Shortly afterward, the Guardian was killed (by someone not of our group, I believe it was a Shaman named Ulfgar.) I was delayed, myself, since I went to pick up the wizard at the Dragon Circle and cast Spirit of Wolf on him, while Nikolette dragged his corpse back to the spires where Al'Kabor would return.

Return he did, though apparently the ore disappeared when the possessor tried to give it to him (my suspicion is it needed to be carried by a wizard.) I arrived in time to hear him tell us that he needed our assistance for one more task, and that he would be back shortly with some supplies we were to give to his assistant. One of the other players there said he could not stick around any longer, and Al'Kabor rewarded him with platinum. Al'Kabor chose the wizard of my group as his main assistant, and asked him to group five others he named, I was pleased to be one of the players named. Then he awarded another person who had helped but could not stay with money, and disappeared in a clap of thunder and bust of flame. He came back in short order, giving our wizard a box, and each of the rest of us a coin. Then he teleported away, saying he would summon us shortly and we should be prepared. Not long after, we all dissappeared, arriving near a crystaline formation in the Western Wastes.

Al'kabor bade each of us to give our coins to his assistant, Matil, and so we did. Each time, Matil shouted to the zone that we had acquitted ourselves well and deserved a place in the Hall of Heroes, and each of us received a reward (mine was a collar, AC 6 wisdom +10, Hit points +10.) Our wizard received "The Scepter of Al'Kabor," a powerful item (15/30, +25 int, +100 mana, +75 hp, +15 sv vs Cold, Magic, +10 sv vs Fire, effect: "Al'Kabor's Expanding Mind." ) that looked like a shovel to the rest of us, but apparently like a scepter to him. Then the Arch-Wizard thanked us, and bade our wizard to take us away, which he did.

Overall, a fun time was had by all, though a few people died in the Wurm caves and Matil (who apparently was not a GM controlled NPC) started running away prematurely and caused Nikolette to be attacked by a Wyvern. We began to fight it, but Al'Kabor blasted it out of existence in one shot. Everyone stayed in character, and was polite to the Arch-Wizard. :)

Fennin Ro Wizard Faire Tommorrow!

Received from Dynamite Shortfuze:

Hail, Wizards of Fennin Ro!

I come before you once again, as I have in the past, with news of this month's Wizard Faire! I have been collecting runes earnestly in anticipation of this month's faire, and I hope all of you have been doing the same!

Firstly, I would like to apologise to one and all for my lack of presence last month. I was there for the first half hour or so, but then my ISP went down for the rest of that night and I was unable to attend properly. I received several tells and emails, however, thanking me for organising the event, and with a couple requests pertaining to the time.

These requests shall be honored, but first I would like to share with you some exciting news.

Taedirk Fyreslinger, fellow wizard of Fennin Ro, has been wanting to organise a language faire, and we have decided that we could hold our faires together for all wizards to benefit. To this end, we would like to organise the language training area near a zone line. This must be easily accessible by most, and must be in a neutral area where none shall worry of being KOS. As the rune trade is normally held at the Toxxulia wizard spires, Taedirk suggested that we do this at the Kerra Ridge zoneline, which I feel is a good idea. If anybody has any objections, or feels that there is a better place to do this, then we will welcome all suggestions.

At the present time, the details of the faire are as follows:

Where: Toxxulia Wizard spires / Kerra Ridge
Starts: 4 PM PST ( 7 PM EST ) Saturday 4th August
Finishes: When we're done! =)

For newcomers to the faire, the concept is simple: bring all your runes and research materials to the faire. If you are in need of a rune, then ask for it, and if anyone has one they are not in need of, then you will get it. Consequentially, if you have a rune that you have no need of, and someone else asks for it, then it is expected that you will give that rune to your fellow wizard. The language trading part, I will leave to Taedirk to decide the rules at the faire, but I imagine people will be split into groups of five students and one teacher and then go about learning.

This faire is held for the community, by the community, and we are all here to help each other. Even if you haven't any research to do, I strongly encourage you to come out and meet all your fellow wizards.We can only grow stronger as a community, and we can only grow better as we gain friends.

Even though this faire is primarily geared towards wizards, I invite everyone to attend. We have had mages, enchanters, even warriors and monks visit us in the past, and we continue to welcome any who wish to join us. The community is not restricted to any one class, and friends do not come in any specific race or class.

As I have in the past, I will put forth an effort to brew sufficient refreshments to appease the masses, but I am only one person. If you practice a trade skill, then I encourage you holeheartedly to bring with you the product of your trade. Taedirk, I know, makes some of the finest lizard on a stick I've had yet, and I hope he will bring some of his own work to share with us, as I hope that you will bring some of your own.

If you have any questions, comments, concerns, or ideas pertaining to the faire, please contact me at fenninrunes@hotmail.comI will get back to you as soon as I possibly can, and I hope to see you, one and all, at the faire.

Dynamite Shortfuze
Fennin Ro

GM EVENT ON BERTOX - SPIDER ATTACK
Khrimson [8/4/2001-9:34:59 AM]  Comments[10]

Ok, it all started out at about 1:30 in the moring when the moon was high in the sky over the Northern Desert of Ro. Suddenly shouts from across the sand could be heard of strange spiders attackign and killing hunters all over the zone. I suited up my most powerful gear and set out from the tunel to the platform. No sooner had i arrived then i was attacked by 3 spiders a King, a queen, and one strange spider named spidey. Hardly a match for me i set out to kill them. King and queen were dropped easily but spidey ran from the platform to the shorline with me and my comrades in tow. Losing him in the water i returned to the platform. No sooner had i gotten back when i was attacked again by the same three spiders, omnly this time they seemed to have SOC (spirit of the cheetah). As fast as my fett could carry me with my sow, i could not keep up with them. Calling in my magical friend ginder, a wizard of his 35th season we took to rooting and killing the spiders. All were slain but king and queen dropped nothing. Spidey was slain last and on his fallen corpse i recieved a haste potion and some dung beatles. The spiders then dissaperared and the land returned to its normal state.

GM Event On Rodcet Nife in Oasis! The Battle of the Titans!
Gregory Hallisey [8/4/2001-9:36:17 AM]  Comments[8]

At approximately 1am EST in Oasis on the Rodcet Nife server, a shout was heard across the land. "The Referee" announced a FIGHT between two giants to be held behind the Gypsy Camp. Two competitors, one champion. Ahkim played the Cyclops-headed sand Giant, and Haz played the Frost Giant. The two behemoths duked it out for almost 30 minutes before Ahkim was named the Champion!

 

Pixie GM Event - Vazaelle
Shay Deloria [8/4/2001-9:43:27 AM]  Comments[4]

On August 3rd, there was a GM event on the Vazaelle server. A group of large pixies with names surrounded the Newbie lift in Kelethin. At first shouts went out about what was going on, but when I finally made it there, there were about three large pixies with names. People were walking away in terror, and blinded by them. (Because the pixies were casting Fear and Blindness on everyone.) And even I myself was subdue by the fear spell. Some were scared and attacking them, and even the guards were attacking them. But no luck.

Eventually the Pixie called, Rayeri Windlite Challenged people to a race. The race was from Newbie lift to what she called the "High City". (Felwithe.) I quickly got a SoW and the race was on. I heard shouts of how she was cheating by rooting everyone. I had serpassed her, but was quickly stopped when she caught up, and cast invulnerability on me. When I made it to Felwithe, she said how she won, and she cheered and said Yay! for me, many times. From there she asked me to dance, and when that was over she wanted to play hide and seek. After she hid, then me, and then her again, I found her a Felwithe. She rewarded me with two things...

A Glowing Black stone (INT +9, and SV MAGIC +10), and a kiss on the cheek.

About four people won things, me, Lerua won a Mithril BP (17 ac, AGI +15, HP +30), and Uolan won 2 Fairy dusts, and a Fine Steel Great staff.)
