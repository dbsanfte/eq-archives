/pet sit down
/pet get lost (doesnt work with charmed pets but will kill other pets like a mages or necros)

You can also give those charmed creatures rusties and it'll help. Apprently they aren't affected by the recent nerfs to our pets. I would say if you charm more than make a pet, guve him a dagger or something to increase his hitting ability. Only problem is that when the charm breaks they're going to hit you hard too. Just remember also when Charm breaks they are VERY aggro to you.
