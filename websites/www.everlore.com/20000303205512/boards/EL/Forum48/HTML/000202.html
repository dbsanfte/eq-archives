On the Faydwer side of Norrath, I see lots of people hunting the giant spiders in Lesser Faydark for the silks, and I do hunt them when I am in the area on other business. However, for a faster collection process, go and hunt the large spiders in Butcherblock. They are extremely easy to kill, more numerous than the giant spiders, and they drop silks in about the same frequency. So, you can collect silks much faster at lower levels, cause you have no healing/curing poison to slow you down. I can run up to the little arachnids, kick them, they ususally get one swipe at me that misses. Then I slash them once, they start running and they are dead in seconds. Not as much fun as fighting the bigger spiders, but it is the fastest way I have found to collect silks.
