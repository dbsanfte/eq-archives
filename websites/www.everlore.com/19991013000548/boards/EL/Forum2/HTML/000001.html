Shon
unregistered 		posted 08-04-1999 09:38 AM           
All armor uses the same patterns. To make patchwork armor you must put the following into your kit:

A pattern
A RUINED pelt

That is it. Please note that the type of pelt will determine the size you get (small from wolf, medium from cats, large from bears). Only RUINED pelts will work for patchwork; skins, medium or high quality pelts are not used for patchwork. At a skill of 5 you will probably fail. I currently have a skill of 26 and still fail 20% of the time. If you fail, everything in the kit is destroyed and you have to get new components to try again.

Good luck
