Well I guess it depends on what level ye be. I have a lvl 12 enchanter on Rallos and although I dont play much I've found that illusion form works well for getting into other cities and such. But if you've got a name (killed one to many gnomes and they know to watch out for ya) then no illusion will help ya. The only problem I can see with your tactic is by stunning them or rooting and running away behind them is they can just hit F9 and change views and rotate it to see where you go, and they could still be stunned. I would suggest since you eventually will need to travel for experience, learn to defeat your enemy and sneak up on him when he least expects it. For instance, I was out in Steamfont planning on taking out some drakes and such, on my way over there I ran into a dark elf (how he made it that far was beyond me) and he was attacking an elemental, I watched by the side for a bit, and realized the elemental was 1/2 health and the dark elf was a little less, I proceeded to stun the dark elf and dot him and he proceeded to die. I finished off the elemental and got the exp from it. Needless to say I recieved many tells from this character about why I did that. I asked him what kind of server we were on and if I had helped him wouldn't that have defeated the purpose of playing there? He didn't respond, and while on my journeys he did return to loot his corpse and was attacked by an elemental, I took the elemental off of him and he later sent me a tell thanking me. I told him he owed me one. heheh. I dont enjoy the PvP too much but it can be fun when you've got the right spells and tactics. I just dont think the 4 levels above or 4 levels below is a good idea. I mean I've been wiped out by a lvl 16 warrior who was totally twinked beyond belief. He kicked my ass and proceeded to kill just about everyone around. Was quite disturbing since no one could do anything about it.
But It's all about tactics, I've found that if I can't take someone on, I usually do your hit-n-run technique and hope i live to fight another day. If they are equal or lower I will usually attempt it.
