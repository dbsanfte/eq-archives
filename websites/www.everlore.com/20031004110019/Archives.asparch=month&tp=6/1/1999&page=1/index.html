Erudite/Dark Elf Alliance Against Qeynos?!? (Fennin Ro)
Webmaster [6/15/1999-1:08:11 PM]  Comments[0]

Residents of Norrath,

 I have heard reports that something dark is afoot here in Norrath. My friend told me yesterday that he saw a group of Erudites & Dark Elves talking in North Karana... Apparently, they are planning to some day take Qeynos, not by force, but by having the Erudits bring their people here slowly, and set up home. By doing this, the Human and Half-Elf city of Qeynos will soon be inhabited by large amounts of Erudites. If we are to be attacked, which I do not know if we are going to be, they will help the enemies no matter who they are, and take Qeynos from us.

We can't let those corrupt Erudites do this to us! Apparently, it was a group of religious fanatics and rich, greedy, and corrupt politicians who met with the Dark Elves.

What to do?

Well, attacking Erudin would get us no-where, as some of the Erudites don't even know about this. What I suggest is pretty much a general message saying "get out" - I am considering organizing an anti-Erudite march at the docks in Qeynos. Of course, for this to happen, I need backing from enough Humans or Human Allies to do this. Please e-mail me at anders_imboden@uswest.net if you are interested in the Anti-Erudite march.

Until then, I bid you farewell - and remember... watch your back.

(This is a Fennin Ro event)

Predakk Ironfoot
Half-Elf Warrior
Steel Warrior Guard of Qeynos

 

The Merchants of Norrath: Freeport Bazaar
Webmaster [6/15/1999-2:42:24 PM]  Comments[0]

The Merchants of Norrath welcome you to attend our Freeport Bazaar in the tunnel between North Ro and East Commons Saturday night at 7:00pm CST on the Tarew Marr Server.

Come with items to trade, items to sell, and money to buy!  Our members will be on hand to produce custom goods and to sell rare and magical treasures.

A scavenger hunt will follow the bazaar with a prize going to the team that scores the most points.  Items will be assigned a point value and teams will score points based on the quantity of items they return.  Details of the hunt including the prize will be given after the bazaar.  Low level characters are encouraged to participate as the items will not be difficult to obtain.  Although, item point values will be based on the challenge.

We hope to see you there!
Faellen - The Merchants of Norrath
Tarew Marr
