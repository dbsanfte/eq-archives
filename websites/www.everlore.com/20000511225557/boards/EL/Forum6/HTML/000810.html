Stay out of lavastorm till about halfway through 14. You get a great DD vs. summoned at 14, with which you can pretty much rule Lavastorm, but it sucks before then.
I left the RV area for ButcherBlock mountains when I hit lev 5. There are several goblin camps, a couple bandit camps and dwarf skeleton spawns that you can pull from safely with harmony. There are also guard posts all over the zone, making it one of the safest I have hunted. The guards also don't range nearly as far from their posts like the little KSing Zorro wannabies in Misty Thicket. There is a bone chip quest in Kaladim, that gives decent experience up to 9 or so, plus coin and an item, generally small patchwork (good way to get fully armored, if you have any naked spots) or a weapon. Since there are a few static spawns in ButcherBlock of dwarf skeletons, you can get good experience killing them as well as from turning in the chips. Another option is to kill aqua goblins in the ocean of tears, they are good exp up to around 11, and all drop bows that are worth about 2 gp. Get yourself bound in Kaladim (I bound in Kelethin, there is a guild there), take the boat like you are returning to Freeport, and jump off at the first rocks. The rocks to the right of the boat has 2 sirens and 2 goblins. The sirens are non-agro, and the goblins are far enough apart to pull one at a time. When you get encumbered, gate back to Kaladim and sell. When the goblins stop giving good exp, you can return to the same spot for the sirens, which will take you to 14. You will need harmony for the sirens.

Odd thing about the sirens is the 2 right next to where the boat passes don't give you a faction hit, and they con indiff. There is another rock behind the first that has more sirens, which are a little higher level, and do give a faction hit. Kill one or two in the back and the ones in front go to aprehensive.
