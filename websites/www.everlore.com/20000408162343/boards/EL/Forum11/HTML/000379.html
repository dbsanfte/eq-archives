I too have played both a troll and barb shaman, and both have their repsective advantages. But I prefer barbs for a couple of reasons.

First, the KOS factor. Yes, there are ways around it, but it annoyed me whenever I was running through a zone or city and some guy in a building or just walking (who I had neglected to fix my faction with) chased me out of the zone. It is just so much nicer not to have to think about it.

Secondly, making money. As a troll, most merchants dont like you. Trying to sell loot while hunting in CB or Everfrost can be a bother (gate home to Grobb? GAH!) Also, banking. Even if the guards like you, the bankers may not.

Just keep these points in mind. If you can live with them, then a troll is great fun. Natural regen is great, as is troll lingo.
