Hail all, Wanderers of Norrath !

Let me tell you what happened yesterday, so it may not be forgotten soon...

Yesterday, as i awoke in the ever-busy and buzzing Greater Faydark, i received word that something was happening to me beautiful home of Everfrost. I immediately gated back home....only to find it too warm in the whole of Everfrost ! Something - or someone - seemed to have influenced the weather ! As i shouted what was going on, i received Battle Crys and shouts about the Living Dead and Werewolves haunting our lands ! I prayed to The Tribunal to send me a Frenzied Spirit to aid me, and after some preparation, me trusty Companion and me went on to rid our Land of the Invaders. Many Werewolves i encountered, and uncommon strong Skeletons were wandering around as well - they showed no fear and even dared to attack me...they all didnt last long. 

After a while, and talking to numerous Barbarian Sisters and Brothers and other friends o´ mine, a Barbarian named Shamus seemed to know where these beasts came from. I received a Diary from Shamus, the one who belonged to the Leader of the Beasts, a evil Sorceress. The diary reveiled that she was trying to become like the Gods, through three special Runes and a foul Ceremonie were said to make her invincible. Yet there was hope, she also wrote down about what seemed to be her Arch-Enemy who was named MORDAG. I found this man shortly after - he was a Barbarian like me...and he told me more about the danger that was threatening Everfrost and the whole of Norrath. He told me to bring him the 3 Runes so that the Evil Sorceress Plans could be stopped. He didnt know where to find them, but through the diary i knew there were 3 Camps of the Undead. The most powerful servants of Evil, the Mad Spectres, guarded the Rune of Ultimate Evil (though it was mentioned in the diary as Rune of Ultimate Chaos), the Rune of Endless Life and the Rune of Endless Mana. They also were in the possession of other unique items - i found meself a PEGASUS FEATHERED HEADDRESS, a magical and unique item that not only granted me WIS+4, but also increased my MANA+4 and my SV DISEASE+4, without ANY weight.....


The Servants of the Evil Sorceress were no match - i received all 3 Runes and went back to Mordag. As i gave them to him, he payed me some Gold and Platinum pieces as reward. Just as we were about to go to Halas to destroy the Runes so that they NEVER might fall into the wrong hands - the Evil Sorceress materialized, cursing and attacking me instantly ! I did what i had to do...and moments later, her accursed form was lying before me, bleeding to death. With her dying breath, she muttered some more curses and that she will be back one day....but i didnt pay attention, for the Snow Leopards were closing in on her - and they seemed hungry....

/ooc Many thanks to all GM who thought of those wonderful Events and such throughout Norrath ! Keep up the good work ! :)

Sullon under change!
Xott the Wizard [11/2/2001-5:42:43 PM]  Comments[1]

Hello all, I'd save this for Monday for Absor to post, but since I'm here on Saturday with the team working to get Luclin ready to ship, I thought I'd go ahead and announce it.

We've been concerned about the number of people playing on Sullon Zek lately. When the server first opened, and for many weeks afterwards, the server was flooded with tons of people who appeared to like the idea of a locked down team ruleset with penalties for losing in PvP combat. However the numbers have been steadily dropping.

Last week we queried the playerbase and the overall feeling was that while they like the ruleset in general, it's just too tough to advance. With experience loss on all deaths, and death being far more likely, making forward progress just becomes too difficult overall.

Soon, most likely on November 6th barring any problems popping up, we'll be making some changes to Sullon Zek: 1) Experience loss (on all deaths) will be cut in half. 2) There will be an across the board 20% experience bonus for NPC kills.

We're hoping these changes will revitalize Sullon Zek and allow us to keep the server around for some time to come.

Gordon Wrinn
Associate Producer, EverQuest
Sony Online Entertainment

No more monk Droppings!!!
Xott the Wizard [11/4/2001-7:20:18 AM]  Comments[39]

Hello all,
There are a number of scams out there where nefarious inviduals attempt to get people to perform a series of operations that results in the victim unknowingly dropping their coin on the ground. In order to help our players avoid these scams, we're taking action in the patch on Tuesday. We first considered popping up a confirmation box when attempting to drop coin on the ground, however upon reflection we could not determine a reason why anyone would want to do this, except perhaps when attempting to "self-trade" items and coin between characters. However dropping things on the ground is as you can imagine an extremely unsafe and unsupported way to trade items, and has created more headaches for players and our CS staff than it has done them good.

To that end, beginning on Tuesday the dropping of coinage will no longer be allowed by the client. Players will of course still be able to destroy it if they wish, for instance, to releave themselves of unwanted copper.

We look forward to hearing your comments. If there are any reasons that dropping coinage should be allowed that we haven't thought of, please reply and let us know.

Thanks,

Gordon Wrinn

November 2nd, 6:00pm
-------------------------

Tonight we have patched something that I think all players have been waiting for. The ability to play that gnoll, orc, or the cute and fuzzy rat. On your character select screen you will notice another button in the upper right part of your screen called "Monster". Clicking this button will randomly place you in a low-level NPC. You will be given full control of the creature to do with it what you like. You may hunt and kill other creatures. You may even attack other players.

Some things to remember when playing your own personal monster (NPC).
-You will not be able to interact with players or NPC's other then moving and hitting attack.
-You cannot speak, trade, sell, or do anything else that a player can do.
-You will spawn with normal loot for the monster you are playing and Player Characters will be able to kill you for loot and experience.
-Once you die, you will return to the character select screen where you may choose to return as another NPC or play your normal character.
-You may gain experience, levels, and skills as this NPC, but once you die, it is all lost. You will need to start over the next time you choose to be a NPC character.


This ability to play a monster is something being considered for PvP servers only as we have no plans to implement non-consensual PvP on a non PvP server.

Also, please report any bugs regarding the Player NPC abilities, quests, merchants, anything else via /bug and /petition.

Thanks,

-The EverQuest Team
