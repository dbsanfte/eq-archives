Frankly, I just don't get a few things about PvP. Now don't get me wrong. I think it's a blast playing on PvP servers. I brought a necromancer to 49th level on a carebear server before I finally discovered the pleasure of PvP. So, I started playing on Vallon. Anyway, on with it...

1). DoT's (coincidentally, a necro's most powerful type of spell) - Why can't we loot if our DoT is responsible for killing a player? If it's because a DoT would cause xp loss if it were to allow us to loot from DoT-death, then that is a weak excuse. Personally I think being PK'd should cause xp loss. This would separate the real players from the "Run-Into-A-Crowd-of-Low-Levels-and-Kill-One-Loot-It-Then-Die-Return-And-Do-It-Again-People". There needs to be a penalty for dying ALWAYS.

2).Pets (coincidentally, our second most powerful asset as necros) - Why can't we loot if our pet makes the kill? Our pet comes from our mana just as our DD's do. This is probably a coding issue that is way to hard to get around. So Oh Well.

3). Ultimately, a necro without a pet who doesn't use DoT's is simply 10 levels lower for purposes of battle. This needs to be dealt with. All too often I've brought down someone in full bronze or someone in an FBR and my pet or a DoT makes the kill. Very frustrating - not to mention, it caters to classes such as wizards in a BIG way.

Just some thoughts =)
