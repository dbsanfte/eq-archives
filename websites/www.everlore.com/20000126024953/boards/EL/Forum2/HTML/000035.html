Three good reasons to fish;

1. If your waiting for the ship to come its a godsend. lets face it, sitting on the dock o the bay watching the tide roool away is boring as hell. Fishing gets you skill and chow. and you WILL one day eventually sit on the dock waiting for the damn ship.

2. Its a cheap food source, and once your good it can be done very fast.

3. Cash. Fish can be sold. Once your skill level is up to around 80 you will catch in excess of 200 fish in an hour. If you can bake, making baked fish, they sell for up near 4 silver. Do the math here folks;

200 fish = (aprox) 300 baked fish fillets

300 x 4 silver = 1200 silver, 12 pp in an hour.

if your fishing and baking are both over 100 you can pull down even better numbers, and if your a Ranger and can forage for grubs, well, it gets better and better.

It IS boring, true. But there is money to be made, thats why you see folks fishing after all.

I fish for baking purposes, and to pass time waiting for the boat. The above figures for fish fillets are wrong. You get two fish fillets per 1 fresh fish and 1 jug o'sauces. This doubles your output. Take away 5-10% of the fresh fish due to baking failures and you end up with approx 380 fish fillets which you can sell for 5sp 3cp per (1pp 6sp for a stack)---this varies depending on what merchant you sell to and your standing there. 19pp altogether (minus your pole/bait/jug o'sauces cost)... 18pp profit.
