Well, I got taken and I want to warn anyone out there that buys stuff through the auction channel. Turns out Giagas, as he is known on Quellious, does this a lot. I will say that there were a TON of players who rallied behind me afterwards and offered support, money, dueling privileges , etc., so I thank all of those wonderful people on Quellious.
