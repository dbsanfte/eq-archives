I also put a bunch of points into it when I reached level 30 (about 22 practices). It climbed slowly but noticeably. Because of the long delay, you won't be attempting it that often - so it climbs slowly. Consequently, You want to make sure you do not use Dragon Punch or any of the other special attacks. Also, make sure you don't fight anything that cons green - your skills will not climb this way.

I'm about half way through level 35 now, and it is almost maxed (my skill is somewhere in the 170s).
