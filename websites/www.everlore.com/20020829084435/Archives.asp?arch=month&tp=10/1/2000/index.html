Daily Vision submission guidelines
Catalyst [10/1/2000-6:26:27 PM]  Comments[2]

Many people have contacted me because they are unsure as to how they should submit an image for the Daily Vision. If you have an interesting screen shot you would like considered for the Daily Vision visit our Add Screenshot page. Our submission wizard will automatically convert your images into a .jpg format, and has all the proper fields and information needed for an image to be posted. This is the best possible way to submit a screen shot, as e-mailing them to Everlore staff is not recommended, because people tend to leave out necessary information and do not convert their images to the proper format.

Anyway, the most interesting/humorous/etc shot for that day will be posted. Good English and well written commentary make a difference as well. Feel free to submit anything you think other people will enjoy as long as it deals with EverQuest, and is entertaining.

Test of Tactics - Best of the Best

Verant Interactive Presents
The Test of Tactics – Best of the Best Event

The Test of Tactics was the first official event held by Verant Interactive, being held over a year ago on Xegony. The event is designed as a 6 vs 6 event, with minimal amounts of rules involved. Due to the massive time cost on the event, it was decided upon to move to smaller events that would take less time to run and place a smaller burden on the server staff.

With the incredible demand that the players have shown for the events, we are going to bring back the Test of Tactics, and do a Best of the Best for all servers to find the greatest 6-man team. We have modified the rules slightly so that we don’t have 200 teams per server enter, and it will be able to be run quickly and efficiently, while still trying to keep the original spirit of the event alive.

The 6-man team has now become a 7-man team. The “Captain” of the team must be a level 58 or higher. Two “Elite” team members need to be 55 or higher. The final 4 of the team make up 3 “Support” team members of 49th level plus, and a “Substitute/Coach” position that can be of any level. This does allow for a 7-man team of all 60th level players.

Only 6 will fight at one time, and the 7th can be substituted before the match. Once the team has been chosen, those 6 must fight. The 7th during the match is restricted to observation or coaching.

Rules are fairly simple. No starting buffs, no starting pets, no use of fear, no use of charm, and no use of knock back spells. Once the match starts, you may buff, summon pets, use activatable items, cast spells, engage with melee, hide and sneak, disarm opponents, use uber-quest weapons, do whatever is needed to assure the victory of your team.

The “Ring” for this match is the raised platform of the arena. The only characters allowed in the center arena is the 12 players involved, and the Lead Judge of the event. All players involved must remain in the tiles at all times. Leaving the tiles for any reason will result in a warning, then disqualification of the player involved.

Security for this event will be extreme. Any non-combatant who even stands up on the raised platform will be removed from the arena and banished from the event. No spell casting, no bard song, no use of shout will be tolerated at anytime for those watching the event. Event Staff can and will remove any player that causes even the slightest disruption of this massive event.

Before the claims of uber-guild, uber-item, uber-whatever start, be advised that of the 3 Test of Tactics that have been held, the team that has practiced the most have won these events, even over higher levels, better equipped, and longer playing characters. How well you understand the basics of 6 vs 6 player vs player combat, and getting a well balanced team will determine how well your group does in the event.

The ToT-BotB will start October 16th and run through October 28th on Live Servers. The final event will be a 2-day event on Test Server, October 28th and 29th.

Prizes for this incredible event are still undecided, as well as the question of an entry fee. This will be discussed and made final in the next week.

Good luck to everyone participating, I shall see you on the battlefields!

Lady Daegarmo
Online Events Coordinator
Verant Interactive
