These levels can be tough. I did the following - hunted undead in S. Rho till bored (Dry bones, ghouls, and crypt mummies). Also in OOT there is an aviak island never camped, seldom train, no aggro so you can meditate and no faction hits. I hunted here till lvl 20 without losing no more than one pet. You can bind yourself on the island coming from Butcherblock to Freeport in case you die, that way no waiting for the boat and zoning time.

By the way, I thought gargoyles hurt your faction if you are a DE? Could be wrong it has been awhile since I was in OOT. I went to Lake Rathe after lvl 20 and hunted Aviaks like everyone else on Norrath at that level.

Go to the Rathe Mtns and solo basilisks. If you want to rack up Xps fast, take a Warrior or SK along. The tank and your pet can beat on the basilisks while you cast your 3 DOTs and a fear, then sit down and med for the next one. As you may know, the faster you kill off mobs the higher your xps are. I took a warrior with me and could get a bub xps for each hour played. Now I don't know if that is good or bad, but I was satisfied. Just watch out for giants and giant skeletons that stroll through the basilisk hunting area (the first left in from the Feerrott zone). If you solo the basilisks, it just takes longer and be prepared to mend bones on your pet alot, as he will be frequently turned to stone by the basilisks and get munched on for 10-15 second periods.
