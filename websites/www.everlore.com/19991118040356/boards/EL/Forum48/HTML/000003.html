In reply to Hippun :

At skill 26 Patchwork becomes trivial, and after that you have to start making studded. You're probably mistaking it for Reinforced Leather, where a reasonable starting skill is about 90. Studded masks (now that the pattern is in) can be made from about 20.

Second, if the message the person got is "The items did not combine in those quantities to make anything", it's not the same as "You lack the skill to craft the item". So it is clearly not because the person has too low skill, it's the proportions that are wrong. And since multiple people have verified the proportions were correct, either in the Tailoring book or on web sites, then it is obvious that this item cannot be made because of a bug.
