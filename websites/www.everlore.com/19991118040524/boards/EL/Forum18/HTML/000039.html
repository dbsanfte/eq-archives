As for myself. I love my monk. He's extremely well equiped for lvl 23 without twinking. The only flaw that I can see is that we have nothing to strive for. All these other fighters go out and get crafted and rubicite armor. Pretty much the only things for us to look for is the PoF armor and a few misc items like black pearl jewelry. Ok, here is a long story for you. But if your interested,read on.
I for one almost quit EQ just because I got so damn bored with my monk. I decided to just start walking around, without sow. I went from lake rathe on through Highold (well, I had to make a quick stop to pick up my corpse in the gorge). I made my way to runnyeye. After experiencing the self made trains there I decided to make my way to completely wipe out lvl 3 befallen. I soon found a SK who wanted the gos robe from the thuraugists. We made our way to lvl 3 killing everything in sight until we found her. She died shortly after. We made the age old mistake of chasing her around in the secret wall trying to finish off the last sliver of life. Of course, we soon got jumped and my partner died. I feigned and let the mobs walk off. I looted the thuarug, and got the gos robe that he had wanted. Low and behold...she also had the dagger of marenek that I had needed for the purple headband quest. I carried my partners body back up to lvl 1, and gave him his robe. I realized what I had been missing in my attempt to power lvl. I missed the whole danger scene. I missed the big group battles that I used to be so proud of when I was a newbie. I missed winning a fight against all odds. I realized that there is more to this game than just lvling. So right now I'm back to where I was at lvl 13. Just traveling around the world, picking up any items that have been on my wish list, helping newbies and anyone else that needs it. I made it to Unrest. I pulled two enemies from the house. I concentrated on the ghoul because it was the weaker of the two. By the time he finally bit the dust, I was down to half life and I already used mend. All of the sudden I started getting heals from 2 15 lvls that just happened to be in the right spot at the right time. they soon joined in and right before the mob died I feigned death. They killed the mob and got the exp since I was "dead". They were so gratefull for the exp they got. It just felt sooo good to help them out like that. So now I'm on my way to crushbone and then to mistmoore. It'll probably take me a month or so to reach lvl 30, but it'll be worth it.
