One word for you: Sentinel. I just hit lv 8 and this spell is a blast. Yes, it may actually have some real use (medding, spawn points), I mean, c'mon, is there any other spell with such a cool looking effect? Firstly, while the sentinel is active, you sparkle purple. Secondly, when you're in the area of detection, it looks like you cast the spell every 10 seconds or so- you wave your arms around and everyone (including yourself) lights up like a christmas tree with a big pink sparkle!
