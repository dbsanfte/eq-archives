It is better if at all possible to pick a weapon type and stick with it. Form an idea of what weapon you want to use as your characters symbol and aquire it as soon as possible. Or study up on the weapon type that it is and aquire a similar weapon type and use it. Because you are using one weapon type it will go up really fast because you are always using it to fight and gan xp. Another method is to either put all of your valuables in the bank and use low cost versions of your weapon type and fight red cons (this way you don't have to worry about going broke if you can't retrieve your corpse) or having a high level healer watch over you while you fight red cons. Red cons makes your skills SKYROCKET!!!, and I do mean rocket, up in levels.


Hope this is helpful. Peace and good luck.
