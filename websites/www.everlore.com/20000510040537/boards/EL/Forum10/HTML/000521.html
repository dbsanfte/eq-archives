Well, if you are interested in setting up a semi permanent group, you would want an enchanter because of a)crowd control b)crack, and c)jewlery. Having an enchanter around will make it much easier on the rest of you, as you wont have to worry about being in a fight with more than one mob at a time. Crack (clarity) dosnt come in until lvl 29, but when it does, everyone who uses mana in your group will love the enchanter. (A note on this.. a spell, which i will call lesser crack, is rumored to be on the test server at this moment. This is apparently a lvl 16 spell, which would make it much more attainiable for your friend). And finally, jewlery. Jewling, while it can be done by any class, is primarily the domain of the enchanter, as they are the only class that can enchant the metals required to do this. If you work your enchanter skill up, you will eventually be able to make good jewlery. Now, since you are friends, I am sure that the enchanter would provide the rest of your group with this jewlery for MUCH less than he would charge others, so that realy benifits you. If you are planing on taking this group from lvl 1 to 50 (or even a lot less), i HIGHLY reccomend including an enchanter in it.

As for other classes i would recomend for your group in addition to the enchanter, cleric, shadowknight, and rogue, i would have to recomend a druid(some healing, buffs, teleports) and a mage to provide you with summoned food, water, bandages, etc. This group would be 2pure casters (enc, mage) 2 healers/buffers (cleric, druid) and 2 tanks (SK, Rogue), which i think tends to work well. A bard would also work, but being a "jack of all trades" i am not so sure he would be needed in a group as well balanced as the one above. Plenty of firepower, buffs, heals, and HP's, as well as teleports when you need them.

The level 16 spell "lesser crack" is not on the test server, but is rumored to be added with the Kunark upgrade. There is actually no solid evidence of the existence of this spell, other than its' unexplained appearance on the Caster's Realm Enchanter spell list.

As far as starting a killer group, I'd have to say you need an Enchanter, Warrior, and a Druid. Everything else is icing on the cake, though my next addition would have to be a Bard. The Warrior provides the raw tanking power, the Druid provides damage shields, buffs, heals, snare, HARMONY, and group tp, while the Enchanter buffs, debuffs, but mostly mesmerizes anything that the Warrior isn't currently engaged with. The Bard would just make everyone more efficient and add a little extra damage and heal the Warrior in between fights instead of making the Druid do it. No other class can add any skills that are as necessary as what this group can already do, in my humble opinion =).
