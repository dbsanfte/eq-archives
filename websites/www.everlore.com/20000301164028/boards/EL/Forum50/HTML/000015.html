Remembering beta and all, thinking of clockwork pets, I came up with a nice solution to have pets, that won't interfere with mage and necro pets. A clockwork beast, animal type you can choose useing different parts and pieces, which you can craft for yourself only. So us gnomes can walk tall and say, "look, i'm a master tinker artist." Make yourself a pet that doesn't actually attack or defend you, but one that will carry ten items, of any size or perhaps items up to a certain size (i.e. no halberds) kinda like a ten slot player made back pack that follows you around from zone to zone. If you die, he will wait by your corpse for you to return. I know myself, having a str of 60 on my mage, could sure use the extra muscle to carry around all those fine steel weapons I get off the nasty undead and such. Not sure for a good recipe, but should cost you heavily, VERY heavily considering what you're getting. Just a suggestion, but would be nice to see a little gnome pride about with this craft. 8)
