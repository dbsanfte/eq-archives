Dexterity is nothing more than your chances of doing something a certain amount of times.

The more dexterous you are, the better you are at something, and the more you'll do it.

More dexterity means more double attacks, more dual wield hits, more dmg caused by your weapon, less misses, more chances of your item/weapon going off, etc etc. In battle AGI and DEX work inversely: DEX lets you hit your target more, and harder, while AGI lets your target miss you more, and cause less dmg.

When you work on your game, basketball for example, you'll get better and better, you'll make more and more shots than before you practiced so much, because you're getting more dexterous with your hands and with your coordination. Same thing in eq.

Hope that helps.
