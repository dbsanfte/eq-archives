Hello fellow smiths! It is my hope that this message will unite all players from every server so Verant will hear our call. As we all know smithing is probably the second best income trade skill, following jewelry. What I propose is to give us more options in a few different areas. Now this is for monetary reasons and a more realistic gaming world. One of my "complaints" is that jewelers can make items that cost hundreds of plat to make, therefor there mark up value is very high. On the Xenogy server lots of plat pieces cost upwords of 700 to 900p and there are some pieces that are trade only. Now if you compair that to what a full suit of banded armor sells for on most servers (Xenogy being about 80 to 120p) there is a huge difference. What I propose is for smiths to be able to make bronze armor (combining tin and copper)then as our skills increase making steel armour (chainmail, steel, plate etc). If you think about this most medieval blacksmiths were able to do this in all societies. Also why cant we get an enchanter to enchant metal for us? Think of the possiblities here! Im not asking for this magic armour to be GREAT and replace the camping or quest armor but it would be a nice addon too. As of now I know tailors can make magical guantlets for monks, so why not smiths? My applause go out to Verant for the introduction of forged weapons with stats very similar to Fine Steel, BUT we are only able to make THREE weapons. These are a 1 or 2 hand sword and 1 hand blunt! What happen to piecing weapons, 2 hand blunt, etc? I am sure some of the other mid 20's to low 30's melee class is getting tired of that heavy bronze armour! I will post this message on other boards as well. Feel free to post this on other boards if you wish , but please give me credit for it. As for other players who are not smiths, I am sure this will benifit you all as well. I hope that we all can unite on this. Any commits that would would like to send directly to me, send to _aragornn_@email.msn.com In the end I would like to thank and complain to Verant. Thank you for a truly magical world, but why did you have to make it so addicting!

If you could get an enchanter to enchant say a sheet of metal or ore (we'll start with armor), then throw in a mold for the piece wanted, throw in a jewel, and voila! A magic piece of equipment with similar addition to attributes! (say for paladins, an "Enchanted Jade Breastplate"; +20hp, +20 mana, 18 ac) Of course, this would be a very expensive skill, but you could get equipment just better than bronze but worse than class specific armor such as crafted for just a little bit more than bronze. This would be a lot of work for Verant, so I doubt it would ever be accomplished, but it would be a very welcomed addition to the skill. Once again, this would require a massive ammount of work, and since Verant has other issues such as lag, I don't expect this to happen, but if you guys want, you can bombard Verant with petitions and mail on the subject.

I like Bazill's idea of using blacksmithing to pay your way through another skill such as tailoring. I'm a master smith on the Prexus server, and there are very few true newbies on Prexus anymore. Any worthwhile sale of armor is a full suit to a twinker for 91pp.

Because of the recent decline in demand for banded armor and no other items we smiths can make other than worthless weapons, I've been wondering what other trade skill I could get into. I really like smithing and I took more than a week off from adventuring in order to get to master level. I think tradeskills are a fantastic idea that make the game even more realistic and entertaining.

Unfortunately, the profit from selling armor at 1pp per AC really isn't worth the time it requires to auction it off given the current lack of demand for it. Not everyone has endless hours at their disposal to use playing EQ (although I certainly wish I could count myself among those who do). Because of limited time, limited demand, and relatively low profit given the time, money, and effort that went into becoming a competent master - smithing really isn't worth the time anymore.

Bazill, your idea of using smithing to pay for another skill is a good one. The market is so saturated with bronze now that I think more and more people will stop bothering to make banded as the price of bronze gets lower and lower.

I think I'll start making jewelry since I have an enchanter friend who won't rip me down the back when I need to buy enchanted bars. Also, I really like the idea of making backpacks like Bazill has done even though I see them going for 60-70 in greater Faydark on Prexus whenever they are auctioned.

Hopefully, there isn't an oversupply of jewelry already. Maybe tailoring is a good idea since I have heard horror stories about how insanely irritating and time consuming it is. I see people auctioning, sometimes begging, for reinforced leather armor. There doesn't seem to be too many master tailors around.

Sorry for rambling kind of off the topic. I agree with everybody that we REALLY need blacksmithing to be improved in order for it to remain a useful and profitable skill.

Too bad there is nothing we can do besides wait and see what happens with the expansion. Complaining certainly won't work since necromancers get a new nerf every week (I'm not a necro) and they constantly whine, yell, and scream all to no avail.

Good luck smithing, and here's to hoping we can make plate someday!
