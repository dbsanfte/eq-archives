unregistered 		posted 01-20-2000 11:19 AM           
Good idea, however it does cause some problems. I asked about this a long time ago, thinking that since we had a minor illusion, where was our major illusion *stern look*??

Back in Beta people could become whatever artifact they were closest to, nomatter what that was. So you'd have people turning into, for example, the Felwithe gates and running around. Okay, so the spell is now localized, you are rooted while the illusion is on. Well you could still become gates and turn... just so... and suddenly you can't see the enterance to Felwithe anymore. Sure you can get in, but if you're level 2 and are running from bats? Yah, it causes problems.

Put that into the perspective of being able to transform into a giant. Ta da, you're 30 meters tall and your foot is enough to cover a whole group of people fighting a DW Croc in Oasis. Uh oh... they can't see now. What's more, they can't move to solve the problem. You can follow them around.

Okay, you aren't a giant now... you're still in Oasis and you become a spectre and start motoring up the beach. People stop fighting and run for the zone, some die because the DWs they were fighting are faster than they are.

The core of this problem is that there would be no way for people to tell what was real and what was not, it would affect their decisions and their ability to function on a zone-wide scale. It's not something many of us would do, but there are enough people out there who exploit everything they can. Imagine the cinfusion if people saw a guk lord storming the zone line in LG?

Now if this were limited to critters below a certain level, I'd be all for it. Be kind of fun to be a drake I think. Even a bat
