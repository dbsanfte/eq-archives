Smithing was a mystery to me at first, I've now recently started but found it quite expensive to work on.

Start off by finding a book, Blacksmithing basics. If you can't find it, I can give you the basic in here.

First get some small ore, looks like a block of clay for some reason!

Get 2 pieces and put them in a forge (These 2 items must go in seperate spaces, DON'T stack them!)

Add a flask of water to cool the ore, and click combine!

The first time I did this, I got a metal thing, was well chuffed! This was then followed by 7 failures before my next success!

It's expensive stuff smithing. You will need to work on metal bits until you get to level 20 something of smithing. I store all my metal bits in the bank, or as many as possible anyway. This way you can get them when you start making files and stuff!

This is a start! EQVault (Sorry!) has some good info on these.

Enjoy!
