This post is probably a little late, but killing Will o wisp is indeed an awesome way to make money in the lower lvls.

If you are one of the good races, kill wisp on the wisp island in Eruds crossings then give the greater lightstones, one at a time, to the gypsy lady in the gypsy camp n karana who you hail and she says she is something about the concordance of research. Every time you give her a greater lightstone, she gives you a concordance of research. Sell that to one of the gypsy merchant then repeat this process again till you have sold off all your greater lightstones.(you can only have one concordance at a time I believe)

If you are an evil race.. everything is the same except you hunt wisp in East commons or West commons and take the greater stones to the gypsy dark elf in south ro whom you hail and she says something about the concordance.

The one in the karanas doesnt give any apparent faction but the one in south ro gives plus faction with dark elf guards (inner and outer dread guards) and the dark bargainners.. nice way to raise that faction if you have betrayed or are not liked by these guards very much. Both Gypsies give a little experience for every stone you turn in.

At first I only got about 8pp for these stones but as I went up in lvl I actually got more money.. last time I did this I was getting 9.5pp for each greater lightstone! wisp are around lvl 9 to 12 on average. real easy cash till about 22... then forget this and head for mistmoore or some other dungeon. I usually leave with no less then 40pp a hunt when going to mistmoore, and thats casually looting and hunting for about 2 hours.

An easy quest is the lost child / Cubert quest in Qeynos. It is doable at level 6 I believe. I full description can be found here on everlore, but I'll give you a quick run down.

In South Qeynos, talk to a guy named Zieml (something like that) that stands beside the bridge going into the bank/magic guild area. He says something about a lost child and needing proff of what happened to her. Now go just past the bank and dive into the water. The is an entrance to the catacombs there.

When you get into the catacombs, hop up on the first wlak way. You will see the walk way goes a few feet and turns a corner. Watch the things that walk around here and you will notice a rat (probably) that walks back and forth around this corner in a regular pattern. Kill it. The things that spawn in it's place wil be either a rat (Level 2), a gelatinious cube (L 4-5) and Cubert (L 5-6) a named gel cube. Keep killing the spawns till you get Cubert. He sometimes drops a bloody child's tunic. Take the tunic back up to Zimel and get exp, some coin, and an item. If your faction is good, you will get a +3 int ear ring. The booby prize is usually malicite.

If you need to raise faction, hunting in Black Burrow will bump it up quickly. I went around casting radiation on anyone engaged with a gnoll and busting trains on the surface. Faction goes up quickly.

Anyway, I ran the quest a few times and got 2 ear rings and 1 malacite. Cubert seems to drop the tunic about 1 in 3 kills. Easy quest for a nice item. Maybe it's just the sever I was on (E. Marr) but there is rarely anyone hunting Cubert. Strange because it is so easy to do for magic jewlery.

Oh yeah, the spawns are almost one after the other. I have been looting one kill when the next spawn came out before I was done.
