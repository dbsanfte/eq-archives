GM Event on Vazaelle
TradeMaster [6/29/2002-9:25:32 PM]  Comments[4]

East commons becomes lively on the Vazelle server:

Saturday, June 29 at about 11:30 pm, the usually peaceful glade-like East Commons was overtaken by hoardes of bixies. Swarms of 20 to 30 little murderous bixies rampaged through the zone, getting cut down like bad weeds. Poor things didn't stand a chance! Unbeknownst... well okay knownst to most everyone there, after the first wave of bixies died horrible, bloody deaths, an equal amount of very ticked off griffens magicaly appeared. My pet got two down, then fell to the other three. As soon as we got the griffens under control, more bixies poured from the sky. Oddly enough the pet I summoned also happened to be a bixie. While I was looting the poor mangled body of my last dead bixie, I found a little shrunken goblin skull earring, with clickable effect Grim Aura. I also heard a Hero cloak dropped as well, but can't verify. It was quite fun to stumble into rampaging bixies and all in all it was a good time. :-)

Millennia Silvara - 51 Elementalist
