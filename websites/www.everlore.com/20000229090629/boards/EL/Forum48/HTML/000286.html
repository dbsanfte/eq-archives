Sligh adjustment here:

You can make regular and large Shadow Silks. You can also make Inky Shadow Silks.

Regular Shadow silk = 1 swatch + 1 Gather Shadows scroll + 1 Shadow Wolf Pelt

Large Shadow silk = 1 swatch + 1 Gather Shadows scroll + 2 Shadow Wolf Pelts

Inky Shadow Silk = 1 swatch + 2 Gather Shadows scrolls + 1 Shadow Wolf Pelt

The scrolls are available from the Necromancers Guild in Freeport and Neriak (probably other places as well but I can only confirm these 2).

I sell Regular Shadow Silks for 10pp, Large for 15pp, and Inky for 20pp. No one has complained about the price yet.

Shadow Wolf Pelts can only be found on...you guessed it!! Shadow Wolves. The pelt apears very dark in color and is called "Shadow Wolf Pelt" there is no quality assigned to it. It is the rarer drop of Shadow Wolves, Ruined Wolf Pelts being the main drop. Shadow Wolves can be found in East Commons, Misty Thicket, and Nektulos Forest (they may be in more zones, I'm not sure). The Pelts may also be found on merchants in these areas, so check around before auctioning for one or wasting time hunting all those wolves.
