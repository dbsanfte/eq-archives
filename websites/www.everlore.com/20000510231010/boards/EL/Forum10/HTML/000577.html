The only problem I see with much of the strategy posted here is how much actualy damage you will inflict on the warrior. Your pet will certainly be able to whack him half decently, but keeping up your debuffs and EoC is going to be a mana drain. You may not be able to DD him very much. Remember, enchanters CAN solo but are most effective when someone else is dealing damage, leaving the enchanter free for crowd control. Although I'm sure there have been many enchanters who have defeated melee types, if the warrior is smart AND if he is twinked with armour and some MR stuff, it will not be easy. He he really knows how to play his class, then you cold easily lose once you are oom. All the same, have fun with it. I don't duel. Ever. I just Pk...
