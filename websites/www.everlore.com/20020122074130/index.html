The GUILDWARS of the Gods
Banegrivm [1/21/2002-5:49:44 AM]  Comments[2]

The GUILDWARS of the Gods, is based loosely off of the recent writings of "The Codex of War" which contains various precepts of Norrathian History, located at www.1stfistoflight.com off of the Forums link, under "The War of the Gods".

The GUILDWARS of the Gods will be taking place only on the Firiona Vie Role Play Server. It is a running storyline that started between two guilds and their allies; the 1st Fist of Light and the Fist of Hate. They are nemesis guilds created for the intent of fighting one another in an evolving storyline through the use of creative guildwars that will have predefined rules and set conditions that will apply to them.

Examples of such rules will be:

Death rules, when your dead your dead and you do not re enter a fight. To help add to a sense of realism. This also includes no attacking dead people who are dragging corpses to a designated Graveyard (See Below).

Rules of area engagement, I.E. fights will be set per area. If you flee the area you are out of the battle and cannot re enter the combat area until its resolved.

Graveyards, where the dead can be drug to and looted so that they may then flee the combat area. When you loot and are dead in a Graveyard you must then flee the zone.

These are just a few examples of some of the rules that may be implied during the course of an engagement. Of course you also may not know when there will be an engagement. After all, that trip to go save someone might end up with Guildwar being thrown in secret beforehand and you just never know what exactly is going to be happening.

The entire scope of this evolving multi-guild server wide guildwar shall be engineered in a fashion similiar to that of a Dungeons and Dragons type game. The storyline will unfold based around character backgrounds and histories as they become relevant and exposed through the course of gameplay and interactions upon "The GUILDWARS of the Gods" forums located at www.1stfistoflight.com

This will be a war that spans across the Firiona Vie server, with multiple battles, that all take place, nestled within the shadows of the Prophecy of Trakanon and the end times of Norrath. All those interested whom are interested and currently not in a guild or involved in an allied guild should "sign up" for "enlistment" at the forums under "Recruiters Hall" and specify which side you are interested in fighting for. Those guilds that wich to particpate and not allied yet should also post within the "Recruiters Hall" as well.

The first battle which shall to tie into the storyline shall be "The Battle for Takish-Hiz". A small sample of what is to come, and yes folks it is a guildwar fought INSIDE a dungeon. All events involved with the war as they transpire shall be maintained upon "The Sages Scrolls", found upon the war forums. May the heavens shake with thunder, and ground tremble, for the fate of the world lies to the dogs of war.

Banegrivm Shadowblight Sovereign Lord of the 1st Fist of Light Firiona Vie

EEP Viruses from Everlore Emails???
Georgia [1/18/2002-5:57:27 AM]  Comments[4]

I've had a mailbox full of people screaming "Oh my god! Virus! from your emails!" Never fear... we're not spamming you. If you receive a strange email from an everlore.com email address, which attempts to have you save a file to your computer, DO NOT open it!

This is a virus that has been going around for some time now, and it seems to have nabbed our emails to make it appear as if we sent them. So be careful, and don't open attachments unless you were expecting it from someone beforehand.

 

EQAnnounce Shut Down :(
Georgia [1/18/2002-5:55:39 AM]  Comments[16]

Ouch! Looks like the news post on EQAnnounce backfired into the creator's face. Their newsletter was submitted to us by Llaffer:
I recieved the following E-mail from the EQAnnounce mailing list:

===

Yes, if you didn't know Sony asked me to stop distribution. By using utilities like these, you may be putting yourself at risk.

Just think, "is it *really* worth it?"

There are discussions as to the why's of it all on our messageboard as well as a few others out there.

One of which is SafeHouse at:

[Click Here]

Good luck to you all in the game.

I then received this message from the EQAnnounce writer himself:
Hello guys,

The Author of EQAnnounce here. Making a public announcement -- that I have voluntarily ceased distribution of EQAnnounce.

The reason this announcement is important is to inform the public of Sony's position on this.

Even though I and many others feel it does not violate the EULA or Rules of Conduct, the Director of Legal and Business Affairs has asked me to discontinue distribution. This I am doing.

Although I believe the software does not violate the EULA or RoC, the distribution "without express written consent" -- is.

Interestingly enough, it doesn't stop there. This is the importance of this announcement.

Let me be very clear about this. I was told, by the Director himself, that should I continue running the software that I would be in violation and subject to discipline. And that it was up to me to decide if I thought they could detect me or not.

Although I love a challenge, my accounts are worth more to me than that.

To anyone else out there running EQ utilities, EULA or RoC compliant as they might be, you should really consider the risks should they try to enforce it.

Thanks,

Glimwin Nettlefoot, author of EQAnnounce /salute
