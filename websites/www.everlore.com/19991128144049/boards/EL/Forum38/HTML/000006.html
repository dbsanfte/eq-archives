Rather than listen to their guildmasters wishes, they simply left the guild and continued with their actions.

So all of this seems bad enough, we had the useual show of force going at that point - we killed everything as it spawned, and they sat and waited for the Frenzied to pop.

A pull went bad, and I died in the fight - leaving once again Asmog, Sargaras, Ulize, and Dayon to fight, but Maverick having had his equipment restored by the GM's had joined us as well. Still a very strong group with all vital parts there - just short one tank.
