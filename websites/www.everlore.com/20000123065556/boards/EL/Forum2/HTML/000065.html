I just purchased a Dell Inspiron 3700, with a PIII-500 and a Rage Mobility 3d processor and I have been playing everquest on it for about 2 weeks. The game works great, I never get lagged anymore as I did with my P200 desktop. The only negative issue is there is some clutter flashing to the right and left of my character as I move around indoors, its kind of annoying but I can get rid of it if I put the clipping pane ALL the way down, which I don't do. I am just thrilled to be able to play on a laptop anywhere and the game works great. The only other issue is the 3D audio was causing sound problems so I just turned it down until 3d audio was off and I haven't had the problem again. So overall its great, but not perfect. Note: The Inpiron 3700 is in a latitude chassis and is about 3 lbs lighter than the 7500, the 7500 has the Rage Mobility - P something, I don't know if its better than the M which is in the 3700 but it may be since there is more room in the 7500 case. There may be fixes to these problems but Verant is useless and states the game was not meant to be run on a laptop, what a copout. Also when I was getting disconnected all the time on my P200 desktop, I called and asked if it was the processor and the useless tech kid from Verant said no way, its a non issue everyone gets disconnected. I said yeah, I know, but I get disconnected all the time especially in crowded rooms. He said no no no. Then the P3 comes and I never have a problem with crowds at all, and I'm using the same ISP. So guess what Verant has some pretty useless people working on tech support. I'm just leaving everything alone, becuase it's working well so far. Good Luck.
