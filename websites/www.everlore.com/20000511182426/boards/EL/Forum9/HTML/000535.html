Pets, do the same damage as kick at lv9-15 roughly

Pets can let you run away.
Pets can chase mobs better than you ie, darkness + fear. My fav is give a pet a shadowman wepon (if there shadowmen group you can get some free) and darkness + feer a caster and my pet can kill yellow casters for me as they would take over half my hp in a few seconds, great when i fear the casters out of camps and let pet kill them.

Pets also can hit npc though the roof like they can hit you, good for wierd terrane and escaping it.

Pets are also damn fun and can help you lifetap greens, ie any lifetap you get a green can pop you and take all that health back, so let pet take the damage while you lifetap.

Pets can help group members and help other pets to make them more effective.
