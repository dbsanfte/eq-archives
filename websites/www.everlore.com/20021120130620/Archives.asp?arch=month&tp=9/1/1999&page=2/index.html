The Festival of Light
[9/14/1999-3:59:46 PM]  Comments[0]

Please note the following event on the Innoruuk Server.

Event Title: The Festival of Light
Event Type: Festival/Player Gathering
Participants: Everyone is invited to attend.
Location: Desert of Northern Ro, by Fish Huts
Date/Time: Sunday, September 19, 1999 8pm EST (7pm CST, 6pm MST, 5pm PST)
Game Server: Innoruuk
Description:

Hear Ye! Hear Ye!

In accordance with great public outcry, the Society of the Inner Light is once again holding the Festival of Light, a celebration with games and fun for all.

Events:

8pm - Arch Magistrate Tallinar Darkenbane of the Society of the Inner Light will officially begin the Festival of Light.

8:15pm - Scavenger Hunt - Participants will be given a list of items to obtain and return to a designated Inner Light guildmember. Prizes will go to the first three people to complete the hunt.

8:45pm - Drunken Relay Race - Particpants will form teams of four members. Each team will place one member at the following locations: Camp Fire at the Fish Huts in Northern Ro, the Shady Squashbuckler in East Commonlands, The Arena entrance in West Freeport, and the East Gate in East Freeport. Inner Light judges will be present at each checkpoint to ensure legality. Tallinar will provide each team with a unique 'baton' which will need to be passed between the members of the team. At the start of the race, each member of all teams will consume no less than 20 alcoholic drinks. Prizes will be awarded to the first two teams to return their 'baton' to the designated Inner Light guildmember.

9:15pm - Item Raffle - The Inner Light will again be raffling off several different items, to be announced on the day of the event. There will several different items with tickets costing anywhere from a few silver to a few platinum.

9:45pm - Grand Melee - This event will be held in the Arena in West Freeport. Interested participants will register with a designated Inner Light guildmember and will pay a 1pp entrance fee. Participants will line the walls of the arena and wait for the shout of 'Fighters! GO! GO! GO!'. This is an anything goes, no-holds-barred fight. The last person standing will receive 80% of the purse, the remaining 20% going to future Festivals.

10:15pm - Sand Giant Hunt - If enough participants are interested, we will proceed to hunt the Sand Giants that terrorize Northern Ro. If this hunt goes well, we may expand into Oasis to eliminate the Giants there as well.

All Day Events:

Bazaar - Everyone is invited to bring items to sell and trade. We ask that you use the /auction channel to announce items for sale/purchase/trade and that you use /tell to conduct negotiations.

Language Lessons - Members of the Inner Light will be organizing language lessons for those who wish to learn the rarer languages of Norrath. There is no fee, however, donations will be accepted.

Buffs/Food/Drink/Alcohol - These items will be provided by Inner Light guildmembers for donations.

The last Festival of Light was a blast and we hope you return again to have even more fun this time around.

Arch Magistrate Tallinar Darkenbane
Guildmaster, Society of the Inner Light
(Innoruuk Server)

New Patch Message
[9/14/1999-4:49:05 PM]  Comments[0]

Just in: New Patch update! The Box of Abu'kar returned to normal state!

September 14th, 3:00pm
----------------------
Trade Skills:
- Tailoring: More items have been added to the trade skill. The instructions on how to make the new items were also added. The difficulty of some tailor-made items was lowered making them easier to produce. New patterns now exist t round out the tailor-made items.
- Brewing: New liquor can now be made. The instructions are hinted at in the game.
- Pottery: The Mortar and Pestle is now droppable and Vials are now stackable
- Bowyer: More slots have been added to the Fletching Kit. Dual-cam bows have been added.
- Fletching: The range of normal store-bought arrows has been reduced. Players may now make a cheaper more effective arrow with the fletching skill. Nock vendors now sell all arrow components.

Items:
- Lustrous Russet Armor, the Hero Bracer, Order of Thunder and the Box of Abu-Kar have been returned to their previous statistics.
