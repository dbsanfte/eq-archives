I am a level 15, Human, Enchanter.

Just wanted to let everyone know, for those of you worried about your religious factions, that I completed the Stein quest today and I am not Agnostic. I don't know how many posts I've seen saying to choose Agnostic, it's safer. That,even with Benevolence, they will see your religion through your illusion and KOS you. Well, my CHA isn't that great, I did have a +3 CHA necklace on, I cast Alliance only on Clurg (I don't have Benevolence yet), I illusioned myself as a DE (I don't have Illusion Orgre yet) and ran invisible until I stood before Clurg. I hailed him and he told me about the bouncers crushing me if I got rowdy. Then I gave him the book. CHA CHING! Got the stein. I made sure I refreshed my illusion as I went along, even if it hadn't worn off yet, I didn't want it leaving me at the wrong times, then I cast invisible and kept going until I completed the quest. Stepped out in the hall to a private corner and gated home with my stein. Not only that, but there were high levels killing the bouncers when I got there and they drop the other stein on the ground. So I got to gate back with both Steins on my trip. And I was only conned by all the Ogres I met. I got a SOW outside the Freeport gate and ran all the way. I completed the quest in just about an hour and that was after being sidetracked by two Orc Priests...my invisiblity failed just as I ran thru an Orc camp in Oasis! (which forced me to zone in to Nro) and being being chased by a Ghoul in Sro (which forced me to zone into the swamp) I was virtually unarmed, since this was my first time, and if I was going to lose my corpse, it wasn't going to have anything of value on it...so I banked everything but the book, food and water, and some minor items I didn't have room for in the bank. Turns out, it wasn't necessary, because I had absolutely no trouble. The hardest part of the whole quest was getting the darn fish! Someone had a low level they used just for this purpose and killed the fish for me and let me loot it. I bought the honeycombs from newbies inside Misty Thicket for 5gp each. The halfling merchant didn't like me, so I did the Runnyeye Warbeads quest once to raise my faction with her. With Gate, the trip to Erudin (two items there) was no problem. All in all gathering the ingredients took longer than taking the compendium to Clurg. So go ahead everybody, give it a try? It wasn't as hard as I thought it would be!
