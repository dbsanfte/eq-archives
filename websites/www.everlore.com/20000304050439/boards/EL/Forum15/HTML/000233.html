Heh.... This ain't Elips, but I know how he does it. It's kinda hard at first (I know... I've done it), but it goes a little something like this :
1) Find your target, and get a distance ahead of it.
2)Use the bellows song, once it hits, start running forward. Repeatedly use the bellows song until it falls.
3) If your target is almost close enough to hit you, switch to Selo's and get ahead a bit.
4) Sometime monsters can run faster than your regular run, requiring you to keep Selo's up almost the entire time. Just use 1 pulse of Selo's, then 2 bellows.


Hope that was accurate, and that you can use it =)
