SylvenMaybe, kind of like Wu's Gauntlets for Monks. But what would be the point, given the availablility of cheap combine weaps?

*If* they ever add the weaps, which I doubt, it seems more likely that it would:

1) Require very high smithing skill. The best jewelry doesn't trivial, even at 200 skill. That would probably be the case for the enchanted weaps, too - this serves to reduce the # items out there, so an enchanted forged sword doesn't become the standard newbie weap.

2) Have either an expensive component (jewel) or a rare component (rare drop mold or something) as part of the recipes.

The other alternative is what you said - that neither of the above is true, and that all you need is to mix in a GLS or a vial of viscous mana or some other common ingredient. But the result would be a rather weak weapon (same as forged weapons, but magic) - in which case, what's the point?

I'd prefer #1 and #2 above with a resulting nice weapon, to the other option (weak, easy to make weapon).
