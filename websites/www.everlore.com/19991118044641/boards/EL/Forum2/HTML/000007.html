My character is a 16th lvl bard who has fished most common low lvl places at one time or another. In my opinion the best fishing spot was the Pool of Life in Qeynos. I had heard a rumor that if your skill was high enough you could "catch" Kolidia (sp?) fish w/o having to attack it. Wasn't able to prove the rumor, but pulled up a fish for almost very cast of my pole. (Wonders if the priests are "stocking" the pool w/ fish)

I'm not sure you could count this a "fishing" (anymore then you could a couple of "good 'ole boys" throwing firecrackers in the water, then picking the stunded fish off of the surface), but my best place to fish, if I am starting to get real hungery...is Rivervale. I hop into the water & swim over to where I normally see the pirahna (sp?) & hit my Discord song (DoT AoE) which will cause the pirahna & trout in the area to attack me. I then swim to the shore just opposite of the pier (where there is a nice high spot to stand on - so I can stay under water longer), turn and attack the pirahna & let the other fish get finished by the song. I can usually get 2 pirahna & 2-4 trout per pull. Just remember to loot the bodies quickly & to surface for a breath every so often. This will yield fresh fish, river trout, fish scales, pirhana teeth & if you catch "Chomper" a lore item ruby.
