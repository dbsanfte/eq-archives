Is it just me, but i get really sick and tired of hearing how people became an enchanter just to make money from the stein quest and jewelry.

To me that dilutes the pool of enc on the server, and makes me wish id chose something else.
Im not against making money, dont get me wrong im no rich twink, i barely have 100 plat in the bank, but theres no way I will do the stein of moggok quest 3 times a day just to make a bit of cash.
I have a friend who bacame an enc just to do that, and together with his druid pal to tp him everywhere, he does at least one stein if not more a day.

Now personally i can see this backfiring, with so many people doing the stein quest and selling them eventually the world will be overpopulated with steins and you wont be able to give them away.
I became an enchanter solely on thefact of the illusion spells. Nothing else. I dont charge for binding or enchanting metal, if i do its never a price more a few items that i need, batwings food etc.

I know a few will probably disagree, but i find this kind of selfishness and greed quite dishartening. I mean on the server im on, to get the components for honey jum people will only sell at 1plat for one comb. Nevermind the comb sells for 8cp to merchants. But to someone making 300plat+ 2plat for the honey jum is nothing. But to me doing the quest on foot by myself for myself i find that outrageous. Its just another case of a falsified economy, kinda like charging 1plat+ for 20 bonechips or 20plat for hq bear skins.
It seems people these days dont want to earn their items, they just want to amass a fortune and buy them.
Well i hope i dont fall to the dark side, but its becoming increasingly obvious that most enc are just greedy self absorbed people out to make a buck rather than help people.
