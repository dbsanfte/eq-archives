Here is what i am doing so far:

You can hunt the piclaws guards in High Keep. They do con blue at 30 and they are easy to take down. Moreover, there are guards a small run away and their spawn rate is very slow. The only down side is that they are well camped on evenings
However, you can still visit the jails and in the largest one (with a big door and 2 barred windows on each side) there is a named prisonner spawning there. I have not soloed him once, but he did con blue and was a piece of cake with my 30 pally husband

Althought I would seriously recommend to just group and group and group, this is the best way to get out of "hell level" and the previous post does provide the best places to do it.

I hope this helps.
