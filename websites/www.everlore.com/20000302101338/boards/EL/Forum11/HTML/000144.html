Your shaman (and druid and cleric) weapon skills cap at (lvl*4) + 4...as opposed to melee class characters (monk,rogue,warrior,palladin,ranger) who cap at (lvl*5) + 5

So you aren't having problems getting your skills to rise...you are MAXED for your level! (13*4) + 4 = 56

BTW the "pure caster" classes (nec,mag,ench,wiz) all cap at (lvl*3) + 3....
so don't feel TOO bad!

Nice thing about being a Shaman is that you can Buff the HELL out of yourself and fight on a level that is closer to that of a melee class than any other class around. Especially if you are a big, tough Ogre.
