Absor Responds to Some Player Questions
Behlryn [3/2/2001-7:38:38 AM]  Comments[8]

Absor responded to a list of question from a player on the offical boards. View the entire thread here.

drizzut_1 wrote:
>"1. Are there plans to split any servers in the next month?
2. Are there plans to add any new servers in the next month?
3. Are there plans to split any servers in the next 3 months?
4. Are there plans to add any new servers in the next 3 months?"<

Not that I know of. But that doesn't mean that there won't be new servers some time in the near future, just that there aren't any plans that I know of for one. As I've said several times before, as soon as we make such plans, we let you know.

>"5. Are there plans to increase lvl 55+ spell drops in the next 2 months?
6. Are there plans to sell lvl 55+ spells (that are currently dropped) from vendors in the next 2 months?
6a. Are there plans to make lvl 55+ spells (that are currently dropped) questable in the next 2 months?"<

We're still looking at all that stuff from the class talk threads. Again, as soon as we have solid plans about these things, we'll let you know. I'm not prepared to predict the unknown…

>"7. Will there be an adjustment made to the Cleric/Hate issue on the next patch?"<

Seems likely. There are changes on Test now. They might go live, they might not. Until we're done testing them we don't know. But it seems likely that at least some of the changes on Test currently will go live.

>"8. When the servers drop as they did today can we PLEASE get something posted on the system status/scheduled downtime page to inform us of what is going on?"<

That often depends on why they went down. Often the servers seem to go down because of ISP problems at one end or the other. We do our best to get a notice up. But for very short-term outages it's not always practical or useful to put up a notice.

>"9. Will changes to the epic quests be made in the next month?"<

I don't know. We're working on some changes. Because these changes are complicated and important, we're not going to make them until we're ready.

>"10. Are there plans to make the Pegasus cloak tradeable?"<

Not that I know of.

>"11. Are there plans to change the faydedar spawn so that at least a handful of druids can get their epic?
12. Are there plans to move ranger/druid drops off VS or at least split them up so you don't have 2 classes competing for the same drops?"<

See answer to #9.

>"13. Will more boats be added within the next 2 months?"<

Not that I know of.

>"14. Are there plans to change the SLeeper's tomb key to soulbound item?"<

Not that I am aware of.

>"15. Are there plans to fix the Monk Mend issue in the next 2 months?"<

We're working on locating the problem.

>"16. Are there plans to release the zone THE WARRENS in the next 2 months?"<

That is planned. We'll announce it's release when we nail it down.

>"17. Is POM Itemized at this time?"<

As far as I know it is.

>"18. Has anyone from a LIVE server gotten any items out of POM other than the cards we are hearing about?"<

I don't know.

>"19. Are there plans within the next 6 months to release diety specific quests (such as POG)?"<

Deity specific? I don't understand the question.

>"20. Are there plans to reduce the UBER MOBS HP or offensive power in the next 2 months?"<

We are looking at some of the creatures of Velious for potential change.

>"21. Would you consider setting up special chat room sessions with people who post on this board to discuss customer communication issues in a small group format?"<

We have developer chats just about every month, hosted by fan sites. We are considering hosting some ourselves. But it doesn't seem likely that we would restrict attendance to any sub-group of players.

>"22. Are there plans to upgrade ranger defense in the next 2 months?"<

Again, we're looking at all the class balance stuff. We'll certainly let you all know when we've made decisions.

>"23. Are there plans to implement better written polls on the log in board (the last one on customer service gave many of us no choice to really voice our opinion on the quality of customer service)?"<

No. We plan to start writing polls in thieves cant so that nobody can understand them… I find it amusing that you would ask a question that leaves me no way to answer it under your rules (yes or no) that is so skewed that just by answering I must also agree with your assumption that the poll in question was poorly written. Perhaps you should reword that and ask again.

>"24. Do you play on a live server?"<

Sure do.

>"25. If the answer to 24 is Yes is your character above lvl 50?"<

I don't talk about my characters. I will not reveal level, class, race… none of that. Doing so, unless I could claim to have played all classes and all races to 60th level, would do nothing to improve my credibility. It would only serve to focus people on the facts of my class preferences and be a hindrance to conversation on nearly any topic.

>"26. Are there plans to give the clerics a JOLT type spell in the next 2 months?"<

Not that I know of.

>"27. Are there plans to implement more portal type spells in the next 2 months?"<

Not that I know of.

>"28. Are there plans to revamp POA in the next 2 months?"<

I don't know. I haven't heard any such rumors.

>"29. Are there plans to implement more bind points in the next 2 months?"<

We've already announced such plans. The exact date of implementation is not set. Again, we'll announce them when we know them.

>"30. Are there plans to change the Succor type spells so they will become more effective in cuttong down on travel time (ie DL and WK place group near a current portal rather than the opposite end of zone which would be MUCH more helpful in cutting down travel time)?"<

I have no idea. It seems unlikely to me, though.

>" Are there plans to reduce the Spellbook med from lvl 35 to lvl 20 within the next 3 months?"<

Not that I know of.

Sorry, I don't follow restrictions like only yes or no. They certainly don't make your questions easier to answer. Some of your questions just can't be answered with only a yes or no. My general answer is that we'll announce changes as we decide them.

Alan

Site Update - Problems With Pop-up Banners
Xanthus [3/2/2001-6:23:36 PM]  Comments[13]

Thanks to some concerned users we are aware of some pop-up banners that are acting a little strange. Normally if a pop-banner is closed that is the end of it. What some viewers are reporting are popup banners that spawn more banners when they are closed. If this occurs, users need to email us immediately so we can contact our advertising company to fix the problem.

Although multi-browser spawns are rather annoying it is important for users to note that the above does not apply if you in click on the advertisement in any way except to close it. If an advertisement is clicked, you close the original browser, and as a result multiple browsers open, we can't change that but, if you attempt to close an advertisement directly after it opens without ever clicking on it and, more browser open, then you need to contact us so we can take care of it.

Thanks for your help and understanding as we navigate new waters.

 

Network Update - Remove Your Advertisements
Xanthus [3/2/2001-8:46:31 PM]  Comments[35]

With the current status of the Internet advertising industry, revenue streams used to pay for site maintenance (bandwidth, server equipment, firewalls etc) have been dwindling. It has become harder by the month to maintain our sites. Recently the Lore Network was forced to turn pop-up banners on to get some sort of revenue to keep the sites up and running. We know that these pop-up banners and advertising in general is something most users don't want.

In fact, most users would probably rather not have any advertising at all but, it's been something we have had to do to keep the sites up. We know that for some of our viewers the pop-ups are more than an annoyance as their work network security scrutinizes popup traffic closely. Now we are giving our viewers an additional option. You can continue to visit the site as normal or, for a small annual fee of 15 dollars a year you can view the site absolutely advertisement free. That means, no top page banners, no skyscraper banners and no pop-up banners for a whole year. For just pennies a day users will get an advertisement free account that will work across all RPG Lore sites which includes, Everlore, Asheron's Lore, Anarchy Lore, Rpg Lore, Origin Lore, and any new sites that are released.

So if you want to view the site at work without the risk of banner content, or if you just want to help support the RPG Lore Network through 2001 then please subscribe today.

Pending Breastplate Changes
Giraffa [3/4/2001-9:54:07 AM]  Comments[28]

A continuation of the upcoming changes are being discussed on this thread:

You can follow the previous discussion here: [Thead Found Here]

This is regarding the clerical Plane of Growth breastplates, the ones with the ability to cast Celestial Elixer (the Chestplate of Stability, Akkirus' Chestplate of the Risen, the Breastplate of Forbidden Rites and the Templar's Chestplate). For many of the same reasons that we made recent changes to the Donal's Breastplate, we are going to make some changes to these items with the next patch (which will be next week). Existing instances of these Plane of Growth breastplates will have the casting time for Celestial Elixer increased to one minute.

No new instances of the Plane of Growth breastplates will occur (they will stop dropping).

In places where the Plane of Growth breastplates were being created (off of NPC corpses or as the result of quests) new breastplates will be created.

Basically, the existing breastplates are having their casting times increased and they won't be dropping anymore, new breastplates will take their place.

I'm sorry that these items are being changed so long after their introduction. But this is something that we feel needs to be done for the integrity of gameplay in EverQuest.

Alan

Test Server Now Including a Downtime Game: Gems
Georgia [3/5/2001-10:03:05 AM]  Comments[37]

You've seen everything over the past two years since EverQuest hit the shelves, but now a downtime game has been added to the environment for users to play.

Similar to Tetris, "Gems" involves aligning spell gem icons of the same type so that rows and columns may be deleted in order to keep the screen clean. The game is accessible through the /gems command on the Test Server. No news has been released on if or when this game will go to the live servers.

 

The Tribunal: First Anitonica Walkabout
Bafoemet Pangenitor [3/5/2001-10:04:42 AM]  Comments[6]

The Ring of Loyalty is hosting a first every Walkabout. Everyone is invited to participate.

First Anitonica WALKABOUT-Mar. 7-9

The First Antonica Walkabout-Beginning March 7

HEAR YE! HEAR YE! HEAR YE! COME ONE! COME ALL!

Our adventure will begin in Ro Tunnel at the North Ro entrance. Start arriving at 9:00 est. Each RoL officer present will gather a group of the nonmembers present. The remaining high levels will form a group that will be the emergency killing squad . We will begin our march south thru the Ro desert. We'll stay along the west wall in order to enter the Oasis along the orc highway. We will continue south, wasting orcs as we go. We'll do a trip around the lake for those who may want supplies at the gypsy camp and the Inn. If our numbers are strong enuf, perhaps some revenge will be reaped upon a sand giant or spectre. Our journey continues south to South Ro. Not alot here unless the Ancient Cyclops spawns. Of course there are some more orcs to waste. Next we enter Innothule Swamp. At this point, we will decide if a short trip into Guk is desired. Leaving the swamp we enter Feerott, a dark gloomy place. Here there are plenty of lizards to kill. A scout will be sent into Cazic-Thule to report population density. If the zone is not too crowded, a trip into the courtyard is not out of the question. Leaving Feerott we find ourselves in the Rathe Mountains. Some giant killing is definately in order here. They have been known to drop massive amounts of platinum there. For one, I could use some. Once we've filled our pockets we'll head to the lake for some water sports. Inside the entrance to Lake Rathetear, Rings of Flight will be passed out to those without levitate. From here, we'll head to the island with the famous diving platform. All those drunk enuf will shurely want to make the plunge. Our final stop for the night will be the Arena. Need I say more???

The second night of our Walkabout begins at 9:00est at the Arena. From there we depart to South Karana. Our first stop will be at the Aviak treehouse. Next we visit Splitpaw. The main room in front of the Big Doors should give us plenty of room for a few casual kills. From here we head north to the stone bridge. Crossing the stone bridge we head west. We travel thru North Karana and West Karana, pillaging as we go. Upon entering Qeynos Hills, some may wish a quick trip to town for more beer. From town we'll head north for a quick stop in Surefall Glade, a very pretty zone. From here we go southeast to Blackburrow and waste a few goblins on the way to Everfrost. Everfrost is full of adventure. Some very nice loot drops here too. Time permitting, we definately want to raid Permafrost. Maybe even sneak a peak at old Lady Vox herself. When we're done with Perma, we will pick a spot to camp for the night. If it's not too late, we'll make the run to the entrance of HighHold Pass and camp there.

The third night we will gather again at 9:00 est.. Depending upon the number of "dark ones" in the group, we'll decide to go either thru Runnyeye and Rivervale or thru High Hold to our next stop. And our next stop is of course the dreaded dark Kithicor Forest. Here we'll spend some time totally destroying this zone. Our adventure continues next thru the Commonlands. Lots to destroy on our path thru here, but we won't spend much time. On to the Nektulous Forest, where it may be necessary to kill a few guards blocking our path. Lavastorm Mountain will be our last major zone to rampage. Here we can split into some smaller groups and visit the three dungeons here. Sol A is definately too narrow for a large group, but it could be done. A visit to Sol B and Najena is definately worth a trip too. Those who feel guilty about all the killing and drinking they've done can say a few prayers in the Temple of Ro. As our Walkabout closes, a final meeting at a bar in Freeport or a bar in Neriak is definately on the list.

Please feel free to invite any of your friends or anyone who may be interested in joining the guild along. This should be a wonderful time and a great chance to meet everyone in the guild. If you arrive late, catching up won't be hard to do. If for some reason we get off schedule, we'll continue until our trip is finished.
