I have been looking around for a while and I think I know the 6 strongest NPCs in the game, tell me if there are any that are as strong or stronger than this.

Priest Of Discord - Well, DUH. He has 30250, and hits for 200-356 a pop. When dual wielding he will hit around that for 4 times. He also has a "Lay on Hands" when he is about to die and also has an AE spell.
LOCATION: Every city.
CLASS: Human Cleric.
LEVEL: 50.

Corporal Lancot - A GM once charmed him and made him duel the PoD. Guess what, Lancot WON. He hits for 350 and has 30200 HP.
LOCATION: North Qeynos.
CLASS: Human Paladin.
LEVEL: 50.

Noble Dojorn - This guy has FULL Magic Resist. He has 32000 HP, one of the hardest to beat in the game. Forget casters, and you will need more than 40 people to take him down.
LOCATION: Plane of Sky.
CLASS: Unknown Warrior.
LEVEL: 63.

Sister of the Spire - Very hard to kill, has more HP than the Noble Dojorn, 32334 to be exact. Doesn't cast, but will be hard to cast on.
LOCATION: Plane of Sky.
CLASS: Unknown Warrior.
LEVEL: 63.

The Spiroc Lord - This mob may have full magic resist like the Noble Dojorn due to its level, and 32261 HP. It can also cast.
LOCATION: Plane of Sky.
CLASS: Unknown Ranger.
LEVEL: 63.

And finally...

Brother Zephyl - He's a hard hitter, and will destroy any caster in a matter of seconds. He runs as fast as SoW, and some people report that his round kick does 972 damage and his punches do 200+ damage.
LOCATION: Rathe Mountains
CLASS: Human Monk
LEVEL: 50
