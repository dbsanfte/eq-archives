That 20 point damage shield you got was cast by a magician, the only other class with a decent damage shield (rangers and wizards [ a 2dmg shield] don't count). They're best at level 50, lavashield, does 25dmg (as opposed to 24 for druids), but doesn't last quite as long.

Damageshields are a groups best friend when fighting multiple mobs or mobs that hit really, really fast (ie frenzied ghoul). Tack on a lavashield and that 24dmg druid shield on a good tank, the frenzied gets pierced/burned for 49dmg every attack - a little less than half of what he hits for. If he's got a sash and really lands some blows in, the tank is going to do the vast amount of damage (assuming the cleric heals him before he dies) because of the shield. I know my warrior loves them....
