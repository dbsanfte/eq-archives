Since level 10, I have not done much soloing at all. For the most part once you learn the safety and the beauty of using instruments in a group, chances are that you will stick with it, and not bother with other dangers.

I like groups. Safer, they can help cover you, and we help cover them. With various other goons, like Enchanters, we can do some major crowd control. With Rogues we can do massave damage. It's all there. In the time that you can solo one and rest, with another you will be able to do three of those same critters -- meaning that you will get more XP with a partner (or a group), and that the down time and chances of getting hurt will diminish ...

Solo? Sure, why not. But I have flown so fast in the past 7 levels in groups, and I doubt I could do it solo.
