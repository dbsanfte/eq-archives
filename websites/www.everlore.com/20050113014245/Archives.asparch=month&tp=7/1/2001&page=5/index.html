A Tribute to Lorisyn Oakwynd - GM Event on Saryn
Fayja Quar'tarine [7/22/2001-2:32:56 AM]  Comments[3]

On Thursday night Greater Faydark received notice through e-motes that the ranger had returned home. Immediately the zone was astir with questions of what was going on and where this ranger was. Many more people began searching for the ranger after the zone received another message saying that the ranger had sat beneath a tree and started to cry.

After searching for awhile I came upon a group of people surrounding a wood elf ranger by the name of Lyirae Oakwynd. Lyirae was telling the crowd why she was so sad and why she had returned home. Lyirae's twin was Lorisyn Oakwynd, the ranger who traveled with Firionia Vie and gave his life protecting Firionia when Ghargin Bumblok threw a bolder at her. Lorisyn had pushed Firionia aside and took the hit, killing him. After her brother's death Lyirae had promised to take up where her brother had left off and started traveling and adventuring with Firionia Vie.

Lyirae returned to Kelethin to celebrate their birthday, but was finding it extremely difficult. She had wanted to place a great bouquet of flowers on his headstone, but the flowers were not well in bloom she told us. She said she wanted at least a rose to place on his grave site. Luckily Mikeo, one of Tunare's followers in the crowd, stepped forward and gave the lady a rose preventing a long trip to the outpost of Firionia Vie.

After she received the rose she asked us if we would come with her to her brother's gravesite in Lesser Faydark to celebrate with her. Everyone agreed and followed her to Lesser Faydark. Upon entering the zone Mikeo and another child of light Metellus lead the group to the ranger outpost safely. A couple of Tier'Dal Elite rushed at the group, but was quickly killed before they could do any harm.

Once they were dead the group who accompanied Lyirae went just behind the ranger outpost and paid tribute to this hero of Tunare. Cake and brew were passed around and everyone toasted Lorisyn. Lyirae gave Mikeo some platinum to take the group back to Greater Faydark so we could continue to celebrate there while she took a few minutes to be alone. She thanked us all for our support and our friendship and the rest of us went back to Kelethin and celebrated Lorisyn and Lyirae's birthday by drinking until we could drink no more.

This is a story about my good friend Oothomp and his pack of wolves. Some of you may not like Oothomp considering the body count that the small statured troll left on the ground of the Southern Karanas that evening, however, his story must be told so that future generations will not forget it...

As I was preparing to head back to the bank at Qeynos after a semi-successful jaunt into the Infected Paw, I meet a troll on the bridge that spans the river that divides North and South Karana (screen shot 1). The troll's name was Oothomp and he required that you give him shineez as a toll for crossing his bridge. (screen shout 2) After a little discussion, I realized that the troll wanted coins. I figured rather that risk a conflict with a troll his three wolf pets, I would give him a few plat and try to calm him down. Not long after that Oothomp said that his wolves were hungry. (screen shot 3) So, as the Tribunal had blessed me with food when I needed it, I deceided to give the troll the last of my bread to feed his hungry dogs.

Oothomp thanked me and announced that I was now his friend. (screen shot 4) The good troll was good enough to give me some of his gnome meat sandwichs, which were quite tasty. Soon after a dwarf tried to cross the bridge, which sent Oothomp away from me and to the dwarf in demand for his toll... He refused and well, (screen shot 5) the wolves and the troll made quick work of the unarmed dwarf. I sat and watched as person after person refused to pay the toll and was quickly torn apart by the angry wolves. (screen shot 6 and 7) I tried to make these people understand that Oothomp was not to be reasoned with or ignored, but my advice went unanwsered. After a while of collecting tolls and slaugtering those that didn't, Oothomp said that he now owned the entire Southern Planes and that he was coming to levy a toll on those that hunted on his lands. We arrived at the spires to find a group of brave souls killing the foul gnolls that reside in Paw. When payment was refused...

Oothomp struck again... (screen shot 8 and 9) Oothomp had taken a liking to me tring to explain that these were his lands and got me to follow him around the Planes demanding a toll for hunting on his land. We arrived at the Aviak's Town to find another hapless individual this time begged for my help as Oothomp and his wolves tore the poor druid limb from limb, Little did she realized that Oothomp was my friend, and I would do no harm to him. (screen shot 10) Oothomp then smiled and told me that he had something for me. Confused I looked at Oothomp as he handed me sack that contained a small wealth of money and his last gnome meat sandwich. (screen shot 11) I thanked Oothomp as one of his wolves licked my hand, and Oothomp and I said our good byes and parted company. (screen shot 12)

I'll likely never for get the events of that night, and I hope that someday, Oothomp and I will cross paths again...

The End

EverQuest Patch Message
Xanthus [7/24/2001-10:55:05 AM]  Comments[21]

Here is the Patch Message for today:

July 24th, 3:00 am
------------------------------

** Item Changes **

- Silver Chitin Hand Wraps' haste effect has been repaired.
- The Smite scroll no longer lists Enchanters as a class that can use it.
- The Augment Death scroll no longer lists Shadowknights as a class that can use it.
- Many pelts have been made stackable. Those that are not stackable now are quest items that will not be made stackable.

** Spell Changes **

- Focus of Spirit will now stack with Voice of the Berserker.
- Barrier of Force will now stack with Protection of the Glades.
- Manaskin and Protection of the Glades will now stack.

** Trade Skill Changes **

- Repaired some class/deity combinations on two of the deity specific pottery Idols.

** Changes to Sullon Zek **

- There are no faction hits for killing half-elves in any city.
- If an Erudite of Paineel is killed in Paineel the killer takes a faction hit (existing rule).
- If an Erudite of Erudin is killed in Erudin the killer takes a faction hit (new rule).
- Bards can't cast harmful spells on boats.
- If a character kills a higher level character, the higher level character will always lose experience. This is in addition to the fact that any player killed within the + or - 5 level range will lose experience.
- Goods and Neutrals will now be able to turn in Greater Insignias for a Magician only summon corpse potion usable only at levels over 45th.
- Characters under level 6 will no longer be immune to PvP in many adventure zones. Players will be notified when they enter such zones.

** Misc. Changes **

- /target playername will now work on invisible targets as long as the character doing the targeting has the ability to see invisible.

** NOTE **

Boisterous shouts can be heard across the lands as carriers herald the arrival of the latest volume of their city's newspapers. Citizens rush to get a copy of these publications to read the featured articles, latest gossip, and community news for their hometowns.

- The EverQuest Team

Lanys T'vyl Visits WC on Lanys
Owen DuBose [7/25/2001-10:36:23 AM]  Comments[3]

Greetings!

I was going about my usual business of hunting in East Commonlands this evening when words spread that the daughter of Hate was in nearby West Commonlands. My curiosity overwhelmed my fear and I rushed over to see what the evil wench had to say. Being a young Druid as well as a worshipper of Karana, I found myself fearful yet far too interested in such an incredible event to shie away. I had to see if it was true! And it was! I approached the Tier'Dal camouflaged, though I doubt a lowly person such as myself would have been noticed by her in the large crowd that was gathered around her. The throng of people was quite rude and boisterous and many attempts on the life of Lanys T'vyl were made, all to no avail.

Many brave fools, er, I mean souls, were slain by the evil hand of the daughter of Innoruuk. Many attempts at bribery were made, and thre crowd generally treated her as if she were a famed Troubadour singing popular songs, crowding her incessantly in spite of her warnings to give her room and the many deaths at the touch of her evil hand when her patience wore too thin. Her patience grew quite thin at the angry and often vociferously nasty mob, but, eventually, she spoke. She wished to send a message to her father, and to the Tier'Dal rulers, aligned with Innoruuk, who continually bother her followers who have set up encampments inside the evil Befallen. She therefore named a great task of those who were loyal to her, to kill the Tier'Dal Captains present at the many new evil encampments within the Commons. Only one brave soul stepped forward to do her bidding, a person named Yorak, whom I never really got a good look at since my small stature was taken advantage of by many others who decided to stand so close that I could not see the poor lad.

His mission at her command was to dispose of the Captains of these Tier'Dal camps, at which time Lanys would hand her new Emissary a message to take to Queen Christanios. As the hapless helper of evil left to do her bidding, the crowd grew even more uncontrollable. Ultimately, Lanys T'vyl left discreetly in a huff, disgusted with the throng, admonishing them with a simple message: "In the wake of the vanishing of the Daughter of Hate is left the knowledge that had many of those gathered before her listened to her commands, perhaps other worthy people whould have recieved her attentions."
