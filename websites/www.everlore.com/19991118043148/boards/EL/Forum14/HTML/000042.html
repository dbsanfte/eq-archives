The going rate for banded tends to be about 1pp per AC point - so if you're banded waist armour has an AC value of 6 or greater you did fine.

You can get training in SureFall Glade as well. I have heard that the new patch was going to offer at least one more place to train - but I'm not sure where or who it was.

Sense heading is valuable in my opinion, it just takes a while to up it. My understanding is that it is success based - so failures don't count. Once you get some successes, you can start moving it up reasonably quickly - I find it's a very handy skill. Whenever I'm on a boat, resting/healing, waiting, etc. I click away, mine is about 70 now and I succeed about 2/3 of the time. Just be patient you'll get there.

I'm not a fan of 2H weapons, I think the speed to damage ratio isn't as good. Plus later on (17th I believe) you'll get dual wield - so personally I stay with 1H slash and 1H pierce. Most rangers I know don't use 2H much.

Hope this helps - enjoy!
