I was a member a full group of Rangers hunting this ghoul just within a day or so of the changes. (Six rangers hunting is an impressive sight. :-) )

This ghoul is not to be taken lightly. When we first started hunting it there was 3 of us; 26, 21 and 20/21. This thing nearly killed the 26st level Ranger. As a then 21st level ranger I had trouble hitting it. Later with a full party it caused no trouble. The youngest member was 20/21.

The ghoul spawned regularly. We didn't time it properly but, it was every 15 - 20 minutes. And dropped the map every time. Hence six kills, six happy Rangers.

The sword received for this quest is the Short Sowrd of Morin. It's stats are dmg 6, dly 22, wht 3. +3 agi, +3 str. I'm not 100% sure on the weight or delay, this is from memory.

Go join a party in West Karana and have fun. Just make sure your not all in your early 20s.
