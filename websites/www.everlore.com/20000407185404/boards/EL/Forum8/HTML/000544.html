ok the best way to use this is to combine it with your drain spells.

Here is what I generally do. If your just fighting battles that aren't overly tough and strain your mana supply cast it on yourself after battle and then sit. This will make it so that you only lose 4HP (because you gain 4 back from sitting) but you get the additional 8 mana in addition to your normal medding amount. Hence you med faster than any other caster with the exception of an enchanter with clarity. This lets you fight faster etc. Once you've medded to full just cast drain a few times on the next critter you fight to get your HP back.

If your fighting battles that strain your mana supply keep it on you during battle and cast drain when needed. It could very well give you that last little bit of mana you needed to win.

Don't you hate it when people do not specifically answer your question?

Dark Pact will drain away as many hps as you would gain while sitting. So, if you are sitting and medding you are effectively increasing your mana regeneration rate and not losing any hps in the process. Stand up, and you will notice the 4hps for 4mana exchange slowly occurring.

Allure of Death is the 20th level version and does 8hps for 8mana regen rate. Since you have so much more mana by now, you WILL see your hps go down even while sitting and medding.

In either case, DO NOT FORGET to turn off the spell when you stand up unless you plan on lifesiphoning the crap out of something.

*Note* Allure of Death has NOTHING to do with turning into a skeleton!
