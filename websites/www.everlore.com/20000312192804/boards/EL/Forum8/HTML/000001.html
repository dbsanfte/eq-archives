We have all seen the recent debates about Necros being able to resurrect. I saw a post from someone that suggested that on the PvP servers (Rallos/Tallon Zek) that Necros be able to raise the dead of a PC into a ghoul or something.

I have a refinement on this idea that I think is very cool....Please read on...

I think that Necros should be given a new spell for use on the PvP servers. This would probably be a research spell that you can get at 16 or 20. This spell would be called Will Of Innoruuk. Basically, it would be like making a sacrifice to Innoruuk. Here's how it would work...

Say I need a pet, and there happens to be a halfling just a Shadow Step away. Well, I would just:
1.) Cast Will of Innoruuk on the target PC to flag that this is a sacrifice.
2.) Try to kill the pitiful lightdweller just as one would in any other battle.

IF I SUCCEED AT THE SACRIFICE (win the fight), then I have a new pet ghoul.
BUT,
IF I FAIL AT THE SACRIFICE (lose the fight), then I die and lose a VERY LARGE amount of xp (maybe 1-2 bubs) for wasting Innoruuk's time, not using my skills wisely, and for bringing shame onto the Church of the Firstborn by dying to a lightdweller.

FAQ
Q: So, what happens if I get sacrificed?
A: You return to bind point as if you died from...uh...natural causes.

Q: Will my body still be there after I get sacrificed?
A: Yes, the Necro steals your soul, not your whole body.

Q: Can the Necro loot my corpse too?
A: No, he already got your soul. Isn't that enough?

Q: Is there any chance for revenge if I get sacrificed?
A: Oh, yes! If you see hat Necro again and kill him, you get your xp back from the last time he sacrificed you (with a bonus added). Plus, you can loot him.

Q: If I try to kill the Necro after he sacrifices me, can he use my own soul (in ghoul form) against me?
A: Yes. You might want some help if you plan to get revenge right away.

Q: Won't this spell lead to newbie hunting?
A: Not really. Necros would be limited to sacrificing PCs that con white and above.

Q: So, I risk alot by trying to sacrifice someone. How good is this pet anyway?
A: The new pet should probably be a ghoul, since it is technically a gift from Innoruuk in return for a sacrifice. It would be the level of the PC that was sacrificed.

Q: During a Sacrifice Battle, what about 3rd party players?
A: Innoruuk prevents ANY 3rd party players from getting involved. (Nobody else can heal or attack the Necro or Potential Sacrifice)

Q: How much mana and damage will this spell, Will of Innoruuk, take?
A: The mana to cast this should be pretty low. Probably around 10 or so. However, this spell will do NO damage. It is a mere flag to the game that a Necro is attempting a sacrifice and a flag to the hunted that he is about to get lain on the altar. (If sacrifices become unbalanced, this could be offset by giving the sacrificial lamb a slight buff - like Fight or Flight - that gives them either a chance to get away or stay and fight better. After all, their SOUL is at stake here!)

*NOTE*: These are just ideas that I came up with today. I think they are pretty cool and would add lots of excitement to the PvP world. These ideas are far from polished, but I did try to think of everything and keep it as balanced as possible. Please send me your feedback and, if necessary... FLAME ON!
