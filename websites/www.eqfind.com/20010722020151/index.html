Luclin Trailer -- Posted by Kyle on Friday, July 20 2001
I guess I am probably behind the curve here, but today, I finally went to the official "Luclin" page and watched the trailer movie (quicktime format, hires HERE) I was rather impressed. The thing I most noticed about the expansion is the upgraded graphics. I would not have figured Sony to release such a graphics overhaul until a sequel perhaps (notice Microsoft releasing Asheron's Call 2 this winter, and AC has been around a shorter amount of time than EQ.) This is ok with me, because I, probably like you, have spent countless hours building characters, and don't really feel like starting from scratch. I could care less about the cat race or the "beastmaster" class, the added spells, zones, etc. are cool, but the graphics are whats yanking the bills out of my pocket. I guess the only question I have is whether I will need to buy a new graphics card. (P.S. - I think it would be a nice touch if Sony contracted with Pink Floyd to use "Dark Side of the Moon" as theme music for Luclin.)
