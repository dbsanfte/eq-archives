PRESS RELEASE:

July 10, 2001

Kyle Howard, president of LoLWTF Networks(TM), based
in Wichita, Kansas,  today announced the grand
opening of its new game related website, EQFind(TM). 
EQFind(TM), located at eqfind.com, is a directory of the best
websites related to Sony's(R) hit online roleplaying game,
"Everquest.(R)"  One of the key features of EQFind(TM) is
its unique ability to meta-search 3 of the best EverQuest(R)
websites for information about online items.  EQFind(TM)
hopes to draw visitors who regularly search for Everquest(R)
information and would like to save time.  For the
actual item info, users are sent to the website with the
requested information, therefore each site retains
their web traffic.  This is the first game website by
LoLWTF Networks(TM).
