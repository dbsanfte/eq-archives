Trivial Loot -- Posted by Kyle on Friday, August 3 2001
Ah, what a good idea trivial loot is. In case you don't know, trivial loot will be implemented in Luclin. Many zones will have certain drops that will only be able to be looted by players in the general level vicinity of the npc killed. Therefore, high levels cannot monopolize all of the good items without risk to themselves. I think that this will help the mid and lower level ecomony. EQ comedy section up soon, plus weekly polls.

The Monk debate -- Posted by Kyle on Monday, July 23 2001
The monks are in a rage over feign death not working, plus some other things like the speed of monks attacks making everything agro on them in group fights. Read the letter to Verant. I myself have a monk character I play quite often, and although I haven't experienced the supposed FD bug, I think that if such a bug exists, it should be fixed. But you should read the other stuff they are demanding. My favorite has to be the combo attack, in which Monks could, by click the right buttons in the right order, launch a super combo that would be like: Flying Kick into Round Kick into Eagle Strike...etc. Mortal Kombat 3 style. Well, if verant wants to make my monk a super killer, they can feel free, but give me a break. This kind of thing would put such a huge chain-stun on the victim that monk's would be unstoppable. BOTTOM LINE: If there is a bug, by all means request that Verant fit it. But do they really smile upon whiners asking for upgrades to their class?

Luclin Trailer -- Posted by Kyle on Friday, July 20 2001
I guess I am probably behind the curve here, but today, I finally went to the official "Luclin" page and watched the trailer movie (quicktime format, hires HERE) I was rather impressed. The thing I most noticed about the expansion is the upgraded graphics. I would not have figured Sony to release such a graphics overhaul until a sequel perhaps (notice Microsoft releasing Asheron's Call 2 this winter, and AC has been around a shorter amount of time than EQ.) This is ok with me, because I, probably like you, have spent countless hours building characters, and don't really feel like starting from scratch. I could care less about the cat race or the "beastmaster" class, the added spells, zones, etc. are cool, but the graphics are whats yanking the bills out of my pocket. I guess the only question I have is whether I will need to buy a new graphics card. (P.S. - I think it would be a nice touch if Sony contracted with Pink Floyd to use "Dark Side of the Moon" as theme music for Luclin.)
