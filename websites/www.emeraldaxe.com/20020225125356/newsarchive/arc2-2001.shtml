TOFS Raid Posted Monday, March 19, 2001 by Lenenae McGillicuddy
Just a reminder...

Wednesday night is Tower of Frozen Shadow night! For those of you around level 29 and up, this is a great opportunity to learn some good dungeon crawling skills. TOFS, while a daunting dungeon, is fairly easy to journey through. I'll be there as well as some of the other high levels, last time we went we all came away with some decent items, and had a good time.

To get there, get yer butt a teleport to Ice Clad, or just take the boat from Northern Ro, get off at the island and run to the other side of the island and catch the Ice Breaker to the Gnome Camp. If you're worried about the mobs in the area, just park yer butt there, and someone will come get ya.

Also planning a small Epic Raid, there are 4 mobs I need to kill to get my epic quest started. The first is a level 40 Goblin in Lake Rathe, then a 40 Fire Elemental (easy), then the party will continue in the Temple of Solusek Ro where we have to take down the Plasmatic Priest. He's a level 55 warrior, I think, but apparently other mobs in the area will attack him when he pops. We just have to make sure we outdamage them and get in the last nuke/melee hit. (Tanks, and Nukers will be a must) Last is a Goblin in Solusek A, level 30, cake. I'm mulling over dates to do this, since I'm moving soon, I may wait until after I'm settled into my new digs before making the attempt.

Discussions on the forums are welcome.

KCoG News Posted Thursday, March 8, 2001 by Lenenae McGillicuddy
Here's a messages that the CoG asked us to post on our web sites. It involves an auction message board for Council Members.

--
Tired of /auc 'tioning in EC!!! Well stop! There's a better way now.

After having browsed dozens of community sites for the different servers, we discovered that there isn't one central place where the folks on Karana can come to peddle their wares and/or services.

EQVault is decent with a more than healthy viewership - but their posting system's inability to allow the user to edit their messages ends up creating a lot of spam.

Seeing a need for this - we adjusted one of the forums on the Karana Council of Guild's Main message board and are encouraging the residents of Norrath to start using it to Buy Sell and Trade whatever items are in their inventory.

It's an EZBoard forum so users have the ability to edit/update/maintain their own personal 'Inventory Threads' indefinitely, plus it creates a single place on Karana where folks can go to shop for those hard to find items. We want to start driving a lot of traffic to this forum simply because it's usefulness is determined by the amount of people that actually use it.

If you're tired of spending countless hours in EC auctioning off that 300pp item, then you need to start posting here and leave it be - so you can spend more time reaping what you pay your $10 for - to play the game!

In any event - here's are the links to the KCoG forums

Main Message Board

If you want to take it a step further - have your EZBoard moderators (if your guild is using EZBoard) add a link to their web ring drop down box to link to it.

Thanks and safe journies to all.
