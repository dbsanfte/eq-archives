The Len Cam is no more... Posted Thursday, January 18, 2001 by Lenenae McGillicuddy
Great news everyone I had to take down the web cam, so you can no longer peer into the inner workings of that power house of web gaming EA.com QA.

I'm sorry, but images of me picking my nose and scratching my bee-hind are no longer yours to oogle over. Apparently Electronic Arts IT department just couldn't fathom the reason for the 6+ web cams around our office area. EA of course said there was some policy against them, but we couldn't find anything which probably means there will be something at the next update.

Tune in next week when I shall be broadcasting my cam image from my home system. Plan is to post the cam image on an internal company page just for giggles.

-Len/AOB
