Welcome...

Our Pledge:

We of the Emerald Axe are a group of adventurers of different creeds. We have bound together under a pact of trust to journey Norrath in search of fortune and glory. We have heard many fabulous tales of danger and riches, and each promise of adventure burns like a fire in our souls. To realize these dreams, we must depend on others, and trust in others. Thus, the Clan of the Emerald Axe was founded.
What was initially a chance meeting of old friends on a lonely road, has become a close-knit group of brethren bound to defend each other. Though our members come from different creeds and beliefs, we are of the same mind. There are those among us that seek to help the oppressed, and there are those that seek to exploit them. Our viewpoints and personal beliefs are each our own, but being guild members, we ally ourselves to each other at all costs.
Our pledge is to honor each other's values and uphold the trust of the union. We hold regular meetings to determine our courses of action, based on current events. We do not accept applications for membership, but rather invite those that we trust.

Our Goals:

  • To adventure as a team; to journey to even the most remote areas
  • of Norrath.
  • To provide a role playing environment for our members.
  • To build a sense of community amongst our members.
  • To travel the length and breadth of Norrath.
  • To forge a kinship between all races.

The Clan of the Emerald Axe adhears to the following guildlines...

  • Do uphold your own personal beliefs to the best of your abilities.
  • Do not mock other members beliefs.
  • Do come to those in need.
  • Do offer old items to the guild before auctioning.
    (Sacrificing items for the benefit of the guild is usually repaid ten fold.)
  • Do drink lots of Ale.
  • Do "Stand Up", tell stories, and Roleplay often.
  • Do not Hail Lady Vox, unless you worship Veeshan, in which case you probably know what you are doing.
  • Do not yell, "Praise Karana!" everytime it starts raining, we got it the first time.

Suggestions for this site should be sent to the Clan EMail.

-Lenenae McGillicuddy - Priestess of Marr.
