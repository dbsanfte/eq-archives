Patches! Stackable Pelts, Spell and Sullon Zek Adjustments! Posted Tuesday, July 24, 2001 by Lenenae McGillicuddy

Party!
--
July 24th, 3:00 am
------------------------------


** Item Changes **

- Silver Chitin Hand Wraps' haste effect has been repaired.
- The Smite scroll no longer lists Enchanters as a class that can use it.
- The Augment Death scroll no longer lists Shadowknights as a class that can use it.
- Many pelts have been made stackable. Those that are not stackable now are quest items that will not be made stackable.


** Spell Changes **

- Focus of Spirit will now stack with Voice of the Berserker.
- Barrier of Force will now stack with Protection of the Glades.
- Manaskin and Protection of the Glades will now stack.


** Trade Skill Changes **

- Repaired some class/deity combinations on two of the deity specific pottery Idols.


** Changes to Sullon Zek **

- There are no faction hits for killing half-elves in any city.
- If an Erudite of Paineel is killed in Paineel the killer takes a faction hit (existing rule).
- If an Erudite of Erudin is killed in Erudin the killer takes a faction hit (new rule).
- Bards can't cast harmful spells on boats.
- If a character kills a higher level character, the higher level character will always lose experience. This is in addition to the fact that any player killed within the + or - 5 level range will lose experience.
- Goods and Neutrals will now be able to turn in Greater Insignias for a Magician only summon corpse potion usable only at levels over 45th.
- Characters under level 6 will no longer be immune to PvP in many adventure zones. Players will be notified when they enter such zones.


** Misc. Changes **

- /target playername will now work on invisible targets as long as the character doing the targeting has the ability to see invisible.

KGC: Shattering Steel Guild Posted Tuesday, July 10, 2001 by Lenenae McGillicuddy
Apparently the Karana Guild Council (the group that organizes all the planes and dragon raids) is having a problem with the guild Shattering Steel. SS has basically gone against the KGC rotation and is camping the planes and other major areas out of turn.

KGC has asked all guilds that belong to the KGC to boycott this guild until they start rotating like everyone else. This means no grouping, no buffing, no reses, no selling/buying, and any other no you can think of.

I'm not fond of boycotting an entire group for the actions of part of the group, but it comes down to this, the KGC was setup so that everyone gets an equal and fair shake at the major mobs, at least amongst the groups that can take those mobs. It gives an alliance like the Karana Council of Guilds a chance to exist and go to the planes where on other servers you either have to compete with other larger guilds, or you just don't go.

Given the shear number of guilds behind the KGC I'm guessing that it won't be long before SS gives in and rotates nicely with everyone else. In the meantime I'd like to hear some comments in the forums, unfortunatly the forums for some reason are down right now, but what can ya do, right?

-Len
