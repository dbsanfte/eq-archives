With the release of Velious, four lines of armour became available to each class. This page will focus on the quest armor involving Coldain Faction. Thurgadin is the starting city for the quests and the armour component can be found in Kael Drakkel in the arena area. Armour seems to drop from the Veterans, Adjudants and Legionnaires which are level 53+. Each quest requires 3 gem components plus one armour component. All items for these quests are dropped, however gems may also be purchased. Gems are dropped in Dragon Necroplois, Siren's Grotto, Velketor's Labyrinth, Wakening Lands, and Icewell Keep.
