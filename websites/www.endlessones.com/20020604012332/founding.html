The Endless Ones abide by the following founding principles

  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • The Council was established to provide guidance in guild matters. It consists of 7 members including the Guild Leader. Each member has an equal vote.
  • All founding members have officer status. Future officers shall be chosen based on criteria established by the Council
  • Invitations to join the Endless Ones require the recommendation of a current member.
  • Endless Ones members will not intentionally kill-steal.
  • Endless Ones will try to the best of their abilities to help others,within the guild and without.
  • Endless ones will refrain from immature behavior that reflects poorly on the guild as a whole.
  • Members will try at all times to provide a positive role model to other members
  • Guild events will be planned with the purpose of the guild in mind: to explore, learn and group with other Endless Members.
