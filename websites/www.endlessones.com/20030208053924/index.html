What have we done lately
Updated 1/27/03
Huge Congratz to Faylon and Qwab! On 1/20, a small group went down to Kedge Keep with Faylon to kill the corrupted seahorse..He finally spawned, and though we had a wipe out but wecame back full force and got our revenge!! We stayed a little bit longer and we were able to get another corrupted for Qwab!! With the defeat of this seahorse Faylon now only have 1 mob left to kill.

There was a HUGE EO turn-out to help Ventrahl slay Sir Lucan for his Soulfire. Special thanks go to Ted/Cyvir (for keeping Ventrahl sane), Zukul (for helping Vent when he got a little overwhelmed and tanking the SoB), Telishna (just for being cool like that), Cheal (for keeping us ALIVE), Hodag (for filling the void left by Jagerr's constants LD's [3!], and the MANY others than made this a quick, but very memorable experience. With Lucan's testimony in hand, it was only a short time until Ventrahl had his Soulfire. Now if only he could find a set of gloves that wouldn't make the weapon so painful to wield... 		We had 3 full groups in Kael on Saturday and got some real nice drops. Congratz to Mortican and Qwub on their new torn silk boots. Klowwin got the corroded plate boots. Endrick got his first ever raid loot of torn silk gloves. Miracles of miracles 3, that's right, 3 sets of corroded chain gaunts dropped and NO ONE needed them. They were looted for MQ later on. McHeldon is sporting a new velium war mask.

It was Qwub's day, he also won the random on the Wristband of Strategy . Tisanna has new Gladiator Chain sleeves and Darei has the matching legs. Lineman even managed a ding of 49 in between all the drops.

Other notables? Well Tanistormer started his ranger epic (yes there is one). Lelaina got her POTC spell, while Shanon picked up some Elisyian armor and SOE. And finally, Jagerr completed the 5th Coldain Ring quest and received his Obsidian Coldain Insignia Ring.

Special Thanks to Phoku for keeping our members roster up to date and to Pickels for the additional roster generator that can be seen on our Ezboard.

What a great weekend!
