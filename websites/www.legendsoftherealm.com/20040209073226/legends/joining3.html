Membership into Legends of the Realm

 Thank you for your interest in Legends of the Realm. We are a very old guild on the Innoruuk server and I encourage you to click on the History link to the left and read a bit about our guild. We have long-standing traditions as a guild and high expectations of all our members.

The application process can be lengthy and will require commitment and dedication on your part. You may be given a temporary guild invite to facilitate the process. This does not mean you are a Full Member of the guild, it just means you get to read our guild chat. While wearing our guild tag, you are expected to behave as a Legend and to follow the directions of guild officers. If you are found to act in a dishonorable fashion you will be removed and not allowed to reapply.

Requirements to apply:

  • Level 65 with the character that will be your Main Legends' character
  • Minimum of 18 AA spent
  • You should have the following PoP Flags:
    1. Plane of Justice Trial
    2. Plane of Storms Medals Quest for Bastion of Thunder access
    3. Agnarr in Bastion of Thunder completed
    4. Plane of Nightmare Hedge
    5. Terris Thule
    6. Saryn/Keeper of Sorrow (may be waived)
    7. Aerin'Dar in Plane of Valor
    8. The 3 Trials from the Halls of Honor
    9. Grummus
    10. Carpin
    11. Bertox (may be waived)
    12. Mantetic Behemoth from Plane of Innovation
    13. Tallon Zek in Tactics
    14. Vallon Zek in Tactics
  • You need all the EQ Expansions (may waive LoY/LDoN on a case-by-case basis)
  • Unguilded and no other characters in a VT/PoP-level guild



    How the process will work:

    Once you have posted your intent to join, you need to attend raids so we can find out if you’re right for the guild. After you have attended several raids, the officers will decide whether or not you shall continue with the application process and become an official prospect. There is no set duration for this process. We may decide someone doesn’t mesh with the guild after a couple raids, or they are exactly what we are looking for. In general, your availability will determine the speed with which you get through this step. If you raid every day, it will probably only take a couple weeks. If you’re on only once a week, it may take a couple months.

    • Applicants should NOT expect to attend any Mith Marr (although, if you can zone into HoHb w/o needing a Raid to get you in, then you may join) or Rallos Zek flaggings (key is "expect", not that you will never be allowed to attend).

    If you pass the first step and become an official prospect you will be granted conditional membership into the guild. When the officer's determine you have been attending enough raids that the guild membership has had time to evaluate your potential as a Legend, a poll will be put up and our members will decide whether or not you become a full member. It behooves you to be as available as possible the weeks following your conditional guildinvite. Don’t accept the tag the day before you go on vacation for two weeks…

    Conditional Membership means--You are now a Prospect

    • You wear the guild tag and are expected to uphold the traditions and code of Legends. Your behavior is a direct reflection upon the guild.
    • You are requested to participate in as many raids as possible. When not raiding, grouping with other Legends members is highly encouraged.
    • You will not be granted access to the private sections of the Message Boards.
    • You should not expect any loot that drops on a raid unless it will decay.
    • You should not request guild assistance to complete any quests that require more than a single group.
Full Membership.
A poll will be put up for you when the officers think the guild has had enough time to evaluate your potential. At that time, the general membership will vote on your acceptance as a full member. When accepted, access to all forums will be granted and your name will be added to loot lists and you will be eligible for all flagging raids.

If you do not pass the membership vote, you may reapply no earlier than 3 months.
