Sept 25th

After taking a week off to explore and experience with the release of Omens, we returned to action and it's been a good week for Legends, we did a bit of backflagging and killed a couple Planar mobs and worked on some epic 1.5 stuff and still have the weekend to go.

RDD and LMM were the first to fall!!

Gratz toMoshady who scored an Elven Bow of Mysticism and Thorveld grabbed an Ivory Etched Aegis  both from RDD and to Denari who was awarded "the precious" Blade of the Tempest  and Chantarin pulled his pants up with a Valiant Belt of Fallen Heroes  from LMM.

Then we visited Bertox and gained a Symbol of the Plaguebringer for both Sumamdar and Momento!

 


After a couple nights off we jumped back into action with a warm up in Sol Ro against the Fire Chicken and Angryor recieved Hooded Sanguine Mantle !!  The raid then headed for Plane of Earth to pay a visit to Peregrin Rockskull, who gave Walknot some haste with a Flowing Moss Mantle  and another pali who couldn't hold his pants up got a Girdle of Earthen Stability, gratz to Simani.  For a nightcap we eased over to Momma Crocs place where she warmly greeted us and gave Blackfogg a Manacle of Disruption.

Stay tuned for more updates soon.
