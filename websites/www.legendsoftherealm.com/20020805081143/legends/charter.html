About Legends of the Realm
 
The primary goal of this guild is for mutual benefit, enjoyment, and protection of all it's members. We are looking for people that like to go on expeditions or hunting excursions and enjoy splitting loot in a fair and equitable manner. Our primary mission will be to assist others, especially newbies. This assistance can be in the form of goods, services, or just friendly advice. Role-playing is highly encouraged, but not a requirement.

There will be no guild fees or dues. - This guild is growing and will be open for suggestions and ideas from it's members.

Code is the Code of Conduct by which all members of the guild agree to uphold and to abide by while playing Everquest.

As the guild grows, so will this guild site.
