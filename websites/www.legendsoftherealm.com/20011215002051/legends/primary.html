Welcome to Legends of the Realm!
We are a guild on the Everquest Server of Innoruuk and the Percival server (Hibernian Realm)  of Dark Age of Camelot. One of the earliest guilds created on Innoruuk that is still thriving. Legends was created on Innoruuk in late April/early May of 1999. You are welcome to visit with us on our message boards.  (EQ Officers -- DAoC Officers)
