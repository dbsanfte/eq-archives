Legends are made not born...

Welcome to Legends of the Realm!
We are a guild on the Everquest Server of Innoruuk. One of the earliest guilds created on Innoruuk that is still thriving. Legends was created in late April/early May of 1999. You are welcome to visit with us on our message board.

March 27th, 2001
Password changed to all the Member's Forums.  To get the new password, you must be a Full Member AND you must have your characters listed at GuildMagic.com.  Characters that are not registered at GuildMagic on the Legends Roster may be removed from the guild until they become registered.  Please ensure you register your characters as soon as possible.  It would also be good if you designate a main and the rest as alternates (if you have more than one character).
