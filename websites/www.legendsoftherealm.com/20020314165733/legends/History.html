History of Legends of the Realm by Rosha Cristi, founder
 Legends of the Realm (LOTR) was actually born in March of 1998 on the Atlantic Shard in a game called Ultima Online.  The guild was fairly small compared to other UO guilds.  When EverQuest was released, I ended up shelving UO in favor of EQ.  I met a few people on Innoruuk and we ended up playing together all the time.  I thought it would be good to have our own chat channel to make it easier to keep in touch and find each other.  The core members who helped form this guild were a trio of Elven Rangers named Talyn, Moshady, and Ropriest, a stout Dwarven Cleric by the name of Bandolaf, an Elven Wizard named Celestial, a Halfling Warrior named Tarro, an Ogre Warrior named Thulaak, and a Elven Enchanter named Sakuma.  We joined and became the guild Legends in late April or early May of 1999.  I do not recall the actual date.  When the guild was formed, the name for the guild tag was shortened from Legends of the Realm to simply Legends.  We didn't want a guild name that took up half of someone's screen.

The guild had it's ups and downs.  Many people joined the guild and moved on the bigger and better things.  Some members went on to form their own guilds.  Some of which were far more successful and other guilds that have simply gone by the wayside.  There were people who left for other, bigger guilds that offered them faster advancement than what Legends was able to provide at the time.  Then there were those who stuck with the guild through it all.  Those people who believed in the guild but more importantly, believed in the people in the guild.  Legends was always about friends and having fun.  About the person playing the character, not the character being played.  Legends on EQ has finally begun making it to the higher level things and has become quite successful lately.  I think the strength of this guild will always be the people in the guild.  People who care about each other and are able to look past the game aspects and not get bogged down in loot and leveling.

Two new chapters in the guild's history are now being written.  I have stepped down as the leader of the Legends on Innoruuk.  I feel I have led the guild as far as I am capable.  The guild now needs a leader who understands the mechanics of the high-level game.  A leader who can run raids for the high level encounters.   Time will tell what will unfold in this new era of Legends.

The other chapter being written is about Legends in Camelot.  Some of those from EverQuest have begun playing Dark Age of Camelot and a Legends chapter has been started on the Percival server in the Hibernia Realm.

And so a new era begins on EverQuest and a new chapter is being written on Dark Age of Camelot.  The story continues to unfold...
