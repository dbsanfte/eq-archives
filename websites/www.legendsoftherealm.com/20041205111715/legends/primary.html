Welcome to Legends of the Realm!
We are a guild on the Everquest Server of Innoruuk.  One of the first guilds created on Innoruuk and still thriving. Legends was created on Innoruuk in late April/early May of 1999. You are welcome to visit with us on our message boards.  (Officers) 

NEWS

Nov 20th

As many of you have noticed, Legends has experienced a large hit with the recent release of new games and a number of real life situations that has come up for many of our members.  Never fear though, we will continue on as always. 
 
We are going to be opening recruitment to all classes and making some changes to our application requirements in the very near future.  We still have every intention of seeing all content the world of Everquest has to offer including Plane of Time, Gates of Discord, and Omens of War.
 
For those of you who are interested in joining legends, please follow our website to the joining forum and post an application, then find one of the officers in game.  Currently, the officers are Gerweena, Kaziel, Stormin, Wyllo and Veya (guild leader).  You can speak with any of them for more info on the application process.
 
To those of you who have moved on from the world of Norrath, we wish you well and that you stay safe in all you do, be it other games or real life.  Stay in touch and don't be a stranger.

Oct 10th

Legends has been very busy, we've roamed thru earth, air, and fire lately laying waste to all we could find.  Enjoy the screenshots and congratulations to all on their recent item upgrades.
