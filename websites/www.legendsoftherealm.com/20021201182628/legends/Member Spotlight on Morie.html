Spotlight 
as written by Rosha Cristi

Morie...the player of many faces!  She has so many characters that it is hard to keep track of who is who!  I first met Morie when she was playing her first character on Innoruuk.  It was a bard named Songweaver.  We had some great adventures together...right up until the point I convinced her (and some others) that we could handle RunnyEye.  You must remember, this was when EQ was new and the highest level players were level 12!  Anyway, she (well, Songweaver was a he) died in a very inaccessible spot for our group.  Thus ended the short life span of that bard.  She then started a Human Monk (at the time, only Humans could be Monks--there was no Kunark) who went by the name of Luikang. 

Morie has played almost every class (that I know about).  Let me see if I can name some of her many characters of a significant level (I know I am going to miss a few and mispell some names)

  • Songweaver--Bard
  • Luikang--Monk
  • Morigaine--Enchanter
  • Morie--Druid
  • Morissa--Bard
  • Morisan--Rogue
  • Nazcas--Shaman
  • ??--Iksar Monk
  • Morlyssa--Enchanter (I think)

Morie has been with Legends from the very beginning.  There was a short stint where she left for another guild along with some other of the higher-levels.  But, she returned soon thereafter and has remained a very active and productive member of the guild.

Why did I put her in the Spotlight?  Because Morie does so much for the members of this guild.  She will help players camp items and most importantly, helps other players earn experience.  She frequently sets up camps for players with the sole purpose of earning as much experience as possible.  Morie is always ready to help another guildmember and always ready to help the guild on a raid.
