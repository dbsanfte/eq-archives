Membership into Legends of the Realm

Welcome! I hope you are here because you have had some positive contact with some members of Legends of the Realm and you liked our play style. We do not actively seek to recruit new members; we would rather have prospective members seek us out. If you have not met or know any of the members of Legends, I encourage you to venture out and try to meet some before seeking membership. All you need to do is type "/w all legend" and you will get a good idea who is on and where they are in the world.

Joining Legends of the Realm isn’t that hard, however we want to know you well before you enter our ranks. There are a few steps that are involved.  

Requirements

Legends is currently not accepting applicants of the following classes:

Druid
Enchanter
Rogue
Shaman
Shadowknight
Warrior
  • First and foremost ... you need to get to know members of this guild.  Group with as many as you can as often as you can.  If you are the type of player that would fit into the guild, I am confident that members will seek you out to join on raids and regular experience groups.
  • We do have a minimum level requirement.  You need to be at least level 63 with one character before seeking entry into the guild.   If this seems too distant a goal for you and you feel like you don’t want to wait that long, this likely isn’t the guild for you. Patience is a prime attribute for any successful prospect. Legends’ progress is slow and steady, often measured in geologic time. Likewise, if seeing end-game content, or getting some uber item now is a priority to you, Legends wont be a good fit. We will see it all, eventually, but we’re in no particular hurry.
  • We no longer require a single person to sponsor you for membership, but you need to let it be known you seek membership.
  • You must have all the EverQuest Expansions (Kunark, Velious, Luclin, and Planes of Power).  Legends hunts all over Norrath.

How does the process work?

Once you have posted your intent to join, you need to attend raids so we can find out if you’re right for the guild. After you have attended several raids, the officers will decide whether or not you shall continue with the application process and become an official prospect. This will be accomplished by an informal poll of our members. There is no set duration for this process. We may decide someone doesn’t mesh with the guild after a couple raids, or they are exactly what we are looking for. In general, your availability will determine the speed with which you get through this step. If you raid every day, it will probably only take a couple weeks. If you’re on only once a week, it may take a couple months.

If you pass the first step and become an official prospect you will be granted conditional membership into the guild. After no less than two weeks a poll will be put up and our members will decide whether or not you become a full member. It behooves you to be as available as possible the weeks following your conditional guildinvite. Don’t accept the tag the day before you go on vacation for two weeks…


Conditional Membership means--
  • You wear the guild tag and are expected to uphold the traditions and code of Legends. Your behavior is a direct reflection upon the guild.
  • You are requested to participate in as many raids as possible. When not raiding, grouping with other Legends members is highly encouraged.
  • You will not be granted access to the private sections of the Message Boards.
  • You should not expect any loot that drops on a raid unless it will decay.
  • You should not request guild assistance to complete any quests that require more than a single group.

Full Membership.
After at least 2-weeks of wearing the Legends tag, a poll will be put up for you. At that time, the general membership will vote on your acceptance as a full member. When accepted, access to all forums will be granted and your name will be added to loot lists.

What happens if I don't get the required number of votes for admittance?

Your poll will be removed.  You may apply again after a minimum of one month.  The process will then be restarted.  Two weeks to be accepted once the poll is put up by the guild officers.
