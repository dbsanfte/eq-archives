Welcome to Legends of the Realm!
We are a guild on the Everquest Server of Innoruuk.  One of the first guilds created on Innoruuk and still thriving. Legends was created on Innoruuk in late April/early May of 1999. You are welcome to visit with us on our message boards.  (EQ Officers)
