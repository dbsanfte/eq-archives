Welcome to Legends of the Realm!
We are a guild on the Everquest Server of Innoruuk. One of the earliest guilds created on Innoruuk that is still thriving. Legends was created on Innoruuk in late April/early May of 1999. You are welcome to visit with us on our message boards.  (EQ Officers) 

Legends has changed how we recruit and accept members into the guild. Our previous method served its purpose in the past but we have decided to shorten the process. Legends is also accepting new membership applications at this time.
