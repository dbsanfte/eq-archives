Membership into Legends of the Realm

 

Welcome! I hope you are here because you have had some positive contact with some members of Legends of the Realm and you liked our play style. We do not actively seek to recruit new members; we would rather have prospective members seek us out. If you have not met or know any of the members of Legends, I encourage you to venture out and try to meet some before seeking membership. All you need to do is type "/w all legend" and you will get a good idea who is on and where they are in the world.

Joining Legends of the Realm isn’t that hard, however we want to know you well before you enter our ranks. There are a few steps that are involved.  

 

 1. Read over our Code of Conduct and what our guild is about, why we were formed and what we try to accomplish.
 2.  Post a message to our discussion board stating your name, race, level, that you want to join Legends and why you would like to join.
 3.  This step is the longest one, but the most fun. Seek Legends members out and party with them! This allows us to get to know you, and for you to know us. BE WARNED! This is not a one or 2 time thing, it will require you to group with us numerous time and for us to see that you uphold the beliefs and standards of our Code. This can be done in a couple of weeks or a couple of months, it depends on how often you are able to group with our members. This will also allow you to continue to evaluate us to see if we are the right guild for you.
 4.  Once you have gotten to know us, one of the members will undoubtedly sponsor you. You must have a sponsor who is willing to vouch for your character. We will discuss your membership on our board and if/when you have enough favorable contact with our members then you will be initiated into our ranks.

Membership Requirements:

    1. All members will strive to keep their character information up to date.
    2. All members will provide a valid email address to the Legends members list and be subscribed.
    3. All members are encouraged to read the Guild messageboard.
    4. There are NO guild dues.

The following are the new requirements for admission into this guild. Please pass the word amongst all members. This system WILL REQUIRE active participation from ALL guild members.

  1. A person seeking membership in the guild must first have a current, unconditional member as his/her sponsor.
  2. The SPONSOR informs the Guild Recruit Officer (Yorke) that he/she wishes to sponsor a person.  Yorke will create a poll when the time is right.
  3. The person must acquire 15 Yes votes to be admitted As a Conditional Member, more on this later
  4. Once the conditional member receives 25 Yes vote, he/she is now a regular member. They must receive these 25 votes within one month or they will be removed from the guild


Sponsor Responsibilities
If you sponsor an individual for membership in Legends, it is YOUR responsibility to make it known to the guild and the guild recruit officer. You should post about this person in the Prospect Discussion are of the message board.  Subject should be the character name.  You need to state why you support the person.

Guild Member Responsibilities
It is each guild members duty to vote and especially make comments to better inform your brothers and sisters of important information. If you have a reason this person should NOT be admitted, you really need to let US know instead of holding back. If you wish to remain anon, you can email the officers or just myself with your reasons.
If you voted No Opinion
Please try to avoid voting No Opinion since you cannot change your vote in the poll. Simply don't vote until you have an opinion. If you did vote No Opinion, once you do HAVE an opinion, you will need to post a reply stating you wish to change your vote to a Yes/No since once voted you cannot "revote"

Conditional Membership
Conditional members are NOT granted access to the private sections of this message board. Conditional Members will NOT earn any points towards the Legends Loot System. Conditional members must seek out and get the approval of 10 more guild members to be admitted as a regular member. They have one month to be moved from unconditional to regular member status or else they are asked to reapply and they are removed from the guild. It is the responsibility of the conditional member to seek his/her additional votes to be accepted as a regular member.

Members are encouraged to post replies in the Poll Message to indicate their opinions. Before a member is given full access to the member boards, this poll must have 25 yes votes.
