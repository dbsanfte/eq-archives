Introduction

Welcome to YaBB! YaBB, Yet Another Bulletin Board, is an open-source, easily-accessible bulletin board for everyone and anything. The version you are seeing now is the our newest release, YaBB 1 Gold - Service Pack 1.

The Basics

One of the ideas behind YaBB is to make a bulletin board that is not only free, but good! We do hope what you see before you is just that, and to make sure your experience is even more enjoyable we have created these help files to assist you wherever and whenever you may need assistance.

If this is your first time installing your new board, or if you're upgrading to a newer version, click the Installation link to your left. Otherwise, check out the Users, Moderation, and Administration sections also to your left. Enjoy! =)
