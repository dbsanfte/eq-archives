Our first Guild War was against the good guild "The council of Silver Griffons". It all started in the Netherbian Lair on the Moons of Luclin. 
 
The leader of the Griffons Lord Gavin challenged Matron Mother Edea to a duel. She accepted gladly for she wishes to seek and destroy all that is good. He had clerics healing him on the side and Edea called him a coward and cheater and to bring it on without help. 
 
One on one. 
 
He then bowed to her and apologized but then the War begun as we declared guild war!! He was then slain...
 
Members of the Griffons and Members of DA then came to their leader's aid and did team slaughter 4 DA vs 5 COSG. Our team having Edea the Wizard, Reynn the Magician, Syni the Shadow Knight, and Uandar the Magician. 
 
Their team was composed of Gavin the Paladin, Kegan the Cleric, Kadigan the Druid, Graison the Magician and Macel the Monk.

 Dark Affliction won again and again and again. 
 
Would have been a flawless victory for us had they not all ganged one of our mages, but it was a victory none the less for us. One that the Great Innoruuk and Lord Cazic Thule  would have been proud. 
 
The war was over when Lord Gavin surrendered to Matron Mother Edea in the East Commons, just outside Gavin's home city of Freeport. They are allies of 1st Fist of Light, our sworn enemies but they have been too chicken to fight us even though they have approximately the same levels and numbers as us.

I have respect for Gavin and the Griffons for they had the Huevos to challenge us, and even though they proved to be a trivial challenge to us, they had courage and have earned our respect. 
 
Perhaps when they grow in numbers and seasons, we shall have at it again for everyone deserves a second chance....
