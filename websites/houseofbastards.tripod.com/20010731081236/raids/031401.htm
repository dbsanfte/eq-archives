CASTLE MISTMORE
Mar. 14, 2001


RAIDING PARTIES
Calaran - magician (29), Faan - cleric (28), Galadrial - druid (31), Garall - paladin (29), Lanorolen - bard (27), Luppis - druid (30), Purgatori - ranger (24), Shumirek - bard (26), Wildride - paladin (28), Ziff - enchanter (27), Zoritsa - enchanter (29)

RECOUNT
by Ziff

Mistmoore castle has a reputation among the adventurers of Norrath. Its called the butcher grounds. This castle is so well protected that few ever breach its portcullis and of those that do almost none survive. HoB was going to take that challenge. They had been there before with the Hawk Lords. That was a scene of chaos, carnage and confusion. Now they were back to assault the castle HoB style; with cooperation, coordination and cunning.

And so as a single unit, HoB began its assault. Within minutes the flow for the evening was established. Only 100 yards through the grounds Garall turned the corner with 10 or more Mistmoore residents in tow. Before a succinct rally could be formed several members were already dead. The decision was made to flee in order to preserve those that still lived.

After a brief reorganization HoB struck again and the pendulum swung back in HoBs favor. This time they reached the graveyard and were mounting an assault on the area known as the pit when the pendulum swung back again. This time HoB stood its ground and would not submit to the assault. They emerged victorious but at the loss of two lives.

After reorganizing and reviving they pushed into the pit and forced the pendulum back in their favor. Soon they held the pit and were moving up the dangerous and steep path to the castle gates. At the top they gathered and surveyed the enemy. Only two could be seen and so, after a brief discussion, an arrow flew and the fight was on. Immediately HoB could feel the pendulum swing back against them. With horns blaring the castle guards charged. Those horns brought a host of enemies. The pendulum swung back and smashed HoB with deadly fury. Before retreat could be called for one third of hob had been slaughtered. Another third were cut down as they attempted to retreat, either falling victim to claw and swords or falling to their death as they attempted to flee down the steep and treacherous path.

It was time for HoB to push back. Gathering two Dusk Eternal members of higher experience into their ranks they prepared to give as good as they just got.

Again they carved a bloody path to the castle doors, even stopping to kill Entryti. They hit a few stumbling blocks along the way but by the time they HoB again arrived at the castle gates they were a solid unit; two full teams acting as one.

With glorious precision the guards at the gate were obliterated along with any assistance they brought. It was HoBs turn now.

Garall dove into the castle proper and retrieved wave after wave of Mistmoore denizens. Each wave crashed and broke on the HoB offensive. The pendulum was now bearing its full weight on Mistmoore's inhabitants.

HoB should have been wise at this point. Fate had already fed HoB two cruel lessons but apparently that was not enough. What happened next will be etched in HoB's mind for a long time. Garall emerged through the castle doors with a small host in tow. Right behind them was another collection of evil. Together these would pose a serious challenge to the group. Ziff could feel the pendulum coming back with relentless viciousness. As if on cue more than ten additional denizens burst from the castle. HoB now faced the largest enemy force they had ever fought at once, and each one of them was focused on killing Garall. Ziff shouted "RUN!" as soon as he saw the enemy force. Garall took one more step and was incinerated. HoB turned to flee and before hardly a step could be taken almost half of the raiding party was annihilated.

Again little more than a third of the group lived. The hour was getting late and the group decided one more ride on the pendulum of fate was more than they felt like risking. They resurrected their dead, collected their loot, left the butcher grounds behind and headed off to Feronia Vie to nurse their wounds and find new enemies.
