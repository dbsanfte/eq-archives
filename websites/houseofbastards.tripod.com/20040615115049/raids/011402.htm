TOWER OF FROZEN SHADOW
Jan. 14, 2002


HOB LEVEL: 30-36

RAIDING PARTIES
Alendar - rogue (27), Bria - cleric (29), Byttin - druid (36), Illbe - shaman (30), Luppis - druid (35), Mirloe - cleric (35), Mulak - monk (31), Niclaus - paladin (35), Sque - druid (35), Wildride - paladin (34), Ziff - enchanter (31)


RECOUNT
by Wildride

Just off in the distance, beyond the modest gnoll huts and faintly visible through the snow and fog rolling in from the Iceclad Ocean, loomed the Tower of Frozen Shadow. This edifice, forged from the frozen souls of those who have been brought there against their wills and perished in its bowels, was to be challenged by a venerable yet rag-tag band of fatherless defenders of those less fortunate; the House of Bastards! Assembled in the lowest chamber of the tower, 11 of Dusk Eternal’s finest readied their blades, studied their tomes and polished their armor for the upcoming climb up the tower’s inner chambers.

Mulak, ever the uniquely restless monk, began to taunt the guardians from their nearby chambers while we defended ourselves and sent each to its final resting place. Many were surprised by the rapid pulls since we had not even formed the proper teams to coordinate our attacks, yet the power and experience of the member were overwhelming for the equally surprised ice shades and their pets. After a moment’s reprise, Ziff quickly organized the teams and the bloodletting would begin again in full force.

Having been to the second floor of the tower in the past, HoB knew that a key would be required to gain entry to the library. The key from the previous ascents was held by a hapless Cleric that found himself stranded remotely on the moon of Luclin and incapable of joining the group on this evening. Lacking the key HoB let loose the hounds of hell ripping apart all that remained in the lower chamber until finally the key was discovered on a cowering puppy.

All ascended to the second floor where we displayed the highly organized and efficient tactics that have won the HoB renown the world over. Our training came from the urine stained caves of blackburrow, the slime coated frog haven of Guk and the vast maze of tunnels in infected paw. The disdain for those ghostly souls that sit safely in the confine of a library showed when HoB quickly took out each an every teacher, scholar, student and librarian. Many useful items were found on the hacked corpses and several members of the group found that they could put each to better use that its previous owner.

Mulak announced the charge across the library where we would “close the book” on level 2 and ascend to the next level of the tower. This floor seemed to be the quarters of the servants of those that dwelt far above. Cooks, maids, butlers all were expediently dismissed by the now fearsome fighting machine HoB. As we moved through he floor more servants were discovered and their were quickly downsized.

The evening was getting late and we were about to head out of the tower when a mysterious force whisked our leader Ziff away. Being in a treacherous location to return to without friends, HoB stood back to back and repelled the hoards of servants that were apparently coming to work the 3rd shift. When hope was almost lost of the return of their leader, Ziff was returned unharmed. A hasty exit was made before the force returned to claim another.

There are more floors that must be claimed by HoB but I fear that the destruction of the library and the dramatic reduction in the hired help will prove to anger those that dwell above. We shall see.

Long live the House of Bastards.
Long live Dusk Eternal.

Your faithful knight,
Wildride
