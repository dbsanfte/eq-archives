GROUP 2
Guadzilla - shaman (19), Lanorolen - bard (17), Nekko - shadow night (19/20), Purgatori - ranger (23), Tajo - wizard (22), Ziff - enchanter (23)

RECOUNT
by Ziff

The lower level party did had a rough start but a solid finish. The raid for the lower
levels was more about coordinating everyone and beginning to lay down some basic group
tactics and strategies. For the first hour or so things were4 pretty rough as people
were getting used to some new tactics and processes. Things got extremely tricky when
a frog, wounded and scared went screaming for the ladder. Soon, disorganized and
disoriented, group 2 was overwhelmed.

Seeing the trouble, Ziff called for a run. Many members made it to the exist alive, but
two got lost in the depths of Guk and were hacked down by Frogs.

Zoft arrived, dragged the bodies to the zone and revived them. The group reorganized
again and headed back in.

Soon, Purgatori joined the group and the slaughter began anew.

At one point our puller, Nekko, got rooted to the floor deep in Guk. Lacking the ability
to move or defend himself he was overwhelmed. The rest of the group ran to save him, but
to no avail. What proceeded was a pitched battle. Group 2, lacking one of 2 tanks, began
to lay into 9 frogs. This was a shining moment. Wounded, disoriented and lacking their
primary assist person group 2 pulled together and took out every Frog in the room. When
done, we were all low on health and OOM, but we were victorious. Proof that many
beginning lessons had been learned.

Soon Nekko returned with a small train in tow. Group 2 was still weak, but we rose to
the occasion. We were still disorganized for a while and that proved to nearly be our
downfall, but in the end we were victorious.

Then night was a great success for what it was intended for. We looted 2 collars of
undead protection and 15 plat. We all received about 1 bubble of experience. So, in
those regards, the night was a bust. But much was learned. The group who entered Guk was
not the group that left. The group that left had learned a great deal and had formed a
foundation for their growth as a group and use of team tactics.

Special mention goes to Guadzilla and Nekko. Guadzilla did an excellent job of healing
the party and keeping everyone up to date on mana status. Since this was the first time
he has had to fill that role, this was especially impressive. Nekko did a great job of
pulling and selecting targets for his first time with the group and in Guk. Great
promise is shown there my friend.
