RECOUNT
by Ziff

Some nights go better than others. Last night HoB met some new milestones and learned some valuable lessons. But, as far as experience and loot go, the raid was less than successful.

Bad News:
Numerous deaths. So many in fact that I am unsure of who all died and so I wont try to list them. But we mourn every loss.

Loot was poor. No magical treasure and the plat is still being totaled but not expected to break 150.

Poor organization and cooperation led to confusion, increased risk and less fun for everyone.

Most of the night was spent either fighting greens, running, zoning, or preparing for the next fight. Aside from greens we killed less than 20 mobs all night.

The elusive Nightfall Giant never showed.

Good News:
This was the biggest turn out in HoB history. 16 members showed. Due to message board problems and the openness of the fighting grounds it was decided not to enforce the 12 player limit.

Hob took out its first Hill Giant. Through the night 5 of them fell to our might and although there were several tense moments no HoB member fell to a giant all night.

Massive revenge was had on Kizdin Gix. The paladins killed him at least 3 times through the night resting revenge for the numerous deaths we all suffered at his hands in our
youth.

Numerous spider silks and pelts were obtained to help Lan with his tailoring. This included a high quality bear skin which has been made into a 10 slot backpack. This and the archeologists pack Ziff recently bought will be given to primary looters on raid night to make looting a little easier.

What We Learned:
We need to stick to groups of 12 at the most. More than that creates too much confusion. Maybe later as we get better with 2 full groups we will be able to expand, but for now we need to focus on teamwork with 2 groups.

Kithicor is a bit too ugly for HoB. Lately HoB has been pushing boundaries, going places that others dare not tread. Although this is a common routine for us, we have been pushing a little to hard as of late. As a result, experience, treasure and fun go down. So, for a while we will be sticking to some lower level, "safer" places. (Although still semi insane by many peoples standards) Please note though. There are very few places to hunt in our current level range that are not overcrowded. When HoB gains a few levels there are tons of places to go, but for a little while our choices are limited.

So, some day we will be back to Kithicor. HoB will send the undead back to their rotting graves. For now we travel on, battered, beaten and a little wiser.
