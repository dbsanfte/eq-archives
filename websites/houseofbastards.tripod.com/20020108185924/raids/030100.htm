EVERFROST
March 1, 2000


Frozen Death

The raiding party was smaller than expected last night. Raaj, Luifang and Fibben (all scheduled to appear) were no where to be found. But a brave party of four assaulted the plains of Everfrost with rousing success. There were a few bad incidents. We were overrun by mountaineers and shaman at one point and when it came time to run, many went in the wrong direction. Well, in the confusion I don't think anyone went in the right direction at first.

There was also a nasty incident where Faan sat to close to a spawn point for an Icy Orc. (Level 20-23 MOB) and when it appeared it killed Faan in 2 or 3 blows. But all in all it was a successful learning experience. We have proven that we can stand toe to toe with the beasts of the Everfrost plains and successfully walk away from it. With a full party we would have been very devastating. We gained between 1 and 2 bubbles of exp and made 1.5 plat each. Not a great night for either category but again, a solid learning experience that brought confidence to the troops.
