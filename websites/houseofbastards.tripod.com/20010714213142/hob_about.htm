ABOUT HOB


What is HoB?
HoB is a small splinter group of characters, formerly members of the guild Oracle of Virtue while on the Cazic-Thule server and now members of the guild Dusk Eternal on the Ayonae Ro server. HoB was originally formed as a way for some long time friends to gather together and game every week. The fun soon caused this group to grow into a larger group of new friends. Through the months the roster has changed a bit, but the core players remain. HoB has grown from a small group of friends exploring Norrath to a large group of organized, highly effective comrades who perform weekly raids on Norrath's dungeons and wildernesses. OoV occasionally sent HoB on dangerous and exciting missions as well. As a team we work to take on the challenges the wilds of Norrath have to offer. We fight foes many our level would cower from. We avoid most spoiler sights, generally remain "untwinked" and strive to live closer to Verrants "vision" of EQ by crawling dungeons, cleansing outdoor areas and playing for fun more than profit. The result is immense fun, some great loot, fantastic experience and stories galore!

How can I join HoB?
Ziff Harbinger is generally the organizer of these raids and all information regarding upcoming ones can be found here or on the OoV site. If you care to join our weekly raids, the invitation is open to everyone, as long as your levels are not too far away from ours. If we find you fighting in our ranks often enough and work well with the team, we may extend an invitation for you to permanently join the ranks of our elite fighting force and wear the HoB badge with honor.

What does HoB stand for?
House of Bastards. We know, it may offend some, so we use the initials instead of the full name. Be warned, this site does contain liberal use of the word Bastard though. However, we do hope you can see the humor in the name. We are a group of fatherless individuals who lack guidance. We are an unruly bunch who do tend to wander off, do our own thing and have ruined many a Goblins day. But alas, some will be offended by the liberal use of the word. So, if you find you are one of those individuals, please proceed no further. One thing we can promise though. We do make an effort to avoid any other offensive language on this site. So, you need not worry that other words considered far more profane are in use here. We may be bastards, but we are not uncouth. Well, except for maybe Fibben...
