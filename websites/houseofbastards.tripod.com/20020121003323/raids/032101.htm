FRONTIER MOUNTAINS
Mar. 21, 2001


RAIDING PARTIES
Dicerman - paladin (29), Faan - cleric (29), Galadrial - druid (31), Lanorolen - bard (27), Nekko - shadowknight (31), Shumirek - bard (27), Wildride - paladin (29), Ziff - enchanter (29)

OTHERS: Kodman - paladin (35)

RECOUNT
by Ziff

There is something very intimidating about fighting a foe who is twice your height and wielding a brutally huge sword. HoB had landed on the new continent only to discover that the surrounding country side was plagued with giants. The outpost they had established in Warsilk woods was ancient and well fortified. This was an established foothold the citizens would simply have to learn to live with. But in the heart of the frontier mountains they were busy constructing a new outpost. This must be destroyed.

So weary from the long boat ride hob set out to smash a hole in the giants plans. They couldn't tear down the fort but they could harry the giants and reduce their numbers. The plan was to attack the fort and make stab into the giant mines. Hopefully they could destroy a significant number of their leaders to make an assault by Feronia vie feasible.

Hob stormed the fort and quickly established a foothold inside the fort. Then they began clearing the entrance to the mines. This was all done with hobs usual proficiency and daring. Giants fell like redwoods before a mighty axe. Early in the fight hob even took on a brae and three tumps with little trouble. True new tactics were necessary as the massive brutes were immune to stuns and Ziff's mind games. Still they adjusted admirably.

And so they pushed deeper into the mines into uncharted territory. They soon discovered why this section remained unmapped. Here in the depths of the giant mines were the elite giants. Not one hillock or tump roamed these corridors. Only braes and the incredibly powerful Craigs. In fact it was mostly Craigs.

The first time HoB met two Craigs a small disaster occurred that lead to the death of Galadriel. This began a slow and deadly rock slide. Next to fall was Dicerman as another Brae joined the fight. After the combat as the newly resurrected party members were resting and colleting their things a Craig stormed around the corner catching the group by surprise and killing Ziff. And the rocks kept tumbling.

With his new found gifts from his god Faan resurrected Ziff as well. HoB was regrouping and resting when they were assaulted by two more Craigs. With many still weak from the resurrections and the previous battles this battle proved to be too much. The rock slide was in full force now. In minutes the battle turned ugly. Dicerman Shum and others fell quickly to the monstrous blows. With less than half of the party still standing Wildride called for the group to flee. Exiting the mines brought five more giants into the combat and lead to the death of at least two more members. In the aftermath HoB regrouped inside the fort. Bravely Kodman began extracting the corpses from the depths of the mines caring nothing for his own life. As proof of this fact near the end of his retrievals he emerged from the mines with Dicerman's corpse trailing six giants. HoB foolishly tried to assist him which led to yet more deaths. The rock slide just kept coming.

After quite some time HoB was gathered again in the fort. Rested and prepared to make one last assault on the mines.

This time HoB had little trouble. Within thirty minutes HoB had cleared the mines and battled their ay to the bottom. Their they found two giants of immense age and experience. Amazingly they were not bent on the destruction of HoB. So, with the hour getting late, HoB retreated from the mines having dealt a vicious blow to the giants and having gained valuable information that could be turned into the outpost officials, their mission was complete.
