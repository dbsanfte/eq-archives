NORTH RO
April 25, 2000


RAIDING PARTY
Faan - cleric (12), Fibben - druid, Krug - monk (13), Ziff - enchanter


The sands were stained red with the blood of dervish cutthroats!

A rip roaring good time was had tonight. 4 of us met and headed off to "Derv Camp 2". For those of you who don't know this site spawns 6 dervish cutthroats at an incredible pace. This camp is often left alone because it is so difficult to clear and control. When we arrived the camp was cleared and there was another group who were willing to share. Shortly after, they left the site. Unfortunately, we let the spawn get ahead of us and suddenly there were 3 dervs waiting to kill us. We attempted to clear the camp and while pulling 2 more spawned, including a caster. We attempted to run but half the party was cut down as we fled across the desert. Not to be outdone we regrouped and returned to the camp. For the next 2 1/2 hours we slaughtered dervs. It was a bit hairy at times and Ziff had some trouble with his new charm spell, but we managed to stay alive for the entire time. It was decided that with a 5th member we could have been a little safer, but with 4 we were rarely in serious danger.

The night was a rousing success for money and experience as well. We looted 13 or more derv rings. (I think there is a quest for these now), 7.2 platinum each and netted around 2.5 bubbles of experience for everyone. No one leveled but most are now within 1 bubble of doing so.


Also important news: Zoft's guild has asked if we, as a group, would like to join. I am not positive we will be able to because they normally restrict guild members to 20th level but it has been suggested to me by several guild members. I need to know what people think about this idea.

The Guild is Oracle of Virtue. It is a small guild largely high level characters. (We have 5-6 50+) It is a very friendly and helpful guild, but they will not give away items. They will help us get them, as long as they are not way out of our reach. (I.e. no 1000 platinum headgear will be "given" away to low level characters.)

There are a number of guild members who have other lower level characters we could group with. We would also potentially be able to recruit new members to the guild if we start grouping with others and find they are worthy.

There is no monthly "fee" or any other requirements the guild places on people. No required guild meeting attendance etc.

Basically, I see this as a way for us to communicate better, have consistent larger groups and have assistance getting some of those hard earned quest items.
