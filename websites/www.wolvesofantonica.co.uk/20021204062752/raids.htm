Organiser's Notes:

TT Hunter/Forager.

Ok, I bet there are other people out there who haven't completed their key quest for Old Sebilis. What it requires is 2 medallions - one from the Froglok Hunter, one from the Froglok Forager. Turn them in to an Iksar ghost in Trakanon's Teeth and get a Trakanon Idol - key to Old Sebilis.

I would like to organize my first guild raid in a long time by getting whoever is interested together to get ourselves some keys and maybe some other good loot.

Raid will kick off at 8:00PM EST at the zone line from Trakanon's Teeth to Emerald Jungle. It will go until we have enough keys, then will continue as long as people want to do the cycle. I'm limiting this raid to 35+ so more wolves can experience a guild raid. We need trackers, so Rangers and Druids will help us a lot. Please do the following: 1) Sign up here if you want to go. 2) Don't be late - make sure you factor in travel time to TT - directions are listed below. 3) Don't sign up and when it's time to go say "sorry I just got in a Karnor's group" - please be there. 4) Please get all maps, read the instructions below and please be aware of all loot rules before you sign up.

Other info - Travel: No PoP - Get maps from www.eqatlas.com - take boat to Fironia Vie, bind yourself there - head north into the Swamp of No Hope. Head over to Trakanon's Teeth, Head to the Zone line to Emerald Jungle. PoP - take the Cabilis Book - you will arrive in the Field of Bone - go into Emerald Jungle - watch out for the cliff - either take the stairs or slowly inch down. Run along south wall with invis or avoid all creatures until you get to zone to Trakanon's Teeth.

Cycle: This is how the cycle works - at any one time there can be both a Froglok Hunter or Forager up in the zone (sometimes multiples but this is a bug). Once you kill a Hunter or Forrager, another will spawn somewhere in the zone OR a named creature will spawn. Some of the named can be rather tough, but many drop very good things (Cerimonial Iksar Chestplate, Hangman's Noose, Silken Cat Fir Girdle, Trakasaur's Tooth etc.)

Loot Rules: We will go CAWU loot rules. This stands for Can and Will Use. Certain items will be designated as priest/caster/melee only even though all classes might be able to use (like Hangman noose I think) but most will be just based on class restrictions. Each person who will use the item will be allowed one roll /ran 0 100 AFTER I CALL IT. No rolling early! Once you win your item, in order to get it, you will have to pass down what you currently have - NO EXCEPTIONS except for clicky buff armors - to the guild. At that point, those that didn't win will be allowed to roll on that piece, etc. until noone else wants that item, then it will go into the guild bank. If you win an item on this raid, you are done rolling for items unless you want to give up the item you just won for a chance to roll for something else. I will be keeping track of who gets what. If you have any questions about this loot policy, ask me before you sign up. There will be no loot disputes at the raid - we want rolling for loot to go smoothly and quickly so we can get back to killing for more loot.

Drops: On the Messgae Board under Raids you will find a list of drops for the cycle including who will roll for each piece. Again, loot drops will be Can and Will Use. If an item is limited by me to certain classes and noone wants it in those classes it will go to a roll of whoever needs it outside of those classes. Then if noone needs it still, it will go to guild bank. Remember, in order for you to get an item, you will have to pass down what you currently have with the exception of clicky buff armor (Kunark etc). Once you win a piece of armor and pass yours down, that item is yours, free and clear and you are allowed no more rolls on loot this raid.

If you can offer ports for this raid let me know.

Description

Trakanon's Teeth is a vast, swampy area in the mountains of the same name. The most notable feature is the vast ruins of the ancient city of the Iksar, Sebilis. The ancient ruins of a massive aqueduct lead from the small lake in the area to near the old city. Now, however, the city and the surrounding area are filled with the tormented dead. Other creatures of the swamp also roam freely in the large areas of the jungle.

Travelling To and From TT

The entrance from the Swamp of No Hope is located at the spring that feeds the whole swamp at approximately 1570, -4500 and enters Trakanon's Teeth at 1570, 4500.

There is a connection to the Emerald Jungle at 3955, 1500 along the northern edge of the zone.

Sebilis can be reached through Trakanon's Teeth, found at location -2055, -4450. To enter, you need to find a red sphere buried under some ruins, and also have the correct key to activate the portal. The key is obtainable through a quest with Emperor Ganek.
