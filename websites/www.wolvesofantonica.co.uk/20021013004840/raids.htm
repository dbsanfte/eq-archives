Organiser's Notes:

Ok, we'll be gathering at the entrance to Kael from the Eastern Wastes side of the city. Time of arrival should be approximately 1:30pm EST or 6:30pm GMT. I want to start at 2:00pm EST (7:00pm GMT). The raid will be 5 hours and we'll be after Velious class armor pieces. We'll be fighting in the Kael Arena. The giants there are all pretty much 50+ so aggro management is very important. Loot will be held by me for distribution if there is any allowable interim and/or at the end. Raidchannel will be woakael.

The route to the Arena is pretty direct and you can tell how to proceed just by glancing at the map. Will definitely need an FD puller as usual. I've again limited this raid to 44+ but I'm not allowing for exceptions this time. Simply put, the area is way too dangerous and at a certain point, you'll just be a liability since low level characters generate more hate and well... you'll be summoned and killed most likely or caught in a rampage/enrage.

When engaged with the enemy, the most important thing to note is the direction you are pushing him. These things have a ton of hps so the fight will go on for quite a while. If you are pushing the giant close to a ledge, wall, or towards the center of Arena (where roaming giants lie), you should immediately switch to the other side of the giant and begin pushing it back. Usually, I give this task for the MA to decide and make the call though I sometimes make the call myself if I feel it is needed (hotkey something like: "Other side of NOW!").

Other than the relative difficulty of the MOBs, it's a pretty straight forward affair.

I would like everyone to sign up before hand so we know who is attending (sign up on message board under Kael Raid).

If you can offer ports for this raid let me know.

Description
