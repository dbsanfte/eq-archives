GUILD RAID EVENT

City of Mist Epic Raid
   
Organiser Guildor Trackfinder
Zone City of mist (outdoor)
Date / Day Sunday 4th May 2003
Time 9.00PM GMT
( Time Conversion )
Monster Level 45 to 50
Raid Level 45+
Goal To hopefully get epic pieces for druid/ranger/shammy
Chat Channel WOARAID to organise any time for 1 hour before start

Organiser's Notes:

Objective: Want to get to the area with the black reavers - and bash the hell out of them until they drop epic pieces! If we are good enough - Lord Rak'ashir will spawn, and he drops the Shammy epic piece. More info later.

Groups: If we head for the back of the zone, we can certainly accomodate 3 full groups there, maybe more. If people don't mind the squeeze, we can surely try. Casters may have trouble getting stuff to land on the black reavers. They are very resistant. Pets may not be advisable. Melee's will want haste, ATK and STR buffs. The MA / SA will need HP buffs too. Lets not waist peridots un-necessarily, ok?

Sign-up: This is a guild only event, with a possible few friend invites.

Meeting location: Be there at least 30 mins before at the entrance to City of Mist, just inside, so we can begin organising groups. The sooner we get sorted, the quicker we can get on with it.

Communication: please join chat channel WOAraid for discussions during raid, and for organising ports etc, as necessary.

Requirements: There are no requirements other than level. But if you intend to leave early, it might be an idea for you to have a Gate potion handy (if you are not capableof gating yourself).

Loot: As its guild only, we'll roll on classes present, as no one has one reserved. If more than one jade reaver drops, then all the better. In the unlikely event we have more jade reavers than we need - we'll leave it up to officer / HC to decide their fate.
As for other loots - group leaders can sell / split as necessary. Except for shammy drops. Again, NBG for shammys.
Tactics: In a perfect raid, we'd have an inital group with the ability to tank, slow and heal. After previous experience in this, having the cleric blinded or agro'd on is not good. So, a backup healer like say a druid is always adviable for this group.
Secondary group will have melee and a backup healer to take care of them. This group needs to be self sufficient in terms of agro. If the tank loses agro to this group - they need to back off, and let the tank re-establish agro, or the backup healer will be doing overtime, and will likely draw agro for chain-healing - but thats not gonna happen, right? I will explain in greater detail at the time, of how pulls will happen - and actions we take.

Components: All priests and buffers, please be responsible for your components – only use them when appropriate, and keep track of expenditures. At the end of the raid, notify your group leader of total expense and I will take it out of any money loot gained (if we have enough) and return it to you.

Warnings: I'm not going to accept messing around. A lot can go wrong, and I'd prefer it if any clowns would stay away. I don't have a points system, but I do remember people that mess around. I am usually quite forgiving, but if I see that the actions of any individual are putting the raid in any un-necessary danger, I'll have to TL you out of the raid, and you're not gonna get a chance to roll on anything.
Also, if you have a problem listening to commands, and you are breaking mezzes instead of the MA, I'll not give you many chances before you get kicked out! So, get serious, have a second chat window open, and filter all raid chat to it - so you don't miss anything! Ok? Lets do it!
