GUILD AWARDS

The Guild Member Monthly Awards are awarded to guild members who have been nominated by there fellow Wolves.  
For any nomination to be considered the following information is need:- Nominee's name, Reason for the nomination.
All nominations must be received before 20th of each month via e-mail.
The award winner will be chosen by Darglasian and will be announced on guild motd and full details entered below.

Please send all nominations to award@wolvesofantonica.co.uk
