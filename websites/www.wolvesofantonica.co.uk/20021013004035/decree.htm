Guild Charter


The aim of this Guild is not necessarily to be of the biggest, but to have quality players who have the desire to explore the worlds as a team or family.

This Guild is built on strength and honour. Together, many fine adventures
are to be had.


The Rules

† Above all - HAVE FUN! The ultimate purpose of this guild is to enhance the enjoyment of EverQuest for those of us who play it.

† Under no circumstances kill steal other players' kills.

† No bad or sexual language in guild talk. Many of us have our own families and don't wish younger eyes to see such words.

† Any 'out of character' conversations should be kept for private /TELL's, /OOC's /GUILDSAY's or /GSAY's. Under no circumstances should /SHOUT be used for any conversations that are not in character and game related.

† The entrance level to the Guild is Level 10 - exceptions for existing members wishing to reroll their character.

† Harassment of another player will not be tolerated. If a dispute arises between a guild member and another character, and it can not be resolved by either party, then the guild member should then /Ignore the offending character

† No evil races to be allowed entrance into the WoA.  We are a RPG guild and follow the path of light.

† All members must add there details to www.guildmagic.com, this is important as it helps the guild keep track of class - season - skills - etc.  It is also important that all guild members keep this up to date as possible.


If you like what you see here and would like to be part of this fine Guild, please ask any of our members.


Be proud, strong and honourable
