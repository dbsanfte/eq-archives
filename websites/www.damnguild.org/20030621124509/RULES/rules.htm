1. Guild members will not Excessively Beg, Whine, Or Complain to Players or Guild members.

2. Guild war--Only the Guild Leaders Deadlywolf and Zoltaz may declare or threaten guild war.

3. Events/Raid--Any Officer can set up an event or raid, they just need approval from a Guild Leader

4. Recruitment- New members must be level 30, and need to have grouped with a guild member prior to invite.  The only exceptions are real life family and friends, an existing member's alts. The recruiting officer assumes a mentor status. Officers should try and recruit a new person each week

5. New Recruits--Must be told by recruiting officer who the leaders are, where webpage is, number of members and how old guild is.

6. Represent- At all times as long as a member wears our tag, he or she represents every member of our guild.

7. Probation--Guild member must show ambition in guild.  New members in Probation may not ask for gear out of guild bank

8. Guild bank-- The bank holds standard equipment for low level characters such as: Ringmail, Bronze, Leather type armor-Weapons such as Velium morning stars and velium great staffs. The guild bank does not have money in it. Please donate loot items to bank to help out new members and alts. Any questions about bank direct to an officer.  Bank is a privilege not an obligation.
