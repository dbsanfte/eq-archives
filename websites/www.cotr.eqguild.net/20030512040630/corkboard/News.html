Good Experience for Low Levels in Qeynos Area

Once you reach around level 3, go to Qeynos Hills and kill Gnolls, Gnoll
Pups, Gnoll Scouts or Gnoll Watchers. There is a slight chance they will
drop Gnoll Fangs. Once you have four sets take them to the captain of the
guards that overlooks the area. Give him four teeth at a time. He will give
you lots of experience and four moonstones. Take the moonstones to the
bartender in the back of the bar in South Qeynos and he will also give you
experience.

HINT

Zone: West Karana

Level Range: 11-16- Misty; Guard Pulling 30-40

Class: Caster, Bard (sing to death), Tank (can use house to rest/bandage
and then attack. Then rest/bandage and attack.)

In West Karana to the East of Q-hills there is a little building where
Misty spawns. You can pull anything to this building walk into the house
and they will loose all agro and walk away. Step out of the house and they
run back. Just keep doing this: Nuke, med, step forward, wait one sec, step
back, and repeat. You can also pull guards in your 30’s at higher levels.
