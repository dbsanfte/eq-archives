Guildy of Month
Welcome Champions and future Champions !!!


This guild was created for the player, not for the master player. Once you reach level 40 or 50, you know what "good" armor is, you know what spells to use, you know what weapon to use. This guild was created for new players to have mentors. For mentors to teach new players what good armor is, what weapon to use, where to fight and how to fight. For the experienced player to pass on advice, old armor, tips, tricks.. etc to the new player. All in all to have fun playing the game.

Please remember folks.. This is a GAME. Do the best you can do .. but more importantly, HAVE FUN !!

If you came to this guild for only high level activities..... think again.
-Alejandro-Leader...........Hashiro- Assistant Leader

Guild meetings
General member meetings are the 1st Sunday of each month.

Officers meetings are held when deemed necessary by the LEADER (Alejandro), ASST LEADER (Hashiro)or the OFFICER IN CHARGE (Vinister).

All officers should be autojoined to officer chat at all timess to discuss current happenings in the guild. Members are required to attend meetings if you would like your voice heard... if you cannot come please do not be upset at the decisions made at the meetings.

To join Officers channel

Then... auto join by typing
/autojoin champion:password

To invite someone to the guild...
type /guildinvite name
