This guild was created for the player, not for the master player. Lately we have had players get near lvl 60 and leave for a high level guild. Do not take this against the guild, this guild was created for meduium to low level players.  Once you reach level 50, you know what "good" armor is, you know what spells to use, you know what weapon to use. This guild was created for new players to have mentors. For mentors to teach new players what good armor is, what weapon to use, where to fight and how to fight. For the experienced player to pass on advice, old armor, tips, tricks.. etc to the new player. 

Please remember folks.. This is a GAME. Do the best you can do .. but more importantly, HAVE FUN !!

If you came to this guild for only high level activities..... think again.

-Alejandro/Davon-Leader

-Hashiro/Sedina- Assistant Leader
