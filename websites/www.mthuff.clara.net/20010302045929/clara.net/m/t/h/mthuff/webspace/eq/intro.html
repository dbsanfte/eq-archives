Introduction:

This website is designed to:

  • Enable CoN casters to notify all of the guild as to what spell scrolls they are seeking.

  • Enable CoN players to see quickly if a spell they have looted is needed, and if so by whom.

  • Enable the looter to contact the caster via email to arrange a handover.

  • Set out a mechanism whereby currently held stocks of dropped scrolls can be held centrally by a guild mule, for future use by CoN casters, or for sale/trade at Karas's discretion

The website is arranged thus:

  • A central webpage to see who needs what scrolls.

  • A page of rules and guidance, also detailing the "mule" and how it should be used.

  • A series of pages, one for each caster class, where scrolls may be ordered.

Entry to this website is password protected, with the usual CoN EZboard guild password, in order to prevent spurious requests from NoN-guild players.

You are strongly reccomended to bookmark the main webpage.
