This page is for ordering only. It does NOT show who needs what scroll, but rather it to enable you to sort through what spell you need, and what their reference numbers are. Where a spell is marked as "UNKNOWN" it means that it is not known whether the spell is purchasable from a vendor, or is from an NPC drop. Accordingly these spells have been included as if they were "dropped". If you locate an "UNKNOWN" scroll, please contact EQcastersrealm and Fiddruin (webmater) so that others may benefit from your discovery.

To Order spells use this box:

1. Ensure you qualify under rules.

2. Left click on desired scroll, then hold down CTRL key and click on next needed scroll. When all the scrolls you need are highlighted, click on "cast form" button.

3. If you make an error, simply click on "Fizzle form" button, and start over. Do not click "Cast form" until you are sure you are correct.

4. Ensure you fill out all fields, incomplete entries WILL BE IGNORED.

5. To remove your request from web-site, use form on page where all requests are shown.

6. As considerable work is created for me administering this site, please do not simply put all spells as required, think before you click!
