Rules, advice and instructions for using these pages:

Rules:

  • Caster's must be at least within 5 levels of being able to use a scroll, otherwise it's "muled" until they can use it.

  • This webpage url and password must not be divulged outside of the guild.

Advice:

  • Priority is given to primary characters. Please enter "alt" after your name if requesting a spell for an alt.

  • Scrolls should be "given" rather than charged for, if you need to charge, contact an officer for further guidance.

  • That said, Casters should, if possible, pay market rates for scrolls to the doner, but if unable, might wish to contribute what they can afford.

  • No debts should be entertained - un-necessary friction may result!

  • All players should write down names of scrolls held, find them on the central list, and see if anyone is looking for them, once a week.

  • If no-one needs it - try to get in the habit of handing them over to the Mule.

The "Mule"

  • There is only 1 mule, it lives in Felwithe Bank, Great Faydark

  • It is named "Scribbles", and is operated by Karas only.

  • In the event of Karas leaving EQ or CoN, a new mule will be created, and all scrolls will be transferred to that mule.

  • Spells held by the mule may be traded/sold at Karas's sole discretion.

Instructions:

How to order scrolls:

  • Click on the link to your caster's class, and follow instructions on the page.

How to check who-needs-what scrolls.

  • Go to the mainpage, and look down alphabetic spell list. If a name appears opposite the spell, it is needed by that player. His name is his email link, and if you hold that scroll, he may be contacted, and a meeting in-game arranged.

How to use the Mule:

  • If you hold a scroll that is not needed by anyone in guild, then you have several options. You can sell it on the open-market, or you can give it to the guild's mule for future use by lower level CoN casters.

  • If you decide to supply the guild with the scroll, contact Karas in game, and arrange a meeting with the mule. EVERY effort should be made (if possible) to supply a CoN caster with a scroll he has indicated as needing, under no circumstances should Karas's mule be used as a middle man - else he will be continually be interupted!
