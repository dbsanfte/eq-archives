FRIENDS

When you need help, don't look for friends.
The true ones will show up for themselves.

When you need a shoulder to cry on, don't ask for one.
The true comfort will come from someone who sees your wet eyes without the need of a word.

Don't tell your secrets to careless ears.
Share them with someone who really worries about you - even if it's only yourself.

Don't prostitute your feelings.

There is someone who deserves to know them.
There is someone who is willing to understand them.
And there is someone who will give a hand to help you, even if it's needed to let go of his own support.

Look for your real friend.
*Don't take immitations!*
