IN VAIN

In this torn society
There is no longer pain.

No more pain!... because there is no longer life.

Taken by their destiny,
Laughing at their past
Playing with their present
Fooling with their future

Fight for the future
No.
When did it seem real?
Is it worth to suffer for the unknown?
No.

Nothing comes, nothings cheers.
But it exist.
And waits.
Waits for what?
If there is no life anymore?
Because life understood that fighting...
Leads to nothing.

Only the desire, it is not enough.

It's a vain effort.
Always.
Absolutely, totally, effectively...
Always.
