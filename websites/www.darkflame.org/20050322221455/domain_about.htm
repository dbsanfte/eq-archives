This domain was registered in July 2004 because I always wanted to have a domain and when I finally got a job and my own money, I bought it. It's for my own pleasure, work and improvement. Unfortunately, I don't have time to update my pages often, so they may seem a little neglected, but I'm always working on them when I can. The domain expires in July 2005, but I plan to renew it until the day I can't pay for it anymore.

The name Darkflame was chosen by both me and my boyfriend, when we were trying to pick a name for the domain, and there is no special reason for the name, other than it sounded nice. We even created a logo for it. My boyfriend actually was the one who paid for the domain registration (and I pay for the host), so this domain belongs to both of us. He barely uses it, but has free access to it when he wants.

The layout is not darkflame-related, because I couldn't find anything on this subject that looked nice. The picture is from Corbis, and the fonts used are Alladin and Verdana. Version 1 "Changing Attitude" was lauched on February 14, 2005.
