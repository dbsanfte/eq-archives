THE SERIES

I gave these stories a special section because they're my favorite ones. I created five main characters and I've written two stories with them so far, and that's what you'll find here. There will be more. Reading in order is better, but not necessary.

A Mistake in the Dark
Status: finished
The first story from a series that has now 3 novels was written when I was 14 years old. It's the story of five friends that, due to a sucession of coincidences, have to face death, kidnapping and dangerous people, by themselves, without knowing who else they can trust.

Sons of the Silence
Status: finished but not translated
The third story was written for NanoWrimo 2003. Now, Guilherme accidentaly find out about a secret photography laboratory that works with pornographic pictures involving children. He and his friend get in much trouble after telling the police about that, and have to hide to protect their own lives, having no idea who's constantly chasing them.
