BEYOND THE WALLS

There is not a way to get away from this thing that blocks me
This loneliness that tightens and mistreats me
I see that face in every thing,
I feel your eyes in every thing.
Nothing can fill this space
This emptiness that was left when you went away.
I still wait for your return
Still count on it to end my torture
But I can't
your voice calls me
and I can't do it, I can't hold it
It's a pain, a deep agony this waiting
And the loneliness takes me
Consumes me, destroys me
Maddens me.
Nothing exists, nothing sings
Only your voice, far, distant
that calls me and at the same time tells me
not to wait for you anymore
Nothing makes sense
While the loneliness and the distance are so big and obscure.

05-01-99 | Renata
