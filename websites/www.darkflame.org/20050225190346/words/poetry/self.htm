SELF-PORTRAIT

Since forever procrastinating, but now learning
The silence giving room to long thoughts
Two routines intersperse as the months go by
Days full of promises, nights full of desires

Few movements, the always friendly sight
Sight that, as all friends, get boring
In the shelter finds the wind, the light, and the song of free birds
And reassure to herself that one day everything will change

Fear and courage intermix, and none of them wins the battle
But each new day shows that things solve by themselves
As best friends, understanding creatures

Seven times a week, comes the sun and comes the moon
The life runs and gets old, mistreats but teaches
And like a miracle, always leave hope behind.

05-08-03 | Renata
