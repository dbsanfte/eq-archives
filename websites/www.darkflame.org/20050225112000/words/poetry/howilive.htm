That's how I live

There is not a way not to notice the sun, each time its rays lighten my way.
There is not a way not to notice a bird, each time my ears are delighted by its singing.
There is not a way to pretend not to see the green, in this world in need of forests.
There is not a way to pretend not to see the fruits, in this world in need of food.

There is not a way to avoid a smile when a child is seen
In these days in need of affection.
There is not a way to avoid a tear dropping
In these days when your life is not yours.

It's impossible to look at the sky full of stars without feeling amazed with the bigness of this glorious creation...

There is not a way not to notice.
There is not a way to pretend not to see.
There is not a way to avoid.

It's impossible not to think about you, each time I feel your presence in my life.
It's impossible not to think about you, each time I see your importance in my life.

It's impossible not to think about you
All the time.
