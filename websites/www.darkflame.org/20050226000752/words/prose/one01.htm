ONEWORD - JUNE 2003

24th - PENDANT
She held the necklace tight with her hands. It was a memory of him. A memory she wanted to keep forever, the only think that had left… Because he was gone. And she wanted his memory to be always alive, always present, guiding her, orienting her. That's why the necklace would go with her wherever she went. The necklace, along with its pendant.

25th - WINE
He was not everything he talked about, no way. He was exhibitionist, he always wanted all the attention to him, and made stupid jokes. He was not what she was waiting for, not what her friends and family were waiting for. Because of that, she gave up. She wouldn't bear for much long with bullshit from a little kid that acted like he was half the age he actually was. She didn't want to be a babysitter.

26th - ANGLE
She flew free, in the blue sky. Getting off from a high cliff, she observed the waves that broke in the acclivitous rock, and a boat, far in the sea… Then she looked cautiously to the ground, to the leaves… She got what she wanted and went back. Three little birds were waiting for her, in the nest, hungry.

27th - GUARD
He was standing in front of the other man, who threatened to kill him. Because of an undermost motive, I must say. And he would get it over with him, oh he would. The guard showed up, uniformed guards, uppish, respectiful. And the man ran away. He just disappeared. Never went back to that city again.

29th - BEAM
In the middle of the stadium, the boys were playing and running to the ball, like crazy. The girls in the edge of the field were jumping, screaming and singing, trying to encourage them, despite the enormous disadvantage in the counter. It was just a school game, but so important to those boys.

30th - TOWER
He looked ahead, as soon as he reached the peak. Down there, in a plateau, he saw a tower, a high, thin, round and rustic tower… beautiful. A tower that didn't reach the sky, but could, with all that majesty. He thought that he had never seen anything so wonderful in his life.
