ANALOGY

It's raining today
I can't see you.
Everyday I look for you,
observe and admire you

Nothing is so pure
nothing is so beautiful
as the love I feel

Like the flower that grows
and feels the wind on it
my love remains strong
Faithful to this waiting
It waits the rain to end
Then it may go out, see you,
And start pulsing again.

1998 | Renata
