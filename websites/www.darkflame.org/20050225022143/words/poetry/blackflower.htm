THE BLACK FLOWER

Suddenly, that view
of the sky full of stars
once beautiful
makes no sense anymore.

The flower, the flight, the birds, the moon
no longer have a meaning
they're beings like me, lost
brought to the world to fill the emptiness.

My life oscillates
like the waves formed by the stone on the river
water that is no longer calm
and is now wild, sinking me...

The ridiculous love song
carefree, happy
awakens in me the anger
and nobody knows, nobody sees.

Nobody notices that all this happiness in the world don't exist to me.
Surrounded by my own problems, I look for and I'm attracted by the hardest ways.

Why?

Because everything I desire is to come back
To come back in time, back to being a child
for whom everything is easy and beautiful
It's very hard to be strong.

And you'll never understand.
So, stay away from me.
You're happy, live your life
With me, I want people like me.

People who, uncounciously, found out where the black flower has been seeded.

16-12-99 | Renata
