ADVICES

A window, by my side, shows me the night
A starlit and magical night
With a moon and a mystery
The silence of the night knocks, announces itself
Speaks
The heart listens to its words
Wise words, words that say
That it's not late
That inside me there is still hope
Sweet words that say that I still can
That I'm still able
That I'll find the way
To your heart
My soul clamors, calls out
It knows
Quietly it understands
Your soul that is hidden
covered, silenced
Your heart that weakly and sorrowfully beats
Lonely
Your mouth lies to the world
Your eyes lie to yourself
Your ears insist not to hear my calling
My request for a chance
To set you free
To make you happy
To bring you back to the world
Back to life, back to the music
Back to the colors
Back to the night, back to the light
To the pure and bright light
Of my sweet heart.

19-01-99 | Renata
