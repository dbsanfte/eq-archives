ABOUT ME > BIO

I'm an average girl. I'm not a prep, and not a rebel. I have a family, friends, and problems. I'm happy and satisfied with my own life, but I always want more. Sometimes, I don't work hard enough for it, but things changed in a short time and I'm sure they will always get better.

I love the nature and I hate violence. I don't like being afraid of walking on the streets late at night.

I’m 21 and live in Brazil. I work in a telecommunication company, go to college (last year, finally), and share an apartment with 2 other girls, in a city 30 minutes away from the one where my family lives. I love this city more than my hometown and I don’t want to go back. I want to have a life of my own here. And so far, I've done pretty good.

I love reading, writing, watching movies, listening to music, photographing, surfing the internet, playing with cats. These are things I do with passion.

I don't care about giving out my home address or showing my picture on the internet. Many parts of my life are distributed among my various websites – it's one of my favorite ways to express myself. But there are things I never share with anyone. There are things that are mine. I have a blog and an online photo album and many other public things, but I know how to keep the important things to myself. In fact, there's only one person in this world who knows me entirely: my boyfriend. He can talk about me better than I can. I don't have big secrets or fears; I just don't like showing my worst weakness and my best achievements to people, and I have my own reasons to act like that.

I have a hard time when it comes to forgive and forget. I usually don't do either. It's not good, but it makes me feel safe.

I love languages. I speak Portuguese, English and some German. I'm taking Spanish classes now. I will learn Italian. And more German. And more more German. If you're German, I’ll let you know that I love your language, I envy you because you can speak it fluently, and I want to visit your country so badly. And you can e-mail me and leave a message because I want to always learn German.

And, finally… I'm just a girl, who can't express herself in just a couple paragraphs, in a foreign language. I'm a girl who has a lot of feelings inside, not always ready to deal with them. I'm a girl who's proud of herself sometimes, and sometimes not. I’m a girl who still have a lot of things to learn, but who also has a lot of things to teach.
