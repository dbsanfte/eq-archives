CHAPTER 8 - BETWEEN HIM AND ME

“Paula! I finally found you! What were you thinking, locking yourself in like that? I mean, I kind of know what happened, I just wanted you to tell me exactly what happened.”

“They already told you bullshit, didn't they?”

“No, Paula, I was in the ward and Nic told me some things, but I want you to tell me the whole story, he was too messed up to tell.”

“Alright, since you promise you'll believe me.”

“Of course I believe you, Paula! I'm your friend...”

“Valéria's friend too...”

“Okay, first you talk to me, then I'll decide who’s the right one to me.”

I told her everything, from the argument until that very moment. I was much calmer. I was even ready to talk to Valéria.

“Paula, I think this is all so ridiculous. You should never have argued, but... Valéria isn't like that either, how could she say that to you, hit you...”

“What if she told the others the story the way she wanted it to look like?”

“This is also so weird... Look, I promised Nic I would fix it. He's devastated because you left the ward crying, if I were you I'd go there to talk to him.”

“How can I even look at Nic, I acted worse than a little kid would!”

“Paula, Nic loves you and he won't care what you do.”

“All right, I can try...”

“Great, that's it. While you talk to Nic, I'll go look for Valéria. But don't worry, I'll do something about it. Maybe people just misunderstood each other...”

“I hope so...”, I said, leaving the chalet. “Oh, one more thing... Did you... have sex with Guilherme?”

“Well... let's not talk about it right now, okay?”, Luísa was kind of embarrassed. “After all, if I had stayed at my place, nothing would have happened.”

* * *

“Paulinha, you finally showed up!”, Guilherme was leaving the ward, and smiled when he saw me.

“Whoa, you’re sure in a good mood!”, I smiled too. “Were you talking to Nic?”

“Yes, I was... Poor Nic, he’s so sorry...”

“I don’t even know why, I was the one at fault! Well, I’m going to talk to him now...”

“Cool. Do you know where Luísa is?”

“Looking for Valéria. She’s crazy, wanting to fix the whole mess by herself.”

“Uhn... I’m thirsty, do you wanna come for a soda with me?”

“Guilherme, I have to go see Nic...”

“It’s just one minute, Nic’s fine now...”

“Okay... but it has to be really quick!”

We arrived at the snack bar, and stayed at the counter. After a couple minutes, Luísa, smiling, showed up with Valéria.

“It’s alright now, guys, everything’s okay now!”

Valéria and I looked at each other, kind of embarrassed. She was more embarrassed than me, obviously.

“Paula, I... I'm sorry...”

“She changed some details of the story so Cláudio wouldn’t think anything bad of her!”, Luísa whispered to me, laughing. I smiled. How could such a stupid thing like that make Valéria and I fight?

“That’s okay, Valéria. I'm going to see Nic now, Guilherme just slowed down my plans a little”, I pointed to the soda. “But I'm going there right now”.

“Can I go with you? I think I owe Nic some apologies too.”

“Of course, Valéria, let’s go. What about the rest of the guys, did anyone talk to them?”

“I was with them. Only Adriana was missing”.

“Okay...”

We got into the ward, I was walking ahead Valéria, anxious. So anxious, that I even forgot to knock on the door and entered the room without announcing myself. And that was when I saw it.

“Nic!”

Nic and Adriana were kissing.
