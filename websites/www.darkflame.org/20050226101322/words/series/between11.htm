CHAPTER 11 - TWO WEEKS

“Nic, hurry, hurry!”

“You lazy bum, you’ll never reach us!”

What these guys can think about... Well, all of us, in fact. But the thing is I didn’t know there was gonna be a paddle boat race, and now I was taking part in one of them.

“Watch out, it’s gonna crash!”, I yelled, beside Nic. Then I gripped the sides of the boat, so I wouldn’t fall into the water.

“Sorry, our fault!”, he yelled, laughing, to the boy in the other paddle boat, who didn’t understand why we were faster than what was normal.

“We’re almost there... almost...”, I was laughing. Nic whistled: “We’ve passed you! Haha! Who’s the lazy bum now?”

Guilherme, along with Luísa and Valéria (I don’t know how they found room for three people there), started trying to go faster. Nic was singing that song for Airton Senna’s victory, and kept paddling:

“Tan tan tan, tan tan tan...”

“Whoaaa watch oooout...”

I felt the crash. Nic was turning back to cross the lake again, and Guilherme went right over us. The “race” ended, with everyone laughing. Nic raised my arm:

“We won! The champions!”

Everything had been totally normal since that day. I was feeling so good, I think I was learning how to take part in the fun. Better, I was taking part in it as one of the main characters. Everything that happened just helped my self-confidence to be higher. Soon the vacation on the campground would come to its end, and I'd miss it so much. After our fight, Nic and I were closer than ever. Not even Adriana could bother me anymore. She and Alice would normally sleep with us in the chalet, just like João and Cláudio with Nic and Guilherme, and everything was at peace. Our friendship with Alice was back to normal, we only wouldn’t talk much to Adriana. Just when it was necessary, to be polite. Things were so good that Valéria even got what she wanted before.

“Oh, Cláudio is so handsome, he’s so hot, sweet, the best! We stayed together all night long at the dancing club!”

“Well, I can’t say anything except congratulations, I'm happy for you!”

“I have something more to say: you woke up early today, aren’t you sleepy?”, we laughed. No one at the pool seemed to notice Valéria’s excitement.

“Paula, I have to thank you... If you and Nic hadn’t fixed things, we’d have never talked to Alice again and she’d have never helped me like that!”

“Oh, stop, I didn’t have anything to do with it, girly! We all know that Cláudio wasn’t exactly ignoring you!”

“But, hey, are you going to go out again?”

“I don’t know... but I'd love to!”

“Oh, you wish...”

“What are these three pretty girls doing here, alone?”, Nic, Guilherme, João and Cláudio arrived. “I think we should stay here, maybe there is a shark, an alligator, and...”

“I can take care of them! There won’t be a scale left!”, Luísa pretended she was doing some kind of martial arts fight – I think it only looked like something like that in her mind, actually.

“They’ll die of laughter!”

She pulled Nic, who fell into the pool.

He got out and sat beside me. Guilherme was hugging Luísa. Cláudio looked around, not knowing what to do, and finally decided to sit beside Valéria.

“Watch what you’re doing with my sister, okay?”

“Shut up, Guilherme!”, Valéria was red with embarrassment, Luísa and I were staring at her, holding a smile.

“Okay, I feel out of place here...”, João scratched his head, looking at the pool. “I’ll go take a walk. If you guys need me, I’ll probably be somewhere.”

“Really? I thought you liked to change dimensions from time to time!”

The conversation between us didn’t last long.

“I'm going to the snack bar, I'm hungry. Anyone wanna come with me?”

“Me!”, Luísa and Guilherme left.

“Hey, we’re coming too!”

“So come on, you goobers!”

I grabbed Nic, and we ran to the snack bar. In one second, we had disappeared. Or almost...

“We can see them if we stay right here, and they can’t see us because of all these people!”

“Hey, easy! No need to hurry!”

“Haha, who knows?”

Luísa, Nic and I were looking for the best place, the best view, just like we were watching a soccer game. Only Guilherme hesitated.

“Leave them alone, what if one of them sees us here...”

“Valéria always tells us everything afterwards anyway, right Paula? What will be the difference, if we can watch it live?”

* * *

“Valéria, are you sure you won’t get sick after we go back home? Won’t you miss Cláudio? You two are together all the time! And Sorocaba and Rio de Janeiro are kilometers apart!”

“Sick, Luísa? Not me! We’re only going out, having fun... It’s vacation, I'm far from home, I can do whatever I want without having to explain it to anyone! And this night has to be special... very special! Tomorrow afternoon we’ll be going back home...”

“You’re right... Man, it’s so good to be here!”, I was upset for having to leave. “But Valéria, what it is that’s so special that you intend to do? Will you need the chalet empty or something like that?”

“Paula, I'm not going that far... definitely!”

“Okay, no offense...”

“I'm sorry, Luísa...”, patiently, Valéria explained the obvious to her. “Nothing you did even came to my mind now. I can even say the opposite, if I dated someone like Guilherme or Nic I'd probably do the same!”

“Hey, hey... now, I didn’t like it... Are you calling me silly or what?”, I said.

“Oh Christ, everyone is so sensitive today! What’s this, the next to last day’s syndrom?”

We laughed.

“Okay, let’s be serious. What are you going to do after all?”

“Well, the guys are going to the dancing club.”

“As always. Man, that place is so... I don’t know, I didn’t like it!”

“Okay, Luísa, they do like it, so what? Well, as I was saying, I'm going with him, and then we’ll get out and go to the lake and hang out there...”

“If anyone finds you...”

“There’s no one there at that time, everyone will probably be sleeping!”

“Hmm, okay, what time will they go back to sleep?”

“Luísa, you are not going to...”

She gave me a roguish smile and sat on the bed, jumping.
