CHAPTER 7 - JUST AN ARGUMENT

It was already morning. Without realizing it, I had fallen asleep there, beside the pool. When I woke up the sun had already risen and lots of people were swimming. Luckily, the place I’d chosen to feel sorry for myself at wasn’t usually frequented.

“Oh my God, it's probably around ten in the morning... What do I do now?”, I started crying again.

I was wearing my pajamas, and didn't really care about what others might be thinking about it, seeing me there, sitting and crying and sneezing all the time because I had slept outdoors.

I dried my tears, got up and ran to the chalet. No one was there. I quickly changed my clothes and ran to the boys' chalet. It was locked, so I thought that maybe Guilherme and Luísa were still there..

“Nic! Nic might be already here! He will help me out!”

I ran to the ward. It was easy to find him, even with the nurse not remembering his name, since it wasn’t normal to find people recovering from apendicitis in the campground.

I hurriedly opened the door to his room.

“Nic! Nic, are you there? I need...”

“I’ve been waiting for you, Paula”, Nic had a serious look in his face, which wasn’t his usual one.

“What... What happened, Nic?”, I stopped, holding the door handle.

He was sitting on the bed. Without looking at me, he said, softly:

“I heard what happened.”

I didn't say anything.

“Cláudio and João have been here. They told me you fought with Valéria. Paula, I... I'm so disappointed with you!”, now he was staring at me, but it would’ve been better if he’d have kept looking at the sheet. The expression on his face was disgusted, sad. Finally, I managed to say something:

“Disappointed?”

“Paula, why did you hit Valéria like that?”

“Nic, let me explain, I...”

“Paula, there's no explanation for this! You started arguing, and then you hit her, right when the others came back from the dancing club!”

I couldn't believe it. Valéria just distorted the whole story. No, that was not my friend, that was someone else.

“Nic, take it easy, things did not happen like that...”

“How did things happen then? Paula, how could you hit Valéria...?”

“Wait. Let me explain first, it seems like what they told you was wrong...”

“Paula, I know you were upset, but this is not the right way to relieve your stress!”

“Nic, she got me mad because I didn't want to agree with her. I thought that Luísa shouldn't have slept with Guilherme last night, and...”

“What?”, he said, surprised.

“Yes, that's it, they... didn't they tell you?”

“No... Are you saying that Guilherme...”

I nodded.

“Guilherme...”, Nic half smiled. But before I could continue speaking, he started again: “Paula, look, I swear I don’t understand why you fought.”

“Nic, let me finish it, she got me mad, I said something without thinking, she hit me and then...”

“Then you hit her back. I know all this, Paula! But I could understand if Valéria or Luísa ever did it, not you!”

“What?”

“You've always been the calm one!”

I don't know what was happening to me, I was extremely upset. When I heard Nic's last words, I just forgot to continue my explanations and exploded:

“Do you know why I’ve always been the calm one? Do you know why I always seemed that way? Nic, I can’t deal with an argument, I ran out of the chalet and spent the whole night beside the pool! I can't accept a normal problem like what happened to you, even if someone says it's not serious, Nic! I almost couldn't go on that damn slide!”

“Paula, it's not that! You think it is, but...”

“But what? If you want to say that I’m a fool, go ahead! That's okay, just say it! I know I am!”

“You've never been and never will be a fool or anything like that, I'm just saying that I never imagined you could fight with one of your best friends like that, Paula! I can't believe you could hurt Valéria!”

I hurt Valéria? What about all those things she told me, didn't they hurt me?

“Nic, you're wrong! I... I...”

Once again, I couldn't deal with the situation. I ran out from the room, crying, slamming the door.

“Paula, come back!”

I barely heard Nic yelling. At the ward's front door, I found Luísa and Guilherme, who had just arrived there.

“Paula, what happened, why...”

I didn't wait for Luísa to speak, I didn't answer her. I was distraught, and in a couple minutes I reached the chalet. It was empty. I closed the windows and locked myself in there.

Luísa looked at Guilherme.

“Nic!”, they both said at the same time, and ran to his room.

Since no one answered the door knocks, they entered.

“Where is he?”, the bed was empty.

Guilherme went to the bathroom, the door was half opened. Nic was under the cold shower water, with his head tilted up and his hands on the wall, still in his clothes and all.

“Nic, what happened?”, he asked.

“Nic!”, Luísa yelled, going to the bathroom. “Are you crazy? I heard the doctor saying that you needed to rest!”

“I can't believe it, I just can't believe it!”, Nic yelled, hitting the wall. By the way, it was his mark: hitting the first thing he could when he was upset.

“Nic, get out of there now!”, Guilherme pulled Nic out of the water. Luísa handed him a towel and the three of them sat on the bed.

“That's it. Now, could you please explain all this? Why are you so upset? Why did Paula leave crying?”

“Crying? Shit, I'm just so stupid...”, Nic seemed to be calmer.

“What happened, for God’s sake? Did you break up?”

“Yes, I mean, no... I don't know! Paula and Valéria had a fight last night, they hit each other, and I was mad at Paula because of it, but she was upset, she said that she was a fool, a lot of bullshit, and I just lost my patience, oh my God, I can't believe I did that!”, Nic was upset again, speaking loudly.

“That's not possible...”, the two still didn't know what had happened between the girls. “Nic, how could you do this, you love each other!”

“I don't know, she was really mad, she said what they told me was wrong, and I ended up not letting her explain herself!”

“I'll look for her”, Luísa said. “Nic, I promise you I'll fix all this, ok? Just don't leave this bed again!”

Luísa left running. Guilherme stayed awhile longer.

“Guilherme, is it true that you and Luísa...”, Nic said, lightly. Guilherme smiled, embarrassed because Nic was his girlfriend's older brother...

“Yes...”

“Luísa and Paula are so different... But Paula... I don't know, I'd never leave her for anyone like Luísa...”
