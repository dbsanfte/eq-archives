Why does the project need donations?
If you and your guild have used and enjoy EQdkp and want to see it continue to be developed, donating whatever money you can is a good way to show your appreciation for all of the work that's gone into the development and maintenance of the EQdkp project.
A lot of time went into creating EQdkp, and still goes into maintaining it by fixing bugs and adding requested features - in general, making it better. Hosting fees for EQdkp.com are now covered by the Google ads appearing on the EQdkp.com site and its forums, so any donations will go towards showing the developers appreciation for their time spent.
If you'd like to donate any amount of money, you can use the PayPal donate link below.
Thanks for your support!
