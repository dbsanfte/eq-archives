I know that in many cases its common practice to treat each encounter as a seperate raid. Becasue people come and go through out the corse of a dungeon. And you only want to give credit to those who are actualy there.

What I would sugest is a feature that allows you to either merge log files, or select several raids and merge them into a single event.
