#19 Nusoap.php class redefinition in php5 Core closed 1.3.0 defect critical 12/27/05

When I updated my php-version from php-4.3.10 to php-5.0.5 I noticed that opening my eqdkp-installation left me with a blank page, and apache returned: PHP Fatal error: Cannot redeclare class soapclient in ..../eqdkp/includes/nusoap.php

I later found out that the soapclient-class was now a built-in default-class in php5 and thus its redefinition by includes/nusoap.php caused the fatal error. On my installation I simply solved the problem by renaming the respective class-definition and its calls to soapclient2.
