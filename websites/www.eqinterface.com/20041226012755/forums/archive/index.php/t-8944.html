I know I like to be able to see target HP, my HP, and group HP all fairly close, and my mana and stamina right there as well. I like to see my stats without having to open up the Inventory, especially ATK and AC.

I haven't found much to alter about the UI with respect to hybrid classes. I've played a VS Beastlord almost exclusively (almost lv 40 now) for the entire year+ I've been playing.

Basically it comes down to effective targeting, like most other classes. Everything beyond that, I write social keys to handle the glut of the work (/pet sit before I go running out to pull being a good example). I think putting the casting and target bars together is a pretty common theme, and everything else is limited by the amount of information we can get from EQ regarding the pet's stats. Personally, what I would love (this is a wish-list item) would be an Effects Window for the pet!! Unfortunately we don't have the luxury, and pretty much all we can do is route the wear-off messages to a separate window for easier viewing amongst battle-spam.

Otherwise, it's pretty much as you've already said, enhanced player window pretty much covers it.
