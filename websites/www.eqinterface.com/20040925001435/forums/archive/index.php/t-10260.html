It is not possible. Most casters would definitely not want it to be possible. Think about it, you're a cleric, you've spent 50 or 60 levels finetuning your play, you know when you can afford to buff and when you have to save for emergency heals...and some idiot with a custom UI joins your group, and now he's bugging you:"C'mon, you have 20m, that's enough for a Symbol! You're just being cheap."

Keep in mind that most casters fudge their mana reports, depending on the group they're with, or just give them infrequently or with explanations. This is because most melees and hybrids don't understand that 40m can be a near-emergency situation and that if your cleric or chanter drops to 20m the next mistake could kill someone.

And hybrids...nobody needs to know a hybrid's mana! Only the player himself knows when it's appropriate to cast and when he should melee, when he should pull with spells and when with arrows, whether he can be the group's secondary Sower/buffer/healer or whether he'll have to concentrate on pure damage. Hybrids especially vary wildly in play technique, and the last thing they need is someone bugging them to "play like the last ranger I grouped with."
