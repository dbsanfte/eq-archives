Sounds like it COULD be proper tinting? Without this feature turned on, it often causes skin to be dyed along with armor.

Try this in game:
type /dopropertinting on

The game will give you a message that this function will be on the next time you zone. However, I generally find that you have to relog for it to actually take effect.

I hope that's what you need. If not, good luck figuring it out!
