Auto-Equip is Hard coded to be in the Inventory window only.

Hot Buttons are ALSO Hard coded to be in the hot button window only.

Therefore making it impossible to combine hot keys with autoequip unless VI decides to make this possible.

HOWEVER, I made a wee little inventory mod to go with the Quartz UI that tosses all of your inventory out to other windows and making a nice Auto-Euip box on your main screen. You can find it here.
