1. type /Loads (the loadskin menu will open)

2. Select Default and make sure USE INI is depressed then press Load Skin

3. move the window

4. type /Loads (the loadskin menu will open)

5. Select your previous UI and make sure USE INI is depressed then press Load Skin

Another thing you can try in game is to set your mousespeed to 0 with the "/mousespeed 0" command, then try to grab the window in a place where there are no buttons and such blocking the background.
Most borderless windows will have a few pixles of area around the edge, or between buttons, where you can click and drag them. Your mouse pointer won't change to the drag icon untill you click.

I use a borderless casting bar in my 640x480 UI, and 1 pixle wide borders on the rest of the windows of that UI. It can be difficult to find a place to click and drag them if the mousespeed is higher than 0, heh. :)
