I downloaded the new version (I had an older version) and spent the day yesterday playing with it. It's working perfectly (or was before the patch anyway. I have yet to log on to see if it's still working)

I have several mods installed to my basic Quartz and wanted to move them over to the right sided version as well. By the time I called it a night last night everything was looking pretty good.

For cosmetic purposes, I use a combination of windows pieces from Vert's 2.2 Blue and the Icey Blue skin. I also replaced the T.King spellbook and icons with the Vert and Icey Blue versions. I just like the nice clean look of them. They're easier on these tired old eyes.

I've added the EQTradeskill Recipes to the Friends window, the EQTradskills Location Guide to the BugReport window. EQAtlas to my Help window and the EQ Location mod to the Feedback window.

In order to use the EQAtlas you need to be able to toggle the Help window, so I moved the Help button back to upper corner of the Selection window, along with the Friends and Actions buttons.

Playing a Shammy, Cleric and Druid I have a few additional right clickie items (Legs, BP, earring and bracer) so I added those items to the Selection window.

I resized and moved the inventory slots in both the HotButtons and Selector windows around a bit to make them more convenient for me. I also put the Hotbuttons back to the same layout as the orignal Quartz.

And last but not least, I resized the SpellGem window to allow me to put the compass underneath and tweaked the locations of the buttons on the PetInfo window.

Except for the horizontal buff bar that I have sitting above the main chat window, it all fits nice and neatly now within a 1280 750 viewport.

Thanks for all the work you put into this great mod. The Quartz skin is a great foundation and you did a great job with the changes you made. I for one an in your debt. Much as I loved the original I was feeling like I was working backwards :P

Thanks again!!
