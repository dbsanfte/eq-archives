I have a suggestion on those pesky locked windows problems.

1. Set up the rest of your windows where you want them, then leave room where you want the "locked" window...in this case the pet window.

2. When you have everything pretty much where you want it, /load default 1. DO NOT FORGET THE "1" to keep everything else where it's supposed to be.

3. In the default UI, move the pet window where you want it.

4. When you think you have it, /load (skinname) 1. Again, keep everything in place with the "1"

When you go back to your UI, the pet window will of course have moved. And it may take a couple tries to get it exactly where you want it. DO NOT FORGET THE "1"
