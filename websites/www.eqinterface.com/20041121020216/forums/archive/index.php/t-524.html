Some of you probably already know about this, but if you're constantly logging in and out of EQ to alter your mods, you can run EQ directly without going through the patch program.

Just open the run program dialogue from the start menu, then type "C:\Program Files\EverQuest\eqgame.exe" patchme

This makes logging in and out of EQ nearly twice as fast for me, since the patch program takes a loooong time to scan my files.

If you want to make it even easier, just make a shortcut to Eqgame.exe on your desktop or whatever, then right click and select properties, then add patchme to the end of the command string. Remember to put a space between the ending quotes and patchme, and don't put a - in front of it.
