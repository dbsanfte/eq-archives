INSTALLATION:

This UI's specificity is it is custom built to fit in perfectly in 3 resolutions: 1024x768, 1280x1024 and 1600x1200.
I am also providing the UI_YourCharacterName_XX.ini files, so you don't even have to move around windows, choose fonts, place hitmodes, etc.
EVERYTHING is already in perfect position to produce (I hope) the most convenient and readable outlook.
Therefore a bit of preparation is required:

If necessary:
0.1 launch EQ and switch to the desired resolution
0.2 exit to desktop

Install:
1. Download the UIGrygan.zip file
2. Create a subdirectory of your Everquest\uifiles named grygan
3. Extract all the file inside UIGrygan.zip to this directory
4. Inside the Everquest\uifiles\grygan directory, find the file UI_Grygan_41_1024.ini (or 1280 or 1600 depending on your desired resolution)
5. Fopy this file to your base Everquest directory
6. Find your own UI_Your CharacterName_XX.ini file, and rename it UI_YourCharacterName_XX.bak (just in case)
7. Rename my UI_Grygan_41_1024.ini into UI_YourCharacterName_XX.ini
8. Open your eqclient.ini file and change the line NewUI=default (or whatever old mod you were using) into NewUI=grygan
9. Launch EQ and enjoy!

This could be of interest to all you Melee but non-Monks players:

If you want the same Player Window, but replace the Weight by the STRength for example, you have to open EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml an do a little bit of editing in the <Label item = "STRNumb"> section.
Simply replace <EQType>24</EQtype> by <EQType>5</EQtype> and that should do the trick!

Now, if you want a nice little STRength icon, just download the attached window_pieces06.tga file and overwrite the one on your grygan folder...
