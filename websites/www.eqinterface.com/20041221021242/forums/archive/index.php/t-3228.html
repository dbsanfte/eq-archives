News - 10/22/02:

- UI changes in the 10/21 Patch
Both EQUI.xml and EQUI_Animations.xml were changed in the 10/21 patch; Cormanth and Marcin have posted the changes to the EQUI_Animations.xml file here (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3182), and here (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3192). Both threads provide the details from updating your file manually. Cormanth has also include the 1-line change to EQUI.xml in his post (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3182).
I'm curious to see what effect the patch has on existing UI's and mods... and what mod developers will do with the new windows!

Thomus King (http://pub10.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10) has posted a screenshot of his new Celtic Spellbar.

Remelio (http://pub165.ezboard.com/ftranscendence39908frm31) has updated his EQUI.xml (http://pub165.ezboard.com/ftranscendence39908frm31.showMessage?topicID=38.topic) and EQUI_Animations.xml (http://pub165.ezboard.com/ftranscendence39908frm31.showMessage?topicID=47.topic) patches for T.King/Remelio mod users to reflect the 10/21 changes.

Sokol has updated four files to reflect the 10/16 patch: the Advanced CastSpell WIndow (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=263), a combo T.King Spellbook/Spellbar (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=268) file, and standalone versions of the T.King Spellbook (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=259) and T.King Spellbar (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=267).
