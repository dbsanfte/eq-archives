Originally posted by Dreamlife
Plus how does one make it available for download.

Easy! Go to the Downloads (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?s=) area, choose the Category you want to upload it to (like Hotkeys (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=18&sortby=date&sortorder=DESC) or Player Info (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=20&sortby=date&sortorder=DESC) or Target Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=24&sortby=date&sortorder=DESC) or wherever you think it best fits), and click the "Upload" button. You'll want a screenshot and description ready to go, and just follow the instructions!
