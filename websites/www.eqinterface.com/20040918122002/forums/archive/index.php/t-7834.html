This works for 1280x1024 and 1024x768 resolutions, like the original Soes. As usual, rename the .ini file for the resolution you want, then copy to your main EQ directory.

On the player window, I removed the breathe gauge as the aquamarine/yellow of the stamina bar made it hard to read. There was also the breathe window that already had the breathe gauge.
