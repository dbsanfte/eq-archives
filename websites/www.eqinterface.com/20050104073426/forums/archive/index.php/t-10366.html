The real simpliest way to make it, is by the program that comes with all Windows OS's... Paint. Just make your signature and make sure that it is to the sites signature requirement (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=593). Just practice with screen shots and cut and paste and before you know it.. you made a signature. But mind you, don't expect the mindblowing looks compared to those who make signatures using Photoshop and other high end graphic editing programs. Good luck!

Here's an example of what I did in about a few minutes using the Paint program that came with Windows. Its not spectacular and won't win any awards, but its a signature nonetheless! Click Here. (http://easypichost.com/Zantaklawz/ZantaklawzVasha2.JPG )
