Here is the error:
[Wed Jan 28 13:26:34 2004] [Line:948 Source:UIFiles\Delta3\EQUI_Inventory.xml] Schema error - Duplicate item XP_Percent

Open your EQUI_Inventory.xml file in notepad.
Go to Edit > Replace.
In the Find What field type XP_Percent.
In the Replace With field type IW_XP_Percent.
Click Replace All.
Save the file.
