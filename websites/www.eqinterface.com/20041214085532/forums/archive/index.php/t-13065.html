Design Theory
Include the most commonly needed information in as few windows as possible, leaving more horizontal space for a full-screen view. Elements should be easy to read and use.

Features
*General; horizonal windows to preserve space and make for easy stacking.
*Target window indicates /consider color and integrates casting time meter.
*Group window includes health for all party members, including self. Pet Health show in green. (Self-bar does not have a pet health meter. That's in the Pet Window.)
*Pet Window is tabbed, making showing the pet buffs easy and still giving access to most common commands. Pet name field large enough for long warder names!
*Hot Buttons Winodw includes 8 inventory (bag) slots as clickies.
*Player Window displays Endurance, Mana, Exp and AA Exp. Also shows Stats, Combat Stats, Resists & clicky slots for Primary, Secondary, Range and Ammo slots.

Known Issues
* Long buff or song names don't fit into the label space well.
* Large (5 characters+) HP values look cramped.
* Hot Button window has empty space along the bottom
