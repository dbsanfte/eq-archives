I really like this spellbook, makes spells easier to find and switch between. I just had a couple problems with this spellbook. It was hard to read the spell labels. What I found work best was to open the .tga files in photoshop and decrease the brightness and contrast. Now this may just be because i play at a hi gamma correction, but i found this helped a lot.

I also found that there wasn't enough room for the longer pet spell names. At level 60 all the names are like Great Voceration:Earth. That just didn't fit in the space between the elemental icons, so I moved the labels of the top 2 to above the icon. I also center justified them and increased the font size.

Other than that i really love this mod.

Great work on the spellbook, thanks for sharing it with us.
