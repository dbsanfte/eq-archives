This mod features spheres under the % symbols of Player HP, Mana, and Pet HP - the indicators will display as follows
100% - 75% - Full Green (Shown above in Mana)
75% - 50% - Part Yellow, Part Green
50% - 25% - Part Flashing Red, Part Yellow (Shown above in HP)
0% - 25% - Full Flashing Red

Group window has been converted into combined group/player window
Group Window information is as follows:
HP / Max HP ...... AC
Player Gauge, Player %
Mana Gauge, Mana %
Thin Stamina Bar
Pet Name
Pet Gauge, Pet %
Group Member 1 Gauge, Group Member 1 %
Thin Group Member 1 Pet Gauge
Group Member 2 Gauge, Group Member 2 %
Thin Group Member 2 Pet Gauge
Group Member 3 Gauge, Group Member 3 %
Thin Group Member 3 Pet Gauge
Group Member 4 Gauge, Group Member 4 %
Thin Group Member 4 Pet Gauge
Group Member 5 Gauge, Group Member 5 %
Thin Group Member 5 Pet Gauge
Normal XP Gauge, Normal XP %
Alt XP Gauge, Alt XP %

The player window has been converted into the long target window - now when you hit autoattack the target window will flash. The % under the target also color codes.

The Target window is now a quick target window, showing F keys for group members, a sphere gauge showing how healthy they are, and the group members name - it also displays you up top.

Feedback on this mod is greatly appreciated.

Enjoy!
