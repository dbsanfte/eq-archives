01-09-2004, 07:38 AM
You will see a filter section called Melee which will open a new window where you can choose which melee text you want to go to the new window. You will have to repeat the process a few times to get what you want in the window. Plus there is a filter which you can also choose how you want the text to be (i.e., Your misses: Abbreviated, Number only, or Full [or whatever its called] so your text during combat will look like this:

Full: You pierced the mist wolf and do 130 point of damage
Abbreviated: You damage the mist wolf for 130 points of damage.
Number only: You do 130 points of damage.

Same thing for misses:

Full: You attack the mist wolf but miss.
Abbreviated: You miss the mist wolf.
Miss Only: Miss

This feature can also be applied to others attacks including the creatures.
