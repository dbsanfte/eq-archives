Okay, Kathos had it backwards. With both Kenny and Kathos help was I able to figure this out. Thanks to both of you for posting help.

What I had to do was find this line...

<style_sizable>true</style_sizable>

It is at the bottom under...

<Screen item="PetInfoWindow">

Then change it to false. No re-sizing, just change to false. It is fixed and works great.
