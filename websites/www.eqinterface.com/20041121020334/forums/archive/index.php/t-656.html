Look back in the EQUI_SpellBookWnd.xml:

This is what I did - Look between the <font>
and <RelativePosition>

Just insert the TextColor section and change however you want, of course mine shows blue :)

Do this for EVERY <SBW_SpellName> number 0 - 15


<StaticText item ="SBW_SpellName0">
<ScreenID>SBW_SpellName0</ScreenID>
<Font>1</Font>
<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>255</B>
</TextColor>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
