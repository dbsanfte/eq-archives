To install these PlayerWindows on your computer, download the desired files above by right clicking on that link and clicking "Save Target As" then save it to your desktop. Once you have it saved do the following:

1. Go to your C Program Files\EverQuest\UIFiles\ (or wherever EQ is installed)
2. Make a copy of the "default" directory and rename it "caster"
3. Copy the files you just downloaded into the "caster" directory
4. Load EverQuest
5. Once in-game type "/load caster 1

If you want to have two different UI's to switch between, say one for caster and one for melee, just make two new directories, once called "melee" the other called "caster" and copy the appropriate .xml files to each. Then once in the game you can type /load <directory name> 1 to load the desired UI.
