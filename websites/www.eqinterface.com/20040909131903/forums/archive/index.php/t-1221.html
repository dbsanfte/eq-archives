xlorepdarkhelm
08-22-2002, 08:03 AM
In order to have the compass work, there is 2 different things that must happen.

1) it must be in the upper left corner of the window.

2) it must conform to the correct width, or else it starts to look funny.

As such, all you can do really is stretch the compass height, and add things under it, but without increasing the width of the window (which the compass will take up the full width of the window).
