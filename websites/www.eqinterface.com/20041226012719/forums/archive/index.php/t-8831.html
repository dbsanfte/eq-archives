Yes you can edit the image in Photoshop 7.
As for the alpha channel.. you can select the pieces in the image you want to use, and go to the drop down Selection menu (if it's there in 7- it is in Photoshop 5) , and press save selection. A box will pop open with a small menu inside scroll down to Alpha1 if you have edited the image, and then choose replace. If it's a completely new piece you have made then there will be no alpha channel so just select "new" and it will become an alpha channel.
