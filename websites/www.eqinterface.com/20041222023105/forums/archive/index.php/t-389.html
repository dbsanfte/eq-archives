This (http://www.eqinterface.com/forums/showthread.php?t=2267) thread lists what cannot be done in a UI.
There are two notable exceptions coming with the patch on 14 July 04:
1 - Mana (yours) will be able to be shown as a number (both current and total, just like HPs)
2 - Endurance (yours) will be able to be shown as a number (both current and total, just like HPs)
As always, EQ evolves and in the future what you are requesting may be available. Unfortunately it is not available currently.
