Here's my first public release of a UI mod. I created this a LONG time ago, and completely forgot about it not being normal.. until I saw a normal hotbutton window!

1) Micron. Button size is reduced.

2) Micron! Title bar and page buttons and indicator are gone (use shift-number to change pages)

3) Micron?!? No graphics, no textures, no nuttin, just button. Rounded frame used since it looked "best" to me, ten floating buttons was just too weird.

Download the XML from

http://www.tertiary.net/Torvus/Kokutest/EQUI_HotButtonWnd.xml

It's been fairly patch resistant, I built it before they retired the old UI, and haven't had to modify it at all.

Please post more Micronized windows!
