I've been working on making some more animated windows. Not by putting animations into windows but by animating the borders, right in the WDT. I've used the help window to test my creation.

First I doubled the size of the title bar so there's more room to work with. Besides changing the graphics for the three parts of the title bar itself, that also means changing the border at the top left, left top, top right and right top so things line up.

Then I changed the minimize button to a dancing character and the close button to a spinning skull. The pictures don't do it justice, they really move! You'll have to excuse the lack of transparency on the graphics, this is just pre-beta.

Finally I added a pop-up surprise on the right side. It looks like a normal window most of the time (top picture), but every few minutes a little surprise animation pops out. Sorry folks, you get the edited version. Changing the width of the right side means changing the right bottom, bottom right, right top and top right borders as well, to make things line up.

The ability to use animations for every part of a window instead of just static graphics was one of the first things touted for the new interface, but nobody seems to take advantage of it.

Anyone else working on this sort of thing? Have any good ideas for other animations you'd like to see? Done right, this sort of animation could really make windows come alive.
