Actually, I think you won't, at least not right now. You can't put non-"EQType" items in other windows. Since the actual timers and progress items aren't "EQType" items, they work only in EQUI_AdventureRequestWnd.xml. This is the same reason you can't put auto-inventory drop boxes in places other than your inventory window too.
