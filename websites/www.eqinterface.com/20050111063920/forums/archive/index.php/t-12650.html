There's various threads around here talking about files getting corrupted.

The piece for me that always got corrupted after logging in a few times was the Random graphic button for my custom MP3. It would only happen though when I had texture turn off on the player though. If I kept the background texture on, it never happens. Very weird bug.

I always fixed it by keeping another copy of my UI_Name_server.ini and copying the good copy over again (and again and again and again... it was that consistent for me). Or you can cut and paste only the section concerning the botched up item in its appropriate place in your .ini file.

Very annoying when it keeps happening consistently too.
