The errors in your first post are saying Everquest cant find the interface files in the location you told it to look. This could be becuase...

1. You have a folder inside another folder when you unziped the files. You can see a picture in our FAQ that explains this.

2. You have a space in the name name of your custom folder. You can't use spaces when nameing your folder for the custom interface.

3. You could have more then one everquest folder due to a buggy install script on a couple of the EQ Expansion packs. You need to find the right one.
