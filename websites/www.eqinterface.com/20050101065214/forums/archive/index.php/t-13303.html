Scorrpio
03-30-2004, 10:50 PM
Since the background images are rounded at one end, flipping the buff.bmp file is not enough.

First, buff.bmp has to be converted to tga, with all pink replaced with transparent. In Photohop, that would be creating a new 256x64 transparent canvas, pasting buff.bmp contents into it, selecting all pink with magic wand, deleting it, and then saving the whole thing as a .tga.

In EQUI_Animations.xml, change TextureItem buff.bmp to buff.tga, and change BlueIconBackground/RedIconBackground entries to use buff.tga as well. That should do it.
