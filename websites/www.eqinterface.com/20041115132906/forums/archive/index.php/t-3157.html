About the Spellbook

I like the idea of the old spellbook's arrangement of the spells. Instead of the same 'grid' of 2x4 spells on each page it had spells arranged in 'clusters'.

WE can NOT have 5 spells per page and we can not have a different spell arrangement on each page.

What I would like to do is just 'break' the boring grid arrangement to create a pattern thats easier to remember
let's say for example

O......O
....O....
O......O
...........
O..O..O

(so like the dots on the 5 of a dice then 3 in a row)

So you might like to have your cure / heal spells in the last row, a big blast spell in the center slot, etc etc. the idea is that the arrangement helps to remember where you put your spells and arrnage them by groups, visually.

Let me know what you think

The spellbook you can see on the screenshot here has NOT been modified at all. It just has the default window 'frame' that comes with all windows in this UI.

PS:the idea with the spell slots is that the 'halo' looks like a little light inside the slot.. it's subtle, I hope that shows..
