  Depths of Darkhollow
Posted By: Dolby - 09-13-2005 12:35 PM - Replies (0)
Today the Everquest expansion Depths of Darkhollow of has been released. This expansion many new features and interface changes. You can read the patch notes to get an idea of what has changed with this new expansion.

The interface has not changed much since the beta but you can view a new compare in the patch forum here. Those people with a custom EQUI.xml will need to add a few new lines to be able to open some new windows. Those with custom Hot button windows, Inventory windows, AA windows and Big Bank windows will need to either delete those windows or update them using the compare I provided.

Please allow interface authors a couple weeks to update their interface files. Post's asking if their is going to be an update to their interface will be deleted.

Please stay tuned to this thread as any quick fixes will be posted here.

  Katrina Devastation
Posted By: Dolby - 09-01-2005 11:40 AM - Replies (2)
I just want to take this time to say that our hearts are out to all those effect by this devastating hurricane. I've been watching the coverage via cnn/msnbc/fox and cant even fathom having to deal with seeing or being effected by the destruction that hurricane has caused in real life. I'm sure its even worse then how it looks on TV.

I want to urge those that can help to visit the red cross or salvation army website and give what you can. They are going to need a lot of help.

9/2/05 Update - News from SOE
Quote:
Hurricane Katrina has left an incredible wake of destruction across the Mid-Atlantic States. Sony Online Entertainment would like to ask you to take a moment to make a donation to the American Red Cross in order to help alleviate some of the suffering.

In EverQuest II we have added the /donate command, type in /donate to be taken to the American Red Cross뭩 Hurricane 2005 relief page. For those members of SOE Communities in other games, you can contribute using this link, American Red Cross. The American Red Cross will process your donation and make sure that it is put to maximum use to help those whose lives have been affected by the hurricane.

Additionally, for our 13,000+ players actually in the affected areas, we will be suspending billing until such time as they are able to play again. In addition, any items or structures in any of our games, which decay over time, will be preserved until the user뭩 next login.

Thank you in advance for your support and generosity.

The SOE Family

  Email Notification of updated favorites
Posted By: Dolby - 08-14-2005 04:15 AM - Replies (1)
I have added a new option to our Downloads area. You can now be notified of updates to interfaces in your favorites list via email.

To enable this option go to the front page of eqinterface.com and once you are logged in click the Download UserCP menu option (top left) then choose preferences and you'll see the option to enable email notification. You will then be notified of updates to your favorite interfaces as soon as they are approved.

If you enable this feature the email that is sent to you looks something like this...
Quote:
Hello {your name},

EQInterface would like to inform you that one of the interface/mods you have chosen as a favorite has been updated. This email was sent because you enabled email updates for your favorites.


===================================
{Interface Name}
----------------------------------
Author: {Author's Name}
Version: {Version of mod}

View file info link:
{Link to the file info of the mod}

Direct download link:
{Direct download link to the mod}
===================================
