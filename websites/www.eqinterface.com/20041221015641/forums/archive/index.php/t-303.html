As far as I know, with the current implementation the answer is no. We do not have the ability to add buttons, or even to remove them (i think) because of the way the UI has been coded. Only the original button as it is placed in the default UI (meaning, in that window) will work. And, I believe, attempting to remove a button will also fail, you must leave at least the definition which includes the ScreenType.

As for your problem, make your hotkey a short macro:

/gsay Laying hands on %T!!
/pause 5
/doability X

Adjust the pause how you like it .. and if you didn't mean to hit it duck before that pause is up. You cannot lay hands while ducking.

Yes, it is a short pause. But you already admit to a pause in your current use :) That's the method I chose to save my lay hands when I played a pally though.
