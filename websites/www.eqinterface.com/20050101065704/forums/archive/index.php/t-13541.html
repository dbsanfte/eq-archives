I have this problem. I don't know exactly what causes it.

I was reminded of the older problem of having pink buff borders, or similar. Remembering that flipping your buff BMP vertically was suppose to fix (sorta) that, I decided it was worth a shot here.

It seems to work.

My buff window is custom, I don't even know its origin, but it comes with its own buff graphics bmp called buff.bmp. I flipped it vertically (aka, "across the horizontal axis") and it worked.... The default UI has a buff icon BMP similar in function to this, and custom UIs probably have their own. Try using the "pink buff icon borders" fixes or flipping the image yourself. I used MS Paint.
