The round windows are part of a new mod being developed called "Techno Donuts" and are still a little buggy. the animal skin/spellbook is mine, the buff window is from eqinterface.com. the spell icons are being developed, and almost completed (many are still duplicated versions, the guy is making unique ones instead). The maps are actually my "Actions" window, and were released by eqtoolbox.not.net - so far, I've found a map that isn't included (Umbral Plains) but it has most likely every other one in the game. And yes, the guy in the middle of my inventory rotates. (I usually don't have the inventory, spellbook, and maps up on the screen lol)

I have modified the interface a bit, by making the hotkey buttons round, to match the rest of the interface, and have redesigned the spell gem window to be a simple round window with the gems around the edges of the window. The gems go in 2 columns, from top to bottom. 1-4 on the left, and 5-8 on the right. The spell book icon is in the center, and the spell casting bar appears just under the window when you are casting something (not actually connected to the window)

------
To make my hitsmode "window":


First I make a large "background" chat box

then I have 4 seperate windows listed there. 1 for my hits, 1 for me getting hit, 1 for others hits. I also have 1 on top that is all the "garbage" messages I didn't want cluttering up my main chat. I have the me and my boxes at font size 3, the others at font size 1, and the "garbage" at 2. I then position them on the background chat window, and make all the windows except the background have their background alphas set to 0, thus making them permanently invisible, leaving only the text. Thus, I am showing 1 nice, neat window there instead of 4 bulky, cluttered ones.


(note - the background window *must* be the first window made out of the set, or else when you zone, log, etc it WILL appear on top of the others, and effectively hide them)

right click on window, and select "*Lock" to unlock the window positions. I don't like to accidentally move my windows around. Be warned - moving the chat windows is a bit tricky, I have hitsmode windows all over the smaller visible window. Plus, with the lack of a title bar, you have to move the chat windows by "resizing them into position" (minor flaw in the UI, IMHO). And removing a chat window from my display requires loading in the default UI, and then closing the chat window you want.

Also, type ALT-W for the window selector. I like it hidden, but it is there, sitting on top of the main chat.

If you wanna start from scratch on the chat windows, I would recommend not using my UI_Oguler_31.ini, and rather just starting fresh & new. putting the windows into their respective corners (and sides) isn't all that hard.
