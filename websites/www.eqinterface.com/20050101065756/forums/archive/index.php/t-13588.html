If the window is too small and the Yes/No buttons are cut off the bottom, all you need to do is edit your UI_character_server.ini file, removing any Height or Width tags in [PopDialog0] and any other [PopDialog*] sections. The next time you get a confirmation box, it will show up the proper size and you will have your buttons back.

This is the effect of a bug with confirmation windows.
