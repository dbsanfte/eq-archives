In a way. What you can do is take a window you don't really use much (feedback, friends, whatever) and drop the Player Window information into it. You need to remember to give everything a new name, so you don't get errors, ie: if it was originally PlayerGauge.xml, you would name it FW_PlayerGauge.xml (FW for Friend Window). The "new" player window won't have the flashing red border around it when you have attack on, but otherwise, it should all work fine.
