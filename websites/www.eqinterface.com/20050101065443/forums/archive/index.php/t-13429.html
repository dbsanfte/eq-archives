First of all, have you tried the info in the FAQ??

http://www.eqinterface.com/modules.php?op=modload&name=FAQ&file=index&myfaq=yes&id_cat=3&categories=Installing+Interfaces

Second, the date of the UI's last update has nothing to do with why you can't see it listed in EQ.

Third, are you sure you only have EQ installed in one location on your computer?? Right click the icon you use to start EQ, and select properties from the menu that pops up. On the shortcut tab, what does it say for target. Depending on what version of Windows you're using, the menu you get when you right click the icon may look different. Essentially, find out which file the icon is pointing to.

Once you find that out, does the path you find look similar to where you're putting your UI file?? If not, then you probably have EQ installed (or partially installed) in more than one location.
