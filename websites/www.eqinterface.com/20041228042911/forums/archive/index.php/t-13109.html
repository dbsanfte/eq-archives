It looks as though S.O.E. has some activities planned to celebrate. The following is posted on the Everquestlive.com site:

EverQuest Celebrates Five Years. Join us for the 5th Anniversary in-game celebrations for EverQuest. Starting next week, and over the course of the next few weeks, you will be able to join in a massive scavenger hunt, battle fabled NPCs, experience enormous firework displays and defend your city against armies of skeletons.
- Scavenger Hunt: What will be the prize?
- Fabled NPCs: They're back! They're buff! Are you ready?
- Fireworks display: Lighting up the sky in celebration!
- Skeletons Marching: They're marching, and they are angry!

You can also find some interesting facts at GameSpot:

Over 2.5 million copies of the game and its expansions have been sold in box or digitally downloaded since 1999.

If stacked end to end, the total number of EverQuest boxes created would reach a height of more than 265 miles, which would stretch out of the Earth's atmosphere and past the orbit of the International Space Station.

EverQuest averages about 250,000 new subscribers every year. Currently, there are more than 420,000 people playing the game.
