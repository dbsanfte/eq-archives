I am currently Creating my own Player Window so that is cna monitor all my stats and saves wieght breath meter exp AA every thing that has to do with the numbers


I also have a working tag for the Last name

Here it is


<Label item = "Player_LName">
<ScreenID>LName</ScreenID>
<EQType>73</EQType>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>130</X>
<Y>35</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>150</CX>
<CY>12</CY>
</Size>
<Text>Plater123456</Text>
<TextColor>
<R>255</R>
<G>255</G>
<B>255</B>
</TextColor>
<NoWrap>true</NoWrap>
<AlignCenter>false</AlignCenter>
<AlignRight>false</AlignRight>
</Label>



Of course your going to have to change the LOC of the label.. I have the Title and the First Name Comming before the Surname

The Y is so high because i have the Diety Name(Right Above) and the Level and Class (Above Diety) above it.

and be sure to add

<Pieces>Player_LName</Pieces>

at the end of your xml file.
