This is my custom UI that i use. It is a mix of me taking some of the default .xml files and editing them so they are as small as possible and also other people's ui mods. I have edited each one at least a little bit, i think.

Includes:
*T.king artwork (buff icons, gem icons, spell icons) because his work is the best.
*modified t.king druidish inventory with charm slot (which i think he has already made himself anyway)
*compass window with the loc thing in it by someone else, streached it to be the same size as my target and group window.
*target and group window with % made by someone else, but shrunk as much as possible in size by me
*default hot buttons, but with title bar removed and excess space removed. i made the page left/right buttons a little bigger since there was room.
*self/player window from someone else, the really big one with tons of stats. personally, i don't really care about the stats as they are FAIRLY staic. i did leave all the resists in because they are small and can be VERY important/interesting on raids.
*bags... i think i modified them some to make em smaller cause that was in the dir. been a while though...
*chat boxes have no titles and very small borders. i hate wasted space.
*buff and short buff. i used someone else's buff box, but then when the short duration buff box came out i made it identical to the normal buff box. the short duration buff box is entirely done by me!
*pet window (not shown) was shrunk as much as possible and a yellow % for pet was added.
*selector window is as small as i feel possible. you really have to work hard to move it (by clicking between the icons).
