Etasi UI v2 (created primarily for the 1024x768 resolution).

The zip is too large to be attached to a post, but will be available in the complete sets section as soon as it's approved by the moderators.

All XML and graphics are my own work (except for the slider timer pictured, which is by Arista). I think the screenshot is pretty self explanatory:

- cast gauge under the (larger) target bar
- two tabbed pet window with stats on one page and pet stuff on the other
- two tabs of equipment/inventory slots on the right side of the hotkey window
- an invisible button for the spellbook window underneath the eighth gem on the spell gem bar
- transparent (i.e. all you can see is the text) Follow and Disband buttons that pop up when you get invited to a group
- a matching skin (named Friends_Timer.tga in the zip) for Arista's great slider timer mod
- a folder containing versions of the group window, player window, hotbutton window, and inventory windows without the circle pattern background (instead they use the background shown in the chat windows) in case you don't like the pattern

The spell icons pictured in the screenshot aren't included in the zip, but will probably be made available for download from this site sometime soon.

This UI is all up to date with the latest patches and hardly ever needs to be updated. The only time I ever have to change anything is when something is added to one of the windows that I've modified (like the charm slot, for instance). Changes to EQUI.xml, EQUI_Animations.xml, and EQUI_Templates.xml do not affect this UI.

Enjoy!
