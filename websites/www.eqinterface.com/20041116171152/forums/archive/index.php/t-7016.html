I worked up a few group windows and a player and target window at the request of some friends, just thought I would share...

For each XML file just right click and choose "Save Target As..."

Target window with percent
Very simple, just adds a percent number to the health of your Target.
http://www.probablynot.com/ui/target.jpg
EQUI_TargetWindow.xml (www.probablynot.com/ui/EQUI_TargetWindow.xml)

Player window with percents and exp
Again, simple. Adds percents to the health and mana, as well as shows Exp and AAExp with percents.
http://www.probablynot.com/ui/player.jpg
EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml (www.probablynot.com/ui/EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml newwindow)

Group Window with Percents
Normal Group Window with Percents on Player health. Screenshot not updated, but does include LFG Tool button.
http://www.probablynot.com/ui/groupvertp/groupvert.jpg
EQUI_GroupWindow.xml (www.probablynot.com/ui/groupvertp/EQUI_GroupWindow.xml)

Horizontal Group Window
This is just a group window that is horizontal instead of vertical. Screenshot not updated, but does include LFG Tool button.
http://www.probablynot.com/ui/grouphoriz.jpg
EQUI_GroupWindow.xml (www.probablynot.com/ui/EQUI_GroupWindow.xml newwindow)
