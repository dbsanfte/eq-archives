Well this is a beta release of two Q graphical mods, BlueQ and GreenQ. They came about through my continued playing of warcraft 3 and the like of the gauges they used, so i made my own. These are graphic mods basically, the xml files were included to maintain continuality with the windows.

Blue is humans, Green is nightelves, orcs and undead arn't quite finished yet.

http://members.optusnet.com.au/mjb2k/EQ/GreenQ.zip

Standard XML mods should interact fine with these, just some words of warning, the guages are larger than standard 14 heigh, and the boarders in the inventory are actually seperate graphics.
