To fix the no 3D Target Ring in your custom UI. Copy:

TargetIndicator.ini
TargetIndicator.tga

to your custom UI folder. This copy should be done with EQ closed and camped out. A /loadskin will not bring up the ring if the files are copied during a play session. EQ loads these files once, and that is with the load of your first character, thereafter it relies upon what is in memory. I found this out by looking in a new error log file called texture.txt. Make sure you have 'Show 3D Target Ring' turned on in your options too.
