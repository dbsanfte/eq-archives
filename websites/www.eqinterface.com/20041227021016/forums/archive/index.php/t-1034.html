Set the gauge text y offset to -50.

It will try to write it outside the gague bounds and it won't be displayed. This is what the default skin does for certain gauges.

Now, some headaches.

Note that the coordinates of the window are relative to its left top corner. The playerHP gauge has a height of 41 (the corresponds to 15 each for 2 rows of text, a three pixel space and 8 for the gauge height). If you make the text go away, you will need to do one of two things:

(1) put different text there

(2) (a) resize the playerHP gauge to be 8 tall,
(b) reset the <y offset> in the gauge to 0 and then
(c) move every piece in your gauge up 33 pixels.
