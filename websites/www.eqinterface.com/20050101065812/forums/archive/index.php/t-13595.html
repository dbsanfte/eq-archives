Originally posted by Vemanos
I cant do this cant get it to work so I do have Photoshop but I dont now how to make it work..


You will have to take a screenshot of your character in the pose that you want to work with. (Hint: Try to do it in a zone that gives you a LIGHT colored backdrop)

Next you will have to use Paintshop to remove the background and basicaly cut out your character. (Hint: Try the eraser or the Magic Wand tool to select the background and delete it.)

Once you have your toon, select it and "Copy" it from the Edit menu.

Now open the Background you want, and Paste your toon into it. Now move the toon till you have it right were you want it and then resave the background and your done. (HINT: once you have done all that work to cut out your toon, Please save it by itself some were on you computer for future uses.)

Hope this helps ya, Let me know.
