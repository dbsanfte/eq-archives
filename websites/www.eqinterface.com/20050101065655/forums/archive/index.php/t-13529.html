What you could do was create a social and bind it to a hotkey. Any sequence of up to five /commands can be programmed in a social, which can then be activated either by putting the social on a hotkey or by pressing the button in the socials pane.

If you look in the options window under the Keyboard heading, there are tons of commands that you can bind to arbitrary keypresses. These include opening and closing windows, casting spells, targetting, moving the camera, and most other single actions. This window is available in game, not at the character selection screen. I think these options were set from the character selection screen in DAOC.

Some actions that can not (yet) be bound to keypresses are equipping an item, dropping an item, combining a trade skill container, accepting a confirmation dialog, buying or selling from a merchant, accepting a trade.
