<a name="news">Latest News</a>
Patch Day! Today's patch has a number of UI related items, including a bug fix for the Songs Window, info regarding the Story Window, and others. Read Cairenn's Patch Message thread (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=5618) for the full text!

Frogger Lives! Also mentioned in the Patch Message is the release of Legacy of Ykesha (http://www.legacyofykesha.com)! The "first download-only extension for EverQuest," it will contain new zones and creatures for the 35-60 crowd, and Frogloks as a playable race!
UI-specific changes include Enhanced Looking For Group Tool, New Cartography System, and Expanded bank capacity.
Authors may want to check out the changes... users, give the authors some time to make any necessary fixes!

Patch Fixes!

There are a TON of patch fixes for the thousands of UI's and mods here... luckily, you only have to look two places to find yours!

First off, check the appropriate Downloads Category (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php) there on the left and look for the mod or UI you need patched. Chances are, there's a new version available, or directions on how to patch the current.

Second, Search (http://www.eqgui.com/search.php?s=) the Patch Fixes (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=36) forum for a patch provided by the original author, or by a fellow community member.

If you need further assistance, please contact the author of the mod/UI or post a request in the Help!?!? (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=16) Forum. Make sure you post to the appropriate forum, otherwise your question won't get seen or answered! Please do NOT email or PM the Staff or Moderators - they're swamped, believe me!
