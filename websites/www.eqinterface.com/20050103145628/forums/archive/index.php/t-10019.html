Actually, it can be done, and I'm sorry I missed this post before now ;)

The thing you have to do is leave the actual page names alone, and just swap the <pieces> from one page to the other.

If you want skills on the main page, just find the <Page item=ActionsAbilitiesPage> and copy the <Pieces> below it and paste them under the <Page item=ActionsMainPage>, then cut the <Pieces> from the Main Page and Paste them where the Abilities <Pieces> used to be.

Save and you should be good to go.

I hope this helps.
