You can choose to create as many or as few chat windows as you wish. Just right-click on the main chat window, choose "make new window", then position the new chat window where you want it. Then right-click on the new window and select what you want filtered over to it.

Hitsmode

Right-click on the window and go allllll the way to the bottom of the menu to Hitsmode. You'll have My Hits, Other's Hits, etc... just choose Number for the ones you want filtered, and you're good to go!
