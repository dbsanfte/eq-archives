Any time you see:

Couldn't find class:item 'BlahBlahWhatever' reference in FieldParseItemOfClass()

it means your custom EQUI_Animations.xml file isn't up to date. Apply the fix to it as found in the 24 Feb patch message.

And no, don't make a copy of your default folder.
