Well, I've found the BASE issue with this may be the presence of negative numbers in the LOCATION sections.

In the GROUP window SonaMod, I have only seen this effect on PlayerSlot1 the rest of the bubbles and guages are unaffected.

In many cases we set the X or Y coords to -30 or something to just push the text off the display, and then leave everything else to fall into place. Seems that the negative coordinate is what is making a mess of things for player health gauges.

By taking the coords and setting them to real numbers (0 and above) and then modifying the TextOffsetY and GagueOffsetX etc to move the item around, I was able to straighten out some of the STANDARD gauges.

For the life of me tho with the TKing Style Gauges ( )------- the bubble in player slot 1 is just WAY freaked out.... Even moving all the Bubble gauges to positive integers and then modding the GaugeOffesetX to account for the new location, this one just pushed everything in game to the other side....
