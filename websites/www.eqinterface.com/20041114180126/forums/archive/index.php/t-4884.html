Features:

Custom minimalist borders that line up easier and take up less space.
4 different Player windows - Normal, Pure Melee, Pet Caster, and Twink. (picture below)
4 colors to choose from: Grey, Green, Blue, Red.
9 Inventory slots attached to the hot buttons (Primary, Secondary, Inventory 4, Range, Chest, Left Ear, Left Ring, Inventory 3, Inventory 1).
Easy to read %s added to Group and Target windows with color changing backgrounds.
Modified Player window containing vital stats (HP, AC, ATK).
Alt EXP bar added to Player window.
Normal EXP percentage added to Player window.
Modified Buff and Song windows show the name of buffs to help keep track of them.
Windows condensed to take up less screen space.
Compact MP3 Player.
Compact Pet Window.
Old Style Inventory Screen.
Pet window converted into a small window listing stats -- no need to open the inventory to check them.
Hotkeys added to bags for faster weapon/item switching.
Easy to read compass with loc assistance (shows which numbers will go up or down or stay the same).
Inventory Page in Actions menu.

New in version 3.2:

I received alot of feedback from people that they wanted the ability to have a pet window. Instead of moving the stat window to another window like the friends window, I integrated it into the Actions Window since the Invite, Disband etc buttons take up more space than they need to.

Designed a pet window that features a color changing percentage like the other windows, and doesn't take up much vertical screen space. You can see up to 16 buffs on your pet.

Further reduced the size of the MP3 player and made it the same height as the pet window.

The link all button is now in the loot window but doesn't take up any additional space. It's very similar to the combine button. Any time it is yellow it is active.

Since Sony added numbers the the backgrounds of the hotbuttons a couple patches back, the numbers in the corners weren't needed. Removed the number and added Sony's backgrounds. This means you if you use one of the alternate colors you'll be copying more files over.

The spell bar and book are probably different because the screenshots are taken with TKing's g2 spell icons. I don't include them because they are available here and would drastically increase the download size of the UI.

The pet window also has those icons, but the resizing issue is something else.

While not in game, you need to go into your UI_PlayerName_ServerName.ini file and look for a section that says:

[PetInfoWindow]

down at the bottom of this section will be a line that says Height1280x1024 (the number may be different, it will be whatever resolution you use, 800x600, 1024x768, 1600x1200 etc) and it will say =142

change that number to 50. Now log in and the pet window should be the proper size. (I've added this to the FAQ on my website as it's a common question).

I would like to change the player windows that you show from the normal to the melee but i cant find the anything that tells me what i have to do to change it.

All you need to do is rename the piece you want, EQUI_PlayerWindowMelee.xml for example to EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml (you'll need to delete the existing file first). Then just reload the UI and you'll have your new player window. I've added this to the FAQ, thanks!
