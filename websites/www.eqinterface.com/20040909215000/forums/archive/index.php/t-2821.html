**Version 2.1 is now out. It is compatable with the 01/10 patch** (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=927)

Just released v.2 of my Necro Spectre Pet Info box!

Things that are better:

Lined up a couple graphics and darkened the spec's shroud a bit.

Took out that treehugging bubblebar and made the scythe into the HP meter. This allowed me to overlay the pet's name on top of it, as well I have also added in the pet's HP percent in front of his name.

With the scythe as the HP bar, it allows you to tell at a quick glance whether your pet is still alive or not. Before, only the spec's eyes would light up to indicate life in your little undead buddy.

Go to misc. section to download it, or just click HERE. (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/download.php?id=362)

Hope you like it!

Forgot to mention: You click the eyes as the pet get lost (hence: RIP on his forehead). I did this so I could use the button to light his eyes up, have a smaller button to avoid accidental clicks, and you would know when the eyes glow brighter that you are about to kill your little boner.
