The problem is with EQUI_Animations.xml

Heres what you do:

1. copy EQUI_Animations.xml from your current UI directory (whatever you use) into your new spellbook test directory.

2. Highlight your EQUI_Animations.xml file in your new directory. shift+right-click it and select Open With.. select wordpad(its too big for notepad).

3. Now click on the edit menu at the top of your window, select Find

4. tell it to find "spellbook"

5. Delete from <TextureInfo.. to </TextureInfo> and from <Ui2dAnimation... to </Ui2dAnimation> for every place you find "spellbook"

6. Save the file

7 Repeat instructions 1 and 2 for EQUI_SpellBookWnd.xml
