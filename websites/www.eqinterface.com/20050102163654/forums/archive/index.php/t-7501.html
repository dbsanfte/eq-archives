Many UI's have resorted to larger than 256x256 tga/png files. While many video cards can handle these sizes, some do not do it well, in fact in some cases, the simple act of cutting up those larger graphics can have amazing effects on UI/EQ performance.

Suggestion:
Check the sizes of the graphics files in your UI. If any of them exceed 256x256, then look to replacing that portion of your UI with a mod that uses the minimum spec size.
(If you are using the sonamod funky mana spellbook, you are using a background image that is based on 1024x512 = superlag if your card isn't happy with big graphics.)
