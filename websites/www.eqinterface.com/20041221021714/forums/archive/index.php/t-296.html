I have actually found the pet window to be very useful. It only takes up a small space and seeing I am a 58 necro and I kite lvl 55 mobs, it is very helpful (all the buttons except for the /pet go away button). I use this window when I aggro a lvl 55 mob and I just want my pet to tag behind him and beat him up while I keep the aggro (extra dot). With the pet taunting, I take the chance of losing my pet due to him ticking off the mob. When I go out to pull and if my pet is low on HP, I am also able to hit the pet sit, pet guard here button. If someone decides to train in my area, I readily have the pet follow button accessible, so I don't collect the aggro and lose my pet due to someone's ignorance of not looking around for others.
