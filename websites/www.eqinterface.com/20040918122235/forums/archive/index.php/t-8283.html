Originally posted by Veirna
yeah i noticed the tint window is a bit fubared, and checked on original skin as well, and it too has a brownish tint to it..

can't figure it out, any help and i can update the zip files. =)


Change this definition in the EQUI_Animations.xml file:
<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_WhitePixel">
<Frames>
<Texture>window_pieces01.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>90</X>
<Y>90</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>1</CX>
<CY>1</CY>
</Size>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>



It needs to point to a white pixel..

Hope that helps..
