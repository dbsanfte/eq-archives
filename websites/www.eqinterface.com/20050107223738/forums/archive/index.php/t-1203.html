Installing Dual mods will only work "out of the box" if they mod different windows.

Go into uifiles located in your everquest directory. Create a new directory with any name. Copy all your mod files into that new directory.

Load EQ and type /load <name> 1.

Replace <name> with the name of the new folder you've created.

If in the processes of copying the mods into your new directory you happen to get a "Do you want to overwrite" message, merging won't work successfully and it will require editing the XML to get it to go.

But, I don't think you'll have that problem. As long as you downloaded mods for sperate windows.
