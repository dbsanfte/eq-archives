This is a culmination of lots of work from lots of people, and very very little effort from me, except to find them, download them, and incorporate revisions/bug fixes made by others.

Basically, the changes that are incorporated in this are:

1. Extra Long Target Window (4x's longer), w/ percentage value;

2. Buff Window which includes names of buffs;

3. Comprehensive Player Window, listing hp's, mana, stam & exp (I want to change the exp to AA-exp), as well as all stats;

4. Group Window w/ percentage values;

5. Small hotkey buttons;

6. Old Style Inventory; and

7. Smaller Spell Book.

I might update it later to include (1) alt exp in comprehensive player window, instead of or in addition to, regular exp; and (2) F2, F3, F4, F5 & F6 labels to the left of each group member's hp gauge.

if your mod is not working and causes you to crash..then ya cant get back in on that char..or maybe others...

try going into the char UI_yourchar_73.ini

scroll down to find:

NEWUI=DEFAULT

make sure it has DEFAULT, if it has your mod name, just change it to DEFAULT.

then check your EQCLIENT.ini...same thing here, make sure your
NEWUI=DEFAULT has default not your mod.

basicly if ya have a mod that is not compliant then when it tries to load it will make ya LD before ya get into game. this will load the default instead and then ya can figure out what part of your mod is the problem.

forget who told me to try this but it worked for me...
