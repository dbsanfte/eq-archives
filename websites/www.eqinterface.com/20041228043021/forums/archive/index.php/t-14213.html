We have an update tentatively scheduled for July 8th. Some of the highlights include;

The ability to remove augments from items and retain them.

Pets should properly shrink.

A new adventure zone will move to Legends.

Pets will zone with their masters.

Mana will display a numeric value.

Buffs will show a countdown timer when moused over.

The new Veeshan's Peak!

And many other exciting changes. Stay tuned.

Desdinova has started a thread HERE (http://www.eqinterface.com/forums/showthread.php?t=14212) about the changes. (and linked the sony forum for it) So head over to this post if you want to see further info, or discuss the items listed.
