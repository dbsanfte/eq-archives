Here's a pure melee ui that I made from tweaking an existing
ui called "cleancut". I call my version "clear". I wanted something
really minimalistic, I like looking at the view not the buttons and
stuff. The hotkey set up works great for switching out weapons
or items and is a nice size. I took all the menues and made them
invisible except for the white text. I adjusted the settings so that
nothing pops up on rollover, (except the compass top right corner
and the inventory/options bar top center). The hotkey box and
chat boxes are tinted black with no fading. The menues are a
little hard to click on at 1st being invisible but once you get a feel
for where they are you should be ok. If you want a pure melee ui
for a rogue/monk/warrior, something simple and easy to look at
feel free to use this.
