Download and extract the .zip files, mainly the UI's folder, within the .zip file, to your EverQuest\uifiles folder.

Do NOT place the custom UI files in to your default folder.

A majority of UI's will come, pre-packaged in its own folder, drag and drop that folder to your EverQuest\uifiles folder.
