I decided to keep the current version at the top of the thread.

Its intended mainly as a healer group window, but will work for everyone.

It has all 6 group members in it, It leaves out your name, but has names of other group members over thier lifebars.

Life gauges are slightly thicker than normal for increased visibility in higher res.

Pet lifebar, Exp and AA bars are at the bottom by the invite/disband buttons.

Each life bar has a large gem on the left end that has a life % indicator in it, and the gem changes color as the person takes damage. Green(100%) to yellow(75%) to orange(50%) to red(25%) to flashing red(~20%).

http://home.attbi.com/~mbeiser/Images/CalTKGWbeta3.JPG

If you would like to try it, download it and let me know what you think. Click links below to download. Unzip it to your UI directory, it is a self contained mod with its own graphics files.

Download version with transparent background. (http://home.attbi.com/~mbeiser/Files/CalGWbeta3trans.zip)

Download version with background.(shown) (http://home.attbi.com/~mbeiser/Files/CalGWbeta3.zip)

1/28/03:
Fixed pet lifebars.
Using different graphic for the gauge tubes.
Added F key lables to the color changing gems.
Moved the % numbers onto the lifebars.
Changed gauge backgrounds so the rings show in the dividers in the gauges.
Added blank gauges so the metal rings show in the gauge tubes at all times.

1/29/03:
Changed the size of the % labes on the lifebars so they do not block mouse clicking the lifebar to target a group member.

Changed the order the gauges are so that the pet lifebars for group members can be clicked to target the pet.
