<a name="news">Latest News</a>
Patch Day, July 10th: Per EQLive Network Status (http://eqlive.station.sony.com/support/network_status.jsp), all EverQuest servers will be brought down for a scheduled update. The estimated downtime is 3 hours. The upcoming update (http://eqlive.station.sony.com/updates/upcoming_updates.jsp) is detailed at EQLive, and a thread has been opened for discussion here (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=9239).
(The scheduled downtime for July 9th has been postponed until July 10th.)

Default UI for Download: The Default UI Files (http://www.synex-flux.com/Other/defaultuifiles/index.html) have been updated courtesy of mysticscrolls.com (http://www.mysticscrolls.com/EverQuest/index.asp); both a zip package and the individual files are available.

New T.King address: Per Thomus King (http://pub165.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10.showMessage?topicID=74.topic), all his home- and art-pages have changed from attbi.com to comcast.net; don't forget to update your bookmarks!

Lost Dungeons of Norrath: The next EQ expansion has been announced, and it sounds great! Read Kudane's post (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?threadid=8954), and visit the Official Site (http://everquest.station.sony.com/lostdungeons/)!

New Stories? Anyone else seen the new Stories added lately? Are they leading to LDoN, zone revamps, or something else...?
