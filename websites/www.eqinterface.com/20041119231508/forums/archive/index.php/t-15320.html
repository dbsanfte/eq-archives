First thing you need to do is go into Prefrences>Units & Rulers and change the Units to "Pixels". Then make sure you turn the rulers on View>Rulers. Then open up the info palette. At the bottom there are x and y coordinates. Go over to the ruler and click and drag out a guide. Move the guide to the graphic that you want to know the location of and look at your x or y coordinate depending on wheither you are on the the x or y plane. If you let go of the guide before you get the number, just grab the move tool and grab ahold of the guide.

I have found that in relation to the position of the graphics in game itself, they are usually a little off. But this will get you close.

Hope this helps...
