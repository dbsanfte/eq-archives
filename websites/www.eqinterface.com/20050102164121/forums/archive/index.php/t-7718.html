The larger file sizes make very little difference in game performance. I tested it by making a UI that was as small as posable, then I compiled a UI useing the largest files I could find from here. When I ran them both, they ran just about the same.

Your errors are just saying those items are not defined in the bugreport window. As long as it causes no problems, I would not worry about it. Please do not EVER AGAIN copy the default directory to your custom fold. When patch time comes around, this causes untold headacks.

As for holding the shift key while alt-abed out of EQ, Just click the show desktop icon, which will minumize EQ and make it not necissary to hold shift.

Give your girl time..she will learn all the EQ lingo.
