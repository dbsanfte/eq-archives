Well this is my first real 'mod'.

This is version 1 of Slim. The goal is to get as much information available to the player at once in the least amount of space. IT isn't really pretty or awe inspiring, but I don't think it's more of the same ol same ol either.

Screenshot (http://www.amaprotu.net/equi/slim_v1.jpg)

Notes:
*) I use /viewport 0 0 1024 723 which leaves a 45 pixel high bar along the bottom, the size of my mod bar.

*) You will have to rearange some windows the first time you use it (group and hotbox I think) because xml locs still don't work right.

*) The yellow bar on the health bar is the stamina bar.

*) The number to the right of the name is the character level

*) From top to bottom the three bars are Mana, XP, AAXP

*) The hotbox is small, but I always use hotkeys and just need the image there to know which keyboard button to press.

*) The green bar under the target bar is the pet health bar.

*) The group is arranged horizontaly (1 2 3 on top, 4 5 on bottom)

*) Yes they have pet health bars.

*) I don't use main or the pet window but I moded them to fit the mod.

*) The stats explain themselves

Whats next?
Pre-Version 2:
Melee character mod (no mana, no pet).

Version 2:
*) Spell book mod
*) Inv and Inspect mod
*) Spell cast and Breath bar mod
*) Main window mod

Version 3:
*) New graphics (background, border)


Yeah I think that covers everything, let me know if there are problems or what you think.
