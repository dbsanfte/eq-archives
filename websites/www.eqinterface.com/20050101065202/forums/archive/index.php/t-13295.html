There was a patch awhile ago that made several items required in these windows. So, to fix it....

Change:

<ScreenID>TargetPercentage</ScreenID>

To:

<ScreenID>HPLabel1</ScreenID>

Do something similiar for each error you find. Use the default files as a reference. That should clear your errors up.

So basically, I'm assuming that "could not find child" is expressing that it could not find an expected descriptor or element of some sort even though that descriptor or element may not be essential for the operation of the function.

A "child" is a part of a set of items that are a sub-set of another item. The way it's used here, the "Window" item has "children" of all it's expected components.
