If you encounter errors while following links that lead to an EQ Interface (http://www.eqinterface.com) location, it's most likely because User Registration (http://www.eqgui.com/register.php?s=&action=signup) is required to access the Downloads area. Take a minute to register; it's free, and there's a ton of content.

Updates from 10/21/02 onward can be found in News and Updates (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=35);
News previous to 10/21/02 can be found in the News Archive (http://pub32.ezboard.com/fsolusfrm38.showMessage?topicID=5.topic).

Remember, information regarding an new or updated upload should be sent to Kudane (http://www.eqgui.com/member.php?action=mailform&userid=9).

For info on what's changed in the UI and how to fix it, be sure to check the Patch Messages (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=36) forum!

Statix has come up with a method to make UI mods (mostly) unbreakable by patches! It's likely to still see some tweaking, but you can follow the discussion in this thread (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3848), or visit Statix's site (http://skins.machin-shin.net/nobreak.html).
