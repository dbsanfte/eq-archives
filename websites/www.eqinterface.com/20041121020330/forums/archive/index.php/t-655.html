The spell name items in EQUI_SpellBookWnd don't specify a text color (the default is black) but they can. Scroll down to one of the buttons (SWB_DoneButton will do) and copy the TextColor section. Paste that section into the spellname item right below the Size section. R is for red, G is for green, B is for blue.
