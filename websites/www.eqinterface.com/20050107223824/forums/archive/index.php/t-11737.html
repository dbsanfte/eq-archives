ATI and Target Rings

As some of you know, the ATI 9600's have been lacking with the 3D-Target Rings. I'm not sure if the 9800's had the problem or not, but supposedly the patch fixed it. Well it didn't. However it does work on test. SoE forgot to bring the EQGfx_Dx8.DLL from test over to Live. So what you can do in the mean time, if you are hard up for target rings, is to patch to test. After patch is done, you can exit the patcher. Go into you EQ folder and copy EQGfx_Dx8.DLL to a safe place. Now go ahead and patch EQ Live, before you hit the play button, copy the "safe" EQGfx_Dx8.DLL into your EQ folder and POOF you have target rings.


Special thanks to Lithale of Eqbeastlords for this fix, it really does work

Taken from THIS (http://boards.station.sony.com/ubb/everquest/Forum3/HTML/049581.html) thread on the Tech Help board on eqlive:

1)Patch Test (testeverquest.exe in your Everquest folder)
2)Rename EQGfx_DX8.dll to EQGfx_DX8test.dll
3)Open your eqclient.ini file and add and/or edit your UseGfxDLL line to read: UseGfxDLL=EQGfx_Dx8Test.DLL and save the file. (If that line does not exist in your ini file, add it)
4)Run the regular patcher and log in and rejoice.


*EDIT* FYI I have an ATI Radeon 9200 and this fix worked flawlessly for me
