<a name="news">Latest News</a>
Patch Message, 04/23/03: From the Patch Message (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=8154) as posted by Kudane:Tip of the Day - When you first log in you will be presented with a Tip of the Day window. We've added this window to provide helpful insight to new players, and perhaps a few tips for experienced players as well.
The UI Files listed as changed are EQUI.xml, EQUI_TipWnd.xml, EQUI_HelpWnd.xml, and EQUI_OptionsWindow.xml, though apparently the EQUI.xml was not updated in the initial download (if yours did not update, either run your Patcher again or download from the Test server). See Cairenn's post (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&postid=42657) for specific changes to the UI.

Out of Town: The News Ranger will be out of town until next week (watching his wife run a half-marathon - go Rachaella!), so the next update will be 04/28-29.
