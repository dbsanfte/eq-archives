Here's my mod, sorry it took a bit, I've been playin with all the neato spellbooks that have come out, trying to pick one that worked well. Of course, I couldn't pick a fave, they are all to sweet, so I just included the vert book with a custom background.

This is the rundown again:

Vertlun1.0 is

Vert 2.2 Canni
Sokol's melee playerwindow mod (ac, att, hp, and mana bar) - tweaked with bigger hp on top, and no playername
Sokol's buff box
TKing/Rem Arrow Cursor
TKing/Rem Arcane Casting Bauble
TKing/Rem Horizontal Casting Bar
Sean/Llyric Compact Tracking Window
Horaxe's Anorexic Inventory, slightly rearranged with vert Destroy button
Nattsumi's Bag Mod
My personal Target Window with Vert inspired double size gauge (avaible in Beta section)

I play 1280by1024 with viewport something like 1280, 780ish. I play with the Eqtoolbox map mod and the timer, so both of those should just drop in fine. Might run into some problems loading alternate books, though...we'll see.

.zip was too bit with the tking icons in it, I'm sure anyone who wants 'em can find 'em.

Enjoy!
