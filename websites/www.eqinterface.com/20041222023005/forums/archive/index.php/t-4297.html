If you encounter errors while following links that lead to an EQ Interface (http://www.eqinterface.com) location, it's most likely because User Registration (http://www.eqgui.com/register.php?s=&action=signup) is required to access the Downloads area. Take a minute to register; it's free, and there's a ton of content.

Updates from 10/21/02 onward can be found in News and Updates (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=35);
News previous to 10/21/02 can be found in the News Archive (http://pub32.ezboard.com/fsolusfrm38.showMessage?topicID=5.topic).

Don't forget to check the Released (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=5) Forum for new UI mods and skins that may not be getting uploaded - as time allows, I'll try to include as many as I can here.
Go ahead and upload, folks! That's what it's there for!

Remember, information regarding an new or updated upload should be sent to Kudane (http://www.eqgui.com/member.php?action=mailform&userid=9) or Dolby (http://www.eqgui.com/member.php?s=&action=mailform&userid=1).

Patch Message 11/19/02 (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=4114) has been posted by Cairenn; she also has posted a list of fixes (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=4115)!

Featured Skins
