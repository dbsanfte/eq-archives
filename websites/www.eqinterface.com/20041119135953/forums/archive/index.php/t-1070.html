Languages? In what manor?

There is no set language in EQ. It's all jumbled words.

'The' = 'Jeu' or 'Ufh' or 'Sye' or any other way. The EQ Client uses an algorithim based on your skill to randomly jumble words up, if you're not 100 in said language.

Unfortunatly, there isn't any way to create a dictionay based on the "languages" of EQ.
