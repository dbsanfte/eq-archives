08-03-2002, 02:00 AM
Using your Casting Gauge you have listed in HotButtonWnd.xml.

You have it defined as the following

Size
x=84
y=8

This tells the interface that your gauge is ONLY 84 pixels wide.

Your animations file shows the end caps are both 4 pixels wide and the gauge background is 84 pixels wide.

Seems to me you need to define the gauge size in HotButtonWnd.xml as:

Size
x=92
7=8

You forgot to include the endcaps when setting the size of your gauge.
