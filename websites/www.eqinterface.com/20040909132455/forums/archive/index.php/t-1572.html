First of all thanks for all the nice comments, it keeps me going :)

Vixyn, this UI works in a 1280x960 video setting. That means that even with the 2 dragons on the side, the space in between the dragons is as big as the space you had on 1200x700ish which is what most people use.

Considering all the items and information that the "dragon-windows" contain, i cant really make them smaller, or items would be crouded on top of each other in the windows and it would be confusing to clic on, let alone uggly.

If you dont want the dragon to be so "visible", you can allways make them fade when your not activating them with your mouse over them.

I had to consider a number of factors. First I wanted to group all of the things you might want to clic on with your mouse on one area of the screen (extremely important for classes like bards). This is why you have the spells, hotkeys, inventory and PRI/SEC/RANGE all grouped on the right dragon window, and the static info and group gauges on the left dragon window.

I could have made the right dragon a little slimmer on his chest, but that would have killed the general symetry of the screen.

Plus, it is, after all, a "Dragonish" UI :D)
