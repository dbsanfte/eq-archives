<a name="updates">Latest Updates</a>

Complete Sets (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=22)
A number of sets have been updated recently with fixes and other changes:
Elindel's ELUI v2.03 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=742);
Celrona's Ranger UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=761);
Zelf's Zelf 1.1 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=769)
Also, Jilkscom56 has released JilkBard (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=772), a UI designed for bards, but adaptable to any class.
Containers (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=34)
Mortis42 has created a combined Bank/Inventory window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=762), with slightly smaller slots, for easier access.
Miscellaneous (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=30)
Kaenil Darkwolf has uploaded a Soul Reaver cursor (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=763), based on the "Legacy of Kain" series.
Mix & Match (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=29)
Kuririnsama has put together two new UI's: a standard Kuri UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=764), and KuriGreen (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=765), the "Treehugger version."
Throkmorton has put together ThrokUI (http://www.eqinterface.), a combination of borrowed mods and original work.
Mazaneal has put together two new UI's as well, designed for the "EvOl SK (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=770)" and "EvOl Wizard (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=771)", each with a unique look.
Pet Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=33)
Novawulf has updated the Horizontal Pet Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=768) with rearranged buttons; this is designed to work exclusively with Sar's UI.
