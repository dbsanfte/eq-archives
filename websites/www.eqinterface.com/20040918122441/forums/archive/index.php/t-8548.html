This is just a ghetto fix so I'm not going to post this to downloads or anything. That great 14th patch added the annoying feature of pet window automatically popping up when a pet is generated and automatically closing when a pet dies/despawns/gets lost/loses charm.

Some classes probably find functionality in using a pet window and may not like it. As a wizard, having that ugly thing pop up in my face every time I zone (familiar) or every time I cast sword of xuzl is annoying to no end. Originally my pet window was where I had my timers but this new "feature" would always be closing them on me forcing me to reopen them so I switched.

Basically I'm just uploading my fix to the annoying pet window situation. All I did was make the pet window invisible via killing the height/width and positioning, and therefore making the window non-existant in a virtual sense and with no functionality.
