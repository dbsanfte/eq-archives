I've done this a couple times and it's 100% duplicatable.

Here's layout - Your HP, you Group Member HP and even your Pet HP bars all Have to elements "built in"

The Name ( it "caption text" per say ). This is the Full name, if it's you and first name for your pet, Group members and I believe their pet.

When you first start your playerWindow mods, you have a Single gauge, I mean, didn't you think it was odd there was no EQType Label for your name? There isn't one, it's on the HP bar!

Lets take a closer look at the HP Bar.

<Gauge item="Player_HP">
<ScreenID>PlayerHP</ScreenID>
<!--<Font>3</Font>-->
<TextColor>
<R>240</R>
<G>240</G>
<B>240</B>
</TextColor>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>3</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>108</CX>
<CY>41</CY>
</Size>
<TextOffsetX>8</TextOffsetX>
<GaugeOffsetY>33</GaugeOffsetY>
<Style_VScroll>false</Style_VScroll>
<Style_HScroll>false</Style_HScroll>
<Style_Transparent>false</Style_Transparent>
<!--<TooltipReference/>-->
<FillTint>
<R>240</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>0</B>
</FillTint>
<LinesFillTint>
<R>220</R>
<G>220</G>
<B>0</B>
</LinesFillTint>
<DrawLinesFill>false</DrawLinesFill>
<EQType>1</EQType>
<GaugeDrawTemplate>
<Background>A_GaugeBackground</Background>
<Fill>A_GaugeFill</Fill>
<Lines>A_GaugeLines</Lines>
<LinesFill>A_GaugeLinesFill</LinesFill>
<EndCapLeft>A_GaugeEndCapLeft</EndCapLeft>
<EndCapRight>A_GaugeEndCapRight</EndCapRight>
</GaugeDrawTemplate>
</Gauge>

Now, You see everything how it is by Default.
Take a look at some odities of this bar:
<Size>
<CX>108</CX>
<CY>41</CY>
</Size>

That's a TALL bar .. Wonder why it's 41 hight when the others are only 8?

<Location>
<X>3</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>


Interstings isn't it? 3,0 but your HP bar is so far down.

How that's done? More odities:
<TextOffsetX>8</TextOffsetX>
<GaugeOffsetY>33</GaugeOffsetY>

Note, how these work.

TextOffSetX - This means move the text off 8 to the right. So, in that 108x41 window at 3,0 your name will be at 11,0. See that?

Then your HP bar is a gauge. And now it has an offset of 0,33. So, once again in the 108x41 window at 3,0 your HP bar will be at 3,33.


Okay, enough explaning the text lable.

Why is yours messed up? (damn, your server's busy and I can't load your image. )
Okay, so I'll go off memory. Your Name is over your HP Bar, right?

Sounds like you did this:

You complete removed the TextOffsetX and GaugeOffsetY and changed the Size to 108x8 ( or something around there ).

To fix this, either do one of two things:

1) Put in TextoffSetY and set it to 20. 20Y in a 108x8 window is 108, 20. Far enough off the HP bar and no you do not need some big a.. number, something simple. KISS - Keep It Simple Stupid ( live by that ;) ).

2) Increase the size of the window back to 108x41 and put the Offsets back. This will put things back to the way they where.

And dat about it. VI built the player name(s) into the HP bar. Stupid, but that's how they designed it. Stupid, I know .. but nothing we can do about that.
