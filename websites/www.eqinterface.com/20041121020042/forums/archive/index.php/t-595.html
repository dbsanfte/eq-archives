You can turn any window into any window. For example, on my UI, my group window is a window that I call "quick-click" window. It's a long window, one icon high, and it has one row that shows all my inventory. It sits above my chat boxes and cos its the original group window, it toggles on/off by alt-p.
It's very handy. I moved the group stuff into my customized hotbox window. I'll post it soon for viewing pleasure.

But yeah you can change windows into whatever you want. Just be sure NOT to delete anything thats default. Just set original sizes to 0.
