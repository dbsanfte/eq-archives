Greets!

I've been fooling around with trying a new skin now for months.

Finally, I DL'd this one and gave it a try.

Overall, I rate it excellent in visual appeal. But, I have just a few issues with readability (I hope you don't mind):

target bar is nearly obscured by the claw-like filligree in the center. I'd suggest adding a dark line around it--not a thin outline, but a wider line; or, filling in the negative space in the filligree with the same background you have on the chat windows. Anything that will relieve some of the business there.

(oh, sorry, forgot to mention I am a graphic artist---so please don't be offended. I've been trained to do Advertising art so, take all this with a grain of salt :)

Health and Mana bars: not prominant enough. Sorta small and hard to get an immediate update on how I'm surviving heh. Generally, the info at the bottom is a bit to small--at least for me.

installing instructions: unless I'm a completly bumbling fool (and this may well be true) the UI did not install following your instructions---not completely, that is:

I copied the files from my chosen resolution into the main folder (which I renamed, btw--though I don't think this was an issue). Next, I found the UI_<name>_<servernumber>, altered it (after frantically trying to find the number for my server which you find in your main EQ directory, btw for those of us who are clueless heh) then I replaced it as instructed, loaded up the game and got:

all kinds of bizarro windows...a mix of the new and old, I think. Not quite sure. Anyway, I decided to do what the instructions in the PoP gamebook says: type /loadskin <foldername> <1 or 0> and I chose '1' since I wanted all my slots set up as I chose before. Voila, a matter of moments, and this lovely UI sorta manifested across my screen in graceful fashion :)

Combat messages: could I please suggest a little more info be forthcoming than just the miss, and points of damage? I agree that the default messages are way too spammy. What about:

<short attacker name>: miss or <damage>: <target>

for example:

Loda Kai: miss: <my name>
<my name>: 27 slash: Loda Kai
Warder: 30 slash: Loda Kai
R Hopper: miss Warder (or Jimbob's Warder)
Loda Kai: 5 punch: <my name>

Or something similar? Right now, it's all determined simply by color of the text I chose (which I remember of course).

Other than this, I really do miss the chat windows going transparent, but can deal. :)

But, as I said above, this is a very pleasing and appealing UI and I very much like the spell icons and the animations, the overall design is wonderful.

thanks
