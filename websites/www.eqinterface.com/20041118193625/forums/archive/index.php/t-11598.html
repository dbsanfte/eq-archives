The best way I've found to do it without compression and such is this:

Make the cursor image the size you actually want it.
Make a transparent or blank 256x256 canvas.
Paste the image you want for your pointer in the top left corner of the 256x256 image.

Then in the uianimations.xml you can set the pointer at 0,0 and not have to worry about coordinates in a multiple graphic .tga file.
