Did you also add:

<StaticAnimation item= "ClassAnim">
<ScreenID>ClassAnim</ScreenID>
<Location>
<X>338</X>
<Y>22</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>64</CX>
<CY>128</CY>
</Size>
<Animation>A_ClassAnim01</Animation>
</StaticAnimation>

back in? You have to have both parts to make it work properly. If you don't want it to show, just change the size to be 1,1.

In answer to your specific question:

<Pieces>IW_AltAdvGauge</Pieces>
<Pieces>ClassAnim</Pieces>
<Pieces>IW_CharacterView</Pieces>
<Pieces>IW_DoneButton</Pieces>
</Screen>

shows where it appears in the default inventory code.
