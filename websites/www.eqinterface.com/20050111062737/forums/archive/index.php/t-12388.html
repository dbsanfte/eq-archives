The fastest way, but not necessarily the best, is to change the Font that you are using in your Options window in game. Try a bunch of different fonts and see if there is one that displays larger that works with your UI. This is a Universal setting and will change all the fonts on every display in the game, including the text that appears when zoning, above peoples and mobs names, etc.

If that does not work.

You can try opening up the window that you want to change and change the <Font>#</Font> references to a higher number.
The deafult (SOE not Delta) is 3 and somethins will show up as <!--<Font>3</Font>-->, You would have to remove the <!-- & --> from them as well to change the font size on those.

A final note, sometimes coders size their fonts to fit a certain size, and changeing them can make them not show up completely.
