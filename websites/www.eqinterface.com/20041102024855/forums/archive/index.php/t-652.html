First note, YOU MUST HAVE THE QUARTZ UI in order to have all your inventory information.

Get Quartz here --> http://skins.machin-shin.net

Second note, YOU MUST EXTRACT ALL THESE FILES to the QUARTZ directory in your UIfiles folder.

Third note, Your friends window will be GONE! it is now replaced with the remaining inventory windows that quartz does not already have accessable.

Forth note, It is a good idea to go into options <alt+O> and map a new key to open your friends list as this is now your inventory items. Unless you enjoy hiting <alt+f> to do it, which also works.

Fifth note, You will hafta drag the windows to wherever you want to have them.

Sixth note, to open the auto-equip window, unfortunately you will still hafta hit the old inventory hotkey <Default is "I">

Seventh note, Enjoy it, It takes a bit ta get used to, but it works..
