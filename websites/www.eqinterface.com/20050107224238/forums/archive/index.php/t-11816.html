I do this kind of thing using the storyline window. You can open up the entries in notepad and make your own note entries by following the format. As long as you make your own files, without changing any of the default ones, theyll never get patched over.

For example:
storyjournal.text

Journal Notes
<br><br>
Kwilrz Vn`Ycxa says:<br>
I suggest you go talk to Selephra Giztral (SRO), <br>
Bartsre Songweaver (E Common), <br>
Vual Stoutest (ButcherBlock), <br>
Teria Grinntli (EverFrost Peaks), <br>
or Ruanya Windleaf (NRO).

could be saved as the following:
storyresearchloc.txt

Research Locs
<br><br>
Words: Wordname<br>
Location: 00,00,00<br><br>
Words: Wordname<br>
Location: 00,00,00

Hope this suggestion helps.
