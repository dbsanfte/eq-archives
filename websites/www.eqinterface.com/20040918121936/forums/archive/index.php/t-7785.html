4 or 8 slot Inventory Slots in any Container.

Cut down on time dragging items from Inventory to your Container and combining.

Your Inventory slots are in blue (to distinguish between container and inventory slots). They could be different colors or graphics depending on what UI you are using.

DO NOT use the inventory slots (the blue ones) IN the container to close bags. YOU WILL CRASH INSTANTLY TO DESKTOP! I have sent a bug report in about this.

Screenshots included in zip files.

Note: You are very limited to what you can do with Containers. The size is hard coded into EQ (depending on what container you open) and you can't move the Done or Combine buttons around much.
