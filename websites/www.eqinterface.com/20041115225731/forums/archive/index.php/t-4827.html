I had three goals when I made this UI:

1.) Put everything button or clickable think in a single area of the screen. I hate roaming all over the place with the cursor, trying to target the right thing, especially during lag.

2.) Put has much info on the screen as possible. I'm an information junkie. I look at my stats a ton, to check buffs and decide on loot, so I wanted it up all the time. Not only that, but I have two group windows.... One just for targeting and one for the hp of group mates. I took this idea from Romelo. ;)

3.) Do both of the above, while keeping most of the action visible. I like to see everything going on. I need see corpses, mobs, adds, etc so that I know how to act. I made a ton of stuff transparent, so I think I accomplished this, even though there is a ton of stuff on the screen.

The screen shot is attached... I don't think there is much more to work on, so here goes. Give me some feedback, please. ;)

Just to clarify some questions...

1.) The stats are not in the buff window. They are actually in the Target window. I just like to have them next to each other because I look at my stats a ton to figure out what buffs I need.... just feels quicker to me than decyphering what buffs I have...

2.) The icons are T.Kings, but are not part of my UI. It just happens to be my favorite icon set. :) The labels for each stat are pulled from the icon library, so depending on what icons you use, you'll get different labels.

3.) Yes, the numbers next to the buffs are the slot numbers... I added a little extra tab next to each so that the negative/positive effect can be seen quickly, but it's not over the buff name so that it doesn't obstruct the view a ton.
