To change your screen resoltion - assuming WindowsXP: Right-click on your desktop Select Properties Click on the Settings tab Drag the slider-bar to the setting you desire Click OK

To Change your screen resolution in EQ: Login to EQ Open the Options window (Alt + O) Choose the Display tab Click the Video Modes button Select the resoultion, refresh rate and bit depth you desire from the choices Click on Accpet

NOTE: You can only reach a screen resoultion that is supported by both your video card and your monitor. If one of the other can't display a higher resolution, you'll get some strange activity on the screen, and then Windows should reset the display to the previous value.

Glossary:
Screen Resolition, Video Resolution = The size of the display in Width x Height.
Refresh Rate = How often the picture redraws.
Bit Depth = The color complexity displayed.
