TGA (textures) shoud be sized to be a power of 2.. 2,4,8,16,32,64,128,256
examples:
2x32, 32x64, 64x64, 128x64, 256x32 should all be valid..

Note: I wouldn't go over 256x256... Some (old) video cards (like voodoo3) don't support textures over 256x256

Resolution I believe is 72 pixels per inch

Hope that helps





Edit: I'm not sure if resolution really matters..
