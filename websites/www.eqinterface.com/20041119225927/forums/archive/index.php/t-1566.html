Locate your uifiles directory. Typically at C:\Program Files\EverQuest\uifiles\ or C:\Program Files\Sony\EverQuest\Trilogy\uifiles\
Create a new directory, or use an exisiting one (cannot be default).
Place your mod files into that directory . (note the name of it)
Load up EQ and type in: /load <name> (<name> is the name of your new directory)
