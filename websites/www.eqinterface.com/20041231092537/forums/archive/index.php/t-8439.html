After pounding my head against the keyboard a few hundred times authors can now edit their interface downloads. All interface authors are allowed to update the interface zipfile, update the picture file, and update the description. If you update the zipfile the interface will go back to the moderation queue for approval.

//// <a href=http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?postid=44084#post44084>Read More...</a>

* This news is for skin authors *
These changes do not effect you unless you are an author of interfaces.
Dolby
05-12-2003, 10:04 PM
You can find the link either under your name in every post you make on our forum or in the eqinterface logged in block under the menu show in the graphic below:
<img src=http://www.eqinterface.com/images/edithelp/goto.gif>


Once you click on that link you and only you will see a new column in the list of your downloads as seen in the picture below:
<img src=http://www.eqinterface.com/images/edithelp/list.gif>


When you click on the [edit] link you will be brought to a window as shown in the picture below:
<img src=http://www.eqinterface.com/images/edithelp/edit.gif>


Updating the picture or description does not require moderation. Updating the zipfile will take your interface offline until an admin or moderator approves your update.

As always if you would like one of your interfaces deleted please send a PM to Kudane Sokol or Dolby with the name of the file.
