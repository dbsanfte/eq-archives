I figured out the spell gems problem. Here's how I did it.

In the EQUI_Animations.xml file, the problem is with the gemicons.

Search down to:

<Ui2DAnimation item="A_SpellGems">
<Cycle>false</Cycle>
<Grid>true</Grid>
<Vertical>false</Vertical>
<CellHeight>20</CellHeight>
<CellWidth>20</CellWidth>
<Frames>
<Texture>gemicons01.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>200</CX>
<CY>200</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
<Frames>
<Texture>gemicons02.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>200</CX>
<CY>140</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>

Delete that and replace it with:

<Ui2DAnimation item="A_SpellGems">
<Cycle>false</Cycle>
<Grid>true</Grid>
<Vertical>false</Vertical>
<CellHeight>24</CellHeight>
<CellWidth>24</CellWidth>
<Frames>
<Texture>gemicons01.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>256</CX>
<CY>256</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
<Frames>
<Texture>gemicons02.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>256</CX>
<CY>256</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>


The problem was the size of the gemicons and cellheight and cellwidth.

The cellheight and cellwidth were set to 20, when they should've been at 24. And the gemicons.tga were set at 200/200 and 200/140 when they should've been at 256/256 for both.

Hope that helps. :)
