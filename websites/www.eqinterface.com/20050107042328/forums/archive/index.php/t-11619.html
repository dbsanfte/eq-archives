SOE slipped a new command live a few months ago.

/AUTOINVENTORY

This command will move an item from your CURSOR into your inventory as if it were being looted. It will try to equip it, and if no slots, then will try to stack it inside your bags

The shortest form you can use /AUTOI any shorter and it translates it as the autosplit command.

Best use is for Foragers and Fishers who can now set up a hotbutton that will do the ability as well as bag any items.
