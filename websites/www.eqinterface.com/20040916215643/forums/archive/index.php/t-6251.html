http://www.mammothworks.com/eq/UI/TKing-WC-HG/TKing-WisCast-Header-Grouped.jpg

Info:
Gauges from top to bottom are: life/mana/exp/aa

I included a simple target window so that you don't have to deal with the blinking red square around the player window. Its basically a modified luntar target window with a cast bar as well ( the casting window is cleared out. )

The gauges and art are from TKing's (http://pub10.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10.showMessage?topicID=74.topic) work. I modified his gauges.tga and pulled some graphics from some of his other work.

This particular player window is geared towards Wisdom Casters. The name has abbreviations to help me remeber what is what ;)

WC = Wis Caster
HG = Header, Grouped ( Header is the Square w/ info; grouped is the fact that the percentages are grouped opposed to done ind individual boxes. )

Please continue to send me your comments, and I hope somone likes this enough to test it out a little :D
