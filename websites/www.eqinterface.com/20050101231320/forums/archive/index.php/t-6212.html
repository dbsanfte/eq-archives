To say the least, I got very tired of crashing everytime I zoned. So, using xmlspy, I quickly compared the differences between my custom UI's and the default EQUI.xml files.

I noticed that the custom EQUI only had 51 <include> items in the composite section, whereas the updated Jan 28 EQUI in the default folder had 61.

Basically what I suggest is copying this into your custom EQUI.xml file, just copy over the code that's already there. It completely eliminated my crash issues.
