Go to your layers pallet and hit the tab at the top for "channels". Click on the button at the bottom for "create new channel". the new channel will be called the Alpha channel. If you want your whole graphic file to be 50% transperant... get the paint bucket, select white as your color, turn the transperancy down to 50% and fill the alpha layer. It will look grey. The way the alpha layer works is that white areas are completely opaque, black areas are completely transperant, and all shades of grey are some level of transperant in between. The darker the grey = the more transperant.

When you save it as a .tga, make sure that the box for "alpha channels" is checked.
