08-18-2002, 10:17 AM
Bars give you the advantage of a quick visual cue as to where things stand. Having bars for group health helps you see very quickly with little brain power that things are great or lousy. Without even really looking at it you can see where your mana is and your own health.

The percentages give you really accurate information. Knowing that a tank has 5000 hps and that he is down to 20 percent on the nose lets you calculate exactly when you should start a complete heal. You can get a lot more accuracy with the percentages but you have to be activly looking at them and running calculations in your head.

Based on these two different principles we can determine what info we need and put percentages and bars where appropriate.

In my own ui, I removed bars for stamina, exp, aaxp because I just didn't need that visual reference like you do with player and group health.
