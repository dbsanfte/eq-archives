For your first issue, open the file EQUI_SpellBookWnd.xml in a text editor such as Notepad or Wordpad.

Find the piece of code at the very top that reads

<Ui2DAnimation item="A_SpellBook_bg">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>spellbook_bg.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>400</CX>
<CY>300</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>

This is where the background for the spell book is defined (red text emphasis is mine). You can replace the image with something else, or you can replace the whole spell book file.
For your second issue, the background used for regular books is defined in the EQUI_Animations.xml file, and you'll need to make changes to that to change the background used by regular books.


For your third issue, you're missing at least 2 patches, because the additional dragitem_xx.tga files were released with LDoN and the patches to make them work are in the Patch Help forum.
