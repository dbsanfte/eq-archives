Well, first of all, it's not really a transparent background at all. It's an extra channel in addition to the RGB channels, which you can add and edit via the channels window.

What I do is select and copy my entire image, then switch over to the alpha channel and paste. You should have a monochrome copy of your image in the alpha channel now. You can darken it or lighten it however you see fit to change the level of transparency.

If this hasn't helped you, then plase use the search function and type "transparent" or "alpha" in the search dialogue. There's a lot of information on this subject scattered around the forums already.
