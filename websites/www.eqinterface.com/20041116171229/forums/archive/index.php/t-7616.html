In the style of the SonaFunkyMana spellbook, this mod is actually transparent, looking like it has 3-D shadows and such.

I have corrected spell numbering schemes in this one so that converting from another spell book to this one should be a bit less tragic. (Funky Mana was way out of order, that should be fixed soon too.)

I am not happy at all with the quality of the graphic, but will work on that. It was not my first choice. (Kuv had done a great job making a transparency from TKing's BST SpiritShield for me, but when I shrunk it down, it wound up losing so much detail that I just couldn't use it at all. Thomus, if you are lurking, and have nothing better to do (hahaha) a 55x120 Spirit Shield with a shadow would be great)

So anyway, the shields are modular. Meaning that they can all be moved to fit any configuration you want. I'm not sold on the current layout, so anyone wants to suggest some alternatives just post.
