<a name="news">Latest News</a>
As you may have noted, there’s been some fallout from the 01/28 patch… to quote Cairenn: What happened here is that SOE pushed through 4 different downloads. Some folks got the correct one. It made no changes to the UI. Of the wrong ones, they came in 3 different flavours… But, no matter which of those three flavours you got, you did *not* get the corresponding file (BigBank, BodyTint or CharacterCreate)… At that point, EQ said "gack" and died. Read about the patch explained (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6048), or go right to Cairenn’s Fix (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6012), in the Patch Fixes (http://www.eqgui.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=36) forum.

Be sure to check out the new NPC Conversation Journal (http://everquest.station.sony.com/legacy/f_npcjournal.jsp) announced for The Legacy of Ykesha (http://everquest.station.sony.com/legacy/index.jsp), and join in the discussion (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6068)!
