Well, I'll hand it to you and say that this is a good first skin. Although I myself would not use it, I'm sure others would. I would like to offer some advice however for any future skins ya do/etc.

1) Detail & cleanliness is a must for a UI to be popular. Even though I'm not a huge poster, I look at almost every UI post here and some others in addition. I've found that most people, including myself, prefer UI/UI skins that are more compact and constant.

2) The less professional &/or sleek the UI is, the less it will be downloaded. This is fairly self-explanatory I think. While the graphics you used were appropriate, the quality of the graphics was not up to par. I'd suggest tweaking your graphics as much as possible before trying to utilize them into a UI. If they look shantily made, people won't use the UI.

3) Try something really unique. Ok, while I do like the typical stuff, I feel that trying new and orginal ideas is a great way to become established. If your UI/skin is sporting some awesome new features that nobody except you has thought of yet, then more than likely people will react positively to your work.


By no means was this post a negative post. I'm calling this positive criticism, so please do not be offended by my suggestions. Dunno why I posted on your UI, but.....just felt it was right. :D

Hope this helps.:cool:
