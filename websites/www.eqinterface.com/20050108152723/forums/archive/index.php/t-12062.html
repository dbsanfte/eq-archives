I hate the title bars, but if you do just a false then the border is messed up. change both lines as indicated below and it'll look great.

change this:
<Style_Titlebar>true</Style_Titlebar>
to this:
<Style_Titlebar>false</Style_Titlebar>

and this:
<DrawTemplate>WDT_Rounded</DrawTemplate>
to this:
<DrawTemplate>WDT_RoundedNoTitle</DrawTemplate>

You'll find these lines near the bottom of the xml file

I use this on most of my windows, like i need to know this is the inventory or the container window. Hope this helped, have fun.
