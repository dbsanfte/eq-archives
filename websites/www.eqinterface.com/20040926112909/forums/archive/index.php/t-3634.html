Here's a tip on creating textures that tile:

I'm using Photoshop 6

Load up your texture,

should be 64x64 128x128 256x256 etc.. all power of 2 sizes

Select Filter -> Other - > Offset choose and offset of HALF the image width (so 128 if it is 256)

Then you should see the 'seam' in the center of the image

Now just use a 50precent opacity brush with the Stamp or Brush or Smear or whichever you like, Stamp is best, and make that seam disappear.

Now do the offset again to return the image to the correct place,

then do another offset this time in the Y direction !

Now you should see again the seam but a horizontal seam in the middle of the texture, again, smear/stamp/brush it off

then repeat the last offset to return the texture exactly how it was in the first plac,e except now the borders of the texture will blend properly together whjen tiled.

tada!

PS: dont stretch your texture.. most of the time it looks ugly
