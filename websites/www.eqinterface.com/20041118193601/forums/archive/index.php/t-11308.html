I use my own, FysteUI 1.5 . Keep in mind when you look at the screenshot, that I pushed the Opacity to 100% for that shot. When I'm actually playing, I set the fade value of everything on screen to 25%. So although the windows are on the screen, they're all semi-transparent.

Also, the 36 minute timer isn't visible, but it's got con-color around the timer window. I use 36 minute because that timer is broken into 6 blocks, and 36 minutes makes it 6 chucks of 6 minute intervals... nice for Mend. Also nice because most spawn times are 18 - 24 minutes, so it's easy to calculate.

Anyway, good luck !

07-15-2004, 03:08 PM
I just started using this UI. It's sweet! One question though, I don't really need either timer. How do I remove the one that's not based on the friends list?

The one that's on the Friend's list is EQUI_FriendsWnd.xml
The other one is on the Target window EQUI_TargetWindow.xml

Delete either of those two files in the fyste folder ( or whatever folder you installed the UI in ) and it will automatically use the default UI window.
