I would love to see the following new types for LDoN adventures:

Progress Gauge - showing kills/collections out of max as a gauge.

Progress Label - showing "17 / 51" kills or collections out of max.

Elapsed Time Gauge - showing elapsed time as a gauge.

Elapsed Time Label - "43 / 90" minutes elapsed in the adventure.

It would be nice to have the two gauges, one above the other, so that we can quickly gauge our progress in the adventure.
