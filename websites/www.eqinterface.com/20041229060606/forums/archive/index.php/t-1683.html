I think it comes down to needing to make several different downloads in the case of complete re-skins like Quartz, StoneAngel, and the like. Why? Because the graphics don't seem to come out so seamless when run at lower resolutions, and everything just seems to get bigger the lower you go.

I do agree that there should be more skins out there for the lower resolution people, but I don't think they are gonna look nearly as nice.

Now, the one I am working on and others like it, such as Vert's, are a skin-mod rather than a whole skin. I say this because you can run these UI mods at various resolutions, since it's mostly the default graphics that have been revamped.

Just keep in mind though: the lower your resolution, the more crowded any UI is gonna be on your system.
