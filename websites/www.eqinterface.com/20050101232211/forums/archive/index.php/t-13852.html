I think the hotkey to bring up the player window is Alt-P.

You can also bring it up by clicking on the player button in the selector window, which you can bring up with Alt-W if it is hidden.

If that doesn't bring it back, it might have gotten moved offscreen. You can try editing or renaming your UI_char_server.ini file to bring it back.
