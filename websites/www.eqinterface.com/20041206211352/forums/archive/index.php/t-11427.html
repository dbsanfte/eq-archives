If you're on Dial-Up, it could be that you've got settings for your modem improperly set, and data-corruption of files is the result.

If you're not on a dial-up connection, it could be many other things, such as a severely fragmented hard drive, or bad version of the zip tool.

If this is the only site for which you have problems, that narrows things down a little, but you don't give much information in your question.

So, try defragmenting your hard drive, downloading an updated version of WinZip (or other un-zipping uitility) and try again.
