Me and Chronwaas have been working on building a UI from scratch the past couple months. Its nearly complete with just some cosmetic work here and there. Would like your comments/opinions on the screenshots Im posting. There are many things not shown in the screenshots we have completely redid as far as graphics and layout so bare with us.

Normal view casting spell underwater.
http://bellsouthpwp.net/y/e/yeak2383/files/normal.jpg


View with inventory open.
http://bellsouthpwp.net/y/e/yeak2383/files/inventoryopen.jpg

View with spellbook open.
http://bellsouthpwp.net/y/e/yeak2383/files/spellbookopen.jpg



This UI was built with most vital information up and down the center of the screen. Your still able to view information and see whats going on with full inventory open. The gauge globes truely look like globes being filled or losing liquid apposed to circles that just have a flat fill look to them. Pet window and spell casting bar look as if they are connected but they arent they are two seperate window for those who wish to just use Spells and no pets. Just turn off pet window and slide spell bar over to the edge. Pet window has a secondary health gauge you can watch as clicking your pet commands. Main target window has pet health as well to keep eyes focused on the center. If you have any questions or comments please let us know so we can try and make adjustments before release.

Thanks
