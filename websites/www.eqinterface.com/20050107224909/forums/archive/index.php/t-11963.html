I might get yelled at for this, but what the hell. Go into your EQ directory and look for this file... YourCharacter.ini

You'll have alot of those, one for each character you use, any special settings, bazaar prices, ect ect ect. You want to do this for each crit you have. Delete that file, then run the patcher. It'll automatically replace everything, and load your UI back to default.

It'll at least fix that problem, then you can try to re-load the UI and see what happens.
