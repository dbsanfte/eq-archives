If you go into the XML you will find the HP guage section. You will need to modify the size and location (or else it will just grow down and to the right).

You may need to update the size of the window itself (located close to the bottom) to accommidate the larger size.

Generally, the graphic will stretch to the parimeters set for the guage size. I found this out on accident when I was making my Spectre Pet Info window. I rushed through and forgot to change the actual size of the guage to accommodate the larger HP meter (the scythe) and ended up with a skinny scythe that was incredibly distorted.

10-15-2002, 03:59 AM
From the file you uploaded:

<GaugeDrawTemplate>
<Background>A_GaugeBackground</Background>
<Fill>A_GaugeFill</Fill>
<Lines>A_GaugeLines</Lines>
<EndCapLeft>A_GaugeEndCapLeft</EndCapLeft>
<EndCapRight>A_GaugeEndCapRight</EndCapRight>
</GaugeDrawTemplate>


Nuke that <Background>...</Background> line and it should work... I always nuke everything but the <Fill> line so if just taking out the background does not work, try taking everything but the Fill line out and then adding them back one by one until you get what you want.
