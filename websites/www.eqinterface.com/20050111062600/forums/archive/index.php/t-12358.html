I think the borders are turned off for chat windows in that UI.

Open the EQUI_ChatWindow.xml scroll down to the bottom and look for -
<Style_Border>false</Style_Border>
change it to -
<Style_Border>true</Style_Border>
doing so may change the seemless look of the UI but it should allow you to move the chat boxxes.
