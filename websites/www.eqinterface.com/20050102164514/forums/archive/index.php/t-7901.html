Those are your actual errors. It means that your EQUI_Animations.xml file isn't current. Couple options for you.

1) You can check and see if the author of the mod has released a patch for it. If they haven't, you can try to contact them to get a fix for it.

2) You can fix it yourself, following the instructions in the Patch Fixes forum. You'll have to go through and find where those things have been added to the Animations file.

3) You can say "to heck with this one" and download one that is current.
