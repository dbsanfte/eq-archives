I have had good luck along the same lines with pieces parts and just setting the transparency fairly low on most things unless I mouse over them. I got the usual suspects, nice solid bright static target ring (the gnomish gear one atm) a bag mod, taget and hps windows with percents, a hotbutton mod with two tabs on the right for inv/clickies...and all except my chat and hitsmode windows pretty much fade out except when I mouseover. Makes for good visibility.

Plus the benefit over complex unified UIs in that it doesn't break every patch. I avoid anything that messes with EQUI_Animations.xml
