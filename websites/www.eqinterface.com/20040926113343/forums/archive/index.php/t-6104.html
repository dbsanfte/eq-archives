I can't find any way to change the text color of the titlebar text:

So,
Let us change the color of the text of a titlebar by adding a <TextColor> block to a <Screen item = "ItemName"> block!


EX:
<Screen item = "BuffWindow">
<!--<ScreenID/>-->
<Text>Effects</Text>
<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>255</B>
</TextColor>

Would make the text of the buff window titlebar a deep blue "Effects"!!
