And in addition... Your picture will be saved as a bitmap (.bmp) format. The picture file will saved like so; EQ000000.bmp, EQ000001.bmp and so on in consecutive order. These files will be found in your Everquest folder. Usually this will be in the default location of Program Files (i.e., C:\Program Files\Everquest), unless you placed it elsewhere. Good luck and have fun.
