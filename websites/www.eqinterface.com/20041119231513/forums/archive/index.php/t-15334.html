this should work

in red are the numbers you had in the texture calls, they did not match the graphic

<Ui2DAnimation item="TK_SpellBookOpen">
<Frames>
<Texture>tkspellbar.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>49</X>
<Y>144</Y>177
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>29</CX>
<CY>16</CY>
</Size>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
<Ui2DAnimation item="TK_SpellBookClosed">
<Frames>
<Texture>tkspellbar.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>49</X>48
<Y>161</Y>194
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>29</CX>
<CY>16</CY>
</Size>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
