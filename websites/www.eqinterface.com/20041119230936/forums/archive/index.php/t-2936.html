You can place the casting gauge in nearly any window. However, the gauge itself only shows whilst you are casting a spell. If you wanted to show it all the time, just make a graphic of a fake casting gauge at full, place it in a window, then place the real casting gauge in precisely the same spot. The gauge will show as a full casting gauge until you cast a spell. Then it will act normally. Once the spell is complete, it will go back to a full gauge again.

Just one of the little tricks you come across.

-Cormanth
