Change to:

I dont see why not, they both are pretty much the same, ATI vs Nvidia
Actually the cards (nVidia vs ATI) are not very identical at all. I work in the video card industry and the programing in each card are quite different. The pathing used in nVidia is rather straight forward vs. ATI's. ATI using multi pathing techniques to get the images to work by using less workhorse power out of its chips. Hence the reason probably why SOE uses nVidia's chipset formula. Less work on the programmers end to get the results vs. trying to alter the program to get the same results using ATI's pathing.
