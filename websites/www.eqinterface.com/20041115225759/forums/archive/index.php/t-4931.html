I had three goals when I made this UI:

1.) Put everything button or clickable think in a single area of the screen. I hate roaming all over the place with the cursor, trying to target the right thing, especially during lag.

2.) Put has much info on the screen as possible. I'm an information junkie. I look at my stats a ton, to check buffs and decide on loot, so I wanted it up all the time. Not only that, but I have two group windows.... One just for targeting and one for the hp of group mates. I took this idea from Romelo.

3.) Do both of the above, while keeping most of the action visible. I like to see everything going on. I need see corpses, mobs, adds, etc so that I know how to act. I made a ton of stuff transparent, so I think I accomplished this, even though there is a ton of stuff on the screen.

I've been working on this for a couple of weeks.... I think it's the perfect UI for me, let me know what you think.
