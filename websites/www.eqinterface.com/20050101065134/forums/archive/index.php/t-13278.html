The easiest solution to lines 1 and 2 is to remove your custom LFG window, unless you are really attached to it.

If you really like your custom LFG window, you will have to make the changes described in the 2/10/04 patch thread (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?postid=65708#post65708). You will most likely have to come up with a location for the new checkbox to fit with the custom layout.

If you are not so attached to it, just delete the file EQUI_GroupSearchWnd.xml from your UI directory.

The attack indicator animation fix can be found in this post (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?postid=65712#post65712) in the same thread.

You might also check to see if the authors of those mods have posted fixes since 2/10/04.
