if you have done as much software fiddling as you can and it is still causing problems...there really is only one answer....format.
It sucks to do but it really does help a system run smothly...windows builds up so much junk that format evey 3-4 months does wonders for system performance.

One thing you can do to make the formating a little easier in the future is when creating the partion for your hard drive make a second one with 10gigs of space and put EQ in there. Eq does not write anything in the registry so you can format your windows drive and leave the EQ one alone to save the re-install/patch time.

Before you go and reformat your drive... Try giving your computer a test run at PCPitStop (http://www.pcpitstop.com) and click on the key (which says 'Ready for a free PC tune up?') to start the run. Once it finishes it will tell you what is wrong with your system and what to do. You can click on the Windows link under the Subsystems heading and it will tell you what is running in the background and what you should turn off. I found this site extremely helpful when my system was lagging at load ups and such and found a few programs hidden that were consuming power and making my game play a pain. Hope it helps.
