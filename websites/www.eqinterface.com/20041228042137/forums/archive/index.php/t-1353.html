What is the command to resize the window of the game to leave a spot an empty spot at bottom of screen or where ever i want it ?
Pini Uldar
08-23-2002, 05:58 PM
/viewport X Y CX CY

Where X and Y are the position of the upper left corner of the screen (0 0 would be the upper left corner of the monitor) and CX and CY are the new screen size.

So if you run 1280x1024 but want 1024x768 picture center on the top of your monitor you can do:

/view 128 0 1024 768
