A little more to the point tho....

Do not place your custom ui files in the SKINS folder.
There should be a folder called UIFILES.
Under UIfiles should be Default.
Next to Default should be the new folder you make for your custom uifiles.
C:\
-- Program Files
-----Sony
-------Everquest Trilogy
-----------SomeOtherDirectories
-----------Skins
+-------------Default
-----------UIfiles
+--------------Default
+--------------CustomFiles

OH.....and EDIT:
Looks like you installed Everquest Originally, then bought EQTrilogy and Reinstalled it all....this tends to really 'futz' up EQ in general....
Suggestion: UNINSTALL the Orginal Everquest Folder (Possibly Both) then reinstall ONLY with Everquest Trilogy and whatever other expansions you have. Forget that you even own the first CD you installed.
