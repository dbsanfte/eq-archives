The best way to describe this sort of class system is a citation from the Square game Seiken Densetsu 3, also called Secret of Mana 2. How it worked was this:

You started at level 1, say you were Angela, the caster. You started as a magician. When you got to level 20 you could choose to follow the Light path or the Dark path. When you got to level 50, you could choose to follow Light or Dark again. Whichever way you chose would influence your skills. For example, going light with Angela would give her more elemental/holy spells, while going dark would give her more destructive, dark spells. This is a very similar way of doing it, except instead of light/dark you have different classes. But it's an easy way of explaining if you've played the game. :D
