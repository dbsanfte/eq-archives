a fresh version (and any patches) is always a good idea if having trouble.

a lot of the solaria issues I saw right after the last patch were because people were still using Solaria-BETA which we had some extra files (that we combinded together for the final release)..

Now, I doubt your using any kind of Vert beta... that was just an example.

the other way we found people's compasses were off is the would repace the strip (the background that says n/w/s/e, etc) with the default or a custom one.. but NOT replace the frame..

Vert's compass is WIDER that the default, sooo the center is to the RIGHT of the center of the default if you line them up on the left edges (ie, default is 80 pix wide, vert is 120, soo the center is 20pix to the right). soooo if you put the default strip in there, the center shifts back to the left, but the FRAME doesnt know to move to the left.. sooo it appears the compass is off. (this can happen in reverse too)
