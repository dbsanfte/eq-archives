Okay...I've had so much trouble with these that I finally went to EQ chat live to talk to a representative...and what he told me was SO simple. Okay...when you open EQ the first thing you do is download any new files and whatnot due to patches...if you have autoplay clicked...unclick it. Go to Advanced options and check all of the left boxs except the first one and download the package extras with the expansions that weren't introduced in with the patches. Mine took 33 minutes to complete...but it took care of my 2 week problem!!! I can now download any UI skin and upload it into the game...YAY!!! So for all of those who keep having UI troubles...try doing this and see if it works...GOOD LUCK!!!!
