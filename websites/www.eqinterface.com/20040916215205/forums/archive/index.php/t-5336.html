This is my first publically released UI, so don't hurt me too much =P It's completely up to date with the latest patch. Works best for casters, but it's good for melees too.

Picture: http://www.tirelth.com/peacock.jpg

Zip file: http://www.tirelth.com/peacock.zip

Any questions, contact me online as stormhammer.ainyan or by email at tirelth@tirelth.com.

Features include:

- Bard buff box
- 8 clickie slots + 8 general inventory slots
- Pet box
- Percents for health, mana, XP, and AXP
- Casting bar located inside the player window
- Auto-attack flash surrounding target instead of player name
- Color-cycling hotbutton mouseover
- Selector window is now stat window

Works best in 1280 x 1024, however can easily be used in other resolutions, with a bit of switching.
