Originally posted by Kiegh
does it show that as the buff and nothing else? cause that would be nice. If it shows the buff icon and this then I wouldn't want the buff icon and would only want the timer thingy.
The timer itself isn't connected to any thing in-game, so it has no knowledge of what buffs are up, etc. You'll see both your regular buff icons and the timer on screen. Whenever you cast a buff, you simply move the slider on the timer to point to the current position of the bar. The timer bar will continue moving, and will give you a constant visual reminder of when your buff will wear out. When the bar reaches the slider again it means that one full duration of the timer has elapsed.
