Some of the details are
30-Day EverQuest Trial (new accounts only, no credit card required for activation)
Activates original EverQuest and the following expansion packs EverQuest: The Ruins of Kunark®, EverQuest: The Scars of Velious®, EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin™, EverQuest: The Planes of Power™ and EverQuest: The Legacy of Ykesha™.
Email offer will be sent out the week of May 31, 2004 with complete details
