03-11-2003, 12:09 AM
The most common cause of continual zone crashes these days is corrupt texture compression files. Depending on your video card and drivers Eq may have defaulted to using texture compression.

Do a search in your EQ directory for .txc files and delete any that you find. If that fixes your problem then make sure to go into your options (when you first start EQ) and turn off compression.

Hope this helps.
