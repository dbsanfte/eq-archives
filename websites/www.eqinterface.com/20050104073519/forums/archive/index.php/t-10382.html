The satchel and the augments pictures are in some of the new files introduced with LDoN. I THINK the files are windowpieces 26 + 27. The numbers may be wrong but they are the new windowpieces files that have the satchel and augments in them. Your modded UI probably has files with those names in them already and that is why they aren't showing up correctly.

There are a few threads on these boards regarding this issue. Just do a search and I bet you'll find your answer. Or you could just delete windowpieces26 + 27 and than your UI can use the default files making them show up.
