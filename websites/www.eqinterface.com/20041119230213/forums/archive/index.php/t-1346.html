Don't copy all the files from the default directory into your new directory. That'll make patch days much harder.

Just put your new file(s) into a new directory like EverQuest/uifiles/hotkeymod. Just put your EQUI_HotButtonWnd.xml in there and in game type "/loadskin hotkeymod 1". That will switch your skin to the hotkeymod skin and preserve your settings (that's the cryptic 1).
