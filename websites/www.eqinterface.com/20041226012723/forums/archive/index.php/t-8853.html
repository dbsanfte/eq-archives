I do it by hand, without a special tile editor tool.

1) Create the texture in a file by itself, say 256x256.

2) Create a new image twice as large in both dimensions (512x512).

3) Copy your texture four times to tile the larger image.

4) Copy the center 256x256 region into a new image. All of the discontinuities will show in this part of the image. You can select any 256x256 portion of the 512x512 image. Try to choose that region so that the part you want to clean up is in the center of your selection.

5) Clean up the center region of your new tile.

6) Go to 3). Repeat these steps until your 256x256 image tiles the 512x512 image nicely.

7) Select the 256x256 region of the 512x512 image that you want to be the upper left of your texture. Save it as a new image. Voila, you have a repeating texture.
