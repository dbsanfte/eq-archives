useless info:
The monitor driver tells windows what resolutions and refresh rates your monitor can handle. This driver is normaly stored on an eprom in your monitor and given to windows automaticly (PnP) when you plug in your monitor. However in the device manager you should be able to load a default driver that should allow you to try that resolution.
