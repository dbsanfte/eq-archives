I started with the Informative PW by Intrinzix and tweaked it to suit my needs. Umbralor is modeling my work so that I can show off the pet features.

First Name
Last Name
HP Bar + Pet HP Bar
HP% Pet% Mana%
Mana Bar
XP% AA%
HP/maxHP

Unfortunately, there is no way (that I've found) to turn off pet% and mana% if you have no pet or mana. To do this, remove or comment out the lines in the XML file that say

<Pieces>Pet_HPPercent</Pieces>
<Pieces>Pet_HPPSign</Pieces>
and
<Pieces>ManaPercent</Pieces>
<Pieces>PW_ManaPSign</Pieces>

respectively.
