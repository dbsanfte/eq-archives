Only hotkeys right now. There's no way in the XML to wire up a button to an action. We're hoping they give us EQTypes for this at some point, but we really don't know if they are for or against this.

It should be possibly for them to implement it so that every item in your socials pages has an EQType and then you put those on a button, it's like pushing that social. But until something like that happens, buttons/hotkeys/spellgems/money and the list are all limited to being in the window they are currently in if you want functionality.

You already have the ability to do it on the command line using /alt activate ### but I don't know the number offhand for exodus. Then you can make a social that's
/g No time to chug one for the road, we is outta here! EXODUS ACTIVATED!
/alt activate ###

But until VI gives us EQTypes for buttons and tells us what the EQTypes for each social on each bank of the social buttons are, it's not possible.

If you wanted to, you could make your hotbox larger and put Button 0 away from the rest of the buttons and unmap 0 to it. Then make hotkey 0 on all pages of your hotkeys be Exodus.

You could even put an inventory item like ES arms or something you use a lot into the space where the last button was and then make the Exodus button look different than the other buttons.
