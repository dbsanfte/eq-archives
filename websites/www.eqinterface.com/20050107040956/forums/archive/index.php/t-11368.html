Yes and no. Sometimes, it just "works". Other times, you like bits from one and parts from another that "conflict" with each other. If you want to go the mix'n'match route, couple suggestions for you:

a) do NOT copy your default folder;

b) put in one piece that you like. Go in-game to make sure it works. If it does, make a back-up copy of that folder;

c) add the next piece that you like. Repeat as per b;

d) get to know your UIError.txt log file (found in your main EQ directory). It is your best friend. It tells you what is wrong. So, if you add a piece and it no longer works in-game, your UIError.txt will tell you what isn't getting along.
