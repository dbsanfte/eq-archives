This Caster Kit was design to enable you “the user” to customize inventory items to those that you find most useful for the UI’s I developed. PDF format was selected as a delivery system to provide you with security against installing trojens on your system.

TO USE THIS UI MODIFICATION YOU MUST DOWNLOAD ADOBE READER VERSION 6.0. http://www.adobe.com

Target Window: The original mod to this target window (a 1 minute timer added along with casting bar) was done by Xymarra. I added the pet bar to the window along with 2
inventory slots. This allows me to to keep my focus in one location when I have a charmed pet and click easily on an inventory slot item when needed. The top of the
window is the target name with Hp's. At the bottom is the pet bar and in between is the casting bar. To the right is a one minute timer.

Pet Window: This is my modification to the pet window. I added Cairenn’s pet stats page to Grimmier’s original pet inventory window and customized to my needs.

Container Window: This is my modification to Cairenn’s container mod. I reduced the size of the top inventory slots and made enough room for eight of them. With this modification I have extended my on screen casting abilities by keeping a container up and swapping click able items in and out of the inventory slots.

HotButton Window: This setup allows me to minimize mouse movement when buffing. The spell cast window and hot button window are the same height and placed side by side
in my setup. Hot button 1 is set up with the /rtarget command. To the right of the hot buttons the primary, range and inventory bags are available.
