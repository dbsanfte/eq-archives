Ok two things I would suggest although I will have to admit I am not a graphics person and so these are just guesses.

First of all you have to create a <TextureInfo> instance with the filename info in it for your target.tga. Then use that TextureInfo as the <Texture> in your Animation.

Secondly, I may be wrong, but I could swear that the max size on textures is 256x256 and as such your 500 width will not work.
