Oh, sorry. hehe

I forgot that once you touch the window_pieces they lose thier mask/transparency/alpha flag. So, once you make your change to the individual item (in this case, the recessed box) within the image map, you need to redo the transparency/mask/alpha channel (whatever you want to call it) for that particular window_piece.tga

In my case, what I had to do was select the black background, invert the selection, and save it as the Alpha channel. Then just save it as the normal 24bit compressed .tga as you would for anything else.
