You can do it now, if you want to use the post-patch-copy method. Just create your html hierchy and load all your html file in the help folder. Then, after the patch is done scanning, copy your version of the help.html file into the help folder, and click play. If you create a batch file, it's as simple as clicking an icon on your desktop before pressing play.

It's a work-a-round, but it works.

On the other hand, I agree with your 100%. There should be a way for us to do it without bypassing the system.
