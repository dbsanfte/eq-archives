News - 11/04/02

Thomus King (http://pub10.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10.showMessage?topicID=74.topic) has released the final revision of the Standard v2.0 spellbook, and announces that the final t.king-remelio celtic inventory will be released soon; it will have a feature that will allow custom graphics to be placed in the auto-equip area.

As with all theT.King/Remelio UI creations, you can download the Spellbook right here in the Featured Skins (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=27) area.
There's a nice blurb about EQInterface on his site, too... thanks, Thomus! ;)

Also in Featured Skins, two new additions to the Vertebrae/Sokol (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=31) area: Sokol has updated the Advanced-CastSpellWindow v2 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=486), and Kudane has released an updated Purple Vert 2.2 Nexium (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=483)!
