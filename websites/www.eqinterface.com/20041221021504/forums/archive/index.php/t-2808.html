One thing, and sure, I'm just being picky, but a zipfile and zip/unzip program isn't a zip drive. A Zip Drive is a physical device, made by IO Mega that works somewhat like a floppy drive (gross simplification). Zipfiles use a compression algorythm to make a file or group of files take less space, and a zip/unzip program does the compression/decompression of these files. The two things are really only related in name, but other than that are completely different. You can have zip files stored on Zip Disks that work in your Zip Drive, but you can't download a Zip Drive from the internet, only buy them.
