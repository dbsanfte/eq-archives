Since EQ doesnt require a large amount of bandwidth only a small steady stream a 802.11b wireless connection will work great for EQ (even playing multiple accounts) as long as you have a good signal with the access point/router.

You will notice a difference when downloading large files when using a 802.11b connection. If you are worried about that you may want to look into using the new 802.11g system.

Another thing to note about wireless, is the higher your encryption level, the slower the connection is going to be.

It takes time to encrypt all the data going in and out between the wireless devices.

Depending on how secure your network needs to be, you could lower your encryption level to gain back some speed.

Personally, I use 128bit, but my router has options for 64 and 256 also.
