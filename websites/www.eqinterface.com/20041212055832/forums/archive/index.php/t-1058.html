If you have custom mod files or skins and then EQ patches some of the same files you have customized, the UI may be unstable or crash because you are using an out of date file now. You need to identify which files were patched and either get updated ones from the author who made them or go back to the default UI until such time that you get an updated one. Type /loadskin default 1 to return to the default without resetting the window positions.

08-20-2002, 03:41 PM
If this did start happening after the patch on the 14th, you are not screwed (at least I wasn't).

Just do this...

1. Rename your custom skin folder to anything...this way when you log in it will not find your skin and give you the default, but keep you in the new ui.

2. In the new ui default skin.....hit Alt-D and get rid of the stupid new compass they added with that patch....just close it out.

3. Log out and rename your skin folder back to what it was and come back in (or just do the /load newfoldername 1 to reload your custom skin from whatever you renamed it to).

It was the addition of the new compass window that screwed my gui up, turning it off and going back solved it for me.
