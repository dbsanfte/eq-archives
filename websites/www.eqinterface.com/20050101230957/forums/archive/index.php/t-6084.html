Okay, what you need to do is:

Go to your custom folder.
Find the one called EQUI_SelectorWnd.xml.
Right click on the name of the file and choose "Open With".
Choose NotePad.
Now, under the "Edit" command on the toolbar at the top of the screen, pick "Find".
Type in what the Patch Fix message tells you to find.
At the end of that, copy and paste the other information that is in that message for that particular window.
Save and close.
Repeat as necessary for the other windows affected.

That explain any better? I hate trying to tell folks what I mean via written text, it is so much easier in voice, or by actually physically showing someone. =/
