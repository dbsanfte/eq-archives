12-28-2003, 08:59 PM
<Button item="ACTW_Unclickable1">
<ScreenID>ACTW_Unclickable1</ScreenID>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>5</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>55</CX>
<CY>580</CY>
</Size>
<Style_Transparent>false</Style_Transparent>
<Style_Checkbox>false</Style_Checkbox>
<TextColor>
<R>255</R>
<G>255</G>
<B>255</B>
</TextColor>
<ButtonDrawTemplate />
</Button>

Merely as an example, of course. You can change size and position all you like. Completely invisible button. I use it to get rid of the right-click menus on the main portion of any window, without turning the menus off completely.
