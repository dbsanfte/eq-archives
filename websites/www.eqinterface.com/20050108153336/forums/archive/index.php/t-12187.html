When we upload UI's and mods there is an option to include the resolutions that the mod is compatable with. All you need to is type in the resolution in the Search box on the left hand side of the EQInterface.com home page.

Using 1024x768 (notice I removed the spaces) I came up with a page full of results.

Also note that many of the UI's posted at the larger resolutions have optional versions for smaller reolutions. Cairenn's are a good example and I believe that Xym just patched his/her(?) 1024x768 version.
