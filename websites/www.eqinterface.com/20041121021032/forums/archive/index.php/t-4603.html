lunakin

Sony won't let us.

Some of the window elements are hardcoded so that they only work in their window of origin.

Gems only work in the spellcastwind.xml, autoequip, dest buttons only work in the inventory.xml, ect. You can force the UI to create these items in another window, but they will be NON functioning. As it stands, only inventory slots, labels, and gauges can be removed from their window of origin and retain functionality.
