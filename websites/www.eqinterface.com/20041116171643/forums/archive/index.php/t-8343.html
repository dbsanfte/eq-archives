Hi folks! I just had a fun and eventful day creating my first modification. I really didn't care much for the cartography window that came with LoY, and I thought, frankly, I can do better! So what started as trying to make some small asthetic improvements ended up begin an all day project and a complete overhaul. ;)

UPDATE (1 June '03): I've cleaned up the buttons so they weren't yellow on a light background. The button borders were also darkened so that the style matched the main border a bit more. Also the images are completely independant so they shouldn't intefere with any other mods. The compass was also removed from this mod.
