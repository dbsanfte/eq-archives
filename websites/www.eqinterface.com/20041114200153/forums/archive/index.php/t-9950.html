SoesVelious Beta

My attempt to re-create the Velious UI... If you didn't play EQ during Velious this UI will probably not appeal to you ;)

http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/p.php?s=&id=2436

Posting Beta so people can add/remove/fix things before I finalize it. The spell gem bar could still use some work!

You can resize the windows, change the transparency, change the tint etc just like old Velious.

Unzip to your UIFILES sub-directory in your EverQuest folder, in game type: /loadskin soesvelious
