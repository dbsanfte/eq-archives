This is 3 simple packages, mainly for my guildmates in Arm & Casters to download.

Alterations:
- gives percentages of health in personal, target and group windows.
- gives exp in personal window
- friends window is used as a switching weapons and clicking armor window
- pet window gives stats and weight
- inventory is old inventory style
- bags have the bag icon removed to make them smaller
- casting window is staggered
- help window is a list of locs
- hotbutton window has a tiny column of your inventory slots down the right side
- (edit) added Geddine's buff window
- (edit) added Arista's timer for the casters

Altered files:

EQUI_Animations.xml
EQUI_BuffWindow.xml
EQUI_CastSpellWnd.xml
EQUI_Container.xml
EQUI_FriendsWnd.xml
EQUI_GroupWindow.xml
EQUI_HelpWnd.xml
EQUI_HotbuttonWnd.xml
EQUI_Inventory.xml
EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml
EQUI_TargetWindow.xml

How to Install the Mod:

This is really easy.

1. Go to the folder Everquest/uifiles and unzip all the folders in the zip file, into the uifiles folder.
3. Load up eq, and load up your character.
4. To use the new mod just type /load hybrid 1
or /load melee 1
or /load caster 1
depending on what class your character is.
