09-09-2002, 11:22 PM
Ok, so people want:

1) horizontal hot buttons
2) horizontal spell bars
3) larger icons for hot buttons and perhaps spell icons(?) to account for larder screen resolutions
4) a seperate window for some of the chat because
5) my idea of the chat window appears to be too small

What I am working on, before I even begin to release anything, is a working version of this mod for atleast 3 of the common screen resolutions (800x600, 1024x768, and 1280x1024). This means that, in theory, all of the bars, icons, gauges and buttons will be approximately the same size in whichever resolution you use.

As for the spell buttons and hotkeys... I think I am going to have a vertical version, as well as a horizontal one, so that you can decide which you want to use.

At lower resolutions, there will be spacial limitations to deal with. Therefore, some sacrifices have to be made.

- For one, I am designing this is a cleric mod, so a pet window is not needed and well not be displayed. If you wish to use this mod and do have a pet, you are welcome to show the pet window. However, I am not at this time anticipating a pet window to go along with this design.

- Some other things not to look for with this mod are, a tracking window and possibly the actions window. I may incorporate some features of the actions window into another window or tab section.

I've also recently made another startling revolation... my buttons and gauges look really cool when you make them different colors. So, I've decided to turn the borders graphics around my buttons and gauges into backgrounds. This way you can change the background tint of your windows in game, and get different colored buttons and gauges. Not a bad idea, eh?

Please, keep your suggestions coming as they help me to make an all around better mod.

-Cormanth

I have been working on this UI quite diligently for the past 2 weeks, trying to make everything work as close to what was requested as possible.
