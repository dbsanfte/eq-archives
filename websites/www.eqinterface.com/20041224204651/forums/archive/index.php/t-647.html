I did alot of playing with this function..

here is what i learned

1. it will not distort your view
by this I mean, that if you make a view port that is 100 pix wide and 480 pix tall it will look like your looking through a crack in a fense.. it will not "squish" everything you woudl see at 640 pix into 100 pix

2. It you keep the correct purportion you see everything
640x480 800x600 1024x768 1280x1024... what ever setting you use, if you keep your view port the correct "8x6" ratio you will see everything, just shrunk down. anything else will crop (see above)

3. Smaller Rez seems to help lag
So far all test show that if you are getting horrid lag a quick view port of 500x375 will allow you to see and reduce your lag to save your butt.. again this is being tested soooo give feed back if you see this work.

hope those 3 tips help.. i will work on a more comprehisive(sp) instructions including pictures
