Originally posted by Koradin
Very nice work, I have been using this skin for my necro, but in the previous version, you were able to move the chat windows around to your lliking and add chatwindows etc, any chance you'll fix that in version 3?

1. Open the Grown3 file.

2. Open the EQUI_ChatWindow.xml (You can use your word pad for this)

3. Scroll to the bottom until you see this
<Style_Border>false</Style_Border>
<Style_Sizable>true</Style_Sizable>
<Pieces>CW_ChatOutput</Pieces>
<Pieces>CW_ChatInput</Pieces>
</Screen>
</XML>

4. Change <Style_Border>false</Style_Border> to this <Style_Border>true</Style_Border>

5. Then save and exit.

You will be able to adjust and move freely around now

If you then want to remove the border once your have set up your chat windows just follow these steps again but change the "true" back to "false"

Good luck and enjoy,

Grown g'loth

This is what I would do to insure that it works properly.

1. Exit EQ totally
2. Unizip the UI into your Uifile folder.
3. Change the UI_Grown_93 to your UI_Char_ServerNumber.
4. Place the file your renamed into the folder where your screenshots are located.
5. Click yes to replace the older file.
6. (Optional) Open your EQclient thats located in your EQ folder.
7. Scroll down or use search to find UISkin=Default, change that to read UISkin=Grownfixed.
8. Close that file and save.
9. Log on to EQ as normal and you should be all set!

Hope that helps,

ok ill explain in detial

In the Grownfixed file you DLed there is a file named UI_Grown_93 or something close to that. Right click on that folder and click copy.
Then go to your desktop and right click on it, then click paste.
Now, right click on it once and click rename.

Change it to UI_Name_servernumber now what I mean by this is your In game characters name, like my necros name is Grown and I play on Stormhammer and the Stormhammer number is 93 so it looks like this UI_Grown_93 now if your characters name it Slasher and you played on Tallon Zek it would look something like this UI_SLasher_84. Now to find out what your server number is, open your EQ folder and search for your (lets say your characters name is Slasher) UI_Slasher_## (This "##" is your server number) when you find that, rename the UI_Grown_93 to your infomation. and place it in the same folder, and it will ask you if you want to overwrite the file and click yes.

Hope this helps
