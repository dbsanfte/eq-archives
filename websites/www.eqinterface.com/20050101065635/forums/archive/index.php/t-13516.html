04-17-2004, 04:30 PM
You can look in the .tga file that holds the compass graphics and check to make sure everything is drawn in the right spot. Also if you deviate from the sizes, lets say the compass graphic is 24 x 108 and you extend it to 112 or such, it will distort your compass reading. So make sure u have the correct sizes referenced in the .xml.
