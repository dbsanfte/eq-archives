Here's another tip; Changing the order in the 'Pet_Subwindows' of the Pages changes the order of the tabs at top. First tab is default.

Since you're a cleric I bet you're more like myself and rather have the Player tab 'default' (ie first, always showing) when you load up EQ. To do this, just move the <Pages>Player_Page</Pages> above the other two.
