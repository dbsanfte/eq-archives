/viewport x1, y1, x2, y2 or something to that effect?
Moraethe
07-26-2002, 06:39 PM
/viewport a b c d

a - distance from left

b - distance from top

c - width

d - height

The numbers correspond to resolution size. So an 800 width on 800x600 resolution would span the whole screen width.

Hope that helps.
