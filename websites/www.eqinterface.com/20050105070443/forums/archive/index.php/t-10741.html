yourclass01.tga is the name of the file that has the animation images.


Also don't copy all of the default folder into your custom folder. Just add the actual files you mod into your custom folder. Everquest will use these then get whats missing from the default folder. Much easier on patch day this way. You wont be wondering whats modded and whats not what do I have to fix and whats ok.
