This GUI works best at 1024x768 resolution. If you are using smaller, it may have loaded it off screen. The effects window is actually invisible with no border so unless you cast a buff on your self you won't see it.
One way I have found to get it back is try
/loadskin skinname 1
or if you did that and nothing happened try...
/loadskin skinname
Also, make sure your effects button is clicked in the toolbar. :D


About the spell book. You need to unzip it into the directory you unzipped Bloody Necro to. Just overwrite whatever it asks.

Hope that helps some.
