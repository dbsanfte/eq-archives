I'm guessing you were seeing a black or white box where the compass should be? It's because you need to save an alpha channel in the .tga for EQ to display it properly. Search this section for 'alpha channel' for lots of good info on the specifics.

I attached a .zip of your graphic with an alpha channel added. This should work for you. I just did this for someone else, and it won't let me upload another window_pieces04.tga, so make sure you delete the '_b' from the end of the graphic in the .zip after you drop it in your custom folder.
