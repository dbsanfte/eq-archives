The problem you are having is that the new Actions window has at least one piece in it that is named with the same name a piece of anothe window.

Here is the error that told me this:
[Sat Jan 31 16:48:01 2004] [Line:155 Source:UIFiles\tracie\EQUI_ActionsWindow.xml] Schema error - Duplicate item FT_BlankTabBorder
[Sat Jan 31 16:48:01 2004] Error loading skin: loading default skin instead.

What you will need to do is find FT_BlankTabBorder and replace it with a new name. For example, ACTW_BlankTabBorder. You will have to replace it multiple times. You may want to just go to Edit > Replace and then fill in FT_BlankTabBorder for the Find what and ACTW_BlankTabBorder for the Replace With then click Replace All
