OK your problem is due to SildWidl's want to use mixed case in boolean values and EQ's dislike of such.


<InvSlot item="InvSlot27">
<ScreenID>InvSlot27</ScreenID>
<!--<Font>3</Font>-->
<RelativePosition>True</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>200</X>
<Y>80</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>40</CX>
<CY>40</CY>
</Size>
<Background>A_RecessedBox</Background>
<EQType>27</EQType>
<Style_VScroll>false</Style_VScroll>
<Style_HScroll>false</Style_HScroll>
<Style_Transparent>false</Style_Transparent>
<!--<TooltipReference/>-->
</InvSlot>


Notice it says <RelativePosition>True</RelativePosition>. EQ only recognizes true or TRUE, not True. Since true is more commonly used, I would just use a lower case t and it should all work fine.
