All I know is... Choose what you want as your portrait (make sure it is in .TGA format), then save the new file as inv_portrait.tga. Then copy/paste it into your custom UI folder (and click yes to overwrite the old one) and voila'. Your new inventory portrait.

Exactly what the dimensions of the portriat must be is beyond me. I'm sure someone here can tell you the appropriate size the picture should be.
