I would suggest you check out several different UI's and find one that fits your playstyle. Any of the ones in the Featured section are well done and are usually kept up to date with all the latest changes. Go through those and the Complete Sets section and look at the images to see if they look like they might fit you. I've probably downloaded and tried every complete set out there and quite a few of the individual components trying to find what works for me.

If I had to recommend a UI based on just the info in your post I would suggest one of the Vert or the new Solaria mods. They show off what can be done with a UI mod but still allow you to position the windows to suit your playstyle and I know both of those work with yesterdays patch (except the con color in the target window is off but not so much that it's annoying).
