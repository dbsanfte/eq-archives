For part 2:Provide a link to where the mods are located, this is how you answer: Find the web page you Dl'd the UI's(mods) from. This time don't click the link to download the UI(mod). Move your mouse cursor to the top of the web page. With your LEFT button, click on the web address shown in your web browser's address bar. (example: www.blah.com\something.html) *Make sure the whole address is highlighted. Then right click (press and release the right mouse button) on the highlighted address. A list of options will appear next to your cursor. Move your mouse cursor down and highlight the phrase "copy". Left click(press and release left mouse button) to select copy. [Now everything highlighted is copied to your computer' clipboard] Now goto the web page that you are typing your post(question/response) on. Right click(right button), where you want the copied text/link to be. This time highlight and select(left mouse button) paste. This should paste the address you copied earlier. Congratulations you just copied and pasted, creating a "link". That link will show whoever reads your post the page that you downloaded the UI or mod from.
