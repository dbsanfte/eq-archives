You can edit the file behind the scenes. It's been a few days since I did mine, but I believe it's the UI_<charname>_<servernum> file in the Everquest directory. Look through it for the chat window settings and find where it sets the fonts in each window, then manually change it to what you want.
Karsan
08-01-2002, 07:59 AM
Yep, that's the file. It's in your EQ folder. Go in there and look for these settings:

ChatWindow<x>_FontStyle=<fontsize>

<Example>
ChatWindow1_FontStyle=4

If you don't have "Always Chat Here" selected, you can just click in the text entry on the new chat window and /chatfontsize. That will work fine.
