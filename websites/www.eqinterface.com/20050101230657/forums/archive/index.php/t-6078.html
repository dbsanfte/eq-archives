As you said, the problem is with your Inventory Window. Using a text editor (Word, WordPerfect, NotePad, etc.) to open the file (EQUI_Inventory.xml) in your custom folder.

Using the Search/Find feature, look for:

<StaticAnimation item= "ClassAnim">
<ScreenID>ClassAnim</ScreenID>
<Location>
<X>338</X>
<Y>22</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>64</CX>
<CY>128</CY>
</Size>
<Animation>A_ClassAnim01</Animation>
</StaticAnimation>

(sizes and location may be different)

and

<Pieces>ClassAnim</Pieces>

If either of those aren't in your file, add them. If the ClassAnimation isn't supposed to show, change the size in the first part to 1,1.

As to the other part of your question, re the downloads, you'd have to ask Dolby or Kudane, that's well beyond anything I can answer. I'll point this thread out to them.
