Aditional Checks to make...

1) make sure all windows settings for the card are set correctly. Like not disabling Fog tables, not turning off mip mapping, etc.

2) Depending on system, turn on mip mapping or turn off mip mapping in on already in options before logging into your EQ Account.

3) Its possible with your card that it just can't render layers and fog tables well together. If this is the case like it is on my Laptop then you may always have issues rendering transparent graphics and layers. its hard to accept, but i have to on my laptop. In fact the laptop renders so strange that spell effects are only visable againt the sky layers and not the ground graphics. this due to transparencies and fog tables.
