08-15-2002, 10:18 PM
paste this into your equi_animations.xml file after you make a copy of it and move it into your custom skin folder

<TextureInfo item = "mouse.tga">
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
</TextureInfo>


<!-- Cursors -->

<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_DefaultCursor">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>mouse.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>4</X>
<Y>3</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>

save it and reload.
