and the Player window


Readme.txt

This is a modification of Kuvasie's T.King Player Window; it adds Player Info including Stats, Resists, AC and ATK using modified T.King tubes as backgrounds. It's designed with Melee classes in mind (ATK and AC are displayed prominently), but can be very easily modified to feature other info.


Features:

- T.King Arcane Gauge for player health (by Kuvasie/Remelio)
- Gauges and Percentages for Player health, mana, fatigue, XP, and AAXP
- ATK and AC displayed prominently
- All Stats displayed on the left
- Health, Weight, and all Resists displayed to the right


All graphics by T.King, some code by Kuvasie/Remelio
