Not sure if anyone else has posted this but here it goes...

Ok you downloaded a new UI..from someone do everything they say in the readme.txt..copy and paste ini file..like UI_charactername_servernumber.ini to your eq directory..everything is good to go right?

Nope right after character select you crash..can't seem to figure out what it is =/ bah..you get mad..and go download another UI.

Well it turns out that 9 chances out of 10 your running the game with your sound off..and the UI is set up for sound/MP3 skin.

to fix this you can do one of the two things..enable your sound is one of them..

Or open the UI_charactername_servernumber.ini file in notepad see all that stuff that looks like mumble jumble?

Do a find/search for [MusicPlayerWnd]
Remove that area..

I have shown what it would basically look like..for that ini file..where it says **remove starting here** is where u would start removeing..and where it says ** stop removing** is where you don't want to go any further..
