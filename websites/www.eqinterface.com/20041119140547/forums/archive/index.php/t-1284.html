The simplest way is to go into EQUI_ChatWindow.xml with a text editor such as Notepad or Wordpad. All the way down near the bottom, find the line for StyleBorder and change it from true to false.

This has the side effect that you can no longer resize the window, since it is the borders that you use for that function and they've been removed.

Or you could use my mod. I've made a heavily modified chatwindow that allows you to resize it even though it has no borders, move it by clicking along the top even though it has no titlebar, and other good stuff. I'll upload it when that becomes possible again.
