Doing a few searches on the real errors leads me to believe that you need to perform the updates from the Jan 9th patch.

Real Errors:
[Wed Feb 05 15:45:14 2003] Error: Could not find child StandButton in window PetInfoWindow
[Wed Feb 05 15:45:15 2003] Error: Could not find child SELW_SDBuffToggleButton in window SelectorWindow

The rest are not really errors... its just saying you dont have that file in your custom folder so its going to use the default file. In Martha Stewarts words "Its a good thing".

You can perform the updates listed by Cairenn here (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?threadid=5314) to update your xml. Or you can search the forum (useing the name of your interface) and see if some one has a downloadable patch.
