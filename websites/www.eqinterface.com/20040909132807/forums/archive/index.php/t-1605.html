Modified containers with suggestions from EQTraders posters.

These bags have no borders so it's not possible to move or adjust them with an accidental misclick.
The done button is moved to the top, the combine button is enlarged at the bottom.

It's not possible to move them without clicking in the text area at the top.
It's not possible to right-click them except in the area at the top above the done button.
Lined up side-by-side they look very much like a single window with all 80 container slots.

The extra space on the bottom for containers with no combine button doesn't look very pretty, but clicking there (either right or left) is harmless.

The zip contains a single file, EQUI_Container.xml

Now if you want to use multiple parts from diferent creators, you have to make your own directory and move the .xml, .png, and .tga filesinto it. You can only open one UI at a time from within EQ.

The base UI i use is Quartz. But some things i liked better from other moders like the directional compas, and the 2X8 spell book, and the spell jem bar that has the names of your spells next to it.
Now if I could just make a right clicky window to go down in the botom left corner everything would be great.

Back to what I was saying. what you want to do is just add those peases out of the folders that they are in and put them into the folder you use in eq.

THen when you are loged on just type
/loadskin quartz 1

The space 1 (one) keeps all your placements the same from load to load so you don't have to recoustomize your layout every time.
