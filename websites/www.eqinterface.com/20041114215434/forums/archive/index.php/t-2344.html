Ok, near as I can tell, from looking at the container files, the gap has to do with the fact that the entire bottom of your container is a button. It would seem that the EQ schema wants to put space around the buttons, thus the gap. However, I have been able to make headway by applying a static animation behind the whole area and placing the buttons over it. Thereby filling in the gap instead of trying to remove it. I will continue to look into this further in hopes of finding a more suitable solution.
