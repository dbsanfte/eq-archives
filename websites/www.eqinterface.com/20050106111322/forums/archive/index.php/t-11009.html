Okay, checking through your error log, it is finding a custom target window, just according to you it isn't displaying it.

My suggestions would be for you to:

a) hit Alt T, to make sure that it hasn't been turned off by accident, then if that doesn't bring it up for you;

b) re-load your UI without the .ini file. The chances are that for some reason it has gotten bumped off the viewable area of your screen. By loading it without the .ini, it will force everything to go back to the default locations. Means you'll have to pull everything back where you want it, but at least you will (hopefully) be able to see your target window again.
