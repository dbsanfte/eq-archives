I like the latest changes. However, may I suggest adding an "options" folder that includes the following:

- alternate HB window with Right-Clicks to the right of the Inventory clicks and small paging buttons for the hot-keys at the top.
- alternate Group window with "Invite", "Disband" and "LFG" buttons included.

The rationale is this...a UI can be designed perfectly but if it isn't convenient to use, players may not use it for very long. The suggestions above sound like personal preferences for me but I think most people use them. The only evidence I have to support that claim is browsing through the majority of UIs out there. Most are designed with these features so clearly, folks seem to be asking for them. Plus, with no grouping buttons and the lack of an Actions window (I don't see it but if it's there, forgive me), I'm not even sure how you even join or start a group.

Having said that, I understand that this is your UI and you're designing it around your playstyle. If you're posting it with the idea that it is what it is and if others like it, they're free to use it. If so, then ignore my suggestions and post away. On the other hand, if the idea is to create something for both you and others to use and enjoy, then you may want to consider including an additional options folder with the suggestions above, giving players the choice to use what they like best.

Again, I like your approach to the design. I'm personally always looking for ways to keep my UI footprint low to the ground yet contain the information I need or want. Yours is very close to what I'm looking for so that's why the feedback.

Again, keep up the great work! :)
