My Computer > C > Program Files > Everquest

is most likely the path.

Then look for the file called eqclient.ini.

Right click on it, go down to the bottom of the little window it opens, and click on "Properties".

On the "General" tab, at the bottom are "Attributes". One is Read-only. Make sure that that is NOT checked. If it is, take the check mark back out of the box, hit "Apply" down at the bottom of the window, and then "Ok".
