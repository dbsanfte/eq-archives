08-03-2002, 02:55 AM
The 2 labels that display the percentage of mana and AAXP are:

Player_ManaPercentage
Player_AAXPPercentage

These both have EQTYPE 20 which is the Label EQTYPE for Mana Percent.

Try changing the EQTYPE for Player_AAXPPercentage to 27.
DarkSidhe
08-03-2002, 01:32 PM
/agree Edan. Had the same problem with the skin I'm using. Edit the EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml and search for <Label item ="Player_AAXPPercentage"> then scroll down to the EQType and change it to 27.
