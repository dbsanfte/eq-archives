You can go to your EQ directory. Look for the file UI_<charname>_<servernumber>.ini . Open it up and look for [buffwindow] or [effectswindow] (not sure which one Verant called it as they change between the 2). You'll see a line which has your resolution 800x600 and an X and Y number. Change the Y number to be >0 and you should now see your buff window when you reload EQ.
