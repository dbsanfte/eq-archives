Heh. Space constraints. ^.^ However, at the bottom of the hotbox clicking on either side will turn the hotkeys. The buttons are fairly skinny but I never had trouble using them when I did. I tend to use shift 1-0 myself, since I'm more keyboard than mouse oriented (as an enchanter I found it easier to free up my mouse for targeting mezmobs)

If you look underneath the last two hotkeys, you'll see different shading (dark, light, dark). The two dark areas are the change buttons. Like I said, skinny, but fairly easy to hit unless you're in a hurry (which I suggest using the keyboard)
