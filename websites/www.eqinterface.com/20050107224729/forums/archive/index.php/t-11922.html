Actually I've been wanting something like that also. There is a simple solution really, If you dont mind It taking up a hotbutton slot. Just make a hot button and in the one of the lines type in /autoinventory. Now if you have something on the cursor just click the button and poof it goes into your inventory like you had dumped it on the auto equip area. You can also press the coresponding # to that hotbox to do it from the keyboard too.
