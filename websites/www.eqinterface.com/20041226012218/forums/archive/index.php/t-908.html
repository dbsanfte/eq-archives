There are spell gem entries because that is how the hotkeys are coded. Hotbutton, inv slot, and spell gem items are defined, and the client decides which to put where based on what you have chosen to put into your hotkeys.

Secondary to leg should be as simple as changing the EQType. Look up what the right one is in the thread here in the info vault.

As for the original poster asking about coins, no that isn't possible. At this point, coins is one of the things like auto equip or destroy button that is referenced only by ScreenID, to get it to work the client is hard coded to look in that (inventory) window for that ScreenID. Can't move it across windows. Hopefully VI is moving all these types of things to EQTypes before they take the UI out of beta.
