New Modifications as of 5/31
-Changed the Spellbook, and Inventory Graphics..this now looks alot better then what it did before
-Created new Gauges that are diamond shaped, and others that look like bars
-Added graphic buttons to the MP3 player
-Fixed the spell bar graphics
-Fixed the Hotbutton and Pet window graphics..shouldn't look blurry on the top like it did before
-Changed buttons and gauges on group window
-Added Graphics to the bank window..to make it look like a silver coin. bank window is updated to Test server and will work
with Live servers.
-Added graphic buttons to the inventory window.


Modifications:
Graphics:

-All Paw graphics are made by me..The paw spell book place holders where made by Kuvasie

-Beastlord Animated Epic Curser is made by Oguler

XML Modifications:

-Made the clickies with the Pet window...with all inventory..added Player stats to the pet window also, also the
percentage of your pets health.
-Hotbuttons window, has player resists on it
-group window..has player inventory slots..
-Selector window, has been made really small, to fit right next to the group window.
-Music player is made longer and slimmer to fit at the top of the screen..a graphic attachment is added
for the compass window.
-Targetwindow added percentages..and added some paw graphics.
-Map window..Using my LIl'Map Mod..removed the title bar..and made it slightly smaller then the original Lil'map
-Alarm window is Lil'Alarm
-Buff window, and short duration buff window..made the icons smaller..and added spell names..these are NOT moveable

-Spell cast bar added even more Paws! I have included an alternate folder..with a spell bar, with the spell names
to the right of the gems.
-Compass is from SylversCompass2.zip, I just changed the colors to better match this UI
