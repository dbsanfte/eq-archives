Kaotiqa's Minventory (Ultra-compact Inventory)

I wanted a small inventory window, but I didn't want to fumble with tabs and stuff (nor discard the stats and resists, like some have).

http://www.eternaldusk.com/images/screenshots/minventory.png

I did away with the Exp and AA gauges, since anyone using mods has them on their player window now, where they can be seen all the time.

The class animation above the money slots is the auto-equip area.

If you want it for your UI, just save http://www.eternaldusk.com/programming/EQUI_Inventory.xml (http://www.eternaldusk.com/programming/EQUI_Inventory.xml) to your UI's subdirectory.
