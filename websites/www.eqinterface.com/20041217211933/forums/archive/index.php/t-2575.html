The gauge that relates to EQ_type 1 (the player HP gauge) automatically has the player name attached to it.
Remove it by setting the text_offset in that element to 100 ( or some other ungodly high value) to move the text (in that case the player name) far off the window and thus out off the visible area of the player info window...
