I personally use a viewport and stash most windows off to the right of the screen. I do this because I want my view of the game-world to be un-cluttered by information windows. Because of this, I like windows to be as compact as possible. But, on the other hand, I like my windows to be as informative as possible, so often "compact" has to make way for space for labels or sizes for images that make sense at 1600 x 1200 resolutions.

Making the names a tab makes the window pretty big in width. If the space is not clickable from both mouse-buttons, that means I can't position anything to the right of that window.

I like to postion things as closely as possible to reduce the distace I need to move the mouse. I have a number of reasons for this; improved reaction time to in-game events, reduced strain on my hands, visual appeal of the interface. So, I don't like the tab for the spell casting widow. I do want the spell names. Icons for too many spells are too simialr in many cases without them.

So, if you're commited to the tabbed spell casting window version, please consider also make a mod without the tabs and the spell names available.
