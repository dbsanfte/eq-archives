News - 11/17/02

Complete Sets (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?catid=22)
Grimmier has created G1 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=570), a "wrap style UI with a bunch of goodies."
Spiritwalker has added a Night Elf UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=568), rounding out his WC3 series!
Kudane has gone through and colorized Spiritwalker's, creating the Evil Night Elf UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=572)!
Castilla has created Tranquility (http://eq.stratics.com/eqskins/mod.php?mod=downloads&op=viewdownloaddetails&lid=206) a Quartz 1.5-based UI, including new colors and various window mods.
Miscellaneous (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=29)
Spiritwalker has also released his Wolf Gauge (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=571) separately for use with his WC3 UI's, or any other.
Achebrosh has created a new animated Enchanter Class Symbol (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=579) for use in Inventory Windows, or elsewhere.
Mix & Match (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=29)
Narwael has also created a WC3 Night Elf UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=580), using Spiritwalker's graphics, Raxthorne's Gems and Icons, and various T.King/Remelio mods.
Prisma has uploaded Prismatic UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=575), based on Vert-Bashed 2.2, and including the Location Compass. Two version are included, one for melee, the other for casters.
Earthscum has released another series of UI's entitled "Dungeon Crawler"; it's available in Green (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=576), Blue (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=577), or Red (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=578). He says, "This is a mod that is designed so that you can have your Alphas set to high, or whatever you like, and still be able to see your stuff at a glance."
Player Info (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=20)
Haijak has updated his Master Control Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=573) mod, which includes minor fixes and some new graphics.
Aviendha has updated the Avi Player Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=574), with all Stats, and new bar/gauge graphics.
Spell Books (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=25)
Achebrosh has made available a LARGE number of new Spellbook backgrounds. The book XML can be found here, with one new background (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=583); additional background images, called Manaborder 1-7, can be downloaded from the Spell Books (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=25) category.
