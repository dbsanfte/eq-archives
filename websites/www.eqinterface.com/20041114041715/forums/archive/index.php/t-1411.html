Here is my first mouse cursor attempt. The Shaman Epic.

Hope my fellow shaman like it

http://pm-dorr.net/images/shm_mouse_ss.jpg

add the following to the textures section in EQUI_Animations.xml
<!-- mouse -->
<TextureInfo item = "mouse.tga">
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
</TextureInfo>
<!-- mouse -->

change the A_DefaultCursor Ui2DAnimation item to look like the following
<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_DefaultCursor">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>mouse.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>4</X>
<Y>3</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
