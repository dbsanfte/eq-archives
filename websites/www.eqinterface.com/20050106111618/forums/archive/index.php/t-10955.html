Check and make sure that none of them created a subfolder within your custom folder. The error log you have posted shows that it isn't finding any custom files whatsoever, which is what leads me to believe you may have the subfolder problem. Another possiblity is that your custom folder name has a space in it. It can't. Make sure that it is all one word, no spaces.

And, you had it right the first time. You do not want to copy your default folder, that just leads to major headaches down the road. The only files that should be in your custom folder are files that have actually been customized.
