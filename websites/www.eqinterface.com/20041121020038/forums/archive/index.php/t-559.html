Yeah, spells per page is hard coded. The game knows to fill in SBW_Spell0 - 15 with the spells depending on the page. You could hide some of the spells I guess and only use 0 - 3 and 8 - 11 on each page, but then you won't have many spell slots in your book since you'll be throwing away half of them.
