It's true buttons are hardcoded.. But that doesn't make what you want to do impossible. Both the buttons you want to modify are hardcoded..

So, To do what you want. Move the Get Lost Button where the taunt button is.. Then, instead of deleting the Taunt Button: Move it out of the window. ;)

Example:

<Location>
<X>-500</X>
<Y>-500</Y>
</Location>
