News - 10/30/02

The T.King and Remelio Downloads (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=27) area has been updated (by Thomus) to include all their latest releases; if you like their work, be sure to check it out!

Thomus King (http://pub10.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10) has a number of releases also available on his site: a non animated Arcane Spellbook v2, the round "Celtic" Spellbar, and the Mage class icon/animation; in addition, a preview of his "Briar" Spellbar has been posted!

The "Celtic" Spellbar can also be found at Remelio's (http://pub165.ezboard.com/ftranscendence39908frm31) site, along with an explanation of which gem is which.

Aventae has released a Compass (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=481) that shows the in-between directions (NE, SE, EW, NW) as well as the standard four.

Bryanelthor has done up a Blue Quartz Compass (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=487), to match the Blue Quartz skin.

Aeluin has updated Blue Fantasy UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=484) to version 1.1, incorporating some if the newer "bells and whistles."

Cormanth has uploaded Canopy 2.0 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=485), a new version of his screen-wrapped UI.

Madea has released FatM (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=473), a UI with some mix-and-match mods and some original graphics.

Kaori has released KaoUI Version 4.0 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=482), a "true mix of Vertebrae, T.King and Remelio, Drakah (and) is up-to-date with all patches." Also available in multiple colors!

Xymarra has available XymUI 2.11 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=489), with all original artwork!

Fyste has created a Horizontal Group Bar (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=451) for SarsUI 3.01; it's designed priomarily to allow the UI to run at 1024x768.

Jovec has modified a Stat Hotbox Replacement (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=488) for DrakahMix 3.0, that includes stats, XP bars, hotbuttons, and inventory slots in a horizontal window.

Forry has uploaded modified versions of the Inventory and Pet Windows (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=450) for Vert 2.2, slightly slimmed and rearranged.

Kaenil has created a Dark Elf Inventory (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=492), featuring images of those blue ladies!

Chnottie has released ActionStats (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=476), a mod adding a new tab to the Actions Window with player stats.

Armengar has a Shaman Spellbook Skin (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=452) available, with an Animal Spirits look!

Sinite (Tru_Soldier99) has recreated the Old Spell Icons and Gems (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=490) for use with the new UI; he reports it's somewhat incomplete, but tests well for druids at least.

There've been a large number of Mix-n-Match UI's uploaded lately: Twylight's Vert-Twylight Mix 2.1 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=448), Kaldia's ReMixUI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=478), Bash's Vert 2.2 Bashed (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=480) for Warriors, and the DurnitolUI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=491).
