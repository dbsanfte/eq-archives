Look for EQUI_ConfirmationDialog.xml in your custom mod and delete it.

If you dont have a customized EQUI_ConfirmationDialog.xml, then you will need to camp out to desktop and do the following:

Go to your EverQuest directory.
Open your UI_(yourcharname)_(Server##).ini

Find:
[ConfirmationDialogBox]

Delete that with whatever is underneath it until you stop at the next set of [ ] for something else. Don't want you to delete something else non-related :)

When you get into game, EQ will re-write that code, so don't worry.

~or~

Replace what you have with mine:

[ConfirmationDialogBox]
BGTint.red=255
BGTint.green=255
BGTint.blue=255
Fades=true
Delay=2000
Duration=500
Alpha=255
FadeToAlpha=255
Locked=false
XPos1280x1024=550
YPos1280x1024=350
BGType=1
XPos1024x768=550
YPos1024x768=350


I have never had a problem with not seeing the Yes/No buttons.. yet.

Hope this helps.
