  Child's Play
Posted By: Cairenn - 11-23-2005 07:22 PM - Replies (1)

For the past three years, gamers and geeks around the world have raised nearly a million dollars in toys, games and cash for sick kids in Children's Hospitals across the globe through a grassroots charity called Child's Play.

Created by Penny Arcade, no "Administratvie Fees" or other nonsense is collected. All gifts and donations go directly to the hospitals for distribution to sick kids.

This year Child's Play is expanding worldwide to help Children's Hospitals across the globe. Clicking on one of the links will take you to the Child's Play home page, where you can get a list of Children's Hospitals that need your help. Selecting one will direct you to that particular hospital's Amazon wish list. Any item you purchase from that list will be delivered directly to the hospital you select. Also be sure to choose the shipping address to the hospital instead of your own.

If you prefer to make a cash contribution, you can donate via the PayPal banner on the Child's Play site.

  Keith Parkinson 1958 - 2005
Posted By: Cairenn - 11-01-2005 09:16 PM - Replies (1)
As I am sure most of you know by now, renowned fantasy artist Keith Parkinson passed away late last week.

I know many of you no longer remember me, but I had to post something. The problem is, I have been sitting here all day, trying to figure out what to post about this. I ... just can't come up with the words. I was at BlizzCon when I got the news he had passed on. The world tilted and turned black, and I found myself in a heap on the sidewalk with tears streaming down my face. The fantasy world has lost one of the truly greats, and even worse, the world has lost one of her best.

You can read the post by Brad McQuaid of SGO, where Keith was working on the upcoming Vanguard:Saga of Heros mmorpg, here.

Donna, Nick, our hearts and prayers go out to you.

Rest safely Keith.
