Open up your UI_animation.xml file in for delta.

Do a find on "dragitem"

Scroll thru and you will notice the last one is 26.

Copy the 26 one from the start of it to the end of it (if you look at a few at the same time its pretty easy to figure out the start/end of them)

After the dragitem26 xml data hit enter and paste what you copied. Change the 26 to 27 and then save it. You should have to do this in 2 different places on Delta 3.0. Was haveing the same exact problem and figured it out last night. Hope this makes sence.
