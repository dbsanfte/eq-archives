Take a close look at the file names. Your answers are all there, except maybe the spell gems. VI insisted to use CastSpellWnd.xml. Why not SpellWindow or SpellGems is beyond me.

Player: PlayerWindow.xml
Group: GroupWindow.xml
Pet: PetWindow.xml
SpellGems: CastSpellWnd.xml

What to remove, move around, etc varies on what you want to do.

I would suggest this: Take everything from PlayerWindow and CastSpellWnd and put it into GroupWindow.xml

Reason: When you move the Pet_HP(call it) from someplace outside it's original window, it will not vanish.
It's better to have your perm pet HP bar up than 6 group member perm HP bars.

Also, remember there are restrictions that must be followed:

&middot; Can't move buttons (sorry mate, unable to move the PetWindow buttons outside of that window)
&middot; Can't remove Pet_HP (doesn't mean you can't make a new one)

And, keep an eye on your UIError.txt file located in your EverQuest directory, it's a big big big help when trying to debug errors.
