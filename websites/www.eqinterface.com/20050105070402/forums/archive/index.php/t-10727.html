If I am understanding you I came up with this idea way back for the Spellbook fonts - Here is the post I did. See if you can use this in the window you need.

==============================================
Changing Color of Spellbook Text
Just a FYI in case you didnt know may help for you to use different color backgrounds in your Nice Spellbooks

Take the following and add to EVERY spellname just change around the colors as you may.


<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>240</B>
</TextColor>



--=Most SpellName sections look like this:=-


<StaticText item ="SBW_SpellName0">
<ScreenID>SBW_SpellName0</ScreenID>
<Font>2</Font>
<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>240</B>
</TextColor>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>100</X>
<Y>50</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>100</CX>
<CY>16</CY>
</Size>
<Text>Complete Healing</Text>
<NoWrap>false</NoWrap>
<AlignCenter>true</AlignCenter>
<AlignRight>false</AlignRight>
</StaticText>



If you need some RGB color codes here is nice chart:
RGB Color Codes (http://html-color-codes.com/rgb.html)
