The pet window is now resizable, but only in the horizontal direction. It will take its vertical size from your current UI layout, even though it is not resizable vertically.

To fix the horizontal dimension of the window, make sure it is unlocked, grab it by the side border, and drag it to the correct shape.

To fix the vertical dimension, either do a "/load ui" without a 1 to force everything to default postions or, while you are not logged in, edit your UI_character_server.ini file and remove the size settings for the pet info window.
