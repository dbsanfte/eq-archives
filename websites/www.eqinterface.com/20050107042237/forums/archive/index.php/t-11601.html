According to that error log, EQ isn't finding the files. Check and make sure that:

a) the files aren't in a sub-folder within your custom folder;
b) your custom folder is a one word name, ie "Bard" not "Bard UI". EQ doesn't like spaces in the name of the folder; or
c) you don't have 2 EQ folders.
