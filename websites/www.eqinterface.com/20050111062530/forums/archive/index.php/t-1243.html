UniDyne
08-19-2002, 05:26 PM
1) Right click on main chat window
2) Select the option to create a new chat window
3) Right click on the new chat window and set it's filters to guild chat
4) Right click and rename the new window to Guild Chat.
5) Give yourself a pat on the back - this change should be permanent. (until you close the window or change the filters)

Simple, eh? ;)
