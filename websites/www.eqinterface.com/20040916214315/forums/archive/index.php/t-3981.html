A personal preference note here:

I notice on almost every mod out there, that people put the % tags at one end or the other of the corresponding gauge. Why not put the % ON the gauge? Shortens it up, which helps deal with the size issue for so many resolutions, yet remains highly visable and readable ...

The first thing I inevitably do with any mod is move the %s to the center of the gauge.

Other than that personal preference quirk, I like this, it's nice and clean.
