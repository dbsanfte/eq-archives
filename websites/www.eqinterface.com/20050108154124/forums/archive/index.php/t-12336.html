A few people had trouble with some of my zipped files as well when I have accidentially compressed them with 64-bit compression.

I would reccomend using a decompressor that can read 64-bit compression. I know that WinZip 8.1 (http://www.winzip.com/)+ and WinRAR 3.20 (http://www.rarlab.com/)+ will.
PKZip (http://www.pkware.com/) says that it will on their website, but I do not use it.
The built-in windows utility will not, nor will WinACE (http://www.winace.com/). I do not know about ZipCentral (http://zipcentral.iscool.net/).
