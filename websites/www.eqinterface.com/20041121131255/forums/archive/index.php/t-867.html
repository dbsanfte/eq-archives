The Background images are tied to the window's border and defined in the EQUI_Textures.xml file.

For the ChatWindow, you'll want WDT_Def2.

Locate that in the EQUI_Textures.xml file, copy it, rename it, and change the background to what you're wanting.

Make sure to add this to the top of the Animations file as well.

Once changed, just modify the EQUI_ChatWindow.xml where it says <DrawTemplate> to the new on you just created.
