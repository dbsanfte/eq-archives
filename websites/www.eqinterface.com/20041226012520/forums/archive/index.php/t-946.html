The ScreenID Pet_HP can not be missing from the EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml or the <Pieces></Pieaces> tags at the bottom of the file.

You can however copy that gauge, use the same ScreenID, but a different Item name and place that in your "new" PlayerWindow.

One bug in that is that the new Pet_HP won't auto vanish like it does in the original PlayerWindow. :(

Oh, and the original Pet_HP; just changed it to Size 0,0.

The attack indicator graphic is defined int he palyer window, but you cannot change where it displays.
It always starts drawing at 0,0 and the size is determined by the Texture and not the Animation.
If you want your attack indicator in your target window, what you really have to do is code yout target information (gauge, label, etc) into the player window, and zero-out the player information to no longer show.
