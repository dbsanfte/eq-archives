Yes Ive heard you people who have to play in 800x600 (/comfort). Its a fact that they arent so many customs UI available for you, and its not really fair.

Because of the size of the screen I couldnt come up with something too fancy (ya know with lots of piccies and all like I use to hehe), but nevertheless, its extremely functional, and (at least imo) pretty cool looking too.

* Spellbook has been rewamped to actually FIT in a 800x600 reso
* Inventory has been rewamped, reshrinked, inventory slots removed since they are on the hotkey window.
* AA expe window has been rewamped in order to fit in 800x600.
* Hotkeys have both inventory and right clickies tabs, to maximize space use efficiency.
* Buffbar icons have been shrinked not to eat half of the screen
* Made an horizontal castgembar and changed the gemholder piccie to a bluish square
