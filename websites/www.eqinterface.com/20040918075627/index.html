  Patch Day! (Sept 14)
Posted By: Kudane - 09-14-2004 05:08 AM - Replies (4)
3:01am PST Sept 14, 2004

The servers are coming down for a 9 hour patch. Expect a post on the patch notes as soon as they are available. Also people will begin posting thier UI changes, so PLEASE be patient. As Dolby said, we don't need to see 100 post saying "Help OoW Broke my UI!!!!". If your using a UI that one of our Featured Artist made, expect them to have a patch with in 48 hours. If your using a mix of several UIs, just watch them for a patch. If your UI is still broke, with no sign of being patched by this weekend, then you can start posting for help.

EXCEPTION:
If you get a UI that is suppose to be working and it's not, please post that info asap. Please included your UI_Error.txt file with your post, and a link to the UI you downloaded.

See you in Omens of War in 9 hours!

  Omens of War Tomorrow (14th)
Posted By: Dolby - 09-13-2004 01:33 PM - Replies (0)
As many of you may know Omens of War will go live on the 14th (Tomorrow). SOE has removed the NDA and that has allowed us and those in beta to discuss the changes that are coming tomorrow.

If you’re an author and would like to get a head start on making the changes I have posted a rough draft. You can find this information in the patch forum. Please remember this is compairing the uifiles from the beta and things may change when they patch it to a live server. Once S.O.E. patches the live servers for OoW I’ll make the normal official posts with the changes as soon as possible. I just figured some would want a jump on things so I released the notes I took.

You can also find the new EQTypes in the Complete EQTypes for Everything thread towards the end of the thread.

Just a reminder to those of you that use custom interfaces that you need to be patent and allow a couple weeks for the author of your custom interface to fix it. I don’t want to see a million posts saying “OoW broke my interface HELP!!111!!??111” because its going to break many of them. Just be patient please.



Good luck to every one tomorrow!
