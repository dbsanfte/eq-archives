Here are the solutions I found even though I deleted files and re-downloaded them as suggested several times in other posts I finally found that I had to do some extra editing to my Animations file that I personally could not find in the Patch thread.

For the Compass errors: [Line:5 Source:UIFiles\kailora\EQUI_CompassWnd.xml] Couldn't find class:item 'A_CompassOverlay' reference in FieldParseItemOfClass()

[Line:19 Source:UIFiles\kailora\EQUI_CompassWnd.xml] Couldn't find class:item 'A_CompassStrip' reference in FieldParseItemOfClass()

[Line:33 Source:UIFiles\kailora\EQUI_CompassWnd.xml] Couldn't find class:item 'A_CompassStrip' reference in FieldParseItemOfClass()

I opened both my default and custom EQUI_Animations.xml and found that at the End of the XML file After:
<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_DividerVertical">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>window_pieces03.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>96</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>100</CX>
<CY>24</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
I needed to add:
<!-- Compass art -->

<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_CompassStrip">
<Frames>
<Texture>window_pieces04.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>180</CX>
<CY>24</CY>
</Size>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>

<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_CompassOverlay">
<Frames>
<Texture>window_pieces04.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>24</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>98</CX>
<CY>24</CY>
</Size>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
