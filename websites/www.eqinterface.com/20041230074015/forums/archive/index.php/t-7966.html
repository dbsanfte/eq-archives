I know I’m 30min. early but I just couldn’t wait any longer.

A new layout has been created for eqinterface and eqgui. I’ve spent many months creating a couple designs and finally made one I think works well. I felt we needed something a little more professional looking but fast loading. The new layout isn’t a drastic change so I don’t think any one will have trouble finding anything. The eqinterface and eqgui team hopes you enjoy our new site design. We also want to take this time to thank all our members for your continued support.
