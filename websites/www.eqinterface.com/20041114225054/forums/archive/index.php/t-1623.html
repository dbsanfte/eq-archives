This is my effort to make a UI for small screens. It was designed to reduce lag on raids, but that has more to do with luclin models and clipping planes than anything else. But it does use a very small amount of video memory, good for older video cards.

It's not the prettiest, but remember we're talking 640 by 480. Lots of room for text and battle spam along the bottom. Just enough room for spell and spawn timers and other critical information. No room for conveniences like extra inventory slots and pet control buttons and pictures of flowers -- make macros in the hotbuttons window if you need those.

The viewport is 448 by 336. This is what I'd show as a caster... a melee type would alt-s to get rid of the spells list and alt-t to open in the same space a list of stats, saves, atk, and endurance. She might leave the timer up to time disciplines, or could close it too. I figure that's all you need for a raid, or maybe more than you need.

Anyone else interested in running 640x480? Or have some suggestions for changes?
