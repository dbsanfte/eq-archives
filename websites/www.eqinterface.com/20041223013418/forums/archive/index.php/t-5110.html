<a name="updates">Latest Updates</a>

Complete Sets (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=22)
Maginus has released a Scroll Skin, in three versions: one for Pet classes (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=776), one for Hybrids and Non-pet classes (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=777), and one for Pure Melee classes (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=778), all with the same scroll-look background but varying uses of the Pet window.
Zelf has updated Zelf's UI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=781) to v1.2, designed for 1024x768 resolution.
Talyns has released OldSchool 1 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=787) a UI "designed to sorta resemble the Pre-Fullscreen Interface," designed for 1024x768 resolution with a 780x585 viewport.
Hotkeys (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=18)
Nugaruk has modified the Drakah UI Hotkey Bar (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=792), removing the last two inventory slots and adding the Range and Ammo slots.
Inventory (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=16)
Zanral has two Inventory mods available, each with a custom image in the auto-equip area: Nox Inventory (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=779) and Zan's Inventory (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=780).
Sc2kmayor has released Sona TT Inventory (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=784), a modification of his mod of Thangor's Inventory, with instructions on including your own character image as the center background.
Mix & Match (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=29)
Sc2kmayor has also put together a complete UI, Sona Mods (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=774), which includes a modified buff window and viewport settings as well.
Tyndylla has put together LocBard (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=782), based on Kao4.0 with others mods included.
Larul has put together Wolfgrey2.3 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=167), based on the VertUI, for Shaman/Beastlords.
Neiloch has updated his ViewportUI (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=712) to v1.2, with a few customer fixes.
Kasca has put together SKDragon (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=793), a mix of some of the most popular mods.
Pet Window (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=33)
Waeolwen has created Player Stats (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=786), which removes the standard Pet info and displays Player Info instead.
Player Info (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=20)
Herris has released UPW 2.0 (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=789), which combines all player info into one window.
Spell Books (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=25)
Sc2kmayor has updated SonaSpellbook (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=785) to v2.0, a much smaller version.
Spell Gems (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/index.php?op=showfiles&catid=19)
Valladarya has created the Black Flying Dragon Spell Bar (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=788); an update with new gems is soon to follow.
