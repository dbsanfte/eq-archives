Default window starting loc from hell:

Good news, I did manage to figure how those locs works. Put the resolution to 640x480 and adjust your windows in it (default x,y in screen definition). For example, if you set a window to the upper right corner in 640x480, it will stay there at any resolution after (but you have to do a /load UIname at the new resolution). Default x,y loc are very usefull for when you do a /load UIname and dont want to have a messy screen or out of screen windows.
