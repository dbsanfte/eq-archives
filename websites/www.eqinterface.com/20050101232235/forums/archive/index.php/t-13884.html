To close a window you no longer need - right-click for the dropdown window and choose "Close" :nana:

To move these windows that don't give you the cross-hair mouse pointer - (the ones with no title area) - resize the window by dragging the left top corner side up and over to the position you want it. Sometimes this may mean that the chat window takes up your whole screen, if for example you want to move a window from the bottom right corner of the screen tot the top left. Then grab the right bottom corner and pull it up until you have the window the size, shape and location.

There are mods out there to DL if this is too much of a pain. I use a variation of the Delta 4 - a combo from Delta 2.3 by Rellix and Delta 3 Spaghetti Remix by neorain for the chat windows and containers.
