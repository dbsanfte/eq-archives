Originally posted by Dolby
Did you enable the 3d target ring option in EQ's in game options then totaly restart EQ?

You don't need to restart EQ, just use the following commands in game to reload it without having to exit and re-enter the game:

/indicator off
/indicator on

Do this AFTER you go into your options in game and make sure you have the ring turned on, then do the commands you will see the ring disappear then re-appear, if your ring is functioning correctly, if it isn't check your uierrors log inside of your Everquest folder. Hope this helps some.
