Well, I finally got it all figured out, and now it's working. I made an extremely common mistake and I'm almost too ashamed to admit it. Note the filename I posted above. It's supposed to be 2048x128, not 1024x128. I renamed the file and everything worked perfectly.

Just thought you might find that somewhat informational.
