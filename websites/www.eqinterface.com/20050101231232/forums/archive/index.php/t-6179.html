Here is a stand alone casting bar.

http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=86

Your UI might have the casting bar hidden beyond the edge of the screen, so you still might not be able to see this. If not, you might go into your UI_charactername_##.ini file and look for this :

[CastingWindow]

When you find it, change all the numbers that look like this :

XPos1024x768=482
YPos1024x768=50

to this :

XPos1024x768=0
YPos1024x768=0


This will put your Casting window in the upper left hand side where you can at least see it to move it around.
