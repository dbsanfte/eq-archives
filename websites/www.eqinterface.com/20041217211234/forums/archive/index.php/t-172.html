If you do it yourself, what you do is add two <Label> declarations in the player window (file: EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml), one for your XP percentage and one for your AAXP percentage. Make sure you set the <Location> correctly, the coordinates are relative to the window itself, and not to the screen. Then you add the two new <Label> pieces as <Piece> components to the <Screen> item "PlayerWindow".
