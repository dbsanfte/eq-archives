The Player Health gauge includes automatically the Player name text; it's hardcoded as part of the Player health EQType. What you have to do is in the gauge area of the file, set the text offset to be outside the visible area of the window (like, if the window is 256 wide, set the text offset to X=300).

Hope that makes sense!
