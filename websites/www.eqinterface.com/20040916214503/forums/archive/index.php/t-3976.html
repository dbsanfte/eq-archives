2.2 Update - Fixed a nasty missing texture bug, you should update if you have 2.1 and are wondering where you 100 megs of HD went..

Latest release of my personal UI, posted here for the first time due to popular request. Its basically an assimilation and tweak of various things since the new UI first started, so I've lost track of what came from where. The goal is functionality and less clutter, so you won't find quite as much information scattered all over the screen as you would with most custom UI's.

General features: Graphics from t.king and vert, borderless design, /viewport layout, 10 hour digital timer, and eqtoolbox maps.

The bottom strip of the interface is obscured on my screen due to frequent windowed mode use, so the window positions may not be optimal for everyone.
