There's a big thread on this, but I couldn't find it successfully in here. If I find it, I'll post ya a link.

To summarize it though, there's some sort of math going on that scales the location of screens depending on your resolution. We think it's based on 640x480 and then anchoring to one side or another depending on window size in higher resolutions. The thread goes into a lot more details.

But it's a "feature". There's no way to describe positions based on resolutions in the XML files. It's only in the UI*.ini files. And there's no way that we've found to have a default UI*.ini for your mod directory. So you just kinda have to make due with the weirded coords that you have.
