I discovered this purely by accident and I have no idea if it's been posted before because I'm not really sure what sort of search terms to use.

Anyway, I was running throught Nekktulos Forest at night and I was having trouble reading some of my font color choices. Instead of changing the colors I though I would try turning off the background image and setting a brighter tint color in the chat window. I had the alpha set to 50% and when I used an off-white tint color my view through the chat window was like I had Ultravision cast on me.

I decided to take this one step further and created an empty chat window with NO channels assigned to it. I right clicked on the frame to bring up the menu for that window, went to Window > Background > Image (as I recall) to turn off the texture overlay, went to Window > Background > Tint Color to bring up the color picker window. I chose a bright color close to white, resized the window to fill the center of my screen and there it was ...

:nana: Night Vision Window :nana:

I now have a thin chat window across the top of my screen and whenever I need to see in the dark I grab the bottom of it and pull it down. If anyone is interested or just curious I'll post a screenshot with more specific instructions later.
