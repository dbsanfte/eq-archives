<a name="news">Latest News</a>
The News Ranger Returns! No, I didn't die or drop of the face of the earth - though at times I wasn't sure! Anyway, here's the latest News and UI Updates!

Default UI for Download: The Default UI Files (http://www.synex-flux.com/Other/defaultuifiles/index.html) have been updated courtesy of mysticscrolls.com (http://www.mysticscrolls.com/EverQuest/index.asp); both a zip package and the individual files are available.

New T.King address: Per Thomus King (http://pub165.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10.showMessage?topicID=74.topic), all his home- and art-pages will be changing from attbi.com to comcast.net on June 30; don't forget to update your bookmarks!

SOE All Access: Sony is now offering an All Access pass (http://www.station.sony.com/allaccess/), allowing subscribers to play EQ, EQ2, EQOA, Planetside, and Station Pass games (but *not* SWG!) for one reduced price. In addition, SoE has announced incentives for EQ players (http://www.sonyonline.com/corp/press_releases/EQ2_incentives_051303.html) to join EQ2, including "exclusive crossover quest zones in each game that will be accessible only by subscribers of both games, allowing characters in EverQuest and EverQuest II to gain access to hidden treasures, an EverQuest II in-game reward for loyal fans, and additional features to be disclosed over the coming months for ongoing EverQuest and EverQuest II players."

Patch: June 11, 2003: Yep, tomorrow (or today, depending on when you read this); read the planned changes at EQ Live (http://eqlive.station.sony.com/updates/upcoming_updates.jsp) (especially about the new shared bank slots!); UI files to be updated include EQUI_Optionswnd.xml, EQUIhelpwnd.xml, Bankwnd.xml, BigBankwnd.xml, and Help.html
