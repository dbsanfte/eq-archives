It would seem to me that the colors for the Targeting Ring are stored and modifiable in this file...

TargetIndicator.ini

Here's just an example of the last 2 consider levels... yellow and red.. or as listed in the .ini, "Difficult" and "Deadly".. the RBG values are for yellow and red. The TargetIndicator.tga file is greyscale so I'm surmising that the TargetIndicator.ini file controls the colors of the swirly target thing on your screen. I haven't tried it yet though. Can't connect to EQ atm =(

[Difficult]
Texture=TargetIndicator
TwistFactor=1.0
TwistSpeed=0
FrameCount=0
Duration=0
TextureScale=.1
TextureSpeed=0.001
ScaleMin=0.5
ScaleMax=1.5
ScaleSpeed=1.0
Alpha=240
Red=255
Green=255
Blue=0

[Deadly]
Texture=TargetIndicator
TwistFactor=1.0
TwistSpeed=0.0
FrameCount=0
Duration=0
TextureScale=.1
TextureSpeed=0.001
ScaleMin=0.5
ScaleMax=2.5
ScaleSpeed=1.0
Alpha=255
Red=255
Green=0
Blue=0

This would only be for the new target indicator.. not the con message it gives you when you /con or double click a character. That message color isn't modifiable, no.
