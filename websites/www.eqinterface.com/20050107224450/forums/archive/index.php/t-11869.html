To expand a little further on what Sars said.

The order that each part is listed in the <Pieces/> section is the order that they will be displayed from back to front.

For instance, you have the following code:
<Pieces>A_Something_01</Pieces>
<Pieces>A_Something_02</Pieces>

What this tells you is that A_Something_01 will draw first, followed by A_Something_02 . If A_Something_02 and A_Something_01 overlap, then A_Something_02 will display on top of A_Something_01 .

Hope this helps.
