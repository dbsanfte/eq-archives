I think I found a workaround....
In
<Gauge item="Target_HP">

...change <TextOffsetY>14</TextOffsetY> to something that is way outside of the window.

Then you can add:

<Label item="TW_Targetnamelabel">
EQ type 28 and place it where you want.

Don't forget to add to <pieces>

It works.
