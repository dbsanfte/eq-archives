<img src="http://www.eqinterface.com/images/sections/eqgod.gif" align="right">On the eve of the most anticipated patch since the addition of the new customizable UI. SOE has announced Gates of Discord the next expansion in the Everquest line. It will be availble in Feb 2004.

Some Announced Features:
New Berserker class empowers you to ravage your enemies with two-handed axes or by hurling large objects

20 new zones, ten new instanced zones and ten new traditional zones including a huge ship city zone

20 all new NPC models make for hundreds of challenging encounters unlike anything seen before in EverQuest

Read the entire press release Here (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&postid=61321). I know this will be an exciting expansion for many who have wondered if the Berserker class was a real class.

Rumor still abound that they have not released all the "fun stuff" that will come with this expansion. We will let you know as we find out
