Well... um, I guess the best thing to do is fix your mouse or something so you can =P Unless you're on a mac, or something... then... I dunno.

But until then, you can do it a number of ways.

If you open your options screen, you can set an overall transparency, so you can set the majority of your windows and stuff there.

Then, for individual windows, look in your UI_Name_server.ini file. In there, scroll down until you get to your resolution's section, and find the window you want to modify, and change it there using a plain text editor or something.

But, make a backup, as usual, because if you don't know what you're doing and change a lot of stuff, it might be hard to fix it.

Hope you get the mouse fixed though, it'll make everything better =)
