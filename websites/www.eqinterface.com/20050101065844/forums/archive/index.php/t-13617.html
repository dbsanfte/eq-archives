Well, it works good for me . Basically, the thing needs to get unzipped into your storyline directory. It should put a 'storyfoofyspells.txt' file and a 'foofy' directory in there.

if youre unzipper made an extra directory level, you need to bump it up so those two files are in your storyline folder (along with the normal storyline stuffs).

Once thats set, go into the game and bring up the storyline window (alt+n, but dont quote me, its near the right of the selector bar), and chose the 'foofy's spell info' choice.

Hope that helps :)
