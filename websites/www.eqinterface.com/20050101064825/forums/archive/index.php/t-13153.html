To stop that from happening, many authors go ahead and include the controls that EQ wants in the window, they just place them off screen, so to speak. Place them way off the edge of your winodw so they can't be seen, say 1000 or more pixels, and EQ will find them there when checking the XML files. That stops the errors from being reported and still lets you have the window the way you want it.
