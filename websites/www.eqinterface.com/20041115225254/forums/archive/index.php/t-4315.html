Hide your children and lock the donkey in the oven, folks, this one's HOT! That's right, now you can have a tiny animated dancing girl on your Music Player Window of your very own! She'll dance and dance and not stop dancing until you exit the game, by which point her curvacious little pixelated undulating figure will be burned onto your corneas!

She comes in two packages, one an excellent music player mod by Akshue (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=4150&highlight=music), the other an equally cool music player mod by Yuniargo (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&postid=22211#post22211)(shown in screenshot), for which I kindly thank both authors for such excellent moddage. To install, simply download, unzip, and copy the files (EQUI_MusicPlayerWnd.xml and spritedance.tga) for the music window mod of your choice (screenshots provided in their respective folders to make choosing easy!) into your modded interface directory and ¡Viola! your tiny dancer is ready to show you her moves!

She cycles through a whopping 68 frames of animation, and is guaranteed to offer precisely 850% more viewing pleasure than *cough* a certain other, greatly inferior yellow-coloured dancer (who will remain nameless Die, bananaman!) before she gets mind-numblingly boring! Don't delay, download Casanunda's Music Window Dancer today! (http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=625) ! Or we breaka yo' face!

Note: For best results, playing techno or rock music is recommended; however, dancing girl will also dance to Mozart or Polka, assuming the price is right. Attempting to mate with Tiny Dancing Girl is a federal crime. You have been warned. :cool:
