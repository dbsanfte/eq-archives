There's a bug being seen where some components of the UI move. Mine, in specific, was the buff bar. It slid left every zoning (until it went away).

In your case, every time you zoned it slid, then recorded its new position into the ui_charname_server.ini, under your resolution. When you switch to another resolution, it loads new window positioning info. When you switch back to your old resolution, it uses the (now incorrect, and having the window off the edge of the screen) geometry/placement info.
