Ok, I solved the problem. For anyone else wanting to know, here's what I had to do in Paintshop.

1 - make sure you are not saving as a .bmp (I used .tga and it worked)

2 - make a gradient, turn it into a mask and save it as an alpha channel on your disk

3 - load your buff graphic, load the mask from disk and save the mask to the alpha channel of your graphic

4 - set the transparent property to 'true' on your buff button

And it works (see attached pic :))
