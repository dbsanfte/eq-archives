Buddy of mine in my guild is our target caller, and he said he's been having problems picking his cursor out of the raid crowd, due in part to the fact that he's color blind. I asked him what colors would work best and he said Blue and Yellow. I suggested a flashing cursor of those colors with a targeting area in the center and he said it sounded like a good idea. So I got to working on it. Here's first prototype, the colored area cycles through and the center is semi-transparent.

If there's interest i'm also thinking of making all the dragging cursors and such along the same theme in a grey scale format for others to use.
