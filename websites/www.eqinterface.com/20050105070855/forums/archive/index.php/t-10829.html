The uierrors.txt log you posted tells me it can not find your custom xml/tga files you say is located in the folder ..\uifiles\Faydark.

1.) Check to make sure you don’t have a folder inside a folder. You can see a nice example with pictures in our FAQ.

2.) Make sure you don’t have two Everquest folders. Some of the expansion packs using the default location when installing will result in you having two Everquest folders. Make sure you are using the correct one. You can right click on the Everquest icon and left click on properties and it should show you the correct location of your Everquest folder.
