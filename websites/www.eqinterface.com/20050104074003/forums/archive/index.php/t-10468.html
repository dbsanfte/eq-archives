Just go to the download section and look for the UI changes you want. Click view picture to see if that suits you, then click download UI. Once you've downloaded the UI, unzip it into the custom folder you made for your UI. Repeat for the other UI changes, and log into the game and enjoy! Have fun!

Of course make sure you click yes to overwrite any previous version of any UI you have (i.e, compass, mp3 player, etc).
