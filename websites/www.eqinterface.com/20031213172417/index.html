Melee Changes Live on Test

Kudane

05:30 PM
12-04-2003
Yes last night there were about 100 or so Warriors, Rogues and Monks in West Commons where the new vendors have been placed to help test out the new Melee Skills (a spawned mob called "no loot for you" was our test target). Here are some tips that will help if you want to try out the new melee.

• Remember this is the test server, things are not set in stone yet

• The books cost 2 or 3 copper each, kill some "yard trash" on your way there

• Give the book back to the guy you buy it from to learn the skill

• ALT-C opens the new window, then right click on one of the 8 hot-keys to memorize the ability you learned

• As of 12/3 only the "openings" part of the change has been added, effects are not all done yet.

• /Feedback is your friend, keep your feeback to the facts, and useful.. "this sucks" or "this rules" is nice, but not helpful. Try and be as specific as you can.

If you have the time or desire, please feel free to help give feedback, with out help we can shape the new melee abilities into a very nice workable addition. Feel free to discuss (politely) your findings in this post Here


You can see what UI changes went on to test in Cairenn's post Here


- Kudane

Melee Change Chat tonight

Kudane

03:38 AM
11-25-2003
Alan VanCouvering hosted a live chat this evening on EQLive.

Here are the highlights I got from taking notes, while trying to save out the entire chat session.

  • Stamina Bar is going away. This will become Endurance
  • "Zing" type spells such as Acumen and spells that effected Stamina will not work to improve Endurance recovery
  • At this time there will be no Endurance recovery spells in the game.
  • Changes will be on Test soon, and added to Live Server on Dec 16th Patch
  • Changes in disciplines will effect all classes, new Melee Skills will be provided via Tomes for Rogue, Warrior, and Monks
  • Tomes will be vendor sold under 60, and partchment style for 61 and up.


The whole chat session can be read and commented on Here

/bow

- Kudane
