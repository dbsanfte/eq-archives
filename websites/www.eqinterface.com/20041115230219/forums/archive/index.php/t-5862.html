I've been playing with Sars 3.0 for a long time, and after the last round of messes ( errr changes ) that came with the bard buff window, I decided to post my changes as a complete set.

Basically I loved the SarsUI, but it didn't fit well on a 1024x768 screen so I started playing with it to make it fit. The more I changed the more I found I COULD change... and the more I kept changing things until I think I've touched every piece of the original UI at least a little.

So, here's my mod:

http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/fileinfo.php?id=1006

Screenshot:

http://www.eqinterface.com/sdownload/preview.php?id=1006

This is the first full UI I've posted ( even though it's just a mod ), so I'd love feedback! I've already got ideas for a complete UI done from scratch that I'll start working on pretty soon :)

MANY many many thanks to Sars for the initial work on the UI, and lots of other modders here that I either borrowed pieces from or learned from!

Enjoy!
