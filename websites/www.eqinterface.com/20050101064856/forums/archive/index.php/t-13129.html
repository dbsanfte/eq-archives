Unfortunately %t does not work with tells. Since the target is just that, a target, it doesn't constitute it to that as a real person as much as an figured individual in the game, an NPC or what have you. Your best bet is to just simply target the individual and have your macro /ttell %t, can I get KEI from you,Plz? At least this way you have the individuals name in the tell...
