I've had this type of issue before, not looking at the thread that Cairenn is indicating try the following...

1) Locate Everquest Trilogy (or top EQ) folder.
2) Delete all the files with extension of TXC (DOS = DEL *.TXC)
3) Copy and then delete *.OPT (option files) (You will need to reselect Video Modes/Window Fades/Locks/Filters)
4) Delete TEXTURE.TXT.

Restart EQ
Open Options and Reselect all options.
Once you get into EQ
ALT-O to options and reselect Video Options in DISPLAY.

This solved a ton of Crash to Desktop issues and You are Below World messages.
