Do you also have the required texture info? (I realize that my example below is for the t.king arrow cursor, but the principle remains the same.)

You need:

<TextureInfo item="tk_arrow.tga">
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
</TextureInfo>

as well as:

<Ui2DAnimation item="A_DefaultCursor">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>tk_arrow.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>32</CX>
<CY>32</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>1</X>
<Y>1</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>
