Important Notes:

** All graphics have been recreated by hand to simulate the Warcraft UI. There are some portions that come from screenshots, etc. that are from the game that were submitted and were re-textured for clarity. You can visit the Warcraft website at www.blizzard.com/wow

EXP version of EQUI_HotButtonWnd.xml
AAEXP of EQUI_HotButtonWnd.xml
