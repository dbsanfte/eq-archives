yes, open the EQUI_CastingWindow.xml file with word pad.
find the line

<Screen item="CastingWindow">
<!--<ScreenID/>-->
<!--<Font/>-->
<RelativePosition>false</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>290</X>
<Y>50</Y>
</Location>


change the part that says

<Location>
<X>290</X>
<Y>50</Y>
</Location
to
<Location>
<X>-290</X>
<Y>-50</Y>
</Location>

that should laod the window off the screen.
