05-04-2004, 02:23 AM
OK, I'll spare the background as to why this happens. Here's what you do. In your Everquest directory, find the UI_charname_servername.ini file. Open up that file using Notepad. Use the Find command (Ctrl-F), and look for the following phrase: PopDialog

You should first find a section with a heading of [PopDialog0]. Delete this ENTIRE SECTION of the file. Then, hit F3 to find the next occurence of PopDialog. You may or may not find a section with a heading of [PopDialog1]. Regardless of how many sections you have with a similar heading, delte ALL sections that start with [PopDialogx]... with x being any number from 0 to 9.

Then go into Everquest, and test this out by trying to drop something from your inventory on the ground. Assuming you have confrimation box for dropping items enabled, you should get up a dialogue box asking you to click yes or no.

Note to Admins:
This problem seems to come up once a week, and the section of the FAQ that you typically provide a link to in response does NOT solve the problem. The above fix does.
