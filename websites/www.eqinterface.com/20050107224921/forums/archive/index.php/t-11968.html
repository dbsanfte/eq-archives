Well the actions you have described are reportable... Granted there technically isn't camping, but if you have been holding a camp for a fair extended amount of time, the GM usually rules in favor to yourself.

Make sure when you report you also petition immediately afterwards. I find this gets the response quicker as petitions are a booster to them. Every report/petition that I have done has been answered within 3 hours (depending the severity of the report - the three hours response was always to naming violations) I have gotten a response to a particular trainer within 10 minutes of sending in the report/petition combo. And the individual was immediately reprimanded.

The more thorough you make your report and petition the more helpful it is for the GM or Guide to resolve the issue. And when the GM responds, I always tell people "Pretend you are speaking to an officer. Be courteous and calm. You will get better results with kindness than with attitude."

I'm sure there will be those here that will say otherwise, but I have yet to have any inconsiderate GM respond to my reports. Good luck with your adventures and journey well!
