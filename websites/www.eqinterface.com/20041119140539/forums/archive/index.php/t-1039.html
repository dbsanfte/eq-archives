Vrant replaced equi.xml to add the compass.

The person who made the compact caster bar "excluded" the equi_castingwindow from equi.xml. You should be able to copy the new equi.xml to your uifiles/myskin from the uifiles/default and find the line and make it look as below... that should fix it.

<Include>EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml</Include>
<!-- <Include>EQUI_CastingWindow.xml</Include> -->
<Include>EQUI_TargetWindow.xml</Include>


If you look I bet there is an error in ui_errors.txt in your eq directory that says something like unable to parse or some stuff about equi_castingwindow....
GL
AB

For future reference when you exclude a file from being loaded in equi.xml and include it in another file every time vrant makes a change to equi.xml they of course break your mods. You might consider editing the equi_castingwindow.xml directly and removing the item that is in conflict with your spell icons file so you can change equi.xml to it's default state. Then if vrant changes this file which they will about 10 more times over the next 6 months... you wont have to make this change.

Developers are new at this so some of their changes are very nice but are done in poor places. We didn't know which files would be changing and how often. I am sure future mods will get more resistant to version problems since vrant will probably never change some of the windows like backpacks and hot buttons etc... I doubt they will be changing equi_castingwindow.xml. So changes to this window are way safer than changes to equi.xml. Ahh time to hit eq!
