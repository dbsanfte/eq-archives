Did you pick one each of the Pet and Group windows?

If you are a pet owner, you choose either Pet - Healer (which has the additional F1-F5 group member sphere gauges, as well as the pet control buttons) or the Pet - Plain (just the pet control buttons, no sphere gauges).

If you are NOT a pet owner, you choose either the Group - Healer (F1-F5 group member sphere gauges as well as invite/disband buttons) or the Group - Plain (just invite/disband, no sphere gauges)

Which ever of the two you did NOT choose from the example above, you choose the - BlankSide ... that creates the lower left corner.

ie if you choose Pet - Healer, you would get Group - BlankSide
if you choose Group - Plain, you would get Pet - BlankSide
