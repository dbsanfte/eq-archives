****This is all from the ReadMe of the UI you are using****








The following steps will require Paint Shop Pro, or Adobe photoshop, or similar graphic editing program...

For a more personalized look for your inventory, modify the following file: 'wnd_bg_inventory.tga' Paste whatever picture you want ON TOP OF this skin, and save the file. Just be sure to maintain the original size of the skin, and always save your changes under the file same name (wnd_bg_inventory.tga). Other than those two restrictions, let your imagination run wild.

If you want to add a mini-portrait of your character to your hotkeys window (I added my pic to the dead area of the hotkeys window - to the right of the hotkeys), modify the following file: 'wnd_bg_hotkeys.tga' Paste whatever picture you want ON TOP OF this skin, and save the file. Just be sure to maintain the original size of the skin, and always save your changes under the file same name (wnd_bg_hotkeys.tga). Other than those two restrictions, let your imagination run wild.
