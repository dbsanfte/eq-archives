I'm pretty darn sure there is a cap to how much you can alter the UI.

I used the exsisting pieces05.tga file, I also was using one of the two tga files that aren't really used by the game since it seems Verant put the graphics from pieces04 & 05 onto pieces 03. They must of done that for some reason and I might of figured it out.

I redid the square buttons in the Hot Button Window using a pieces05.tga.....when I loaded my mule that I used for testing the buttons worked great for the 3 custom folders I have. Then I went to play two chars at once, first one on the buttons worked fine.....2nd one there were no buttons at all just the type. I thought maybe a cache problem so changed my options before logging on and same thing. Only way I got it to correct was to put my new graphics on pieces03 over the exsisting buttons and not use the pieces05.tga I had them on.

My point is don't get to carried away now :)
