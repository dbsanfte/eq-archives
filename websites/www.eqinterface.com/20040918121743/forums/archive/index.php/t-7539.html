There is a 'Blank.tga' for both
Inventory = 'Sona_IW_CharFillBlank.tga'
Player/Target = 'Sona_FillerBlank.tga'

Open each of the blanks.
Position the picture you want in the area that is not white.
Save the picture as Sona_IW_CharFill.tga or Sona_Filler.tga
Voila! Your pic now in the UI!

If you have no pic and just want it blank, open each file and copy or save them with the 'NON-Blank' name.

I am leaving it up to each individual to make their own pics.
