Maybe its just me, and I'm not good at looking beteen the player window and the group window, so I decided to make some changes to the group window to make it easier for me, a Shaman, to keep an eye on my own health while I'm watching my group as well. I find that in many cases, I am the primary healer and looking at the group window takes my eyes away from my own health, and I have died as the result of it. Either I'm lazy or all the new features just get me confused sometimes, and I like things simple. *shrug*

So here's what I did, basically I downloaded ( aka Stole =P )Sokol's group window that had Player HP added to the top of the group window, but I still had to rely on the player window for mana bar and other stats. So I added mana up top and stats to the bottom. Also put Current and total HP up top as well. Ok enough blabbing, here it is:

Please feel free to post comments / complaints.

If you DO NOT want the STATS displayed, OPEN the EQUI_GroupWindow.xml file in NOTEPAD, scroll almost the very bottom and find this section: <Screen item="GroupWindow">

Then change THIS:
<Size>
<CX>150</CX>
<CY>212</CY>

to

<Size>
<CX>150</CX>
<CY>185</CY>


All you are doing is changing the window size from 212 to 185
