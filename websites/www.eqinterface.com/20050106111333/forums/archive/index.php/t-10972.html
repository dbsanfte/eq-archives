keep in mind that i'm still learning this too hehe...this is what it looks like to me....i could be wrong tho:

here is the section that effects the actual window itself:

Screen item="ContainerWindow">
- <!-- <ScreenID/>
-->
- <!-- <Font/>
-->
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
- <Location>
<X>350</X>
<Y>100</Y>
</Location>
- <Size>
<CX>250</CX>
<CY>50</CY>
</Size>

now...this is the area that effects the size of the window:

<Size>
<CX>250</CX>
<CY>50</CY>
</Size>

now the CX is the width and the CY is the height...as in the X and Y axis (think plotting points from algebra +) so adjust these accordingly....but keep in mind that lets say you have a border on the LEFT or TOP sides....these coordinates will NOT change that...they only effect the right and bottom borders....if the top and left are where your borders are, then you need to edit the locations of the boxes/buttons accordingly for that....hope this helps!
