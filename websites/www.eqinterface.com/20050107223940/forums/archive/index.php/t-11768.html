You can remove the numbers form the buttons by changing the xml in the EQUI_CombatAbilityWnd.

Change all tha ButtonDrawTemplates to use BigBtn instead.
Example for Combat Button 1:
Change:
<ButtonDrawTemplate>
<Normal>A_CombatButton1Normal</Normal>
<Pressed>A_CombatButton1Pressed</Pressed>
<Flyby>A_CombatButton1Flyby</Flyby>
<Disabled>A_CombatButton1Disabled</Disabled>
<PressedFlyby>A_CombatButton1PressedFlyby</PressedFlyby>
</ButtonDrawTemplate>

to:
<ButtonDrawTemplate>
<Normal>A_BigBtnNormal</Normal>
<Pressed>A_BigBtnPressed</Pressed>
<Flyby>A_BigBtnFlyby</Flyby>
<Disabled>A_BigBtnDisabled</Disabled>
<PressedFlyby>A_BigBtnPressedFlyby</PressedFlyby>
</ButtonDrawTemplate>
