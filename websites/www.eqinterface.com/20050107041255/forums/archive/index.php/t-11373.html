If you already have a copy of the Book in text format, copy it to your EQ/Storylin/ folder and rename it storyXXXXX.txt . The first line will be what will appear in the list of topics in your storyline window (ingame).

Now the Bad news is that there will no line returns, It will just all blur together because of the lack of formatting. One way of doing this is to open your text file, select all (cnt A) then past it in excel where it will fill column A. Then type <br> in B1 & =A1&B1 in C1. Drag B1/C1 down ot the bottom of the book. Copy all of colomn C and repaste in your original file, replacing what was originally there.

Should look a lot better
