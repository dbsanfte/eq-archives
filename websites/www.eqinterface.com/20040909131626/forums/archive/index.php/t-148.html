yes, that is the view port command..


screen res 1280x1024

viewport setting of:

/viewport 110 1 1024 768

here is the break down

/viewport <1> <2> <3> <4>

<1> how far in from left
<2> how far down from top
<3> how wide of window
<4> how tall of window

soo for mine

<1> 110pixels in from left
<2> 1 pixel down from top
<3> 1024 pixels wide
<4> 768 pixels tall

only the view window changes..

if your running 1024x768 try this

/viewport 112 1 800 600

that will center a 800x600 window on your screen (as seen above)

or raise resolution to 1280x1024 (as show above) since the only "GAME" area is 1024x768 on mine.. i get the same lag as if playing 1024x768 but all the windows are outside he area.. makes reading chats easier, looting MUCH easier. and screenshots of action better =)

hope that helps
