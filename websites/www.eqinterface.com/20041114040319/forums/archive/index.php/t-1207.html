To change the font color look below:

<StaticText item ="SBW_SpellName0">
<ScreenID>SBW_SpellName0</ScreenID>
<Font>2</Font>
<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>240</B>
</TextColor>
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>100</X>
<Y>50</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>100</CX>
<CY>16</CY>
</Size>
<Text>Complete Healing</Text>
<NoWrap>false</NoWrap>
<AlignCenter>true</AlignCenter>
<AlignRight>false</AlignRight>
</StaticText>


Every spell from 0 -15 you need to add this:
<TextColor>
<R>0</R>
<G>0</G>
<B>240</B>
</TextColor>

You can change the colors to your desire:
RGB Color Codes (http://html-color-codes.com/rgb.html)
