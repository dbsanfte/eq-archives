Ok, I got this one figured out.

This is the actual area of the cursor attachment. You adjust how much area the cursor attachment has in the sizes listed below:

<Screen item = "CursorAttachment">
<ScreenID/>
<Font/>
<RelativePosition>false</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>15</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>100</CX>
<CY>38</CY>
</Size>


You then adjust the location of individual pieces items to where you want them in relation to the CursorAttachment size. After I got it figured out, i could see its really no different that a regular window. I just wasn't thinking of it in those terms, which had me puzzled.
