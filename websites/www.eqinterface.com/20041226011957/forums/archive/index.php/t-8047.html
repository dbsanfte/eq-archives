100,000 Members! Kudane announced (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?threadid=8027) that EQInterface/EQGui has passed the 100,000 Member mark! Congratulations to Kudane and Dolby, and cheers to all the members who've made this site THE home for Everquest User Interface modding!
