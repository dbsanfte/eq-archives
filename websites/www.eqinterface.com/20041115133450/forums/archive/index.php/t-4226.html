Just finished this today, I'll be uploading soon if I can get a couple little bugs worked out. It's exactly what the subject says: a music player window with vert-like button graphics. (Note that the windows in the pictures are shown using Vertebrae's mod, if you put it into another skin the windows may look a little different.)

Here's some pictures:

(full version, with progress bar and combobox)
http://home.attbi.com/~ryouken_kageru/music1.jpg

(compact version, took out combobox and progess bar to save space)
http://home.attbi.com/~ryouken_kageru/music2.jpg


Included in the zip are both the 'compact' and the 'normal' window files.

By default, the 'normal' (top image) music player window will be unzipped into your mod directory, but you can switch to the 'compact' (bottom image) window by renaming 'Kagemod - EQUI_MusicPlayerWnd COMPACT.xml ' (found in the subfolder called kagemusic\) to 'EQUI_MusicPlayerWnd.xml' and dropping it into your mod directory. (For example, C:\...\ Everquest\uifiles\vert)

Chrysm,

Make sure you have Sound turned on in EQ, and make sure that EQUI.xml (exact file name) in your vert folder has these lines in it:

<Include>EQUI_MusicPlayerWnd.xml</Include>
<Include>EQUI_FileSelectionWnd.xml</Include>

Put these right before the </Compsite> tag in that file and you'll be all set.

As, if there is no EQUI.xml/you fixed your XML, and turning on sound doesn't work ( this option can be found in eqclient.ini... Sound=xx), I would /bug it.

Otherwise that should do it.
