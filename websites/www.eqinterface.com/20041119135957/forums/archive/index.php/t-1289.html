I've never tried this, so your mileage may vary.

The blinking red around the player window is hard-coded. So, the only way to get that around the "target window" is to turn the player window into the target window. To do this, you can reposition all existing player window content off the screen and put in a new gauge with EQType 6, which is target HP I think.

If you want, you can then go about adding your player window stuff into your previous target window and make it the player window I guess. You'd lose some functionality I believe. You may not be able to click your health bar to target yourself and the pet health wouldn't automatically pop up I don't think. But again, I've never tried, so I'm not sure how much you'd lose.

Hope that helps you somewhat.
