I had the same problem...

So I played around with it till it looked right again (at least until they fix it)

1. Open up your UI folder and find EQUI_Playerwinodw

2. Open with notepad

3. Find "Gauge item" (without the qoutes)

Change
<Location>
<X>3</X>
<Y>-30</Y>
</Location>

to
<Location>
<X>33</X>
<Y>-30</Y>
</Location>

Should fix it right up till they mess it up again :)
