<img src="http://www.eqinterface.com/images/sections/eqinterf-construction.gif" align="right">I’m pleased to say that the majority of the nasty site bugs have been stomped and I now need new shoes.

This was probably one of the most complex site updates I have done yet because of the backend being so different. That’s why many of the bugs didn’t show their face until our update went live.

I would like to ask that if you find any website errors or bugs that you please report it posting in our Site Help & Suggestions (http://www.eqinterface.com/forums/forumdisplay.php?f=46) forum with as much information about it as you can.

Some of the new features as result of this upgrade are:

Download system:
- Authors can upload up to 4 screen shots of their interface.
- Users to rate interfaces.
- Users can now comment on interfaces.
- Authors and Users can keep track of their uploads/downloads (http://www.eqinterface.com/downloads/filecpl.php?)
- Updated search capabilities
- Updated sorting capabilities
- Many nice features for our admins and the ability to allow us to add more features in the future.
- and more.

Forum:
- Ability to track your own attachments
- Multiple attachments per post in the forums.
- Thumbnail’s of attached images in posts.
- New thread display modes. (Normal, Threaded, Hybrid)
- Easy drop down menu’s to reduce clutter
- Faster load times
- Updated PM system
- A nice new searchable FAQ system.
(content of FAQ system will be updated very soon)
- and more.

The paint is still wet and we are adding second coats. So watch for small tweaks and updates over the next couple weeks.
