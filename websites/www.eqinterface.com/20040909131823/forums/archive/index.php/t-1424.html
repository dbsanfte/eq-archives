I DLed Vertebrae's set of textures because they looked good on a screenshot. The TGA files look beautiful indeed. Alas, EQ butchers all the fine rendering (on my end) so buttons end up having 2-4 ugly steps instead of the intended smooth transitions.

So I did my own. Some resemblance to rendering is there but everything was tested against the butcher's job EQ does of it when trying to display. The color scheme somewhat resembles Vert's although it's very much simplified.
I also corrected some alignment problems with borders and scroll bars like you would find in the Vert set.

http://www.hentschke-keramik.de/rmh/textures2.jpg

Doesnt it have all the charm of a white toilet seat? But it's sure neat and clean. :)

Something I can safely use at 4am on a CR in Sebilis without having to look twice at a hotkeyed thing.

If you want PRETTY textures like wood or brambles, ask an artist. Not me. :P
