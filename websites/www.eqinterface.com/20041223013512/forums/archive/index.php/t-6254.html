Patch Message, 02/04/03 (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6271)
A number of UI files were patched today, including EQUI.xml, EQUI_Animations.xml, and window_pieces04.tga (yes, again). UI authors have their work cut out for them - lucky for you, Cairenn has already posted the fixes (http://www.eqgui.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6274)!

/who all guild?
The Legacy of Ykesha (http://www.legacyofykesha.com/) update today is the Guild Management Tool (http://everquest.station.sony.com/legacy/f_guildtool.jsp), which will show a full list of all members (on- or off-line), including class and level, and current location if online. Public and Personal Notes can be added, and Guild Management (i.e. demotion/removal) will work even if the subject is not in-game!
