There is a problem with the ATI 9600 Video cards and the 3D target ring (they are not displaying the ring even under the default UI.) SOE is aware of the problem and is looking into it (actually Roger made a post here regarding it and Kudane linked it on the home page.) Hopefully this will be resolved soon.

The external program that some have used (including myself) with some success is 3D Analyzer by Tommti, but it has not worked for everyone and some have noticed other graphic anomalies when using it.

I'm sure this isn't the answer you wanted, but its all we have at the moment.
