The actual border graphics are in windowpieces1. Each border is made up of 12 pieces of frame that get arranged to make the border. Frames are declared in the Animations file, and WDT's (window draw templates) are in the templates.xml. Check out AboutSIDL.doc in your everquest/uifiles/default folder for more on the WDTs.

Vert mods are a prime expamle of altered window borders, I'd suggest opening up the Windowpieces1 from a vert mod folder and the default windowpieces1 file and laying them side by side to get a feel for what is involved in border alterations.

Hope this helps

Lunakin
