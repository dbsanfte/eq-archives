Sorry, I miss understood the original problem. Ok, to move the buff bar,
1. Make sure you have a buff on you.
2. Right-Click the very edge of the buff icon and Unlock it.
3. Left-Click the edge of the icon again and HOLD. (Should show the usual Move mouse pointer, arrows in all directions is default)
4. Now move it to desired location, and unclick.
5. Right-Click edge again to LOCK the window.
