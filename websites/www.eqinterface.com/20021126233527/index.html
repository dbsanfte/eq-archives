***Important Info***:
If you encounter errors while following links that lead to an EQ Interface location, it's most likely because User Registration is required to access the Downloads area. Take a minute to register; it's free, and there's a ton of content.

Updates from 10/21/02 onward can be found in News and Updates;
News previous to 10/21/02 can be found in the News Archive.

Don't forget to check the Released Forum for new UI mods and skins that may not be getting uploaded - as time allows, I'll try to include as many as I can here.
Go ahead and upload, folks! That's what it's there for!

Remember, information regarding an new or updated upload should be sent to Kudane or Dolby.

Patch Message 11/19/02 has been posted by Cairenn; she also has posted a list of fixes!


Announcements
Today's News



Announcements
  • Statix has come up with a method to make UI mods unbreakable by patches! It's likely to still see some tweaking, but you can follow the discussion in this thread, or visit Statix's site.



Today's News

Featured Skins
  • Remelio has updated the EQUI.xml used in the T.King/Remelio mods to be current with the 11/19 patch.

Complete Sets
  • Kanon has updated AYA UI to version 5.00; there's a ton of info included, so follow the link to see what's what!
  • Etasi has released Etasi UI, with original XML and graphics.
  • JarynVZ has made JarynUI available; this is a Drakah-based UI, with some "user friendly" changes.

Group Window
  • Chai has created a new Group Window with a new look and original graphics; included in the package are matching Player and Target windows.

Inventory
  • Xsikal has modified the Abbatoir Inventory to fit 1024 x 768 resolution; the mod may also work at 800x600.

Miscellaneous
There are a number of new MP3Plyer mods out - folks wasted no time!
  • Ashkue has created Musicbox 1.0, with Winamp buttons
  • Kanon has released a Music Player Skin for the AYA UI
  • Yozzy has created a full Winamp skin for the Music Player
  • Haliken has created an MP3 Timer, a mod that includes three MP3 files of 6 min, 30 min, 60 min durations and a playlist to load the files.

Mix & Match
  • Ensonia has uploaded Soni's Bard UI, with design, mods, and graphics aimed at music makers
