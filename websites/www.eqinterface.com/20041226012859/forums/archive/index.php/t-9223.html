Well, I haven't figured out the why, but I was able to fix the problem by letting the first few pixel lines of the original inventory window show through the image.

To make the image, I took the original inventory box, put the frame and the background on two different layers, and fit my picture in on a layer between them. To fix it, I added a masking layer over the picture layer, but under the frame layer, and let the first few lines of the background show through.

Just in case anyone was wondering. ;)
