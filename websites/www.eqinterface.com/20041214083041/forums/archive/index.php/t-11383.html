12-17-2003, 06:46 AM
You can edit the ring yourself to be purple on even con if you really want.

Open the TargetIndicator.ini file you downloaded of the flavor for your choice.

Under the [FairMatch] tag (which is the white/black/even tag), scroll down to the last three Variables (The Red/Blue/Green values) and replace them with this:

Red=255
Green=0
Blue=255

That should make it purple for ya.
