- Major Overhaul of the Backround Grafics

09-15-2002, 11:02 AM
Ensure that you have the following lines in your EQUI_Animations.xml file--they should be right at the very and, just before the "compass art" section:

<Ui2DAnimation item = "A_DividerVertical">
<Cycle>true</Cycle>
<Frames>
<Texture>window_pieces03.tga</Texture>
<Location>
<X>0</X>
<Y>96</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>100</CX>
<CY>24</CY>
</Size>
<Hotspot>
<X>0</X>
<Y>0</Y>
</Hotspot>
<Duration>1000</Duration>
</Frames>
</Ui2DAnimation>

This is when they made the price, name and trader sections in the bazaar window resizable, and if you don't have this line in the window will crash you when you try to load it. I'm not sure of the nature of *your* problem, but if this is it, I thought it might help:P
