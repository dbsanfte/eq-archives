This has happened to me too with a few different mods. The thing that worked for me was to open up 'UI_charactername_xx.ini' with notepad and check the settings.

[BuffWindow]
XPos800x600=100
YPos800x600=100
Width=44
Height=664
BGTint.red=255
BGTint.green=255
BGTint.blue=255
Fades=true
Delay=2000
Duration=500
Alpha=255
FadeToAlpha=1
Locked=false
Show=1
XPos1024x768=0
YPos1024x768=-7

For whatever your given resolution is, make sure that the xpos and ypos are values that will appear onscreen, once i saw the values way off and fixed it, next, check the Width and Height and make sure they are set to reasonable values.

I just set the values so it would appear on the middle of the screen, then you can resize and reposition once you've got EQ running.

It worked for me, hopefully it works for you too.
