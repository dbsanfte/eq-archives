Okay... in your UIErrors, the telling line is:

[Thu Feb 13 21:15:50 2003] [Line:5 Source:UIFiles\Addams\EQUI_PetInfoWindow.xml] ParseNodeList() SyntaxError


As close as I can tell, line 5 is:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="us-ascii"?>
<XML ID="EQInterfaceDefinitionLanguage">
<Schema xmlns="EverQuestData" xmlns:dt="EverQuestDataTypes" />
<!-- Graphics By: t.king -->
<!—- Code: Kuvasae -->


If you'll look, in the last line, the 3rd character is longer than the other dashes; in Notepad it appears as a solid block. If you'll change that character to a regular dash, I think your problem will be solved.
