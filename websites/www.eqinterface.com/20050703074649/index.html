  6-29-05 patch
Posted By: Dolby - 06-29-2005 07:44 PM - Replies (0)
Today a patch was pushed live to the Everquest Servers. This patch brought some changes to the interface. Please be patient for authors and gurus to post fixes. If your having trouble with a mod make sure to attach or post your uierrors.txt.

You can find a compare of what has changed in the interface here. Those of you having color issues may want read Xeraxis post in this thread for a solution.



*** Headlines ***
  • Plane of Time Instancing! - Plane of Time has been fully instanced. Every raid that enters the zone will now get its own instance of the zone.
  • New Tutorial! - A new tutorial is now available, featuring new art, new models, new quests, and a new player experience that will take you from level 1 to level 10.
  • New Newbie Armor Quests! - New armor quests pick up where the tutorial leaves off, taking players from 11 to 20.
  • You can now invite players to your group across zones. We have enabled the ability to use the /invite command with a players name to allow you to invite someone that is not in your current zone.
  • "Auto Consent Group", "Auto Consent Raid", and "Auto Consent Guild" buttons have been added to the options window; when checked, your group/raid/guild will automatically be given consent to move any new corpses you drop.
[Patch Notes]


*** Changed Interface Files ***
  • EQUI.xml
  • EQUI_AdvancedDisplayOptionsWnd.xml
  • EQUI_CharaterSelect.xml
  • EQUI_MerchantWnd.xml
  • EQUI_OptionsWindow.xml
  • EQUI_TicketWindow.xml

  Next Expansion "Depths of Darkhollow"
Posted By: Kudane - 06-18-2005 02:07 PM - Replies (1)
Sorry I am a little behind on posting this, but here is the press release info for Depths of Darkhollow the next expansion for EverQuest.

Quote:
On the world's surface, the battle between Firiona Vie and Lanys T'Vyl continues to rage throughout the lands. As a new evil begins to emerge from the underground, you will journey into a world of immortality and devastation as you struggle against the overwhelming power of Dreadspirekeep. This terror is unlike anything ever seen before and you will engage in an epic struggle to keep it from surfacing to the world of Norrath. EverQuest: Depths of Darkhollow includes the following features:
  • Monster Missions: Play as you never have before and see Norrath through the eyes of some of its fearsome and powerful creatures as you step into their form to do battle against their ancient foes. Experience EverQuest lore first hand through the eyes of another character in Monster Missions and unlock the ability to use more powerful monsters!
  • Spirit Shrouds: Don the form of a lower level creature and join your friends in battle while earning experience for your normal character!
  • Evolving Items: Earn new items that advance as you use them, becoming more powerful over time and unlocking hidden abilities and wield intelligent items with a personality of their own!
  • New Missions: Battle your way through over 60 new missions.
  • New Creatures, New Environments and New Encounters: More than 30 unique creatures, 7 zones, 15 small instances, with a re-envisioned Nektulos Forest.
  • New Crafting, Spells, Items and More!

You can read the full press release here

  May 11th patch
Posted By: Dolby - 05-11-2005 05:24 PM - Replies (0)
A patch to Everquest went live today May 11th 2005. This patch brought some interface changes to some commonly changed files for custom interfaces.

If your interface is not loading please see this quick fix you can perform to try and get your custom interface to atleast load again.

Please be patient and allow interface authors time to update their interface. Badgering them about it will only make it longer until they release an update. Posts requesting or asking for updates will be deleted.

Reminder:
If you have a patch fix for an interface that is not your own you can now submit it for others to download by going to the broken interfaces file info screen and clicking the [upload patch] link. Once the patch is approved by an admin this will show as an optional patch to download under that interface. Please only include the files you fixed and nothing else.

*** Changed XML Files ***
  • EQUI.xml
  • EQUI_AAWindow.xml
  • EQUI_Animations.xml
  • EQUI_CastingWindow.xml
  • EQUI_ConfirmationDialog.xml
  • EQUI_MailCompositionWindow.xml
  • EQUI_MailWindow.xml
  • EQUI_MerchantWnd.xml
  • EQUI_OptionsWindow.xml
  • EQUI_SelectorWnd.xml
  • EQUI_ServerListWnd.xml
  • window_pieces04.tga
  • window_pieces05.tga
[View Changes]


*** Patch Headlines ***
  • Many quests in city zones that give XP as a reward now give significantly more.
  • Veteran's Rewards will be available on May 13th. You can find out more details about these rewards at this location.
  • The required levels on many spells level 50 and below have been changed so that all casting classes will now receive at least 1 new spell every level.
  • Great new functionality has been added to the mail system. Some of the major features are listed below, but also check the bottom of the patch message for details.
  • Multiple recipients, including a dynamically generated guild mailing list.
  • EverQuest mail forwarding. You can now have your EverQuest mail forwarded to the e-mail address registered with the station account of the character you are playing. NOTE: you cannot reply to the forwarded mail.
  • New Address Book. You can now setup mailing lists on your client and store your friend's addresses in your address book.
