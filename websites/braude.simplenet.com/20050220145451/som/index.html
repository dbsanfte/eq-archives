Site of the Month, February 2005
Cool-Tots.com

Welcome to the SimpleNet Site of the Month Program! Each month we’ll spotlight a new SimpleNet web site, with the previous winners archived to the right. The only requirement for becoming our Site of the Month is to be a SimpleNet customer.

Cool Tots began with a baby boy named Marc, a mom who loved to sew, and an idea for a blanket that withstood the tugs, spills, and still looked “cool” wash after wash. The little boy, Marc - now a toddler - still sleeps under his zebra blanket. The design of the blankets and hats was derived from a desire for something different than the typical pastel colors. Cool Tots is bound to be the first blanket and hat you reach for, whether it's for a cover when you are out for a walk, or on a visit to see family and friends. It’s all about being a Cool Tot!

Cool-Tots.com is a SimpleNet Commerce Account. Miva Merchant provides their online shopping cart functionality. In testament to the ease with which you can create a site, the owner of Cool Tots completed this web site in two week’s time.
