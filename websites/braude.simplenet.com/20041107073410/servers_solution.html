Dedicated Servers

Dedicated servers provide a simple solution to a wide range of Internet needs. They also allow you to maximize your return-on-investment through a lease, rather than own, arrangement. By leveraging the speed, space, technology and security of SimpleNet’s Data Center, you put yourself in a position to succeed when you are in need of:

 · Large amounts of disk space or email storage
 · High bandwidth content or streaming media provision
 · Using or providing custom software and databases
 · A focus on security and privacy
 · The hosting of multiple sites in one location

For those customers located outside of the United States, our dedicated server and bandwidth solutions are extremely ideal for a low-cost, yet high-speed web presence despite the location of your target market.
