Dedicated Servers - Additional Services

Data Backup Services $25.00 Setup $75.00 /mo.*

SimpleNet now offers Data Backup services for RAQ Dedicated Servers. Data Backup is an excellent way to further insure that your data is safe. With our Data Backup service your data is fully backed up each week and incrementally backed up each night.

When a backup is performed, your data is transferred securely from your dedicated server to our central tape storage system. If data restoration is ever required, we quickly transfer data from these tape drives and get your server up and running effectively.

Transfer of data to and from our Backup server does not count against your bandwidth subscription level.

The following directories are stored on our backup server:
/etc /var /bin /sbin /home /usr

NOTE: *Restoration requests are billed at the additional rate of $25.00 per event. Software installed after a restoration on SimpleNet Dedicated Servers may require re-installation and/or re-configuration. Any archives or binaries that you wish to have backed up should be stored in one of the directories shown above.
     

Packet Filtering $25.00 Setup $50.00 /mo.

Packet filtering restricts traffic to only the desired ports on your SimpleNet dedicated server. These filters or ACLs (Access Control Lists) can help to augment the security of your server by helping to limit unwanted queries and probes to your server. With our Packet Filtering service you can choose from 3 different levels of filtering and change between them at any time. These filters cannot be customized.

Level 1 Filtering:
Allows the Internet to connect to your web server, secure server, send and receive email, move files via FTP and you may connect to your server via Telnet/SSH.
 
Level 2 Filtering:
Allows the Internet to connect to your web server, secure server, send and receive email, and you may connect to your server via SSH. Files are moved via SCP.
 
Level 3 Filtering:
Allows the Internet to connect to your web server, secure server, move files via SCP, and connect to your server with SSH. Email traffic is blocked with this setting.

Please allow 1 business day for application and change of selected Packet Filters.
NOTE: Security is achieved through multiple steps. It is important that you utilize effective passwords and keep server patches current.

Have a high traffic site?

SimpleNet can provide you with active, network load-balancing between your Dedicated Servers. This is an excellent way to divide HTTP traffic over 2 or more dedicated servers. This can greatly increase responsiveness and redundancy for your web presence.
