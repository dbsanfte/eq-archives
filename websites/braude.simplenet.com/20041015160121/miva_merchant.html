Miva Merchant

Miva Merchant allows you to build the most comprehensive, professional ecommerce storefront on the Internet. It includes modules for customer registration, shipping integration, inventory tracking, affiliate management, transaction processing and much more. Miva Merchant has a complete set of wizards to simplify the process of building your store, maintaining your product catalog, and fulfilling orders.

Miva Merchant is included in the SimpleNet Commerce package and can be added to the S-150 and S-300 packages.

Miva Merchant 4.22 includes:

NEW - Miva Marketplace™
Miva Marketplace is a pay-per-click direct marketing system designed to drive targeted traffic to your online store. Miva Marketplace product listings reach over 77% of all Internet users through distribution agreements with Dealtime and hundreds of traffic affiliates.
Learn more by visiting the Miva Marketplace web site.

NEW - Affiliate Program
Build your own affiliate network and attract new customers to your storefront! You save time and money by managing your affiliate program at the same time you manage your store. This new features makes it easy to control commissions you pay to affiliates for sales and click-thrus.

NEW - Miva Mailer™
Miva Mailer allows you to conveniently email customers and affiliates relevant information about your site, storefront and special offers.
