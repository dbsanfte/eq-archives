Divinia's Den 1:45 PM - obtained from http://www.station.sony.com/everquest/
ShadowKnights will welcome this new location to discuss their issues:

With the recent problems with the Soerbaird forums (Including the Knightwatch forum); it has recently been hard for Shadow Knights to gather and exchange ideas. To that end, I have created a site with a Shadow Knight forum to continue our discussions. It may be found at Divinia's Den (http://members.xoom.com/divinia). Please join us!

Thank you,

Divinia D'Muerte
Shadowknight in the service of Innoruuk

Mythiran Tower Update 1:45 PM - obtained from http://www.station.sony.com/everquest/
With the changes being made to AllaKhazam's site, The Mythiran Tower will also under go some changes:

Howdy folks at the Station and all you loyal readers!

Finally got some news for the Mythiran Tower, the most complete and best RESEARCH site on the net (all spell ingredient listings, bboards, FAQ, helpful files, etc). Anyway, if you follow Allakhazam at all, you'll know the story and why I had some downtime (I was with him at Gamestats). The good news is, the Tower is back up! The domain name will be mythiran.allakhazam.com once we get the domain transfer, but for now you can link up using http://216.155.25.103/

Anyway, all you researching magic guys and gals come on by and check out the new look and new info and let us at the Tower know what you think! Happy Researching!!!

Zehnman of the Mythiran Tower

Order of The Magi Event 1:40 PM - obtained from http://www.station.sony.com/everquest/
Citizens of Bristlebane server, come help the Order of The Magi guild celebrate their birth:

Come one, Come all to the Inaugural Dungeon Raid for the Caster Guild Order of the Magi!!!

This is our first guild event and it will be exciting for all levels!!

The Raid is to Cleanse the Dungeon of Najena
Pure random distribution of lore items for quests and rare drop items!

High level, want to show off your skill and prowess?

Low level, want an escort into a deep dungeon for a tour??
Corpse retrieval will be provided!!!

Non-caster classes welcome to the Innagural Event!!

Enjoy a wonderful collection of player made brews and spirits

A verity of exotic foods will also be provided!!

The meeting will be in the East Commons for travel and escort to Najena, from 9:30 - 10:00 p.m. EST on Saturday January 15, with the dungeon raid following at 10:15 p.m.

Please come and help us "cut the ribbon" on our new casters guild Order of Magi

All questions e-mailed to orderofmagi@niia.net

Good Luck and I hope to see many adventurers of Norath in attendance

--
Xefer
Necromancer of the 16 Circle
Officer in the Order of the Magi on Bristlebane
