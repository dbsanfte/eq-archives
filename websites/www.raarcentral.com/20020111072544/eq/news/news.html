2001-12-25 Holiday Update (a little late)

And so the day came when COP turned towards the forbidden halls of the Temple of Veeshan. When we looked into the darkness there. We saw only death at first. The dark cold night of the dragons' breaths and their flurrying drake guards. And then looking more we saw the glitter. The flicker of magical flames. We heard the tinkling of coins and we saw the raiments that would clothe us well. And we knew what it was to crave.

On that day, the land trembled as we moved into those hallowed halls. Our shouts of challenge echoed in the halls of testing as we slew Dozekar. His death cries foretold the wyrm blood that would be split upon the temple's floor. For we went into the forbidden halls and RAVAGED!!!!!!!!

It felt good to slay! The Claws of Veeshan failed to provide us with rewards for our genecide against the giants. And all of Vulak's companions felt our anger. And when his companions were no more and only Vulak remained we could sense him fearing his certain death.
