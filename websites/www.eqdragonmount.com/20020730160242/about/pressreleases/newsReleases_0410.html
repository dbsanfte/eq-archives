CGN IS BUZZING WITH BIZLAND.COM WIN

Boston, Mass. --April 10, 2000--


CGN has been named agency of record for Burlington, Mass.-based BizLand.com; the leading Internet hub for small business. CGN will be responsible for the development and launch of the BizLand.com brand positioning platform. Annual billings are estimated at $10 million.

BizLand.com empowers its 450,000 small business members with a complete array of services needed to successfully grow their business. In addition to free Web site hosting, BizLand.com offers Web site hosting, e-commerce storefronts, business promotional tools and other Internet services for small businesses -BizLand.com members also receive substantial discounts on business products and services as well as access to a valuable forum to exchange ideas with like-minded professionals. BizLand.com's online small business community also offers top-notch customer support.

CGN will be responsible for the development and launch of the BizLand.com's brand positioning platform as well as an integrated creative campaign including radio, print, direct mail e-marketing to customers, affiliate and affinity programs, web design, as well as planning and placement of all BizLand.com media.

President and CEO of CGN, Ken Dec said, "The goal is to ensure that BizLand.com is the dominant small business advocate on the Web. We'll help the company achieve this goal by delivering a completely integrated customer acquisition, cultivation and monetization campaign from broadcast and print to customer, affiliate and affinity e-marketing and e-care programs and everything in between."

"We are thrilled to have CGN as part of the BizLand.com team. Their depth of experience in business-to-business marketing made them the perfect choice for BizLand.com," said Steve Sydness, CEO of BizLand.com. "CGN will play a key role in building the BizLand.com brand, strengthening our relationship with the over 450,000 businesses we have as members today and attracting millions of small businesses to our community."

CGN is a fast-growing, Boston-based marketing and creative services company whose capabilities focus on delivering comprehensive marketing solutions through a unique blend of business and marketing strategy, brand-building creative and one-to-one Internet marketing expertise.

BizLand.com, Inc.
BizLand.com (www.BizLand.com) is the leading Internet hub for small business, created to enable small companies to capitalize on the power of e-business. The Burlington, MA company provides its growing customer base with a complete array of free Web-based services like Web site hosting, e-commerce storefronts, business promotional tools and other Internet services for small businesses. BizLand.com also cultivates a strong small business community through its forums, articles, message boards, advice from industry experts, as well as discounts on business products and services. Since its launch in July 1999, BizLand's membership community has skyrocketed to over 450,000 members.
