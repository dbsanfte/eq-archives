BizLand.com Names Steve Sydness Chief Executive Officer

BURLINGTON, Mass. --August 23, 1999

Online Community of Small Businesses Now at 45,000 members - and Growing


BizLand.com™, a rapidly growing online community of independent businesses, has named Steve Sydness Chief Executive Officer. Sydness joins BizLand.com from Great Plains Software, Fargo, North Dakota, where he was Executive Vice President of Worldwide Sales and Marketing. Ravi Agarwal has been serving as CEO since founding BizLand.com in 1997, and he will remain chairman of the company. Sydness will be responsible for day-to-day management, long term strategy and future growth of the company.

"BizLand.com has already surged to 45,000 small business members, and we expect 175,000 members by year end. Our rapid growth and ambitious plan to become the definitive Internet resource for small businesses require somebody of Steve's impressive management experience and talent," says Agarwal.

"The opportunity to realize the full value of the Internet for small businesses around the world has just begun," says Sydness. "BizLand.com's unique vision to offer a comprehensive Internet resource for small businesses and leverage the cooperative strength of its rapidly growing online community give it a decided competitive advantage. It will be exciting to help build on Ravi's grand vision."

Sydness enjoyed a 12-year career of increasing leadership and management responsibilities with Great Plains. While at Great Plains, his accomplishments included helping navigate the company through a successful IPO, which featured the development of the first-ever IPO Web site approved by the SEC; helping manage growth from 100 to 1,200 employees in 12 countries; and successfully leading worldwide sales, marketing, channel management and strategic alliance teams.

Sydness was also instrumental in Great Plains' global expansion, including managing international partnerships, joint ventures and the acquisition of subsidiaries. He also helped lead Great Plains' efforts to raise venture capital financing, participating in all phases of the funding process.

Sydness, prior to joining Great Plains, served as a consultant at Kissinger Associates and McKinsey & Company, working with market-leading clients worldwide. He was graduated from the Harvard Business School in 1981 with an M.B.A. He also holds a B.A. in Business Administration and History from Principia College in Elsah, Illinois.

Sydness joins BizLand.com on September 7th. He can be reached at 781-272-5585 or email at SteveS@BizLand-inc.com.

BizLand.com
BizLand.com (http://www.BizLand.com), based in Burlington, Massachusetts, is an online community for small businesses worldwide. BizLand.com offers everything a small business needs to succeed on and off the Internet. This includes a Co-op with group buying power, free professional Web sites, promotion, communications tools and e-commerce services for running a business successfully online. Low-cost premium services and enhancements are also available. There are currently 45,000 BizLand.com members. Membership is expected to reach 175,000 by the end of 1999.
