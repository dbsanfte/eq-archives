BizLand.com Launches Shopping & Online Transaction Suite for Small Business

BURLINGTON, Mass. --December 20, 1999

Open Market, Signio and Heartland Payment Systems Partner with BizLand.com


BizLand, Inc., the leading Internet hub for small business, today announced a powerful suite of e-commerce tools and services designed to help small businesses sell their specialized products and services online.

"BizLand's e-commerce suite will do for our members what MP3 has done for the music industry," said Steve Sydness, BizLand.com chief executive officer. "Our new e-commerce suite will enable BizLand members to realize the power and the potential the Internet presents to their small businesses."

"BizLand.com's new e-commerce suite opens the doors of the New Economy to its nearly 200,000 members," said Keith Lietzke, vice president of product marketing at Open Market. "We're pleased that BizLand has chosen Open Market's ShopSite as the store-building tool of choice for this suite. Open Market's ShopSite™ is specifically designed to make setting up and maintaining a commerce Web site simple. It offers every small business the tools they need to be competitive in today's marketplace."

By partnering with Open Market™, Signio™ and Heartland Payment Systems™, Bizland.com can now offer to its 200,000 registered members a full suite of state of the art e-commerce tools and services. Bizland.com users can showcase their business offerings in a catalog format and easily process transactions online.

The new e-commerce suite offers business owners the ability to open "storefronts," develop online catalogs, offer shopping carts and, securely and easily accept credit cards over the Internet. By completing simple forms, BizLand.com members are able to "open shop" quickly and begin transacting business immediately from their small business website.

The suite will be offered at $99 for three months. "This pricing is testimony to our commitment to bringing the most cost effective products, services and solutions on the Internet to BizLand.com members," said Sydness. The suite elements are also being offered on an "a la carte" basis. Pricing options for the individual components are available at www.BizLand.com.

Open Market & ShopSite
Open Market, Inc. (NASDAQ: OMKT) offers an integrated set of open, scaleable applications that enable enterprises to move their businesses to the Web. Open Market's ShopSite is an easy-to-use online store creation system that allows merchants to build and maintain catalogs of products to sell over the Internet. With ShopSite, any merchant can create a professionally designed, fully commerce-enabled store in a matter of minutes. More information on ShopSite can be found at www.shopsite.com.

Signio's Payment Platform
Signio's payment platform harnesses the power of the Internet to deliver a robust, easy-to-deploy and low-cost e-commerce payment solution to on-line merchants, merchant aggregators, business exchanges and buyer and seller communities. Signio offers consolidated payment services for credit cards, debit cards and Internet checks for a monthly flat fee. Automated clearinghouse (ACH) transactions are also available through the Signio service. Unlike other HTTP based payment solutions, Signio's TCP/IP based service maintains the data during the entire process, guaranteeing a risk and trouble free transaction.

Heartland Payment Systems
Heartland Payment Systems is a member service provider for Heartland Bank, St. Louis, Missouri and Key Bank, National Association, Cleveland OH. Heartland Payment Systems is a full-service merchant card processor, offering merchants a comprehensive set of payment processing services. Heartland Payment Systems is located online at www.visa-mc.com

BizLand.com
BizLand.com (http://www.BizLand.com), based in Burlington, MA, is the leading Internet hub for small business. Founded in 1999 by entrepreneur Ravi Agarwal, BizLand.com offers small business owners access to free and discounted products and services to help them do business on the Internet. Among the services offered by BizLand.com are free web hosting, free email, free web sites, discounted office supplies, chat rooms, access to industry experts and a community of small business owners with whom to share success stories and do business with. BizLand.com's membership has grown at an average rate of 50 percent per month since its inception. Today the site is home to over 150,000 small business owners from all over the world and nearly every industry.
