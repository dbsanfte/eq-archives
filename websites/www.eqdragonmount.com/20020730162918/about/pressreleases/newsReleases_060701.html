BizLand Inc. Accepts Offline Payments For Online Services

NEEDHAM, MA and BURLINGTON, MA—June 7, 2001

BizLand Inc., a leading provider of online resources to small businesses, is using Western Union® QuickPaySM to accept cash payments offline for their online services. Using Western Union Quick Pay, customers can pay for annual BizLand memberships in their local currency at more than 56,000 Western Union agent locations worldwide. BizLand's membership plans offer Web site hosting, site building tools, domain names as well as other marketing and e-commerce tools for small businesses.

"We were searching for a way our international customers could pay for our services," said BizLand Inc. CEO Steve Sydness. "While e-commerce is global, one major hurdle it faces is that credit cards aren't as prevalent elsewhere in the world as they are in the United States. We've successfully used Western Union QuickPay to clear that hurdle."

Now small business owners around the world who are interested in one of BizLand's three paid memberships and do not have credit cards can pay for an annual membership with cash. Users simply complete the standard purchase process and select Western Union as their payment method. Then the user prints a form and takes it to their local Western Union agent, an agent locator is available online at www.payment-solutions.com. BizLand receives notice of the payment electronically and activates the users account.

"We're very pleased with the results we've seen. BizLand now has members in Zimbabwe, Jordan, Uganda and 85 other countries. It just goes to show what can happen when you open the doors of e-commerce a little bit wider," commented Mr. Sydness.
