BizLand.com Membership Surges to Over 150,000

BURLINGTON, Mass. --November 22, 1999

Small Business Internet Portal More Than Triples its Membership Since July; Endorsed by the Independent Homeworkers Alliance


BizLand, Inc., the leading Internet hub for small business, announced today that its membership has surged to over 150,000 - more than tripling the number of members since the company's launch in July. In the month of October, the site hosted over 750,000 unique visitors.

BizLand.com (http://www.BizLand.com) has also received the endorsement of the Independent Homeworkers Alliance (IHA). IHA is a membership-driven organization dedicated to serving people who work out of their homes or own a small business. IHA has partnered with BizLand.com to bring value-added content and services to IHA and BizLand.com members.

"The small business portal space is becoming increasingly cluttered with companies making promises and providing handfuls of the services that small businesses need to succeed," said Ravi Agarwal, BizLand.com founder and chairman. "BizLand.com offers "no-strings" access to all the basic services a small business needs to start doing business online. The staggering increase in membership since our July launch is testimony to the value of our site. It's a strong vote of confidence from the small business community."

BizLand.com focuses on helping small businesses get even better, offering members free products and services for their online business. BizLand.com's community of members has access to free web hosting, free business email, outbound faxing, customer list management, and discounted products and services. Moreover, BizLand.com creates a community where small business owners can share their success stories, best practices, and cross-sell their products to other small business owners.

"In order for our members to successfully compete in the millennium, it is critical for them to understand how to do business using the Internet and have access to the tools they will need to grow," said Stephen B. St. Louis, executive director and CEO, Independent Homeworkers Alliance. "We are committed to help lead our members into the next century. Our partnership with BizLand.com affirms that commitment and opens the door to the New Economy for our nearly 30,000 members. BizLand.com is a "must have" for IHA members. We endorse BizLand.com as the 'most valuable small business Internet hub' for our members."

The Independent Homeworkers Alliance is a membership driven organization dedicated to serving people who work out of their homes, or own a small business. IHA has members who are self-employed, skilled and unskilled, full and part-time professionals, telecommuters, as well as those who have established a work place at home. The IHA, now with a powerful 50,000 work-from-home jobs database, constantly strives to provide all members with a comprehensive membership benefits program to enhance work and simplify life. Visit their website at http://www.homeworkers.org.

BizLand.com
BizLand.com (http://www.BizLand.com), based in Burlington, MA, is the leading Internet hub for small business. Founded in 1999 by entrepreneur Ravi Agarwal, BizLand.com offers small business owners access to free and discounted products and services to help them do business on the Internet. Among the services offered by BizLand.com are free web hosting, free email, free web sites, discounted office supplies, chat rooms, access to industry experts and a community of small business owners with whom to share success stories and do business with. BizLand.com's membership has grown at an average rate of 50 percent per month since its inception. Today the site is home to over 150,000 small business owners from all over the world and nearly every industry.
