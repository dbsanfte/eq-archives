Mike Caudill Attacks the Spokane Real Estate Market from the Web

"As I surfed and studied the Web sites dealing with real estate marketing, I discovered most sites boasting services for the By-Owner real estate sellers fell short of providing a complete marketing package," says Mike Caudill, creator of www.spokaneseller.bizland.com. "Selling property By-Owner requires a lot more than just placing an ad on the Internet, especially when most of the sites could not be found or ranked by search engines."

Now Caudill maintains one of the most powerful real estate marketing tools in eastern Washington state. The Spokane Seller Web site has become a powerful marketing tool for its clients, local businesses and property owners. "Spokane Seller is the largest eastern Washington multi-media real estate marketing service. We maintain nearly 100 Web sites, place hundreds of newspaper ads and telemarket to the real estate industry," he says.

Caudill didn't always have this level of confidence with the Internet, or computers for that matter. He got his first computer and logged on to the Internet in July 1998. Prior to that he had gone out of his way to avoid computers. Once he got his real estate license, he knew the trip into cyberspace would be unavoidable.

Originally, Caudill simply wanted to create a Web site that would give his residential listing around Spokane County some exposure to the Internet market. "At first it was very intimidating," he says. "I knew absolutely nothing about the Internet or Web site construction. I spent weeks struggling with each different service trying to find, not only the easiest site to use, but a company that would promote my Web site effectively after I was done."


In BizLand, Caudill found what he was looking for. "I needed a lot of help when I first started and I always got quick responses when I e-mailed or called," he says. "BizLand offers one of the most complete business marketing systems I have found on the Internet. From the Web site construction services to the affiliate partnership program, they're all excellent." He has also used Directed Traffic, search engine submission and site-metering reports to improve his online presence.

"Six months after I started creating Web sites I closed my real estate business," he says. "My Internet business had literally taken over. Now, instead of working with real estate buyers and sellers, I am working with all the companies that work with the buyers and sellers."
