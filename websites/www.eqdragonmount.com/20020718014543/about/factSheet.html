BizLand, Inc. (www.bizland.com), is a leading provider of online resources to small businesses and a leading vendor of private-labeled Web sites to large enterprises. Serving more than one million small business members, BizLand offers a complete array of Web-based services ranging from e-commerce storefronts to online promotional tools. BizLand also cultivates a strong small business community through its community message boards and its advice and articles from industry experts. BizLand's private label solution, "Powered by BizLand," combines BizLand's considerable small business expertise with the customers' products or services to produce a customized, small business Web Site. Powered by BizLand customers include Xerox, Inc. and 3Com, Inc.

Powered by BizLand: A Private Label Version of BizLand.com
The Powered by BizLand program allows companies to capitalize on BizLand's small business expertise and still retain their own brand identity. By maintaining its own look and feel, the partner offers its customers a seamless experience. The small business owner benefits by receiving a much more robust selection of products and services; yet, BizLand's involvement is transparent to the end user. Current partners have also found that it is much faster to customize BizLand's suite of tools and services to their needs than to build these capabilities themselves. BizLand shares both the investment and the reward with its partners.

Strategic Partnerships
Partnerships with more than 30 industry leaders provide unmatched pricing and service agreements to BizLand's more than one million members. Members can quickly and securely build and promote Web sites, open storefronts, develop catalogs, offer shopping carts, accept credit cards over the Internet and much more.

BizLand partners include:
  • Open Market - provider of ShopSite, an easy-to-use E-commerce store creation system that allows member merchants to build and maintain catalogs of products or services to sell over the Internet
  • VeriSign - provider of PayFlow, a system that enables immediate and secure online transactions and payment processing
  • Trellix - provider of a private label version of Trellix Web Express, an easy-to-use Web site building tool
  • WebEx - provider of Online Meeting Center, allowing members to hold meetings in real time via the Internet
