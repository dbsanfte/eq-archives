January 11, 2002 - hmm, well it's been a while since things have been updated. The holidays, leveling and just plain playing let alone RL take up some time. We were able to register the domain and Tam has sent out an email to everyone. Read below:

Greetings All. We are pleased to announce that each member of the CCOL has their own guild email address. Please follow the steps below to get the email address (if you have received an email already then please disregard, this is for new recruits and those without an email so far):

STEP 1
======

Send an email to tamazinis@crimsoncircleoflight.com with the following information.
Your online character name. This will be used for your email address : charactername@crimsoncircleoflight.com
The password you desire to use for this email address. NOTE: DO NOT send extremely personal words/numbers etc. as your password because since we are administering the email, we are obviously seeing the password during setup. DO use a password that you will remember.

STEP 2
======
Wait for an email from Tamazinis or Feenyks with Full instructions on how to use your new Guild email address. While you are waiting, please ensure you have the program : "Outlook Express" on your computer. This program comes free with ALL windows based computers like : Windows '98, Windows 2000, Windows XP etc.

STEP 3
======
Lets communicate and grow our family

*hugs*

Tamazinis
