This list is for reference only. We felt like there should be a list of EQ characters that guild members of CoN have noted to be less than honest, ninja looters, trouble makers or not worth the effort to group with. If you have anyone to add to this list please send the info via the contact page.

Ninja Looters
Ninja Looters are defined as those characters that loot during battle, most often before the slain NPC touches the ground. They do not allow others to loot and when asked to share they won't.
