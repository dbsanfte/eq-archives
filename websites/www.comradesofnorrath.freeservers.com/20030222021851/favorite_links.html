Favorite Links:

Welcome to the links page. If you have any other links you would like added to this list please contact me by e-mail at the address on the contact page.

Allakhazam's Magic Realm
- Is an excellent resource has info or links to help with most topics.
http://everquest.allakhazam.com
EQ Atlas
- EQ Atlas is a good source for maps that have the location grid included.
http://www.eqatlas.com/
EQ Caster's Realm
- EQ Casters Realm a good place to go when creating a caster and their spell list for each class is updated frequently. Also check out the mana calculator.
http://eq.castersrealm.com
Illia's Everquest Bestiary
- Illia's Everquest Bestiary a good place to go before you head to a new zone. Allows you to see what faction hits you might take and what monsters you might face.
http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com
EQ Prices
- EQ Prices is a great way to find out just how much that item you just looted is worth.
http://www.eqprices.com
Experience Table
- This is a great way to see just how much experience is needed for each level. It is broken down by class and race.
http://www.geocities.com/sstair/
The Mage Compendium
- Fanstastic site for all caster classes but, mages in particular. Download section is well worth checking out and the mana calculator is excellent.
http://www.magecompendium.com
ECU
- EQU is a client editor (lets you change text colors and other options in EQ) and has a server status program. It also has a feature that allows you to see how much damage you, your group and the mobs dole out and a screen shot viewer. Very nice program.
http://members.phoenixdsl.com/~qbe/EQU
EQs Best Link Site
A site that has links to all classes, maps, trade skills and general info sites
http://bleidich.tripod.com/everquest/eqlinks/index.html
