Shadowknight Spell List

Level 9

Invisibility Vs. Undead
MANA: 40 SKILL: Divination
A very useful spell. It allows you to pass undead creatures undetected. Be warned though that it won't work against other types of monsters.

Locate Corpse
MANA: 5 SKILL: Divination
No hybrid should be without this! It points to the nearest corpse. Useful for any corpse retreval where you know the approx whereabouts of the body.

Disease Cloud
MANA: 10 SKILL: Conjuration
This spell will make your opponents regeneration capabilities non-existant. It also does a small amount of damage.

Lifetap
MANA: +9 SKILL: Alteration
This is the first Life draining spell you recieve and the weakest. It's still good for combat though

Leering Corpse
MANA: 40 SKILL: Conjuration
Your first pet. Very VERY weak, but keeps your enemy distracted nonetheless.

Sense the Dead
MANA: 5 SKILL: Divination
This spell points to the nearest undead creature.

Siphon Strength
MANA: 5 SKILL: Alteration
This spell allows you to suck the strength out of your opponent and add it to your own for a while.

Level 15

Clinging Darkness
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
This spell is a pretty good DoT spell that also lowers the speed of the target

Bone Walk
MANA: 80 SKILL: Conjuration
This spell summons a significantly stronger pet.

Lifespike
MANA: +18 SKILL: Alteration
This is the second in line for the draining spells. It is significantly more powerful than the last.

Endure cold
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell increases the targets resistance to cold based magic.

Fear
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
This spell allows you to incite fear into your target. Making them run for cover and thus buying you some time.

Shadow Step
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
A very useful spell, it allows you to teleport over small distances in the same zone. It is a great spell to get a head start in escaping an enemies wrath.

Numb the Dead
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
This spell calms undead creatures so that they will not attack people they would usually attack.

Level 22

Deadeye
MANA: 35 SKILL: Divination
This spell gives the target infravision and the ability to see invisible creatures. Especially useful for human based shadowknights.

Dark Empathy
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
This is the closest you'll ever get to having a healing spell. This transfers your hit points to the chosen target.

Convoke Shadow
MANA: 120 SKILL: Conjuration
Yet another spell that summons a skeletal pet. Once again this one is significantly stronger than the last.

Spook the Dead
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
This spell is exactly like the level 9 paladin spell. It incites fear into the heart of undead creatures. Very useful if you bit off more than you can chew in a battle.

Engulfing Darkness
MANA: 60 SKILL: Conjuration
The upgrade to the clinging darkness spell, this DoT does more damage than its predecessor.

Ward Undead
MANA: 30 SKILL: Evocation
This is a Direct Damage spell that is undead specific, it has a good mana:damage ratio.

Vampiric Embrace
MANA: 30 SKILL: Alteration
Casting this spell allows you to randomly suck the life out of your opponent for a set amount of time. It is a good spell as it allows you to regenerate somewhat during combat.

Level 30

Gather Shadows
MANA: 35 SKILL: Divination
Since your evil, you are limited to where you can travel. With this spell, however, that is only a past time. This spell allows you to become invisible and to travel to places you were unable to goto before.

Endure Disease
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
A good spell that increases the targets resistance to disease

Feign Death
MANA: 60 SKILL: Abjuration
Exactly as it implies. This spell allows you to put on a show to your opponent to make him think your dead. If it works, he will leave you alone and you'll have time to regenerate. If it doesn't, you always have the option of running :).

Lifedraw
MANA: +63 SKILL: Alteration
This is the most powerful draining spell you recieve. It replaced voice graft (a useless spell) so you should be thankful :).

Restless Bones
MANA: 160 SKILL: Conjuration
..and speaking of pets. This spell allows you to have an even stronger pet than before.

Heat Blood
MANA: 72 SKILL: Alteration
Another DoT, except this one is stackable with your previous DoTs. Allow you to make the enemies health drop even more than before.

Wave of Enfeeblement
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
A Nasty spell that weakens multiple close targets. Great for occasions where a group member is outnumbered.

Level 39

Animate Dead
MANA: 200 SKILL: Conjuration
Lets you summon a stronger pet than your previous pet summoning spells.

Heart Flutter
MANA: 80 SKILL: Alteration
A Wonderful DoT spell that also lowers targets STR and AC.

Cancel Magic
MANA: 30 SKILL: Abjuration
A very useful spell. It negates one spell effect cast on you. Very useful against powerful DoTs or Debuffs.

Expulse Undead
MANA: 60 SKILL: Evocation
This spell practically rips through undead opponents at a nice mana cost

Resist Cold
MANA: 50 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell increases your resistance to Cold based magic attacks

Shield Skin
MANA: 41 SKILL: Abjuration
A spell which grants you temporarily invincibility. A very limited amount of damage is absorbed by this shield, after that you will resume taking hits like normal. This is the only buff the shadowknight recieves.

Shadow Vortex
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
This spell debuffs your targets AC significantly.

Level 49

Dooming Darkness
MANA: 120 SKILL: Conjuration
A STRONG DoT spell that is an upgrade from engulfing darkness. A great opening spell for fighting monsters.

Dismiss Undead
MANA: 90 SKILL: Evocation
This spell is your strongest DD spell against undead. It features a superior damage:mana ratio and the best amount of damage out of the three.

Breath of the Dead
MANA: 45 SKILL: Alteration
No, this spell does not make your breath stink. It allows you to breath underwater temporarily.

Shadow Sight
MANA: 50 SKILL: Divination
A nice spell that gives the cast ultravision. (Both infravision and ability to see invisible characters)

Invoke Fear
MANA: 100 SKILL: Alteration
A strong fear spell that works better and longer than your previous fear spells.

Word of Spirit
MANA: 133 SKILL: Evocation
This is a great spell if you have multiple targets around you. It is an area effect spell that centers around you, the caster.

Summon Dead
MANA: 290 SKILL: Conjuration
Your final reward for all your hard work. This spell summons your strongest pet that is approximately level 26-29.
