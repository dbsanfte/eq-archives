Ranger Spell List

Level 9

Minor Healing
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
The weakest of heals you can recieve. Although it isn't entirely useful for in-combat healing, it makes a great spell for downtime.

Skin Like Wood
MANA: 10 SKILL: Abjuration
The first AC and HP buff a ranger recieves. The reciever of this spell recieves a modest boost in AC and hp.

Flame Lick
MANA: 10 SKILL: Evocation
Definetly not the strongest offensive spell you'll ever recieve. This spell eats away at a small portion of your opponents hp and causes a minimal decrease in AC.

Glimpse
MANA: 5 SKILL: Divination
Surprisingly this is a spell druids can't use. It increases the users range of sight, and is an excellent spell for scouting dangerous areas.

Endure Fire
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
A good buffing spell. This spell increases the users resistance to fire.

Lull Animal
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
This spell decreases the aggressiveness of the target animal, allowing for others to pass the creature unharmed.

Snare
MANA: 15 SKILL: Alteration
A *VERY* useful spell. This decreases the speed of your opponent greatly. Great for monsters already slow (it'll bring them to a halt). This spell is also known for lasting a good amount of time. Very dependable.


 Level 15

Dance of the Fireflies
MANA: 10 SKILL: Conjuration
A spell often missing from the spellbooks of many rangers. This spell summons a NODROP light source. Although the lightsource may not be entirely useful. The spell allows for a ranger to build up his conjuring for his first DoT spell 'stinging swarm'

Camoflauge
MANA: 15 SKILL: Divination
This nice little spell allows for the user to become invisible. It does have it's limitations though... It can only be casted in an outdoor zone.

Cure Poison
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
A spell that is sure to save your life somewhere along the line. This spell eliminates the effects of poison on the chosen target. Be warned that it will take multiple castings for stronger poisons.

Feet Like Cat
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
A suitable buff for the ranger. This will temporarily increase your agility.

Burst of Fire
MANA: 15 SKILL: Evocation
Your first *REAL* direct damage spell. It doesn't cause much damage in comparison to normal fighting techniques; but it makes a great opening attack.

Invoke Lightning
MANA: 32 SKILL: Evocation
This spell is great for multiple targets that are close to one another. This spell will hit the target and all close by enemies. It causes a good amount of damage as well. Just be careful that you don't annoy the wrong monster in the process.

Thistle Coat
MANA: 25 SKILL: Abjuration
Your first damage shield. It causes a very small amount of damage; but as an added bonus it increases your AC.


 Level 22

Ignite
MANA: 30 SKILL: Evocation
Ignite is the second DD spell the ranger recieves, it features a slightly better damage:mana ratio.

Light Healing
MANA: 25 SKILL: Alteration
This is the second healing spell a ranger recieves. It is more mana efficient and a welcome replacement to minor healing.

Harmony
MANA: 25 SKILL: Abjuration
A nice alternative to lull animal. This spell is an area effect spell which reduce the aggressiveness of a group of monsters. It is also castable on anything, not just animals.

Enduring Breath
MANA: 35 SKILL: Alteration
This spell allows for the user to breathe underwater for a good amount of time. Useful for water exploration.

Bind Sight
MANA: 15 SKILL: Divination
This allows the ranger to see from the targets perspective. A neat trick; but not much else.

Ward Summoned
MANA: 30 SKILL: Evocation
This spell works only on summoned monsters. It causes a good amount of damage on these types of creatures and is more mana efficient than your other damage causing spells.

Skin Like Rock
MANA: 60 SKILL: Abjuration
Skin like Rock is an upgraded spell from the previous buff Skin Like Wood. It gives your more AC and more hp than its predecessor.


 Level 30

Cancel Magic
MANA: 30 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell is very useful. If you ever get any undesirable spell cast upon you or someone else, this spell has a chance of cancelling it.

Barb Coat
MANA: 50 SKILL: Abjuration
The second damage shield the ranger recieves, it features a minor increase in both damage and AC.

Shield of Thistles
MANA: 40 SKILL: Abjuration
This is a pure damage shield spell. It's effect is of a much shorter duration than the 'coat' type spells; but the damage shield itself is *much* better in combat.

Invigor
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
This is the perfect spell for a long swim or an extended battle. It will restore the targets stamina.

Eyes of the Cat
MANA: 35 SKILL: Divination
An excellent spell for human type rangers. It will temporarily give the player infravision.

Strength of Earth
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
The target of this spell will experience a temporary increase in his/her strength.

Stinging Swarm
MANA: 65 SKILL: Conjuration
Your first DoT spell, it will cause your opponents hp to ebb away for a set amount of time. It's effects will cancel if you turn tail and run though.


 Level 39

Careless Lightning
MANA: 70 SKILL: Evocation
This is your best DD spell in the game. It causes about 100 hp of damage per casting. A great opening attack.

Calm Animal
MANA: 45 SKILL: Alteration
More effective than the earlier Lull Animal, this spell lowers the aggressiveness of the target animal.

Healing
MANA: 60 SKILL: Alteration
The last healing type magic you will recieve in the game. It has a better mana:healing ratio then the previous two healing spells in the game.

Levitate
MANA: 30 SKILL: Alteration
A fun spell at the worst, a practical spell at it's best. This spell can be used to easily cross dangerous terrain or just to float unexpectedly on to someone's head.

Spirit of Wolf
MANA: 40 SKILL: Alteration
Chances are you've been waiting for this one. This spell features an increase in speed to the target of the spell. Meaning you can now run away from some enemies easily. It is also a great spell for corpse retrevals.

Skin Like Steel
MANA: 100 SKILL: Abjuration
Another AC and HP buff. This one adds more than both of the previous two buffing spells.

Dismiss Summoned
MANA: 90 SKILL: Evocation
The last 'Summoned-only' DD spell that the ranger can use and does a pretty good amount of damage for the mana consumed when cast.


 Level 49

Ensaring Roots
MANA: 60 SKILL: Alteration
A decent root spell that also does direct damage to the target.

Resist Fire
MANA: 50 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell is an upgrade to the earlier Endure Fire. It gives the target a better resistance to fire.

Immolate
MANA: 120 SKILL: Evocation
This is the best DoT a ranger recieves. But as an added bonus it also decreases the monsters AC level.

Bramble Coat
MANA: 75 SKILL: Abjuration
This is the last in the 'Coat' line for the ranger. It has a slightly better damage shield effect and AC bonus.

Shield of Brambles
MANA: 75 SKILL: Abjuration
This is the absolute *BEST* damage shield a ranger recieves. Like other damage shields, however, it has a low lasting time.

Wolf Form
MANA: 60 SKILL: Alteration
This is the spell you've been working for all this time. You actually turn into a wolf and recieve most of the benefits/setbacks that a wolf would recieve. Your speed increases as well as your attack rating; however you may be attacked by certain NPC's that lack an appreciation for wolves.

Superior Camoflauge
MANA: 40 SKILL: Divination
Exactly the same effect as Camoflauge; but this spell doesn't limit you to where you can cast it.
