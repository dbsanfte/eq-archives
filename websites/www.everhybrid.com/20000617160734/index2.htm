Stuff, just...Stuff!
June 16th, 2000 6:45pm  Baldemar

It's been awhile since my last update; but I do have some news for the Rangers out there. Erikas Lightarrow (formerly known as Erikas Shadowstalker) sent me a nice guide on how to play a ranger from levels 1 to 20. You can view it here or under the Ranger Guide section of the site. It's nicely done, so anyone interested should check it out. Thanks Erikas!

Also, if anyone is tired of spending too much time searching for various tidbits of information, then you should check out the Equest search engine. The link can be found amongst the "other links" section of the site. They also feature some nice contests, so anyone wishing to make some extra plat (oh, how much you ask? 1000pp to be exact) should certainly pay their site a visit!

Also, there is a new weapon for Rangers listed in the Ranger section (The Icy Blade). It's listed in the weapons section. Thanks to Shebakhan Shadowstalker for the submission to the site.
