Bard Strategies


Kiting, Bard Style

Requires:
  • Selo's Accelerando
  • Brusco's Boastful Bellow (CoD if you're careful.)
  • Skill in Juggling Songs
Recommened:
  • Spirit of the Wolf (as a nice alternative to Selo.)
  • An Escape Route.

Strange, it seems everyone has their own way of kiting monsters. Even magicians have their own little technique (but let's not get into that now). It seems only fair that Bards get this too. I happened to be on the tribunal server as my ranger character while watching a bard use this technique. Enjoy :).

First thing you have to do is to be able to "juggle" songs. What I mean by this is starting a song, and when it comes into effect, switch quickly into another song. Upon success you'll have two bard song effects instead of just one.

Second thing you have to do is to find a mob that isn't gonna call for help or be assisted by other mobs. Make sure the mob isn't grossly out of your league (forget about the high reds, i heard they can resist this tactic). You also have to be in a reasonably open area which is free (or almost free) of extremely high lvl aggro mobs. This is to ensure that, while you kite, your not going to jump head first into a nasty giant/griffin situation.

When you find a mob, target it and use your Bellow song, quickly change to Selo once you hit it. Now RUN away from the monster and juggle Selo and Bellow, this should slowly decrease the monsters health. Certainly not the quickest method of kiting. But by far, one of the most efficient (no mana, ideally little hp loss). Use this tactic when there are no groups available to group with. A good group can get you xp faster than this tactic ever could. Happy gaming!
