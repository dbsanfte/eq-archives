Paladin Spells

Level 9

Courage
MANA: 12 SKILL: Abjuration
This is the first buff that the paladin recieves. It gives the target a nice bonus in AC and HP.

Flash of Light
MANA: 12 SKILL: Divination
A very interesting spell. Cast it on the enemy to temporarily blind them. If it is an NPC your casting on they will flee if the spell holds. The real beauty of this spell is that if it's a PC your casting on, they won't be able to see a thing.

Cure Poison
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
A good spell to keep loaded when fighting spiders and such. This spell negates poison effects on the target. Keep in mind that multiple casts may be needed to cure stronger versions of poison

Yaulp
MANA: 5 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell temporarily increases the STR and AC of the caster. It doesn't last for very long, but it has a very cheap mana cost compared to other spells.

Minor healing
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
This is the first of four healing spells you recieve. It's only practical function in the game is to reduce the downtime. Although if you are in a fix against a weaker enemy, it might be worth casting this a few times during the fight.

Sense Magic
MANA: 5 SKILL: Divination
This spell is absolutely useless. It gives you the stats of any given item. Unfortunately, left clicking on an item will allow you to see the stats anyways.

Spook The Dead
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
An alternative to flash of light. This spell causes undead creatures to run off screaming for their mothers. It is especially useful in a fix where you need some time to heal.

Level 15

Hammer of Wrath
MANA: 50 SKILL: Conjuration
Have you been practicing your 1h blunt? If so this spell could be useful to you. It summons a magical weapon (6 Dam 32 Delay) that has does decent damage to your enemy. The real good thing is that it does damage to magical creatures, but not much else :).

Holy Armor
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell comes from a different line of spells withing the cleric class. It adds an AC bonus to the target, stack this with a courage buff and your set.

Cure Disease
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
A very useful spell indeed. This spell allows the caster to rid himself of any type of disease. A definite bonus considering undead creatures like mummies often have a history of infecting their attacker during battle.

Lull
MANA: 10 SKILL: Alteration
This spell lowers the aggressiveness of your opponent. It is not guaranteed to work but useful if you need to get by an aggressive mob.

Sense The Dead
MANA: 5 SKILL: Divination
The undead can surely run, but they cannot hide if you have this spell in your possession. It points you into the direction of the nearest undead enemy.

Light Healing
MANA: 25 SKILL: Alteration
This is the second of four healing spells you recieve. It heals significantly more damage than minor healing and thus is more practical in a battle situation.

Ward Undead
MANA: 30 SKILL: Evocation
This is your first undead specific direct damage spell. It features a decent damage amount for the amount of mana spent. Great for areas like Befallen.

Level 22

Endure Poison
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
A very useful spell for avoiding poison altogether. It increases your ability to resist poison effects.

Invisibility to Undead
MANA: 40 SKILL: Divination
Is there a ghoul hanging out near your corpse? Do you need to run past that mummy to get to your group? Invisibilty vs. undead hides you from the many undead creatures in norrath. A very useful spell.

Invigor
MANA: 20 SKILL: Alteration
By now fights are taking considerably longer to complete. This spell replenishes depleted stamina points.

Root
MANA: 30 SKILL: Alteration
This spell allows you to stop a monster in its tracks. Very useful for soloing: Just root, stand back, heal, and resume fighting.

Reckless Strength
MANA: 30 SKILL: Alteration
This spell gives you a sudden burst of strength which, over time, slowly ebbs away.

Center
MANA: 40 SKILL: Abjuration
The upgrade to Courage. This spell features more AC and HP than it's predecessor.

Halo of Light
MANA: 40 SKILL: Conjuration
This spell summons a no drop light source that is wearable on the head. Pretty handy for human type paladins, otherwise it's useless.

Level 30

Healing
MANA: 60 SKILL: Alteration
The third of four healing spells. This spell features even more HP per mana spent. At this time your healing is getting very effective in battle... Use it!

Hammer of Striking
MANA: 100 SKILL: Conjuration
A better magical weapon than the hammer of Wrath. However, at this point in time it is practically useless.

Expulse Undead
MANA: 60 SKILL: Evocation
Send undead baddies writhing in agony with this spell. It is an upgrade to the Ward Undead spell recieved at lvl 15.

Stun
MANA: 35 SKILL: Evocation
This spell is pretty useful. It stuns your opponent for a short period of time

Spirit Armor
MANA: 75 SKILL: Abjuration
An upgrade to the Holy Armor spell you recieved earlier. Like the last spell, it increases the AC amount of your character temporarily.

Soothe
MANA: 30 SKILL: Alteration
More effective than Lull. This spell reduces the aggressiveness of your opponent, and is useful for slipping by nasty areas or for pulling moba.

Symbol of Transal
MANA: 55 SKILL: Abjuration
Yet another line of buffs you recieve, it increases the HP of the target by 100. The power of this spell is significant, as it is considerably more potent than all your previous buffing spells. However, this spell requires a component to cast (Cat's Eye Agate).

Level 39

Daring
MANA: 60 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell is an upgrade to the Courage/Center line of spells. Once again it features an even higher increase in AC and HP.

Greater Heal
MANA: 150 SKILL: Alteration
The final healing spell you will recieve in the game. It heals a massive amount of HP at a nice mana cost. With this spell your now a better healer than any other class in the game (other than the Cleric of course).

Cancel Magic
MANA: 30 SKILL: Abjuration
A very useful spell. It negates one spell effect cast on you. Very useful against powerful DoTs or Debuffs.

Endure Disease
MANA: 20 SKILL: Abjuration
This spell increases the targets resistance to disease.

Yaulp 2
MANA: 15 SKILL: Abjuration
An upgrade to the yaulp spell. This spell has a nice mana cost and increases your STR and AC even more for a short time.

Counteract Poison
MANA: 50 SKILL: Alteration
Finally! A stronger version of the spell Cure Poison. Be sure to have this on hand if your fighting monsters who use strong types of poison.

Symbol of Ryltan
MANA: 111 SKILL: Abjuration
The next spell in the symbol line for the Paladin. This greatly increaes the Hp of the target (by 150); but also requires a component to cast (Bloodstone).

Level 49

Calm
MANA: 50 SKILL: Alteration
This is an upgrade to the Lull/Soothe line of spells. It is the most powerful of the bunch, you should note though that this spell takes significantly more mana to cast. You should note when to use this spell and when to use less costly spells such as Lull and Soothe.

Valor
MANA: 90 SKILL: Abjuration
This is a nice step up from Daring. It is the last in line for the Paladin and features superior buffing capabilities in both AC and HP.

Dismiss Undead
MANA: 90 SKILL: Evocation
Leave a crater where the ghoul used to be. This spell is by far the most powerful undead DD spell you recieve in the game.

Holy Might
MANA: 60 SKILL: Evocation
This spell is an upgrade to Stun. It inflicts damage to the target and stuns them for a short time.

Guard
MANA: 80 SKILL: Abjuration
It was previously believed that this spell was the beginning of another line of spells. While it is stackable with the courage line of spells, it is not stackable with the Spirit Armor line of spells. Asides from that though, this spell features a nice increase in AC to the target.

Symbol of Pinzam
MANA: 200 SKILL: Abjuration
Adds *ALOT* (200!) of Hp to the target for a limited time. It requires a component to cast though (Jasper).

Revive
MANA: 300 SKILL: Alteration
..and finally, for all your hard work this is a GREAT reward. This spell allows you to ressurect fallen friends, they'll thank you for it, you'll be glad you did it and you might just make some friends in the process :). However, unlike the later cleric ressurect spells, this spell doesn't give any experience back to the person who died.
