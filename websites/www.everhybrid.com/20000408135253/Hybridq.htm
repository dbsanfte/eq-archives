Call of Flame (lvl 50 Ranger Only Spell)

So your ready to get your class spell eh? Get ready for a long and extremely involved quest with some serious camping involved. Lily and Devon Ashwood in Kelethin give out this quest. Here are the steps involved in initiating the quest.

1. Hail Lily and ask her about Ganelorn Oast, the ranger she is in love with.
2. Talk to Devon and tell him [I want to learn from him, how do I prove myself as a forest?]
3. Get the letter he mentions in his respond by Hailing Lily and saying [I will deliver the mail to him]
4. Deliver the message to Ganelorn in the East Karanas (he can be found near the Barbarian Fishing Village)
5. Give the egg you recieve to Kithicor of Kithicor Forest.
6. Give the Medal of Merit you recieve to Ganelorn... Whew.

Now comes the real tough part.. (heh, you were just running errands, time for the real sacrifice)

Ganelorn now requires four items from you...
1. A gift from Kaladim (give the note you recieve from him to Aanina Rockfinder. She's in the armor shop BTW)
2. An adamantine band (off the hand in lower guk, it's a rare drop)
3. A burnt sash (off of the imp protectors in Sol B)
4. ... A electrum bladed wakizashi (no joke ppl...)

All of this work makes a poacher named Vance spawn. He spawns considerably far away from Ganelorn so your going to need all your ranger tracking skills to find him. Bring with you a full group of strong players. Vance is tough, he cons red to a level 50, so he's up there among the tougher creatures in the game. When you finally kill him, turn the head he drops to Ganelorn and claim your CoF scroll... Good luck!
