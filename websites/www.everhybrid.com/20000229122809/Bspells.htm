 Bard Song List

Level 1

Chant of Battle:
This song features an increase in STR, DEX and AC for the entire party.

 Level 2

Chords of Dissonance:
This is your first Area Effect DoT. Make sure your not using it near any guards or extremely powerful mobs.

 Level 3

Jaxan's Jig o' Vigor:
This song increases the regen rate of the party's stamina. Great for long fights.

 Level 4

Lyssa's Locating Lyric:
This song locates the targets corpse, if any.

Level 5

Selo's Accelerando:
This song increases the movement speed of the entire group. With a drum, the speed effects exceed even those of spirit of cheetah. A must have!

Top

Level 6

Hymn of Restoration:
This song increases the regeneration rate of the entire party.

 Level 7

Jonathan's Whistling Warsong:
This spell only effects the bard. It increases STR, AC and attack speed.

 Level 8

Kelin's Lugubrious Lament:
This lulls the target creature as long as the song is being played.

 Level 9

Elemental Rhythms:
A useful song to go against spell casters. It increases the party's magic, heat and cold resistance.

 Level 10

Anthem de Arms:
This increases the party's strength and, to a degree, their attack speed.

Top

Level 11

Cinda's Charismatic Carillon:
This increases the targets faction temporarily with NPC's in the zone.

 Level 12

Brusco's Boastful Bellow:
This spell causes DD damage to the target. It takes awhile to recharge, so use it in emergencies only.

 Level 13

Purifying Rhythms:
This spell is good to use against impure monsters. It increases the poison, disease and magic resistance of the party.

 Level 14

Lyssa's Cataloging Libretto:
Pretty useless, this song is used to identify items.

 Level 15

Kelin's Lucid Lullaby:
This song puts the mob into a trance, removing the party from the mob's "hate list". Very useful in tight situations.

Top

Level 16

Tarew's Aquatic Ayre:
This song grants the party the ablility to breathe underwater.

 Level 17

Guardian Rhythms:
This spell increases the party's AC and Magic resistance.

 Level 18

Denon's Disruptive Discord:
This song is an area effect DoT song that lowers both the affected mobs AC and attack speed.

 Level 19

Shauri's Sonorous Clouding:
This song grants the party invisibility.

Level 20

Largo's Melodic Binding:
This is an area effect song that decreases the effected mobs AC and attack speed.

Top

Level 21

Melanie's Mellifluous Motion:
This song, much like Shadow Step, causes the party to randomly teleport within the same zone.

 Level 22

Alenia's Disenchanting Melody:
Currently not working, this song was supposed to cancel magic within the party.

 Level 23

Selo's Consonant Chain:
This song causes a reduction in the target's attack and movement speed.

 Level 24

Lyssa's Veracious Concord:
This song grants ultravision (See invis and infravision) to the party.

 Level 25

Psalm of Warmth:
This powerful song grants addition AC and cold resistance as well as a fire shield to the entire party.

Top

Level 26

Angstlich's Appalling Screech:
This song causes enemies within a certain range of the bard to run in fear.

 Level 27

Solon's Song of the Sirens:
This song charms the target monster for as long as the song is played. Very nice. Doesn't work with high level mobs though.

Level 28

Crission's Pixie Chase:
This song mesmerizes and stuns the target.

 Level 29

Psalm of Vitality:
This song grants the party increase AC and Disease Resistance.

 Level 30

Fufil's Curtailing Chant:
This DoT song lowers the magic resistance of the target.

Top

Level 31

Agilmente's Aria of Eagles:
This song grants the party levitation.

 Level 32

Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity:
A GREAT song for groups. This song increases the mana regeneration of the entire party.

 Level 33

Psalm of Cooling:
This song gives the each member of the party a cold based damage shield. In addition it also grants ultravision, heat resistance and additional AC.

 Level 34

Lyssa's Solidarity of Vision:
This song allows you to bind sight to the target creature.

 Level 35

Denon's Dissension:
When playing this song, all nearby monsters begin to slowly have their mana drained.

Top

Level 36

Vilia's Verses of Celerity:
This increases the party's AGI and attack speed.

 Level 37

Psalm of Purity:
This song increases the party's AC and poison resistance.

 Level 38

Tuyen's Chant of Flame:
This song lowers the target's heat resistance while inflicting a heat-based DoT.

 Level 40

Syvelian's Anti-Magic Aria:
This song cancels magic on the selected target.

Top

Level 41

Psalm of Mystic Shielding:
As it implies, this song increases the party's magic resistance.

 Level 42

McVaxius Berserker Crescendo:
In addition to increasing the party's attack speed, this song also grants increase strength.

 Level 43

Denon's Desperate Dirge:
When in a difficult fight, always have this song available. It unleashes an area effect DD spell for more than 300 damage to every mob that gets hit.

 Level 44

Cassindra's Elegy:
A nice alternative to the rest of the songs. This song increases the INT and WIS of the party.

 Level 46

Tuyen's Chant of Frost:
Exactly like Tuyen's Chant of Flame, except the damage shield is cold based and the targets cold resistance is lowered.

 Level 48

Selo's Chords of Cessation:
This powerful song lowers the attack speed of every mob within it's range. In addition it inflicts DoT and reduces attack speed.

 Level 50

Verses of Victory:
Your reward for reaching level 50! This song greatly increases STR, AGI and attack speed.
