We've got lots of articles on starting the game, the game's interface, creating your character, memorizing your starting spells, using your spells, a list of the most common commands you'll need to learn to survive in the game and lots more! There's nothing that veteran players dislike more than whining newbies who haven't done their research and homework about the game before they jumped in and started to play. Being a respected, great player takes a lot of dedication to learn everything you can about how the game works. And there's plenty of resources out there for you. Learn the game BEFORE you get started, be self-sufficient in-game and conduct yourself and your character like a pro!

Before You Start The Game
1.  Does Your Computer Have What It Takes to Play EverQuest? To make sure you've got a system that's powerful enough to run this game, click on this link and read Section I carefully, and then familiarize yourself with the remaining information. 
2.  Buy the Game  Here's a link where you can buy the game.
3.  Buy the Player's Guide  This book is full of information that's valuable for the beginning player.
4.  How much does it cost to play? This site details the cost to play EverQuest, and the accepted methods of payment.
5.  Install the Game  Here's a page from the EverQuest Official Web Site which gives you information on installing EverQuest.
6.  What are Classes? Many people new to Roleplaying Games are unfamiliar with Classes. Check out this site for more information. You'll need to understand this before creating your first character.
7.  Choosing the Best Class for Your Playing Style  When you play, do you want to be the one who smashes and bashes the monsters or do you want to kill them with finesse? Or do you prefer healing your teammates or providing buffing spells? This article will help you understand how to choose the best class based on your playing style.
8.  What are Races? Along with Classes, choosing your character's race is a very important decision. This web site explains the various races available to you when you create your character.
9.  What are Character Stats? Why are they important? After clicking on the link, scroll down the page till you find the section called "Stats", there you'll find a good explanation of character stats and what stats are important for particular classes.
10.  What are the Base Stats for the Character Race/Class Combinations? How Many Bonus Points Do I Get to Boost My Stats?  This site gives you handy reference charts on the base stats for each class. 
11.  What is a Deity ?  The choice of Deities is another important decision you'll have to make when creating your character. Learn about the Deities,  and how they can affect your faction standing with NPC's (non-player characters, like shopkeepers and guards).
12.  What Deities Are Available for My Chosen Class/Race Combination?  The chart at this site is a handy reference, showing which Deities are available for the particular class/race you are considering for your character.
13.  What are Factions? This is a nice article explaining the theory of Factions, and how different factions will affect your character in the game.

 

Now That You've Read About and Understand the Basics (above), It's Time to Crank  Up The Game For The First Time
1.  Does it Run Right? If not, check out this troubleshooting page and read Sections II, III, IV and V for possible help.
2.  Once you've got the game running, you'll need to download all the patches, upgrades and bug fixes that have been implemented since your game CD left the factory. This is an automatic procedure when you start up the game, but bear in mind, this could take some time to complete once you've started the game for the first time.
3.  Creating Your EverQuest Account  Read this page to find out how to create your billing account.
4.  EverQuest Rules of Conduct  Before you create your first Character and enter the game for the first time, you need to read and understand these rules of conduct for playing the game.

 
5.  Character Naming Policy  Again, you need to read this and understand it BEFORE trying to create your character. Otherwise, you may not know why the name you've chosen may be rejected by the game.
6.  Choose a server to play on. Basically there are 3 considerations when choosing a server: 1. Do you want to PK (player kill)? If so, choose one of the PK servers, because there are very few PK'ers on the regular servers. 2. Where are your friends playing? If you already have friends playing EQ, you need to choose the same server that they are using, otherwise you won't be able to play with them. 3. If neither of the first two considerations matter, then the final consideration is how many people are already on the server? The most crowded servers are the hardest to play on because there's only so many monsters to go around. 
7.  Creating Your First Character  Here's a guide on the mechanics of creating your first game character. It also includes a primer on setting up your hot keys, what to do with your beginning inventory items, how to start training at your class Guild, some info about Trade Skills and how to deal with the death of your character after those first few fights.
8.  I've Created My Character, Now What? Here's another good article about the first things to do once you've arrived with your character in the land of Norrath, and fighting isn't at the top of the list.
