EQIC Item Recharging Information
Some initial qualifications about the first chart... The name of the item, the number of charges and magical effect is taken directly from screenshots of the inventory information for each item (click on the item name if you want to see the picture). Below the first chart is another one with "unconfirmed" information (we have no screenshots, just information from other players). The recharging information in the two right columns has been gathered from player communications or are opinions expressed by EQIC. We do not guarantee the accuracy of this information, but we've tried to present it as accurately as possible. If you have any information we can use to fill in the blanks, please contact us with further details. Use this information at your own risk! Please be patient, this page has a long load time.
