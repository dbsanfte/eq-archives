EQ Spells - A downloadable program by Ashran. This program lists every spell in the game, including the GM/Guide spells, and shows what level each class has to be to get the spell (if it says lvl 61, you can't get it), the base damage of the spell, the mana cost requirements and any messages you get and others see when you cast the spell.  The program looks at the spell data in your EQ directory to give you the information, which means that you don't have to redownload the program each time the game is patched or new spells get added.
