Welcome to EQIC!

I have attempted to cull information from various sources off the internet to provide you, the viewer, with a "one stop shop" for all your EverQuest needs. All of the links take you to the original locations of these files, this site is merely a compendium of links of quality EQ content to hasten your quest for information. Read on and enjoy, and remember, these may be considered spoilers to your game. If there is anything else you'd like to see on this site, please email your request to eqic@fullbyte.com and I'll try to find the information you need. Also, please stop by and visit our Guestbook Bulletin Board if you have any feedback you'd like to offer about this site.
As you know, EQIC is a web site unconnected with any major gaming corporation or web presence. This site is created and maintained merely for the needs of the players. We do not get paid for what we do here. In order to support the costs of maintaining a web site on the internet, we have associated ourselves with several online stores where you can find some great products. If you find yourself in need of computer equipment, software, books, music, movies, video games, tools, model airplanes or kids stuff, please consider buying those products at our stores. It will help defray the costs of this web site, and eventually allow us to move to our own domain name site. Thanks for your support!

Please Note:

If you would like to be notified of any updates to the EQIC web site, please click on the Community menu item which says "Add Me To List" and fill out the form. You will then be added to a list of people who will be notified whenever the site is updated.

EQIC UPDATES

6-10-2000
Another quick update... we've got some more content to tell you about. We found a map for Cabilis (the starting city in Kunark) and the Cabilis underground. Both maps can be found in the City Maps section of the site. We found a handy Kunark zone link map and you can find that in the Specialty Maps section. We also found a map for the Timorous Deep zone which can be found in the Zone Maps section. We've got some new things in the Hints and Tips section as well as a guide to playing an Iksar necromancer in the Classes section. We also updated the Patches section with the last couple of months patch messages.

That's pretty much it for now, and we'll be working on updating the Quests section with the new Kunark quests for the next update.

5-31-2000
Just a quick and dirty update for now. Found the most important things for players who bought the Kunark Expansion, Kunark Maps! Head over to the Zone Maps section and check them out, they look REAL GOOD!

4-9-2000
Well, we finally did it! We went out and bought internet hosting for the eqic.net domain name that we've had for the past 6 months. We're very excited about having a home of our own with an easy-to-remember URL address! All of our old address pages will redirect you to the new site, so if you forget to resave your bookmarks, you'll still get here for awhile. Be sure to bookmark the new site so we can eventually take those old pages down.

Ok, in the update today, we had many, many bad links that have either been corrected or deleted. Both EQ Stratics and EQVault decided to revamp their sites and change all their page addresses in the process, which accounted for a good number of our bad links. We've also added some new content to the site which we hope you'll like. We've added two sites to both the Armor/Weapons/Items page and the Bazaar page which list the going prices for player sold items. We've updated the In-Game Commands file to include pet commands. You can find that on the Hints and Tips page. We've added a couple of sites which list the major location coordinates of specific areas within the zones, very helpful info which is found on the Combat page. In the Downloadable Files section, we've got 3 different versions of a spreadsheet that lists NPC's in the game as well as their levels, zones and hitpoints, and we've also found an EQ Server Checker. As a preview to the coming expansion, we've found a list of the beta spells being used in RoK. We've rearranged the Navigation on the side, Contact Us is now located in the Home section which allowed us to fill that space with a new category called Community. Check out the Community page, it has several new links to help you find EQ friends in real life, roleplay on bulletin boards, and get a head's up on who's good and who's bad in the game. We've updated some of the zone maps that were linked to sites that no longer exist and we've found some new trade skill links for you to check out. There are also other scattered updates and additions, just cruise through the pages and look for the or signs. Enjoy!
