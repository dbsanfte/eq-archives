son message :

Heres the latest version, and the last one Ill be doing :(

Some have asked what will happen to the spreadsheet. Im basically releasing it into the public domain for anyone to pick up. I dont want to delegate or appoint anyone, as there is a big difference between intent to do the work, and actually doing it reliably over a long period of time and I have no way to determine who falls into which category. So I figure probably multiple people will take on the job, and some will fade along the wayside, and some will be persistent. Ive always view the spreadsheet as belonging to the greater bardic community, and Ive just been its custodian. I hope this spirit continues :)

For anyone who wants to take this up the hardest part of doing the spreadsheet though, not the formulas, and not Excel. Its reading everyones posts with numbers, and verifying that the numbers still hold. The problem is not everyone is accurate, and it takes some time to weed the accurate from the inaccurate. Test everything; look for multiple sources of confirmation, etc. I dont mind asking questions (mail to eqreed@yahoo.com). Im checking that less these days, so it might be a few days before I get back to you, but Ill try to respond.

Reed...
