Clan Icewind was created April 7th 1999 - just a little after release. From  hunting mammoths in the Tundra of Everfrost, to participating in the first slayings of Nagafen, Vox, Cazic Thule, Innoruuk and the breaking of the Planes of Fear, Hate and Sky, Clan Icewind has grown from just a dozen members to about a hundred today. Im early april 2001, CIW will be two years old.
