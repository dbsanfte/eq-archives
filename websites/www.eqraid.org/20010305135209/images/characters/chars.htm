Introduction:

How the idea of taking multiple screenshots of a character and animate them popped in my mind don't ask because I don't know. But what helped me is the knowledge of some tools and technology; particularly Photoshop, Bryce 3D and Gif Animator. I just wanted to have a unique picture for the message boards.  The very first animation that I made, was one of my character standing still, while the background behind him was moving (the sky). Instantly I received some requests from guild mates to make animations for them. My second animation was Qwai's animation in which he does a jump kick. The animations finally evolved to animated characters with animated 3D backgrounds and finally transitions between multiple animations.

The ultimate step that one could take to making EQ character animations would be to actually model the character in a 3D program such as Studio Max, and create your own animation sequence. My lack of knowledge with those programs and the amount of time I know it will take to invest in such a project - currently prevent me from doing it. I still like to play EQ once is a while!

Commission portraits:

Because of the amount of time that I spend on each of these animations (3/4 hours for a simple one - 6/8 hours for a more advanced) I cannot realistically ask for real money since if I were to charge on an hourly basis based on my full time job salary - I would end up asking $100/$300 or more per animation. 

However, I am willing to accept some items in game. Since I play on the Rathe server, this restricts my animations to players on the Rathe server. Contact me by email if you are interested - please serious inquiries only. I also beg you not send me in-game tells to make you an animation.
