  Double postings on calendar:

Monday, June 26, 2000
Several guilds have posted double reservations on the calendar. As a reminder, here are the basic guidelines for posting raids:

1. Reservations for planes will be limited to 2 days Mon-Fri, and 1 Day on Sat and Sun.
2. A guild with a posted raid, may not post intentions for another raid in the same plane till the first trip is complete.

Please read/summit grievances on the RTA message board.

Thanks you all for keeping the Rathe a place we can coexist.
