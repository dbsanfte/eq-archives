Clan IceWind Guild Charter   

Our Mission:
We will keep the northlands safe from the encroaching goblin and orc hoards! We will protect and aid those in need. We will defend our comrades in combat till death! Above all else we will always act with honor.

Honor Code:
1.  I will help the weak
2.  I will be brave against the strong
3.  I will fight those who do wrong
4.  I will help those who call upon me
5.  I will help fellow Clans men/women
6.  I will be honest
7.  I will be faithfull to my Clan
8.  I will act with honor and dignity
9.  I will treat all equal even my enemies
10. I'll come to the aide of my allies
