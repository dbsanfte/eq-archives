  Rathe Rapsody anniversary!

Friday, April 14, 2000

From Raene:

Almost a year ago today, on April 14th '99, The Rathe Rhapsody opened as a site of collected information pertaining to the Rathe Server. How we have grown and changed! Gone are the Personal and Business pages, little used by Rathe players. In the start of our second year, we've added a permanent link to EQRaid.org...a site to organize and plan your guild raids and events in sought after planar camps.

A year ago the "high-level" players wore expensive bronze and sported surnames above their heads. LGuk was empty. Sol B was empty. West Commons was dangerous, Kithicor Forest was safe. The planes were in the planning stages. Guilds didn't discuss the attack of Nagafen or Vox except in a "someday" voice.

Soon the lands of Kunark will open, and new adventures await us. To celebrate Rathe Rhapsody's one year anniversary and in looking forward to feeling the excitement again of the unknown, we've given RR a new look. Enjoy! Thank you for your input, your news, and your visits. Without you this site would not be possible!

The Rathe Rapsody can be found at: http://2amstudios.com/eq/rathe.htm
