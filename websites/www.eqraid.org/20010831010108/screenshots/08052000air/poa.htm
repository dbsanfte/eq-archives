Plane Of Sky raid 06-07/30/2000

PoS Raid
After having some problems with Sirran, we could go no further than island 3. We still managed to entertain ourselves with Noble Dojorn who dropped golden effretti legs and bracers. Lano has now earned the "Golden Boy" nickname!
on the second day, CIW swiftly clearing island 3 and 4, we then confronted the Overseer of Air.
After a short battle OoA surrendered his life and another pair of golden effretti legs, as well as an effretti long sword. Note: do not engage OoA when he is ENRAGED.
After OoA, we then moved to island 5 and entertained a healthy number of spiroc chickens.
The Spiroc Lord dropped a Blade of Abrogation and a Spiroc Air Totem.
After island 5 we then attempted island 6 and the bees. That's a lot of bees!
Aegirson meets up with his big brother and finishes the warrior quest: Test of Force.
