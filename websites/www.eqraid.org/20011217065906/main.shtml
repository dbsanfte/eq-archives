  The big boys take a dirt nap....

Tuesday, December 11, 2001

CIW coming back in full force to dish out pain to some "old school" mobs.
First on the list was our old pal Doze. He went down with minimal effort as usual.
Next on the menu was Keeper whatevahdafrackyournameis. He too proved to be a simple task.
Then out of boredom, the remaining Clannies decided King Tormax needed to be laid to rest.
Trivial but fun time for all. Grats to all those that participated.

-- Hadoken

Monday, December 10, 2001

After having a total blast in the SoL (outside of the bugs and such) the clan paid a couple mobs a visit. Doze went down as easy as usual with Keeper (our first time attempting him) following closely behind. Grats to all the Clannies on a job well done.

  SoL

Wednesday, December 5, 2001

Many people were SOL for SoL patching last night. However, the clannies that did get in enjoyed exploring (and making fun of each others new looks). So far SoL seems a bit rough around the edges. There are some hardware issues that folks were having along with poor frame rates under certain video cards. Looks good but could be better. We shall see.
