  So What's Up With The Clan?

Sunday, October 14, 2001

Been a long time since we had a update here, so here we go! Kills recorded in the past couple weeks include: 1 Klandicar, 3 Sontalak, 3 Zlandicar, 3 King Tormax, 3 Dozekar, 2 Statue, 5 Vindicator, 2 Gorenaire, 1 Trak, and a Velketor or 2.

Clan Icewind has also started up on Dark Age of Camelot. We have around 40 Clannies on the Bedevere Server, Albion realm.

  Wedding, CIW keeping busy

Sunday, September 30, 2001

Been a long time without an update. Took a while for everyone to get themselves stable mentally after the terrorist crap. Anyways, I would like to present to you a scattering of what CIW has been doing in the past few weeks.






Belin and Kharpern were married by Aosinator in the Plane of Sky. The wedding party of 24 then proceeded to island 6 before calling it a night. Numerous KT and Dozekar slayings, (yawn). Attempted Gorenaire for the first time in 4 months, slaughtered the poor dragon with 0 deaths.


We also farmed two more ST keys, going into high gear slaying the Elder dragons, expect screenshots from the Tomb next update! More pictures here

  Hoshkar down! CIW playing with faction

Sunday, September 2, 2001


Big two days for the Clan. We have been getting pretty bored with the 4 'bosses' killable with Dragon faction ... so CIW decided to take a step on the wild side and try out some new fights. We headed over to ToV and slaughtered Telkoranor on the first try, then decided to heck with faction and killed Gozzrem as well. We killed Talendor in preparation for Veeshans Peak this Saturday, logged at VP, then spent the rest of night having nightmares of Hoshkar (AKA the Big Daddy of VP) gating.




Today we went into Veeshans Peak and conquered. Silverwing dead, Hoshkar DEAD, Druushk dead (even with a racnar attacking us during the fight). Phara dar .. beware, we will be back for you. Screenshots

  Everyday life in CIW

Friday, August 31, 2001

Dragon faction is paying off for us these past two weeks. Same old critters down, doze, KT, statue, vindi. CIW had midnight raids on Fearplane twice, getting broken golem for Niam and Evee (grats on wizard epics!), a few Trakanon kills, and a TON of Halls of Testing farming.



About 5 members completed full skyshrine armor this week alone, gearing up for the ToT. Doze quests finally starting to pay off for us: Niemain got the Serrated Dragon Tooth, Gris made his uber newbie buckler, Kolwyn has a new bracer, Sadelia with our first Cloak of the Firestorm. Can't remember the rest offhand. Anyways, enjoy the pics!
