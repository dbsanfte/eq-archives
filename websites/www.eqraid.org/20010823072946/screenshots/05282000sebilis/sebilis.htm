Sebilis 05/28/2000

Sebilis - the path to Trakanon's Lair Raid
First we creep our way thru the lower jail rooms - killing the mushrooms on the way
We are starting to get into tunnels and meeting many more mushrooms
We are starting to get close - we find Sebilite Juggernauts
We are finding tougher and tougher mobs on the way and we finally discover the entrance to Trakkanon's Lair.
We fing a frog there called "tolapmuj" (jumpalot spelled backwards)who drops a new caster robe.
After tricky pulling, we finally kill the Sebilite Protector - Trakkanon's body guard.
The Protector dropped a Hardened Clay Bracer. Trakanon was not in his/her Lair today.
