Clan IceWind  membership application guidelines

If you are level 55+, and enjoy playing with a guild that is family oriented CIW may be the place for you. CIW is on Claws of Veeshan faction. If you are applying you should also be on CoV faction. Post all the pertinent information about you and your "MAIN" character here including former guild affiliations, amount of time you play on a daily basis, and your goals in EQ. Then contact Qwai, McMad, or Matthi in game for further instructions. Pledges should plan on 3-4 weeks of raids/grouping with CIW before an invite is extended to them for full membership. New members are on a 45 day probation, and are not eligible for Epic fights for 60 days. Do not expect high end loot during your 45 day probation.

If you have read and agreed to these guidelines, please do apply here.

 

The High Council.
