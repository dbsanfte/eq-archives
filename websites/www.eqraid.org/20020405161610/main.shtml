  The End of an Era

Saturday, March 30, 2002

After 3 long years Clan Icewind is closing the book on Everquest. We wish to bid you all farewell, and thank you for all of the fun and adventures. A special thanks goes out to all our close friends and allies we have made!! We wish everyone the best in real life and in game. Our time spent in Everquest will never be forgotten!
             qwai

  Candyland Conquered

Friday, February 1, 2002

The Clan finally got a shot at NTOV this week! Needless to say we rolled through the north wing wiping out the Wurms with ease. Vulak died on the 1st attempt even though we had 2 named adds throughout the fight. Great job to all that attended!! We will try to get some pics up soon.

  Lord Yeliquack

Thursday, January 10, 2002

LY was neatly laid to rest by CIW and their friends from LD. Grats to all that participated in removing this thorn from our side.

Prior to the LY slaying, the clan has been busy with killing the "old usuals" such as KT, Doze, Statue/Vindi, OF, Ect...

We have also been completing epics left and right. Grats to those that have gotten new epics!

We also took a poke at AoW with our friends from Spirits of Fire and made a valiant effort at attempting to take him out.
