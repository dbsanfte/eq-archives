  Review: Black & White  5:35 PM MST - Posted By Atomic
Anticipaction of the next great game to roll down the pike is never easy on the nerves for a guy like me. I follow web sites, read all the information available, and dream of the day I get my hands on the finished product. More often than not, the eager desire to play the title is quickly washed away by some glitch or missing feature which completely undermines all reasons for wanting the title in the first place. Two games immediately come into mind - Trespasser and Daikatana. But having a great premise, license or designer does not make a great title. What pours into a game to make it truly special is the caring touch and attention to detail that can only be described as a labor of love. Peter Molyneux didn’t just want to make a game; he wanted to make the game he’s always wanted to play. His three-year effort resulted in Black & White, a mix of role-playing, adventure and strategy where you are a god in search of followers. Come one, come all. Welcome to your new religion.

  Review: No One Lives Forever  5:35 PM MST - Posted By Rontu
Have you ever wondered what would happen if you took a handful of your favorite games and combined them? No One Lives Forever seems to have successfully blended the best elements from games like Soldier of Fortune, Tomb Raider, Rainbow Six and then threw in a handful of Austin Powers humor just to keep things fun. AI has been taken to a realism level that is flat out amazing. Enemy guards can be distracted by throwing coins or setting fires, they will inspect corpses that have fallen to the ground, hide behind upturned furniture and even change from one hiding spot to another to get a better shot angle. Enemy conversations can be eavesdropped on; civilians can be spoken to and will cower in fear when weapons are drawn. Your weapons include machine guns, sniper rifles, pistols, grenade launchers, camera sunglasses, lipstick bombs, and even a cigarette lighter that doubles as a cutting torch. Snowmobiles and motorcycles can also be used to run down your enemies. Corpses will tumble from rooftops and roll down stairways. This is definitely not your average shooter game.
