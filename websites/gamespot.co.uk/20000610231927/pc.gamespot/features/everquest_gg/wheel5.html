Inventory
Along the right-hand side of the inventory screen, you will see an Inventory button, a picture of your character, a Destroy button, and a series of eight inventory slots that are holding your beginning inventory.

If you press the Inventory button, you will see a list of all the locations on your characters body along with spots for two melee weapons, a range weapon and ammo, and spots for all the various types of gold you have in your purse. To equip an inventory item, left-click on the weapon in your inventory and move to the Melee Weapon 1 slot and single click again. This will equip the weapon.

TIP
Holding the right mouse button down on any inventory item will give you a description of what the item is.

You can also drag an item onto your character portrait to equip it. You will notice that you begin with a couple of scrolls, your spell scrolls if you haven't already placed them in your book, some food and water, and you're starting weapon. The Destroy button in the middle of the screen is used to destroy a particular item. You do by single clicking on the item and single-clicking on Destroy to remove it from your inventory and remove it from the game.

NOTE
If you want to read your quest scrolls you must right click on them once, and you can see them over the worldview. Press Done when you're finished reading your scroll to put it away.

Any of your inventory positions can be made into a hot button. If you'd like to give an inventory item to someone, single-click on the item, move it to your view of the world, and single-click on whom you wish to give it to. You will do this with your quest scrolls to give them to the person that they are to be delivered to. When you're finished with your inventory screen, you can click on the done button.
