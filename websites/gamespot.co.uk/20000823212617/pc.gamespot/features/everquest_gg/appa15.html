Rivervale
The quiet village of Rivervale is home to the furry-footed halflings. The often analytical halflings are a race of law and order, and you will surely come upon your share of deputies and marshals while there. You may even chance upon the good Mayor Gubbin, who's often spreading good cheer to his people.

The Fool's Gold is a must-see while in town, as it serves an assortment of food and ale and offers the gambler a chance to relax upstairs. Outside of town, don't miss the dome-shaped halfling hovels that are scattered around the countryside.


Rivervale
The great wall and several large towers outside the city offer great views of the surrounding countryside. Rivervale is one of the most pleasant towns in Norrath and a great place to get escape to.
