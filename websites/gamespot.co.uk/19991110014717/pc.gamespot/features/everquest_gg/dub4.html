Features



Paladin
Paladins are the very picture of a holy knight, fighting for the cause of good. They share some of the power of a cleric and a good deal of the fighting ability of a warrior, but have a few powers unique to their class.

Ranger
The ranger is a meld between a warrior and a druid, sharing some of the skills of both. They are at home in the outdoors and often excel with the bow and arrow.

Rogue
Rogues are the most secretive, mysterious class in the world. Some say they are assassins, and some say that they are nothing but common thieves, but the truth lies somewhere in the middle, and they are capable of both. They are quite skilled with many weapons, but they cannot stand toe to toe with a warrior and prefer to make a kill by a sudden attack from the rear, at which they are extremely skilled. They would much prefer to not be discovered at all, or if they are, to not be there for their opponent's blow to land. The darker skills of picking locks and stealing are also their forte.

Shadow Knight
The evil twin to the paladin class, shadow knights derive all their powers from the evil gods who they serve. They are a meld of warrior and necromancer and share some of their abilities and spells.

Shaman
Similar to the cleric, a shaman is more of a tribal witch doctor and is typically found amongst the more primitive races. A shaman's primary focus is largely healing and other clerical-type spells, but they also have a good deal of offensive spells.

Warrior
Warriors are the masters of armed combat in all its many forms. They are at home on the battlefield and are trained to take punishment as much as dole it out, so their hit points are the highest of any class.

Wizard
Wizards are the master seekers of knowledge, and their talents and spells are focused on acquiring more. They use powerful spells to not only see things from afar but to transport them there as well. This thirst for arcane knowledge has also made them the true masters of the strongest form of defense: offensive spells of pure destruction. In their ability to do magical damage, they have no rivals.
