Basic Survival

Levels 1-5
Only take on easy prey at the lowest levels, as your combat and healing skills are meek. Learn how to time your spells so that your adversary's strikes don't interrupt your casting. As you get to level 3 or 4, group with one fighter-class character, and let him start the fights. Your healing skills should be adequate to keep you both fighting, and you will advance quickly.

Levels 5-8
At this level, you should be adding many spells to your repertoire. You must kill monsters that give decent loot, so concentrate on combat with fire beetles, goblins, and so on. When you gain meditate (at level 8), make certain to use it while sitting to quickly replenish your mana.

Levels 9+
This is the level where you get Word of Pain, a powerful spells that attacks adversaries around you. If you are still grouping with others, be careful with offensive spells, as your party is still counting on you to heal its members. Don't spend all your mana in one place.

Combat Skills
Train first at one-hand blunt. Use just one training point, as the skill will rise quickly after that with repeated use.
