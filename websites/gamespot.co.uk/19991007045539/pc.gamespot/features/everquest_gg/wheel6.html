Game Options

Pressing the Options button will bring up ways to customize your gameptions. Under General, you will find options for adjusting your sound and the way you deal with others. Under Display, you will see options on how your video will display and whether you want PC or NPC names to appear over tops of the characters in the world. The Mouse button will let you adjust your mouse sensitivity and let you turn Mouse Look on so that you can look around by holding down your right mouse button. The Keyboard option will let you remap all your default command keys. The default keyboard settings are listed here.

You can change any of these default keyboard settings to a different key simply by going to its selection and replacing the key with the key of your choice. The chat filters will let you edit what types of communication you will receive from other characters. You may want to turn these all on at first and then turn off the ones that you don't find necessary. You can press the Back key to return to the windowed mode.

TIP
Consider moving the auto-attack key to something other than A. You will be typing the letter A a lot when talking to NPCs, and it is unfortunate when you accidentally attack them because you forgot to hit Enter before typing A.
