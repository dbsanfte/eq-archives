Chain Armor
Chainmail provides much better protection than leather, but is also much more expensive. The higher cost is associated with the process in which the chains are linked together to form the armor. It is very time consuming and thus reflected in the price. Chainmail provides good protection against most types of attacks, but can be vulnerable to fine pointed thrusting weapons.
