Halas
Be sure to bring your heavy furs when travelling to Halas, as the ice and snow will freeze the unprepared. The native barbarians that call Halas home have adapted quite well to the cold and snow, often wearing less clothing than can be seen in the desert near Freeport. Halas is not large in size, but it does contain almost any supply an adventurer would need.

There are several places that are worth seeing. The rough and tumble sorts should pay a visit to the Field of Slaughter. Other places of note include McDaniels Smokes and Spirits as well as the renowned McQuaids Dark Stout, which offers the darkest ale in the land - if you can stomach it.
