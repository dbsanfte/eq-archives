Features



If you click on Combat, you will see Melee Attack, Range Attack, and four combat skills. Clicking on the combat skills will bring up a list of combat skills you can use, and you can create hotkeys out of any of these also.

Clicking on Socials will bring up a list of 12 customizable keys. Any of these can be made into hotkeys, and you can customize them to make your own socials by right-clicking on them once. This will bring up an editor where you can specify your own keys and specify your own colors. The following are commands available as socials or for use at the command line.

/shout

Text is displayed to all players in the zone

/t (player name)

Text is displayed to a particular player

/ignore (player name)

Text from named player is not displayed

/shownames

Toggles names on or off above players and NPCs

/g

Text is displayed to all in your group

/c

Targeted creature is considered

/consent (player name)

Named player is allowed to loot your corpse

/d

Targeted player is challenged to a duel

/ooc

Lets you say something out of character

/a

Become anonymous, no longer displaying level and class

/time

Give the time in Norrath

/played

Tells how long you have played this character

/split # # # #

Splits the specified amounts of coins with the party

/autosplit

Turns on the group auto split function

/assist

Attack whoever the targeted player is attacking

/corpse

Will move your corpse toward you from up to 30 feet away, if it's not in lava; useful if it falls in a bad spot.

/friend

Adds or removes a person from your friends list

/em or :

Emotes text

/wave

Wave at target

/rude

Make a rude gesture at target

/gesture

Same as /rude

/cry

Cries

/nod

Nods

/panic

Scream in panic

/bow

Bow to target

/cheer

Cheer

/rofl

Roll on the floor laughing

/pet guard here

Orders pet to guard the area

/pet guard me

Orders pet to guard controller

/pet follow me

Orders pet to follow controller

/pet attack

Orders pet to attack target

/pet back off

Orders pet to withdraw

/pet as you were

Cancels previous orders

/who (filter) (all)

Lists players in your zone or world

/who friend all

Lists all the people in your friends list who are online
