Following is a sample conversation with a quest NPC. Note the keywords that the NPC provides in the brackets.

You: Hail Ardanna Kelvar!

Ardanna Kelvar: Greetings! I am [waiting] in this inn because it is dreadfully cold outside, and the rain soaks through my lovely dress. I do wish I could [leave] so I could return to my home in Kelethin, but I fear my clothes will be ruined, and I may catch a chill!

You: How long have you been waiting here? (You could just say the word "waiting," but some players like to role-play it out.)

Ardanna Kelvar: Yes, I am weary of [waiting] and do wish I could [leave].

You: Is there anything I can do to help you leave?

Ardanna Kelvar: Oh, that is so kind of you to ask! If you could obtain a [cloak] for me, I would truly appreciate it!

You: A cloak? Any cloak in particular?

Ardanna Kelvar: I hear that [Fritz Fraggle] has a cloak that should fit me just right.

You: Fritz Fraggle?

Ardanna Kelvar: Aye, he owns a tailor shop in southwestern Freeport.

So, you now have a quest to find Fritz Fraggle in southwestern Freeport, retrieve a cloak from him, and bring it back to Ardanna Kelvar. Sometimes it is more or less complicated than this. And other times, the conversations will be shorter, longer, or may lead nowhere (that is, you won't be given a quest at all).
