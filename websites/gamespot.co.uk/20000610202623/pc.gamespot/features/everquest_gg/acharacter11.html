Player-Run Quests
The gods (GMs) can also run quests. It is impossible to predict when these quests will occur or even where, but rest assured, they will be full of adventure and intrigue!

Finally, quests can be player run. Already, organizations such Forever Quest and Norrath Roleplay exist. Players run these organizations, and their goal is to provide ongoing quests and adventures for other players in Norrath. EverQuest did not get its name without cause!

Special Items
Through great diligence and involvement in the game world, some blessed players may receive such coveted items as flaming swords and other magical creations. Only the most active and diligent of players can be rewarded - these rewards do not come easily, but they are worth it!

Through my travels, I have heard of a land called Oasis, and therein lies many magical artifacts. Be wary, however, this place is not to be lightly entered - and any below at least level 12 should avoid it.

Player-Owned Buildings

Owning a building is one of the greatest and most lasting accomplishments of any adventurer or guild. Whether the buildings are erected in their honor, for greed, pride, or the good of the people of Norrath, awe is be inspired in all.

Only the most dedicated and active of guilds can ever attain such a lofty goal. The cost of such buildings is unimaginable by some, and their upkeep is also equally lavish. These everlasting monuments remind all citizens of Norrath what hard work and cooperation can accomplish!
