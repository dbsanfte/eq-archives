Bind Affinity
You will soon realize that you are limited in how far you can adventure from your home city without risking permanent loss of your items upon dying. If you travel from Erudin to Kaladim only to die, you have a very long journey ahead of you to reclaim your belongings. Well, you do have an option here.

Some spell casters have the ability to cast a spell called "bind affinity." This is one of the most useful spells in all EverQuest. It effectively sets your home location to where you are standing when the spell is cast. So, if you travel from Qeynos (your home city) to Kelethin and cast bind affinity in Kelethin, you have changed your home location from Qeynos to Kelethin. Upon death, you will then return to Kelethin and not Qeynos.

TIP
Travel with someone who can cast bind affinity. This assures you a short trek to your corpse if mishaps befall you.

In addition, other spell casters can cast this spell upon you. If you cannot cast spells, simply ask around. There are usually mages who will gladly bind you to a new location, although sometimes for a fee. The modest cost is usually well worth it!

Walk Softly and Carry a Big Stick

A simple creed perhaps, yet a rule of thumb you should remember. Never advance into an unknown area at full speed; it may be teeming with monsters. Running around a corner to find half a dozen monsters is a good way die young. Another rule of thumb is to wear the best armor you can afford and to wield your best weapon. Did we also mention that it never hurts to bring along some of your buddies as well?

TIP
When in doubt, run!
