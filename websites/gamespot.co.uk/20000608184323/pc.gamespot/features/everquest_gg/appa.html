Appendix A
Maps - Finding Your Way

Three continents fill the hemisphere of Norrath, and whether other lands lay beyond the oceans to the east and west, no one knows, or at least admits to such knowledge. Rumors of different planes and dimensions even surface occasionally, but most do not believe such ramblings. Finding your way through Norrath is no easy feat, especially for the new adventurer. Since EverQuest does not come with an automap feature, we give you the next best thing. Within these pages, you'll find crucial information and maps for the continents and cities of Norrath.

Being the brave adventurer you are, we don't want to take all the fun out of the game and finding the hidden entrance to a certain area of a dungeon is part of the gaming fun. These maps should prove useful, but don't be afraid to stop and ask for directions if you happen to take a wrong turn. A lost adventurer quickly becomes a dead adventurer.

Continents

The main continents (or islands) of Norrath are divided up into smaller, interlocking areas called "zones." These zone maps should prove useful in navigation from one portion of the continent to another. While they do not provide specific zone features, they do show how each of the zones connect to one another.
