Jewel Craft

This craft lets you fashion fine jewelry and precious metals. Jewelry carries a unique charm, and the more exquisite the stone, the more likely the setting will be to release the force of the mineral essence.

To make jewelry, you must combine the following components in a jewelry kit.



Stone

There are many types of stones available, including precious stones, pearls, and crystals

Melted Metal

The melted metal, when poured into the mold in your kit, will take the shape of the setting

Jewelry Mold

A mold for the melted metal to be shaped in. Many varieties are available
