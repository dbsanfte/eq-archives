Skills and Statistics
The next button up the left side of the screen is the Persona button. You can activate this by pressing it, pressing the letter I, or by pressing the icon indicating your character class. All these will take you to the inventory screen.

At the top of the left-hand side of your inventory screen, you will see two buttons. One is labeled Skills, and the other is labeled Stats. Activating the Stats selection will bring up your character information. Below the Stats key will be the name of your character, your current level and class, your hit points (current and maximum), as well as your armor class and your ability to attack. Below that is a bar indicating how much experience you'll need to obtain your next level.

NOTE
The subdivisions within the Next Level bar are commonly referred to as "bubbles" by other players. You'll often hear them say, "I am two bubbles away from the next level."

Below this is information indicating the statistics, or attributes, you started with, modified by any magical items you have or any spells cast on you, your resistance to certain forms of attack, and your weight allowance. It also displays how much weight you are carrying now and how much you are allowed to carry.

You can change the left-hand side by pressing on the Skill button. This will bring up a list of all the skills your character knows. You can scroll using the scroll bar along the right-hand side of the skills. It is normal for a starting character to have all his skills rated "awful," although if you are a non-human, you may have an enhanced ability in one or more skills.
