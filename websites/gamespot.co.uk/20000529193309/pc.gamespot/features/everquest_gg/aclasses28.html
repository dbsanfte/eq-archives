Combat Skills

Train first on only one combat skill: either one-hand slash or blunt. Use just one training point, as the skill will rise quickly with repeated use.

Taunt
This skill is quite useless at lower levels, but becomes very important at higher levels, once fighting in a party is crucial. Use it during a fight often and keep using it to increase the skill for use later.

Lay Hands
This may be the paladin's most enduring skill. Though it can only be used once during a Norrath day, it completely heals the target. Needless to say, this is a very effective safety net, but since it does not regenerate for 24 hours, it must be used only in cases of dire emergency. And, since it heals a combatant completely, it should be used when that combatant is quite low on hit points (which is also quite risky if you are fighting a griffin).

Dodge
Dodge functions automatically in combat once you've trained in the basics, so you don't need to activate it. Basically, if it succeeds, the monster misses. You gain this ability once you reach level 10. Train only once.

Bind Wound
This is a very useful skill, since it gives you the ability to heal yourself and others. This is not really a healing spell, but it gives you another way to heal yourself and those in need. This skill is available upon reaching level 10. To advance in this skill, you must first learn the basic skill, then use it.

Double Attack
Double attack functions automatically in combat once you've trained in the basics, so you don't need to activate it. Basically, it gives you the ability to hit twice during a normal battle sequence. You gain this ability once you reach level 20. Train only once.

Disarm
Disarm becomes a wonderful skill when you reached level 40. The ability to lessen the amount of melee damage the enemy can dish out can turn the tide of a battle rather quickly. You gain this skill when you reach level 40.

Riposte
Since few have ever seen a paladin of such high level, this skill is quite unknown in Norrath. It is only available upon reaching level 30.
