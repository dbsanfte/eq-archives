Basic Survival

Levels 1-5
Acquire the Burst of Flame spell as soon as possible, as it is your main direct damage spell. Upon reaching level 5, acquire Burst of Fire (another direct damage spell) and Grasping Roots (it will damage your enemy and has a chance of rooting him to the ground). Grasping Roots is a great spell to cast at a fleeing enemy as it goes for help, since the spell causes a good amount of damage to the enemy, and most of the time, the enemy is entangled in the roots and can't run away. Other good spells to have are camouflage (an outside invisibility spell), cure disease (one of the few things that cures the dreaded mummy diseases), and ward summoned. Ward summoned is a very powerful spell and can be used against all types of summoned creatures (elementals and necromancer skeletons), and it can do a vast amount of damage to these enemies with each cast.

Levels 6-9
At level 8, you gain meditate, which lets you quickly regain mana. You should use about five skill points immediately upon reaching level 8 in meditation - you will be very happy later. Meditation makes it possible to regain mana back at a much higher rate, and the memorization of spells, which used to take a couple of minutes, now takes a mere second or two. You will want to start saving all your money when you hit level 6, for when you hit level 9, spells will cost in the range of five platinum per spell. This is quite expensive, but the spells are much more powerful than those you get at level 5.

You receive your next direct damage spell at this level, ignite, and you will be very happy to also gain a new and much-improved healing spell, light healing. Furthermore, the druid gains his first level of damage shields. These damage shielding spells will become a very important part of the druid's repertoire. When you cast a damage spell on yourself or a comrade, any enemy attacking a shielded person takes damage over time. This damage adds up very fast, and by attacking you, the enemy is hurting itself. Other very important spells for level 9 include a new armoring spell, Armor of Thistles (which acts as a damage shield also), enduring breath (water breathing), firefist (adds damage to your attack), and Strength of the Earth (increases your strength). Almost every spell you get at level 9 is important. Any druid would be wise to get them all.
