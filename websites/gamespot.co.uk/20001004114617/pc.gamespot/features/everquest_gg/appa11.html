Kelethin
Far and wide, the tales of the lofty wood elven city of Kelethin are told with splendor and wonder dancing in the eyes of the teller. High above in the great trees of the Greater Faydark, small elven homes sit upon platforms linked with bridges and ramps. Great enchanted lifts bring those upon the mossy floor of the forest up to the lower ramps, high in the air safe from the evils below. Beware, however, of the perils of this fabled village. Though wood elves are sure of foot, other races do not share such boons. Often in the dark of night, haunting sounds fill the air, as an unfortunate traveler - moving with too much haste or too little care - slips and falls to his death to the forest below.


Kelethin
In the fair city of Kelethin, most passersby will find their basic needs attended to. A bank sits on an upper platform not far west of the main lift, and there are various shops throughout then entire village. The famed Soldiers of Tunare guild is located here as well - nowhere else in Norrath can one learn as valued druid skills as this spot - and it is the only place one can learn to track. The Heartwood Tavern is a favorite meeting place in Kelethin.
