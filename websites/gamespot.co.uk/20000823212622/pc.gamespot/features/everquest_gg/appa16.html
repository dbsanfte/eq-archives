Surefall Glade
The small, secluded village of Surefall Glade is home to the rangers and druids. While the village doesn't consider any particular race native, it considers anyone who has an appreciation of nature and animals friend.

Surefall Glade is small, consisting of only a few buildings catering to rangers and druids. There is an archery range to practice your bow and a small lagoon in which to fish. There are also bear caves in the cliffs behind Surefall, but do not let the residents catch you hunting those bears, or you will soon feel their wrath as well.
