Monk

The martial arts purists, monks are focused, mentally disciplined physical fitness enthusiasts who mainly use their own bodies as weapons. This requires enormous amounts of training and the strictest regimen and diet. Pugilism, karate, or just about any form of combat that involves the body and its potential is the forte of the monk.



Available races

Human

Primary statistics

Strength, stamina, agility, dexterity

Starting equipment

Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, and five loaves of bread
