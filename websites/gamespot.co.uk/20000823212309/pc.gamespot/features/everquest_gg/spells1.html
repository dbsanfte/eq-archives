Obtaining New Spells

You can obtain new spells in several ways. The first is to buy spells from your guild hall or any merchant who sells spells. Most lower level spells can be found readily from these sources. Higher-level spells are more scarce and sometimes require completion of a quest. The second way to buy spells is from other players who scribe spells onto blank scrolls. This can be more convenient, but often is also more costly.

Spell List
Following is a comprehensive list of the spells available in EverQuest. While we have made every effort to verify the sources for such information, many high-level spells have not been cast in ages.
