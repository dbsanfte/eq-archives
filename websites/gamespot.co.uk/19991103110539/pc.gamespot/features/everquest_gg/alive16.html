Hiding

Practice your hiding skill (if you have it) regularly. The hiding skill is a valuable one to assign to a hotkey, where a single keystroke will attempt to hide your character. When you're walking in the forest, press your hiding hotkey and attempt to hide. Hide while taking a stroll through town. Hide while standing in the merchant shop. Hide whenever you can. This is one skill that can save your life if you get into a fight that's over your head.

Remember though, that to hide, you must be standing completely still. So, if you begin to flee from a combat situation, you must get far enough ahead of your enemy to stop, and hide. Some creatures, once combat is initiated, can see you well enough to prevent a successful hiding attempt. If, after combat, you cannot hide from the creature pursuing you - your best course of action may simply be to run to town, to friends, or to the zone line.

TIP
Some spells let you cloak yourself, which in effect makes your character invisible to creatures, just as the hiding skill does.

Bank Deposits

Whenever you earn a decent amount of currency, either spend it on equipment or deposit it in a bank. While players cannot steal it from you (unless you have become PvP+), you run the risk of losing it when you die. When you die, your corpse remains lying there for a set amount of time, which is dependent upon the level of your character. Nonetheless, it is risky to rely on your corpse still being there when you finally get back to the location. There is really no point in carrying around a surplus of currency or your spare sword. Put them in the bank!
