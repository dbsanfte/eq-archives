Deities
Following are brief descriptions of the deities that have followers in Norrath.

Agnostic
Your character does not follow any of the deities of Norrath.

Bertoxxulous - The Plague Bringer
Followers of Bertoxxulous believe the only truth on Norrath is that everything dies. They view the decay of flesh as a thing of ultimate beauty, and many of his followers practice the art of necromancy.

Bristlebane - The King of Thieves
Followers of Bristlebane believe in having fun, regardless of the consequences. They are almost always very charming, clever, and witty. Mischief and practical jokes are commonplace, though followers of Bristlebane are rarely considered evil.

Cazic-Thule - The Faceless
Followers of Cazic-Thule fear their lord and believe that only by causing terror in others will they be spared from his wrath. Pain, misery, violence, torture, and living sacrifices are some of the tools of a Cazicite.

Innoruuk - The Prince of Hate
Followers of Innoruuk include nearly all the entire dark elven race who regard him as their father. They believe that hate is the creative force in the world and that love and kindness are for the ignorant.

Karana - The Rainkeeper
Followers of Karana believe in the power of storms. They worship the life-giving power of the rain and respect the destructive force of a sandstorm or hurricane.

Erollisi Marr - The Queen of Love
Followers of Erollisi Marr believe that love conquers all. It should be pointed out that while love is a generally considered a peaceful concept, Erollisi worshippers are not pacifists.

Mithaniel Marr - The Lightbearer
The followers of Mithaniel Marr believe that valor is what separates civilized beings from beasts. His followers live by a strict moral code founded on truth, honor, and charity.

Rodcet Nife - The Prime Healer
Followers of Rodcet Nife take a solemn oath to fight disease and death. They are very generous and humble, asking little more than the recipients of their aid pass the kindness onto another.
