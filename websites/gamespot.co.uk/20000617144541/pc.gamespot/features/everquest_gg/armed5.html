Piercing
Piercing weapons are weapons that have a sharp point and may or may not have a sharp edge. Damage is inflicted from these weapons by thrusting the point of the weapon at your opponent. Piercing weapons include weapons like daggers, rapiers, and spears.

These weapons may be either one or two handed, so you may use a shield with some of them. The following classes can use piercing weapons: bard, enchanter, magician, necromancer, rogue, ranger, warrior, and wizard.
