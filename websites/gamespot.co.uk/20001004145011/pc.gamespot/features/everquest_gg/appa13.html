Oggok
Like the trolls, the ogre city of Oggok is not for the weak of stomach. It seems the two races have a similar liking for randomly thrown body parts throughout their city. If you are looking for trouble, you might want to look elsewhere. The well-armored guards in Oggok are some of the toughest in all of Norrath. The heavy stone slabs used for construction in Oggok reinforce the notion that the ogres are not a race to be trifled with.


Oggok
While the city may not look like much, ogres do have love ale and partying. After the day is done, the taverns of Oggok serve some of the strongest potables around. Ogres do not have much skill at architecture, much like the Trolls, and their city reflects such. If you ask them, however, their city is the most beautiful in all of Norrath.
