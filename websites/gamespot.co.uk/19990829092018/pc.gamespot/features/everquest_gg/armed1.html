One-Hand Blunt
One-hand blunt weapons are weapons that do not have a sharp edge. Damage is inflicted from these weapons by simply bludgeoning your opponent to death. One-hand blunt weapons include weapons like the club, mace, and flail.

Since these weapons are one-handed, you have your other hand free to wield a shield or maybe another weapon. The following classes can use one-hand blunt weapons: bard, cleric, druid, monk, paladin, ranger, rogue, shadow knight, shaman, and warrior.
