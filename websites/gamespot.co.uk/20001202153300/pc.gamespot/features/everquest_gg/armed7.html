Armor

Armor is what protects you from your enemies blows. The more and thicker armor you have on, the better protected you will be. Armor is typically made from one of four main materials: cloth, leather, chain, and plate. Magical armor or armor made from other materials has been rumored to exist in Norrath as well.

Armor can cover almost any portion of your body. The following table lists which body part a particular type of armor will protect.

Body part

Type of armor

Head

Helmet, coif, helm

Face

Mask

Ears

Earrings

Neck

Gorget

Chest/torso

Tunic, breastplate

Back

Cape, cloak

Shoulders

Gambeson, pauldron

Arms

Sleeves, armbands

Wrists

Bracers

Hands

Gloves, gauntlets

Waist

Belts

Legs

Leggings, pants, greaves

Feet

Boots, solerets

The following tables show the types of armor made from the four basic materials. Armor comes in three sizes as well: small, medium (typically not given a size designation), and large. What size you wear depends on what race you are. The following information applies to the armor listed in this section.

Weight

How heavy your armor is (lower number is better)

Armor Class

The armor class (AC) rating of the armor (higher number is better); your total AC is a sum of all the armor and shields you are wearing
