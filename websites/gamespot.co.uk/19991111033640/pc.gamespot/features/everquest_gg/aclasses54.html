Creating a Character
Shamans are the spellcasters of the so-called lesser races, the barbarians, ogres, and trolls. They are not the best spellcasters, but they can defend themselves better then the other spellcasting classes.

Getting Started
Once you have chosen your race, you must to split your bonus points. A shaman's powers come from his wisdom, so spend your bonus points there. If you wish, spent a few on intelligence, which will let you learn spells faster. Now, name your character and enter Norrath. When you arrive in Norrath, you will find that your pack has some food and water, a club, the Tome of Discord, two spells scrolls (inner Fire and minor healing), and last but not least, a guild note. Follow the directions on the guild note as soon as possible.

Beginning Adventuring Tips
A shaman is not a master of any one kind of magic; instead, he uses many from healing magic spells of affliction. Shamans can cast spells that make a warrior stronger or sap his strength. But, unlike most of the other spellcasters of Norrath, shamans can hold their own in a fight. In a party, there will be many times when you can assist your companions in battle without ever hitting the target. Spells are your main weapon, but use melee weapons a bit when in a party. Most likely, you'll be needed in a party as a healer, so use offensive spells only when mana permits and your party is not having trouble with the battle.
