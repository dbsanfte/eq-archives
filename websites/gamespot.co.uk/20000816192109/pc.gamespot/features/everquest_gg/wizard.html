Chapter 4
Off to See the Wizard

Once you're transported to the world of Norrath, you'll find yourself within a town or city limits, depending on your race/class decision. It is important to spend some time in town, both for safety's sake and to help familiarize you with the gameplay. In the last chapter, you were introduced to the interface, now it's time to put foot to path and begin your adventures. Whatever you do, don't wander out of town until you're prepared. There are many dangers in Norrath, and the weak and unprepared don't survive long.

In the following chapter, you'll learn the basics of movement, communication, trading, combat, magic, and tips on surviving the first few hours in Norrath.

Movement

There are several modes of transportation in Norrath. The most basic and often used are your own two feet. There are also ferry services that continually transport passengers between the continents. Underwater regions are traversed by swimming. Chapter 3 - Taking the Wheel briefly discussed movement and lists the default keyboard settings. You may customize your movement controls (and most players do) once you get a feel for the game.

Walking / Running
Use your arrow keys or preprogrammed keys on the keyboard to move your character around the world.

Click the Run button on the bottom right of your screen (on the main menu of your windowed view or your hotkey if you put it there) to run and again to resume walking. If you have a long way to go, you may want to auto-run. To start this, press on the forward arrow to run. Then press and hold the Alt key. Let up on the forward arrow key, then the Alt key. You are now running until you press either the forward or backward arrows. You can adjust direction with the right or left arrow while still running.

The Page Up and Page Down buttons will let you look up and down, and the Home key will center your view again.

Holding down the spacebar will let your character crouch, and releasing the spacebar will let you jump. To execute a running broad jump, lightly tap the space bar while running.
