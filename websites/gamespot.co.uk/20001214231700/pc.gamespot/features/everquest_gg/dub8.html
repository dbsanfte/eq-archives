Gnome
Gnomes spend most of their lives in their underground burrows and rarely come to the surface. They resemble dwarves to some extent, but have a ruddy brown skin and are more wiry and gnarled. Their hair is white and somewhat sparse. Gnomes are considered small and wear small-sized armor and clothing. They possess ultravision and begin play in Ak'Anon. Their special power includes the improved tinkering skill.

Half Elf
Half elves, being a blend between humans and woodland elves, share some of the strengths and weaknesses of both races. They most closely resemble humans, except for their slightly pointed ears and sharper facial features. Half elves are considered medium and wear medium-sized armor and clothing. They possess infravision and begin play in Felwithe, Freeport, Kelethin, or Qeynos (depending on your class and deity selection). They possess no special abilities.
