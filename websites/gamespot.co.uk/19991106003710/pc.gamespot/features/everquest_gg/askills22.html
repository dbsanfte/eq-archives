Tailoring

This skill lets you sew fine leather items such as patchwork and studded armor from hides and pelts.

To make items, you must combine the components in a sewing kit. The size of the armor will depend on the size of the pelt you use. The following pelts should be used to make the specified armor size

Small Sizes

Use wolf pelts

Medium Sizes

Use cat pelts

Large Sizes

Use bear pelts

The following are some items and their ingredients that can be crafted with the tailoring skill.

Patchwork Armor

Ruined pelt, appropriate pattern

Studded Armor

Medium-quality pelt, appropriate pattern, appropriate number of studs as follows: masks (one stud); gorgets, belts, bracers (two studs); caps, shoulderpads, sleeves (three studs); cloaks, gloves, pants, boots (four studs); tunics (five studs)

Reinforced Leather

High-quality pelt, appropriate pattern, appropriate number of steel bonings as follows: masks, gorgets, bracers (one piece); caps, shoulderpads, sleeves, cloaks, gloves (two pieces); pants, boots (three pieces); tunics (four pieces)
