Duels
So you say that the life of a PK is not for you? Well, if that is case, you have two additional options if you wish to engage other players without resorting to the PvP+ flag. These options are duels and guild wars.

A duel is merely a one-time fight between two opponents. You may target the player and type /duel to issue a challenge to the death. If the other person responds with the same command, you will both be informed that the duel has been accepted. At that time, you may initiate combat and fight until one of you lies motionless on the ground. At the conclusion of a duel, a broadcast message is sent through the zone and a few of the nearby zones informing the world of the outcome of your duel. If one player runs from a duel, a message will be broadcast indicating the player has fled like a coward. Just as with PvP fighting, there is no experience gained or lost from duels, but the victor is can loot just as if it were a PvP fight.

TIP
Duels are excellent ways to gain bragging rights and settle role-played disputes.

Guild Wars
Another option for player combat is a guild war. The guild leader has the power to declare both war and peace with other guilds in Norrath. In a guild war, players in a guild can attack members of the opposite guild under the same rules of PvP combat. Be forewarned, the opposing guild members will be keeping their eyes open for you as well. There are no restrictions on locations where guild battles may take place, but it should be noted that guards frown heavily upon any fighting within or near their cities or patrol areas.

There is a special set of commands that player guilds can use to manage the guild. The following commands are reserved for the guild leader only and let that person recruit new guild members as well as some other guild functions.

/guildinvite <name> <rank>

Adds the person to the guild with the specified rank

/guildremove <name>

Removes the person from the guild

/guilddelete

Disbands your entire guild

/guildleader <name>

Transfers the leadership of the guild to that person

/guildwar <name> <duration>

Initiates a guild war with another player guild

/guildpeace <name>

Ends a guild war
