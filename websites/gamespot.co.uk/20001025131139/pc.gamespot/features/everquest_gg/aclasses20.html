Safe Fall (3)
I'm not sure if this skill works or not. Though I put 15 practices into it and jumped off high places many times, never once did I receive any indication that it did something nor did I ever get better at it.

Round Kick (5)
Round kick is the first magical special attack you'll get; it hits hard, but recycles slowly. You can't beat the animation, though.

Sneak (8)
A rather difficult skill to build up, sneak lets you walk very slowly but quietly past monsters, though I have found very few situations in which it is truly useful.

Tiger Strike (10)
Tiger strike is the second magical special attack you'll get. This recycles about every other turn and does as much as a normal punch, but seems to hit more often.

Block (12)
The monk's version of parry, block can be a truly useful skill for evading blows.

Double Attack (15)
Double attack rises on its own in combat once you train in the basics. It lets you take an additional attack per turn when it succeeds.

Feign Death (17)
Feigning death is a highly useful skill worth dumping many practices into. If you succeed, you flop on the ground and appear dead. Monsters leave you alone, and you can lie there until you recover all your hit points.

Eagle Strike (20)
Eagle strike is another magic attack.

Dragon Punch (25)
Dragon punch is yet another magic attack.

Disarm (27)
There are two major times during the game when a PvP monk will give any other class of the same level a serious beating. One of them is around level 15, when you get double attack before anyone else. The other is when you get disarm at level 27.

Flying Kick (30)
The true masters of the art can unleash devastating damage with a flying kick.

Riposte (35)
Riposte is a counter attack. If this skill succeeds, you deflect your opponent's shot and hit him instead.
