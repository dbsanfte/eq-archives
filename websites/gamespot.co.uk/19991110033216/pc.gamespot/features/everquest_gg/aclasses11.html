Creating a Character
Enchanters come from the races of the dark elf, erudite, gnome, high elf, and human. Enchanters must be intelligent and charismatic.

Getting Started
You start out with a dagger, the standard food and water, guild note, two scrolls (shallow breath and minor shielding) and the Tome of Discord.

Your first order of business is to officially join your class's guild. Read your guild note and then search around. You might want to type / to see if there are any enchanters around who can point you in the right direction. When you find your guild master, hand him the guild note and click Find to choose a few skills you wish to train in. Don't train in battle skills unless you do not have the skill listed on the right. If that is the case, just spend a single point on the skill. Train once in each of the magical skills that correspond to the starting spells (minor shielding and shallow breath; you train once in both abjure and alter).

Beginning Adventuring Tips
Being a spellcaster and a very weak wielder of any melee weapons, you must learn to keep your adversaries at a distance for as long as possible. In practical terms, it means you can cast shallow breath on your target several times before that target is within melee range, giving you at least two good attacks before you must face the target's teeth, fangs, or claws. Of course, when that target is within striking distance of your dagger, you will want to auto-attack. Keep casting your combat spells and learn the timing of casting between attacks so that you are not interrupted.

Since you have little in the way of armor, the minor shielding spell is important. Never enter combat without a shielding spell in effect.
