Basic Survival

Levels 1-3
Cast inner fire on every player you see, which will help your casting ability. It gives others just a few more hit points and makes you a popular person. Purchase burst of flame and strengthen spells as soon as you can afford them. This will make your life a bit safer. Though you might be able to go solo, when you reach level 3, you should be finding at least one partner to join you in battle.

Levels 4-7
This is the time to buy some patchwork armor after you have acquired the needed spells. This will better protect you from the creatures you will be battling. At level 5, you may purchase some new, more powerful spells. Scale skin is a good protection spell, and sicken is a damage-over-time spell you can cast on monsters at the beginning of a battle. Fleeting fury briefly gives you (or your teammates) a boost in strength, dexterity, and armor class. Frost rift is a good direct damage spell that you might want to acquire as well.

Levels 8+
At level 8, you can learn the valuable skill of meditation. This will let you recover your mana faster. At level 9, you will gain a spell that lets you summon food, so you can save your money for more spells. Get light healing and tainted breath, which poisons your victim.
