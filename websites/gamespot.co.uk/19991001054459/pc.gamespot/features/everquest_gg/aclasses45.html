Shadow Knight

A wicked and dark day it was when these foul beings first appeared in Norrath! Some say that paladins were born to oppose these dread souls. Others say that shadow knights are indeed fallen paladins. Yet all agree that these two factions detest each other, though their fates are interwoven. A knight of the shadow is a formidable foe - his command of weapons is great, as is his strength and stamina. Just as the gods of light smile upon the noble paladin, so do the gods of dark grant their follower's dreadful powers. Higher-level shadow knights are fearsome necromancers, able to summon forth undead minions and suck the life force from their opponents. Death and decay are the allies of the shadow knight, and their terrible powers reflect this in the most haunting of ways. Shadow Knights are indeed branded as "disciples of death," feared and hated by most inhabitants of Norrath. They use this fear to their advantage!

Available races

Dark elf, human, ogre, troll

Primary statistics

Strength, stamina, intelligence, charisma

Starting equipment

Short sword, Tome of Discord, guild message, five flasks of water, five loaves of bread
