The people of Norrath have long memories. Slay townsfolk or even a guard and do not expect to approach the city for a long time. Be wise and don't kill foolishly, it will follow you to your death in the end.

The following table describes how other races will treat you based on your race. Find your race along the left-hand column and move to the right to see how others will react to you in-game. There are three classifications: good, evil and attack. Good means that the other race will treat you almost as well as one of its own. Evil means that while members of a race look down on your race, they will not attack you. Expect to pay higher prices for goods. Attack means that if you come within fighting range of them, members of that race will seek to slay you on the spot. If there is no designation in a cell, it denotes good relations.
