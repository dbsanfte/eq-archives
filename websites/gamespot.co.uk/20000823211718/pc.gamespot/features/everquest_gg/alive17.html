On Death and Dying

You mean that after reading all our tips you still died? Well, don't feel bad. Even the most experienced adventurers will occasionally fall to an ambush or merely engage in a battle beyond their means. However, when you do die, there are a couple things to remember.

Finding Your Corpse
After a few minutes of load time, you will find yourself either back at your home city or at the last location you were bound to. You will notice that all the items you had in your inventory are no longer there. They are with your corpse! Characters below level 5 will be reequipped with all the starter equipment and supplies that a level 1 character is granted upon creation. The task ahead of you is to find your corpse and retrieve your articles before your corpse decays.

TIP
Remember where you were when you died. If you don't know the territory, you cannot recover your belongings.

Decay time varies based on your character's level. Level 1 corpses will decay much faster than level 30 characters. This keeps the newbie zones relatively free of excess corpses lying around, yet gives the advanced character time to retrieve his belongings from the depths of a dungeon.

You may also give another player authority to loot your corpse in the event you have a long journey back to it, or you cannot make it alone. To do this, simply type "/consent ". The person you let loot your corpse should be a true friend and will return your items.
