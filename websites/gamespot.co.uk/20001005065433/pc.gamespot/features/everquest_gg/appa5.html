Ak'Anon
Ak'Anon is nestled in the heart of the Steamfront Mountains and is home to the mechanically inclined gnomes. This is one big city of wonder, as the gnomes have created a plethora of robotic humanoids and creatures that wander the underground city and aid travelers in need. Ruled by King Akanon, the gnomish city is surrounded by active geothermal geysers, which the crafty gnomes use to power their mechanical creations.

Ak'Anon has many interesting sights to behold, starting with the huge underground lake and waterfall. The many bridges and mechanical lamps will help guide you to various portions of the underground haven. Ak'Anon is the only city in Norrath to boast its very own zoo. If you can get away from all the wonders of this city, the Steamfront Mountains also boast many highlights, which include the gnomish windmills and the ancient dragon skeleton. You better plan a good week to see all there is to behold here.
