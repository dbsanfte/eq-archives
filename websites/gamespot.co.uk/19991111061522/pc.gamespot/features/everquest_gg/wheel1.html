Hot Buttons
In windowed mode, if you look to the bottom left-hand corner, you will see a series of boxes. These six boxes are called your hot buttons. Within them, you can place buttons that you can customize to perform functions for you in the world of Norrath. You can activate the buttons by clicking on them, or you can press the corresponding number with the box in the upper left-hand corner being number 1, the one beside it being number 2, and so forth from left to right, 1 through 6.

You actually have six sets of these boxes of six for a total of 36 customizable hot buttons. You can scroll through your sets of six buttons by pressing the left or right arrow keys above the box of buttons or by pressing shift-1 through shift-6 to go to a certain set of buttons.

If you press and hold the left mouse button on an object, it can be made a button for an extended period, and a button will replace the mouse cursor. This button is what you would use for a custom command in your hot buttons. You can take this button, move it down to a position within one of your hot buttons, and place it in the box by placing the upper left hand corner of the button somewhere within the center of the box and clicking. If you try to place the button exactly within the hotkey box, it may not take. You can remove a hot button by picking it up and throwing it in the world view by clicking once. The hot buttons are fully functional from the half screen or see-through mode and let you move any commands there that you feel are missing.
