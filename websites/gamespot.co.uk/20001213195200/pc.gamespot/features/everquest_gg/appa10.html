Kaladim
Kaladim, the city of the dwarves, is nestled in the scenic Butcherblock Mountains. Carved into the mountains themselves, Kaladim is a bit rugged and dark. Taller races may get a sore back as many of the tunnels are designed for the shorter dwarves. The dwarves are quite proud of their heritage, which is apparent when you see the giant dwarven statue set atop the city gates.

The dwarven smiths are renown for their abilities, and many an adventurer will travel across the world to buy their wares. Be warned, however, quality has a price, and it's not cheap. Hot spots for metal wares include Redfist's Metal and the Everhot Forge.

Kaladim
Other sites of interest in Kaladim include the gem-filled mines and the palace of King Kazon Storm. Pub Kal offers the best ale around and can even offer some advice on brewing some spirits yourself. Regular ferry service is offered from Port Faydwer (just outside Kaladim) to Freeport.
