Chapter 2
I Dub Thee…

In the world of Norrath, you'll meet a host of wonderful characters. How these character react to you depends largely upon your character's race and profession. As in any role-playing game, character creation is one of the most important set of choices you will ever make. Building your character will involve many hours of gameplay, so you should choose wisely upon starting to avoid having to start over.

In this chapter, you'll learn how to create your character. It helps to read through this entire chapter prior to undertaking the character creation process. You'll find valuable information about attributes, classes, races, deities, and starting locations.

Character Creation

When you enter the world of Norrath for the first time, you'll be greeted by the character selection screen. You can have up to eight separate identities on one account, so after you've become more familiar with the world, you can choose to create additional characters. The first step in the character creation process is to choose the Create Character button. Now you're ready to determine what kind of character you'll create.

Before you jump in and start selecting, you should review the character attributes that are available for modification and think about what combination of race and class you wish to be.

Attributes
Attributes are broken into two categories: primary and secondary. The primary attributes are the brighter colored statistics displayed in your character creation window and are the most important to the class chosen. The secondary attributes are the less bright ones that still effect your character, but are not as important to your class.

Once you've decided upon your race and class, you have a group of bonus points to spend, located at the top of your attributes column. The attributes cannot be lowered beyond their initial values, but can be raised as high as desired. We recommend that you focus on your primary attributes, as increasing those will be most beneficial to your character's profession.
