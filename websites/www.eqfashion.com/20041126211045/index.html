1 Year Anniversary - March 26, 2003 by Hauki
Today marks Eqfashion's 1 year anniversary, thanks goes out to everyone who uses the site. We have lots of new exciting features planned, stay tuned!

Last night we modified the new user account creation process, a valid email address is now required to create an account on this site. This was implemented in order to put a stop to people creating fake accounts to "vote bump" their images to the top of the list. We also added the ability to ban accounts based on abusive behavior (i.e. vote bumping). One thing currently in the works is updating the forum software by getting rid of EZBoard and moving to something hosted locally on our server, there's no use in paying EZBoard to remove ads when we can run our own software here and not have any extra costs involved.

Anyways, thanks again for using the site, if you have any comments, suggestions, whatever's, send them to us at info@eqfashion.com.

Equipment Database and Frogs - March 22, 2003 by Hauki
I just finished cleaning up the equipment database. Hopefully all of the duplicate entries have been removed. I’d like to thank the people who renamed the duplicates; it made the process much easier. If you find duplicate items listed, please either rename them to “duplicate” or let me know via email so I can remove them.

With the addition of Froglok as a playable race a problem has arisen here at EQF. None of the current items in the db are listed as Froglok usable. It’s going to take some time before everything is listed as Froglok usable. In the mean time… If you’re uploading an image of a Frog, please check to see if your items are listed in the DB, if they are go to the edit section of the item and add a check mark into the Froglok box and click the “submit” button at the bottom.
