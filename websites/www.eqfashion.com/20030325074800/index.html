Equipment Database and Frogs - March 22, 2003 by Hauki
I just finished cleaning up the equipment database. Hopefully all of the duplicate entries have been removed. I’d like to thank the people who renamed the duplicates; it made the process much easier. If you find duplicate items listed, please either rename them to “duplicate” or let me know via email so I can remove them.

With the addition of Froglok as a playable race a problem has arisen here at EQF. None of the current items in the db are listed as Froglok usable. It’s going to take some time before everything is listed as Froglok usable. In the mean time… If you’re uploading an image of a Frog, please check to see if your items are listed in the DB, if they are go to the edit section of the item and add a check mark into the Froglok box and click the “submit” button at the bottom.

Broken Images - September 15, 2002 by Hauki

Server Wide Chat - August 10, 2002 by Hauki
A couple of weeks ago Verant opened up server wide chat and today I had the thought of starting an eqfashion chat channel. I added "eqfashion" to my /autojoin on both of my accounts, so if anyone wishes to contact me in game, please do so by typing the following: /join serverwide.eqfashion If you want this to auto-join when you load up your character use the following: /autojoin serverwide.eqfashion See you in game!
