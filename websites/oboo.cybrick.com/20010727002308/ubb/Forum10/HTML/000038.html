Here's the deal on soulstones needed to port to Hate;

Spell-Alter Plane: Hate
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Hate, home of Dark Elf lord Innoruuk. Component needed: Fuligan Soulstone of Innoruuk. You can only purchase the Soulstone from within the Cleric guildhall in Neriak's Third Gate. The Souldstones cost approximately 250pp. Depending on your god/diety, most light races are KOS in this area of Neriak.

As far as a map after porting up Ell, check out the link and follow the blue line to the wall break camp out site. Just remember its a series of right turns. Once you see the wall after your 3rd right turn out of the portal site, just get halfway on the wall, take a right and just follow that wall. Last time up with Maeven, I sat on the camp site. Got killed when moving around, but most saw my sorry butt sitting there and camped at the right spot. Also, rogues should be watching your progress, and will let you know if your going the wrong way once you get on the wall. Just remember to never, and I mean never, train mob to camp site. If you get attacked on the run, get a location and just die. Thats why we have rogues up there, to do the scouting and cr's. The are the only class that can truely run around unseen (for the most part) in Hate.

Ahhh good map Karbo, that's the one I was looking for Please EVERYONE who hasn't been there take a look at the map. I've been up there about 10 times and the break can still be confusing.

Sabrar, don't worry about the stones, I believe Ari was going to buy them, but if not someone else will. The cost will be made up through money items and/or gems that we loot over the course of the night.

Just a few pointers for any first timers:

1) Like I said, the break can be confusing, look at the map before you go. If you go running the wrong way and die it may be difficult to find your corpse.

2) Clerics MUST live at all costs!!! This includes the break-in and the entire night.

3) During the break-in you will probably die! Make sure your cleric makes it to camp if at all possible. I've died about 7 out of 10 times on the break, so don't be surprised. Lellie on the other hand has made it like 8 out of 10 times /boggle Hehe some of it is good luck

4) If we get overrun by too many mobs during the night, the raid leaders may yell for the clerics to camp. This means CLERICS ONLY!! It also means that if you aren't a cleric YOU ARE ABOUT TO DIE!! Run around and beat on and taunt as many mobs as possible to get them off the clerics so they can camp.

5) There will be a main assist and a secondary assist, make hot-keys for them both and ONLY attack when assist is called. Even if you see one beating on your cleric, trust the chanters, that's what they are there for.

6) The initial camp will probably be on the wall which is actually pretty steep. Don't go more than halfway up this wall as new mobs have been added to the second floor that will DT you if agro'd. Wait for assist to be called, run down the wall and fight, then get back up on the wall as soon as the fight is over. Not sure what Maev and Ari want to do here since the Hate changes, we may just stay on the floor at the base of the wall.

7) If you get feared wait until it wears off, stop and turn around to make sure you aren't agro'd by anything, then make yer way back to the camp. If you are agro'd and train the camp you will possibly kill everyone there. Stay there, get a loc, and die!!

8) Pay attention to the raid leaders when they shout loot out. You will need to send a tell to whoever is doing the rolls if a piece that you need drops. Sometimes this is done during battle so pay attention. If you miss out on a roll it's your fault.

Hehe okies, that's all the advice I can give, wish I had it when I went up the first time Other than that lets have some fun and rock this place

Lol...How come you die some much Braskers?

I have prolly 10 to 12 trips up to Hate so far, died the first couple of times until I learned the correct route to the camp site.

All of Brasks tips are right on the money. Here's a tip you should know; once your camped and come back, cancel SoW. Imagine feared with SoW on and with SoW off..Big diff, huh.

Just a few other things:

NO PETS. Period. Pets tend to roam, and during the action, and you'll see plenty of that, you tend to lose control of summoned pets. Don't worry, if the Maestro is up, you'll get a chance to put a pet into action. We're not talking about Shaman pets, more on the lines of mage pets.

When casting on mob, most times its better to turn your back on the baddie to cast. Don't ask me why, I'm just a dumb warrior. But for some reason it helps. Prolly cause of all the other DD's smacking it. This falls in the "Assist" key catagory.

Lastly, in all the times I've been to Hate, there hasn't been one time that I haven't been rezzed or a CR been done. The key is to have fun, kid around a bit, enjoy the ride, and don't expect any "uber" drops to come your way. That way, if you DO win some uber gear, your like ALRIGHT!!!!!, and if you don't, its no big deal, you visited Hate and lived to tell about it. Hell, as many times as I've been up, I've got two drops to my name. But its a great way to meet other people from other guilds. I've yet to go up and not have a good time.
You'll do well grasshopper...Lol...And soon, you'll become a Hate veteran and be able to talk trash like me..Lol
Now Fear, hmmm, like I really want to visit that place...
