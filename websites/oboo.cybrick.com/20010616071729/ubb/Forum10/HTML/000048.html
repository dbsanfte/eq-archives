Ornate runed shield will be randomed to INT casters who can use it (except me, since I have one). If a second one drops (which means no torch), I’ll decide whether to random it or hold for guildbank instead of weapon.

Now I know both shields are uber items and are worth about 30k or more. But we need funds for guildbank if we want to have future raids and not much money is rolling in. Either one will cover us for a long while, enough to cover cost of Peridots, Hate stones, coffins, etc. Also gives us a little leeway should members need a loan.
