A dark figure walks into Sotarsh's Bar.
Evil exudes from her lithe body in waves so thick that even the mighty Xixx shudders.
Shrouded in black robes which swirl around with a life of their own, she extends her arm and one glimpses skin the deepest of blue. Throwing her hood back from her face, she turns eyes devoid of life upon those gathered in companionable silence as she points at the Bears gathered around.
"Mighty Oboo," she seemed to scream, yet it came across as a mere whisper, "feel the wrath of an ancient power unlike you have ever seen."
With that the dark elf let the magic flow from within her as flames shot forth from her lithe fingertips and touched upon the bar in random spots. No Bear was safe from her wrath nor was there a spot in the Bar that was a safe haven.
The stench of acrid smoke filled the room and as quickly as it was started, it was over. Raising her hands above her head, the slim figure gathered her dark forces around her and vanished as quickly as she had come.
Emotyn looked around from under her overturned table to make sure that her brothers and sister Bears were not in dire need of her healing touch as she pondered what had just happened, or whom the figure was. Looking for Lirah, who had been playing her delightful music, she quickly found that the bard seemed to be unscathed.
Speaking softly, yet allowing her voice to be heard over the confusion, she asked....
"By M'Lord Bertoxxullous and all that is evil, anyone know what the hell just happened here?"
