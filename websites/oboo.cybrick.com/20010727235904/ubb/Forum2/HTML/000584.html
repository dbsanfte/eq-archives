Ok, so I'm finally up to level 52, I've got cash in the bank and it's time to start looking for some new items. Guess it kinda hit me when I bumped into a lev 35 warrior the other day that laughed and said "Wow, I've never seen a plus 50 warrior still wearing Crafted!" Haha so anyways I'm looking through the lists at all the wonderful goodies and trying to pick out some of the stuff that would enhance my stats the best, and something kinda struck me as funny: Isn't it amazing what ends people will go to, and how much money they'll spend on items but they won't neccessarily go out of their way to make sure a Shaman is in the group at all times? They have so much to offer and I don't think people truly appreciate what they're getting when they see those buff symbols start to pop up on their screen.

In addition to the classics ( Sow, UV, Regen, etc... ) There are so many other nice buffs they have to offer as well.

Say for example I put together this ensemble:
2 Forest Loops +3 Wis +3 Dex +3 Agi = 2000 PP
Treeweave BP +25 AC +10 STR = 4000 PP
Hero Bracer +9 AC +10 STR = 2000 PP
FBSS Effect of Haste = 8000 PP
Cloak of Flames +10 AC +9 Dex +9 Agi +50 HP +15 FR = 20000 PP

For a grand total of 36000 PP I just bought myself +20 Str +15 Agi +15 Dex +10 AC +50 HP +15 FR and the best haste item out there. Not too bad right???

Now if I just bought a set of FS plate mail without any of the stat bonuses the AC would be slightly less and it would cost me around 2000 PP.

Now for the amazing part...Here's a full line of FREE Shammie buffs:

Dexterity +50
Strength +67
Agility +45
Guardian +23 AC
Talisman of Altuna +250 HP
Stamina +40
All Resistances +40
Alacrity Haste Buff

Pretty amazing when you actually look at the numbers!

So anyways, if you haven't yet, give your shammy a hug today and save some of that PP
