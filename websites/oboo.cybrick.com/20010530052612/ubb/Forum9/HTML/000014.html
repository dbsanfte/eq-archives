Ok folks we are going back to paw again, and this time we are going do it up right! So on 04/01/01 at 5:30pm EST we are going to play a April Fools Day joke on all the foo's in Paw! This raid should last about 3 hours in length.

For this raid I want 2 groups and more if we have more intrest. Here is the group assingments. I would also like one person to step up and help me with leading this raid.

ROLES
1) The shaman from group 1 will be on slow duty
2) Sabrar will be pulling
3) The main tank from group 2 will be peeling, and will be main assist
4) Tank 1 from group 1 will be secondary assist

MAPS ( goto the following links for the map of paw and mob locations )
http://members.spree.com/sip/lordkhan/Maps/paw.html
http://www.lares.dti.ne.jp/~h-masa/grph/PAW.gif

RULES
1) Stay behind me at all times unless I say not to.
2) Myself, the puller, and the two main assists will be using shout so keep all comments in /ooc.
3) LISTEN to my instructions.
4) Do not loot a corpse unless you are told to do so.
5) Be on time. Therefor if the raid starts at 5:30pm EST try to be at paw at 5pm. If you cant make on time, but would still like to go, tell me ahead of time so I can make arrangements.

LOOT ( goto the following link for an idea of what can be found in paw ) http://eqdb.allakhazam.com/itemzone.html?zone=21

Just to let everyone know some items are already spoken for, which are the following
1) Summon corpse scrolls. We have two necros in the guild that need these scrolls therefore the first two drops will be saved for them ( even if they are not at the raid ).
2) Robe of Ishva Mal. If this robe drops it will be going to Kibray as my way of saying thank you for her support for the past few raids she has helped us with.

Everything else will be distributed among all persons present on a NBG basis

RAID PLAN
So everyone has an idea of how I plan to do this I will lay out my steps
1) Push through to the double doors.
2) From double doors we will clear to the next set of doors.
3) Work our way to the jail entrance.
4) Clear area from jail entrance to the bridge
5) Clear the bridge and the opposite side.
6) From the bridge we will take a swandive into the waters below and swim to the LEFT.
7) Clear all snakes from the waters.
8) Then we swim through a small channel and pop up into the excutioners room.
9) From the exc. spawn we move up and take out the first named spawn.
10) From here we then begin to clear the dungeon in a clockwise fashion.
11) When we reach the well we will swim up and clear the area surrounding Ishva Mal.
12) When we reach Ishva Mal Groups 2 and 3 will be released to Evac. One evac person needs to return to Evac Group 1 out when we are finished with the camp of Ishva Mal.

THINGS TO EXPECT
If you are new to raiding or new to me at least let me set establish what to expect. One dont expect to get experince. Sometimes you end up in a exp group and sometimes you dont. Raids like this are not for exp but to obtain tools and gear for our future outings. Dont expect to get a PHAT ITEM everytime you come on a raid. Personally I would love to hand everyone a nice item, but it doesnt always work that way.

Last bring a good attitude. Nothing sours a raid faster than one person who is disgruntled about the way things are going. When you are positive it affects the people around you, and improves their outlook. Enjoy the fact that you are among friends and guildmates, doing something that you could not accomplish on your own.
