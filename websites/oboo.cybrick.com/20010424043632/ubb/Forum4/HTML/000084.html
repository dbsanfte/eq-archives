The trouble with the colors is actually getting them.

Black and Rust are VERY rare to get the item to make it with (I've spent 4 straight hours trying to farm it and came up with 1 black and 2 rust) They are found of any elemental (Najena, Steamfont, The Hole) Charcole and Russet Oxide

Yellow isn't so bad, you just have to be patient. (randomly spawns on the ground in the fairy city in lfay, comes of scarcrows) Jackolantern Fungus

Red is EASY! Though a healer would be nice for getting it. (randomly spawns on the ground at the fairy city in lfay, found on Fungus in innothule swamp and upper guk, holding the fungus room with a healer is super easy can can produce enough red in a few hours for an entire suit)

Blue.. found in permafrost.. that should say it all. Permafrost Crystals I believe is the item.

Green.. good luck.. ground spawn in Greater Faydark.. completely random.

That's all the colors I can think of right now, there might be a few more.

You use the item with Alchemy or Make poison along with a flask of water and.. 1 other item.. to make an Extract (trivial at 36)

Place the extra in a pottery made Medium Clay Jar along with 1, 2, or 3 resins (dark, medium, and light color respecticely) for the actual Dye (this cannot fail)

Then comes the actual smithing using the piece of armor and the dye. You would need to ask a smith about this part of the process.
