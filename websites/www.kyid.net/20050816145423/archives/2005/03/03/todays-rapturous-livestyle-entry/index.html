Today’s rapturous livestyle entry

I kept up my 4/1 walk/run intervals today. By the end I was feeling some pain just above my knees. I guess in the area where my quads come into the knee area. It’s kind of a dull ache. Hopefully it will go away by tomorrow.

I think I botched my diet a bit today. I added to much extra-virgin olive oil to my lunch and could only eat about half of it, and by dinner time I was very hungry and decided to snack on some cheese, and ate about 4cm off the block before I stopped. I then made roasted vegetables, but again added too much oil, so I didn’t eat them all… bleh.

I’m gonna have to watch how much olive oil I splash around.
