Shaun of the Dead

This movie rates a 6.5/10.

A man decides to turn his moribund life around by winning back his ex-girlfriend, reconciling his relationship with his mother and dealing with an entire community that has returned from the dead to eat the living.

Shaun of the Dead was a bit of a let-down to me. Given what the trailers were like I was expecting something that would play as a commedy/farce the whole way through. Instead there seemed to be these disjointed moments when the writer was trying to make what could only be described as dramatic moments throughout the film. It just didn’t work as far as I was concerned. If you want to be dramatic in a zombie film, then something like Dawn of the Dead would be a better approach.

Despite my disappointment in Shaun of the Dead, I do think there were some extremely funny sequences in the film. One of the best moments was when the slacker friend get’s a phone call on his cell while the group is being surround by zombies, and tells the group, “Just a moment”, and sits down to talk on the phone like nothing is wrong. I think I projected too much expectation of all-out comedy on the film, which is why I still think it is worth seeing. Rent it.
