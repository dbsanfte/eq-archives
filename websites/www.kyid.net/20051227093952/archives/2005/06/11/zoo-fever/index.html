Zoo Fever!

I spent over 4 hours at Assiniboine Park today. The bulk of the my time was spent at the zoo photographing animals. I got over 200 photos from inside the zoo itself. I then walked over to the Leo Mol Sculpture Garden and burned the rest of the memory on my photo card (1 GB of photos!).

I then met my mom, my sister and her boyfriend at a Dim Sum restaurant downtown for a late lunch. And while at the restaurant I managed to find about 20 photos I could safely delete off the memory card so I could take a few photos at lunch.

After lunch and after we all parted ways, I wandered around the area taking pictures until I filled my memory card up again, and then I came home.

I have already whittled down the zoo photos a bit. I am down to about 163 now I think. I am going see if I can get down to under 50 before the end of tomorrow so I can do processing on them over the course of the week. I might start slowly uploading images a couple at a time instead of just a big bulk load when I have them all processed.

I’m beat! I think I will go see Mr. & Mrs. Smith tonight.
