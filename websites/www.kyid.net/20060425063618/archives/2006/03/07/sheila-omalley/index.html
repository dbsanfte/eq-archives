Sheila O’Malley

The Sheila Variations is a blog that I read off and on. Sheila is rather amusing on a frequent basis. Not too long ago she wrote a post over the course of a couple weeks (I think) about the movie “While You Were Sleeping“. I thought it was a very, very fun read, and decided to buy her something on her Amazon Wishlist as a thank-you for, well, just being her.

She got the book yesterday, and sent me a thank-you email, and then mentioned that I bought the book in one of her blog posts. Ok, she doesn’t mention me by name… but I know I bought that book. I’m glad she enjoyed the gift!
