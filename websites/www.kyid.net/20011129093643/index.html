Icelight Divine
Posted Wednesday, November 28, 2001 @ 13:32 by Raene
Monday night members of Lumiere Divine and Clan Icewind went iceskating outside Dragon Necropolis. Amid the holiday cheer, Bing Crosby music, and gnome-shaped cookies blundered a Grinch in the form of Klandicar. After declining our invitation to come skate with us, we sent him packing.

With Klandicar gone to his Aunt's for the night, LD and CIW went caroling in ToV. Telk was the only one to take exception to our singing (pssst Myrrh...sing on key) and we soon hooked up Telk with music appreciation lessons as well.

Last night members of LD and CIW decked the halls of Kael. We trimmed a huge tree that oddly enough, looked like a dead King Tormax with evergreen boughs sticking out of his clothing. The lights and merriment attracted the Vindicator's attention, and he umm... tripped and fell, 27 times... over the presents for the tree. Turns out this reporter later found the presents to be wrapped giants from Kael. No wonder they were so large!
