Soreness

I just got back from the gym a little while ago (maybe 15 minutes). I have today off work, so I decided to go a bit later than normal. My legs felt fairly normal when I went today. I continued my 4/1 walk/run intervals, but screwed up my count on the last interval which left me with 6 minutes of walking, and I didn’t like that, so I did a 2/1/3 walk/run/walk for my last interval. That combined with the fact I was pushing myself to run just slightly faster than I have been resulted in me being sweaty at the end, which I suppose is a good thing. It however did take me until I was finished changing back into my street clothes to stop breathing heavy.

Oh, and back to my legs. They are sore again (dull ache) above the knees/lower quad area. I am hoping it only the muscles telling me “WTF - you haven’t excercised in years?!?” and that my quads get over it soon!

I have only had a big glass of Low Sodium V8 to drink so far today, so I am going to make myself a late breakfast after I post this. I am thinking omlette with asparagus, green onion, tomato, green pepper, a little shredded cheese, and perhaps some hot sauce. Throw in a couple slices of Canadian Back Bacon and I will be good to go.

I am also going to try and make a point of blogging what I am eating a bit more closely from now on. Perhaps I will even make a special category for it.

Oh yeah, and against my better judgement, I weighed myself today after getting out of the shower this morning…

233.6 lbs.

Gah, now I know why it is a bad idea to weigh too often.
