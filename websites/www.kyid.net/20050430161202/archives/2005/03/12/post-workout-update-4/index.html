Post Workout Update

I have had a sore achilles/lower calf since yesterday. This morning when I woke up it had tightened up and was very sore. I decided to go ahead with my workout anyways in the hopes that between my calf stretches and my walking that it would loosen up and not bother me. It didn’t quite pan out that way. When I did my calf stretches I could really feel how tight the achilles area was, and when I started walking I could tell I was instinctively trying to limp slightly to keep some pressure off that area. I went ahead with the job portion of my intervals, and the first one was extremely painful. I did figure out that if I shortened my stride there wasn’t as much pain. So that is how i did the run portion of my intervals - baby steps jogging.

I followed the intervals I have been doing since Wednesday; that is, 3/2, 3/2, 3/2, 3/2, 2/3, 5/0.

I am also sore above the knees (as per usual). The achilles area feels very tender at the moment. If it doesn’t feel better by tomorrow I may not do a workout on Sunday.
