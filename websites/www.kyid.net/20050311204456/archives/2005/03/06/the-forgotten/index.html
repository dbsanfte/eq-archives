The Forgotten

This movie rates a 5/10.

A grieving mother, Telly Parada, is struggling to cope with the loss of her 9-year-old son. She is stunned when her psychiatrist and her husband tell her that she has created eight years of memories of a son she never had. But when she meets the father of one of her son’s friend who is having the same experience, Telly embarks on a mission to prove her son’s existence and her sanity.

The soundbite above had me thinking this movie might be interesting to watch, and it was, for a while.

The Forgotten was fairly enjoyable until the halfway point, at which point it takes a sharp turn south and never really gets back on an even keel. I will spare the details to avoid giving away the plot, but suffice to say, this should be a cheap night rental.
