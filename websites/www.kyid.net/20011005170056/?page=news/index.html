Early Trick or Treat
Posted Sunday, September 16, 2001 @ 8:43 by Raene


As you, dear reader, are well aware; the shops of Norrath bulge with sweets and confections the likes of which dance in young children's dreams and rot their teeth. Encouraged by this early display, last night Lumiere Divine and friends went on an early trick-or-treat.

Wherelse to start the evening but Hate? Lumiere knocked on the door of every denizen. Clunkk, dressed up like Charlie Brown, was reported to have said, "I got a bag of rocks". Aamria got a new robe, Lunna received a shiny purple BP, Hands of Maestro to Maskiim and Monalisa, but Frawdy Rentedfingers was the big winner of the evening with his Magi Staff! Under one of the welcome mats we also found a Decrepit Hide which members are planning to dangle in front of Ranan to see how fast a troll can *really* react.

After Hate, we knocked on the door of one of Velious's premiere citizens: Velketor the Sorceror. He invited us in rather coldly. When he ringed Clunkk's neck with rosemary and mint jelly tho, pushing him into his oven, we knew enough was enough. It was a long fight, but in the end on our very first try, we took Velketor out.



Loot was Crown of Confusion (grats Dara!!), Velketor's Spell Book, Gloves of Bladecalling (Grats Aaeamdar!) and Bracer of Frostbite.

From there members trooped on over to Lord DijonMustard, and he offered us a flurry of words about trick-or-treating in September. It was all for naught tho :) Grats Wung on your frozen booties!

In an unrelated story, Clunkk has declared himself King and made Kentt his new General and right-hand man.


(Yes we realize Kentt is at Clunkk's left hand...but would *you* tell an Ogre that?)
