Strength - Your Strength will not affect you at all at low levels. Since you wont be doing more damage and you wont be able to carry more then 14 pounds without taking penalties. However starting level 20 or so you will start doing a bit more damage then Monks with less Strength.

Stamina - You will get about 1 hp every level for each 5 points of Stamina. Not a very bad investment, but most people prefer to spend their bonus points on Agility.

Agility - Your Agility affects your starting AC. It will also affect how much AC the armor you are wearing adds. But more importantly, it affects your chances of dodging/blocking/riposting a blow. This will be more and more important as you level up, but will still benefit you at low levels.

Dexterity - High Dexterity increases your chances of hitting your enemy, as well as inflicting more damage on average (it will NOT increase your MAX damage, but it will make you come close to your max damage more often). Dexterity will also make you duel wield and double attack more often.
