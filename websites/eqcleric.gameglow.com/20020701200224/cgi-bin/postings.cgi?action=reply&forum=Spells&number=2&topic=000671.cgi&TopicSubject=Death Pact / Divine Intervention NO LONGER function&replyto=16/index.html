CayleuActually, DI is QUITE useful, if you know when to use it.

IT is only used for endgame encounters that will be difficult. For fights that my guild has trivialized I dont bother. For exceptionally difficult fights, I will have an enchanter charisma-buff the tanks who will be getting DI'd (if multiple clerics will DI different tanks). The raid leader gives the clerics a one-minute warning, at which point we DI our tanks. That gives the tank about 9-ish minutes of DI for the difficult fight with maybe a 50-50 chance of it firing if he gets dangerously low.

Most of the time it is a waste of 2 emeralds, but occasionally it will save the raid. Just dont use it for trivial encounters, use it for MT fights where there is a fair chance of losing.
