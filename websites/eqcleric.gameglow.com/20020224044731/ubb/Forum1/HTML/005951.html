My main character is Maandinka, a Level 42 Paladin of Mithaniel Marr on the Tribunal Server. She is also my first EQ character. As a young Paladin roaming EC and NRO I don't even remember the weapon I was using in my early teens.

One Afternoon a great and mighty Paladin approached me and offered to give me a Bone Bladed Claymore. I thought I had died and gone to heaven.

After I upgraded to a Gigantic Zweihander I seached out a newbie paladin and offer him the BBC in the same fashion it had been offered to me. Now, I frequently give things away such as Jagged Bands, Embroidered Black Capes, Thick Leather Aprons, etc. What I try to do is find a nice newbie..LVL 15 or lower...check his equipment (it's usually quite obvious if he is a twink or not) and offer a nice item. It helps if they role play...I'm much more generous. I know the stuff I'm giving away isn't the greatest stuff but to a real newbie it's can be the nicest item they have...and it reminds me of how proud I was when I first wielded my BBC!
