Okay I've done this once so I'll help you out with the ordering. You DON't click give at the same time. Just make sure that no one else is standing around with the OFF chance that they will hand something in and screw up the quest. But anyway. The person that should get the NO DROP item hands there items in LAST.

I did it with my Cleric(lvl 50) and my Necro (level 9)(two different accounts) to get the Words of Darkness book for my necro.

I handed in the Book of shadow with my cleric and got the note, then handed in the Book of Darkness, and the book of frost and the note (all with my cleric) as soon as I did this the NPC responded that "Solusek Does not reward half deeds" (or something like this).

Then I handed in the 300 gold with my necro and poof my necro had a nice little NO DROP open book for his off hand.

Yes it works well, (I have heard rumors that you can't do it if the person that gave the original items is no longer online/in the zone so keep that in mind if you want to do it for a twink on the same account (it might not work).
