Trying to piece together all the info I have found here and elsewhere, so please forgive and correct any mistakes here on this thread.

There are two factions, the Triumvirate and Plasmatic Priesthood. One worships fire one ice. It would apear that the Plasmatic Priests are after the goblin king.

1. For now Shmendrik Lavawalker seems to be starting point of our quest.(BTW his spawn time is 3hrs) in Lake Rathe in the entrance to the tunnel ( 3600, 0 ).

2. You need to kill Shmendrik to trigger elemental to spawn. Kill elemental and loot damaged crown. Alternatively, you can spawn Natasha in step 4 and kill the elemental before it attacks her, but this is safer so she doesn't kill the elemental and poof the crown.

3. Kill Lord Bergurgle and loot Lord Bergurgle Crown. Lord berg spawns in lake rathe in the under water tunnel where the wizzie rod for the staff of the wheel used to be. on top of the tower there, there are 3 new spawns 2 on the sides are on a 6 minute timer the one in the middle is on a 28-30 minutes timer, and is the PH for berg, who is a rather rare spawn.

4. Give Bergurgle crown to Shmendrik which triggers Natasha Whitewater to spawn and she will kill both Shmendrik and elemental. Shmendrik also gives you a potion.

5.Give damaged crown from elemental to Natasha in reply she gives you some shell and ask you to give it to Omat Vastsea (in Timorous Deep -12000 , -2000 )

6.After you give shell to Omat he gives you a statue and asks to locate one guy who is meeting in Temple of Solusek Ro with Lord Gimblox of the Fire Peak, give him a statue which is supposed to hinder his power and then eliminate him. He asks you to bring him his robe as proof.

7. Lord Gimblox spawns in SolA zone in Fire Goblin King room. He has a PH - large fire goblin (new mob). Spawn time is around 40min. He is easy to solo - only around 1200HP. Drops a no drop signet ring.

8. Sol Ro. In the throne room upstairs you'll find a keeper center, keeper right, and a seeker female on the left.. durring EQ daytime she despawns and respawns as a Male. He responds to anything containing "plasmatic priest". Have a nice group handy, when you turn in the idol to this guy, you get a nice exp boost.. and he turns into "a Plasmatic Priest" and promptly attacks you and your group. He is level 55, couldn't touch a group of 6. He dropped A Bloodstained Plasmatic Priest Robe.. not equipable.

9. Return the robe to Omat, which causes Natasha to spawn next to him in Timorous Deep. Then give Gimblox's ring to Natasha... and note: giving the shell that you get from Natasha to Naxton Deepwater(next part of the quest) will spawn a lvl 56(?) elemental in Burning Woods that hits for around 600+ and anasty fire proc also.(note: Naxton Deepwater will continue to suicide upon the elemental aslong as hes spawned) So when you turn it in, be prepared.

Notes about Zordak Ragefire, the npc in SolB which might be part of quest -

It seems that Zoldak Ragefire is NOT spawned from the death of Nagafen, rather is triggered when the last FG's are killed before Nagafen's lair. He spawned immediately once the last few FG's (in the 4 spawn) were killed, may be triggered off Skarlon's death.
