Perhaps this topic has been addressed elsewhere. If so, please let me know where, so I can read it. =)

I have made it a practice to charge for my Reses.

My standard fees are:
50pp if the player's corpse is brought to me.
100pp if I must go to the corpse.
50pp for every zone I must cross to get to the corpse. If a port is prearranged by the player seeking the Res, then distance is calculated from the zone of the porting destination to the zone of the corpse.

Fees are not aplicable to group members that were slain during battle, and other clerics (time permiting).
(Of course, there are always exceptions. I have been known to wander across a corpse when I was not busy at the time, and offer to XP res the player on the spot free of charge.)

I decided to establish this "standard" fee, mostly due to the standard fee of 10pp per person established by druids/wizards for ports. My decision for the base 50pp charge was in direct compairison to the 50pp made by a druid/wizard in porting 5 other players per port.

Two of the biggest gripes players have with EQ is the amount of time it takes to crosss the lands, and the amount of time lost via player death.

It seems to me that the long held debate about anon/un-anon would be greatly diminished if the EQ player community expected to pay a "standard fee" for XP reses, just like they expect to pay for a port.

    I never charge for a rez. Most people will either /tell me "am paying XXX for a rez - can you help me?" or will /ooc to the zone "paying XXX for a rez".

    My rezzing policy: If I am heading your way, I will rez you no questions asked. If I am not, then I will ask you to provide transportation TO and FROM the rez location.

    I will also expect the corpse to be at the zoneline. Do not ask me to enter a zone or dungeon to rez you. If you can't get your corpse to the zone, then your problems lie deeper than just a rez request. I do not follow people deep in a dungeon when I'm not with my friends. . I can't tell you HOW many times I've eaten it while doing a rez inside a dangerous area. Please do us the favor and drag your corpse to the zone to expedite the rez.

    I do not ask people for money. I simply tell them "I don't charge for rezzes. You may donate if you so desire."

    Then they ask me "how much?".

    And my 100% foolproof reply - "Whatever you think this service is worth to you".

    The human heart is boundless in its compassion for others when someone helps you out of a difficult situation. It is during these times when you will receive some of your most VALUABLE donations. I once was given 2000 plat for a donation after doing two rezzes in an ugly Kunark dungeon. That is no lie.

    You will get stiffed occasionally, receiving the "All I have on me is 11 plat, is that enough?" "Yes, Thank you" scenarios. They are one end of the bell-curve. After all is said and done you will have made one more friend, you will have brightened someone's already gloomy night and you will walk away a few coins heavier.

    As a role-playing cleric on Quellious, that is the absolute BEST thing that I can hope for.
