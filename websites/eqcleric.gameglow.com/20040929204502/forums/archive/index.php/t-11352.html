Actually, it's simpler then it sounds, and quite a common situation in an experience grind. If my warrior has 3000 hit points, the most you will be able to heal her for is (.75 * 3000) = 2250 hit points. So, if she is at 25% of health, you'll heal her to full. If she's at 10%, you'll take her to 85% of health, or good enough since regen is on her, with a 2250 heal. The hard hit point cap doesn't come into play until the warrior has enough hit points where 75% of total hitpoints is more then 2925 (in other words, more then 3900 hitpoints), AND that warrior is below 25% health. So, take a warrior with 4000 hit points, and heal at 1 bubble, and you'll hit that hard cap, but still be close enough to full as to make no nevermind. It doesn't really make a significant difference until you're hitting raid level hitpoints (6 or 7k), and most CH chains average a 3K heal anyway……..
