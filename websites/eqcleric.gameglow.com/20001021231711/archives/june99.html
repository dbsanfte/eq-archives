New Spell List

The new spell list is now up (see Cleric Spells under Specific Topics).  It is a script, with functionality similar to that found in the Magic Items script.  The Cleric Spells script has the following features:

  1. Contains a complete list of all clerical spells (at least those found in Kaladim).
  2. Contains the mana cost and the approximate price.  The test character used to check pricing was a Dwarven Cleric with 70 Charisma (25 higher than normal).  Normal Dwarven clerics should expect to pay a little more; Human and High-Elven clerics can probably expect to pay a little less.
  3. The list can be displayed for all or just one levels.
  4. The spells can be cross-referenced with other classes, showing the level another class gets the spell.  Either all other classes or just one can be displayed.

Coming soon will be the actual effects of each spell.

Please send me any comments/suggestions you have for this feature.

June 10

Site created. 

Content currently contains the following:

  • Four player created guides to zones.
  • Links to popular Everquest sites.
  • Four maps (zones on Faydwer).
  • A link to the EQ'Lizer's list of Clerical spells.
  • A link to the mana calculator contained on the EQ Enchanter's site.
  • A link to the cleric guides contained on EQSS.  The advanced guide is particularly noteworthy.

Concerning the maps: The four maps of Faydwer are not my original creations; they were copied and modified from maps found on the EQ'Lizer site.  I modified them so that a printed image would fit on an 8 1'2" x 11" sheet of paper.  I personally believe they are more useful this way.

For a much more complete listing of maps, I recommend both the Eq'Lizer and Allakhazam's Magical Realm.  They both have a wide variety of maps, although Allakhazam's is more useful due to the listing of multiple versions of some of the zones.   Surprisingly enough, I find the Japanese versions of the zone maps the most useful, despite the language issue, due to the excellent attention to detail.
