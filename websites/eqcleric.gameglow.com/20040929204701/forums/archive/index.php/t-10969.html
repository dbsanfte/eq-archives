I have a small guild and we're trying to figure out how best to use our resources in battles. I've written a program to analyze fights. So far, it looks at the battle round by round and summarizes how much damage someone takes before they get healed.

It also looks at the battle in N second intervals and tries to figure out how much damage the mob dishes out in those intervals (helping to get cheal timing right).

Any suggestions for other ways to look at the data? It's been a really fun project. I ran it on a Velks fight we did and you can
really see how Velks doesn't do anything the first minute or so of battle, which suggests that having a defensive warrior early on might not be the best thing if the battle goes on longer than 3 minutes.
