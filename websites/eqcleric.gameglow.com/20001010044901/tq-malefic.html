Solusek Ro Cleric Armor Quests

Things you should know before starting any of the quests.

1.      The NPCs you are told to kill may or may not have the quest items on them every time.

2.      Some of the camps can be very long.

3.      Two of the quests have Armor that is NO DROP.

4.      All Quest Items are NO DROP. (Icons & Ingots)

5.      The Cleric armor is not the best, by the time you can do all the quests you will probably all ready have much better armor.

6.      Before giving any of your quest items to the NPCs double check to be sure you give them what they want.

7.      Unlike other quests you get NO exps. for completing the quests.

Quest No. 1 Regis the Reverent Quest for the Bracer

  • Go to Regis the Reverent's Girl friend a Gypsy named Lianna Ferasa she is found in the Rathe Mtns (Loc 3982.16, 1464.10). Ask her "What has become of Regis" She will tell you that Regis was slain by Zahal the Vile. 

  • She wants you to go to North Karana and kill Zahal (he is a level 16 Ghoul) to free the soul of Regis and return her Flute to her. Zahal can be found over by the Treant in NK, he is rare spawn.

  • After you kill him go To the Northern Tree line to the East and look for Regis (Loc 1499.59, -1511.01) he should be a level 28 Zombie. Kill Regis (Don’t ask me why you have to kill the poor guy you would think he has been thou enough all ready) and get the Icon and the Flute. 

  • Return the flute to Lianna and she should give you the Ingot. 

  • Buy 2 Enchanted plat Bars and give the Icon, Ingot and 2 Plat bars to Gavel for the Bracer of the Reverent. 

  • (HINT) All you need to do is Kill Regis he has both items you need for the quest. There is a NPC group of werewolf hunters just at the foot of the hill where Regis spawns and they end up killing Zahal for you most of the time. BE WARNED if you are a Dark Elf Cleric you will be KOS to the werewolf hunters. At level 40, 2 of the 4 NPCs coned blue to me and they have a HUGE aggro range and they killed my cleric 3 times. I have fixed my faction with the Guards and the Venders in NK but it has no effect on the hunters and as of writing this I still have not been able to do this quest because of them. (Thanks for thinking the quest thou verant) (If you want two bracers you have to do this quest twice) Also you can use sense undead spell here to see if Zahal or Regis are in the zone it’s the only time I have ever had a reason for casting this spell.

Quest No. 2 Althuryn the Constant Quest for the Chestplate.

  • Althuryn was killed by a Aqua Goblin called Sludge, he can be found in Runnyeye down by the Goblin bank. 

  • Outside the bank is a star shaped fountain. Jump in the fountain and swim to the bottom where Sludge can be found. Also there is a Cube that spawns down there I am not sure if its his place holder but it seems to me that it is. Kill Sludge and get The Ingot of the Constant. 

  • Then go to Dagnors Cauldron and look for another aqua Goblin named Dwigus Lowater. Kill him and get the Icon of the Constant. Dwigus spawns between the NPC camp by the zone to Steamfront and the Goblin camp by Unrest. I just stood on the hill between the two and he walked right up to me. He does roam the zone once he spawns so a tracker would not hurt. 

  • Buy 2 Enchanted Plat bars and give them to Genni in return she will give you 2 Galvanized bars, give the Icon, Ingot and 2 Galvanized bars to Gavel For the Breastplate of the Constant.

Quest No. 3 Nebbletob the Devout Quest for the Shield Devout

  • Go to the mines of Meldrath (steamfont mountains) and kill the Minotaur Sentry and get the Ingot. 

  • Sentries are the guards to the Necro in the back of the mino cave. Which in a way is good for us because the Necro is needed to do a ranger quest so the odds are someone is camping him and this will make your job a lot easier. 

  • Go to Everfrost to the Ice River there is a hole in the Ice at LOC 1171.24, -5026.08 you WILL need enduring breath to do this, swim north when the river forks take the left side you will come to a cave where you can breath once again. In the cave go to LOC 5095.11, -4460.11 and face southwest looking at an intersection. This is close to where the Wizards go to get one of the rods for a quest. BE WARNED there is a necro in this cave that is RED to level 50s and he WILL kill you, but he does not roam so as long as you don't go near him you are fine, what you are looking for is a spawn of the Glacial Bear. You can't miss him he's about as big as the hallway. He's on an 8-hour spawn cycle his placeholder is a skelly. But don't get confused just before the placeholder spawns a roamer skelly spawns close to the same area. Kill the Bear and get the Icon of the Devout. 

  • Buy 2 Enchanted Plat bars and give them to Vilissia  in return she will give you 2 Vulcanized bars, return to Gavel and give him the Icon, Ingot and 2 Vulcanized bars for the Shield of the Devout.

 Quest No. 4 Turgan the Reliant Quest for the Boots


  • Go to Kithicor Forest and search by the burned down cabin (Loc: +1725, +1125). There you will pick up a diary, the diary will give you a place to look for the ingot which you will find on a tree stump at (Loc -90, 1100).

  • You have to run up a fallen tree and jump over and on to the top of the stump. 

  • BE WARNED Kithicor Forest is not a nice place so try to do this during the day time hours.

  • Take the diary to Turgan in Lake Rathe (Loc 3641.08, 149.10) and give him the Diary in return for the Icon Of the Reliant. 

  • Buy 1 Enchanted Plat bar and go to Blaze, give her the Icon, Ingot and1 Enchanted plat bar for the boots of Reliant



Quest No. 5 Theo the Ardent Quest for the Gauntlets of the Ardent

  • You must find and kill a Gnome Necromancer in Befallen named Babbinsbort. He is on a 4 hour spawn cycle, luckily he no longer has a place holder.  He is found on the 3rd level in Befallen in a room across from where the Shadowknight used to drops the barbed pants.  He is behind the fallen pillar (LOC -800, -184). 

  • Next, proceed to Everfrost and look under the Ice for the icon(Loc 2996, -5771). You will need enduring breath once again.  The icon is very hard to see under the water so look closely.  It looks like a voodoo doll.  Search the area around the loc I gave you and you will see it. 

  • Buy 1 Enchanted Plat bar and give it to Genni and in return she will give you 1 Galvanized bar. Return to Blaze and give her the Icon, Ingot and 1 Galvanized bar for the Gauntlets of the Ardent



Quest No. 6 Jovan the Fervent Quest for the Vambraces of Fervent

  • Go to the ocean of tears to the Aviak Island (LOC 2000,4000 is the LOC for the Island, not for Gull) and find an Aviak named Gull Skytalon.  Kill him and get the Ignot of Fervent. WARNING Gull Coned blue to me at level 40 and killed me twice but I bound myself to the island after the first time and when he killed me I memed Wrath and finished him off. 

  • Go to Highpass and find Jovan.  He is typically found in the sawmill upstairs by the still. He wants 1 Tumpy Tonix (1 Kola Nut 1 flask Of water) for his hangover. You have to go to the Dwarf City in Butcherblock and to get this made or find a player who is a  master brewer. Tumpy runs a tavern and he will make the tonic for you. The Kola Nut is found in the Ocean of Tears on the Sisters Island (the first stop from Freeport to Butcher Block).  WARNING if you're a Dark Elf you will be KOS on the Island where you need to buy the Kola Nut and KOS in Butcherblock. You can get the nut and not miss the boat if you're fast. When the boat docks run to the shop closest to the dock the there should be 2 venders inside, the one on the left sells the Kola nut buy a few and run back to the boat. 

  • Buy 1 Enchanted Plat bar and give it to Vilissia and in return she will give you 1 Vulcanized bar, return to Blaze give her the Icon, Ingot and 1 Vulcanized bar for the Vambracers of the Fervent.

 

Quest No. 7 Astrid the Penitent Quest for the Greaves of Penitent

  • You need to go to Upper Guk for the Ingot of Penitent.  Go to the Scryer room.  Inside you will see two pillars of water .  The ingot is in the right pillar (LOC -209, 458).

  • Next you must go to Kerra Ridge (WARNING if you are a Dark Elf Cleric you are KOS to the guards and venders outside of Kerra). Swim out to the small Island on the other side of town there you will find a Erudite heretic named Maugarim (sorry forgot to get a screen shot). Kill her for the Icon of Penitent. To get her to spawn just kill the 3 roamers and she will pop.  Took me about an hour to get her. 

  • Buy 1 Enchanted Plat bar and give them to Ostorm and in return he will give you 1 Magnetized bar, go to Blaze and give her the Icon, Ingot and 1 Magnetized bar for the Greaves of the Penitent.



Quest No. 8 Caduceus of Sacrament


  • This is a hidden Cleric quest in the Temple of Solusek Ro. When you hail Gavel, and ask him about the Icon of Sacrament, he will tell you that the icon and ingots of Sacrament is only a legend, and if you can somehow find and return them to him, plus 2 magnetized platinum bars, he can make something for you. 

  • The Icon of Sacrament is to be found in Castle Mistmoore. At the back of the tomb in the Graveyard there's a secret wall. Go through that secret wall you will enter into a small room with 4 doors. Open the door that you see as you enter the room; that will trigger the other doors open as well. Then turn to your left, there's 2 rooms. Go into the left hand side room, the icon is on the floor, which looks just like a voodoo doll. WARNING Castle Mistmoore zone is a dangerous zone. How I got the Icon and managed not get killed was I bought some Invisible potions and first thing in morning, ran down to the room, and picked it up first try and then ran out of the zone.

  • A Darkened Giant Skeleton in Mountain Rathe drops the Ingot of Sacrament. (Loc -620, -1600). When you zone in from Feerrot, head down the path and take your first left. Go until you see a tower on your left keep going and you will see an old Druid Ring on the left hand side with many undead spawns. Clean them all up (As I have heard, it spawns on random places in this ruin). You will see a Giant Skeleton that is darker in color, just like a Darkbone.  He is the one you need to kill. 

  • After you have both Icon and Ingot, you will need to get 2 enchanted platinum bars. Give the 2 Enchanted Plat bars to Ostrom in Temple of Solusek Ro; he will then magnetized them for you. 

  • Give the icon, ingot and 2 Magnetized platinum bars to Gavel; he will then make you a Caduceus of Sacrament.
