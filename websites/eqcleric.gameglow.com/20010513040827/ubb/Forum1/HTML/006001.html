AO won’t be out by June, no way.

On the PvP tip... you miss out on absolutely no content by participating in PvP or not. Each zone you enter has something called "suppression gas." It’s an in-game mechanism that determines the level of PvP in a zone. Starting cities and newbie zones have 75-100% suppression gas in effect. That means no PvP. As you move farther away you’ll encounter 0-25% where you can attack anyone and everything. You are notified of the suppression gas level upon entering the zone, much like the areas of EQ.

PvP isn’t yet balanced, so it’s got a long way to go before it hits the shelves. An aggressive estimate would be August, at best.
