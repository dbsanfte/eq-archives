Suprised I haven't seen this yet. This quest rewards you with the following:

Earring of the Solstice
AC 5, STA +5, WIS +8, INT +8, SV Fire/Cold/Magic +5, Hitpoints +55, Mana +55
Effect: Flowing Thought I
WT 0.1 (I think)

The quest really isn't that hard. It's just time consuming and EXPENSIVE (emphasis on the costly part). Basically you just take 9 items made from tradeskills, throw them in a forge and hit combine. The result is one of the best earrings in the game for Clerics (and most casters).

I finished mine this weekend (lemme tell you, having your whole guild work collectively on earrings greatly simplifies this).

Need to say this to those considering buying a package...

It's not worth 65k. If you figure in the reasonable cost of failures and add in the time to go collect all the items, we're talking 15-20k. I personally bought components I couldn't seem to get completed and all told spent maybe 40 hours and 5k buying the stein, having the gem chain made, paying a 250 Tailor to try the robe, buying the sickle and purchasing various items I needed along the way. Had I opted to continue making all the items myself or having guildies do them I could have finished under 2k (assuming no strings of bad luck on the 250 combines).

The quest is really more of a time sink than anything with the few 250+ failure rates (seems to be 40% success on all the 250+ combines) to throw you off and eat your money. All told, no one component costs more than 500pp.

65k for a FT1 item is not only a ripoff but an insult to those who invested their time in this quest.

One last thing... this is a DRUID quest (you get a Protection of the Cabbage scroll upon completion). You WILL have to have the help of at least 1 druid along the way (since clerics can't forage plains pebbles, wild cabbage and dragon eggs).
