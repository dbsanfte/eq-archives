Mages get Mod Rods. Definatly not something you just summon when you need mana, cause they cost a lot... but its possible to have on on you for later, and have them on friends. More useful to give back other peoples mana in raid times (ie: Mage, summon rods and drop them)

Enchanters get thier clarity lines, they also have thier insight/brilliance lines, and the new +mana lines (Gift).

Necros are kings and queens of getting mana.

Druids get some post 50 spell... mask of the hunter or something? Like the wizard shield spell, it adds 1mana/tick regen.
