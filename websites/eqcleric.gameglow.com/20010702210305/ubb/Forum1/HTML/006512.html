I know this has been discussed in the past but wanted to relate a story.

In KC the other day (not a train story). I hear in /ooc "Ressing for donations at zone." I don't think much about it and continue playing for awhile but soon leave to go restock in FV.

In Dreadlands and hear again /ooc "Ressing for donations at zone." I do a /who and this person is anon. So being the sarcastic cleric I am I /ooc "Rezzing for free." Now here's where it gets funny.

Anon-cleric "Bah, only a 49 res."
Me "So what, at least they don't have to pay."
Anon-cleric "*rollseyes*"
NonAnon-cleric "Ressing for free."

Did a slash /who on this new response and it's a level 60. I laugh hard at that and the fact that anon-cleric was no longer in the zone.

I'm not attempting to flame the anon-cleric but I don't like "demanding" money for rezzes. It peeves me that everytime I ask someone if they *want* a rez they all ask me "how much will it cost?" I didn't ask them to *pay* for the rez, just if they *wanted* it.

I seldom accept cash for rezzing people but have yet to turn down an item (peridots) or any type of alcoholic beverage. Best way to get me to rez you is carry an extra stack of Elven Wine with you. (Almost have drinking tolerance maxxed).
