Okay. Before the servers went down because of CERFNet, and because of the emergency patch, I had an opportunity to group in Sebilis, and we got as far down as the chef, when CERFNet blew up a router, and 26 of the servers went down, including mine.

But anyhow, our group consisted of a level 59 Shadowknight, a 51 Enchanter, a 51 Shaman, a 56 Necromancer, and a 53 Monk, and myself, 55 Cleric.

Part 1) Debuff spells on ALL casters (including hybrids) no longer taunt with the potential they used to.

Problem: ShadowKnights can no longer taunt off casters/Clerics as they used to. Originally, before this most recent patch, it would take one disease hit from a Shadowknight to get a frog off me. Now it takes 2 Shadow Vortexes, and at least 2 Disease Clouds, maybe just an original TAUNT.

How would I get a frog on me so hard, that I couldn't be taunted off of? Read on:

Part 2) Healing spells now generate more hate than they used to. Complete heal now is designed to generate hate per amount of HP you heal.

Scenario/problem: - 59 Shadowknight is a Troll, and I'm guessing with full Cleric/Shaman buffs (which he had), he had about 3900 HP. Around 780 hit points for one bubble left. So, I start a complete heal around 1.5 bubbles, which is roughly 1170 hit points, leaving me 2730 hit points to heal, and to generate hate.

After I complete healed this Troll for the first time, the krup summoned me, and proceeded to bash on me. I tried to get some remedies off on myself, but it wouldn't work. I turned on Divine Barrier, and usually when you do that, the mob goes for the next highest person on the hate list......Well, I guess I was it.

Even with Divine Barrier on, the frog proceeded to attack me while invulnerable. The Troll ShadowKnight then threw a shadow vortex, and I'm still being whacked on. Then threw a Death Shroud (I think that's what it's called. It's an upgrade to shadow vortex), and the frog still proceeded to bash on me. Divine Barrier wore out, and the frog was still hitting me. I then had to use divine aura.

One disease cloud later, the Shadowknight finally got the krup shaman off me. After I clicked off Divine Aura, I complete healed myself (Had 2640 HP, and 1 bubble of health left), which was a mistake on my part, and the frog then switched targets from the Shadowknight, to me.


I hate Verant from this point on. If they don't fix one of the biggest wrongs I've seen yet, they're going to lose a paying customer.
