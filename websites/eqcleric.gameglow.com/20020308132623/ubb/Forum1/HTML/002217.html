Ellana... Please take this constructively. It's absolutely *not* a flame, it's just a set of suggestions to hopefully make your next communication have the intended outcome.

8 out of 10 for good intent.
-3 out of 10 for clear communication of your intent.

As a guild officer I tried to read the letter as if it were talking about one of our members.

It came off as a "stream of consciousness" rant, and try as I might, I couldn't or wouldn't read the whole thing.

When you make a convoluted statement, *and* point out that fact in your letter, you've diminished or eliminated whatever main point you were trying to make.

For the most effect: state your facts, make a complaint, and suggest something constructive to the guild.

My reaction within my guild would have been:
"Hey, Guildmember, Ellana was a bit upset with the way you healed last night. Ya might want to stay away from her for a bit."
