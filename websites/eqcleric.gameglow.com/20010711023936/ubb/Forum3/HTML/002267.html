a few thoughts for you...

At 41 your equipped to hunt in Skyshrine, perhaps a touch on the low side... but since it is almost always BYOGroup anyway, iwould recommend gathering up your buds to head there.

Following the SS "theme" for a bit, there are 3 major Guardian pieces as a cleric you will be looking for:

1) Guardian Breastplate (22ac +7str/sta +6svMagic)
2) Guardian Leggings (14ac +4wis +15svFire/Cold)
3) Guardian Helm (12ac +5wis.. forgot other stats)

IMHO, these are some of the best and most accessible non-planar/quest pieces for clerics.

Other items:

1) Plat/Velium Jasper rings (2)
2) Gauntlets of the Black (common drop off of Firebrand in EW at the orc fort)
3) FLoops (2)
4) Charred Guardian Shield
5) Mammoth Cloak
6) Springwood Club/Paw of Opolla (for your primary hand)

Get all the above items and you'll be over 200 in wisdom, and everything above is obtainable by you at your level (with a group), with the possible exception of the Springwood Club.
