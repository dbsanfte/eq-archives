Of course we all have 200 wis. So lets break it down into actual stats. I like Wisdom, Mana, AC, HitPoints, resist...in that order. The rest is just fluff.

Sarnak Battle Shield +25 AC +5 Str +5 Sta +10 vsMg +10 vsFire +10 vsCold +40 HP

Hierophant's Cloak +10 AC +4 Strength +4 Stamina +4 Agility +4 Dexterity +4 Wisdom +4 Intelligence +4 Charisma +75 Hit Points +75 Mana

Lodizal Shell Shield AC: +23 Str: +10 Agi: -5 Wis: +10 Cha: +5 Magic Resist: +10 Fire Resist: +20 Cold Resist: +20

…………………………………………………………………………………………

Lod + Heiro = 14wis, 75mana, 33ac, 75hp, 10mr, 20cr, 20cr

Lod + SBS = 10wis, “0”mana, 45ac, 40hp, 20mr, 30fr, 30cr

…………………………………………………………………………………………

Lod + Heiro = +4 wis, +75mana, -12ac, +35hp, -10mr, -10fr, -10cr

Lod + SBS = -4wis, -75hp, +12ac, -35hp, +10mr, +10fr, +10cr


Personally I believe the best combo is Lod+Heiro, I would hold the SBS for the raids against dragons (ToV) and other mobs that resists are needed.

Now with that extra wis, you can drop your velium jasper ring and pick up a jaded velium ring and add 5ac 30 mana 30 hp etc etc etc.
