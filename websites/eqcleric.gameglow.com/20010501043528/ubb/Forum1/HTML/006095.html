At 54, you can solo effectively outside Karnors on the Drolvarg Ragers. Occasionally you will find some of the chickens (Petrified? or something along those lines cockatrices). They will occasionally be blue to you.

Inside Karnors - the Drolvarg bodyguards? (can't remember name) will be blue, but hit pretty hard and have a lot of HP. The Drolvarg sentries will be half blue and half high green.

Skelies are fairly easy to solo. Just have to find them is the problem. They tend to float around the moat or be in the back of the castle area. If you have jboots or sow, and are careful of the other people, you can do some soloing in Karnors. Just be wary of the trains.

Burning woods - quite a few things will be blue at 54. Hornets - couple different varieties. Ember, and Scarias(sp) will still be blue. Some of the skellies will be blue.

Stay away from the tatterback gorillas - they are blue but can summon and they hit very hard. Not sure if the moldering gorillas will still be blue or not.

If you are very lucky and have Sow or Jboots, you MIGHT be able to solo the funky looking teethy things in Skyfire mountains. But they tend to have a lot of HP also - plus it's tough to pull them through the rest of the stuff in that area.

Those are just a few of the places off the top of my head that I did some soloing off of around 54.

As far as the Ragefire goes. There is no set pattern that anybody has been able to decipher.

The ONLY given is that Naggy has to be killed before Ragefire will spawn. Other than that, it's anybodies guess as to when he will actually spawn.

Having had to camp the Pained Soul in Trak's Teeth I can tell you some of the ghosts around the Sebilis entrance are still good experience up to 56 (where none are higher than high green). Be careful, tho, as they can cast spells so not quite so easy for exp. Get your resists up.

If your server is half as chaotic as CT then Ragefire will be near impossible to get. There are 2 problems that make straight up camping infeasable (unless you have an entire raid camped down there)

1. FGs respawn in the lair. You have to have a FD capable person camp the lair or have a group capable of slaying FGs with you (FGs are doable with a solid group of high 50s)

2. When Ragefire spawns you must outrace everyone and their dog to get a raid down there to kill him. This is where the bulk of the problems you've heard about come into play. Most people don't care how long you've sat down there and will take him anyways so you've thrown 1-4 days of your life away.
