Electrae kept her head shaved in protest of her servitude to the raiding guild until she managed to buy her freedom. Now she has let it regrow, but it's short and spikey looking.

She spends her days resting in the Retired Clerics Home, sipping Geritol spiked tea, still bitter over the experience. When she was able to buy her freedom she slipped into the obscurity of being a botted cleric and is taking a long sabbatical from raids and guilds and such.

Although, she has heard rumours of new adventures that suit her far better than being enslaved in a raiding guild... she remembers her adventures with her friends in dark, dank places like Sebilis, Howling Stones and Chardok and dreams of finding adventures like those again. Until then, she must content herself with yelling at elven children that run across her lawn.
