Dreadlands is actually very good and very safe for exp grinding at your level. However here’s a piece of advice, GET OFF THE NORTH WALL!

Let me explain, on my server there are a ton of people camped on the North Wall. On a busy day, it’s not unusual to have about 7 or 8 groups perched on the North Wall of Karnors Castle. In this case, pulls start to get scarce, things get confusing, and arguments erupt over who stole what Drolvarg. Lately what I have been doing is forming or joining a group at the North Wall and then moving the group to the small set of ruins in the South East corner of the Castle just a ways out (you’re sitting in desert, not on the castle grounds). There is literally a field of creatures to pull from here and experience just flows in. Nothing wanders through here and you stay away from the majority of the players. (I did this exact same thing in the Overthere, I would always take my groups to a spot on the West wall between the SW Corner and the Skyfire zone just to avoid the other groups and Goons).

Other choices are to set up camp near the undead ruins in the East or along a different part of the castle (the South wall for example). Just get off the North Wall unless it’s not very crowded.

Aside from the Dreadlands, you can do.
Lower Guk
Sol B
Kedge Keep
Kaesora
City of Mist
Get a similar level melee partner and stick to the Overthere. If they are the same level or higher, they can pull blues and experience is GREAT!
