Problem with snow griffons is they wander all over the place in EW.

I don't know the exact loc, but i know of 2 general spawn areas.

1) Coming from the EW Bridge there is a couple small huts between the bridge and Kael. These huts are inhabited by Coldain and you will often find people camping them for coldain heads. Just over the ridge directly by the huts, and between the huts and Kael I have seen 2 griffons spawn.

2) Directly between the EW bridge and the Ry'Gorr fort I have encountered a good number of griffons.

My personal suggestion is hire a ranger. I paid a ranger 40pp per Egg i got and let him keep all the other loot from the griffons. Took me about 2.5 hours and 10 griffons to get my 4 eggs.

Rumor has it that the Griffons spawn is on the same spawn table as the other animals in EW.. Meaning the more Mammoths, wolves, cougars, and pretty little snow bunnies you kill, the greater the chance a griffon will spawn. This is not confirmed, but in my experience with the ranger we killed 3 griffons in the first 1.5 hours... then we started killing wolves/bunnies/etc... and got 7 more griffons on track in the next hour.

Good luck and my your Snow Griffon Souffle not *poof*
