I thought I'd post a little warning for young clerics about doing the Boots of Reliance quest since the FA quests have been implemented.

I started the quest for the boots at lvl 16 having judged it the easiest quest to complete and very doable on my own. I embarked on the last stages of the quest shortly after the patch that included the FA quests.

I headed for Lake Rathetear to Turgans cabin to give him his diary and feared for little as to my knowledge there was nothing that would give me problems in that area of the zone.

Unfortunately 'A Spirit of Flame' had spawned and was sat right outside the door to the cabin. When approaching from the South Karana zone it wasnt in view coming up the hill and I didnt see it until I was on top of it.

It was very aggro and cleaned my clock in seconds. I got a monk friend to check if it was still there for me and it had gone about 10 minutes later. I should think its unlikely for this npc be hanging about now that people know about its involvement in the cleric FA quest but I thought I'd mention it.

If it isnt killed, how long does it stay spawned for, was I just the victim of bad timing?

Level 30ish undead arent going to seem such a problem having had that shock

The Spirit of Flame despawns in about 30 minutes. If you drag him to a zone to fight him somewhere other than at his spawn point like I did (hey, at 45th he's a hard fight), he WILL NOT return to his spawn point when you zone. He will stay right there and attack anyone who passes through. I had to do a few resses as a result of finding that one out the hard way.
