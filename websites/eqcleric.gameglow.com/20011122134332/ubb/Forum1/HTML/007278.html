Regarding Sarnak Faction...

As you'll notice any time you kill a sarnak, there are two seperate sarnak factions. The first is Sarnak Collective, and to the best of my knowledge, the only way to ever raise this faction was by killing Slaythe in Misty Thicket. I also understand that killing Slaythe no longer provides any faction change except down with Frogloks of Kunark. This faction appears to apply to any Sarnak found outside of Chardok.
The other faction is Brood of Di'Zok. This is the faction that applies to Chardok, and can be raised by killing goblins in the Temple of Droga and the Mines of Nurga. There are also apparently a few goblins in Frontier Mountains (where the two goblin dungeons are located) that will raise Brood of Di'Zok if killed.
Of course, the problem for people who want high Chardok faction is that the majority of Sarnaks aren't on Brood of Di'Zok, and killing ANY sarnak will lower both factions. Thus, if you have high Chardok faction and want to keep it with a minimum of hassle, it is important to avoid Sarnaks outside of Chardok (since afaik, it is currently impossible to raise Sarnak Collective faction).
At any rate, while I have heard of characters with high Brood of Di'Zok faction, I haven't heard any stories about things they might have found inside Chardok.
