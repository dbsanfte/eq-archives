Let me bitch about another issue: guild names.

A LONG time ago I (along with a few RL friends) was invited to join a guild of very nice people: The Transcendents. They were a mix of younger and older people, casual and harcode, but all nice people. Things were fine for a year and a half, but when we migrated from EQ to AO some differences of opinion came up, and a subset of us (mainly those of us who played using Battlefield Commander, more commonly known as BCom) decided to split off and form our own guild.

So, in AO and DAoC we have our own guild, named B Company. B Company was a name coined when someone in the guild was bitching about play styles, namely the play style of those of us using BCom versus those not. According to him, we seemed a little more coordinated (not having to type in the heat of battle helps a bit imo, but we also played together a lot and that made a difference). So the guild thereafter referred to the subset of us that used BCom 24x7 as B Company when they were talking (or bitching) about us. So naturally when we split.....

Why the long preamble? I'll tell you: on a server that has guilds named Deathrow, Kill, and The Powers That Be some $#*($# at Verant decided B Company "doesn't fit the theme of Norrath." WHAT!?!?! Deathrow is acceptable but B Company isn't?!?!? What a shmuck. I have to run around the #$($#)$ server looking at Brittani Spears (high level player mind you, ran into "her" in Cazic), but I can't have a B Company tag over my head.

Eventually I'll get to talk to a GM about this and possibly convince him to accept it, but this really hacks me off. Sure, we "could" come up with another name, but we don't want to. And I'll be damned if I'm going to worry about thinking up a more "appropriate" name when I'm on a server with Deathrow, Kill, Britanni Spears, and Lillinae HasPoopInMyPants (I may have the first name wrong, but I ran into THAT player for months around SolB).

Give us our name damnit!

quote:
Originally posted by Serina Moonprism Elay:
So as you see I think your gm is on crack!

Unless there's been a recent change here, server GM's don't approve guild names. That's done by a centralized guild CS group. So, even if you're lucky enough to be able to talk to a GM about it, the answer will probably just be email eqguilds@soe.sony.com (think that's the address). That said, I can't see any reason B Company isn't an acceptable name. Sounds like a mercenary company, so I'd think it was actually very in keeping with a middle ages/fantasy world.

As for player names, these ARE controlled at the server level. Basically, you will get any name you choose unless: A) There is a guide/GM online and they reject it -OR- B) The name is in the filter (because somebody has already used it and had it taken away). If you see a name that bothers you, petition it. It's possible that the guides on your server are idiots and didn't know that Imdapimpdaddy violated the naming policy they're supposed to enforce, but more than likely he got to level 58 with that name because nobody ever petitioned it. Maybe I've just been lucky. In 3 years of playing I've only petitioned 4 times and got the help/answer I was looking for on all but 1 (a request to remove my surname).
