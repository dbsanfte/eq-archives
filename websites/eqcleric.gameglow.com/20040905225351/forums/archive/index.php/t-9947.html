About the anonymous thing...

Unless it's someone I know, I will always ask the person to go un-anon before agreeing to help them. Why?

1) Yes, I do have a no-rez list, and this does include entire guilds. My philosophy: The sins of the few are paid for by the many. Invite too many goobers into your guild and eventually your guild gets a bad reputation. I've had people who know I won't rez certain guilds go anon to try and hide it.

2) I like to know what class I'm dealing with. My rez fee is based on the following:
Other clerics and pallys -- free rez, even if they want to pay.
Druids -- 150pp flat out, more if I have to travel a bit.
Everyone else -- Whatever they want to donate. Unless the rez involves me traveling across the world, I never ask for money. If I have to go from, say, OOT to somewhere in Velious I'll ask for some sort of compensation. I leave it up to them to decide what it is.
