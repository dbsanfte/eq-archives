SerenyaIf you like questing start in on the Thurgadin Shawl quest. While the shawl isn't particularly good at lower steps, at step 4 you can get a seal which makes a very nice ranged slot item. I understand that there is now a Qeynos Badge quest series with good results as well, but I'm afraid I don't know anything about them.

You didn't mention your race -- if you are a small race I actually like the Dusty BP, which drops in Frontier Mtns, better then the gold BP.

If you hit the Orc Fort in Velious, also keep an eye out for Ry'Gorr Chain leggings -- same AC as your gold ones, but with hitpoints and a different selection of resists. For questing, there are the Thurgadin quest leggings involving the Brood Mother. If you're trying to do the shawl quest you'll be killing wolves in GD, and might get a shot at the harness needed for the leggings. This is particularly popular item with druids and rangers, so should keep your ranger friend happy. You'll also need to pay a visit to Crystal Caverns (zone in inside the orc fort) for this one.

It's been a while since I helped someone with the leggings, and I've not done the shawl quest yet, so I'm a bit vague on the details I'm afraid, but there are plenty of guides floating about for both.

ixifAlso if you're small race get a Belt of the Centobite. You probably won't be able to camp it because it's on the rare cycle and you'd need help, but it's well worth the purchase.
