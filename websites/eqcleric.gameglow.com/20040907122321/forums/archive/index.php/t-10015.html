Originally posted by Serina Moonprism Elay:
So as you see I think your gm is on crack!


Unless there's been a recent change here, server GM's don't approve guild names. That's done by a centralized guild CS group. So, even if you're lucky enough to be able to talk to a GM about it, the answer will probably just be email eqguilds@soe.sony.com (think that's the address). That said, I can't see any reason B Company isn't an acceptable name. Sounds like a mercenary company, so I'd think it was actually very in keeping with a middle ages/fantasy world.

As for player names, these ARE controlled at the server level. Basically, you will get any name you choose unless: A) There is a guide/GM online and they reject it -OR- B) The name is in the filter (because somebody has already used it and had it taken away). If you see a name that bothers you, petition it. It's possible that the guides on your server are idiots and didn't know that Imdapimpdaddy violated the naming policy they're supposed to enforce, but more than likely he got to level 58 with that name because nobody ever petitioned it. Maybe I've just been lucky. In 3 years of playing I've only petitioned 4 times and got the help/answer I was looking for on all but 1 (a request to remove my surname).
