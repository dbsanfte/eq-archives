3.16.2000  Quellious Server Wide Meeting - Agenda: High Level Raiding Courtesy and Scheduling
Not So New Quellious Raid Site - The Raid: http://unseen.dhs.org/raids/

  The server population of  Quellious has grown tremendously and many people now have mature and veteran characters on Quellious.  Quellious is experiencing over-crowding not unlike other mature servers, and some concerned citizens would like to invite those on Quellious who wish to voice their opinions and preferences for how to handle the courtesy in organizing raids of key High-Level Raid zones and places.  Specifically Plane of Hate, Plane of Fear, Plane of Sky, Lord Nagafen, and Lady Vox.

  All are welcome from newbies to vets as insight may be garnered from each of you.  This first meeting I am scheduling for Monday March 20 at 9PM EST and the Chat Room will be called "Quellious Raids".  If you wish to attend the meeting, then please send an email to tuula@norrath.net and I will send you a reply with the password to the chat room (as I don't want stragglers who are not prepared for the meeting just popping in).  In your email please give me your characters name and level on Quellious.  This initial meeting will be an attempt to discuss and come to a majority consensus as to how to handle courtesy and scheduling of high-level raids in an attempt to draft up some kind of rules of courtesy for Quellious and the results of the meeting will be posted on The Quellious Raid Website (The Raid) http://unseen.dhs.org/raids/.

  If you cannot make this meeting time but wish to express your thoughts and views via me as proxy, then send me and email and I will add your views on particular aspects of discussion where appropriate.

  This notice is also to let those on Quellious know that The Quellious Raid site (http://unseen.dhs.org/raids/) has been created with the intention of improving the communication between the raid-ready guilds, individuals, and raid leaders on Quellious and all are welcome to come and read the news, articles, and post there.  Depending on the how successful the Raid meetings go we will be putting up and actual Calendar for raid scheduling that all can book into in order to further help prevent raid schedule conflicts and divide raid times as fairly as possible amongst all on the server.

So come out and show your voice!

Tuula Draconfel
Quellious Raid Site Moderator
