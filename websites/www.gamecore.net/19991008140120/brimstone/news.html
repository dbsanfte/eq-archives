NEWS - Guild Leadership

5-21-99
More Updates...

Today, I updated the "Ideas" section to include yet more "Themes" for guilds and clans. Yesterday, I added a new article called, "Finding a Guild to Join".

I also wanted to thank Gasper from the UO Vault, The staff of EverWorld, as well as Cass from the UOSS for posting news of this Leadership Community. With their support, I hope that UO and EQ can become a more "Social" experience, and together, we can at least decrease the 'dewdish' ways of many players/guilds. All it takes is inspiration and creativity... I think we can handle that, don't you?

Brimstone

Updates

Thanks to Malachi, I have added a new section called, "Resources". This page contains a collection of free resources found on the web that can BOOST your guilds productivity tenfold! Mailing lists, message boards, counters, trackers, etc. - most of it free! Anyway, thanks for the suggestion, Mal... I ran with it!

5-14-99
Grand Opening of the Guild Leadership Community

Today is the grand opening of the Guild Leadership site. I hope to populate all of the sections with quality works from guild leaders and player from around the world. The news will be updated weekly, by myself. In a few weeks, I  hope to have an automated script designed that can support multiple reporters who will be hired by myself and asked to report key items that relate to guilds and creative player ideas for guilds.

In a couple of days, the "Guild Community Forum" will be created, and made available to everyone. I plan to interview (via email) several guild leaders who will be given highlighting codes and admin abilities to help patrol the forum, as well as respond to player questions and needs.

If you are interested in becoming a Forum Admin, send me an email with your background information. When the time is right,  you will be notified with your codes, if accepted.

Brimstone
