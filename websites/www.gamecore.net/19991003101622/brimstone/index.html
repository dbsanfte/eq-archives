About this Site:

For well over 2.5 years, I have been focusing on the leadership of communities, namely, guilds. I have written several articles on leadership, guild creation, organized warfare in games without "warfare" systems, roleplaying guides, and recently, an article about the reformation of the traditional, "Player Killer".

For those who don't know me, I was one of the Founders of the Lost Order of Akalabeth. A guild that was my first, ever, inspired by the game: Ultima Online. From the day we set forth to create this guild, we wanted every single aspect to be flawless... and we strived for success. To this day, the Lost Order (LOA) lives on, and is doing quite well - despite our fair share of troubles and heartaches.

I have long wanted to create a "Core" or "Central" source that EVERYONE from around the world could use as a guide when creating, or managing their guilds. A place where I could share my tips, as well as the tips and ideas from other well-reknowned guild leaders from around the globe. This is what I have come up with, and I hope you will bookmark it, embrace it, and add to this community.

Prior to online gaming, I have played pen and paper RPGs for nearly all my life, at least 14 years before UO came along. Then I joined the United State Marine Corps, where I learned a lot about organization, respect, honor, discipline, etc. I truly believe that it is from my background and experiences that I have been able to come up with a successful recipe for online guilds and communities, as well as ideas that make online gaming much more fun. I spent 90% of my online experiences working on the political and managerial aspects of guilds, rather than the actual gameplay of Ultima Online, and now, EverQuest. To me, THAT is where the real fun lies.

And with that all said, welcome to the site, I hope you enjoy all that you read and learn while visiting. I will try to make updates each week, as well as assigning various guild leaders to areas throughout this site. I am looking for forum guides, news agents, creative writers, etc.

Sincerely,

Brimstone
aka: Anthony
