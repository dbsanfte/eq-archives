GUILD SPOTLIGHT

This weeks spotlight is aimed at the following guilds:

Fires of Heaven (FoH)(EQ)
EverQuest Guild - Posted: 5-17-99

Founder: Myrlynn
Info: Such a controversial guild... you either love them, or you hate them. One thing is for sure, they have the right formula for making a successful guild. If you view their website, you will see that it is wonderfully crafted. Nice graphics, great design and layout, and a creative approach to many of their guild pages. In game, the FoH are found on the Veeshan Server and are by far the most powerful guild - on all servers (As of now). They have several level 50 members, which adds to the controversy over this guild. The FoH leader is quite pleasant, we have talked several times via email. They enjoy playing the game from a "Powergaming" standpoint, and aren't planning to run many RP events, at least not now. All in all, a wonderful guild for the player who enjoys pursuing power as well as being associated with VERY powerful players.

The New Age Outlaws (NAO)(UO)
Ultima Online Guild - Posted: 5-26-99

Founders: Harvey, Roscoe and Makaveli
Info: I featured this guild based on their excellent web design. They have a clear goal, great site design and graphics, key resources needed to run a guild, and more. In addition, Roscoe, one of their founders, also designs guild pages for in-game gold (On Atlantic). I have a soft spot for people who take their creative energies and use them out of game, for in-game gold. They aren't focussed on roleplaying, but instead enjoy the day to day adventures with friends. The NAO are a new guild on the Atlantic Shard.
