Guild Rules

1.  First and foremost, Dragons of Northstar is a raiding guild.  We raid daily at approximately 6pm CST.  If you are on at that time you are expected to be at the raid regardless of whether there is loot there you want or a flag you need.  We need all that can attend every raid so that we can work as a unit when it comes time for the big mobs and the higher planes.

2.  XP, AAXP, and LDON adventures are extracurricular activities to be done during your spare time NOT during raid time.  The focus of this guild is to RAID and get to the elemental planes and you need to be at the raid when you're online.  NOTE: There will always be exceptions and Real Life always comes first.  Plan ahead and don't plan xp time during our set raid time of 6pm CST.

3.  Rule pending.

4.  Members WILL NOT go /anon when online during our regular scheduled raid time of 6pm CST whether you are at the raid or not.  This helps officers track DKP and attendance.

5.  Members will be courteous to one another whenever possible and if differences do occur those involved need to keep it to tells and out of Guild Chat.  If there is a problem with another member that you cannot resolve yourself speak to an officer and we will try to help if possible.  When grouping with others outside our guild try and represent us well.  Everything our members do reflects upon our guild as a whole, for good or worse.

6.  Loot is awarded via our DKP system.  The DKP is basically an attendance and participation tool.  Currently 1 DKP is awarded per Named killed (subject to adjustment as needed).  An item that drops is linked by an officer with a request for tells and the item's DKP value is attached to the message (Send tell to Erikose for Rat Ear (1)).  The number in parentheses is the DKP value of the item.  The officers then vote on the winner based in part on DKP, past loot wins, and need.  IF YOU DO NOT ATTEND RAIDS YOU WILL NOT GET DKP AND WILL NOT GET LOOT!

7.  To facilitate the tracking of DKP members WILL NOT leave raid without letting an officer know and without checking that their DKP in the Guild Management Tool has been updated.  At the end of a raid members will stay in RAID till they verify that their DKP has been updated.  This is the only time to dispute DKP.  IF YOU FAIL TO VERIFY OR LEAVE RAID BEFORE DKP IS UPDATED IT IS YOUR FAULT AND WILL NOT BE CHANGED AT A LATER DATE.

8.  If you cannot abide by these rules for whatever reason then Dragons of Northstar is not the guild for you.
