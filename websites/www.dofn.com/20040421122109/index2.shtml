Recruiting is now open!!! Posted by Vanston on 09/27/2003 @ 11:57 PM CST

Dragons of Northstar is an Everquest raiding guild located on the Saryrn Server. We primarily raid zones within the Planes of Power as our primary focus is to attain access to the Elemental Planes. DofN also raids the Temple of Veeshan as we are on Claws of Veeshan faction. Loot is awarded using a Dragon Kill Point (DKP) system to award loot to our members. DofN raids daily and members are expected to attend raids when they are online. We wish to make Dragons of Northstar into a cohesive unit capable of surviving in the Elemental Planes and the only way to do that is to raid together as often as possible. Dragons of Northstar is actively recruiting levels 62 and up in all classes. See the Application section to apply.
