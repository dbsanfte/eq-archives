EverQuest is an online only game that I have been playing for a while now.   You can choose from several different servers to play on, each of which normally host over 1,000 players.  Each of these servers will house up to 8 of your characters, but you can not transfer a character from one server to another.

I will continue to add information to this page as I come by it.  I currently play a Level 28 Half-Elf Ranger named Taneril Passingwind on the Povar server, who resides just about everywhere.  I plan on continuing to play Taneril until a higher level, both so I can learn more of the game, and also so I have a more powerful character that can "feed" my other newbies.  Therefore, most of the information that you find here for the next couple of weeks might seem a little bit (read: completely) ranger-biased.  Have no fear, I will continue my exploration of the other races and classes of Norrath, and provide you with the information that I find.
