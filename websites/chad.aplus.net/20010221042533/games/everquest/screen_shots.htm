If you have ever made the trip from High Pass Hold, down the ravine, and into the East Karanas...  you should recognize this next series of pictures.  It was my first time seeing the Ravine, the largest drop known to all of Norrath.

I was awe-struck by the sheer magnitude of the cliff walls that stretched out below me.  As I approached the edge of the cliff, to get a better view...  I thought to myself, "Be careful not to get to close, and fall over the edge!"  But guess what I did?

Fell over the edge!

I did somehow manage to survive the fall, although it knocked me unconscious.  I landed on a ledge, not even halfway down.  Here is what I could see, when I finally came to:

Luckily, my trusty pal Aenaden was nearby, and was able to laugh hysterically as he watched me fall over the edge of the cliff.
