Here are a some sites that I check out frequently.  They contain a ton of good information about EverQuest.

The Official EverQuest Site
  • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm - Most useful info I have found yet.
  • Blade n' Bow Online Magazine - Articles written by rangers, for rangers.
  • EQ'Lizer - Lots of useful data on this site!
  • EQCasters - Baelish's website, specializing in the four caster classes.
  • EQCharInfo - Nice parser for the eqlog.txt file.
  • EQItems - You guessed it, info on the items in EverQuest.
  • EQPortal - More EverQuest data.
  • EQ Weapons Database - Great site about weapons!
  • EverLore - EQ data, lots of forums!
  • EverQuest Express - Online EverQuest newspaper.
  • EverQuest Player Photos - Real pictures of EverQuest players.
  • EverQuest Stratics - Hints, tips and news.
  • The Rangers Glade - Dedicated to life as a ranger.
  • Teshia's Tips/Screenshots On Recent Ranger Quests - Lots of info on the Ivy Etched quests, and also the Rain Caller.  Includes screen shots!
  • Welcome to the Concert Hall -  Bard specific info.

If you have found any other good sites, please let me know about them as well!
