Emotes are the commands that EverQuest uses to try to bring some life into the game.  Emote is just short for emotion.  Some of these are animated, and some just produce the text.  Either way, they can bring quite a bit of life, to white might be an otherwise dull game.

These examples were taken from the text log that my friend Borlos kept, while I had Taneril Passingwind go through each of these emotions.  First, you will see the emotion as performed with no target, and then with Borlos as the target.

Typing the command below causes a message to appear as stated.
