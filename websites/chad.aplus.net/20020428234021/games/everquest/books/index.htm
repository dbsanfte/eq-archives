There are books, sold from the shops of Norrath, that provide some insight on some of the skills that characters can develop.  Everything from tailoring your own set of clothes and cooking, to forging your own weapon and creating jewelry.  These books can sometimes be too expensive for someone just starting out, so I have decided to provide the information that they contain here.

Hopefully, the list of books here will continue to grow, as my character gains more experience.  Please feel free to add any of the information that you have to this page as well.  You can either copy the information from the books Verbatim, in the format that I have listed here, or you can take screenshots of your books and send them to me.  If your character is near Qeynos, you could also look me up, and let me borrow the book for a few seconds so that I could save the screenshots.

Please enjoy the information provided here, I hope it saves you a few coins!
