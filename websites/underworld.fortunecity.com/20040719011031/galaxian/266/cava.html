Scout
Cost: 100 Food
Hit Points: 60
Attack: 3
Armor: -
Range: -
Speed: Fast
Notes:
The scout is the first cavalry unit you will be able to create. It is rather expensive for what it does and you should only build them if it is absolutely necessary. The scout can be taken out by a couple of villagers.


Chariot
Cost: 40 Food, 60 Wood
Hit Points:100
Attack:7
Armor:0
Range:-
Speed:Fast
Notes:
High resistance to conversion;
Double attack vs. Priests (whoever would be using them?)
The chariot is a fast hand to hand unit and is not huge in cost. By the time it is available to you, you should be able to afford a few of them.


Cavalry
Cost:70 Food, 80 Gold
Hit Points:150
Attack:8
Armor:0
Range:-
Speed:Fast
Notes:
+5 attacks vs. Infantry.
The next thing up from the Scout. It is no where near worth the cost of that much gold in the early stages of the game. Later on though you will have the option of better units. It would be better to buy 2 Chariots.


Heavy Cavalry
Cost:70 Food, 80 Gold
Hit Points:150
Attack:10
Armor:1
Range:-
Speed:Fast
Notes:
+5 attacks vs. Infantry.
+1 armor against missile weapons, Heleopolis, Ballista.
Not a vast improvement on its predecessor but is the same price. Good for defending you village from mass infantry attacks. You can best use them by spreading half a dozen around your base to take out the enemy that get into your village and try to kill your villagers.
