CAPTAIN




The Space Marine Captain is the Captain of one of the Chapter's companies. He is the bravest and mightiest warrior, inspiring all under his command. The Captain wears Power Armor. He is armed with a Plasma Pistol and a Power Sword.


LIBRARIAN




The Librarians are powerful Psykers that use their mighty psychic powers in battle. With all of their powers the Librarians are the ultimate warrior mystics, endowed with abilities, which set them aside from their Brother Space Marines. The Librarians wear Power Armor and are armed with a Bolt Pistol and Force Axe.


CHAPLAIN




The Chaplains are the spiritual leaders of their Space Marine Chapter. They perform the ancient ceremonies of Initiation and Vindication, as well as leading their brethren through the prayers of faith and sacred psalms of the Emperor. The Chaplains accompany their Brothers into war, leading the way into the ultimate consummation of battle and death. The Chaplain wears Power Armor and is armed with a Bolt Pistol and the Crozius Arcanium.


APOTHECARY




The Apothecary is a warrior of supreme bravery. If a Brother Warrior falls, the Apothecary can administer to him by tending his wounds and applying battle dressings to staunch the flow of blood. The Apothecary is armed with a Bolt Pistol.


TECH MARINE




The Techmarine is a priest of the Machine God skilled in the arcane sciences, and pertinent to the dark secrets of technology. The Techmarine is armed with a Bolt Pistol and Power Axe.


TERMINATOR




Terminators wield a combination of massive armor and powerful weapons.


TACTICAL MARINE




Tactical marines make up the majority of squads in most Space Marine Chapters. They are extremely flexible and tactically adaptable troops.


ASSAULT MARINE




Assualt squads are equipped with close quarter weapons such as bolt pistols and chainswords. They wear jump packs which enable them to leap over difficult terrain and move faster than ordinary troops.


DEVESTATOR MARINE




Devestator squads are equipped with large number of heavy weapons, which they use to burst open enemy tanks and strongpoints.
