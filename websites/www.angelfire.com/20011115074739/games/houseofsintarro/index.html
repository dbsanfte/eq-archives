House of Sintarro

Welcome to the House of Sintarro, a site dedicated to those who seek information about us. We are a guild on the Xegony server that only accepts the pure casting classes of Norrath (Magicians, Enchanters, Necromancers, and Wizards.) We unite in the common goal to study the arcane arts to the furthest degree, and choose not to fight as our ancestors did. In addition, we enforce strick roleplaying for our members. Feel free to take a look around, never know what ye might find ::grin::.
