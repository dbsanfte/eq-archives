    of the region

    The Field of Bone is the site of an ancient reservoir and other buildings that supported the Iksar city of Cabilis. An epic magical battle took place here long ago, and dried up the reservoir and scarred the surface. It is located in the middle of the island continent Kunark, northeast of the Iksar city of Cabilis. This is where young Iksar go to hunt. There are few dangers here that can't be avoided if you're careful. There are only two types of creatures here that are aggressive and will attack on sight. They are the undead and the outcast Iksar. Watch out for these and you shouldn't have too much trouble. The undead can be a problem, as they naturally blend in with the surrounding terrain. As with everywhere else on Norrath, the insects here will aid those of their kind, so be alert for swarming. The most profitable creatures are the scorpions, carrion hatchlings, and scaled wolf pups. All of these carry an item of value. The best thing about this zone is that it has monsters from level 1 to 20, so you can group and take on bigger stuff if you want. Another thing to note is that monsters in the reservoir never stray outside of it, and vice versa. The reservoir has monsters from levels 5 to 14, so you could stay in here for a long time.

    Final Verdict

    Watch out for aggressive wandering Iksar and Undead. Nothing much special about this zone, just kill, kill, kill.
