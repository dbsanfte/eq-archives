Laney

  • Race: Human

  • Class: Monk

  • God: Agnostic

   

A long time ago a young lady decided that she wished to battle against the evils of Norrath and to protect her hometown of Freeport.  She therefore became a monk, one of the noblest classes, smiled upon by the Gods.  Although understanding nothing of the challenge she had undertaken, she went forth into the world.  She bravely fought against the orcs who threatened her home town.  She was not an adventurer until her good friend explained to her that her role was not simply to protect her hometown, but to protect all of Norrath as well.  And so she travelled across the lands spreading cheer and reeking havoc.  She's made many friends yet only recently did she decide to join a guild.  She heard about The Last Ones and their pride from her sister Lizzy.  She is ready for this challenge and hopes that she will be a true knight in the spirit of TLO.  She has made many friends on her travells who she cares for very deeply.  Yet when she's down on her luck and it seems like nothing is going to get better she remembers one thing, "She's All That"!
