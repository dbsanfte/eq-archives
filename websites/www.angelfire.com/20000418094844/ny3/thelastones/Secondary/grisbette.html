Grisbette

  • Race: Dwarf

  • Class: Rogue

  • God:

    Grisbette, "Acquisitioner of Fine Things" (or simply
Gris) is in that awkward not-quite-an-adult stage,
since she grew up fending for herself in the
backstreets of Kaladim. Eventually she started her own
business of performing tasks for others.....yeah,
yeah, tasks, that's it. She's fearless and she's
quick, and she's all business when it comes to
carrying out a deal. Usually she is more quick to
bargain if a dwarven  ale or two is thrown in with the
deal. It is important to note that there is still
honor among some thieves, for she is loyal to her
friends and her guildmates. She may be young, but
anyone trying to pull a fast one on this streetwise
kid will likely find an uncomfortable jab to his back.
