Welcome to the home of the Knights of the Ironforge, a guild on the Xegony server of Everquest with the purposes of aiding others to the best of our abilites and to aid fellow guild members to the best of our abilities. I am Guild Master Rennoc Ironguard. I created this guild to combat the selfish acts of others. We all have seen these selfish acts. From the gnoll hoards of Blackburrow to the deadly orcs of Crushbone, cries of help have fallen upon deaf ears. I have seen this and felt its bitter sting personally. I joined a guild thinking that this would solve my problems. Yet that guild was no better. They refused to aid another guild member in need. That is why I formed this guild. There is no need to feel the bitterness of loneliness. There is no reason to feel rejected. Here, you have a home with friends and family. We are the knights of virtue and honor, of integrity and compassion, of might and steel. We are the Knights of the Ironforge.
