Joining: House of Sintarro

In order to join our grand guild, we have set forth minumum requirements:

  • Have at least 5 trainings,
  • Be a pure caster (Magician, Enchanter, Necromancer, or Wizard),
  • Able to roleplay,
  • Have a sponsor (Someone currently in the guild who also wishes for ye to join),
  • And any other requirement the inducting magistrate (officer) may set.

Once you have met these requirements, ye must contact us so we may review and set up a time and place to induct ye. In order to contact us, there are a number of ways. You may send a private letter (/Tell ...to get a list of current members in the game type "/Who All "House of Sintarro") to one of our members and have them sponsor ye. Or ye can send us an e-scroll (Email) and let us reply back with further directions. If you choose to use an e-scroll, please send it to Niomar@kscable.com.

*Note: If you can not find a sponsor, just email us anyway; we can work something out.
