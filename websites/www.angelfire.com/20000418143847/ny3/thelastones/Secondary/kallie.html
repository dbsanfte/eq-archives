Awards



Medal of Honor


"Medal of Honor" awarded to Knights Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie, and Bombure. On the dates of Jan 23, & 24th, this party of brave adventurers stood toe to toe with an evil KSer Wizard Dangt, courageously guarding their camp spot against the invading KSer. On both of these evenings the vile Dangt train killed our party, not once but twice, and still these brave knights returned to reclaim not only their bodies, but their rightful camp spot and to confront the evil Dangt and demand that he cease and desist. The GMs were petitioned, and on both nights these courageous knights risked life and experience again in order for the GM to witness such unjust conduct of the evil Dangt. And I am proud to say that on each night, the GMs acknowledged their right, and sternly issued a warning against the player record of Dangt. Hail and Well Met brave Knights Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie and Bombure! For your brave acts you are hereby awarded the TLO "Medal of Honor"!



Defender of Justice


"Defender of Justice" awarded to Knights Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie, Bombure, Carnus, Galivious, Izlaaru, Klithor, and Goad. On the date of Jan 25th, Izlaaru, Klithor, and Goad answered the call for protection from a vile KSer Dangt in Beholders Gorge from a rightful camping party of players Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie, Carnus, and Galivious. These 3 knights gave of their time selflessly to travel to the gorge, defend the rightful party, and courageously confronted the vile Dangt to cease and desist, and tactfully petitioned GMs to please appear and defend the rightful party. Although the party members were the target of the KSer, they are still to be recognized with the Defender of Justice award for their courage and determination, and also for their participation and tactfully petitioning the GM on the behalf of justice. I am proud to say, that justice was indeed bestowed upon the valiant knights of TLO as the GM Cammii appeared, warned Dangt of his past transgressions, and then before everyone's eyes to behold, she summarily banned Dangt from Norath. the GM reiterated that it was not tonight's event of KSing that caused Dangt to be banned, but his transgressions in the past, and for twice warnings against Train Killing parties Sunday and Monday nights, and to reiterate that KSing is not a bannable offence, but Train Killing is. Hail and Well Met brave Knights! For your brave deeds this night, you are all hereby dubbed "Defenders of Justice"!
