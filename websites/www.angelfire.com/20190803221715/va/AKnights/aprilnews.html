The Dark Elf controversy

I see that my decision to prohibit the Dark Elf from joining the Arcane Knights is being questioned, and I believe that your reasons for concern are good ones. I will attempt to clarify my concerns and the way I feel about the issue. The Dark Elves are an incredibly gifted race, no doubt. I personally have no feelings of hatred against them whatsoever. I have no doubt that among such race there are many fine citizens, many who are noble and who deserve the rights that we all enjoy in Norrath. However, as it has been said before, the actions of few may have consequences for many. If the Dark Elves have proven one thing in their history, it is that they cannot be trusted. They have long since been considered evil, dishonest, and disloyal. They have misused the powers of the arcane for the purpose of evil. This may all be coming to an end now. I have met Dark Elves who I would be pleased to call my brother, and I would love to let into my guild. However, as a leader sometimes decisions have to be made that you do not always want to make. I have to look at what is good for the whole, and what is fair. It is hard for me to put aside years of dishonesty and wickedness and allow them into my guild. The Arcane Knights stands for what is pure, just, and right. What place does an "evil race" have in a guild like that? Nevertheless, I am willing to allow the Dark Elves in if that is the wish of our guild members. Their entrance will not be as simple as it is for other races, and they will be required to complete a "test" before we will allow them in. I am a very open minded individual. I thought that my decision to exclude Dark Elves would be the wishes of those in my guild and be unquestioned. I now see that it may not be as I foresaw. I would like to know how other members fell. You are a part of this guild, and you should be allowed to express your feelings freely. Let me know, and then I will make my decision.

Thank you
