GUILD BANK

Aaam is the character in Freeport that holds all the Guild's extra gear for members to pick from. The Guild Bank is only for people to use on their characters, not to sell nor trade with. If you use something on the bank please return it when you are done with the item or get an upgrade, trading in things when you pick up an item whenever possible. People donate things to the bank with the assumption that it will be not only used but kept within the guild. After all they could have sold it instead of letting you have it.

Aaam stays near the Freeport Bank and will show up whenever we can get him on to help people out. Anyone who donates an item can come get it back if they want.
