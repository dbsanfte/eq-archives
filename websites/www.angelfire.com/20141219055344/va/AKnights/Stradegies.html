Stradegies

Here are some survival skills that you can use as a beginner when you enter the land of Norrath-

Beginner stradgies-

Always know where the guards are, they will can save you if you get into a close fight.

Try not to wander around aimlessly. Know where you are so you can find your corpse if you die. This is especially important later when dying means you lose everything.

In the beginning, don't be afraid to die. Don't feel you suck if you die A LOT. It is just a very unpleasant part of EQ that you will have to endure constantly in the beginnning. (Death is a pain starting at Lv. 4 because from that point on you start with NOTHING, after you die, except for your spell book.)

"Consider" EVERYTHING. Never fight anything alone that is not at least considered a gamble. When you consider something, the text that tells you the risk of that object will be displayed in different color. What the message says does not matter that much. The color alone can tell you if it is safe to fight.

Red- What would you like your tombstone to say?
Yellow- Do not attack when you are alone if you treasure your life
 White- This is a gamble when you are on your own, but you sometimes have to take risk, especially in the beginning. Just make sure you start this battle when your HP and mana are full.
 Blue- battles you should go into ready for a pretty easy fight, but there is still a risk, so be prepared or green text when considered are fights you should win
Green- Ahem….GO! (But don't expect any EXP for such an easy kill)
