Krelian

 



Name: Krelian
Race: Dark Elf
Class: Necromancer
Deity: Innoruuk
Level: 18 (as of Jan. 3 '00)

My Life is a rather difficult but rewarding (honest) one, due to my choice of race and class.
I do have memories of regret, most often brought about by murderous NPC's that I had done nothing against. Refusal to do buisiness with a 'vile' Necromancer is common thinking among most merchants of Norrath. My beliefs conflict with theirs, so in acts of stubborness and willingness to lose a sell they shun you to be eaten by wolves (or Crocs...).
My home town of Neriak judges me admirably (seriously!), and as such protects me from opposing forces and offers exceptional deals of trade. Initially, I had difficulties in defending my city from attackers of any sort, be they human or lowly monster. Now, after the 18th season in my profession, I can honestly say that this is the only way for one to adventure... With a Skeleton pet and debilitating spells, I am able to tackle foes of greater strength alone.
I feel extremely powerful when travelling, and have particular strengths versus Undead foes, which demonstrate quite frequently. In the Oasis of Marr I have stayed for some six seasons, gradually becoming more knowledgeable, and powerful, which each slain enemy.
I will continue to make a name for my guild and guild bretheren, a name of honesty, fairness, and excellence. Only time can tell of the future's path for myself, of each waking moment I shall strive to grow and better myself, and the world I live in.
