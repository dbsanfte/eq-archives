Omarguk Bonesmasher

 

Race: Troll
Class: Warrior
Deity: Rallos Zek

Omarguk, aka Omar, spent much of his younger bashing days killing froglok's
in the swamps of Innothule. Every once in a while he would gather enough
courage to provoke a swamp alligator or venture into upper guk to get some
fresh "meats".  As a young troll, Omar had many pleasant memories and a few
not so pleasant.  One of his finer moments was during his eleventh lvl
during the Troll Ogre Bash O'rama, where he won both of his matches with his
competing Ogres.  Omar quickly outgrew Innothule Swamp and moved to SRO,
then to Oasis at lvl 14.  This area was great for experience and still
provided a huge supply of gator meat. Also, this is where he gained most of
his bronze armor. However, Omar learned that most of the "humies" here did
not like him.  This racial hatred was something Omar learned to deal with
quickly.  He began to learn how to travel without being noticed by those
discriminatory races.  These travel tactics allowed Omar to move throughout
Norath without being noticed.  These places were essential for him to
complete his crafted armor quests.  Upon his adventures Omar had much fun
hunting/questing with the "TLO" guild members.  Just to name a few: Goad,
Izzy, Ultigar, Rykar, Koramar, and even little Sheltem.  Currently Omar is
hunting in Cazic Thule.  He has successfully ventured into various sections
of Lower Guk. In the near future, Omar and Goad plan on an adventure to
MistMoore Keep to complete his final Crafted Armor quest-the breast plate.
Goad and Omar have gallantly fought side by side during all the crafted
armor quests. Omar knows there is nothing we can't accomplish together as
knights of the "TLO's".
