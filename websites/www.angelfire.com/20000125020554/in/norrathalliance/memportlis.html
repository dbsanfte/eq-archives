Portlis

Portlis is and was the mastermind scammer behind our rise to power in Ultima Online. Scamming and screwing people over (in more ways than one) are his specialties. Also, he will deal in rare and magical items. Some of his characters include a money maker character, a scammer >=), and a general all-around kick-ass Pk (Portlis) to "beast over" anyone who crosses his path.
