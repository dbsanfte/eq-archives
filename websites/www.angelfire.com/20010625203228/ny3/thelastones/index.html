To make a long story short. Our guild founder Kadelplexil, returned to EQ after almost a year of inactivity and logs on one day and begins to demand control of the guild, well suffice it to say that our members objected strongly, most members had never even met this person claiming to be the leader of TLO. Well, like a childish little kiddie with his feelings hurt at rejections, Kadelplexil uses the /guild disband command (as his original character still had guild leader status in our guild) and disbanded us right then and there. After much petitioning by members and arguing with GMs, we were informed that there is nothing that they can do about it, small wonder eh. Being a close knit guild, built on friendships and bonds, we hike up out bootstraps, pull together (difficult using all /tells I might add hehehe) and reform the guild as "Unbroken" in testament to our unbreakable bonds of friendships, loyalty to honor, justice, and unity that is our guild. HaaaaZaaaaa! Long live the Knights Unbroken until time unending!
