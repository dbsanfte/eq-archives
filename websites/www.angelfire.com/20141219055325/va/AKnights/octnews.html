Saturday, October 9th

About our Last Meeting

For those of you who could not make it to our meeting last night, here is a summary of what has been discussed:

  • All members who have been with us for a while have been automatically promoted to the rank of Knight.
  • Squires (new members) will now be assigned to a Knight for a period of two weeks to journey with them
    and make certain they are the kind of member we need. After this period, the Squire may be promoted to
    full Knight as per the recommandation of the mentor Knight.
  • A system of points will be instituated in order for Knights to attain higher ranks. Points will be awarded as
    per a Knight's participation in guild activities.
  • It was suggested that we use the correct rank address when talking to each other (even in guildsay) as a way
    to make ranking more meaningfull. Knights should be address as Sir/Madam, First Knights as First, elders as
    Elder and of course, sages as Sage. Squires will be addressed as Squire.
  • Cdor LionsBane has been promoted officially to the rank of Sage. Welcome to the Council Sage Cdor.

I hope I didnt forget any of the important issues that were discussed.

All in all we had great fun before, during and after the meeting. I was glad to see so many of you attend. The Guild has been in a transition period lately but thing will stabilize once more. We are going to do more official activities soon and hope to become one of the more active and respected guild in Norrath.

See you all in game,

Balduk Swiftmind,
Council of Sages.
