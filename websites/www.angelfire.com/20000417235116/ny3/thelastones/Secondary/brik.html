Brik

 

  • Race: Troll
  • Class: ShadowKnight
  • God: Innoruk

       Brik was once a happy troll. He had a loving mother and father, and a
    happy home in the swamp. His main goal in life was to inherit the family
    business, that of making Bricks and building structures.
    But one day a long time ago, a tragic event would shake Brik's life forever.
    While on the job making his famous Brik's Bricks, his contractor came by and
    laid him off. Outraged, he demanded an explanation for this course of
    events. The response he got was that his Bricks were too costly and labor
    too high. He also learned that a band of froglocs were moving into his
    territory, and they where making bricks that were cheaper and working faster
    then one troll alone could do. So in a fit of furry, Brik did what his
    father always preached him to do, "If ya can't beat them, Kill 'em!" and
    that is exactly what Brik did. He went to grobb and became a shadow Knight
    that promised to vanquish all Froglocs from his swamp, and to reclaim his
    birthright as the best Brik maker and layer in the swamp.
