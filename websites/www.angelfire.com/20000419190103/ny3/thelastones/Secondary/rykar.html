Rykar Derashken



Race: Half-Elf
Class: Ranger
God: Tunare

Character Info: Rykar is a ranger of moderate experience. He wanted to be like his older brother Ethan who was a well known ranger/mage from the Sword Coast campaigning the Neverwinter Nights. Rykar journeyed to the lands of Norrath like so many other pilgrims in search of new places to discover and riches to be had. He found himself in the beginning lost forever in the Greater Faydark. Orcs everywhere, Rykar drudged his way early on killing the blue meanies and getting rewarded for his work by his guildmaster Maesyn Trueshot. He advanced his skills enough that he ventured to the main continent of Antonica. His first trip to and through Freeport was an arduous journey in and of itself. Getting lost at each corner, Rykar eventually got himself a guide to lead him around. From Freeport, Rykar trekked onward to the happiest place on Norrath, Rivervale. From Rivervale, he based his travels and adventures from there to the rest of the continent even going as far as Erudin. Life passed by quickly for Rykar till he saw a band of people who shared his ire for the greedy and wicked of the world. He joined these people in their cause and he now runs amok all over Norrath with his fervent companions. Still he gets lost a lot which causes a lot of deaths to those around him. May he someday actually figure out that a maxxed out sense heading does not make him a guide.
