Kunark Adventuring for Rangers by Chett Darkforest

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------Grab your gear and a few of your friends, and set sail for the lost continent of Kunark. After a long voyage at Sea (Timorous Deep) the Great ship will eventually arrive at Fironia Vie the only safehaven for those of the good race on all of Kunark. Once the Ship has docked, get a bind, and have everyone restock their supplies (water, food, arrows, bandages...ect), for it will be a long time ere you return to the saftey found with in the mighty walls of Fironia Vie. Once everyone in your party is prepared to travel the long and hard journey ahead, cross the heavily guarded bridge that connects Fironia Vie to the mainland of Kunark. Be on your highest alert and upmost guard as your party travels on their expedition of Kunark, for there are many dangerous creatures to be found roaming within these wild and untamed lands.

EPIC QUEST - Part One (Starting your Epic)
Find Telin Darkforest (no relation to Chett) in the Burning Woods, Kunark and hail him.

EPIC QUEST - Part 4 (The Platinum Speckled Powder)
If you have given Merdan Fleetfoot the "Rose of Firiona" and have received the "Wood Painting", look for a Human Skeleton while your in the Frontieer Mts. If you give the Human Skeleton the Wooden Painting, it will disintegrate. Once the Skelly has disintegrated pick up the "Silver Chained Locket" that it will leave behind. This Locket needs to be given to Niera Farbreeze in the Surefall Glades, (this is to recieve the Platinum Speckled Powder, which is part of the Ranger Epic).

EPIC QUEST - Part 5 (Obtain a Jade Reaver from CoM, Kunark)
Within Kunark there are many lost ruins, of which Bards spin marvelous stories about in the taverns of Antonica, Faydwer, and Odus. But perhaps the most famous ruins are those which the bards refer to as the City of Mists. For it has been told that within the ruins, there can be found many terrible, and foul creatures of Doom. The most Infamous of these abominations are known as the Black Reavers, which are feared for their tinted Axes.
