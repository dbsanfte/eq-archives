VELIOUS IMAGES

The New Lands of Velious are, for the most part, snow and trees, not very exciting. However, within the dungeons and in rare places, there are some very exciting and interesting spots.
Those whacky gnomes came up with a ship to bust through the ice on their way to Velious. Why they take off from Ro to get to this ship mystifies even the gnomes, but there you go.
The Enraged Chef, on the second floor of the Tower of Shadows in Velious. This hideous wench apparently is upset that she has Drolvargs doing all her cooking.
This empty suit of armor is a non undead monster called the Armored Shadow in the Tower of Shadows. It is reeling from a blow by Moonglum here!
Deep in the Crystal Caverns is this odd beast that looks like a tiny Faydedar. It apparently is fond of the velium crystals that are all throughout the mines.
Here we see the Queen Drachnia, queen of the spiders deep under the Crystal Caverns. She is under asssault, her lot in life to show up and die within seconds.
The Port to Velious has a huge, roaring dragon statue over it. Many dragon statues and themes are in the lands, this one apparently roaring a warning that the porter is rather dangerous to use.
One of the more interesting creatures wandering the snowy wastes of Velious is this Manticore. Uncommon and considerably more dangerous than they appear, these are dangerous brutes

The Warrens are open on the Test Server, and one of our people is over there getting information and pics to bring to us. They are presently only accesible through the Paineel newbie area (off the room that leads to the watery entrance to the Hole). The Warrens is a sort of Kobold Crushbone/Blackburrow, full of the furry creatures, bats, and other small things. It is roughly level 5-30, and drops a variety of new lower level items and new things. Most of the benefit of this dungeon so far appears to be good experience for the level, some place fun and new to go, and faction with both Paineel and Erudia. The items are unique looking, and sell for pretty good money although they aren't anything you'd twink a character with. Here are a few of the things seen so far.
