Goad DemGood

  • Race: Troll

  • Class: Warrior

  • God: Ralos Zek

     Goad was but a small tad trollie growing up in the Innothule swamps. Him and his friend Gazkum(Izlaaru) had much fun barshing frogloks and kobolds to their hearts content, until a friend, Kadelplexel invited him to join a much larger community called "The Last Ones". It opened up the whole world of Norath to him, and Goad soon began to wander, making new friends, and discovering all manner of strange lands. Kadelplexel soon vanished from the face of Norath, never to be heard from again, and so a meeting of TLO Knights was called, and Ralfo proudly assumed the role of Guild Leader, he built a beautiful home for us knights to call home, and a tavern for us all to gather, drink and be merry in. TLO prospered, grew, and more friends were made. Goad was growing up now, and yearned for more events and excitement, so he decides to sponsor TLO's first event and called it the Ogre/Troll Bash O Rama. Word was passed throughout the lands, and upon the day of celebration, many a brave knight attended. Drinks were passed, and knights dueled all the day long, and into the evening. It was a joyous day in the memory of this troll, and again TLO prospered as our name and honor was spread across Norath. Again our numbers swelled, and many more friends were joined. Goad continues to travel across the lands spreading good cheer, leading brave parties to cleans foul dungeons of evil, and drinking his share of ogreswill. Again a time of change is upon The Last Ones, for our brave leader Ralfo tires of the weight of his burden, and requests to retire. Ralfo appoints Goad as the new leader of TLO, and a joyous day it is. I proudly serve my brothers and sisters, and promise to diligently guard our house from harm, and lead us into the new millennium with courage and honor until I too pass the banner of TLO to the next proud knight. And most important of all, I promise to guard our tavern, and ensure a continual supply of spirits for all HaaaaZaaaaa !!!

Awards



Defender of Justice


"Defender of Justice" awarded to Knights Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie, Bombure, Carnus, Galivious, Izlaaru, Klithor, and Goad. On the date of Jan 25th, Izlaaru, Klithor, and Goad answered the call for protection from a vile KSer Dangt in Beholders Gorge from a rightful camping party of players Belledonna, Darkian, Kallie, Carnus, and Galivious. These 3 knights gave of their time selflessly to travel to the gorge, defend the rightful party, and courageously confronted the vile Dangt to cease and desist, and tactfully petitioned GMs to please appear and defend the rightful party. Although the party members were the target of the KSer, they are still to be recognized with the Defender of Justice award for their courage and determination, and also for their participation and tactfully petitioning the GM on the behalf of justice. I am proud to say, that justice was indeed bestowed upon the valiant knights of TLO as the GM Cammii appeared, warned Dangt of his past transgressions, and then before everyone's eyes to behold, she summarily banned Dangt from Norath. the GM reiterated that it was not tonight's event of KSing that caused Dangt to be banned, but his transgressions in the past, and for twice warnings against Train Killing parties Sunday and Monday nights, and to reiterate that KSing is not a bannable offence, but Train Killing is. Hail and Well Met brave Knights! For your brave deeds this night, you are all hereby dubbed "Defenders of Justice"!
