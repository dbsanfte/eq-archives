Wednesday, December 15

Christmas Party
(12:54 AM EST)

Greetings everyone,

Sunday, December 19th will be our Christmas party. It will be held in the Tox Forest outside of Erudin. The place was chosen because its relatively empty and there aren't the dangers of other places. Hopefully we will have the Arcane Knights Airline up and running to aid in getting everyone there. It will be at 3pm EST, 12pm PST and there will be gift exchange in a Chinese Christmas.

I will explain the gift process. Everyone draws a number. The person who gets the lowest number is first. He chooses a present from someone and opens it (trade window). he announces what it is and then the next person goes. He has a choice. Take a gift from someone else and open it or take the gift that the first person opened. If he takes it from the first person, the first person get to choose another gift. You may not take back the gift that is taken from you. This continues till all the gifts are handed out.

So everyone who comes bring something, anything that you may feel someone would enjoy having. There will be a little variation in the process do to Lore items seeing how you can have only one. And please no No Drop items =P

If we get the ingredients we will get some Chocolate Cake made by Lorana. I will supply the beverages. I hope to see you all there.

Good Journeys and Happy Hunting,

Snydar Nightwind
