Constitution

Section I: Guildmaster

Article 1: The head of the Arcane Knights, and final authority will be the Guildmaster.
Article 2: The Guild Master will be the founder of the Arcane Knights, Serro Deimos.
Article 3: The Guild Master will also serve as the supreme judge on all matters.

Section II: Lieutenants

Article 1: The Guild Master will appoint two Lieutenants, whom will be his advisors and council in making guild decisions.
Article 2: These Lieutenants will be the second in command, all below them must submit to their authority.
Article 3: In times of crisis, the Guild Master may chose to have only one Lieutenants or no Lieutenants at all.

Section III: Elders

Article 1: Elders will be appointed by Guildmaster
Article 2: Each class that is in the Arcane Knights will have an Elder to represent them.
Article 3: The Elders will be in charge of seeing their class is treated fairly and that the guild meets the needs of that class.
Article 4: The Elders will select a head elder, who's main pupose will be to serve as third in the chain of command, and will serve as the represenative from the elders to Guildmaster and Lieutenants.


Section IV: Members

Article 1: Members main responsibility will be to serve, protect, defend, and build the Arcane Knights.
Article 3: Member may recieve titles and higher rank by completeing guild quest that will be issued by the Guild Master, Lieutenants, and Elders
Article 4: Members who preform well will officially become Knights, and will recieve the title "Sir".

 

 

Code of Conduct

o All members will be expected to submit to the authority of their superiors
o All members will be expected to serve the guild.
o Member will be expected to show honor, dignity, and respect in all things that they do.
o Members will be allowed and expected to make suggestions as to how the guild can be improved.
o Members will be expected to participate in guild activities, but not required to do so.
