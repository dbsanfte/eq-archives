Hierarchy

 

 

  1. Guild Leader
  • The guild leader is in charge of calling the guild to official meetings and organizing the voting process.
  • All complaints may be taken directly to him to be brought upon the elected Hierarchy.
  • The guild leader is given one vote in all decisions.
  • Responsible for organizing guild quests and getting guild quest items.
  1. Elected Hierarchy
  • The elected Hierarchy is responsible for recruiting members as well as helping in the organization of events and getting guild run quest items.
  • Each of the 3 members is given one vote on all decisions.
  • Any problems or Ideas brought to them they must bring to the guild leader.
  • Each member of the elected Hierarchy man set up a sub system for their respected followers allowing for a sub hierarchy with their group (casters, hybrids, healers).
  • Each Member of the elected hierarchy has a right to their own email address highmagi@orderofmagi.org, highpriest@orderofmagi.org, and captainofguard@orderofmagi.org The elected hierarchy must email webmaster@orderofmagi.org to get this set up.
  1. Other Officers
  • These include all founders as well as any outstanding member given officer status.
  • They may add members as well as bring comments, problems, and ideas to the guild leader and act as a stand in replacement if a Elected Hierarchy member is not on-line.
  • Help with origination of events and getting guild run quest items.
  • They are allowed to vote as regular members
  • Each officer may have a email redirect so that email sent to charatername@orderofmagi.org will be sent to thier email address. They must email webmaster@orderofmagi to get this set up.
  1. Regular Members
  • This includes all members who have achieved one month in the guild and are in good standing.
  • All regular members combined have one vote. (A vote is taken of all members and majority wins and that is taken as one vote.)
  • All problems may be taken to the Officers or Elected Hierarchy for answers.
  • Problems may be taken directly to the guild leader only if he is online.
  • They may sponsor new members for recruitment as well submit any ideas for web page design and quests.
  1. Recruits
  • This includes all members of less than one month of membership and all potential members as well as all members who for some reason or another are in temporary bad standing with the guild.
  • Recruits have no voting rights, but may bring problems to officers and suggest quests and events.
  • Recruits may be promoted early by requesting a guild quest as well as just exemplary behavior in favor of the guild. (All we ask is that you show us that you care)
