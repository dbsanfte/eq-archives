And the race is on!

This is a short look into one of the races that may study the grand art of Enchantments.

today's race Human (Qeynos):
The human city of Qeynos is possible the most populated and therefore crowded cities in Norrath. The streets of this city are easily confusing to any newcomer and sometimes even to a vetern of our world. The merchants of this city sell most anything and most of the guilds of norrath are represented here, even down to the Necromancer guild deep in the Qeynos sewer system.

humans begin with stats that are generally well balanced with no one stat showing a great improvement over the others. though this seems bad it does allow the greatest amount of flexibility for bonus points to be placed also humans are blessed with access to most of the cities of norrath depending upon the deity of their choice.

Starting stats

Human Enchanter

Starting city: Qeynos(note: humans may start in Freeport also)
Strength: 75
Stamina: 75
Agility: 75
Dexterity: 75
Wisdom: 75
Intelligence: 85
Charisma: 85

Note: stats in blue are considered the main stats needed for the enchanter class.

Items beginning with: (5) fish cakes, (5) Milk skins, rusty dagger, Shallow Breath spell, Minor Shield spell, guild note and book of discord(pvp)

Spell of the week

Spell name: Tashan
Level: (4) Four
Where Found: Your guild hall
Description: Lowers targets Magic resistance
Required ingredients: none

Damage: none

Special: reduces targets magic resistance by -10 to 13

Notes: This is a must have for young enchanters and old alike until you get the upgrade at lvl 20 (Tashani). If you spend any time in a group with other magic users they will see the reduction in resists on thier spells. A final note at 12th lvl, I gained charm and have found that if i mesmerise a mob(sohe wont agro on nondamage spells) and cast tashan on him i get a pet for a longer time than if i just cast charm.
If you have information on a spell of the Circle of Enchantments please fill out the Spell submission form.
