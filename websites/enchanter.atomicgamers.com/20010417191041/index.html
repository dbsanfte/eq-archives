Some contest info Posted Monday, April 16, 2001 by draconal
I am webmaster of a couple of new game sites (Since Jan 1st) based on voice
communication and voice control in games. Game Voice Club & Team Command
Club (Soon to be joined together into one large voice enabled community) is
dedicated to bringing new and exciting things to the gaming community. Our
current offerings for members include Online Meetings, Voice User Database,
Club dedicated Game/Chat Servers, Voice enabled Clans, Contests,
Tournaments, Voice Application Information/Help, ICQ ActiveList, Clan
hosting, among many other features. We are currently operating non-profit
(No cheap advertising, all volunteer) and membership into the Clubs is free.
We are very proud of what we have built this past few months and wanted to
inform you of several competitions/contests we are holding. The Everquest
Contest in particular should interest you and your readers I think :)

Everquest Contest

Voice of Norrathians (Part of GVC Network) will be hosting a guild wide
quest with physical real life prizes as a reward to the best groups of two.
The goal of this quest will be a newbie scavenger hunt. The purpose of this
quest is to get all guild members together regardless of the server they
play on. Winning team of two (2) Grand Prize winners will receive a Game
Commander 2. One (1) 2nd place winner (see below) will receive a copy of the
PC game Moon Project (Approx. value $39.99 US).
(The 2nd place prize will be determined by the winning team rolling a 1-100
based die to see which partner gets the game, the losing partner will get a
consolation prize (Nascar Racing 3)for participating). Two (2) 3rd place
winners will get collectable edition action figures of an Iksar Necromancer
and a Dark Elf Mage. Go here for details:
http://www.gamevoiceclub.com/gamevoice/eqcontest.asp

Mechwarrior 4 Tournament

This is the first of many game tournaments in which GVC will sponsor and
award winners with fun prizes. The Mechwarrior 4 tournament will begin on
Saturday May 5th at 9am Eastern time (GMT -5 Summer). This is your chance to
prove your mettle and win a either a Microsoft Force Feedback 2 Joystick or
a copy of Age of Empires 2: Age of Kings. This is a 4 tier competition which
of which you can view all the details here...
http://www.gamevoiceclub.com/gamevoice/mw4tour.asp

Upload Contest

The Grand Prize winner will walk away with their own Hercules 3D Prophet II
MX videocard (Approx. Retail Value (US) $99.99). The runner-up will receive
a PC game to be determined by GVC staff (Approx. Retail Value (US)
$19.99-39.99). Go here for details :
http://www.gamevoiceclub.com/gamevoice/contest1.asp

Anyway, thought we would say hello and ask you to mention one or more of our
contests/tournaments to your readers :)

`Smaug
Marty
webmaster@gamevoiceclub.com
http://www.gamevoiceclub.com & http://www.teamcommandclub.com
