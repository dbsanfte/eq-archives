And the race is on!

This is a short look into one of the races that may study the grand art of Enchantments.

today's race Erudite:
The erudite are peaceful humans who have for whatever reason hidden themselves on the island of odus, so let us first talk of their city Erudin. Edudin is a fairly easy to navigate city with alot of open space, the Erudites in their search for knowledge discovered the art of teleportation and bound crystals all thruout their city to help with travel (Tho I have heard others say that the transport that these crystals provide makes them feel ill I have felt no such sickness). Erudites in the safety of their homeland have amassed a great knowledge of magic and have built a library with many wondrous spells within, and within the very palace itself is the three great halls of the spell casters one of which is the guild of the craftsman, The Enchanters Guild hall. Within this guild you will find most of your spells and all the tools needed to begin your trade skill, jewelcrafting which at higher levels and with the addition of the enchant silver, gold, platinum and electrum spells and certain gems found around the world will lead to the creation of much sought after Magical Jewelry.

Erudites begin their existence with a high Intelligence and therefore are very well suited for a life as a spell caster and of a tradesman but lack the physical strength to defend themselves well in hand to hand combat as seen in the stats below.

Aged Jade silver rings:

ingredients: (1) enchanted silver bar and (1) jade gem

Spell of the week

for the first volume I will talk briefly about the Enchanter's beginning spell Shallow Breath

Shallow Breath

Where Found: in your inventory when you begin play

Damage: 1-8 points per hit + special (this increases as you reach new spell levels +1 per spell lvl max is I believe +4)

Special: this is the first in a long line of DOT (damage over time) spells the opponent will suffer extra damage for about 5 - 10 sec or so. the amount of damage is unknown at this time.

Notes:
This is the beginning attack spell of the Enchanter class. This will be your savior in battle, you first have to learn to time your attacks I do this by waiting till I see the red line appear in the text area then click my spell NOTE: don't time it to the attack motion of the creature as this is rarely at the same speed as its actual attack. Once you have your first lvl pet spell the use of this spell skyrockets but all that for next volume. Another great use of this spell is to lure monsters to you by casting this upon them.
