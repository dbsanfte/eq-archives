The Breeze LOC debate solved Posted Friday, May 5, 2000 by draconal
It appears that there may be some confusion regarding the Breeze spell. It is available in 2 places. One is the elven outpost in "Fironia Vie", where the boat from Butcherblock docks, and it costs around 11pp. The other place you can purchase it is in "Overthere". This is where the boat from Oasis docks, and the spell costs around 22pp there. Fironia Vie is for the good races, and Overthere is for the evil races. I have no idea about the coordinates, but both posts may be right. The first one does not mention what zone they were in, but the price of 22pp sounds like they were in Overthere, hence the different coordinates.

Hope this helps.

Shai

Less time for the meat of the game and less time I can spend doing real life things, and most importantly LESS FUN! (The purpose of the game is FUN in the first place it’s not a documentary) All this running doesn't add to game play or enjoyment it takes away from it. Let us bind in the timorous deep so I can still play the game and yet have a little more time for my husband and my dear baby Ben..

This is my plea, let us have more time for the meat of the game, and spend less time doing drudge work! Please make the timorous deep balanced once again.

And before anyone tells me about how the room makes the game unbalanced, or what ever, Look at the bottom line, I Play this game for one reason FUN, the teleporting room made the game more fun for me, that’s all I want to know about, The fun factor. And not having the teleporting room available to me makes it less fun more drudge; about this there is no argument. :P

I have been working on this quest for the Iksar
illusion for the last 3 days. Last night, I ran for

offset by the gain in all practical purposes.

strive until I succeed. Good hunting and safe
journeys to you..

Enchanter Nerf or Enhancement?? Posted Wednesday, April 19, 2000 by draconal
So WHY after many many months of this stacking it is suddenly removed? I mean there is going to be a spell that gives both later in enchanter new expansion spells life, so why NERF us now.??? granted why do i need both, well its my mana and I like both buffs, since I am an intellegence based casters I recieve no cheating buff or anything, so WHY . Please explain this??????

It's our opinion that each spell granting the same primary stat that it used to, plus a lesser value of the secondary stat is an enhancement. It actually gives enchanters a way to buff INT prior to them being able to before.

It's not about game balance, so I suppose if enough people want it to go back the other way we'll seriously consider it.

-Gordon
