News: Sigil Games

Sigil Games Snatches Another EverQuest Person

Sigil Games Online, a mysterious developer founded by a group of ex-EverQuest creators, announced today that it has hired away EverQuest lead programmer Steve Clover from Sony Online Entertainment. Clover, who worked on the original EverQuest design and was the head coder of the game and its first four expansions, will bring his 13 years of experience over to the independent PC software house.

"Steve and I go way back as both friends and co-workers, developing computer games together since 1989," said Sigil founder Brad McQuaid in a statement. Clover was pretty excited himself: "This is the third company I've been involved in with Brad...I'm confident our mutual philosophies, passion, and dedication when it comes to game development will be harnessed once again to create popular and successful products." With this latest hire, Sigil Games now includes the co-designer (McQuaid), team manager (Jeff Butler), character designer (Keith Parkinson), and lead programmer from the original EverQuest staff. So what are they up to? Who knows-the only announcement they've made is a publishing deal with Microsoft back in May. Hopefully they'll spill the beans on something soon.
