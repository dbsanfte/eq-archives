Welcome to the Knights of the Green Mist!

     "So it is that was born a special group of Iksar bent on the freedom of their race, no longer closed minded about the enemies of their people. These Knights remember that it was a Devine Act that gave them their freedom and they look back to the time of the Green Mists for inspiration in the days ahead. So did they form the Knights of the Green Mist, drawing not only from the ranks of all the groups of the Iksar but also enlisting the aid of the softskins of like mind. The Knights operate outside of the boundaries of the rulers of Cabilis. They have one, and only one true goal: the destruction of the greatest threat ever known to all of Norrath, the Dragon menace. To this end have they sought out many of the soft skinned races and found those that too realize the threat the Dragon pose to all races and joined together to exterminate this threat…"


~ Zarkorsis Kunzar

Guild Announcements: Important KGM Site Updates
Posted by: Xorrin Aarikis on Monday, January 14, 2002 - 04:31 PM CST
I am working on some modifications to the main site which will require Guild Members to register their PRIMARY character on the site and for users to log in. This is the primary character you play that is in the Knights of the Green Mist. If you are a guest to the site (ie: a member of one of our Alliance Guilds), please indicate so under the COMMENTS section of your registration.

This registration is very important for many reasons, including:

- It will provide me the necessary information to set up your free email account and web space for your character.

- It will enable be to grant you the appropriate access level to the site.

Many of the changes being made to the site are restricted to active guild members. If you do not register, you will not be able to access these areas. Some of these sections include Guild Announcements, Raid Announcements, Calendar of Events, IRC Chat, Message Forums, etc.

Guests will be granted limited access to certain areas of the site, based on their affiliation (ie: Allied guild members will have access to special forums, visiting guild leaders and officers will have access to Alliance news sections, etc).

Also, when registering, be sure to leave a VALID email address! Some of you have not been doing this! This address is not shown to visitors. It is used by the system to email you your temporary password so you can log in, and also to email you a replacement password, should you forget it. Accounts found with invalid email addresses will be deleted, and you will have to re-register.

Finally, when registering, please use the full name (first and surname) of your main KGM character. This will ensure that your username is unique, people will know who you are when posting messages, and your email address and web space will match your character. ONLY ONE ACCOUNT IS PERMITTED PER PLAYER. Officers may be granted additional web space, should the need arise.

Thanks,
- Xorrin

RAID: Saturday at Unrest
Posted by: Xorrin Aarikis on Friday, January 04, 2002 - 01:53 AM CST

U N R E S T     R A I D ! ! ! ! !

DATE:            SATURDAY, JANUARY 12th, 2002

TIME:            12pm PST (3pm EST)

ZONE:            Estate of Unrest (FAYDWER)

LEVELS:        14 to 35 +

XP BONUS:    N/A

MEETING AT:          We will be meeting at the zone line to Unrest on the Dagnor's Cauldron side (approx /LOC -1800, -500).  Please plan on arriving at least 30 minutes prior to the scheduled start of the event so we can organize the groups and answer any questions before zoning in.
Once everyone is together, we will all zone into Unrest as a group.

ATTENDEES:          All active members of the Knights of the Green Mist are encouraged to attend.  Each Knight may bring two non-member guests.  We will be joined by our allied guild, Tempest of Fate for this event.  Guests are not permitted to bring their own guests without prior authorization of an Officer of their guild.

WHAT TO BRING:          Just bring yourself.  verant will provide the mobs and loot.  We'll provide the Clerics, refreshments and bad jokes.  If you're a brewer or baker, feel free to show off your talents.  Just remember to bring enoughfor everyone!  =)

CONTACT:
          KGM:  Xorrin Aarikis          (xorrin@kgm-vazaelle.org)
           ToF:   Ilyrana Ty` Quella                   (torgoc@hotmail.com)

TRAVELING TO AND FROM UNREST:          The Estate of Unrest is reached by passing through the length of Dagnor's Cauldron to a small tunnel on the south end.  (Dagnor's Cauldron is accessable from Butcherblock Mountains at approximately /LOC -300, -2800.)  Most people run along the west end of the lake close to the water, but you need to be careful of the numerous aqua goblins moving throughout the area.


Note: Please arrive about 30 minutes early to ensure your spot in a group. Contact Xorrin in game upon arrival.
