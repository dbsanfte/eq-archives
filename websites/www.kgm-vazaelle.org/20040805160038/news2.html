EverQuest Breaks Into Six Figures

Over 100,000 EverQuest players were logged into the online game at the same time over the weekend, according to an announcement by Sony Online Entertainment today. The new record marks the largest number of players SOE has hosted simultaneously since their all-encompassing RPG began public operation in March of 1999. "Reaching such an important milestone is proof that EverQuest continues to enjoy wide popularity bringing people together from all over the world," said SOE chief operating officer John Smedley. "As we continue to expand EverQuest, most recently launching in the Korean market, we expect to surpass even greater milestones in the future. The EverQuest phenomenon is just beginning."

SOE has never been busier trying to get the most out of their most popular online franchise. EverQuest II is currently under development for a 2003 release, right alongside a PlayStation 2 port and an original PC real time strategy game. PC gamers will never have another moment of free time as long as they live, will they?
