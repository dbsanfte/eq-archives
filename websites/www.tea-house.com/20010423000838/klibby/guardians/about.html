About The Guardians of Norrath

 

     Welcome to our world of Everquest.  If you are wondering what exactly we are, you've come to the right place.  The Guardians of Norrath is a guild on the game known around the world as Everquest, yes that's right the game more addicting than nicotine.  This is a massively online multi-player role-playing game.  Supposedly taking place on a far away planet starting sometime in the year 3000 (the in-game date keeps going, currently it is around the year 3370, yet the saga continues)

Where are we?

     The Guardians of Norrath is a guild on the Xegony Server of Everquest (In game we are known as "Norrath's Guardians").  We are a semi-role-playing.  By this I mean, in the guild channel we do not require that you speak in a 14th century style and you may hold conversations irrelevant to the game, it doesn't matter.  However, we will hold events in the game that hold a purpose to the guild charter which is quoted below. 

What are we?

     The Guardians of Norrath is not a holy guild nor an evil guild, it is a combination of both good and evil.  How is this you ask? Simple, this is a combination of the holy, and evil playing good united together as one guild to fight against the corruptions of Norrath and furthermore to protect our planet and the natural state of it, hence the name, "Guardians of Norrath".

Why are we here? (Charter)

The following is an excerpt from the guild charter for the "Guardians of Norrath":
    "Long ago, a great dragon known as the Crystinine Dragon brought fourth a new life unto this lifeless desert oasis of a planet we now call Norrath, and our home.  Through the evolution of Norrath, like any world, a holy and an evil side hath developed.  Unto this day, May 5th, 3177, good and evil have been sworn enemies in rage and anger devoting their lives in defeating the next.  Alas, by the powers of Mithanial Marr come the birth of a new guild of the holy and evil united as one to fight the corruption of the world and to return Norrath to what it once was! 'Tis the guild of The Guardians of Norrath"

Am I qualified to join the Guardians of Norrath?

     Most likely you are.  We do not limit who can join the guild very much but we do have some requirements which are a follows:

  • You must be level 15 (If you have a level 15 or over character in the guild already, you are welcome to bring in your other lower level characters)
  • You must not be a "newbie" to the game (All I mean by this, is basically, if you just opened the package and installed Everquest, you won't know the first thing about the game and most likely wont be able to participate in any events because of this)
  • Before you join you must either...
    • Have two approvals from other members within the guild
    • Be a friend in real life (or a long time in-game friend) of an existing guild member.
    • Group with an officer of the guild, or one of its leaders (Golggen and Iceassasin)
    • Speak with Golggen or Iceassasin about joining.
