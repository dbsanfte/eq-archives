Actually with 16 blood life syphon deals 156 damage and at 12 illusion conjure phantum deals a total of 120 damage. Fast casting is a great skill, have you considered trying like 9 fast casting with 12 blood and 11 illusion so you can spam those hexes on people even faster. Also maybe throw in arcane Arcane Conundrum and cap it with another hex so they can't remove it very easily and then use some of those nifty domination or inspiration interrupts to stop their spells.

Here is an example build I just made up

Mesmer/Elemental,
Glyph of renewal
conjure phantasm
phantum pain
mantra of persistance
arcane conundrum
power drain
distortion
res signet

This build would work by casting arcane conundrum on the target and then capping it with conjure phantasm and then phantum pain. If they remove the last hex they get a deep wound. With your fast casting it is easy to get all 3 hexes on the target quickly. Now that the target is hexed you use glyph of renewal and power drain to interrupt their spells which take twice as long to cast.
