I awoke to find my bed empty. I had not sent Oifur to the couch the night before as I often do. He just was not there, and I felt less for it. I spent most of my life shunning personal contact, knowing that my god would demand that I hate them whether I wanted to or not. It was Oifur who made me realize I could not go on hating everyone, it was Oifur who removed the blinders I had worn my entire life and because of him that I was able to move beyond the grasp of Innoruuk. And now he was simply not here. I felt beckoned to the west towards the slowly rising sun. I left my armor hanging on it’s rack, for my battles on Norrath where finally at an end. I walked into a new dawn. The suns light raced to embrace me. My exposed flesh was was kissed with radiant kisses as the light embraced me. A single tear streaked down my cheek for those that I was leaving behind. As the tear struck the ground marking my last touch upon this land, Yathrin D’Plith vanished into nothing but a memory of those she left behind.
