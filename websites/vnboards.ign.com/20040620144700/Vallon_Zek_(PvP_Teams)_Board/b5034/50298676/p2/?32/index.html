Not exactly sure why I'm here...but...

My fondest memory was being an onlooker during the Ring of Scale quest that eventually led to Aaliyl becomine the Scaled Claw. For a while there, the only PvP was stopping other people from attacking Aaliyl (or healing her). From Permafrost all the way down to the Rathe Mountains.

I desperately wanted to play a role as well...and was chided for it...but for the first time I really felt drawn into the game, that I was watching history unfold on that day. From then on I'd be able to tell anyone who asked where Aaliyl's title had come from, and give them a vivid depiction of the journey and the fighting, or the dragons and the vampire lord, of bestowing Veeshan's Claw upon a mortal (granting Aaliyl some pretty wicked PvP abilities).

For a while there, light, dark, and shades of gray were all on the same level, looking upon one of the more finely illustrated pages of the book that made up the actual story behind Everquest. The world never shined as brightly as it did on that day.
