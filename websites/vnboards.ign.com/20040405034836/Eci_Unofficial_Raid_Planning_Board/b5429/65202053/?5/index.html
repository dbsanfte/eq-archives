FACT 1: This scheduled has EVOLVED through long habit and protracted negotiation among the guilds and groups which have gone, currently go, and are interested in going to the Plane of Fear.

FACT 2: Neither I, nor anyone else, can DICTATE or ALLOCATE days in Fear. If all days are claimed, NEGOTIATE with guilds/groups that have 2 days. Disputes should and will in all likelihood be settled by negotiation.

FACT 3: This schedule is accurate as of the time of posting. Please POST if you see an error or have something to interject.
