Hello everyone out there playing on Stromm!!

My wife and I just reactived our EQ accounts after two years of wandering around in DAoC and dabbling briefly in SWG (*shiver*). We used to play over on the Nameless, even still have our old toons there (that was a shock!!!!), but we were only mid 40s when we left, and figured we'd be better off just starting out fresh on the "Newbie" server.

And Let me tell you...its been a blast so far! People are generally really friendly and helpful (i've got lots of questions, and I get lots of answers - thanks folks!!), and even without a guild, the sense of community is really great! Even all the true mmorpg newbie's out there make it great. The other day, a guy came up and asked how I got tatooes on my chest (I'm a barbarian Warrior, and so was he). I told him, " Dude! You got tatooes too!" He was utterly amazed, and so was I. It is so good to be back around people who can be awed and have fun in mmorpgs.

Well, enough of that, gotta run. If you need any help with anything, give me a holler if i'm on. I'm hanging out around Freeport these days and the surrounding zones.
