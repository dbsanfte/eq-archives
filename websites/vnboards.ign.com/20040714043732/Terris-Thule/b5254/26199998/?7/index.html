This board isn't what it used to be! I'm surprised boy set fire or whatever his name was didn't get totally flamed off the internet... heh. Anyway... most the flamers have gone to DAoC I think.

If you want a guild of friends... make friends and then join their guild... low level guilds are the type that have people recruiting in /ooc or /shout in newbie zones. These guilds typically go nowhere because they lack the manpower and skill/levels to do large targets. They are more social and to some people more fun however.

At level 10, I wouldn't worry too much about a guild and just work on learning how to be an effective cleric. If you learn the game mechanics well enough you will gain levels quickly and make many friends.

Clerics find groups easily so you will have little trouble there... unless you consistantly get people killed and get a bad reputation.

Clerics also make money easily as most people donate generously if they can for resurections. The clerics that gripe about being poor and the cost of peridots are usually the ones that ignore people that ask for resses because they cant be bothered to take the time.

Anyway... there is my 2 cents
