To have a self-proclaimed RP server.

First there was a very organized movement from NorrathRP when EQ went into Beta4, and again in release 'claiming' Fennin Ro as the RP server, this migration was part of the reason Fennin had the highest population of any server, but the RP attempts did not last long. Although the Soebaerd is still going strong there.

Then when another set of servers came out, he message boards rang with 'make Rodcet Nife the new RP server, but was less organized.

In neither of these cases did the rampant RPing appear, NorrathRP disappeared from the web, and every server is much like each other with a mix of RPers, powergamers and casual gamers.

The sad truth is EQ is not really set up to be RP friendly. It is predominantly a hack'n'slash in a high-fantasy setting(and anything in a fantasy setting is commercially called RP). Properties cannot be 'owned', enemies cannot be vanquished, world events cannot be changed by even the most powerful characters, it is a static game only (kill something and it respawns xx minutes later, only to be killed again)

I am REALLY looking forward to Horizons by Artifact-Entertainment (www.artifact-entertainment.com), this might finally be a decent high-fantasy RP game, full PvP with real consequences to detract from random PKing, races that are diametrically opposed are at war, with assisting the enemy having real consequences to your own well being. Powerful characters can have a real effect on the world at large, and piety to the Gods has real consequences to your character's life affecting decisions to be made. (You won't see many Trolls and Elves joining to kill off some creature.)

I applaud those who are trying to bring RP to Norrath, but sadly, EQ is not the game for it.

Hi! I am new to EQ, but in the short time that I have played it, I have become engrossed in it (poor Mrs. Levlarr).
I have never participated in Role-Play before, but the concept intrigues me. I, therefore, created a character on Morell-Thule, really looking forward to playing my "part". The only problem was that, players there seemed to act as they do on every other server I've been to - hacking and slashing just for the sole benefit of gaining levels and loot. Now, I really don't mind that sort of gameplay... that is what I am used to since I've been playing this game, nor do I hold a grudge against anyone who prefers this type of gameplay... to each his own. But I was to understand that this was the "ROLEPLAYING server and was looking forward to a different experience.
I am not giving up hope, though. I will continue to play on the server hoping to meet people like yourselves who enjoy actual role-playing. I would just like a few suggestions. Galanteer (one of the players pushing for an RP-Only server), on an EQ Vault mssg board encourages us to:
A) Turn off player names
B) Turn RP tag on
C) Turn off /ooc, /shout, /auc, /g, /t
D) Refrain from using /who all & /who
E) Turn in Red Book

I agree with most of these if you want to truly role-play.
A) If you want to know someone's name....ask them.
B) If this is the RP server, this should be on anyway.
C) Realistically, the only people who should be able to hear you are those in your immediate area...not those across the entire zone! I disagree with /g & /t, however. You should be able to talk to your group (or partner) without anyone else hearing. This would be akin to whispering.
D) I really don't see what difference this one makes, but....okay!
E)This is the only one I whole heartedly DISAGREE with. I play a cleric. I chose to play a cleric because they are, basically, secondary characters and I would be forced to interact with other players. They are pretty useless by themselves and would (should?) be sought after by most groups (Guilds) for their healing abilities. Until this becomes a true RP server, I would be at the mercy of the "hack & slashers" since most clerics (exc. Dwarves) are horrible at melee. Think of how frustrating it would be to be constantly killed only because someone recognizes you as a PvP player and likes that piece of armor you have, or needs food quickly, or just finished playing "Arena" and wants to kill just for the hell of it!! That's not role-playing! That's "Quake"!
Don't get me wrong. I'm not whining about the thought of getting killed. But kill me because I'm an Elf, or I've broken one of your laws, or because Innoruk or Cazic-Thule decrees it!
Wow! This is pretty long, so I'll just ask you're opinion, or if you have other suggestions a newbie might consider.
