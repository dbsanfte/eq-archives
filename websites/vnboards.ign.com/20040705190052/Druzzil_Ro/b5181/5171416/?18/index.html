from Fennin Ro (Mythic Legion and Seekers of Norrath) left Fennin

other individuals and smaller guilds also came. And even though
we were all organized and knew about this for a while, STILL
we lost many people in the split, even people that used the
/movelog command at the right time, husbands and wives were
split up, and it is not over yet. You can read other threads about
the "success" of the /movelog command. Moving is very risky.

The FIRST night Druzzil was up, both dragons fell within 3 hours,
Mythic did Vox, Seekers did Nagafen (we pre-arranged this).
Maybe 25% of the entire server population was ML and Seekers.

We already were crowded in Lower Guk last night, and having
to share and negotiate camps.

One of our guild said that Druzzil is also on the CERFnet connection,
rather than UUNet (Fennin Ro), and they had much poorer connections
and lag. Myself I didn't notice that much of a difference.

I thought you may want to know what you are getting into before
you decide to take the risk of splitting up your guilds and
your friends.
