Just a quick couple of notes here:

yes there is a pok stone (they aren't books in the Plane) to the nexus (on luclin) "The Nexus" is written on the stone so it is pretty obvious.

If you don't have Planes of Power, it is still pretty easy to use the spires to get up to Luclin.

If you seem to be having problems getting the gem from the scion, try hailing him/her. If you get no response to the hail, it means you are not available, ie. you are sneaking, hiding, or invis. Correct that and try again. Then re-invis if you need to park your butt there for very long and are KOS in the area, (like your lvl 11 necro at the NK spires).

Ok, that was all pretty stupid and simple but I've been suprised by the questions I get in game so I thought I'd post as if people didn't know all this.
