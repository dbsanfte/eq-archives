Well, necros are arguably one of the top 3 soloers in the game and a good majority of necros spend most of their time soloing. SKs can solo a bit, but at later levels not so well. I personally wouldn't suggest a SK as a soloer even though they can do it in a pinch. If you really want a soloing melee go for Beastlord.

Gnome. Without question.

First of all, you will get more drops. Groups with gnomes always get more drops.

Secondly, you will look better than any other shadowknight without someone having to Project Illusion onto you.

Of course, there is always just the all-around gnomish superiority. Sure, dark elves tell everyone they are the mightiest, and iksar kill everyone else for being different, but gnomes just KNOW they are superior, so don't have any insecurity relative to other races.

Finally, as a necromancer, you will probably be killing a lot of things. As a gnome, you usually will not have to bend down to loot them.
