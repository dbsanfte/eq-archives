As a resident of MT and 2 other servers (dont ask) I must say that MT is a treat to play on. The server was GREEN when it came up, meaning no transfers, so everyone was Level 1 at the onset.

The majority of the players are friendly, and very few uberguilds exist. Small guilds and theme-based guilds tend to dominate the landscape. Average level would probably be 20 at this point, but there are a few players in the mid and upper 40's. Twinking is almost unheard of, and it is possible to play for quite some time in many of the zones without bumping heads with another player/group.
