I don't know about "best" as that is a very subjective term and really depends on what you want to do with your character. I enjoy monk for mine, but am primarily using necro spells. Any class has something to offer, but if it is not your style or not something you know how to take advantage of then would be weak.

Quick thoughts:

Warrior - stances for using the touch skills.
Elementalist - area damage spells
Monk - healing for minions, rez options, protection, backup healer
Ranger - pet for another, non-degening minion, stances, traps
Mesmer - hexes to complement necro hexes

Date Posted: 11/2/05 8:45pm Subject: RE: Whats the best secondary for a necro?
As with any class, what you want to do is pick the best 2nd profession for PvE that will get you to Ascension (L20+). After that, you can do quests that let you change your 2nd profession whenever you want. The only downside is you will have to buy most of your skills for the new 2nd professions, which can get expensive. You will have the cash by then though.

So that makes Monk the best 2nd profession to start with, IMO. That way you can always find a group as a healer. Just make sure you advertise yourself as a healer. Necros tend to be last choice for groups. Then again, I rarely had a problem finding groups as a necro/mes, until after Ascension really.

I picked necro/mes for PvP, but then I found the early arenas are kind of hokey because you get level 6 chars running around with level 20 weapons and armor.

Plus for PvE I never used that many mes skills in the first place. Just the occasional backfire or chaos storm. You could go pure necro and be perfectly happy.
