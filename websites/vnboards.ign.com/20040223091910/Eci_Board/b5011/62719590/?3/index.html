As all of you are aware, over the past twenty years, literally millions of people have been thrilled and entertained by my patented emote. Yes, the classic, Iconic “Wergard pinches TARGET on her homemade booster seat”, is the phrase that has been featured on television, films, been the subject of seven books, been seen at countless sporting events, and was even adopted as the National Motto of Lower Mantiboo, I am certain that all of you both love and respect my famous EQ emote. With that said, it is with a heavy heart that I must report that it can no longer be used.
Before a mass panic starts, allow me to make my simple appeal. It is not self interest that forces me to do this. No, my chief concern is the emotional well being of every human being on the planet. Millions of people have already contacted me to express their dismay. Some of them have even offered to take matters in their own hands. I plead with you all, please do not commit any illegal acts. Stay calm, and hopefully all things will be ok.
Earlier today, a ShadowKnight of obviously low moral character and questionable mental health took exception to my much beloved emote. Words were exchanged and a challenge was made. I won’t go into many details, but during the coarse of a duel with this ShadowKnight in which I sought to retain the honor of using my much beloved emote, I somehow slipped on a pile of banana peels and fell into a pit of poisonous vipers. I fought my way out and was ready to win the duel with a thunderous strike of BeastLord might when several Invised clerics and druids began a Ninja CH rotation on my foe. After twelve minutes of Complete Heals, their mana was drained and my opponent was ready for defeat. Then, mysterious hackers, no doubt jealous of the life affirming popularity of my world famous emote, and part of some nefarious plot, sent an electrical spike to my PC. With 500,000 volts of current shocking me at my computer desk, I was momentarily stunned. It was then, with drool pooling at my feet, Fifty Seven Time geared Dwarven Rogues appeared and backstabbed me. Thirty Three MB Wizards then nuked me, and the SK used HT… whereupon I died and lost the duel. As a result, my world famous emote, which many children use as a prayer mantra before going to sleep, has been barred to me.
When word leaked out about this horrible event, a petition was started and millions of people signed it. This is not enough, as it is clear to me only a true brute could ever take exception to my emote, a true brute will not respect the will of millions of people. Nay, what I need is the moral authority granted to me by the players of this server. What I need is for everyone of you to sign the petition, write and/or call your congress persons, march in the streets, etc etc etc… do all these things for me and perhaps, just perhaps, mean Sayure will have a change of heart and once again allow me the use of my beloved emote.
This may be the only time many of you actually get the opportunity to not only change the world, but to make it a better place. Your brothers and sisters in the human race are counting on you to do the right thing. Please, don’t let us down.
Thank you, and Bard Speed.
