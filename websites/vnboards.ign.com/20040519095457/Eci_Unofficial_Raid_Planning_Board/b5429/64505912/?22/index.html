On Sunday, Feb. 22, I'll be hosting a de-leveling constest. Meet at the wizard spires in North Karana at 3 p.m. Central time if you wish to participate. Join channel "delevel"

The rules are:
1) All contestants must begin the competition at level 65. No lower level characters will be accepted.
2) All constestants will be invited into a "raid" body and will join the "raid".
3) Once I call the start, the first character to de-level himself to level 12 and then hail me at the North Karana wizard spires will be declared the winner.
4) If for any reason a contestant leaves the "raid" he will be disqualified.
5) Any character who leaves the North Karana zone will be disqualified.
6) Everytime a contestant dies, he must completely loot his dead corpse before he dies again. Corpse-trackers will be checking that the contestants are looting their corpses. Any character with four or more corpses in the zone at one time will be disqualified.
7) Characters may use any means they wish to de-level, with the exception that they may not delete the character.
8) The winner will receive all my money, which will be at least 815,000 platinum pieces, and which will be paid at the main Plane of Knowledge bank immediately after a winner is declared. All other competitors will receive nothing.
9) All decisions of the judges (me and Goner) are final.

Please post any questions here, or PM me. You may sign up here if you wish, though it isn't necessary to do so.

-Jhales-
