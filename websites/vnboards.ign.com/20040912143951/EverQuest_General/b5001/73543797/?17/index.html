Today we have the first of a new series of new Alternate Advancement Abilities for Omens of War.

New alternate advancements will be added offering another method of character progression; these alternate advancement will be harder to get than previous releases and offer new and unique skills to master.

The first ability we have is for the Rogue:
Rogue
· Master of Disguise ? This ability allows you to maintain your illusionary disguises across zone boundaries.
