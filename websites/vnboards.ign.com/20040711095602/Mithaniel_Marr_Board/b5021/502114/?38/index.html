First of all (as a 33 monk, I paid a lot of attention to the monk boards), you need to coordinate with fellow necros, not fellow mithanial marr people.

I would suggest going to the Necromonitor boards or someplace (wherever the most necros hang out) to suggest this. Talk about it with your fellow necros and make sure you have a few things clear before you begin.

1. A clear list of reasonable 'demands'

2. ONE speaker to speak for the group if/when a verant representative shows up.

3. Make certain that your sit-in isn't hindering anyone's progress with the game beyond the normal lag from having so many people in one area. Don't block any doorways, etc.

4. An understanding that your protest will be your presence, and NOT anything you say or do at the protest. Verant is much more likely to consider 300 quiet, behaving necromancers, than 300 yelling, screaming, spamming, cursing children.

These four things were the keys to the monk sit-in. If well organized in advance, and if you had a single representative to speak for you, then it would probably run smoothly. I know that the monks had approximately a week of planning before they did their sit-in.
