Grand Ball of Shadows - Invitation


Posted throughout various sections of Neriak, a poster reads:

In celebration of all that is wicked in our realm, I will be hosting the first annual Grand Ball of Shadows.

What: An event including music, storytellers, contests, food, drink, and brew.
Who: Teir`Dal, Trolls, Ogres, and select Iksar.
Where: Darkmoon Tower (located in 3rd Gate)
When: 5:00 PM PST, March 14th, 2001 (ooc of course)
Why: To celebrate Teir`Dal, Norrath, and relax, fun most of all.

All are invited to bring baked goods, brewed fineries, and a hint that your best archery equipment may come in handy.

Several scenarios are in the works to be acted out during the ball so be sure to pay special attention to all that goes on, and represent yourself and your character in the best of spirits. Go in Hate my friends, arrive in shadows!

At the bottom of the decorative poster lies a waxed seal bearing the mark of the High Sorceress of Darkmoon. It is signed in a flowing hand, Morgaene Rh`Iannon, Spurned of Darkmoon.

/ooc More information can be found via our hall (www.graffe.com/darkmoon) We would love to see other realm's best rpers, the focus isn't on season here, be born in the Xegony realm and join us if you like.
