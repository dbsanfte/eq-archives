CS Key = Shrine Key
Obtained from Zilarg Wisperwing a mob inside skyshrine. if you go in shyshrine, and make a right at the gate, go up the stairs and look left. He's right there.

Kill him get the key.


Then proceed to the Pool Room area, use the Shrine key click it on the chalice in the middle of the pool.

When you come out in Cobalt scar, Make a left and follow it untill you see the druid ring and the Tooth is on the ground in the middle of it.
