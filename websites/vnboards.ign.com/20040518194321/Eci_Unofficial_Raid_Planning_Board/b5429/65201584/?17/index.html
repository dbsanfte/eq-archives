================================================================
Min Level 51
================================================================
Poison resist 80 UNBUFFED <-- EXTREMELY IMPORTANT
================================================================
MINIMAL CLASS AND NUMBER REQUIREMENTS:
- UDB up
- 25 avg level 58 or 21 @ L60 able to go
- An experienced COH Mage
- At least 2 63+ tanks, experienced MT
- A Necro with Conjure Corpse or equiv. item
- A 56+ Bard w/ AOE Purifying Chorus
- A healer for each group ie: Cleric, CH Druid, Sham.
HIGHLY DESIRABLE:
- A Necro with Group DMF
- A wizard with AE TL

I will NO LONGER attempt this without CoH mage. It's possible, but it
takes too long to get the banished back from shrooms.
================================================================
Raid is organized in the KILLTRAK channel BEFORE traveling to Seb.

If we don't have mob up or the REQUIRED NUMBERS AND CLASSES BY one
hour from raid start, I'll call the raid FIZZLE and close bard roll
then. So please help out to spread the word. I'm in the channel ONE
HOUR EARLY so there's no reason not to be ready to go at start time.

Three groups of planar chars can slaughter Sebilis. PLEASE let's get
it together and get your high-level friends to help.
================================================================
If raid is called a GO, bards, wizards, and druids who know the way
will be expected to help people get to Seb, including running back and
forth to nexus and PoK.

Seb key REQUIRED BEFORE raid start (killtrak channel is open 24/7 and
there are often people to help farm keys in that channel at off times
-- please
** DO ** ASK for key help in the channel at OFF TIMES and
** DO NOT ** ASK for key help at raid time).

You must have a Tiny Jade Inlaid Coffin (TJIC) in your bank. If we
have a wipeout, retrieve your coffin and bring it to the Seb zone-in
point.

If we call a GO, bring your GROUP LEADER 1 peridot when you join
group. Donations NOT needed on a NO-GO call and early donations will
be refunded.

Bards, if you have an alt that you might play for points please post
the name of that alt here, too.

Bards needing to be bumped to the (Last Piece) LP list please get with
Madrib, Clabrett, or Raluvan and show us all your pieces (3 pages and
2 lute parts). Non-LP bards in attendance WILL accumulate points, but
will NOT be eligible to loot the dragon until they are LP. When they
become LP, they will be inserted into the list in points order (see
below for tiebreaking)
================================================================
On the LP list, The point is the number of raids already attended and
the date is the first date accruing points. In case of ties in both
points and start date (including no verified start date), we will roll
off before moving in.

SHAYDAD is up for his epic loot. If he doesn't show up, it will go
down to Tindril and Purrsalot.

***** PLEASE NOTE... Non-LP bards are accumulating points, and when
they become LP they can jump higher on the list than a previous LP
bard. *****
================================================================
LOOT RULES
- Group Leaders will distribute Gems, cash, and Vendor Trash to
healers

- Group leaders (looters) will distribute spells and droppable loot at
raid end; I will stay and direct the distribution. This will reduce
the risk of mistakes due to overflowing bags, extra transfers, and
fatigue. (loot is Need Before Greed, Multiple wins permitted)

- You MUST BE PRESENT for No-Drop rolls. If you are away from the mob
for any reason (banishment, corpse recovery, you name it), we may
not be able to delay a roll for you. Chalk it up to bad luck and
come back next time for another go at it.

- Phrawgg and Sgraelynax have loot rights on first two Teeth dropping
from Trakanon on my raids.
================================================================
I have no qualms about calling the raid in the middle of it. It is
easy to wipeout BEFORE we get to the dragon if people make wrong turns
or don't stay TIGHT or don't assist the main tank on his / her call.
Sebilis is a tricky and dangerous dungeon. You are strongly advised to
print the EQATLAS maps and have them handy DURING the move to the
lair.

THE fight-down TACTICS ARE SIMPLE: STAY TIGHT, FOLLOW YOUR GROUP
LEADER, WAIT FOR ASSIST CALL!

If the raid is NOT going well on the way down, I will call it off. If
you don't like it, or can't follow rules, Don't come plain and
simple. EVERYONE GETS ONE WARNING!!! Then you are out.
================================================================
This raid can be done in less than 2 hours from Start to finish and we
would like to keep it that way.
