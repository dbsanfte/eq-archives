I just picked up FoC yesterday afternoon. So far I like it a lot but I haven't had a chance to try to nab Life Transfer to compare. I used henchies and I started from Camp Rankor. Just fight your way up north to Dreadnaught Drift. I found that Dreadnaught place to be light years easier then Talus Chute. First it's a tiny zone and a lot of the mobs are either scripted to patrol or sit on their asses. I found all you had to do was fight 4 groups of mobs in order to get to the area where the Wurm is hiding. You can avoid all the rest of the sitting or walking mobs. This was a breeze compared to fighting through Talus Chute. Maybe you can find a runner to join the team just to get you anbd henchies through to DD quickly.
