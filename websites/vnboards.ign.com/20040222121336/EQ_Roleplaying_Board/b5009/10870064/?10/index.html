Well, as to the corpse issue, I guess you can look at it two ways. One is when you die, it can be considered an out of body experience. You are floating through the nether realms, but you feel grounded by your body, so you need to return to it by either running through the spirit realms and renter your body, or a cleric calls you back from the spirit realm.

Second, you are comatose. Then, you see your body, below you, and you finally awaken back in your body. (the corpse run)

Hope that helps somewhat.

When you're relating adventures in roleplay, just say you were knocked unconscious even if you were killed.

When you arrive at your bind point, you awake at the town where friendly healers brought you back to consciousness.

When you get back to your body, just consider it finding your stuff by good fortune.

I find the overall story more important than the trivial details. Okay so you killed Lord Darish, Crush, and DVinn 57 times. Just relate it as one victory in an ongoing war. Of course you can always say you THOUGHT they wee dead, but they showed up when you least expected.
