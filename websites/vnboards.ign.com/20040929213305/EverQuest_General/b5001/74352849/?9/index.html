You have that in your log:
"The parchments of Rallos are scribed in a language that you cannot comprehend; they do however have detailed drawings of the manaetic behemoth that you have already destroyed."

You need to do that so according to a comment on Alla:
"
This message means you need to visit Grand Librarian Maelin in PoK. Hail him (important), say "I have new lore", then say "I have new information." One of those will fix your flags; I can never remember which one. Then, of course, visit the Seer and do the "unlock" routine.
"

- And you can indeed be PoTime flagged without being fully PoP flagged if you can get in a raid with enough flagged people for the elemental zone, kill the God and hailing the essences to get Sphere of Coalesced Water (Reef of Coirnav/Plane of Water - Coirnav the Avatar of Water), Globe of Dancing Flame (Doomfire, the Burning Lands/Plane of Fire - Fennin Ro, the Tyrant of Fire), Mound of Living Stone (Ragrax, Stronghold of the Twelve - Avatar of Earth), Amorphous Cloud of Air (Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind - Xegony the Queen of Air).
