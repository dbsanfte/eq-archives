May 30th : Just moved to Nebraska. Now this is a place that knows how to live! Beautiful sunny days and warm balmy evenings. What a place! Watched the sunset from a park, lying on a blanket. It was beautiful. I've finally found my home. I love it here.

June 14th: Really heating up. Got to 100 today. Not a problem. Live in an air conditioned house, work in an air conditioned office, drive an air conditioned car. What a pleasure to see the sun every day like this. I'm turning into a sun worshipper.

June 30th: Had the backyard landscaped with western plants today. Lots of cactus and rocks. What a breeze to maintain! No more mowing for me. Another scorcher today, but I love it here.

July 10th: The temperature hasnt been below 100 all week. How do people get used to this kind of heat? At least its windy though. But getting used to the heat and humidity is taking a little longer than I expected.

July 15th: Fell asleep by the pool. Got 2nd degree burns over 60 percent of my body. Missed two days of work, what a dumb thing to do. I learned my lesson though, you have got to respect the sun in a climate like this.

July 20th: I missed Elwood the cat sneaking into the car when I left this morning. By the time I got to the hot car at lunchtime, Elwood had swollen up to the size of a shopping bag and exploded all over the $2000 leather upholstery. I told Alex he ran away. The car now smells like Kibbles and Shits. No more pets in this heat.

July 25th: The wind sucks. It feels like a giant freaking blow-dryer. And its hot as hell. The home AC unit is on the fritz and the AC repairman charged me $200 to drive by and tell me he needed to order parts.

July 30th: Been sleeping outside by the pool for 3 nights now. $1500 a month mortgage payments and we cant even go inside. Why did I ever come here?

Aug 4th: It's 115 degrees. Finally got the AC fixed today. It cost $500 and it does get the temperature down a little bit, but this freaking humidity makes the house feel like its 90 degrees. Stupid repairman tinkled in my pool. I hate Omaha.

Aug 8th: If one more dumbass cracks "Is it hot enough for you today?" I'm gonna tear his throat out. Damn heat. By the time I get to work, the radiator is boiling over, my clothes are soaking wet, and I smell like a dead cat.

Aug 9th: Tried to run some errands after work. Wore shorts and sat on the black leather seats in the car. I thought my ass was on fire. I lost 2 layers of flesh on the back of my legs and butt. Now my car smells like burnt ass and dead cat.

Aug 10th: The weather report might as well be a damned recording. Hot and sunny, hot and sunny. Its been too damn hot to do shit for 2 months and the weatherman says it might really warm up next week. Doesnt it ever freakin rain around here? Water rationing will be next, so $1700 worth of cactus and landscaping might dry up and blow into the pool. Even the cactus cant live in these conditions.

Augh 14th: Welcome to Hell! Temps got to 113 today. Forgot to crack a window and blew the damn windshield out of the car. The glass installer came to fix it and said "Hot enough for ya today?". Bo had to spend the $1500 house payment to bail me out of jail.









(this is a humorous bit, no, my cat didnt blow up in the car)
