Hrmm....there has been 7 expansions....

Ruins of Kunark
Scars of Velious
Shadwos of Luclin
Planes of Power
Legend of Ykesha
Lost Dungeons of Norrath
Gates of Discord (launching on 02-10-04)

The game has changed a LOT. From tons and tons of new zones, to a new UI, to Alternamte advancement points, insanely powerful armor, jewelry, weapons, spells, etc, in-game map, mounts (horses and drogmors)......there is just too much to list.

You will not recognize the game when you start playing again. Hell....you do not have to do nearly as much running around to get from place to place anymore. With Planes of Power, you also got the Plane of Knowledge which is a friendly place with portals to just about every major city in the game. You can enter Plane of Knowledge even at level 1. Also, a zone called "Nexus" has portals to all wizard spires throughout the lands. The Nexus portals port every 15 minutes. Before Planes of power came along, the Nexus was the travel hub of Norrath.

Remember all the /auction stuff going on in Greater Faydark and in East Commonlands on Saturdays? That is a thing of the past. There is a zone called the Bazaar where you can turn yourself into a "Trader" and go afk or /auction too if you wish. But you can basically assign a price to each item you want to sell and go into /trader mode and you turn into an NPS. People can right click on you and browse your wares and buy the stuff just like other NPC's in the game. It is a neat feature.

Planar progression is a big thing too. We got that with Planes of Power. Imagine mobs hitting for 1000-3000 per hit....it is insane, but a lot of fun.
