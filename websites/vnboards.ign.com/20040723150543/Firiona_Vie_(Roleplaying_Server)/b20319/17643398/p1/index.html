Wow, that was a very pessimistic summary! Some might call it realism, but it sounds as though Shadoe has, for the most part, given up on any notion that Firiona Vie might become the roleplaying community it was intended to be.

I've seen examples of brilliant roleplaying since yesterday, and inconsiderate acts of stupidity that exist in EQ anywhere you go. People will develop reputations, and knowing how and who people are will help you choose the most favorable company to keep. Obviously, seeing Bonny and Klyde running around together doesn't really strike me as two people with creative inspiration, but then, they could be very nice people. Shadoe did indicate that your choice in a name for your character can make a profound first impression.

I do what I can to educate those that don't know, but are trying. I've seen several that fit into this category. I've seen people who have inspired me with their brilliant dialogue and colorful emotes. And, like Shadoe, I've seen those that openly defy the spirit of the server. These are the people that will almost always rebel when you politely ask them to tweak something here and there to render their atrocities a little less painful to those of us that would like to keep our characters surrounded with a fantasy environment.

Unlike Shadoe, I am hopeful that many of them will go away, and in a month or so, after the race to get out of the newbie zones has dwindled, there will be a lot more socializing.

I'm not saying Shadoe is wrong and I am right; I merely choose to greet this brand new server with a healthy dose of optimism.
