The graphics look very good, but what MMORPGs really need are dynamic worlds where the players can build towns and tunnel into caves, where forest fires and storms and earthquakes affect the terrain, and where the monsters and quests respond to changing world events in a realistic way instead of magically resetting themselves back to their original configuration every 72 minutes.

I guess what I'm saying is we need the backend of MMORPGs to become more sophisticated and realistic, not just the graphics and environment.
