I think max ram for windows 98 and ME (95 no longer supported for EQ) is 512mb. If you have XP, or windows 2000 you can go to over a gig. You will have to do some research on what your motherboard can handle and it will probably require a bios update if you tried to upgrade your CPU much but i seem to remember i switched motherboards when i went from a 600mhz P2 to a 933 mhz P3. I cant remember which one used which type of slot as it has been a while but you probably wouldnt be able to go much over 600mhz with your current MB. You can build some pretty good rigs these days for relatively few dollars. Two places i have had success with online are TC computers and Micropro online. Neither are the cheapest places on the net but they have decent prices, excellent service and i have returned items to both of them over the years and have never had a problem getting credited.
