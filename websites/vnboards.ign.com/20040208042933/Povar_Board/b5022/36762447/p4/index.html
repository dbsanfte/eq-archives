Well...

Been around since May or June of 1999. Yeaa I am one of those who are almost older than dirt in lands. Back then that weapon you waited so long for from swashbuckler in tunnel in east commons was uber.

When someone yelled "giant" in west commons it was all ready too late to zone as you died looking around for one!

Those were the days when you could go into a shop and wonder if one day you would be able to afford the steel bp in the window.

Those were the bronze armor days.

There was this one guild think they were called "the steel something or other". Needless to say it was a UBER guild at that time.

When killing that silver plated freak npc in west commons was really a challenge.

When a priest of discord could stand up for themselves and live when attacked.

Guise of the Deceiver..gotta love being DE now and then.

When a rez was worth "everything".

485 hours camping for haste belt from guk.
387 hours camping j-boots in najena.
178 hours camping Ykesha swords.
Getting DE guise of the deceiver 1 week before they removed it...PRICELESS
Being ks'ed 32 times camping Pegasus for that blasted cloak.

While doing my warrior epic "wondering how in the hell I was supposed to walk up a straight 200ft cliff in west karanas because the npc I needed to hand stuff to was stuck there".

Lets see my history.

Was born in Halas. I slipped on every 3rd piece of ice near there for about 8 levels. Then I decided to get adventurous and spread my wings and visit blackburrow. ::cough:: hey no one told me about that deep hole and that you weren't supposed to accidently fall in.

At level 13 I was escorting young ones from Halas to Freeport and believe me that was fun taking level 2-8's and keeping them alive til they reached their destination and also running rescues for people's corpses.

From there it all a blur but I do remember when I finally turned 48 and had a full set of bronze armor and was so proud of it but got laughed out of sol b for a while because by then there was better armor to be gotten.

Bahh...was in one guild for about 4-5 months forgot the name of the guild but was a continuation of a guild from Gemstone III, text base roleplaying game for over 7 years, which we were all in. (I do remember Tedra, Challen and Brimztone were in it as some others)

Then just before the first BOTB came out left that guild. Took second place in warrior's BOTB. Yeaa..some of you may remember me using a truncheon of doom for those battles.

"Snowlion" way back at the beginning travelled 1/2 way around the world to sell me my first piece of armor.

In that time period in the beginning I remember quite well "Illusionist" THE ENCHANTER was always wanted for something by everyone including me!

One reason was "Tomedius" was a lot harder and more backlogged with orders.

And yes..we had some good laughs on the price wars between those two.

And yes a lot of you may remember me from Hate where I got in more than one shouting match about camps up there with uber guilds as I used to run my own raids (anyone invited) up there and you guys would stuff 1/2 your guilds in my free raids then when time came grab the camp. Awwe..the memories.

After that I joined up in 2000 with NA then stayed with them through the changeover into FU which I finally seperated from about a month ago.

Currently am not in a guild but might join one sometime. But for a while I will first work on my AA and my levels to become an even better warrior.

I have known too many people to list them all. Give or take a 1000+ I guess.

But I have had a blast along the way.

And yes..I AM THE ORIGINAL GIGANTUOUS however don't be insulted if I don't remember you if you come up to me in game and say for example "thank you for helping me get my armor 2 years ago in real life and expect me to remember you" After all I am a barbarian and we have low intelligence and an even worse memory.

I wish I still had all my old logs my puter I used way back then blew up ::groan:: and so did all the info I had on hard drive.

This turned out pretty long, I smoked a joint and just started typing, enjoy

- The Redglows... as if you didn't hate them with a passion.. me and Highfever were gonna turn in our books just to kill them everyday with their god damn monopoly on the jboots camp. ALWAYS GOD DAMN TARNI IN THE ROOM. I remember Tarni was dueling Siran at the zone in and Siran disarmed him and it was me that picked the weapon up of the ground and bagged it.. I hated Tarni THAT much.

- Anyone that can honestly remember what the jboot camp itself was like. It was pure hell, there were lineups of groups outside that door, some people commiting 42 hours+ just to wait for a shot at Drezlna. Before it was groups waiting in line tho, it was straight up dirty tricks and ffa kills. That orge that would path in and open the door.. allowing invis people to come in ( nobody ever had see invis up ) then the second drez spawned 10 people come uninvis and start nuking. I remember me and highfever just took the camp by force and the people called in Vayne, he was level 45 at the time and simply owned, could guarenteed get XP on any kill.

-The infamous 2 week fear break. Basically a coalition of any type of guild that was considered "uberish" would stay in fear around the clock killing respawns so it wouldn't have to be broken again. Breaking fear was the #1 "We are the best and you are nobody" feat you could achieve at the time. I think Harmonium did it first ( Not including the old school SA breaks which were a joke ) then Grey Council and then us, but I'm not entirely sure. I know we were pretty damn stoked when we broke it, it was our first real big step into competing with harm and GC at the time.

-When Baelish used castersrealm to make a public attack at Cammii because GM's wouldn't rez/kill mobs on a bad fear break and people were gonna lose corpses/gear all because "mobs attacked you when you zoned in". Was a big spectal.. For all you newer people, fear was really bad ass back in the day.

- Prepping at the sro spires for a hate raid. For some reason it was always the sro spires.. no idea why.. it was common to see a large mob of people at sro and people asking "hate or sky?" Then making sure your group all had invis, crossing your fingers then porting up. Then it was this : out the door, sharp right, up the wall, into the corner then stop. See who in your party died, then look to see if you aggro. If you aren't, cool, you can already see the rogue up ahead waiting to see if you needed a drag. Then you slowly inched to I think the south west corner, can't remember.. was funny though.

-Doing xp at the treeants in south karana when it was "lowkey" from levels 25-35. You made such insane cash and you could sell those staffs to the aviaks nearby. Also when you could get insane xp on crafted armour at aviaks, the wizards were KSing the aviak avacots like crazy for the gems.

-20 hour VoX raids, with people losing 3 levels. After a Naggy raid, what you did was drag all your corpses into a pile, like 7-10 of them and patiently wait for a cleric to walk by and rez all of them. Speaking of Vox raids, it was NEVER possible to get feared into the pits. Read that last line again.. there was no bug, no trick, you straight up just were holding forward when fear wore off.

-2 Groups at the Magi in lower guk and feeling bad ass that you could kill the hand too. Brownivy and Daebe always soloing the lord room. Even older back was brownivy powerleveling her bard with her super bad ass level 49 pet soloing the Avatar of fear and his whole room.

-Rombus getting the first Yael robe on povar as a proud member of the Wanderers of the Stoic. Bribing Astennu into triton with a Jumpy robe and basically going on a wizard stealing frenzy so we could kill Trakanon.

-Joint raids! Back in the day when RA+Triton would joint raid, things like Cazic Thule, or Innoruuk. That would be absolutely unheard of today hehe. We always had the asian guilds on back up for late night shit.. Anytime we needed numbers to stay awake for the 10 hour fear clears to cazic, we would call Bad Company and another guild and they would come help. We actually randomed 1 1k for all loot up until trakanon and I was a very firm believer that this was the most fair loot system heh.

-When Dantiel ebayed. Don't know why this one sticks out, but out of all the chars that ebayed I always thought this one was one of the most talked about.

-Ashoc. If there was ever one guild that should have made it but didn't it was that guild. Ashoc was made up of alot of the big names today on povar. Half my officers are from that guild as well as people who went to RA and Harmonium. Somehow they managed to kick ass even with the soap opera that was there leadership.

- The server split. That was a big one. Alot of people lost alot of friends with that one, as well as some guilds fell apart or broke up. It was wierd the last day, I remember me and noctal going hard to duo break fear before he left and we did it. Then me and zarach did it the day after, then the split happened. We needed it though. It was no secret that Harmonium dominated with a timezone advantage, and they straight up abused there dominance of it. Shit they had Venril Sithir locked down for months, literally.

-Hate with pets, and how respected the pullers were that could pull hate. Nowadays alot of pulls are done with 1 million tricks and bard fade, circet of shadows, pets etc etc but back then it was pure skills, especially in hate. A whole raid was hindered useless if you didn't have your #1 puller. I think the big names were Arlan and Kung. I know later on Bentos made Varsity to pull and by that time there were a few others like splos and some from other guilds that could do it to. It really did take alot of talent though.

Lots more stuff I thought off but I'm tired so just a bunch of fast ones then I'm off:

- Xeffo looting stabs corpse

- Losing like 5 Howling Stones keys everytime you raided there cuz you just kept passing keys to other people when you got rezzed out then rezzed back in to pass the key.

- Being really paranoid when you were exploiting, because back then there really were GM's online all the time, and they actually were god damn everywhere.

- Having a GM showing up and giving you this big speech about how your town and nation is so proud of you hereby granting you a surname when you /petitioned at level 20 for it.

- Varsity, were always the most hardcore crew. 6 man fire giants was so l33t. Comparable now to what the kung army of one does.

- Tricking people into the arena in rathe, or just camping near the zone and killing them to farm rubicite. Back then you could take one item in arena

- Just a triton one here, but we were relentless in fear. Traturn, Heihachi, Arlan all in other guilds got the same tell every single day.. "you up for fear". It was a death fest, day after day after day. I kid you not, at no time in tritons entire history up until today did we ever go so hard at something. We were still a pure caster guild at the time too. Was pretty funny stuff that people kept coming back for more. I think it was just the pure rush of the thought of winning. Even dropping 1 mob was a total accomplishment. "Don't train the ledge!"

-Going to loot the second blighted robe on Povar during fearfest2k with Baven on the corpse sending me a tell saying "hand me your SMR and I'll get off the corpse"

-Hearing over "the radio" that there was a quest going on in some zone and immidietly you dropped everything, it was even FFA with people in your guild, and as soon as you got there first, you'd start being a queer roleplayer in the hopes that you'd get picked to go on the quest and win some loot. I got chosen for a quest at the opening of Plane of Hate with Xeffo, Cheszmyr, Aethar and some others and it was so dumb. We zone into the zone in of hate, and it was so buggy back then, we even warned the GM's.. so there is a train of about 20 mobs, and GM's acting like we're supposed to be fighting them. The quest GM chick is dead on the floor after trying to fight them, and the other 2 GM's are trying to "on the fly" roleplay it into the quest while keeping us alive with 20 mobs there. They couldn't /kill them all fast of enough so we totally wiped. ( The whole time we're all playing along in queerroleplaytalk that we can actually "defeat this foe" cuz we want to still get the loots gg) At the end when we got rewards, I was handed a box of abukar, but I didn't have any free inventory slots, so it fell on the ground and someone took it. I think that was actually my stupidest day in EQ ever come to think about it.

Okay thats enough I need to sleep, I feel like I wrote an essay. Last thing is I'd say the best times and total rushes I've had from the game would be: killing Phara Dar, Tormax, AoW and Emperor.

Oh man, this thread is great! I haven't played EQ in probably 2 years. But since I was on Povar from the beginning, all of these anecdotes bring back memories for me. Man, I miss Povar a lot sometimes. If it wasn't for the wife and kid, I would still be playing.

I remember being a founding member of NA along with Oxide and Disputatious (of course). I also remember being insta-hated by the uber guilds. I guess that was because we had loud-mouth Disputatious as a guild leader. But I'm sure I did my fair part to be disliked ... right Cylus?

I'll never forget NA's first successful Nag and Vox raids. Boy, we thought we were elite then. Afterwards, we broke Fear for the first time and absolutely kicked ass. But then I tried to lead a Fear raid and it was a complete cluster-fuck. Death after death after death. "Don't train the ledge!" God, Zaar, why was it that some people just didn't get it. You'd think after dying a dozen times from training the ledge, that some of those clowns would learn to die somewhere else on the way to the ledge. Ahhh memories…


As for specific stories, I remember when I first got my planes stones (level 46 I believe?) and I was in the LGuk King room with fellow NA members. I promised one group member that I would port him out to GFay so he could sell his loot and go to sleep. Well, at that time, I didn't recognize the difference in look between the planes spells and the normal teleports. So I accidentally sent my entire group to the Plane of Hate. I'll never forget feeling good about myself during the loading of the zone we ported to because I did a guildmate a favor by teleporting him out and helping him by not forcing him to run all the way. I'll also never forget the guild chatter that I saw as soon as we arrived in Hate (before I even knew where we had gone). "Fuck!"; "WTF?"; "HELP!!!!" We lasted 10 seconds before all 5 of us were wiped out. Yep, there were 5 of us. The 6th was lucky to be left in LGuk because she was only level 44 and could not go to Hate. Lucky, that is, until the King spawned a few seconds later and wiped her out.

That night was the first time I respected the power of a wizard. I went from feeling useful and wanted to feeling like a complete asshole.

Other good memories:

Trying to kill the cats in Kerra (God, is that the right name) when us Erudites first started playing. Every 15 minutes someone would yell, "HIGH GUARD! RUN!" We were so noobish that we didn't understand why 2 wizards and a mage couldn't kill anything. Because we needed tanks damnit! "Tanks? What are those?"

Thinking the blackburrow gnolls were incredibly difficult. The trains up that tunnel from the bottom of blackburrow were the first I saw of such amazing carnage.

Getting the last Rubicite BP on Povar after a camp of 20 hours. Then giving it to my Ogre friend who kept it until he quit playing 2 years later.

Running through SK behind a ranger who swore he was tracking the Pegasus (I forgot the pegasus' name, it's been so long!) not 30 minutes after I was telling my friend (that same guy who owned the Ogre) that I would give anything for the Pegasus Cloak. Then seeing the Pegasus was a white con. I killed him with one last root (5 feet away) and one last blast with only 13 hit points remaining. Then when I saw that cloak, it was and still is the highlight of my (solo) gaming life (guild action was much better). Of course, levitation was not important to a wizard, but it was great to be able to cast that spell while falling off the Aviak town. Also, hardly anyone had that cloak back then.

Missing out on the guild war between Triton and NA. Man, I wish I was there for that. That was the only successful guild war that I knew of up to that moment. Lots of anticipation leading up to that one.

Spending more time administering the affairs of NA than I did playing EQ. My work life sufered incredibly since it was so hard to concentrate on work when we were planning a plane or dragon raid.

Cylus being the best Fear puller on the server before the split ... and he was a Wizard!

Talking about pot-smoking in NA guildchat only to be lectured by Dunric that there are impressionable teans who shouldn't hear such talk. Then to hear after I left that Dunric was in the guild with the most ardent supporter of pot smoking (Triton and Zaar, respectively).

Taking 10 minutes to run through West Karana. That sucked and really made a young wizard want his teleport spells.

Hello to everyone. Too many names to list. But my favorite player was certainly Alexfusheng. Also, I dearly remembered Splos and how much he tried to help us in Fear. ("Tried" is correct because we were too newbish to be helped.)
