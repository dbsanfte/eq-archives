Syko,

I have returned to EQ after a 2 year hiatus, and am loving it. The game has changed a lot for the better, and the newbie experience was a lot more enjoyable than the old days. Right from the get go, there is a ton of newbie quests for armor and weapons. Also, the newbie type mobs have been toned down a lot, and its quite fun.

Currently, I'm lvl 34 with my ogre beastlord and I really like the class. The only complaint I have is that *so* many people play beastlord because they are pretty powerful.

Anyway, the only real issue I have with the game is the same issue I had before... the players. People still are too scared to go to a dungeon without a cleric and enchanter, and the twinks are too busy bragging about their uber twinked items to pay attention to the fights. Lot of folks wont fight anywhere but zone lines, and high lvl's will still whipe out all the spawns in low level dungeon to farm items.

Haven't tried out LDON yet, though, so maybe thats another ballgame entirely.
