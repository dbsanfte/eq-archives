Hail Shui,

I am from AC also and have just started to play EQ. Theres things I like about it and things I dont. Things I like about EQ

1) all diffrent Player Races and Occupations
Its cool to see Trolls, Gnomes, Humans and others all interacting and looking diffrent. AC you can change your looks but everyone is Human.

2) The Enviroment: AC has small village like buildings around Dereth. EQ has huge Cities and Buildings within that City. Some Citys have Catacombs where the Dark Arts are practiced and you need to find your way down there if you are intrested in playing a Dark Character. According to your Race and area where you live everythings diffrent..Barbarians live in the Frozen Wastes up North. Trolls live in the Steamy Swamps down South, Lizard Men live on another Island, etc. AC world although very cool has similar areas but not as detailed as this.

3)EQ has a Boat System that takes you to various Islands. You dont have to be a certain lvl to go there. Its at your own Risk.

4) EQ has more intresting Quests given out to PC. I'm looking for a Leader of the Spore People in the Swamps. My Troll Shadowknight will need to Bring back his Head to receive Trophy from the Troll City Vaults.

5) Banks:) EQ has Banks where you can put your stuff. Any city visted will have your stuff in its Vaults.

Things I dont like about EQ

1) No Portals. You need to run,boat or be transported by a Wizard or now the Spell yourself. Not all Characters can learn these spells.

2) When you Die you loose everything and you start back at your homecity. No Lifestones here. There is a Bind Spell that can be cast on you by another PC that will Bind you to whatever city your visiting. Once bound if you die you return to this City.

3)User Interface kinda sucks. AC has a more injoyable UI that is easier to use.

4)Zones...This really Sucks EQ has this thing where you run from place to place and the screen freezes and loads a new Zone. AC Did it right with traveling in real time all over Dereth.

5) Dungens are not as plentiful as in AC.. I have been playing for a week and have only found on Dungen in one of the Zones.

6) No Radar or in screen Map. This is good and bad. Good for the thrill of exploaring . Bad when you need to find your way home in a emergency.

Oh well. Hope that Helps
