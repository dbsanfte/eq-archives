Things that need to be changed with the warrior class in EverQuest:

1) Warriors currently have no ability to heal themselves, besides bandages that heal to 70% at level 57. Most of the time reduced to asking a cleric for healing or sitting waiting for health to come back. I think there should be 500, 1000 HP and complete heal potions sold to warriors throughout Norrath at special sale prices such as 1 CP per point of healing. Also I think warriors should get the monk's mend skill at level 1.

2) Travelling through the land of EverQuest can be very dangerous for a warrior, so I think all warriors should be given a permanent speed the equivalent of the bard's "selo's accelerando".

3) Since we are a tanking class, I feel there should be warrior only armor that helps use tank better. I think the crafted breastplate should give us a no casting time complete heal right click effect, and increase the AC to something reasonable such as 55 AC.

4) Why are paladin's the only class to get weapons that are on fire? There should be a level 20-30 quest for a 2 handed firey axe with 55 damage, 40 delay. And don't give it some silly spell like "smoulder" for ice flame. Give it a good spell with the effects of ice comet (only with fire), stun, and a 1500 HP DoT. Name it the Asswhomper.

5) Camping for some creatures is just boring, waiting for hours and hours for Grimfeather to spawn to get the eyepatch of plunder. I think all warriors should be able to summon a creature to them using a command like /summon Grimfeather.

6) Why do caster classes get pets and we don't? I think warriors should be able have a skill for training creatures. Giving us pets like Hill Giants at level 10, spectres at level 20, Rallos Zek at level 30, and the Sleeper at level 40.

7) Warriors always get whomped in duels, and why? Because spells always get us, druids, wizzys and mages use us as thier punching monkeys. Therefore warriors at level 20 should be immune to all spells.

Hopefully Verant will look at the problems with the warrior class and give us all of these things.

When I made a Warrior, I fully expected that I would not be able to solo.

The Warrior should be the definitive "TANK" - hold the line, hold the aggro, take the damage, dish it out kind of class.

I am not talking huge, giant dropping in one shot kind of damage, but steady, reliable, good damage output.

A Warrior should have access the best AC and best HP's - that is part of their stock and trade; "hold the line, hold the aggro, take the damage".

I like to think that all classes should be able to solo - but there is a cost. Downtime. It takes too long to heal? That is part of the reason Healers exist. Remove the down time of healing and you take away from the value of the Healer class. Balance. A good group needs a Healer class - just as a good group should NEED a tank. Balance. Every class has benefits and disadvantages.

The Warrior should be the King of standing toe to toe with things tougher then he is and Hold The Line. He may need someone watching his back. He may not always get the killing shot in. But he should have value that is unique to being a Warrior.

What have Warriors become?

Companies need to stop catering to the whiners and study game play and character balance. Not nerf based on the top 1% or the abuses of 1-5% of a class. They should be able to say to whiners - look at the numbers, there is balance.

I know that game balance and character balance is THE TOUGHEST thing to accomplish. But Verant put out the game and takes the money - they should step up to the plate.

DAoC has "Team Leaders" for classes. Dedicated players that represent a class. They do the numbers and poll the forums and issue reports. Not a bad idea, as long as they are balanced and objective.

Everyone has their favorite class and thinks they could be better. Some classes obviously have fallen off the balance scales and need redressing.
