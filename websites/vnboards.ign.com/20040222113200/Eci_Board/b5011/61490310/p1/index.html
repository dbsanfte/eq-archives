He would massage me or tickle my back (I'm not sure if everyone uses that term, maybe caress is better? with nails it feels so good!) Having a relationship with someone is more than just (should be more) having sex with them. It means they fulfill you in everyway possible. When you're truly in love with someone they will and you will know it. It is as if there was a hidden candle inside and meeting this person sparked a flame that will go on, undying, forever, and you know that everything in your mind says you must be with this person no matter what, because everything else is wrong.


So much of it is a feeling, and you'll never experience it unless you're willing to open your mind to it
