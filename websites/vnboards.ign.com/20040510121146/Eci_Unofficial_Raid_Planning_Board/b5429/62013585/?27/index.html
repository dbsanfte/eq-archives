I will be leading a semi-public Hole raid on Wednesday. Semi-public means the public is welcome to sign up, yet bear in mind I am a Stormseeker first and member of the public second. I reserve the right to earmark a piece of epic loot for a Stormseeker. For any particular raid, I will post ahead of time if a piece of loot is reserved.

I am not reserving any pieces for Stormseekers for this raid.

The Stormseekers is always looking for quality members. If you are looking for a nice group of competent people, please ask me, Arrk Mindmaster, or any officer, for an interview to see if the Stormseekers is the right guild for you.

We will assemble at the wizard spires in Toxxulia forest, since some people may be KoS in Paineel and/or do not know the way to the entrance to the Hole. If we do not have the force to raid by 8:15pm EDT, I will cancel the raid.

Raiding deep into the Hole can be time-consuming. If you cannot stay until 12am EDT (4 hours), please do not sign up. We need at least one druid or wizard for the raid, but if we get no wizards, you CANNOT leave early, except in the most dire emergencies (real-life emergencies).

I do not honor last piece. We will use Elder House points to bid. You may bid up to 10 more points than you have accumulated. All no-drop loot will be bid on when it drops, except epic pieces which will be bid on before the pull. All droppable loot, including things like Imbued Granite Spauldors, Loam Encrusted Armor, and Withered Leather Armor, will be held until the end of the raid, when people will bid with Elder House points. Droppable loot will be awarded to the highest bidder, regardless of need, race, or class. To see your current points, please check the following link:

http://pub28.ezboard.com/felderhousefrm5.showMessage?topicID=5.topic

I reserve the right as raid leader to award loot to anyone I choose, to preserve a sense of fairness. I do not want to see, for example, Red Dragon Scales going to someone who cannot use it just so they can sell it, while others on the raid have fewer points but need it.

Raid objective will be determined by signups. To have a raid, we will need the following:

* At least one cleric
* At least one druid or wizard
* Main tank
* Puller (can be the main tank, or even me, if necessary)
* One healer per group (cleric, shaman, or druid)

If we are missing any key classes when the raid is due to start, I will reevaluate our objective.

Someday, perhaps I will write something up about raiding the Hole. Until then, these pointers may be somewhat helpful:

* Many monsters can be affected by anti-undead spells.
* Constructs are usually immune to mez, and should be killed first.
* One should avoid resting at the side of buildings, to avoid adds from inside them.
* We NEED to keep moving, for the dungeon has a normal respawn time of 25-30 minutes. If we pause too long, we get trapped and have to re-clear. We have to keep moving despite AFKs and LDs. If necessary, we will drag the body of anyone left behind to the main raid to be rezzed (if, of course, the person dies trying to catch up again).
* Use assist. Only the main tank should break a mez. As an enchanter, I am especially sensitive to them, too!
* When we move, everyone is to follow, regardless of aggro. Do not stop to root, snare, mez, or anything else, but bring anything aggroed to the next camp.
* FOLLOW INSTRUCTIONS! I know this zone pretty well. Follow instructions now and ask questions/make suggestions for improvement later.

If interested in going deep into the Hole and are level 50+, please sign up below with name, class, and level.
