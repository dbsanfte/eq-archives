Please post here if you are level 46 and want to go. All loot will be distributed using Elder House point bids. To view your current points, check the following link:

http://p072.ezboard.com/felderhousefrm5.showMessage?topicID=5.topic

Remember, you are allowed to bid up to 10 more points than you have, so first-time Elder House raiders can bid up to 10 points.

We MUST have at least one wizard to have the raid. I will take up as little as one full group, but in order to kill the Hand of the Maestro we will need at least 3 groups.

We will meet at the Toxxulia wizard spires. To get there, take the Paineel stone in the Plane of Knowledge, head east until you reach the wall, and head north until you reach the spires. Channel will be EHHate.

In case we only get one wizard (as is usual), I will provide the following instructions here, for Odd-Man-Out (OMO) information:

* Each group leader will designate one person in their group as the OMO. This means when the group is formed, one person leaves the group to join the OMO group.
* I will designate one person as the one to form the OMO group. This person will announce his location (near the spires) and invite all the OMO people.
* I will designate which groups will teleport up when. Group leaders invite the designated wizard for teleporting.
