Karana Council of Guilds has a new home on the web!

Karana Council of Guilds is dedicated to making EQ fun for everyone on Karana, whether they are in a large guild or not. We provide the ability to participate in raid-level encounters to those who do not have the power to do them with their own guild or alliance, occasional raid-style events for all level ranges, as well as a variety of fun events for all levels.

We regularly run raids on Nagafen and Vox, and less often to Plane of Fear. We are also on the rotation for Venril Sathir, and looking for enough people of appropriate level to join some of the other rotations. We also provide a place to ask for help on mobs you need to kill on your Epic quests. Some of the "just for fun" events we have sponsored have been "human" chess matches, and a newbie scavenger hunt.

You do not need to be in a large guild, or even any guild, to join us. Everyone who plays on Karana is welcome to count themselves a member.

In-game, /join kcog and chat with some of our regulars, and visit us at our new web site at http://www.guildportal.com/Guild.aspx?GuildID=8955
