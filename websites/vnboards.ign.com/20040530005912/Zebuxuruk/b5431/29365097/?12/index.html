Have fun with the game. Take it slow if ya like. Advance to new zones if you think you can handle it. Dont worry about new armor and stuff just sell what you get as drops and if your group is on Free for all loot dont be shy to loot a few.

If your a Melee save up for Leather then get banded if they are still being made. Then Bronze, Dark alloy and then try for some old world quest armor like Armor of Ro *Paladin* or Lambent *Bard* and go on from that.

Dont back talk to the higher ups if you have questions dont pressure them. Theres only a few Highs like myself that will answer all your questions and not snap back at ya =)

Remember in EQ Leveling is a long hard process once you reach about lv 20. Can take one to three days for each level till you get to 45 then it may take longer. Once your lv 50 it will take about 3 to 15 days per level depending on how you play and what you kill. So dont get discouraged just try to avoid dying lol.

Make sure your armor and weapons is up to par with the level your in. Otherwise you may not be able to keep up with the killing and damage taking therfore making people think lowly of you.

If you have friends in RL that play the game ask them for support but ONLY if you want to be helped. As for me I had no one at all but I never begged or anything to the higher ups and I made lv 60 just fine. Yes 60 is the Max lv.
