The Rez spells can be found in various Cleric Guilds..being from Gfay, I was unable to get them in Felwithe, but Kaladim had them all.

Celestial Remedy is obtained on Luclin. If you go to the bazaar, go upstairs to the bartenders and out on the balcony. There is a little gnome merchant that carries a number of various class spells. CR is one of them.

Actually... I still used CR until 29 due to the MUCH lower aggro profile of the HoT spell line. At 29, you get Celestial Health (205 Mana with 460 Total Heal Divided in 4 ticks) which beats all of your heals to date until Superior Heal at 34. Even then, I will probably drop a CH then a SH as my "chain heal" to avoid unneccesary aggro. At lvl 44, you get Celestial Healing (225 Mana with 720 Total Heal Divided in 4 ticks) which must be found on mobs or purchased in the Bazaar.

The only real downer on HoT spells is that my Book of Obulus (or any similar focus item) does not proc for HoT spells.
