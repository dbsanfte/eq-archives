We are customers. It's Verants job to please us. However, they can make our gaming experience infinitely less pleasurable, and if we complain through present channels, we run the risk of getting banned, if we're even heard at all.

Of course, while we're banned, we're still paying for our account.

Oh, people threaten to quit EQ all the time. But the fact is, hardly no one is going to delete a level 50+ character. Someone quits, the account is either sold, or given to a friend. Either way, the account continues to get paid for, and EQ's subscriber base increases. Verant knows this, and that is the reason for their arrogance.

They KNOW we're addicted, so, they act accordingly. They talk about *their* vision, instead of customer satisfaction; the want to treat us any way they want, and they don't want to hear complaints, when we don't like it.

Out of a subscriber base of 300,000+, they pay one guy to answer between 4 to 10 questions a day, and they better not be complaints. Never is a complaint answered. Personally, if i was paid to do the same job, I would answer a minimum 50+ questions per day.

So, as it stands now, Verant can do whatever they want to us, with absolutely no repercussions...........or can they?

I, as a paying customer, refuse to pay for this situation. August 1st, I will cancel both my accounts, for at least one month. I am an Everquest addict, i think all i can take away from the game is one month. How will I survive? Well, maybe Shadowbane, Anarchy Online, or Neverwinter nights will be out by then. if not, II guess I'll play Diablo's expansion, or maybe even a little Red Alert or Counterstrike. There are lots of options. Maybe i'll even find something I like better than EQ, in which case, I may even find the courage to delete my character.

So, that's 20 dollars less for verant next month, big deal. But, suppose someone talks their whole guild of 122 players with 215 accounts, into taking one month off? That's 2000+ dollars less. Suppose a whole server, of 8000+ players, decides to take a month off? That's $80,000. May be Absors' salary. now, they get worried. They lose their swagger.

What do I want? I want better player representation, and costomer service that doesn't assume I'm a criminal, when i lose all my equipment, due to a bug. While whineplay was a huge mess, Absor getting paid to answer 4 questions a day is also not the answer.

If Absor can answer 300+ questions per day, fine. If he can't, maybe get 10-20 volunteers to help him. Volunteers won't be hard to find. I'll volunteer.

I refuse to pay for nerfs to my character, without Verant having to hear about it. This makes my gaming experience less pleasurable. If i buy a Big Mac, and they stop putting secret sauce on it, I can just go to Burger King. That's the customer's option.

You have your own reasons for dissatisfaction. However, we're all in the same boat. We can do nothing about it. If you're a new player, and think the game is absolutely brilliant, then ignore this post. If you're unhappy, then cancel for only one month, on Aug 1st. When you come back, you will still have all your equipment, same name, same level. If you're an even worse EQ addict than me, and you have 2 or 3 accounts, maybe you can cancel one or 2.

In any case, to bump this post, please indicate how many accounts you have, and how many you're canceling.

EXAMPLE: "I have 2 accounts, canceling one"
"I have 3 accounts, canceling all 3."

If you want your power as a customer returned, bump this post. If not, there is no hope.
