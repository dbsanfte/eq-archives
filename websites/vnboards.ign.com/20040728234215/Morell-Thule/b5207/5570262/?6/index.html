I'm looking to start a small RP guild. I am only looking for about 5 others who would be interested in creating characters who are life long friends. This group would journey around Norrath together. This group would most likely start out as an alt for everyone, playing a couple of times a week together. The group would play together almost exclusively. The group will focus on RPing to bring life to the server. Expect the leveling to be slow, but hopefully steady. I really want the group to stay close in levels so we can continue to group for hunts. I am not a web-head, but learning. I hope to develop a site based on our adventures.

I have been thinking on a couple of group formats. One being a well balanced group with different classes, just childhood friends growing up together. Or maybe a set of quints +1 who are all the same class. The make-up will be discussed amongst the group.

Again, this group will start out as casual with strong RP when we get together. I want folks who are willing to build some background history into their characters.

Please post here with an email addy if you are interested. Also, you can give me a in-game tell to one of my characters. Look for Tatter(bard) or Aaqi(shaman).
