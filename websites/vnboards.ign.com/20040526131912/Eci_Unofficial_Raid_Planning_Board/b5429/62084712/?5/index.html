Please check start time before posting. Please read entire post contents has changed!!!!!!!

This raid will be run by Dreddid. I am just posting this information for him.

Please be in at camp site and in channel 30 minutes prior to start. Channel=Voxraid

WHERE:
Meet in the King's room in Permafrost. If you need a port or a guide to the King's room ask in the raid channel. There will be people there to help you. Peridots will be available to supply the raid but contributions would be nice so I'm not paying for the raid.

REQUIREMENTS:

Basic requirements:
Level 46 to 52
Minimum Cold resistance of 90
Minimum Magic resistance of 70

Cold resistance is to diminish the 500 damage AoE attack. Magic resistance is to help prevent being affected by the AoE Fear attack. Resists being up to par is more important than ever, as Vox's AoE attacks have had their power increased slightly. Depending on how desperately we need them, I may be willing to take some people as low as level 44 if they have good resists.

Class Requirements:
We will need the following classes and levels to help make this raid a success:

1 Level 52 druid, with Circle of Winter and Circle of Summer spells
1 or more monks, preferably 51-52, for pulling the lair
1 or more raid-capable necromancers, to provide Man Floating or the group version
1 magician, at the least to summon mod rods, Shards of the Core, and Elemental Blankets
1 level 49 or higher shaman, to provide ability enhancing spells
2 enchanters, who MUST have the Mana Sieve spell to fulfill this requirement.
2 Clerics or more, to provide Heroic Bond, Heroism, or at least Resolution, plus a Symbol
Lots of everyone else (24 total people minimum needed to raid, or we do not go into the lair)

************************************************************************************
PLEASE READ THESE RULES as I have made some changes to some existing rules.
************************************************************************************
RAID RULES:

1. Dreddid IS THE RAID LEADER. Everyone must follow our commands in order for the raid to be successful and ensure we have a good time. Danlyn is the co-leader until further notice.
If you dont follow our commands (and Dreddid or Danlyn tag you) you will not finish this raid with us. Our Decisions are Final.

Note: Danlyn will not be available for raiding for a few months. Dreddid will announce at the raid who will be filling in as co-leader until Danlyn's return.

2. Group Leaders are responsible for answering any questions about the raid.

3. DREDDID WILL SETTLE ALL DISPUTES.

4. Group Leaders will be responsible for the following:
a. Ensuring group is buffed properly.
b. Educating members on Raid Policies.
c. Keeping Members in line and under control.
d. Acting as Middle Man to settle disputes and informing RL of disputes and issues.
e. Accountability of group members.

5. ONLY GROUP LEADERS CAN LOOT ICE GIANTS AND SPIDERS and such. The group leader of the group that got the experience will LOOT the mob and report the loot in the raid channel. You will loot immediately!

6. ONLY THE RAID LEADER MAY LOOT LADY VOX (unless someone is designated by the RL)

7. DON'T SEND TELLS directly to us before and during Grouping or During Crap Buffing. If you got a question, ASK IN RAID CHANNEL.

8. Keep chatter in the voxraid channel. The raidsay channel is for making announcments and issuing commands, not discussion

LIST RULES:

1. There are 2 ways to get on the list for the Torn, Frost Covered Book and White Dragon Scale.
a. You have your burnt book or red scale and have attended 5 Vox raids.
b. You have attended 10 Vox raids
You MUST REQUEST to be placed on the list. Not everyone wants to do this quest.


EPIC LOOT RULES:

1. Paladins/Bards may have 1 *late* attendance that is arranged with Raid Leader prior to raid. After this, they do not receive credit for other raids they are late to.

2. YOUR CURRENT RAID IS NOT COUNTED TOWARDS RAID ATTENDANCE UNTIL THE RAID IS OVER. THE RAID IS OVER WHEN EVERYONE HAS SAFELY LEFT AND LOOT HAS BEEN DISPURSED. Once the raid is over, your raid attendance will be applied and posted for the next scheduled raid.

3. If no one on the list is present to loot the epic pieces will be handled as follows in order of precedence:
a. Anyone who has the Burnt Book or Redscale will roll first. You must prove it before you roll.
b. If no one is on the list and no one has Redscale or Burnt Book, all present that have attended 3 previous Vox Raids (and are eligible, class-wise, for the item) will roll for it.
c. If no one has 3 previous Vox raids, ALL in the appropriate class will roll.
d. If no appropriate class is present, members who are capable of Multi-Questing the piece will roll. Nuf Said.

4. You get a point for each of these public Vox raids, so the more often you attend the higher you move up in the lists. There was talk of removing people who have not been to Vox in more than a month. This does not affect your points and if you come again you will be placed back on the list. This is *not* a suspension, simply not showing people that are most likely not coming again.

5. To get on the list you need to attend 2 raids. If you miss 2 consectutive raids while on the list you will be taken off and need to start over. (If you are not able to make a raid a pm and/or posting relaying that info would be fine.) You will not be able to loot the book/scale until you are the list. If a book drops and noone on the list is present , it will be looted by myself and mq with a Paladin/Bard that has need of it. What I hope to accomplish is to get more of a core to raid with and to prevent a first time raider to get book by default. There are several characters who have made high level buffs available and have ask nothing in return but have Pallies that need a book, but have moved on for whatever reason.

6. It will be publicly announced before we charge Lady Vox, who is next in line for the Book and Scale. Hence, Dreddid will announce who loots, or who rolls. Discrepencies will be aired in the Buff Hall. Dreddid has final say.

OTHER LOOT RULES:

This is a "NEED BEFORE GREED RAID"

If you are coming here to get an Epic piece or White Dragon Hide for resell, you are not welcome at this raid!

If 2 things drop like an RBB we will random the first one to the melee and the second one to the entire raid. If a caster was to win the second one he could sell it and get something he/she needs, therefore it is not greed. Same thing goes for the eye patch, weight reduction bags etc. If ONLY ONE of these items drop and YOU DON'T NEED IT, others would appreciate it if you don't roll....

Missing 2 consectutive raids, you will be taken off list unless you send me a private message.

The white dragon hide will now be random rolled on by pure casters ( Clerics, Druids, Enchanters, Mages, Necros, Shamans, Wizards.

If there are any concerns please pm me. Thanks
