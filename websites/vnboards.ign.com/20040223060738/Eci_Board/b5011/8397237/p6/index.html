Within the first few weeks of EQ's release and I was a fledgling druid wandering around Faydwer, I ran into two high elven wizards who wanted to create a guild which would work for the protection of all that was good and blah blah blah . It was a nice concept and the two fellows both liked to RP. So I told them that I would help them create was to be called the "Shield of Norrath." The guild was created but only to have both of the creators go MIA for two weeks and only 3 members playing. We never were contacted about what happened despite attempts to reach them over email and we disbanded.
So I then went on with my adventures as Ayala the still young and unknowing druid and I ran into my old friend (characters brother in game) Miridian and he invited me to join the Shadowhunters. I had some great times in the guild and met some great people. Miridian and I would shortly leave after we both had some differences with a fellow who is no longer in that guild. We then adventured together for a long time and eventually met some really cool people who wanted to create our own guild, Sacred Brotherhood of the Wolf. The original founders being myself, Twiste (who now plays Twistwo), Miridian, Banamor (who now plays Krieg), Gillain (retired), Ragis (retired).
Our family slowly but surely grew into the guild it is today and I am really happy with the way it has all panned out. I no longer play as frequently as I used to but my friends outside Sbow and the people in my guild are what keep me paying that monthly subscription to EQ
