Know what the nice thing about being on a point based system like Valon's is? It's that loot is almost completely objective and fair. Valon are probably the worst guild to join if you're in it for the loot, since there's no way to manipulate the system to your favour without actually putting in time and effort.

No sucking up to officers, no arguing that you'll get so much more use out of item x than player y and none of the backbiting and 'I can't believe Randomguy got the Fuzzy Bunny of Uberness over Anotherguy! What the hell was Officerguy thinking?' You can claim that it doesn't happen in your merit guilds, but you're either blind or lying, because I know it happens.

That's not to say that Valon doesn't have people that are loot driven - all guilds do, I'm sure - but they work within the system or they get frustrated and leave the guild (or are forced out). You want to be rewarded you either work your ass off for the guild or you watch those who do work their asses off get rewarded. (Or you beg and plead for people to not oppose you on rolls, but this doesn't work so well anymore).

Anyways, to accuse Valon of being filled with loot whores is pretty ridiculous, since it's pretty impossible to be a loot whore given the system we work under.
