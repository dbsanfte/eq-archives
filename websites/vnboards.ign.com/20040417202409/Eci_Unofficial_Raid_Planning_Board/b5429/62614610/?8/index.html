/Killtrak ** SIGNUPS ** Sat 17 Jan 3pmET

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
THERE ARE SMALL CHANGES TO THIS RAID, PLEASE READ EVERYTHING
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Change Summary:
- Peridots will go to group leaders now
- Gems and cash still to me for distribution to clerics
- Group leaders (looters) will distribute spells and droppable
loot at raid end; I will stay and direct the distribution. This
will reduce the risk of mistakes due to overflowing bags, extra
transfers, and fatigue. We have been getting a LOT of loot lately
and it takes a fair time to distribute it.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
READ ALL THE RULES, UNDERSTAND THEM CLEARLY OR DO NOT COME!!!!!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
BARD ROLL CALL & formal START TIME on Saturday will be
- Noon Pacific
- 1pm Mountain,
- 2pm Central,
- 3pm Eastern

Roll call will remain open for 20 minutes if UDB is not up. Otherwise
it will close at raid end. Only bards staying the duration will get
an attendance point on either day.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Raid Channels (PLEASE KNOW THESE)
- Raid = /join killtrak
- Travel = /join trakport
- Chitchat = /join traktalk <--- ALL non-essential talk here
- All Bards = /join LPbards <--- Roll call here
- Healers = /join trakheal
- ONLY leaders will use /raidsay. Non essential spam in /rs will not
be tolerated.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Min Level 51
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Poison resist 80 UNBUFFED
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
MINIMAL CLASS AND NUMBER REQUIREMENTS
- UDB up
- 30 avg level 57 or 24 @ L60 able to go
- An experienced COH Mage
- At least 2 63+ tanks, experienced MT
- A CS + DMF Necro
- A 55+ Bard w/ AOE Purifying Chorus
- A wizard for TL
- A healer for each group ie: Cleric, CH Druid, Sham.

I will NO LONGER attempt this without CoH mage.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
LP bards LIST is as follows:
- Zynn (5) 11/02/03
- Acoustic (5) 11/02/03 [tied w Zynn]
- Baolino (2) 09/13/03
- Tindril (2) 01/08/04
- Ratticand (1) 08/15/03

ZYNN and ACOUSTIC will roll off before we move out for the loot right.

Non-LP list is as follows:
- Gingerbay (10)
- Meeowmix (3)
- Saryma (2)
- Meeowmixmastah (1)
- Shaydad (1)
- Peredhel (1)
- Radyn (1)
- Ellandir (1)
- Fengor (1)
- Larrkin's bard (1)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Raid is organized in the KILLTRAK channel BEFORE traveling to Seb.

If we don't have mobs up or the REQUIRED NUMBERS AND CLASSES BY one
hour from raid start, I'll be calling the raid No-Go and closing bard
roll then. So please help me out here to spread the word. I'm in the
channel ONE HOUR EARLY on Saturdays so there's no reason not to have
everyone ready to go at start time. I cannot be the only one sending
tells, making phone calls, sending email.

Three groups of planar chars can slaughter Sebilis. PLEASE let's get
it together and get your high-level friends to help. A wipeout in Seb
is hours of CR and a very unpleasant and expensive disappointment. I
want Massive OverWhelming Force (MOWF). I am not in the least proud
and I am eager to have an experienced and powerful toon take over. MY
GOAL IS EPICS FOR BARDS, and I can't do it without all y'alls help
getting the forces we need. 'Nuff said.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
If raid is called a GO, bards, wizards, and druids who know the way
will be expected to help people get to Seb, including running back and
forth to nexus and PoK. Please coordinate efforts in TRAKPORT channel
and all needing assistance join that channel too.

Seb key REQUIRED BEFORE raid start (killtrak channel is open 24/7 and
there are often people to help farm keys in that channel at off times
-- please DO ASK for key help in the channel at off times and DO NOT
ASK for key help at raid time).

You must have a Tiny Jade Inlaid Coffin in your bank. If we have a
wipeout, you will need to retrieve your coffin and bring it to the Seb
zone-in point.

If we call a GO, bring your group leader 1 peridot when you join
group. Donations NOT needed on a NO-GO call and early donations will
be refunded.

Poison 80 is a minimal requirement for your safety. This is easy to
boost with inexpensive bazaar gear and is MUCH more important than any
other stat for the final fight against the dragon.

Sign up here with CHAR NAME, LEVEL, and GUILD. Bards, if you have an
alt that you might play for points please post the name of that alt
here, too.

Bards needing to be bumped to the (Last Piece) LP list please get with
Songweever, Aabana, Madrib, Clabrett, or Raluvan and show us all your
pieces. Non-LP bards in attendance WILL accumulate points, but will
NOT be eligible to loot the dragon until they are LP. When they become
LP, they will be inserted into the list in points order (see below for
tiebreaking)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
On the LP list, The point is the number of raids already attended and
the date is the first date accruing points. In case of ties in both
points and start date, we will roll off before moving in.

Zynn and Acoustic are up for their epic loot. If they both come, I
will hold a roll off before we move out. If only one shows up, he
will get it. If neither show up, it will go down to Baolino, Tindril,
and down the line.

***** PLEASE NOTE... Non-LP bards are accumulating points, and when
they become LP they can jump higher on the list than a previous LP
bard. *****
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
I have no qualms about calling the raid in the middle of it. It is
easy to wipeout BEFORE we get to the dragon if people make wrong turns
or don't stay TIGHT or don't assist the main tank on his / her call.
Sebilis is a tricky and dangerous dungeon. You are strongly advised to
print the EQATLAS maps and have them handy DURING the move to the
lair.

THE fight-down TACTICS ARE SIMPLE: STAY TIGHT, FOLLOW YOUR GROUP
LEADER, WAIT FOR ASSIST CALL!

If the raid is NOT going well on the way down, I will call it off. If
you don't like it, or can't follow rules, Don't come plain and
simple. EVERYONE GETS ONE WARNING!!! Then you are out... we always
have a TL'er available and I'm not afraid to use him/her for that
reason!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
This raid can be done in less than 2 hours from Start to finish and we
would like to keep it that way.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Clerics who come out will have Dot's reimbursed to them and Gems will
be split between you!

Spells and droppable loots will be distributed in PoK at port-in at
the gnarly tree. Items will first go up for roll on NEED BEFORE GREED:
to classes who can use. If those people already have the spell or
item, then it will go up for open roll.

Normally, each player may win ONE item per raid. Lately, we have had
more spells than people who could use them. The players decided to
allow people to win multiple spells on open rolls. NOTE this policy
can result in some people having quite a windfall, so if we decide to
go this way please be aware and don't grouse about it.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
If you have any questions, concerns, comments, or see any errors, post
here or PM Aabana or me. I will run this raid from my druid toon
Clabrett if Aabana doesn't show. If she does, I will bring my monk or
a Paladin (Raluvan).

Thanx guys see you all Saturday!
