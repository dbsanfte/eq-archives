Not all of the people listed here are 65. It's true that most are higher level, or 'old school'; it's because most of the people posting are of higher level.

Anyways - my list (And I'm keeping this to the more obscure ones; You know I love you Arbiters, Destiny Awaits, and Sylvan League!)

- Vexis (I believe that was the name; I may be slightly off). I had been coveting a Robe of the Oracle for some time; I was around level 20 and it was just shortly after Kunark was released. I was helping my friend Qiinae, who had just gotten the game, in front of Felwithe, when all of a sudden I was approached by this really high level guy (He may not have been 50; but he was at least in his 40's), who gave me a Robe of the Oracle! They sold for about 1k back then, which was a huge sum of money for me. It turns out Vexis had just recently gotten a new robe, an SMR, and was looking for a new home for his Oracle robe. I think I still wore that robe till I was in my 40's, lol.

Feanore - here's someone whose not 65; in fact, I don't think he ever got out of the 30's. Anyways, he was the first leader of my first guild, Sylvan League. I remember him bringing me to the 'bazaar', which was in EC before it became a 24/7 trading zone. I think at that point, it was really only on Saturday that it was busy; I remember fighting in EC without too much spam. He bought me a Savant's Cap, for a whole 30p! I was under level 10 at the time, for sure; I think he was high 20's. At about the same time, Ultress, another person from that guild, gave me a whole set of silk armor.

Ashorty definitely stands out. I remember going on some of his earlier raids - they were awesome!

Anni's hate raids were great.

I remember watching for Verina Tomb in Neriak for my chanter epic, and hanging out with Angecia. There was another person there regularly - but I can't quite remember the name.. Generally I have found epics to bring out the worst in people at times (I've been there), but my time camping Verina was certainly not one of them.
