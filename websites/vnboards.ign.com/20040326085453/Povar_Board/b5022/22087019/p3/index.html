Trust Noone

You have a best friend,
to them money you lend.
They say they adore you,
noone is before you.
When things go wrong they are there,
to them your soul's pain you bare.
They are greedy but you don't see,
you see only what you want them to be.
You pick them up when they are down,
never let them wear a frown.
You think they know you mind and soul,
you help them achieve all of thier goals.
Til one day they say,
they are going away.
They refuse to give back your money,
they need it to go some place sunny.
You cannot believe this is real,
don't they understand how this makes you feel?
Now they have money a'plenty,
you are such a fool hindsight is 20-20.
Trust noone I say,
for some place some day,
they will show thier true self,
and put you on a shelf.
All for a lousy 2k.

But after all this,
I do admit I miss,
that person I thought I knew,
before into a jerk they grew.
