 		Date Posted: 7/17/03 1:54pm Subject: Fires of Time is now recruiting for a bit.
If you don't know already, <Avatars of Might> has now become <Fires of Time>. The Avatars of Might name was only temperorary but it took a while to come to an agreement on the final name to use. Which we finally did and then made the switch. If you still don't understand, oh well.

But as such, we are again opening applications to pickup a select few people to add to our ranks. We have very good numbers for the most part, but we would like to add a few more to our EST time slot for the most part.

We've accomplished alot so far and intend to keep moving forward at a rapid pace. Right now we are sort of in a gearing stage to better equip our members before we make a even larger push towards Time. Which, might I add will be somewhat crowded soon I feel.

If you'd like to see some of what we've done please visit our guild page and check it out.

We are going to be leaving apps open until July 28th. After that, they will be closed again, except to those already Elementally Flagged or exceptional apps very close to it. We will then peform a reflagging process starting on August 1st.

We will be mainly looking for folks who can log on before 5:00pm EST. Our ranks are very strong in the PST primetime. That does not mean we won't take a few folks in that time zone, but those who have the dedication and can log on early will have better chances.

The main classes we are seeking are:
* 1-2 Bards
* 2-3 Clerics
* 1 Beastlord
* Assorted DPS Classes

Other classes may have a opportunity but these classes will have higher priority. Again we aren't looking for alot.

I have even decided to give a bonus for those who already have flags. The bonus will be added when a applicant achieves initiate status.

* Elemental Flagged - 30 dkp bonus

* Grummus + Carprin + Bertox = 5 dkp
* PoN Maze + Terris Thule + Saryn = 5 dkp
* Vallon Zek + Tallon Zek + Agnarr = 5 dkp
* All 3 HoH Trials = 3 dkp
* Mith Marr = 3 dkp
* Each Sol Ro Mini = 1 dkp (5 total)

This is added to the dkp you will earn while raiding on reflagging. So those of you who already have flags, I encourage you to apply now. Even if you are in another guild, yes I'm talking to you.

Please visit our application board at our forums for further information on the application process and sending in a app.

You are welcome to apply anonymously in order to protect yourself should you be denied. If you are accepted, you will be required to deguild from any guild you may currently be in as well.

Note: This thread will obviously recieve pointless flames to which won't be responded too.

Good Hunting.
