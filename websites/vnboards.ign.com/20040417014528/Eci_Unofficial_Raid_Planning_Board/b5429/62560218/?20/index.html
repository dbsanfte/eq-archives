Date: Sunday Jan 18th
Start Time: 4pm Eastern
End Time: 9pm Eastern
Channel: publicsky
Meeting Place: Nexus
Raid assistant: None

Objective: Isles 5-7

<<<<READ THIS PLEASE>>>>


What to bring: One tiny jade inlaid coffin, 2 peridots for cleric buffs. I will provide all the Cloudy Stones of Veeshan for the ports up.

Please state level and class, what quest pieces you need, and if you have keys to any of the islands.

This week, we will begin by getting everyone to isle 5 and starting from there. We will farm the spiroc lord 2-3 times and then move on. My main focus is to get past Isle 6 this week.

I will have a list for the epic/quest drops. You will earn two points for attendance. You will also earn one bonus point if you are level 60+. You can also earn one point if you have all of the keys to the isles that we are raiding that day. The bonus point for keys is only applicable once for isles 1-4 and once for isle 5-7.

The other epic drops will be handed out based on the point system. Ties will be randomed out. Any questions on that PM me please. I dont want to make this more complicated than it needs to be....show up enough get your epic, its simple enough.

Basically, the schedule I want to keep is isles 1-4 one week, isle 5-7 the next, and then one week off. As long as the attendance is there I will continue to do these. Once the attendance dies, so do the raids.

We do need one wizard for the raid, and one healer per group. For this leg of the raid we do need 2 60+ enchanters (or possibly bards) to charm/mez the bees on Isle 6, so if you need epic pieces tell your friends. Some essential classes have nothing to gain by coming to Sky, so be sure to drag a friend along if you want this to happen.

Without two 60+ enchanters or bards to charm/mez the bees on Isle 6 this raid will not be going anywhere. Period. It cant be done. You also may want to invest in some disease resist gear. The Boss on Isle 6 casts one nasty AE disease spell....just so you are warned.
