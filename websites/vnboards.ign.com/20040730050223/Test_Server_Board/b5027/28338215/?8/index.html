I have had this problem for a week or so (with charmed mobs) and Monday I finally figured out what seems to be going on.

This is what I believe occurs:

VI is changing the packet structure again and in doing this they seem to have altered some mob info. "NameTooLong" is a common name for a spawn, now...

Any time I send a charmed pet after a mob with a relatively short name, they work fine.

When I send them after anything with a longer name, or one that is named NameTooLong, they pet will say, "That is not a legal target, Master." If the mob hits me however, they will go and attack it, however.

I've just taken to either rooting and stepping close, or slowing/tashing and sitting down to let it whack me once.
