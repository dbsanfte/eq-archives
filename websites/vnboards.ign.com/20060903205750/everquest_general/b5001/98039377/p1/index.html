Hi I play on Tunare. Population seems pretty decent. About 350 traders in bazaar. 100+ in PoK at peak times. In sub 40 game, I see a lot of clerics running around (maybe because I am a cleric as well). In addition, I see a lot of tanks sub-60. So in the early game, you would probably find a dps class to be a good choice. I haven't seen too many druids (maybe they still solo, I don't know), so they might be a good choice as either dps or healing.

For the level 70 game, it is a different story. I play an enchanter, and sometimes I am the only lev 70 slower LFG. Other cases, I see like 4. (Note: Current lev 70 game is a bit weak, but I suspect that will change drastically when TSS comes out). Healers can be hard to find, but not that difficult. The really difficult class to find is tank at level 70, and that is mainly a problem of content. If you are a raid-geared tank, you won't even have to put LFG up to get group invites. Content is tough, and tanks that can handle it are greatly prized. On the other hand, if you are a casual tank that doesn't raid, and doesn't put in a lot of hours to get AA and gear, you will get a lot of invites, but you will then end up saying - sorry, I cannot tank there. With level 70 DPS class, I don't have much personal experience, but it seems to be pretty common to hear complaints of "I was on for 4 hours and got only 2 invites". On the other hand, I don't see one particular class that has tons LFG.

Going from lev 1-70, I would encourage you to consider a pet class (mage, beastlord, necro), as they can add dps to a group, plus solo when they are waiting for a group. Of the 3, beastlord is the most gear dependent, with the other 2 able to get by better with sub-standard gear.
