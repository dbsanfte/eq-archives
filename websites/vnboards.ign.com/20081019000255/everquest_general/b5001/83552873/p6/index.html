Crowding, Camping, Boat rides, Waiting Lists in dungeons, Kill stealing, East Commonlands spam, Training, Guard killing, Corpse recoveries, Major experience loss, etc, the list goes on.

Doesnt everyone remember going to oasis and fighting over a caiman? I remember there being 90+ people in the zone. You would PRAY Lockjaw would spawn on a few people to thin the numbers out for a couple of minutes.

I remember sitting in Lguk for HOURS waiting to get into an Ass/Sup or Frenzied group. Hours wasted in SolB looking for a group. I remember getting a nice camp in Unrest only to have someone come training the entire house through.

And believe me the "fun" of running from Freeport to Qeynos will most likely wear off after one attempt.

The reality of it is alot of people want a classic server and once they get it, they're going to abandon it pretty quickly. The only way they could REALLY pull it off would be with instancing and that would just destroy any thoughts of it being a truly classic server.
