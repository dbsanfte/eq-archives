>>my sta will max with buffs, the resists and raw HP does me better<<

It was my understanding that the number of hit points that you get from STA, including STA from worn items but excluding STA from buffs, is affected by ND and PE. Which presumably serves as an incentive to add STA even when your STA is maxed with buffs.

I don't have all the latest and greatest formulas here, but playing with Magelo, it would appear that you gain 13 extra HPs from a Bonespur Mantle vs. a Stormrider Wing Mantle at 65 (with ND3 and PE), which may offset the loss of 10 pure HPs on the former even with maxed STA.

Oh well, not a big deal either way, but you may want to borrow a Bonespur Mantle when fully buffed to see if you like the results better. Feel free to borrow mine
