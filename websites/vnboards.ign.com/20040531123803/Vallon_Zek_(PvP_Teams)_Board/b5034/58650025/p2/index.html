In all the histories I've written about Grey Companions, I've always mentioned MoD prominently as a guild we used to get our asses consistently kicked by. They were tight-knit and always answered the call to battle, so my strategies usually consisted of performing a feint to get MoD looking the wrong way. This was the tactic in GC's major invasion of Nektulos Forest with Judges of Norrath. We made a feint in North Ro, MoD showed up en masse and killed a good chunk of us, then we teleported to West Commons and bumrushed Nektulos while MoD were waiting for us to come get our corpses in North Ro.

Iggles were also one of my favorite guilds to PvP against, although they like MoD were always in the high end of my level range. Iggles would beat your ass, but usually be good-natured about it in tells, and funny.

I've created a proposal for a new PvP ruleset server, and your comments, feedback and flames would be appreciated. You can find it here:

http://vnboards.ign.com/message.asp?topic=62932832&replies=0

It's a temporary server like Discord with old world and LoY only, but without permadeath. The world is divided into 9 hard coded teams, and the server's lifespan is limmited to 6 months. At the end of 6 months, guild and team winners are declared and the server is brought down.

This is an anti-zerg server. Guilds like Hate which used the security of Kunark on Sullon Zek to level up in complete safety will find no such safety here.
