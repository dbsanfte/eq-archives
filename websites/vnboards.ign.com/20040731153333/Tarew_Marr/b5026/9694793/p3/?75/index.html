Aurum, I know you're a great person, and your intentions are good, but I'm pretty sick of this crap.

I mean this in the nicest way possible, but I quite honestly think you can take your TOS and blow it out your ass. Want to ban me for stating that opinion? Go for it, because I don't see this board as a place that I can think of as a comfortable, community environment anymore.
This censorship has gone way too far in the last few days. We were doing just fine, and Johanry was doing a fine job of keeping us from getting too far out of hand. That is, until moderators from another message board, who have no place in this community, came in and began throwing their weight around. We - as a community - have established our own rules and guidelines over the course of the last two years or so, much like a state will often times put their own rules into effect. We don't go out of our way to break the rules that you have already established, but nor do we go out of our way to restrict ourselves to those rules.
The people who post here fully understand that there may be offensive topics or language. Do you know how they handle those topics? They don't read them, and it has worked quite well up until now. We have our own ways of dealing with rude or vulgar language, offensive topics or posts, and the like. We, as a community, understand that many things are posted in jest, and we know enough not to take them too seriously. As for someone from outside of our community who doesn't understand this -- well, what reason does that person really have to be here? If an "outsider," as you may call him, doesn't like the content on this board, than his best option would be to not read the material, or to just not read this particular board.

Now, I've heard the argument that this is not acceptable EverQuest content many-a-time. Here's what I think about this:
EverQuest is a game based on many things. One of those things is social interaction. Social interaction, in fact, is probably the single-most important factor in this game. The people of Tarew Marr have interacted, and have made their community; this is it. We carry on as a community, and we function as a community- this is the most part of a game like EverQuest. Therefore, stating that this is innappropriate content for an EverQuest message board is unfactual.

Those are my opinions. Feel free to take action against how I instructed you to use your Terms of Service. Like I said, this doesn't feel like a very welcoming community board for a game based on social interaction with prune/ban-happy moderators policing something that has been going on much longer than most of them have been around.
