Yes the 4 robed caster classes can only research spells of their class. So if your wizard wants to research some high end spell but don't think your skill is sufficent simply find a higher level wizard who has worked harder on research for the combine.

However about a year ago they added the ability for any of those 4 classes to research healer spell scrolls- shaman, druid, cleric. So a magician could research shaman spells for example. This is actually how my shaman aquired all the rare 45-61 spells that had research recipes. Just collected all the research components and handed them to a friend with maxed research for the combine.
