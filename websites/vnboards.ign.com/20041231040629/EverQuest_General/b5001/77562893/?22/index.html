Once, ages ago, I played a shaman.

I don't remember what level I was but I was fighting on the bird island in OOT for xp. Some of you may not even know where that is. It is in the Northwest portion of Ocean of Tears. Very private place to xp back then.

Things were going well, until I decided to take on a bird that conned white.

We were going toe to toe. Him with his beak, me with my trusty gatorsmash maul. I had a few dots on him as well. Hit points were dropping fast. He was down to just a sliver of health when he landed a lucky blow that knocked me unconscious.

I cursed and hit the desk with my fist. I knew it was a matter of time now until he finished me off while I lay there helpless, and dreaded the long CR ahead of me.

But, a small miracle happened. He missed. Not once, not twice, but 4 times. Just then, a server tick caused my dots to do a bit more damage and he fell over dead just as I reginerated enough to become concious again.

The best times on EQ, were the days in Hate, trying to get my cleric armor. The posting on the public board (actually this public board) waiting to see if I got picked.
Getting ready to go for the first time...everyone telling us what to do...*drop and roll* I was so scared when I went my first time. They kept telling us to just die if we got attacked..and all I could picture was tons of monsters, waiting for us up there, ready to pounce.
I was so lucky, every Hate raid, I was able to get a piece of armor. I think I even got teary eyed when everyone was clapping and wooting on my last piece, that was my greatest EQ moment. Gawd I worked so hard to get all that armor.
I hated/loved those Hate days, because they were so long, I would get in trouble at home, (how do you explain to a pissy, hubby, that you have to have your cleric armor, and dinner would have to be a pizza) but the best times, because that is when/where I made the most friends on EQ.

Does anyone even do Hate raids for armor anymore? I know now you can just go to the bazaar and buy what you need..but in those old days, armor was scarce and money was tight. and at that time..was the best armor my little cleric could get . AND OMG it was purple...I was a proud grape.

During a GM event, West Commons was filled with werewolves running around causing all kinds of madness. My 65th Ranger just sat back watching other players chasing the werewolves for fun, until shouts about an "unkillable" werewolf began. Someone asked for help in the kill, so I sat back and used my heals as not to steal the kill. No matter what kind of damage he delt it, it regerated it's health back to full with each tick. Other players started in on the kill, but the GM controlled werewolf indeed looked to be invincible. Players began dying off, even with almost a full raid against it. I decided to chime in once my mana was gone from healing, as I knew I would be next on the kill list. My first two arrows did decent damage, but again, the werewolf was almost back to 100% health. My third arrow was a headshot, and did an instant 32k worth of damage. Down went the werewolf, up went my confidence. After realizing I had killed a GM controlled mob(I figured he/she wasn't happy about that), I left the werewolf corpse to be looted by others, and got the hell out of dodge.
