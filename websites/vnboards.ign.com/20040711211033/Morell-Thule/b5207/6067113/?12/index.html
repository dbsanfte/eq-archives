Brother Gartheb.

Monks are some of the most legit players in the game. I suggest that you go to http://thedojo.extropian.org/ for more pointers. I have played a monk to 40th or so before becoming disenchanted with my previous server & no one melees like a monk.

Between dodging or blocking most attacks & mending, you won't be the mana sink that the group tank is, but won't be able to take many hard shots either. (Miyagi-san: "Best defense against a punch. No be there when it comes.)

Best of all, you have Feign Death, arguably the best skill in the game. Necro's get it as a spell but it wears off!! The monk is the only class in the game with some type of "death insurance." Also, once you learn how to Feign Pull well, groups will flock to you. You just have to make a flashy show of skill at Orc camp one by causing a train, feigning & smacking the leftover legionnaire as the others wander back.

(You will get "oooooh's" & "ahhhhhh's" every time. Believe me. One slick pull like that & you will have groupies.)

It has been said many times, but bears repeating. Plane raids don't succeed if a monk isn't with the breaking team. That is written in stone on a table in the basement of Verant.

The only reason I'm playing a paladin & not a monk on this server is because I wanted a change. Otherwise, I'd be a member of the Brotherhood.

Once you find your role, you'll feel better. Also support your local Barbarian Shaman & Enchanters of any race. (except the Tier'Dal. Yuck.) When a shaman buffs you fully or an Enchanter is there to help with crowd control, a monk can't be stopped.

I hope this helps.

I was a big STR / AC monk in my day. You may want to invest in items that will give your punches more stopping power.

For starters, get yourself a Bracer of Erollisi & a Bracer of Battle from Crushbone, as well as some other STR enhancing jewelry. Unrest will be a good stop for you because you may be able get a pair of Jagged Bands cheaper in the zone than from someone selling his excess loot elsewhere.

One extreme thing you may want to try is going to Steamfront & seeing if someone will let you loot the shackles from the Minotaur Hero. After turning in Gnoll teeth in Halas for Steel Warrior faction, you'll be the proud owner of a Hero Bracer (the secret is not to ask about the catman. After getting the quest, say "what about the second gladiator?" That should give you the Mino Hero part of the quest & avoid the trip to Kerra Island & bad faction. The Kerrans have a nice bracer & shoulderpad quests that you will need later on.)

They may be heavy, but it will be one of your best friends until you quit the game or start getting plane armor.

Get up that 1HB and 2HB, too. I loved my Wu Trance Sticks.

Remember, Wizzies & the like can only cast DoT's. You ARE DoT. Walking, virtual DoT.

Feign Death.
Mwhaaha

my Shadow Knight was almost a master of Feign death.

The point behind Feign death is to grab ONE mob at a time.
MOST of the time, its hard to get one mob at a time...
So, you send in a monk, have him go to the place you want to hunt, have him grab a train, and he feign death's far away enough from the group so they don't get aggro'ed by the train of mobs.
Then, when the train leaves, at least ONE mob will stay behind. make sure you time it just right before you get up, or the train will aggro you again, and you'll have a train on your hands.
Hope this helps.
