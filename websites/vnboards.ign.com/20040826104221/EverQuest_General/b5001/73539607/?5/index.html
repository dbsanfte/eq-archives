Dont forget that by using Alt-O you can always remap your keys to make the control of the game they way you like it- one of the best ways is to move the attack key :):) We alllll had to do that back in the day /grin

As far as some other low level dungeons..

Well, there aren't many. If you don't mind exploring caverns.. if you're under 15 click on the shar vahl stone- run straight out (you should have the map for this) to Paludal caverns.. you can begin there around 5 and go to 15)

Then once you're 14 or 15 you can enter paludal through Shadowhaven (connected to nexus or the bazaar) and group on that side of the dungeon until you're 20 or even 25..

Then if it's dungeons you want, I would recommend checking out the LDON adventure camps (once you are 20 someone in your hometown would like to speak to you first) which are located all over- Butcherblock, Southern Ro, East Commons, Northern Ro, and Everfrost.

Just you and your party (min 3 people) receive a private instance of a dungeon to explore and find experience and items...

If this isn't really your thing, than you can check out Unrest (in Dagnors cauldron, off of butcherblock) or perhaps Qeynos Aquaducts (feels like a dungeon)

One of the things that makes it easier to decide where to exp that i like is checking out the zonelist at the eqbeastiary off of allakhazams website (should be a link around here somewherE) and you can sort it by level i believe... As you level up you can use it as a reference as to where your options are to go.

In the 20s-40s there are more options- Upper Guk, Splitpaw, Castle Mistmoore.. just beware of trains
