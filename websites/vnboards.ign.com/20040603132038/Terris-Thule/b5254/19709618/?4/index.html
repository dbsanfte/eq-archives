Anyone else wonder where we are going to find the time to also level our new skills out? A person (pre 60) moving through the levels is going to find it even slower to get to 60 while trying to obtain their new skills along the way. A person at 60 will be focused on raiding with his high level guild and the experience in skill points is going to be few and far between if you aren't getting exp on every kill. So this means that the 59-60s will be hitting the exp grinds more and more to get their new skills maxed out.

I think we are going to see a slowdown on high level mobs being killed because the uber guilds are going to need to leave some time for their players to acquire these skills if they think they are necessary. We are also going to see an increase in armor and weapons since things will be more camped due to the increase of players doing exp grinds. This should have an interesting effect on the economy in EQ. (my advice: if you have it now sell it)

Anyone else have some thoughts on this?
