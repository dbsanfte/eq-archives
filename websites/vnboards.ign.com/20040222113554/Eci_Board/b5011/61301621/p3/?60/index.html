I have been in the Army 15 years and 11 of it in Army Avaition. As Yarslayer said earlier you can't tell who would be a good Soldier and who would be a dirt-bag just from knowing them prior.

Quote from Rrail "I do not want to fly a troop transport, and I don't want to fly a cargo transport. I want to fly an attack helicopter, that's it."

Rrail, I know first hand attack pilots have crappy lives. They go to gunneries all the time, the field all the time and they fly the worst pieces of crap imaginable to man. CH-47D "Chinooks" are the way to go. It is the fastest rotor wing in the US inventory. It is a beast that can take alot of punishment. The guys you will work with are great professionals and trust me , you can have lots of fun and see lots of countries your Attack counterparts never will.

Now I understand in flight school you train on all aircraft. The top grades in Flight School get to pick thier aircraft of choice, the rest get what's available. As was stated earlier too, you can come out of H.S. right into the Warrant Officer Program in regards to flight school. WOC school is one tough school requiring self discipline and motivation.

GL on whatever you decide. Remember too if you do join, respect goes both ways and is earned, not demanded by rank. You can go far with your crew by that thinking.

Have any questions about Army aviation feel free to PM me.
