622 damage would make you lvl 48 and using Conflagration. Try a combo of frost storm and conflag. when you have someone else taunting or taking agro you will do far more damage using rain AE spells on single targets!

Cast frost storm and it will do 250 damage, cast conflag as it does another 250, then just before conflag hits you should get another 250, then 622. This has the added bonus of lagging anyone with spell effects on and giving you more time to do damage cause they cant!

Depending on your competition of course you might not get a chance to cast another round... but in 14 seconds you would have done 1372 damage (barring resists) rather than 1244. This isn't a huge difference but thats due to your level. At level 56 its much more effective.

Cast tears of solusek and it does 645 damage, start casting draught of fire (or jiva) and it does another 645, draught hits for 668 and then another 645. In 11 seconds I just did 2603 damage (again barring resists). Where as I would have gotten 2 casts of draught in the same amount of time (remember to add the base recast on all spells which I usually count as 3 seconds) and thus only done 1336.

Now if you have a real taunter working for you, you could also use dual AE rain spells. So rather than using a DD at all, when you are able to cast your second spell, use tears of druzzil. This combo will do 3735 damage in 13 seconds. But after casting the second one, you only have to wait an extra second or 2 before you can recast the first one. so potentially you could chain cast and do a constant stream of damage... but your tank better have really good agro! Also make sure there are no other mobs in the AE range before you try this! This is quite effective in velks if your group can pull singles. I also use it in Umbral and Sebilis.
