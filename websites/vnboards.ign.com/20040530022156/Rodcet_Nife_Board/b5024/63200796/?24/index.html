looking at the old school thread made me all nastagic.

I'm sure I'm not the only one who did goofy moderated when I was a noob.

My money sceme. I planned on stocking up on derv rings and exporting them to Kaladim, since it was such a long journey, I figgured I'd clean up. After spending all my money buying up what I could in shops and saving all I could carry (groups would actually let me have them for FREE!) I made my way back to BB to begin selling. I think I may have sold 1 or 2 max but for some strange reason no one really wanted them :\

When I first found out about the boat, I went down to the dock and hailed all those high levelers just standing around to find out how much it cost to ride, the snobby bastards would not even hail me back or answer any of my questions!

I use to always log out in a house, because I'd always see other people who obviously were not logged on standing in them too.

Tried to hide in a house from Gix, but he killed me, ran right through the walls!!

First learning you could use both sneak and hide in conjunction and move around. Then traveling all the way to CB from BB hide/sneak...took me about an hour.

Going down to LGuk to find the ass room, I had a map but got hopelessly lost. No problem I just hide/sneak and cruise around till I find my way out. Unbelievably I actuall got all the way to the king room and through the secret door, zoning back to Uguk withought getting agroed. It was heavily camped back then but still that was some luck.

After learning about NPC's (I actually told my friend about my asking those guys at the dock questions and them not answering, which he had a good chuckle about, then explained they were not real people) I thought for like a week there was a DE necro npc killing the dorf chick near x-roads.

I have a ton more, EQ was the first game of this type I ever played and my RL buddies who begged me to play weren't around much to guide me.
