Please post here if you are level 46+ and want to go. All loot will be distributed by Elder House points. We have killed all of the golems and the Shissar in the past; what we can kill depends on our raid force and what is up.

All loot is won by the winning bidder with Elder House points. To view your current points, check the following link:

http://pub28.ezboard.com/felderhousefrm5.showMessage?topicID=5.topic

Remember, you are allowed to bid up to 10 more points than you have, so first-time Elder House raiders can bid up to 10 points.

We will meet in the Feerrott at the rock at the entrance to the Plane of Fear. If you do not know how to get there, come to the Feerrott by the Oggok book, or by druid teleport to the Feerrott ring, and ask the channel how best to get to the southeast corner and past the spectres. Channel will be EHFear.
