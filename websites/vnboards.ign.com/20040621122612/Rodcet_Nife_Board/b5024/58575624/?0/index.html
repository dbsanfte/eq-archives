--== Vault Network Board Rules ==--
These rules are also available here: VNBoard Rules

The Vault Network Message Boards (VN Boards) are a public forum and, while we encourage a free flow of information and ideas on the boards, we also ask that you follow a few basic rules. By using or participating on the VN Boards, you agree to the Rules of Conduct as stated below, as well as the IGN Entertainment User Agreement.
Should you have questions, suggestions or other comments regarding the VN Boards, the appropriate individual to contact is usually the editor of the site associated with the board in question. Please include the name you use on the VN Boards and your real name in any inquiry.

Here are some things you'll want to keep in mind while posting on the VN Boards:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Copyrighted Material
Vault Network board users agree not to post any material that is protected by copyright, trademark or other proprietary right without the express permission of the owner(s) of said copyright, trademark or other proprietary right.


Trade Posts
You may not post EBay auctions or any other sort of real-life monetary transactions anywhere on our boards. This includes EBay links, EBay auction numbers, and anything else relating to EBay sales. You may not create posts regarding account transfers, since such transactions violate MS/Turbine/Zone Terms of Service. This includes beta account information.

Offering Prizes for the most bumps is also against board rules. Requesting that other users bump the thread is ok. Offering in-game items as a starfarm incentive for the best *bumper* is not, as it promotes farming.

Please also note that some Forum Communities (AC Vault) do not allow cross server trades. In general, if a Cross Server Trade forum is provided, then use that. Please check with a Moderator or Manager if you need to.

Flaming, Bashing, and Trolling
Hate posts and personal attacks will not be tolerated on the VN Boards. Treat others on these message boards as you would expect them to treat you. Talking trash about another user is a quick way to earn a banning, as is posting topics specifically to provoke a negative response from someone (aka trolling). Any RL threats will constitute a reason for banning.


Personal Information
You may not distribute private messages, instant message logs, e-mail, etc. unless both parties have provided consent. You may not post any form of real life information.


Profanity
Keep offensive language to a minimum. While we might let you get away with anything you might hear on network television (no, NOT cable) or radio, don't go overboard (that means no S word, no F word, no racial slurs, no slurs of any kind, etc.). A simple rule to follow is: "when in doubt, leave it out." You don't have to use excessive profanity to get your point across, and it's usually better to avoid it altogether. Also, please censor the images you display and chat logs you copy/paste. Any screenshots/images/chat logs with unsuitable words will be edited out of your post and you warned or punished. Please keep this in mind when you link to an image and/or display a chat log. It is your responsibility to nullify any violations.
Mod Note: If your post is edited like ***THIS*** or you find the entire Pokemon Deck in it, consider yourself warned.

Note that Masked Profanity like F&#* is not allowed either.

Spam
Purposely spamming a message board with senseless, vacuous, or empty messages to gain more stars is highly frowned upon!
This includes such things as party posts, chain letters, two-word "I agree" or "me too" responses, continually posting the same topic on a particular board, and posting the same question across several boards. Doing this could result in LOSING post counts.


Piracy, ROMs, and Warez
IGN has a very strict policy against piracy, ROMs, warez, and the illegal use of copyrighted material. Linking or giving information about any site that distributes illegal software, ROMs, media, or emulators, seeking help to circumvent any copyright laws, admitting to possessing and/or distributing illegal copyrighted material, or encouraging software or media piracy is grounds for an immediate ban. This goes for all copyrighted material, including but not limited to games, movies, music, and videos. You may not participate in discussion pertaining to or encouraging exploitation of Asheron's Call. Duping, hacking, and stealing are a few examples. Threads found doing so will be deleted, and the involved parties will be subject to bans.


Impersonating Other Users / Accessing Another User's Account
You may not impersonate another board member or create an account specifically for the purpose of provoking other users. Also, accessing or using someone else's account or attempting to access another poster's account is strictly prohibited.


Off-Topic Posts
In general, Server Forums are more -relaxed- for Off Topic Posting, however, dedicated forums (class, trade, etc) are usually strictly ON topic. Excessive "bandwagon" posts (takeoffs on existing posts) may be deleted and some or all OT may be removed in the event that it becomes a large part of the board content. Follow up posts like "Why was my Cabbage for Breakfast post deleted", will most likely be deleted as well.

Please check with your local moderator or Forum Manager for local rules reguarding On/Off Topic Posts.

Signatures
Please be aware that Signatures are under the same rules as regular posts. Attacking people in a Signature is also against our rules. If you are asked to change a signature by a moderator and refuse, you may be banned until you do.

Spoilers
Just because you know that Darth Vader is Luke Skywalker's daddy doesn't mean that everybody does (whoops, hope you all knew that). Be considerate of the other users who may not have seen a movie or played a game that you're talking about. Simply type something eye-catching like **BEGIN SPOILER** before any text that may give away any significant information about the plot and **END SPOILER** after the text in a post. You should also avoid posting spoilers in a subject line altogether.


Posting Huge Images / Fake Signature Pics
Out of respect for your 56k brethren, please let them know if your thread contains a number of large images. A simple **56kers Beware!** in the subject line will go a long way in helping those who are broadband-challenged know not to open a thread that might bring their web browsing to a crawl. Also if you must use fake signature pictures, please limit it to one 15k or less image since huge pictures slow down the boards.


Circumventing a Ban (aka Evading)
If you log onto the VN Boards and receive a message that you have been banned, please submit an unban request and wait for a Vault Network Message Board moderator to get in touch with you. It is not acceptable to return to the boards under another username. If you do, your ban time most likely will be extended.
You may not publicly complain about or demand a reason for another user's ban. Banned users have the responsibility of answering for themselves on their own. If you have an issue with a Ban, please PM a Moderator or a Manager.


Moderation
You may not re-edit a post that has already been edited by a moderator (a mod's decision is final). You may not bait, insult, flame, or otherwise abuse a moderator; mods are here to make the boards a better place for everyone. If you have a problem with the moderation, please contact that mod or one of the following managers with your concerns (we take them seriously):

Questionable Content
Since we can't have a rule to cover everything, this is the rule to, well, cover everything. These are public boards, so act like you would if you were in a public place. These issues are left to the discretion of the individual moderators, but may include any material that is knowingly false and/or defamatory, misleading, inaccurate, abusive, vulgar, hateful, harassing, obscene, profane, sexually oriented, threatening, invasive of a person's privacy, that otherwise violates any law, or that encourages conduct constituting a criminal offense.

NOTE: Posting links to graphic sites - either sexual, violent or containing illegal or invasive (i.e. massive window spamming) material will get you instabanned and may get you permabanned

Clarification on OT Posts

We have people who post here from all over the world. We come in all Races, Religions, Nationalities, Genders, Sexual Prefences and Orientations, Political Persuasions and more. The one thing that we have in common is that we play games that are designed to be an escape from the Realites of our daily lives, whatever our circumstances may be. Everyone, from anywhere, should be able to come here and post on a Game Board about a Game without feeling attacked, be-littled, or confronted with Real Life Issues that are totally irrelevant to the Games we share.

The Vault does provide places for us to discuss Real Life. ACFriends and the other Game Community Forums (free), as well as Insider Boards including the VN Community Board, and the new Politics Board are available for anyone to use should they feel the need to start a debate. Also, over on the other side of the house, the IGN Boards have many more boards with special topics like Sex and Dating that we can visit. Remember though, even on those boards, OT is not the same as spam

We would like to stress that this does not mean that Off Topic posting is forbidden. Moderate and appropriate community based discussion on World Forums is something we love. Just be aware that if it becomes too heavy, or goes to pharming or spamming, the mods will prune it down to a reasonable level. OT is by nature ephemeral, and subject to locking or deletion if it begins to overwhelm the Forum on all but the pure OT boards.

But, though we do allow Off Topic posting on places like the World Community boards, fairness to all our community members requires that we not go into certain areas there.

Because of this we are asking everyone to keep these topics to the appropriate forums, and leave them off the World Forums and other Game Specific areas.

Examples would include Politics, and Hot-Button Social Topics, including Race, Religion, Abortion, Gun Control, Sexual Preference/Orientation, and similar topics that we all know elicit strong feelings in many people.

Thank You for your consideration.
