*As the faithful druid looks out of his window he realized something is wrong. A army of white haired, black skinned warrior was marching upon the small village near Freeport. This small village had no army, only a few warriors, paladins, and rangers. This druid watched as the meager troops started to run out to the Teir'Dal. The druid of Karana puts on his armor and heads out into the battle*
I walked up to the first person I see. He was a Wood Elven rogue.
"How are you?" I asked the rogue.
"Could be better, how about you?" the wood elf responded.
"I am well, my name is Itlan Rainstorme, what may yours be?"
"I am Rasi, just leave it at that," he responded.
*We marched out into the field of battle. Rasi was sneaking behind the Teir'Dal dragoons backstabbing them, taking their dirks as he went. Then he got Harm Touched from behind, MORE TEIR'DAL WERE COMING. I quickly grabbed Rasi and we ran with everyone else. Fireworks exploded from behind the Teir'Dal, a backup diversion. We thought it worked. It didn't. We all ended up surrendering so we would keep the women and children safe.*
*They took us to the Neriak jails. Pure horror. We got whipped twice every day. The only reason we survived was passion to see our homes. Rasi was still unconscious. He was the only rogue we had so escaping would be impossible. The Teir'Dal treated the Elves and Paladins the worst. They ignored me. Apparantly they didn't mind Half Elves. Rasi stayed unconscious for days. Finally when the guards weren't looking I healed him. The guards didn't take any of the dirks he stole. he handed them out, time for payback.*
