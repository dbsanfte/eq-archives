If you are looking for a mellow guild and just tired of the stress of being around immature people, look into joining the Random Insanity guild.

This is a small guild (under 100 members thus far I think), but that is only because they are caeful recruiters. Technically, they have, at times, accepted all levels, but when they recruit lower levels they do not hesitate to boot them out if out of control. They have very strict guild rules and are run by a council of 4 leaders. All in all, it would most likely be the least stressfull guild you've ever been in.

The downside is they are not a "raiding" guild, per say. For the most part, the members concentrate on public raids. However, they are in the process of forming a really good raid guide for future use.

The people of this guild form lasting friendships...and best of all, you can't be a member for more than a month without forming friendships with several members. In some cases, friendships have lasted several years.

They have a very nice website at:
http://www.random-insanity.us

If you ask around, you'll learn that these people are top of the line and have earned the respect of many other guilds.
