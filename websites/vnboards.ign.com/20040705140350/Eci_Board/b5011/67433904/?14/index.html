I think it's a decent game. I like the concept of sieges and castle defending and completely different class trees for each race. Not really a more complex game then Everquest, but it does have more of a "fun" factor.

For one, I haven't been able to log-in in over a week because of server issues.
Secondly the PvP system could use some tweaking. There are some bugs that make you go "red" for defending yourself from a PKer, not fun.
For those that haven't played, when you PK someone and they don't defend themselves, your name turns red and people are able to attack and kill you without changing red. Also, when you are red you are more likely to drop an item when killed.
