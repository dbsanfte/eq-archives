 Date Posted: 11/22/99 6:14pm Subject: Why don't we all just quit EQ?
This is Klairen, I not going to register on yet another damn board.

Look what this game has turned into. People waiting in lines to kill stuff. There's an overflow of greed everywhere. Tempers are always high. I thought games were supposed to be fun, a way to get away from it all.

Verant's "quick fixes" are nails in the coffin. Mobs that get stuck are teleported on top of you to prevent cheating - they should fix the damn pathing instead. My favorite are the hidden nerfs that are slipped in under our noses. There has been no official announcement, but I swear that the healing range has been reduced. I have to be almost touching someone to heal them now.

I quit a few months ago and just should have stayed gone. Peer pressure from a real life friend does have an affect on me though. That and the promise of great quest armor. The same armor that I have to compete with level 50 wizards to get. So far, I have been extremely lucky. I have all but about 3 pieces and spent maybe 6 hours of camp time.

I run through Guk and the ingot is there. I run through Kith and the ingot and book are there. I swim under the frozen river and the ingot is there. I spend 3 full bars of mana clearing the roamers on kerra isle before Maugarim spawns. I play my friends druid and in Dag's Cauldron, I hit tracking one time and Bilge is there and almost on top of me. I gate to Misty and Sludge is waiting on me in Runnyeye.

I have a level 1 monk parked on the aviak island in OOT (named Gullwatcher), I hope he gets lucky too. I log in with him, make a lap around the island and log out. Know where that 6 hrs of camp time is from? Me continually checking Befallen for that little bastard of a gnome that never spawns. If that is the way that the cleric armor quests are supposed to be, then I pity all the clerics out there hunting for it.

Even with all my shiney new armor, I still think this is a terrible game. I refuse to stand in line to wait on anything. I don't go to dungeons unless I get an invite or I think I have a good chance of soloing. And I'm *damn* sure that I am not going to spend hours camping some crappy little item that will replace my +3/+3 int/wis savants cap with a +4/+4 int/wis whatever.

So until Diablo 2 hits the shelves,

Klairen Charlemagne, Cleric

The group class with the solo temple quests.
