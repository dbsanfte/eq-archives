I don't know about you but I couldn't buy EQ the day it came out. I think it wasn't until about day three when it was in stores around here which is when I bought it. Of course with all their network and server problems the game wasn't really playable until it had been out a month or so.

As for Freeport, it's a fine city I suppose. I'm currently working on making it so my Iksar can walk the streets without fear of being mugged by some guard. I'm not really into spending huge amounts of time working on faction but I REALLY need a bank in Eastern Antonica (the one in Qeynos sewers serves me well in Western Antonica). I didn't choose Freeport because it was the nicest, but because of its location. Besides, Freeport gives the most bang for the buck. It's most centrally located, being roughly equidistant between Sol B and Guk; you can buy just about anything there; it has a monk guild there which someday I might be able to enter without dying a quick death; and if I am not KoS there then it makes using the boat to Faydwer much less of a pain. Despite the fact that I usually can get a teleport, there are times when it's handy to be able to use the boat (and yes I know many of this younger generation have never used a boat which is sad).
