As another Test Server player now "without a home", I'd like to make a few comments.

I know that a lot of folks are going to treat us like refugees of RL "wars", we're neither wanted nor liked. We can't like help that, but we are trying to make the best of a very bad situation (hopefully) without offending anyone.

I'd like to say that the folks who would like to move here from Test are some of the best players in the game, bar none. We weren't twinked, nor as Verant seems to think "over equipped". We *earned* our plane armor and dragon gear the same way everyone else did...we killed the mobs without GM assistance, buffing, etc. While it's true we didn't have to sit in lines for most spawns, we also had to fight to and from those spawns, as on Test a lvl 1 newbie can't walk from zone to the lord room...he'd be lucky to make the bottom of the ramp on the way to the bedroom. The most a GM did for us was to pop zones for us so we could have somewhere to hunt, as Test was almost always downed at least once per day for changes, often still being downed late into the prime time hours for east coast players. We've gladly swallowed rollbacks, loss of items, falling through zones, etc. more times than we'd like to count. And did it willingly, because Test was our home.

Now our home is devastated, many of our close friends we've made in game gone, and we need to move on.

All the most of us can offer is our friendship and our experience to help the folks of your server out, and to hope to be someday be accepted to be a welcome part of the community you folks have worked hard to establish. I think you may find we aren't "elite kewl d00ds", and we don't charge for buffs and ports, either.

We were testers, but first and foremost we were players and family.

I made a DE Necro on your server as soon as we got pwiped, just to check things out. While only making level 4 might not be a representative sampling your your player base, I was favorably impressed compared to most of the other servers. I was only KS'ed twice, and once was inadvertent. However, I was the subject of a few "drive by buffings", and did thought this is quite the way we did it on Test. The screen wasn't filled with /auctions and spam, but with some lighthearted banter....once again, much like Test.

I just ask of you to not judge us hastily, we abhor KSers, item campers, etc. as much as the majority of you do. (If you like, check the Test Server board here on Vault for our opinions of those who chose to participate in the SolB dragon loot debacle. But be warned, you'll have to scroll back quite a few pages, the message area is mostly now filled with anger at Verant for our treatment and a lot of very sad goodbye note to our friends.)

If Verant decides to allow us to move, I hope you'd /con amiable to us on your server.

Look me up there once I get my ranger back into a bit of armor and whatnot. My DE Wizzie is Tsatorn.

Good luck and good hunting to all,
