In no particular order...

Bactros

I remember the first time i'd ever been to plane of Mischief. Bactros showed me and my friends around the place, after we sat around outside killing the yardtrash for about a day, since we had no clue how to get inside the little castle part, and had no idea how the spawns worked. He even gave us cards to complete quests, and he even insisited on lottoing for the only card he needed to complete some of the quests, although I think we might have convinced him to just take it, can't remember that was like 2 years ago, but at the time I thought it was going to be the last time I would ever play EQ since I was leaving for basic training & ait for 6 months, and he made that whole week one of the best EQ memories for me and some friends.

Kovea

Kovea literally gave me the shirt of his back one day. Back when Heirophants Cloaks were one of the best things since sliced bread, he gave me his, and wore around something far less useful. He would raid with almost any guild from the uber to the small cliche's of friends and not because he was out for loot, but because his friends were more important than phatlewt00.

Vison

Alot of people on the server hated him, but those who knew him, and were on his good side loved him. He was as skilled as any monk I ever met, and if you were fortunate enough to be his friend found you had an absolutely loyal person. He would die a thousand deaths than to see anyone try to mess with his friends.

Blyte

I don't know how many guilds i've been apart of in my EQ career, but of all of the ones I have, I will tell you that Blyte was the best leader i've ever met. I've not played with Blyte in a very long time because he quit playing, but if I were to ever play another game, and he were playing, I would want to be playing in the same guild as him.

Last person is too hard to choose, so it goes to anyone who has ever done something selfless or sacrificed for someone else. Also included, would be all my friends and people I've been guilded with, all of you have been in some part what made EQ great to me.
