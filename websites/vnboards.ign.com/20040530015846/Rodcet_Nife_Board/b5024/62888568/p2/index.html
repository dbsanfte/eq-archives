Kylvore, I think you are talking about Tumpy Tonics, a level 50 druid, Grumps came up with this plan to level me (level 18, and his level 18 cleric) and we did TTs for 4 days. Using two druids, and killing the dwarfs in BB (axes would get us 44pp then) we made TTs around the clock. Finally a GM asked us what we were doing. She could not quite say it was an exploit, but both the cleric and I made 14 levels in 4 days, then we backed off, we heard a couple of days later that two things happened, the prices of axes dropped way off and the tonic quest no longer worked. We were just so damned tired of pushing buttons and going back and forth between ocean of tears, kaladim and freeport. It was a quick 14 levels tho. My son was a bit ticked tho. He had finally figured out what we were doing, but it was too late, the game was up. Now that was old school.

Sometimes I run thru the Butchblock Mountains now and mourn that the dorfs are unoccupied. In the old days you waited for days for your turn at them. They were good kills. Friends and I worked up a rotation so that we kept the dorf in the tent and the two in the hut occupied for three weeks. When we went to bed a team from Germany took over, they turned them over to a team from Korea and they gave them to us, as long as there was a waiting list we never gave them up. That was sweet.
