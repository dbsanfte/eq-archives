Hi, I'm Bimagan 60 Ogre Shaman in service of the almighty Cazic Thule. Until recently I had been a lowly servant of an evil ShadowKnight. Fate however has smiled upon me and I am no longer under the ownership of a madman.

To celebrate my newfound freedom and life I wish to lead an army of fellow raiders and bring havoc upon the denziens of Kael Drakkal that worship the rival god Rallos Zek.

I care not what guild you are, or what god you serve or even your motivation for taking a breath of air. Also those known as "alts/twinks" are also welcome, althought I must confess I despise that denotation as I was once referred to as such! I only require your determination to lay many so called "giants" to waste.

The spoils of our upcoming victory will be determined by the fates. /random with a seed of your lvl * 10. So as an example I would type /random 0 600.

Date: Sunday 04/04/04

Duration: 3hrs maximum.
