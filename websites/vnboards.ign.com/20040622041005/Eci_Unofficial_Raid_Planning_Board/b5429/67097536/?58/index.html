I am posting this here, knowing that there aren't nearly as many people who visit here as there used to be, but out of pure and simple curiosity.

I am trying to judge interest in forming a public raiding force that would be interested in Velious through POP (GoD would depend on those who own it, not really realistic at this point) content raids, and all who attend would be able to ROLL for loot that drops, not DKP.

I am hoping in the future to provide an alternative to DKP raids..

If you have an opinion on this, please feel free to share, and remember that if you like to raid with DKP, that is your perogative, but the purpose of this post is to see if anyone would be interested in a ROLL public raiding force.

If there is enough interest, in the next few weeks I may be looking to set something like this up and give it a try for all those interested.

There aren't a lot of specifics here but a few basic guildlines to point out would be:

-You must meet minimum level requirements per raid (it will change depending on target, this would not be strictly 62+ like most other public forces)

-You may roll on an item that drops that your class\race\deity may use with only the character(s) you bring to the raid. This would be the rule for almost any item, unless in rare exception there is an item that is 100% agreed upon is best suited for a partciular grouping of classes (pure melee, priest, caster.. etc)

-You must be in the raid the majority of the time in order to ROLL (if you're late by 30 mins tops fine, get a coh if you can, but don't show up at the last minute with a killshot expecting something)


There's just a few basic guildlines..


So, tell me your opinions on a public roll raiding system please!

Ashorty's DKP is older than Elder House's.

Casual gamers can even get the nicest items first, if they happen to be on a raid when it drops and do not spend their points before then. It is not as good a value, especially for a casual gamer, but it IS their choice. I know of no DKP systems where points expire.

Also, Elder House's system was originally designed for tracking attendance more than "dragon" kills. A cleric who helps with Fear or the Hole or the Plane of Sky, where a cleric has no good loots expected, can earn points for the Chardok raid he really wants, so he can get the King. Message board listing current points is at http://pub28.ezboard.com/belderhouse.

Ishiro, I would rather award bonus points than dock people for mistakes. I am not perfect, and sometimes I forget things. BUT...I HAVE been docking points for those things that may ruin the whole raid. -20 points for an early hail, and -5 points for extra hails (which deprive other raiders of flags). These things are announced well in advance, so those that do these things wrong deserve the penalties.

Elder House is not intended to be a one-raid-leader organization, but I have not yet been able to authorize other raid leaders under the system. I am working on it. I have thought the public raiders ought to just work under a single system, instead of mulitple ones. And no, it does not seem feasible yet to convert Elder House points to Zeek DKP, for those that keep asking!
