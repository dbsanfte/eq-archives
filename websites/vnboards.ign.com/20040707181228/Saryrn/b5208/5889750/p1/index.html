If you are relying on that warrior who ONLY has a taunt skill to save your ass, then you basically deserve to die IMO. Warrior taunt by itself only goes so far, and its usefulness is greatly diminished by someone who insists on using absurdly fast, low dmg 1h weapons in their primary hand. With Kunark, there are a LOT of nice new, ranger wieldable, 2handed weapons. I can understand why Verant is so reluctant to deal with this issue that basically came out of the blue only a month or so ago. Rangers in general haven't been changed, therefore it must be the player or the equipment they are choosing to use that is causing the mega-taunt effect.
