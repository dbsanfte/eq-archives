You ask me if I remember the days of old? The days when warriors, priests and adventures, alike banded together for the common good of their friends? As a result of this convergent, groups, guilds, and alliances were formed. We could call on our allies to aid us on the most distant battlefield whenever we needed them and they would come. I remember those friends of mine those friends whom I would call upon in times of need. Even to this day I remember their names and their faces. Our friendships blossomed in times of pleasure but more so grew in times of battle and turmoil. The need for unity began to grow with the coming age of darkness and so it did.

Ancient guilds some of the first in Norraths history came together to fight the ever impending clouds of doom. Our strength and our passion for each other was put to the test during the siege of High Keep at the start of the Great Wars. Other wise known and bloody Kithicor, the forces of good came together and held their ground in the rocky cliffs of the pass and finally atop the majestic battlements of the keep. After the ordeal Karana graced us with a rainstorm to wash away the blood and tears on the fallen, a sign of continuance and prosperity. I remember looking into the eyes of those men who were still alive some of them new recruits, some of them life long friends. On that day we all had witnessed the most horrific battle Norrath had ever seen. Only tightening our friendship and allowing us to march on and defeat the enemy once again in the Qeynos hills.

It has been years since the Great Wars and like the blood of my comrades on that fateful day, the stories and texts commemorating those who fought washed away and was forgotten. No unified tests of triumph exist today. No feats of strength that require the hearts and swords of true warriors from Odus to Faydwer. We have forgotten our rich past and those warriors who shaped Norrath into the world it is today. Those warriors of old will not be immortalized in the minds of the new age but rather in cracks of High Keep, the grassy fields of Karana and Qeynos, the dirt and rocks of Western Freeport, and the ocean water of the Timorous deep.

You ask me about the days of old? Aye I do remember them but they will never come again, in this world anyway. And the face of falseness we have a chance to start again! To relive the comradely and the friendship we all shared. Those who remember what I spoke of today, you who lived the battles with me, we can come again in a new age of Norrath! We can rebuild our former alliances and set the standard for valor in a world where it is uncommon. We will never bring back those who were lost but we can give them life in our time. In closure I leave you with this

Call to arms: Come, Those who remember the Norrathian Senate and those who remember the wars, or those who seek union and chivalry in an age of selfishness. Those of us who seek these things, the new age is coming and we can bind together to have them once again. Good luck on your travels brothers and sisters of Norrath I only hope I can see you again in the new world.
-Elvendors Pureheart
Cazic-Thule server
