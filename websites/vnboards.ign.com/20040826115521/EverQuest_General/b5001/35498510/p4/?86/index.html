 		Date Posted: 8/7 1:10pm Subject: RE: EQ Primer For Newbies - Date Edited: 8/7 1:12pm (1 edits total) Edited By: Sabbatha
make sure to max your casting skills each level and specialize in alteration.

You are the healer in this game. Yes, you can play other roles but in most situations your partner, group, or raid is going to rely heavily on your healing abilities for success.

So, you don't want to fizzle all the time and you don't want to be grabbing agro from over nuking. If someone dies it is generally your fault (this perspective from a retired cleric) if they just weren't getting heals.

Best advice I can give in general is focus on wisdom and mana items until you max your wis via item or AA and try to find a few people that play their classes well (or show interest in improving) that you like hanging out with. Once you find a rhythm with a group you can accomplish a lot, have fun, and maybe even play a different role than the norm and be effective..

Just have fun and make sure you have enough mana to heal through fights.
