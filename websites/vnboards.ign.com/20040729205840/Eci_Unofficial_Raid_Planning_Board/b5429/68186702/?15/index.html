I've been on several PoSky raids, with my warrior toon. I empathize with everyone's frustration with these raids. They're infrequent, they're difficult to put together and organize, and they're expensive.

The number one reason I've seen PoSky Raids fizzle is because of the lack of a keyed necro. Without a keyed necro, the raid force does not have the ability to hop to desired islands. So instead, the raid force will either not go up, or will start from island 1.

Unlike raids such as chardok, where only one cleric or one warrior gets their epic drop, PoSky has the ability to reward many people on the same raid. This is a double edged sword. While it's great that 6 rangers can get their drop off of isle Four, that means that the next raid, you may have six less rangers as dps. And without a Keyed Necro, It's a safe bet that the raid will only get up to isle 4 on a regular basis.

Sylvans, I admire your desire to take up the reigns on this epic stumbling block. I would like to help when ever possible. But I'm convinced that without a commitment from a keyed necro, it makes this raid a very, very difficult prospect.
