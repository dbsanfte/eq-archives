helpful to have (NOT in order of importance, and notice i said "helpful")

1. snare. period. you need this. helpful when fear kiting, which is something recommended for duo's at some point when hunting in the great outdoors.

classes (by effectiveness): druid, ranger, necromancer, shadow knight, wizard (you'll have to wait for 29 for that), bard.

2. fear. goes with above.

classes (by effectiveness): enchanter (YES enchater, sorry necros), necromancer, cleric, shadowknight, bard, druid/ranger (animal only).

3. healing. rather obvious even if you have just heard of the game.

classes (by effectiveness): cleric, druid, shaman, paladin, ranger, bard, necro, monk (self only).

4. sow or some kind of movement increase spell. this is another requirement for a good duo. sooner or later you need to run, and it helps traveling greatly.

classes (by effectiveness): shaman (because they get it earlier), druid, ranger, bard.

5. some form of mana regen. tho this isn't strickly required it does help cut downtime greatly.

classes (by effeciveness): enchanter, bard, necromancer (above shaman because they have both self only and can give mana to others), shaman, wizard.

6. tanking ability. ok this is more or less required, i wont say much on this one because i don't want to piss too many tanks off hehe, i will avoid talking about pets . hopefully you can figure out the classes by yrself.

7. misc nice things... crowd control: nice, but not exactly required if you are careful. .

classes (by effectiveness): enchanter (duh), bard, necromancer.

tracking: ditto.

classes (by effectiveness): ranger, druid, bard.

porting: yaddda yadda yadda.

classes (by effectiveness): druid, wizard.
