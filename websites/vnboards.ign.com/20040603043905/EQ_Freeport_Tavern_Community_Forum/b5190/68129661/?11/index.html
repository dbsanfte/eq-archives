No, One expansion ahead of the other does not get you the previous ones.

Kunark, Velious, Luclin, Planes of Power, Legacy of Ykesha, Lost Dungeons of Norrath, and then Gates of Discord is the order.


If you are planning on playing again, you will need all except Gates unless your 60+ or 50+ and wanna exp there. Otherwise your gonna be very limited and wont be able to do much hope that helps.
