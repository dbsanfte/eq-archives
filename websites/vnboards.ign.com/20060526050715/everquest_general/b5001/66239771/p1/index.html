Here's how I have it:

Main: Send in pet, move in to melee range
BST2: Send in pet, begin spell hotkey action
BST3: Send in pet, move into melee range
BST4: Send in pet (if mob is still alive) heal Pet #1 as needed

Main BST gets full attention.. One mob is engaged, I switch hands to keyboards 2 and 3, which thru hotkeys, I can have them engaged in the fight in less than 2 seconds. Switch to BST 4, get him into combat, within 3 seconds after that. So within 5 seconds, i can have all 4 fighting with pets. Take a few seconds to figure out who needs to move around, who needs to also engage in combat (BST 2 and BST 4) who needs to back out and heal, etc...

I've 4-boxed with lots of different combos of classes, I think streamlining it to 4 of the same will greatly improve my efficiency.. ALL hotkeys will be the same, making it super easy to move hands to new keyboards and not have to look..

I'm still trying to figure out what is the best 4-box combo. I'd like to cover key skills in the game so I started out with a warrior/cleric/chanter/druid to have tanking, rezzing, virtue, kei, slowing, crowd control, evac, porting, tracking, snare. But the dps of that group sucked. I could hunt 6 hours in Nadox and only get about 1/2 AA for each of them at 65.

Someone on this board told me I should lose the warrior and cleric and do a enchanter/druid duo to charm for high dps and faster exp, but I like chatting a lot when I solo. For me that's the whole point in soloiing--is so I can hunt mindlessly and talk to lots of people. When I group, I only play my chanter and need to focus to keep up with my group's frantic pulling pace in LDoN or BoT, or charming a mob that hits for 1200 in God trials that I can not talk a lot.

I should probably just make a BL or a necro to solo to chat with friends. But I love grouping too, and I'd like my soloing time to help building characters that can help a group. It seems when I try to form a group with friends we often are looking forever for a tank. I used to bring my warrior to help, but didn't like the way he couldn't hold aggro well in a full group with other people. So I'm thinking about replacing him with an SK or pally for better aggro control. And replacing the druid with a wiz for better dps.

I PLed a SK that is 49 now, but when my chanter grouped with some SKs in LDoN and GoD trials, I saw their one handicap is in their using darkness (a dot) to gain aggro. Once a mob is dotted, and if a add is a Named that we need to take care of first, it can be a problem. Some SKs will feel very bad about it. FD pulling is also not my forte.

I like the graphical spell effect of a pally's stun. I thought I'd switch to a pally/cleric/chanter/wiz team for the times I solo. So I started making a pally and a wiz. Pally is 18 now, wiz is 31. But I like my druid too much because he was my main character for ages. So I just deleted the wiz and replaced him with a level 1 BL. Maybe I'll do a pally/cleric/BL/druid team instead. This way the BL can slow. No crowd control for this 4-boxing team though.

What do you think is the best 4-box group for being able to adventure in all kinds of zones for fun? If I don't worry about dps and exp rate, my old 4-box team was really fun. I took them for a fun trip one night working through the shrine in Nadox from the shrine steps to the lava stream. If things got bad my druid would evac. A 4-BL team probably won't need crowd control at all for that trip! Neither will you need to evac. Just have them all kill, kill. Hehehe.

SK can be really good for an adventuring 4-box Main tank. In case things get bad, CR is easy. I would not go to Ferubi without a SK or necro in the group! So maybe a sk/cleric/BL/druid team would be good too.

A wiz can do better dps and can evac too, doh I just love my druid too much to want to retire him. :-)

I would love to hear what are some of the other 4 box teams people have tried.

Oh yeah, a friend of mine plays a bard, wiz, cleric trio. He just needs a tank and is good to go in any group. Another friend of mine 4-box a BL/chanter/cleric/druid team and mops up LDoNs all by himself. Apparently one BL is all the dps he needs.
