I know we've had a bit of a bump in our perfect ideal of a server. I've noticed everyone is sort of holding their breath, like after a storm has come. Is it really gone?

I think it's more important than ever for us to come together as a community, to band together and help each other back up from a trying period. No I'm not being melodramatic. I just don't want to see the spunky spark flare out. Is that silly of me? Maybe..but that's one of the things I love SO much about our server.

I would love to see more big events happen. Bazaars, tournaments, raids, player driven quests..What I've seen so far has been wonderful and refreshing.

We don't all same the same Vision (tm) We all have different goals and dreams of how we want to play on Torv. But enough of us love this server and want to see it molded into something special I think..from the posts and ideas I've read so far I feel this to be true.

I'd like to get an idea of who would like to be involved in making more special events happen, of organizing and informing people of these events. Not necessarily big raids, they could be small things too. Making sure weddings are announced, new players are welcomed, etc. Anyone out there who would like to start making plans to see more happen within the community? It doesn't need to be anything official, but just a group of players who enjoy helping other's to enjoy themselves too. I'd love to hear any and all comments on this

Enough of my rambling for one night. Have fun everyone, and if you see me feel free to ask for a sow, maybe even a few buffs ;D (Just don't ask for money..Grrr..Beggers *growls*)
