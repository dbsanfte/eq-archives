Actually, Okami, you're quite wrong. I've been studiosly avoiding hitting L60 *ever*, for three years now if you count the beta days, more than 2.5 years if you don't.

As someone else said, if you would like ONE rule changed (and note the rule I'd like changed no longer inconveniences *me* in any serious way), then this server is the best available choice. Why would one go to a server (or go back to a server) where the ruleset is even LESS enticing, unless one is an idiot? So, I see people condoning and encouraging idiocy? Well, pardon me if I ignore such encouragement, and I suspect most others will as well.

As for roleplaying, if I were one of the l33t guys racing to 60, which I am not, I would not have chosen to be good (when neutral was a choice) on a server that is comprised predominantly of evils or neutrals associated with evils, nor would I have toggled way up the xp flow into the alternate advancement system (which obviously seriously slows down leveling). It's no race, or if it is then I'm not participating and don't care enough who wins to even see the finish line.

Those who flee problems they see are never around to see problems fixed. I was one of those who complained about the eternal spellbook, and thus helped get it removed after L35. I was one of those who, even while playing an enchanter, complained about how overpowered the enchanter Whirl spell line was (and it was WAY overpowered), and thereby helped get it fixed. I was one of those who complained about the wall exploitations in a variety of places, and thereby got those fixed (well, as best they could given game mechanics issues). And on, and on, and on. If everyone who ever saw a problem just kept their mouth shut and moved on then EQ would seriously suck.

Simple stereotyping such as this I have just seen here sometimes -- perhaps even often -- comes from simple minds. I roleplay. I am over L50. I have eight characters all total over L50. I have never seen L60. I very probably never *will* see L60 and not only doesn't that bother me, but that's been a goal of mine: NOT to reach 60. Rules that create bugs that will never be fixed do bother me. That would be true if I was L1 or L20 or L45 or whatever. It would be true if the bugs seriously affected just me (they don't), and it is true even when the bugs only seriously affect others (which is the case).

In addition, as this is a public forum where people who play on the server have every right to state their opinions (as long as they do so without resorting to name-calling or profanity), you look rather lame telling people they either don't have that right or shouldn't use it. 'Waaa, I don't like what that guy is saying, make him go away, Mommy!' Grow up. I could just as easily say: if you don't like what you read, then go away. I don't say that, but it's absolutely analogous. I don't say it, because I'd consider THAT rather lame too.
