i cant speak for baven, nor do i speak for The harmonium, but i can say in my own opinion that the reservation system is disfunctional and arbitrary. this is an opinion from a power gamer, not a social butterfly nor a believer in social engineering.

it is disfunctional because it is a piece of social engineering that is non-sanctioned and non-inforcible and therefore cannot be universal. it is non-sanctioned because it is not Verant policy and it is abused. the abuse is that anyone at any time can post that they want to reserve a zone, but there is no end to this and it becomes a slippery slope to reserving a mob. it is also non-sanctioned because not everyone, from every guild believes it is just and right, even if some of the smaller guilds or patchwork guilds do. reserving is an attempt to claim territory in advance as if you have a right to be there, more than anyone else, because you arbitrarily decided that on day X at time Z you wanted to be there AND as if that were sufficient ground to take the zone. the disfuntionality of the reservation system is a matter of the weaker attempting to displace the stronger and apply a ruling over groups of people that have zero reason to follow the ruling. if you want to reserve a zone, get there first or communicate with guildleaders in game. there is no reason that a reservation made here should be honored necessarily, because there is no reason that someone else should have to respect that or yield to an arbitrary system that is non-inforcible.

the system is arbitrary because it can only reach people who read this board and people who accept this board. it is arbitrary because there are no laws and there is nothing to establish such laws or rules such that they should be respected. there is no government nor are there police to regulate. the absurdity is that people feel that the reservation system carries any wieght as if their own mental state should have power over people actions.

the reservation system is a piece of arbitrary and pseudo public claiming in advance of something that you couldnt get unless the bigger and stronger decided to yield. the reservation system gives you no moral right to anything and if some guild takes what you reserved, you have zero moral right to complain.

it is my personal opinion that this reservation system turns upside down what should be valued in EQ and makes what should be valued into something evil. EQ is a game in which only the most organized and best skilled should triumph and get the best rewards. this is the way it used to be and these most organized and best skilled groups of people used to communicate with each other to determine what was up and yielded accordingly. now there are too many guilds who can do stuff, but there are still few who can do it well and quickly and efficiently. the problem is that the less organized and less efficient are attempting to institute and claim higher moral ground and then claim victim status.

EQ is not about equal-opportunity or medicare or social nets or welfare. EQ is not a democracy. Eq is about the fun and the struggles to get to the top and part of this fun and part of this struggle is the power and ability to get things done. this is what pays off in EQ in the high level zones. and part of the way to get things done is to communicate with leaders IN GAME and gain respect from them. if not, who the hell cares who you are and what you reserved?

the cream floats to the top. and though surely many wont like this, i will say it anyway: the reservation system should not be honored. if you want to do a zone, talk to the leading guild guildleaders. and if you dont know who they are, you made my point for me. whether you like the top end guilds or not; whether you have any respect for their acievements or ability; if you think for a second that you can disrespect them AND hhave them respect your wishes, you are deluded.

the only reason that the reservation system has functioned with fear and hate up to now is because the older, more mature and powerful guilds have other things to do. but if they want to take a plane, provided that no one else is in there, i see no reason why they should have to yield to the new kids on the block. and if it is one of the older guilds in there, i am sure that the guild leaders would be talking because THEY respect each other.

anyway, as i said, i am not speaking of a member of The Harmonium, though i am one. i could have said this anon like a corward, but i didnt. this is my personal take on the matter, and i think i would have the same opinion no matter what guild i was in, or even if i were not in a guild at all.

again, there is no reason that people have to even consider the reservation system. it is created to develop a welfare system for the smaller, weaker guilds.

survival of the fittest, nature red in tooth an claw: if you want to do a zone, gain the respect of the older guilds. from their perspective you are a noob, new kid on the block, whiner and wlefare case. earn what you want.

some guild's reservations hold wieght because those guilds have respect already. if you want to be respected, gain it. gain it by showing those who already gained respect that you respect them and that you are worthy of respect. if you cannot see that this is important you dont deserve to have your reservation honored.

flaming this only proves my point.
