With that particular duo combination, you could Temp-Flux-Staff kite in the Plane of Innovation, the Plane of Nightmare, or the Plane of Disease, in the areas surrounding the graveyards.

If you've never Temp-Flux-Staff kited before, it's simple: The Wizard snares a non-summoning mob, such as a Fly in PoDisease. Then the Wizard backs away from the mob, staying just out of reach, while furiously spamming a hotbutton that triggers the Wizard's Staff of Temperate Flux. Once you've got solid agro, the Warrior walks along behind the mob and hits it in the back with his weapons, preferably the weapons with the lowest taunt and the highest damage possible. If the mob turns on the Warrior, the Warrior stops attacking and the Wizard regains agro. I've done this before, and it works well if you haven't got a healer, slower, or anything else other than a pure melee and a Wizard.
