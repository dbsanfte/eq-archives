Good raid, we WON, LORON is epicked !

The fight down was smooth after we picked up some errant swimmers
and regrouped in the shower room. Tanking and RL'ing is quite hard
because I need to be IN FRONT and IN BACK at the same time. Thanks
Songweever and Zynn for fabulous help.

Trakanon was VERY MEAN, banished our MT at least twice and killed
our 2T, but we paid him back. Very good heads up CS in the lair
to get Gurgunk and Lydine back from shrooms.

Lots of spells and a very rare ranger BP, which casts GATE, to
lovely Naliah. I also found a bag of extra spells that I failed
to distribute because the open bag picture was hidden behind
another one. Most of them are duplicates, but I have a copy of
Dictate for Minimezzy if he needs it. I found the copy of
Quivering Veil and got it to Ashz.

Any undistributed loot will be randomed at the next raid or sold
in the bazaar for the raid fund to cover miscellaneous coffins and
Peridots.

Next time I shall probably seek a delegate to collect peridots and
have Group Leaders distribute spells to reduce the potential for
mistakes.

THANKS ALL MUCH APPRECIATED !
