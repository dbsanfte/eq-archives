There indeed used to be a skill point cap on languages (5+5 per level), and you could only increase in languages at the rate of 1 point per hour per zone. The changes to the ruleset a few months back made language learning much easier--one of the few good things to come out of that at all, really.

As for how to learn languages, group with the person you wish to learn from. Have them switch to the language you want to learn (with the new UI, /lang # doesn't seem to work anymore--you have to right click your chat window and select your language from the menu), then have them spam you in /group speak. Easiest on them is to make a social hotkey with 5 lines of /group speak. Then, spam away! You'll see messages every now and then saying your language skills have improved, but you can't actually see the improvement until you zone.

Many guilds used to hold regular language faires to learn and teach languages. Check http://pub94.ezboard.com/bfirionavie to see if any are coming up.
