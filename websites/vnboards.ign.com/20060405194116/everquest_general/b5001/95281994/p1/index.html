There are 8 gems, and later in levels, you can use aaexp to buy one more.
"buff" spells, people usually put in their gem slot, then cast them, then change them out for another spell to use during fights.

The hotkey banks arent just for spells.
Most get used to using alt+# to cast. # representing the spell gem number. So if I have a heal in gem 1, I would instead hit Alt-1 to cast it. If you hold the Alt key, the numbers and name of the spell will appear next to them.
You can use the hotkey banks for other things.
For example,
I have a clicky instant invis item, I have that in slot 1, so I can just hit the 1 key to use it. Can put an item in that spot by holding the left click on it until a little box with that item in it appears on your cursor, then move it to that bank.

You can also do abilities or / commands on them, so you don't have to type them out each time you want to use them.
I have one key for pet commands.
One line says:
/pet hold
the next line will be
/pet back off
(ya, kinda redundant, but the second line actually clears memory)
So one one key, I can call of my pet, and clear his hate list.
Another one, I have combat abilities listed.
So for mine, bash and disarm are together. The lines are:
/doability 3
/doability 6
So whenever I hit it, it does both.
