assuming you're a chanter and have POP at 12 you can get illusion dark elf and run stein of moggok quests in 30-40 minutes - usually sell in my bazaar (VS) for 100 plat or so.

that's the best way to make money..I'd use it to get some affliction haste II and affliction extension II gear first(should be able to buy both for less than the 100 plat) - means a lot of your spells (root, charm, enthrall (16) (though not mez bizarrely), languid pace) just got 40% faster to cast and longer lasting! I assume that you did the fine cut diamond mask quest as soon as you got invis - if you didnt do it now (spell haste II, good buffs, and no more market for ruby silver veils:)

you can also learn basic jewelcrafting practically free to skill 34 and tout around +2 wis and +2 int rings for 4-6 plat each (they cost 1-1.5 to make) - I would make maybe 15-20 plat most times I zoned doing this

at 16 if you want to spent 300 plat on the jumpt to electrum you can do the same with +2 wis earrings and electrum jade amulets (+2 ac + 10 hp +10 mana)(good sellers) for maybe 30- 40plat/zone..

as a chanter you should group for xp and crowd control practice not cash at 12....to be honest so few groups have any clue that I find the best xp is gained solo - I just pull camps on my own with my pet and soothe -if my pet dies it's a good opportunity to practice charm soloing to clear camp and always keep gate memmed in case it gets hairy !
