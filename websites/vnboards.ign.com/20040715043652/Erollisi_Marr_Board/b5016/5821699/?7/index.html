Soulmate

Gamthor, Cleric of Erollisi Marr


Sit, sit and rest a while my friends for I've a tale that even I can not believe at times.

Long ago I was a Rogue, a Cad, a Pirate, a Bandit, a dock crawler who would prey on the weak and
helpless with no regard for their life. Many a bar fight have I been in both giving and taking
blood. I have slept with Shadow Knights, Ogres and Trolls ... to just name a few. My life was
meaningless and I intended to end it.

You look at me with your eyes wide and stare at my many scars and eye-patch and wonder how I have
now come to this, a simple cleric roaming the areas. It is simple my friend, she told me to.

Who you ask? that I can not answer...

I was in Halas one day, drinking and chasing the wrong type
of women when the biggest barbarian I've ever seen entered the bar. He was refined in the purest
furs to come out of Everfrost and I had to have them for my journey and the gold it would fetch
on the open market. I struck up a friendly conversation with this giant and began to ply him
with many pints of ale. My plan was to get him drunk and just "borrow" his furs, and I skillfully did just that.

I left the area in all haste giddy with glee at my new found fortune. I escaped to Everfrost where
I built a cozy fire and settled in for the night, with my ales to keep me company. Drunk was
I dreaming of the riches I would soon have, and to squander in the establishments that catered
to my kind. I must have dozed off for the next thing I knew I was floating in the air, with the
help of the barbarian I robbed, whose hand was firmly grasped around my neck. I kicked and squirmed
trying to escape but to no avail. I could see the fire in his eyes and knew then that my life
would end shortly, and did not care. He shook me like a rag doll and the world started to fade away into blackness.
The last thing I recalled was my body being slammed into a hole in the river where the coldness quickly
enveloped me.

I do not recall ever waking up! I opened my eye and I was in a mist, a fog you may say, and I was
cold. No, not cold but frozen. It seems I was entombed in a block of ice, cast along the shore of
the river. It is then that I first saw her.

It looked to me then that she was tending a small fire, but my vision was blurred trying to see
through the ice that surrounded me. I must have made a sound for she quickly turned toward me
and said "I have finally found you my love, but you disappoint me". I have never seen this woman
before, and actually still have not.

She continued on to say "For many seasons I have searched the lands for you, for I have seen you
many times in my dreams". that she has consulted with many a shaman, wizards,
and dream tellers and all have given her the same answer. The answer to who was this man in
her dreams was that I was her "Soulmate", the one who she needed to join with in order obtain the highest
levels of joy in a mortal life.

Soulmate, I have heard the term before but only in passing and by starry eyed lovers. It used to make me
sick to hear such drivel.

I was still entombed in the ice and could not reply but I could feel her love burning through the
ice... and then she bent over my form and kissed me. The world exploded into a rainbow of colors,
my heart melted, the ice around me started to fall apart and just as quickly she disappeared.

I crawled to my knees and shouted out "Who are you?", "Where are you going?", "Do not leave me!".
The only reply was a whisper upon the wind, it was her for I recognized the voice. She said "I am your
Solute, I must attend to others in the world who are suffering and will continue to do so for the
rest of my life, I must leave you until you can prove that you are worthy of me and that we can
travel together bringing others happiness". "Repent your ways my love she cried and hurry for I yearn
to hold you in my arms and soar with ecstasy at your touch, repent..... become worthy."

So my friends, I am a cleric. I give all I have to others and only keep that which will sustain life
for myself. I travel the lands healing and crushing evil where ever I can and I pray that each
action brings me closer to my Soulmate.

I have run across many who have claimed to be my Soulmate but those tales are for another time.

So when you see me sitting by a fire, drag a dwarf over and brew some ale....and we will talk.
