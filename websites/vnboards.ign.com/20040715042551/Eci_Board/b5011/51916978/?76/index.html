Level 55 cleric, 255 wis: 2629 mana
Level 61 cleric, 255 wis: 2881 mana
Level 65 cleric, 255 wis: 3049 mana

Assuming that the cleric at 55 and 61 has essentially the same gear (haha!), the 61 cleric has 252 mana more, the 65 420. I just discovered that Caster's Realm calculator hasn't been updated for the increased caps.

420 mana based on level alone is a fairly big deal. Add in the fact that the gear of your typical level 55 isn't as good as the gear of your typical level 61+, AA, and the wisdom cap goes up..

Complete Heal isn't the only heal. A cleric who uses only complete heal is a very bad cleric. Shamans and Druids do have their semi-complete heal, and that's at what.. 58? Divine Light (CLR 53) and Nature's Touch (DRU 60) are fairly similar. Nature's Touch heals a bit more, but is a bit slower and costs 50 more mana.

But definitely, if I was looking for healing in a tier 2 + group, I would pick a 60+ druid or shaman over a level 55 cleric. By level 58, I would probably go for the cleric, unless it's a 65 druid with a full spell book...
