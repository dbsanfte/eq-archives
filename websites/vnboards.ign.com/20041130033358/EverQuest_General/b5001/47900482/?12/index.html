Date Posted: 4/5/03 6:28am Subject: RE: I got 20k to blow on my bard, what should I get?
Get descent weapons and a haste items first. I'd recommend something in the line of:

Di'Zok Mace (or whatever you call them) which is 10/18 for main hand along with a Sionachie's Partizan (spelling?) which is 9/19 or something along those lines. They are both bard only and can be found pretty cheap.

Also get Silver Chitin Hand Wraps which give 22% haste and some nice stats for pretty cheap money. Get 6 AC 65 HP rings which are usually found for around 350pp in the bazaar. Those rings are great for that money!
Dusty Soriz Boots are pretty good (12 AC, 40 HP, 10 Sv Dis, 5 Sv Mag, 5 Sv Fire unless I'm mistaken) can be found for around 1k and are definitely worth the money. If you feel you lack AC and Dex you could consider Crystal Chitin Boots (20 AC, 7 Dex, 3 Str) but they are quite more expensive and not worth all those extra cash IMO.

As for range slot, Goranga Idol is a pretty nice item found for 200pp or so.

Barbed Ringmail BP, Engraved Royal Velium Greaves (or BP), Crystal Chitin Armplates, and Orc Fang Earrings are all pretty nice and cheap. Silver Chitin Wristbands are GREAT (will do great until lvl 60, easily) but their price differ a lot depending on the server. If you can spare the money they are a good buy, anyway.

Regarding instruments it's not really my thing, but Selo's Drums of the March can usually be found pretty cheap nowadays and that's a -great- drum, if you can spare the money.

First off don't get a Symphonic Saber. It's a crap weapon with a crap proc. Get a Troubadour's Mace. It's a nice weapon with a 1.8 brass mod on it. I know it says recommended level 50 but you can still use it. Drums of the beast or Selo's drums of the march are great pick ups. If you can't find either one of those get a Walrus skin drum instead.

When it comes to armor try and find AC,HP and DEX armor. At lower levels you'll need it to help raise your song skills faster. When you get into the 50's try and get CHA armor for when you have to mezz and charm.

You should stop off at EQDiva. They have alot of good info on Bards.
