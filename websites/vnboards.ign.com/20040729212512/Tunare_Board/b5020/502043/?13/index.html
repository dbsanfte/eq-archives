We didnt wait for a masively large guild to get enough power to go do Dragons/Planes by itself and instead organized all the high level people that we had and did it that way. We have also CONTINUED to do this bringing in a steady flux of new people (like Spicie yesterday from the post below) and not limiting things to a guild-only event. The fact that someone like me can survive without being IN a guild is proof of that.

Yes some from across the pacific did exploit to get levels fast but so did people on THIS side, its more a question of the language barrier and quite frankly since my chinese and japanese is a hell of alot worse than any of the english that our asian friends use I'm more than willing to cut them some slack.

Ammount of high level suits of armor. When you look at our event screenshots you see people full or nearly full Plane armor suits, you see mages in cryosilk or Plane robes or at least Shiny Metallic ones except for freaks like Rellik. When you see screenshots from other servers everyone is wearing "newbicite". Do all the people who get plane armor on other servers immediately retire and never play again? Do they hog the armor even when they cant use it? I don't know why but for some reason we always look beter dressed than they do with the exception of certain uber guilds...

We have long well thought discussions/arguments on these messageboards about various topics and rarely hold "secrets" from each other and instead when we find a new strategy/quest we share with all. Look at the ALL the other boards and you'll see that such information is regarded as top secret.

So what's up with us?

 Date Posted: 11/22/99 5:03am Subject: I'm so very happy with Tunare
I've played on many servers, I've run one guild on Tunare and been a member of The Outriders of Karana on Veeshan (though I don't get to play there very much), and just from my own experience, Tunare is so very pleasant a place to play.

I've had people I played with on other servers move to Tunare and tell me, filled with honest amazement, that Tunare has some of the nicest people! They started as newbies and there were total strangers helping them almost as soon as they entered the game.

We have our share of spats and the like (and personally, I love the arguments on this board and in the game, not only does it keep the relationships aired out among people, it's like watching a soap opera sometimes which is fun), but people here are always forgiving each other. Like Qhue was pointing out, we don't have any super-guild that dominates the server and dictates how things are done and who gets to do what and when, so everyone gets an opportunity to do the big stuff.

I've yet to find a better server than Tunare for just overall good play environment.
