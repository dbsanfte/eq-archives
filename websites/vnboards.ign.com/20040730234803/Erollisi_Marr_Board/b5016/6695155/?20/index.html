Well I could really care less if people twinked or not if it did not affect me. If the population on any one server of npc's were in the millions then the twinkimg of a thousand or 2 characters would not really matter. Due to the fact that the npc's are not that numerous and they do not give you fair prices for items sold twinking creats an economic delema. Certain classes and characters are loosing aspects of the game because of twinking. Any type of craft skill is loosing its flair especially in its earlier stages because the economy does not have even import/outport, production/ consumption elements. Since twinking is becoming more and more wide spread, there are more and more higher end items coming in, cheapening the over all economy. Basically it is supply and demand. With twinking you have more items coming in creating less of a demand for the lower end items. Basically if you have a highend character and you do not use him to twink any one then his items are being conumed by him and the balance is maintained. If you are twinking then you are undercutting the natural economy for your own good. This creats and influx of items that are not being consumed. It really is basic economics. Now I do not mind twinking because I have highlevel characters that are not in my opinion currently effected by this because the influx has not grown to that level yet. But eventually it will. Basically with twinking you have more higher level items coming in destroying the balance. It does effect every one due to the fact that the game technology is not growing at sufficient rate to keep brining new products into the eq auction marketplace. All those people who twink now that seems fine, I am not here to talk about insecurities or power gaming or being able to take down something that the game was not designed for you to do. In fact I am guilty of twinking. I do it as little as possible and basically I do it in order to keep up with the jones. But basic economics will tell you that this will dilute the economy.
