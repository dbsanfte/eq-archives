Leah, to say MBing Sev for cash has destroyed your dream sounds erroneous to me. (Again, I feel it is best to give the scale to someone who needs it rather than sell it to them.) If your dream was to some day take him down yourself, then your chance has been reduced. If your dream was to some day own your epic, then your statement is wrong. Regardless, prior to MBing, the uber guilds were almost always the ones taking down Sev. At first, they were the only ones able to do it. Later, they simply were the fastest. This is because they had the numbers online and also because they needed fewer members to reach Sev to take him down. As long as I have been aware of Sev, it has always been a foot race to get to him with the numbers to take him down.

With MB wizzies on the prowl, mobs like Sev don't are generally killed even quicker than they were prior to MB. The sooner Sev dies, the sooner he respawns. Since Sev's life span has been reduced, there will now be more green scales in the game than there would be otherwise.

Most MBing wizzies have someone in their guild who needs the scale. Most of their guilds frown heavily on their selling it to non-guildmates or selling it to guild-mates. I am after the green scale to give to a guildmate of mine.

Also, because it only takes 5 wizzies to kill Sev, it does not require an uber guild to kill him. If 5 smaller guilds each get one MBing wizzy to form an alliance, you can then setup a rotation to do this yourself. Suddenly, the lesser guilds have just as much a chance to get there as the uber guilds. All it takes is a little cooperation between guilds.

Another topic you mentioned was your gear. If your guild is not capable of raiding, there are plenty of raids you can join. Public Kael raids are being led at least once a week. Public ToV HoT raids are on Monday nights. Ashorty leads many raids against some of the more uber targets and you do not have to belong to an uber guild to join his raids. To find out more about Ashorty and his raiding requirements, go to:
http://ashorty.amberorder.com

Ashorty raids are about fun and loot. You do not even have to raid frequently to receive some benefit. You just have to attend the right raids, or save your raid points to use on that one item. It may take time and it may not.
