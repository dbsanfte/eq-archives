There really isn't much to talk about EQ other than :

1. Progression (character/guild)
2. Balance issues (usually just ranting)
3. Plain Bitching about something you don't like (people/guilds/playstyle/life in general/blah)

There are the few exceptions of a good discussion that is EQ related, but the game has been out so long now that most have been already discussed.

I find this board more of a social gathering with a few EQ statements here and there. It's quite interesting to read.

If you really want to find a good active board with posts every 30 seconds to 1 minute, www.genmay.net. Over 15,000 registered users, 9,000 active. That's what I read, some funny stuff to read and there are lots of EQ players there in the game section, just not in the same server.
