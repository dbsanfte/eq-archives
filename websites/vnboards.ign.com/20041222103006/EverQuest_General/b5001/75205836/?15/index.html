Date Posted: 9/14 5:41pm Subject: RE: How much Platinum should an untwinked newbie have around level 7??
Think RATS! Little ones, big ones, and in-betweens. In it's never-ending quest to make the game easier, SOE long ago began hanging 'Plague Rat Tails' on all the rats in Steamfont, and on various rats in other newbie areas. These sell for nearly 2 plat each to vendors. New gnomes have it easy in Steamfont, but anyone can go there and kill level 2 'young plague rats' which, I would estimate, drop a tail every 5 kills. I've started several gnomes in the last year or so (another alt-aholic here), and every one has had at least 100 plat in the bank at level 10, with all spells purchased. By comparison, my first couple of chars were lucky to be able to afford half of their lvl 8 or 9 spells.

The best spot for rat hunting in Steam is around the hill to the right as you come out of Ak'anon. A little train of ypr's scoots along the wall every ten mins or so.

As for the equipment you see other newbies wearing, it's probably the newbie quest gear (unless they're twinked) - another tremendous benefit of fairly recent origin, especially on new servers where low end items are scarce and/or ridiculously expensive. Check out allakhazam.com for info on all of them. Most involve collecting items from your char's area, and converting them with a pattern or mold to a piece of armor. You can generally get all or most of the set by level 10-12 or so. This is not bad stuff at all - I have chars still using some of their newbie gear at lvl 30 or better. It's certainly better than nothing, and the price is right.

Date Posted: 9/15 12:11am Subject: RE: How much Platinum should an untwinked newbie have around level 7?? - Date Edited: 9/15 12:13am (1 edits total) Edited By: StoneySilence
I can take a character killing nothing but rats from lvl 1 to 5 (when rats become trivial and no longer drop the tails) and get about 50-75pp by lvl 5. Best place for rats is in the newbie zone for Paineel. Rats will spawn by the dozens once you start killing them. If you get the spawn rate going and killing nothing but rats you can almost literally coat the whole floor of the zone with rat bodies. I have seen up around 12-20 rats up at a time. It's isn't the fastest way to level but the money is really good.

After you hit lvl 5 go to East Commons and kill wolves, bears and other stuff for their furs till your reach about lvl 8 or 9. Then go to Butcherblock and hutn Goblins (the green colored ones) at the camps only and they drop blood and heads worth 4pp each. But the camps usually have 3-4 goblins per camp and they all aggro if you attack one so you need to be strong enough you can take them all out. You can do that till about lvl 12-13. It is slow because the spawn rate is not that fast but the money is real good.

At 13 or so go to Kurns tower and camp skeletons. Good exp plus lots of bonechips which sell for 3-5g each in bazaar. That will be a good place till about 18-20 then go outside to the base of the tower and camp the mobs out there till 22 or so.

At around lvl 22-25 go to Upper Guk and camp the spider rooms and get about 80 silks an hour which on FV used to sell for 3pp each in Bazaar. Plus there is a named there that drops armor and a shield that you can sell (not sure if they are no drop, I played on Firiona Vie so I didn't have to worry about that). The spiders all aggro in the room and run so if you don't have root and poison protection it can be deadly. So you have to fight up to 4 at a time and if one runs into another room then you will get those aggroed too.

At around 25 then head out to Karana to the Splitpaw and camp the gnolls for their Fine Steel weapons. You should be able to sell to the merchants in the Centaur village or to the Aviaks if you are a good race. This will be a good place till 30-32.

At the low 30's head off toward Gunthak on the beach and start collecting Waterlogged Rigging and Quils and sell them for 10pp each in bazaar. Also Zombie Skins and bone chips there. That is good till 38 or so then you move on into Dulak.

The pirates in Dulak drop some pretty expensive gems which can sell for up to 200ish pp each.
