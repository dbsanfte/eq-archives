Personally I wouldn't worry about getting into a guild until around level 40.When you achieve this level start looking for a guild that goes on naggy and vox raids alot.You can learn how to efficiently raid by doing those type of raids.Also this level of guilds can help you with alot of your epic encounters too.

It's possible that you may stay with that guild forever too.Ussually though many seek out the higher level game experience and move on to stronger guilds.

The above flamer is correct though in the fact the most guilds that shout throughout zones for apps ussually are not going to be that great of a guild.


To get into a good guild you need to try and group with members of the guild you are interested in and get a good reputation before applying.Once several are familiar with your name then you go through that guild's app process.

Presently it's very difficult to get accepted into any of the upper tier guilds unless you have developed close connections within the guild's membership.
