Tholuxe Paells Community Center (TPCC) Newsletter

Hail and well met!

Please allow me to introduce myself. I am Joliet, the designer and developer for TPCC, a server specific website. I am also an active player on Tholuxe Paells with a monk character.

My goal in publishing and developing the TPPC website is to provide a common place for all players to gather and communicate, find information specific to our server or Everquest, and in general give us an offline place we can call home.

The TPPC website is solely supported by me, and is advertising free. You will never see annoying pop-up boxes or banner ads on this site.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


TPPC Contents and General Information

The site went online on November 1st, and officially opened on November 14th. To date, we are getting hit over 1400 times a week and this number is increasing as we add more content to the site.

Some of the site’s features include the following:

Guild Listing Page
At this writing there are 28 guilds listed on this page, with links to your guild’s website and forums. There is also a link to a guild specific page, where the details on membership requirements, guild characteristics, and descriptions are displayed. We are going to add a search function to this page soon.

Message Boards
The forums included are General Topics, Hosted Events, Find A Pal or Group, Noobies, Advanced Players, Buy/Sell/Trade, Rants and Flames.

News and Updates
This is the frontpage of the website. Individuals and Guilds can submit news items to be posted here. Recently 3 guilds have had their newsworthy activities posted in the News & Updates. We are encouraging Guilds to submit their newsworthy activities to promote community wide awareness. We define a newsworthy activity as an event that players may have some interest in. These activities could include quest accomplishments, hosted raids, etc. We would like to see every guild submit a newsworthy item at least once a week. Individuals may also submit to this section.

Community Calendar
The Calendar is still under development and we are hoping it will be launched by this coming weekend. You can learn more about the Calendar by visiting this link: http://pub30.ezboard.com/ftholuxpaellspalsfrm1.showMessage?topicID=21.topic
We are looking for constructive feedback on the calendar and will incorporate value added suggestions.

Server Status
A live update of the server status is presented on the front page, with Verant’s scrolling information headlines displayed.

Featured Player of The Week
Originally this was put in place for players to submit the story behind their good fortune in acquiring a nice item. But we are now going to broaden this by asking folks to submit a candidate for player of the week by writing an interesting story about the player that includes some unusual or outstanding achievement. We are encouraging Guild Leaders to tell their members about this feature. It’s a great way to promote your guild as well!

CHAT
We have a very easy, simple to use chat application on the site powered by HearMe™. The application can be used as a TEXT window, or—if the user has a microphone, he can use voice to chat. The choice is yours. We are suggesting that you use the chat feature to hold meetings, contact each other outside of the game, use it when the EQ chat server is down, etc. We have included a forum where you can post a time to schedule a chat if you care to advertise for participation. Scheduling a chat session is not necessary however to use the chat application.

(Under construction)
Links to Maps, Where To Hunt, and Mob Locations are in the works. We hope this will be a valuable resource for everyone once it is completed.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Online Auctions

We have received several requests via email to include an Auction Page on the website.

The auction would be server specific on TPPC and trading would be with other items or plat, not real money. We are looking for feedback on the possibility of including an Auction Page on TPPC.

1. How do you feel about auctions for EQ equipment or items?

2. Would you use a website auction to trade your items more than you would a "shout" in the game?

3. Would you rather do a "saturday afternoon in game trade/buy/sell in the traditional Commons Tunnel than use an auction site here? would you use both?

You can post your comments on this topic here:

http://pub30.ezboard.com/ftholuxpaellspalsfrm1.showMessage?topicID=38.topic



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Community/Guild Participation – How you can help us grow

We need your help in promoting the TPPC website.

Word of mouth is the best advertising there is.
If you have a website, a link back to us would be appreciated.
Encourage your guild members to post on our forums and message board.
Send us or submit news!



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


In closing, I would like to thank all of you for your support and encouragement. It is much appreciated and your feedback is always welcomed!
