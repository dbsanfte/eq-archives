Well thanks to Vaam we were able to get directly to Isle 7 and in clearing that isle we were able to get three mages their crowns.

Banda
Potenthianne (aka) Lemagaport
Aeloea (sp??)

It was a huge "learn on the fly" experience for all of us, but we managed to get through. On to the future of me leading this public raid...

Learning on the fly is kind of what this whole thing has been for me from the beginning. And with that being said, the learning process has led me to the conclusion that I dont have the knowledge, or patience, or the time to lead public raids for a while. So I am stepping away from leading for now. I would really like to level myself up, learn from better raid leaders on how things are done and maybe get my necro on my second account up in some levels to help with this particular raid. My real life schedule allows me very little time for my own agenda and goals, and spending the day in Sky has hampered my fun in and out of this game.

So while I am stepping down for now, doesnt mean I am stepping away forever. I may be inspired to run this raid again when I am more experienced and the demand warrants it. Sky is tough and having fizzles and new raiders every week just takes its toll. Some time away from it will do me some good I think.

I do have one regret at this point and that is that the one person who really kind of helped push me to do this never got his Spiroc Wingblade from Isle 5. I apologize to all the people hoping for epic pieces from Sky, but most of all I apologize to Saeborne. I still owe you one.
