Gilanden -

EQ has changed a lot, but it's still one of the deepest and most rewarding MMOs on the market. However it's also almost impossible to break into the big-time at this point (you're SO far behind).

So it's a great game to get back into if you're looking for a fantastic RPG - but be aware at this point you'll most likely never be able to access most major end-game content. Don't let that bother you, though... there's so much content in the game at this you'll most likely never see it all regardless.

EQ2 is not really a sequel to Everquest. It's a totally different type of game. You might actually love EQ and hate EQ2, or visca versa. It's definitely NOT pointless to buy EQ because of EQ2. EQ will continue to exist and be supported for the forseable future - so don't worry about it. Imo EQ2 looks inferior.
