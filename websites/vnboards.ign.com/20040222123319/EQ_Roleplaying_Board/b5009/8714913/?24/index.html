Motes of dust dance in the warm, rustic sunlight of a large pane window. Just within a shadowed niche something only half seen through the stark contrast of shadow and light stirs. Adjusting his ample ebon cloak, battle worn and travel stained, Lladerek X`Lottl, proud knight of Innoruuk attempts to muster all the dignity one in his situation can muster. Propping his sore back upon the curve of the wine barrel behind him, he bunches up his signature cloak and places it gently behind his pounding head. Running long indigo fingers through his matted and disheveled locks he looks out upon the common room, ghostly azure eyes taking in his surroundings in a few short seconds and falling upon Lothannon, Gale, and Lenore. “This is really great. I’m still here, with all of you.” As the sound of his own voice reverberates off of his skull ten fold he regrets speaking, or even waking up at all. He spreads his cloak over his lithe form and lowers his head slowly producing a loud ‘bump’ as it accidentally hits the wall. Uttering a quick curse in Teir`Dal he readjusts himself and curls up, half bathed in light and half consumed in shadow.
