Roleplaying in no way mandates dialect nor does it mean you are RPing if you use it. I role play alot on my play server (outside of my guild) but dont speak any different than I would in RL.

People also tend to forget the game already has racial languages. The first thing I'd do in creating the RP server is make common the human racial language and instead of every race starting with it, give each race an additional starting language to their normal creation ones. One that makes sense for someone of their race to speak.

Like Trolls could speak ogre and vice versa. Human from Qeynos could speak Barbarian. Evil humans from Freeport could speak Teir'dal. Humans from Freeport and Qeynos could even speak different languages. More intelligent races like Eruds and High Elves could start with an extra language of their choosing perhaps and so forth.

Lack of a common language would immediately open up many cans of role-playing worms and actually make all these languages, that we never use, important skills in the game.
