With blades and bows
In the fading light,
Friends and allies
Together unite.
The undead to destroy
The Estate to make right.
Enemies beware,
The Dragons come tonight!

With hearts steadfast
Inside they go,
Face to face
With a deadly foe.
Some may die
And this they know.
But with Solus at their side,
The Dragons do go!

With banners held high
They shout out the cry,
And quickly learn
That they can bleed, and die.
But on they fight.
The spells they let fly.
Till none are left standing
But the Dragons on high!

With blades and bows
Into the rising sun,
Friend and allies
Their victory is sung.
The undead destroyed,
The morning has come.
All praise to the gods,
The Dragons have won!!
