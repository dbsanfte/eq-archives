Oh how to do this
Its been a long road. Vel's been tailoring since he was created some 4 months or so ago. While I've not been the most dedicated to tailoring, I have certainly tried quite a bit. Last night I succeeded in gaining a 101 tailoring skill and immediately moved to FP brewed up 80 of Ole Tumjims finest and purchased an additional 400 Honey Meads and proceded to get the entire zone drunk in honor of my success. Lasted over an hour I do believe, and was definately fun. BTW, don't try passing out drinks when blizted yourself, don't work too well
Also, I'd like to thank all of the 'Tavern' for helping me. Either it was making some armor for you, or you helping me out with some lil project of my own, you all helped keep me on this path.
With special thanx going straight out to Rathacker, without him, I'd probably still be working on cured silk (and failing!). He spent some of his game-time rounding up kitties or hunting spiders for silk rather than avoid the blasted HG's From the very begining when I was chasing down spiders in EC/WC so long ago, through to spiders in Everfrost, and finally onto the cats of EK, Rat's been there for me. Hell, he even mastered pottery so I could make some Wu's Gauntlets. I also hear he bullied an enchanter into buying a much needed spell for that project as well ;-)
I can't tell you how many times I wanted to give up on tailoring. It was taking so long, none of my ingredients could be purchased, frustration of failure, and watching hordes of other players master things like smithing/fletching/pottery/etc in a matter of a week(s) was also kinda discouraging. But you know what? On those days I felt the worst about my chosen profession... Came a grining Rat around the next bend, with a backpack full of something and cheering me on. So here's to you Rat, without you I wouldn't have gained this honor and 50 people would've gone sober last night!

Next up, Grand Mastery

First off don't buy silk, tis a money pit. Particularily if you are trying to LEARN and not profit.
Secondly OC is right, EK is a happy place for crag spiders. Approx 23rd level, lethal posion, typically drop 2-8 webs per. Avg. is about 3-4. Best place (and worst) for crag spiders is the mountain/hill in the middle of EK (next to the path) is covered with em, also has 1-2 HG's on it at any given moment as well. Crag spiders are also famed for the 'team-up' approach. Not to mention EK in general is just over-loaded with MOB's so resting is a bit of a pain in the ass (you get bothered every 30 seconds sitting down). So I would seriously recommend getting a partner to help. The extra set of eyes can come in handy.
