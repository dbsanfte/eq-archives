Hail all and well met!

This post will be all ooc: for ease of reading sake.

I wish to start a Guild on Tallon Zek. The basis of this guild will be to start a "Family" of characters to adventure with and make a name for.

If started... these Characters would have similar first names, the same last name, be in a "House" Guild of the same last name, and even have similar, if not the SAME faces.
There would be a family tree on our website and we could add "relations" any time later!

It would be a roleplaying guild - not nessesarily in a bad way. We would be a jolly, fun loving bunch amongst ourselves and our friends, but harbor no doubts about destroying our enemies.

Ideally what this would mean that anytime you get on, you could find a "family" group that would be realtively well balenced.

I took a look at the races that are available taking in account gods, classes, and stats.

I think that Gnomes would be the best choise, for they offer the best all around classes and choise of god. With a mixed amount of Mages, Wizards, Warriors, Rogues, Clerics, and Enchanters (or necromancers, if we decide on evil-ness) they would make a perfect "family." And all can be good OR evil, giving us that Choise.

As such, i went ahead and "secured" 3 batches of names, totalling 24 names on Tallon. Two male batches and a small female batch.
If the names Dallakin, Ballakin, Xallakin, Jallakin, Gallakin, Rallakin, Zalmara, Xalmara, Kalmara, Lalmara, Purliben, Hurliben, Furliben, Kurliben, Zurliben, Murliben, Curliben, Eurliben, Yurliben, Durliben, Turliben, Xurliben, Urliben, and Iurliben are not acceptable, we could scrap them and start over, but i have them all "reserved" with placeholder Gnomes.

This would be SO neat from a Roleplaying standpoint and something other players really talk about and recognize!

Decisions (like the one about what the last name will be) will be made in family meetings and such.

THE KIND OF PEOPLE I WILL LOOK FOR, would be:

-Roleplayers. Ones familiar how to make chitchat with other players without really breaking character. How to make threats and kill in character as well of course. It is PvP.

-FUN PEOPLE! I am a nice guy and i enjoy a nice crew! Laughs and jokes are MANDATORY! Especially for the Rogues worshipping Bristlebane!! hehe

-"Middle of the roaders" - I play 2-3 nights a week. I am not a POWERGAMER. But i don't waste large amounts of time on chitchat. This will not be a guild for people out to powerlevel to the 50's, and won't be for "Phat leewt" collectors either. We are here to adventure, enjoy each others company, build a RPing name for ourselves, and have FUN.

- Generosity a MUST. I know i will help equip everyone to the best of my abilities. I will help people complete quests. I will try to help everyone level together. No greed. We're family.

- And YES we will all start from go... new characters, BUT... With enough of us around, and at the same level, leveling should be quick and painless.

That is it for now. Sound interesting? Lemme know!

I will see if enough people are interested in this guild, and if they are... i will give up my other characters.

E-mail me at danostrowski@zyan.net!
Tell me when you play, your play style, what character class/name you are interested in and a little about yourself (age/sex/locale).

Thanks,
Dan Ostrowski
