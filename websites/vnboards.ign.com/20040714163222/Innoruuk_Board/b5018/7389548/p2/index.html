I knew I stopped reading these boards for a reason.

Since people can't stop flinging crap at each other, I've been asked by the non-flaming members of my guild to make a post, and also to ask the ones joining in the flaming to please give it a rest.

So, here's a post. Anyone who has questions afterward can ask me in game, as there's no way in hell I plan on returning and reading more of this trash.

Yes, "stuff" went down between LoD and Pantheon a week or two ago. I had intended to keep it private, but that's apparently no longer possible now that nonmembers of either guild have joined in.

The "stuff" from my perspective is as follows:


1) The 'spirit' of the guild summit was not, as it's been opined here, solely to "keep the planes from being ever-camped." There was also the small part about "ensuring all on the server had their fair shot at the planes" without having to rush in and grab things as they spawned.

Unless a person was at the first summit, I have no interest in debating this point with them.


2) The original summit states that there is one slot per plane per weekday. If a raid is 'over' the plane is not 'ffa until the next people's reservation starts.' That is called poaching.

All of the guilds agreed that the sixteen hours prior to someone else's scheduled raid were off-limits.

That is the rule that prevents a guild from going in four hours before another guild's reservation and wiping the plane clean.

That is also the rule that allows mobs time to spawn, so everyone gets a 'clean shot' at a plane, or as close to it as is possible.

Everyone agreed that they would not want to be in the position of zoning in and seeing no mobs. Therefore, we also agreed that we wouldn't poach from each other.


3) The letter of the new summit states that if you do not pre-register a list of > 6 names from another guild in a plane, they don't get to be there.


4) An officer of LoD and seven others violated both of the above rules by poaching a spawn of cazic-thule after a justifiers raid ended, within 16 hours of my guild's reservation. Justifiers' raid was over.

For a roster of who was in zone at the time, and to read who was calling the shots, please see http://pantheon.fnord.net/articles/fear_log.txt

8 LoD
6 Justifiers
10 Others

That's not a Justifiers raid.

A few hours before that, one of the "others" attempted to recruit me (soloing in Skyfire at the time) to come help poach CT from my own guild. (This is how I heard about what was going on in the first place.)


5) At that point, I exchanged tells with an LoD friend who was in fear at the time. She informed me that fear was a big mess, Justifiers' raid was over, more than half the zone was up, CT would be there the next day, and she was just going to bed.

I've heard it said that my reply to her was taken as an agreement that what they were doing was okay. The only thing I agreed with was a very tired wizard getting some deserved sleep, and getting out of a situation even she didn't want to be in.

Perhaps I should have been clearer. The only thing that I was "okay" with was "We're getting out of here, this place is a mess."

There are two problems with that argument.

The first problem is: If people cared about the fact that they were breaking the rules, they wouldn't have brought 8 in to clear the last half of the plane.

The second problem is that I doubt the accuracy of the statement "We could have done it." Quoted from the log:

[Thu Oct 05 07:55:26 2000] Silvera says out of character, 'we dont have the resources to randomly pick anyone' (to eat the first DT)

8) Later that day, Syd and I spoke in Pantheon's IRC channel. We agreed that something "wrong" occurred, and we'd figure out a way to take care of it without a big, nonsensical, public mess that would serve no one.

This solution would "likely" involve us getting the kill of the Wraith of the Shissar the next time LoD had a fear pop with CT up. There were no assurances beyond that at this time.


9) Four days later, LoD's next reservation for Fear came around. As luck would have it, there was a reset and repop that day. In my guild's IRC channel, I asked Syd two questions. 1) Once they've cleared past Dread, might I zone in one of our shaman to pop the iksar child (2 minute spawn).
