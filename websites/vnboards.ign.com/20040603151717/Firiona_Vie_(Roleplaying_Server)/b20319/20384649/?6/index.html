Me and my wife are starting back up after a few months off and plan on making FV our home. We are casual players (although I tend to play more). We are looking for a multi race guild and not a Val Shir only one. We prefer a good based guild.

We also enjoy guilds that has many couples playing as it is quite interesting for us to group and play with other couples although we do have fun playing with you singles adventurers as well;)

If anyone knows any guilds could you kindly drop us a link or something to their website? Its much more fun to have freinds and associates in the game to chat, hunt, and generally experience the game with.

On a note about RP, our idea of RP is to stay somewhat in character and have fun enjoying the flavor. We do not play in complete absorption of the role. We would like a guild that does permit OOC context within guildchat and keeps criticism to a minimum.

Its all about having fun to us and to go to the extreme so carry RP is just not fun to us. If we can't wish good will to a fellow guildmember that is sick IRL or that has good/bad news then that detracts from our enjoyment of the game. In normal chat we are likely to make boo-boos where we don't adhere to the strict RP guidelines of some people and we acknowledge and can live with that without losing any sleep So, if you happen to see us running about and we stop to talk that Ogre or Troll without calling them names or making rude gestures kindly shake your head sadly about our delusions, wave and carry on. hehe.

Thanks,
