Yes a Mithril is worth a Ykesha and a FBSS

Ykesha's are dropping like rain and their value is depreciating greatly.

Mithril 2HS has an Awesome Damage delay Ratio and is 7% faster then the FBSS.
21/40 with a 25% Haste effect is rather nice while the Sash has a Haste effect of only 18%.
So yes the Mithril is worth a good Sum - not as much as it once was but still rather nice. And no - there is no way in hell it is worth a manastone.
Manastones don't drop and I personally value them much higher then a Rubi BP - because any high level Druid or Shaman can toss a regen on you but NOONE give you more mana in the middle of a fight when you are out of mana and need just 1 more heal and the party will live... This and this alone makes manastone invaluable. (At least to a cleric)
