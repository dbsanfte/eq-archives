Today my dear husband, Vandyre and I decided to make some jewellry and bows, reinforced leather, and had a couple of magic weapons for newbie giveaways in our home of Kelethin. (ooc = we're only level 24 so good fortune is relative ).

Off Vandyre went to make some hickory bows and arrows, while I ran to buy fletching kits as prizes for archery users level 1 - 5. I made jewellry (albeit only silver and electrum), and sorted our packs.

Once prepared, I announced that we would be giving these items away for doing simple things, such as showing us bat wings, tapping us on the shoulder or simply hugging me. Now to be fair, there were a couple of people who were truly grateful for the little things we gave away, and to you, thank you. It was your attitude that made it fun.

To those who moaned and groaned about how shitty the items were or that you could get better on your own at level 2, go for it. I'm sorry you felt we wasted your time for such crappy items. *smirk*
