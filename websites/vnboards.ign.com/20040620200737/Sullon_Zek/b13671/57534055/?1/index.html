Running the Gauntlet on Sullon Zek (xp guide) –DE Cleric

Hey, came back to EQ from Shadowbane about a month and a half ago and started a SK (kharrach), decided to start a cleric (Saemael) a few weeks later, throught now that I had gone through the bulk of the levels I would write a mini guide to leveling up as seen from a Dark Elven Cleric’s (that’s EVIL for anyone reading new to the server) point of view, levels 1-52. Hopefully I havn’t forgotten too many of the specifics

general rule of thumb for finding groups – add EVERY coneven ally you see to friends, then roll down the list when you need a group, works almost every time. Also the larger midlevel guilds come in handy for your preraiding days.

second quick note- keep your defense max’d! defense is such a HUGE boon for clerics it’s incredible how many let it be left in the dust and suffer for it all the way up to their higher levels. Worst comes to worst find a friend with a low blue pet and let it duel you, pet’s WILL raise your defensive skills (dodge helps a bit, maxs very low tho)





1-10.

nek forest was the best I found, even with the red’s coming through every few minutes and the under6 bards training around you could respawn and be killing again for more xp gain than you could at most other locations.

Feerott worked excellently at the skeleton camps in the corner of the zone between RM and Ogguk. Say around 6-10, also if you were playing a necro or shadowknight it’s easy to stack up on bonechips.

FoB (pit)- 4-9 – Hard for me as a cleric solo at times but doable if you stay along the fringes, tons of pks come through here but binding in PoK at cab book and your only 50seconds from your corpse in the event of death.





10-14/15.
Kurns was awesome, again if your playing SK/Necro it’s a huge bonechip generator. The root/nuke combo for soloing will work now as well with our first undead nuke. Kurns was the fastest xp I could find 10-15 (while I wasn’t getting trained by FoH that is)

Could also do Nek Forest at this levelrange in the strip west of the Lavastorm zone, it isn’t as fast as Kurns and would probably slow down a lot further during 15 . Soloing is a snap here as well just pull from the fringes and watch for adds.





16-27
Paludal Caverns owns all other XP it is unreal how good it is, also there is just as much evil control as good control and usually you can go for a long track without worries because most everyone there is trying to get xp as quickly as possible before the next ToW/Hate/ER comes through and rolls them. The best spots were recondite bandits 1 and 2 near the SH zone and the ring which is towards the opposite side. PC slows way down at 27 and by 28/29 you basically can’t xp there.

OT- Overthere experience 16-22 is halfway decent but I wouldn’t recommend it heh, not when PC is available anyways. Also at this point if you don’t have a group OT is out of the question for practical leveling.

WW- Giant Fort was cool, where I picked up my first coin on SK, small chance at forest loops (or so I was told:p). The xp isn’t really that good except in the 18-23range but the pathing makes it easy to get singles inside the fort. As a result you have to compete with 30ish level BL/Necro’s trying to solo which can cause problems.






27-34ish

Dalnir- Entry levels are awesome if you have a full group, also it’s used fairly infrequently so pk’s don’t come through much (PoK has everyone lazy in that noone including the pk’s want to run that far) on the downside you basically have to form your group before you head out there because not that many stay in the zone to try for groups on a reguler basis.

LdoN- 20+ I was already past these levels when LdoN came out so I’ll just put a short note in here saying that I have seen fairly few low level ldon groups forming, not sure if there’s a reason for that. Generally I would advise against ldon especially during peak hours because there is probably a ToW group at the adventure camp just dying to grief green’s and then CC them for a few hours.

SolA- Awesome xp if you can get into a goblin group, say 25-34? (can’t remember when it started to slow down). Oger’s can’t go in past the very beginning which sucks for quite a few of the tanks out there who don’t have shrinking abilities.





35-42
SolA 32-42-again, this time on clockworks from the saferoom, with a decent or better group the experience here owns LdoN and the pks came fairly infrequently while I was there.

OT –35-44ish Skyfire ramp area – Solid xp and fairly easy to find groups, also within seconds of a pok bind. This was a favorite spot of mine despite the frequent pks

LdoN(same as above)






42-52
SolB- Kobolds are sweet 42-47 or so, after that the xp slows way down, bats are doable up to at least 50 but the xp isn’t worth it with all the pktrouble.

LdoN in a good group you can rock in LdoN, pk free once you get inside is worth the slower xp than some places imo

CoM – Not sure how low you can start leveling here, high thirties maybe? Anyways it’s sweet xp, decent gear farther in and what was even cooler was the high number of undead in some places means we can actually solo in here, with kei it was a snap with banish undead and high ac (mine was around 960 when I started and 1030 when the mobs got too low around 50, that’s with reso and armor up) Just watch out for the boss spawns while soloing, very hard to pull off (Dread Wraiths, Warlords, etc. they always drop some piece of at least decent gear, most of it valued 800pp-3k).

PoJ – According to Eum------, Krikket and Havvoc this is a goodie zone but I find groups here a lot, the zone setup allows for easy pulling to the zone to PoT, so there’s really no chance of you dying if your paying attention but newts/goods train a ton here, really slows stuff down.

PoN – Xp would be awesome but the mobs hit hard as hell for a group of under 52



Hope this helps!
