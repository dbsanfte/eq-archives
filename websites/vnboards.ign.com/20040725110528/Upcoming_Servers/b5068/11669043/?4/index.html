After reading the recent interview "Lum the Mad" had with Abashi, I can think of only one word to describe the new server. Hardcore.

The interview can be found at http://www.lumthemad.net/news/2004.php


Just to give you an idea of what I mean, here are some pieces of the interview:

"We’re going with full PvP. You can kill anyone at any level. A level 60 could kill a level 1 if he wanted to. . ."

"we’re getting rid of all the customer service rules associated with PvP. So training, rules against training, rules against bind point camping, zone camping, all of that will go away."

"Snaring the kiting druid, which right now could get you suspended or worse, will be a very legal strategy."

"There will be experience loss when dying to someone plus or minus 5 levels."

This server will not be for the faint of heart. It's gonna make Rallos Zek look like a regular blue server. There will be 3 teams (good, neutral, and evil) depending on your deity. You won't be able to cast any beneficial spells on other teams and they are discussing the possibilty of not allowing you to group with other teams, so you can forget xteaming.

Doesn't look like we will have to wait much longer for this server to come out. According to Abashi, "It will probably come out in two to three weeks, maybe a bit longer."

And if you think this interview is bogus, check out the comment made by Alan here:

http://boards.station.sony.com/ubb/everquest/Forum1/HTML/000150.html

Ever since I started EQ I've been waiting for a server like this one. It's the answer to my prayers, good job VI.
