Sup MadAssassin,

Yes EverQuest can be played with either AGP or PCI slots. I play on a PCI slot on my old computer and I still use it all the time. Works great. Though if your using an onboard video card you might want to check to see the performance stats on it. If you do decide to upgrade your video card, PCI's are around $60 and that inlclude things like the GeForce 4 MX 4000 PCI, and the ATI Radeon 9200 SE PCI which are great vid cards for games like EverQuest. But if your looking for more high end, just shop around =).

Just check on the card maker's website and be sure the card is directX 9 compatible. EQ updated the graphics engine to that standard several months ago and that will disable alot of the older PCI cards.

The Geforce 4 will be fine, and I am "pretty" sure Radeon 9200 series will work with latest drivers. Didn't Radeon 9700 come out in a PCI format? That would probably be best bet for performance in PCI if you can still find those out there.
