I am hosting a Raid on Lady Vox in Permafrost. I will be playing a guild mule druid and running the raid as a 52 Druid.

ATTENTION PALADINS ABOVE LEVEL 52! Since this is an impromtu raid, I have decided to add a twist to the epic loots. Please Read Epic Loot rules below for the Torn, Frost Covered Book.

Raid Leader: Toado aka Palamus
Asst RL: Axeminster

WHERE: Meeting place in the Kings Room in Permafrost.

LEVEL REQUIREMENT: Only levels 47 to 52 will be accepted except in extreme cases of class rarity, when we MAY take the following down to level 44: Enchanters, Magicians, Clerics, Shamans and Druids. We have had many attending lately that are level 45 - ***please understand that the RLs have the right to refuse your attendance if necessary.***

MINIMUM CLASSES REQUIRED:

We will Need at a minimum:

3 Clerics
2 Enchanters or 6-8 Paladins (for Manasieve)
4 Nukers
16 Melee

Paladins -
If you are a Paladin of the appropriate level attending this raid, DON'T COME to this raid without the following spells:

o Resist Disease
o Resist Poison
o Thunder of Karana - Confirmed it works on Vox to Manasieve

RESISTS: Everyone attending should have 90 (not 75 anymore)Magic Resistance and 100 Cold Resistance **unbuffed**. Vox can only attack one person at a time, her real damage comes from the 500 point AoE and not so much her melee. If you do not have the resist gear please don't come.

COMMUNICATIONS:
Raid Channel: VoxRaid
Pullers and Bards: Voxpull
RL, GLs, Buffers: VoxOps (2)
Paladins: Pallychat (3)
Bards: Voxbard
Warriors: Voxwar

Speak in the VoxRaid Channel ONLY. RAID SPEAK IS FOR THE RAID LEADERSHIP to use and it would be best if your /rs colors are brighter than your /1 chat channel colors. All your instructions will come from Raid Speak. Don't clutter it up with chat please.

THINGS TO KNOW: READ THIS PLEASE - DO NOT ASK THE RLs ABOUT IT DURING THE RAID!!

LARGE RACES must be able to SHRINK themselves. Please bring with you a shrink potion, as we work in tight quarters sometimes.

THE FOLLOWING RAIDING RULES APPLY:

1. Please follow directions given in Raid Speak by MT, raid leadership, and your friendly buffers.

2. When in doubt about anything be it buffing, loot policies, etc. ask your GROUP LEADER first. If they do not have your answer they will get it for you. Just remember group chat is your friend. We will mix the groups so that you have people along with you who have dragon raiding experience

3. Do Not Send Tells to the MT/PULL TEAM/RAID LEADER or ASST RAID LEADER. By minimizing tells to raid leaders things will run much more smoothly and quicker.

4. Speak in the VoxRaid Channel ONLY. RAID SPEAK IS FOR THE RAID LEADERSHIP to use. All your instructions will come from Raid Speak. Don't clutter it up with chat.

RAID COSTS: To alleviate some of the raid costs any donations of plat or peridots would be greatly appreciated. There is no standard number, just if you can afford to bring a few, it would be helpful.

BUFFING: Buffing is such a wonderful thing in the land of Norrath. Many classes are blessed with the ability to buff entire groups at one time. There is something that allows you to buff an entire group other than your own **without being in that other group.** It is called /targetgroupbuff. When you need to buff a group different than your own with a group spell, simply type "/tgb on"... you will then be allowed to cast on groups. USE THIS TOOL !!!

LOOT RULES:
EPIC LOOTS:

Paladins - Unless I get the last Vox Lists, the Frosted Book will be handled in this manner. I will go by completion stages of your epic first then if their is a tie, I will random. I hate doing this so, before I random a Torn, Frosted Book to the Paladins in attendance I WILL EXPECT TO SEE in Raid Channel a link of your Ghoulbane and Soulfire and/or Torn, Burnt Book. Precedence is such as follows in order:
1. Ghoulbane, Soulfire, Torn Burnt Book
2. Torn, Burnt Book
3. Ghoulbane, Soulfire
4. Level 53 or Above /randomed
So, as you can see, if no Paladin 47-52 shows with atleast both his Ghoulbane and Soulfire a Level 53 and above may random for it if more than 2 are present. If only 1 Level 53+ is present, I will loot the book myself and MQ it as directed by the next official Vox Leader. Toado has the faction. That means a 53 can join raid channel and see if another 53+ is present before hauling his butt to Perma for nothing but I expect you to be there and assist with the raid. If there are only KoHB 53+ Paladins Present I will loot the book and wait for the next Vox RL to tell me who gets it or settle the issue publicly. Don't come when we are at Buff Hall expecting to get an epic drop 53+ Paladins cannot roll on anything else. Sorry

Bards - If no list is posted, Bards will settle this issue in their appropriate chatchannel, choose a representative that will report to me the bard who should get the scale.

Warriors - If no list is posted, Warriors will settle this issue in their appropriate chatchannel before the raid starts, choose a representative that will report to me the bard who should get the item.

Casters - The White Hide will be randomed to all casters.

If an item is only usable by one class, it will be randomed to those of the appropriate class.

Except for epic or no drop pieces, loot that drops in multiples will have the second randomed to everyone on the raid who has not yet won it, even if it is normally restricted by us to certain classes. Also, if there is nobody of the restricted class in need, a piece of loot will be randomed to all who can use.

It may seem silly to even have to say this, but believe it or not the question comes up. If you are awarded an epic piece, you may not roll on any other loot. The epic pieces will be given out before anything is rolled on.

Epic Lists Will Be Posted Separately if located and confirmed.

Hope to see you there!
