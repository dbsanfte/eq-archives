I'd go to Stromm if I were you. When I started on Stromm (I'd been playing on other servers for years), I was one of the first smiths (Duane) and joined a trades guild (Denmother's Children). At one point, one guy desperate for banded armor on the first day (I was already making banded at that time and was only one of three capable of doing it successfully every time) came to me and offered me an interesting deal. He gave me 10k on Torv for a full suit of banded. Already having made a few k from smithing (which somehow is all gone these days), I did it. On Torv, I had a little druid that was only level 2, so I went out and bought everything that's mid to top of the line for my little druid, and I went to play it. But then it started to dawn on me that I was one of only very few in all the noobie areas, and when I wasn't it was with completely twinked people with 65th level characters powerleveling them.

I haven't been back to Torv since. I'm back on Stromm struggling through the low levels now instead (I took six or seven months off soon after Stromm was released to play Star Wars Galaxies before dumping that game and coming back a week or so ago).

Stromm is so much more fun, even if it's because more newbies show up there so I can at least feel good about offering information instead of dealing with some poor guy who keeps killing himself due to lack of knowledge but won't take help because his "main" is a 65th level necro.
