Xasojhet left behind the stink of Odus. Her visit there had been succinct. Her journey was purposeful. She had no desire to wander the lands of the stinking rivel merely for amusement, but rather to obtain knowledge that was power. As much as she loathed the mud colored peanut headed Erudites, she still recongized their grasp of the arcane. Evokers with the most potent intellect to ever grace Norrath sprang from these people. She would be a fool to not investigate. It would require her being civil to some of them unfortunately, but there was compensation for such irritation.

The Heretics were the practitioners of the high art of necromancy. Crushing a few of them in waves of elemental fury would gain the tolerance and even trust of the learned but hopelessly gullible arcane masters of Erud. Compared to Xasojhet's sinister devouring mind they could be regarded as such. That which could not be outright annihilated could be subsumed through treachery. Her indigo lips twisted in a tainted smile. It had unfolded in such a manner.

The Aracane masters of Erud were more than willing to accept her as a like minded scholar practicing thier art after she had viciously punished a number of Heretics. The wasted charred husks of their bodies had been incense of pure ecstacy to her. It took all of her will to regulate her magic force. Her deep and delicious desire was to have a complete raw unleashing of all her power to consume them with such force and hatred as to only leave a blackened ruined crater where they once stood. She however needed proof from her game, and in addition an Evoker that was not judicious was foolish, and short lived. She consumed them with measured fury and yet still some satisfaction.

She had studied at the great libraries in Erud, at the councils of their highest Masters of Arcane schools her people called 'spurned'. She admitted to herself not only had it been a pleasure to punish Daydwellers, it had been a pleasure to punish Necromancers. She had no love for the members of the Dead that had placed the Tower Arcane in such a lower status to their own. She had gathered a deeper understanding of the so called outcast inferior magics among the Erudites. She would use the power to further her own, raise up her house, and reclaim strength for the Tower arcane to throw off this title of taint.

What was it that lead to the current state of affairs? What bid was made and failed? How was her Grandfather involved? All of these questions plagued her as she made her way through the North Karana night. She had decided on a whim to investigate some of the land on her way back to Neriak. The sudden sound, a shuffling gait grew rapidly behind her. Did she hear the maniacal laugh of a boneslave?

Whirling about she saw indeed her intuition was correct. Quickly this wicked slave closed with her. Behind him stood a hooded black robed figure. The Necromancer was slight, and the lilt in his tongue as words of magic poured from his mouth gave away his identity. He was no Erudite seeking revenge. Xasojhet understood they would not wish to see her live. She understood what a real threat she would be if her full potential was realized. Mote it be. Her arms opened wide and she stared down her regal nose at her attackers. Suddenly the night air was full of raw electric energy ripping through its velvet veil. The battle had begun...
