Get to the southern frozen area asap.
Buy the top rate armor.
Grab the spell bone fiends.
Run to the next mission.
Get a group with level 20s and pass it.
Get a group to copperhammer mines.
Get the spell Veratas Sacrifice to heal your minions.
Head back to the regular story line and kick some serious ass with a superier death rune and death helm making huge bone fiends and healing them.

I did this and beat the game in about 2 days and made a ton of money.

Armor depends on your playstyle. Currently I am doing every mish that gives me 1000 exp + or a skill unlock. I have fiends and the sacriafice. Currently I have over 50K in gold and no need to worry about drops for armor and such. I can easily solo regular mish's and only need a group of 3 for the co-op's. I just need them to help me kill 3-4 then it gets ugly for the mobs quickly. Generally (unless it is a mish full of skellies and ghosts) I can pull up about 15 fiends and not worying about anything stopping me.

Granted starting out is a real bugger trying to get 3 corpses is difficult, but generally there is some "help" about that will give you a hand.
