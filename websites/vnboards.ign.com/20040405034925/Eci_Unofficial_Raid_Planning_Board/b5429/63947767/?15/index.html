Congrats Baolino, it was a fun way to spend a little time and I'm glad to see another SSS finished.

As for dueling out a porter, a very long time ago (about the time of Kunark launch) I used to lead the Sunday public hate raids. Well at that time, translocates weren't in game yet, so to get people out of the zone we always dueled out the porters. Here is the proceedure:

Create a balanced "stay behind" group to guard the port-out site. These are the last people to leave, and thier job is camp defense until all are out. Make sure this team has a cleric and an enchanter.

Organize melee into portout groups of 5

1) Kill the porter in a duel (laugh as the rogues and monks fight over who gets to kill the druid)
2) Start a 10 minute timer (VERY IMPORTANT)
3) Res the porter
4) Cast clarity on porter, KEI is unecessary. They only need to med just enough time to get porting mana
5) Porter loots all but one item from corpse
6) Porter meds and mems a port (ALL MELEE GO SAME PLACE TO SAVE TIME)
7) Melee group of 5 invites porter
8) Once porter has enough mana they port out
9) As soon as porter lands in destination, he calls for a res
10) Porter is resed a second time
11) Porter loots last item

Repeat as necessary to get everyone out. One important note however about this method:

DUELED CORPSES ARE ONLY RESABLE FOR 10 MINUTES! The timer person is responsible for keeping track of time and keeping the process moving along. An alternative is for the porter to take an XP death and get dragged back to camp by a rogue. PVE death corpses are resable for 3 hours.

Here are some general times for about how long it takes:

00:00 death
00:30 res
04:00 port out
06:00 res back

Try to keep to this schedule, and you'll have plenty of time to spare. The only risk factor is server down or extended LD.

Well, there is plenty of room on the server for a non-LP UDB raid, but *** /killtrak is remaining LP ***. If anyone would like to run non-LP raids in a different channel, please feel free to do so with my blessings. Here are my reasons:

1. Eveyone wants to see that "Singing Short Sword" in the raid chat at the end of the raid. For many, that's their ONLY reward.

2. You have to have the body of the mystical lute to hand in to the UDB, and that requires many of the toughest pieces. There's not much difference from there to LP.

3. Bards who have come before on this raid and had to wait to get to the LP list -- I want them to come back and help, and they might feel slighted if later bards have an easier ride.

4. I inherited this raid from Avner, Songweever, and Aabana. They made it LP, I'm keeping it that way. People need to know that they can count on me and predict my behavior.

5. /incharacter it's fitting respect for the Kunark boss dragon that it be the last thing one does on an epic quest. Even though he's a steenky evil poison huffer, he is a DRAGON !

......Invoice (Braenar, say "What Doll")
