You can get perfect weapons with perfect mods from the collectors in the Crystal Desert. Here's some info I got from guildwarsguru.com:

Luven Underwood trades 5 minotaur horns for
• Bone staff (energy+10, cold dmg:11-22, req. 9 death magic)

Arliss Vaughn trades 5 iridescent griffin wings for
• Truncheon (cold dmg:11-22, req. 9 death magic)

Sir Pohl Sanbert trades dune borrow jaws for
• Grim cesta (energy+12, req. 9 death magic)

The staff has +20% faster casting and skill recharge chances for death magic skills, plus you can put 2 mods on it (a staff head and wrapping) to give it even more powers. I'm not sure what the mods on the truncheon and cesta are, but they will be max whatever they are.

Ok to clarify there are 2 types of necro weapon set ups

1. Is 2 handed weapon which means you get all your energy bonuses special abilities from the staff. You can modify these with heads plus wrappings.

2. Is a one handed weapon which allows you to wield a Grim cesta or cesta in your off hand. This method allows you to gain more energy than the previous but you are at the mercy of what your find as you cannot modify these items.

for example the truncheon I listed above was +5 combined with the collectors Grim Cestas of +12 energy for a total +17 to energy . You can get a higher lvl Truncheon that is equal to the collectors cestas so you can have +12 +12 total but you are now -2 energy regen which I find hard. Maybe higher soul reaping would alleviate that. These are from memory I havent been able to find their exact collector locations.
