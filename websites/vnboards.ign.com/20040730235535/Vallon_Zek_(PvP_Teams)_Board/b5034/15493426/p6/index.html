Good point J, I've come to realize that fact more then anything when it comes down to being branded. Look at posts like Alundria's. Never heard of her. She puts Vexare in her "FeF Guild" award. I'd say the majority of the people on this server would do the same simply because of the fact its 4-team. The problem with the name X-teaming is that it was born and eventually came to be associated hand in hand with the prophets and the tactics they employed to maintain server dominance for a while. Prophets = X-teaming. People still do that, regardless of what new guild it is or the intentions behind that. It's a losing battle to change the mindset of a server imo, but I can only hope through my own personal actions and those of my guild who are trying to change some of those biases, that we may actually change someones mind.

I don't have much hope for ever becoming a pure teamer again. Mainly due to that single fact that I've got a "Taint" on my name because of my guild association now. As long as I was a pure teamer, I never bought into the server standard of light vs dark. But you'll be damned sure I would pick the prophets to kill 1st over defiant any day. Why? Because of that stigma they had attached to them and there actions.

Again I think everything goes back to how this server has evolved. I agree, the Dark Team for me was back when Vllad, Kilara, Squiggles, and co. would lead multiguild efforts on things. Those people were honestly the backbone of the Dark movement. Like I mentioned in another post, eventually this dissolved more into Guild 1st, team second. Its a natural thing. You want to help out the people you associate with and become friends with the most. Its too bad that Verant introduced a new continent that was raid intensive and required levels, numbers, and man hours. How can you blame Defiant for anything? How can you really blame any large guild for that? They are doing just what Verant intended. Raid based/Large Guild events. The people who complain are the people who aren't doing those and would like to. Hell I'd love to do ToV. Am I going to get killed by Defiant or AO if I even get close to SG on? You better believe it. They're bloodhounds. But thats the nature of this server. We choose PvP knowing full well that zone control would be issues. Defiant/AO/Fated have the numbers and faction in some cases, to throw off anything the Dark team can heave towards them.

I applaud those that still have a belief in the dark team, keep on trying is all I have to say. But it will eventually come down to numbers and active player bases and you will realize that this is going to be the deciding factor in 90% of what you do at the upper level game. And in the end, I honestly don't think a single team (no hardcode comments please) at the present time has that kind of numbers to hold off against the combined forces of 3. I can see where our wonderfully anon groin scratcher can be frustrated, but if you want to blame any one thing, blame the fact the you created a character on a PvP sever and weren't ready to deal with all the reprecussions that came with it.

/rant off
