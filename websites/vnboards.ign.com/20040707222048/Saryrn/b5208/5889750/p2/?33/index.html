It's real simple. There is nothing wrong with the Ranger class. They Track, They Snare, the thorn, the heal a little, they dot and nuke a lil. They wear plate-like armor at higher levels, they dual wield, they double attack. etc. etc. etc. Many, many pluses to the class. 98% of the dungeons in this game require a snarer. Pretty much all of the require melee. The term 'tank' is overused, in my opinion. You have warriors, large race SK, Dorf Paladins, and Barbarian Rogues. Nothing else is close. And at high levels, the barb rogue falls off that list in a hurry.
Rangers are not tanks, rogues are not tanks, DE shadowknights, High elf paladins, etc. etc. are not the ones designed to take the dmg. You play a role, like other classes. You cannot do dungeons without a snarer. You cant hunt mobs efficiently (rares) in outdoor zones without a ranger (ask druids, it's not the same skill they got by a long shot). A great compliment to a large race SK or a warrior is a ranger. Rangers are a piece of the puzzle. Lie a SK, like a Paladin. Like a Rogue. Like a Shaman. Like a Druid. Like a necro. Like a Wizard. Like a Magician. etc. etc. etc. Enchanters are a must. Clerics are a must. Warriors are a must (for efficiency only, really). Snare is a must. Evac is nice. Secondary healing is nice. Lay on hands is nice. Harm Touch is nice. Very little is necessary, a lot is desired. Rangers are desired. I think part of Janx's Disillusionment is based upon being in FOH and spending every waking minute fighting big god mobs in dungeons and planes. Rangers eat the Death Touches, en masse. That's a proven fact. For that reason alone shouldn't hinder the overall gloss of the class. Of course, I have a level 45 rogue, and I can vouch they are a hella fun role to play.
IT's healthy to have multiple options. If you don't like a ranger, try out a new class. If you hate taunting off a ranger, don't group with one. If you hate healing a ranger, let him die (Here's a tip : that's even LESS mana efficient).
In all seriousness, MOBS have weird aggro lists. the original question was about taunt factor, and I've seen many, many mobs in my lifetime. They hate healing, they hate being debuffed (Hi snare!), and they abhorr people who use low delay attacks on them. Think of it this way. If one guy it clubbing you over the head once ever 2 minutes, that's annoying. If another guy is jabbing you with scissors every half a second, you are even more annoyed. Same philosophy. If they wanna do one thing to rangers, it shouldn't be more delay happy taunt weapons, it should be better ac and +hp armors. I've never hesitated to invite a ranger in my group, and never will in the future. I wont look for them before an enchanter or one of our thousand Ogre Warriors, but when I think 2nd or 3rd melee slot, or SNARE, by god I'll think of them and throw a /tell.
