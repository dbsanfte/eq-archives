Ldon at 28 is only going to net you 16-28 plat per adventure. That's what I've noticed. Not really good money but good exp and seen a lot of augments drop lately.

Besides the bb gobbos, I would suggest the aviaks in Lake Rathe (not sure of level though) and gnolls in South Karana around Splitpaw. They tend to drop a few plat each and fairly often have a fine steel weapon.

You could also do griffennes and griffawns in North Karana. Griffons should still be red to you so be wary. Watch out for The Silver Griffon and Grimfeather also.

East Karana for spiders to get silks to sell in bazaar.

Also, highly overlooked, the gorge of King Xxorb a.k.a. Beholder's Maze. The evil eye camp there is about a 7 minute spawn with two spawn points close to each other. And the occassional wanderer and third spot pop between the two. The named will probably be evenish to you. They have pets and tend to mez so be prepared. Tend to drop silvers to plats, random gem, parts and polished bone items for bazaar sales.

Hope that helps a bit.
