Mine was when I first started EQ. I made a Dark elf necro Named Metooandyou. It some how got past the filters(i can get just about any name i want past anyhow, so not like it was a big thing).

Well I didn't understand much about the game at the time. My sister said what do you want to be. I said a necro. She said ok, here is 3 pp go buy your spells.

Well a couple hours later she came back and showed me exactly where my guild was and such, so I could actually get those spells. I finally got outside to start "leveling" and I was stoked. She went to bed and let me go at it.

Well a few hours later she woke up and I was sitting on a newbie logg. She said what level are you, and I replied "level 2 ". She noticed that I was also grouped up at the time. Asked me what I had been killing to get level two. I told her I hadn't been killing anything, this nice guy came by and asked me to group and I just started getting exp.What I had been doing was working on hide.

Soon after the nice guy left my group, although, at the time I just thought he was logging or something, I never thought until a few months later, when I actually knew how to play EQ, that I figured I probally made him mad.
