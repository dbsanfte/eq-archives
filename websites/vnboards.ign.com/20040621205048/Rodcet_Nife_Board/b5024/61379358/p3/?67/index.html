I'd more or less agree with everything Glayde posted, other than AD/DK with a 10 compared to a AoD/AL/EL's 9 on RoK. Yeah, we didn't clear VP, but neither did anyone else -during- RoK. Up until SoV the AoD/AL/EL alliance definitely dominated the vast majority of RoK content. As for Velious, I'd say Ascent 9.9, AoD/AL 9.5, with most of the server firsts being split about 60/40 between the two groups (in favour of Ascent), and the only mobs seperating the two being AoD/AL with Sleeper and Ascent with Tunare and AoW. Pre-RoK, no one really came close to the sort of domination that AoD/AL had; we enjoyed a good ~2-3 months of content domination before anyone else even attempted the big mobs. Also, I'd like the effort of AL to not be undermined in the initial pre-RoK days; they were a key part of the alliance and for the first while were a good 30% of the manpower, with 90% of that manpower being invaluably powerful. It wasn't until close to RoK that AoD could really do all of the mobs on its own.

The better question would be who really cares these days, other than I guess the people posting on this thread
