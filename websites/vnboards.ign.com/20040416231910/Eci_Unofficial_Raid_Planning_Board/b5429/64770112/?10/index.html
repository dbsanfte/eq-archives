 		Date Posted: 2/23 5:15am Subject: Goodbye, Farewell, So Long...
It is with a heavy heart that I must write this post. For the past 4 years I have enjoyed the experience that we call EverQuest. However, events within the last week have now forced me to be put in the position of having to give up one of the most enjoyable hobbies I have had.

I will miss EverQuest very much. I will miss the friends that I have encountered. I will miss the journies, battles, victories and losses. I will miss the rewards of great victories with low numbers and I will miss the losses that come as well.

Some have said that EverQuest is addicting. Who knows? Maybe it is. Perchance we are all chasing the dangling carrot in the hopes that it will eventually be within our grasp. This perverbial carrot is not worth the heartache it has been causing the past few months.

I would like to thank some poeple before I take my leave. I would like to thank the person behind Rivan/Sling/Vogue. B, you are the one that got me here and for that I thank you. I would also like to thank Grishnor and Maddadh, the original and current guild leaders of Guardians of Order. It was there where I truely learned the game. I would like to thank Baaldok and Fleflah, without Na Koa I would never have completed one of the hardest quests I had ever attempted (warrior epic). I want to thank Shupansea and the rest of Ethereal Realms for my time there, I did many things there for the first time and truely enjoyed myself. I want to thank the current leadership of Final Evolution, Katur, Marymoon, Destraum, Kluvwen, Meliketrees and Ada, there is nothing I can say that could express my thanks for constantly listening and taking the time to do so.

Then there are friends that I will miss, Flottsom, Khuji, Anenil, Amethaul, Kukgar, Roane, and Angernath to name but a few.

A note to the rest of Final Evolution, work as one and there is nothing that you cannot accomplish. You have fine leaders, some of the best I have had the pleasure of following.

I wish you all good luck.

So for all you M*A*S*H fans I bid you the same words that entitled the final episode....

Goodbye, Farewell, So long.........

I remember when I first met you, Sir. I was a guest at a Kael raid you were leading. You were MT and when the ST went linkdead, you made me ST. When ST came back from LD awhile later, you kept me as your ST. I was a horrible ST that night, but you stuck through with me.

Next Kael raid and subsequent raids when you were leading and MT'ing, you made me your ST. Any doubt I had disappeared when a higher level guest showed up and offered to ST. I sent you a tell that I would not mind backing down since he is better equiped and higher level. Your reply sealed my loyalty and admiration, Sir. You said, "YOU are my ST. YOU, I can depend on."

The bulk of my best tank training came from being your ST, Sir. You taught me what to pay attention to, what to look for, how to look for it, and the endless many reasons why doing one thing over another would work better or not. You told me how it was without sugar-coating or formal flowery sayings. You are direct and simple and taught me what I needed to know in any given situation to make it work.

I will miss your mentorship and you greatly, Sir. I wish you well in your new journeys ahead. Please know, you shall remain my Main Assist.
