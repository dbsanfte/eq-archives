*Walking slowly down the stairs with tired look about her eyes and a smile on her lips she returns to the chair which she vacated what seems like eons ago. Gracefully folding her tall form into it, she stretches her legs before and looks about at the inhabitants.*

"The Lady Lenore is with us once again. The wine she drank was tainted, as you all must know by now, but twas only gone bad I believe, not poisoned. But, then again, there are many poisons that my magic cannot cure as yet." Pausing to pull something from her bag she takes a deep breath. Noticing some people beginning to rise and head toward the stairs she stops them with her soft, but carrying voice, "If we're going to bother the lady and her husband, I shall advise against it. And, just for your information, ye may not see the lady for several days hence even. She has been through much and will be allowed visitors on the morrow if she is strong enough." Noticing that several of those same people move back to their own tables, she continues.

"I have something that i believe is in order on this good eve."

*Rising and walking outside she re-enters moments later with a small handcart. In it you notice 2 wooden kasks. She hauls it to the bar and turns to Cyst*

Good Troll, would ye do the honors and place these upon the bar? After would ye care to tap them as well? Thank you."

*Cyst does as he was asked and remarks, "Smells gud. What dis?"

That, my large friend is from my private collection. Tis the best Dwarves Ale Kaladim has to offer. And this is from my vineyard. Tis Elven Blood Wine. All are welcome to partake of them both. I wish to celebrate Lady Lenore's recovery"

*Gathering her goblet she fills it with the wine and returns to her chair. Tired as she is she enjoy's her wine and promptly falls into a deep sleep. Her head slumps to her chin as she begins to dream of things that make her smile*
