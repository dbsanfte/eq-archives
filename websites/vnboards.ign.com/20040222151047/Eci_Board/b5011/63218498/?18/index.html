The Luau at the Polynesian changed last year after the release of Lilo and Stitch. Disney tried to make it more kid-friendly. Check out http://allearsnet.com/din/luau.htm for more info. My wife and I went last May. The food was ok. Not bad, but not great. The first part of the show wasn't what I was expecting. The performers were wearing Hawaiian print shirts and shorts and singing upbeat songs. I did enjoy the main part of the show which came after dinner. That's when the traditional style dances and costumes came out. And if the timing is right, you can sit on the beach afterwards and watch the fireworks at Magic Kingdom.
