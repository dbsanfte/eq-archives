if you take your EQ icon.. right click it, make a copy of the shortcut.

Then right click and select properties on the new shortcut. In the target where it lists the file pathname of the "Target" (will look like c:\program files\sony\everquest\eqgame.exe) .. Afte the parenthesis hit a space and then type patchme

So it will look like
Target: "c:\program files\sony\everquest\eqgame.exe" patchme


Now hit ok.

Now when you click on this icon when you know there hasn't been a patch it will skip the patcher altogether and bring you right up to the logo screens which you can just mash a key to get past.
