I haven't played a wizard in a long time, but they have seriously upgraded their abilities at the higher levels. Once you get to mid level or so, you start to really shine and can solo quite well. Not nearly as gear dependent as some classes can be.

I just recently made a Bard and I can tell you without a doubt that this is the most challenging class in the game. It can be very rewarding in the upper levels, but getting there will be a chore. If you like micro-managing and twitch based gameplay, then you should give it a try. Ultimately this is a caster class, but its still quite gear depenedent.
