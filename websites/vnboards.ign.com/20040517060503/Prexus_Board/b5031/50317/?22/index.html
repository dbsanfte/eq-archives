Actually for the age of the server Prexus is doing quite well.

E'ci was one of the original final release servers and they have yet to defeat Vox. To their credit they've managed to slay Nagafen a few times and have done the planes extensively.

Another thing that has effected Prexus was the timing of the server. It was put up in sync with school childrens summer holidays in North America. So there is a higher than normal population of high level characters played by younger people.

Some are good, some are bad. No one remembers the good ones and everyone remembers the bad ones.

Another thing to keep in mind is that experience makes a huge difference. Most of the hardest encounters in the game have been tuned (made susbstantially harder) since they were first implemented. This has made it progressively harder for newer servers to accumlate experience fighting as a large team. Older servers that got in early on dragon slayings got this benefit of practice as a team without the fight being quite so difficult. You have to walk a much tighter rope to slay a dragon if you want to call it a success today.
