I understand this is not the *NEW and IMPROVED* message board, so climb off my back Xabten and Gartheb. But this is where it began, and this is where I'll see it finish.
After much work I've finally managed to contact the GM Ashlanne of our board, who is in charge of guild creation/naming. She understands our plight and hopes the best for us, but cannot help us. Changing a guilds name is beyond her. I was informed to send an email to eqguilds@verant.com, which I have already done, petitioning a name change a sighting the fact that the "Drunken Lizards" are out there. Though we know what track record this email address has of answering us =)

So at this point in time I have come up with 3 different paths we can take.
1) Verant understands our plight and does the leg-work to change the name
2) Verant cannot or will not change the name and we as a guild live with it.
3) We disband the 'Freeport Tavern' and make another guild called the 'Freeport Tavern Drunks'

That 3rd option is yet unexplored, within my email to eqguilds@verant.com I contained a second query about whether we'd be allowed to do that. Kinda like a second option for them. There are some minor consequences of such actions. The foremost of those is that no account is allowed to lead more than one guild. Ever. Meaning, if I were to disband the guild in favor of creating the guild anew with the 'Drunks' part of the name. We'd be picking a new guild leader.
Now this is still a viable option, I've tried to design the guildleadership role as largely ceremonial, being nothing more than an officer with a title =) And I've always tried to instill a democracy as well. And so I would willingly do this, if changing the name is what a majority of people want (or Karana-Forbid, you people are sick of me already )

So while I await Verant's answer to my email, I'd like a poll performed. Considering worst case scenario and the name can not be changed. Would you rather stick with the current name, or go through the guild creation process once more. If the name change is the preferred option, and the email comes back negatively, then we'll go through a simple vote and come up with a new leader. I love this Tavern more than a simple silly title, and would do anything for the best interest of the guild. I'd be proud just to be a member =)
So people, vote.
