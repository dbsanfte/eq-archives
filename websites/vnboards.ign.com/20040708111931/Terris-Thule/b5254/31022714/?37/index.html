Hi everyone,

New to the boards here and probably a sticky discussion to jump into for my first foray, but I felt from reading the various MB threads here a few points that weren't made needed to be.

I do know a few of the MB wizard casually, and I have been on the receiving end of what many of the protesters are calling a KS (Though I've come to realize it really isn't).

First off, I know paximir from old days, and I find the claims of MB wizards rushing into a buffing group raid and kiling the mob out from under them extemely unlikely.

On a recent Talendor raid attempted by Scarlet Underground, Paximir showed up in zone. When I told him we were trying Tallendor I got this tell "Good luck.. thats why I'm here too." This is not the attitude of a MB team about to swoop down and KS us. They waited at zone and watched us pull and fail on our first try at controlled pull of Tallendor (We lost to him once before on an unexpected fight right after killing ragefire). While we were ressing and rebuffing they proceeded to kill tallendor. They did not even come remotely close to what anyone would consider a KS.

However, I would like to express what I think many guilds (and my own personal feelings were that night) have at the above experience.

SU is medium level guild slowly improving in power. We can raid HoT successfully (albeit at the door), have killed wuoshi and are beginning to get more and more 60s. However, we are still on the small side and a night when we all can get together and attempt something off a Kunark Dragon level is not a common occurence. We fully intended to rez (had a click stick there) rebuff and give tallendor another go. For us the time investment is anywher from 30-60 minutes to get everyone there organized and buffed to do this encounter.

I had asked the MB wizards to let us have another chance at tallendor before they took him down and was ignored on the request (Albeit rightly so to many of you . I don't think you are fully realizing the frustration level of a raid leader and the guild members to be denied the opportunity to retry a mob and adjust strats to try to win.

I am not gonna stand here and say that loot from tallendor was a part of the reason we were there; but I can honestly tell you that the MAIN reason we were there is it was the next level of encounter for us and many of our members get the most fun out of mastering these.

I really feel the MB team while obeying what seems to sadly be the current trend on our server (If the guy in front of you shows any weakness.. move in and grab it yourself), I honestly feel letting us try a second time would not have been asking anyting unreasonable. Two outcomes would have occurred:

A) Our guild (with more members already in zone to try 2nd time) would have won; and in my personal opinion achieved the most fun thing in EQ, beating a mob through skill and learning from mistakes. 25 people would have ended the night a great feeling of accomplishment and 5 people on he MB team would have wated 30-45 minutes and lost 1 of dozens of tallendors they have or will kill in the coming months.

B) We would have pulled and wiped a 2nd time, then MB team moves in a zots Tallendor 20 minutes after they wanted to.

Instead, they kill him after 1st wipe and in mid res-rebuff for our 2nd pull and leave the entire guild feeling slightly cheated and slightly bitter about the entire MB Team.

The above is not a flame/condemnation of the current methods of the MB team, but rather hopefully a better understanding of how the guilds aiming for this content feel. Many of the flames on this board are just heat of the moment reactions of feeling cheating w/o first calming down and thinking it over.

On that final note, I would like to publicly apologize to Monty. At the time of the above encounter I was feeling as frustrated as anyone here, and he happend to be my unfortunate target for a rant. It was uncalled and I hope you will accept my apology for childish behavior ( I haven't yet manage to catch you online).
