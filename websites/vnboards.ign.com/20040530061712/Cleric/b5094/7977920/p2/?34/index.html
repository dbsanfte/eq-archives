I have enjoyed every minute of being a halfling cleric... but there are some downsides...

1. Steps... the whole fricken world of EQ is filled with steps that considering the scale.. are like 3 feet high... who the hell can get up a 3 feet high step? the ones in kael by the bank are good examples. you have to hop up each step.

2. Lack of decent vision - this really gets to be old, but gamma correction help u if you dont have a guise.

3. Constantly being mistaken for a druid... I actually had one idiot in a group once scream "EVAC EVAC EVAC dammit" at me.. evidently the constant 'CH on %t' spam didnt clue him off after more then an hour that I was a cleric.

What are the good sides?

1. Your short... so nobody has to shrink you during the raid.

2. You look fricken awesome in full plate.

3. You can hide... You can sneak... You can pacify... between the 3 if you cant get someplace its probably not worth going.
