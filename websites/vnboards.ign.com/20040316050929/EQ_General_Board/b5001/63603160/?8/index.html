 		Date Posted: 2/2 10:13am Subject: RE: Well guys....Everquest...Bye DAoC.
Even though I'm about to quit EQ, I'd recommend Sullon, which is where I've been for a couple of years now. The team concept works well enough at low to mid levels - higher level folks will help you out, in general. There's a bit of cross-teaming going on in the end-game now, but I guess that's going to happen everywhere.

The "race war" servers - Vallon and Tallon - are filled with cross-teaming, which I would find frustrating. If you want to be a renegade, be a PKer on Rallos. Most of the population there is "anti-PK" - they don't fight unless attacked first, or if you're made they hit list.

To me, it sounds like you'd be bored on a regular server. Sullon has ganking, but if you have, or can develop, a thick skin, it's a fun place.

Make a new character on each, go to the Nexus, and ask around. If you're a Good, go to Kelethin or Halas and ask around for what people think of the server.
