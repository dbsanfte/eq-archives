Wow...

I really just dont know what to say coming from what i remembered about EQLive a long long time ago right after the luclin release.

I logged on of my lowly gnomes who happened to be bound in Lake of Ill Omen. I remember this place used to be filled with people and the chat box was constantly moving with different people using /ooc. I remember the druids giving out SoWs at the windmill and the groups forming up.

Needless to say i did a /who to find that there were only 5 people in the entire zone.

I then headed to the luclin gate to go to the moon and on my way i passed through Frontier Mountains. Again i /who'd and found only myself in the zone. The days of Giant groups searching for Forest Loops (Its been 2 years i forget the name ) have passed i see.

Then on my next trip i moved to the dreadlands on the last stage of my journey to the gate. Again i performed a /who to find only 20 people in the zone which once bosted more than a hundred players....

It is as if an entire continent in the realm of Everquest has purely vanished from the face of Norrath, only to be traveled through by a few adventurers.


For the last part of my journey after arriving on the moon i traveled back to Steamfont to revisit my favorite zone in all of Norrath. I ran first toward the small noobie spot where rats and spiderlings once roamed to find it filled with creatures i had never before seen in this part of the world.

Then as i neared the entrance to my little gnome's home i found a pillar standing there so awkward and strange, which i assume leads to the Planes of Power.



I once left EQ out of boredom with the game and have come back two years later to retest the game. It was strange feeling like a newbie in the world of Norrath, yet it felt as if i was no longer in Norrath.


Dont get me wrong the games great and is prolly better than ever but i wonder if you guys have really noticed how much the game has changed over the past years. I guess its sorta like when you visted your Aunt or Uncle and they thought youve grown so much yet you feel pretty much the same since youve been there as you grew yet someoe who saw you only a few times every few years would really see the difference



Anyway what server should i go on? Currently tunare but seeing as my inactivity pretty much got rid of all my ties to the server i wouldnt mind starting fresh (seeing as how im once again a noobie).


Almost forgot to mention that when i left i ran the game on a pretty bad machine and have recently pumped it up . Game is so much nicer than i remember lol
