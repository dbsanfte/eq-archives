As a recent convert from AC (Microsoft Asheron's Call) where last year we went through the AC2 fiasco all I can say is, wait and see.

Most AC1 players were super keen based on the massively improved graphics screenshots we were fed but once the game was released there was probably a 2-3 month period where there was a small but steady flow of people to AC2, and then it stopped.... then a few months more passed... and they started coming back.

Why? Well, yes, the graphics were pretty. 100% for the eye candy. But the gameplay? Well, MS/Turbine made a massive mistake, they tried to make too many changes (aka "improvements") and totally blew it. AC2 was the most boring game I've ever played. One bizarre change they made was to remove all NPCs from towns as they believed that as more and more people played, there would be enough people to support their new "player driven" economy and town development. Bah-bow! Most towns were empty when you visited. In fact, I used to get quite a surprise when I ran into ANYONE in game. They also turned Combat into a click once and wait affair (worse than EQ).

In a nutshell they got it wrong. Really, really wrong. Eventually one of the AC2 servers was shut down. Then Turbine bought back all the rights to AC and broke away from MS. Then a new world was opened in AC1.

How to MS/Turbine get it so wrong? God only knows... They had a massive beta program but just made too many changes to the gameplay and ruined it. Not even the eye candy could save it. Still AC2 limps along..

My point? Don't assume EQ2 will be better than EQ1 just because, well, it's version 2 of a product.
