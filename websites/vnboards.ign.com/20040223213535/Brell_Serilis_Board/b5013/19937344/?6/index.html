subtitled: "Let them eat (stale) cake"


Last night I spoke
with the Lady of the Moon
I paid for a bargain with her,
I asked that she would always
keep you safe from harm.

So while you sleep
she promised to shine
her light down upon you
and watch over you
for the rest of your days.

If I could,
I would give you
all the jewels of heaven,
sealed up in a box and wrapped
with a bow of gleaming silver.

If my arms would reach,
I would hold you
from across the world
and do my best
to make your world right.

Soon, one wishes in earnest.
Soon.
Oyasuminasai, kimi.


Also, this is running through my mind every time I think about being in love. It's written by a woman for another woman, but it's still (I think) what a man wants to be for a woman.

Now the parking lot is empty,
everyone's gone someplace.
I pick you up and in the trunk I've packed
a cooler and a two-day suitcase.
'Cause there's a place we like to drive
way out in the country
and five miles out of the city limits we're singing
and your hand's upon my leg.

So we're okay, we're fine,
baby I'm here to stop your crying
chase all the ghosts from your head
I'm stronger than the monster beneath your bed,
smarter than the tricks played on your heart,
we'll look at them together then we'll take them apart
adding up the total of a lovethat's true
multiply life by the power of two.

For Liz:

How bitter the taste of the day
how dark the path
how hard it is to spend a life
without you, yet always with you.

How difficult it is.

Alone you are a vague memory (meaning more like "I only have vague ideas of who you are)
I flee and I cannot watch
a core broken in a thousand pieces
a love in the forgetfulness

How difficult it is

How difficult it is to return again (to the way I was before)
how difficult it is to understand
how difficult it is to love again
how difficult it is to believe again (literally return to belief)

Someone who will listen to me talk
and want me to believe
at my center I always wanted
to believe in love yet never be loved

How difficult it is.
Believe in me, believe in you,
always believe in love.
