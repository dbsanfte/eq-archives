I've tried it... CoH is actually much better than I expected. I got into it because a friend of a friend was doing it, got the friend into it, and he got me into it.
No loot per se... so that's kinda out-of-the-box thinking... seems to work too.
The graphics are really nice, the animations are sometimes even better, and the character customization is disgustingly godly. I could hit the random button for hours and not come up with a toon that looks the same.
I'm not sure how the long-term appeal will be... guess I'll see in a month if I still want to play. It's been exciting the past few days at least.
