I made a gnome. I don't want to argue about what's the best race to play a cleric. I just want to know if what I did during stat allocation will cause me to hate my cleric life later on.

I put 10 into str, 5 into stamina and the remaining 15 into wisdom. This resulted in a gnome cleric with 75 str, 80 stamina and 92 wisdom.

The idea was that any melee stat below 75 would result in a penalty. Thus I upped my str to 75. I figured that an extra 5 stamina couldn't hurt since I will get pounded on due to heal aggro. It may be that 5 stamina doesn't really mean a lot though.

My wisdom is very low I realize. I also realize that ultimately, at the high end of things, this probably won't be a big issue. However, from lvl 1 to 50, it could present a problem with the size of the mana pool. I know there are lots of things to improve regen.

I don't really plan to be a "battle cleric". I'd like to be able to swing a hammer from time to time when I'm full mana in a group or soloing carefully. Early on, melee is basically required for a cleric (1-25) from what I've seen so far (my highest cleric ever being lvl 24). Currently I'm almost lvl 12 with the gnome cleric in full newbie quest armor.

Should I just bite the bullet and redo this cleric? I don't even know that this cleric will see the high end game. If I do redo the cleric should I put 5 into str and the rest into wisdom? The 5 I used in stamina were probably a waste.

Keep in mind that I'm new to the server that I'm playing on. The most money I've ever had at once was 30pp. Twinking is not an option, so low str early on could result in me being chronically overweight since I need to loot whatever I can to raise funds.

no, you haven't gimped your character. there are plenty of wis items in the game, that you can indeed reach the cap. what you may find yourself getting more annoyed at could be your str actually. clerics wear plate armor for best ac, but plate is heavy. at 75 str you may find yourself often encumbered. Assuming you have Luclin, it may be worth your time to try focusing on leveling up solely on mobs that drop tradeskill sellable items for the bazaar (pelts and silks, especially silks since you can handle better being poisoned better than pretty much any other class).

if you don't want to recreate your character, don't. it may take a bit more time farming stuff for the money to upgrade gear to keep yourself happy with how your stats stand is all. will it balance out? /shrug. depends on how competitive you are about raising your stats.
