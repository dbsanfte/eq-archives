I was awaiting someone to stop by and throw down the argument. Cool!

Yes, you're absolutely right when you say that you can't mass target a tank w/ 5 or so clickers and overlap your healing and such. By the same token, donal's bp isn't always active and doesn't always have a constant heal on someone. You can circumvent much of the downtime presented by the Donal's bp by constantly keeping CE on a tank through the PoG bp. I'll elaborate.

You can assign one tank per PoG bp cleric. You shouldn't assign more as Desitny has pointed out as the CE isn't stackable. So if a tank constantly has a CE on him he is constantly getting the heal each tick regardless of whether or not he's full health. This draws the CE bp dangerously close to the Donal's bp in efficiency because even if you chain Donal's a tank, you only gain efficiency over the pog bp with said tank if that tank is taking more than the 1500hp or so that CE offers over it's duration (say that 10 times fast).

In the case of Velketor, that's far from the norm. You normally have him bouncing over each of a dozen targets, so in effect the PoG bp catches up and gets the tank to health again before he takes the additional beating.

Additionally, you could also assign one CE bp to each primary tank thus keeping a constant CE on each tank and then allocate another healer, be that a cleric or shammy or druid or whatever, to keep the tanks healed should they begin to take a constant agro for which the CE BP couldn't compensate. This would work out far better than the Donal's bp because you wouldn't have to wait for the 30 second window w/o a heal. You can eliminate the critical period all together with this system.

Finally, the healing of CE isn't a 6 second wait after it hits to begin the effect. It heals upon the first tick expiring to my experience. Sometimes you have to wait and sometimes it's instant. But this is irrelevant in such an argument as this is a situation where you would constantly click on the tank anyhow.


Ultimately, Capn Raloon took a shot at us (or so that's my impression as it seems to be that of others as well) for something and he "forgot" to mention that you all had a system of your own that, in my opinion, comes remarkably close to serving the same purpose as ours. It's still not going to stop me from congratulating you all on a fine performance for which you all certainly deserve the merit!

And yeah, I believe I have a rather sound way of defeating him without using clickable bps altogether. I'll leave this up to all of you without such items tho!


edit: Ody, we beat him after the nerf of the donal's bp just fyi. I'm pretty sure we've beaten him twice since the nerf. I can't remember precisely tho = (
