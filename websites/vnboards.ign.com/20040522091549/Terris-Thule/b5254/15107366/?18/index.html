I'm not a tank, but in the end game a tank wants AC and HP... they aggo a mob, stand and get beaten on, whilst everyone else does 'un-aggroed damage'.

So you need to look at how to get the most possible HP and AC. An Ogre has the highest starting STA and STA items seem hard to come by; however, when a 56+ SHM is buffing all your stats by 50 or more (Avatar is an increase of 100 to all physical stats but is a lvl 60 spell)you begin to see that base STA may not be an issue.

The leader of the guild Triton on Povar is a lvl 60 Halfling warrior called Giantt, Giantt Ice at the moment after Team Ice had his name changed I think He is the main tank for one of the most powerful guilds on any server, so it is possible to go as far as you want with pretty much any Race / Class combo.

If you want to be a gnome tank, go for it, you'll never wipe out a party by getting stunned on Sol B zone line as a Gnome!!
