Brewing only takes a few hundred pp to hit 248, but it takes a TON of combines, and from around 180~ to 248 the "best" way to skill up is Mino Hero Brews, which are a 9 item combine, and a huge pain.

Once you have it up, you can make various drinks like Grobb Liquidised Meat, Qeynos Afternoon Tea, Kaladim Constituionals, maybe even Solstice Earring parts, etc and sell them for probably anywhere from 25 to 100% profit.

Yep, another one that's currently got some profit in it, is Celestial Essences. No fail, 2 item combine, you can buy Scent of Marr from a vendor in EC by the WC zone line, and then buy Celestial Solvent in PoK and combine in a mortar and pestle. They cost roughly 1.75 plat per combine, and on Povar you can sell them at anywhere from 4 - 5 plat per essence, making 100% or more profit. With the new tradeskill interface people may still buy them to save time, but I definently can see the market going down for them. I currently buy them most of the time just because it's quick, and 200~ stack is nothing.
