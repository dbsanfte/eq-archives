This is my first post on this board, but I have been lurking for awhile. I took a break from EQ last spring and plan on playing again in the new year. I want to get some perspective on the playing styles of my fellow chanters. Low down dirty dungeon crawling has always been my favorite senarios. So, I will focus this thread on that situation. What I want from you is to list the 8 spells you have up. I also want to know what 10 hot keys you may have. Please feel free to post your opinion and playing style no matter your expertise, as I want all levels to benefit from this thread. The following is my setup:

Senario: Typical Dungeoneering group (no AoE group) with your occational multi-mob pull.
My level: 56th
Spell List:
1)Color Slant
2)Shiftless Deeds(no shammy) or 2)Color shift
3)Dazzle
4)Paralizing Earth (root)
5)Tashania
6)Theft of Thought
7)Boltran's Agacerie
8)Variable Buff slot

I use spells 1-5 as hotkeys' 1-5.
Sit as hoykey #6.
Hotkey 7 is my charm notification
/g I am atempting to charm %t
/cast 7
/pause 5
/pet who leader
HotKey 8 is /pet attack
HotKey 9 is /pet back off
HotKey 0 will be my epic.

As you can prolly tell I like charming!
I use spells 1-5 as hotkeys so I can hit the spell as soon as it refreshes, saving time and lives. I also use the mouse to move and look around. Please feel free to comment as I have been away for many months.
