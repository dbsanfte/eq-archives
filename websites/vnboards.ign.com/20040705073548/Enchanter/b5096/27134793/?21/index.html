3. Seems to get resisted easy on blues, and you cant tell what resisted it!

4. When resisted you have a swarm of mobs in your face, and if you try to ae mez again to include the mobs that are now right on you, have a chance to mez yourself, even with magic resist

5. gets you caught in a loop where you have to keep chain casting it, that combined with resists and its short duration seems to make this spell very dangerous to use

any thoughts ?

I use it, even though it can cause major aggro headaches. In a dangerous pull, nothing will stop the train faster. And in most cases, at least some of the mobs will get stuck. Now that being said, I never use it when fighting more than three adds. Making the switch to single Mez with four mobs is just too difficult for me at this point.
