Ok, im gonna attempt to clear up problems with the zing album. Here goes.

When you go to the site at http://www.zing.com/album/?id=4294773799
you are presented with the first page of the album.

This is for people who have never used zing or uploaded previously.

On the left hand side of the page there is a Section called "Submit a Picture" with a small drop down option below it. Click that and click UPLOAD DIRECTLY

This will get you to a Sign In screen, if you haven't signed up already, click the option that says CLICK HERE TO JOIN just below the sign in area.

Fill out the sign up sheet (you can use your eq name if you wish, makes it easier to see who submitted what)

After you click the JOIN ZING button you'll be presented with an Invite screen, you don't have to do anything here if you don't wanna. Just click START USING Zing at the bottom right.

OK, now you'll be presented with the Add Pictures to "Everquest screen shots" place.

The Drag and Drop option is easiest, just follow the instructions for your browser.

after that follow any instructions on screen.

Once your done uploading then its very self explanitory. You can caption your phots (highly recommend )

That's it. Next time you visit the album you do same thing, Submit a Picture, upload directly, but you'll go straight to the upload page since you already have a membership.

I sure hope this helps you guys and gals.
