1. Shadow Strike: Target foe takes 12...41 shadow damage. If that foe's Health is above 50%, you steal 12...41 Health.

2. Lifebane Strike: Target foe takes 12...41 shadow damage. If that foe's Health is above 50%, you steal up to 12...41 Health.

3. Vampiric Gaze: Steal up to 18...52 Health from target foe.

4. Signet of Lost Souls: If target foe is below 50% Health, you gain 10...82 Health and 1...8 Energy.

5. Offering of Blood: Sacrifice 20% maximum Health. You gain 8...18 Energy.

6. Awaken The Blood: For 20...39 seconds, you gain +2 Blood Magic and +2 Curses, but whenever you sacrifice Health, you sacrifice 50% more than the normal amount.

7. Blood Ritual:Sacrifice 17% maximum Health. For 8...13 seconds, target touched ally gains +3 Energy regeneration. Blood Ritual cannot be used on the caster.

8. Sunspear Rebirth Signet: Resurrect target dead ally at your location with full health and 10% energy for each Sunspear rank you have attained. This signet can only be used once per mission unless recharged by a morale boost.

My favorite build is a Blood Necro

16 Blood
12+3+1
13 Soul Reaping
12+1

skills:
Signet of Lost Souls, Life Transfer (elite), Life Siphon, Vampiric Gaze, Lifebane Strike, Blood of the Agressor, Barbed Signet, and Blood Renewal/Res Sig.

Start the fight by using Life Siphon. It is a 2 second cast, so its ideal to cast this first, before the enemy has been aggro'ed. Then, depending on the number of foes you can
(if it is only one foe) follow up with a life transfer. Then start a chain of Lifebane strike, blood of the agressor, vamp gaze, blood of the agressor, barbed signet, blood of the agressor, vamp gaze, etc, until your foe is below half health and then fire off a signet of lost souls.
The idea of this build is to never run out of energy while spamming powerful life stealing spells.

If there are several foes, spam life siphon and transfer on everyone until you have maximum regen and then single out a target to begin the chain.

This build is excellent when facing enemies of all varieties.

Blood renewal is very useful, so when a res sig is not needed I reccommend using it. If you see an assassin or a pesky warrior approaching, use blood renewal before they are in range and begin using your skill bar when they are close enough. This gives you a +6 regen so bleeding and poison are basically negated with +8 life transfer and +3 life siphon. About halfway through the confrontation, even if you appear to be losing, BAM you will be up +200 hp when blood renewal finally ends.
