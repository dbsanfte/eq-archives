Toon is short for "cartoon" I think... it might have been Who Framed Roger Rabbit? which originally coined the phrase.

Don't know why it's the current jargon in EQ since I haven't been in game for a year and a half.

I remember...

... bind-rushing Nagafen at one of the DA-killings. Well, it might have been DA-- that cleric with the Q in his name was leading it. Acid-tongued kid from the boards, I think. I thought when I did it that they had already won before, but perhaps I was at that first "ugly" one.

... camping for jboots at level 22 and binding in that room. Sun came up on a sunday morning when my turn came and luck was with me-- I looked out bleary eyed and though "what am I doing?" It wasn't the last time I thought that while playing.

... arguing with Honch over EL doing a camp 4 break in Fear when we were about to set up for a camp 2 break in Fear. EL was there first, and we had it planned and publicized on this board. I'm sure they started gathering early because they knew we would be coming along-- it turned into one of those fun (many) "server-politics" discussions between guild leaders. Since he was extraordinairily unresponsive, I ended up saying "I understand you don't recognize the claiming of fear breaks, but if you and I can't work out some kind of bi-lateral rotation for future, we will come in and break from camp 2, and hope you guys haven't cleared south far enough to make it boring for us. Then we'll split fear whether you like it or not. If we CAN work something out for next time-- my guild will walk away right now." Something like that. He wasn't having any, and so the class warfare between the concept of the "casual" gamer and the "hardcore" gamer continued on.

... being fascinated by the political social dynamics of what should have been such a simple game. And realizing what a cycle the whole form-guild/grow-guild/split-guild thing was. I'm still fascinated by that.

... wincing at Saladina's first open Nagafen raids.

... leading the first couple successful open Nagafen raids and then stopping once Eelyen starting posting that he would lead open Nagafen raids. Last thing we needed at that point was open raids warring for content. Chuckle.

... logging in for the first time not realizing there were multiple "servers." I made a monk and got him up to 4th level before I logged out for the night. Next time I logged in the monk was gone! So I started another character. I think I found that monk a couple of months later when I realized what had happened.

And finally, I'm so old school I remember thinking the female characters were actually being played by females... but that it was WIERD when other people started being interested personally in one, even though I still had the fantasy that maybe that dark elf really was hot IRL!!!
