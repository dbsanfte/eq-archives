You'll have to do adventures in NRo until you have enough to buy your 1400ish armor. Then the points from other zones can be used to purchase more 1400ish items if you have enough.

The points in a camp limits what you have access to purchase in that camp. You can use the points from others to buy things up to what you have in that camp.

Example:(numbers and items made up) I have 120 points in BB and 60 in EC. Items costing 120 or less in BB are available to me. Items 60 or less are available to me in EC. BB has 3 spells I want costing 90 each. As they are under the 120 cap I can buy them. I have a total of 180 points available and the 3 spells cost me 270 so I can only buy two of them.

The points can be used together to purchase things that cost no more than the highest available to you thing at that camp. It's like a bunch of small store credits that you can use but they don't add up to allow you to buy one big thing, just a bunch of small things.

Hope that helps.

Keiyuki

You earn "fame" with the NPCs each time you do an adventure. "Fame" never decays. If you buy 4 400 point items, you've earned 1600 "fame" and can see everything in that location. "Fame" is based on per location. Points are what you spend at each camp, and are global.

How much "fame" you earn is the same as the points you earn per adventure.
