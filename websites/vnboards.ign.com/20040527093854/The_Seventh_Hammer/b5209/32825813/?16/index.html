For those who know me, you know my light-heartedness and eagerness to make you smile. For 3 and a half years I have played EQ and always found customer service to be...while slow at times... always fair. But when does this end, when does the internal bias and the focus of service turn to the personal will of the inner circle of verant employees and their friends.

At 8:49pm est Ledian From the everquest coordinating staff sent me a tell regarding a character I had on my account which was a comical spoof of my ex-guildleader Ranadin.

This character had been in existance since the period of time I was in Ranadin's guild "The Dark Exile" and was known to Ranadin. The character was logged on a private channel on Thursday July 11, and never sent a tell to Ranadin nor made slanderous comments directed to him. However, using whatever ties and whatever means it was brought to verant attention and escalated to the scale that my account was banned.

The irony of the situation lies in due process... if the name was so offensive... why 6 months later. And what of the other countless ranadin spoofs constantly being created... some even leveled, why do they go unpunished. Why on the nameless and other servers, do stalkers and spoofs go by with a simple soulbound warning and I get a suspension? Why when a certain special individual says jump, the VI Customer Service asks how "high"?

We pay our money each month, to play this game and recieve customer service where neccesary. One would hope for your money's worth you would recieve an unbiased and just treatment from those individuals representing the company you are paying.

But my question stands, Is it just to ban the account of someone with no previous standing soulbound markers? Is it just to ban the account of someone so directly and so personally slighted by the individual being mocked and yet leave the true guilty party unpunished?

My challenge to everyone, and the verant staff who will subsequently be recieving this in your email, is to reprioritize what a major offense is. A definite and clear look into your policies should be taken.

The person you hold in such high regard and place a halo on his head is no angel. He has unwritten policies in place regarding loot in exchange for account and personal info, and while you will defend yourself with the legal bullshit of "never give out your personal info" you will still continue to defend your own best interests... including those of your favorites. You will toss blind eye to the multiboxing of accounts under different names (Account sharing), toss blind eye to the item duping, pathing cheats, and clipping abuse. But yet... a name carries so heavy a penalty?

Forget the abuses suffered daily in a high pressure and highly competitive environment... Your angelic dictator offers salvation..."just say your account name and password and you get the items"...the exchange of a few words for the status symbols and items a person desires...but wait, banish the thought of saying "yes"... because in a realistic world a normal human being would "never give out personal info". The sheer notion of someone saying no, when such a choice is presented is laughable at best. Quite simply put... the will to achieve and be "the best" is inate in human nature.

In the end I am left dissapointed and curious. Curious to what depths the corruption extends. Who knows what sorts of gm favors are going on under the table all the time, assistance killing mobs, character twinking, and more all come to mind.

If you are as disturbed with the system as I am... voice your thoughts acrosby@soe.sony.com ... that's ledian's unnamed supervisor who dictated this. Please be so kind as to share your thoughts with him.

As for me I dont know if I will come back once my suspension is over, no sense wasting my money to fuel the will of someone I hate so much.




Oh one final question Ledian, when you login, do YOU click "yes" to the Policy Agreement?
