Ok, for some reason I cannot post to the board on the Station, so I came here. (It helps that I enjoy this server.)

I wanted to post a comment about twinking in the ongoing threads there, but I guess I am going to have to start one here...

I was wondering what the trouble is with twinking. I do not understand twinking your OWN characters. That I do not like. Seems like cheating... But, I do not have a problem with getting twinked by another person. I just started playing, (if it is not obvious by the fact this is my 1st post.) and had a random person dump a few items on me. Where's the problem with that? Look at life the same way. At work you do not guess how to do your job. (Well, that's debatable depending on your job.) Every now and again, you cow-orkers or maybe even your boss will give you a hand, or a tip, etc... I have kids. They count on my help. No matter how old my kids get, I will still be helping them, buying them things, etc. For you younger players (or even the older ones), have your parents ever gotten you something you wanted or needed? How is this different? I was not counting on any help. Fact is, I didn't want any help. I wanted to prove I could do it on my own. But, that person decided to give me a boost. I guess this is my way of addressing the twinking subject and giving a huge thank you to that person. I appreciate someone who is willing to take the time to give a hand to a newbie. And when I am in a position to return the favor, I will do the same.

I will let this ride on it's own momentum now. I tend to ramble. See everyone on TT!
