Date Posted: 1/16/04 4:52am Subject: RE: Is it too late for EQ
Exactly. I'm not even getting exited about EQ2 until I hear it's gold.

FFXI was, to me, the most boring MMORPG ever made. And just to qualify that, I have tried dozens of different MMORPG's. For what is currently out there, I think EQ and UO (Utlima Online) are the best all around for fun. UO is too dated graphically for most people to get into today, but it is still a great game with the best design IMO.

It's not too late to get into either, in fact with the bundle deals they are offering it's really a great time. EQ Evolution, with the base game, all but one current expansion, and a free month of play for $30 is an excellent deal. Try it out, even if the game isn't for you I'm sure you'll get at least $20 worth out of it.

I started playing EQ the first day it went retail, and have quit many times, for pretty much every other game that came out. I recently came back after more than a year away, and expected to see nothing but 60+ levels and twinked alts. The player base now is actually better now than 2 years ago, with plenty of power gamers, casual players, and more total newbies than I've seen in awile in EQ.

I see lots of questions being asked that only a total newbie would ask, and the good thing is that they all get answered, and not with "RTFM NOOB!"

To answer your question JJ. Yes and no. Lame huh. EQ still has my nod as the best mmorpg on the market right now. I play FFXI atm but I'm an EQ vet to the core. While there is tons of things to do in EQ you may find it difficult to come into any server but the newest and make friends. Most folks on established servers have long time characters and the majority of low lvls are alts just lvling up. It is a blast though once you get the hang of it. GL if you choose to play. Lots of great folks still in eq and many are helpful to true noobs.

Now a downside or two. The graphics are dated. No big deal unless your a graphic whore. Making money at low lvls is pretty tedious. Not nearly as bad as it used to be. But you know how easy low lvl farming is in FFXI. EQ farming is Brutal IMO. GL if you join the ranks.
