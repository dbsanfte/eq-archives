Well, actually at the upper levels, Monks are one of the better soloing classes, and as an Iksar you have the benefit of improved regen and less downtime. Also, at upper levels Monks are generally looked upon as the best pulling class (in most situations) and will usually be welcome in raid situations for that reason. Leveling a monk isn't really that hard, you just need to find good places to hunt, as with any class.

However, if you're set against the monk and looking for a class that will be welcome much more often in raid level events and such, you should consider the Cleric and Warrior classes. Raids cannot happen without a good amount of both classes. Neither of these classes are really great at soloing though, they work best in combination with each other.

If you have good hand-eye coordination and like a little danger, you might also consider the Enchanter, they're another must-have class in most high end and raid groups. Other fun classes that are (almost) universally welcome in most high end groups are Rogues (need someone to pick those locks and they're nasty in combat) and to a lesser extent, Bards since they can, in a pinch, generally fill in for most other classes in one way or another and they are awesome at group support.

If soloing ability is a primary concern, you should look at Necros, Mages, and Druids. They're the most renown for soloing, but Shaman, Wizards, Shadow Knights, and Bards can also be very successful at it. Keep in mind, soloing is very boring, and exp gains are not that great, especially when you factor in all the deaths it takes before you get good at it (and when anything goes wrong.) Also, you won't build up good dungeon fighting skills this way.

In any case, all the classes are fun, it really depends on what you find enjoyable. Its hard to really get a good idea how any class/race combo plays out by only getting to 30th level... considering you already have a year invested, you should really consider sticking with the Monk, get yourself to 50 or so, and then look at it again.

Good luck!
