New kland isnt hard if you know what your doing, failed first attempt lastnight cause we hadnt tried him in like 3 months, owned him with like 5 deaths the second try.

North wing mobs are different, take zlexak for example. Insane fight, hoshkar on steriods. Has 3x the hp as hoshkar from vp, with the same 1250 disease ae that slows melee by 70%. We still get stupid and fail to him more than most mobs, but always kill the next time. Then you have Lord Vyemm. We died alot to him, he is a serious pain in the ass. Quad 1100 with knockback ae and has alot of hp. He pretty much owns any mob besides tunare/aow.

Anyway its pretty obvious you dont just go from zerging vind with 70 people and almost losing to trying north stuff. The way i made sure my guild went about it was through several months of farming normal mobs like dain/ct/yel/etc, and at least start to get some of our melee primal weapons. Sure we could have checked it out earlier, but it was more appealing to me to build us up for it rather than get owned and waste our time. It wont effect my guild either way if try to jump the gun and throw yourself at north to see what its like because we're in there every other week either way. The game was meant to be played in a certain order somewhat. I mean my guild went from killing vs, to killing trak, to killing kunark dragons like goreniere, to clearing vp, to killing velk, to killing vind, to killing first brood dragons, to killing west wing nameds, to killing dain, to clearing st, to killing tormax, to killing yelinak, to clearing ntov. Now obviously you dont have to do everything the way we did it, but if you have half a brain and even looked at how any successful guild on ANY server did it, you would see we followed the same path pretty much.

Alot of guilds out there have some pretty good potential, and they could be great if they had people who actually knew how the game works and how mobs work. A few will prolly end up clearing ntov eventually just like my guild and Vane do every week now, but just remember how we got there, we didnt get there by trying to skip months of work by throwing ourselves when we werent nearly prepared for north, and believe me you need to prepare a TON for north. We took alot of time to prepare for it(prolly 4 months of hardcore farming every single uber mob that we were capable of killing), a few weeks of knowing that we were ready and trying to figure out how to kill aaryonar, then finally it all came togather and we did it. Thats what the game is all about if you have any future at raiding anything major. Its about making sure you have the right experiance and knowledge as well as gear and skills. It isnt about killing vind once then thinking you can do north and running in there totally unprepared.

Stuff like VP, as hellish as it was, it made us where we are right now. Getting everyone togather to accomplish a goal that we set out when we first killed trak and working for over a month at trying to achieve it. Through all the long cr's, wipeouts, close killings, and of course HOSHKAR. Thats how we dealt with stuff like Ntov, because we had essentially been there before and knew what it took to win. Obviously i dont expect people to go do vp before they do north but it was prolly the best thing that my guild did as far as building up our skills. Dont take this as a flame post or whatever because i could care less at what anyone in Arete think, i'm just giving my opinions on the game and this type of stuff. And dont take this as a, "DONT ENTER MY NORTH WING", post because honestly, its gonna be awhile before anyone else is in there and as long as my guild sticks togather i'll never be worried about people nipping at our heels.
