Ok, well I've decided to have a little contest, and thought if anyone here would like to sign up feel free. I started this on my workout forum, and it went great. This is great for people that want to get into shape, or stay in shape that don't have a lot of time, or equipment to workout. Anyway here is how you play.

First off the exercises in this game are things anyone can do, and they don't include weights, or anything you need to buy. Ok what I will need people to do is sign up with a post, their board name, age range, and their sex (m or F).

Please dont do this unless you plan to actually workout, the only person you will fool is yourself. Anyway once we have the players I will set a start date, and your goal is to do listed exercises threwout the day for a certain time or # of times. Each exercise has a points value, and an additional pt system for the length you do it, or how many reps.

Here is a list of the exercises that you can do.

Cardio
-Cardio can be walking, any sort of bike, or treadmill, you get the picture.

****This one is extra credit, Its called 21's. A few people on this board have tried them after I told them about it, and they said I put them in a wheelchair their legs hurt so bad! hahaha

-21's (Stand straight up feet shoulder width apart, and your hands straight out, just like the above mentioned squat. Ok here is how you do it. While standing there do 1 squat, walk 4 steps straight ahead, and turn to face where you were just at. At this point without rest do 2 squats, and take another 4 steps back to where you started. Turn and face were you were just at, and do 3 squats, and so on and so on. You do this in an attempt to get up to 21. Dont feel bad if you dont make it I puked the first time I did it! hahahah
-5 (15pts)
-7 (25pts
-10 (35pts)
-13 (45pts)
-16 (60pts)
-19 (75pts)
-21 (100pts)

*also a side note I listed specific #'s for a few reasons. #1 you must do a listed # to receive credit for the pts. The reasons being if you are doing push ups, and want more pts so you shoot for the 25 mark. This will push you from the 20 to 25 mark, because if you fail you only get the credit for the previous # on the list, and you wasted whatever else you did between 20-25. If you go beyond the #'s listed above you will recieve no more pts then that are listed. The extra you do are bonus for your health. Again this game is for fun, and to help people get excited about exercising. Please dont sign up and just fake it, and put the max for everything.

I will make a new thread once everyone is signed up, and we will use that to talley pts. Every 4 weeks we will name a winner, and start over, but we will keep the total scores for the entire time this is up.

21's

Stand still legs apart and squat for a 1 count. once you are straight up again walk 4 steps straight ahead turn and squat 2 times, stand up walk 4 steps turn squat 3 times, and so on. Its a boxing style workout I learned from a guy while playing in the minors. He was a very good boxer in his time.

Anyway make sure that you are touching your butt is touching your heels (aka dropping down all the way, and you stand completely up before going back down.) Also make sure you are walking at a fairly quick pace. If the 21's are cake for you then thats great.
