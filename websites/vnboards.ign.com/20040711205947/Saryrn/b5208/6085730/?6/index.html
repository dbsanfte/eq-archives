The word is 'asthetic'.

In regard to the robe's desireability, it is easily explained. For a caster who can achieve 200 int without the +int on a robe, the Ishva robe IS nice. Not many items give more than 30 mana. 50 is a rare find.

For a caster who can accomplish this, +50 mana is as good as the best robe in the game: Crimson Robe of Alendine. Despite all of its stats, if you don't need the +15 int off the CROA, then all you benefit from is....

+50 mana.

Now, you might say 'but the kedge robe is +100 mana!'. Now for my money, if I were a caster with an exceptional base int, the Kedge robe would be my most prized posession. +100 mana puts the Kedge robe at the top of the list of +mana items. However there is one stipulation: It's NO DROP. Which means you have to kill Phinigel and hope he has it. I don't know about the rest of the EQ playing community, but I do _not_ like Kedge keep. It's inconviniently located, and only some of the loot is really worth the trip and wait. Most pops in this zone are several hours, so it gets old having to rally your buddies to go take down Phinigel repeatedly.

Therefore, we must fall back on the only oft-traded robe with good +mana, the Robe of the Ishva.

Hope that helps.

ps: I like the kedge robe's look way better than the Ishva/insidious actor.

pps: While we're on the topic of +mana items, the Cone of the Mystics from Sebilis also gives +50 mana, in addition to +6int. I've heard some ignorant grumblings about how this is a noob quality item. Well surprise, it's better than Rokyl's Channeling crystal, another often overlooked item.
