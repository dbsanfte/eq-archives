(ooc) Hi all!

I don't think who we are, our goals, and immediate plans have been discussed yet, so here goes...

We're roleplayers and adventurers first. Loot is secondary. We typically meet evenings around 11pm to 3am eastern, 7 nights a week. As you probably guessed, we are a shard of another guild from another server. Our mother guild was small as well, and it has spawned two guilds on FV (the other one evil). So we are doing a little recruiting to build up our numbers and welcome some new blood into our ranks.

We're hoping to build a small, close knit guild. We have been in both large and small guilds, and the small ones appeal to us more. The guild we are building here will likely be capped at 20 members. We have no grand plans for the endgame. There is far too much fun to be had before raids become an issue. When that time comes we will likely join an alliance. What we DO plan on is to build a small family we can roleplay and group with, a guild you can draw upon for craftsmen, friends you can count on for help when you need it.

If we're your kind of people, we'd be glad to have you along :-)

It looks like we have our 10 now, and we have planned on meeting Sunday evening at midnight eastern time to take the first step in forming the guild (with Monday eve at midnight as backup time). For those who have never had the pleasure, the first step means that at least 10 people (accounts) have to meet in a chat room on the chat server. When 10 are there, the leader can run a command that submits a guild application to Verrant. Once Verrant approves us, there will be a second meeting. Our 10 characters will have to gather in one place in game, and a GM will be petitioned to come create the guild. But that in game meeting is probably a week away (the application takes a few days to process).

Since there will be language barriers in game, it is best that we make our plans here. The chat room to look for is Vallya, the password will be Mindeno.

If you think you would like to join the gang, then meet us there :-)

If you have and questions, or if we need to reschedule we can do that on this thread. Vallya will almost certainly be in game before the chat room meeting Sunday/Monday, so if your around be sure to say HuFbl!

This is a draft of our guild charter. I hope this would answer your questions.

In character:

Great evil poisons the land. Since the time of creation this has been so. Through their children-races the gods have struggled against one another. Ours is a world forged by ancient enemies, a place where history is written in blood. The struggle has been eternal. Power has shifted this way or that, but always for naught. And now, now there comes a whispering, a whispering unlike any before. It speaks of a change, of a final reckoning. The struggle that was eternal now near an end! What will come to pass? No mortal can say. But in all there is the knowing that what comes will be terrible, a great conflagration that will burn, burn the defeated into oblivion, and in the doing, burn us all. The time has come to make an ending, for good or for evil to finally stake its claims to the scarred and bloodied lands of Norrath. So gather together under your banners, ye children of the gods! Make fast, and struggle to the last, for all fate now rests in your hands! Your enemy is at the gates! And woe be the vanquished! Vae Victus! Vae Victus!



Out of character:

We propose to form a guild whose theme will be to seek out and defeat evil wherever it exists. The following are our goals:

Goals:

1) To provide our membership with a pool of like minded players with whom it can group and role-play.
2) To build a player base from which a member could get advise, technical assistance, and direct in game aid when requested.
3) To help build a beneficial community and economy on the Firiona Vie server.
4) To promote role-play by active participation in RP opportunities.
5) To function within the bounds of the rules, and common sense fair play.
6) To not interfere with anyone’s right to enjoy the game.
7) To have fun!

Thanks goes to Vallya for her continual effort and great thought.
