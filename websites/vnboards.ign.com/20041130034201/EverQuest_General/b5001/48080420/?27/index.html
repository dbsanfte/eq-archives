Date Posted: 4/8/03 3:03am Subject: RE: Favorite Expansion and Worst Expansion
Best expansion Velious:

Best dungeon zones in the game here both fun to explore and fight in. Added new great looking armor textures, faction choices were great with very nice quest rewards and as long as you didn't wait till 60 to start one not a tedious unrewarding faction hunt. Only complaint was this expansion also introduced the epic time fight: I have been involved in many single encounters in Velious zones that lasted in the neighborhood of 1 to 2 hours on a single to 4 mobs with the standard 1 to 2 hours of prep and recovery time before, between and afterwards.

Runner up Ykesha,
disqualifed because with only 5 zones this one can't be counted as an expansion but you get a ton of nice features and these 5 zones are all great xp and cash areas (blue diamonds dropping off low 40- non PoP difficulty mobs) for the intended levels. Not packed with undercons and ultra-high resist mobs like the majority of Luclin. After the patch quests all worthwhile and fully functioning with very little of the extreme timesink variety. What you have here are a handful of nice originally-themed fun zones 90% of which a casual group of folks can get together and work through at their own pace with decent rewards appropriate to the challenge at a really cheap price.

Worst expansion Luclin:
If not only the fact this expansion made EQ unplayable for me for days while the patcher was MIA would get this one my vote, there's also the fact this expansion forced tons of folks to learn how to install RAM and video card upgrades on their PCs or in many cases buy whole new comps just to keep the Evercrack fix coming. Put simply, there is very little in Luclin I remember having "fun" doing- when one of the most popular hunting grounds in an expansion is a city's guards you have issues.

The majority of the zones had mobs with nearly twice the normal hps, hitting for nearly double normal damage, and of course most fun of all, twice the resistence. That works for 55+ mobs where its expected you have a full group backing you up but its a joke on mobs as low as 20+.
The bazaar a major promised feature was not was not implemented till months after release as was the new customizable/skinnable UI also intended for this expansion. The vast majority of Luclin was outdoor "grind" areas which never excite me whether they do gimmicks such as "traps" every 10 feet or the lack of air in a couple zones. Took the epic time fights of Velious, made them far worse by requirements such as needing certain bane weapons to do any (minimal) damage to a type of mob with literally million(s) of hp, that only drop in one spot, once every 8 hours roughly as one of the worst examples.

On a good note Luclin had one of the most underutilized and interesting zones in the game (and most bugged at release) grieg's end- working through here will surprise you and get a good jump out of folks every time.

BEST : Velious
CLOSE RUNNER UP : Kunark
WORST : Luclin

I'm talking about hunting zones here, the extra bank space, port stones and Nexus/Bazaar are all nice, but they don't make or break a whole expansion.

Velious is my favorite because of the variety of neat zones, some indoor, some outdoor. The faction interplay there was pretty well thought out, and you can choose which group you want to ally with. Lots of nice gear, lots of interesting quests that actually result in something worthwhile.

Kunark I like, but I almost never hunt there, though I did a lot when it was either Kunark, Antonica, or the original Planes. The epic quests turned out to be disappointing, the keyed zones a pharmfest with lots of competition and grief just to get keyed. Lots of gimpy features designed to punish certain player behaviors. (Verant hated people hunting in a dungeon in relative safety right at a zone line, where they could just zone out if it got ugly...so they made the zone in one-way only; Verant hated people pulling mobs in outdoor zones to a zone wall, or running along a zone wall, again in relative safety, so they placed hundreds of spawn points all over the zone walls.)

In addition the lack of visibility in most of Kunark from those annoying lag inducing swaying trees makes most of the outdoor areas extremely dangerous to hunt in.

The dungeons are cool though, ignoring the one way zone in thing.

Luclin, what a disappointment! Paludal Caverns is fun, and not just for the mad experience, I think it's fun to hunt there, with it's varied areas with different critters in it. Again they tried to punish certain styles of hunting. It's impossible to fear kite in Tenebrous, for example. Half the mobs can not be slowed with snare-type spells, others are immune to fear, and then mobs below 50 will start rampaging. When one green can kill a level 49 necro pet, there's a problem with the damage there. I can't even imagine trying to hunt there with a char that would get exp from those greens.

PoP: It's ok. It just doesn't excite me. At least the planes aren't all like Fear or Hate

LoY: Haven't gone to any of the 5 new zones they added yet, so I can't comment for myself. But friends have reported constant trains of stuff, and being attacked a lot by greens. The charm-slot items only seem to come from here, and by and large they seem pretty pathetic, and all of them being no-drop is just kind of dumb.
