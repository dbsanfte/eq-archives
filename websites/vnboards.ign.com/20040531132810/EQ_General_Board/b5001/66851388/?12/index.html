There are a couple of otherthings you might want to concider. When you are logged into the game the corpse 'rots' faster than when you are not logged in the game, I think it is 48 hours of in game time, 1 week out of game time.

There are various methods of finding your corpse, Necro's, shadow knights and bards can detect corpse and help find it for you. You can also buy a (bone rod) not sure if that is the name, for about 1pp from some of the older towns. This is a cast once clicky item which will turn you in the direction your corpse is in for that zone.

If you type /corpse it will confirm that your corpse is in that zone. If it is you will get one of two messages, one is your corpse is too far away the other is dragging your corpse. Or words to those effect. If you have no corpse in the zone you will be told so.

A necro or shadowknight can summon your corpse, but this is expensive for the summoner and costs at least 175pp and can only be done at higher levels.

If you are going to die and have no way of escape type /l this will give a numeric reference as to where you die. you can then use this reference to locate your corpse using the co-ordinates.
Hope this helps.
