RatHacker, you need to get silk. There are a few people that are making it, but right now Spider Silk seem to be a very rare commodity. I've killed countless Large Spiders and have gotten literally 4 spider silks.

There is a great silk tailoring guide at http://www.loreseekers.com under their Library section.

I'm doing it also, but like i said, spider silks seem to be pretty rare.

You should look into buying Raw silk or Cured silk if you can afford it. Cured silk is the monk only with great AC and such.

Also, a backpack is ok, and if you go over 15 pounds you loose AC, I wanna say 1ac per pound over, but i'm not sure. If you don't mind loosing a little ac then carry more. But don't buy leather armor, way too heavy. Get cloth if you can't afford anything else or save your money for silk
