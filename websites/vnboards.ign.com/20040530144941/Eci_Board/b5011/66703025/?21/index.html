"Or, you could look into a Powerbook. They have EQ for Mac now"

EQ for mac runs on completely mac dedicated servers. To be more clear, you can not log into the E'ci server, nor can you access your existing characters. If I understand correctly, you can't even use your existing account to log into EQ for Mac.

Aside from account / server issues, the mac laptops are not the best choice. While IBM's G5 chipset have certainly closed the gap in processing power, the Powerbooks still use Motorolla's crappy G4 chipset, with a blazing top speed of 1.33GHz. (at this point Mac fanatics often cite the RISC vs. CISC argument... yadda yadda... slow is still slow)

We use Mac's at work, and I've got a one year old titanium powerbook (hey, we're design professionals! Whadya expect). Overall, I enjoy the operating system, and the stylized design of mac hardware. But when it comes to hardcore gaming, PeeCee boxes always win.

Oh... another interesting phenomenon... I've played regular EQ since just before luclin was launched, and have thouroughly enjoyed the game. Loggin into EQ for mac, I became very nauseous, and sea sick within minutes. Same game. Same models. One powered by DirectX, the other Powered by OpenGL... OpenGL makes me feel ill... can't explain why.

In a nutshell... A mac laptop will not work in this particular instance.
