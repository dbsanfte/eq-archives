One things I've seen many times thrown at CTV is that we're two boxing.

Sure, there are some (read some, not many) people that have 2 computers and will perhaps play their main and maybe another character.

Now tell me that no one in your guild two boxes it, in general or in raids and you know you're not telling the truth.

As a matter of fact, I very recently witnessed a person from Valon, three-box (no, not two, THREE). In Velketor. Holding Left-wall with a cleric, holding back wall with an enchantress and a shadowknight running between the two hogging and killing spawns.

Of course I know, three boxing at a raid is not practical and could be even detrimental to the raid, but people in glass houses should not throw stones. Valon guild members two box, CTV guild members two box, and so do, I am sure, members of just about any other guild.

Regarding "Zerging" etc.

CTV has, unlike some other guilds, until recently had a very closed recruitment policy. This doesn't mean that we didn't add new people to the guild. It means that we were very, very particular to whom we added.

Regardless of whether this was the wrong or right policy, with general attrition (people leaving to play other games, quiting EQ altogether) our numbers dwindled to an extent that we felt it would be in the guilds best interest to open the recruitment process, or at least streamline it.

This doesn't mean that we're allowing every Tom, Dick and Harriet who didn't totally ruin their reputation, (Kanrne would come to mind) to join the guild. No, we are still rather strict on who does and who does not join.

The result is, that we are once again at our pre-luclin capacity. And yes, we are again at our pre-Luclin capability. What does this mean? In simple words, we can do, and will do, any and all targets that are available, anywhere, anyplace, anytime.

Ridicule us all you like; redicule stems from fear. What fear you might ask? Fear of getting beaten to spawns perhaps? Like we used to do? Oh yes, you'll remember. That was the time we decided to be nice and set up a rotation.

Well, Lamorta has it right. Lets be adults about this and make some arrangements, so we won't have another creator situation. It'll be in everyone's best interest.

Just one word of advice, if you're going to make arrangements, they'd better get stuck to this time because we're done getting the shaft.
