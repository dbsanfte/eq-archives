Most info like that is available either on the Sony forums or website..here's a cut and paste from there:


Zone Alarm:

Open and click on Program Control.

Click add and find the eqgame.exe file.

Simply give it access to the internet and select the option that it changes frequently.

You may also want to add the range of IP's to the trusted site of the server that you play on

Click on Firewall and select the Zones tab.

Click on the add button on the bottom and add a range of IP's.

You will now need to find the IP addresses of the server you play on. Here is an example for Terris Thule, which is eqworld 84. The eqzone thing is due to the fact that their are about 33 or 35 servers per each world server.

ping eqzone-84-01.989studios.com the IP is: 64.37.149.106

ping eqzone-84-34.989studios.com the IP is: 64.37.149.229

Note, if you do not know the number for your server type: ping servername.everquest.com.

You don't actually need to do any of the IP stuff. If you do what Renx said, it will work. There is only really one issue to deal with. And that is you cant enter your pass/login and just press login if you havent given access rights for EQ yet. If you do, the machine will likely freeze as ZA and EQ are fighting..:) To spell it out:

What you do is:
1) Start EQ patcher
2) Click Play
3) When you get to login screen, press ALT+ENTER to play in a window.
4) Now type your login and password.
5) Zonealarm will popup twice, and you can select Allow Acces (and Remember this setting bla bla...). Either by ALT+R followed by ALT+Y or by releasing the mouse from EQ window and clicking.
6) Done. ALT+ENTER to play fullscreen again.


Repeat every time eqgame.exe gets patched.
