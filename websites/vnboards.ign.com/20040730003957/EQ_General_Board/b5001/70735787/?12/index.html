Lag isn't affected by system specs. It's deals with the internet and the time it takes to transfer large amounts of data between you, the server and everyone else. You will experience this at some point or another on all online games. Even if you had the top of the line computer and a T1 connection. There is just no way around that.

I have almost the same specs as you, but more ram. And a cable modem. It still lags for me. But it's not a big deal.

And now I get it in POK which I used to not have before the DX9 engine. I also get video lag when my weapon procs (Steel Rod of the Knight) or whenever I am in a group and get alot of spells casting at once. The video lag in those situations is only noticable when I try to move my mouse for it is slow at these times but everything on screen looks fine other than that.

In POK I get actual stutters depeding on how many people are in the zone and in Bazaar all the time.

The new DX9 engine put this game in the dumper for me. I get missing mobs, NPCs, Players. Warping of all sorts. Zone into a zone and all ground is gone. You just float there.

All this no matter what my ping is. It could be 50/100 or 150/1000 it does not matter.
