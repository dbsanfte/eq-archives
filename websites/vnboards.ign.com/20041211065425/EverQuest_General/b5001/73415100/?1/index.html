We want you to help make EverQuest Vault the best source for EverQuest info on the net!

No matter what you want to do, we’ll find a spot for you! (Unless it deals with making up lame rhymes, that’s my job)

Here are a couple of the more specialized areas that we’re looking for help in:

Guide & Strategy Writers

Responsibility: Write guides to whatever you would like! If your specialty is the level 25-29 game, write a guide for it! We will accept anything and everything as long as the info is good.

Community News Reporters

Responsibility: Are you level 65 with a gazillion AA and keep up with all the latest drama on every server? If so, we want you! Come report what’s going on each server. Who killed what first, what guild just advanced to where – you name it!

EverQuest Online Adventures Reporters

Responsibility: Post the latest and greatest news dealing with SOE’s Playstation 2 EverQuest title.

We are always open to suggestions for new writers. If you want to do anything else, such as general news or fan fiction, send me an e-mail as well!

Please note all positions are purely volunteer, but established staffers get some perks. And please remember we do not cover EverQuest 2.

If you’re interested in any of these positions e-mail draknorr@vaultnetwork.com with what you would like to do.
