Well, I took my level 12 paladin into Runnyeye. Most of the stuff on the top level was dark blue and was neting me roughly half a blue per kill.

However, I noticed that the mobs, including ones that just popped so they had no buffs, seemed to regen at an accelerated rate. I was swinging a 13dmg 26dly 1hs (no haste) and in the period of time between swings (I watched for the ones that were back to back hits)I saw mobs regen up to 6 percent health. It wasn't in one tick of regen, it was several small bumps. Definately be careful if you're trying to solo in there.
