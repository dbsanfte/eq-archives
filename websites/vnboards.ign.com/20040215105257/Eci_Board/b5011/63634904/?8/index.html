Dear Zeta,

I am a former member of the Crimson Legacy from several years ago, character name Llyrean. Even though Razail, Grant Butenhoff, no longer plays Everquest and hasn't been a member of the CL from years, I thought I would inform you of his passing.

Grant succumbed to cancer this morning. Since I knew him mainly through gaming I thought this would be a good way to help deal with his loss; by letting those who also had the privilege of gaming with him and experiencing his generosity of spirit and comradeship know of his passing from this world.

In remembrance of fellowship and good times,

Corey de Danann
