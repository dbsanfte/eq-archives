12.9% of 1,046,705 state prisoners used pot at the time of the offense (1997) =135,024 prisoners
18.9% of 88,018 federal prisoners used pot at the time of the offense (1997) =16,635 prisoners
18.5% of 389189 local prisoners used pot at the time of the offense (1998) = 72,000 prisoners
135,024 + 16,635 + 72,000= 223,659 prisoners used marijuana at the time of the offense
11.1 million people smoked marijuana in the past year (1997)
223,650 users committed a crime when on pot out of 11.1 million people who smoke pot overall = 2.01%
