It's a subjective call, but I'd say (in order of importance):

- Shadows of Luclin: eases travel somewhat by allowing you to travel to/from The Nexus at certain Wizard spires (specifically, Greater Faydark, North Karana, Toxxulia Forest, and Dreadlands). Also, it provides The Bazaar which greatly improves your ability to get good items for your cash and also to make cash by setting yourself up as a vendor. Luclin also has a lot of great zones for all levels (not to mention that Luclin is also what provides the improved player-character models).

- Planes of Power: eases travel across the entire game by providing portal stones between the Plane of Knowledge and all of the newbie cities (and a few other zones). The downside is, that's all the expansion is going to give you since the remaining zones for Planes of Power are level 46+.

The last two are Scars of Velious and Legacy of Ykesha. If you aren't planning on getting to Level 30 anytime soon, then Scars of Velious won't really be of any use since it's primarily just a geography expansion for Level 30+.

Legacy of Ykesha might be useful to you if you want to play a Frog or want the newer UI enhancements such as larger bank space or quest-logging. (It also has a Map feature that can be pretty useful, but they're about to give that to everyone for free this week).
