I agree. No transfers ever. This server is great. Even better for the casual gamers.

I am also an on and off EQ player. Been playing since beta 3. The hardest part about coming back each time, and what ultimately drives me away again, is that every character on every server under level 50 is a twink of a twink of a twink of a main pimped to the gills with uber guild junk items, which are still miles ahead of whatever I may be using.

Stromm has completely renewed my interest in EQ. I'm not talking a "Ya I'm into playin EQ for a month or so again" but a "I really want to dedicate my time to developing a long term character here" .

There's lots of true newbies. The sense of community that has been long overshadowed by uberness is here again. Sure, people are trying to sell off useless junk for 5pp in the bazaar, but that's half the fun. Everyone is helping everyone. Old weapons and armor are being given away again rather than just destroyed or sold to a vendor. The community that has long been overshadowed by tremendous uberness is back again.

Don't get me wrong. I know there is a lot of generosity on all the other servers. Not once have I played on my reg servers as a lowbie and not been at least buffed up the wazoo by some mega cleric passerby, or thrown a bunch of items, etc. I'm not complaining at all about that... It's just nice to see it on a lower level. Everyone on an even playing field. It's not about fighting with this guild or that guild over who gets to raid where tonight. It's about finding a good group, and getting to know the people you're playing with again. Learning zones you've probably never been in since you've spent the last 4 months in BoT.

Sure, the zones are crowded. Very crowded (120+ in Paludal during peak)... but it sure beats running around South K for an hour trying to find a group while maybe 8 people pass through. EQ is fun again. Lots of people hammering through levels, but it's slower than I'm used to seeing. I'd say under 20% of the server population are power gamers. Lots of people trying to get ahead of the curve to get into some less crowded zones, but that's not tough. Seems that the majority of the population is in the low to mid 20s. Most people I've played with seem to be casuals. This server is shaping up real nice, and I look forward to a long future here.
