Culture Shock
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As some of you are probably aware I moved to Stormhamer with the opening of Legends. I never intended it to be permanent, but the goings of over a few days made me decide to try making it so. I scouted out some of the guilds, weeding out the obvious 'jerks' or 'wannabes' - can't stand raiding and socializing with people that know jack about the game.
Finally found a couple guilds I thought might/would be good. Legendary Twelve seemed to have the most solid foundation of ideas, and from the descriptions had the most chance of surviving the longterm and maintaing civility internally. I was approached by Enrapture, I'd actually ruled these guys out but had like 7 tells from them in the course of < 48 hours and they seemed pretty sincere in wanting mages, I figured "wth, why not" ... big mistake.

First off.. Legendary Twelve, seemed like nice people - I went on one raid with them where was supposed to be Ssra Temple and a Rhag but it was turned into a Va'Dyn killing for lack of the proper classes. I wasn't really a probationary member so wasn't invited to the guild, and w/o the guild channel there was little to no communcations on tactics or goals, and actually those with guildchat seemed really disorganized. MT was never a constant thing, no one knew who to assist.. but I had C3 at least ^_^ I dont' know.. seemed like nice people, but lacked serious raid organization, while it helps to be raiding together a raid can still be organized properly and communcation given.

Enrapture.. omg.. I really dont' know where to begin. I just spent all day raiding with these guys, and I can honestly say I've never felt so insulted in my life. "sit down and shutup" was a term used often in guildchat, you were discouraged from using guildchat in fact and congratulations weren't encouraged for people that were merited loot. It was the third time in all my raiding life I've seen "NO PETS" (maybe second) just before a large encounter.. why?

This really pissed me off btw. I sent a tell to the person who spammed me with inquiries to join, also the person who instructed "NO PETS". I tried to communicate how on Xev mages are considered to a degree 'experts' in their class.. just like you dont' tell a warrior "TURN AUTOATTACK ON" you dont' tell a mage "NO PETS". He informed me "this mob taps and your pet will be RIPOSTED", as if I don't understand the game.. I've only lead raids for the last 6 months and was a member of one of the largest most powerful guilds on my original server before that. What a moron IMO, I know from experience pets can and are useful even on tap proccing mobs.. heck I've used pets on VS and still do when we kill him on Xev, the key is where you attack.. duh - I informed him of my experiences politely and was only sent back an even more rude statement.

In all my time leading raids I've never instructed a person on how to play their class, because most people understand their class and where they fit in, for me this extends to mages and any pet class for that matter. People ask if I want them to have a pet I ask them "do you want a pet?" They normally don't summon so I'll send them a tell later "where's your pet?" and they tell me they're uncomfortable or whatever.. not a big deal, I love pets though and if you know how to use them and you understand how the game works a smart mage/pet class can offer something with them.

To sum up that experience I'd say I was quite jarred. Never have I felt so unappreciated. Mages are so much more than summoning rods, it's not all about efficiency and if it were I know personally I would've quit a long, very long, time ago. To any mages on Stormhammer considering Enrapture, run.. Uthvuud a magician I'd grouped with before joining Enrapture for that raid suggested that to me, and I paid a price for not listening.

Stormhammer is filled with farm more 55-60's than I had first thought. Most mid-level zones are almost empty. During peak times Dreadlands has 20-30 people in it! I don't forsee a healthy server and it's why I've chosen to cancel my legends subscription. I burned some bridges with my leave so where I head off in EQ I dont' know.. probably not far.
