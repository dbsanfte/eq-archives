everything I have been hoping for, and some. PvP combat is a

is painfull. The game revolves around 2 things. Guilds and
Money, and you can't run a guild without alot of money. Just
starting a guild costs about 2 million gold. The cities are
god awfully amazing. The movement is fluid, the gameplay is
unbelievable. Leveling is a piece of cake, exploration,
scouting, and politics are some of the most powerfull
weapons.
