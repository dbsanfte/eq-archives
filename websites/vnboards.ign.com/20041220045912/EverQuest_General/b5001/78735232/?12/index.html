From the days back in 1999 in EQ...

Do you miss:

Staring at the book to meditate until level 35 before you can just sit and regain mana?

Zoning into Mistmoore or Unrest and being greeted by 20+ corpses on the ground?

Screaming profanity at Guard Sheg for kill stealing your bat?

Screaming profanity at Deputy Budo for kill stealing your giant wasp?

Screaming profanity at a roaming Dragoon for kill stealing your skeleton?

Yelling "Camp Check" in Befallen and getting 15 replies?

Watching Sergeant Slate duke it out with a Griffon and you were cheering for the Griffon?

Watching a group Derv 3 in North Ro scatter like a kicked ant hill when a hill giant shows up and starts pounding people?

Seeing a train of specters in Oasis massacre the beaches?

Killing Hadden for the J-Boots drop?

Rubicite Armor dropping like rain?

Having a party in Najena where she pops every 20 minutes to try and get that darn book and not her robe?

Camping a certain frog in Guk for that darned Ghoulbane drop you seem to never get?

Charming a rat in West Freeport, buff that shiznat outta it, then turn it loose and watch it kill noobs who thought it was an easy kill?

Drop Mod Rods on the ground and watch newbs pick them up and self destruct 30 seconds later?

Drop a sword on the ground as it always points North to get un lost in a zone?

Paying 90pp for a full suit of Banded, the uber armor?

Offering to pay for a Bind before you hunt?

The GM visits to Lake Rath to break up the Aviak Guard Camp arguments?

I could go on and on and on... hehehe
