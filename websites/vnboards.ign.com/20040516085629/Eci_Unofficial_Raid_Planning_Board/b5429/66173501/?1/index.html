Well, for whatever reason, we were unable to muster enough force to take on the spawned Trakanon for Tindril. I sent out a record 87 tells, at least twice each, but we came up short ONE tank and one cleric after two hours of trying. Almost no one on my lists was even online. I credit the beginning of Spring, the 5th Anniversary quests, and just bad luck.

The GOOD news is that many, many of my regulars showed up, plus some newcomers, and everyone stayed the whole two hours and helped me recruit.

This is the time to get MORE eager, determined, and enthusiastic. If we let the fizzle get us down, we will REDUCE our chances of future success, and we don't want to do that.

Regulars: Fjorde (Meeowmix), Knifymoloko, Shaydad, Kloude, Marruk, Noctornia (Shassiash), Purrsalot, Soulron (Bhangle) Diunah, Cassady, Clarisia, Raygefist, Tindril (of course), Quinnt, Saryma, Saneril. Newcomers Myon, Alwyn, Sylbane, Fada, Ryleigh, Nixk also showed up.

Next time, Cassady is going to show me a place where we can pull Trakanon to avoid some banishment problems (apparently, he can be pulled to one of his banishment spots, but I need Cassady to show me where it is -- I had thought it was the wrong side of the locked door way back by the shrooms)

TINDRIL remains top of the list until she wins (rules clarification next post)

SARYMA and CASSADY are LP now and will slot in order ahead of Quinnt.

BARD POINTS to Tindril, Cassady, Quinnt, Saryma, Shassiash, Meeowmix, and newcomer Ryleigh.
