The MMORPG industry is still growing, and more importantly learning about its customers.

Games like DAoC have offered fix to things that were incredibly frustrating to players, such as loss of levels, CR runs, or difficult binding spots of melees. On the other hand, they lack the content a game like EQ has built in the years it has been around for.
