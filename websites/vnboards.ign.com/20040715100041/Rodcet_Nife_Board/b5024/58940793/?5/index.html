The VN Forums are everything under the VN Boards link at the top.

That's in contrast with the other half of the forums, over on the IGN side.

The Vault Network (VN) Boards support the Vault which consists primarily of the RPGVault and ActionVault

The IGN Boards support The IGN Sites

You can think of the primary division between the two as being along single/multi player lines. In general, the Vault supports multi-player online games, and IGN covers Consoles(PS2, XBOX, Gamecube, and Gameboy), PC Games, and networked FPS type games. That's not hard and fast, but generally true.

The IGN side also has a lot of sites devoted to "Entertainment" including Films, DVDs, Music, Cars, and Gaming Gear. Also a few oddities such as Wireless Gaming and N-Gage

The Vault Network was originally a separate company - Snowball - that was acquired by IGN. They also acquired Voodoo Extreme which has it's own Forums and operates as a separate entity as well with it's own Administration.

------------

On the Vault Side there is an overall Vault Network Manager - Aurum-VN. He's responsible for the whole Vault Side of the world, including sites like EQ Vault.

Each Site has a manager, who is responsible for the Web Content.

You can think of the Forums as a site full of Message Boards. There is an Admin under Aurum responsible for the Forums. That would be -Mithan-. Then there is a group of Forum Managers. That's the list you see in the sticky up top. They are responsible for the operation of the Boards, of which the EQ Boards are a part.

Individual mods are assigned as needed, to Games, or to individual boards depending on traffic.
