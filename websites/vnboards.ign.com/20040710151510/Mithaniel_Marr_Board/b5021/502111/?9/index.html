 		Date Posted: 11/22/99 4:04am Subject: Welcome to a free market economy on a crowded server.
A petition like this will never get anything changed at Verant. As the server gets more and more crowded, the desire to acquire items increases, though only a fixed number of items can be dropped in a given period of time. With more and more players earning cash elsewhere, but still wanting these items, they are offering large amounts of this cash for the stuff they want.

Supply and Demand.

Over time, as more and more people want these items, the price will continue to rise, since there is little if any cash outflux in the economy.

Inflation.

About 4 months ago, I bought a pair of executioner hoods for 300pp each. I saw someone selling one the other day who said the minimum bid he would accept was 1500pp. Know what? He'll probably get that too.

Try to buy a braided cinch cord or impskin gloves as a caster, but be prepared to shell out 1500 to 2k at least. Heck, the cord is even the common drop on the monster and its green to me.

These are all simply symptoms of overcrowding, and there is little we can do other than encourage others to quit or play AC. =)

I see thousands of low level monsters that nobody hunts in tens of different zones, yet the waiting lines in level 30+ zones make this game not worth playing on weekends. I dont even bother playing weekday evenings anymore, I will log on at midnight PST and play until 3AM and thats it... its the only time that any of my characters can find groups.

Oh, sure, I got invited to a PoH group today but that is just cuz they needed a wizard. Talk about pointless, I only recognized 6 names of the 24 people there, everyone else I had never heard of. I am sure there are hundreds of level 46+ players on this server now, too bad the game can only let about 100 of them adventure for xp/loot at the same time.
