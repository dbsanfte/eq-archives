lets say 4 mobs with a theoretical DPS of 60, cast Tigir's on them it would be reduced to 30 per mob if it stuck on all of them, so 120DPS overall.
It has a cast time of 5 so in that five they've done 60 * 4 * 5 = 1200 damage
and then in the next 25 seconds they've done 30 * 4 * 25 = 3000
so 4200 damage in 30 seconds. Barring going over and casting Turgur's on all of the mobs.

Now, since it takes 3 seconds to cast Turgur's on the first mob so in that then its 60 * 4 * 3 = 720

then 60 * 2 * 9 + 15 * 2 * 9 = 1350
then 60 * 9 + 15 * 3 * 9 = 945
so overall its an approximated 4770 damage done over the time it takes to get them all slowed, barring resists and fizzles.

Congratulations, you've just saved a superior heal (minus improved healing).

then 30 * 3 * 9 + 15 * 9 = 945
then 30 * 2 * 9 + 15 * 2 * 9 = 810
then 30 * 9 + 15 * 3 * 9 = 675
so overal thats 3990 damage

But that brings in the matter of mana efficiency, its 350 + 250 * 4 = 1350 to do the Tigir's/Turgur's combo

and its just 1000 mana to just Turgur the mobs.

But lets see the sustained DPS of these mobs slowed just by Tigir's and then slowed by Turgur's one by one.

ok, so Tigir's at the beginning and nothing else has 4200 damage in the first 30 seconds and 30DPS per mob

Turgur's has 4770 damage in the first 30 seconds and 15DPS per mob

Lets say these mobs take 1:30 to kill for ease of math.

The Tigired mobs do 120DPS for 1:00 (taking down the time since its been half a minute already) so thats 120 * 60 = 7200 damage in that minute
next it will be 90DPS for the whole 90 seconds so 90 * 90 = 8100

next its a piddly 30DPS for 90 seconds so 30 * 90 = 2700
so overall its 27,600 damage for the entire fight.

Now, the Turgurd mobs are doing 60DPS for 1:00 so thats 60 * 60 = 3600

so overall its 16,470

Now since I feel like it we'll do the Tigir's/Turgur's combo.
