Okay so last night I returned to EQ. Har Har Har... I've heard all the jokes by now =)
I don't know about the rest of the world, as far as last night went... it seemed a bad night for group members (Tavern members excluded). I sign on and hook up with Tlaen and Seani, and then get Kaiyen a 13th rogue to join up and we break Orc1. Well about 5 min into it, seani has to leave . So we aquire an 8th warrior Xzaria. Now I do believe that the only words I heard from that one the *WHOLE* time was, invite him/invite her. Invite that guy, we need more group memebers. This was of course between her constant looting of everything. Even in the middle of combat she broke off from the fight to loot something. Worse yet, Tlaen was taking a beating and she ignored him to loot a corpse, even when requested by me to help him. Wind up inviting a 13th necro iksar to help things out a bit and she stops making exp. And the whining commenced from there. Though it was her idea to get him in the group, cause we needed more members! (sarcasm)
On a bright side, I had some comedy relief. A pair of level 4's hung around the camp. We let them have 1 Cent. It almost killed em both. They would attack everything we attacked, though due to party damage I doubt they got even 1 orc worth of exp. Spent the whole time dragging MOB's onto the group (mainly myself and another spellcaster) and asking for a group invite.
Finally salvation! Garthe calls on the guild chat wanting a few good soldiers for the Derv Camp in WC. So leaving a few apologies and vague excuses Tlaen and I hopped the zone to Garthe.
Well looking at this derv camp of 6 Derv's. We were a lil wary. So Garthe contacts an enchanter, who takes the better part of an hour to get there, from EC. Don't ask I don't know all the details, I think it had something to do with a death and LD. In the meantime our party aquires a 13th wiz. Pretty powerful too, spent the whole time casting her strongest spell and meddding. 3/4's of that time was the medding. Now I understand the whole, casters don't get to play much concept Well she and Garthe decide the enchanter's a no show and find a 12th level mage to join. Though just before we jump this camp the enchanter shows. Having tried so hard to get here I couldn't possibly turn her down. So we are up to a party of 6.
12 mage Me
12 mage
14 Monk Garthe
11 Warrior Tlaen
13 Wizard
12(14?) Enchanter

Now, I've never fought the Derv's, but I'm thinking 8 (including pets) on 6 is a pretty sure thing. So I'm wondering why we are waiting and powering up to FM and max HP's. Though, due to lack of knowledge I deferred. Finally after 1.5 hrs we are ready to attack, when another party of 4 people come along and ask to share the camp. We agree to break the camp together. After some brief co-ordination and preparation we attack the camp.
12 on 6. It was a slaughter, I actually feel sorry for the poor Derv's. Not to mention guilty for killing a derv that looked like our beloved seani (couldn't do it, wound up breaking the attack and looking away, Garthe killed her!). I'm not a knight or a paladin. But does anyone else feel there was an honor (or challenge) in that kind of fight? =) I mean the person with lowest health was Tlaen at about half life. He went INTO the fight with only 4 bubbles though!
So all in all, wasn't that great an evening. Cept for the one fight Tlaen and I got in back at Orc1, someone in the party told Tlaen to sit out a fight due to his low health =) I was OOM, so I told Tlaen to get his woodelf butt in there while I bandaged him from behind =) Who says Tlaen can't fight without HP's?! Good show too...
Overall, wasn't a bad evening, just no challenge to it. How was everyone else?

(I ask a moment of silence for our poor departed Derv's =))
