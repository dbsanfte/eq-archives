I understand the Fellowship of Brothers took out the Manaetic Behemoth on Friday. By my calculations, MB will be up again about 9:45ish on 1/5/03, we can try to take him out again.

Please sign up if you are level 60+ and want to give him a try. All loot will be distributed by Elder House points, except flags which are outside the points system. Do not sign up as a "maybe" for no-shows get -4 points, and signups only get 1 point. Points and points provisions are visible at the http://pub28.ezboard.com/felderhousefrm5 site.

Please note MB may be a no-show, so that we could not take him. If he is not up by 10pm EST, I will cancel the raid, record points, and see if enough are still around to declare another target. Raid assembly is, however, still to be at 9pm EST so I can form groups and such.

To take out the MB, I will need lots of damage dealers, a healer per group, and a minimum of 4 enchanters.
