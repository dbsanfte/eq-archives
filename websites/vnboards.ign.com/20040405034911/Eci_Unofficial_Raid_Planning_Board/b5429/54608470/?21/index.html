Public PoG Raid:

Date: Monday, August 11
Start: 7:30PM Eastern
RL’s: Flemina and Reptilus
Channel: mondaygrowth

Sign-up: Feel free to sign-up here with your NAME, CLASS, LEVEL, and whether you have POP or not. Walk-ons are always welcome. We will use PoP raid feature, and if you don’t have it, you will have to be grouped with others who do not have PoP.

Set-up: Meet on the lake edge near the bridge of PoG tower in the Wakening Lands before the scheduled raid start ~(700, -1300). If you have unwelcome friends trailing you, please /ooc a heads up.

Loot: Only RL’s and designated players will loot any mob. All looted items will be handed to Flemina and Reptilus. Armor “first pick” will go to the one with the most growth pieces. Be prepared to prove this by bringing your growth armor to the raid. Ties in the number of armor pieces will be settled with a random roll. Each class will handle its own list, to save time on set-up (eg. mages handle the mage armor, etc.)

Rules: You will be fed to a pit of rabid goatmen and evicted from the raid if you endanger the raid or exhibit extremely poor behavior. In addition, you won’t be invited back. Clicking on the mushroom tree will cause you to zone into PoG. While this may not mean immediate death, it is a setback for the raid. Please, do not get near the mushroom tree until ready to zone in.

FINAL WARNING: You will become KOS in Kelethin (that’s the High Elf city in Greater Feydark) after one raid here if you actively attack or heal during combat. Tunare is the mother of the high elves, after all. If this doesn't bother you, though, come on down!

Definitions: RL (Raid Leader), GL (Group Leader), MT (Main Tank), ST (Secondary Tank), TT (Third Tank), PT (Pull Team), VT (Vendor Trash)
