A lot of hybrids can solo very well.
But really, it is very gear or tactic dependant.
I don't know about paladins solo. In the basement of Halls of Honor, the armors are undead, so they can probably single and solo them for good exp at higher levels.
BL, Ranger, and SK all solo well.
Ranger usually just kiting and shooting arrows.
BL has pet tank, and heal/buffs it.
SK is very versatile. Can kite or agro kite (keep agro and spells, while pet chases it) , or straight head to head tanking.
Fear kiting isnt a big option, because it requires large areas where you won't get adds.
Head to Head though, again, really depends on gear. There is a huge difference from me soloing in MPG while lfg (a zone in the oow expansion), and a non-raid geared sk trying the same.
