Capitalism - makes Norrath great. And "the invisible hand of the marketplace" works.

Should we all help each other? YES Is selling a needed product evil? Well, is the product itself (the Queen blade or the King note) evil? No. So why is selling it evil? They have perfected their tradeskill and are marketing their product.

What's right for one person, may not be right for another. I don't understand why any of the name-calling or derogatory comments are necessary. If the ONLY way to get the piece was this manner and we had to pay the price no matter what, that would be a different story. But as long as we have options, we all have the option to say "yes" or "no" to the offer.
