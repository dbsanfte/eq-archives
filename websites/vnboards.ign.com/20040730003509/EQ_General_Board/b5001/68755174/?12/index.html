Good questions actually.

A) Ac2 doesn't have zones while EQ does. If you see something in Ac2 ... like a mountain ... you can run to it. Not so in EQ. Zones do have an advantage in that they give you a completely different look and feel from zone to zone. In A2 you can run from a bad pull. In Eq the MOB's will chase you forever unless you zone. In AC2 you can solo pretty good with almost all classes. In Eq only a few classes can solo from 0 to 60. AC2 is a skill based game that you get to pick what you want your char to be ... even though the hero skills really messed that up. In EQ its strictly class based and a level 20 warrior is pretty much like everyother level 20 warrior. In Ac2 you can logon for a short time and get something done. In Eq ... from what I've heard and its the one thing that keeps me away ... you have to spend tons of time esp in the later levels to do anything. I may be wrong about that though, but thats what I've been told.

B) EQI might lose some players but since EQII is a completely different game I don't see EQI going away or becoming barren anytime soon.

C)& D) IMO, everyone should play Eq at least once. Its the King of MMORPG's and has been for a while. There is a reason for that.

Calliv,

Since your questions have been answered, let me follow up with some other information you might be interested in...

What class to play? Try a beastlord. You can solo, but are also welcome in groups. Not a very difficult class to play. Buff yourself and your pet and go kill something =).

What should I do first? The newbie quests! They will make your first 10 levels just fly by and you get some very decent armor out of it. Dont bother with the weapon part of the quest though, its awful. You can do the race specific armor quests, as well as going to shadeweavers thicket and doing the newbie quests there as well. As soon as you save up 10 or 15 plat head over to the bazaar and pick yourself up a pair of Polished Steel Ulaks. These are great little weapons that will serve you well for quite a while.

Where are good places to hunt? Your starting zone is just fine! You can do the newbie armor quests and get decent xp, and the mobs are not too hard. Once you start getting up to around lvl 10 or 12, or just bored of doing newbie quests, you can try your luck at some of the low level mobs in Paludal Caverns. Stay there until you are lvl 26!! Best xp in the game! They try The Overthere after that. Overthere xp is good up until 45!

Shadesawhatsit? Paludaldawhosit? eq.allakhazams.com is a great resource. Use it, learn it, love it! Also, try www.eqatlas.com to take a look at norrath, and if you dont mind a little spoiler... try eq-toolbox.com for downloadable maps of all the zones.

Anything else? Dont be afraid to ask ppl for help in the game! For gods sake, dont buy an uber character on playerauctions, and DONT let any bored rich character twink you or getting new items wont be very rewarding.
