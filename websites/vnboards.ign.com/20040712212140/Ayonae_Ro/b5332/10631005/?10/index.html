Hi,
I would think, yes it is worth your money. I have played AC as well and like their stats system more than EQ, but trust me, EQ is much more exciting. At least in my opinion. You will use items to raise your starting stats throughout your characters life. There are also many spells that raise your stats. Also your hitpoints, mana, attack, defense raise each level. Your skills increase as you use them.

Yes, the game costs basically $10 per month. There are a few PVP servers, but the majority of them are no pvp.

Hey Radius,

I have played AC since the start and EQ since it was released and can say they both have great qualities. EQ's social system is a lot better if you like to group. AC is a game where I have played for hours and not seen another person. In EQ, that doesnt happen. People are everywhere and friendships develop quickly. On the downside, to me EQ doesn't have a storyline at all. AC is nice becuase it has a monthy update to the storyline to make things interesting. EQ does seem to be getting better as more GM event quests are being done. All in all, I guess the best recommendation I can give is buy it and use the free month to see if you like it. I think the basic EQ game is like 10$ now so good luck and hope to see you in game.
