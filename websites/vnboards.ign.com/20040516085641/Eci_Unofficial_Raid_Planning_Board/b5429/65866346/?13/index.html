My experience has been that only wizard lures would land on VS. I do not have Strike of Solusek however, so cannot speak to how it would land on him. Lure of Ro is 1070 with 4.5 sec cast and 2.5 sec recovery time not counting focus stuff. Figure about 1200 points every 6 seconds per wizard so thats 9600 points for 2 wizards in 24 seconds.

VS's proc goes off on his Ripostes (front 90 degrees arc) and not every time but pretty close. He does maybe 300 a round melee, and I have seen his proc go off 4 times in a round (yep - 6k, killed a 65 SK..unlucky RNG)

Enchanter Rune (850) blocks the heal and damage to the MT, up to the amount of the Rune of course, and Wizard Spellshield (680) blocks the damage to the MT but not the heal for VS.

Oh yeah and as to spawn, consensus is he spawns 72 hours plus or minus 12 hours from when he was last killed or server reset.

Your best bet is to do what I had to do, park a tracker alt (maxed tracking druid or better) at the zone in and check track periodically. When you spot him up call out the troops, he usually lives no more than an hour. He has some nice drops besides epic peices (very nice int caster gloves etc).
