YOU MIGHT BE OLD SCHOOL IF:

1. You played EQ before 2000( A given)

2. You aquired a stein from a quest in inno swamp with +15 cha - before they changed it

4. You remember the very first sucessful dragon raid in SOLB(extra points if you were actually THERE)

5. You felt a tremendous sense of satisfaction and recognition from killing Dorn while everyone else was running like a screaming little girl from him.(ok it's not old school, but I never get tired of killing him, and I'll go 2 zones out of my way sometimes just to do it)

6. You spent level 20-35(or any part of) in High keep, and thought it was the best XP anywhere.

7. You remember killing the High Guard cats on the island off Tox

8. You killed a Sand Giant with a Dozen or so level 20 necros (before the nerf) and lived to tell about it(still a myth- never could verify that)

9. You were the envy of everyone with your LEET SMR, EE bag, Staff of Writhing, Efreeti boots, etc(insert archaic LEET item here)
