I am outnumbered, but I am not afraid.
I am outranked, and outclassed, but the solemn depths of fear do not touch my soul.
I see you foolish followers of light all around me, and I pity you. Your pride, your arrogance, your longing for order in a world that is built upon chaos. I pity you all, and I wonder if you might ever be saved.
You live your lives in humble servitude, espousing piety and raising hearty calls for noble causes, and restraints.
Truly, my heart is filled with sorrow for your clouded minds, for you will never be free. I know my place in this world, beneath the mighty gods and lords alike. But to revere them? The very thought is repulsive. They, who would cast shadows upon the earth, and place danger all around, deserve no reverence from me.
And you, holy peons. Living your life as slaves to the will of those above. You flaunt yourselves as much as the next man, but then claim to be pious? You slay the beasts of your divine gods, and maintain the belief that you are still serving them? And most of all, you face me down, and proudly state that you are 'good' incarnate. Do you not see your sins? It is not your beliefs that offend me, in all honesty, but your hypocrisy.
I hear your threats, followers of light. And they delight me. They show me the darkness in your hearts. The deception, the malice, the hate, and the evil that exists there, and in your minds, that is so obvious to me. I cannot help but smile when you make your threats. Foolish followers of light...your pride and your hate are in themselves acts of evil.
We're not so different, you and I, as you can see.
And so, it is not for being followers of light that I shall seek the ruin of Pious Sodality, but instead for their hypocrisy. And in ridding the world of a few of the multitude of hypocrits, I shall make my mark, and begin to cleanse the infected plains of Norrath, and spread the word of what is neither good nor evil, but simply true. We are here for ourselves, to live our lives as we see fit, and I shall live mine as I wish, come hell or high water...

Bazzer
Rogue of Dark Malus

"I AM SIR ADRIAN EVERHEART THE FIRST, DIVINE BE THE RELIC BURNING INTO MY CHEST, DIVINE BE THE WILL OF MY WAYS, DIVINE BE THE DESTRUCTION OF YOUR EVIL. I AM SIR ADRIAN EVERHEART, DIVINE SHALL REIGN TRUE IN THIS WORLD, ALL WILL BE DIVINE IN THE FINAL DAYS... AND NO EVIL HEARTS SHALL BE SPARED! I AM SIR ADRIAN EVERHEART, BE WARNED THE DIVINE HAS BEEN REBORN FROM THE RAINS, BE WARNED THE DIVINE HAS NO END, BE WARNED THE DIVINE HAS NO FEAR" and as the howl begins to stop the locals stand and look toward the skys for answers, a single raindrop falls upon his cheek, in unison with the flow of a teardrop... and in an instant the rains pour down upon all of Norrath, from the yard of Paineel to the furthest Drolvarg Castle in Kunark... and Karana appeares in the sky looking down upon the land at one figure, one dark figure cloaked in silver and black; his sword drawn with blood on his blade, glowing from hot death incarnate. The figure looks up into the sky with a slight smile and once again only the finest elven ears can hear his words... "Father, I am Sir Adrian Everheart, my way be Divine, my way be for you and all the good of Norrath... show me my next journey and I will do you no wrong" and in that instant the rains stop... only a few droplets still fall covering the heads and grass of Norrath, Karana points in one direction... Adrian watches the hand and can see it is aimed at his heart... in that moment he knows his next journey is to seek out his truest of love, he seeks one only to compare with the purest of Lilys, the kindest of innocent child, the bravest of souls... he seeks his love.
