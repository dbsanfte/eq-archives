Is it just me, or is E'ci going through some major changes?

Vindicated got plastered by a massive exodus to Arete last night.

Crimson Legacy/Omenborn merger seems to have failed, and Omenborn is reborn last night.

Destinati Conquerers are virtually destroyed.

Etc, etc, etc.

Those were the largest hits this server has taken in the past few weeks. What is going on?

Honestly, the Arete/Vindicated absorption is something I could see coming. Arete is a much more established guild and IMHO has a very capable guildleader in Waelquin. They are prepped to go to NToV and after some more First Brood kills, they'll be in Sleeper's.

Crimson Legacy has a good number of 55+, but not enough melee. Omenborn has enough 55+, but not enough Clerics or Chanters. You would have thought this would be a merger made in heaven. Given time, it would have allowed them both to go to ToV and eventually even further. What split them? That isn't for me to say. I was a member during this time, and to be honest, I am very upset today that it ended the way it did yesterday. Even further, I am disgraced with the way I behaved last night while people /guildremoved themselves left and right. Given time, the differences could have been worked out, but it took discussion, consensus and compromise, something that didn't happen across the entire guild.

E'ci is changing before our eyes. The base of power is being heavily concentrated into those guilds that have already been into ToV for a good bit of time. When another guild wants to get there, it tends to be stripped of its players and they leave for those already there before they are give their own guilds a chance. We all want to see the end game, but it seems that too many people are forgetting that the end game isn't nearly as much fun if you miss what comes just before it.

My 2cp. I think E'ci is in a slump, and without some stabilization, we're going to continue to see people leave. Remember, we're all suppose to be friends here. We're suppose to be enjoying our time.

Shrug, Things change.

for the past two years we've seen total server population P raise up and up, meanwhile # of guilds Gu has gone up and up, and # of people in guilds Pgu has also consistently gone up.... they are all directly related... now the game is thinning some on a per-server basis... this is not all due to DaoC---a lot of people are picking up EQ now and are not even up to 40th or 50th or 60th level..however they start on newer servers or they split off onto alternates, therefore an established server like E'ci will shrink with time...

it's the expected roller coaster of population that Verant has engineered even before DaoC came out and threw its hat into the ring... as more people leave, guilds will consolidate or just fade away.

perhaps what we may be moving toward as well is more multi-guild raiding and less uber-guilds, as the % of uber people (U of course) in the population P, known naturally as Pu, well it's going down probably.

all of which is to say, this isn't too expected, we shall deal with it. Look at baseball, and don't be surprised if servers are consolidated eventually at some point. I bet it will happen.

Vinx

--btw, I think as more guilds are unable to supply 40 level 55+ characters of their own, a lot of us may want to really examine the idea of multi-guild raids with 3-4 guilds supplying the 40 raiders. If everyone behaves responsibly and honorably, your guild is invited back. It's not too hard a concept. Good leaders are required though, as well as improved conduct by the raid populace as a whole. The bickering public raids suck for a reason. The bad apples aren't tossed out on their ear fast enough.
