I will be leading a semi-public Chardok Royals raid on Wednesday for level 50+ raiders. Semi-public means the public is welcome to sign up, yet bear in mind I am a Stormseeker first and member of the public second. I reserve the right to earmark a piece of epic loot for a Stormseeker. For any particular raid, I will post ahead of time if a piece of loot is reserved.

I am not reserving any pieces for Stormseekers for this raid.

The Stormseekers is always looking for quality members. If you are looking for a nice group of competent people, please ask me, Arrk Mindmaster, or any officer, for an interview to see if the Stormseekers is the right guild for you.

We will assemble in front of the Chardok castle in the Burning Woods. That means OUTSIDE Chardok. If we do not have the force to raid IN PLACE or at least have needed classes coming by 8:30pm EST, I will cancel the raid. Raid channel will be SSChardok.

Raiding deep into Chardok can be time-consuming. We will need at least an hour to get to the Royal Palace, and one round of Royals will take at least 2 hours after that. If you cannot stay until 12am EST (4 hours), please do not sign up. We need at least one druid or wizard for the raid, but if we get no wizards, you CANNOT leave early, except in the most dire emergencies (real-life emergencies).

I do not honor last piece. We will use Elder House points to bid. You may bid up to 10 more points than you have accumulated. All no-drop loot will be bid on when it drops, except epic pieces which will be bid on before the pull. All droppable loot, including things like Incarnadine armor, Argent Protector, and high level Kunark spells, will be held until the end of the raid, when people will bid with Elder House points. * Droppable loot will be awarded to the highest bidder, regardless of need, race, or class. * To see your current points, please check the following link:

http://pub28.ezboard.com/felderhousefrm5.showMessage?topicID=5.topic

Raid objective will be determined by signups. To have a raid, we will need the following:

* A rogue with over 200 lockpick skill
* At least two clerics
* Enough evacers to get the raid out (one wizard, or enough druids for the melee)
* Main tank, preferably level 60+
* At least one monk puller
* One healer per group (cleric, shaman, or druid)
* Someone able to Snare
* A total at least of 15 people

This is the MINIMUM needed to successfully raid the Royals. If we do not have enough to kill at least the King, we will not raid.

These pointers may be somewhat helpful to those new to raiding Chardok:

* Many things here cast spells, including an indoor version of Spirit of the Wolf. ALL MONSTERS MUST BE SNARED!
* Do not beat monsters into the wall. Not only can rogues not backstab, when low on health they may teleport to an unknown location if pushed into the wall.
* The sarnaks deep inside are TOUGH: level 52-57. Do not underestimate a 4-pull; we can handle it, but do not get overconfident.
* We NEED to keep moving, for the dungeon has a normal respawn time of 25-30 minutes. If we pause too long, we get trapped and have to kill repops. We have to keep moving despite AFKs and LDs. If necessary, we will drag the body of anyone left behind to the main raid to be rezzed (if, of course, the person dies trying to catch up again). I know the places where locked doors are, and will stop the raid’s progress only when necessary.
* Use assist. Only the main tank should break a mez. As an enchanter, I am especially sensitive to them, too!

If we get enough of needed classes, we will at times use AoE (Area of Effect) tactics to clear through parts faster. To do AoE, I need at least 3 wizards or wizard-equivalents and 3 enchanters of level 55+ or equivalent. This is how AoE is performed:

* Turn /showspelleffects off, and under Options-Display turn off spell and other particle effects. If you do not, you will be useless when the spells start landing because of graphics lag.
* The basis of AoE tactics is some very brave pullers go out to pull and bring back as many monsters as they can bring while (hopefully) staying alive. Enchanters AoE Stun all the incoming monsters, and druids, clerics, and wizards begin nuking with point-blank AoE spells when someone gives the signal. All of the monsters start dying at about the same time.
* Melee people’s job is to bash monsters near the outside edges of the group in toward the enchanters and nukers, to make sure they all get hit by stuns and nukes.
* Druids and clerics are healers FIRST and nukers SECOND. If the enchanters die, it is very quickly a massive wipeout.
* Nukers and stunners should face a wall when nuking and stunning, to push the monsters toward the wall.

If interested in raiding the Royals, please sign up below with name, class, level, and the points you want to bid for epic items. If most of the raid has access to the Planes of Power and group makeup allows we will use the raid feature, at my discretion.
