A little before we got to the room where we would be pulling the mob to, we noticed an enchanter who had taken it upon herself to invis up to the named and carefully charm a nearby mob to use on it. Needless to say, she was dead in 2 seconds flat. However, because of my having casted on the previously-charmed mob, the 2 mobs ran towards me, once she was dead, so we had a little bit of work on our hands. It all worked out in the end, and we had a nice laugh. Yeeeah! Serves her right, eh?
