Ahhh memories...lol.

Still remember how cool it was to swim from NK almost across WK in 1 sec.

How many times we all died killing Vox the first time. First of many all-nighters pulled to get the job done. Best thing about the Vox killing was it pretty much brought all the top players on the server together in one place to meet each other.

How everytime Yaden showed up to KS at the aviak treehouse and Beryl would show up a little later to debuff him until the birds killed him a few times so he'd leave. Pretty sure that's why he quit playing, think she stalked him to death...lol.

How Macross, aka Kung, would come get my butt out of really tough spots and rez me when I got in over my head long before we knew each other.

Woofwoof's crazy ideas about CC, I feared his charmed pets more than 10 of any other mob. "should we evac, woof?", "no i got it, wait...", "no evac, i can't mez my pet", "so no we should evac, or no not evac?"

Trying to decide who had less tact, Xeffo or Dyeth. Having them both in the same group was definately not PC, lol.

Always being amazed at how well Anayin could play his two chars considering his second comp didn't have a mouse.

Momerath corpses being basically anywhere in the game. We used to navigate thru lguk based on his corpse locations, heh.

I always wondered if you got gabby, ayama and narya in the same group if a black hole would have formed in the game?

How much the game changed some people. Baven was a pretty easy going guy back in the guk days. Really, I'm not kidding.

It seems strange considering how things turned out, but it used to be easy to get jboots. Nobody was high enough to deal with drelz the first couple of months. The hard part was getting a couple more people to go with you since nobody was in najena.

I still laugh everytime I think about commons griffins eating newbs by the barrel.

Finding out that PoH was safe for DE inno lovers. Also how nice Beryl was to show us that being DE wasn't enough, lol. She got the last laugh, since she was our ride up and refused to let Xlaar res her back up until we found a safe area (later known to all as the SW corner).

The day we were working the SW corner of PoH and the VI spell guy(I forget his name now) showed up to watch what we were doing and figure out how to make PoH less safe. Couple patches later they added the two guards to that corner. Ohh I'm scared.

The night Cylus got the first carmine robe in fear fiasco. Can't say I blame wizzies for getting upset by the whole thing, we must have done 50 fear trips before that robe finally dropped. Odd how much less fuss Severian's getting the first and only Cryosilk robe was.

Never got over the bizarre colors bard armor came in. Seemed evertime I say Celandro he was making a new fashion statement that would never catch on. Guess that's why the whole first year of playing I only knew 2 bards...lol.

Bah, I could write a book. This will have to do.
