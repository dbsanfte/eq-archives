It never seems to go the same way twice. This time, we had a flawless and fast run to the lair, killed UDB on the ramp, but got early aggro on the dragon (somebody's hiney was just a little high on the ramp I suspect). Our tanking crew was on the ball and pulled him back into the lair for a relatively easy kill ... but guess what? The bard was banished and killed (along with MT Fjorde and oobah aggromeister BL Hugg) OH NO! Would the epic loot rot before she got there? Whew, no, thanks to heads-up corrective measures by Kloude and CoH mage Senina.

Our sole cleric, Brenie, passed on all loot and did a great job ... we are grateful. Thanks Group Leaders for handing out the loots. By doing this for us, you free me up to handle the stream of tells that always follow the raid. Everyone worked hard and made it a memorable affair.

I learn something every week. Next time we do UDB by the bridge. I had a ball pulling on my monk main (usually I am the druid in the rear).

Shaydad, Quinnt, and Purrsalot advance to the LP list, and Shassiash, Meeowmix (on Fjorde), Saryma, and Bhangles (on Soulron) attended for points (will post points next announcement). Lovim sent me a tell with his pieces, too, so I will put him on the list, but I have not seen him on the boards.
