My time for playing is very limited, so im just gonna try this.
Every night, at about 10pm Eastern, im gonna be in Kithicor
woods, trying to get groups to camp Laarthiks cabin. Actually, if
im online, im probably gonna be trying to get groups for the
camp. But always at 10pm so some people can have a set time
to join. Message me b4 coming to make sure i am able to get
enuf people.

Anyways, for those who might want to go after him when im not
online, this is what u do to get him to spawn.

There is a burnt cabin in the middle of the woods( 900, 2350 ).
If u go there during the day(EQ time) there should be like 5
newbie undead outside and 3 inside the cabin(skeletons,
zombies, ghouls). At about noon EQ Time, if u kill them all, then
u should have set the trigger. Either camp on the hills next to
the cabin, or return to the cabin, at about Midnight EQ Time, 7
lvl 40+ Undead will spawn(Zombie Guards, Skeleton Guards,
Undead Priests). They all spawn at exactly the same time in the
spots the newbie undead were in. In the middle spawn spot
inside the Cabin is where Laarthik will spawn. If he doesnt
spawn, his place holder(a lvl 40+ skeleton guard) will spawn. If u
do not kill these undead b4 sunrise, they will disapear and the
newbie undead will respawn, and must be killed again at noon. If
u kill the newbie undead b4 noon, then all that will spawn is
newbie undead somtime b4 midnight.
Some info on the Undeads. The Zombie Guards are Shadow
Knights, nasty Harm Touches. The Skeleton Guards are casters,
usually have pets and cast fear ALOT. The Undead Priests are
clerics, Superior Heals, and alot of Smites and Wraths(they
usually drop the +6 wisdom Morningstars).
Also, while camping on the hills waiting for the spawns, the
Wandering undead will come by and bother us. They all range
from lvl 30 up. They get annoying after a while, especially since
they like coming around after u started fighting the guards.
1 Group of lvl 40+ has a hard time camping the spot without
having run away. You get over run real fast. Im hopeing to get
at least 2 groups for this, although it might still take more.
Info on Laarthik, he is a lvl 45 Shadow Knight, 600+ damage
harm touch, will cast a pet, hits for about 145 damage, has an
Axe(he had one when we saw him, dont know if its random, or a
magic axe) is half as tall as a hill giant, and suicides if u get him
in the cave leading to rivervale.
I still want to know more about this Child of Hate thing. Is
Laarthik after death gonna re-pop as the Child of Hate, or does
he trigger the spawn, or does he drop a quest item, i dunno, i
want to find out. I want the Rathe to be the first to get the
story line going, if the Guide was telling me correct info. We
might be the only server to have made him spawn so far.

I want everyone to come whenever possible. Im not good at
setting up events. So pretty much every night at 10pm eastern
ill be in Kithicor ready for anyone who wants to come. And i
encourage anyone who reads this to set up events with thier
guilds, friends and anyone they see, no matter what time. If
anyone gets an event going, leave a message somewhere
stating the time, if i can log on, i definitly will join.

If anyone wants, post this message on other message boards for
different guilds. The more people the better. I want to see MFH,
KEG, Silver Thread, CoN, and all other guilds out there =)
See ya in Kith.
