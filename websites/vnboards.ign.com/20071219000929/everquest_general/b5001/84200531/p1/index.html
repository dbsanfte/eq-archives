Monk/Shaman

Monk - Can pull singles all day long with FD. Huge Damage dealers. Mend to save mana on heals. Good AA damage avoidance skills that make it possible to take on high level boss type mobs in the later levels.

Shaman - Slows. Whatever the Monk lacks in AC/HP slow makes up for it. Tons of buffs that play right into the monks damage dealing. Cannibalize to help mana. Spells like Stoicism make the mana flow.

This combo can dungeon crawl. Take on some huge MOBS. If you want to join a group a shaman/monk are normally welcome.

With shaman & shadowknight as your duo, you have a lot of options as to what to add in for a third class. Add in a cleric for a CH Bot to do tough mobs. Add in a ranger with autofire or a wizard for nukes or whatever for more dps for easier mobs.

But the basics -- pulling, tanking, snaring, slowing, resist debuffing, healing -- are all covered nicely by the shd/shm duo. So much so that for a lot of places you can hunt, you are completely covered with shd/shm so that you can then just invite whoever you like to join you from your guild or friends. Four rogues LFG? Your shd/shm have them covered, time to pull non-stop. Four of random class X are LFG? You are set.

You got the critical stuff covered with this duo, which makes adding more to the group much easier.
