Final Fantasy I Theme Friday Night Alt Group


Greetings All!

Yes, I know I’m showing my age some, but I played Final Fantasy when I was young, it being one of the very first video game rpgs (not necessarily THE first, just one of the first). I remember it as the first rpg I ever played (even before Legend of Zelda), and my first experience with the whole concept.

During the week I play different characters with set groups. We always get together at the same time, same people, and only these same people. No one plays these alts when they are not in the group. No hour and a half LFG, no building most of a group and not being able to find a healer or crowd control or dps, none of that junk. We meet and when everyone is there we hit some ldons or whatever we want to do. All money collected by the group stays with the group, and in turn the group finances armor/spell purchases for the members.

Now here is where the FFI theme comes in.
Normally I allow whatever twinking the characters want, as it is their character. In FFI you started out with 400gp and 4 PC’s to equip. However, in this group, we’ll be starting with our newbie tunics and the wonderful *short swords we are first issued. That’s it. Not even 2gp for spells. Even more than that, we’ll be starting out as Freeport Humans. In FFI the PC’s looked pretty human, (well, not the black mage, but no one could see under that hat anyway). I know what you’re thinking, why not qeynos, why not DE’s, why not any race you want, why not any classes. Well, a) we have to start somewhere, and b) it usually takes at least one person an extremely long time to figure out where they are and where they need to be, this will help to simplify things. I know FP fairly well and can therefor lead anyone around in it with ease. Also, there are plenty of newbie quests in FP, access to the PoK book is close by and in a non-kos area.

So, we’re starting out with a group of 6 humans from Freeport. (I know that in FFI you could only have four, but since this is a theme group, and not an actual reproduction, we can make some allowances)

We are sticking to the classes as best we can though.

1. Fighter – This will be a warrior.
2. Thief – (C’mon, guess) This will be a rogue.
3. Black Belt – Monk of course
4. White Mage – Obviously a cleric
5. Black Mage – Easy enough, a wizard.
6. Red Mage – This one wasn’t so easy, but eventually I decided that an enchanter would serve best here.
In EQ speed control and crowd control is paramount to a good group. It was either a bard for CC and a shaman for speed control or an enchanter….and well, since chanters can be human and shaman can’t…
Also, Red mages had the power of both black and white mages, and in some ways they still do. Chanters can Rune (preventative healing) and nuke (sort of), so close enough. (Yes, I know that they could also be druids, bards, necros, paladins and just about every other class in eq, but having an enchanter makes things easier, so that’s what we’re going to go with)

Everyone is asked to be good/agnostic as the PC’s in FFI were hero’s not villains.

The group meets 8pm CST till 11-midnight. If you’re interested in joining, please email me at Averam24@yahoo.com Don’t post a response here, cause I’m probably not going to read it.

The information I need to see in your email is:
Your (EQ) name. (I don’t really care who you are in RL)
What servers you have characters on. (We’ll be starting on a new server that none of the group members have characters on, so as to enforce the no twinking rule. Also, the server won’t be announced until just before the group starts)
What class you’re interested in playing, and what, if any secondary class you’d like to try. I for one would like to try out 3 of the classes, so I’ll probably just take whichever one is left.

There are a couple of questions that I’d like to ask and get a yes to.
1. Do you know what Mez means?
2. Do you know what “Don’t hit the mezzed mob,” means?
3. Do you have all the expansions?
4. Are you going to be able to attend the group most of the time? (As in, do you get off work 15 minutes before we start and you often work late on Fridays? Do you have to make dinner for 3 kids or walk your dog before we start? Do you have enough time to get home and be ready for 8pm?)
5. Do you know what “Don’t hit the mezzed mob,” means?

**Update**

Wizard

We need only a cleric and a monk to be ready to go.

As I said before, my email is Averam24@yahoo.com and that is how you get in touch with me to join.
