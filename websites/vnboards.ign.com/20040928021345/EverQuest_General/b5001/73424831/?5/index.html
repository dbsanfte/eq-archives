You might want to try sol a, this has been nerf'd slightly since I used to farm there but it still has some decent loot drops. The goblins drop high quality ore and fine steel weapons, and their is a vendor in the zone. I used to make about 600pp every 20 mins there, before the now i think it is about 600pp an hour, not the best place to hunt but not the worst. Get a map of the zone then follow this route:

Goblin Foreman (Foreman belt) --> Kindle (Onyx ring & int earring) --> Goblin Commander (Blackened boots) --> Reckless Efreeti (Platinium armband & nodrop sword, good for pet)--> Singe (roamer, fire resist belt) --> Clockwork (Named roamer drops charred guardian shield and BP) --> Gabbie (drops dagger, junk and 4 int wis cloak)--> vendor (sells tink bags and traveler boots) --> Goblin Foreman -->...

The mobs are on a 20 min spawn timer and are relatively easy. beware of adds especialy around the gnomes. At level 40 you may have some dificulty seperating some of the mobs but apart from that it is easy loot.

Some of the items drop still sell reasonably well in the bazaar, the rest I would hand in for tribute.
