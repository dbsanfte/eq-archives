Old school days in MoH were great. We were one of the few guilds that ran around in full gear. Most people had PK gear and it was well known if you could take one of us down you would be able to loot something decent.

I have been playing my old account recently thanks to the free 15 days to entice us back. I won't be staying but I have been playing my old Red Shirt SK, Lyssa. Crushbone has been her home and it has been a blast so far.
