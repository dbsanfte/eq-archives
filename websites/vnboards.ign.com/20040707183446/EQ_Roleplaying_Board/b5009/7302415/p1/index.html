A large explosion and the sound of steel on steel is heard outside. A massive beast falls through the thatched roof sending straw and dust motes across the small inn and badly splintering the musky old wood floor. The beast struggles to stand its pale white skin rippling with sinew. Cold dead eyes fall upon those who were, moments ago, enjoyng memories of the past and who now struggle to pull their weapons from their sheaths. A black velvet like cape shifts to reveal a chrimson underside and the firelight plays across the vampire's long fangs.

In the same instant, before even Lothannon could ready Wormslayer for an assault, A small form dwarfed by the monsterous vampire flies through the hole rent in the ceiling. Silvery hair billowing behind him, Lladerek X'Lottl charges the beast from mid-air. Like a shooting star, sword and shield at the ready, he comes upon the Vampire. Like flowing water his strike lands, elegantly cleaving the undead monstrosoty's neck.

As he lands, Lladerek reaches back just in time to make a blind catch at the vampire's falling head. Sheathing his sword, he uses his free hand to brush his long white hair away from his handsome indigo face.

Lladerek looks around for the first time, the fire dying out of his eyes. In a low voice he mumbles something about surface dwelers and damn buildings getting in the way. He struts over to the bar and slams the severed head on the table.

Looking Gale right in the eyes Lladerek whispers in the common tounge, just loud enough for the rest to hear him. "Hey, barmaid, get rid of that mess over there. And in the name of Innoruuk get me some of your best elven wine." He glances over at the rest of the inn's shocked patrons, weapons still drawn and directed at him. "So I guess I'll have to deal with you all untill I'm rested enough to fly out of here again"

Lladerek sits on a tall stool near the bar, throws his shield and bundle on the ground, and mutters something in his native tounge. Pointing over to the hooded figure sitting by himself, he says in the common tounge "You better not be Feir'Dal, boy." He then rests his head on the table looking at the hole made in the roof. "Hurry up with that wine!"
