You can, but note that to have a pet you need a minimum of 2 skills, charm (just to use pet) and comfort (heal and rez pet). If you want to enhance them further, like call of protection, that's at least 1 more slot. If you have a rez, that is half of your skill bar taken up before you even add any offensive skills.

Perhaps troll's unguent would be your healing spell? Otherwise some points in blood magic for stealing health may be needed. That leaves 3 skill slots left.

You could get by with 1 raise minion skill and then use 2 death spells like swarm for the other slots to deal some damage.

So it seems like a lot to pay to bring that ranger pet along. Personally I went Ne/Mo and use heal area to prolong the life of my zombies. Heal area and rez spell are the only monk spells used and healing attribute is only like 4 to get 60 point heals. Death is main attribute, and have switched between blood and curses.

You could just bring charm and if the pet dies it just remains dead until you zone. Perhaps that is worth it, but I wouldn't invest much in Beast for just the pet.
