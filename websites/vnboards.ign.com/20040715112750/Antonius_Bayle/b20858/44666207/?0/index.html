Let's Take Action Regarding The Affliction Focus Nerf

I would like to encourage all Shamans, Enchanters, Druids, Necromancers, and anyone else who feels negatively impacted by the Affliction Focus effect nerfs to let SOE know how you feel in the following way.

Follow the directions below to cancel your pre-order for Legacy of Ykesha:

1. Visit Station Store (https://store.station.sony.com/index.jsp)
2. Go to Billing Info and sign in
3. View your Current Pre-orders
4. Click the “Cancel” button (you will receive a cancellation email)

Frankly, I was not too excited about an expansion with no high level content anyway.

We have made gear choices for almost nine months only to have the rug jerked out from underneath us on ALL focus affects for debuff/charm/fear/snare/root spells.

If SOE believes that the high savings on mana and casting time were too good on these Affliction focus items, then they can do something like this:

Create an NPC in a readily accessible zone that will exchange any and all Affliction Focus Items for replacement items with a new focus effect. The new Focus effect should work on all detrimental spells (not just DoTs), but possibly at a lower level of mana/casting time/duration enhancement. Other stats, etc. should be the same.

I would find this an acceptable compromise solution, and would re-instate my Legacy of Ykesha Preorder when this happens.

I realize that a change like this takes significantly more time to program than a simple nerf to all Affliction focus items. Since it was okay to let Affliction focus items incorrectly affect debuffs for nine months, please restore these items to thier previous function until the above programming is complete. Then, please announce the change in the appropriate patch message.

If a nerf to these items is warranted, then please take the time to implement the nerf in a way that is less disruptive to your playerbase.

I would like to demonstrate how seriously we feel about this issue, so please post below if you are in agreement (and have canceled your LoY preorder over this issue).
