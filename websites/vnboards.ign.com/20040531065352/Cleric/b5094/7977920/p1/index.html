That depends on the type of person you are.

I have a level 54 halfling cleric - when I created her, I was looking at the roleplaying side of the halfling, and felt that best suited my personality (that I could carry through the game). I didn't look at stats, and I made a few semi-poor choices. However, I have learned to play within my limitations, and I feel I am a very good cleric.
My groups do not die if it is within my power to keep them alive, and I bring some lightheartedness to my group and my guild (as halflings are the fun-loving type).

I have been told it is unusual to see a halfling cleric, and there are some armor items that, sadly, I cannot wear, because VI in it's infinite wisdom, felt would not suit halflings (and sometimes dwarvf clerics).

Overall - I adore my little halfling cleric.
So, if you have not already created your character, and you'd like to give a hobbit a try, I say Go for it!!!

just my 2cp
