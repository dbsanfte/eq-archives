Not very hard anymore. They were hard years ago when they were released. Some of the epics are time consuming.. most are at one point or another. But if you get somewhat lucky, you can finish them in like one or two days. Most people take a few weeks. There are better weapons then epics out there for most every class. Casters have it harder to replace their epics then melee, due to the clicky effect on them. A clerics epic is never truly replaced, even if they wield something besides that in their main hand. The druid epic effect is a joke really, and there are a couple of items in PoP to replace it, and one that's bassicaly a direct upgrade including the clicky. I happily replaced mine with a Hammer of Hours.

Wizards can't really replace theirs until Plane of Time, Enchanters replace theirs(effect wise) when they get Speed of Vallon, though the clicky (rarely) comes in handy sometimes still. Necros replace theirs at like 61 or whenever they get the new darkness, overall it's not worth bothering as a necro. Mages replace theirs once they get a 61+ pet spell. Shaman can replace theirs sorta easily. The effect is kinda handy at times, but not that great really.

Melees usually just need to find a better weapon, which isn't hard these days. Rangers, rogues, and beastlords all have haste on one of their epics(rogues only have one weapon as epic though), so they might keep that one for awhile if they can't find decent haste elsewhere. Warrior epic is a joke really, much too difficult for the reward provided. Same with Paladin and Shadowknight. Monks is a pair of gloves, with a decent clicky effect...that you can find on a pretty cheap piece of armor from the bazaar. Bards is another one that's not replaced for a LONG time, due to it being a 1.8 mod to all instruments(including acting as a singing "instrument"), but eventually you can replace it by getting instrument modifiers on your armor/weapons. Think that about covers it.
