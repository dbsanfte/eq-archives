Before you read this, just know that this is not another sob story. It is a lesson learned, not from anyone else but me. And after my story I would like to her some of ya'lls if ya have any.
May 18, I just bought my buddy lunch at roundtable and I was taking him back to school. On the way there, a car pulled up next to us, and two kids my age were smoking and laughing it up. I couldn't figure out what they were laughing at, until I dropped my buddy off. He looked in the back seat and said, "those mother fuckers threw a lit cigarette in your back seat." I looked back there, and sure enough, there was a burning cigarette and a fat burn hole. I looked at my friend and took off after the guys. I didn't go far when they passed me, so I threw a U turn and followed them. While I was driving I pulled my tire iron out from underneath my seat, and my 12" survival knife out from under the passenger seat. I followed these guys into a rich neighborhood, and they parked in front of a house were about 8 guys were waiting. You are probably wondering why i didn't leave and come back later, you have to understand my mindset. Anyway, I pointed to the driver and told him to come over to where I was. (I was still in the car) I asked him as calmly as I could; "Did one of you just throw a lit cigarette in my car?" He got an attitude and started to cus me out. By that time all his little rich kid friends surrounded my car. I grabbed my knife and told him to tell his boys to get away from my car. They backed away, and I started to drive offf. Then I heard a comotion, and saw one of the guys running after my car with a baseball bat. I pulled over, opened my trunk and got my bat out and told him to come here. He stopped running, and I could see the hesitation in his eyes. By then, the other guys all had weapons. I looked the youngest kid there, he must've been 17, he had a knife. I asked him, "Are you ready to kill somebody kid?" I think the question scared him, becuase he looked like he was gonna cry. I don't care what kind of person you think you are, when you see a human being cornered with nothing to lose, it's a very intimidating sight. I'm not a scary guy, but when I'm in that mode, it's almost sub-human, primal. Anyway, one of the little spoiled bitches started going off "My brother's in the Aryan Brotherhood and he's gonna kill you!" I said, "thats funny, I used to run with the AB." Then, the guy with the baseball bat came towards me, so I got in my stance and was ready to go. I didn't notice the little bitch getting in my car and taking off. I ran after the car for a short distance, than just stopped in the street and laughed like a mad man. I waited for her to come back, and when I saw her I put my hands out and dropped my bat as to say, "I'm harmless." She got out, and I got in. That's when the kid with the baseball bat came over and tryed kicking the door on my leg. I let him and just looked at him and smiled. Then, he slapped me in the face like a little bitch, didn't even leave a mark. I started busting up laughing my ass off. I honestly can't believe how calm and collect I was through the whole thing. I looked at him calmly and said, "Hey, there's always a price to pay." He had the baseball bat pointed at me like he was gonna shove it through my face, but I knew hw wouldn't. By that time I heard the cops coming, so I left, but I was too late and got pulled over on the next street. The cop detained me for an hour. I was completely honest with him, and he liked that. He even gave me my baseball bat and I my knife back.;) I was really stoked to find such a decent cop. I went my way, and realized that something was wrong with my car. I don't know what, but now it makes a repetitive knocking sound. Anyway, the ride home, many things were going through my head. I was going through all the ways I could exact revenge. Then, I thought, wait a sec. what if I don't do anything? I though about it. Last night before I went to bed, I prayed to God to take the hostility out of my heart. I woke up today, and realized that I'm not going to do anything back. I mean, this is how I know I'm growing; the old me would've got a bunch of skinheads in a truck and would've firebombed the whole heihgborhood. But, I know I that happened, some who didn't deserve it would be hurt or killed. I don't want anyone getting hurt over my pride. I've decided to let nature take it's course. The words I spoke to that guy were true, "there's always a price to pay." He will pay the price without me doing anything. And even if he doesn't, oh well. I want to set an example. I've seen too much violence, I don't want it anymore. I hope people can learn from this, I know I have. So, that's what's been happening lately. I feel better now that I know I wont do anything back. No guilt, no hostility, and everything will be alright. This was just another turning point in my life, and I think I made the right decision.
(By the way, I don't mean to make myself sound all high and mighty, and just want to show you that no matter what happens, you can leave it alone and move on.) I mean, how humiliating, letting some punk rich kid slap you. But, I have nothing left to prove, and the alternative will me unescisarily messy. Fuck it. I'm done fighting for my pride, I hope the people that read this already feel the same way.
Peace
