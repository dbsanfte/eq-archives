One problem may be the minions blocking the monsters from reaching your traps. If you lay a trap then shoot a mob to draw them to it the minions will run off and attack. If you try to trap while in combat, laying it at their feet, you need a defensive stance to prevent being interrupted. So I'm not sure that traps and minions work well on 1 character.

Honestly for my necro I've almost always done pure necro + rez spell. Only used heal area in the early part of the game for minion healing.
