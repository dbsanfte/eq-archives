I'm currently using Lamentation in my Primary and a Sarnak Skullsplitter (28/12 +8svDisease/Poison) in my Secondary.

I keep an Electrum-Bladed Wakizashi in my EE Bag for those times when the 28 Dly of my Skullsplitter is too slow (EBW is 21/7 or something). I'm not rich, nor have I ever seen as much as 10k, let alone 20. I bought my Lammy with a peice of Donal's and a Wurmslayer for trades.

As for "What is a Yak", Yak is the easy way of saying Ykesha. There are all sorts of Ykesha weapons. Clubs, SSwords, Piercers (Ranger only, go figure). Yak is a status weapon for lowbie-mid level Warriors. Anyone dueling Yak's at 20-35(ish) is rich.

I used one from 45 to 47 just because one dropped in the Lord's camp when I was there.

If you can afford it, I find the best combo (until you can get your hands on truely uber weapons, such as the stuff from VP or ToV) is a CSS and Lammy (both available in Sebilis if you can camp, or for about 15k if you buy them).

Just so you know, there is no "Best Duel Wield Combo" before or after level 40. Everything depends on situation and personal preference.
