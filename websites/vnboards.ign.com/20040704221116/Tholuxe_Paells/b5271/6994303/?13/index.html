I just sent this to all who were interested, contact me if you are interested or visit the www page in the first post.


Greetings Everyone,

For those of you I contacted before, this e-mail will be much more well thought out as that one was done in 5 min before classes started. Alright so, the 'Roman' theme has been mentioned as a good idea by several of you, everything is up for discussion, this is what Ive thought about so far.

3 'Guildmasters' Triumverate Style

Each Triumberate member is the 'general' of a certain ideology, Evil - Neutral - Good, Creating a 'balanced' government and not excluding any one group. Positions are VOTED for by the members of that faction, (Im going to appoint 2 other people to the other two triumverate positions, In a month or two after we are all in our teens - midtwenties we can hold the first election, I dont see the point of sticking with the same guildmasters if the members do not back them... this way problems are resolved rather than festering.

I would also like the triumverate be able to award 'titles' and awards to those who go above and beyond the call of duty.

The main goal of this guild would be friends, fun, and people to play with... We are going to have to decide as a group if we are going to recruit or have people come to us, if we want a large 'uber' guild, or a small tight group of friends... keep in mind guilds that overextend themselves are those that fail first.

Alot need to be discussed and planned out, after I get some feedback from you guys on these ideas above (or something I forgot) Ill draft up the Charter then the membership can vote on it...

As for the two other Triumverate members let me know if your interested, Im looking for long term EQ players who know the game well.

I myself am new to eq, I played UO for 3 years and was a seer there for 2 years... I am currently a staff writer for Dr.Twister: Horizons. Im in college (Economics.Finance major) and I mainly play weekday nights and late night during the weekends.

I love the Idea of a new server, and lets be a major force in this new world!
