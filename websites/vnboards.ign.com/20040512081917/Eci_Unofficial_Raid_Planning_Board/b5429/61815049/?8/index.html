Public Growth Raid:


~*~*~*~*~ NEW info below, please skim it! ~*~*~*~*~

Date: Tuesday, January 6th 2004
Start: 7:30PM Eastern *raid will be called by 8pm if we don't have enough*
End: Usually port out around Midnight eastern - spells/vt done afterwards in Nexus, east side
Raid leader: Canisa (and Daishi if he can attend)
Channel: mondaygrowth

Joining the raid:
Step 1) Type "/join mondaygrowth"

Step 2) Find a way to get to the Wakening Lands. You are responsible for getting to the Wakening Lands; I don't personally arrange teleports for people. Usually there is a wizard or druid in the mondaygrowth channel volunteering to port people, but if not you'll need to ask around or run through Kael.

Step 3) Make your way to the lake edge near the bridge of PoG tower in the Wakening Lands - the loc is roughly (700, -1300). Do not go to the top of the tower (some people who are KOS to the tower get attacked through the floor there.)

Step 4) When you arrive, turn /lfg on. That way I'll know to add you to a group.

Step 5) If you're a caster, please consider binding on or near the tower. On the off-chance we have a wipe, having casters bound near the zone-in can speed up the recovery.


Loot: Only Canisa and designated players will loot. All looted items will be handed to Canisa to divide up after the raid is over. Each class will handle its own no-drop armor list (eg. mages handle the mage armor, etc.) Typically armor “first pick” goes to the one with the most growth pieces with ties broken by random roll. If arbitrating a dispute is necessary Canisa has the final say.


The Break-in:
We try to zone the entire raid simultaneously and invisible to help avoid fighting adds and to make sure everyone is there for the first round of pogs. Please don't zone in until instructed as this can complicate matters.


Finally, note that druids, rangers and paladins have no armor in growth - they have quests inside instead. Spells for these classes up to level 60 do drop, though.


Faction warning:
You will become KOS in Growth by fighting there. This primarily affects druids, rangers and paladins, who have quests inside. If this doesn't bother you, though, come on down!


Sign-up: Feel free to post here with your name, class and level if you plan to attend. Walk-ons are always welcome. Clerics in particular are always in demand, so if you know a cleric who might like to help go ahead and ask. This raid has been unsuccessful for about the last month due to various issues - mainly lack of interest and Magget's Monday flagging raids. Therefore, we are trying a TUES for a change. Make it work people, or this raid will fade into the sunset. Bring friends!


Hope to see everyone there!
