Lots of opinions and while this was a PVE question, I want to add another answer from the PvP side.

Been running my Necro with monk secondary last few days - Blood, Protection and Soul Reaping. I cast Vital Blessing on my self (+147 health) and Life Bond on some (or sometimes all) members of my 4 person group. Life Bond takes half the damage on your enchanted ally(or allies) and redirects it to you, but the damage is reduced by 3-25 points (21 in my case) - many times I see 0's as the transferred damage I receive. And then just prior to engaging, I also cast Demonic Flesh (+184 health for 50ish seconds) - so I have just over 800 hp's when we engage. As long as no one is removing enchantments, the group without a main monk does pretty decently, but with a main monk does very decently. And with 800 hps, you can take a bit of a beating from a tank.

I use Life Transfer and Vamp Gaze as my dps. I also have Rend Enchantments to help kill others using the enchantment strategy. Pacifism I use to control any tanks that head my way and that aren't the called target. If I have all others enchanted (not usually), I have no energy regen, but soul reaping kicks in once folks start dying.

In PUG's (pickup groups) I have been having some good success with this build. 3 warriors and me ran up to 11 wins. Monk, Ranger, Warrior and me got to 14 wins last night - the last 4 against invited teams. For no teamspeak and not previously working together, I feel decent about the runs. The monk also had protection - he cast Life Bond on me and I had it on him so both of us were taking only half damage and were able to keep up with lots of damage.

Whatever you do, just make sure you are having fun,
