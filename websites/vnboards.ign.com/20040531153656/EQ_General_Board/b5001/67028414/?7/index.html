Well, I mostly build my own groups and still pick up strangers all the time as I also play at odd hours of the day but if you are just sick of EQ but want something similar I'd suggest FFXI. That's the closest of the major online games out there that is most like EQ. It does do some things better but there will be alot you quickly realize EQ is way out in front on. You will rely on finding other people to play with in FFXI even more then EQ- soloing is far more limited and restricted to 2 classes compared to EQ on the other hand you only need 1 character as you can change to any class you want just by returning to your house.

If you want a game better suited to your odd hours where any character can solo for decent advancement but also highly encourages grouping I would suggest Anarchy Online. As long as you don't have an issue with guns and lasers being used alongside swords and nanotechnology (magic) I had alot fun with it just don't have the free time to devote to 2 online games.
