To any would-be EQ re-subscriber:

I've recently come back to EQ (mythic IMO ruined the pvp server. ruined it.)

The game has defintly changed. Basically there are new fun spells, and an almost totall purge of the little things that made getting from place to place, or getting exp, or most activitys in the game a chore or needlesly frustrating (other than alot of zones being realy boring/ugly/disjointed).

So anyways: EQ is fun and worth picking up again to spend a few months in. and although alot of the aggravation has been removed, so has alot of the charm.
and the two are not mutually exclusive.

They worked on the game a bit too much i think and many of the old devlopers are gone, so the verant "newbies" have pretty much erased all of what was enchanting about EQ while they also removed what was a pain in the arse.

On a side note: CoM is dead.

After soloing most of my levels in enjoyable places instead of grinding out exp, i found a group in CoM.

Verant it seems found CoM also and tainted it with a foul and disgusting "revamp".

So if and when you come back, youll be disapointed i think on that aspect.

Basically the old CoM was fun, my favorite pre 50 zone.
They didnt change it much. just enough to make it REALLY REALLY SUCK. and boring.

Noone i knew ever complained about the old CoM, and it was a very fun zone, which became rarer and rarer in EQ.

I guess that made it a prime canidate for a revamp.
