Let me play the Devil's Advocate:

It's amazing to hear a class who's range of skills include "attack" "taunt" and, if you are lucky, "bash" talk of the Necro as being a 1 trick pony.

People who talk about Necros being a boring class of "kite insignificant_mob_01" don't really know anything about the Necromancer class. We are exceptionally well suited to grouping (if you know how to play the class) and although you won't be able to get a group as easy as a Warrior you just might find the experience much more rewarding.

What other class can add to a group: Mez, Snare, Fear, Healing (self and others), Emergency Mana Source, Feign Death, Decent Pet, incredible DoTs, Great Mana Regen, Corpse Retreval, and the added plus of being able to turn any mob into a Skeleton! You are constantly on your toes as you fit any grouping situation you find yourself in to changing your tactics to compensate for the group's weakness. And with the patch they made to mobs recently Necros are now a Large Enounter viable class too!

It isn't the easiest road...but it is perhaps the most rewarding road if you really learn the class and learn how to group.

A Soloist Necro is a Necro that doesn't know how to play the game. Not to say that if you want to get that level you don't have the ability to go out there and get the experience you need soloing, either, but the reward in Necromancy is being a powerful teammate.

I woudln't pick any other =)
