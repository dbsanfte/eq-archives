Enchanted day milords and ladies

I am Smaug (otherwise known as Elricinoor) and I have been playing EQ for about a year now. Recently though I took some time off to create a voice game site to meet, chat, and play with other gamers (mostly I started it for EQ, then it exploded). Well now we have come full circle, I am playing EQ again after a 6 month hiatus and that game site I mentioned I run has created quite a multi-server guild in everquest called The Voice of the Norrathians.

This post is coming to you because it is time I gave back to the community that has given so much to me. To help me in this, one of my VoN members and I are sponsoring our own Everquest contest with real prizes. I am going to relay the details to you in this post in the hopes that you will join us in the adventure. The end desire for the guild is to introduce and expose our guild name to as many people on Norrath as possible. We intend on being here a while!

But I digress.....

If the contest intrests you then you are welcome to sign up (free) and join us. There are no server or level restrictions because we are going to make ALL participants start a new char on a yet unnamed server and team into groups of two for a scavenger hunt. This contest is open to ALL EQ players and guilds and I encourage you to discuss any of this with us in our forums (I will add links to everything at the end of this post).

Here is a little bit of detail from the contest page, make sure to check the full page for complete details and we hope to see you at the contest on May 6th!

The Prizes

There are three prize levels. Grand Prize, 2nd place, and 3rd. All winners are final.

Grand Prize: Winning team of two (2) Grand Prize winners will receive a Game Commander 2. An Awesome voice control utility that works excellent with EQ!

2nd Place: One (1) 2nd place winner (see below) will receive a copy of the PC game Moon Project (Approx. value $39.99 US).

The 2nd place prize will be determined by the winning team rolling a 1-100 based die to see which partner gets the game, the losing partner will get a consolation prize (Nascar Racing 3)for participating.

3rd Place: Two (2) 3rd place winners will get collectable edition action figures of an Iksar Necromancer and a Dark Elf Mage.

The Quest

The quest is going to simple- collect 6 different items and a certain amount of coin and be the first team to turn them back into the collector. Everyone that participates must create a new character of the same race on a unnamed server at the start.

Once everyone is in and in a group, the quest will begin. The server, race, and items will be given in the voice chat (Or Irc chat) at the scheduled time. This is to make sure that noone cheats and gathers all the items prior to the quest. I think that this shall be a little bit of fun we can have in a 2-3 hour period.

It is not a requirement to be part of the guild. All of you had to have been told by a guild member or someone close to GVC about this contest, so all are welcome to join into the fun. This could be useful in recruiting more members into the guild.

The quest in itself should be easy enough, and the rules are pretty easy too. A group of 2 (although you are permitted to join in a group with other non-questing members to help speed up the gathering) will collect all items and return the items to the collector which shall be me (Name of character and server to be determined at start of contest).
