When Tallon Zek opened, there were 5500+ people online the first night, including Verant president John Smedley. Due to that overwhelming show of excitement, Verant opened Vallon Zek a few days later, and thus, 2 racewar servers were born.

Many of those people who play on them today, who were not there in the beginning, cannot imagine the fun of having 2500-3500 people online at once, in Antonica. It was a constant riot.

That was July 1998, and there were 44 zones in EverQuest.

It's now December 2001, and there are 144 zones in EverQuest. However, instead of 2500-3500 people online at peak hours on the Zeks, there are about 800-1000 at peak hours. On Sullon Zek, its even worse, maybe 500-600 online at peak hours.

EverQuest is 3 times bigger, and the server populations are 3 times smaller.

I propose that Verant offer all Race-War players a /movelog with 2 choices:

#1 Move to a single Racewar server, Unite Tallon and Vallon.

#2 Move to Sullon Zek


This would increase the population, and the fun, on both servers.

Your humble servant.
