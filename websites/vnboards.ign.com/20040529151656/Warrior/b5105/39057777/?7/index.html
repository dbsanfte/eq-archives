As I started as an ogre warrior twink I think I can answer your question.

It's all about hit points. Find gear with hps on them and use them. A tattered mantle can be bought in the bazaar for about 20p. I'm 55 and still wear one.

But find anything that gives hps and don't worry about AC at lower levels. Use 1 handed weapons as you'll do more damage. Look for stuff with a low damage to delay ration. You want quick stuff. Also get yourself an FBSS (about 2k).

My prices are based on blue servers don't know what PvP servers are like.

Any other questions PM me, I'll be happy to help.
