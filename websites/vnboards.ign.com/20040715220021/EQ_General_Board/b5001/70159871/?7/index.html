 		Date Posted: 6/9 12:38pm Subject: RE: Druid or Shaman? Which to 2box with a SK?
Considering in your 2 boxing group, you have healing covered, that sure isn't a concern.

With druids, you have 2 main gains in that. Being damage shields and teleportation of the non-gaters. Being able to evac when things go bad on a pull, well, thats a great option. CR's are always a pain. They have a good amount of buffs and ok DPS. Being able to track aid a lot in getting certain mobs as well.

With shamans you gain a lot of buffs, good DPS, and slows. With slows, you can tackle on higher mobs and have a chance to recover from bad pulls. Sure, you maybe surrounded from 4-5 mobs, but if all slowed. You could pull thru. I've done many times. Shaman buffs are the best in the game. While other classes have equal main buffs, like virtue, protection of ___, etc, shamans adds more amount as well with other stat buffs. Also, shamans have better mana recovery than druids with their canni line. Since you have no chanter for mana regen and crowd control.

Both classes have lower HPs, but I think that shamans have a bit more. Both have pets to add to your groups DPS, but shaman's pets are tougher. My druid guildies never pull out their pets. I'm under the impression that druids are better healers than shamans, but thats up to debate at higher levels.

I've seen people used both druids and shamans to PL and 2box. But, in my experience, I've seen and heard people liking shamans more cuz a slowed mob is easier for 2 boxers to tank and heal. Just less work I guess.

The key thing to 2boxing... many, many, MANY hotkeys!

Good luck to you!

Druids are much better healers then shaman at high levels... all other things being equal. Ala a slowed mob =-p If the druid is forced to heal on an unslowed mob,and shaman gets to slow theirs...shaman wins.

Druids only get one pet spell, and it was added as litteraly just a joke. Charmed pets on the other hand, will do much more damage then a shaman could hope to add, especially combined with nuking or DoTing. PAIN to two-box with charm though.

Overall shaman is your best choice, even if you do lose out on the mana regen for everyone from Nine at higher levels, it's not a big concern.
