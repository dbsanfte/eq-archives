Max! I am so proud you are attempting this and applaud your courage.

I like the idea of this being a public raid that could lead to LIS Raids which are fun, challenging and drop great ancient spells for casters and a nice Breastplate for Melees, not to mention the earring of veracity quest, which are comparable to elemental loot. I don't believe the negative comments in Alla's posted by folks who are used to mobs dropping 10 items each when they fall. Seru drops are sweet and once a raid force has been successful at him, it gets easier on each succeeding raid.

I'ld like to attend. If its not possible to get these 4, we can attempt the lesser 4 mobs required for the 3rd earring quest. They drop nice 55-60 spells as well.

Sign us up:

65 Pally, Palamus
63 Cleric, Blessod

For those that don't know, this raid is required for the 4th Earring of Veracity Quest. There are 5 total.

http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16266

NOTE: The first 3 earrings are not that difficult. The 4th earring is harder and requires this raid and the 5th is very difficult which requires the death of LIS, however the extra loot that drops from these four mobs ("bridle") is worth it to get a free mount, plus the Arx Key can allow a raid to port into LIS lair for a raid attempt and Lord Inquisitor Seru loot. Lord Inquisitor Seru raids are a true challenge to a raid force and a test of your playing abilities.
