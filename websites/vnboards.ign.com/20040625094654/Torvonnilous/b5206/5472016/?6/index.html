Hello everyone.

I would like to take this space to let everyone know that I am looking forward to starting a guild on the brand new server Torvonnilous on the day that Kunark goes live, which will consist of only Iksars of any class.

What type of people I'm looking for:

Just average ordinary players who like the ideas of having in game friends to help alleviate boredom, and to aid with each others gameplay. Roleplaying is not necessary, but I don't want people who don't like to somewhat interact with others to join either. I'm also looking for people who aren't easily offended, and can handle sarcasm, dirty jokes, and all around "locker room" talk on guildchat.

What type of guild will this be?:

It will be an Iksar only guild of course, and we will be a generally nice, helpful, hopefully non-troublemaking guild, but also I want this guild to be powerful and respected. This is best accomplished by trying to be non troublesome and helpful towards others as much as you can.

What are the stipulations?

The only stipulation I ask is that at level 20, you give yourself a predetermined surname, as I want this guild to be a "brotherhood" of sorts. That's all. The surname is yet to be given out, however, I can assure you it won't be anything rediculous, and I think most people will be happy.

Any guild events?

Well, aside from the supposed new Verant-run guild events soon to be held, of which of course i would urge everyone to try and join to make our guild proud, and just to have a break from the norm of constantly leveling, I and a few of my real-life friends who will be in the guild will definately be thinking of some neat-o quests or games for the guild. Hopefully we can even find a nice guild to go to war with!

Where do I sign up?

You can reach me at iksarguild@hotmail.com if you are interested. I will keep in touch when and if new things come about until the 24th.
