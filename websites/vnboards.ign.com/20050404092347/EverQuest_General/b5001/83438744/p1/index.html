It's nowhere near nearly dead. It just doesnt hold the same share in the MMO market because of competition. Partly of cannibalizing of its own offshoot, EQ2.

In the most recent PCGamer theres an article showing who controls how much of a share % in the MMO market.
FFXI controls some 18%, w/ WoW at about 15%. EQ some 13% and EQ2 at 11%. It included every major mmo short of lineage.

You can base all sorts of opinions on this. But it's a credible source.

All it showed me was that EQ2 doesnt hold the same subscribers its 6 year old predecessor has. Something that doesnt surprise me.

EQ is still fun as long as the expansions keep it fresh and alive. The last 2 expansions I think have done this.

Long gone are the days EQ would hold a 25%+ market share in MMo's. Then again with what competition is today, no one will. It was a matter of time. People were looking for something new. And a portion of those have come back.

When will EQ fold its doors? Who knows. I wouldnt predict less then 3 years tho. And if it lasts another 6 (more consolidation I'm sure) I wouldnt be surprised.
