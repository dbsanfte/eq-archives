There were a lot of factors that lead to the breaking appart of Prophets.

Alann disapearring was one of the big ones.

Velious coming out was another one.

Defiant constantly raiding seb was another one.

There were people in Prophets that wanted to leave seb and move to velious and do the new things there. Where there were people in Prophets that wanted to hold seb. In the past the Prophets were usually unighted with a goal in mind. They would support each other very strongly to achieve this goal.

With Alann leaving, there wasn't one voice to direct the guild, with Velious coming out there was many opinions on what the guild should do but not one person focusing the guild to a specific goal. So you had less Prophets trying to defend seb than normal and they were getting overrun. Levels and gear would only help so much. The more often they got overrun the more that would stop defending and move to join the rest in velious, after awhile they gave up in trying to hold seb and just went straight to velious.

If Alann hadn't left and they decided to focus on keeping seb the fight would have been a lot longer. They would have most likely lost in the end due the huge numbers difference.

Defiant didn't really force Prophets out of seb, they forced the faction that wanted to keep seb to agree to go to velious and forget seb. The older Prophets were tired and bored with seb and wanted to move on, all Defiant did was encourage a lot of the newer Prophets to agree with the old. Prophets would have left seb in a month or two anyways. Only coming back for some xping or spells etc. It was just coincidence that Defiant started to serriously take seb durring this period of upheavel in the guild. Defiant prob added to it, but it was already there. Defiant started when it did 'cause it felt that it was getting to the point were we could win.
