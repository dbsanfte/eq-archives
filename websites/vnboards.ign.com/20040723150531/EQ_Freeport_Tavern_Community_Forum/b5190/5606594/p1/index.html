Ok, just so you know, my Mage on Mith Marr was always my primary char so I could not twink him.


Ok, at about your level, probably a little lower, I picked up a Skull of Jhen'Tra for 10pp. +20 Mana and something else too.... You hold it in your left hand. So no more 2h blunt weaps. Hopefully you have a cracked staff.

Ok, I held a Giant Snake Fang and my Skull from about level 7 to level 14. At that time a friend gave me a Crysknife. Magic 4dam 21 delay piercing.

At this point I am still using a Sr. Apprentice robe which is +1 mana (WOW!) and AC6. Purple. (bah). I had also picked up a couple levels back a pair of Bracelets (can't remember name) that were +15 mana, +15 HP each. I paid 15pp each for those. Also got a pair of polished bone hoops which are +10 mana each. 10pp for the pair. You can easily find +2 INT rings all over for 6pp a pair. But you probably don't need INT stuff yet. Concentrate on MANA items.


At this level I was in Oasis till 17 and not making much money at all cept for the occasional raw-hide leather off orc warriors.

At level 17 I could finally start making MONEY! I went to Highhold pass and South Karana and started raking it in. I had a couple hundred plat in the bank before long. I went and bought several items at that point. Robe of the Keeper (+2INT), Stein of Moggok (+10 INT etc.) Savants Cap (+3 INT and WIS). BI Medallion (+3 INT and WIS). So finally at level 20 I had some decent items. Not long after that I earned a couple hundred more plat and passed it down to my various other chars to twink them out.
Guilty as Charged for Twinking.

Ok, so for advice. Earn for a Skull of Jhen'tra and Polished bone hoops. You'll get +40 mana that way, enough for another spell cast. later start looking for +mana jewelry for your wrists. However, always check the prices for your next set of spells so you can always have enough stowed away. I always had enough money at spell levels cause I didn't spend it all between on items. Just the extra money.

I hope this helps at all. Kinda long
