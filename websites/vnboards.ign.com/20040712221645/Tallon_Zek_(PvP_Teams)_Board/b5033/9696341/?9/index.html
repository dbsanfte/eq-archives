I agree that this should be easy to fix. The computer knows when you have lost a level (we get the "You have lost a level!" message) and it knows not to give you more training points when you get your level back and takes away recently gained skills. However, it is easy to lose one level and, if you recently spent your training points for the new level those points would be lost irrevocably. I would suggest limiting the skills to 5 over the current level -- thus, honest players who lose a level would not be penalized, but delevelers could gain, at most, 5 points above the level maximum. All the incentive for deleveling 10 or more levels would be wiped out.
