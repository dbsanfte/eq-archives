Message Board Guidelines, please read!

VN Terms of Service Broken Down Quick & Easy:

User Conduct:

  • Profanity will not be tolerated, Links to profane topics on other boards/web sites, Vulgarities used in the Subject and body of a post that cannot be used on network television without punishment will not be tolerated on the forums. This would include the derogatory use of terms such as 'gay', or any other term related to those previously mentioned. Self moderation such as putting a * in the place of one or a few of the letters in a vulgar word is not allowed. If we can understand what the word is, it's not allowed. These are family oriented forums, and we need to keep the language that way as well.


  • No real-life threats/flames. (We’ve had FBI investigations started in the past...)


  • Harassing another user via open forum or private messages is not allowed.


  • Slander or 'libel' (published slander) will not be tolerated. That means do not slander/libel other posters, VIPs, Moderators, Verant or Sony employees. Examples of slander/libel include attacks on their moral character, "sell out" or similar, or anything else with the sole purpose of negatively effecting someone's name or reputation.


  • No Spam. This includes referral games!


  • No overly obscene remarks, that goes double for anything with sexual overtures in it. This includes links from Somethingawful.com!! Do not link anything from this site, as it turns very obscene, and will not be allowed


  • No posting of private messages, unless BOTH PARTIES agree to having them posted. That is why they are called private.


  • Links to sites with material meant for the adult crowd is also not allowed, also no links to exploits/warez for games.


  • Spamming messages across the Vault Boards is not acceptable. If you wish to advertise something, post it in ONE place and create a signature link to your topic if you wish.


  • Discussion of or links to 3rd Party Apps are not allowed, as 3rd party apps are not allowed in the game. We at IGN Vault respect the rules of the games we host fansites for, and will not act as a medium for any of the 3rd party apps, unless deemed legal by Sony.




Account Trades, Buying, Selling, etc.:

  • Account selling/trading/buying/giving is not allowed on our boards.


  • Links to eBay, eBay numbers posted or other auctions sites with the intent of promoting a sale prohibited. This includes your signature. Once again: you are not allowed to post account trading/buying/giving/selling on this board.


Consequences and Moderator Actions:

1st Offence: Users are usually warned via Private Message if a violation occurs. If the post is locked the users warning may also be in the first post of the locked topic. Also a warning is placed on the user's account. If the violation is severe/extreme enough, the user will be immediately banned without a warning when the thread or any part of it violates the Terms of Service or Board Rules.

2nd Offence: User is banned for 24 hours to 1 week depending on severity of infraction.

3rd Offence: User is banned for 72 hours to 2 weeks to permanent depending on the severity of the infraction

Ban Evasion: A user caught evading a ban (creating a new account or using an old account after being banned) will have the evading account permanently banned and the original accounts ban lengthened by one week for each evade. If evade accounts are continually created or used, the original account may be permanently banned.


Notes:


  1. As a general rule, Moderators do not enter into discussions about Moderator actions and/or their decisions. If you have a question about why something was moderated, address it via a Private Message to a moderator or Aurum or Sheyla and we will respond accordingly.



  2. If a post is locked/deleted, and you post "WHY WAS "X" POST LOCKED/DELETED?" it too will be locked/deleted and you will be banned. USE THE PM SYSTEM. This includes reposting the thread that was locked in the first place



  3. The manager and moderators of EQ Vault and of the Vault Network are NOT employees of Verant or Sony. Verant or Sony do not in any way direct or influence board policy and/or policy enforcement.



Thank you for your cooperation,

The Everquest Vault Moderators
