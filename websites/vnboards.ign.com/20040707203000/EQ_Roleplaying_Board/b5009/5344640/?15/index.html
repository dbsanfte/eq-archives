I have waited nearly, if not more then one month for my reply email which I sent to Gordon, refering to his post made about the enforced roleplay servers. In that time he has managed to answer multiple message board posts but has he had time to check his email?

I have come to a conclusion that to make the final impact we DO need to organize some sort of system. Thus now being formed is EQRU, The EverQuest Roleplayers Union. We will begin by voting on possibly six to ten people who have good roleplaying skills and leave some impact or change to people around them.(can be just keeping your focus on roleplay and not wandering off)

Our goal at first will be to choose one of the new non-split servers and hopefully have the support to move our majority there (if people are willing to begin a new character- which I know will not be what everyone wants). Advising a code of ethics to go by preferably, ie what you should not do.. and give helpful information and hints.. Like not to say PP instead of Platinum Coins.

Our second goal, once we first settle and gain at least a raised eyebrow from Verant will be to petition, or peacefully protest on message boards or by email. Finally as our numbers will be in one location we should get noticed. If in the future Verant chooses to flag our already existing server into an (RP) flagged server and offer a server transfer for those wanting to leave before things set in to place. Either that or they could.. if they were kind enough create a fresh world and allow us to transfer into there, of if they are mean they could give us a fresh world and just tell us to start new character, since we would have already practiced that!

All roleplayers will be invited to sign up their character name or something, and email onto our person page. You, and They will be our supporters in the decisions that the 'chosen ones' have come up with to help us out.

I hope at least I will get some feedback from this post, it took my mind, and heart to write this to you to tell you all what I feel should be done. I do not want to see this just flushed out of the screen to page two before at least I can get through to at least one person.

Sincerely,

Good thoughts, guys. I'd be willing to give it a shot, but I can't promise I'd stay unless it worked out to my taste. Enforced RP servers are something thats been debated heavily on some other boards, and there are some real problems with the idea -- not least of which is there almost as many ideas on how to roleplay as there are roleplayers.

Some insist everybody has to be either good or evil with no room for neutrals. Others say it can't be a RP server unless its PvP. Since I mostly roleplay neutrals and I detest PvP I wouldn't like their server much at all, not that Verant would ever put forth the effort to make an enforced RP server anyway. A simple idea that doesn't involve Verant doing anything, like a grass-roots organized migration of RP'ers might actually work.

I play on Brell Serilis now, where we have quite a nice RP community, and I'm an officer in one of several excellent RP guilds that are cooperating to create RP events. It would have to be pretty good to beat that, but it could be done.
