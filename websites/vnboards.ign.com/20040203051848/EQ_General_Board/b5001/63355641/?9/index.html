IGN's boards are not really even an after thought for most EQ players really, there are dozens upon dozens of web sites devoted to EQ and have their own forums and I'd said the majority of people post on their own EQ guild webpages.

On which servers are most populated, click the button to sort the server list by population, those at the bottom will be the most populated almost always those will be the 3 European language servers with the special rule set-PvP servers at the top being least populated.

People suggest going to Stromm the "newbie" server if you are just starting out, I couldn't say personally, but I suspect as Stromm has been out for about 6 months or so now it's not really any more or less "n00bish" then any of the other older servers- it will just have less items up for sale and for higher prices because of the newer economy.
