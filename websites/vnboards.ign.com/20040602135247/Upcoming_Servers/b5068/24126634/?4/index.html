I am creating a well-organised, efficient guild.

First, about the guild: the purpose of the guild will be to provide an elite and challenging atmosphere where one can enjoy the game while socializing with friends. We will stage large raids and we will have many small events focused on getting experience and improving our characters. There will also be roleplaying events and community service (what?! community service! - Yes we will do our best to improve the state of the Stormhammer server by helping out the community).

The guild will award equipment based on a DKP system where members earn points by participating in the events above.

The guild, which I will refer to as "The Twelve" will have 12 founding officers from levels 57-60. For the first couple months, theses officers will be the only guild members and their focus will identifying potential recruits into the guild. Once each officer has identified 6 recruits, the number of the guild will expand to 72 regular members from levels 55-60 and 12 officers (who should all be level 60 by now).

Three to six months later each regular member will have the opportunity to sponsor one friend to admit into the guild. This initiate must meet the approval of the appropriate guild officer and must be able to integrate into the guild with the general approval of all existing members. At this point twelve new officers will be named and the guild will expand to 24 officers and 144 members (levels 55-60). At this point the guild willl also be divided into four houses, each house having three founding officers and three regular officers.

The guild will have its own domain, either Everquester.com or The Twelve.org. On this website will be the DKP database (for awarding participation points), a chat server, and a BBS.

The foundation blocks of this guild will be based on friendship. I have seen many "uber" guilds fall apart because their members did not start as friends. It is not the purpose of this guild to go out and immediately wipe out north ToV and such. In the beginning we will focus on tactics and teamwork, honing the skills of the guild so that we do not have to resort to "zerg" tactics to accomplish our goals.

As the guild grows we will tackle larger and larger obstacles. We will also focus on giving back to our community. By this I mean we will help individuals or smaller guilds accomplish goals that they might not be able to do on their own. We will stage roleplaying events and exp-gathering parties.

I will be interviewing for the positions of the initial Twelve officers over the new few weeks. You can contact me at snaggle@everquester.com. You can also drop in on the Zebuxoruk server and send me a tell. For those that want to do a background check on my capabilities as a leader and my integrity, contact any of the older "uber" guilds on The Rathe or Zebuxoruk. I can also provide GM referrals upon request.
