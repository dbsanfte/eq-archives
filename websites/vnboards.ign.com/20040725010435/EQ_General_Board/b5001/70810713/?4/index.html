(Hi, this thread is NOT intended to be EQ vs FF issue dispute or anything like that... if you are FF11 fan and have got something to say other than its AH system, go check out the other boards dedicated to FF11)

I spent a month or so to play Final Fantasy 11, and really liked its Auction House (AH) system. It works like this:

1. Each player "sells" up to 7 items to AH.
You can set its price yourself. A certain amount of
money is charged as AH fee. (ex. 1 copper to sell
1 item, etc.)

2. Other players can see a list of all the items being sold (put on AH), and a brief "history" of past sales record of each item (like the closing price for the past 7-10 entries) is shown as well.

3. People who want to buy the displayed items can enter a price. Among the same items (sold by different players), the one with the lowest price (and that is equal to or cheaper than the amount you entered) is sold ot you.

4. The AH can hold your itmes, up to 7 items, for approximately 2-3 RL days (I could be wrong with this. a bit shorter or longer than that). You get the money if any of the items is sold via the internal post service system. Items not sold within a certain period of time will be returned to you.

Obviously, EQ doesn't have the user-to-user or AH-to-user post service so each user might have to physicall visit the AH to receive whatever money you got by selling them/ or unsold items, etc... but still, this system would be great for any EQ player who can't put his/her PC turned on for the whole day/weeks just for the purose of selling items..

What do you think? Has there been any attempt to implement such automatic auction house-type system? Or is it simply out ot question due to system/ technical limitations, etc.? Or do most ppl simply prefer the current sell-stuff-yourself system?

LIke I said, I am NOT here to say EQ or FF is superior to the other or anything... I personally feel the AH system in FF (that doesn'T require the user to keep his PC turned on for long time) would just benefit many in EQ as well, and jut want to hear other ppl's frank opinion. Again, I'm okay with current bazaar system (at least I can search for items being sold and buy from other ppl) and it's not like saying "no AH-type system in EQ? ok I'm out of EQ" or anything like that.

You can still haggle if you can catch the person still on their trader. I've done it a few times and had it done to me a few times. My funniest one was the guy who wanted to give me 4k for my gold tipped boar horn when I was asking 5k and everyone else was selling for 8-10.5k. He said it wasn't worth more than 4k and that those people were messed up. I laughed and wished him luck on getting one cheaper. Now, a few days later, I've sold two of them for 8.8k (my cost 19p, bought them from npc merchants) and he's got one for sale for 8k. I laughed very heartily.

Don't forget npc merchants sometimes have items for sale cheaper than what pc traders sell them for. I've bought singing steel arms for 300p off pok npc merchant, elder spiritualist pieces for 400-800p which were going for 600-3k in bazaar.

Shop around, learn the prices on your server and don't be afraid to buy from npcs. It can lead to profit.
