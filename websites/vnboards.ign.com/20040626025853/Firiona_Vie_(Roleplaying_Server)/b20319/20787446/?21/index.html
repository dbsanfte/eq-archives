I was at this GM event and thought it was neat also. It was the first one I have ever been to actually (I have only been playing EQ for about two months).

The adventure begain for me when I heard a shout from a Gnome about strange warders being spotted in Steamfront while hunting Wilowisp in Greater Faydark. I decided to take a stroll over there and see what was disturbing this poor Gnome. Just a short walk away from the entrance to Lesser Faydark I found two warders playing tag with about five people. I think one was named "A Mischevious warder" and looked like a tiger and ther other was named "The Sarcastic Warder" (I think) and looked like a basilisk.

After a few more minutes of tag the mischevious warder proposed a foot race to the Windmills. After a count of five we all took off running (there were about ten of us by then) and I believe the mischevious warder won the race (As a tiger he was rather quick). I actually thought about trying to snare him to give myself a "competitve advantage" but decided against it (I didn't want to make him mad and ruin everybodies fun).

Then the guessing game started. Basically one of us would start guessing and he ocasionally gave us clue. I think the first item we guessed about was "a muffin". I can't remember the second, it may have been "a halfling", and the third was "a statue". I almost thought I had the third one, I said a rock and the mischeviuous warder said I was very close.

Overall I thought it was really neat and fun even though I didn't get anything. I hope they have more events like that.
