So I was just talking to my brother about resubscribing Rrail (I wanted to know if there were any zones where I could just train people till I got bored), and this question popped into my head.

I rememeber back in the day I would always train Velk the Sorcerer to the zone line (back when there were always at least 45 people in the zone). I loved watching the zone go from 45 to 2 or 3 people (any monks in the zone) in a matter of 30 seconds, as Velk ruined on everyone.

I also remember, whenever there were people in Upper/Lower Kobold in Velks, I would always go to the glass wall, so that all the statues/gargoyles would train through those 2 camps. On occasion, if there was a real jerk in the group that I didn't like (I'd always go through the camp after the train had cleared everyone out, to see if there were any dead mobs with hawt l3wt on them, like gems), I'd offer to drag his body to zone line. Since this guy was a jerk (whoever it might have been, I believe I did it to Devani), I would pull the body as far into the castle as I felt comftorable going, and leave his body there, and tell him it got stuck in a wall or something. Then they'd /petition and a GM would laugh at them because it was sitting in the castle. After wasting that much time, they'd then have to find a necro to summon it, or a ballsy monk. I loved that. That was a real winner to me.

I also loved going up to the top of Velks with a buffbot (usually a friends Cleric) running on a second EQwindow (before the official one was implimented, which was a dozen times better than this new one) and killing everything that popped in Frenzy (if, beforehand, they had said anything to me about pulling from their camp. Normally I just pulled from anywhere in the zone, and no one complained, but about once a night you'd get people in Frenzy who would send me a tell and tell me to stop pulling from there. I'd kindly inform them that I pull from the whole zone and do not recognize camps in an EXP zone. They would threaten to call the GM's, which is when the buffbot came out. If they were especially rude, I'd kill their named and then /auction the loot right there in velks (though, if someone in the group hadn't been a jerk to me, and /telled me and was "hey, look, i dont care about the EXP but I've been camping this to get the haste gloves" or whatever, I'd give it to them). I loved when GM's came and asked me to leave, and I could just say "No, go away".

Also, this isn't in EQ, but Offensive Warcraft (that's not what we really call it but I think I'd get banned for saying the big R word) is hilariously fun. That's where you build, and inbetween building, you say as much offensive stuff as you can, to slow them down. They will, inevitably, slow down the building to yell at you about saying something offensive, and then you continue. When you are ready to attack, let one of the big offensive bombs off. When they start to lecture, attack. As you are beating them, call them all the offensive things you can think of and laugh at them because they are getting beaten by _____ (whatever they've been calling you the whole time).

And, for a final one, cheating Counter-Strike, though I've only done it twice (on my brothers computer, I can't seem to find my Half Life CD anywhere), is awesome. It's even better if you make a macro key that, after every kill, you can say "You just got killed by a cheater". Make sure, also, to make your name "I AM CHEATING". Try as much as you can to shoot people through walls, too, for maximum anger. Its best when you can find a very popular server with no admin.

So what do you do, like this, for fun? I'm always interested in finding new games to terrorize.

Sidenote: I know only a fraction of the people here enjoy doing this kind of stuff, so I don't expect a big return on this thread. But if you've got a popular multiplayer game, that you know exploit/cheats for that really make people mad, please tell.
