Honestly, I'm not sure it's worth it. They only last 5 seconds each. Unless you have a lot of people doing physical damage it's not worth it-- that's right, if someone is using an elemental weapon they don't work. If you build an entire group around it, it might be alright, but unless you have at least 3 people in the group who will benefit, I don't think they are worth carrying. At 5 seconds long that's like 2 swings from a melee weapon or bow, 3 if you're lucky. Assuming two hits from each person that's 60 damage at a cost of 5 power (not bad) but also 17% health.

If you really want to maintain these two constantly I guess I'd try going N/Mo and use Mending on yourself, then whenever you get low use heal area or something. Still, my feeling is you can probably do a lot more damage and help the group a lot more using other skills. That is unless you run with a full warrior/ranger group where no one is using elemental damage mods.
