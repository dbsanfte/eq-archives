okay found the list, this is what is needed.


Kiola Nuts - these nuts are found on the island in the zone Erud's
crossing ; very simple to get.
Erud's Tonic - This item will be harder to get. The most obvious place to get it is Erud, however, it is a crime punishable by death to those how try to smuggle it off the island. There is, however, a man, Captain Rohand, inside the Mermaid's Lure that can obtain it.
Honey Jum - This is a drink that can be found in the taverns of
Riverdale; once again, quite easy.
Koalindl Fish - This will be one of the hardest of the items to get. This
small, defenseless, little fish is found in the waters of the Temple of Light in North Qeynos. The fish will not kill you, its all the people around
that will. I strongly caution you, you can not do this part alone, unless you kill everyone in that area first, and that's a feat in itself. These goody goodies really like their fish. REALLY LIKE THEIR FISH!!!
