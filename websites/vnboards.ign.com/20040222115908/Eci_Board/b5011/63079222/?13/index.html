In just the way I expected it to be.

The first 30 minutes of the movie I was saying to myself "Oh crap I wasted money, this movie isn't going to suck". But don't worry, they soon remedied that, the second they went into the tree grove. YAH MOST SWINGARMS CAN STAND UP TO A NICE SLAP INTO A TREE.

Despite the movie just going downhill from there, there are some very redeeming qualities:

The two girls. Good. God. You could not pick 2 hotter girls for that movie (that would fit in, I mean. Those girls looked the part. No big boobs, ect). I <3 that blond girl but theres just something about the evil girl. Either way, that was worth the 8 dollars in itself.

Ice-T is a comedy goldmine. I look at him and laugh. My favorite part of the entire movie is when he bursts through a police blockade and summarily says "F*** THE POWLEASE!".

Go see it, it's only 8 dollars and 2 hours of your life, and you will get to say you were a part of the new trend which resulted in the deaths of 100's of 18 and 19 year old kids who thought it would be a good idea to run out and pick up a 1000cc supersport.
