Alikat_Astrae

Registered: Jun 01
 		Date Posted: 7/23 11:54am Subject: RE: what weapon should I focus on for a beastlord?
I should clarify: Hand-to-Hand is the top Beastlord melee skill, and your epic weapon is H2H. But when you are fighting mobs that enrage, riposte for hundreds and hundreds, and so forth, you might need to switch out to a high damage, high delay weapon, which is where 2H Blunt comes in.

Wielding a couple of 1H blunts is just silly. Your H2H skill caps higher and has lots of great weapons to choose from, so 1H blunt is kind of a waste. but if you still want to do good melee damage on a mob that you just can't afford to be riposted on much, use 2H blunt.

Keep all your offensive skills maxed, it makes good sense. But from what I've seen, H2H and 2H Blunt are the top two you'll use when you hit level 65.
