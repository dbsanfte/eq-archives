By Sony's definition, a gank is if a raid actually aggros and begins fighting a mob, and THEN another raid outdamages them and gets the kill. Our server defines that as a Kill Steal, the most heinous of crimes.

Let's use our common sense here though. If you are heading towards a named mob and you pass a group in the room right before the mob and don't stop to ask, there is a VERY good chance you are gonna get a complaint.

If you call orc 1, head there and there is a group there already, that doesn't give you the right to go to tent #2 and pull the Chief Ooognawhasit for his loot you say you don't need. Assuming that what Katur says happened was actually what happened, this is clearly a gank by the definition on our server.

Just use your common sense though people. If you want to keep your honor intact, keep a good reputation, whatever, take a couple minutes to talk to the folks near whatever you are going for and wait for a response. It isn't like the named will depop in the 3 minutes you use to clear up each group's intentions. If they say they are going for it, ask them if you can make an attempt if they fail. That is how it is done honorably.

Our server is a very good server. In general, we all stay off each other's toes and co-exist peacefully. This is clearly a common sense issue. If you stop and take the time to clear up intentions, you won't get complaints.
