I haven't posted on this board since the 80's and thought this would be an appropriate thread to start again. It took me almost 3 years to hit 65 with my main (6 months with my number 2) and during that time I had many guildmates pass me up because they did the exp grind while I chose to adventure and enjoy the simple pleasures the game offers. Most of those who past me up are no longer playing EQ. Although I don't necessarily agree with the whole role playing bit (I have enough to worry about besides "staying in character"), but if that makes the game more enjoyable for you then I say go for it. If being a whiny tard that thinks his 650+ AAs make up for his lack of social skills (I'm not mentioning any names)is the way to go, then I say go for it. If we were all the same this would be a very boring planet.
