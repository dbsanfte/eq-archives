At level 51 I am never LFG more than 5 min. Often as soon as I log on I am flooded with tells by groups looking for a cleric.
Cleric is by far the easiest and fastest leveling class I have ever played.
I like doing the LDoN adventures because I am more than just a heal pet. I get to paci, root CC, nuke and stun.
Note: I only nuke and stun if I am at full mana.

Solo? yes I can. I have soloed (old world) undead dark blues without much problem as long as I have KEI.
However, I have made sure to keep my def skill and evoc skill maxed for my level and this helps alot.

To keep def skill up I will toss HOT on myself then toss a CH on the MT when there are 3-4 adds and let them run up and beat on me. The enchanter can then mez them off me one at a time. This also helps keep the enchanter from dieing since I have alot higher AC and HPs and can take a lot more or a beating than he can.
