Why make necro a secondary when you're using mostly necro skills? That's lost efficiency. I find Soul Reaping to be immensely useful. Say you're pouring mana into killing a single enemy: when he dies, you get some MP to get started on the next target with.

Granted, Soul Reaping works only when there's something dying. But when is there something -not- dying when you need MP? Do you ever need MP when nothing is in danger of being killed? Don't bump necro down to secondary unless you're just using a couple spells. Stick with primary. The Reaping is very much worth it.

Furthermore, we're damn stylish.

yes it has lol... everyone has their opinions just like with everything else. i do not believe the dev's got to making the necro, and when it came time for the primary attribute that they just went "ah scew it! lets just do this and get it over with."

during all the betas since E3 of 2004 i experimented with necro's whether using it as a primary, or with it as secondary. at first i did not understand the soul reaping or how it could have any real value compared to other classes and their primary attributes. then again this is a necro we are talking about. it isnt a no-brains class like warriors, this is one of the finesse classes, along with the mesmer, and the ranger IMO. on that note and with different experiments i have come to really enjoy the fruits of soul-reaping.

not all but quite a few warriors i know do not even use strength. they much rather go with tatics. so there to me is another debatable primary attribute.

energy storage is great, in fact i think it is probably the best of all the primaries out there. as a battle goes on though that pool can get drained quickly. also if you are using a lot of exhaustion spells and unless you have a necro throwin blood ritual on you, or some other fast energy regen then you might find yourself in a bind.

i hardly ever see a necromancer left clicking their energy bar during a fight or after saying "my energy is 4 out of 85" or anything like that. but at the same time we are also casting/hexing during the battles just as much as the other casting classes.

we get energy from everything that dies. be it player, pet, summoned undead, mob, ranger spirit, or npc. during a battle a low 5 in soul reaping can return a lot of energy to help spam skill after skill.

just my 2 cents on the subject.
