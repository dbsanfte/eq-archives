You should lay off the crack pipe. The problem with the above build is that it really doesn't seem to be focused at all. What's the purpose of the build? To do damage, to protect people, what?

I don't see it as being very effective because your atts are so spread out.

My suggestion to you: Pick a role for your build, the kind of thing you would be doing in a group. This can be something like: anti-caster, anti-warrior/ranger, energy denial, health degen, protection, or whatever, but just pick something, and create the build around that.

It's even possible to create a build that can do multiple things if you're creative, such as energy denial AND health degen AND anti-warrior. That said, there needs to be synergy between the various atts/skills used in your build (which means they need to work together), and I just don't see much synergy in your build.
