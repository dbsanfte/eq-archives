Gnomes naturally have very high Intelligence, which is the prime statistic for a career in necromancy, making a Gnome Necromancer a perfect combination of race and class. Moreover, gnomes are a lot of fun to play, and to play with.

One thing to keep in mind is that, from the Steamfont Mountains (where young gnome necromaners enter the world of Norrath, next to the gnomish city of Ak`Anon), the only way out to the rest of the world (aside from magical teleportation from a high-level wizard or druid) is to run through Lesser Faydark, which is dangerous at ANY level. Once you do leave, I highly recomend that you get your soul Bound elsewhere right away.

Once you do decide to brave the terrors of Lesser Faydark to see the outside world, I would try to get to Freeport. Find some Dark Elves and get them to teach you Dark Elvish.

While you are in the area, kill a lot of Deathfist Orcs. This will make you more accepted by the Humans of Freeport without losing the approval of any evil factions (well, aside from faction with the Deathfist Orcs themselves). You can either sell the belts you loot of the orcs, or use them yourself to improve factions. There are five different quests using the Deathfist Slashed belts, and four of them improve faction.
