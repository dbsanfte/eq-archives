Warriors are a unique and valuable class. Their pure tanking ability is unmatched, and they do nice damage. Out of combat they have almost no useful skills so must rely on their party for heals, teleports, SoW, buffs, etc, and in general make poor tradesmen due to low Int and Wis (unless you are a DE, hehe), so they are not the class for everyone. But inside combat they have a definate place in a party.

For more information on warriors, try the warrior email list: eq-warrior@onelist.com. If you are still unhappy after some discussion on there, perhaps you have chosen the wrong class. In that case you should experiment with some other characters until you find what is right for you.
