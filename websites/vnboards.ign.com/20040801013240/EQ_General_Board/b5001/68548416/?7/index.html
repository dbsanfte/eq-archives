Dying in an outdoor zone isn't that big of a deal, -except The Grey. Or under water.

The real pain comes in dungeons.

If you can't find your corpse outside, you can purchase a rod in most starting cities called, Chip Boned Rod. This has three charges of "Locate Corpse" on it.

Exploring in dungeons is very hazardous so be careful.
