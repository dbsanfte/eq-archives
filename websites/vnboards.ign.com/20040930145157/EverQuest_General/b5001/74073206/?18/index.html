Not really favorite zones here, but the reason I like alot of zones:

Tox Forest/Erudin - Used to be busy old school, but now is relatively empty due to uselessness.

Kerra Ridge - Empty, but once again, way back in the day had some great action.

Erud's Crossing - Back in the day wisp groups. People would be standing in line for wisps. Then the undead sailors would come up and terrorize the beach.

Qeynos - Memorable town, really feels like "home". Used to be newbies all over the gates, hunting beetles, running from Fippy.

Everfrost Peaks - Memorable hunting zone on first character.

Freeport East - First ever character, Rogue, started here. Couldn't find guild for a while.

Oasis - Lots of hunting. Good layout.

Guk - Great old school and new school dungeon. Used to have great times here trying for loot.

Kelethin - One of the coolest towns. Great music.

Field of Bone - My favorite newbie zone.

Dreadlands - One of the most interesting zones ingame.

Karnors Castle - Was awesome when Kunark came out.

Old School Skyshrine - Was awesome when getting no-drops. New zone revamp ruined it.

Wakening Lands - Coolest Forest Zone in game

Cobalt Scar - Really seems like a far-out place. Fun.

Plane of Storms - Love the ambience.

Plane of Valor - Love the zone to death, was the farthest zone I got to in EQ.


And of course, Qeynos Aquaducts, the dropout zone =)
