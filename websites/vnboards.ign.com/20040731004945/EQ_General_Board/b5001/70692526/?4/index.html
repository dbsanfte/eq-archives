"1. Being such a newbie, it it worthwile to buy the 2 expansions not included in Evolution? Is there anything in them for people below lvl 50?"

I would buy LDON because you can start doing the dungeons at 15 with a group. GoD I would wait on unless you really want the exp bonus for group leader and to play the Berserker class.

"2. If I hold off and buy the next expansion that comes out, will it include the content in the previous 2 or will I still have to buy them as well?"

Doubt it. Not for a long while after they have come out do they group them like Evolutions.

"3. At what lvl should I look to start grouping? My main character is a lvl 6 Dwarven Paladin. Also, I've read on a few websites that there is a level limit that determines if a higher or lower level character will recieve experience in a group ie: as a lvl 6 I can only group with somebody below a certain level or they will not recieve experience. I've done /who in Butcherblock several times but the lowest I've seen so far are in the mid teens. Can I group with such people or would it just be a waste of their time?"

You can see who is looking for a group at your level by typing /lfg then click on the Get matches button. You can also turn on your lfg flag there and let others know you want to group. You as a dwarf paladin will be quite self sufficient most of your life, soon as you get your spells and your heal at lvl 9. Stick to undead soon as you start getting the Ward Undead line of spells. You will own undead, only other class with better wards are Clerics and maybe necros. Generally people don't group below lvl 15ish because there is no need. All classes can solo decent till then. Starts becoming more difficult at 15ish.
