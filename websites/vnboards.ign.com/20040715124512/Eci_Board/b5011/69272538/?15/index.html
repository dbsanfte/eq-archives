Arrk-
I stopped leaving my bazaar mule with /afk on about a year ago. I was going LD *all the time* after only a few hours. The same was happening to my friends. We have long held a theory that being /afk for an extended period of time would cause linkdeath, and most of us do not use it anymore when leaving our mules on overnight/during the day when we're at work. I think that's why an awful lot of bazaar traders don't use the /afk tool when they're in bazaar mode. Of course, we have no proof that there's a link between being /afk a long time and linkdeath, but since I don't use /afk I go linkdead a whole, whole lot less in the bazaar. Test it out and see if it helps.



Also, I think the message you are getting is related to sitting at server select too long after going LD, as that happens to me too sometimes. I haven't ever seen an inactivity message except that one. It doesn't take long either.
