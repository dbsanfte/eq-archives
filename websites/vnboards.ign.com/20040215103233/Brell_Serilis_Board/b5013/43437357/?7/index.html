Sadly, the RP situation and number of RP guilds in EQ is nothing like it was 2 years ago, when you could have guildwars and multiple guild RP events/storylines. The number of guilds geared towards RP along with their memberships have dwindled significantly.

EQ is simply not a good vehicle for longterm RP. Evoluxa continues to be an RP-friendly guild (many people play the game in character and RP with each other)and if you cast out a line, people will take the bait and RP with you to your hearts content. Our membership is very strong (in spite of a long and arduous recruitment process)but the on-going storylines and events are something that has been fading out.

So for Evoluxa, though there are no longer RP events (other than guildings, meetings and weddings) you can still make RP happen.

My suggestion is to find a group of people you can enjoy spending time with and grow the RP from there. The hours you play are more of a key factor than anything. You won't be any happier in an RP guild with few people on during your playtime, than you would be in a hard-core raiding guild, so though you might want a guild to be RP-friendly, don't use RP as your end-all for finding a home.

Look into the guilds of people you enjoy hunting with, take them out for a spin, try some RP on people while in a pick up group.
