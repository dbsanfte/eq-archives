Hey Sass, this is Tsu from ACR.

I'm a true EQ newb in that I dont put much emphasis on leveing as I do questing.

My characters are all on Stromm:

12 Dwarf Paladin
10 Barbarian Shaman
10 Wood Elf Druid
7 Barbarian Rogue
8 Dwarf Cleric
5 Human Monk
8 Halfling Ranger
11 Vah Shir Beastlord

I do every quest I can for each zone I go to. I signed up for the premium service at http://everquest.allakhazam.com and that helps alot. Even the free service is a ton of help.

I find that on Stromm, if you are nice and polite, the higher levels will help you alot. I had a nice level 31 druid help me out by killing the 4 bandits near the chessboard in ButcherBlock so my newb Cleric can get their tongues for his armor. I just asked very politly in OOC. Just dont beg.

I make plenty of money (for a newb at least) by using my Ranger to track large skunks, goblins, and aviak chicks in ButcherBlock.

Pristine Skunk Claws
Pristine Aviak Chick Talons
Green Goblin Heads
Green Goblin Blood
All sell for 5pp. I can make 50-60 pp an hour with my Ranger. This has allowed my to get some pretty decent gear for all of my newbs and to help get all the newb armor for all of my characters.

You can get some great deals on equipment from the Bazaar. Just type /baz while in the Bazaar to bring up the search window.

I found out the Paludal Caverns on Luclin are tremendous XP for low levels 8+. It has a high XP mod so you get a ton of experience for each kill.



While we are few and far between, there are still a few true newbs out there.
