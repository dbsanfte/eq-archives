You want a story?
Here is the personal message I sent to my guildmates after reaching a milestone in my studies:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Even though I was weary from my studies, I had plainly healed at least 40 weary hunters that day to practice my skills, I was eager to join my friends once again at the Lake of Sick Signs <Ill Omen>. Having bound my soul to the foreign
land for ease of travel, I simply called upon Karana to take me there.

When I arrived, Parserus, Shalkai, and Haliken were already engaged in battle. I received a warm welcome from my brothers, and they kindly
regrouped to have me join them. Sarnaks, exiled Iksars, skeletons, sabertooths and goblins all engaged in battle with us. With Shalkai's songs
of healing - that man's voice is as sweet as bixie hunny - I was doing very little to help the party, although I did practice some of my spells which damage and got in a few crushing blows.

Eventually the members of the party changed. Shalkai had to leave us - I think he was meeting a girl! - and Wile, Nendiane, and Gogak had arrived. With a full hunting party we battled many foes. Gogak, and the rest of us,
were making good with the Iksars in Cabilis by slaying their enemies. Will I ever go to Cabilis? Probably not. But for Gogak's sake, it was working.

We were fully engaged for most of the night. A few of the pulls got a bit tense...but generally Aven ruled the beach. Absolutely no one died, thanks to everyone's skills. After one of the kills, I remember not exactly which
one it was, Karana whipered in my ear, "You are ready to progress, my child." I was thrilled, for it was my friends who had helped me become
worthy of a surname and Karana's blessings.
I believe Parserus and Wile - or maybe Nenediane, I am just too tired to remember - were also blessed by the gods. It turned out to be a wonderful evening in the Lake of Feverish Charms.

I thank you, my friends, for letting me join you in your hunts ~ for I am not quite as experienced, yet you treat me as an equal.*bow*

Me, Im an old man, Ive traveled to many lands and seen quite a few things. This looks like a nice spot to rest my bones for a bit. Good conversation is hard to come by, if theres any doubt, try talking to an ogre.

Tis only fair that I share a story. Twas not so long ago that I was wandering this new land they call Kunark, in an evil place known to many as the Burning Woods. There have been rumors that scrolls containing ancient and forgotten magical secrets have been found there. Being a magician by trade I felt compelled to explore this area, and perhaps gather some of the this forgotten arcane knowledge. The land was crawling with all sorts of foul beasts. I spotted a forest giant out in the distance, by his attire I could tell it was one of the ancients, and these creatures were rumored to possess the scrolls I was after. So, I made my way towards him, my trusty pet following close behind. As I moved into range I commanded my pet to attack the foul thing. And believe me, he was foul. I didnt know anything that was actually alive could stink like that. The fight was going well, my pet was attacking him as I cast my spells. Then, an evil creature appeared out of nowhere and joined the fight. An undead ape began to attack my pet. I knew I was in trouble, my pet was getting close to death, and both the giant and the ape were still alive. I turned and ran, using what little time I had to get away. Luckily Ive aquired an item that allows me to run considerably faster than I normally would. I managed to out run them, and I sat down to meditate and regain my mana.
Time passes and I summon forth another pet, and head back out in search of adventure. I see an undead ape milling around, so I send my pet in. The fight is going well when friends arrive. The giant and the ape from before return and begin attacking me and my pet. Once again, Im forced to flee. And once again, I was able to outrun them.
I heal and meditate and summon another pet. I head out again. I come across a hornet and attack. As the hornet is getting close to death, 2 wurms come wandering along, from behind me. As I turn and run, the 2 apes and giant from earlier arrive. Somehow I managed to outrun them. A recieved a communication from someone asking me if I was ok. I said that I was and they said they were suprised. They had seen me running with 2 wurms, a giant, 3 apes, a hornet, and 2 sarnak chasing me. Was quite a time.

/ooc nice thing about burning woods, you dont have to zone to get away from things, you can outrun them with sow or jboots, but they do remember you, if you get close to them again, they will come back after you, as I learned this day.

Ahh, I have many stories to tell, adventures to share, but I will leave you with this little tale for now.
