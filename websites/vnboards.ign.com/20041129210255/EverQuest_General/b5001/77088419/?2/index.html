Basically, in the present EQ, he and his Freeport militia thugs own 2/3rds of Freeport with the paladins and other hippies running North Freeport.

The short version of how this came to be was Lucan was the paladin left in charge of the city while the paladins/clerics/warriors all went off on a crusade in Fawydwar (spelling is way off, its been awhile). Lucan became a dictator and allied with the rogues and dark elves to insure his position becoming the figure paladin players on their original epic quest know and love so much today.

As to Npcs or events in the present EQ revealing how the changes come to be that lead up to the storyline in EQ2 I know of none, but I think they added all those swarms of rats to the original starting cities right around the same time Ratonga were revealed as the new race in EQ2.
