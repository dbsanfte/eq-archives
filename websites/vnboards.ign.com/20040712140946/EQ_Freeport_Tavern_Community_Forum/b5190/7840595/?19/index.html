Ahhh...i remember the night i found these buggers

i was playing arrow, and hanging around in Kelethin (playing "Jump Off The Side") and all of a sudden i get a tell from a person i didn't know (now MOST of the time i assume it's someone i talked to and forgot, my memory leaves me unless i know where and why i met this person)

tell was something like this

Whoever tells you, "I am an intresting person.."

i sat there for a long time and did a few who alls, they were anon...hmm, checked the people in her guild.....no one on that i knew at least...so i replyed and said

"thank you for sharing"

then she said, "I read your message"

i thought long and hard (was 3 in the morning so no other way) and tryed to think where in the hell i could have left a message...

i decided that she had seen me on one of the boards (Eqvault, or the others i post on) and asked where she had seen my message

she sent me, "In Qeynos"

i scratched my head, at a loss and didn't say anything for a long time...she then sent me N/M.....well at this point, i needed to know what the hell she was talking about

i sent her question after question, still assuming it was on some board...she said it's in the Tavern in Qeynos

i said "in the game?"

she said, "yeah!"

i said i have no idea what the hell you are talking about, i'll make a newbie human, is it in North or South Qeynos?

she told me "South" and logged... (guess i upset her)

i logged a human, took me forever to find it (i had never been to Qeynos in my life, and had no idea where anything was) after i went up to a "Message board" that was crudly put on the wall (just looked out of place, ya know?) and i clicked it, expecting nothing...and it said, Loading, Please wait (or something like that) and then brought out a list of things such as buying/selling, personals, general, shit like that...

i looked all over for this post, and found it in the buying/selling...the writer WAS Arrow....i looked and looked to see if it was spelled different, or ANYTHING to explain...

the message sounded about like a Personal add, and stuck on the bottem of the post was

-Arrow the Bard-

well this confused the hell out of me...bards CAN'T use bows! why the hell would there be a bard named arrow

Best i can figure out is that before there was Arrow Ablaze, there was Arrow the Bard, and being as late as it was, no gms to take it off (didn't really wanna hear about this again) so i decided to amuse mysefl by attacking the enchanter by the docks with my human monk a few times waiting on a GM to answer my petition, after about half an hour, the enchanter got boring, and i was tired, so i logged, as far as i know, it's STILL there

I've also found one in the High Hold Keep when i was wondering around the upper floors of the keep...from what i can tell, they are not linked (what you write in Qeynos isn't in HHK) because the Qeynos zone one had MANY post (bring a starting newbie zone, i am guessing why, while HHK isn't an easy thing to get to at low levels, it's doable, but the Qeynos one is going to be found more often)

So these are the only ones i've seen, might be more, so far, both in taverns

*shrugs* i've seen these message pop on other boards, all with the same responces

(What message boards?)

So yep, they there, no, not used, but i like the fact that you can write them in ANY launguage you have

*shrugs* hope that got someone a laugh
