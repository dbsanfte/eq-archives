Author Topic: Disgraceful treatment of an honorable man.
Anon209.233.215.x
 Date Posted: 12/20/99 3:56pm Subject: Disgraceful treatment of an honorable man.
I have a brief commentary to make on the treatment that one of the most honorable people I have ever met received from members of several guilds as a result of a Fear Plane raid that took place on December 18, involving many members from KEG, MFH, and some from Shining Thread.

Felron Majere has been playing on the Rathe since May of this year, and is currently a level 50 Wizard. I’m sure that most, if not all of us who are over level 40 know exactly who he is. We have grouped with him, died with him, and I have never ever seen anyone have a bad thing to say about him. He has sacrificed himself for the good of his group countless times, and I’m sure that most of us owe him our lives at least once, if not many more times than that.

During the Fear raid on December 18, he was grouped with several friends. They arrived at the gate to Fear with very few other people present. The raid had been in progress for quite some time, and it was his first trip to PoF.

When his group zoned in, they were all killed at the gate. When their group reassembled to try for corpse recovery, Felron volunteered to go in and attempt to pull their corpses to the west wall camp. After 4 deaths by shiverbacks and scarelings, he had pulled most of their corpses to the south wall. Note: deaths by shiverbacks and scarelings – nothing else.

On one of his attempts to get corpses, he asked those in the zone for assistance, and was flatly told that he was on his own. Not, “We’ll help you when we have cleared that area”, or “Contact Snaggle and we will organize something”, simply “No – get out of the zone.” I doubt any one of you would have accepted this and written off your corpse as lost, and I defy you to say anything contrary.

On a subsequent attempt to retrieve corpses, Cazic was aggro, and killing players, shouting their names across the zone. As a matter of fact, we thought it was kind of exciting to see Cazic talking to the zone. As I understand it, when Cazic goes aggro on you, he summons you and beats you into a bloody pulp, or he simply kills you with a spell from across the zone. At no time was Felron ever summoned or attacked by Cazic-Thule. Not once. And at no time did he receive a /tell that he was causing problems.

It was after about his sixth death (we lost count) for the good of his group that players started popping up at the gate in the Feerott. Apparenly, Cazic-Thule was racking up a few kills. We don’t know who had aggroed him, but it was certainly was not Felron. If anyone has a screenshot or an unaltered /log of the event showing different, I suggest you publish it. But since such a thing does not exist, I’m not worried about it.

Despite this fact, someone got it in their head that Felron was the one who had aggroed Cazic, and the /shouted accusations began at the Feerott gate. It was a feeding frenzy of the entire zone deriding him for trying to help his friends. A feeding frenzy by those whom he had considered his friends. A feeding frenzy on an innocent man.

To those of you who participated in this disgraceful event and attacked this innocent man with no proof of his guilt, you should be ashamed of yourselves. I was utterly disgusted with the bullsh*t that was thrown at Felron in the ensuing maelstrom of /shouts. I thought your guilds were based on honor, fair play, and justice – apparently not.

To the one player in the entire zone who defended him, I salute you. Hammerlove, you are the most honorable player I have ever met, and I’m proud to have played on this server with you. To those of you who called Felron your friend and didn’t stand up for him, you should be even more ashamed of yourselves.

Felron deserves a public apology from everyone who took part in this public execution of an innocent man, particularly Magiana, the one name that stands out in his mind from the event.

All this being said, the fun of EverQuest is mostly based on the fun you can have with the other players. This situation has destroyed all of the respect Felron had for all of you, and your guilds in general. He has decided to stop playing on this server, and move to a different server. I am going with him. Nevertheless, this man deserves much better than what he received, as he was doing the only thing that he felt right – attempting to help his group. Felron leaving is a great loss for The Rathe, and I’m sure many of you will agree.

Some of this may fall of deaf ears, and I understand that. Personally, I don’t give a sh*t if it does. But I know that some of you will read what I am saying, and take it to heart. Felron was innocent of every accusation that was made. If you feel like responding to my comments, or apologizing in private, you can reach me at medding@mail.com. I will forward any appropriate apologies to him.

He asked for help from more experienced players in the zone, and was flatly turned down. Put yourself in his shoes:

What would you have done?

The end of the story is that most of the servers crashed later that night, and Felron got all his corpses back when the server came back up with Fear empty. For those of you who lost your corpses there, I hope you got your corpses back.
