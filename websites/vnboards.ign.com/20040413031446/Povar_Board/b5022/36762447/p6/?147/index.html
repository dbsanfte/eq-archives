Hi.

I could write a whole book, like I used to do back in the day, but I won't. It's been what; 2 and a half years since I disbanded NA and basically stopped talking to most people from EQ? Long time, and I'm sure almost no one will read this, but I'll write some stuff out hehe.

Some of the original Erudites on the server (there were only about 12 of us total for the first month or so or release) that I remember...Niefeng, Natascot, Runemaker, Mummeroth, Oxide, Mistabone, Darb, Dhaedolon, Eldriash, Makahai. We played for a few weeks on Erudin before anyone dared to adventure out to Qeynos.

Runemaker and I formed Lore Keepers and quickly got a decent sized guild. It eventually broke up when people started hitting the upper levels and Runemaker wasn't around (he was the leader). We didn't know how to raid and some didn't want to. I basically tore the guild apart and started up a new guild, Nightmares Asylum.

We took 18 people to our first raid, the efreeti in SolB. Then we honed our skills fighting fire giants and whatnot. Eventually we got pretty good and decided we wanted to start trying to kill dragons and the planes. Our first Naggy raid was a joint raid with RA which failed. Next day we went in and killed Naggy by ourselves.

My first time in PoF was when the Cylus carmine robe stuff went down. After that Nitross invited us to break fear with DV. We did joint raids on and off with DV for a bit and then they broke up. A lot of their members came over to NA, including Nitross a couple weeks later.

Don't need to go into the whole Nitross fiasco, but he basically tried to take the guild over. He got humiliated and I kicked him out. He reformed DV and kept threatening me and telling me he was gonna take most of NA's people. Only 2 people left, people he knew in real life.

Lots of leveling and farming happened and then people started killing Trak/VS. We went down for our frist attempt at trak and Harm CoH'd in front of us. The GM ended up porting out Harm because he decided we had been there first. We didn't PnP, but in all honesty I was about to try to pull it out because Baven was being such a jackass. We ended up getting the first cleric and ranger bp's on the server which pissed off Harm even more.

Past that not too much that was too exciting. Harm left the server, I disbanded NA because I was tired of the BS and the guild was too divided between powergamers and casual gamers. Basically left Triton and RA as the big guns on the server, and I don't know what it's like now.

Heh, I even remember the URL to our old board, though it got torn down: http://pub12.ezboard.com/basylum9978 Anyone remember when NA disbanded and Natascot made the entire board public? Heh that was great.

Anyway, I'd love to talk with some of the people who I played EQ with; friends, enemies, whatever. I'll be playing WoW, but it would be cool to get a lot of the old Povar players on the same server once it comes out. Playing EQ was some of the most fun I've had.

Email me: RegalNelson@yahoo.com

-Disputatious

Whee, let's see. All the Steel Accordance/Harmonium history has been laid down, and I don't think I can really offer anything new to it~

I saw no/few references to T'or Drakkon in this thread! Or the Fireblades. Very good guilds, if a bit too casual for my tastes. I still remember being told off my Lynn for saying "damn" in guildchat

During Harmonium's prime was fun...Rampant elitism, cliques, etc. All the drama of high school brought directly to you in MMORPG form! It seemed as if 99% of Harm were elitist bastards who would only reply to you with sarcastic responses. Though I must admit that it was very cool to get the serverwide first Innoruuk kill on Povar.

I remember camping Jboots and spending insane amounts of hours there. There was always a huge line of people there waiting for a super rare spawn. And there would always be those bastards who would kill steal and take the jboots. And that damned Ogre Captain who opened the door would let every damned newbie in, allowing him to join the "fun," or even KS the spawn.

Well, my "uberguild" history started out by getting invited by Ledzep and Recnelis to a new guild called "Nightmare's Asylum." I think I joined early enough to be considered one of the founding members. NA still was around a year ago or so, in a different form: Forces Unknown.

Apparently the currently leadership of T'or Drakkon, Balzac, had gone crazy and thought that people were attempting to take the leadership of the guild from him, so Recnelis, Ledzep, and I joined NA, because we didn't want to be associated with such insanity.

The early days of NA consisted of daily Fire Giant raids. I think we had the most Razing Swords of Skarlon after awhile, of anyone on the guild. (I got one when, after we wiped, and Lexus had won it, it was about to decay, so I looted it!) Hell, half of the people there were randomly invited. I remember doing /who all's and inviting high level people that were guildless or in non-uberguilds. In fact, I believe this is how we discovered Dunric. :P Dragoons Elite or something! I think.

We recruited a lot of people this way actually. Darren joined up, with a monk named Faeron(I think!), and a variety of other people. Disputatious and Devious ran the guild very well for quite some time, and we experienced much success with our new recruits.

After awhile, we killed Nagafen and Vox, securing our position as one of the better guilds on the server. It was kind of funny how all the other guilds were totally against the ascension of NA. Kind of like the real world I guess.

Next, we attempted PoF. This was VERY painful for us, as I am sure it was for a lot of other guilds. But probably moreso for us, because we were much less stringent in our recruitment policies. The ledge would be trained regularly. Heck, I remember when Devious had to loot my corpse because it was going to rot in 2 hours. Good times.

I soon became an officer of NA, and we had even more success. We cleared as much in PoSky as Triton had originally done, during a trip to get Fiery Avengers for Dunric, Ramstein, and Zealis. It was something like a three day ordeal. I had done an ungodly amount of research, and everything worked perfectly. I think we got to the sphinx island.

The whole Nitross/Dark Valor fiasco was pretty interesting too. I remember when Nitross, a then-officer of NA left and said he was going to take everyone with him. He took Giantt, unfortunately, signalling, in my opinion, the first step toward the downfall of NA. Nitross - You're a psycho! But you were also way too smart, and very good at manipulating people.

And I still remember when I left NA. I was such a loot whore back then. I am sure I am still resented by many for it. Ramstein beat me on a /rand 1 1000 on a valorium bp, and I blew up. I joined Triton with Darren after that, I believe with Varsity's recommendation, which was a HUGE honor.

Triton was very interesting at the beginning. I remember Zaar guildremoved Valarin about 10 minutes after I joined. I have been afraid of Zaar ever since.

I really loved Triton, though it definitely had its ups and downs. A few years ago, during the summer, I was convinced that Triton was going to disband. We had trouble getting more than 10 people on in any given day. We'd constantly fail things when we DID have enough people on, and it was general mayhem. I'm glad that we overcame that, though.

The day we discovered PoM was also extremely cool. Here's how it happened: I had become fascinated in finding PoM for some reason or another, probably due to the emulator. I remember one day I got bored and looked through every file using EQinside, when I spotted a picture of an upside-down joker's head in Temple of Veeshan. I told this to Giantt, and sure enough, the next day, he discovered PoM. And then I killed Irontaiil's mage, so only about 8 of us got to see it for months. I am sure people still resent me for that!

During the days that Triton was fighting, and beating Trakanon, I asked Everadias to run me out and log me off, because I had to get up early in the morning. A few days later, I logged on with every droppable item gone, including my precious Cloak of Flames! That day was fun. I ended up lying to GM Imia, saying I never shared my account info, and I was able to get my stuff back. After that, she found out I lied and suspended me for a week. Good times.

Planes of Power, SoL, etc, were all good fun. I think this post is long enough, though, without the various memories of those expansions.

But alas, I finally quit EQ, disappearing after an unfortunate incident of my college roommate attempting to kill me with Nyquil (which I am allergic to!):P Oh well. As a great man once said, RL > EQ.

Sorry for not listing very many names. But be assured that I remember all of you, from Kaenar in the Jboots camp and Khelnozz in Fireblades, to the fine crew in Triton.

Oh the nostalgia!

p.s. Damn you Celdor, Brodie, Ldiian, and the rest of FU or NA or Honored Circle or whoever it was for killing me! After a 45 minute fight with Mavelin when I was level 54, and then getting ganked after Imia ressed, me, I was a little pissed :P

p.p.s. I will always love you Xeffo! Join me in WoW!!!
