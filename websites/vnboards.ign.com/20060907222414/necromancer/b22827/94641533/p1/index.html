Just wanted to share my meleemancer build (N/W). Meleemancers are necros who also use a sword and fight there like death knights =D.

Attributes(not including ruins)
Blood Magic: 12
Soul Reaping: 3
Swordsmanship: 12

Ruins:
Blood ruins, + health ruins, and if u want Soul Reaping ruins.( use minor unless u want low health )

Armor:
Blood magic for head, Tourmenters or Bone will do fine

Mods:
Zelous (mana steal),Vampiric(life steal) or Sundering(armor pericing)I reccomend using furious(double adrenaline). For bottom Fortitude(+health), Sheltering(+ armor vs physical damge), or Defense(+ Armor)

Skills:
Dark Pact
Vampiric Gaze
Life Siphion
Life Transfer
Blood Renewal
Galrath Slash
Final Thrust
Res Sig

Strategy:
Havent used this guy in pve yet i use him in pvp. I heard meleemancers do well in pve tho. Dark Pact + Vampiric Gaze are ur spike u can spam these 2 and do damge fast if u want. Start off with Life Siphion then Life Transfer then start spaming the target(take out rangers,necros,mesmers,monks first) with Dark Pact then Vampiric Gaze. If u need to heal and u cant really drain any 1 use Blood Renewal then Vampiric Gaze. You can also use Vampiric Gaze to get health. Once your low on mana or just wana get mana back or just wana melee run up to them and use Galrath Slash. Once ur target is below 50% health use Final Thrust. Once u get mana back if he is not dead by now spam Dark Pact and Vampiric Gaze. ALWAYS KEEP UR TARGET DRAINED AND ALWAYS HAVE YOUR HEALTH REGENING. ussally takes me 10-40 second to kill some 1 depends on who im attacking.

I hope u enjoy my little guide. If u think u have something to improve my build plz post what and why.
