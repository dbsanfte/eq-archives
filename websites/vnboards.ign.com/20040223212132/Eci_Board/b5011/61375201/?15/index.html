I am currently reading the 3rd book and I don’t want to see the movie until I finished. I have several events that I like.

1. When Gandalf Fought the Balrog. I started to see the power in Gandalf but I felt he should have displayed more power. I envision Wizards as masters of magic (well, throw in some magicians too).

2. When Gandalf came back from the dead and how he went to the halls of King Theoden of the Mark and healed the king from the spells of Wormtongue. This showed me his power was greater than Saruman who was in the background of these spells over the King. I felt Gandalf was a bit too weak against Saruman in his first battle with him. Now he was the shiznit.

3. I always thought that Samwise was a sissy and a self proclaimed servant of Frodo. His character bothered me but finally they gave him some worthwhile existence when he battled Shelob. That was good to read.

4. Gollum is just a great character. Finally he showed his true colors by entrapping Frodo and Samwise with Shelob. His duel personality and the corruption from the ring and the power it had over him is just classic.

5. I find it hard to believe they actually won the battle of Helm’s Deep. They seemed out powered, outnumbered, and so forth. Kind of hard to believe.

6. I look forward to the Army of the Undead as I have not read about that yet.

7. And yes, I am a geek with LOTR trilogy.

Bahh, I like much more.
