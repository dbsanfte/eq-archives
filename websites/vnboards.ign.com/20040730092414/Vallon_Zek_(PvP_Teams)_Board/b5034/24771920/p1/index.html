Bhuuk,

I'm not in favor of making trading more difficult. Rather, I'm in favor of making it easier.

I'm in favor of Verant finishing the coding for the Bazaar and making the Bazaar operational. Let's hope they get that working soon. That will make trading SO much easier -- you can put your stuff for sale and go AFK and then come back in the morning and see what has sold. No haggling, no whining, no begging, no hassles.

Why is trading with everyone important? Simple... the more efficient the economy becomes, the more gear will flow down from the high level players to the lower level players... and plat will flow to the higher level players, presumably to be spent on raids or horses or other gear.

The more competition there is to sell something, the more prices will fall -- meaning that lower level players will be able to afford nicer equipment sooner. Which is more fun for everyone. And with better gear, they will level sooner -- meaning less boring monotonous exp nipple camping and more fun pvp.

So I'm in favor of open and free markets.
