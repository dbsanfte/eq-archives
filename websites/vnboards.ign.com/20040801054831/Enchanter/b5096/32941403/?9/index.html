I have actually had some more success since the last time i posted. I am now level 15 and in West Commons. How did I get to 15? From lvl 13 I have been soloing in WC fighting Young Kodiaks, Willowisps, and Giant Spiders. Why? Because you can make quite a bit of money. Any kind of bear has a (very small) chance to drop a HQ bear pelt that sells for 25 to 30 pp to other players. Players will also pay 2 to 3 pp for MQ pelts. Wisps drop Lightstones and Greater Lightstones that can be turned in for a lot of money in South Ro, or you just sell them to a merchant for a good price. (4.5pp for Greater, 1 for regular). Spiders drop silk that can be sold for quite a bit to players.

Anyway, soloing at this level is usually faster levelling than grouping, plus you are making good money. There is a derv camp in West Commons that you can take on with a group. Sometimes there aren't enough people in the zone that are of appropriate level, but you should be able to find one about 50% of the time.

I know what you mean, N. Ro used to be really crowded and now it is pretty empty. So try my suggestions. I have a feeling that grouping will become much easier in a few levels.
