Where are the American principles upon which our propositional nation was founded spelled out? Read the preamble to the Constitution, then scoot your eyes on down the page and read it to the end of the document, amendments and all.

You'll see that the overriding aim of that document is not to hand law from down on high to the people, like most other official documents in the world of that time. The overriding aim of that document is to establish laws to bind our federal government to prevent it from becoming a tyranny.
