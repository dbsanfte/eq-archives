As for good and evil... *shrug* Perceive it how you want to perceive it. It all comes down to what the character believes is right and wrong. A dark elf cleric, for example, would probably want to spread the glory of his god. That, to him, is the right thing to do. Trumpeting the praises of Tunare is the wrong thing to do. If you go into inherently good and evil, you get the whole nurture vs. nature argument going, and that's a whole other can o' worms.

The best thing to do with people who disrupt RPing events is just to ignore them. There's nothing else you can do, really. Unless the person is harassing someone, the Guides and GMs probably won't do anything.

As for RPing events...

It depends on the server, it really does. I see a lot of events advertised for some servers, none for others. I'm speaking, of course, of player-run events here. If you can gear up a group of folks to regularly RP with you, great!

Now, Verant-run events are a different story. All servers run events; the problem is that they're rarely reported. Few people take the time to write up a quick blurb. As such, you never hear about them. They're run by Guides, and the Guides try their best, but there's only so much a group of volunteers can do.

As for an RP server...

The problem with a role playing flagged server is that not only RPers go there, but also the annoying grief-spreading players who believe RPers are pushovers. Because role players have a reputation of levelling slowly and taking their time, powergamers would go there just to be first and best with little competition. See the problem? There's no way one can enforce role playing, either, because there's no one set definition (except for the most broad, "playing a role") and setting up rules on that basis is impossible.

What else would you want on a RP server? More Verant-run events? Better be willing to pay more cash for that server so that Verant can staff it accordingly. Also, with such a good vs. evil-centric type story as Norrath has, a RP server would simply have to have PvP of some sort... but I doubt they'd institute PvP without it being balanced (and evil's a little weaker on the racial side). Plus, you have neutrals to think about, and this, and that..

EQ, although having a good storyline, discourages RP by its design. An RP flagged server might be nice, but would do more harm than good.
