"Also, I need dummy instructions to The Tower of Frozen Shadow if possible, thanks! "

On each floor there's a mob that drops a key that will allow you to get to the next level.

This zone is not for soloing. It's a great PL zone, but you need to be on your toes.

Level 6 is the safest level, IMO. CC isn't really an issue. Level 5 is also really good.

Level 3 is pretty easy.

Level 4 however, is a bear. But really good coin drops. You can make a lot of pp on this level. But beware, lots and lots of Wizard mobs. They will chain nuke you down fast. But it's a great xp level.

Level 2 also has a lot of necro mobs and I believe some chanter mobs mixed in.

Now, there is also a Level 6b. Another side to level 6. I remember a very arrogant level 65 ranger from Blades of Law on Povar that was being a real tard in the zone one day.

He was killing spawns that we were working our way to get and giving us attitude about how uber he was. Anyways, he thought he'd show off and port to 6b. About 2 min later he came running back with his tail between his legs with a huge train behind him.

Watch out for the bats. You're like, Oh yea these aren't to hard to kill... until you kill one. Then where the bat dies a vampire spawns. These have like 10k hp or better and hit fairly hard.

You're saying, bah a 65 could take those...yea until 3 or 4 more bats aggro on you. And they hit pretty hard, and you can't just kill it because then you got another vampire....

Anyways, unless you have a group that can handle these double spawns, don't port to 6b.
