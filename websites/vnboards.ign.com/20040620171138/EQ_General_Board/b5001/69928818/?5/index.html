Spec Alter and Evoc are your only real choices.. you could go conjure..but..no =-p

I was spec evoc until like.. low 50s, and then I went spec alter. Healing spells are much more draining on your mana then nuke spells, and you can't ever get any mana preservation besides Spell Casting Mastery AA and spec alter on the % heals. Most druids now-a-days are spec alter, though there are quite a few evoc specialists too. It depends on more what you want to do with your druid. I like to nuke a fair bit, so you don't have to limit yourself to be strictly healing or nuking despite your spec (really crappy druid if you do =-p). If you find yourself nuking more then healing, go spec evoc. If you find yourself healing more then nuking, go spec alter. Or if you find yourself being drained of mana too fast because of one of those..etc

And put one point into all specs, and then just chain cast the type of spec you want to hit 51, until it does. Then you can let the rest rise naturally to 50, and your main one to 200.
