It's really simple. Think like a warrior with higher energy.

Perfect griffon/minotaur/wurm farming build:

sword 8 (or whatever your req is)
tactics 12 (yes,12)
Curses 10
Rest in SR

A Furious sword helps but isn't necessary -- I use a furious defense sword with +15% vs hexed foes.
Shield: Crimson Carapace Shield, 45 health / -2 damage when in a stance.

Skills:
Deadly Riposte
Gladiator's Defense (E)
Bonetti's Defense
Shield Stance
Desecrate Enchantments
Price of Failure
Parasitic Bond
Healing Signet

The spiteful spirit's a good idea but Gladiator's defense is kind of the same in context as well as that ever-crucial blocking. With this style and a little practice on the timing, you can take anything in the desert alone. I've managed a wurm, griffons, a hydra, sand drakes, minotaurs, even the non-bleeding armors. This build suffers against Wild Blow, obviously, so don't try it!

Screamingclaw (the monk boss) will take you a while -- the key is don't degen either him or his two underlings. That'll kick in his awful Healing breeze.

Spam your parasitic bonds, whack a price of failure on if you have time (combined with Glad's defense, it's a cool 50+ damage return to that creature per attempted swing), desecrate for that little AoE nudge. Shield Stance is your 'emergency' skill -- if you get adds or devourers, it'll save your life, because you can Healing Signet during it with relative impunity. If you don't use a sword, switch Deadly Riposte for Shield Bash -- same result. The next hit is a guaranteed block. Enter a blocking stance, hit DR or SB, hit signet. Doesn't always work, but it's just another weapon in your arsenal.

That's how to 'farm' as a Necro/Warrior.
