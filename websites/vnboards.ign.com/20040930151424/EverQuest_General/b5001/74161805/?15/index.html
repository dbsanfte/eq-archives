I could be wrong, but I expect the tutorial will answer most of your questions.

As far as communication goes, the most basic things to know are:

/ooc <text> to say something to the entire zone, out of character
/tell <name> to say something privately to a single person
/say <text> will say something to everyone nearby

There is also /shout and /auction, but if you are just starting out, you may as well ignore them.

Of course, I have not answered your initial question, if the tutorial does not in fact have what you are looking for. I know of no guide, however, that goes into communications. Just the basics on how to play your class, etc., such as Caster's Realm.
