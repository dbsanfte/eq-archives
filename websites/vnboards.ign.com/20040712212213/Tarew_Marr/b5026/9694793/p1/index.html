I believe we've had this discussion many a time with you guys over the last few months so again I'll try to be brief as I can and will only recap what's needed.

Point #1: For years on end we weren't allowed to post a ToS or Board Rules. Because of this, and the insane number of boards we have, people came to expect to be able to post certain things freely.

Point #2: Because from the webmasters standpoint and IGN's legal standpoint, it is necessary for us to restrict certain content from the boards to create a place where all people can feel free to visit and post. After months of badgering the powers that be the ToS finally became live. This document is absolutely critical to the operation of a boad system as large as this.

Point #3: Several issues came up surrounding the ToS, specifically the incident where mpegs of real-life situations where real people's heads were decapitated. We discussed the issues of over moderating, censorship and unacceptable content.

Because of the suddenness of the ToS's implementation I wanted to give people time to adjust and get used to the new rules. I felt that discussions held here went well and I didn't install any more moderators on Tarew Marr at the time on good faith.

I believe that catches brings us up to speed.

Once again I'm going to be as diplomatic as possible, but we have to make a few points clear, namely:

The Terms of Service (ToS) are the rules.

Everyone is expected to observe the ToS. Each time you post you agree to it. I gave people time to adjust and mentioned that we would let a few things slip on occasion, however we are at the breaking point. To make things even simpler the ToS can be broken down to three basic rules to remember:

#1) No adult content rated R or higher. This includes sexual content, cursing and other vulgar phrases, slurrs, etc.
#2) *Excessively* harassing and taunting users (We’ve already had an FBI investigation started. We’re not saying you have to be kind to each other but we can’t allow “Board Stalking” and other excessive kinds of threats.)
#3) No excessive spamming of your EQ items up for trade or sale.

Shey and HallMonitor roared in here yesterday and started cleaning things up. To some of you it may have been harsh but the bottom line is there has been a lot of content that once again crossed the line.

I must also say from my personal POV how dismayed I am when I saw the things you posted about the two moderators. You people are better than this. You can clearly state your objections to people and actions without degrading into something subhuman. Shey and HallMonitor aren't power-crazed Big Brother-wannabes. Their goal as moderators is to make the boards a good place for everyone. Making them scapegoats is pointless and unfair. They don't make the rules.

We've tried to make the transition the best as possible for everyone but as stated some things have to change. The rules don't mean that the community is instantly blown to heck. The board can go on with the same people, discussing most, if not all the same subjects. I cannot stress that enough. Unless everyone decides having a few basic manners is too much to handle this board will continue to thrive.

I hope we can work together and continue our cooperation. I also want to point out again that HallMonitor announced we are willing to unban anyone who has been banned over the last two days and give him or her the a second chance. Just PM one of them and they’ll take it from there.

Have a good day!
