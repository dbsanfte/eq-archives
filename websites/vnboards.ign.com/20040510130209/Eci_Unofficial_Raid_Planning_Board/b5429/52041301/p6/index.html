Getting your epic 101... Or “how to avoid paying for your epic”


Rule 1) Level your character by killing spiders, snakes and other nasty creatures to group levels.

Rule 2) Level your character through interaction with others. I/E looking for a group, finding a group and helping the group kill things.

Rule 3) Be as courteous as you can. Make friends, find regular people you enjoy playing with. Bond with them, make "eq friends for life"

Rule 4) Join a guild, or gather said friends when the time is right to mutually kill mobs to start your epics. Most mobs are easily done with a minimal force. Once you have put your time in with said guild, or have made enough friends (which most should have at this point) you can attempt harder mobs.

Rule 5) If you guild is not big enough to do some of the needed mobs for you and your friends, you all call upon "other friends" you have made. this generally can gather up quite a bit of "kill thirsty" mob hunters willing to help a friend help a friend.

Rule 6) Once you have completed your epic, be willing to help your friends, and those that came to your kill get the piece they need. This keeps the "epic ball" bouncing and makes you even more friends.

Rule 7) Enjoy your epic and be happy for those you've helped. Congrats, you have now mastered what EQ IS REALLY ABOUT.
