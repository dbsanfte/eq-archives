Hear Ye, Hear Ye, the Freeport Tavern (Drunks) guild on the Morell-Thule server will be hosting an event this Saturday June 10 at 9 PM EST. It will be held in West Freeport. The events will happen as described below.

Our first event for the evening will be a newbies vs. Garthe match. Any people level 5 and under will register prior to the event with a Freeport Tavern member near the Arena in West Freeport and must not be /anon. Then all the participants will enter the arena, and when told GO will attempt to kill Garthe, he will be fighting back, so prepare for a death. However, the person who strikes the killing blow will win a Greater Lightstone! We will have 2 rounds of this event. There is NO entrance fee for this event, however Harm Touch users will be disqualified if it is used, and there will be a cleric on hand to reverse HT effects.

The Second event will consist of Last Man Standing in the Arena. We will break people into level ranges and users must register with an appointed guild member before participating. All participants will enter the arena and when told GO will try to kill everybody else in the arena. The LAST person standing will win a Greater Lightstone. Second place winners will win a Regular Lightstone. This event will continue as long as we have enough people for each level range. This is also a free entrance. Level ranges are as follows:

1 to 4
5 to 7
8 to 11
12 to 15
16 to 19
20 to 23
24 to 27
Etc…


The Third event for the evening will be a Drunk Chase, also in West Freeport and will start at the Gate. Upon registration into this event, alcohol will be given to the participant. Just before starting each participant will drink his or her alcohol when told. We will already have a runner somewhere in West FP who will ALSO be drunk, and when told GO all people will run all around West FP trying to find our guild runner, the first person to INSPECT successfully will win a Greater Lightstone and the pot money. There will be a small entrance fee for this event that will be paid to the guild member you register with. The fees will follow this formula and FTD will not be keeping any cuts:

Levels 1 to 5, 5gp
For each range after add an additional 5gold. (6-10 1pp, 11-15 1.5pp etc)

Registration for each of these events will be handled by a single person for an event, a line will form in front of this person to easily register names.

Alcohol will be given out to people as long as our supply lasts, but please don’t get greedy, we will have 1 or 2 people giving it out so it won’t be hard to remember names of people who ask for alcohol repeatedly.

This is the Freeport Tavern Drunks first event and we’re hoping to have a lot of fun! Come out and enjoy yourself. You can check out our website at http://ftdrunks.freeservers.com for member info etc or for updated info on this event. Thanks!
----snip-----
