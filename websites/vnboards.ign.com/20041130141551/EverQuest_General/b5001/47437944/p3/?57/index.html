I played Everqyest at the start, and I quit after a month because it was such a grind. It wasn't fun, it was WORK, and I don't play games to work...I have a job for that.

I came back after a year but didn't stay long because 'the vision' was still intact and the game was more work than fun.

After having been gone well over a year, I came back again after hearing about some of the new features. And I think this time I will stay. The game is now playable in the hour or two I can put in at a time. I can make XP that I can see and get some positive reinforcement. I can take a low level char and explore areas I could never see before without penalty if I happen to open the wrong door.

Everquest is still not what I'd call an 'easy' game. But the old game was nothing more than a challenge to my tolerance for tedium. Now it's a challenge to my mind.
