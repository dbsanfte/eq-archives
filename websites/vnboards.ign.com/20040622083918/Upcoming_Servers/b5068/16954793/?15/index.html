No way are mages best PLers in the game. PLing means to advance someone other than yourself in levels very quickly with your help. Lots of ppl will argue this one, but druids hands down are the best at PLing. They can sow, port, heal, buff(dmg shield, chloro, skin line, str). And any melee class is gonna need offensive and defensive skills so grouping with a mage and letting him do 51% of the dmg ain't gonna work.
But back to your point of an all mage guild. It could be a fairly good idea, but will miss out on a lot of aspects of the game. Mages are uber given the right situation - ie: Killing Lord Rek'Ahiir(sp) and Naggy butbeyond things like that will be a lot of challenges. Post 50 game is non existant without a cleric and chanter. These are arguably the most important classes in the high end game. Without which you'll be stuck soloing DE's in Neriak 3rd quarter until green at 55 and then no where to go from there.
But hey its a good idea for some intents and purposes, however EQ wasn't designed for true roleplaying and if it was they did a piss poor job at it.
