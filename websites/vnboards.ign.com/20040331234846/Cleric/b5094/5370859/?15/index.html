Ears you really can't do too much for unless you have thousands of plat to try for Ivandyr's Hoop (Lynuga in Innothule).

Neck piece you need an upgrade, my favorite piece for that is the cape of midnight mist +5 str +7 magic resist (Maid Issis in Mistmoore).

Mask you have to get rid of, even a silver ruby veil will do, but plat is better if you can afford it. (Jeweler-made items)

Nightshade Wreath - A-ok. It having no graphic is good too.

Those bracelets are in serious need of replacement and should probably be #1 priority. You have plenty of choices, the best two being runed mithril (+3 str +5 wis if I recall correctly) and the cleric quest bracers ac7 +5 wis. RMBs are gotten off the kobold king in Sol B (or maybe the priest? dont remember for sure).

Arms, best choice are the quest arms... if you're really a wis hog go for the plat armband, but in my experience it's too much AC to lose for the +3 wis. Plat armband is off the magi in lower guk.

Gloves, pretty much the same. Keep the paw gloves around for your wis suit but make the quest gloves your primary armor.

Legs, two good choices: either the quest legs or the gatorscale legs - gators give +4 wis, only pants available to do that, but is weak on the AC and leather looks terrible on most cleric races. Gators are a damn boring camp in upper guk near the live side lower zone.

Adamantite epolets - A-ok, best you'll get maybe for the rest of your cleric life. One of the few items that are actually made for clerics, and are effective too.

Tunic! Ack you need an upgrade! Bloodstained is ok, but for the long term the quest BP is the best, even better in my opinion than the other high level option, which is the runed mithril tunic. Plus the price of the mithril is bound to be prohibitive, as melee classes love it too. Doesn't really do anything special for a cleric. If you are super lucky you may find an enameled black BP off the savant in lower guk, AC25 is hard to beat. Mithril tunic is a rare drop off the king in Sol B. Bloodstained is much easier to acquire, off the undead knight on the top floor of Unrest.

Lizzie cloak: that's one good option, probably your second best. Best would be the cloak of the icebear, as +20 save cold is more valuable to a cleric than +6 dex, and the AC is the same - comes off the Icebear brothers in Everfrost. Sure go for molten or mammoth if you can find one, but they are super rare and rarely available.

Belt: If you can snag a girdle of the diligent go for it; otherwise some options include the thick leather apron ac8 (Unrest) and blackened iron girdle ac6 +5 sta (Runnyeye). Reed belt is a rare item that gives +5 wis as well, but you'll never get one unless you know a lot of high level and generous druids - it comes off the rarest NPC in the game, the notorious Bilge Farfathom in Dagnor's Cauldron.

Boots: Two upgrades you can go for; quest boots are easy to get, and dwarven work boots are a good deal harder to get. DWBs give str but weigh a lot too. Quest boots are slightly better than bronze, and come with some near-useless disease resist. DWB is found off the ghost in Unrest, as a rare drop.

Keep in mind that wis is not the be-all end-all of stats for a cleric. Sure it gives you more max mana, but in any decent camp you will never be at max mana anyway. At one point I had 186 wis in my max-wis suit, which is perfect because the enchanter wis buff takes you to exactly 200 wis then (if you are fighting in a tough spot at high levels without an enchanter around you are just plain dumb, no offense). At that point you can start swapping in mana items for the surplus wis items, moonstone rings are one common and useful place to start for that.
