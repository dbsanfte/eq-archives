Int/wis over 200 do help. If I remember correctly, the cap is 280 up to level 60. Then you gain 5 to the cap per level so at 65 you will have a cap of 305. In addition, if you trained in AAs you can raise that cap up to 355. (This AA training only works on int/wis, not on any other stats caps.) Every time I raise my cap I notice the difference in my efficiency in hunts. It is worth raising.

And yes, items work the same as your base attributes. They add up up to your cap. The cap applies to their sum.

Cap is 255 until 61
Then 280 at 65
innate enlightenment gives +10 to int/wis cap per level (5 levels)
planar power gives +5 to all stat caps per level (5 level)

Only reason to get Innate Enlightment/Planar Power is for tradeskills. Going from 280 -> 330 wis was only a couple hundred mana for me, worthless.

To be exact...
Mana without innate enlightenment 5 = 5095
Mana with innate enlightenment 5 = 5347
Total mana gain = 312
3 AA per level x 5 levels = 15 AA

Not worth it unless you want faster tradeskill gains.
