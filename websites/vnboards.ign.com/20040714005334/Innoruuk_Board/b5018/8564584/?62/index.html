You've done me a service here Dabigbadman or Cataclysm or Catastrophe or whoever the hell you are...you've forced me to reevaluate my gaming philosophy. I guess I can only offer my thanks for that. From levels 1-25 or so as an enchanter, I viewed EQ as a soloing game...a game where the individual could strive toward that very Ralph-Waldo-Emerson idea you have of "self-reliance." I thought I could accomplish everything on my own. At about level 25 or so I hit a wall..(And no, fuck you..I did NOT buy my account from some other dumbass like myself. I realized I had to group up with 5 or more other people in order to really prevail in the game. I realized that Verant put the screws to me and made me become a social being.

But I liked the game and I liked my character...so I bit the bullet and went out and mingled with the masses. Overall it was a positive experience. I learned how the classes are interrelated and how we all must sort of depend on each other in some way. Unlike Diablo 2 or Quake or god-forbid Civilization or any other game I've played...you've gotta be all nice and touchy-feely social to play the EQ. It came as a shock to me at first, but I learned to deal with it.

Then something went awry in my head. I formed an opinion that 90% of the other players in the game don't abide by the same social ideals as I do. Its not a fun, well-balanced, groovy little Kindergarten world out there where all are shiny happy people singing, chanting, killing and looting until the sun goes down. No sir. Actually...90% of the people in the game are out to buttram you with a hot flaming cattle prod. Don't you dare /consent someone to loot your corpse. You'll never see it again. That little gnome necromancer will loot it for you...and he's only been here 5 minutes.

These ideas festered in my head for another 20 levels or so. Why is everyone an ass? Maybe its me thats the ass? Why do I have to constantly group with asses in order to "have fun" in my spare time? Is this really what this game is all about? These were the questions I plagued myself with. Then at about level 45 or so I threw those notions out again when I found 5 or 6 others that didn't fit the mold. Those people I would call friends now. With them...the game is pure heaven. The mobs die with wondrous rapidity and efficiency. The loot is divvied up in a sane manner. They actively seek out the moment when you log on so they can invite you to group for the Efreeti room or something. You actively seek them out for the same reason. The game became great again. There was a certain majesty in the idea that you could rely not only on yourself...but also on other people again. The gaming intensity became an orgiastic experience. Hell I had so damn much fun in the frenzied room at 5 in lower guk at 5 AM that I don't think I'll ever enjoy any other dungeon again.

Then a few more levels went by and I hit 51. Kunark and Velious came out. Then another horrid reality set in: I can't be "self-reliant" anymore...and I can't be group-reliant either. The only way to further advance myself now requires not 6 people...but 18-24. As a matter of fact...now that I'm level 54...I realize that you can't rely even on 18-24 people.
