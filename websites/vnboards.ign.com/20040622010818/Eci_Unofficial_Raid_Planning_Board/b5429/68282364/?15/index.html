Here is what I currently know:

* MB died at about 1:44am EDT, so really on 5/2/04.
* Tin soldiers were trained onto the raid by about 1:45am.
* Nitasha hailed Giwin Mirakon at 1:48am.
* Everyone was allowed to hail Giwin Mirakon 20 seconds later.
* Measured by unique hails to Giwin Mirakon, 50 people got flagged.
* An unknown person ninjalooted MB, leaving 3 ethereal parchments.
* I have /petitioned the ninjaloot, but have so far heard nothing.
* The ninjalooter was not in the main raid, for no loot message is recorded in my log.
* If I hear nothing from a GM today, I will email Sony concerning the ninjalooter.

Ninjalooters never get away with it anymore. It may take some time, but they WILL lose the stuff they got. When I find out who it was I will post it here.

MB fell with good timing, thanks being to Happyone for tanking, healers for keeping the raiders alive, enchanters for letting no spiders through (YOU SHALL NOT PASS!) and of course the raiders for putting out the damage to the thing. I am sorry for those who did not get the flag, but it could not be helped under the circumstances.

I know of at least 2 people who did not get the flag who should have, and there are probably at least a half dozen or so others who I do not know of. All of these people would have been flagged if everyone had maintained raid discipline and followed instructions. It is a fact that one person can wipe a raid, and training the Factory with monsters from the entrance is one way to do it.

I estimate about 20-30 people, myself included, who did NOT need the flag who attended the raid. Many of those who needed the flag have attended MB raids in the past, failed either through MB not dying or from not getting flagged after the fact. I conclude we had a sizeable number of people who had never before been on a MB raid, so I am quite pleased we were able to kill him.

I know many people were there just for the flag and did not care about the loot. Kudos to them!

Those who got flagged, according to my log, are the following:

Aendienye, Belldandyx, Beula, Boxley, Braneless, Ciielle, Crystyl, Cybereus, Dalelron, Derni, Doronpa, Drachealer, Gamador, Gardadar, Gibshon, Haneldar, Huris, Ikee, Incar, Kinth, Koichi, Kouta, Kozo, Kribaebie, Lubar, Lungbusta, Maeloch, Merlinia, Mobinc, Moctus, Nitasha, Nura, Oban, Onemore, Pbaram, Pleinjour, Raygefist, Rolaid, Ryzaal, Scooba, Secen, Sisee, Sleples, Tankheal, Tanks, Terbulance, Ulma, Valeris, Wortz, Yannyen

EDIT: In reviewing my logs and the points, I see Terbulance not listed as on the raid. It is entirely possible he was in an outside group and did not note this. It is equally possible he was not related to the raid at all and thought he could get flagged by hailing outside the raid. Because I cannot verify his status, he gets no points for the raid, but he might have gotten flagged so that would be irrelevant to him.
