T’Vyl Alliance of Guilds

This alliance of guilds should be a committee made up of one member from each guild serving as an ambassador of that guild. This representative should speak for their respective guild in all actions taken in the meeting of the guild council.
The council itself should be less of a governing body, but more of an overseer and information group.
Individual guilds will still have their own rules with which they use to govern themselves, and the council should serve to promote the guilds themselves.
The council should serve to organize such mass events as raids on Naggies lair, or set up mass first aid areas in low-level zones. The council may also set up such server wide events as Olympic games, scavenger hunts, level specific quests, etc.
There should be meetings held to discuss these events. The elected representative from each guild will be present to be the voice for the guild and take the information learned back to the guild.
Each guild participating in the council should elect a player to be that said representative, and should have a backup rep just in case that player is not available.
The council may be held to a set of guidelines, not strict rules, when promoting the guilds and should make it his/her duty to learn more about each guild in the council.
The council may also provide arbitration for problems that require an outside mediator.
It should be their responsibility to aid in the development of guilds, and possibly aid in the alignment of some of the problem guilds that are currently on Lanys.

Note: the council should not involve itself on a person to person bases, but on problematic guilds themselves. A generally decent guild with a few bad apples should still be dealt with within the guild itself, but attention may be drawn to the guild by the council’s interaction with that/those person(s).
Many other servers use guild councils to bring together the guilds and expose them to each others weaknesses and strengths and general attitude, to better promote server unity.

Since EQ was written on the foundation that groups and guilds would benefit better than the lone wolf tactics of many solo artists, it is reasonable to see why such a mass alliance would benefit the entire server. The EQ concept is unity, so why not spread that unity throughout.


Disclaimer:
This is a draft style template designed to give a rough sketch of the possibilities of such a formation. By no means is this any sort of hard code to follow, merely hints and suggestions to aid in the development of the council, or at the very least spark an interest in such a formation.
