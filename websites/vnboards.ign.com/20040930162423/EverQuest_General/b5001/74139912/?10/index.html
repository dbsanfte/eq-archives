I'd like to see the bazaar system changed to where you didn't have to leave a character physically logged into the world to make sales. I don't know about the rest of you, but I have mules all full on bank space just because I never want to go through the hassle of leaving a character up and my computer on all night long.

Just add code to allow people looking over the various bazaar zone search menus to directly buy just by double clicking on the item with the price they like and have the money be deducted from them with the item immediately popping up on their mouse pointer and their payment either directly showing up in either your "shared account" cash slot or that particular character's bank that put the item up for sale originally. Or alternatively you select the item and can see the prices of those people logged into the game in vender mode's prices and have the option to bid any value you insert to see if the un-listed offline sellers have it up for your price.

So basically now you could set up bazaar mode the same way you do now, but then log out and have your gear still be listed in search feature. To have there still be some advantage to physically having a character up in "vender" mode in bazaar, perhaps there should be a minor fee associated with listing an item while offline, like 2% of the price you had it listed for or some variable amount based on the rarity of the items I dunno. Or like I said above, offline items will not have their prices and seller's name listed, only those online venders will, but you can "bid" a price and if someone offline is selling for that amount or lower they will get the sale.

If you could actually use the character for something outside of bazaar zone and still have their items listed for sale with this method I would gladly welcome the fee deduction method.

Basically it's FFXI online's auction house system I am thinking of here. The price histories of that system aren't needed but would be a nice bonus as well. The anonymous "bidding" that system forces for these purchases would also be fine as it really effects prices rises and falls according to supply and demand.
