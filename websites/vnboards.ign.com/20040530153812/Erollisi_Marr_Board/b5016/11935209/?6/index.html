From my experience with both of the monks, which isn't all that extensive btw, I noticed a couple different things.

For one, the Human is the "baseline" for a lot of factions and such, and you will have an easier time getting around. The Iksar is an "evil" class and as such you are limited to where you can go which can be a big pain in the ass at times.

However, the Iksar has awesome regen I beleive he also has some sort of infravision and has a higher dex that attributes to better dodge, etc. I beleive the human has a higher base str? I don't remember offhand.

If you are experienced with the game already and think you can deal with faction battles wherever you go, then I think that the Iksar is a better monk, especially with the "new" hand to hand weapons.
