#1. Knights of the Old Republic

Even if you hate star wars, you will love this game (I F&*king hate star wars, personally). its great, and is meant to be replayed atleast once (light side/darkside) first time through will take you 30-40 hours without spoiler sites if you just play the game and do the subquests. second time through closer to 20 hours. The game is so fun that you actually want to replay it, almost immediately even. 60 hours entertainment for 50 bucks, not bad.

#2 Morrowind

great great story line, if not original. if you've read the Wheel of Time series the story is very similiar, especially the earlier parts of the series. However, the world, schenery, mood, etc are truly unique and fascinating. Bewarned, the game is huge (think EQ like amount of content for a single player) easily 100 hours to "beat" the game. game is completely open ended tho so you can do anything really. You can even continue to play after you've "Beat" it. Graphics, music etc are Fantastic. Game is almost too huge to replay after you've gone through it, atleast for a few months after you've finished this monster RPG. Truly truly epic, 100 hours of entertainment for like 19.95.

#2.5 Morrowind Expansions
There have been 2, i haven't picked up the second one tho. The first expansion "tribunal" wasn't as open ended as I would have liked, seemed pretty linear. still, decent enough game so it gets a mention. It's great being able to continue to improve your character once you've beaten Morrowind. I wouldn't suggest installing this or the other expansion until you've beaten the original.

#3 Planescape: Torment
gameplay and graphics are pretty dated, however this game has probably the best story/plot twists of any game i've ever played. highly recommend just for the unique story that is told. If you liked the movie Momentos then you'd love this game.

#4 Sacrifice
I know you said adventure/RPG but this RTS is extremely fun and the single player version plays like an adventure game. extremely innovative, probably the most innovative RTS ever made, and its pretty old (1999 game) Graphics are unique if sorta boxy on the units, and the "spell Effects" are still beautiful. pick this up for 5 bucks in the discount bin.
