CoH ushered in the new era -- which was a good game before the nerfs started, but all instancing all the time is a bad idea -- an idea that EQ2 ran with... I am sorry but EQ2 DOESNT feel like a WORLD...

Wow DOES feel like a world but the critical mistake that blizzard made and is just starting to show is that it is a casual game for 59 levels followed by a hardcore game with an even lower reward per investment than EQ had at its worst... I feel they will lose a ton of people when they hit 60 for a while... Really what WoW should do is add another continent every 3 months and raise the cap by 30 each time.

Vanguard is a bigger threat to EQ1 than WoW or EQ2 were (EQ2 hardly deserves the name everquest and I predict that in 6 months EQ1 will have more people playing it than EQ2 does. -- which is kind of cute because I remember a similar scenario for asherons call -- release a sequal that fails and takes both games down)..

I still think EQ2 was a big mistake for sony... They should have done a massive rework on EQ1 with a new world and graphics and then let people move their characters into it. They could have made a more casual game as well to compete with WoW...
