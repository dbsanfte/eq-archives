I played EQ from when I was about 13 til I was about 17(currently am 18), and am considering coming back. In between my playing and potential return, I played WoW to 60, and it was insanely easy, guild wars was too short and limited, SWG....well, was SWG...CoH was too monotonous, just beat up the same badguys over and over with no tradeskills or exploring. Horizons was alright, EQII didn't do a whole lot for me, EQ was by far my favorite MMORPG that I've played. I've got a few friends at school who play FFXI, and they told me about it, and I got very interested, and am considering picking up a copy this weekend. But that got me thinking....Maybe I should go back to Everquest, I loved it so much, and maybe it still has its touch. In your guys' opinions, which one would be better? I'm looking for a game that I can get things accomplished in by myself, but also would really like to make a solid group of friends to group with often. A game with a good community, and most importantly, a game that's fun. I understand nobody will be able to judge what is fun for me as opposed to what is fun for them, but does EQ still have its old touch? Is low-game content still as enjoyable as it was? Is it still easy enough to find a group to level a character with? What are the pros and cons of both games if you don't mind me asking. All help is greatly appreciated!
