Date Posted: 5/9 6:51am Subject: Legends of Norrath Championship Qualifiers
We're getting ready to host the biggest Legends of Norrath event ever, to crown the 2008 Champion, starting Saturday, August 16, 2008 at 10 a.m. EDT! You must have qualified in either the Monthly Championship Qualifiers or the Gen Con Qualification Heats to be eligible to participate in this thirty-two player, single elimination event. See the Events Calendar and Official Rules pages for details on these events.

There are no fees required for these qualifier rounds.

Eligibility: Challenge is open only to natural persons who are legal residents of the fifty (50) United States (including the District of Columbia) for the duration of the Challenge. Void in Puerto Rico, Guam, all other U.S. and all foreign territories and possessions and wherever prohibited or restricted by law. Challenge may only be entered from and in eligible jurisdictions. Entrants must be eighteen (18) years of age or older at time of entry in order to participate.

You must have qualified in either the Monthly Championship Qualifiers or the Gen Con Qualification Heats to be eligible to participate in this thirty-two player, single elimination event. See the Events Calendar and Official Rules pages for details on these events.


Prize Schedule:

1st Place: $25,000
2nd Place: $15,000
3rd & 4th Place: $6,000
5th through 8th Place: $4,000
9th through 16th Place: $2,000
17th through 32nd Place: $1,000

This event is open to pre-qualified Gen Con Indy attendees only.

What Else is Happening at Gen Con?

New Legends of Norrath Raid
Be the first to play the new Legends of Norrath Ralkor Bloodmoon Raid.
You and three other players can challenge the mighty Ralkor Bloodmoon and his feral tribe in this never-before-seen raid! Legends of Norrath raids offer unique multiplayer gameplay that plays like MMO boss events. Get a complimentary t-shirt for sitting down and playing a full Ralkor Bloodmoon raid (limit one per participant), and if you defeat Ralkor, all four (4) players will receive some phat lewt (limit one per participant while supplies last). Raid events are scheduled to be held as four-player events during convention hours on Saturday and Sunday. T-shirts are available while supplies last.

Demo Legends of Norrath
Come to the SOE booth at Gen Con (booth #929) and demo the game on our systems. Legends of Norrath demo experts will be on hand to show you the ropes and get you started! (New players only, please!)

Meet the Developers of Legends of Norrath
Speaking of the demo experts, would you like to meet some of the folks from the SOE team that built this amazing game? Members of the Legends of Norrath Development Team will be at the booth all Gen Con long.

Nightly Gaming Events
After the major events have run, we'll be running special Legends of Norrath events in the Main TCG Tournament Hall at 6 p.m. EDT. Special formats, phat lewt and lots of fun await you.

The Oathbound Is Here!
Get your picture taken with one of the Oathbound heroes from Legends of Norrath. You'll have to come to Gen Con to find out whom!

Exciting Announcements
Want to know what's next for Legends of Norrath? You'll hear and see it here first!

About Gen Con Indy
Gen Con Indy is the largest hobby games convention in North America. Held every August in Indianapolis, Ind., USA, tens of thousands of gaming enthusiasts spend four days immersed in all manner of gaming. For more information on Gen Con, visit their website here.
