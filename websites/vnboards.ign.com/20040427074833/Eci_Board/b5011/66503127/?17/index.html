Don't worry too much about it. The devs are hard at work on making the engine work better - and they are strongly aware of the issues at hand. The problem is that the bigwigs (aka the corporate officials) decided they wanted the DX9 engine by a certain date, regardless of whether ALL of the bugs have been ironed out yet or not. Hell, you should have seen the DX9 engine a day before it went live.. let alone what it was like when it was first implemented on Test.

The under the world problems are due to an issue with the automatic rewind that has been implemented. Up until the move to live, there was a command on test called '/rewind' which would simply move you back to your last known position. However, it was heavily exploited on test (Re: Trakanon Banish, Dain Banish, Rootable Summoning Mobs) and so they removed it and put in an automatic version to try and assist with people getting stuck in the geometry en masse. However, there are some problems with it. If, for example, you zone in on top of someone the game senses you're stuck on something, and automatically rewinds you. However, you've just ported into the zone, so your last 'known' loc is 0,0,0 - which is under the world for the practical majority of zones.

But we've been getting 3-5 patches PER weekday to try and get these issues resolved, so don't worry too much about it... I have confirmation from a dev personally that there will be a patch sooner than later to get all the issues fixed.

Just letting everyone know my wife and I developed a solid plan on how to get around all the patch bugs. If they are still around after this patch, (ie. falling through ground and not able to get out), this will be especially helpful to those that can't gate out. Ask a druid friend or wizard friend or even guild mate to come to the zone you're in. Have them run up against the edge of a wall in a tunnel or something (I know DL specifically has this problem). Duck and unduck using the "D" key while running against the wall. Won't work if you bounce off (another bug) so pick a different spot. As soon as you can see through the wall "Ctrl - S" to sit and you'll fall through the ground. Best to have a levitate or a SoE on so you don't take a lot of damage. We've done it this way about a dozen times and it's fail proof. then run your porter/TLer to the person stuck and group with them and port out. *nods* Hope this helps you out. Will take a minute or two to get through the wall but when you do you'll be like "Well I'll be damned..." Didn't think it'd work til my wife followed me down with those instructions. =) Yay to Caprico for getting out from under DL after 2 days of petitioning and no GMs helping him.
