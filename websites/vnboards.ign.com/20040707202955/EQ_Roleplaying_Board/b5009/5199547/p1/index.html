*strides toward Gale and comes to a halt before him, throwing aside his low-hanging hood to reveal a face of midnight blue and long locks of white hair*

Pardon me for interrupting your nostalgia, but I too have happened upon this filthy hut to recant the days of old. If your memory endures, which I am dubious of because of the wood elves' inferior intelligence, then you should remember me, for it was in this dwelling that we once saw battle. As I recall, the Teir'Dal ruling councilwent to such lengths as putting a bounty on your head and assigning several high priestesses, with a full band of soldiers, to eliminate you. All this was done in the name of Innoruuk, for it was you that flaunted your position as a defender of Tunare, and thus it was you that the council singled out to serve as an example to surface dwellers.
However, you proved to be more resourceful than anticipated and, with the assistance of your pathetically loyal friends, managed to evade the Father's justice.
I was one soldier assigned to the duty of your assassination, and have climbed through the ranks of the Teir'Dal since that day. My record of duty is spotless, from surface raids in which isolated groups of elves were slaughtered, to the assassinations of significant clerics of Tunare. Only one taint exists that prevents me from rising to the coveted position of weapons master, that being the fact that blood still pumps through your herectical vains.
I plan to change that little problem today, Gale of Northwind, and indeed it is a little problem. You see, if I felt that you would pose any threat, I would have simply had the soldiers in my command depose of you, but that is not the case. You ran from our last engagement, ranger, and you had better have an excellent plan of retreat in store, for I believe you will find the Dark Elves I have stationed outside this hut are quite skilled with their crossbows. At long last, I will claim that which is my birthright!

*removes his twin dirks, dripping with poison, from their scabbards*

Rejoice, my blades, for today you bathe in the blood of an elf!

*lunges at Gale's heart with a deadly quick strike of his right blade, while preparing to parry with his left*
