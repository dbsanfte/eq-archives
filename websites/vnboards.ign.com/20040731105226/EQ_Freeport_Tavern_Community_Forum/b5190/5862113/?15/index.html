Ok, in a duel the winner can NOT loot your body. No coin, no loot, nothing.

No drop items can NOT be looted if you give /consent to someone. This will also effect an entire backpack if one item in it is no drop, i.e., they wouldn't be able to loot the backpack.

Answer your questions?

Also, FYI it used to be you could give a no drop item to an NPC (well you still can) like a fire beetle, the other person would kill it and get the item. That has been "corrected" to not allow it cause of people exploiting it. Take care ya'll
