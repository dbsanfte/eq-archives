Just posting here to take a look at those that are interested in going on weekly HoT raids in ToV. Ive been Watching the boards lately and havent seen any action of them being posted and so here goes. Basically everyone that wanted to attend would need to bring any Quested/Unquested armor so i can make note of who has what,Those that bring armor will recieve 1 point per piece of armor, Bracers are seperate and are 2 points for the pair. Those that attend the weekly raid would get 1 point per raid, there is no way to buy your points ( I. E. bringing peridots, porting and so on ) 1 point per raid. These are your points so you may or bid however many or little that you want to at any time on any item as long as it is for your class. the person that win's would be removed from the Armor list for that piece of armor. Now im not sure if this has ever been done with HoT public raids but I wanted to attempt to Do some of the Tier quests for people, Tier quests being
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/qsearch.html?zone=116
Many people havent seen any of these and many have, this was just a thought. The hard part about this is that these mobs arent always up and arent always gonna be up while we are doing a raid, so i cannot promise you that you will complete the quest. How this will work is the points that you gain from attendance will be used in rolling for these, you may bid as many as you have but this takes away from any armor drops. NOW with these there is a catch, you cannot just show up to a raid if you have already gotten one from an outside event and bid for them, hence you have no points.
I was also taking interest in moving the minimum level down to level 52, Resists are fairly easy to get nowadays and I havent seen many Kael raids so not seeing many of the younger raiders being equiped for their efforts, and now that you can get ornate armor at 55 would just seem to make some sense.
