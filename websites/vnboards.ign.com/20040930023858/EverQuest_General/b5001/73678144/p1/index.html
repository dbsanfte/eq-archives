 		Date Posted: 8/11 2:31pm Subject: Topic of the Week 8/11 - What class is the best at soloing?
Recently a friend of mine who used to be a major AC1 player asked me what class he should play when he goes to try out EQ. His only major condition is that he could solo well.

Now, EQ is one of the last games you want to plan on soloing everything, but some people prefer not to deal with the hassles of trying to find groups when they only have a short time to login.

So what class do you believe is the best at soloing in EQ? If you want, feel free to give multiple answers.

As for what I think –

Best overall – Bard. This is not an option for many people as it requires a pretty extreme amount of skill to play well. Not a choice for a newbie player.

Best for newbies – Necro. Pretty easy game-play in general. Like all classes, the better the player you are the easier of a time you’ll have, but even an idiot can pick-up a Necro and have a little success soloing if they take time to learn the spells.

So what do you think? Agree? Disagree?

-Drak
