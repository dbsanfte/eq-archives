Hi, This is Nununu. I'm a 65 druid and friend of Havana Banana and Sunbaab. I'm posting under her name since I haven't really come here much at all.

Tonight I wanted to tell you the results of the vox raid since we killed her. Raiders were gathered from 6:30 est until 10pm est in order to kill her . From a 65 stand point, what a long time to get ready for the raid!

Myself and Arrk were the only out of level range there, save for a 65 bard who was helping to pull the lair before we approached the buff hall.

I spent several bars of mana buffing groups with Bot9 (no mgb yet =\), circle of summer since no druids there were 52, as well as protection of the seasons. And not to mention a few regen buffs here and there. Arrk certainly helped with the various forms of clarity as well as magic resistence.

We engaged, we won. Yay. Sunbaab earned another point towards getting his epic scale.

Vox dropped a white scale, bard horn, eyepatch, white dragonhide and the nice haste item she has.

It came time to roll on loot when the few dead were ressed. Shaydad got her\his scale (Congrats!) another bard got the horn but I can't recall the name. Then dred seeded for the dragonhide.

I asked in raid say if I would be allowed to roll for it since I felt this would be an upgrade to what i had. I was even willing to give up my cloak to the raid if he had spoken to me about it.

Anyway he seeded a 617 and I rolled a 613. For whatever reason, without telling me at any time that my roll wasn't going to count, some person who rolled a 400 something was given the loot. Someone to the effect of Discipl or whatever.

I objected to this and was simply passed off with "Thank you for your time and buffs." The only thing I am happy about is getting Sunbaab another point towards his scale, but at the same time I really don't feel like returning and the 3 of us hunt n many places most always together.

There was one caster item which I rolled on anyway for fun despite the fact that after I had won the previous item they deemed it not mine. I got a 501 out of 533. It would have been an upgrade too.

Just because I am 65 doesn't mean I am uber. I dont have a guild, and like to hang out with my friends, not powergame.

I would encourage you all to read this and consider before you attend any more of Dreddid\Uenea's raids whether or not this might happen to you. This time it was my level, despite the fact I put my almost 4 hours in, helped buff and did everything except heal ( not really useful if you get thrown out of the dungeon) just like all the other raiders. My intention was never originally to get loot. I wasn't even sure if we could kill vox after wednesday's attempt. However, seeing when caster loot dropped that would be an upgrade to mine made me smile and no one told me I wasn't allowed to. Who knows what may happen next time.

There were a few people who agreed with their decision but also many who sent me sympathetic tells telling me that what they did was wrong. I almost have to agree. I feel dicked over. If it were easy and I could be like one of the more 'leet' people out there and duo Gorenaire and collect epic pieces and loot whenever she was up this wouldn't really be an issue.. but I'm just me.

I hope you take this into consideration when attending future raids. And if any of you show up to help, Make sure you get a response from the raid leader first about loot before they go and do this to you.
