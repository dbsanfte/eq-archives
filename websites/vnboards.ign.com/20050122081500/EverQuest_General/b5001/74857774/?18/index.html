The learning curve in Everquest is deep, but not steep. With the new tutorial and the new newbie quests and the newbie yard revamps and the new ruleset regarding characters under level 10 and the travel convenience of the Plane of Knowledge, the learning curve has flattened considerably, but there is still a lot to learn over the course of 65 levels and a few hundred AA's, especially once you hit the higher levels and especially if you quest and raid.
