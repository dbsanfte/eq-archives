Created a Troll Berserker went and fought the same mob. Nobody had been into the dungeon since I was there and I didn't kill it so unless it despawned and then spawned a new one quickly it was the same mob. The Troll died as well but the mob was down to about 10% health.

Created a Ogre Berserker went there and fought the same mob. Again nobody had been there etc... The Ogre successfully killed the mob with about 50% health remaining. I attribute the ogre winning while the others couldn't due to his unstunability.

Actually I think I am begining to warm up to the Berserker, just not all that soloable it appears though. Just requires a good race (Ogre) I guess. Didn't test the Vah or Barb's.

I have a awesome staff that he can use so I think I might switch over to him. Steel Rod of the Knight I think it is called. 2HB weapon.
