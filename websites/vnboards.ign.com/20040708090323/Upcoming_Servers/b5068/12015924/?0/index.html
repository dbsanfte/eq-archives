GUILD CHARTER


I am forming a new guild Named "Diabolic Servitude", people like the name...Our guild is to be based on 3 Main principles:

1) Your point in life is not to simply PK but to actually substantiate a Roleplaying environment. (Thus we do not Kinslay, we duel for honor; not just because you want your name posted for winning in a duel.)

2) Next, we would act as mature responsible players; thus we would not do something like: I am going to kill you because I notice you are a blue con...But would attack someone else if they offended one of our members or ourselves or friends. And Finally: Go to point 3 which lists the guild Hierarchy----

3) We will have an Officer for each level range (e.g.- Officer of the newb levels; 1-5,& an officer of the next set; 6-10, and so on until roughly level 60
More than likely we will also end up having Officers for zones (People that usually stay in a particular zone; I usually stay in Greater Faydark-thus we would have the Following Order of Heirarchy:
---Regular Members---
---Upper Class Members (Those 30+)
---Level Officers (Lvl Rng.)
---Zone Officers (For each zone)
---Continent Officers (We do avidly explore others Continents)
---And Finally the Highest 3 Members (The Heir Presumptive)

This guild is to be Dark Races only, each server has a Seekers type guild, and since I am in one on Druzzil Ro and Rallos Zek, I may name the guild “Seekers of Diabolic Servitude”, or just Diabolic Servitude (which I personally think is better) We are, as previously stated, Dark races only and will as we continue have scheduled raids. If you are interested in joining such a guild please email me at: eqman5186@yahoo.com

If you are planning to make a guild and think that you might like to merge with my guild in the beginning let me know here or through email.
