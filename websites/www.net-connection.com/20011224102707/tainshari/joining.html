Joining

Tain Shari is made up entirely of members from the same geographical area, namely Huntsville, Alabama. We know each other in "real life" and are friends and family. Though we are very happy that you find us worthy enough to wish to join us, we are unable to take members that do not live within driving distance of Huntsville, Alabama. We would hate to take members from farther away who would feel left out of the regular Wednesday guild lunch meetings / planning sessions.

Though we do not take members from farther away we are always happy to group with people from outside the guild so feel free to ask if we have a spot open should you see us online.

Organized Play Nights

Tain Shari plays regularly on Monday and Thursday nights starting at 7:30 pm CST. Monday nights are for our original starting group of characters (the ones in the oval pictures) and Thursday nights are for "Juzzie's War Band" our favorite secondary characters. Where we play differs from week to week so please check the Next Week link on the main page if you are trying to track us down.
