Our History In the beginning ...

Tain Shari was the last Sept to leave the world. We held back the shadow as long as possible, to try and help as many people to escape. Only a handful, of the Tain Shari, escaped, and most were forcibly removed from the world. The honor that Tain Shari held to, would not allow them to run like the rest. The few that were caught up in wizard's spells and those whose corpse might have been summoned and revived, were drawn to each other in Greater Faydark of the server called Test.

We have been spending our time honing our skills fighting the Clan Crushbone. In this effort, we have witnessed a few Tier'dal in the home of these orcs. Has a feel of what happened in our home lands. We dare not fail this time, or we may have no place left to go...

We fought hard to the depths of Befallen, slaying all of the foul minions that lay in our way. Only to be turned away at the last moment by a massive influx of Deathly ghouls, and harbingers of death. Sad to say, but we fled. We will return when we feel we are stronger!

Across the seas we sailed on a ship with mighty sails. At Firiona Vie we met with those making a last stand against the evil forces inhabiting that land. Though they warned us against it, we headed further inland to the Swamp of No Hope. Our skills failed us when we encountered the massed forces of the Froglok city. Many were lost and we fled to return when we have grown and become more skilled.

To the city of Aviaks we traveled, both on the islands of Lake Rathe and within the boundaries of the Karanas. They fell like flies instead of mighty birds to our swords and magic users. Much glory was gathered for the Tain Shari in these battles.

Next to an island in the new ocean said to be populated with a tribe of fearsome ogres. We found them not as fearsome as forecast and slew them with great dispatch, all except their chief who was in fact a mighty warrior and claimed the lives of those who sought to pull his subjects to their doom.

Now we find ourselves again with the lands of the lizards. We hunt the Lake of Ill Omen, ridding the land of Sarnaks and evil Iksars. They have lined our pockets with platinum and helped us grow in skills as well. We have heard rumors of late that there be giants nearby, and sleep with one eye open and a guard always on watch.

Our leads led us to the Giant's own fort, where we found the large ones living in a large community. We tested the waters and found the giants to be unworthy adversaries. Though full of loot, they were weak and fell to our attack like flies. Next week we seek the goblins to see if they are better sport.

Alas the goblins, as a whole were disappointing fodder. They fell quickly and ran easily. We slew them in mass until they sent against us one of their great ones, Grachnist the Destroyer. Though we fought a mighty battle, he was too strong for us. Sulin and Amethysax gave their lives to buy time for the mages to escape. He too shall join the list of those we will visit when we are older and more wise in the ways of battle. Next week we journey across the seas to Mistmore in search of essence of sunlight to aid in Yssarah's armor crafting.

Ahhhh, at last adversaries worthy of the might of Tain Shari. Last eve we entered Mistmore and found it well suited to our abilities. Hordes did we slay and well were we rewarded in both battle experience and loot. Many a gargoyle parted with their eyes under our swords and flying fists. We left in such good spirits that we didn't even take time to make a plan for our next adventure.

Since the evil ones of Mistmore spawn like rabbits, we have stayed an extra week to make sure we clear out the evil lurking here. Unbeknownst to us, the foul beasts brought in an imp to aid them. He was a mighty battle and the clever bugger went after our healer, Grinch, first. Though he slew Grinch the group ended the imps life and took for him a dagger with intelligence enhancing properties for Lassenera. The graveyard proved beyond us and aid was give to our group by Fadi the druid and Inkey the necromancer. Their kindness will be long remembered within the guild.

No one knows how to have a good bar fight like those goblins in Sol A. We battled there and gained much experience and much valuable loot which Yssarah sold for a tidy profit. Once we discovered the safe room we suffered no losses, though Grinch did give his life once to save us all. I would not be opposed to remain here another week and see if we can avenge ourselves in the bar.

With the aid of a new friend, Cale, we strove deeper into Sol A and encountered many worthy and rich foes. Countless goblins and even a Kobold Predator fell to our relentless attack. In the depths of the cave we found a Reckless Efreeti ... though we slew him in the end, his elemental minions destroyed us. Among the many riches we took away with us were: Kobold Hide Boots, an Obsidian Scimitar, and Platinum Armbands.

A fine, fine night for the Tain Shari. Seven of us entered Cazic-Thule and we piled the bodies of the lizards and great apes deep. Much fine experience was gained and good money as well. This was our first try at a split group and it worked out very well. It will be a fun experience when we can raise our newer member to the point where we can field 2 mostly full groups to take on an adventure.

Despite the fact the Sulin pretended to be dead a little too accurately on one occasion, the night was a great success. Tain Shari pressed all the way into the throne room building at one point and spent a lot of the night in the palace courtyard. Many lizards and their ape friends fell to our blows and much platinum was garnered to help Yssarah in her jewelry making.

After a brief one night journey to Paw to confront the evil there, the group once again returned to Cazic Thule. This time we fought our way to the room with the blue pool. There we found and defeated, a steel golem and the many gators that called this place home. Next we travel to The Estate of Unrest, where we have been told much evil awaits us.

For the first time we ventured forth with 2 small groups instead of one large one. Unused to this strategy we were quickly routed in Unrest. Still we did not give up or give in. We reformed our groups and under the command of Ninui we returned and vanquished many hags, skeletons and werebats.

We battled our way deep into the icy caverns of Permafrost. Goblins fell to our mighty onslaught in droves, until they called out the warriors worst fear.... a diplomat. The great huge, bugger was still no match for us and we did proceed to beat his large, cold arse. Then in true diplomat fashion, the bugger turned tail and ran ... straight to the helpful arms of a dozen or more goblins. Grinch, Amethysax, and Ninui gave their lives while the others gated away. This diplomat has moved way up our list of foes to re-visit.

A wonderful night. We started in Cazic killing in the throne room area, then moved to the green pool, then we headed all the way into the temple area gathering quest requirements as we went. Then off to the Rathe Mountains to finish off a few paladins and some hill giants. A great night!

Christmas night was spent killing giants in the Rathe Mountains. New Years night was spent in Vellious and it was most exciting. With the help of Ninui's killer Snow Bunny we slew our way through cats, walrus men, walruses, and minotaurs to reach the Dwarven city.

After another session of inflicting mass carnage upon the evil creatures of the icy wastes, Tain Shari traveled to Kunark to thaw the ice from our armor. We traveled through tunnels and into the crypt of Dalnir. As it was nigh upon the anniversary of Sulin's birth we celebrated with gifts, ale, and cake in between "releasing from the burden of life" the Kly and their coerced minions. Alas they dropped none of the fabled armor we sought from them, so perhaps we will remain to fight another day.

Perhaps we should have left after the last battle. Many a fine fighter was slain in tonights battle, though a travel weary Grinch rezed us. On the brighter side, Sulin was pleased to find many exciting monk weapons from off the evil Kly and I must admit that we removed a great number of the evil ones from the world. Where shall we go next week? Who knows.

Now we wish we never knew. Tain Shari has always walked the very edge of our abilities and on this night Kaesora was both our destination and our downfall. The experience we gained in this dungeon was exceptional and all was well until we were jumped by multiple creatures from out of nowhere. Many, many lives were lost as retreiving the bodies of our fallen in this dungeon was a nightmare. The vile creatures even hid Ivar's corpse in a wall, requiring us to bring in Cynth, one of our small but capable necromancers. The next time we visit Kaesora we shall be larger and there will be NO MERCY!

After a breif trip to Solusek's Eye we travelled back to Cazic Thule and reaped many fine rewards for our rougue, Quicksylver. The lizards fell like dominoes as Sulin ran her legs off pulling them to all the Tain Shari massed in the throne room courtyard. Next I think perhaps we will head for colder regions.

Ahh the Crystal Caverns, so cold ye can see yer breath and full of evil critters. Our first evening here we took on the orcs, alas we suffered several casualties. Found a small enclave of dwarves living beneath the ice and took advantace of their hospitality while we rested up. Next we went in search of crystal spiders and terrors. We found them in abundance, living in the frozen solitude of the caves. We killed so many the ground was covered with the corpses. When, with the help of our friend Fadi, at last we found their Queen she ran like a frightened rabbit. Still we hunted her down and killed her, not once but 4 times. Much good loot was gathered and not a single death among us.

In preparation for our upcoming Juzzie Juicer Olympics, we headed for the Mountains of Rathe where we hear the giants have platinum dripping from their pockets. The rumors proved true and we spend many fruitful nights raking in the money to fund our games. When at last we had prizes aplenty we met in arena for a night of competitions. Under the watchful eye of a visiting GM, we slew our largest member, Durgle and several visious cats the DM was kind enough to summon for our amusement. Next we headed for the lake and fished foro prizes. Our guild fishing master, Freaya, mopped the shore with the rest of the guild. A bloodbath followed as we fought the whole guild in melee and then drank to our healths and fought again. A race round the arena was next with Durgle winning and Sordar and Elestria close on his heals. Great prizes were awarded and everyone had a terrific time. Our thanks to all those who helped make this event so much fun!

Kunark was the next place to feel the force of Tain Shari. We spent a night wandering in the Frontier Mountains but the terrain made it hazardous and several members met with untimely ends. We moved to the Overthere and used the open area to great advantage. Splitting up into 2 groups and taking the east and west we managed to wreck much joyous havoc upon the evil creatures wandering there.

Damn those frogs and skellies! We traveled to Lower Guk, the Dead Side and the outcome was very poor! The skellies started the fight and by the time they were done with us, few Tain Shari lived. We will return again another day and seek our vengence.

Ahhhh, a most enjoyable night was spend resting on the couches in Sol A. Of course the goblins had to be persueded to allow is this rest but they were no match for us. Next we travelled to Eastern Wastes and despite a rocky start fighting a pack of ravenous wolves, much exp and fun was had. We split into our usual 2 groups and hunted orcs and walrus. Those wishing to stay in the area were suprised to find that the ice giants had overrun the dwarf city of Thurgadin and killed all the city's people. The giants will have to die!

The group has been camped for many days in the Overthere. No matter how many vile creatures we slay, more set upon us. The only bright spot is that our sword arms are growing stronger and our casters more versed in their arts. Next we headed back to that pit of evil, Cazic Thule. There we used our newly raised skills to wreck havoc among the lizard and their ape menions.

Tonight we head for a fabled ruin in Kunark called the City of Mist. Who knows what adventures may await us. Alas we never reached the Misty City. Evil running rampant near the outpost of Firona Vie took all our attention and many of frogs, giants and drolvargs fell to our swords with nary a loss among us. While we battled in Kunark work reached us that a new land had been discovered in Odus, called the Stoneburnt Mountains. With all due haste Ninui and Lassenera scooped us up and transported us to this new and unexplored zone. I am pleased to report that Tain Shari were 8 of the first 44 to enter this new land. We battled several cats, as we explaored and eventually discovered a city of cat people. They were similar but not evactly the same as those cat folk of Kerra Isle.

The icy continent of Vellious now hosts Tain Shari. We have slain many creatures there, from the Geonids in Crystal Caverns to Wyverns of Cobalt Scar. Our guild has now grown in skill to the point where even the plains are opening to us.
