The primary mission of the Dark Ring Raiders is to band together as an army of loving friends, to harness the power of potential, and to succeed in becoming a powerful and unstoppable army of Raiders. An army of Raiders that are able to join forces and overpower the toughest mobs in the game. So that when a fellow member of the guild needs an item or a piece of their epic we can sound the horn of war, band together, set forth on a journey, take down the intended target, thus succeeding in helping the fellow friend while having a great time. Our forces are stronger than ever and great patience and an extensive amount of time has occured in the outfitting of the Raiders. Now that we are strong as individuals we must set our sites on the mobs that will test the strength of our unity. As we test our strenths and discover our weaknesses we will learn through practice and experience how to fight together as one. Through unity we will then harness the power of the guild and succeed in overpowering difficult mobs that would be impossible to defeat as individuals.
