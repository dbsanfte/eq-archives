The Guild Treasury is the collection of donated platinum and equipment that will be used for the following:

-- Raid Keis and Aegos when they are not readily available.

-- Hiring people outside the guild to preform tasks that we might need at a raid but are not readily available. Such as Lockpicking, Corpse Summoning, or High Level Rezes.

-- Funding Tournaments with prizes and award money.

-- Rewarding the younger guildies with gear when good integrity and credibility is noticable.

To Donate to the Guild Treasury contact Capernicus inside EQ and expect to meet at the Treasury location at the nexus bank in bazaar. This is where Capernicus will log on Jupiner who will hold donated funds and equipment.

Donation should be 100pp and up due to having to log off Zepalin and Haawkings. Donations of 500pp or 1000pp or more, as well as expensive equipment, will be posted here to acknowledge your support. The total guild funds will also be updated when necessary. Capernicus has made the first donation of 5000pp to begin the Treasury.
