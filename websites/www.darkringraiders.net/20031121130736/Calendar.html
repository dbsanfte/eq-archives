As the Leader of DRR I strongly encourage Raiders to lead Raids of their own in order to gain the experience of doing so. If you dont know how to lead a raid or would like to learn, then all you have to do is observe Cap on one of his raids. Cap's been leading raids since his mid 20's, and that was 40 levels of alot of raiding and learning let me tell you 8). Also we need Raid leaders for level 50 and under raids and event. Those who throw multiple-successful level 50-30 raids or events will be rewarded greatly for their efforts and will look good when applying for your Officership, Senator, etc. The same goes for level 50 - 65 Raids and Events.

If you attend any Raid or Event listed below Please be sure that you have read the Rules section of the website before attending. We Raid in an organized manner, that means Respect your Raid Leader, be Attentive, Listen to orders, dont Goof off during turnins, no whinning about exp or loot or anything else, an if there is ever a moment that you are not satisfied with being at the raid, dont ruin it for others by complaining or expressing negativity, simply ask for a tl or escort out of the raid and we'll get you out of the raid or exp grind or whatever. So before you take your frustrations out on a raid, be sure to leave the raid first, and then take it out on something else, because for one it's only going to look very bad on you and not anyone else. If your disruptive consistently on raids of course you will be disbanded. Just fair warning we dont tollerate childish behavior. To Schedule an Event or Raid you wish to organize and lead, simply email capernicus@darkringraiders.net the information about your Event, as you see it below. Day, Time, Zone, Briefing, etc. When you schedule an event the day of the event must be at least 3 days from the day you submit it me in order to allow Raiders proper time to discover that the raid is going to take place. If you have any questions just ask.
