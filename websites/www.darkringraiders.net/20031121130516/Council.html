To become an Officer of the guild you must be at least level 55, and take an active part in the guild. There will be no level exceptions on guild officers. If you would like to become an officer just send a tell to Capernicus.
Officers duties are: 1. Inviting new members into the guild when an invite is needed. 2. To report any bad behavior from guildies or non-guildies.

To become an Senator of the guild you must be at least level 60, and take an active part in the guild. This means going to most of the raids, communicating within the guild. Exceptions on level requirements may take place in certain cases. For instance a level 45 or so can become a senator if he shows and active roll and supports the guild tag with a positive attitude toward others. There will only be 15 senator positions available, but positions may become available if a Senator resigns or is promoted to a higher duty.

Senator duties are: 1. To vote on issues that arise within the guild. 2. To amend the rules created by the leader of the guild.

To become a Nobleman of the guild you must be at least level 55, and must be willing to assist Capernicus whenever he calls for your help. You will not be required to assist Capernicus in every instance but repeated unassistance will result in the demotion of the nobleman status. Nobleman status will be decided by Capernicus and will depend on factors such as behavior, kindness, participation, and most importantly Nobility.

To become a Lord of the guild you must be level 65, 100 aa points, and must be a Nobleman for a duration of one month minimum. A lord has no duties unless he/she wishes to remain a nobleman. Lord Status will be decided by Capernicus and will depend on factors such as behavior, kindness, participation, and your ability to raid.
