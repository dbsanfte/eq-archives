JAGGED BLADE OF WAR

Questing CheckList:

 

Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt

  Recover Unjeweled Dragon Head Hilt from bottom of Lake Rathe beneath the flaming Aviak tower.
  Obtain Diamond. 
  Obtain Black Sapphire. 
  Obtain Jacinth. 
  Turn in all four items to Wenden BlackHammer in East Freeport to create the Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt. 

 

Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt

  Retrieve Damaged Dragon Head Hilt from the Chessboard in Timorous Deep (-6500, 3000).
  Loot a Monocle from a Mountain Giant Patriarch (Dreadlands). 
  Give Monocle to giant, Mentrax Mountainbone, in Frontier Mountains giant fortress in return for Rejesiam Ore.
  Loot Ball of Everliving Golem from either Fright, Dread, or Terror in the Plane of Fear. 
  Turn in Hilt, Ore, and Ball to Wenden BlackHammer in East Freeport to create a Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt.

 

Ancient Sword Blade

  Purchase a Keg of Vox Tail Ale...can be purchased from vendors throughout Norrath (Freeport Port Authority). 
  Loot a Block of Permafrost from an Ice Giant within Permafrost. 
  Purchase or create 2 Rebreathers (made through Tinkering skill and sold for 2-3k each). 
  Turn in all four items to Denken Strongpick on aviak island in the Ocean of Tears to receive Ancient Sword Blade. 

 

Ancient Blade

  Loot the Ancient Blade from Sarnak Queen of Chardok. 

 

The Red Scabbard

  Obtain Wax Sealed Note from Kargek Redblade in East Freeport. 
  Deliver the Note to an ogre named Oknoggin in Feerrott who can be found standing next to a stone bridge. Receive a Tiny Lute in exchange for the Note. 
  Return the Tiny Lute to Kargek Redblade in exchange for a Book. 
  Deliver the Book to Tenal Redblade in East Karana who can be found at the bottom of the chasm near HH zone. Receive a Frozen War Totem in exchange for the Book.
  Loot a Heart of Frost from a goblin wizard in Permafrost. 
  Turn in the Frozen War Totem and Heart of Frost to Tenal Redblade in exchange for a Totem of Fiery War. 
  Loot a Red Scale from Nagafen (Solusek), Zordakilicus Ragefire (Solusek), Talendor (Skyfire), or Nortlav Scalekeeper (The Hole). 
  Loot a Green Scale from Severilous of Emerald Jungle. 
  Turn in the Red Scale, Green Scale, and Totem of Fiery War to Tenal in exchange for The Mark of the Sword. 
  Loot the Hand of the Maestro from the Hand that spawns when Maestro is killed in the Plane of Hate. 
  Turn in the Hand and the Mark of the Sword to Tenal in exchange for a Note to Kargek. 
  Loot a Spiroc Wingblade from the Spiroc Lord in the Plane of Air. 
  Turn in the Note and Spiroc Wingblade to Kargek Redblade in East Freeport to receive the Red Scabbard. 

 

VICTORY!

  Obtain Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt. 
  Obtain Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt. 
  Obtain Ancient Sword Blade. 
  Obtain Ancient Blade. 
  Obtain Red Scabbard. 
  Combine all five items to create Jagged Blade of War!!! 

Well done, Warrior.
