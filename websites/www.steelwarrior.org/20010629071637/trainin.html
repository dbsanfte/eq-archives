TRAININ'

This here section be fer understandin' the Might and Invincibility o' the Warrior class. Come and see the power o' the Warrior, so's that ye might point and laugh at all the weak and fragile sissy non-Warriors! Aye!

 

ABILITIES

Unlike most classes, the Warrior ain't burdened with no Experience Penalty. We also gits:

 ABILITY

DESCRIPTION

 Berzerker Frenzy
 When ye git low on health (below two bubbles), ye have the power within yerself ta strike out in rage against Death itself! Faster, Stronger, and more viscious than ever -- this is when the Warrior is most dangerous!

HP bonus
 No other class in Norrath can take the immense amount o' damage that a Warrior can. As ye progress in seasons, ye will notice a greater difference inna amount o' Hitpoints compared ta those other wannabe meleers...(HP boosts at levels 20, 30, 40, 53, 57, & 60)

 Critical Hits
 After ye learn yer basic fightin' techniques, ye'll gain the talent ta see certain "openin's" inna heat o' combat that will allow ye ta inflict more than double yer usual damage! (Ability obtained at level 12)
 Crippling Blows

These are powerful critical hits that are unleashed while ye be inna bloodthirsty rage o' Berzerker Frenzy. Tis possible ta whack yer foe fer quadruple damage! (Ability obtained at level 12)

 Magic Resistance
 Our focus and dedication ta masterin' that which lies purely within the Physical realm has given us a greater resolve ta resist that which is Mystical...we seem ta gain an extra point o' Magic Resistance fer every two seasons we progress.
