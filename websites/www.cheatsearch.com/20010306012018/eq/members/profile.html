Submit Personal Profile

Please complete this form to submit your personal profile information. The information will be linked from your primary character's name on the general membership listing. For an example profile, click here.

The following information is required for the submission to be accepted:

  • Primary character name.
  • Your real life email address.
    So that I may contact you if I have any questions.
  • Your guild sponsor.
    List your sponsor under their current name, or last known name, whether they are still with us or not.
  • Your home town.
    This is where your character first generated.
  • A picture (screenshot) of your primary character.
    The image must have the following characteristics:
    • The character's name and guild tag must be visible.
    • It must be in JPEG (.JPG) format.
    • The image size must be less than or equal to 640x480.
      It may be any dimension you like, as long as it is less then 640 x 480.
    • The file size must be less than 60k.
    • There is a good shareware program that will convert, crop and reduce images. It is called Paint Shop Pro and you can download it from http://www.jasc.com. However, you may use any program you want for your editing.
    • Please use your primary character's first name as the picture's filename.
      For example, a picture of Bere Ironwood would be bere.jpg.
    • After you have submitted this form, e-mail your picture to Bere Ironwood and attach the photo to the message.

The following information is optional:

  • A history of your primary character. It can be as long or as short as you like.
  • Any alternate characters you may have.
    The form has slots for eight (8) alternate characters. If you have more than eight alternates you wish listed, please submit the form twice.
