Membership Application Form

Membership Requirements

The only way to become a member of the Guild is by sponsorship (invitation).

Being sponsored means that a current guild member, in good standing, vouches for the candidate and takes responsibility for all of his/her actions. Any infraction of the rules by the candidate, is also counted against the sponsor. If you are interested in becoming a member, you must first meet with one of our existing members (please do not contact the Guild Master directly).

Restrictions

All races and classes are qualified to be members, with the following restrictions.
  • Please, only characters of "played" good alignment should apply. For example, a character may be a Troll Shadow Knight (characteristically evil), but played as a good, kind and friendly being towards others PCs.
  • Candidates must have a minimum level before initiating the application process.
    • Tallon Zek: must be at least 35th level.
    • Karana: must be at least 10th level.
  • On the Tallon Zek server, the member cannot be of the Dark Elf, Iksar, Ogre or Troll race. They can own such characters, but they will never wear a Peacekeeper guild tag.

Review Process (Tallon Zek)

The candidate's information will be posted to the guild Recruitment message board for one calendar week (7 days). All current members are encouraged to read information on the candidate and post all comments, positive and negative.

It is important that all issues be aired prior to indoctrination of the new member. The sponsor(s) of the candidates will remain undisclosed to avoid bias unless the sponsor personally wishes to reveal him/herself in a post.

Once a descision has been made, the open review of the candidate will be deleted before they are provided access to the private message boards. This is to protect the integrity and honesty of the posts. What is said on the Recruitment message board, stays on the recruitment message board.
