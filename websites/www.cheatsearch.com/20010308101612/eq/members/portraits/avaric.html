Name Avaric Paramonte
Race Erudite
Class Wizard
Sponsor Alexi, Korvak, Mezmer
Home Town Erudin
History A young Wizard toiling through the dark Toxxulia Forest alone eventually befriends a helpful Cleric named Alexi. They teamed on a venture to Kitty Isle a few times and soon enlisted the help of a level 11 Mage named Korvak to seek out some larger prey. Eventually the hunt moved to Qeynos and in the dank sewers were joined by an Enchanter named Mezmer who knew the sewers well. These friends earned my trust and soon was introduced to a talented Druid named Bere Ironwood and without hesitation joined the Peacekeepers Guild. From there many adventures followed, far too many to list here but these five travellers soon became known as the Death Squad of the Peacekeepers Guild.
