Strength of Earth Alt 40 Target Increases the target's strength by 10 points, +1 per level above 14th.
Thistlecoat Abj 25 Caster This spell increases the caster's armor class (AC) by 5 and does 1 point of damage to any monster that strikes the caster. Stacks with Shield of Thistles.
Treeform Alt 40 Caster This spell transforms the caster into a tree. The caster can meditate, attack and cast while in treeform. In addition, it increases health regeneration and grants infravision. Does not work in all zones.
