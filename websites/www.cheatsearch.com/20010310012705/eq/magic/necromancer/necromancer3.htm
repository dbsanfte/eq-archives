Magica Necromantic

3rd Circle

Required level to cast: 8
Approximate cost of spells: 6gp -> 4pp

Spell Sphere Mana Area of effect Description
Bone Walk Conj 80 Pet Summons pet. Components: 2 sets of bone chips.
Dark Empathy Alt 20 Caster and target Draws health from the caster and gives it to the target.
Dark Pact Alt 5 Caster Sacrifices health to create additional mana.
Deadeye Div 35 Caster Grants the ability to see invisible creatures and provides Infravision.
Gather Shadows Div 35 Caster Turns the caster invisible for a limited period of time. This spell is not omnipotent; some powerful creatures will be able to see you even while this spell is active. For example, Cyclopses and high level undead can see invisible.
Impart Strength Alt 15 Caster and target Draws strength from the caster and temporarily grants it to the target player.
Lesser Shielding Abj 25 Caster Spell of protection that temporarily adds to the armor class, hit point maximum and magic resistence of the caster.
Mend Bones Alt 25 Target undead Heals undead creatures. Meant for healing your pets.
Shadow Step Alt 10 Caster This spell will teleport the target in a random direction, for a short distance. Good for escaping from unwanted battles.
Vampiric Embrace Alt 30 Target Similar to Lifetap, but requires the caster to physically touch the target creature.
Ward Undead Evoc 30 Target undead Deals direct damage to undead creatures (only). Damage is 30 points base, plus 1 point per level of the caster. For example, an 8th level Necromancer does 38 points of damange while an 11th level Necromancer does 41 points of damage.
