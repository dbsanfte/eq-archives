History After spending my youth in my homelands, I caught the travel bug that seems to affect so many other young Druids. I wanted to see Norrath, so I packed up my belongings and headed off for Antonica, I spent a few seasons in Erud's crossing, still my wanderlust tugged at me. I decided to relocate to the Planes of Karana but the rain drove me to drink. Just in time, I decided to make my home in Everfrost Peaks. I had never seen snow and ice before, I found the climate much too cold for me, but found that most Barbarians are quite friendly. This is where I met Ruiner, he was a good friend of my adopted sister, a Ranger by trade, and we instantly hit it off. I enjoyed listening to sing his beautiful melodies and play his lute (the drums can get a little much). We would sit around in the evening and chat before we turned in for the night. We have become quite close and now we travel through Norrath together making our home in the Burnning Woods for now, however, we have been known to take vacations fairly frequently.
