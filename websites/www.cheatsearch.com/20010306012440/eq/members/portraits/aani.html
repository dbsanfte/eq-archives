History From simple beginnings Aani has slaved tirelessly at her craft and as a result of sweat and blood has achieved the pinnacle of jeweller success. Although never hunting the evil ones out at an early age, she quickly grew tired of and then irked at the advances of the darks ones after leaving her homeland and now goes out of her way to wreak havoc where and when she can.

She can be a bit chatty at times, and maybe even a little of a perfectionist as she usually tries to futher every grouping into becoming a well oiled machine. At the root is a kind but strong woman who will not back off nor leave a fight early, nor leave a friend in need.
