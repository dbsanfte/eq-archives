Welcome to the Guild Hall of the Peacekeepers of Norrath. In this area you will find all information pertaining to the organization, membership, allies and enemies of our guild.

The Peacekeepers Guild is an Officially Recognized Guild. We are a politically, racially, and religiously neutral body. We can be found in the Norrathian incarnations of Karana and Tallon Zek.
