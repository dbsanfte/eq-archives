September 8, 2000

Tallon Zek Server
Peacekeepers Dive Kedge

With cheers of support from our lower members, a Peacekeeper force of 11 members 45th level and above gathered in Kedge Keep to vanquish the evils of that sunken city. Our first target, the reviled and twisted Mermaid Estrella, fell easily to our might.

Our attempts at the King of Kedge, Phinigel Autropos, in the end we unsuccessful. After many skirmishes with his guard and much learning, we were forced to depart leaving the sea monster of Kedge Keep unvanquished.

Everyone did a wonderful job and we now know better how to slay Phin. Victory will be ours next time!

February 4, 2000

Karana Server

It is with a heavy heart that we are disbanding the Karana chapter of the Peacekeepers. Karana is where many of us cut our teeth in Norrath, and we will always remember all members, past and present, fondly. However, it appears that disbanding of the charter is the right thing to do.

Rastsar will meet with Jeng to transfer the Guild Leader tag back. Members who wish to retain their Peacekeeper guild tags may do so. We do ask that no Officer on Karana admit any new members.

Those that wish to follow Jeng and Rain may do so with our gratitude and thanks for all of the support they have shown the Peacekeepers.


Wishing everyone all the best,

July 8, 1999

We have times and a date for the guild language lessons. Our two language masters, Gunter and Rastsar, will be conducting lessons at separate times and in different locations to best accommodate our members.

Thursday, July 8
Instructor Gunter
Location 1 East Commonlands, in the tunnel to North Ro.
Start Time (1) 7:00pm, PST
Start Time (2) 2:00am, PST
   
Instructor Rastsar
Location 1 North Qeynos, at the city entrance.
Start Time 8:00pm, PST

Each instructor can teach only 5 people at a time, so please be patient and help each other out. If you have finished, stick around and see if you can, in turn, teach someone else in the guild a language they are looking for.

For all 23 languages, the lessons take about 2.5 hours (about 7 minutes per language). As are reminder, only members above 20th level are granted Dragon and Elder Dragon language training.
