History Masoud is the son of Bere and Krystall Ironwood. From an early age, Masoud wanted to follow in his mother's footsteps, that of a holy warrior. However, he felt that reliance upon anything but his own body and skill was to deny the natural order. To stand before your foe shielded by pounds of steel was to dishonor yourself and your opponent. Let the best being win, not the best equipped.

Much to his mother's chagrin, Masoud abstained from entering the order of Knights and joined the monastery of the Silent Fist. Within its humble walls, he perfected his lessons, shaping his body into the ultimate weapon. His hands became like swords, his fingers like daggers, his feet like axes and his fists like hammers. He emerged from his training a humble man of honor and peace who could deal death with his bare hands.

Immediately upon his promotion to the novice rank of White, he started traveling the lands under the guidance and tutelage, of his father, Bere. He was shown both the glory of the lands and the desolation inflicted by evil. Upon this revelation, he committed himself to the cause of his parents, which is to unite the good races and drive back darkness.

In keeping with family tradition, Masoud joined the Peacekeepers of Norrath, eventually being promoted to an officer. Upon the retirement of his stepbrother, Khazade, Masoud ascended to co-leader status with Mezmer.

Masoud and Mezmer now cooperatively lead one of the oldest guilds in all of Norrath.
