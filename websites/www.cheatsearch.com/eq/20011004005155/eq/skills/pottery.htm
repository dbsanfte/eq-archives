Pottery Guide

by Bere Ironwood, GM, The Peacekeepers of Norrath

Pottery is the art of making containers and other useful items from raw earth, water and fire. The skill is used in conjunction with other trade skills and a master potter will have demand for products from many other tradesmen, particularly Bakers, Rogues (for poison vials) and Tailors (also for poison vials).

Before you undertake your work as a potter, you will need a pottery wheel and a kiln. Both can be found in most cities. Place and combine your raw materials on the pottery wheel. NOTE: You will ALWAYS need a flask of water to work the raw materials with.

Once you have your product, you will need to fire your work in the kiln with an appropriate firing sheet.

The best places to work on Pottery are Neriak, Kelethin and North Freeport.
