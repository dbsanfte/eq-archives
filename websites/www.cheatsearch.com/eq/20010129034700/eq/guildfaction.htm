Faction Definition
Allies We have an alliance with this guild. We are good friends and do whatever we can to help them out. They are as close to being a member of the Peacekeepers as possible without actually having membership. Do not attack, ever.
Amiable We are on very good terms with this guild. Treat them as friends. Do not attack, ever.
Trade Parntner We are on good terms and have a trade agreement with them. Do not attack.
Indifferent Middle ground. We are not quite friends, but have no reason to distrust them.
Questionable What we know of this guild makes us unsure of them.
Distrust We know enough of this guild to be wary of them. They are borderline enemies and should be treated carefully and their motives should always be questioned. Do not group with any members of this guild. You may attack if you wish.
Enemies We are at open war with this guild. Do not group with any members of this guild and seek to destroy them whenever possible.
