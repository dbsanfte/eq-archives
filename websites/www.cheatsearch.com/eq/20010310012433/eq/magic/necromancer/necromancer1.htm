Magica Necromantic

1st Circle

Required level to cast: 1
Approximate cost of spells: 1gp and 5sp

Spell Sphere Mana Area of effect Description
Cavorting Bones Conj 5 Summoned creature Summons a minor skeleton "pet" to act as the caster's guardian. Skeleton is equivalent in strength to 1st level fighter. Requires 1 set of Bone Chips.
Coldlight Conj 5 Small area about caster This spell creates a dim glow of light about the caster. Used for seeing in dark areas.
Disease Cloud Conj 10 Area Casts a damage dealing cloud in the target area. Does not work against undead. The affected creatures continue to take damage for several rounds (DOT). This spell can be resisted. Approximate amount of damage per round: 5 points
Invisibility to Undead Div 40 Caster Makes the caster invisible to undead creatures. NOTE: If you cast this spell while you have an undead pet, your pet will commit suicide.
Lifetap Alt 9 Target Drains HP from target and adds them to the caster. Does not raise caster's HP beyond normal maximum. Approximate points of damage: 4
Locate Corpse Div 5 Target's corpse Will point the caster in the general direction of the target's corpse. Can target self (caster) or other player. Limited range on detection.
Minor Shielding Abj 10 Caster Adds hit points and minor protective armor to the caster.
Reclaim Energy Conj 5 Summoned creature This spell will banish the caster's summoned creature and replenish some of the mana used to bring it into existence.
Sense the Dead Div 5 Caster Points the caster in the direction of the nearest undead creature.
Siphon Strength Alt 5 Target creature Similar to Lifetap, this spell saps the strength of the target creature and temporarily grants it to the caster.
