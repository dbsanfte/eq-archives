Brewing Guide

by Khazade, GM, The Peacekeepers of Norrath

Brewing is the art of getting people sloshed. You combine various components in a still, and when successful, your prize is a beverage of immense intoxication. Sometimes these beverages are so caustic that they are used in conjunction with other skills to produce armors.

To brew successfully you need the raw ingredients, a container, and a still. The components are combined with a container in a still. If you fail, the container is returned to you for re-use, but the ingredients are lost.

Great places to practice your brewing skill are Neriak and South Qeynos.
