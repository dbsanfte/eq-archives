Counteract Disease Alt 50 Target A very potent cure disease spell.

Extinguish Fatigue Alt 35 Target Restores fatigue (stamina) on the target. This is an extremely important spell to have for keeping warriors up in combat and in underwater dungeons (example, Kedge Keep).
