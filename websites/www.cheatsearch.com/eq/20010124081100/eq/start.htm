Welcome to the EverQuest Guild of the Peacekeepers of Norrath. Here you will find information on our guild, our policies, our Charter and our purpose for existing the lands.

The Peacekeepers of Norrath is an Officially Recognized Guild. We are a Light Race guild, dedicated to spreading tolerance, protecting the weak, and promoting unity among all the good races of Norrath.

We can be found on the Norrathian incarnations of Tallon Zek and Karana.
