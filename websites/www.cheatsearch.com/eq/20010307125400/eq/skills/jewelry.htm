Jewelry Guide

Welcome guild members, I am Harkinon, Gnome Enchanter, Tinkerer, and Jewel Crafter. In this lesson you will learn the art of jewel craft, as well as some of the closely guarded secrets of creating magical jewelry.

Jewelry is the craft of creating decorative and sometimes magical items that can be worn on the fingers, neck, and wrists. This is a very expensive hobby to get started in. First you need a jewelers kit which you can buy one at the local gem shop for a little over one platinum. Second, you need a bar of metal and a jewel to combine with in the kit. Many combinations are possible. Combining any jewel with any bar of metal (copper, silver, electrum, gold, platinum) will result in a new product, either a ring, necklace, or bracelet.

WARNING: I strongly suggest you spend at least 21 training points at your guild on jewel craft before you get started as it will save you alot of money. Failing at making a silver piece means you lost, at least, one platinum.

Magic & Jewelry

The most useful type of jewelry is the magic kind. Magic jewelry is made from enchanted metals. Enchanters can enchant silver at level 8, and other metals at higher levels. Enchant silver and other metal spells can be found in Neriak, 3rd Gate zone, in the library. The thing to remember is that if you cannot make magical jewelry, you will lose money when selling it, because regular jewelry sells to merchants for less than the materials cost and finding a player that will buy it will be almost impossible.
