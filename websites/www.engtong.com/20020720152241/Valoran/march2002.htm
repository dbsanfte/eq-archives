Nathyn Illuminious 03/18/2002 by Merileaf

Today we went to Katta again, and killed Lcea. She droped a staff. Then we noticed Nathyn up. What followed was the most crazy fight I've seen in a long while. So we're 2 groups, 12 people, including 2 clerics and no enchanter. We decide to pull Nathyn at the zone to try him out. We get him slowed pretty fast and the tanking goes well, but then we find out Nathyn has tons of HPs. There was several calls for zoning, but the clerics somehow always managed to get a CH in time while meding furiously, so we kept on. The fight goes well for the first few minutes, but the clerics run oom real quickly. Fortunatly about 20 minutes into the fight we have a mage join up to make mod rods, and then a bard. After what we thought was the longest fight ever, he has nearly no red left. Of course, we forgot one small detail. Nathyn is an enchanter...

So he gates, we manage to bring him back to the zone, but now he is back to 30%.... we decide to continue anyways and bring him back down, but this time we have our bard and mage drain him. We also have a couple of bystanders join in so he goes down faster. Unfortunatly, it appears bard and mage draining isn't enough for Nathyn...

This time slow went off *just* as he started to gate. Needless to say, he summoned everyone to a very nasty death. The whole fight lasted over 75 minutes! So we rez, rebuff, and decide to fight him in his room. After another very long fight, he finally dies. He droped a rather non uber robe, but that really wasn't important. I think we all had the most fun killing this guy with 2 groups, even if it took over 2 hours to do it, and it probably was the weirdest fight many of us will see in a long time.

In other news, we also killed one worthwhile mob. Talendor droped some nice things worth the 20secs it took to kill him.

Cloak of Flames to Meurspas
Mystic Pouch to Steelfists
Tobrin Eyepatch to Axemaster
Dragon Scale to Guild bank

Champion of War! 03/14/2002 by Merileaf
Well today we decided to go check out the Ring of Fire quest again. We heard it got updated and improved, so we gave it another shot. We defeated a couple of boss mobs, and received some rather poor items, before we were overwhelmed with skeletons. In other news, the GMs today were holding a melee PvP event in the arena. There was people from most major guilds there, and everyone went at each others to see who was the best fighter in that type of event.
