Apps are Open again with Modifications! 12/01/2002 by Avasheon

First of all, read over the Charter before continuing.

Valoran's applications are open with new level limits.

Level Requirements for most classes has been raised to Level 65.

*Special Considerations may occur however are rare*

Valoran is a raiding guild, that tries to mix in enough time off for people to enjoy themselves in whatever they like to do besides raiding. Do not be confused, Valoran raids often. If you do not like raids, do not apply. Any questions about this or anything else, feel free to send me a tell in game. Before/After you post your app, or if you are comtemplating posting one send a tell my way as well.

*WARNING*
Valoran is currently only interested in those who like to raid, and raid often and who know how to play their class. Along with playing your class well, you must be able to play with the guild well. We are not dying for members, if you choose to apply you are the one who has to prove yourself not the other way around. Certain classes are open and accepting applications, however know that this does not mean an unlimited number of slots are open for each class and at any given time may a specific class be closed again.

Membership

Valoran is always on the look out for brave, honest and non-greedy adventurers. In order to apply for membership, you must be at least level 60, with the exception of Clerics 59+, Mages 56+, and have a history clean of kill stealing and/or intentional training. You need to have the following items: Sebilis key, resist gear bringing your resists to 100+ for fire/cold/magic and 80+ poison/disease *unbuffed*. These resists do not have to be at the same time, but should be magic+one simultaneously. For example, magic 110 and fire 120, or magic 105 and poison 90, etc. If those conditions are met, you are welcome to place an application into our applicationsforum for consideration by the guild, after which you may be invited to guild events for evaluation. You will not be given any loot during your application/probationary member period unless it is surplus. After all officers have gotten to evaluate you, your membership will be voted on. Your membership can be exterminated for reasons such as not showing up for events while online, not knowing how to play your character, or conduct unbecoming a Valoran member.


APPLICATION AND MEMBERSHIP PROCESS

I. Application is posted in the application forum

a. Application is moved to an applicant review forum


Access to this forum is restricted to full members only.

This move will be done by forum admin in first ~24 hours of posting.

b. Applicant review proceeds for 7 days minimum

7 day period begins from time of original posted application.

All members are encouraged to comment on applicants.

Applicant must attend at least 2 scheduled events in this time.

Any applicant not attending 2 events will receive a week extension to the review process.

Any applicant not attending 2 events in 14 days will be rejected unless extenuating circumstances apply.

c. If performance and comments are positive, applicant becomes a probationary member

II. Probationary membership period

a. Probationary member is tagged and given access to full member boards
b. Probationary members will be evaluated for a period of 21-35 days

After 21 days, probationary members may be inducted as full members.

Any probationary member not inducted as full members after 21 days will receive a 2 week extension to their probationary period.

After 35 days, any probationary member not inducted as full members will be removed with an explanation given as to why they are removed.

c. During the probationary period, comments will still be collected on the review board and used to determine a probationary member’s final evaluation
d. Officers will read each applicants review and vote to guild or not guild
e. Anyone wishing to reapply after a rejected application may do so after 1 month has passed and they can show the reason for rejection is no longer applicable
f. All applicant reviews will be moved to board admin forum for future reference at the close of the probationary phase

III. Full member period

a. Full members will be given access to Applicant review boards and allowed to comment on new applicants

BE ADVISED…on VERY rare occasions an applicant will be considered without having to go through the full application process. These will be special exception only and handled directly through the officers council.

 
Events
There are events going on all the time in Valoran. These events bring everyone in the guild together, get people to know and work with each other. Events are posted on our message board and calendar. it is the individual's responsibility to check it everyday and be on time for the scheduled events. When an event is scheduled, everyone is expected to attend whether it benefits them personally or not. All classes should try to attend all events. . If there is a trend that someone only goes to events that has his/her armors and weapons, he/she can be removed from the guild. At the start of these events, all irrelevant conversations should be kept outside of guildchat. Be aware that any member that does not attend one guild event in a months time may be subject to removal.
 
Loots
Loots are distributed by Kallinn. They are given out based on members' PARTICIPATION, DEDICATION and SENIORITY in the guild. The more events you attend, the more chances you have at getting items. Members that go out of their way to help the guild shall have that accounted for as well as attendence.

Any loot that is from a guild hunt or given by the guild is not allowed to be traded or sold without the permission of the Lootmaster Kallinn. You are to pass these items back to the Lootmaster for further distribution once you get upgrades. Any loot that you get outside of the guild is yours to freely do whatever you want with. However if a guild member needs them, you are encouraged to give them away.

Members with 2 characters in the guild may only choose one which they wish to be the primary loot char. The other char will only be given loot if a) all the others in that class have recieved an item on the hunt and b) all the others in that class already have the item. The primary loot character will be in consideration for items no matter which char the member is playing on that hunt.
Example: PlayerX has an SK and a Cleric in the guild. He wishes his SK to be the primary loot char. He is asked to play his cleric in Fear because of the shortage of clerics. A Soul Leach (war/sk weapon) is dropped by Cazic. PlayerX will be in consideration for the Soul Leech even though he is playing his cleric. However his cleric will be treated as secondary when a cleric item is dropped. Any switching of primary characters will be dealt with by the officer’s council and is done only in rare and special circumstances.

We will have no fighting over loot. If you have a problem with how loot is handled, please speak to the Lootmaster or Officers in a mature manner. They are only human, mistakes will happen from time to time.

and words are a direct reflection on us. Be careful how you conduct yourself in public. Show people what it's like to be a Valoran. Any violation of this charter may result in your removal from the guild.
