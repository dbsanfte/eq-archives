More dead Snakes 08/03/2002 by "The Walker"

Howdy folks! Welcome back once again to News from Norrath, Valoran style.

This is a short update since I missed most of the raids and my backup photographer seems to be slacking as well (just kidding Aeriann). I don't have a lot to report but here's what I have for you. I'm sure my other guildmates will be responding to this thread to tell everyone what I missed.

We were up on our rotation for the Emerald Jungle Dragon, Severilous. This guy is just a pain in the ass when he's up. Personally I had a hard time getting Mana burn to go off because of his retarded fear spell.
