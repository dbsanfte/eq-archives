Yadda Yadda Yadda 12/23/2002 by "The Walker"

Ok its been like forever it seems since I did an update. Guilds been working hard on a lot of stuff lately and with the holidays here I haven't been in the mood. A few guildmates threatened me so here it is for you. I guess they didn't like looking at a dead Terris Thule for the last month. /shrug

Valoran has been working over time on getting people flagged for all sorts of cool ass zones. Recently we got just about everyone in the guild flagged for Code of Decay and Halls of Honor (no thanks to certain other guilds for blocking us). We've also been working hard on a few other flags but I won't mention those until we are in and done.

Anyways we killed these two bad boys, people got loot, people got killed and now I can go to bed.
