That damned “cursed” is still giving us fits and managed to defeat us for the second time in as many tries. We learned a lot from this encounter and I’m confident that we will kill him next time.

First up this week was some snake people call The Creator. Like hey man stop trying to rip me off already. Well he heard I was logging on to kick his ass but the guild was already there spanking him. I wasn? able to get any photographs of this wannbe so you will have to settle for the loot.

First up is the talented enchantress, Desiree, sporting her lovely new Singed Petrified Leaf.

Next up we have my student in wizardly manners, Calisson, showing off his new Grey Dusted Wraps.

Then lastly we have Noslom and his new thing of power. I think that cave troll from Lord of the Rings is going to want this thing back dude.

High Priest was then forced to fall to on his own sword otherwise he would have killed us all. This fight was very sloppy. I don't know what went wrong where but from my prospective our warriors were dying every few minutes. Like in the game of basketball if the shot goes into the basket then points still count.
