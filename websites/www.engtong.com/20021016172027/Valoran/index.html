Greig and Reptiles 09/30/2002 by "The Walker"
Well, it’s been a long ass time since I did an update. Valoran is still alive and kicking ass as usual. We’ve been really busy trying to get our members into Vex Thal these past few weeks. It’s taking us a long time to get everyone equipped with keys and weapons but it’s starting to pay off. Keep up the hard work guys we are almost there.

We took a little break from ore and key farming in Ssra to kill Greig. This has to be one of the longest most boring fights on Luclin. There is nothing like getting tossed around like a pinball for 20 minutes while trying to cast a spell. The first two times on this encounter is fun but once you have ridden this roller coaster too many times it makes you want to puke. I’m sure some of us had fun and of course the rewards are always nice.

That leads us up to our last encounter for the two week period. Ssra went down and Valoran swarmed the zone. We managed to get the basement cycle started and what a nightmare this encounter turned out to be. We need a lot more practice on these encounters, ladies. That aggro was bouncing off everyone like a pinball game. MOB placement was the worst I have ever seen. We managed to take down Glyphed with a huge degree of difficulty.

Then Exiled spawned and we engaged. Much better aggro and MOB placement then Glyphed but still not what Valoran is capable of doing. We ended up getting Glyphed down to 0% health and the son of a #@#!@# started kicking our asses one by one. Then when we had one person left and not on aggro, the bastard regened to full health.

At least we killed Glyphed.
