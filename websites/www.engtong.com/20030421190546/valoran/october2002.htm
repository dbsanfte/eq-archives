Plane of Innovation 10/23/2002 by "The Walker"

Yep it's that time again people. Valoran hasn't killed anything new recently so the news was slow. We are picking up things a bit since 30 or so of us have bought the expansion. The rest of us are still waiting on shipments to arrive plus we have one major pain in the ass snake still to kill so these updates may not be a daily thing.

Anyways, we had enough people on to try a new dragon in the Plane of Innovation. This is one of the best looking dragon models I have seen in the game. We slayed countless broken down clockworks to reach this guy. Love to kill those terminator wannbe models. Overall this zone gets an 3 out of 5 stars from "The Walker".


click image to enlarge


We also had one of those Ethereal Parchments drop off this sucker but by the time the dragon was dead there weren't enough people alive to handle the repop so we had to get some melee *cough* to loot the guilds future spell.

Until our next great victory, I'm out of here!
