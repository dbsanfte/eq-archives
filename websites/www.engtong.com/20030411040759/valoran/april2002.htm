Dain Frostweaver IV 04/30/2002 by Galarian
*temporary reporter*

Day went pretty uneventful until Moridim got a call on the Batphone. The little schmuck Dain Frostweaver IV was up. So like a hooker at a traffic filled west side corner, we we're all over it. Was Valoran's first attempt at the little turd. So after getting the feel of the encounter, Mori got a feel of the Dain while he was down..

Moridim also got head tonight. Big grats to him =P Cloak to Gilantril Hammer to Yari.

Moving along, we got word Sontalak was up. So we waddled along to WW. Now I've been a grumpy son of a bitch as of late, but that kill was really impressive and actually brought me up a bit. Wasn't even a 1 minute fight I don't believe. Well executed whoring of Sontalak 1947.4 or whatever.

Grats Shadowwalker for being our new trans bitc....newest ST roamer.

O ya ring war going on I believe. Hopefully can report something good out of it =)

That's all for now. "The Walker" should be your new reporter within the coming days. Now I can go retire in peace~
