Spells are listed by level. Offense, defense, summoning, then other.

"Guilds" means that spell is available at Magician's guilds in all cities.
"Clerics" means that spell is available at Cleric's guilds in most cities
"Libraries" means that spell is available in the libraries at Erudin, Neriak, and Ak'Anon
"OOT" means that spell is available in the Ocean of Tears
"FV/OT" means that spell is available in Firiona Vie or The Overthere
"Pirate Outpost" means the gnomish pirate outpost on Velious
"Level 50+ drop" means it is available as loot from level 50+ mobs on Kunark
"Velious drop" means it drops from mobs of the appropriate level on Velious.
"Research" means that spell can be researched

Note that if a spell is available in more than one place in a city, only the most convenient is listed. ie, if a spell is available at both the Magician and Cleric guilds, it will just say "Guilds," not "Clerics."

Offensive spell types:
"Direct Damage" do damage to the targetted creature, without needing line of sight (though they normally have range limitations).
"Direct Damage Bolt" do damage to the targetted creature, but if there's anything between you and the target, or any elevation changes, the bolt will often miss.
"Area Effect Direct Damage" do damage to the target (if it's an NPC) and all targets in a small area around it.
"Rain Direct Damage" do damage to the target (if it's an NPC) and all targets in a small area around it (though usually bigger than Area Affect). The damage comes in 2 or more "waves," so if creatures move from the area of effect, they will not be hit by the later waves.
"Point Blank Area Effect" do damage to all NPCs in a small radius around you.

Defensive spell types:
"Personal Shields" Increase your AC and max HP
"Personal Armor" Increase your AC and decrease the damage you take from attacks.
"Fire Shields" Cause anything attacking the target to take damage, and sometimes increase the target's resistance to fire.

Submissions:
If you find a spell usable by magicians on a vendor, and it's not listed here (in particular, I don't have any of the gypsies), please send me a zone, /loc, and NPC name if it's just one vendor, and I'll confirm it.
Also, anything marked with a "?" is unconfirmed or unknown, and if you send in a confirmation or correction I'll update the chart. All submissions should go to wander@watson.org with "eqspells" as the subject line. Thanks.
