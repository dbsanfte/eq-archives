Our Elp is pretty young in its creation. Even younger then elp itself is the addition of the auction system. We used to assign points to everything but found that it was quite difficult to assign points to new items that we got on the fly. The auction system is perfect for us because it is self balancing.

1. If we get a great rare item it might go for 100 elp. Well two weeks from now it might fall again. We start the auction at 50% of the last auction end bid. So that item starts at 50elp and might only go for 70elp this time. The first person to get the item paid an extra 30 elp yes but he also got to use that item for an extra two weeks and there was no gurentee that it would fall again.

2. As everyone bids huge elp for great items that fall the end auctions will go down. Right now we all have a crap load of elp saved up because all we did was kill trak for months. Over the next month however I expect to see the average elp a person has to fluxuate from 0 to 20. And items going for around 10 to 20 elp.

3. Like bakk said if a person bids on a item then they have the elp to get the item. That means they earned the points and the item.

4. Bid wars are the coolest thing ever. Before we started the auctions we had alot of problems with elp. with set prices it was possible for one person to get defaulted 4 or 5 items in a row. We didn't want that. With this system everyone has a chance to get an item but if someone with a ton of elp really wants something they will pay for it and we have seen that quite a few times already.

I would say that elp is working well for us, but you should come up with a system that will work for you rather then just cut and pasting this system. I know we took alot of ideas from afterlife's dkp but we had to mold it to fit our vision.
