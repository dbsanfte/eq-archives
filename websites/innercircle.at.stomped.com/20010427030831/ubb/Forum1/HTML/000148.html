As Tawl noted, we killed Cazic Thule last night...But not on our first try.
On the first try we only got him to 20% and realized we needed more of the
guild to assist, so we iniated the Inner Circle Phone Chain...

The phone chain always begins with the most important people. So of course
it was left to the Ogre Shamans to begin making the calls. Svensk dialed up
Emmerald to see if he could round up her, SH and Soulaw.

*ring* *ring*
Emmerald says, 'Hello? Who is it? I'm busy eating, what do you want??!'
Svensk says, 'we need you guys in fear, can you come help?'
Emmerald says 'I have BBQ sauce all over my hands, i cant play! Here,
talk to SH.'
Strongheart says 'her pc is covered in food anyway, we'll be there.'
*click*

Since they brought Soulaw with them, I'll assume SH called him up and they
argued for a bit about who did less damage and who could die faster when
CT hit them.

Next was my turn to call Kenuvan, but since I've bothered his roommate so many
times, I decided to farm the job out to Arie. So Arie logged a twink on in gfay,
where Kenuvan's phone number is routinely broadcast in an effort for more phonesex.

*ring* *ring*
Kenuvan says 'ugh, hello...i was asleep'

Kenuvan says 'arie? What? We're killin Cazic? I'm loggin in...brt jeje!'
Arie says 'Squeak Squeak heifer!'
Kenuvan says 'W@W boobies!'
Arie says 'SQUEAK!'
*click*

Next we tried to contact Cobus and Zendak, but the phone just rang.
It turns out Cobus's dad had ripped the phone cord out of the wall and shoved it
in the garbage disposal so we couldn't get a hold of him. Zendak, at only
6 years old, was unable to reach the phone.
We logged him in, gave his epic snake to a druid pet and we didn't notice
the difference.

Next Svensk called Learic...
*ring* *ring*
Learic says 'wassup wassup....'
Svensk says 'We're in fear, gonna kill CT, wanna login?'
Learic says 'wassup wassup....'
Svensk says 'Im telling you, we're gonna kill cazic, could use your help.'
Learic says 'WASAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHH'
Svensk says '...'
Svensk says 'No.'
Learic sighs.
*click*
Svensk tells the guild, 'ok im playing Learic.'

I called Warlor, but found that he was busy driving his grandmother's hairdresser
to her psychic friend's dog groomer and then over to Dairy Queen...or something.
Yarrloc played Warlor instead and told the guild that he got Warlor's taunt skill
up to 28 during the fight. Cunnan immediately began worrying as his lead was cut
to only 7 points now.

I started to call Diggs, but realized a naked wood elf rogue wouldnt be much help?
He'd probably fall asleep and die to respawn anyway.

My last call was to Dystopia...I don't know why I bothered, but he was on my list.

*ring* *ring*
Dystopia says 'Hello?'
Bakk says 'Login for Cazic!'
Dystopia says 'Cool, i'll bring Deedlitte.'
Bakk says 'the gnome warrior?'
*click*
Bakk tells the guild 'Dystopia can't come.'
He came anyway...and he beat the crap out of Cazics left bigtoe.

In the end, Tawl got a Fearsome Cloak to match his fearsome one eye...
And Cobus got a Red Bull Etched Obsidian Choker.
Gratz everyone....except CT.
