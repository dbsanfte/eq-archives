Wuoshi and Kelorek'Dar
Wednesday, December 13, 2000 - Bakk
The Wakening Lands is safe again...

I'm sure most of the people who have ported to the WL gate in the past day or 2 have noticed Wuoshi...and weren't to happy about meeting him. Well after listening to cries of anguish from our wizards on guildchat for awhile, we decided to kill the Wuoshi.
He dropped a nice bow, Sky Darkener, gratz Indal. A Tanglewood shield and a Scimitar of the Emerald Dawn, Gratz Tiane * 2. He also dropped two Emerald Dragon Scales, which if our information is correct, make a pretty nice BP. Look for Arie and Endar sporting one soon as they finish the quest. Also dropped some spells: Archilich*2 and Protection of the Glades.

The day before that we dropped Kelorek'Dar, an ugly seadragon in Cobalt Scar. He forked over two CoF clones, the Cloak of Crystalline Waters, gratz to Yeoman and Breeze on finally becoming fully hasted. He also had a Monsoonish Sword, Typhoon, Sword of the Tidalwave, which went to Yeoman. He also dropped off two sea dragon scales, which will eventually become two bracers, if we can find a couple "Ornately Runed Necklaces" or something like that...Oh yeah and he dropped some crappy 9/20 piercer which we gave to Kenuvan because no one else wanted it.
Velious Day
Wednesday, December 6, 2000 - Bakk
Well, VeliousDay has arrived, we all jump in the boat/wizard/druid and ported over. I never liked Everfrost...at least there are less barbarians in Velious (so far). We finally got a couple groups together and Icewell Keep, also known as "The Pit" to the members of Dorftown, VE. We killed a bunch of frost spikes, Frosticubes and avalanche golems...then we found some gigantic gopher and took him out. I'm hoping thats what I look like in bear form, somehow I doubt it though. :( Zero loot to speak of in the pit...We found out way out through some caves under the ice and then all got lost again in the Great Divide. Then it was time for Trakanon, so we popped back to Kunark and killed him. Lag issues, even in small numbers, were an issue, thanks Verant..good patch. But the lizard went down and dropped an SK BP and a Druid BP. Gratz Dizzin and Fors.

Big Weekend
Monday, December 4, 2000 - Bakk
Well, here is the weekend recap...
Saturday was our first VP attempt. We really didn't have enough keys complete, but we tried anyway. Met some nice wurms and Dystopia got real friendly with a racnar on the way in. We found a good place to start working and we took a shot at Silverwing.
Official Result: IC-0 Silverwing-2
Next time we'll have more people and we'll do better. Overall, most fun we've had in a long time.

After we left Veeshans Peak, we setup for our Trak raid. Havelight had been camping the lair respawn, so we waited till after they tried and then head down and killed him. Yeoman got a Poisoned Mantle. Guildfund proudly wears its 2nd Shaman BP.

After beating Trakanon soundly, and our pride still hurting from Silverwing, we decided to smack down Severellious. Yeoman lucked out again, winning a green dragon scale. Redlance got a Dragon Hero Bracer, Derrick picked up a NagBag, Taeil got Demi-lich and I got Pox of Bertox. I think we were all quite happy by the loot there.

Look for us to be back in Veeshans Peak again soon. And SCREW YOU VERIX KYLOX YOU NON-SPAWNING BASTARD.
