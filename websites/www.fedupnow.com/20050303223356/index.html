How to prevent Bluejacking and/or Bluesnarfing
 

( Posted by: cheft on Monday, February 14, 2005 - 03:01 PM CST )

Bluejacking: Sending an anonymous message from mobile to mobile via bluetooth.

Bluesnarfing: Using a Bluetooth enabled laptop and specially written software, anyone can take your data at the push of a button.

How do I stop people from bluejacking and bluesnarfing me?
You can either go into your Bluetooth menu and switch it off, or if you need Bluetooth on for one reason or another you can simply set your phone to 'Undiscoverable' in your Bluetooth menu. This means that other Bluetooth devices won't find you when they search for Bluetooth devices.
