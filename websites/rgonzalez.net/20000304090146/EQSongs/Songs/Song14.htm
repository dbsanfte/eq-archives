LYSSA's CATALOGING LIBERTTO

Level: 14

Effect: Item Identify

Skill: Singing

Target: Single

Location: Erudin

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: N/A

Quantified: N/A

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: This song will allow targeted person (you or a party member) to identify an item held on their mouse pointer. This comes in very handy now that many quest items have the same name, but may not be the same item, ie Rune of the One Eye has three pieces (Top, Middle, Bottom).

Strategies:

You must have the item you wish to identify "in hand" (picked up with the mouse cursor).  Left click on the item to pick it up, then sing the song.
You can help other people identify items (useful with no-drop items).  Have them hold the item "in hand."  You then target them, and play the song.  You will then get the item lore text.

Cautions:

You now have to target yourself before playing the song to identify something you're holding.
