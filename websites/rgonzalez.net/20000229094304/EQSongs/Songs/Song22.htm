ALENIA's DISENCHANTING MELODY

Level: 22

Effect: Cancel Magic

Skill: String (Required)

Target: Party

Location: Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: ?

Quantified: N/A

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: This song will remove any magic based DoT's and de-buffs that have been cast upon a party, as well as any beneficial spells as well. This is not a bug, here is a post from GZ with more details.

Strategies:

Will remove magic based DoT's (Darkness line, Heat Blood line), Shaman drowsy line.
[Goatnatos] ADM will remove Ghoul Root, very handy in Lower Guk.

Cautions:

Has no effect on poison or disease based DoT's or effects.
Removes positive Druid and Cleric buffs.
