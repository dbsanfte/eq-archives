DENON's DISRUPTIVE DISCORD

Level: 18

Effect: Damage over time, lowers targets' AC

Skill: Brass

Target: Area Effect

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: High

Quantified: NonPvP: Int((lvl - 18)/2) +18 [Provided by Hahn]

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: This is your second AE Dot song and allows you to use your Brass skill. You will have a lot of trouble starting this song till you get your Brass skill up. You can do this at the Arena zone (off of Lake Rathe) or some people will use the large room in the N Ro tunnel (where the Shady Swashbuckler is) to practice this skill up. Just park yourself there start this song with horn equipped and let it play for a few hours (4-6). At the end of that time you should have a maxed brass skill. 

Strategies:

Now the good part, this song allows you to do the 'all instrument attack'. Create a hot key pad with your secondary weapon slot, an inventory slot, and hot key the following songs: Denon's Disruptive Discord (DDD), Chords of Dissonance (CoD), and Hymn of Restoration (HoR).
Find your target, equip your horn and have your lute on the mouse pointer. Start up DDD, when you get the wince message stop the song start CoD and equip your lute (before the wince message). When CoD winces stop it and start HoR. After HoR fires off stop it and start DDD and reequip your horn. Repeat. This will allow you to solo with little or no downtime, can also work well in party settings. For a few extra points of damage use hand to hand attacks. With this you can solo many blues with little or no downtime.
The all instrument attack will also work well in a party, and is a awesome PvP tactic against Melee classes.
[Kaearia] Most people neglect the AC debuff in this song.  While minor, it can make a difference, especially if the other melee party members are lower level than you.  Sing this song and they will hit more often for more damage.
[Kaearia] This song by itself, or in the all-instrument attack outlined above, is great for clearing out hordes of low green mobs; especially undead since live greenies tend to run before they die.

Cautions:

As with all area effect songs there is the danger of picking up unwanted guests in battle.  Be sure to use the 3rd person view to keep an eye out for wandering NPCs.
[Kaearia] DoTs will wake up mesmerized mobs with every pulse.  If you've put a bunch of mobs to sleep with Lullaby, or an enchanter has mesmerized them, playing this song will just wake them all up again
