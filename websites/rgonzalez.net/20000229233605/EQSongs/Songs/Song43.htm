DENON's DESPERATE DIRGE

Level: 43

Effect: Direct Damage

Skill: Singing

Target Area effect (up to 4 targets)

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: High?

Quantified: 311 + 2*(lvl-43)  non-PvP damage.  Requires 800 mana to sing.

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This is the only mana using song, it requires 800 mana. Currently Bard mana is Intelligence based. This song does damage to each monster within its radius to a max of 4 mobs. Due to it's power, Verant restricted its usage, making it the only Bard song to require mana. One use of this song is engineered to drain most, if not all of a Bard's mana. 

Strategies:

This song will damage the bard singing the song, but since the effect is centered on the target you can avoid this.
[Ididar] This song also has a knock-back effect, which will interrupt spell casters.

Cautions:

Mana regeneration spells, songs, or items have no effect on a Bard's mana regeneration rate. The only way to speed regeneration is to sit down.
The mana drain has been engineered such that no matter what buffs are cast or no matter what INT enhancing items you have, there will be no way to sing this song 2 times, back to back. 
This song is currently bugged as singing it will do damage to the singer, if you kill yourself this way you will lose experience.
With an Int of 90 at 43rd level this song drains 4 bubbles of mana.
