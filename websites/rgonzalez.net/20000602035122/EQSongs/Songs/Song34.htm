LYSSA's SOLIDARITY of VISION

Level: 34

Effect: Bind sight

Skill: Wind

Target: Single

Location: Erudin

Does not stack with: N/A

Quantified: N/A

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song allows you to see though the eyes of the target. This can be a PC or NPC. You can 'chain' this by targeting something you see from the current target and restarting the song. This song has a zone wide range.

Strategies:

[Kaearia] First you use Locating Lyric like you normally do. It'll point you in the direction of the corpse, but more importantly, it'll make the corpse your current target. 
Next you use Solidarity of Vision to bind sight to the corpse. Switch to an external camera view, rotate around, and usually you can tell from the surroundings exactly where the corpse is. VERY useful for finding corpses in dangerous sections of a dungeon, or if the corpse isn't where the person thinks he died.
Can be used for scouting, if the NPCs cooperate.
This song could be pretty useful for learning new areas that you don't know. Just bind sight with the puller to learn the dungeon.
Members of your party can be selected even when they are far away using the party hotkeys or party list.
