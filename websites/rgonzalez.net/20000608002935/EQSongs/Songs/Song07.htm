JONTHAN'S WHISTLING WARSONG

Level: 7

Effect: Increases strength, AC, and attack speed

Skill: Singing

Target: Self

Location: Freeport, Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: Low

Quantified: Str: Int(Lvl/2) +5
Attack speed: +24.35
Delay change: -19.58

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server:

Comments: For those soloing, this is an upgrade to the Chant of Battle song and is actually better that the 10th level Anthem De Arms. The attack speed component lowers the effect delay of your weapons.  So a 20% reduction will turn a weapon with a 30 delay into a weapon with a 24 delay.  Each point of delay is .1 seconds of real time, ie a 30 delay weapon will swing every 3 seconds.

Strategies: 

When soloing with this song, use Chant of Battle as well for an additional boost to strength, dexterity and AC.

Cautions:

This song will partially stack with Anthem de Arms.  The strength component will stack, the attack speed will not.
