JAXAN'S JIG o' VIGOR

Level: 3

Effect: Replenishes stamina

Skill: Percussion

Target: Party

Location: Freeport, Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: Nil-Low

Quantified: N/A

Notes: THIS SONG DOES NOT INCREASE MANA REGENERATION.  It has been confirmed by Verant to be a client-side refresh issue.  It as also been confirmed via a direct email form GZ at Verant

Analysis by Kaearia <Platinum Sphere>, Karana server:

Comments: Generally not very useful. When you are below 33% stamina, your str, dex, and agi start to drop.  When your stamina hits 0 you will 're-gain' a bubble and half of stamina if that stamina is used up your stats are adjusted lower and you 're-gain' another bubble and a half of stamina, so it's a good idea not to let this happen in a fight.  This song also means bards are relatively immune to the effects of starvation (health and stamina do not regenerate) should you ever find yourself in that position. Not having used this song much, I can't say whether it has a taunt effect.

Strategies:

Good to have memorized if your party is fighting underwater, as stamina does not seem to regenerate while swimming. Augmentation (if you have an enchanter) is probably better for this though.
[Eolyn] Underground this song may allow you to have a better chance of escaping, since you can run-jump all the way to zone with out worrying about going OOS (out of stamina).
[Sartori] Of course this song is a must when exploring Kedge Keep. Watch your stamina and when it drops a bubble or two give the Jig a pulse. No need to keep it going constantly. Later on in life many of your friends will appreciate you having this song available. If you are in a group with tanks sporting hefty 2 handers or fast dual wielded weapons (many of whom will be hasted somehow - sash, enchanter speed, etc.) you can do the same thing. Give it a pulse every so often during battle

Cautions:

Clerics, enchanters, and paladins get spells which do the same thing, so you may be better off using the song slot for something else and relying on them to invigorate you should you run low on stamina. If everyone in your group is chronically running low on stamina every fight, then the song is probably better since it uses no mana.
