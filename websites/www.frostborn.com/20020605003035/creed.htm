THE CREED


The Frostborn Protectorate holds justice and honor in their hearts. By following the rules of the guild as well as the dictates of the Tribunal, we hope to gain the respect of all for our guild. The burdens are not heavy, but they shall be observed with the utmost care.

No kill stealing. It is dishonorable to take what another has earned.  Vengeance towards another who hath unjustly dealt with thee is beneath the standards that Frostborn live by. Justice is not served in such ways.

Whilst banding with others, act with honor. Know thy job and perform it the best of thine ability. Only those which hath grouped with a Frostborn shall be invited into the guild for only then shall we have the measure of their worth. Accordingly, they shall have the measure of our guild and an understanding of their worthiness to enter.

When looting the spoils of our vanquished foes, thou shalt maintain the honour of the guild. All member of thine group shall be granted the ability to share in this bounty. Shouldst thou loot an item of great value, the entire party should be apprised and given the opportunity to receive those spoils in whatever manner hath been agreed upon. Not following this guideline is unjust and shall not be tolerated.

Thy guild mates are thine brothers and sisters. Help them to the best of thy ability. Thou shouldst not abuse this most valuable of all resources. They who continually beg for help are too weak to bear the mantle of Justice and Honour.

Artifacts of Power are by no means outlawed, however numerous heirlooms shouldst not be bestowed upon the younger members of our family. Those of the Frostborn Protectorate live by the strength of our arms, the fortitude of our minds, and the will of our gods. How glorious is the sword plucked from the twitching hands of thy vanquished foe, your brethren standing clustered around you bleeding, tired, and victorious!!

This Brotherhood is for those of a mature and responsible character. Those wishing to join its ranks MUST have attained 15 levels of expertise in their chosen profession. Furthermore, initiates who desire the duties and rewards of the Guild must complete the Rite of Passage which Shall include a minor quest determined by the Tribunal. While these measure shall numbers of our brothers lower than many guilds, it shall ensure the virtue of our Order.
