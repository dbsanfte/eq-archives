Sacred Legions

In every realm of existence there is a universal constant, that people with similar experiences tend to gather for the welfare and betterment of those gathered. This gathering recognized the benefits of their sharing, their caring, their need for companionship. Thus they formed a guild of support, a guild of family, a sacred gathering, a Sacred Legion of people. The members of this guild have more than experiences in common, however, they are like unto a family, with each individual being unique, and uniquely gifted. Each has his/her own goals, desires, likes, dislikes, strengths, and fears, but each also cares for their brothers and sisters in the guild.

Guildmaster: Star Gaizer

Guildmaster: Sorontar

Seekers of Neverwinter

Only recently come to Everquest, the Seekers of Neverwinter trace their history back to America Online's Neverwinter Nights, where it was a dominant force eradicating evil in the name of purity and piety. Since 1992, the Seekers have raised their blue and gold standard in endless campaigns against that which would oppress the weak, corrupt the innocent, and support the legions of evil. While many members are from Neverwinter, the Seekers are constantly accepting applications from those who wish to fight alongside them. If you feel you meet the high standards of excellence demanded by our crusade against evil, e-mail RipTorm@aol.com for an application or contact Draygo in-game.

Guildmaster: Draygo

Sentinels

We are a family oriented guild, and make enjoyment of the game a top priority. We consider each other brothers and sisters, and place helping each other out over power gaming. We accept all race/class and levels and recruit via the application on our website.

Guildmaster: Blacksun

Shadow Nights

Shadow Nights was formed by people who were tired of the politics of the larger guilds. We are a small and very close group who love to have fun with each other. We are selective because don't wish to lose this special bond we have with the people who are in our family. Team playing, competent fighter and a generally good attitude are needed. We believe in treating others the way we wish to be treated, with respect and honor. Age Limit of 18 years or older.

Guildmaster: LadiRose

Shae'en M'taal

We are a guild of friends. Ours is a guild for people tired of petty guild politics. We are not obsessed with powerleveling, item farming, or the recruitment of new members just to increase our numbers. We play the game to have fun and enjoy the company of others.

Solidus Aeternum

We are a guild that tailors to every type of player, and we are designed to just have fun! We have guild events every Sunday (or at least we try, haha), and we do not set a limit to the levels who can join us. We are a bunch of great guys just out to have fun.

Southern Legion

Southern Legion is an old Tarew Marr guild with all players from Australia. The guild has a very strict code of conduct and induction process with ensures all members are of the highest order of merit. The guild groups allmost constantly to enjoy helping each other and socialising, we also use Rodger Wilco for our comms. We are a very friendly guild who offer free services like Healing, Binding, Buffing and Mana regenration. So if you see a Southern Legion member do not freet, say hail.

Spirits of Light

Spirits of Light is a close knit guild, we are committed to loyalty and friendship above all else. We are always ready to help a brother or sister in need, and organize frequent guild events for almost all levels. We welcome any who want fun and friendship into our group.

Guildmaster: Morcree

Star Warriors

Star Warriors have been together for countless years, and are a close-knit family. While a member of the guild may hold the title of leader, this does not make him any greater than any other member. The guild is made mostly of all officers. New members are taken in after a period of one week (real time), where the officers will study their actions and decide if they have what it takes to join our ranks.

Guildmaster: Avalan

Sublime Darkness

Sublime Dark is a new guild on Tarew Marr.  As our name implies, we will consist of mostly evil races, though goodly races who wish to walk on the dark side are welcome to join.  We hope to inspire awe in the pales due to our elevated beauty, nobility and grandeur.   Our main purpose is to enrich Tarew Marr with roleplaying characters who maintain integrity, charity and self-being.  May Evil Guide you.

Symphony of the Night

The purpose of this guild is to unite honorable individuals with the common aim of looking after each other, ensuring our members prosper and live well. Our strengths lie in our might, our awareness of our combined talents, our sense of honor, integrity, teamwork, loyalty and our dedication to the guild and each other. This is all tempered by the sense of humor which binds us together. We are here for the good of other people and not solely to make ourselves prosper.

Guildmaster: Raziel

Syndicate

The oldest and largest guild in Everquest. We are a mature, professional, dedicated team of friends. We believe in having fun and that every member is important. Despite our large size, we are very unified and strong because of that. You will find us all over the world of Norrath. Seek out a member, get to know us, and join Online Gamings Premier Guild.
