Emperor Chottal Vanquished!!

Greeting friends, it is I - your Fatty Warrior, Uglar. I bring news from the land of Tarew Marr regarding the slaying of the great Emperor Chottal in Sebelis. This Foe is a level 60 Necromancer who hits in the upper 200's and has some NASTY dot spells. The thing about this slaying other than its the first time to be done on Tarew Marr (and any server that I know of) is that we did it only 1 group!!! With Great planning we were able to slay this mob in a bloody battle and only receive minor cuts and scrapes to our party (no deaths) =).

Within this battle me, Uglar Ba'shdemgud and my lovely wife Carressa Ba'shdemgud Raised our blades with honor. The Crazy er' BRAVE people to follow my trollish arse into this battle were: Aaronian Healwell, Gloriana Inamorata, Aiin Wildfire and Dalamin Chordweaver. All of these people have earned the respect of me and all deserve to be praised as gods!

Emperor Chottal is not one to be messed with. This battle although won with a small group takes GREAT skill and knowledge to pull off. I would like to thank EVERYONE that was there in battle along side of me! Sincerely Yours,
