If you'd like to make it easier to have your game friends get in touch with you out of the game, why not get yourself a tarewmarr.net email address? It's simple, just email Aviger, tell him your main character's name, and give him your normal email address. He'll set you up an address that will forward all of your tarewmarr.net email to the address you give him.
