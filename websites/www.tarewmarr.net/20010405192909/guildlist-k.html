Keepers of Knowledge

The quest for knowledge and deciphering the mysteries of Norrath. We believe in peace throughout all the lands, and a general politeness
towards all beings great and small. Those who seek strength in wisdom and intelligence are welcome in our ranks. None shall be viewed through an unkind eye, unless We have due reason to. The arcane magics of Norrath summon all who wish a life of peace, And the knowledge which can come from unity..

Guildmaster: Illarian

Kel'Daoine

We are Kel'Daoine, primarily (but not limited to) races from Faydwer. We've been around from the beginning, grown slowly, and take pride in the close bonds between members. We're not about rules, we're about enjoying the game.

Kindred Spirits

The guild is named as such because we are a mixed race and class guild for even though the paths of life may lead us in different directions; all brothers within our assembly are kindred in spirit.

Guild Officers: Amaranth, Arkie, Envictus, Gilbane, Omar

Knights of Antonica

The Knights of Antonica is a Guild dedicated to making sure its members have fun with the game and that they Keep the Faith. By this we mean that team work is a must and we will never intentionally train, harass or by any means disrespect others. We will never let a Guild member die without trying to help. We will always help others if they truly need help. We are not just about trying to level up some of us do like to role play. But we do keep an eye on new guild members to make sure that they keep the Faith. All classes are welcome as are all races.

Guild Officers: Farlee

Knights of Dawn

Fellowship and friendship is the heart of what we strive for. We believe in having fun and enjoying ourselves. We do raid's at least 1 a month we DON'T limit the raid's to a set level either we raid everything from Befallen to Plane of Fear. Stop by and say hello.

Guild Officers: Daybringer and Chasester

Kuru Legacy

We are here to help one another and to make being on Everquest a fun place to be. "We will assist each other in exploring new lands and worlds that others would fear to venture into alone." We have created a family like atmosphere that we have all learned to enjoy. We are a neutral guild with good intentions, where loyalty and honesty are held close to our hearts. Being a Kuru has always been fun for me and I think the guild would agree with me whole heartedly.

Guildmasters: Dirose
