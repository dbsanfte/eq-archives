Rara Avis

Rara Avis means rare phenomenon in Latin, and it is the chosen name of this tight-knit family who have survived much and came out of it still together. We are together for nothing more than friendship, fellowship, and fun. We often adventure in full groups of guildmates, and have events scheduled at least weekly. We are accepting new members, so ask any members online about our squire program.

Guildmaster: Wolff

Reapers of Fate

We're a midsize guild with players covering all ranges, races, and classes. We try to promote a strong sense of family within the guild - any Reaper is willing to lay down life and limb if needed for another Reaper. Check out the webpage and contact any officers if you're interested in joining up :)

Guildmaster: Aumaar

Reckoning of Honor

We are a close knit guild of like minded gamers founded by former members of the Holy Order of Armageddon. We accept all races, classes and levels. Since the split of HoA, we have grown to include more members than ever before, with more requests to join coming in daily.

Guildmaster: Majnuard

Reign of Chaos

It is our belief that nothing in life is constant except change, and change results in Chaos. Whether we call it fate, or chance or simply blind luck, Chaos is the governing force behind all things. Utter confusion is the natural order of the universe. There are those who try to fight it, that believe our lives can be lived with order or structure. It is our contention that they fight a futile battle. Chaos rules over all. Our purpose is not only to recognize chaos as a ruling force, but also to revel in it. We do not grow angry when we fall to a foe, be it worthy or unworthy, rather we recognize that we are just as likely to leave that foe on the ground the next time we meet. All things are chance. The only hope one has in a world of chaos, is to gather together others of like mind and spirit and fight the good fight. We are bound together as friends, brothers, partners with one goal, to thrive in the Reign of Chaos.

Guildmaster: Kiarah

Reverent Justice

Reverent Justice seeks to bring a sense of Pride and Respect back to the lands of Norrath. Our members are each highly skilled in their professions, Men and Women of honor, principal and integrity. Unified our efforts are revered. Our leaders have risen from the ashes of other failed guilds to bring forth a new guild, which honors Norrath, and provides a home where they may invite friends to join.

Guildmaster: Kalyope

Ring of Justice

To Protect and Serve the Noble Citizens of Norrath. The members of the Ring of Justice will render aid to those in need and maintain integrity in all actions, words and deeds. We seek Justice in its truest sense and swear allegiance to all members. We ally ourselves with those who share our beliefs, yet shun those who choose a dishonorable and malicious path.

Guildmaster: Elndorf
