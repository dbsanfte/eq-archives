Forest Shadows

Rising from its Origins in Brittania, The Forest Shadows breached the gap between dimensions, establishing a new foundation on the world of Norrath! Striving to keep all of Tunares Forests safe for those she allows to live within her bosom, is the prime call of duty for any Shadow, the only call above service to fellow Forest Shadows. We are a misfit band of forest dwellers ranging from low to very high levels of experience. You can find our members in all corners of Norrath, always carrying the Shadow banner high and prepared to do battle for Tunare at a moments notice! Please fill out an application at: www.forestshadows.com and introduce yourself to Arial Shadowolf the guild mistress. Wump the friendly troll, or Taurval Shadowfoot can also help expedite your allegience to The Forest Shadows, so feel free to give us a tell in game:)

Guildmaster: Arial
