Cavaliers of Virtue

The Cavaliers of Virtue are people who believe in Honesty, Honor, and Loyalty.  We are all willing to sacrifice ourselves for any other regardless of consequence.  We are very selective on who becomes a Cavalier, however, we are always recruiting.  We look for only the best of players in the world of Norrath.

Guildmaster: Jadak

Celestial Diciples

The Celestial Disciples are a small guild, with the intent of aiding those that are in need and helping one another in any way we can.  We are a close-knit family, and welcome those true of heart to seek us out.  We have a stringent screening process, and the guild is guided by a council of 5 members, but all decisions regarding the guild are made by the guild as a whole.

Children of Sorrow

A Guild with a focus on good roleplaying by its members, and enjoyment of Everquest as a multiplayer Roleplaying Game. A small guild, with a tight group of friends that enjoy playing this pioneering MMPORPG.

Covenant of Honor

We are a guild of friends who found one another along the way during our travels in Norrath. We continue as a closeknit group of hunters always seeking adventure and challenge.

Crimson Sky Empire

Crimons Sky was born in Diablo under clan Krieger as their First house of creation lead by Xanax Jaxlarz, then dra'khan of Krieger. Xanax's duties became so overwelming that the Sky almost fell until out of a portal came Rasmon-Sorcerer, Overlord of Krieger to carry on the traditions of the Sky. Under Rasmon the Sky lived and became a Castle of Krieger with many houses and members. A huge palace was ericted. One dark day Khan Artix Krieger, leader of Krieger , along with his minions sought to undermine the Sky and its leaders by discrediting them. On this sad day Crimosn Sky separated from krieger and became it's own clan. Spawned from the roots of diablo , the CSE continues to be a dominating force in the lands of Ultima Online. With the continued virtues of the Sky---Honor, Truth, Justice--- the CSE now carries on its traditions in Everquest.
