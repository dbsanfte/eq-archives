A close knit group of friends dedicated to having fun and exploring every corner of Norrath, and to improving the game experience of members and non-members alike.

Aegis Terra

Aegis Terra is a relaxed atmospheric guild, who enjoy  the friendships we make, within our guild and through our adventures in Norrath. Each Class has a House Leader so that specific attention may be given to all members.  AT is growing weekly, by having leaders for each House, we strive to keep all members active.

Guildmaster: Braizen

Amicus Familia

Amicus Familia - Family of Friends. AF is a guild who believes in quality not quantity. We are a caring guild with a lvl 30+ invite only system to ensure all members are able to group together, with the exception of secondary char's and RL family members.   AF members behave with decorum and respect for both guild members and other Tarew Marrsian's. We group together constantly, have guild events, but most importantly help each other when needed.

Officers: Tinuvan, Everia, Candaan, Galadraal, Ikland
