Here is a list of some upcoming Player Events on our server, arranged by date, along with some reports of events you might have missed!

Highkeep Raid - Sept 2nd - 3 PM EST

Nearly a year after our dear sister Lanys T'vyl and brother Laarthik led an ill-fated invasion to capture Highkeep Hold, it is time for the Dark Elf Army to come together again to avenge their deaths.

Rogue scouts have discovered that Carson McCabe was unsuccessful in persuading the forces from Qeynos, Erudin and even Freeport to help him protect Highkeep. With a weak force, our scouts feel the Keep is vulnerable. We also have reports of a Teir'Dal being held against her will inside.

It is now time, brothers and sisters, to finish the grand plan of Lanys T'vyl... to revealed a plan for a dark elf army to attack Highkeep, capturing the fortress and cutting Antonica in half. Once this is accomplished, we will have an easy time of overcoming Rivervale and Freeport.

Our goal will be to infiltrate Highkeep and destroy Carson McCabe's army. We will then find his Mistress Anna and the Lady McCabe and let them meet the same fate as Lanys T'val and Laarthik did. We will also free the Teir'Dal Tyrana Slil from her captors.

In the name of our father Innoruuk, unite and bring death and suffering to the pale races which infect our world!

Brokyyn Deathbringer <Sublime Darkness>

What: Invasion of Highkeep
When: Saterday, September 2 3pm EST
