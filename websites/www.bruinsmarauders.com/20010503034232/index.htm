 Welcome!  You have entered the world of Bruins Marauders!  We are a fast growing guild based on the Luclin server, and are pleased to have a home on the web.  On this site is the repository of our information that we have come across, our roster, and many events we have set.  Although this site benefits the members of the guild, it will also be a fun site for those not in the guild, or even those who are wishing to join the guild!  Also, we have an Armory section which deals with our guild armory.  In the armory, we keep many things; weapons, armor, jewelry, and many other things.  But, we are always looking for other items to better benefit our guild.  If you wish to view our roster, or information on members, you can visit our Guild Members section.  For more information regarding our guild, you can visit the Guild Information section.  As our guild grows and time passes, this site will become more extensive and informative, so keep checking back!  Thanks for coming by!
