There is an overwhelming number of sites on the 'net that have something to do with Everquest. I've tried to include links to some of the more useful ones here.

General

Allakhazam's Magical Realm
No listing of EQ sites would be complete without this one. A wonderfull all-around Everquest resource.

EQ Atlas
Nice maps of Norrath.

EQ Caster's Realm
Good information on spells and other things useful to the spell-casting classes. Also of interest is their mana calculator.

EQ Prices
They've become a pay site, but they still are a useful resource for finding prices of items since their basic information is still free.

Everlore
Great information about everthing Everquest.

EverQuest
The official Everquest site from the game's makers.

Everquest Stratics
Good all-around resource.

Trade Skills

EQ Traders Corner
Good general resource about trade skills.

Fletching Guide by Aoenla Darkwood
Excellent information about fletching. It's presented all at once as one big page and is too wide to print easliy, but the content is invaluable.

Lokari's Tailoring Guide
An excellent guide to tailoring.

Quip Quarrel's Unofficial Tome of Mastery: Poisons
Fantastic information about making poisons. The link above is actually a link to an EZ-Board discussion. Since these tend to be transitory, I've also included links to his pages:
      Poison Reagents
      Poison Effects
      Poison Recipes
Even these links are too temporary though. They've already moved countless times and I always have a very difficult time finding them again, so I've also zipped up the pages and put them on this server for download. These are his pages as they existed on June 22, 2001 (72 KB): Quip's Poison Guide.

Other

EQ Women
A discussion board for the women of Everquest.

Guild Magic

Luclin.com
A whole site devoted to the guilds and happenings of our server.

The Safehouse
