Luclin's Candlelight Vigil (9/12/2001)
In the wake of the violent attacks on the World Trade Center in New York City and the Pentagon in Washington D.C., the people of Luclin held a wake by candlelight in West Commons. May the victims rest in peace and may their loved ones find peace.
