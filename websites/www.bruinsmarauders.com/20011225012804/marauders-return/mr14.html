Interval Twelve

Bruin holed up in Kelethin and sent Askander to set up shop in Qeynos and had runners going back and forth collecting data spying on enemy camps and doing their best to discover what evil was afoot.

A portal quietly shimmered in front of Bruin.

"Looks like Lord Bayle is finally rallying the troops. Well sort of. What we have is an "official" investigation." Askander said from the other side of the portal. "Local news has it that they are getting ready to round up some suspects. We'll see. Oh, also, Sethatign just got back. Seems the plague is increasing in intensity. Looks like the crops are going to be ruined this year. We could be dealing with a fairly nasty famine this winter. The clerics in the temple of Nife are doing everything they can to try and get some supplies from freeport in here by boat but a couple of the local merchants think they're going to be stopped by the winter storms off the southern tip. Any help you got would be appreciated. This place is total chaos!"

"Slow down, Ask. Take a breath!" Bruin smiled warmly into the energy vortex "I sent you there to help shore up the chaos but it's not all on you. Remember that there are tons of people working toward the same ends. Do what you can but remember the bigger picture. If Bertie makes it to this realm a little famine will be the least of our worries."

"Well I'll do what I can. Talk to you in a week." With that the portal closed.

Rangorn stepped quietly into the room as Bruin pondered the latest news that came to him and shuffled through the stack of papers and maps that laid before him.

"Bruin," Rangorn said sheepishly, "I have news for you."

"Well you look unusually downcast young one" Bruin said "What is so grave to wipe the ever present smile from your face?"

"Well it appears Lanys has the second piece to the staff she's seeking"

"Was your contact able to give you any more information on what the staff was capable of?" Bruin said hopefully.

"No, this staff is shrouded in enigma" Rangorn said looking even more sullen.

"Well" Bruin said standing up "We'll have to do our best to make sure she doesn't get it. C'mon let's go have a pint at the tavern. Looks like we could both use it."

As they shut the door to the office a figure came out of the shadows in the corner of the office. It looked briefly over the notes but took instead a gem that lay on a table in the far corner.
