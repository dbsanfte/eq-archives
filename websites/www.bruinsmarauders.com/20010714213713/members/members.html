When telling me your tradeskills, please list only your tradeskills, not your weapon skills and whatnot. Such skills are indeed something to be proud of, but the tradeskills column is intended to convey information that might be useful to others in need of some craftsmanship. If you want a link to a website listed, please email me that as well. And if you have a picture of your character, please send that to me. (You can either send me the picture itself or a web address hosting your picture.)
