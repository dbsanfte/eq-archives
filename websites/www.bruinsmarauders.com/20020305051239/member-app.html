Our requirements for membership are as follows:

  • If you don't yet have any characters in the guild, you must be at least level twelve.
  • If you already have a character in the guild, any other character must be at least level eight.
  • You must register your character at Guild Magic.

Use the form below to apply for membership in our guild. Once you fill out the form, press the "Submit Application" button to send it to us. Processing may take a few days and one of our officers might want to ask you some questions, either by email or by tells in-game. And, of course, if you have any questions, feel free to send one of our officers a tell in-game. Once we make a decision, we'll send you an email or, if we see you in Norrath, we might just meet with you and handle it all right there.
