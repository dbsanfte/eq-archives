Tower of Frozen Shadow Raid (7/15/2001)

Thousand Suns invited us along for a raid on the eerie Tower of Frozen Shadows in Velious. This raid had a great turn out and we pretty much demolished the residents of the Tower. They will have nightmares about us for decades.



















Runneye Raid (7/20/2001)

Rumors that the Runnyeye goblins had been ousted by the Pickclaw goblins piqued our curiosity; though our gathering would be more appropriately called an invasion than a scouting mission. Only a couple images were captured during this raid.
