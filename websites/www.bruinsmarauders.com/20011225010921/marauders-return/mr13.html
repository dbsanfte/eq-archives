Interval Eleven

Balamorm and Aloynia were resting and recouping by the lakeshore in the commons. The fight with some particularly nasty undead in befallen had left them both battleworn. So they were particularly enjoying the peace and quiet of the afternoon.

"Hail and well met, fellow marauders!", came a cry from behind them.

Balamorm turned to see Darrasa, one of the smaller members of their motley band approaching.

"M'lady." Balamorm said bowing low. "How is one of the finer halflings in all of norrath doing today?"

Darrasa blushed. "Balamorm you old flirt! I'm glad I found you. A trader over by the tunnel said I might find you two here. I need your help."

"We'd love to help." Aloynia said "What're you after?"

"Well Rangorn, Gaikutsu, and I were over hunting in the feerott when we ran into a nasty lizard hunting party" Darrasa began. "Rangorn and I slayed a bunch of them but I'm afraid that there were too many. I was knocked unconcious in the fray and fell back into some bushes. When I came to there were no bodies to be found and both Rangorn and Gaikutsu were gone. I think the lizards captured them."

Balamorm lept to his feet exclaiming, "Well let's go get them!"

Aloynia looked at her husband with a cloy smile "Dear, if the three of them couldn't fend off a roving band of lizards, what makes you think the three of us will be able to penetrate the temple?"

Balamorm stopped for a second then lowered his sword dejectedly. "You're right. But what do we do? The marauders are spread out all over the lands right now gathering information."

"I heard somebody mention in the pub the other day that some mercenaries I know of were in the area lately. Why don't we go see if we can enlist some aid." Aloynia said getting up.

"And just how do you know a group of mercenaries?" Balamorm said arching an eyebrow.

"Well dear, I haven't spent my entire life with you now have I?" Aloynia smiled as the walked on down the road ...
