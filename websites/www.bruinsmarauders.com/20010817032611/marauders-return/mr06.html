Interval Four

The goblin pulled back as Balamorm took, what he was sure to be, his last breath. Suddenly an explosion at the rim of the pit rained down dirt on him. When Bal opened his eyes again and looked up the goblins were nowhere to be seen. A voice spoke at him from above.

"You're not a lion."

A hand reached down and pulled him out of the pit by the collar of his breastplate. Balamorm squinted in the mid afternoon sun as a rather large troll came into focus.

"Right. This is about on par with my luck lately."

The troll looked at him questioningly.

"Look," Balamorm said "if you're going to kill me can you do it quickly? I'm kinda in alot of pain here and don't really have the patience to deal with this anymore."

"Why would I kill you?" the troll opened his mouth to say something else then seemed to think better of it. They stared at each other for a few seconds before the troll said matter of factly "I think we got off on the wrong foot. My name is Kuraz, and I will be your rescuer today."

"Uh huh", Bal said not believing this was happening to him "You know you're not like most of the trolls I've ever met. Of course most of the trolls I've met want to tie me down on a spit and roast me alive."

"That's disgusting. Look I'm not like those trolls cause I'm not those trolls. I was actually raised by some really nice farmers in the plains here. After I got a little to big for them to handle they turned me loose on the world. I've found that hunting around here is kinda fun and spend my days wandering the plains helping out where I can and generally living off the lands."

"Fair enough I suppose." Bal said. "Look you mind helping me up to the keep in the pass? I'm a little banged up."

"No can do. The goblins have the pass all up in arms and the guards aren't too fond of me. But you can stay at my place, I'll bandage you up and you'll be feeling better in no time."

"So coincidentally what was that explosion I heard earlier?" Bal said as he and Kuraz slowly wended their way down the path.

"Gnomish fireworks"

"I've never seen fireworks that worked quite like that." Bal said skeptically

"Well this gnome I ran into said they were a little strong and prone to go off at odd times. I use them mostly for fishing but they come in handy at other times too." Kuraz said grinning from ear to ear as the two wound their way down the path and out of sight.
