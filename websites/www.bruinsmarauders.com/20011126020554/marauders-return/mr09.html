Interval Seven

Askander had spent the week healing and learning. His first day up and around he noticed that, for a temple, this place was very busy. People coming and going, not one person standing still. Askander pushed Justineous for information but the cleric waved him off with a "Later, you need to rest now". Over the next few days Justineous told Askander how the temple in Freeport had been following the movements of notable figures around the globe.

"There is a war coming, friend." Justineous said while they were walking the balconies one afternoon "There will be no neutrality in this fight, for the gods of evil come to claim that which they feel is their birthright. We've tried to rally others including those of your hometown. But the followers of Rodcet are busy tending those that have taken ill from the plague and doing what they can to help bolster the dwindling food supply."

"Surely Rodcet would do something to stop this? I mean truly it is noble to help those in need but if Bertoxxulous means to walk the lands Norrath, surely Rodcet would move to help stop him."

"Perhaps he has. Askander we could really use your help. The Dark elves are mobilizing in the WC. We can't figure out why. Perhaps some of your connections might be of service?"

"I don't know anyone who might...", Askander thought for a second, "I remember Bruin once told me of an enchanter he knew who...hmmm I might be able to help. But I've got to find a friend of mine and have no idea where to find him."

"Actually we might be able to help you with that. Last we heard Bruin and some of the other Marauders were over trying to find a way to stem the tide of undead pixie swarms in Lesser Faydark. We can get somebody to teleport you over there. But hurry we don't know what the children of Innoruuk are up to but I can't imagine it being of benefit to us. Come on we'll get your gear packed up and get you on your way."
