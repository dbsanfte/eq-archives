Bind Sight
Mana Required: 15
Skill: Divination
Increase Stamina Loss by 2 (L8)
Range to Target: 10000 feet
Allowable Targets: All
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 10.0 seconds
Description: Cast this on any animate object, be it a friend, a monster, an NPC vendor... anything. The will not have any inclination that it is casted upon them and whatever they see.... YOU now see.
Uses for this spell include:
Cast on your tank as he pulls from within the dungeons. You can explore the dungeon with his eyes as well as reporting on what is being pulled back.
Cast on a newbie and give him pointers as he fights in a zone.
Cast on a mob in a new zone and recast onto a mob further out (note you cant get too far for it has its distance limit) to get a feel for what the zone is like.

Identify
Mana Required: 50
Skill: Divination
Allowable Targets: All
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Range to Target: 100 feet
Description: Use this spell to find out more info on certain items. Casting this on books will inform you what language is needed to read. Casting on items such as the Spokes of Tartan's Wheel from the Ice Comet Rune Quest will inform you of the rune's name. To use it, hold the item to be identified with your cursor, target yourself, then cast Identify. You can also cast this on other players if they have an item in their hand they want identified. Simply have them hold the item on their cursor, target them, then cast Identify. They will get the message in their chat screen area.

Invisibility
Mana Required: 30
Skill: Divination
Range to Target: 100 feet
Allowable Targets: All
Spell Duration: 200 ticks (random time. longest being 20.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Description: Will make you or anybody you cast it upon within your group invisible for a random length of time. Be careful with it, although it will give you warning as to when it is about to fail, the time between the warning and when it does fail is actually quite short (approx 5 seconds). Undead see right through it also. When a party member is looting his or her corpse you can cast upon them and they will be invisible until the spell wears off or when they get up from looting.
