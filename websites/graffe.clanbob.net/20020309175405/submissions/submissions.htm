If you wish to send in information on an item, this is what I ask:
1. Open your inventory or backpack and find the icon of the item.
2. Right click and hold on the item icon to show the stats. While still holding down on the right mouse button, dragl your pointer away from the icon (so when you do the next step, your cursor doesn't shroud the icon).
3. Press the minus (-) key on your keypad to take a screenshot. (This is saved in your Everquest root directory as a bitmap file. Example: C:\games\everquest\EQ000001.bmp)
4. You can e-mail the full, unedited bitmap (preferred) if you wish or you can convert it to a JPEG if you have the knowledge. I only ask that if you convert it, please leave the resolution at 100% and do not edit the picture.
5. Along with the picture, send any information on what MOB, NPC, Quest or any other vital information to what enabled you to get the item.
6. If you wish to recieve credit for the information, please include your characters name and server you play on and I will be more than happy to give you the credit you deserve.
7. EMAIL it to me! =)
