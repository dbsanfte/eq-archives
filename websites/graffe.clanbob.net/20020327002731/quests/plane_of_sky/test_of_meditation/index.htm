This quest is given by Neasin Leornic within the Plane of Sky. This quest will reward you with the Raiment of Thunder.

You say, 'Meditation'

Neasin Leornic says 'A wizard is nothing without meditation. Meditation allows us to focus and concentrate when casting our spells of power. Fly to those above and return to me a Hyaline Globe, a Sky Topaz, and a High Quality Raiment. If you are successful, I will reward you with the Raiment of Thunder.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Items Needed:

  • Hyaline Globe: Found upon random mobs upon the third island.
  • Sky Topaz: Found on Soul Carriers and Essence Harvesters on the fourth island.
  • High Quality Raiment: Rare drop off of the Spiroc lord.
