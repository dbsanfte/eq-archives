Emerging Changes in Last Patch:
I've noticed these changes since the last patch, I'm sure there are more.

  • You no longer lose mana when casting a spell and get the "You cannot see your target." message.
  • The bind sight line of spells now requires line of sight in order for it to be cast on a target.
  • Dagger of Frost is now NO DROP and not MAGIC.
I don't understand the reason behind the dagger change, but I believe the bind sight problem was an unintentional side effect of the *fix* for losing mana when casting on targets that are not in sight.

Let's hope Verant fixes our bind sight spells in the next patch.

Patch Day:
Today's patch has a very large amount of changes and enhancements to the game. Not much happened in the way of wizards, but here is the things that directly effect us -

  • If you are interrupted while trying to cast a spell that uses a component, you will no longer use that component. Jewelers should be very pleased with this change.
  • Reduced the casting difficulty (fizzle rate) of all spells over level 56. Spells have always previously been set up so that it's usually more efficient to use a spell from a previous circle than the current one. The formula did not take into account the fact that everyone stops at 60.

More Portal Purchasing Info:
STILL don't have your Velious portals? Fret not, there are still many ways to get it and even the bard will work, faction permitting. Aside from having a friend purchase them or by spending eons killing giants to raise your faction high enough, there are other ways to buy them from the Skyshrine Vendor (I feel like I'm beating a dead horse), here's the added info -

  1. Bards - This will work. So long as the bard has never hurt his faction, they can sing their song and 'sneak' behind the vendor to purchase.
  2. Rogues - They can sneak to purchase as well.
  3. Monks - Feign Death behind the vendor and purchase from it.
So from these examples and the others listed in the past news.. I'm pretty confident you guys can figure out how to get your spells. Good luck!

Thanks to everyone that sent in the sneak/fd info!
