You say, 'Hail Hasten Bootstrutter'
Hasten Bootstrutter says 'Stopped I have done. Greet you I will. What business do we have? Hmm? Speak up!!

You say, 'What journeyman's boots'
Hasten Bootstrutter says 'Jorneyman's Boots are what you seek? Ha!! I have those upon my feet and disinigrate will they if my life is taken. Extra pair have I and [rare trade] can we make.

You say, 'What rare trade'
Hasten Bootstrutter says 'I call for three things and Journeyman Boots are yours. I call for a [Shadowed Rapier], call for the [Ring of the Ancients] and call for Gold,.. 3250 Gold Coins!! This the trade that I call.

You say, 'What shadowed rapier'
Hasten Bootstrutter says 'Shadowed Men, foes to all, have appeared. Many lands do they walk. Invisible are they, but items they wield can be seen. Seek them out and return to me a Shadowed Rapier. Return it with haste before 'poof' goes the rapier!! No time to camp have you.

You say, 'What ring of the ancients'
Hasten Bootstrutter says 'Ancient Cyclops do march the land. Long have they lived and crafted the Ring of the Ancients have they. I must have one and you shall go and fetch one for me. Seek the lands of the oldest Cyclops clans. Seek the plains, seek the island in tears and seek the dunes for there is one who is last. His clan was blown from the sands.


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


 Ancient Cyclops - In South Ro and the Ocean of Tears. Green to Level 50- Hits for 110. Ring is AC 6 Int +5 No Drop and goes in wrist slot.

  • If you choose to get him in Ocean of Tears, you can sit in one spot and just kill the placeholders until he spawns. Keep in mind he is approximately 10 levels higher than than the one in South Ro and the camp is horribly boring.
  • If you choose to get him in South Ro, you must keep the complete sand area clean of 'yard trash'. This includes Tarantulas, Mummies, Sand Giants, Zombies, anything that inhabits the sand. You will begin to notice a cycle of spawns after all is clear. Keep that cycle going by killing them off quickly. Soon, the Ancient Cyclops will spawn. I have had him spawn twice in two hours this way. While doing this you will also get Terrorantula and Hassam to spawn as well. Get as many people as you can to join you because he spawns in random spots. (each time I have seen him, he has spawned near the Dervish camp near the west wall)

Shadowed Rapier - These can be found off of Shadowmen scattered all through Norrath. Most hunt for the rapier at the camps in Innothule swamp due to it's close location.

Hasten Bootstrutter (picture): A gnome that wanders Rathe Mountains. Most find him near the Feerrott Zone side. Note that Hasten Bootstrutter wants GOLD not platinum. His spawn point is in the Hill Giant/Cyclops area of the zone close to the guard tower in the Southwest part of Rathe Mountains. If he is not there, look for one of his placeholders and kill it:

  1. The most common place holder is called "A DRAKE" not to be confused with the other Drakes there, this is the ONLY one called "A DRAKE" Simply kill him and wait 8 minutes for another spawn
  2. Second most common Pop is Maldyn the unkempt druid. He is level 34ish and drops a Ranger Quest item. Again kill him and wait for another pop, one should be kind enough to offer the bow parts "a" that he drops to some ranger for their glove quest of course :) Note that killing Maldyn effects no factions at all.
  3. 3rd most common is Hasten, Thank goodness
  4. The most rare spawn is Brother Zephyl for the Robe of the lost circle (Monk Quest). You can either wait for him to despawn (unknown time) or attempt to kill him. If you attempt to kill him, note that he double hits VERY hard (rumors say somewhere in the 300 range) and is red to a level 54. I suggest waiting for him to despawn *smile*.
