Joining The Asylum is not as simple as most guilds. We are a strict guild and ask that you read guild info area very carefully. If there is anything within our laws that you do not like, then The Dark Asylum is not for you. We ask that when you fill out your application in the EMAIL section, you roleplay your character within the application. This will help us to recognize your abilities of roleplay and also give us an idea if your character is one to be in the ranks of The Dark Asylum.
