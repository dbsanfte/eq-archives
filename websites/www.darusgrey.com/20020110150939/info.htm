About:

The Imperial Order is a Everquest guild, based on the Ayonae-Ro server.

We are a group of friends and friends of friends banded together in the goal of exploring norrath's highend gameplay.

We are an EST based guild with a fair mix of Mid-afternoon and early evening players.

 

Requirements to join :

Must be level 46+ upon joining, and show an active effort to level to 58+

Must have or have the resources to quickly acquire appropriate resist gear, ALL members should Possess the following items or an equivalent:
Blue Diamond Earrings, Bracers, Necklace, Diamond 8all rings, Crown of the Froglok Kings.

Must be willing to work as a TEAM, and for the benefit of the guild, we are a TEAM oriented guild, if you can't play for the team, then you don't belong here.

As per the above, must be willing to follow orders as stated, be alert and be competent at all times during a raid.

Must have all appropriate keys, seb key, colbalt scars tooth, etc.

Must possess any other appropriate gear (eg: shrink potions for an ogre)

 

Structure:

There are 4 Levels of membership:

Recruit - You have applied to the guild and have been tagged for recruitment purposes during recruitment you will receieve no loot on raids unless it will rott.

Member- You have attended no less then 5 raids and have shown qualities to be admired in a member. You now have oppurtunity to obtain loot from raids.

Full Member- You have attended no less then 15 raids, have shown qualities to be admired, have went out of your way to help fellow members, and have been voted in (to be voted in..you must be voted in by EVERY other full member of guild), full members have access to guildbank .

Officer - IO is run by a council of officers, these officers make all important decisions regarding the guild, including schedule, loot, etc.

All decisions are made on one simple principle What's best for the guild , loot is assigned to where its use will most benefit us all.

Loot:

You will  earn a point alotment based on attendence.
Scheduled raids - 10pts
Non-schduled raids-5pts
Epic-Raids/Helping-2pts

There will be a minimum attendence time..which i  keep secret to avoid abuse.

Loot rolls will all be on a 100-999 scale. your point total will be added to this roll. when you win an item your total will go down 10pts for most items, 35 for larger items (bps, legs, dragon loot)...there will be 2 numbers then...your total points and your actual points....total points will be used as a gauge for officers to decide on certain items that *should* be awarded as opposed to randomed.

Regular Members will be restricted to Winning 2 peices in case of armor raids, and 1 item per non-armor raid. Per raid.

This allows us to have a more efficent randomized senority system, people who dedicate thier time will still get equiped faster, well allowing ALL members a chance.

Some things will not be changed. Certain "Uber" items will be designated as "award". These items will be discussed by officers, possably in advance as to who should loot.
