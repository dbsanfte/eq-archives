Order of the Black Rose - Shadow Bane Guild Charter

 

Who are we?

 

 

Requirements for Joining:

Be a rogue primary: Thief, Scout, Bard, or Assassin 

Be able to keep your yap shut.

Why would you want to join us?

 

Training:

A poor skilled rogue is a dead rogue.

We will teach you tactics to make the most of your skills

Companionship:

Strength in numbers.

We keep a complete list of guild members and specialties.

Information:

Knowledge is power, and power is riches!

The Order, like information is out there if you know where to look, and who to sell it too.

Contracts:

The Order  is a mercenary force. Our alliances will be with those who can afford our services, for as long as they can. 

What do we expect?

Team Work! 

Each class has its own code or rules with respect to The Order.

In order for the Order to not only survive but thrive this is our greatest expectation, know your place.

You are expected to aid other guild members in time of need. 

You are expected to teach the lesser rogues of the Order. 

You are expected to find other rogues. Show them to this place and let them see if the Order is for them.

Laws of the Order....

We have only 1 law, NO member of the Order will take the life of another member nor directly cause another member to be harmed.

Ask yourself, do you wish to betray and be hunted by a house of assassins, thieves and spies?

 

Goal of the Order

 

The goal of the Order is simple, to run all illicit trade: smuggling, petty theft, gambling, assassination, etc. 

Gain the fealty of all rogues around the realm. 

To create small, disposable, hidden, "Hole in the wall" villages where people who are unwelcome elsewhere are welcome.

In other words to create a world wide organized crime organization to be respected even by kings.
