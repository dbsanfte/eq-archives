Order of the Black Rose - Vallon Zek Guild Charter

So you want to join the order….  Think you have the stomach for it? Anyone can be a petty thief or a thug for hire, but only a few can be a rich thief and fewer have the stomach and talent to ever become a master assassin. So you know how to sneak around maybe even hide in the shadows of an alley, but can you take what you want without notice? Can you kill before the victim sees you or anyone else for that matter? Sloppy rogues are short-lived ones, and what is worse is they can get other rogues killed with their carelessness. Are you prepared to learn from the masters and teach your skills to those who are less experienced than you? It is not as easy as it sounds. Are you prepared to join up in arms with your fellow brethren of the shadows to make the big score? Do you have the honor required among professional rogues? If you think you do, then feel free to pull up a pillow and listen, learn and someday teach. However, know that should we find out that you don’t, well its better not to think of that.

 

Requirements for Joining:

Quite simply all we require in order for you to join our order is that you be a Rogue willing to assist other Rogues, regardless of race.

Why would you want to join us?

Training:

A poor skilled rogue is a dead rogue.

We will teach you tactics to make the most of your skills

Companionship:

Strength in numbers.

We keep a complete list of guild members and levels

Information:

Knowledge is power, and power is riches!

Find out where to hunt for experience, wealth and quests

Find out when a war band is about to come so you won't be there, and if you are your ready for them.

Smuggling:

We as act as a smuggling ring, moving hard to get things across the racial boundaries, and we make good money at it.

What do we expect?

 

As a member of the Order, you are expected to share information about places to hunt, loot and quests.

As a member of the Order you will not kill nor cause to be killed  another member, duels being the exception.

You are expected to teach the younger rogues of the Order.

You are expected to find other rogues. Show them to this place and let them see if the Order is for them.

Lastly, you are expected to aid the Order in becoming a force to be feared and respected across Norrath.

Vallon Zek Loot N Scoot Policy (Note this does not apply to Rallos Zek)

Due to the racial PvP nature of the server many guilds have adopted the "Loot and Scoot"   (LnS) this guild included.

Simply put if you die, you are required to loot your body and remove yourself from the "zone" for one hour.

This is to prevent a underhanded and illegal practice (per Verant) called bind rushing. 

If you lose exp while being PK'ed, you will still LnS. Except you may leave one item on the corpse to get an exp. rez. if you so decide.

The current Guild Master has dictated the following rules  in reference to whither or not your guild tag should be on or off.

PK Members: If you are actively seeking the death of another person and you have not been hired to do so--tag off.
If it's revenge--tag off.
This includes rushing to someone's aid.

If you're attacked with your tag on--tag on.
If you are killed and come back--tag off.

Hired Assassination--tag on.
Guild Raids--tag on.

Officers: You have the authority to warn and remove anyone not adhering to these rules.

It is your duty to explain these on Guild Chat while online as well--there will be no excuse for noncompliance.
