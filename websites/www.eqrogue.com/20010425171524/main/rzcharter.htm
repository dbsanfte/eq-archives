Order of the Black Rose - Rallos Zek Guild Charter

So you want to join the order….  Think you have the stomach for it? Anyone can be a petty thief or a thug for hire, but only a few can be a rich thief and fewer have the stomach and talent to ever become a master assassin. So you know how to sneak around maybe even hide in the shadows of an alley, but can you take what you want without notice? Can you kill before the victim sees you or anyone else for that matter? Sloppy rogues are short-lived ones, and what is worse is they can get other rogues killed with their carelessness. Are you prepared to learn from the masters and teach your skills to those who are less experienced than you? It is not as easy as it sounds. Are you prepared to join up in arms with your fellow brethren of the shadows to make the big score? Do you have the honor required among professional rogues? If you think you do, then feel free to pull up a pillow and listen, learn and someday teach. However, know that should we find out that you don’t, well its better not to think of that.

 Who are we?

We are a band of rogues who teach and train younger rogues to live and thrive to be older rogues. If you are looking for a power leveling guild or even a grouping guild you are looking in the wrong place. We are a group of rogues who place the rogue class above all else, seeing to it that questions about the class, skills, tactics, etc. are answered in as efficient a matter as possible. If you are looking to learn how to be a rogue and to be in the company of other rogues then please read on, otherwise...move on.

 

Requirements for Joining:

Quite simply all we require in order for you to join our order is that you be a Rogue willing to assist other Rogues, regardless of race.

Why would you want to join us?

Training:

A poor skilled rogue is a dead rogue.

We will teach you tactics to make the most of your skills

Companionship:

Strength in numbers.

We keep a complete list of guild members and levels

Information:

Knowledge is power, and power is riches!

Find out where to hunt for experience, wealth and quests

What do we expect?

As a member of the Order, you are expected to share information about places to hunt, loot and quests. 

You are expected to aid other guild members in time of need. 

You are expected to teach the lesser rogues of the Order. 

You are expected to find other rogues. Show them to this place and let them see if the Order is for them.

Laws of the Order....

We are not here to dictate your playing style be it PK, Anti PK, or otherwise.

Therefore we only have 1 law, NO member of the Order will take the life of another member.
