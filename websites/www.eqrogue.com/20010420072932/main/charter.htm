          Welcome to the guild website          
for the Order of the Black Rose, a 100% PvP Rogues guild.

We are not your average guild, we do not exist solely to group with each other as a complete group of rogues is generally a bad idea. We exist to aid other rogues, not only to teach them to survive in the PvP but to thrive in it. We hold gathering of rogues from around the world so that Rogues may meet each other. We are ever working to form a Rogue society which exists under the one everyone sees.

Please feel free to look at our charters and understand who we are.
