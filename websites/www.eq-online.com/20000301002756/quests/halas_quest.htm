Ice Goblin Quest
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
1 Lysbith I wish to serve Halas, What of the goblins?
Item gained? Details of the quest:
Yes, Bag This NPC asks you to bring her 6 ice goblin necklaces, once you have six you put them in the pack which has a combine button in it. hit combine and you create an item called "Pack of ice necklaces". Experience, small amount of money or rations/water/torch, lead on another quest.

Freezing Young Warriors
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
3-6 (estimated) Dargon I will deliver elixir to the freezing young warriors
Item gained? Details of the quest:
Yes, potion to reduce frostbite. This NPC asks you to take a potion out to the freezing young warriors in the Everfrost Peaks. He gives you a list of the people you must deliver the potion to. Each person you find and deliver it to you get experience for. And they also give you a clue as to where the next closest person on the list is. The last person you have to find in the quest is Megan O'reilly. Her wolf (Snowflake) will take you to her, this is a cool part of the quest. When you get to snowflake and say "Where is Megan?" Snowflake barks and starts to take off, follow him! And he will partially protect you from the beasts that you encounter along the way. He is a pretty bad dog, I watched him slay a Wooly mammoth calf all by himself. So he will protect you just stick close to him. When you find Megan and give her the potion she drains the last out of it and its time to head back to Halas, I suggest gating because Snowflake will have probably headed back by now.
Reward for completing this quest?
Exp, a little bit of money, and either a Rsuty weapon/shield/gem/lantern.

Fungus Collector
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
2-5 (estimated) Waltor young shaman, I will help collect fungus
Item gained? Details of the quest:
Yes, jar to put fungus in and combine when jar is full. Waltor wants you to go out and collect the fungus of the wooly spiderlings. You have to fill 8 slots in the jar before it is full and then return it to him. This quest is not really worth the effort you have to put into it. You get a few copper peices and less experience then you got slaying all the spiders to get the fungus.
Reward for completing this quest?
Exp, a little bit of money, not really worth the effort you have to put into it.
