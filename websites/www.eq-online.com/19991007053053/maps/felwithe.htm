Felwithe City of the High Elves

Felwithe is the City of the High elves, standing majestically like a beacon to the weary in the Greater Feydark.

Felwithe
Felwithe is the home of the High elves, and one of the only places in the Greater feydark you will find to practice fishing. Felwithe also has a Temple to Tunare, and some nice shops
