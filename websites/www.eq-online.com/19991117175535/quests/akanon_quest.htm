Ak'anon Quests

Gearheart
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
1 Baxok Curhunter I wish to serve the Crown
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No Baxok tells you of a clockwork that was stolen by fairies. The clockworks name is Gearhart and he is in a fairie city in Lesser Feydark. Gearhart is surrounded/protected by the fairies, and will be very hard to kill if he is in the city. If he leaves though he is an easy prey. Do not confuse him with Cognoggin who is a clockwork near the steamfont zone entrance.
Reward for completing this quest?
Experience, small amount of money, and I got one leather bracer out of it, lame if you ask me.

Series C black boxes
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
1 Manik Compolten, Warriors guild I am a young warrior
Item gained? Details of the quest:
Yes, Keycard Manik asks you to take the keycard to the Series C clockworks around ak'anon. These are located in and around Ak'anon you will recognize them as the Clockworks whose name ends with a C. If you give it to the wrong one it says something about a "unauthorized attempt to disassemble" and attack you. There are 4 Series C clockworks in Ak'anon, go to all 4, once you give the cards to them, they give you a blackbox, take all 4 back to Manik.
Reward for completing this quest?
Some money and faction (Gem Choppers, Eldritch collective, Merchants of Ak'anon gets better, Dark Reflection gets worse)

Toko's Spellbook
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
6-8 (estimated) Tergon Brenclog Can get this after compelting the above one
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No Tergon, after completing the quest for his spellbook will give you a quest for Toko's spellbook. Toko is a renegade gnome magician hanging out with the pirates of Gunthak. They are located on an island near the Sisterhood of Erollisi. The island that Gornit stands near. If you can make it to Toko, he is not hard to defeat, he usually has a pet.
Reward for completing this quest?
Exp, cash, and an Elemantal Grimoire.
