Tip for Player Killers

If you're near a guard and another PK challenges you, don't fight back. Let the other guy take the first swing or cast the first spell, and the guard will take him/her out for provocation.

Also, if you're getting massacred by another PK and know you're going to die, if you are within running distance of an NPC, commit honorable suicide by attacking the NPC, that way an NPC kills you and your loot can't be taken by the PK, since the PK didn't actually finish you off.

Tip for Wizards

After you reach level 8, you will find that you cannot survive at melee with nearly anything that will give you experience. As you know, for a wizard, parting is essential. During a fight with your party, do not attempt to melee at all. When you are struck by a NPC it will interrupt your cast, and because you cannot use your melee weapon very effectively, you probably won't hit it or do much damage that way. It is much more effective to stand back altogether and cast for 35 damage with Shock of Ice and hit without interruption. When you use all of your manna, do not engage in melee. Instead, step back and meditate so that you can get one more good cast in before the fight is over. However, tell your party about your combat style before you attack so that they will know that they have to take the hits in advance.

Neriak Text walk through

To get to the necromancy and shadow knight trainers: Turn right at the wall of green skulls. Go to the T intersection and turn left down the tunnel. This leads to Neriak Commons.

You will be in a room with a waterfall on the right. Go right and down the ramp into the doorway at the bottom. The next room has two symbols of a face with tongue hanging out (will be referred to as 'tongue' from this point on) flanking a sun symbol. Go through the wooden gate under the sun.

Go straight through up the ramp, under the star and down the ramp then go through the wooden gate under the sun symbol. You enter a room with a tongue symbol under a sun symbol. Go through the wooden gate that is to the left as you face the symbols. Turn left at Adamant Armor, you'll pass BF (Blue Flame) on the right and Neriak Down Under on the left. The bank is in Neriak Down Under. The entrance to 3rd Gate is opposite the statue of a man in a fountain. The entrance is flanked by 2 suns on each side. Go through open doorway with wooden doors on each side.

3rd Gate You pass right through another tongue symbol to enter a cavern with a crystal building decorated with 5 tongues (I believe this may be the magician's guild). Go right, not entering the building, following the ledge above a moat. When you are at the back of the crystal building, turn your back to it and go in the doorway facing the back of the building. You'll be in a room with a waterfall with a red eye symbol hanging in the middle. Go in the opening to the right. Pass through the curving hallway ending in a ramp. At the bottom of the ramp will be a room with a sun in the middle with a wooden gate. There are tongues on each side and open fires. Go through the wooden gate. The next room has sun, tongue and hand symbols over doorways. Go under the sun symbol. The next room has stairs to a rabbit eared symbol. Go around the stairs to the right and througha doorway with an eye symbol over it. When you come out of the doorway you will have a tongue symbol on left, a bridge and swimming figure on right. Facing the tongue will be a doorway with stones that look like teeth in an open mouth and ghouls. Go through into a tunnel. When you emerge you'll see a red pagoda like building. Go down the ramp and straight in. In the center on the main floor are the necromancer and ShadowKnight trainers. Necro spells are on the main floor to your left as you enter.

Word of advice starting a Wizard

Here is a good trick I learned after a few tragic newbie attempts. Mainly works for magic using classes like wizards. I will tell you what I did and you can incorporate it how you will.

I created a gnome wizard and started the game inscribed and memorized my first two spells. Then found me a nice spot to stand around and hot keyed both my spells. I just repeatedly cast them on myself over and over, I also used the sense heading skill. And to my surprise my spell levels (abjure and evoke) and channeling continued to rise (currently lvl 2 wizard with 18 abjure 10 evoke and 17 channeling).

Getting this stuff (especially channeling) can really help you out for countless levels.

Dragon Gnomish Wizard

Corpse Command ( /corpse )

If you ever die should die in a very bad spot...say in a pool of man-eating sharks or pirahana infested waters for example and you need to recover items from your corpse and not re-enact your original death scene over and over as in some sort of grim, dark comedy, use the /corpse command. This will summon your corpse to you. Of course you must be close by for this work. But work it does. Believe me it can save you a lot of grief and frustation.

Tips for low level Warriors

Tired of waiting forever early on(up to level 5) for your precious health to regen*? Why bother? Go into combat and die. Take on that extra mob you don't think you can take. Only a few things can happen.

1. You kill the mob. Yah!!

2. You die. No worries, you don't lose any exp and save a short trip to your corpse you haven't wasted much time. You could always find the coveted respawn point and head for it once your on your suicide run(makes the trek even easier). You get new food, waste no real time healing and wala your back in the mix killing things and gaining exp with a full bar of health.

*This tip of course doesn't apply to trolls who have the natural ability to heal much quicker.

Tiny daggers

Tiny daggers use the Archery skill, equip one and you can improve your archery skill very well. you will only do 1 point of damage so fight something you know you can kill, but your archery will go up quicker.
