3-19-02 Patch Message

-I have added a new forum called Ye Olde Coffeeshop, this forum is to be used for general chatter about your characters. Please do not confuse this with the General Discussion forum which is used for discussion about our server and issues resolving from that, or the OOC/OT forum which is used for, well basically out of character and other topics (RL/Internet Life) anything that doesn't concern our server. The Hero Hart Tavern is for relaxing topics, stories, adventures, gossiping, fun, games etc. The Bard for a Day is where you can post your stories, poetry, art work, background history (in detail); basically a showcase for creativity.
