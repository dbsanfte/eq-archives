DSL Issues

 

The most common issue with DSL is the software that used with it (i.e. the software you use to connect to it).  Of these softwares the most problem-causing one is PPOE (Point-to-Point Protocol Over Ethernet).  It is known to harass other programs that access the Internet (such as EQ).

Here's the kicker - you don't need ANY of the software provided to you by your DSL company.

Follow the MSCONFIG tool to keep PPOE or any other softwares (like connection monitors and such) from starting up at all.  If you don't have Windows 98 then please call your DSL provider and ask them to help you uninstall their software fully and set up a standard TCP/IP connection to their service.
