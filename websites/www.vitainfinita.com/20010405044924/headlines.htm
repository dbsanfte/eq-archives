I had mentioned the work of a man named Shadoe in our forum a while back and I promised to try to make contact with him.  Well, I have done so and he is still the kind-natured and giving soul that he always has been.  I was simply elated to find him again.  It turns out that EQX didn't really shut down but rather morphed into Otherworld Express (OWX).  The incredible story that he is writing is called "The Pact of Zeranon" and is currently in its 17th of 28 chapters.  If you are a fan of medieval fantasy literature you simply have to read this story.  The link above is to the archives where the first 12 chapters are and you can find 13 - 17 in the current issues of OWX.  Once all the chapters have been written and published Shadoe may allow me to host the complete e-book on our site.  Wouldn't that be wonderful?  /cheer Shadoe

News - March 30, 2001:

Many people (including myself) have been experiencing video issues in EQ since the last patch.  While Verant says they're working on it they have not released any information about the actual problem.  I've done some homework and it appears that these issues only exist for people that use a video card that has 2 video chips that run in tandem.  Some of the cards known to be affected are all SIS, the GeForce Ultra (but not the Ultra 2 because the chips are not tandem), the TNT Explode series and the Voodoo 5.  I think I have found the root of the issue and it deals with how the new version of eqgame.exe is attempting to access your system to work with the tandem chips.  I sent the information off to Verant (along with my DX and Event logs) and you can view it here.
