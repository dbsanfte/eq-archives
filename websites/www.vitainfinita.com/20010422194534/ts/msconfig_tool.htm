Microsoft Configuration Tool

* This tool is available only in Windows 98.

Goto Start | Run |, type msconfig and press the Enter key or click on "Ok".

 

General Tab

  • Put a dot in the 3rd circle from the top - "Selective Startup"
  • Uncheck "Process Config.sys file"
  • Uncheck "Process Autoexec.bat file"
  • Uncheck "Process Win.ini file"

{To correct shutdown lockups click on the "Advanced" button and put a check in "Disable Fast Shutdown".  Also put a check in "Disable Scandisk after bad shutdown".}

Startup Tab

  • Uncheck anything EXCEPT "Scanreg", "Winmodem", one "LoadPowerProfile" and "em_exec" (or "TIPS").
  • Unchecking the following entries does not in any way disable the program.  They may still be accessed through the Programs menu and/or desktop icons.

Ownership (All unnecessary programs)

McAfee: MWebscanX, VShwin32, Vstat , Vcom                 

Iomega:  Iomega Startup Options, Iomega Watch, Zipdisk Icons

Norton:   NProtect, NUtil

AOL:       AOL startup tray

Devices: Vortex tray, Voyetra tray, V128, AVConsole, Pointers, Changelines, anything HP, Wintouch, Changelines, Pagekeeper Jobs

Add-ons: Disknag, Encompass/BVRP, Tswinrp32 (may show differently), TSystray, MS Greeting Reminders, Office startup, FindFast, BillMinder (Quicken), MS Calendar Reminders, Reminder (MS Money)

Any other entry not listed here is not part of the factory loaded software.  Watch for and correct double entries of LoadPowerProfile, MWebscanX and Vshwin32.

 

When you have finished editing click the "Apply" button, then click the "Ok" button, then click "Yes" to reboot.  You must reboot for the changes to take effect.
