Monday 24th May, 2004

New procedure for joining FoJ at 14:57 GMT by flamewalker
To become a member, please do the following:

1: Read our Guild Documents using the link found at the top of the page.
2: Register on the forums.
3: Write down some names of officers listed on our Member Roster.

There are 2 steps to becomming a member:

1: An officer will invite your character with the requirement of using the 'Anonymous' tag. If you break anon before probation is over, you will get a warning. After a second time your character will be removed and have to get re-sponsored if you still want in. Now, a sponsor could be anyone in the guild that has (preferably) grouped with the said character, not just an officer.

2: Depending on the amount of time you play, we ask that you group with members of FoJ for between 1 to 2 weeks, determined by the sponsor. After which point a vote will be initiated by your sponsor. After 3 positive votes, the member would then be allowed full membership, and may remove the anonymous tag.

Thanks,
Nendidari
