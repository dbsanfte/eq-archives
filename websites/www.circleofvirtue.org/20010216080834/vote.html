Medal of Virtue
This award is given to someone who goes above and beyond the call of duty to help someone in need.
Award for Martial Prowess
This award is given to someone who exhibits the upmost skill in their field. They show considerable skill
whether on the battefield or in a duel.
Medal of Character
This award is given to the person who demonstrates the will to roleplay. They need to be good at it, just
the want to do so.
Award of Excellence
This award is given to the person who has shown great skill and has levelled beyond expectations for that
week. Does not mean the most levels gained.
Medal of Honor
This award goes to the person who shines above all other members...they are not a master of arms, but
rather a Jack of All Trades.
