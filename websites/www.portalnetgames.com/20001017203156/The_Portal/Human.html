Humans are the youngest race in all of Norrath, yet the have become the
single most dominate faction in the land, much to the annoyance of the older
races. Humans are related to the similar Barbarian race of Norrath, but are
rumored to have been the last race created by direct divine action before the
various deities were forced back to their respective Planes.

Humans have been hard pressed to catch up with the rest of the Norrathian
society and have striven by leaps and bounds to make a place for themselves.
Since the founding of Qeynos by Antonius Bayle, Humankind has developed
from a simple tribe based class - to a driving force behind the happenings of
the world of Norrath.

Humans are unique in that they are incredibly resilient, they are not deterred
by the odds, and will attempt most any challenge. They are also a brash and
passionate race, quick to rush into a situation without out perhaps
understanding the consequences. Humans are a high spirited, short lived
creature, driven to make the most out of their short lives.

The Human race in general has not particularly outstanding attributes or
abilities. The attraction of this race is its ability to assume the role of many
classes. A human can be almost anything, from an Evil Shadow Knight
destined for destruction, to a pure Cleric dedicated to his or her god. A Human
is the only player however, that can be a Monk. A Monk is a unique class of
Humans that have dedicated themselves to the art of unarmed combat.

In many cases, Humans are used as the baseline by which other races are
judged. They are in a pure sense, generalists. The only class not open to the
Human is that of a Shaman, Humans seem to have lost the primitive natural
insights required to succeed in this area over the generations.

Humans begin their adventure in one of two major Human cities of Qeynos or
Freeport. These cities represent major ports of commerce and lie on the
Western and Eastern coasts of Anotonica respectively.
