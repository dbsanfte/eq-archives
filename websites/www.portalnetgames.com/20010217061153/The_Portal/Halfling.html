Halflings can be a bit irritating, especially to the more serious races of Norrath.
They tend to joke a lot, most of which are bad, ask endless questions and are
notoriously light fingered.

Since Halflings have gotten used to being run out of town, and dodging the
town guards when something goes "missing", they are quite good at hiding -
slipping into the background until the people looking for them pass by.

On average, a Halfling is equal in strength to a Human, but tend to be much
more dexterous. Given their inquisitive nature, Halflings in general tend to be
reasonably intelligent individuals - meaning they not only are quick about pick
pocketing your stuff - by are good at getting away with it as well.

Halflings make excellent Rogues, given their natural inclinations - this is
perhaps their strongest area. However, some of stepped into the Warriors role
and chosen to fight face to face with their adversaries. Oddly enough, the
Halfling race has produced a number of powerful Clerics and many of their
kindred of taken to the protection of all things natural as Druids.

Halflings begin in a town known as Rivervale, a Woody glen in the mountains
between Freeport and Qeynos.
