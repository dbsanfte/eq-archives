Creating your character in EverQuest is one of the single most important steps in the game! Why? The attributes
and statics you provide for your character directly impact how successfull or unsucessful the character will be. For
example, if you create a Warrior with very little strength, you will not be able to carry or effectivly use the diverse
set of weapons and armor avaliable to this class. EverQuest is very unforgiving in this area. Your base character
statistics are very difficult to modify after you character is created. The game offers magicial and statistic enhancing
items, but these only provide incremental improvements and can be very expensive.

The following provides you with the detailed information you will need to build your character. If you need more information, visit our Forums boards and post your questions. Good Luck!.

EverQuest Classes
EverQuest Offers a wide range of classes. A class in EverQuest represents the role your character will play in the
world of Norrath. You have the option of taking on the role of a powerful magic user, such as a Mage or Wizard;
or you can take on the role of the Warrior, Druid or Ranger and take the battle to your enemy. Want to play the
bad guy, try the Evil Shadow Knight or if the dark arts are more to your liking, the Necromancer lets you play with
