The Erudite represents the pinnacle of human academician accomplishment.
The Erudite prides himself on his intellectual abilities and social graces almost
to the exclusion of the physical disciplines. An Erudite is focused on mental
tasks and abilities, being pre-occupied with intellectual growth and
development. Given the emphases on mental ability in Erudite society, they
tend to take a dim view of other "lesser" races. Others have at times
insinuated that an Erudite is overly self absorbed, likely as not these other
races have mud on their hands and are no brighter then the earth they shovel.

The Erudite are sometimes known as "High Men". This sometimes leads to
jealousies amongst the other races. I have even heard that some of the lessor
races will take up arms at the site of an Erudite.

The Erudite shuns common tasks and menial labor, finding overdeveloped
muscles to be grotesque. Their natural intelligence is the highest of all other
races in Norrath, but their other abilities suffer from their views towards
physical efforts.

Mental disciplines are the most prized aspect of Erudite society, particularly in
the areas of the magical arts. Erudite are possibly the most formidable magic
users in all of Norrath.

Erudite are not by nature Evil, however an evil sec has developed in the
shadows of the Erudite lands which delve into the magic of death. These
Necromancers are despised by their brethren and a long history of war and
battle exists between them. The Erudite Necromancer is an extremely
formidable individual even as they are shunned in their own lands.

An Erudite can excel in any class of magic, be it a Mage, Enchanter, or
Wizard. Erudite also make powerful Clerics and some have even taken up
arms to become Paladins, despite their inherent dislike of physical exertion.

The Erudite home land is on a small island west of Antonica called Odus.
Their city, Erudin is a magnificent castle located deep within the Toxxulia
forest.
