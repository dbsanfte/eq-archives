The Woodland Elf almost looks human, and have been mistaken for them in
some instances. They are about the same size as a human, but have much
sharper features and pointed ears. Woodland Elves however are not as strong
as a Human, but more then make up for it with their amazing Agility and
Dexterity. They are a very wise race and quite intelligent, as well as being very
pleasant in appearance and demeanor.

Woodland Elves live amongst the trees and are truly in tune with the natural
world around them. When they do come down from the trees, these Elves are
the most easily integrated amongst Human populations. especially with those
Humans who have chosen the path of the Ranger, a class with strong ties to
Woodland Elf society.

As with all Elves, Woodland Elves can see clearly in the dark.

Life amongst the trees has made the Woodland Elf extremely Agile and
Dexterous. They can move quickly and with purpose, making them very hard
targets to hit and harder targets to catch when they do not want to be. They
are also very intelligent creatures, although not matching that of their High Elf
cousins.

Woodland Elves have a well defined sense of their place in nature. They make
excellent Rangers and Druids due to their knowledge of woodcraft, but are not
particularly well suited for the more disciplined classes of the mystical arts.
Wood Elves however, are excellent musicians, giving them a distinct edge in
the Bard class.

Some of the Woodland Elf people have taken up the sword to become
Warriors and others have fallen to the temptations of the Rouge, although
neither is the norm in their society.

Woodland Elves begin in a unique wooden city, hidden in the great trees of the
eastern forest of Faydwer. This wondrous place is known as Kelethin and is a
true site of beauty for those in tune with the natural world.
