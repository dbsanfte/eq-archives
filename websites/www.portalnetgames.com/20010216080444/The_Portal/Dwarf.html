Dwarves are short, stocky and extremely strong creatures, much more so then
a human. They are keenly dexterous and benefit from a great deal of stamina.
A Dwarf looks much like a small, stocky human and are always found with a
beard and mustache. Dwarves are a hardy people and they take life very
seriously whatever the their endeavor. Be it fighting, drinking or digging for gold
and gems, Dwarves operate with a singlemindedness that is astounding.

Dwarves spend much of their time underground, in the caverns and tunnels
they mine. After centuries of living in this environment, Dwarves have developed
a keen sense of vision, allowing them to see into the ultraviolet spectrum.
Given the endless maze of tunnels and caverns that make up their
underground homes, Dwarves have also developed a superb sense of direction,
allowing them find their way with few clues or land marks.

Dwarves come from a heritage of miners and workers, as such they are a
strong and study lot. However, they are not particularly wise or intellectual and
they tend to shy away from the mystical arts with one exception. Dwarves are
a deeply religious people. They tend to be close to their gods and sustain a
deeply rooted belief system. This makes those that choose to follow the
callings of their gods, very good Clerics.

The home land of the Dwarves is a vast underground city located in Kaladim on
the continent of Faydwer. The Dwarf city lies on the far western coast of the
continent as far away from the haughty Elven races as possible.
