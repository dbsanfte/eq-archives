High Elves are the pinnacle of Elven society, they are the royalty of this fine
and ancient race. High Elves tend to keep to themselves, they are not
particularly forthcoming with information about their society or culture,
preferring stead to stay sheltered from the influx of Humans and Human
values. High Elves tend to be aloof, often speaking amongst themselves in
their own tongue, excluding others from their conversations.

High Elves look similar to their Woodland brethren, but are thinner and taller in
general, tend to more attractive and are much more pale skinned. Other races
may remark that they are arrogant in their manner and speech - yet I think
much of this is due to the High Elf's natural immersion into their deep, rich and
ancient culture. However, their internal focus has lead to an contemptuous
attitude towards the younger and less established races amongst some of
their ranks.

A High Elf has a particularly long natural life span, making them initially slow
to develop - but able to gain great intelligence and wisdom over their long lives.
The High Elf is also a master of the mystical arts, their mages, enchanters
and wizards are renowned for their knowledge of the arcane arts. A master of
magic, one would be wise not to underestimate the power a High Elf can bring
to bear when threatened.

High Elves make powerful magic users off all kinds, from pure magic wielders
such as Wizards and Enchanters, to Cleric and Paladins. The High Elf does
not have a Warrior class, Elves that desire a mastery of physical combat fall
into the ranks of the Paladin. Rogues and other brutish classes are unheard of
amongst this proud people.

As with all Elves, High Elves can see clearly in the dark. The High Elf starts in
a grand city of Marble, gleaming brightly in the eastern forests of Faydwer.
