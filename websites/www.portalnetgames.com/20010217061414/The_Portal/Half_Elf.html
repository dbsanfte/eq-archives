Half Elves are a blend between humans and Woodland Elves and as such
share some of the characteristics of both races. The most closely resemble
humans, except for their slightly pointed ears and sharper facial features.

Due to the fact the Half Elves are not a pure race, both Human and Elves
regard them with a certain amount of distrust. High Elves, who are not
particularly respective of Woodland Elves, think of a Half Elf as something less
then a person. In general, the Half Elf can have trouble fitting into Norrath's
diverse societies.

Half Elves are not quite as strong as an average Human, but they are a bit
smarter. However, like the Elf, the Half Elf can see clearly in the dark. The
Wood Elf heritage of the Half Elf limits their flexibility in class selection - but
their Human ancestry opens the door to the life of a Paladin.

As a Half Elf is both Elf and Human, they can begin in any city occupied by
Humans or Wood Elves, including Qeynos, Freeport, Kelethin and Felwithe.
