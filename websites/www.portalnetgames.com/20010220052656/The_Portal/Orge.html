Orges are a sorry lot in general, being generally disliked by all other races,
including others of their own kind. However, they did manage to stop killing
each other and those around them long enough to gather into a city of sorts, a
develop some sort of hierarchy amongst its residences. Orge society is based
around the simple concept that might makes right. Bouncers keep the peace
by pounding the unruly, while Shadow Knights plot each other's undoing.

Most other races stay clear of Orges, particularly the smaller ones who tend to
be looked at as a food source. The Orge can be a dangerous ally, their
strength and power is unmatched in battle, but their tendencies towards
murderous rage at anyone who might somehow upset them can turn an ally
into an enemy in moments.

Orges have the ability to bash, and bash hard. They are by far the strongest of
the races, and when one is coming at you will know it. Given their Evil nature,
they can see clearly in the dark places they populate. An Orge is also the
dumbest of all the races, making their existence little more then killing, eating,
mating, and then killing some more.

As you might guess, Orges are not producing many Wizards or other magic
users, in fact that race as a whole has pretty much stayed clear of the
mystical arts. Although stupid, some have managed to become Shaman, and
learn the ways of the primitive magics - other then these few, most are
Warriors - where strength is all it takes.

Orges begin adventuring in the city (if you can call it that) of Oggok, which lies
in the southern reaches of Antonica.
