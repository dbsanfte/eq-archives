Trolls have no real civilization as such, they live in a swamp amongst the vile
and evil creatures of their home lands. Their evil ways have forced them to
spend much of their lives in hidden places away from the light of day. This has
allowed then to develop night vision and an ability to peer into the shadowy
places of their people.

Trolls are evil, but they are also resilient. They have an innate ability to Slam
their opponents and a unique ability to Regenerate - making them heal much
quicker when injured.

Trolls redefine the definition of ugly. They are painful to look upon and worse to
be near enough to smell. Many a warrior has commented that the approach of
a Troll war party was heralded by the stench long before the beasts
themselves were in sight. Trolls are also very stupid, even more so then the
Orge. It is a surprise they don't choke themselves to death eating due to
forgetting how to chew and swallow.

Tolls, much like the Orge, is to dumb to know better and to strong to stop.
They go from sleep to a hemorrhaging rage in milliseconds, and are known to
kill companions and enemies alike when angered.

The Troll begins its misery in a swampy refuge called Grobb, which lies at the
southern most expanse of Antonica.
