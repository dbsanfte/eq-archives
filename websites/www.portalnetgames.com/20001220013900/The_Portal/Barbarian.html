Barbarians come from a harsh and cold land, as such they are stronger and
taller then their Human brethren. Warriors receive bonuses for Strength,
Stamina and Agility to reflect their warlike nature, while Rogues are more Agile
and Dexterous and Shaman more Wise and Charismatic.

Barbarians, in general, have little use for magic or the mystic arts, they tend to
be Warriors, pure and simple. Those with a inclination to cause trouble
become Rogues. Some Barbarians hear the call of the land and turn to the
arts of the Shaman.

Barbarians begin in the city of Halas, a city hidden far in the north reaches of
Antonica. A Barbarian can be a difficult race to play due to the remote location
of their city. At early levels you will face a harsh and unforgiving land where
cold and danger hide behind every corner.

The Barbarian can also be a vary powerful race, the Barbarian Shaman is often
popular choice. This race/class offers the strength of a Warrior with the healing
and enhancement spells of a Shaman, together, a powerful combination. Of
course the Barbarian Warrior is renowned for their ability to damage an enemy.
