EverQuest Continents
The EverQuest world consists of four continents, each providing unique environments. Odus, the smallest of the
continents hosts the cities of the Erudites, while the ancient Faydwer is the home of the older races. Antonica is the
largest of the continents and is the home of many races, while Kunark is an ancient and lost land and home only to
the Iksar.
