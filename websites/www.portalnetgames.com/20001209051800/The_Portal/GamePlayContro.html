Currency Conversion Table
In order to convert currency from one value to another, find the coin type in the left-hand column and look to the right for it's value in copper, silver, gold and platinum. Trading in money at bank: While viewing your banked cash inventory, you can convert all one type of coin into another by dragging the one type of coin onto a different coin type's name. For example, if you have 47 copper pieces, you may click on them with your cursor and place the coins over the bar that says "Silver" on the Inventory screen. Doing so will deposit 4 silver and leave 7 copper on your cursor.
