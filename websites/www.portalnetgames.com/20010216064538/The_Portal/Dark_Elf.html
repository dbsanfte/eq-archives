Dark Elves, the evil cousins of the Woodland and High Elves. Their skin is
bluish-black, their hair white and they share the pointed ears and sharp
features of their Elvish brethren. Dark Elves are a twisted and evil race, created
by the Prince of Hate as a mockery of the Elven races. Dark Elves are haughty
and condescending of other races. The look of a Dark Elf can be alarming,
they have a sense about them that they are not altogether sane.

Dark Elves live their lives in the dark reaches of the earth and other evil places,
as such they have developed a keen sense of vision - allowing them to see in
the darkest of caverns and the blackest of nights as clearly as the middle of
day. Due to the evil of their character and the hatred other races have for them,
the Dark Elf has developed a unique ability to hide in the shadows - thus
avoiding the sight of others.

Dark Elves are not especially strong, but are more a like their Elven kindred in
that they are quick and agile. Dark Elves are an evil and twisted race, as such
they shun the lighter classes, such as Druids, Shaman, Paladins, Rangers
and Bards. Dark Elves do have a keen sense of the mystical arts and make
great Enchanters, Necromancers, Wizards and Magicians.

Some of the Dark Elf race find simple combat to be a better way to satisfy
their lust for destruction, and as such turn to the way of the Warrior or the
Rogue. The Dark Elf can also follow the path of a Cleric, paying tribute and
loyalty to dark and evil gods of great power - filling their lives with chaos and
pain.

The Dark Elf begins in the city of Neriak - a dark and vast place hidden in the
black forests of Nektulos.
