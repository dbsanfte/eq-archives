    05/02/2002 I've gotten a few emails from people who are claiming to see popups even though they're logged in as premium users. These popups are not coming from this site, they're a product of a new breed of software which intercepts your web browsing experience (shares out your CPU, amoung other things). I highly reccommend installing a program called Ad-aware, which will help you in identifying and uninstalling programs that may have been installed without your consent. As an interesting side note, one of the programs is installed when you install Morpheus, and along with adding popups to pages, it also redirects Amazon.com links, breaking their referral program.

    With the ad industry in the tank, and many sites moving towards a donation based service, I'm the first to admit I'm just not comfortable with donations. We wanted to offer something significant to people who chose to give us money, so we wracked our heads trying to figure out what the best middle ground is. There is one point I want to emphasise: all information on this site and the main site remains free. It's how you get to it that we're charging for, and more particularily we're charging for what we think are some pretty useful services.

    To highlight what I think are some of the cool features we're bringing in:

    • No more Ads!! (if you sign up for premium services, all banner ads are replaced with a nice little "Welcome, Username" box.

    • Fun Posting Names. You have the option to select modifiers for your posting name. "Illia the Tulip", "Lord/Lady/Sir/Baron Von Illia", "Illia the Vile" are some examples.
    • Premium users get 24 character slots, more slots for selling items, and the ability to mark yourself as a merchant in any tradeskill you're over 100 points in for permanent listing on the classifieds.
    • Much Much better searching. Many of the searches aren't finished yet, but I'm adding the "what factions feature in what zones search", "search the bestiary by level", and others.
    I plan on adding a couple other features I simply couldn't because of server load if I offered them to everyone for free. A Guild/Raid tracking system, Guild based forums, Comments Search, and more.
