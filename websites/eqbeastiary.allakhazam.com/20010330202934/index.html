    11/17/2000 I get alot of email asking the question "How to I search by level?", so I wanted to mention that if you enter a number in the search box to the left, the programs that search will treat it as a level.

    You can search for all the level 30ish creatures in the world, simply by typing "30" and hitting enter in the search form.

    The search form also looks at zone names, and loot. In order to find all the creatures in Mistmoore which are level 28, you can jsut type "30 Mistmoore"

    I've got a couple enhancements to the site coming along.

    11/08/2000 Well, I survived my excusion to the Scars of Velious launch party thingy. To sum it up in as concisely as possible: Meeting Everquest Staff and players - Good, Las Vegas - Ick.
