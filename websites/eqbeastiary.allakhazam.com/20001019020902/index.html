    09/12/2000 As a few people have noticed, I've added a link on the monster pages which lets you quickly send me an email to point out corrections to the database. Thanks to everyone who has sent me updates! I'd really like to make sure things here are as accurate as possible.

    One oft asked question of this site is "How do I recover my faction with the Indigo Brotherhood?". Well, thanks to the research efforts of Lias Roxx (the always enterprising troll ;) a semi-reliable method has been found.

    So there are now 3 methods to raise faction with the drow warriors guild:

    1. Kill the nobles in Innothulle Swamp. They are rare and only some give the Indigo Brotherhood boost.
    2. Kill the rangers at the camp in Lesser Faydark (they're level 25, and there are alot of them)
    3. (uncomfirmed) Kill the woodelf guards of Kethelin. (they're level 40 to 60, this would NOT be an easy task)
    I'll be trying to quantify the faction hit associated with the Rangers, as the Indigo Brotherhood make nasty enemies for dark races.

    --Illia

    08/17/2000 Phew! It's done. The comments engine which appears beneath most every page on this site has just been upgraded. It's a big change. It has a number of new features, which should make it easier to cut through the comments to the good ones.

  • You must login to be able to assign your name to the comments, though once you do, you have the option of posting under the anonymous name as well.
  • You may rate messages and filter messages based on the ratings they recieve.
  • As the messages that you post begin to gather ratings, their combined rating will give you a title (if their combined rating is a good one :). This should help you track the really good posters.
  • You may now reply in a threaded manner to comments.
  • If you really liked the old way of doing the comments, just set your preferences to "flat, always expand @ awful, oldest first, filter @ below average" and check the save box when you change your preferences - you won't be bothered again.

    Well, hope everyone likes it... please let me know if any bugs have crept in :)
