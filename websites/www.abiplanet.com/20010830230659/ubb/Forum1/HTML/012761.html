For people going full screen....

Set your options at the first EQ screen to Primary video card. This does NOT work in glide mode.

Also..you must set in eqclient.ini your graphics to 16 bit, not 32.

If you do those things... You should have no problems.

Also... If you put those files on your EverQuest directory your asking to get yourself banned. They CAN legally look at the files in that directory. Put it elsewhere...
