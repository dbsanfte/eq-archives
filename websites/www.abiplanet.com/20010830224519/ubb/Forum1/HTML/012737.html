--------------------------------
The following is the list of agreements as it stands now.

Point 1:

There will be only one calendar in use for TM. The calendar agreed
upon is Everquestraids.com. The gm's reserve the right to change
calendars by vote if at some point this calendar becomes unusable.

Point 2:

There will be a maximum and a minimum time limit to post raids to
the calendar. The maximum time in advance a guild may post is 30
days. The minimum time to post a raid is 4 days in advance. Any npc
or spawn not scheduled within 4 days from the current date is FFA,
open to the first guild to amass the necessary forces to kill.

Point 3:

Scheduled patch days will be calendar scheduled and respected.

Point 4:

Unscheduled patch days/server resets are FFA for the Plane of Fear
and the Plane of Hate only. All other places and npc's are still
calendar scheduled for those days. Much discussion went into this
point and valid arguments were made for both sides of this issue
however in the end it was agreed upon by all guilds.

Point 5:

Multiple day raids cannot be scheduled on the calendar with the
exception of Plane of Air. Guilds may post raids for 1 24 hour day
within a 2 week period. Plane of Air may be reserved for up to 3
consecutive days at a maximum of once per calendar month. Guilds
may raid these planes more than this, but may only "reserve" that
amount of time. For the Plane of Fear and Plane of Hate, the
reserving guild has the plane for 24 hours following their posted time.
(ie a posted start time of 9am means no other guild should enter the
plane until 9am the following day)

Point 6:

Mobs that are nonspawnable, nonserver dependent such as Lodizal,
VS, Trak are noncalendared mobs. Mobs that can be spawned,
triggered, or are dependent on server resets can be calendared. All
planar mobs are calendared with the exceptions listed within these
rules.

Point 7:

All guilds agreeing to the agenda set forth here, will back the guild
who was on the calendar should a conflict arise.
