From an old posting (not mine) on Illia's EQ Bestiary:

Ashenbone Broodmaster - a very large version of the Ashenbone Drakes. Sometimes drops the Necro focus item Encyclopedia Necrotheurgia.

Master of Spite - a large version of the Spite Golem. Drops two pieces of shaman armor and sometimes the Tunarian Scimitar or Gardash. Completely MR or atleast from what I've seen. Be ready to chase this one down as it will run before it dies.

Grandmaster R'tal - a large version of the Kiraikuei. Drops two pieces of warrior armor and sometimes Sode of Empowerment, Sunderfury, or Rakusha Cloak.

High Priest M'kari - a large version of the Cleric of Innoruuk. Drops two pieces of cleric armor and sometimes Truesight Hammer.

Avatar of Abhorrents - a large version of the Abhorrents. Drops two pieces of rogue armor and sometimes Vexthorne.

Mistress of Scorn - a large version of the Scorn Banshees. Drops two pieces of bard armor and sometimes Dawnchaser. She has a very strong disease that takes a high level cleric/shaman to dispell.

Lord of Ire - a large version of the Ire Ghasts. Sometimes drops the Shield of the Immaculate or Eye of Innoruuk.

Magi P'tasa - a large version of the male Forsaken Revenant. Drops two pieces of mage armor and sometimes the Staff of Elemental Mastery: Earth.

Coercer T'vala - a large version of the female Forsaken Revenant. Drops two pieces of enchanter armor. She may also drop Rod of Infinite Thought.

Lord of Loathing- a large version of the Loathing Lichs. Sometimes drops the Eye of Innoruuk and Beckon.

All the mini-bosses are around level 55 and are very magic resistant. Most of them are comparable to the Maestro, just slightly less difficult. From what I could see a majority of them spawned infront of the thrones through out the Plane of Hate. Although some of them were in houses right next to the regular static spawns. Each of them dropped something that the old Innoruuk used to drop. Also, those mini-bosses that corresponded with an armor dropping mob, dropped two pieces of the appropriate armor.
