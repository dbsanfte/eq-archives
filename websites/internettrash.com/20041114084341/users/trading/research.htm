here are two way to increase your Research Skill, an easy one and a hard one : You can raise your Research Skill by researching Spells or you can raise it by making Practice Runes, you can also put all your Practice Points in this Skill but it will become very expensive and you will probably need your Practice Points for other Skills.
 

- Making Practice Runes :

First things to know is to not confond Pratcice Runes with Wizard Runes, all Classes that can Research Spells can do Practice Runes.

Then you need a Concorcance of Research. To get your Concordance of Research you need to give a Greater Lightstone to the appropriate NPC : For good aligned Races, Myrsila in the Gypsy Camp in Northern Karana (head North from the spires, Loc -675, -150). For evil aligned Races, Smith Tv'ysa in the camp in Southen Desert of Ro (head South from the Oasis of Marr zone, Loc +100, +500). When you give the Greater Lightstone, be ware to keep an Empty Slot in your Inventory, if not you will not get the Concordance. The Concordance is required to create the Practice Runes, it is a Container used to combine the Components.

You also need, for each Practice Runes, a Quill and a Piece of Parchment, you will get back the Quill but not the Piece of Parchment.

The Practice Runes is composed of two parts : the Azia part and the Beza part for each level of Practice Runes. Your Research skill will increase only when you will combine the two parts, not when you create the two parts. There is no skill needed to create the Azia and the Beza rune. When you created the two parts you need to combine them in the appropriate Tome for your Class: the Tome of Endless Enchantments for Enchanter, the Elemental Grimoire for Magician, the Book of Dark Bindings for Necromancer and the Lexicon for Wizard.

First you should make Practice Rune Azia  : Combine 1 Lapis Lazuli, 1 Batwing and 1 Bone Chips in your Concordance of Research.
Then you should make Practice Rune Beza : Combine 1 Turquoise, 1 Fire Beelte Eye and 1 Rusty Slashing Weapon in your Concordance of Research.
When you've made the Practice Rune Azia and the Practice Rune Beza, Combine them in the appropriate Tome for your Class. The result is a Complete Practice Rune that you can sell to a merchant for some money. Complete Practice Runes becomes trivial at skill 21.

The next step is to make Apprentice Rune Azia and Beza. For them you need a Pixie Wing and a Froglock Eye but it seems that nobody can't yet find them.

Then you should make Training Rune Azia and Beza. Complete Training Runes becomes trivial at skill 62.

Then you should make Journeyman Rune Azia and Beza. Complete Journeyman Runes becomes trivial at skill 95.

- Researching  Spells :

First things to do is to buy the appropriate Tome for your Class : the Tome of Endless Enchantments for Enchanter, the Elemental Grimoire for Magician, the Book of Dark Bindings for Necromancer and the Lexicon for Wizard.

Then you have to know what components search : Pages for Enchanter, Words for magician and Necromancer, but there are different Words for both, and Runes for Wizard, but not Practice Runes.
You can find them only on NPC of the equivalent level to the researched Spell.

After that you should reserach Spells till they're become trivial. But this way is very difficult because you will have a higher failed rate than the Practice Runes way and the components are more difficult to find.

The Research skill follow this formulae : (level - 15) x 5, so at level 16, the level you get the skill, you will have 5 in your Research skill.

So at level 36 you will become a MASTER RESEARCHER :)
