aking is an easy and a cheap skill. You don't need to put any Practice Point in this skill but if you want you can put at least 1 Practice Point.

First things to do is to buy a portable Spit from Traders Holiday in East Freeport and a Mixing Bowl. Like this you will be capable to do everything you want where you want. And remember to always use a bought Flask of Water, not a Summoned one neither a Foraged one.

The most of Components are stackable so you will need to pick one off the stack to do your Baking. Just control-click on a stack to pick up one item. But remember to put your food in a higher inventory slot than your baking items or you will eat all your components.

To start I recommend to cook Edible Goo : just combine 1 Fire Beetle Eye and 1 Rat Ear in your Spit what are easy to find in newbie areas. Edible Goos becomes trivial at skill 21.

Then you should cook some Sandwiches : Bear, Mammoth, Rat or Wolf sandwich. Just combine 1 Piece of Bread and 1 Bear, Mammoth, Rat OR Wolf Meat in your Spit.  Rat Sandwiches becomes trivial at skill 26 and Bear, Mammoth or Wolf Sandwiches becomes trivial at skill 31.

Next an easy way to increase your skill is to cook Batwing Crunchies. Combine 1 Batwing with 1 Frosting in your Spit. Batwings can be found in newbie areas. Batwing Crunchies becomes trivial at skill 46.

Next step is to cook Beer Braised Meat : Bear, Gator, Lion, Mammoth, Rat or Wolf. Combine 1 Spices, 1 Short Beer, 1 Bear, Gator, Lion, Mammoth, Rat OR Wolf Meat in your Spit. Beer Braised Meats becomes trivial at skill 68.

Now, the next step is an important step but not an expensive step : you will cook Fish Fillet. Combine 1 Jug'O Sauce with 1 Fresh Fish in your Spit and by a success you will obtain 2 Fish Fillets. Fishes can be fished or bought, but with this one you will begin to accumulate some profits. Fish Fillets becomes trivial at skill 82.

Then you should cook Fish Rolls : combine 1 Batwing with 1 Fresh Fish in your Spit. You will make a lot of profit with this one. Fish Rolls becomes trivial at skill 135.

Now you are a MASTER COOKER :)

The best Character to become a Master Cooker is a good Potter and a good Blacksmith. A Wood Elf, a Ranger, a Druid or a Bard would be nice because they can Forage Components needed to cook a lot of Food.
