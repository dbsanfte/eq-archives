Dye

 

The Extract needed for Dyes are made by a Shaman or a Rogue, using Alchemy or Make Poison, respectively. These Extracts are then useable by anyone (no specific Skill) to be Combined into Dyes.

Extracts : Must be Combined in either an Alchemist's Medicine Bag by a Shaman, or in a Rogue's Mortar and Pestle by a Rogue.

Yellow Jack O'Lantern Fungus, Empty Vial, Flask of Water 35

Dyes : can be made by anyone, using the above Extracts, and Combining them in a Medium Clay Jar

  • Base Color - 2 Resins, Extract
  • Dark Color - Resin, Extract
  • Light Color - 3 Resins, Extract

Dye your armor : Now you need to combine a piece of Fine Steel Plate, a Dye, a Jar of Acid, a Jar of Lacquer in a Forge to dye your armor.

Where to find Dyes Components ?

Evergreen Leaf in Greater Faydark :

  • Visual Cue : these look like Lighter Green Slanted Squares on the Ground.
  • Locations : (63, 166); (596, 200); (638, -96); (-411, -57); (962, 814); (-191, 7); (90, -400); (30, -2); (403, -440); (555, -234); (669, 774); (717, 748); (197, -483); (200, 400); (427, -321); (-384, -30); (630, -101)

Jack O'Lantern Fungus and Sarcoscypha Fungus in Lesser Faydark :

  • Visual Cue : these look like Small Colored Mushrooms (Red and Blue) on the Ground
  • Locations : on the Ground in the Faerie Camp, generally around the Bigger Mushroom

Permafrost Crystal in Permafrost :

  • Visual Cue : these look like Red Lumps on the Ground.
  • Locations : on the Ground in the Dire Wolf Pens in Permafrost, as well as on the Ground in various Spots of the "Goblin Castle" (left of the "Flag Room")

Oxides Iron and Russet in Steamfont :

  • Visual Cue : these look like Red Lumps on the Ground.
  • Locations : on and around the Huge Gears on the Ground near the Minotaur Caves.
