f you decide to train your Brewing skill, you don't need a lot of money and only a little time because it is a relative easy skill to increase. So that is not necessary to put any Practice Point in this skill.

For this skill you don't need to buy any container but it means that you can train your skill only in a town. And remember to always use a bought Flask of Water, not a Summoned one neither a Foraged one.

A lot of components are stackable so you will need to pick one off the stack to do your Brewing. Just control-click on a stack to pick up one item. But remember to put your food in a higher Inventory Slot than your Foraged Brewing Items or you will eat all your components.

To begin you should make Bog Juice : combine 1 Snake Scale, 1 Flask of Water, and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. Snake Scale can be found in newbie areas. Bog Juices becomes trivial at skill 21.

The next step is to make Kalish : combine 1 Fruit, 1 Vegetable (list of foraged items here) , 1 Flask of Water, 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. Kalishes becomes trivial at skill 30.

Then you should make Honey Mead : combine 1 Hops, 1 Royal Jelly, 1 Flask of Water and 1 Cask in a Brew Barrel. Royal Jelly can be found on Wasp. Honey Meads becomes trivial at skill 40.

The fourth step is to make Heady Kiola, very useful for our friends tailors. Combine 2 Kiola Saps, 1 Flask of Water and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. Heady Kiola becomes trivial at skill 46.

The next step is to make Ogre Swill : combine 1 Cask, 1 Froglok Meat, 1 Malt, 1 Yeast in a Brew Barrel. Ogre Swill becomes trivial at skill 51.

Then you should make Fish Wine : combine 1 Flask of Water, 1 Grapes, 1 Fish, 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel; the result is 3 Fish Wines . Fishes can be bought or fished. Fish Wines becomes trivial at skill 62.

Then you should make Gypsy Wine : combine 1 Grapes, 1 Wine Yeast, 1 Fire Beetle Eye and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel; the result is 2 Gypsy Wines. Gypsy Wines becomes trivial at skill 68.

Then you should make White Wine : combine 1 Grapes, 1 Wine Yeast, 1 Fruit (list of foraged items here) and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. White Wines becomes trivial at skill 82.

Then you should make Red Wine : combine 1 Grapes, 1 Wine Yeast, 1 Berries (list of foraged items here) and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. Red Wines becomes trivial at skill 95.

Then you should make Gnomish Spirit : combine 1 Rice, 1 Spider leg, 1 Rat Ear, and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel; the result is 2 Gnomish Spirits. Gnomish Spirits becomes trivial at skill 102.

Now you are a MASTER BREWER :)

The next step is to make Vodka : combine 1 Flask of Water, 1 Vegetable (list of foraged items here) , 2 Short Beers and 1 Bottle in a Brew Barrel. Vodkas becomes trivial at skill 135.

The next step is to make Hulgarsh : combine 2 Vodkas, 1 Flask of Water, 1 Fishing Bait (not fishing grubs) , 1 Cask in a Brew Barrel; the result is 3 Hulgarshs. Hulgarshs becomes trivial at skill 155.

Then you should make Ginesh : combine 1 Vegetable (list of foraged items here) , 1 Flask of Water, 1 Shot Glass and 1 Short Beer in a Brew Barrel, the result is 2 Gineshs. Gineshs becomes trivial at skill 168.

The best character to become a Master Brewer is a Wood Elf, a Ranger, a Druid or a Bard because they can forage components needed.
