ake Poison is not an hard neither an expensive Skill, but will not make a lot of profits with this Skill, as there is only Rogue, who have reached the Level 18, that can use Poisons. This skill is available only to Rogue who have reached the Level 20, and you will be able to increase in only at Level 24. So it will be good to put between 1 and 5 Practice Points in this Skill.

Poisons can be use on Weapons to add some extra Damages on creatures.

The First things to do is to buy a Mortar and Pestle, a lot of Suspensions and Vials, that you can be bought from a Potter, to be capable to do what you want where you want.

The most of Components are Stackable so you will need to pick one off the Stack to do your Poisonery. Just control-click on a Stack to pick up one Item.

To start I recommend to make Spider Venom : just combine 2 Spider Venom Sacs, 1 Suspension, 1 Poison Vial in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Spider Venoms becomes trivial at skill 20.

Then you should make Spine Break : combine 1 Lined Poison Vial, 1 Constrict Suspension, 1 Thorny Ergot, 1 Delphinium in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Spine Break becomes trivial at skill 32.

The next step is to make Putrid Bane : combine 1 Lined Poison Vial, 1 Suspension, 2 Putrid Biles in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Putrid Bane becomes trivial at skill 56.

The fourth step is to make Lyxt Wing Dust : combine 1 Lined Poison Vial, 1 Suspension, 2 Embalming Dusts in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Lyxt Wing Dust becomes trivial at skill 84.

Then you should make Leprous Puss : combine  1 Lined Poison Vial, 1 Suspension, 1 Bundle of Wormwood, 1 Darkbone Marrow, 1 Grave Mold in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Leprous Puss becomes trivial at skill 105.

Now you are a MASTER POISONER :)

The next step is to make Brain Freeze : combine 1 Sealed Poison Vial, 1 Constrict Suspension, 2 Gelsemium Rootsin your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Brain Freeze becomes trivial at skill 131.

Then you should make Xegonie's Curse : combine 1 Sealed Poison Vial, 1 Constrict Suspension, 1 Ash Hornet Venom Sac, 1 Cinder Hornet Pollen in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle.  Xegonie's Curse becomes trivial at skill 162.

The next step is to make Calcium Rot : combine 1 Lined Poison Vial, 1 Suspension, 2 King Thorns in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Calcium Rot becomes trivial at skill 172.

Then you should make Zek Frost : combine 1 Sealed Poison Vial, 1 Constrict Suspension, 1 Succulent Sap, 2 Crystal Nightshades in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Zek Frost becomes trivial at skill 183.

Then you should make Cranial Cryofreeze : combine 1 Sealed Poison Vial, 1 Larent Suspension, 3 Frosty Daturas in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Cranial Cryofreeze becomes trivial at skill 204.

The next step is to make Paradoxial Peptide : combine 1 Sealed Poison Vial, 1 Constrict Suspension, 1 Chromodrac Wing and 1 Skyash Bile in your Mortar and Pestle. You get back the Pestle. Paradoxial Peptides becomes trivial at skill 250.

The best character to become a Master Poisoner is a good Potter to make Vials needed for all Recipes.
