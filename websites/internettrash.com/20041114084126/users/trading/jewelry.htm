ewelry is probably the most difficult skill to increase and at the beginning there is a high Failed Rate. So I recommend you to put at least 10 Practice Points in this skill.

First things to do is to buy a Jewelry Kit to be capable to make what you want where you want.

The most of Components are stackable so you will need to pick one off the stack to do your Jewelry. Just control-click on a stack to pick up one item.

The best way to increase the Jewelry skill is to make large quantities of a simple Jewelry Item and when you recieve a message that your skill is trivial, move on to the next Gem/Metal combination. Begin with cheap Gems and Silver Bars, then work up to the more expensive Gems.When you've mastered Silver Jewelry, then move on to Electrum then to Gold and then to Platinum Jewelry.

Don't forget that you are not obliged to make Magic Jewelry Items, you should make Non-magical Jewelry Items when you train your skill and make Magical Jewelry Items when you have an order.

This is some Jewelry examples to make.

To start, if you put 21 Practice Points in your Jewelry skill, I recommend to make Cat's Eye Agate Silver Item : Combine 1 Silver Bar and 1 Cat's Eye Agate in your Jewelry Kit. Cat's Eye Agate Silver Items beccomes trivial at skill 22.

Then you should make Carnelian Silver Item : Combine 1 Silver Bar and 1 Carnelian in your Jewelry Kit. Carnelian Silver Items becomes trivial at skill 31.

Then you should make Emerald Silver Item : Combine 1 Silver Bar and 1 Emerald in your Jewelry Kit. Emerald Silver Items becomes trivial at skill 48.

After Emerald Silver Items is trivial you can move on to Electrum Items. So you should make Malachite Electrum Item : Combine 1 Electrum Bar and 1 Malachite in your Jewelry Kit. Malachite Electrum Items becomes trivial at skill 74.

The next step is to make Onyx Electrum Item : Combine 1 Electrum Bar and 1 Onyx in your Jewelry Kit. Onyx Electrum Items becomes trivial at skill 90.

The next step is to make Jade Electrum Item : Combine 1 Electrum Bar and 1 Jade in your Jewelry Kit. Jade Electrum Items becomes trivial at skill 106.

Now you are a MASTER JEWELER :)

Then you should make Opal Electrum Item : Combine 1 Electrum Bar and 1 Opal in your Jewelry Kit. Opal Electrum Items becomes trivial at skill 120.

Now you can move on to Gold Items. So the next step is to make Malachite Gold Item : Combine 1 Gold Bar and 1 Malachite in your Jewelry Kit. Malachite Gold Items becomes trivial at skill 146.

Then you should make Onyx Gold Item : Combine 1 Gold Bar and 1 Onyx in your Jewelry Kit. Onyx Gold Items becomes trivial at skill 162.

The next step is to make Jade Gold Item : Combine 1 Gold Bar and 1 Jade in your Jewelry Kit. Jade Gold Items becomes trivial at skill 178.

Then you should make Star Ruby Gold Item : Combine 1 Gold Bar and 1 Star Ruby in your Jewelry Kit. Star Ruby Gold Items becomes trivial at skill 199.

The best character to become a Master Jeweler is an Enchanter because they can enchant Metal Bars needed to do Magical Jewelry Items.
