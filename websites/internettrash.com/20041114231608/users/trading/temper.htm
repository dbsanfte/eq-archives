You can find various Smithing Components here :

  • Akheva Blood : Xin Thal Centians in Maiden's Eye, "bleeding" undead in Akheva, Shadow Masters in Grieg's End
  • Blood Water : Sold at many Merchants in Cabilis, also given out in some Iksar Newbie Quests
  • Blue Diamond Powder : Made by a Potter
  • Brandy : Sold at many Merchants, can also be Brewed
  • Broog's Old Peculiar : Can be Brewed
  • Celestial Essence : Made using Poisonery
  • Coldain Heater : Can be Brewed
  • Coldain Velium Temper : Sold at some Merchants, can also be Brewed
  • Drop of Mercury : Drops off various Elementals in Steamfont, Najena, The Hole, etc.
  • Dwarf Blood : Various Dwarves. For the Faction-Conscious, try the Dwarven Bandits in Butcherblock
  • Dwarven Ale : Sold at Pub Kal in Kaladim, probably other locations as well
  • Elf Blood : Various Elves. For the Faction-Conscious, I recommend Bandits in Greater Faydark
  • Essence of Darkness : Dark Assassins, Dark Masters in Umbral Plains; Centi Dators, Transients in Akheva; Greater Spire Spirits in Mons Letalis
  • Essence of Fire : Guardian elementals in Solusek A
  • Essence of Moonlight : Vampires in the Castle of Mistmoore, Dread Wolves in Kithicor Forest
  • Essence of Shadow : Shadowed Men
  • Essence of Sunshine : Sand Giants in Southern Desert of Ro, Squire in Upper Guk, chromodracs in Skyfire, Fairy Dragons in Wakening Lands
  • Essence of Wind : Air Elementals in Twilight Sea; Crag li, meeda hune in Tenebrous Mountains; crag li in Scarlet Desert
  • Essence of Winter : Ice Giants in Permafrost and in Everfrost Peak, Tundra Yeti in Dreadlands
  • Fish Wine : Sold at many Booze Merchants, can also be Brewed
  • Flame Agate : Various Goblins in Solusek A, Ice Giants in Everfrost, Pandas in Stonebrunt, Various Mobs in Mistmoore, Various Mobs in Lower Guk, Aviaks in Southern Plains of  Karana ...
  • Froglok Blood : Found off Frogloks in Upper Guk, some Frogloks in Kunark
  • Griffene Blood : Various Griffenes
  • Grinning Goblin Brew : Backciters Temple of Droga
  • Human Blood : Found on Humans. For the Faction-Conscious, try Bandits, Dervish Cutthroats, Raiders, Hermit (Southern Karana, etc.)
  • Ice Goblin Blood : Various Ice Goblins in Permafrost and in Everfrost Peak
  • Iksar Blood : Various Iksars
  • Imp Blood : Fire Imps in Lavastorm, Imp Protectors in Nagafen's Lair
  • Innothule Mushroom : Foraged in Innothule Swamp
  • Lava Rock : Fire Elementals in Steamfont, Najena, etc., Blazing Elementals, Fire Giants in Solusek B, Skyash Drakes in Skyfire
  • Morning Dew : Foraged in Greater Faydark and Lesser Faydark and sometimes ground in Lesser Faydark
  • Mug of Sea Foam : Seafury Cyclopes
  • Misty Acorn : Found on the ground in Misty Thicket
  • Mountain Death Mineral Salts : Various Mobs in Temple of Droga
  • Neriak Nectar : Sold at some Booze Merchants in Neriak, probably other locations as well
  • Oak Bark : Foraged in Kithicor Forrest and in Warslik's Wood, found on the ground in Butcherblock
  • Ogre Swill : Sold at some Booze Merchants in Oggok and Freeport, possibly other locations as well
  • Purified Water : Can be Brewed
  • Rain Water : Escaped Splitpaw Gnolls in South Karana, Gnolls in Lake Rathe
  • Saltwater Seaweed (No Drop) : Fished in Ocean of Tears
  • Sarnak Blood : Found on various Sarnaks in Kunark
  • Scale Temper : Can be Brewed
  • Sea Foam : Found on Seafury Cyclops in Ocean of Tears, Isle Goblins Chieftain in Ocean of Tears
  • Swamp Vegetables : Ground Spawn in Innothule Swamp
  • Swirling Mist : Steam Elementals in Steamfont, Mist Elementals in Najena, Huge Water Elemental in Lower Guk; Crystalline Cloud, Gust of Wind on Gorgolosk Island in Plane of Air, Water Elementals in Twilight Sea
  • Tae Ew blood : Various Mobs in Cazic Thule
  • Windstone : Grimlings  in Tenebrous Mountains, Moktar Tribesmen in Umbral Plains
  • Vial of Clear Mana : Summoned by Enchanters
  • Vial of Distilled Mana : Summoned by Enchanters
