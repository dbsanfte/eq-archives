he Tailoring skill is a very difficult skill to raise but does not cost a lot of money and the success rate is not so bad. So I recommend to put between 1 and 5 Practice Points in this skill.

The first things to do is to buy a Small (2 Slots), or Large (8 Slots) Sewing Kit and to keep with you all the time.

The most components are not stackable so when you work on your Tailoring skill you need to empty out all your Backpacks.

To start this skill I recommend to make Silk Swatches. Go kill a lot of Spiders and combine 2 Spider Silks in your Sewing Kit. Remember to not use Spiderling Silks, Spider Silks are not stackable but Silk Swatches yes. Silk Swatches becomes trivial at skill 15.

Next step is to make Patchwork Mask : combine 1 Ruined Bear, Cat OR Wolf Pelt and 1 Mask Pattern in your Sewing Kit. The Pelt depend of the Mask Size : Bear Pelt for Large Mask, Cat Pelt for Medium Mask and Wolf Pelt for Small Mask. Patchwork Mask becomes trivial at skill 26.

The third step is to make Raw Silk Mask, very nice Item for a Caster. Just combine 1 Silk Swatch and 1 Mask Pattern in your Sewing Kit. Raw Silk Mask becomes trivial at skill 39.

Then you should make Studded Mask : combine 1 Medium Quality Bear, Cat or Wolf Pelt (depend on the Mask Size), 1 Mask Pattern and 1 Stud in your Sewing Kit. Be ware because there is a high failed rate for this step and Medium Quality Pelts are hard to find. Studded Mask becomes trivial at skill 56.

The next step is to make Cured Silk Mask, a nice Item for a Monk. Combine 1 Silk Swatch, 1 Mask Pattern and 1 Heady Kiola in your Sewing Kit. Cured Silk Mask becomes trivial at skill 82.

Then you should make Hand-Made Backpack : combine 1 High Quality Bear Skin and 1 Backpack Pattern in your Sewing Kit. With this step you will gain a lot of money. Hand-Made Backpack becomes trivial at skill 88.

Once Cured Silk Mask becomes trivial you need to make Reinforced Mask, a good Item for Mid-Level Druid. Combine 1 High Quality Bear, Cat Or Wolf Pelt (depend on the Mask Size), 1 Mask Pattern and 1 Steel Bone in your Sewing Kit. Reinforced Mask becomes trivial at skill 122.

Now you are a MASTER TAILOR :)

The best character to become a Master Tailor is a good Blacksmith, a good Brewer, and a good Potter. And a Wizard or a Druid is nice for this because they can teleport themselves all over.
