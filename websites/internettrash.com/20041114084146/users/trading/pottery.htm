ottery is probably the best easy skill to increase and it doesn't cost a lot of money. So you don't need to put any Practice Point in this skill.

For this skill you don't need to buy any container but it means that you can make train your skill only in a town. The only things you have to buy all the time, except components, is a Flask of Water, don't use a Summoned Flask of Water neither a Foraged Flask of Water, they do not work.

The first step you should make Block of Clay : combine 3 Small Blocks of Clay and 1 Flask of Water OR 1 Large Block of Clay and a Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel. Small Blocks of Clay can be found in Beholder Maze. Block of Clay becomes trivial at skill 21.

Then I recommend to make Small Clay Jar : combine 1 Small Jar Sketch, 1 Block of Clay and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Small Container. Combine the Unfired Small Container and 1 Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. But be ware because the result is a Container, so you have to keep at least one Empty Slot in your Inventory. Small Clay Jar becomes trivial at skill 31.

Next step is to make Medium Clay Jar : combine 1 Medium Jar Sketch, 1 Bock of Clay and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Medium Container. Combine the Unfired Medium Container and 1 Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. But be ware because the result is a Container, so you have to keep at least one Empty Slot in your Inventory. Medium Clay Jar becomes trivial at skill 36.

The fourth step is to make Large Clay Jar : combine 1 Large Jar Sketch, 1 Block of Clay and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Large Container. Combine the Unfired Large Container and 1 Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. But be ware because the result is a Container, so you have to keep at least one Empty Slot in your Inventory. Large Clay Jar becomes trivial at skill 41.

Next step is to make Pot : combine 1 Large Block of Clay, 1 Metal Bits, 1 Pot Sketch and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Pot. Combine the unfired Pot and 1 High Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. The result is not a container. Pot becomes trivial at skill 56.

Then you should make Small Protection Deity : combine 1 Block of Clay, 1 Flask of Water, 1 Small Deity Sketch and 1 Carnelian in a Pottery Wheel, then combine the result with 1 Firing Sheet in a Kiln. Small Protection Deity becomes trivial at skill 68.

Next step is to make Smoker : combine 1 Large Block of Clay, 1 Smoker Sketch and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, then combine the result 2 High Quality Firing Sheets in a Kiln. Smokers becomes trivial at skill 82.

Then you should make Pie Tin it is a long step but courage. Combine 2 Blocks of Clay, 1 Pie Tin Sketch and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Pie Tin. Combine the Unfired Pie Tin and 1 High Quality Firing Sheet  in a Kiln. The result is not a Container. Pie Tin becomes trivial at skill 115.

You are now a MASTER POTTER :)

Then I recommend to make Medium Bowl : combine 1 Medium Bowl Sketch, 1 Block of Clay and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Medium Bowl. Combine the Unfired Medium Bowl and 2 High Quality Firing Sheets in a Kiln. The result is not a Container. Medium Bowl becomes trivial at skill 122.

Next step is to make Mixing Bowl : combine 2 Large Blocks of Clay, 1 Large Bowl Sketch, 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Mixing Bowl. Combine the Unfired Mixing Bowl and 1 High Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. But be ware because the result is a Container, so you have to keep at least one Empty Slot in your Inventory. Mixing Bowl becomes trivial at skill 128.

Next step is to make Large Bowl : combine 1 Large Block of Clay, 1 Glass Shard, 1 Large Bowl Sketch, 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel, you will obtain an Unfired Large Bowl. Combine the Unfired Large Bowl and 1 High Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. The result is not a Container. Large Bowl becomes trivial at skill 148.

Then you should make Lined Poison Vial, used to make Poison by Rogues. Combine 1 Small Block of Clay, 1 Lined Vial Sketch, 1 Animal Skin (list here) and 1 Flask of Water in a Pottery Wheel. Combine the result and 1 High Quality Firing Sheet in a Kiln. The result is not a Container. Lined Poison Vial becomes trivial at skill 168.

The best character to become a Master Potter is a good Blacksmith. A Wizard or a Druid will be nice because they can teleport themselves all over.
