letching shouldn't be an hard Skill, but you will not make a lot of profits, due to the fact that only a few Class are able to use Bows. So I recommend to put about 5 Practice Points.

The first thing to do is to buy a Fletching Kit to be able to do what you want when you want.

The most of Components are stackable so you will need to pick one off the stack to do your Fletching. Just control-click on a stack to pick up one item.

To start I recommend to make Arrows till you have more in the Skill, because Bows are more difficult to make. Start to make Class 1 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Round Cut Fletching and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 1 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 16.

The second step is to make Class 1 Wood Point Arrow (Type 2) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Round Cut Fletching and 1 Medium Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 1 Wood Point Arrows (Type 2) becomes trivial at skill 36.

Then you should make Class 2 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Parabolic Cut Fletching and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 2 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 46.

The fourth step is to make Class 1 Bone Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Bone Arrow Shaft, 1 Sevaral Round Cut Fletching and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 1 Bone Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 68.

The next step is to make Class 3 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Shield Cut Fletching and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 3 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 80.

Then I recommend to make Class 1 Bone Hooked Arrow (Type 2) : combine 1 Hooked Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Bone Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Round Cut Fletching and 1 Medium Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 1 Bone Hooked Arrows (Type 2) becomes trivial at skill 102.

Now you are a MASTER FLETCHER :)

The next step is to make Class 4 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Set of Wooden Arrow Vane and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 4 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 122.

Then you should make Class 1 Ceramic Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Ceramic Arrow Shaft, 1 Several Round Cut Fletch and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 1 Ceramic Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 135.

The next step is to make Class 5 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Set of Bone Arrow Vane and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 5 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill 162.

Then you should is to make Class 6 Wood Point Arrow (Type 1) : combine 1 Field Point Arrowhead, 1 Bundled Wooden Arrow Shaft, 1 Set of Ceramic Arrow Vane and 1 Large Groove Nock in your Fletching Kit. Class 6 Wood Point Arrows (Type 1) becomes trivial at skill above 200.
