lchemy is not an hard Skill but a little expensive. This Skill is available only to Shaman who have reached the Level 25. So you don't need to put any Practice Points in this Skill.

Potions will operate on the User as if they were Spells Cast by a Shaman of the Level of the imbiber. Be ware of Potions are not Stackable with each other, however, each seems to be Stackable with Shaman Regular Buffs, and a few Potions do seem to interact with each other provided Stats do not overlap. Drinking another identical Potion will Cancel the Effects of the previous. You must be standing still in order to Drink the Potion.  Also, it should be noted that Levitate, and Water Breathing Potions require the same Components than the appropriate Spell to be used. Potions have the same Limitations as the Spell (Shrink can only be used in a Dungeon, SoW can only be used Outdoors, ...).

The First things to do is to buy a Medicine Bag to be capable to do what you want where you want.

The most of Components are Stackable so you will need to pick one off the Stack to do your Alchemy. Just control-click on a Stack to pick up one Item.

To Start I recommend to make Lesser Stability : Combine 1 Lucern and 1 Sage Leaf in your Medicine Bag. Lesser Stability becomes trivial at skill 17.

The next step is to make Minor Aura of Cold : combine 1 Birthwart and 1 Allspice in your Medicine Bag. Minor Aura of Cold becomes trivial at skill 27.

Then you should make Minor Null : combine 1 Fenugreek and 1 Mandrake in your Medicine Bag. Minor Null becomes trivial at skill 32.

The fourth step is to make Unlife Awareness : combine 1 Elderberry and 1 Fennel in your Medicine Bag. Unlife Awareness becomes trivial at skill 42.

Then you should make Kithor's Disease Treatment : combine 1 Lady's Mantle and 1 Echinacea in your Medicine Bag. Kithor's Disease Treatment becomes trivial at skill 57.

The next step is to make Ant's Potion : combine 1 Sumbul and 1 Celandine in your Medicine Bag. Ant's Potion becomes trivial at skill 70.

Then you should make Potion of Heat : combine 1 Sage Leaf, 1 Benzoin and 1 Blue Vervain Bulb in your Medicine Bag. Potion of Heat becomes trivial at skill 96.

The next step is to make Potion of Purity : combine 1 Lucern, 1 Night Shade and 1 Blue Vervain Bulb in your Medicine Bag. Potion of Purity becomes trivial at skill 103.

Now you are a MASTER ALCHEMIST :)
