o raise your Swimming Skill it is very easy, you just have to Swim and the Skill will go up alone. You don't have to put any Practice Point in this Skill. So Swim, Swim, Swim and you will become Master Swimmer easily and soon you will Swim more faster than the Boat and a lot of Creatures.

And remember Swimming is very useful for certain Zone like Kedge Keep or Ocean of Tears.

Near the Monk Guild and the Theatre of the Tranquil in Freeport is a small pool area that when entered you will Swim without fear of drowning. This can also be done in the water when you first enter Grobb.

Your Swimming Skill will go up following the formulae : (Level x 5) + 5.

So at level 20 you will become MASTER SWIMMER :)

The best character to become a master Swimmer is an Iksar because they begin at Level 1 with 100 in the Skill and at this level they can anyway increase the Skill level.
