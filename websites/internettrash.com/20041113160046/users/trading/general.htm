he most improtant thing to keep in mind when you create a character if you want to become Master in the most Trade Skill, is that you need a high Wisdom, a high Intellingence and a high Charisma. The wisdom will decrease your failure rate, the Intellingence will increase your Skill more faster and the Wisdom will decrease the price of components needed for recipes.

If you fail to an attempt of making something, don't be discourage and try, try again till you succeed. Then you have to be careful to the received message on an attempt : "Those items do not combine in those quantities" means that you have not used the right components; "You lacked the skills to fashion the items together" means that you have used the right components but you are not advance enough in your skill or that you are not lucky enough to succeed; a success will give you the following message : "You have fashoined the items together to create something new!".

Then never forgot to not stack components that can be satcked, don't hesitate to use different slots, if you stack items, you will fail your attempt.

It will always more profitable to sell your craftings to Players than to Merchants. If you sell to Merchants you will generally get not much than 2 or 3 gold per item.
