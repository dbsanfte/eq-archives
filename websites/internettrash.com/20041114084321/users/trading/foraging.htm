ith the Foraging Skill you don't have anymore to buy Food or Drink. That is not necessary to put any Practice Point in this Skill.

Only some Characters are able to Forage. Rangers get the Skill at Level 3, Druids at Level 5 and Bards at Level 12. Also, Wood Elves and Iksar have the inherant ability to Forgage. But only Rangers and Druids are able to increase the Skill, and the start in the Skill depend of your character, Druids start at Skill 30, Bards and Wood Elves start at skill 50.

The most of Basic Foraged Items are useful for Baking and Brewing and the other just for Eat and Drink. Plus there are a lot of Zone-specific Foraged Items that can be Foraged only when you are a Master Forager. Zone-specific Foraged Items can be sold to a Vendor for some money.

Your Foraging Skill will go up following the formulae : (Level x 5) + 5.

So at level 20 you will become a MASTER FORAGER :)
