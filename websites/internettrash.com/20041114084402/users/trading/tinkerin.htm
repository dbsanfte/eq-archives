inkering is Skill available only to Gnome who have reached the Level 16 and you will be able to increase in at Level 25. At Level 16 you start with 50 in your Tinkering Skill It is a Skill a little hard to increase and it cost a lot of money at high level in the Skill. So I recommend to put bettween 5 and 10 Practice Points in this Skill.

The first thing to do is to buy a Tool Box from a merchant or from a Smith to make what you want where and when you want.

To start I recommend to make Animating Bait : just combine 1 Cork and 1 Spriket in your Toolbox. Animating Baits becomes trivial at skill 50.

The 2nd step is to make Mechanical Lockpicks : combine 1 Gear, 1 Sprocket, 1 Lockpicks and 1 Rat Ear in your Toolbox. Mechanical Lockpicks becomes trivial at skill 62.

Then you should make Standard Bow Cams : combine 1 Gear, 1 Grease and 1 Gnomish Bolt in your Toolbox. Standard Bow Cam becomes trivial at skill 75.

The fourth step is to make Spyglass : combine 1 Collapsible Fishing Pole (Item Tinkered), 1 Bottle, 1 Twine, 1 Metal Rod and 1 Reflactive Shard in your Toolbox. Spyglass becomes trivial at skill 95.

Then you should make Stalking Probe : combine 2 Gears, 1 Bottle, 1 Metal Rod and 1 Firewater (Item Tinkered). Stalking Probes becomes trivial at skill 102.

Now you are a MASTER TINKER :)

Then I recommend to make Powered Glove : combine 1 Steel Lined Glove, 1 Sprocket, 1 Gear, 1 Metal Twine and 1 Firewater (Item Tinkered) in your Toolbox. Powered Glove becomes trivial at skill 122.

The next step is to make Catapult : combine 1 Shaped Ashwood Recurve Bow (Item Fletched), 1 Sprocket, 1 Gear, 1 Metal Twine and 1 Firewater (Item Tinkered) in your Toolbox. Catapult becomes trivial at skill 135.

Then you should make Aqualung : combine 1 Fine Coral Mesh, 1 Silk Lined Steel Helm, 1 Metal Twine, 1 Gnomish Bolt, 1 Metal Rod and 1 Fresh Fish in your Toolbox. Aqualung becomes trivial at skill 148.

The best character to become a Master Tinker is a good Smith, a good Potter, a good Fletcherand a good Fisher. And a Rogue is probably the best character to use Items tinkered.
