ense Heading is a very useful Skill when you are lost, you just have to go in the same Direction all the time and you will find your Way. I recommended to put 1 or 2 Practice Points in this Skill because at the beginning it is REALLY hard to increase this Skill, but after it will Raise relativily easily.

You can Train this Skill during Meditate, so it is for Caster that don't know what to do during this time, just click on the Buttom over and over.

Your Sense Heading Skill will go up following the formulae : (Level x 5) + 5.

So at level 20 you will become MASTER HEADER :)
