ll Characters, except Human, begin with at least 2 Languages, Common and its own Language. For example, an Erudite begin with Erudite and Common. But everybody can learn as many Languages he want.

There are 2 methods to learn a Language :

- You can put Practice Points in the Language you want to learn at your Guild Trainer. Additional Languages start to become available to you as you move up in Levels. but it is the unrecommended method because Practice Points will be more useful to other Skills.

- You can ask to someone else that speak, the Language you want to learn, to group with you and to program a random sentence into a hot key and spam it to you over and over. But you will not see the change in your Skill until you zone. That is an easy and quick way to speak a Language. So you don't need to put any Practice Points in this Language.

But remember that you can become master only in your principal Language, because the max for a non-principal Language is 100.

Speak different Languages is useful if you do magical Research, to read Research Book and learn formulae. Also, sometimes you can find Scrolls and Books written in different Languages. And that is nice for roleplaying aspects and to learn more about the history of Norrath.
