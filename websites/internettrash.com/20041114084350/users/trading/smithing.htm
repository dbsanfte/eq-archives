mithing is not an easy skill and it cost a lot of money, but you will probably get a lot of money because they are a good demand for your creations. So i recommend to put about 3 or 5 Practice Points in this skill to start to train it.

For this skill you don't need to buy any container but it means that you can make train your skill only in a town. The only things you have to buy all the time, except components, is a Flask of Water, don't use a Summoned Flask of Water neither a Foraged Flask of Water, they do not work.

The most of components are stackable so you will need to pick one off the stack to do your smithing. Just control-click on a stack to pick up one item.

The first step is to make Metal Bits, that you should keep to make about all Smithing Items. Combine 2 Small Bricks of Ore and 1 Flask of Water in a Forge. Metal Bits are trivial at skill 21.

The second step is to make Scaler : combine 1 Metal Bits, 1 Flask of Water and 1 Scaler Mold in a Forge. Scaler becomes trivial at skill 41.

The third step is to make Lantern : combine 1 Metal Bits, 1 Flask of Water, 1 Lantern Casting Mold and 1 Bottle in a Forge. Lanterns becomes trivial at skill 68.

The fourth step is to make Skewers : combine 1 Metal Bits, 1 Skewers Mold and 1 Flask of Water in a Forge. Skewers becomes trivial at skill 115.

Now you are a MASTER SMITH :)

The next step is to make Pot : combine 1 Metal Bits, 1 Flask of Water, 1 Pot Mold and 1 Standing Legs Mold in a Forge. Pots becomes trivial at skill 122.

The next step is to make Sewing Kit : combine 1 Metal Bits, 1 Flask of Water, 1 Needle Mold and 1 Thimble Mold in a Forge. Sewing Kits becomes trivial at 135.

Then you can make Banded Cloak : combine 1 Flask of Water, 1 Cloak Mold and 2 Sheets of Metal in a Forge. Now you will really make a lot of profits. Banded Cloaks becomes trivial at 175.
