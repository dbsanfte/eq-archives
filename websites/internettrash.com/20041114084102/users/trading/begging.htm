egging is a Skill only for patient adventurer. But you need absolutely nothing to raise this Skill. So you don't need to put any Practice Point in this Skill and you don't need to buy anything to Practice it, all you need is a NPC.

When you're Begging you will get only 1 Copper or when you will become a good Beggar you will occasionaly get 1 Silver.

A good method to increase your Begging Skill is to find a place to Beg and Fish at the same time. Another one is during a Fight with an Humanoid NPC, turn Attack off, Beg, and then Attack again, all in rapid succession, it will helpful for Classes that don't have Taunt Skill.

But remember that when you're Begging, to be ware of your Faction because NPC will not like to be bored for Coppers, so Train this Skill with a NPC that like you and regard you Aimably or Warmly is the best.

I'm not sure but the Skill raise like the most of Skills, with this formulae : (Level x 5) + 5.

So at level 20 you will become a MASTER BEGGAR :)

The best Character to become a Master Beggar is an Enchanter, a Magician, a Necromancer, a Shaman or a Druid because they can Beg to their Pet.
