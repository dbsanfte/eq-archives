55 Dark Elf Shadowknight

A slice to your left upper arm with her sword removes your arm from its socket. “Fool”, she says, disappointed at your combat prowess. Suddenly you begin to feel lightheaded; the wounds you have sustained have left you low on blood and defenseless against her whims.
She approaches you slowly, steadily in her stride, her pitch black eyes seem to pull at your soul as you stare into them, your paralyzed, unable to move or speak. From the rims of her eyes to the tip of her chin run two scarlet streaks, a never-ending faucet of blood. Slowly, gently, she runs the tips of her fingers across your face, coming to a stop below both your eyes with her index and middle finger, in one quick violent motion she digs two troughs of flesh out from your eyes to the chin, just like all the others. With your remaining limb you attempt to drag yourself away, to no avail...she has you now. Again she approaches and takes a knee, those eyes...once you look into them you cannot look away or even blink, she comes close and cradles you in her cold embrace, her soft dark blue skin meets yours. Forcefully she grabs your hair and pulls your head to the side and begins to feed, she pulls away finally, her face crimson with your blood. You can barely move the loss of blood so great, leaving you weak and trembling. You can already feel your body stiffen with the coming of death and you welcome it with a sigh. There you lay; soaked in the very blood that had once coursed through your veins, giving you life and which now brought your death....
