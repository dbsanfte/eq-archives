  Calendar Submission Rules  

  • The Calender system is for your benifit and only serves to encourage cooperation in events which need to be planned out days in advance.
  • Mobs with excessive spawn times should not be listed on the calender, ie (Lord Nagafen, Lady Vox, Trakanon, VS)

  • If you want the zone to be fully repopulated when you raid it, you MUST reserve the "Buffer" time as part of your reservation. It is your responsibility to convey the actual starting time of your event to all it's participants.

  • You may not use the "Other" zone for scheduling PoF, PoH and PoS zones.

  • PoF, PoH & PoS may only be scheduled once untill that event has expired. You can not schedule 2 seperate events (non consecutive days) for any of these zones untill the event has expired (meaning the date of the event has passed)

  • PoF & PoH may be scheduled up to a 24 hour continuous block of time, to do this you must schedule it on the same form and not 2 seperate entries. By checking 2 dates, the start time is relavent to the first day, and the end time is relavent to the second day and will include all time inbetween.

  • PoS may be scheduled up to 48 hours, to do this you must schedule it on the same form and not 2 seperate entries. By checking 2 dates, the start time is relavent to the first day, and the end time is relavent to the second day and will include all time inbetween.

  • All times on the calender are Eastern Standard Time
