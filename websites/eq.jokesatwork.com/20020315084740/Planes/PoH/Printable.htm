THE PLANE OF HATE DROP TABLES
By: Tashia of Xegony [Recent to June/2001]

A few things to note before looking at these tables...

In March of 2001, the Plane of Hate was re-itemized and the second floor was opened up with new MOBs. As a result, the drops off Innoruuk and Maestro of Rancor were moved around somewhat. Innoruuk now drops all new items. What he previously dropped has been moved to the "mini-bosses" throughout the plane. Maestro still drops the same stuff he did before, but his hand for the Warrior epic has been moved to a new MOB and is now a little easier to get off that MOB. The "mini-bosses" are a new change. They're all level 56, there's one for every first floor MOB type, and they always drop two pieces of armor for that MOB class, as well as old Innoruuk loot. Some pieces have yet to be found, such as Briarzephyr, Diamond Rod, and the Rod of Unbound Thought. There is no "mini-boss" for the Haunted Chests or Revultant Rats, as far as I can tell. The spawn points of the "mini-bosses" are also random. Some spawn on the first floor, and some on the second, but it's different everytime hate is populated. One day the cleric "mini-boss" might be on the first floor; the next day it might be on the second. A lot of the stuff that drops off the MOBs on the upper level are VERY rare. Some things drop constantly (ie. Nerial Ceremonial Robe), but most are extremely rare drops. You can clear all 200 MOBs on the upper level and maybe get two drops of significance.

Various MOBs on the lower level are well known for dropping a series of valuable gems, including Diamonds, Blue Diamonds, Black Sapphires, and Jacinths.

A lot of information is still coming in concerning changes to Hate and the upper level. As a result, some of the information you see below may be wrong.

Named MOBs (unless appearing in the "Named Maestro MOBs" or "Named Boss MOBs" tables) are noted with "[named]" beside their title. Not all named MOBs drop items, but only one MOB with this name can be in the zone at any one time.
