Update: If you have an old item that has been upgraded you can spawn upgrade it without re-doing the whole quest. Spawn the appropriate mob  for the quest and turn it in for the upgraded item. Please check with the mob as he WILL give you a quest if it is an upgradeable item!

IE: To upgrade of the Ranger Regen Cloak to the Ranger Haste Cloak simply Spawn Relinin Skyrunner and turning in the old cloak and have a main inventory spot available! :)
