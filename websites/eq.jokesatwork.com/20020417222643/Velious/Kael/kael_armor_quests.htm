Kael Armor Quests


 

The simplest explanation I can give to describe the requirements for the class armor quests in Kael is that there are three different sets of gems and four different sets of armor.


 

The three different sets of gems are:
  • "healer" gems (cleric, druid, shaman) marked in RED
  • "melee" gems (bard, monk, paladin, ranger, rogue, shadow knight, warrior) marked in BLUE
  • "caster" gems (enchanter, magician, necromancer, wizard) marked in GREEN


 

The four different sets of armors are:
  • ancient tarnished plate armor (bard, cleric, paladin, shadow knight, warrior) marked in CHARCOAL
  • ancient tarnished chain armor (ranger, rogue, shaman) marked in DARK GRAY
  • ancient leather armor (druid, monk) marked in GRAY
  • ancient silk armor (enchanter, magician, necromancer, wizard) marked in LIGHT GRAY.


 

As long as you keep this in mind when a gem or a piece of armor drops, it should be easy to determine who should be allowed to loot the item.


 

Gems are tradeable, but the armor pieces are not.
