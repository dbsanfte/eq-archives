Considering recruiting with Clan of Shadows?

Please visit: "CoS Recruiting Info and Initiate Bio's" For Complete Recruiting Instructions

All Initiates / Recruits are required to fill out a Recruitment Application found here:
www.clanofshadows.org/application.html

Recruiting Requirements:

Character level to join CoS for all classes is 65 along with essential Class AA abilities.
CoS is currently doing Elemental Planes content. Because of this, we ask that you be flaged for Tier One and Two.
Keep in mind that CoS does have back flagging agreements in effect with other Tarew Marr guilds, so exceptions may be made for lack of flags.

General Information:
(Please keep in mind, COMPLETE CoS Policies and Procedures are listed on our Message Board, which you will be required to read)
Recruits may not Random for or Loot any items on Guild raids unless Mack or an officer instructs you to do so. 
You will not be permitted to ask for help on quests, raids, etc. that require Guild participation until you are a full member. 
You will be responsible for obtaining any essential flags / keys on your own, unless its feasible for the Guild to help you.

Recruits and all Members of CoS must always use /role, or Full Tag.  /anon is not permitted.

Recruits should have a full CoS member who will sponsor them into the guild.

All Possible Recruits MUST post a Bio post in the "Clan Recruit Bio's" forum. Once you have posted your Bio, and are tagged as a CoS recruit, your access to our Message Borards will be upgraded to "Recruit" status.  You will then be able to access our "CoS Rules, Policies and Procedures" forum, which explains in detail CoS Policies, including loot, general rules, and recruiting. You will be required to read this forum thoroughly and reply that you have read and understand all CoS Policies and Procedures. If there is anything you do not understand or have questions about regarding CoS Policies, see Lunaa.

The current recruiting period for CoS recruits is one month, in which time you will wear the CoS Guildtag full time. After the recruitment period is up, your membership status will come up for Voting.  A Poll is put up for all full membrs to vote on your membership.  These polls are anonymous and should never be discussed with Recruits.  IF you have questions about your Poll or your membership status into CoS, you must see Lunaa directly.

As you can see from our newspage, we are quite open and a bit over the edge.  We openly joke with each other, however blatant disrespect and disregard for your fellow Guildmates is absolutely not tolerated. 

If you are interested in joining, we ask that you keep a few things in mind.  We are a very close guild, and will help our fellow guildmates in any way possible.  Also, we are a raiding guild.  We do have mandatory raids, and you will be required to attend any mandatory raids if you are online. 

Guild colors for CoS are Black and Purple and we are uniform in our armor.
Gloves, Bracers, Boots are to be dyed Purple 43/3/43.  All other armor is Black 0/0/0.
