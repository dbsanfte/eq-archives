Considering recruiting with CoS?  
   
In order to become a full member with CoS, we ask a few requirements be made.  
 
Minimum level to join CoS varies for each Class.  We are presently recruiting Warriors, Rangers, Paladins, Druids, SK's, Necro's at lvl 60.   
Clerics and Rogues 56+ may apply, preferably with Epic.  All other classes 58+ may apply. Exceptions are always made for special circumstances,  
for instance husband and wife teams. Check with Luna or Abibabi to see if your class is needed at a given time.  
 
Recruits may not roll on any items on guild raids unless no full member needs and an officer or Mack instructs you to do so.  Also, you may not ask   
for help on quests, raids, epics, etc. that require Guild participation until you are a full member.  
 
Recruits should have a full CoS member who will sponsor them into the guild.  
 
All Possible Recruits MUST post a bio post in the recruit forum.  
Once you post a bio and are invited into CoS as a recruit, your access to our Message Forum will be upgraded to "Recruit" status.  You will then be  
able to access our "Recruit Info" forum.  In that forum, there are several posts which explain CoS Policies and procedures in DETAIL.  
You will be required to read this forum thoroughly and reply that you have read and understand all CoS Policies and Procedures  
 
The current recruiting period for CoS recruits is two weeks.  On the following FRIDAY after you have been with CoS for two weeks, your membership  
will come up for Voting.  On our Message Board, we have a forum where all Full Members will vote on your Membership.  These polls are anonymous  
and should never be discussed with a Recruit.  IF you have questions about your Poll or your Full Member status into CoS, you should see Luna or  
Abibabi directly.   
 
If you are interested in joining our family, we ask you keep a few things in mind.  We are a very close guild, and will help our fellow guildmates in  
any way possible.  Also, we are a raiding guild.  We do have mandatory raids, and you will be required to attend any mandatory raids if you are   
online.  
 
Mack is the Guildleader of CoS.
