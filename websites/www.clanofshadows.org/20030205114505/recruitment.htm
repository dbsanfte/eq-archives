Considering recruiting with Clan of Shadows?   
   
In order to become a full member with CoS, we ask that a few requirements be made.  
 
Minimum level to join CoS for Clerics is 60+, Warriors 65, and all other classes is 62+, preferably with a few AA's under your belt.  
Exceptions are always made for special circumstances, for instance husband and wife teams. Check with Lunaa to see if your class is needed.   
 
Recruits may not roll on any items on guild raids unless no full member needs and/or Mack or an officer instructs you to do so.  Also, you may not    
ask for help on quests, raids, epics, etc. that require Guild participation until you are a full member.  
 
Recruits and all members of CoS must always use /role, or Full Tag.  /anon is not allowed.  
 
Recruits should have a full CoS member who will sponsor them into the guild.  
 
All Possible Recruits MUST post a Bio post in the "Clan Recruit Bio's" forum.   
Once you are invited into CoS as a recruit, and post your Bio, your access to our Message Forum will be upgraded to "Recruit" status.  You will then   
be able to access our "Clan Rules, Policies and Procedures" forum, which explains in detail CoS Policies, including loot, general rules, and recruiting.  
You will be required to read this forum thoroughly and reply that you have read and understand all CoS Policies and Procedures.  
 
The current recruiting period for CoS recruits is two weeks.  On the following SUNDAY after you have been with CoS for two weeks, your membership  
will come up for Voting.  On our Message Board, we have a forum where all Full Members will vote on your Membership.  These polls are anonymous  
and should never be discussed with a Recruit.  IF you have questions about your Poll or your Full Member status into CoS, you must see Lunaa  
directly.  
 
As you can see from our newspage, we are quite open and a bit over the edge.  We openly joke with each other, however blatent disrespect and   
disregard for your fellow Guildmates is absolutely not tolerated.   
 
If you are interested in joining, we ask that you keep a few things in mind.  We are a very close guild, and will help our fellow guildmates in any way  
possible.  Also, we are a raiding guild.  We do have mandatory raids, and you will be required to attend any mandatory raids if you are online.
