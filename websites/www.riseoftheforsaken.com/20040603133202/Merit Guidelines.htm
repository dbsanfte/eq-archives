Merit guidelines:

All major items will be merited by the guild leader or officers.  We use this system to insure that the proper items go to the proper class and to insure that loot is distributed fairly.  Please note that all items will not be decided by merit, but some of the better/rarer items need to be.

So everyone has a better idea of how meriting will be done the criteria used in deciding merit is as follows:

Guidelines to use for meriting:

1.  Good of the guild.  Merit should go towards advancing the guild.

2.  Common Sense.  A flowing thought IV item is not a warrior item.  High wisdom items are not pure caster items, etc.

3.  Class. If the item is best for a particular class.

4.  Upgrade.  If the item is a major or minor upgrade.  Also when the last major upgrade the player obtained with the guild.

5.  Attendance.  While no one should be required to attend all raids, raid attendance should be considered since we are a raiding guild.

6.  Morale.  Sometimes a player or the guild needs an item to be merited a particular way for the guild or player morale.

In closing, the above are guidelines that will be used when and how an item will be merited.  These are only guidelines and not LAW.  Each reason for and against merit should be weighed.  The guild leader and officers will make every effort to distribute loot in a fair manner, that is best for the guild and "Spreads the Wealth" when using merit.
