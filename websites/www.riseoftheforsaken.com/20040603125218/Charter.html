Rise of the Forsaken is an Everquest guild on Bertoxxulous server, created by and for players who want to experience all aspects of the Everquest community.  Rise of the Forsaken is a raiding guild with a family atmosphere.

     Rise of the Forsaken’s members strive to enjoy the Everquest experience to its fullest while maintaining a friendly and helpful attitude to fellow guild members and non-guild members.  Rise of the Forsaken members must carry themselves in an upstanding manner within the guild and the Everquest community at all times.   All members are considered family and as such must help their family and treat each family member in a respectful manner.

     Members of Rise of the Forsaken will need to be of a minimum level and go through a recruitment process.  Each member will be required to read all rules and policies and agree to adhere to them.

     As long as all members of Rise of the Forsaken maintain themselves in a favorable manner within the guild and the Everquest community and continue to be friends and family to all, we will achieve all we set out to do
