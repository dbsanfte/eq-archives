1.  You must be level 51+ to join or be in the guild.  Alts are automatically invited when they obtain level 51.

2.  Guild chat will be profanity and spam free.  repeated abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

3.  Any arguing between members in guild chat is not allowed, take it to tells, or talk to an officer. Repeated abuse will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

4.  Any name calling, making fun of, or teasing of another player not deemed in good fun by both members is not allowed. Repeated abuse will be warned, and then can be grounds for removal.

5.  Any real life racial, religious, economic, gender, sexuality, social status, or age comments are not allowed.  repeated abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

6.  Any KSing (kill stealing) will not be allowed. Repeated abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

7.  Any camp jumping (stealing someone's camp spawns) will not be allowed. Repeat abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

8. Any arguments or fighting between a guild member and an outside member is not allowed.  If you are having problems with someone please bring it to the attention of an officer or the guild leader.  Repeat abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

9.  Any scamming or stealing of loot is not allowed.  Repeat abuses will be warned, and then may be grounds for removal.

10.  Any complaining or crying over loot issues at a raid is not allowed. If someone is merited an item, they earned it.  Continued abuse will receive a warning to please stop.  Further abuse will be a firm warning to stop.  Third offense will be removal from the guild.
