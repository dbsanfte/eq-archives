Family Charter

The Family Clan Brigatta is an EverQuest guild that strives to promote peace and unity within all of Norrath. Clan Brigatta is founded upon the concepts of friendship, fun, generosity and helping each other become stronger and wiser in the ways of Norrath. Our guild policies are hereby set forth with these intentions in mind.
 

Leadership Structure

    The leadership structure of Clan Brigatta is as follows:  We have the Leadership Council made of eight members, two of which are called the Guild Masters. The Leadership Council will be known as the Circle of Eight. The Guild Masters are an equivalent member of the Circle of Eight. All guild practices, policies and procedures are the province of the Circle. Enforcement of the guild policies is the domain of the Circle. Any Circle member can request a meeting based on their own or guild member requests. The Circle strives to be fair, honorable and just in all decisions that it makes and policies that it implements. Circle decisions are final. If a Circle member leaves the guild or the game, there will be a vote within the Circle to replace said person. All guild members in good standing will be eligible for nomination. If the GM leaves the guild or the game, there will be a guild-wide vote to replace the GM. The candidates for the GM position will be the current circle members. Guild member input and participation is requested and encouraged through the means of the members-only board. The policies/guidelines are, however, subject to review and revision by the Circle. No member of the Circle is above the standards listed in this or other policy documents. We are all equal members and all subject to the same rules.

  Guild Membership

    New member recruitment will be covered by the Membership Rules, which is maintained and updated by the Circle and subject to review/revision by the Circle. People will from time to time become inactive. We must ask members to notify us when they are leaving for an extended period of time.  If a member leaves for an extended period they will be put on AFK status. This will be done so that everyone will know that we have a missing family member.

Membership Standards

    As per the ideals of the guild philosophy, Clan Brigatta members will be expected to adhere to some basic, common sense guidelines. Every member is equal and deserves to be treated with common courtesy and respect. To that end, inappropriate behavior or unreasonable abuse by members towards other members or other guilds/players will not be tolerated. Such behaviors are further explained in the Rules of Conduct.  

Conflict Resolution

    If guild members encounter problems or situations that they do not know how to handle, or have a disagreement that needs to be mediated, please contact a Circle member. Based on the member's request, he or she will either make a determination on their own or take the issue to the Circle for discussion and resolution. If a large group of guild members has a serious issue or concern, please submit such to the Members Only Board. We will take guild input, meet, and come to a determination.

Member Probation and Removal

    If it is found that members are breaking the standards set forth above, it will be necessary for the Circle to take action. Upon report of inappropriate actions, the Circle will take into consideration both sides of the complaint. All members of the dispute will be dealt with calmly, rationally, fairly and with respect. The Circle will gather evidence it deems necessary to make an equitable decision.

    Once the Circle makes a determination, a Circle member will contact the guild member privately and discuss the finding. The decision will then be posted on the message board, and the Circle will set forth with the appropriate action. Clan Brigatta will pride itself on being honorable, wise and just.

    If a member of Clan Brigatta is accused of kill stealing or power looting, the Circle will investigate this charge immediately. If power looting is found to have happened, the member will be immediately guild removed.

    Kill stealing is an area with many shades of gray. The Circle will not immediately call for guild removal on a report of kill stealing. The Circle will very carefully investigate all reports of kill stealing and make a considered determination. If kill stealing is found to have happened, the member will be immediately guild removed.

    If other Rules of Conduct are broken, a member of the Circle will privately discuss the situation with the guild member. The guild member will receive a warning. If said guild member commits a second inappropriate action, the guild member will be put on a month long probation. If a guild member again breaks the standards in this month long period, they will be guild removed. After a month, the guild member will return to good and honorable Family status. If a Circle member is found to have broken the standards, they will be removed from their position, then placed in the probationary process. They will not get their position back. If a guild member is found to have again broken the standards after having received both a warning and one Probationary period, they will be guild removed.

Award of Loot

    Loot issues will be covered by the Circle, which are maintained and updated in the Loot Policy. Loot issues being such a large portion of the game, it is again necessary to set forth some basic standards, as per the ideals of the guild philosophy. Particular ideals to concern us here are friendship and helping others in the guild. The following principles are intended for highly desired items (for example, FBSSs, Golden Efreeti Boots, Shiny Metallic Robes). They are not intended for easily obtainable/common items.
 
    The distribution of God/Dragon items are the province of the Circle and will be given out on the basis of these guidelines. Again, these guidelines are subject to the review/revision of the Circle and will be posted in the Loot Policy. Refusal to follow the Loot Policy will subject you to the Member Probation and Removal policy. 

Leaving the Family

    If a family member leaves of their own accord to join another guild on Druzzil Ro, we will wish them well and let them go peacefully. If, in the future, they wish to return, then they will go through the same Recruitment processes set forth by the Circle.

    As with every guild, we will have internal problems. If a member has a problem, they should work out their problems using the above methodology. If they are unable to work their issues out, then we bid them farewell. In rare cases, we will consider re-accepting members. In this case, former members will have to go through the Recruitment processes set forth by the Circle.

Conclusion

        The Family Clan Brigatta is fortunate to have talented, honorable and wise members. This document is a collection and formal recognition of many of the ideals and standards of our family. By being guilded in Clan Brigatta, each member affirms the above standards and agrees to abide by them.

    We, the Circle of Eight, hereby pledge to do our best to serve all members of Family Clan Brigatta in a honorable, wise and just manner, with courtesy and respect. We further pledge to uphold both the text and the spirit of the philosophies set forth herein. We pledge to resolve disputes to the best of our abilities with the input from all guild members.
