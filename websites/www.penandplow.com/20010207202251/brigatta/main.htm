Jan 20, 2001

The family has been busy... we added a few new members. Aleion, 54 Magician. Wizat, 58th Wizard. Embrosa, 43rd Cleric. Welcome to the family. May your stay with us be long and fulfilling.

On Jan 12, we went into Eastern Wastes. We killed a bit and were killed a bit. It was a wonderful learnin experience for all. First time groupin with the family were Gibbi, Wulolil, and Vesudus. They have said they had a lot of fun and look forward to more.

On Jan 13, we headed into LGuk. We had 2 full parties and quickly found out that LGuk was camped to the gills. We did find an opening in the King room and proceeded to head there. It was rough, but it was fun. Casiopia joined us for a family raid for the first time. Among normal drops, we acquired A Black Tome with Silver Runes (Trentonic), a Mith 2 hander (Gibbi), a Plat Tiara (Trentonic), and 2 Crowns of Froglok King (Ladi Sarinja & the second went to guild bank). Later in the evening, we headed to the Exe/Sav spawn. There, we acquired an Exe Axe (Starnn). After a long day and night, we camped out with plans to get together to head to Kaesora the next day.

On Jan 14th, we converged on Kaesora. We camped the Librarian and the Burned-Out Hut. We acquired 2 Spirit Tomes (Illusorer & Ladi Sarinja), several Dusty Ransacker Bags, a Kunzar Mantle (Tynen & Ladi Sarinja), and a Spectral Shroud (Guild banker). We had a lot of fun learnin this new zone and look forward to goin back again.

Durin the week, several talks occured between Clan Brigatta and Clan Battlehammer. We have since furthered talks by creatin a seperate area on the message boards for everyone in both Clan's to talk. Ask an officer or fellow family member to acquire the password for this board.

On Wed, Jan 17th, we had an all-guild level 40+ meeting. Many topics were discussed.. including where the 'elders' of the family would like to camp in the future. A lot of ideas came out of this and one was campin City of Mist. We headed there after the meeting and have been there since. Several family members acquired levels and we did obtain one drop so far. It was the Master Wu's Trance Stick, which went to Soularr. We will be stayin in CoM till Monday morning, Jan 22nd.

Another topic discussed was the possibility of adding a second message board to the web site. This message board will be for the discussion of classes inside the Family. Look for it in the comin week, as the final plans are made and it is created.
