June 12th

Yesterday started the week-long raid in Trak's Teeth for Seb keys and rares. We will continue until Fri nite, when we do The Hole for the first time.

Sunday, we learned all about Droga. There was a lot of fun and some nice items dropped. We are going to go back one last time, this Sunday.

The July calendar is being formed. Post your ideas for raids OR talk to an officer, in game. Let's get the plans set for this coming month early, this time.

June 6th

The web site is pulling together. The bugs are being worked out and the graphics are looking nice. So far, the only snag we have hit is that the coding is not Netscape friendly.

The graphics are being done by Allandra of Battlehammer. Allandra is the owner and operator of HMG Designs, when she's not playin Everquest.

I have not been online, so I am waiting to hear from the The Family on the daily news from abroad.

June 5th

The new web design is starting to look good. A template of this page was made for ease in creating new pages, thus making the transition faster & easier. There are still some things I would like to do, but that can wait till later, I guess. I'm anxious to show off what has been dreamed up for the web site, this time.

I decided to go live with the new site, rather than wait. I was hoping to see family reaction to the new design and perhaps get diff ideas for the new banner. The links are not complete, but things are looking great, so far.
Remember, I did offer 1kpp to the person who came up with the best looking Clan B Logo and Banner for this new design.

June 4th

Reworking and redesigning the web site. Hoping for a fresh new look. Ladi Sarinja & LaGhee are working long hours and pulling out their best knowledge base.

Today starts 2 guild hunts. The first, a week long hunt in Wakening Lands. We will attempt to slaughter all monkeys in existence. The second, a SolA hunt. The 'youngers' of Clan B will be hunting in SolA for various drops.
