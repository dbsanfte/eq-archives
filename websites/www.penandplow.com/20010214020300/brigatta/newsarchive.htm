Jan 4, 2001

Been a few days since last update, and the Family has been busy. We started off on Sat with a family raid on Dalnir. 2 days later, we walked out with a few items wanted, and a few family members with new levels. Of particular note, the necro dagger quest was finished by Vesudus with the help of the family. Click here for a few pictures of that adventure.

We left there and headed to Velius, stopping in the Crystal Caverns off Eastern Wastes. After a lengthy descent, we came to the crystal spiders. The family fought here for some time and managed to kill the Queen Spider and obtain a robe for Scorpious.

After the Dalnir raid was finished, some of us headed to Karnor's Castle. For some, it was the first time in KC. Tynen, Jenkin, Torf, and Soularr especially. Watch here for pics from that fun party. We have since been to KC a few times and just today, a Lupine Dagger dropped in the Captain's room. Ladi Sarinja won the roll on it and is currently deciding what will be done with it. The KC parties will continue, whenever there are members enough to enjoy it.

A reminder, the wedding of Ladi Sarinja and Torf will be held on Jan 6th, at Lesser Faydark old tower. It will be at 640pm, PST and afterwards, we will be heading to the Lake Rathe Arena for some fun. This party will be the last good-bye to Grandfather Amilius. He will be going on long-term AFK as of Sunday, the 7th. We want to send him off with love, so stop on by. Perhaps we will all take to tacklng his powerful pet and finally winning against it. Transport will be provided, so hollar up and let us know.

On a last note, during this past week, several levels were obtained by our family members. Soularr & Amilius made 52, Ladi Sarinja made 51, and Flynnc acquired 46. He made it out of the dreaded 45 by fighting Bloodgills in the Lake of Ill Omen.
