Disciples of Shadow Guild

by Sephiras

 
 
Guild Name: Disciples of Shadow
Interviewer Name: Ix
Job: ShadowLord
Members: 22
Address: http://freeweb.digiweb.com/games/damas7/DoS.html


What is the relationship between members in the guild?

More formal ranks status, though the different levels might be a bit tight knit... the top 3 people for example. We always consult each other and stuff.

What type of guild is it? Are you in more then one-game?

Role-playing, and just in EverQuest

What is the purpose of the guild?

Our main goal is to spread the word of the corruption of the light and the so called good people of the world.

Any other goals?

Yeah, we want to bring the people of our guild into a bit of a spotlight in the word by perhaps becoming a well known guild, at least on whatever server we end up playing on. And we want to make sure all our members are well taken care of... at least when and if we gain some good standing in the world.

What does your guild plan to do when first entering Norrath?

Ok... well in the beta whenever most of us are in it then I mostly just want for all of us to get used to the world and the interface and such, and in the final I really don't have anything planned at the moment, I'm waiting to see how things go in the beta

What type of structure is with in your guild?

Ranks. When someone first enters the guild they are called an acolyte, so far the only other ranks are captain, shield bearer, enforcer, and shadowlord(the last being the leader) so far there are only 2 captains and that's most likely how it will stay until the final

Is your guild known of anything unique?

We are one of several trying to role-play evil(though we are not completely evil just dedicated to the spread of our dark values)and we see ourselves as very good. Fair is foul and foul is fair sort of thing.

What kinds of classes do you accept or restrict?

So far we do not restrict based on class or race, but it all depends on how things end up in the game... it might prove way to hard to have a guild with no such restrictions

How do you differ from other guilds that are similar to yours?

Well most of those guilds do restrict based on race and class, and not all of them are after the same goals, another thing is we want our members to feel like the guild is their family in EverQuest. Also our guild members have a lot of say in the way the guild is run... though some have more say than others depending on their rank.

Anything you do differently then other evil guilds?

Well, some evil guilds are just evil because they want to pk, we don't allow that unless their is a good reason... like if its a race thing or a class thing then its ok but if its just random its not allowed. Role-playing is very important to DoS.

What is the second alignment would be best for your guild?

Neutral

Where do you see your guild in 6 months?

I see us a being a bit bigger, though not too big.. having several mid level characters, maybe have a start at saving some money for the day we can afford some sort of meeting hall, but that's probably going to take a long time. I hope we have a good RP war going on with some guild of light.

Sephiras' Final Thought

The Disciples of Shadow's are a strong based guild, in that meaning they have been around for awhile and have gone through a lot and keep standing strong. They're good role-players from what I can tell and have a dedicated leader and members. They have a well organized and appealing web page that doesn't take much time to load up, even on a slow computer. They also have a nice "Literature" section with "The Book of Shadows" which is their interesting history. I see the Disciples going a long way in Beta and if the make it to final, they will add to the culture. We have had differences in the past but through this interview I find them to be a rather good group, and one of the best evil role-playing guilds yet to come. Well, at least that's my opinion anyway.
