A Power Gamer's Warrior

by Dhalken

 
 

This was written durring the phase 3 beta test of EQ, so things may be a little different now

So, you're a powergamer and want to know what you should do to be the best possible warrior out there.

Let's first chose the best race. You've got twelve races to chose from, let's throw out the weak ones: Gnomes, Halflings, Woodland-Elves, High-Elves, Dark-Elves, Half-Elves, Humans and Erudites. I think we can all agree that for powergaming those races are not good warriors(These "weaker" races can still be great warriors). So now we're left with: Barbarians, Dwarves, Trolls and Ogres. So now let's look at these four races vs. each other statistic wise.

Skill Barbarians Dwarves Trolls Ogres
STR 108 100 115 138
STA 100 100 115 130
AGI 87 65 88 75
DEX 68 90 75 70
WIZ 67 83 60 67
INT 60 62 52 60
CHA 60 50 45 37
ADD 25 25 25 25


Remember that STR, STA and AGI are the most important skills for a warrior. Now let's look at The aspects of each race.

Aspect Barbarians Dwarves Trolls Ogres
Regenerate Health No No Yes No
Hated By Other Races No No Yes Yes

Regenerate health is something only Trolls can do, but it is great. All it basically does is speed up how fast your hit points rise when you get hurt. This isn't a skill so you don't have to work on it or wait until you're a high level to use it, it comes automatically.

Being hated by other races is not fun. Trolls and Ogres have it hard, if you go to almost any foreign city the guards will kill you. The only way to get around this is to kill the local "enemy". I believe the local enemy for almost all the other race's cities are Goblins and Deathfist's (an Orc). You must kill a lot of these to be allowed in the city and a heck of a lot more before any shops will do business with you.

So now we have to decide what all these things actually have to do with your playing experience. Let's look at the two strongest races, the Troll and the Ogre. The Ogre is stronger than the Troll but doesn't regenerate it's health as fast as Trolls. Regenerate health makes a world of a difference, so let's say the Troll is better. So what about the other two races? Well the Dwarves aren't as strong in comparison to the Barbarians and they have much less agility, so lets throw them out. The Barbarian isn't as strong as the Troll or Ogre, but can basically go into any city they want without worry about NPC's killing them.

The next question is, does being hated by other races make it really hard to play? It depends. If you plan on going to other realms and fighting by other cities, then it's going to be hard.

So which is the best? Well, for actually killing things the Troll is. But if you want to take vacations or fight by other cities; as a Troll you're going to have to do a lot of work. So now you decide, which race is for you?
