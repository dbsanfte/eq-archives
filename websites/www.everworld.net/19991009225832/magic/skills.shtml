Abjuration

Abjuration deals with the defensive side of the magic arts, a skill which increases your proficiency in protection spells, such as a block against physical assaults or even other magical forces.

Alteration

Alteration deals with the shifting of reality, whether it be by illusion or an actual physical changes in the state of matter. An example would be a spell that teleports something, or that enables a character to move faster, or forces him or her to move more slowly.

Channeling

Channeling is a skill that effects the amount of time it takes for a caster to complete a spell. The more skilled a caster is in this area, the less time it will take a caster to concentrate on the completion of the given spell, and in turn the faster he will be ready for the next spell.

Conjuration

Conjuration summons an energy or creature from another part of the world, or even other plains, to do the bidding of the caster.

Divination

Divination allows a caster to better detect things that might have been previously concealed, as well as to conceal things previously visible.

Evocation

Evocation deals with the area of offensive magic. Being well practice in this skill allows a caster to better let fly a speeding ball of flames to envelope his opposition.

Meditate

Meditation allows a caster to memorize any spell more rapidly then he normally would which is helpful if he has forgotten a spell and a battle is taking place.
