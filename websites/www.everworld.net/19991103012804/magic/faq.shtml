Please note that the content of this section is taken directly from the official FAQ.

6.1 How many types of magic are there?

5 primary schools of magic.

6.2 Can you tell us some of the spells?

We can list some at this time: levitate, water breathing, healing, and fireball and other offensive spells.

6.2.1 When will you be releasing some spells for each class?

We won't be releasing information on magic until we are very close to open beta.

6.3 Will magic be mana based, or reagents, or spellbooks?

The magic system is primarily mana based, but that doesn't necessarily rule out reagents completely.

6.4 Do I have all the spells available to my character at the beginning or do I need to learn them?

You start with some, you earn others as your character progresses.

6.5 How do I learn new spells? From NPCs or can other players teach me?

We can't go into that yet.

6.6 Do spells deteriorate when not used?

Probably not.

6.7 Can I create new spells?

No.

6.8 Can I enchant weapons?

The Enchanter class can enchant weapons.

6.9 Area effect spells -- will PKers be able to kill non-PKers with them?

No, PK area affect spells will only affect other PKers and NPC's.

6.10 Can you elaborate on how balanced magic and non-magic attacks will be?

The idea behind having a class system is that the different classes will have advantages and disadvantages. If we do our jobs and balance these classes, then none of them will have inherent advantages over the other. A mage might beat a warrior under certain conditions, and vice versa.

6.11 Are you going to try to emphasize the use of ships for long distance travel, instead of making a transport type of spell commonly accessible?

Transport spells will exist, but they will be very high level and hard to come by.

6.12 Concerning spell targeting, will spells automatically hit it's target or will some actual aiming be required in order to hit your target with a spell?

I'm afraid we cannot comment on the mechanics of targeting and spells at this point.

6.13 Will the Necromancer be able to Summon, Command, or Create Undead?

Yes.

6.13.1 Will there be a limit as to the number of undead a single necromancer can create/control at one time, and if so, will greater experience allow you to create/control more?

Yes, a limit. The higher the level the more powerful the undead -- not necessarily the quantity here, but rather the quality.
