Impress this...

by Iolanthe

 
 
After having read my sisters column last week I wasn't about to leave all you hungry readers thinking -I- was a floozy, who hugged and kissed everyone in sight.

There is a simple reason everyone loves Lillith. She's a flirt. You can ask her anything, and she'll make time for you. She likes to help people. She likes the attention. If you buy her anything, she is yours forever. Use her abuse her, toss her away and come back later for more. She'll be there. It's always been like that.

However, I for one am not about to stand by and let everyone in Norrath think all women are out to be -had-. If you want to know what I think read on.

This is the prime reason we are not taken seriously by anyone In Norrath. When your boobs are hanging out, cleavage shown for all to see, you are bound to get a whoop, a holler, a whistle, drooling etc. and an occasional suitor who thinks he can win you over with sweet words and a kiss on the cheek. I'm here to dispel those vicious rumors and smite anyone who might think otherwise.

Now don't get me wrong, I'm not out to be the evil twin sister who oppresses her sister, and then finally is killed at the end of the story... OH NO! I just want to strangle her sometimes.

After her article was published, Lillith got a bit too excited about being a girl. She ran out with her scantily clad elven friends, tromping all over Antonica. They hung out in taverns, and the docks practically seducing anything with a pulse.

Every night she'd come home, her pockets filled to over flowing with gold and silver coins. I'd thought she'd taken to hopping in to bed with just anyone... But she assured me; her treasures were from hunting and donations for binding people to the area. (She always has a good excuse) so I let it go, assuming she was smart enough, and knew better. But MY experiences thus far have NOT been quite as flowery and pleasant as Lillith's.

While in the Qeynos hills I was practicing my swordplay on the local wildlife, taking care to avoid the patrolling rangers who seem to feel that a dog's life is of more value than a human's. Mind you, I suppose this is true in a lot of cases. A splendid example, in fact, wandered by and began staring at me while I was cleaning a wolf's blood from my blade in the form of a *ahem* gentleman calling himself some obnoxious and rather unintelligible name. Enacting the age-old precept of "Ignore them and they will go away" failed me for approximately the 3,487th time as he decided to strike up a conversation worthy of his infantile name.

-He says "Are you a girl in RL?"

-Iolanthe considers the concept of justifiable homicide

-Iolanthe says "Would you be equally interested in knowing whether in 'RL' I am a sadistic hater of all things living, and the twisted expressions of agony on a human's face as the power of pure death flows from my fingers into them evokes a feeling of unrivaled delight in my arguably evil heart?"

-*long pause*

-He says "You sound sexy."

At the time, I'd have gladly paid 10 platinum to see the red mark on him, allowing me to strike him down at that very moment. However he'd made a deal with the God's like so many have to be immune to my unwanted attacks.

Regardless of the outcome, this is one of many encounters I've had with the less than intelligent male populous that seem to seek me out. Lillith must have exquisite luck in finding men that respect her, although I doubt it.

But being the evil twin that I am, I suppose a lot of my perception of this whole topic has to do with my personality. If I weren't so immune to flattery, perhaps I would take advances of that nature to be a compliment, rather than offensive. As my sister said, your reaction depends on you. I can go throughout my life ignoring everyone... but what fun would that be?
