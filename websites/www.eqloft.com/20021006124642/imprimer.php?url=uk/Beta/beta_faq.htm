Beta Test FAQ

Objectives
This beta test is a unique occasion for you to really test and understand the benefits of this new service and to fully appreciate the role of our skilled team of Game Masters ! Come and try: it’s free !
Of course we will also request you to report any bugs due to translations (even in the English version!)
With your help, we want to make sure the European version of EverQuest is the best it can be!

Registration
Beta test registration started September 19th and lasts till October 11th.

Who
The beta test is open to everybody.

How to register
You have to register directly online though www.eqloft.com .

Process
You will receive a free CD by air mail with the basic files for EverQuest. This CD will come with a login which will allow you to access the game and the dedicated forum.

Timing
The beta tests should start October 21st and end on the night of November 20th.

Game Masters presence
During the whole length of the beta tests, the Games Masters will be available:
From Monday to Thursday: 4.00 pm to 2.00 am
From Friday to Sunday: 2.00pm to 2.00 am
As soon as the game is launched, they will be on-line 24/7.

All the beta test dates

09/19/2002 Start of the beta test registration
10/11/2002 End of the beta test registration
10/21/2000 Start of the beta test
11/20/2002 End of the beta test
