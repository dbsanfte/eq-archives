- The beginner's corner -


The Norrath Survival Kit or Beginner's Corner is the section for beginners! So, dear beginner (Newbie or Noobs in Norrath slang) you will find the essential sections you should read in order to become the best Noobs Norrath ever had.

  • One presentation to rule everyhting you need to know about EverQuest... Just click!
    Here you will find a complete description of EverQuest New Dawn and a sum up of all the features and the extentions available for the game... An absolute must!

  • Noobs Survival Training Courses
    Once a week our guides and Game masters organize a special training session for Newbies. They are usually organised each Wednesday but, just to be sure, we invite you to check regularly our Events Calendar.

  • The Call of Events
    Events especially designed for newbies are organized regularly. To be sure you don't miss the events check our Events Calendar! You may also have event ideas or you may wish to organize your first event as a beginner. Our guides and Game Masters can help you with their advices or with some little help in managing your event. Have no hesitation, mail us!

  • Beginners guide
    Newbies, you are not alone! All veterans were newbies one day and they thought about you. As usual in Norrath, they decided to share their experience with you and in our beginner's guide you will find everything you need to know in order to become the best Noobs Norrath has ever seen. An absolute, central and necessary must!

The concept is easy… Each veteran gets 1 free month for 2 beginners he brings to the game and each beginner gets £9.99 reduction on our referral promotional pack!
