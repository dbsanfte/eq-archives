- EverQuest New Dawn FAQ -
What are the benefits of EverQuest New Dawn?

EverQuest New Dawn aims to meet the expectations of European players:

  • European players can play on all servers worldwide including the new English, French and German servers.
  • In-game customer support will be provided 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.
  • Events will be organised frequently by a dedicated team of professional Game Masters during European gaming hours.
  • Brand new servers will open in Europe to improve connection speeds.
  • Subscription can be paid with several local methods of payment (national credit card, direct debit, etc).
  • Hotlines will be accessible at European business hours and at local cost.



    • What is the release date of EverQuest New Dawn?

    The European release of EverQuest New Dawn is November 21st.


    Will EverQuest the Planes of Power be available?

    The highly anticipated EverQuest new add-on, the Planes of Power, will be available at the very same time as the other EverQuest games on November 21st.


    What games will I find in my store?
    You will find in your store the following packs:

  • EverQuest: New Dawn
    • This pack includes EverQuest + the Ruins of Kunark + the Scars of Velious + the Shadows of Luclin. This is the first compilation and will be released on November 21st.

  • EverQuest: the Planes of Power
    • This pack includes EverQuest + the Planes of Power.
    This is the latest expansion pack for EverQuest and will be released on November 21st .


    Which servers will I play on?

    When you purchase either EverQuest New Dawn or the European EverQuest the Planes of Power expansion pack you will have access to:

  • the new UK server called Venril Sathir
  • the new French server called Sebilis
  • the new German server called Kael Drakkel
  • AND all the existing servers


    • You have a free choice of which server you want to play on. If you want to play in a different language, all you must do is select the correct server when you launch the game.


    What are the Subscription Rates?

    The subscription fees to play EverQuest New Dawn are as follows:

  • 1 month: 12.95€ / £8.45
  • 3 months: 35.85€ / £23.49
  • 6 months: 65.70€ / £42.99
  • 1 year: 124.95€ / £79.99



    • What are the possible means of payment?

    With EverQuest New Dawn you will be able to pay your subscription by the following means of payment:
  • Bank Transfer
  • Credit Cards (both National and International)
  • Direct Debit (for The Netherlands and Spain Only)


    • You are no longer restricted only to international credit cards. Ubi Soft and SOE now give you more freedom to enjoy EverQuest!
    More details regarding the means of payment coming soon on www.eqloft.com


    What if I already have a US account?

    If you are already subscribing to EverQuest and playing with a US account:

  • If you buy EverQuest New Dawn, you will be able to use your existing account to play in EverQuest, Kunark, Velious and Luclin zones as follows:
    • create new characters on the new UK server
    • create new characters on the new French server
    • create new characters on the new German server
    • create or play with your existing characters on all the existing servers
  • If you buy the European Planes of Power expansion pack, you will be able to use your existing account to play in EverQuest Classic zones and Planes of Power zones as follows:
    • create new characters on the new UK server
    • create new characters on the new French server
    • create new characters on the new German server
    • create or play with your existing characters on all the existing servers
  • If you do not buy any European pack, you will be able to use your existing account to play as follows:
    • create new characters on the new UK server
    • create or play with your existing characters on all the existing servers
    This gives you complete choice of which server you want to play on!

    Example: I already have a US EverQuest account and I have already bought EverQuest Classic, Kunark, Velious and Luclin. On November 21st, I buy the European Planes of Power expansion pack. I will be able to play in EverQuest Classic, Kunark, Velious, Luclin and Planes of Power zones as follows:
    • create new characters on the new UK server
    • create or play with my existing characters on all the existing servers
    • create new characters on the new French server
    • create new characters on the new German server

    Will I be able to transfer my characters from the existing servers to the new European servers?

    Yes, you will. However, we feel the importance of discovery and adventure on a new server is worth preserving. This is why we will only allow character transfers some time after the launch of the new servers.


    How much will it cost to transfer my characters?

    The first thousand transfer requests will be free of charge.
    Details on subsequent transfer charges will be available soon on www.eqloft.com


    What happens if I have a technical problem?

    In case of technical problems, you have three options:
    • Go to www.eqloft.com: FAQ and forums are available to provide you with the help and information you require.
    • Contact your local Ubi Soft technical team: dedicated hotline teams have been set up in each country to answer your questions quickly and efficiently. Contact numbers are available at www.eqloft.com
    • When in game, contact the team of Game Masters and Guides by using the /petition command.

    Will EverQuest New Dawn be a dynamic world?

    Yes! Our Game Masters will be connected 24/7 to help you as well as entertain you with original events all week long.
    • To get help, use the /petition command when in game
    • To keep up to date with all of the current events, make sure you visit www.eqloft.com
