I - Introduction

Just when you thought you'd covered it all - five massive continents, the moon, and the homes of the Gods themselves - a new series of adventures leads you into some of Norrath's greatest sources of speculation and mystery. Discover EverQuest: Lost Dungeons of Norrath. The story that began in Broken Skull Rock now yields new clues about an event threatening to change the face of Norrath. Assist a band of adventurers on their quest to unearth long-hidden artifacts of power from forgotten dungeons, secret catacombs, and long-buried ruins. A mystery is unfolding and a new age of exploration and adventure has begun!


 

II - Availability
  • Order Everquest: LDoN from our E-shop and receive it on the 11th of September 2003
  • You will not need an existing version of EQ Classic to play Lost Dungeons of Norrath, as it is included free! If you don’t already own EverQuest you need only purchase the new LDoN expansion!
  • Play LDoN on all servers from the 11th of September.

 

III - Features
  • Explore 48 diverse dungeons set in 5 new environments.
  • New « Dynamic Dungeon System » emphasizes personalized group encounters without interruption from other players.
  • Enjoy the excitement of “dungeon crawls” that are tailored and customized for your group’s attributes & levels.
  • New environments, objects, and interactive items await the hardy adventurer.
  • Reap the benefits of the new, Adventure Rewards Technology
  • Track your progress in the dungeons via a new in-game interface.
  • Encounter dozens of new dangerous monsters and NPCs
  • Collect explosive new spells, new trade skills, fearsome weapons, and other incredible loot!
