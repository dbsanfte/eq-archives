- Beta Fun -
The Everquest beta test: great fun for all!

This will be the first and exclusive experience of the Everquest European servers and localised versions in German and French.... AND ITS GOING TO BE GREAT ! (sorry for shouting but I am so excited, Tartharon is trying to calm me down)
It will last one month and is completly free of charge, all beta testers will receive the cd at home.
You will have the chance to meet us, Sharodika, Tartharon, Thunak and Feyrull your very own GM team, who will be available at all time during the beta, to help you with any problems and to liven things up with events and dynamic quests.
Of course we dont forget the Guides, who are there to help too.
There will be lots of events, quests and fun every week. You will have a privileged and direct relation with the European Everquest Team.
