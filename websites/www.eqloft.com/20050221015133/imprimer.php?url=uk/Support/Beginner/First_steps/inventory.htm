- Inventory -
Now that you've reached your starting point, let's take a look at your inventory. To do so, type the letter "i" - a new window appears! Like everyone else;, you start off with 20 rations of food and 20 measures of milk, a newbie weapon, which varies according to your class (daggers for caster and rogues, clubs for healers, short swords for tanks and bards, nothing for the monk...), a candle to see in the dark if you cannot do so naturally, two spell scrolls if you can cast, and a guild note to hand in to your Guild Master..

To get an item’s description (to read a scroll, for example), right-click on its icon and keep the button pressed until the description appears. If you wish to move the item, left-click on it and it will stick to the cursor. Move it to its new slot and left-click again to drop it there.

To equip your weapon, move it from the inventory window to one of the Mêlée weapon slots and you’ll see it appear in your character’s hand.

To read your guild note, right-click on it. It will give you your first quest : find your Guild Master and hand him the note to begin your training.


Casters aren’t through yet! You still have to learn your first spells. To do so, open your spell book by typing “ctrl + B”, then slide your first spell to its destination slot in the book. It takes a while to write it down but you’ll get better at it. Now slide the spell icon from the book to the spell bank (on the left) and drop it in one of the 8 slots to memorize it. Once the gem appears, just click on it to cast the spell. When you’re through playing with your spell book, click on the “Done” button to close it.

If you wish to reorganise your spell book, right click on the spell’s icon then right-click again on the slot you wish to reassign it to. If you right-click on another icon, the spells will just trade places.
