- European Guidelines -

The EverQuest community continues to grow each day, and we receive many requests to transfer characters to different servers. We have implemented a value added service and have enacted a new policy to meet these requests. If you desire to transfer a character or characters from your primary account to your secondary account, or wish to transfer to a different server, the following service is offered.

Due to the many requests to transfer characters from account to account, or server to server, we have implemented a value added service and have enacted a new policy to meet these requests. If you desire to transfer a character or characters from your primary account to your secondary account, or wish to transfer to a different server during non server split opportunities, the following service is offered. This is a value added service which requires a fee that can be billed to your credit card.

For more information about taking advantage of this service, please review the policy, guidelines and restrictions below.

All terms and conditions of the EverQuest User Agreement and Software License (EUASL) and the Rules of Conduct found on everquest.com apply to any transfer or attempted transfer of characters. You will not be allowed to transfer any characters to an account that is not yours and that was not originally registered by you.

Fee Schedule

Moving characters on the same account, from one server to a different server:
1 to 2 Characters $50 (total charge)
3 to 8 Characters $125 (total charge)

Moving characters from one of your accounts to another account owned by you, and remaining on the same server:
1 to 2 Characters $50
3 to 8 Characters $125 (total charge)

Moving characters from one of your accounts to another account owned by you, and changing to a different server:
1 Character $75
2 Characters $125 (total charge)
3 to 8 Characters $250 (total charge)

Guidelines and Restrictions

  • For moving characters from one account to a different account on the same server, Characters will be moved with items and coins. In order to process a transfer from one account to a different account, the destination account must have access to the same expansions as the source account.

  • For moving characters from one server to a different server, characters will be moved WITHOUT ITEMS AND COINS. (IMPORTANT: Coins and all items will be deleted from characters before the characters are moved. This includes all items located in the character's bank. All scribed spells will transfer with the character). It is highly recommended that you do not give away or destroy existing items upon requesting a server-to-server transfer, as it is possible that your move request may be denied. Items deleted or given away in anticipation of a future move will not be replaced.

  • Guild affiliations: We will be removing any guild affiliation as part of the character move process.

  • When moving characters between accounts, billing information must be identical on both accounts and you must be the original owner of both accounts. You may use the credit card on file or submit a different credit card using the Billing Information fields. If the credit card holder is different from the holder of the card on file, please enter a short explanation in the bottom field. If you have only paid for your account with a Game Card, you must submit a credit card using the Billing Information fields.

  • We are sorry, but we cannot process character moves while you are still in the free 30-day trial period.

  • No refunds will be permitted once your request is approved and processed. If you change your mind about moving your characters after they have already been moved, you will need to fill out an additional Request Form to move the characters back to the original server or account, and you will again be charged the applicable transfer fees.

  • All approved requests will be processed when reasonably practicable to do so. UbiSoft reserves the right to deny any attempted request to transfer in its sole and absolute discretion for any reason or no reason.

  • The Request Form cannot be used to move two or more characters to multiple or different servers. If you would like to move multiple characters to multiple servers, you will need to fill out additional Request Forms.

  • A character can not move more than one time every six months, and a maximum of only eight characters will be permitted to move every six months per account.

  • There are no discounts for multiple transfer requests. The fees described above apply for each Request Form.

  • If you attempt to transfer characters in violation of these guidelines and restrictions, any laws, the EUASL or the Rules of Conduct (for instance, attempting to transfer characters from another person's account, or to or from an account purchased by or for you or given to you where you are not the original registered user of that account), the characters will not be moved, your request will not be processed or approved and your account(s) will be subject to review and appropriate disciplinary action taken.

  • Character names are not guaranteed on server to server transfers. If the character's name has already been used on the server you wish to move to, an "X" will be added to the end of the name. For same-server transfers, character names will not be affected.
