- A Somber Shadow -
Princess Shreika had been wandering around the lands of Norrath looking for some brave adventurers to free her from the curse of the evil necromancer. Being transformed into a spectre was really against her religion as a paladin and defender of the brave and good, but alone she could not escape from the curse.

Finally coming across a group of adventurers in East Commonlands, Shreika asked for aid from anyone who was ready to help. The first wave of undead took them all by surprise, even Shreika who knew the extent of the necromancer’s unholy powers was horrified when they killed every living thing, even the guards who were trying to protect the brave adventurers fell.

Some time later, all the adventurers somehow managed to return, even bringing reinforcements to fight the hordes of undead, skeletons, mummies and zombies.

Shreika needed certain objects found on the invaders decaying corpses to release her from the curse, but the evil necromancer had not used all of his tricks yet.

One of the items needed had just disappeared, there was no trace anywhere on any of the decaying corpses, the necromancer had used his magical powers to hide all the embalming dust in the land.

The courageous adventurers solved this problem and presented Shreika with the items needed to free her from the chains of darkness. Princess Shreika was overjoyed to be free and rewarded everyone who was present that night with a small gift and hearty thanks.
