- Interaction -

To grab an object, activate a mechanism or open a door, left-click on it or type O.

To examine an object, right-click and hold to make its description window appear.

To consider a character and know its attitude towards you right-click on it. If it’s a merchant, his or her inventory will appear.

To target a character (in order to fight, cast a spell on it, hail it…) left-click on it. To deselect your target choose another or hit the Esc key.

To start a conversation with a character, target it, hit the Enter key and type your text.

To camp, sit down (press the sit button or type the /sit command in the text field) and click the Camp button or type the /camp command in the text field.
