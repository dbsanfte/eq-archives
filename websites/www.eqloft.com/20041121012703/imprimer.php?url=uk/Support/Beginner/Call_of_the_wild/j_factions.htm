- Faction -
This is one of EQ’s most important elements : your relation with all the different social groups of NPCs on Norrath. Your character starts off with a reputation based on his race, class and religion. Through his acts he will modify this reputation with the different social groups.

A dark elf won’t be especially welcome in most human cities, some merchants might not even trade with him and paladins will certainly take pleasure is slashing it to pieces, but it will be able to walk around unmolested (most of the time). The same dark elf won’t stand a chance in Felwithe or Kelethin, where members of the other elven races will slay him without warning. Faction is very poor between the Teir’dal and the other elves…

Let’s say this poor dark elf happens to be a necromancer and worships Innoruuk… Its chances of survival in Freeport are nil, not to mention the elven cities… Class and religious hate compound with race antagonism to make it KoS (Kill on Sight) almost everywhere. Even in Neriak, its home town, certain NPCs will be indifferent or dubious toward it.

Certain factions hits are invisible and temporary: spiderlings aer usually indifferent unless you attack them, I which case all neighbouring spiderlings will become aggressive towards you and may even attack. Once the fight is over, the others becom indifferent again.

More evolved creatures remember your foul deeds toward their race: kill an orc near Freeport, for example, and you’ll get the following message:
    Haaarghh! The DeathFists shall hunt you down. My death will not go unnoticed!
    Your faction standing with DeathFistOrcs got worse
    Your faction standing with KnightsOfTruth got better
    Your faction standing with Guardians OfTheVale got better
    Your faction standing with AshenOrder got better
    Your faction standing with CommonsResidents got better
This “murder” has just lowered your faction with the Deathfist clan but raised it with Freeport’s citizens. Kill enough orcs and your Dark Elf Necromancer might become an honorary citizen of Freeport! You may also improve faction with certain groups by completing quests.

Keep in mind that no matter your race, class or religion, whatever you do, someone, somewhere will hate you – if only the Deathfist Orcs…
