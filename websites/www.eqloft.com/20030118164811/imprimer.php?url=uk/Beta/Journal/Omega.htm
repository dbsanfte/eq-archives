- The Day the World Ended -
The Butcherblock Mountain Goblins had been restless all day. A motley crew of adventurers decided to act upon the reports of a scout and proceeded to search the area north of the docks. There they found row upon row of Goblins, obviously prepared to launch an attack on Kaladim. After a long struggle, the Goblins were crushed. The party tended to its dead and wounded, although a few corpses seemed to have been carried away as trophies by the surviving felons.

Members began to disappear, summoned by a greater force to the den of Lord Nagafen. Gathering their courage and pooling their resources, they engaged the fierce dragon in battle. Many died, but still they fought and finally ended victorious. There then followed a brilliant display of fireworks, courtesy of Guides Traal and Blizz.

As the end of the world drew near, all the adventurers were summoned to Lady Vox's lair. The whole population of Norrath from the youngest warrior to the most powerful magician engaged the icy dragon in a long epic fight. With a cry of agony, she fell. And there was much rejoicing.

All Norrathians gathered around the corpse of the giant lizard to revel and rave in the joy of having accomplished in one night more than many have accomplished in a lifetime. United until the end, they faced the end of the world with great serenity, comforted by the knowledge that their friends would be expecting them in a new, exciting and more diverse universe.
