The Plane of Hate is my 3rd Best Liked Zone in the Game, behind the Plane of Sky and Chardock. I have always had a great time going and running Hate Runs.  Always, as on any Plane Run, go expecting to die at least 2 or 3 times. 

The 3 main reason to go to Hate:
1. To have fun (Dark Elves always get a kick out of killing their god.)
2. To get Items (Items for every class here)
3. To get experience (Great experience in this zone, and most runs are set up to share exp between groups.)

Break-In:
    The only purpose of the Break-In is to get as many Clerics camped out as possible, and DO NOT TRAIN THE CAMP.

    Before porting to Hate, you will need a few buffs.  Mainly you will need Invisibility, See Invisibility, and Spirit of the Wolf.  Yes, certain mobs in Hate see through Invisibility, but I lowers your aggro range.  When you arrive in Hate, you will be in an empty building.  The only mobs in this area are general roamers.  Once you realize where you are in Hate, look for the exit.  Once you find it, stay as close to the right wall as you can until you reach the zone wall, this will keep the mobs from the building (See Map)  next to you from aggroing on you.  Once at the wall, go 2/3 the way up the wall and take a RIGHT.  DO NOT GO ANY HIGHER.  Hate has 2 floors or levels, and Innoruuk is up there.  He will summon and DeathTouch you in a heartbeat.  Begin running and you will come to the southeast corner of the zone, take a RIGHT.  This will be about a 30 sec run to the southwest corner of the zone.  Your path will cross the southern roaming path.  If there is a mob up on the wall, STOP.  If a group member in starts to get attacked, do not stop and help, proceed to camp.  When you arrive at the southeast corner take a RIGHT.  There are 3 mobs in this corner, 2 clerics and a kira, if you stay up on the wall, you will not aggro them.  The camp spot is just north of corner.  There will be a monk/shadow knight/necromancer FDed on the floor marking camp spot.  Come down to 1/2 point on wall and camp out.  Once camped out, go to chat to the Chat Room called "********-Hate" and wait. 

   If you get attacked, DO NOT TRAIN CAMP and DO NOT DIE ON THE WALL.  Get off the wall and head to the floor of the zone, and try to make it as close to the camp as you can.  Try to make it to the southwest corner and die.  Once you die and zone, a rogue will ask for consent so they can pull your corpse to the camp spot.  After all groups are up, the clerics and people from chat will log-in and start rezzing and buffing.

Groups:
Groups can be done in several ways, but you need a few key people to make the run go:
1.  Wizard - to port people up to Hate.  Each Hate Stone cost around 230pp each.
2.  Monk/Shadow Knight/Necromancer - FD puller to pull Hate.  Monks are the Masters at this, but you do not need one, Necro and Shadow Knight do fine.
3.  Clerics (3) - Need at least 3.  Need I say more on the roll of a cleric.
4.  Enchanter (2) - One will work but need 2 for crowd control.
5.  Rogue - Corpse puller.
6.  Druid - extra porter out of melee
7.  Warrior - To tank and for armor drops, I hate to see plane armor rot due to the fact that a person is not there that can use it.
8.  Bard - Great to help with main tank group and the armor thing again.
9.  Magicians - Armor thing.
10. Shaman - Buffs and Slow

Battle Rules:
1.  Buff Melee with everything you have including Resist Magic.
2.  Pin Maestro up against a wall.
3.  Melee use there disciplines.
4.  Keep Maestro away from caster.  If Maestro gets to caster area, melee and casters switch areas.
5.  At 70% Health, send in the pets (Mage and Necro Pets).
6.  At 50% Health, caster begin nuking.
7.  Must kill him fast near the end because he will run.
