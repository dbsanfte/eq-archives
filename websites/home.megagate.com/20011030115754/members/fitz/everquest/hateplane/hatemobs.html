Cleric of Innoruuk

Cleric - (Level 49-51 - 10,350 HPs) - They look like a Dark Elf guard. They have high magic resist, so harder to mez, and they love to complete heal themselves or other mobs in the area.  Keep them stunned (bash/slam/stun) while fighting and kill them fast. They do not see rogues or even normal invisible. They will buff other mobs with cleric buffs including Death Pact. They have a normal aggro range. Also drop Prismatic Shield (Cleric only)

Ire Ghast

Undead Shadow Knight - (Level 49-51 - 10,250 HPs) - They look like an oversized ghoul and have low magic resist, so easy to mez, but love to HarmTouch, and do it a lot. Undead magic works on them.  They do not see rogues, but do see normal player invisible (although invis to undead would work). They have a small aggro range.

Revultant Rat

Undead Warrior - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs) - They look like a giant diseased rat and have low magic resist, but proc Deadly Poison.  They do not see rogues, but may or may not see normal invis. They have a small aggro range.  Can be DTed with Cleric/Necro spell Banishment of Shadows.

Spite Golem

Shaman - (Level 51 - 16,100 HPs) - They look like a rock golem. They have insane magic resist (although poison, disease, and the wizard Lure spells will usually stick). This is the most important target most times. If you get 2, one warrior, usually the backup assist, will keep second one busy while the group kills number 1. It is possible to kill several, but very, very tough, so be careful. They love to cast SoW and other Alacrity on everything close to them.  They do not see a rogue, but see everything else. They have a large aggro range. Also drops Barbarian Shaman and Druid Shields.

Loathing Lich

Undead Wizard - (Level 51 - 14,4000 HPs) - They look like a spectre and have very low magic resist but more hp than most mobs in Hate, plus are undead so undead magic works well, so usually easy kills. But they will cast the wizard damage shield (the annoying 2 damage spam shield).  They do not see a rogue.  They have a small self-aggro range, but will pass chain-aggro other mobs a very large range. Can be DTed with Cleric/Necro spell Banishment of Shadows.

Abhorrent

Rogue - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs) -They look like gargoyles and have very low magic resist, but love to backstab, so make sure the puller is careful pulling them.  They do not see rogues or normal invisible. They have a small aggro range. Also drops the Shrieking Ahlspiess (Rouge Weapon).

Haunted Chest

Warrior - (Level 48-52 - 14,400 HPs) - They look like a bog box with teeth. They will proc a Nullify Magic, so they will strip your buffs.  They do not see a rogue, but do see others. They have an extremely small aggro range and are very slow. When pulling, the puller has to stay close, or the box will forget and go home. They even aggro other mobs at a very short range. Boxes spawn only in fixed locations. 

Kiraikuei

Undead Monk - (Level 48-52 -  11,250 HPs) - They look like a mummy and have low magic resist. They will proc Strong Disease. They are undead, so undead magic works well on them.  They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis, though invis to undead would work. They have a very large aggro range, but a small pass-on range, so they will aggro with anything close, but not aggro the mobs around them... usually.  They also drop monk throwing weapons, Shuriken of the Tranquil.

Scorn Banshee

Undead Warrior - (Level 49-51 - 14,500 HPs) - They look like a female zombie and have low magic resist, but proc Strong Disease and hit a ton. But undead magic works.  They DO see rogues and all invis, including invis to undead, regardless of what con says.  They do have a small aggro range, though. Also drops the Martune Rapier for Bards.

Male Forsaken Revanant

Undead Magician (Pimps) - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs) - They look like male vampires and have normal magic resistance. They also cast a mean damage shield and nasty AE direct damage. Undead magic does work. They drop mage gear. They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis. They have normal aggro range.

Female Forsaken Revanant

Undead Enchanter (Strippers) - (Level 49-51 - 10,250 HPs) - They look like female vampires and have high magic resistance, but they do not cast charm. They do cast a damage shield that must be canceled. Undead magic works.  They do not see rogues but do see normal invis. They have normal aggro range.

Ashenbone Drake

Undead Necromancer - (Level 51 - 15,750 HPs) - Looks like a skeletal dragon or a small DracoLiche from Fear. They have high magic resist and love to cast Fear.   They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis. They have an insane aggro range and have been known to aggro stuff across the zone. Be extremely careful on this one's aggro.

Thought Destroyer

Undead Warrior - (Level 55 - 18,000 HPs) - Looks like a scorn banshee.  Spawns after killing a certain Scorn Banshee and drops the breastplate that is used the the Paladin Epic Quest.  I have seen 4 of these spawn in one run.

Maestro of Rancor

Bard - (Level 53 - 32,000 HPs) -  He hits a TON and has an AOE lifetap on everything in melee range. And if he has dual weapons, he hits extremely fast. While fighting him, you must keep him pended in and away from the casters.  He normally drops 2 items, mostly Bard stuff, though a few monk items and a nice dropable shield. He sees rogues and all normal invis, but his back is usually to the door so no problem. He is the lesser boss of Hate. He will run near the end of the fight and will head toward the door of the Organ Hall.
