Welcome to Club Jester!!                

This site is designed to allow the members of Club Jester to post information about its members, its charter, special events and just plain old great stories.

The Jesters Club consist of a group of characters who believe that having fun together is better than adventuring alone.  That Everquest is about adventuring and friends.   That life is to short to waste it getting angry.  That making others happy feels better than making them sad.  That you can't make others happy, if you not happy yourself.  That Everquest isn't a bunch of rules glued together with silicon, its what we make it, and we want to make it fun.  There is only one prerequisite to joining the Jesters, you must either love spinning a good yarn, or enjoy listening to yarns told by others.
