so i was messing around last night with my little halfling down there..

from the docks in the city you can jump down to the very bottom. If you have enough gollem clay for 2 groups (with an enchanter) they can very easily clear the path for everyone else to jump down. There's about 4-5 mobs.. a roaming gollem and imp.. two stationary ele's by the water and i think a roaming ele.. that goes up and down the tunnel.

even without the clay, two groups can go down there and clear it but they just have to be quick to jump into action. The two stationary ele's are the closest to the pond. Then there's the imp who floats kinda nearby but may not be in agro range..

There's also maybe about 6-10 mobs that travel through the tunnel up to the main house. they are spaced out fairly well and there seems to be some good spots for the group to get prepared for the battles.

Once you get in front of the main house, (when i went with another guild for kindle and kern for me) we ivu into the main house.. and went up to kindle's room and sat far against the left wall. Only one of the revenants agro'd on us.. it was alot easier way to go then battling through there.

just wanted to give this info.
