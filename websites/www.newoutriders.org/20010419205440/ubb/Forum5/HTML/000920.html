some rules for fear:

NEVER use your attack button. always use your assist button and know who your assist is. pet classes pay special attention to what your pet is attacking. not following this rule religiously is the number one reason for the whole raiding party dieing.

SHOUT channel is reserved for the pull group.

if you are asked to camp e.g. when cazic gets uppity, then do so immediately. in the case of the camp being overrun the clerics will be asked to camp. others will only camp once the clerics are out. tanks will camp last and usually sacrifice themselves.

if you can feign death, always /exit after everyone else has camped and once the camp is clear. other classes can log onto alts and ask the monks and sks if the camp is clear.

do not enter the plane e.g. if late, unless you have been explicity intstructed to do so.

do not train the camp. get a loc and die instead.

NEVER roll unless you are explicitly told to do so. i have seen shiverback gloves rot because the person who won thought they lost to someone who was just joking around.

and HAVE FUN!

A few more final notes...

Please log somewhere in the Feerrott tonight if at all possible. The best choices are right outside the specter cave, right outside cazic thule, or at the druid ring. If you can't log in the Feerrott, please try to log out as close as possible to a druid ring for easy pickup before the raid groups are formed.

As Litewraith said, remember to assist, but make sure you don't hit the mob before the main assist does. Make sure they have a chance to taunt, snare, spell or whatever the mob to draw agro from the enchanters. If the enchanters die, everyone dies.

If you are any class with a symbol / rune spell, make sure you bring plenty of gems for your spells.

Make sure you keep the camp tight. This is especially true for the break-in group. The more spread out the group gets, the more adds will come.

Remember that if you get teleported out of the camp, or feared away, do not run back to camp. Turn around a few times, make SURE nothing is coming toward you or attacking you, then walk back to the camp. If you are getting attacked, or see something heading to attack you, get a location and die there.
