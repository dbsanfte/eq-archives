Branch Leader Mandate

These duties are in addition to the duties assigned to you in your office mandate:

1) Attend the weekly High Council meeting.  Present a weekly report, including, member count and current branch status.  Ensure that a substitute officer attends and presents the report, if you are unable to attend.

2) Supervise the branch Herald.

3) Carry out the duties assigned assigned to the branch leader, as described in the Guild Expansion Policy.
