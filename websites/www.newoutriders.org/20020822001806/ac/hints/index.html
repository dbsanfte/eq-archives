Asheron's Call Hints

Stamped Letter Quests

Since this is a new section, which will grow over time, it will start with a simple random list and grow into separate pages as it expands.

Use your time wisely in the game. When standing around in town or anywhere, you should be working basic skills which cost no money. Such as Item Identification skill which can be raised by examining your highest valued jewelry. This extra skill building gains you skill and xp while just standing around.

Examine a scroll to raise Arcane Lore skill. This is totally free and Arcane Lore is an important skill for all characters. Use this once every 5-10 minutes for best gain. Also, you can examine a healing kit to raise your healing skill, and examine lockpicks to raise your lockpicking skill.

Try to run from a battle with the Q autorun hotkey. It is more reliable than using the arrow keys, and can make the difference between life and 'find the body' hunts. Also the Q hotkey lets you chat or examine items while running.

Cast drain health/stamina/mana on yourself to help raise your Magic Defense skill. This will speed up the rate it increases, and you will start to see it become useful at about 75.
