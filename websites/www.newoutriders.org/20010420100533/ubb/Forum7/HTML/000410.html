OK, this is a little in advance of things, but we have a resource available to us on Xegony to allow our higher levels to take part in raids on several zones, including the planes.

There is a neutral organization on Xegony called the Xegony Alliance that organizes raids on places such as Kedge, Karnor's, and the Planes. All high levels are invited to participate as long as they follow their rules which are set up to be sure no one takes advantage of the organization and to keep unscrupulous people out.

So far everything looks cool and as soon as I can arrange to take part, I will and will let you all know. This is a great resource we can take advantage of. Any person of any guild is allowed to take part, so there is no compromise of NOR's historic neutral stance. This is not an alliance, simply a method whereby people with the same goals can get together and have fun with like minded people.

This should help solve some of the issues we have had in the past with higher levels leaving so they can take part in such activities. The URL is www.xegonyalliance.com.
