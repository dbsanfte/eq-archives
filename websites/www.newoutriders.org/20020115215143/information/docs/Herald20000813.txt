NOR Herald Mandate

1) Keep track of the members in your Duchy or Branch, including their current email address, their current rank, any awards, and the date the member joined NOR.  Send Update files to the HC Scribe on a regular basis. (At least once per month.)

2) Keep track of the promotions of members within your Duchy or Branch. To assist you, the members or their officers are to e-mail you when they are promoted or receive awards.

3) Any other scribbish tasks that the Duke/Duchess of your Duchy or branch leader may assign.

4) Maintain the Official Duchy web site.

5) Keep logs of the Open Session High Council meetings.

Note: The official duties mentioned above may be divided between several members as the Duke, Duchess or branch leader, may determine is necessary.

Approved by High Council August 13 2000
