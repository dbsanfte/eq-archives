Come get the official EQ board game!

Thats right folks. Step up to Moby Enterprises and for $9.99 you too
can be the proud owner of an Official Everquest Boardgame. Look here
what you will get:
(1)One playmat map of a room
(1)One rubber chicken
(6)Six Playing pawns
(10)Ten marbles
(1)One plastic sword
(1)One six sided die

RULES:
1) Players will set their pawns out on the playmat.
2) The "leader" will roll the die. The result is number of hours the
group waits for the big monster.
3) When the allotted time is reached the leader will place the rubber
chicken on the mat signifying the monster has come and combat
commences.


COMBAT
1) All people who are playing melee place their pawns around the
monster.
2) All casters are to sit 10 feet away from mat with the marbles
ready.
3) The players and the monster take turns with each player taking a
turn, then monster, player2, monster, player3, etc.
3) The meleers rolls a six sided die. A "1" is a hit. Anything else
is a miss.
4) The monster rolls a six sided die. A "1" is a miss. Anything else
is a hit.
5) The casters throw their marbles at the monster. If the marble hits
the monster, nothing happens. The monster is Magic Resistant.
6) If anyone has declared themself to be a cleric, they are allowed
to heal one player back to full health. Upon doing so they the cleric
pawn will be removed from play.
7) Each hit by a meleer does 10 pts of damage. Each hit by the
monster does 100 pts of damage.
8) Hit points are distributed as follows:
Melee class: 104 hit points
Caster class: 2 hit points
Cleric class: 1 hit point
Monster: 1007 hit points
9) If the party can slay the monster they get to "loot" for the
plastic sword by diving onto the mat and wrestling it from each
other.


VARIATION:
Due to the popularity of the plastic sword, you might want to make it
more interesting. Call several parties of players on the phone and
let them know you have the EQ Board Game and the coveted plastic
sword. Tell them the first party there gets to begin the dice roll
and waiting. If another party shows up and complains, go and get your
mom and let her decide who gets to stay.


Hurry, supplies are limited.

All times are PST (US)
