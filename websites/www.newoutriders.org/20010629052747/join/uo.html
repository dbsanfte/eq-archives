NOR Quiz

Below are few questions on the Philosophy of our Guild. Please take a moment and answer each of these questions. You will be contacted after we receive your answers about the next phase in joining the New OutRiders.

1. In your understanding, describe the membership requirements for potential recruits into the New OutRiders. (I.e. alignment, class, etc.)

2. The New OutRiders have a mission in Ultima Online. What is it and what do you think you can do to further the goals of the Guild?

3. Every guild needs to recruit to grow; describe a "good " potential recruit for NOR (i.e. someone you like to travel the Realms with)

4. The conduct of one player reflects back on his/her guild. NOR is a large guild with a fine reputation for helping out new players. What types of conduct do your fellow NOR use to ensure that, that is the impression they leave in UO?

5. The New OutRiders have a couple rules about Player Killing and handling encounters with Pkers… can you name any of them?

6. Your NOR Jacket is the screen name you use with the NOR prefix attached, what is the proper procedure in you see someone in game with a NOR Jacket that you are unfamiliar with? What do you do if he/she hasn't been squired yet?

7.  The Charter you received with your Recruitment packet tells you that you should always greet the other members of your guild with a phrase upon encountering them in game. What is the phrase?

8.  Sometimes you may run into a situation for which you are just not sure of what to do… that is fine, but do you know who to ask for help? Can you name your Branch Leader? Your Count(ess, or Baron(ess)? Any Knight in your Barony?

9.  Explain the significance of the High Council and it's role in NOR
