The NOR Charter, Standards & Conventions, Philosophy, Jacket Creed and the S&C Supplement for TR contain the answers to most of these questions. Please answer each question as best you can, and then click the Submit button below.

1.  In the Realm, a player may have many different character races, classes and alignments. In your understanding, describe the membership requirements for potential recruits into the New Outrider. Are there any alignment, race, or class restrictions in NOR? If so, what are they?"

2.  The New Outriders have two main missions in The Realm. What are they and
what do you think you can do to further the goals of the guild?

3.  The conduct of one player reflects back on his/her entire guild. What types of conduct should a member of NOR use to ensure their conduct leaves a positive impression in The Realm? How do they conduct themselves on Channel 47 and in public places?

4.  The New Outriders have a couple rules about Player-vs-Player combat. What are they and how should a member of NOR handle someone who wrongs them?

5.  What are the guilds rules concerning your alternate and non-NOR characters and how does associating them reflect on your NOR character?

6.  Your NOR Jacket is the screen name you use with the NOR prefix attached,
what is the proper procedure if you see someone in-game with a NOR jacket
that you are unfamiliar with? What do you do if he/she hasn't been inducted
yet?

7.  The Standards & Conventions tells you that you should always greet the other members of your
guild with a phrase upon entering The Realm. What is the phrase and how does
one go about finding the NOR Channel when you enter?

8.  Sometimes you may run into a situation for which you are just not sure
what to do...that is fine, but do you know who to ask for help? Can you name
your Realm Leader (RL), Duke(ess)? Your Count(ess)? Your Baron(ess)? Any one Knight in your barony? (For this question assume you are placed in the barony that sent you the recruiting package.)
