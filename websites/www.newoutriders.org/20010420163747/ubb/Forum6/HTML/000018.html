Author Topic:   NOR-EQ Chapter I: To All Things There is an Ending
Bard
 posted 03-26-2001 08:58 AM               
For years untold, the lands of NeverWinter were home to many folks of power and might. Some were good of heart, others malicious. Between these two factions the intrigues of the palace of NeverWinter and the beasts that roamed the lands, many an epic battle was fought. Lives were spared and lives were lost. This was a land of magic, a land of glory, and a land doomed to become a victim of it's own excesses.

The continual use of such powerful magicks on a land by so many folks for such a long period of time finally began to take it's toll. It began slowly at first, a few head of livestock missing from outer farms, a family gone from it's farm, a guil's scout disappearing. These, in and of themselves, weren't usually cause for alarm, such was the way of life in NeverWinter. What caught the eye of the wiser folk was that it was happening on all sides, and with n increasing frequency. The final event that quelled any doubts was the formation of the Nothing.

A great pitch-black cloud had appeared at the edges of the realm, completely surrounding it. Nothing that ventured in ever ventured back out. Nothing. In time, the cloud began slowly, ominously closing in on the lands. As the outer lands became engulfed, people began to flee towards the center, to NeverWinter, in the hopes that it's fabled walls could hold back the coming gloom.
