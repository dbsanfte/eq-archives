I did the quest. From what I hear you cannot get the boots by killing him.

It is not to bad. Just get the ring first. The Ancient Cyclops conned white to me at Lvl 31 both time that I have been around to kill him. The first time I had the help of some of NOR's finest. The second time I was with a buddy that was around lvl 35 and we had no trouble with him.

When you hear that Hasten is up (by whatever means you have available) go get a shadowed rapier from a Shadowed man. The rapier is no rent so you must get it at a time when you know Hasten is up. Go to the bank to get your 3250 gp and head to RM. I happened to be bound in RM so the heavy weight of the gold did not affect me to much. Turn in the ring, the gold and the rapier to Hasten. He s pretty easy to Hail despite what you may have heard.

I was also able to keep him longer than his 2hr spawn time. By hailing him over and over again. I did this for an hour while several buddies went to get a rapier and GP so that they could get their boots also.

This item is well worth the effort!
