The New OutRiders is a neutral guild which has two basic purposes; to have fun, and to help others to do the same.

NOR has members from all walks of life and backgrounds, all united with the common goal of having a good time and sharing the fun with others. NOR began on Sierra's ImagiNation Network© in the game 'Shadow of Yserbius' with only 18 members. Now, NOR has over 200 members across four different networks! Plus the hundreds of players who are retired from the guild, and tend to pop up in every game on the Internet!

No matter what the situation, you can always rely on a NOR brother or sister being there when you need them.
