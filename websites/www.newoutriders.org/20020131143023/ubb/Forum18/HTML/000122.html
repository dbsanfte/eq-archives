Skorj

I've usually hunted the water beetles until about 9. The beetle drop the best loot I've seen for that level, and don't seem to be social. There is, however, one "family group" of a Beetle and several Collectors that usually hangs out in the water sorta between the mudman huts and will ruin your day.

The only time I've ever seen multiple beetles join in a fight, other than that pre-programmed group, is when AE spells were used or multiple beetles were otherwise pulled with damage.

At about level 9 (maybe level 8 for a strong character like an enchanter), the fields behind Tir na mBeo become a good hunting ground, and a good place to find a group. The woods in that area hold Blackthourne-Lunaticshee groups which can be very dangerous.

Hope that helps some.
