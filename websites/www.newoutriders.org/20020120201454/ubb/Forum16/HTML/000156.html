The Mask of Deception is droppped by the Ghoul Assassin as well as the Serrated Bone Dirk (a nice lower level Rogue weapon). There is no way, that I know of, to improve your faction with the Dead side mobs of Lower Guk. But each time you kill a Dead side mob, your faction with the live side goes up so this is a plus. Also, the Ghoul Assassin sees through all invis's so unless someone is already camping the Ghoul Assassin, you will have to be prepared before you walk up into his room. The Mask is a nice item for Rogues and Bards. It allows us to become DE's and walk around in evil cities so you don't have to go so far to sell/bank sometimes
