Basic Information about The New OutRiders Guild

The New Outriders is a neutral guild which has two basic purposes; to have fun, and to help others to do the same. NOR has members from all walks of life and backgrounds, all united with the common goal of having a good time and sharing the fun with others. NOR began on Sierra's ImagiNation Network(c) in the game 'Shadow of Yserbius' with only 18 members. Now, NOR has over 250 members across four different networks! If you are interested in joining NOR, please take a few minutes and fill out the Online Quiz for the server you play on.

NOR is organized into Duchies, each with a Duke/Duchess as it's leader. In overall control of all Duchies is the High Council, the governing body of NOR. Each Duchy is consequently divided into Counties, led by a Count/Countess, with the number of Counties dependant on the size of the Duchy. Each County is further divided into Baronies, led by a Baron/Baroness, each with about 10 members. In addition to ones normal gameplay activities, NOR members are expected to be helpful to those less knowledgeable or less fortunate in the game. When joining NOR, all members swear to 'Defend the weak, and protect the innocent.' NOR members are forbidden from participating in anti-social activities, as these tend to limit the enjoyment of other players, such as thieving and PlayerKilling (PK) unless in self-defense or in the defense of another.

NOR is a social guild and members form close ties online and in the mundane world. No matter what the situation, you can always rely on a NOR brother or sister being there when you need them.
