Some of you may not know this, but I am an old fart of *NOR/ myself

I began my time with NOR on INN. That was before NWN. The guild, as I knew it, was on about 5 of the different Yserbius servers you could go into and you didn't have to create a new character if you moved from one server to another. All your stats and gear remained whichever location you went in to. I used to play and joined the guild in RolePlay Inn or RPI as it was called

I remember when Coffeeman was my Duke and he invited me to join, I think it was about 1994 or 1995.... boy how time flies *giggle* I felt so pround because I believed, and still believe, in the way NOR worked. I am still proud to be a member of this guild. I have always considered NOR an extension of my family and really enjoyed going to my first bash last year and meeting alot of you for the first time.

I remember knowing Fandrall before he was Duke Fandrall, knowing Lord Edu and Lord Avatar on INN and getting to play with them in game. Let me not forget Lady Taggarty, sweet Isis/Helle/Selkyee, DavisPrime, Punky, Roxy, Zifnab and others. All of them I called, and still call friends or family

Of course, there were those that we had join the guild and turned out not to be *NOR/ material... anyone remember Sith? *snicker*

I've come and gone from *NOR/ because of games coming and going, but I've always been proud to be a part of the guild. Proud to call each and everyone of you my friend and I can truly say I am proud to shout out "Hail *NOR/" whenever I see someone in the zone I am in *blush* *biggrin*

Hail *NOR/!!!!

See you ingame everyone!!!
