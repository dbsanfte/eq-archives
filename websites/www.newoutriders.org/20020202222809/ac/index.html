Asheron's Call Branch

The New OutRiders has an allegiance tree on the Frostfell server, giving all guild members and their alternate characters the Monarch of 'New Outriders'. We plan to grow the tree in an orderly fashion to enable every member to achieve a good allegiance rank, while sharing xp at the same time. We are a neutral guild, and help others out on a regular basis, so the Patron / Vassal relationship should not be a major issue to our prospective new members. New members will have everyone in the New Outriders allegiance tree to help them when possible, so NOR is like having many patrons.

We have regular group outings and complete one or more major quests together every week. We have member who have been in AC since final beta, and have many members with mature characters. Our support characters are taking care of business of course. From fletching help to creating atlan weapons, NOR members will always help you achieve your gaming goals!

Interested in Joining NOR? Use our online form to let us know!

Lord WilhelmG is in charge of NOR in Asheron's Call.
