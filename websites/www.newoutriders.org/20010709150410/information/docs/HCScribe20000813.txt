NOR High Council Scribe Mandate

1) Update the NOR Master Roster, from information provided you by the Herald of each Branch.     Communicate with Branch Leaders and Heralds, as necessary, to keep an up-to-date Master Roster. 

2) Distribute mails and documents to the herald of each duchy at the request of High Council.

3) The Scribe of the High Council is authorized to request rosters from the herald of each duchy.

4) Perform any related task that the High Council may assign.

Note: The official duties mentioned above may be divided between several members as the High Council may determine is necessary.

Approved by High Council August 13 2000
