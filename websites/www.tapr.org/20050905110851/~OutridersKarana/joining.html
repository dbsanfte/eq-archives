Outriders of Karana - Joining

  1. Any member can nominate someone for admittance to the guild, buy submission to the Guild Leader.
  2. Once the potential member is reviewed by the officers, they are put up for seconds by the membership.
  3. After the first 2 steps are taken, the nominee becomes a prospective member.
  4. Prospective members will then have to group with and spend time with as many other guild members as they can and not just those around their level.
  5. After a time set by the guild officers, the guild members who have had contact with the nominee must give their input via the guild list about him/her.
  6. After all information on their play style, personal demeanor, etc is gathered, the Officers will again review the nominee.
  7. After the nominee has been approved, the sponsor of the nominee must approach the Nominee and ask whether they still want to join our ranks.
  8. On an acknowledgement of their interest, a Guild Officer will then explain in detail the guild rules. Some of these include:
    1. We don't talk about guild matters to outside people
    2. The use of Guildsay not to be used to bitch about whatever.
    3. The use of the guild list to air gripes and bitch sessions and whatever else.
    4. The need to put the guild members, no matter the level, above any outsiders.
    5. and a few others.
  9. At this point an officer finds the nominee and guilds them -- with much rejoicing to commence.
