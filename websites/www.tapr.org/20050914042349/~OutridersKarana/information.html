Outriders of Karana - Information

The Outriders of Karana is an eclectic group of players who were brought together in the pursuit of one thing; fun. The mission of each member of this guild is simple: Have fun and attempt to create an enjoyable environment for your fellow guild members. Each of us plays in our own unique way and can bring something different to the proverbial table, some are excellent role players, others have great skill at slaying beasts, a few can tell a great story or simply talk your ear off.

If you look at a guild with the mindset "Its just a guild" its not much. However if you look at a guild as a group of your good friends and extended family you will enjoy your playing experience more. Friends, trust and fun are worth far more then any item, title or level.
