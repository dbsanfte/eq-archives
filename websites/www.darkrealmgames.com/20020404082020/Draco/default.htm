Thursday, April 26th, 2001:

CoM is back! And for good!

Last evening while in Overthere a GM contacted me and inquired as to if I it was ok to re-activate the guild to Kuahara. It seems that Kuahara after selling the guild to Mallian that he started to remove those that Mallian added, and ended up removing himself and contacted a GM to get it back. Since the guild was registered to me the GM asked my permission. Of course I said no and that I'd like control of it again.

Radical, Cummel, and Daxmar are now officers, and will be trying to get all our old membership back, in the future we'll have a few changes, such as a level requirement, and a required application process to enter the guild.

If I ever have to leave the guild will go to Radical, if Radical or I leave him and I will choose someone. This guild must live, it has been here for several years and has a rich history of some of the greatest players in Fennin Ro taking part in it.

Peace to all my brothers and sisters of EQ.

Dax
