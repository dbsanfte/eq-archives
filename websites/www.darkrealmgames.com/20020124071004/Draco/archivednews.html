Sunday, January 28th, 2001:

I updated the events page, however make sure you check it when ever you hit the site, since there may be updates that you are not aware of.

Thursday, January 11th, 2001:

I have added a new link for a Mana Calculator in hopes that this will add to the functionality of the web site, as well as add value to your time spent on the guild page.

Currently we have two events running the guild Lottery and the guild Item Raffle. I have been told that there are several that have entered each however I am waiting for the full information to come in to post the results of those that entered to show the ammount in the Lottery pot and how many raffle holders there are for the cloak. I wish you all luck in both these things. 

Friday, December 15th, 2000:

I decided it was about time we moved to a new URL format and links since I have already started to change the site to the new guild name we will have in the future, the new URL will be http://DarkRealmGames.com/Draco. The old URL of course shall work for a time until you all get used to this... the advantage is less to type, less to remember, and just simply easier. Thank you all for your patience as I slowly go through pages and making minor changes here and there. 

Thursday, December 14th:

I went on an adventure to the great waterfall that covers the entrance to Thuragin (spelling could be wrong) and decided it was so amazing I had to take a screan shot, of course it looks better in game, but you get the idea:

 

The actual clarity of the view is so amazing, I wish that I could have gotten a .gif or .jpeg of it instead of converting from a .bmp... but again you really should just use this as an example of what I saw, not as it really truly looks.

Saturday, December 9th:

Well we are still waiting for the new guild name of "Draco Draconum" to be available for us. The GM staff at Verant told us that due to the Scars of Velious release everything is taking more time then normal, however we did send an email to Verant to hopefully get some additional movement on this.

When the move to the new guild name takes place you will be contacted individually online by members of the guild and told when it happens.

Also I will be re-building this web page very soon to reflect not only the new guild name, but to have a new look, so if you have ideas please post those on the web site.

For those also who do not know KoFR is no longer a "sister guild" that idea has been decided against... we are just waiting for Draco Draconum instead.

Take care all,

Mid

Friday, November 10th:


Lots of new exciting things have happened to CoM, most recently we built a new guild called Kingdom of Fennin Ro which has the purpose of giving our CoM members a place to put their "Tanks" such as Warriors, Monks, and Rogues while still being tied to CoM. So if you see someone with a Kingdom of Fennin Ro guild tag they are basically Casters of Magic members.

I would also like to thank Josue for the construction of the KoFR (Kingdom of Fennin Ro) web site.

- Be Safe

-Thacs, Mid, Enchantings, Daxmere, Daxmaer, Smashemupgud

Thursday, October 19th:


The guild has had some great new members added, and the direction we are heading as a group is going well. We are starting to reach the point of being able to hold regular guild events, the first one will be this next Saturday in Befallen Dungeon on the first floor; Befallen can be reached from West Commons. The time of the meeting will be 11am PST, on Saturday. The guild will gather for around 30 minutes, roughly till 11:30am in which we will continue farther into the dungeon. The event will last 30 minutes to one hour after the meeting time length, all members are to be sure that everyone that isn't able to leave on their own is able to get out, and no one is left with a body they can't recover. We will also use this day to add several new officers and hopefully a few new members.

- Good Luck

-Thacs, Mid, Enchantings, Daxmere

Saturday, June 17th:
Currently we have started a new event, a guild raffle! Please see the raffle page for current information.

The Guild Event in High Pass Hold had a LOT of members show up, but due to the level of the orcs here many were unable to stay as long as they had wished too. Hopefully next time there won't be this problem, but hey there will always be next event! So prepare and get ready to bash, slash, nuke, and burn.
