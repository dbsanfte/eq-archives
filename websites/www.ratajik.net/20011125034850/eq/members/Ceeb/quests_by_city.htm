This entire quest takes place inside of Crushbone. First thing that you need to do is go see the dwarven smith. He is located in the small building that is near the mine area. To get here from the entrance you must take a left and go across the bridge. Once you cross the bridge just basically follow the path. After you go down the hill you will see a building. Inside this building is a jailor who you will have to kill. Then you will see two dwarven smiths and an elven priest. Talk to the middle dwarven smith(actually you can probably talk to either smith but I always spoke to the one in the middle, so for safety purposes speak to him). Tell him that you are here to set him free and he will begin rambling about how he wants his freedom but the taskmaster scares them, yada yada yada. Then ask him about the king or emperor and he will speak of the high quality ring mail that is worn by this beast.

There are several parts of this quest but you really only need to concentrate on the final two(bracer of battle and dwarven ringmail). Each piece that you find can either be kept or turned in for the item that it returns depending on your class, race, and needs.

The first mob that you might want to worry about is the Taskmaster. He hangs out to the right of the castle near the slaves. He seldom spawns but when he does he has a chance of carrying some magic +2 AC earrings. You can either wear these earrings(for the armor class, although I feel that the onyx earrings are a better choice) or you can return them to the dwarven smith. If you return them to him he will give you a blade mold which can be used for blacksmithy.

The second mob in this quest is the Instructor/Trainer. This is a large orc who can be found training other orcs on top of the large hill in the middle of the zone. He very rarely carries a shiny brass shield. You can elect to keep this weapon(a wise choice or a poor choice depending on your class) or you can turn it in to the dwarven smith in exchange for a sludge mold.

The third item that you will need is the one that will be the most dangerous. You now need to kill the Emporer/King and hope that he is sporting the dwarven ringmail tunic. If you are a small race you might think about keeping this item to wear on your person until you get a second one. However you can turn this item in to the dwarven smith as with the items above. This time he will give you a dwarven mace which has a damage of 8 and an attack delay of 35. Not a bad weapon, however it's just a stepping stone. The smith will also send you to go speaking to the elven priest standing in the next stall. The priest can enchant this mace for you(and turn it into the screaming mace) but first you need to do something for her. She wants you to kill the Warlord and bring proof of his death.

The fourth item that you need is the hardest one to get. You need to find the Warlord. He can be seen patrolling the area from time to time. He is a level 14 or so mob, but that isn't the problem. The problem is if you attack him he is going to run and bring help. So you must be careful to root him in place and then try to take care of him before root runs out. If you are lucky he will be carrying a bracer of battle. This is a fine piece of armor for this level, however it can also be turned in. This time you will turn the bracer into the elven priest rather than the dwarven smith. The priest will give you a prayer cloth in return for the bracer.

Many players get to this step and think, what? What did I do wrong. Well it is important to NOT give the priest the mace at the same time as the bracer of battle. You need to give her the bracer alone first. Then after she hands you the prayer cloth you can give her the mace. When she asks, "do you have something else for me?" then you can hand over the prayer cloth and voila you get one shiny screaming mace.

Ask him about his wolf armor and he will fill you in on details regarding the quality and cash requirements for each piece.
  Chanda Miller is an NPC who frequents Qeynos Hills near the zone to Surefall Glade. She can often be found near the campfire. Chanda can make you bear hide armor if you provide her with a bear pelt and a cash compensation. She will not talk to you unless you tell her that her brother(Baobob) sent you. Ask her about her bear hide armor and she will fill you in on details regarding the quality and cash requirements for each piece.
  This is the quest involving Gnasher Furgutt (level 8 or 9 gnoll in Qeynos Hills). Kill him, take his head and the note on the corpse to surefall glade, and give to the ranger behind the guild (standing by himself, his name is Larsk Juton) give him the head. He will give you faction/experience/cash. Then when you give him the note he gives it back to you and tells you to go see Captain Tillin in S. Qeynos (same guy you give gnoll fangs to). Give Capt Tillin the note and he gives you cash and an Amulet of +5 charisma, then he calls the executioner to go kill the guy that was dealing with the gnolls. You can follow her and watch the execution if you want but its not necessary.

Rivervale

Class Task(s)
 

In Misty Thicket, if you talk to Blixxin Entopop (he wanders around with a little klicknic trailing him named Ember), he will tell you about his bug collection.  If you ask him about it, he gives you the opportunity to make one yourself, and gives you a Bug Collection Box.  If you ask him, he will give you the list of things that go in the bug collection.

These things are: Fire Beetle Eye, Spiderling Eye, Spiderling Leg, Giant Fire Beetle Leg, Giant Wasp Wing, Giant Scarab Egg Sac.  If you get all 6 things in the Bug Collection Box and hit combine, you get the complete Bug Collection.  Give your Bug Collection to Blixxin Entopop for your reward.
  Nillipuss is an irritating little guy who roams around the city of Rivervale. His level ranges in the high teens so he will be a worthy adversary. However when you kill him he will often drop an item. One of these items is a jumjum stalk. He has stolen these items from vendors in Rivervale. You can return this to the vendor and receive a faction adjustment.
  This quest originates in Rivervale at the pier near the lake. Near the pier or right inside of the shop you will find Fiddy Bobbick. Tell him that Wickystick sent you. He will inform you that the lake is infested with Pirahna. For every pirahna you kill you will receive a pirahna tooth. You can return this tooth to him for a cash compensation as well as a faction adjustment.
  This quest originates from Reebo Leafsway in Rivervale. Shakey the Scarecrow has two problems, first he needs some stuffing and second he needs a new head. The head part I can't help you with(other than to point you to Erudin) the stuffing can be found off the Scarecrows in West Karana.
  The squad ring quest originates in Rivervale. Visit the castle like building in town. Walk up to Marshal Ghobber and tell him that you are new to his squad. He will send you to report to Budo in Misty Thicket. Budo can be found in the tower on the left of the entrance to the wall. Budo will inform you that you can return deathfist slashed belts to him(for a nice faction adjustment) and runneye(goblin) warbeads to Sheriff Roglio in town. After many(and I mean a bunch) of battles here you can return to Marshal Ghobber and ask him if you are part of the wall. If you have killed enough then he will reward you with a shiny new Squad Ring.

Surefall Glade

Class Task(s)
  Return poacher heads to Gillarian Naelev in Surefall.  Dont know what the reward is.
  In Surefall Glades, in the rangers guild, there is a guy standing next to the guy who is upset about his son being eaten by a bear. Hail him and he will say something about needing an errand run for him. He'll give you a claim receipt and tell you to take it to the wooden weapons maker in South Qeynos. When you deliver this claim receipt to the weapons maker he will give you a wooden totem carving of his daughter, or his deity or something, when you get this, take it back to him in Surefall Glades and he'll give you exp and some gold. You also get exp when you turn in the claim receipt. This is something for the newbie rangers to do to get a little cash and some exp.
