If people are looking for a "fresh newbie server" then I would also recommend Tholuxe Paells, where I just started a new character myself. The reasons are evident.

- You join a server where the MAJORITY of the server population is still well under the teen levels.

- The server is not crowded.

- You dont have to compete for spawn sites.

- There are (yet) no arrogant uber-guilds romping around and hogging the best sites or intimidating everyone else. Give it time and they will eventually arise.

- Easier to find a bunch of groupmates at YOUR level. The faster you make friends at the lower levels, the better.

Of course, there are disadvantages to being on a young server, but I will leave that discussion for another time.
