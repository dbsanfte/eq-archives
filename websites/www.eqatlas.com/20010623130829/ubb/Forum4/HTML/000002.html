First off do not stay in FV to hunt. To many very high level creatures wondering about. Instead follow the river to the Lake of Ill Omen. The Sabertooth canines sell for 1pp+ each. I hunt in that zone all the time so if you have any questions let me know.

Shivv
Adventurer 		posted 08-25-2000 04:45 PM            
You can hunt in Lake of Ill omen at that level. Hike up along the river, and then head west, till you come to the lake, then head NNE. You should see these large towering columns, which marks the newbie areas from the mid teens area. There are goblin hunters (around lvl 9ish and social), scalebone skeletons around lvl 9 or so. Iksar bandits around 11. Plenty of things to kill.

Better yet, get a young iksar to guide you there. Once there, you can head to the goblin hunter ruins and the scalebone ruins. They are good hunting for those levels. The GREAT thing about LoIO, is that you can solo on the cats (range in level from 2-4 and cover every level up to 22), pretty safely, as they are not social -- nor are the KoS, though the con scowls. They also carry coin, which is strange for an animal. Also, there are undead mobs, that range from lvls 7 all the way to mid 40s (elsewhere in Kunark). A cleric could fight nothing but undead here in Kunark until he is planes ready

I agree with a few of the suggestions; the Pit in the Field of Bone I feel is the best cause there's stuff in there from around lvl 6 to 18ish, and you can keep 'em comming constantly (if you're like me and don't like to wait for people to med). Big plus: there's always people around around to help. Big minus: like stated above, can't bind in the city (unless everyone has invis to sneak in), and to my knowledge there are no non-Iksar vendors nearby.

The person was correct about the Lake zone, you will be able to find plenty of things that match your ability near Cabilis (past the ruins) and unlike FoB, guards no not patrol out here. From the river from FV, head right by the Lake, and where you need to turn is just a short ways up. Head NE from the Lake, this will take you towards Cabilis and past those ruins. If you're not heading NE, you're going the wrong way.

I think it will be next to impossible to get to the Field of Bone from FV. You need to be invis to go from the Lake zone through the cave to the Warslik Woods zone. It's fine if you have the invisibility spell, but if you get lost, you're trapped in a dungeon of mobs in the 20+ range that will kill you when you appear.

Head west from the FV zone all the way to the other zone wall (because I don't know how far west you're supposed to go before you can go North East through the canyon). Don't go too far north east, because you're heading towards Cabilis when you go this way. However, it will take you to creatures that are blue to yellow when you con them.
