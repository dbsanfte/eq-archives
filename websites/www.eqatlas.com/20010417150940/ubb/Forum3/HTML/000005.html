Najib,
I'll assume you're refering to the catacombs beneath Karnor's. Yes it can be handled by a single grp of low to mid 50s. It must be a skilled grp, however, with no panic-ee types. Puller should be a monk (though I, a ranger, have sucessfully pulled a lot down there). Cleric and Enc are required. Pulls, if down with care can be kept to singles, pairs and an occasional 3some. DO NOT enter room with Verix unless you are prepared and know what you are doing. If you agro Verix, evac or take the death. If you run to zone and make it, Verix will follow, agro many at zone, gate back to his room and summon all to their deaths. To clear a camp spot, have grp park on ledge just above the water tunnel entrance. Have puller clear water around tunnel, then tunnel, then hall, then first room to right inside. Move grp in and have fun. Loot and exp are excellent. What I like best about the cataombs is that there are no trains If I have a tight grp, we have a great time.
