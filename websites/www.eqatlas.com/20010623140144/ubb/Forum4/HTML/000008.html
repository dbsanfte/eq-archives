Adventurer 		posted 10-17-2000 07:54 AM            
I brought my druid there at 22, and leveled quickly to 29 in Lake of Ill Omen. That zone has level 1 to 40 mobs in it and is arranged somewhat is areas per level.

I started an Iksar Necro and have her hunting in the newbie area, but there are iksar guards there and should only be hunted by iksar characters.

I see levels from about 18 and up to 30 looking for groups in Lake of Ill Omen. The only problem is getting there from Fiorna Vie. You have to run from town, across the bridge, take a left and follow the shore until you get to a river and follow the river until it turns into a water fall and follow up the side of water fall. Along the river are these big ugly part spider part necro (or druid maybe) things that will kill you if you are not careful. I ran my druid through the first time without invis and was promptly killed, luckily I bound to FV prior to the run. I ran her through with invis and the spiders regarded her indifferently, but I didn't get close to them just to be sure.

I brought my barbarian shaman over this weekend and she is 24. I will start leveling her there in sarnak groups.

I recommend Lake of Ill Omen very highly for leveling quickly.

Also, for those warriors and enchanters out there, there are always groups looking for your classes in that zone.
