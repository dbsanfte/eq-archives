Hail friends,
Let me begin by commending all these stout folk who wander away from the conforts of home to embrace the world at a young age.

My travels have been closer to my home city, Freeport, though more thorough. Although this account is of my travels to the continent of Faydwer. I befriended a high-level druid and secured a port to Greater Faydark. Subsequently we parted ways for he was on his way to the Dwarven city of Kaladim. My journey was much darker, for I was destined to enter the Orc stronghold of Crushbone in order to fulfill my Monk quest and obtain my Orange Sash of Order. I had a decent time running towards the fair tree city of Kelethin and made my way to what is called the Orc Lift. It is here where I secured what is lovingly called a "bind". Also, this is where things got interesting. As I got to the Orc encampment I quickly liquidated several of those foul smelling dregs for the simple fun of killing those abominations.

I quickly made my way to the mouth of the Crushbone stronghold and joined in the fray the instant I zoned. I later came to the stronghold itself where there was much room for deeds of valor and reknown (and sometimes foolishness). Alas, the lord of the Stronghold came out along with his ambassador and emissary and countless legionnaires and I fell by the moat, but not before I smote several of those foul "leggos", as they are hatefully called.

The Tranquil One breathed new life into my broken body and I resurrected at the Orc Lift, where I was last bound. Thence, I returned to the place of my fall and I recovered my possessions. I stayed for several days at Crushbone dealing out vengeance with a group of like-minded adventurers but then we parted ways and I called on another Druid friend to port me back to the Western Commonlands. My return to Freeport was a happy one for I had fulfilled my quest and acquired the Orange Sash.

Here ends my account. Good hunting and may the Tranquil light your paths.
