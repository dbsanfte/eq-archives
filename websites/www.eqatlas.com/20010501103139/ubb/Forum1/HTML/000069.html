For those of you who are interested in raising your faction with Cabilis, this is what I know.

By killing lots, don't ask me how many, I didn't count, Sarnaks, Gobs and Iksar, of the renegade variety, you will raise your faction with Troopers that guard Cabilis. Not the Crusaders like some people have suggested. After becoming dubious with the Troopers I was able to sneak, without invis, through Cabilis with my monk. And let me tell you this. Don't do it. It is not a time saver.

In order to raise your faction with everything else in Cabilis, including the Crusaders, you must do the Bone Chip quest which is given by Trooper Mozo in Field of Bone. Turn in stacks of 4 bonechips and you raise faction with ALL factions in Cabilis.

There could be some other MOB that I don't know of that raises faction with Crusaders of Greenmist, which are the Crusaders which guard Cabilis, but I don't know about it.

I hope this is helpful.
