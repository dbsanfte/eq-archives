More on Map Usage
Thursday, June 1st

I'm done with my hell month (finally) and hopefully I'll be in a better mood...

With that in mind, I wanted to clarify what I stated about map usage. Printing up my maps for personal use only is perfectly okay. That's what they're there for. Linking to any of my pages is fine, so if people are linked to me, that's okay, I encourage it.

Hopefully all of this will be viewable on my new server soon, there have been some delays getting the DNS to work correctly (didn't I predict that?), so hopefully by the weekend it will be right. In the next few weeks, I hope to add some improvements to the site, as well as clean up some lingering issues (like certain half dozen missing maps from the original game), in preparation for my book (yes, it's still brewing, but real-life takes precedence).

I'm looking for any information on zones and locations tied to the history of Norrath and the EQ world. Little tidbits and where you found them are exactly what I'm looking for (e.g. the Deserts of Ro used to be the ancient elven homeland before they left for Faydwer). Send it in.

The Use of My Maps
Friday, May 26th

I never thought I would have to restate this on my page, but it is apparent that people aren't listening. I do not allow anyone to post my maps on their pages for any reason. Please check out my Maps Usage Statement for more information regarding this.

I've spent all morning sending letters to people who are apparently ignorant of this. Some people seem to think that this is somehow the sincerest form of flattery. It's not, it's theft. I don't spend hours making these maps only to have people download them, edit them, and repost them, often with no credit given as to where they got them from. In case you can't tell, this has really gotten under my skin, and I'm done being tolerant.

To that end, if anyone has ever seen my maps posted anywhere in any form other than at this site, those places are in violation of my copyrights. Please contact me so that I can inform them of such. I want to spend my free time playing, not policing the crappy web sites of a bunch of punks who spend their time mooching off of people who actually do work. And yes, I'm a bit cranky today... 8)
