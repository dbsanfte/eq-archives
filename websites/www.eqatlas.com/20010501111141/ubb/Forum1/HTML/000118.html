"Role-Playing" is the concept of acting out the part of the character your playing. If your playing a Dark Elf, your generally a mean spirited individual bent on others distruction. If your a Wood Elf, your one with nature, at home with the forest. If your human, your an average joe either a goody goody, a real a-hole or something in between.

These are, mind you, gereralizations, it's how the races are perceived in other Fantasy games. There are exceptions if you want to play a Dark Elf good guy, hey Drizzt did it, why not you?

The main concept is, pick a Race/Class that would suit your style. Then tailor a persona around that. There are no rules on how you should run your character. A great example is a character who posts over at Knight Watch, Starflower a Human SK Valley Girl. It's an absolute riot reading her posts and background story.

The main differance would be someone who plays their part, actually makes an attempt to get in the game and PLAY their character. The other would be the person who runs around with 6 different characters with as much diversity as a Keano Reaves character...
