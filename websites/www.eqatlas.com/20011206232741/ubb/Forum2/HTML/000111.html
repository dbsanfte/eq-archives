Ok, here is a good way to go there. first, get on the boat in Oasis of Marr, and ride it to the ogre island and get off. Head across the island and get on those docks and wait for the big boat to come. Get on this and it will take you to a dark elf camp in Overthere. You can bind yourself in this outpost, and it is only 2 zones away from LOIO. Now, head out from the Outpost and head eas, following the shore until you hit the zone wall. Once you hit the zone wall, head south. You will go south for sometime, then you will see many people hunting in a crtain area with a big canyon right on the zone wall, this is the Zone line to Frontier Mountains. Zone into Frontier mountains and run to LOIO, I dont know the exact directions through FM, so you might have to ask someone in the zone at the time to take you to LOIO zone line. That way is probly your best bet. I know there is a place to bind in FM but not sure where it is, anyone know? well I hope I helped ya out!

Ok I'll pick up from where he left ya. When you get to FM go left, hug that wall! If you just do that you'll come to the ill omen zone, I dont know the exact LOC but as long as you are close to that wall and the first part of the number is -400ish, just head left and you'll see another canyon like thing and keep goin threw that and into Ill Omen. Now that you are in Lake of Ill Omen you just run to the right a little until you can see the lake. Just run along the shore on the right side of the lake until you come to some ruins(big statues of iksars) once you get to those head right and you'll se a big ol tower/mill/rocketship that crash-landed looking thing. This is the Mill. As a rouge you can't bind here, you can't get a bind here. The only reason I mentioned the mill is cause at 17 thats where ya wanna hunt from. There is a spot in FM that you can get bound but it isn't a spot you wanna be bound in. Its behind a giant fort, near a bunyi mine, and in a zone with very agressive goblins, apes, giants, and burynei. So running strait threw this zone isn't a very safe or smart idea. Check out the map in the maps section for the loc.
