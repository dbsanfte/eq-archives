Description

The Crystal Caverns are the ancient home of the Coldain, long since overrun by giants and then orcs. It now lies mostly empty save for the orcs and other horrible creatures that call it home. There are three separate areas within the tunnels, each controlled by either the Ry'Gorr orcs, the geonids, or the Coldain dwarves. The orcs control the upper parts of the tunnels, the dwarves the lower parts, and the geonids the very bottom.

Dangers

There is a confusing pit area near the entrance which allows access to all other parts of the zone, but is also very confusing and can lead you astray. Although you can fall or levitate down through the pit and skip much of the upper dungeon, it is adviseable to do this while invisible or you will have quite the greeting party waiting for you at the bottom.

Hunting Queen Dracnia is also one of the more dangerous ventures in the dungeon. There is a small area near where she spawns that most groups set up in, and a group in the high 30's can do well there fighting rapidly spawning blues. However, there appears to be a timed spawn/aggro effect in this area, where just before the Queen spawns the spiders stop attacking, but immediately after she spawns, the room is rushed by spiders. Since the flee route is in the direction of the Queen's chamber, this can lead to some deadly consequences when the Queen, who is about lvl 42, aggros after the runner.

Benefits

There is an entire Coldain dwarf area of this caverns where you can shop and bank. The only problem is that they are initially dubious and so won't sell to most people right away.

Travelling To and From Crystal Caverns

The Crystal Caverns are reached through a teleporter located in a small hut at the back end of Ry'Gorr Keep in the Eastern Wastes. The teleporter appears to be a small snowball. Ry'Gorr Keep is near -3435, -465. Upon return to the Eastern Wastes, you either appear in a small tower just outside the Keep or near the snowball, depending on which exit you use out of the Caverns.
