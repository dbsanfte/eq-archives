By now you're probably level 20, but in case anyone else needs to know, you can buy some cheap armor from the merchants in the Field of Bone. As for a robe, buy yourself a tailoring kit and kill spiders and loot EVERY silk you get. Combine them 2 at a time unstacked. You should have at least 20 silk swatches by the time you get your skill to around 10. Go buy some tunic patterns from Klok Wartol in East Cabilis. He's near the Swamp of No Hope entrance. Put in 1 pattern and 2 silk swatches. Make sure you stack NOTHING. You'll get the message "you've created something new!" or "you lacked the skills to combine the selected items.". You'll get a nice raw silk robe. They sell to merchants for 6 gold each and make nice cash even for me at level 35. Hope this helps. (I'm not saying 6gp is a lot but when you sells 200 robes it is)

Well, there aren't any monk-usable robes in any merchant's regular inventory. You either have to tailor one (only monk-usable tailored robe is a raw silk robe, see above post on how to make one) or buy one from another player. If acutions in Cabilis don't work, try auctioning, or listen to the already existing auctions, in LOIO, which is pretty much Kunark's version of Faymart. Other zones in Kunark to try are Overthere or Firiona Vie; non-iksar uses these as cities and often auction from there. Be sure to verify the robe is monk-usable. Many caster robes are. My personal favorite, and a popular one among monks, is the crystal covered shroud; 12ac 4str 4wis 4int 10sv cold, wt 1.0, good ac for its weight (sells for around 400pp). But they are many others, depending not only on what stats you want, but on the appearance you want, many caster robes have very unique looks to them, and quite a few can be used by monks as well as casters.
