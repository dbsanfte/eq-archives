Try Crushbone in Greater Fay, there's some decent loot and exp there. You could also take the boat to Freeport and head south through Ro to Oasis of Marr and go croc and caiman hunting, not much loot at all but great exp. There's also Orc highway good loot and exp. And you could also go to Lake of Ill Omen, make sure to join group w/ good healer and get bind at FV. Rock on!
