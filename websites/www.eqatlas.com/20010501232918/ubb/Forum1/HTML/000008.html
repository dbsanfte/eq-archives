vizco
Adventurer 		posted 08-23-2000 05:20 AM               
I've failed fourteen times at skill levels from 108 up to current 131. Sigh. Tips I've gotten include:

1) Buf up your WIS (will raise skill faster). INT allegedly increases success probability, WIS skill increase probability. You can buy cheap +WIS items for a ten or more point level increase (+3 jasper earrings and rings, savant's cap, etc.), total expenditure under 100pp. Skill level, according to reasonable tests, has much much more effect than stats, so buf WIS to increase skill level faster.

2) Keep your bowyering book open while making it (and your fletching book open while fletching). No confirmation of this rumor, but "people say" it increases success prob a bit. No cost, so why not do it?

3) I suggest collecting as many copies of the "materials" as you can carry (buy extra backpacks). One long camp for micro servos is easier than several short ones (for me at least). Also, the more Kiola nuts you can cram into containers the less often you have to take that boat ride. (There is enough time to buy 30 of them during the brief boat stop).
