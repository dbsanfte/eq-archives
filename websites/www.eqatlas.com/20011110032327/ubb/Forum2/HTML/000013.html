Blackburrow is excellent.... for getting run over by trains at level 12... Also the gnolls will run away from you, and if that Root is resisted you could have alot more than just one Gnoll to contend with..

Again, (I sound like a broken record) one of the best places to hunt is in the Everfrost tundra. There are guards close to the tundra edge in case the MOBs are overpowering you, and the leopards, Snow Orc troopers, and Mammouth Calves are all perfect for level 12. Also if you can get a group of sufficient size and power you can even take Ice Bone Skeletons, Snow Orc Mountaineers and Shaman for some really kick ass exp and loot... I was in EF constantly from level 9 to 14.... When I wasn't off exploring the rest of the continent...

Also never discount the Wisps, they are good hunting until almost 16.... They are most plentiful in Erud's Crossing and in West Commonlands, and the loot they drop just can't be beat... I have a backpack full of Greater Lightstones, now I just need to get my faction up so that the Karana gypsies will talk to me so I can do the quest for the big bucks....

I'll probably be hunting the West Karana bandits for a while to get that faction up, and they are decent hunting for a level 12 too...

OK , a little secret I was let in on for levelling from 12-15. Alluring sirens in OOT. However it's not just any sirens but the ones at loc -1200, 2000. You jump off the boat as it passes between two sets of rocks, aiming for the right spire where there are only 2 sirens. They spawn roughly every 6 mins and always con indifferent until you attack. They hit hard but with a pet you should be ok, the xp is fantastic cause the loot sucks ( a bit like crocs in oasis). It would be slower with 2 of you as it works better as a solo spot, just remember to bind at the FP docks before you get on boat. And one more thing, only take these sirens, others seem to have a faction hit attached to them, these ones don't.
