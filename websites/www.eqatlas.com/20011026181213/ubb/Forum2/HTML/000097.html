Crushbone is the orc filled zone next to Greater Faydark on the continent of Faydwer. Just take the boat from Freeport over there and ask around for directions. It's a -long- stroll to get there, so I suggest if you find a kind druid, asking for a SoW. Kill elven slaves there if you can there, though be careful since that place is like Blackburrow, except full of orcs instead of gnolls.

If you also wanna work up some of that faction with the guards, you could always try to kill the halflings in the Nektulos forest. Their camps is usually for mid to higher teen levels, but you have to be careful of the Leatherfoot Deputy or Leatherfoot Medic. They're higher in level.

To raise faction with the DarkBargainers (which is the Neriak Merchant faction), you could always do the wine quest but I'm not sure if you could, since you'd be struggling with faction in the first place. I recommend killing some slaves, then halflings, and see how far that will take you into the city.

If you can get a caster who's non-kos in Neriak, s/he can invis you to the library where the wine quest npc is (Loktar or something like that? He's in a little room to the left of the entrance). He's probably kos to you, but he's also probably green to you (he's level 10ish) so he won't attack. Open up a trade window with him (you can give him 4 wines per trade slot at a time, so that's 16 - don't give him anymore at a time or he'll ignore the rest) and then WHILE THE TRADE WINDOW IS OPEN have your caster buddy invis you. *Poof* You have indifferent faction which is sufficeant to do the quest. Hit *trade* and watch your factions increase with Dread Guard Outer, Dread Guard Inner, and Dark Barginers, and no faction decrease.

Paladins have to do this for their Fiery Advenger quest, because on of the quest npcs is a DE merchant in Neriak.

Anyways, once you give Loktar enough wine, you'll only be kos to the guilds, which are easy enough to avoid, and if you don't care about other factions, easy enough to raise (go to everquest.allakhazam.com for more info on that). Currently, in high elf form I can wander around most of the foreign quarter and the commons (where the bank is =) ) and the merchants regard me as an ally, and my factions are perfectly intact in all the good cities. =) Good luck
