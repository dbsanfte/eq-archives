Rubies are sold in gem stores, the one in Felwithe sells jewelry quality rubies. However, they aren't cheap. They go for around 135pp, with a good charisma and ok faction. I'm sure an enchanter can maybe get them for 130, but that's about the best you will do.
Star Rubies are MUCH cheaper, at 18pp or so, but they aren't as nice for magic jewelry.
