South Felwithe. High Elf city, when oyu zone into North Felwithe just take the right path and follow it and when you get to the river take a left along the wall to the huts and the fishers near the water. Go into the tunnel and hit the zone. After you zone it finish through the tunnel and then go to the left up to a big building, there will be some guards, run the the steps and open the door, get up to th the room with the teleport pads. When facing the pads take the one on the right, run down there and enter the building, the merchant in the upper left corner (main room) sells the tiny daggers. Then to get out you can either swim in the water or go into the the little tunnels and when you reach the door take the path without the door to a teleport pad and then go on the pad. Then exit through the tunnel and then north felwithe and you have tiny daggers
