The Lake of Ill Omen is a great zone starting around 17-18. There is a newbie area but unless you are an Iksar I'd stay away from there.

Once you get to around 27th level at the Lake (won't take long) you can start to venture into Frontier Mountains doing mountain giants.

I was able to level my druid from 21 to 32 in less than 3 weeks doing the windmill area, sanark fortress and goblins of LOIO plus time killing MGs in FM and spider bastages in FV (a little revenge, hehe). BTW MGs can drop the much sought after Forest Loop.

When I get to about 34th level will probably move up to Forest Giants in FV. FG also can drop forest loops
