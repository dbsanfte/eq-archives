Actually for a level 6 there are other places to hunt that are more profitable, if you're willing to travel. Alot of them are considerably safer than inside Blackburrow.

Blackburrow can be a good hunting spot, if you are good at avoiding trains, but the loot there can be kind of weak. I hardly think that a level 6 would be able to take out Burlys and Patrollings though, unless ina full group. Look for the Gnoll Guards, and con the Scrawnies most of them will be blue.

I actually preferred to spend my time at 6 hunting/fighting the Ice Goblin Scouts in Everfrost. In Everfrost there are also Polar Bear Cubs and Spiderlings that are green but give experience till 7. All of these are rather rich loots with the Goblins dropping bags and some gems, the Bears with their pelts (ruined ones are good for tailoring). The Gnolls near the entrance to Everfrost are easier to handle than inside BB too.. Also if the Goblin Caster camp is free, camping there can be real profitable because sometimes a higher level shaman will pay you to tell them when a caster pops... Easy Money...

Also, Qeynos Hills can be worthwhile hunting for a level 6... Fight the Large Bats and collect the wings, stacks of these can be sold to Shaman and Druids of sufficient level. I have sold bat wings for as much as 4pp a stack. There are also the occasional Gnoll Scouts and Watchers to hunt here, and the bears and wolves (just watch out for the Rangers who discourage hunting them, and will kill you for it )

Last option in the Qeynos area is West Karana, and although the loot here is not quite as good I did find that hunting Young Lions near the guard towers was worthwhile for exp only. This zone is only for Day hunting though, as the Werewolves that inhabit this area like to eat level 6 characters for breakfast, hehehe
