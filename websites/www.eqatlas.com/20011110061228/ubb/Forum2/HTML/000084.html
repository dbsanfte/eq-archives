The hardest part of the Screaming Mace quest is getting the Bracer of Battle (BoB). The Orc Warlord drops it, but not all the time and he is sought after quite heavily. The Tunic is usually not hard, I have given many away as Emporer Crush drops them fairly often (at least he seems to for me). Crush spawns in the Throne Room behind the throne chair and up a ways. Beware of Dvinn, he is pretty strong and spawns behind Crush.

The Orc Warlord is a random spawn, sometimes in the throne room, and I was told he has like two other spawns in the zone. Possibly near the area where the taskmaster spawns and the other spot might be in the area where all the slaves are held.

It has been a while since I did it and when I did I was told to hail the Dwarven Elf and say "The King is Dead" and he will ask for the tunic as proof. I, however, was unable to get the dumb guy to respond to my hails so I gave him the tunic anyway and he gave me the item (Dwarven Mace I believe).The other guy (Elf on the far right side as you face all of them) is the one that takes the BoB and gives the shawl or whatever it is. I think you then give the dwarven mace and the shawl or whatever it is to the same elf (far right) and he will give you the screaming mace and some exp.

Now, I am pretty sure I have it right, but don't take my word for it as it has been a while, when you get in the zone ask around, I am sure there is someone either in Crushbone or in Greater Fay that will be able to help.
