Just this past week my guild Justifiers of Faith had the honor of having their first all guild raid at Perma on the Terris Thule server. I will tell you what I know. The trains here are phenomenal. There is are Ice Giants guarding the front so you will have to travel north along the wall to find the secret entrance (you can enter and exit thru this entrance).

The beginning of Perma is a long tunnel with Gobbies stationed along the way. Our group of 12 to 15's were able to pull from this continously with at times being a little overwhelmed and then regaining control.

We moved to the left to the flag room. This room is generally a nice room to pull to. Our group of 16 thru 19's were able to keep this room under control quit easily. There are 4 or 5 (blue/yellow to lvl 17 in this room)..some can even be pulled from adjacent hallways during slow times.

We tried to take the priest room and it was beyond our limits even tho the gobbies there didn't con any higher or lower than the ones in the flag room. The reason being is gobbies are frightenly aggressive with a good ratio of casters to melee'rs. It's the casters what will bring you to your knees expecially if they are standing back and healing the nasties that are already on you.

This dungeon can not be taken quickly. The gobbies may con green and you can take a whole bunch of em at a time, but they nearly always run when on low health and one lost gobby screaming curses at you from inside the dungeon will bring down a rain of uglies such as a sight you may have never seen before. Gate, evac,rezz and a lotta healing needed here.

If you come plan to stay awhile.
That is as much as I know so far and I am lucky to know that much.
Great Raid Guys..see you in two weeks for the next

We actually conducted a similar raid that I attended with our guild The Lords of the Anchients on The Nameless server.

The entranceway contains 5 goblins all grouped together, that are between the levels 15-17. Then as you proceed up the ladder from the entranceway into the tunnel there are 5 more spawn spots all for lower level goblins.

Immediately after this is the flagpole room where there are as many as 8 goblins at one time, and some of them are casters of approx level 17-19.

I really was unable to venture any further however, as I was stationed with the lower level 12-15 members of my guild, but I can tell you that these goblins all drop decent loot and are good at protecting their friends.

Ahhh Permafrost- spent many an hour as a lvl 13-18 wizard in that zone.

The zone line is great exp for the low to mid teens- the key being to break the spawn- at 16 a lvl 18 shaman and I were able to break it- then we got trained by careless higher ups.

The trains- what tends to happen in this zone is that since it is so undercamped that those in the 20's will pull too much and then start to run.

Now there is some great loot to be had- bard lambiant, shaman totem, and warrior crafted quest items drop here. There are also nice drops for both wis and int based casters and hybrids (there is a pair of boots in their I covet).

Vox is buried deep within this dungeon and is wayyyy to fat to fit through the door- so there is no chance of her popping up on you.
