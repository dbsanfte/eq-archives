Updates and More on the Banning
Sunday, October 8th

I went through some of my email and put up some updates, most of which is from the latest patch and the new beings incorporated into it. I've also decided that with all the new NPC's around and their items, I'm going to list them in the main map page, but they aren't going to be noted on the maps at all. I try to walk the line and not be a spoiler site, but trying to figure out which NPC's to include and which not to is too hard, I'll include them but am not too interested in where they are.

Also, I'll have the drawing for the Game Voices tomorrow (Monday) afternoon when I get home from work. All you have to do to have a chance to win is sign up for the Forums!

Also, wanted to clarify a few points on my comments about the character banning. I've heard back from some people, and got copies of the stories to read (don't ask, I'm not sending them to anyone). The story that is worse is about the rape of a fourteen-year-old girl, who winds up killing her rapist. There was some discussion about the fact it stated she was "fourteen seasons" but it is clear that this means how old she is and not how many levels she is.

Someone pointed out the position IGN was in with removing the messages, and I agree with IGN, I would have removed them from my boards as well. You can't have stuff like that posted freely on a site, because someone could sue me for posting it, but not him for writing it. Don't ask me to explain. My problem comes when Verant says it is a "derivative work" and bans him. It's kind of like the "it's not you, it's me" line in breakups, we all know it's a lie. It is a lie, however, that gets Verant out of a sticky legal problem with the free speech part of it.

For those of you who wrote to disagree, and state that free speech doesn't apply to him, you don't get free speech. It applies whether or not you like what he said, whether or not you want to read it or not. Using your own argument, I could say that you don't have the right to tell me you think I'm wrong, and that just doesn't make sense.

Thursday, October 5th

Verant recently banned a player for posting, on EQ Vault's message boards, this story. I'm not sure if this info is accurate, however, and I think the actual post has been removed, and involves the death and rape of a fourteen-year-old DE boy. It's kind of irrelevant to me, actually, which story it was.
