The name is actually Gynok Moltor, and he spawns at the top of the very first steps as you walk into Befallen. He has a ton of placeholders, all of whom yell something like "You have disturbed my slumber!" and then say something threatening that I don't remember. He is mid- to high-20's mummy when you finally do get him to spawn (6-8 hour waits are common, killing every PH along the way). The ring is rarely for sale, but when it does sell you're talking about 5k pp at least, since that ring is by far the hardest to obtain, once you get that a few more hours you'll have the Paw.

Hope this helps.
