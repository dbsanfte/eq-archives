Best advice, stay in Halas till 5. The newbie area in Halas is one of the best, assuming you survive. The unholy proliferation of those stupid polar bear cubs makes you level, or die.

Then, at level 5, print out Muse's map of Everfrost. It's not as hard as you'd think. Oh yeah, run like hell. If you meet one of the Composers or other tormented band members, you're hosed. The gnolls inside Blackburrow and outside both entrances are gonna want a piece of you, too. I'd wait for a primetime playing time, say after school gets out, or on a weekend afternoon. If you can get a SOW from one of your friendly shamans, you'll increase your chances of survival drastically. Just don't beg them over tells, there's nothing more annoying in the game.

Don't fret about hanging around Everfrost for a few more levels... seriously, it's an awesome place to lvl easily from 1-5 in particular. It only took me 2-3 hours to do so with my Shaman and that includes some time spent exploring Halas itself. I suggest paying attention to the polar bear cubs and spiderlings, they're good xp. And take all white bear pelts you loot to Cindl in the kilt shop for even more xp, pieces of armor, and money.

Although Qeynos is worth going to, right now you'll earn a lot in Everfrost for now.
