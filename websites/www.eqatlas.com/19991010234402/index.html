Travel Tip:

How to ride a canoe: First of all, walk into the canoe. Pan your camera down to point at the bottom of the canoe, and right click on the bottom. You will get a message that says "You have the helm" or something like that. Pan up using PageUp and use your regular keys to move around.

A word of advice while in the canoe, never ever hit your right mouse button. This is very important if you are using auto-move, because you will run right off the end of your canoe, which in something like the Ocean of Tears is bad. Although I've been able to get back into the canoe (believe me, it took more hand-eye coordination and timing than I can usually muster), it is not easy, and you will likely wind up swimming...
