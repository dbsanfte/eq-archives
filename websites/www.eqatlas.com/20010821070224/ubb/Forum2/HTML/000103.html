High hold pass and the karanas aren't bad at this level. In the pass itself there are gnolls and orcs, though you could probably only hunt them for at least one more level. High keep goblins aren't bad for low-mid 20s and all the way up to around 30.

Crag spiders in EW near the ramps are pretty good exp, often drop silks..if you're in the tailoring biz.

South karana is awesome, bird town is good for low 20s as well.
