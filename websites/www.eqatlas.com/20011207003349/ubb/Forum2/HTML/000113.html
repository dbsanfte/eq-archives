OK, first check the time (/ti). If it's between 5pm and 8am, don't start your trip yet, Kithicor forest is extremely dangerous at this time of day, many high level undead and Tier'dal roam the zone then. Untill you hit like level 40, you don't want to be in Kithicor after dark. Also, I'd suggest an invis from someone at your level, as it can be kind of dangerous in West Commons, with bears and plains cats. But you can avoid them if you keep your eye open and are really careful.

Start at Kithicor forest (the zone is near the cleric guild in Rivervale) and simply hug the north zone wall and head east until you're in the NE corner of the zone, then head south until you see the zone into West Commonlands. Start on the path heading east (watch out for wandering kodiaks, plains cats, and the ocassional wandering orc centurion, also keep an eye out for ocassional agressive wandering undead, such as ghouls and zombies. You can usually avoid them if you're vigilant and careful.) until you zone into East Commonlands. Continue on this path (keep an eye out for lions, and the ocassional young kodiak) east until the path dead ends into a desert area; continue east through the desert and you'll zone into West Freeport (the gates are east of where you zone in at, just keep going straight ahead).
