Jboots are a pair of boots that are LORE/NO DROP that can only be obtained from a quest...

The boots themselves are unique because they bestow a constant SOW upon you (so you never have to get a SOW again)...

The quest requires that you bring a shadowed rapier from a Shadowed Man (level 29-35) and a ring from an Anchient Cyclops (level 30-34) to a person in Lake Rathe who will give you the boots... The shadowed rapier is a NO RENT item so it will disappear when logging or going LD (which is why Fluv was so upset)...
