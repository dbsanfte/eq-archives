There is indeed a teleport room under the waters of southwest Timorous Deep. It's a long swim. The tops of 4 spires mark the area you need to dive to get into the room. Inside are pots that teleport you to different cities by simply touching them. Beware though...a very nasty sea creature lurks in that area and has feasted off many an adventurer.

Yep, have used these fire pots many, many times to aid in my travels. Having some sort of SoW and levitate would be recommended so you don't have to swim all the way there.

As far as the nasty creature is concerned, his name is Faydedar and is red to me at 50. Other sites suggest 53-55. I only ran into him once and it didn't take much for him to drop me. I think he spawns near the spires as I was headed that way just after they did a re-pop of the zone. If you are to venture to the fire pots I'd suggest not doing it right after a re-pop!! Death in the middle of the ocean makes for an interesting corpse retrieval...

At level 44, it only took him 2 hits plus one spell (if I remember correctly, I was "slashed by ice" --> Ice Comet?) to die. I made the trip to the firepots twice. The first time I had Levitate and jboots and was not bothered by the beast. The 2nd time, I was swimming and was dispatched about 20 seconds away from the firepots.

So, I'm lead to the conclusion that if you are not in the water (i.e. levitate or dmf) you're ok. However, if losing exp is a big gripe of yours, I would NOT recommend using the firepots because you will not be able to escape Faydedar if he attacks you. You will die and you will have a very tough time getting your corpse back.
