Albrecht -
Lake of Ill Omen is excellent, and will support you well into the 20's, but, unfortunately, it is quickly becomming as popular as the Oasis (in my experience, on Quellious, in any case). I would suggest to you that you take the advice fo the fellow (Cinn)that suggested you hunt in Lavastorm - at least for the next level or so. Your Expulse Summoned will do you very well here, as you can easily solo elementals, fire imps, and lava crawlers, all of which are Summoned creatures (affected by the Expel Summoned spell,and most of which will be even or yellow (gamble) cons to you until 17th (at least, and you could easily plug away until 18th there).
Good hunting to you!
