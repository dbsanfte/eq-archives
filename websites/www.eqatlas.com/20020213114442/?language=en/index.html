Some Corrections
Thursday, January 31st

I put up a slew of little random corrections around today.

Please do not email me questions about where to find stuff. I simply do not have the time to respond, and most of the time I have no idea anyway. I'm also saving the hours it takes to respond to these emails for mapping, since more people derive more benefit from new maps. Please use the Search feature on the left if you're looking for something on this site. Check the FAQ, since it does address the most frequently asked questions (trust me!). Ask in the Forums for questions related to mapping and getting around. If your question has nothing to do with getting around or maps (like on spells, quests, etc), then this is the wrong site to ask it. Check out the Links to see where to go for these, or head over to Allakhazam's, as he has a massive Links section.

That being said, if you have a correction on any part of the site, feel free to send it in. Please let me know what zone you're talking about when you do so.
