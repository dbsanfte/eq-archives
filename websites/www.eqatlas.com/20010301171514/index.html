Additions
Monday, February 12th

Made some corrections/additions to zones that I haven't touched in a while, check them out and send in what you can.

Many people don't realize how much contributions to this site help it out, and assume I somehow get all this info myself. A good example is the Tower of Frozen Shadow, where I've personally been as far as the second floor, and that for all of 20 minutes until half my group went LD. All the other info was sent in by you guys, so keep it coming, we all appreciate it!

Forums Alive
Sunday, February 11th

The Forums are back up and operational, enjoy!

Also I want to thank Danariel for helping me get my key to Cobalt Scar. She's the reason I'll be mapping further areas, so if you see her in game, give her a big kiss. I also want to thank Pris for rezzing my stupid exploring butt and Bertel for really working over a drake for me. Thanks guys!

I'm also hoping in the next few days to get a survey up and running which we can use to decide which area I will be mapping next. I'll only put up the areas that I'm able/willing to map, and then you all get to vote on which ones. After you choose one, I'll reset it and map that one. After I finish mapping that one we'll take the new winner and map that, and so on. This will give me a better idea of exactly what you guys want to see out of this site and which maps will help out the most people.
