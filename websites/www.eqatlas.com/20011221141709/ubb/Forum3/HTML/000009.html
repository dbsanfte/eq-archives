I agree with Muse and you both a doable dungeon and a nasty one. First time I went in (Lvl 40 or so), I did a /who and found out only mid-40s and higher were found inside the dungeon. Next I noticed there were 2 corpses on the floor near zone. I thought to myself, I had better find a group. I shouted lvl 40 druid lfg. Silence, no response from anybody (Probably everybody was snickering at me). After waiting for a few minutes I figured I will explore dungeon. I camoflagued myself and slowly walked down ramp. I went to first intersection; bam, wham, slam, I was accosted by an undead mob. It hit hard and it hurt. I turned and ran. I barely escaped the dungeon with less than a bulb of health. I thought Muse must be a wise-guy; he just wants to see how many people can die doing this dungeon.

Five seasons later, I returned with a group of 5 looking for silk from healer spiders, I told everybody this place is tough. We buffed up and had our evacs ready to go just in case
2 - Lvl 45 Druids (Healer,Nuker)
1 - Lvl 46 Bard (Mana battery)
1 - Lvl 41 Monk (Tank)
1 - Lvl 39 Shaman (Tank Buff)
Next we slowly went inside, clearing each room and hallway as we went. We snared everything to prevent trains. Finally found
our campsite and monk pulled mob towards us. It was a very successful raid (about 2 hr) and we picked up 1 Frost Dagger, 1 Kylong War Dagger, 2 Healer Silks, Ancient Coin for Bard, No Drop Whip for Druid, and some other stuff that I forgot.

Ok, I had to respond to this one.

I have spent well over 10 hours camping outside the Library.
The ONLY times trains came is when we wanted them, using
our high level Warrior to pull them for us.

Unless they randomly decided to change some weird
pathing, either you were being trained or someone was
doing something weird.

Kaesora is like an ATM to us. Run in, fight down to the
Library, HOPE that you get a strathbone shield (yah, right),
kill the librarian until we each have a Spirit Tome. Grab
whatever other rare drops fall. Head over the the Church
and kill there for awhile, gaining a little XP. Heck, if
we planned ahead we have all sold our Dusty Ransackers
packs. Everyone WILL get one of those if you camp
here for any real length of time.

Perhaps kill some spiders to KEEP TRYING to get the
stupid shield. Continue to not get it.

And you will get about 40 Fine Steel 2handed Staves..;-)

Love the place..
