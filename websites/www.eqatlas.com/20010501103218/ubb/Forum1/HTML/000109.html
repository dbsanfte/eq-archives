Although I've never used it, I think ShowEQ is much more powerful that just that. I think it gives Real-time data that the user shouldn't inherently know.

One example: I heard that during the recent Mithaniel Marr/Tunare Split, people were able to tell how many times the /movelog yes command was entered. All hearsay, of course.

Powerlamers is just a more derogatory version of powergamers. These are people who spend nearly all of their time leveling up. They do so by going to particular camps where the experience can come in a hurry, and they don't worry too much about questing, role playing, or interacting with the EQ Community. These are the people that can get from level 1 to level 50 in a matter of a few short weeks.

They don't get a lot of sleep.

These are nicknames that have followed from Ultima Online, the previous MMORPG that was a place that was rampant with farmers, ebayers and 'k3wl d00d$'... K3wl D00d$ are the guys who NEVER role play, type with two fingers, and use two word sentences to describe everything (Kewl dood being their favorite). Tehy also happen to be the ones who are most likely to be farmers, ebayers, and powergamers...

The only new derrogatory name that has emerged is "powerlamers" because they are the ones that come just to power level (i.e. powergamers) and have no idea what cooperation or Role Playing actually is... These are the people who will usually play solo (usually with either a necro or a druid), and then after level 20 will have problems finding groups (or refuse to find one), so they camp a single spawn for hours on end or become item farmers...
