You can nuke, you have a pet. You can solo Crushbone but I would suggest hiring at least 1 dumb warrior tank to accompany you just in case Orcs surround you. Offer to split the CB belts with him. Forget the cents. Get the Legionnaires.

Northern Desert of Ro or Oasis is good for your level. Tons of blues to yellows at your level. Most creatures wont gang up on you in the desert.

West Commons! At level 14, the majority of west commons critters were still blue to my beefy Paladin. Go on a Kodiak bear hunt all day! Pick off wisps, loot the Greater Lightstones. Kill bandits - good if you're with a group.
