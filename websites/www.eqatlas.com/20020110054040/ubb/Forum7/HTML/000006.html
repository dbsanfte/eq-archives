Well, something you might want to check is if you have you Hard Drave set to turn off after a given amount of time. Go to control panel, then power management and turn all of that off. Sometimes EQ has to do a swap to the Hard Drive, and if the drive has been powered down when it does, it takes a few seconds for it to power back up, causing a delay it the on screen action.

Of course you can reset all that back the way it was before you played just remember to do it before you play everytime or like I said the HD powers down and can cause problems.
