Someone in the message boards posted that Ak'Anon the Gnome city hates elves to the point that they KOS. At first I thought it was a fluke.

Then I took my first trip to Ak'Anon. Two elf bodies lay dead at the entrance hall.

Hmmm, I wondered to myself....coincidence?

I'm playing a dwarf paladin. I encountered no problems with the Gnomes in Ak'Anon.

Next day, I took my friend to visit Ak'Anon.... she's one of those hot looking sexy female wood elf Druids, the kind that we Dwarf men love to look at. =)

She tries to communicate with an Ak'Anon Guide (these are robotic guides). Guide immediately kills her as soon as she hails.

The body of a fragile and pretty wood elf druid lies dead on the floor besides me. I am a dwarf, the Gnomes did not harm me.

I think this is a glitch in the game. Maybe a faction glitch?
