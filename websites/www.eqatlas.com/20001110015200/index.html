High Level Game?
Friday, November 3rd

Sometimes you see things that are just so "off" that you say to yourself "they must be joking." This, from Gordon:

Please remember that the lower levels are a transitional period. People move through them, and once they are through them, they move on to other places. For this reason, there's always been suitable in-game content for lower level folks, as people keep moving out of the lower levels. It's the high level game that is most in need of new content due to the growing number of "members" of that range.

I'm not sure where to begin... Is it that the game was designed for uber-guilds alone to enjoy? Am I wasting my time by only having characters less than 40th level? Can I never expect any more content for myself, because I'm not level 50+? Again, he must be kidding... Discuss this in the Forums.

As an aside, my email may not be working right for the next few days, so if you have questions about things that can be addressed in the Forums, I'd take them there.
