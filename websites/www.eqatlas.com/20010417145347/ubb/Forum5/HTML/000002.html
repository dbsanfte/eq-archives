Seek out the dungeon of Runnyeye Citadel in Misty Thicket. The Goblins get progressively harder as you descend the depths.

Many hearty souls prefer Blackburrow in the Qeynos Hills as well, although you are too high of level for that now.

Lastly, venture to Faydwer and assault the Orc stronghold of Crushbone! Found within Greater Faydark, that would be an adequate challenge for your Group. Good luck friend!

You could also try the entrance at Mist Moore. The Yard trash I think is low enough for you.

I know that if you went up to Permafrost you could have a lot of fun, just remember to get bound in Halas and try and bring a group. The only other people in there will be the high levels farming rare items.

Runnyeye is also a good one, but agian there will be a high level running in and checking the hot spawns.

Najagen is a very fun one. Almost no one in there, except for the occational high level coming to farm.

Do you see the pattern here?

For being a warrior there are some good things to hunting in these dungons too.

Perma - Frost Totem - Crafted Boots
Runny - Three runes - Crafted Shoulders
Najaren - Dark Circlet - Crafted Leggings
Mist more - Werewolf fang - Crafted BP

There will be other good loot in those dungons too. I suggest that you make a plan to raid those dungons. Get a full balanced party and take over a dungon for a week or so. Just keep heading in there to get to know everything about it.

I know that when I was 18-20 I was in SolA Killing the front zone trash.

But if you get a full party and learn all you can about the dungons you will be fine. Just remember to get a map and have a high level friend that will help you get your bodies out if your party dies.
