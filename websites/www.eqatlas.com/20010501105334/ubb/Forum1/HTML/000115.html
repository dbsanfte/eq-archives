I just wanted to expand on this:

Before anyone picks up a magic item, right-click on it and see if its NO DROP.

If it is NO DROP, then decide if you can use it or if you need it for some quest.

If you cannot ever use it, then why would you pick up a NO DROP item? You just picked up something that you can never use, you can never sell, you can never trade, you can never make any money on, you can never give away to a friend that needs it more than you. In other words, you just wasted that precious item!

Courteous and kind players will realize that the proper way to handle these items is to SHOUT across the zone and give out the location of the item to someone else in the zone that can use it. Or maybe someone in your group is a class/race that can use that item, in which case they should be the one to loot it.

Example:

"Hey everyone in Kithicor! There's some neat Paladin-only NO DROP sword at loc 2500,590. First Pally to run there and grab it and its yours courtesy of me! "
