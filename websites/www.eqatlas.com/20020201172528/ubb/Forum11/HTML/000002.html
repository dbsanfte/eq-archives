Maiden's is an outdoor zone (not an indoor one like the info sheet says). Sow is usable and you can see Norrath in the sky

Couple points from having been fighting in there;
Lots and lots of those monster traps here. But if you go over them fast, the monsters dont' have time to react to you.
Invisibility works against alot of the 'Undead' mobs and almost everything in the zone - except the Centi (who DT you for 500-700 pts of damage ;P). The Xu Xui are not KOS but are social and will join other fights.
The Cromag (description, not actual name) guys are a range of classes, the Hunter is a Ranger and snares. They are non-KOS to the Enchanter's Imp form.
The Mar's attacks Aliens (again, description cause I can't remember the name) have a HUGE aggro radius.
The zone is about 4000 across, with multiple forts and castle like camps and tons and tons of wandering mobs.
