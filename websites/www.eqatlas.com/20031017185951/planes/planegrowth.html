Description

This is the home Plane of the god Tunare.

Dangers

Followers of Sol Ro and evil races are KOS here.

There is no exit from this zone, except by teleporting out.

Benefits

Unknown.

Travelling To and From Plane of Growth

The entrance to Siren's Grotto is underwater. If you travel with the water on your left to the cliff face, and then follow the cliff face beneath the water, you will reach the entrance to the grotto.

There is a Druid Ring at -2500, +3015.
