Assuming you're still operating out of Halas, Blackburrow is probably the place to be. At level 8, you should be able to hook up with a group... between the experience bonus for grouping, the experience bonus for the dungeon zone, having team mates to help you survive, and being able to take on tougher mobs than you can solo, the experience can roll in quickly. You can also make a decent amount of loot for your level... gnolls usually have coin, often have rusty weapons, and occasionally have large cloth armor, all of which sells well.

You can also begin to consider hunting the fringes of the plains in Everfrost, again with a group. Mammoth calfs, snow orc troopers, and snow leopards are within the range of a few grouped characters. The hunting is good here, and you don't have to deal with the frequent trains that are the calling card of Blackburrow.
