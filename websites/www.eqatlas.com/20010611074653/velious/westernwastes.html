Description

The Western Wastes is the last remaining home of a large number of dragons on Norrath. They are frequently found roaming the wastes.

Dangers

Virtually all the monsters here are dragons or dragonkin, and are very powerful. Travelling here without a group is a very dangerous idea.

Benefits

Unknown

Travelling To and From Weastern Wastes

The Western Wastes can be reached by passing through Siren's Grotto.

There are two areas that are off of the Western Wastes. The first is a one-way entrance to the Temple of Veeshan located at ?. The second is the entrance to the Dragon Necropolis located at -2800, +380.
