Travel Tip:

People often ask me how I was able to map all of these zones and have my highest level character only be in the mid-teens. Basically, you have to use defensive travel, and it's a lot of work. Get used to right-clicking on everything to see what they /con to you as you run. Avoid widely anything higher than blue or anything that looks like it might be.

I also prefer to use the 3rd-person view when travelling so I can see things coming from the side as well. And get used to what the /loc numbers mean and how to sense heading with repeated /loc's (you should have it hot-keyed). Getting in trouble is not a big problem if you know where the nearest zone edge is...
