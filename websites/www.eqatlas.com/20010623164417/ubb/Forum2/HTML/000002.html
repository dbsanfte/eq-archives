As far as the Erudite Sk's and necros, anyone from Paineel is not KOS in Erud as long as you dont do too many quests. My necro did about a dozen quests and was able to safely walk through the city. Do NOT go into the palace, the guards there will kill you, and watch out for the palace couriers running in and out of the city as they will attack and the guards will join in against you then. Once you get to Qeynos you will be KOS and have to sneak through the city using the sewers. Kill yourself a couple of levels worth of Gnolls and you are fine.

All classes from Paineel are considered Apprehensively in Erudin and Threateningly inside Erudin Palace!!

I have a level 21 Cleric of Cazic Thule on the Tallon Zek PVP server and can verifiy this first hand - there is nothing to worry about!

About the Paineel quests... Most of the Cleric and Necro Quests provide faction hits with the various opposing guilds in Erudin, Not towards the erudin guards. If you do receive a faction hit you are worried about, dont complete the quest over and over until you have obtained level 10+ (or get an 8+ group) and killed a few kerran Kitties in The Ridge.

For those conserned about what to hunt - the skeletons at the zone to Paineel and the abandoned heretics pet provide great hunting right up to level 12...
