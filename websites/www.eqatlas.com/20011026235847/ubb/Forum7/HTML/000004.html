A story with which I too can relate. I was an enchantress of her 5th season when my homelands began to bore me. I gathered the courage I need to leave my fair land of Faydwer an journey across the Ocean of Tears in search of great adventure. Upon stumbling out the South Gates of Freeport I ventured into the dry hot Desert of Ro. Unaccustomed to the heat of the day and quite parched, I sought refuge within a staked tent among those I normally wouldnt associate with, but in a foreign land though to lower myself and be friendly. Unbeknownst to me, the strangers I chose to seek shelter from the heat among were none other than the famed band of ill-repute, the dervs. I had the priveledge and honor of being slain by their leader Dorn B'Dynn, an known high elf aggressor. This sad tale I share hoping only that other travelers, young in their seasons, will be more cautious about who they seek refuge with.
