I actually had a guide like this! Hehe with places I travelled that I thought would be great for good and evil races.

For Good Races, try the following:
1) Qeynos Cleric Guild: Right in front of the waters of their weird floating structure. I love that water stair climbing thing, and it has symbolic value.
2) Surefalls Glade: Either near the waterfall, or in the forests, this is a very lovely place for nature lovers.
3) Highpass Keep: I'm not kidding. This place, while not as gorgeous as others, has a very nice castley feel. Like if you're actually marrying in royalty. I suggest in the courtyard, right before you go into the Keep itself.

For Evil Races, try the following:
1) Unrest: Definitely a good area. If you want it undisturbed, I suggest an Invisibility to Undead spell, and hang out in the courtyard or right outside of the entrance tunnel. If you want to make it even more gruesome, have someone pull a few critters after or during the wedding, bathing in the blood of fallen creatures as you take your dark vows (Recommended only if you have at least two groups worth of people there).
2) Qeynos Evil Guild: You can have the bride walk along the spectre filled hallway like it was the aisle, then into the very center of the guild where that spire is pulsing like a heart. Very coo' place.
3) Kithicor Forest: This is for the BRAVE of heart. Get that Invisibility Versus Undead up there, and stay right at one of the zone lines, preferably the one that leads to the West Commonlands. Don't try the Unrest bit hehe.
