If you have a magic weapon at level 9 a good way to get pp is to get a group and fight willowisps.

Other ways to earn money are just collecting stacks of stuff...

Bat Wings for Levitate (Druids & Shaman will pay well usually)
Bone Chips (to Necros and SKs for their pets)
Venom sacs (to Rogues)

Stacks will usually net a decent profit...

Also concentrate on killing things that drop weapons you can sell for money, and also pick a zone with a slightly higher spawn rate. Misty Thicket and Everfrost are both great hunting for 5-9.
