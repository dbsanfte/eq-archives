Much too difficult. At lvl 16, my troll warrior went from Oasis to Lake Omen.
Here was my route:
Take the raft from Oasis to Timorous Deep.
The raft will come to the shore of a small island of ogres. Go to the south of that island to the long dock. Wait there for Da Barge boat to take you to Overthere. You will dock at the outpost. Get a bind there.
Now go south along the west wall. Ride high and avoid everything. You can get all the way to the south and head east. You'll come to the Frontier mtn zone.
This zone gets dangerous because mobs cling the walls. But go east then south to the Lake zone. I avoided everything and made it first try. Sow and invis will make things easy.
