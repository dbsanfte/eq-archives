In brief, the two possible routes:
North Karana to East Karana is a given for both routes. Basically watch out for griffins and cyclops, and if you are very low-level and can't outrun them the lions and wolves.

Now for the two other routes. The gorge is very quick and safe, I've found, and is arguably just as quick (although I haven't timed them). Follow the leftward path through EK to the gorge. Once in the gorge, climb up on the righthand wall and stay up there all the way to Runnyeye. Be careful with low hps or fast movement, since there are a couple corners that will kill you if you fly off of them too fast, but take it slow and you'll be fine. In Runnyeye, keep to the left and you'll quickly (like 10 seconds) zone into Misty Thicket. From there run roughly straight to pass through the wall and to Rivervale. Rivervale I can't describe, look at the map. Once out the other side, you're in Kithicor (see below).

The path through Highpass may be quicker, although is slightly more dangerous. In EK take the straight path up to the pass into the mountains. Here, run and run for seemingly forever. Eventually you enter Highpass Hold. This is confusing, so get the map. This is the dangerous part, right after you enter and before you exit. On this side are gnolls which, if they aren't being camped heavily, will attack you. On the southern side the same holds true for orcs. They are all high-teens in level.

In Kithicor: from Rivervale turn left, stay up on north wall (from 6pm to 9am or so) and follow it all the way to WC zone. From HH go left along wall, which will take you past Rivervale entrance and then just follow wall to WC. As always in Kithicor, stay high up on the wall and don't pet the skeletons.

Hope this helps.
