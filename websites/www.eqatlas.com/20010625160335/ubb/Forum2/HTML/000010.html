There are a few decent places for you to hunt. It all depends on your location, so I will try to list a few locations. GFay: Try Orc Hill and move on to Crushbone. Antonica, Freeport Area: Kill wandering MoBs in East Commons, moving up to the Orc Camps 1 and 2. Qeynos area: Again, kill the wandering MoBs, moving up to Blackburrow. I am not sure about Odus. It should be relatively easy to find a group, but it all depends on your timing. Good luck and safe hunting.

At that level I moved on to Butchblock Mountains. I was a woof elf ranger and the crossroads are an excellent place to go. I tracked large spiders and scarabs and the best are the dwarf skeletons. they have good exp and gold for that level. I also sold the silk I saved up from the spiders and sold it for 2pp per swatch to tailors. this will help you get some banded and a pgt for your first upgrade(if not twinked). Hope this helps ya. after 12th I moved off that continent to Freeport and started hunting in Nro and Oasis till 20th. Dont forget always get bound when you move a long distance like that.
