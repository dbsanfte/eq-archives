How to get a bind in a city where you are KOS...

You only have to be in the city zone. I have bound many trolls and ogres outside freeport, at the zone line to the commons or ro.

As for paying for binds... Try doing /who for binders (any pure caster above 14). Send a private tell, maybe with offers of something other than money if you don't want to spend 15pp for a bind. Something you've brewed always works for me. As long as you're polite and honest, and take "Sorry, I'm across the zone and in a group" as a no, and ask someone else, you'll probably get a bind pretty quickly.

I only accept money for a bind if a person is A) Way twinked out, giving me reason to believe they can get more pp from whoever gave them all that fine armor or B) Much much higher level than me.

I hope that helps!
