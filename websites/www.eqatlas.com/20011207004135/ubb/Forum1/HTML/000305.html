Adventurer 		posted 11-01-2001 04:49 PM            
Make sure your video card and usb hub are not using the same irp. (right click my computer/properties/Device Manager/properties). If they are, shut down, unplug, move one of the cards using the same irp to an unused slot, boot up and go into BIOS settings and reset pnp. Go check that the main cards aren't sharing IRPs again, if so try yet another slot. The main IRPs for me are: video card, sound card, network card. For you USB hub's irp will matter too.

You are correct that you will need an ip for each account playing EQ. To do this, buy a router.. I have a linksys, and they are about $100 USD I believe (a little more here in Canada).
