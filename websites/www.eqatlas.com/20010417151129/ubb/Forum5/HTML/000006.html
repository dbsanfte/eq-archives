Hope I can enlighten you,

I went to Lower Guk at lvl 35, and decided I would fight on the dead side (this is useful as it increases your faction with Live frogloks enabling you to move easily through upper and live side guk) Also being a paladin your Invis Vs Undead is priceless in Deadside Guk.

At your lvl the best place to hunt are the spider room, and the bedroom, as they allow you to get a feel for Guk, and offer great exp. although no named loots.

Once you feel secure you should venture further into the dungeon, choose the Ghoul Assassin, Executioner, Cavalier, Sage as these all offer nice loot, and good exp. Once higher you can then safely move on to the Frenzied Ghoul, the Arch Magi and finally the Ghoul Lord.

Evacer's are useful as a situation can always get out of hand, but a well balanced group who doesn't pull out of their depth will be able to fight indefinately, and the travel to the zone is simple once you know your way.

Guk is a very worthwhile, exciting dungeon.

Good Luck

Listen up, all of you on here who wanted a mask of deception but didn't want to go in at a low level:

I got my mask at level 13.

It's actually very simple to do, here's a quick guide:

1: NEVER try to bribe people to help you get the mask, unless you are willing to pay a LOT. It does NOT work.

Okay, now, the guide......

Of course, you need to get to guk. When I first tried to run there, I passed right by and almost ran into a basher at grobb but that's beside th point...
While it may seem like a quicker run to the assassin/supplier room from the live/dead border, Always, ALWAYS zone into lower guk at the deadside (labeled C on the EQatlas map). There are usually people here to defend you from trains, etc. Find the group (there usually is one) that's camping the ass/sup room and give em a tell. Usually, none of them can use or need the mask anyway, and they'll say that if you can get there, you can loot it whenever it pops. Then, get an IU...
