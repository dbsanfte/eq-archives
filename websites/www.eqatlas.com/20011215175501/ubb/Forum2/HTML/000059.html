In BB at the docks:
The northern most pier is where the boat to Freeport stops. The Southern most pier is where the shuttles stop. These shuttles take you into the TD zone, and then pull up along side the big Firona Vie headed boat. The stop where you transfer from shuttles to the big boat is at about 4000,-6000. There are other stops where someting called a steamer comes upto the big boat. I've never taken them but have seen others hop on them thinking they were the shuttles.
