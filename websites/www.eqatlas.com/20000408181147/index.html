Updates (lots of them)
Thursday, April 6th

I went through today and answered almost 3 weeks of email. I've got more to go... Didn't I say I wasn't going to do this again? Oh well. Now I'm on a brief vacation, I can catch up on lots of stuff...

Of note, I've fixed some problems with some of the city maps. These include Grobb's grid coordinates being half of what they should have been, and the missing grape farm in Kaladim.

I also wanted to thank the druids who helped me out with the teleports, even the ones who I never caught up with. I appreciate it greatly...

Also wanted to post the following announcement:

The United Norrathian Brotherhood is planning an EQ Festival to be held in the first week of November, 2000. Please follow the link below to learn more of this event!

Nestled in the deep forests near the City of Houston Texas, is a small and friendly town which takes you back to the days of mystery and imagination! Not unlike the cities of Freeport and Qeynos, visitors can eat, drink and be merry to their hearts content! Guntar Pontdecardon, leader of the United Norrathian Brotherhood, will be your host as he takes you on a tour of this wonderful place. There will be Food and fun, and links to Travel and lodgings will be added to assist in making your venture to the Houston area as easy as possible. (I assure you it will be easier than finding someone to port you there!)

Follow me here to learn more of this event!

The next few weeks should be good for this site and for EQ in general, I'm excited (mostly cuz my DSL connection got put in today...). :)
