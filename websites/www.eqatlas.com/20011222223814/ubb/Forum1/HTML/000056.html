A blue con never means you can solo it no problem. Wait until you get to mid levels. You will be very un-nerved when a few extra blues get pulled. Even the just-turned greens at high levels can kick yer buttocks sore.

/con messages are _rough_ guidlines.
Color indicates a _rough_ guess of the exp you will gain. The text message gives you _an idea_ of the level of the MoB.
/con's don't tell you they are a master of defense, offence, and all weapon skills. They don't tell you if the MoB casts spells, or uses magic items. They don't tell ya if it has 1,000,000 hp. Hit Points are not tied to level for MoBs. So experiance and /con are not influenced by hps.

That guard may con blue, but he's been doing his job for about 2 years now. You've been playing that Pally for what, 10-12 hours tops? Your 110 str and 25 in 1hslash is nothing to a 4th level guard with a 200 str and a 200 1hslash. (I have no idea what the guards stats are, just making a point.)
