I'm pleased to announce today that the great site Allakhazam's Magical Realm and EQ Atlas have become affiliates! What does this mean? Comments
Wednesday, January 24th

I'm always interested to see what comments I get from people when I post something that I intend to be deliberately irritating. I'm glad that a lot of you feel strongly and understand enough to know why you feel strongly. We may disagree, but that's cool. I don't appreciate the death threats, but what can you do...

Sadly, though, some people feel my comments are out of place on a page devoted to a game, since "games aren't politics." I beg to differ, and direct you to check out these selection of articles describing how games and politics are butting heads lately, all out within the last month: Surgeon General declares violent media harmful to children, Defending arcade law has cost $308,000, Study claims link between entertainment and violence, and Lawyers downplay role of Everquest in homicide. It's interesting to note how differently the trial in the second of these articles might go if we had an Attorney General who agreed that video games were bad (don't know if he does, just a guess). Unless you're really in denial, you can see that video games are about to become harder to get your hands on than cigarettes are for kids, and if parents aren't buying these games, the money to make them great is going to fade fast. Take your head out of the sand folks, cuz one of you might be able to reverse this ugly trend, and if I have to piss a few of you off to make you all aware, so be it.
