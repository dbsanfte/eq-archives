It has come to my attention that Muse wishes to make the EQ atlas available offline or at least in a downloadable format. I think that putting the info together in a .PDF format based document would be outstanding and easy to reference due to to Bookmarking. The PDF Acrobat Reader is free for all to use and the format is easier to download vs the bulky .DOC. Plus with PDF formatted documents..people can't change it as easily and say they made the thing when we all know it will be burned into the pages of the document owned by EQ ATLAS.
Another thing is if the maps were drawn or created by someone else...then I don't see a problem with putting those maps into the guide..as they are the artwork of another, and not by Verant..even though they have made the game..it took someone the time to draw out the maps that obviously Verant didn't want us to have initially..that makes it the artist's work..and not Verant's. Therefore, it would be the artist that you would have to deal with. Unless the maps were supplied by Verant, they should have no say in what the Guide book has in it since people NOT working for them contributed.

I think PDF is the way I would go with something like this.

Just a clarification on a few of the things you've said. I am the artist for all of this stuff. The debate is whether or not my material is infringing on copyrights of Verants. I'm not sure that it is, but it does meet criteria for a derivative work based on their work. Now the debate would be whether or not it was sufficiently different to be an addition to their world instead of just a derivation, etc. There's lots of rules involved that I don't fully understand, and that don't clearly apply in this case, so it would be tough to figure out.

The other notable problem would be in using names of monsters and items from the games. I would be allowed to use the names of places, but the other stuff I don't know about. I'm not sure how useful the book would be without that stuff located on it.

Unfortunately, it's not as easy as me making a book of my maps cuz I made the maps.
