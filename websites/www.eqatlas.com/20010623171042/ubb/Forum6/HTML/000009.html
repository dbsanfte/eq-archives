I recommend the Icy Orcs in Everfrost. I got from lvl28ish (both monk and druid) to lvl35 with them. You can solo them if you are careful and just pick them off one at a time.

Standing at the Icy Orc platform on the ramp looking at the orcs, there are 5. The one in the middle is Redwind - a pretty nasty SK he's about lvl27 if I remember right.

The front left and back right are warriors, level 24ish I think and the back left and front right are shammies lvl25ish.

First harmony Redwind. This will harm all of them. Snare the front warrior and pull him out away from the platform - straight back. Root him and dot it - med. When dot fades, redot, when root breaks reroot. Orc dead.

At level33 you should be able to take both warriors before needing to sit and med. Once your confortable with that, go after the Shammies - for these you snare and nuke though. They will cast back on you - so nukage is in order .

Note: when pulling the back orcs, pull away from the other orcs

If you take Red he will harmtouch you as soon as you hit him. And he will also try to darkness you so be careful. If you have a partner you should be able to take the whole platform and not have to much down time because of there respawn rate.

Good Luck!
