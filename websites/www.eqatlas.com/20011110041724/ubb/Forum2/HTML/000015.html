When heading out From Rivervale or Highpass they will be in the building to the left side of the main road, just past the marker that points to Highpass/Freeport.

When heading from W Commmonlands it will be the second building on the right hand side.

I would suggest that you wait until daylight to go looking for it though.... When night falls, evil things wander the forest, and Skeleton Squad leaders have pets that see through ANY invisibility (even my Hide/Sneak). :P Bad Skeletons...
