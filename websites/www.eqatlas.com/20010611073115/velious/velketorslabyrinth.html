Description

This is the ancient home and laboratory of the spellcaster Velketor. It was designed with an eye towards security, and is very difficult to penetrate deeply inside.

Dangers

This dungeon consists of one section where you must move vertically over narrow ledges. The dangerous part is that these ledges are often covered in very slippery ice, so you must move carefully or risk falling off. Also, these ledges and overpasses are filled with many pit traps, which you can fall through to the lower levels as well. Any form of levitation doesn't work here at all.

Spiders here can turn invisible to attack, and many monsters can also see invisible as well. Some spiders are rogue-types and can backstab. There is also a very powerful poison that many of the spiders use that is very difficult to resist and can readily kill a high-level caster type if untreated. It may not be curable either.

The dungeon consists of two separate areas, the first being the maze (described above), and the keep area, which is where Velketor lives. The keep itself is surrounded by hordes of roaming high-level monsters, many of which can see invis, gate, summon, and many other evil things.

Velketor is the magician who rules this Labyrinth, and he is very powerful.

Benefits

Unknown

Travelling To and From Velketor's Labyrinth

Velketor's Labyrinth can be reached via the Great Divide at location -6770, +3130.
