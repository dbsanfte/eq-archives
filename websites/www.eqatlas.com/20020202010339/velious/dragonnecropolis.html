Description

The Dragon Necropolis is the ancient burial ground of the dragons from this continent. It has since become the home of several groups of rats that fight amongst themselves and with outsiders.

Dangers

This dungeon is littered with traps of many various types. They include root traps, dispel traps, DD traps, swarming beetle traps, and dragon construct traps (where a Dragon Construct is spawned). There is also some complex faction while hunting here between the two different types of rats. One type of rats is nice to outsiders and the other does not like them, but hunting the others gains you faction with their enemy rat.

Benefits

Hunting rats is very good experience, and they also rarely drop very nice items. High-level spells are often dropped here..

Travelling To and From Dragon Necropolis

The Dragon Necropolis can be reached by passing through the Western Wastes at loc -2800, +380.
