I have spent a (in)decent amount of time in West Karana, South Karana, East Karana, and North Karana. At level 15, there are several good places to hunt. You should still be getting decent exp in West karana for picking on bandits, (always in a group however. 6 bandits WILL kill you if you try to solo them.) In North Karana, you can solo pincer beetles. (watch out for the griffs however. griffs in my exp con about 5 levels below where they hit. in a group they only hurt. solo they will KILL!) About this level you can also start to hunt some of the wild life in East Karana. Be careful and stay close to the zone, hunt in a group, and you should be able OK. Most of the stuff in SK seems a bit high for a single level 15 character. However it is much easier to get a just a single creature pulled to the group.

The problem with most of these suggestions is that beetles, spiders, and wild life dont drop much money. But you should get exp from all of these. above all try to find a group. Even in everfrost, there is stuff that a full group of 15 level characters can take on. Ive also heard that Lake Rathe has a few nice spawns for level 15 characters. (the major aviak guard spots are about level 20.)

Highpass, hunt gnolls, level 15, level 16, level 17, hunt orcs, level 18, level 19, hunt pickclaw goblins level 20-30. Never have to leave the highpass/highkeep zones unless you want to go train or are running from a train. Usually fairly easy for a shaman (da buffmeisters) to find a group, and always group. The gnolls, orcs, and goblins do, why shouldn't you?

You get real good money, gnolls and orcs both drop decent loot. Side benefit, you improve your faction across a large expanse of territory from West Karanas to Freeport. If you have a trade skill you want to raise(other than alchemy I'm afraid) you can run it up in this area.

There are other places to be, but this mountain pass and the city are an amazingly compact place to level.

Don't kill the volunteers, ever. Don't kill the Highpass citizens or guards until you are ready to leave Highpass forever, you will drop to KOS so fast you won't believe it. I watched it happen to someone who got bored waiting for spawns and started killing volunteers.
