SBM is very nice. Was hopelessly lost for hours (I am spoiled having always had a map before when exploring a new zone lol), and nearly died three or four times. The zone, from a purely aesthetic perspective, looks great. Level range...I hear 20-40 or so. That may be a little high though. All the mobs I saw were blue or green to me at 33, though I hear there are rarer ones who are higher level.

Almost all of the animals and Kobolds are aggressive. The Kejekans (cat people) were indiff to me (Dwarf Paladin), but apprehensive to certain other good races, and KOS to necromancers and certain evil races (I know that they are KOS to DEs but NOT KOS to non-necromancer Iksar). They are KOS to Wolf Form also (dogs and cats lol). The Kejekans have a village with merchants and quest NPCs. No bank though. They came to my aid when I trained a Highland Kobold Shaman (looks like a white Kobold) to them who was raping me with his cold-based nukes.

The tigers (who are not on Kejekan faction), were blue to me and solo-able by my 33 Dwarf Pally. Their low MR makes rooting them, backing up and healing myself a snap. Half a blue bubble per kill is not too shabby. Drops I got included Low Quality Cat Pelt, a Tiger Skin, and a Tiger Fang. I think the skin and fang may be for a Kejekan Quest (not sure though).

Loot overall in the zone isn't too hot since the mobs are mostly animals. There were some evil players killing the Kejekans (NNOOOOO!), and they didn't drop much either I hear. Kobolds dropped soft wicker I heard, but couldn't verify this: Warrens Loot Code is on in SBM and the only blue Kobold I saw was the one Highland Kobold Shaman I had to train to the Kejekan Fishermen. If there is any phat lewt to be had here at all, it will be from building faction with the Kejs and doing their quests I imagine. Hopefully the Kejs' quests will turn out to be worth doing.

I will try to get back there tonight.
