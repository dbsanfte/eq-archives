It is my first time to play this game. I like traveling, so I did just that. I began as a High Elf Wizzard in Felwithe. After lvling up a bit, i decided to travel. So i went to Kelethin, dying a few times. But i got the hang of the game. So I then found a port to West Commonlands. After dying there and getting back again from Felwithe, I travelled to Freeport. I thought that I would stay there and get some money and enjoy the town. But i hated it, so I began traveling to Rivervale, but someone told me that I would die in Kithicar so I just ported to Erudin. I got to lvl 4 there and got some spells. Then i took a boat to Queynos and stayed there for awhile, exploring the areas around it. I then got a port to West Commonlands and stayed in Freeport a little longer. THen I ported to Butcher block mountains. I traveled to Kelethin and Felwithe again, and am now currently on my way to the great Dwarf town (forgot name heh)
