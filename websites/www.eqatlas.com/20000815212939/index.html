Najena
Tuesday, August 15th

I went myself and checked out Najena, but from what I could see there wasn't a heck of a lot going on in terms of new spawns or drops. It is possible that they are triggered or only appear at certain times of the day or something.

It's likely this was a transient GM event, kind of like the event in North Ro a couple of weeks ago that I forgot to mention. In that event there were spiders roaming around that were necromancers, but this only lasted a few hours if that. If this sort of thing became a regular event, I'd be happy.

New Graphics Drivers
Monday, August 14th

For those of you using the NVIDIA-based graphics boards, there's a new set of Detonator drivers out for them that really rock (in my opinion). You can download them at Detonator 3 Page. I tried them out today and noticed a large improvement in the problem with the swaying trees and having to cut down my clipping plane (despite what Verant officially states). Basically, in Field of Bone where I usually have to have the clipping plane down as low as it goes to even move, I logged in with it set out further inadvertently, and it was surprisingly fast, about what it should have been. Check them out! (Note that I cannot vouch for the safety of you or your computer if you use these, there apparently are stability problems, although I've noticed nothing, and that I cannot provide any support or further information about them, go to the site above for that).

Also, seems that mobs and loot have recently changed in Najena. If you have any info, please let me know (and this after I spent all that time getting it perfect).
