Hey, I have a suggestion. I have a 34th level Pally and he just recently got his Soulfire (Woot! ). Before I had that I used various 1h weapons with my Crested Mistmoore Sheild I recieved as a gift. Then I bought a Blackend Alloy Bastard Sword around level 27 and trained in 2h, found that I liked it ALOT better. Make SURE you train with your 2h slashing because once to see the stats on the firey avenger and defender, the pally epics, you will definately want your skill ready for them. Since pallys cant duel weild, they make better 2h slashers. But dont think you went through all the work getting the ghoulbane for nothing, you will need it and SoulFire to unluck (Da Duuunn!!) the firey avenger, and it's preceeder, the almighty firey defender! Have fun dude, thats what pallys are all about. Oh yeah, just as a tip, High Keep (HK) makes a very nice place to get out of your first hell level, so check that out when the time comes. Luck be with you, noble paladin.
