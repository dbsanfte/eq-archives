First, some basics:

Thurgadin is the most accessible and easily best-equipped city that I've visited in Velious. There's more in Thurgadin than in most starting towns. Armor quests are also on the basic Coldain faction, which everyone starts non-KOS to. Royalty faction is also not KOS to most.
Normal=Coldain
Royalty=DainFrostreaverIV

Kael Drakkal is the only way to get from Eastern Wastes to Wakening Lands by foot. I've heard of many quests, but my understanding is that the NPC vendor selection is rather lacking. Kromrif and Kromzek giants are KOS to all but Rallos Zek worshippers, and the king is KOS to all. I haven't heard of any armor quests here yet.
Normal=Kromrif
Normal=Kromzek
Royalty=KingTormax

Skyshrine is the way to get from Wakening Lands to Cobalt Scar by foot. I have found a bank and a muffin vendor here, no other merchants I've found. Armor quests require Yelinak faction, who starts even more KOS than the rest of the dragonkind here.
Normal=ClawsOfVeeshan
Royalty=Yelinak

Raising normal factions is as simple as killing their enemies. Killing Kromrif giants raises both Coldain and CoV faction, killing Coldain raises both Kromrif and Kromzek, killing Kromzek only raises CoV, and killing dragons raises only Kromzek.

Raising royalty factions appears to only be possible by completing quests, or by killing the few (and extremely tough) creatures on opposing royalty faction.

From what I've seen, the way to be liked in the most places as possible is to kill giants. You will have full access to the very well-equipped town of Thurgadin, and have safe passage through Skyshrine. An added bonus to good CoV faction is being able to use the dragon portals in Wakening Land and Cobalt Scar, both of which are surrounded by dragons. Anyone who knows about Wuoshi knows what I'm talking about. The only disadvantage to this is that you need to be invisible to pass through Kael between East Wastes and Wakening.

Being a giant-killer, I can only guess at some of the advantages of getting in good with the giants. Safe passage through Kael is one, though it is at the cost of safe passage through Skyshrine. One thing I understand is that the dragons and coldain drop better armor in general, while in comparison giants don't seem to drop much very often. Probably the best advantage of positive Giant factions is to give more hunting options...they are essentially at war with everyone else anyway.
