One thing that should not be overlooked is the Crushbone Belt and/or Legionnaire Pad quest. You can pick up a surprising number of CB belts in Crushbone. You take them to Kaladim and turn them in to... er... the warrior guild master, I think (I've done it several times, just can't remember exactly who to give them to... maybe someone else can fill in the missing info).

You give the CB Belts one at a time and gain exp for each. Plus you get a few coins and an item you can sell for about 1 gold.

Lego Pads you give two at a time. I haven't looked, but this would be much better experience than a CB belt, I'm sure. Lego pads are harder to come buy and, as far as I know, are only found on Legos (but maybe on Slavers, Emmissaries, etc., too).

On one run I took 2 lego pads (or was it 4?) and 12 CB belts and got a bubble of exp... at level 13. You can take lots of cents in CB if you're in a group. You'll do better looting if you solo, of course, but CB can be tough on you soloing at lvl 8. This is due to the high number of orcs there that will swarm you when you try to pull just one.
