ESTATE OF UNREST MANOR HOUSE

Location Key
Lettered Stairs lead to each other
1 Back Room spawns Reclusive Ghoul Magi who drops Dusty Bloodstained Gloves or Savants Cap
2 Barroom where spawns: Undead Barkeep who drops Opalline Earring and Thick Leather Apron, and Zombie of Unrest Noble
3 Room with Reanimated Hand which drops Ivory Bracelet
4 Tower that spawns Priest of Najena (?) who drops Polished Brass Key
5 Tower Room
6 "Tower" with Undead Knight who drops Bloodstained Mantle and Bloodstained Tunic
7 Main Basement Room, filled with Werebats and Festering Hags
8 Blood Trap, be sure to walk *through* the lava, going around it will make you fall in pits
9 "Dwarf Room" with Garanel Rucksforth who drops Jagged Bands and Dwarven Work Boots, also has Tentacle Terrors who drop Tentacle Whips, and Dark Terrors
