Some things that I do know about the Particle Weapon quests are this:

1. Only characters level 50 or above will be able to complete the entire quest, so it is quite possible that you will have to wait to COMPLETE it (although I am not sure when you may actually begin doing the quest).

2. These quests are termed as "epic" quests, so they will not be easy to complete and will require vast amounts of resources (cash, weapons, and items necessary to give to quest NPCs).

3. Only one of the epic quests has been solved up to this point, and it happens to be for the Rogues (way to go my shifty brethren)!! The other quests have very limtied information so far, and it was only through the collaboration fo the Rogue community that theirs was solved (because of the Safehouse a rogue message board).

I would suggest that you check with some of the spoiler sites for quest information about the particle weapon quests, although at level 26 I'm not sure how much (if any) of it you'll be able to access/solve at this point..
