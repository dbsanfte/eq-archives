EQ RPG and Updated Store
Monday, July 22nd

I got an email from Chris at White Wolf regarding the upcoming Everquest RPG, which is a d20 system. For those of you who don't know, d20 is basically the core rules for Dungeons and Dragons Third Edition, which have been opened up for others to use and create an RPG. That way, if you've played D&D or a d20 game, you can easily get into the swing of another one like it.

He also sent along a couple of goodies. One is this Froglok Jin Shaman image that will be in the EQ RPG Monsters of Norrath book, and you can only see here at this site.

The other is that you can now preorder the first three books for the EQ RPG, the Players Handbook, the GM Book, and Monsters of Norrath. If you do this through my Store, it helps the site greatly, so please do.

Stay tuned, we've been promised a small blurb from the Players Handbook soon as well.

I've also gone through and done an update on the Store for EQ Atlas, since there are a lot of new games that have gone up for preorder, and some old ones that have kind of faded out. Remember that every purchase there goes towards keeping the site running.

Busy Busy...
Wednesday, July 10th

Please scan your computers for viruses.

I can't state this strongly enough. If you read this and don't have virus software that's up-to-date (as in an upgrade in the past 3 months) then get some software and do it. The fact that there's two free versions (freeav.com and housecall.antivirus.com) means that there is no excuse to not do it. If you, for some amazing reason, can't figure out how to do that, then put a firewall on your computer (ZoneAlarm) which will warn you when you try and send a virus.

I get almost 100 viruses a day currently, each around 121kb in size, and this morning had an email program catastrophe where the table of contents corrupted and I lost about 500 emails (yes, again), including everything up to this morning. If you sent me something before this morning, please resend it.

Please scan your computers for viruses.

Also, I just started a new job and have been super busy with that, and I'm studying for my medical boards (the hardest test doctors have to take) in about a month, so time for the site has been slim.
