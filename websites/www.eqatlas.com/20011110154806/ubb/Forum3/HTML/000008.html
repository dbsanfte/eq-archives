I am a lvl 43 Iksar shaman on Torvonnilous. I play most mornings for an hour or hour and a half and I have to solo then because:
1) not many people on
2) by the time a group is set up, I have to go to work.

I have found a great place to solo for exp.
It's in the Mines of Nurga. I really started soloing here at lvl 38, but 35 or 36 would work, I'm pretty sure. You need to be able to invis. to hunt here, at least to get to your hunting spot, and then get back out, unless you can gate. The goblins here and the slaves are mostly lvl 29 to 31. There is one room with 5 or 6 spawns that can be lvl 31 to 33 goblins. There are no roamers here, and you can find pairs of stuff to pull fairly easily. Some of the caster goblins can be a little tough (flashdrowers, slaves, & rockchanters) but not too bad. The briarweb spiders are all still blue to me at lvl 43 and are great exp. for me. I think the spiders go to lvl 36 or 37, but I don't know what lvl they start. I'll have to see if any are green when I reach lvl 44.

The loot here is also pretty good, cash wise. I have gotten lots of gems here (peridots, emeralds, flawlesss aquamarines, opals, and black pearls), and cash is about normal for Kunark MoBs. Except for the slaves, they have no cash. Don't expect any magic loot though. Except Burynai Legion Gi's and Mountain Death Belts from the Burynai slaves. There are burynai rockshapers and cenobites deeper in the dungeon, but I haven't really hunted them any.

Anyway, on my server I'm almost always the only one there. So I'm just letting others know of this great exp. spot.

Safe journeys all.
