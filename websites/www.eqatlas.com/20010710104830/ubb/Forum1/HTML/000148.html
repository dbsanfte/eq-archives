With a Monk, you can still only use the Bind Wound skill up to 50%, just like all the other classes... The extra healing above the 50% comes from the Monk only skill of Mend.... Using you med skill can effectively heal you all the way from being near death to 75% of health....

You second question would depend on exactly what kind of stat increases you were looking for from the armor pieces. I would go and do an equipment search for light Monk type armor... I know that Allakhazams has a really good items search by class, and maybe you could find light weaight equipment there that would be easy to get at your level...
