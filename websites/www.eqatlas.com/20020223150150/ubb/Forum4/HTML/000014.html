Would like to make a small note for Muse in regards to the monster list for both Emerald Jungle and Trakanon's Teeth.

The Erollisi Mantrap drops a mantrap root, part of the druid Dark Oak Shillelagh quest. Through my times in the Emerald Jungle, have never seen the Mantrap on tracking. However, upon zoning into Trakanon's Teeth, picked up one on track and dispatched it pretty quickly for the mantrap root. It reappeared shortly in the same area, only one I have ever seen, think the game treats it as a named from looks of it.

Currently, the monster list for EJ lists the Mantrap, the one for TT does not, something to verify if you have the time. Thanks
