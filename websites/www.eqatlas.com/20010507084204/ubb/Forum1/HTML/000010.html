You've done a great job fixing up the few errors that were in your maps, and adding new maps for areas that had been previously left undone (I *love* the new Freeport tunnel maps), but a few minor things remain undone...

First, your West Freeport map (detail map, not the "tour guide") says under Location Key item #2 that Ping Fuzzlecutter (outside the West Gate) sells "Tinkering Supplies". No, actually, he doesn't. I wish he did.

The location shown for the Dragon Bones in Steamfont is still incorrect (you've got its X/Y coordinate positions reversed). Could you slide it over so it shows correctly on the map?

I was scouting out Upper Guk the other day, and got terribly lost several times using your map (which is otherwise excellent) because the way that various passages passed over and under each other was not clearly indicated -- things that looked like intersections weren't, or vice versa. Is there any way to shade or highlight overlapping passages so that it's clearer which goes under which, and which are and are not connected to each other (as opposed to simply passing over each other?

Another thing I'd like to see is instead of describing some zones (or parts of zones) as simply "very dangerous" or "reasonably safe" or somesuch, you ought to indicate WHAT PLAYER LEVELS you are saying this in relation to. Some places you do, but often you don't. Needless to say, the player's level can make a world of difference.

Finally, could you make a map for the Timorous Deep, please? And that goes also for any other spot you've left uncharted -- I've come to rely on your maps so much that I get a terribly lost feeling (literally) when I travel to a new area in the game, bring up that zone on your site, and see "no map available". Eek!

Other than that, great stuff -- don't let these minor things give you the impression I'm not 99.9% thrilled with everything else you've done and made available.
