That's a tough trek for a level 11 Ranger. I know because I did it when my ranger was that level. East and West Commons are pretty easy. Kithicor is not bad as long as it is daytime (NEVER go into Kithicor Forest at night below about level 30). HH can be tricky and actually was my demise. The only way I got through there was with the help of one of my higher level guild mates. He even helped me get my corpse first

I guess you have 4 choices:
1. Cross your fingers and run like hell,
2. Buy a teleport from West Commons to North Karana and run the rest of the way from there,
3. Get a kindly higher level play to help,
4. Wait until you hit level 15 and get Camo.

By the way, the route from FP to Surefall Glade is...FP to EC to WC to Kith to HH to EK to NK to WK to QH to Surefall Glade.

Good luck.

The best advice I can give is to get a map of Highhold (as this is the major obstacle you will need to traverse) and be sure to have SOW on you... When you travel through HH trya dn chose a time where the orcs and gnolls will be camped there (primetime), and run like hell.....

The rest of the trip is not exceedingly hard, if you are good at avoiding enemies.... In the Karanas (North and East) always run directly at the monsters that are indifferent to you (spiders, beetles, snakes) and get as far from the cats and griffawns (and everything else) as you can....

Also be careful on the cliff headed down from Highhold, as fallign from here will mean a quick death...

HH might can get a little hairy, personally, I'd skip that route & go via Rivervale, Misty, Runnyeye (take all rights), the Gorge (hug left wall & take first left), then into the Karanas.
Perhaps a little longer, but not by much & it's tons safer. In Runnyeye, the gobs you pass (if you see any) more than likely won't attack (low level to an 11) but if they do, you're outta that zone in about 20 secs or so anyway.
The "toughest" part would be in the Gorge where you ~may~ have to go up the left wall to get around a Muddite Major, a Mino or an Eyeball. Most of the time you can just run right through going up & down the wall if you have to - with Sow, you can blast right through & outrun everything (except if an eyeball casts on you, which I've never seen happen). I've done this trek quite a number of times (well over 60) back & forth with various chars & never had a problem - saw a Mino 2 times & an Eyeball once & a handful of Muddite Majors. Mino's are always on the run & you're either following it or it passes you by when you go up the wall.
Lowest level I made the trek at was un-SoW'd level 7, although the Gorge leg was a little hairy.
Sure enough that was the time I saw the Eyeball & a one of the Mino's <sigh>.

Good Luck.

I'd like to add a further warning about HH. As Muse says in his guide, it's a real maze. Your first time through you WILL get lost, even if you have the (excellent) map. I spent many levels there and in the Keep, and I still take the wrong turn every now and then.

One advantage of HH is that you can duck into the Keep and get a bind.

If you can afford the plat or have a guildmate who will help, take a teleport to EK -- you still have a lot of dangerous territory after that.

As far as the trip through Runnyeye and Gorge of King Xorbb I would say wait until you are at least level 20 to begin using this way. It is hardly shorter than the other route, excepting the trek down the mountian witht he dangerous cliff.

The difference between going this route is the fact that at level 11 the Gorge will be filled with aggressive enemies, and every one of them WILL aggro you. Having a train of goblins and muddites chasing you could be pretty dangerous, and the muddites (both minors and elders) WILL blind you with their mud.

ALSO the Evil Eyes (level 24 inside the Gorge) WILL see through invisibility, will cast charm on you, and will tear you apart should you happen upon one of them. The Gorge also will have NO OTHER PEOPLE IN IT, and if your SOW wears you will be in tons of trouble.

I would definately recommend going through HH for the younger adventurers.

A few comments: I think the Rivervale -> Misty Thicket -> Runnyeye -> Gorge is not only easier, but safer and shorter (unless you have 10 min zone times :-P ). That maze of HHP and the ramp down (or up) in EK is brutal. Also, you can get bound in High Hold Pass itself... you don't have to go all the way into the keep.

Of course, the best way is to get a Group Gate to NK or WK and a SOW for the rest of the trip. It's really not too dangerous, just keep your eyes open and don't stop to fight.
