Fluvius
Adventurer 		posted 10-27-2000 04:03 PM               
...I think.

Once again, I fell off a boat, this time in Timourous Deep. I swam to the friendly elf island outpost, being a half-elf. And since I was bored and have always been into exploring, I decided to log out and get Muse's coordinates for the famed Combine teleport chamber for a quick trip to Antonica.

Ah, my foolishness.

Logging back in, coordinates in hand, I began the long swim. The good news is the landmass I was swimming toward that had the raptors on it was visible from a long enough distance that I didn't stop on the island.

The bad news is that my scheme for Faydedar detection -- aside from seeing LOADING, PLEASE WAIT... -- was foiled by the weather.

Alas, pulling the old swim-just-close-enough-to-the-surface-so-you-can-see-the-horizon-underwater doesn't work too well in the rain.

At any rate, when I neared the coordinates I saw a tiny form in the corner of my screen that looked like a dark, distant fish. Obviously, I didn't investigate, fearing it was the Faydedar. It was dark brown and looked like a mutant shark, but I only saw it for a second.

I eventually reached Muse's coordinates and swam around them repeatedly, but if there was a teleportation chamber there, I couldn't find it.

Ah, well, I said, and swam to the ogre outpost -- on the other side of the ocean.

At least I can say I saw the Faydedar and lived.

I think the lesson here is never get off the boat....
