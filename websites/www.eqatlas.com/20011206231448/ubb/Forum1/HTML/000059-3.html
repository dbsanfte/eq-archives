I believe Bards are the best for both solo and group. The only thing limiting a Bard in soloing is wrist stamina period, I successfully soloed yellows and reds from level 12 to 37 in outdoor zones. I also charm kited pretty well in dungeons but that was way more risky.

In a group I will admit that enchanter crowd control is more reliable due to the length it lasts, but a bard who is paying attention and not trying to be a tank while crowd controlling can keep the mobs very busy. Plus the decent ac helps them survive agro a little better than enchanters.

I really enjoyed playing my bard except for the sore wrists after non-stop killing for 5 and 6 hours straight. Charm kiting is just awesome and you can do quads if you want and get exp for all 4, worst thing that ever happened to me was I lost one of the mobs as it died as a pet because I had not timed the charm correctly. I did not make that mistake again
