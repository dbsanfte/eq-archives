I am 51 and I still get exp in DL, off certain brutes and Drolvargs.

But to answer your question, dependant a little on class: 48/49 is about the bottom
for KC. Some lower try to sneak in Anon
at times, but all this does is cause problems. A lower Enchanter cannot mez the mobs, a lower Cleric won't be able to heal enough, and lower Fighting classes won't be able to take the dmg. And all three of those things can be disasterous to not only your group, but everyone else who gets trained.

The trains in KC can be extremely deadly. It is very considerate to once again learn to use your "Train to zone" hotkey, because as you either zone or die, the mobs will wipe out everyone at zone LFG. It's a shame if the person who was killed is the cleric that would have rezed you, eh?

If you want to be safe, stay out until you are sure you are ready.
