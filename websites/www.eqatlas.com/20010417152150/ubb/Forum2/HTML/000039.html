Well I'm starting a thread for future wish list ideas. I do know that Verant is receptive to SOME ideas, but not others.

EQ is a continuously updating and upgrading game, so who knows? Maybe one day we will see the things we always wanted...

#1) New Class = Pirate

Why a Pirate Class?

Think about it. Norrath has HUGE oceans, and ships and ports are an essential part of the game. Role-playing a pirate character would be an awesome addition to EQ.

#2) One more evil humanoid race. Evil but not too evil that every single race hates them on sight. In other words, create a race (like Gnoll or Half-Orc) that would we welcome not just in their home city, but also welcome in Neriak, or the Troll city of Grobb. Get the picture?

I already feel sorry for the Iksar limitations on travelling... they cannot venture into other cities outside of their own home turf. That defeats the glory of traveling, adventuring, and trading in distant lands and continents. Sure, Iksar are a powerful race. Sure the Iksar have some badass capabilities. But who cares? If an Iksar character can NEVER enter 90% of the great cities of other races, they pretty much lose out on half of the game.
