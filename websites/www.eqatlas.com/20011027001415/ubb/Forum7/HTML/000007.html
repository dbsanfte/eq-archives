Greetings all: Well last night I visit the TD Island of the Orges for the first time and it was a scary event. Caught the barge in Oasis and when we debarked the area was littered with dead bodies, plus it was raining which didnt help things any. Needless to say I got seperated from the group, but was lucky enough to rejoin the grp without getting killed. We did well, but when it came time to leave I was unable to board the barge as there is no dock on the island. You have to get on the barge thru the barrels (and I am a Halfling)so I had to keep trying to jump up on the barge while everybody else just walked on, dam those short legs of mine. Missed 4 barges total. Finally I petitioned the GODS for help and they saw fit to assist me and TP'd me back to Oasis. I was very grateful, as being alone on that beach was not my idea of fun. How about somebody telling me how a Halfling is supposed to get on that barge without the dock, when it is in the water? Why, because I want to go back to TD island to fight again. Great hunting, gives good exp and loot to boot.

You can run up the back of the barge to get on. There are 2 barrels in the back that are slanted down into the water, forming ramps. Wait until the barge stops (to make it easier) and swim around to the back. Then just swim up.

This works for me, and I'm a gnome.
