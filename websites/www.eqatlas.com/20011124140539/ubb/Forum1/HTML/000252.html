I don't know where some of the major recipies for the super high items come from but I did find a book that told about stuff like spiderling silks combine to equal a silk thread.

It was a book ok a vender in Misty and it told a story about some halfling found out that she could make thread from the silk of spiderlings. When her husband was hurt she would combine threads and she would make a bandage.

I guess that stories like that must be scattered all around Norrath. That book wasn't even on a sewing venor. It was called something like "Spiders can heal" so it wont just say "How to make (insert item here)"
