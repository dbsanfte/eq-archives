Defrag your Hard Drive. Most of the time your computer is spending zoning is accessing the correct information. I have an Athlon 600 with a god-awful amount (read as 320 MB of RAM) I don't spend nearly as much time as 4 minutes. Trust me, most of the delay you're experiencing is read/write on your HD. If you're really serious, get a high RPM Hard Drive...or if you've got the bucks to burn, a SCSI HD. That's a little extreme IMO, but it would help.

Also there are some tricks that could help speed up access, but most people aren't willing to go to the, admittedly tedious, lengths, though they are free. Hint: It involves formatting and partitioning your Hard Drive.
