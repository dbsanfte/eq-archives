Not 100% sure on the details but Necromancers do get a spell called Summon Corpse. It is available to them at lvl 39 and the spell is kind of a rare drop, not bought. It requires a coffin (100pp +) which I think can only be purchased in Neriak? Anyways, when cast it basically summons your corpse to the feet of the necro who casts it. Other than that if it is in an unreachable spot you can try to petition a GM. I've heard them going in for lava baths to get corpses...

If it's simply lost in the Karana's somewhere, look around for a bard or a necro. They both get spells early on that can simply point you in the right direction of your corpse.

And I wouldn't suggest deleting your account, as was said before...it's just stuff. You don't lose any experience and that is certainly more difficult to get.

Hope this helps and good luck!!
