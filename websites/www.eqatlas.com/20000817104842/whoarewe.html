Who are we?

I get many e-mails asking me what server I play on, what people I play, what levels are my characters, etc. Therefore, to clear up the confusion, I decided to add in a page devoted to my characters. Note that all of my characters are on the Bristlebane server at present. That way you can all see what characters I have and I don't have to respond to all those e-mails anymore...

KITHURIEL

Kithuriel is the consummate world explorer. At a young age he headed from his homeland in the wood of Greater Faydark to explore the world abroad. He alone is responsible for providing most of the information on this page, and has mapped out Upper Guk, the caves below Everfrost, Lavastorm Mountains, Rathe Mountains, and many other out-of-the-way places in the game. He is an expert at moving through areas that he probably shouldn't be in with ease.

19th Level Wood Elf Ranger

Currently in: Oasis of Marr
DAIAS

Daias is similar in adventuring spirit to Kithuriel, but is much less of a loner. Although he still spends much of his time by himself, usually in the plains dedicated to his god, he can frequently be seen with friends destroying the undead in Unrest or Mistmoore. Blessed from an early age with equipment from a retiring master, he has spent much of his money helping others and mastering blacksmithing, a skill only halfling druids seem to have an interest in. Here he is pictured with a friend of his, whom he sits with and chats often while hunting.

Level 29 Halfling Druid

Currently hunting in Dalnir
MOOJOO

Moojoo is a shaman who is truly arrogant. He believes that the great ogre empire will rise again and rule the continent of Antonica and beyond, and can often be seen sitting before their ancient marking stones, hoping for enlightenment. He hopes to lead the ogres one day to their ultimate supremacy over all the "squishies."

9th Level Ogre Shaman

Currently in: somewhere in the Desert of Ro or the swamps beyond
FAENWIN

Faenwin is a young half-elf, who has come from Freeport to the land of the elves to seek his fortune. He bears no love for the orcs, and finds the local Crushbone Orcs more deadly and organized than the orcs in his homeland. His favorite instrument (currently) is the drum.

14th Level Half-Elf Bard

Currently in: Desert of Ro
NEGALL

Negall is an elusive sort, and little is known of him, as he always appears in different forms wherever he is, and claims to be friends to both humans and ogres alike. It is believed that he is a dark elf, spying on humans for an unknown reason.
MUSE

Muse is the person who started it all, and his voice and thoughts are still with this page even if he is not. To the right one of the few pictures of him, as he mapped out Southern Karana (back when there were no other people in the zone, if anyone can remember that). Back in those days, the only sounds rolling across the plains were the sounds of a young bard's drum as he followed his curiousity into the world...

5th Level Half Elf Bard

Currently in: Everquest Beta-Test
