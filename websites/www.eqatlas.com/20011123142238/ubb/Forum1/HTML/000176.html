I have brought a couple of characters up through levels 18 to 21 in Highpass Hold hunting orcs. They drop some ringmail armor, some rawhide, some bronze weapons. They drop a lot of rusty weapons also, but they drop reasonably good coin for that level and the exp is good. And there are a couple of named orcs that drop halbards woth about 10pp and more rarely a 25% weight reduction backpack. Both of my characters ended up with a backpack but I got lucky on the rolls.

As an SK I don't know if your faction will let you get to the merchants at first. Killing the orcs will help faction and the Orc Scalps will get you faction when turned in to the Captain (don't remember his name).

You might have to get on a waiting list to get in a group but the turnover is pretty fast and there is always a group going. If you do what you're class is supposed to do in a group and hold up you end poeple will recognize this. Once you get a good reputation the group leaders who know you will often move you up the list. It pays to be friendly and cooperative and do your job in this camp. Of course you won't be there for long if you get good groups because you should get about a level per evening.

The crocs in Oasis are better exp but zip for loot and heavily hunted.
