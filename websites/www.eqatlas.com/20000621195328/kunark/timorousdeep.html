Description

The Timorous Deep is the name of the ocean that lies between Kunark and the other continents of Kunark. There are several islands that are inhabited by the outcasts of the continent of Kunark. There is also a port that the Ogres operate that lies on the boat route from Oasis of Marr to the Overthere.

Dangers

There is a dragon-like creature that occasionally swims past the teleportation room (described below) and is KOS to all, as well as being in his 50's in level. You are also unable to bind in this zone...

Benefits

There is an underwater portal room located in between some sunken Combine Towers. These pots will teleport you, one way, to many of the cities in Norrath (excluding most human ones). There is also apparently a beautiful underwater oasis with in located at -11530, -3755, or tunnel at -11330, -1380.

Travelling To and From Timorous Deep

The Timorous Deep lies on a trade route and transport route between Kunark and the other continents. As such, it has many connections. Refer to the Kunark Boat Travel Guide for more information.

There is also a teleportation room that is located within the Timorous Deep, with which you can teleport to several of the major cities within the world. A description of this room can be found at the Timorous Deep Teleportation Room Guide.
