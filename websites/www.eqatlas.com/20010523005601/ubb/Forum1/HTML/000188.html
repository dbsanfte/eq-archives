At Lev 26 the Hill Giants in the Rathe Mountains might be a tad harsh unless you're in a VERY GOOD group. However, the Mountain Giants in Warsliks Woods (Kunark) will be very doable to someone at your level.

They do not drop coin. However, they have various items that can be sold to merchants for about 10pp. Sometimes, you can even get these cool earrings that sell for almost 1kpp.

Sorry, HG's aren't do-able at 26..spells stop being entirely resisted at 28. They do drop up to 48pp as well, not including gold/silver/copper. At 26 your best bet for loot would be to forget loot till around 30 and sit on Icy Orcs in Everfrost for a couple of days and lvl quickly to 30. Then go get into a HG group. You can make over 100pp in an hour with a decent group there. Also, if you really need loot and don't want to take the time to lvl there's always the bandits in WK, turn in the sashes to Cheesgard in S.Qeynos..U can net 100pp in a couple of hours...or if you don't mind being KOS to druids, kill treants. Great loot there also.
