taobob
Explorer 		posted 08-28-2000 04:02 PM            
Wisps.... definately wisps for level 10... experience and great loot... Erud's Crossing is a good place, althougg waiting for the boat on a corpse run can be especially hellish. There are lots of places to hunt wisps, W Commanlands being another good spot, although at 10 you could be easily overwhelmed by 2 wisps, or 1 wisp and a Young Kodiak.

The best advice I can give you would be to try out hunting in Everfrost. The tundra is perfect for level 8-10 mobs, as long as you stay near the guards and get grouped with a healer. Polar Bears, Snow Leopards and Snow Orc Troopers are all plentiful, and worth a good deal of exp. As a Ranger you should get to know Everfrost anyway, because the real money can be made by tracking the elusive Karg and Martar Icebear there (Martar is KOS though so be careful) for the higher levels to kill. I did this at 10th with my Rogue (hiding and sneaking around the tundra) and was able to make over 100pp in one night. It's also good to make friends with a Shaman about your level, because not having to pay for SOW can be a godsend...

Another little known spot, although much further away from civilization is South Ro. The orc camps there are rarely camped, and usually once the spawn is broken soloing there is almost too easy. This is also a great place for you to group with a healer (making friends with Troll Shaman can also provide a SOW for emergency evac).
