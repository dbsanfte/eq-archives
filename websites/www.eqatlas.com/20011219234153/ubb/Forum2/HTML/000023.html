The prices of standard armor will depend heavily on your charisma, or more probably, the prices you're able to haggle out with another player.

As for armor quests, go to the Temple of Solusek Ro to learn of your Cleric Armor. Or, if you prefer, go to Everquest.allakhazam.com
and read up on the quests there.

Have to warn you, though, cleric armor is one of the hardest to get, takes a lot of time and money. If you're under level 30, I'd suggest making do with banded or bronze for a while, it'll serve you well until then.
