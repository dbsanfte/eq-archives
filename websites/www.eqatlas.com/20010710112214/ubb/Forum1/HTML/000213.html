Guilds come in two flavors: Good and Bad. A good guild helps its members, organizes events, teaches newbies how and where to fight for best advancement, and is also a fun place to be. When you find yourself asking 'why am I here' you're probably in the wrong guild.

Guilds set their own requirements for membership. Some require you to reach a certain age (level) before joining, some require a certain class or race, some (like mine) require you to be female or male.

My suggestion would be to shop around. Find one that fits your style of play and personality. This way you get the most out of the experience while contributing back to the others you are playing with.

Good luck and safe hunting!
