BREASTPLATE OF FORBIDDEN RITES
Go to Thurgadin. You must have at least kindly faction to receive the quest.

Loremaster Dorinan can be found in the cleric guild/library. He asks you to bring him 3 pieces of black marble and corroded breastplate so that he may fashion a new breastplate for you.

The 3 pieces of black marble are dropped of various 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The corroded breastplate is a very rare drop from giants in Kael.

Hand the 3 pieces of black marble (unstacked) and corroded breastplate to Dorinan and you will receive the Breastplate of Forbidden Rites.
