Joining the Sisterhood

What do we offer?

Far Dareis Mai offers a tightly knit community of women on the Quellious server. We host weekly events, usually to get sisters a quest item, or to just generally rock a zone. Despite our size, we present terrific force, and organizational ability. But best of all, we offer the best guildchat anywhere!

What do you need to do?

In order to join Far Dareis Mai you meet a few basic requirements.

  • You must be level 35 or higher
  • You must be playing a female avatar
  • You must group with our members several times for consideration
