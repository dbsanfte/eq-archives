Introduction

Who are we?

We are the Maidens of the Spear. We have travelled a long way from our home in the Three Fold Land to this place we know as Norrath, on a plane known simply as Quellious. We have gathered here to dance the spears again, against the Shadow, in its myriad forms.

In English please!

We are the only all women's guild on the Quellious server. We're a bunch of girls (and guys pretending to be girls) that like to do stuff and have fun together. Our adventures have taken us all over Norrath, and our sisterhood has kept us strong. We fight, kill things and take their stuff, and generally have a great time chatting about inane stuff, like Bessy.
