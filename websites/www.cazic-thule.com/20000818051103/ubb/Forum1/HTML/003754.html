A man was standing on a beach. This beach was littered for as far as the eye could see both directions with Starfish that had washed up on the shore after a recent storm.

Looking down the beach, this man noticed another man stoop down, pick something up and toss it in the ocean. Time and time again this man did the same thing and he realized that he was picking up the starfish and throwing them back into the ocean.

Well the first man was thought this was insane for you see there were literaly hundreds or thousands, maybe even millions of starfish up and down the beach.

So when the 2nd man came close, the 1st man walked up and asked him, "Sir, may I ask why you are doing this, throwing these starfish back into the ocean? There must me hundreds of thousands covering the beach. You cannot hope to think you can get to each one of them? Do you think your efforts really matter?"

The 2nd man stooped, picked up another starfish, tossed it into the ocean, looked at the 1st man and stated, "Well, it mattered to that one."

===========================================

Friends, please remember this story the next time you see someone needing help finishing a hard mob, needing a desperate heal to avoid death, needing SoW or Clarity or a Res, needing answers to a question or price on an item or just needing some congraduations for making a level or gaining a hard sought after item. You know the majority of satisfaction most get out of this game is not coded into the program, but from RL friendships and interactions developed within EQ.

Farewell my brothers and sisters, stay safe.
