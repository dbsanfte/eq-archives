Powder of Nok - Froglok nok shamans - Firioina Vie

Heart of Ice - Icebone skeletons - Lake of Ill Omen.

Ton Warrior Totem - froglok ton warriors - Swamp of No Hope.

Sabertooth Mane - Lake of Ill Omen & Overthere.
