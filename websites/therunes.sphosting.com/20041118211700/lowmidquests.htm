This is the second quest in the badge series, and you will need to complete the Investigator's Badge first to be able to finish this one.

Talk to Vegalys Keldrane, who can be found in North Qeynos, and ask him "how can I advance further in rank". He will tell you about the Interrogators and that they are in need of more recruits. Now hand him your Investigator's Badge and you'll receive the Briefing Documents. The documents will tell you to search out an Interrogator in South Karanas, and also tell you to obtain a Confession Document from an Investigator. Run over to South Qeynos and talk to the investigator and say "please give me a confession document". Once you have the confession document, run to South Karanas and locate the Interrogator, who is near the Hermit's Hut.

Hand him the Briefing Documents and he'll start talking to you. He'll tell you about Theodore Exanthem and he will ask you to obtain Theodore's confession, as well as the heads of the two fugitives. The interrogator will then walk over to the Hermit's Hut and call out Theodore. There will be a small verbal battle and then the Interrogator will tell you to hit Theo. Kill him and Theodore will immediatly respawn and ask you what you want. Tell him "take us to the location", and he'll tell you to follow him. He leads you over to the druid rings and you'll see Markus and Morley. Now hand Theodore the confession document and you'll receive the Signed Confession in return. Now attack Markus and Morley (who will summon some undead to fight with them). Kill them both and loot their heads. Now hand the Interrogator the two heads and the signed confession and you'll receive the Interrogator's Badge as your reward.
