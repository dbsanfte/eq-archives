September 28, 2002

There is growing unhappiness being expressed by many enchanters over the direction our class is heading with the Planes of Power Expansion. (A sentiment I agree with) There are several threads at the boards on CastersRealm and now one concerning the fact that enchanters need to be more unified- If enchanters want to be herad... The original poster hit the nail on the head, there is no one site that is considered "the enchanter site" While I certainly would LOVE for this site to be it, I would also gladly patronize any enchanter site that was.

In the meantme I suggest anyone who is upset, concerned about the future of Enchanters to visit The Developers Corner and let them know your concerns.

If you are a European player, I guess you have heard the latest decision to let Ubisoft handle distrubution of POP. However, in a mind boggling twist, not only will the expansion be available to you 2 months after release, to add insult to injury you will only be able to play it on a "Euro" server. That's right. You will not have your choice of servers. I have yet to be able to figure out any reasoning for this. /boggle. Many European players in my alliance are placing orders now through US distributors or making other arrangments to have the "real" expansion shipped to them.

In addition to the zone guides I'm begining to add (yes I'm behind I know) I would also like to add a slow/mezz/charm guide. Please help me make this a complete list by sending me a list of mobs you know to be unslowable, unmezzable, or not charmable. I found out the hard way lastnight that the Dark Assasins in Umbral are NOT charmable in addition to be unmezzable. *sigh* How wish I had done this research before LOL.
Send your list!

September 6, 2002

There were a couple of changes in the patch that affected enchanters. The first one being the Cast Sight line of spells. Not that big of a deal, but it's now working as it did originally. You cast it on any target, and you see from their prospective. You can then target anything in their line of vision to switch your view.

Now the BIG news that surprisingly little has been said about. Single mezzes are overwriting the AoE mezzes. Also we are now able to overwrite the bard mezz. This is HUGE news. For the time being at least, we are able to AOE that nasty train, then go back and single mezz at your convience. Whether or not this was intented remains to be seen. Verant has said many times that the ability to overwrite AoE with a single was not going to be in the cards for us because it would be "too easy" Let's cross our fingers that this was intented.

I recieved an e-mail today regarding pets and giving them high damage/low delay weapons. I remember waaaay back Abashi saying that pets were not subject to damage/delay bonuses. However, so many things have changed since Abashi left, but I don't recall ever seeing anything after that, so I was unable to answer the question with any certainty. That got me thinking that that would be a good area to add to the site. If you have solid information, data, any thing that you could send me I will put it together and try to get some solid information on this. EMAIL

September 1, 2002

As reported on CastersRealm, some really neat changes are active on the test server! All I can say is WOW! There are several changes, but here are a couple of the biggest ones. You can see the entire article here

New particle engine
 This will allow you to control the particles that are in your face, allowing you to distance them. A new option that allows you to control the density of the spell effect. The range is 0.0 - 1.0. New spell effects. WOOT. CastersRealm has a few screenshots of them. The SOW is soooo neat!

Ability to Delete Spells from Spellbooks!

Finally! We will be able to delete store bought spells from our spellbooks. It will not return to a scroll. Once its deleted it's gone.
