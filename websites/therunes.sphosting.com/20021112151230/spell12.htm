Charm
Magic
Random
Fizzle adj: 25%
Used to be thought that your CHA directly affected duration of this spell. Lawerence Poe, the spell guy in Verant, recently stated in an interview that it had very little to do with it. It was based on your level and level of the mob.Be careful with this spell. When it works, it's wonderful! But keep in mind that the situation can quickly get out of hand. When charm breaks you will have both mobs on you. While under charm, your pet will follow the normal pet commands. When charm breaks be ready to stun with the color spell, then mezz and mem blur if you can. Once the fight is over and hopefully your pet still alive, break charm by casting invisible on yourself or hide if you are a DE. Also a DOTed mob cannot be charmed, useful information if someone is using charm to KS mobs.
Memory Blur
Magic
Instant
Fizzle adj: 25%
1: Wipe Hate List + 10 levels

This spell is excellent to save people in your group as well as yourself. Many of the enchanter spells are mega-taunts, this will wipe the hate list and take you or the current mobs hate list and remove it. Mem blur is the reason that any group with an enchanter can't be KS'ed from. Problem with this spell is that as of 3/01 there is no way to know if it actually took. It is on the "class balancing" list however.
