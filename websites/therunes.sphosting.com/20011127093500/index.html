Oct 25, 2001
Taken from Cazic-Thule Corner

Some VERY interesting information (and spoilers) about the upcoming Luclin expansion. Here's some of the highlights:

Metal Info
:There's a new metal in Luclin called Acrylia. It comes from giant mushrooms. There's a zone called the Acrylia Mines off of the Grimling Forest (which has some of these mushrooms). Expect it to be enchantable.

Enchanter:
-aggro effecting spells work like the following: when you have the aggro buffer, when a melee hit generates aggro, the buff multiplies the aggro created by a percentage and that becomes the new aggro. the aggro reducer operates the same way, just that it reduces amount of aggro generated. these effects DO NOT simply add an amount of aggro to each action that generates aggro.
Example calculations:
With a 35% aggro enhancer buff, a melee hit/spell affect that would normally produce 100 points of aggro will now generate 135 points of aggro.
With a 25% aggro reducer buff, a melee hit/spell affect that would normally produce 80 points of aggro will now only generate 60 points of aggro.
As far as what percentages the spell effects will have is yet to be determined

Reaching Luclin:

Wizards and Druids will NOT be able to port between the current world and Luclin. Every 45 minutes, at the large (like Greater Faydark) wizard spires, a scripted event will take place, giving people the opportunity to go to The Nexus, the entry zone. There's no delay to zone back to the various spires from The Nexus. There will be druid and wizard ports within Luclin. Death MAY send you back to the nexus if you die in Luclin and are bound in the current world, instead of to your bind point. Cleric Rezes will bring people to their corpse, regardless of location. (People leaving corpses to quickly move between planets is a concern though.)

The post contains a lot more info, including how the bazaar will work, and he also talks of a couple of zones that will require DMF or EB to breathe in. Excellent read!
