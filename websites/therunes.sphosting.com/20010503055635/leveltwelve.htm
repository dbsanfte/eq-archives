Charm
Magic +25
Random
The duration of charm directly correlates to your CHA stat. Be careful with this spell. When it works, it's wonderful! But keep in mind that the situation can quickly get out of hand. When charm breaks you will have both mobs on you. While under charm, your pet will follow the normal pet commands. When charm breaks be ready to stun with the color spell. Once the fight is over and hopefully your pet still alive, break charm by casting invisible on yourself or hide if you are a DE. Also a DOTed mob cannot be charmed.
