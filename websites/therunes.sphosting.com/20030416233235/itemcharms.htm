Taken from EQSpoilers

1) Speak to Firon Dernkal in Neriak Commons. He can be found on the Indigo Brotherhood building. Tell him that you are "willing to help you locate Gerod" and he will gvive you a Symbol.

2) Kill Brownies until you have two Brownie Torsos. This is a rare, random drop.

3) Give the Symbol to Eirod Haerod in Mistmoore. He is near the zone-in.

4) Give the two Brownie Torsos to Eirod Herod in Mistmoore. He will give you Sealed War Documents.

5) Give the War Documents to Firon Dernkal in Neriak Commons.

6) Kil mobs in Dulak's Harbor until you have all four Medallions of Service. These are Gold, Copper, Silver and Platinum.

7) Give the Four MEdallions to Firon Dernkal in Neriak Commons for your reward.
