In order to do research you will first need a Tome of Endless Enchantments. Easily purchased in any town that has an Enchanters Guild. Place one of each corresponding pages in the book and click "Combine". The success rate is determined by you skills in research as well as your level. I have had success combining my next highest level of spells, and very limited success trying to go higher than that. Some spells such as Gravity Flux and Feedback are hard to get. The pages are more rare than others and they seem to have a higher fail rate. I suggest either waiting until you level up again, or having an elder enchanter combine them for you.

To unfade pages, put one faded page and nothing else into the Tome, click "Combine". Yes it is possible to fail unfading pages also. *sigh*
