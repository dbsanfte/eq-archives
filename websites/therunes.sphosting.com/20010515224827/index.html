IN THE NEWS

From the Official Everquest boards:

Changes in Pet Charming
08.05.01
Prior to the 5/8 patch when I recharmed a mob it immediately went back to it's target. Since the 5/8 patch I always either have to order the pet to attack the previous target, or take a hit.
Is this intentional, or a bug?

That was intentional, and most certainly something we should have mentioned. Sorry about that.

Yes, when charm breaks and you re-charm the target, you will have to command it to attack again. We're changing this as part of something larger that is, basically, an exploit. The extra step of hitting the /pet attack macro is something that we had to do to make something else work right.

Sorry that I can't be more precise, but we're still working on it, and exploit fixes aren't always things we announce.
