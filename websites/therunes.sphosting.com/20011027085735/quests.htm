The Quest
Incandescent Gloves
Temple of Solusek Ro

You say, 'Hail Sultin'

Sultin says 'I am Sultin - keeper of the three [incandescent]s of power and the [rod] of Insidious Glamour. If you are an enchanter, perhaps we can deal.

You say, 'What incandescent'

Sultin says 'There are three incandescent items of power - a [wand], [mask] and [gloves]. Are you [interested] in any of them?

You say, 'Gloves interested'

Sultin says 'My personal favorite! Incandescent gloves. If you bring me the components to make another pair, I will give you my gloves. Incandescent gloves are made by sewing together four other sets of gloves. Glowing gloves can be found on our mortal enemies, the shadowed men. Radiant gloves can be found on Radiant in the mazes of Cazic-Thule. Enchanted and gleaming gloves can be purchased for 50 golden coins from Tizina of the Lavastorm gypsies and a bloodstone from Tarn Vislin of HighKeep. Bring me all four sets of gloves and I will give you a pair of incandescent ones.
You'll Need


Glowing Gloves -Multiple Zones - Shadow Men. To the best of my knowledge these will NOT poof when you log.

Radiant Gloves - Cazic Thule - Radiant

Enchanted Gloves - Lavastorm Mountains - Tizina (purchased - 50 gold)
You say, 'hail Tizina'

[Hand Tizina 5 platinum]

Tizina says 'Since you have given me gold, I will give you gloves. For more magic gloves, give a bloodstone to Tarn Visilin in the Highkeep library.
You gain experience and recieve Enchanted Gloves

Gleaming Gloves - HighKeep - Tarn Vislin (purchased - Bloodstone)

You say, 'hail Tarn Visilin'

Tarn Visilin says 'Hello. I am Tarn Vislin.

[Hand Tarn Visilin a Bloodstone]

Tarn Visilin says 'The gleam of blood - and now the gleam of gloves.

Your faction standing with BetaNeutral got better.
You gain experience and gleaming gloves.

Now go back to Sultin, and hand him all 4 gloves, you will recieve Incandescent Gloves
