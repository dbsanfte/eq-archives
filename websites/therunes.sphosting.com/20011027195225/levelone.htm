Minor Illusion-

None
Duration: 36min

Turns you into the nearest inanimate object. As first this spell seems to be not much more than a good parlor trick and seemed useless. However, you can cast this on yourself and meditate in peace. This makes you non-aggro. There is supposedly a way to be able to cast this and still be able to move. I myself have witnessed it. However, I'm not able to remember how this is done. If you do know please pass the info on. Occasionally, there isn't an object close enough to you to allow the illusion. When this happens put a piece of copper on the ground, cast it and then pick it back up. Word of caution here, as well as Illusion:Tree. When you die, you're corpse will retain the illusion. Be sure that you will be able to find yourself. I heard of someone who had cast Tree illusion in a forest, died and was unable to retrieve his body..
