June 29
Walking through the streets of Qeynos, I am somewhat distracted by my thoughts. These last few weeks the Lady Siniana has continued to visit me in my dreams and I cannot go half a day without thinking of her. I can’t help but wonder what’s wrong with me; I am acting like a lovesick child. I, the great Temyez, conqueror of women, ruler of hearts! "Bah", the thought brings a smile to my lips and a slight spring to my step.
As I round a corner, I am thrown against a wall by a robed figure, I can feel the tip of a dagger pressing against my gut from underneath the robes. He puts his face directly in front of mine, I can smell his rancid breath as he quietly whispers "Ahhhhh, Temyez, my old friend, how have you been all these years?" I am completely stunned, caught by surprise unable to answer. I quickly look over the face in front of me, slightly haggard and unshaven, one eye covered with a patch the other bloodshot and staring wildly, the eye of a madman. My eyes keep returning to the eye patch that covers his left eye, it has the design of a skull on it. I try to wiggle free of his grip but the tip of the dagger slides in a tiny bit, piercing me a little, holding me more solidly than any chain might. Quickly looking past my assailant I see two guards standing aways off, they both have noticed what is happening and are staring at us waiting to see if there might be trouble. "Would you bring the guards down on yourself?" I say, glancing in their direction. He turns his head slightly and upon seeing the guards relaxes his grip significantly, moving back slightly he assumes a friendlier posture towards me although the dagger stays where it is. "Who are you? What do you want?" I say to him, he glares at me for a moment. "You don’t remember me?" he says, he looks completely amazed, "You took my eye." I look at him while digging through the depths of my memories trying to put a name to this face, trying to make sense of what he is saying, "perhaps you have me confused with someone else", I offer. "You are Temyez, drunkard and braggart" he states plainly. "Yes, ……. I mean no, …….. argggg,I mean yes, I am Temyez, but I am no drunkard and I do not brag". Lifting the eye patch he reveals the wound that has left him blind, a large gash starts just under the eye and goes upwards to the mangled eye socket, "you did this!" he hisses, replacing the patch over his eye. Still trying to remember who this man is I shake my head "no, this is a mistake. You have me confused with someone else." He lowers the dagger and backs away "You don’t even remember those you have hurt. There is no mistake, you took my eye, you embarrassed and shamed me, you took the only thing I ever loved, and for that you will die." He stares at me coldly "We will meet in the arena tonight and I will have my revenge." "I don’t think so" I say, "I do not wish to harm you and I will not fight you." His cold stare becomes one of dark rage, "You will meet me in the arena at sunset or I will kill all your friends. I will hunt them down and kill them one by one. Klymeth, Conwen, Haemish, Siniana, oh yes, I know all of them, and unless you fight me tonight I will find them, and I will cut their throats." Images of my friends being attacked by this man flashed through my mind, images of him sneaking up while they slept or jumping them in a dark alley, images I could not stand. He stops while walking away and turns toward me, "Tonight at sunset in the arena." He smiles coldly and walks away. I am left shaking my head, confused, not knowing what to do next. "Why?" I ask myself, What is this about? I cannot remember this man, nor do I recall any of the things he claims I have done. I don’t know what to do, yet there are only a few hours until sunset. "I will meet this man for my friends sake. I will find out what he is talking about and I will calm him down, but I will not fight him."

Time waits for no man, and before I know it the hours have passed by. I find myself entering the arena shortly after sunset; it is empty at this hour, all the battles and games of the day have long been over. Storm clouds have gathered in the night sky, lightning flashes overhead and thunder booms in the distance. My self appointed opponent is sitting in the middle of the battlefield, waiting for me. As soon as I enter he addresses me, "I am so glad you came Temyez" he yells " Here I am free to kill you without worry of retribution from the guards." Even from this distance I can see the wicked smile that comes across his face. Rain starts falling, I yell back to him, "What is this about? I don’t even know why I am here." I am determined not to fight, to somehow talk my way out of this, I move closer to him so we don’t have to yell. "I don’t even know your name, I will not fight until I know what this is about." He smiles even wider, "I care not if you fight, only that you die" he pauses, glaring at me, "but, if it is an explanation you seek, then perhaps this will do." His smile disappears, "My name is Anean and many seasons ago, you came into an Inn one evening, an Inn that I was at with my beloved. You drank and boasted of your travels and adventures. As the night progressed you became drunker and louder, you began arguing with another traveler. As we stood to leave you threw a glass at the person you were arguing with, in your drunkenness the glass flew wide and crashed into my eye, shattering and blinding me. A fight broke out through the entire Inn, I was knocked unconscious and unable to protect my love. She was accidentally killed during the fight, when I awoke days later, I had lost her, I had lost my life" he looks up to the sky and screams, "ARGGGHHHHH, YOU TOOK EVERYTHING FROM ME, YOU DESTROYED MY LIFE!" He stands up quickly, before I can even speak he begins chanting and suddenly I am thrown back and slammed onto the ground, something is choking me, I can’t breath, I can hardly see. The rain comes down in buckets, I feel like the life is being drained out of me, crawling to my knees I look across to Anean. The lightning flashes and I can see him smiling, "Ahhhhh, I feel much better now", I can hear him say. He walks towards me still chanting and I am bathed in darkness, I can feel the darkness on me suffocating me and in the background Anean laughing. My mind is racing with thoughts about what he has said, it is true I used to be a drunk, but that was long ago, it seems almost a lifetime ago. Anean is shrieking, "YOU WILL DIE TONIGHT TEMYEZ!" he continues chanting and a powerful wave of nausea sweeps over me, I roll over onto my back, I can’t see anything everything is completely black but I can hear him chanting. I can feel my blood begin to boil, my God the pain, it feels as though I may explode. I can hear him yelling at me, "YOU WILL DIE HERE, THEN YOUR FRIENDS WILL DIE, HAHAHAHAHAHA!" Even through the pain my mind grabs hold of this last statement, "My friends will die?" What is this? He is killing me and then he will kill my friends? He is yelling uncontrollably now calling upon his God, laughing and shrieking obscenities at me, ranting and raving to the night. How did I get into this? He is killing me and then he means to kill my friends. What can I do? I pull myself up onto my knees and whisper, "Tunare, please help me, give me strength, I need you now." The darkness starts to fade and I can see him standing not far in front of me, the nausea dissipates and my blood cools. Anean looks coldly at me and says, " Now, it is time to die." Suddenly a lightning bolt shoots out of the sky and blasts the stone embankment near us, the stone explodes and Anean turns. The distraction is a gift from Tunare and it is all I need, I pull my dirk from its sheath and with the last bit of strength I have, I let it fly. The throw is true, and when Anean turns back to me the dirk finds its mark and lands with a thunk in the base of his neck. The sharp blade cuts cleanly and the dirk sinks all the way to the hilt. His one eye opens wide and there is a look of shock on his face, he falls to his knees just a few feet from me. I can see him mouthing words trying to speak, but no sounds come forth, only blood. He sits on his knees for a few moments longer then falls over. I look up to the sky as the rain falls and say a silent "Thank you" to Tunare. I try to stand but the fatigue and exhaustion of the battle overtake me, everything starts to spin and I fall, passing out. When I awake the rain has stopped and it is almost dawn, I drag myself up, pain shoots through my body and I almost pass out again, but I stop and wait for the weakness to pass. Anean’s body is laying not far from me on the arena floor, I slowly walk over and pull my dirk from his neck, his one eye stares up blankly. As I half limp, half walk from the arena I see a drunk walking down the street, he sways and blubbers to himself as he goes along, his life a waste of whiskey and forgotten memories. I lower my head and as I stagger down the road towards an Inn and help, I begin to cry.
