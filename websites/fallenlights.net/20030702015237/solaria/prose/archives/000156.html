One of the Quiet Ones

She was one of the quiet ones
The soft spoken word
Distant light.

Hid from the god and left alone
She with the masks
Unknown truth

She was a quieting one
Brushing of voice
Fingers, touches

Dancer with darkness, burned from fate
Lost in the pit
Falling fast

She was a quiet one.
She was.
