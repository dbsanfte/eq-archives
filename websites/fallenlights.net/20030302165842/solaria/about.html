Solaria is my writing site.


Part of what I've rewritten of my novel as well as a whole lot of short stories are housed here, dating back to around 1996 or so and up until the present.

Sadly, I forget to update this site (mainly because I don't write as much as I used to.)

But, at this moment, there are a few new things here and there.

A lot is personal babblings that I created in deep stress periods, others are just fits of fancy that came about when bored.

I hope you enjoy.

I do not like writing about myself... so I won't.

And a lot of this work will probably not make sense. I will never call myself a proper writer. Just someone that throws letters onto the page every now and then.

Image is a drawing of mine that I inverted for lack of anything better to do.
