knock

There was a knock on the backside of her head, a hard one that will probably leave a bruise come morning. Told she was worthless once again, reasuring that there will be a letter scrawled to explain things to the one that cared here. THE ONLY ONE THAT CARED. Then she would leave. Gone. Not caring because that was the treatment she received.

Or maybe they did care and she just didn't see it through the malicious sounding voices.

Words are jumbling. Life is twisted and filled of hate and dispair.

Things for the while looked down upon her, making her meek and small, tired of hiding the lie. If she spoke the lie, she would be removed from someone that was this little light that kept her smiling forcefully.

There is nothing here for her.
