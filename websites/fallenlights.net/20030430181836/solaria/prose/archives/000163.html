memories

Shards of memories,
in lancing thoughts.
Why are you now
dancing within the fool?

Fingers drift slowly
as beauty bubbles frothy ghosts.

You dream like I
on cold summer's eve.
Prophet children with large eyes.
Lanterns that watch

shattered thoughts
drifting backwards till they crash.

There is a thought,
a far away thought.
Running from me,
fading dreams through my fingers.

Make the time stop.
Fragments of memories.
