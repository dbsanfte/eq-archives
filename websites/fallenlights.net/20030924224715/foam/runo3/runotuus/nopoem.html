You wont make a poem
you're present like a cat
you won't harden, to make mother-of-pearl
on the bottom of the mind
like another girl

who was a dream from which I woke up
and noticed I'd slept in a cellar
impressions of concrete on the skin

you wouldn't shine like her
you won't turn into glass
because sometimes your body
is in bloom like a weekday

when I didn't have you
