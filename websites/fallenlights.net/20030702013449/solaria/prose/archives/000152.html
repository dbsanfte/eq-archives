Of a child yet to be

fingers dance
a dreamless touch
drifting unmerrily
playing a tune
a haunted tune
of a child yet to be

creatures hunt
a bloody lust
stalking silently
red-filled eyes
bone white teeth
chasing a child to be

people hide
people scream
people dance with glee
a wicked song
strung all day long
for a child meant to be

prophets die
singers whine
crone to a tuneless hum
watch the earth
and darkened skies
for the child yet to come
