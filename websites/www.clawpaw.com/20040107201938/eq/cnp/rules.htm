Claws N Paws

Everquest Role-playing Guild
 Cazic-Thule Server

Rules and Regulations

 

 We Role-play. Meaning that we create and go by a somewhat set history and or background of the character we are playing at the time. We do our best to stay in character in all public chats. ...We will always maintain a distinction between OOC (Out Of Character) information and IC (In Character) information.

  We stay in character (there will be a designated OOC channels we will advise those of you to use that do not wish to speak In Character, even when grouping with others not of our guild. 

  We accept all levels of Role-players (from novice to expert) and will assist everyone, who is willing to listen and learn more about the art of role-playing. Helping members in expanding their history and characters. We will actively seek and create stories, and backgrounds as characters advance through life.

 We will accept all time zones no matter what time zone you fall into, we will try to keep you up to date on things within our guild. You will not be left behind in the story; someone will fill you in. You will not be looked badly upon if you cannot make an event due to time zone issues, or have Real Life obligations, we all know this is just a game and it’s a healthy mentality that’s important!

 We enjoy grouping together. We group often with other guild members for numerous reasons. Perhaps we’ll need fish for our brewers to make oils for our tailors, so we’ll go fishing and have a lot of fun role-playing a swim as well! We try to participate in as many guild outings as we can. We strive to come prepared for guild outings and groupings. When you join a group guild or otherwise, expect to be there for a while, do not expect to just up and leave after being there for 15-20 minutes.

 We are not greedy & WE ARE NOT a Raiding guild.  We believe that making other guild members stronger makes the whole guild stronger, but not at the expense of other Guild Members. We didn't join the guild to get handouts and power leveled. We don't expect to have items handed to us. We will happily group with other members to help them get the items they need (Such as Epic MoB's) and donations are always welcomed. We prefer to trade, sell, or give our old items to guild members before offering them to others outside the guild. We however, do wish to see /auction selling tidbits on the Guild Channel for they are more often out of character than in character. Few of us are “vendors” after all. If you feel you weren’t included in a loot roll you should have been, please poke your group leader/raid leader and point out the error, explain where you stand. Mistakes and misunderstandings (and missed poses) can often lead to hurt feelings.

 We have great pride in our guild name is always in consideration when we act out of character. The guild also requests you use /roleplay (or without it) instead of  /anon at all times.  Let your tag be seen with pride and let the association of any alliances be easily recognized and our quality of players, role-playing and policies.  We are always conscious of having "Claws N Paws" tag over our heads. We don't engage in behavior that reflects poorly on our guild. This includes:

1) Purposefully engaging a mob after it has been engaged by another party (kill stealing).

2) Demanding to share a spawn or area.

3) Using inappropriate language in public channels or guild channels.

4) Lying or cheating, either within the guild or outside the guild.

5) Having the right type of character name. Names for goofy reactions and based on characters of anime and cartoons are for non-in character guilds, but not appropriate for ours.

 We do not permit harassment of players, be they in the guild, or out will not be tolerated. If a player asks you to stop /telling them, or /gu to leave them be OOCLY due to distasteful or disturbing conversation (some may be minors), you must immediately discontinue. If a player has a problem with another player, we the officers of Claws N Paws request you come see us. WE as a guild and as friends cannot aid you ic or or out without knowing your situation. We will investigate and react accordingly.

 We strive to be excellent strategists and group players, learning everyday from experiences and each other. Group leaders will be asked to develop group composition. Players will be asked to use the spells and skills that make the most sense for their class. We ask all melee’ to assist the designated main tank, (MA – Main Assist). Casters are asked to buff parties appropriately for their class. We will strive to adapt to the ever-changing situations within the realm of our world of Everquest. We want our characters to be able to assist others as best they can, you will see your kindness will be rewarded in many ways.

 Message boards are online and available to guests and members. Guild Members will have a separate board than the public one, able to be hidden from Guests/non members, and Guild Officers will have a secured and yet another board. We ask you regularly check boards and read up on adventures and travels and our calendar.  

 « Any of these actions that are caught being done are grounds for discussion among officers and possible grounds for removal from the guild, through warnings will be issued to allow a player to grow and change »
