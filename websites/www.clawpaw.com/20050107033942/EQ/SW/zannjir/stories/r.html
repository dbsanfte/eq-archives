Night or Light



Winds of water let me be, let the storms set me free. In the Darkest depths of my soul. I feel the beat of the war drums roll. Yet in the dark, there is a light. My inner strength helps me fight. My will is strong, my wrath unforgiving, my skills are sharp, I fight from the heart. Just to see the dawn of another day, I would go against what everyone would say.
