Hidden Mare



Somewhere in a time unknown to most. A world lost, and long forgotten, cultures primitive yet so advanced ........ so much more then our own. 
  
Clear blue water ripples, crimson shades of orange and red streak through the sky seeming endless. Motion in the shadows some near by trees. A light breeze, ever blade of grass bowing to it's will. 
  
One tender, cautious cloven hove reaches from behind the brush. Everything eerily quiet. A head raised, held high and proud, neck muscles pulse, strong, yet young. Light footed it prances out of hiding. 
  
All knowing eyes, it smells the stories on the wind. White silk mane blows wildly about. A sparkle against the shadows, a glittering ray of light. It's golden spiral horn, a symbol of magic and mystery. 
  
Ears perk up, a noise from near. Stomping the ground it rears up. Dashing away back into the shadowy brush from where it came. The lake is calm again, the sky still clear, all is quiet once more.
