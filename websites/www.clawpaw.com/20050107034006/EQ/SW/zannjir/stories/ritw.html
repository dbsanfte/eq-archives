Ride in the Woods



It's quite, dark as a light wind whistles and rustles the leaves on the trees. 
Eyes dart, they watch the darkness of the night as if at any moment it will come to life and attack. 
Nothing but the clatter of horses shoe's are heard this night as the riders pass through the gloom of the descending night. 
  
A branch is heard cracking in the distance. Horse's whine, panic stricken eyes look to and fro ..... as if waiting to see some hideous creature attack. 
All is quiet save the whinnying of the frightened steeds as they stand very still ........ another wind whistles through the foliage of the trees. 
  
Screaming men ........ the whines and clattering of the horses are heard for miles as they rear and dash off into the forest .......... Leaving their dead and trampled riders on the blood soaked ground ....... The faces of the dead men stark white, pale with not the slightest hint of life. 
  
Their eyes cool and glazed, wide with fright. 
One can only wonder what they saw before they met their swift unseen end. 
   


Tajifa Nyteshade
