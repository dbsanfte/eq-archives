Dark Hope


Prince of Darkness take my hand, pull me from this awful land. Fighting around me are children of light, dresses so pretty in gold, silk, and white. Lord of the night to whom I make this plea, come and make them set me free. Darkness engulfs me from above, then I feel it's curse, it's love. Thank yee, Lord you answered my plea, for a child of the Darkness I must be, for the light does hurt so much; to my sight, to my touch. 


  
Tajifa Nyteshade 
Feb. 21 2000
