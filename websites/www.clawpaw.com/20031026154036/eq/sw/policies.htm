What we are not ... 

Lets begin with a tale of a unified family. Unlike some guilds, Silent Watchers on Cazic-Thule Server is not about UBER loot,  Power leveling or the "GIVE ME" mentality. If that is what you seek, we are not for you. We are not about grabbing as much as we can and making oodles of cash. We are not about power leveling new alts or about seeing to it you get something done to the exclusion of other guild members. We are not about 'Look what I have" mentality, it's not our nature to show off to make others feel weak, but about sharing successes. Some guilds love this mentality and perhaps you might as well also, we are glad if you find each other and be joyous. However, if you want something else, family, unity .. let me tell us what we are...

 

What we are.. 

Silent Watchers is a family. We have our parents which are our leaders and even though leadership does exactly that, lead, we all have an equal voice. We are NOT lead by dictatorship, our Guild Master Gnarlianna prefers to lead with open communication and respect. We even hold votes when things are between unwritten lines. This has always been the way of the Silent Watchers. If this sounds like something you'd enjoy, please read on!

Meetings:

Silent Watchers tries to hold it's meeting once a month, usually on a Sunday unless Saturday works best for most involved. We have an agenda and open discussions and votes. New members must be within the guild 30 days before they're allowed to vote, though they can speak up at anytime during the meeting. An example of a vote is simple. New member applied to the guild, had 3 sponsors and now 30 days has passed, their probationary period as well as our own has come to an end. The guild now votes to either accept our new member or perhaps wish him/her well in seeking a new home, perhaps the fit was not as what we all would have best enjoyed. To be honest, very very few have not found a fit with us, our sponsorship program aids our new members find their niche.

 

Humor: 

Lets be blunt here, Silent Watchers is full of silly fun people. We're like family so we tend to giggle and laugh quite a bit. The overly serious tend to shy away from such members who might give you a weggie but not us! During raids the mood sometimes grows quite serious, no one likes to die of course, but a good well placed noogie always brightens my day!

 

Tradeskilling: (see our webpage on Tradeskill Tips)

Silent Watchers is a very Tradeskill intense guild. It's totally optional and if you wish to try to skill up, we're more than happy to try to help you. Others are skilling also so we do have a Tradeskill Coordinator, Ykina/Ixiola who is an Officer within the guild. She is a master Tinker, Fletcher, and too many others to name! Remember, you never HAVE to Tradeskill, some do enjoy donating items so they too may reap the rewards of stated food or tailored items. Talk to the guild and see where you can help or be helped, just remember the simple outline. Never abuse others and you will never be abused. So sayith the Lynx.

 

Please know that the Silent Watchers Guild, unlike so many others help our fellow tradeskillers. Any donations made are just that, voluntary and completely your choice. You never MUST donate. Period. However, many have found donating items reeps them the benefits of token goods in return such as Halas Pies! Also know that many of our tradeskillers would gladly hit the combine for you at NO CHARGE EVER. You supply the goods and we will hit the button, though success rates vary, like with all tradeskills, they can be FAILED. If you lack the goods but wish a tradeskilled item from one of our tradeskiller (example: Anayarie making you a Tunare Idol, she is our highest Guild Potter), you could pay the "COST" of making the item and that's all! It's how we say thank you for donations, it's how tradeskillers of the guild can afford to swallow some loss. 

 

When in doubt www.eqtraders.com for recipes and fantastic guides.

Guild Meeting August 16th, 2003 Policy amendments: 

  • Raid rules during alliance raiding: Guild calling raid uses their loot and policy rules for that raid. Raid Leader establishes who's guild is heading the raid. Please see the appropriate loot/raiding rules for each Guild as needed. There is never a time when any raid or hunt is required. You decide if you wish to attend or decline any invitation for any reason.

  • 2 Mains are allowed per PLAYER within Silent Watchers. No level limit is required to be considered a main OR alt.

  • Mains can be changed every 6 months at will, 3 months by announcement at a guild meeting, or a lesser time only by petition at a guild meeting.

  • Guild Roster is the official holder of titles and you MUST keep this roster on your characters up to date as time permits PLEASE :)
