New Member Info

So you are just joined Silent Watchers! 

Congrats to you and to us both! Here is some information I hope will help you along!

  • New members get a 30 day probationary period - what this means is simple really. When you join our new family, an adjustment period is needed so that both YOU and SW is happy with the union. Sometimes the fit isn't just right and nobody is happy or sometimes it's a match made in heaven. At the first guild meeting that is no less than three weeks from being tagged as a guild member, your membership is up for simple majority vote of the body of full members. Votes can be cast at the meeting, for those who cannot attend, they can be cast by proxy through any officer. After the vote, we hope to welcome you as a full member of the guild. Failing that, we will be most pleased to help you find a more suitable guild. 

  • New members can openly discuss any topic at the meeting agenda, however until you are voted into the guild with full membership rights, you may not yet vote on official poles.

  • New members and Old are asked to refrain from asking, begging or hounding anyone in guild or otherwise for Power leveling. While we don't hold the PL issue in either good nor bad regard, official SW does not PL any player or alts at anytime.

  • New members and Old are asked to refrain from /shouting or /oocly cursing or putting down any other player. Personal attacks are not tolerated. If you have a problem with someone, simply /tell them. If there is an issue, ask an officer of the guild to aid you in solving your problem.

  • New members and Old are asked to avoid KS'ing. We don't KS other people's kills. *KS - Kill Stealing* A good way to avoid this is to /assist your kill target (say a giant in rathe) if someone's name appears in your target box, it's taken, don't touch it. If it remains the giant's name, it's yours. Simple and effective. Mistakes are made sometimes however, we ask you simply apologize and refrain from further confrontation and KS. It's not about the loot and goods with SW, it's about being proud of your Guild Tag and the family who will stand with you, beside you and behind you at all times.

  • New members and Old are required to wear their Guild Tag at all times. /role-play is allowed (it doesn't hide your tag), but /annon does. We ask you not hide the tag. It makes others feel you're up to something or ashamed of your guild. It's a hot topic for those so proud to be in the family, it's like your sister turning her face and pretending she doesn't know you, it's simply frowned upon.

  • New members and Old are asked to refrain from personal attacks and vendetta's on all fronts, from forums to chatter, if there's an issue, keep it to /tell's and officers, that's what they're there for. Cooler heads always prevail.

  • New members are asked to join the LDCHAT via /join ldchat and SERVERWIDE.SWCHAT, so that we can communicate across servers and with our allied guilds Loyal Defenders and Children of Tunare.

Feel at home and welcomed yet? Seems like a lot but the truth is, it's the simple guidelines stating, be nice, play nice and we'll all be nice too! Welcome and hope you stay!
