How to Join

 
  • Contact one of our officers either online or through the online boards with the basic information: Class, Race, Level, brief character history. 
  • Acknowledge you realize this is a role-playing guild
  • Role-play a touch with one of our members and get one to refer you or an officer. 
  • Get tagged!
  • Please realize you're on a 30-day probationary period which you and the guild both can decide you're not made for one another, no hurt feelings and a fair parting of ways may occur. You will be welcomed as a full new member once your 30 days are up if all went according to ic and ooc plans.

Those who shouldn't join

You know there's a mismatch if... 

 
  • If you're looking for a raiding guild
  • If you're looking for an uber guild
  • If you're looking for a guild for cyber sexing 
  • If you're looking to collect goods and gifts to resell
  • If you're into power posing (posing 3 or 4 things without letting someone react)
  • If you ignore poses
  • If you can't take gentle tips and aid to make us all better role-players and guild mates.
