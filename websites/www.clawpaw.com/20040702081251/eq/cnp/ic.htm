In Character Consequences

 
  • In Character actions = in character consequences, simply put what you do may bite you on the rump OR aid your wishes. Example, if you're a rogue and try to pick the pocket of an Iksar Shadow Knight and he notices, you may get a pummling! 
  • Festivals will be planned and with festivals come marvelous role-play and opportunities for many things. Trading, drunken brawling, plays and bards with their songs and stories. If you tell a bad story, you  may get a mug tossed at you, and for that you may get a tickling. Drunken boxing won't be to death, but till 30 percent, and then you must live with knowing that XX beat the tar out of you! *laugh*
