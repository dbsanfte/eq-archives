Night or Light



Child of night vs. Child of light, who do you say will win the fight. I Draw my power from the night, he draws his from the light. To Vanquish me is his right, should he fair and win this fight. Blows are thrown from one to another, then there is a blinding light that smothers. I cough, and scream, my end is near, but suddenly I feel my prince is near. My Prince I call thee to my aid, to get rid of this light, please make it fade. Prince of Darkness lend me your hand, and together we will make this stand. 


Tajifa Nyteshade
