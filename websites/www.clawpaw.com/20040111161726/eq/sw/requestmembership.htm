Requesting Guild Membership

If your interested in joining up with the Silent Watchers, the forum "Interested in joining the guild?" is the place to talk to request it.  

 

What we need (minimum):

 
Tell us the name of your character
Tell us your race
Tell us your class
Tell us your current level

Tell us why you find our guild interesting

Tell us how you think you can benefit the guild and how we can benefit you as well.
