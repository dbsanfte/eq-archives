Baking to 250, my take on it!

 by Spiritlynx Heartmate    

First my tips!

  • Get yourself a Baking Geerlok if you wish to succeed but it will not speed up your skill increase, just your chances of success. Example. If your baking is 190, adding a Geerlok would make it 195.

  • Try to max out your wisdom or intelligence (depending what kind of caster or which is higher for you) when you bake. This will speed up your skill increases. 

  • Start collecting what you need before you actually need it. Example, if you see Blood Raven Parts for sale in POK (Plane of Knowledge) vendors for 1 plat and you're only at 180, by the time you're over 220 you may have a stack or 2 and thus easier to get you to 250 than if you started searching for POK meats at 220.

  • Ask your guild to check coffers for items such as Mushrooms and other foraged items you need, many druids donate their forages (and Rangers), for such training for their bakers!

  • Buy yourself a "Spit", it's a portable small oven you can bake in. It's tons better than looking for an oven. Some meals however, can't be made in a spit, oven specific meals for example or Halas Pies & Misty Thicket Picnic Baskets (to many ingredients).

  • Items you may need (or should get) - Portable spit, pot, filet knife, non-stick frying pan, dairy spoon and mixing bowl.

Trivial and Recipe List

  1. Batwings Crunchies - Batwings + frosting IN spit or oven yields 1, trivial 46. 

  2. Fish Rolls - Fresh Fish + batwings IN spit or oven yields 1, trivial 135.

  3. Fillet of Wolf, bear, lion or mammoth (You'll need these later on for Halas pies so save em). - Wolf/Bear/loin or Mammoth meat + filleting knife (have a blacksmith make for you) IN spit or oven yields 1 for bear and lion but for mammoth and wolf it yields 10.  You get your knife back no matter what after each combine. Trivial at 143.

  4. Patty Melt - Bear meat OR Lion meat + loaf of bread + Cheese (cheese is purchased at Jagged Pines) IN spit or oven with a Non-Stick Frying Pan (any smith can make and the mold is sold also in Jagged Pines), yields 1 banquet meal, trivial at 191.

  5. Pixie Power Sticks - 3 pixie dust (Lfay fae drop pixie dusts) + 1 Cinnamon Stick (foraged from Greater Fay) IN a Mixing Bowl, yields 6, trivial at 202 OR you can go the way of the Ohabah Truffles which is more expensive but less foraging is involved - dark chocolate cream (Dark Chocolate Cream - Cream (see below) + frosting + brown algae (purchased in Cobalt Scar at Otters) + Eucalyptus (alchemy supply), IN a mixing bowl, yields 6, trivial at 138.) , mushroom (Foraged), frosting IN spit or oven yields 4, trivial at 202. 

  6. Halas Pies - 2 clumps of dough + 1 wolf fillet in cream + 1  bear fillet in cream + 1 lion fillet in cream + mammoth fillet in cream + sage leaf (see outline below), yields 6, trivial 226.

  • 2 clumps of dough (flower + milk + egg in mixing bowl, yield depends on the egg used and can be foraged, see those great druids we've been talking about!), trivial 17

  • wolf fillet in cream (Fillet of wolf (see #3 above) + creamy fennel sauce IN spit or oven, yields 1, trivial 152.

  • creamy fennel sauce (cream (see below) + Fennel (alchemy supply) + Flour IN Mixing bowl, yields 6, trivial 75)

  • Cream (2 milk + benzoin (alchemy supply), dairy spoon (returns after each combine fail or success) in a mixing bowl, yields 8, trivial at 83)

  • Sage Leaf (alchemy supply)

I did a combination of the following: Griffon Stir Fry - griffon Meat (Jagged pines), vegetables (foraged), plat shoots (foraged) IN a spit or oven  with a non stick frying pan, yields 1, trivial 244. OR Blood Raven Stew - 2 blood raven parts + celestial essence + flask of water + (foraged) vegetables  in a TANNIN/POK oven, yields 2, trivial 250 OR Boar Chops - boar meat + spices + garnish, yields 1, trivial 250 OR Hobgoblin Surprise - hobgoblin meat + pie tin + bread (don't bother to bake this, not worth the effort and time, just buy it made) + brown gravy (pot + milk + flour + bear meat in spit) IN Tanaan/POK oven. Yields 1, trivial 250 (this item is for the Beginner's Magic Book quest in POK, people will pay for this and one of the few items we bake that sells for cash!) OR Vann Toe Stew - vann toes + milk + spices in Tanaan/POK oven, yield 1, trivial 250.

Notice you see no Misty thicket picnic baskets here. Since the POK meats can be found on vendors or hunted for, it's so much easier to trivial up using Blood Raven parts than the baskets by 100 to 1, trust me on that! When in need, ask your guild mates to help find the parts you need, we're a family, they help you and when you make the goodie food with stats, offer it back to them as a reward. They'll help you more often than you'll like! LOL
