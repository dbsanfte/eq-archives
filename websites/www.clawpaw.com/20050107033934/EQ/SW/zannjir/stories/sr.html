Sibling Rivalry



A beautiful sun breaks the horizon, a new day dawns. She blinks, and focuses her eyes. She brushes her hair back behind her again. 
  
"What" "What" she wonders. ........"is the purpose of life, is is something special ?" 
  
Her road worn boots kick the ground at her feet. "Then why do so many abuse it.. " she whispers. 
The sun shinning on her face, as individual rays of light begin to trace the names on the stone tablets before her. 
  
"Life is precious", said a dry voice from behind her. Partara never moved, just stood there silently. Hair blowing wildly about her. 
  
"Why do you bother to come here?" the venom in her voice could have dropped a dragon from the skies. 
Tepanga stopped, stood silently behind the girl peering over her shoulder. 
  
He squints his eyes reading the stones and remembering things no child should have to bear.
