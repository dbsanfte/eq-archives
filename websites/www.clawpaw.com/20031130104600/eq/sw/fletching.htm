Fletching

Learn the Art of Fletching

 by Selyorb Heartmate    

 

Hi to those who want to learn the art of fletching. I was given this list of recipes, and will post them here so that you will know the trivials on arrows you may attempt to make and as a guideline to help you along your way to being a 200 skill fletcher. 

IF YOU NEED TO KNOW ANYTHING ABOUT FLETCHING, IXIOLA IS THE PREMIER FLETCHER/BOWYER!. She knows the ins and outs of arrows and bows, and is THE knowledge source for any bow or arrow you may want to attempt. This trivial listing is being posted as a pathway to a higher skill level only.


I received this list from a player in-game, while trying to get Miyke high enough to make Selyorb some damage one/ range 150 arrows. : )

There are two routes you can take, one with recipes for skill only, the next is for usable arrows. 

The first, skill only list, goes as follows.

Triv 16 Rng:50 Damage 1
Field point; Wood shaft; round fletches; Large nock

Triv 36 Rng:75 Damage 1
Same ingredients, but change to Medium nock

Triv 46 Rng?100 Damage 1
Same as TRIV 16 arrows, but change to Parabolic Fletch

Triv 56 Rng:100 Damage 1
Same as TRIV 16 arrows, but change to Small nock

Triv 68 Rng:50 Damage2
Field point; Bone shaft; Round fletches; Large nock

Triv 82 Rng:0 Damage 2
Field point; Wood shaft; shield fletches; Large nock

Triv 102 Rng:50 Damage 2
Hooked point; Wood shaft; Round fletches; Large nock

NOTE THAT THE LAST TRIVIAL IS 202 - PASSING 200 MEANS YOU HAVE TO USE AA POINTS BEFORE YOU CAN RAISE ANOTHER SKILL PAST 200 if you already have a 200+ tradeskill.
