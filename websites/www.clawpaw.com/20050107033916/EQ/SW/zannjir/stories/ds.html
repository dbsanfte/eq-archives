Dark Side


  Sun, stars, moon and earth. Allow me to see the universe. At night I fly, and sore through dreams, so many places that are seldom seen. When at last dawn does come, and bring with it the light. I disappear from mortal sight. Alone in this world yet I live, now and forever. Through all of time I travel again till I fine the love that is mine. 



Tajifa Nyteshade 
11-19-99
