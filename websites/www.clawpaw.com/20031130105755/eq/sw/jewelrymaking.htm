Jewelry Craft

One way to get to 250 Jewelry Crafting!

Tips by Spiritlynx Heartmate (Outline donated by Zanai)

TIP 1:     I've learned that the Art of Jewelry Crafting helps when you enchant every single bar. Others say it's not worth the time and effort but I've found the sell back is slightly higher and therefore worth every copper you can squeeze out of it.

TIP 2:     Purchasing the best Clarity you can get when enchanting metals your best bet, it's worth the donation to enchant mounds of bars and don't forget to sit and med!

TIP 3:     Enchanters make the best Jewelers. Having a Vah Shir Shaman as my Jewelry Crafter has taught me that they enchant what they need and of course the new AA system allows ENCHANTERS to up their chances NOT to fail, while other classes cannot. Keep that in mind! 

TIP 4:     UP that Charisma to ensure LOWER costs bars and jewels and HIGHER returns at selling to the merchants.

TIP 5:     NEVER buy/sell from the Shar Vahl merchants while making jewelry, they cost more to purchase and pay you less return, trust me, it's not the place to do your jewelry.

TIP 6:     USE your Geerlok for jewelry making to increase chances for an actual success in the creation of your jewelry, this will ensure more sellbacks to the vendors, and thus less cost to learn jewelry making.
