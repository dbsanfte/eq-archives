welcome to candyland

Update by Linse






Welcome to Candy Land, where every child's dreams come true and candy flows like waterfalls. Tonight I am reporting live, once again, from the Plane of Time. We have received horrifying reports of evil barely clothed women luring un-suspecting travellers to their deaths with candy. Our love of women, and of course candy, demanded that we send in an expeditionary team to discover the real story behind these reports. Be sure to click on all the mouth watering
tasty treat candy bars below, as they link to the TRUE candy rewards.

Our expeditionary team entered the Plane of Time and began their search.




Meeting only light resistance, our brave band of explorers proceeded through Tier 1 of the Plane of Time, uncovering numerous hiden caches of candy.



Our heros rushed through Tier 2, and sprinted to the famous Valley of 'Gib' to sink their teeth into Tier 3.


The Valley of 'Gib' churned out more DPS then Super-Tank Minnoch can dish out at an all your can eat chineese buffet, but our heros were a heardy bunch and stood fast against the vile creatures that inhabited the valley. The
Valley of 'Gib' was swiftly turned into nothing more then a few dozen busted pinatas that poured out the candy.






On to tier 4 our explorers marched, and at last we discovered the true treasure chests hidden in these lands!


Knowing the women were a dangerous ploy trying to lure us into their evil grasp, our heros figured they would check out Tallon Zek and Vallon Zek first and see what would await us.






Ouch that hurts! Looking closer at Saryn, we spoted some candy!



Blinded by the candy, our team rushed in:




The battle faired well, until suddenly the screams were heard: 'ADDS!'




The tides of battle had turned, hope was quickly disapearing. Pandemonium hit speed-dial to call in Gnome-Tech (tm) and unleash the gnome-cannons.



But even Gnome-Tech (tm) could not disperse enough gnome loving to hold back the minnions of Saryn.



That Bitch! She lured us in with candy, and glocked us.

So Pandemonium had been sucker punched, and it stung. Blinded by visions of candy, we had fallen victim to Saryn the Siren. Pandemonium collected its fallen, and began planning for the next assualt. We consulted our Cliff Notes, but the 2 copies we had didn't cover anything about Saryn the Siren, so we were on our own.




Since Gnome-Tech (tm) had failed us, Pandemonium had to look to new sollutions. Pandemonium Officers by a council vote of 7 in favor, and 3 against, voted to authorize the deployment of Weapons of Mass Destruction (WMD's). With our new military deployment authorized, we once again engaged Saryn.

Saryn summoned many minions to her aid but this time we we ready. Pandemonium fed Minnoch buckets of KFC, and unleashed the WMD's within our Super-Tank.





This time, Saryn the Siren could not out match our Weapon of Mass Destruction.

Just like Minnoch on a bucket of KFC, down Saryn went.




Great outrage was heard through-out the lands, that Pandemonium had unleashed WMD's. France threatened sanctions, and DiscoNation flead like cowards to hide in some 2+ year old content zones while GM's discussed our use
