About the Kinship(Graphics)

The Anu Kinship is really Ariauna Ki'Shar and Egras An'Shar and their decendants. Its also a way to link lore to our world. Nothing really much more than that.

Egras An'Shar (Pandemonium)

Totality of the Upper World and Host of the Sky. God of the horizon, foremost of the heavens. King of the Gods and perpetual light.

 Ariauna Ki'Shar (Pandemonium)

Totality of the Lower World and Hostesss of the Earth. Goddess of the lands, formost of the firmland. Queen of the Gods and eternal darkness.
