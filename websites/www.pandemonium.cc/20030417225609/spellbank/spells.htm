Welcome to Pandemonium's Spell Bank!

All spells listed herein are available for FREE to Guildmembers ONLY. Guildmember twinks, friends or other residents of Norrath will have to purchase any spells for a price in relation to rarity, level, etc. or a price decided by the officers in Pandemonium.

If you are interested in a spell, please send Vump a tell in-game or drop me an e-mail to pdm@indy.rr.com.

When sending me a tell in relation to spell purchases, please DO NOT spam me with tells that you are interested in a spell. When I have a chance, I will respond to you. Typically, if I am busy, I will write your name down and the spells you are interested in and respond shortly thereafter.

All Mules are located in the Bazaar on Luclin. You will have to make your own travel arrangements to get there. Once there, the mule that has your spell will be loaded and the trade with commence. Again, I cannot deliver spells to you unless for special circumstances as in the case for Guildmembers only.

If any Guildmembers have spells they would like to donate to the Spell Bank, please drop them off to Spelvumpa, Spelvumpi, Gulump, Kamyra, or Nump in the Bazaar or give them to myself.

If you have any suggestions, comments, etc. please send them to pdm@indy.rr.com

Thank you!

Vump Ump - Elder Druid of Tallon Zek
