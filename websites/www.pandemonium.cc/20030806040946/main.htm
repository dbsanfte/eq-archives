Which brings me to my next point. Do I sound bitter in this update? Well, there is a damn good reason for it. After last night, I found out what a big surprise the Xegony raid is. To whoever designed this event, please kindly kiss my ass as we now have to do the event over again. Hell, the raid wasn't even full, so that is the only thing I can come up with.
