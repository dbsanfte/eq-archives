Halls of Honor B (Temple of Mith Marr)

This Guide is for the flag that results from the death of the Dragon Rydda 'Dar and 2 "trials".  To gain entrance into Halls of Honor B (Temple of Mithaniel Marr), you need the flag from this Dragon and 2 "trials" that take place in the basements of Halls of honor.

NE Basement Trial

Trydan Faye AKA Rydda'dar

NW Basement Trial

Rhalig Trell AKA Valor trial AKA many mobs pop

SW Basement Trial

Alekson Garn AKA Bard Kite trial

NE BASEMENT TRIAL

To begin the script that triggers Rydda 'Dar, go to the Northeast Basement in Halls of Honor.  In the back room spawns Trydan Faye (Human NPC).  He is the trigger spawn for this encounter.

You need a raid force gathered (high Magic Resist), with players ready to engage and kite spawn that might pop up in the room next to where Trydan Faye is.  Clear all spawns in the room Trydan Faye is located in and spawns in the next room that might add.

Have your main melee tank or raid leader go to Trydan Faye and triggers this event by hailing Trydan and saying the line "I am ready", a Custodian of Marr spawns that you can kill with players or pets.  Once this NPC is dead the Dragon spawns.  Rydda 'Dar casts AE fear and has a high magic resist.  Usually shamans disease line of slows work best.

Once this Dragon dies, another Trydan Faye spawns that you "hail" for a TEXT response (no "flag" message will appear) .  Because this is one of three flags you need to gain access to Halls of Honor B, don't be alarmed if you only see text.

In the other basements in the farthest rooms in the back are other spawns that can trigger small ring events for flags.  You need to complete TWO of these, as well as slaying Rydda 'Dar for a complete flag. 

In the Southeast basement and in the Northwest basement, you do a similar process to the above.  Gather a raid force but instead of a Dragon, you are spawning a cycle of NPC's.  You must keep up with the spawns and kill them to spawn the planar projection at the end.  Within these cycles, named can spawn that drop loot.

SE BASEMENT TRIAL

Alekson Garn is the trigger spawn for this trial.  Gather your raid force and have kiting player ready to kite the spawns that occur in the room before this one.  Clear the room and Hail Alekson Garn, follow any script to trigger the event.  You have to kill these waves in a certain amount of time (I believe 10 minutes).  The best way to do this is to have a bard aggro the spawns and kite them while a puller pulls off the bard and brings the NPC's the the raid force.  This will prevent many NPC's from rushing in and bashing on people in the raid.  If you do the cycles in the correct time you will end with a planar projection that you need to "hail" for text (no flag for this event either). 

NW BASEMENT TRIAL

Rhalig Trell is the trigger spawn for this trial.  This trial spawns many NPC's at once but unlike the previous trial, they spawn in the same room as the trigger NPC. You will need players to tank many spawns at once and enchanter to provide any mesmerizing services that might be needed.  Buff up, Hail Rhalig Trell and follow any script that occurs to trigger this event.  Like the above trial, this must be done in a certain time frame of about 10 minutes per spawn cycle.  At the end of this event and the death of any mobs that spawned, a planar projection spawnt that you need to "hail" for text (no flag for this event either).
