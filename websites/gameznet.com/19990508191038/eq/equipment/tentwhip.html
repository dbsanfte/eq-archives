Equipment Guide

Tentacle Whip
 
MAGIC ITEM
Skill: 1H Slash    Atk Delay: 25
DMG: 4
Effect: Lifetap
WT: 5.0
Class: WAR PAL RNG SHD BRD ROG
Race: All
Worn in Melee slot.

Tentacle Whips can be found on Tentacle Terrors. Tentacle Whips are often used by mid-teen level characters, as a second weapon, because of its magical nature. Lifetap drains hit points from the monster and gives them to the wielder.
