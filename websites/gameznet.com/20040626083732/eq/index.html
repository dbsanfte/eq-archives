06/24/2004

Bestiary Revamp Going Quickly
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

The bestiary revamp process is going quickly and expected to be done data wise, by tomorrow. The front end will come with the site redesign which will be in early July. Most of the features are already working on the test site and I think your going to be happy with the results. In the meantime though you can enjoy many of the data changes right now, in the form of new mob race types, and the fact that we're retagging everything to reflect it's correct type (Boss, Sub-Boss, Rare Monster, Quest Monster, Monster, Quest NPC or NPC), flagging NPCs as vendor types and adding any mobs that were missing before. There is so much data changing every day though that it makes it impossible for the generator to produce New Bestiary for you until this process is finished (tomorrow). I do have an equipment update for you though.

New Equipment: Jointed Ivory Gauntlets ~ Putrescent Chain Helm ~ Scorched Vambraces of Battle ~ Blindeye ~ Mace of Dark Glory ~ Dedgerex's Flintforged Cudgel ~ Engraved Spiral Conch ~ Kobold Skull Charm ~ Marble of Scholarly Insight ~ Venom Filled Scales ~ A Canine.

06/23/2004

POP and LDON Conversions Complete
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

We hit a milestone in the conversion process today. All PoP, LDoN and GoD zones should be fully converted. If you find any errors or problems on any of these pages feel free to leave a comment or email us and let us know about it. I'll fix any errors as soon as possible. I'm going to be turning my attention the next couple of days to bestiary conversion and filling in missing holes. We have several new bestiary features coming with the upcoming site relaunch and I'll be getting things ready for that. Then I'll turn my attention on the final remaining zones and we hope to relaunch the site in all it's glory somewhere in early July. This update was really large, so the equipment update is only listing the end of it. Bestiary wise we converted around 1500 mobs today to include race and vendor flags for the new site, and the bestiary included in this update is only the changes after that took place.

In non-Everquest news, Dark Age of Camelot shipped their free PVP expansion: Frontiers today. If you are looking for Dark Age of Camelot information then feel free to stop by our very own DAoC Site, which is also currently going through a revamp process (as are all GameZNet sites).
