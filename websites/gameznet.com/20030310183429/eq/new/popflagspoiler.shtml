Plane of Power Flag Guide


Tier 1 Planes

Plane of Knowledge

Access: Planes of Power Expansion

Quests:Box of Souls

Find Borik Darkanvil, ask about Box of Souls

Collect Block of Prelium (rare from Plane of Innovation mobs), Crystaline Spider Brain (from crystal spiders in Plane of Valor), and An Agony Mephit Heart (from Agony Mephit).

Either give these items to Borik with 3000pp or combine them with hammer and water in forge (trivial at 250 skill).

Receive Box of Souls

Plane of Tranquility

Access: Level 46+

Quests:Thelin’s Torment

Speak with Adroha Jezith, following dialog (get flag)

Speak with Thelin Poxbourne in Plane of Nightmare with 3 groups.

Defeat the Maze, killing Construct of Nightmares at the end.

Receive flag to enter Crypt of Terris Thule

Quest:Alder's Request

Speak with Alder Fuirstel, following dialog (get flag)

Journey into Plane of Disease, battle through the castle, and defeat Grummus

Return to Alder, who asks you to speak with Elder Fuirstel.

Speak with Elder, receive flag to enter Crypt of Decay

Quest:Saving Tylis

After killing Grummus, Elder Fuirstil tells you to kill Bertoxxulous.

After killing Bertoxxulous, return to receive flag to enter Saryn’s Tower.

Enter the tower and kill Keeper of Sorrows Curing Phanti (unfinished)

Collect Putrified Bubonian Bile from Rythak

Give to Iramai Sacredsoul, she returns purified bile and tells you to talk to an Indigo Orc in Plane of Justice

Give bile to orc Brugga (requires Master of orc language), he returns it and requests 3 items.

Give him Mechanical Clockwork Venom (drop in PoI), Nettling Stinger (Nettling Wraith), and Terror Matriarch Venom Sac (Terror Matriarch).

Return cure for item that allows entry to Plane of Tormet.

Plane of Innovation

Access: Level 46+

Notable NPCs:Xanamech Nexmirthafen

Quests:Wake the Dragon

Speak with Niatrim Azinok, he requests 3 items. Return to him a copper node, a bundle of super conductive wires, and an intact power cell.

When you give these 3 he begins walking towards the untargetable dragon. After killing your way to the dragon (and keeping the gnome alive), the gnome wakes it up.

Kill Xanamech Nexmirthafen.

Hail Niatrim to receive flag to enter Factory Floor.

Plane of Disease

Access: Level 46+

Notable Mobs:Grummus, Rythak

Quests:Grimror’s Collection

Collect 4 items from 4 named mobs in zone

The four mobs are sengian, harrion, scaevian, and generian larva.

PH for Harrion larva = a malarian larva(not larvae like all the others, spawns near zone in a bit out near flies)
PH for Generian larva = a planarian larva(again just larva, spawns east of castle east of river near pussling camps)
PH for Scaevian larva = a Tsetsian Pupa(just pupa, not pupal, spawns a bit east of generian)
PH for Sengian larva = tachnian caterpillars (in the north part of the zone, not single one close to zone)

Give the 4 parts to Grimror for a Bangle of Disease Warding which acts as a key to Crypt of Decay.

Plane of Nightmare

Access: Level 46+

Notable Mobs:An Agony Mephit, A Nettling Wraith, Terror Matriarch

Quests: Aid Thelin Poxbourne

Follow the script after you hail him, bring 18 people (he teleports only 3 groups) to him and have each group leader say the trigger line he gives.  All groups teleport to a Maze.  Keep Thelin Poxbourne alive as he wanders the maze taking you to various areas where NPC's spawn.  At the end a Construct of Nightmare spawns near a foutain Thelin Poxbourne leads you to and this NPC drops a  thelins dagger, which you need to pick up and give to Thelin.  Handing in dagger spawns a untargettable Terris Thule.  Wait for Thelin to run around, speak some then hail him for a flag to Lair of Terris Thule.

Plane of Justice

Access: Level 46+

Quests:Trial Quest

Speak with Mavuin at the back of the Jail area

Say, "What information", receive flag.

Complete 1 of the 6 trials

Speak with The Tribunal and say "Mauvin", receive flag.

Return to Mavuin and 'hail' him for another flag.

Receive flag for Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms.

There are several "trails":
1) To start a Trial go to one of the Tribunal's and say "Prepared".
2) He will then say what trial you are at
3) Get your group on the pad and say "Ready for trial of XXXXXXX" the x's are the trials name
4) Your group will now be ported in the trial.

Hanging
30 sec after you enter 3 mobs spawn:
1 spirit of suffocation and 2 random(breathless voids or stiffling nemesis)

Spirit can spawn as named version which is invisible except for name – named spirit is unmezzable
Normal versions can spawn as a named, mezzable with any mez higher than GoK

You have roughly 45 sec – 1 min to kill spirit before he kills prisoners
Spirit has roughly 10k hp, is mezzable, and summoned spells work
Second spirit spawns a set time after the wave begins, even if first spirit is still alive
When second spirit dies, another wave spawns about 45 sec later, regardless if your previous wave is dead or not but only if the prisoners are still alive.

Breathless voids tash and charms – mezzable and summoned spells work
Nemesis procs hundred blows (stun + dd) and is unmezzable

There are 4 waves. Upon the 8th spirit of suffocation death, Gallow Masters something spawns, hits for 750 and is an enchanter mob, also charms.

Execution
1) 4 waves of 4 mobs each
2) Each mob has 8k hp, even boss mobs
3) Rocks can be banished by druids/mages – this can sometimes reset the executioner’s walk and sometimes not – possibly related to whether or not the mob has taken damage.
4) You have exactly 30 seconds to kill each mob or the executioner will chop the head off the prisoner. Killing a mob resets this timer. about 333 dps needed.
5) Executioner spawns as the boss at the end (he can be slowed) hits for 700’s
Rocks are not mezzable, whereas razorfiends are.
You can’t banish named rocks.
6) Kill rocks first because they 96 second mez. (get high MR)
7) when done, fail or pass go to the mob in the corner and say "Return" this ports you back to the tribnual area.


Flame
Fire elementals/sprites, spawn in waves of 4, hit around 300ish with fire procs, can also dispel and possibly nuke as well.
The goal is to keep them from getting to the middle of the room.
Fire doesn't seem to have a time limit, but it's pretty tough.. The boss might spawn after the last mob dies, but it could have been coincidence.. Not sure.
You also have an uncurable 200/tick fire DoT eating at you the whole time.

Remove curse gets rid of the ae dot

Lashing
Exactly like Execution with the removal of the time limit, removal of the boss
When a Scourge of Honor spawns, you need to kill a wisp mob in one of the side rooms to kill it (possibly both of the side rooms) – the Scourge itself is untargetable.

Stoning
A spirit of suffocation (enchanter) spawns with 2 other mobs which are either rocks (enchanter) or fungus beasts (melee, procs one-hundred blows (dd + stun)). Then after a bit (30-45 seconds I'd guess), a second spirit of suffocation spawns that needs to be killed before the prisoners die.

Torture
Mobs come in from the door in the room
After killing a wraith of agony on the third wave, everything in the room got healed by an AE including the prisoners, the mobs, and the group.

Tier 2 Planes

Plane of Valor

Access: Complete Trial Quest in Plane of Justice (1 group)

Notable Mobs:Aerin’dar

Quests:Aerin’dar’s Lair

Collect Razorfiend Heart, Crystaline Spider Heart, and Planarian Larvae Heart.

Hail Johon Popoah twice

Go find Paralin Notion and follow dialog.

Go find Master Sergeant Aaramis Daryln and follow dialog.

Go back to Paralin Notion, give 3 hearts to him.

Collect crystalline orb piece from Luminii Crawler, one of the frogs near the bridgehouse, and one from the skellies.

Return to Master Sergeant Aaramis Daryln, follow dialog

Find Captain Ryglot Cupperhide, follow dialog.

Give 3 orb pieces to Captain Ryglot Cupperhide, receive Orb (key to enter Aerin’dar’s lair)

Quest:Grenic Drere’s quest

Locate Grenic Drere and follow dialog.

Complete the Box of Souls quest (see above for info).

In Plane of Justice, defeat 3 Tormented Wraiths (near trials room). Each has a chance to spawn "an exiled soldier/trooper/officer of marr". Hand the exiled the Box of Souls, receive box and soul stone.

Give 3 soul stones to Grenic to receive Ring of Marr, which is a key to access Halls of Honor.

Plane of Storms

Access: Complete Trial Quest in Plane of Justice (1 group)

Quests:Wembly’s Revenge

Locate Wembly the Forlorn. Follow dialog

Collect 4 medalion pieces, uncommon drop from uncomon spawn Diaku Raiders.

Give 4 pieces to Wembly, receive flag for Plane of Tactics.

Quest:Aiding Askr

Collect A storm giant’s head, a beard from the ocean giants, a bone from the desert giants, and a sash from the forest giants.

Locate Askr, give him the giant’s head, receive containers.

Combine beard, bone, and sash in container and hand to Askr.

Ask What Bastion of Thunder, receive container

Collect 2 of 3 medallions from named giants, combine them.

Give to Askr, receive flag to enter Bastion of Thunder.

Crypt of Decay (Ruins of Lxanvom)

Access: Complete Grimror’s Collection in Plane of Disease (single), Complete Alder’s Request in Plane of Tranquil (raid)

Notable Mobs:Bertoxxulous

Crypt of Terris Thule

Access: Complete Thelins Torment in Plane of Tranquility (3 group)

Notable Mobs:Terris Thule

Factory Floor (Plane of Innovation sub-zone)

Access: Complete Wake the Dragon in Plane on Innovation (raid)

Notable Mobs:Manaetic Behemoth

Quests:Destroy the Behemoth

Speak with Giwin Mirakon, he tells you to destroy the Manaetic Behemoth.

Receive flag to enter Plane of Tactics (Drunder, Fortress of Zek)

Tier 3 Planes

Bastion of Thunder

Access: Complete Aiding Askr (single)

Notable Mobs: Karana, Agnarr the Stormlord

Key To Agnarr the Stormlord:

In each wing Spheres drop-lore,magic, no-drop (Lightning, Tornado, Blizzard, Sandstone).  Gain the container to combine them through the small named bosses of each wing with a Symbol of Torden.  In this Symbol of Torden combine the spheres that drop off named mobs (and random Kriegers) and you gain the key to the Tower where Agnarr the Stormlord resides.

Halls of Honor

Access:Gain access to Aerin’dar’s Lair. Kill Aerin’dar. Hail the magician pet NPC that spawns after death.  Open the floor door and drop down, fight your way to the end of the tunnel and enter Halls of Honor (raid).  This gives you a flag so you can use the zone stones in Plane of Tranquility to enter in the future. Complete Grenic Drere’s Quest (single)

Notes: Trials to complete in this zone, supposed to allow access to Temple of Marr
