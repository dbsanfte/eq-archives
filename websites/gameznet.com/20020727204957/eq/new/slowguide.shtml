Slowing Guide

The following is a list I have complied from my travels and by information given to me by my fellow shamans, about certain large encounter NPC's that can be "slowed" (spell: Tugurs , Tigirs, or Plague of Insects).  Included in this list are some epic NPC's that I have battled to assist guildmates.  This list is set up alphabetically by area.  At the end of the slow list is a list of NPC's that are confirmed as unslowable.  If you have any additions, please do not hesitate to send them in!
