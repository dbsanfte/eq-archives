Strategies to Defeat Rallos Zek 

Strategy One: This is a "ring" type battle.  You must go up the stairs across the entrance (straight across the arena) and work your way up to the trigger mob to start this ring battle.  You will trigger the event by clearing the mobs on your way up and hitting a certain area.  Be careful, if you go to far and sit too close to a set of stairs you will hit traps that train some NPC's on you.  Once you clear and kill the trigger NPC, Tallon Zek (archer) spawns.  You need to kill him, I would suggest mezzing any adds and keeping then slowed.  Once he is dead and you clear any adds Vallon Zek spawns.  He is like the other Vallon Zek.  The longer you take the more he splits into other Vallon Zek's.  Once you kill the real one it will stop splitting.  Then the "fake" Rallos Zek spawns.  Again, kill any adds before he  spawns if you can and mezz what you cannot kill to focus on the fake Rallos Zek.  He is slowable.  Once this is down more NPC's will spawn but I suggest mezzing them. 

After he is dead cast AE bind and having your guildmates bind at the stone to Drunder.  The reason is if you kill VallonZek, Tallon Zek, and the fake Rallos Zek, the real Rallos Zek spawns IN THE ARENA, despawning any NPC's in there.  You need to get back to the arena ASAP, Translocating to a bind at the Drunder stone means people will be near the arena in a few minutes.  You need to drop down into the arean (south tight corner) and prepare for the Rallos Zek fight.  He is slowable and waves of NPC's spawn during the battle.  Some can be mezzed.  There are several waves of NPC so be ready.  Having a high cleric/bard/enchanter count for this battle is best.  

Strategy Two : Submit a Strategy!
Decorin Berik - Decorin Grunhork - Rallos Zek all need to be up in order to start the event.

The event starts on the top floor of the Plane of Tactics. As you head in from the zone in run around to the back side of the arena and the head up the stairs. Then have your monks pull both sides simultaneously. You need to clear each side until you monks reach Decorin Berik or Decorin Grunhork. Have you monks pull them to the raid force but do not kill, but instead have your enchanters mez them until they are both in the camp. Then you will need to kill them within 2 minutes of each other.

After killing the 2 triggers you have 18 MINUTES to kill BOTH VALLON and TALLON!

Once you have kill Berik and Grunhork within 2 minutes of each other you will need to move your raid to the right. Go until the corner before Tallon Zek and have a rogue pretarget the 4 adds and have enchanters ready to mez each. If the monks can split Tallon from the adds, and bring the adds first, that would be great, but if not, bring all 4 and be ready to mez. You will need either a Paladin or an SK to tank TZ because you need as much aggro as possible. With that in mind, be prepared for Vallon Zek to come with some adds. He comes if you waste too much time with Tallon. Have you tanks ready to tank mez adds or your enchanters ready to mez. The main thing yo should focus on is getting Tallon down and avoiding his AoE. If you manage to get him down, then it is time to focus on Vallon. Vallon splits into a few versions of himself so be prepared.

Once TZ and VZ are both down, it is time for a fake Rallos Zek. Your enchanters and tank mezzes need to be prepared to CC lots of mobs. Upwards of 10+. Rallos quads for 1200 and has an AoE dot that does 400 per tick.  Keep your casters out of AE rampage space and only have melee's ekkk into melee range with the main tank the only person in full range of the Rallos Zek.  Once this fake Rallos Zek is down you need to AE translocate before the mad rush of adds and get everyone back to the zone in to levitate, res the dead and run down to the arena where the real Rallos Zek spawns (despawning all npcs in the arena).  I suggest dropping down the left corner as you face the arena from the zone in.
Lev up your raid and be ready for some action. Tanks need to go first and be sure you POSITION HIM IN A CORNER!!! You need at least 4 enchanters to handle the Crowd Control. The adds come every 54 seconds. You should be prepared for at least 4 waves of adds unless you've a very large amount of DPS available. You should assign mezzing classes and kiters to kit the adds and mezz any NPC's that do not aggro on kiters and attack the main fighting force. Position Rallos Zek into a corner and again engaged the only main tank in full melee range and the rest of the melee group barley in melee range with casters well outside of AE rampage.
