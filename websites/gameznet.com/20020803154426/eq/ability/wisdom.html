Wisdom

Wisdom is the primary ability for priest classes. It is also an important ability for Paladins, Beastlords and Rangers as it will directly affect their ability to cast spells.

A higher wisdom equals out to more mana for priest style casters. This is the priest equivalent to Intelligence. I recommend that your wisdom is always you highest statistic. However priest classes generally get hit a bit more than pure casters so it is wise to make sure you have a decent Agility  Strength is also important to a cleric if they wish to wear platemail.

So while wisdom is not as important to a priest class as Intelligence is to a mage class it remains the most important ability score they have. I recommend at least a wisdom of 100 and the higher the better.
