Gearheart Quest
This quest originates in Ak'Anon. First, go talk to Manik Compolten in the Warriors' Guild. He wants you to kill a defective Clockwork named Gearheart whom has been captured and put to work by the Faeries in Lesser Faydark. You must travel to Lesser Faydark to kill him. Gearheart is a defective clockwork and when killed lowers faction only with Faerie.

Kill Gearheart(approx. level 20) and he will drop Gearheart's Gearbox. Take the box back to Manik Compolten in the Ak'Anon Warriors' Guild. He will give you a good faction adjustment, and if you're in the level 20 range, good XP, cash, and a random piece of Small Raw-Hide armor.
