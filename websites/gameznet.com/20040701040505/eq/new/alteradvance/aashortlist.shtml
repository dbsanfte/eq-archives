Archetype Abilities

The Archetype Abilities are separated into three different archetypes. The first is Caster (Enchanters, Magicians, Necromancer and Wizards). The second is  Melee (Bards, Beastlords, Monks, Paladins, Rangers, Rogues, Shadow Knights, and Warriors). The third is Priest (Clerics, Druids and Shaman).

Please *note*, Bards, Paladins, Ranger, Beastlords and Shadowknights have some Caster/Priest abilites on top of melee because they are hybrids

Abilities are as follows:

Caster

Channeling Focus
Increases max channeling to reduce the chance of interruption. Percentages are approximately 5, 10 and 15 percent reduction.
(Cost is 2 for level 1, 4 for level 2, and 6 for level 3)

Mental Clarity
Increases natural mana regeneration by 1 point per ability level.
(Cost is 2 for level 1, 4 for level 2, and 6 for level 3)

Spell Casting Deftness
Reduces casting time of beneficial spells with a cast time greater than 4 seconds. Percentages are approximately 5, 15, and 25 percent.
(Cost is 2 for level 1, 4 for level 2, and 6 for level 3)

(Cost is 2 for level 1, 4 for level 2, and 6 for level 3)
