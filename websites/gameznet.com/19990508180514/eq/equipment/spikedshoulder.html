Everquest Equipment Guide: Spiked Shoulerpads

The spiked shoulderpads can be found off Gnoll Commanders in Black Burrow. They are leather based(same ac) but carry with them a +5 bonus to strength and an(ouch) -10 penalty to magic resistance. They are great to wear if you will be fighting non casters, but be sure to switch them with something else anytime you face spellcasters.
