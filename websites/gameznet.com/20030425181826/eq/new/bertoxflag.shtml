Crypt of Decay Basement Flag (Bertoxxulous)

This page is to guide you through the process of the Bertoxxulous battle and the resulting flag.  You need a large raid force with access to Crypt of Decay Basement (the skeleton bone chair by zone-in is the entrance to the basement).  You must have completed the Carpin Cycle and have a flag to enter this area.

To spawn Bertox, you must complete a ring battle.  This means you will trigger the event and have to keep up with the many cycles of spawns that are thrown at your raid force.  If you do NOT kill the cycles in a certain amount of time the event ends. You have TWO HOURS from starting this event to finish the cycles and spawn Bertoxxulous, if you do not get through the cycles fast enough, Bertox will not spawn.

To keep track of the NPC's for the cycle, assign main tanks and pullers who pull the spawns to the raid force.  If you keep your force tight in a corner of the room you port into and have the pullers bring the NPC's to you, you can keep track of the cycles and spawns a bit better.

To start this event the Spectre of Corruption must be up.  Pulling this NPC and killing starts your ring event (keep track of time).  You will have many cycles of NPC's that will include names such as Kavilis and Rawdol.  The named are harder than the other NPC's that spawn (mostly Knights of Decay, Priest of Decay and Necromancers of Decay). Kill these cycles as fast as possible and after several waves, Bertoxxulous will spawn in a hallway off of the area you port to the basement in.  This gives you a bit of time to prepare BUT he moves and wanders down.  Have high disease resist up, Bertox is UNSLOWABLE.

After you slay this monster, a small NPC spawns that is not aggro, hail him for your flag !

This flag is one of a group you need to enter the elemental planes.
