General Abilities-Level 51

Innate Stamina 5 This ability raises your base Stamina by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Agility 5 This ability raises your base Agility by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Dexterity 5 This ability raises your base Dexterity by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Intelligence 5 This ability raises your base Intelligence by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Wisdom 5 This ability raises your base Wisdom by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Charisma 5 This ability raises your base Charisma by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Fire Protection 5 This ability raises your base Save Vs. Fire by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Cold Protection 5 This ability raises your base Save Vs. Cold by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Magic Protection 5 This ability raises your base Save Vs. Magic by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Poison Protection 5 This ability raises your base Save Vs. Poison by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Disease Protection 5 This ability raises your base Save Vs. Disease by 2 points for each ability level
Innate Run Speed 3 This ability will slightly modify your base run speed. This modification does NOT stack with movement rate spell effects.
Innate Regeneration 3 This ability raises your regeneration ability
Innate Lung Capacity 3 This ability increases the amount of air you have by 10, 25, and 50 percent
Innate Metabolism 3 This ability decreases your food consumption by 10, 25, and 50 percent
First Aid 3 This ability increases the maximum that you can bind wound by 10 percent for each ability point

Archetype Abilities-Level 55

Natural Durability 3 This ability increases your maximum hitpoints by 2, 5, and 10 percent. (The percentages are based off of your base hitpoints, which inclue stamina and stamina effects.)
Natural Healing 3 This ability raises your natural regeneration by one point per ability level
Combat Fury 3 This ability increases your chance to land a critical hit. Non-Warriors will nearly match the original critical hit abilities of Warriors, while Warriors will remain significantly ahead of other classes
Fear Resistance 3 This ability grants you a resistance bonus to fear type spells of 5, 10, and 25 percent. It also increases the chance of breaking fear earlier
Finishing Blow 3 This ability gives you a chance to finish off an NPC that is below 10 percent health and fleeing with a single blow. (Non-Warriors must first train one level of Combat Fury to use this ability.)
Combat Stability 3 This ability increases melee damage mitigation by 2, 5, and 10 percent
Combat Agility 3 This ability increases your melee damage avoidance by 2, 5, and 10 percent

Class Abilities-Level 59

Ambidexterity 1 This ability increases your chance to use dual wield successfully
Critical Mend 3 This ability gives you a chance to perform a superior mend 5, 10, and 25 percent of the time. First Aid at Level 3
Purify Body 1 This ability removes all negative affects from your body except for fear, charm, and resurrection affects. Refresh Time: 72 minutes
Rapid Feign 3 This ability reduces your reuse time on all feign death by 10, 25, and 50 percent
Return Kick 3 This ability gives you the chance to automatically perform a bonus flying kick on ripostes 25, 35, and 50 percent of the time
Double Riposte 3 This ability will give you an increased chance to execute a double riposte 15, 35, and 50 percent of the time
Dragon Punch 1 This ability augments your Dragon Punch with the chance to automatically perform a Knockback
Acrobatics 3 This ability will reduce the damage that you take from falling
