Plane of Power Flag Guide


You must get a Pre-Flag in the Plane of Tranquility before you can attempt this quest battle.  See Plane of Nightmare-->Pre-Flag for Plane of Night B Battle guide for more information

You say, 'Hail, Thelin Poxbourne'

Thelin Poxbourne tells you, 'Who is it? Are you.. really there? You are! Please I beg of you to help me escape from this horrid place. Terris Thule is holding me here, she delights in the nightmares she sends me. To further torture me, she has offered me a pact. She has said that if I can retrieve my [dagger], then I am free to go. She does this only because she knows that I cannot retrieve it on my own.'

You say, 'What dagger?'

Thelin Poxbourne tells you, 'She has taken the only thing that has brought me any joy in my life. She took it and broke it into seven pieces. She placed them deep within the labyrinth of my nightmare. I must retrieve it, will you [help] me. Please I beg of your mercy.'


You say, 'What help?'

Thelin Poxbourne tells you, 'I do not know who you are, but I am thankful that you have stumbled upon me. I can bring you into my dreamstate, but my powers are limited so I can only handle eighteen at once. Please when you are prepared have the leader of each of your band of adventurers tell me they are ready.'

*Have each the leader of each of the three groups say*

'I am ready'

You are ported to a hedge maze area.  Your task is to protect Thelin Poxbourne.  He will guide you through the maze, taking twists and turns.  In some of these twists and turns there will spawn NPC's that will attack your group and Thelin Poxbourne.

Thelin eventually reaches a dead end and will push a wall over revealing a room. After Thelin walks to a corner, 3 mobs pop in the room, a Sinister Nighstalker and a larger version of the ravens that wander the zone and sometimes other spawns. Thelin will take off before this fight is over, so you have to be ready to aggro him and someone tank him while you fight then blur him when the mobs are down.

After the 6 or 7 mob battles, he takes you to a new room which has a "Construct of Nightmares" in it.  You must battle this monster and slay him.  He is slowable, hit for 800ish and has large amounts of hit points. In each corner of this area were bats but if you stay tight in your battle, they will not become aggro.

After killing the Construct, loot him and handed the dagger pieces into Thelin.  He will then spawned an untargetable Terris Thule. They will start a dialogue with one another, which ends in Terris Thule gating. Thelin drops on the ground a dagger called Thelin's Dagger as a reward.

Everyone needs to 'hail Thelin' and he ports you out of the maze with a new character flag which let's you zone into Plane of Nightmare B.  This is the zone home of Terris Thule.

The zone in for this is located in the back of Plane of Nightmare, a large gate that is guarded by Giants.  Click on this gate to zone in.
