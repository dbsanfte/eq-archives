Cadeceus of Sacrament
This quest was submitted by: Caneni Phoenixdown

This is a hidden Cleric quest in the Temple of Solusek Ro (At least that's what I have heard).  When you hail Gavel, and ask him about the Icon of Sacrament, he will tell you that the icon and ignots of Sacrament is only a legend, and if you can somehow find and return them to him, plus 2 magnetized platinum bars, he can make something for you.

The Icon of Sacrament is to be found in Castle Mistmoore. At the back of the tomb in the Graveyard, there's a secret wall. Go through that secret wall will lead you into a small room with 4 doors. Open the door that you see as you enter the room, that will trigger the other doors open as well. Then turn to your left, there's 2 room. Go into the left hand side room, the icon is on the floor which look just like a voodoo doll.

And the Ignot of Sacrament is droped by a Darkened Giant Skeleton in Mountain Rathe. When you zone in from Feerrot, head south until you see a tower. From there, head to west and you will see a ruin with many Skeletons spawns. Clean them all up (As I have heard, it spawns on random places in this ruin). You will see a Giant Skeleton that is darker in color, just like those Dark bones.

After you have both Icon and Ignot, you will need to get 2 enchanted platinum bars. Give the platinum bar to Ostrom in Temple of Solusek Ro, he will then Magnetized it for you.

Give the icon, ignot and 2 Magnetized platinum bars to Gavel, he will then make you a Caduceus of Sacrament. It is a 1H Blunt weapon with 12 Damage and 34 Delay. When equiped it looks just like a normal mace.
