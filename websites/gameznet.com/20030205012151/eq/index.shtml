Patch Message
 13:26 EST - Meshell

February 4, 2003

Highlights of the Patch Message, to read the entire message, click HERE.

NOTE: We have changed a lot of UI Files.
- The melee button for Ulaks, Martial weapons and Two-Handed Piercing weapons should appear to be depressed when attacking.
- Lowered the mana cost of Yaulp I, Yaulp II, Yaulp III, and Yaulp IV to increase the value of these spells
- Damage over Time type Area of Effect spells will no longer do damage when player character resists the spell.
- Fixed a bug that caused pet health bar to disappear if when trying to charm when a pet already exists.
- Divine Barrier now does all of its healing in a single burst instead of as regeneration.
- Noirin Khalen now gives out the proper jewelry book.
- Supreme Loachsmith Psorin now returns items that he does not need or want.
- Removed the "secondary" tag from the Flametongue, as this item is for Int. casters only and they can't equip this item in their secondary slot.
- The Nathsar Greatsword and Fester now have the proper inventory icon.
- Added new potions for Vah Shir Shaman (and all other Shaman): Shadeweaver Portal.
- Fixed the recipe for the potion "Troll's Essence".
- Fixed the cast time on any 5 and 10 dose potions that were inconsistent with their single dose versions.
- Terrorantula Silk, Feather, branch of sylvan oak, branch of planar oak, Drachnid Silk, and Drachling Silk are now stackable.
- Grandmaster Alchemist will now have a way to prove their ability.
- Extended Notes now works on area of Effect songs as well as group songs.
- Repaired some "safe spots" in Echo Caverns, Paludal Caverns and Grieg's End.
- Fixed a pathing issue in Kurn's Tower that was causing some NPCs to disappear.
- The Sands of Fire event will now be restricted to the intended four groups. Alternate methods of getting characters into the event have been removed.
- Corrected some respawn issues in the Plane of Air.
- Corrected a spelling error in the text of the Purify Body ability (affect to effect).

Seems that many people are crashing to desktop every time they zone. This is a known issue and it has been reported that repatching after this occurs should help the issue. Another issue that has come up is anyone who has a Girdle of Flayed Iksar will find it missing from their inventory. This has been verified as known and they are working on a fix for it.

Large Patch Message!
 12:30 EST - Meshell

Highlights of Todays Patch:

- In order to better serve ALL of our customers, including those who do not have a credit card, we've decided to make a relatively small number of The Legacy of Ykesha CD-ROMs available at retail as well

- Further optimizations to reduce bandwidth and 'lag'. It is difficult to describe the value of this change, but it should show a noticeable improvement for many. We've also resolved many of the hit point update and movement issues that occurred recently.

- Corrected a problem where right-clicking on an AA hotkey would highlight the wrong ability on the list in the window.

- Spirit of Snow and Spirit of Flame will be dropping more frequently now.

- Merchant's Crate of Supplies will now fit into containers that can hold giant-sized items.

- Target Group Buff and the AA ability Mass Group Buff can no longer be used with levitate spells to push traders out of their stalls in the Bazaar.

- Corrected text for Arinna Trueblade, she now tells you how to make armbands instead of helmets.

- Prime Patriarch Vuzx should be less likely to leave his usual spot.

- Gindlin Toxfodder should be less likely to be out of his usual spot.

- Deputies Budo and Drebo should no longer get stuck under the floorboards of their tower.

- The River Source in the Warrens can no longer be attacked.

- Choosing the "random" button at character creation will now randomly choose a face rather than always using the initial selection.

The patch message in full can be found in the News area under the month of January.
