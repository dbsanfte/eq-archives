Time cleared in 2 Days
Sakharra, Mon Aug 09, 2004 4:32 am
By force of will alone the old wizard broke his trance and tore his gaze away from his crystal ball, he glanced around the familiar room, his gaze lingering over various objects as his mind fully rejoined his body. The disorientation was familiar to him, such is the art of scrying, the mind journeys while the body stays static and the rejoining of the two often takes a period of adjustment. Beads of sweat dotted his ebony skin as this journey had been a difficult one, crossing boundries that are crossed only by the most powerful of minds. A single haunting image stayed fixed in his mind, at first he thought he was seeing the past but slowly the realization came to him that he was indeed seeing the present, again Zebuxoruk was calling to be freed and this time, haste was of the essence.

Belquillon stood a little too quickly, still a little dizzy from the scrying, and he was forced to steady himself on the back of his chair. He moved to another part of the tower, a small room partitioned off from the prying eyes of his apprentices and pupils. A globe began to glow a soft, dark blue as if it were greeting him, as he looked deeply in to the orb he could see the mathmatical symbol for infinty slowly rotating inside. He laid his hand upon it and uttered one simple phrase.

Come my friends, we must return.

Eternal Sovereign, reinfoced by a number of guildies who only recently completed their flags, tore in to the Plane of Time with a vengence this weekend plunging deeper in to the plane than we ever have on any single day. On Saturday alone we cleared though the armies of the gods themselves to have Terris Thule, Saryn, Vallon Zek and Rallos Zek fall before us. The clever tactician Tallon Zek, apparently smelling defeat in the wind never even took the field of battle.

Sunday arrived to find us delayed by mysterious forces beyond our control but ultimately our patience paid off and we were able to ambush Tallon before he could slink off again. Back on P5. Cazic and Bertox and Inny fell quickly to us and we found Quarm standing in his familiar place. 45 Minutes later, the plane was clear.

In short: 2 Days, 6 Phases, 8 Gods, 1 Nasty Dragon and 45 Upgrades for our members including 4 chest pieces.

Hail, Quarm !!! Innoruuk Falls to Eternal Sovereign !!!
Sakharra, Mon Aug 02, 2004 7:32 am


Its been a long, hard road for us, but we are finally here in "Phase 6" of the Plane of Time, with one 4 headed dragon standing between us and being able to say we beat the Planes of Power expansion. I am pleased to introduce our new friend, along with two of the guys to whom we are thankful for helping us get to see him.



To get to Quarm tonight, we still had to go though a number of Gods some of whom we had only killed a single time, thus Vallon Zek, Rallos Zek, Bertoxxulous and Cazic Thule all died at our hands for the second time, leaving only one god left in the Plane of Time. Behold Innoruuk:



We knew from a small number of attempts that he was a tough customer, in fact ES had yet to take Inny below 50 percent. He lived up to his reputation and proved every bit as challenging as our previous tries had indicated, but ES was on a mission, as Pagona had observed, we wanted to clear to Quarm, and we would not be denied. It started nicely enough, we had him well under control for a long time but near the end this powerful god almost managed to snatch victory from us. When he fell it was with only a handfull of ES remaining in the zone, but a handfull of ES was just enough to get the job done.

Congratulations ES on another guild first !



So Innoruuk, the final god standing between Eternal Sovereign and Quarm fell under our blades today, on our first attempt of the night, allowing us our first glimpse at Quarm... What a glimpse it was.



Soon Quarm, very very soon... We actually got him to 8% but this little rogue went splat before then.

So who got what since Wednesday ? Everyone in this guild needs to give our highly dedicated DKP officer Barad a big hug because she had a huge job to do this week.
