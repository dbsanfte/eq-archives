Monday October7th , 2002
more dragon killing... by tiink

Don't ya just love when stuff dies?
We spent another night in North ToV, and as usual, got rewarded well
Eashen was the first to die, and rewarded Crimsonwolf with a Cloak of the Sky, and gave Darkmore a Bracelet of the Sky.

Next came the giant fish of doom, who dropped Greaves of the Deep Sea for Rezel, and Siren Song, Dagger of the Sea which we awarded to Konstantin, from Via Nocturna.


Then we took a stroll down to Lady M's pit, who dropped two Mithril Helm's. Grats to Rilian and Aallaan on those.

As usual, a good night for ES =)

Sunday October6th , 2002
Potato appetizer before Ntov dinner by fazin

Rhag Zhezum deaded before I logged on but I know that we have a brand new Scale Mesh Vambraces furnished just for Taetynwych.

We got hungry so for appetizer, We downed Cazic Thule and as a door prize, Tudana won Pauldrons of Ferocity, Rezel won Cloak of the Fearsome, Hillbilly won Bile Etched Obsidian choker.

After the appetizer, the main course came with 5 dragon hosts. Ikitiar fought rather weak, but gave us a nice Mask of venom for Berben. Aaryonar was quite rude with his minis but graciously gave us an Amulet of dreadgazer for Piromeomp and Reaper's ring for Amiel. Lord Feshlak fought roaring and raving but died whimpering. His loots was Amulet of the storm to Laiderone and Staff of the Silent Star to Sendyden. Dagarn the destroyer was pathetic...Verant need to change his name to Dagarn the free loot. Anyway, he dropped Bow of the Destroyer for Elendos and Ancient Wurm Hide Robe for Fazin. Lord Kreizenn died quickly and gave nothing worth looting.

Yard trash loots was considerable:
Gratz to Hillbilly for kael plate greaves and Ring of Superiority, Grakdrak for Shield of Fury, Taetynwych on Kael Chain Bp, and our monks, Azerel and Valkyri on this Silent Fang Necklaces.

Very successful Saturday for us all. Gratz to everyone all around.
