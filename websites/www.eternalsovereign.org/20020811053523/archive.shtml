Patch Day by sayble

We had a busy, productive night on Wednesday despite the difficulties people had in downloading the patch and logging on.

First we went to Ssraeshza Temple and killed Rhag Zhezum. He dropped 2 copies of Blessing of Aegolism (to be distributed later) and a Serpent Skin Armlet that went to Crimsonwolf.

After that we had many choices of where to go but opted for Kael Drakkal to throw yet another surprise party for the Statue of Rallos Zek. The Statue even decided to join us for a little while this time!

Derakor the Vindicator dropped Boots of the Vindicator which went to Jvarin.

The Statue of Rallos Zek dropped a Breastplate of Eradication, a Swiftblade of Zek and two Reavers. Congrats to Gorag on the BP (finally!), Kaimira on Swiftblade and Grakdrak and Glimk on the Reavers.
