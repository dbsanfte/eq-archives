And Now for Somthing Completely Different...
Newbrow, Sat Oct 04, 2003 6:37 pm
Did DXX and Seru this past while, along with MB and crazed trial (grats to the flagged) but I wont go into much detail about those...

It was a friday afternoon, and Eternal Sovereign and Acropolis were sitting in wait for our good friend, Rallos Zek. 10 Hours Later we are still sitting there. We decide to indulge in a game of "Redlight Greenlight" off balcony steps. So the hours passed and people didnt log.

1 AM EST: 11 Hours after Mobilization
We all went to sleep, with our alarms set. 6 hours later, 70 some odd Groggy, irritated, and annoyed raiders looked into the face of death itself. And we laughed.

After wiping twice to falling asleep on the keyboard, 11AM came around the corner. We paced ourselves and ran in. Tallon fell with ease, and Vallon just left because nobody noticed him. Word is that he has committed suicide.

We faught, sweat and blood dripping from our bodies and soon the giant expresso we were waiting for stood infront of us. We drank it, and proceeded quickly to destiny.

Witness; almost 24 hours of camping in 1, tiny jpg. Nuts? perhaps. Determined? I think so.
