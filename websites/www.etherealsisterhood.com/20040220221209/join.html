in the Ethereal Sisterhood

Recruitment Guidelines

  1. You must have a sponsor before applying for membership. A sponsor is a member or officer of the Sisterhood who you know and who can speak for your character's character.
  2. After appyling for membership, you will be invited to attend one of our guild event(raids and mini raids). If no events have been scheduled then joining 2 guild groups (groups with 3 or more sisters) would be an acceptible alternative.
  3. Two or more officers will discuss their opinions and if at least two agree then you will receive an invitation to the guild.
