Index

The Ethereal Sisterhood welcomes all who seek to further their knowledge concerning our leader, officers and members at large, and more regarding what we are all about, and how to become a member of this Awesome Guild.

We have tried to make this guild more like a "family." In this regard we have striven to be more supportive of each other, rather than the usual power-leveling, item giving, me-first everyone else second, epic only type of guild, that can be joined by merely answering a general /ooc or /shout and saying , "Yeah, I'll be in your guild."

We want our members' experience in the Sisterhood to be as positive as possible. And while we realize that our Guild is not right for everyone, we do believe that it certainly is a cut above the usual "gang" of people that just "hang out" together.

The following index will guide prospective, as well as seasoned members, in their quest to find out what we are all about! Happy and Safe hunting to all.
