Xita Frostglade

Guildmistress and Big Mamma of the Ethereal Sisterhood, Xita was the Daughter of the village waterbearer. She fell in love with a Captain from the Royal guard of Faydwer. As his betrothed, she agreed to watch his chicken coop while he left to war with the Crushbone Orcs. When his sword was all that returned from battle, she took up this steel, left the chicken coop. Then she committed herself to become a Shadowknight of Tunare and champion of all women who desire to stand on their own two feet.
