Guild Raid Rules for the
Ethereal Sisterhood


Guild Raid Loot Policy

1. All items will be rolled based on NBG (Need Before Greed). This means 2 things. First, if an item drops that is only warrior usable then ONLY warriors at the raid will be allowed to roll. Just because you are a warrior though does not mean you can roll with hopes of selling the item. If you will not use the item then do not roll. Anyone who wins an item and does not use it may not be allowed to roll on future items.

2. You may only loot ONE peice of loot per raid unless all have won. If you are the only paladin at a raid and 2 paladin only items drop then you may receive both.

3. Dropable items not rolled on during a guild raid will be banked.

4. Any spells not scribable by a player at a raid will be banked. Example: Malo (level 60 shaman spell) drops during a raid and we have no level 60 shaman then the spell will be banked until a level 60 shaman needs it.

5. Any cash loots as well as pp looted at the raid should be given to the bankers.

6. Items used for tradeskills can be given to guildies at no charge. Please do not interrupt the raid to ask for items! Send the looter a tell and let them know of your interest. At the end of the raid such items will be distributed.

7. Only members of the Ethereal Sisterhood will be allowed to roll for items drop during raids. (See #1 for the no drop exception to this rule.)
