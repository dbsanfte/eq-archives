Guild Charter of the
Ethereal Sisterhood

The Ethereal Sisterhood is dedicated to the advancement of the image of the Female Adventurer in society by being positive role models for young women who wish to follow in the footsteps of our pioneering and adventurous foremothers.

The orphanages of Norrath have been a constant source of young, starry-eyed dreamers, whose only role model, however, who has been offered is a sexy looking woman with red hair in a revealing, chain mail bikini, who is portrayed as a brawny man-hating loner.

While there is nothing wrong with being sexy, or red-haired, and the choice of clothing is truly a personal matter, other role model choices must be given to these young women that are more realistic, and feminine to them. An intelligent sorceress, a wise woods-woman, a cunning rogue, a virtuous paladin, a resourceful nature-loving druid, the clever enchantress, the caring and sharing cleric and the courageous beastlord all make truly excellent role models for young women, as do the many other professions pursued by the membership of the Ethereal Sisterhood.

Through our skills, we demonstrate that we are every bit the equal of our male counterparts. We sometimes even exceed their skills at our chosen trades. Everything women do is done with style, grace, and compassion; and while we recognize that men do have their little Achilles' heels, it is not our role to belittle them in any way, nor even to highlight their weaknesses. The Sisterhood even has a small number of men within our ranks. But these men have proven they believe in, and fully support, the image of the capable female adventuress.

We attract membership through our good deeds, and our "Family-like" atmosphere of caring and sharing with others. We provide members, and others, with less means than ourselves, assistance and aid, without being asked. We are kind to all, friendly to most, and caring beyond measure to our fellow sisters. We neither seek to be the biggest nor most uber guild around. But we do consider ourselves to be the most considerate, fun-loving, and supportive guild in all of Norrath.
