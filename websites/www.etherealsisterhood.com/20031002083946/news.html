9-3-2003

*** 3,000 Platinum All yours! ***
Starting Thursday September 4, 2003 there will be a recruitment contest open to all members and officers of the Ethereal Sisterhood.

So here's how it works:

First you find 5 players over level 40 and get them to join the sisterhood. Tell them to come to the message boards and post their application. Two officers then interview the recruit and if all goes well they are tagged into the guild. Make sure when they post their application that they were referred by you.

The first Sister to refer 5 40+ players gets 3,000pp!

Conditions**:

1. These must be new players, former sisters do not count.
2. No alts! If you find someone with 5 level 40 characters that still only counts as 1 player.
3. The new recruits must stay members for at least 1 month. Make sure once your referals are tagged that the dates of tagging are posted on the message boards.
4. This contest is only open to current sisters. If someone joins tomorrow they may not participate.
5. As it stands this contest will end November 4, 2003. Thats 2 months to find 5 members.
6. The money will be paid out of my pocket not the guild fund.
7. No one is allowed to /ooc for new members. Prospects should be friends or players you have at least grouped with.

Good luck to everyone! Please be careful who you recommend, we want good players who will stay with our family. No jerks!

**conditions subject to change if I find I've left anything out that would be unfair to the guild

5-6-2003 GUILD RAID 5/10/2003 in Old Sebillis!

Picture of Seb Key Turn in Spot.

Well we raided and we had a pretty good time.

The raid started off with me logging on after 5...so bad raid leader hehe. We were all at the TT/EJ zone by 5:15 so that wasn't so bad.

It did take us another 45 minutes to actually reach Sebillis due to agro and a few people getting lost. Not bad for a first time trip but could be better.

We zoned in and of course NG was camped. The bank was open though and I knew our force could fare well there. Gradually we began clearing the Zone in towards the Jail. As we pulled 3-spawn though we had a small problem and Jaed was killed. We were however saved by someone's friend Epyionn and he rezzed Jaed and hit us all with BoA. With our new buffs and after the death we were determined. We cleared through and managed to clear the bank for quite a while.

We got a few spells and we also made a bit of money for the guild bank.

Thank you all for coming, it was fun. Sorry to those who were unable to attend.
