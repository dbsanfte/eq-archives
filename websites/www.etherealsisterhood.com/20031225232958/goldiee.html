Goldiee Holyspell

Goldiee is the fourth child of ten born to a jeweler and a fish monger. Since she was the only daughter she helped to care for and raise her brothers .When she was a teenager, her entire family was killed by the brownies.  She wandered for a long time, learning her skills as an enchanter.  One day she met a druid named Shawdoom and found the Sisterhood. The Sisterhood has become her extended family,  role models and mentors.
She met a young cleric name Fdail and after some time became engaged and shortly afterward married him.
