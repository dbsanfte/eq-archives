Guild Rules for the
Ethereal Sisterhood

1) Always treat others with dignity and respect. We all pay to play this game for one reason: to have fun. Make sure your fun is not at the expense of others. Do your best to make others have a good time too.

2) Always handle disputes discreetly and directly. Use tells! OOC and shout only makes you and the rest of the guild appear foolish.

3) If somebody does not treat you with the respect you have shown them. Discuss the matter with either an officer or with that person's guild officers.

4) Call your trains and do not train deliberately. Not only is this a guild rule but it is also part of Sony's "Play Nice Policy."

5) Do not steal other people's kills(called KSing). If someone else is engaging a mob, do NOT try to kill it.

6) Abide by party loot rules. Never loot a NO DROP item without first consulting the group about it. Do NOT be a loot ninja (looting everything as soon as the corpse hits the ground).

7) Do not beg. From guildies or from others. We all had to work for what we have, if you need advice we are always willing to share.

8) Be fair in all dealings.

9) Show up for raids and meetings on time and prepared, both within the guild and with other guilds.
