TRIBUNAL OF NORRATH

About Tribunal Of Norrath

Honored Members, Friends, and Potential Members of the Tribunal,

Tribunal of Norrath was founded out of many friendships formed in Norrath, on the Saryrn Server of Everquest. As a guild, together we grew, thrived and prospered. Tribunal of Norrath is still strong and proud to this day. We pride ourselves on our family like atmosphere, our strong loyalties, mutual respect, and the sense of belonging that we try to give to all our members, reguardless of level, race, class, or religion.

As the knowledge of the Tribunal of Norrath grows, so does the request for membership. We welcome all in their desire to be part of a strong and honor bound guild. However, we must also ensure that we stay this way.
As a member, each of you are expected to act to all within the guild with a high level of respect and cooperation.

Loyalty

Peace and truth

Honor and integrity

Brotherhood and Friendship

By following these basic tenets, regardless of class or race, good or evil, we not only bring a level of excitement to the world in which we delve into, but also a environment that will bring most of us back time and time again. The Tribunal of Norrath will attempt to do what other guilds tend to neglect:

To support it's members.

To deliver a feeling of family and brotherhood.

To give all members something to attain and bring a level of respect for being part of a guild that has standards.

To be a guild that seeks quality and not quantity when it comes to it's members.

Some will follow the paths of evil, others the path of good. In the end, no matter what path any of us choose, let us all show honor, valor, loyalty and justice each and every day we walk the lands of Norrath and carry the Tribunal of Norrath's name next to ours.

MEMBERSHIP

A standard must be set to ensure a quality guild, one that each member that is involved can be proud of. As of now we have a set of rules to follow when inviting a new member or for any of you reading this that wish to become a member of the Tribunal of Norrath.

LEVEL REQUIREMENT: All new recruits must be be at least level 35 to be considered for membership. (Special circumstances can negate this rule, but it is rarely done.) At least two ranked(Officer,Guardian or Leader) members must give approval before admittance shall be granted. Members of the guild should take the opportunity to group with the recruit before the actual invitation is granted. There will be a mandatory two week probation period for all new members, during which at anytime in this period membership may be revoked due to derogatory actions witnessed by veterans of our guild. Friends of outstanding members (friends in which they group with on a regular basis or have personal contact with ) below the level 35 membership requirement may seek membership if they obtain two vouched approvals from ranked members and will be placed on the mandatory two week probation period. Members Second Characters, should be admitted with a level requirement of level 10 if they wish to do so, but all members and alts. under the level of 30 will use the /roleplay command. We are a 35+ guild, and we strive to not be labeled as a newbie guild.

CHARACTER MERIT: All potential members should have high degree of loyalty to their character and enjoy playing it. Whether evil or good, one must exhibit a dedication to friends and a passion for adventure. They should exhibit a excellent grouping style and a desire to help others.

QUEST OF ENTRY: A optional quest should be given to any potential member that an officer or guardian wishes to invite that is not well known. A Quest helps to show the motivation and willingness to join our guild. It promotes a feeling of deserved membership and gives the rest of the guild the ability to gain trust in their new friend and member. These quest should be fun and rewarding for any who may participates.

Always remember that we are a guild based on friendship and family values. We all should have faith in our members and the new ones we wish to include in our guild. If in doubt on someone's loyalty, ask yourself if that person would lay down their life for you and would you do the same for them, without any reservation? If you are unsure of the other's feelings on this, then more time is most likely required before a suitable invite should be given. If the answer is yes to both parts, then that is a good indication of the type of person we would love to have in our family.
