Tribunal Of Norrath's Rules Of Conduct


Guild rules of conduct, are established mainly for the purposes of showing respect for the guild you are part of, to not bring shame upon them, showing respect for those you play around, and abiding by the Verants play nice policy so we dont get banned.

Here are some basic common sense rules and few extras that we as a guild should use at all times, if any member breaks rules intentionally or reflects negatively on our guild, we will as a guild decide how he/she is to be dealt with, the final decision will be made by the officers/guardians/leader who are online.

1.Show respect always for your fellow guild members, if you have a dissagreement work it out in private, or bring it to a ranked member to mediate, a dissagreement isnt worth losing a valued member of our family,and we are all valued.

2.Dont Beg, point blank, no ifs ands or butts, from anyone. If there is an item you need, ask for assistance in obtaining it, work your tail off and save to buy it, trade for it, get help to do a quest for it, but dont beg. No one likes a begger, no one wants to tolerate a begger, neither will we.

3.There will be no Kill Stealing, this is just inexcusable behavior from anyone, really makes a guild and its members look bad, there are some instances when it accidentally happens(for example the AC in S.Ro), but avoid it, please.

4.There will be no Loot Stealing, this is probably one thing that that really makes a guild looks bad, really bad, i see no exceptions for this, you cant accidentally loot steal. If you did not do the majority of the damage to the mob, or kill it, then do not loot it!!! This can be considered as grounds to being removed from the guild!!!

5.Please do not argue, spam, or swear in open channels(/ooc,/shout,/auction,/say,/emote), playing around is one thing, but we all know the difference.

6.This isnt necessarily a rule, but more of a suggestion, it really is important for a guild to take the time to play together, hunting, events, raids and even quests, are the main things that will bring us as a guild closer as friends, and family.

The Tribunal Council
