Tribunal Of Norrath Membership

Welcome. If you are seeking a membership in Tribunal Of Norrath, then please take a moment to read the "Tribunal of Norrath", and the "Rules Of Conduct", sections of this site.
If you are within the level requirements and feel that our standards as a guild match what you are looking for in a guild please take a moment to submit an online request at our message board, also include your class/level, online name, and which of our members directed you to our guild.
It is also suggested that you take the time to talk to a few of our members and play with them to give you an opportunity to get to know some of us, and also to let us get to know you. To submit an online request please click on the picture above. Thank You!!!
