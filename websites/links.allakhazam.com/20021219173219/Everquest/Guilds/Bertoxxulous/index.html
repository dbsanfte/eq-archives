To let the one days passing be a forever cherished event, no matter where you are, where destiny leads you. That there is always going to be a band of adventurers that thrive for your same thirst of knowledge, adventure and togetherness that has long since been rumored a myth by the elders. Guilds are not for the convenient, but for the dedicated, the strong of heart, which flows through the blood of true friendship. It is with this that we need to teach the younger generations to follow, what some consider lore and elders ramblings.
