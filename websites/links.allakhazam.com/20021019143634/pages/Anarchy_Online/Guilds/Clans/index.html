Additionally, Phalanx will work to use its wealth, strength, and political influence to bring the rebel clans together. ====================================== OOC MISSION: The OOC mission of Phalanx is to bring role play dedicated Anarchy Online gamers together to collectively enhance their enjoyment of the game. While Phalanx is a role play oriented clan, it is not a die hard, 100% RP oriented organization. It is our belief that in a game like AO, you would be miserable if you tried to stay in character at all times. There are far too many players that are not role playing, and far too many complicated aspects of gameplay that make OOC communication a frequent necessity. Phalanx seeks to stimulate RP through character development, RP events, and frequent "spur of the moment" or impromptu role playing between members and non-members. We also hope to interest other AO players in role playing by providing an entertaining and interesting example of how much fun role playing can be. Most importantly, the goal of Phalanx is to have fun. It is not a competitive forum for people to grand stand or to prove their "superior RP ability" to others (IC grand standing is, of course, a completely separate matter). We work together to create interesting stories and situations that involve and interest as many members as possible. Role playing is best when it is a group effort. AO is far more enjoyable when you have fun people to chat with, RP with, and explore the lands with. Phalanx seeks to provide all of these things to its members.
