  • Harbingers of Innoruuk
    The Guild Charter The purpose and rules of the Harbingers of Innoruuk Written By: Jarlaxlle Mordae The Harbingers of Innoruuk is a collection of souls in the land of Norrath who enjoy the concept of role playing. Role playing is an undefined word in Norrath, and many do not understand its meaning, so let me define it for any who read this text. There is a spreading sickness in the land. Many people believe that Norrath is not "real life", and speak of another world. The Harbingers was created as a haven for those who have the cure for this disease...it is called Role playing. Therefore all Harbingers must display they possess this cure, for at the first sign of the sickness a brother or sister is severely chastened by those around him/her. If they repeatedly come down with the sickness, they will be forced to leave, as we do not want the guild infected with the disease. Innoruuk decreed we would start this guild for one reason (see prophecies of Innoruuk). We shall one day destroy the pale skin way of life and pave the way for our Master's return. Until then, we should use the pale skins as shields, never letting them know their eventual fate as it has been decreed by Innoruuk. By using this excuse, we are allowed to hunt with the pale skins, and even keep them alive, for a broken shield is of no use to us. Use the pale skins well, for one day they will be our slaves. To fulfill our dream, we must follow a few simple rules. All Harbingers are held accountable to the following rules: 1. Always help a brother or sister in need. 2. Always display the cure to the plague in Norrath...ROLEPLAY. 3. Be active in guild functions. Attend meetings and complete quests. 4. No swearing unless with well known companions. We must remember that there are children in Norrath, who although have the bodies of warriors, still have minds of children. 5. Begging is unacceptable, we take what we want. There is an armory full of useful items. Complete quests and gain access to the armory's most powerful items. 6. Remember.....as a Harbinger, you represent the entire guild. That sums up the rules we have in place, to keep the guild from reverting into chaos. Follow these rules and you shall be a successful Harbinger, and be revered by those around you when we conquer the pale skins. -Jarlaxlle Mordae
