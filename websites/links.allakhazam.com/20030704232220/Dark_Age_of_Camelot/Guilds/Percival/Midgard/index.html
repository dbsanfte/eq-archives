  • Diamond
    Diamond (short for Diamond Katana, with the Katana being dropped on the Percival server for RP reasons) is an RPG guild, and not just a Camelot guild. The core of this guild has played together for years. We're small, numbering in the low 30s. Most of us age in our 30s, with some in their 40s, some in their 20s, and a couple in their teens. It is maturity, however, and not age per se that is important to us. We strive to follow the code of Bushido, based on the principles of bravery, compassion, equanimity, courtesy, sincerity, honour, and loyalty. Diamond is a casual RP guild, whose most important aspect is friendship. The emphasis for us is on having fun. To us there are no winners or losers; you only lose if you don't enjoy yourself. We strive to continue to learn to work together better, but never at the expense of forgetting that these are games. Please feel free to look at our website, and contact myself or Ylsa in-game if you have any further questions, or e-mail me at: diamondkatana@hotmail.com. Therin Doomhammer (50 Thane) Tyral (46 SB) GL, Diamond
