    We have created three "guilds" to allow ongoing roleplaying and conflict in Norrath on Bristlebane. "Starfire", "Forces of Light" and "Forces of Darkness" guilds have been created for roleplay purposes. These are for those wishing to participate in ongoing roleplaying and to allow events to span more than one guild vs another guild. Players may freely come and go between these guilds and their own guilds as they wish to participate in the greater events. Note: these guilds will occasionally be at "guildwar" with each other, thus if you join either side you will be at risk of being attacked by the other side (though note rules on honourable conduct).
