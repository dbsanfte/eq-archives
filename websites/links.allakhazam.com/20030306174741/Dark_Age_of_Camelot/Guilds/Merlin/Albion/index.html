  • Soldiers of Fortune
    Soldiers of Fortune wants YOU. "People of Albion, we are beset on all sides. We are challenged from the lowly bandit who accosts travelers across our fair land, to the Midgards and Hibernians who would overthrow all of the peace loving people of Albion. The resources of our kingdom are stretched to their limits in defending our borders and relics, and King Constantine is... He is no King Arthur. We truly face our darkest hour. Thus it is by proclamation that a select few of those who are loyal to the great church of Albion shall raise an army of mercenaries, forged in the heat of battle, tempered by the wisdom of the holy church and fueled by the spoils of war to combat those apostates who threaten our way of life. And these soldiers of fortune shall exact a mighty toll upon our foes." --Reginauld Kilroan, Cleric of the Church of Albion Soldiers of Fortune is a casual guild on the Merlin server. We all have lives, or at least pretend to, and can't afford to spend all our free time getting to level 50 as fast as possible. We are seeking new members who can help contribute to our guild whether by their razor sharp sword, or their razor sharp wit. We strive to be an organized guild and believe the use of a website with bulletin boards is key to that end. We believe in supporting all members of the guild regardless of level or profession. Our members actively play alternate characters in order to be able to group together more often. Ultimately, we feel that the guild that can arrange its own entertainment is the one that will succeed. Applicants or interested parties can either visit us at our website: http://137.99.92.251/daoc/sof.html Or look us up in the realm of Albion.
