  • The Huntsmen of Rivenmyrth
    A History of the Huntsmen of Rivenmyrth Long ago, between the Age of Blood and the Age of Enlightenment, there existed a period of time known to a few as the Age of Balance. Those who have this knowledge say that during this age the gods maintained an active presence in the affairs of Norrath and they held direct influence on the inhabitants of the lands. Norrath was swiftly moving from being a chessboard in the god’s games to an active war zone. The Nameless, creator of the gods, decided to make his presence known and selected a group of powerful and wise champions to the cause of balancing the powers in conflict upon the world he had created. He gave them a land to call their own, a beautiful idyllic land, from which to pursue their mission. All was well until Veeshan, Queen of the plane of sky and the first god to discover Norrath, corrupted one of the champions. In a bid to recover what was hers “by right of discovery” the former champion was given untold power and bid to aid her in its deliverance. The former champion had the guile and skill to accumulate even more from the other gods and deceived Veeshan herself. His deception included persuading the champions to ally with Veeshan and forsake their oath to the nameless to maintain balance. With his stolen power he was as powerful as the gods themselves. By the time the true champions of Norrath discovered the taint of his betrayal it was nearly too late. In an epic battle the gods and the champions contained the traitor but could not destroy him so powerful had he become. The Nameless one was not pleased and he cursed the champions to an eternity of strife and a striving for the balance they had failed to maintain. He tore their beautiful homeland from the world and hid it amongst the planes. The former champions became his hunters, their lost land they call Rivenmyrth, meaning torn from happiness. Their souls scattered across the Ages the huntsmen are continually seeking redemption. Unfortunately, they may only find it when they are all gathered in the same time and place. The nameless is not completely unmerciful; he allows them to gather every thousand years or so to accumulate power and influence and to attempt to correct their error. The last such attempt failed and resulted in the Combine Empire. This time the problem will be attacked directly, the Sleeper must be slain!
