  • Cold Comfort Guild
    Cold Comfort is a guild based upon honesty, bravery, and teamwork. We work to fulfill our destiny in Norrath, to make her lands a better place. We greet and respect all other guilds and clans in Norrath. We believe that we are all children of Norrath and with time, patience and understanding we can peacefully coexist with our brothers and sisters of other clans. We are a unique family, almost as old as Norrath herself. We have a rich heritage of members past and present who have worked hard to make Cold Comfort strong. We hope that all who look upon Cold Comfort and its members will see friendship, kindness, intellect, grace under pressure and compassion for our brothers and sisters in Norrath. We set an example for others to follow in our dealings with each other as well as with those outside our family, and we want to always bring honor to Cold Comfort Clan.
