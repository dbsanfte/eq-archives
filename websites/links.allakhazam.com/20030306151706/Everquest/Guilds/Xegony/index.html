  • Dark Templars
    Requirements for joining Dark Templars. We are at present no longer accepting applications for membership, that does NOT mean you can't still apply, just don't expect a response on your application any time soon. This is a short term closure of applications while we reshuffle the guilds internal mechanics. To apply, goto our Forums section, and leave a message on the Applications board, put your name class, level and time zone in your in the application header. Then a brief description of why you want to join, what experience you have (in brief). etc. Your application will then be moved to the private Application area, where existing members will vote on your application. Once your application reaches 20 total votes, a decision will be made based on the result. If the decision is positive you will be guilded for 1 month. during which time you are expected to group with other guild members wherever possible. After this 1 month period, a re-poll on your application is taken, where we will decide if we wish to offer you a permanent position in the Dark Templars. If your application was declined you will be notified by an Officer and given the reasons why, where possible. (23rd Aug 2001).
