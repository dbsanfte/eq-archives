  • Dragonlords of Antonica  updated
    It is said that blue diamonds are the tears of gods. While as mortals, we cannot hope to understand what would make a god cry, we must acknowledge the fact that the proliferation of blue diamonds in Norrath today signals a grave sadness in the heavens above, and the abyss below. It is our pledge to Norrath's deities, that we will not ignore the anguish they are suffering. We will form the Dragonlords of Antonica, and make all the citizens of Norrath aware of the despair that must have befallen them. We will carry examples of their tears so that we may attempt to convince the people of the world that there are forces at work that are beyond our meager understanding, and these forces are upset about something. It is in Norrath's best interest to reveal what has caused this sadness, and to attempt to cure it. The Dragonlords of Antonica has embarked on this quest.
