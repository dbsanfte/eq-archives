  • House Zebulun
    House Zebulun is comprised of persons descending from the bloodline of the hero Zebulun and his ten sons. Members of the House are dedicated to supporting Zebulun as the champion of good. The House is charged with fulfilling Zebulun’s missive to prepare our family for the final and greatest conflict between the forces of good and evil. At the appointed time, Zebulun will awake from his slumber to lead the forces of good against the Three Glooms and their evil minions. It is known this conflict will rage across all the planes of existence. This will be a war of annihilation, with the loser having no chance of redemption. Although Zebulun’s bloodline has of course been greatly diluted throughout the centuries, all true descendants are said to be “of the blood”. Those persons of the blood have the innate ability to travel throughout the many planes of existence. For many, this ability lies dormant until some great need awakens it. A fortunate few are raised with the knowledge of their ability and the responsibility it imparts. House Zebulun seeks to gather all of the blood, who are true of purpose, to the family stronghold at Caer Bythol. From the ancient castle, we wage a limited war against the forces of evil, ever-mindful of Zebulun’s charge to preserve the family bloodline at all costs.
