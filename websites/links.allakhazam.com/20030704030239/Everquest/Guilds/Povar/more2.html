    Born to a race driven by fear and hatred, Vanyun, became an outcast by expressing his heartfelt belief that all races are equal and to hate was wrong. Determined to show that love and friendship are more powerful than hatred he began his quest to unite others to follow this cause. Strangely, he found his strongest support was from his fellow elves and thus Elves United was born. The main purpose of this new fellowship of elves is to spread the beliefs that all races are equal and the bonds of friendships are eternal.
