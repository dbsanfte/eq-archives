  • Avatars of Destiny
    The Avatars of Destiny are a guild based in Midgard. We are a group of players who hope to make our mark as a powerful and respected force, or as a dangerous foe, depending on which realm you are from. We hope to be involved in realm-versus-realm combat, and we also encourage and support roleplaying, though if it is not your cup of tea, you don't have to participate (Editor's note: I am a roleplayer myself, and I hope to make sure that roleplayers have a good time in this guild, I may well organize some events myself). We hope also to build up a good foundation of tradesmen and tradeswomen, in order to make our guild strong economically, and also to ensure that members can always get good quality, cut price equipment. If you would like to join please go to the forum and post. =) Thanks --Kashue--
