  • ITEC
    The Independent Trade and Exploration Coalition (ITEC) is a privately owned company striving to provide the independent trader and explorer with a consolidated union. Run by council, the company gives traders of all professions a forum to gather resources, design and market wares, and interact with other craftsmen of like minds. ITEC also prides itself on establishing one of the largest funding efforts to expand trade and discover untapped natural resources in the galaxy. Having members specialized in exploration of uncharted or hard-to-reach systems, ITEC has been first with many key discoveries, giving them a unique foothold in several remote areas. While many speculate that ITEC involves itself in "more sensitive" matters, including political and militaristic plights, these reports are largely unsubstantiated. Still, ITEC’s unique presence in outlying systems and somewhat private corporate nature are cause enough to make one wonder.
