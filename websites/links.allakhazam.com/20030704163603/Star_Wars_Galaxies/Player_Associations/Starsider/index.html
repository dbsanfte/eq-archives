  • Imperial Guard
    The Imperial Guard is a battalion of the Imperial military, commissioned directly by Emperor Palpatine himself. The Imperial Guard consists of three companies dedicated to Army, Navy, and Civil Services respectively. The Imperial Guard is strongly, but not solely, a combat unit dedicated to the destruction of the rebellion. We have room for non-combatants in a wide range of positions from crafting to medicine to entertaining to politics. The Imperial Guard welcomes players of any profession, and all but Mon Calamari and Wookie races. Only humans are allowed into the Army and Navy companies of the Guard, but other species are welcome into the Civil Services. Beyond these basic limitations, the Guard is looking for players who are enthusiastic about participating in the galactic civil war as members of the Empire. We do not require roleplaying, but strongly encourage it. Casual gamers and power gamers alike are welcome to the Guard. However, the Guard does enforce a code of conduct requiring players to treat others with respect and fairness; including our enemies. By respect, we mean a non-harassing, non-griefing attitude (such as the swearing or personalized attacks). This policy does in no way imply Guard members should not kill rebels on site. Failure to follow the conduct policy could result in removal from the Guard.
    http://www.imperial-guard.net
