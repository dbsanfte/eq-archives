Application to Mortalis

REQUIREMENTS

*Level 60.

*Innate Run Speed 3 (AA).

*Sebilis Key.

*Charasis Key.

*Some form of permanent enduring breath item.

*Melee: Epic or better. For casters if you don't have it don't expect us to get it for you.

*One or more instant cast buff items. (In-and Outdoor)

*Resist gear, MINIMUM of 100 fire/cold/disease/poison unbuffed, while simultaneously having 100 unbuffed in magic resist.

*Zone interconnectivity knowledge, be able to navigate a zone with /loc, 100 + Sense heading. (IE must be able to go from Nexus to umbral alone)

*200 swimming

*Solid connection (DSL, cable), stable comp, if you comp/connection can't handle 50 players in a small room, we can't use you, so don't bother applying.

*Playtime: You have to be able to play from 4 pm cet to 12 pm cet, at least 5 days per week. We are a raiding guild, however, you will always see some Mortalis members online doing exp and other quests. This is the kind of players we are looking for. People who play A LOT, who enjoy EverQuest and enjoy playing their class. (if we invite you as a given class, that's because we need that specific class, main switching is not allowed) If you feel you will quit because of real life or another game or if you will burn out for whatever reason, don't bother applying.

*We want players who aren't scared to die repeatedly. Players that never give up or complain when things get though. That's how we play as a guild.

*General Playing skills: We want players with good knowledge of their class, and generally a good knowledge of the EverQuest content. High end content knowledge is preferred, but if you lack it, it can be made up by fast learning skills and good concentration on raids.

*Advanced Skills requirements (luclin): We ask you to have a minimum of 18 AA skills (6 general + 12 arch skills) Natural durability is preferred for melee and spell casting mastery for casters. Note that the more AA you have the better it will be. Not that it improves your character a lot but it certainly shows your dedication.

*** You need to be willing to work and spend time on Emperor / VT key, we will help you, don't be scared to ask for help! It only shows us your motivation to join us on keyed encounters***
RECRUITMENT SYSTEM

Here is the process you have to follow if you wish to be a member of Mortalis:

1) You must read the requirements. Next thing is that you have to submit an application answering every single question above and of course meet the requirements. If you do not meet them, you won't be considered.

2) IF you meet the requirements AND if there is a spot open for your class your application will be moved towards our private forums for discussion. If you get enough good feedback about your reputation, skills etc... then you will be asked to come to raids so we can check you out. This process lasts one week, you will remain unguilded but you will be given an invite to our raid channel.
If the class is full but the officers feel this person is some kind of God, we will debate about it with the people of his/her class.
In any cases, if you get denied, you will receive an answer in game within one week maximum.

3) Once you pass the application process, you will be granted a trial. The trial is now negativ 150 elps. There is a minimum of 6 weeks on trial. On trial you will be given a 'guildinvite' during raids and asked to guildremove after. At negativ 100 elps, you can stay 'perma-tagged'.

4) And of course, when you reach 0 elp, we will vote on you. Your trial may be extended. One more thing, if you make it to full membership and do not meet what you promised us, you still have a chance to be guildremoved. This means the weeks after you get voted on are still crucial.
