What is League Of Levity ?

Well, there we were, peacefully blasting each other into small little giblets in Q2 RA. When Jody (Woodsman), Randy (Pyewacket) and Tim (Rellika) started ranting about the coolest game in the world, a little something called EQ.  At the time it was still in BETA 3 or 4 and there was a healthy dose of skepticism amongst the rest of us following the Great UO Debacle.  Well, They BETA'ed on and brought a few people they had met on the BETA servers into LOL Clan.  Thus the Insidious Blight called EQ Addiction commenced.  Final release of EQ hit the streets and a couple people were tempted into buying it. Those few then suborned 2 folks each, and they converted 2 more folks and they convinced 2 folks each, well, you get the picture.   Pretty soon most of  LOL Clan was hardwired into The Rathe server and running around Nek Forest mushing Moss Snakes with gleeful abandon. 

When they started doing Guild creations we were there almost at the head of the line.  When we were created as Lost Souls we had one of the very first Guilds on Rathe server.  Over time, differing philosophies and playing styles began to emerge and we ended up with a pretty wide gulf between 2 separate groups.  Rather than limp along with each group trying to co-exist under one guild banner we, the old core {LOL} Clan group, split off and created the League of  Levity

And so here we are.


 

LOL Guild Charter 

Norrath is a world full of violence, death and an uncontrollable and growing greed. We, the members of The League of Levity, endeavor to bring a sense of levity and humor to our troubled world. Malice, prejudice and dishonesty are symptoms of a disease which infects our land. The League of Levity intends to fight this escalating disease with a medicine much stronger than Magic or Might: The Power of Laughter; The Tonic of Friendship.

In our unity, we will provide assistance to those in need, with a smile on our face and laughter in our heart.
