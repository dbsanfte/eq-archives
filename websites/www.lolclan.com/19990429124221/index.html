As a Member of the {LOL} Clan, I shall:

1. Be polite to all others at all times until such time as they prove unworthy and even then i will react with restraint and not allow myself to sink to their level.
2. Gracefully accept victory with a kind word for my opponents skill.
3. Even more gracefully accept my defeats with poise and sportsmanship.
4. Refrain from profane or vulgar behavior both in Chat and while Playing.
5. Never ever cheat.

I understand that these 5 rules could not possibly cover every situation but will be my guide in choosing a response to any given situation.

I agree with these guidelines and promise that I will adhere to them. I acknowledge that verified occurrences of non-compliance could be cause for dismissal from the Clan.
