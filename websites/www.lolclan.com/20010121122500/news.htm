1 Oct 98   EXEC COUNCIL MEETS !!!

Your beloved Prez has been dipping into his own pockets for most of the actual expenses involved with this happy family.  Due to some unforseen "stuff" he has not been able to dip quite so deep this year.  Alot of very fine folks have volunteered to dip a bit, but rather than have just a few stalwart folks carry the load it was decided that we would levy ALL folks for some money.  It isnt going to be very much and it is NOT mandatory.   It will be $20 a year per member or $30 dollars a year for multimember households.   Let me repeat, it is NOT mandatory!! But those that care for the Clan and all it stands for will find a way.   Checks may be sent to Barb. Details on address and such are available from her (dont think they would appreciate haveing their home address published )

The actual annual  costs are as follows:

Domain Hosting:                     $240   (courtesy of mike and Unreal City)

KALI Server license:              $100

INTERNIC Domain register:   $150 intially and $50 a year after the first 2 years

Any money collected in excess of immediate needs will be held for possible hardware upgrade donations to servers maintained by our members.   If we have enough money on hand and nothing in particular that needs paying, we will probably declare a dividend and cease collecting for a while.  Hopefully we will be able to figger out a way to password a page on the web and post a Treasurers Report or some such thing to keep all members posted on where there money is.

So crank up those checkbooks and lets support our selves :)

10 Oct 98     THE SATURDAY NIGHT TOURNEY IS BACK!!!!!

Well, yall asked for it and it was done.  The first ever Saturday Night Q2 2 v 2 tournaey was held last night and what a hoot it was.  Despite some initial false starts and a bit of rule changeing on the fly the tourney took off.   Rules are simple, 2 people per team, 1 LPB teamed with one Not So LPB.  Maps are standard 1v1 Q2 maps and are decided and announced in advance, first team to 20 points wins and progresses to next round.  

LET THE FRAGGING BEGIN!!   When all the smoke and giblets had cleared the winning team was   Brian {LOL}Duked2 and Kevin {LOL}Phalanx   played most valiantly by Alex {LOL}DarkNight and Sam {LOL}ElHefeDogg.  The map was  Railgun Stadium.

The Tourney will be a weekly thing on Sat nights, at 8 PM EST, 7 Central, 6 Mountain and 5 Pacific.  Teams MAY be permanent or will be made up that night.   Also trying to get the old LOL Duke Ladder suitably modded so we can use it also, more on that later.
