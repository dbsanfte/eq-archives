Who is {LOL}
{LOL} Clan started as a group of fun loving Duke players who got fed up with the growing population of rude and inconsiderate 'net gamers. We joined together to promote gamesmanship and netiquette across kali. Oh, we occasionally play games too! Our membership is an eclectic group of folks with members all over the US and Canada, including Alaska and Hawaii. Members ages range from 50 to 9 years old, we have about a half dozen or more married couples with both spouses on line (ALOT!), we have one family with 8 members. And even one members mom!! League of Levity's Guild Charter

Norrath is a world full of violence, death and an uncontrollable and growing greed. We, the members of The League of Levity, endeavor to bring a sense of levity and humor to our troubled world. Malice, prejudice and dishonesty are symptoms of a disease which infects our land. The League of Levity intends to fight this escalating disease with a medicine much stronger than Magic or Might: The Power of Laughter; The Tonic of Friendship.


In our unity, we will provide assistance to those in need, with a smile on our face and laughter in our heart.


 {LOL}Clan's Code of Conduct

As a Member of the {LOL} Clan, I shall:


1. Be polite to all others at all times until such time as they prove unworthy and even then I will react with restraint and not allow myself to sink to their level.
2. Gracefully accept victory with a kind word for my opponents skill.
3. Even more gracefully accept my defeats with poise and sportsmanship.
4. Refrain from profane or vulgar behavior both in Chat and while Playing.

5. Never ever cheat.


I understand that these 5 rules could not possibly cover every situation but will be my guide in choosing a response to any given situation.


I agree with these guidelines and promise that I will adhere to them. I acknowledge that verified occurrences of non-compliance could be cause for dismissal from the Clan.
