By the wishes of the Dark Father I brought together the souls that embody his most deadly of evils … 

envy, sloth, gluttony, wrath, pride, lust and greed …

 they and others empowered with sins of mortals now follow my will and in turn the will of the Father. 

Righteousness has no retreat … 

Goodness has no sanctuary … shadows are cast because of light … 

and from the shadows my children of sin shall arise. 

~ Master of the Shadows
