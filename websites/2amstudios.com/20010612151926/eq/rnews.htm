Portrait Winner!
Monday, June 11, 2001 by Raene

Teromil Trueblade of the Quellious Server won a Free Portrait by Raene at the Everquest Fan Faire in San Diego. It was great to talk with him in "RL" and find out what he would like for his paladin.

Of his character, he writes: "I try to play him as a noble and honorable character, just as a paladin should be. Always willing to help others, and glad to sacrifice himself in battle to save another... Of course he has a special hatred for Goblins and Undead."

Congratulations Teromil! Your small version is in your email, and the full size version can be viewed at http://2amstudios.com/eq/ordering.htm

A Rant from Mareth
Wednesday, June 6, 2001 by Raene

I reserve the right to piss and moan...

...Because god dammit there's nothing to do. No games, no movies, no books, no console games, nothing in EQ to do. I hate these lulls right before summer hits full swing.

Lets look at what there is to do:
Shrek: Ok flick. An ogre goes on a quest for a king so he can get the fairy tale creatures out of his swamp. Turns out the ogre isn't really evil, he's just lonely. I liked this movie as I was able to mentally dodge the insipid moral stamp dreamworks and disney love to enforce on my soul. I'd like to burn down thier offices but then that would mean no more Buzz Lightyear so I'm at an impass.

Mummy Returns: I loved this flick. It constantly makes fun of itself for being a sequel PLUS its just GOOD in its own right. If you liked the first one, this one I'd recommend. However my problem is, once I watch a movie, I've kinda killed it for something to do. Kinda like jerking off too early in the day.

Aside from that, there aren't much for movies that make me want to get out of my melancholy. I AM looking forward to Tomb Raider but don't believe me if I tell you its for the "story". Basically a chick in tight clothes whupping everyone's ass and shooting guns is pretty much bliss for me. AND it comes out on my birthday. I haven't been this happy on a birthday since I beat metroid the day I turned 15 and found out Samus was a chick (/moan).

During this lull of stuff to do, OF COURSE it rains. So I go out and buy the latest copy of PC Lamer. Anyone want to make a computer game mag with me that DOESN'T SUCK? These idiots are usually captivated by eyecandy. "It looks good!" Ya well so did the D&D movie. Check it for content and interface next time jackweed.

I'm so bored, in the past 2 months I've Re-played my stock of over 25 pc games, 14 console games and I've read 6 books. And GOD, please don't tell me "play EQ more!". I'll have to kill you by putting a rabid hampster in your underwear. I've played almost every game on the market PLUS I have high level EQ characters. Ok yes I have the attention span of an excited gnat. Shouldn't SOMETHING however come along? I've entered a zillion betas. Haven't been accepted into any of them (/rude Blizzard for taking so goddamn long for an expansion).

Someone get me FTP access for warez or into some beta so I can use my time for something GOOD! In the daytime I watch my kid so I can't commit the horrid time that EQ's high level game requires (well unless I want to log on and /cheer...yay THAT'S fun! I'd rather jump on a grenade.). HELP ME GUYS! HELP ME, HELP YOU! HELP ME, HELP YOU! Ok now I'm so bored I'm yelling at everyone. Dude I'm getting too apothetic (sp?) to RANT. WTF is up with that? God knows I hate everything but now I'm too the point of "/yawn, why bother bitching about it? I'm tired...".

At least I know now why meth was created. It was to get us over that spring/summer lull. I'm going to go eat a box of vivarin. Someone POST SOME SUGGESTIONS for me or I'll show up all cracked out where you live and demand all your Dr. Pepper and porn mags.
