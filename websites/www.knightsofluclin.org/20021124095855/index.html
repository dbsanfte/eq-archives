KoL and allies take on Plane of Hate
On Monday, October 07, 2002 - 07:01 PM EST Couzan wrote ...
From Jazrael: Nice job today guys.

Just wanted to thank everyone that joined today. Other then a few little bumps in the beginning (now I know where the port stones are bought). We shouldnt have that delay again. Course, I am sure most of us all said the same thing when the first 3 mobs aggro'd and all three were Spite Golems. But we handled it well, no one died on the break.

The one serious wipe we had (pictures below) was caused by the giant SK and he brought friends. Every Hate raid I have gone after he was added, has caused problems.. but we got him, Lord of Ire and the Maestro's hand and had a fairly successful day (no hide though.. for those keeping track, I have killed 41 Ashenbone Drakes and no epic piece yet).

Sorry if thing seemed a little slow at times. This was my first time running a Hate raid and the first times Estah and I were pulling Hate.

Once again, nice job everyone.. looking forward to the next one....

KoL vs. Plane of Fear...again
On Tuesday, September 17, 2002 - 12:30 PM EST Couzan wrote ...
Quoted from Jazrael:

All I really wanted to do today was make sure we really could break the zone and that the last time wasnt a fluke. Well, the various Gods were smiling upon us . We managed to clear about 20+ mobs from the front of the zone in about an hour and 15 mins. One Death (Sorry Brill, couldnt get the heal on ya fast enough) and no evacs needed. And we did this with 17 people.

We had a few more people join at that point, and we proceeded to start clearing the south side of the zone. Only one mishap from what I understand (during my brief absence).

We pulled Dread and managed to kill him on the first attempt (got lucky). Pretty sure all the melees died on that attempt, but the casters managed to finish him off.

Then we pulled the Tempest Reaver, lost Adega on the pull, but we managed to kill the mob and the couple mobs that came with the Reaver. Wish we knew what that drop was for.

And then came Fright, and I am still Dizzy. He managed to wipe us out (making it an official Death March). We did manage to get him down to about 1 bubble of health (at least that is what I was told, I was staring at the soulbinder in Ogguk at that point) After regrouping, we added Ticken, changed our tactics a little (nice job Nil and Couzan) and took the punk down.

Great job everyone.
