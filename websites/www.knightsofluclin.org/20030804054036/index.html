Lodizal Down! ( TWICE!)
On Monday, March 03, 2003 - 12:15 PM Houndhammer wrote ...
A big WOOT to the KoL crews today!
We started the day by dropping Lodizal with a combined KoL /Allies team. Packratt was tanking tough, and the turtle dropped in no time flat.
We liked it so much we did it again Sunday night with an all KoL team of 9 people.
Another crew was there to try and take it but the admirable tracking skills and noble sacrifice of Larola prevented any dispute about who engaged first.
Again Packratt took a beating for the team and again the we had Turtle stew. The only death was Larola who only died to pull Lodi away from our competition.
Great work team and gratz to all who got drops!
