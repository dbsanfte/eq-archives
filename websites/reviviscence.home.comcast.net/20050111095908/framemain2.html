    How does one summarize the fortitude and perseverance of so many people? By bashing SOE of course! This encounter is just ridiculous. It’s simple and mechanical. You have to do 3 things to win, well 4 actually, that’s it. None of these things require very much skill or organization in of themselves. What do you need to beat the Rathe? What is our UBER secret strategy?

    1. An extended focus item for your brain!
    2. Reading comprehension skills that don’t diminish too badly over time
    3. A unidirectional sense so that when you get shadow stepped you can find the raid again
    4. A bladder the size of Texas and enough drinks and food to come around for a few dreadful hours.

    Do something, do it over and over and over again, don’t mess up. Do it to 12 mobs, kill them all and have enough DPS left for the Avatar. Those with ADD (Attention Deficiency Disorder) need not apply (they suddenly recruit with other guilds). This is an encounter where you are literally your own worst enemy.

    I can only heap praise on all the participants on sticking to this and not burning out. The relief in surviving Murphy’s Law and beating this was palpable. We will take a break from this mob and do some brainless stuff for a few days and head over to the other stupidity in water. Of course, we will return to the dreaded encounter of mind numbing to ensure that EVERYONE gets flagged. We don’t leave our comrades behind.

    At this point, progression is just a matter of getting enough tries on the boss of water. Sadly, Bobby Bouche, The Water Boy™, is a blowable spawn with a seemingly random timer. This mob remains highly contested as a result.
