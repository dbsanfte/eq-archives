Shadow Knight spells are based off the Necromancer spell line. Like the Paladin and Ranger, the Shadow Knight doesn't get access to spells until 9th level. Because of the low amount of mana, and the significant disparity between 9th level enemies and 1st level spells, the Shadow Knights don't really become effective with spells until rank three or four, particularly when it comes to pets.
