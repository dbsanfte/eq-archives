Rules which apply to all members:

• At all times members must show the guild tag above their head.

• All members must avoid actions which will bring the guildname into disrepute.

• All members must try within reason to assist one another wherever possible.

• The guild is a friendly environment, try to say hi and bye whenever someone arrives or leaves and congratulations where due are always appreciated.

• Arguments should remain on the e board, if not entirely avoided altogether.

• Politeness to other guildmembers is a requirement of membership.

• Support the guild in its Ventures

• Pass on equipment wherever possible into the lower ranks of the guild.

• If possessing guild equipment, when it is no longer required return to the guildmaster or an Officer for redistribution.

•Higher members please try to attend lower level outings to assist the lowers if you can please.

• All members of 29th level and above please spend one hour per week of your choice or can be guild allocated to Power level a lower member.

• Support your fellow guildmembers, try to maintain a positive attitude, no one organising things or coming to help out is getting paid anything for the privelige.
