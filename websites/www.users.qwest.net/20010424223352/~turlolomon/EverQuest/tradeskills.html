Trade Skills

 What is my goal? To become the master of every trade skill and have the faction to walk any place in any city. The Master Merchant. It's a loft goal, but it definitely keeps me interested. I have summarized the trade books I have come across, reworked the recipes to make them a bit more understandable, and posted my findings here. More understandable you say? Well, if you take a careful look, a lot of the trade books do not use the proper names for materials. They also fail to mention what to do with these materials. This isn't so important for blacksmithing, but pottery and baking have different ways to combine materials, and I want to make sure the recipes are crystal clear. Take a look at my notes below and let me know what you think.
