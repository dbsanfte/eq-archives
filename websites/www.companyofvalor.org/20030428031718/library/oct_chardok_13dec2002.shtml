Chardok Royals Raid for Octaviouss's last Epic Piece

Date: December 13, 2002

Pictures

For a Friday the 13th this one was pretty lucky! With the help of many CoV folks (around 15 or so) and our friends and allies we were able to successfully take down the King and Queen of Chardok and Octaviouss was able to complete her warrior epic!

Obviously I've gotten a little rusty and did not take very good notes, logs, or really that good of pictures. I was sorta more concerned with killing mobs and keeping folks alive and so I missed taking pics of actual fights. Some other folks have said the took some pics so hopefully this gallery will get a little better.
