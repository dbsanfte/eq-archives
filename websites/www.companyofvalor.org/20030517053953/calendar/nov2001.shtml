November 28th, 9pm Central: Get your Sebillis Key
Modim is putting together a Sebillis Key (technically it's called a Trakanon Idol) acquisition hunt. This involves killing a Froglok Hunter and Froglok Forager in Trakanon's Teeth and then handing both medallions that they drop.

  • Leader: Modim
  • Level Range: 45+
  • Sign-Up Thread and Info

Some additional information:

In return for the Medallions of Kunzar and Nathsar, Emperor Ganak will award you the Idol of Trakanon, which is the key needed to enter Sebilis.

The medallions of Kunzar and Nathsar drop from the Froglock Hunter and Forager in Trakanon's Teeth. These Mobs spawn one after another, so they are fairly easy to get.

The medallions are no-drop and lore, so every memeber of the group will need to loot both the hunter and the forager.

The idol is now soul bound and will stay with you even after you die.
