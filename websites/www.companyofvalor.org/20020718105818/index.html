Wednesday, June 12, 2002 3:54:08 PM
Hiyas :) I've finally made a page for Kenlaien and Darlea's Wedding. In the process of doing this I discovered something that has caused me to disable the Image Uploader while I fix something with it. Has to do with filenames that have spaces in them and the differences between Windows, UNIX, and forms in how they handle them. In some places it substitutes "%20" in place of a "space" which confuses things at times. I've noticed EZBoard doing this and sometimes it thinks it can't find the image. "%20" is the Hexidecimal value of the "space" character. Yes, I know, this is as clear as mud to many of you, but I know what I'm doing... usually.. hehe. What I will probably do is have it convert any spaces in the filename to underscores before it saves it on my end. This will also make it easier when posting them to EZboard. *HUGS* - Mysstie :)
