October 1st, 8th, 15th, 22nd, 29th: 7pm Central: Kael Raid
This will be a weekly event held every Monday night. The Event is lead by Darianne of Rel in Por (I think that's what I remember next to her name LOL) in conjunction with FoP and has extended an invitation to any in CoV who wish to attend. They will enter Kael from the Wakening Lands side.

I couldn't really find a sign-up thread or anything like that, so just check with those of us, or Darianne, if there is room while in game that night.

Warning, do not come here expecting massive XP because you won't get it since all the groups will be taking out the same mobs. Also, drops are fairly slow. So, bring your patience with you. Just a time to get together, smack around some giants, adjust factions, get some good loot at times, and a smatter of XP. :)

October 4th, 11th, 16th, 25th, 30th, 7 or 8pm Central: CoV 50ish Group Hunt
This is currently a tentative schedule. Basically, Jinnikinic brought up this idea. Following is a copy of his post:
Hello everyone,

I am looking for 5 to 11 people to form a regular group or two to hunt together for exp.

Way way back before I was even in COV, I used to get together once a week with a regular group of adventurers and we would hunt together that night. Each week a different person would pick where we would go the next week and then everyone would get to the designated meeting place the night before and then the next night away we would go. All of those people eventually left the realm of Quellious and of course I became involved with COV, but those weekly adventures were some of the best times I have had in Norrath.

I would like to start doing this on thursdays at 7 or 8 central depending on what the rest of you want. There are many many places we can go with the strength of one group but even more if we can get 2. We can even do this twice a week if enough are interested. Its tough these days to find a group and this way at least 1 or 2 nights a week we wont have to.

...

If you are interested in joining me for a regular group please respond here.

Jinnikinic Leornian
54th level Wizard
Greater Scholar
Company of Valor
