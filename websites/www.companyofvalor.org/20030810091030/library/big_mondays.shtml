Big Game Mondays

Date: March 10, 17 2003

Pictures | Other's Comments

Well, hopefully this was the first in a series of Big Game Mondays or whatever day they may ultimately turn out to be LOL. When it was initially decided that we'd go to Umbral Plains, at least one of the named mobs was up (the name escapes me now, maybe someone can make a comment); however, by the time we got a fair way into this cavern the mob was nowhere on track. While we didn't make our objective we all had alot of fun and many of us got to experience a new zone.

I decided to go ahead and post the pics from the 2nd Big Monday event. I am playing with how I want to go about presenting these. I am wanting to keep all the pics and info from these Big Monday things in one place.. but, I want to list the most recent event's pictures first while keeping the pictures from that event in chronological order.
