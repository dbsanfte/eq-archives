April 1st, 4pm CST: Wedding of Juror and Yahriel
Juror (aka Tonsure of CoV) is marrying the lovely Yahriel at 4pm at Erud's Crossing or Wisp Island. (take the boat from Qeynos docks, first stop). :) Forum link at Healers United's Message Board.

April 6th, 7pm CST: Wedding of Bulgath Wintersbane and Darlea Gentletouch
This is a multi-guild event with the marriage of recently joined Bulgath of CoV and Darlea of HU. :) It will be held in front of Castle Felwithe in Greater Faydark. Forum Link on Healer's United message board.

April 10th, 9pm Central: Guild Meeting
This will be held in a Everquest Chatroom called Company of Valor. It will have a password. The password will be emailed to those registered on our message board using the email address you registered on it with. If you are not registered on the message board (*taps her foot*), send me an email requesting it and be sure to include your character's name in the email.

April 11th, 6pm Central approx: Lord Nagafen Raid
More information on our message board. Please sign up if interested! Hopefully we'll be able to take him down. Wargurine is organizing the raid with the help of Drealeon, a friend of his. As Drealeon is an experienced puller there, he will probably be the raid leader. Zone information and map. Info Sheet from EQMaps.

April 12th, 13th, and 14th: Plane of Hate Raid
Fellowship of the Pheonix is organizing a raid and have welcome us along. Please see their message board for information and to say if you are interested. Zone and Map Information

April 17th, 9pm Central: Officer's Meeting
Please see the message board for more information.

April 19th and 26th, 7pm Central: CoV College of Linguistic Studies
Classes will begin at 7pm Sharp in the High-Elf City of Felwithe. Please be at your nearest druid or wizard portal at 6:30pm. These will be regularly scheduled classes each Thursday, until I hear otherwise.

April 20th, 8pm Central: Company of Valor's 1 Year Annivesary
Along with alot of celebration, there will also be a Tournament along the same lines as our previous one and some language classes I am sure. Also tonight at 9pm central, Atanall and Tracey Sweetblade will be getting married. This will be the first female/female wedding I have ever been apart of. Should be entertaining! Oh yes, the celebration will be held in the arena of course!

April 24th, 8pm Central: Nurga / Droga Dungeon Crawl Tue. April 24, 8pm cst
Please see the message board for more information and to sign up. Mines of Nurga Zone information and map, Temple of Druga information and map at EQAtlas.
