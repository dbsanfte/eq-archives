2 Year Anniversary Party

Date: April 20, 2002

Pictures

Woohoo what a party :). Thank you everyone that came out. We had a really great turnout and many folks got to meet others for the first time. I know at one time we had 27 members in the arena but that doesn't count those that came and went during the night so the overall turnout was even better. :) I took alot of pictures but I managed to miss a number of people though.... *sighs*. If anyone else has some pics, please send them in!

This also happened to be Tracey and Atanall's 1 year wedding anniversary!

There is alot more I wanted to say but haven't decided if it's something to say here or over on the message board. I will say this much.. during these 2 years I have really enjoyed being your Guild Leader and I think CoV rocks! (yeah, ok so I am biased LOL). But really, it's all of you that have made CoV what is has grown into and become, a great family to be a part of. I really feel lucky and honored to have so many great people in the guild. It's all of you, members and officers alike that directly affect my EQ experience and I have to say that overall it has been an enjoyable one :). *HUGS* - Mysstie
