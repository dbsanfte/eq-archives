Online Chat

Warning to Netscape Users: there is a known issue with this chat thing that if you resize the window, it disconnects you and you have to reconnect. Here is what they say: "Netscape shuts off its Java virtual machine on resizing, so the user will be logged out if they resize in Netscape."

To whisper to someone, highlight their name with the mouse, then type your text where you normally do and then click the whisper button. If someone as a (A) in front of their name, then they are away. To check their away reason, send a whisper to them. :)
