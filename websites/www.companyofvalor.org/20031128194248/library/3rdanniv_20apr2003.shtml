3rd Anniversary Party

Date: April 26, 2003

Pictures | Other's Comments

Well, Happy Birthday CoV!! :)

Hard to believe we've been around for 3 years... well.. not really. :) Lot has happened over the last year.. it's been a roller coaster year for sure; however, we've gotten through all the rocky times and I think emerged better than ever! Thanks to all those who managed to make it out to the party. I think everyone had a great time and if nothing else just wanted to see for themselves that I still existed LOL. For those that don't know and who might read this in the future sometime, I've been real busy lately (lot going on at work, overtime and such), and have not been online that much as a result. :(

It was great to see everyone again, missed yah all! I took alot of pictures during the party and I hope I managed to get a pic of everyone that was there. That was my goal anyway. If anyone wants to add any comments, please do so using the form at the bottom!

*HUGS*
Mysstie :)
