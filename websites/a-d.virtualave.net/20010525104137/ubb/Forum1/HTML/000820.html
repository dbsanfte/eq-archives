Moo there! Some of you probably know me, others don't. I was the original officer in charge of applicants when Altered Destiny first formed. Things have changed since AD first formed. I retired, thus leaving the handling of applicants to Eretria, (who has done a fantastic job so far!) and the volume of applications to AD has increased dramatically. Things have come to a point where we feel a change to the entire process needs to be made to keep AD from growing too large and growing too far apart. Part of this change involves restricting applications based on class, raising our expectations of applicant attendance and attitude, making more guildmembers responsible for keeping applicants informed of activities and helping them get to events, and an overview of current applications given this new set of rules.

The following posts outline some of the changes that will be made, as well as to let current and future applicants know how to proceed.
