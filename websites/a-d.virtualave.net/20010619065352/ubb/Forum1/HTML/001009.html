Who I am: Ashli - lvl 60 human monk. Started out playing pally. Got pally up to 54 and decided to switch to monk. Monk is primary now an very much enjoy playing.

Way back in the day pally was part of Dark Fear. DF combined with another guild became Fear an Disorder. People split off FaD and made Dragon Knights and the rest of us in FaD split up. I went to Order of the Dragon (had RL friend there) and stayed there. In OoD made monk an stuck with it.

Left OoD because: 1)leadership decided to go AWOL for two months literally 2)raids were put together and no one would show up. Felt there was a total lack and disregard for fellow guildies and didn't wish to be a part of that anymore 3)the guild basically was splitting up

Raid experience: newbie to Velious. RoK- know very well except HS. Old Norrath- know very well. Fear- know very well. Hate- played around there a bit. Air- been there a time or two.

Why AD: 1)Wish to be involved with a guild that raids things and performs tasks more appropriate to my characters lvl. AD does this. 2)Want to be in a guild made up of positive people who work well together. AD does this. 3)shall I keep going!? =)

It's not always the size of the dog in the fight but rather the amount of fight in the dog that makes the difference. While AD boast's an impressive roster of quality people they also have a known reputation for being a tactically superior guild. This I like. I am always willing to learn new things and am one to generally keep my ears and eyes open an my mouth shut unless it poses a serious danger to my guild, my friends or myself.
