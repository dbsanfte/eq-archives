Restrictions

As has been explained on this board, there has been a move within AD to bring the guild closer together. Part of this was trying to ensure that people who chose to focus on making AD a better guild would always feel needed and wanted. This is a very hard thing to do with an excess of certain classes. For example, a warrior who shows up to every raid is a wonderful thing for the guild, unless the guild doesn't have enough healers to cover him. In that case, the warrior can end up feeling very vulnerable and feeling like he/she cannot contribute without undue risk of death (thus slowing the raid down waiting on a res/rebuff rather than helping). The officers have taken a look at the classes that exist in AD, the number of people who are considered "core" in that they are present for a vast majority of raids that AD holds, and wish to close down applications for classes we have an exess of until we have enough core members of their supporting classes to warrant opening up invites again.

Applications from the following classes will no longer be considered by Altered Destiny:
Warrior
Paladin
Ranger
Druid
Shaman

Classes that are very near capacity are:
Monk
Necromancer
Magician
Enchanter

For classes who are very near capacity, we are really looking at only adding 1 to 2 more "core" people and then closing down applications.

Open Applicants

I will be taking a look at each applicant we have currently within AD and deciding if their application is still supported, if the lack of activity is such that the application should be denied, or if the applicant should be extended an invite. Our applicants board will be used to help judge who our current applicants are, basically the guild will be looking at anyone who has had feedback within the last 60 days who has not already been invited into the guild.

The list of open applicants I currently have is as follows:

Maelphantous
Bddott
Wyatt
Hoolihan
Wilfred
Quell
Sacara
Avien
Freeze
Makkon
Shidz

I will be contacting each applicant soon to let you know of any decisions that have been made and what the guild needs to see to bring resolution to your application.

AD will move through our current applicants as quicly as possible and get our app process going forward smoothly. The hope is that these changes will, while making AD a harder guild to join, ultimatly make applying and joining a much more personal and pleasant experience for the types of people we are looking for.
