A Busy Week For Altered Destiny - submitted by: meeks @ 15:08 EST 4/26/01
Since I've been away for the past few days taking a much needed mini-vacation, I'll try to briefly fill you in on what we've been up to this week.

On Monday, people were a bit worn out from the big raid on Temple of Veeshan and we took it easy, grinding and not doing much of anything (please, hold your applause). On Tuesday, I'm going to assume, everyone was feeling pretty good. Our own Dragon Force killed Venril Sathir, Talendor, Kelorek'Dar, Trakanon, and Wuoshi. Animals! I don't yet have all the details on drops but, most notably, we got some Mrylokar's Greaves, Eretria scored some Tolan's Darkwood Greaves, and Noruat nabbed a Blood Ember Breastplate. Congratulations to them.

We were pretty busy last night as well. First, a bunch of us swam out to Timorous Deep to help Fenani complete one of the last steps for her Druid epic weapon. Then we assembled as Dragon Force and killed the Dreadlands-inhabiting dragon Gorenaire. We killed him extremely fast, and after the fight I only saw one casualty. I think we've got this one down to a science. ;) I was disappointed to see that, once again, no book dropped for the Paladin epic. I'm really beginning to suspect something is wrong. He dropped a few things however...

- Druid Spell: Entrapping Roots -- Tram
- Shaman Spell: Avatar -- DK Guildfund
- Wizards Spell: Sunstrike -- Tessera
- (x2)Hierophant's Crook -- AD, DK Guildfunds
- Kyvruul's Mystical Pouch (10-slot, 100% weight reduction bag) -- Xpoc

Immediately following the dragon slaying, we sent a small group into Karnor's Castle to kill Venril Sathir's remains. This yielded the final components for two of our rangers to complete their epic weapons. Congratulations to AD rangers Quoits and Blitze on completing the quest for this most impressive blade... Swiftwind

Then remainder of the night consisted of various questing, mostly for epic weapons and Veeshan's Peak keys. Another great night! I think you can definitely expect us to be quite busy this weekend.

Addendum: Congrats to Krowbar on finally completing the quest for the Warlord's Vambraces last night as well. They are simply amazing! AC33 +5str +6sta +25hp +2svMagic +2svPoison
