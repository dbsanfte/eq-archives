I would like to post this as my official application to Altered Destiny. My primary character is Imar, a lvl 58 erudite enchanter. My twinks include Evix, 47 dark elf warrior, and my first character, a 32 half-elf paladin named Diodem who I haven’t played in about a year.

The first guild I joined was with my pally, Defenders of the Faith (now defunct). Even though it was a small guild (in numbers and lvls), I had learned a lot with those guys, especially as it was my first guild experience. My first multi-group raid ever was to Dalnir’s. We cleared through the zone and I had a blast. The sense of accomplishment was great and being able to share that success with friends sold me on the idea of being in a guild. Then we tried to raid Droga and were wiped out and the CR ensued, by about 4am mine was the only corpse left and while there were people who had to call it a night, there were still several members who were there until we got every last corpse back. What would have been a very discouraging evening turned out to be a great bonding experience, and I learned how in times of trouble your guild will still be there to back you up.

Well, I got bored with the pally and started an enchanter which has been my primary ever since. The first guild he joined was Guardians of the Malt and I learned the post 50 game with them. I had a lot of fun with those guys and made some great friends. The reason I left was because of a lack of leadership, which hindered our success despite the great players and friendly attitude of the guild. Imar is currently in Noradvo, which is a guild started by my RL friends, this guild was formed mainly so our group of friends could communicate more easily on-line. They understand that I have been looking to join a larger guild and I would leave them on good terms. On a side note I should mention that there are some Noradvo members that were recruited hastily out of the excitement of a new guild and as a result we have already had to guild remove a player. I hope there are no further incidents and that they restrict their invites, but I wanted to disclose this info in case there were any questions.

All told I have been playing EQ for about 2 years. I live in the Tampa Bay area of Florida and am 25 years of age. I play somewhere around 50 hrs/week. I am requesting the opportunity to get to know more AD members, grind, raid, etc. Let me know if you have any questions. Thanks for your time, see you in game.
