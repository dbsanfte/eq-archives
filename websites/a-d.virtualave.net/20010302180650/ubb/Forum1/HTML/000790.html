I think a good tactic for the future.

Once group is in cubby past reet room, someone pulls jugs along path to ramp up. Monk feigns at top of ramp. Paladin runs up to monk, tosses up DA and runs straight to the back. Magi can either CoH the paladin or paladin can simply make sure they get to the back and just bite the bullet back there.

That'll move trak way outta position in the back for about 25 minutes, and the prot/2jugs in the front will return a minute or so after paladin dies. Monk simply aggros prot or jugs there and brings 'em back. Once those three go down, you have plenty of time to buff up and get ready and go take trak.

When we tried the first time with demott I think he may have put his DA up way too early and not gotten far enough back into the lair to really lure trak back enough. That's why I suggest having a monk as the line where the paladin tosses up DA to insure he's got enough time to get to the back of the lair.

An addition to this tactic could be that the paladin can actually wait outside the seb bubble until groups get in position and are ready for prot/2jugs. Then he gets a sow outside the bubble, zones in, and gets CoH'ed down to groups. That way when he's running trak back, he'll be sowed and can get trak back faster.
