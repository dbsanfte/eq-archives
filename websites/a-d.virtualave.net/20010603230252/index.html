Saturday, June 02, 2001

Velketor the Sorcerer Slain... Twice! - submitted by: meeks @ 12:36 EST 6/02/01

My apologies for the recent lack of updates. While I’m being held at gunpoint, I figure I’ll do a little update to let everyone know what we’ve been up to. We’ve been extremely busy lately with things such as completing the Kael Drakkal armor quests (got a LOT of these done), grinding (quite a few AD members hit the big 60), and exploring new areas. Here’s the low-down…

The big news, of course, is that one of Dragon Force’s newest targets, Velketor the Sorcerer, the mystical kingpin of the aptly named Velketor’s Labyrinth, was slain for the first time by Dragon Force this week. Velketor, who boasts an admirable 100,000+ hit points and quad melee attacks for up to 850 HP per hit, put up an incredible fight. After spending some weeks discussing and practicing some new tactics, we finally laid the mighty sorcerer to rest. Velketor kindly dropped a Silver Sword of Yalrek, 13/24 +15vsFire for Fulgore, the Vermillion Robe of Torrefaction, AC15 +10Int +40HP +75Mana +25vsFire, Effect of Conflagration, for Hawkwinde, as well as several high level spells. A big congrats to DF on this historical event!

The next night (last night), all was quiet on the Dragon Force chat channels when a call to arms was sounded by our fearsome leader Corona. We were quickly dispatched to Velketor’s Labyrinth, once again, where we killed Lord Doljonijiarnimor (the man who has *never* received a /tell). He dropped an Icicle Tunic, AC25 +5Str +5Dex +5Agi +80HP +3vsCold +3vsFire +3vsDisease +3vsPoison +3vsMagic, which went to AD monk Xiaohai, and a Glowing Red Stone, AC10 +6Wis +6Int +40HP, awarded to AD cleric Aruthra.

Following the victory, we learned that Velketor the Sorcerer had returned and was angered by the previous night’s defeat. Needless to say we felt that it was our duty to pay old Velky another visit and reiterate our point. The message was delivered with unmistakable clarity. Dragon Force pounded Velketor into the ground a second time, this time suffering only 3 casualties! Congratulations to Dragon Force on yet another epic triumph!

The loot? Boots of Bladecalling, AC10 +9Sta +12Int +55Mana +10vsMagic, Effect of Shock of Swords, Magician Only went to DK mage Ronathon. Vermillion Orb of Torrefaction, +35Mana +4Int, Effect of Inferno Shock was awarded to DK wizard Tessera. Bracelet of Frostbite, +9vsCold +30HP (Don’t ask me) went out to AD applicant Loosifer! High level spells accompanied the aforementioned items as well.

In other news… recently, a couple AD groups journeyed down into the dark tunnels beneath the cold soil of Dragon Necropolis in search of gems and other treasure. We spotted the fabled rat boss Vaniki down one wing and took a shot at him. After a battle, which lasted much longer than any of us could have expected, we felled the rat Vaniki. He dropped two Flawless Diamonds and a Willsapper, 13/20 AC5, Effect of Energy Sap (35% slow proc)! Our valiant warrior, Elldog, now wields this most impressive blade. Congrats to Elldog and the rest of the DN raid party!

The rest of our time has been spent collecting gems for the Kael Drakkal armor quests and on some intense grinding. We now have lots of members who are equipped in some of the finest armor to be found in Norrath. Several new members have also attained the heralded goal of level 60!!! Congratulations to Beleni, Caliburnus, Glayde, Gylen, Lilli, and Quoits! Way to go guys! (Sorry if I forgot anyone.. I’ll wait for the incoming hatemail spam to find out who I missed ;) I believe this brings the guild’s average level up to 59 (or close to it). Cheers!

Have a great weekend. See you in Norrath!
