Greetings all

Over the years *literally * I have come to know several members of AD and have hunted both in cooperative raids and as members of groups. I'll give you a little background information on myself.
I began playing EQ about June 01 1999 as Dundanoor, he is my main and usually the only character I play. As I went a long in EQ I noticed dwarves were seriously under represented in terms of guilds and I decided I would create Clan Stormhammer. I ran Clan Stormhammer for around 5 months before deciding I wanted to do something more with the people there and handed the guild over to my second in command at the time. I formed Ancient Souls a few days later and that's probably a much more recognizable name. I was guild leader for AS for about a year before I handed the guild over to Eelyen. Who did a very good job I might add. AS took on some internal matters and for their own reasons decided to disband the guild a few months later.

As far as leading raid experiences and other forms of experience. I've led raids in Hate and Fear, LGuk*when it was the only high lvl zone *, Kedge Keep, Mistmoore *for lower lvl members*, Crushbone *same reason* and more. I have a lot of experience dealing with other guilds and the general running and upkeep of guilds. I always make time to sit and listen though to people who know more about a topic than I do and I like to hear what people have to say.

So I find myself looking to see where my friends are and people I like being with. Altered Destiny is that group of people. I feel I would make a good addition to the AD team and would welcome the friendship of those I don't know yet and members who are already friends. I hope to see you all in combat as a friend soon.
