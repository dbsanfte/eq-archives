Nods, Diggins and I had a chat about resist equipment last night.

The key to getting 115 MR is not blue diamond jewelry. Blue diamond jewelry is more helpful for keeping your resist gear down under 4 backpacks. ;-) The key to 115 MR is Plat Jacinth or Velium Jacinth rings and Wolf's Eye Agate bracers. Jacinth rings are either +13 or +14 MR (Plat Jac is cheaper than Vel Jacinth) and Wolf's Eye Agate bracers are +7 MR each. That's minimum +40 MR just on your rings and wrists. In order to make up for the hit to elemental resists by not wearing rings or bracers, look at a Froglok King Crown, White Gold Necklace, Cloak of the Icebear, Drakehide Belt, Charred Boots, Gold Diamond Mask, Gnomish Environment Suit or Emp Crush's Tunic, etc, etc. Your AC will tank, but these items are fairly cheap and +5 here, +10 there to your elemental resist go a long way towards getting you there. Also, don't underestimate the impact of shields for you pallies/sks. Sarnak Battle Shields drop anywhere in Chardok and are 25 Ac, +10 fire, cold, magic. Also for you non-paladins, get a Runed Bone Fork.

To me it's far easier to concentrate on MR in the ring, wrist, range for non-pallies/shield for pallies, shoulder, ear slots and then filling CR, MR, and PR with all other slots.

It takes time but it's doable for not too much money. About your most expensive purchase will be the Jacinth rings and the diamond mask.
