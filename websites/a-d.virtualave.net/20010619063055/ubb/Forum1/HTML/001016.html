Hello all my friends! I have finally managed to find my way to your message board.

Many of you knew me back in my old days of EverQuest when I was in the Brotherhood of Steel just before the merging which formed AD and I recently returned as Gloog the shadow knight. The welcome I received was heartwarming. You guys (and girls!) are just great. I love you one and all.

So much has changed in EverQuest since I was last here! There is much new to do and see. I'm very excited about all the new zones, new craft items, new skills, and new equipment which I have hardly even begun to discover. There's nothing like venturing into a new place when you don't know what's around the corner. The danger! The excitement! And there's nothing like the thrill in the discovery of new treasures which you can not only find, but also have some use for.

When I am on guild chat with all of you high level players and I hear about dragons slayed and fantastic rewards reaped, I want to be there. It drives me to want to level fast and hard to get to high level and join in. It leads me to accept your gifts of amazing equipment to power myself along. All the people would like me to be fighting at their sides once again in the glory of battle, so they help push me along by aiding me through giving me equipment or spells.

People were very generous and gave me some fantastic equipment which made my character Gloog quite the low-level powerhouse. I inflicted much pain on the monsters leaving a trail of corpses from The Feerrott to Lake of Ill Omen and Warslilks Woods. I made my way to level 26 in less than 2 weeks. There is definitely no discounting the power of the twink in this game. However, I found myself missing what I had come back to enjoy.

Gaining levels is not the joy for me of this game. The joy comes from discovery and exploration. It comes from meeting people and sharing fantastic adventures with them. It comes from playing this game as it was meant to be played and savoring every new discovery along the way. I realize this now, so I am making a change in my direction in EverQuest.

As much as I love you all, I can't remain in this guild. I can't constantly listen to you talking about epic quests and godly creatures without wanting to be there and doing things that will limit my enjoyment of the game to get there. You guys can't help but to want to help me out. Zretch still has my old character waiting there for me and more than one person has offered to lend or give to me other powerful characters to be right back there in the thick of things, but to jump back in at the top would eliminate or cheapen much of the lower and mid level excitement that now exists with so many new unexplored areas in Kunark and Vellious, limiting my long term enjoyment of the game.

I've come to the realization that in order to enjoy this game fully I have to start at the bottom. I have to take my time enjoying and savoring every moment of it. Maybe this sounds crazy, but I think at the point where you have not discovered much you need to be weak to enjoy the game! When you are level 10 and you are using a rusty 2H sword and some patchwork armor, then every little trinket you find is a golden nugget. When the people you group with are not twinked to the moon you can feel true danger and great accomplishment when you overcome it. As such, I am putting Gloog back in the box and I have started some new pure untwinked characters.

Many of you gave Gloog some very nice equipment to help me out. I appreciate all your help, but I'm afraid your equipment wont get the use that maybe you were hoping it would. I don't want anyone to think that I am trying to take advantage of them just to get some equipment which I did not plan to use or feel disgruntled that I have betrayed their kindness by not making good use of the equipment they gave me, so I have tried to contact (and continue to try to contact) people who have given me things to return them if desired. Gloog is camped in South Ro near the druid circle and wizard pyramid for easy portal access should anyone want or need any of the equipment they gave him back.

If anyone wants to chat, wants their equipment back, or needs to reach me for any reason, my new characters are a halfling druid named Teemu and an iksar monk named Ssarth, possibly with more to come. Please don't offer to power level me or give me equipment as I will have to decline. My e-mail address is matt911@home.com if anyone wants to reach me and can't seem to figure out where I've gotten off to.

It has been an honor and a priveledge to be involved with all of you in this fine guild. I will miss talking to and seeing all of you on guild chat, but I hope one day in the future I will again be at the right place in this game to join up with you once again to conquer the wilds. Thank you all, my friends.

Teemu
