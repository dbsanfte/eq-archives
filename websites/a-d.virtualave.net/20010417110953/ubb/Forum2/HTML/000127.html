Last I heard xpoc, balzing wand is no drop off of undertow. Does not mean you cant multiquest it

As far as the camps in BW go, Slixin is a joke. Take another mage/necro to help you and you can kill this guy over and over again until he drops the dirt.

The burning ember piece is a bit more of a problem, but can be done with patience. This is one step I havent finished yet either. Anyway, this is supposedly the system to get the embers to drop. I have heard a lot of people say this method really works. Here it is:

Hiya,
I know tons of people have been camping for nek for weeks now having no luck getting burning embers. While I myself camped nek for 2 weeks and just yesterday I figured out the secret to get nek and that bard wurm to spawn. I hope this helps a tons of mages finish off their epics and I hope most of you read this before this gets nerfed. Here is what you do:

1) Get a full group up and head to Burning Woods. You can bring less than a full group but it is safer with a group and you can't do it solo.

2) Head to this loc in BW: NEG 463.94 , NEG 2549.86 -- That is Nek's spawn point.

3) If nek or the named bard wurm is up pull him to that spot and kill him there. If not pull any mob in the area to that spot and kill them there.

4) Once you get the correct mob killed every 1-2 mins a mob will spawn at that loc. The PH can be anything ...wurms, bee, skelly, etc. Once they start spawning you can stop pulling the trash mobs. Just defend the spot when the trash roams to you. Nek is a random pop and only spawns at that loc. Keep killing the PH'ers until nek pops. The named bard wurm will also spawn there. The bard wurm will summon but nek won't. That and the roaming trash is why you need to bring a group.

I have seen nek pop 3 times in a row and not pop for over 2 hours... it is random. Now as for the nek loot this is what it is:

Common - Nothing
Uncommon - Burning Embers
Rare- Shoulder piece 20 sv fire, 10 sv magic, 8ac, 0.0 weight all/all

Sorry forgot the name of the shoulder piece...wurmscale cloak I think. Tell your friends about the shoulder piece it is great resist gear for Faydedar/Tal/Naggy/Ragefire. It will help them want to get your embers and camp Nek. I normally got 2 burning embers every 4 hours. I have seen 15 neks in one night so I know this works.

I hope this helps you guys who are stuck trying to find nek and get your embers.
