Early friday morning at 12:15am EST, DA/AL/EL/AoD/AS and friends mounted in force for their third attempt at Trakanon. A staggering 80 to 100 players showed for the event! Trakanon battle lasted roughly 3 mins in which 63 lives were lost during the battle. Shortly after, the rest of the troops fell to Protector whom spawned only a moment after Trakanon Fell.

Congrats DA/Al/El/AoD/AS for taking down the beast!

Drops were:
Blood Ember Breastplate
Cobalt Breastplate
2 Trakanon Teeth

Cheers to the winners!
