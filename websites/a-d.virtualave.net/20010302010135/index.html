Dragon Force Takes Down Talendor! - submitted by: meeks @ 11:51 EST 2/20/01
On Sunday we gathered with our allies, Dragon
Knights, to slay one of our most despised foes
ever, Talendor. As many of you know, we've taken a couple shots at this guy in
the past, and lost... barely. Well Sunday it was all Dragon Force. Buffed to the
teeth in the Skyfire Mountains, we came at the mighty Talendor in three waves.
Team one, consisting of mostly melee power, hit with so much ferocity that
by the time Team two came in, Talendor was down to 30% health. Team three merely
got to watch the action, and was lucky to have gotten one spell off as the total
time it took to kill the fire-breathing menace did not exceed one minute!
Talendor fell with an earth-shattering thud in record time. Congratulations to
the participants on a huge victory!
