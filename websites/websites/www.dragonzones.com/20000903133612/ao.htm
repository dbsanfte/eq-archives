Anarchy Online 
Anarchy Online is a massive multiplayer role-playing game for the Win95/98 environment, set nearly 30 000 years into the future.

It is the year 29,475 A.D.

Humanity has populated many of the worlds of the Milky Way. You are an inhabitant of a tiny planet in the farthest reaches of the galaxy, - the mining outpost of Rubi-Ka.

The planet is ravaged by the ever-present conflict between the governing corporation Omni-Tek - and the rebel clans fighting the regime.

The player - you - can participate on any level you want in this conflict.

Rise through the departments of Omni-Tek®, set up your own clan, - or try your luck as a freelancer, seeking fame and money on your own..
