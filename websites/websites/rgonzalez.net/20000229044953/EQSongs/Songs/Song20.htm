LARGO's MELODIC BINDING

Level: 20

Effect: Lowers targets' AC and attack speed(-30%)

Skill: Singing

Target: Area Effect

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Attack speed debuff does not stack with Consonant Chain's attack speed debuff and will override Shaman/Enchanter attack speed debuffs.

Taunt: High

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: This  Song reduces attack speed and AC of all creatures in the effect area.

Strategies:

A good substitute for Consonant Chain with you are not the designated snarer in a group.
In PvP this song will make warriors cry.  They will attack so slow it is almost comical, especially if they are using slow 2 handed weapons.
[Kaearia] The AC debuff of this song can make a difference, especially if the other melee party members are lower level than you.  Sing this song and they will hit more often for more damage.
[Kaearia] The attack slowdown can make it much easier for your casters to cast spells without being interrupted.  I usually start off playing this song in multi-mob pulls.  The enchanter then mesmerizes the extras one by one, rarely being interrupted.

Cautions:

Due to the high taunt factor be careful when using in a multi mob pull.  The good thing is that it will not break Enchanter mesmerize since it is a straight debuff with no damage component.
[Kaearia] Word is that the attack slowdown of this song will override the slowdown of the shaman and enchanter speed debuffs (which are much better).  I have not tested this, but a couple high level bards swear it's true.  So proceed with caution if your shaman or enchanter wants to cast speed debuffs.
