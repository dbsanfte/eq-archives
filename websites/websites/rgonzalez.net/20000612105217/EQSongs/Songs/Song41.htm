PSALM OF MYSTIC SHIELDING

Level: 41

Effect: Increase magic resistance

Skill: Singing

Target: Party

Location: Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Other songs in the Psalm line and the Rhythm series, except Guardian Rhythms.

Taunt: Low

Quantified: MR: Lvl+10

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: Increases Magic resistance.

Strategies:

Good to weave in against casting NPCs.
The Psalm series of songs is great to use when you know you need a specific resistance against a specific opponent.  As the resistance bonus that the Psalms give is superior to the Guardian series (even with a hand drum) at the expense of only boasting one resistance category.
