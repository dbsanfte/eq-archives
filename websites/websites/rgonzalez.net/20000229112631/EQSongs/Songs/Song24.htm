LYSSA's VERACIOUS CONCORD

Level: 24

Effect: Ultravision See invisible

Skill: Wind

Target: Party

Location: Erudin

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: Nil

Quantified: N/A

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: Allows you to see invisible, as well as give you ultravision (useful for blind humans). The bluish tint this gives is caused by the Ultravision.

Strategies:

Useful for spotting invisible kill stealers that may be lurking about, this is not as much as a problem now due to the group damage rule (ya Verent!).
There are a few rare spawns in the game that spawn invisible, the Guano Collector in Nagafen's Lair and the Froglok Warden in Upper Guk.

Cautions:

Do not play this song while trying to stay invisible yourself, as playing an song will break inivisibility.
