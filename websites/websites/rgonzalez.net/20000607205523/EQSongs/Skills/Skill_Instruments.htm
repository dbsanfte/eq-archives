INSTRUMENT SKILLS

Available at Level: Singing - 1st; Percussion - 5th; String - 8th; Brass - 11th; Wind - 14th

Effect: Directly effects ability to not miss notes when starting a song using that skill, even if you are singing the song.

Maximum skill: 200

Quantified: ( lvl + 1) * 5

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: These skills are important to keep maxed, as your skill in the instrument for a song (ie Brass skill for Denon's Disruptive Discord) will effect your ability to avoid missed notes even if you are singing the song.  Check the Instrument page for the list of known instruments, their skill, and any special effects.

Strategies:

The best way to practice up your skill in an instrument is to leave yourself logged in for several hours playing that instrument.  With the Brass skill this can be dangerous as the only songs that use that skill are AE damage songs till much higher level.  The solution to this is to visit the Arena zone off of Lake Rathe.  This is a large open zone with no NPC's so you can safely play Denon's Discord for 8 hours without worrying about NPC's coming by to criticize your playing.
[Svearike] Another good spot to practice Brass skill is at the bottom of the hill in South Ro near the Oasis zone line.
