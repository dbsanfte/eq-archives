PSALM OF WARMTH

Level: 25

Effect: Increases cold resist and AC, grants a fire based damage shield and Infravision.

Skill: Singing

Target: Party

Location: Erudin, Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Elemental and Purifying Rhythms, Psalm series

Taunt: Low

Quantified: FS: INT((Lvl-1)/5), CR: Lvl+10

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: Increases resistance to cold-based spells, gives a small AC boast, Infravsion, and most importantly will give a party-wide fire shield (starts at 4 damage).

Strategies:

The Psalm series of songs is great to use when you know you need a specific resistance against a specific opponent.  As the resistance bonus that the Psalms give is superior to the Guardian series (even with a drum) at the expense of only boasting one resistance category.
This song really shines in party battles against 2 or more NPCs.  As every time a mob hits any party member they will take damage (even magic immune mobs like EXG clockwork in Solusek's Eye).
The fire shield stacks with with all non-cold shields, making them that much more effective.

Cautions:

The Infravsion component will break the Ultravision granted by the Guise of the Deceiver, re-activating the Guise will restore the Ultravision.
The fire shield component will weaken cold shields, AFAIK the only cold shield is Necromancer and self only.
