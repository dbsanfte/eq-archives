Bard Skill Reference Sheet

To view the detailed analysis of a Skill click on the Skill name.

Skill Name Level Attained Cap/level Max Description

Combat Skills
1H Blunt 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Combat skill used for one handed bashing weapons.
1H Slashing 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Combat skill used for one handed slashing weapons.
Piercing 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Combat skill used for piercing weapons.
2H Blunt 1 0 0 Combat skill used for two handed bashing weapons.
2H Slashing 1 0 0 Combat skill used for two handed slashing weapons.
Hand to Hand 1 (lvl+1)*3 100 Combat skill used when no weapons are equipped.
Throwing 1 (lvl+1)*5? 200? Combat skill used for throwing weapons.
Dodge 10 (lvl+1)*5 120 Allows you to dodge successful attacks.
Dual Wield 17 (lvl+1)*5 200 Allow you to wield two one handed weapons.
Instill Doubt 26 N/A 100 Also known as Intimidation, causes target to flee in fear if successful
Instrument skills are checked when you attempt to start a song that uses that skill even if you are singing.
A higher skill in an instrument will lessen missed notes on songs that use that skill.
Singing 1 (lvl+1)*5 200  
Percussion 5 (lvl+1)*5 200  
String 8 (lvl+1)*5 200  
Brass 11 (lvl+1)*5 200  
Wind 14 (lvl+1)*5 200  
Trade skills have no per level limit, given enough platinum you can max these skills at any level.
Not all skills can reach 200 yet.
Baking 1 N/A 200 Used for making food, player made food is very light weight (wgt 0.1).
Brewing 1 N/A 200 Used for making alcoholic beverages.
Fletching 1 N/A 200 Used for making bows and arrows, sadly Bards can not use them.
Jewel Craft 1 N/A 200 Used for making jewelry. Enchanters can enchant the metal giving the jewelry magic bonuses.
Pottery 1 N/A 200 Used for making items other trade skills use (pots, pie tins, ceramic linings, vials)
Smithing 1 N/A 200 Used for making armor, weapons, and items other trade skills need (pots, studs, boning, pie tins, etc..)
Tailoring 1 N/A 200 Used for making armor (patchwork, raw silk, studded/reinforced leather, cured silk), weapons (whips, Wu's fighting gauntlets), and backpacks.
Miscellaneous skills
Alcohol Tolerance 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Allow you to avoid the negative effects of Alcohol (-int, -agl, blurred vision), while getting the good effects (+str, +sta)
Beg 1 ? ? Affects your success in begging from NPCs. Warning: Some NPCs hate beggars even more then players due.
Bind Wound 1 (lvl+1)*5 100 Speeds HP recovery when under 50% HP.
HP regained = Int(skill/4)
Fishing 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Nice way to pass the time in the Ruins of Old Guk or Nagafen's Lair.
Sense Heading 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Allow you to sense what direction you are headed, helps to avoid getting lost.
Swimming 1 (lvl+1)*5 200 Affects your movement rate in water and breath holding ability.
Forage* 12 N/A 40 Very helpful for getting food. Also all foraged foods (expect Roots) can be used by Bakers and Brewers.
Sneak 17 N/A 75 Allows you to move silently,  if out of sight of NPCs they will not attack you.
Safe Fall 24 N/A 40 Allows you to lessen/avoid damage from falling
Hide* 25 N/A 40 Allows you to hide to avoid being attacked or seen. You cannot move while hidden, or hide while moving.
Disarm Traps 30 N/A 100 Allows you to disarm traps with a simple right click of the mouse.
Track 35 N/A 100 Allows you to track PC/NPCs that are nearby. Great for finding those rare spawns.
Pick Locks 40 N/A 100 Allows you to use lock picks to pick locks.

*Wood Elves get Forage and Hide as racial abilities at skill 50.
