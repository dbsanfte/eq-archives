GUARDIAN RHYTHMS

Level: 17

Effect: Increases magic resist and AC

Skill: Percussion

Target: Party

Location: Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Chant of Battle, Psalm series, except Psalm of Mystic Shielding.

Taunt: Low

Quantified: Singing: MR: Int(lvl/2)+5. Drum: MR: lvl+6

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Comments: This is good for fighting Evil Eyes and other spell casting monsters. Many Everquest references describe this song has having a 'spell shield' which is a confusing way of saying it pumps up magic resistance.

Strategies:

A good PvP tactic, when facing a spell caster, is to weave this with either Elemental or Purifying Rhythms and melee or bellow till they are out of mana, then pick them off with ease.
Since this song will stack with either of the other Rhythms it can allow you to boost MR very high when using a drum. Allowing you and your party to fully resist many spells even from red conn'd casters.
It will also stack with Endure/Resist Magic line of spells many casters have, for insanely high MR levels.

Cautions:

[Kaearia] Do not use this song on its own.  Only use it in conjunction with another Rhythm song.  Guardian Rhythms only raises your magic resist, while Elemental and Purifying Rhythms will raise three resists (including magic) by the same amount.  The only advantage Guardian Rhythms has is a slightly better AC buff, and its stackability with other songs.
[Kaearia] As you gain levels and the resists from this song get better, several resist-increasing spells will not take hold until you stop singing this song.  Save your resist buffer some mana and make them aware of this before they start casting.
