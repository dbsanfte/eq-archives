MELANIE's MELLIFLUOUS MOTION

Level: 21

Effect: Short distance random teleport

Skill: Wind

Target: Party

Location: High Hold

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: ?

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server.

Analysis: Every 6 seconds that this song is played everyone in your party will be randomly teleported a short distance. The teleport is restricted by line of sight so no teleporting through walls or locked doors. It will automatically correct your Z-axis so do not worry about falling to your death teleporting from a high place to a low place. Using a wind instrument will increase the maximum range, so outdoors you can go quite far, or 2 feet away.

Strategies:

This used to be useful if you got dragged up a portcullis (Paw and Permafrost), but they changed it so it doesn't work through walls anymore. Once you're dragged up, you have walls on both sides so you won't teleport anywhere. Still, it's worth a shot, especially if you're not completely pulled up inside the portcullis. Make sure you disband from the rest of your party so they are not flung around.

In a situation where the entire party is trying to flee a monster or group of monsters outdoors, theoretically, this song can help, but it is very risky, as the entire party will be separated and will be on their own to make it to the zone border. Use with caution.

In PvP this song can be useful against classes with Flash of Light/Blindness type spells.  As you will be helpless while blinded, and this song will disorient your opponent to give you a chance to recover.

This makes it useful for getting down off the top of the Aviak Tree House in South Karana, but will also mean that if you teleport into water you will be at the bottom of the lake/ocean.

[Radia] This song is nice to use During GM Events. When the GM decides it is your turn to die, this song may be the only thing that can save you.

Cautions:

In a dungeon, this song can get you in a whole lot of trouble due to it random nature.
Do not use around lava, since according to Murphy's Law you will end up in the deepest lava pit in the zone.
