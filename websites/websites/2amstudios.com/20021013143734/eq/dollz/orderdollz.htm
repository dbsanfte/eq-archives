What is an EQDollz?

EQDollz is the name of the "doll" like creations I make for players of Everquest. When a doll type image is not preferred, but they would like pic or banner, I create that instead and display it here. My screenshot images can be seen here.

Hmm, how much will it cost?

Pricing varies depending on what you would like.
Single Character Dollz are $32 and have a transparent background such as Nimphe's

Add-on options available:
Animations: such as winking, glitter, sparkles, kissing, and spellcasting. $15
Additional characters: if you'd like a significant other or 2nd character. $25
Pets: (bear, wolf, skeleton, elemental) $10 (horses $15)
Game Backgrounds: If you'd like to have a game background that either you or I provide. The total image will be smaller when complete than it looks in game $15
EQDollz Sig Banner, Unanimated: If you would like to have your EQDollz placed in a sig banner with your guild or player information / title. I'd be happy to choose a background for you if you don't have one in mind. $15
EQDollz Sig Banner, Animated: If you would like your signature banner to be animated. Have something specific in mind (humor, theme, style)? Please mention it on the order form. $15

Brierlea wanted a pic of her ranger to go under her name in messageboards. She also wanted it animated, but wasn't sure what would be best. We emailed back and forth, and decided to illustrate her "title" which is "Raynjur Girl and DT Sacrifice".

End product:

This involved:

What You Must Do

I will need for you to take some screenshots of your character, crop and convert to .jpg format, and be ready to email them to me. Please do not compress the image so much that the details are blurred and cannot be seen.

Take the screenshots in ample lighting and full screen mode. Please make sure it's close up but can still view all of the body. Ideally, I would prefer one screenshot each of the front, back and right side of your character so that I can provide the details of your armor and weapons on your dollz.
