Wedding in Hate
posted 10/03/00

This past Sunday the denizens of Hate Plane were evicted in preparation for the nuptials of Chesse Defenestratrix and Bearjangles Shardic, of the Muppets From Hell guild, (Emef'Aitch). Clerics of Innoruuk invited to perform the ceremony declined, stating they'd rather die than perform such unholy matrimony. Muppets were only too happy to oblige. Chesse is taking Bearjangles' surname, much to the relief of reporters and to the sorrow of broken window fixers. Married in RL as well as game now, we give our brightest blessings and worst puns to the happy couple.
