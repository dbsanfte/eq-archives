New Scam
Posted Thursday, September 20, 2001 by Raene


Please be aware there is a new email scam. The person asks you to read an EQ Manual Supplement and provides an attachment. This attachment is not a .doc or a .txt, and will most likely give the sender a backdoor to your EQ passwords and information.

Please delete the file and do NOT open it.
