What I Need from You To Complete Your EQDollz

There are some things you will need to provide me so that I can create your dollz. Information about your character will be collected on the detailed order form.

However, in addition to this information, I use screenshots of your character as a visual reference. I will need for you to take some screenshots of your character in the game (please convert them to .jpg format before emailing them to me, but do not compress them so much as to become blurry).

The screenshots should be taken in sufficient lighting, in full screen mode, and as close up as you can make it and still see all of the character in the shot.

Ideally, I would prefer one screenshot each of the front, back and right side of your character so that I can provide the details of your armor and weapons on your dollz.

You should take these screenshots for each character you want a dollz or pet of, and you should have them ready to email at the time you place your order. You will email them to me at eqdollz@2amstudios.com when you place your order. Don't forget to include your name, and the names of the characters in the email so that I know whose order the screenshots are for.

Background Screenshots

If you are requesting a game background for your dollz or banner, please supply that as well or I will choose a transparent background.

When taking your background screenshot, keep in mind that the total image will be smaller when complete than it looks in game. You may wish to turn off PC and NPC name tags if there are any seen in the background.


Convert the background image to jpg format before sending as they can be very large in size in the EQ directort. Do not send .bmps as they are too large. Try not to compress the image too much when you save to .jpg or I will not enough quality to work with.

Method of Payment

At this time I am only accepting payment electronically. You can make a secure online payment using PayPal

PayPal provides the world's first instant and secure online payment service. If you do not have a paypal account to send in your payment with, it is free to sign up for one. Individuals and businesses can send and receive payments through the Internet. This provides a safer, faster, easier, and cheaper way to move money in today's digital economy.



Please Note:

I will not begin work on your portrait until payment has been received in full. This is to insure that I don't put time and effort into a portrait and then not receive compensation for it, for whatever reason, after the portrait is completed. This is also so that I do not have to spend time collecting payments for portraits after they are done, and can therefore send the portrait to the customer immediately upon completion.
