Congrats to Ilystil!
posted 02/28/00

  Early this morning, Ilystil Drakonblayde of Emef'Aitch became the first ranger on The Rathe to complete the Call of Flames Quest!!! This Quest, when completed, produces a tradeable scroll for her lvl 49 Ranger DD spell that does 206 dmg. The Rathe is the third server to obtain this spell.
