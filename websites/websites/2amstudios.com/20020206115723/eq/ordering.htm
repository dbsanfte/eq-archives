The First EverQuest Portrait Artist!
Creating Custom Made Portraits Since Beta
For the Players on Zebuxuruk

Portraits are perfect for webpages, printouts, and wallpaper. What a great way to display your character! My graphic adaptations are embedded with the Digimarc Watermark. I do not discriminate on the basis of race, class, or guild tag ;-P

You value your time and items as do I. I endeavor to create for you a unique work of art that expresses the spirit of your character's persona. These treasures will be a cherished keepsake of your time in EverQuest.

To commission a portrait is not an easy task. You must be on the Zebuxoruk Server, as am I.

There are 3 package options. Message Board Pic, Special Commission Set, and Guild Portrait Set.

 

Message Board pic is 4k plat: Standard size is 140 x 160 pixels. Can include your guild logo. I email you your message board pic, and you put it on your own webspace. For 6k extra I will host your pic for 6 months on my website (total 10k).

Special Commission Portrait Set is 20k plat: includes a wallpaper in either 800x600 or 1024x768, a reduced version of your portrait for message boards, an inset of your char's face from the portrait, and the portrait itself. I do not host your reduced version on my website.

Special Comission portraits are larger, contain more detailed elements, and action shots. Examples of past Special Commission Portraits are Naughty and Nice, Ploogie, Moonlighte, and Valenya to name a few.

 

 

Guild Portrait Set is 3k plat per member pictured, minimum 10 people. Trades of Lodizal Shields or equivalent offers are considered.: includes a wallpaper in either 800x600 or 1024x768 to distribute to your members for home/office (also makes nice iron-ons for guild T-Shirts), a reduced version of your portrait for special sigs, and the portrait itself. Guild themes and suggestions are welcome. Examples of Guild portraits are Norrath Adventuring Company and Mirth and Mayhem

 

Payment is made at time of photo session.

 

 

To order a portrait:
Email me the following:

Whether you wish a Message Board Pic, Special Commission Set, or a Guild Portrait
Your char name/race/class: ie Baladar/Dwarf Cleric
Your time zone and time found on game so we can make an appointment: ie "5pm EST to 10pm EST M-F, and Weekends after 8am EST"
Whether you wish 800x600 or 1072x768 wallpaper
Your idea and vision...

A note about Cancellations: If you must cancel an appointment, please give me at least 24 hours notice in email. Cancelled appointments without this much notice means I have wasted time that could have been spent in game with friends or in RL (it does not make for a happy artist). When you set up a time days before and you *Know* when/where it is, a /tell of "oh sorry I'm deep inside [insert zone here]" is NOT ok. I do understand that RL things happen to prevent one from being in game, and in these cases I will be glad to reschedule for when we can both next meet.
