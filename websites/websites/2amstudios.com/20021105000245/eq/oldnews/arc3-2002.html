Clan Icewind Retires
Posted Monday, April 1, 2002 by Raene


After nearly 3 years in EverQuest, the guild known as Clan Icewind (CIW) retires from the game. What began in Halas so long ago with only barbarians, ended it's long and tumultuous journey last night in North Freeport when they disbanded.

The CIW guild site currently is here

No mention of what will happen to the website, or where the members will go. Many former members of CIW now reside on the Stormhammer (Legends) Server.
