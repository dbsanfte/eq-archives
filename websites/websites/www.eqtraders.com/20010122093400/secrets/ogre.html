Ogre Cultural Smithing

Many of these recipes are as-yet unverified. Please make sure to send all confirmations/corrections to the DenMother. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by ogre smiths, and they MUST be combined using your racial Oggok forge unless otherwise noted.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.

ogre war plate armor

largel plate sectional mold, smithy hammer, leather padding, adamantite chain jointing, blood temper, appropriate number of folded sheets of adamantite:

  • 1 sheet for visor, bracer, collar, and boots
  • 2 sheets for helm, pauldrons, girdle, vambraces, and gauntlets
  • 3 sheets for breastplate, cloak, and greaves

some pieces come out a reddish brown in color, others are black

trivial: >250 on breastplate
ogre war splintmail

appropriate large chain pattern, appropriate piece of large leather armor, smithy hammer, blood temper, adamantite sheets in the following quantities:

  • veil (mask), neckguard (gorget), bracelets (bracers) - 1 sheet
  • coif (helm), mantle (shoulderpads), skirt (belt), sleeves, gloves, boots - 2 sheets
  • tunic, cape (cloak), leggings - 3 sheets

 some (maybe all?) of the non-imbued versions have incorrect race/class information that make it unwearable by ogres and trolls. Imbued versions do not seem to share this problem
imbued ogre war splintmail

appropriate large chain pattern, appropriate piece of large leather armor, smithy hammer, blood temper, imbued amber or jade, adamantite sheets in the following quantities:

  • veil (mask), neckguard (gorget), bracelets (bracers) - 1 sheet
  • coif (helm), mantle (shoulderpads), skirt (belt), sleeves, gloves, boots - 2 sheets
  • tunic, cape (cloak), leggings - 3 sheets
