EQ Alchemy Recipes

A huge THANK YOU to Belgar Frostvenom, the original caretaker of alchemy for Norrath. Upon the untimely demise of his authoritative alchemy site, he provided us with his files to get a jump start for this page.

Potion Stacking: Word is that since the patch which removed the negative effects from most potions, they are also stacking much more often than they did previously. At this point the only reports of stacking issues I have heard are that potions which have a + Strength component will not stack with spells, songs, or other potions which have + Strength components. In some cases they will even overwrite a much better spell effect because of this. Please report any other stacking problems you find to the Alchemy Forum.

The NEW! Legacy of Ykesha potions have their own page here: LoY Alchemy Recipes. Notice that the LoY armor dyes are located with the rest of the LoY Recipes. You can also find further details for alchemy in general beyond recipes over on the Learn Alchemy page.
