East Commonlands Trade Supplies

(also see Freeport list, which is the next zone over)

WARNING: if you have never been in East Commonlands, read this carefully.  East Commonlands is a relatively low-level hunting area. However, there are high-level griffons that fly through the area, preying on the unwary. Additionally, while the shops are totally enclosed (walls, ceiling, doors), critters often phase through the walls to attack you. Do not assume that because you are in a shop, you are safe. I died several times while checking out all these merchants, from critters coming through the walls, and griffons through the ceiling.

Be aware that the merchants in this zone love to go walkabout, so if the person isn't in the spot listed, they may be dancing on the roof, or otherwise unavailable.

Baking

  • bread, short beer - Innkeeps in each of the 3 inns on the main path out from Freeport to W. Commonlands, plus the inn near the orc camp
  • spices, vinegar, sauce, garnish, frosting, flour, milk, 13 recipe books - Parthar (next to 1st inn out from Freeport)
  • bat wings, rat ears, fire beetle eyes - Katha Firespinner (Erudite, in building next to 3rd inn)

Brewing

  • grapes, bottle, cask, kiola sap - Innkeeps in each of the 3 inns on the main path out from Freeport to W. Commonlands and in the inn near the orc camp

Fishing,  Smithing

  • none

Fletching

  • arrowheads (3), shafts (4), fletch (3), vane (3), groove nock (3) - Joryd Longarms (next to inn between orc camps, /loc 985, 3760)

Jewelcraft

  • peridot, hematite, jasper - Pardor the Blessed (one of the first buildings out from Freeport)
  • carnelian, malachite, peridot, lapis, turquoise, hematite, bloodstone, jasper, star rose quartz, amber, pearl, cats eye agate, onyx - Katha Firespinner (next to 3 inn out from Freeport)
  • many gemstones - Lyth Spellstar (next to 3rd inn out from Freeport)

Pottery

  • clay (3), firing sheets (3), glass shard, Basic Pottery book, vial sketches (3) - Merra Clayfinger (next to first inn)

Spell Research

  • parchment, quill, elemental grimoire, mage spell research books - Lyth Spellstar (next to 3rd inn out from Freeport)

Tailoring

  • sm sewing kit - tailor in each of the 3 inns on the main path out from Freeport to W. Commonlands and in the inn near the orc camp

Tinkering

  • lockpicks - Ponila Quickfingers (first building on right when entering zone from West Freeport)
  • water, large lantern, bottle - Romya (335, 4058)
