Human Cultural Smithing - Freeport

Human cultural smithing is now broken into two pages - one for Freeport (below), one for Qeynos. Any human smith can make any of the items for either city, but they must use the proper cultural forge, in the proper city, for each recipe. Note that, as with all cultural tradeskills, cultural tradeskill containers are not fooled by illusions.

Stats for these items can be found on the Freeport Human Armor/Weapon stats page
