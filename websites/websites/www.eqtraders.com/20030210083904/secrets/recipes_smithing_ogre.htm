Ogre Cultural Smithing

Ogre smash, ogre bash ... ogre make armor and weapons! As with all cultural tradeskills, all recipes listed under the Oggok forge section below can only be combined by ogres, using the cultural "Oggok forge". Note that illusions will not fool a cultural tradeskill container. May 8, 2002 new cultural recipes are included in this list, and the new NPC text is at the bottom of the page.

Stats for these items can be found on the Ogre Smithed Armor/Weapons Stats page

Trivials on the old cultural recipes were changed on June 5. Please report new trivials on the Smithing Trivials message board thread

NPC text from May 8, 2002 patch:

You say, 'Hail, Bubbgrib'

Bubbgrib says ''Yooz small, and yooz look weak. Mez tink dat yooz need new [armor].' Bubbgrib peers at you with a blood-red eye from within his heavy helm. 'Yooz REALLY need new armor!''

You say, 'What armor?'

Bubbgrib says ''Mez make da [plate] and [chain] armourz. Which yooz need, weak one?''

You say, 'What plate?'

Bubbgrib says ''Yooz use yer hammer to bash some enchanted adamanty chain jointing, wiv blood temper and large plate mold, leather padding and some folded [sheets of metal]. Don't forget youz magical shinystone! Amber, jade or da saffire. Gud bashin!''

You say, 'What sheets of metal?'

Bubbgrib says ''To make da special sheetz yooz need to start wiv da right sheet of velium. If yooz need to make unfolded sheetz, den start wiv a unfolded sheet of velium. If yooz need to make a folded sheet, den start wiv a folded sheet of velium. Wiv yer sheet, yoz den hammer in a large brick of dat adamanty from de Darky Elf place. Yooz need to use some [special temper] too.''

You say, 'what special temper?'

Bubbgrib says ''Dis mez not know a lot about. Mez get some from Darky-robe. He nice friend of Bubbgrib's. Yooz just put it into da Oggie Forge wiv da sheet and da hammer and da adamanty ore and it works! Darky-robe call it Sellesty Temper, or someting like dat.''

You say, 'What chain?'

Bubbgrib says ''Yooz take da hammer and da large leather armour and da blood temper and da large chainmail pattern and one imbued shinystone and den yooz bash in some unfolded [sheets of metal].' Bubbgrib scratches his ample backside. 'Mez tink yooz may need more dan armor to keep yooz alive!''
