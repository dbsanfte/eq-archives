I need your help!

You'll find several areas of this site that have large gaps in the information. I can't do/learn everything at once, so I'm asking for help. The more folks who help, the more useful this site is for everyone.

  • Basic trade skill information: Articles on any trade skill are welcome, but I'm especially looking for articles on alchemy and tinkering at the moment.. Please send email that contains the submission, as well as your character name and server, and email address. (Please let me know if you'd rather I didn't link in your email address-- I'll be happy to oblige). Additionally, please send me any corrections/additions you may have to posted information on the other trade skills. Currently (Jan. 2001), there's a large backlog of trade guides, to be posted. I hope to get to these soon.
  • Locations for trade materials in each city and zone: If your favorite zone is missing items in my listings, and you'd be willing to explore/document for me, please send email. I'd like to have up-to-date lists for all zones that have merchants, and this has already been a huge undertaking. Even if you can only list information for a couple shops, every little bit helps! However, please make sure you include complete details. (zone name, merchant name, location and items sold)
  • Merchants wanted! You want to make money, I want to help you hook up with players who need your goods. If you've got trade items to sell, or a skill to offer (i.e., wedding services, weapon sharpening, guide services) go to the message boards with your ad. Please be sure to post in the relevant server folder and include all relevant information, specifically what you are offering (i.e., not just "I'm a tailor", but what types of tailored items you can/will make), and your in-game name. And if you wish, include your contact information in the post. (Please do not submit merchant listings via email.)
