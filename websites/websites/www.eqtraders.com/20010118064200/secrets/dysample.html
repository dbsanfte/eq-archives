Dye Samples

Many folks are curious about the new dyes, and what they look like when put on armor. I'm hacking apart screen shots as I find them, in order to provide a small sampler of the rough color of the various dyes.

The shades will look different on different races. (The 3-resin blue, for example, is "softer" and less metallic on erudites than it is on wood elves.) Additionally, lighting, and your vision quality (UV, etc.) may effect how these shades appear when viewed in-game. Please do not expect the colors seen below to be consistent for all races (Yes, I've got some lovely volunteers who are working on collecting color samples from the various races, more details will be forthcoming later.)

Many thanks go to Gnorth Grumblespike of Cazic-Thule for the work the hard work that went into these images. As one who is smithing and dyeing, I have a good idea how much effort went into making these!
