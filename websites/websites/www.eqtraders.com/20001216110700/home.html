Hail and well met, citizens of Norrath! Do you want to learn a trade skill? Do you want to sell the fruits of your labor to other players? Are you trying to find specific items to pratice your craft? Or are you just trying to find someone who sells player-crafted items for a reasonable price on your server? If the answer to any of these questions is "yes", then I hope this site will be of assistance to you. As the game changes and grows, so too will this site, so remember to check back often.

12/10/00: More on Velious

Things are crazed in the DenMother's corner of the world. Thurgadin has been explored and I think all known trade supplies have been listed. The new tinkering, poison and velium leathers books have been transcribed. Many zones still need to be explored for trade supplies, and help is appreciate. Please remember however, that without detailed information, posts/email cost me more time than they save me -- remember that zone name, merchant name and loc, as well as a complete listing of the trade supplies they sell are all needed in order for the information to be of aid to the site. That having been said, I'm scrambling to get information up as I am able, and am processing a huge backlog of posts and email now.
