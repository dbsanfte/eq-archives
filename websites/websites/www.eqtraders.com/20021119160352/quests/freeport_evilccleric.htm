Freeport Evil (Innnoruuk) Cleric Armor Quest

This is one of several armor quests for younglings that was recently added. This one is for evil Freeport human clerics, and will be HUM/CLR, no drop and lore (other than bracer). Please note that while the bracers are not lore, the bracer materials often are, so make your first bracer before you get the parts for a second one

Questgiver is Zhem Xecia in East FP tunnel. The "Dismal Priest" armor is a matte dark rust color of fine plate, and the helm has little horns near the forehead.
