Velious Tailored Armor Fashion Guide
(by FeistyGirl)

For those master tailors who have long been awaiting new recipes to push them over the 115 skill level, the wait is finally over. The new frozen continent of Velious brings with it new pelts, patterns, threads, tannins, and recipes geared towards highly skilled tailors. The components are rare, and the armor is very difficult to make (it is speculated that it doesn't trivial until well after 200). Huge thanks go out to all the brave adventurers who risk life and limb to gather pelts and brewing components, as well as the struggling master tailors who repeatedly hit the combine button in hopes of a success or skill increase. One of the largest credits goes out to our very own Niami Denmother and her guild Voidrunners, on Tunare, for being the pioneers and working endlessly to test, verify, and compile the results of all the recipes. Thank you Denmother!!
