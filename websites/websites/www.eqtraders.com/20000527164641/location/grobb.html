Grobb Trade Supplies

This zone is sadly limited for trade skills, with nary a forge, pottery wheel, kiln, oven, etc. to be found. Wandering through Oggok, Feerrott and Innothule, however, does help flesh out supplies a bit.

Alchemy

  • medicine bag, lucerne, sage leaf, birthwart, fenugreek, maidenhair fern, mandrake root, allspice, benzoin, nightsharde, mullein, wolf blood, blue vervain bulb, valerian root, yarrow, figwort, agrimony, celandine herb - Grabah (Shaman's Guild)
  • medicine bag - Zumzal (Shaman's Guild)

Baking

  • milk - Ootok (street vendor near warrior's guild)

Brewing

  • barley, hops, rice, malt, yeast, wine yeast - Gartonka (Gunthaks Bench)
  • bottle - Ootok (street vendor near warrior's guild), Bimuk (outside Krungs Clubs & Junk)
  • bottle, cask - Gratak (Gunthaks Bench)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Oootok (street vendor near warrior's guild), Bimuk (outside Krungs Clubs & Junk)

Fletching

  • arrowheads (3), shafts (4), fletch (3), vane (3), groove nock (3) - Zazhar (outside Krungs Clubs & Junk)

Jewelcraft

  • 18 gems, jewelers kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Uzak (553, -324)

Pottery

  • blocks of clay (3) - Okun (outside Night Keep)

Smithing

  • ores (3), sharpening stone - Okun (outside Knight Keep)

Tailoring

  • patterns, Tailoring book, sm sewing kit - Jarlok (Krungs Clubs & Junk)
