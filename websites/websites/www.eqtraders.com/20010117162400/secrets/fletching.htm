Fletching Notes

Note that arrows of the same class will have different ranges based on the size of the nock used (the smaller the nock, the greater the range, when dealing with arrows of the same class). As you work your way from one class of arrow to the next, various fletches and vanes will further adjust the range and the damage of the arrow. You will find once you start comparing costs as well as stats, that some arrows just aren't worth the effort, but I've tried to make a semi-complete list of even the useless ones. ;)

Most of the below information is provided courtesy of Autobond, on Mithaniel Marr. Additional information provided by Ruldari on Tunare and Tilerianea on Brell Serilis.

I include a link to Aoenla's Fletching Guide as phenomenal source of information - however, all the information is on a single page, it doesn't always load well, and it's so big as to make it almost impossible to print out, for example, the arrow charts, without killing a small forest. Alternately, the Ranger's Glade has a good fletching section, but their links in THE Fletching Guide are mis-coded, making it hard to find some of the subpages.

Silver tip arrows listed below are magical - a handy thing to know when hunting creatures that can only be hit by magic items.

Bows:

The different bow strings will affect both damage and delay

  • Rough hickory recurve: hickory staff and hemp, linen or silk string
  • Rough elm recurve: elm staff and hemp, linen or silk string
  • Carved elm recurve: elm staff, hemp/linen/silk string, whittling knife
