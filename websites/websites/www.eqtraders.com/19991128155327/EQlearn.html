The articles linked in here are meant as very basic introductions to various trade skills in Norrath. Since Norrath is a dynamic world, things DO change with time, so experimentation is encouraged. For that reason, I am not readily making available master lists of item combinations (i.e., recipes) to the casual browser. (Who knows what I might link in some hidden corner, if I find a lively spirit of experimentation and knowledge contribution by my readers?)  I also plan on putting in more in-depth guides, eventually, but not until after things settle down more. As many of you can guess, admin of this web site, which is a labor of love, severely cuts into the time when I can actually play and putter with all these skills!
