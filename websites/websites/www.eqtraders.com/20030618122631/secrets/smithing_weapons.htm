tarnished weapons

rusty weapon, sharpening stone

(rusty halberd and rusty two handed hammer unable to be sharpened at last check, trivials not available for rusy Kunark weapons. One note to Kunark smiths: Half the weapons you loot of Kunark skeletons and the like will already be tarnished, NOT rusty. You cannot sharpen a tarnished weapon further)

On a skill fail, you lose the sharpening stone, the weapon is returned to you.

tarnished weapons grant a weapon speed increase of 1 over the rusty version of the same weapon, and sell back to the vendor for slightly more coin
