PoP Foraging Quest

Randi Ellan in Plane of Knowledge, wants help stocking an exhibit in the museum. Ask Curator Merri for a collectors box, and then place the following four foraged items within it:

  • black root - Misty Thicket forage
  • maneater bud - Swamp of No Hope forage
  • yew leaf - Wakening Lands forage and ground spawn
  • twilight orchid - Twilight Sea forage

Hit combine on the collectors box once you have placed all four items in to make a Collection of Plants, magic, lore, no drop, wt 2.0, size small, none/none.

Give the Collection of Plants to Randi Ellan to receive:

Fine Antique Corset, magic, lore, no drop, waist slot, AC 6, sv disease 15, sv poison 15, effect: spell haste II, size small, wt 3.0, all, all.
