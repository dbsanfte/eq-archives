Alchemy - by Shavanna (Tunare Server)

The elder shaman (level 25 or above) has the ability, if he/she cares to exercise it, to make many magical potions. Each potion requires that the shaman mix two or more items in a medicine bag. This skill has undergone many changes since it was first introduced, and the old guide that was here is horribly out of date. A new one will be put in it's place shortly, outlining some of the basic information regarding basic alchemy potions, charged potions, skill increases, etc.

 

In the meantime, shamen who are interested in additional information, including "recipes" are encouraged to visit the Belgar's Ultimate Guide to Alchemy site
