Costs listed are using best charisma and faction. They are listed in platinum pieces in decimal form with no currency label: 9.524 would mean 9pp 5gp 2sp 4cp. Costs for all pottery recipes include completion of the product: firing and applicable glazing. Unconsumed tools are not included.

"Product" is the product made. Yields are given in parentheses when other than 1.

"Min Cost" is the price of all dropped items gathered yourself (farmed), and any subcombines performed yourself. One subcombine in pottery is to gather the research materials used in celestial essences. The cost of components that are themselves made as a tradeskill recipe assumes no failures making them; your mileage will vary.

"Max Cost" is the price of all dropped items purchased from vendors, and without possible cost saving subcombines. This is only used where options apply, and will provide a large variance in some items. Some Max Costs in pottery are nearly irrelevant, as for example you will likely be fishing your own zone-specific fish for brewed essences in spiritstones.

"Discrepancy" will note differences between Min Cost and Max Cost when they exist, usually noting farmed ingrediants and/or cost saving subcombines. Buying dropped items from players will obviously impact the prices greatly, usually to the costlier side. All indredient costs are listed after combines for your own comparisons. Cost of unconsumed tools such as smithy hammer or file is not accounted for, but will be listed among ingredients. NO DROP ingredients are not accounted for, as they must be gathered in the field. An asterisk (*) denotes an ingredient which is not NO DROP, but will not sell to vendors so has no vendor cost (for example, foraged roots).

Technical Exceptions: Clay and ore can be looted in Gorge of King Xorbb from Muddites. Bricks of ore drop from Rock Dervishes in Lavastorm. Numerous gems commonly stocked on merchants are also dropped widely by monsters everywhere. Even flasks of water drop from Desert Madmen in NRo, Oasis, and SRo. Discrepancies will not be given for clay, ore, water, or vendor stocked gems. Leave the desert moisture farming to Uncle Owen and Aunt Beru.

Boxes shaded in cyan highlight values which are inaccurate due to incomplete data.
The following indredient costs are unknown: frost turnip, black root, surefall sap, cactus pulp, cinnamon stick, bag of sea salt, glow lichen, innothule mushroom and swamp vegetables.
