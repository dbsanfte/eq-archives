Ogre Cultural Smithing

Ogre smash, ogre bash ... ogre make armor and weapons! As with all cultural tradeskills, all recipes listed under the Oggok forge section below can only be combined by ogres, using the cultural "Oggok forge". Note that illusions will not fool a cultural tradeskill container.

Stats for these items can be found on the Ogre Smithed Armor/Weapons Stats page
