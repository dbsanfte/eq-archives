Stance on recipes/formulas: This site has always left the posting of cheats/recipes/formulas to other sites. My reasoning behind this was that, without player experimentation, we don't find the new and undiscovered recipes. I've provided some links here and there throughout the site for recipes and such for those who want to learn things without costly experimentation. When I return, I might directly link in some of the recipes that I, and other contributors to this site, have found. However, this is a 1-person site that has tried to stay ad-free (other than the remotely hosted message board) and low-maintenance. On top of all other considerations, the amount of extra time I'd have to spend per week on handling corrections/additions to those recipes, handling often irate and incoherent email asking "why do you have these recipes and not those recipes that I really want?" and a myriad of other issues, makes me very hesitant to expand the offerings of this site. I love helping folks learn new things about tradeskills, but I love playing even more. Thanks for your understanding on this.

I hope everyone has a safe and happy holiday season.

September 14: Yes! Finally some more trade skill changes, though the dust hasn't settled yet so we can tell how wide the changes are. Here's what I've managed to verify:

Fletching: kits now have 8 slots. The merchants who used to sell fletching or bowmaking supplies now carry fletching kits. The merchants who sold the fletching kits and groove nocks now sell the arrow components. For some odd reason, arrowmaking components are now considered magic items. <bemused> Please have patience as I try to juggle everything on my site accordingly.

Tailoring: merchants who carry quiver and backpack patterns now carry mask, gorget, belt, cloak and shoulderpad patterns. Why we need a second gorget pattern, I don't know, but perhaps it was supposed to be bracers and they ooopsed? Also, rumors abound that we can now tailor silk armor, though the instructions are rumored to be critter-dropped only. I'll post more if I get verification. I've also seen a whip pattern, but have heard of no successful combination ... yet.

Baking: new foraged items are being found in various zones. No word yet if it means more recipes or not. I can tell you that tea leaves don't mix with either just water or water and a pot, but other than that, who knows?

Please email me, or post on the message board, with any other new trade skill finds.
