Shadeweavers Thicket - Claw Needle

This quest produces a claw need that is used to make shaded hopperhide armor. The needle is used up when you make a piece of the armor, so you might wish to loop this many times. (A failed tailoring combine on the armor returns the needle, but uses up the armor pieces, success returns the new armor, but uses up the needle.)

Give Tiamon Aroo (tailoring supplies merchant in Shadeweaver's Thicket) 4 needle thin claws. These drop off needle clawed rockhoppers. Reward is one claw needle (stackable) and a minor amount of experience.
