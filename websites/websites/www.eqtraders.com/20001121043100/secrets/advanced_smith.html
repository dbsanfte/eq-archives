fine steel plate

smithy hammer, leather padding (from tailor), flask of water, appropriate mold, and medium quality folded sheets of metal in the following amounts:

1 sheet for visors, collars and bracers; 2 sheets for helms, pauldruns, girdles, vambraces, gauntlets, and boots; 3 sheets for breastplates, cloaks and greaves

Please make sure you are using medium quality folded sheets, from the recipe listed above. Normal ore made into folded sheets will not work, it must be medium quality ore made into folded MQ sheets.
