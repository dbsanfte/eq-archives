Freeport Trade Supplies

City Evaluation: Freeport is a trader's paradise. Between the 3 zones in the city, and the East Commonlands, every skill appears to be readily do-able, as long as you're willing to do some zoning. Any listings with an asterisk (*) in front of them, were verified after the December 18, 2001 Freeport merchant changes. More verification will need to be done after the holiday and Luclin chaos has settled down.
