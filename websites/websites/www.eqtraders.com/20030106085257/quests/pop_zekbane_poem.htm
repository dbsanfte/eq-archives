Cleaving the Warlord - A Poem by Nherko E. Llafsnot

If ye have bested Tallon,
and Vallon, too (or not?),
then get thee down to Eriak,
and there ask him, "What blood?"

He'll draw his blade and cut himself,
and spill for you his own,
now take this vial, mount your steed,
and ride to Saryrn's zone.

Find you there her constructs,
of which there are four breeds,
Insanity Fangs they'll drop for you--
these stasify your needs.

Into the barrel--fangs and blood,
celestial solvent, too,
to get one of the tempers,
proceed you now to brew.

Now off to Valor go with haste,
and ride up to the swamp,
o'er everything that spawns up there,
shall you proceed to romp.

Valorium shards, no drop, take these!
but whatever you do,
be sure the one that takes 'em,
is someone who can brew.

Then ride again to Knowledge,
and then much like before,
except without the blood, of course,
brew up one temper more.

Now head on back to Tactics,
and take a bunch of friends,
for in its depths drops ore with which,
this Zek-Bane quest will end.

Tagrin Maldric spawns down there,
a troll both big and bad,
kill him there--but not too fast!
because you'll need his adds.

Smiths will loot his guardians,
these flying swords--Decorins,
then (with water) find a forge,
to put Decorin ore in.

Drunder's Steel--that's the thing,
with which you'll end this trial--
into the forge with proper molds,
and aforebrewed blue vial.

Almost done! It's good so far,
but there is more to do!
forge it now with your black vial,
and so your quest concludes.
