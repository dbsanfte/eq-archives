Tradeskill High Fashion

Oftentimes, folks will ask "what does x cultural armor look like?", or "what does the 3-resin blue look like?" and other such questions. As time goes on, this section will be used to display player-made armors that come in a variety of colors and designs.

Our original fashion editor went missing, but Darmer is handling screen shots of dyed fine steel plate (you can send things to fashion@eqtraders.com), and include information in the email including item specifics (race, dye shade, etc.).

  • Cultural armor (smithing and tailoring, also includes Wu's Fighting Armor
  • Velious tailoring
  • Dyed fine steel plate (dwarven model). For color samples on other races, see the new fashion table being built by Darmer. Eventually, we hope to house it here on the site
