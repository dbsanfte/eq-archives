Surefall Youngling Ranger Armor Quest

This is a new armor quests for young rangers that originates in Surefall Glade. All pieces of this set should be lore (other than the bracer), no drop, and HUM, HEF/RNG only.

Speak with Errin Pinewhisper in the Ranger guild hall. She will give you a no drop curing box in which to make the items, and will give you the appropriate patterns upon request.

The armor is called Pine Scout Armor: all use pieces ruined gnoll chain as a base, a tattered armor pattern (given) and curing box.
