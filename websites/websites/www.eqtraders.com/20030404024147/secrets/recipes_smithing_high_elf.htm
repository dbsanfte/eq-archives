High Elf Cultural Smithing

Anything in the "Smithing - Koada'dal forge" listing must be combined by a high elf, using their cultural koada'dal forge in Felwithe. As with all cultural tradeskills illusions will not fool a cultural tradeskill container into believing that you are something that you are not.

Stats for these armor and weapon items can be found on the High Elven Cultural Armor/Weapons Stats page
