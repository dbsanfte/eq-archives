Cultural Armor Fashion Guide
(by FeistyGirl)

Many people are eager to see what the different cultural armors look like. The creation of a single piece of cultural armor is a difficult, expensive, and time-consuming process, making the cultural armor quite a rarity in the worlds of Norrath. Those intrepid master smiths who have succeeded in creating their race's special armor should be applauded! A big thank you goes out to all of the master smiths pictured in this section, both for their ventures into this expensive craft, as well as for generously sharing their discoveries with the rest of us!

If you have a screenshot of some cultural armor and would like to share it, click here to submit it!
