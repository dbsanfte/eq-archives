Joril Stoutbear - barbarian ranger (warrior)

 

Read the story: A Wolf of Everfrost

Joril’s parents were slain by an Orc war party on the tundra of Everfrost. He is ridden with guilt at his inability and inexperience to lend them aid. He has vowed to avenge their deaths by bringing vengeance to the Orcs and will seek them out in all the lands of Norrath.

He also mistrusts Dark Elves. While on a journey to Faydwer, he entered Crushbone to battle the Orcs that dwell there. He was captured, beaten and tortured at the hands of Ambassador Dvinn, Dark Elf Emissary to the Orcs. Joril managed to escape the dungeons and made his way to Kaladim and then on to Freeport, where he remained and nursed the wounds of his body, heart and soul.

He had left Everfrost for the love of a young elf maiden. He followed her all the way to Kelethin. Once there, she became too involved with her own hunts and friends. He rarely saw her and eventually he came to the realization that he had been disillusioned. After the escape from Crushbone, he returned to Freeport, broken-hearted. He began to drink heavily, trying to drown his sorrows and his homesickness.

During a moment of sobriety, he was befriended by Aerynea and Genneth of House Anarawyn. They unknowingly pulled him from the brink of the dark chasm he faced.

He went on several hunts with this new group of friends and once again, knew the joy of battle! He gained valuable experience and knowledge. But he was still homesick.

Eventually, he had gained enough experience and risen high enough in rank to attempt a return trip to his beloved Everfrost. With the help of some adventurers, he made it to Highpass without incident. The adventurers went on to Rivervale and Joril decided to push on towards home. He made it through Highpass but then ran into problems in East Karanas, where he was beset by lions. Some adventurers came to his aid and he passed into North Karanas.

He ran and ran into the night and finally he came to the familiar lands of Qeynos Hills. He ran on to the entrance of Blackburrow, home of the wicked Blackburrow Gnolls. He ran past the guards and wound his way through the passages and out into the cold and snow of Everfrost. He whooped with joy for he was home! Joril can be found running and hunting the tundra of his beloved land. He continues to practice tailoring and has begun fletching as well. He constantly seeks out Orcs and sends them to Innoruk. He is still plagued with visions and nightmares of his days of captivity. His heart is still heavy with the loss of his once dear elf maiden. But under his dark and brooding visage, he is a kindly soul. He feels a special affinity for young adventurers and will do what he can to aid them when asked.
