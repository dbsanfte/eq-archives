September 4 , 2002 - Patch Day!
Some days it's the little things in life that make you dance around, sometimes it's the big. I'll let you all decide for yourselves where you put the following updates. (Note: more information may be added to this later in the day, but this is what the sleep-depreived halfling has found so far):

  • jars of acid stack
  • jars of lacquer stack
  • low quality rockhopper hides can now be used for making leather padding. I also verified that dropping a medium quality rockhopper hide into the sewing kit with a skinning knife does yield low quality rockhopper hides. (I've not tested high quality hides yet, but I'm presuming the downward conversion is working for them as well)
  • stormreaper chainwoven skirt now fits properly in the waist slot
  • tired of trying to tell half your guild what the starts are on some brand new tradeskill item are? You can now give them a hotlink with a few extra keyclicks! In main chat, start your message to the appropriate chat channel (/gu, /auction, various created chat channels, etc.), alt-left click on the item you want to announce, click on the icon of the item in the item description box that you just caused to open, and it should drop the item name as a hotlink, into your current line of chat text. If these instructions make little sense, then find someone less sleep-impaired to explain it to you. ;)

Over the next few weeks, I'll be knuckling down and beating the database into submission more (no ETA on when you folks will see it yet, but it will definitely be worth the wait), and dealing with a lot of IRL stuff, so if I'm unusually quiet for the next few weeks, don't be surprised. Expect to see me post more pleas for information as I get stuck trying to find certain tidbits.

August 17, 2002 - A Word of Warning
It's come to my attention that one (or more) folks are sending out suspect file attachments and spoofing the sender id to niami@eqtraders.com. If you receive such an email, DO NOT download the file attachment. It is not from me. I have never, and will never send out email using that email address. Look VERY carefully at the email headers and return path, and it's likely that you can find the true source of the email. I recommend that if you're internet-savvy enough to find the real sender, that you forward the email to the mail abuse mailbox for that domain (such as abuse@earthlink.net), so that they are aware that someone is likely using their mail service to distribute viruses/trojan horse software using someone else's name. Addendum: it's been pointed out that it's also possible that someone has been hit with the KLEZ family of viruses, and that they are unaware that their machine is sending out additional infected files to/from various addy's on their hard drive. Regardless, always be very careful when downloading file attachments.

July 24, 2002 - Patch Day
Today's patch is quite large, so it will likely be a while before we catch and mention everything in it that impacts tradeskills. While I sift through the many posts on the subject, here are a few things of note:

  • The new UI has been released, including the bazaar functionality. Remember that you must set "newui=TRUE" in your eqclient.ini file before you can buy or sell using the new bazaar trader functions.
  • The Jaggedpine zone has been released (it is an optional patch, so you must actively ask for it under the Advanced options when loading EQ). The baking forum already has a thread started for new recipes and trivials from this zone.
  • Stats on stat drinks are reported as working.
  • Faydwer was updated with quests for young adventurers. Information on the quests can be found in the Quests message board under the following threads: Felwithe Newbie Quests, Kelethin Newbie Quests, and Kaladim Newbie Quests. I'll give it a day or two before I make up quests pages for all of these.
  • Cazic-Thule tailored items did not get an expected upgrade with this patch.

That looks to be about it on the updates for today, other than the decomissioning of the May 8 recipes page at long last. (All recipes stemming from the May 8 patch should now be on their permanent recipes pages.)

July 12, 2002
Just a few quick things...

The fish & game wardens in Erud's Crossing are happy to announce that their efforts to reintroduce barren flounder into the waters have been successful. (i.e., Erud's Crossing was patched today) Erudite clerics of the non-evil persuasion can now join their priestly bretheren in the possession of a coveted Faithstone. (Note: folks on the Quellious server apparently did not get the zone patch and are still fishing up Timorous Tuna. The other servers, so far, seem to have been restocked properly.)

The mysteries of Cazic Thule tailoring continue to unravel ... so to speak.

Those of you who have seen stats of items on other sites and have come here looking for the recipes may find that the following will aid in your search:

  • Those looking for "Luminate" armor (as per Alla'khazam's and EQ'Lizer) really want to look for "Luminant" heraldic armor, which is Human Cultural, Qeynos.
  • Those of you looking for Stormreaper Chainwoven or Stormreaper Steelwoven will not that it is not smithed (as per EQ'Lizer). These items are made via halfling cultural tailoring. (It seems their data entry gnomes are a tad busy right now, but these will get fixed in their database soon).
  • Those of you looking for Tae Ew items, the tailored Tae Ew goodies are still on the Cazic Thule recipes page.
  • If you're looking for ___ Heraldic armor, or "that new Veeshan bard armor", you'll find it, again, under human cultural, Qeynos
