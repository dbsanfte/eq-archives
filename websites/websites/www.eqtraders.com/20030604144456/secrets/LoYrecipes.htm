LoY Recipes

All recipes specific to the LoY expansion other than tailoring and alchemy are listed on this page. For those two categories, please use the links below. Note that there are two different types of "dyes" - one set is used for racial robes, the other is the long-awaited armor tinting. To prevent some confusion, the Dyes link below is for the armor tints only.

Armor Dyes
dye tints are applied to the player's armor slot (not directly to the armor) by use of the "dye" button on your inventory screen. This is the point at which you select the color, with no need for making different shades of dyes, etc

prismatic dye prism shard (vendor-sold), glaze lacquer - combine in brew barrel using brewing skill, or combine in a medicine bag using alchemy skill 54
vial of prismatic dye prismatic dye, dye vial - combine in brew barrel using brewing skill, or combine in a medicine bag using alchemy skill 68
