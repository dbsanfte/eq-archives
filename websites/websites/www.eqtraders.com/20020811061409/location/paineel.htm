Paineel Trade Supplies

City evaluation. I have one word for folks starting in this city that wish to practice tradeskills. Ugh. Jewelcraft is readily done here, but you'll find no native enchanters. Bat wings and snake eggs abound, but without the rest of the baking supplies, all you can make is sandwiches at the single oven in town. You can sharpen weapons for smithing but not a lot elsefor that skill. You can brew bog juice and heady kiola, but not make other items without imports. While there is a kiln here, the pottery wheel seems to have gone AWOL, so don't expect to do any pottery at all. While there are small sewing kits here, there are no patterns and pelts to use within them. There are wonderful alchemy supplies here ... but no shamen. Basically, any evil erudite who calls this their home city will NOT be practicing much in the way of trade skills without someone bringing in supplies, or by working their faction enough to shop elsewhere. Good luck, gang.
