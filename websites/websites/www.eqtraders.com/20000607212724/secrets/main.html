EQ Trade Secrets

While I'd originally planned on never providing "cheats" or "recipes" on the site, the overwhelming number of requests have made me rethink my decision. HOWEVER, I leave the caveat up that there may be some errors contained within here, especially on the items that I have not had a time to personally research. Feel free to let me know when you find a correction or addition, but leave your flames and complaints at the door. <g>

  • Baking - all known recipes, some queries on "goes well with" recipes that are still undetermined, plus most trivial levels and yields
  • Brewing - all known recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields
  • Fletching - arrow chart, including damage, range, delay, average cost to make, and some trivial listings
  • Jewelcraft - gem/metal combinations, plus a link to a better trivial list
  • Poisons - several recipes from a rogue
  • Pottery - translation of the basic pottery book, plus a couple trivial levels
  • Smithing - translation of the Blacksmithing, armor and weapons guides, plus some trivial levels
  • Tailoring - all known tailoring recipes, plus most trivial levels. Also includes AC information for cured silk

I refuse to try to improve on a good thing <g>, so here are links to recipes for other trade skills not listed above:

  • Alchemy - visit the Shaman's Crucible for alchemy recipes. (As of January 2000, the first recipe, Somber Origins, makes a charisma-enhancing potion, not a hangover cure. <g>)
  • Tinkering - visit the Tinker Guild for recipes

Corrections, additions, comments? Let me know!
