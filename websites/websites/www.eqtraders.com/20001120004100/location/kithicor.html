Kithicor Forest Trade Supplies

Zone analysis: Do NOT get caught shopping here while it is "night" in-game. This zone has been turned into a very nasty zone, with undead and their companions ranging up to level 50 (and maybe beyond?). You should be fine shopping here during the daytime, as long as you avoid the undead camps and the shadowed men camps to the south. (Follow the roads if you're new to the area and need to shop).
