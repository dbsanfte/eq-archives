Xanthe's Earring/PoTC - The Sacrifice of Nine

After all nine gifts have been made, they must be "sacrificed" to the forge. Original writeup courtesy of Xanthe Ravenwolf

 

Place all of your finely crafted nine gifts (demi-sec champagne, mistletoe cutting sickle, golden idol of Tunare, blessed fishing pole, star ruby encrusted stein, ceremonial solstice robe, lotus pie, Misty Thicket picnic, lucky cabbage) into a forge (any normal, non-cultural forge), and hit combine (fear not, it is a no-fail combine). The faithful (or is that crazed, by this point in your questing?) shall recieve:


Xanthe's Earring of Nature: AC 5, STR 8, STA 8, WIS 10, HP 55, mana 55, sv fire 7, sv cold 7, sv magic 7, recommended level 51, effect: circle of the combines; DRU/ALL, NO DROP, LORE**

and

Protection of the Cabbage spell scroll - level 59 spell, no drop, 500 mana.

 

**Should the above earring not be to your taste (or you're not a druid), you can hand Xanthe's Earring of Nature to Rebecca Cabbageleaf in Fungus Grove. She will give you the following in return:

Earring of the Solstice: AC 5, STA 5, WIS 8, INT 8, HP 55, mana 55, sv fire 5, sv cold 5, sv magic 5, recommended level 51, flowing thought 1, tiny, wt 0.1, ALL/ALL, LORE, NO DROP.
