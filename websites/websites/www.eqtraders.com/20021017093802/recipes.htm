Secrets of the Trades

June 5 update: Patience, please, grasshoppers. A LOT of information flooded in, and it WILL take time for the pages to be manually updated, especially with how many folks are bogging down the site with repeat page requests for recipes that aren't even webbed yet. ;)

  • Cultural recipes - race-specific tradeskill recipes (other than Tinkering mentioned below). Also see the Q&A on cultural and advanced smithing if you have questions regarding cultural smithing
  • Alchemy - recipes, effects and most trivials
  • Baking - recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields
  • Brewing - plus most trivial levels and yields. Also see the recipes for smithing tempers and tailor's tanning agents.
  • Fishing - fishing lures and other tacklebox combinations - now includes May 8 recipes!
  • Fletching - bows, arrows, and some quest recipes
  • Jewelcraft - gem/metal combinations, also see the listing for imbued (deity-specific) jewelry - now includes May 8 recipes!
  • Poisons - some recipes, including translations of books in several languages
  • Pottery - known pottery recipes, including the deity-specific idols
  • Smithing
    • The Basics - recipes for blocks, bricks, sheets, etc. of various metals, as well as rings, bits, boning and chain jointing
    • Tools and Implements - files, scalers, containers, baking supplies, etc.
    • Weapons - all non-cultural weapons
    • Armor - all non-cultural armor (banded, ornate chain, fine steel plate)
    • Smithed Armor Stats - stats for non-cultural armors
    • Smithed Weapon Stats - stats for non-cultural weapons
    • If you missed the link at the top, the Cultural recipes have been moved out of Smithing and into their own section
  • Tailoring - main tailoring recipes (including Luclin) and trivials; also see the Velious tailoring recipes.
  • Tinkering - only contains the newer Velious and Luclin recipes. Visit The Tinkering Compendium for recipes and more!
  • cross-skill items:
    • Cazic Thule Recipes - what we know of recipes from the revamped Cazic Thule
    • Tradeskill Trophies
    • Sonic Wolf Bane Weapons - Vah Shir weapons that need the cooperation of every Vah Shir class
    • dyes armor dyes for use on fine steel plate armor only (made via alchemy or make poison),
    • clue sheet for dye ground spawns
    • Celestial essence recipes
  • Recipe resource - for those wanting to know the original sources for the recipes contained herein
  • Liberus Acrylia Arma text (June 5 - acrylia armor & weapons book translation)
  • Imbued gemlist - outlining what gems are imbued for each deity, and what recipes (armor, jewelry, etc.) can use them
  • Flyswatter Needed - sometimes even the best-laid plans go awry. Here's a few in-game recipes that seem to be having bug issues.

 

I'm not going to get into the debate about spell-research being a "neglected tradeskill". There's just far too much information out there for me to re-do what others have already done. I highly recommend Mythiran Tower as a source for spell research recipes.
