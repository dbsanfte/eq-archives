"Newbie" Armor Quests

As various "newbie" zones have been revamped in Spring 2002, armor quests have sprung up in various starting cities that are geared towards young adventurers. Some of the quests include tradeskill combines that can gain you low-level tradeskill increases. Due to this, it has been decided to include all of the youngling quests, regardless of if they have a true tradeskill component or not.
