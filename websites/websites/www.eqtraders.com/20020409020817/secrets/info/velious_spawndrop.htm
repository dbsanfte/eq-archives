Velious Tailoring and Smithing - Extra Info on Spawns/Drops

  • Arctic wyvern hides - off wyverns in Cobalt Scar. They are on Claws of Veeshan faction with regards to agro, but no faction hit is taken for killing un-named ones.
  • Black panther skins - black panthers wander in Wakening Lands. Their pelts are a random drop. They are an agro mob.
  • Cobalt cod - fished in Cobalt Scar
  • Cobalt drake hides - rare drop off cobalt drakes in Cobalt Scar. They are tied to Claws of Veeshan faction, but there is no faction hit for killing the unnamed ones. These creatures have an AoE attack/debuff. Do NOT kite them, or you will get not only yourself but other hunters killed due to their AoEs.
  • Coldain Velium Temper - is only purchased in Thurgadin (near the mines. It is NOT brewed by players, there is no recipe for it.
  • Crystalline silk - drops off various Velious spiders. It is stackable. Remember that to form swatches of this silk, you must combine the pieces of silk unstacked.
  • Drake eggs - are a rare forage in Skyshrine, Western Wastes and Temple of Veeshan. They also are a rare drop off named drakes in Skyshrine. They are considered food, so be very careful that you do NOT eat these on your way to the brew barrel!
  • Haze panthers - wander in Wakening Lands, and are generally invisible. They are a rarer spawn than normal panthers, they randomly drop pelts and eyes (for tinkering). They are agro, as are many other things in this zone. Anyone trying to track these should have see invis.
  • Holgresh fur - drops off holgresh in Wakening Lands. These are spellcasting monkeymen and very agro.
  • Ice burrower silk - drops off ice burrowers (they look like HUGE leeches) in Western Wastes. They are a level 62 mob.
  • Othmir fur - drops off various othmir (ottermen) in Cobalt Scar. They are social, so be careful when pulling. Do NOT kill Bloogy Shellcracker or Blumblum Swigwater. They are merchants and not only will not drop anything, but they carry trade supplies sold nowhere else in the lands. Please respect your fellow tradesfolk and let those two live. The othmir are friendly/indifferent ... until you start killing them. (You will take othmir faction hits for harming them. Killing walruses and ulthorks in Eastern Wastes will help you regain your othmir faction, if you need it.)
  • Tigeraptors - Roam the northwest section of Wakening Lands. You will also find a fixed camp of them just north of the tunnel to Skyshrine in this zone. They are agro mobs.
  • Velium hound fur - drops off velium hounds in Western Wastes. It is stackable.
  • Velium ore - is dropped off various golem-types in Velious (Icewell, Skyshrine, etc), orcs in Crystal Caverns and other various Velious mobs. The small and large blocks are light pink in color, and you can oftentimes find some in merchant overstock in Thurgadin and Skyshrine.
  • Yew Leaves - are a random ground spawn, and a rare forage, in Wakening Lands. The ground spawn appears as hard-to-see green diamonds on the ground. Locations for these have not been consistent, but some folks have reported slightly better luck finding them near the sifaye camp and/or the east side of the lake.
