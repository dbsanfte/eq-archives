Celestial Essence Combines

The May 8, 2002 patch introduced many new tradeskill recipes that require the use of a "celestial essence". This essence is made by combining celestial solvent (vendors listed on the poison trade supplies page) and one of many spell research supplies, in a mortar & pestle (vendors listed on the poison trade supplies page). Much research has already gone into sorting out which research components will and will not work, and this list will change as more information becomes available. Many thanks to Mitstune and the rest of the EQTC community for their hard work in testing these.
