Akanon Initiate's Watchman Armor Quest (Warrior)

One of many new youngling armor quests, this one is for gnome warriors. Some minor smithing skillups have been seen from this quest

Hail the guy in gemchopper hall (Narron Jenork), and tell him you are a gnome warrior, then you are ready to be tested. He'll give you the assembly kit (which you must combine all the ingredients in), then when you ask about the specific piece (ie "i want to craft a breastplate" , he'll give you the mold. First you combine all your bronze, legs, mace, scalp whatever in the assembly kit, and you get "*piece* material". And then to the forge (not the Ak'anon forge, use the one over by the clockwork merchant on the other side) with the mold and material. Combines are trivial at skill 21, but give skill increases until that point. All items are no-drop, and all but the bracer (iirc, doesn't make sense for it to be) is lore. Nice newbie armor, especially for a gnome. Has the look of a gray-ish fine plate.
