adamantite chainmail

(caster wearable)

apropriate chain pattern, teir'dal smithy hammer, silk swatch, shadow temper, and adamantite rings in the following quantities:

  • Veils, neckguards, boots and bracelets require 1 set of adamantite rings.
  • Coifs, mantles, skirts, sleeves, and gloves require 2 sets of adamantite rings.
  • Tunics, capes, and leggings require 3 sets of adamantite rings.

no non-enchanted version of this armor can be imbued

trivial >175 for all pieces

  • bracelet, neckguard <=202
  • gloves, veil, legs, tunic >202

enchanted adamantite chainmail

(caster wearable)

apropriate chain pattern, teir'dal smithy hammer, silk swatch, shadow temper, and enchanted adamantite rings in the following quantities:

  • Veils, neckguards, boots and bracelets require 1 set of adamantite rings
  • Coifs, mantles, skirts, sleeves, and gloves require 2 sets of adamantite rings
  • Tunics, capes, and leggings require 3 sets of adamantite rings

imbued version [Innoruuk] works by adding an imbued sapphire to the above recipe

This armor acts as a minor light source

The enchanted adamantite rings are made using enchanted brick of adamantite. (Enchanter level 49 spell)
