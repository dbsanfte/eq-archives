Buggy Luclin Tailoring?

And no, as we tell folks on the message boards every single time they say they've found the shade silk, shadeling silk off the xakra silkworms, lesser shades, etc. is NOT the same thing as shade silk. Unless you can combine two pieces of the silk in a sewing kit and come up with a shade silk swatch instead of a shade silk thread, please don't report that you've "found" the missing silk. (Can you tell we've been hit with this a few times? ;D)
