Firiona Vie (Docks) Trade Supplies

Important note: Druids and rangers beware! This "good city" does not like wolf form - anyone in wolf form is automatically KOS. You have been warned.

Trade skill evaluation: Pottery is not a viable trade skill in this zone and you have to bring your own smithing supplies if you want to smith at the forge, but other trade skills can flourish here, at the boat stop for the "good" races. As with Cabilis, almost all trade supplies are conveniently placed so that merchants that sell related items are beside each other.
