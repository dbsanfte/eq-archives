Protection of the Cabbage Quest

This is an in-progress rough outline of the quest for the Protection of the Cabbage spell (single-target Protection of the Grove spell for druids.) Both the spell, and the additional earring reward (Earring of the Solstice: WIS 5, INT 5, Mana 40, HP 40, all/all), are no drop. This guide will be updated with more details as time goes on.

On May 8, 2002, during the massive tradeskill patch, nine Liber Brassica Felix books were introduced throughout the lands, each written in a different language. They outlined various "gifts" from the three druid races, and gave hints as to the proper combination of them. The folks here at EQTC and the druids from the Druid's Grove did much pooling of knowledge to solve this extremely tradeskill-intensive quest. It was the first cross-server quest of it's kind, and now that it has been completed, the spell will become available in some "easier" manner, with the June 5 patch. The quest, however, will stay in place, for those who wish to obtain the earring.

Note that many of the gifts are non-trivial at 250 skill in the proper tradeskill, so it is recommended that you enlist help for the completion of this quest.

Many, many congratulations to the lovely Lady Xanthe on the Bertoxxulous server for being the first on any server to complete this difficult and time-consuming quest.
