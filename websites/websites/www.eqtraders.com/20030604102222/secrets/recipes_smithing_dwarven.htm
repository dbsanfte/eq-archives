Dwarven Cultural Smithing

The smithing portion of these recipes, unless otherwise noted, must be performed by dwarves, using their cultural forge in Kaladim (no, you cannot fool the forge with an illusion).

Stats for the weapons and armor on this page can be found on the Dwarven Cultural Armor/Weapons Stats page

October 27 note: Folks are STILL reporting massively conflicting trivials, especially for the old dwarven plate armor. The information below is only the most recent "correction" posted by members of the community - take it with a grain of salt.
