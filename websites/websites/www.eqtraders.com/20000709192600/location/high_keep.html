High Keep Trade Supplies

This keep is a separate zone reachable only from Highpass Hold. Be careful wandering the basement. A wrong turn and you could be near the prisoners, and the gaurds will happily let them loose enough to kill you..

Baking

  • bread, mixing bowl, 4 recipe books, Delivered Note - Baker Jena (2nd floor, 42, -258)
  • oven (2nd floor, 31, -216)

Brewing

  • bottle, cask - Merchant Dominik (outside building)
  • barley, hops, rice, malt, yeast, wine yeast - a clerk (1st floor, -73, -185), dealer (2nd floor, -94, -321 and -68, -354)
  • brew barrel (2nd floor, -54, -216, across from the gambling area)

Jewelcraft

  • 18 gems, jewelers kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Merchant Edina (1st floor, -91, -294)

Pottery

  • blocks of clay (3) - Miner Harton (basement, 43, -274)

Smithing

  • ore (3), sharpening stone - Miner Harton (basement, 43, -274)

Spell Research

  • one rune and several words - Maryl Quinley (basement, 13, -361) I am not sure if her inventory cycles or not, so this will need verification by another on any server but Veeshan

Other

  • Possible high-level wizard trainer - Ran Flamespinner (basement, -45, -351. Since I was not a wizard, he wouldn't talk to me, so cannot verify). There are also enchanter and bard spells in the building.
