Secrets of the Trades

Please post to the message boards if you find a correction or addition to the posted recipes

  • Baking - all known recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields
  • Brewing - all known recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields. Also see the brewing recipes for smithing tempers and tailor's tanning agents.
  • Fishing - fishing lures and other tacklebox combinations
  • Fletching - bows, arrows, and some quest recipes
  • Jewelcraft - gem/metal combinations, also see the listing for imbued (deity-specific) jewelry
  • Poisons - some recipes, including translations of books in several languages
  • Pottery - known pottery recipes, including the deity-specific idols
  • Smithing - this section got large enough that it needed to be split into multiple pages
    • The Basics - recipes for blocks, bricks, sheets, etc. of various metals, as well as rings, bits, boning and chain jointing
    • Tools and Implements - files, scalers, containers, baking supplies, etc.
    • Weapons - all non-cultural weapons
    • Armor - all non-cultural armor (banded, ornate chain, fine steel plate)
    • Cultural Smithing - race-restricted smithing recipes
    • Also check the Q&A on cultural and advanced smithing if you have questions regarding cultural smithing
    • Dyes - armor dyes for use on fine steel plate armor only (no other armor will accept a dye)
    • Tempers - brewing recipes for use in cultural smithing (the brewing portion is NOT race-restricted)
    • Smithed Armor Stats - stats for normal and cultural armors. If you have stats for something not listed here, please post to the smithing message boards
    • Smithed Weapon Stats - stats for normal and cultural weapons. Again, if you have stats for something not listed here, please post to the smithing message boards
  • Tailoring - main tailoring recipes and trivials; also see the Velious tailoring recipes. (Luclin tailoring recipes still on the main Luclin Recipes page)
  • cross-skill items: dyes for smithing (made via alchemy or make poison), a dye color sample chart, and a clue sheet for dye ground spawns
  • Recipe resource - for those wanting to know the original sources for the recipes contained herein
  • Imbued gemlist - outlining what gems are imbued for each deity, and what recipes (armor, jewelry, etc.) can use them
  • LUCLIN RECIPES! Temporary holding spot for Luclin recipes as we find them, so you can work with them while the dust settles. This section will slowly be phased out as the recipes are placed in their proper spots in the above recipe pages
  • NEW Flyswatter Needed - sometimes even the best-laid plans go awry. Here's a few in-game recipes that we know are broken.

I refuse to try to improve on a good thing , so here are links to recipes for other trade skills not listed above:

  • Alchemy - visit the Belgar's Ultimate Guide to Alchemy and/or the Shaman's Crucible for more information on this shaman-only skill
  • Spell Research - Mythiran Tower is a good source of info
  • Tinkering - gnomes should visit the Tinkering Compendium for recipes and more
