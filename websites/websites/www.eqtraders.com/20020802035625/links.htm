General EQ links:

  • /gucomics - everyone needs a dose of humor now and then, and Woody is back with his humorous views of life in (and out) of EQ
  • www.everquest.com - Sony's main EQ website contains news of interest to EQ players (scheduled downtime, policy changes, etc.)
  • www.eqvault.com - This site contains recent news about the game, player guides, maps and more. Their message board may be slow, but it's quite active, and a good source for information.
  • eq.stratics.com - a good site for depth of coverage on a myriad of topics, up-to-date news, and more.
  • EQ'Lizer.- is full of information on quests, abilities and much, much more. It's also the spot to which I regularly submit screenshots of new tradeskill items. (updated link Jan. 14, 2002)
  • EverLore - offers a deeper look at EverQuest including news, guides, discussions, quest info, etc.
  • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm has merged with EverSTOMPED to bring you information on cheats, quests, and more.
  • EverCrest - a semi-daily comic strip that any die-hard EQ'er can empathize with
  • EverQuest Map Shop - some amazing maps of the various zones. While the majority of the site is Japanese, it is a must visit for anyone looking for good maps.
  • EQ Atlas - another good spot for maps and travel guides. If you need information before visiting Kunark, be sure to read their zone guides!
  • EQ Maps - contains not only maps and travel guides, but item and NPC listings as well. Many parts of this site require a flashlink subscription to access, but the content is definitely worth the fee.
  • EQ Portal - Are you positive there's an EQ site out there that contains information you need or want, but can't find it anywhere? You may be able to find a link to it off this site.
  • Still can't find the site you're looking for? EQ's Best Link Site may have what you need.
  • Seekers of Lore - a guild that seeks out knowledge of the world of Norrath, their site contains maps, quest information and more
  • The EQ Links is a good reference material to find links to guides, quests, items, etc. A lot of research has gone into linking to some of the best EQ information on the net. Additionally, they provide direct links to some game-specific news posts that should be of interest to many.
  • EQ Convention is a site where tradesfolk can set up "booths" to advertise their player-made wares - it was a good idea, but at last check it really hasn't gotten off the ground
  • The Newbie Zone - a message board and site set aside for newbies and those who wish to help them learn about EQ
  • Off-Line has some lovely RP t-shirts, including some EQ-specific ones ("Got Clarity?") (updated link May 7)
  • Library of Arcane Knowledge - translations of many in-game texts

Specialized EQ links:

  • NEW! Tradeskill Fashions is a lovely new site with screenshots of folks modeling various player-made items
  • Raskii's tailored armor list contains a search database of tailored items by race and class
  • Everquest Monks is a monk-specific message board.
  • Monkly Business is a great monk site with up-to-date news and information of interest to monks
  • EQ Casters Realm has great information on spell research, etc. for pure casters. They also have some tradeskill info, but since these aren't the prime focus of the site, you might want to check here first for up-to-date recipes.
  • Mythiran Tower is another site that provides recipes for spell research, among other things. They also have message boards for those trying to get niggling questions answered, trade words for runes, etc.
  • Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium contains a lot of wonderful information for tinkers
  • Oggok - The Ogre City has much information of interest to all you misunderstood ogres out there.
  • Jezra may be gone, but the Tal Kor Jewelry Academy goes on! This lovely jewelry resource has been adopted by EQTraders Corner and will slowly be updated with all the jewelcraft changes that have happened since it's creator left EQ. (For now, at least, I want to leave it with the look and feel of the original Academy.)
  • Aoenla's Fletching Guide contains a lot of wonderful information on making bows and arrows.
  • The Rangers Glade is a lovely resource for rangers.
  • The Smithy is a site dedicated to the smiths of Norrath. It's still getting off the ground, but it's looking good so far.
  • The Armigers of Mirth guild members really like their drinking. They like it so much that they've put together a lovely resource listing taverns and what they sell.
  • The Safehouse is a very important and active resource for all the rogues ... erm ... scouts ... out there.
  • Kiky's Poisonous Pages do a lot better job at providing poison recipes and info than my site does.
  • EQ Druids is a lovely druid-only reference site
  • The EC Tunnel is an EZBoard similar to our EQ Traders Corner Marketplace message boards, for buying and selling in-game items for other in-game items. Unlike my boards, they allow selling of non-tradeskill items. ;)
  • You may have heard of the TV show "The Iron Chef", but have you seen "The Iron Chef of Norrath"? This is a lovely little baking spoof.
  • The Sledgling contains "patchly news" from the lands of Norrath, most notably, the text from any of the town newspapers.
  • The Ubersmith contains information specifically about the forging and dyeing of fine plate armor
  • The Shaman's Crucible contains information of interest to the shamen of Norrath
  • EQ Diva! A gathering place for the bards of Norrath
  • The Clockwork Tinkering Guide has some lovely database features for tinkers who wish to learn more about specific aspects of their craft
  • The Champions of Cabilis - this Iksar-only guild on Nameless has a wonderful quest section for all the iksar-related quests.
  • Gumkak's Stuffz an' Tinkz - Gumkak the troll has put together a lot of wonderful information, especially regarding jewelry trivials.
  • EQ Fashion - a new site with screen shots of various EQ fashions, including some player-made gear.

Other worlds: DAoC

  • Dark Ages of Camelot is a fantastic new MMORPG that has stolen a chunk of the DenMother's attention
  • The Camelot Herald - get the latest news, right from the source, including guild realm war rankings
  • Lum the Mad did a lovely guide for DAoC tradeskills
  • DAoC Tradeskills - a rapidly-growing site with an active (and moderated) message board. It definitely gets my seal of approval!
  • The Camelot Guild - from the folks who brought you EQ'Lizer. Quests, item databases, and more!
  • The Camelot Vault - from the folks who brought you EQ Vault. Be forewarned that their message boards are a tad ... chaotic ... if you're used to the moderation and house rules from this site.
  • Camelot Lore is another new DAoC site

Non-gaming Links - the EQTC Community helping the RL world at large

  • Help find the cure for cancer while you're playing EQ. Join Intel and United Devices in the Volunteer Your PC project, searching for a cure for cancer. Once you join up and download the program, feel free to join the EQ Traders Corner Team. DenMother tested and approved - you'll find me listed on the team as GypsyLynx)
  • Care2 has Race for the Big Cats and Race for the Rainforest, operated on the same principle of sponsor donations per click to the site. Another lovely way to donate with only a small expense of time per day.
