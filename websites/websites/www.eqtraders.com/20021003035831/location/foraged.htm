Zone Specific Foraged Goodies

Items on the "All Norrath" table can be foraged in pretty much any zone, by any person with the forage skill. Various zones will also produce a few extra surprises, many of which can be sold for money, some of which are used in quests, and some of which can be used for brewing, baking, cultural smithing, or other tradeskills.
