Zone Specific Foraged Goodies

Items on the "All Norrath" table can be foraged in any zone, by any person with the forage skill. However, druids and rangers with a forage skill of over 100, and iksar (with their racial foraging abilities) can often find that various zones will produce a few extra surprises, many of which can be sold for money, some of which are used in quests, and some of which can be used for brewing, baking or cultural smithing.

Many thanks to Jarno Wiggletoes on Tunare for the intial work on this information
