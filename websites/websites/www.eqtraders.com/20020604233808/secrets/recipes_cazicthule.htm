Cazic-Thule Recipes

The Cazic Thule zone was massively changed on March 7, 2002. Along with new, nastier mobs, there were new recipe books that dropped. All require drops from the Cazic Thule zone, and most need either more translating, or the drops are missing/too rare for us to find. Until things are sorted out, this will be a TEMPORARY holding spot for the recipes.

Below are the pertinent comments from books 2 through 5. (Book 1 contained no tradeskill information.) As you can see, much more research is needed before we have these recipes nailed down. We've had confirmation for some of the drops, but still not all of them (no hides/pelts have been found, there are issues with the blood, the drop rate on the meat is reported as having, well, dropped ... also unsure if the chunk of raw silver is used for jewelcraft, or if the ore is MIA)

Further discussion on these can currently be found on this message board thread.

Baking - all recipes verified

  • Day 5. Our rations are low. Kinopio bless his brave gnomish soul, dared to step outside the room today. He was gone for some time, and came back with - well, with a dead Tae Ew runtling. The door is secured again. I wonder if we dare cook this lizard?
  • Day 6. Hunger is a cruel thing. Kinopio went to work on cooking the runtling this morning. It tastes so good! Cooked on a skewer with a little spice and some sauce, the lizard meat is most pleasing. Kinopio ate the meat rolled on a batwing - such an odd gnome
  • Day 8. (more) Todays meal consited of a raw lizard sandwich. Revolting
  • Day 10. (more) Our meal today consisted of a soup. Lizard meat in water from the drain, with a little sauce, in Kinopios pot. Ugh.
  • Day 12. Woke in the middle of the night to Kinopio screaming. I must admit that my dreams have not been so pleasent lately either. In a fit of creativity, Kinopio created us a crude pie made from now-festering lizard meat in a little dough in his pie tin
  • Day 17. (more) I cooked lizard in a little frosting with spice - superb

translating the above, we get a few Tae Ew baking recipes. Note that the meat is called "Tae Ew Meat" and it does not stack (the finished products stack, once they are baked).

  • Tae Ew Skewers: chunk of Tae Ew meat no stack), spices, jug of sauce, combine in spit or oven with skewers (yield 2, hearty meal, trivial <=162)
  • Tae Ew Roll: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), bat wing, combine in spit or oven (miraculous meal, trivial >163)
  • Tae Ew Sandwich: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), bread, combine in spit or oven (feast)
  • Tae Ew Soup: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), water flask, jug of sauces, combine in spit or oven with a pot (yield 1, miraculous meal, weight 1.0)
  • Tae Ew Pie = chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), clump of dough, combine in spit or oven with a pie tin (miraculous meal, yield 2, weight 1.0 ... cookie cutter icon?!)
  • Candied Tae Ew: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), frosting, spice, combine in spit/oven (miraculous meal, yield 8, weight 1.0, trivial >172)

Blacksmithing - NO recipes yet verified

  • Day 13. Kinopion tells me that he used to be a smith of some reknown in his youth. I find it easy to believe. He spent all afternoon laying out plans for a set of chainmail: A mold, a vial of distilled blood, and several sheets of metal. If only we had sheets of metal!
  • Day 17. Though ill, Kinopio has gone to work with a will. Using my lizard-blood temper, he hammered two pristine lizard scales togeather to form a large plate - it looks exceptionally tough. Perhaps we can use it to escape. (more)
  • (Spanners book) This book is property of Spanner Scrapsnatcher. Hands off!!
    • Making a long sword - so nice. So very nice! With a few molds, a sheet of metal, and a little lizard blood temper, I created one in just an afternoon! Use a long blade mold, a hilt mold, a pommel mold, otherwise it won’t sit right in your hand. You can try it with a heavy steel blade mold, or a dagger mold instead if you like. I bet they work just as well.
    • The Swampwood staves are great things. Take a sheet of metal, and wrap it around using a little of that lizard-blood temper (Tastes nice too) and hammerhead mold and you’ve got a really handy sledgehammer! I use mine to keep my tent pegs in the ground I suppose even a mace head mold would work if you were that way inclined. Mind you, who wants a mace when you can have a good gnomish hammer.
    • Now then, shields. Shields are tricky things. Big, heavy beasts they are - not so kindly if you're not so tall. I reckon with a few sheets of metal, some of that tasty lizard-blood temper, a mold and my smiting hammer that I could knock up a shield. I'll give it a go! I hate this jungle. - Spanner
  • Day 20. Kinopio showed me plans for a suit of full plate armour based on the lizard plating that he constructed several days ago. With a smithing hammer, flask of water, leather padding, mold and several of the lizard platings, he claims to be able to make anything.

Going to be a lot of guesswork, but here's the rough translations to start:

  • chainmail: chainmail pattern, vial of distilled blood (see below), varying numbers of "sheets of metal." (Possibly the plates mentioned below?)
  • "large plate": lizard-blood temper (see below), 2 pristine lizard scales (scales are no drop, have been found on a level 60 mob)
  • long sword: lizard blood temper, sheet of metal, long blade mold, hilt mold, pommel mold.
  • broadsword: lizard-blood temper, sheet of metal, heavy steel blade mold, hilt mold, pommel mold
  • dagger: lizard-blood temper, sheet of metal, dagger mold, hilt mold, pommel mold
  • sledgehammer: lizard-blood temper, sheet of metal, swampwood staff, hammer head mold
  • mace: lizard-blood temper, sheet of metal, swampwood staff, mace head mold
  • shield: lizard-blood temper, "a few" sheets of metal, smithy hammer, appropriate shield mold (impossible to accurately guess what mold will need how many sheets of metal. Shield recipes that we currently have vary wildly: 1 sheet, 2 sheets, 1 folded sheet, 2 MQ sheets, 1 MQ folded sheet)
  • full plate armour: lizard plating, smithy hammer, water flask, leather padding, mold (varying numbers of plating based on piece)

Brewing - ONE recipe verified

  • Day 9. Kinopio discovered a barrel suitable for brewing, in one of the piles that line the walls, using two vials of runtlings blood and a single vial of distilled mana, I managed to distill the substance into a think viscous fluid.I shall investigate tomorrow
  • Day 14. What a day. It started well-from a scale temper and vial of lizards blood (Thankfully still in good condition after all this time due to my preservations) I created a lizard blood temper. Then Kinopio passed out, he shows no signs of consciousness

translations:

  • vial of distilled blood: 2 vials of runtling blood, vial of distilled mana (requires 2 sapphires per vial) - unverified, no sign of runtling blood has been found
  • lizard-blood temper (NO DROP): scale temper , 1 Tae Ew Blood (NO DROP, NO STACK), trivial <153

Jewelcrafting - NO recipes verified

  • Day 2. Within the mounds of jumble I found a strange ore. It seems local to this area's geology - and has fascinating properties. Using my firestaff as a makeshift forge I found that two lumps of this ore could be enchanted with a vile of distilled mana - interesting.
  • Day 4. The enchanted ore seems to bond well with certain jewels I have with me: Diamonds, blue diamonds, sapphires, black sapphires, and rubies. I shall note these for further investigation on our return to the academy.

Whee, this one will be expensive ...

Tailoring - NO recipes verified

  • Day 7. Gods, a week has passed. Kinopios nightmares are worse. They are effecting his judgement. My day was spent curing the hide of the runtling - a long process, using a vial of the poor creatures blood the result is a hard leather. Perhaps I can put it to use.
  • Day 8. Kinopio is restless, so I put him to work with the cured hide. He has hidden talents: From one section of hide he created a tough belt for himself, and from another section he produced a gorget. Amazing. (more)
  • Day 10. Kinopios skills know no bound. Using the distilled blood and two sections of hardened hide, he created a backpack the likes of which I have never seen before. From two portions of the distilled blood, a section of hide, and a whip pattern of his own he even made a rudimentary weapon. (more)
  • Day 11. The day went slowly. Kinopio and I discussed possible uses for this cured hide. He seems to think that one could fashion an entire suit of armor from it, simply using sections of cured hide in various quantity with appropriate pattern. I slept most of the day.
  • Day 21. Kinopio woke before me and ran out of the door. He was gone all morning, and appeared soaked in blood and sweat in the afternoon. Locking the door behind him he sat in a corner, staring into space, clutching a strange silk substance in his hand.
  • Day 22. Kinopio sleeps fitfully. From two of the stands of the silk, smeared in some form of unidentifiable ichor, I was able to make a crude swatch of silk. From whatever creature this originates, I would rather not know - it is strong, and yet flexible. Inordinately so.
  • Day 23. I fear for Kinopio's mental state, he appears to have gone quite insane. Whilst constantly gibbering about some kind of sacrifice, has shaking hands crafted something from the silk swatch and a pattern of his own making - imagine my suprise when the poor soul presented me with a hand-sewn cap!
  • Day 24. A tranformation has occured in my companion. His former state of incoherence and madness has been replaced by a firm determination. He seems resolved, though in what I cannot say. The cap fits well - it seems that the silk can be used to make other garments.


Translations ... calling these "runtling hides" until someone actually sees a pelt drop and can give me a name for it (no confirmed drops of pelts or hides in any form, at this time) ...

  • cured hide: runtling hide, lizard blood
  • cured armor: cured hide(s), pattern (varying numbers of hides, all guesses now)
  • backpack: 2 cured hides, vial of distilled blood (and I presume a backpack pattern, though one is not mentioned)
  • whip: 2 vials distilled blood, hide (may or may not be cured, unclear), whip pattern
  • "crude silk swatch": 2 viscid silk swatches - silk drops off jungle hunter spiders in maze - currently DNC. May need some sort of ichor combined with it. Experimentation continues
  • __ silk garments: crude silk swatch, pattern (may or may not require more than one swatch for larger items)
