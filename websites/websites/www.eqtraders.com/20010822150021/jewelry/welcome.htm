Welcome to the Tal Kor Jewelry Academy!

The art of jewelcraft is an expensive, often frustrating, but interesting hobby. While not terribly profitable (and not at all profitable at lower levels), it is still a fascinating trade to pursue and will allow you to make items that are either not readily available from merchants or that are sold by merchants for phenomenal amounts of money. The Academy's purpose is to guide you as you develop your skills.

Within these walls you will find information on jewelry components, gem/metal combinations, and anything else pertaining to jewelcraft. You will also find the approximate cost of jewelry components. Please note that these prices are approximate and will most assuredly vary depending on your charisma and location.

All prices are based on merchants from the various cities.
