Cultural Armor Fashion Guide

Many people are eager to see what the different cultural armors look like. The creation of a single piece of cultural armor is a difficult, expensive, and time-consuming process, making the cultural armor quite a rarity in the worlds of Norrath. Those intrepid master smiths and tailors who have succeeded in creating their race's special armor should be applauded! A big thank you goes out to all of the folks who have submitted items for this section, both for their ventures into this expensive craft, as well as for generously sharing their discoveries with the rest of us!

If you have a screenshot of cultural armor that we're missing, and would like to share it, click here to submit it! PLEASE do not send screen shots of dyed fine steel plate to the above address, use fashion@eqtraders.com instead, as it's being handled by a different person.
