Wood Elf Cultural Tradeskills

Note that these recipes are for wood elves only. Unless otherwise noted, fletching must be done using the feir'dal fletching kit, tailoring must be done in the feir'dal sewing kit, and smithing must be done using the feir'dal forge (illusions won't fool these containers, you have to be a reall, 100% wood elf to use them). This includes the May 8 upgrade to wood elf cultural tradeskills. (NPC text for the upgrade can be found at the bottom of the page)

Some of the older cultural smithing recipes had their trivials changed during the June 5 patch.

NPC text from May 8 patch:

You say, 'Hail, Cerila Windrider'

Cerila Windrider says ''Greetings, Steamwalker. You come at an important time for the Faydark and its inhabitants.' She regards you with distant eyes, as if weighing your qualities in her mind. 'Maesyn has authorised me to tell you of [armor] and [elven fletching].''

You say, 'what about the armor?'

Cerila Windrider says ''As you may be aware, violence is at our very door of late. The defenders of the Faydark need to protection from this evil. In your Feir'dal Sewing Kit, use embroidering needle, excellent sabretooth hide, oak bark tanin, imbued emerald, a pattern and an appropriate [weave].''

You say, 'what weave?'

Cerila Windrider says ''For making boned leather, work a small brick of mithril and some [celestial temper] in the Feir'dal forge, using a file. For chain links, with which to sew into a chainweave piece, work a large brick of mithril and more temper with a smithy hammer.''

You say, 'what celestial temper?'

Cerila Windrider says ''This is a mystery to us. The Faydark Council of Elders convened just last week to discuss a stranger that had been spotted by the Faydark Champions. Before exiling him from our sacred forest, we took from him a small number of tempers, though we cannot make our own. You shall have to find this out for yourself, Steamwalker.''
