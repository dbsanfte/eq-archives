Freeport Cleric Armor Quest

This is one of several armor quests for younglings that was recently added. This one is for Freeport human clerics, and will be HUM/CLR, no drop and lore (other than bracer)

The following items must be combined in the Gem Encrusted Mail Kit obtained by talking to Salinsa Delfdosan outside the Temple of Marr.

Each item is made from the special material obtained by combining the correct items in the kit, and the specific mold, also obtained from Salinsa Delfdosan, combined in a forge. Trivial <=21 Smithing skill or possibly non-failable. Armor is grey with a slight purple tinge.

Note: *'d items are NO DROP.
