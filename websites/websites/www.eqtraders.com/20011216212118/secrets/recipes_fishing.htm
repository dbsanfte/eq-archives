Fishing "Recipes"

With the addition of a merchant-sold tackle box and hooks, experimentation began on various recipes using the tacklebox (look in baking and brewing for other recipes using fish). Skill checks for making these are based on fishing skill. At this time, there is no known benefit to using fishing lures, as they are used up exactly like bait during fishing.

You want to do what with your fish?! (Also known as "odd things you can make in your tacklebox". The fish listed are all zone-specific fish. The skinning knife listed is made by a blacksmith)
