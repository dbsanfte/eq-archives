Hail and well met, citizens of Norrath! Do you want to learn a trade skill? Do you want to sell the fruits of your labor to other players? Are you trying to find specific items to pratice your craft? Or are you just trying to find someone who sells player-crafted items for a reasonable price on your server? If the answer to any of these questions is "yes", then I hope this site will be of assistance to you. As the game changes and grows, so too will this site, so remember to check back often.

 

10/16: Find that Dye!
(From Niami) Thanks to feedback from a lot of great folks on the message board, there's now a page with more details on finding ground spawns for dye components, including /loc information for evergreen leaves. A few more fishing lures were also confirmed. As always, there's lots of other small changes to numerous pages as we correct/confirm more recipes, and find out stats on more of the new smithing items.

10/13: Smithed Armor and Weapon Stats
(From Niami) Known stats for smithed armor and weapons have been broken out into separate pages by popular demand. More information is needed for many items, but it's at least a start.
