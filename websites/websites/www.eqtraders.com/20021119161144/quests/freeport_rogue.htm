Freeport Rogue Armor

Much like other "newbie" zone revamps, this is a quest for young Freeport rogues who wish to make armor. Armor is lore (other than bracer), no drop, HUM, ROG only. It has a dark leather graphic when worn. Please note that while the bracers are not lore, the bracer materials often are, so make your first bracer before you get the parts for a second one

Hail Tovan Tenlah, he mentions something about needing proper equipment for my employment as a Coalition Trader. He handed me a note to give to Venora Rankin, another new npc a few rooms away.

Upon handing the message into her, she tells me that I can make all sorts of new armors for myself, and gives me an 8 slot curing kit. Much like the new Dark Elf armor quests in neriek, each time you ask what <armor piece?> you're given a nodrop lore pattern and told what you need for ingredients.
