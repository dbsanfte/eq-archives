Xanthe's Earring/PoTC- The Gifts of the Halflings

The last three gifts come to us from halfling lands. Original writeup courtesy of Xanthe Ravenwolf. Known trivials are now included, as well as links to the various sources for ingredients.

First Gift of the Halflings

Lotus Pie

  • Lotus Pie (miraculous meal, CHA 9) = Thick Lotus Paste + Clump of Dough + Pie Tin (oven/spit, baking trivial 236, yield 6 pies)

Other combines/items that you will need

  • Thick Lotus Paste (meal, CHA 9) = 1 Sweet Lotus Sap (*or* sticky lotus sap *or* soft lotus petals *or* black lotus stem) + Allspice + Flour + Cream (oven/spit, baking trivial >101 <=141)
  • Cream = Benzoin + 2 Milk (unstacked) + Dairy Spoon (mixing bowl, baking trivial 83)
  • Dairy Spoon = 2 Metal Bits (unstacked) + Scalar Mold + water (forge, smithing trivial 67)
  • clump of dough = 1 Egg of whatever kind + Milk + Flour (mixing bowl, baking trivial 17)
  • Pie tin can be either smithed or made via pottery

(sweet lotus sap, sticky lotus sap, black lotus stem and soft lotus petals are all dropped by the deep cavern lotuses in Fungus Grove. Drops off the cave lotus in Dulak will NOT work)

 

Second Gift of the Halflings

Note that halfings REALLY love their food, and many of the below recipes yield more than one on a success. Given the difficulty of the final combine, this is a good thing, as you will likely lose many to failures, but, for example, don't go overboard when making marmalade, or you'll find that you have no room for the rest of the ingredients. Also the picnic is a large item, so keep room in one of your bigger containers free for it.

Misty Thicket Picnic

  • Misty Thicket Picnic (miraculous meal, STR 5, STA 5, WIS 5, INT 5, size: large) = 1 Picnic Basket + 1 Marmalade Sandwich + 1 Jumjum Spiced Beer + 1 Jumjum Salad + 1 Royal Mints + 1 Mature Cheese + 2 (unstacked) Jumjum Cake Slices (oven, baking trivial >250, yield 4 picnics)

Other combines/items that you will need

  • Picnic Basket = 1 Woven Mandrake + 1 Steel Boning (sewing kit, tailoring trivial 76, yield 10)
  • Woven Mandrake = 2 Mandrake Roots (unstacked) (sewing kit, tailoring trivial 66)
  • Steel Boning = 1 Small Brick of Ore + 1 File + water (forge, smithing trivial 37)
  • Marmalade Sandwich = 1 Apricot Marmalade + 1 Loaf of Bread (mixing bow, baking trivial 99, yield 12 sandwiches)
  • Apricot Marmalade = 3 Frosting (unstacked) + 1 Fruit (oven, baking trivial 99, yield 6 marmalade)
  • Jumjum Spiced Beer = Barley + Hops + Spices + Water + Bottle + Jumjum Stalk (brew barrel, brewing trivial 162, yield 10 beer)
  • Jumjum Salad = 1 Vegetables + 1 Lettuce + 1 Fennel + 1 Jumjum Stalk (mixing bowl, baking trivial 142, yield 10 salads)
  • Royal Mints = Eucalyptus + Frosting (mixing bowl, baking trivial 76, yield 10 mints)
  • Mature Cheese = Rennet + 2 Milk (unstacked) + Dairy Spoon (mixing bowl, baking trivial 84, yield 10 cheese)
  • Dairy Spoon = 2 Metal Bits (unstacked) + Scalar Mold + water (forge, smithing trivial 86)
  • Jumjum Cake Slice = 1 Jumjum Cake (oven/spit, baking trivial <=152, yields 10 slices)
  • Jumjum Cake = Jumjum Stalk + Frosting + Clump of Dough + Winter Chocolate + Cake Round (spit/oven, baking trivial 166, yield 20 cakes)
  • Winter Chocolate = 1 Brownie Parts + 2 Frosting (unstacked) (mixing bowl, baking trivial 95)
  • brownie parts drop off pretty much any brownie (Lesser Faydark, Greater Faydark, Steamfont?), as well as from Nillipus in Rivervale


Third Gift of the Halflings

Lucky Cabbage

  • Lucky Cabbage (meal, CHA 10) = 1 Wild Cabbage + Blessed Dust of Karana (mixing bowl, baking trivial 182)

Other combines that you will need

  • Blessed Dust of Karana = 1 Imbued Plains Pebble + Celestial Essence + Jar of Acid (kiln, pottery trivial 103)
  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
