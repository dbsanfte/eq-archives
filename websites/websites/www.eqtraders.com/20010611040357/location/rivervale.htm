Rivervale Trade Supplies

City evaluation: Fishing, basic tailoring, basic brewing (i.e., bog juice) and basic cooking can be performed by those who live in Rivervale and travel to the neighboring zones of Misty Thicket and Kithicor Forest.  All other trade skills require that you travel further afield, through zones not necessarily suited for younglings.

Baking

  • oven - /loc -188, -157; /loc -75, -420
  • milk - Wibble Bramblebush (next to dock)
  • fish - Fiddy Bobbick (next to dock), dock merchant (214, -223)
  • (other cooking supplies in Misty Thicket, bread in Kithicor Forest)

Brewing

  • brew barrel - next to Meeka Diggs (near Fools Gold), on second floor of Fools Gold
  • bottle - Wibble Bramblebush (next to dock)
  • bottle, cask - bag merchant (across river from dock, 214, -223)
  • kiola sap, bottle, cask, shot glass, grapes - Mac Deeppockets & Flyndia Deeppockets (Fools Gold)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Fiddy Bobbick and Wibble Bramblebush (next to dock), dock merchant (across river from dock, 214, -223)

Fletching, Jewelcraft

  • none found

Pottery

  • pottery wheel - /loc -159.08, -77.75
  • clay and other supplies in Misty Thicket
  • pottery wheel & kiln - /loc 391.65, -468.49 (near deputy Shelmy)

Smithing

  • forge - next to druid's guild
  • Imbue Peridot [Bristlebane] - Blippo Stumbletoe (Cleric guild)
  • small chainmail patterns - Charlotte Diggs (-180, -230, near Kevlin Diggs)
  • (banded armor molds in Misty Thicket)

Tailoring

  • basic armor patterns, small sewing kit, Leathers of the Vale - Twippie Diggs (near Fools Gold)
  • small sewing kit, recipe book for silk thread, etc. - Meeka Diggs (near Fools Gold)
  • 10-slot loom - next to Meeka Diggs (loc 0, -234)
