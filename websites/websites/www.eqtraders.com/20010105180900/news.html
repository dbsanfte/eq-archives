When the smithing changes are implemented, I will need help! A lot of the new smithing recipes are going to use materials not currently on the live servers. When they go live, merchant inventories will also change ... across the entire world. Anyone who wants to "adopt" a zone to re-explore, please remember the following. If you do NOT give me all the correct information, then it will take me even longer than normal to run around and re-verify stuff -- which means it takes longer to be posted on this site. For ANY report of new merchants, new items on merchants, etc., I need 1) zone name, 2) merchant name, 3) merchant /loc, as well as any landmarks, 4) exact item names. Sending me something that says: "The windmill in the Lake of Ill Omen has poison stuff" just means that I have to spend time to get one of my personas over to that zone and then figure out where this doggone windmill is located. ;) It's still nice to get the head's up, but it adds a couple days of delay onto anything being posted, as I have to add it to the pile of things "to do" for the site ... and for those not familiar with the force behind this site, it's a single-person operation that costs me a lot of my personal time and pp to maintain. If you find the info on this site useful, and you want to contribute, jotting down that extra information requested above and sending it to me means that I can free up more of my time on some of the more difficult or more expensive information to verify.
