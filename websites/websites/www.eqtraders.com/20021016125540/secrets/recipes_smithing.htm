Blacksmithing Recipes

 

Many smithing trivials changed due to patches in spring/summer 2002, and we do not yet know all of the new trivials. Please remember with these, and all other trivial listings on the site that: "<=" means less than or equal to -- all we know about the trivial is that someone of X skill attempted the item and it was already trivial. It may be that the trivial is 1 point below it, or it may be 100 points below. The only way we find such trivials is by folks reporting in their skill levels and trivial status after they make combine attempts. Similarly, if you see ">", it means that someone at Y skill attempted the combine, and it was not trivial. Again, there's no way to easily tell if the trivial is 1 point or 100 points above that in trivial unless folks actually report in their results.

  • The Basics - recipes for blocks, bricks, sheets, etc. of various metals, as well as rings, bits, boning and chain jointing
  • Tools and Implements - files, scalers, containers, baking supplies, etc.
  • Weapons - all non-cultural weapons
  • Armor - all non-cultural armor (banded, ornate chain, fine steel plate)
  • Cultural Smithing - race-restricted smithing recipes
  • Dyes - armor dyes for use on fine steel plate armor only (no other armor will accept a dye)
  • Tempers - brewing recipes for use in cultural smithing (the brewing portion is NOT race-restricted)
  • Smithed Armor Stats - stats for normal and cultural armors. If you have stats for something not listed here, please post to the smithing message boards
  • Smithed Weapon Stats - stats for normal and cultural weapons. Again, if you have stats for something not listed here, please post to the smithing message boards

Questions? - If you're having problems figuring out things like imbuing, try the basic Q&A on cultural and advanced smithing first, then try the smithing message boards
