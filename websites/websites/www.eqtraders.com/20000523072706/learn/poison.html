Make Poison - by Chazon Shadowstalker, Barbarian Rogue (Tarew Marr)

(When this was written, the patch containing poison for the rogues was only two days old. Since then, there have been no new submissions for this section. I'm still hoping some rogue out there will submit an article soon.)

In order to make poison you need a mortar/pestle, a poison sac, suspension, and a vial. Different sacs create different poison. Vials are not sold, and only pottery experts can make. This aspect doesn't seem to be fully implemented yet, and I will update you when I can, as well as the different poisons that can be made.

The the book, mortar/pestle, and suspension are found in West Karanas on a vendor called Gindlin Toxfodder. I have also hear of a dwarf vendor on the island in Dagnor's Cauldron, but have not confirmed this.

 

EQPortal has a good page on making poison for those who need further details.
