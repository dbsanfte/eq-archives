PoK Fishing Quest

(Note that this quest requires the Planes of Power expansion.) Find Ramos Jerwan in the Plane of Knowledge (a barbarian near a pol at loc -31.28 1431) and hail him.

"Hail Ramos Jerwan"

Good Day Traveler... perhaps you have come to help me with my studies?

"I can help you with your studies"

It's quite simple really. I'm interested in studying some aquatic specimens from around the planes....

"What hand?"

There are some particularly interesting chichlid swiming about in the Plane of Nightmare. I also hear the discus in the Plane of Valor are quite Plentiful. I need you to bring me two sets of bones from each for study. You do know how to extract them, yes? If not, I believe the lovely Peras Glickon may have something that can instruct you . Best of luck lad.

Nightmare cichlid are fished in Plane of Nightmare, and are prepared as per our standard prepared fish recipe. (fish, roots [foraged], celandine herb [from alchemy vendor], filleting knife [smithed]). No details are available yet on the Valor discus, so the quest reward remains unknown at this time.
