Velium Leathers

These recipes MUST be combined in the Coldain Tanners Kit

These things really are that ludicrously insane to make. The failure rates are high, the expenses are higher. That having been said, there's some nice pieces in every set.

The links below will guide you to more information about the various pieces to the recipe: the recipes for drake egg oil, yew leaf tannin, cod oil, velium studs and boning, drop information on hides, etc. If you have other questions, come visit the tailoring message boards. If your question isn't already answered there in a recent post, speak up and folks should be glad to help.

[Yes, I truly do find it is necessary to spell this out this clearly, after seeing the same questions in my mailbox and on the boards over and over.]
