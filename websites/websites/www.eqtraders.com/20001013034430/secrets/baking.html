Trivial listings from several sources are varying widely, either Verant has been changing them lately, or trivials are not set in stone - rather, perhaps they are affected by your race/class/intelligence, or some other undetermined faction. When two sources I trust give me two numbers that vary 5 points from each other for the same item, I'm beginning to feel my suspicions are correct. I still want to know when something trivials for you, but am going to change trivials to a range of numbers, when I'm given more than one number.

As with ALL trade skills, be very sure that a) you do NOT stack any items when combining, and, b) you use water flasks (from a merchant), not summoned or foraged water
