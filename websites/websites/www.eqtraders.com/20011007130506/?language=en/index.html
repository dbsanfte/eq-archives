August 29, 2001

I just got the following in email and wanted to forward it on to everyone:

GU Comics closes it's doors.

After more than a year and some 18 million+ page views, I feel that GU Comics has run it's course. There are a ton of people I'd like to simultaneously thank and say goodbye to; but for the sake of brevity, I'll do that else where and at another time.

I would like to thank Everlore at this time though. They were wonderful hosts for my little web comic. I appreciate all the hard work they put into keeping GU live. You guys own. Thanks.

And to the fans... I love you guys. At the darkest moments and the lowest point you were there to lift me up and support my work. I enjoyed the time you shared with me, and I hope I brought atleast one smile to you. And, hopefully helped you realize that EQ is a game... and that it's okay to take yourself lightly.

Thanks again and maybe I'll be seeing you around.

-Woody Hearn - Former Cartoonist GU Comics

Your humor and art will be missed, Woody, but I can't say that I blame you at all. My best wishes to you, and I hope we do see you around!

- Niami

8/15/01 - Patch Day
Imbued field plate boots are supposed to be working as of today - I'll try to get some updates done later today once folks have a chance to test them and report back with stats. ... Now, if only we could get imbued armor, any imbued armor, for some of the forgotten deities, such as my own Bristlebane! ;) {said very tongue-in-cheek, I know there's lots going on}

The village newspapers have brought forth information on another new recipe, this one for vegetable roughy. Again, site updates will happen later in the day.

I was getting pretty antsy without my /gu comics fix until Woody gave me a happier URL for it. For those of you used to hitting http://www.gucomics.com, try http://gucomics.everlore.com for your humor fix. I'm really burnt out on EQ, tradeskills and the site, and am going to be taking it easy for a while longer, trying to remember where the "fun" part of all this is. (Yes, the tell bombs are still bad, and most folks think that even the simplest question requires 10 tells to dance around the subject before they come to the point, adding to the load of spam scrolling across my screen, and making it hard for me to concentrate on everything else going on ... like raids that they've interrupted.) I'm not running away yet, I'm not closing down the site or anything silly like that, but I'm going to take my grumpy redheaded self and hide a lot until I can become ungrumpy again. :)

7/24/01 - Patch Day
Top news from the patch message is that non-quest pelts will be stackable, and that two of the deity-specific pottery idols have been changed. (We know some non-quest tailoring pelts were overlooked, but we're not sure if it was accident or intent - I've submitted an enquiry) about it. At least one of the idols we consider "bugged" has not been fixed (Rallos Zek), and another (Cazic Thule) had a couple surplus classes removed from the list of who could wield. More details as they become available.

New issues of the newspapers should be available in all starting cities. Please use this message board thread to track anything regarding tradeskills that is found in local papers.

Site updates have been slow due to an RL death in the family last week. I hope to get a bit more caught up on information from the boards, screen shots, etc., as time goes on. So far, the biggest news item since the last front page update, was information on three different imbued versions of the erudite steelsilk armor.

7/10/01 - Patch Day
As usual, I'm going to be swamped, the boards are going to be VERY busy, and very slow. It looks to be a good patch day tradeskill-wise, but it's going to take time for us to verify everything mentioned in the patch message ... and the stuff that wasn't mentioned. :)

On the confirmed list, we've got the new weight stats for the black panther armor (two pieces stayed at their old weight of 0.3, all others had their weight reduced), the ice silk lined slippers had their stats updated, spider silk and silk swatches are now stackable, and at least some types of sheet metal and folded sheets of metal are stackable (we've not yet tested all the racial sheets yet). More information will be on the message boards as the day goes on. Meanwhile, I'm going to be working on editing and submitting screenshots to EQ'Lizer and Alla'Khazam's, so that they have the updated stats on the changed tailored items ... and maybe get some more sleeeeeeep.

Nothing too earth-shattering to report with this patch. Pottery idols are now equippable, for the most part, by the proper races and classes. (I've added in a stats page for the idols, and we're slowly filling in the blanks on it.) One new baking recipe (orange flavored cod) was found in the village newspapers.

Administrivia:

  • There are still folks directly emailing me with questions about things that are better posted to the message boards where they'll get responses from more folks. Please, folks, use the boards. 05/30/01
Patch Day. As usual, this means I'll be really busy once the servers come back up, seeing what's changed on the live servers. Also, as usual, it means the message boards will be VERY slow loading, as it's likely to hit the 20K hits in a single day mark today. Patience, grasshoppers. I'll get updates up as able.

I normally don't talk a lot about what's going on over on the Test server, though we randomly get updates from the Test tradesfolk when something is coming through that will impact the trade community. After all, it is Test, and things can/will/do change often on Test before they hit the live servers. (And I absolutely refuse, under any situation to post recipes/stats on the main part of my site until it's verified as "live".) However, an accomplishment last night on Test deserves a little cheering. The fleeting quiver has been very much a "holy grail" type item for tailors and archers for 2 years now, and finally, the recipe was put in place on Test. Given the difficulty of obtaining the needed ingredients on Test, and the fact that there is a much smaller tradesfolk community on Test, extra kudos go to Uind and Gyodokan for reporting the first known success on a fleeting quiver on Test last night about half an hour before the servers went down. Well done! I'm sure the folks on the live servers will quickly follow suit once the servers are back up. :)

Addendum: The new imbued pottery idols are in-game. Recipe is on the pottery recipes page and has been verified as functional. Discussion and stats are on the message board. Snake meat can now be pickled and made into steaks. Another fish recipe (marinated muskie) has been found that trivials at 142. The fleeting quiver recipe is live, and folks who have tried it today confirm that it's not trivial at skill 209.

05/22/01
Tailoring Recipes Found. My thanks to Suppina as well as a large number of other folks who took the challenge of the missing recipes and ran with it. We have stats, plus rough trivial levels for an entire new set of silk armor (Wu's Fighting Armor) that will get tailors into the 150-range with their tailoring skill. You folks on the message boards have done the EQ tradeskill community proud with your cooperation and information sharing! My one comment on all this, though, is that they just recently stated that the Coldain Tanners Kit could be used for ALL recipes other than the cultural tailoring ... but they don't have enough slots in them for the 3-silk pieces of this armor. Hmmmmmm ... Are tailors stuck needing multiple sewing kits ... again?

Last week, Aragir also posted results on the enchanted Antonian longswords. It seems we had the right recipes all along -- sort of. (The "ruby" and "emerald" mentioned by the NPC turned out to be "star ruby" and "fire emerald", respectively)

New baking discoveries seem to have trickled to a halt for the moment, but it's likely we'll figure out a few more once the dust settles.

Potters are eyeing a couple new items on Test with some interest. There's a lot of speculation going on, but we'll just have to bide our time to see what actually makes it to the live servers.
