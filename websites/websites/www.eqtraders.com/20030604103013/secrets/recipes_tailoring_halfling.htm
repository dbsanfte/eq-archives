Halfling Cultural Armor

Everything that mentions "Vale Forge" or "Vale sewing kit" must be combined by a halfling using their cultural Vale Forge and Vale Sewing kit. As with ALL cultural tradeskill recipes, illusions will not work to "fool" a tradeskill container into thinking you are something that you are not.
