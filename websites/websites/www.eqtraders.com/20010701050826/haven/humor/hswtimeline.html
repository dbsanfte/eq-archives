Star Wars EQ - a Timeline
by Robert Spelman
as posted on alt.games.everquest

Day 1: Games fly off the shelves, 150,000 people attempt to logon at once.
Login server, rather than crashing, makes users wait in logon queue so they
don't have to update their network outage page. Also, special "Fiery
Avenger" Lightsaber quest advertised.

 
Day 2: Total chaos as fledgling Wookies make up 45% of the universe
population. Forest world dwellers of Kashyyk have lifespan of .02 seconds.
Most popular trade skill: making laser crossbows and bandoliers. Most
commonly used emote: /growl

 
Day 3: Jedi discover first spell bug: Jedi mind trick erases mob's hate
list, enabling them to KS from anyone.
 

Day 5: Complaints of Yavin, Hoth, Dagobah zones not appearing in game as
promised. Management states Dagobah never promised, Yavin and Hoth will
appear "later".
 

Day 9: Management explains that it's necessary to allow Star Destroyers to
fire through planets in order to keep players from exploiting the
programmer's laziness of not providing proper pathing for their mobs.
 

Day 10: First player kick/banned for ticking off a GM.
 

Day 19: Management denies any bugs in game, yet takes servers off-line for
a 4-hour scheduled maintenance period. 16 hours later, servers re-start.
Message of the day =
 

Day 19.00001: 2.9 million posts appear on alt.games.starwarseq protesting
the nerfing of Wookie beserk, Jedi mind trick, and Droid Lightsaber Toss
abilities.
 

Day 50: Complaints of not being able to find the start of the "Fiery
Avenger" Lightsaber Quest met with puzzlement by management, who claims
it's in there someplace.
 

Day 75: First report of Death Star being killed on a server.
 

Day 76: Secret buffing of Death Star during 3-hour I mean 14-hour scheduled
server downtime.
 

Day 95: First player kick/banned for training Super Star Destroyers to Ord
Mantel to keep high-level players from killing the Interdictors guarding
the Imperial newbie areas.
 

Day 99: Plane of the Dark Side opened.
 

Day 99.2: Plane of the Dark Side closed.
 

Day 99.4: Plane of the Dark Side opened.
 

Day 101: Plane of the Dark Side closed.
 

Day 103-119: Plane of the Dark Side opens and closes, sometimes 3x a day.
Total chaos but great loot if you can get in. Level 1 Sith Lords map the
Plane.
 

Day 120: Death Star and 2nd Death Star killed on every server daily.
 

Day 121: Death Star and 2nd Death Star buffed to 250% during server
downtime.
 

Day 121.00001: 35 50th level players lose their level on Death Star and 2nd
Death Star kill attempts.
 

Day 128: Hoth zone populated at last. 200 Level 20-50 characters arrive and
are all slain by faulty pathing and a Level 75 Wampa spawn at zone line.
 

Day 139: Millenium Falcon spawn campers warned "No more 'Princess Leia
Earmuff' drop after tomorrow. "Leia 'Muffs" Ebay auction bids jump 10-fold.
 

Day 140: Yavin zone populated. Millenium Falcon spawn moved to Yavin, rate
set to Ultra-Rare, appears once every server reboot.
 

Day 175: Jedi Knight armor quest implemented. Quest items too difficult to
obtain, Jedi stuck running around in old bathrobes unless they want to wear
encumbering Stormtrooper Armor (-5 AGI, AC 1 more per piece than Bathrobe).
 

Day 201: Still no sign of the "Fiery Avenger" Lightsaber Quest.
