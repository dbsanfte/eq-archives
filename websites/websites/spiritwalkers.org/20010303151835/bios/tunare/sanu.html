Spiritwalkers Guild Member Bio

Name: Sanu Spiritwalker
Race: Barbarian Northman
Profession: Shaman
Likes: Helping people, not only those of her clan. Learning to listen to the spirits and to learn what wisdom they have to teach her.
Dislikes: Orcs of all kinds, those of the North the most.
Quirks: Has a good sense of humor. Likes to poke fun at her Unkle to make sure he is enjoying life as well, knowing that he is sometimes too serious for his own good.

Sanu is the youngest of three children, the only daughter. When she was around 4 seasons old, her parents were slain by an Orc war party while they were hunting mammoths on the tundra

Before her parents' death, Sanu was a joyful child, bright and eager to learn and full of laughter. Everyone in the village was always glad to see her running around and the guildmasters of the Tribunal were looking forward to Sanu's training.

When her parents died, Sanu withdrew a bit. While it wasn't a total withdrawl, she was no longer as cheery as before. Going to live with her unkle, Sanu learned of the more serious side of life. Her Unkle Mortekai was a kind, but strict man, burdened suddenly with the responsibility of raising three children. She soon came to love her unkle as she had her parents and looks upon him as a role model. As she grew older, the powers of her mother continued to stay dormant and the elders of the Tribunal despaired of ever awakening.

And then, one night, her unkle's power awakened and as he grew in power, so too did Sanu's. Within months, Sanu was following both her mother's and her unkle's footsteps on the Shaman's path.

Since that time, her humor has returned as she had learned there is more beyond death. Her humor is, however, tempered by a wisdom beyond her years. As a result, she tends to be a bit more cautious then her older brother Joril. While her brother will go charging off to kill and Orc, she will bide her time until she is sure of a victory. This is not to say that her hatred of Orcs is less than her brother's. She hates them with a passion, wanted to make them pay for her parent's untimely deaths. However, she wants to make her actions count with her as the victor.

When she became old enough and judged by the Tribunal to have advanced in her training enough, she began her travels and adventures to learn the ways of the world and the ways of the spirits..

As she has advanced in the Circles, Sanu has learned of judgement and that judgement can come from all people, all races. With a bit of urging from her unkle, she has learned not to judge the race, but the individual. As a result, she has found that some of the darker races and professions can serve justice in their own way.

Sanu is tall for her people, almost matching her unkle's height, she is 7'10. She keeps herself fit as it takes both a fit mind and body to be able to listen to the spirits. As well, the constant battle to keep the North free of orcs and goblins gives her plenty of exercise as well. Her dark red hair is kept short for better control in the fierce winds of the North. Her dark blue eyes are filled with humor and a wisdom beyond her age. While she doesn't wear the tattoos generally seen on her people, there is no doubting the power she holds nor the power within her eyes.
