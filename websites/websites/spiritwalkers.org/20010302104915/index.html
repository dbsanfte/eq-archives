January 30, 20001:

  Unkle has tea and biscuts with Tunare!

  Deciding to venture forth into new and wonderous places makes getting your mail a little difficult. Seems Norrathian Post forgot to file the forwarding order from Halas to the city of Thurgadin, but the invitation finally arrived. Mortekai Spiritwalker was granted the honor of an afternoon's tea with Tunare. For a sight of the Goddess, see our Featured Image section.

January 13, 2001:

<sing with me now> They're going to the chapel, and they're gonna get married!

  Congratulations and warm wishes go out to Krystallina and Iothar who will wed this evening at 9pm Eastern. The blessed event will take place at the North Karana brindge, on the top level of the tower. Please join us all in wishing them a great life of wonderful adventure together!

December 23, 2000:

Happy Holidays - From the Spiritwalkers!

  Unkle has been somewhat remiss in updating the web-site, but here's a bit of an update for us all. I still have more work to do, but he's generally lazy, and often busy. Please bear with him as he works on adding content to the site slowly but surely.

  New today, Ixla's New Clothes and some Roster and Biography additions. Check out Ixla's new coat (and a spiffy one it is too!) in the Featured Image!

  Several older Spiritwalkers are currently adventuring in Vellious, feel free to come on down! The fabeled city of Thurgadin is a wonder, and you can find the materials for all trades there. The dwarves, while a bit cold and distant at first have warmed to Unkle's charm, and even enjoy conversing with Morrighan.
