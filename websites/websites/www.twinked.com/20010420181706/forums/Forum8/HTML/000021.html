Nice to meet yah man. I am Abstin Nence. I use to be in DoT, but I moved to the new server.

Let me give you some pointers when asking to join a guild.

1. Introduction
2. Explain your game play
3. Provide the guild that you want to join some detail about yourself. Never have them do research on you.
4. Find a guildmate that is near your lvl & ask to grp with them.

Dont go join a guild based on ppls words. go attend raids, grp with them & get to know them first. There are some great guilds out there but not all of them will fit your play style. Take your time & be selective.

I wish yah luck. DoT are a great bunch of ppls.
