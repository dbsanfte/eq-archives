Guild Charter

We, Days of Tranquility, are a family more than we are a guild. We believe in truth, justice, and helping others. We seek to advance as individuals and as a guild, but never at the expense of others. We are all equal in the guild, regardless of race, gender, creed, class, or level. Our officers are there to assist in the administration of the guild, to be our voice when dealing with external groups, and to recruit like-minded individuals of high morals into the guild. Blood is thicker than water -- and nowhere is this maxim more evident than in the Days of Tranquility. We live and die beside each other and for each other. Our word is our bond. Our honor is unquestioned. Our name is our pride.
