DoT Wiki is up and running.
- yueh @ Thursday, March 1, 2001
In mirroring the many message boards the guild has at the moment, I got the idea of using a Wiki to maintain things like the Rosters, Loot lists, etc. Message boards are not really the right format. For those not familiar with a Wiki, it is basically a system of interlinked pages that are editable by everyone. It works on an honor system and is the perfect way to spread maintainance around to everyone. Before you can edit the pages you must hit the preferences link and enter a username, personal password, and administrator password. The administrator password is the same as the one used to access the guild-only message boards. You can find the text formatting rules at http://www.usemod.com/cgi-bin/wiki.pl?TextFormattingRules . The short version is, to make a link to another wiki page use multiple caps in a word (ie. GuildRoster) to create a list, just indent each item by a space. To start a new paragraph, insert a blank line. Head on over to the Roster on the left there and add yourself and your secondaries. If you just want to play with the wiki formatting use http://www.twinked.com/guilds/DaysOfTranquility/wiki/wiki.pl?WikiWikiSandbox.
