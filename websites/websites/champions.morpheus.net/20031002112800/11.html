** Raid News:

  • Last Tuesday we warmed up by killing General V'Ghera in Kithicor for Pigsticker's rogue epic.  

     

This was followed by a very successful raid to the Plane of  Fear.  Special thanks to Tuven for clearing most of Fear ahead of time; that made it very easy to get to the golems and mini-bosses - WOOT!  Loot included an iskar tear for Gholli, a shissar head-for Callail's epic, two Ball of Everliving Golem for Kalguril and Boldwind's warrior epics, Lyssa's Darkwood Piccolo for Knote, a Barbarian Spiritist`s Hammer for Neander, and DawnFire, Morning Star of Light (cleric mace) to Kalguril.  Tuven also looted a second iskar tear and a broken golem wizard staff piece to MQ with a shaman and wizard at a later date.  CoNgratulations to all who got drops...   • Saturday we overthrew the monarchy in Chardok by killing the Prince, OverKing and Queen twice.  Folks arrived in zone promptly and it only took about an hour and a half to get down to the Royals area -- great work!  Special thanks to Aaranon for leading and doing groups for this, as well as MTing.  Thanks to Laod for pulling and others who worked as backup tanks, to Seanus for doing loot, and all the great healing support - CHEERS!  CoNgratulations to all the epic item receivers -- Laod got his Metal Pipe for epic; the 2 Prince's heads were awarded to Sais and Bluehue (Hardfor's alt); the Singed Scrolls were awarded to Maegwina and Weisses; and the Ancient Blades from Queen were awarded to Kalguril and Boldwind.  WTG !!
