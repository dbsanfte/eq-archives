Tuesday, April 15

Last Friday we celebrated the guild's 4th Anniversary by a trip to the Plane of Fear. We sought to gather Cicka's last epic piece and we fought some old enemies in Fear like the fetid fiends.   

And with the help of our old friend Inco, we decimated quite a few Amys all in a row.  Who's pull was that?   

 

Then although we were threatened and harassed by Cazic who said "Beware all infidels who dare to taint my plane, for I shall rend your minds with fright, dread, and terror!", our brave Drugan simply replied "OK Cazic, Could you please gather Dread, Fright, Terror, Broken Golem and yourself. Form a line for summary execution and tell everyone else.. Thanks, but your services are no longer needed."  We then moved on to tackle Dracolich, Fright, Dread, and Terror. 

And finally we Champions made a brave attempt at the new and improved Cazic Thule himself.  Although Cazic won this round... What a way to celebrate an anniversary!!   And we undoubtedly will go back to try Cazic again so we can add his name to our wall of infamous evils we have bested.

April 11, 2003 -- HAPPY ANNIVERSARY CHAMPIONS!! 4 YRS AND GOING STRONG!!

** Now and Then **

       

Each and everyone of us present in the guild today, as well as those who have come or gone, has had a hand in the progress you see in these pictures and text.  Because a guild is made up of living, breathing individuals, it is like a living thing itself.  While other guilds have become extinct, Champions of Norrath has remained strong and grown.  It has grown to what we have today, because the members of this guild have adapted and evolved to the massive changes of Everquest itself.  I am proud to be a part of this testimony of  survival and I hope all of you will join me in celebrating OUR guild's rich past and and a future that promises to be full of new adventures, new friends and exciting hunts!
