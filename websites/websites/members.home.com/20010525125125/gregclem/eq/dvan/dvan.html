Dvan - wood elf ... wizard?

 

Dvan was born to two wood elf parents, his father a druid, his mother a bard. When he was a baby he was dotted on and both parents made sure they spent plenty of time with him. All was good, except one little problem on one insignificant day.


He fell out of the crib.

Normally this would be a minor thing, he'd be hurt, he'd cry, his parents would make it better, like any good parents. The problem was twofold though. A passing druid had cast SoW on the baby Dvan to amuse him, and his crib was by one of the railings in Kelethin. So when he fell out of the crib, he FELL out of the crib.

Somehow his spirit was able to return, probably by intervension from Tunare herself. His parents vowed to never let such a thing happen again, to protect him, and give him anything he ever wanted. Basically to spoil him rotten.

Dvan never did turn rotten. He did get different ideas in his head then other wood elves as he grew up, but none of them bad. He also got along with High Elves as well as the Wood Elves so had several friends to amuse as he grew up. It was a happy life.

As he grew up, his parents tried to find a career path that was right for him. They tried Druid, Bard, even Ranger or Warrior. He never wanted to be any of those. He always wanted to be a.. Wood Wizard!
