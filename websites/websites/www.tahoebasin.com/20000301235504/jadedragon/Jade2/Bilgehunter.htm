2) I have just yet to get to the submission, I have about 30 shots I have yet to get to. Please don't send me the shots again unless I request it.

I got the following letter, and am considering the ideas in it...give me your thoughts on this folks...is this what I should do with the Hunter? :

Conceptually (sp?), the web page is great. To my knowledge, there are no other websites out there focusing on rare spawns. I humbly suggest, that a way to really improve your site is to add more background info on each spawn, rather than just having a collection of pictures.

Each spawn should at least have the basic info: What zone is the spawn in? What is the lvl of the spawn? is it a place-held spawn, random, timed, or triggered spawn (If known). What does it drop? Is the drop a quest item? Which quest? Is the quest class specific? Even if it is class-specific reward, can anyone do the quest? (Why bother to kill a rare spawn if it drops something only a cleric can use and ruin the chances for a cleric of getting a no drop item he/she needs when your some other class). Most people only play EQ for a few hours a week and are not in a position to ever see most rare spawns. Also, some where between lvl 25-30 people start seriously pursuing quest items to get the treasured items they are looking for and to break up the monotony of hunting for exp. The information can be presented in a way that would encourage more cooperative role-playing ("Lets see if we can find x spawn today, and kill x and pick up x, y, and z items for character x, y, and z along the way"). This info would be really useful to everyone that plays EQ.

It would also be nice to hear the stories of the people submitting the spawns, I know I get really excited when I trip across a rare spawn and want to tell every friend every detail. And I've found that people get really excited for you (after the jealousy wear off :>).

Your site could really fill a niche.

Regards,
James
