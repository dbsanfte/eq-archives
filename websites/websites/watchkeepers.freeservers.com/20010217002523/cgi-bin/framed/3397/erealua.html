Background:

A proud Erudite, Erealua nevertheless makes her home in Qeynos. She misses the beauty of her home city, but does not miss the constant murkiness of the weather and the dismal darkness of the Toxxulia forest.

An enchanter, Erealua struggles with her ability to alter people's perceptions. She does not believe in falsehood, yet falsehood and illusion are what she is good at. She is looking for a moral way to use her skills. Like most Erudinites, she is a bit haughty. Though she does not feel that all other races are less than her own, she does carry many of the prejudices of her race. Because of these prejudices, she struggles with her growing love of a human paladin, Pergindine.

She tries to deny her feelings, yet secretly longs for him. Her own family is comprised of merchants in Erudin who don't quite understand her abilities or her feelings. They bask in the glory of having produced a talented daughter and have never forgiven her for leaving their beloved city.
