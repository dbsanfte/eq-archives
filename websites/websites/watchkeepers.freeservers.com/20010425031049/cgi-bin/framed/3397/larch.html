Description:
Larch Arrowsmithe is the eldest child of a famous family of fletchers. Leaving his home of Kelethin at a young age, he soon began to travel all the roads of Norrath selling his wares to the people that could afford and appreciate them. Kind and courteous, Larch never spares a second thought for helping a friend in need. His famed skill with the bow is only out-matched by his ability to create them.
