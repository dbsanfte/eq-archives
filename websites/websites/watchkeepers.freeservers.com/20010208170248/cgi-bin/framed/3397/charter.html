1. Guild members are encouraged to roleplay their characters while traversing the lands of Norrath. While it is not strictly required to stay in character at all times, we do require that you have a history for your character as well as a good idea of how that character would react in a given situation.

2. To the extent that it is possible, Watchkeepers shall aid other members in their endeavors. This may mean helping to retrieve their corpse from the depths of a dungeon or gathering to defeat a foul beast. Guildmembers will offer these services outside of our guild to our allies, friends and strangers.

3. Your character must have attained their 5th rank in training before they may apply. Each character will be required to complete quests and provide a written history for their character before the initiation process is completed.

4. Your character will start out in the guild as an initiate under a probationary period. During this time you should try to make an effort to get to know your fellow Guild members better. You will wear the guild title during this period, but will not considered a full-fledged general member until the official induction ceremony takes place during a regular guild meeting. Once a general member, you will gain voting rights as well as the right to sponsor applicants to the guild.

5. We do require that you add a character description to your character in game by changing your camera view F9 key and clicking on yourself and adding the description to the black box that pops up with the shown inventory. All Guild members must provide a background story for themselves…. a history of who they are and perhaps how they came to be with WatchKeepers. Initiates must provide this history before becoming full members.

6. Guild is open to all classes and races.

7. Anything which goes against the tenets of the game such as foul language (cussing) or cheating is grounds for dismissal from the guild.
