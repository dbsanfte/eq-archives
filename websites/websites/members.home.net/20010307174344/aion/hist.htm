The Marr Twins and the Human Gods

 The History Library provided by Biblion is set up into two categories.  The Historic Overview will go through known history from the dawn of time through the different Ages of Norrath.

The second method of categorization is by deity.  It is tiered from the first gods to appear on Norrath and then their peoples history (ie. Tunare = elves).  The newer gods fall under the heading of the Human gods.  They are the ones who appeared at the dawn of The Combine Empire following the Marr Twins, creators of The Barbarians, who branched into Humans and Erudites, such as Solusek Ro, Karana, and Bertoxxulous.
