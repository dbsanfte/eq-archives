MISSION:
The Ancient Order of Guardians was created to Protect and Serve the citizens of Norrath.
We accomplish this mission by following three basic principles. These principles are
Courage, Charity, and Education.

Courage - We must have the courage to protect those who are unable to protect
themselves. This can be accomplished in many ways, and is not limited to physically
defending someone. For example: Standing up for someone who is being harassed by
others or verbally attacked without just cause.

Charity - Helping others whom are in need. It is often said that charity begins at home,
and this is true in our guild. We all help each other. If a guild mate is in need of a sword,
and I have two, it is my duty to give my extra sword to him. If I need a helmet, and my
guild mate is a Master Blacksmith, it is my guild mates obligation to make me a helmet.
However, that is not to say that all is free. Everyone should remember that charity comes
from the heart, and that we do not take advantage of each other. Nobody is expected to
just give something to a guild mate because he WANTS it. We give it because he NEEDS
it. Also remember that Charity is a road that travels two ways. If you never give, don't
expect to receive.
Charity also takes place outside the guild. By helping the other citizens of Norrath in their
times of need, we set the example. They may remember our kindness and pass it along to
others. They may even join us in our mission!

Education - This is possibly one of the easiest of our principles to follow, but one of the
most overlooked. This is simply passing on your knowledge to others. It can be as simple
as giving directions to a traveler, or as involved as teaching a language. Take the time to
answer a new citizen's questions. Remember how it felt when you asked a question and
nobody answered. Most of all remember to pass your knowledge on to your guild mates.

MEMBERSHIP:
The Ancient Order of Guardians is open for all Races and Classes to join, as long as they
are willing to help in carrying out our mission. A candidate wishing to join must be at least
level 10.
