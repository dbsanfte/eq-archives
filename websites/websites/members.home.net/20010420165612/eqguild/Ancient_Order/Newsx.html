April 12, 2001 AOG Promotes New Officers

The AOG is proad to announce the promotion of several Guardians.
Releanie, Andrasy ans Solden have all been promoted to the rank of
Sergeant. Sgt. Releanie will take charge of Squad 4 , Sgt. Andrasy will take
charge of Squad 2, and Sgt. Solden will take charge of Squad 3. Sgt.
Rizzo will assume the duties Acting Lieutenant in Platoon A. Congratulations
to our new Sergeants!
April 12, 2001 Guardians Receive Medals

The AOG has recognized two of it's Guardians for their outstanding
efforts to make the AOG the best guild in Norrath. Sgt. Manus and Sgt.
Solden have both been awarded the Medal of Merit for their outstanding
contributions to the Ancient Order of Guardians. Their actions have brought
great credit to both themselves and the AOG. Congratulations!

March 30, 2001 AOG Creates New Awards

In order to better recoginize the actions and accomplishments of our
fellow Guardians, several new awards are being implimented. These will
include The AOG Service Award, Recruiters Ribbon, and various Raid
Campaign Ribbons. For full descriptions, see our Medals and Awards page.
