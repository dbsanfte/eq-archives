The Council of The Nameless

The function of the council will involve leading discussions on guild issues such as: the passage of laws, the appointment of officers, the distribution of items, and, if need be, the trial of members. The house would be the link between the regular members and the council. They would participate the debates of guild issues and do their best to express the feelings of the regular members before the council. The regular members have a part too since their opinion is equally valid.  

When we hold meetings Aion will serve as speaker and will maintain order. The person who has the floor would have permission to speak. Only the council and officers should participate to avoid confusion and they will be asked to be discerning and orderly. We willl keep the meetings open house to regular members, but ask that they refrain from speaking. Than after it's been discussed,  regular members will have a chance to express their opinions within a set time (5 min limit). They will have only one 5 minute period then it will go to whomever has something to share. Then after all is said, the council will either vote then and there, or recess the issue and have further discussion within the council. 

Then however the vote turns out will be how it is decided.
