Biography:

This is the childhood of Beowoulf:

      In the begining was only of the superhuman races, Barbarians, Euridites, Dwarves, and Elves. The only thing making this world corrupt was the evil god Graphitalodar the god of death, his sinister plans and wreched army of skeletons and rotting corpses.
Barbarians were one of the most respected race for their strength and bravery, they were very nomatic, for these reasons the god of death hated them and made many attempts to destroy them but his undead army and evil sorcerers always failed.
     This is a tale of a young man who had his calling as a warrior to defend the world from death, here is the tale.....
     It was a cold day like always on the plain, the barbarians had set their camp between three hills which gave them protection from any wind or attack. Beowoulf, a young lad strong and agile was hunting foxes and gophers, he tredded slowly across the crunchy snow and stared at the fox as the fox was eating a small bird. Beowoulf had two rawhyde belts across his chest creating a V shape and a polar bear loin cloth with a sheath and a 5 inch dagger inside.
     He drew his dagger and jumped for the fox, the fox jolted and jumped and darted left to right and did tricks as though he was mocking him for his slower legs and movement. Furious, Beowoulf threw his dagger just as the fox was jumping to his small hole in the ground and got him in the back. He skinned the fox and had a smile of vitory on his face.
     Beowoulf was carrying the many hides from that day to the camp, his grandmother had promised to make him boots of fine leather and fur lining them if he supplyed the hides.
     Just as he was bouncing over the small snow banks he saw a charriot pulled by four polar bears the man inside had a giant axe smeared in blood and wore a round metal plate chest it looked as thick as his whole hand. The man had black hair and his mucles were big and dirty from fights. He made it back to the town and saw the man with the carriage talking to the others, his expression was fear and excitment. He waved his arms about signaling something very large and the village started suiting for war and the wemon took shelter.
    Beowoulf entered his mothers small bamboo hut and asked what was wrong, she said with panic in her that a monster was comming the Tulac, She said it was a snowman the size of 100 men and was going to attack the village. Tears streamed from her eyes and beowoulf said "I will help protect the village!" and with that he ran from the hut with her mother crying and screaming for him to come back.
    He perched himself upon a falled tree that acted like a bridge above the entrance to the camp. The entrance of the camp was between hills creating a V shaped small ravine.
He squinted his eyes and waited, he was getting cold after 4 hours of whatch. Then he heard a rummble and a roar, he froze in his tracks and he felt his muscles tighten. The monster came crashing through the entrance, Beowoulf held onto the fallen tree as the monster passed right underneath him. The moster was very tall and his head hit the log and beowoulf fell from the log onto the ground with a loud "Pumf!".
    He saw the monster grab a whole hut and threw off the roof and looked inside and whent on. The village warrior told the men to "Get him!" and they threw their spears and flailed their swords but the monster tore threw them like nothing was there. Beowoulf knew he must fight or die with the rest if them so he drew his dagger and looked at the monster as it tore open another hut. He knew he could not kill it with his dagger so he decided to make a plan, he took a long peice if vine and strung it between two stumps in the ground and he took another peice and coiled it around this wire and he did this again and again untill the wire was as big as hid head. The wire was placed about 30 feet from the fallen tree that was now on the ground since the monster had hit it, he carved a branch that aimed twards the sky so it was sharp as a spear.
    The men had fallen back and Beowoulf walked up to the monster and stabbed it in the foot the monster roared in pain and looked at the running boy in hatred, Beowoulf ran to the trap and jumped over the wire and crawled over the giant log and kept running then stoped and made a face at the monster. The monster ran twards him and hit the tripline with a snapping noise the monster came down on the spike. The spike drove through his chest and the monster let his final gasp for air and with a sigh retired from life.
