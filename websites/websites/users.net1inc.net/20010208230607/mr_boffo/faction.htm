RAISING FACTIONS

*Clerics of Underfoot (Rivervale)*

Bone Chips: Talk to a paladin in the paladin's guild Kaladim and he will send you out to collect bone chips. Give him 4 bone chips (stacked will do). You will get experience, guild faction and some money. Note that you must be given this quest to complete it. Players with low faction will not get anything by giving him bone chips. you will increase faction with Clerics of Underfoot, Kazon Stormhammer and Miners Guild 249.

Healing: Nultal Malfoot in the cleric guild of Kaladim is willing to heal you for a price. Tell him "I need healing" and he will demand 3gp If you pay, he casts healon you and your faction goes up with ClericsOfUnderfoot, KazonStormhammer and MinersGuild249

Aviak Chickens: It would appear that the aviaks have been defecating on the huge dwarf statue over the entrance to Kaladim and the dwarf paladins are VERY upset about it. Bumle Reminjar, in Underfoot Hall, will reward you for every 4 aviak chick talons you bring him. You will increase faction with Clerics of Underfoot, Kazon Stormhammer, and Miners Guild 249.

*Clerics of Tunare (Freeport)*

On the second floor of the Hogcaller Inn is a dwarf named Pandos Flintside, by feeding him a single muffin you get a tiny bit of faction with the Clerics of Tunare (among others). I fed him 120 muffins tonight, at 6cp each this is trivial cash. This brought my faction up enough that the High Priest would deign to give me a low level quest, but not a high level one. You can feed him 16 at a time (4 in each give slot).
------------------------------
*Corrupt Guards (Freeport)*

1.) One easy way to raise faction with all the 'bad groups' without losing faction to the good groups is to do the inert potion quest in Erudin.

In Erudin go to the top of the palace and look for an enchanter guildmaster in the open area named Tereon (or something like that) hail him and he'll say he is a scholor of enchantments and will ask what it is that you study. Reply to his question with something like, "I study enchantments". He'll say excellent, please take this inert potion to salisan in the roots and herbs shop. (Two floors down in the palace). Take the potion there, Salasin reveals himself as a thief and says the real Salasin is outback. You'll gain faction with corrupt qeynos guards, corrupt freeport guards, coalition of tradesfolk and a few other groups. And you won't lose faction to any of the good groups.

2.) Go under the bar at the docks to the underground tunnels*. Behind a door there is a Dark Elf Rogue Guildmaster(GM) whose name is Nestral TGaza. Target her and say "Where is Janam and Rebby?" or hail her to go thruogh the entire quest. She will hand you a note(lore item). Take the note with you and from the docks zone into West Freeport using the first zone(Should be on your right). Once you zone into West Freeport, look for the Theater of the Tranquil and The Ashen Order(Monk guildhouse) Rebby and Janam are right there sitting under a tent next to a fire. Hand Rebby the note and you recieve "experience" no factions and something called "Rebby's whiskers". They are stackable and NOT lore. Take this whisker to Nestral TGaza and you will recieve some cash and most importantly better factions for the following: Coalition of Trade Folk, Carson McCabe(Rogue), Corrupt Qeynos Guards, and the Freeport Militia.

*Neriak*

Killing the halflings in nek forest does *not* raise any neriak factions.  The only faction it raises is the orc faction in the commonlands. It also  hurts numerous faction in Rivervale, including the guards, merchants, and  the mayor.

So far, I have only found 3 ways to raise faction with neriak. One of those  ways is to give lightstones to a DE NPC in south ro, as part of the research  quest. This raises the guard factions for foreign quarter and commons, and  the merchant faction. She is part of the merchant faction in neriak,  though, so this probably isn't an option for good races. Second, you can  give red wine to the scribe in the library in 3rd gate (this is a small part  of the quest to find the tome of ages and get the oracle robe BTW) this  raises the same factions as above (dark bargainers, dread guard inner, dread  guard outer) but he is also part of the merchant faction, plus you have to  sneak through 2 zones in neriak to get to him.

Finally, you can raise faction with the indigo brotherhood (the neriak  warrior guild) by killing elven slaves in crushbone. Only the elven slaves  give the faction, and the only faction that will get raised the the indigo  brotherhood, not any of the guards or merchants.

You also take a BIG hit with all of the 'good' elven factions by killing  them, so do so at your own risk.

Go to 3rd gate to the library. Find the Necro who is part of the Oracle quest. Give him 4 red wines at a time. You will get faction with DreadGuardsOuter and Inner as well as NeriakMerchants.

*Rivervale Residents*

As soon as you come out of Rivervale into Misty Thicket turn south and follow the mountain.  As you reach the corner of the zone there will be a house.  This is there Joogl Honeybugger is.  He says his hand is [hurt] if you hail him.  If you ask him about hurt, he asks you to get him a bandage.   Give him bandagess in 4 stacks of 4. Your factions should skyrocket. Faction with Merchants of Rivervale, Deep Pockets, Guardians of the Vale, and Mayor Grubbin goes up.   Faction with the Coalition of Tradefolk goes down. *Note: You can do this invisible*
------------------------------
*Enchanters in Erudin*

If you go by the dock in Toxx there is an NPC Necro there.  Kill him.  He respawns every 6 minutes so kill him for a while and eventually you'll get to Amiable.  It took me about 15 kills to get my faction from dubious to amiable

*Steel Warriors*

Easiest place to build steel warrior faction is in Freeport.  You can giving the head of the steel warriors Deathfist Legionare shoulder pads and Deathfist slashed belts(shoulderpads come off Legionares in West commons and east comms and mistythicket, belts are dropped by any deathfist except pawns and apprentices).  Best place for belts in West commons and East commons. Go to the west commons camp, follow right wall from WC or left wall from kithkor(closer to kith) and you will find a large camp of orcs.   Plow threw it collecting belts as yougo.  Legionares con blue at level 18 so there usually easiest to get kills of and newbies are thankful for it.  The first camp in east commons spawns these the most.  When you turn in thebelts you gain steel warrior faction and a few others whilelosing freeport militia faction.  If you dont want to drop below dubious you can turn the deathfist belts to a Captain in the freeport militia hall, which raises militia faction but lowers paladins(priests of marr are only ones that are even semi-difficult to raise and there not the guards).  You should hit amiable fairly quickly. Also you can kill D'Vinn in CrushBone.

*Temple of Life (Qeynos)*

1. Diseased and rabid pelts - Turn in these pelts to one of the priestesses for some faction, plat and for rabid grizzlies, an Endure Disease spell.
2. Putrid Skellies in South Karanas - Each Putrid skeleton killed gives some faction.
3. Bone Chips - Hand Lashun Novashine two bone chips at a time for faction boost.
4. Blessed Oil quest - Deliver flask to Brother Estle. He sometimes gives a return quest to one of the priestesses, which again can be turned into another quest still (to Brother Hayle - have not finished that part yet)
5. Kizzie (Montop?) quest - Bring an ingredient back from Rivervale. I have not done this yet, but delivery quests tend to be low increase/payoff.

The gains from the above must be microscopic. I spent a goodly amount of time on each of them yesterday and went from amiably
to amiably.

6. Lord Grimrot - Ask Camlend Serbold about Lord Grimrot and he gets all excited. But you need better faction than amiably to get the quest.
7. Bloodsabres - Ah, revenge. I spent a few levels in the Catacombs and managed to anger quite a few of these jerks. Nobody I regularly adventure with even knows about them. At 28 I made the mistake of trying to go there just to con the first one you see. It diced me up good. I'm betting this would be some good faction improvements if I can coerce a group to go with me. Killing Tomax and Varsoon fall in this category but Tomax is so low and Varsoon is too rare to find. Turning in their "rat-shaped rings" also will net some faction (Tomax doesn't carry one).
8. Give lashon novastar 2 bonechips and he will cast minor healing on you and also give you faction.He can usually be found in killing fields of qeynos telling everyone to give up violence.
9.Go to Karanas and hunt bandits - I found a nice camp at -3500 -11000 or thereabouts that had 5 in it. You collect these sashes (which are no drop) and take them to the guy standing next to the shield merchant in front of the paladin guild. I can't remember his name - but he is standing directly to the left of the shield guy. Not only do you get a significant faction boost with BOTH the clerics of Tunare and the Hall of Thunder, you get a random 1pp item and some cash. Took me about 15 sashes or so to get from apprehensive to amiable - can't beat that (you need about 30-35 to get from apprehenisve to kindly).
