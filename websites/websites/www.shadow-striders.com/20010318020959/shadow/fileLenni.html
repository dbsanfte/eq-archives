Lenni
Ogre Warrior
 
History: During Lenni's childhood, the other ogres noticed he was somewhat of a village idiot, far more intellectually challenge than most ogres. He grew up in the ogre community not intelligent enough to be malicious and cruel, more of an ambling fool taken advantage of by the others. He commits his share of violence though not driven by any evil desire, more lack of anything else to do and the fact he has no conscience. Many of the acts he commits will be the bidding of others, who asked the idiotic Lenni out mischievousness or deviousness. Lenni is looking for some purpose, or more accurately to be ordered about by the more intelligent evil beings.
Lenni is agnostic, he is too simple to understand religion.
