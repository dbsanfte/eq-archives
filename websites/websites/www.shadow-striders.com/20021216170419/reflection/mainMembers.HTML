The High Court
Officers of the WR
The four parts of the High Court are the main governing body in the Wind Riders. They each rule over their division, and Sugan chooses them from amongst her most loyal Wind Riders. Only two members are allowed per quarter, with one being a chief advisor to Sugan. These chiefs are known as the Genesis officers, or Four Winds.
