Ninna Shu'Raven
Teir'Dal Shadow Knight
 
History: I started my path in this world as a mage against my mothers strict wishes. She had demanded that I become like her a Shadow Knight and a strong future Matron, but I had no desire to rule the house Shu'Raven. Knowing that my eldest sister would inherit the job anyway my plans lay far away from home discovering new places. My younger sister, Synnuous, whom I had always thought was more like me than our mother surprised me, she was too involved with another to hear a bit of my plans. One night while I was away from home a rival family, Q'aesatori, attacked our house in a hideous attempt to gain more power and wipe out my family in the process.

When I returned there was my younger sister trying to heal the few standing members. I immediately sent in my minion pet from the astral plane and began to help, but my power was not yet strong enough. My sister pleaded with me that she had depleted all her strength and if we were to ever do anything in the future we had to leave now. With everything that I had known suddenly smoldering in fire I had to agree with her when my pet dismissed himself, the coward. As she tugged on my sleeve I looked back one last time to see another warrior fall dead and broken. Our Mother's and sister's decapitated heads hung high on the posts of the gate as a show that they had won the battle and had devoured our house and moved up another slot closer to the first house. My eyes narrowed and I stumbled over some rocks as my sister, Synnuous, and I had no choice, but to flee.

The Q'aesatori were quick on our heels sending their minions after us not wanting two females of their rival family ,no matter their rank, to escape. We made a narrow escape. Hidden by a former rival family that had sunk to almost to oblivion due to the Q'aesatori family took us in hoping to gain revenge. Helped in altering appearances so that we would not easily be discovered still within Neriak.

I soon changed my profession to that of a Knight of Shadows and along with my sister we will one day have our revenge upon the Q'aesatori. My heart is filled with nothing but cold emptiness and forever shall be.

Faileas' View: There is something about revenge that can motivate a person. It seems Ninna has found that motivation.

Envy's View: A sister shadow knight like her is good to have. However, her devotion to the Dead seems to only be half of what it should.

Sugan's View: *shakes her head in sorrow* More Teir'Dal shadow knights... Karana protect us.
