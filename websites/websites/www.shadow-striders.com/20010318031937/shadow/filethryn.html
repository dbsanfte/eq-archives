Thryn Taciturn
Half Elf Bard; Wind Rider officer
 
There seems to be no end to the number of bards Sugan meets. This one, Thryn, is especially dangerous. He has a great amount of allies, and seems to be one of the luckiest men upon Norrath. He also has great sway on my sister, being one of her officers. And Thryn is the husband to the former leader of the Fists of Fury (and now Wind Rider again), Laneni.

- Lord Faileas
