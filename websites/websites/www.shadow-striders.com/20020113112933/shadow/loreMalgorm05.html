Journal Four
                
 
The Dark Rose Begins to Wilt

When Envy received word that Faileas had raped Silveril and she'd had his children it enraged her. She turned her back on the shadows and fled. For some reason this ate at Malgorm; he could not stand it. Like he had lost some part of himself. He was sickened by what she had done. Malgorm wanted to hurt her, he would kill her, how dare she leave him. He would cleanse her of life with his hell fire- with his rage for life he would show her hate. Malgorm begin to use his power of corruption on Envy as he had so many others. He raped her emotions and turned her against herself. She grew so vengeful that she vowed to kill Lord Faileas. Eaten up inside, Envy ran to hide in the same place where Malgorm and she had once made their kingdom.

The Rage of Emotions

Malgorm had grown sick of Stormangell's innocence, but his words were useless. The boy refused to kill his old friends. He would only sit and stare away from the wizard when Malgorm talked to him. Finally, the boy could not take the torture of the demon any longer and fled to hide from his own inner demons as well. Meanwhile Malgorm and the shadows killed his old Wind Rider friend and Lord Darain's old enemy, Torien. But the boy would still pay for leaving the side of his teacher….

The Weeds Will be Pulled

Malgorm made his way to the city of his birth, to the sewers of Qeynos. He was there to hunt Envy and find Silverann. Malgorm found her with ease for he knew her as well as himself. From the shadows, he stared upon her and felt the rage build and the hell fire within growing. Malgorm confronted the renegade dark elf, eager for her death. Then.. it stopped. All that came out where hollow threats. Why had he felt for the first time that he couldn't kill? Confusion entered him; he could not show weakness, but he remembered what Silverann had said so long ago about love. What was wrong with him? He could not bring himself to kill her. So to save face he enticed Envy to help him in his plans of vengeance. He would make Stormangell pay for leaving him.. Once again he felt something. Observing the water where he stood so long ago, Malgorm stared at his refection- realizing he loved both of them. He was not fueled with hate of life, but anger that the young twins had left him. Envy noticed his pain with disgust. She once again mothered him in her own sick way, and as he sat and stared into the dark waters he heard the name 'Azzothoth'... It was the same place he'd heard the name 'Bertoxxulous'. He left that day with a dark plan to make Stormangell pay for leaving his side.
