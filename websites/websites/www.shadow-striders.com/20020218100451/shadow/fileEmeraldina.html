Emeraldina
Half Elven Nihilist Ranger
• 10/5/00
History: It all began so simple...but then doesn't everything. She was raised by her parents, in Surefall, to love nature, to hate gnolls and to protect the bears and wolves. Easy enough I thought. At the tender age of 16, I set out into the world to make it a better place for all... such noble ideas for one so young. I encounter many lessons in the hills between Qeynos and Surefall. When I felt I was trained enough I ventured into Blackburrow to stop the gnolls at the starting point by eliminating all of them at a tender age...I spent much time in Blackburrow. I even fell in love... His name was Bumgurof, he was a barbarian. We were married at the ruins in Qeynos Hills and set off to Faydwar for our honeymoon...we had heard of a little brownie that was terrorizing the land....Two weeks after my marriage everything changed... Bumgurof deserted me... Since then I have changed... I still care about the animals, but everyone else can go to hell. My dealings with love have left me hardened...I no longer look at the world thorough rose colored glasses...Deciding to join others that are of the same feelings I have sought you all out...What will happen in the future, who knows, who cares. . .
