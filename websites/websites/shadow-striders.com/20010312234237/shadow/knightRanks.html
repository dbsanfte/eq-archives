Ranking of the Knights of Shadow
                            
 
Shadow Knights and Fallen Paladins belong in the Knights of Shadow Circle. The Inner Circle's structure can be found here. As for the Outer Circle, this is the structure:

  • All new members are given the rank of Page.

  • After Page, you can be promoted to Squire.

  • And after Squire, you can be promoted to Knight.


All rank decisions are made by the ranking officer of the Inner Circle. At this time, Dark Lady Envy T'Druis is the ranking officer for the Knights of Shadow.

Decisions are based on the following criteria:

  • Impress higher ranking members with good role playing. If you are good enough, you will get the attention of Lady Envy.

  • Providing equipment to improve the guild. An example would be offering Lady Envy a gift that the guild can use, like weapons, armor, etc. Lady Envy and the Inner Circles will determine what to do with the equipment.

  • Defeat a Wind Rider in a battle of wits. Must be witnessed by another.

  • Defeat a Wind Rider in a fair duel. Must also be witnessed, but that is easier to with the world wide anouncment. By fair we mean equal to or very close to your level.

  • Higher ranks must perform more of the above to advance.

  • If you are a Squire, you must play at least 5 hours a week.

  • If you are a Knight, you must play at least 10 hours a week.

  • Exceptions can be made if you are having RL difficulties.


Once you are a Knight, you can advance to the rank of Lord or Lady. But to be a Lord, you must be in the Inner Circle, and only the Shadow Lord Faileas can bestow that honor. Only Knights will be considered for the Inner Circle when positions are available.

Disrespect to higher ranking members is punishable by demotion and/or disbandment.

- Lord Faileas
