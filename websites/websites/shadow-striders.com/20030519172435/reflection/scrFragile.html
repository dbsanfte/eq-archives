The Fragile
Take nothing for Granted
 

His last words asking Sugan to look at the ring on her hand, the great paladin fell to Stormangell's unnatural power. The forest went silent, save a Demon's laughing... But even Malgorm fell quiet when he sensed Sugan's thoughts. The bonds that held her mind to Bertoxxulous were broken, and she knelt beside Cane's lifeless body.

Betrayed by Sugan's change and wondering what he did wrong, young Stormy shyly retreated to Faileas' side. He watched bitterly as the Wind Riders mourned the loss of Canelon. Sugan began to pray. Ralnia joined her, as well as the rest of their friends. Looking more tired than anything, Lord Faileas bade Sugan a quiet farewell. He joined the Demon, Stormy, and Silverann as they were enveloped by magick and swept back to their dark realm. A beautiful light was seen weaving it's way through the trees.


Breath was whispered back into the high elf by Tunare's musical voice. As he stood once more, Sugan thanked the Goddess of Creation with a kiss to her beloved. Canelon wiped away her tears and apologies, and it seemed she could remember none of the horrible things she'd done. Determined not to allow regret to ruin victory, the Wind Riders knelt before Sugan one by one and restated their vows to fight the darkness.
Serenade observed quietly from afar.
