Shizo
Tier'Dal Warrior
 
History: Those who fear me, those who despise me, those who fight me, they call me by many names: Bastard elf, mud elf, abomination of all that is evil. Those who live to converse with me call me Shizo. I’m a typical scapegoat to all those “perfect” races. So blind are they, always considering my race, the drow, to be utterly evil to the core and to be exterminated on sight. But you know what? They are correct. I will impale and spill the innards of any person foolish enough to challenge me. If they respect me, my race, and prove to be worthy, I may not start trouble. I’ve always been a loner, and have managed well. Now I seek a place, a home, somewhere where I can find peers, regardless of race, where we can combine our knowledge and strength and claim these lands as our own. I come from Neriak, located deep within the Neklutos Forest. Many years ago, those damned forces of light invaded, attempting to wipe out all drow. They did not succeed but managed to wipe out my families settlement, but made a costly mistake: I survived. Those images of my kin lying dead on the ground and those infidels hacking and slashing their corpses will forever be burned in my mind. If not for my elder brother, who sacrificed his life for me, I would have suffered the same fate. But I escaped. Survival of the fittest, and as a result I have taken up my swords and will carry on my brothers legacy. Every kill I perform I pretend it is one of the cursed Windriders, and it makes the kill that much sweeter. I see in the Shadowstriders my opportunity to get my full revenge upon those who call themselves righteous, and I look forward to the day when I hack and slash their families corpses in the Warlords name. They took everything from me, now I shall take everything from them. Yes, I will take them down to the Abyss with me! Shizo
