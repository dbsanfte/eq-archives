A Brief History of the Shadow Striders
How it all began.
 
The Shadow Striders were once a small sect within the Bloodsabers. Like the other Bloodsabers they followed Bertoxxulous, the Plaguebringer, and lived beneath Qeynos.

One day, a twelve year old boy named Faileas Imshee'Cro was kidnapped and taken into the Bloodsaber ranks. He was to become a necromancer, like his great ancestors before him. However, Faileas proved to be both adept at the Dark Arts and physical combat. Faileas was hailed as one of Bertoxxulous' choosen Shadow Knights. And he quickly became the most gifted student under Sragg Bloodheart.

Then, when Faileas was sixteen, he was unleashed upon the lands of Karana. There, he caused chaos, despite his sister's actions. Sugan, Faileas' twin sister, had become a noble Paladin, sworn to stop Bertoxxulous. They had few battles in these early days, but nothing worth mentioning here.

After a year of escalating tensions, Sugan and Faileas found themselves on opposite ends of the struggle in Qeynos. At the age of seventeen, Faileas earned the leadership of the Shadow Striders, and Sugan was appointed leader of the Wind Riders. Faileas knew the Shadow Striders were a weak group at the time. The elders within the Bloodsabers did not trust him. He realized that in order for his goals to be reached, he needed to look beyond the Bloodsabers. He took the Shadows, and began to create his own army, away from the control of the Bloodsabers.

It has been four years since Faileas took leadership of the Shadow Striders, and they have certainly grown beyond the expectations of his previous mentors. As certain as the sun sets, the Shadow Striders will continue to spread what Faileas calls the, "Dark Disease," for many years to come, unless his twin and her Wind Riders can stop them.
