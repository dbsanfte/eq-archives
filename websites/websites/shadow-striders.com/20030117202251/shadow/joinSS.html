Joining the Shadow Striders
The Qualities of a good Shadow
 
Shadow Striders Application

To join us in our quest to spread the Dark Disease of Shadows, there are a few things required of you. Here they are:

  • Roleplay - We are a heavy RP guild, and we place a lot of importance on it. Even further, we are an evil guild, so you must role play being evil in some way.
  • On this site, and on the Wind Riders site, there is a great deal of information that your character would not know. Keep this OOC knowledge away from your character. You can try to set up yourself to be informed of the knowledge to further your character's growth.
  • This is new, and does not apply to some members. Your character must be at least level 5. This shows some desire to join, and some desire to stay with your character.
  • Fill out the application on this site. Without it, we have no way to keep good track of you.
  • And most importantly, levels mean nothing to us. Respect your leaders, no matter what they con to you. This is about roleplaying.


And to be an involved member of the Shadow Striders, these are some suggestions:

  • Sign up to the mailing list.
  • Go to the Message Board. You can get there easily by clicking on the Message Board icon (looks like a push pin) to the right.
  • Participate in role playing with officers and other noted members.
  • Give your character life. Give them a story. Why are they here? What were they doing before? What do they plan to do? Fill out that space on your application.
  • Update your character as he/she grows. Add stories to our story section.
- Lord Faileas
