The Inner Circles
The Leaders of the Guild
The three Inner Circles comprise the main governing body in the Shadow Striders. They each govern the outer circle they're associated with. Shadow Lord Faileas is the one who determines who enters the Inner Circles, with one member being a chief advisor. These chiefs are known as the Shadow Trine.

The Outer circles have their own internal structure, and ranks. You can find more about this structure in the lists to the right.
