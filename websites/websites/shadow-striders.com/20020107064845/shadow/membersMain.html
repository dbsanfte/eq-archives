The Inner Circles
The Leaders of the Guild
 

The three Inner Circles are the main governing body in the Shadow Striders. Each has it's own outer circle under it's influence. Shadow Lord Faileas is the one who determines who enters the Inner Circles. Members are allowed within each Inner Circle, with one being a chief advisor to the Shadow Lord Faileas. These chiefs are known as the Shadowed Trine.

Each of the circles has its own internal structure. You can find the structures of the Outer Circles in the Lists on the right.
