Gwubb
Troll Shaman
 
History: Me's is Gwubb da Basher. Me's has bashed many froggies in da swamp. Me's hopes dat da fear dey felt before dey died pleased Cazic-Thule. Him is not good to anger. Gwubb was born in Grobb and has much froggie blood on his hands. But, after while, Gwubb growed tired of da swamp and ventured up ta Neriak to visit da Tier'Dal. Gwubb heard dey felt da same way he feel 'bout fear and hatred. Da Tier'Dal take in Gwubb and show him Innorruk's vision. Gwubb was intrigued by Innorruk and his hatred towards all things. Me's tink dat Innorruk and Cazic-Thule should talk and use Gwubb to wreak havoc and terror on da lightbringers. Gwubb will show da Wind Riders what it mean to truly experience fear. Cazic-Thule would want it dat way.

Faileas' View: Gwubb is a very talented shaman, being able to wield the primitive, yet still powerful, magic of his heritage.

Envy's View: This troll has the right idea about what his god wants.

Sugan's View: He is certainly timid for a troll.
