Betrayal
Respect your Allies
 
Silverann in his innocence had befriended of all people, Envy. In another game to prove to herself that she had no fond feelings for a light walker, Envy made a deal with the demon Malgorm in which she would lure Silverann to Qeynos. Malgorm nearly killed Silverann as a message to Stormangell. He must return to the SS, or Silverann would die. Silverann refused to tell Stormy though, showing the proud and stubborn nature of his Teir'Dal heritage. Envy realized her error and formed a pact sealed by blood with Silverann. She would judge him as her own kind, and he would guarantee her safety, even beyond death. Envy didn't understand that, yet...
