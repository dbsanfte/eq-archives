This is the web site for the Shadow Striders Guild on the Tunare server. If you want to join us, you can fill out the application. Fill it out with as much info as you can, especially the Story section. We are a Role Playing guild, and level is not as important as being able to role play. If you wish to know more about the current story, please visit the Lore section. Your story counts, and who knows, you might be swept up in the main plot someday.
