Shadow Striders
Significance
 
The Shadow Striders have long since been our most insufferable adversaries. Lord Faileas Imshee'Cro rules them, as does his apprentice Envy. Faileas is Sugan's twin brother; she's ordered that the Shadow Knight not be judged or harmed by any Wind Rider but herself at the Will of Karana. She wishes to yet save this poor excuse for a human. His Lordship dominates with fear and tyranny, causing much distress for the good citizens of the land.

Many notorious villains have spawned from the ranks of the Shadow Striders. The guild is a reknowned sanctuary for evil and corruption, where terrorism is encouraged and sin justified by the arrogance of the sinners' heart.

Since the tragedy that occured at Faileas' trial, after his capture ended the War of Ambition, he has regained power over his people and continued to spread influence. His sister's downfall was a shame that he took as his own, and Lord Faileas decided that it was time to establish himself as the head of the family. Sugan proved more resiliant than expected. With her honor restored, the Shadow Striders' focus turned to vengeance for her outrageous acts against their corrupt Freeport allies, the Militia. Envy, who'd lead the SS during Lord Faileas' absense, was nowhere to be found.

After Sugan's redemption in the service of Karana, events between the Wind Riders & Shadow Striders have tested patience and resolve on either side. Envy has mysteriously awakened from the comatose state she was in, which was discovered to be the result of poisoning by one of her own kind. With the Plague in the Karanas, and Lesser Faydark's corruption, the Wind Riders have had their influence spread thin across Norrath. Darkness has been busy.

Most encounters with the SS have been short, tense, and ended with violence. A recent skirmish took place in East and West Commons, during which Sugan was briefly captured. Lord Faileas ended the battle as quickly as he began it, stating that Sugan's capture and the fight was "merely a test" for some Gateway he's creating. According to glum Public opinion, things are spiralling further towards War...
