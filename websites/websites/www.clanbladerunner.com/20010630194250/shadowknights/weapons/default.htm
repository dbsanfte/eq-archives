Weapons

Shadowknights are a melee class and as such, their weapons are very important. Verant has chosen bladed weapons as the instrument of choice for Shadowknights, but as we can use many types of weapons, all categories will be represented here. If you have a weapon that is not listed on our pages here, please don't hesitate to take a screenshot and submit it to us. Thank you.
