The first image of "Autumn series"

Ok this image came from a fall picture I had seen and wondered if I'd be able make trees like this in Bryce 4. Well after about 2 days of messing with the tree textures, I finally figured out how to change the colors. This is what I came up with. Everything except the the bridge was created totally in Bryce 4. The bridge is a product from 3D Cafe. E-mail me and let me know what you think.

This image took me a total of about 5 days to create (while at work). Couple days texturing the trees, and then a few more days getting the trees and bridge set just right. This is actually my second version of this picture. The first one doesn't have the bridge.
