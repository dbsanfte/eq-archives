Darkforge Armor Quest

When Rubicite was removed from the game, Verant decided to replace it with class specific armor for each of the classes that could wear it. Darkforge armor is the Shadowknight equivalent. It offers great armor class, and some fair stat bonuses. There are two quest givers for this armor, and they are both located in the Temple of Solusek Ro (located near the Solusek Dungeons in Lavastorm) on the top floor on the north side. They are both in the same room, and are both called "Undead Knight". One Knight gives the Boots, Gauntlets, and Greaves quest, and the other gives the Vambraces, Bracers, Breastplate, and Helm quests. Each piece requires two pieces of decayed armor, and some additional items.

As the Undead Knight indicates, the Decaying armor pieces are won from Lizardman Crusaders, who happen to spawn in the Cazic Thule zone off of Feerrott. They are approximately level 35-37, and spawn at the TR2, Avatar, Archon, and Temple spawn points. When I say temple, I mean the temple beyond the Pit, though this is the location where they are most rare. The Decaying loot is No Drop and Lore.

IMPORTANT NOTE

When giving items to the Undead Knight, make sure they are NOT stacked. For example, if you are completing the Darkforge Breastplate quest, you will give the Knight the Decayed Breastplate, the Decayed Mail, and in each of the slots in the Give window, put ONE Enchanted Platinum Bar. If you put 2 bars stacked in one slot, you will lose the items and be really pissed off.

- Selenar Xllyveryn

The armor pieces have the following requirements:

Darkforge Helm - Decayed Helm, Decayed Visor, two Freeport Militia Helms

Freeport Militia Helm - obtained by slaying Freeport Guards. The item will appear in your inventory as a Damaged Militia Helm. They are not wearable. The guards are hunted by players in their 40's, and if you cannot slay them, you can probably get a PC to let you loot them as they are very commonly dropped. Outside the gates to East Freeport is a good place to do this.

Darkforge Breastplate - Decayed Breastplate, Decayed Mail, two Enchanted Platinum Bars

Enchanted Platinum Bars - obtained by purchasing a platinum bar and having an Enchanter PC cast Enchant Platinum on it

Darkforge Vambraces - Decayed Right Vambrace, Decayed Left Vambrace, Qeynos Kite Shield

Qeynos Kite Shield - this shield is used by the Qeynos guards. If the guard has one, you will see him using it. It is a shield with a yellow, red, and green emblem on it.

Darkforge Bracers - Decayed Right Bracer, Decayed Left Bracer, Broken Minotaur Lord Horn

Broken Minotaur Lord Horn - These can be obtained by slaying the Minotaur Lords which frequent the Mintoaur Caves of the Necromancer Meldrath, in Steamfont. They spawn at various locations in the dungeon, and are level 35.

Darkforge Gauntlets - Decayed Right Gauntlet, Decayed Left Gauntlet, one Enchanted Platinum Bar

Enchanted Platinum Bars - obtained by purchasing a platinum bar and having an Enchanter PC cast Enchant Platinum on it

Darkforge Greaves - Decayed Right Greaves, Decayed Left Greaves, two pieces of Melatite

Melatite - This is an ore that can be found on various clockwork Gnomes in the employ of the Solusek Mining Company. Currently, the mining company is working in Solusek's Eye (Solusek A). The ore definitely drops on models MB and MC, though rumor has it that it also drops on C class Clockworks that guard the Gnomish stronghold.

Darkforge Boots - Decayed Right Boot, Decayed Left Boot, two Fairy Dusts

Fairy Dust - This can be obtained by slaying the fairys at the fairy camp in the Lesser Faydark.
