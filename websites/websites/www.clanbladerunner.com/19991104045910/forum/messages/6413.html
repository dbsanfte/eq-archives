I logged into EQ Tuesday night. And was impressed by the sweet graphics. Then I tried moving around, no problem , no lag. But the interface for this game eats shit. Its like playing UO and Q2 mixed together. Sony people went so far to eliminate macroing they made the interface suck. Click here, click there, left click , right click, CTRL , Side arrow. It sucks. I agree with Achillies EQ is cool for a change, but I think people will grow old of it fast and go back to UO. It takes forever to get your skills up, everything costs so damn much. 775(?) platnuim for armor? Its crazy. EQ is cool, but it isn't Q2 or UO.
