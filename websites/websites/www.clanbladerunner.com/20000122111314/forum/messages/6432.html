If you can sport for the 3000 series of the banshee 2, er, I mean Voodoo 3 go with that. But if you don't have an AGP slot, stick with the TnT card. Another thing to consider is that the TnT card does support higher resolutions than the V3, but the v3 is faster by a good bit upto 1024x768. IF your not planing on playing things at 1600x1200 at like 12-14 fps (hehe) opt for the v3, its a better card RIGHT now, though I expect a better card from the TnT people in 6 months or so, maybe a bit longer.


or if your realy slick, pickup a pair of v2's (matched brands) for cheap. People are dumping single Voodoo 2's for 50-100 bucks, and a pair of those puppies STILL beats or rivals the newer cards, but to do the v2's you need two adjacent PCI slots. I opted for the dual V2 method myself and spent about $200 total and got a great solution, but that was months ago, you prolly could get a better price if you shoped the auction boards, or looked around in your area.


Hope this helps, -Achillies
