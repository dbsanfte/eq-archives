Guard of the Marines

Hail Captain Rottgrime and he says that "we [shall not allow those sarnak to overtake the Danak Shipyards]. Is that clear marine?!!"

Go out and fight outside the city walls, namely looking for Sarnak Berzerkers. Each one of them carries a Sarnak War Braid. Hand the captain 4 braids and he says:

"Great job marine!! Word of your heroics shall be passed on to the Admiral. If you don't have a shield then take one, if you do then get back to the frontlines. There is no time for R n' R!! Move it marine!! Or you'll be pushing Danak till the cockatrice crow!!"

You then get a few gold, your faction with Venril Sathir rises, and you get the Guard of the Marines.

The main reason for this quest is for the Venril Sathir faction. If you hunt in Karnor's Castle, do this quest a few times to get the faction you lost with Venril Sathir back. I wouldn't hunt in Karnor's too long if you want to keep your Overthere faction up, though.
