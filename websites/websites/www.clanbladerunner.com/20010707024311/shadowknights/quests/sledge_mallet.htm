Worker's Sledge Mallet

The Worker's Sledgemallet is achieved by this quest.

You must have amiable or better faction with Venril Sathir to do this quest.

Hail the undead foreman in the outpost near the dry-docked ship. He tells you something about filling his eyesockets with his favorite gem, but doesn't tell you what that gem is.

Buy two jades from the gem vendor, hand both of them to the undead foreman, unstacked, and you will recieve the Worker's Sledgemallet.
