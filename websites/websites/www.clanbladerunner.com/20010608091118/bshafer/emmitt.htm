This is my Emmitt Smith page. All ideas and opinions are my own. I am in no way affiliated or endorsed by Emmitt Smith, or anything he has to do with. Shit, I doubt he even knows I exist.

Emmitt Smith is the starting running back for the Dallas Cowboys. He was drafted in 1990 in the first round out of Florida State University (stat from Kevin, at bottom). I've been watching Emmitt since he started with the Cowboys. I Loved the way he ran the football. There was nothing that was gonna stop him from getting to the end zone. He was by far, the best running back in the NFL since Payton. He would blast thru the line like a lightning bolt, and run like hell. He always had a great persona on the field, as well. No matter what happened to him, who tackled him, or what they said would make him lose his edge. Always a great attitude towards all on the field. He was a great role model for all.

Then one day, he was at the negotiating table with good 'ol Jerry Jones. Mr. Jones decided that he was gonna do what it took to make sure Emmitt would be with the Cowboys forever. And he did. He gave Emmitt the largest contract ever made. I don't know the details, shit, I can't even remember how much the contract was worth. All I know is that Emmitt is now the biggest pussy in the NFL. I am sorry, but that is my opinion. He runs like a girl, falls down every chance he gets, and I swear to god, if he gets hurt one more time.... Anyway, you get my point.

It seems to me that ever since he has signed this new multi-freakin super million dollar contract, he has fallen down on the job. He gets hurt on damn near every stinkin play, and probably is thinking "I wonder who will make a big deal out of me now." while he is laying on the astro turf. The only thing that is the same about the Emmitt of today, compared to the Emmitt of yesteryear, is his attitude. I have to give credit where credit is due. If you notice during the game, after every tackle, he either pats the guy on the head, tells 'em "good job", or something. I like that. You don't see many of the pro's with an attitude like that.

If you don't like my comments, go somewhere else. If you like to comment on my opinions, drop me a line, and I will post 'em no matter what they say. Like myself, everyone has a right to their opinion.
