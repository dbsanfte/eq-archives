Reinn
Page of Storms
 
History: Hail. I am Reinn Thunderblade. I was born in the Karanas, and grew up with my mother and my sister. We were not that rich, and had to work together to stay alive. My sister was a great hunter, while I spent days out in my rowboat catching fish. However one day, my mother ran onto the beach, tears streaming from her eyes. She cried out to me, 'Reinn! Come back! Your sister is gone!' At that, I jumped off my boat and swam over to the beach, and ran to my mother. My life was never the same again. I ran away to become a pirate but somewhere along the way, I decided to repay my debts to myself. I became a knight. I ran to Qeynos, and looked for somewhere to learn. And, then I heard of the amazing knight, Sugan. We met and accepted me as her apprentice. I now hope to become a great knight.

Sugan's View: In the tradition of Holy Knights before me, I hope to pass some of what I've learned on to another young knight. Reinn has interesting rumors following him, but is really enthusiastic to our cause.

Faileas' View: Sugan? Apprenticing someone? I'd always pictured her on the learning side of the bargain. You have to know something before you can teach it.
