Chronicles of the Obsidian Knight
Part V
 
The Children of blood

He held Sugan tightly as she drifted slowly to sleep in his strong arms lulled by his soft tunes of passion. He could never describe how he felt in that moment feeling her soft warm breath against his chest. Kinor guarded the door faithfully, in fear for the Lady’s life after a dark vision. He listened to the soft footsteps of the guards pass outside the safe haven of the guildhalls as he himself felt the dream like pulls of the netherlands.

“Jamier! Wake up!” he groggily wiped his eyes to see himself standing in the dark forboding hallways of the forbidden castle of mistmoore. The creatures of death lurked to and fro seeming to ignore his presence in the unholy place. Before him stood a ghostly image he never met. His heart pounded with a sense of fear, yet he was too much of a warrior to show it. “Why have you brought me here?” …”Be silent young Paladin and follow” her vioce stuck a chord of malice within the deepest reaches of his soul.

Her irridencent ghostly robes floated down the hallways, and as quickly as she appeared she vanished. Jamier stood in the hallway, lost in the depths of evil. He heard the wails and screams of countless tormented souls in the distance. Cautiously he stepped forward. He heard the sounds of shuffleing. Like a deck of cards in a poker game. Soft was the sound at first. But suddenly it echoed with an earth shattering sound withing the halls. With a thud he felt something drop to his plated boot. He looked down to see a frayed deck or Tarot card. Not much unlike those he had seen used by the fortune tellers in the Oasis of marr.

Jamier knelt down to pick up the deck, with a flash of light the deck disappeared only leaving a few cards remaining. Taking a deep breath as if to reassure his own courage he looked at them. There were no pictures on the cards , only each card had a name written in the ancient almost forgotten language of the Tier’dal. The Lovers, The Magician, The Tower, The hanged man, The Fool. Gently he pulled the Lovers card and was faced with every carnal temptation. His desires and visions became for a moment a reality. He watched as the card burst into flame leaving a charred mark on the palm of his gauntlet. He looked deeply at the ashes and mark that was left. Only to see the symbol of twins etched within the remains.

Jamier forced himself to walk forward. Stopping at a door. Distracted from the moans and screams coming from the other side. He saw a Woman there, much like the painting of Silveril in the guildhalls. She was chained to the bed, wrapped in the arms of ecstacy of the fabled Paladin Stormangel who loved her. The sounds became louder almost deafening him. ‘M’lady!” He screamed. He reached to free her, but she was gone. The once wealthy room laid vacant with only the whisper of winds and leaves blowing across the floor. The leaves parted leaving a picture of her, her heart exposed and a card with the Three of Swords laying across it.

A sense of overwhelming fear gripped Jamier as her turned for the door and found himself standing in a new room. A gentle breeze blew across his face as suddenly the room was filled with song dance and laughter. From the top of the stairwell there stood Sugan dressed like a queen. The beautiful soft white gowns of a virgin waiting to be married. He reached his hand for her, only to see her grab the hand of another. Canelon, the high elven Paladin whom she loved so much. Jamier turn to the paladin only to face a skeleton standing in the once lusterous clothes. The skeleton crumbled to ashes leaving only the Card of Death. Jamier pulled his hand back in discust. The sound of the party faded leaving the shadows of 3 spirits.

Jamier stood in awe at the sights of Sigma, Tau and Tarlyn as the Shamans Cattiebrie and Ralnia described them. He watched as the spirits swirled around creating a misty blue light. He saw Ralnia and cattiebrie emerge from the magical cloud holding each other hands tightly. He moved towrds them, to touch them. But instead they kept on walking. There totems continuing to guard them from the evils withing this accursed hell. Suddenly Ralnia stopped and pulled a card from her pouch . She held it in her hand as she softly blew against it. It floated like a feather before Jamier. “the Herophant” he said softly under his breath looking at the card. Then there was nothing but blackness.

Suddenly he saw a doorway into another hall…it creeked opne hauntingly, he tries to pass but was blocked by 9 staves. With all his strength he tried to pull against them, but they would not budge.He looked beyond the staves and saw an alter. Sugan was kneeling in front of it crying. The staves disolved letting him pass. He reached for her hand only to be left with emptiness and a single card. The picture was a single staff wrapped in vines of ivy. The ace.

Jamier began to sweat, feeling feverish in the fear of the moment. Every hair on the nape of his neck stood up as he felt the eyes of a beast watching him from the shadows. The beast came forward bearing its teeth. Biting and lashing hard at the dark elf. Jamier screemed as the claws sank into his flesh creating a seering burning poisoness pain. He realed back to draw his blade, only to find himself weaponless. The beast stood there snarling at him. Across the breast of the 7 headed monstrocity that took the shape of a Hydra was written the word “Judgement”. Jamier stood in silence awaiting his death. But it never came.

Jamier braced himself stepping forward , only to find himself faced with the decaying corpse of silverann. Hey was being held and stroked like a cherished pet by Envy. He looked at Jamier pleedingly at his imprisonment. In his hand he held a Glowing orb. He reached out offering the orb to Jamier. Hesitantly Jamier took it. It too disolved into a card. The Moon. Within its pictures ever changing images of the shadows.

Jamier closed his eyes murmering words of prayer, to be released from this hell. He only experienced the sensation of his Nostrils being filled with the scent of decay and death carried upon scallding winds that coursed through his hair. He looked down to see the beautiful alibaster floors shape and shift to sulferous lavarock. The screams of tormented souls now pierced his heart as he looked up and saw a battlefield flowing in a river of blood. Among the corpses were children playing, laughing and singing. He heart sank with the haunted moment.

He followed a path with was before him, leading him up the steep lava flowing mountain. He came to the top to where he saw them. Ralnia, Sorcere, Sugan , Faileas, Cattiebrie, Kinor, Laraden, Malgorm, Laele, Malcron, Darain, Silveril, Canelon, and Envy. They all stood there like children. Each covered in patches of dried blood. Ghostly and staring at something that seemed to overshadow them all.

Jamier looked up to see an alter with a symbol of the Twins erected high from the base. He stepped back in fear. Almost losing control as he saw the lovers Crucified to it. Their lifes blood dripping into the rivers of lava and blood that the children played in.

“Jamier! Wake up!”

Jamier opened his eyes to look into the soft green ones of Sugan.

“Are we all children of blood?” he asked in a soft voice Quivering with fear. Sugan looked upon him without understanding.

Jamier took her hand to kiss the Palm... only to stop as he saw within it a scar shaped like the sign of the Twins.
