We hear this fearful tempest sing, Yet seek no shelter to avoid the Storm.  -Shakespeare
We are the Mists, the Rain, and the Thunder. We are the Four Winds that scour the land. With us travels Redemption, Grace, and Compassion. Opposing us is Darkness, Treachery, and Corruption. Our goals are Purification and Enlightenment.

We ride the Wind; embody the Storm.

The path is dangerous, but clearly set. Persistance and Courage will be our shoes, and Mercy our walking staff. Would you like to come along?
