Knights of the Storm
Knights' Status
• 4/26/00
Paladins, Warriors, and Monks belong to the Knights of the Storm. The highest rank of Knighthood is the Genesis Knight of Storms. He or she is most responsible for this court. Directly under the Genesis Knight is the Exodus Knight of Storms. He or she has nearly the same responsibilities as Genesis, and assumes the role fully when Genesis is offline. As for the rest of the court, this is the structure:

  • All new members are given the rank of Page.

  • After Page, you can be promoted to Squire.

  • And after Squire, you can be promoted to Knight.
All rank decisions are made by the ranking officer of the High Court. At this time, Genesis Knight Kainos Murasame is the ranking officer for the Knights of the Storm.

Decisions are based on the following criteria:

  • Impress higher ranking members with good role playing. If you are good enough, you will get the attention of Kainos.

  • Providing equipment to improve the guild. An example would be offering Kainos a gift that the guild can use, like weapons, armor, etc. Kainos and the High Court will determine what to do with the equipment.

  • Defeat a Shadow Strider in a battle of wits. Must be witnessed by another.

  • Defeat a Shadow Strider in a fair duel. Must also be witnessed, but that is easier to with the world wide anouncment. By fair we mean equal to or very close to your level.

  • Higher ranks must perform more of the above to advance.

  • If you are a Squire, you must play at least 5 hours a week.

  • If you are a Knight, you must play at least 10 hours a week.

  • Exceptions can be made if you are having RL difficulties.
Once you are a Knight, you can advance to the rank of Exodus or even Genesis. That would grant you a seat in the High Court, but only Sugan can bestow that honor. Only Knights will be considered for officership when positions are available.

Disrespect to higher ranking members is punishable by demotion and/or disbandment.
