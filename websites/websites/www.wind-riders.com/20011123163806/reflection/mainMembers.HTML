The High Court
Officers of the WR
 

The four quarters of the High Court are the main governing body in the Wind Riders. They each rule over their division. Sugan determines who enters the High Court. Only two members are allowed within each quarter, with one being a chief advisor to Sugan. These chiefs are known as the Four Winds.
