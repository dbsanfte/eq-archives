Wildwynd Stormspirit
Inceptor of Mists
 
History: "Fear not, my little wild one," Lyriian's mother had said the day she and her father stepped out of the Greater Faydark to sail accross the vast Ocean of Tears in order to protect the Feir'Dal against their dark cousins, the Teir'Dal, "We will return as soon as we can."

They never returned.

It was cold the morning her parents left. Bright snowflakes fell through the trees, whirling on the frigid wind. Wildwynd, they had nicknamed their little green-eyed child, and they had raised her to love the wild, untamed lands over all of Norrath. To continue this, she was left in the custody of Faydark's Champions, the Rangers that protected the land in the name of Tunare against those who would warp and destroy it.

For the next several years, Lyriian immersed herself in her training, vowing to grow strong enough to one day venture accross the Ocean to find out what had befallen her parents and to destroy whoever was responsible. When her ruidmentary training was finished, Lyriian was given some food, clothing and a sword and was sent out to put her training to practical use in the wilds of the Greater Faydark.

That day, like the one when her parents left, was bitter cold. And on that day, the child was gone and the adult stepped onto the lift.

Lyriian was no more, and Wildwynd stepped out to meet her destiny.
