Jamier D'Phoenix
Knight of Storms
 
History: Alas I tell my tale once again. It is no secret to you all that i am of Tier'Dal descent by now.

My mother served as a slave in Neriak, a beautiful bard she was. She escaped.. but not before she found out she was whith child, ME. My mother couldnt take me back to her homeland of Kelethin, fearing for my life. So instead she raised me in West commons. My mother became ill one night.. one of her companions took her to a cleric in desperation. They made me wait outside, when they returned they told me she was dead. Later I saw the priest again with a beautiful high elven child ...The child died without healing.. he coud have saved her. When the damned cleric claimed the child's soul in the name of Bertoxxulous... My anger overwhelmed me.. and i struck out.

I am now wanted for murder... I tried running to safety in neriak, but the Wood Elven views of my mother only created the problem of me being seen as a heritic and something to be hunted. I heard of the Wind Riders and Sugan, and asked for her to provide sanctuary. I thank you all for the chance that has been given me.

Jamier
