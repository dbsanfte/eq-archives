Below is an assembled list of Weapons with Effects, and what levels they will begin to "Proc"(begin to use their built in effects).

As anyone who has been given a weapon a bit earlier then normal, or perhaps twinks newbie characters has found out,  if you give a weapon that has a built in effect to a newer character, and this weapon has an effect that goes off randomly.  It is quite possible that they will instead get a message that says "You lack sufficient will to command this weapon".  What this really means is that you are still not at a high enough level to use that weapons effects. What this list will do is help you to establish what weapons you can use to their full potential and at what levels.
