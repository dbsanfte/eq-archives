New way of meeting and a big event to start planning

12:00 PM EST

As many of you may have noticed, sometimes our meetings can be a little slow moving and empty. I think we can easily blame that on the distractions that EQ provides while we are trying to talk.

In order to remedy this, and make the guild closer, I have came up with a new way of us meeting. We are going to try it out this week. When you log onto the server screen, most of you have probably noticed the chat button at the bottom. I am going to be making our own chat room in EQ chat for us to have a meeting. It will have a password, which I will send to you by e-mail.

Many of you may be thinking, "What about meeting in game?!?" Fear not. Here is what I envision. Our chats in EQ chat will be OOC chats about the guilds, ideas and things like that. It will also be a chance for us to get to know each other better PERSONALLY (which I feel will make our guild stronger).

We will still have in game meetings, but they will be for pure fun and role-playing. I feel that this is going to be better. Our meetings will be shorter (I will try to keep them to no longer than 30 minutes), and you don't have to log onto EQ to be at a meeting. This is a trial thing. Once we meet, e-mail me and tell me what you think of it. So, here is all the info about the meeting:

DAY: Friday, July 2nd
TIME: 11:30 PM EST
LOCATION: EverQuest chat room, "Arcane Knights"

Another thing that I have been thinking about is an in game college started by us. Now that sounds impossible when you think of it as colleges as WE know them, but that is not what I have in mind. Essentially we will name the college, make it's home page within our home page, and will only have classes when we announce them. We can probably get them announced on the EverQuest home page even!!

This will be a big topic at the meeting on Friday. I have several ideas about it that I would like to share with you, and give you a chance to share what you think. Until then...

Friday, June 18

Summary of the meeting

3:00 AM EST

As was announced, the Arcane Knights met last night at 11:00 PM. Thanks to guild chat, all members were able to interact and discuss in a way that had never been experienced in our guild before. Many made the journey to Queynos, while others decided to play it safe and stay where they were. Nevertheless, it was one of the most successfully meetings of the Arcane Knights in its history. For those of you who did not attend, here is a summary of what happened there:

  1. Snydar was promoted to Second Lieutenant
  2. The Research division was started and Snydar was appointed head of magician research, and Crystala was appointed head of enchanter research.
  3. Changes in our constitution were discussed. All agreed to a change that would get rid of majors, and replace them with elders. Each class would have an Elder which would server as the head of that class.
  4. We discussed allowing other classes in and decided on letting Shaman enter the Arcane Knights.


We did have an excellent meeting, and I hope these do continue. I will be on vacation next week, so it will be up to Aslan and Snydar to arrange and hold the meeting (if they chose to do so). Good luck to you all, and I will see you in Norrath!

Serro Deimos - Guildmaster
