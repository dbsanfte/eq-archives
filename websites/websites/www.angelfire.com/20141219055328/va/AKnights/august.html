Monday, August 16

Some changes

As many of you may know, I am back in business. I have a working modem, and it is business as usual for me once again. While I was on my little "vacation" I was introduced to a new program, Guildboss. It is a guild management program that will allow me to keep track of ALL aspects of the guild. It has made me think of some new ideas, and made me reconsider some old ones. For instance, our dues system is far to complicated. I want to change it to a flat rate for everyone. Higher levels would be expected to give more, but not required to. If you are wondering what money is used for I am in the process of working out a loan system where the guild will loan guild members money for items (assuming that members promises to either donate the item once they receive something better, or pay back the money). Also, if Verant ever gets around to adding the ability to buy real estate, it will be used for that purpose.

Also, we will now have a new "check in" system. This will help us weed out the active, and inactive members. With each check in, members will update the info that needs to be updated (level, e-mail, ICQ, ect.). Every member would be required to check in once a month, but I recommend you update at least every level or so. That way members no what level you are at. It will be a very simple process and usually require just you typing your name and level unless some other info changes.

In summary, here are the immediate changes:

- A new set due of 3PP that every player must pay every month (earth time). Member between level 1 and 4 will not be required to pay. Members under level 8 can pay every other month. After 8, it is mandatory to all including all Officers and Guildmaster. Consequences of not paying will depend on the level of the member and the circumstances. Members who do not pay will not be removed from the guild, but may be restricted from certain activities, benefits, ect. HOWEVER, repeated, deliberate failures to pay may result in ejection from the guild. NOTE- For all of you who worry I take the money you give me and use it for myself, I don't. I have a separate character I never play that holds on to ALL of the guilds moneys and items.

- A new check in system that characters will use to show their presence in the guild, and to update their info. Every member will be expected to check in at least once a month. If a member does not check in for a period of 3 months, they may risk ejection from the guild.

- No member will ever be removed without PLENTY of warning and time to respond. I do understand that real life commitments do exist, and promise to never remove a member without hearing their side first.

Another thing that I plan of doing soon (I know I have said this a thousand times) is making some changes to the constitution. Many of you deserve promotion, and I need to make places for you to be promoted to.

Well, that about does it for this date. If you look around the other pages that relate to the changes mentioned above should correspond to what I have said.

As always I would love to hear your comments as to the changes. Either e-mail me, or post your opinion on the message board. If there is a problem with any of the changes, I will make changes accordingly. Hope to see you all in Norrath soon.

Serro Deimos - 7:30 PM EST
