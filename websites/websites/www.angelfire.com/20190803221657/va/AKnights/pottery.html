Pottery by Sajet

This is what I have compiled from this evening about pottery.

Introduction to pottery

I suggest you start with at least 10 skill in pottery. I started with 9. I was in Halas when I decided to do pottery. When you first enter Halas, there are two merchants by the gate, one of them is named Sconi or something in that direction. She sells the sketches need for pottery. Next you shall need to go to the cigar shop. When you first enter it the woman to the left will sell you clay, firing sheets, and glass shards which are all crucial componets. I will list the directions for different items later in the guide as well as the prices.

Devloping the skill

The first thing you should make are small jars. I think you can make this to about 20-25 of skill, then it becomes trivial. Next, move on to medium jars until you get to about 40 in skill. Then you can make large bowls until 50. This is where you make a lot of money. At 50 skill you'll be able to make pie tins to the point where the return is greater than your loss. Pie Tins sell for 5-6 gold depending on your charisma. Do not bother with other things like deities or skewers or anything else. It is a waste of money, and some things like deities does not work yet. Look for that in a later patch.

Pie tins will get you way past 115 in pottery and you can make well over 200 plat in about 3-5 days depending on how fast you work. The reason pottery is not a favorite among most people is that the clay is not stackable and niether are the firing sheets, the sketches are and so is the water. It does get annoying digging through a bunch of backpacks but you need patience for this trade.

Materials needed

Small block of clay
Block of clay

Firing sheet
High quality firing sheet
Glass shards.

VERY IMPORTANT- Always remember to include a flask of water when you use the pottery wheel. I forgot a couple times.

Making pottery

Small Jar- block of clay, small jar sketch, flask of water

These are placed in pottery wheel. If you are successful you will need a quality firing sheet. When you are in the Golden Torch, there is a door leading to a backyard. The kiln is out there. When you get to it place the product you got from the wheel, place the quality firing sheet in as well, and then hit combine. If you make it you get a small clay container that holds two things.

That was a recipe as well as an example as to how you create things. I will now list the medium jar, large bowl and pie tin.

Medium Jar- block of clay, medium jar sketch, flask of water, quality firing sheet

Large Bowl- large block of clay, large bowl sketch, flask of water, glass shard

Pie Tin- 2 blocks of clay, pie tin sketch, flask of water, high quality firing sheet
