Dues System.

3pp per month (Earth time)

Member between level 1 and 4 will not be required to pay. Members under level 8 can pay every other month. After 8, it is mandantory per month to all including all Officers and Guildmaster.

Consequences of not paying will depend on the level of the member and the circumstances. Members who do not pay will not be removed from the guild, but may be restricted from certain activities, benefits, ect.

HOWEVER, repeated, deliberate failures to pay may result in ejection from the guild.

The dues system is setup so that we can be sure that the guild has the funds it need to run its programs and events. Donations of item and money are both also needed to help us run well.

NOTE- For all of you who worry I take the money you give me and use it for myself, I don't. I have a seperate character I never play that holds on to ALL of the guilds moneys and items.
