Saturday, May 29

The road to registering

I have copied this information directly off of the EverQuest web page so that all of you can see what we need to do to become official. I may send the first e-mail today. If I do I will make a post saying that I have.

If you decide that none of the existing guilds are quite what you are looking for, you may found your own guild. In order to establish your guild you must have an acceptable name, charter, and a membership of 10 players who wish to form this guild.

The following process needs to be followed to gain approval for you guild:

  1. The leader of the guild must email eqguilds@verant.com. The subject of this email must contain the NAME of the guild. In the body of this email the leader must include his station name, his chat handle, the name of the character that will be the leader of the guild, the name of the primary server of the guild, and a list of the other 9 players who have agreed to establish this guild.
  2. In addition to the above information, the guild leader must include a charter for their guild. This charter should detail their guild's goals and role in Norrath.
  3. The 9 founding members of the guild must email eqguilds@verant.com. The subject of this email must contain the NAME of the guild. In the body of this email the founding members must include their station name, their chat handle, and their character name. Please do not have more than 9 people send in the information.
  4. Once all of the information has been received, it will be reviewed by the staff at Verant Interactive. If any reservations about either the name or the charter arise, the guild leader will be notified via email and be offered an opportunity to modify either as required.
  5. Once the name and charter have been approved, the guild leader will be notified via email that the guild is ready to be created.
  6. Once the guild leader has received notification that the guild has been approved, it is his responsibility to meet with the designated guild creation GM on his server during the designated time. The schedule (all times are PDT) for guild creation is: The Rathe- Wed 7-8pm

As you have seen above, we need a charter. I wrote one last night at the meeting with Aslan and Snydar:

The Arcane Knights will dedicate itself to the training and education of its apprentices in the ways of the arcane arts, as well as the way of honor and nobility. We seek to increase knowledge of all peoples of Norrath through the sharing of information and research.

EverQuest patch message

The servers will be coming down as scheduled tonight at Midnight PDT.

The following changes/bug fixes have been made:

-Spell research components (runes, words, etc.) are now stackable - in doing so, your old ones will no longer trade, nor will they stack with the new ones. Use the old ones up or ditch them.
- Players will no longer crash when an extremely long group message is received.
- Added the ability to pull one item "charge" from a stack by holding down the "Control" key when left-clicking on the stack.
- After casting a spell at an NPC, if the target were lost before the spell made contact, the caster would crash.
- The "/ignore" command will now ignore emotes also.
- The chat command "/e" will trigger "/emote" instead of exiting to the character selection screen.
- We have added new sound tracks to the Planes, Crushbone, Guk, Mistmoore, Steamfont, and the Estate of Unrest.
- We have reduced the amount of experience lost when dying by 50%.
- A number of new quests have been added to various zones.
- Magician pets (elemental) will now cast minor spells (to make up for their lack of kick)
- New Magician spells on vendors and some Magician spells have moved to different vendors
- New Magician weapons for Summon Fang & Spear of Warding & Dagger of Symbols - New enchanter whirl spell (check the vendors)
- Whirl now works on MOBS better than it did on the last patch.
- The saving throw against Memory Blur and Atone was reduced (harder to save against) from the last patch.
- AC added to the Rune Series (Enchanter)
- Alliance series works better (Enchanter)
- Enchanter Attack Speed spells now have longer durations (Quickness, Alacrity, etc.)
- Tashan (the entire Enchanter series) is now quicker to cast, costs less mana, and is harder to save against.
- New bard songs have been added to the vendors
- Paladins lose the spell Bravery, and gain the spell Valor
- ShadowKnights and Necromancers gain the Word of Spirit spell (replacing Word of Shadow for the ShadowKnight)
- ShadowKnights lose Haunting Corpse and gain Summon Dead
- Necromancers and ShadowKnights get a new spell - Shadow Vortex.
- ShadowKnights lose Intensify Death (replaced by Shadow Vortex)
- Rangers lose Shield of Barbs and gain Shield of Brambles
- Shorter stun effects will no longer remove longer stun effects (Bash won't get rid of whirl, for example)

The EverQuest Team
