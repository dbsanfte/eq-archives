Announcement: DoF NDA lifted!
Posted: khalysta @ Sun Aug 21, 2005 2:27 pm
Moorgard posted this saturday night about the NDA being lifted:









Quote:
Participants in the Desert of Flames beta have been released from its Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) as of today.

We wanted to announce this in public so there aren't any doubts as to whether some of the posts you'll be seeing from testers are okay to read or not.

The beta boards will continue to require beta tester access both to view and post in. This isn't to keep information secret, but for two reasons:

1) People posted messages there on the assumption that it was a private forum. We don't want to change that midstream.
2) To keep the same feeling of a smaller community of people all testing on the same server.

Feel free to discuss DoF details in all appropriate public boards and channels.
