Announcement: Not a lot of recent news
Posted: khalysta @ Thu Jan 20, 2005 5:10 pm
Not much has happened in EQ2 since the turn of the year. SOE did however announce they will be releasing "Adventure Packs" in the future as a way of adding in new zones to go along with free additions as well as expansions. Everyone will get the new adventure pack zones but to get the full benefit you will need to purchase the pack.

Also if you haven't noticed there are strange new lights in spots across the shattered lands that appear to come from the ground. If you see one of these then examine a scrying stone and with some luck you might just win something.
