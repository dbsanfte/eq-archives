EVE Online Fan Fest 2004

By: El-Sayed ibn Aly @ 21:16

EVE Fan Fest

The official EVE Fan Fest 2004 will be held in Reykjavik, Iceland from the 22nd to the 24th of October, the same weekend as the Iceland Airwaves festival. The Fest will cover various aspects of EVE, its developers, its players and the explosive mixture of all three.

Fan Fest packages are available via Icelandair. They include the Roundtrip Flight, accomodation at the Nordica Hotel with breakfast, EVE Fanfest fees and Iceland Airwaves pass. Attendees arriving through other ways than the Icelandair package will be able to buy access to the Fest via our store. Complete Packages from $ 602 but book now as the price is only secured until 27th of August.

For inquiries regarding extended stays or special requests, you can contact Icelandair travel agents via the linked form. Note that if you are travelling alone, you need to pay the "single supplement" which is the additional price for being in a single room. You are not put in to a room with someone else unless you book together or contact travel agents directly and both ask specifically to do so.

The EVE Media Center will be open during the weekend in one of the downtown coffeehouses, with wireless internet access, computers to use, CCP staff and some bling bling.

Active Interstellar Services Division personnel receive VIP access that gives access to the ISD get together with the devs, special ISD schwag and $40 USD Credit at the on-site store in return for their Fest admission voucher.

See the EVE website for more info and itinerary.

El-Sayed here - is anyone interested in going to this? I'm playing the free trial of EVE atm, I'm not a big fan but game is pretty decent and lots to do - also complicated - which I sorta like. I wouldn't mind going to this just as a good excuse to visit Iceland, and have some fun exploring. Course I can't afford it but what the heck. Any interest from the ranks? If two of us go we each save about $90 splitting a package deal (meaning sharing a room). Two, four, six, whatever...anyone??? I can't guarantee I could go this minute but if a couple of you cool gals (errr..ok and maybe a dude or two) wanted to go then it would be more tempting.

Star Wars Galaxies Character Purge

By: Joxer @ 16:56

Stolen from the SWG forums...

In mid-October, we will be doing a "character purge" of the system. This will be the big deletion of stale characters and items and it will affect all Galaxies. What this means is that any account that has been cancelled for longer than 6 months will be deleted from the database. Character names, items, lots and everything the character possessed will be permanently deleted. The Character Purge is being done to free up names and game space for incoming and existing players.

After the mid-October purge, we will be performing a character once every month. We will announce all purges at least a week prior.

Kurt "Thunderheart" Stangl
Community Relations Manager
