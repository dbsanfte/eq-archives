ATTENTION: Character Purge is comming!

Letter to the Players March 14, 2002

On Wednesday, March 27th, we will be taking the worlds offline from 8 AM to 11 AM PST to do a database purge.
What this means is that any account that has not had an active subscription since March 21, 2001 (either AC Classic or Asheron's Call Dark Majesty), will have their characters wiped from the database.
If you have an account that has not been active (i.e., no subscription on the account) since then, and you would like to preserve your characters, you will need to renew your account on or before 11:59 PM PST on March 26th, 2002.
Any account that has had an active subscription, regardless for how long, since March 21, 2001, will not be wiped.
--The AC Team

If you have had an AC account which has not been active for over a year, and you do not want it wiped, go to the zone and reup your account today. I expect that many active characters are going to have to find new patrons in the very near future. Please contact me if you need help getting back in the allegiance tree after the purge.

New MUST HAVE decal plug-in: Big Brother

Tasslehof has found a new decal plug in that will revolutionize AC and the way we view allegiances and guilds. The plug-in is called Big Brother, and was written by the Smirking Revenge. For download links and some basic information take a look at this post. Both should the .Net framework download and the Big Brother MSI file should be clean but scan them anyway because good scanning habits are healthy.

Here are some of the most important features:

  • Guild Chat - use .gc to talk in an open channel to all in the guild
  • Quest Scheduling - list of upcoming quests and events
  • Online Status - a list of what members are online, and their basic stats
  • Group Requests - notify others that you want some company hunting
  • Instant Messaging - kinda like a tell but better
  • Extended Friends List

Everyone that in the guild that can run decal 2.0 should download this plug-in today and install it. Even if you hate the concept of decal and look at it as cheating, this is one plug-in we as a guild can not afford to be without. So what are you waiting for? Go download the .Net Framework and Big Brother (or click here for more details) and give them a whirl!
