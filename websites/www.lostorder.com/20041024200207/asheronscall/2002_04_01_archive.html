BigBrother 2.1.821 Release

There's actually been two updated to BigBrother since my last post: Here's the change log, once again I'd highly recommend this pluging for anyone in the allgiance.
Version 2.1.821: Changes:
Added the HUD! The HUD is basically a little configurable thing I draw on top of the AC GUI directly. It can contain your local time, your local coords, and the time left on your buffs (see below)
Added buff time tracking. You can basically pick a spell to be your "buff timer" spell (mine's lightning protect 7 for example) and that spell's remaining time will show up in the HUD.
BB no longer uses character stats to track current health/max health etc. etc. This should fix some issues people have been having with beta decals.
Fixed a couple minor bugs

2.1.821 Install

version 2.1.820:

Changes:
Much improved component tracking/pea splitting.
XP tracking on kills ([BB] You bring Tusker Guard to a fiery end! (For 12824xp)
BB now tracks your resist rate against monsters. ([BB] Banderling Mangler cast Fire Vulnerability Other IV on you Your resist rate is 67/79 (85%))
BB tracks the last 10 death locations/when you died. If you die inside a dungeon, it will attempt to track what landblock/plaintext dungeon name it is, but the database of
landblocks is pretty weak at this point. If you notice that it doesn't know the dungeon, please type .landblock inside the place and .im me the name/landblock so I can add it.
You can emphasize the name (change the color) of a person sending you a tell/that you're telling.
Slightly improves endurance/self tracking for mana/stamina/health tracking.
Lifestone tracking as a nearby item is now optional.

BigBrother 2.1.819 Released

Yup, another new version of BigBrother
version 2.1.819:

Changes:
Fixes to the last BB release involving spellcasting filters. Please retry the overide self spellcast features with -magic UNFILTERED. (@unfilter -magic)
Lots of improvements (IMO) to general in-game messages. Optionally add all sorts of useful information to the various attack/get hit/cast spells/etc. (Check your options)
Added typeable ways to do the various spell recalls. Valid commands are: .lr (lifestone recall), .pr (last portal recall), .pr1 (recall 1st tie), .pr2, .ar (aerlinthe), and .sr (sanct recall)
Added automatic pea splitting. This is the first release of this, so report issues to me. I know one bug is if you have multiple stacks of components it may think you're low on a comp.
Filtered peas as low components.
Optionally filter self evade messages from AC
Optionally filter the new green annoying @recall messages (recall to lifestone/house/mansion)

2.1.819 Install
-- Smirk

Once again this is a killer app that we should all run.

BigBrother 2.1.818 Released

Smirking Revenge has released yet another version of BigBrother. From the BigBrother news page:
version 2.1.818:

Spell words! ([BigBrother] You say, "Malar Zhavik" (Revitalize))
