ur goal as a Highland Clan partly role-play guild is to successfully come together and function as a close knit family of brothers and sisters - a true clan.

The word "clan" originally meant, in Gaelic, offspring or descendants, or more accurately translated, family. We must encourage a sense of unity and family within our clan. Each clansmen or clanswoman reflects the honor of the clan itself. This means we represent the clan at all times. With every action we must show bravery and honor. Remember that the actions of one member can destroy the honor of the entire clan. If we can manage to function as a true clan we will be known throughout Norrath as a guild of valor. Honorable and helpful standing united and strong.

So how are we to interact with our fellow clan members? The same way the Highlanders did with these qualities......

COURAGE Accustomed as the Highlanders were to interminable feuds arising out of the pretensions of rival clans the native courage which they had inherited from their Celtic ancestors was preserved unimpaired. Instances of cowardice were therefore a very rare occurrence. They never ran from a battle. They never feared death.

LOYALTY Highlander's always retained an enthusiastic attachment to their clan and country, which neither distance of place or length of time could dissolve. The reciprocal ties which connected the chief or Laird and his clan were unbreakable.

HOSPITALITY In no country was the savage virtue of hospitality carried to a greater extent than in the Highlands, and never did strangers receive a heartier welcome than was given to a guest who entered a Highland mansion or cottage.

HONESTY A Highlander's word is his bond. Even a verbal agreement was never unfulfilled or denied.

In short we are to behave as this.......

We must always be COURAGEOUS, never running or backing down from a fight. Never being afraid to charge forward into battle shouting our war cry from the depths of our hearts. We must always be prepared to defend our clan and everything we hold dear to us.

We must always be LOYAL, with unbreakable ties that bind our clan together. The needs of our brothers and sisters will always come first above all others including ourselves.

We must always be HOSPITABLE. All strangers will receive a hearty welcome. You will give of yourself to all without asking for payment in return.

We must always be HONEST. We must always have fair dealings. A verbal agreement will never be unfulfilled or denied. You hold the honor of this clan high upon your shoulders. We will be known throughout Norrath in the way that each of us chooses to carry our weight in the clan.
