Welcome!

Thank you for visiting the Deadly Justice website.  Our guild is comprised of seasoned players and good friends, with a healthy variety of races and classes.  We are always on the lookout for new members who can offer new power, excitment, and commradery to our guild.  However, we are very selective when it comes to bringing players into our family.  

In order to begin the recruiting process with Deadly Justice, you will need sponsorship from two different Deadly Justice members.  Once you have this sponsorship, we will begin the evaluation period.  Our recruiting committee will organize groups and/or raids for you to join with Deadly Justice members.  After two weeks of evaluation, the recruiting committee will make a decision.  If the committee approves you, barring any guild disapproval, you will be welcomed into the Deadly Justice family. 

If you are interested in becoming a member, please send one of our officers a tell in game or post on our messageboard.
