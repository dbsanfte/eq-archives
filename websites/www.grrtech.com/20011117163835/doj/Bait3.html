The history of Bait - Chapter 3

 The little gnome on the shabby pier talked of adventure and excitement. I looked at the little gnome and thought why not I haven't been there...

The little raft was wet and half sunk in the ocean. The landing was rough on this desert island with only one soul inhabitant. He was kind of stand-offish but nice enough to direct me to the pier where their gnomish contraption would transport me to the main land. As it paddled through the water, ice gathered around the keel scrapping down the sides. I realized I had made a mistake, Erudites are warm weather people, ahhhh Tox the trees and fog.

The freakish ship bumped against the pier next to a huge glacier. The other Gnomes had build a little village right near the pier where they could watch for supplies and other ships since they did make a living quite dishonestly, they were pirates. They where kind and offered little services but enough to stay alive in the cold waste land.

The land was full of these cats, white as the snow and twice as mean. They where easy to avoid though but the problems started with the possessed snowballs. Then the Giants already angry at the world and wanting to take it out on my frail Erudite body. I first came upon their scouts but then I saw some of their guards around the greatest bridge I have ever seen, almost grand enough to make an Erudite think about connecting Odeus to Faydwer. But then those Elf's would try and cross it...

Across the bridge was a wonderful wild land, beasts of all forms unseen by any Erudite before. Monsters lived here too, blue Orcs that were incredibly stronger then their main land cousins and Half Walrus Half human things (only dumber then both). This land was still cold, so cold that the Dworfs that lived here were blanched to a blue. There where more giants and a City of theirs, they still didn't like me or my kind. They don't deserve to live in my opinion.

Through this waste land on the other side was a divided land with these Marvelous creatures Dragons that wander the frozen river and the same dwarves in fact the Dwarves have their city there hidden and buried like all dwarves deep in the mountain. They are suspicious of me but they still tolerate me and like my business I suppose. Plat is hard to find to them.

I am still looking for some more clear water, my line is unwet and my bait grows old. Maybe further exploration is needed.
