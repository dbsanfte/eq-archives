Welcome!

Thank you for visiting the Dawn of Justice website.  Two powers of Tholuxe Paells, Dawn of Power and Deadly Justice, have converged to produce a new greater power, "The Dawn of Justice".  Our guild is comprised of seasoned players and good friends, with a healthy variety of races and classes.  We are always on the lookout for new members who can offer new power, excitment, and commradery to our guild.  However, we are very selective when it comes to bringing players into our family.  

In order to begin the recruiting process with the Dawn of Justice, you will need sponsorship from two different Dawn of Justice members.  Once you have this sponsorship, we will begin the evaluation period.  Our recruiting committee will organize groups and/or raids for you to join with Dawn of Justice members.  After two weeks of evaluation, the recruiting committee will make a decision.  If the committee approves you, barring any guild disapproval, you will be welcomed into the Dawn of Justice family. 

If you are interested in becoming a member, please send one of our officers a tell in game or post on our messageboard.
