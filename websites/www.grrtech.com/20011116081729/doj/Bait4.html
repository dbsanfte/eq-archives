The history of Bait - Chapter 4

 After looking around the dragon city I found a way to a new place. A little place with Wild dragons, Elephant seal people and the Omithar. The Bulthorks are as angry as the Ulthorks but stronger and a lot meaner. The Omithars are the sworn enemy to the Bulthorks and the Ulthorks alike, they even rewarded me for proof that I have killed soem of them. the Omithars are a people that I could live with forever. They follow Prexus and warship the bounty of the sea. They dont have any fishing bait but that is I think because they can just swim after what they are after for dinner. Below the cliff is a great place to live, the sea even keeps it relatively warm down here, or is it that the cold is seaping into my very soul.

Something was still missing. I wanted to search more. Maybe I am not looking for poeple like the Omithars who follow my ways but something else. I have to wander some more and try to find what I need. My soul is still wandering.

Back to wandering the frozen wastes. I know where the Omithars are and I may end up forever living with them but until I see what I am after I will not stay. The ice helps to cool the burning desire to see more and do more. I head back into the caves under the Orc Fort. The Orcs are even orginized enough to have built a frort of their own, not stollen or claimed from raiding someone elses town. They did however build it on top of entrance to a mystical cavern where there are many Orc miners digging up the Velium and a Dwarf town where they are in constant battle with the Orcs.

Deaper into the Caves are translucent spiders and parts of the caves that have come alive. The terrors are viscious and unthinking just like the spiders. Creatures that dont think earn my disgust. I still have killed few of these because they are smart enough to gang up on any single person. Kindness is something that the others here have not heard of and My services are seldom wanted. Still nothing was here for me to find. My search was growing old, I need to find something that I do not know.

The cold has worn my nerves thin. I have decided to head back to the main land and maybe even home. I could start a little shop and fish. Maybe I could even head out to the Omithars and live with them.

I start scaling the magnificant bridge, almost the acme of the span. Suddenly pain strikes my back. A dagger icy and frozen buries itself in my back, my blood flows over my robe and across my body as I lay face down. I try to look and see where this strike came from, nothing anywhere. I see a vision right before everything goes black, an angel, pale as the snow but real and caring.

Who? Why? Revenge? Angel? Dead? Alive? Where?
