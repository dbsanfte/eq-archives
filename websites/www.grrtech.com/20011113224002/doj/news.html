5/5/2001
 Results of Guild Meeting

Note: edited, for full version see message board

This primarily deals with the meeting. We will be staying in this guild, there will not be a merger at this time and if we change our minds es doesnt want us en masse. We will be posting on their application boards and go through the regular recruitment process should we decide to split and head there.

Our own recruitment is an issue we need more chanters, clerics, tanks to be able to do more. Our aux healers and tanks along with a few jabobers can substitute fairly well, but we need chanters. The feeder guild idea seems fairly unpopular so far, at this time I dont know that we have enough to warrant one in guild and not many seem to want to be a feeder to es. A feeder to es seems to me to be fairly equitable. Personally to allow all of you to do the most being a feeder and placing the highers in es seems like it might be a good idea. The odds of me and my crew if you will (rad, sacer, leia, vinger, ditty, devoor, etc) joining es however are slim at this point. If you want to know why ask me in game.

Speaking with callandria, she was interested in an alliance or possibly being our parent guild. To that end we have been invited to a raid sunday with them sign up on their board at www.eternalsovereign.com I would recommend you go if you can.

I personally want to go back to being deadly justice, that will wait due to our state of flux right now. Also ask me if you have questions on that. That was my guild before we merged, I want to get away from the stigma we have gotten lately and well theres a new sheriff in town.

As far as what we can do I saw kedge for the first time at 52 and had a good time. May be later, but sooner doesnt necessarily mean better. If I end up getting my em legs at 60 because thats how long it takes it doesnt matter much to me, time heals all wounds and I'd rather stomp through things with good friends that I respect than people I tolerate. Upcoming, we will be doing more mid level raids. I want to do alot of the dungeons most havent seen but is achievable currently. This will be a balance between experience and raid days, to that end I'd like to see a big one like kedge and maybe a 1 or 2 group raid like cc over the weekends so people can attend raids and advance in level. Obviously we need the participation of most for the big ones.

My work schedule is a real problem for this I wont be on today simply because of it. I will be looking to others to start leading these and helping in general especially as I start deploying for a week or more at a time coming up. I will be looking for a strong assistant leader to take over in these periods, someone I know can and will be on and take care of business.

Yet another good one to tell me about in game. Well good huntin all hope to see you tomorrow and please post here or tell me in game if you want to or just for the hell of it. Use your officers thats what their there for.

4/10/2001 Kedge Keep

DoJ will be raiding Kedge Keep on Saturday, April 21 at 2pm PST / 5pm EST

Posted By Vinger:

On Saturday, April 21, at 2pm PST (5pm EST), I will be planning a Kedge Keep raid. This is one that many people have asked about, so here it is!! This raid will be for anyone 35+, and needs a good turnout, as we will need 3-4 groups to take down Estrella and Phingel. Start your planning now, with the following tips:

Save up to buy a Fishbone Earring(1.5-2k pp) if you can. If you cant buy a Fishbone, then bring plenty of fish scales please. The magicians Radiac and Koronado will help us greatly on this for their ability to summon waterstones, but a fishbone earring is still better since you never have to worry about your Enduring Breath being lost during a fight or even dispelled, and if you go AFK (the biggest killer in Kedge).

This will be a very fun and challenging raid for everyone so anyone that can go and are 35+ please try and make it and this can be a successful and a raid to remember. Thanks!

3/5/2001 Guild Meeting Summary
Thank you to all who were in attendance for our meeting on Saturday.  Everything went quite smooth and much was accomplished.  The main subject of our meeting was in reference to our recruiting procedure.  

Recruiting

We will soon have a committee of 6-7 guild members who will be in charge of recruiting.  They will be evaluating our new prospects, attempting to get new prospects in DOJ groups/raids, make a decision based on what they have seen after a 2 week evaluation period.  If after the 2 week evaluation, the committee determines that the recruit should be invited, the name of the recruit will be posted on the messageboard for 3 days.  If, after three days, no one has posted an objection to the invite, the recruit will become a new member of the Dawn of Justice.

Alternate Characters    

Everyone in the guild should have a guilded secondary character.  These characters will be needed for plane raids.  Please keep your alternate characters below level 25 on roleplay.

That about sums it up.  For more information please visit the members only section of our messageboard.
