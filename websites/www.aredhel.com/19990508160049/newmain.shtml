Welcome to the Aredhel

We are the Aredhel, a society of noble Elves found in the vast realm known as Norrath.  We are always searching for refined High Elves,  clever Wood Elves, and courageous Half-Elves who will join our cause, bringing light to the dark and perilous world of Everquest, promoting brotherhood and roleplaying for the enjoyment of all.

Welcome, traveler, to our home.  We invite you to explore our halls and learn of us and our heritage.  Perhaps ye shall be enticed to join our ranks someday...
