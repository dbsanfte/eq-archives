For those interested... Posted Wednesday, October 30, 2002 by Brynja
Amicitia et Delectatio is a guild formed of friends. We are a small but very tight group. We will not openly solicit for new members and we do not mass recruit but we have a lot of fun.

If the guild interests you, please stop by our message boards and get to know us, post a question or comment or just tell us a little bit about yourself. You will find that most are warm and inviting folks who just like to spend quality time in the company of good friends.

We welcome comments and look forward to meeting you in Norrath on Solusek Ro.
