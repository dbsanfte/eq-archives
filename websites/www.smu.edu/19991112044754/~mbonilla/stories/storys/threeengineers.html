Three Engineers

Three engineers are riding in a car. Suddenly the car stops running. They ease over to the side of the road and look at each other, wondering what could be wrong.

"Well," says number one, "I'm an electrical engineer. I think we should strip down the electronics and see where a fault might have occurred."

"Well, I don't know much about cars," says number two. "I'm a chemical engineer. But I think maybe the fuel is emulsifying and blocking something somewhere."

"Well," says number three, "I'm a Microsoft engineer, and I don't know much about anything. But I've got a great idea. Why don't we close all the windows, get out, get back in, open all the windows, and maybe it'll work?"
