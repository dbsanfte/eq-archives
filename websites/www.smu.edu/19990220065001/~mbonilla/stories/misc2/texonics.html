Texonics (i.e., Texican)

Texonics: Texas' answer to Ebonics. Currently the language is broadcast in many forms, the most notable being Big Tex, a large Texan who broadcasts solely at the State Fair of Texas in October of each year. His grass roots efforts over the last forty years have led to a full program being considered by the Texas school system. Here is a sample curriculum:
