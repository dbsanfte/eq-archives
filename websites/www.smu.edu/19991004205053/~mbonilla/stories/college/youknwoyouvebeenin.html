You Know You've Been In College Too Long If...

1. You consider McDonald's "real food."

2. You actually like doing laundry at home.

3. 4:00 AM is still early on the weekends.

4. It starts getting late on the weeknights.

5. Two miles is not too far to walk for a party.

6. You wear dirty socks three times in a row and think nothing of it.

7. You'd rather clean than study

8. "How did it get so late!" comes out of your mouth at least once a night.

9. Half the time you don't wake up in your own bed and it seems normal

10. Minesweeper is more than a game...it's a way of life.

11. You schedule your classes around sleep habits and soaps.

12. You know the pizza boy by name.

13. You go to sleep when it's light and get up when it's dark.

14. You live for getting mail.

15. Looking out the window is a form of entertainment.

16. Prank phone calls become funny again.

17. It feels weird to take a shower without shoes on.

18. Whole wars can take place, and you are clueless (no connection to the outside world).

19. You start thinking and sounding like your roommate.

20. Blacklights and highlighters are the coolest things on earth.

21. Rearranging your room is your favorite pastime.

22. You find out milk crates had so many uses.

23. Wal-mart is the coolest store.

24. The weekend lasts from Thursday to Sunday, (or Wednesday morning to Tuesday night).

25. You are sitting around making lists about how you know you've been in college too long.

26. If you have ever price shopped for Top Ramen.

27. If you live in a house with three couches, none of which match.

28. If you consider Mac and Cheese a balanced meal.

29. If you have ever written a check for 45 cents.

30. If you have a fine collection of domestic beer bottles.

31. If you have ever seen two consecutive sunrises without sleeping.

32. If your glass set is composed of McDonald's Extra Value Meal Plastic Cups (ie. Olympic Dream Team I or II).

33. If your underwear supply dictates the time between laundry loads.

34. If you cannot remember when you last washed your car.

35. If you can pack your worldly possessions into the back of a pick-up (one trip).

36. If you have ever had to justify yourself for buying Natural Light.
