75th Anniversary

As they cut the cake for their 75th anniversary, the old man nuzzled the old lady's ear and whispered:

"Darling, there's something I've wanted to ask you all these years. Our eleventh child never did look like his brothers and sisters. Did he have a different father?"

The old lady lowered her eyes and murmured,

"Yes dear, he did."

The old man just stood there for a while without saying a word. Finally, he asked,

"Who was it?"

The old lady said softly, "You, dear."
