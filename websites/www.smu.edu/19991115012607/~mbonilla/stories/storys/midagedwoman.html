A Middle Aged Woman

A middle aged woman living at home with her elderly parents

The mother walks by her room and sees her on the bed with a vibrator, and asks, "What are you doing?". "I'm old, ugly, and I'll never finda man", she replies - the mother walks on.

The next day the father walks by her room, she's again on the bed with a vibrator, he asks, "What are you doing?". "I'm old, ugly, and I'll never find a man", she replies - he walks on.
