International Relations

There is a plane crash in between several Caribbean islands. There are only 15 survivors who manage to make it to a small deserted island. The survivors are:

3 Italians (two men and a woman)
3 French (two men and a woman)
3 Germans (two men and a woman)
3 Greeks (two men and a woman)
and 3 Brits (two men and a woman).

Six months later....

One Italian man had killed the other, and was living with the woman.
The French had a delightful menage a trois.
The Germans had a system of strict rotation. Hans on odd days, Franz on even. The Greek men were living together and had the woman doing the housework.
And the Brits..... were still waiting to be introduced to each other.
