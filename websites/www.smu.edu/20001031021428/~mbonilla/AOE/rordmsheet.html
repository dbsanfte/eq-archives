RoR DM Civs Guide Sheet

total points 60
Rushing 0-10
Rushing is the speed at which a civ can expand in the first few minutes of the game, taking up as much land as possable. A few factors determine the rushing factor of a civ. First being what units does the civ have. HAs, Legions, Eles, and Towers are the best rushing units. Civ bonus is another factor, if a civ has bonuses that apply to rushing units, you can be sure they will have more powerfull rushes. Architecture is the last factor, civs with out it, have a much slower build up.
Power 0 - 30
Power is a measure of a civs ability to fight when the game has gotten past the rush stage, and into the large scale battles. The factors involved are units and bonuses. Most Dms are decieded by the power of a civ, so that is why so much importance is placed here.
economy 0 - 10
A good economy is definitely a factor in DM play. Factors here are first market upgrades, coinage being the most important. Followed by Civ bonuses, and their usefullness. Pho's wood cutting bonus for example is much more usefull than shang's cheap villagers. Use of res is another factor. A civ with out Balista towers for example, will have to use more gold/wood/food units thus creating more strain on those areas than civs that have them.
versatility 0 - 6
Versitility of a civ measures how many different styles and strategies it can implement. A civ that is highly versitile can easily change tactics partway through a game, to exploit opponents weaknesses better. The greater number of primary and secondary units a civ has, the greater its flexability. Catafracts are not included, because of their overall worthlessness in DM. While they are an option some civs have, and a nesesity to one(yamato), they are not worth using unless no other options are available.

endgame 0 - 4
endgame measures how well a civ does with out gold. Chariots are of course the key units here, civs with scythe chariots run over non SC civs. Balista towers do play a large role here as well. This section is worth so few points since most games do not reach the low gold stage
