FATALIK

For those of you that don't know me, I shall explain. I will start at the beginning to let you know how it was that I came to be.
In the hills of Qeynos I was born. I lived with my parents, scratching a living the land. My father, a mighty warrior, tried to teach me the ways of steel, but always were the weapons clumsy in my hands.
We lived a peaceful life, and here is where my story must turn dark. I can still remember the howls of gnolls as they raided our home. I remember my father's heroic stand before those foul beasts, watching them fall in a heap at his feet until his sword failed him. I hear in nightmares my mothers screams as she tryed to guard me while the beasts slashed at her. Had it not been for a wandering monk of the Silent Fist, I would have joined my family that day in death's grip.
Trapped under my mother's corpse I watched as fists and feet succeeded on the beasts where my father's sword had not. After the battle had been won, I was offered refuge within the walls of the Silent Fist. It was there that I learned the ways of the monk, a fighter never in need of a weapon that could fail or break.
I enjoyed my time there, though it was an incredibly hard life. I recall my body aching terribly and being unable to move many a time after a hard training session. But gradually my skills improved, and there I left the safety of the guild walls to further train and learn in the dangers of Norath.
In my journeys I have experienced much. From Blackburrow I learned peace as I emerged from it's depths with revenge finally achieved. From my battles in the plains of Karana I learned patience and honor. From the peaks of HighHold I learned of the stregnth inside me. And from the commonlands I learned of familyn and friendship.
Though I fight among those who call the darkness of Neriak their home, I have found a sense of belonging. Proudly shall I wear the banner as an officer of House Kel'dar, dedicating myself to the holor of battle, the trust in myself, the power in my hands, and the friendship and loyalty of our House.

CALIPTO

RACEDark Elf
CLASSRogue
TITLEShadow

I am the master of the darkness, the shadow that slips by unoticed. Raised in Neriak, a city of eternal darkness and entrigue. My heritage is unknown, and will always stay that way. I am a creature of the dark, prowling the streets as a youth and carving a name for myself in the backs of my enemies. And as destiny calls, I will follow it from the streets of Neriak to the alleys of Freeport to the cursed lands of Felwithe, all in search of my next victem.
Those that I allow near me know that I share no love for the lighter races. They merely serve a means to the end. I will fight for those close to me to the end, and those that spite me shall know my wrath.
I refuse to wield the cumbersome swords of the warriors, to depend on a god to give me powers, or dedicate myself to the magi arts as my body wastes away and I can barely stand against the weakest foe should my spells fail. I prefer the dark, the night, the life of a rogue........the shadows.
You will know you are being hunted. But you will never know how I choose it to end. I may slip behind you, ending your thoughts before they have even begun. Or I may play with you, dodging your feeble attempts to attack me as I slice your body bit by bit. And then you will realize that suddenly you are weak, and you will see the mocking smile in my eyes as I show you what a creature of the shadows can do. You shall know true fear, the only thoughts to escape. As I strike the fatal blow, my cruel laughter will be the only sound you hear as I slip away and the darkness consumes you. Not a sound, not a whisper...a shadow of utter darkness.
