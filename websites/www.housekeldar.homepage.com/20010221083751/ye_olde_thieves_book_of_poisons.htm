Here you will be able to make the Regular Vials. Be sure to have a good supply of Zombie Skin as its one of the best you can use as It is light and stackable.

Combine on the Pottery Wheel...

1 Vial Sketch (Regular, Lined or Capped, Difficulty represented in the prices)

1 Small Block of Clay

1 Flask of Water

1 Zombie Skin

You will get an Unfinished Vial. Combine in The Kiln with a HQ Firing Sheet and you will get your vial. When they are Trivial, you can move onto Lined Vials that are made in exactly the same way. Vials are stackable so you can keep a good supply in the bank.
