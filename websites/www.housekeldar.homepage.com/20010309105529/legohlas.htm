LEGOHLAS

TITLEStalker
RACEWood Elf
CLASSRanger

OTHER CHARACTERS
MortuusHigh Elf Enchanter

I was born in the city of Kelethin. A marvelous city in the trees whose beauty, I believe, is unmatched by any other city in the world. I was raised as any normal child. My father was a fletcher; one of the best in the lands. I was schooled in archery and fletching by my father, and was taught foraging and tracking by teachers. Throughout my youth I enjoyed wandering in the woods of faydark. I would spend days at a time out there, hunting and observering interesting specimens of flora and fauna.

Then the orcs came. A horde of orcs known as the Crushbone clan invaded our peaceful forest. They built a stronghold right outside of the forest, and set up camps in it. The faydark was no longer safe for us. Along with the help of our high elf cousins, we set up a resistance against them. About the time I became of age, I helped out by tracking the progression of the soldiers, and joining in hit and run missions. We set up ambushes for them and have since killed many of them. Their strong alliance with a faction of dark elves has kept them alive.

Since then I have searched the lands to find brave people to aid us in our struggle. I have journeyed far and wide, becoming stronger every day, and hope to one day return to my homeland in order to destory the orc threat.
