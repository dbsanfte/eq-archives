Kel' Do 'Ha' TaraeDark Elf Wizard




TITLEKel' Do 'Ha' (Angel of Mercenaries)
CLASSWizard

I am first born of the mighty mercenary rogue everyone knows as "T'pae", my mother died at my birth and my father was rather disappointed in me being a girl. He brought me up as if I was his first son and introduced me to the art of evil and deception. I got to know every dark mercenary in the land of Norrath and soon I became one of them as their "little angel".
They took me on their assignments to bring good luck and fortune and I used that time to watch and learn. My path was clear, even if I was a girl it was obvious that one day I would lead this army of mercenaries until the day of my 14th birthday came .... It was a dark day for my father since it was then that my true destiny was revealed. I remember I was on one of the missions when the subject of dying escaped and took me hostage, in a cloud of rage and anger I whirled a destructive spell at him and he dropped dead at my feet. Not long after that a powerful wizard came to my fathers house where they talked for hours. The wizard took me with him and brought me up as if I was his own, teaching me the art and everything he knew. Now, 19 years later I am called the "Kel' Do 'Ha'". the angel of mercenaries, as I am a wizard and the leader of the mercenaries of "House Kel'dar" How I got that position as the "angel" or became a family member of the "House Kel'dar" is a long story ... Ask me to tell you when we meet and maybe I will let you live long enough to hear it so you can take the tale with you to your grave. For one thing is certain, when you see the angel you know that it's going to be your last day spend upon this world!
