General Commands Pets Communication Guild Emotes

/anon or /a
toggle anonymous
/auction
Auctions item for sale to the zone
/assist
Current targets target becomes your new target.
/autosplit
Automatically splits any loot you get with the rest of your party.
/bug or /b
Takes you to a bug-reporting screen.
/consent [name]
Gives that person permission to loot your corpse.
/consider or /con
Gives vital statistics of a targeted creature, PC or NPC.
/corpse
Summons your corpse to your side
/decline
declines offer to group.
/duel or /d
Challenges targeted PC to a duel.
/exit
Emergency game exit. May cause hull breach.
/follow
Follow targeted group member
/friend [name]
Adds or removes a ‘friend’ from your friends list.
/friends
 lists the names of everyone on your friends list.
/loc
Your current x,y coords.
/loot
loots a corpse which is located in shallow water or lava, keeping you from having to go in yourself.
/petition <msg>
Used to request assistance for a GM.
/played
Gives you the birthdate & hours played w/ your character.
/quit
Same as exit.
/random (x)
Generate random number 1-x. Why bother?
/roleplay
Makes your name darker to signal people you wish to roleplay.
/shownames
Turns on or off visible names above PC's and NPC's heads.
/shownpcnames
Turns on or off visible names above NPC's heads.
/split # # # #
Splits plat, gold, silver, & copper eg: split 0 5 3 10
/stopsong
stops your song (bards).
/time
Gives you the time of day in Norrath.
/who [all](all zones) [class][race] [corpse] [friends]

 

/pet as you were -- forget other commands

/pet attack (with NPC targeted) -- attack target

/pet back off -- quit attacking target

/pet follow me -- start following you

/pet get lost - pet suicides

/pet guard here – Only attack nearby NPCs

/pet guard me -- Attack ANY nearby NPC

/pet whos the leader – Report pet summoner

/pet report health - tell you hit points

/pet sit down -- Sit and rest (just like you)

/afk – Toggle "away from keyboard"

/feedback -- Takes you to the feedback/comments screen.

/filter – toggle bad language filter.

/g (or /gsay)-- Talks to everyone in your group. Or right click message window to make default.

/ignore [name] -- Turns off all text from that player

/msg --

/ooc -- Makes an out of character statement to everyone in the zone.

/reply or /r-- Sends a /tell to the last person who sent you a /tell message.

/say --

/shout -- Shouts to all of the zone. Should be used less.

/tell [name] -- Sends a message to the named character.

/guilddelete --

/guildinvite -- Officers only. Invites someone into your guild.

/guildpeace [name of guild] -- Guildmaster only. Declares peace with your enemy.

/guildsay -- Say to all guild members.

/guildstatus -- Brings up guild information.

/cheer -- Your character cheers. Animated

/frown -- You frown. *
