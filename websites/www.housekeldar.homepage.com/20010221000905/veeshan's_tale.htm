The Tale of Veeshan's Veil

Compiled by Master Fatalik Surestrike of the House Kel'Dar during a GM event on Xegony held on 2/22/2000.

Back to Tips & Tricks

Greetings all. This night I was lucky to be part of a GM event. I had died and on my way through Steamfont I found a female bard, with a drake that seemed to be quite affectionate for her. She bade us to sit down so that she could tell us a tale.

After a few moments around ten-twelve of us sat down, and she began her tale. While she went on, she played her lute..and all our eyes were glued upon this new and enchanting stranger. Below is a copy of the tale that she was gracious enough to tell us. I enjoyed it immensly as it was not the same as most (just kill all the gm run monsters). I look forward to the future of this story. I am somewhat unsure. Possibly this is the introduction of the new expansion and the Iksar race. If this story continues and I am not there to hear the tale, please get screen shots and record what there is, as I am interested. Continuances of this story can be sent to the webmisstress for further publication.

Sainy says, Friends pay heed, for the song I have to sing is of the greatest importance....

The song is called The tale of Veeshan's veil

From the world's core, the rock children rose
Shielded from wurms eyes by dirt and stone
With gnarled hands and broken bones
They carved their place into Veeshan's home

Soon after, the deserts and woodlands and swamps
All nurtured life planted, by interloping Gods
Veeshan left her children, in peaks carved by her claws
Turned her back on Norrath, and her fellow Gods laws

The Crystalline Dragon, content in her lair
Sealed off the Sky, and still slumbers there
Some dragons went mad, apart from their Queen
Others took charge and formed a wurm Ring

The rebels were hunted and sent from the peaks
To dwell in the lands inhabited by the weak
One of these rebels, a lady white as snow
Swept up a shard of nature unknown

As years dragged on, one by one the wurms fell
Into a deep slumber, from what no one could tell
Their magic had faded, because of the shard?
***A drake circles Sainy protectively***
Or perhaps from the magic they call Veeshan's Veil

Now a new verse to this legend I'll share
In regards to the Sky, and what is happening there..
Veeshan still slumbers, the veil has worn thin
The Wurm Queen's neglect wakes her children again.
***Sainy pauses for a moment***
***A drake wraps it's tail around Sainy's leg***
***A drake nibbles on Sainy's hair then floats out of reach***

My song is done, but I will warn you to heed the words of my verse for they speak the truth!

Mortals now visit where Veeshan slumbers, and only dragons once dwelled....
But the door to the Sky is not a one way portal.
There is much to learn from the great Wurms.....and much to fear
***A drake whistles***

Come my friend...it is time that we move on.
There are many more to hear this tale
***A drake screeches***
Thank you my friends

And from there our new friends departed, with not a sword or spell lifted in a murderous intent towards this wondrous couple.
