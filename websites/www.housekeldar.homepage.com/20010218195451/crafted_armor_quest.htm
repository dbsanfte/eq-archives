Crafted Armor Quest

Compiled by Protecter Starlyli of the House Kel'Dar

Back to Tips & Tricks

If you go into South Karana from the bridge head out straight and slightly to the left. When you get far enough out you will see a few large buildings. This is the centaur Camp (120, -2200). Inside the building to the right there are 2 large named centaurs and 2 little sherites. The 2 named are brothers, Ulan and Shakrn Meadowgreen. Ulan sells bow staves and assorted goods. If you hail both of them they will each tell you that if you are a worth warrior you can get some very nice armor.

Ulan will have you complete some tasks to further progress his studies in blacksmith for the different items. These items and tasks are as follows:
Bracer: 1 bunch of optic nerves from Evil Eyes and 1 Sapphire gem. (per bracer)
Breastplate: Werewolf Talons (which are found from the Gypsy Musician of Mistmoore) and 3 rubies
Greaves: Dark Circlet from Trazlan (who is near Bonecracker in Najena) and 2 Star Rubies
Pauldrun: Need the 3 pieces of the "Rune of Dwarves" that were stolen by the Runny Eye clan (Borxx drops this) and 1 Fire Emerald

Shakrn requires that you bring back proof of all the kills you make for him. The proof and cost are as follows:
Helm: Fire Totem (from Sol-A) and 1 Ruby
Boots: Frost Totem (Shaman in Permafrost) and 2 Sapphires
Gauntlets: Aviak Charm (from roaming Avocets in S. Karana) and 2 Star Rubies
Vambrace: Eye of a Griffon and 2 Fire Emeralds
