STARLYLI

TITLE

Protector
CLASSWarrior
E-MAILcelestria@home.com

I am called Starlyli, for that is the name stitched on the backpack in which I was found. I was found at a very young age by a group of Ogres in the Rujarkian Hills. The story they tell me is that they were out hunting when they heard the screams of battle. As they looked down into a valley, they saw a Dark Elven female fighting for her life. As they came running down the hill, the creature ran away and my mother collapsed. When the ogres approached, my mother whispered "please help my baby" and died. The ogres didn't know what to make of this strange request, as they saw no baby, and began to loot the dead body. Upon removal of her backpack, they found out what she was referring to….me, sound asleep, amongst some ruined cat pelts.
Growing among the ogres, I was not brought up to believe in any specific god, but only in the evil ways. My physical training was not a thing of teaching, but of survival, as the ogres are a very rough, crude race. I was tiny compared to ogre children of the same age. I learned that evil was the true way of life, and if I wanted to survive, fighting was the way to do it. Once I was old enough to hold my own, I was given a backpack with rations and a crude weapon, and was told the way to get home. I was on my way to my true home, Neriak.
When I walked into the city gates for the first time, the beauty was overwhelming, as was the complexity of the city compared to Oggok. I looked around and approached the first friendly face I saw, asking where the warriors hall was. That face belonged to a handsome male with eyes a blue like the sky just before the moon rises. He smiled, introduced himself as Caspiyn of the House Kel'Dar, and said that he would be honored to escort me there. I immediately felt my heart go out to him.
He took me to the hall, and introduced me to the guildmaster who heard my tale, gave me a clean tunic to wear, and suggested that I train there for a bit to learn the more classic fighting styles of the dark elves. As I bowed to the master in thanks, I realized that Caspiyn was still there. He smiled at me again, and said that if I liked, he would return in a fortnight to introduce me to his friends. I agreed.
He indeed did return, simply smiling and saying "follow me." As he led me through the labyrinth of a city into the forest, he told me tales of his house, both as a family & as adventuring companions. Once out into the forest, he led me to a large log where there were many elves congregated. He took my hand and led me through the crowd to a beautiful female named Tempestade. Caspiyn bowed before her, and asked if he could present his new friend to the house. Tempestade rose, embraced me in a hug and simply said, "welcome to our family sister." Before I knew it, I had a rather large family. Many of these elves are very special to me, as if I knew them in a past life. It is a strange feeling.
Today, I am an officer of this house, known as the Protector. I pledge my heart to Caspiyn eternally, and my life to my family, the House Kel'Dar.
