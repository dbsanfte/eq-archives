VYLAAD


TITLEMalefactor
CLASSNecromancer
E-MAILWowbagger@home.net

Unable to defy, disobey, or denounce the commands of M'Lord Innoruuk, I followed his droning call into the waters outside my birthinghome long ago in my most early youth. Whether above water or beneath, I followed. Over bogs and swamps, under swift-currented subnorrathean caves, I followed.
I came to the surface and crawled upon dry earth upon the banks of Neriak's Third Gate. Just to my right, some deserving soul was being drained of his very lifesblood by a party of three Ghouls. I was not daunted and followed my Father's beckoning through the moaning hall of the Ghouls. I passed the beasts, and instantly felt my very heart blacken and freeze - there was no past for me, only this. Necromancy. I live to kill - life offends me. In my hands, I hold the power to control the undead minions of Norrath - use them for my own ends, or destroy them to fuel this newfound inner hate. All hail the black, festering malice of Innoruuk!
House Kel'Dar gained acceptance to me through the actions of one Nugan - a beastly Troll who felt it necessary to assist me in my quest for souls. He mentioned the House and offered the name of Commander Colubridae - I approached him near to Freeport in East Commons and his consort, Tempestade the Cleric, offered me a chair in this fine group. I accepted. Any others finding need to destroy the pale, life-loving races and beasts of Norrath shall find an ally in me. I kneel in ultimate homage to House Kel'Dar - long may it stand.
