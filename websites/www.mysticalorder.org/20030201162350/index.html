Yes, it is true. The face painting, flaming batton twirling, snivelling excuse for a god is dead. While some people may be lead to the believe that his demise was the result of a nervous breakdown brought on by the agony and indecision of his future profession, be under no illusions. Those prancing fairies of death, the most effeminate force to be reckoned with, the High Elf Death Squad laid waste to their own god. With the moral support of the rest of the guild, of course.

He dropped two of those golden runed daggers or whatever the hell the rogue pokers are called. They look really snazzy. And have magically relegated me to the most underequipped rogue in my guild. Weapons wise at least.
