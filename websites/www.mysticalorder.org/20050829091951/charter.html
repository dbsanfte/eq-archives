The Charter Sent to Verant

We are The Mystical Order. From the palaces of Erudin, to the murky swamps of Grobb, we are all races and all classes. There is no good or evil in this guild, but more of an alliance between all. We travel together everyday in search of priceless treasure and new exotic places that have been created for the sole PURPOSE of being the omniscient beings of Norrath. Our niche is to do what we we think is beneficial for Norrath despite any commonly held beliefs. Those who despise and oppose our duty will suffer the utmost consequences as we are the masters of battle.

The Revised Current Charter

We are The Mystical Order. From the palaces of Erudin, to the murky swamps of Grobb, we are a blend of races and classes. There is no good nor evil in this guild, but of an alliance between the neutral aligned. We are explorers and maximizers, looking for the best treasures and to claim ourselves as the Omniscient Ones. Our niche is to maintain order to our standards for Norrath. Those who oppose or mock our duty will suffer the utmost consequences as we are all skilled in the art of combat.
