THE MYSTICAL ORDER DOWNS EMPEROR!!!!   -Gemdiver

      It was one of the most amazing moments of my Everquest life. A little before midnight I headed from Luclin to Greater Faydark and after refreshing my epic headed over to Crushbone. Using Improved Invisibility I stealthily made my way through the orc centurions who were on full alert. One spotted me but a quick Ice Spear managed to dispatch him before he could alert the guards - which was good because I was on a very urgent mission. Creeping towards his thrown I noticed a group of distressed fellow adventurers running in the other direction - a horde or orcs chasing after them. This gave me just the chance I needed to slip behind the Emperor's throne where I caught the supreme lord of Castle Crushbone - Emperor Crush himself! 

     Unfortunately I couldn't stay for loot, because I had a date with the Spirit of Akhela`Ra. I was going to to go to Ssraeshza Temple but then I remembered that I already had my shard from there so should prolly go to Akheva instead. There wasn't anything up in Ssra any way except for a couple confused temple patrollers, yeah I'm sorry but unfortunately the entire zone was wiped clean of all rares because tonight The Mystical Order.....

KILLED EMPEROR SSRAESHZA!!!!

 

 

 

   Thats right boys and girls, Grats TMO on another Fennin First. Emperor Ssraeshza is dead, early Thursday night 69 TMO went to Ssraeshza Temple and decided, you know, I'm tired of this server being so fucking gimp, lets kill the Emperor. So about a couple hours later we engaged the Emperor...28 minutes and a couple thousand sunstrikes and complete heals later...T7 got his king size pin cushion w/ fold out bed back. 

 

       Besides the pin cushion he also left us with some decent loot, 2 Velvet Slippers of Harmony which went to Rubies and Mecalis, Gebron's Demented Cloak which went to Galilo, and Garrison's Superior Sundering, which went to Gemdiver.

 

    That's all he dropped this time, and it was pretty decent loot. Was it worth the months of camping ore and keys though? Naaa not really. The best loot came from his 5 gimp friends whom each dropped a planes rift which made all the months of commander camps and AE'ing the mines worth it! Most likely most of you don't know what a planes rift is. Well here let me show you:

   Pretty lame right? No flowing thought V or enraging blow, not even any hp or mana. What does a Planes rift do? Ask Zeelot he can tell you since he got there first.

   For anyone who is still having problems following, that means TMO is the first guild on server to get into Vex Thal, the hardest zone in the game. The Emperor kill was the culmination of months of work. We had tried with more people, and we had failed, sometimes failed miserably, sometimes letting some small error - a patch undone or a wiz over nuking - dash our hopes of getting into Vex Thal. But tonight was different. Every single person played at the highest skill level possible. Not only did the Emperor die...he was slaughtered. 1 Main tank, *1* died, and our next tank stepped up and locked him perfectly, clerics swapped their targets and wizards unloaded on the poor guy. It was a slaughter, and with 15 less people than our prior best attempt. Bill Murray put in best in the Ghostbusters...We came, we saw, we kicked its ass. We are The Mystical Order.

Bobo says...Fucktards burn Fennin to the ground.  -Gemdiver

       Right now its 9:37 am est. I'm eatting this egg McMuffin from McDonald's cause its the first time in forever I'm up before noon when I didn't have to rush to work and since I was in such a wonderful mood I figured artificial chicken and orange juice could only improve on the moment.. What makes this moment so enjoyable? Well I should start about 28 hours ago...Fennin crashed. There was surprisingly little competition on mobs yesterday - our only challenged spawn of the day was King Tormax by ZS in the early morning. In any event the first mob to fall was Creator.

       The headband - 100 hp and other stats, Jesus in a piece of cloth im told - went to Colinee. The Grey Dusted wraps, also a very nice item with 80 hp/mana (sorry didnt get pics on these) went to Naeviticus. The Harnessed Flame Dirk well it was defaulted to some mage because nobody wanted it. We had originally planned for Emperor today but due to unforeseen circumstances an attempt could not be made - leaving us with a higher than average attendence. Since the rest of Ssra hadn't spawned yet we made a quick stop in Kael to take out the Avatar of War. He dropped a Blade of Carnage which went to Sorf, barbarian mask which went to Platt, and his helm - which went to Torlan. At that point one of our newer members asked a very important question as to our next target, Opalopa took care of it.

 

        Unfortunately as I mentioned we could not kill Emperor last night, so we had to settle for killing the High Priest instead. Our force took him down in a new record time - 9 minutes! And whats more important, he dropped really nice loot - 2 FT6 Shield of Ssraeshza's, one to Edroheht and the other to Roses.

    About halfway through the High Priest fight we got a tell saying that servers would be coming down in three hours for maintenance. Seeing we didn't have enough time left to kill any more big mobs we thought we should nab the Arch Lich. Pretty average loot for a pretty average mob. Sorry no loot pics on this - but Faded Skull Medallion to Khomodo and Pristine Jade Idol to Zeelot.

   We finished Arch Lich around 20 minutes before server down, so found our various places to camp out and I went back to IRC, feeling pretty good. I had wanted to kill the Emperor last night, we weren't allowed to, but we still killed Creator, AoW, High Priest, and Arch Lich - and there was going to be a patch. Opa and Tseven had said something about trying to get Cursed on server pop but Tseven says lots of crazy shit so I never really thought he'd do it, so I headed off to bed. About three hours later I got a call from Opa telling me to login for Cursed. I thought it was some cruel practical joke but logged in anyway and found 34 of the most dedicated TMO members (and 1 very dedicated app, woo Tandria!) waiting in the basement with a spawned Cursed.  The fight was long due to a little lack of DPS but our warriors held lock like pro's and we were able to down the mob without too much trouble - not bad considering it was 8:00 in the morning on the east coast. 

 

    Very nice drops, no Bloodfrenzy, but best caster wrist and pants in game outside of Vex Thal, decent spells too. The Opaline Charm went to Iriel, the Black Runed Pants to Opalopa, and the Claw to Sinadel.

 

          

   This is what TMO is about, and why I am in such a good mood right now (besides the egg McMuffin). Its not about killing the hardest mobs or having the best equipment. Its about being a group of dedicated highly skilled players who can organize and be where they need to be when they need to be whether its at Creator and AoW and 9 pm at night and 10 hours later killing Cursed - with half the usual number of people used. Its called skill ladies and gentlemen, skill and dedication. I don't mean to belittle any of the other guilds on the server, many are also skilled and dedicated, but none with so long a history and track record of skill and dedication as us. We are The Mystical Order.
