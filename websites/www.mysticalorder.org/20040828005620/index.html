OMG an Update!  -Zeelot

Sorry to disapoint you if you were expecting one of Gemdiver's updates but it's about time someone did one. Let's start off with a recap of all the fun shit we've been doing over the past few months! After Barxt (Who we killed a few months ago if you missed the update that has been up for 2 months) we moved on to qvic and decided to visit our two best friends Cynosure Kvanjii and Iqthinxa Karnkvi. Names that are as original as EQ's badword filter can create. After a bunch of Qvic farming we moved on to Inktuk'ta or however it's spelled. I'll skip the details but we killed a bunch of stuff in there and ended up with this as our result:

After taking down Noq we entered the hell called Txevu. I've never had so much fun endlessly killing yard trash in my life. We started off by killing the "BP droppers" who never decided to drop the item that they were named after, before moving on to bigger and better things. After a few attempts we finally got Runt event down. We ended up winning due to my much needed SK skill, which led the raid to victory. The final result of course was a dead mob with yet another name created by EQ's badword filter.

Hopefully there will be another update soon showing a dead High Priest so stay tuned. TMO is moving quite nicely through GOD and if all goes well, we'll be seeing Tacvi shortly. I hope you all enjoyed my lame excuse for an update...at least you get to see something different for a while.
