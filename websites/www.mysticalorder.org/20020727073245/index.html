SOE announces theme song service.  -Hyarth & Tseven

 

 You, like many others, may have first stared the game with zone music enabled, but have since turned them off to allow greater game performance during high intensity raid situations and are no longer greeted with the ambient atmosphere of the dungeon or town. Or, you may have chosen to play your own mp3s as you venture through the lands of Norrath, not allowing for a complete immersion in the game as it relates to your guild’s activities. You may have thought that the soundtracks provided did not fully reflect the personality and appearance of your guild’s growth in stature and reputation. Now, as part of Sony Online Entertainment’s continuing commitment to provide EverQuest players with the finest gaming experience available, SOE is pleased to announce a new, reasonably priced, guild theme song service. This service will allow your guild to request a theme song to represent them in the lands of Norrath.

 This new service will broadcast the theme song of your guild’s choice when certain criteria are met. There are three different situations which allow for a guild’s theme song to be played. First, when any member of your guild enters a zone whose average Player Character level is equal or less than on third of your guild’s mean Player Character level, your guild’s theme song will be broadcast through the zone heralding the arrival of one of your members. Second, when twenty or more guild members pass through an outdoor zone, en route to a dungeon, the theme song will be played in the zone to announce the movement of your forces. Finally, whenever your guild defeats a tier seven monster (as defined in the Legends reservation system), your guild’s theme song will be broadcast throughout the continent upon which this creature resided, declaring your erstwhile victory to all.

 Your guild master simply fills out SOE’s form (which you use to request, for each guild, an unused theme song on your server that complies with all of the terms and conditions of SOE’s guild theme song policy) and SOE will charge your guild the applicable fee. SOE may discontinue this service, without notice, at any time.

 Legends Premium Server guild theme songs will be free. All other servers are eligible for this paid service.
