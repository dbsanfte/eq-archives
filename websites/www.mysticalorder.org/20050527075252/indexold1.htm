Of Honor and Bobo  - Gemdiver

 

We start this update with a history lesson. There are few out there who remember who Bobo was or what Bobo stood for. Bobo belonged to Hyarth, a former officer of The Mystical Order in the Velious and Luclin days. Now Bobo was a very very old dog and had lost some control over some of his biological functions leading him to occasionally do things that people wouldn't like.

It didn't change the fact that Hyarth loved him dearly and knew whenever he had to clean something up that it was only because Bobo couldn't help himself from doing the things he did. In one of his updates - yes ladies, gents, and manginas, there were updates before me - he contrasted Bobo with another guild on the server which possessed many of the same traits of Bobo...that is to say they also did things that society would frown down upon, but the difference was that they were doing it conscientiously as opposed to Bobo's involuntary accidents. Fast forward 2 years...and meet the Master of Foresight.

The Trial of Foresight is a very very well designed and fun encounter where your raid is enclosed in a room and you must kill 6 monsters. The catch being every so often emotes go off such as "You sense a kyv aiming for your head, DUCK!" or "The room begins to heat up, the EAST side looks safe" at which point you must take the proper action or take damage in the 4-9k range.

Its an insanely fun trial and it requires your whole raid to be at the top of the game. However...there is a major exploit that people can be taken advantage of - where if you stand in a specific area none of the emotes hit you. There is no doubt about whether this is creative use of tactics versus an exploit as the EQ Developer himself who designed the trial said on the official boards that what players were doing was an exploit and that it was going to be fixed. But I digress...

Some people on a couple of servers - I'm not going to name names - like to brag about how good they are for beating this trial by using the exploit...and to those people who think that they have won something, I have this to say. You may collect your loot, but you will never realize the complete and utter satisfaction of overcoming a challenging raid that required everyone to focus and play their best. You won't be able to experience things like this...

Or things like this....

 

And for that I feel sad for you all, because EverQuest is not about how much mana you have or what spot you have on EQRankings. I don't play this game to stand around for a while nuking things and then collect loot. I play the game to experience moments like those captured in the above screenshots...and I pity those whose greed for loot caused them to miss those experiences.

Tragedy on Fennin Ro - Gemdiver

Since my return I had been planning to do an update for the site detailing all the great work TMO was doing. Unfortunately, this update is dealing with a much more serious and somber matter than EverQuest.

Tragedy has struck the family of two members of the Fennin Ro community: Andrea and Jonathan Parmer, known to all of us as Laeenu of The Mystical Order and Erkantos of Caelum Infinitum. A fire broke out Saturday morning from their heater which destroyed the entire house and all of its contents. Thankfully, Andrea, Jonathan, and their five young children survived, although Jonathan did suffer some serious burns. All of their belongings inside the house, however, were destroyed.



The structure itself was insured, but the contents were not. This includes all their furniture, clothing, Andrea's school books (she is working on a nursing degree) and other belongings. Most of all right now they need rent money for a new place to stay and for clothing, furniture, and bare necessities.

We would like to ask anyone, who can afford it, to consider making donations for them. Andrea's mom has set up a donation account through Paypal and you can donate securely through it with the following link.

If you're interested in helping in other ways such as donating old clothing or other items, you can talk to either Gemdiver (AIM: ChrispixNC), Opalopa, or Phaelia.
