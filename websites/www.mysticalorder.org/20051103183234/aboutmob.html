Origins/Accomplishments

The origins of TMO trace back to Sony's online action game Tanarus, back before EQ was even released.   The core who would go on to form TMO were known as MOB (acronym for "Money over Bitches") and were league champions.  The Mystical Order was formed during the EverQuest Beta, and has thrived on the Fennin Ro server ever since, one of the oldest guilds in EQ.  From the days of Lord Nagafen to the Citadel of Anguish, we have persevered, survived, and conquered every challenge that was placed before us, continuing on where others have fallen. 

TMO Guild Leaders:
