The tale of Xatmakin and the Dragon!  -Gemdiver

Hello again. Let's pick up where we left off last time...


(If you go to the link don't say I didn't warn you!)

So tonight we went back to plane of time. Some of you may have noticed its been quite a while since our last update of getting to Quarm and we hit a few roadblocks...But starting Thursday people started kicking ass again. Thursday we cleaned up what was left of tier 5. Pretty good loot although Inny was a cock sucking whore and didnt drop a shield. Rallos dropped his spiffy ring.

So Friday night, 64 of us headed up to Quarm after a 2 week break from our last time facing him (when we wiped 46%). For those of you who don't know me...I've been working on tradeskills lately, and after sinking 150k into them so far I find myself with 75k in the bank and still 30 skillups in tailoring to go...So I make money on the side taking bets on TMO encounters (people can bet either for the win or the loss, I am indifferent and make money on the spread most of the time) But I digress...I was pretty confident that TMO would rape Quarm pretty well that night, but not on the first try...so I put a very enticing 15:1 odds...luckily for me only Elfminster took the bet at 500pp.

Given this 15:1 bet you all know what the outcome was of course...in what was a truly hardcore and excellent team effort, the 64 of us downed the 4 headed dragon...At the end it was not a pretty sight. Corpses scattered all over the place, naked wizards nuking left and right...and one brave gnome releasing the lethal blow sending the four headed dragon back down to hell where it belonged and putting Fennin back up onto the map.

The loot was very disappointing, not a single wiz-usable item. That coupled with the loss of 7k to Elfminster, made me think very seriously that I would have made much more progress sitting in Plane of Knowledge doing tailoring or trying to sell magelo backgrounds for 5k each to finance it. (Contact me in game if you want one!) Anyway...for those non-wizards out there who care about loot. He dropped a pretty dope bracer that Edarion picked up, a very cool shield (with a very lame graphic) which Exotika grabbed, and some Stone that Zahib got.

iOnce all the loot was awarded, Xatmakin took care of the rest.

And surprise surprise...out popped Zebuxoruk, freed from his prison!

There you have it...Planes of Power is officially beaten. I got to say...PoP was our best expansion (even if the storyline was gay). Scourge of the planet (aka NoV) finally gone (click here to see my outdated goodbye to them) and for the record I'm cool with Lamanan now, he's trying to turn over a new leaf and people should give him a chance. In fact I dont think I have any enemies in EQ right now, cept maybe this one guy who keeps flaming TMO in one of the guilds which isint in time but thinks they're better than us.

With PoP officially owned, and no real expansion in the imminent future (No Virginia...LDoN is not a real expansion) we can all look back to some healthy farming, enjoyable raids and *rant on* hours on end trying to get our tailoring to 220 before we get too fed up and skull fuck grimel all the way back to that Halfling city nobody cares about or ever visits except me when I feel like taking out my pent up rage over stupid skill-ups post 187 in tailoring! *rant off*

<~~~ For Erikain

Anyway, TMO is now on top of the world and can look forward to lots of fun shit now. Now if you'll excuse me, I'm going to go whore myself out for plat...tonight's moment of Zen needs no introduction. Just as it needed no introduction in Luclin when we cleared VT...here you go everyone...the money shot.

Power Grids and Cazic Thule both down!  -Gemdiver

Sorry about the lack of update lately. I was actually in the middle of uploading our update from killing Vallon and Tallon when the whole north eastern US / Canada lost power. I'd especially like to apologize to the various people in guild who were so upset about the lack of an update for not having a backup generator ready to make the update even with the rest of New York City blacked out. Next time I'll keep one on stand by.

There is, however, lots to update on. First and foremost, as Riggs stated in his quick update, we are now in Tier 5.

With the zek twins dead we marched right on to Tier 5 and took down Bertox the very same night. He dropped this purty little breast plate for Arsnocturna among other things (see bottom)

A few nights later, TMO came back up to tier 5 to visit an old friend, Cazic Thule.

 Unfortunately my internet was still down so I wasn't there to take pics...For those of you who can't read so well and like visual stories rather than text ones, I have done my best to put together an artist's rendering of the events.

It had been a long dark night of fierce fighting through the first four tiers of the plane of time. Edarion and Torlan looked forward, undaunted by the god of fear's massive shit-brown body.

On Edarion's order the guild rushed in and began the assault on Cazic. Sinadel began unleashing a rain of arrows while Shaista, fresh from AFK began to cast a spell.

As Leleny moved forward to refresh debuffs and Shaista unleashed his spell,  Renholder ran up and began humping Cazic's right leg for good luck. Unfortunately, Sinadel was killed. When blinded momentarily by the glare coming off of Shaista's forehead, he mis-shot his arrow, causing it to come back in a circle through his head. (Damn Erudites <3)

Cazic was unable to withstand the onslaught of wizard spells and fell, his huge form crashing to the ground. As Edarion tried to distract Torlan to stop him from looting Renholder ran forward to pull a robe out of Cazic's asshole. Meanwhile Exotika (not pictured) resurrected Sinadel and Shaista went AFK.

 

Meanwhile back in TimeA, Arizen and Rennee were discovering the new bugged illusion spell that performs a sex change.

             

Between all the sex changes and other random things going on...we also got some loot (imagine that) Here it is.

That just about finishes up this long overdue update. We're now only two monsters away from Quarm, so you can imagine the morale is running pretty high in the guild right now. Expect Rallos and Inny's heads on a plate sometime in the next week or so, and Quarm will follow soon after.

I've got to admit, I have loved Fennin server lately. Hardly any drama with NoV gone.

Well...almost gone anyway =) . We're on great terms with all the other guilds on the servers and it looks like (not to jinx it) we might actually have a healthy server again. It's certainly been nice not having any competition at the top of the game to hinder us. The stratification of guilds has made for a server where we all respect each other and can all co-exist happily without anyone stepping on anyone else's toes too much. That makes it kind of lonely in the plane of time...but like the poster says...

Naa, I'm just playing, I love almost all the guilds on the server now. Dleung with CoL, Shnotz from RoA, Roukuko in Aversion, and so many other people in other guilds. As long as these friendships across guilds continue to grow then the Pax Fennin Ro will continue and only make the server greater as a whole. Ah shit, I'm getting all philosophical now aren't I? Oh well, see you all next week, here is your moment of zen.
