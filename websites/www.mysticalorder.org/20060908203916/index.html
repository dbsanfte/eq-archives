The Council and Damlyn's Secret Weapon -Gemdiver the Disgruntled Erudite

(narrated by Exotika)

The world is changed. I feel it in the water. I feel it in the earth. I smell it in the air. Much that once was is lost, for none now live who remember it. It began with the forging of the Rings of Power.

Three were given to guilds of power, The Mystical Order, Blades of Wrath, and Sacred Fyre, immortal, wisest, and fairest of all guilds. Seven were given to the Tier 2 guilds: Immortals Rising and Talons of Ferocity, hard workers and dedicated raiders.

And nine, nine rings were gifted to the leaders of the Defenders of Ak'anon, Night of Vengeance, and Severed, who above all else, desire power. For within these rings was bound the power and the will to rule each guild.

But they were all of them deceived, for deep in the land of Drama, the Dark Lord Emgug forged another ring, a master ring, and into this ring he poured his drama, his exploitation, and his defeatist will to wither away all thoughts of greatness from a guild.

One by one, the free peoples of Fennin Ro fell to the power of the ring, but there were some who resisted. A last alliance of men and elves marched against the armies of Aversion and Severed, and on the slopes of the Elemental Planes and Taelosia, they fought for the freedom of Fennin Ro. Victory was near, but the power of the ring could not be undone.

It was in this moment when all hope had faded, that Edarion, son of the king, took up his father's sword. And Emgug, enemy of the free peoples of Fennin Ro, was defeated.

The ring passed to Rarartiz, who had this one chance to destroy evil forever, but the hearts of men are easily corrupted. And the ring of power has a will of its own. It betrayed Rarartiz, to his end. And some things that should not have been forgotten were lost.

For two and a half years, the ring passed out of knowledge. Darkness crept back into the forests of the world. Rumor grew of a shadow in the East, whispers of a nameless fear, and the Ring of Power perceived that its time had come. It abandoned Rarartiz, but then something happened that the Ring did not intend. It was picked up by the most unlikely creature imaginable. An elf. Opalopa Potter, of The Mystical Order.
