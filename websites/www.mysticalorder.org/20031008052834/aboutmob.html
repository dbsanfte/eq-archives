Origins/Accomplishments

We originally came from Sony's online action game Tanarus. We are known as MOB (acronym for "Money over Bitches") and were league champions and held many other individual titles such as the Duelist Champion - Edmiester aka Edarion. We held some of the best tank fighters the game has ever seen... Along with this, the core MOB team has continued to play other online games together including but not exclusive to Starcraft: Brood Wars, Half Life: Counter-Strike, and Rainbow Six.

MOB would like to thank the following Verant employees/non-employees who were or are an active participant in the development of the guild:

Kevin McPherson, SonyFriar aka Charles Flock, Arriana aka Ryan Palacio, GZ - Geoffrey, Jalen aka Jeff Butler, Winter, Jora aka Goddess_One on Tanarus, Razor, Wulf, and anyone else that we have left out.
