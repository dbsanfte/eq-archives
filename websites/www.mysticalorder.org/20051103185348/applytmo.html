Non-level-based requisites:

1. We're very foul mouthed, especially me =p You cannot take offense to this. Be prepared to be reemed for mistakes.

2. We recruit you for what you apply for, once you are in, I'm not going to tolerate any switching of mains, unless we are in dire need.

3. Server transfers are welcome and encouraged.  We're eager to have a great player from any server join us, but don't expect a free ride.  Once here, you will need to show us what you got.  I promise it will be worth it.

4. Once again and I *stress* this... Active and on average, able to raid 4 - 5 days a week (FULL raid days, not just one here one there)

5. Must know how to play your class. I don't care if you're a cleric with 5 billion AA, and level 70 with FT500 or whatever. If you can't follow CH orders, or know how to heal in a raid situation, we don't need you.

6. Flag requisites: None at the moment, however it would STRONGLY help if you have completed the Signet Quest for Citadel of Anguish access.  This is not required before you app, but once you do app, this will be your number one priority if you haven't done it.  We can piggy you in, but you need this part done for us to do it.  This is all one groupable.  Also, you should start working on access to Dreadspire ASAP. 


Depending on your situation, there are exceptions to requirements, though they may not be so negotiable. Classes that are higher in demand may/may not get this break.

What To Expect As An App

Assuming everything goes well and you meet all the requirements, you should be given access to post your application to TMO.  TMO members will be able to vote and post on your app once it's posted.  DO NOT RESPOND TO YOUR OWN APP WITH A NEW POST.  EDIT YOUR ORIGINAL APP POST TO KEEP IT UP TO DATE AND ANSWER ANY QUESTIONS PEOPLE MAY HAVE.  Also at this time, you will be extended an invitation into our Applicant Guild, <Afkite Masters>.  We have found it easier to keeps our applicants under a guild tag during the app phase for better communication, head counts, and a chance to get to know fellow apps.  When you log on, the first thing you should do is ask for an invite to TMO Channels (there are 2 of them).  Any officer will be able to invite you, as can several veteran members.  If your confused, just ask.

As an app, your entitled to pretty much nothing.  You can ask on loot, but will only get it if no full member needs (apps have got some very nice stuff though, and when we do Plane of Time, it's truly app heaven!).  If we have to many people on a raid, apps wil be the first ones asked to sit out (unless they are a cleric, or it's a retro flag an app happens to need.  We do have a little bit of a heart).  The app period can last as little as 30 days or as many as 60.  It depends on your level of activity and the votes you get, but in the end the final decision lays in the hands of the officers and guild leader. 

Also remember, when you are apping to TMO you still represent TMO.  This means that you will maintain a high standard of integrity and respect towards your fellow players, and follow the rules of the game as set out by SOE.  Use of third party programs to alter gameplay can result in immediate closure of your application, or a loss of your tag should you have made it that far.

If you have a question feel free to ask, but consider asking a veteran member of your class before you go to an officer.  Chances are that if they are your class, they know the role you should be performing better than some stupid high elf wearing a robe anyways.  If there is still any confusion, don't be afraid to ask an officer.  They don't bite.

Any further questions regarding TMO, the app process, or anything else can be directed in game to Opalopa/Phaelia/ Galamoth/Palijim/Macery/Montaigne.  Good luck!
