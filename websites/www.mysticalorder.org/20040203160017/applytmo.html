A forward from Edarion:

For those of you applying. The most important thing YOU yourself as an app can show is that you level to the approximation of the requirements first. NOT only that... but if you cannot dedicate yourself to raiding ***4 - 5 times a week MINIMUM ***(that means full from the raid beginning to end) around 4 - 5 pm pst to around 10 - 11pm pst (maybe more)... Then I don't think we need you. (maybe if you were a certain class, yes, but I still need ACTIVE members... *NOT* casual players)

TMO is a raiding guild. We need active people like YOU out there, And I know you wanna be part of the best. This is your chance. We are ACTIVELY recruiting people who are....

1. Skilled
2. Meet requirements
3. Of key classes
4. Active (as previously mentioned)
5. Meet flag requirements AND MUST HAVE ALL EXPANSIONS.

Equipment needs to be somewhat standardized into equivalent of around full POG and 36 - 41% haste for melee and around that equivalent for Priest/caster classes. No Epic? No problem. We might help you a bit, but don't expect it. There are VERY successful people in TMO without epics, but that doesn't hinder how they can play their character to their full potential. Nor is it guild obligation to get ppl their epics. The only exception to this is Bards, they absolutely positively need their epic. If you're joining TMO to be equipped, I don't need you either. We're here to get further in the game, and loot is only a secondary. (It will come naturally of course, if the guild performs well.)

Applicants are to post on the message board, and write an introduction to yourself (level, AA, Flags, Previous guild history), A small essay regarding your class and it's role in a raid situation, and the top 3 reasons a raid fails. If you get no's on your apps, it doesn't mean we hate (not yet anyways!) it just means we have some concerns about your apps. Whomever votes no on you, you should talk and get stuff straightened out.

If you are thinking about joining TMO and just happened to read this on a pass-by but think you're not good enough or TMO is too l337 for you? Well you are wrong. As long as you have activity and the will to learn, you will always improve your game. Don't settle for apping for other guilds just because "you don't think you have what it takes" Apply for yourself then figure out if it's right for you or not! I know there are many TMO who talk to me and tell me, "I didn't apply before because I thought I wasn't good enough." Most of those people fit right in and enjoy the high-end raiding guild on Fennin Ro.

Good luck to you guys on your applications.

-Edarion, Leader of The Mystical Order

If you are planning to apply to TMO, please follow these steps:


[1] Start by reading Edarion's Recruiting Post which is stickied at the top of this forum or at the top of this page. Read it thoroughly!

[2] In Edarion's post, read the requirements (see definition below) for your class (specific AA skills and flag requirements). Be sure to check whether or not your class is currently being recruited! Recruiting changes frequently so GO CHECK!!

[3] If you meet the minimum requirements for applicants of your class, send me a private message on these forums stating your wish to apply and include a brief summary of your AAs and flags. After that, if you meet the requirements, I will update your forum access to allow you to post in this forum. Then you may start a new thread in this forum enclosing your application. Write your application in an organized and easy to read format. Be absolutely sure that your application contains all of the required information!! You will also at that time receive access to our private applicant forum where you will find important information for applicants.

Please Note: If recruiting for your class is currently closed, but you feel TMO should consider you anyway, you are required to include the names of 10 TMO recommendations (2 from your class) in your private message to me. You will be able to post your application once all 10 sponsors have verified their sponsorship of your application with me. Applications from closed classes will not be considered or be allowed to be posted until the recommendations are included and verified.

Cross Server apps: We're sorry but at this time, we are not accepting applications from other servers if your class is currently "closed" (check Edarion's recruiting post to see what classes are open/closed).

***If you do not meet the minimum requirements to apply, you will not be given forum access to post an application until you meet them. There are no exceptions.***

[Note:]"Requirement":
\Re*quire"ment\ (-ment), n.
1. The act of requiring; demand; requisition.
2. That which is required; an imperative or authoritative command; an essential condition; something needed or necessary; a need.
(Source: Dictionary.com)

Incomplete applications will not be considered.

[4]After you have posted your complete application, you will then be contacted in game to discuss your application, to answer any questions you may have about the application process, and to invite you into our applicant guild.

TMO members may begin voting on your application from this point forward.

[5]During your application period, please keep your application up to date by using the "Edit" function on your original post. Keeping a log at the bottom of your application of new AAs and Flags attained is highly recommended. EDIT your post! Do not reply to your own thread.

Note: If you receive a lot of "No" votes, it will appear that your application is not being supported. In this situation, you will be contacted and your application may be closed.

Disclaimer: Edarion has the final say regarding invitations to our applicant guild.

Keep in mind that once invited into our applicant guild, you will be expected to uphold the standards of a TMO member.
You will be expected to attend every raid that you are online for, and you will be expected to be online and contributing to the guild when you are not at work, at school, or otherwise committed. This being said, do not apply to TMO if you are not an active player. Remember that the manner in which you carry yourself in game, and any actions that you choose to do, will reflect upon TMO, good and bad alike.

The most frequently asked question is, "How long will I be an app?"
The keys to earning a TMO tag are high raid attendance and exellent raid performance. Expect a minimum of 1 month of meeting expectations before being considered for a TMO tag, but your app time can range anywhere from a few weeks to a few months depending on the individual and is at the sole discretion of Edarion.


Contact Exotika via private message with any questions regarding the application process. Click here to send Exotika a private message now. (You must be logged on to TMO forums)

Thank you for your interest in The Mystical Order.

-Exotika
