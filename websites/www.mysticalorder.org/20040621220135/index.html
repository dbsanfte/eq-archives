June 20,  2004    
    

I'm Barxt Simpson, Who the hell are you?    -Gemdiver

It seems that its time for an update again. I'm not quite sure what made me decide it was time.

But something just gave me the impression that an update for the site was long overdue given how we had killed Barxst and were in Inkuta.

It seems for some reason when I get a little late on updates after the guild passes a major obstacle people seem to get a little upset with me.

Whatever the reason, here we are again. We've really kicked it up over the past month or so, waking up from the catatonic state we seemed to have slipped into. Besides some old friends stopping by and saying hi and the regular potime loot farm, we've been advancing steadily through GoD.

Uqua was a major pain in the ass. Some of it seemed to be mindless and senseless clearing while other parts of it went by quickly. For the fast bits we were driven on by our burning desire to succeed...

And for the slow bits...well we found other ways to entertain ourselves.

Things certainly aren't getting any easier as we make our way through GoD.

but as the mobs get harder...the loots get that much better.

And around every corner there were new zones to explore, and new strategies to be discovered...

and more loot...

and even more loot...

And that takes us up to where we are now. In`kuta...well actually at this instant we're farming Qvic at 2 am, but I promise Triangle I'd update the page tonight if we won Ikkinz trial and I had to give a shout out to Zombee for all the various times he has manadumped me.

We've only been out of Uqua a little while but the end of GoD is looking very fun. I'm pretty pumped about Omens coming out in a couple months and just hoping TMO as a whole can kick it up a notch Emeril style and push through to get some good Tacvi farming in so we're ready for the level 70 grind.

I suppose you all can expect an update in a couple weeks (Gah where am I going to find enough material!) since its only a very short matter of time before Txevu and Tacvi. Until then, here is your moment of zen, something that proves that the impossible really can happen - Gemdiver and Edarion in the same room not trying to kill each other.

 

 

Lucinius likes wands....Scotty don't!    -Gemdiver and his lovely assistant

Well we did have a great update planned about the new coming of a gnome wizard to TMO but it has just been brought to my attention that we killed the Cragbeast Queen and my better half is suggesting that is more important than a gnome wizard (which is absolute nonsense in my opinion but what choice is there?)

With only 36 TMO (plus some apps) we killed the Cragbeast Queen last night. Grats to Chryseis and Raratiz on their shiny new rings.

And now on to the stuff that really matters....We've recently received a gnome wizard applicant and I felt that this was such a news-worthy event that it was time for an update (we also beat the twins in Uqua but thats a side note...TMO has been getting Fennin firsts consistently over the past 4 years, we haven't had a gnome wizard since Kunark!

We've been working our way through GoD. Attendence has been slacking as of late due to people playing WoW (see announcement about clerics), but there has been plenty to do for those of us who are still finding things to do like Breakdown in Communication along with sewers, Vexd, and Tipt.

Currently we've been dealing with a particularly vile hell hole known as Uqua.

We should be in Qvic by the end of next week so I suppose all of you can look forward to another update...well most of you anyway, to some people I'm just like a snake holding an olive branch.

So what have we been doing since the last update in March? Well we've been raiding Ikkinz along with the standard PoTime farm to gear up our newer members.

 

There have also been a lot of GM events to take part in recently, one in particular appealed to Yarg.

A couple people have gone back to complete their Veeshan's Peak keys in preparation for the zone revamp coming soon. Vizier has been great in encouraging them (see below).

Amid all this fun and games we also managed to pick up quite a nice pile of new spells and equipment in Ikkinz and Uqua. So much in fact that I won't be able to link it all, but here's a sampling.

 

Things still heating up in PoTime but not because of the weekly farming, we're entertaining ourselves in other ways now.

We'd like to welcome back some old friends who haven't been around lately...

One special reminder as we wrap up this update. TMO is actively seeking clerics. If you're interested in applying feel free to contact any officer in game or PM Exotika for more information, you can find information on our cleric requirements here.

That's about all for this week (coughmonthcough) 's update. Actually in all reality the next update will most likely be next weekend assuming we get into Qvic. Until then, live well, be happy, and don't overburden the poor donkey.

 

 

Peace out, my next update will be coming to you in GoD. If you think this update isint funny or too short, please, insert your head up a yak's posterior. What is that you ask?
