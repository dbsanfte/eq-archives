Welcome Torvonites! TMO Recruiting! - Opalopa Potter

Today is a cosmic day of sorts.  It's been happening elsewhere for the past couple of months already, but today it's finally happened to us, so it finally actually matters!  Just like the Universe may one day end by contracting back in on itself, so will EQ.  And so we wake up today to find Fennin Ro and Torvonilious no longer exist in the state they did yesterday, but now as an amalgamation of the two, a Torvon Ro of sorts.

While many people may see this as the beginning of the end for EverQuest, I'd be amiss to not look at the plethora of potential this merger brings.  No longer will we be playing on an under-populated server that's been around since the days of the dinosaurs.  The community has doubled over night!  And I for one think it's exciting as hell.  There was a time not so long ago when I could do a /who all 70 wiz, and pretty much knew every name that would ever show up on that list.  Not anymore though, not for a while.  Half the people now playing on Fennin Ro are strangers to me and everybody else, so I think it's only appropriate to introduce ourselves to them.  So to you, the transplanted Torvonites... I extend a welcome and a hearty handshake to our new neighbors!  Since many of you may be visiting our site for the very first time, let me give you a bit background on us.

(Warning:  What follows is a shameless plug of TMO for potential applicants.)

The Mystical Order is the oldest high end raid guild on Fennin Ro, and we've done everything imaginable from Nagafen to Veeshan's Peak, Temple of Veeshan, Vex Thal, Plane of Time, Tacvi, Citadel of Anguish, and some stuff that's so boring and assanine it would make you chew your own arm off at times.  We've been through good times and bad times, but TMO has ALWAYS persevered and is one of the few guilds in all of EverQuest to be able to truthfully say that we've been playing at a high level since Phinigel Atropos was a raid mob.  You may learn to love us, you may learn to hate us (not really though, because we're all super duper nice people who do massive amounts of charity work when we're not playing) but we're always there, and we always strive for the top. 

The merger seems like an absolutely golden opportunity for us to flesh out our ranks with some new blood who may be looking for a new home on this crazy merged up server, and an equally golden opportunity for those who may be looking for something new.  Let me tell you, the only way won't get in for sure is if you don't even make the effort.

IF YOU ARE LEVEL 70, ACTIVE, HAVE A REASONABLE AMOUNT OF AA'S AND KNOW HOW TO PLAY YOUR CLASS WELL, AND WANT TO SEE THE HIGH END GAME, TMO MAY BE FOR YOU.

I'll elaborate a little bit.  At this point, Level 70 isn't too much to ask for.  If for some reason your one of those level 68's with 600 AA's and just have been so busy working on your AA's that you never got around to 70 yet, go ahead and app anyways, as long as your ready to get 70 in short order.  We generally raid from 4pm PST till 9-10 and we do it about 5-7 nights per week, whatever we're able to pull off.  Of course raid times are flexible, but thats usually how we roll.  Special love out to the following classes:

  • - Clerics
  • - Rogues
  • - Necromancers
  • - Enchanters
  • - Druids
    - Wizards 

But just because your class isn't listed above don't think you shouldn't try.  We'd never turn away a skilled and dedicated player.  Those could just be defined as high needed classes, and they'll get the reflexive "OMG A CLERIC!!!" responses if they app, while love shown towards you may be slightly more restrained.  We will have love everyone though of course!

ALL APP INQUIRIES WILL BE KEPT CONFIDENTIAL SHOULD YOU SO DESIRE IT.

If you're interested in apping, or just asking a couple of questions, great!  You can talk in game to myself (Opalopa), or you can talk to our benevolent guild leader Rarartiz, our gay bard Juke, or the always sensitive and understanding Gemdiver.   Also, you can click here to check our our app page for all the information you could possibly need.  I promise you won't be disappointed!

We still have love for those of you from Fennin Ro also!  Fennin natives (hell, natives from just about anywhere) are encouraged to app if you think you've got the stuff.  Same rules apply!

(Shameless plug ends)

TMO has been taking care of business lately of course, the way business has meant to be taken care of.  Of course, there have been a few snags along the way.  Sometimes we may be missing a couple of a key class, but that's never a huge problem.  I myself haven't been on a raid in almost a week, but I've got a hell of a good excuse for that!

No apologies here.  Besides, how can't you love that face?  Anyways, this is merely a shameless update, and what better way to end a shameless update than with some loot shots!  Here's a couple of the epics we've completed recently, plenty of more to follow.


I promise a better update next time.  Honest to gosh.

Opa... OUT!
