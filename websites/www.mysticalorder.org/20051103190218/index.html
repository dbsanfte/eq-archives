October 18,  2005    
   

God damn you people, don't you know I have a life?  - Opalopa

If one thing has become apparent to me in the past 3 weeks, it's the fact that TMO guild members are a ravaging, savage, desperate crew who just won't forgive for the lateness of this update.  And this has been one hectic little stretch we've been through.  So let me give you just a little taste of the past month...

In one of the more shocking moments in recent memory, Rarartiz, leader of The Mystical Order found a higher calling in his life, and simply didn't have the time to devote to playing EverQuest any longer, especially to the degree necessary to lead a super-duper-uber-wuber-pretty-nifty-groovy-keen guild like TMO.  It took him a long time to find himself and his true calling, but courage won out.  Dr. Fernando Hidalgo performed the surgery at the First General Hospital of Santiago Chile, and freed of the masculine organ that had haunted him his whole life, Rarartiz finally was able to achieve his lifelong dream.


We at The Mystical Order wish Rarartiz the best in his future endeavours.

As happy was we all were for Rarartiz, this left the guild without a leader.  So who would assume this mighty burden?  Let me introduce you to the NEW leader of The Mystical Order.... ME!  Not everyone thought I was quite up to the task though.




 

After rethinking the criticisms though, I promised myself.... I WOULD be a better wizard!  I spent the next 4 days in solitude, reading every article posted at www.graffe.com and taught myself how to play the wizard class from scratch, and was finally comfortable enough with myself and my abilities.  I would lead the guild!  But my promotion on the battlefield had left TMO with ZERO officers.  This wouldn't stand, and I prompltly promoted some very able lieutenants.  Cherish these quotes, as these officers will likely burn out and quit within a month.

.



Now everything was back on track, and we were able to squarely focus on the obstacles standing between us and the phat loot we all wanted.  Even though one very very very very special member had an obstacle of their very own to deal with...

THE DARWIN AWARD GOES TO...



This kind of shit makes me miss the monkey clerics we used to have until a severe epidemic of Primate-Flu wiped them all out, but you gotta work with that hand your dealt.  Let me get this Brenny shit out of the way real quick now that I'm on a tangent (we do keep him around for a reason......)




 

Where was I?  Oh yes.  With everything in place, we were finally able to ventured forth into The Accursed Nest and the result was destiny.



After getting this whip, Nasti just couldn't control himself any longer.


Yeah, there is more than one kills worth of loot on this guy, and thats because we killed him almost a month ago, and this chump is squarely on the TMO Farm List now, dying for our amusement every week.  And damn me for waiting this long to do the update, for the complaining and bitching has been insessant, despite the fact that I've been hard pressed to find a free minute.  But I still love each and everyone one of you who cares enough to want an update, so here are some more quotes!

We've also managed to kill some shit in Depths of Darkhollow, and plan on either killing more or dying more, but rest assured we'll do one or the other. 

And finally, Edarion returns to the quote section!

Until next time... Happy Halloween!

- Opalopa the Court Jester
