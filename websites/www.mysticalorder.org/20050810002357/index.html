It's that stale time again.  Ever since our successful exploiting of the Jelvan event with the use of dozens of primates several weeks back, TMO has been on a bittersweet roll.  How can a roll be bittersweet you ask?  Well, I welcome you to Farmville, USA.  Population, US.

Farmville is home to many unique personalities and quirky individuals.  We have Brenny, the Village Idiot. 


And Brenny, village idiot though he may be, certainly belongs in Farmsville.  When not dreaming about loot, he's cursing out the guild for thinking we had awarded the Blade of Forgotten Faith he's wanted since infancy to someone else whilst he was away from his home of Farmville. (Of course, this was all just a carefully well thought out scheme to fool this simple minded gnome.  Ture drop a pally blade for us?  NEVER!)

(Editors Note: We regret to inform you, our valued reader, that Ture did indeed drop a Paladin Sword on the subsequent kill, and Brenny was indeed able to loot it before any of us could grab it and put it to some real use on a mage pet.  We apologize sincerely for this oversight, and assure you that Brenny will not receive any loot, default or not, in the future.  Thank you.)

And with this very special child constantly trying to pimp DSL service on us, his barnyard neighbors, it was bound to create a backlash against him. 

Not all of us are idiot man-children though (although that would be an accurate description for a fair number I admit!)  We have our share of intellectual discussions about the pressing issues of the day, life, and religion.

And then there is the woman I love, setting back race relations 60 years and describing yours truly to perfection.

God I love her!  Although recently I came to suspect that there may be another man in her life.  Thankfully, Nasti was able to clarify this situation for me, and put my mind at ease.

Ah yes, like I said.  Personalities of every sort inhabit Farmville, each demanding to be seen and heard, flailing their arms wildly and screaming at the top of their lungs until all around are given no choice but to take notice!

Here in Farmville, we have two cardinal rules.  They are...

1. Don't fuck the cows while the bulls are watching.

2. While your in Farmville, you have to farm. 

Apparently while enforcing Rule #2 (There is no need to enforce Rule #1, even Brenny knows better than to piss off a Bull) I somehow managed to piss off a members group mate for pulling him away from an oh-so-uber XP group to fulfill their sacred obligation. 

This actually got me thinking...  What the hell is a leadership tree?  I mean, Farmsville has tree's of all sorts, but this one was a mystery.  I went out and searched, and was eventually able to find it.  Believe me, it doesn't look like ass at all.  See?

 

 

And for the first time ever, I give to you all an unedited (well, slightly edited) look at the elite, inner circle ruling class of Farmville, the leadership tree itself, as we have one of our normal discussions about the fate and future of TMO.  The work never ends, and the burden is unbearable. 

And my friends, Farmville is a fun filled place, with the loot flowing like milk from the overflowed udders of a cow unattended to for days.  Just like that old women who lives in a shoe, we've got so much of the stuff, we really just don't know what the hell to do!

 

It's amazing, the stuff that would have had people raging like a convict just released from 20 years solitary on his first trip to the brothel a few months ago, I now have to BEG people to take.  I've got down on all fours, humiliated myself, whimpered, subjected myself to repeated beatings and lashings, and still I'm barely able to force this stuff on people.  What makes this all bittersweet though?  Well, we've grown very accustomed to Farmville, and are very very very good at killing everything we've killed before.  It's easy, and ph4t l33tz flow.  However, those two insufferable BASTARDS, Overlord Mata Muram and Vishimtar the Fallen still live.  Their existence mocks us, and rest assured, they shall fall to us soon.  No matter how many cleric hammers it takes, no matter the sploit, on this is swear!  Anyways, you all just want the loot shots, so here are some more, check it out!
