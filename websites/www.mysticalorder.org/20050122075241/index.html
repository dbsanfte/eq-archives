We Love Clerics - Opalopa

Clerics, we absolutely love you guys. With the recent changes made to backflagging and piggy back rules, you don't even need to worry about the utterly obnoxious amount of backflagging that was required in the past. All you need is a love of EQ, the desire to slaughter everything that stands in your way, and to play pretty much nightly, we raid from 4pm PST till whenever. (Also, you need to meet our minimum level and AA requirements! It may seem intimidating but it's really not all that tough! Click Here to check it out! It's my understanding that a chimp trained how to use a mouse can meet these requirements in next to no time)

Don't think your good enough to join us? Shouldn't sell yourself short. I mean, I'm a wizard so I'm not all that knowledgable in these matters, but I've been told by some of the better players in this game that all the real knowledge thats needed to play a cleric on our raids is the ability to count to 10, and one finger, toe or penis to press a button.

And what if your not a cleric? Don't despair! Recruitment is open for pretty much every class, although you won't be as loved, coddled, and adored as cleric would be. And I've gotta admit (Ninjuh would vouch for this) that I'm downright cruel to our wizard apps. The way I see it though is why the hell should I translocate the raid when theres a perfectly good app to haze? So yeah, if you think you have what it takes, Click Here and check it out! Or if your still curious, feel free to send Rarartiz, Opalopa or Zeelot a tell in game and we'll be glad to help you out to the best of our feeble abilities. We'll also keep any conversations 100% confidential if desired.

We have actually been killing shit as well, so I now return you to our regularly scheduled (yet shitty, because Juke did it) update.
