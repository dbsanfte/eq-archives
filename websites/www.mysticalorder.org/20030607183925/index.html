Free Loots-Kyriele
  So after we kill Fennin Ro yesterday we get word that the rares in PoEB are up and decide to go finish em off since there wasn't much else up.  Now I wasn't really sure what to expect since I wasn't online the first time we did these, but after it was over I was sorta surprised.  All I can say though is that they make Diabo Xi Va aka Diabo Xi Free Loot of Vex Thall look gimp, and they drop damn nice stuff.. Especially for their difficulty level.  So ya, we killed them and they dropped free loots~

            





So we haven't really been up to anything too exciting except for the same old type of farming. But we have spent many hours sitting near the Guardian of Corinav's spawn point for a chance to get lucky with the dice.  It's sorta funny what some people do to keep themselves entertained while sitting around for hours waiting for a spawn.


     Expect to see another update as soon as Corinav goes down.  Our last attempt we had him down to 35 percent before the timer on the ring expired and we were ported out..  Next time he won't be so lucky~
