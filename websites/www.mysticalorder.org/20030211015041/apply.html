So YOU want to be TMO?  

The next step is to convince US to want YOU.

        First, there are 2 things you should know about TMO.

  1. You WILL Respect Other Members.
    You may not like everyone in this guild, and they may not all like you. However, you will be expected to follow one basic principle in all that you do in EQ: your guild comes first.

  2. You Must Contribute to TMO's Success.
    TMO is a raiding guild, we need active people. If you're working or going to school and aren't willing to give up some of your free time, we don't want you.
 
  • General Requirements:

    -Equipment needs to be equivalent to full Velious: PoG, Giant, SS, for Priest/Caster. Melee also need 36 - 41% haste.

    -Epic's are not required unless you are a Bard.

    -TMO raids daily... You must be willing to raid AT LEAST 5 days a week. Our raid times are generally 5pm to 10pm PST, but we do go later and you are expected to be there. If you can't, this is not the guild for you.

    -You NEED a good connection, someone who goes LD often only creates lag for the rest of us. Cleric's MUST be able to withstand raids of 60+ people and still be able to heal their groups or fill in on a CH order.

    -Most importantly, obscene language and vulgar topics cannot bother you! If you're a sensitive person, TMO may not be the guild for you. We joke around. A lot. We cuss, we're vulgar, we will make fun of you and even yell at you if you fuck up. BUT we have fun!
  • Class Requirements:

    (Very High) - Cleric - 62 / Good connection / Venerable Status / SCM3 + MGB recommended
    (Very High) - Beastlord - 63 / Venerable Status / ND3
    (Very High) - Rogue - 64 / Veteran Status / ND3
    (High) - Monk - 64 / Good connection / Veteran Status / ND3, CA3
    (High) - Wizard - 64 / Master Status / SCS3 Recommended
    (Med) - Necromancer - 65 / Master Status / Raise the Dead, Call to Corpse
    (Med) - Warrior - 65 / Veteran Status / AE taunt, PE, at least two of ND/CA/CS up to 3 lvls)
    (Med) - Shaman - 62 / Venerable Status / Canni V, MGB
    (Low) - Ranger - 65 / Veteran Status / AM3, EQ
    (Low) - Bard - 65 / Veteran Status / 30+aa / Epic
    (Very Low) - Magician - 65 / 60+aa / SCM3, Pet Hold
    (Very Low) - Paladin - 65 / 60+aa / Max Defensive Archetypes, Slay Undead 2
    (Very Low) - Druid - 65 / 60+aa / HG3, HA3, Spirit of Wood, MGB
    (Very Low) - Shadow Knight - 65 / 70+aa / Soul Abrasion 3 / Max Defensive Archetypes
