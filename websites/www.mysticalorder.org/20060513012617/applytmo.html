RECRUITMENT AND APPLICATION INFORMATION

I. Introduction

The Mystical Order is the oldest raiding guild on Fennin Ro, which is one of the original EverQuest servers. We have a long proud history including several serverwide first kills and a long history as the top guild on this server. Its a history of hard work, dedication, and tradition. If you want to be a part of that history, you will need to adopt the same level of dedication and respect for the guild that those who came before you did.

We are not a family guild that raids. We are a raiding guild that is a family. If you are joining this guild, you are joining it to raid, not because you think we're a bunch of nice people and not because you're ready to quit EQ and want to prove you can make it before you retire. We raid, and we win.

If you are active and dedicated, we have use for you. Many people think if their equipment is sub-par or they don't have raiding experience or 1000 AAs, they will never make it into TMO. This is not the case. Equipment can be gained, experience learned, and AAs grinded. Dedication to the guild is the one absolute. If you don't have that, then none of the other things matter; conversely if you do have it, everything else can be gained or taught. Ask yourself before applying -- are you ready to dedicate yourself to make TMO the best guild it can be?

Non-AA Requirements

1. We're very foul mouthed, you cannot take offense to this. If you are easily offended, you will be offended. If you are not easily offended, you will still be offended.

2. We recruit you for what you apply for, once you are in, I'm not going to tolerate any switching of mains, unless the Guild Leader approves.

3. Server transfers are welcome and encouraged.  We're eager to have a great player from any server join us, but don't expect a free ride.  Once here, you will need to show us what you got.  I promise it will be worth it.

4. Once again and I *stress* this... Active and on average, able to raid 4 - 5 days a week (FULL raid days, not just one here one there)

5. Must know how to play your class. I don't care if you're a cleric with 5 billion AA, and level 70 with FT500 or whatever. If you can't follow CH orders, or know how to heal in a raid situation, we don't need you.

6. Flag requisites: None at the moment, however it would STRONGLY help if you have completed the Signet Quest for Citadel of Anguish access.  This is not required before you app, but once you do app, this will be your number one priority if you haven't done it.  We can piggy you in, but you need this part done for us to do it.  This is all one groupable.  Also, you should start working on access to Dreadspire ASAP. You can find guides to both on Casters Realm.

7. Be able to endure criticism -- everyone makes mistakes and it takes a lot of learning and making mistakes to become the best. Understand that raid situations can be tense and that if you make a mistake, you will get yelled at. It doesn't mean we don't love you, it is just a sign about how importantly we take the guild's success. Don't worry, the next day is a new day and there are no hard feelings kept.

8. New applicants must have a sponsor in the guild. The sponsor is not necessarily vouching for the applicant, but more of a guide to answer questions and to function as the main contact person to guide them through the process and assimilate them into the TMO Family (Resistance is Futile). If the applicant is new to the server or knows nobody in TMO, either the recruitment officer or one of the recruitment staff will serve as sponsor. Sponsors must have at least 66% 30-day attendance.

III. Life in Afkite Masters (By Opalopa)

Assuming everything goes well and you meet all the requirements, you should be given access to post your application to TMO.  TMO members will be able to vote and post on your app once it's posted.  DO NOT RESPOND TO YOUR OWN APP WITH A NEW POST.  EDIT YOUR ORIGINAL APP POST TO KEEP IT UP TO DATE AND ANSWER ANY QUESTIONS PEOPLE MAY HAVE.  Also at this time, you will be extended an invitation into our Applicant Guild, <Afkite Masters>.  We have found it easier to keeps our applicants under a guild tag during the app phase for better communication, head counts, and a chance to get to know fellow apps.  When you log on, the first thing you should do is ask for an invite to TMO Channels (there are 2 of them).  Any officer will be able to invite you, as can several veteran members.  If your confused, just ask.

As an app, your entitled to pretty much nothing.  You can ask on loot, but will only get it if no full member needs (apps have got some very nice stuff though, and when we do CoA & Tacivi, it's truly app heaven!).  If we have to many people on a raid, apps will be the first ones asked to sit out (unless they are a cleric, or it's a retro flag an app happens to need.  We do have a little bit of a heart).  The app period can last as little as 14 days or as many as 56.  It depends on your level of activity and the votes you get, but in the end the final decision lays in the hands of the officers and guild leader. 

Also remember, when you are apping to TMO you still represent TMO.  This means that you will maintain a high standard of integrity and respect towards your fellow players, and follow the rules of the game as set out by SOE.  Use of third party programs to alter game play can result in immediate closure of your application, or a loss of your tag should you have made it that far.

If you have a question feel free to ask, but consider asking a veteran member of your class before you go to an officer.  Chances are that if they are your class, they know the role you should be performing better than some stupid high elf wearing a robe anyways.  If there is still any confusion, don't be afraid to ask an officer.  They don't bite, that hard.

IV. Protocols

Applying
To apply to the guild, speak with an officer in game or through PMs on the forum. They will guide you to the correct person.  To apply, write out your full application and send it via private message to the person given to you by the officers. You can find a copy of the template in the Applications Forum. Once your application is approved, you will be flagged as an applicant which gives you the ability to post in the Applications forum. You will then get your Afkite tag in game from an officer and begin your application process. DO NOT ASK OBTENOR FOR ACCESS TO THE APPLICATIONS FORUM.

Sponsoring
New applicants must have a sponsor in the guild. The sponsor is not necessarily vouching for the applicant, but more of a guide to answer questions and to function as the main contact person to guide them through the process and assimilate them into the TMO Family (Resistance is Futile). If the applicant is new to the server or knows nobody in TMO, either the recruitment officer or one of the recruitment staff will serve as sponsor. Sponsors must have at least 66% 30-day attendance.

Voting
There will be no flaming on applicant threads, simply yes votes or no votes. A No vote should include the reason why the person is voting no. Discussions on apps are treated on the internal boards by members. These discussions are strictly private and any app who speaks of any private app discussions will have their app day on the calendar reset to 0. Furthermore, any member caught passing along such information will be punished by DKP loss.

Resolving No Votes
It is the responsibility of an applicant to speak to members who vote no on their app. Having enough yes votes to still get in is not an excuse for not attempting to resolve outstanding issues. In the event that the applicant feels the member is being unfair such as refusing to discuss the reasons on their vote, the applicant can request a sit-down with the applicant, the sponsor, the member, and the recruitment officer (as arbitrator). The purpose of the sit-down is not to change votes, but to assure that the applicant understands completely the reasons behind the no vote and what things they can do in order to change the vote.
