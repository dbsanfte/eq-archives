Gaining Faction (Step 1)

Missions

To start off you will need atleast indifferent faction with the respectable camp that you choose to align yourself with in order to start the progression line which leads to the alternative abilities.. This can be accomplished in a few ways. You can group with friends who are already able to get missions from the camp and upon completion will reward you with a Dark Reign Token, which can then be turned into Xeib Darkskies in the Dark Reign camp, or this can be turned into Wayfarer Mercenary Limann if you are Kill on Sight or Threatingly to the Dark Reign. Reward is posative faction to Dark Reign, Lany's T'vyl and negative faction to Norrath's Keepers, and Firiona Vie.

Note: You must do the Dark Reign missions to recieve this, if you do a Norrath's Keepers mission you will recieve the wrong kind of token. I do not recommend turning it in, it will lower your faction with the Dark Reign and add to the faction work you have to do.

Wayfarer Tasks

Another Option you have available are the various tasks the Wayfarers give out. These are generally able to be soloable tasks that can raise your faction to indifferent. Once you reach indifferent you will be unable to recieve any tasks and must do missions from the Dark Reign camp.

Note: There are 6 Wayfarers located in the camp. Four of them give out tasks, Two are for Dark Reign and 2 are for Norrath's Keepers Tasks.The other 2 are the ones for the tokens from either camp as it is noted above in the missions section. When attempting to get a task please make sure that you are getting the task from Wayfarer Mercenary Jeryx or Wayfarer Mercenary Varien.. Attempting to complete a task from one of the other task givers will result in faction for Norrath's Keepers and will lower your Dark Reign.

Back to Top

Guards

The last option you have available to gain that precious faction is that you can move over to the Norrath's Keepers camp on the opposite side of the Dark Reign camp, and kill there guards. However this is tedious, and the guards hit fairly hard and have alot of hit points now. They hit for about 650 maximum, Enrage, and mitigate slow. They will also leash (any guard from either side will if aggro) if it is pulled to far from its spawn point or pulled to far away without being activly aggro.

Once you have obtained the required faction (indifferent) you can start the series of progression quests. The time it takes you to get to indifferent is dependent on how your faction started out. Naturally a "good" aligned Class/Race is going to have slightly more work to join the Dark Reign then a Iksar would. Once you've obtained the faction see step 2 below.

Calling Emoush Event

 

So your ready to try the first "event" encounter now. In order to recieve this task you must speak with Captain Aleeth Zyrv. He can be found to the left of where Officer Vacax Rol'Tas spawns and to the right of where Celrak Blightbrood spawns on the Northeast wall in the Dark Reign camp. Upon hailing him your task window will open up you will have the following event available.

This event is a 24 player limit. Depending on the group make up and the level and gear ranges 2-3 groups would be a fairly accurate number to what it takes to complete. Once you have your party gathered and recieve the task make your way to Tirranun's Delve again. The zone in can be found in the Broodlands on the northeast section of the zone at location +906, -1618. You can identify the zone in from the black cave looking area on the side of the hill. Once your task will be updated once again and you will be required to Confront Emoush. Emoush can be found on the north most section of the map. You can view the spawn locations by refering to the map to the side.

The yellow dot represents Emoush's Spawn point. The red dots represent his guards spawn point. Upon entering the main room area your task will close and you will be unable to invite or remove any players from your task. I suggest that you make any adjustments you need and keep your raid back out of the main room until you are certain you are ready. Once you are ready move your raid into the main room area to the edge of the sand area is where we pulled to. This is not to difficult of an encounter. He is surrounded by 3 guards named Mystic Shiflor, Mystic Griknok, and Mystic Braggle. These guards hit for roughly 1020 or so and were able to be pulled single very easily with no effort.

The idea of this encounter is that you need to have all 3 of his guards dead in order for Emoush the Destroyer to be attackable. There are a few ways you can go about doing this, the method you choose is comletly up to you. You can tank each one and bring each one down to low hit points then finish off all 3 one after another or you can pull one after another as soon as you can and kill each one. Only once they are all dead will you be able to attack Emoush the Destroyer. Note: The guards will respawn roughly 3mins after being killed so if they respawn in the middle of killing Emoush you will need to defeat all the guards again in order to attack. If a guard respawns Emoush will be unattackable and will move back to his original spawn point until all are dead then he will be attackable. Emoush hits for aproximitly 1200 damage, is slowable. He also has an area effect spell that is called Rumbling Stone. This will do 1000 damage, and a 1.0 second stun if it lands. You need to make sure you have every resist of yours buffed since it is a Prismatic Resist check with a -350 adjustment. What this means is that if you add all your resists together, then divide them by 5 that will equel your average then minus the -350 adjustment will equel the resists your actually useing to resist it with. It did not seem to bad, but with a raid of very low "average" resists it could be a challenge. The Rumbling Stone will go off every 1 min as long as you have engaged or pulled 1 of his guards. He will gain strength (attack/hit points) over the course of the duration you are engaged as well as regen a decent amount if you have to kill goblins mid fight. Once he is dead you will recieve the following task completion message.

Upon looting emoush you will recieve 6 Glowing Stone Fragments, and will recieve 1 item drop that can consist of a Lava Crystal Sceptre , Band of Ashen Rain, or Ring of Living Malachite.

Note: There are only 6 orbs that drop. This is required for BOTH good or evil progression. So if you have friends who have joined you that are going for Norrath's Keepers faction that wish to loot this they can loot it. There is no requirement to looting them. If you require more then 6 you will have to do the event multiple times. Once completed you will also recieve a 3 day or so lockout timer so that anyone that was with you in the event will not be able to do the event again for that time.

Volkara Event

So, you want to try Volkara next eh? This starts out with Captain Aleeth Zyrv. Who can be found to the left of Officer Vacax Rol'Tas. Upon hailing your task window will open up and you will have Volkara's Bite available. This is a 42 person max event. The very first time I completed it was with about 30 people with a few boxed players of Tacvi level equipment. Once you have your raid gathered and you have the task its time to head to the zone. This fight is located in the Lavaspinners Lair instance. Upon zoneing in you will zone into the spider section of the zone. Volkara is located in the southeast section as detailed on the map below inidicated by the red dot. Its very easy to find there will be nothing but Volkara up. Once you enter the big room where he is located your task will close so make sure your raid does not wonder around down there unless your set and ready. Once you get down there you have options how to handle the fight. Currently theres a few different ways you can handle it which I will detail below.

First off what you need to know about Volkara. He is not rooted so he can be pulled, rampages (fake possably flurry instead), partially slowable, hits for double 1650 or so and missed fairly often. 1 cleric on CH was more then enough to keep up. He also has an area effect spell called Volkara's Bite. This is a 300 range area effect spell that will do 2000hp of DD damage, 200 damage per tick, 100 endurance per tick, and 100 mana per tick with a poison count of 25. This is poison based, with a -300 resist check and will go off every 1 min and will last 5 mins when it lands. Make sure you hare buffing your raid fully with posion resists. With a bard this was easily resisted. I would have people lined up to cure your clerics and mana users due to the mana drain effect first. Utilizeing your mass group heals if required. We tanked him to the right of the big ramp leading up to him useing the corner.

Now the fun part. Once you have him pulled or tanked wherever you choose. Every 20% life that he loses he will spawn "eggs" in the center of the main platform where he spawned. Theres a few different ways like I said to deal with this. You can do what we initially did and get all your damage class's to get on them and kill them one after another as soon as you can. They hit for about 4-500 max, and have very low hit points. However if you dont kill fast enough sometimes an egg will "burts" and baby spiders will pop out. These baby spiders can be from 2-4 per egg "burst" and they hit for about 500 and do not summon, are snareable, very low MR and flurry. Dosent sound like much I know. But when you have multiple eggs "burts" you can get a very very large number of spiders. The way we intially delt with them was to kill the spiders when they spawned, then get back on Volkara. Volkara dosent have to many hit points so I would watch if you use this way as to how much damage your doing.

The other way that you can deal with the baby spiders is to assign kite groups to get on them when they spawn and snare and kite them around while you maximize your damage on Volkara. They do not despawn however once Volkara dies so you could end up haveing to kill alot of spiders. You also run more of a risk of someone messing up and haveing your raid wiped out with them. Its completly your choice how you deal with them.

The last way, and one that most seem to currently use that I expect to see a fix to is that since Volkara is not rooted you can pull her out of the main room and up as far as you want through the zone. This makes it so that when the baby spiders spawn they do not aggro anyone since the will not assist Volkara. This lets you kill her completly out of range of any and all of the baby spiders and not have to worry about them. Although I have heard a few peopel mention that once she was dead the baby spiders all of a sudden showed up. But this seems to be one of the more common ways people seem to deal with the event currently.

Once Volkara is dead and you have looted you will find 9 Lava Spider Spinners, as well as 2 various loots that can consist of Ring of Unsung Legends, Ancient Feline Leggings.

Once you have your Spinner head on back the Dark Reign camp and speak with Commander Zaerr Ty'dar again. You need to turn into him you Lava Spider Spinner and you will recieve the following text and character flag.

Guardian of the Sands Event

So your ready to try the Guardian of the Sands event. This event starts out by hailing Captain Aleeth Zyrv who will open your task window and give you the options of selecting the correct mission. This event is a 42 player maximum event. I suggest that when you gather your raid you make sure you have some good tanks, some very good enchanters, and good healing. I would aim more towards getting a good group of enchanters 2-3 decently geared would most likely suffice, the rest of your raid can be made up of the standard class's of dps etc. Once you have your raid set accept the mission and you will need to zone into the Stillmoon Instance. You can find this instance at location 1030, 54 both posative numbers, its a teleporter pad.

Once you zone in your task will get updated to indicate that you must confromt Shogurei, Guardian of the Sands. It's alot easier then it sounds. There is normal spawns up through the zone. If you look at the map I have included off to the side so you can see what I am talking about. (Clicking the map will display a larger version) I have marked Shogurei, Guardian of the Sands as the yellow dot that can be located in the top right. I have also enclosed that top right section in a green box. This box indicates where you have a very large amount of goblins. These goblins will respawn within seconds of being killed and will spawn at the various red dots I have marked depending which section your in. The first big room being one section, Shogurei's area being another.

There are static spawned mobs on the way up to the northeast corner. Just kill them as you go. These are the same as any normal trash mob. Once you get up to the green section I marked you have a few options since the Goblins once dead will respawn within seconds.

The method that I used when I completed it was to move in and have the enchanters get the adds under control then we would move up to the next corner and we would pull the ones we had aggro on to that point kill them then quickly move to the next section. Between the 2 large room is a camp fire area that has 4 Goblins around a fire. These are static and will not respawn. Just continue to move a distance, pull the ones you have on aggro kill them then quickly move. Its very easily to get a very large amount of adds in a very short time, a good set of enchanters can make pretty good work of controling everything. These all hit for your normal trash mobs. Once you make it to Shoguri's section you can either let your enchanters control all the adds and pull Shoguri to you, or you can clear along the west wall and get your raid bunched on that side, then pull Shoguri and use the corner. When I did it we only had 1 enchanter and by the time we reached that point we were starting to get a few to many adds so I promptly pulled Shoguri and we got the raid on it.

Shoguri now hits for aprox. 1900 damage, Flurry's, has a single target rampage and will have an area effect spell called Explosion of Sand. This is a 1500 DD, 30% melee slow, and 30% snare. It also has a knockback effect is Magic based and has a -250 resist check. The range on it is 500. He did not seem to have to many hit points. We just burned him down while adds were controled by enchanters and tanks. Once he is dead you can finish off the goblins that your enchanters had locked down and they will no longer spawn.

You will also recieve a Dark Reign Faction orb and 9 Crystals upon completion of the task.

Upon looting you will recieve 9 Quintessence of Sand, You will also recieve 2 loot drops that can include Cape of Serenity, Wrapped Glass Shard, and Polished Sunsword.

Once you have your Quintessence of Sand you can now head back to Commander Zaerr Ty'Dar and turn it in and recieve the following text.

Note: This text might be slightly different depending if you had completed the Goblin Dojo event first.

Goblin Dojo Event

Your ready to begin the next adventure that Commander Zaerr Ty'Dar has requested you complete in order to prove yourself.

You can find this event from Captain Aleeh Zyrv. Upon hailing your task window will open up and you will have the option to select the Goblin Dojo task. This task is a 24 person maximum event. I would suggest that you bring a well rounded set of people to help you. I would bring atleast atleast 3-5 decently geared time equivelent tanks. You will also need to bring 4-5 healers, a shaman or 2 and a enchanter. The rest can be made up of various dps class's and what not.

Once you have your raid gathered hail Captain Aleeh Zyrv and accept the Goblin Dojo event. The details of the event can be found off to the side. Once you have accepted it have your raid make there way to the Stillmoon instance. This can be found at location 1030, 54 both posative, it is a teleporter pad. Once you zone in your task will be updated to indicate that you must enter the Dojo.

You need to make sure that your raid is fully ready before entering. Once you have your entire raid into the dojo doors your task will be updated to indicate you must survive the Stormreach Challenge. The Dojo doors will also lock preventing you from leaveing the area. When this happens you will recieve the following text (sorry no screenshot yet).

The Storm Reach Sensei says, 'Good, you have decided to meet the challenge. I will not bore you with unworthy tales of our training methods or our meager accomplishments, certainly your own deeds far outshine our own. We are but meager servants to the Strom Mistress, Yar`Lir, and are of little consequence. We have been asked to test you and, certainly, ourselves in a battle to the death. The residents of this place are away at tasks beyond those such as we, and for a short while we have this room to ourselves. We must finish our contest before they return, and so there is little time for pleasantries.'

The Storm Reach Sensei says, 'A winner will be declared if they are the only combatants alive in this room. Your first challenge will be to defeat the Disciples of The Five Elements. Certainly they will be easily defeated by such mighty beings, but it is within their hearts to try. Your battle will begin in one minute. Prepare yourselves.'

The various Disciples of Earth, Metal, Fire, Wood, and Water will now move from the sides of the Dojo to infront of the Sensei. Shortly after they reach there location they will all emote various emotes which I have included below.

In a clear, ringing voice, The Disciple of Metal says, 'Please forgive my poor humor, but I am very eager to test your mettle.'
The Disciple of Earth rumbles, 'When we have defeated you, I shall see that your corpses rot in the soil as is proper.'
The Disciple of Fire shouts, 'I make no apologies. I shall burn your hearts out!'
The Disciple of Water says, 'Ah, yes. You must forgive Fire, for he cares little for pleasantries. It is part of his charm. You most certainly will be worthy opponents. I am honored to face you.'
The Disciple of Wood says, 'May our efforts here earn favor with those that watch over us all.'

They will very soon after emoteing aggro you and try to kill you. These all will need to be off tanked and can be targeted before they actually aggro. They all hit for roughly 1000 to 1200 damage and have various area effect spells.

Disciple of Water will cast Mists of Enlightement which is a cold based -275 resist area effect spell that will decrease your attack speed by 20%, decrease your hit points by 250 per tick, decrease your AC by 450, decrease your mana by 100 per tick. It is also 18 curse counters so I would suggest that you have someone that can cure this from your various clerics and mana users first before you do so on your melee.

Disciple of Fire will attempt to cast an area effect spell of Nova Inferno. This is a 2000 hit point DD, that is fire based with a -350 resist check. Just make sure you have your Fire resist buffed up before you start. He will also Rampage.

Disciple of Eath will have a single target process effect called Cumbling Blow that is a 2.00 second stun and a 175hp DD and is magic based.

Once you have them 5 dead you will get the following emote.

The Storm Reach Sensei says, 'Congratulations! You have defeated the Disciples of the Five Elements. You have proven yourself worthy to face the Four Emotions. They have mastered their emotions and from them they gain strength. Each has become a conduit for the emotions of our kind, a paradigm, if you will. Certainly you shall defeat them, for you are the champions of your people and they are slaves. We shall begin again in two minutes. Rest and consult with each other, if you have need.'

You will now see that there will be 4 more goblins moveing to the Sensei platform. These are alittle harder then the previous one and will range up to a maximum hit of about 1500. These will emote the following.

The Master of Rage shouts, 'I hate you! I will rend you into bite-sized chunks of flesh and feed them to the puma!' Rage's face darkens with blood and flecks of spittle fly from his mouth as he pants and stares at his enemies.
The Master of Deceit smiles broadly. 'You certainly will find Rage difficult to stop. I see it boiling up inside many of you. Allow it to take over, and Rage will ruin you.'
The Master of Doubt mutters, 'I. . . You. . . ' he sighs, 'I do not know what will happen next. I see the same misgiving in some of you. Could it be that you are like me? No, that seems unlikely.'
The Master of Contentment drawls, 'Ah! I am pleased to meet you all. This is a fine challenge for us, is it not? I find this sort of exercise invigorating, though rarely do I find invigoration to be a good thing.'

These can once again be pretargeted and will aggro as soon as your 2 min window is up. These all have small abilities as well.

Master of Rage will have Area Effect Rampage.

Master of Doubt will attempt to cast an area effect spell called Doubt. This is a 300 attack debuff, 200 strength, 200 charisma, and is 30 curse counters. It is a Chromatic resist with a -225 resist check. He also has a melee process that is called Mental Discord which will reduce the mana pool by -450 of the person he is currently aggro on.

Master of Deceit is naturally a rouge and will try to use his various abilities such as backstab. He will also at times "disappear" and will move to a different place in the Dojo.

Once you have defeated the 4 of them you will recieve another emote.

The Storm Reach Sensei says, 'Well Done. It is rare that an opponent is able to defeat the goblin Emotions. Your next test will be greater, I fear. You must face our three Animals. These are grandmasters of our arts and have never suffered defeat against opponents outside of these grand islands of the sky. You look weary. You shall have three minutes to prepare for the next contest.

You now have 3 mins to regenerate your mana and hit points. These will not emote but will move to the center platform near Sensei. They are Grandmaster Beatle, Grandmaster Puma, and Grandmaster Snake. I do not recall what they all hit for but it will be alittle harder then the last set. I would estimate then hitting for about 1400-1500 as well.

Grandmaster Beatle will use a Whirling Beetle Strike which is a 600 hit point DD along with a 2.00 second stun. With a prismatic (average) resist with a resist check of -225. He will also Rampage.

Grandmaster Snake will use a Venomed Mist area effect that is a -50 mana per tick -500 hit points per tick and is 36 poison counters. It is posion based and has a resist check of -300.

Grandmaster Puma will attack fast and also flurry.

Once you have defeated the 3 you will recieve yet another emote.

The Storm Reach Sensei says, 'Amazing, simply amazing! Never before have our grandmasters been defeated by anyone other than your next opponents. Prepare yourself, for next you must face Silk and Steel. You have four minutes to prepare, use those minutes wisely.'

You now have 4 minutes to rest up and get ready to defeat Silk and Steel who will also make there way towards the middle Sensei platform. They will both emote the following.

Steel looks at each of you in turn, his cold eyes taking in every detail. 'Individually, you are flawed. I see rust. I see cracks. Weakness is abundant in you. I shall exploit that weakness and shatter you one at a time.'
In a voice more fluent and soft than any you've heard a goblin use, Silk says, 'Forgive my brother. He has a keen mind but a blunt mouth. We look forward to this test of our skills. Thank you for honoring us by accepting the challenge.'

Once they attack you they both have there own abilities once again as the last waves did. They both will hit for roughly 1500 damage and will "switch" there aggro lists at one point so your tanks will be switched around on opposite ones. It didn't seem to bad.

Steel will area effect rampage and will mitigate melee attacks

Silk will Flurry and has VERY high resists.

Once they are both dead you will recieve the following emotes and text.

You must now defeat the Storm Reach Sensei. He has a single target rampage, hits for 2000 damage and is slowable. He can also stun the tank for a fairly long time. He was over all pretty easy if you can get to him. Once he is dead you recieve the following text and emote.

The Curse of the Ju'rek Event

So you've decided to take on the The Curse of the Ju'rek 24 person event. You can find this event from Captain Aleeth Zyrv who will make it available to you once you have beaten Yar'Lir along with the appropriate faction. Upon hailing Aleeth your task window will open up and you can accept the mission. This event is geared for a 24 player limit maximum. I would suggest that you bring atleast 1 bard for resists, 1-2 enchanters, atleast 1 shaman, and a well equiped tank.

Now that you have the raid ready. Accept the mission from Captain Aleeth Zyrv. You will now need to zone into The Accursed Nest. You can find the instanced zone in at location -532, -1266. It can easily be reached as you exit the Lavastorm/Broodlands connection tunnel you can go straight out from the caves in the Broodlands and alittle to the right, there you should be able to see a ruined pillar type structure that is supposed to be a dragon with the small teleporter pad in the centetunnel you can go straight out from the caves in the Broodlands and alittle to the right, there you should be able to see a ruined pillar type structure that is supposed to be a dragon with the small teleporter pad in the center.

Once you have zone in your task will be updated to indicate that you must find and confront Ju`rek the Corrupted. I have included a map below and to the side that details the exact position in which you can locate him. Looking at the map he can be detailed by the bright yellow dot. You can click the map for a larger version.

Now once you zone in. Theres very minimal clearing. I believe we cleared roughly 6 trash mobs that it took to get to him, and 3 or so that he had in his room. These are just normal average mobs nothing to big. Once you reach his room your task should be updated to indicate that you must kill him.

Now for the fight details. Ju`rek the Corrupted hits for aprox 1940 damage, was slowable and didn't seem to bad far as melee damage. Now his AE's were the other story. The first time we went at him we got him to about 30% before we were taken over. He has 2 different abilities he use's. The first is Decaying Stench. This is a 1800 hit point per tick damage over time, and is 45 disease counters. It is disease based -600 resist check and is 100 range. The other is Ju'Ok's Cry. This is a 1500 DD and a 10.00 second stun. Posion based -550 resist check and is a recast of 60.

The best way we found to avoid them or attempt to was to maximize your resists. A shaman and bard can help greatly since they both specialize in resist buffs of both kinds. We used a paladin to DA in ahead of the raid and pull him over on the side near the wall where we tanked. Melee could get on the side/back while the tank used the wall as corner type area and keep it positioned in one area. This allows your casters and clerics to maximize there range without haveing to worry to much. The fight lasted 2:24 seconds according to my parse of the fight from the time we engaged. He has extreamly low hit points I would estimate in the 800k range at most. The other fun part about the fight is that every 20% I think it was he can spawn 2 adds. These are controlable with a enchanter and hit for a maximum of 837 and cast Freezing Breath. This is a Cold based area effect spell with a 200 range and a -150 resist adjust and will do 750 DD and a 70% melee slow. The best bet is to try to get your raid more caster/range oriented for damage because of this. We just burned Ju'rek down while we had enchanters/bards keeping the adds occupied since his hit points were so low and the fact that we wanted to have him dead before he could cast Decaying Stench again. Once he is dead you can kill the various adds or they despawn eventually if you wiped.

Once he is dead you will recieve the following text and crystals.

Your task 'The Curse of Ju`rek' has been updated.
He has been slain. You found nothing? How worthless.
You have gained 18 Ebon Crystals.

Upon looting you will recieve 6 Diseased Wing Fragment, and 1 loot drop which will be of Quarm level quality and can include Hammer of the Last Clutch, and Dark-Touched Mantle.

Once you have your Diseased Wing Fragment you can head back to Commander Zaerr Ty'Dar and turn it in. You will recieve the following text.

Note: This text might be different pending if you chose do to do the Rival Party mission first.

Rival Party Mission

Now your ready to begin the Rival Party mission. This mission is a 3-6 person mission that takes place in The Accursed Nest. You can recieve this mission by speaking with Officer Vacax Rol'Tas. You should be able to accept the "Rivail Party" mission from there. Once you have accepted it you can head to the instance which can be found in the Broodlands.

This instance was slightly harder to find then most. Once you know where it is though its pretty easy. I suggest that you take note of it since you will be useing it later on. You can find this zone in to the far west of the Broodlands in the forest area. To reach it though you must go to the northwest portion of the zone where you will find a small valley between the hills that leads back to the dragon statue. The location of the valley can be found at location -259, 962. Once you reach the valley section you can follow it back to the dragon statue which holds the teleporter pad inside at location -881, 1624.

Once you reach the instance and zone in your task will be updated to indicate that you must kill Drogorus of Norrath`s Keepers, Vincent of Norrath`s Keepers, Syllan of Norrath`s Keepers, Corgoth of Norrath`s Keepers. These are level 70 mobs and were very very easy compaired to the normal mobs in this zone. The normal mobs in this zone you can expect to hit for 1200-1350 damage and be slightly harder then your normal Riftseekers. The experience here is a good deal better though considering the difficulty. You can see on the map I have included below as to where these 4 spawn. They hit for roughly 900 damage each. You can see where they are on the map by the bright green dot indication. Clicking the map will display a larger version.

Once you have killed the 4 of them you must collect 6 Egg of a Saved Clutch. This proved to be the real challenge when I completed this section. The Eggs appear to be random drops from any creature in the zone. They just seem to be extreamly rare, or we just had some extreamly bad luck on looting. We managed to kill the 4 Norrath's Keeper's within about an hour of accepting the task (took a few of the group 30 mins to find the zone in). The other 2 1/2 hours we spend in the zone after that was spent finding the eggs. My experience they seem to be much more common off the stone golem models then anything else. We did however have moderatly decent lucky on the far west portion of the zone where the various drake models were. The only thing that kept our group from going insane other then the fact it was 3am when I dragged them into it is the fact that it was really good experience. I just hope that you have better luck then I did in collecting them.

Once you have looted the 6 eggs your task will be updated to indicate that you must return one of them to Pericolo L'Morte. She can be found in the Dark Reign camp on the northeast section surrounding the camp fire. Turning the egg into her will result in a character flag for your whole party as well as the following text.

Note: This message could be different depending if you completed the Rival Party mission prior to Ju'Rek.

Upon completion of Ju'Rek and the Rival Party missions you will be able to recieve the following text from Commander Zaerr Ty'Dar.

You will now have access to Vishimtar The Fallen raid task which can be accepted from General Lereh Dirr.
