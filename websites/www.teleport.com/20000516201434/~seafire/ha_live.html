Heather Alexander: vocals, guitar, fiddle, bodhran.


In 1992, Firebird Arts & Music sponsored a special live concert at Desitrek Studios in Portland, Oregon. This album was recorded digitally in front of a live audience, and captures the essence of Heather's concerts.
