Heather Alexander - Life's Flame

Heather's first album since her highly acclaimed Wanderlust, Life's Flame is sure to please her fans. The album was recorded at three live concerts in Berekely, CA during July of 1996 and then the best recordings were selected and mixed at DesiTrek studios in Portland, OR. The reportoire contains songs both old and new, some previously recorded, some available here for the first time.
