The address for the Farm Home is 4455 NE Highway 20, and Kelly (in the development office) is the best contact person for directions or questions. Her number is (541) 758 - 5950.

From the north: Take I5 south past Albany. About 4 miles past the last Albany exit, take the Corvallis/Lebanon exit to HWY 34. Turn right onto HWY 34 toward Corvallis (roughly 10 miles). Go over the bridge. At the bottom of the bridge, turn right onto HWY 20. Pass two traffic lights. About 3/4 of a mile past the second light is the Chapel driveway (on the right). Follow it around the building to the gravel parking area directly behind.
