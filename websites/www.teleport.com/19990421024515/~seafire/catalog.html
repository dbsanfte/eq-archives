Heather Alexander's debut solo cd. Her distinctive vocals and pyrotechnic fiddling highlight the twelve cuts on this album. From the rowdy drinking song "Pour Your Brother", or the rollicking tale of "John Barleycorn", to Heather's lonely version of the W. B. Produced by Heather Alexander and Mike Demmers and recorded at Desitrek Studios, Wanderlust features Heather on guitar, vocals, fiddle, mandolin and percussion, Rob Wullenjohn on bass, Jenny Lindner on harp, and Lief Sørbye on flute and harmonica.
