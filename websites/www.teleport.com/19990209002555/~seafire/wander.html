Heather Alexander: vocals, guitar, fiddle, mandolin, various percussion.
Rob Wullenjon: bass.
Jenny Lindner: harp.
Lief Sørbye: flute, harmonica.


Heather Alexander's first solo cd, Wanderlust has been critically acclaimed both in the US, and overseas (see Reviews). Recorded during the winter of 1993-94 at Desi-Trek Studios in Portland, Oregon with engineer Mike Demmers (mdemmers@teleport.com), Wanderlust was the first major project under Heather's control.

When the original financing was unexpectedly withdrawn from the project just days before the final mixing, Heather's company, Sea Fire Productions, was forced to come up with funding quickly in order to come close to the projected release date.

Wanderlust was released in July of 1994, thanks to the assistance of many friends, without whom the album may not have reached completion.
