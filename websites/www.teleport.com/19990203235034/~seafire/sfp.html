What is Sea Fire Productions?

Sea Fire Productions is the music publishing company formed by Heather Alexander and her husband, Philip R. Obermarck. Originally it functioned as the protector of her music for copyright purposes, but has grown to encompass album production, booking, promotion, manufacturing, publishing, and wholesale and retail sales, as well.

Naturally, Sea Fire specializes in anything Heather-related. We dispense information, sell cds, tapes, buttons and songbooks, and generally try to promote Heather whenever and wherever we can. We are still a small company, but as time goes by, we keep expanding and branching off in new directions all the time‹ in fact, contact us if you like these Web Pages. We are more than willing to consider designing Web Pages for other folks.
