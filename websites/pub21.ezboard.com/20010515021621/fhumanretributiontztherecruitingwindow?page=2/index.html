What does it mean to become a member of Human Retribution? You will be KoS to many guilds. You will be corpse camped. You will face invulnerable healers. You will be rediculed and in all this you have to stay calm, civil and not resort to unending trash talk. It also means you will be part of a great guild of people that are out to have fun and to shake things up in the more boring zones such as Qeynos and surrounding areas. If you think you have the backbone and conviction to stick it through, then you will be welcome.

1. Tell us how you heard about our guild.
2. Why do you want to join this crusade?
3. What is your RP background? (It could be any length you like, from a short paragraph to a novel.)
Tell us anything you think may be useful to us, such as will this character be your main or secondary? At this time we don't have a preference on main or secondary, although a main character would be best. We currently don't have a level restriction.
