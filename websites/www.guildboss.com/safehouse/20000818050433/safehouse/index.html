Psst! Over here...behind the fence.

So, ya found yer way to da Safehouse did ya? Good little rogue. Before ya goes inside there's a coupla t'ings ya might wanna know:

One, don't go cussin' in there! Cussin' draws the attention of da law and we don't need no guards comin' 'round here. Know what I mean?! Good kid.

Two, pay attention to da layout. Da Safehouse got many special rooms and halls for certain types of "biz". In udder words, dontcha go askin' for help in the Rant Hall and dontcha go a rantin' in the Training Studio! Got it?

T'ree, first thing yer gonna wanna do is check da Bulletin Board for da latest news and events and especially for any official announcements from da Boss, Remmi. He don't like slackers...

Four, if'n ya want official membership into da Safehouse ya gotta register first. Membership is completely optional but if you dooz register, here's what ya gets:

  • Reserved member name - nobody can ever use yer name to get inside and impersonate ya. Keeps would-be imposters off yer "rub out" list...
  • Magic icon - ya can have yer own little personal icon appear wherever ya goes within da Safehouse. Nice little trademark...
  • Magic signature quill - This little baby writes a customized signature on all yer papers automagically! A real time saver...
  • Magic message courier - Get replies to yer messages sent right to ya! And don't kill da messenger if it's bad news, eh?

So if ya want the "bennies" of membership all ya gotta do is register an' make a new account. To do so, ya first gotta be inside da Safehouse. Once inside, just whack the little Login sign up on yer right and ya gets to choose an alias and a password. Yer alias is the name ye'll be known by while yer in da Safehouse so choose wisely, eh. After loggin' in ye'll have a chance to customize some of yer "bennies" as described above. If'n ya needs more help just ask--yer among friends in da Safehouse so relax, and make yerself at home. Just keep a one hand on yer purse and the other on yer blade and ye should be alright.

Now, my little rogue friend, hop on over dis here fence and join your brothers and sisters inside da Safehouse....
