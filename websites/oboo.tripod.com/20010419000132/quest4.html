The Screaming Mace Quest

Instructions for the Screaming Mace quest, a great 1H blunt weapon:

This quest takes place completely in Crush Bone, a zone off Greater Faydark.

Step One - Kill Emporer Crush Get his Dwarven Tunic (Magic Item). He is behind the throne room in the castle.

Step Two - Find the Rare Spawning Warlord, kill him and hope he has the very rare Bracer of Battle. He spawns in the same place as the Emporer.

Step Three - Give the tunic to the middle Dwarven Smith in the house by the Lego Camp. In return he gives you a Dwarven Mace (Non-Magical).

Step Four - Give the Bracer of Battle to the Elven Priest on the end. He will in turn give you a Prayer Cloth (Magic Sholders).

Step Five - Give just the Dwarven Mace to the Elven Priest. He will then ask for something else. Only then do you give him the Prayer Cloth Sholders. He then gives you a screaming mace.

Emporer Crush is about 18th Level Orc Warlord is about 14-15th Level.

The items you get during this quest are all nice items and you may want to do this quest a few times to try and get them all.
