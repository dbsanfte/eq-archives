Fight Night in Oasis

Official Fight Night in Oasis is still Wednesday nights, and we encourage any and all Bears of Oboo to show up and to bring their funk! Many a great battle has been waged in Oasis, and we hope that this is a tradition that will go down in Oboo lore.


Taradin and Nananea's Wedding

Our very own Taradin is marrying an officer of the Reach of Oboo, Nananea! The Wedding is currently scheduled for some time during the day on Saturday, April 15, and will be held in the Temple of Solusek Ro. Details will be forthcoming.


Oboo Scavenger Hunt

The Bears of Oboo will be sponsoring a scavenger hunt open to anyone who doesn't oppress us! It will be headed up by Toboo, and will involve lower-level people running all over Norrath in groups, hoping to attain the appropriate items for a fairly substantive prize that has not yet been determined. Details on this will be forthcoming from Toboo.
