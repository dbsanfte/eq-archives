Newbies at War

On Wednesday, March 15, the beautiful and talented Vyvyd hosted a newbie event in Halas. There was much drinking. There was much dueling. Pictures were taken ...

At any rate, we hope that events like this foster the feeling of brotherhood among the Barbarians, and it also nice to have a good reason to get very, very drunk. The nice thing about this duel is that the loser doesn't have to deal with a hang-over.

Thanks to Oaklahoma and Belok for participating! Safe to say that a good time was had by all ...
