Beginner Everfrost Quests

Following is a list of the beginner quests that you can run in Everfrost:

Polar bear skins (the white ones):

Give them, one at a time, to Cindl in Mac's Kilts for exp and a piece of patchwork armor.


Ice Goblin Necklaces:

Collect 6 and get a pouch from one of the warriors in the Halas warrior guild to put them in. Combine and turn in back in for a little money and exp.


Wooly Fungus:

Collect 8 funguses off Wooly Spiderlings and get a jar from one of the shaman at the Halas shaman guild to put them in. Combine and return the jar for a level 5 shaman spell and some exp.


Gnoll teeth:

You can turn them in at the warrior pit in Halas, but you're much better off bringing them to the gullible humans in Qeynos. Turn them in (one at a time) to Captain Tillib on the balcony of the warrior guild in South Qeynos. He'll give you decent exp (through level 14!) and give you a lightstone for each one.

Take your new collection of lightstones to the Fish Ale bar next to the south dock. Find McNeal Jocub in the back room and give him the lightstones one at a time. You'll get more exp and he'll give you a bottle of blackburrow stout for each one. You can sell these bottles right back to him for 5 silver each.


Wrath Orc Wristbands:

Again, you can turn them in to the Halas warrior guild for the Seax (a so-so piercing weapon), but you're much better off giving two at a time to Cindl in Mac's Kilts. You'll get exp and a piece of rawhide/leather armor for each set of two.
