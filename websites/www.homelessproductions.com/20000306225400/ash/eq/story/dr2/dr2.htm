Drog Trollsbane #2

"Why you getting all worked up over it? Its not like they're laughing at you or anything. They just know who's the REAL hero here," Drog boasted with a sly grin on his face. "And besides I'm not the one who changed his name to Greenhelm....thats why they call us Drog and his friend, they cant remember what your name is!"

At that the two young adventurers burst out into laughter. They had been traveling together for at least four months now and had become great friends and travel partners. Drog had shown Greenhelm how to forge for food and live of the land and, in turn, Greenhelm had shown him the finer points of combat. They were quite a team and had fought off several orcs and many more kobolds in saving the crops and lives of many of this lands people.

"So what was SO bad about the name Bastion anyway?" Drog began as he stuffed some bread into his mouth.

"Oh no nothing was wrong with it at all unless you like sounding like a rich boy whose family could spoil you with anything you want...nothing at all.",Greenhelm stated.

"Whoa there tiger.....no need to go all berserk on me...I like the change. At least you finally know what the bucket does.",Drog said as he pointed at the magic helm sitting beside his partner.

"Yeah useful thing too.I like knowing anytime I get hurt the thing try's to heal my wounds. Its my security blanket.",Greenhelm remarked as he began to stroke the crest of his fathers gift. Then the rain began.

Of course they knew it was coming and had already prepared a place to stay dry and Greenhelm immediately went for the camp. Something had made Drog stay though even after his companion had urged him to come to the camp he waved him off and told him he'd be there in a bit.

Something made him rise and unsheathe his sword.

He wasn't sure what it was, but he would take no chances. His many years of living near the forest had taught him to be wary of anything.

Suddenly it was upon him.

A huge Giant at least five times his size stepped through the bush and eyed him with its one good eye. Stunned by the sheer size of the monster Drog couldn't move. He just sat there as the mountain of a man bore down upon him and smashed him with its large club. The pain was unbearable but it had brought his senses back to him, as he left to the side and landed on a log sword at the ready.

"Come get me you large overgrown excuse for a rat dropping, you'll not take me without a fight!" he shouted in hopes that his buddy had heard him.

Then once again it came at him , but he was ready and just leapt over the club and slashed its gut with his sword. The giant didn't even flinch as it came at him again. He knew this was the end as the club came down towards his skull. He knew he could not survive such a crushing blow.

But then the beast fell to the side as if something had just swatted it away, . And in the darkness was the figure of a large man, maybe a barbarian by the looks of him.

As the giant slowly regained its feet the large man just stood and watch finally muttering, "Okay if you want more you got it!".

The sound of its death wail was horrible.It screamed as it toppled back from the mans huge blow. It was incredible how he fought without weapon and yet had killed the giant in just two hits!

"Thank you sir, .Whoever you are. I owe you my life.", Drog said as he saw his Greenhelm coming to his side.

"I'll call it even if you let me have a swig of your ale and bite of your bread," the man had begun,"I have only drank water for days now...I need my nourishment.".

Drog offered it readily as Greenhelm spoke,"Thank you, kind sir, for saving my friend.. I fear I was to be a little late coming.Oh where are my manners...my name is Greenhelm and this is Drog Trollsbane.".

"Then my quest is over, for it is you two I have come for. My name is Marco and you will have to come with me I'm afraid."' the large man replied.

"I don't like this one bit.," Drog thought as he unsheathed his sword again. He noticed his companion following his lead.
