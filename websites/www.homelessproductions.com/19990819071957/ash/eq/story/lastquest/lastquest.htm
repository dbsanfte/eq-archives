Last Quest?

My, my, my. Sometimes I really wonder what's going on with my guild.

First, they send me off to Rivervale to do battle with goblins, as they seem to be attacking the halflings there...but after getting there, I realized that the halflings have the "threat" completly under control. As I have said before, I felt as though they sent me there to "get me out of the way". This has further been proven, at least to me, because upon arrival at the Academy in Freeport, my guildmaster has sent me on yet another odd "quest".

They want me to spend time in Kithicor, and find out who the elf "Kithicor" really is. "What?" I asked, thourghouly confused.

The guildmaster stood and shruged. "We want an in depth report on Kithicor, what creatures are there, and find out who the man Kithicor is. You seem to be the only one who has seen him, so...find out where he lives, what his motives are..."

"What about the ghouls and the werewolf in the area?"

"Bah,!" the guildmaster said. "You'll be ok. You have your mission. Complete it." He then turned and walked to a large bookshelf--which turned out to be a door--and disappeared.

So I am going to be away for awhile. Hopefully I'll return soon, and with information on Kithicor. Aye, a scholar's work is never done...so much for seeing the sights of Norrath.
