Drog Trollsbane #1

Herein lies the story of how Drog Trollsbane became a warrior and a man.

Even since he was a wee lad barely able to hold a sword, he felt a longing for the road. He would travel to his family's woods and act as if he was his hero, Shamus of the sparkling robes, fending of dragons and giants or rescuing princesses and kings from their doom.

Then one day , his 16th birthday to be exact, he met a young man who would change his life. The boy, perhaps no more than 14 himself, came with his father who was a merchant Drog's father traded with on his trips to town. The youths name was Bastion, and to his amazement the boy wore chain armor and carried a gleaming sword at his side. But the most mesmerizing thing was his helm which was glowing a light green! As soon as he could he approached this brightly adorned boy and began to fire many questions at him.

"Whoa there sir! One at a time, please.", Bastion said as a slight smile broke his perfect complexion.

"Where....the helm?", was all Drog could get out.

"It was a gift from my father and his father before him.They tell me that it is magical and its power shall one day make me a hero. I hope that I can live up to their expectations of me.", Bastion spoke.

"A hero? I have always dreamed of such a life! If only I had such a gift I would become one as well. But unfortunately my father is just a farmer.",Drog spoke as he began to dream of quests and dragons and treasures.

"Well......it is your birthday and I have yet to give you a gift....here you go.", Bastion said as he unsheathed his sword and handed it to him.

"Are you sure you want to give ME such a gift?", Drog began as his eyes widened on the perfect edge of the swords blade.

"I see the look in your eye and feel the passion in your heart sir....you deserve to fulfill your destiny and become the hero you always wanted to be.", Bastion said with a smile as wide as a boat.

"How can I ever thank you?", Drog questioned.

"Come with me on a quest for adventure. Long have a wanted to fulfill my fathers hopes but he has never let me journey without him. If you come along I will feel safer having someone with me.",Bastion replied.

Drog turned to look at his father sitting around a table with other adults more than likely talking of crops and rain and other things that would bore him to death.

"I'll do it! I will need but a hour to get my things and leave my father a note. Together we shall both go down in history as the greatest heroes since Shamus himself!", Drog spoke with a fire in his eyes. And so true to their words the two young adventurers set forth on what would become the defining moment of their lives.

by Drog Trollsbane
