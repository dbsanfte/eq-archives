The House of Shadow

Domus Umbra is a very dual natured house. In some ways it represents order and in some ways, chaos. The driving unity within Umbra is that neither good nor evil should be allowed absolute, unopposed power. Umbra allies and wars as needed, maintaining what it feels is the proper balance.

This House is open to almost all races. Only worshippers of Brell Serilis, Bristlebane, Prexus, Solusek Ro, The Tribunal, Veeshan, as well as agnostics devoted to the ideals these Gods embody will be permitted. Further, we will not tolerate Shadow Knights, Necromancers, Rangers, Paladins, or Druids to become members as their chosen proffession places them decidely in favor of the good or evil Gods.

Followers of Rallos Zek are permitted to join on occasion. Rallos Zek is often categorized as evil, but in many texts he is a force of neutrality. This dual nature leaves Rallos Zek and his followers slightly out of sorts with the fuedal house system D.I. embraces. As such, followers of Zek, that oath to Umbra, may join.
