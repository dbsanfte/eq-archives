Joining Dark Inheritance

Note: If you already have a character in Dark Inheritance, do not fill out this form, simply contact Serrik in game for a guild invite.

Overview

This document accomplishes the following things.

  • Explains our 30 day probation policy
  • Explains what character traits we expect to see in new members
  • Walks you through the guild application
  • Submits the application to Altus Concilium for processing

Probationary Membership

When you join Dark Inheritance you begin with us as a member of your designated House and on a 30 day probationary period.

During this time you are expected to get to know the guild as we get to know you. At the end of the 30 days you ,may decide to stay and become a permanent member, or you may leave and decide we are not what you are looking for. We would wish you well and not pressure you into staying, as that is simply not our way.

Our guild is a happy place, though sometimes quiet. Despite the conflict in character among some members, the guild itself is a tight knight group and always eager to get involved and help eachother.

Dark Inheritance is not so much a guild as a roleplaying environment. We provide a standard story line so all members can RP from the same source. We protect the guild chat from those who would take from the roleplay feel of it. We are strict about who attends our raids and events.

We are looking for people that have the following traits

  • Are 18 years of age or older in Real Life. This is the minimum age to join our guild and the minimum level of maturity we expect from our members. If you are 24 and act 12, you will not find a home long with us. Also some of the roleplay writings are adult themed.
  • Enjoy the journey to level 65 as much as being there. If you are someone who feels you need to rush to level 60 and get into that high end game as fast as possible, we wish you well. But you will not enjoy our guild. We like to raid Befallen and Droga as much as we like to raid higher level places.
  • Are competent and fair players, or new players willing to learn the game and of good morale fibre.
  • Feel profanity is out of place in guild chat. We do not tolerate it. Guild chat is a PRIVATE chat channel for our members and you are expected to be respectful to our standards of decency in guild chat.
  • Respect your fellow players even when you don't agree
  • Expect, and like to, earn what they have through their own efforts - we do not do quests for people but will help them to finish them if needed. We do not do quests that are not appropriate for your level.
  • Expect to earn their levels - We do not power level. Ever. If you want to PL your friends feel free but as a guild, we do not do this. As I said above, we enjoy the journey through levels as much as the attainment of them.
  • Are not loot driven - D.I. is not epic capable. We may never be epic capable. We do not measure success by what uber zones we can conquer. We raid and our raids are fun, and generally successful. But not the sole reason of our existance by far.
  • Enjoy participating in the comunity that is the guild - Keeping your roster up to date, reading the official web site news and the forums regularly are important and expected of all members. If you do not like keeping up on current events and what the guild is doing, you will not get the most out of this guild. Much of our roleplay is board centered. All our decision making is and all our events are announced on the website and boards. There are MANY guilds that do not care if you participate or not, we are NOT one of those guilds. We expect you to be active in our guild.
  • Enjoying playing in a casual way - Most of our members play casually. They have limited play times and do not consider EQ to be the only source of entertainment in their lives. If you are looking for a guild that has 30 members online 24/7 then we are not for you. Sometimes you may be the only member on. Sometimes there will be many of us. We are growing but in no rush to.
  • Are honest and fair minded as a person - Even if the character you play is not, the OOC side of you is quickly learned by us. Greedy, selfish, dishonest people are removed.
  • Have tasteful character names - We wont be allowing characters named Crackho or Pimpdaddy into the guild. Our banner will not fly over the heads of people with such names. Names should be mature and preferably roleplay friendly.
