Dark Inheritance

Dark Inheritance is a roleplaying based guild on the Luclin Server, of Everquest. Est Oct, 2002.

 

 

August 27th, 2004

Given the lack of use of the website and forums since the announcement of Council that the guild would be closing, I will be closing this webspace on September 31st, 2004.

Please make an effort to back up any articles or stories important to you, check your private messages, and trade email addresses with your friends.

August 24th, 2004

With the retirement of Lord Serrik Ryuujin and Lady Raiya Ryuujin, Altus Concilium elected to close the doors of Dark Inheritance of Luclin for all time.

Members have been offered a home in Descending Essence, or encouraged to choose their own path.

I thank you, all my friends, allies and members, for the past years of wonderful roleplay and friendship. Please continue to use the forums to keep in touch for the next few months while I decide what to do with the webspace.

Your former Marshal,

Serrik Ryuujin aka Danny
