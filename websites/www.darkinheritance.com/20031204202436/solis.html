The House of Light


Domus Solis is the House of those who follow the ways of Truth, Light, Love and Compassion.

Some are fervent guardians and seek to destroy evil wherever they find it. Some are bent upon the salvation of even the most wretchedly evil creatures.

Formed by the Gods of Goodness and Light, Domus Solis accepts followers of the following Gods as well as agnostics who pledge their strengths to Solis in like purpose.

Worshippers of Erollisi Marr, Karana, Mithaniel Marr, Rodcet Nife, Quellios, Tunare, as well as agnostics devoted to the ideals these Gods embody.

Domus Solis is lead by Fubarz, a cleric of Tunare.

Members of Domus Solis are expected to be living examples of all this House stands for.
