The Official Guild Plot

To know the History of the Triad is to understand it. For the Triad sprung from the minds of the Gods, and not of mortals.

Many centuries ago, the dark Gods gathered together to plot. However, the dark Gods, so painfully self-serving, were unable to agree on nearly all ideas. Save one.

For they shared one common goal, and that was the destruction of the Gods of Light, and the Gods Neutrality or Shadow. Knowing that these Gods drew strength from their followers, and unable to attack them directly as Veeshan forbade it, they created Domus Noctis.

Innoruuk through His Priests placed Domus Noctis, after much arguing, deep within Neriak and elevated to Noble Standing, and the church emptied its coffers to fund the birth of Domus Noctis. The Dark Ones found a particularly evil and devoted young Shadowknight, Subashai, and sat her upon the seat of this House. She quickly had Domus Noctis residences placed within each of the evil cities of the land. Each of these are located in secret and often used for meetings of the House Council.

Today House Noctis has its hand in many things. Brothels, slave trading, thief rings, smuggling. Anything that requires subterfuge, or dark intentions, in order to make a profit, likely has a member of Domus Noctis involved at some level.

The purpose of Domus Noctis, was to do what the Gods could not do directly. Destroy the realm of the light and shadow. Bring harm to the followers of such Gods. Recruit followers for the Dark. To win over the lands, hearts, and minds of mortals by any means necessary.

The Gods of Light and Shadow were not unaware of what had occurred. The saw the genius in the plans of the Dark Ones and moved to action, lest be caught at a disadvantage.

The Tribunal formed Domus Umbra in a flash. Urthor, a Shaman of Halas, was sent forth and told to turn over a large stone buried beneath the snows of Everfrost. Upon doing sow he found the boulder to be riddled with gold. The Tribunal had him form Domus Umbra with the wealth, and explained to him the purpose for such a thing, to win over the hearts, minds, and lands of mortals, to the ways of Neutrality and Order.

Tunare, the mother of all, was asked by the Gods of Light to find someone to lead and build Domus Solis, the House of Light. Knowing that the very nature of the House would require clash, she called out the Half Elf Paladin, Noray. Noray was a follower of Karana, and his heart was pure and his mind that of a raging storm, bent upon the destruction of all things dark and evil.

Noray was from a family of great wealth, and he established Domus Solis.

Many Centuries have gone by since the Triad was formed. Leaders of each have come and gone yet the original goals are always held true.

Many times have the Houses gone to war, or called truces to shore up their strength.

Such a truce rains now. For with the discovery of life and access to the moon Luclin, and the doorways opening to the great demi-planes, each House finds itself short to establish itself upon these new lands.

Altus Concilium, the Great Council, oversees the truce.

How long this truce will last is obvious. It will last until one House has become much stronger then the others. That is how long each truce has lasted in the past and likely will again.

And so we find ourselves upon Norrath today. Three Houses rebuilding, tied together by a truce that defies their very nature.


(Out of Character)

The guild plot has been made deliberately light, so as to provide only a backdrop for the guild members of all three Houses to Roleplay against / with.

All members that wish to RP within our guild are expected to know this brief history and to leave it unchanged in all their writings or roleplay.
