The past week or so
Posted by: Serrik on Thursday, July 17, 2003 - 07:15 PM
We spent many nights as a guild and got much accomplished over the past 2 weeks.

A successful raid upon the Tower of Frozen Shadows resulted in further clues towards the possible restoration of an old friend.

Keys to Sebelis and Howling Stones were attained for almost all members of the appropriate seasons.

Grats to Dariana and Elyndriel for both breaking their 50th season this week!

Grats to Meneltel for acheiving her 55th season this week and watch out for her brutal new critical hits.

Grats to Thbon for shedding his probation status and becoming a full time permanent member.

Grats to me on becoming Brother Serrik =P

We aided Thousand Suns in a successful Grimling War.

Meneltel looted a spectacular mask in Maidens Eye, and Elyndriel looted a flowing thought uber staff during the Grimling war.

Tonight some of us venture into Howling stones.

I apologize for the lack of news updates lately as I have just been exhausted each night when we log out =P
