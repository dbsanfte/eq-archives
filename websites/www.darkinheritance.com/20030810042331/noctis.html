The House of Darkness

Domus Noctis is the House of the followers of the Dark and Evil Gods. Evil does not mean like minded tho, each God has its own final goals and this can lead to turmoil within this usually strong and united House.

This House holds Shadowknights and Necromancers and all those with a festering hate for the light in their hearts.

Formed by the gods of darkness, hate, diseases, fear, Noctis is the Oldest of the Houses, and the most ruthless.

This House contains worshippers of Bertoxxulous, Cazic Thule, Innoruuk, and Rallos Zek, as well as agnostics devoted to the ideals these Gods embody.

Domus Noctis holds regular guard killings as a sign of devotion to its Dark Gods.
