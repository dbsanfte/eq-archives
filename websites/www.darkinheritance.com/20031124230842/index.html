Welcome to Dark Inheritance!

Dark Inheritance is a roleplaying based guild on the Luclin Server, of Everquest. Est Oct, 2002.

Within the halls of Dark Inheritance you are under the protection of Altus Concilium and are expected to conduct yourself in a manner befitting a guest.

Our goal as a guild is to provide an atmosphere for our members that respects, and encourages roleplaying.
