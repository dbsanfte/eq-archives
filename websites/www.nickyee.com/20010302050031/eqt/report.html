This is an online presentation of an ongoing EQ study. Different aspects of the game are explored qualitatively and quantitatively in cyclical phases. Questions from one phase are derived from findings from past phases. Participants are encouraged to remain involved in the project, and this allows analysis of data across different phases. This website represents a midway report of the ongoing study.
