Originality

Here is where I give you, the Ruckii the chance to express yourself.  Whether it be a picture you've drawn or a song you've composed, feel free to e-mail it to me and I'll set you up a section here to shine.

I'll organize the site by making separate pages for each person who sends in things they'd like to have showcased.
