Journal of the Talechaser

I will write this introduction with optional words depending on if I am still with you, or if I died today in-game =)  So, if you were in a group which  was unfortunate enough to see the demise of the Talechaser today, you should replace the appropriate words with the new edited words in the parenthesis ().

Here is kept the (last) writings of a certain minstrel we all know (knew) and love(d) named Obidil Talechaser.  Obidil is (was) a good man, who is (was) well known for his interesting behavior and vast amount of knowledge during his time exploring the world (that was) known as Norrath.

December 2002, where does the time fly?

  • December 8, 4:24 pm - Well, it's been long enough with no journal entry I think. <chuckle>  I've been having a blast this week, though I was out of town for 4 days, when I came home, I took Robag and his new spells and Warder to Rathe and found out, I have the power to take them on solo now.  Needless to say, Robag is now halfway through level 40 after many a dead Hill Giant and a full suit of Acrylia armor that he financed with the loot from these beasts.  The other night Ironfrost and I took to the forests of Jaggedpine to fight the poachers with the assistance of Rranita.  Lots of fun has been had lately and Hopefully I'll get more entries written.
  • December 2, 2:30 am - Well, here we are in yet another December of EverQuest Joyousness.  We started off the month with a bang tonight.  It all started with a Ding.  The Ding of Obidil's third AA point thanks to the Poachers of Jaggedpine and to the quick and witty assistance (And her ability to root) of Wyndd our good and now appropriately renamed, Druidess.  We had a grand time, but once I dinged and put my AA point into Intelligence like the goober that I am, We both logged off for about an hour.  When we returned she had troubles staying connected and finally her modem just went poof, as I joined Ironfrost on Robag for some poacher death of a Tribunal kind.  We took out mane a poacher, handling the additional pulls like the true gentlemen we are (SICK EM MUTTS).  A few times we grabbed a bit more than we could handle, but once I realized how to pull then safely, we had a great time.  Later on, Lysianari joined us and we took them even faster.  The Ruckus was truly brought when Kiritateru made it to the camp and we had ourselves a decent Tank!  Many Poachers fell to our might as we cast spell after spell taking them by force.  During the night, I dinged to level 39 and after we were through, Wyndd made it back in time to help me get to Halas and find my new spells and massively powerful pet.  Wyndd stayed outside with Malinn and chatted up a storm and then they were joined by Rranita and her game-husband Silvium.  Once I found all the spells I needed, we all pulled up camp and headed for Rathe Mountains.  We had a great time, later joined by Ironfrost and Nenamaro we all became very rich, before I passed out from exhaustion.
