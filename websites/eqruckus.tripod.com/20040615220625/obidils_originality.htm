Ode to a Cyclops Corpse

Tis boredom that brings the blade...

to slay the noble Cyclopse

His feet are large, and his hands are big, as he thinks,

"They look like big strong Hands, Don't they"

he smiles as he fishes in the river

a noble strong intelligent thing

but when a  bard is passing

He shouts, "ME WILL CRUSH YOU TO GOO"

the bardly bard passing in the night figures, "Awe, What the hey"

He slew that Broon, the Cyclopse

and it red did the river run that day.
