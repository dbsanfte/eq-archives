Master BAKING List for EverQuest 

Baking used to be only practical for Role-Playing in the game.  Recently, with the addition of food strengths and weaknesses (duration of nourishment), it has been made into a rather profitable trade.  A friend of mine likes to make Beer-Brazed food in mass quantities and sell them for quite a profit.

Some recipes result in food that raises various statistics now, once these stats are catalogued , I'll post them here.

Note that when an Item has a Number after the name [Rabbit Stew (68)], the number represents the baking level this item becomes trivial. 

Other Skills used when baking:

  • Blacksmithing
  • Pottery
  • Fishing
  • Forage

Creation Facilities (Combine Containers):

  • Mixing Bowl – The Mixing bowl is used for the creation of basic ingredients like the Clump of Dough and Winter Chocolate.  A Potter creates this, but you may also purchase one at local merchants.
  • Oven – The oven is an often used and seldom celebrated commodity.  The oven is where you will do the majority of your baking from the ever-popular Edible Goo to the upper level Cakes.  These are located in every city (Including Grobb now).  There are also Ovens located in the Overthere and in the Firiona Vie Outpost on Kunark.
  • Spit – The spit is a portable oven.  Bakers who like to prepare fresh meals on the go most often use it.  They are sold in Kelethin, East Freeport, and in the Cottage in Qeynos Hills.

Creation Utensils (Placed in Creation Facilities to help shape food):

  • Pie Tin – Reusable - Used to make the various pies
  • Muffin Tin – Reusable – This is used to bake muffins and cupcakes.
  • Cake Round – Reusable – This is used in the higher-level Cake recipes.
  • Skewers – Reusable – Used in the creation of Kabobs.
  • Pot – Reusable – Used to create soups.
  • Smoker – Reusable – Used to smoke meat (Wood Elf, and Shark)
  • Bread Tin – Not Reusable – Used to bake loaves of bread.
  • Cookie Cutters – Reusable – Comes in many shapes and sizes – Used to shapes dough into shapes for baking cookies.

Guide to a Quick Mastery of Baking:

There are five steps to getting on your way to become a Double Master Baker (200) using the easiest path.  These recipes have the most common ingredients, and their failure rates seem to be a bit lower as well.  This works WONDERS if you are already a Master Fisherman (Like I was).

  1. Edible Goo till 20
  2. Batwing Crunchies till 45
  3. Fish Fillets till 82
  4. Fish Rolls till 135
  5. Your choice of Pies until 142
  6. Cakes Cakes and more Cakes till you're sick of Cakes (These do not go trivial)

Tae Ew Baking

This is made with Tae Ew meat.  Note that the meat is called "Tae Ew Meat" and it does not stack (the finished products stack, once they are baked).

  • Tae Ew Skewers (<=162): chunk of Tae Ew meat no stack), spices, jug of sauce, combine in spit or oven with skewers (yield 2, hearty meal)
  • Tae Ew Roll (>163): chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), bat wing, combine in spit or oven (miraculous meal)
  • Tae Ew Sandwich: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), bread, combine in spit or oven (feast)
  • Tae Ew Soup: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), water flask, jug of sauces, combine in spit or oven with a pot (yield 1, miraculous meal, weight 1.0)
  • Tae Ew Pie: chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), clump of dough, combine in spit or oven with a pie tin (miraculous meal, yield 2, weight 1.0 ... cookie cutter icon?!)
  • Candied Tae Ew (>172): chunk of Tae Ew meat (no stack), frosting, spice, combine in spit/oven (miraculous meal, yield 8, weight 1.0, trivial >172)
