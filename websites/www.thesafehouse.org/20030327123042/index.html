WoW Beastiary

Story by - Kaylan


"World of Warcraft Beastiary Launched!  The doors are now open to the World of Warcraft Beastiary . EQ Item & Spell Database
2003-03-22 10:57:32
Story by - Aidden


In our ever increasing efforts to bring you new features and content, The Safehouse has implemented the EQ Items database and EQ Spell database. This is the first phase of implementing Imex Jenkins EQCPS. EQCPS is an open source character profile system similar to Magello. The Next step will be to implement the complete character profile system.

We plan on offering this system to our members free of charge. The only thing required is you have a Safehouse website account active.

The character profile system should be fully implemented within 30 days. In the mean time enjoy the EQ item and spell databases. You can find the databases here:
