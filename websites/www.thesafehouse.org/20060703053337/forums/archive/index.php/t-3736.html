If anyone has anything to add as far as the bane weapons and Key please do, but this is all that I have found, and it seems to be concrete and confirmed via multiple sources.

********************************************
BANE WEAPONS:

Quicksilver Temper is brewed and trivial at 228. The Yttrium Steel Sheet has a high smithing trivial (around 250). Both of these items are droppable. The final combines' trivial levels are in the mid to upper 100s. The final weapons are no drop, so you have to produce them yourself.

Quicksilver Temper is made by combining Shissar Blood (dropped off Shissars), Fungoid Sap (from Ancient Shriekers in the Deep), ShikNar Ichor (from the Shik'Nars in Fungus Grove) and Meteor Dust (random drop in The Grey).

The Yttrium Steel Sheet is made by combining two Small Bricks of Yttrium Ore and the Quicksilver Temper.

The final combine produces the weapon of your choice: Use a Pommel Mold for the weapon you want, a Hilt Mold, the Quicksilver Temper and the Yttrium Steel Sheet and you will get your weapon.

Reward(s):
Yttrium Dagger
* Yttrium Falchion
* Yttrium Long Sword
* Yttrium Mace
* Yttrium Short Sword
* Yttrium Spiked Gloves
* Yttrium War Axe
* Yttrium War Hammer
* Yttrium-Imbued Oak Bow

************************************************** *
Key Quest:
Kill the following 4 NPC’s, loot a head, turn in the heads to a NPC in Katta, give you an Arx Key. One key is a 10 dose group port to LIS’s room. Each NPC banishes to the jail room. Simple way to kill each, send in the pets, and anyone else nukes/dot’s. Pets are immune to being banished. So long as the pet’s are high on the agro list, no one gets banished. Alternate method, bring a mage, have them coth every time someone is banished.

Praesertum Bikun
Praesertum Matpa
Praesertum Rhugol
Praesertum Vantorus

First earring is some task you have to do in Netherbain and Seru, running back and forth basically.
Second earring just involves giving something to a filing cabinet I think.
Third earring is report farming in each of the houses.
Fourth earring is killing the generals of each house and getting their head, basically the fight is like a dain fight (fight, banish, run back, etc.), have melees bind before the general chamber, then have a wizard sit at the jail spot, give the melees a TL box then engage, all but shoulder I think are slowable.
Fifth earring is getting Seru's head.

You get Seru Arx key along with the 4th earring, unlimited charges now I think,

Seru bane requires master smithing, it's like 175 or something needed to make the sheets and 125+ to get the seru bane weapon made. Everyone that needs Seru bane needs to master smithing. Look up the components for Seru bane at Allakhazam or something.

Strategy from when I killed him long ago before mod rod nerf (I'm assuming it's still the same fight, but shorter now with no more mod rods) was to position casters diagonally from pin pillar (block AE, stay out of LoS, similar to HP blocking AE) and then have warrior fight Seru in the pillar with someone timing the AE. Melees fight Seru from behind, AE called, strafe left to block AE, then come back in to fight, similar to Cursed fight. Casters just nuke whenever AE goes off. Seru is disease slowable and his DPS isn't too bad, flurries occasionally, has like 1.2 million HP or something. Shouldn't be too hard, just don't move too close to doors or you'll aggro guards. Seru fight is like Cursed fight with a mob that fights like Tunare (a lot less flurries though).
