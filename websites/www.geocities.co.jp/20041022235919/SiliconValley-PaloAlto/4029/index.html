- NEWS 00/03/27 -

FAを装備しててもBashができるかもしんないｗ．

March 27 - Have some good news from Aradune about Fiery Avenger and Bashing. Thanks to Fenar for telling me in-game. The Forums are already talking about it!

After some thought and consideration, we've made it such that one can bash when wielding the FA, even though it remains 2H.

We're also contemplating adding FA type weapons for all the classes, including special 'particle' effects for those weapons, but it's going to take us a while to get them all in (meaning the quests, graphics, and such), so please be patient.

Also, making some 2h weapons 'bash ok' is something we're going to limit to FA type weapons, so I'm going to go ahead and say 'no' to all the requests to make other 2h weapons the same -- sorry in advance.

- Brad

FAが1hand slashingの武器になるという意味ではなくて，Bash okな2H武器っていうカテゴリーが各クラス用に登場するようなことを示唆してます．FAはそのひとつになる模様．
