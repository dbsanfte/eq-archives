Still looking for an auto-notification program which will let people know when the page has been updated by the way...

Ok, so all the contact details are done now, and I can get back to the stockpile of photos I've got left. Bear in mind that from now on I will be adding your e-mail address and any other contact details you provide (not phone/address though) unless you tell me not to. You have been warned!

Anyone is welcome to send in their photo to me at any time, just as soon as you pluck up the courage! Send all photos to steve@hildrew.demon.co.uk with the subject title "everquest photo", and any relevant information, like persona name, server, race, and class in the message (also TELL ME if you don't want your contact details (e-mail, ICQ etc. added to your entry) - you're also welcome to send in an accompanying screenshot...
