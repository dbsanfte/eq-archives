PLAYER PHOTOS!

This page contains photos of everquest players who have up to this point been brave enough to send me their real life photos - the people behind the pixels! (triangles, polygons, whatever). The page is kindly hosted by EQrealms and staffed by Frisky Mittens, a remarkably talented cat...

If you have sent in a photo in the last couple months and it hasn't been added yet, please re-send it to the new address.
Updated this week - Brell Serilis, Bristlebane, Druzzil Ro, E'ci, Fennin Ro, Lanys T'vyl, Povar, Prexus, Rallos Zek, Saryrn, Tallon Zek, The Rathe, The Tribunal, Veeshan, and Xegony!

Anyone is welcome to send in their photo to me at any time, just as soon as you pluck up the courage! Send all photos to steerpike@webbworks.com with the subject title "everquest photo", and any relevant information, like persona name, SERVER!!!!, race, and class and the SERVER!!! you play on in the message (also tell me if you don't want your contact details (e-mail, ICQ etc.) added to your entry) - you're also welcome to send in an accompanying screenshot.
