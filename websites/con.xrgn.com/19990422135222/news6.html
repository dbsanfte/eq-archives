EverQuest Official FAQ Updated
The Official EverQuest FAQ on the EverQuest Home page was Updated yesterday. Here's some of the changes:
2.0 About the beta test.

2.1 What phase of beta testing are you at currently?

We are currently at Phase 3 of our beta test, which just began in early November.

2.2 So what's after Phase 3?

Following Phase 3 will be Phase 4, which is open to all who applied for beta testing with a small fee ($4.95 which covers the cost of the CD and shipping). Unfortunately, due to timing and cost constraints, Phase 4 CDs will only be shipped to residents of the United States and Canada.

2.3 Do you know how many people you'll accept into Phase 4?

Anyone who signed up for the beta test (the application was online between March and October of 1998) will have the opportunity to participate. Unfortunately, due to timing and cost constraints, Phase 4 CDs will only be shipped to residents of the United States and Canada.

2.4 How long will Phase 3 and Phase 4 last?

Phase 3 will officially end February 9th, with Phase 4 beginning the day after and continuing until shortly before commercial release on March 16th.


-Groupal
