ITEM LOSS BY TRADE

 How many of us have lost items by trade? Some examples:

1)...I purchased a Rubicite chest for my friend. I was going to transfer it to my mule character to transfer over. I forgot that there was already a full set of rubicite in the bank from a while back... POOF no rubi chest.

2)...A friend walks up to me and asks me to hold a bag for him. I'm playing a druid, he hands me a bag. When I give him the bag back, he asks me where his rubi and blackened crown went.. again Rubi in the bank, I had no idea what was in the bag and didn't think to ask, and he seeing I was a druid assumed I would have no rubicite.

3)...Last night a friend was transfering items from his Wizard to his newly made Enchanter.. He handed an evil eye bag to his paladin without giving consideration to the fact that he already had one. BOOM, 8 very hard to get, very rare items gone.

My point isn't that it's Verant's fault that we sometimes mess up and don't pay attention to what we're doing, but that shouldn't cost us dearly. It is in my humble opinion that if we are trying to loot an item that we already have the item / bag in question will fall to the ground or back in the trader's inventory and give a signal that we are doing something we shouldn't. I know people who have lost 2 rubi chests that way. I saw a post by someone yesterday that put it about as plain as it gets. Games are supposed to be an escape from the world we live in.. the world we don't have that much control over. EQ we should have more control over and less things waiting to bite us in the butts (Like this setup)

If you want Verant to take this Item loss setup away then please show your support and sign this petition.
