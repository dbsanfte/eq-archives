OGRE

Ogres are incredibly massive beings, with their average height being about 10’, and weigh well over four hundred pounds, which makes them a truly immovable object. Due to their size, they are the strongest of all the races and are tied with the Trolls in pure Stamina. Conversely, they are the stupidest race, whose lives mostly consist of smashing things and eating them.
