St. Valentine's Day
Festival of Lovers
February 14.
In olden days, before the rise of Christianity, the pagan people believed this day to be the day that birds chose their life mates. This day was set aside to honor of love, commitment, and monogomy. Celebrations included dancing, feasting, and exchanging tokens of affections with loved ones.
Superstition claims that if you sleep with a rose under your pillow the night before, you will dream of your soul mate.
