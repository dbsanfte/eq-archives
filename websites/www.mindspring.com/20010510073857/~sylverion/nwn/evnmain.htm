PVP Singles Ladder

The singles ladder is an ongoing realms wide event pitting the best of the one-on-one PVPers against each other. Participants wage battle against each other in an effort to climb the "ladder" and claim the number one spot. This event has had hundreds of participants in its history and still rates as "the" ladder to prove your mettle in Neverwinter. For more information see the PVP Ladder folder on the Events board. To join the ladder or inquire about the rules, send email to Sagx.

The Bregan D'Aerth has had considerable success on the realms wide singles ladder. Both Kh0lari and Sylverion have held the number one position there. Other past and present participants on the ladder include: Abzzsta1n, Adonaste, Darkuth, Dem0nica, Drakhen, Glaeros, Goodygater, Lazzeraith, Sagx, Xerdek, XYLOT, Zotreth and Zress.


PVP Doubles Ladder

As its name suggests, the PVP doubles ladder pits teams of two individuals against each other in combat. This is an ongoing event in Neverwinter that stresses team combat. Most of the top teams in this event are or have been members of the singles ladder. To receive more information see the PVP ladder folder on the Events board. To join the ladder or inquire about the rules, send email to Sir Rellik.

Success has also followed the Bregan D'Aerth to the Doubles ladder with the team of Kh0lari and Sylverion holding the number one spot at various times.


WPC Singles PVP League

Singles PVP fighting is also done it the WPC League. This league pits opponents against each other once a week at a predetermined location. Emphasis is placed upon KO as the form of win To join the league or inquire about the rules, send email to Traelena.

Demonica, of the BDA, has done exceptionally well in the WPC. She has taken and held the top spot in the League for a record tying amount of time.


Quad WPC PVP League

The ultimate in team PVP competition can be found in the Quad WPC PVP League. 4 on 4 team guild competition at its finest is the theme of this weekly event. Fights are usually held on Sunday evenings and are some of the better fights in the game. To join the league or inquire about the rules, send email to Slayve.

The Bregan D'Aerth fielded two teams for this league with both of them doing outstanding. Team 2 had an impressive mark in their league and reached the semi finals. Winning their League and Conference, BDA Team 1 went all the way to the final championship match, but fate reared its ugly head.


Guild of Heroes (GOH)

The Guild of Heroes presents ample opportunity for members of the NWN community to earn pearls to purchase those elusive items. Monthly contests include things such as joke telling, story writing and graphic design contests. These month long contests provide pearl prizes of 25, 20 and 15 pearls to the winners of the contest. GOH only mini booths are also done twice monthly by the NW staff. For more information check out the GOH boards.


Who/What/Am/I (WWAI)

WWAI is a monthly trivia contest sponsored by the NW staff. This event happens in the Great Hall of Neverwinter on the third Thursday of every month from 10 pm EDT to 11:30 pm EDT. Participants are asked three part trivia questions with the top three in points received, getting pearl prizes of 25, 20 and 15 pearls. These is also a random roll off for a prize of 5 pearls.


NWN Trivia

NWN Trivia is a monthly trivia contest sponsored by the NW staff. This event happens in the Great Hall of Neverwinter on the first Tuesday of every month from 10 pm EDT to 11:30 pm EDT. Participants are asked three part trivia questions with the top three in points received, getting pearl prizes of 25, 20 and 15 pearls. These is also a random roll off for a prize of 5 pearls.


Neverwinter Summer Festival

Neverwinter celebrated its fifth anniversary in grand style with a Summer Festival. This event ran the gamut of PVP booths, a trivia contest, various spell related booths and a random drawing for some top notch prizes. Congrats to all of the BDA members who turned out to fill their coffers with pearls.
