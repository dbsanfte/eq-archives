Portal Room
As you enter the portal room you are bombarded with an intense aura of magic. Drow magicians and priests have spent countless centuries enchanting the items in this room. The items soon become appearent when you walk to the center of the chamber. Portals... numerous portals all leading to different lands. Through the haze on their surface you can see many strange and different lands. One thing remains constant however, the Bregan D'Aerth are there, and looking for new challenges to conquer.
