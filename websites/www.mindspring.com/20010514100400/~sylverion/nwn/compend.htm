Monster Compendium







When the Bregan D'Aerth appeared on the surface it determined that to accomplish its goals it must learn about the denziens of this strange new world. With this in mind a section of the caverns library was set aside for the express purpose of recording the characteristics and abilities of the monsters encountered in the Neverwinter area. Ten volumes currently house all of the known monsters which have been slain around Neverwinter. An eleventh volume holds monsters whose existance has been mentioned in other tomes, but never encountered. Select one of the books above to learn more about these monsters.
