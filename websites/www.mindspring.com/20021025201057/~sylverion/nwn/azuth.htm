Azuth's Challenge II


Some individuals of the realms live and breathe for combat. For the members of the Bregan D'Aerth, PvP is part of their very being. Destruction and conquest have become synonymous with the BDA and true to form this happened again with Azuth's Challenge II. Team members Sagx, Abzzsta1n and XO2 Zeus barreled their way through the competition and claimed the crown of this 3 on 3 PvP event. Prizes for the day were 15 pearls, awarded to the guild account.

ALL HAIL THE MIGHTY BREGAN D'AERTH!!!
