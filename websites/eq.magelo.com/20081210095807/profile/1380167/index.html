Biography
 
Originally from the Cazic Thule server, I played there from Dec 2001-April 2006 and was probably in 80% of the raiding guilds there. I've always had a mouth and pushed limits in guilds with unfair loot systems, bad leadership, and favortism/double standards towards certain members. This attitude and play style made me server transfer to join the resident Drinal server asshole guild Cats in Hats in April 2006. My Original character name was Susansilver then Jacelyn then Jacelynx and finally Jace. I was class leader of paladins in Cats in Hats but decided to leave after some unfavorable loot calls and poor leadership. I rejoined a month later to play with some old friends, but poor leadership and lack of progression led me to server transfer to Maelin in December 2007 to join Crimson Tempest with some old CIH friends. I was the owner of the serverwide.paladins channel off and on from 2004-2008 and still hug ogres.

I formed Team Aakla around the spring of 2007, Team Aakla is a crew of skilled players from Drinal that at one time were all in the same fellowship. Due to the limit of 9 for fellowships we spread out over multiple others but some still wear the surname of their mascot, Aakla the rainbow ogre.

As of late May 2008 I took a break from raiding after being unable to attain more then 60% attendence in Crimson Tempest. I was tagged with Stronghold of Savages for almost 3 months and held only a 20-40% attendence due to my decline in hardcore raiding and lack of modivation to play the game. As of August 2008 I am back in the hardcore raiding game with Crimson Tempest.
