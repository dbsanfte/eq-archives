Biography
 
This Priestess of Tunare is a officer of Immortal Sunrise.

We are an Everquest I Guild coming from Kael Drakkal.

We define ourselves as a Semi-Raid Guild which means that apart from raiding, there is also enough time for other activities.

We're raiding 3 to 4 days a week at approximately 7pm - 11.30pm GMT+1, depending on the raid target.

We're mainly raiding Citadel of Anguish, Tacvi, DoN, DoDh and PoR Progress.
