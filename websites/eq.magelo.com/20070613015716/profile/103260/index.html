<br>Pomaikai started his career as a member of the now sadly defunt Fate's Sentinels on the Zebuxoruk server. He is now a proud member of <b>Infinite Alliance</b>. For more information on <b>Infinite Alliance</b>, please visit our website at: <a href=" http://www.eqia.com/" target=_new>Infinite Alliance</a> to see who's ass we're kicking now!
<br>
<br>Pomaikai was born on August 26, 2001. He has never been PL'd, is untwinked, and has over 150 days played to his name, albiet some of that is his Bazaar Trader penance...
<br>
<br>Pomaikai Po`okela is a Hawaiian name which roughly translates into "The chosen champion of the God's." Feel free to PM with a more exact translation and I'll be happy to put it here. No, I am not Hawaiian, but I am a huge fan of their shirts, and have an extensive collection of Grade A Hawaiian Aloha shirts!
