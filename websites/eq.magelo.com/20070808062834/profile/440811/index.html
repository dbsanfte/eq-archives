Biography
 
Made this toon when Luclin came out. First toon, a warrior, was getting tiring. I made a druid to ease my mind, and somewhat enjoyed being a caster. I can truely say that I have enjoyed Merescata more than any other toon I have tried. The blend of caster and melee fits my play style quite nicely. My stats are reasonable for the time being, so I believe that I have done fairly well.
