Biography
 
Judge Ueauvan Steinfiller was a Senior Officer in the Stein Guild. He is now wandering Norrath in search of new adventures in the company of his new Guild Far East. He has been married for over a year to Xasia a Feir'dal Preserver of Tunare. He was the first Fletcher on Drinal to achieve 250 skill in Fletching. He was the first on any server to make the Blessed Faydark Cloudburster and the first on Drinal to make the Blessed Faydark Thunderbolt.

Ueauvan's hobbies include smurf hunting, Paladin baiting and Colonial bashing. Xasia and he have one son, Minijudge a ranger of 43 seasons. They reside wherever their tradeskills take them and there is a high chance to find one or both of the Steinfillers at a Brew barrel
