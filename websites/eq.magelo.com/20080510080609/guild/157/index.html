Description

Wreckers is an Australian timezone guild and are looking for a few more key players to help us complete TSS and beyond.

Of particular interest are 2 warriors, 1 necro and 1 chanter, while other classes are also open for the right people.

We're looking for people who are committed to both their toon and the guild and are able to maintain a solid raid attendance (ie. > 70%) of our rather light raiding schedule.

Current farm targets are DK and FC1/2.
Current progression targets are Beltron and Vergalid.

Visit our website for more information or contact Aamasie, Thoraf or Weolyen in game.
