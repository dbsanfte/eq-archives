7:30-11:30 eastern (Sun-Thur) but we only ask for 10-12 hours a week from applicants.

Talk to us if you don't meet requirements below (but are close), as we do have a secondary app status which gives you guild tag/support to meet requirements.
  • Level 80+ (85 come February 1st)
  • MMM group zone access (preferred) or started (required)
  • 300-500 AA for high/medium need classes, 500+ AA for low/no need classes and tanks.
  • Basic group obtainable gear (around 300 hp/mana unaugged) with augments in every slot

We are a relaxed PG13+ guild that working in MMM (Breakneck dead) with Solteris and BMK3/Soulbleeder on farm. We pride ourselves on respect and being fairly low drama - and expect the same from any new applicants.
  • Skill is important but good character is imperative.
  • 3rd Party users look elsewhere

7:30 - 11:30pm eastern (4:30 - 7:30pm pacific) raid times Sunday - Thursday
  • Friday/Saturday are always off

We raid 20 hours a week and ask applicants to be available for 10-12 of them, at their choice. Our members come/go as they need, and there is no pressure to be there from start to end.

We are DKP based on a slow point rot (45 days), so those who currently raid to help us progress are the ones who receive the rewards from that current content. That said, mid attendance and high attendance raiders earn similar opportunities for gear upgrades.

If any of the following interests you and you want to know more, please use the following forms of communication:

1) In game email to: erollisi.crystilla or erollisi.caeelar
2) In game tells to: erollisi.crystilla or erollisi.caeelar
3) Our message board: www.resolutionguild.org then go to forums, and you can register and send a private message.
