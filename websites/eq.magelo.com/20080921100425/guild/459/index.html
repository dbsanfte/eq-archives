Description

We are looking for players who have all current expansions who are level 75 with at least 250 AA's. For specific class requirements see the guild website.

They should be flagged for the following:
GoD - past content - no longer backflagging this area
Anguish (must have all signets)
Dreadspire Keep - Must have DSK access
Demi-plane - We are flagging for this area now.
Solteris is not required yet, work on access.

One must have the ability (through spell or potion) to do the following:
Gate, Invis, Heal, Float, have maps to get from point A to point B

The minimum of 10 raiding days and all required levels, flags and AA's are required for full membership, as well as and active and up to date Magelo profile.

Utopian is a guild that was founded in 2000 by Talaire, an enchanter of wonderful talent, passion and compassion. It is still one of the oldest guilds on the server.

We are a family based guild that loves to raid. We put family and friends always above the loot, but that doesn't mean we dont love the quest for knowledge and success.

We are now entering Demi-plane with a full force of solid players. We are striving to push through Demi-plane and will juggle that with the new expansion and the push for level 80.

If you feel you are committed to putting the guild first, family first, friends first, so we all can achieve, then feel free to apply.
