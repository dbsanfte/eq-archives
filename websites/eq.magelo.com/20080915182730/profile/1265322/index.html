Biography
 
Norblis was born in the wilds of the Butcherblock Mountains. The son of a paladin father and a ranger mother, he was at home in the woods and enjoyed a rather strict upbringing in the service of Brell.
As he grew, his powers became more apparent and his family sent him to the city of Kaladim to study as a cleric. He quickly learned that he received a lot of enjoyment from helping others. He soon left the city in search of those needy individuals who could benefit from his talents and kindness.
He continues to offer his services to anyone he meets on his quest to become the epitome of the phrase "Great things come in small packages".

After many months of wandering Norrath, he found that he lacked a good group of travelling companions. He decided to start up a guild and the "Travelers of Quellious" were born.

Not long after he formed his guild, he ran into a group of drunken travelers in the Plane of Time. He soon found a new group of folks to call his friends and joined forces with them to explore the various lands of Norrath.

If you happen to run across him in your journeys through Norrath, feel free to ask him for help.
