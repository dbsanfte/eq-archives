Biography
 
Like many Dark Elves, as soon as he was of age Celebrindraug was sent from his family into one of the guilds in Neriak. Luckily, from his point of view, he was regarded as talented and taken by The Dead, the Necromancer guild.

As soon as he knew enough he left the city. The Dead might have been the most prestigeous guild in Neriak, but Celebrindraug despised it, he despised the masters and he dspised the pittance of time they spent educating him. Like most of his race he also despised all the other races, but he also had a burning desire to learn as much as he could; knowledge, after all, is power.

He hunts mostly on Luclin, finding the Vah-Shir's tolerant indiference to his race intriguing, he has even taken to hunting with a Vah-Shir Bard.

To his shame, he has discovered an enjoyment of languages.
