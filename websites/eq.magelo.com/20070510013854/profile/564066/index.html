Biography
 
Originally of the Karana Server, I was born in Halas and raised on the Plains of Everfrost. I am an explorer of my world, a student of its people and lore, and a maker of fine items from its natural riches.

At the tender level of 5 I was honored with membership in the Circle of Friends, until it became necessary for us all to go our separate ways. I was then most fortunate to find home and family among the Celestial Knights, including many of my beloved comrades from the Circle of Friends. With the merger of the Karana server and The Rathe server, I decided the time had come to pursue my heartsister Aradyss (sometimes known as Calandria) and moved to the Tunare server, where I am now settling in.

I am most happy when crafting new things, particularly fine armor to protect the children of Halas. I can also often be found roaming Norrath from the strange lands at the frontiers of the world to the familiar, snowy plains of Everfrost. From Qeynos to Cabilis, Steamfont to Skyshrine, there are few places not filled with warm memories of friends made and foes vanquished. And everywhere always is the anticipation of friends, knowledge, and memories yet to come.
