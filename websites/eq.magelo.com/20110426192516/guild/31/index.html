QUIET SOLITUDE:

We are a guild on Xegony that uses a true, open DKP system. There's no officer interference, no hoarding, no fixed pricing. Bidding is done in raid chat for all to see. Earn DKP by attending raids. We raid Sunday through Thursday starting at 7:30 PM EST and usually finish around 10:00 PM EST. You should be able to make atleast 3 days a week.
