Biography
 
<!-nobleblade->Born David to farmers in the Western Karana plains, I was kidnapped during a raid on my family's home by brigands and bandits. I was only a young boy, and was forced to serve these brutes and their wenches at their camps in the mountains. A boy I was, but I eventually grew old enough to realize my plight.

I eventually stole away one night and made my way to the nearby city of Qeynos, where I found my way into the Temple of Thunder and was taken in by the Knights. I worked hard, serving the Knights by day, and training to become a Knight myself at night, barely sleeping for several years. Eventually, I earned the right to call myself a Knight of Thunder.

I went back to that bandit camp and had my revenge on those false believers that claimed to worship the Rainmaker. I now serve the Rainmaker in the best of my abilities as a True Knight of Thunder.
