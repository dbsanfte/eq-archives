Biography
 
Orphaned as a child by a pack of roaming gnolls, Innania dedicated her youth to learning the powers of illusions so she could one day sneak into the home of the gnolls and destroy thier leaders. As she grew, the pain of her loss faded and she became enamoured of the vastness of Norrath. She used her illusions to explore the many realms, and learn about the peoples and creatures of the world.
