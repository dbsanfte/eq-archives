Biography
 
Married to the Mighty Barbarian Warrior Zudil Ebediyet Jan 1st 2006 the new year welcoming the new partnership they share, both proud members of Silver Tears of Flame with other characters in other Guilds on Quellious Server.

Ebediyet means "For Eternity" as is the bonds of friendship between these two. Bravely facing new zones and new dangers side by side with their friends and fellow guild members of Silver Tears of Flame or others.

"Fuzzy" can usually be found in the thick of combat assisting her group members demolish those assorted mobs who resist giving up their loot, peeking out from behind the broad shoulders of Zudil, or anyone big enough to hide behind, to cast when able... or summoning her clothes to the lobby so she can go back and do it all again.
