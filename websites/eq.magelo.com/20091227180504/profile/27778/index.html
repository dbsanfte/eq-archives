Biography
 
This is my first and main character.

Created back in 2000 before there were any expansions.

I spent those first few months in a guild called Reckoning Dawn with a couple of real life friends and some people that were destined to become long-time online friends. It was a fun time, the greatest days of EQ and MMO's in general. No one had expectations from the game. MMO's were pretty new and with next to no competition we created the phrases, terms, concepts, strategies, and dialect that would go on to many hundreds of games and millions of people worldwide as the MMO phenomena grew.

When that guild folded I moved to Revelry and Honor for a month or two. At that time they had done many joint raids with my first guild and I already had many friends there. But, that did not last. Not long after I joined them, that guild folded as well.

After that some of us attempted to make a new guild called Revelers from those that were left. While that guild lasted for some time, I spent less than a month there before moving onward. A small group of us from Revelers made our way to Adventurers Anonymous.

I joined Adventurers Anonymous just in time to be part of the group that left that guild to join Stasis, where I would find a home for nearly a decade.

It brings me to tears to think back over my time in Stasis. I grew to love that guild more than any other in any game I've ever played. When I arrived in Stasis it was not long after the release of Velious. My first experience at truly high end organized raiding. Sure, I had raided the original planes, and various dungeons across Kunark. Being the first on the server to get various loot from various places including the Planes of Fear, Hate, and Sky. But that was all still a learning experience for everyone involved. When I arrived in Stasis they had already made quite a memorable name for themselves across the forums. And I was introduced to organized raids and strategies.

My first raids with Stasis included Kael, Vindi, AoW, Dragon Necropolis, Halls of Testing within the Temple of Veeshan, Dain, Icewell, Velketor, and the Coldain Ring War (the best event EQ has ever had or likely ever will).

We progressed as time went on. To Emperor Ssraeshza, Lord Inquisitor Seru, and all those other wonderful places on the moon. This was of course long before instanced zones. There were patches and downtime nearly every day. And as soon as the server came up Stasis would rush to the mobs trying and almost always succeeding in beating other guilds there. Which leads us to the infamous Prexus raid calendar dispute and the introduction of FCFS and all the hatred Stasis received from others for being the guild to break the mold and set the way for the future.

From the planes of power to the depths of darkhollow; we came, we saw, we eradicated the enemy and moved on. Across the Serpent's Spine Mountains to the Devastation and Rage prophesized by Ayonae Ro. Back to Faydwer to discover it's secrets and even through time itself we traveled for many, many years. Overcoming obstacles both in game content and guild politics. Nearly collapsing several times we lived on to be remembered as the last original Prexus guild to stop raiding during the summer of 2009.

After much personal dilemma, I pressed onward, moving to Fates Unbroken as of 09/09/09 to continue raiding. It was with a heavy heart I left behind my beloved Stasis tag. But it was friends I was greeted with in Fates Unbroken, both old and new.

We shall see what the next ten years holds for this lowly wood elven druidess. After all, there's still nearly five centuries to go until EQ2! =)
