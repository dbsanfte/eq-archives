Biography
 
Adetia Drey was born a human monkess in the land of Qeynos, and made the journey to Stormhammer from Luclin with her protector, Syrius Swiftscale. From there, the journey was a rugged adventure spanning many years and slain monsters before her wanderings led her back to her homeland of Luclin, where she currently makes her home amongst her family of rebels with her husband, Tabris.

What doesn't kill us makes us stronger.
Above all, I will persevere and conquer.
Hell has no fury like a determined woman with an icepick.
