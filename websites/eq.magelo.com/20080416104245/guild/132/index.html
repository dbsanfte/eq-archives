Description

General

Talisman is the top raiding guild on the Tunare server. We raid on a near daily basis. Our schedule requires a commitment & much enthusiasm from our members. If you aren't willing/able to spend ~4 hours a night dedicating your attention to playing at your best, Talisman isn't the place for you.

Talisman Leadership:

Officers: Rafather, Portis, Dokk, and Annabella
Raid Officers: Tallie, Kukan, Naturio, Lace, and Hump

Do you need / are you recruiting more of xxxx class?

  • We don't accept applicants into the guild based purely on their class; we look for quality players of ALL classes.
  • We raid almost every night from approximately 7:30 p.m. until 12 a.m. Eastern Standard Time.
  • For more info please visit our website.
