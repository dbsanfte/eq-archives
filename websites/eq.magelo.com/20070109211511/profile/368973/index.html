Biography
 
Orphaned by Lucan D`Lere and his militia, Eowenna grew up younger than many. Since her parents had always recommended her to the Knights of Truth if aught befell them, she fled to the Hall of Truth with her younger siblings to seek refuge.

Discontented with the life of a tradesperson, "Enna" applied to the knights and offered herself as an apprentice. They were uncertain of her qualifications, especially given her youth. She was led to the infirmary, where she was asked to pray for the healing of one of the patients. A bright glow spread from her hands as she prayed, thus she gained the title "Paladin of Light" and was simultaneously admitted for training.

Eowenna continues to serve the cause of Truth wherever she goes.
