Pactus Aquilus, the Dark Pact, was formed in May 2000 by founder and guild leader Treesmasha Toothpickmaker and several friends he had found hunting in the dark lands of the south of Antonica. The name is a reference to a pact between the gawds Innoruuk, Cazic Thule, and Rallos Zek. Pactus Aquilus was originally formed as an all-evil guild permitting only members of the dark elf, troll, ogre, and iksar races to join.

Our original intent was to create a guild that would include persons of all levels (in the beginning we did not get many post-level 20's joining our guild of teenagers) and that we would build upon friendship and trust and eventually do great things. It has been SEVEN years now, and we are still going strong holding to our core philosophies.

During all this time, I have seen many people come and go from Norrath. Sometimes I have cried, sometimes I have cheered, but truth be known, I have always wished each person I have met in Norrath the best even if it was not to be in Pactus Aquilus. Also, we eventually saw that EverQuest cannot truly be played in a role-playing fashion and we eventually became an all race guild. Nor can a guild remain truly an all level guild. We are now a level 70+ guild and we focus primarily on Demiplane plus content. This being said, we have never removed an existing member for being below these benchmarks.
