Biography
 
Welcome, Welcome, pull up a chair and a grand tale shall I twist to gray yer hair!

I am Siilkor Darksatin and a fiery bard am I. Emotions that fly like an eagle or fall dark as the deepest notes in my songs. I am sometimes unpredictable and highly demanding in expectations but alas, fear not for I am only one of many on our council. Even tempers always prevail thanks to the diversity amongst our officers.

It twas a long time ago that I and the other forefathers (and foremothers ) founded this wonderful Vinceremo family under the mighty Grizzat that has grown stronger with each new addition to its ranks.

I can regale you with tales and tributes of all our members from days gone past and as time moves forward, but now is not the time for that. Catch me o'er a nice flagon of Hero's Brew at the tavern if you so choose to bend an ear!

I cannot say how proud I am of the achievements we have made since our original gathering nor how awed I am at the capabilities that many have brought into our kin. We have already progressed far further than we originally every thought possible and there is yet no end in site with much more to explore.

It is a strong and bold family that we maintain and as such the team members we have rarely leave our ranks once they have spent some time here.

If ever you have need of anything with regards to our guild, you can find us at http://www.vinceremo.com or on our forums at http://forums.vinceremo.com
