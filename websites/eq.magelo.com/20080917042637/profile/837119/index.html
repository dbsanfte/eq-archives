Biography
 
Born the second son to the king of a forgotten, gentle kingdom set high in the mountains to the wild north, Camireno set out upon the world upon reaching his coming of age. His explorations took him across the width and breadth of the land, gleaning for him experience and wisdom so that through the years he grew not only in physical prowess but also in knowledge of the Arcane.
Sworn to protect all from the scourges of the undead ravaging areas of the Realm, he pledged his life and sword-arm to Tunare that he should serve as a living example of what is righteous and strive to protect all that is beautiful in this land....
The journey has but begun.
