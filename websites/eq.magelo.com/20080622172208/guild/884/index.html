What Wayfarers of Veeshan Looks for in a Guildmember

If you want to apply to become a Wayfarer of Veeshan, there are a few things you need to know about us.

Honor: Our code of honor is the most important thing to us. We believe in honorable behavior at all times, and every Wayfarer, whether level 1 or level 75, is expected to consider how his actions will reflect on the guild.

Life Balance: We're looking for members who know how to keep an in-game/out-of-game balance so that relationships with spouses and loved ones never reach crisis levels and so that careers and educational efforts aren't ruined. We take our game time seriously, but we also believe that balance is vitally important for the long-term stability of all guild members.

We are a strong family guild who does small raids in-house and larger raids with Alliance Raiders. We are not restricted on levels, classes, or AAs. We do epics - 1.0, 1.5, 2.0. We have several strong tradeskillers among our members, and we love to help others.

We believe to enjoy the game, you have to value the journey, not the destination.
