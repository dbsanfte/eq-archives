Biography
 
Born to Amy and Jancen Deors, in the year 2135.

Merisha Was Orphaned at the age of 4 when her parents, A Warrior and a Cleric, were killed while Exploring the newly Descovered Plane of Time, by the 4 headed Dragon Quarm. She was Adopted by Jebar Deors, a Long standing friend to the Deors Family, although being a Vah Shir and classified as a "Goodie", he vowed to protect the family after being saved by Jancen while battleing against the goddess of Torment. He Failed. When Deep within the Plane of Time against Quarm, Protector of The namless, he was mortally Wounded.

Now at the young age of 16, and the top of her class in the Dark Arts and the Skills of Combat, she has risen to be the one threat that the gods are soon to see. She Already Hold's Innoruuk's Curse which no Shadow Knight has been able to do before, at the level of 45.

Although Being a Shadow Knight Merisha is not Evil, she is Chaotic Good, but does what she has to in Order to Survive. Having been Taught by Jebar Deors, has also contribuited to her Quick developement, as well as inheriting the Family Fortune which is Estimated in the 100 billion Plat mark.
