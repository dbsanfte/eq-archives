Description

We raid 6 days a week, Friday generally the free night. What we expect from applicants is pretty specific. We want a commitment to attend raids at least 5 times a week 7pm-12am, basically 25+ hours a week of raiding. Where that isn't realistically possible for everyone every week, it should be the normal.

For AA's, we like our applicants to have at least 1000 AA covering all the basics. We value game knowledge and play skill very highly, but we value personality above all else. It doesn't matter how good a player you are if your personality doesn't fit with ours.

If you feel you'd be a good fit for us, or if you have any questions, please send a tell to or in game email to our Recruit Officer Sydios for further recruitment questions.
