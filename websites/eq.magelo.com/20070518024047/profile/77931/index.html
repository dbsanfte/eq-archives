Biography
 
Brall started a long time ago with a drunken cleric friend helping him out (Libn) back on Cazic Thule. He quickly joined a group of absolute nuts that went by the name of Nightblade (guild leader Keideno). Soon that group of friends split up but many stuck together and went to the land of Ayonae Ro. Brall stayed with his old friends and joined up with the Sanguine Crusaders. This new group learned a few things of raiding and sacking large creatures. But there came a time when the group of SC decided to break apart and most of the friends went to Exhiled Alliance (guild leader Kalatarain). This was a long lasting time of foul language and bonding. Banding together and destroying zones took on a new meaning and opened up more of the meaning to the purpose of the EQ Lands. After many Many good times and visits from the Freeport Militia, this group too disbanded. Brall began to wonder about his desire to hold a guild tag.

Everytime he thought that he found his home the group split apart. Not only did guild names end but friends departed from the realm all together. Brall never quit, never left a guild, and never thought of leaving the realms. Soon he ran across a group of friends that followed the FTOGWCF code of FU. This gang brought light to the game once again! After a long period of solo times and seek and destroy missions with the FTOG, away from the guild life, something finally happened. Brall found what he was searching for in the term family.

Valiant Crusaders.

This guild tag has years of play behind it with great leadership and with Kadath as the el presidente. It carries honor, maturity and good times to a new level.

So here is Brall. Living it up and truly enjoying life. His times in the realms are lessoning but the good times still roll on and will continue too.

UPDATE: Sep 2006
Since Serpant expansion slammed me into no EQ land once again and I just don't have the money for a new computer I am forced into retirement.
