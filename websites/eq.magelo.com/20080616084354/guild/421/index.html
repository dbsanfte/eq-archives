Eternal Sovereign is an eastern standard time DKP raiding guild that moves along at a relaxed pace. We have chosen a long, slow and steady course for the guild, that does not contribute to player burnout although we require members who are online and playing to attend raids.

We are one of the oldest guilds from the Tholuxe Paells server. We do not have weekly quotas to meet on loot, kills or attendance. The idea is to have fun and kill what we can while maintaining a casual atmosphere.

Since our primary goal is endgame advancement, our DKP system is weighed to reward those who play more while allowing those who cannot to continue their membership and receive rewards.

On very rare occasions, we do not raid for lack of participation, however we move on and raid the next day.

We are currently raiding Depths of Darkhollow, Prophecy of Ro, with an occasional hit on TSS, and OoW targets.

If you would like to join us, you need to understand and accept the path we have chosen
