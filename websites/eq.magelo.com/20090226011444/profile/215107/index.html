Biography
 
Karlea worked hard in the Legion of Cabilis which was chosen for her by fate and reached the rank of a legionnaire. Since then she travelled the world of Norrath where she found a mate in the Shadowknight Khissh and many clutches of eggs were the result. She is also a formidable blacksmith, one of the best among her people.

When Khissh Shadowforge felt that he could serve his master no longer he left Cabilis to find a place to die. Upon their last meeting he gave Karlea his legendary blade Greenmist that remained a heirloom of her house ever after.

Recently she has been seen causing mischieve in Uqua and the realms of Discord. She's also strangely interested in odd trinkets from the Temple of Cazic Thule lately and was seen polishing her smithing and jewelery tools.
