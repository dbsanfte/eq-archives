Biography
 
Crystilla was formed in 2001 out of friendship for her husband and several real live friends. Her class was pre-determined (what else would she be with a mage, ranger and enchanter team).

She grew to love both the simple side of the game (chit chatting, roleplaying, exp, camping and tradeskills) to the fiercer side (raiding) and finds time to do all of the above to keep her sane.

She also came to recognize that while her class may have initially been pre-determined, there is no other class she would rather be. A cleric she is - a cleric she will always be.
