Biography
 
Born in a small hut in Halas, Chickennuggets grew quickly into a strong Barbarian. Having been chosen by the Tribunal to be granted the gift of being a Beastlord, he quickly grew attuned to his powers, and his best friend Precious. They have both become very powerful adventurers, and are always on the lookout for new ways of getting bloody together. They search for their adventures with their human sister Galaliely, a very powerful wizard.
