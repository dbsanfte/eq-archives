Biography
 
Created in Queynos
Guilded about end 2001 at lvl 16 in the Warrens by a dwarf named Panzir and immediately visited by guildleader of WE Marnival. After Wildar, Dadara Mesave and much soloing and many raids finally became elder of Warlord Elites.
Deguilded because of massive member's exodus to different guilds and EQII, no push to rebuild guild and not enough people online at the same time to build a group.
Gracefully accepted into WoT in February 2005.
Under the leadership of our Lady Wenamcea with the help of the best officer's team I met in Norrath Rariel became an accomplished and well geared mage.
Since we made it to Time, completed this and many other zones like DoN,CoA, DSK etc. In Theater of Blood the maestro and other mad mobs bowed to us. Looking forward to many more boss mobs with my guildies of Wind of Tranquillity.
