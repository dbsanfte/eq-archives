Description

The Travelers of Norrath are always looking for skilled players with good qualifications, high raid attendance, and a good reputation. New members should fit well for experience groups, for raids and raid attendance, and for game progression. We expect new members to conduct themselves in a manner that will reflect well on the guild and the guilds general reputation.

General requirements  Level 80 - all current expansions (SoF, TBS, and TSS), and >400AAs

Flags & Keys  Theatre of Blood key is required. You should be well through your Solteris tasks and your Meldraths Mansion and Crystallos keys.

Our raids days and times are Tue, Wed, Thurs, Fri at 8pm EST. Sundays 5pm EST. For more information, please see our guild web site at http://www.tonsaryrn.net

The Travelers of Norrath began over five years ago. Our first significant guild kill happened on October 4, 2002, when Aaryonar was beaten, and the north ToV dragons were opened to the guild.

Since then we have progressed through all the expansions. We currently are in Ashengate and Deathknell, and progressing into Solteris.
