Biography
 
<!-- BEGIN BIO -->
Onetree is a proud member of Valoran. He is also the Original Keeper of the FAQ at www.planeofmischief.com (such as it is), a Sage at Allakhazam's, a former member of the EQTC Database effort, and your friendly neighborhood Magelo Advisor. These days he is mainly an overworked senior software engineer and high-tech researcher in silicon valley, but still raids as often as he can.

Onetree was born on May 3rd, 1999. He is grade A, untwinked, undyed original stock with over 400 days played to his name.

Onetree moved from Karana to Maelin Starpyre server on March 6, 2003 along with <a href="http://www.valoran.net/" target=_new>Valoran</a>.
