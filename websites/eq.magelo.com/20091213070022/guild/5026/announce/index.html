Alcaeus, which is Greek for strength, is a dedicated and loyal group of friends, determined to succeed at all of our goals. We have been playing together for a few years now. The majority of our Core players have been playing Everquest since 1999 and know what it takes to balance fun and progression. We are a unique type of guild that goes at their own pace. We believe that team work will be the key to our success. We are looking for mature/skilled players with a desire to help others and be team players.
