Biography
 
Maedelyn, lover of life and fun, singer of songs and most of all explorer with her entire heart.

Growing up as a little wood elf in Kelethin she has always had this wanderlust, this drive to travel, see the world or maybe even beyond the world.

At the young age of 14 seasons she left her home and has been wandering ever since, singing wherever she goes, helping the occasional adventurer and most of all enjoying herself, enjoying life.
