Biography
 
As a young child, Magpye had a talent for getting herself into trouble, much to the chagrin of her widowed father. He had always expected her to become a rogue like himself - he even named her after the thieving bird - but when the druid guildmaster said she showed the potential to become a druid, he let her pursue her own calling. Magpye went on to have <a href=" http://www3.sympatico.ca/sonya.roberts/magpye.html">many adventures</a> throughout Norrath, Luclin, and the Planes, many in company with an erudite paladin named Iztariani, then later with a rogue named Snoww, the distant cousin of Magpye's long time friend Split Pea.
