Veil of Alaris is now live!
Nov 15, 2011

Veil of Alaris, the 18th expansion of Everquest is finally out today!

In addition to a level cap raise from 90 to 95, over 800 AAs have been added and you can now quest through 12 new zones, enjoy new UI features, guild tools, and see also the introduction of guild housing and guild trophies in Veil of Alaris.



We are preparing an update for Magelo Sync to make it compatible with Veil of Alaris and we will let you know as soon as you can synchronize your characters again!

Browse bigger bags in Profile and other changes!
Aug 23, 2011

We recently updated the website to fix few bugs and introduce the possibility to browse bags bigger than 20 slots in the profile. This is now available and you will find below a quick overview of all the changes made.

New features

  • Ability to browse bags bigger than 20 slots in the profile
  • The Heroic statistics are now available in the Ranking

Bugs fixed

  • Heroic stats in the signature is not showing "0" anymore
  • Food and drinks no longer appear incorrectly as charm and ear in modifiers list
  • All skills are now displaying correct cap
  • Edit equipment was showing a system error for a few users. It is now fixed.
  • 1H blunt skill is now back after a long vacation,...
Have a great week everyone!
