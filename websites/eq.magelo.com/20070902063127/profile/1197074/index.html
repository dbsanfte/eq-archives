They say that the wilds have been tamed, but a wild spirit can never be broken.

Fear that which you cannot tame, for when you are not looking I may pounce.

Tigrah has been my favorite toon for quite a while. I have always loved white tigers, and Tigrah is exactly that. On top of that he's a beastlord which I can identify with, and his pet is a tiger also.
