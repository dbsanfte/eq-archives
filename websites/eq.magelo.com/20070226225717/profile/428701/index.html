Biography
 
She had traveled from a dreamland to many outside active worlds, always finding new adventures and interests, as well as meeting many new people and making friends along the way. It was a wonderous time for her and she was very happy in her new life! But as time passed on, she noticed that slowly many Lords and Ladies in her realm, her friends and sisters and brothers, began to disappear. There was also bickering and discention among those left around her and it made her weary as she became more puzzled and worried and didn't know what to do. One morning, after a particularily bad night of disturbing dreams about dragons and trolls and being ported thru time, she woke to find almost everyone gone... with just a single note left for her telling her to come... to meet them in the new lands. She didn't know what to do or where to go and she felt lost and alone for the first time in a long time and she didn't understand. She needed to find everyone, she missed them so much!! So she went in search of info about this new land and after making numerous inquiries, she learned of this very special place that many had visited. She remembered hearing her friends talk about this new land, but it had seemed too mystical and scary when they first talked about it. Demons and dragons to be slayed, abundance of food and drink as well as many of the old ones once again re-united. Such things they had talked of and she had turned away from it. Now they had all left her worlds and they had warned her and she had not listened!! They had gone to this special far away place that she had no knowledge of and she knew they were never coming back. She also knew her only options were to start her journey, seek this wonderous land and find this place called Norrath!!
