Biography
 
In the year his mother Elvienc was pregnant with Neric she lost all but a few of her family to a terrible feud with another house. Slowly her relatives where being assassinated. His father, Jeck'tal was a city guard and had no alliances or powerful friends to help aid in the defense of his wife's family. Jeck'tal, was once a well respected fighter but had been injured badly in a fight with the Orcs many years ago. He was given an easy job as a second in command to the head guard of the 2nd Gate but slowly was being lowered down the chain of command. This social misfortune led people to not respect him at all. With no hope for the future of their soon to be newborn son, they left for the Outpost in the Overthere. There they hoped to raise their son and start a new life.

It was during his youth at living at the Outpost that Neric developed his hate for the Iksar and Sarnaks native to this land. It was well known that the Dark Elves hated the Iksar and Sarnaks as much as the Iksar and Sarnaks hated them, the Dark Elves had taken land that was theirs. Using the strong defenses of the Outpost the Dark Elves are able to maintain a strong presence there on Kunark. During adolescence Neric became a guard at the Outpost, wanting to follow in the steps of his father. He vowed to kill more Iksar and Sarnaks than anybody ever had. Doing this he hoped he could return to Neriak with his parents and restore respect and status to the Ikolis house. Things did not go as planed though. During a massive raid on the Outpost by Sarnaks one day his father was killed. A month later his mother was found dead in her sleep. It was believed to be the final and last assassination of his mother's house. With no where to go and nobody for support Neric left the Outpost hoping to find a teacher to train him in the dark arts of fighting so that he could inflect vengeance on the murders of his mother.

Neric is still looking for a teacher who will help him become a great fighter so that he may one day return to Neriak and destroy those who have destroyed his family. His hate burns strong and festers till the day it shall be unleashed.
