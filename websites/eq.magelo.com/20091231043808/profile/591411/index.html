Biography
 
Kraag Katrein is an apprentice in the Khati Sha, the Beast Lords guild of the Vah Shir nation.
Looking forward to being one of the most famous Feral Lord under the orders of the wise king Rajah, Kraag is working hard on his skills.
Loyal to the Khati Sha, to the city of Shar Vahl and to king Rajah, he shows no mercy when fighting the ennemy of the Vah Shir.
Kraag never shows any remorse when fighting for Shar Vahl. Nothing is more important to him than his duty of defender of Shar Vahl. If innocent people have to die, so be it! Shar Vahl is of absolute priority.
