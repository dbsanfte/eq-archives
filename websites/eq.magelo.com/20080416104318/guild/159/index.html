Description

Realm of Ages is one of the oldest most well known guilds on the server thanks to its members that have made it such a great guild. The Realm of Ages guild tag is a legacy - a badge of honor to our members, signifying our dedication, our reputation as skilled players and our consistent commitment to success. RoA is one of the oldest guilds on Fennin Ro that is still active, mainly due to the members who have shown dedication, loyalty, and teamwork.

Realm of Ages is also an allied guild with Team Dragon in an effort to promote a larger family community of goodwill. Being a part of Team Dragons is an option for all members of Realm of Ages so long as you meet their Requirements and Membership Rules.

Realm of Ages is one of the oldest of the Fennin Ro guilds, founded in 1999. Our Guild tag is our badge of honor, representing some of the finest and friendliest people on the server. We are affiliated with Team Dragon and Legends raid teams for those members seeking optional raiding. We are a causal raid guild raiding Thursday and Saturday nights, offering our membership steady progression in a fun, family style environment.

As a guild of our caliber, our guild is based on mutual respect, honor, dedication, friendship and teamwork. As a mature guild our members all have lives outside of EverQuest with families, college, jobs, etc. so we are very flexible in our members playtimes and strive to maintain the guild as a recreational pastime and not a job.

Honor and respect are our foundations, teamwork and dedication are our weapons for success.
