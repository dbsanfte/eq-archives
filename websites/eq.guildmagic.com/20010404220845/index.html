Guild Alliances go live!

Guild Alliances have gone live. Now you can show which guilds are your friends and which are your bitterest enemies. Guild administrators click guild alliances on your admin controls to start setting them up.

There have been a couple of small updates to the my user page that now allow guild admins to re-send or withdraw invitations to potential guild members.

Watch out later this week for the launch of awards. Guild administrators will be able to reward their guild members with awards that show up on the character's resumé. Guild admins will even be able to upload their own award graphics.

More GuildMagic improvements
Thanks to Nanlaen guild admins can now update their guild members levels and set them as mains or alternates. Click edit characters and then edit the character you want to update.

We're now recording when character entries are updated and displaying it on the character page. This should help you see how up-to-date a character's entry is. Dates will not appear on character entries until the next time they are updated.

We're also recording when users last logged in. This is simply so that we can automatically delete users that have no characters attached to them if they've not logged in for 3 months.
