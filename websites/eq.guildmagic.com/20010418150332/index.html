In Development page updated
Verant have updated their In Development page. They're working on a new free zone Stonebrunt Mountains which will be off the Warrens and will be for a similar level range to Lake of Ill Omen. Verant are also working on 3D engine and user interface enhancements including the ability to buy stacks of items. They're also looking at the high magic resistance of Velious mobs, Warrens loot rules, PvP rules, Class Balance changes, more Velious quests and a clever doohickey that will make NPCs hand items back to you that they don't want.
