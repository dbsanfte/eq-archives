Vault item scoring
In an effort to speed up our approval process for items submitted to our vault database I've added a scoring system. All new items submitted to the vault items database will now be given a score between 1 and 5 based on the quality of the submission. The idea is that those people who consistantly make good quality submissions will soon have their submissions auto-approved as soon as they're entered.
