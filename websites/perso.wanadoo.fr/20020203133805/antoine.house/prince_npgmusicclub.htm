Here is the list of the songs and Videos you can download from www.npgmusicclub.com. You'll have to register and become a member to access (yes, it's NOT free ;-). You have two "levels" of Membership : Normal and Premium (last one is more expensive but you have more songs, List below is for Premium Members).
Most songs are new and unreleased, some are old ones played Live and you'll also find some remixes. Then you have 1 hour "radio style" mixes where you hear rare Prince's/The Artist songs from "official" albums. Format of the songs is MP3 (high Quality : 190 to 256 Kb/s). Finally you have some videos (.mov files) from Lives or TV Videos.
