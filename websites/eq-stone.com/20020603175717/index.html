Two Paladins completed their epics this week. Congrats to Gereint and Tilayan on their Fiery Defenders!

  4.15.2002   Last Week's Events - Teakken
4 dead dragons, 1 dead necro lizard, and 2 epics completed.

Congratulations to Gido (warrior) and Tilania (wizard) on completing their epic weapon quests this weekend!

Monday, Severilous died coughing up a Green Dragon Scale for Gido. Lady Vox was slain and dropped 2 Books of First. Congrats Gereint and Tilayan on your epic pieces. And Talendor got ST-OwNED for the very first time!

Tuesday, Severilous showed up again and was nice enough to hand over a Green Dragon Scale for Kathwar.

Wednesday, Venril Sathir asked for yet another rematch, but finally gave up and gave his Gnarled Staff to Tilania for her epic and some Jaundiced Bone Greaves for Uber.

Friday night, our turtle watcher, Dreth, gave alarm. After a quick rally, Lodizal dropped the map piece for Bluudy, some Lodizal Shell Boots that no one wanted, 4 spells, and Dreth got the shell piece for his new Lodizal Shell Shield and a nice new Belt of the Great Turtle.

Saturday, Stone went to Plane of Hate for about 5 hours. I'm not sure all what happened, but I do know that Zeppo received a very nice Hauberk of Harmony for his chest. Grats halfling! I can't wait to see what it looks like on you.

Sunday, the Broken Golem appeared in Plane of Fear, so Stone went to fix him for Tilania's last epic piece.

A nice week overall. Thanks to all that attended and had fun. Grats to all that got some nice loot. This week, concentrate on getting XP and increasing your CoV faction.
