Guild Leadership

Guild Leader - Sssive
Responsible for the general direction and goals of the guild; maintaining a guild relationship with leaders of other guilds; settle disputes between Stone and other guilds; appointing council members to assist in the daily operations of the guild; and to have the final decision in any operation of the guild where the appropriate council/officers cannot reach a consensus.

Active Guild Officers - Budin, Hicky, Rahz, Teakken, Tilayan, Wyndaar, Zeulon
Work with the guildleader and fellow officers to perform the daily operations and decisions of the guild.

Inactive Guild Officers - Nubus, Orkk, Zeppotase, Yobba/Ryche, Quezil
Welcome to come back and resume their old duties. =)

War Council
Responsible for Making Shit Dead. Coordinates the researching, planning and distribution of information on guild raid events in line with the goals set by the Officers.

War Chief - Rahz
Responsible for the overall effectiveness and organization of the War Council.

Raid Leaders - Truehawk, Bhuhdiy, Latch
Lead raids, research targets, revise strats, schedule events

Raid Assistants - Thead
Assist raid leaders, handle "gimps", keep track of what's up/down, manage groups during raids, research targets, assist with strats, lead raids if no Raid Leaders are online.

Raid Observers - (nightly positions)
Random person from guild, class leaders as needed (namely clerics). Jobs include managing the class that is needed, and watching to see what's going on and how WC works to better understand the complexities of running a raid.

Raid Trainee
Watch whats going on, help where able, learn the ropes, not to call raids witout officers or other WC online.

Seniority Order - Officers -> TH -> Bhuh -> Latch -> Thead
Seniority Order is for calling raids only.

Loot Council / Guild Treasury
Manages Stone's DKP system (SKP). Manages Guild Monies, selling of Guild Loot and purchasing Guild Supplies (i.e. coffins, spell reagents, etc...)

Sssive, Hicky, Tilayan, Zeulon, Dreth, Fazzelan

Membership Council
Responsible for maintaining and performing Stone's membership process policies. First official point of contact for inquiries about joining Stone.

Budin, Wyndaar, Amanta, Baradinus, Eolair, Ndiani, Saphira

Dispute Resolution Council
Responsible for making sure members' complaints with eachother are heard and an attempt to settle the dispute has been made.

Hicky, Teakken

Website/Forums Administration
Maintain the integrity, security and structure of Stone's website and forums.

Admins - Rahz, Dreth, Teakken
