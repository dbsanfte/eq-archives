About Joining Stone

We are not in such a hurry to grow that we will sacrifice the bond of friendship between our members. Therefore, if you are interested in joining Stone, we ask that you:

· Make sure we are accepting you class. Current Class Requirements
· Meet, or be willing to meet, our Member ship Requirements.
· Read the following to help decide if our policies fit your playstyle:
· Membership Rules
· Loot Policies
· Raiding the Stone Way

If you have any questions after reading those, send a tell to an officer for clarification or ask on the boards.

 

Prospect Period, Raid Pass Holder

· Register with the first name of your main character and 'Apply to Stone' on the first page of the website. Apply Now will create a post for you in the Stone forums under Raid Pass.
· Update your Raid pass at least once every 30 days, failure to do so will close the Raid Pass.
· Get to know us by grouping with our members and joining us on raids. Send a tell to anyone in Stone and ask for an invite to our private raid channel daily.
· Continue to keep a presence on the message boards by getting involved in our discussions.

Keep in mind that we are a class-balanced guild. This means you will be expected to play your MAIN toon, not ALTS, when attending our raids. This is also expected if you are made a full member. Please do not apply with one toon, thinking you can begin playing another toon for purposes of getting them outfitted. We do expect that you will continue to advance your main toon in levels/AA.

At this stage you are known as a Prospect, getting to know us, while spending time learning the ways of Stone. This stage will last a minimum of one month and as long as it has to, based on your participation and play times.

If at any point in your journey towards Stone the Membership Council or Officers feel you will not fit in our guild, you will be told immediately.

 

Applicant Period, One you are Invited to Apply

After having done the above, and you have raided for some time and decided that you wish Stone to be your final guild, send a tell to a member of the Membership Council to make them aware that Stone is what your looking for. Be patient as often many have come to raid before you and have been waiting longer.

At this time you will receive an invitation to join our alternate guild, Rocks.

You must have a working Magelo profile. This is a simple tool used to record your items. Build yours at www.magelo.com. No one will be made an applicant until they have a working Magelo.

Do not apply with other guilds while applying to Stone. If we find out you have an application to another guild, your application will be denied.

This period will last as long as long as required.

 

Initiation Period

If you are accepted for a trial period, then we'll invite you to wear our Tag and join in our /GUILDCHAT so that we may get to know you even better. Read about the Initiate Program for more details.

If you make it through the trial period and pass the membership poll and officer vote, then you will be accepted as a full member of Stone.
