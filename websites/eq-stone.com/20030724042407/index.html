 Thursday, June 19th 2003   The Q   
Guild Firsts and Invite Madness.
The High Priest died to the forces of Stone. The Emperor was not pleased, to say the least. We sent our warmest regards and promised we would come visit him very soon, bearing him gifts of deep sleep and eternal peace.

In the Planes of Power, Grummus the Fat Bastard was lost in a brutal DPS test we were conducting. Sad as it is, these things do happen. May he rest in pieces.

Previous to these amazing events, we discovered a cure for Burrower Parasites. Seems a finely tuned raid force with Stone tags clears it right up. Who knew?

Let's welcome the newest trial initates to Stone as well. Vashtoril, Raaln, Truehawk, Monster and Udekar.
