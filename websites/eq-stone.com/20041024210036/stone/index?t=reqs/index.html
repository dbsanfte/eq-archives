Level
Your primary character (i.e. the one that is applying for membership) must have attained a minimum level of 65. Alternate characters of Initiates (those players that have been tagged on a trial basis) will not be allowed to be tagged until such time as the player is voted into the guild and the alternate toon meets all guild requirements.

Mobility
You need to be able to get to wherever we might choose to raid. This includes:
· Some means1 of being able to reach RUN3 speed (or better)
· Some means1 of being able to become Invisible
· Some means1 of being able to Gate or Teleport yourself
· Key to Sebilis
· 150+ skill in Swimming (the slowest swimmer gets eaten by sharks!)
· Good knowledge of the geography of Norrath (and Luclin)

Resistance Gear
We require that you have the ability, before spell buffs, to attain:
· 100+ Magic Resist and 100+ Cold Resist simultaneously
· 100+ Magic Resist and 100+ Fire Resist simultaneously
· 100+ Magic Resist and 100+ Disease Resist simultaneously
· 100+ Magic Resist and 100+ Poison Resist simultaneously
· Item with Insta-click buff ability (indoor and outdoor required)

Faction Alignments
Screw faction. Everything dies!

Maturity
We are a guild of adults and our language in /GUILDCHAT often gets into "R" rated status. We are all fine with that. If cursing or lewd conversations bother you, then this guild is not for you.
