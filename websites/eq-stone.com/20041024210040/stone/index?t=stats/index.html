This Graph: This graph shows the raid figures for the last 7 days displaying the total for each night.

Number Of Raids: Depending on the graph shown, it is either the number of raids per night or the average number of raids per night for the given time range. Notethat by raids, its not really a raid count but rather a counting of SKP entries thus usually pretty close to thenumber of raids. However, things that have more than one SKP entry would show up as more than one raid in thisnumber

Average Raid Size: Depending on the graph shown, it is either the average number of raids for all the raids that night, or the average number of raiders for all the raids in given time range.

Total Raiding: This is the number of different people that raided with stone and were awarded SKP for either the night or the average of the range of nights of the given time range. You may have an average raid number of 45, but the total raiding number may be 100. How? Some people will log on, go to a raid and log out, while others will do the same. The 45 number is the average size of raids for the time range, the 100 is the total number of members that logged in during that time range and attended one or more raids.
