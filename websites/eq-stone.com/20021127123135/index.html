  11.12.2002   Welcome New Members and Initiates! - Teakken
Congrats to the following for receiving full membership: Baradinus, Loon, Nukan, Rahz, and Vaelaru.

No one got voted OUT this round, so if you didn't get voted in, it is simply because the guildies feel they need more time to get to know you better. Just keep grouping with peeps and getting to know them and we'll vote on you again in December.

And welcome to the new trial crew: Jaxar, Huppinu, Koudeboule, Vortox, Eolair, and Savannah.

If you didn't get accepted for trial membership this month (and you applied), don't fret. It just sometimes takes awhile as we look to stay balanced class-wise, and also generally only accept about 6 new people a month so we don't get overloaded with new personalities. If we don't want you, we'll always tell you. I'm not a passive/agressive type of person. If you have any questions about your status with us, send a tell to Quezil or me or ask in your application thread. Hang out in our public channel "Stone:raid" and actively seek groups with members of Stone.
