Beliefs:
CLASH is a guild that believes 'Chaos' is the one true path. It is that belief that allows us to accept any type of adventurer that we feel can benefit CLASH.  We desire to have members for any situation that could confront us on Norrath.

Rank:
Rank within CLASH is non-existent, except for labeling initiates. All active members will have a say in events, with some members having particular functions within the guild which will add weight to their input depending on the situation. Initiates MAY become members shortly after adventuring with guild members online.

Multiple guild memberships:
We will not be accepting members of other guilds into CLASH. Joining other guilds while a member of CLASH is also not acceptable. If a member has the desire to leave the guild, it will be granted. However, the individual will not be accepted back at a later time. We currently have no plans to use spies to infiltrate other guilds.

Player killing:
So far it appears there will be a switch that allows for PKing, or for immunity from it. Most of us will have the switch set to 'no PKing'. We are more concerned with roleplaying in Norrath.  Of course, exceptions may apply here if needed in a strictly roleplaying aspect, not senseless slaughter.

Cheating:
Cheating in any manner is NOT roleplaying.

Real world outlook:
Most of the guild members here are very busy with education and employment. We take the available time we have very seriously, and do not wish to be burdened down with excessive rules. We wish to have a guild that feels authentic to the fantasy era (character names, roleplaying technique, etc...). To avoid any dissatisfaction for anyone, we are currently looking for mature roleplayers that have the same desires as we do. We are trying to make a large guild, so at least some of us will be online most of the time.

Gaining membership:
Applications will be screened for completeness and content. Initiate status will be granted upon satisfactory review of name, comments, and answered questions. Initiates may become Members after adventuring awhile with Members online. Currently, three founding Members must agree to accept the individual, without any adament objections.

Code of Clash! (in order of importance)
1. Loyalty to all Clash members regardless of race, class, alignment, or sex.
2. Loyalty to all DSoC members regardless of race, class, alignment, or sex.
     (NO exceptions - even to the extremes of Paladins and Shadow Knights)
3. To live adventurously. (we do not believe in steady employment)
4. Loyalty to Clash allies. (unless it interferes with 1 and/or 2)
5. To NOT be involved in player-killing. (exceptions may apply here if needed)
