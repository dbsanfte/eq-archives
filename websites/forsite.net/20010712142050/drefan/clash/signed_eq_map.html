Hail Drefan -

As you know, I went to San Diego to try to meet with the EQ designers last
Friday Night.  I only live about 100 miles away.  I had a great time talking
to John Smedley, Brad McQuaid and about 15 other members of the EQ team.  We
were there about 3 hours.  I talked up the Clash alot, and had some of them
sign my Norrath map.  I know John and Brad signed, along with the lead
programmer (I don't remember his name).  Also several artists and animators
and the customer service manager.  The E'ci game master was working so she
was not there.

They talked about Everquest 2 being a possibility, the add-on coming, and
the dynamic quests coming within 2 weeks.  They promised that they would
give the Clash a special one.  We were the only quild represented that I
could see, and I know there were no other geeks getting their maps signed!!!

I am attaching a copy of the map.  I apologize in advance for the quality.
I had to scan it in 4 pieces and hook it together in Photoshop, and then
squish it down.

The EQ team is very excited and proud of their accomplishment and they are
looking forward to constantly improving the product.  They do try to respond
to every email they get, and they are very concerned about the players and
their input.

I had a HUGE time and I am going to keep in touch with a couple of them via
email so I will be sure that the Clash will get first dibs when something
comes up!

Always a Clashman,

Rag
