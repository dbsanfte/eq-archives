Fitz has to work out his marital problems when his wife Judith abruptly leaves his life.

Meanwhile, a modern day "Bonnie and Clyde" (Susan Lynch (pictured below) and Andrew Tiernan) go on a killing spree, videotaping their victims before brutally murdering them. But when a police officer becomes the next victim, Fitz is brought in to help crack the case. In what becomes a deadly game of cat and mouse, the killers eventually turn their sights on Fitz.

 Aired originally in England in episodic format on the October 10th, 18th, and 25th of 1993.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 150 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
