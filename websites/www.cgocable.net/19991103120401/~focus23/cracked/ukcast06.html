Lorcan Cranitch (Jimmy Beck)
Detective and All-Around Jerk
Favourite Saying: "Bollocks."
There aren't a lot of credits out there to Lorcan Cranitch's name. At
least, if there are, they're difficult to track down when you live in
Canada and only have the internet as your resource guide. However,
we DO know that before his appearance as Jimmy Beck on Cracker, Cranitch
appeared in a film called "Chernobyl: The Final Warning" (1991) and has
since appeared in a film called "The Heart Surgeon" (1997). Outside of,
those three credits, it's hard to say what other projects he's been
involved with.

As a result of this seemingly total lack of information on this man, if
anyone knows of anything else he has done, e@mail us and let us know about
it. We'd love to tell everyone else about it as well.
