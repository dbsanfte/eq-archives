Parasites (now defunct) was a short term project of material compiled by J.Morrow of work done by himself and C.Virtanen. C.Virtanen ran a radio show in Guelph, Ontario for quite some time, called Tape Delay. Some of the show consisted of artists like Nurse With Wound, SPK, etc. but on the odd occasion, hours of air time would be devoted to periods of self-made noise, tape cut-up experiments sometimes involving tape players/deck/machines in the teens all playing at once, being crossfaded, delayed, and more. Some synth work was also explored, mainly by C.Virtanen, that material being used on the radio show as well. Later, material done solo by Virtanen and Morrow, as well as together was compiled into a few cassette projects meant to memorialize the moment. A lot of this work shows a definite shift in direction for J.Morrow and was a good predecessor to his starting Focus 23 a few years later on. The limited catalogue of work by Parasites is now out of print, but is presented below as a post mortem of sorts.

 Towers Open Fire (MUCA201-1990) (45')
 (two long live tracks from the Tape Delay radio show, very noisy stuff)

 Ritual/Exhaustion (MUCA211-1991) (45')
 (a long sampling of the best moments from one 4 hour episode of Tape Delay that was so
out of control, it's a wonder nobody called to complain or sue over blown stereo speakers)

 Mandalas (MUCA214-1991) (45')
 (a lot of synth work, some of it quite good as well as some experiments with metal sounds)
