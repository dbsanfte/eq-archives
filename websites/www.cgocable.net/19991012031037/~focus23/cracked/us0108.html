"An American Dream"
Season One (US), Episode 9
Story by Barry Pullman
Teleplay by James Sadwith and Barry Pullman
There is currently no cast information on this episode.

A famous porn star has been murdered, and the evidence seems to indicate that her estranged father is involved. Also, Fitz has a heart attack, which brings Judith back for a temporary reconciliation.

Aired originally in the U.S. and Canada on the ABC Network on December 11th of 1997 from 9 to 10 PM (EST).
