Recent News
(Updated February 2/99)

Good news for US/Canada fans of US series

It's a great month to get into Cracker. Not only has Canada decided to run the UK series in it's entire episodic format, but now A&E has decided to start running the US series. I was just on their web page and noticed that starting Feburary 22nd, they plan to run one Cracker episode a night for that entire week. One of these episodes appears to be "The Club", one of the now imfamous "canned" episodes that was never shown on ABC. Hopefully this means they will show more than the five they have allocated, but they don't appear to list anything past this month so we may have to wait and see. Get out your VCRs people, this one won't come around again anytime soon. You can read up on the details on A&E's Schedule for this month, there's a link to their page in the links area.

 Great news for Canadian viewers!

Showcase is showing the series in it's episode format every Sunday evening in Canada. As I write this, they've already shown the first three episodes, but it looks like they're going through the whole series from start to finish, possibly even including "White Ghost". What's even better about this run is that it's the first time in Canada that we've been able to see the original episodes uncut, unlike the A&E episodes, which have about 15 minutes per episode taken out per compiled movie. Don't ask me why A&E does it, but they do.
