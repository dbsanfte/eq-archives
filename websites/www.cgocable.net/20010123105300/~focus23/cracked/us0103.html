"Lemmings Will Fly"
Season One (US), Episode 4
Written by Jim Leonard Jr.
Directed by Stephen Cragg
There is currently no cast information on this episode.

When a teacher is suspected of killing a young male student, Fitz' mesmerizing interrogation leads to a startling conclusion. Meanwhile, Fitz considers a proposition from Tyler after discovering that Judith is dating their marriage counselor.

This episode is a condensed version of an episode originally shown in the UK series. The episode in question is "Someday A Lemming Will Fly" (Season One (UK), Episodes 6 and 7).

 Aired originally in the U.S. and Canada on the ABC Network on October 9th of 1997 from 9 to 10 PM (EST).
