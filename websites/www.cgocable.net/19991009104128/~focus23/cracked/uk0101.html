"The Mad Woman In The Attic"
Season One (UK), Episodes 1-2
Written by Jimmy McGovern
Directed by Michael Winterbottom
Ron Meadows as Roberts
Louise Downie as Jacqui Appleby
Kika Markham as Ann Appleby
John Grillo as Simon Appleby
Adrian Dunbar as Kelly
Nicholas Woodson as Hennessey

Eddie Fitzgerald (otherwise knows as "Fitz"), a psychologist whose personal life is crumbling around him under the weight of his gambling and drinking, learns that a student of his has been murdered, and that the young man believed to have committed the crime is claiming the have complete amnesia. In order to determine whether the suspect is an innocent man or a ruthless killer playing games, Fitz delves deeper into the suspect's psyche. As the mystery unravels, Fitz begins to connect personally with the case at hand.

In the process of dealing with the case, Fitz begins his stint with the local police as a consultant on similar mysterious cases.
Aired originally in England in episodic format on September 27th and October 4th of 1993.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 100 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
