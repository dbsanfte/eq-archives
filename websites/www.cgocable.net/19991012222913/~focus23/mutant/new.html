Mutant doesn't release a lot of new material these days. It's pretty well slowed to a crawl, but it's not closing down or anything. What would that solve? Anyway, what little there is coming out or most recently out is listed below.

MUCA253
Jim Jones - White Night (cassette - 78'+)
(A collection of some of the best public domain audio material out there on Jones, from
material cut-up by others to the entire "white night" recording itself, a great collection.)

MUCA254
Soundtracks - Crimes Of The Future (compact disc - 21'+)
(This is the soundtrack to David Cronenberg's "Crimes Of The Future", really early stuff
from Cronenberg with a bizarre and interesting soundtrack somewhere between early Kraftwerk and early
UK industrialists. Never before released, we've tried to get the best copy we could of this incredibly rare
film and release the soundtrack to it.)
