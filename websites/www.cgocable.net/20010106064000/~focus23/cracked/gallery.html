Thanks to the fine efforts of Noelle (and hopefully other contributors to the web
site, we have a couple of behind the scenes shots from the US series. Not only are
they just behind the scenes shots, they're from the last of the five canned episodes
in which Coltrane was brought on for a guest spot as the killer for the episode.
Such a fan of the UK series was Noelle that she got her picture taken with Coltrane
and Pastorelli, as well as getting a few extra snaps of the episode during the shoot.
Noelle worked on the series as a double for Carolyn McCormick (who played
Judith Fitzgerald). Our immense thanks go out to her for providing these priceless
photos! Noelle has been so nice to us in the past and she deserves a lot of kudos.
