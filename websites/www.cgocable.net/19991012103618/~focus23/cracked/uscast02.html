Angela Featherstone (Hannah Tyler)
Detective
Angela Featherstone, who was born in Hamilton, Ontario, and raised in
Nova Scotia, Canada, plays the feisty Detective Hannah Tyler.
Again, there is no connection between the fact that the author of
this web page is also from Hamilton, Ontario.
Honestly.

In 1993, Featherstone moved to Los Angeles to pursue an acting
career and within three months was offered a role in one of my favourite
films, "Army of Darkness" (directed by Sam Raimi).

Most recently, Featherstone appeared in the action film, "Con Air,"
starring Nicolas Cage. She has also completed production in Newline's
"Wedding Singer"; "Ill Town," starring Michael Rappaport and Lili Taylor; and
"Zero Effect," starring Bill Pullman and Ben Stiller.

Her television credits include guest-starring roles in "The Wright
Verdicts," "New York Undercover," "Northern Exposure"; and "Friends,"
as "Chloe The Xerox Girl." She has also appeared in the made-
for-television movies, "Family of Cops" and "Family of Cops II."

Featherstone currently resides in Los Angeles. In her spare time, she
enjoys reading autobiographies and "old Hollywood stuff."
