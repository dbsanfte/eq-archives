Ricky Tomlinson (DCI Wise)
Detective Chief Inspector and Fitz' Second Boss
Like Christopher Eccleston, Tomlinson was introduced to the world through
Cracker. Most people just can't forget him as the lovable DCI Wise who,
as angry as he usually was, always believed in Fitz far more than that of
DCI Bilborough.

Tomlinson started out in films like "Riff-Raff" and "Raining Stones"
before his work on Cracker, as a replacement to Christopher Eccleston.
After Cracker, Tomlinson worked with Amanda Plummer in "Butterfly Kiss", one
of the best (albeit depressing) films to come out of the UK. He made a brief
return to Cracker for the last episode, "White Ghost", before going back
to film, with his efforts in "Bob's Weekend".

Once again though, McGovern called, and Ricky Tomlinson went to work on
"Hillsbourough" with Jimmy McGovern authoring the project, and also
appearing alongside Christopher Eccleston, also of Cracker fame.

Since then, he has appeared in many films and continues to make a name for
himself in British cinema. Some of his recent film credits include
"Mojo", "Das Leben Ist Eine Baustelle", "Preaching To The Perverted",
and "The Fix".
