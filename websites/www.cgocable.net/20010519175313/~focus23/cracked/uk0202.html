"The Big Crunch"
Season Two (UK), Episodes 4-6
Written by Ted Whitehead
Directed by Julian Saunders
Jim Carter as Kenneth Trant
Samantha Morton as Joanne Barnes
James Fleet as Michael Trant
Maureer O'Brien as Virginia Trant
Cherith Mellor as Norma Trant
Darren Tighe as Dean Saunders

When a charismatic preacher has an affair with one of his teenage parishioners, it leads him to commit another sin.

 Aired originally in England in episodic format on November 1st, 8th, and 15th of 1994.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 150 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
