Josh Hartnett (Michael Fitzgerald)
Fitz' Son
Josh Hartnett, who plays Robert Pastorelli's son on the new, hard-
hitting drama series, was born in St. Paul, Minnesota.

After graduating from South High School in June of 1996, Hartnett attended
SUNY in Purchase, New York. In February, 1997, he traveled to Los Angeles
with his manager and within two months was offered his role in "Cracker."

In Minneapolis, Hartnett performed on many local stages, including
Children's Theater Company, Youth Performance Company and stepping
Stone Theater. He also did national commercials.

In addition to acting, Hartnett enjoys visual arts, basketball,
football and hockey. When he isn't living in Los Angeles for work,
Hartnett spends his time in St. Paul with his family.
