Geraldine Sommerville (DS Jane Penhaligon)
Detective
Apart from playing the role of Jane Penhaligon in the UK series
of Cracker, Sommerville has also made an appearnace in another notable
UK mystery series as Pauline Wetherby in an episode of "Poirot" ("Yellow
Iris").

She has also appeared in many TV and screen movies, such as "Close My
Eyes", "The Black Velvet Gown", "Romeo and Juliet" (as Juliet of course),
"A Business Affair", "Haunted", and most recently in "True Blue".
