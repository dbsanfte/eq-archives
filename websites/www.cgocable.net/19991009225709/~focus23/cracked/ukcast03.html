Barbara Flynn (Judith Fitzgerald)
On/Off Wife To Fitz
Apart her role in Cracker, Flynn has also played alongside Robbie Coltrane
in other movies and TV series. She appeared with him in Lindays
Anderson's "Britannia Hospital" and also made appearances in "Keep It
In The Family".

Her other TV credits include "Open All Hours", "The Beiderbecke
Affair", "The Barchester Chronicles", "Season's Greetings", "A Very
Peculiar Practice", "The Beiderbecke Tapes", "The Beiderbecke Connection",
as well as "Chandler and Co."

Apart from her appearance in "Britannia Hospital", she has only appeared
in two feature films to date. She played one of the hostages in "Quick Change"
and appeared most recently in "The Vanishing Man".
