Fitz casually dismisses the letters he has received from a female admirer but she's not ready to be dismissed, and murders a young man to get his attention. Jane rejects his offer of sexual "therapy".

This episode it is the last official UK episode of Cracker. It was also used as a springboard for the US series, when it was copied and released as the pilot episode in the US. It is considered to be the second best story ever written in the UK Cracker series, surpassed only by that of "To Be A Somebody" (Season Two, Episodes 1-2).

 Aired originally in England in episodic format on November 19th and 26th of 1995.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 100 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
