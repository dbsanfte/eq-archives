"Best Boys"
Season One (US), Episode 13
Written by Jim Leonard Jr.
Directed by John Hancock
There is currently no cast information on this episode.

This is the second in a series of "canned" episodes that were filmed, ready for air, but never shown due to the premature cancellation of the series by ABC.

This episode was based on a story originally shown in the UK series of Cracker, known also as "Best Boys" (Season Three (UK)).

 Never aired in the U.S. and Canada on the ABC Network and was canned instead, never to be seen.
