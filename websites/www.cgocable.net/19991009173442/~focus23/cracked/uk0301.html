"Brotherly Love"
Season Three (UK), Episodes 1-3
Written by Jimmy McGovern
Directed by Roy Battersby
Mark Lambert as David Harvey
Ruth Sheen as Jean MacIlvanney
Polly Hemingway as Denise Fletcher
Sharon Percy as Joyce Warkins

Gemma Peacock Wood as Jean's Daughter
Robert Cavanah as DC Alan Temple
Isobel Middleton as Catriona Bilborough
David Calder as Father Michael Harvey

Fitz has to deal with the death of his mother while also trying to figure out the mind of a man accused of brutally murdering a prostitute and the man's angry wife, who's the first to insist that her husband be freed when another prostitute is murdered in a similar manner.

 Aired originally in England in episodic format on October 15th, 22nd and 29th of 1995.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 150 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
