Season One (US), Episode 16

This final "canned" episode is very special indeed. Noelle (a cast double for Carolyn McCormick on the series) wrote us by e-mail to speak about the episode. She tells us that in this story, Robbie Coltrane makes a guest appearance! He plays a movie producer that kills a young girl, which is not revealed until the very end of the episode.

This episode was never aired and was the last in a series of "canned" episodes that were filmed, ready for air, but never shown due to the premature cancellation of the series by ABC.

 Never aired in the U.S. and Canada on the ABC Network and was canned instead, never to be seen.
