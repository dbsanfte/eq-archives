Hey folks, my first news item of the year and I feel the need to rant about what everyone else is ranting about...Ion Storm.

This gentleman seems to be on some form of power-trip, believing that he is some form of GoD (pardon the pun) and can do no wrong. In case you didn't notice from the hemmoraging of your employees Mr. Porter, you can't treat people like shit for very long before they give up on you. 

John Romero needs to get his ass in gear (as much as he may dislike it) and start interacting in the daily events at Ion Storm. Unfortunately there are times where you HAVE to take sides and piss of some people. Clearly the point of contention at Ion is Porter. So get together with everyone else and do SOMETHING about it. Fire him, demote him, smack him around a little, but stop acting like a whimpering puppy and get your company in shape. If Daikatana actually ever ships with Ion still in the shape it is I know it's going to be another Battlecruiser 2000 A.D.
