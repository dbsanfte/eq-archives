When a fourteen-year-old boy is found hanging from a tree, it seems like a simple suicide...but is it? Facing increasing resistance from the police, Fitz finds himself working alone to unravel a dangerous web of lies, intrigue, forbidden passion, and murder. As the police attempt to strong-arm an innocent schoolteacher into confessing, Fitz tries desperately to bring the real killer to justice. Meanwhile, his marriage continues to collapse, as does his relationship with Detective Chief Inspector Billborough.

 Aired originally in England in episodic format on the November 1st and 8th of 1993.
Aired later in the U.S. and Canada on the Arts and Entertainment Network as a 100 minute "Mystery Movie" special presentation.
