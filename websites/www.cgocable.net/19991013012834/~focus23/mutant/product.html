If you've read through the Information primer on Mutant, you'll probably understand that there isn't as much stuff produced through Mutant these days. The product catalogue is also in such a state of disarray that it would be way too hard to get it in order. At one point, we did actually find a working copy of the catalogue. The only problem is that it's inside an encrypted ZIP file that none of us can remember the password to. Maybe it's for the best -- let sleeping dadaist lie....or something like that. We will post the most recent additions to the catalog however, that is, items from the past couple years, which only includes Focus 23 and some recent additions.

MUCA247
Focus 23 - 50/50 (cassette - 76'24")
(Limited to 29 copies and now out of print, this was Focus 23's first album and came with
a photographic inset cover.)

MUCD248
Focus 23 - 50/50 (compact disc - 136'20")
(Limited to 2 copies and now out of print, this is the CD version of the first Focus 23 album
which was released much later with over an hour of added material reworked and remixed from old Parasite
live-to-air recordings. Comes with a photographic inset cover.)

MUCA249
Focus 23 - A Snake In The Glade (cassette - 73'09")
(This is Focus 23's second album. Long awaited and delayed, it eventually came out and
shows a more Lustmord-sounding Focus 23. It also comes with a photographic inset cover.

MUCD250
Focus 23 - A Snake In The Glade (compact disc - 73'09")
(This is the CD version of Focus 23's "A Snake In The Glade". This is absolutely no
difference between the tracks on the CD version and the cassette version. Again, it comes with a
photographic inset cover.)

Downloadable Sounds/Visuals

Due to a recent discussion with the internet service provider that allows us to make this web page available to you, we are being forced to remove our ftp service that allows downloadable MP3 files. This has nothing to do with the legaities of the MP3 file format. After all, we made the files and own the copyright on them. However, running an external ftp server for the handling of these files supposedly breaks a user agreement with the ISP and is therefore illegal (or something like that, we're not sure -- listen, it's just a big mess, you don't really want to know how stupid this whole situation is). If we can find a way of possibly offering these files through some other means (say a more compressed, streaming Real Audio format) then we may go that route, but for the present, we're not sure what to do. In the meantime, none of the sound/film icons below link to their respective files any longer. Again, we appologize for this intrusion to our service and your enjoyment of our product and hope it will somehow be rectified in the near future.


Focus 23 - Dead Cat, Live Cat, Maybe (8'10") (from "50/50")
(Drums slowed down and turned up, echoing metal, and heavily manipulated James Last.)


Focus 23 - Black Spot (5'22") (from "A Snake In The Glade")
(Recorded by M.McAlpine and J.Morrow in an abandoned warehouse in Guelph, it's
mixed over some other slowed down found sounds. The warehouse was later sealed up tight when it was
found out months later that companies had been illegally dumping huge amounts of toxic chemicals there.
Soon after, J.Morrow's right eyebrow turned white and is still partially white today. This track was also
featured on the Xcreteria sampler, Xcreteria Volume One.)


Focus 23 - Meditative Freefall (8'10") (from "A Snake In The Glade")
(Drums slowed down and turned up, echoing metal, and heavily manipulated James Last.)


Focus 23 - Eileen's Flags (7'12") (from "A Snake In The Glade")
(More of the Lustmord-ish side of Focus 23, probably our favourite.)


Focus 23 - Live (15'03") (from "A Snake In The Glade")
(The only live track from Focus 23, sort of. The title was meant to be multi-purpose.
It could mean "live" as in "i want to live", or "live" as in "this is a live track", or possibly even as a
command ("live damn you! for the love of god! live!!).)


Parasites - Live At Humungophone (7'05") (from Focus 23's "50/50" CD)
(Early live-to-air mixed madness from C.Virtanen and J.Morrow, later re-issued on the.)
CD version of the Focus 23 album, "50/50".)
