ShadowBound Boots Quest

1) Talk to Syllina in the Temple of Solusek Ro to get the quest.

2) Head to Mistmoore and retrieve a sceptre of tears. You are sometimes asked for a sceptre of sorrow. This can be found in Lower Guk.

3) Head to the Rathe Mountains and retrieve a Skeletal Toe from the Giant Skeletons.

4) Buy Shadow Silk from a tailor or make it yourself (Shadow Wolf Pelt, Silk Swatch, Scroll of Gather Shadows)

5) Head to West Commons and kill Shadowed Men to retrieve an Eye of Shadow.

6) Return all of the above items to Syllina and you will receive the ShadowBound Boots.
