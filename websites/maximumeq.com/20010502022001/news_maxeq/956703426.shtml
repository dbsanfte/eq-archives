Do you have a vulgar station name?
by Chris - 04/20/00 05:43 EST
If you do, better read this.

Before this issue gets out of hand I'd like to clear it up. We have no intention of policing station names to the level of character names, because as many people mentioned so few see them. I would however like to refer everyone to the Station community policies that make it clear that the transmission of vulgar content to station servers is against policy and will result in an account being deactivated.
As for the people who were banned as a result of a vulgar station name, we are working with them now to get them set up on accounts with acceptable names. A few things were done out of order which resulted in the accounts being deactivated prior to other arrangements being set up. We've handled that problem internally.

The reason why this is being enforced is that people do have the ability to post on this board as well as other Station operated boards with the station name that they create, hence we don't want people subjected to vulgar names.

-Gordon

BetaNeutral Faction
by Chris - 04/19/00 08:03 EST
Being KOS to the Priests of Discord definately isn't a good idea (they hit hard!)

BetaNeutral is the standard faction that is applied to NPCs that we want to remain available to everyone, regardless what they do. It is generally only applied to merchants that need to like (or at least not hate) everyone, the PoDs, etc. It generally isn't applied to anything that people tend to kill in a normal day on EQ.
Chances are that the BetaNeutral NPC you found in Befallen was set that way by mistake, which sometimes happens. We have added ways to the game to increase the betaneutral faction to handle these specific cases.

Multiple Procs
by Chris - 04/19/00 07:53 EST
To my knowledge, Vampiric Embrace is the only spell that works like a proc, is there another?

>>>Is it possible to make weapons with more than one spell effect procs?
 No. The way our item database works only allows us to assign one spell to an item. You can however use a weapon that procs in conjunction with a spell that makes you proc (Vampiric Embrace comes to mind). They just can't both go off in the same round.
