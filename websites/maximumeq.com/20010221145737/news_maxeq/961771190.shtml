Cursed weapons?
by Chris - 06/17/00 15:14 EST
In a thread dealing with the weapon weight issues for monks, Abashi mentioned something about cursed weapons. This should be interesting...

>>>We were told by you Abashi that the Bammbo Bo stick was a mistake and was being lowered in weight.
Actually I never said that. I said that some of the monk-only weapons in Kunark were too heavy, and I never meantioned what specific weapons. Anyway, the live team went through the weapons and lowered the weight on some of them. Some of them are remaining rather heavy, and are that way on purpose.

I'll ask the live team to look it over again, but I wouldn't expect a ton of dramatic changes. The fact that a particular character has the ability to use a particular weapon doesn't necessarily mean that they *should* use it, nor that it's the *best* thing that they could be using.

Wait until you see some of the cursed items that will begin turning up soon.

Paladins and piercing
by Chris - 06/17/00 15:10 EST
No, Verant isn't going to allow body piercing *grin*.

They also aren't going to allow paladin's to use piercing weapons either.

It sounds like it might be possible that some of our apprentice game designers might have been unaware that paladins couldn't use piercers. We probably won't give them that ability, but we should address the fact that they cannot use these weapons.
We're doing a weapons search to see what weapons out there are actually usable by Paladins so that we can correct any discrepancies.
