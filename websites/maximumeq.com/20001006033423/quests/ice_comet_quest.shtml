Ice Comet Quest Quest

1) Talk to Sulgar in Everfrost to get the quest.

2) Azia - found on the ocean floor in Erud's Crossing.

3) Beza - found in the underwater caves.

4) Dena - found in Dagnor's Cauldron near Kedge Keep.

5) Ena - found in Lower Guk in the room with the reanimated hand.

6) Fana - found in Oasis of Marr in the underwater tower.

7) Geza - found in Highhold Pass in the water near one of the waterfalls.

8) Heda - found in the Qeynos Aquaducts in the shark pit.

9) Izah - found in Neriak Commons a bar called The Blind Fish.

10) Jaka - Found in Unrest

11) Once you have all the rods go back to Sulgar and get the container. Combine the rods and you will receive staff of the wheel and star of eyes. NOTE: You can fail in the combination even if you have the right components.
