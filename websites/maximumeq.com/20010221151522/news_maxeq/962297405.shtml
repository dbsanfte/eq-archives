by Chris - 06/21/00 01:16 EST
The post speaks for itself really:

>>>I asked one of Verant's EQ level designers this question at E3 in LA and his response was that they just hadn't gotten around to this feature yet and that it would possibly create more spam. But I suspect another reason Verant hasn't added it. Spam by reporting dual wielding pets/meleers far out strips a 5 second cast time spell.
 There are many places in game where we could have provided numbers but elected not to. This is one of those places. To be honest, we didn't even want to report the damage to the casters, but did so based upon public request.
