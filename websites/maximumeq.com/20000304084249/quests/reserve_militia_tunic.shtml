Reserve Militia Tunic Quest

**Submitted by Sinahtra**

"Quest for Reserve Militia Tunic (and you bastards had better put my name
up!)


Reserve Militia Tunic AC9, +3 DEX, WT 5.0 RACE: ALL Class: BRD, CLR,
MNK, RNG, ROG, SK, SHM, WAR
(magic)(lore)(no drop)
Looks like red banded.


1. Talk to Guard Valorn at the tollbooth in West Commonlands. Complete
the 4 orc pick quest to get the next quest.


2. Take the bag Valorn gives you and fill it with 8 Dervish Cutthroat
rings. Combine and give back to Valorn to receive the reserve militia
token.


3. Take the token to Armorer Delin in the Freeport Militia house in West
Freeport to get the Reserve Militia Tunic.


There you go!"
