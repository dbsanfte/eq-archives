EQ store status
by Chris - 04/28/00 10:17 EST
John Smedley posted about the status of the store on the official message boards.

Hello All,
Many of you have been wondering why the EverQuest Store isn't up right now, so I'd like to take a moment to address this issue.

Obviously things didn't go smoothly in regards to the shipping to many people who bought Kunark. We're very sorry for the problems we have had.

We felt that until we have completely straightened out the problems with this process that it isn't fair to put it back up. We don't want to throw gasoline onto a fire.

We're taking the time to correct the back-end problems that caused this in the first place. Until we're satisfied that this process is completely smooth we won't be putting it back up.

As of now we don't have an ETA, but once we feel confident that we can deliver orders in a timely and efficent manner we will put it up. We will make sure to keep you all informed as to our progress towards this goal.

John Smedley
President and CEO
Verant Interactive, Inc

Kunark performance
by Chris - 04/28/00 09:58 EST
It has been said since Ruins of Kunark was announced that they would be pushing the EQ engine harder than before, and raising the system requirements. That usually means slower performance, but more eye candy.

As Brad mentioned in another thread, the swaying trees really have nothing to do with the framerates that people are getting. What is probably more responsible is the fact that the zones are very large and that we allow you to push your clipping plane out quite far.
The beautifully crafted landscapes of Kunark push the abilities of your graphics card and CPU further than the lands shipped with EQ proper. The outdoor zones are also densily populated with NPCs.

Bringing your clipping plane further in should help the framerate issues. We are also looking into seeing if we can make any further performance tweaks, but the issue is primarily caused by there being so much more to see on Kunark.

-Gordon

A level 60 hybrid with a 2HS skill of X is better with his sword than a level 40 warrior with the same skill value.
