Corpse rotting
by Chris - 05/17/00 12:00 EST
The latest bandwagon everyone on the Official forums seems to be getting on is corpse rotting. More specifically, why they shouldn't rot.

>>>Reason Why Corpses Should NOT Disappear.
>>>Though I have not personally ran into the problem of having a corpse disappear, I am sure I will. But is this realistic? No! Though the flesh and bone of a corpse may rt all it wants, the fact is: ARMOR DOES NOT ROT AS QUICKLY AS FLESH!So why not have the corpses turn into skeletons after a while? The armor would still be on it, and we can all live in peace.
Corpses decay in EQ due to the need to maintain a playable framerate for people in the game. As many people have mentioned in some other threads, the number of corpses in the vicinity directly impact the framerate that people experience. Each corpse must be drawn on the screen. Packing tons corpses in close proximity basically has the same effect on framerate as packing a ton of live characters in the same area.

-Gordon
