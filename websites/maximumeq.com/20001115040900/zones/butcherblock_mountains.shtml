Butcherblock Mountains

Levels 5-40 can find experience in this zone.

It is primarily a low level zone. Goblin camps, an undead chessboard, and drakes seem to be the biggest draw to the area.

High levels can kill Dwarven guards for great loot and exp.

Guard stations are well placed in the zone making it a relatively safe zone for low level players. That is, of course, if somebody isn't killing the guards.
