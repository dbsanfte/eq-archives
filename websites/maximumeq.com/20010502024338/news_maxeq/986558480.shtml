More Gems Debate
by John - 03/09/01 09:06 EST
It seems I'm not the only one boggled by the addition of Gems, or Medtris, or We-have-too-much-downtime-must-distract, to the game.

Thanks to all who sent me letters. All were of the same opinion as I with the exception of one reader who pointed to the mini-games in Final Fantasy.

Yes, FF has mini-games, but they are not in place to break the tedium of downtime.

The Verant 'Vision' of Everquest must have changed.
