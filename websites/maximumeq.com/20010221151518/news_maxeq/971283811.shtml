Online store back online
by Chris - 09/20/00 10:53 EST
Verant finally has the online store back online. This is how Gordon annouced it on the boards:

Hello Everyone!
We're pleased to announce that the Station Online Store is now up and available at http://www.station.sony.com/store/

Through this store you will be able to purchase:

o EverQuest: The Ruins of Kunark Upgrade (for existing EverQuest account holders)
o The Prima Official Ruins of Kunark Strategy Guide
o Lithographs of the Original EverQuest and Ruins of Kunark artwork.

We will be adding additional products to the Store as they become available.

Check it out!

Gordon Wrinn
Internet Relations Manager
Verant Interactive, Inc.
