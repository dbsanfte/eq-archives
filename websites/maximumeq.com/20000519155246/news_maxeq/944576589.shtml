Bards: How To Twist Guide
by John - 11/24/99 09:40 EST
A great guide for bards on how to twist multiple songs is now online. This is an excellent piece of work.

Twisting is the practice of weaving two or more songs at the same time by skilled bards, stacking refers to having two or more song/spell effects in place on a target. The ability is highly dependant on prevalent network conditions, however it is generally possible to maintain three songs at any given time and some exceptionally intuitive (more on this later) individuals have been known to twist four songs at once albeit briefly.
