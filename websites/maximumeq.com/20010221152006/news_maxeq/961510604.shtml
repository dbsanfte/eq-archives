GM Ressurections
by Chris - 06/13/00 12:59 EST
Abashi explains GM Rezzes a little:

>>>GM ressurections constrained by 49 cleric spell...
 No, they are not. They return 100% of the experience lost. They used to be constrained, not by the 49th cleric rez, but by the XP modifier for the zone. All rez's were constrained by this.

It was corrected a few months ago.

>>>2- The 49th level cleric spell has a 2 hour time limit on xp return.

All rez's have this two hour limit, not just the 49th cleric rez. We're currently looking into the possibility of having the GM Rez excluded from this time limit, but it's difficult, so I don't have an ETA.

>>>Also, when you make a petition and log out, that petition is cleared.

No, it's not. Petitions are held for several hours before they expire.

-Gordon


Saturday, June 10th, 2000

Gunthak Harpoon
by Chris - 06/10/00 12:36 EST
A mistake was made by Verant on the Gunthak Harpoon. If you have one, you had best read.

Please, don't go and flame Verant on their boards. They made a mistake and admitted it well in advance of fixing it.

Hello Everyone,
As many noticed in the last patch message, the Gunthak Harpoon (a 15/31 piercer) was made a rentable (meaning permanent) item. As it turns out, this was a big mistake on our part. Basically, one of the guys on the Team received an email about it being NORENT, figured that this was a bug, and fixed the item.

Even with the item's disadvantages, its stats make it something that should only have been available in the deepest parts of Veeshan's Peak, if at all.

In the next item patch, it will again be made NORENT, meaning that it will disappear like a summoned item the next time you log off. I do apologize to those who obtained the item in the meantime.

-Gordon
