Tailoring

So you want to be a tailor?  Great choice, now get started!

Tailoring is very straightforward.    A pelt plus a pattern makes a tailored item.  There are cases where you will need more components, but a pelt and a pattern get you started.

The size of the tailored item is based on the type of pelt that you use. Bear pelts make large armour.  Cat pelts make medium armour.  Wolf pelts make small armour.

Are you a tailor?  Let everyone know by sending us your character name and server.  We will post it here!
