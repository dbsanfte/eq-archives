Upcoming changes to EQ
by Chris - 07/18/00 00:52 EST
Gordon posted the following upcoming changes. These definately aren't the only changes going in tomorrow.

Just got some nice news from one of the programmers:
Using emotes on NPCs will work again in the near future (not this patch, but the next). You'll be able to /laugh at the dancing skeleton in Paineel or /point at the Guk Ghoul Lord instead of haling him. We've also increased the range of socials, so you'll be able to do that stuff when they are further away.

He also tells me that /who all "guildname" will work for /roleplayers as well. I know you all have been asking for that one for a while.

-Gordon
