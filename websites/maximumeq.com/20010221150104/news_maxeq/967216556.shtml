Dragon poll results
by Chris - 08/09/00 00:59 EST
Remember a while back, when the dragon poll was put up? Well, here are the results... finally.

After over 180K votes, "No Opinion" people had a plurality. Of those with an opinion, those who liked the new changes versus those who did not like them were evenly matched (within 1%). This indicated to us that while *something* needed to be done, neither the old way nor the new way was a good nor final solution for the matter.

We still hold this to be true today, and are considering various solutions.
