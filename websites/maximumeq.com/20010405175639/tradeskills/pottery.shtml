Tradeskills

Pottery

Pottery is a two part process.    Items must first be created in the Pottery Wheel by combining the items below with a Flask of Water.

The resulting product is then placed in the Kiln with the appropriate firing sheet.

Remember, Water *never* goes in the Kiln and firing sheets *never* go in the Pottery Wheel.

  • Cake Round: block of clay + round cake sketch + glass shard + high quality firing sheet
  • Ceramic Lining: small block of clay + ceramic lining sketch + quality firing sheet
  • Clay Jar: block of clay +  jar sketch + quality firing sheet
  • Cookie Cutters: small block of clay + cookie cutter template + high quality firing sheet
  • Large Bowl: two large blocks of clay + large bowl sketch + glass shard + high quality firing sheet
  • Lined Poison Vial: small block of clay + lined vial sketch + animal skin + high quality firing sheet
  • Medium Bowl: block of clay + medium bowl sketch + two high quality firing sheets
  • Mixing Bowl: two large blocks of clay + mixing bowl sketch + firing sheet
  • Muffin Tin: block of clay + muffin tin sketch + metal bit + high quality firing sheet
  • Pie Tin: two blocks of clay + pie tin sketch + high quality firing sheet 
  • Poison Vial: small block of clay + animal skin + poison vial sketch + high quality firing sheet 
  • Pot: large block of clay + pot sketch + metal bit + high quality firing sheet
  • Sealed Poison Vial: small block of clay + animal skin + sealed vial sketch + high quality firing sheet
  • Skewers: block of clay + skewers sketch + quality firing sheet
  • Small Bowl: small block of clay + small bowl sketch + high quality firing sheet
  • Smoker: large block of clay + smoker sketch + two high quality firing sheets
