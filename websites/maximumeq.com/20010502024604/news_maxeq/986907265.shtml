Wednesday, March 21st, 2001

New Server Named
by John - 03/21/01 09:44 EST
Better late than never.. I'm not sure how I missed posting this one.

Absor posted regarding the new split server. It will be named Zebuxuruk. His comments are below, along with a link to the discussion.

The new server will be named after the evil demi-god Zebuxuruk. Little is known about this god. The best way that I can describe him is that he is enshrouded in mystery.

The new server will be coming up on Wednesday the 21st. I'll let you know when I know what time on Wednesday this will be happening.

We have over 4,000 players registered to move. Unfortunately the move will also require that we take down the Rathe and E'ci for a while as well.

Alan
