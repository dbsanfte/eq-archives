Xegony: Serverwide Auction
by Geoff - 11/13/00 21:01 EST
Get your hands on the item of your dreams!


The return of the Xegony Auctions!
Yes, that's right Ladies and Gents, the Xegony Serverwide Weekly Auction is back and in full swing. We, the people of the Combine Empire, have decided to return this service to the community.

When: Friday, November 17th - Bidding begins at 8pm, and ends at Midnight CST (-0600 GMT)
Where: Eastern Commonlands
More: The auction will be held single item bidding style, platinum sales only. Simply report your item and your opening bid to the Itemmaster present, and the Auctionmaster will sell it off to the highest bidder. If you would like your item to be featured early in the auction (when everyone still has platinum burning holes in their pockets) - it's recommended that you preregister it by mailing your item name and minimum opening bid to Arykus DeLark - rmarks@swetland.net

Some of the items ALREADY listed for sale include: A Shiny Metallic Robe, a Golden Chitin Bracer, a Charred Guardian Shield - and more!

The Combine welcomes all!

Arykus DeLark
