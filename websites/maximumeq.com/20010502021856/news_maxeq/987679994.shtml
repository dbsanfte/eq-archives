2nd Chance to Move to Zeb
by John - 04/03/01 08:07 EST
The folks at Verant are offering up a second chance to move to Zeb.

Since there is still plenty of room on the new server, we will be opening a second move log for people to move from The Rathe and E'ci to Zebuxoruk. This will give those who missed the move another chance to get to the new server. We'll open the log at noon on Friday and close it at noon on Monday, so you will have the weekend to execute the /movelog command.

- The EverQuest Team
