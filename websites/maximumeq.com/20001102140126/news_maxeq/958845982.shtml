by Chris - 05/12/00 14:52 EST

Bascially, if you are thinking of going to an area where that guards are KOS to you, excersice extreme caution. The guards can now see through invis as well, so that won't help.

Evolving factions
by Chris - 05/11/00 14:34 EST
It would certainly make for an interesting game in faction would evolve, but then you would wonder what the purpose of doing faction work would be?

>>>Could it ever be the case that factions change? Couldn't it stand to reason, that at some point, the dark elves would form an alliance with the Iksar. Or, even better, how about a quest, which, when completed, allows faction alignments to be dynamically altered. Factions are fine, but for them to NEVER change is just not realistic.
 We have the ability to do this, but the door would swing both ways and people get very upset when they become KOS in an area that they used to be able to go through unmolested. As a result, we've left the global faction issues the same in most applications.

The closest thing we've done to changing something in this fashion was the update to Kithicor.

>>>Also, When a god such as Cazzic or Inorruuk is killed, shouldn't those characters that worship him suffer in some way-such as a innate decrease to magical resistance, or stats, etc. Shouldn't there be vast worldwide consequences when a god dies?
Possibly, but before we could do that we'd probably have to put up a "Plane of Love" or something along those lines to give people another option.

Also keep in mind that Innoruuk is the god of Hate. What better way for a follower of hate to prove himself than to slay his own god?

-Gordon
