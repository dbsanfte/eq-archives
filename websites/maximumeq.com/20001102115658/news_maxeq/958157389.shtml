Masterlooters and /autosplit
by Chris - 05/03/00 13:50 EST
I've never completely understood how /autosplit and /split figure out how to give what to whom.

>>>Every day, I get into groups with greedy master looters. When I master, I split after every battle and sometimes short myself just so the group gets an even split (i.e. if I loot 5 pp 4 gp in a group of 6 I /split 6). I just think it's fair.
 We did the loot items due to the number of complaints about the ninjalooting of rare and expensive items. Coin really doesn't fit this description, so the best suggestion that I can offer is that you only master-loot with people that you trust.

>>>And yes, /autosplit is terrible. If you have 6 people, and the MOB drops 5 plat, 5 gold, 5 silver, and 5 copper, guess what happens? The looter gets 5 plat, 5 gold, 5 silver, and 5 copper, the other 5 players get NADA (because 5 coins of each type can't be evenly distributed among 6 members).
This was actually done based upon numerous requests from people playing the game in beta. They got tired of ending up with tons of heavy coin due to the magical splitting of it. If you are masterlooting, autosplit can work very well providing the person doing the final /split is aware that coins are not automatically reduced to even the split. If you are not masterlooting, an extra coin here, one coin less there, isn't a big deal and evens out in the long run.

-Gordon

Level 51+ buffs and twinking
by Chris - 05/03/00 13:47 EST
Verant added a way to prevent people from buffing players below level 39 with level 51+ buffs. Gordon explains why:

One of the limiting factors on what spells could do before was based upon what it would do if used as a twinking mechanism. The level 49 spells put us right up against this ceiling. When we created the new buffs, we decided to put the cut-off. You can still use your level 49 series on people a ways below you in level.
-Gordon
