Crushbone

A low-level dungeon of Orcs.

Levels 5-22 can find experience and loot here. Trains are commonplace. Exceptional loot can be found including Shiny Brass Shield, Dragoon Dirk, and the components of the Screaming Mace quest.

This dungeon is a logical place to hunt for Wood Elves and High Elves as it is located very close to their home cities.
