Not possible to change religions
by Chris - 09/23/00 13:19 EST
Keeping on the religious note for the day, there will be no converting in EQ.

There are certain choices that are made by characters in the begining that cannot be changed. Race, class, and deity are among these per the original design. They are used to determine the faction that your character will be a part of, and that faction cannot be changed one your character has entered the world.
I suppose it could be looked at as either a techical or an RP reason. Essentially, the original design decision was one of RP, and due to that decision, changing of religion was not made technically feasible.
