Framerate improvements for EQ
by Chris - 06/29/00 12:57 EST
Looks like we will see EQ speed up a little (damn trees kill my framerate in Kunark because there are so many of them)

We're consistantly enhancing the performance of the EverQuest client from week to week to improve framerates by a frame or two here and there. We'll move these changes to live as we are happy with their effect upon performance. Right now, we're restricted from making the latest client-changes live due to the fact that we are working on a new sound engine for EverQuest. Hopefully we'll be able to promote the newest client within 2-3 weeks.

-Gordon
