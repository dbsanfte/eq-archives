Concordance of Research fix
by Chris - 05/23/00 02:40 EST
Looks like the concordance of research will finally work for all your robed ones out there :).

1) Apprentice Rune Azia requires Bixie Wings and Froklock Eyes. Well as anyone who is a Magi class level 21 or above can attested to, there is no drop of the Bixie Wings in the Game. GM's have confirmed that they don't believe the Bixie Wings are in the Game. Also with this Rune, no one seems to be able to fine Froglock Eyes, but can find Froglock Tadpole Eyes. What this effectively means is that if you use concordance, you have to use a 3rd Level Practice Rune to go from skill level 21 upto skill level 62. Using 3rd level will result in skill increases but a lot of failures, with no completed runes being produced until high 30's or low 40's

2) Training Rune Beza has a minor issue that according to the Research Volumes, you should be able to use any raw hide Cap, Mask, Gorget, Tunic, Shoulder Pads or Cloak. Well from my experience, using Cloaks failed 100% of the time for me, but all other items were fine.

3) Study Rune Azia has a similar issue to (1). The Giant Batwings and Giant Scarab Eyes dont appear to be dropped by the mobs which would logically drop these. This means you have to use the Expert Runes to get from Skill Level 95 through to Skill Level 130

4) Master Rune Beza (I say this based upon information gatered from forums and sites) Does not appear to combine to form the Beza Rune. This means using the Magi Rune to get through 2 levels of concordance (from Expert to Magi)

5) Magi Rune Beza has been described as being able to be created using FS Piercing or Blunt Weapons. But the Blunt weapons do not work to create this rune, but the piercing does.

This lists all the issues as I have been able to find/experience. GZ has/had been messaging with
Zehnman From Mythiran Tower in February in order for the Concordance Research to be looked at. Since then nothing has been heard.
The issues noted in the original post have been fixed on the Test Server. Most of it will go live on Monday as we patch zones, some of it requires an update to items, which will probably happen next week.
-Gordon

Clerics and plate armour
by Chris - 05/23/00 02:36 EST
A player asked why clerics can't wear all of the set of mithril, as mithril is plate, and clerics are a plate wearing class.

>>>Abashi are Clerics a Plate wearing class? If yes then why cant we wear any of the following armor items, none of them are "class specific" and in some case's non-plate wearing class's can wear.
 Being a plate-wearing class isn't supposed to mean that you can wear every piece of plate armor out there. There are still items that are restricted to the melee classes (and yes I'm including the bard in this description, though they're really in a class all their own). You'll notice that even though Shamans can wear chain, they aren't able to wear every piece of chain that rangers or rogues can wear.
