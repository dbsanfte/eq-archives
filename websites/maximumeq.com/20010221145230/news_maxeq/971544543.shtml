The perils of being agnostic
by Chris - 09/23/00 13:18 EST
I know that a lot of enchanters are agnostic so it will be intersting to see how this effects them as a large group.

>>>Can we get a quest to convert agnostics, please?
No. Just like race or class, deity selection has advantages and disadvantages associated with it. Players have to live with whatever choices they make in this area.

>>>Had the down-side been KNOWN when the characters were originated, an informed choice balancing the pros and cons of each could have been made.
Certain things are ment to be experienced after the choice is made. The first people playing the game or entering a new area do not know how their selections will affect what they can do. Not everything is expected to be known in the begining by the people making the decisions.
