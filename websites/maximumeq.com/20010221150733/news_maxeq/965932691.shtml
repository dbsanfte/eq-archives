Everquest playerbase
by Chris - 07/27/00 14:15 EST
Not too shabby at all :).

We're running over 270K active subscribers as of this morning. Roughly 80% of those play in any 48 hour period. When we ran the poll on the dragon issue, I believe it was done on a Wednesday, and we had over 180K responses in the 24 hours that it was up.

Fridays and Saturdays remain our biggest days for total number of people played.
