Aegis of Life Quest

You need extremely high faction with the Priests of Life to complete this quest.

1) You can skip this step, but the quest begins in South Karana, at the top of the white bridge tower. Hail the guard captain to find the beginning of the quest. He will send you to talk to Camland Serbold.

2) Hail Camland Serbold in the flying saucer in North Qeynos. You need extremely high faction with the Priests of Life before she will give you the quest.

3) Obtain the Pestilence Scythe from the live Lord Grimrot (you want the Lore/No drop version, not the tradeable version).

4) Obtain the Diseased Heart from the undead version of Lord Grimrot.

Both versions of Lord Grimrot spawn in the undead camp at the north end of South Karana.

5) Take the pestilence scythe, and the diseased heart and give them to Camland.

Note: There is currently a faction issue with this quest. It is possible for Camland to give you the quest, but not give you the reward. Give her the scythe first. If she asks you "What else do you have?", then give her the heart, and you will receive the Aegis of Life. This issue is fixed on the test server, and should be fixed any time on the live servers (it possibly was in the May 16 patch, but hasn't been verified).
