Iksar sounds
by Chris - 04/27/00 10:50 EST
As I still don't have my expansion CD, I haven't been able to take a look at the Iksar. Apparently they dont' make a sound when they get hit. Gordon had this to say:

>>>Is it against Verant policy to ship a finished product?
EQ is not a single player game where development stops once it ships. It is continually changing and evolving, long after it ships. Since the focus of our company is on massively multiplayer games, they will all follow this philosophy.

As for sounds, we didn't feel that the issue merited postponing release for the odd sound or two. We'll have them in in the next week or two.

-Gordon
