Cazic Thule

A very busy dungeon with exceptional loot. It is home to a tribe of lizardmen.

Levels 15-35 can hunt here. Trains are common, but since the removal of Rubicite armour from the game the dungeon is far less crowded.

Wizards can teleport directly into this dungeon.

Good races need to be careful of Bouncer Hurd who sometimes hangs out in the zone entrance between Feerott and Cazic Thule.
