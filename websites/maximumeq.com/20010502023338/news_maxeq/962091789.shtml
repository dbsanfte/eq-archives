Ask Milo
by Chris - 06/20/00 10:48 EST
Yet another edition of Ask Milo has been posted:

A brand new issue of Ask Milo was recently posted here at Everlore, your home for all things EverQuest. For those of you not aware Milo Cooper is the 3D character artist at Verant, and his work includes the models of EQ and it's expansion the Ruins of Kunark. Milo lets us constantly bug him with new questions every week, so if you have something you want to ask the man, check out the page for futher details. This week Milo covers the normal slew of questions from you guys, here's a snip from one of them.

Hiya Milo i just ask you one question about the graphics of eq. I played the other weekend at my friends house for my first time for a couple hours and i haven't played since then, but some times i couldnt tell the difference between the real world and the EQ world so my question is have you ever had this happen to you before and hats off to ya with the graphics man.
