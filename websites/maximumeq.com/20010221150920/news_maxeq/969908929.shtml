Naked corpses poofing
by Chris - 09/06/00 10:45 EST
Because my main is a cleric, I always try to summon my Hammer of Wrath (hit Vox for 1 with it! hehe) so my corpse doesn't poof. No rent items work, as they don't disappear when you log out if they are on a corpse. 1cp works well too.

>>>I realize this was put in to reduce corpse lag my making NPC corpses with nothing on them dissapear, or newbie corpses that are naked dissapear, but WHY do high level players' corpses dissapear in under a minute if they have nothing on them????? Countless people have lost countless experience because of this bug/design oversight.
>>>Will this be fixed?
 First, PC corpses disappear in three minutes when naked, not "under a minute". It's done to keep people from clogging up areas with corpses. Obviously this only catches those who do it unintentionally, but those who do it intentionally can be addressed via other means.

This isn't something we plan on changing going forward. The best solution I can offer you is to not die naked. Pick up something before running into a dangerous area to retrieve your corpse.

-Gordon
