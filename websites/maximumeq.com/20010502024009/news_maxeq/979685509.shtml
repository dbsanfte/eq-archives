EQ Email Virus Warning
by Geoff - 01/08/01 15:08 EST
There is an email scam going around right now invloving a fake EQ 2 movie trailer. Apparently, you receive a file called eq2demo.exe. This is actually a virus being sent around. The idiot doing this is using an @everlore.com address, and you know they would not be involved with anything like that. So, be warned. Update those virus scanners. The text of the message is printed below.

cool, you play everquest? everquest is such a cool game, its so addicting =) so what server is your char on and whats its name? Im on Fennin Ro and my char is named tersik. Send me a tell if your on that server. Did you hear that there gunna release an EQ2? I found the trailer for EQ2 on the net. If you wana check it out i sent it to you. whats your race and class and what lvl are you? ight well i got to go so ill talk to you later. tersik
