Symphonic Sabre
by Chris - 05/18/00 13:50 EST
Yet another weapon that wasn't working properly upon release. If you have one of these, you should read the rest of this news post.

This bard weapons has the same "Item is out of charges" problem that the tranquil staff had. I've /bug'd it, /petitioned, posted her numerous times, and even sent an email to Abashi. All with no acknowledgment whatsoever. Nice that the monk weapon got fixed, what do we have to do to have the Symphonic Sabre fixed too?

Its very frustrating knowing that you have legitimate bug concerns (which the bard class is full of btw) and not having any avenue whatsoever to have them looked at.

The guys just fixed this in the database. It will go live the next time that we patch items. People who already have this item will need to trade it out for a new one with the GMs once items go live.
