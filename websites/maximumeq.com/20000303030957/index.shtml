Quest for Quests!
We are running another contest. This time around, it's 'The Quest for Quests'. We need complete information, including a description of how to complete the quest from start to finish, what reward(s) you receive upon completion, and any faction adjustments that result. Each person that submits a new player quest for our online database will be eligible to win 3 months of free EverQuest, to be drawn at the end of February.  Send all submissions to update@maximumeq.com.

This resource will only work with the continued support of the community. We thank you for any information you can enter.
