Careful using charm now
by Chris - 09/26/00 13:02 EST
Charm just got a little harder to use. Nothing like your pet making a train.

>>>Charmed mobs now flee when low health...even if they are WINNING THE FIGHT.
This is just part of the AI fix that went in as part of the last patch. Previously, NPCs were far more 'brave' than they should have been, and the patch addressed this issue. There was no special code in to keep charmed pets from fleeing, they just never did due to bravery being artificially high. Summoned pets do not however flee, which is part of design.

That said, we're going to adjust how bravery is calculated due to the inclusion of last weeks patch. After the one this week they should not flee as much.

-Gordon
