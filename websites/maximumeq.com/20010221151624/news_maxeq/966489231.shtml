Shaman gate potions
by Chris - 08/03/00 15:26 EST
All I can say about the "safe place" being in the shadowmen camp is ouch!

gt;gt;gt;My beef is about the gate potions. Gate to Everfrost is at a safe point, gate to innothule swamp drops you in the shadowmen camp. It seems that for a level 49 barbarian warrior, they are low enough not to aggro. For a level 55+ iksar rogue, they are good enough to kill him!!
I don't quite understand. Are you saying that the shadowmen don't attack the barbarian but do attack the iksar?

gt;gt;gt;In addition to this problem, there's the super long casting time of 30 seconds. If Verant wants the potion to cast longer than a similar spell, why is there a Cazic potion that casts almost instantly?

I assume you are speaking of the lizard blood. That is an item that is adventured for, and as such, such items are typically better than those that can be crafted. The casting time on the alchemy gate spells is to ensure that they don't become an instant escape spell, but rather one used for travel.

Of course, it's possible that we should consider reducing the time, as I imagine that few would be willing to pay for creation fo the potion merely for travel. Does anyone know off the top of their head how much those potions cost to make (best case scenario)?

-Gordon
