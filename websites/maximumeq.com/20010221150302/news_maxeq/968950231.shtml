XP lose while duelling
by Chris - 08/25/00 11:17 EST
This is such a weird issue. Abashi is always changing his answer it seems (to me at least).

>>>Lose of exp. in a duel Abashi please read
>>>I think you should test a character who has very little exp. on the current level. It may be a bug or something. Maybe the exp. loss is so minute that you don't even notice unless you have
jsut gained a level. Thanks.
 Though it's probably not the most desirable situation, it's not a bug.

Experience loss from PvP combat (including duels) on non-PvP servers has the potential to cause experience loss if you die in certain ways. For instance, if you are killed by a DOT, are rooted in water or lava, killed by a pet, or bleed to death, you will lose experience.

These issues do not happen on PvP servers because we've turned off all environmental deaths on those servers due to the fact that PvP occurs there more regularly. On non-PvP servers, it's the exception to the rule.
