Befallen

A low-level dungeon that is full of undead.

Players in groups ranging from level 5 to level 20 will be able to gain experience in this dungeon.

Be wary of pitfalls, false floors and doors that lock behind you. An adventurer unfamiliar with the dungeon can easily get him or herself into trouble.

Clerics will think they have found heaven as their spells versus undead will be effective against virtually all of the monsters in this dungeon.
