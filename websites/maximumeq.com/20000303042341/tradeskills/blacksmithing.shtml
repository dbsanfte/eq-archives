How-To

Simply combine the components in a forge.  Check our Zones section for maps of the nearest city and where the forge(s) are located.

Banded items generally sell for approximately 1pp per AC on the item.  Check out our armour pricing section for the latest pricing.

**Banded items are listed in easiest to hardest to make order.   The Gorget requires the least skill to make, while the leggings require the most.**
