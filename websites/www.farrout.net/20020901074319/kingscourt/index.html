June 5, 2002
King's Court Website Has A New Home

For those of you looking for the King's Court website, you can find its new home here. Since I don't play EverQuest anymore, finding the motivation to keep updating this site got hard to do. A lot of the features of this site aren't available at the new site yet, but I think they are planning on implementing them.

If you were looking for the Epic Tracker, it will temporarily be down. I put too much work into it for it to be gone forever though, so I'll probably be looking to make it available to the public for you to keep track of your own character's progress. Stay tuned!
