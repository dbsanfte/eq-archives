April 3, 2001: tyrone

Few things to go over so first off: Don't send me any mail about Quake. I have nothing to do with Quake. I update for EverQuest. Talk to mIstabone or Doc about it. The news has been archived so the front page doesnt get cluttered.

Now for the weekend... Dain was up again Friday and was quickly put down after he went on a killing spree across the zone. Bad dwarf. Saturday and Sunday we spent quality time with each other in Temple of Veeshan. The nice thing about this zone is wurms don't split into 125869375 other wurms, yay. Unfortunetly no breast plates dropped but plenty of plate/chain legs did, be sure to check out Aegeis' radioactive plate greaves. We found Tolkorenar on Sunday and wanted to play a few games with him, he thought otherwise. He'll be keeled soon. Klandicar finally popped, so we ended the night with another Sleepers key. Decent weekend for us all.

Monday saw the fall of Cazic (again) with more melee loot (again). A brain or three would be nice.... Oh, the good thing about holding off updates until the last possible minute:

Master of the Guard died today in Sleepers. Awesome awesome fight, he dropped a nice new sword for our fearless leader.
