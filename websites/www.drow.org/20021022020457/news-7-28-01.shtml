July 30, 2001: tsigo

For the first time anywhere since the upgrade, Ventani, the fourth warder in Sleeper's Tomb, has been killed by Blood of the Spider. Time of Death: 10:50 PM EST, Monday, July 30, 2001. Congratulations to everyone involved.

I'd like to take a quick moment to thank Sssorn and Ketsui (edit: guild names removed to squelch the whining of the ignorant) for joining us to help us develop the strategy we used to kill Ventani.

[Edit: People seem to be stupid enough to think that a) Sssorn and Ketsui developed the entire strategy and/or b) We used a similar "exploit" that Conquest used to defeat Ventani. Neither is true. The entire guild contributed to the development and execution of the strategy, and at no point was an exploit used, as I've explained below. People are beginning to think that this is the strategy we're using to defeat Kerafyrm. Stupid or dense? You decide.]

Lots of people have asked if we're going to get banned now. Thankfully not. Several GMs sent us congratulations, and some said how proud they were that it happened on their server.

What happened after Kerafyrm was released was complete chaos. The first place we're sure he was spotted was in Skyshrine, where he subsequently went around killing everyone in the zone. After that, rumors poured in about where he was, and honestly we can't be sure where he went. I've been told that he went everywhere from Great Divide (I have no idea why), to Wakening Lands, to Western Wastes, and lots of other zones. I'm sure I speak for my guild when I say it was easily the most entertaining time we've ever had playing the game.

Thank you all for the praise we've been receiving, it means a lot to us to hear that kind of thing from people from all over the game.

Screenshots? Yeah, we've got screenshots - over 40 of them. Thanks also to Vespid for mirroring the screenshots, so check the mirror if drow.org's loading slowly.

We then moved towards Lendiniara the Keeper. On the first pull and first attempt of the night, we killed him/her/it for some pretty nice loot, along with another Sleeper's talisman. Gromm received Gloves of Stability, Ozzyd got a Mask of Superiority, Elfian got a ranger-only ring whose name I won't even try to spell, and Trivilus got his Sleeper's key. Rathe-first #2 of this week.
