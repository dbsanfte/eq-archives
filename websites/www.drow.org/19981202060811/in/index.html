OUR MISSION
The mission of those known as 'The Drow' is one of simple world domination.

With the advent of online gaming, came groups who were dedicated to playing this game or that. Quake, Ultima Online and others, spawned terms like 'clan' or 'guild' to describe their members.

Organization Drow takes this to it's next logical step-- domination of all online games by a single organized group.

Do you have what it takes to be Drow?

WHAT IS A DROW?
Well, in the traditional sense, a Drow is a mean, evil little elf. We just borrowed the theme of these mean, little, evil, sons-o-bitches' elves running around hacking people up.

If you are not interested in online gaming and only want to read about the traditional Drow elves, then visit the history section.
