November 21, 2000: Dr. Delete, Ph.D.

Well Folks, I hate to bring you bad news before the holidays but
unfortunately, this is the case. And it's real bad news.

For going on 5 years now, CLAN DROW has provided some of the finest
servers to the Quake community. An ISP called OneNet communications
has been kind enough to let us host our server boxes in their data
centers during this time.

Not too long ago, OneNet was purchased by MDM communications.
The new company is tightening the belt and as a result, OneNet can no
longer sponsor connectivity for the DROW Servers. :-(

Unfortunately they have given us a deadline of next week to remove our
servers. So, sadly I have to report that our servers will be unavailable
until we can find a new home for them. Currently, there is no ETA.

One thing is for certain, DROW will not settle for hosting on substandard
connectivity. HOWEVER, wherever the servers end up, it will be a different
IP address. This means that if you currently have a great ping to our
servers, you may not in the future. We won't know for sure until we
find a new home for them.

How can you help? I'm not sure that you can. But if you'd like to send
email asking OneNet to try and find a way to keep them alive, knock
yourself out. Help@one.net is the only address I can give out
publicly for them. But, those of you who are more resourceful might
surf around on the website links I mentioned earlier in search of more
important addresses to add to your destinations :)
