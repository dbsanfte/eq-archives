August 28, 2001, Submitted by Tirmelon Whisperwind


Famed bardess Jaka regales Steelleg with her famous work, "Steelleg's Adventure Amidst the Giant Pygmy Midgets of Rivervale." 

No matter what your age or background, you know him. You may not know how you know him, but it's guaranteed a vivid image comes to mind when you hear the name Steelleg Tinkerson. His retirement may have ended his days as a household name, but his work lives on thanks to the ballads of Jaka and other bards that are not-quite-so-great-as-Jaka.  But when one thinks of Steelleg today, one cannot help but wonder "Where is he now?"  To answer that question, BSN has contracted with me, Tirmelon Whisperwind, to investigate and bring to light the recent escapades of that Proper Sized warrior also known as Steelleg or That Little Bastard.

Little is known of the period immediately following Steelleg's retirement from adventuring.  It has long been rumored that the diminutive yet prickly fellow retreated to Freeport and reestablished connections with one Gnarl Bearenson at a House of Ill Repute in one of the seedier sections of town.  According to the rumor Steelleg's offer to provide "protection" in exchange for "freebies" was originally accepted by the owner of the establishment, but the deal fell through when the "Ladies" of the house unanimously declared that they couldn't "work" with such a "small man".  

After months of exhaustive research at the aforementioned House of Ill Repute - make that VERY exhaustive research - this reporter has found that rumor to be untrue.  The fact of the matter is that Steelleg did indeed visit the house on several occasions, but, as it turns out, his motive in doing so was not to find work or even pleasure, but rather an attempt to reestablish connections with his old friend and one of the house's more successful ahh, employees, Jaka.  Apparently Steelleg was not satisfied with the role he played in Jaka's magnum opus "Jaka Liberates the City of Mist with Only Inconsequential Assistance From Her Friends."  

Steelleg then decided that he would try his luck with his old flame Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro.  Steelleg invited Vira to the renowned Gemchopper Ball, a raucous affair known primarily for copious consumption of alcoholic beverages and rowdy cricket competitions.  Steelleg bowled 26 overs unchanged and claimed the important wicket of Manik Compolten as the Ak'Anon Expeditionary Force downed the Home Guards 4-1.  After the test, a thoroughly plastered Steelleg was heard to exclaim that Manik Compolten was a "poosae" and that "l33t d3wds r0xx0r3d h15 n3wb a55" after which Steelleg was immediately discharged from the Gemchoppers.  Embarrassed by his antics, Vira left Steelleg and returned to the Temple of Solusek Ro.


Steelleg and Vira pose for a picture at the Gemchopper Ball.

Distraught and heartbroken Steelleg journeyed once again to the city of Freeport and took up a new position: curled up in a fetal ball and covered in his own vomit and excrement.
