History of Renown

Renown has traversed the confines of worlds.  We are a group of friends tied to one another beyond the limits of sex, origin, or creed.  We are an eclectic family with one main goal in mind  To enjoy Norrath to its fullest with others we care for.  We do not attest to good or evil, only to balance.  If you are one that would welcome the embrace of Renown please feel free to read on.

Joining Renown

   Step One: Meet, speak to, and/or spend time with members of Renown     

   Step Two: Have an Advisor sponsor your membership

   Step Three: Advisor recommendations are put before the Tribunal

   Step Four: After recommendations are reviewed, sit before the Tribunal or Advisor Council for judgment
