Many Worlds, One Family, One Quest

Rules and Suggestions 

Show respect to other game players.

Killstealing will not be tolerated.

Promoting our role-playing image is encouraged.  Please stop and role-play with any fellow role-players if you enjoy doing so.

Help out those that are in need of aid whenever possible.  We are here to maintain balance, please help players be they good or evil.

Flaming will not be tolerated in any form.  Please remember we are adults; set a good example for others.

All equipment or funds are the sole property of a player.  In no way will the guild interfere with how that player chooses to use their personal property.

Members should stay in character as much as possible.

PvP Rules

Grief play in general goes against the spirit of everything that Renown stands for. Renown members who engage in grief play will be held in judgment by the Guild and can be expelled. Specifically, this includes:

Corpse camping. At no time will a Renown member be allowed to stand by the corpse of a slain opponent waiting for a chance to kill the defenseless foe. In games like DAoC, where the character respawns with all equipment, there is no penalty resulting from remaining in the area of the opponent’s death.

Bind point camping. At no time will a Renown member be allowed to remain at a foe’s bind point, killing him/her as they respawn.

Training monsters on PvP foes. There is no honor gained if you cannot defeat the opponent yourself.

Zone camping. At no time will a Renown member be allowed to wait at zone line points for the purpose of attacking foes as they enter the zone.

Other instances of grief play will be reviewed as they come up. Renown believes in the spirit of fun in games, and this extends to those you play the game with.

Dueling Rules

If you choose to engage in this activity it is between you and the character you are dueling to iron out all details.  In no way will the guild become involved in a personal dispute unless the guild member’s behavior becomes unacceptable.  If a guild member behaves poorly that member may be subject to sit before the tribunal for judgment.

Guild War Rules

Guild wars are not something Renown has plans to involve itself in.  Renown subscribes neither to evil nor to good.  We strive to maintain the balance of power within all worlds.  If at a future time a guild war is suggested, a vote of the members will be taken and brought to the Tribunal.  The decision to engage or not will be the Tribunal’s, but the vote of the guild members will be highly considered.
