Who are the Advisors?

A:  

Advisors are members of Renown who have been in the guild for over a year. They have the ability to invite new members and are the voters in any situation involving extreme discipline. These are the only real functions of advisors.  In all other matters, every member of Renown is an equal.  Currently the advisors are:  Kyna, Alatiera,  Gugur,  Nelilia, Daegan, Averdi, Kitri and Tirmelon.

Q:

Where does the phrase “three sheets to the wind” come from?

A:  

It comes from the nautical world. An improperly tacked sheet is referred to as “in the wind”, and when all three sails are improperly tacked, the ship swaggers and sways, like a drunk man.
