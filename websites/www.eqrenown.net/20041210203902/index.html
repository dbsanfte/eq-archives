Renown began its journey in 1999, in the world of Norrath, with little more than 3 friends bound by the same spirit of discovery, pride, and companionship. As time went on, these 3 friends found more individuals like them, and gradually the family that arose spread beyond Norrath, to the lands of Derreth, flung themselves forward in time to battle as a Tribe and explore the lands of Rubi-Ka, and of Midgard, Albion, Hibernia, and now to Istaria and City of Heroes.

Renown has traversed the confines of worlds. We are a group of friends tied to one another beyond the limits of sex, origin, or creed. We are an eclectic family with one main goal in mind… To enjoy online role-playing games to their fullest with those that we care for. If you are one that would welcome the embrace of Renown please feel free to read on. Welcome home.

 

It's the Story of Us All, the Story of Renown

“Of the tiniest sparks are the brightest stars born.”

It all began with Three.

Three barbarian lasses, born in snow, baptized in fire, forged like steel. Freca, the brawling, lusty warrior queen; Tingurani, the quiet dangerseeker, thought by some to be half-mad; Elowyn, the Hammer of Justice, fierce and proud. Together the three battled in the snows of Everfrost, defeating the snow orcs in their lairs, driving the ice goblins from their holes, routing the gnolls from their dens. There, in the swirling snow, the three barbarian lasses met new faces and forged bonds with new comrades. Calaern the warrior bold, Rashir the shaman, wise and half-mad himself, Akivasha the scout, wild and unpredictable (and a passable cook), Sanalyni the mystic, cold as the frozen plains of Everfrost. Together this band of barbarians fought, from frozen land to rolling plain, mountain pass to darkest den, and always, shoulder to shoulder, stood the Three.

Time passed, and the three lasses passed from memory. Their spirits, though, settled in the hearts and souls of three wood elf maidens. Ting Goldenleaf there was, the nature priestess, capricious as the spring breeze, fierce as the summer storm, cold as the winter gale, and yet beneath it all, gentle as the falling autumn leaf. Beside her walked Jaka, the dancer, free and smiling, sad of heart, light of tongue. Everpresent with them was Kyna, the flame-haired, archer and ranger, beguiling to the eye and snarer of prey and hearts. As the barbarian lasses before, the Ladies Three also began to gather others of like spirit.

They joined a group of companions led by Chrysania the bardess, advised by her Talons, Belrich and Tandor. These Brethren of the Mystic Dawn welcomed the Ladies, and together they ventured far into the world, battling the minions of Mayong Mistmoore, reavers living and dead in the Karanas, the savage orcs of the searing desert. More companions well-loved fought with the Three; Igraine the rogue, silver-tongued and steel-bladed; Jeppo the Devout, Halfling priest of Bristlebane; Deral the ranger, mute of tongue but fierce of pride. Together the fame of the Brethren they spread, and the Ladies Three became the Ladies of Renown, storied in song, inseparable and unconquerable.

As the Ladies explored new worlds, they left behind their Brethren companions, seeking new lands and new adventures. In the depths of the Runnyeye Citadel the Ladies would meet three others that would change the lives and destinies of them all: Calendria the Enchantress, dark elven by birth but hero by deed; Steeleg Tinkerson, pride of the Ak’anon Guard, calm stalwart, and cautious; and Glymmarach Gizzervizinius, magician, summoner of fire and bearer of lemonade, light of heart and kind of spirit. The six, each mighty as individuals, became unstoppable as one unit.

This group joined Seraph, a loose but familial band led by the priestess Pegier. There the six met with others of like heart and mind: Daegan the ranger, resolute tracker and implacable foe; Averdi the paladin, holy, kind, yet fell in battle; Nereli the druidess, sweet and joyous like spring long sought; Kitri the nature priestess, beloved of Averdi, warm of soul. Seraph, like the winter rose, faded before its time, beautiful and lost, much mourned.

Yet from its ashes arose a phoenix.

For it was that the Ladies Three, gathering those together whom they loved, rose the banner long-promised and finally delivered: the banner of Renown. Under this banner would walk the greatest assemblage of heroes and vilest cast of villains. Over time, the Ladies Three, the Ladies of Renown, would retire, leaving the sight of the world, but in their place stood new members to carry the banner: Anyk the cruel, Shadowknight, crazed with power and the touch of Innoruuk; Nelilia Agramon the necromancer, sharp of tongue and dark of heart; Tirmelon, priest of Erollisi Marr, husband of Kyna, defender of friends and faith; Gugur the Ogre, ugly of visage but steadfast to allies; Alatiera the Beguiler, haughty and proud as only a High Elf could be. The banner of Renown was carried to far continents, frozen and forsaken; the banner was borne into the skies and moons themselves, unfurled in the deepest and darkest dungeons of Norrath; even the planes of the Gods Themselves saw the banner rise, proudly arrayed by her stalwart members. New warriors came and fought beneath it: Boombad the ogre warrior, relentless and fearless; Benedicto the Erudite priest, courageous and unflappable; Igraine, their Brethren of old, reunited again. These names and more grace the halls of Renown, their exploits told and remembered, their scars and trophies proudly displayed.

And still, somewhere in a Norrath wracked by war and sundered by cataclysm, a fire licks the night sky in a small camp under the stars. Seated there, needing no words to converse, but speaking volumes by smile, by stare, and by shrug, the Ladies Three sit and wait, the banner of Renown much-stitched near at hand, ready to renew the call for heroes, for warriors, for those of such dauntless heart that the world in all its peril and horror will not break them but will only forge them into a force that will not bend nor break but only triumph, no matter the price, no matter the cost, no matter the challenge. Together they will fight, united under a banner that time will not fray nor assault tear asunder.

The Banner of Renown.
