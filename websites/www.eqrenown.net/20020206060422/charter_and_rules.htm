Rules and Suggestions 

  • Show respect to other game players.

  • Killstealing will not be tolerated.

  • Promoting our role-playing image is encouraged.  Please stop and role-play with any fellow role-players if you enjoy doing so.

  • Help out those that are in need of aid whenever possible.  We are here to maintain balance, please help players be they good or evil.

  • Flaming will not be tolerated in any form.  Please remember we are adults; set a good example for others.

  • All equipment or funds are the sole property of a player.  In no way will the guild interfere with how that player chooses to use their personal property.

  • Members should stay in character as much as possible.

PvP Rules

Grief play in general goes against the spirit of everything that Renown stands for.  Renown members who engage in grief play will be held in judgment by the Guild and can be expelled.  This includes training monsters or realm guards onto PvP foes.  There is no honor gained if you cannot defeat the opponent yourself.  

Other instances of grief play will be reviewed as they come up. Renown believes in the spirit of fun in games, and this extends to those you play the game with.

History of Renown

Renown began its journey in 1999, in the world of Norrath, with little more than 3 friends bound by the same spirit of discovery, pride, and companionship. As time went on, these 3 friends found more individuals like them, and gradually the family that arose spread beyond Norrath, to the lands of Derreth, flung themselves forward in time to battle as a Tribe and explore the lands of Rubi-Ka, and have set their sights on a new land as well: one that contains the warring peoples of Midgard, Albion, and Hibernia.  

Renown has traversed the confines of worlds. We are a group of friends tied to one another beyond the limits of sex, origin, or creed. We are an eclectic family with one main goal in mind… To enjoy online role-playing games to their fullest with those that we care for. We do not adhere to good or evil, only to balance. If you are one that would welcome the embrace of Renown please feel free to read on. Welcome home.
