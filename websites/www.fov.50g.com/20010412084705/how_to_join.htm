How to Join

Joining the Fellowship of Valor is not a decision to be taken lightly. We are increasingly becoming more and more of a role-playing guild and since we are a guild of good alignment we do not allow Necromancers nor Shadow Knights to join. We do not believe in discriminating against race because the actions of ones life determines their alignment not they're birth. 

To join the Fellowship of Valor you must petition a member in good standing with the guild. If they recommend you to an officer and you agree to the conditions set forth here you will be added to the guild roster as a probationary member. 

You must group with members in good standing who have the opportunity to witness you perform three valorous acts. 

Acts of Valor can include but are not limited to a) Sacrificing oneself to save another. B) perform a great act of Charity or C) Great acts of faith or kindness.

These acts must be witnessed by a non-probationary member and petitioned to an officer when they occur. 

If you feel that you would like to petition for membership please follow this link and leave a post stating what you name is, your class, and a time an officer can contact you in game.
