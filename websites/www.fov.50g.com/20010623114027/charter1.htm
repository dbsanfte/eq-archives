The Charter

The Fellowship of Valor was formed to promote valorous acts throughout the land of Norrath and as such the following mandates apply to this guild.

We will provide aid to those in trouble.
We will fight evil wherever it is found.
We will always strive to do the honorable thing in all situations.

The guild is lead by the the Guildmaster (Hawkeblade Steelheart) who is advised by the senior officers of the guild (Faradin Lawbringer and Quentin Westhorn) who hold power when the Guildmaster is not available.  The officers in the guild are chosen by the officers of the guild to fill the positions they are most suited to.
