Welcome to the home of the Fellowship of Valor

The Fellowship of Valor is an EverQuest guild dedicated to the laws of fairplay and valorous acts. Our guild is located on the Brell Serillis-server and admits virtuous players of all races, your deeds define you, not your race.  If you are a virtuous player and are interested in joining our guild, please read the charter and the information about how to join by clicking on the appropriate link on the left side of your screen.
