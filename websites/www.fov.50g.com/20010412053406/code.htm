CODE OF HONOR AND ETHICS

Above all else follow the ways of honor and valor. Those who dabble in the dark arts of death are not considered honorable or valorous.

Help others in need.  If you see someone struggling for their life stop and ask if they need a hand.  If you have the capability, heal and buff them.

The people within the guild are your brothers and sisters regardless of rank and or level.  Which means that you shouldn't charge them for teleports, binds or buffs.  In addition to that we have several allied guilds.  Anything that applies to this guild applies to theirs as well.

While you are in the guild you represent the guild itself.  Actions that you take will reflect on the guild as a whole.  So please follow the simple rules we have laid out.

Ninja looting will not be tolerated in the guild, if you are caught and found guilty you will be removed from the guild.

Begging will not be tolerated in the guild, if the behavior persists after one or two warnings you will be removed from the guild.
