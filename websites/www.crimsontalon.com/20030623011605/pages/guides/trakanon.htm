Trakanon Guide
Last update: 14 March 2003

Written by Brewall Rainsinger // Bard // Cazic-Thule

Greetings all.  I've written this guide to explain how a typical Trakanon raid is done.  Raids could be done differently than what I've detailed here based on equipment, levels, and classes, but it should give you a good idea of what is required.

Hopefully, many of the players on this type of raid are experienced dragon hunters and are familiar with large scale encounters. If you are thinking of trying such a raid and would like to paractice, the dragons of Western Wastes offer ample opportunities.

SPAWN TIME
Trakanon spawns every 3 days +/- a random number of hours.  Once killed, the Undead Bard will spawn within 0-18 hours.  The undead bard will respawn every 0-18 hours upon death, until Trakanon spawns.

WHAT YOU NEED
1. You should be between levels 55 and 60.
2. An unbuffed Magic Resistance of 100 and a Poison Resistance of 100. The higher the better.
3. Bring peridots. Guilds will typically supply these for Trakanon raids. If an open raid, bring one for cleric and one for enchanter.
4. Check with raid leader on how loot will be distributed. Alliance-type guild raids should work out how loot will be split prior to the raid, especially teeth.

HOW TO GET THERE
1. Trakanon's lair lies deep in Old Seblis. You should already have a key to Seblis.
2. Melees should bind on Kunark and casters should bind outside Seblis.
3. Recommended that the leading guild have mules/pp ready to supply coffins in the event of a failed raid.

DETERMINING IF TRAKANON / UNDEAD BARD IS UP
Because Trakanon's lair lies deep in Seblis, it is only possible to track him from a few locations:
1. Rangers can track him from D1/D2/Crypt/Fungi King room
2. Druids can track him from Fungi King room
3. Bards can track him near bridge area to lair
4. It is no longer possible to do the /target Trakanon and interpret the visible/not visible message, VI changed that in mid 2001.

TRAKANON INFORMATION
1. Trakanon is a level 65 Dragon Necromancer (no pet) with 32,000HP.
2. His most deadly weapon is his AE poison (75000 HP in 5000 ticks = 1500 HP per minute). The DoT adds 9 poison counters and has a range of 300ft.  He will cast it every 35 seconds. The poison will hit you even if you are on the bridge outside his lair. It is possible to use obstacles to block the AE.
3. He will also cast Blindness and a 1500HP lifetap.
4. Every 45 seconds, he will cast Trakanon's Touch, which will teleport someone into the mushroom area.
5. Trakanon hits in the 600's and has an immense range. He will not run around much when agroed, he will claw you from wherever he is standing.
6. Trakanon does not use AE fear.
7. Trakanon does not dispel.
8. Trakanon has high MR and PR resists. He is weak against FR and CR.
9. If Trakanon gates, he will reappear at spawn point in lair and will regain approximately 5k HP. I do not recommend that anyone fights him in bridge area due to a few bad spots where he might repop back into lair regaining HP.
10. As with other larget encounter fights, once the fight begins, no one should sit.

UNDEAD BARD / SPAWNED TRAKANON INFORMATION
1. The spawned Trakanon is exactly the same as a regular Trakanon, except for loots. Some people regard the spawned Trakanon slightly easier because he will not have Tupalumj's enchanter buffs.
2. The undead bard is neutral to everyone and drops no loot.
3. The spawned Trakanon will always drop no-drop guts needed for bard epic and 1 random BP.  It is possible to MQ the guts.  He does not drop any teeth.
4. To spawn Trakanon, a bard with a Mystical Lute Body, will give the item to the Undead Bard.  The Undead Bard will return the Lute Body back to the bard and will then despawn and an agro version will attack.  Once the Undead Bard has been killed, Trakanon will spawn.

SETUP PHASE
1. Once a CoH mage has arrived, the raiding party will begin fighting down to the lair. As new people arrive, they will be CoH'd down to the main raiding party.  It is suggested that the group leader remain at zone-in until just prior to buffing.
2. The raiding party will make its way through the dungeon and stay at the bridge area until everyone has arrived. This is an excellent opportunity to get 55+ kunark spells.
3. Go to your graphic and filter options and turn everything off or to minimum (spell effects, level of detail, damage shield messages, etc).
4. Set up "I'm blind" and "I'm poisoned" hotkeys.

BUFFING PHASE
1. A list of casters will be named and will be told what buffs to do.
2. Unlike Nagafen and Vox, where it is possible to create buff groups, buff, camp, and log back in, Trakanon buffing must be done on-the-fly due to constant attacks from guardians.
3. Trakanon does not dispel; the buffs can be done in any order. With Planes of Power, there are considerably better spells that could be done, but a good pre-PoP buff list was:
#01 shaman - talisman of kragg [365hp] (375mana - 72min)
#02 shaman - talisman of shadoo (group buff) [45pr] (150 - 36min)
#03 druid - protection of the glades (group buff) [450hp 24ac 6mana] (1200mana - 100min)
#04 cleric - bulwark of faith (tanks only) [38ac] (350mana - 81min)
#05 cleric - naltron's mark (group buff) [446hp] 1 Peridot per group (500mana - 54min)
#06 wizard - spellshield [700magic absorb] Peridot (350mana - 110min)
#07 enchanter - rune V [700dmg] Peridot (350mana - 110min)
#08 enchanter - group resist magic (group buff)[55mr] (200mana - 36min)
#09 enchanter - sshisar (epic)
#10 enchanter - gift of pure thought (group buff)[group clarity 2] (300mana - 33min)
#10 necromancer - dead men floating [70pr] (375mana - 12min) OR dead man floating
#11 shaman - potion of greater purity [25pr]
#Range Slot - magician - get a "Shard of the Core" [15pr 15cr]

ASSAULTING TRAKANON
1. Cure poison and cure blindness should be memmed.
2. Put mage in same group as positioning group in case they get teleported.
3. Once attack has started - no one should sit!
4. Groups will rush up the ramp in order of: (1) Positioning group (2) Melee and (3) Caster. Casters should stay back to give melees room.
5. First group will rush in and try to pull Trak deep into lair. Either to NE or E. Once in position, they will attack. The first person who attacks Trak will be teleported deep into the mushroom area. Normally, monks are sent in and then they are CoH'd back. The goal is to kill Trak before he ports the next person.
6. While Trak is being positioned, melee group should stay behind rock wall to shield themselves from AE. Casters should stay slightly further down ramp.
7. Other melee groups will rush in as soon as Trak is positioned.
8. Melees should use disciplines to generate as much damage and taunt as possible.
9. If Tupalumj was up, Trak could be dispelled to remove enchanter buffs (grm and haste minimum)
10. Clerics and paladins should cast group heals.
11. Let tanks beat Trak down to 80 and then open up nuking.
12. If Trak gates, remain in the lair.

CLEAN UP
1. Cure DoT as soon as you are done. Nothing worse than winning and then dropping dead from poison.
2. Get anyone who was shroom ported. Ideally, it was just the initial monk attacker who was CoH'd back.
