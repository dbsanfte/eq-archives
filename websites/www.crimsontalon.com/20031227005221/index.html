NTOV Patch day
Posted by: Chantress on Friday, December 19, 2003 - 06:01 AM (2 Reads)
Hi, and welcome to patch day.  I will be your tour guide for today.  Today Sony decided to implement a nice new discipline system.  This new system requires all melees go buy books and read them.  Now we all know that melees are not casters for a reason (they dont read so good).  Not only did sony decide to make this complicated system (for melees) they did it during the holidays when casual guilds typically have lower attendance.  That coupled with the timely release of LOTR3....well you get the picture, practically no one was online tonite.  Fortunately those who were, were outstanding players that are very capable of executing without panicking in dire situations.  So we took our meager 25, or was it 24 or 23 due to excessive patch day LDs,  to NToV to honor our first day of our rotation.  Thanks to flawless execution (even though most of our melees had not read their books so we didnt have disciplines) we still held our ground and killed Roy and his kinsmen.  Grats rot and alts and allternate use items.  We got a bunch of crap tonite, the good stuff will come tomorrow.

Welcome to the farm!
Posted by: Chantress on Friday, October 17, 2003 - 05:57 AM (11 Reads)
