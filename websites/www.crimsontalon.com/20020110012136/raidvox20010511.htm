Raid #1 - Lady Vox - May 11, 2001
Report from raid leader, Brewall Rainsinger, of the Crimson Talon.

LADY VOX IS DEAD

Let it be said, that Lady Vox died at the hands of Crimson Talon and other hearty adventurers who joined us in battle.  Vox had not gone down on the server for several months and it was time for her to feel cold steel in her belly.

As a first time dragon raid leader, I would certainly say that the raid went very well.   There were approximately 12 no-shows from the everquestraids list, but fortunately, enough extras showed up to bring the total raid attendence to 39 (1 dissapeared just prior to buffing, so final was 38).  It took about an hour for everyone to arrive, clear Vox's guards, and create the groups.  This is definitely the time of the raid where everyone is wondering what is taking so long, but in fact is the most hectic for the raid leader (forming final groups, determining buff groups, coordinating with pull groups, arranging ports to get late comers there, telling level 20's that they can't attend, telling people how to get to the king area, and the list goes on). Luckily, I had created 40+ social keys with instructions (which I'm going to tweak) and had built a spreadsheet that helped form groups and determine loot numbers.

Almost 80% of the attendees had never been on a dragon raid, and almost 90% had never been on a successful one (or ever done a buff line). Vox is definitely harder than Naggy, and it takes a lot more work to pull off a good raid.  I foresee the next raid going even smoother and quicker than this one, and if enough people arrive on time may be able to push the time well below 3 hours.  The raid was completely finished at 11:30pm CST, a 3.5hour total duration. Out of the 9 raids I've been on, this was one of the fastest (average time 4+ hours).  Buffing took a little longer than I would have liked, but we only had 1 cle and 1 enc that had 2 of the critical buffs. I did tweak the line, midway through and it did speed it up a little more.

May have also been a bit easier if I didn't have a huge thunderstorm outside my house at the same time that took out my cable modem and caused me to lose power twice (thank goodness for my UPS).

Special thanks go out to Altus (CT guild leader) for his excellent pulling of the Ice Giants and Vox's guards. And to the buffers that evening as well: Ceeni, Combatt, Khallin, Greymask, Milennia, Morgynn, and Treesa. If any of you are out any plat for the peridots, let me know and I will reimburse you.
