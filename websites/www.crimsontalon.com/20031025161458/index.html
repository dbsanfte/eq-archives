The UNsung War
Posted by: Chantress on Friday, October 24, 2003 - 05:34 AM (1 Reads)

For those of you who dont know, there has been a long time war between those people who like to think they are good pullers (what i actually mean here is anyone other than a proficeint enchanter) and Enchanters.  You see, for some reason these "pullers" continually try to "challenge" Enchanters.  Take for our example tonite, the one and only Proetus.  While he has pulled nice trains before that have wiped raids, nothing he could do could match the crowd controlling force of the Crimson Talon Enchanters!  Proetus, being the clown that he is decides that since he is a monk he will pull half of PoI on top of us.  No problem.  As a matter of fact we were so involved in this unsung duo of Enchanters and "pullers" we ended up having to change targets.  Skip ahead to BoT.  Loot was had, bosses were killed.  Grats Alone and Drakefyre on yer new shoulder items.  Links below.  Major props to Awll, this is truely one of the servers great clerics.  He had the tireless duty of keeping me alive tonite, and as always he was up to the task.  If you have never had the pleasure of grouping with Awll, you are truely missing out.

Xanawho?
Posted by: Chantress on Saturday, October 18, 2003 - 05:08 AM (2 Reads)

Having hastily dropped off the corpse of Grummus, our good friend Earl rushed away from the Crimson Talon Farm Department.  Being in such a hurry for fear of what the gnomes might do to him, he made a wrong turn and stumbled upon nothing other than the forces of Crimson Talon Escorting the Gnome!  Slamming on the breaks, with his tires screaching he raced in the opposite direction only to have the gnome and Crimson Talon following him.  In his state of panic he didnt even notice what was in front of him until he had already run into it.  Needsless to say the first snack of our favorite mechanical dragon was none other than Earl.  God rest his soul.  Well the good forces of Crimson Talon could not stand for this and immediately slayed the foul beast with all haste. Poor Earl.  Xanamech was kind with the loots, Grats to Khallin and a big thank you to Denor for showing what Crimson Talon is all about.  For those of you not familiar with us, we divide up all loot on a /random based system.  Denor won the roll on the ring and graciously passed it off to Khallin.  Truely an admirable act.  Grats to Alone on his well deserved 41% haste neck item!  Links below.

Welcome to the farm!
Posted by: Chantress on Friday, October 17, 2003 - 05:57 AM (2 Reads)

It was a nice evening when Earl walked in the front door hurriedly.  "Marla!" he said in great haste, " Look what's new on the farm!"  As Marla got up out of her knitting chair she looked out the door to see the fat bulbous putrid rotting corpse of none other than Grummus himself.  This was no ordinary Grummus though.  He had some strange markings.  As she moved closer, she noticed the huge red stamp on the forehead.  "Earl!" she exclaimed, "That says 'Property of Crimson Talon Farm Department'  You better return it before they find it missing and come and get you!"  Earl, looking worriedly at his new found prize immediately threw it back in the old beat up 82 Chevy and we have now placed him in a protection program so that the gnomes dont hunt him down.  Rumor has it he also took all of Grummus crappy loot annd left Romell and Zypheal with a nice pair of rings!  Grats CT on the newly added MOB to the farming list.
