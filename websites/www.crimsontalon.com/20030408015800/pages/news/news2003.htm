Jan 10, 2003
With a few recent mini raids on PoS giants and the PoI dragon with folks from SA, we decided to familiarize ourselves with one another a bit better. What better way than to use the command that every guild leader has been itching to try. Yes. Drumroll.... \GUILDWAR!

The forces of Crimson Talon will take to the battlefield against the forces of Second Age. The clash will be in WC, EC, NRo, Oasis, and SRo. The war will be judged based on confirmed kills and/or the death of the guild leader. SA will defend SRo and CT will defend WC. Guild leaders will remain in these zones.

The war will be an entertaining event for all of us and should be an all-around entertaining event. Win-or-lose, this Jan 16th should be an exciting time.
