Welcome to the new Crimson Talon website
Posted by: ctadmin on Friday, June 20, 2003 - 09:48 AM (17 Reads)

The new Crimson Talon site is live!  Everything has been migrated over, but the old forums will remain active for another week while everyone sets up their new bulletin board account.  This change allows the guild to be much more flexible and provide more services:

*Ability for guild members to maintain character profiles.
*Ability to run dozens of guild reports, including keys/flags.
*Ability for guild members to post public/private calendar events.
*Ability for guild members to submit news, stories, and screenshots.
*All this with wysiwyg editors, advanced polling features, and an easy to maintain bulletin board.
