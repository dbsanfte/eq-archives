April 02, 2003
The Cazic-Thule server has always been quite cooperative with rotation agreements because they save everyone an enormous amount of time. Tonight, due to a guild going rogue in NToV, per rotation rule #11, we alerted all other NToV rotation guilds of this transgression. Rule 11 states:

11) Should a "rogue" guild decide to try and interfere with the set rotation, instead of attempting to cooperate with it, there need to be a means set in place to stop them from doing so. In such a case, all guilds/allies in the rotation will be asked to come and fight for NToV as one large alliance, to force that rogue guild to compete with 200+ people, hopefully forcing that rogue guild into cooperation.

Within 15 minutes, over 250 people from the rotation guilds of Crimson Talon, Obsidian Coven, Exhumed, The Dragon Alliance, Legacy of Darkness, Second Age, Blood Guards, and Council of Grey were in the zone! After discussions with the GM's, the rotation agreement and enforcement stands. A special thanks to all the other guilds that showed up.

More dragons fell:
Carx'Vean: Silent Fang Necklace- Tronsmang (OC)
Lord Kreizenn: Silver Disk- Dovan (OC), Verlekarnorm's Horn of Disaster- Brewall (CT) only CT bard present, wins by default
Zlexak: Windraider's Belt- Ippit (CT)
Cekenar: Blackstar, Mace of Night- Sveva (OC), Meljeldin, Bane of the Giants- Machedor (CT)
Sevalak: Axe of Resistance- ?

Also, Brewall has posted his map drawing for the Dulak's Habor along with a cleaned-up TXT file (30k) and an additional overlay file with grid/coordinates (3k). These will be made public in a few weeks.
