Mar 14, 2003
We are all very thankful as a guild to be made up of players that like to play EQ as a game and treat each other like family. As we have progressively climbed up the encounter difficulty ladder, we remember where we came from and how long we have played together. Cherishing our friendship, we stay together, and look forward to the next new encounter and challenge. In just the last month, we have taken on and killed: The Statue of Rallos Zek, the Avatar of War, Aaryonar, and the 10th coldain ring war. NToV and other new encounters are coming up as well. Next!
