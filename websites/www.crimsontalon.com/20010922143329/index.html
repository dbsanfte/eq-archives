Raid 25: Vox. 9pm* EST. No date set. Postponed 9/11 raid.

(2001.09.12) Looking for a new Nag/Vox Dragon Raid leader to replace me. Have full thread with requirements and expectations in the message board in this thread. Can also find my new hosting guide with dragon raiding spreadsheet here.

*Note: Brewall will get on line between 7:30-8:00pm CST to start collecting names. Show up earlier if you want to, but it will not guarantee you will have a spot. Priority to get onto raids is based on (in order): necessary classes, raid points+attendance points, and levels. Check-in time is not a factor.
Crimson Talon News

SEP 18, 2001
Congratulations to Dainin for finishing the monk epic quest! It is the first monk epic in our guild and about 3 groups killed mob after mob throughout the evening to finish it. Started with a quick trip to the Plane of Air to kill Gwan on second island, trip to Nurga to slay Trunt, and finished up in the lake of ill omen against Vorash, Deep, and Xeneverash. Celestial Fists: ac15, agi10 dex10 str20 sta10 hp100 mr10 cr10 fr10 pr10 dr10 40% haste. 

SEP 17, 2001
10 Days, 3 epics. Tonight, we raided City of Mist and finished Zulon's epic for the shaman Spear of Fate. A few other members are close to finishing their epics and more raids coming up in the coming weeks.

SEP 14-16, 2001
Raid on the Plane of Hate. Late start on Friday due to login server snafu that prevented many of us from logging in. Throughout the weekend we cleared the zone several times, and the lack of sleep paid of well. We came away with 4 BP's, 40+ items, and numerous gems. A list of non-rotting drops is below:
Bard: Imbrued Platemail Vambraces [Devaberiel], Imbrued Platemail Boots [Devabriel], Six Note Blade [Brewall, Devaberiel]
Cleric: Ethereal Mist Gauntlets [Aorthin, Rezzie, Stevenfost], Ethereal Mist Boots [Rezzie, Skylynn], Ethereal Mist Vambraces [Valael]
Enchanter: Insidious Robe [Greymask], Insidious Halo [Greymask, Mysyn], Insidious Slippers [Mysyn], Insidious Manacle [Greymask], Insidious Sleeves [Mysyn]
Mage: Apothic Robe [Catren, Immolar], Apothic Sleeves [Immolar], Apothic Gloves [Catren]
Monk: Rakusha Cloak [Physst], Shuriken of the Tranquil [Chiun, Physst]
Necro: Encyclopedia Necrothurgia [Random necro in zone]
Paladin: Tainted Darksteel Brestplate [Hawkien, Yofoopineapple], Theologian Claymore [Hawkien], Greaves of the Rightous [Chadmoe]
Ranger: Revultant Whip [Klaminar]
Rogue: Woven Shadow Vambraces [Zoranna], Woven Shadow Bracer [Zoranna], Woven Shadow Gloves [Zoranna], Woven Shadow Boots [Klepta], Book of Souls [Zoranna]
Shadow Knight: Ashenbone Abbasi [Valoria]
Shaman: Rune Etched Chestplate [Khallin], Rune Etched Gloves [Barelil]
Warrior: Indicolite Vambraces [Sventhal], Indicolite Gloves [Sventhal], Indicolite Bracer [Lumper], Indicolite Boots [Valstorm], Indicolite Helm [Sventhal], Indicolite Greaves [Valstorm], Hand of Maestro (Warrior Epic) [Lumper, Valstorm]

SEP 12, 2001
Raided City of Mist this evening, and Barelil got final item needed for shaman epic. Grats to Barelil! May try for Zulon's if we can take of some unfinished wizard epic business first.

SEP 11, 2001
Canceling Vox raid for this evening. Spend some time with the ones you love and pray for the victims of the senseless act of terrorism.

SEP 10, 2001
Lady Vox dies for the sixteenth time to Brewall led raid (24-0 versus Nag & Vox). Late night. Were short on clerics and enchanters (1 & 0 respectively) until raid members got friends to come. Lair pulling still very difficult with Vox agro changes.  The inevitable only pushed back so long and Vox perished slightly after midnight. Drops were Frost Book [Merril], McVaxious Horn of War [Tegerien], Staff of Forbidden Rites [Ammene], and Warhammer of Divine Grace [Draconai].

SEP 08, 2001
Descended upon the Hole once again for another long crawl of the dungeon.

SEP 07, 2001
Hole raid. Alone got last item for epic and now has Swiftwind! Quite a bit of other loot dropped as well including Imbued Granite Spaulders. Returning again Saturday night.

SEP 06, 2001
Site was down while it was moved to another ISP. Lost a couple days worth of messages on the boards. Sorry for technical problems, but short notice couldn't be avoided.

SEP 05, 2001
Howling Stones raid. Took out Drusella Sathir, the boss mob, once again. Will have to take out her still-living husband Venril sometime soon.

AUG 30, 2001
Vox dies for the 15th time last night to a Brewall led dragon raid. Everyone was still in blood-lust mode from last night's server problems and were eager to return. Lair still tricky to pull with agro changes. Buff stations now at the bottom of the ramps, and did not have Vox attack us this time (see Map in updated Vox raid guide). Buffs were very quick, and the entire raid was done in well under 3 hours. Vox managed 1 CH, but died neverless. Drops were: Book [Tico - 19 time attendee], Kayrul's Mystical Pouch [Roobee], Crystalline Spear [Banamana], and 2 Ice Comets [Seviriyn, Zeretul]. Now 23-0 versus Nag and Vox.

AUG 29, 2001
Absolute Vox raiding mess last night. Were very close to clearing the lair out, when the servers decided to come to a grinding halt. The raiding party was down to just 12 people, and many decided to give up  after trying for 30+ minutes to get back on. Without critical mix of classes, Brewall postponed raid until Thu night.

Updated all the dragon raiding guides for Vox, Nagafen, and Faydedar.

AUG 27, 2001
Lord Nagafen falls yet again to another open attendance/open loot raid led by Brewall. Now 22-0 versus Nag and Vox. Nagafen fell quickly and deaths were minimal. Loots were: Torn Burnt Book [Ciladin], 2 Selo Drums of the March [Cask, Corus], Red Dragon Scales [Venswayla/Rado], Bladestopper [Tico], Blight, Hammer of Scourge [Krazus], Jacinth [Physst], and a Diamond [Guuma]. Also got to clear out giants in the process, drops were: Crown of King Tranix [Faedilsong], Mithril Greaves [Iversen], and Polished Mithril Mask [Tularane].

AUG 26, 2001
Venril Sathir's remains were up and we quickly mobilized to take him out. Definitely getting better at these quick raids and it is great to see everyone helping each other. Both Dunskye and Dodja now have items needed for druid and ranger epics.

AUG 25, 2001
After over a month of planning, Crimson Talon did its first in-house (with friend invites) raid on Fear. Spent Friday night breaking the plane and giving ourselves some breathing room. As our first break, we did pretty well, and learned a lot. Sat morning, we got back on and proceeded to clear it out. On a whim, we decided to take on the enraged golem (150k of hit points), and WON! Only suffered 4 deaths during the 16+ minute fight. Groovit is now just 1 item away from the wizard epic (staff from VS)! Drops for the weekend were: Thorny Vine Leggings [Udian & Klaminar], Carmine Gloves [Groovit], Carmine Leggings [Groovit], Carmine Robe [Groovit], Oil from Enraged Golem [Groovit], Umbral Breastplate [Sylvanna], Vermiculated Arms [Azenyth], Cryosilk Sleaves [Jaade], Flayed Turmoil Skin Belt [Barelil], Rune Etched Boots [Barelil], Amy Whip [Lumper, Valstorm, Devaberiel], Rogue Bracer [Zoranna], Eyerazza [Zoranna, Dreamthief], Shiverback Leggings [Chiun], Girdle of the Diligent [Ghoem], Thorny Vine Gauntlets [Klaminar], Umbral Leggings [Sylvanna], Vermiculated Leggings [Windarii], Umbral Gauntlets [Sylvanna], Indicolite Gauntlets [Lumper], and Valorium Vambraces [Savardar].

AUG 22, 2001
Three words: "Permafrost was changed". The mobs, mob placement, roamers, and timing are all a bit different in Permafrost. Not big changes, just enough to make pulling more work than ever. Vox agro range is a lot bigger than before as well. While we were finishing up buffing the buffers, Vox suddenly appeared in the hallway and started killing people. Took a while to get her moved back into the lair and get everything back on track. For all the trouble, Vox had the last laugh dropping: 2 Kayrul's Mystical Pouches [Kauf, Sventhal], 2 Crystilline Spears [Cask, Brewall], a Dragon Bone Bracelet [Nieche], and Ice Comet [Silvercaster]. Just for clarification, Nieche came with monk & a 50 enc, wanted to play with monk, but I was desperate for enc, I let him roll as monk - this was all set in stone prior to groups being announced. I have never turned away a 48+ enchanter from a Vox raid, seive is too important.

AUG 21, 2001
Udian finished the first sword of the ranger epic, Swiftwind (1hs 13/21 str15 sta10 dex10 mr5 fr5 cr5 pr5 dr5 hp50 2.0wt w/ Swift Spirit: 40% haste & atk40). Needs only a Shattered Emerald of Corruption from Hate mini bosses/Inny to get the other sword, Earthcaller.

AUG 20, 2001
Descended upon the Swamp of No Hope to kill Ulump for another part of Tippe's epic. Was the first time we were able to take him out with no deaths at all, a very clean fight. Tippe is now just a sea horse and a sea dragon away!

AUG 19, 2001
The forces of Crimson Talon gathered in the Burning Woods last night to slay Ixiblat Fer. Spent a little time setting up buffs and then Tomwin pulled the mob all the way to the Skyfire zone for the battle. A short fight later, Ixiblat lay defeated. Grats to Skylynn for another part of her epic and Wyntyr for winning the Circlet of Fire (Head 20mana 15fr w/ 5 charges supernova).

AUG 18, 2001
Late night activities in the Eastern Wastes as Hawkien finished his fifth Coldain ring quest. A small group was on-hand to kill Scarbrow and the other orcs.

AUG 16, 2001
The six day killing-spree on dragons has ended with 2 dead Nagafen's and 2 dead Vox's.   Brewall was hosting his 20th open-attendance/open-loots/no reserved items raid last night against Vox.  With just one lone enchanter on the raid (other than 53+ buff enchanters), the fight was going to be a long one. The battle was of epic length and Vox managed to get off two complete heals, but it was for naught. Great job by all attending keeping her off the walls and continuing to relentlessly beat her into submission. Drops were: White Dragon Scale [Woodsing],  Dragon Bone Bracelet [Ferdinand], 2 Crystalline Spear [Klaminar, Corus], Kayrull's Mystical Pouch [Olwe], and Warhammer of Divine Grace [Aenlador]. Aenlador left prior to the roll and Brewall still has item, you have 1 week to come claim it. Brewall raids now 20-0 versus Nag/Vox.

AUG 15, 2001
Patch day, and another dragon falls. This time Nagafen, the dragon cursed by clerics everywhere. Plenty of people on hand, and at least 15 were turned away. Buffs went quickly and the battle in Solusek began. Nagafen fell quickly and was only able to claim a few victims. Loot was plentiful, but paltry (no CoF or Books): Heirophant's Crook [Windarii], 2 Gauntlets of Firey Might [Zypheaal, Namffoh], Prayers of Life [Sarduakar], Bladestopper [Ciladin], Treasure Hunters Satchel [Kathandros], Ruby Crown [Infernostar], and a Diamond [Hawkien].
