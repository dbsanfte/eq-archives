JAN 13, 2002
Acrylia Circle raid today, and it can be summarized in two words:  very cool.   If you are not familiar with the ring, there is an empty room in Acrylia with a ring of totems sticking out of the ground. Once someone steps inside the ring, a few non-attackable grimlings appear that regularly summon undead mobs to attack you.  If you step out of ring, it ends, and if you leave the room, you will get a ton of adds. After about twenty minutes of constant attacks, a boss will spawn, and then the next round begins, this time, even harder.  This is absolute non-stop fighting with changing mobs and tactics.  We made it up to the third boss and even though the loot wasn't great, we had a great time trying something new and different.

JAN 12, 2002
Raid on the Juggernaughts of Seblis for spells.  With so many of our casters hitting 58 and 60 and staring at empty spell slots, we decided to do something about it.  We fought down and cleared the Juggs a few times and managed to get about a dozen spells. Then, we decided to try a spawned Trakanon.  The attack went very smoothly and we had Trak down to 10 percent, when, he started warping all over the place. He was in his lair, down in the hallway, near the bridge, and then just started cycling between all three and sometimes nowhere to be found.  A very fustrating fight. We never could get him nailed down to finish him off.  Later, we found out this was a bug when you: send someone in to sacrifice, Trak ports them, Trak tries to follow (appears in hallway near bridge where we attacked), and if anyone attacking him steps on the rocks, he starts warping.  We are adapting our pull/attack strategy and should own him in the next fight. Brewall is writing up a strategy guide and is looking for input in the guild-only area of forum.
