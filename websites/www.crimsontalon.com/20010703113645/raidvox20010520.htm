Lady Vox Raid May 20, 2001
Report from raid leader, Brewall Rainsinger, of the Crimson Talon.

LADY VOX IS DEAD

Vox dies a second time at the hands of Crimson Talon and other hearty adventurers who joined us in battle.  Vox had not gone down on the server for several months and the last two Brewall lead raids have eased the oppression of this cruel dragon over the lands of Everfrost, as well as, liberated some of her property.

As now a second time dragon raid leader, I would say the raid went well for a pickup raid. If you read many sites, you will see that many attempts fail on Vox, especially pick-up raids, Vox is much harder than Naggy.  Leading a raid is definitely not for slow typists or those that are unorganized. I was averaging at least 1 tell every 20 seconds, and while trying to formulate groups (and adapt strategy based on classes at hand) at the same time, this is no easy task. Fortunately, I used a spreadsheet I had built from the raid before that automates quite a few tasks (except for manually coming up with groups & buffers). It calculates all the loot numbers for me automatically, making it possible to do the open loots accurately. I've actually been on raids that took 2+ hours to divide up the loot in absolute, utter chaos. Once people get comfortable with loot procedures, I can do it quicker, by just doing the number, announcing winner, and posting numbers on website for everyone to see.

Only mistake on the attack was Vox getting pushed onto a wall. It allowed her to CH twice, next time, I'll shout this 5x in a row, multiple times before the attack and multiple times during the fight. My shouts to get her off wall were definitely getting lost in the spam. Absolutely need to keep Vox in the middle of the room.

Special thanks go out to Altus (CT guild leader) for his excellent pulling of the Ice Giants and Vox's guards. And to the buffers that evening as well: Loen, Bolgor, Sileguy, Vyce, Syncere, Khallin, Mysyn, Greymask, Saleel, Tomwin, and Psychol.
