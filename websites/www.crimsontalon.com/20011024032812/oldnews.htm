Lady Vox perishes in the frosty wastelands of Permafrost this evening. Unbelievable number of people present wanting to kill her, but only so many spots. To date, 250+ different people have attended my 12 dragon raids! These raids are truely the most open on the server (or any other server for that matter). As they win, they tend to float off (loot is still and always will be random). My advice: Show up to 1 raid, get an attendance point, and watch raid from zone-line to familiarize yourself with process. Then use time to get some good resistance gear and get a couple levels. If you come to a later raid saying you are 49 with unbuffed cold/fire resistance at 130+, witnessed a couple raids in past, and have at least 1 attendance point, you are in pretty good shape.
