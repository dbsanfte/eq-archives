Crimson Talon News
JUN 30, 2001
Changing Naggy raid to Sat. Server reset on Fri means that date needs to be either moved up, or someone else will kill and then date would have to be pushed back. May not be able to get on much before 9pm cst though. Subsequent Vox and Nag raids will need to shift as well.

JUN 27, 2001
Vox perishes for a sixth time at the hands of another Brewall led raid. She went down quickly and everyone did excellent job of surrounding her and keeping her in middle of room. Sorry to all of you that were turned away from the raid (at least 12 people). Loots were: Frost Book [Kanine], Rune Bolster Belt [Dainin], Karyrul's Mystical Pouch [Nandarr], and Staff of Forbidden Rites [Immmolar].

JUN 26, 2001
Raided the fire giants in Solusek B with 2 groups due to the patch date change. Drops were Polished Mithril Torque [Tenara], Mithril Vambraces [Udian], and Polished Mithril Mask [Immolar].

JUN 25, 2001
Adjusted all dragon raid dates to reflect VI moving patch date from Tue Jun 26th to Wed Jun 27th.

JUN 22, 2001
Failed attempt on Fay in Timorous Deep. Lot of discussion on boards on how to do it better on the next attempt. Will be working with other guilds to help out each others druids.

JUN 18-24, 2001
Various raids on the Hole, Velketor's Labrynth (great gem drops for guild bank), and other zones.

JUN 17, 2001
The master of the deep, Phinigel Autropos, was resident in his lair last night and Crimson Talon decided to pay him a visit. Drops were: Staff of Elemental Mastery [Immolar], Kedge Backbone [Brewall], Wand of Mana Tapping [Saleel], and Wand of Malise [Khallin].

JUN 16, 2001
Lady Vox slain again this evening by a hearty band of adventurers led by Brewall of Crimson Talon, who is now 5-0 versus the beast.  Loots were perhaps the worst to date: Runed Bolster Belt [Chiun], Staff of Forbidden Rites [Altus], Ice Comet [Eiyre], Ice Comet [Guldaenya], and a rune (junk). Ice Comets were open rolls, as no wizards were present on the raid. Perhaps the most annoying factor that night were number of people that did not have peridots or any platinum to pay for peridots.  In the future, if you do not have peridot/platinum, you will not receive these buffs.

JUN 14, 2001
Many members of the Crimson Talon attended a Lord Nagafen raid hosted by Ixnus of Jedi Praxium. Loots were: Red Dragon Scale [Cris], Red Dragon Scale [Krukak], Cloak of Flames [Grenack], and a Heirophant's Crook. Amazingly enough, Ragefire spawned within an hour of Naggy's death. Biggest problem was that many in the raiding party still recovering from rez effects and were killed by trains brought by people rushing to kill Ragefire. I realize it can be a race to kill him, but would be nice for guilds to shout that they will get rezes resulting from the trains and who to send names to.  Liked some of the buff techniques and will be making some tweaks to my process. Next Vox will have 2 buff stations, one at first landing other at second landing, also will do shaman buffs after junks.

JUN 11, 2001
Another night in the Hole. Grats to Dodja for getting ranger epic piece!

JUN 10, 2001
Once again, Crimson Talon raids the Hole. Another excellent dungeon crawl through an empty dungeon. Drops were: Imbued Granite Spaulders [Zypheal] and Withered Leather Bracer [Skylynn]. Zypheal then lottoed up the shoulders that were replaced Obulus Death Shroud [Tippe]. Killed the ranger epic mob twice for both Udian and Madmat. Congrats to both! 

JUN 09, 2001
Grats to Zypheal for reaching 60!

JUN 07, 2001
*Agreement with Bloods Guard to rotate on Vox spawns. They took Vox Thu Jun 7, and Brewall's open raid will hit her on Sat Jun 16. From there, it will alternate between the BG closed raids & CT open raids. In the event of a patch, the guild with next spawn has 2 days to kill her, otherwise, that spawn is FFA and the rotation resumes as normal upon her death. Both raid leaders will share information regarding time of death & warn of any respawn. Goal for both raids is to maximize kills. BG will also not be killing Naggy Wed/Thu next week leaving it open. Ixnus of Jedi Praxum is leading this raid (signup on Everquestraids) and Brewall will assist Ixnus in any way possible (but is not leading raid nor is raid using Brewall's raid points). An organized dragon schedule is a lot more preferable than a FFA system that can lead to a lot of fighting and one-upmanship by guilds & players. CT is made up of ex-big guild players seeking to avoid this type of play style, hence our NGB, open loots, and public disclosure.

*Night not a complete loss, since raid cancelled early in evening and a few different epic mobs were slain for enchanter (Grats to Greymask for finishing 1st part of enchanter epic) and monk epic quests. 

JUN 06, 2001
*Decended upon CoM and slayed final mobs needed for Khallin to complete epic quest for his Spear of Fate. Congrats Khallin! A Atramentous Shield (ac28 int5 sta5 str5 hp50 mana50 cr5 fr5 mr10 Deadeye/ shd) also dropped [won by Lsoth from AoF].
