JUN 18, 2002
We needed a few epic items from Hate and decided to raid it this evening.  On the first floor, we managed to get Kestel's vampire essence, 2 Eyes of Innoruuk [Imotep & Kenatal] and Alone's Ranger stone.   After laying waste to the first floor, we decided that we wanted our first Inny kill.  We ventured out to the second floor and cleared the way to the god of hate. One excellently executed fight later, Inny lay dead at our feet.  Grats to Immolar for the Pauldron's of Power!

JUN 11, 2002
Grats to Kilwean for obtaining the bard epic singing short sword!

We also decided to try the Grimling High Priest in Acrylia.  We brought two groups (spoiler sites suggested three), and we were complete overkill.  Mobs barely lasted 5 seconds as we killed the would-be repelers.  After over an hour of killing them, watching the blood dry, and playing gems, the Grimling High Priest finally took matters into his own hands.  Only problem was that once we had him down to 10 percent, he poofed. Not gated. Poofed.  At least we got a few bone chips. Still, Brewall is making a few adjustments to his map from the explorations.

JUN 10, 2002
Grats to Denor for finishing the Shaman epic!  At the same time as the CoM raid, we broke into Fear and proceeded to clear the zone.  Lot of BP drops, too bad no one needed them.

JUN 08, 2002
Grats to Dreamthief for reaching 60!

JUN 07, 2002
Cleared out Fear this evening for some epic loots: Broken Golem [Minmin], Amy whip [Kilwean], and Shissar [Kestel]. After that, we went to Kael arena to slay giants once again and came away with chain legs, plate legs, and a plate bp.

JUN 06, 2002
Killed both Severilous and Talendor today.  Sev was stingy and dropped a dragon bone bracer [Chadmoe], Pouch [Greymask], and 6 spells - no green scale though.  We followed that up with Talendor, but no books. Loots from these raids, even with our mana burning crew, is always given to guildmates - and are never sold.  These two kills on top of a big dungeon crawl of Grieg's End. If you have never done a crawl of a dungeon before, you are really missing out. We've gotten much faster as a guild on quick assembly and rapid fire take out of the Kunark dragons. We have killed all 4 within the past 3 days - and yes, we are killing them because we want the items, not to sell them.

JUN 05, 2002
We decided to raid the Hole to get a slew of needed epic items. We quickly fought our way down to Nortlav and dispatched the rusty weilding fighter. Grats to Kilwean for the Red Dragon Scale.  Also killed many other epic mobs on the way back up for everyone else who needed something.

JUN 04, 2002
As a guild, we have been working hard and refining our tactics. Looking back on the last few months, we have steadily moved up the ladder of difficulty, and tonight was no exception. Coming off last nights two dragon raids, we wanted more. First, we decided to make our first attempt on Vindicator, and we were wildly successful. Loots were: Head [Tomwin], and Boots of the Vindicator [Awll].

After we plowed Vindicator into dust, we decided to make our first attempt on Velketor.   With no one with any prior experience with the fight, we still managed to kill him.   Two big mobs in one night - again.  Loots were: Shield of Freezing Wind [Chadmoe], Vermillion of Refraction [Mmatil], Crystal Crown of Confusion [Jarheuu], and 5 spells.

JUN 03, 2002
The dragon hunting force was in full swing last night.  We started in Dreadlands with Gorenaire, and dispatched the dragon with much fanfaire. Drops were nice for epics: WDS [Kilwean] and 2 Frost Books [Hawkien, Khandrahcir]. After Gore, we decided to do Juggs for more spells, only to discover that Trakanon himself was up.  We then decided to dispatch him as well and killed him in record time.  Drops were: Jaundice BP [Khallin], Deepwater BP [Chadmoe] and 2 Teeth [Brewall, Mmatil]. Along the way, we also managed to get 2 Dragon Sinews [Greymask, Kestel].  After Trak, we then rolled on some extra Kael armour to MQ: Plate legs [Tico], Plate arms [Kilwean], and Plate gaunt [Zypheal].  Excellent raiding by everyone with very minimal deaths (ZERO deaths on Gore and just 2 on Trak).

JUN 02, 2002
Small plate raid run in Kael this evening, and the drops were very good for a change: 2 bracers: [Devaberiel, Lilsong], 2 legs: [Awll], 2 gaunts, 2 boots [Sylvanna, Khandrahcir], arms [Hawkien], and a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang: [Windarii].

MAY 01, 2002
Grats to Wyntyr for finishing the wizard epic quest this evening.  Phinny was very stubborn for her and he finally decided to reward her for her efforts. In looking over our guild news from the past month, it is easy to see we've been rampaging on epics of late.   With 10 in just the last 30 days, including some of the toughest in the game (magician, warrior).  For a guild of our size, this is an epic achievement of its own, and demonstrates how well we all work together. There aren't many epics left to get, and if could just catch Ragefire...

Also this evening, the Halls of Testing: Chain Bracer [Dreamthief], Plate Boots [Gish], Silk Boots [Groovit, Silvercaster], Plate Gauntlets [Stien], Silk Wrist [Jarheuu], and Chain Gauntlets [Shcammer].
