Recruiting Information

** Last updated 12 June 2001

The Crimson Talon does not aim to be an uber-guild on the server. We are a small guild, filled with friends that help each other out. Quite a few of us have been in big guilds and didn't like that kind of atmosphere. With that being said, we don't have a pre-set limit on members.

The general application process involves securing a sponsor within the guild. The best way to do this is to come with us on hunts and group with us.  No one should be shy to group with us either -- we have yet to turn someone who wanted to come with us, and they get full loot rights to boot.  We usually group with a person for a while and get to know them. If they seem to fit in, we invite them to the guild.

Joining any guild should be the match between your goals and the goals of the guild. Those looking for phat hand-me-down ub3r l33ts and powerleveling would be a poor match.

See our thread on our bulletin board for more information.
