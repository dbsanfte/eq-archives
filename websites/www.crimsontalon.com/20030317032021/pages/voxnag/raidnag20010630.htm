Raid #07 Lord Nagafen
Raid Date: June 30 2001

Report from raid leader Brewall Rainsinger // Bard // Cazic-Thule

Lord Nagafen felt the cold grip of death tonight as Brewall led his seventh dragon raid against the master of Solusek.  

Decided to move date of raid from Thursday to Saturday, since the original date was based on Nag dying on the original patch date of Wed. With patch on Friday, however, he would have been killed by others, and the Thu date would have had to been pushed back.

Took much longer than normal to get everyone to raid, but was partially a function of getting enough clerics and bards. Still, the ragtag group that was assembled performed admirably.

If you ever wonder what is going on or why things are taking so long, just keep some things in mind:
1. Getting 40 people who don't have gear, never been to a dragon raid or know where lair is in Sol B is no easy task. Not to mention sending tells to people trying to get them to come, and answering loot questions they have.

Excellent job by all listening to instructions, although, there were a few individuals who were giving the pullers a hard time and refusing to listen. If you have never done a dragon, pay attention to those that have, there is a reason for everything.

Special thanks go out to Altus and Physst for tremendous job pulling the Giants. This is an extremely difficult and important task and I certainly appreciate their efforts. And to all the buffers that evening as well: Khallin, Cerritus, Syncere, Greymask, Daliiah, Chaser, Zulon, Balidmar, Loen, Saleel, Mysyn, and Remuvdar. For all the first time buffers that were there, great job!
