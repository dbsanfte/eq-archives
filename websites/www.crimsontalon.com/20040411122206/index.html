Happy Birthday, Guild
Posted by: brewall on Tuesday, March 09, 2004 - 11:32 AM (3 Reads)

Posted by: Altus on Monday, December 29, 2003

A while ago, a few people decided to engage in the electronic equivalent of wearing an "I'm with stupid ==>" t-shirt and formed a guild. It is with more than a little sense of amazement that I realize that the guild is now four years old.

I normally save my ramblings for the privacy of our members forum because those people have to listen to me. Four years does not seem like such a long time to me anymore. It seems like just yesterday that we were waiting for the world to end in an orgiastic meltdown of silicon wafers and ATM cards when the clock struck the doom of Y2k. And we were Livin' La Vida Loca, or at least Ricky Martin was. What ever happened to him, anyway? Is he off living somewhere with Dexy's Midnight Runners, pondering a comeback?

But, I digress.

Four years ago does not seem like very long ago in some ways, but in other ways it does. All the while, we have spent more days together than days apart. That time, and the relationships with those who spent it with us, is the only lasting value I see in an experience like this.

To all those who have truly been a part of our guild experience, and all the others who have made this ride a fun one, thank you.
