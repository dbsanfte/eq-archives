SEP 19, 2002
Another night in ToV. Got off to a slow start, but then we cleared the Halls of Testing in record time. Drops were very light for plate, but the other classes were very happy. Loots: Chain BP [Shcammer], Leather Gloves [rotted], Silk Wrist [Chantress], Chain Helmet [Dreamthief], Chain Legs [Klaminar], White Symbol [Brewall], Leather Legs [for MQ], and a Silk Turban [Kestel].

After we zoned out of ToV, we realized that Escorpa was up, and with information consisting of 'has a drop I need', we decided to attack. On our first attempt, we had Escorpa down to under ten percent when it gated on us. We quickly recovered and pummelled Escorpa on the second attempt without losing a single person. Not bad for learning a mob on the fly, and testifies to the good players within the guild. Drops were a Talisman [Groovit] and a Book.
