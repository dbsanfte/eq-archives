The Qeynos badge quest is perhaps the most interesting and complex quests I've ever seen in the game.  For the first badge, you control an NPC pet, have him give you items, summon guards, and watch him interact with other NPCs. The second (get confession document before you leave Qeynos) is even better, you get to interrogate an NPC with a little fist-persuasion. Third step blew me away: you turn in item to spawn a gameboard and you then play othello using rats, diseased rats, and items you looted from an evil necro (see screenshot). Absolutely the most different quest you will ever do and I encourage people to try it out. Only problem with the quest is if you mess up, you have to start all over.
