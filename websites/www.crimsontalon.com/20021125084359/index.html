NOV 20, 2002
The dentist's of Crimson Talon paid a visit to Seblis for a tooth extraction this evening. Trakanon was complaining about a tooth ache and our perscription was death and subsequent removal of his teeth. Virtually everyone in the guild has completed their VP key now and the few remaining people are in the final steps.

After Seblis, we made our way to Chardok to pay the royals a visit. We hastily made our way through the dungeon and slayed the prince, king, and queen in short order. Excellent job by everyone on the fighting! A few turn-ins later, Hattrick finished his enchanter epic! Ciene is also now just a few shards away from a cleric epic as well.

NOV 19, 2002

NOV 18, 2002
Another foray into Veeshan's Peak. Stayed near the entrance with minimal number of people for chances at some rare spells. Once more have keys, we will be attempting penetrations much deeper into this dungeon.

NOV 14, 2002

NOV 12, 2002
Kael raid on Vindicator this evening and we easily slayed him with a minimal crew. Unlike other guilds that kill with sheer numbers or uber gear, we are able to accomplish tasks with teamwork and tactics. We obtained our first Sleeper's Tomb key in addition to a few other nice items. Definitely starting to move up the scale in boss mobs.

Killed Dozekar, which resulted in two tears; Emerald Tear [Wildfern] and Runed Tear [Ceeni].

OCT 28, 2002
Returned to Veeshan's Peak this evening to obtain spells. Grats to Barelil for obtaining Torpor! We also killed Silverwing for some other nice loots.

OCT 27, 2002
Fear was completely populated and we decided to clear the zone for some epic mobs. Cazic-Thule was up as well, and we made our first attempt on him - and won flawlessly. Among the loots this evening were: Tarrask (epic item), Chantress (robe), Humm (enc epic item), Zypheal (sk epic item).

Grats to Zypheal for completing the enchanter epic!

OCT 23, 2002
Dragon trifecta. Started the night out with Trakanon for some teeth and it was a tactically flawless kill without a single death. Then, we realized Gore was up. Even with the manaburn change we were able to kill her with minimal forces without a death. Then, rumors abound that Talendor was up. We moved over to Skyfire, and found him roaming about the zone. A few quick buffs and long pull later, he met his demise as well. 3 dragon kills in one evening for a dragon trifecta. Numerous drops: runed bolster belt [Stein], WDS Hide [Lumadin], Shield of the White Dragon [Sanalorn], Red Dragon Scale, Orb of Tishan, Gauntlets of Firey Might, 6 Spells.

Grats to Hawkien for completing the paladin epic!

OCT 18, 2002
Entry into Veeshan's Peak. We have worked hard to finish a large number of keys for VP, and we put them all to use for the first time this evening. Almost 30 of us entered the zone and we proceeded to learn and experiment within the zone. Overall, we had an enormous amount of fun doing something new and reaching this guild milestone. In addition to our information building, we managed to kill Silverwing and came away with some decent drops this evening: Scimitar of Lifestealing [Maxist], Robe of Smothering [Greymask], Hammer of Dragonborn [Khallin], Voice of the Berserker [Barelil], Word of Redemption [Aorthin], Sunstrike, Lure of Ice, Malo, Mala and a few other items. A great time was had by everyone.
