Bylaws



 

Etiquette
  • First off the simple stuff treat others fair. 
  • Help Others
    • We know it is not always possible to help people but one of the big things this guild was based on was helping each other out. No we will not force anyone to help others if they do not want to. But 9 times out of 10 if a guild mate is asking for help with something it will not take you long to do it. Remember one day when you are 6 zones away from your body with nothing on you who is going to be there to give you that TP or that res you need so bad or maybe even something simple as a SOW.
    • We try to keep most good items in the guild. Again this is not mandatory because everyone wants better equipment and that might take a long camp, lots of money or a trade. Just try and keep in mind the guild when you are tossing some of your old items out.
    • DO NOT BEG!!!! IF YOU BEG FOR ITEMS YOU ARE IN THE WRONG GUILD!!!! 

 

Guild chat
  • At this time guild chat is open chat please do not use it as an AUCTION block. You may say what ever is on your mind there just remember that everyone in the guild will see it.

Normal Grouping
  • You are responsible for finding your own group. At times people in the guild that you would like to group with might be full do not take it PERSONALLY. It has nothing to do with you and they are not turning there back on you.

Raids
All guild planned raids will be posted on Monday prier to the raid. Unscheduled raids are not subject to  of the raid rules.
  • Everyone of the appropriate level is expected to be at a guild planned raid if they are online (Levels will be posted with raid info).

    If you will be unable to attend the raid you need to let the raid leader know.  There are some raids where you will get no items and very little exp (IE Enchanters in Fear) but the reason behind a guild is to accomplish tasks that could not be done as a single group.  It is understandable if there is a real life situation, but camping for other items or exp is not acceptable.  Logging in late will not be an  acceptable excuse to miss the raid.  If you log in 15 or 20 min late you are expected to join the guild at the point they are in the raid.  Non-guild members that are in a zone we are raiding should be treated with fairness and respect.  Just because they are not in our guild does not mean we can abuse them.  If someone joins us from another guild they need to be made aware of our loot rules.

     

  • Loot Rules (For any raid that is held by the POI)
  • One item per raid (Uber spawns are a separate lotto IE Naggy>)
  • Quest items
    • Quest items go to the person of the appropriate class that have the largest number of quest pieces.  If no one is working on the quest the item will go to someone of the appropriate class.  At this point if still no one needs the item it will be zone lotto between the people who would like the item.
  • Armor, Weapons, and Misc. Items 
    • Will be lottoed between the people that CAN USE and WILL USE the item on their main character.  If no one CAN or WILL use the item in question then it will be a zone lotto item.
  • Spells
    • Spells will go to the person that are the closest level of the appropriate class if they have that spell then it will go to the next lowest.  If the class for the spell in question is not present or that class does not need it, it will be zone lotto for other casting classes.

 

 

Quests
  • One of the big things in the game now is the Epic Quests. If you need help with a quest put a post up on the message board or let Dedlelove know. 
  • Have all the information ready for the part you need help on. <don't have 30 people show up to kill a mob that will not spawn for another 12 hrs>
  • Don't expect everyone to drop what they are doing to take out a quest mob. If after you post on the message board that help is needed and you still can not get enough people let Deadlelove know to add it on the Monday raid list. Everyone wants there quest items so please help others because when the time comes you will want help too.

 

Leaving the Guild
  • If you make the choice to leave the guild then we wish you all the luck in the world. But you will not be welcomed back in to the guild.
