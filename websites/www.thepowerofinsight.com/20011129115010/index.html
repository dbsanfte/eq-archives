Ranks


Everyone has been wondering how they can move up in rank so I have decided to go ahead and list some of the info without all of the bylaws being finished.
Head Officer

To become a head officer of the guild you must either be appointed by myself or I will bring it to the guild for a vote. What I expect from the top officers is involvement in the game the guild and to further our cause.

Officer
This is someone that goes above the call of duty. Someone that is willing to help out others around them when every they can. This is give by vote or appointment.

Master of Beer

Sorry there can be only one and that is Tyni if for some reason he passes this spot will not be filled at all. This is also an officer.

Chancellor

This is by appointment only. It can be given by any one of the high-ranking officers. To obtain this rank we look at people that participate in the guild functions, meetings, and groups. This will be for the people that want to help the guild grow.

Vice-Chancellor

This will be the rank when you gain the knowledge of the 20th season. Is many cases you might skip this rank if you are one of the people who put time and effort in to the guild. To put it simple participation pays off.

As for apprentice everyone knows how you get this rank =)
If you have any questions please let me know.

11/16/2001 Posted by: Shtocoh
  Last night was a very big learning experience for me and I am sure many others that were with us on our little adventure into the frontier to find Midgard. There was a call for us to help locate the Midguard in our frontier. With only a force of about 16 Hella leading the pack, we began the search. I split off on the way to see if by chance I could locate them since we could cover more ground split up. We knew they were after a second keep for the night that is where the search began. One of the things I learned but it was not until the end of the night was I came across one keep with the doors opened up. Since I had really never been inside one I did not know that they were not suppose to be open and the gate keeper is the one that would teleport you in. Shortly after this I spotted two of the Midgard troops. It looks like they where scouting but couldd not be sure. Since they where only there for a minute and one was dead I did not stay at this keep very long. I started my move down to the Albiaon wall. At this point I did not see anyone but with in seconds I was dead as 4 Midguard crushed me. Hella and the other group came to res me shortly after. We decided to start heading towards the Midguard gate but we got separated. This is where I learned the most last night. We started to pull back to the Albiaon gate to regroup but right as we got there we went to battle. Most of our troops dropped only a hand full of us managed to escape back to a keep. At this point no one in the groups really has a clue what is going on. Hella did a great job trying to inform so many people of what was happening and where to go but still a few where lost. I think the biggest break down was in the group with Hella and I. With Hella leading they whole party it was hard for her to inform the group sometimes of what was going on. Trust me talking with many people in tells and then a chat group on top of your group can get very ruff. This was Raina's first time to the area so she was completely lost. That is where our problem came in not that Raina was new but that we did not give her the information she need to know what to do or where to go. I think this happen to a few people and even at times I was a little lost on what we were doing. Am I blaming anyone NO NO NO. Rather I am thanking Hella and Raina. Next time our guild goes in to battle and one of our group is leading it we know better how to get the information to everyone. The leader can not run the whole show sorry don't care how fast they can type you just can not talk to that many people at one time. Every group needs a leader of that group to inform them of what is going on. Even the group with the leader has to have someone in giving the group the information. Then all the leaders of the groups brought in to one chat so the leader can pass the information across one time with out having to send 20 tells. Also I think it would be a good idea to have any scout that is going out in to the field brought in to the chat group so they do not have to send tells but could inform all the group leaders of what is going on. Again I want to thank Hella for leading us and Raina for sticking it out with us.
