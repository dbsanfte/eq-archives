Overview

Here is the most recent information that I have.. again, if you have actually verified something different then what is here, please let me know!

Basically, here are the steps... I’ll describe them in passing here, and in detail later in the page:

  1. Get your components.
  2. Have a Master Brewer make your Temper.
  3. Try to make your cultural armor.
  4. Start with Step 1 again when you fail making it ;)

Each Temper is a bit different, and require 2 - 4 components that are foraged, brewed or dropped from mobs.

Assembly Instructions

It’s really pretty simple. Take all your components and give them to a brewer.  Make sure to use TWO of the components that need two.. it’s easy to forget.  He will put these components in a Brew Barrel, and combine. If he’s successful, you will have a completed temper.  The hard part is getting the components.

Making tempers is said to be trivial at 135 Brewing, which is pretty easy to get to.
