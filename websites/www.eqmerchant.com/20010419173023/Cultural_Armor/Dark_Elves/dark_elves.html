Teir’Dal Adamanatite Armor Overview

Here are the recipes for Dark Elven Adamanatite armor. If you find one of these recipes are not correct, please let me know. To create this armor, you MUST be a Dark elf, and you have to use the Teir’dal forge (at the Third Gate, loc 835, -1570). Fortunately, you don’t lose your components when you try an incorrect recipe (how I wish that was true for the rest of the forges), so you can experiment to your hearts content.  If you try an incorrect recipe, close the forge and reopen it and all your components should be in there.  The component recipes use neriak nectar instead of water, and most of the final recipes use shadow temper (which you can find about on the tempers page) and a Teir’dal smithy hammer.

Component Recipies

  • Adamanatite rings = large brick of adamanatite ore + file + neriak nectar
  • Adamanatite chain jointing = Adamanatite rings + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + file + neriak nectar (file is lost on the attempt, but the hammer is returned (assumed bug)
  • Folded sheet of Adamanatite = block of Adamanatite ore + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + neriak nectar
  • Sheet of Adamanatite = 2 small bricks of Adamanatite + neriak nectar

Note on enchanted adamanatite: An enchanter must enchant the large brick of adamanatite ore BEFORE you start working with it in the forge.  After the enchantment is done, working with the ore is done in the same way.  Enchanting metal does not restrict the diety of who can use that item.

Adamanatite chainmail

Adamanatite chainmail piece = appropriate chain pattern + appropriate number of adamanatite rings + silk swatch + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + shadow temper

  • veils, bracelets and neckguards = 1 set of Adamanatite rings
  • Coifs, sleeves, mantles, skirts and gloves = 2 sets of Adamanatite rings
  • Tunics, capes and leggings = 3 sets of Adamanatite rings
  • There is no known boot recipe

Enchanted adamanatite chainmail

Enchanted adamanatite chainmail piece = appropriate chain pattern + appropriate number of enchanted adamanatite rings + silk swatch + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + shadow temper

  • veils, bracelets and neckguards = 1 set of Enchanted adamanatite rings
  • Coifs, sleeves, mantles, skirts and gloves = 2 sets of Enchanted adamanatite rings
  • Tunics, capes and leggings = 3 sets of Enchanted adamanatite rings
  • There is no known boot recipe

Adamanatite platemail

Adamanatite platemail = appropriate small plate section mold + appropriate number of folded sheets of adamanatite + adamanatite chain jointing + leather padding + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + shadow temper

  • Visors, collars, bracers and boots = 1 Folded sheet of adamanatite
  • Helms, pauldrons, girdles, vambraces and gauntlets = 2 Folded sheets of adamanatite
  • Breastplates, greaves and cloaks = 3 Folded sheets of adamanatite

Stats:

  • Tunic: 20 AC, 5.2 weight (deep blue in color)
  • Arms: 11 AC, 3.4 weight

NOTE:  For the imbued versions of adamanatite weapons and shields, USE Moonlight temper instead of neriak nectar and add ONE imbued gem.

Adamanatite weapons

  • indigo sabre = curved blade mold + hilt mold + pommel mold + 1 folded sheet of adamanatite + neriak nectar
    • Stats: 7 dam, 24 delay
    • Can be imbued with: (add imbued gem and change neriak nectar to shadow temper)
      • Imbued Sapphire - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Innorruuk.
        • Adds +3 str, +3 int

Adamanatite shields

  • dragoon shield = medium kite shield mold + 2 sheets of adamanatite + Teir'Dal smithy hammer + shadow temper
    • Stats: 11 AC, +2 sta, 10 weight, ROG, WAR, CLR, SHD only
    • Can be imbued with: (add imbued gem)
      • Imbued Sapphire - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Innorruuk.
        • Adds: +2 int, +2 str
