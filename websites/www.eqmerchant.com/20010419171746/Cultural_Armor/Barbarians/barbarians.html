Northman Smithing Overview

To create this armor, you MUST be a Barbarian, and you have to use either a regular forge, or the Northman Forge (located outside McPherson’s bloody blades). Fortunately, you don’t lose your components when you try an incorrect recipe (how I wish that was true for the rest of the forges), so you can experiment to your hearts content.  If you try an incorrect recipe, close the forge and reopen it and all your components should be in there.  The component recipes use water, just like normal smithing, and some of the final recipes use water, and some of them use frost temper (which you can find about on the tempers page) and an regular smithy hammer.

NOTE: Be very careful about your forges (which one to use) and whether to use frost temper or water. You WILL get back your components if you incorrectly try to combine with the wrong recipe in the northman cultural forge, but you WON’T in the normal forge.  In general, all the finished products (ringmail, shields, and weapons) and the rings use the northman forge, and the other two medium quality sheet components use the normal forge.

Component Recipies

In a normal forge:

  • Medium Quality folded sheet of metal = block of medium quality ore + flask of water
  • Medium Quality sheet of metal = 2 small bricks of medium quality ore + flask of water

In a Northman cultural forge:

  • Medium Quality metal rings = large brick of ore + file + flask of water  (NOTE: everything I have seen says “Large brick of ore” NOT “Large brick of Medium quality ore” - HOWEVER, logically, I just don’t see how you could make medium quality rings out of normal ore. If someone knows which way it is, PLEASE e-mail me!

Northman ringmail

NOTE:  For the large leather armor pieces called for in the ringmail recipies, it is very important to that you use a storebought leather piece. The tailor made version will not work.

In a Northman cultural forge:

Northman ringmail piece = appropriate large chainmail pattern + appropriate number of medium quality rings + appropriate piece of store bought large leather + regulary smithy hammer + frost temper

  • veils (mask), bracelets (bracers) and neckguards (gorget) = 1 set of medium quality rings
  • Coifs (helm), sleeves, mantles (shoulderpads), skirts (belt) and gloves = 2 sets of medium quality rings
  • Tunics, capes (cloak) and leggings = 3 sets of medium quality rings
  • There is no known boot recipe

Imbued Northman ringmail

The recipe is the SAME as Northman ringmail, except you add ONE imbued gem to the recipe.

The available imbued gems that I know about are:

  • Imbued Jade - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Rallos Zek.
  • Imbued Ivory - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of the Tribunal
    • Adds to each piece: +1str, +2wis, +2 save vs. cold

Northman weapons

In a Northman cultural forge:

NOTE:  For the imbued versions USE Frost temper instead of water and add ONE imbued gem.

  • Northman Long Dagger = Dagger Blade Mold + Hilt Mold + Pommel Mold + Medium Quality Sheet of Metal + Flask of Water
    • Stats: 6 dam, 24 delay
    • Can be imbued with:
      • Imbued Jade - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Rallos Zek.
      • Imbued Ivory - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of the Tribunal
  • Northman Claymore = Dual Edged Blade Mold + Hilt Mold + Pommel Mold + Medium Quality Folded Sheet of Metal + Flask of Water
    • Stats: 14 dam, 44 delay, 11.5 weight
    • Can be imbued with:
      • Imbued Jade - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Rallos Zek.
      • Imbued Ivory - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of the Tribunal
        • Adds +4 sta, +2 save vs cold
  • Northman Battle Axe = Axe Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Folded Sheet of Metal + Flask of Water
    • Stats: 16 dam, 48 delay, 15 weight
    • Can be imbued with:
      • Imbued Jade - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Rallos Zek.
      • Imbued Ivory - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of the Tribunal
        • Adds +4 sta, +2 save vs cold
  • Northman Throwing Axe = Throwing Axe Mold + Medium Quality Sheet of Metal + Flask of Water
    • Stats: 8 dam, 38 delay
    • Recipe yields one
    • Thrown
    • Some places say it cannot be imbued, some say it can.  I lean towards it NOT being able to be imbued... besides... who would waste a frost temper on a thrown weapon?
  • Northman Mace = Mace Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Sheet of Metal + Flask of Water
    • Stats: 9 dam, 36 delay, 8.5 weight
    • Fits in Ranged slot
    • Can be imbued with:
      • Imbued Jade - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of Rallos Zek.
      • Imbued Ivory - This will restrict the finished armor to followers of the Tribunal
        • Adds +4 sta, +2 wis
