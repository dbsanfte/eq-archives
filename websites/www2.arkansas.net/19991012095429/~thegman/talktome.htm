WE AT THE LAIR NEED HELP!

 

We are looking for help from you on three fronts.

  • Submissions

    • If you have general strategy tips or have found a good place to hunt, let us know.  We offer full credit to all authors.  Please list the subject as "submission."

  • Maps

    • We are looking for maps and write ups on your favorite zones.  Zone descriptions are subject to editing for better flow and appearance but full credit is given to the author.   Please list the subject as "zone" and attach the map in JPEG format.

  • Like It Is

    • Though this is an editorial section we want to hear from you.  If you are a Necromancer of the 9th circle (Level 34) or better and you have an opinion on issues facing our brothers,  then you tell it LIKE IT IS and we will publish it. Please list the  subject as Like It Is and we will take a look.  We will obviously edit for content so please no heavy profanity.   Full credit given to the author.

  • Screenshots  

    • We need your screenshots.   Please list "SS" as the subject and attach it in JPEG format.  I give full credit for shots taken.  If you have a caption you want, please include it.
