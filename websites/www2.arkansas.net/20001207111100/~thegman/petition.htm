OUR PETITION

Dear Verant:

   We, The Dark Brotherhood, Necromancers of Norrath, do hearby request the following changes to our class.  We are not attempting to become a super class but to merely stop the bleeding.  Our class continues to be a target of changes and as such has fallen out of balance with the other pure casters.  We feel these changes will better that balance and put us on an even playing field.  I will attempt to explain the logic behind these requests as I make them.

 1)  Research needs to be revamped.  Words for a rank of spells should be on monsters of the levels before that rank.

Currently some of our most powerful spells are by research only.  Obviously our pet is our most powerful spell and at current few necromancers have attained the Words for these pets.  The other classes do not have to research their most useful spells and Magicians can buy all of their pets to level 49.   We feel this places us at an extreme disadvantage.  A quest has been added to help us get our pet Words but to get level 44 quest components we must kill a level 49 NPC and to get the level 49 quest components, we must enter the Plane of Fear.  We feel that once we have earned a level we should already have the words and a chance to do the research.  At current a Word has a chance to load on a NPC the level of the rank though the level lower than the next rank (i.e. a L34 word could be on a NPC L34-38).   We propose that they should start having a chance to load the rank before (i.e. a L34 word could be on a NPC L29-33).

2)  Our resistance's should be the same as other casters. 

    Our resistance level for our spells is entirely too high.  Darkness resists more than a third of the time (35%) and this is unacceptable.  We propose a resistance of 18% chance on equal NPC's.  Less as the NPC's level gets lower and more as it gets higher to a 100% resist at six levels the same as Wizard's.

 

3)  Don't touch our pets and make them available at vendors. 

   We Necromancers have faced the turmoil's of life in Norrath from our birth.  We have paid the price in our social "stigma" many times.  Deaths due to NPC's that hate our class and believe me there are a ton of them out there.  The reward has always been raw power.   Well, I have seen the risk and the reward, I thought,  was a tremendous pet at levels 39, 44 and 49.  Now that many of us are finishing the journey and have earned the reward, the pet components for research are too rare and if we do get them they have been weakened.  If you have a problem with melee classes then strengthen them, do not weaken our pet.  We propose that you return the pets to their pre-patch level immediately and either increase the load rate for research components (see request #1)   or make them available at vendors as magician pets are.

4)  We should have the gate spells. 

    This only makes sense based on the back story of Everquest.  According to their own history the first necromancer, Miragul, was both a Wizard and a Necromancer.  If this is the case it makes sense that he would have had full knowledge of all the Wizard spells.  Since others have been jealous of our power for the ages and seek to destroy us on sight, I know our forefathers would have sought methods of safer travel.  The gates are the answer.  Making us able to gate ourselves around Norrath would aid in our travels and counter act the disadvantages we have to live with from our choice of class.  Once again I do not want to be a super class, these gates should be caster only and not group portals.

5)  We need some Direct Damage.

   Though we are a great soloing class there is only so far you can go on your own.  Our damage over time spells work well in solo combats but aren't worth much in groups.  You don't have 4 minutes to finish of a mad froglok in Lower Guk.  We need a direct damage spell that will make us more appealing to a group.  I go into a dungeon and shout level 44 caster looking for a group and I get a ton of replies, I shout level 44 necromancer looking for a group and it is silence.    The dungeons are where our items and research components are and we need to be made viable to groups that roam them.  A lot of you will say Ignite Bones is 495 points of direct damage and I say, wait to you have it.  It is resisted more than the darkness line.  We propose a more dependable direct damage spell or to lower the effect time on Scourge, Boil Blood and Heart Flutter.

    We feel these changes will be effective in returning us to a viable class that will be sought after and welcomed in groups throughout Norrath. 

Thank You,

The Dark Brotherhood
