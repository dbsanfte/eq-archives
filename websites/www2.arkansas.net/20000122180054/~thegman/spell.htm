FEATURED SPELLS

IGNITE BONES   BOIL BLOOD   CASCADING DARKNESS

Ignite Bones
Level: 44
Mana Cost: 210
Research Only:    No - Available at the guilds.

Necromancer direct damage.  We asked for it and we got it.  This spell dishes out 495 points of damage.  It also stuns the target for three seconds and turns them into a skeleton.  A lot of people asked what the purpose of turning them into a skeleton was, it gives the target those qualities.  Like a skeleton, the target has no magical resistance and no armor class.  Now your pet can hit for max damage and your spells get in.  This power comes at a cost, it is slow casting and highly resisted.  I am not real sure what the exact rate is but it is up there.   It doesnt take but one resist for this spell to become a liability.  Requiring 210 mana means that you will soon find yourself out of mana having done no damage with just a few resists.  I do not have exact data yet from Verant but the general feeling is that the resist rate was lowered on the last patch.  I myself have noticed that it is sticking a lot more.  I look forward to getting hard numbers from Verant soon so we can know for sure.  Even with the resists this is a great finishing spell when all goes well.


Boil Blood
Level: 29
Mana Cost: 150
Research Only:    No - Available at the guilds.

This is a necromancer staple spell.  One of Lotar's must haves'.  This is a very mana efficient DoT that does aproximatel 500 points of damage for 150 mana.   3.3/1 is a good ratio.  It is also very stable, I very rarely see Boil Blood resisted except in unique situatuions (i.e. the Kobalds in Solusek B seem to have a very high resistance to fire.....imagine that).  It also has a good duration, aprx. 90 secs which means it is condusive to group situations.  An all around great spell, buy your copy today.


Cascading Darkness
Level: 49
Mana Cost: 300
Research Only:    No - Available at the guilds

The last in our Darkness line of spells.  This one is worth the wait.   The slowing effect takes a NPC to below a walking speed.  It's damage is 1600 and it has a good duration of 90 seconds.  When I started looking at these kind of numbers, I finally saw part of the reward for all the greif we have endured getting there.
