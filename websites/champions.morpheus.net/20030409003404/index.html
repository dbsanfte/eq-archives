Monday, April 7

  • Ardecki got the AA points needed for Endless Quiver.                     WTG Ranger Lord!!

  • PoV and PoS flags were obtained by Skyeshadow, Henelan, and Tuven.

  • Also Jinudan, Aeguvas, Skyeshadow, and Drugan were flagged for BoT.   It's just been a flag-waving kind of weekend.  CoNgratulations to all those with new flags and to all who supported them!

Sunday, April 6

  • Please CoNgratulate Neander on obtaining his flag for Plane of Valor!! He obtained the flag late last week with the help of some fellow guild mates and friends.

  • On Friday, we took down Zlandicar for a FOURTH time!  WOOOHOOO!!  He dropped several nice items and one ST key part. GREAT TEAMWORK CHAMPS!!

  • Last week Tuven also dinged !   WTG Tuven!

Friday, April 4

We've had a VERY good week, both in ToV and the Plane of Hate.  

  • If I counted correctly, there were 9 drops for Champions in ToV, with 4 more pieces being held for future MQ-ing.  Lucky takers were Aspenwyd, Tealke(2), Tslaneesta, Aislada, Jinudan, Edhelorn, Hyppy, and Skyeshadow.  We Champions used some interesting tactics to "size up" our foes.  

  • In Hate, 2 Shattered Emerald of Corruption dropped -- one for Skyeshadow and one for Aspenwyd.  Also a number of cleric pieces (WOOT for Ameritlai and Maegwina) and numerous items for PoK experience turn-ins were also collected.  We made such a terrifying force in the Plane that Innoruuk closed up shop and went into hiding just as we approached the church.

Thursday, April 3

  • Don't forget Hate is tonite !!                                                       Change in start time to 8pm US Eastern from WC.

  • Guild members -- Pls note that the RTA, Epic Quest, and Raid Request forums have been moved to a new spot on the boards.

Wednesday April 2

  • Big CoNgratulations to Sheela and Dwaelin who obtained their epics !!

  • Tuesday Trial Update - "Thanks to Skyeshadow, Tealke, Bahh, Laganed, Deamian and others who made it a good night..just wish we had been more succesful." -- Talmin
