Tuesday, June 3

We Champions ventured into the new Plane of Hate on Sunday in search of epic pieces and to check out the new zone.  Here are the reviews from our 'on-the-scene reporters':

  • Maegwin -- "I just wanted to thank Serril for his leadership, money and enthusiasm for the hate raid today. You made it happen and that is saying a lot. Sometimes it isn't easy but I want you to know that it is appreciated. I had a great time. I got quite a bit of EXP and saw a new zone with my friends. Thank you for the opportunity =)"

  • Drugan -- "That was a great job up there in a totally unexplored zone. Tslan, great pulling, and Aar and Talon great tanking....It was definitely a fun time in a new/old zone..... It was DEFINITE overkill out there with 4 groups. The only thing that managed to take us down was a train of named and higher level mobs, and even then we took a few down with us..."

  • Shaenathera -- "Thanks to all who made this possible! Hate looks really cool now, and the fact that it is 'roomier' seemed to make it less of a chore to pull, although not being a puller, I'm not sure. Just seemed we had less trouble with adds than before."

  • Serril -- "When I was getting smacked around by mobs.. I noticed they're still no where near as hard hitting as PoP mobs... And, wow, it seems so much easier to pull now....I don't even think a full group is needed at zone in..."

  • Aaranon -- "The graphics are truly impressive now. Large broadways, courtyards, stairs, fountains and a very large cathedral are all nice eye candy. The buildings are HUGE and look like real structures, not tinker toys. There are chairs, books, beds and all kinds of enhanced graphics throughout the zone."

  • Tslaneesta -- "Have to say I had a blast yesterday. The new hate is a pullers dream. Large open spaces to camp the raid/group, much lower aggro ranges, except for one mob there was little chain aggro, .... The graphics and little enhancements are amazing."

  • Callous -- "I also wanted to thank everyone for the great raid. I will go to Hate ANYTIME.....Only need that stoopid Emerald........Thanks Serril, Maegwin, Tslan...and all the other folks."
