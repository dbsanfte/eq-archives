Monsters

Thursday, October 9

Our raid in Plane of Storms last night was a success.  Woohoo! We are on a roll !!     All present were able to get their missing giant heads and beards, plus a few medallions dropped and an ethereal parchment.  After one wipe-out, we took out two named mobs - Paruek the Strong and Faruek the Bold.  

Nice comeback Champs!

  

Tuesday, October 7

On Monday night CoN RAID kills Inny....Dead!!   That is the Nadox version of Innoruuk, as our pics below show:

     

Thanks to all for coming out and mobilizing very quickly.  Members were gathered in PoK on time and we made the final approach to Inny at an hour past scheduled raid start time.  WTG Champions!!! 

Monday, October 6

  • Ring War - Saturday 10/4

We had a second successful Ring War; this time for Carin.  Thanks to all who assisted with this hunt and made it all possible.  Narandi dropped a Lance, Mask, Choker, Earring, Eye, and Crown that were awarded using the new CoNPoint system.

     

A special thank you to Skytalon for MTing, Aaranon for Raid Leading, Mellilu for doing groups and ports, Ardecki for scouting incoming giants, Tomal for checking if all giants were dead on each wave, all looters for keeping the field clean, and Tslan for doing final loots.

  • Severilous - Friday 10/3

Severilous never had a chance.  Great turnout for this quick event -- Sev was down 18 minutes after the scheduled start time!    Boldwind (my alt) was awarded the green scale for her epic --- WOOOOHOOO! 

Thanks and group hugz to you all!

  • Ssra - Thursday 10/2

We had a successful raid in Ssra last week, although Legends of Marr was doing the cycle so we did not get to attempt either Rhag mob.  We broke into groups and hunted on separate floors.  However, we were able to provide backup support to each other by using a common chat channel -- GREAT TEAMWORK on that EVERYONE!!  Even a zone re-pop (thank you very little SOE) was not enough to daunt us from trying a named mob in the basement as a grand finale.  Unfortunately the mob heard us coming and poofed....
