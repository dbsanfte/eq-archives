    April 16, 2002 - TROMPEN GETS EPIC!

  • Talk about a totally awesome day. Coming straight off of a pretty solid weekend, we basically went straight into hitting a gold mine. And such a HUGE turnout to help take down Ragefire. I'll let Torquil tell us exactly what happened

    Ragefire - 4/16/02 10:07am est Rage popped minutes before Trompen was to log for work. We made a few tells to various guilds: Tindi, NB, FK, BotS, asking to come and assist. 43 people were in zone when we engaged the evil beast with more en route. The server response and support was truly something awesome to witness. Obviously overkill, the battle lasted just over a minute. We engaged at 10:51am est and 1 minute later a dead dragon lie at our feet. Heart went to Trompen, Helm went to Sheanna, and there are 2 dragon bags to be awarded yet
