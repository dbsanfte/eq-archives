    Member News:

  • UPDATE! Shaenathera has received the final word on the status of her bugged epic turn-in.

    •       ...And it's a big, hearty, 'IT SUCKS TO BE YOU' from Verant. Tuven received notification via email that they would not be reimbursing him for the lost staff and note needed to complete this, and lots of consoling, empty phrases, with frequent use of the word 'regrettable'. Although Multi Questing in the game is possible, they do not support it (sound logic there). Too bad, so sad.

    So, what I need to do to complete this requires the Broken Golem to be up in Fear, not a very common thing. Taking it down isn't so hard, when Tuven got this, they had ten characters present. I have to hand in a gear to the Broken Golem (I have the gear), and it spawns an enraged golem, which is what we kill. Then I loot some Oil and a note from it, and hand the oil in to a gnome in Butcherblock. He hands me the last staff, then I go to Halas and hand the note to an npc, which spawns the real npc I need. I give him all three staves, and he gives me a bag. Then I go to the Temple of Ro and hand in the bag, and ta da...epic is returned (SUPPOSEDLY. God knows what will happen...).

    So, as you can see, once I get the Broken Golem/enraged golem thing down, the quest is virtually over. Maybe half an hour of running around, tops.

    The trick is to get the Broken Golem....
