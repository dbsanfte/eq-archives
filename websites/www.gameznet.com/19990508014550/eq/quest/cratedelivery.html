Everquest Quest Guide: Crate Delivery

This quest takes place near the Qeynos gates.

NPCs involved: Nesiff something, located in Wood Shop near Clock Tower in South Qeynos. Guard Welethin located near North Gate

Hail the guard.  Eventually you'll have to say "I will return the crate."   He'll give you a crate.  Return it to Nesiff, he'll give you some invoices.   Return them to the Guard.

Results: some EXP and faction adjustments
