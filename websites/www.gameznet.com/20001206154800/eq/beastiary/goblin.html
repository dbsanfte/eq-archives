Goblin
Goblins can be found all over Norrath. There are as many varieties of goblin as as there are environment types. These creatures live a communal life so rarely, if ever, will you find goblins wandering far from more goblins. For this reason it is important to use judgement before engaging, as they will gather any reinforcements they can. Goblins, although aggressive by nature,  are cunning and know instinctively when they are on the losing end of a battle. They run easily and will not generally attack a player who cons more than a few levels higher than them, but always use caution.  Its best to test the waters slowly when entering what appears to be lowby gob territory. Goblins are adept in primitive magic, with many varieties of caster.

Some common goblin clans and types:

Butcher Block: whelp, shaman, wizard, etc

Misty Thicket: more various low level types

PermaFrost: (ice) shaman, wizard, priest, elite guard, warrior, many more.

Dagnar's Cauldron: (aqua) shaman, mariner, boson, tidal lord.

Lavastorm: (fire) runner, scout, warrior, shaman

Solusek A: (fire, inferno, solusek) shaman, priest, wizard, high priest, champion, etc)

Runnyeye: (dazed) shaman, guard, worker, (elite) guard, warlord, king, bartender, etc. Ranges from low teen to mid twenties.

There are more clans and many more individual types than listed, scattered all over Norrath. Beware, goblins seem weak as individuals, yet are known to come out of the woodwork when least expected. I've had entire parties wiped out or forced to flee for our lives by what started as a simple, solitary pull. Goblins carry various (level dependent) items and loot, ranging from copper to high level lore items.
