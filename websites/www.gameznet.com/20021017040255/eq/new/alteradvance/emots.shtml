Emot Commands


On this page you will find information on creating various emots in the form of chat lines in the game. 

/command is how you perform emots.  Just like you sent a /tell you use the "/" command to trigger an emotion by follow it with a certain word.

There are various ways you can do this.  You can create lines about another player's pet, weave in a "him" or "her" into you emotion and even mention the race of what you are refering to.  These lines can be added to your /command by using these guidlines:


%M – Returns the character’s pet's name.
%O - Returns the objective gender for the target (Him, Her, It).
%P - Returns the possessive gender for the target (His, Her, Its).
%R - Returns the race of the target.
%S - Returns the gender for the target (He, She, It).
%T - Returns the current target. 

Also you can type in an emotion that does not fall in the list below by using the /em command.

For instance, if I wanted to say how Gnomes are the other white meat, I would say it this way: Target a Gnome friend and type this.

/em thinks %R is the other white meat!

(Pixie thinks Gnome is the other white meat!)

or I can do this:

/smile at %T and tell's %P that %R is the other white meat!

(Pixie smiles at Shortstuff and tell's her that Gnome is the other white meat!)
