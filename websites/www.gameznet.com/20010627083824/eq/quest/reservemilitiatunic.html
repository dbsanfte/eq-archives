Reserve Militia Tunic
This quest was user-submitted. It was submitted by Akanz Ebonwolfe.

This quest takes place in WC and West Freeport.  To start the quest you must have a very good faction the the Freeport Militia.  If you don't Guard Valon will not give you the quest.  Go to the toll booth in West Commons and hail Guard Valon.  He will tell you to bring him orc picks, so bring him 4 orc picks.  He will then tell you about killing Dervish Cutthroats, and if you faction is good enough he will give you a bag to put 8 dervish cutthroat rings in it.  If your faciton isn't good enough he won't giv eyou the bag.  When you get the bag just put 8 rings in it and hit combine, then give the new bag back to Guard Valon.  He will give you a token.  Take this token back to the Militia house in freeport.  Go upstairs and you will find some guy who's name is Armorer... something i can't remember the name, but he is the only guy that has the first name of Armorer.  Anyway give him teh tunic and he will give you the freeport milita tunic which is 9ac +3dex.  The stats aren't all that great, but it looks like a very bright red banded tunic when worn, and has a very cool look to it.
