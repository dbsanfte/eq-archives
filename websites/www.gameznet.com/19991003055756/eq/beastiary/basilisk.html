Basilisk
Basilisks are a race of powerful lizards which frequent Butcherblock and Rathe Mountains. Basilisks are dangerous not only because of their powerful jaws but also because of the fact that they can paralyze their prey. This can be very dangerous especially for casters who have low hit points. The lowland basilisks of Butcherblock range between levels 7-9 but the ones in Rathe can be quite a bit tougher. Basilisks generally do not carry any loot save maybe a chunk of meat. Also worthy of mention are the lava basilisks of Lavastorm, which range in the low to mid teen levels, and often carry basilisk eggs.
