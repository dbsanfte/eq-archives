Everquest Zone Guide: Upper Guk

Upper Guk is the home of the Froglocks the hated enemies of the trolls and ogres. The entrance is a cave in the Innothule Swamp.

This dungeon is targetted for players 5-25 and is absoleutely huge. The prized possession of this zone is the many magical items that can be found here. These include woven, netted and mesh amor. You can also find troll hide belts, ghoulbane, elemental summoning bowls etc. This zone is very exotic and has a feel that is all its own.

The better magic items can be found on named NPC's such as the ritualist, lord, scryer etc. Other types of monstes that can be found in this zone are crocodiles(including high level ancient crocs), a dark elf quest NPC and reportedly a young dragon!
