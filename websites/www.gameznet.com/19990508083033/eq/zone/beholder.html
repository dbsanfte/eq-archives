Everquest Zone Guide: Beholder

Beholder is a maze like gorge that lies between Runnyeye and East Karana. If you hug the left wall coming out of Runnyeye you will be led directly to East Karana. Likewise if you hug the right wall coming from East Karana you will come accross Runnyeye.

There are a few goblins here guarding the Runnyeye entrance. Deeper inside of the maze you will also find giant snakes. There are also mud slinging(and thus blinding) muddites in this zone. Muddites are highly prized for their ore and clay which is used for trade skills.

The true prize of this zone however are the minotaurs. There is a location known as minotaur alley where 7 different minotaurs spawn. There are also minotaurs patrolling the zone. These minotaurs vary in range from the level 9-13 lesser minotaurs to the level 23-25 chasm minotaurs.

Chasm minotaurs are not the most difficult beasts in this zone however as the evil eyes will keep you on your toes.
