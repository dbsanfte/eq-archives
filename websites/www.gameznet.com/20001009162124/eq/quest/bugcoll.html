Everquest Quest Guide: Bug Collection

The following quest was submitted by Rayvin:

In Misty Thicket, if you talk to Blixxin Entopop (he wanders around with a little klicknic trailing him named Ember), he will tell you about his bug collection.  If you ask him about it, he gives you the opportunity to make one yourself, and gives you a Bug Collection Box.  If you ask him, he will give you the list of things that go in the bug collection.

These things are: Fire Beetle Eye, Spiderling Eye, Spiderling Leg, Giant Fire Beetle Leg, Giant Wasp Wing, Giant Scarab Egg Sac.  If you get all 6 things in the Bug Collection Box and hit combine, you get the complete Bug Collection.  Give your Bug Collection to Blixxin Entopop for your reward.
