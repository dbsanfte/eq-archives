Everquest Quest Guide: Crushbone Slaves

This quest was sent forth by Ben:

This quest takes place inside of Crushbone. One the slavers you will occasionally find shackle keys. If you give these keys to one of the slaves they will be set free. Setting a slave free will raise your faction with that slaves race and it will also grant a bit of experience.

Here is some info on which key goes to which slave.

?- Not Personally confirmed, but was told or used deduction.
*- The gnomes are a guess. I have seen 14 and 15 keys, but never a slave to match. I assume it is a gnome to fit with the surrounding population.
