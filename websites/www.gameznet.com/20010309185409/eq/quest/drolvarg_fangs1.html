Drolvarg Fangs Quest

Submitted by: Akaran DeHawk

Captain Nealith in the warriors guild of Firiona Vie needs someone to [join the defense of the outpost]. Tell him that you will and he will assign you to seek out four drolvarg teeth. Drolvargs can be found in a variety of zones including Firiona Vie and the Dreadlands. 

Return to him with four teeth and you will receive some "Elven Trail Mix"(food), minor experience and some change. You will also receive the following faction adjustments:

Positive faction with: Firiona Vie, Emerald Warriors, Stormguard.
Negative faction with: Legion of Cabilis, Pirates of Gunthak.
