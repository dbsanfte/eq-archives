Everquest Zone Guide: Qeynos

Qeynos is the most famous of the human cities. It spreads accross a couple of large zones(as well as an underground region). Qeynos leads to the zone of Qeynos Hills as well as containing the docks to the erudite continent. This city has perhaps more quests than any other city in the game so it should not be overlooked. A couple example of Qeynos quests include the Gnoll Fang Quest and the Monk Headband Quests.

The newbie stomping grounds here contain the standard newbie opponents of bats, rats, snakes and fire beetles. You will also find gnoll pups and decaying skeletons.

The newbie region is not without it's danger however as Fippy Darkpaw(an angry level 4-5 gnoll), Holinix Darkpaw(another angry gnoll but this one is a caster) and the Queen Kliknik make an occasional appearance.
