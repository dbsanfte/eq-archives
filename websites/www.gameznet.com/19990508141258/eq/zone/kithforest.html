Everquest Zone Guide: Kithicor Forest

The Kith forest is sandwiched between the west commanlands and rivervale. It also connects to the border town of High Hold. The zone is mostly barren but you will find the occasional bixie, gnoll, bear or werewolf. This zone is also contains several quest NPC's so it should not be overlooked when exploring.
