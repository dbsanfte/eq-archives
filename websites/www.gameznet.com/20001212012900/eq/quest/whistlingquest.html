Robe of the Whistling Fists Quest
This quest was submitted by Taurean Fistfall of Druzzil Ro.

The quest requires the following items:

Robe of the Lost Circle (Obtained via another quest)
Metal Pipe off of an Iksar Betrayer in Chardok (Identifies as Fi)
Metal Pipe off of an Drolvarg Pawduster in Karnors (Identifies as Zan)

Hand these items in to Brother Balatin in The Dreadlands and he will give you the Robe of the Whistling Fists.
