Iksar



Iksars are the lizardmen of EverQuest. They have unique abilities because of this, namely the use of their tails in monk melee. They are considered an evil race and can have a hard time venturing the lands safely.

The Iksar race can be a beastlord, monk, necromancer, shadowknight, shaman or warrior. They make wonderful shamans, starting out with the highest base wisdom of all the shaman races.
