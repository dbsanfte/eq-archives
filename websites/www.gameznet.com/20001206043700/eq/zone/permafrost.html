Permafrost
Permafrost is a level 15+ dungeon which lies in the rigid region of Everfrost. The entrance is guarded by several Ice Giants to scare off any newbies who might wander in here unknowingly.

Inside the dungeon is a bit more tame however. This dungeon is not only the home of the ice goblins but the home of the Ice Giants as well. The entranace area is good for players level 15-30. However after you get past the goblin king and high shaman you will be presented with two huge doors. Beyond this lies the high level portion of the dungeon. Beyond this point you will encounter many Ice Giants and things could get ugly.

Other miscellaneous monsters in this dungeon include giant timber wolves.
