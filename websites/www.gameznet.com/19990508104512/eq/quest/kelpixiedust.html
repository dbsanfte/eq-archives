Everquest Quest Guide: Pixie Dust

The following quest was submitted by Negolwen:

Go to the ranger guild in Kelethin talk to Ran (one of the trainers). Hail her ask about the pixie tricksters, then tell her you will exterminate the pixies. She will give you a bag, that you can fill with pixie dust found in lesser faydark near the brownie village. Return the bag full and combined and you receive experience a small amount of money (few coppers) and a common item (Rusty Dagger, Copper Ring....etc.).
