Everquest Beastiary Guide: Ghoul

Ghouls are one of the more common forms of undead. They can be found in many zones of Norrath including Befallen, Unrest, and the Oasis Of Marr. Ghouls range from level 13-15. They are desired for their good loot and the fact that they are undead which makes them a favorite target amongst clerics and necromancers.

They are definately not without their share of dangers however. Ghouls require magic to be hit. They also can root you with a successful hit which means no escape for the weary.
