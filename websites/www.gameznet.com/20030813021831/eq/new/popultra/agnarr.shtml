Strategies to Defeat Agnarr The Storm Lord

Strategy One: 

Break-In Strat - Once you have the key to the Lair the key unlocks the Lightning Portal when you step on portal by any of the wing bosses. This unlocks it for anyone that is there and clicks on it for a certain amount of time/ Does not have to be in raid or group. 

Once you are inside you port into a room that has 3 portal sphere's 1 left, 1 right and 1 where you are standing. Each portal sphere is gaurded by a Firestorm Portal which con Green to me at 65. These Spheres are unusable at the time so not completely sure they are portals or eye candy.

Evyn Firestorm (red at 65) will be standing in front of you across the room when you zone in. He is gaurded by 2 Firestorm Elementals (blue con at 65)
Storm Giant Gaurdian Blue con at 65- however this mob is invisble. I could change views throu out the room and target him. but not visiually.

You zone in to room and you get aggro the Guardian off the bat. The Firestorm Portals don't see invis but the guardian will and he will attack you. once he attacks the portals will attack and you will get aggro from Evyn Firestorm and his 2 body gaurds.

After you zone into this room there is only one way out either win or go sit in GY no other way.

Evyn casts a DoT Fire Wind that is 20 range PBAE and is a 300 hp per tick DOT/ not sure if someone said it was resistable or not but I would assume a high FR would be the check on this. 

Dire Charming some of the Add mobs helps.  Once you get the first area cleared, you can move up to the next level by saying a trigger like to Askr.  There are four levels to reach the final battle.  The trigger line to move up to the next level is "transport me".  Each level has a similar battle.  The next level after you kill all NPC's requires you target a "Chaotic Vortex" and hit the "U" key to unlock it.  This mob is hard to target.  Be ready for another battle right away on the next level (3rd).

The final battle requires that you kill Agnar the Stormlord.  Basically he will spawn off of him named NPC's depending on his life bar percentage.  Have a second group of palyers ready to engage the named adds.  They are:

Jolur Sandstorm at 100% life of Agnarr left

Ekil Thundercall at 75% life of Agnarr left

Olijin Stormtide at 50% life of Agnarr left 

Hibdin Cyclone at 25% life of Agnarr left

IF you succeed, run up the stairs after and Hail Karana to say the line, "I will follow the path" for a flag.
