Tower of Frozen Shadow

The Tower of Frozen Shadow is a dungeon in the Iceclad Ocean. It can be found at location -2500,3000 in the Iceclad Ocean. There are several portals here which require keys.
Miscellaneous information:

Each level has at least two mirrors. One mirror leads to Iceclad Ocean and does not require a key. To use this mirror each person must individually click on the exit mirror. The second mirror leads to the next floor. These mirrors typically require a key but only one person per group needs to have the key. This person clicks on the mirror to take everyone in the general vicinity of the mirror to the next level. (The exception to this is the 6th floor, which I will explain later in that section)

All mobs in the Tower with the exception of a few rare named mobs give Tserrina faction hits. Tserrina is the boss mob of the ToFS. She is a caster and has several nice drops most of them pure caster based. There are a whole bunch of named mobs throughout the different levels and some of them have some pretty nice drops. From the 4th floor up I have seen every mob have a chance to drop various level 39 Velious spells.

This zone brings many rewards to those who come here. It can also bring much suffering. There is no zoning between floors. That means that mobs can bring aggro from previous floors. While it does not happen much, there is the possibility for this to happen. The keys are all lore and no drop. That means that you cannot have a spare in the bank and they stay on your corpse.

When traveling here HIGHLY suggest someone with an evacuate spell or a second set of keys in someone’s bank.

Entrance:

In Iceclad Ocean there is a small door around 2500,3000 on the side of the tower. Clicking on this door will take you into ToFS.

1st Floor:

Key to this floor: No key required to get here
Zone out: When you zone in if you don’t move and turn around you will see the exit mirror.
Zone to the next level: Straight across from the zone in take the right hall.
Keyholder: Enlarged Shadow Beast
Placeholder: Any mob on the floor
Safest Travel: Invisibility to Undead

On this floor there are Ice Shades, Shadowbones, and Fading Shadow Guards. All of them are undead. The zone in for this level is a safe spot and nothing will attack you.

Immediately out the safe passage is a large room with several halls branching off of it. Be careful when not in a hallway because the center room is very slick and there is a pit in the middle of it.

On the first floor instead of going to the right to go to the next level if you go to the left there is another mirror. With the key from Tserrina you can go straight to the 7th floor.

2nd Floor:

Key to this floor: Crystal Key
Zone out: Run straight across the zone. Do not enter the passageway. Instead follow that wall all the way to the left there you will see a bookcase along that wall. Click on the bookcase to reveal a hidden passage.
Zone to the next level: Run straight across the zone. At the back wall is a passage.
Keyholder: Angry librarian
Placeholder: Any mob on this floor
Safest Travel: Invisibility to Undead

The zone in spot is a safe area.

All mobs on this floor are undead. Most of the mobs on this floor are casters. They range from teachers and scholars to students. This level is the ghostly remains of a school. The first mob you come up against is the Librarian and her assistant. She is the toughest normal mob on this floor.

There are a few other named mobs on this floor but I have no knowledge of their uses.

3rd Floor:

Key to this floor: Three Toothed Key
Zone out: Go right. Take the first left. Take the next left immediately followed by a quick right. Follow this passage to the end and take a right. Go down the stairs and immediately go left, you should see a pool of blood in front of you. Go into the back room and on the left wall is the exit mirror. The exit mirror is partially blocked from view by a support brace when you first walk into the room.
Zone to the next level: Go right. Take the first left. Take the next left immediately followed by a quick right. Follow this passage to the end and take a right. Instead of going down the stairs go left. Follow this passage around to the mirror. There is a hidden passage you can sneak through that is a tapestry on the wall.
Keyholder: Enraged Shadow Beast
Placeholder: Any mob in this zone.
Safest Travel: Taking the route laid out, Invisibility

This floor has mostly undead mobs but there are a few Armored Shadows that are summoned creatures and can see through invisibility to undead.

There is a named mob on this floor called an Angry Chef. She is a caster but in over twenty slayings of her she has yet to drop anything. If you are of the level to be fighting here she sometimes does not seem worth the hassle. She is a tough mob for her level.

4th Floor:

Key to this floor: Frosty Key
Zone out: Go to the right and you will see a door. It is locked and requires the Frosty Key. Follow the passage to the exit mirror.
Zone to the next level: Go right. Take the first right. This curves around and takes you the same way you were heading when you zoned in. Do not deviate from this path all though you will zig zag a line to the back of the zone. There it will curve so you are facing the zone in spot. At this point it looks like a dead end. You will be facing what looks like a garbage incinerator door. This is a hidden wall and you can walk through it. Continue around to the exit mirror.
Keyholder: Cara Omica
Placeholder: Amanhotep (his placeholder is any mob)
Safest Travel: Invisibility (kind of)

As for the safest travel, I will say this: There are both undead mobs and normal mobs on this level. I would say about 90% of the mobs are normal. The plus side is that most of the undead mobs cannot see through invisibility. I said MOST, be careful! On the other hand 100% of the normal mobs on this level can see through invisibility to undead.

The zone is not a safe spot so do not zone in visible expecting to be safe. Where you zone in there are at least three mobs that wander by. Be very careful one mob can quickly turn into eight on this floor.

This floor has traps down some of the passageways. The traps lead you down to a sub-floor. If you fall through you are in a passage. Follow this passage to the center of the sub-floor. There is a pot. If you click on the pot it will take you back to where you zoned in.

I have heard people say that only the undead are the placeholders for Amanhoteph but if you track after every mob you will see that any mob on that floor can spawn him. I have however seen him on the path to the next zone more times than any other spot.

Cara Omica spawns past the hidden wall on the way to the exit mirror. When killing her in her room you can see the mirror to the next level. It is not uncommon to come into this level to have Cara already up. This level is a maze and not everyone knows the way to the next level and the fact that as soon as Amanhoteph dies Cira spawns.

5th Floor:

Key to this floor: Small Rusty Key
Zone out: After zoning in do not move and turn to the right, there in the left hand corner is the mirror.
Zone to the next level: It is straight across from the zone in. You just have to go around either side of the wedding room.
Keyholder: Enraged Relative
Placeholder: Any mob in this zone.
Safest Travel: Invisibility to undead

The zone in point is a safe area, with exception. I typically have everyone move to the left-hand wall without using their mouse to control their character. It is horrible on the expedition to have someone use their mouse and accidentally click on the exit mirror to make the party go all the way down and get them. As long as you don’t go to far you will not provoke and attack from any mobs.

All mobs here are undead. If you can get to this floor then you should not have any troubles with this level at all. This level is much easier than the previous floor. Some of the mobs on this floor may even be green to you. The golems are the highest normal mob on this floor and they are right next to the zone in point.

The Enraged Relative however is a different story. After becoming relaxed on this floor he will take you by surprise. This mob is definitely the boss mob for this level. He will take quite a beating and kindly return one back to the group.

6th Floor A:

Key to this floor: Bone Finger Key
Zone out: Go straight ahead and take the second left. This hall curves around to a big open room full of specters. Hug the left wall all the way around the room and in the back right of the room is a closet with the mirror. In this room to the right is a dragon statue. Be careful because he is a trap. If you step to far away from the wall or to close to it you will get hit with a 500pt breath weapon that also dispells. To top that off if you have not cleared the room the specters will gladly help the trap finish you off.
Zone to the next level: If you take the first right and follow that path all the way around and up the stairs you will find the next mirror. Be careful on the ledge getting to close can provoke an attack from the specters below. This is the weird floor. The next mirror is not a keyed mirror. Everyone has to click on the mirror to progress.
Keyholder: No key needed for the next level.
Placeholder: No Keyholder.
Safest Travel: Invisibility to undead.

There are two kinds of mobs on this level Icy Shades and Shadow Guardians. Both of these mobs offer great experience all the way through 50. After 50 they give experience about every two in five mobs.

The zone in is a safe spot once again. I think this is truly the best place to pull mobs to in this zone. From the start a well-balanced group can pull almost the whole floor.

There is a mob on this floor called the incoherent spirit. He is the placeholder for the Lucid Sprit of Abrams. Abrams spawns around twenty percent of the time immediately after the Incoherent Spirit dies. Abrams is used in a quest and is indifferent to almost everyone. You can however kill him for some experience with out worries of a faction hit.

6th Floor B:

Key to this floor: No key required to get here.
Zone out: You zone into a round room with a bridge out of the room. Under the bridge against the wall is the mirror back to 6A. The “exit” mirror actually takes you to the zone in for 6A. The real exit mirror is on 6A.
Zone to the next level: Up the bridge follow the path around the back to a Hand holding a gem this is the next “mirror”.
Keyholder: Val Sherra
Placeholder: Any mob on 6A can be the placeholder.
Safest Travel: None

On this floor you zone into a fight. There are Shade Bats. Upon their deaths they immediately spawn an Enraged Vampire. The Vampires proc a spell that that completely drains your stamina. After killing all of the bats in this area start to buff up. With a good puller you can pull the rest of the bats from Val Sherra’s area with out pulling him.

Val Sherra is a caster and is most definitely the hardest mob up to this point. I believe that he pulls most of his spells from the necro/sk line of spells. He also can charm so get your magic resistance up. It is best to pull the bats then go up and kill Val Sherra where he stands because he likes to gate when he is low on life.

7th Floor:

Key to this floor: Large Metal Key
Zone out: Up the spiral staircase is a locked door that needs the same key to get to this level. Through the door is a room full of mirrors, pick one.
Zone to the next level: No Exit. The Final level.
Keyholder: Tserrina (this key allows you to take the left tunnel on the first floor and come straight to 7th floor mirror room)
Placeholder: The Shrouded Bats on this level can all spawn Tserrina
Safest Travel: Neither (Drakes on this floor can see through both Invisibility and Invisibility to Undead.)

When you zone in it is another safe spot. To get to Tserrina you must pass a trap. A quick disarm and you are on the way. In the room with the spiral stair case there is a hidden wall in the back. Behind that are Shrouded Bats. Killing these all have a chance to instantly spawn Tserrina.

If you have a force big enough to kill Tserrina you should have no problems keeping this floor clear. I have killed Tserrina both near the entrance and in the back of the room where all the bats spawn and both times seemed to go smoothly. Fighting her in the back seemed easier due to the fact that you really do not have to worry about the other mobs on the floor and can concentrate on killing Tserrina.

Biggwin Spivy
Innoruuk’s Halfling Rogue
