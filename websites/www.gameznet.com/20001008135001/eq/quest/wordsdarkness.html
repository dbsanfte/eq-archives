Words of Darkness
This quest was submitted by Arews:

This quest is given in the Solusek Ro Temple by Vurgo. Talk with him and you'll find out you need to go get "The Book of Shadow" as dropped by the Shadow men found in certain zones. Here is the thing with this part, most people talk of how long this camp is, how difficult it is to get one but I found it dropped fairly common in West Common's from the shadow men there.

Once you have it bring it back to Vurgo, if you leave the game the book with disappear however giving it to Vurgo he will give you a note explaining that you have given him the book and then proceed to explain what else you must do to obtain the Words of darkness. From there he told me I needed to obtain the book of frost, the book of darkness, 300 gold pieces, and of course the note he gave earlier and give it all to him when done.

He told me that the "Icy" yes "Icy" not Ice Goblin, but the Icy Goblin drop's the Book of Frost and he hides in Perma Frost. The Icy Goblin spawns in what some call the Goblin Jail area, I call the Goblin city area, goblin's everywhere. If you don't know the area, find an area with a few bared gates, go threw the right one's and you'll find a door at the end of a hall way go threw it, make a right follow the corridor and you'll come into a big room with a big slope in front of you. If you go up the slope, you'll see a big bear head on the wall, then go down a ramp and the Icy Goblin is in the first room on your left. I would guess him to be in his upper 20's, but has two guards and a friend in another room who will heal him.

Anyway once I had that book I headed to Lake Rathetear, walk into the zone and go to the dock and if you have your clip plane out far enough you will see a tower on one of the islands. Go to it and you'll see two Erudite's, one is a Wizard and one is a Magician with an Earth Elemental. I believe they are between level 23-29. The book was found on the Magician. Once I had both book's, I ran to the bank and got 300 gold (Not 30pp) and then to Sol Temple and handed Vurgo the two books I got, the note he gave me earlier, and the 300 gold and got my Words of Darkness.
