DirectX 9:

The following is a list of the processors and video cards that the EQ Compatibility Lab used in their test matrix:

AMD K6 3 500/566
AMD 2100XP
AMD 1.2GHz
Intel Celeron 1.4GHz
Intel Celeron 1.7GHz
Intel P2 333/350/400
Intel P3 600/866/933/1GHz
Intel P4 1.6 GHz
Intel P4 2.26 GHz
Intel P4 2.4 GHz
Intel P4 2.53 GHz

ATI Radeon 7000/7200/7500
ATI Radeon 8500
ATI Radeon 9000
ATI Radeon 9700
Matrox Parhelia
NVidia Riva TNT/TNT2
NVidia GF 256
NVidia GF DDR
NVidia GF2 GTS
NVidia GF2 MX400
NVidia GF2 Ti
NVidia GF2 Ultra
NVidia GF3/Ti200/Ti500
NVidia GF4 MX420
NVidia GF4 Ti4200/4400/4600

Ryan from the Lab at EQ is quoted as saying:

"All latest available drivers were used for each test, and based on what we saw, across each operating system (all 4 were tested, where applicable) [sh: 98, ME, XP, 2k], and we saw no visible issues. We did not experience random crashes, and visually did not see any anomalies. The only thing that we've seen is that there may be a limited amount of frame rate loss in some configurations."

There will be an abandonment of cards such as the Voodoo line a once-over, Verant has stated that they are unable to provide official support for them.

Here is a further quote on the Voodoo line:

"If people have an orphaned card like a Voodoo, then upgrade to DirectX 9, and EverQuest breaks, there's unfortunately not a thing that anyone will be able to do about it, as there aren't even official drivers for it anymore."
