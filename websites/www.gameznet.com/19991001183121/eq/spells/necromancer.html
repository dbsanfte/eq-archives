Necromancer Spells
Level 1

Cavorting Bones

This is the first in the line of necromancer pet spells. This spell summons a level 1 decaying skeleton. 

Cold Light

This spell summons a dim light which can be used as a lightsource.

Disease Cloud

This spell will disease your opponent and do a small amount of damage initially as well.

Invisibility To Undead

This spell renders you invisible to undead mobs. This spell can be cast on anyone in your party.

Lifetap

This is the first in a line of spells which is unique to necromancers. This spell will suck hit points away from your opponent and give them to you.

Locate Corpse

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest corpse, or in the direction of the corpse belonging to a targeted player.

Minor Shielding

This is the first in the line of the wizards shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.

Reclaim Energy

This spell is cast on summoned pets. When cast on a pet this allows you to reclaim some of the mana you spent on him and "unsummon" him.

Sense The Dead

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.   Unfortunately this also includes skeleton pets.

Siphon Strength

This spell will lower your targets strength and add it to yours.

Level 4

Clinging Darkness

This is a duration of time spell which causes damage over time. It also slows the targets speed down as it is somewhat of a blinding spell.

Endure Cold

This spell adds to the targets resistance to cold.

Fear

Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. Be warned however that he will return when the spell wears off and he may bring friends.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Grim Aura

This spell will add to your ATK rating similar to the firefist spell of druids.

Leering Corpse

This spell summons a level 3-5 skeleton.

Lifespike

Lifespike is the upgrade to lifetap. This spell takes transfers some of the hit points of your opponent and adds them to your hit points.

Numb The Dead

Numb the dead is an undead calming spell. By casting this spell on undead beings you will cause them not to attack players they would normally attack.

Poison Bolt

This spell will poison your opponent doing damage to him over time.

Sense Magic

This spell will give you information on magic items. 

Level 8

Bonewalk

This spell will summon a level 7-9 skeletal pet.

Dark Empathy

This spell will heal your target while damaging yourself.

Deadeye

Deadeye grants the caster not only infravision but also the ability to see invisible beings. This is important for races who do not have the benefit of infravision.

Gather Shadows

Gather shadows is an invisibility spell that can only be cast on yourself. This is very important to evil players as it will allow you to travel places you previously were cut off from.

Impart Strength

This spell will give some of your strength to your target.

Lesser Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor shielding spell. It adds armor class and hit points.

Mend Bones

This spell allows you to heal your pet. 

Shadow Step

This spell is a short mini teleportation spell that will teleport you a short distance away from your current location. It is mainly used as an escape spell.

Vampiric Embrace

Vampiric embrace will make your hands glow for up to two minutes. During this time when melleing you will randomly lifetap your opponent for a decent amount of damage.

Ward Undead

This is a direct damage spell for use versus undead beings. It is the first in a line of spells.

Level 12

Bind Affinity

This spell allows you to change your binding point where you will return if you gate or die.

Convoke Shadow

This spell will summon a level 11-13 pet.

Endure Disease

This spell will increase your resistance to disease.

Engulfing Darkness

This damage over time spell is an upgrade to the clinging darkness spell.

Heat Blood

This is a damage over time spell. The importance of it is that it can be stacked with the clinging->engulfing darkness line of spells which will allow you to do a lot more damage stacking the two of them.

Lifedraw

Lifedraw is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells which will heal yourself by stealing a monsters hit points.

Sight Graft

This somewhat useless spell will allow you to see through your pets eyes. 

Spook The Dead

This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with numb as you can numb one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one. 

Wave Of Enfeeblement

Wave of Enfeeblement is an area effect weaken spell.

Level 16

Banshee Aura

Damage sort of shield that deals out 8 damage everytime the caster (you) are struck by a non melee hit. Requires one pearl for infinite castings.

Cancel Magic

This spell can be used to cancel magical effects that have been cast on you or your party members. It's great to eliminate damage over time, charm or root spells.

Cure Disease

Cure disease will allow you to cure yourself or another player of a disease.

Feign Death

This spell allows you to play dead. If it works the monster will assume you are dead and will leave you alone. You can lay around and regain hit points and then get up and run.  If the monster is not dead before you get up it will drop everything to kill you, so zone.

Heart Flutter

This is a triple threat spell. It not only weakens your opponent and lowers their armor class but it also does damage over time.

Hungry Earth

This is a root spell which works only on undead.

Infectous Cloud

***This is an area effect disease spell.**** I believe that this is not AE so...
This is a targeted disease spell with an initial DD component. (25 damage)

Restless Bones

Restless bones will summon a skeletal pet.

Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor shielding->lesser shielding line of spells. It adds not only armor class and hit points but magic resistance as well.

Shieldskin

This spell allows you to absorb a certain amount of damage without being injured.  

Spirit Armor

Spirit armor is a buffing spell which can be cast on yourself and others which will add armor class.

Voice Graft

This somewhat useless spell will allow you to speak through your pet. It is great for toying around with(especially when you are invisible) however it does not have much use.

Level 20

Allure Of Death

This spell works like a beafed up version of Dark Pact, -8hp +8mana each round. Works great with speeding up down time especially if you use Siphon Life series of spells for offense to regain health then repeat the cycle.

Animate Dead

This spell will summon a level 20ish pet.

Dominate Undead

Dominate undead is a spell that can be used to charm undead monsters and use them as pets.

Expulse Undead

Expulse undead is an upgrade to the ward undead spell. It not only does more damage but is also more mana efficient.

Harm Shield

Harm shield grants the caster a brief period of invulnerability, during which they may not cast spells.

Identify

This spell identifies an item. I really do not see much use for it in this game.

Shadow Vortex

This spell lowers your opponents armor class.

Siphon Life

Siphon life is another upgrade to the lifetap line of spells which drains a monsters hit points and gives them to the caster.

Word Of Shadow

This is an area effect damage spell that centers around the caster.

Level 24

Breath Of The Dead

This is a water breathing spell.  Requires fish scales to cast.

Haunting Corpse

Haunting corpse is the level 24 upgrade to your pet summoning spells.

Intensify Dead

This spell allows your pet to dish out more damage and it also acts as a regeneration spell on your pet.

Leatherskin

Leatherskin is an upgrade to the shieldskin spell. It absorbs more damage.

Major Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor->lesser->shielding line of spells. 

Resist Cold

This spell will add to your resistance to cold.

Rest The Dead

This is an upgrade to the numb the dead spell.

Screaming Terror

Screaming terror acts as both a fear spell and a forget spell. It wipes the aggro list similar to the enchanter spells.

Shadow Sight

Shadow sight grants ultravision to the caster.

Shock Of Poison

This spell is a poison based direct damage spell.

Level 29

Blood Boil

This spell is an upgrade to the heat blood spell. It can be stacked with darkness spells for maximum damage over time.

Dismiss Undead

This is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It is a high mana high efficiency spell which is very useful versus undead foes.

Dooming Darkness

Dooming darkness is an upgrade to the clinging darkness->engulfing darkness line of spells. It does damage over time.

Panic The Dead

Panic The Dead is an upgrade to the spook undead spell. It has a longer duration and will work more on higher level foes.

Renew Bones

This is an upgrade to the mend bones spell.

Spirit Tap

This is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells.

Summon Dead

Summon dead is the level 29 pet summoning spell.

Vampiric Curse

This spell is a DoT liefteap.

Level 34

Beguile Undead

This is an upgrade to the charm undead spell.

Call Of Bones

This spell turns the caster into a skeleton. During this period you trade hit points for mana.

Greater Shielding

Greater shielding is an upgrade to the lesser->minor->shielding->major shielding line of spells.

Invoke Fear

Invoke fear is an upgrade to the fear spell. It has a longer duration.

Invoke Shadow

This is the level 34 summoning spell.

Resist Disease

This is an upgrade to the endure disease spell. It adds more resistance.

Root

Root will prevent an NPC from running.

Steelskin

Steelskin is an upgrade to the leatherskin spell. It absorbs damage.

Surge Of Enfeeblement

This is an AE weaken spell.

Venom Of The Snake

This is a poison spell which will do damage over time.

Level 39

Augment Dead

This is an upgrade to intensify dead. It is a buff for your pet.

Counteract Disease

This is an upgrade to the cure disease spell. It is more effective with high level poisons.

Drain Spirit

This is an upgrade to the Spirit Tap spell.

Expel Undead

Expel undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss undead line of spells. It does DD to undead foes.

Malignant Dead

This is the level 39 summoning spell.

Nullify Magic

Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Scourge

This is a disease attack.

Word Of Souls

Word of souls is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Level 44

Arch Shielding

Arch shielding is the final upgrade to the shielding spells.

Crackling Bones

Crackling bones is the level 44 pet summoning spell.

Dead Man Walking

This spell is an upgrade to the level 20 allure of death spell.  It also changes the caster into a skelton.

Diamondskin

This is the final upgrade to the shieldskin->leatherskin line of spells. It absorbs damage that would otherwise hurt the caster.

Ignite Bones

This is a direct damage spell which also temporarily roots your opponent and turns him into a skeleton. The funny thing about this spell is if you cast it on a large opponent(such as a giant). When they return into giant form they will still be the size of a skeleton.

Pact of Shadow

Pact of shadow is similar to a lifetap spell in reverse. It gives your hit points to your target(200-300 hit points). It does take about 10 seconds for the transfer to complete.

Level 49

Banish Undead

This is the last in the line of the ward->expulse->dismiss->expel undead line of spells. 

Bond of Death

This is an upgrade to the vampiric curse spell. It drains hit points from your opponent and gives them to the caster. The drain happens over time and not all at once.

Cajole Undead

Cajole undead is a high level undead charming spell.

Cascading Darkness

Cascading darkness is an upgrade to the clinging->engulfing darkness line of spells. It does damage over time.

Drain Soul

Drain soul is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells.

Expel Summoned

Expel summoned will do good damage to summoned beasts such as elements.

Ignite Blood

This is the last in the line of the heat blood->blood boil line of spells. It is a DoT.

Invoke Death

This is the level 49 summoning spell.

Lich

This is the last in the line of the allure of death spells. It turns the caster into a skeleton as well as trading hit points for mana.
