Everquest Quest Guide: Neriak Shark Slaying

The following quest was submitted by Arioc:

This rogue quest takes place at the rogue guild in Neriak:

Talk to your guild master (the one who you need to deliver the guild note to) about "foreign takes" and "retrieving the chest". He will give you a note to take to a contact on the freeport docks at night and mention a secret enterance into freeport via the aquaducts.

There are 2 false walls in the west freeport newbie area where the level 1/2 monsters roam. One on the right and one on the left of the town gate. Take the right passage (follow the right wall till you find the false wall in the corner) and skirt the cistern. Proceed to the rogues guild and out onto the docks via the false wall in the house or the false wall in the bay. Wait on the rocks till nightfall and bait mastersen is gone. Give the note to your contact when she appears and she will inform you that the take was lost overboard and her comrad has become fishfood. You need to dive down and kill the shark below the water in front of her. You need to kill the shark fast or he'll swim off and dump the chest god knows where. Return the chest to your guild master and receive some experiance, a faction raise and some copper or silver.
