Everquest Beastiary Guide: Mummy

Lesser Mummies are a favorite among soloing clerics and necromancers. This is becauase they fill that level 11-12 undead void between zombies and greater skeletons.

Mummies can be quite profitable for their level range. They carry rawhide armor and other items. The loot varies but it generally rather good.

However mummies are not good for a group who does not have some type of caster who can cure disease. Lesser mummies can stricken you with the M1Mummy disease which lasts for a very long time and will not allow players to heal unless they sit down. This makes them all but immpossible for meelee classes to solo.

HIGHER LEVEL MUMMIES

Higher level mummies can be found in several different zones accross Norrath. These mummies are identical to lower level mummies except that their diseases(as well as their level range) are more powerful and they carry much better loot.
