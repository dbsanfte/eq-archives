Totemic Armor
This quest was submitted by Zaknefein:

If you go into Lake Rathetear.  From the dock near the rathe mountain zone get a boat and follow the left zone wall.  After you pass a dock near the gypsy camp angle out into the lake little to the right and you'll find an island with buildings on stilts.  This is the barbarian camp (237, 2187).  Set back from the camp is a shrine with two barbarians next to it.  The man and wife Kyralynn and Vrynn are the two you need to hail to get the quest. If you are a troll or an ogre shaman, DO NOT approach them unless you have done something to improve your faction with barbarians, cause they will attack you and are very mean.

Kyralynn

Bracer - turn in a banded bracer, 2 Dufrenite, Cauldron Shell from an aqua goblin in Dagnor' Cauldron, and a Bull Elephant Tooth from South Karana

Mail (tunic) - turn in a banded mail tunic, 2 Dufrenite, a Lancer Swordfish Fin from Kedge Keep, and Grizzleknot Bark from South Karana.

Helm - turn in a banded helm, 2 Dufrenite, a Glacier Bear Pelt from a glacier bear in everfrost, and an ogreskin chamois from a lizzard page in the temple of Cazic Thule.

Cloak - turn in a banded cloak, 2 Dufrenite, Basalt Drake Scales from the Rathe Mountains, and Driftwood Pipe from an isle goblin chieftain in the Ocean of Tears.

Vrynn

Boots - turn in banded boots, 2 Dufrenite, an orc cheif's tongue from Lesser Faydark, and a terror spine from a tentacle terror in nejena.

Gauntlets - turn in banded gauntlets, 2 Dufrenite, a Mammoths Rib Bone from Everfrost, and Griffenne Downfeather from North Karana.

Sleeves - turn in banded sleeves, 2 Dufrenite, Ghoul Carrion from Estate of Unrest, and Charger Hoof Chips from South Karanas.

Leggings - turn in banded leggings, 2 Dufrenite, Permafrost Snowball from a goblin wizard, and Bone Barbs from a barbed skeleton in Estate of Unrest.
