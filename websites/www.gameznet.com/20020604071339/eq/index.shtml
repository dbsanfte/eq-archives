EQ End Game Rumor...
 15:25 EST - Hermana

There had been some rumors, even on my guild website I saw a few, that the end of EQ might be after the next expansion. Here is the statement recently made in response to that.

We do not plan to stop working on EverQuest until you folks decide it’s time for us to stop. It’s your participation in the endeavor that will keep us going. For as long as you want to play our game, we’ll continue to do our best to entertain you.

Free EQ Account
 15:18 EST - Hermana

"Yes, It's Real! A Free EQ Account Key!

Sony Online Entertainment is currently in the process of sending out an email to its EverQuest subscriber base who have opted to receive special emailing from us. Within this email is an account key for EverQuest and an invitation to try EverQuest free for a 30 day trial.

To ensure that you are on our mailing list for future opportunities, sign up for the Harpy's Head Herald, EverQuest's official email newsletter."

New Zone on Legends
 9:29 EST - Hermana

The Jagged Forest is now opening up on the Stormhammer Server!

"This zone combines outdoor and indoor adventure areas for brave souls of 40th Level and up. Jaggedpine is currently in final testing and we plan to have it live on the server soon. Explore the mysteries of this dense forest and its reclusive inhabitants. Become embroiled with the local citizenry's conflict and town defense.
