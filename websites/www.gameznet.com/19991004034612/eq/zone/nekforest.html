Everquest Zone Guide: Nektulos Forest

The Nektulos/Neriak forest is sandwiched between the dark elf city of Neriak, Lavastorm and the East Commonlands. This is a popular zone for dark elves level 1-10 and dark elf enemies levels 15-40.

First off for dark elf newbies there are many things to hunt here. A few of these include bats, black bears, young kodiaks, black and shadow wolves, spiderlings, decaying skeletons and skeletons. There are several camps in this zone including a leatherfoot halfling camp which will become a hotspot for dark elves after level 10. There are also several skeleton camps which occassionally will spawn a zombie or a lesser mummy.

Fear not young dark elves for if you get in trouble there are several roaming guards and stone guardians(a form of element) who will come to your aid. On the flip side of this they might also steal your kills(doh!).

The "good" races come to this zone for two reasons. The first is as a transit point to lavastorm. If this is your intentions you will want to avoid the dark elf guards at all costs for they will kill you on sight. You will probably elect to swim accross the river on the far side of the zone. Beware of the pirahna when you do!

The other reason is to farm the dark elf guards. These guards vary in level from the low teens all the way up to the level 35 dragoons. In general they carry bronze weapons at the least and thus can be decent loot for characters in this level range.
