Everquest Beastiary Guide: Jungle Spider

Jungle spiders can be found in Feerot. They vary between level 3-5. Jugle spiders can carry up to four pieces of spiderling silk which sells for over over a silver piece at some shops. This makes them a favorite among loot hunters.

Beware however that jungle spiders are social creatures. They will not only help and receive help from other jungle spiders but also from jungle spiderlings.
