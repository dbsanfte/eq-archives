Cazic Thule NPC Drops
Thanks to Andi Deathstalker for compiling this list.

Templer, Lizardscale cloak (Common) , Ravenscale Leggings (Rare)
Diviner , Lizardscale mantle (Common), Ravenscale Bracer (Rare)
Archon , Lizardscale belt (Common), Ravenscale Vambracers (Rare)
Cenobite , Lizardblood Potion (Commons , Ravenscale Boots (Rare)
Crusaders , Decaying Armor ( ShadowKnight Quest item (NO DROP) (Common)

Clay Golem , Ball of golem clay (Common) , Lizardskin Tribel Mask (Rare)

Other

Avatar Of Fear , Bladed Thulain Claws (Common) , Ravenscale Brestplate (Rare)

ALL MOBS have a level rating in that i suggest all group members are in those levels.

Levels 20~30

The Radiant can be found in the maze located next to the court yard.

Levels 30~35

The Templer is a Cleric,He cast many DD spells,and can heal him self.

Levels 30~35

The Diviner is a Enchanter,I have never seen him charm anyone,but rumor says he can. He cast Enchanter based spells and has a pet. 

(Both Diviner and Templer are located next to each other in the LTR ( Lower Thrown Room)

Levels 33~37

Archon lies deep with in the Cazic Thule temple located past the maze. He is a wizard and cast many harmful DD spells.

Levels 35~40

The Cenobite is the Avatar of Fears partner,you must kill him before killing the Avatar of Fear. He is a Cleric.

Levels 33~40

The Crusader spawns random in the UTR (Upper thrown room),Avatar of Fear Bubble and the temple. He is a ShadowKnight and cast ShadowKnight based spells like Harms Touch and Heat Blood. We have charmed him before and had him solo the Archon. Bewear of this tuff foe.

Levels 25~30

The clay golem is very easy,he spawns in the maze.

Levels 33~35

The stone golem spawns in the UTR (Upper thrown room).

Levels 33~37

The steel golem is about equel to a sand giant. Make sure all the gators are cleared before pulling this foe. You need to swim down the well,and past the underground tunnel,you will be led to a green lake.He spawns at the very end of the lake.

Levels 35~40

The Avatar of Fear. Also known as the offspring of Cazic-Thule himself. Is a ShadowKnight. He spawns in a Green Bubble,located down the hall from the courtyard. Be sure and kill the Cenobite and all healers around him before killing him.
