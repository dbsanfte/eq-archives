Okay today I posted up an experimental update. =) I have been toying with a newbie paladin I created a few days ago on the test server and have been having a blast with the quests. With the increased rewards for these low level quests it makes it worth doing them and i've been having more fun than I have in a long time playing Everquest.

While playing this character I have found myself trying to plan strategies for what I should do next. Wondering where I should go to try the quests, what items I should be hunting for, etc. Then I remembered a suggestion that many users have submitted over the months asking us to split items or quests by class type. We never did this because quests often vary because of factions and other reasons, and i'll be honest it's one heck of a pain in the butt to keep updated.

However for experimentation purposes I have gone ahead and created a Paladin Reference Guide. This guide aims to make it easy to find a list of quests which are available to the paladin class. It also gives a suggested list of equipment to attempt to acquire for paladins in certain level ranges. Any feedback is appreciated.

I also added a few new quests when updating the page. Included in this quest update is a the new paladin Armor of Ro quests, at least the beginnings of them.
-J.C.
