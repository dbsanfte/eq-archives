Everquest Quest Guide: Battle The Undead

This quest takes place in Erudin.

This quest is available from the same cleric that gives the Peacekeeper Staffs quest. If you chose Battle the Undead, instead of the Peacekeeper quest, she'll give you a box with six slots. The object behind the quest is to prove you've battled undead by going out, killing decaying skeletons and regular skeltons at level 5+) and returning with the bones.

This isn't really a spoiler, its just the mechanical directions to complete the quest:

Place 1 bone shard in each slot. Hit the box's combine button, turning the box and shards into 'A box of bones' and give this box back to the cleric. In return you'll get a minor item, some copper and a faction bonus. This quest is repeatable, she asks you to try and track down who is creating the skeletons, but I haven't solved that part yet.
