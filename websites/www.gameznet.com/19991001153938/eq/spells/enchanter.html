Enchanter Spells
A note on enchanter animations: While these are similar to pets in that they defend the caster, they cannot take /pet commands the way other caster's pets do. Charmed beasts however will respond normally to /pet commands.

Level 1

Illusion: Half Elf

This spell will allow you to appear as if you were a half elf to others. It will even effect your appearance if they do a /who on you. Monsters and NPC's will respond to you as if you were a half elf. The caster also receives special advantages of that particular class such as infravision.

Lull

This is the first in the line of spells which are aimed to calm your foe. This spell can be used to lower your opponents rage and(hopefully) prevent him from attacking you.

Minor Shielding

This is the first in the line of the wizards shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.

Pendril's Animation

This spell will summon an illusionary pet that can is similar to the pets of a magician or necromancer only less powerful.

Sense Magic

This spell will give you information on magic items.

Shallow Breath

This spell not only damages your opponent but also lowers his strength. It does damage over time.

Strengthen

This buffing spell allows you to add to the strength of yourself or another player.

Taper Enchantment

This allows you to break out of or shorten the duration of an enchantment. Meaning that if you are charmed this spell might allow you to break free before the spell would normally expire.

Weaken

This spell can be used to weaken(reduce the strength) of your target.

Level 4

Color Flux

This is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster.

Enfeeblement

This spell will lower your opponents strength.

Fear

Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. Be warned however that he will return when the spell wears off and he may bring friends.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Haze

This is an armor class buffing spell.

Illusion: Human

This spell will make you appear to be a human(if you are not already). It will also cause NPC's to respond to you as if you were a human.

Invisibility

Invisibility is a very important spell. This allows you to get into areas that you normally could not get into and also allows you to skip by monsters you might otherwise have to fight. Beware however that this spells duration varies and it has a way of wearing off at the wrong time. Some monsters can also see invisible creatures and will see through this spell.

Mesmerize

Mesmerize is the enchanters version of the calm spell. This spell will cause a monster to begin mesmerized and not attack people that it would normally attack.

Suffocating Sphere

Suffocating Sphere is does a bit of damage and also lowers the victims strength.

Tashan

This spell will lower your targets magic resistance.

Level 8

Alliance

This spell will raise your faction will the particular NPC for the duration of the spell.

Bind Sight

This spell will allow you to bind your sight to another being and see through their eyes. The caster will slowly lose stamina while using this spell.

Cancel Magic

This spell will cancel a magical effect which is bothering you or one of your party members.

Chaotic Feedback

Chaotic feedback is a direct damage offensive spell.

Enchant Silver

This spell is used by those who are interested in jewelcrafting. The enchant metals spells can be used at higher skill levels to create magical jewelry.

Eye Of Confusion

This spell will make your target run around aimlessly for up to thirty seconds. It is similar to fear except that they will run in more than one direction.

Illusion: Gnome, Wood Elf

These spells allow you to appear to be a gnome or a wood elf. Other races will treat you as if you are a member of the race they are part of.

Lesser Shielding

Lesser shielding is an upgrade to the minor shielding spell. It adds armor class and hit points.

Mircyl's Animation

Mircyl's animation is an upgrade to Pendril's animation. It summons a more powerful illusionary pet.

Root

This spell will root a being in one place not allowing them to escape.

See Invisible

This spell allows you to see invisible beings.

Sentinel

This spell sets up a non-moving watch area where the spell was cast, and alerts the caster whenever something crosses that space. It can be used for monitoring spawn points, but will not inform the caster of anything unless a being moves while in the area where the spell was cast.

Soothe

Soothe is an upgrade to the lull spell. It makes a monster less aggressive.

Level 12

Bind Affinity

This allows you to change your bind location(the location where you will be returned if you gate or die).

Charm

This spell allows you to charm another being and use it as a pet. Be careful with charm spells as the duration is very unpredictable and your former pet will not be happy about the way you have been using him.

Choke

This is a damage over time spell which also lowers your opponents strength.

Ebbing Strength

Ebbing strength is a debuff spell which can be used to weaken your opponents.

Enduring Breath

Enduring breath is a water breathing spell which can be cast on yourself or others.

Illusion: Dark Elf, Erudite, Halfling

More illusions, same rules apply as with their predecessors.

Languid Pace

This spell will cause your target to attack more slowly.

Memory Blur

Memory blur can be useful for getting rid of aggro NPC's. For example if you had to run from a monster and you have escaped you could then cast this spell to keep him from attacking you next time he sees you(without having to zone).

Mist

Mist is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.

Serpent Sight

Serpent sight grants you infravision.

Whirl Til You Hurl

This spell will root a monster and cause it to spin in place(which makes it difficult for him to land blows).

Level 16

Chase The Moon

Chase the moon is a fear spell which will cause your opponent to tuck tail and run.

Disempower

Disempower is a great debuffing spell which will lower your targets strength, armor class and drain his stamina.

Enchant Electrum

This is an upgrade to the enchant silver spell.

Enthrall

This is an upgrade to the mesmerize spell. It acts as a calming effect on your target.

Levitate

Levitate allows you to levitate above ground. This is useful for travelling over bodies of water or lava. It can also be used to prevent you from being knocked off of ledges.

Identify

This spell identifies a magical item.

Illusion: Barbarian, Dwarf, Tree

More illusion spells.

Mesmerization

Mesmerization is an area effect mesmerize spell.

Quickness

This spell adds to a players attack speed.

Rune I

Rune spells are among the most useful enchanter spells in a group. These spells can be cast on yourself or others and allow a player to absorb a certain amount of damage without actually being hurt(in this case ~50 hit points). It requires cats eye agate.

Sanity Warp

Sanity warp is a direct damage spell.

Shielding

This is an upgrade to the minor->lesser shielding spells. It adds armor class, hit points and magic resistance to yourself only.

Level 20

Berserker Strength

This spell adds to your targets strength, but lowers their agility. It also gives a temporary invulnerability similar to the rune spell.

Calm

Calm is an upgrade to the lull and soothe spells. It lowers your targets aggressiveness.

Cloud

Cloud is an upgrade to the haze->mist line of armor class buffing spells.

Color Shift

Color shift is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster.

Endure Magic

This spell adds to a players magic resistance.

Feckless Might

This spell is a debuff which will drain a monsters strength and dexteriry.

Illusion: Ogre, Troll

More illusions. These are among the most useful for non-evil races. The troll illusion is also particularly useful since it grants the casters regeneration.

Shifting Sight

This is an upgrade to the bind sight spell. It allows the caster to see through the target's eyes using infravision in this case however.

Sympathetic Aura

Sympathetic aura adds to a players charisma ability.

Tashani

This is an upgrade to the tashan spell. It lowers your opponents magic resistance by 20 points.

Level 24

Alacrity

This is an upgrade to the quickness spell. It will allow your target to attack quite a bit faster and has a longer duration.

Beguile

Beguile is an upgrade to the charm spell.

Chaos Flux

This is a direct damage offensive spell.

Enchant Gold

Enchant Gold is an upgrade to

Illusion: Earth Element, Skeleton

Yet more illusions. These ones are a bit different in that they allow you to appear to be a monster.

Invigor

Invigor will restore a players stamina.

Major Shielding

This is an upgrade to the shielding line of spells.

Rune II

Rune II is an upgrade to Rune I(wow really?). It absorbs more damage than Rune and requires a more expensive component.

Strip Enchantment

This spell will allow you to nullify all magic effects cast on yourself in one fell swoop. It will remove one or two spells cast on another party member.

Tepid Deeds

This is an upgrade to the languid pace spell. It will slow your opponent.

Level 29

Augmentation

This is an upgrade to the strength spell. It also adds armor class.

Clarity

This spell increases a players mana regneration rate. It is one of the most desired spells in the game.

Curse Of The Simple Mind

This spell is an intelligence/wisdom  debuff.

Dynn's Dizzying Draught

This is an upgrade to the Whirl til you Hurl spell.

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. It has a longer duration but requires more mana.

Feedback

Feebdack is a damage shield.

Illusion: Air Element, Water Element

More illusions.

Listless Power

This is an upgrade to the ebbing strength spell. It weakens your opponent.

Nullify Magic

This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Obscure

Obscure is an upgrade to the haze->mist->cloud line of armor class buffing spells.

Suffocate

Suffocate is an upgrade to the choke spells. It lowers your targets strength and also does damage over time.

Ultravision

This spell allows you to grant ultravision.

Whirl Til You Hurl II

This is an upgrade to the Whirl Til You Hurl spell.

Level 34

Anarchy

Anarchy is a direct damage spell. It does about 273 a pop.

Cast Sight

This is an upgrade to the bind sight line of spells. It also grants you infravision.

Enchant Platinum

This is an upgrade to the enchant silver spell.

Entrance

This is an upgrade to the stun line of spells.

Greater Shielding

This is an upgrade to the shielding line of spells. It is a self only spell.

Illusion: Fire Elemental

This allows you to take the form of a fire element and for others to respond to you as if you were one.

Insipid Weakness

This spell weakens the target and also lowers their armor class.

Mana Sieve

This spell can be used to deplete your targets mana.

Radiant Visage

This is an upgrade to the sympathetic aura line of spells.

Rune III

This is an upgrade to the Rune line of spells.

Level 39

Cajoling Whispers

This is an upgrade to the charm line of spells.

Celerity

This is an upgrade to the quickness->alacrity line of spells which increase attack speed.

Gravity Flux

This is an area effect direct damage + stun spell.

Illusion: Dry Bone, Spirit Wolf

More illusions.

Immobilize

This is a root spell.

Invoke Fear

Invoke fear is a powerful fear spell which will cause your opponent to run away.

Insight

This is a 10 point intelligence buff.

Mind Wipe

This spell will make a monster forget that you are on it's aggro list.

Pacify

This is a more powerful version of the lull->soothe->calm line of spells.

Rampage

Rampage is an upgrade to the besererker strength line of spells.

Resist Magic

This will add to a players resistance to magic.

Shade

Shade is an upgrade to the haze->mist->etc line of armor class buffing spells.

Level 44

Arch Shielding

Arch shielding is the last in the line of shielding spells. It is self only.

Brilliance

Brilliance is an intelligence buffing spell.

Color Skew

This is an upgrade to the color shift spell.

Discordant Mind

This is a direct damage spell.

Extinguish Fatigue

Extinguish fatigue restores a players stamina.

Incapacitate

This is an upgrade to the root->enstill line of spells.

Pillage Enchantment

This spell will allow you to break out of a charm spell.

Rune IV

This is the last in the line of Rune spells.

Shifting Deeds

This is an upgrade to the tepid deeds spell. It is an attack rate debuff.

Weakness

This spell will weaken your target.

Level 49

Adorning Grace

This is the last in the line of charisma buffing spells. It adds about 40 charisma.

Allure

Allure is the last in the line of charm spells.

Blanket Of Forgetfullness

I am not positive but I believe this is an area effect spell which will remove you from all the affected monsters aggro list.

Dazzle

I have no information on this spell. Anyone who does have information please mail me at jcsmith@gameznet.com.

Gasping Embrace

This is a damage over time spell.

Group Resist Magic

This spell adds to your entire groups resistance to magic.

Paralyzing Earth

I believe this is an area effect root spell.

Reoccuring Amnesia

This is a reoccuring memory blur spell.

Shadow

Shadow is the final upgrade to the haze->mist->cloud->etc line of armor class buffing spells.

Swift Like The Wind

This is the last in the line of the quickness->alacrity->celerity attack speed buffs.
