Everquest Equipment Guide: Opal Scythe

This scythe can be found on Lord Grimrot in South Karana. It is a warrior and shadowknight only 2 handed slashing weapon. Damage is 11, delay is 45. It has an innate disease cloud spell as well as appearing to have a strength tapping effect when wielded by Grimrot himself(although it does not appear on the weapons list of effects).
