Robe of Benevolence

This quest begins in Skyshrine and was submitted by Keyno.

Hello, here is the quest and the reward. The quest starts down the hall from the food vendors in Skyshrine
 
You say, 'Hail Commander Leuz'

Commander Leuz says 'Hello Keyno , Im the new commander of the Sky Shrine Militia. This group of Shrine residents and newly aligned outlanders are given various tasks to do for the benefit of the Shrine. Are you here to request a task?

You say, 'I am here to request a task.'

Commander Leuz says 'We have had recent news from one of our scouts in Western Wastes that there have been Storm Giants wandering the coastal area. We have not heard back from this scout in some time. What we need is a volunteer to go deliver some tools so the scout can accurately communicate what is happening back to us. 

You say, 'I will volunteer'

Commander Leuz says 'Very good! Take this to the scout and wait for them to return the report.
In Weastern Wastes there is a Scout Charisa that stands on a hill near the Sirens grotto zone in Weastern Wastelands. She was KoS to me and to everyone that conned her, Having a enchanter to charm would be ideal. Handing her the toolset gave this dialog.
 
Scout Charisa says' Great! I was wondering when they would send someone out to give me these. In my haste and excitement of this new assignment I forgot these. This scout business is great I must say. It sure beats setting in Southern Ro studying the ruins. I've been following these Storm Giants for days. They've been wandering up and down the coast looking for something.

Faction and exp
CoV+
Yelinak+
Krozmek-
This will spawn 8 giants near the shoreline back over a hill near the SG zone cave. 7 Soldiers and 1 Kromzek Captian. Kill the captian and he drops a Broken disk.
 
Return to Scout Charisa (recharm her) and hand her the disk, this will reward you with a Scouts note (To return to Commander Leuz in Skyshrine) and  the Robe of Benevolence.
 
Faction and exp
CoV+
Yelinak+
Krozmek-
