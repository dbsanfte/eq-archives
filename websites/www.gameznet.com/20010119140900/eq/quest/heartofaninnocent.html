Heart of an Innocent
This quest is part of the Shadowknight Epic Quest. 

This sub-quest beings with an NPC named Knarthenne Skurle in the Tox Forest. This NPC spawns near the docks. 

Hailing him will result in him telling you that you do not have the heart of an innocent.

If you have sufficient TrueSpirit faction you can inquire what heart of an innocent. He will then give you a soulcase(2 slot container). He informs you to combine the soulcase with the heart of one who is pure. "If your hand is steady and your mind is clear then you will have what you seek."

There is an NPC in the Hole by the name of Caradorn who can help you with this. He can be found in the jail area. For those who are newbies to the hole if you go inside of the fortress(down in the pit area) and take a quick left you will come to a T intersection at the end of a long hallway. Going left will lead you to the pool and beyond. Going right will take you down a winding corridor. At the bottom of the corridor is the jail area. There are some ratman patrollers so be ready for a fight. Caradorn spawns in one of the cells.

There is a whole line of background story here which we will completely cut out and simply explain to you how to complete the quest. To make a long story short Caradorn needs you to find the key to his cell to release him.

This key can be found on Mimics which are also in the Hole. They are past a secret door in the bank. The key is a rare drop so be prepared to kill a few of them before attaining it.

Be sure to have a good amount of backup before handing the key in to Caradorn. Upon turning the key in to him Kyrenna will spawn and they will both attack you. You should be safe with two parties but one party can probably defeat them as well if it is well balanced and experienced.

On Kyrenna's corpse you will find the Blood of Kyrenna and the Heart of Kyrenna. Place the heart in to the soulcase and hit combine and you will now have the heart of an innocent.

The blood is required for the Will of Innoruuk and subsequently the Head of the Valiant quests.
