Everquest Quest Guide: Jaggedpine Crook

This quest takes place in Qeynos Hills.

It can be gotten by killing gnoll hunters until you have the "Top of Broken Staff" and the "Bottom of Broken Staff". Both items are very rare so just keep trying. Hail Corun Finisc for him to put it together for you, give him both pieces.
