Everquest Zone Guide: Lesser Faydark

Click Here For A Map

Lesser faydark is mostly a navigational point between greater faydark, steamfont and mistmoore. However there are several monsters here that can satisfy characters level 5-40. On the low end you can find pixies, giant spiders, giant wasps, orcs and bandit camps. On the high end you can find powerful brownie camps.
