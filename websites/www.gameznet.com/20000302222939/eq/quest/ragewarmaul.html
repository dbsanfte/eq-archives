Rage War Maul
This quest was submitted by Khazrak Lungburster:

This is the shadow knight quest for the shadow knight only Rage War Maul.  I   will leave out alot of the history parts of the quest and just give the   essentials for doing it. 

First go to highpass hold and find an NPC named  Mutt Rootlit who stands outside the Golden Rooster.  Kill her and loot Bayle  List I off her.  You can also get part 1 of the list by doing a quest given by Commander Kane in the Qeynos Aqueducts where you take some Blackburrow  Stout to Barn Bloodstone who is located right next to Mutt. 

Next go to the  hidden smugglers' cove also located in Highpass Hold.  Go to Dyllin  Starshine who spawns under the awning in the cove.  I have heard if you talk to him and your faction is high enough with him you can talk the Bayle List III out of him, but mine was not so I killed him and looted Bayle List III off his body. 

After getting these two parts of the Bayle List, head to Surefall Glade.  Behind a waterfall is a system of caves.  There is a rare spawned named bear named Mammoth who spawns at the waterfall entrance of the caves.  Kill Mammoth and loot Bayle List II off him.  Take all 3 parts of the Bayle list and 20gp to Rathmana Allin who lives in the desert of south ro on the wall near the western dervish cutthroat camp.  He will translate the list and give you a complete Bayle List. 

Lastly, take the Bayle List to Pietro Zarn who is in the shadow knight guild in the tunnels under East Freeport.  You're faction must be in the upper part of amiable to get rewarded with the maul.  I hear a way to find out if your faction is to Hail Venox Tarkog who is located near Pietro Zarn.  If he says the following your faction is high enough:

Venox Tarkog says 'Oh. You must be the shadowknight who shall return the list to [Pietro Zarn]. The Bayle List is actually three scrolls with coded words. Alone they are nonsense. Together they can be coded and tell something of value. Antonius Bayle had them created and gave them to three of his most trusted friends. Of these I know, Lady Shae his old love. Then there is Captain Linarius Grafe, captain of the outer Qeynos Guards. Lastly there is Frenway Marthank, Antonius Bayle's oldest friend. He is a resident of the Jaggedpine. When you recover the scrolls you must take them to Rathmana Allin. He is a master scribe. He will decode the scrolls for us. He can be found in deserts of South Ro. Now you best be off, before some other finds the scrolls. You must get there first and return the decoded list to Pietro Zarn.
