Everquest Quest Guide: Freeport Monk Sash

Monks who begin in freeport are able to complete quests for several different sashes. You must do these in order and each is better than the last. The training(white) sash requires to turn in a snake fang, a bone chip and 2 orc scalps. The white sash grants +1 stamina and +1 charisma. The next sash is a yellow headband and will require you to return the white sash, a giant snake rattle, a deathfist slashed belt and a chitin from a desert taurantula.

The next ones were submitted by Sabranth:

The Orange Sash quest requires you to get a Legionairre's Bracer (Crushbone), a Greater Lightstone (Willowwisps (magic-only monster)), and a Cutthroat's Insignia Ring (the Cutthroats in the Desert of Ro), and of course, your yellow sash.
