Everquest Zone Guide: Dagnor's Cauldron

Dagnor's Cauldron borders with Butcherblock. This is a good(though somewhat bare) zone for level 10-20 characters. More importantly it is the entering point to two famous dungeons: Kedge Keep and Unrest. This zone is often used simply as a navigation point between dungeons but it also sports a few interesting beasts of its own. There are several NPC and orc camps in this zone. Perhaps more importantly it is the only zone on the island which sports giant snakes(which are desired by mages for their fangs).
Dagnor's cauldron also is the home to many aqua goblins.

To reach Unrest you should take a right after entering the zone. Follow the coastline and you will eventually see a stone entrance inside of the mountain wall. This is the entrance to the Estate of Unrest.

Kedge Keep is harder to find. This is an underwater dungeon and will require some sort of water breathing item or spell. This dungeon lies deep beneath the waves. When you see some ruins on the ocean floor you know you are near the entrance. The entrance has two large pillars and the remains of two great doors.
