Plane of Power Flag Guide


 
Plane of Justice
Find Mavuin in the basement. Hail him and say 'what information?' Do any one of the trials and get a mark from it.

There are several "trails":
1) To start a Trial go to one of the Tribunal's and say "Prepared".
2) He will then say what trial you are at
3) Get your group on the pad and say "Ready for trial of XXXXXXX" the x's are the trials name
4) Your group will now be ported in the trial.

Hanging
30 sec after you enter 3 mobs spawn:
1 spirit of suffocation and 2 random(breathless voids or stiffling nemesis)

Spirit can spawn as named version which is invisible except for name – named spirit is unmezzable
Normal versions can spawn as a named, mezzable with any high level mesmerize spell.

You have roughly 45 sec – 1 min to kill spirit before he kills prisoners
Spirit has roughly 10k hp, is mezzable, and summoned spells work
Second spirit spawns a set time after the wave begins, even if first spirit is still alive. When second spirit dies, another wave spawns about 45 sec later, regardless if your previous wave is dead or not but only if the prisoners are still alive.

Breathless voids tash and charms – mezzable and summoned spells work
Nemesis procs hundred blows (stun + dd) and is unmezzable

There are 4 waves. Upon the 8th spirit of suffocation death, Gallow Masters spawns, hits for 750 and is an enchanter mob, also charms.

Execution
1) 4 waves of 4 mobs each
2) Each mob has 8k hp, even boss mobs
3) Rocks can be banished by druids/mages – this can sometimes reset the executioner’s walk and sometimes not – possibly related to whether or not the mob has taken damage.
4) You have exactly 30 seconds to kill each mob or the executioner will chop the head off the prisoner. Killing a mob resets this timer. about 333 dps needed.
5) Executioner spawns as the boss at the end (he can be slowed) hits for 700’s
Rocks are not mezzable, whereas razorfiends are.
You can’t banish named rocks.
6) Kill rocks first because they 96 second mez. (get high MR)
7) when done, fail or pass go to the mob in the corner and say "Return" this ports you back to the tribnual area.


Flame
Fire elementals/sprites, spawn in waves of 4, hit around 300ish with fire procs, can also dispel and possibly nuke as well.
The goal is to keep them from getting to the middle of the room.
Fire doesn't seem to have a time limit, but it's pretty tough.. The boss might spawn after the last mob dies, but it could have been coincidence.. Not sure.
You also have an uncurable 200/tick fire DoT eating at you the whole time.

Remove curse gets rid of the ae dot

Lashing
Exactly like Execution with the removal of the time limit, removal of the boss. When a Scourge of Honor spawns, you need to kill a wisp mob in one of the side rooms to kill it (possibly both of the side rooms) – the Scourge itself is untargetable.

Stoning
A spirit of suffocation (enchanter) spawns with 2 other mobs which are either rocks (enchanter) or fungus beasts (melee, procs one-hundred blows (dd + stun)). Then after a bit (30-45 seconds I'd guess), a second spirit of suffocation spawns that needs to be killed before the prisoners die.

Torture
Mobs come in from the door in the room
After killing a wraith of agony on the third wave, everything in the room got healed by an AE including the prisoners, the mobs, and the group.

Hail the Tribunal who administered the trial and say, 'I have come to plead the case of Mavuin'. Go back to Mavuin in the basement jail and hail him - this results in a flag for Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms .
Completion of all the trials allows access to the 7th Hammer - this is not needed to progress to elemental planes.

Plane of Nightmares
First you must hail Elder Poxbourne. He can be found in "Sick Bay" (the area of PoTranq near beach with sick elders in it) Then hail Adroha Jezith and say 'tortured by nightmares' - this gives you a preflag. Now find the hedge inside of Plane of Nightmares - it is across the river along the left zone wall. Hail Thelin Poxbourne.  Follow the script after you hail him, bring 18 people (he teleports only 3 groups) to him and have each group leader say the trigger line he gives.  All groups teleport to a Maze.  Keep Thelin Poxbourne alive as he wanders the maze taking you to various areas where NPC's spawn.  At the end a Construct of Nightmare spawns near a foutain Thelin Poxbourne leads you to and this NPC drops a  thelins dagger, which you need to pick up and give to Thelin.  Handing in dagger spawns a untargettable Terris Thule.  Wait for Thelin to run around, speak some then hail him for a flag to Lair of Terris Thule.

At the end of this event you receive a flag for PoNB (Terris Thule's lair). Go back to Sick bay and hail Elder Poxbourne and Adroha again. Zone in to PoNB through the gate near the hedge and kill Terris Thule, this flags you for Plane of Torment .

Plane of Disease
Go to Alder Fuirstel in PoTranq - standing besides the sewer pipe into PoD. Say 'what ward' to receive your pre-flag. Now go to PoD and kill Grummus, hail the Planar Projection, and zone into Crypt of Decay through the cave behind the pipe in PoTranq. Return to sick bay and hail Elder Fuirstel at sick bay.

Alt method in: 

Quests:Grimror’s Collection. Collect 4 items from 4 named mobs in zone. The four mobs are sengian, harrion, scaevian, and generian larva.

PH for Harrion larva = a malarian larva(not larvae like all the others, spawns near zone in a bit out near flies)
PH for Generian larva = a planarian larva(again just larva, spawns east of castle east of river near pussling camps)
PH for Scaevian larva = a Tsetsian Pupa(just pupa, not pupal, spawns a bit east of generian)
PH for Sengian larva = tachnian caterpillars (in the north part of the zone, not single one close to zone)

Give the 4 parts to Grimror for a Bangle of Disease Warding which acts as a key to Crypt of Decay.


Plane of Innovation
Obtain a key for entrance to the factory.  Enter the zone and hail the gnome at the start.  Say "I will test the machine" - this will get you a preflag. Speak with Niatrim Azinok, he requests 3 items. Return to him a copper node, a bundle of super conductive wires, and an intact power cell. When you give these 3 he begins walking towards the untargetable dragon. After killing your way to the dragon (and keeping the gnome alive), the gnome wakes up Xanamech . Kill it for a flag to the Factory. Then kill the Manaetic Behemoth deeper inside the factory and hail the gnome again. You are now flagged for Plane of Tactics .

Plane of Valor
Kill the crystalline dragon named Aerin`Dar which resides in the back of the wastelands to gain entrance to Halls of Honor.  Aerin`Dar needs one key to access his/her lair. This will spawn a Planar Projection, hail it for a flag to Halls of Honor. You MUST zone in Halls of Honor the first time by opening the floor in the lair (click on it), going donw the water, up through to a cave and clearing your way to the end through the golems.  At the end is Non-KoS Human NPC's, pass them and click on the lightning rods to enter Halls of Honor .

Quests:Aerin’dar’s Lair

Collect Razorfiend Heart, Crystaline Spider Heart, and Planarian Larvae Heart.
Hail Johon Popoah twice
Go find Paralin Notion and follow dialog.
Go find Master Sergeant Aaramis Daryln and follow dialog.
Go back to Paralin Notion, give 3 hearts to him.
Collect crystalline orb piece from Luminii Crawler, one of the frogs near the bridgehouse, and one from the skellies.
Return to Master Sergeant Aaramis Daryln, follow dialog
Find Captain Ryglot Cupperhide, follow dialog.
Give 3 orb pieces to Captain Ryglot Cupperhide, receive Orb (key to enter Aerin’dar’s lair) Alt Enterance to Halls of Honor:
Locate Grenic Drere and follow dialog.

Complete the Box of Souls quest (see below for info).

In Plane of Justice, defeat 3 Tormented Wraiths (near trials room). Each has a chance to spawn "an exiled soldier/trooper/officer of marr". Hand the exiled the Box of Souls, receive box and soul stone.
Give 3 soul stones to Grenic to receive Ring of Marr, which is a key to access Halls of Honor .

Quests:Box of Souls
Find Borik Darkanvil in the Plane of Knowledge, ask about Box of Souls.
Collect Block of Prelium (rare from Plane of Innovation mobs), Crystaline Spider Brain (from crystal spiders in Plane of Valor), and An Agony Mephit Heart (from Agony Mephit).
Either give these items to Borik with 3000pp or combine them with hammer and water in forge (trivial at 250 skill).
Receive Box of Soul.

Plane of Storms
Obtain entrance to Bastion of Thunder through Askr the Lost.

Quest:Aiding Askr
Collect A storm giant’s head, a beard from the ocean giants, a bone from the desert giants, and a sash from the forest giants.
Locate Askr, give him the giant’s head, receive containers.
Combine beard, bone, and sash in container and hand to Askr.
Ask What Bastion of Thunder, receive container
Collect 2 of 3 medallions from named giants, combine them. Go through the text with Askr he clues you into. 
Give to Askr, receive flag to enter Bastion of Thunder .

Crypt of Decay
Get through the Carprin -> High Priest Ultor Szanvon event (you will need a raid) and hail the tiny pusling to gain access to Bertox' area. Kill Bertoxxolous and hail the planar projection. Then zone out, go hail Alder and Elder in Plane of Tranquility again to complete flag.

Plane of Tactics
Kill Tallon and Vallon Zek and hail the Planar Projection. Then kill Rallos Zek (signifigantly harder) and get that flag Halls of Honor
Complete the three trials (Crazed Norrathians, Village people, Rydda`Dar) to obtain entrance go Halls of Honor B - Temple of Mithaniel Marr . In Halls of Honor B, kill Mithaniel Marr and hail the planar projection for a flag that requires several to grant you access to the elemental planes. 

Plane of Torment
Go to sick bay in Plane of Tranquility. Hail Fahlia Shadyglade. Say 'what condition', 'what plane of torment' and 'I will go' and you will receive a flag. Go to PoTorment and gain entrance to Saryn's tower.(need a key for this through a quest from items in the zone, only need one key for a guild raid) Kill Mareeq to spawn Tylis. Go through his dream event to kill the Keeper of Sorrows - get a flag - Tylis ports you back down to the room before Saryn. Kill Saryn, hail Planar Projection. Go back to sick bay in Tranquility and hail both Tylis and Fahlia.  This gives you a flag that requires several to grant you access to the elemental planes. 

Bastion of Thunder
Obtain key to Agnarr's area.

Key To Agnarr the Stormlord:
In each wing Spheres drop-lore,magic, no-drop (Lightning, Tornado, Blizzard, Sandstone).
Gain the container to combine them through the small named bosses of each wing with a Symbol of Torden.
In this Symbol of Torden combine the spheres that drop off named mobs (and random Kriegers) and you gain the key to the Tower of Agnarr the Stormlord  where Agnarr the Stormlord resides.
Kill Agnarr, go up the stairs to the door next to Agnarr's room and Hail Karana. Say "I will follow the path of the Fallen."

At this point you should have killed Mithaniel Marr, Saryn, Tallon, and Vallon Zek. Now find the seer in PoKnowledge - she's in the building right at zone in from Tranq. Sit down and say "unlock my memories" She won't necessarily have anything to say - this is for fixing flags if you do them out of order. Now go to Maelin. He is a gnome in the PoK library. To get to him go to the bottom floor of the library and find the elevator (it has a button in the wall) Ride the elevator up to the top and he's behind the table in front of a bookshelf. Hail him and say 'I have new information' and 'I have new lore'. You may need to do this a few times. Eventually he will tell you that you need to go find Rallos Zek. If you don't get this go back to the seer, sit, then say "guided meditation" you should see some text giving you an idea of what you have. 

Solusek Ro's Tower
Kill the five mini-bosses in this zone - Riziona, Arlyxir, Jiva, Xuzl, and Dresolisk. These 5 flags along with the Rallos Zek flag allow you to enter Solusek Ro's room. Kill him to gain access to the Doomfire, The Burning Lands , aka Plane of Fire.

In addition to Solusek Ro's chamber, killing Rallos Zek also unlocks the way to the other three elemental planes. Once you have killed Rallos along with the other listed flag mobs (Agnarr, etc) Go to Maelin, and you should see this text giving clues.

Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind
Eryslai, otherwise known as Plane of Air is the home of Xegony. Kill the avatars to gain access to Xegony. Kill Xegony for part of the key needed for Plane of Time.

Vegarlson, the Earthen Badland
Vegarlson is the Plane of Earth, and home or the Rathe. Complete the key for PoEarth B and kill the Rathe for part of the key needed to enter Plane of Time.

Reef of Coirnav
This is the elemental plane of water and home of Coirnav. Kill Coirnav to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time.

Doomfire, The Burning Lands
Doomfire is the Plane of Fire. Kill Fennin Ro to gain a part of the key needed for entrance to the Plane of Time.
