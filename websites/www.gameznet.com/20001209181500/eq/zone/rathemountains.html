Everquest Zone Guide: Rathe Mountains

Rathe mountains is a very large zone that connects southern antonica(and the evil races that live there) with the rest of the world. Rathe mountains border with Feerot and Lake Rathetear. This zone has things to hunt for players levels 5-50. It also features a gypsy camp and an inn which actually sells to evil players(the inn, not the gypsy camp). It is the only zone in in southern antonica where lower level ogres and trolls can come to hunt for bear pelts(to make armor).

Lower level characters will find several species of bears, basilisks, skeletons, zombies, lizard men, orcs and bandit camps.

Higher level characters will be able to hunt giant skeletons, Cyclops, griffons and hill giants.

Really high level characters may want to try their hands at the two unique monsters in this zone: the sphinx. There are two sphinx in this zone one is evil(if you are coming from Feerot take a right and keep hugging zone and going right and you will run into him). He is level 40 and will not disturb evil characters. The other is good(he is on the high flat mountain) and is level 50.

Rumor has it that the two sphinx hate one another. If you kill the evil sphinx you will lose a lot of respect from some evil factions but you will gain favor with the good sphinx and vice versa. Rumor has it that the sphinx will grant quests to powerful characters I have been unable to receive a quest from either sphinx at this time and was unable to even get them to speak to me.
