Bounty Hunter Quest
This quest was submitted by Draegos Inglorien.

This Quest originates in Highkeep, and is acquired from Captain Boshinko, who  can be found on the righthand (facing towards the keep) balcony overlooking the front courtyard.

Hail Boshinko, he will ask if your the Bounty Hunter requested.  If you have no qualms about lying to him, follow his leading words right through.  It seems a wee lad by the name of Bronin Higginsbot has escaped the custody of the Highkeep jailors, and in the process of escaping killed a guard with a shank.  Take the Bloody Shank (LORE ITEM, Atk Delay 24, Dmg 3) the Captain gives you and head to Misty Thicket. 

Head to the Wall.  Once at the gates head south along the Wall till you reach the last tower.  Upstairs you will find Bronin, and what Bronin claims is his bodyguard.   Hand Bronin the Shank.  Bronin realizes the game is up and will attack you.   Defeat him handely and you will recieve this message..

Your faction standing with Deeppockets got worse
Your faction standing with CircleofUnseenHands got worse
Your faction standing with MerchantsofRiverVale got better
Your faction standing with CoalitionofTradeFolkIII got worse
Your faction standing with CarsonMcCabe got worse

Loot the corpse for the Halfling Head and a few coins.  Head back to Boshinko in Highkeep and hand him the Head.  Your reward...

Your faction standing with HighpassGuards got better
Your faction standing with CarsonMcCabe got better
Your faction standing with MerchantsofHighpass got better
Your faction standing with CorruptQeynosGuards got better
Your faction standing with TheFreeportMilitia got better
You gain experience!
You recieve 4 platinum from Captain Boshinko.

I also recieved a Ringmail Skirt..he probably gives out minor random items with the coin.

TIP:  Killing Bronin is way harder than it might seem at first.  I am
currently a 26th level Druid and he conned green.  He did only a few hps of damage to me in each of the several skirmished i had with him.  The killer is that he always calls for his Bodyguard.  Now i had assumed he meant the miscreant behind him (who coincidently never raised a finger to help Higginsbot)..also green, BUT a Deputy always joins the fray halfway through..he is found on the ground level and tough.  Each time I got Bronin down to half health he would run right off the wall and easterly, all the while the Deputy is hacking at me.  None of my rooting worked on Bronin for more than a second or two.  Several attempts on Bronin's life (and a few successful completions) saw me Zoning to get away from the Deputy and thus leaving Bronin to die to my swarm, God knows where, leaving me unable to loot.  I finally got it right when I snared him, swarmed him, LB'd him and ran like hell.  I enlisted the aid of a partner to do one thing only.  Stay out of the fight and chase Bronin till he died from the swarm and loot for me.
