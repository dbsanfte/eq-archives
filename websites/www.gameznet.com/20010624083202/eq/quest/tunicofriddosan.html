Tunic of Riddosan
This quest was submitted by Terroria and begins in Paineel.

This is the quest for the Tunic of Riddosan. It is NoDrop any only wearable by Eru/SK. Only Erudite SKs should do this quest! Anyone else is wasting his or her time.

This quest begins in Paineel in the palace. After completing the molar quest, the NPC will prompt you to speak with Royal Guard Sheltuin who is directly below the NPC for the Molar quest on the first story.

You say, “What more important task?”

Royal Guard Sheltuin says ‘According to the evidence we’ve uncovered, it appears the kobolds are nothing but pawns to harass us. Or perhaps even all of Odus. If they wish to play these games, then we’ll humor them. We’ll take their pawn with one of our knights. Will you be the knight, Terroria?

You say, ‘I will be the knight’

Royal Guard Sheltuin says ‘Then you will deliver this chest for us. Don’t worry about what’s inside it. Although we enjoy our solitude, we do make allies. We also make use of those allies. We will do so now. Take the chest to Lyris Moonbane below the human city of Qeynos. You will then follow her instructions and complete any tasks she assigns you. In return, she will send you back with what we wish. Fear is our armor.

Now take the chest you will receive to the Qeynos Aqueducts. Find Lyris Moonbane. She is the necro guildleader and is in the room with the glowing circle on the floor with the candles. Give the chest to her….

Lyris moonbane examines the lock on the chest and pulls a black key from a pouch at her hip. The lock clicks open. Her eyes widen as she carefully opens the chest, revealing a pile of platinum coins and a small note. After admiring the coins she takes the note and reads it. The Necromancer glances up, then at her spectral pet who then hands you a Glass Jar. She says, ‘Are you ready for your first [assignment] Erudite?’

You say, ‘What assignment?”

Lyris Moonbane says ‘We have just the thing for your friends back home. It will take some doing, but the pay off will be lovely indeed. In that jar you must combine 4 substances. You’ll need two portions of a [goblin..source], one portion of [green river sludge], and one [portion of an experimental nature] that an agent of ours is working on. Bring the mixture back to me and we’ll speak again.

You say, ‘What goblin source?’

Lyris Moonbane says ‘The goblins of Antonica are known for their filthy habits and unclean practices. They often carry a menagerie of diseases and conditions. A more concentrated form of this aspect of goblin life cannot be found anywhere else but in the sewers of their citidel in the Serpent Spine. They must have workers who maintain the sewers. Find them and find the substance.

You say, ‘What Green River source?’

Lyris Moonbane says ‘The ogres of Oggok don’t spend much time on sanitation, so naturally their kind harbors many diseases. This is easily apparent in the river that runs near their city, Greenblood. Down at the very bottom of the river is where the ancient plagues have come to rest and hibernate. Once the sludge is mixed in with the other substances, those glorious blessings of Bertoxxulous will most assuredly awaken.

You say, ‘What portion of an experimental nature?’

Lyris Moonbane says ‘We have an agent studying in Freeport. Well, she actually isn’t in Freeport proper but rather under it, in the sewers. Her name is Driana Poxsbourne. When you find her, tell her I have sent you for the substance. She will most likely have a task for you to complete before she can give you the fourth portion. Obey her.

Now head off with the jar (four slot container NoDrop) to Runnyeye. In the sewers locate the goblin janitors. Kill them enough to obtain two “a vile substance” the aren’t nodrop and are pretty worthless so hopefully you can find someone with some already ( thanks Tranex!).

Next head to the Feerrott. You’ll have to take a swim in the river for this one. I found the sludge on the bottom of the river on a rather flat area approx. at loc (137, 704). The Sludge is NoDrop!

Next visit Driana Poxsbourne in the evil guild under East Freeport. Very easy to find once you’re down there.

You say, ‘Lyris has sent me for the substance of an experimental nautre’

Driana Poxbourne says ‘Did she now? Well before I can give you the substance I have a task you must perform. I need [reagents] for my experiments to continue, and the merchants up in the city don’t’ appreciate my kind..I can’t imagine why.

You say, ‘What reagents?’

Driana Poxbourne says ‘The infamous Mage, Najena employs several apprentices. These apprentices are most likely well equipped with just the reagents I need. Perhaps you can relieve them of their burden for me? Bring me 3 pouches of reagents from the Mages and you’ll have the substance.

Now head over to Najena and start killing mages. If you’re lucky, a mage will be camping the mages for focus and/or epic items and may have 3 already. If not prepare for a boring camp. The pouches are fairly uncommon….not as uncommon as the focus items though. When you have all three of the pouches return them to Driana for the fourth component for the jar.

Driana Poxsbourne says ‘Ahh wonderful work Terroria. Here is the substance….don’t spill it HAHAHAaahhahehehe…yes……you don’t want to spill that heheh.

Your faction standing with BloodSabers got better.
Your faction standing with GuardsofQeynos got worse.
Your faction standing with OpalDarkBriar got better.
Your faction standing with PriestsofLife got worse.
Your faction standing with CorruptQeynosGuards got better.

Now combine all four components in the jar and return them to Lyris in the Qeynos Aqueducts.

Lyris Moonbane’s eyes light up with childish glee as she takes the jar containing the disgusting and deadly substance. From her pouch she removes a gob of putty, a gem carved into the likeness of a flying insect. After sticking the putty to the lid of the jar, she mashes the carved gem into the putty and hands the jar back to you. She then says, ‘This is the Harbinger of Bertoxxulous. When the gem is brought near the kobold lair it will enchant the substance. You must then [deliver] the substance.’

You say, ‘Where do I deliver the substance?’

Lyris Moonbane says ‘You’ll need to find the source of the kobold water supply and pour the substance into it. Once you do that, the disease will infest whatever living organisms it comes in contact with so do yourself a favor and leave the area quickly. You can then take the empty jar to your master for your reward.

Now head over to The Warrens and find the water source in the water near the price room on the left side. Just give the bottle to the water source and you’ll get an empty jar. Return this to Royal Guard Sheltuin for your reward.

Royal Guard Sheltuin says ‘Well done, Terroria! Now we need only wait for the kobolds to start dying off and our plan will be complete. Should only take 8 years or so. One such as yourself will wear this ancient armor well. The Tunic of the crusader Riddosan is now yours. He shall watch over and praise you in your triumphs..your defeats on the other hand..well, never mind that.
