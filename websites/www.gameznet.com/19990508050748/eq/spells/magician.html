Everquest Spell Guide: Magician

It should be noted that all summoned objects will dissapear after a player logs out.

Level 1

Burst Of Flame

Burst of flame is the first direct damage spell that a magician receives. It is a low damage spell but will have to do until you make level 4.

Flare

Flare launches a lightsource.

Minor Shielding

This is the first in the line of the shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.


Reclaim Energy

This spell is casts on summoned pets(thus it comes a few levels too early). When cast on a pet this allows you to reclaim the mana you spent on him to a certain extent and "unsummon" him.

Sense Magic

This spell will give you information on magic items.

Summon Dagger

This summons a magical dagger. This dagger is identical to the one that you begin the game with except that it is magical and can be handed out to other party members. All summoned weapons are no rent and this dagger is no exception.

Summon Drink

This spell allows you to summon some water. Summoned water is no rent meaning that if you log out of the game the water will dissapear.

Summon Food

This spell allows you to summon some food. Summoned food is no rent meaning that if you log out of the game the water will dissapear.

Level 4

Burn

Burn is a direct damage spell.

Fire Flux

Fire flux is an area effect spell based on the caster. That means that this spell will damage all monsters in the immediate area of the caster.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Sense Summoned

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest summoned creature.

Summon Bandages

This spell will summon bandages which can be used with the bind wound skill.

Summon Wisp

This spell will summon a light source which can be good for human eyes.

This is also the first level in which you are able to summon your pets. From this point on you will receive four new pet spells every circle. Pet's differ in a couple of ways. Fire elements for example are not great warriors by any means but every time a monster hits them it will damage the monster due to their fire shield. Air elements are invisible, etc.

Level 4 pets are elementalkins.

Level 8

Dimensional Pocket

This spell summons a magical backpack that can hold more items than usual.

Eye Of Zomm

This spell summons a controllable eye which you can use to scout areas out before entering.

Flame Bolt

Flame bolt is a fire based unguided damage spell. This spell launches a bolt which must be correctly aimed but does more damage than average.

Invisibility

This spell will render a player invisible.

Lesser Shielding

This is an upgrade to the level 1 minor shielding. It adds armor class and hit points.

Shield Of Fire

Shield of fire is a damage shield that can be cast on yourself and others.

Shock Of Blades

This is a direct damage offensive spell.

Level 8 pets are elementalings.

Level 12

Bind Affinity

This spell allows you to change you or another player in your parties bind location.

Burnout

Burnout is a pet buffing spell. It grants your pet additional strength and armor class. It also causes your pet to hit for more damage.

Cancel Magic

This spell will cancel a magical effect that is hindering you or a party member.

Column Of Fire

Column of fire is a heat based area effect spell which centers on your target.

Rain Of Blades

Rain of blades is an area effect rain spell.

Summon Blade

This spell will summon a magical weapon which can be passed out to party members.

Ward Summoned

Ward summoned is the first in the line of direct damage spells for use against summoned monsters(elements).

Level 12 pets are elementals.

Level 16

Identify

This spell will identify a magic item.

Phantom Leather

Phantom leather will absorb damage that would otherwise hurt the caster.

See Invisible

This spell will allow you to see invisible beings.

Shielding

Shielding is the third in the line of shielding spells. It adds armor class, hit points and magic resistance.

Shock of Flame

Shock of flame is a fire based direct damage spell.

Summon Heatstone

This spell will summon a heatstone which can be passed out to others. This stone will grant a player holding it infravision.

Summon Throwing Dagger

This spell will summon a throwing dagger which can be passed out to other players.

Level 16 variants are minor summoning spells.

Level 20

Elemental Shield

Elemental shield will add to a players resistance to heat and cold based attacks.

Bolt Of Flame

This is a fire based bolt spell. It will do direct damage to your opponent.

Rain Of Fire

This is a heat based area effect rain spell.

Shield Of Flame

Shield of flame is a fire based damage shield.

Summon Arrows

This spell will summon magical arrows which can be passed out to party members.

Summon Spear

This spell will summon a magical spear that can be passed out to group members. This is great for rogues who do not have a combine spear.

Level 20 variants are lesser summoning spells.

Level 24

Cornucopia

This spell will summon four food items at one time.

Everfont

This spell will summon four water items at one time.

Fire Flux II

This spell is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Major Shielding

Major shielding is an upgrade to the shielding line of spells.

Malise

Malise will reduce a players magic resistance. This is great to cast on an NPC before your wizards nuke away at him.

Shock Of Spikes

This is a direct damage offensive spell.

Level 24 variants are summoning spells.

Level 29

Burnout II

Burnout is a pet buffing spell.

Dismiss Summoned

Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the ward summoned spell. It is very mana efficent versus summoned beings.

Inferno Shield

Inferno shield is a damage shield which will punish monsters for hitting a player.

Phantom Chain

Phantom chain is an upgrade to the phantom leather spell. It is a damage absorbing spell.

Rain Of Spikes

This is an area effect rain spell.

Summon Coldstone

This spell creates a coldstone which can be passed out to group members. It grants the wielder ultravision.

Level 29 variants are greater summoning spells.

Level 34

Blaze

This spell is a DD spell, however not a bolt. It does around 275 damage.

Cinder Bolt

This is a direct damage bolt spell.

Greater Shielding

Greater shielding is an upgrade to the(self only) shielding line of spells.

Nullify Magic

Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Level 34 variants are minor conjuration spells.

Level 39

Barrier of Combustion

Barrier of combustion is an upgrade to the damage shield spells.

Expel Summoned

Expel summoned is an upgrade to the ward->dismiss summoned line of direct damage anti-summoned spells.

Fire Flux III

Fire flux is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Rain of Lava

This is an area effect rain spell.

Summon Mystical Dagger

This spell summons a high quality magical dagger which can be passed out to group members.

Summon Ring of Flight

This summons a magical ring which can be passed out to other players. The ring acts as a ring of levitation.

Level 39 variants are lesser conjuration spells.

Level 44

Arch Shielding

This is the last in the line of shielding spells.

Banish Summoned

This is the last of the anti summoned direct damage spells.

Elemental Armor

This spell adds to a players resistance to heat and cold based attacks.

Malisement

Reduces your opponents various resistances

Phantom Plate

This is an upgrade to the phantom leather->chain spells. It is a damage absorbing spell.

Shock Of Swords

This is a direct damage offensive spell.

Level 44 variants are conjuration Spells

Level 49

Burnout III

This is the last in the line of pet buffing spells.

Lava Bolt

This is a direct damage(heat based) offensive spell.

Rain of Swords

This is an area effect rain spell.

Shield of Lava

Shield of lava is a high level damage shield.

Level 49 variants are greater conjuration spells.
