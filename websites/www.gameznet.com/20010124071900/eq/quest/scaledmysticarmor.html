Scaled  Mystic Armor
This quest was submitted by Bahss:

These quests begin in Cabilis at either Shezlie or Zazlan Furscale. Each gives different quests. They result in Scaled Mystic Armor, some very good iksar shaman only armor.

Shezlie's Quests:

Helm - Bargynn's Digger, Sythrax Building Plans(from the troll in Field of Bone underwater tunnel), Sapphire, Banded Helm.
Gloves - Venemous Parchment, Warlord Drawing Quill(goblin warlords), ruby, banded gauntlets.
Legs - Sarnak Recruitment Letter(sarnak recruits), Exiled Iksar Head(exiled iksars), fire emerald, banded leggings. 
Breastplate - Watchmen's Spectacles, Nectar of Isolation, Star Ruby, Banded Tunic.

Zazlan's Quests: 

Arms - Frenzied Gnawer Tail, Froglok Treated Planks, Sapphire, Banded Sleeves.
Cloak - Giant Hammer(actually Forest Giant Hammer from Warslilk Woods), Worn Charbone(charbone skellies), Ruby, Banded Cloak.
Boots - Spirit Caller Beads(goblin spirit callers), Rhino Hoof, emerald of fire, Banded Boots.
Bracer - Coercion Implant, Gooey Adhesive, Ruby, Banded Bracer.
