Everquest Equipment Guide: Dagger Of Marnek

This necromancer only dagger can be found on the third floor of Befallen. It has a damage of 3 and a delay of 30. I know this sounds really bad, but don't get too discouraged yet. It can be placed into your thrown weapons slot and grants +3 intelligence and +15 mana.
