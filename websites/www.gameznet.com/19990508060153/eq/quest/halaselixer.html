Everquest Quest Guide: Elixer Quest

The following quest takes place at the warriors guild in Halas. Talk to an NPC named Dargon, who is at the enterance of the warriors guild. He will ask you if you are a warrior tell him that you are and he'll want you to give an elixer to a few people in Everfrost.  You'll give the elixer to an NPC right at the enterance to Everfrost and he'll give you directions to another NPC, which will give you another set of directions to another NPC.  This will continue untill you give a drink to a Megan O'Reilly. She'll then tell you to give the empty bottle back to Dargon.  He'll award you with a Rusty Butler (AC 1), some cash, and a faction adjustment.
