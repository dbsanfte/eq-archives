Deck of Spontaneous Generation

This quest begins in Rivervale and takes place in the Plane of Mischief. It was submitted by Armastevs.

Go to riverdale to the female vendor who sells varied fruits and veggies in the farm area. Say to her, "I will take turn". She will give you a sack of turnups that you take to Frejorner in plane of mischeif and he will give you the Deck of Spontaneous Generation which is used to combine the cards in.

The cards can be found in the plane of mischief itself. Different combinations will create different rewards. Known rewards are:

Blue Flower of Functionality - Black Throne, Red Throne, Blue Throne, White Throne.
Breastplate of Distraction - Red Throne, Black Throne, Black Crown, Black Knight. 
 Buckler of Doom - Black Throne, Black Crown, Black Knight, Black Squire.
Cloak of Confusion - Blue Throne, Blue Crown, Blue Knight, Blue Squire.
Globe of Darkness - Black Throne, Blue Throne, Black Crown, Blue Crown.
Greaves of Distraction - Blue Throne, Black Throne, Black Crown, Black Knight.
Green Wristguard - White Throne, Red Throne, White Crown, Red Crown.
Mushroom Bracelet - Black Throne, Blue Throne, Blue Crown, Blue Knight.
Onyx Wristbands - Red Throne, Blue Throne, Red Crown, Red Knight.
Robe of Distraction - Red Throne, Black Throne, Red Crown, Black Crown.
Silver Wristguards - Black Throne, Red Throne, Red Crown, Red Knight.
Vambraces of Distraction - White Throne, Black Crown, Black Knight, Black Squire.
