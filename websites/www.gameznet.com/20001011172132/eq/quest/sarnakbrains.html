Sarnak Brains
This quest is a great way to raise faction with the guilds of Cabilis. Killing Sarnaks will raise your faction with the Legion of Cabilis(warriors/guards). However I have yet to find any mobs that raise faction with the other guilds or merchants for those who are KOS to those factions. This one will work however if you are at least dubious to the Legion of Cabilis.

Warlord Geot can be found in the Lake of Ill Omen right next to the entrance to Cabilis. He is on the top floor of a tower so be wary. Shadowknights patrol out here and though you may not be KOS by the legion you might be KOS to them.

Warlord Geot will reward you(with a piece of food usually) for every four sarnak brains that you return to him. These brains can be found on the low level Sarnaks that patrol near by. Turning four brains in raises faction with everyone in Cabilis. Be forewarned however that it raises slowly and will take a LOT of work to raise from KOS to a more reasonable level.
