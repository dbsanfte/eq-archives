Why EQ'Lizer Joined Flashlink

As some of you may know, EQ'Lizer was almost shut down by our ISP in early 2002. When this happened, we have been actively talking to every banner provider we can to try and locate a consistent source of revenue to pay for the bandwidth used on this site. We searched high and low, but when we got the next bill for over $ 4,000.00 for a single months expenses, the simple truth has hit us pretty hard... there is no banner provider willing to pay enough to even cover the bandwidth charges on this site.

EQ'Lizer has the oldest EverQuest item database on the web and we didn't want to be forced offline. There are too many people that enjoy this site, and we love running it, especially with all the amazing new features we keep adding all the time.

In searching for every possible way to cover the costs, we found only one possible solution. This solution is to join Flashlink and charge a small membership fee to access the information on our site. NOW WAIT!! Before you get all upset, please read the rest of this and actually understand what it means. We had two choices, add Flashlink to the site, or take the site offline. Would you really rather see EQ'Lizer completely offline, or contribute less than the cost of a single cup of fancy coffee to help us stay around?

In addition, once you sign up for Flashlink here at EQ'Lizer, you also get full access to both EQ Maps and EQ Prices. That's complete access to all THREE of the top EverQuest sites on the internet with one membership. Between our three databases we have over 20,000 items listed, over 10,000 NPC's, over 3,000 different spells, and hundreds of quests! This is more information than ANY other EQ site can offer (including the one which is charging up to 25% more for their "premium membership")

We are asking for your help here to allow us to stay online, and doing our best to give you something of great value in return. Our site helps you elevate your game to the next level, and with how inexpensive a membership is, you won't even notice the difference in your wallet!

Your help by signing up is greatly appreciated and will allow this site to remain online with the most up to date EverQuest information for many years to come.

We thank you for your support and help,
The Staff at EQ'Lizer
