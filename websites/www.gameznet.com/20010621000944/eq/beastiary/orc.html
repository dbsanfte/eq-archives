Everquest Beastiary Guide: Orc

Orcs are an evil and twisted race. There are several clans of orcs throughout Norrath. There are the orcs of Oasis which are purplish in color and range up to level 20. There are the Crushbone orcs that range up to level 18. There are the orcs of the Deathfist clan which frequent the Commonlands and Misty Thicket and range up to level 16 or so. Last but not least there are the orcs from Everfrost which range up to about level 15 or so.

Wherever orcs exist they are hated. They are quite often parts of quests by the neighboring civilizations.
