Everquest Quest Guide: Claim Receipt

The following quest was submitted by Doug:

I dont' know if you're updating the site or not, but I thought I'd let you know about a quest I found as a newbie. In Surefall Glades, in the rangers guild, there is a guy standing next to the guy who is upset about his son being eaten by a bear. Hail him and he will say something about needing an errand run for him. He'll give you a claim receipt and tell you to take it to the wooden weapons maker in South Qeynos. When you deliver this claim receipt to the weapons maker he will give you a wooden totem carving of his daughter, or his deity or something, when you get this, take it back to him in Surefall Glades and he'll give you exp and some gold. You also get exp when you turn in the claim receipt. This is something for the newbie rangers to do to get a little cash and some exp.
