Plane of Justice Flag Guide

You say, 'Hail, Mavuin'

Mavuin strains his eyes to look up at you in disbelief, then reaches down into a rusted vent and pulls out a journal. 'Please I implore you, take word to the Tribunal. I have done nothing wrong, yet I have been sentenced to eternity in this cell. I wish to present all of the [information] about my case to them. They must listen to my plea if they are truly the gods of justice.'

You say, 'what information?'

Mavuin tells you, 'I know it has been said for years before me, and will be said for years after me, but I was truly framed for my crime. You see the gods feared the knowledge that I carry with me. So to prevent me passing this knowledge to others, it was set up to show that I had plotted to murder one of the other elders. Please go before the tribunal and plead my case. If you do this I will share what I know with you, I apologize for holding it against you, but I am desperate to be released.'
You have received a character flag!

Then we went to the Tribunal to talk to them about his case...

You say, 'what about Mavuin?'

The Tribunal tells you, 'So, mortal, you wish us to allow a prisioner to plead his case? What power do you think that you hold that we should field your interests? You must prove yourself in one of our trials before we will consider hearing this plea.'

You say, 'how do I prove myself in one of your trials?'

The Tribunal says 'The trials of the Tribunal are no easy affair. They will severely test your might and skill. Are you prepared for such an ordeal?'

You say, 'I am prepared for such an ordeal'

The Tribunal nods slightly. 'Very well. When you are ready, you may begin the trial of lashing. You must protect the victims from their tormentors. Be wary of the scourge of honor - you cannot fight it directly. You must find and destroy its life force to defeat it. We shall judge the mark of your success.'

You say, 'I am ready for the trial of lashing'

The Tribunal says 'Then begin.'

Be buffed, grouped in a balanced group and ready for battle.  Once you say that line you will pop into another area that consists of rows of benches in front of gallows. 

*Note there is more than one Trial you can do as a group depending on which Tribunal you speak to. 

Each trial requires you to kill the spawning NPC's before the Executionar executes the prisoner(s) on the gallows.  Once NPC's spawn the Executionar starts walking from the back of the room to the gallows and will speak to the prisoner(s) briefly, then execute them.  You have to target and kill one mob quickly to reset the Executionar.  If you do not kill a mob fast enough to reset the Executionar, you will fail. 

The trial consists of either 3 mobs in 4 waves (lashing trial) or 4 mobs in 3 waves.  If you save the prisoner(s) from being killed, a named Prime Executor boss mob will spawn.  He drops the Mark of Execution, which you need to loot to prove you completed the trial task.

After you have looted this item, go back to the Tribunal and say, "What about Mavuin?" and you will receive a character flag.  Then go back to see Mavuin.  Hail him and follow the quest dialogue to receive your last flag.

YOU NEED THREE FLAGS TO COMPLETE THE QUEST TO ENTER THE PLANE OF VALOR AND THE PLANE OF STORMS.

There are several "trails", hail the Tribunals to find out which triggers which trail:
1) To start a Trial go to one of the Tribunal's and say "Prepared".
2) He will then say what trial you are at
3) Get your group on the pad and say "Ready for trial of XXXXXXX" the x's are the trials name
4) Your group will now be ported in the trial.

Hanging
30 sec after you enter 3 mobs spawn:
1 spirit of suffocation and 2 random(breathless voids or stiffling nemesis)

Spirit can spawn as named version which is invisible except for name – named spirit is unmezzable
Normal versions can spawn as a named, mezzable with any mez higher than GoK

You have roughly 45 sec – 1 min to kill spirit before he kills prisoners
Spirit has roughly 10k hp, is mezzable, and summoned spells work
Second spirit spawns a set time after the wave begins, even if first spirit is still alive
When second spirit dies, another wave spawns about 45 sec later, regardless if your previous wave is dead or not but only if the prisoners are still alive.

Breathless voids tash and charms – mezzable and summoned spells work
Nemesis procs hundred blows (stun + dd) and is unmezzable

There are 4 waves. Upon the 8th spirit of suffocation death, Gallow Masters something spawns, hits for 750 and is an enchanter mob, also charms.

Execution
1) 4 waves of 4 mobs each
2) Each mob has 8k hp, even boss mobs
3) Rocks can be banished by druids/mages – this can sometimes reset the executioner’s walk and sometimes not – possibly related to whether or not the mob has taken damage.
4) You have exactly 30 seconds to kill each mob or the executioner will chop the head off the prisoner. Killing a mob resets this timer. about 333 dps needed.
5) Executioner spawns as the boss at the end (he can be slowed) hits for 700’s
Rocks are not mezzable, whereas razorfiends are.
You can’t banish named rocks.
6) Kill rocks first because they 96 second mez. (get high MR)
7) when done, fail or pass go to the mob in the corner and say "Return" this ports you back to the tribnual area.


Flame
Fire elementals/sprites, spawn in waves of 4, hit around 300ish with fire procs, can also dispel and possibly nuke as well.
The goal is to keep them from getting to the middle of the room.
Fire doesn't seem to have a time limit, but it's pretty tough.. The boss might spawn after the last mob dies, but it could have been coincidence.. Not sure.
You also have an uncurable 200/tick fire DoT eating at you the whole time.

Remove curse gets rid of the ae dot

Lashing
Exactly like Execution with the removal of the time limit, removal of the boss
When a Scourge of Honor spawns, you need to kill a wisp mob in one of the side rooms to kill it (possibly both of the side rooms) – the Scourge itself is untargetable.

Stoning
A spirit of suffocation (enchanter) spawns with 2 other mobs which are either rocks (enchanter) or fungus beasts (melee, procs one-hundred blows (dd + stun)). Then after a bit (30-45 seconds I'd guess), a second spirit of suffocation spawns that needs to be killed before the prisoners die.

Torture
Mobs come in from the door in the room
After killing a wraith of agony on the third wave, everything in the room got healed by an AE including the prisoners, the mobs, and the group.

In order to reach the Seventh Hammer, you must beat all six trials. After you get all six Marks from the trials, go up to any of the Tribunal with them in your inventory and say "I seek knowledge". This will give you the seventh mark, The Mark of Justice, which you click on a blue exit portal with to get to the room containing the Seventh Hammer, whom you must then fight.
