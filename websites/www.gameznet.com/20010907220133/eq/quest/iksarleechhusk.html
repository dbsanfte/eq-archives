Iksar Leech Husk Quest
This quest was submitted by Brad:

Master Bain at the monk guild in Cabilis will give you a container and tells you to place 4 leech husks in it.  Once you combine it, you return it to Klok Mugruk in the Haggle Baron.   He gives you back another bag, and tells you to combine it in a sewing kit along with 3 granite pebbles.  You get granite pebbles from The Toilmaster.  He tells you that if you bring him 2 broken shackles from escaped froglock slaves in the Swamp of No Hope, he'll give you a granite pebble.  Finding these slaves is pretty difficult.. they're a rare spawn, but it seems the shackle drops most of the time.  Once you give the shackles to The Toilmaster, and receive your 3 piles of granite pebbles, you combine them along with the training bag in a large sewing kit.  Make sure the piles are NOT stacked.  I made the combine on my first try with 5 points in Tailoring, I don't know if it's a 100% success thing or not, but considering how many hours it took to get 6 shackles, I decided I'd better put some points into the skill.  Doing this gets you a monk training bag.  You give this back to Master Bain, he gives you some exp, some faction, a couple of gold, and I got some "Sparring Arm Guards", which are not very good!  They're AC 3, weight .4, which is the same as the curscale sleeves, which are MUCH easier and less time consuming to get.  I have heard that you can also get several other items from this quest so it might be a grab bag of good and bad.
