Qeynos Hills

Click Here For A Map

Qeynos Hills borders with Qeynos, Surefall Glade(a tiny town of the druids and rangers in the region) and West Karana. It also serves as an entry point to the gnoll dungeon of Black Burrow.

This zone is targetted for players levels 4-10. You will find several species of bear, wolf, snake, bat and rat. You will also find wandering gnolls.

Beware however when fighting the wolves and bears because there are two high level NPC's by the name of Cros Treewind and Holly Windstalker who patrol the region and will attack anyone who dares to fight bears and wolves. This can be especially irritating since these monsters are agressive and you can be killed simply because a monster attacked you.

Another thing the lookout for is rabid bears and wolves. These rabid beasts are extremely aggressive and will infect you with rabies. This irritating disease will kill your regeneration when standing and reduce it to half when sitting. These rabid beasts are part of a quest in one of the clerics guilds in Qeynos. Return diseased wolf pelts to one of the clerics inside the temple(use the teleportal) to receive some faction, experience and a free cure disease. Return diseased bear pelts for miscellaneous goodies, experience and faction. Beware diseased bear pelts will not grant a cure disease so try to keep a diseased wolf pelt ready.

There are a couple of semi-rare NPC's who travel in the region including the dreaded Tovax Vmar. One of the rare goodies that can be found from these NPC's includes the Glowing Black Stone.

This zone also is the centerpiece of a couple of quests including the Jaggedpine Crook, Gnasher Furgott and the Chanda and Babob Miller quests.
