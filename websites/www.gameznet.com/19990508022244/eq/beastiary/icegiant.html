Everquest Beastiary Guide: Ice Giant

Ice giants are a very powerful form of giant indeed. Ice giants can be found inside of Permafrost and guarding it's entrance in Everfrost. They generally are in the level 40ish range and hit extremely hard(120s). They should be fought with extreme caution as one wrong move can easily turn into a quick and painful death.
