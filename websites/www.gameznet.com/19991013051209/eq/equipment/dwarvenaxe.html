Everquest Equipment Guide: Dwarven Axe

There are two types of dwarven axes.

These items can be found on the Orc Lord at Crushbone.  1 handed dwarven axes the best non magical 1 handed slashing weapons in the game. They feature a damage of 6 and an attack delay of only 26. They are also much lighter than minotaur axe which is important when duel wielding. 2 handed dwarven axes can be found rarely on the Lord at crushbone. These axes have a damage of 14 and a delay of 43. They are non magical but are very efficient.
