Everquest Zone Guide: Castle Mistmoore

Mistmoore Castle is one of my favorite zones in the game. It is a great zone for characters level 20 all the way to level 50. The entrance can be found in the zone of lesser faydark.

In the entrance area you will find several level 20ish dark elf guards. Beware that most of these are casters. At night time undead skeletons and zombies will spawn in place of these guards so it is wise to kill the guards off during the day. More importantly in these spawn locations you may also have the opportunity for the much sought after werewolves.

This is a general consistancy in this zone. The night time monsters are much stronger and more powerful than the day time ones. Lower level characters would be advised to leave this zone at night. In the graveyard and castle area you will find gargoyles and vampires.

The head honcho in this zone is Lord Mahong Mistmoore a level 53 vampire who spawns in the tower near the entrance. This tower must be entered through a secret passageway near the graveyard. Lord Mistmoore is no pushover he can punch for 160 points of damage and he once cast an area effect spell which did 320 points of damage to our entire party. Lord Mistmoore only spawns occasionally and always spawns at night. During the day a level 31 vampire by the name of Tisnya Darvie spawns in his place. Also guarding the tower are two level 31ish Ritualists who are vampires as well and can charm.
