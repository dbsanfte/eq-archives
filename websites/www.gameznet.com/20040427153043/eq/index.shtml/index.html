Monday, April 26, 2004

Uqua Items
 23:08 EST - J.C. Smith

Today's update features the first drops from Uqua.

New 1HBlunt: Flanged Prayer Mace.
New Arms: Watchman's Supple Armbands.
New Back: Lavender Cloak of Destruction.
New Face: Twisted Mask of Irony.
New Feet: Sodden Turepta Boots.
New Hands: Gloves of Watery Distraction.
New Secondary: Bulwark of Uqua, Siren's Shield.

Friday, April 23, 2004

GoD, PoP and Acrylia
 7:32 EST - J.C. Smith

The highlight of this update is the Berserker's Time quest breastplate.

New Augmentation: Gleaming Stone of Vengeance.
New Chest: Galaldan's Stormwrath Tunic.
New Feet: Grimling Plate Boots.
New Head: Helm of the Guardian.
New Waist: Belt of the Broken Slave.

Wednesday, April 21, 2004

Few More GoD Baked Goods
 5:13 EST - J.C. Smith

There's nothing new to post but I did add a few new baked items from Gates of Discord.

New Food: Smoked Tuna Pie, Smoked Tuna Rolls, Smoked Tuna Sandwich, Smoked Tuna Salad.

Tuesday, April 20, 2004

Cafe Update
 5:40 EST - J.C. Smith

So I got all moved in to my new house this weekend, but I hit a slight snag. They won't have my phone line and DSL installed for two weeks so this update is coming to you from a cafe. Items are incoming slow at the moment as everyone is stuck currently in progression, so this shouldn't be too much of a hassle. I had to scrounge hard to find an update today.

New Ear: Electrum Shimmering Earring.
New Food: Smoked Tuna Stew, Smoked Tuna Steaks, Smoked Tuna Soup, Smoked Tuna Souffle.
New Neck: Platinum Shimmering Pendant.

Saturday, April 17, 2004

Berserker Epic Quest
 0:22 EST - J.C. Smith

Today's update includes a spoiler for the Berserker Epic Quest.

New 2HSlash: Kerasian Axe of Ire.
New Inventory: McArik Crafted Bandage.
New Legs: Galladan's Stormwrath Leggings, Leggings of the Timekeeper, Hardened Serpent-Scale Greaves.
New Secondary: Dark shield of the Scholar.
New Wrist: Galladan's Stormwrath Bracer, Traveler's Bracelet of Thought.
