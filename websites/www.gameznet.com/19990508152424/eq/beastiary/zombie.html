Everquest Beastiary Guide: Zombies

Zombies are another favorite target of clerics. They are the next logical step up from skeletons as they range between level 7-9(where a skeleton ranges between level 5-7). This is a perfect level range for a level 6-10 cleric or a level 8-10 necromancer who is itching at a chance to powerlevel with their favorite spell at this level: Ward Undead. However zombies can also be a worthwhile target for other classes between levels 7-12 because they carry a decent amount of loot and they also carry items which can be sold or used.

Zombies are a bit less common than skeletons but still can be found in a variety of zones. They can be found both outdoors(as seen in the East Commonlands for example) and indoors(as seen in Befallen).

HIGH LEVEL ZOMBIES

High level zombies can be found in the zone of Mistmoore. These zombies are more or less identical to lower level zombies except they are much stronger and carry better loot.
